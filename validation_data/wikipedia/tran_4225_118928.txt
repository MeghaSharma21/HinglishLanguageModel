 devtaa, div dhaatu, jiskaa arth prakaashmaan hona he, se nikalataa he. arth he koi bhi paraloukik shakti kaa paatr, jo amar or paraprakritik he or isaliye poojaneey he. devtaa athwa dev is tarah ke purushon ke liye prayukt hota he or devi is tarah kii striyon ke liye. hindu dharm main devataaon ko yaa to parameshwar kaa loukik rup mana jaataa he, yaa to unhen ishwar kaa sagun rup mana jaataa he.
 brihadaarany upanishad main ek bahut sundar samvaad he jisamen yah prashn he ki kitne dev hai. uttar yah he ki vastav main keval ek he jiske kai rup hai. pehla uttar he 33 karod; or poochne par 3339; or poochne par 33; or poochne par 3 or ant main deb or phir keval ek.
 veda mantron ke vibhinn devtaa he. prayek mantra kaa rishi, kilak or devtaa hota he.
 devataaon kaa vargeekaran kai prakaar se hua he, iname chaar prakaar mukhya he:-              pehla sthaan kram se           doosraa parivaar kram se.           teesraa varg kram se            chauthe samooh kram se            1- sthaan kram se varnit devtaa -- dyusthaneey yani upree akaash main nivas karne vaale devtaa, madhyasthaneey yani antariksh main nivas karne vaale devtaa, or tisare prithvisthaneey yani prithvi par rahane vaale devtaa maane jate hai.           2. parivaar kram se varnit devtaa -- in devataaon main aditya, vasu, rudra aadi ko gina jaataa he.    3. varg kram se varnit devtaa -- in devataaon main indravarun, mitravarun aadi devtaa aate hai.   4. samooh kram se varnit devtaa -- in devataaon main sarv deva aadi kii ginati kii jaati he.
 rugveda ke sookhton main devataaon kii stutiyon se devataaon kii pahchaan kii jaati he, inamen devataaon ke naam agni, vaayu, indra, varun, mitravarun, ashvinikumar, vishvadeva, saraswati, ritu, marut, twashta, brahamnaspati, som, dakshina indrani, varunani, dyu, prithvi, pusha aadi ko pahachaanaa gaya he, or inaki stutiyon kaa vistrut varnan miltaa he. jo log devataaon kii anekta ko nahin maanate he, ve sab naamon kaa arth parabrahm paramatma vaachak lagaate he, or jo alag alag maanate hai ve bhi paramatmatmak rup main inako maanate he. bhartiya gaathaom or puraanon main in devataaon kaa maanvikaran athwa purushikaran hua he, fir inaki murtiyan banane lagi, fir inke sampradaay banane lage, or alag alag puja paath hone lage dekhen, sabase pehle jin devataaon kaa vargeekaran hua unamem brahma, vishnu or shiv kaa udai hua, iske baad main lagaataar inaki sankhya main vruddhi hoti chali gayi, niruktkaar yask ke anusaar," devataad kii utpatti aatma se hi maani gayi he", devataaon ke sambandh main yah bhi kaha jaataa he, ki "tistro devtaa".arthath devtaa teen he, kintu yah pradhan devtaa he, jinke prati sristi kaa nirmaan, iskaa paalan or iskaa sanhaar kiya janaa mana jaataa he. mahabharat ke main inkaa varnankram is prakaar se kiya gaya he:-
 adityagan kshatriya devtaa, marudagan vaisya devtaa, ashvini gana shudra devtaa, or angiras brahaman devtaa maane gaye hai. shatapath brahman main bhi isi prakaar se devataaon ko mana gaya he.
 shuddh bahuishwaravaadi dharmon main devataaon ko puri tarah swatantra mana jaataa he.
 brahma,vishnu,rudra,savita,mitra,varun he. 
 dekhiye : ishwar.
