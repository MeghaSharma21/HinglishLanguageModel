 bharatmuni ne apane prasiddh granth naatyashaastra main aath prakaar kii nayikaon kaa varnan kiya he, jinhen ashtnayika kehete hai. aath prakaar kii nayikaayen ye hai-
 'nayika' kii pariklpana sanskrit sahitya, visheshkar bharatmuni kii dein he, jo naatyashaastra ke rachayita or bhartiya shastreey nrity or naatak ko anushaashit karne vaale siddhanton ke pratipaalak the. bharat ne purush or mahilaon kaa vargeekaran kiya, jinhen nayak or nayika, yani kramshah premi or premika kaha gaya. yah vargeekaran unki shaareerik or maansik visheshtaon, gunon, manodashaon, swabhaav or bhavatmak avasthaaon or sthitiyon ke anusaar kiya gaya. iske antargat nari ke manobhaavon kaa or uske prem k vibhinn charnon kaa vargeekaran bade utsaah or sookshmata se kiya he.
