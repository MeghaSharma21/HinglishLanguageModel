 rasanayake mudiyanselge sanjeev bandara kaviratn ek srilankai raajnneetijna the. wo kendriya prantiy parishad or sansad sadsy the.
 sanjeev kaviratn saanskrithik maamalon ke bhootpurv mantri swargeey pibi kaviratn ke putr the. wo purv krishi mantri jepi vijeratn band ke damaad the. unhone apni shiksha anand college, colombo se praapt kii. wo kendriya prantiy parishad ke 1993 ke chunaav ke saath raajnneeti main aaye. unhone 2001 ke aam chunaavon main safalataa praapt kii or matale jile ke pratinidhi ke rup main sansad main pahunche.
