 thayouria ek carbonic yogic he jiskaa anusutra SC2 he. sanrachana kii drushti se yah uria ke samaan hi he, antar keval itnaa he ki uria kaa oxygen parmaanu ke sthaan par gandhak parmaanu he. kintu thayouria ke gun, uria se bahut alag hai. carbonic sanshleshan main thayouria ek abhikarmak he. C=S sanrachana vaale sabhi yogicon ko sammilit rup se 'thayouria' kehete hai.
