 kuch ped-paudhon se ni:srav nikalataa he. kuch se to swat: nikalataa he or kuch se chhedne yaa kaatane se nikalataa he. inamen se kuch ni:sraavon ko balsam kehete hai. balsam main rezin, alp maatra main gond, kuch vashpashil tel or vibhinn matraaon main sourabhik amla or unke aster rahete hai. yadi ni:srav main vashpashil tel kii maatra adhik or thos sourabhik amla kii maatra bilkul na ho to aise ni:srav ko 'oliorazin' kehete hai.
 balsam saadharantaya shyaan drav, athwa ardh thos, hota he. isamen vishesh saurabh hota he or tikshn, par kuch ruchikar swaad hota he. saurabh pradaan karanevaale padaarth benzoik, cinemic or isi prakaar ke anya carbonic amla or unke aster hai. balsam kai prakaar ke hote hai, jinamein benzoin, peru balsam, storacs, tolubalsam, jenthoria, canada balsam or copaba balsam mahatv ke hai.
 benzoin ko arabi bhashaa main lobaan tathaa sanskrit main 'devdhoop' kehete hai. yah pedo se praapt hota he. ye ped korea, sumatra, java aadi dwipon main paae jate hai. vyapaar kaa lobaan korea, sumatra, palamwang, padang or penang balsam ke naamon se khyath he. sab balsam sanghathan main ek se nahin hote. unamem vibhinnata pai jaati he.
 benzoin pedo se swat: nahin nikalataa. pedo ke tanon ko kulhadi se gaharaa kaatane se jo kataav ban jaataa he, usase balsam nikalakar ikattha hota he. paryaapt kathor ho jane par iskaa niryaat hota he. chote chote tukdon athwa kundon main yah baahar bheja jaataa he. achche kism ke balsam main mand, ruchikar gandh hoti he. nimna koti ke sumatra benzoin ko 'penang benzoin, kehete hai. palamwang benzoin bhi sumatra se hi aata he. ye benzoin dhoop ke liye upyukt hote hai.
 vyapaar ke benzoin main bahut se bahya padaarth mili rahete hai. yadi usamen koi milawat na ho, to gandh or alcodaul main vileyata uski pahchaan he.
 benzoin main praaia: 20 prati shat cinemic amla or 10 se 15 pratishat benzoik amla, pradhanthya aster ke rup main rahete hai. inke atirikt styrine, venilin, phenol-propeel cinemet, cinemil cinemet, benzoracenol cinemet, benzaldihide or benzin rahete hai. korea ke benzoin main cinemic amla bilkul nahin hota.
 oshadhiyon main prayukt honevaale balsam main nimnlikhit visheshtaayen rahani chahie :
 benzoin kaa upyog oshadhiyon or sugandhit dravyon ke nirmaan main hota he.
 yah bhure rang kaa choe jaisaa shyaan drav he. isamen prabal ruchikar or balsam si gandh hoti he. sugandhit dravyon ke nirmaan or alp maatra main oshadhiyon main iskaa upyog hota he. isse nakli amber bhi banataa he. iskaa aapekshik ghanatv 1.14 se 1.17 or apvartanaank 1.580 se 1.586 he. isamen balsam aster 53 pratishat se kam nahin rahanaa chahie.
 ped kii chaal ko jhulasane ke baad balsam nikalataa he, jo tane main lapete kapadon main ikattha hota he. is kapade ke nichodne se balsam praapt hota he. jal ke saath ubaalane se iskaa shodhan hota he.
 turki desh main ek ped hota he, jiske chhevne yaa peetane se balsam nikalataa he. yah paraandh, dhoosar rang kaa shyaan drav hota he, jisamen ped kii kuch chaal mili rahati he. isamen 20 se 30 pratishat jal rahata he. oshadhiyon main iskaa vyavhaar hota he. british pharmakopia ke anusaar isamen nimnlikhit visheshtaayen rahani chahie : jal ooshmak par ek ghanta sukhaane par jo namoona praapt hota he, usamen 30 pratishat balsamik amla rahanaa chahie. jal ooshmak par sukhaane se 5 pratishat se adhik kaa hraas nahin hona chahie. sookhe namoonay kaa amlamaan 55 se 90, asterman 100 se 132 or sabunikaran maan 170 se 200 rahanaa chahie.
 venizvila, equador or brazil main paae jane vaale ek ped ke tane se yah balsam praapt hota he. yah komal, par drudh, rezin sa padaarth he, jo rakhane par kadaa or jaade main bhangur ho jaataa he. iskaa swaad khatta or gandh ruchikar hoti he. sugandhit dravyon ke nirmaan main iskaa vyavhaar hota he. gandhon ke sthayikaarak ke rup yah kaam aata he. isamen 10 se 15 pratishat asamyukt cinemic amla or saat se das pratishat asamyukt benzoik amla rahata he. cinemic or benzoik amlon ke benzil aster isamen aath pratishat tak rahete hai. venilin kaa lesh rahata he. yah alcodaul, benzin, chlorofarm, ether or glacial aseetic amla main viley hota he.
 australia main ek ped hota he, jisase yah balsam nikalataa he. is balsam ko 'akeroid' rezin bhi kehete hai. yah laal or pilaa, do rang kaa hota he. isamen sungadh hoti he or sugandhit dravyon ke nirmaan main benzoin, storacs or tolu balsam ke sthaan main prayukt ho saktaa he. yah dhoop ke liye bhi vyavahrut hota he or mohar ke saste chapade ke nirmaan main kaam aata he. donon rang ke balsam ek hi sanghathan ke hote hai. avyavon kii vibhinnata se rang main antar aa jaataa he. ek main cinemic amla rahata or duusare main paracumeric amla. isse picrik amla ban saktaa he.
