 aamani ek bhartiya abhinetri he, jo mukhyatah telegu or tamil filmon main pradarshit hui he. i-ve.ve. satyanarayan dwaara nirdeshit film jamba lakdi pamba main vah pehali baar naresh ke saamne ek pramukh bhoomika main upasthit hui thim. unhone baapu dwaara nirdeshit film mister pelum main abhinay kiya, jisane telugu main sarvshereshth feechar film kaa rashtriya film puraskaar jeeta. 
 vah bengalooru main basane vaale tateey andhra telugu parivaar main paidaa hui thi. unhone apane karier kii shuruuaat filmon main choti bhoomikaayen kar ke kii.
