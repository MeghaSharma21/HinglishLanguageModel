 konkani goa, maharashtra ke dakshini bhaag, karnataka ke uttari bhaag, kerala ke kuch kshetron main boli jaati he. bhashaayi taur par yah 'arya' bhashaa parivaar se sambandhit he or marathi se iskaa kaaphi nikat kaa sambandh he. rajnatik taur par is bhashaa ko apni pahchaan ke liye marathi bhashaa se kaaphi sangharsh karnaa padaa he. ab bhartiya samvidhan ke tahat konkani ko aathvin anusuchee main sthaan praapt he.
 1987 main goa main konkani ko marathi ke baraabar rajbhasha kaa darja diya gaya kintu lipi par asahmati ke kaaran ajtak is par amal nahin kiya ja sakaa.
 konkani anek lipiyon main likhi jaati rahi he; jaise - devanagari, kannada, malayalam or roman. goa ko rajya kaa darja milane ke baad davanagari lipi main konkani ko vahaan kii rajbhasha ghoshit kiya gaya he.
 bharat ke pashchimi tat sthit konkan pradesh main prachlit boliyon ko saamanyat: konkani kehete he. aitihaasik or saanskrithik sambandhon ke parinamswaruva is pradesh main boli janevaali bhashaa ke teen rup hai-
 govavala pradesh anek shatee tak portugal ke adheen tha. vahaan portugaliyon ne jor jabardasti ke bal par logen se dharmaparivartan karaayaa or unke mul saanskrithik rup ko chhin-bhinn karne kaa prayaas kiya. in sab ke baavjood logon ne apni maatrubhaasha kaa parityag nahin kiya. ulte apane dharmopadesh ke nimitt isai padariyon ne vahaan kii boli main apane ganth rache. dharmantarit hue nae isai praaia: ashikshit log the. unhen isai dharm kaa tatv samajhane ke liye portugali padariyon ne konkani kaa aashray liya.
 pracheen kaal main goa se sashti tak ke bhubhag main jo boli boli jaati thi use hi log vishuddh konkani maanate the or use gomantki naam se pukaarte the tathaapi solahavin shatee tak uske liye koi vishisht naam rudh nahin tha. portugaliyon ko jaisaa samaj main aayaa, vaisaa hi naam use diya or pukaara. 1553 i. ke jesuit padariyon ke aalekhon main use kanari naam diya gaya he. 17veen shatee main padri steffens ne dautrin christaan naamak pustak likhi. usamen unka kahana tha ki use unhone kanari main likhaa he or gomantki boli kaa jo vyakaran unhone taiyaar kiya use unhone ‘kanari bhashaa kaa vyakaran’ naam diya. is kanari shabd kaa sambandh kannada se tanik bhi nahin he. varan samajha jaataa he ki samudr ke kinare kii bhashaa hone ke kaaran hi use kanari kaha gaya.
 tom pirish naamak yatri ne apni pustak ‘sum oriental’ main, jo 1515 i. main likhi gai thi, goa kii boli kaa naam konkoni diya he. 1658 main jesuit padri migled almaid ne bhi gomantaki ke liye 'konkani' shabd kaa prayog kiya he. ab yah shabd praaia: puure konkan pradesh kii bhashaa ke liye prayukt hota he. kuch log ise marathi kii upbhasha maanate hai to kuch kannada kii. kuch anya bhashavaigyanik ise aryvansh se udbhoot swatantra samrudh bhashaa bataate he.
 satrahavin shatee se purv is bhashaa kaa koi likhit sahitya uplabdh nahin he. is bhashaa ke saahitiyik prayog kaa shrey isai mishnariyon ko he. padri stiphs kii pustak dautrin christaan is bhashaa kii pratham pustak he jo 1622 i. main likhi gai thi. uske baad 1640 i. main unhone portugali bhashaa main iskaa vyakaran 'arti da lingav kanari’ naam se likhaa. isse purv 1563 i. ke aasapaas kisi sthaneey dharmantarit nivasee dwaara is bhashaa kaa kosh taiyaar hua or isai dharm ke anek granth likhe gaye. portugali shashan ke parinamswaroop sahitya nirmaan kii gati atyant mand rahi kintu ab is bhashaa ne ek samrudh sahitya kii bhashaa kaa rup dhaaran kar liya he. lokkatha, loakgeet, lokanaty to sangrihith hue hi hai, aadhunik naatak or ekanki kii rachana bhi hui he. anya vidhaon main bhi rachanaayen kii jane lagi he.
