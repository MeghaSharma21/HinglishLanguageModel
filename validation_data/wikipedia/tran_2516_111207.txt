 ahamed shah masood afghanistan ke ek rajnatik or sainik netaa the. 1979 se 1989 tak chalane vaale afghanistan main soviet yuddh ke dauraan ve soviet sangh ke viruddh ladane vaale ek mukhya vyakti the or phir baad main ve kattarpanthi taliban ke viruddh ladane vaale guton ke bhi sardaar rahe.
 ahamed shah masood ek tajik parivaar se aaye sunni muslim the. unki paariwarik jade uttari afghanistan kii panjashir vaadi main thim. 1970 ke dashak main unhone kabul vishvavidhyalay main enginring kii shiksha lee or vahaan samyawad virdohi sanghon main bhaag lene lage. jab 1979 main soviet sangh ne apni faujen afghanistan bhejin to unhone unke viruddh abhiyaan chalaaye. apane zordaar sainik hamalon ke liye unhen 'panjashir kaa sher' ke naam se janaa jane laga. soviet senaaon ke jane ke baad 1992 main 'peshawar samjhaute' ke tahat unhen rashtriya raksha mantri niyukt kiya gaya.
 janvari 1995 main charampanthi taliban kii faujon ne rajdhani kabul ko gher liya or 1996 main taliban lagbhag puure afghanistan par haaavee hokar shashan karne lagi. ahamed shah masood ne taliban kii kattarpanthi vichaardhaara ko thukraa kar unke viruddh hathiyaar uthaa liye. ve sanyukt islamic morche ke netaa ban gaye jo 'uttari mitrapaksh' ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa tha. 9 sitambar 2001 ko al-qaayada dwaara rachit ek aatmghaatee bam-hamale main ve maare gaye. iske do din baad sanyukt rajya america main 11 sitambar 2001 ke aatankwaadi hamale hue jiske baad america or uske saathiyon ne masood ke morche ke saath mitrataa karke taliban ko satta se nikaal diya.
 afakhaan raashtrapati hamid karzai ne aadesh jari karke ahamed shah masood ko 'rashtriya nayak' kaa kitaab diya. unki punyatithi afghanistan main 'masood diwas' naam kii rashtriya chuttee he. unke samarthak unhen 'aamir sahib-e-shahid' ke naam se bulaate hai.
