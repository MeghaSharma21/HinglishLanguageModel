 ramswami venkataraman, bharat ke 8wein raashtrapati the. ve 1987 se 1992 tak is pad par rahe. raashtrapati banane ke pehle ve 4 varshon tak bharat ke uparashtrapati rahe. mangalvaar ko 27 janvari ko lambii beemaari ke baad unka nidhan ho gaya. ve 98 varsh ke the. raashtrapati, uparashtrapati or pradhanmantri samet desh bhar ke anek rajanetaaon ne unke nidhan par shok vyakt kiya he. unhone 2:30 baje dilli main sena ke research and refral hospital main antim saans lee. unhen mutrashay main sankraman kii shikaayat ke baad vigat 12 janvari ko aspataal main bhartee karaayaa gaya tha. ve saans sambandhi beemaari se bhi peedit the. unka kaarykaal 1987 se 1992 tak rahaa. raashtrapati pad par asin hone se purv venkataramana kareeb chaar saal tak desh ke uparashtrapati bhi rahe.
 venkataramana kaa janm 4 disambar, 1910 ko tamilnadu main thanjore ke nikat pattukottay main hua tha. unki zyaadaatar shiksha-diksha rajdhani chennai main hi hui. unhone arthshaastra se snatakottar oopaadhi madras vishvavidhyalay se praapt kii. iske baad unhone madras ke hi lau college se kanoon kii padhai kii. shiksha puri karne ke baad unhone madras ucch nyaayalay main san 1935 se vakaalat shuru kii or 1951 se unhone ucchtam nyaayalay main vakaalat shuru kii. vakaalat ke dauraan hi unhone desh ke swatantraaa andolan main bhaag liya or 1942 ke bharat chodo andolan main sakriy bhoomika nibhaai. swatantraaa ke baad vakaalat main unki shreshthata ko dekhte hue bharat sarkaar ne unhen desh ke utkrisht vakeelon kii team main sthaan diya. 1947 se 1950 tak ve madras praant kii baar federation ke sachiv pad par rahe. kanoon kii jaankaree or chaatr raajnneeti main sakriy hone ke kaaran ve jald hi raajnneeti main aa gaye. san 1950 main unhen aaazaad bharat kii asthaayee sansad ke liye chunaa gaya. uske baad 1952 se 1957 tak ve desh kii pehali sansad ke sadsy rahe. ve san 1953 se 1954 tak congress party main sachiv pad par bhi rahe.
 1957 main sansad ke liye chune jane ke baavjood ramswami venkataramana ne lok sabha seat se istipha dekar madras sarkaar main ek mantri kaa pad bhaar grahan kiya. is dauraan unhone udyogon, samaaj, yatayaat, arthvyastha va janata kii bhalaai ke liye kai vikaspurn kaary kiye. 1967 main unhen yojana ayog kaa sadsy niyukt kiya gaya or unhen udyog, yatayaat va railway jaise pramukh vibhaagon kaa uttardaayitv saunpa gaya. 1977 main dakshin madras kii seat se unhen loksabhaa kaa sadsy chunaa gaya. jisamen unhone vipakshi netaa kii bhoomika nibhaai. 1980 main ve loksabhaa kaa sadsy chune jane ke baad indira gandhi kii sarkaar main unhen vitt mantralay kaa kaarybhaar saunpa gaya or uske baad unhen raksha mantri banaaya gaya. unhone pashchimi or purvi europe ke saath hi soviet union, america, canada, dakshin pashchimi asia, japan, australia, newjiland, yugoslavia or morishas kii aadhikarik yatraayen kin. ve agast 1984 main desh ke up raashtrapati bane. iske saath hi ve rajyasabha ke adhyaksh bhi rahe. is dauraan ve indira gandhi shanti puraskaar va jawaharlal nehru award for international anderstanding ke nirnaayak peeth ke adhyaksh rahe. unhone 25 july 1987 ko desh ke aathven raashtrapati ke rup main shapath lee.
 centre sarkaar ne purv raashtrapati aar. venkataramana ke sammaan main deshmen saat din kaa rashtriya shok ghoshit kiya he. ek sarkari pravaktaa ne bataaya ki is avadhi main koi bhi sarkari manoranjan kaarykram nahin hoga or sabhi sarkari imaaraton par tirangaa aadha jhukaa rahegaa. iske saath hi ganatantr diwas samaaroh ke baad hone vaale beating ratreet tathaa rashtriya cadet kor kii pradhanmantri raily samet sabhi sarkari kaarykram radd kar die gaye. buudhvaar 29 janvari ko nai dilli main ekta sthal par puure rajkeey sammaan ke saath unki antyeshti kii gai. unke damaad kebi venkat ne unhen mukhaagni di. sarvadharm prarthana or 21 topon kii salaami ke bich venkataramana kaa parthiv sharir panchtatv main villin ho gaya. isse pehle raashtrapati pratibha patil ne unako shradhanjali di. shuukravaar 30 janvari ko purv raashtrapati kii asthiyaan haridwar main ganga nadi main pravaahit kii gai. unke damaad dow ke venkataramana or professor aar ramchandran tathaa putree lakshmi ve venkateshwar dilli se unki asthiyaan lekar yahan pahunche. vishisht ghat par uttarakhand ke shiksha mantri madan kowshik, haridwar ke jiladhikari, shailesh bagoli or esaspi sanjay gunjyaal bhi upasthit the. purv raashtrapati ke parivaar ke sadasyon ke saath dilli se aae pandit sundar raghav sharma ne ghat par puja-archana kii or baad main nadi main asthiyaan pravaahit kii gai. isse pehle sena kii chhathi artilari brigade ke sheersh adhikariyon ne pavitra shahar ke bahari ore sthit raywala kshetra main venkataramana ke asthikalash par pushp maala arpit kii.
