 monti haul paheli is paheli ko kehete hai:
 maan lijie ki aap ek game sho main hai, or aapako teen daravaajon main se ek chunane ke liye kaha jaataa he. ek daravaaje ke peeche gaadi he, or baaki do ke peeche bakriyaan. aap darwaja number 1 chunate hai. host darwaja number 3 kholta he, or uske peeche se bakrii nikalati he. wo aapse poochta he "kya aap darwaja number 2 chunanaa chaahate hai?" kya darwaja number 2 chunane se aapke jitne kii sambhaavna bad jaaegi?
 is sthiti main darwaja badalane se gaadi jeetne kii sambhaavna 2/3 ho jaati he, jabaki darwaja na badalane se jeetne kii sambhaavna 1/3 rahati he. isliye darwaja badalane se pratiyogi ko laabh hota he.
