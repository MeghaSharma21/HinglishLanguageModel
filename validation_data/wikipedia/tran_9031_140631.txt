 bahah praant kaa naam bahah shahar se aayaa he. yah praant pahaado, maidaanon, vaadiyon or registani kshetron kaa mishran he. mukhya rup se iske do bhugolic hisse hai. 'tihama' pashchimi maidani kshetra he jo aage jaakar laal sagar ke kinare par pahunchataa he. 'al-sarvat' purv kaa pahari ilaaka he jo hijaaz pahadiyon kaa bhaag he. isaki oonchaai samudratal se 1,500 se 2,450 meter tak unchi hai. tihama main garmi main kadi garmi or sardi main mausam lubhaavna rahata he. al-sarvat main laal sagar se nami aati he jo sardiyon main kohre or aandhi-vaali barish ke rup main prakat hoti he. yahan kii pahadiyon main garmi main mausam acha rahata he, yani adhik garm nahin hota.
 is praant ke sabhi log sunni muslim he or islam shuru hone ke 10 varshon ke bheetar hi yah dharm yahan pahunch gaya tha. samaaj qabeelon, upqabeelon or pariwaron main vyavasthit he. har qabeele kaa ek sardaar hota he jise 'sheikh' kii oopaadhi milti he. yahan do qabeelon - gamid or zahraan - kaa jor he jo ek bade 'azd' naamak qabeele kii do shakhaayen hai. yahan se aaye logon ke paariwarik naam aksar 'al-gamadi' jaise rup main milte hai.
