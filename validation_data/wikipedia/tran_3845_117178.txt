 alphed north hwhiteead england ke ganitagya evam darshanik the. ve prakriya darshan naamak darshanik sampradaay ke pramukh darshanik hai.
 hwhiteead kaa janm 1861 main england main hua tha. trinity kalege main 1911-1914 main phelo rahe or unirvasity kalege, landan main 1914-24 main vyaavhaarik tathaa mikenix padhaane kaa kaary kiya. imperial kalege ov science or technalaji, landan main vyaavhaarik ganit ke adhyaaapak pad par bhi kaary kiya. 1924 main ve harvard vishvavidhyalay main darshan ke adhyaaapak niyukt hue. isi pad par unhone 1938 main avkaash grahan kiya.
 hwhiteead kii sarvaadhik prasiddh darshanik rachanaaon main 'princepia mathematica' teen bhaag, 'ain inquayri consarning di principles ov natural nalege', 'kacept ov nechar', science and di modern world', 'religen in di mekking', 'simbalism', 'process and reality', 'adventures ov idiaj', 'di principles ov relativity', or 'mods ov that' hai.
 hwhiteead darshan ke kshetra main kaam karne ke purv vaigyanik ke rup main prasiddh ho gaye the. ve ganitiya tarkshastra ke pravartakon main se ek the. tristh varsh kii umr main unhone ganit kaa adhyaapan kaary chhodkar darshan kaa adhyapakapad sweekaar kar liya tha. abhi tak darshan ke kshetra main antim satta kaa nirdhaaran manas yaa pudgal ke rup main kiya jaataa tha. unhone is vibhaajan paddati par vichaar karne kaa virodh kiya. gatisheel bhautikee se prabhavit hokar unhone apni darshanik paddati kii sthapana kii. unke matanusaar sat ek hi he or jo kuch prathit hota he yaa hamaare pratyakshikaran main aata he vah yatharth he. vyakti ke anubhav main aanevaali satta ke pare kisi vastu kaa astitv nahin he. sansar main na sthir pratyay he or na dravya; keval ghatnaon kaa ek sanghat he. sab ghatnaayen dikkaaleey ikaiyaan he. dik or kaal kii alag-alag avadharana bhraamak he.
 hwhiteead kii darshanik paddati 'jaiviny' kehalaati he. sab ghatnaayen ek duusare ko prabhavit karti hai or swayan bhi prabhavit hoti hai. yah sansar jaiviarup se ek he. aadharbhoot tatv gati yaa prakriya hi he. vah sarjanaatmak he. srijan kaa murtarup ishwar he. srijan sarvapratham ishwar rup main hi vyakt hota he. hamaare anubhav main aanevaale tathy anubhuthaikan kahe ja sakate hai. unke pare hamaara anubhav nahin pahunch saktaa he. vaastavik sattaaon ke sanghat main vastuon kaa nirmaan hota he. vaastavik satta kaa udhaaharan nahin diya ja saktaa he. ek samvednaa bahut kuch vaastavik satta he. vaastavik sattayen libnij ke chidvinduon jaise hi hai kintu ve gavaakshaheen nahin hai. inkaa jivan kshan bhar kaa hota he. inaki rachana shunya se sambhav nahin he. sansar kii sab vaastavik sattayen milakar ek vaastavik satta kii rachana karatin hai. srijan main naveenta kaa kaaran yah he ki ek vaastavik satta adhik ghanishtataa se sambandhit he or duusari dur or apratyaksh rup se sambandhit he. sansar kii rachana main srijan or vaastavik sataaon ke atirikt sambhavit aakaaron kii bhi aavashyaktaa he. in aakaaron kii dikkaaleey satta nahin hoti. ve shaashvat hote hai.
 hwhiteead kaa darshan prakrutivaadee he kintu kintu purv prakrutwad kii tarah bhautikwaadi nahin. yadhyapi ve bhautikta or aadhyatmikta ke vibhaajan kaa virodh karte hai, tathaapi unka siddhant aadhyatmavaad kii ore adhik jhukta he.
