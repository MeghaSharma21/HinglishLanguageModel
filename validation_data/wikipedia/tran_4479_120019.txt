 sirus palonji mistre ek vyapaaree he jo 28 disambar 2012 ko tata samooh, ek bhartiya vyapaar sanghathan, ke adhyaksh bane hai.vah samooh ke chathe adhyaksh hai or dorabji tata ke baad, tata naam nahin padane tak duusare hai. di iconomist ne bharat or britain donon main "sabase mahatvapurn udyogpati" ke rup main unhen varnit kiya gaya he.
 vah bhartiya nirmaan thilishah palonji mistre ke sabase chote bete hai.
 mistre ne mumbai main cathedral evam and jon conan skool main adhyayan kiya. unhone civil ejineering main bi es ke saath imperial college, landan se snaatak kii oopaadhi or landan bijness skool se prabandhan main vigyaan main ek master degree rakhte he. vah civil ejeeniyar santhaan ke ek sadsy he.
 sirus mistre palonji mistre, ek shaktishaalee udyogpati; or patsi parin dubash ke sabase chote bete he. 
 mistre ne, vakil ikbaal chhagala kii beti or prasiddh vidhivetta em.si. chhagala kii potee, rohika chhagala se shaadi kii. mistre or unki patni ke saath main unke do bete hai.mistre ek irish naagarik, or bharat ke ek sthaayee nivasee he. ek irish akhbaar, The Independent, main ek khabar ke mutabik, mistre khud ko ek vaishvik naagarik ke rup main dekhte he or apane passport ke rang ko mahatvaheen sochate he.
 mistrishapurji palonji and company ke prabandh nideshak kaa kaam kar rahe hai, jo shapurji palonji samooh kaa hissaa he. apane pita ke sevanivrutt hone se 1 saal pehle, vah 1 sitambar 2006 ko tata sans ke board main shaamil hue. 
 2012 main, mistre tata sans ke adhyaksh ke rup main niyukt kiye gayi tha.
