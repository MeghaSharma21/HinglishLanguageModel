 agar ek kalileey padaarth he jise vibhinn prakaar ke laal shaiwalon se praapt kiya jaataa he. isamen sookshmjivon kaa culture karte he. sukshmajivon ke aavashyaktaa anusaar agar main vibhinn padaarth rakhe hote he. agar ke padaarth anusaar agar bhinn hote he. 
 isamen gallectose or sulphate hota he. yah vibhinn prakaar se prayogon main laayaa jaataa he. arechak ke rup main iskaa upyog atyant mahatvapurn he. prayogshaala main iskaa upyog sookshm jeevon ke bhojya padaarthon kaa thos banane ke liye kiya jaataa he. mishthannashaala main tathaa maance samveshthan udyogon main bhi agar kaa upyog hota he. bheshjiy utpaadan main yah pranilambak abhikarta ke rup main prayukt kiya jaataa he.
 agar ke paudhon ko ikattha karke turant sukhaya jaataa he. iske baad kaarkaane main bhej diya jaataa he, jahaan par ye dhoe jate hai. vishesh prayog main laae jane vaale agar kii uplabdhi ke liye ukt paudhon ko viranjit karke pun shuddh kiya jaataa he. tatpashchaat mucillej ko kuch ghanton ke liye ubala jaataa he or anek chhananon se chaante hue vibhinn fremon main jelly ke rup main pravaahit kiya jaataa he. tatpashchaat thandha karke jamaa diya jaataa he. pani ko phenkakar jelly sukhai jaati he or ant main ise churna kaa rup diya jaataa he. iskaa prayog bhinn-bhinn prakaar se kiya jaataa he. isse agarbattiyaan bhi banaai jaati he.
