 himachal pradesh disambar 2007 vidhansabha chunaav disambar 2007 me himachal pradesh kii himachal pradesh vidhaan sabha ke liye hua chunaav tha. rajya ke 65 vidhansabha chunaav kshetron main 19 disambar 2007 ko tathaa kinnaur, lahaul spiti or bharmour janajaatiy kshetron main 14 navambar 2007 ko do charnon main matdaan hua tha.
 rajya main satta virdohi lahar kaa phayda uthaate hue bhaajapa ne 41 seat jitkar congress kaa kadi shikast di. 
 rajya vidhansabha chunaavon ke ghoshit nateezon ke mutabik 68 sadasyeey vidhansabha main congress ko 23 siton se hi santosh karnaa pada, jabaki bahujan samaaj party ko ek or anya ko teen siten mili. lok janashakti party rajya main khaataa bhi nahin khol pai. 
 himachal pradesh main 1990 ke baad bhaajapa ab pehali baar apane boote par sarkaar banaai. varsh 1990 main bhaajapa ne janata party ke saath chunaav purv gathbandhan kar 51 siton par chunaav lada tha or use 46 site mili thi. 
 janata party ko 11 siten mili. isi ke saath bhaajapa- janata party kii gathbandhan sarkaar gathit hui. us dauraan congress ko maatr 8 siten mili thi. chunaavon main teen nirdaleey bhi jite the. 
 baad main varsh 1998 main bhaajapa ne himachal vikaas congress ke saath gathbandhan sarkaar gathit kii. us dauraan bhaajapa ko 31 siten hasil hui thi jabaki himachal vikaas congress ko 5 siton par jeet mili thi.
