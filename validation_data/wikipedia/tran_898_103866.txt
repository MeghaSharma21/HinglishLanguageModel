 hafathali madhya asia main 5veen or 6thi sadi isvi main rahane vaali ek kanaabadosh jati thi. bharat main yah shwet hun or turushk ke naam se bhi jane jate the. chini sutron ke hawaale se yah pehle china kii mahaan diwaar se uttar main rahane vaale ueshi log the. yah kis jati se sambandhit the iskaa sahi-sahi gyaat nahin he lekin bahut se itihaaskaar maanate hai ki yah ek purvi irani bhashaa bolaa karte the. dharm se hafathali hindu or buddha dharm ke anuyaayi the. apane charam par uttar bharat kaa bahut sa ilaaka inke samrajya kaa hissaa tha.
 hafthaaliyon ki jati ke mul strot ke bare main bahut si dharanaayen hai ki yah turki bhashi the yaa hind-irani bhashi. pehle adhik itihaaskaar inhen turki mul kaa samajhate the or vastav main bhi shaayad inamen turki mul ke bahut se log mishrit the. 1959 main kaazuo anoki ne daawa kiya ki inkaa mul sthaan sambhawatah bactria ho or yah ek purvi irani bhashaa bolte hon. hafathali logon ke puraane naamon se bhi lagata he ki ve irani mul ke hai. anya itihaaskaaron ne yah maan to liya he lekin vartmaan dharna he ki yah uttarpurvi iran or uttarpashchimi bharat kii ek mishrit jati thi.
