 shabd geela randi upkarnon kii ek kism he ki ek bhatthi influenza gas se yaa anya gas dhaaraon se pradooshan ko dur varnan karata he. ek geela randi, pradooshit gas dhara scrubbing ke taral ke saath sampark main laayaa jaataa he, yah taral ke saath chhidkaav, yah taral kaa ek pool ke madhyam se majaboor dwaara, yaa kuch anya sampark vidhi dwaara itani ke rup main pradooshan ko dur karne ke liye. geela scrubar yaa kisi bhi vaayu pradooshan niyantran upkaran kii dizin audyogic prakriya kii sthiti or hawa shaamil pradooshan kii prakruti par nirbhar karata he. Inlet gas visheshtaon or dhul gun praathmik mahatv ke hai. Scrubbers baat kan or / yaa gasiy pradooshan ikattha karne ke liye dizine kiya ja saktaa he. geele scrubar unhen taral buundon main capcharing dwaara dhul kanon ko hataa den. geele scrubar bhang karne yaa unhen taral main avashoshit dwaara pradooshak gaison ko hataa den. koi buundon ki randi pravesh gas main kar rahe hai ek or dhundh eliminator yaa entrainment vibhaajak ke rup main sanderbhit divaais ke madhyam se outlet gas dhara se alag hona chahie. iske alaava, parinami scrubbing taral pehle kisi bhi param mukti ke liye ilaaj kiya janaa chahie yaa sanyantr main reused kiya ja rahaa he. scrubar or scrubbing pranaaliyon ke kai vinyas, sabhi taral or pradooshit gas dhara ke bich achche sampark pradaan karne ke liye dizine kar rahe hai. yah unki dhundh eliminators sahit he. chitra 1 VENTURI randi dizin he. VENTURI randi ke liye dhundh eliminator aksar ek alag divaais ek chakravaat vibhaajak bulaayaa he. 
 
 ek geela he randi chote kanon ko ikattha karne kii kshamta aksar sidhe randi main shakti input karne ke liye anupatik he. spray toweron ke rup main kam urja upkarnon ke liye 5 micrometers se bade kanon ko ikattha kiya jaataa he. VENTURI scrubar yaa sankshepan scrubbers ke rup main sanvardhit upkarnon jaise 1 sukshmamapi kanon ke ucch dakshata hataane ko praapt karne ke liye aam taur par ucch urja upkarnon kii aavashyaktaa he. iske atirikt, ek acchhee tarah se dizin or oparation entrainment vibhaajak yaa dhundh eliminator ucch hataane kshamta ko praapt karne ke liye mahatvapurn he. adhik se adhik taral buundon kii sankhya he ki dhundh eliminator ucch sambhavit utsarjan kaa str ke dwaara kabja kar rahe hai nahin. geele scrubar ki gasiy pradooshan ko dur avasorak ke rup main bheja jaataa he. acha gas taral sampark avasorak main ucch hataane kshamta praapt karne ke liye aavashyak he. geela randi dizin ke ek number ke liye pack tower or plate tower sabase aam kiya ja rahaa ke saath gasiy pradooshan hataane ke liye upyog kiya jaataa he. yadi gas dhara donon kan baat or gaison, geela scrubar aam taur par keval ekal vaayu pradooshan niyantran upkaran he, jo donon pradooshan ko dur kar sakate hai kar rahe hai. geele scrubar yaa to kanon yaa gaison ke liye ucch kshamta hataane ko praapt karne or kar sakate hai kuch udaahranon main, donon ek hi system main pradooshan ke liye ek ucch hataane kii kshamta hasil kar sakate hai. haalanki, kai maamalon main, kanon sangrah ke liye sabase acha oparating sharton gas hataane ke liye sabase garib hai. saamaanya main, ucch ek saath gas or kan hataane kshamta praapt karne kii aavashyaktaa he ki unamem se ek aasaani se ekatra ho yaa ek scrubbing jaise abhikarmak ke upyog se chooney yaa sodium hidracasid ke rup main.
