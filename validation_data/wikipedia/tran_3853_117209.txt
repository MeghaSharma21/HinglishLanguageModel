 kordoba kii girizaa-masjid, ਜਾਂ kordoba kii masjid, varjan meri ko samarpit ek girizaa he jo aandalsia, spain main sathit he. yah vicigoth mul ke logon dwaara ek girizaa ke rup main banaai gai thi par baad main madh kaal ke dauraan isko islamic masjid main tabdeel kar diya gaya tha. spain par dubaara adhikaar ke baad isko phir se ek girizaa main tabdeel kiya gaya. is girizaa ko muri nirmaan kala kii sabh se mahaan imaaraton main se ek mana jaataa he. 2000 ke baad se spani musalamaanon roman catholic carch se is sthaan main prarthana karne kii ijaajat kii maang kar rahe hai. musalamaanon ke is maang ko spani girizaa adhikariyon or roman girizaa adhikariyon ne kai baar thukraa diya he.
 yah imaarat vicigoth mul ke logon dwaara girizaa ke rup main saint vincent ko shardhanjali ke taur par banaai gai thi. muslim shaskaon dwaara vicigoth rajya par qabza jamaane ke pashchaat is imaarat ko do bhaagon main baant diya gaya - ek muslim or doosraa isai. vanvaas main gum rahe umayyad shahzaade abd al-rahamaan pratham ne kisi tarah apni jaan bachaate hue ibiria pahunch gaya. iske pashchaat usne al-andalus ke rajyapal yusuf al-fihari ko parajeet karne main kaamyaab rahaa. abd al-rahamaan pratham ne paaya ki kordoba vibhinn vishwaas par aadhaarit dharmik guton main bat chukaa he jinamein gnasti, pricillani, donaati or lusiferi shaamil the. uskaa drud sankalp tha ki ek aisa prathana sthal kaa nirmaan ho jo shanoshaukat ke maamle main baghdad, jerusalem or dimishq kaa muqaabala kar sake or dharmik mahatv ke maamle main makka kii tarah pavitra mana jaae. isai girizaa ghar usee sthal par sthit tha jahaan roman sabhyataa ke anusaar janoos devtaa ko samarpit ek mandir kisi samay par rahaa karti thi. abd al-rahamaan apni masjid kaa nirmaan usee sthaan par chahta tha. usne parajeet isaayiyon se us girizaa ghar or sthal kareedne kii peshkash kii. samjhauta tay karaane kaa kaary sultan ke vishesh sachiv umeya ebn yajid ko saunpa gaya. samjhaute ke antargat isai samudaay ko saint fransis, saint januaaris or saint marcellus ko samarpir girizaa gharon ke punah nirmaan kii anuti de di gai thi jinhen vir gati ko praapt hone ke kaaran isai ati mahatvapurn samajhate the. 
 abd al rahamaan ne isaayiyo unke ko barbaad ho chuke dharmik sthalon ke punarnirmaan karne kii anumati di or is kaary ke badale usne saint vincent ke girizaa ghar ke aadhe hisse ko isai se kharida. caliph kaa khazaana maalamaal tha. yah khazaana haal ke yudhon ke dauraan goth logon se chin lee gai daulat ke alaava vah desh kii dharti kii upaj par ek dashamansh nikaale or usee prakaar kaa kar utpaada par lagaaya. andalusia main musalmaan zakaat ke kar kaa bhugtan karne ke liye baadhya kiya gaya or jazya ke rup main ek kar isaayiyon or yahudiyon par lagaaya ki ve videshi akramanakariyon se andalusia main surakshit rah saken. iske alaava, muslim shaasha bahut se iberia ke moolyawaan khaanon, sangemarmar kii khadaanon, or dhan ke anya sroton ke adhigrahan se samrudh ho chuke the. in rajasav dwaara abd al rahamaan or unke uttaradhikariyon, hisham, abd ararahaman dwitiya, jo is shahi khandaan ke sarvocch or kadi main tisare rahe the, al manazar ne, masjid ke nirmaan main koob paisa karch kiya tha. 
 abd al rahamaan pratham ne hazaron mejaquita main hazaron kaarigaron or mazaduro ko kaam par lagaaya tha. itnaa tej raftaar kaam tha ki isse zile ke sabhi sansadhano kaa koob vikaas hua. bade-bade pathar or ati-sundar kaam vaale sangemarmar ke pathar sierra morena or ke aas-paas se laae gaye the. vibhinn prakaar ke dhaatu zamin se khodakar likaale gaye the or isi kaaran se kordoba main kai nae kaarakaane saamne aae or shahar ke utpaada jagat main ek nae utsaah kaa anubhav kiya gaya tha. syria kaa prasiddh nirmaan-visheshgy ke masjid kii yojana tayyar kii. kordoba ke kinare sthit apane nivas mahal kaa tyaag karke caliph shahar main aakar bakh gaya, taaki sabhi kaaryon kii vah vyaktigat samiksha kar sake or nirmaan kii rup-rekha par apni raay de sake. abd al rahamaan pratham ne kaam karne waalon ke bich apana aana janaa rakhaa or unhen har din kai ghante kaam karne kaa aadesh diya. 
 yah masjid baad main kai parivartanon se ho kar guzari. abd al rahamaan dwitiya ne ek nae minar ke nirmaan kaa aadesh diya jabaki 961 main al hakam dwitiya ne puri imaarat main failaav laae or mehrab ko ek nayaa sundar rup diya. al mansoor ebn abi aamir ne antim sudhaar laayaa. yah ek ubhari hui padchaalak-maarg banaai gai jisase ki calipha ke mahal se is masjid ko jod diya gaya tha jaise ki adhikansh rup se muslim shaskaon kii parampara rahi thi. isai shaskaon ne baad main yahi parampara ko apanaakar girizaa gharon ke nikat apane mahal kaa nirmaan kiya or usee prakaar kii ubhari hui padchaalak-margon kaa nirmaan kiya. 987 main apane vartmaan ayaamon par yah parisar pahunch gaya tha jab bahari stamb or andar ke khule maidaanon kaa nirmaan puraa hua.
 masjid kii yojana banane main vaastukaron ne roman stambhon ke aakaaron ko apni vishesh ruchi ke thaharaavon ke saath kai jagahon par sammilit kiya he. stambhon main se kuch pehle se hi gothic sanrachana ke pratik the; kuch duusare ibiria ke vibhinn kshetron se vahaan ke rajyapalon dwaara bhent ke rup main bheje gaye the. hathidanth, yashab, porfori, sona, chaandi, tambe or pital kii sajaavat ko istemaal kiya gaya he. adbhut mozike or azulejos dizin kiye gaye the. sugandhit lakadiyon kii pattiyon ko shuddh sone ke nakhun ke saath ghaskar or nikharaa gaya tha, or laal sangemarmar stambhon ke liye kaha gaya he ki yah to allaah kaa kaam he. imaarat kaa aadim hissaa abd-er-rahamaan pratham ke disha-nirdesh ke antargat banaaya gaya tha santare ke rang ke maidan ke kone main tha.. baad main, vishaal masjid morismo vaastukala kii sabhi shaileyon ko ek rachana main sammilit karata he.
