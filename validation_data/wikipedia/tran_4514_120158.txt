 naagamani kisi jantu kii haddi yaa pathar ko kehete hai. africa, dakshin america or asia main sarp dansh kii lokachikitsa main iskaa upyog hota he.
 naagamani ko bhagwaan sheshnag dhaaran karte hai. bhartiya puranic or lok kathaon main naagamani ke kisse aam logon ke bich prachlit hai. naagamani sirf naagon ke paas hi hoti he. naag ise apane paas isliye rakhte hai taaki uski roshni ke aasapaas ikatthe ho gaye keede-makodon ko vah khaataa rahe. haalanki iske alaava bhi naagon dwaara mani ko rakhane ke or bhi kaaran hai.
 naagamani kaa rahasy aaj bhi ansulajhaa hua he. aam janata main yah baat prachlit he ki kai logon ne aise naag dekhe hai jiske sir par mani thi. haalanki puraanon main manidhar naag ke kai kisse hai. bhagwaan krishna kaa bhi isi tarah ke ek naag se saamana hua tha.
 chhattisgadhi sahitya or lokkathaon main naag, naagamani or nagkanya kii kathaayen milti hai. manuj naagamani ke madhyam se jal main utarate hai. naagamani kii yah visheshta he ki jal use maarg detaa he. iske baad saahsee manuj mahal main prasthit hokar naag ko parrast kar nagkanya praapt karata he.
 naagamani ke chamatkaar : naagamani ke bare main kaha jaataa he ki yah jiske bhi paas hoti he uski kismat badal jaati he. kehete hai ki naagamani main alokik shaktiyaan hoti hai. uski chamak ke aage hira bhi feekaa pad jaataa he. manyata anusaar naagamani jiske bhi paas hoti he usamen bhi alokik shaktiyaan aa jaati hai or vah aadmi bhi daulatmand ho jaataa he. mani kaa hona usee tarah he jis tarah kii alladin ke chiraag kaa hona. haalanki isamen kitni sachai he, yah koi nahin jaanata.
