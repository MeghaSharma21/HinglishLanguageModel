 lagbhag 4.3 karod
 yoruba pashchimi africa ke nigeria desh ke dakshinpaschim or uttaramadhya bhaag main or padosi benin desh ke dakshini or madhya bhaag main basane vaala ek samudaay he. kul milakar inaki aabaadi san 2016 main 4 karod se adhik anumaanit kari gai thi, jo us samay nigeria kii puri jansankhya kaa 21% tha. yah africa kii sabase badi maanav jatiyon main se ek he. yoruba log yoruba bhashaa bolte hai jo ek surabhedi bhashaa he or sarvaadhik boli jane vaali nizer-congou bhashaa bhi he.
