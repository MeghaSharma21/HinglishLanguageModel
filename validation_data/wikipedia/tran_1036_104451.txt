 is bacteria se grast 37 main se 17 log bharat yaa pakistan se britain laute hai, jinamein kam se 14 logon ne bharat yaa pakistan main cosmetic surgery karaayi thi. ab tak 50 se adhik logon ko aspataal laayaa gaya he, jo is bacteria se sankramit hai.
 lanset ke taaja ank main prakashit ek lekh main bataaya gaya he ki bharat yaa pakistan se cosmetic surgery karakar laute longon ke saath britain pahunchaa yah bacteria sharir main endm-1 naamak anzime banaata he, jis par dawaaiyon kaa asar nahin ho rahaa he. yahaan tak ki carbapenems naamak sabase shaktishaalee anti biotics bhi is par beasar sabit hua he.
 vaigyanikon ne chetavanee di he ki yah bacteria duusare bacteria ke saath mil kar aisa sankraman paidaa kar saktaa he, jisase nipatna mushkil ho saktaa he. vaigyanikon ne ise khatarnaak sthiti bataate hue kaha he ki agar jaldi hi isse sankramit marijon ko pahchaan kar alag karte hue unka ilaaj nahin kiya gaya to puraa desh isaki chapet main aa saktaa he.
