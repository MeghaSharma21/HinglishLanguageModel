 ajola ek tairti hui fern he jo shaiwal se milti-julati he. saamanyat: ajola dhan ke khet yaa uthle pani main ugaai jaati he. yah teji se badhati he.yah jaev urvarak kaa strot he
 ajola pani main panpane vaala chote baarik paudho ke jati kaa hota he jise vaigyanik bhashaa main fern kaha jaataa he. ajola kii pankhudiyo main aenobina naamak nil harith kaai ke jati kaa ek sookshmjeev hota he jo surya ke prakaash main vayumandaleey natrajan kaa yogikeekaran karata he or hare khaad kii tarah fasal ko natrajan kii purti karata he. ajola kii visheshta yah he ki yah anukool vaataavaran main 5 dinon main hi do-guna ho jaataa he. yadi ise puure varsh badhane diya jaye to 300 ton se bhi adhik sendriya padaarth prati hectare paidaa kiya ja saktaa he yani 40 ki0gra0 natrajan prati hectare praapt. ajola main 3.5 pratishat natrajan tathaa kai tarah ke carbonic padaarth hote hai jo bhoomi kii urvara shakti badhaate hai. 
 dhan ke kheton main iskaa upyog sugamawa se kiya ja saktaa he. 2 se 4 inch pani se bhare khet main 10 ton taaja ajola ko ropai ke purv daal diya jaataa he. iske saath hi iske upar 30 se 40 ki0gra0 super phosphate kaa chhidkaav bhi kar diya jaataa he. isaki vruddh ke liye 30 se 35 degree celsius kaa tapakram atyant anukool hota he.
 ajola ke upyog se dhan kii fasal main 5 se 15 pratishat utpaadan vruddh sambhavit rahati he. 
 is fern kaa rang gaharaa laal yaa katthai hota he. dhan ke kheton main yah aksar dikhaai detii he. chote-chote pokhar yaa talabon main jahaan pani ekatrit hota he vahaan pani kii sataha par yah dikhaai detii he. 
 pani ke pokhar yaa lohe ke trey main ajola culture banaaya ja saktaa he. pani kii pokhar yaa lohe ke trey main 5 se 7 se.me. pani bhar deven. usamen 100 se 400 gram culture prativarg meter kii dar se pani main mila deven. sahi sthiti rahane par ajola culture bahut tej gati se badhata he or 2.3 din main hi dugana ho jaataa he. ajola culture daalane ke baad duusare din se hi ek trey yaa pokhar main ajola kii moti tah jamana shuru ho jaati he jo natrajan sthirikaran kaa kaary karti he
