 ravishankar maharaj gujarat rajya ke kheda jile ke samajasevi the. unhone vaatrak nadi ke aasapaas ke gaanvo main logon kaa jivan sudhaarney kaa, daakuon ko punah mukhyadhara main laane kaa, andhshraddha nivaaran aadi kaary kiye the. ravishankar maharaj ne bharat ke swatantrata sangram me bhaag liya tha. ve arya samaaj se prabhavit hue the. wo san 1915 me mahatma gandhi se mili the. 
 ravishankar vyas kaa janm 25 farvari 1884 ko, radhu gaanv main mahashivaratri ke din, pitambar shivaram vyas evam nathi ba ke ek hindu kisaan parivaar main hua tha. unke parivaar kaa mul mahemdawad ke paas sarsavani gaanv tha. balak ravishankar ne apane mata-pita ko krishi kaary main madad karne ke liye chhatthi kaksha ke baad padhaai chhod di thi. unhone surajba se vivaah kiya. 19 varsh kii umr main unke pita kii mrityu ho gai or 22 varsh kii umr main unki maa kii mrityu ho gai thi.
 vah arya samaaj se prabhavit the. vah 1915 main mahatma gandhi se mili or unki aazaadee or saamaajik sudhaar ke kaaryo main sammilit ho gaye. vah 1920 or 1930 ke dashak main gujarat main rashtravaadi vidrohiyon ke mukhya aayojak, darbar gopaldaas desai, narhari pareekh or mohanlal pandya ke saath gandhi or sardaar vallabhbhai patel ke sabase shuruaati or nikatatam sahyogiyon main se ek the. unhone tateey evam kendriya gujarat ke baraiya or patanwadiya jatiyon ke punarvaas ke liye saalon tak kaam kiya tha. unhone 1920 main sunav gaanv main rashtriya shaala kii sthapana kii. unhone patni kii ichha ke viruddh paitruk sampatti par apane adhikaar chode or 1921 main bhartiya swatantraaa andolan main sammilit ho gaye. unhone 1923 main borasad satyagrah main bhaag liya or hadiya teksh kaa virodh kiya. unhone 1926 main bardoli satyagrah main bhaag liya or unhe chah maheene tak british praadhikaran dwaara kaid kiya gaya. unhone 1927 main baadh ke raahat kaaryon main bhaag liya jisamen unhone khaati praapt kii. vah 1930 main namak satyagrah ke liye dandi kuch ke liye gandhi ji se jud gaye or do saal tak kaid main rahen. 1942 main unhone bharat chodo andolan main bhi bhaag liya or ahmedabad main sampradayik hinsa ko shant karne kaa bhi praytn kiya.
 1947 main bharat kii aazaadee ke baad, unhone swayan ko saamaajik kaary ke liye samarpit kiya. vah bhudaan andolan main vinoba bhaave se jud gaye or 1955 or 1958 ke bich unhone 6000 kilometre kii yatra kii. 1960 main, unhone sarvoday andolan kaa aayojan or samarthan kiya. 1 may 1960 ko jab gujarat rajya kii sthaapan hui tab unhone gujarat rajya kaa udghaatan kiya tha. unhone 1975 main congress kii sarkaar dwaara daale gaye apatkaal kaa bhi virodh kiya. unki mrityu tak, yah ek parampara rahi ki gujarat ke har nae niyukt mukhyamantri karyaalay kii shapath lene ke baad unhen aashirwad ke liye jate hai. 1 july 1984 ko gujarat ke borasad main unki mrityu ho gai. unke liye samarpit smarak bochasaan ke adhyaapan mandir, vallabh vidhyalay main sthit he.
 unhone shikshan, graamy pun:nirmaan or kolkata ke bare me likhaa he.
 bharat sarkaar ne 1984 main unke sammaan main dak tikat jari kiya. saamaajik kaary ke liye 1 lakh rupee ke laayak, ravishankar maharaj puraskaar, gujarat sarkaar ke saamaajik nyaay vibhaag dwaara, unke sammaan main sthapit kiya gaya he.
 jhaverchand meghani ne janajatiyon ke bich apane saamaajik kaary ke dauraan ravishankar maharaj ke saath apane anubhavon ke aadhaar par "mansai naa divaa" naamak pustak likhaa he. pannalaal patel ne unke upar ek jeevani upanyaas 'jene jivi janu ' bhi likhaa he jo donon granth gujarati bhashaa main hai..
