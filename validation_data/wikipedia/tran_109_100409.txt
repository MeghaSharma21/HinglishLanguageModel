 vah swarna mandir ke charnon par jarnal singh bhandarawaale ke anuyaayiyon main se ek ke dwaara maare gaye the. tatkaal dinon main thik oparation blue star ke prarambh hone se pehle maar diya tha. 
 harichand singh longowal or gurcharan singh tohara jaise akali netaaon turant record par chale gaye taaki hatya ke kisi bhi anishchit padon kii ninda na karen. kattar sant jatan singh bhindaranwale ne kattarta kaa ekmaatr dhyaan aakarshith kiya. jisane is kaam ko "sikhon ko badnaam karne ke liye sarkaar kaa hastkala" bataaya.
 unhe veerta ke liye raashtrapati ke police padak maranoparant se sammanit kiya gaya. vah apni patni or bete chhod gaye. unki patni amrutha attawal baad main punjab civil seva main shaamil huin. unke putr, harabir attawal, bhi ek inspector ke rup main punjab police main shaamil hue.
