 kairen lujen nyaburg ek america yantrik ejeeniyar or nasa kii antariksh yatri he, jinka janm 7 oktober 1969 ko minesota main hua tha. nyaburg ne 1991 main apana antariksh karier shuru kiya or 2008-2013 tak unhone antariksh main 180 din bitaye . or vah apane pehle mishan 2008 main antariksh main jane vaali 50vi mahila antariksh yatri bani.
 unhen july 2000 main NASA dwaara antariksh yatri ummidavaar ke rup main chunaa gaya tha. prashikshan or moolyankan ke do saal baad unhone mishan visheshgy ke rup main yogyata praapt kii or unhen antariksh yatri karyaalay stesan sanchaalan shakha main takneekee kartavyon kaa kaary saunpa gaya. ieses par unke chah maheene ke mishan ke dauraan vah abhiyaan-6 kru ke liye kru kii samarthan antariksh yatri thi. 2013 main, noiburg ne ieses ekspedishan-36 or ekspedishan-37 par flit engeenior ke rup main kaam kiya, jo soyuj tieme ke subh ke 9 baje launch kiya gaya tha. 16 june 2013 ko vastoka-6 kii 50veen varshganth par velantina tereshkova dwaara pehle spesashot ke baad,nyaburg antariksh main keval do mahilaon main se ek thi jo kii sheno-10 par tiaangong-1 mishan par savaar thi. sitambar main usne ek bhare dianasor kii ek tasviir post kii jo usne antariksh stesan ke scrap se kaksha main banaai thi. unhone yuandi yuva purv chaatravrutti puraskaar sahit kai anya puraskaar bhi jite he jo kii:
