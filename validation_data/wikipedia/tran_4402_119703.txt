 kritrim tatwantaran kaa upyog kar kritrim rup se kisi anya tatv main parivartan karne main kiya jaataa he, yahi kritrim tatwantaran kehalaata he.
 radarford ne san 1919 main nitrogen par alpha kanon kii bochhaar se oxigen praapt kiya. yah kritrim tatwantaran kaa pehla prayog tha. iske pashchaat hi anya vegyaniko ne tatwo kaa tatwantaran kiya.
 radarford va chadvik ne san 1920-1924 main boron se potacium tak sabhi 15 tatwo par alpha kan ke dwaara vighatan kiya.
 chadvik ne berilium par alpha kan ke dwaara neutron praapt kiya. yah ek anaveshit kan he, jiske prayog ke pashchaat yah pataa chalaa ki parmaanu main neutron paaya jaataa he. is khoj ke pashchaat kritrim tatwantaran saral ho gaya.
 isame sabhi tatv asthai hote hai va radio sakriyata pradarshit karte hai. yah kritrim tatwantaran ki ek visheshta he. parantu yah prakritik kritrim radiodharmita se bhinn he.
