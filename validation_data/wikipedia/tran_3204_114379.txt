 nyaayalayik bhautik vigyaan nyaayalayik vigyaan kaa bahut hi mushkil shetra he. is shetra main bhautik gunon jaise ghanatv, apvartanaank, shyanta va prakaashiya gunon kaa parikshan hota he. ghatnasthal par paae gaye kaanch ke tukade, paint, chips, kagaz, kapadey, lakdi,plastic ke tukade, rassi, dhaatu taar aadi kaa pramanik sabooton kaa bhautik chijo se samellan karnaa bhi nyaayalayik bhautikee kaa shetra he. nyaayalayik bhautik vigyaan kaanch ke vishleshan main bhi madadgaar hota he. toote hue kaanch kaa bhi vishleshan karnaa bhut hi mahatvapurn hota he kisi bhi apradhi sthal par| is vishleshan ke dwaara ham us sthal par hui baaki gatividhiyon kaa pataa laga sakate hai. jaise kaanch ke tadakane kii madad se ham us ghatana ke hone ke aadesh kaa pataa laga sakate hai, tadakane kii disha se yah bhi pataa laga sakate hai ki vah kaanch andar se tuta he yaa bahaar se|iske alaava bhi nyaayalayik vigyaan ke is shetra main ham kisi bhi aujaar ke chinh kaa, phislan ko chinh kaa, tair chinh kaa, pad chinh aadi kaa parikshan kr sakate hai.
