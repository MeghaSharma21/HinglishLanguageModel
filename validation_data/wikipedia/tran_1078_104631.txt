 garbhashay or aas-paas ke utakon jise zaccha puti yaa prasavottar garbhashayshoth ke rup main janaa jaataa he, sabase aam sankraman he. jokhim karkon main anya ke saath shalyajnan, kuch bacteria jaise ki yoni main samooh bi stryptococus kii jaise maujuudagi, jhilli ke samay se pehle fatna, or lambey samay kaa prasav shaamil he. adhikansh sankraman main kai prakaar ke bacteria shaamil hote hai. sanvardhan yoni yaa rakt dwaara nidaan main shaayad hi kabhi madad milti he. un main jin main sudhaar nahin hota he, medical imaging kii aavashyaktaa ho sakati he. prasav ke baad fever ke anya kaarnon main shaamil he:stan adhiraktata, mutr nalee kaa sankraman, pet chira ke sankraman yaa bhagachhedan, or shwasarodh .
 shalyajnan ke baad ke jokhim ke kaaran yah anushansa kii jaati he ki sabhi mahilaon ko shalya ke samay antibiotic jaise ampiselin kii khuraaq praapt karen. sthapit sankraman kaa upachaar antibiotic dawaaon ke se hota he jisamen zyaadaatar mahilaayein do se teen dinon main sudhaar praapt karti hai. un halke rogon waalon main maukhik antibiotic dawaaon kaa istemaal kiya ja saktaa he anyathaa antahshira antibiotic dawaaon kii anushansa kii jaati he. saamaanya antibiotic dawaaon main yoni prasav ke baad ampiselin or jentamycin yaa jin kaa shalyajnan hua ho un ke liye clindamycin or jentamycin kaa sangyojan shaamil he. jin mahilaon main uchit ilaaj se sudhaar nahin ho rahaa he, anya jatiltaaon jaise pas par dhyaan diya janaa chahie.
 viksit duniya main ek se do pratishat ke bich yoni prasav ke baad garbhashay main sankraman hota he. yah un ke bich jinke adhik mushkil prasav hote hai, paanch se teraa pratishat or nirodhak antibiotic ke upyog se pehle shalyajnan main pachaas tak bad jaataa he. in sankramanon ke kaaran 2013 main 24,000 mrityu hui thi jo 1990 ke 34,000 kii mauton se kam he. is sthiti kaa pehla gyaat vivaran kam se kam 5 veen shatabdi isa purv hippocrates ke lekhan main miltaa he. kam se kam 18 veen shatabdi main shuru or 1930 tak jab antibiotic pesh kiye gaye the, bacchon ke janm ke samay yah sankraman maut kaa ek bahut hi saamaanya kaaran the. 1847 main, austria main, ignaj semimalves ne chlorine se haath dhone ke madhyam se beemaari se mrityu main bees pratishat se do pratishat tak kami kii.
