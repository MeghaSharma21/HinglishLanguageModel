 seva mandir udaipur main 1969 main dow mohan singh mehta dwaara sthapit ek svaichhik sanstha he, jo gramin vikaas, mahila shashaktikaran, swasthya, bal-vikaas, praudh-sakshartaa, peyajal, rojgaar-vikaas, paryaavaran-sangrakshan, graamy-swatchta aadi kai kshetron main udaipur or rajsamand jilon main swayamsevakon ke madhyam se samaaj-kaary gatividhiyon kaa sanchaalan kar rahaa he|
  kaa uddhran: 
 "......lagbhag 18 varsh udaipur se baahar rahane ke pashchaat dow mohansingh mehta san 1967 i. main udaipur aa gayi. isi varsh aapane seva mandir kii sthapana kii. "
 "seva mandir kii kalpana or vidya bhawan kaa vichaar ek hi vakt ke uthaaye gayi do kadam the. san ] main hi seva mandir ke liye jameen kharid lee gai thi jisaki kul laagat 600/- rou. thi. yahi vah jameen he, jahaan aaj seva mandir he. seva mandir ke kaary main kisi prakaar kii baadha na aaye isaliye unhone ek trust seva mandir trust banaaya. us samay unke paas itnaa dhan nahin tha ki bhawan nirmaan karaayen, at: apni marcedes gaadi, jo vah holend se laaye the, 37000/- rou. main bech kar vahaan seva mandir kaa bhawan banaaya or swayan kaa awaas bhi vahiin rakhaa. sabase pehle nirakshartaa unmoolan kaa kaary prarambh kiya gaya or paas hi ke gaanv badgaanv main 22 farvari 1969 ko literysi house, lakhanu kii sahaayta se is kaary kaa shriganesh kiya. us samay canada kii veldi fisher isaki adhyaksh thim | dayaal chand soni iske pehle upanideshak niyukt kiye gayi. paas hi ke lakhawali gaanv main is pariyojna kaa udghaatan hua. isi varsh bharat sarkaar ne dow mehta ko `padam vibhushan’ se bhi sammanit kiya."
 "seva mandir kaa kaam prauji shiksha ke madhyam se shuru hua. dow mehta kaa mul soch yahi tha ki logon kii agyanta hi garibi kaa mul kaaran he. is aadhaar par jab dow mehta ne sakshartaa, natya-dal ke madhyam se prachaar, shaikshik vaartaayen aadi ke madhyam se apana kaam shuru kiya, to aage kaa rasta apane aap dikhataa chalaa gaya. isse unhen nai disha mili ki sakshartaa ke saath-saath logon kii roti-roji ke liye unki dakshata kaa bhi vikaas kiya jaay. gaanvon kii janata kisaan he, at: pathyapustak `kisaan praveshika’ ke madhyam se yah kaary karne kaa nirnay liya gaya, jiske parinaam sukhad rahe. 'kisaan praveshika' jo pehle hindi main thi, use mewari evam vagadi kii sthaneey boli main bhi nae sire se bananaa padaa, jise rajasthan kii anya sansthaaon ne bhi apane kaaryakramon ke liye chunaa." 
 "sevamandir ke kaary kii pratishtha ke kaaran sarkaar ne vikaas ke kai kaary bhi seva mandir ko diye. isamen `jal vikaas’ kaa kaary apane haath main liya. sarkaar ke sahyog se 'hamajoli' vikaas kii yojana unhone kriyanvit kii, jisamen krishi, abhiyantriki evam sahakaarita par vishesh jor diya gaya. seva mandir ke itihaas kaa pehla anicat nirmaan isi kaal main hua. 
 isi prakaar mahila shashaktikaran kaa kaary bhi rajasthan main sarvapratham isi sanstha ne prarambh kiya. isi prakaar seva mandir main hi gramin vikaas kaa prakaashan evam shiksha ke kaary ko prabhavi dhang se karne kii disha main alag-alag prakoshthon kaa nirmaan kiya gaya. chittod or jaisamand ke vaarshik camp main yah nishchay kiya gaya ki seva mandir kaa apana prashikshan kendra bhi hona chaiye, jahaan gramvaasiyon ko unki aavashyaktaanusaar prashikshit yaa daksh kiya jaay." 
 "seva mandir ne 80 ke dashak ke prarambh main `saamudayik vikaas ke liye vikaas yojana’ par charchaa kar yah nirnay liya ki seva mandir, lok-maang or lok-aavashyaktaa ke aadhaar par jahaan log vikaas kii jimmedaari bhavishya main lene ko taiyaar hon, vahiin sanstha kaary kii jarurat ke mutabik kaary kare, logon main kshamta kaa vikaas kare tathaa vikaas ke naye vikalp logon ke samaksh rakhe."
