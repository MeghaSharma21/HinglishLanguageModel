 dragon world ek 1994 america/british/scotland/romanian kaalpanik vigyaan galp saahasik film. yah sidhe video karne ke liye jari kiya gaya tha. dwaara jari yah teesri film he: moonbim antertenment, jo bacchon ke video ke vibhaajan he: poornima visheshtaayen.
 yuva jon macgovana, aadhunik samay main scotland ke liye yatra karne ke liye apane dada ke mahal main rahane ke baad vah ek yatayaat durghatana main apane donon mata pita ko kho detaa he. apane dada kii jaaydaad par jaadui icchhuk vyaktiyon ke ped par, vah ek bachche ko ajgar, jise vah upnaam "yora" jaadu karnaa. ve ek saath ho janaa or ek din dastaveji film nirmaataa bob armastrang, unki beti beth or uske payalat browni mikaji yora par thokar. prasiddhi or paise ke liye utsuk, bob manaa jon "kiraye par" sthaneey bhrasht vyapaaree, lester mcinatir yora. jon, jo bhaag main mahal par badhate karon main band kaa bhugtan kiya he kii peshkash ke dwaara majaboor he ke lester yora andar vah karata he to bhi apane beth main badhati ruchi kii vajah se aanshik rup se lene ke liye anumati detaa he. yora dukhi he or uske liye nayaa vishay banaaya park main pareshaan he or jab yah spasht ho jaataa he ki mcinatir unhen thaga gaya he kram main ajgar, jon or apane nae doston karrwai shoshan.
