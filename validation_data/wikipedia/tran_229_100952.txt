 kasheedakaari kala kapadon par kapade sui or dhaagaa yaa soot ke saath anya saamagree kii hastkala he. kadhai main dhaatu strips, moti, moti, pankh, sepiyaan, sitare, evam vibhinn anya saamagree ko shaamil kar sakate hai.
 kadhai kii ek visheshta yah he ki buniyadi takneekon se hi aaj bhi kaam kar sakate hai. isamen taanke-channstich, fanda yaa kambal taanka, satin taanka, ulte haath silaai aadi bahut se tareeke hote hai.
