 shinjo abe, janm 21 sitambar 1954) japan ke 57 wein pradhanmantri or 2012 se libral democratic party ke pramukh netaa ke rup main seva karne vaala ek japani raajnneetijna he, or purv main 2006 se 2007 tak. japan ke baad ke yuddh main ve tisare sabase lambey samay tak pradhan mantri rahe hai. abe ek raajnneetik rup se pramukh parivaar se aate he or sitambar 2006 main rashtriya sansad ke ek vishesh satr ke dwaara chune gaye, 52 varsh kii aayu main japan ke sabase kam umr ke yuddh ke baad ke pradhan mantri ban gaye or dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke baad pehali baar paidaa hue the. swasthya kaarnon se 12 sitambar 2007 ko abe ne istipha de diya. unka sthaan yasuon phukudda, dwaara sthanantarit kiya gaya tha, jo solah maheene se adhik samay tak karyaalay banaae rakhane main vifal rahe paanch pradhanmantriyon kii ek shrinkhalaa main pehle tha.abe ne ek raajnneetik vaapasi kaa manchan kiya, or 26 sitambar 2012 ko aldypi ke raashtrapati pad ke liye purv raksha mantri shiju ishba ko haraaya. 2012 ke aam chunaav main aldypi kii baari jeet ke baad, vah 1948 main shegeru yoshida ke baad se karyaalay main waapas jane ke liye pehle purv pradhan mantri bane. vah 2014 ke aam chunaav main phir se nirvaachit hue, gathbandhan saathi comita ke saath apane do-tihaai bahumat ko barkaraar rakhte hue, or phir 2017 ke aam chunaav main.
 shinjo abe kaa janm tokyo main ek raajnneetik rup se pramukh parivaar main hua tha. unka parivaar mul rup se yamaguchi praant he, or abe ke panjikrut nivas nagata, yamaguchi, jahaan unke dada kaa janm hua tha. unke dada, kaan abe, or pita, shintaro abe donon rajanetaaon the. abe kii maa, yoko kishi, 1957 se 1960 tak japan ke pradhan mantri nobuseku kishi kii beti he. dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke dauraan kishi tujo cabinet kaa sadsy rahe the chunki jiechakue kii niti badal gai or communist virdohi ban gaye, kishi ko sugamo jail se reeha kiya gaya or baad main japan democratic party kii sthapana kii. apni pustak utsushiiithii main, abe ne likhaa, "kuch log mere dada ko 'kaksha-ek yuddh ke rup main ek apradhi' sandigdh ke rup main istemaal karte the, or mujhe is tarah ke anubhavon kii vajah se mahasoos ho rahaa tha. iske vipareet, 'roodhiwaad' se bhaavnaatmak rup se judaa ho gaya. "
 1955 main, shigeru yoshada kii libral party or kishi kii democratic party ko vietnam virdohi vilay ke rup main vilay kar diya gaya or aldypi ke rup main punarsthapit kiya gaya. abe ne sexki elementary skool, sexki junior hai skool or sekeli senior hai skool main bhaag liya. unhone saarvajanik prashasan kaa adhyayan kiya or 1977 main seiki vishvavidhyalay se raajnneeti vigyaan main snaatak kii degree ke saath snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii. unhone baad main sanyukt rajya main sthanantarit kiya or teen semester ke liye dakshini california vishvavidhyalay ke skool of public policy main saarvajanik niti kaa adhyayan kiya. april 1979 main, abe ne cobe steel ke liye kaam karnaa shuru kiya. unhone 1982 main company ko chhod diya or videsh mantri ke kaaryakari sahaayak, aldypi general council ke adhyaksh ke nijee sachiv or aldypi ke mahasachiv ke nijee sachiv sahit kai sarkari padon par rahe.
 1993 main apane pita kii mrityu ke baad 1993 main shinjo abe yamaguchi praant ke pehle jile ke liye chune gaye the, esantivi bahu-sadasyeey jile main nirvaachit chaar pratinidhiyon ke sabase adhik vot jeetne ke liye. 1 999 main, vah saamaajik maamalon ke vibhaag ke nideshak, yoshiro mori main up mukhya cabinet sachiv or 2000-2003 se janichiro koizumi cabinet ke nideshak bane, jiske baad unhen libral democratic party ke mahasachiv niyukt kiya gaya.
 abe libral democratic party ke mori gut kaa sadsy he. is gut kaa netritv purv pradhan mantri yoshiro mori karte hai junihiroo koizumi ise chhodane se pehle mori gut ke sadsy the, jaisaa ki ek ucch party post sweekaar karte samay custom hota he 1986 se 1991 tak, abe ke pita, shinto, ek hi gut kii adhyakshata karte the.  2000 main, abe ke ghar or yamaguchi praant main shimonojki main unke samarthakon kaa karyaalay, kai avasaron par moltove cocktail ke saath hamalaa kiya gaya tha aparaadhiyon ko kai yakuja sadsy kudo-kaai, ek kitkkishu-aadhaarit namit borokudain sindicket ke the. mana jaataa he ki hamalon kaa kaaran mana jaataa he ki 1999 main shimonoji meyar kaa ummidavaar kaa samarthan karne ke badale badale main abe ke sthaneey sahyogi ne shimonoji achal sampatti dalal ko nakad dene se manaa kar diya tha.
 abe japani sarkaar ke liye uttar korea main le gaye japani apahani pariwaron kii ore se japani sarkaar ke liye mukhya vaartakar the. prayaas ke ek bhaag ke rup main, vah 2002 main kim jong-iel se milane ke liye koizumi ke saath. unhone rashtriya lokapriyata hasil kii jab unhone maang kii ki japan main aane vaale japani apahrit log uttar korea kii avhelnaa main rahete hai.
 vah aldypi ke bheetar ek project team ke netaa the jinhone "atyadhik yon shiksha or ling mukt shiksha" par ek surveykshan kiya tha. jin madon par is team ne aapattiyaan uthaai thim, ve shaareerik gudiya or anya pathyakram saamagree "bacchon kii umr ko dhyaan main nahin le rahe", paaramparik ladakon or ladakiyon ke tyoharon or mishrit-ling sambandhi shiksha par pratibandh lagane vaali skool nitiyon main shaamil the. team ne democratic party of japan se iske vipareet pradaan karne kii maang kii, jisane is tarah kii nitiyon kaa samarthan kiya.
 23 april 2006 ko, abe ko sattadhari libral democratic party ke adhyaksh ke rup main chunaa gaya. is pad ke liye unke pramukh pratispardhiyon main sadakaju tangiki or taaro aaso the yasuuo phucuda ek agrani praarambhik daavedaar the, lekin antat chalaane ke liye nahin chunaa. purv pradhan mantri yoshiro mori, jinke gut ke donon abe or phucuda the, ne kaha ki yah gathbandhan dridhtaa se abe kii ore jhukta he.
 14 july 2006 ko, abe kaa udghaatan japan ke pradhanmantri abe ke rup main hua, 52 varsh kii aayu main chune gaye, 1941 main fummero konoi ke baad se sabase kam umr ke pradhanmantri the.
 abe ne apane purvavarti juniiroo koizumi dwaara sthapit rajkoshiya sudhaaron ke liye ek saamaanya patibaddhta vyakt kii. unhone japan ke bajet ko santulit karne ke liye kuch kadam uthaae hai, jaise ki kar niti visheshgy koji omi ko vitt mantri ke rup main niyukt karnaa. omi ne pehle rashtriya upbhog kar main badhotaree kaa samarthan kiya he, haalanki abe ne khud ko is niti se dur kar diya he or kharch main katautee ke jariye apane bajet ke adhikansh santulan hasil karne kaa prayaas kiya he.
 1997 se "japan or itihaas shiksha ke aadan pradaan ke bare main sochane vaale junior vidhansabha sadasyon ke santhaan" ke beuro pramukh ke rup main, abe ne vivaadaaspad japani socity for history text book riform and da new history textbook kaa samarthan kiya.
 march 2007 main, daav-panth ke netaaon ke saath abe ne rashtrawaad ko protsaahit karne or japani yuvaaon ke bich "desh ke desh or grihnagar ke liye prem" kaa prastaav pesh kiya he bahut aalochana ke baavjood "desh kaa pyaar" shaamil karnaa). 
 abe ne japani uttaradhikaar vivaad main roodhiwaadee vichaar rakhe, or akishino ke rajkumaar hishahito ke janm ke turant baad unhone mahilaon ko christmasam singhaasana vaaris karne ke liye anumati dene ke liye ek prastaavit vidhaayi sanshodhan ko tyaag diya.
 shinjo abe ne aam taur par uttar korea ke sambandh main ek hard-line kaa rukh kiya he, khaskar japani nagarikon ke uttari korian apharan ke bare main.
 japan or uttar korea ke bich 2002 kii baatcheet main, pradhan mantri koizumi or mahasachiv kim jong-iel ne japan jane ke liye abductees kii anumati dene ke liye sahmati vyakt kii. kuch hafton kii yatra main, japani sarkaar ne faisla kiya ki apahritiyon ko uttar korea lautne se pratibandhit kiya jayega, jahaan unke pariwaron ko jivit rahegaa. abe ne apane policy faisle kaa shrey apni best-sailing book, toverd a butir nesan main liya. kootnithik vaade ke ulanghan ke rup main uttar korea ne is japani nirnay kii aalochana kii, or vaarta nirast kar di gai.
 abe ne saarvajanik rup se pipuls republic of china ke saath behatar sambandhon kii aavashyaktaa ko sweekaar kiya he or videsh mantri taaro aaso ke saath, chini ke agrani netaa hu jintao ke saath ek antim shikhar baithak kii maang kii. abe ne yah bhi kaha he ki china-japan sambandhon ko bhaawanaaon par aadhaarit nahin rahanaa chahie.
 kabhi-kabhi, taiwan ke rajanetaaon main abe kaa sammaan hota he jo taiwan kii swatantraaa kii maang karne vaale pan-green gathbandhan kaa hissaa hai. chen shui-bian ne abe ke mantri kaa swaagat kiya. taiwan main abe kii apil kaa hissaa aitihaasik he: unke dada nobusek kishi taiwan ke samarthak the, or unke bade-chachaa eesaku sato ne pichley pradhan mantri the, jabaki karyaalay main taiwan kii yatra kii thi
 abe ne dakshinpurv asian kshetra ke bheetar raajnneetik, suraksha or aarthik sambandhon ko majboot karne kii aavashyaktaa vyakt kii he. abe ne uttar korea ke parmaanu cardon kaa muqabla karne ke liye apane sahyogiyon ko apane antarrashtriya abhiyaan main vruddhi kar di he. abhi tak, abe ne safaltaapurvak philippines or indonesia kaa daura kiya he, or haalanki china dakshin purv asian kshetra ke bheetar nahin he, japan ne bhi apane samarthan kii maang kii he. haalanki, china ke saath sambandh senkaku dweep vivaad or abe ke yasukuni mandir ke daure ke kaaran kalankit hote hai.
 abe, japan ke pradhan mantri ke rup main apane do shabdon main, samrik japan-bharat sambandhon ko apgrade karne kii maang kii. abe ne 2007 main japan, america, australia or bharat ke bich chaturbhuj suraksha vaarta shuru kii. agast 2007 main bharat kii teen divasiy yatra ne bharat or japan ke bich maitreepurn dwipaksheeya sambandhon ke lambey itihaas par ek nae dwipaksheeya asian gathbandhan kaa udghaatan kiya. abe kii pahal ek ubhartee hui paridrishy main "paanchaven" dwipaksheeya sambandh sthapit karnaa he, jisase america-australia, america-japan, japan-australia or you.es.-bharat link sahaayak ranneetik sanrekhan hai. bharat-australia kaa ek chhathi link taarkik parinaam hoga, samrik dhaanche ke ek nae chaturbhuj ke rup main aupchaarik rup hoga. is vyavastha main vietnam, dakshin korea, philippines or indonesia ko shaamil karne ke liye antim vistaar un rajyon ke media main anumaan lagaaya gaya he. chini ranneetik visheshgyon ne viksit bhu-ranneetik pratimaan, "asian nato" kaa naam diya he. abe kii vyaavhaarik bharat kii videsh niti, asia main mahatvapurn bhagidaar banane ke dauraan japan ke punarutthan vaale aarthik sanketakon ko badhaava dena he. bharat, sabase pramukh dur-purvi or asian rajyon ke vipareet, japan ke saath gambhir sainya vivaad kaa itihaas nahin he.
 abe ne japani samvidhan ke anuchhed 9 kii vyakhya ko sanshodhit karne yaa vistrut karne kii bhi maang kii taaki japan ko sainya sainya balon ko banaae rakhane kii anumati mil sake. unhone kaha tha ki "ham japan kii suraksha or hamaare samvidhan kii vyakhya ke bich antar ko kam karne main seemaa tak pahunch rahe hai." pradhan mantri ke rup main apni pehali avadhi ke dauraan unhone japan kii raksha agency ko purna mantralay kii sthiti main unnat banaaya. apane purvavartiyon kii tarah unhone sanyukt rajya america ke saath japani gathbandhan kaa samarthan kiya.
 
 abe ke faisledaar libral democratic party ko upree sadan ke chunaav main baari nuksaan hua, yah pehali baar 52 saal main niyantran kho gaya tha. ek anya krishi mantri, norihiko aakaagi, jo raajnneetik vittaposhan ghotaale main shaamil the, ne chunaav ke baad istipha de diya.
 apane prashasan ko punerjeevit karne kii koshish main, abe ne 27 agast 2007 ko ek nai cabinet kii ghoshnaa kii. haalanki, vitt mantri lehico ando, jo vitt ke ghotaale main shaamil the, ne keval 7 din baad hi istipha de diya.
 12 sitambar 2007 ko, ek nae sansadeey satr ke shuru hone ke teen din baad, abe ne ek anirdhaarit press sammelan main pradhan mantri ke rup main apni sthiti kaa istipha dene kii ghoshnaa kii. abe ne kaha ki unki alokapriyata aatankwaad virdohi kanoon ke paarit hone main baadha rahi thi, jisamen afghanistan main japan kii nirantar sainya upasthiti ke alaava anya chizon ke bich shaamil tha. party ke adhikariyon ne yah bhi kaha ki bhangur pradhan mantri garib swasthya se peedit the. 26 sitambar 2007 ko abe ne aupchaarik rup se apana kaarykaal khatm kar diya kyonki japan ke nae pradhan mantri yasuuo phucuda ban gaye.
 abe ne baad main bataaya ki pradhanmantri ke rup main apni pehali avadhi samaapt karne vaali beemaari main alcerative brihadantrashoth tha, lekin baad main vah ek dawa asakol tak pahunchne ke kaaran baraamad hua, jo ki pehle japan main uplabdh nahin tha. jab vah pad par laut aae to unhone apane hi maamle kaa istemaal karke sambhavit naveen dawaaon ko sweekruti dene ke liye samay kam karne kaa tark diya. pradhanmantri ke rup main istipha dene ke baad, abe sansad main bane rahe or 2009 ke chunaav main apane yamaguchi chauthe jile main phir se nirvaachit hue jab aldypi japan kii democratic party se haar gai.
 26 sitambar 2012 ko, abe ko vipakshi libral democratic party ke raashtrapati ke rup main phir se nirvaachit kiya gaya, jisamen purv raksha mantri shigeru ishiba ko 108 maton main se 89 voton se haraaya. raajnneetik ashaanti ke samay abe aldypi ke netritv main laut aae, parmaanu nitiyon par party kaa vibhaajan or cabinet kii chaal 5 se 10 pratishat tak badhane ke liye shasit dipije ne nichle sadan main apni bahumat kho diya tha. pradhanmantri yoshihiko noda ko upbhog kar vidheyak paarit karne ke liye aldypi par bharosa karne ke liye majaboor kiya gaya tha or badale main abe or vipakshi dalon dwaara ek tasviir saamaanya chunaav karaane ke liye dabaav daalaa gaya tha. noda sharton par yah karne ke liye sahamat he ki aldypi ek band-vittaposhan bill paarit kar diya, or saamaajik suraksha pranali main sudhaar or agle sansad satr main chunavi malapportionment pataa karne ke liye ek ayog kaa samarthan karenge.
