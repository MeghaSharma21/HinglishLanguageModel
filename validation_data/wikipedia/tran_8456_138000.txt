 bask pradesh spain ke purvottaree bhaag main sthit ek swytt samudaay he. swytt samudaay spain kaa sabase ucch-stareey prashasnic vibhaag hota he or lagbhag bharat ke rajyon ke baraabar hota he. yah spain va france ke bask samudaay kii parmparik mathrubhumi kaa bhaag he. isamen teen praant aate hai: alwa, biske or gipuzakoa. bask pradesh kii rajdhani vitoria-gasteis he - 'vitoria' is shahar kaa spani naam he jabaki 'gasteis' iskaa bask bhashaa main naam he.
 spani bhashaa main 'ba' or 'va' donon ko ek mishrit-dhwani se uccharit kiya jaataa he - dhyaan den ki na yah 'ba' he or na hi yah 'va' he balki in donon ke bich kaa ek uccharan he. isliye kai naam do roopon main milte hai. masalan is swytt pradesh ke 'bask' or 'vask' donon hi naam thik hai.
