 nirdeshaank: 23°30′N 91°15′E / 23.5°N 91.25°E / 23.5; 91.25agartala bharat ke tripura praant kii rajdhani he. agartala kii sthapana 1850 main maharaj radha krishna kishor maaniky bahadur dwaara kii gai thi.bangladesh se keval do kimi dur sthit yah shahar saanskrithik rup se kaaphi samrudh he. agartala tripura ke pashchimi bhaag main sthit he or haroa nadi shahar se hokar guzarati he. paryaton kii drushti se yah ek aisa shahar he, jahaan manoranjan ke tamaam saadhan hai, andventure ke dheron vikalp hai or saanskrithik rup se bhi behad samrudh he. iske alaava yahaan paae jane vaale alag-alag prakaar ke jeev-jantu or ped-paudhe agartala paryaton ko or bhi rochak bana dete hai.
 agartala us samay prakaash main aayaa jab maaniky vansh ne ise apni rajdhani banaaya. 19veen shatabdi main kuki ke lagaataar hamalon se pareshaan hokar maharaj krishna maaniky ne uttari tripura ke udaipur sthit rangamati se apni rajdhani ko agartala sthaanantarit kar diya. rajdhani badalane kaa ek or kaaran yah bhi tha ki maharaj apane samrajya or pados main sthit british bangladesh ke saath sampark banane chaahate the. aaj agartala jis rup main dikhaai detaa he, darasal isaki pariklpana 1940 main maharaj vir bikram kishor maaniky bahadur ne kii thi. unhone us samay sadak, market building or nagarnigam kii yojana banaai. unke is yogadan ko dekhte hue hi agartala ko ‘bir bikram singh maaniky bahadur kaa shahar’ bhi kaha jaataa he. shahi rajdhani or bangladesh se najdiki hone ke kaaran ateet main kai badi namchin hastiyon ne agartala kaa bhraman kiya. rabindranath tagore kai baar agartala aae the. unke bare main kaha jaataa he ki tripura ke raajaaon se unke kaaphi gahare sambandh the.
 1901 main nirmit ujjayant pales, agartala kaa mukhya smarak he jo mughal-europea mishrit style main nirmit he. 800 acre main failaa yah vishaal parisar ab rajya kii vidhaan sabha ke rup main prayukt hota he. isamen bageeche or maanav-nirmit jhilein he. aamtaur par ise janata ke liye nahin kholaa jaataa parantu yadi aap saayam 3 se 4 baje ke biit mukhya dwaar par jaaen to aap yahaan pravesh ke liye pravesh-paas praapt kar sakate hai.
 pales ke maidan main naarangi rang ke do mandir arthath ummenshwar mandir or jagannatha mandir sthit he, jinamein koi bhi vyakti darshanarth ja saktaa he.
 airport road par lagbhag 1 kimi uttar main venuban vihaar naamak ek buddha mandir he. gedu miyaan masjid, jo anokhe takike se cracari ke toote hue tukdon se bani he, bhi darshneey he.
 echjibi road par sthit state museum main ethnographical or orcyology sambandhi vasteen pradarshit kii gai hai. yah somvaar se shanivaar tak praat: 10 se saayam 5 baje tak khulata he, isamen pravesh nishulk he. paryaton karyaalay ke peeche sthit tribal museum tripura ke 19 adivasee samoohon kii smriti ke rup main banaaya gaya he.
 purana agartala purv main 5 kimi dur he. yahaan chaudaa murtiyon vaala mandir he jahaan july maah main shraddhalu kadchhi-puja ke liye ekatra hote hai. yahaan autoricshaw, bas or jeep dwaara pahunchaa ja saktaa he.
 is mahal ko maharaja radha kishor maaniky ne banwaaya tha. agartala jane par is mahal ko jarur ghoomana chahie. iskaa nirmaan kaary 1901 main puraa hua tha or philhal iskaa istemaal rajya vidhansabha ke rup main kiya ja rahaa he.
 mukhya shahar se 53 kimi dur sthit is khoobsutar mahal ko maharaja bir bikram kishor maaniky ne banwaaya tha. rudrasagar jhil ke bich main sthit is mahal main maharaja garmiyon ke samay thaharte the. mahal nirmaan main islamic or hindu vaastushilp kaa mila-jula rup dekhne ko miltaa he, jisase ise kaaphi khyaati bhi mili he.
 agartala ke sarvaadhik poojaneey mandiron main se ek jannath mandir apni anuthi vaastushilpeey style ke liye janaa jaataa he. yah ek ashtabhujeey sanrachana he or mandir ke pavitra sthal ke chaaron ore aakarshak pradhakshan path he.
 jaisaa ki naam se hi jaahir he, is college ko maharaja bir bikram singh ne banwaaya tha. 1947 main is college ko banavaane peeche maharaja ki manshaa sthaneey yuvaaon ko vyavsaayik or gunvatttayukta shiksha uplabdh karana tha.
 is mandir main hindu dharm ko maanane vaale niyamit rup se jate hai. saath hi yah agartala kaa ek lokapriy paryaton sthal bhi he. is mandir ko krishnanand sevaayat ne banwaaya tha.
 raaj bhawan ke bagal main sthit rabindra kaanan ek bada sa haraa-bharaa garden he. yahaan har umr ke log aate hai. kuch to yahaan mouj masti ke makasad se aate hai, vahiin kuch iskaa istemaal play ground ke taur par bhi karte hai.
 pichley kuch saalon main chaaval, tilhan, chaay or jute ke niyamit vyapaar se agartala purvottar bharat ke ek vyavsaayik garh ke rup main bhi ubhara he. shahar main kuch falte-phoolate bazaar hai, jinhen ghoomana bekaar nahin jayega. in bazaaron main bade paimaane par hastshilp or oon se bane vastra aapako mil jaaenge.
