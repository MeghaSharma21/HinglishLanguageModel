 anek jantu ande dete hai jinamein pramukh hai- pakshi, sarisrup, ubhaychar, stanpayi tathaa machhliyaan. hajaaron varshon se maanav tathaa anya jantu in andon ko khaataa aa rahaa he. pakshiyon tathaa sarisrupon ke andon ke upar ek raksha-kavach hota he, uske andar shwethak tathaa and pitak hote hai. murgi ke ande khaane ke liye sabase adhik pasand kiye jate hai. iske alaava batakh, teetar, aadi ke andey bhi khaaye jate hai.
