 pandit ravi shankar ek sitar vadak or sangitajna the. unhone vishwa ke kai mahtvapurn sangeet utsavon main hissaa liya he. unke yuva varsh europe or bharat main apane bhai udai shankar ke nrity samooh ke saath daura karte hue beete.
 ravishankar ne bhartiya shastreey sangeet kii shiksha ustad allauddin khan se praapt kii. apane bhai udai shankar ke nrity dal ke saath bharat or bharat se baahar samay gujaarne vaale ravishankar ne 1938 se 1944 tak sitar kaa adhyayan kiya or phir swatantra taur se kaam karne lage. baad main unka vivaah bhi ustad allauddin khan kii beti annapurna se hua.
 is dauraan unhone sathyajith re kii filmon main sangeet bhi diya. 1949 se 1956 tak unhone ol india radio main bataur sangeet nirdeshak kaam kiya. 1960 ke baad unhone europe ke daure shuru kiye or yehudee menuhin va bitles group ke jorge harishan jaise logon ke saath kaam karke apni khaas pahchaan banaai. unki beti anoushka shankar sitar vadak hai to duusari beti norah jones bhi sheershasth gayikaon main shumaar kii jaati hai. unhen 1999 main bharat ratna se sammanit kiya gaya. ravi shankar ko kala ke kshetra main bharat sarkaar dwaara san 2009 main padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. bhartiya sangeet ko duniya bhar main sammaan dilaane vaale bharat ratna or padmavibhushan se navaaje gayi pandit ravishankar ko teen baar grami puraskaar se bhi nawaja gaya tha. unhone bhartiya or paashchaatya sangeet ke sanlayan main bhi badi bhoomika nibhaai. unke parivaar main anya nimnlikhit sangeetkaar he -
