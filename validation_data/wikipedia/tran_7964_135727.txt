 deshi: overnite 4.1 sthaneey: divasiy: 3.4 
 lodhi udyaan dilli shahar ke dakshini madhya ilaake main bana sundar udyaan he. yah safadarajang ke makbare se 1 kilometre purv main lodhi maarg par sthit he. pehle british kaal main is baag kaa naam ladi willington park tha. yahaan ke udyaan ke bich-bich main lodhi vansh ke makbare hai tathaa udyaan main fawwaare, talab, phool or jogging trek bhi bane hai. yah udyaan mul rup se gaanv tha jiske aas-paas 15veen-16veen shatabdi ke syed or lodi vansh ke smarak the. angrejon ne 1936 main is gaanv ko dobaaraa basaaya. yahaan neshnal bonjai park bhi he jahaan bonzai kaa acha sangrah he. is udyaan kshetra kaa vistaar lagbhag 90 acre main he jahaan udyaan ke alaava dilli sultanate kaal ke kai pracheen smarak bhi hai jinamein mohammad shah kaa makbara, sikandar lodi kaa mazebara, sheesh gumbad evam bada gumbad pramukh hai. in smarakon main praayah makbare hi hai jin par lodhi vansh dwaara 15veen sadi kii vaastukala kaa kaam kiya dikhataa he. lodhi vansh ne uttari bharat or punjab ke kuch bhubhag par or pakistan main vartmaan khaybar pakhtoonkhwaa par varsh 1451 se 1526 tak shashan kiya tha. ab is sthaan ko bhartiya puraatatv surveykshan dwaara sangrakshan praapt he. yahan ek udyaan bhi he jahaan pedo kii vibhinn prajatiyaan, gulab udyaan or green house he jahaan paudhon kaa pratikul ritu bachaakar rakhaa jaataa he. puure varsh yahaan anek prakaar ke pakshi dekhe ja sakate hai.
 lodhi udyaan kaa naam pehle ladi willington park tha. ladi wellington, wellington ke marques kii patni thi, jo us samay british shasankaal main 1931-36 tak bharat ke governor general the. bhartiya swatantraaa uparaant iskaa naam badal kar lodhi udyaan kar diya gaya he. kalantar main is udyaan kshetra kaa punarnirmaan 1968 main amareeki vaastukar joseph alan steen or getate eco dwaara karavaaya gaya tha.  is kshetra main kai smarak hai hai, jinamein mohammad shah or sikandar lodi ke makbaron par lodhi rajvansh ke vaastushilp dikhaai dete hai. inkaa nirmaan 15 veen shatabdi main kiya gaya tha. mohammad shah kaa mazebara 1444 main alaa-ud-deen alam shah dwaara banwaaya gaya tha. udyaan se purv yah kshetra gaanv tha jiske nikat 15-16veen shatabdi ke syed or lodhi vansh ke makbare bane hue the. 1936 main angrejon dwaara is gaanv ko dobaaraa basaaya gaya jo kalantar main ladi willingdon ke liye arakshit kar diya gaya tha. ladi willingdon bharat ke governor general kii patni thi or baad main iskaa naam ladi willingdon park rakh diya gaya, lekin san 1947 main swatantraaa ke baad ise lodhi udyaan naam kar diya gaya.
 saiyad vansh ke tisare shaasha mohammad shah the. jinka shashan 1434-44 tak rahaa. inkaa shashan kaal isliye bhi janaa jaataa he ki us dauraan sarhind ke afgaan soobedaar behalol lodhi ne punjab ke baahar apane prabhaav ko badha liya tha. vah lagbhag swatantra ho gaya tha. isi dauraan mohammad shah kaa putr or unka uttaradhikari allaudin alam shah dilli ke shashan kaa bhaar apane ek saale or shahar police adheekshak kaa bhaar duusare saale par chhodkar badaayun chalaa gaya tha. uske jane ke baad donon hi alag-thalag pad gaye or 1451 main behalol lodhi ne singhaasana par kabja kar liya.
 bahalul khan lodhi ke shashan kaal main hi rajya main kai vidrohi taqatvar hone lage the. jiske chalate uske uttaradhikari sikandar lodhi kaa adhikansh samay jonpur ke prantiy shaasha or anya saradaaron ko dabaane main hi laga rahaa.
 mohammad shah ke makbare se 300 meter par yah makbara sthit he. isamen jiskaa shav dafan he, uski pahchaan nahin ho pai he. parantu yah spasht he ki vah sikandar lodhi ke shashan kaal main koi ucch padaadhikari tha.
 vaastukala kii drushti se isamen do manjila imaarat kii akruti jhalkati he. iske andar kai kabr hai. inke bare main itihaas main jaankaree uplabdh nahin he. magar mana jaataa he ki inhen bhi sikandar lodhi ke shashan kaal main banaaya gaya tha. is makbare par shishe kii tilon dwaara alankaran hone ke kaaran ise sheesh gumbad naam mila he.
 sikandar lodhi ke makbare se thodi dur purv main saat meharaavon vaala ek pul he jise naale par banaaya gaya he. iske upar bich ke meharaavon kaa failaav adhik he. is pul main aath khambhe hai. ise mughal kaal ke dauraan banaaya gaya tha. is pul kaa nirmaan badshaah akbar ke shashan kaal ke dauraan nawab bahadur naamak vyakti ne karavaaya tha.
