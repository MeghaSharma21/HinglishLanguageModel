 jai narayan vyas ek bhartiya rajaneta or rajasthan ke mukhyamantri rah the. jodhpur sthit jai narayan vyas vishvavidhyalay unke naam par rakhaa gaya he.saamaajik, raajnneetik, karmanyavaadee jainarayan vyas apane yug kii jivit gaatha the. yug kaa spandan unki aatma kaa swar banakar unki kavitaaon main, unke geeton main, unke upanyaas main, unke naatakon main, unki kahaniyon main mukhar hua. vyas ji sahi arth main saamaajik punarjagran evam rashtriya bhaawanaaon ke kavi the. ve samaaj sudhaar evam rashtriya andolan ko samarpit kavi, lekhak, patrkaar the.
