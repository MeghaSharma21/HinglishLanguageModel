 dhruvaswamini jaishankar prasad dwaara rachit prasiddh hindi naatak he. yah prasad kii antim or shreshth natya-kriti he. 
 iskaa kathanak guptkaal se sambaddh or shodh dwaara itihasasammat he. yah naatak itihaas kii pracheenta main vartmaan kaal kii samasya ko prastut karata he. prasad ne itihaas ko apni natyabhivyakti kaa madhyam banaakar shaashvat maanav-jivan kaa swarup dikhaaya he, yug-samasyaon ke hal die hai, vartmaan ke dhundhalake main ek jyoti di he, rashtriyata ke saath-saath vishwa-prem kaa sandesh diya he. isliye unhone itihaas main kalpana kaa sangyojan kar itihaas ko varttman se jodne kaa prayaas kiya he.
 rangamanch kii drushti se teen ankon kaa yah naatak prasad kaa sarvottam naatak he. iske paatro kii sankhya simit he. iske samvaad bhi paatra anukool or laghu hai. bhashaa paatro kii bhashaa ke anukool he. masalan dhruvaswamini kii bhashaa main virangana kii ojaswita he. is naatak main anek sthalon par ardhvakyon kii yojana he jo naatak main saundarya or gahare arth kii sristi karti he.
