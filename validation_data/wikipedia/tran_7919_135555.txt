 niyantran siddhanth main sudrudh niyantran se taatpary niyantrak dizin kii us pranali se he jisamen prakram se sambandhit anischitataaon kaa vishesh dhyaan rakhaa jaataa he. duusare shabdon main, sudrudh niyantran kii vidhiyaan aise dizin kii gayin hai ki anischitataayen yaa vyavdhaan yadi ek di gayi seem main rahen to niyantran pranali thik se kaam karegi. 
 aarmabhik kaal kii bode tathaa anya logon kii niyantrak dizin kii vidhiyaan kaaphi sudrudh thim. kintu 1960 or 1970 ke dashak main state-spes vidhiyon se niyantrak dizin kii shruwaat hui jo kabhi-kabhi sudruth nahin hotin hai. isliye unamem anusandhan kii aavashyaktaa anubhav kii gayi or is prakaar 'sudrudh niyantran' ke siddhanth kaa janm hua. sudrudh niyantran kaa janm 1980 ke dashak main hua or isane 1990 ke dashak main praudhata hasil kii. aaj bhi yah ek sakriy vishay he.
 yadi sudrudh niyantran kii tulana anukuli niyantran se karen to ham dekhte hai ki sudrudh niyantran ek 'sthitik' niyantran he jabaki anukuli niyantran parivartanon ko maapkar uske anusaar apane ko badal letaa he. sudrudh niyantrak yah maanakar dizin kiya jaataa he ki kuch char agyaat hi rahenge kintu ek seemaa ke andar.
