 vrihadkshatra ne kaikey desh ke paancho kaikey bhaaiyon kaa netritv kiya. yah sthaan aaj ke pakistan main kahin sthit he. kaikey bandhuon ne bahut se yoddhaon se yuddh kiya. vrihadkshatra ne kshemdhurti kaa vadh kiya. vrihadkshatra pandav paksh kaa yoddha tha. 
 teenon bhaaiyon, purujit, dhristaketu or vrihadkshatra kaa vadh chaudaaven din ke yuddh main dronacharya ke haathon hua. anya kaikey bandhu apane anya bhaaiyon dwaara maare gaye jo kaurav sena kii ore se yuddh kar rahe the.
