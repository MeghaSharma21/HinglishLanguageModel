 sindhiya bharat main ek shahi maratha vansh he. is vansh me gwalior rajya ke 18 veen or 19 veen shatabdi main shaasha shaamil hai.
 sindhiya rajvansh ranoji sindhiya, jo maharashtra ke satara jile main kanherkhed gaanv ke patil janakojirao ke putr the, dwaara sthapit kiya gaya tha. peshwa baaji raav ke antim varshon main maratha mahasang majboot shakti ke rup main ubhara. ranoji 1726 main maratha vijay abhiyaan ke kaarybhari the. ranoji ne 1731 main ujjain main apni rajdhani sthapit kii. unke uttaradhikari jayajirao, jyotibarao, dattajirao, janakojirao, hadji or or daulataraav sindhiya the. gwalior kaa sindhiya rajya main 18veen sadi ke uttarardh main ek pramukh kshetreey shakti ban kar ubhara or teen anglo - maratha yuddh main pramukh bhoomika nibhaai. unhone rajput rajyon par apana bolbalaa rakhaa or ajmer ke rajya par vijay praapt kii.1818 ke tisare angl-maratha yuddh main angrejon ke haathon maratha rajyon kii haar ke baad, daulataraav sindhiya british bharat ke bheetar ek riyasat ke rup main sthaneey swaittata sweekaar karne or ajmer ko angrezo ko dene ke liye majaboor ho gayi. daulataraav kii mrityu ke baad, maharani baiza bai ne samrajya chalaaya or british satta se bachi rahi, uske baad unke god liye hue janakoji raav ne satta sanbhali. janakoji kii mrityu 1843 main hui or unki vidhwaa tarabai raaje sindhiya ne safaltaapurvak sthiti ko yathaavat banaae rakhaa or jayajirao naamak balak ko god liya.sindhiya parivaar ne 1947 tak bharat kii swatantraaa tak gwalior par shashan kiya. tab maharaja jivajirav sindhiya ne bharat me vilay ke liye sweekruti de di. gwalior anya riyasaton ke saath vilay ke saath madhya bharat kaa nayaa rajya ban gaya. jorge jivajirav ne rajyapramukh ke rup main 28 may 1948 se 31 oktober 1956 tak kaary kiya, tab madhya bharat kaa madhya pradesh main vilay kar diya gaya.1962 main maharaja jivajirav kii vidhwaa, rajmata vijayaraaje sindhiya loksabhaa ke liye nirvaachit huin or is prakaar sindhiya parivaar kii chunavi raajnneeti main jivan - yatra prarambh hui. ve shuru me congress party kii sadsy thi, parantu baad main bhartiya janata party kii ek prabhaavshaalee sadsy ban kar ubharin. unke putr madhavrao sindhiya 1971 main congress party kaa pratinidhitiv karte hue loksabhaa ke liye nirvaachit hue or 2001 me mrityu tak loksabhaa ke sadsy rahe. unke putr jyotiraditya sindhiya bhi apane pita kii lok sabha seat par congress party ke tikat par 2004 chune gayi.vijayaraaje kii betiyon ne bhartiya janata party kaa samarthan kiya he. vasundhara raaje sindhiya ne madhya pradesh or rajasthan se paanch sansadeey chunaavon me vijay pai. 1998 ke baad ke vaajapeyi sarkaar ke shasankaal me vasundhara ne kai alag alag mantralayon kaa prabhaar sambhala. 2003 me unhone rajasthan main bhartiya janata party kaa netritv karte huye baari bahumat hasil or rajya kii mukhyamantri bani. uski duusari beti, yashodhara raaje sindhiya ne madhya pradesh ke shivpuri se vidhansabha chunaav ladaa or 1998 or 2003 main vijay praapt kii. madhya pradesh vidhaan sabha chunaavo main bhaajapa kii jeet par, ve paryaton, khel or yuva maamalon ke liye rajya mantri bani. vasundhara ke putr dushyant singh ne 2004 main rajasthan se loksabhaa main pravesh kiya.
 sindhiya parivaar ko paramparaagat rup se raja sindhiya or puraane maratha vansh kaa vanshaj mana jaataa he. sindhiya ne peshwa kii seva kii he or deccan ke bahamani sultanate ke shiledaar bhi rahe.

 sindhiya parivaar kii isliye aalochana hoti he ki usne 1857 ke bharat ke pratham swatantrata sangram main angrejon kaa saath diya tha or jhansi kii rani lakshmibai ko maravane main sindhiya parivaar ne pramukh bhoomika adaa kii thi. rani ke balidaan hone ke baad angrejon ne tathya tope ko hiraasat main le liya or 1857 ke swatantrata ke sangharsh ko puri tarah se kuchal diya gaya.
