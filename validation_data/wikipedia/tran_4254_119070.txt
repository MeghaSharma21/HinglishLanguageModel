 sar jiauddin ahamed:  ganitagya,, sansad, tarkazna, prakritik darshanik, rajaneta, raajnneetik siddhantvaadi, shikshavid or ek vidwaan the.. vah aligarh andolan ke sadsy the or emeo college ke principal professor the. or bharat ke aligarh muslim vishvavidhyalay ke rector the. inhone aligarh muslim vishvavidhyalay ke kulapati ke rup main teen padon par kaary kiya..
 1917 main, unhen kalakatta vishvavidhyalay ayog kaa sadsy niyukt kiya gaya jise sadler ayog bhi kaha jaataa he.. vah british bhartiya sena ke bhartiyakaran ke liye bhartiya sandhrst samiti or shiya ayog ke rup main bhi jane vaali skin samiti ke sadsy bhi the.
 jiauddin ahamed kaa janm 13 farvari 1873 ko british bharat ke meerut, uttar pradesh main hua tha.. inhone praathmik shiksha madrase se kii or baad main mohammad anglo-oriental college, aligarh se praapt kii.aligarh ke saath dow jiauddin kaa sahyog 1889 main shuru hua, jab 16 saal kii umr main, emeo college college main 'pratham varsh' main shaamil ho gaye. inhone pratham shrenee main hiskool paas kiya or inhen laing padak or sarkari chaatravrutti se sammanit kiya gaya.inhen sarkari college, elahabaad main shaamil hona padaa, kyonki mohammad anglo-oriental college main vigyaan paathyakram uplabdh nahin the. vah aligarh laut aae or 1895 main vigyaan vibhaag ke chaatro ke bich khadey hue, or unhen straichi gold medal se sammanit kiya gaya. bie paas karne ke turant baad, inhen mohammad anglo-oriental college main ganit main sahaayak vyaakhyaata niyukt kiya gaya.yogyata ke aadhaar par inhen dipti colector ke pad ke liye namit kiya gaya tha, lekin jiauddin ne prastaav ko asweekaar kar diya or college kii seva main jari rakhane ke liye chune gaye. sar saiyad ne inhen 60-100 rupee ke grad main sthaayee niyukti kii peshkash kii, basharte unhone hastakshar kiye paanch saal kii avadhi ke liye seva karne ke liye ek bandhan kiya..
 jiauddin ne 1895 main mohammad anglo-oriental college se ganit main apana bie puraa kiya. vah diessi praapt karne vaale pehle muslim the., elahabaad vishvavidhyalay se. inkaa kshetra jatil logaridam anuprayog tha. unhone antar jyamiti or bijganit jyamiti main prakashit kiya. inhone 1897 main littan straichi gold medal jeeta.. shikshan ke dauraan, inhone apni shiksha jari rakhi or kalakatta or elahabaad vishvavidhyalayon se eme degree or baad main 1901 main diessi bhi arjeet kii.
 1901 main, jiauddin ne sarkari chaatravrutti par england ke liye gaye or cambridge vishvavidhyalay se ganit main apni sammaan kii degree praapt kii. inhen 1904 main ijac newton chaatravrutti se sammanit kiya gaya, pehle bhartiya puraskaar vijeta ban gaye. or vah royal estronaumical socity or landan ganitiya socity ke phelo chune gaye.uske baad vah 1904 main germany main gointingen vishvavidhyalay main shaamil ho gaye or germany ke gautingen vishvavidhyalay se piechdi oopaadhi praapt kii. inhone aadhunik jyamiti main unnat adhyayan ke liye paris vishvavidhyalay or bologna vishvavidhyalay kaa daura kiya.. inhone bologna, italy main khagol vigyaan main shodh kiya. or apane academik paddatiyon ko samajhane ke liye al azhar vishvavidhyalay, kahiraa kaa daura kiya..
 1907 main bharat lautne par ahamed apane eemyu foundation main shaamil ho gaye. 1911 main, inhen eemyu foundation or samvidhan samitiyon ke sachiv niyukt kiya gaya tha. vah mohammad anglo-oriental college main ganit ke professor bane or 1918 main principal kaa chayan kiya gaya. 1915 ke kings berthde onors suchi main unhen order of da indian empire kaa ek sahyogi niyukt kiya gaya tha..
 inhone un chaatro ko prashikshit kiya jo rudki ejineering college main pravesh chaahate the. inhone ejineering or vaaniki main seminar or prashikshit chaatro kaa aayojan kiya.. ahamed ne mohammad anglo-oriental college main chaatro ko laane ke liye bhugtan kiya. sabase ullekhneey main se ek haisarat mohan, jo kanpur se sammanit the or lakhanu jane kii yojana bana rahe the. ahamed ne mohan kii ganit pratibha ko dekha or emeo college main bhaag lene ke liye unhen or unke parivaar ko manaane ke liye kanpur gaye.ahamed ko emeo college main sahaayak master niyukt kiya gaya tha or 1913 main ek samay ke liye abhinay principal ke rup main kaary kiya tha.

 1913 main bharat sarkaar ke sankalp ke samay paanch vishvavidhyalayon ne bharat main sanchalit kiya. college vibhinn vishvavidhyalayon ke niyantran se baahar the. is samay landan vishvavidhyalay ko royal kamishan kii prati siforeeshon kaa punargathan kiya gaya tha. bhartiya vishvavidhyalayon ko sudhaarney kaa ek nirnay 1917 kalakatta vishvavidhyalay ayog ke duusare vishvavidhyalay ayog ke liye netritv kiya. ayog ke sadsy sar jiauddin the, dow gregory, ramse muir, sar hartog, dow horneal or sar asutosh mukherjee..
 1913 main bharat sarkaar ke sankalp ke samay bharat main keval paanch vishvavidhyalay the or collejon kii sankhya unke kshetreey simaaon ke bheetar vibhinn vishvavidhyalayon ke niyantran se baahar thi. natijatan, us avadhi main vibhinn prashasnic samasyaon ko dher kar diya. sar asutosh mukherjee kalakatta vishvavidhyalay ke kulapati the.. inhone 1916 main vishvavidhyalay main snatakottar shiksha pradaan karnaa shuru kiya jaisaa ki 1902 ke vishvavidhyalay shiksha ayog dwaara anushansit kiya gaya tha. isane sarkaar kaa dhyaan aakarshith kiya he. is samay tak landan vishvavidhyalay ko lord halden kii adhyakshata main royal kamishan kii siforeeshon ke anusaar punargathit or sudhaar kiya gaya tha. isliye, yah bhartiya vishvavidhyalayon ko bhi sudhaarney kii aavashyaktaa ban gai. in sabhi paristhitiyon ne duusare vishvavidhyalay ayog ke gathan kaa netritv kiya. yani, kalakatta vishvavidhyalay ayog, 1917 ayog ne skool shiksha se vishvavidhyalay shiksha tak puure kshetra kii samiksha kii. sadaral ayog ne yah vichaar kiya ki vishvavidhyalay shiksha main sudhaar ke liye madhyamik shiksha main sudhaar ek aavashyak shart thi..
 1911 main, emeo college ko ek vishvavidhyalay main badalane ke liye kendriya samiti kii sthapana kii gai, raja mahamudabad ke adhyaksh, saiyad ali bilgraami sachiv or ahamed ke rup main sanyukt sachiv ke rup main. college ko vishvavidhyalay kii sthiti ke liye 30 lakh, jise 1915 main hasil kiya gaya tha. us samay chaatr nikaay 1500 se kam tha. ahamed ne dhan jutaney ke liye bharat bhar main yatra kii.
 ahamed 1934 main kulapati chune gaye, 1946 tak shesh rahe, jo sabase lambey samay tak seva kar rahe visi ban gaye. inhone snaatak or snatakottar str donon main paathyakram padhaaye. . vah eemyu kaa ekmaatr shikshan visi the. unki madad se, lahore main islamia college kii sthapana hui thi. ahamed ne college ke liye or islamia college, peshawar ke liye aadharshila rakhi.
 1920 main, moulana mohammad ali johar or unke bhai moulana shaukat ali ke netritv main bhartiya musalamaanon ne turki main khilphaat ko behaal karne ke liye ek andolan shuru kiya. turkon ke paas khilphaat ke liye koi upyog nahin tha or unhone mustafa kamaal pasha ko apane netaa ke rup main chunaa tha; arab ise nahin chaahate the or angrejon ne iskaa virodh kiya tha. congress party ne apane prayaason kaa samarthan kiya or 9 sitambar 1920 ko, ek prastaav paarit kiya jisane asahyog andolan shuru kiya. is andolan ko bharat main hindu-muslim ekta ke pratik ke rup main sammanit kiya gaya tha.
 11 oktober 1920 ko, ali bhaaiyon ne swami satya dev or gandhiji ke saath aligarh kaa daura kiya. in netaaon ko emeo college student union ko sambodhit karne ke liye aamantrit kiya gaya tha. chaatro ne british sarkaar ke saath asahyog ke samarthan main ek prastaav paarit kiya, turki ke prati british drushtikon kii ninda kii, maang kii ki college sarkaar se koi anudaan sweekaar nahin karegaa or elahabaad vishvavidhyalay ke saath sambhddhata ko band kar degaa. iske alaava, sankalp ne emeo college ko sarkaar se swatantra rashtriya vishvavidhyalay main badalane ke liye kaha.
 ahamed ne ahamed khan kii thisis sweekaar kar lee thi ki anya bhartiya samudaayon ke saath shaikshanik samanta tak pahunchane tak musalamaanon ko raajnneeti main shaamil nahin hona chahie. unhone vishvavidhyalay ke adhikariyon se sampark kiya, or unhen is sangharsh se baahar rakhane ke liye aashwast kiya. jab sankat gaharaa hua, usne college band kar diya or chaatro ko ghar bhej diya.
 dow ahamed ne trustee board ke sadasyon ke bich sulah laane ke liye bade prayaas kiye, or adhikansh chaatro ko parisar main waapas laane main safal rahe. ahamed ke sammaan main, jise ab doctor sahib ke naam se janaa jaataa tha, parisar main sankaay or karmchaariyon ne ek ratribhoj diya jisamen college trustee ke saath-saath aligarh or agra ke british adhikariyon ko aamantrit kiya gaya tha. khajaja abdul majid, un trustee main se ek jinhone prarambh main unka samarthan nahin kiya tha, ne kaha: "main pradhanacharya ke rup main dow sahib kii niyukti ke khilaaf tha, lekin unke netritv main hue sudhaaron ne mujhe vishwaas dilaayaa he ki yah chaatro ke bhavishya ke liye acha hoga, karmchaaree, maanad sachiv, janata or sarkaar ke saath sambandh.
 dow ahamed ne ek lokapriy andolan kaa virodh kiya tha or muslim janata ko algaav karne kaa jokhim uthaaya tha. unhen ek lokapriy andolan kaa samarthan karne or sarkari sahaayta khone yaa sarkari sahaayta ke saath ek muslim vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana ke bich chayan karnaa padaa.jab kakshaen phir se shuru huin, to badi sankhya main chaatr ghar par rahe. aisa prathit hota he ki college namakan main tej giravat se college kii unnati naa hogi. dow sahib ne kai shaharon kaa daura kiya or adhikansh chaatro ko waapas lautne ke liye manaaya, jabaki nae chaatro ne daakhila liya. haalanki, dow ahamed ne un logon ke krodh ko arjeet kiya jinhone uske baad unka virodh karnaa jari rakhaa. usee samay unhen ek thos aadhaar mila jo unhen samarthit tha.
 1 disambar 1 9 20 ko muslim vishvavidhyalay adhinium paarit hua, or is prakaar emeo college aligarh muslim vishvavidhyalay ban gaya. raja mahmudabaad pehle kulapati or ahamed, upkulapati bane. raja sahib vishesh rup se dow. sahib ke paksh main pivisi banane ke paksh main nahin the, balki ek angrej pasand karte the. jab koi europea is sthiti ko sweekaar karne ke icchhuk nahin tha or koi anya sakasham muslim uplabdh nahin tha, to unhone dow ahamed ko sweekaar kar liya. vishvavidhyalay adhinium ne kaha ki pivisi "vishvavidhyalay ke pramukh shaikshanik adhikari" ban jayega. yah aage nirdhaarit kiya gaya tha ki kulapati kii anupasthiti main pivisi academik parishad ke adhyaksh ke rup main kaary karegi. dow sahib or raja sahib aksar vishvavidhyalay maamalon ke prabandhan par alag-alag vichaar rakhte the. ek saal baad, raja sahib ne istipha de diya or nawabzada aftab ahamed khan ve.si. bane 1922 main, dow sahib ko rajya vidhansabha main phir se nirvaachit kiya gaya tha.
 1915 tak vah saarvajanik maamalon or takneekee or vyavsaayik shiksha main ruchi le rahe the. 1919 or 1922 main unhen elahabaad vishvavidhyalay ke pratinidhi ke rup main upi kii vidhaan sabha sadsy niyukt kiya gaya tha. unhone 21 or 22 april 1935 ko maarison islamia skool main marera main yojit duusare muslim kamboh sammelan kii adhyakshata kii.1924 main vah uttarpuri vidhaan sabha ke liye menpuri, eta or farrukhabad ke muslim nirvachan kshetra se chune gaye the.
 ahamed 1930 main kendriya assembly ke sadsy chune gaye the. unhen baar-baar vibhinn nirvachan kshetron se nirvaachit kiya gaya tha or 1947 tak kendriya vidhanmandal main kaary kiya gaya tha. 1946 main, vah kendriya assembly main muslim lig ke mukhya kaaryakartaaon main se ek the.inhone sansad main bhartiya videsh sambandh adhinium ko pryojit kiya. ahamed ne bhartiya railway ke liye or bhartiya rigerv bank ke saath bajet par kaam kiya. jab bhartiya rigerv bank kii sthapana hui to vah kanoon ko or adhik kushal kaary karne ke liye aage badhane main shaamil the..
 dow ahamed swatantra rup se swatantra party ke sadsy the, jinamein hinduon, muslim or sikh shaamil the. jab is party ko bhang kar diya gaya to vah muslim lig main shaamil ho gaye or iske sansadeey sachiv ke rup main kaary kiya.
 jiauddin ahamed kii 22 disambar 1947 ko landan main unki mrityu ho gai. unke sharir ke rup main anurodh kiya gaya tha, jiske baad ahamed ko aligarh main dafnaaya gaya tha.
