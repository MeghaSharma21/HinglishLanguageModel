 
 urad yaa urad ek dalhan hota he. urad ko sanskrit main 'maash' yaa 'balaadhy'; banglaa' main maash yaa kalai; gujarati main add; marathi main udid; punjabi main maanh, angrezi, spanish or italian main vigna mungon; german main urdbohane; french main harikot urd; polish main fasola mungon; portugali main phejon-da-india tathaa latin main 'fasiolas radietus', kehete hai.
 iskaa dwidal paudha lagbhag ek haath unchaa he or bharatvarsh main sarvatra jowar, bajra or rui ke kheton main tathaa akelaa bhi boyaa jaataa he. isse milanevaali daal bhojan or aushadhi, donon roopon main upyogi he. beej kii do jatiyaan hoti hai : 
  kaali or badi, jo varsha ke aarambh main boi jaati he or 
  hari or choti, jisaki boai do maheene pashchaat hoti he.
 isaki hari faliyon kii bhaaji tathaa bijon se daal, papada, bade ityadi bhojya padaarth banaae jate hai. ayurveda ke matanusaar isaki daal snigdh, paushtik, balkaarak, shukr, dugdh, maance or medvardhak; vaat, shwaas or bavasir ke rogon main hitkar tathaa shouch ko saaf karanevaali he.
 rasinic vishleshanon se isamen starch 56 pratishat, albuminaeds 23 pratishat, tel sava do pratishat or phosphorus acid sahit raakh saadhe chaar pratishat pai gai he.
 isaki taasir thandi hoti he, atah iskaa seven karte samay shuddh ghi main hing kaa baghaar laga lenaa chahie. isamen bhi carbohydrate, vitamins, calcium va protein paryaapt maatra main paae jate hai. bavasir, gathiya, dama evam lakva ke rogiyon ko iskaa seven kam karnaa chahie.
