 arm ek RISC architekchar vaala computer procesar he. multah ARM, 'ecorn risk mashin' kaa laghurup tha jo baad main 'advansd risk mashin' ke laghurup ke rup main prayog hone laga. britain kii ARM Holdings naamak company iskaa architekchar viksit karti he or anya companiyon ko license detii he taaki ve iskaa upyog karke apane utpaada nirmit kar saken. iskaa upyog karke systams-on-chip tathaa systams-on-audyul sahit anekon utpaada banaaye jate hai.
 arm ke kor anekon utpaadon main upyog kiye ja rahe hai, vishesh rup se PDAs tathaa smart phonon main. kuch udhaaharan ye hai- microsoft ke sarphase or sarphase2, aipal ke iPads,or iPod, canan ke digital camere aadi.
 ARM ke chips kaa upyog raspberi pai main, bigleboard main, biglebon main, pandaboard main, tathaa kai anya ekal-board computaron main ho rahaa he kyonki ve bahut chote, saste hai evam bahut kam vidyut kharch karte hai.
