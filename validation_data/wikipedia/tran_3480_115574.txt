 luiziana sanyukt rajya ke dakshini kshetra main sthit ek rajya he. isaki rajdhani beton rouge he. ek doosraa mahatvapurn nagar new arliens he jo 2005 main hurricane katrina main dub gaya tha. america main luiziana ekmaatr rajya he jiske raajnneetik upkhandon ko parish kaha jaataa he jo ki anya rajyon ke countiyon ke baraabar he. luiziana ke uttar main arkansus, purv main mississippi, pashchim main texas or dakshin main mexico kii khadi he.
 rajya kii adhikansh bhoomi mississippi nadi ke kaaran talchhatikaran se bani he. isamen samrudh dakshini biom shaamil he. 1803 main kshetra kii america kharid se pehle, vartmaan louiciana rajya ek francisi upnivesh tha or ek sankshipt avadhi ke liye ek spanish bhi. iske alaava, 18veen shatabdi main upniveshvaadiyon ne kai africa logon ko gulam ke rup main aayaat kiya. 2015 main lagaaye gaye anumaan ke anusaar rajya kii jansankhya 46,81,666 hai. iske mutabik ye sanyukt rajya kaa 25waan sabase zyada aabaadi vaala rajya hua. yah kshetra ke aakaar anusaar 31waan sabase bada he. luiziana main 8% log french or 4% spani bolte hai. 86% logon dwaara boli jane vaali angrezi rajya main koi aadhikarik darja nahin rakhti.
