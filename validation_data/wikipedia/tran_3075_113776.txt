 pramod tiwari ek bhartiya congressi netaa or pratapgarh vartmaan main rajyasabha saansad hai, uttar pradesh main sthit pratapgarh janapad ke rampur khaas chunaav kshetra kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai. pramod tiwari kaa janm 16 july 1951 main hua. pramod tiwari kii ajukational qolification biessi or elelbi he.
 rampur khaas vidhansabha kshetra ke liye ab tak hue 12 chunaavon main 10 baar congress pramod tiwari kaa kabja rahaa he. varsh 1980 main congress netaa pramod tiwari ne is seat par pehali baar jeet kaa jhanda gadha to uske baad congress ne aaj tak kisi dal ko khaataa nahin kholane diya. isse pehle do baar nirdaleey ummidavaar ke rup main tejabhaan singh nirvaachit huethe.
 lagaataar aath chunaav jitkar ricard banane vaale pramod tiwari ko gherne ke liye varsh 2007 main samaajawaadee party ne vigyaat singh ko rampur khaas se chunaav maidan main utaara tha lekin pramod ne unhen 30 hajaar se zyada maton se haraaya. 
 pramod tiwari rampur khaas seat se 9 baar lagaataar jeet chuke hai. pramod tiwari is seat se pehali baar 1980 main jite the. pramod tiwari 1984 se 1989 ke bich 2 baar rajya mantri bane. pramod pratapgarh main kai collejon ke adhyaksh hai. pratapgarh kii rampurkhaas seat se lagaataar nau vidhansabha chunaav jitkar ginis book main apana naam darj karaane vaale pramod tiwari pichley do dashak se congress vidhayak dal ke netaa rahe hai.
 vartmaan main wein rajyasabha sadsy hai.
 rampur khaas se pramod tiwari lagaataar 9 baar chunaav jeet chuke hai. inkaa naam ginis book of world record main darj kiya gaya he. ek dal, ek kshetra or ek chunaav chihn ke saath nau baar jeetne kaa ricard. vaise nau baar jeetne kaa ricard bhaajapa netaa oodal ke naam bhi he, lekin ve lagaataar nahin jite the.
 uttar pradesh ke elahabaad main do din pehle congress mahasachiv rahul gandhi kii raily ke dauraan samaajawaadee party kaaryakarta kii pitayee ke aarope main namjad varisht congressi netaa pramod tiwari ne buudhvaar ko kaha ki yah karrwai raajnneetik vidwesh ke tahat kii gai he.
 pramod tiwari ne patrakaron ko bataaya ki jo yuvak suraksha gheraa todakar rahul gandhi ko kaale jhande dikhaane pahunche the unke khilaaf sarkaar ne koi karrwai nahin kii, ulate do kendriya mantriyon sahit congress ke chaar netaaon ke khilaaf aisi dhaaraon main mukadmaa darj kar diya jaise ki ve peshewar or khoonkhaar apradhi hon. tiwari ne yah bhi kaha ki isse maaloom hota he ki yah karrwai suniyojit evam raajnneetik vidwesh ke tahat kii gai he.
 ullekhneey he ki sapa se jude yuva netaa kii shikaayat par elahabaad ke jhusee thane main 17 navambar 2011 raat pramod tiwari ke alaava kendriya mantri jitin prasad, aar.pi.en. singh va vidhayak naseeb pathan ke khilaaf bhartiya dand samhita kii vibhinn dhaaraon ke tahat mukadmaa darj kiya gaya.
