 kamyak van kuru rajya kii seemaa par basaa hua van he. yah rakshas tathaa bhoot-preton ke rahane ke liye banaaya gaya tha. mahabharat kaal me is van ko maha daitya van bhi kaha jaataa tha.
 kamyak van par keval teen rajyo ne hi raaj kiya tha, wo the-
 ghatotkach kii mrityu ke baad bhim phirase waha kaa raja ban gaya.
