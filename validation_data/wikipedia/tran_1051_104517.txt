 rashtriya kariyar seva bharat kaa ek rashtriya isiti aadhaarit portal he jo yuvaaon kii aakankshaon ke saath sunahare avasar jodne ke liye mukhya rup se viksit kii he. yah portal naukari chaahane waalon, naukari pradaataaon, kaushal pradaataaon, karier salaahkaaron, aadi ke panjikaran kii suvidha detaa he. portal ke ek behad paardarshee or upyogkarta ke anukool tareeke se naukari milan sevaayen pradaan karata he. karier paraamarsh saamagree ke saath-saath ye suvidhaayen karier kendron, mobile upkarnon, siessi, aadi jaise kai channelon ke madhyam se portal dwaara vitarit kiya jayega.
