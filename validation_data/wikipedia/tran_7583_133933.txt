 kravyadant yaa 'criodonta' ek pracheen pashu-varg he. is varg ke jantu aaj ke mansabhakshi pashuon ke purvaj samajhe jate hai. yahi is jati ke jeevon kii visheshta he. aisa prathit hota he ki is varg ke jeevon kaa ek alag hi vansh tha, jo criteshas or peleosin yugon ke ketahariyon kii pehali shakha main thi. kravyadant vikaas kii praarambhik dasha main the or ketahariyon ke samaan hi ve aakaar main bhi chote the. isi prakaar yah bhi kaha ja saktaa he ki mansabhakshi pashuon ke vikaas kii parampara main ye kravyadant nimnatam sthiti main the. mansabhakshan kaa swabhaav unke daanton tathaa shaareerik rachana ke kramik vikaas se prakat hota he.
 mansabhakshan hi is jati ke pashuon kii visheshta he, parantu in aadi kravyadanton main maance kaatane ke daant nahin hote the. inke daanton kii panktiyaan pratham premoller or antim molr ko chhodkar iske cheer faad karanevaale daanton kaa vikaas ho gaya he or ve anya kravyadanton tathaa iske purvaj kitahari praaniyon ke isi prakaar ke daanton ke sadrush tathaa saral nahin rah gaye hai. praaia: purna viksit thim. kalantar main inke daanton kii banaavat main vibhinnata hone lagi or kuch kute ke cheerfaad karanevaale kenine daant kii bhanti bade or nukile hone lage. in pracheen kravyadant mansabhakshiyon kii dantarachana mulat: pracheen kitbhakshiyon kii bhanti milti thi. coup tathaa mathew naamak visheshgyon ke matanusaar peleosin tathaa eosin yugon ke micidi jantu kravyadant ke hi parivaar ke the. inhen donon ke anusaar micidi vyaghr parivaar ke jantuon or kutta parivaar ke praaniyon se inak udgam hua. is prakaar nukile daantvaale aadhunik mansabhakshi varg ke jantuon ke purvaj peleosin yug ke kravyadant hi hai.
 ye chote mansabhakshi jeev choti-choti jhaadiyon yaa jangalon main rahete the. apane shikaar kii khoj main tathaa apni raksha ke liye ye pedo bhi chadh jate the. inamen kuch mansabhakshi, kuch sarvabhakshi or kuch kitbhakshi the. sada galaa maance khaanevaale in kravyadanton main se kuch kaa mastishk bahut hi chote aakaar kaa tha or unki samajhadaari bhi nimnatam avasthaa main thi. samajhadaari kaa abhaav is shrenee ke sheeghra nasht ho jane kaa ek kaaran ho saktaa he.
 kravyadanton kaa vibhaajan nimnlikhit teen upvargon main kiya gaya he :
 inhen arctoconeodei bhi kehete he. ye tershari yug ke kravyadant hai, jo peleosin yug main apni sankhya kii charam seemaa par pahunch gaye the. iske baad inaki sankhya teji se ghatane lagi phir bhi eosin yug trak ye vidyaman rahe. europe ke peleosin yug ke upree tahon ke arctoceon aajkal ke richon ke samaan he. richon ke saath keval inke kaamon kaa hi saadrishy he. inke anguuthon main khur the or inke daanton kii rachana bhi atyant saral thi.
 inhen messonikidi kii bhi sangya di gai he, kravyadanton kii duusari jati he. is jati ke kravyadanton main bade aakaar main viksit hone kaa bhi abhas prathit hota he. inke daanton kii rachana main bhi ab visheshta dikhaai padati he. inke daant cheerfaad ke upyukt nahin hote the, parantu inke dadh ke daanton main ek vishesh prakaar ke kund dikhaai padate hai, jinaki sahaayta se is upvarg ke praani apane shikaar tathaa unki haddiyon ko bahut saralata se todate or chabate the. inke pair bhediyon ke samaan the, jinamein ungliyaan dur dur thim or nakhun chipte the. inamen aajkal ke lakadabagghon kii bhanti panje nahin the, varan chote-chote khur the. messonikidi upvarg ke in kravyadanton kaa mahatv unke vishisht aakaar ke kaaran he. mongolia kaa antim acreodi kravyadanton main sabase dderghaayu tha. isaki khopadi teen foot se bhi adhik lambii thi.
 kravyadanton kii teesri jati he. chirane-faadane vaale daant inaki visheshta hai. duusare jaanwaron par akraman karke ye jivan nirvaah karte the. inaki sharir rachana is kaary ke liye vishesh rup se anukool thi. vikaas kii praarambhik avasthaa main hi yah upvarg do-shakhaon oxinidi tathaa hinodontidi main bant gaya tha. in donon shakhaon ke kravyadanton main keval itnaa antar tha ki pratham shrenee ke kravyadanton ke upree bhaag kaa pratham molr tathaa nichle bhaag kaa duusari molr daant ab purna rup se kaatane ke liye ban gaye. isi prakaar duusari shrenee ke kravyadanton main upree bhaag ke duusare molr or nichle jabade main tisare molr kaatane ke kaam aane lage the. peleosin yug main is jati ke kravyadanton kaa vikaas apni paraakaashtha par pahunch gaya tha, parantu iske pashchaat hi inkaa hraas teevr gati se prarambh hua. is jati ke praaniyon ke jivit rahane ke chihn hamen eosin yug main bhi milte hai. is jati ke kravyadant ati pracheen shaf varg yaa khuravaale praaniyon ke shikaar par jivan nirvaah karte the. in ‘shaf’ varg ke praaniyon ke samaapt hone par kravyadanton kii yah vanshashakha bhi dhire-dhire lupt ho gai or eligosin main to in kravyadanton ke sthaan par inase milte julate nae jivan kaa pradurbhaav ho gaya tha, jinhen phiciped kehete hai. inamen jivan rachana ucch shrenee kii thi, kintu ye bhi chote aakaar ke the or apane purvajon ke samaan mansabhakshi swabhaav or aakaar ke the. in pushtaini roopon se paristhitiyon ke anusaar apane main parivartan karte-karte kalantar main mansabhakshiyon kii anek shakhaayen ho gayi.
