 sharatchandra chattopadhyaay bangla ke suprasiddh upanyaaskaar the. 
 unka janm hughali jile ke devanandpur main hua. ve apane mata-pita kii nau santanon main se ek the. atharah saal kii avasthaa main unhone intrens paas kiya. inhen dinon unhone "basa" naam se ek upanyaas likh daalaa, par yah rachana prakashit nahin hui. rabindranath thakur or bankimchandra chattopadhyaay kaa un par gaharaa prabhaav padaa. sharathchandra lalit kala ke chaatr the lekin aarthik tangi ke chalate vah is vishay kii padhai nahin kar sake. rojgaar ke talash main sharathchandra burma gaye or lok nirmaan vibhaag main clerk ke rup main kaam kiya. 
 kuch samay burma rahakar kalakatta lautne ke baad unhone gambhirtha ke saath lekhan shuru kar diya. burma se lautne ke baad unhone apana prasiddh upanyaas shrikant likhnaa shuru kiya. burma main unka sampark bangachandra naamak ek vyakti se hua jo tha to bada vidwaan par sharaabi or uchhinkhal tha. yaheen se charitraheen kaa beej padaa, jisamen mess jivan ke varnan ke saath mess kii naukaraani se prem kii kahani he. 
 jab vah ek baar burma se kalakatta aae to apni kuch rachanaayen kalkatte main ek mitra ke paas chhod gaye. sharat ko bina bataae unamem se ek rachana "badi didi" kaa 1907 main dharawahik prakaashan shuru ho gaya. do ek kisht nikalate hi logon main sansani fail gai or ve kehane lage ki shaayad rabindranath naam badalakar likh rahe hai. sharat ko isaki khabar saadhe paanch saal baad mili. kuch bhi ho khyaati to ho hi gai, phir bhi "charitraheen" ke chhapane main badi dikkat hui. bharatvarsh ke sampadak kaviver dwijendralal raay ne ise yah kahkar chhapane se manaa kar diya kiya ki yah sadachaar ke viruddh he. vishnu prabhakar dwaara aawaara maseehaa sheershak rachit se unka pramanik jivan parichay bahut prasiddh he.
 sharatchandra ne anek upanyaas likhe jinamein pandit moshaay, baikunther bill, mej didi, darpachurna, shrikant, arakshaniya, nishkruti, maamlar fal, grihadaah, shesh prashn, datta, devdas, bamhan kii ladki, vipradaas, dena paavna aadi pramukh hai. bengal ke krantikari andolan ko lekar "pather daavi" upanyaas likhaa gaya. pehle yah "bang vaani" main dharawahik rup se nikaala, phir pustakaakaar chapa to teen hajaar kaa sanskaran teen maheene main samaapt ho gaya. iske baad british sarkaar ne ise jabt kar liya. 
 sharat ke upanyaason ke kai bhartiya bhashaaon main anuwaad hue hai. kaha jaataa he ki unke purush paatro se unki nayikaaye adhik balishtha hai. sharatchandra kii janpriyata unki kalatmak rachana or nape tule shabdon yaa jivan se otprot ghatnawaliyon ke kaaran nahin he balki unke upanyaason main nari jis prakaar paramparaagat bandhanon se chatpataati drushtigochar hoti he, jis prakaar purush or stri ke sambandhon ko ek nae aadhaar par sthapit karne ke liye paksh prastut kiya gaya he, usee se sharat ko janpriyata mili. unki rachana hriday ko bahut adhik sparsh karti he. par sharatsahitya main hriday ke saare tatv hone par bhi usamen samaaj ke sangharsh, shoshan aadi par kam prakaash padataa he. palli samaaj main samaaj kaa chitra kuch kuch saamne aata he. mahesh aadi kuch kahaniyon main shoshan kaa prashn ubharkar aata he. 
 unke kuch upanyaason par aadhaarit hindi filmein bhi kai baar bani hai. inke upanyaas charitraheen par aadhaarit 1974 main isi naam se film bani thi. uske baad devdas ko aadhaar banaakar devdas film kaa nirmaan teen baar ho chukaa he. pehali devdas kundan laal sahagal dwaara abhinit, duusari devdas dilip kumaar, vaijayanti maala dwaara abhinit tathaa teesri devdas shahrukh khan, maadhuri dikshit, aishwarya raay dwaara abhinit. iske atirikt 1974 main charitraheen, parinita-1953 or 2005 main bhi, badi didi tathaa manjhali bahan, aadi par bhi chalchitron ke nirmaan hue hai.
