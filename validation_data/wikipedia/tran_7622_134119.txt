 helmand afghanistan kaa ek praant he jo us desh ke dakshin-pashchimi bhaag main sthit he. is praant kaa kshetrafal 58,584 varg kimi he or isaki aabaadi san 2006 main lagbhag 14.4 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. helmand ke lagbhag 90% log pashtun samudaay ke hai. is praant kii rajdhani lashkar gaah shahar he. vaise to yah ilaaka registani he lekin helmand nadi yahan se nikalati he or uskaa pani fasalon ke liye bahut laabhadaayak hota he. yah gair-kanoonee afim kii paidaavaar kaa bhi bahut bada centre he or vishwa kii 75% afim yaheen paidaa hoti he.
 helmand nadi pracheen kaal se hi is kshetra main kheti-baadi ke liye pani kaa anmol saadhan rahi he. isliye isapar hazaron varshon se anginat band bane hue hai. band or pul ke liye pracheen farsi main 'hetu' shabd istemaal hota tha jo sanskrit ke 'sethu' shabd kaa sajaateey shabd he. thik use tarah farsi kaa ek or shabd he 'mand' yani jiske paas kuch ho . is nadi kaa pracheen farsi naam hetumand tha yani 'bahut baandhon vaali nadi'. yah naam samay ke saath badalakar helmand or hirmand ban gaya.
 helmand kshetra main buddha dharm or hindu dharm prachlit tha. kuch vidwaan isko saraswati nadi kaa kshetra maanate hai jiskaa zikr rugveda main kiya gaya he. aage chalakar yah parsi dharm kaa bhi ek centre rahaa.
