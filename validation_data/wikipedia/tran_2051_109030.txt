 vijaipur bharat ke uttarakhand rajya ke bageshwar jile main sthit ek parvatiy sthal he. yah bageshwar-chaukodi rajmarg par bageshwar se 30 kimi or kanda se 5 kimi kii duuri par ghaney deodaar ke jangalon ke madhya sthit he. yah 2050 me kii oonchai par sthit he, or barf se dhaki himalay kii chotiyon, jaise trishul, nanda devi or nanda kot ityadi ke manoram drishyon ke liye janaa jaataa he. vijaipur main sthit dhaulinag mandir kumaun ke prasiddh naag mandiron main se ek he. beninag, kalinag, feninag, basukinag, pinglanag or harinag anya prasiddh mandir hai.
 dhaulinag mandir vijaipur pahari ke sheersh par sthit he. mandir vijaipur se paidal duuri par he, or mukhya rup se navaratri ke dauraan bhakton dwaara bharaa rahata he. panchami mela yahan kaa ek bahut prasiddh tyohaar he.
 vijaipur main chaay ke bagan british raaj dwaara beesvin sadi main sthapit kiye gayi the. bahut baad main, in baganon kaa vijay laal shah, jo ek gujarati vyapaaree the, ke dwaara adhigrahan kar liya gaya, jinhone baad main khud ke naam par nagar kaa naam rakh diya tha.
 himalayan vyuu vijaipur main ek anya sthal he. vijaipur-kabhata road ke tirahe kii duusari ore sthit is sthal se himalay parvatamalaon ke vihangam drishy dekhe ja sakate hai.
 vijaipur rashtriya rajmarg 309A par bageshwar se 30 kimi or kanda se 5 kimi kii duuri par sthit he. vijaipur tak jeep dwaara paas ke shaharon, jaise kanda, kotmanya yaa uryari mode, se pahunchaa ja saktaa he. iske atirikt bageshwar, almoda, berinag or dilli jaise shaharon tak uttarakhand parivahan nigam or kemu dwaara basen chalaai jaati hai. yah kailash maansarovar ke maarg par sthit he.
