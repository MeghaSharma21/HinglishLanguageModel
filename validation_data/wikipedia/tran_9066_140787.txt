 udanchhu ek bhartiya hindi bollywood film he, jiskaa nirdesan vipin parashar kar rahe hai. is film main mukhya kirdaar main gautam gulati or sisha sahagal hai.
 31 march 2015 ko mumbai main is film ke naam kaa elaan kiya gaya. is par rachanaatmak nirdeshak rabindra ne kaha kii ham is film ke liye bahut utsahit hai. yah avashya hi darshakon ko pasand aayega. iske baad gautam ne kaha kii yah film main bahut adhik manoranjan he jo aapako film se najar hataane nahin degaa, is liye menne is film ko apane pehle bollywood film ke liye chunaa he. iskaa nirmaan 12 april 2015 se dilli main shuru hoga or 15 dinon ke pashchaat pune main baaki kaa nirmaan hoga.
