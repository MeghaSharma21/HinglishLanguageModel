 shupiyaan, jise kabhi-kabhi shopiyaan bhi likhaa jaataa he, bharat ke jammu or kashmir rajya main ek nagar or zila he. yah kshetra apane seb kii paidaavaar ke liye prasiddh he. ise san 2007 main zile kaa darja mila tha. yah pir punjaal parvatamala kii pahadiyon se ghira hua he.
 kashmiri bhashaa main "sheen" kaa arth "barf" hota he. is sthaan ko "sheen van" yani "barf kaa van" kaha jaataa tha or us se vikrut hokar "shupiyaan" bana.
 hirpura rashtriya udyaan isi zile main sthit he. aitihaasik mogul maarg bhi shupiyaan se guzarakar srinagar jaataa he or pir kii gali naamak pahari darre se nikalataa he jo isi zile main he. is darre se 2 kimi dur nandansar naamak ek romantic jhil he. shupiyaan main mogul rajyakaal kii ek jama masjid he.
