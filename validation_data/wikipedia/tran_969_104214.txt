 bhakthi ke kshetra main mahapubhu shrivalbhacharya ji kaa saadhan maarg pushtimaarg kehalaata he. pushtimaarg ke anusaar seva do prakaar se hoti he - naam-seva or swaroop-seva. swaroop-seva bhi teen prakaar kii hoti he -- tanuja, vittaja or maanasi. maanasi seva ke do prakaar hote hai - maryaada-margeey or pushtimargeey. maryaada-margeey maanasi-seva paddati kaa aacharan karne vaala sadhak jahaan apni mamata or ahan ko der karata he, vahaan pushti-margeey maanasi-seva paddati vaala sadhak apane shuddh prem ke dwaara shrikrishna bhakthi main leen ho jaataa he or unke anugrah se sahaj main hi apni vaankshit vastu praapt kar letaa he.
 vallabhacharya dwaara pratipaadit shuddhadvaitvaad darshan ke bhaktimaarg ko pushtimaarg kehete hai. shuddhadvaitvaad ke anusaar brahm maaya se alipt he, isaliye shuddh he. maaya se alipt hone ke kaaran hi yah advait he. yah brahm sagun bhi he or nirgun bhi. saamaanya buddhi ko paraspar virdohi prathit hone vaali baton kaa brahm main sahaj antarbhaav ho jaataa he. vah anu se bhi chota or sumeru se bhi bada he. vah anek hokar bhi ek he. yah brahm swadhin hokar bhi bhakt ke adheen ho jaataa he. vishishtadvait kii tarah shuddhadvaitvaadi ne bhi brahm ke saath saath jagat ko bhi satya bataaya he. karanroop brahm ke satya hone par kaaryrup jagat mithyaa nahin ho saktaa. brahm kii pratikruti hone ke kaaran jagat kii trikalabadh satta he. jeev or jagat kaa naash nahin hota, sirf aavirbhaav or tirbhaav hota he.
 pushtimaarg shuddhadvait ke darshan ko hi bhakthi main dhalta he. pushti kaa shabdic arth he ‘poshan’. shrimadbhagwat main ishwar ke anugrah ko poshan kaha gaya he- “poshann tadnugrahah”. vallabhacharya ke anusaar, “krishnaugraharoopa hi pushtih kaladi baadhak”. arthath kaladi ke prabhaav se mukt karne vaala krishna kaa anugrah hi pushti he. pushtimargi bhakthi kaa muladhar bhagawatkrupa or unke prati purna samarpan he. bhakt ke bhagwaan kii ore dhyaan le jane ke pehle hi bhagwaan bhakt par apni krupa varsha kar detaa he. krishna kii murali dwaara gopiyon par krupa varsha hoti he. krishna kii yah murali anugrah sancharika he. pushtimaarg main bhakt sway ko purnataya bhagwaan ke aasare chhod detaa he.
 pushtimaarg main jeevon ke teen prakaar bataaye jate hai- pravaah jeev, maryaada jeev or pushti jeev. janm maran ke bandhan main bandhe jeev ko ‘pravaah jeev’ kehete hai. jo jeev veda, upanishad aadi ke adhyayan se brahm gyaan praapt karne kii koshish karata he use ‘maryaada jeev’ kehete hai or jeev bhagwaan kii bhakthi or sneh ko apana avlamb banaata he, use ‘pushti jeev’ kehete hai.
 pushti maarg main bhakthi kii bhi teen prakaar kii avadharanaayen hai- pravaah pushti bhakthi, maryaada pushti bhakthi or shuddh pushti bhakthi. ‘pravaah pushti bhakthi’ ke antargat janm maran ke chakra main bandhaa bhakt ishwar kaa samaran karata hua kramik moksh praapt karata he. ‘maryaada pushti bhakthi’ ke antargat bhakt shastron se brahm gyaan praapt karne ke baad bhagwaan kii ore unmukh hota he or moksh kii ore badhata he. ‘shuddh pushti bhakthi’ main bhakt swayan ko purnataya bhagwaan kii sharan main samarpit kar detaa he. yahan bhagwaan apane bachche kii tarah bhakt kaa paalan karte hai. bhagwaan ke charnon main purnataya samarpit bhakt bhagwaan ke sneh kaa bhaajan banataa he. shuddh pushti bhakt kii mukti sabase shuddh, saral or sugam hoti he. bhagwaan kii sharan me aate hi bhakt ke saare dukh dur ho jate hai. pushti margi bhakthi ko maadhurya bhakthi, premabhakti yaa raganuga bhakthi kehete hai. is bhakthi main shringaar ke swaroop kaa aadhyatmik roopaantar hota he. yahan bhagwaan krishna bhakt ke ekmaatr avlamban hote hai, jabaki jeev or gopiyaan aashray. bhakthi ke vikaas ke teen charan hote hai-prem, aasakti or vyasan. vyasan is prem kii charam avasthaa he, jahaan pahunchakar bhakt ko bhagwaan ke sivaa kuch nazar nahin aata.
