 kumaun vishvavidhyalay nainital, uttrakhand main sthit he. vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana 1 march 1973 main upra rajya adhinium ke antargat hui thi. vishvavidhyalay ke do campus he nainital or almoda main or puure kumaun kshetra main 27 mahavidhyalay isse sambhddhikrut hai. bhimtal main ek or campus banaaya ja rahaa he, jo vyavsaayik, takneekee or vyavsaayik shiksha kii maang ko puraa karegaa. utkrisht shikshan or ucch gunvattta shodh ke saath-saath chautarafa vikaas is vidhvavidhyalay kaa dhyey he or mukhya bal kumaun kshetra par he.
