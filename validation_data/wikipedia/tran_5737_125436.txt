 aakhiya khaaras assiria ke raja sinakirib ko paraamarsh denevaala ek pracheen manishi. aakhiya khaaras kii katha i.pus. 5veen shatabdi se bhi adhik puraani he.
 isaki jeevanakatha tathaa sukhtiyaan syria, arab, ithiopia, armenia, romania or turki kii pracheen bhashaaon main uplabdh hai. isane apane bhattije nadaan ko dattak putr ke rup main rakh liya tha. par nadaan ne iskaa vinaash karne kaa praytn kiya, kintu vah bhoomigrih main chhipakar kisi prakaar batch gaya. vah prakat hua tab jab raja ko uske paraamarsh kii aavashyaktaa padi. at: usne apane prabhaav ko pun: praapt kar liya. usne adhar main prasaad kaa nirmaan karke tathaa baaluu kii rassi batakar misr ke samrat ko santusht kiya. iske pashchaat usne nadaan ko samuchit dand diya or uski lagaataar bhartsana kii.
