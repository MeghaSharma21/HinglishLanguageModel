 shri swati thirunal balram verma travnakor ke maharaja the. yogya shaasha hone ke saath-saath ve sangitajna bhi the. unhone bharat kii donon shastreey sangeet shaileyon - hindustani sangeet or karnataka sangeet ko badhaava diya, yadhyapi ve swyan karnatak sangeet ke gyaata the. unhone 400 se adhik sangeet rachanaayen kii. ve malayalam, sanskrit, hindi, marathi, telegu, kannada, bangla, tamil, oriya or angreji sahit kai bhashaaon main dharapravaah bol sakate the.
 shri swati thirunal balram verma ne hi tiranantpuram kii khagoleey vedhashala, sarkari press, trivendram janata pustkaalay, paurvatya pandulipi sangrahalay aadi kaa armbh kiya. maharaja san 1843 se royal aciatic sosayadi ke sammanit sadsy bhi the. 
 maharaja ne dakshin bhartiya bhashaaon ke saath hindi main padya-rachana kii jinaki gananaa aadhunik hindi kii aarmabhik rachanaaon main he.
