 anand kumaar ek bhartiya ganitagya, shikshavid tathaa bahut si rashtriya tathaa antararashtriya ganit kii patrikaon ke stambhakaar hai. unhen prasiddhi super 30 kaarykram ke kaaran mili, jo ki unhone patna, bihar se 2002 main prarambh kiya tha, jiske antargat aarthik rup se pichde chaatro ko iaiti sanyukt pravesh pariksha kii taiyaari karavaaya jaataa he. 2018 ke aankadon ke anusaar, unke dwaara prashikshit 480 main 422 chaatr bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan ke liye chaynit ho chuke hai. discovery channel ne bhi inke kaaryon par laghu film banaai he. unhen sanyukt rajya america ke masocusets praudyogiki santhaan tathaa harvard vishvavidhyalay dwaara unke kaaryon par bolne ke liye nimantran mila.
 anand kumaar kaa janm patna, bihar main hua tha. unke pitaaji bhartiya dak vibhaag main clerk the. unke pitaaji nijee vidyalayon ke adhik kharchon ke kaaran unhen vahaan padhaa na sake. is kaaran anand kumaar ne hindi madhyam ke sarkari vidhyalay main daakhila liya, jahaan unhen ganit se atyadhik lagaav ho gaya. snaatak shiksha ke dauraan unhone sankhya siddhant par kuch paper jamaa kiye, jise mathematical spectrum tathaa da mathematical gazat main prakashit kiya gaya.
 unhone cambridge vishvavidhyalay main daakhila surakshit kar liya magar pitaaji kii mrityu tathaa kharaab aarthik sthiti ke kaaran ve daakhila le na sake. is dauraan ve subah ganit par kaam karte tathaa shaam ko parivaar kii madad hetu maan ke saath papad bechney main haath bataate the. unhone atarikt paise kamaane ke liye bacchon ko ganit padhaana bhi prarambh kiya.
 1992 main unhone ganit padhaanaa armbh kiya. unhone 500 rupe prati maheene par ek kaksha kiraaye par lee tathaa apane santhaan ramanujan skool of mathematics kii sthapana kii. ek hi varsh main do chaatro se badhkar chhattis chaatr ho gayi or tisare saal ke ant tak yah sankhya 500 tak ho gayi. phir 2002 main unhone garib chaatro ke vishesh nivedan par, jo ki iaiti sanyukt pravesh pariksha kii mahangi kochingon kii fees nahin de sakate the, super 30 kaarykram prarambh kiya, jiske kaaran unhen pratishtha mili.
 march 2009 main discovery channel ne super 30 par ek ghanta lambaa kaarykram dikhaaya. isi varsh america samachaarpatr da neuyork times ne aadhe panne par unke bare main ek lekh likhaa. abhinetri v purv miss japan norika fujivara patna aayin tathaa unhone anand kumaar ke kaaryon par ek laghu film banaayi. unhen bibisi ke kaaryakramon main bhi sthaan mila he. unhone apane anubhavon ke bare main bhartiya prabandh santhaan, ahmedabad, kai iaiti, british columbia vishvidhyalay, tokyo vishvavidhyalay, tathaa stenford vishvavidhyalay main bhaashan de chuke hai. unke aarthik rup se kamjor chaatro ko nickshulk shiksha dene ke kaam ke kaaran limca book of world record main bhi unka naam darj hua. time patrika ne super 30 ko best of asia 2010 kii suchi main bhi sthaan diya. inhen 2010 main institute of research and dacumentation in soshal sciences dwaara es ramanujan puraskaar diya gaya.
 super 30 ko purv america raashtrapati baraak obama ke vishesh sipahasaalar raashid hossain ne desh kaa sarvshereshth santhaan kaha. newshwik patrika ne anand kumaar ke kaaryon kaa sangyaan lete hue unke santhaan ko chaar sarvaadhik abhinav santhaan main sthaan diya. unhen navambar 2010 main bihar sarkaar kaa sarvocch puraskaar "moulana abul kalam azaad shiksha puraskaar" mila. 
 unhen germany ke sexoni praant ke shiksha vibhaag dwaara sammanit kiya gaya. anand kumaar ko raashtrapati ram nath kovind dwaara "rashtriya bal kalyaan puraskaar" pradaan kiya gaya.
