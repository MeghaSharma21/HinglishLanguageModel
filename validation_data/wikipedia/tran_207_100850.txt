 krishi bhartiya arthvyavastha kii ridh he. bharat main krishi sindhu ghati sabhyataa ke daur se kii jaati rahi he. 1960 ke baad krishi ke kshetra main harith kranti ke saath nayaa daur aayaa. san 2007 main bhartiya arthvyavastha main krishi evam sambandhit kaaryon kaa sakal gharelu utpaada main hissaa 16.6% tha. us samay sampoorn kaary karne waalon kaa 52% krishi main laga hua tha.
 bharat main krishi main 1960 ke dashak ke madhya tak paaramparik bijon kaa prayog kiya jaataa tha jinaki upaj apekshaakrit kam thi. unhen sinchai kii kam aavashyaktaa padati thi. kisaan urvarkon ke rup main gaay ke gobar aadi kaa prayog karte the.
 1960 ke baad ucch upaj beej kaa prayog shuru hua. isse sinchai or rasinic urvarkon or kitnashkon kaa prayog bad gaya. is krishi main sinchai kii adhik aavashyaktaa padane lagi. iske saath hi gehun or chaaval ke utpaadan main kaaphi vriddhi hui jis kaaran ise harith kranti bhi kaha gaya. 
 bharat main vibhinn varshon main daal-gehun kaa utpaadan -
 bharat main krishi main paramparaagat aujaaron jaise fawda, khurpy, kudaal, hansiya, ballam, ke saath hi aadhunik mashiinon kaa prayog bhi kiya jaataa he. kisaan jutai ke liye tractor, kataai ke liye harvester tathaa ghaai ke liye thresar kaa prayog karte hai.
 2010 eggeo vishwa krishi sankhyikee, ke anusaar bharat ke kai taaja fal or subjia, doodh, pramukh masaale aadi ko sabase bada utpaadak tehraaya gaya he. reshedaar fasale jaise jute, kai stepal jaise bajra or arandi ke tel ke beej aadi kaa bhi utpaadak he. bharat gehun or chaaval kii duniya kaa doosraa sabase bada utpaadak he. bharat, duniya kaa doosraa yaa teesraa sabase bada utpaadak he kai chijo kaa jaise sookhe fal, vastra krishi-aadhaarit kachche maal, jaden or kand fasale, daal, machhliiya, andey, nariyal, ganna or kai subjia. 2010 may bharat ko duniya kaa pochava sthaan hasil hua jiske mutabik usne 80% se adhik kai nakadi fasalo kaa utpadan kiya jaise coffee or kapaas aadi. 2011 ke report ke anusaar, bharat ko duniya main paanchave sthaan par rakhaa gaya jiske mutabik va sabase tej vruddhi ke rup main pashudhan utpaadak karata he.
 2008 ke ek report ne daawa kiya ki bharat kii jansankhya, chaaval or gehun kaa utpaadan karne kii kshamta se adhik teji se bad rahi he. anya sutro se pataa chalata he ki, bharat apni babbti jansankhya ko aram se khilaa saktaa he or saath hi saath chaaval or gehun ko niryaat bhi kar saktaa he. bas, bharat ko apni buniyadi suvidhaon ko badhaana hoga jisase utpaadak bhi bade jaise anya desh brazil or china ne kiya. bharat 2011 main lagbhag 2lakh metric ton gehun or 2.1 karod metric ton chaaval kaa niryaat africa, nepal, bangladesh or duniya bhar ke anya deshon ko kiya.
 jaleey krishi or pakad matsaypalan bharat main sabase teji se badhate udyogon ke bich he. 1990 se 2010 ke bich bhartiya machhlee fasal doguni hui, jabaki jaleey krishi fasal teen guna badha. 2008 main, bharat duniya kaa chatha sabase bada utpaadak tha samudri or mithe pani kii matsay paalan ke kshetra main or doosraa sabase bada jaleey machhlee krishi kaa nirmaataa tha. bharat ne duniya ke sabhi deshon ko kareeb 6,00,000 metric ton machhlee utpaadon kaa niryaat kiya.
 bharat ne pichle 60 varsho main krishi vibhaag main kai safaltaae praapt kii he. ye laabh mukhya rup se bharat ko harith kranti, power janareshan, buniyadi suvidhaon, gyaan main sudhaar aadi se praapt hua. bharat main fasal paidaavaar abhi bhi sirf 30% se 60% hi he. abhi bhi bharat main krishi pramukh utpaadakta or kul utpaadan laabh ke liye kshamta he. vikasshil deshon ke saamne bharat abhi bhi peeche he. iske atirikt, garib avasanrachana or asangathit khudara ke kaaran, bharat ne duniya main sabase zyada khaadya ghate se kuch kaa anubhav kiya or nuksaan bhi bhugatna padaa.
 bharat main sinchai kaa matlab kheti or krishi gatividhiyon ke prayojan ke liye bhartiya nadiyon, talabon, kuon, naharon or anya kritrim pariyojnaon se pani kii aapurti karnaa hota he. bharat jaise desh main, 64% kheti karne kii bhoomi, monsoon par nirbhar hoti he. bharat main sinchai karne kaa aarthik mahatv he - utpaadan main asthirataa ko kam karnaa, krishi utpaadakta kii unnati karnaa, monsoon par nirbharta ko kam karnaa, kheti ke antargat adhik bhoomi laana, kaam karne ke avasaron kaa srijan karnaa, bijli or parivahan kii suvidha ko badhanaa, baadh or sookhe kii rokthaam ko niyantran main karnaa.
 vipanan ke vikaas ke liye nivesh kii aavashyaktaa str, bhandaaran or cold storage buniyadi suvidhaon ko baari hone kaa anumaan he. haal hi main bharat sarkaar ne puri tarah se krishi kaarykram kaa moolyankan karne ke liye kisaan ayog kaa gathan kiya. haalanki siforeeshon kaa keval ek mishrit swaagat kiya gaya he. navambar 2011 main, bharat ne sangathit khudara ke kshetra main pramukh sudhaaron kii ghoshnaa kii. in sudhaaron main rasad or krishi utpaadon kii khudara shaamil hui. yah sudhaar ghoshnaa pramukh raajnneetik vivaad kaa kaaran bhi bana. yah sudhaar yojana, disambar 2011 main bharat sarkaar dwaara hold par rakh diya gaya tha .
