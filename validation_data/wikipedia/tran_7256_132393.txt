 may yaa mayaasur, kashyap or dun kaa putr, namuchi kaa bhai, ek prasiddh daanav. yah jyotish tathaa vaastushastra kaa acharya tha. may ne daityaraj vrishaparvan ke yajna ke avasar par bindusarovar ke nikat ek vilakshan sabhagrih kaa nirmaan kar apane adbhut shilpashaastra ke gyaan kaa parichay diya tha.
 isaki do patniyaan - hema or rambha thim jinase paanch putr tathaa teen kanyaayein huin. jab shankar ne tripuron ko bhasma kar asuron kaa naash kar diya tab mayaasur ne amritkund banaakar sabhi ko jivit kar diya tha kintu vishnu ne uske is prayaas ko vifal kar diya. brahmpurana ke anusaar indra dwaara namuchi kaa vadh hone par isane indra ko parajeet karne ke liye tapasya dwaara anek maaya vidyaayein praapt kar leen. bhaygrast indra brahman vesh banaakar uske paas gaye or chhalpurvak maitri ke liye unhone anurodh kiya tathaa asli rup prakat kar diya. isapar may ne abhaydaan dekar unhen maaya vidyaon kii shiksha di.
 ravan ki patni mandodari mayasur ki putree thi. 
 mahabharat ke anusaar khandav van ko jalaate samay yah us van main sthit takshak ke ghar se bhaaga. krishna ne tatkaal chakra se iskaa vadh karnaa chaha kintu sharnaagat hone par arjuna ne ise bachaa liya. badale main isane yudhisthir ke liye sabhabhavan kaa nirmaan kiya jo maysabha ke naam se prasiddh hua. isi sabha ke vaibhav ko dekhakar duryodhana pandavon se daah karne laga tha. is bhaavana ne mahabharat yuddh ko janm diya.
