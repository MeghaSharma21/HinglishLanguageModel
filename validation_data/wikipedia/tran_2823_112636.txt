 thakur jagmohan singh hindi ke bharatenduyugin kavi, aalochak or upanyaaskaar the. unhone san 1880 se 1882 tak dhamtari main or san 1882 se 1887 tak shivarinarayan main tehseeldaar or magistrate ke rup main kaary kiya. chhattisgarh ke bikhare sahityakaron ko jaganmohan mandal banaakar ek sutara main piroyaa or unhen lekhan kii sahi disha bhi di. jaganmohan mandal kaashi ke bharatendu mandal kii tarj main bani ek saahitiyik sanstha thi.
 hindi ke atirikt sanskrit or angreji sahitya kii unhen acchhee jaankaree thi. thakur sahab mulat: kavi hi the. unhone apni rachanaaon dwaara nai or puraani donon prakaar kii kavypravritiyon kaa poshan kiya.
 thakur jagmohan singh kaa janm vijayraghavgarh riyasat main thakur sarayu singh ke raaj parivaar main main hua tha. 1857 ke pratham swatantrata sangram main thaa. sarayu singh ne bad chadh kar hissaa liya tha. falswaroop angrejon ne unhen giraftaar kar liya or unhen kaale pani kii sajaa sunai gai. lekin angreji hukumat main sajaa bhogane kii bajaay thaa. sarayu singh ne maut ko gale laganaa uchit samajha. jagmohan singh aise hi krantikari, medhaavi evam swapn drushtaa suputr the.
 apni shiksha ke liye kaashi aane par unka parichay bhaaratendu or unki mandali se hua. banaras ke queens kalege main adhyayan ke dauraan ve bhaaratendu harishchandra ke sampark main aae tathaa yah sampark pragadh maitri main badal gaya jo kii jivan paryant bani rahi. 
 1878 main shiksha samaapti ke baad ve vijayraghavgarh aa gaye. do saal pashchaat 1880 main dhamtari main tehseeldaar niyukt kiye gaye. baad main tabadale par shivarinarayan aaye. kaha jaataa he ki shivarinarayan main vivaahit hote hue bhi inhen 'shyama' naam kii stri se prem ho gaya. shivarinarayan main rahete hue inhone shyama ko centre main rakh kar anek rachanaaon kaa srijan kiya jiname hindi kaa atyant bhautik evam durlabh upanyaas shyaam-swapn pramukh he.
 unke teen kavyasangrah prakashit hai : 
 iske atirikt inhone kalidas ke "meghdoot" kaa bada hi lalit anuwaad bhi brajabhasha ke kabitt savaiyon main kiya he. hindi nibandhon ke pratham utthan kaal ke nibandhkaron main unka mahatvapurn sthaan he. 
 style par unke vyaktitv kii anuthi chhap he. vah badi parimarjeet, sanskritgarbhit, kaavyatmak or pravahpurn hoti hai. kahin-kahin panditau style ke chintya prayog bhi mil jate hai. "shyamaswapn" unki pramukh gadhykruti he, jiskaa sampadan kar dow shrikrishnalal ne nagariprachaarini sabha dwaara prakashit karaayaa he. isamen gadhy-padya donon hai, kintu padya kii sankhya gadhy kii apeksha bahut kam he. ise bhavapradhan upanyaas kii sangya di ja sakati he. adyopant style varnanatmak he. isamen charitrachitran par dhyaan na dekar prakruti or premamay jivan kaa hi chitra ankit kiya gaya he. kavi kii shringaari rachanaaon kii bhavbhumi paryaapt saras or hridayasparshi hoti he. kavi main saundarya or suramya roopon ke prati anuraag kii vyapak bhaavana thi. acharya ramchandra shukla kaa isiilie kahana tha ki "pracheen sanskrit sahitya" ke abhyaas or vindhyatvi ke romantic pradesh main nivas ke kaaran vividh bhavamayi prakruti ke rupamadhurya kii jaisi sachhee parakh jaisi sachhee anubhuti inamen thi waisi us kaal ke kisi hindi kavi yaa lekhak main nahin pai jaati . maanviya saundarya ko prakritik saundarya ke sandarbh main dekhne kaa jo prayaas thakur sahab ne chhayavaadi yug ke itan dinon pehle kiya isse unki rachanaayen vastav main "hindi kavy main ek nutan vidhaan" kaa abhas detii hai. unki brajabhasha kaaphi parimarjeet or style kaaphi pusht thi.
