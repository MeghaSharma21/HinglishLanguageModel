 udakgati abhiyantriki civil abhiyantriki kii ek shakha he jisamen dravon ke bahaav or parivahan kaa adhyayan kara jaataa he. isamen svachh jal va mel-vaale gande pani par adhik dhyaan diya jaataa he, haalanki anya drav bhi iskaa bhaag hai. pulon, baandhon, naharon, ityadi kaa nirmaan iske ang hai or iskaa sambandh swasthya va paryaavaran abhiyantriki donon se he.
