 nashi bhashaa, jise lomi, moso or mosu bhi kaha jaataa he, dakshini china main basane vaale nashi logon dwaara boli jane vaali tibeti-burmi bhashaa yaa bhashaaon kaa samooh he. san 2010 main nashi bolne waalon kii aabaadi lagbhag 3 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. tibeti-burmi parivaar main yah lolo-burmi upashakha kii sadsy hai or inhen yi logon kii nosu bhashaa or burmi bhashaa ke qareeb mana jaataa he.
 do prakaar kii nashi bhashaayen paribhaashit kii gai hai. pashchimi nashi bhashaa main kaafii maanakeekaran hua he jabaki purvi nashi main bahut si upbhashaayen hai jinke bolne vaale ek-duusare ko samaj nahin sakate.
 nashi ko uski apni nashi lipi or ek anya geba naamak lipi main likhaa jaataa he. geba ek sachhee varnmaala na ho kar ke ek shabdaansh-maala he jiskaa prayog aksar dharmik mantron ko likhane ke liye kiya jaataa he.
