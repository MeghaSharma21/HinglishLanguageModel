 choti bahu 1971 kii hindi film he. yah film ke bi tilak dwaara nirdeshit he. film main sharmila tagore, rajesh khanna, nirupa roy or ai es johar hai. sangeet kalyanji aanandji dwaara he. film box offis par safal rahi thi. kahani mul rup se bengali upanyaas "bindur chhele" par aadhaarit he, jise prasiddh bengali lekhak sharathchandra chattopadhyaay ne likhaa he. yah film 1969 or 1971 ke bich rajesh khanna kii lagaataar 17 hit filmon main se ek he. isamen do nayak vaali filmon maryaada or andaaz ko unki 15 ekal ekal hit filmon main gina gaya. 
 radha ek amir vyapaaree kii ekmaatr beti he. vah mirgee se peedit he. madhu ek yuva doctor he, jo gaanv main abhyaas karata he. vah apane bade bhai shriram or bhabhi sita ke saath rahata he. ek sthaneey ayogy chikitsak jiskaa kaarobaar madhu ke abhyaas se bighad gaya vah, beemaari ko chhupaakar madhu or radha ke bich rishta karata he. madhu uski samasya jane bina usase shaadi karata he.
 shaadi ke turant baad, madhu or uske parivaar ke sadasyon ko radha kii beemaari ke bare main pataa chalaa or ve use sweekaar karte hai kyonki shaadi pehle hi ho chuki he. yah dekhne ke baad ki radha kaa daura gopi ke sampark se kam ho gaya, sita ne use rakhane ko de diya. radha bahut khush mahasoos karti he or gopi kii dekhbhaal apane bete ke rup main karti he.
 saal biit chuke hai or gopi ab saat saal kii umr main, radha ko apni maa ke rup main sochataa he. jab tak ki madhu or shriram kii bahan paro apane pati or bete ke saath rahane ke liye nahin aati he tab tak halaat thik hote hai. unke bete niku ne apane chanchal ravaiye ke saath gopi ko bhi bigaad diya. baad main, paro ne radha or sita ke bich galatfahami shuru kar di, jo radha kii nirdoshta or sita kii udaseenta ke kaaran bade str par jaati hai. iske alaava, paro gopi ko daraati he ki agar vah apni maa ko dekhtaa he, to vah mar jaaegi. bhaybheet gopi radha se dur rahata he, isse radha kii pidaa badti he. dhire-dhire, vah biimaar padati he or uski haalat gambhir ho jaati he. jabaki gopi ke vyavhaar se har koi pareshaan hota he, niku saavdhaneepurvak uske vyavhaar ke kaaran ko nikaal letaa he. vah sabhi ko bataata he. har koi paro ko daantata he or gopi ko aashwast karata he ki agar vah usase baat karata he to uski maa mar nahin jaaegi. gopi radha ke paas aata he or uski beemaari dur ho jaati he. radha or sita ke bich bhi kadavaahat dur ho jaati he or ve pehle jaise hi khushi se rahanaa shuru karte hai.
