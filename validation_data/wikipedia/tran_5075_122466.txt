 purush evam naagarik adhikaar ghoshnaptr francisi kranti ke sabase mahatvapurn kagajaat main se ek he. is patr main dharm kii swatantraaa, abhivyakti kii swatantraaa, vidhansabha kii swatantraaa or shaktiyon ke vibhaajan ke rup main adhikaar, kii soochi kii vyakhya karte hai. sabhi purushon ko ye adhikaar he. yah sabhi logo ke liye bhi kuch adhikaron ke bare main vyakhya karata he. is patr main kuch prakritik adhikaron ke vichaaron kaa upyog kar likhaa gaya tha, ye adhikaron ke sabhi purushon ke liye rahe hai: ve har samay or sthanon main maanya mana jaataa he. ve maanav swabhaav kaa adhikaar hone ke liye kaha jaataa he. ghoshnaa ke antim vichaar ko neshnal assembly dwaara 26 agast 1789 ko sweekaar kar liya gaya. yah logo ke samvidhan likhane se pehle karne vaali pehali mahatvapurn baat thi. in kagajaat ne na keval purushon ke liye balki pure france ke logo ke liye bina kisi apwaad ke unke buniyadi adhikaron kii vyakhya kii, lekin is adhikaar ne mahilaon kii bhoomika ke bare main kuch nahin kaha. isane daasatv ke bare main bhi kuch nahin kaha.
