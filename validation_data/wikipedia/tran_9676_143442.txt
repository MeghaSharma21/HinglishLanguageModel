 mahabharat chakravarti rajagopalaachari dwaara rachit puranic katha pustak he. iskaa pratham prakaashan 1958 main bhartiya vidya bhawan main hua. yah pustak vedavyas dwaara rachit mahabharat kaa sankshipt angreji punarlekhan he. raaj ji ne unki ramayana or is pustak ko deshseva ke rup main bade kaary ke rup main dekha.
 pustak kii lekhan style pravaah yukt he evam shabd vajandaar hai.
 2001 ke anusaar pustak kii 10 lakh pratiyaan vikray kii ja chuki hai.
