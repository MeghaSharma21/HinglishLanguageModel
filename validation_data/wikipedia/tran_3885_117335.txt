 i dil he mushkil ek bhartiya hindi film he. jiskaa nirdesan karan johar or nirmaan karan johar ke saath saath hero yash johar ne bhi kiya he. isamen aishwarya raay bachchan, ranbir kapur or anoushka sharma mukhya bhoomika main hai. yah film diwaali ke nikat, 28 oktober 2016 ko cinemagharon main pradarshit hui.
 "yah ek paaramparik prem kahani yaa do teen logon ko se pyaar hone vaali kahani nahin he. yah ek bahut gaharaa rishta, dil tuutane or kaise pyaar aapako puraa karata he aadi o dikhaata he. maine apane puure jivan main kabhi is tarah ke film ko nahin banaaya he."
 is film ke bare main karan johar ne navambar 2014 main bataaya tha. is dauraan iske lekhak or nirdeshak karan johar ne bataaya ki ve lagbhag dedh varshon se is film ki kahani par kaary kar rahe hai. is kahani main or 3 se 5 maheene kaary karne kii aavashyaktaa he. haalanki jab ve new york main the, tab unhen iskaa vichaar aayaa tha or keval tees dinon ke bheetar hi yah patakatha likhane main safal rahe. jo isse pehle ve kabhi nahin kar paye the. uske baad jab ve bharat aae to iske mukhya kirdaar hetu teen abhinaytaon se mili or unhen film hetu chun liya.
 karan johar ne anuraag kashyap kii bombai velvet main ranbir kapur or anoushka sharma ke abhinay ko dekh kar unhen is film hetu chun liya. iske baad yah aishwarya raay bachchan ko is film main le liya. lekin isse pehle ke sabhi pichley film kuch kuch hota he, kabhi khushi kabhi gam or kabhi alavida na kahana aadi main bhi ve aishwarya raay bachchan ko lenaa chaahate the. lekin ve unhen lene main asafal rahe the.
 film kaa sangeet preetam nen diya he or geet amitabh bhattacharya nen likhe hai.
