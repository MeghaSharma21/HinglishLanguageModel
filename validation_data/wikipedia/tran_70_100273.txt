 woolvarin ek utparivarti he jo apane jeevatatv kaa prakritik or kritrim donon sudhaar kar saktaa he. uski praathmik utparivarti shakti ek twarit upachaar prakriya he, jo vishisht rup se apane utparivarti chikitsa kaarak ke rup main ullikhit he vah apane sharir ke nasht kshetron kaa nirmaan yaa kshan main kshatigrast karne kii kshamta rakhata he jo ek saadhaaran maanav kii kshamta se pare he. is kritrim sudhaar karne kii prakriya use vepan eks kaarykram ke dauraan hasil hui thi jab uske kankal ko avinashi dhaatu ke antargat pravaahit kiya tha. 
 aisa varnan kiya gaya he ki woolvarin ke thik karne kii gati chot ke pariman par nirbhar karti he. aarambh main yah maamuli ghaavon ke sheeghra upachaar ke rup main chitrit tha, lekin kai varshon main lekhakon ne nirantar isaki kshamta main vruddhi kii he. kai varshon ke baad yah dikhaaya gaya ki ek gambhir ghaav bharne ke maamle main woolvarin kii chikitsa kaarak din yaa ghante main hone lage.anya lekhakon ko kahana he ki lagbhag kisi bhi kshatigrast sharir jiske utakon ko nasht kar diya gaya he woolvarin kaa chikitsa kaarak second ke bheetar sharir ko puri tarah se punerjeevit kar saktaa he.woolvarin ke chikitsa karkon ke sambadh main bahut anokhaa varnan shaamil he ki kisi parmaanu visphot utthan ke centre ke paas kshat-vikshat sharir jo lagbhag kankal ho chukaa tha woolvarin ne upachaar ke baad minuton ke bheetar use punerjeevit kar uske sharir ke komal utak ko thik kar diya.jevirs protocol, main jevier ke dwaara banaai gai eks-main kii shaktiyon or kamajoriyon kii profile kii ek shrinkhalaa main kaha gaya he ki woolvarin ke ghaav bharne kii kshamta "avishwasaneey str" par badi he or use rokane kaa ek hi tarika he ki uske sharir se uskaa sir katkar nikaal den.yadi carbonadium kii bani ek vastu uske sharir ke bheetar daal diya jaae to uske upachaar karne kii shaktiyaan naatakeey tareeke se dhimi ho jaatin hai haalanki ve puri tarah se nahin dabati hai.uske upachaar karne kii kshamta bade naatakeey dhang se uski umr badhane kii prakriya ko bhi dhimi kar detii he. 1800 ke ant main paidaa hone ke baavjood uski shaareerik upasthiti or jivan shakti ek aadmi ke yuva avasthaa ke samaan he. haalanki uski sab yaaden uske paas he, uski chikitsa kii kshamtaain use maansik aghat se ubarne main varddhit rog nivritti pradaan karte hai jab vah gambhir sankat ke anubhavon se gujarata he.
 woolvarin kii chikitsa karne kii shakti shaareerik takalifon se to ubaarti hin hai saath hi saath asaadhaaran rog, vishakt dawaaon or padaarthon ke liye pratirohak kaa bhi kaam karti he. haalanki, vah abhi bhi aise padaarthon se tatkaal prabhaav grast ho jaataa he, adhik maatra main sharaabi peya padaarth ke seven ke baad use nashe main dhutt ho jate dikhaaya gaya he or kai maukon par drugs or vish ke kaaran vah aksham ho chukaa he.rog-nidaan karne kii kshamta uske sharir ke ghaavon ko bhar detii he, parantu ghaylavastha main use jo dard hota he, uskaa daman nahin kar paati.
 woolvarin ke utparivartan main he ki uske sharir kaa roopantaran jaanwaron sa he bilkul kutton kii tarah, ek baanh kii kalai main sthit kabja karne yogya teen panje shaamil he. jabaki mul rup se aisa chitrit he ki bionic pratyaropan vepan-eks program ke dwaara banaai gai he, baad main pataa chalata he ki panja uske sharir kaa ek swaabhaavik hissaa he.panje keratin ke bane hue nahin the jaise ki ve jaanwaron ke samrajya main hote hai, par atyadhik saghan haddiyaan jisamen bahut majboot padaarth jaise dhaatu, lakdi or pathar ke kuch kismon ko bhi kaatane kii kshamta thi.woolvarin ke haathon main panje hameshaa nahin dikhte the: jab bhi use apane panjo kii jarurat mahasoos hoti ve uske haathon ke maance ko katkar baahar kii taraf nikalate the or or har baar aisa karte samay use teevr dard kaa ehsaas hota tha.
