 internet sambandhi paton kii sanrachana main, privet network ek aisa network he jo refsi 1918 or refsi 4193 dwaara nirdhaarit maanakon kaa anusaran karte hue privet ypi adress spes kaa istemaal karata he. jab vaishvik maarg se bheje jane yogya paton kii anivaaryta nahin hoti he yaa phir ye apekshit network anuprayogon ke liye uplabdh nahin hote hai to in paton kaa istemaal aam taur par ghar, karyaalay or udhyam ke local aria networkon ke liye kiya jaataa he. privet ypi paton ke sthanon ko mul rup se ipivi4 paton ke shunyikaran ko vilambit karne ke ek prayaas main paribhaashit kiya gaya tha lekin inhen agali peedi ke internet protocol, ipivi6 main bhi dekha ja saktaa he.
 in paton ko privet ke rup main isliye paribhaashit kiya jaataa he kyonki inhen vaishvik str par nahin diya gaya he, matlab yah ki inhen kisi vishisht sanghathan ko aavantit nahin kiya jaataa he or inke dwaara sambodhit ypi packeton ko saarvajanik internet par prasaarit nahin kiya jaataa he. in paton kaa istemaal koi bhi riginal internet registry se sweekruti ke bagaiyr kar saktaa he. agar is tarah ke privet network ko internet se jodne kii jarurat hoti he to iske liye yaa to ek network pataa anuvadak getwe yaa phir ek proxi sarwar kaa istemaal karnaa anivaary he.
 in paton kaa sabase saamaanya upyog awasaiya networkon main hota he, chunki zyaadaatar internet seva pradaata prayek awasaiya graahak ko keval ekmaatr maarg se bheje jane yogya ypi pataa aavantit karte hai, lekin zyaadaatar gharon main ek se adhik network upkaran hote hai, udhaaharan ke liye, kai computer or ek video game consol. is sthiti main ek eneti getwe kaa istemaal aam taur par kai upyogkartaaon ke liye internet sampark ko sakasham banane main kiya jaataa he. 
 nijee paton ko saamanytah corporate networkon main bhi istemaal kiya jaataa he jo suraksha kaarnon se internet se sidhe taur par jude nahin hote hai. network ke aantarik upyogkartaaon ko internet kii simit pahunch pradaan karne ke liye aksar ek proxi, socks getwe yaa isi tarah ke anya upkarnon kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he. 
 donon hi maamalon main nijee paton ko aksar aantarik network ke liye behatar suraksha ke rup main dekha jaataa he kyonki internet host ke liye sidhe taur par ek aatanrik pranali se sambandh jodna mushkil hota he.
 kyonki kai nijee network ek samaan ypi pate kaa istemaal karte hai, is tarah ke networkon ko ek saath milaane main ek aam samasya paidaa hoti he, paton ke sthanon kaa takaraav yani kai upkarnon par paton kii nakal kaa taiyaar ho janaa. is maamle main network ko anivaary rup se dubaara number dena chahie jo aksar ek samay lene vaala kaary he, yaa nakal kiye gaye paton ko chupaane ke liye networkon ke bich ek eneti router ko rakhaa janaa chahie.
 nijee paton ke sthanon main banane vaale packeton kaa internet par li hona asaamaanya nahin he. kharaab tareeke se configar kiye gaye nijee network in paton ke liye aksar vipareet dnes main dekhne kii koshish karte hai jisase internet root nemsarvaron ke liye atirikt trephic kii samasya paidaa ho jaati he. eyes112 project ne nijee paton ke liye vishesh blueole anicast nemsarvaron kii vyavastha kar is bhaar ko kam karne kii koshish kii thi jo in sawaalon ke uttaraswaroop keval nakaaratmak parinaam code hi pradaan karte hai. 
 sanghathanaatmak kshetra ke routeron ko aam taur par in networkon ke liye ypi trephic ke aagaman ko kam karne ke liye taiyaar kiya gaya tha jo yaa to achaanak ho saktaa tha yaa phir ek nakli strot pate kaa istemaal kar mailicious trephic ke jariye aisa kiya ja saktaa tha. aiespi age router graahakon ke paas se is tarah ke trephic ke nirgaman ko band kar dete hai jo graahak ke network par un trutipurna tareekon se banaae gaye yaa mailicious host ke internet par prabhaav ko kam kar detaa he, lekin aisa kaaphi kam hota he.

 classphul adressing ab aprachlit he or 1993 main shuru kiye gaye classles inter-domen rooting ke lagu hone ke baad se internet main iskaa prayog nahin kiya gaya he. udhaaharan ke liye jabaki 10.0.0.0/8 ek single class e network tha, sansthaanon main saamanytah ise chote /16 yaa /24 networkon main vibhajit kar diya jaataa tha.
 is tarah ke networkon ke liye nijee networkon or vishesh paton ke aarakshan kii avadharana ko protocol internet ipivi6 kii agali peedi main sthanantarit kiya gaya he.
 adress block fc00::/7 ko refsi 4193 main varnit ke anusaar ieene dwaara arakshit kar diya gaya he. in paton ko unic local adresses kaha jaataa he. inke charitra ko unicast ke rup main paribhaashit kiya jaataa he or do privet networkon ko aapas main jodne par hone vaale takkar ko rokane ke liye rooting prifics main ek 40-bit random number maujuud rahata he. upyog main swaabhaavik rup se local hone ke baavjood unic local adresses kaa ipivi6 adress scope global hota he. pate, section "ipivi6 adress scops".)
 ek purv maanak ne fec0::/10 range main tathakthith "site-local" paton ke upyog kaa prastaav kiya tha lekin maapniyata kii pramukh chintaaon or site kii ek kharaab paribhaasha ke kaaran refsi 3879 dwaara sitambar 2004 se iske istemaal ko anuchit qaraar diya gaya he.
 ek anya prakaar kii nijee netwarking refsi 5735 or refsi 3927 main samhitabaddh local-link adress range kaa upyog karti he. in paton kii upyogita network upkarnon dwaara swayan-swachaalit concigareshan main he jab dynamik host concigareshan protocol sevaayen uplabdh nahin hoti hai or ek network vyavasthaapak dwaara minial concigareshan vaanchhneeya nahin hota he.
 ipivi4 main block 169.254/16 is uddeshy ke liye arakshit he jahaan is range main pehla or antim /24 sabanet apwaadon ke rup main he. agar iiqui 802 network diechasipi ke madhyam se koi network pataa praapt nahin kar paata he to 169.254.1.0 se lekar 169.254.254.255 tak ek pataa sudorandem tareeke se aavantit kiya ja saktaa he. maanak yah nirdeshit karata he ki paton ke takaraav ko kushalta purvak niyantrit kiya janaa chahie. 
 ipivi6 adressing architekchar ypi pate ke swachaalit concigareshan ke liye fe80::/10 block ko kinare vyavasthit kar detaa he.
 aitihaasik drushti se bhavishya ke anya sambhavit upyogon ke liye nijee paton kii range kii tulana main anya adress blox ko arakshit kiya gaya he. kuch sansthaanon ne bhavishya main paton ke sambhavit takaraav kii aadhikarik chetavaniyon ke baavjood inhen nijee netwarking anuprayogon ke liye istemaal kiya he.
