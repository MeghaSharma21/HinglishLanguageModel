 balatrix, jiskaa bair naam "gamma oryonis" he, kalpurush taramandal kaa teesraa sab se roshan tara he. yah prithvi se dikhne vaale taron main se 27vaa sab se roshan tara bhi he. yah ek parivarti tara he or isaki chamak 1.59 se 1.64 magnitude ke bich badalati rahati he. yah prithvi se 245 prakaash varsh kii duuri par he.
 balatrix ko angrezi main Bellatrix likhaa jaataa he, jo latini bhashaa se aayaa he or jiskaa arth he "stri yoddha" .
 balatrix ek B2 III shrenee kaa nila mahadaanav tara he jo hamaare suraj ke dravyaman se 8 yaa 9 guna dravyaman he. isaki andruni chamak hamaare suraj kii chamak kii 4,000 guna he.
