 torman bharat varsh par akraman karne vaale hunon kaa netaa tha jisane 500i ke lagbhag malwa par adhikaar kiya tha. mihirkul torman kaa hi putr tha, jisane hun samrajya kaa vistarafghanistan tak kiya.torman ne kai vijay abhiyaan kiye the, ek bade vistrut bhu-bhaag par apana samrajya sthapit kiya tha.apni vijayon ke baad usne 'maharajaadhiraj' kii oopaadhi dhaaran kii thi.bharat ke kaafii bade kshetrafal par usne apni vijay pataakaaye fahraai thim.uskaa prabhutv sambhavat: madhya pradesh, namak kii pahadiyon tathaa madhya bharat tak vyaapt tha.bahut badi sankhya main torman ke chaandi ke sikke baraamad hue hai.torman kaa suprasiddh putr mihirkul athwa 'mihirgul' lagbhag 502 i. main uskaa uttaradhikari bana tha.
