 yask vaidic sangyaaon ke ek prasiddh vyutpatikaar evam vaiyaakaran the. inkaa samay 5 se 6 veen sadi isa purv tha. inhen niruktkaar kaha gaya he. nirukt ko teesraa vedang mana jaataa he. yask ne pehle 'nighantu' naamak vaidic shabdkosh ko taiyaar kiya. nirukt usee kaa visheshan he. nighantu or nirukt kii vishay samanta ko dekhte hue sayanacharya ne apane 'rugveda bhashya' main nighantu ko hi nirukt mana he. 'vyakaran shaastra' main nirukt kaa bahut mahatv he.
 yask nirukt ke lekhak hai, jo shabd vyutpatti ,shabd vargeekaran va shabdaarth vigyaan par ek takneekee prabandh he. yask ko shakatayan kaa uttaradhikari mana jaataa he, jo vedon ke vyaakhyankarta the ; unka ullekh yask kii rachanaaon main miltaa he.nirukt main yah samajhane kaa prayaas kiya gaya he ki kuch vishesh shabdon ko unka arth kaise mila visheshkar vedon main diye gayi shabdon ko. ye dhaatuon, pratyayon va asaamaanya shabd sangrahon se shabdon ko banane ke niyam tantra se yukt he.
 nirukt ke teen kand hai- naghantuk, negam or daivat. isamen parishisht sahit kul chaudaa adhyay hai. yask ne shabdon ko dhaatuj mana he or dhaatuon se vyutpatti karke unka arth nikaala he. yask ne veda ko brahm kaha he or use itihaas richaon or gaathaon kaa samucchy mana he.
 yask ne shabdon ke chaar virgon kaa varnan kiya he:
 1. naam = sangya yaa mul rup.
 2. aakhyaat = kriya
 3.upsarg
 4. nipaat
 yask ne satv vidya se sambandhit do virgon ko ek kiya : kriya yaa kaary, tatv yaa jeev . iske baad sarvapratham inhone kriya kaa varnan kiya jisamen bhav: prabal hota he jabaki duusari ore satv: prabal hota he.
