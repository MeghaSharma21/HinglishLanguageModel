 arsh malaciyaani  janm naam: bal mukand, urdu ke ek prakhyat bhartiya shaayar or lekhak the. ve urdu or farsi ke vidwaan tathaa urdu shaayar labhu ram josh malciana ke putr the. varsh 1948 se 1968 ke sevanivruti tak unhone bharat sarkaar ke prakaashan vibhaag main pehle "aaj kal" urdu patrika ke sahaayak sampadak or uske baad 1954 main josh malihabaadi ke baad sampadak kaa daayitv nibhaayaa.
 unki kavita ke chaar sangrah kunda rang, chang o ahang, sharaar e sang or ahang e hijaaz prakashit hue hai. moulana abul kalam azaad par unki aatmakatha kii pustak 1976 main prakashit hui.
