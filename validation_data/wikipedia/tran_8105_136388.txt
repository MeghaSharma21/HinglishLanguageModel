 tigrinya purvi africa main uttari ithiopia ke tigray pradesh or padosi desh iritriya ke dakshini bhaag main boli jane vaali ek sami bhashaa he. dhyaan den ki yah ek anya bhashaa, tigrey, se milti-julati lekin bhinn he. in dono bhashaaon ko is kshetra main basane vaale tigray-tigrinya samudaay ke log bolte hai. tigrinya ke 65% maatrubhaashi ithiopia main or 35% iritriya main based hue hai.
