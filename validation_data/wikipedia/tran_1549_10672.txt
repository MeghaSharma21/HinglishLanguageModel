 sonbhadra bhartiya rajya uttar pradesh kaa ek jila he. jile kaa mukhyalay rabartsganj he.
 sonbhadra jila, mul mirzapur jile se 4 march 1989 ko alag kiya gaya tha. 6,788 varg kimi kshetrafal ke saath yah uttar pradesh kaa dusraa sabase bada jila he. yah 23.52 tathaa 25.32 ansh uttari akshansh tathaa 82.72 evam 93.33 ansh purvi deshaantar ke bich sthit he. jile kii seemaa pashchim main madhya pradesh, dakshin main chhattisgarh, purv main jharkhand tathaa bihar evam uttar main uttar pradesh kaa mirzapur jila he. rarbatsganj jile kaa pramukh nagar tathaa jila mukhyalay he. jile kii jansankhya 14,63,519 he tathaa iskaa jansankhya ghanatv uttar pradesh main sabase kam 198 vyakti prati varg kimi he.
 son nadi jile main pashchim se purv kii ore bahati he. isaki sahaayak nadi rihand jo chhattisgarh evam madhya pradesh ke pathaar se nikalati he son main jile ke kendra main mil jaati he. rihand nadi par bana govendi vallabh pant sagar aanshik rup se jile main tathaa aanshik rup se madhya pradesh main aata he.
 jile main do bhugolic kshetra hai jinamein se kshetrafal main har ek lagbhag 50 pratishat he. pehla pathaar he jo vindhyas pahadiyon se camoor pahadiyon tak hote hue son nadi tak failaa hua he. yah kshetra ganga ghati se 400 se 1,100 feet ooncha he. doosraa bhaag son nadi ke dakshin main son ghati he jisamen singrauli tathaa dudhdi aate hai. yah apane prakritik sansadhano evam upjau bhoomi ke kaaran vikhyath hai.
 swatantraaa milane ke lagbhag 10 varshon tak yah kshetra alag-thalag tha tathaa yahaan yatayaat yaa sanchaar ke koi saadhan nahin the. pahadiyon main chuunaa pathar tathaa koyala milane ke saath tathaa kshetra main pani kii bahutayat hone ke kaaran yah audyogic swarg ban gaya. yahaan par desh kii sabase badi cement factriyaan, bijli ghar, aluminium evam rasinic ikaiyaan sthit hai. saath hi kai sari sahaayak ikaiyaan evam asangathit utpaadan kendra, vishesh rup se stone krashar ikaiyaan, bhi sthapit hui hai.
