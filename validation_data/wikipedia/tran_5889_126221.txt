 2073 jeshth 15 vikram samvath ke diwas sanyukt rajya america ke sinsinati chidyaghar main harambe naamak gorilla kii goli maar kar nirmam hatya kar di gai.
 nirdayee maanvon ne 17 varshiya harambe ko ek meter oonchi diwaar vaale baade main bandi bana kar rakhaa tha. abhibhavakon kii laaparvaahee ke chalate ek chaar saal kaa maanav balak is diwaar ko paar kar harambe nivas ja pahunchaa. shri harambe ne balak ko koi haani nahin pahunchaai. ulta pratyakshdarshiyon ke anusaar shri harambe ne balak kii raksha karne kaa prayaas kiya. parantu chidyaghar prashasan ne balak kii salaamati kaa bahaanaa banaate hue shri harambe ko goli maar di. kuch hi samay baad shri swarg sidhaar gaye. marane se purv shri harambe ne balak kii raksha karne hetu use nikat ghaseet kar apane pairon ke bich rakh liya tha. jab ve goli se ghayal hue, to ve balak par gir gaye. is kaaran balak ko bhi gambhir chot pahaunchi, or use chikitsaalay main bhartee karavaaya gaya.
 chidyaghar ke aagantuko dwaara liya gaya harambe vadh kaa video saamaajik media par chaa gaya. is krur hatya ko dekhakar pathar dil logon kaa hriday bhi pigal gaya. nyaay kii maang karte hue pashu premiyon ne chidyaghar ke baahar pradarshan kiya. 200,000 logon ne harambe vadh kaa virodh karte hue ek yachika daayar kii. sampurna vishwa se hazaron logon ne samvednaa vyakt karte hue chidyaghar ko sandesh bheje. sanyukt rajya raashtrapati chunaav, 2016 ke chunaav main kai logon ne shri harambe ko apana mat dekar shradhanjali arpit kii.
