 yami gautam hindi filmon evam dharawahikon kii ek abhinetri hai.
 yami ke pita mukesh gautam zee news channel main sanyojak hai. yami pehle ek ai e es adhikari bananaa chaahati thim kyonki vah ek bahut acchhee vidhyaarthee thim. vah mul rup se himachal pradesh se hai. unhone doordarshan karier kii shuruyaat chaand ke paar chalo dharavahik se kii par vah colors channel par aane vaale ye pyaar na hoga kam dharawahik se prasiddh huin. unako aakhri baar doordarshan par ye pyaar na hoga kam dharawahik main hi dekha gaya, uske pashchaat vah filmon kii ore bad gayi. 
 unhone kannada bhashaa kii film ullasa utsaaha main mukhya kirdaar kaa paatr nibhaayaa. unhone en di tii ve imagin chaiyn ke vaastavik kaarykram mithi churi number van main bhi kaam kiya.
 2011 main unhone telegu cinema main pravesh kiya or ravi babu ke saath film nuvvilla main kaam kiya. unka bollywood main pravesh viki donor film se hua. unhone ek bengali ladki kaa kirdaar bahut khubi se nibhaayaa jo punjabi ladke viki se pyaar karti he.
