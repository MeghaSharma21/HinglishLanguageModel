 'peeth dard peeth main honevaala vah dard he, jo aam taur par mansapeshiyon, tantrika, haddiyon, jodon yaa ridh kii anya sanrachanaon main mahasoos kiya jaataa he.' 
 is dard ko aksar gardan dard, peeth ke upari hisse ke dard,peeth ke nichle hisse ke dard yaa telobone ke dard main vibhajit kar sakate hai. yah achaanak honevaala dard yaa sthai dard bhi ho saktaa he; yah lagaataar yaa kuch interval par bhi ho saktaa he, yah dard kisi ek hi jagah par ho saktaa he yaa anya hisson main fail bhi saktaa he yah ek halkaa yaa tej dard ho saktaa he yaa isamen chhedne yaa jalan kii anubhuti ho sakati he. yah dard bhujaa or haath main, peeth ke upari yaa nichle hisse main fail saktaa he, or dard ke alaava isamen kamajori, sunn ho janaa yaa jhunjhuni jaise lakshan bhi shaamil ho sakate hai.
 peeth kaa dard logon ko aksar hone vaali shikayaton main se ek he. america main peeth ke nichle bhaag main tej dard chikitsak ke paas jane ke sabase aam kaarnon main paanchaven sthaan par he. das main se nau vayaskon ko apane jivan ke kisi na kisi bindu par peeth dard kaa anubhav hota he or kaam karne vaale das main se paanch vayaskon ko har saal peeth dard hota he.
 ridh nason, jodon, mansapeshiyon, shiraaon or asthivandhon kaa ek paraspar sambandhit jatil antarjaal he or ye sabhi dard ke utpaadan main sakasham hai. badi tantrikaayen jo ridh kii haddi se aarambh hokar pair or haath main jaati hai, ve dard ko adhiktam seemaa tak failaa sakati hai.
 sharir rachana kii drushti se peeth dard ko: gardan ke dard, peeth ke upree hisse ke dard, peeth ke nichle hisse ke dard yaa telobone ke dard main vibhajit kiya ja saktaa he
 isaki avadhi kii drushti se: acute, , sthaayee .
 iske kaaran dwaara: emesake, sankramak, cancer aadi
 peeth dard kisi gambhir chikitsakeey samasya kaa sanket ho saktaa he, haalanki yah aksar antarnihit kaaran nahin hota:
 peeth dard main aamtaur par tatkaal chikitsa hastkshep kii aavashyaktaa nahin hoti he. peeth dard ke adhikansh prasang swayan-simit arthath khud kam ho janevaale or na badhanevaale hote hai. adhiktar peeth dard ke lakshan, visheshkar acute arthath teevr charan main sujan, kii vajah se hote hai, jo saadhaaran taur par do saptaah se teen maheenon tak rahata he.
 kuch avlokanatmak adhyayana kaa suzav he ki aksar peeth dard ke liye jimmedaar tehrai janevaali do sthitiyaan lumber discs herniation or apakshayi discs rog, peeth dard vaale logon main saamaanya aabaadi kii apeksha adhik vyaapt nahin hai or is dard kaa kaaran banane vaali prakriya ke bare main bhi jaankaree nahin he. anya adhyayana kaa suzav he ki 85% maamalon ke liye koi shaareerik kaaran nahin dikhaaya ja saktaa he.
 kuch adhyayanon se sanket miltaa he ki eks-re evam anya chikitsakeey scanon main pataa chalanevaali sanrachanaatmak asaamanyataon kii apeksha naukari par tanaav or bigade hue paariwarik rishton ko peeth dard se adhik nikatataa ke saath paraspar sambandhit kiya ja saktaa he.
 peeth dard ke kai sambhavit strot or kaaran hai. haalanki, dard ke kaaran ke rup main ridh kii vishisht utakon kaa nidaan karne main samasya utpann hoti he. aisa isliye he, kyonki ridh kii haddi ke utakon se utpann hone vaale lakshanon main kaaphi saamanta prathit hoti he or sthaneey chetana shunya karanevaali aushadhi jaise antar naidanik hastkshep prakriyaaon ke prayog ke bagaiyr inamen antar karnaa bahut mushkil he.
 peeth dard kaa ek sambhavit strot peeth kaa scalettel masal he. mansapeshiyon ke uttakon main dard ke sambhavit kaarnon main mansapeshi tanaav mansapeshi akadan, or mansapeshi asantulan shaamil hai. haalanki, imaging adhyayan peeth dard ke kai maamalon main mansapeshi utak ke nuksaan kii dharna kaa samarthan nahin karte hai or mansapeshiyon kii anthan or mansapeshi asantulan kii neurophysiology yaa tantrikatantr shareeravigyaan ko bhi thik tarah se samajha nahin ja sakaa he.
 peeth ke nichle hisse main dard kaa ek anya sambhavit strot ridh ke snovial jod hai. inhen peeth ke nichle hisse main sthaayee dard vaale logon ke lagbhag ek tihaai main or gardan kii moch ke baad gardan main dard vaale adhikansh logon main dard ke praathmik strot ke rup main chihnit kiya gaya he. haalanki, jigapophygial jodon ke dard ke kaaran ko puri tarah samajha nahin ja sakaa he. gardan main moch ke baad gardan main dard honevaale logon main capsule utakon ke nuksaan ko kaaran ke rup main prastaavit kiya gaya he. jigapophygial jodon se aarambh honevaale ridh ke dard vaale logon ke bare main, ek siddhant yah he ki unki shlesh jhilliyon ke invegineshans or fibro-adepos mesicuyds jaise antar jod utak visthapit, sookhe yaa phase hue ho sakate hai or falswaroop nosicepshan ko badhaate hai.
 peeth dard ke kai anya aam sambhavit strot or kaaran hai: jinamein spinal discs herniation or apakshayi discs rog yaa isthemic spondilolisthiesis,osteoarthritis yaa ridh kii haddi main stenasis, aghat, cancer, sankraman, asthi bhang tathaa sujan kii bimaariyaan shaamil hai.
 radicular dard 'non-spacific' arthath gaiyr-vishisht peeth ke dard ke rup main chihnit he or invesive nidaan pareekshanon ke bagaiyr iskaa nidaan kiya ja saktaa he.
 non-discopeenic peeth dard par nae taur par dhyaan kendrit kiya gaya he, jisamen rogiyon kaa saamaanya yaa lagbhag saamaanya emarai or siti scan kiya jaataa he. jin rogiyon main koi radiografic asaamanyataayen nahin hotin, ek nayaa parikshan unamem dorsal remas kii bhoomika kii jaanch karata he. posterior remi syndrome dekhen. 
 peeth dard kaa ilaaj karte samay prabandhan kaa lakshya dard kii tivrata main jitani jaldi sambhav ho adhik se adhik kami praapt karnaa; rogi main apni rosemara kii gatividhiyon main kaary karne kii kshamta ko behaal karnaa, avshisht dard kaa saamana karne main rogi kii madad tathaa kanoonee or saamaajik-aarthik baadhaon ke madhya se rogi ke raste ko aarogy laabh ke liye sugam bananaa he. adhikansh ke liye lakshya punarvaas hetu dard ko ek sanchaalaneey str par rakhana he, jo baad main lambey samay tak dard se raahat kii or agrasar kare. iskaa lakshya gaiyr shalya-chikitsa upachaar ke upyog dwaara dard kaa prabandhan or badi shalya-chikitsa se bachaav he, jabaki dusron ke liye shalya-chikitsa behatar mahasoos karne kaa shighratham rasta ho sakati he.
 sabhi upachaar sabhi sthitiyon main yaa ek hi sthiti ke sabhi vyaktiyon ke liye kaam nahin karte or anek log pate hai ki apane liye sarvottam upachaar nirdhaarit karne main unhen kai upachaar vikalpon ko aajamaane kii jarurat he. sthiti kaa vartmaan charan bhi upachaar ke chunaav kaa nirdharankarak ghatak ho saktaa he. peeth dard ke rogiyon main keval ek alpsankhyak varg ko surgery kii aavashyaktaa hoti he.
 kabhi-kabhi shalyachikitsa in rogon ke marijon ke liye upyukt ho sakati he:
 aksar peeth dard ke kai lakshanon or kaarnon ke liye nunatam atikrami shalya prakriyaayein ek samaadhaan ban rahi hai. is tarah kii prakriyaayein ridh kii haddi kii paaramparik shalyakriya kii apeksha adhik sateek nidaan or kam samay main swasthya laabh jaisaddhik laabh pradaan karti hai.
 garbhavastha ke dauraan lagbhag 50% mahilaon ko peeth ke nichle hisse main dard hota he.garbhavastha main peeth dard, kaaphi tej or gambhir dard tathaa viklaangata kaa kaaran ban saktaa he evam agali garbhavastha main rogiyon ke peeth dard kaa kaaran ho saktaa he. garbhavastha ke saath peeth dard kaa mata ke badhate vajan, vyaayaam, kaam se santushti yaa garbhavastha ke parinaam karkon jaise janm ke samay vajan, janm ke samay lambaai or apgar score ke sambandh main koi ullekhneey varddhit khatra nahin paaya gaya he. 
 garbhavastha main peeth ke nichle hisse ke dard ke saath samvaddh darshaae janevaale garbhavastha ke biomecanical ghatakon main udar kaa madhyatlya or qamar kaa anuprastha vyas tathaa gaharaai shaamil hai. khadaa hona, baithna, aage jhukna, uthaanaa or tahalnaa garbhavastha main peeth dard ko badhaanevaale mul karkon main he. garbhavastha main honevaale peeth dard ko, jaangh or nitambon main failata dard, raat ke samay marij ko jagaane laayak tej dard, raat ke samay badhanevaale dard yaa din ke samay badhanevaale dard ke rup main chihnit kiya ja saktaa he. ucch prabhaav, vajan-vaahak gatividhiyon or vishesh rup se ve, jo samvaddh sanrachanaon ke visham bhaar main shaamil hon: jaise atyadhik ghumaav or uthaanaa tathaa ek-pair par sthit mudraayen, seedhi chadhana or peeth yaa kuulhe ke antim chor ke paas kii doharaawadaar gati kaa parihaar dard ko kam kar saktaa hai. ghutane mode bina jameen par sidhe jhukna garbhavastha main peeth ke nichle hisse par gambhir prabhaav kaa kaaran banataa he or saamaanya vyaktiyon main yah vishesh rup se qamar ke paas ke bhaag main tanaav kaa kaaran banataa he jo tanaav ko kai guna badha saktaa he.
 rashtriya sarkaron dwaara kamgaaron ke aswasthta kii vajah se chuttee lene ke kaaran utpaadakta ke nuksaan ke rup main peeth dard kaa prabhaav niyamit rup se uddhrut hota he. kuch rashtriya sarkaron, vishesh rup se australia or united kingdom, ne samasya se nipatane main madad karne ke liye saarvajanik swasthya jaagrukta kaa abhiyaan shuru kiya he, udhaaharan ke liye swasthya or suraksha ke akjicutive kaa better baks abhiyaan. sanyukt rajya america main peeth ke nichle hisse ke dard ke aarthik prabhaav se pataa chalata he ki yah 45 varsh se kam umr ke vyaktiyon main apni gatividhiyon ko simit karne kaa pehla kaaran he, chikitsakon ke karyaalayon main dekha jane vaala doosraa sabase bada kaaran, aspataal main bhartee hone kaa paanchvaan pramukh aam kaaran evam shalya kriya kaa teesraa pramukh kaaran he.
 udyog or rashtriya swasthya sansthaanon donon ke dwaara pryojit kai chikitsakeey pareekshan ho rahe hai. rashtriya swasthya sansthaanon dwaara pryojit peeth dard se sambandhit naidanik pareekshanon ko dekha ja saktaa he NIH naidanik peeth dard pareekshan.
 dard vyaktiparak he or tatasth bhav se iskaa pareekshan asambhav he. koi naidanik pareekshan aisa nahin he jise tatasth rup se satyapit kiya ja saktaa he. naidanik pareekshan rogi kii report yaa dard kii gambhirtha kaa 1 se lekar 10 tak ke paimaane par upyog karata he. kabhi-kabhi or vishesh rup se bacchon ke saath imotican kii ek shrinkhalaa ko marij ke saamne prastut karte hai or use kisi ek imotican ko sanketit karne ke liye kaha jaataa he. haalanki kuch naidanik pareekshanon ke utpaadon ke liye niamak manjuuri milane main safalataa mil jaati he lekin yah is baat kaa saboot nahin he ki vah upachaar adhik prabhavi he yaa usamen koi laabh bhi he. sabhi pareekshanon main rogi kii dharna par vishwaas kiya jaataa he. doctor yah satyapit nahin kar sakate ki 5 kaa score 1 yaa 10 se adhik upyukt he or na hi yah nirdhaarit kar sakate hai ki ek marij ke 5 ke darje kii kisi anya marij ke 5 se tulana kii ja sakati he.
 2008 ke ek aniyamit niyantrit pareekshan main alexander takanic ke saath sambandhit peeth dard main ullekhneey sudhaar paaya gaya he. vyaayaam or anti ke 6 abhyaas ke ek sangyojan ne peeth dard ko 72% yaa 24 anti abhyaas ke baraabar kam kiya he. un 24 abhyaas praapt karanevaalon ko peeth dard 21 din ke niyantrit ausat se 18 din kam rahaa.
