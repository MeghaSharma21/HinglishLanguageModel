 yah galleri dhan ke apvyiya ko darshaati he. rana sajj na singh ne 1877 i. main englandak ke epa and si oslar and. company se kaanch ke in saamaanon kii kharidari kii thi. in saamaanon main kaanch kii kursi, bed, sopha, dinar set aadi shaamil tha. baad ke shaskaon ne in saamaanon ko surakshit rakhaa. ab in saamaanon ko fatah prakaash bhawan ke darbar haul main paryatakon ko dekhne ke liye rakhaa gaya he. lokation: fatah prakaash mahal pravesh shulk:: vayaskon ke liye 325 ru. tathaa bacchonl ke liye 165 ru.. samay: subah 10 baje se shaam 8 baje tak. sabhi din khulaa hua.
