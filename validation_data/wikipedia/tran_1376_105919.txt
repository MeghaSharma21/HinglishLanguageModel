 yah gaanv letiri se 6- 7 kos dakshin kii taraf oonchaai par basaa he. yah ek pahari nadi saanpan ke kinare basaa he. is sthaan ko madhya bharat evam gujarat kii seemaa par based giraasiyon ne basaaya tha. unki "gadhi' kaa pramaan abhi bhi miltaa he.
 shamshabad kaa purana naam hohar tha, jisapar badgu thakuron kaa kabja tha1 shams khan ne pehle inase mitrataa kii tathaa baad main dhokhe se maar daalaa. is prakaar 17 veen sadi main shams khan ke netritv main musalamaanon ne isapar kabja kar liya. usee ke naam par iskaa naam shamshabad padaa. usne ek choti si gadhi banaai, jisaki purvi dwaar nadi kii ore thi tathaa iske uttari dwaar ke taraf kanoongoon kii haveli thi. donon daravaaje 20 veen sadi ke tisare dashak main gir gayi.
 shams khan do bhai tha. vah swayan jahaan nivas karata tha, vahaan san 1641 i. main ek masjid banaai gai. iskaa doosraa bhai, jo shams khan pathan ke naam se janaa jaataa he, ne 18 veen sadi main "shamsgarh' naamak ek kila banaaya, jo abhi toote- phute avasthaa main he.
 mangalgarh kii rani ke naukar dost, mohammad ne madgu thakuron ke vanshaj bakhtawar singh kii madad kii. yuddh main shams khan maaraa gaya tathaa bakhtawar singh ko punah rajya mil gaya. shams khan kii kabr kile ke andar hi bani he.
 saakshyon se pramanit hota he ki pehle yahan kai mandir rahe honge. maraathon ke samay ke yahan do mandir hai. ek bich gaanv main devla ke naam se janaa jaataa he, doosraa gaanv ke pashchim main sthit he. iskaa nirmaan vahaan ke sthaneey logon ne shraddhabhaav se karavaaya tha. jainiyon kii ek badi- si haveli bhi he, jise "chutta gutta kii haveli' ke naam se janaa jaataa he. isi main ek jain mandir bhi bana he. san 1857 ke vidroh main is haveli ko lutaa gaya tha. mewati baagiyon ne bhi kai baar ise nuksaan pahunchaaya.
