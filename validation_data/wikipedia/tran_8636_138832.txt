 avastai ek purvi irani bhashaa he jiskaa gyaan aadhunik yug main keval parsi dharm ke granthon, yani avastaa, ke dwaara pahunch paaya he. itihaaskaaron kaa maananaa he ke madhya asia ke baktria or margu kshetron main sthit yaaz samskruti main yah bhashaa yaa isaki upbhashaayen 1500-1100 isapurv ke kaal main boli jaati thim. kyonki yah ek dharmik bhashaa ban gai, isliye is bhashaa ke saadhaaran jivan se lupt hone ke pashchaat bhi iskaa prayog nae granthon ko likhane ke liye hota rahaa.
 san 1500 isapurv ke yug kii keval do irani bhashaaon kii likhit rachanaayen mili hai. ek "pracheen avastai" he jo uttarpurv main boli jaati thi or ek "pracheen farsi" he jo dakshin-pashchim main boli jaati thi. irani bhashaaon main yah purvi irani bhashaaon or pashchimi irani bhashaaon kaa ek bahut bada vibhaajan he.
 armbh main keval ek "aadim hind-irani bhashaa" thi - isi se hind-irani bhashaa parivaar kii sabhi bhashaayen janmi hai, jinamein sanskrit, hindi, kashmiri, farsi, pashto sabhi shaamil hai. pracheen avastai us yug kii bhashaa he jab purvi irani bhashaayen or vaidic sanskrit bahut milti-julati thim. pashchimi irani bhashaaon main kuch badlaav aa rahe the jis se vah vaidic sanskrit se thodi bhinn ho chuki thi. kaha ja saktaa he ke pracheen avastai in donon ke bich thi - yah vaidic sanskrit se bahut milti-julati he or yah pracheen farsi se bhi milti julati he. avastai bhashaa main rachanaayen kam hai isliye avastai shabd-vyakaran samajhane ke liye bhashavaigyanik vaidic sanskrit kaa pehle adhyayan kar uski sahaayta lete hai kyonki avastai or vaidic sanskrit main is kshetra main nikat kaa sambandh he.
 19ve shatabdi main avastai or vaidic sanskrit donon par pashchimi vidwaanon kii nazar nai-nai padi thi or in donon ke gahare sambandh kaa tathy unke saamne jaldi hi aa gaya. unhone dekha ke avastai farsi or vaidic sanskrit ke shabdon main kuch saral niyamon ke saath ek se dusre ko anuvaadit kiya ja saktaa tha or vyakaran kii drushti se yah donon bahut nazadik the. apni 1892 main prakashit kitaab "avastai vyakaran kii sanskrit se tulana or avastai varnmaala or uskaa lipyantaran" main bhashavaigyanik or vidwaan ebraham jackson ne udahaaran ke liye ek avasatai ke dharmik shloke kaa vaidic sanskrit main sidhaa anuwaad kiya -
 avastai kii apni "pahalavi" lipi thi or uskaa istemaal ab parsi dharmik prayogon ko chhodkar lupt ho chukaa he. ise farsi or pracheen bhartiya lipi kharoshtee kii tarah daayen se baayein likhaa jaataa he. isamen 13 mul akshar the jinke avastai main 53 bhinn rup prayog hote the. yah akshar aramai lipi par aadhaarit the, jis se bharat kii bhi sari aadhunik lipiyaan utpann hui hai. bharat main sthit parsi samudaay ke log prachinkaal main avastai ko brahmi par aadhaarit lipiyon main bhi likhte the.
