 nishthanand bajraachaarya nepalbhasha punarjagran kaal ke ek maharathi the. in ko nepalbhasha ke punarjagran ke chaar stambh mai ek bhi kaha jaataa he. nepalbhasha mai sabase pehle thasa akshar mai pustak chapaaneka shrey in ko jaataa he. inhonne ne.san. 1029mai pragnaparmita pustak chapaaya tha jo nepalbhasha kaa sabase pehle chhaapi gayi pustak he.
 inkaa janm nepal sambat 978 thimlathv chauthi ke din mai huwa tha. inke pita kaa naam muktanand bajraachaarya evam maa kaa naam thakumati bajraachaarya he. inkaa ghar kathmadon kaa  bahaah mai narbahii mai tha. inke patni kaa naam ratnaprabha bajraachaarya tha. inkaa dehant nepal sambat 1055 thimlathv chauthi ke din huwa tha.
