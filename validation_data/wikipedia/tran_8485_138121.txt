 ludwig vitgenshtin  austria ke darshanik the. unhone tarkshastra, ganit kaa darshan, man kaa darshan, evam bhashaa ke darshan par mukhyatah kaary kiya. unki gananaa beesvin shatabdi ke mahanatam darshanikon main hoti he.
 unke jite ji ek hi pustak prakashit ho pai - Tractatus Logico-Philosophicus. baad kii prakashit pustakon main Philosophical Investigations kaaphi charchit rahi. vitgenshtin cambridge vishvavidhyalay ke professor rahe hai. vitgenshtin ke pita karl ek yahudee the jinhone baad main protestant dharm apana liya tha.
 vitganstin kaa sabase bada yogadan bhashaa ko darshanik paripekshya main rakhane kaa he. aaj koi bhi bhashaa vigyaan main jab darshan kaa ullekh karata he to vitganstin kaa naam swatah samaran hota he. vitganstin ke lekhan se kaaphi tark-vitark upja he, yahan tak ki kai baar vitganstin ke kaam ko darshanavipareet bhi kaha gaya he, kintu aisa kahana isaliye galat hoga ki vitganstin se pehle bhi tarkshastriyon ne bhashaa ke taarkik vishleshan par jor daalaa tha. vitganstin ke guru bartrand rasal rahe hai kaa tarkshastra ko darshan main uchit sthaan dilaane main baari yogadan he.vitganstin kaa darshanik jivan kaaphi dilachasp he kyonki unhone apane baad ke kaaryon main apane puraane kaaryon kaa khandan kiya he. apni pehle kii pustakon main vitganstin satya ke liye bhashaa kaa mahatv bataate najar aate hai kintu apni antim pustak "philosophical investigations" main vitganstin ne bhashaa main vyaapt bis kaa vivaran kiya he. vitganstin kaa ye nishkarsh darshanik jagat ke liye nutan or mahatvapurn siddh hua. vitgenstin ne ek prakaar se is vichaar kaa pratipaadan kiya ki bhashaa se satya kaa vivaran nahin balki satya kaa nirmaan kiya jaataa he. samast paashchaatya darshan bhashaa main hi nihit he, kintu bhashaa hamen satya kaa ek rup hi pradarshit karti he, vah satya jo ham apni manyata yaa anubhav se nirmit karte hai.vitgenstin baar baar ek bhashakreedaa kaa ullekh karte hai, jisamen bhaag lene vaale kisi satya kii abhivyakti ke liye bhashaa kaa nirmaan va upyog karte hai. is krida ke madhyam se vitgenstin bhashaa se vivirat tathyon va satya main antar bataate hai. apni nirnaatmak pustak "philosophical investigations" main wo kehete hai ki " adhiktar baar jab ham shabd ke "arth" kii baat karte hai, tab ham keval ek bhashaa main us shabd ke yojan kii baat karte hai ".
