 sheer kurmaa yaa sheer khorma farsi main "seviyon ke saath doodh", id ul-fitr or id al-azaha ke din afghanistan main muslimon dwaara taiyaar ek seviyon kaa pakwaan he. bharat, pakistan, bangladesh or madhya asia ke kuch hisson main yah ek paaramparik muslim tyohaar kaa naashtaa, or samaaroh ke liye mithai he. yah pakwaan sookhe khajuron se bana he.
 id kii namaaz ke baad parivaar main naashte ke dauraan sabhi atithi mehmaanon ke liye yah vishesh pakwaan parosa jaataa he. yah dakshin asia or madhya asia main bahut lokapriy he.
 sheer khurma banane main istemaal kijane vaali mukhya saamagree seviyaan doodh, chini or khajur hai. is ke saath ilaychi, pista, baadaam, laung, kesar, kishmish or gulab ke pani ko bhi jod diya jaataa he.
 viki books rasoi kii kitaab main ek nuskhaa / modul he
 seviyon ko shuddh ghi main tala jaataa he yaa taiyaar seviyaan lee jaati hai. phir doodh joda jaataa he or senwai ko aage pakaaya jaataa he. chini khajur ko anya dry fruits ke saath milaayaa jaataa he.
