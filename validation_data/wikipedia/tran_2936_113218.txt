 gayatri kaa ek naam bhavani he. is rup main aadya shakti kii upasna karne se us bharg-tej kii abhivruddhi hoti he, jo avaanchhniyataon se ladane or parrast karne ke liye aavashyak he, ise ek shakti-dhara bhi kah sakate hai. bhavani ke paryaay vaachak, durga, chandi, bhairavi, kaali aadi naam hai. inaki mukh mudra evam bhav cheshtaa main vikaraalata he. sangharsh main unki gati-vidhiyaan niojit hai. unka vaahan singh he. singh parakram kaa-akraman kaa pratik he. haathon main aise aayudh hai jo shatru ko videern karne ke hi kaam aate hai. lok vyavhaar main bhavani talwaar ko bhi kehete hai. uskaa prayojan bhi avaanchhneeyata kaa pratirodh karnaa he. asuron ke shastr utpeedan ke liye prayukt hote hai. unke liye bhavani shabd kaa prayog tabhi hoga jab unka upyog kii aniti ke virodh or niti ke samarthan main kiya ja rahaa ho.
 dharm kaa ek paksh seva, sadhana, karunaa, sahaayta, udaartaa ke rup main prayukt hota he. yah vidhayak-srujanaatmak paksh he. doosraa paksh aniti kaa pratirodh he, iske bina dhamr na to purna hota he, na surakshit rahata he. sajjanta kii raksha ke liye dushtataa kaa pratirodh bhi abhisht he. is pratirohak shakti ko hi bhavani kehete hai. durga evam chandi ke rup main usee kii lilaon kaa vanarn kiya jaataa he. 'devi bhagwat' main vishisht rup se or anyaanya puraanon, uppuraanon main saamaanya rup se isi mahashakti kii charchaa hui he or use asur vidaarani, sankat niwarini ke rup main chitrit kiya gaya he. avtaron ke do uddeshy hai- ek dharm kii sthapana, doosraa adharm kaa vinaash. donon ek duusare ke poorak hai.
 srijan or dhwans kii dwividh prakriyaaon kaa avlamban lene se hi suvyavastha ban paati he. bhojan jitna aavashyak he, utana hi malvisarjan bhi. utpaadan evam sanvadharn ke liye kiye jane vaale prayatnon ke saath akramanakari tattvon se bachaav kaa bhi prabandh karnaa padataa he. rajasatta ko prajapaalan ke atirikt updravon ko rokane ke liye sena, police aadi ke surakshatmak praytn bhi karne padate hai. kisaan ko khet or mali ko bageeche ko ugane, badhaane ke saath-saath rakhwale kaa bhi prabandh karnaa hota he. anyathaa unka kiya hua sara parishram, avaanchhneeya tattvon ke haath main chalaa jaayga or ve us apharan se adhik protsaahit, paripusht hokar haani pahunchaane kaa dussahas karenge. astu, sajjanta kaa pariposhan jitna aavashyak he, utana hi dushtataa kaa unmoolan bhi abhisht he. inamen se kisi ek ko lekar chalane se suvyavastha rah nahin sakati.
 sanghashar kaa pratham charan durbuddhi se jujhna he. nikrusht str kii dubharvnaayen or kuvichaarnaayen interval main jade jamaa kar vyakti ko patan, paraabhav ke gart main dhakelti hai. buree aadaton ke vasheebhoot hokar manushya duvyarsano or dushkarmon main pravrutt hota he. faltah naanaa prakaar ke klesh sahataa or ksht uthaata he. vyaktitv main ghuse hue kashay-kalmashon, kusanskaron kaa unmoolan karne ke liye vibhinn prakaar kii tap-titikshaayen apnaani padati hai.
 lok vyavhaar main yahi dushpravritiyaan aalasy, pramaad, aswatchta, ashishtataa, avyavashtha, sankeern swaarth parata aadi roopon main manushya ko upekshit, tiraskrit banaati hai. inaki maatra adhik bad jane se vyakti bhrasht, dusht aacharan karata he or pashu-pishaach kehalaata hai. vaasana, trishna or ahanta kii badhottaree se bhi manushya asaamaajik, avaanchhneeya, uchhrunkhal evam aakraamak ban jaataa he. faltah use ghrinaa evam pratadna kaa dand sahanaa padataa he. us sthiti se ubarne par hi vyakti ko susanskrit evam suviksit hone kaa avasar miltaa he. aatm shodhan kii saahasikta bhi bhavani he. aatmavijay ko sabase badi vijay kaha gaya he.
 samaaj main jahaan sahakaarita, sajjanta evam rachanaatmak prayatnon kaa kram chalata he, vahaan dushtataa, durabhisndhiyaan bhi kam nahin hai. avaanchhneeyata, anautikta, mudh manyataon kaa jaal buree tarah bicha rahata he. unheen ke kaaran anekanek vaiyaktik evam saamaajik samasyaayen uthati evam vikrutiyaan badhati rahati he. inase ladane ke liye vaiyakti evam samuhik str par prachand prayaas hone hi chahie. isi prayatnshiltaa ko chandi kehete hai. bhavani yahi he. srijan or sanghashar ke anyonyashray tattwo main se sangharsh kii aavashyaktaa ko sujhane vaala or use apnaane kaa protsaahan dene vaala swaroop bhavani he. sadbuddhi kii adhishthatri gayatri kaa ek paksh sanghasharsheel, shaury, saahas ke liye bhi magardasharn karata he. is shakti kaa gayatri sadhana se sahaj sanvadharn hota he.
 bhavani ke swaroop, aayudh evam vaahan aadi kaa-sankshipt tattvik vivechan is prakaar he-bhavani ke ek mukh, aath haath hai. shankh se-dev paksh kii sahaayta kaa udghosh, gada se shakti, padam se nivirkarita, bandhi mutthi se sanghathan, chakra se gatishiltaa, talwaar se doshanash, pash se asuri shakti ko baandhakar baadhit karne tathaa ashshivard mudra se sajjnon ko aashwast karne kaa bhav sannihit he. vaahan-singh-shaury kaa pratik hai.
