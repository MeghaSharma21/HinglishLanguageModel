 sant singhaji bharat main madhya pradesh ke nimar ke ek mashhoor sant the. unhe pashu rakshak dev ke rup main puja jaataa he.
 singhaji kaa janm gram khajuri jila badvani madhyapradesh me hua.  kaa namakaran unhi ke naam par kiya gaya he. singhaji madhya pradesh main khandava se 28 mile uttar purv main harsood tehsil kaa ek chota gaanv he. harsood kaa nirmaan harshavardhan dwaara kiya gaya tha. isi gaanv main singhaji ek saamaanya gopal yaa ahir parivaar main janme the. 16veen shatabdi main singhaji kii pratibha se prerit hokar gaanv ke jameendaar ne unhen apana sardaar niyukt kar diya tha. singhaji ne baarah saal jameendaar kii seva kii tathaa apni aadhyatmik karamaati shaktiyon se jameendaar ke liye kai ladaaiyon main vijay bhi praapt kii. kuch sroton ke anusaar unhone bhamagarh ke raja ke patravaahak kaa kaary bhi kiya tha. parantu kalantar main unhone sanyas le liya va tapasvi ban gaye. 40 varsh kii aayu main unki mrityu ho gayi.
 sant singhaji ko 16veen yaa 17veen shatabdi ke kaal kaa mana jaataa he. kaha jaataa he ki singhaji ek kavi va karamaati sant the. samooche madhya pradesh main anekon sthanon par singhaji ke dere va samadhiyaan bani huyi hai jahaan par melon kaa aayojan bhi kiya jaataa he. kavi sant singhaji ke aadhyatmik geet aaj tak gaye jate hai.
 narmada jalavidyut vikaas nigam dwaara narmada nadi parati viksit kii gayi narmada sagar pariyojna main sant singhaji jalashay bhi pariyojna kaa bhaag he ji 913 varg meter main failaa he. yah bharat kaa sabase bada va asia kaa doosraa sabase bada jalashay he. yahan par singhaji kii paavan samadhi kaa bhi nirmaan kiya gaya he.singhaji pariyojna kii anumaanit laagat 5 karod he.
