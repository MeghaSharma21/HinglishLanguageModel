 tulsi maanas mandir kaashi ke aadhunik mandiron main ek bahut hi manoram mandir he. yah mandir varanasi cant se lagbhag paanch ki mi durga mandir ke sameep main he. is mandir ko seth ratan laal sureka ne banwaaya tha. puri tarah sangemarmar se bane is mandir kaa udghaatan bharat ke tatkaaleen raashtrapati mahamahim sarvapalli radhakrishnan dwaara san 1964 main kiya gaya. 
 is mandir ke madhya me shri ram, mata janaki, lakshmanaji evam hanumanji virajman he. inke ek ore mata annapurna evam shivaji tathaa duusari taraf satyanarayanji kaa mandir he. is mandir ke sampoorn diwaar par ramcharitamanas likhaa gaya he. iske duusari manzil par sant tulsi das ji virajman he, saath hi isi manzil par swachaalit shri ram evam krishna lila hoti he. is mandir ke chaaro taraf bahut suhaavna ghaas evam rangeen phuhaaraa he, jo bahut hi manamohak he. yahan annakut mahotsav par chappan bhoga kii jhaaki bahut hi manamohak lagati he. mandir ke pratham manzil par ram charich maanas kii vibhinn bhashaaon main durlabh pratiyon kaa pustkaalay maujuud he. sampoorn mandir ke paridhi main bahut hi kalatmak dhang se ack pahari par shiv ji ke murti se jharne kaa alokik chata dekhte hi banati he.
