 miyaan manzur ahamed vattu, age pakistani rajaneta or pakistani raajnneetik dal pakistan muslim lig ke sansthaapak hai. ve punjab vidhansabha ke adhyaksh evam punjab ke mukhyamantri bhi rah chuke hai. ve adhyaksh ke pad par, pehali baar san 1985 main nirvaachit hue, tatpashchaat, ve ussi pad par teen baar nirvaachit hue. unhone pakistan muslim lig ke tikat par, sarvapratham, 1993 main, mukhyamantri ke pad ko hasil kiya. sangheey sarkaar or prantiy sarkaar ke bich chale matbhedon ke ek daur ke kaaran, unhen, kaarykaal ke bich hi apane pad se do baar tyaagptr dene padaa tha, jiske baad 1996 main unhone sthaayee taur par tyaag de diya tha. unhone, 1995 main, pakistan muslim lig kii sthapana kii, parantu unki party, rashtriya raajnneeti par kuch vishesh pakad rakhane main kaamyaab nahin ho pai.
 may 2008 main, vattu apni putree, rubina shaheen vattu ke saath, muslim lig ko chhod kar pakistan piples party main, shaamil ho gaye. tatpashchaat unhone, rajya evam seemaant kshetra mantralay main bataur mantri kaam kiya. tatpashchaat unhone pipalas party ke punjab ke vibhinn chunavi muhimon kaa netritv bhi kiya he.
