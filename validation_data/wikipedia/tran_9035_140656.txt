 nagar palika ek prashasnic ikai hoti he, jisamen spasht rup se paribhaashit kshetra hota he or isaki jansankhya bhi ankit hoti he. aamtaur par yah ek shahar, kasbe yaa gaanv, yaa unamem se chote samooh rup main hota he. ek nagarpalika main praayah ek mahayor prashasnic adhikari hota he, va iske upar nagar parishad yaa nagarpalika parishad kaa niyantran hota he. praayah nagarpalika adhyaksh hi prashasnic adhyaksh hota he.
 bharat main, ek nagar palika aksar ek shahar ke rup main janaa jaataa he. yah na to ek gram or na hi bade shahar ke baraabar hota he, varan unke bich kaa hota he. ek nagar palika 20,000 yaa usase adhik logon ko milakar banataa he, lekin agar yah 5 lakh se adhik jansankhya vaala ho jaataa he tab ek nagar nigam ban jaataa he.
