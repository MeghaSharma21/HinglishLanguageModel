 shivakataksh ek aushadhiy paudha he. yah paudha madhya-pradesh main bahutayat se paaya jaataa he. shivakataksh ke paudhe main bahut tikhe kaante hote hai. iskaa dharmik mahatv bhi he. shiv ji kii puja karte samay is paudhe ke phool shivlinga par chadhaaye jate hai. iske phool pile tathaa safed rang ke hote hai.
