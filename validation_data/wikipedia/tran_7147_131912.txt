 lord cornwalis fort wiliam presidency ke governor general rahe the. vah bengal kaa governor general tha. inhone 1793 isvi main bengal mensthaayee bandobast ke rup main ek nai rajasav paddati kii shuruuaat kii. iske samay main jile ke sabhi adhikaar colector ko diya gaya or ise hi bhartiya civil seva kaa janak mana jaataa he. isane company ke karmchaariyon ke vyaktigat vyapaar par pratibandh laga diya.
