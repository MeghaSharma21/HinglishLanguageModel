 paracelsus switsraland ke, 16 veen shatee ke, labdh pratishth alchemist, arthath kemiagar yaa rasayanajna the. inkaa vaastavik naam 'theofrastus bombastus fon hohenhim' tha. isa kii pratham shatabdi main rome desh main ek prasiddh saahitiyik, celsos, ho chukaa he. thiafastus bombaystom apane ko is vikhyath vyakti se bada maanate the, at: unhone apana naam 'parcelsus', arthath 'celsos se vada', rakh choda tha. 
 jurikh ke nikat moriya aainsodeln main 17 disambar 1493 i. ko paracelsus kaa janm hua. inke pita vilhelm phon hohenhim chikitsak the. inaki maan kaa inke bachapan main swargvaas ho gaya. ve mata pita kii sambhavat: ekmaatr santan the, par bade pratibhashali or ahanmany. paracelsus ke balya jivan kaa vitaran to praapt nahin he, par 1514 i. main ye tirol kii dhaatukaramshaalaon or khaanon main gaye or vahaan inhone dhaatuon se sambandh rakhanevaali rasayan kii shiksha pai. tirol jane se purv sambhavat: inhone basal vishvavidhyalay main bhi adhyayan kiya tha. peracelsus ko apane pita se chikitsa paddatiyon ke seekhney or dawaaiyon ke banane main sahaayta mili. tirol kii dhaatukaramshaala main sijismund fugar se inhone bahu kuch siikhaa or phir ye germany, france, belgium, england, scadinavia, italy, rus or kuch poorviy deshon main bhi sailony kii tarah gum ghumkar rasvidya kaa anubhav praapt karte rahe. bahut se yudhon main bhi inhone chikitsak kaa kaary campon main kiya . italy ke faraara vishvavidhyalay se unhone em. di. kii oopaadhi bhi praapt kii. 1526 i. main ve strnesburg main kuch varsh rahe, par ek sthaan par jagakar rahanaa inaki pravriti main hi na tha. paracelsus ne kai baar asaadhya rogiyon kii safal chikitsa kii, jisase unki khyaati bad gai. europe main sarvapratham inhone chikitsa ke kshetra main dhaatuon se bane yogicon kaa prayog kiya. ve apane saamne puraani paddati ke aacharyo, jaise gallen or hippocraties aadi ko tuchh samajhate the. jab ye apane nagar ke pradhan chikitsak niyukt hue, to inhone gallen or gandhak ke saath jala diya. inke is prakaar ke vyavhaaron ne nagarvasiyon or visheshtya chikitsakon ko, inkaa shatru bana diya. inke bhaashanon main bhi log vighn daalane lage. inke antim din nirdhantaa main bitne lage. inpar taras khaakar rajkumaar palatin ne april, 1541 i. main inhen salzarg bula diya. yahan inke din aaraam se katane lage, par ye bahut dinon tak jivit na rahe. 14 disambar 1541 i. ko inkaa dehavsaan ho gaya. is samay inaki aayu keval 48 varsh kii thi. saint sebastian ke girizaa main inhen dafna diya gaya.
 paracelsus ke lekhon kaa sangrah 10 jildon main 589-91 i. main basal main chapa gaya. in granthon kaa doosraa sanskaran 1603-1616 i. main strnesburg main chapa. san 1926-30 main aadhunik german bhashaa main ek sanskaran or niklaa he.
 paracelsus rasinic tatvatatvon ke parivartan main vishwaas rakhte the, par inkaa kahana yah tha ki rasvidya yaa kemiyagiri kaa uddeshy adham dhaatu ko uttam dhaatu main parivartan karnaa nahin he, jisase rog or mrityu par vijay praapt ho. chikitsashastra main paracelsus ke pehle vanaspatiyon or jangal ke aushadhon ke kaadhon kaa upyog hota tha. paracelsus ne dhaatuon se bane yogicon ke upyog kii paddati chalaai. europe main inhen aushadh rasayan kaa pravartak mana jaataa he. ve rog or swasthya par grahon ke prabhaav ko bhi maanate the. vishwa ke samast padaartho main ve jivan kaa astitv maanate the. ve vaayu, jal or agni main sharir or praan to maanate the, par aatma nahin. sab padaartho ke mul main ve teen tatv maanate the : lavan, gandhak or paara . ve dhaatuon ko gandhak or paare se milakar bani maanate the. unka kahana tha ki dhaatuon ke vibhinn rang or unki aabhaaen gandhak, paara or lavenon kii saapeksh matraaon par nirbhar hai. gandhak jvalansheeltaa kaa tatv, lavan adahyata or sthirataa kaa tatv evam paara vashpashiltaa or galaniyata kaa tatv mana jaataa tha.
 paracelsus ko nae paribhaashik shabd gadhane main bada majaa aata tha. oshadhiyon ke bheetar vidyaman roganashak tatv kaa naam unhone erkanam rakhaa tha. inkaa vichaar tha ki erkanam ek prakaar kaa gasiy padaarth he, jo upar uthakar bhaagyavidhaataa grahon or nakshatron tak pahunch saktaa he or swasthya kaa sthaapak he. rasvidya kaa uddeshy is erkanam ko hi praapt karnaa he or yah siikhnaa he ki roganivaranarth is erkanam kaa kaise prayog kiya jaay. paramatma dwaara banaai gai kachi, prakritik saamagree se taiyaar maal bananaa, rasvidya kaa doosraa uddeshy he. paramatma ne lohe kaa ayask banaaya, usase loha praapt karnaa rasagyon kaa kaary he. paracelsus ne 'alcohal' naam angoor kii asut sura ke liye diya, jo aaj tak prachlit he. is shabd kaa arth koyalewala, yaa kaagaj, he. is prakaar manamaana naam de dene main paracelsus kaa anand aata tha.
 paracelsus ne padaartho ko shuddhavastha main praapt karne par bal diya. padaartho ke sanshodhan kii vidhiyaan bhi bataai or is prakaar paracelsus ne jo paramparant nirdhaarit kin, unpar aadhunik rasayanashastra kii neenv padi.
