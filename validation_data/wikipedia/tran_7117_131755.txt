 samvaad do yaa do se adhik logon ke bich ek likhit yaa bole gaye vaartalap vinimay he or ek saahitiyik or naatakeey rup he jo is tarah ke len-dein ko darshata he. samvaad kapolkalpana main, do yaa do se adhik kirdaaron ke bich ek maukhik vinimay he. saadhaaran bhashaa main jise baat karnaa kaha jaataa he; jaise filmon main, dharawahik main yaa kisi baithak main.
