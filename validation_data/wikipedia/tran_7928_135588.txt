 padmabhushan buddhadev dasgupta, ek bhartiya shastreey sangeetkaar or sarodavadak the. unhone pandit radhika mohan mitra se sarod vaadan siikhaa tha. bharat sarkaar dwaara unhen varsh 2012 main padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. iske purv unhen varsh 2015 main sangeet mahasamman or bengal vibhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. ullekhneey he ki varsh 2011 main, unhen bharat sarkaar dwaara padmashri kii peshkash kii gai thi, lekin unhone ise "bahut der ho chuki he" kahkar lautaa diya tha. tatpashchaat unhen janvari 2012 main, padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha.15 janvari, 2018 ko hridaygati ruk jane ke kaaran unka kolkata main nidhan ho gaya.
