 swami omanand saraswati bharat ke haryana praant ke swatantraaa-sangram-senaanee, shikshak, itihaaskaar evam saamaajik kaaryakarta the. 
 swami omanand saraswati kaa janm dilli ke nikat narela main hua tha. unke pitaaji kaa naam choudhary kanak singh tha jo jat khatri the. ve apane mata-pita ke akele santan the. unki mrityu dilli ke jaipur golden aspataal main hui.
 unhone itihaas ke kai granth likhe hai jinamein nimnlikhit bahut prasiddh hai -
