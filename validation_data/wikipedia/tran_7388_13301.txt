 dow odolen smekal checkoslovakia ke suprasiddh hindi vidwaan or rajanyik the. sudur desh hindustan or uski vishisht hindi bhashaa se prem ke kaaran unhone apni do santaanon ke naam bhi bhartiya evam hindi bhashaa main hi rakhe. checkoslovakia kii rajdhani praha ke charles vishvavidhyalay se hindi main eme va piechdi karne ke baad pro smekal praha vishvavidhyalay main hindi ke praadhyaapak or bharat-vidya vibhaag ke vibhaagadhyaksh rahe. 
 vishvavidhyalay se avkaash lene ke pashchaat ve anek varshon tak bharat main checkoslovakia ke rajdoot bhi rahe. 1996 main ve do-do baar anterrashtriya str par puraskrit va sammanit kiye gayi. 13 june 1998 ko lagbhag 70 varsh kii aayu main unki mrityu hui. 
 dow smekal kaa janm checkoslovakia ke olomouts nagar main 18 agast 1928 ko hua tha. unka vivaah helena naamak yuvati se hua. unki patni helena bhartiya maanav vigyaan kii visheshgy thim. mukhyat: bharat or uski bhashaa hindi se prem ke kaaran hi unhone apni do santaanon ke naam bhartiya evam hindi main rakhe the. unki beti kaa naam indira he jo 1961 main janmi or bete kaa arun jiskaa janm do varsh baad 1963 main hua. unhone checkoslovakia kii rajdhani praha ke charles vishvavidhyalay se hindi main eme kiya tathaa vahiin se piechdi kii oopaadhi bhi praapt kii. uske baad ve praha vishvavidhyalay main hindi ke praadhyaapak niyukt ho gayi. tees varsho tak praadhyaapak ke pad par rahane ke uparant ve kai varsho tak bharat-vidya vibhaag ke vibhaagadhyaksh rahe. adhyaapan kaary se avkaash lene ke baad ve anek varshon tak bharat main checkoslovakia ke rajdoot bhi rahe. unhone hindi sahitya kii anek rachanaaon kaa check bhashaa main anuwaad kiya. iske atirikt unhone anek footkar kavitaaon or lok kathaon ko na keval anudit kiya balki unka prakaashan bhi karaayaa. inhone bhartiya samskruti tathaa samaaj ke sambandh main prakashit nibandh sangrah bharat ke navaroop ko 1974 main purna kiya. 13 june 1998 ko lagbhag 70 varsh kii aayu main unka nidhan hua.
