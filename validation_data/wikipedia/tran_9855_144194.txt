 khagolashastra main dhaatuta kisi vastu main hydrogen va helium se adhik baari rasaynic tatwon kii adhik maatra main upasthiti hone kii dasha ko kehete hai. hamaare brahmand kaa adhikansh bhautik padaarth hydrogen or helium ke rup main he jo sabase halke do tatv hai, isliye khagolashastri aksar sankshipt rup main inke alaava anya sabhi tatwon ko "dhaatu" kah dete hai. dhyaan de ki yah shabd prayog rasayanashastra main dhaatu ke prayog se bahut alag he. masalan carbon, nitrogen, oxigen or nion kii adhik maatra rakhane vaale taare aksar dhatupurn kehalaate hai haalanki yah sabhi tatv rasayanashastra main dhaatu kii paribhaasha main nahin aate.
