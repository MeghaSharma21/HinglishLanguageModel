 tanu vaids manu anand el raay dwaara nirdeshit or shailesh aar singh dwaara nirmit bhartiya romani hasya film he. isamen madhavan, kangana ranavat or jimi shergill ne mukhya abhinay bhoomika nibhaai. film kii kahani himanshu sharma ne likhii he, sangeet nirdesan krishna solo ne or geet lekhan kaa kaary rajasekhar ne kiya he. film 25 farvari 2011 ko jari hui thi. film ne jari hote hi safalataa arjeet kii or mukhya rup se dilli, uttar pradesh or punjab main film safal rahi. isko german main bhi anudit kiya gaya or tanu und manu troyan sik naam se jari kiya gaya. film kii uttarkruti tanu vaids manu riterns sheershak ke saath 22 may 2015 ko jari kii gayi.
 manoj "manu" sharma landan main rahane vaala enarai doctor he. vah ek bhartiya dulhan khojane ke liye or shaadi karne ke liye bharat ke liye aata he. jab wo aata he, tab wo tanuja "tanu" trivedi se milane ke liye apane mata-pita or dost pappi ke saath kanpur jaataa he. tanu shuru main apane ghunghat ke peeche chup or sharmili lagati he, lekin manu antat jaan jaataa he ki vastav main wo so rahi he.
 tanu vaids manu ne 1850 lakh rupee relies ke pehle hafte main kamaaye. film ne 85.0 lakh ke aasapaas rupee apane duusare saptaah main kamaaye. iske do saptaah ke kul kamai 272,50 lakh thi. apane tisare saptaah main 50 lakh. teen saptaah main kul 322,5 das lakh. tathaa chauthe saptaah main 35.0 lakh rupee is film ekatra kiye. apane chaar saptaah sangrah le rahi he. 3600 lakh rupaye ikktha kiye.
 film ko mili-juli samikshaayen praapt hui.
 is film kii safalataa ke baad ek agali kadi tanu vaids manu: riterns 2015 main release hui. isamen kangana ke do kirdaar he.
