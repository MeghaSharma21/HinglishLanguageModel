 vaidic dharmapar ek shrenee kaa bhaag

 dhyaan yoga 
 
 mangalo bhumiputrashch rinherta dhanapradah.sthirasano mahakaayah sarvakarmavirohakah .1.
 lohito lohitakshtch samgaanaan kripakarah.dharatmajah kujo bhumo bhutido bhuminandanah.2.
 angarko yamashchaev sarvarogpaharka.vrushteh kartapaherta c sarvakamfalpradah.3.
 etani kujanamani nityam yah shraddhaya pathet.rinan na jaayate tasya dhanan shighramavapnuyat.4.
 dharnigarbhasambhutan vidyutkantisamaprabhm.kumaaran shaktihastan c mangalan pranmamyaham.5.
 stotramangarakasyaitatpathaniyan sadaa nribhih.na teshan bhoumaja peeda swalpaapi bhavati kvachit.6.
 angarak mahabhag bhagavanbhaktavatsal.twaan namaami mamasheshmrinmaashu vinaashay.7.
 rinrogadidarighryan ye chanye hyapamrityvah.bhayakleshmanstapa nashyantu mam sarvada.8.
 ativaktra durarardhya bhogamukt jitatmanah.tushto dadasi samrajyan rushto harsi tatkshanat.9.
 virinchishkravishnunaan manuyana tu kaa katha.ten tvan sarvasattven grahrajo mahabalah.10.
 putrandehi dhanan dehi tvamasmi sharann gatah.rindaridrayaduhkhen shatrunan c bhayattatah.11.
 abhirdwadashabhih shlokairyah stauti c dharasutam.mahatin shriyamapnoti hyaparo dhando yuva.12.
 iti shriskandpurane bhargavaproktan rinmochak mangalastotram sampoornam
