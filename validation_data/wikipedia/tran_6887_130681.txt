 guatemala madhya america main sthit ek desh he, jiske uttar-pashchim main mexico, dakshin pashchim main prashanth mahaasaagar, uttar-purv main beliz, purv main carabian or dakshin purv main honduras or al salvador sthit he. mahaj 1,10,000 varg kimi kshetrafal vaale is desh kii jansankhya kariban 1,40,00,000 he. is pratinidhi loktantric desh kii rajdhani guatemala city he. guatemala kii samrudh jaiviki or adwitiya paristhitiki ise jaev vividhta ke lihaaj se mahatvapurn banaati he.
 guatemala naam kii vyutpatti nahuaati yaa nahuati bhashaa ke shabd 'kwatemallan' se hui he jiskaa arth he anek vrikshon kaa sthaan, jo ki kish maaya samaaj main anek vrikshon ke liye prachlit shabd he.
