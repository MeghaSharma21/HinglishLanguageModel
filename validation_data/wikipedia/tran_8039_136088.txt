 mujhe jeene do san 1963 main bani ek mashhoor hindi film kaa naam he jiskaa nirdesan mani bhattacharya ne kiya tha. ajanta art ke binar tale bani va dakaiton ke vaastavik jivan par aadhaarit baliwood kii is film main sunil dutt, wahidaa rahamaan, nirupa roy, rajendra nath evam mumtaz ne abhinay kiya tha.
 chambal ghati ke daaku samasyagrast ilaake bhind evam muraina jilon ke khatarnaak bihadon main madhya pradesh police ke suraksha kavach main filmaayi gayi, tathaa mohan studio mumbai main bani is film main wahidaa rahamaan va sunil dutt ke abhinay kii behtareen pratibha kaa pradarshan hua tha. jayadev ke sangeet nirdesan ne ise sarvshereshth film kaa darza dilaane main apni mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai thi.
 rajasthan se sate madhya pradesh ke bhind va muraina jile british bharat main ek swatantra riyasat ke antargat aate the. us riyasat kaa naam tha gwalior. is kshetra ko tomarsghar bhi kaha jaataa tha. yahan ke nivasee swabhaav se ugr the or kisi bhi rajya satta kii parvaah nahin karte the. unhen daaku ban kar dar-dar bhatakana pasand tha kintu sarkaar ko tex dena manjuur na tha. apni is aan, baan or shaan ke liye ve jaan kii baaji lagaana behatar samajhate the. puure ke puure parivaar tabah ho jate the. sunil dutt ne is samasya kaa gambheer adhyayan kiya or dakaiton ke vaastavik jivan ko lekar ek mahatvapurn film banaayi. is film main daaku jaranalasinh kii mukhya bhoomika swayan sunil dutt ne nibhaai thi jabaki chameli jaan naamak veshya kaa role wahidaa rahamaan ne kiya tha.
 sunil dutt or unki patni naragis kii saanskrithik sanstha ajanta arts cultural trup kii puri team ne madhya pradesh kii chambal ghati main jaakar is film kii shooting kii thi.
 is film kaa sangeet jayadev verma ne diya tha jabaki gane mashhoor urdu shaayar sahir ludhiaanvi ne likhe the. parshv gayika aashaa bhonsle dwaara gaaya gaya geet-"nadi naare na jaao shyaam paiyyaan paruun" sabase behtareen sichuation par filmaaya gaya tha.
 is film ke anya gane parshv gayakon ke naam ke saath is prakaar hai-
 yah puri kii puri film chambal ghati ke daaku samasyagrast ilaake bhind evam muraina jilon ke khatarnaak bihadon main madhya pradesh police ke suraksha kavach main filmaayi gayi thi. chambal ghati main jaakar film kii shooting karnaa un dinon koi hansee mazaaq nahin apitu dussahas kaa kaam tha parantu apni dhun ke pakke sunil dutt ne use bakhubi anjaam diya. is kaam main madhya pradesh sarkaar ne unhen puri sahaayta pradaan kii thi.
 is film ke nirdeshak mani bhattacharya ko isse purv chunki do bigha zamin tathaa madhumati jaisi filmon main sahaayak nirdeshak ke rup main kaary karne kaa anubhav tha at: unhone daaku kii saamaajik samasya ko maanviya drushtikon se dekhne, samajhane or filmaane kii ore vishesh dhyaan diya. 
 film abhinayta sunil dutt is film ke nirmaataa bhi the at: unhone vaastavik prishthbhumi main hi is film kii shooting karne kaa sankalp kiya. yahi nahin ek daaku kii romantic bhoomika nibhaane main bhi unhone jabardast mehnat kii.
 in sabhi baton kaa sakaaratmak parinaam bhi niklaa jab yah film puri tarah se hit hui or unhen iske liye sarvshereshth abhinayta kaa puraskaar mila.
