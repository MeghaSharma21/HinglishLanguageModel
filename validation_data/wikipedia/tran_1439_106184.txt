 carbon-14 dwaara kalanirdhaaran kii vidhi kaa prayog puraatatv-jeev vigyaan main jantuon evam paudhon ke praapt avasheshon ke aadhaar par jivan kaal, samay chakra kaa nirdhaaran karne main kiya jaataa he. isamen carbon-12 evam carbon-14 ke madhya anupaat nikaala jaataa he.carbon ke do sthir aradiodharmee samasthanik: carbon-12 or carbon-13 hote hai. inke alaava ek asthir radiodharmee samasthanik ke ansh bhi prithvi par milte hai. arthath carbon-14 kaa ek nirdhaarit maatra kaa namoona 5730 varshon ke baad aadhi maatra kaa ho jaataa he. aisa radiodharmita kshay ke kaaran hota he. is kaaran se carbon-14 prithvi se bahut samay purv samaapt ho chukaa hota, yadi surya kii cormik kirnon ke prithvi ke vaataavaran kii nitrogen par prabhaav se or utpaadan na hua hota. brahmand kirnon se praapt neutron nitrogen anuon se nimna parmaanu pratikriya karte hai: 
 carbon-14 ke utpaadan kii adhiktam dar 9-15 ki.me. kii bhu-chumbakeey oonchaaiyon par hoti he; kintu carbon-14 puure vaataavaran main samaan dar se failata he or oxigen ke anuon se pratikriya kar carbon dioxide banaata he. yah carbon dioxide sagar ke jal main bhi ghul kar fail jaati he. paudhe vaataavaran kii carbon dioxide ko prakaash-sanshleshan dwaara prayog karte hai, evam unka seven kar pachan ke baad jantu ise nishkasit karte hai. is prakaar prayek jivit praani lagaataar carbon-14 ko vaataavaran se len-dein karata rahata he, jab tak wo jivit rahata he. uske jivan ke baad ye adla-badali samaapt ho jaati he. iske baad carbon-14 kii sharir main shesh maatra kaa radiodharmee beta kshay ke dwaara hraas hone lagata he. is hraas kii dar ardh aayu kaal yani 5,730Â±40 varsh main aadhi maatra hoti he. 
 carbon 14 kii khoj 27 farvari, 1940 main martin camen or sam ruben ne california vishvavidhyalay radiation prayogshaala, barkale main kii thi. jab carbon kaa ansh prithvi main dab jaataa he tab carbon-14 kaa radiodharmita ke kaaran hraas hota rahata he. par carbon ke duusare samasthakanikon kaa vayumandal se sampark vichche1da or carbon dioxide na banane ke kaaran unke aapas ke anupaat main antar ho jaataa he. prithvi main dabe carbon main uske samasthanikon kaa anupaat jaanakar uske dabane kii aayu kaa pataa lagbhag shatabdi main kar sakate hai.
 carbonkaal vidhi ke madhyam se tithi nirdhaaran hone par itihaas evam vaigyanik tathyon kii jaankaree hone main sahaayta milti he. yah vidhi kai kaarnon se vivaadon main rahi he vaigyanikon ke anusaar radiocorban kaa jitani teji se kshay hota he, usase 27 se 28 pratishat zyada iskaa nirmaan hota he. jisase santulan kii avasthaa praapt hona mushkil he. aisa mana jaataa he ki praaniyon kii mrityu ke baad bhi ve carbon kaa avasorana karte hai or asthir radiodharmee-tatv kaa dhire-dhire kshay hota he. puratathvik namoonay main upasthit corban-14 ke aadhaar par uski date kii gananaa karte hai. 356 i. main bumadhya sagar ke tat par aaye vinaashaakaari sunami kii tithi nirdhaaran vaigyanikon ne carbon dating dwaara hi kii he.
 radiocarbon dating takanic kaa avishkaar 1949 main chicago vishvavidhyalay ke williard libi or unke saathiyon ne kiya tha. 1960 main unhen is kaary ke liye rasayan vigyaan ke nobel puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha. unhone carbon dating ke madhyam se pehali baar lakdi kii aayu pataa kii thi. varsh 2004 main eumea uniwarsity ke vaigyanikon kosweden ke dalarna praant kii phulu pahadiyon main lagbhag das hajaar varsh purana deodaar kaa ek ped mila he jiske bare main vaigyanikon kaa kahana he ki yah vishwa kaa sabase purana vriksh he. carbon dating paddati se gananaa ke baad vaigyanikon ne ise dharti kaa sabase purana ped kaha he. iske alaava carbon-14 dating kaa prayog anek kshjetron main kaal-nirdhaaran ke liye kiya jaataa he. whisky kitni puraani he, iske liye bhi yah vidhi kargar evam prayogneey rahi he. oxford radio carbon esicrator ke up nideshak tam hiham ke anusaar 1950 ke dashak main hue parmaanu pareekshan se nikle radioskriy padaartho kii maujuudagi ke aadhaar par whisky banane kaa samay janaa ja saktaa he.
