 visheshgy kii raay usee vyakti ke dwaara abhivyakt kii jaati he jiske paas apane kshetra se judaa vishesh gyaan ho. visheshgy dwaara raay die jane ke baad us raay ko saadhaaran vyakti nahin badal saktaa. visheshgy ko apane nishkarsh sabit karne ke liye vaigyanik maapdand kii aavashyaktaa padati he, jisase vah apni baat kii sachai sabit kar saktaa he. aisi pramanit baton ko aadhaar banaakar nyaayalay apana nirnay kisi ke paksh main kar sakati he tathaa kisi dandit kar saktaa he. nyaayalay hi yah sunishchit karata he kii visheshgy dwaara diya gaya pramaan mananeey he yaa nahin. nyaayalay ke alaava visheshgy ke faisle par usee kshetra ke visheshgy hi mananeey rup se bhinn mat vyakt kar sakate hai.
