 nongakhilem vanya jivan abhayaarany meghalaya rajya ke ri bhoi jile main lilad gram ke nikat sthit ek abhayaarany he. yah purvottar bharat ke sabase sundar evam lokapriy abhayaaranyon main se ek he. iskaa vistaar 29 kimi ke kshetra main he or yah yahaan ke paryatak gantavyon main atyant prasiddh sthaan he. yahaan ke vanya jivan main vividh prakaar ke pashu evam pakshi hai jinamein stanadhariyon, avian, krintak, sarisrup or kai anya prajatiyaan dikhaai detii hai. vanya jivan ke alaava yahaan kaa van pradesh hariyaali evam padpon kii dheron prajatiyon se bhi paripurna he. kshetra ke sabase neeche bhubhag lilaad gram ke nikat hai jo sagar sataha se maatr 200 me par sthit hai jabaki sabase oonche bhaag purvi evam dakshini hai jinaki adhiktam oonchai 950 me tak bhi he. is kshetra ko 1981 merebhayaarany ghoshit kiya gaya tha. 
 nongakhilem abhayaarany main meghalaya ke wanon ke bahut se maanav se achoote bhaag bhi hai. inamen se bahut se bhaagon main jonk aadi kaa bahulya he. yahaan basane vaali dheron vanya jeev prajatiyon main se kuch hai royal bengal tigar, bhartiya sand, himalayan kaala bhaaluu, clouded lapard, aadi. inke alaava pakshiyon kii bhi bahut si rangeen va shandaar prajatiyaan mil jaati hai jinka nivas yahaan ke wanon kii jhaadiyon va vrikshon main he. is tarah yah sthaan pakshidarshiyon ke liye bhi vardaan he. inamen se kii prajatiyaan jaise bhuri hornbill, manipur bush quail, rufas necd harnbill, aadi hai. bahut se videshi pravasi pakshi bhi dur kshetron yaa deshon se is abhayaarany main aate hai. keede or makadiyon kii bhi prajatiyaan yahaan aam hai. 
 yah abhyaarany prakruti kii bhavyata ke darshan karne kaa anokhaa sthaan he. is abhayaarany ke bhraman karne kaa sarvottam samay he march-april ke maah, kyonki is avadhi main pravasi pakshiyon kii dheron prajatiyaan yahaan kii shobhaa badhaati hai. yahaan na keval desh se varan videshon se bhi vanya jivan premiyon kaa aagaman hota he. abhayaarany main pravesh purv nongpoh karyaalay se van range adhikari se anumati leni hoti he. abhayaarany kaa sthaan evam sthiti awajahi kii drushti se sulabh he evam nikatavarti kasbon yaa shilong aadi se sadak maarg dwaara sugam he.
