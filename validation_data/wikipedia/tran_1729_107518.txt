 holikaa hiranyaksh or hiranyakashyap naamak daitya kii bahan or prahlaad naamak vishnu bhakt kii buaa thi. jiskaa janm janapad- kasganj ke soron shukarakshetra naamak pavitra sthaan par hua tha. usko yah vardaan praapt tha ki vah aag main nahin jalegi. is vardaan kaa laabh uthaane ke liye vishnu-virdohi hiranyakashyap ne use ajna di ki vah prahlaad ko lekar agni main pravesh kar jaae, jisase prahlaad kii mrityu ho jaae. holikaa ne prahlaad ko lekar agni main pravesh kiya. ishwar krupa se prahlaad batch gaya or holikaa jal gai. holikaa ke ant kii khushi main holi kaa utsav manaaya jaataa he.
