 kisi rajya dwaara vyaktiyon yaa vividh sanstha se jo adhibhaar yaa dhan liya jaataa he use kar yaa tex kehete hai. raashtra ke adheen aane vaali vividh sansthaayen bhi tarah-tarah ke kar lagaatin hai. kar praaia: dhan ke rup main lagaaya jaataa he kintu yah dhan ke tulya shram ke rup main bhi lagaaya ja saktaa he. kar do tarah ke ho sakate hai - pratyaksh kar yaa apratyaksh kar . ek taraf ise janata par boj ke rup main dekha ja saktaa he vahiin ise sarkaar ko chalaane ke liye aadharbhoot aavashyaktaa ke rup main bhi samajha ja saktaa he.
 bharat ke pracheen rishi kar ke bare main yah maanate the ki vahi kar-sangrahan-pranali adarsh kahi jaati he, jisase karadaata va kar sangrahakarta donon ko kathinaayee na ho. unhone kaha ki kar-sangrahan is prakaar se hona chaiye jis prakaar madhumakhi dwaara paraag sangrahan kiya jaataa he. is paraag sangrahan main pushp bhi pallavit rahete hai or madhumakhi apane liye shahad bhi juta leti he.
