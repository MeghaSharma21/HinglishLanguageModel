 tajik madhya asia main rahane vaale farsi-bhashiyon ke samudaayon ko kaha jaataa he. bahut se afghanistan se aaye tajik sharnaarthi iran or pakistan main bhi rahete hai. apni samskruti or bhashaa ke maamle main tajik logon kaa iran ke logon se gaharaa sambandh rahaa he. china ke tajik log anya tajik logon se zaraa bhinn hote hai kyonki ve purvi irani bhashaayen bolte hai jabaki anya tajik farsi bolte hai.
 tajiki log purvi irani bhashaayen bolne vaale pracheen soudai, bactrian or parthian logon ke vanshaj hai. iran ke shaktishaalee hakhaamani or saasaani samrajyon ke prabhaav se ve samay ke saath-saath farsi ke bhinn rup bolne lage, jo ek pashchimi irani bhashaa he. phir bhi tajiki farsi main pracheen soudai or parthianke kai shabd milte hai, jo irani farsi main nahin milte. madhya asia main iraniyon ke alaava doosraa bada prabhaav turki bhashiyon kaa rahaa he. 'tajik' naam turkiyon ne hi farsi-bhashiyon ko diya or iskaa arth 'gair-turki' hua karata tha. kisi jamaane main 'tajik' shabd ko ek gaali mana jaataa tha lekin jab tajikistan soviet sangh kaa bhaag bana to ise garv se madhya asia main bas rahe farsi-bhashiyon kii pahchaan ke liye istemaal kiya jane laga. usee soviet kaal se 'tajik' shabd sammanjanak bana hua he.
 tajik log farsi kii ek purvi upbhasha bolte hai, jise 'dari' yaa 'dari farsi' kaha jaataa he. 'dari' shabd 'darbari' ko sikodkar banaaya gaya he. tajikistan main tajiki bhashaa ko likhane ke liye sirilik lipi istemaal kii jati he, haalaanki puraane jamaane main ise arabi-farsi lipi main likhaa jaataa tha. aadhunik tajiki bhashaa par roosi bhashaa, uzbek bhashaa or uigur bhashaa ke gahare prabhaav milte hai. kuch farsi-bhashi log tajiki ko irani farsi se zyaada shuddh maanate hai, kyonki iran par arab akraman or qabze ke baad bahut baari arabi bhashaa kaa prabhaav padaa. irani farsi kii tulana main tajiki farsi main arabi ke shabd kam hai or pracheen farsi mul ke shabd adhik hai. tajikistan main do tarah kii farsi boli jaati he. ek to rozaana boli jane vaali farsi he, jise 'zabaan-e-kuuchaa' kehete hai, yani 'gali-kuche kii bhashaa'. duusari aupchaarik taur par bole jane vaali 'zabaan-e-adbi' he, yani 'adab kii bhashaa'.
 aitihaasik rup se tajik logon main pracheen arya dharm, buddha dharm or zarathustri dharm kaa jor rahaa he. islam ke aane ke baad adhiktar tajik log sunni muslim ban gaye, haalanki inamen shiya musalmaan bhi paae jate hai. san 2005 main tajikistan kii aabaadi 95% sunni or 3% shiya thi. dhyaan dijiye ki yah iran kaa ulta he, jahaan zyaadaatar log shiya hai.
