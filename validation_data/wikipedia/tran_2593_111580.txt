 raghuveer sahay hindi ke sahityakar va patrkaar the.
 raghuveer sahay kaa janm lakhanu main hua tha. angrezi sahitya main em e lakhanu vishvavidhyalay. sahitya srijan 1946 se. patrakarita kii shuruyaat dainik navajivan se 1949 main. 1951 ke aarambh tak upsampadak or saanskrithik samvaadadaata. isi varsh dilli aae. yahan pratik ke sahaayak sampadak, akashwani ke samachar vibhaag main upsampadak . 1955 main vimaleshwari sahay se vivaah. 
 doosraa saptak, sidhiyon par dhoop main, aatmahatya ke viruddh, hanso hanso jaldi hanso, rasta idhar se he, dilli meraa pardesh or likhane kaa kaaran unki pramukh kritiyaan hai.
 iske alaava 'baarah hungary kahaniyaan', vivekanand, 'jeko', tathaa 'varanam van' sheershak se hindi bhashaantar bhi samay-samay par prakashit hue hai.
 raghuveer sahay samakaleen hindi kavita ke mahatvapurn stambh hai. unke sahitya main patrakarita kaa or unki patrakarita par sahitya kaa gaharaa asar rahaa he. unki kavitaayen azaadi ke baad vishesh rup se san ’60 ke baad ke bharat kii tasviir ko samagrata main pesh karti hai. unki kavitaayen nae maanav sambandhon kii khoj karnaa chaahati hai jisamen gaiyr barabari, anyaay or gulaami na ho. unki samoochee kavy-yatra kaa kendriya lakshya aisi janatantric vyavastha kii nirmiti he jisamen shoshan, anyaay, hatya, aatmahatya, vishmata, daastaa, raajnneetik samprabhutaa, jati-dharm main bante samaaj ke liye koi jagah na ho. jin aashaaon or sapanom se azaadi kii ladaai ladi gai thi unhen saakaar karne main jo baadhaayen aa rahi hon, unka nirantar virodh karnaa unka rachanaatmak lakshya rahaa he. ve jivan ke antim paaydaan par khadey hokar apni jijeevishaa kaa kaaran ‘apni santanon ko kute kii maut marane se bachaane’ kii baat kahkar apni patibaddhta ko marate dam tak banaae rakhte hai.
 kavita sangrah 'log bhul gaye hai ' ke liye 1984 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit.
