 sogyal rinpoche  tibet ke nyingma samrdaay ke ek jogchhen lamas hai. ve pichley 30 se adhik varshon se europe, america, austrailia or asia main ghumghumkar dharmik shiksha de rahe hai. ve 'rigpa' naamak aadhyatmik sanghathan ke sansthaapak bhi hai jo vishwa ke 23 se adhik deshon ke 100 se adhik buddha kendron kaa antararashtriya network he. unhone 'da tibatan book of living and daaing' naamak ek pustak bhi likhi he jo sarvaadhik vikray hone vaali pustakon main sammilit he. yah pustak vishwa ke 56 deshon kii 30 bhashaaon main chap chuki he.
