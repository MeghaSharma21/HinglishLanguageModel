 tango charlie varsh 2005 kii hindi bhashaa main bani, yuddh aadhaarit film he jiskaa lekhan evam nirdesan mani shankar ne kiya he. film main kai adaakar jaise ajay devgan, babi dyol, sanjay dutt, sunil shetty, tanisha, nandana sen, tathaa sudesh bairi aadi shaamil he. film kaa saar tarun chouhan naamak ardhsainik se bhartiya seemaa suraksha bal main bataur nav niyukt sipaahi ke tathakthith yuddh main kathor anubhavon par kendrit he. film main prastut kiya gaya he ki sachche sipaahi janm nahin lete, bas ban jate hai.
 film main bharat ke vibhinn bhaagon main panpate aantarik droh evam atvaad ko dikhaaya gaya he, tathaa bibisi patrkaar jasprit pandohar ke varnan main yah "yah bharat main faile aatankwaad, hinsa or unase jujhte logon kii veerta ko jaanane kaa nayaa ruchikar adhyay kholti he." angreji akhbaar da hindu ne film ke vishay main likhte he "is kathor vishay ko saamana karne ke liye paryaapt saahas kii jarurat he" tathaa "yah ek saahasik kadam he jahaan bhartiya patrakarita jane se bachati he."
 film kii shuruyaat kashmir kii barfili durgam ghaatiyon se hoti he, jahaan aatanki muthbhed kii jaanch abhiyaan ko aae bhartiya vayusena ke khoji helicopter ke payalton ko vahaan maujuud ek ghayal sainik miltaa he, jise medical camp le jane ke dauraan usase diary milti he jisase sainik kii pahchaan tarun chouhan banaam tango charlie, sipaahi 101wein biguef batalian ke rup main hoti he, or isi diary ke saath beete muthbhedon kii ghatnakram shuru hoti hai. 
 tarun chouhan kii posting manipur main hoti he. jahaan uski mulaakaat hawaldaar mohammad ali ke lagaae jaal main funsane ke baad hoti he. is laaparvaahee ke chalate mohammad use bevakoof kahata he, mohammad ali usase sawaal karata he kii usne sena kii naukari kyon chuni ? tarun jawaab detaa he kii wo sirf deshbhakti or deshseva ke khatir yahaan aayaa he. is par mohammad use yatharth se parichit kar kahata he ki aisi baaten kehane vaale zyaadaatar galatfahami main rahete he vastav main is naukari ke badaulat sirf karj chukaa rahaa he. yahaan chunki zyaadaatar vakt paharedaari ke chalati he to tarun ko khansame kaa kaam diya jaataa he. or isi ke saath jang main ladane ke liye wo apana kodenem rakhte he, tarun chouhan urf tango charlie or mohammad ali urf mike alpha. phir petroling ke ek roj unki ek tukdi bodo gut ke dwaara unke bheje ghayal sipaahi ke jaal main fansati he, mike alpha kii team bikhar jaati he, tarun bhi bodo dal ke leader ke hatho fansata he par mike use bachaa letaa he. bodo leader unka sabase kam umr kaa jawan biju ko le jate he, or ped par baandh uski pet kii ante katkar use tadapata chhod apane dal ke saath chup baaki sipaahiyon ke aane tak dhaat lagaataa he. tarun ke liye yah pal bahut vichlit or vivashtaa bhare hue hote he, mohammad use jajbaati hokar kisi bhi jaldbaaji karne se manaa karata he or unke agali harakat tak ve bhavaheen hokar intjaar karte he. lekin biju ko tadapata dekh unka sipaahi use marphin kii syringe dene kii naakaam koshish main bodo leader use maar giraata he, biju dard se tadapata hua aakhir main mar jaataa he jahaan bodo gut ke saamne aate hi mike, bhikhu or tarun un par goliyon kii bauchhaar karte he, gut ke log maare jate hai lekin leader apane kuch logo saath bhaag nikalataa he. bhikhu peechha karne dauraan ghayal hota he. or phir raat ghirne par mike or tarun andhere main bodo daste ko ek-ek kar khatm karata he, leader apane do bandookdhaariyon saath naav main nadi paar karne ke kram main mike or tarun ke haathon pakade jate he, is hathapayee main mike leader kii gardan kaat detaa he, tarun bhi ek ko khatm kar tisare ko dhund nikaalata he, lekin uskaa dushman ek solah saal kaa ladkaa miltaa he.
 medical liv ke baad tarun apane paitruk gaanv haryana lautataa he, jahaan uske dost or parivaar garmjoshi se uskaa swaagat karte he. isi ke lakshi narayan se byaahne ke liye apane pita ko rajamand karata he. par chunki lakshi ek computer ejeeniyar hone ke saath kaaphi swatantra vichaaron kii he, tarun use prabhavit karne ke liye uske anuroop badalane kii koshish karata he. jald hi donon kii sagaai bhi hoti he, lekin phir dakkan kii posting milane par tarun usase dubaara milane or shaadi karne kaa pran detaa he.
 dakshin bhartiya rajya andhrapradesh main naksaliyon ke badhate hinsak varchasv ko rokane kii aise hi muhim main tarun or mike/mohammad ko apane senadhikari karnal ke parivaar or bacchon ko hyderabad le jane ke raste main naksaliyon kaa ek dasta unki sawari trek par hamalaa karte he.
