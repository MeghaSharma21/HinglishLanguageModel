 praarambhik pashchimi dharmik sangeet aksar saamanantar purna antaraalon ko vishisht banaate hai; ye antaraal mul planesong kii spashtataa ko sangrakshit karte hai. ye kaam cathedral main kiye or pradarshit kiye gaye or hormoni paidaa karne ke liye sambandhit cathedral main inke gunjyman rup kaa upyog kiya gaya. jaise hi poliphoni viksit hua, samaanaantar antaraalon ke upyog kaa sthaan dhire-dhire anuroopya kii angreji style ne le liya, jisane tisare yaa chathe kaa istemaal kiya. aisa mana jaataa he ki angreji style main apekshaakrit mithi dhwani hoti he or us polphoni me zyada anukool hoti he jo part-riting main adhik lenior lacheelaapan pradaan karata he. praarambhik sangeet ne tritone ke upyog par bhi roak laga diya, kyonki isaki karkashta shaitan se sambandhit thi or sangeetkaar aksar iske upyog se bachane ke liye mujika phicta ke madhyam se adhik lambaai kii ore chale jate the. nai triedic hormonic pranali main, haalanki, tritone sweekruti yogya ho gaya, kyonki vyavhaarik karkashta ke maanakeekaran ne prabhaavshaalee cord main iske upyog ko vaanchhneeya bana diya.
 yadhyapi adhikansh swar-sangati do yaa adik note ke ek saath dhvanit hone ke parinamswaroop ghatit hoti he, arpegios yaa hocket ke upyog dwaara keval ek molodic line ke saath swar-sangati ko majbootee ke saath suchit kiya ja saktaa he. ekal string vaadyayantron ke liye barok yug ke bahut saare note jaise ki ekal vyleen ke liye back kaa sonata or partits, purna cordal sanrachanaon kii bajaay parinaam ke dwaara uchit swar-sangati kii soochna dete hai; neeche dekhen:

 vistaarit antaraal banaae jate hai or nimnlikhit ke rup main jane jate hai:

 samaswar antaraalon ko samanswar, saptak, paanchavaa, chauthaa evam menger evam miner third mana jaataa he. tisare ko apurna mana jaataa he, jabaki purvavarti ko purna mana jaataa he. shastreey sangeet main chauthe ko karkash mana ja saktaa he jab iskaa kaary contrapuntal hota he.

 pashchimi parampara main, swar-sangati cord kaa upyog karte hue kushalta purvak upyog main laai jaati he, jo peach shreniyon kaa sangyojan hoti hai. tersian yaa tertial swar-sangati main, ek tisare swar ke antaraal par namit, cord ke sadsy major or miner tisare swar antaraalon ko krambaddh karne ke dwaara paae or namit kiye jate hai, "mul" se shuru hokar, iske baad mul par "teesraa swar" or mul par "paanchavaa swar", ityadi.kripaya dhyaan den ki cord ke sadsy mul ke antaraalon ke aadhaar par namit kiye jate hai, cord banane main unke sankhyatmak antarveshan ke dwaara nahin. paramparaagat rup se, cord kahalaane ke liye ek cord ke paas kam se kam teen sadsy hone chahie, haalanki 2-sadsy diaids ko kabhi-kabhi cord maan liya jaataa he, visheshkar rock main . teen sadasyon vile cord ko triad kaha jaataa he kyonki isamen teen sadsy hote hai, isliye nahin ki ise aavashyak rup se tisare main nirmit kiya jaataa he . antaraal kii chaudaai ke aadhaar par kiye ja rahe krambaddh suron ke kaaran vibhinn kism ke cord banate hai. lokapriy or jaij swar-sangati main, cord ke naam unke mul ke saath hi unke gunvattta ke vibhinn sambandhon or charitron kaa sanket dene vaale ke aadhaar par rakhaa jaataa he. namavali ko jitna sambhav ho sake utana saral rakhane ke liye kuch vyatikram sweekrut kiye jate hai . udhaaharan ke liye, ek si major triad ke si, i, ji cord sadasyon ko vyatikram dwaara saadharantaya "si" cord kaha jaataa he. ek 'e ♭ "cord" main, e♭, si or i♭ sadsy hote hai.
 sangeet ke kai prakaaron, vishesh rup se barok or jaij main, cord aksar "tanaavon" ke saath sambandhit hote hai. tanaav ek atirikt cord sadsy hota he jo ek yaa adhik cord sadasyon ke sambandh main apekshaakrit karkash antaraal utpann karata he. tisare swaron ke stack dwaara cord nirmaan ke tersian chalan kaa anusaran karte hue, maujuud mul swar, tisare or paanchave swar ke upar sabase saral pratham tanaav ko stanking dwaara ek triad se joda jaataa he, anya tisare swar ko paanchave swar ke upar, ek nayaa swar dete hue, mul swar se alag ek saatven swar kaa shashakt rup se karkash sadsy or is tarah cord kaa "saatvaan" swar kaha jaataa he or ek four-note cord paidaa karata he, jise "saatvaan cord" kaha jaataa he. cord nirmaan ke liye akele tisare swar ke stack kii chaudaai par nirbhar karte hue, cord ke saatven swar or mul swar ke bich kaa antaraal major, miner yaa kam kiya ja saktaa he. . namavali isaki anumati detii he, vyatikram rup se, "si7" mul swar, teesraa or paanchavaa yukt cord kaa sanket detaa he or saatven swar si, i, ji or bi ♭ likhaa jaataa he. saatven cord ke anya prakaaron ko adhik spasht tareeke se namit kiya janaa chahie, jaise ki "si major 7", "si sanvardhit 7", ityadi.namakaran kii purna pradarshnee ke liye dekhen cord .
 saatven cord ke upar tisare swar kaa satat stack "vistaarit tanaav" yaa "upree tanaav", nauven, gyarahawein or terahaven ko laate hai or unke upar naam rakhe gaye cord kaa nirmaan karte hai. . terahavin ke pare kaa ekstansion maujuuda cord sadasyon ko dubaara paidaa karata he or namavali ke baahar chhod diya jaataa he. vistaarit cord par aadhaarit jatil swar-sangati jaij, aadhunik arcestra se sambandhit kaamon, film sangeet ityadi main bahutayat main pai jaati he.
 aamtaur par, shastreey sangeet kii aam chalan avadhi main ek karkash cord samaswar cord main "rijolve" ho jayega. swar-sangatikaran aamtaur par kaan ke liye sukhad lagata he jab samaswar or karkash dhwani ke bich ek santulan rahata he. saral shabdon main, vah tab ghatit hota he jab "tanaav" or "vishram" ke kshanon main santulan hota he. is kaaran se, aamtaur par tanaav paidaa kiya jaataa he or iske baad usko 'khatm' kiya jaataa he.
 tanaav kii taiyaari kaa matlab he samaswar cord kii shrinkhalaa ko rakhana he, jo karkash swar tak jane kaa maarg prashast karata he. is tarah se sangeetkaar aasaani se or shrota ko pareshaan kiye bina, tanaav kaa parichay karana sunishchit karata he. ek baar pis apane up charmotkarsh par pahunchataa he, shrota ko tanaav se chhootane ke liye ek pal kii jarurat padati he jise samaswar bajaakar praapt kiya jaataa he jo purvavarti cord ke tanaav ko khatm kar detaa he. is tanaav kaa samashodhan aamtaur par shrota ke liye sukhad lagata he.
 swar-sangati anuroopya par aadhaarit he, ek avadharana jisaki paribhaasha pashchimi sangeet ke itihaas ke dauraan kai baar badal chuki he. ek manovaigyanik ke drushtikon main, anuroopya satat parivartansheel he. anuroopya ek vistrut range main bhinn ho sakati hai. ek hi cord vibhinn kaarnon se samaswar lag saktaa he.
 ek to avadharanatmak khurdarapan kii kami he. khurdarapan tab ghatit hota he jab parshals ek jatil bandwidth ke andar pad jate he, jo vibhinn aavritiyaan alag karne kii kaan kii kshamta kaa ek paimaana he. jatil bandwidth ucch aavritiyon main 2 or 3 semitons ke bich rahete hai or nimna aavritiyon par bade ho jate he. do samakalik swar-sangtiya jatil tone kaa khurdarapan or tone ke bich antaraal or swar-sangati vipulata par nirbhar karata he. chromatic scale main sabase khurdara antaraal miner dwitiya or iske major saatven kaa vyatikram he. kendriya range main theth varnakrameey invelp ke liye, doosraa sabase khurdara antaraal major dwitiya or major saatvaan or uske baad tritone, miner teesraa, major teesraa or purna chauthaa he.

 teesraa kaaran parichiti he. cord jo ki aksar sangeet sandarbhon main sune jate hai adhik samaswar dhwani utpann karte hai. yah siddhant pashchimi sangeet ke hormonic jatilta main kramik aitihaasik vruddhi kii vyakhya karata he. udhaaharan ke liye, 1600 ke aasapaas aprastut saatvaan cord dhire-dhire parichit ban gaya or isliye dhire-dhire adhik samaswar ke rup main mana jane laga.
 pashchimi sangeet major or miner triad par aadhaarit he. iskaa kaaran ki ye cord kyon kendriya hai, yah he ki ve sanlayan or khurdarapan kii kami donon ke sandarbh main samaswar hote hai. ve sanlayit hote hai kyonki unamem chauthaa/paanchvaan antaraal shaamil hai. unamem khurdarepan kii kami he kyonki unamem major or miner second ke antaraal kii kami hoti he. chromutik scale main in tonon kaa koi bhi sangyojan in mandandon ko santusht nahin karata he.
