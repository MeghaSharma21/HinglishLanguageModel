 bhartiya hockey team bharat kii rashtriya maidani hockey team he. 1928 main bhartiya hockey team antarrashtriya hockey mahasang kii pehali gaiyr europea sadsy team bani. 
 1928 main, team ne apana pehla olympic swarna padak jeeta or 1956 tak olympic main bhartiya purush team naabad rahi, lagaataar chah swarna padak jite. bhartiya hockey team ne abtak aath olympic swarna padak jite he, jo sabhi rashtriya tiimon se adhik he.
 bharat ne olympic main kul 11 padak jite hai jiname 8 swarna, ek rajat or do kaansya padak shaamil hai. varsh 1928 ke amstermud olympic main pehla swarna padak praapt kiya or olympic khelon main varsh 1928 se varsh 1956 tak lagaataar chah swarna padak jitkar itihaas rach diya tha. anya do swarna varsh 1964 main tokyo or varsh 1980 main moscow olympic main mili.
 asian khelon main bhartiya team ne teen baar swarna padak jeeta he. pehla swarna padak 1966 ke bainkak asiad main bharat ne pakistan ko 1-0 se haraa kar jeeta tha. doosraa swarna padak 1998 main bainkak main hue asian khelon main naraj pille kii aguwai main swarna jeeta tha. teesraa swarna padak asian khel 2014 main pakistan ko penlti shoot out main haraakar jeeta. iske saath hi team ne rio olympic 2016 ke liye kwalifai kar liya. 
 varttman main holland ke paul vine es bhartiya purush hockey team ke coach hai. iske pehle australia ke hockey team ke terry walsh coach niyukt the.
