 jagatseth, 'jagatshreshthi' shabd kaa apbhransh he. jodhpur rajya ke vanikvanshi hiranand sa ke saat putr the. saare desh main inaki hundi kaa vyapaar failaa tha. inke ek putr manikchandra ne dhaka main ek kothi banaai tathaa inhen se is vansh kaa naam failaa. ye bengal ke nawab murshid kuli khan ke kripapaatr, mitra evam daahine haath the.
 san 1715 main samrat mohammadshah ne dhankuber fatehchand ko jagatseth kii oopaadhi se vibhooshit kiya tathaa ek marakat mani bhi pradaan kii jisapar "jagatseth" ankit tha. aage chalakar inhone raajnneeti main vishesh bhaag liya. ye nawabon ko banane or bigaadane lage. alivardi khan se milakar sarfaraj khan kaa vinaash kiya or pun: sirajuddaula ko bengal se nikaalane tathaa mirajafar ko bhi hataane main inkaa haath tha. ant main mir kasim ne inke putron ko kaid karvaa liya or baad main unka vadh bhi kara diya. taduparant inke vanshajon ko badi musiibat ke din dekhne pade.
