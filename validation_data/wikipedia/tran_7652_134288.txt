 petlavad dhamaakaa 12 sitambar 2015 kii subah ko jhabua jile ke petlavad naamak nagar main hua tha. jisamen lagbhag 105 logon kii mrityu ho gai. iskaa kaaran khaanaa banane ke liye rakhe gas silendar ko bataaya gaya he.
 police ne is par kaha he ki isamen kul do dhamaake hue he. pehla rasoi gas ke dwaara or doosraa dhamaakaa pehle dhamaake ke kaaran jilignite naamak chad main aag lagane ke kaaran hua tha. isi chad ke kaaran hi kai logon kii mrityu hui. 
 isi dauraan grih mantri babulal gaur ghayalon kaa haalchaal puch ne aae. mukhya mantri shivaraj singh chouhan ne mare hue parivaar waalon ko ₹2,00,000 or ghayalon ko ₹50,000 dene kii ghoshnaa kii. iske baad raashtrapati pranab mukherjee or pradhan mantri narendra modi ne bhi is par apni pratikriya vyakt kii.
 jaanch ke dauraan police ne paaya ki rajendra kasava hi is ghatana kaa doshi he. usne gaiyr-kanoonee rup se apane ghar main jilignite ke chad ko rakhaa tha. isi ke kaaran imaarat main dhamaakaa hua. is tarah ke vastu kaa rahane vaale ilaake main rakhana gaiyr-kanoonee hota he. police ne us jagah ko band kar diya. khoj ke dauraan kai is tarah ke chad or dhamaake karne vaale padaarth praapt hue.
