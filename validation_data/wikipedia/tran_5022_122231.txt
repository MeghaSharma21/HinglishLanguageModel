 lijiang china ke unnan praant ke pashchimottaree bhaag main ek vibhaag-stareey shahar he. san 2010 main isaki aabaadi 12,44,769 anumit kii gai thi. yah unnan kii sichuaan praant ke saath lagi sarhad ke paas he or chingei-tibet pathaar or unnan-guizou pathaar ke sangam par sthit he. yahan kaa mausam bahut acha mana jaataa he haalanki kabhi-kabhaar sardiyon main taapmaana shunya se neeche chalaa jaataa he. lijing main nashi samudaay ke bahut se log rahete hai, jinke apane reeti-rivaaj hai or jo kisi jamaane main bharat-tibet vyapaar main ahm bhoomika nibhaate the.
