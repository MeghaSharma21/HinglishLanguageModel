 jeevigyaan main samamiti kisi jeev main samaan rup ke angon kii santulit upasthiti ko kehete hai, masalan manushyon main santulit vyavastha se ek bayen or usee ke jaisaa ek daayan haath hota he. jeevigyaan main kai prakaar kii samamiti dekhi jaati he or aitihaasik rup main iskaa jeevvaigyanik vargeekaran main kaafii mahatv rahaa he.
 vyasiya samamiti aise jeevon main pai jaati he jinhen agar unke kendra se nikalne vaale kisi kaalpanik samtal dwaara kaata jaye to ve kai sthanon par unke kate hue bhaagon main qareeb-qareeb samamiti dekhi ja sakati he. udhaaharan ke liye taramin main vyasiya samamiti hoti he. aise jeevon kii koi dai-bai taraf nahin hoti.
 dwibhageeya samamiti un jeevon main pai jaati he jinhen agar bich main se upar se neeche ek samtal kaate to do lagbhag ek jaise baraabar ke hisse ban jate hai. yah samamiti maanvon, kutton, ityadi main dekhi jaati he. kram-vikaas ke najariye se dwibhageeya samamiti praaniyon ke panpane ke liye bahut hi safal rahi he or vartmaankaal main prithvi ke 99% praaniyon main dwibhageeya samamiti dekhi jaati he.
 kuch tenophora jaise jeevon main dvivyasiya samamiti dekhi jaati he jo vyasiya or dwibhageeya samamiti kaa mail he. isamen jeev ko apane kendra ke ird-gird susajjit chaar bhaagon main baanta ja saktaa he jisamen ulte chor ke do bhaag ek-jaise hote hai lekin ve anya do bhaagon se alag hote hai, jo ki swayan aapas main ek-se hote hai. kuch jeevvaigyanikon kaa mat he ki jivan ke kram-vikaas main yah vyasiya or dwibhageeya samamiti ke bich kaa padaav he, yani armbh main jeevon main vyasiya samamiti hi thi, jis se phir dvivyasiya samamiti viksit hui jo aage chalakar dwibhageeya samamiti main viksit hui.
 saare jeevon main samamiti nahin hoti. kuch aise bhi jeev hai jinamein asamamiti milti he. kuch had tak samamiti-vaale jeevon main bhi asamamiti hoti he kyonki unke ek-jaise bhaagon main bhi zaraa-bahut antar to hota hi he, masalan kisi maanav ke donon haathon main thodaa sa antar hota hi he or maanvon kaa hriday bhi sharir ke keval baayein bhaag main hota he. lekin kuch spanj or anya jeev aise hote hai jinaki sharir-rachana main koi bhi samamiti nahin hoti. dhyaan den ki kuch anya spanjon main vyasiya samamiti dekhi jaati he.
