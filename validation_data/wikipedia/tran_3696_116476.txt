 sandhi shabd kaa arth he 'mail'. do nikatavarti varnon ke paraspar mail se jo vikaar hota he vah sandhi kehalaata he. jaise - sam + tosh = santosh ; dev + indra = devendra ; bhanu + udai = bhaanuday. 
 sandhi teen prakaar kii hoti hai -
 do swaron ke mail se hone vaale vikaar ko swar-sandhi kehete hai. jaise - vidya + aalay = vidhyalay.
 swar-sandhi paanch prakaar kii hoti hai -
 sutara-ak: savarne deergh:arthath ak pratyaahaar ke baad uskaa savarna aaye to dono milakar deergh ban jate hai.hrasv yaa deergh a, i, u ke baad yadi hrasv yaa deergh a, i, u aa jaaen to donon milakar deergh aa, i or uu ho jate hai. jaise -
  a/aa + a/aa = aa
  i or i kii sandhi
  u or uu kii sandhi
 isamen a, aa ke aage i, i ho to e ; u, uu ho to o tathaa ri ho to ar ho jaataa he. ise gun-sandhi kehete hai. jaise -
  a + i = e ; nar + indra = narendra
  a + u = o ; gyaan + upadesh = gyanopadesh ; 
  a + ri = ar dev + rishi = devarshi
  aa + ri = ar maha + rishi = maharishi
 a, aa kaa e, i se mail hone par i tathaa a, aa kaa o, ou se mail hone par ou ho jaataa he. ise vruddhi sandhi kehete hai. jaise -
  a + e = i ; ek + ek = ekaik ; 
  a + o = ou van + aushadhi = vanaushadhi ; aa + o = ou maha + aushadhi = mahaushadhi ;
  i, i ke aage koi vijaateey swar hone par i i ko ‘y’ ho jaataa he. 
  u, uu ke aage kisi vijaateey swar ke aane par u uu ko ‘v’ ho jaataa he. 
  ‘ri’ ke aage kisi vijaateey swar ke aane par ri ko ‘r’ ho jaataa he. inhen yan-sandhi kehete hai.
 i + a = y + a ; nadi + arpan = nadyarpan 
  
 u + e = v + e ; anu + eshan = anveshan 
 e, i or o ou se pare kisi bhi swar ke hone par kramshah ay, aay, av or aav ho jaataa he. ise ayaadi sandhi kehete hai.
  e + a = ay + a ; ne + an = nayan 
  i + a = aay + a ; gai + ak = gayak
  o + a = av + a ; po + an = pawan 
  ou + a = aav + a ; pau + ak = paavak
 ou + i = aav + i ; nau + ek = navik
