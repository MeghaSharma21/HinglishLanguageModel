 uttar pradesh kaa mukhyamantri uttar bharat ke rajya uttar pradesh kaa pramukh hota he. uttar pradesh ke mukhyamantriyon kii suchi yahan di gai he.
 uttar pradesh main ab tak 20 vyakti mukhyamantri rah chuke hai. in 20 vyaktiyon ke atarikt, teen vyakti rajya ke kaaryakari mukhyamantri bhi rahe hai jinka kaarykaal bahut chota he. vartmaan mukhyamantri yogi adityanath ji hai jo ki 19 march 2017 se is pad par asin hai.
 sanyukt praant, jiskaa mukhyalay elahabaad main tha, british bharat kaa ek praant tha. vartmaan main yah hissaa uttar pradesh va uttrakhand main he.
 bharat kii swatantraaa ke pashchaat sanyukt praant uttar pradesh naam se janaa jane laga. 26 janvari 1950 ko sanyukt praant ke pradhan govind vallabh pant rajya ke pehle mukhyamantri bane.
