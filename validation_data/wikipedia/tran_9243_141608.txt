 saamaajik media vipanan sanghathanon ke ekikrut vipanan sanchaar yojanaon kaa ek naveen ghatak he. ekikrut vipanan sanchaar apane lakshith bazaaron ke saath judane ke liye sanghathanon dwaara paalan kiya ja rahaa ek siddhant he. ekikrut vipanan sanchaar graahak kendrit sandesh taiyaar karne ke liye mishrit prachaar tatv - yatha vigyaapan, vyaktigat bikri, jan sampark, prachaar, pratyaksh vipanan or bikri protsaahan ko samanvit karata he. paaramparik vipanan sanchaar model main, saamagree, aavriti, samay, sanghathan dwaara sanchaar madhyam ek bahari agent, yani vigyaapan agenciyaan, vipanan anusandhan farm or jan sampark farm ke sahyog ke saath hai. tathaapi, saamaajik media ke vikaas ne graahakon ke saath sanghathanon kii sampreshan paddati ko prabhavit kiya he. web 2.0 ke udbhav main internet, logon ko online saamaajik or vyapaar sambandh banane, soochna saajha karne or pariyojnaon par sahyog karne ke liye upkarnon kaa ek set uplabdh karaata he.
 saamaajik media vipanan kaarykram aam taur par aisi saamagree taiyaar karne ke prayaason par kendrit hote hai jo dhyaan aakarshith kare, online samvaad janit kare or pathakon ko apane saamaajik network ke saath unhen saajha karne ke liye protsaahit kare. sandesh upyogkarta se upyogkarta ke bich failata he or sambhawatah pratidhwanit hota he, chunki yah brands yaa company ke bajaay ek vishwasaneey strot se aa rahaa he.
 saamaajik media ek aisa manch ban gaya he jo aasaani se internet tak pahunch rakhane vaale kisi bhi vyakti ke liye sulabh he, jo sanghathanon ko apane brands ke prati jaagrukta badhaane ke avasar dete hue, graahakon ke saath baatcheet kii suvidha pradaan karata he. iske atirikt, saamaajik media sanghathanon ko apane vipanan abhiyaan ko karyanvit karne ke liye ek apekshaakrit saste manch ke rup main kaary karata he. sanghathan apane graahakon or lakshith bazaaron se sidhe pratikriya praapt kar sakate hai.
 saamaajik media vipanan graahak kii madad ke liye atirikt madhyam, graahak or pratispardhi antardrushti paane kaa saadhan or online unki pratishtha ke prabandhan kii vidhi uplabdh karaate hue sanghathan or vyaktiyon ko laabh pahunchaata he. uski safalataa ko sunishchit karne vaale mahatvapurn kaarak hai uskaa apane graahak ke liye praasangik hona, unako vah jo mulya pradaan karata he or us neenv kii majbooti jis par vah nirmit he. ek majboot neenv ek aadhaar yaa manch kaa kaary karata he jis par sanghathan apni soochna ko kendrikrut kar sake or lekh tathaa press vijyaptiyon ke prakaashan jaise anya saamaajik media pranaaliyon ke madhyam se apane haal ke vikaas kii ore graahakon ko nirdeshit kar sake. 
 sabase lokapriy manchon main shaamil hai:
 ek majboot neenv kaa lakshya ek aise manch kaa nirmaan he jo apane graahakon ko sanghathan ke saath sampreshan kaa mauqaa dete hue unhen aakarshith karata or samarth banaata he. yah manch isliye mahatvapurn he kyonki yah sanghathan ko apane graahakon par sanghathan ke prabhaavon ko maapne or un par nigaraanee rakhane kaa bhi avasar detaa he. sabhi manch har sanghathan ke prayaason ke liye anukool banaae gaye hai, jahaan manch dwaara prastut mahatvapurn avasaron or chunautiyon jaise karkon ko dhyaan main rakhte hue, praathmik janasankhyikee ko lakshith kiya ja rahaa he or apane graahakon se praapt soochna ke akalan ke liye metrix kaa istemaal kiya ja rahaa he. kuch manchon dwaara prastut upkaran unke graahakon dwaara nirdhaarit lakshyon ko praapt karne main dusron kii tulana main adhik upyukt hai. is tarah ke upkarnon or graahakon ke udhaaharan nimnatah hai:
 sanghathanon dwaara vipanan ke liye adhik prabhavi rup se saamaajik media kaa upyog karne ke liye, unhen yah samajhnaa or sweekaar karnaa hoga ki ubharate manch vartmaan vipanan pahal ke pratisthaapan ke bajaay, unke samgr vipanan sadhano ke anupoorak or vistaar hai. ve lakshya, jinke liye saamaajik media kaa istemaal kiya ja saktaa he:
 sanghathanon ko safalataa ke liye saamaajik media hetu apane lakshyon ko paribhaashit karne kii zarurat he. manchon ke bich lakshya bhinn ho sakate hai. udhaaharan ke liye, graahak seva or prasaaran adhyatanikaran ke liye Facebook kaa istemaal kiya ja saktaa he jabaki prachaar ke liye Twitter kaa upyog ho saktaa he. YouTube sanghathan ke parade ke peeche kaa nazaaraa dikhaa saktaa he. safalataa kaarak lakshyon par nirbhar karata he: ek lakshya ho saktaa he prachaar ke parinamswaroop nae graahakon kii ek vishisht sankhya; ek or lakshya ho saktaa he kisi graahak seva sthiti main ek nirdisht samay ke andar sabhi graahakon kii shikayaton kaa jawaab.
 chunki saamaajik media tej gati se ubhar rahaa he, sanghathan aksar samrik ke bajaay swabhaavgat upasthiti sthapit karte hai. zyaadaatar maamalon main, yah vikaas us had tak upyukt he jahaan sanghathan ko apane saamaajik media ke upyog or samgr vipanan yojana main ekikaran ke liye ranneeti viksit karne kii aavashyaktaa he.
 saamaajik media aabaadi ke kranthik jansamooh dwaara apanaaya gaya he, jiskaa matlab he ki saamaajik media main upasthiti sanghathanon ke liye mahatvapurn he. aam taur par, bilkul nahin kii jagah thodi bahut aadhikarik upasthiti behatar he, bhale hi ek vishesh ranneeti paribhaashit nahin kii gai ho. ranneeti or yojana ke saath yaa uske bagair-anya vipanan pahal ke samaan hi-saamaajik media ke prayaas safal yaa asafal ho sakate hai.
 adhikansh sanghathanon ke paas unke vipanan prayaason ke liye karmchaaree maujuud hai. jansampark, vigyaapan, web or mudrit sanchaar aise kshetra hai jinke prati carmik or sansaadhan samarpit hai. haalanki saamaajik media, vipanan ranneetiyon main haal hi kaa kshetra he, tathaapi adhik safal sanghathanon ne saamaajik media se samanvay sthapit karne or uske upyog main madad ke liye samarpit karmchaariyon ko rakhaa he. in vyaktiyon ko samprati lokapriy upkarnon kaa istemaal karnaa hoga, lekin jab bhi nae saadhan aae or viksit hon, un nae ubharate sadhano ke saath prayog karne kii unhen chhot rahegi.
 kuch sanghathan graahakon or saamaanya logon ke man main brands ko tazaa rakhane ke liye apni vipanan yojana main saamaajik media kaa sakriy rup se upyog karte hai. ek saamaajik media site par sanghathan ke saath judane se, sanghathan ko aise vyaktiyon ke saath jude rahane kaa mauqaa miltaa he, jo unke graahak ho bhi sakate hai yaa nahin bhi, bajaay iske ki jab unhen atirikt soochna kii talash ho tabhi unase sampark ke liye us par nirbhar hon.
 saamaajik media ek vishaal vigyaapan manch he. sanghathan saamaajik media par saajha vishisht dilchaspiyon ke aadhaar par vyaktiyon ko lakshith kar sakate hai. udhaaharan ke liye, yadi ek vyakti jogging ke bare main ek YouTube video dekhtaa he, ek juuta company ek vigyaapan de sakati he, yaa ek coffee company aise vyaktiyon ko lakshith karte hue vigyaapan de sakati he, jo saamaajik media citon par post karte hai ki ve thake hai. saamaajik media ne vigyapandaataaon ko atyant sankeern rup se vigyaapan jaal parishkrut karne kaa mauka diya he.
 kuch sanghathanon ne saamaajik media vipanan pahal karyanvit karte samay faayde se adhik nuksaan hi pahunchaaya he; anya nae media manch par bahut safal rahe. unki safalataa kii kunjee thi, dwaar par hi korporate ravaiya kaa parityag. saamaajik media vipanan main, graahakon kii nazar pramanikta or sanghathanon ke saath ek vishwasaneey sambandh par he, jiske saath ve rishte bana rahe hai.
 Dell computer kii apane Direct2Dell manch sahit majboot bloging upasthiti he. na keval yah blowers ke liye nae utpaadon ke bare main jaanane kaa ek mauka he, balki isane sanghathan kii pratishtha ke sudhaar main bhi madad kii he. site kii shuruuaat ke baad se nakaaratmak blogon kii maatra main 49% se 22% tak giravat ai he. Dell apane graahakon ke saath judane or blog ke zarie tatkaal unki chintaaon, tippaniyon or prashnon kaa samaadhaan karne main sakasham rahaa he. Dell ne apane @DellOutlet khate ke madhyam se bhi micro bloging site Twitter par apni maujuudagi darj karai he or apane anuyaayiyon ko vishesh chhot kii peshkash dwaara Twitter se bikri main lagbhag $3 million arjeet kiye hai.
 Target graahakon ke saath Facebook par "vyaktigat sampark" sadhate hue or graahakon ko unke pasandida utpaada prastut karte hue safal rahaa he. Target ko is madhyam ko samajhane or graahakon ke saath judane ke liye faaydemand tareeqe se uske upyog main samay laga. unhone seekhney kaa prayaas kiya or samooh ko vyavasthit karne main graahakon ko madad kii anumati dekar vishesh rup se acha kaary-nishpaadan darshaya.
 Starbucks ko Facebook, YouTube, Flickr, Twitter or unke apane bloging site My Starbucks Idea par paaya ja saktaa he. Starbucks ko sabase achche saamaajik media ranneeti rakhane waalon main se ek mana jaataa he. iskaa dhyaan maujuuda companiyon kii aavashyaktaaon, zaruuraton or pasand or nae graahak paane main madad ke liye us rishte ke nirmaan par kendrit he.
 Old Spice ke 'Man your man could smell like' jaise vigyapanon kii jabardast safalataa ke baad Old Spice ne mangalvaar 13 june or buudhvaar 14 june 2010 ko vastav main paraspar kriyaatmak viral abhiyaan chalaaya. unhone fasebuk, twitor, utyub, Yahoo Answers, Reddit, 4chaiyn or kai anya jagahon se "Old Spice Man" kirdaar ke prashnon ko sangrahit kiya or do dinon kii avadhi main, Youtube par 180 video pratikriyaaon ko post kiya. pratikriyaaon main Twitter anuyaayi kii ore se shaadi kaa prastaav or Old Spice Man kii bhoomika nibhaane vaale abhinayta kaa apni beti ko sandesh shaamil tha. video ne apane pehle 24 ghanton main kul milakar lagbhag 6 million vus hasil kiye, jisane use haal kaa sabase lokapriy viral video banaaya.
 Volkswagen, MySpace par apane prayaas main asafal rahaa tha. ve ek vigyaapan main bhoomika ke liye dekh rahe the jo ve sahi disha main nirdeshit kar rahe the, lekin unki vifaltaa kaa kaaran tha prishth kaa anurakshan na karte hue, anya sanghathanon ko unhen apane abhiyaan se bhatka kar jagah gherne kaa avasar dena.
 Wal-Mart ne apane graahakon ko khulaa samvaad karne kaa mauqaa nahin diya. iske bajaay, unhone wal par tippaniyon kii posting ko pratibandhit kiya. unhone mahasoos kiya ki isse nakaaratmak tippaniyon par niyantran rakhane main madad milegi; jabaki, graahakon ne jald wal todate hue kai nakaaratmak post darj kiye. saamaajik media vipanan main Wal-Mart kii vifaltaa main judane vaala ek or kaarak tha ki unhone apni mul dakshataon kaa paalan nahin kiya. Wal-Mart ke Facebook prishth par "Save Money. Live Better" kaa koi zikr nahin tha. iske bajaay unhone style or faishan par prishth ko kendrit kiya.
 Nestlé paryaavaranvaadiyon ke saath vivaadaaspad sthiti main fansaa he, jo jald hi Facebook par unase chidh gaye or Twitter par ek "twitstorm" kii shuruuaat kii. iske atirikt, ek YouTube video banaaya gaya jo Nestlé dwaara tad ke tel ke nirantar prayog or varsha-van ke vinaash ke kaaran un par prahaar karata he. Nestlé ne apane prashansakon se apane wal par keval sakaaratmak tippaniyan post karne ke anurodh ke saath is nakaaratmakataa ke prati jaise-taise pratikriya jataai.
 Facebook ne in janasankhyikee kaa istemaal apane laabh ke liye karne kaa nirnay liya. Facebook kanoonee taur par apane website par post kii gai soochna par sarvadhikaar rakhata he or usne ek vigyaapan kaarykram taiyaar kiya jo us soochna ke samoohan dwaara Facebook ke prayoktaaon ko vyaktigat rup se or unako aakarshith karne vaale vigyaapan dwaara har vyakti ko lakshith karata he. is ranneeti ne vigyaapan kii duniya main ek sansani failaai. is prakaar ke vigyaapan se pehle, pop-ap or binar main tatv kii kami ke kaaran online vigyaapan kabhi bahut zyaada prabhavi nahin the. internet kaa upyog karte samay adhikansh logon ne ise kshtprad or dhyaan bhang karne vaala paaya. Facebook dwaara 400 million prayoktaaon se majboot apni website par prayek vyakti ko lakshith karne ke saath, usne har company ke liye ek lakshya bazaar taiyaar kiya. isliye agar ek bridal company Facebook par vigyaapit karnaa chaahati thi to vah sachmuch sunishchit kar sakati thi ki unke vigyaapan keval ve log dekh saken jinaki sagaai hui he yaa jo dating par hon, is aadhaar par ki ve kitne aage janaa chaahate hon. isane Facebook ko prati-clic-bhugtan ranneeti ke upyog kii shuruuaat karne kaa mauqaa diya ki internet upyogkartaaon par prabhaav kii kami kii vajah se adhikansh vigyapandaata sankoch karte hai.
 Facebook ke liye pe-par-clic ranneeti ke parinamswaroop, jab bhi koi Facebook prayokta ek vigyaapan par clic karata he, to har baar company vigyaapan ke liye Facebook ko bhugtan karti he. adhyayanon se pataa chalaa he ki unsattar pratishat se adhik online kharidaar Facebook jaise saamaajik media kaa upyog na keval logon tak pahunchane ke liye karte hai, balki saath hi yah bhi dekhne ke liye ki kya lokapriy he or kya faishneble chiz he. mul prati-clic-bhugtan ranneeti prabhavi nahin thi kyonki upyogkarta nahin chaahate the ki unhen ek or prishth par le jaaya jaae or vahaan par ve aisi koi chiz dekhen jo unke liye aakarshak naa ho. Facebook kii nai lakshyikaran pranali ke zarie prati upyogkarta clic kaa unka anupaat, kisi anya pratiyogi kii tulana main, yahaan tak ki Google se bhi, zyaada he. yah samaj main aata he kyonki vigyaapan is tathy ke liye aakarshak ban jaataa he ki unki sagaai ho chuki he yaa ve faishan main ruchi rakhte hai.
 Facebook ne na keval apane vigyapandaataaon tak pahunch ke liye, balki Facebook upyogkarta ke liye bhi angesement aids kahalaane vaale vigyaapan bhi taiyaar kiye. yah Facebook prayokta or saamaajik media ko teen adwitiya anubhav anumat karata he. pehle aap vigyaapan par tippanee kar sakate hai jo upyogkarta ko yah kehane kii chhot detaa he ki use vigyaapan yaa utpaada main kya pasand yaa naapsand he. ye tippaniyan Facebook prayokta ke anya mitra dekh sakate hai. jisase unke saamaajik samooh ko yah pataa chalata he ki yah yaa to bekaar he yaa bahut koob he or unhen uskaa upyog karnaa chahie. duusare, mitron ko online chote uphaar, muft i-tohfe dena ek lokapriy baat ho gai he. vigyapandaata ab muft i-uphaar bana sakate hai jo upyogkarta apane doston ko de sakate hai. ant main, upyogkarta apane pasandida utpaada or sanghathanon ke mitra ban sakate hai, jo unhen apane pasandida company ke Facebook prishth se jod saktaa he. yah unhen duniya bhar main aise logon ke samooh main daalata he jo isi tarah ke utpaadon kaa anand uthaate hai or Apple jaisi companiyon ko yah dekhne kaa mauqaa detaa he ki darasal unke lakshith darshak koun hai.
 Facebook par ek or pramukh vipanan sangyojan he anuprayog. aaj tak Facebook par sabase adhik prayukt kuch madon main se ek he anuprayog. anuprayog kaa ek badhiya udhaaharan he Farmville. Farmville, jiske 80 million se adhik upyogkarta he, jo Twitter kii website par samprati maujuud prayoktaaon se zyaada he. Facebook or uske vigyaapan par is tarah ke anuprayog kii shakti ab tak kabhi suni nahin gai. sanghathan in-game madon ko taiyaar kar sakate hai jo upyogkarta ke farm kii madad karte hai or jo koi vyakti us farm kaa daura karata he vah us vyakti ke farm par mad, company kaa vigyaapan dekh saktaa he. yah ek prabhavi vipanan ranneeti he kyonki kai log ise "vipanan" ke rup main nahin lete, balki maanate hai ki company is khel main aapaki madad kar rahaa he. yah isliye bhi prabhavi he kyonki Farmville ke upyogkartaaon kii ek vistrut shrinkhalaa he or unke site kaa daura karne vaale har koi vigyaapan ko dekhenge.
