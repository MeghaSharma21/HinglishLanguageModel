 prakritik drishy bhoomi ke kisi ek hisse kii suspasht visheshtaayen hai, jinamein iske prakritik swaroopon ke bhautik tatva, jal nikaay jaise ki nadiyaan, jhilein evam samudr, prakritik rup se ugnevaali vanaspatiyon sahit dharti par rahane vaale jeev-jantu, mitti se bani upyogi maanav nirmit vastuon sahit bhawan evam sanrachanaayen or asthaayee tatva jaise ki vidyut vyavastha evam mausam sambandhi paristhitiyaan shaamil hai | maanviya samskruti kii chhavi, jo banane main sahasrabdiyaan lag jaati hai, donon milakar drishy kisi bhi sthal ke log tathaa vah sthal donon kii sthaneey tathaa rashtriya pahchaan ko pratibimbit karte hai. prakritik drishy, inaki visheshta or gunvattta, kisi kshetra kii aatm chhavi or, us sthaan kii bhaavnaatmak anubhutiyaan, jo ise duusare kshetron se alag karti he, ko paribhaashit karne main madad karti he. yah logon ke jivan kii gatisheel prishthbhumi he. 
 prithvi par prakritik drishyon kaa ek vyapak vistaar he jisamen dhruviya kshetron ke barfile prakritik drishy, pahari prakritik drishy, vistrut marusthaleey prakritik drishy, dwipon or samudrataton caprakritik drishy, ghaney jangalon yaa pedo ke prakritik drishyon sahit puraane udichy van evam ushnkaribandheey varshaavan or shitoshna evam ushnkaribandheey kshetron ke krishi yogya prakritik drishy shaamil hai. 
 prakritik drishyon kii samiksha aage . in shabdon kaa sandarbh bhoomi par maanav nirmit parisaron kii ek pranali se he - kuch parisar jaise ki simaankit khet, jinhen haalanki baadon yaa deevaaron se paribhaashit nahin kiya ja saktaa. ise ek prakritik ikai kah sakate hai, iskaa sandarbh bhoomi ke kisi kshetra yaa hisse jaise ki kisi nadi kii ghati yaa kabhi adivaasiyon ke kabje main rahi athwa baad main samprabhu saamanton ke adhipaty main aayi parvaton kii shrinkhalaa se bhi he. arth kii drushti se yah shabd german landshaft ke samatuly he jo choti prashasnic ikai yaa kshetra ko sanderbhit karata he. baad main yah shabd upyog se baahar ho gaya or 11veen sadi main dumsde book ke samay tak yah shabd latin bhashaa ke kisi bhi anuwaad main dikhaai nahin diya. 
 is shabd kaa aadhunik swarup apni drishyaatmak vyakhyaaon sahit 16veen shatabdi main saamne aayaa jab landscape shabd kaa parichay antardeshiy prakritik yaa gramin bhudrishy kii chitrakaariyon ke sandarbh main dach chitrakaaron dwaara karaayaa gaya. landscape ko sabase pehle 1598 main darj kiya gaya tha, jise 16veen sadi ke dauraan ek chitrakaar sambandhi shabd ke rup main dach bhashaa se liya gaya tha, jab dach chitrakaar prakritik drishy nirmaan kala ke kshetra main paarangat hone kii sthiti main the. pehle dach shabd landscap kaa matlab keval bhoomi kaa ek kshetra yaa hissaa tha lekin phir ise kalatmak bhav ke liye apana liya gaya, jise angreji main bhoomi par maujuud kisi drishyaatmak chitra kii vyakhya ke liye shaamil kiya gaya. 
 jackson ke anusaar: "harrison dwaara britain kii vyakhya ke saath 1577 ke baad se, prakritik drishy kii soundaryatmak prakruti kii nayi vyakhya ek nae tarah ke nikhare sthalakritik lekhan ke rup main ubharkar saamne aayi...". multah is shabd kaa landskip ke rup main anuwaad kiya gaya jise oxford english dictionari ne shabd kaa ek apbhransh swarup bataaya, phir dhire-dhire isaki jagah landscape ne le lee. bhautikeey prakritik drishy ke liye angreji shabd ke upyog kaa zikr pehle kahin darj nahin he.
 german shabd landshaft par ek gahan vishleshan par dhyaan kendrit karte hue ek richard hartshorn ne landscape ko ek "prithvi kii ek bahari, dikhaai dene yogya sataha ke rup main paribhaashit kiya. yah sataha un bahari satahon se nirmit he jo vaataavaran, vanaspatiyon, banjar bhoomi, barf, yaa jaleey jeevon yaa maanav nirmit suvidhaon ke tatkaal sampark main aati he. 
 hartshorn ne is shabd ko rigen se alag rakhaa jo unki samaj main ek adhik bade or lacheele aakaar kaa he. unhone akaash ko is aadhaar par hataa diya kyonki vayumandal keval maatr vah madhyam he jiske jariye prithvi kii sataha ko dekha jaataa he or isamen bhumigat khanij utpaadan kaary, wanon ke neeche kii mitti or varsha shaamil nahin he. haalanki, unhone chalyman vastuon ko is tipanni ke saath shaamil kiya ki broadway kaa koi drishy iske trephic ke bagaiyr adhuraa he. unhone prakritik drishy main mahasaagaron ko shaamil kiye jane ko nazarandaaj kar diya. unhone drishyon ke atirikt prakritik drishyon kii vyakhya, jaise us bhoomi par aawaaj or gandh, kaa virodh kiya kyonki ye sabhi kisi ekikrut avadharana ko spasht nahin karte. prakritik or saanskrithik mahatv vaale prakritik drishyon kii avadharana ke sandarbh main karl saura ne dusron se alag dharna rakhi, unhone kaha "jaise hi drishy main insaan kii maujuudagi hoti he, prakritik drishy kaa swaabhaavik astitv samaapt ho jaataa he". primeval landscape shabd kaa zikr karte hue unhone kaha ki jab iskaa arth maanav-purv kaa prakritik drishy ho saktaa he to varttman natural landscape kaa arth he "ek aisi saidhantik pariklpana jo kabhi maujuud nahin thi".
 1920 or 1930 ke dashak ke dauraan, aisi kaary-pranaliyaan viksit kii gayin jisase prakritik drishy bhugol kaa vishisht nahin to anivaary vishay ban gaya. saura ke nazariye se yah vyakhya nikali ki bhugol kii bhoomika "prakritik drishy kii saiddhanthikta" kii silsilewar jaanch thi. saura ne prakritik drishyon ko mote taur par un kshetron ke rup main dekha jo bhautikeey evam prakritik, donon prakaar ke swaroopon kaa vishisht samooh he or prakritik drishy ke adhyayan ko swaabhaavik prakritik drishyon se saanskrithik mahatv ke prakritik drishyon main viksit hone kaa pataa lagaana kaha. 
 1940 ke dashak se, is avadharana ko maan kar paarit kar diya gaya tha kyonki bhautikvettaon ne paaya ki ateet kii punarrachana ke saath judi kathinaaiyaan bhayaavah hai or vartmaan duniya ke saath unki praathmik chintaa ko darshaati he. paryaavaran par maanviya prabhaav kii jaankaree hone ke saath-saath swaabhaavik prakritik drishy kii avadharana par teji se sawaal uthaaye jane lage. haal ke dinon ke bhautikvettaon ne maanviya bhugol ke antargat kisi sthaan kii vyaktiparak visheshtaon ko samajhaayaa he or is prakaar kisi kshetra ke vastuparak or vyaktiparak moolyankan ke bich ke pul ko paar kiya he. 
 shabdkoshon main bataayi gayi prakritik drishy kii lokapriy dharna ek vishesh or saamaanya arth detii he; prithvi kii sataha ke kisi kshetra kaa vishesh sandarbh or saamaanya arth he vah cheez jise kisi prekshak dwaara dekha ja sake. 
 haal ke dashakon main manovaigyanikon dwaara paryaavaran kii samaj par adhik se adhik dhyaan dene se, prakritik drishy ko us kachche maal ke rup main dekha jaataa he jisase maanviya dharanaon or maanviya soochna pranali kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. is prakaar danniel or cosgrove ne landscape ko ek bhautik shabdaawalee ke rup main nahin balki maanviya dharna kii saarvajanik abhivyakti ke rup main is prakaar paribhaashit kiya: "prakritik drishy ek saanskrithik chhavi he or aasapaas kii chizon ke pratinidhitiv, sanrachanaatmakataa yaa sanketan kaa ek sachitr madhyam he." minig ne manovaigyanik or bhautik, donon pahaluon ko sanyukt rup se dekha: "koi bhi prakritik drishy na keval hamaari aankhon ke saamne kii cheez he balki vah he jo hamaare mastishk ke andar maujuud he..
 haal ke dashakon main paryaavaran shabd kaa upyog kaaphi teji se badha he. je aplyton ne paryaavaran ko prakritik drishy se yah kehete hue alag rakhaa ki prakritik drishy "ek janaa-pahachaanaa parivesh" he. jaisaa ki borasa ne tipanni kii, prakritik drishy ke banispat paryaavaran shabd kaa ek laabh yah he ki paryaavaran ko shahari drishyon se adhik behatar samajha ja saktaa he, haalanki shahari prakritik drishy shabd bhi aam taur par upyog main he. chunki paryaavaran shabd kisi kshetra vishesh ke sampurna bhautik, jaivik, saanskrithik or saundarya ke ghatakon ko apane andar samahit kiye he, aam taur par ise atyant vyapak or prakritik drishy ke liye ek daayre ke andar kaa shabd samajha jaataa he.
 seen, sinik or sinari jaise shabd prakritik drishy kii sateek vyakhya prastut nahin karte hai. theatre main is shabd ke mul ko dekhen, jahaan seen kaa matlab he kisi naatak kaa ek hissaa, is prakaar seen kisi prakritik drishy kaa ek hissaa ho saktaa he. sinari, jo kisi stage par upyukt ek sajaavati prishthbhumi ko darshata he, saath hi yah kisi sthaan ke saamaanya swarup, visheshkar ek chitratmak sandarbh ko bataata he. haalanki prakritik drishy ke saath iskaa upyog ek duusare ke sthaan par kiya ja saktaa he lekin yah arth kii usee gaharaai ko vyakt nahin karata he. 
 prakritik drishy saundarya shaastra yaa sirf saundarya shaastra shabd kaa sahitya main aksar istemaal kiya jaataa he. prakritik drishy kii tulana main saundarya shaastra kaa mul kahin adhik vivaadaaspad he. ise greek shabd asthesis se liya gaya he, jiskaa arth he "bhaavnaatmak samaj." is shabd kaa upyog ek yuva german darshanik elexander baumgarton dwaara asthetica ke sheershak ke rup main kiya jaataa tha, jinhone is greek shabd kaa upyog galat tareeke se saundarya kii aalochana yaa swaad ke siddhant ke rup main kiya. is prakaar jo shabd vastav main bhaavnaatmak samaj ke vistrut kshetra par lagu hota he, use swaad ke kshetra tak hi simit kar diya gaya tha. emenual kaint ne 1781 main is upyog kii aalochana kii or ise iske sundartam arth "bhaavnaatmak samaj kaa darshanashastra" ke rup main istemaal kiya". haalanki, 1830 ke baad england main pravesh pate hi ashuddh shabd asthetics kii lokapriyata badhane lagi or oxford english dictionari ke anusaar, baumgarton dwaara iske arth kii nayi vyakhya ke ek sadi ke andar hi, samooche europe main iskaa vyapak rup se prayog hone laga tha. 
 saundarya shaastra kii dictionari vyakhya baumgarton kii truti ko dur karti he or ise "sochane yogya yaa abhautik chizon kii bajaae indriyon dwaara samajhi jane vaali chijen", "sundarata kii anubhuti se sambandhit yaa saundarya shaastra kaa vigyaan" maikeri dictionari, 1981., yaa "saundarya shaastra athwa sundarata ke arth main yaa isse sambandhit" chizon ke rup main paribhaashit karti he". saundarya shaastra ko darshanashastra kii ek aisi shakha ke rup main dekha jaataa he, jo "prakruti or swaad se kala ke niyamon evam siddhanton, lalit kala ke siddhant kaa pataa lagaataa he; saundarya vigyaan ..." athwa " saundarya kii prakruti ke saath or saundarya sambandhi nirnyon ke saath upyog main aata he". 
 is prakaar prakritik drishy saundarya kii samaj ke prati utsukata ko mitaane main saundarya shaastra ke vyapak dhaanche ke bheetar aksar ek jigyaasa kaa vishay rahaa he.
