 sachchiyaya mata ke naam se bhi jaani jaati he inkaa mandir jodhpur se 63 kimi dur osiyaan main sthit he. yah mandir jodhpur jile kaa sabase bada mandir he iskaa nirmaan 9veen yaa 10veen sadi main upendra ne karavaaya tha. sachchiyaya mata kii puja oswaal, jain, parmaar, panvar, kumavat, rajput, chaaran tathaa parik ityadi jatiyon ke log puujate hai. 
 sachchiyaya mata kaa purv kaa naam saachi tha tathaa ye asur raja pauloma kii beti thi. raja pauloma vrutra ke sena pramukh the. vrutra saachi se vivaah karnaa chaahati thi. parantu saachi usase vivaah nahin karnaa chaahati thi is kaaran pauloma ne vrutra kaa kaary chhod diya.
 pauloma dadhichi se vivaah karana chahta tha. iske pashchaat dadhichi or vrutra ke bich ghamaasan yuddh hua, yuddh main yah prastaav rakhaa gaya ki jo yuddh jitegaa vah saachi se vivaah karegaa. aakhir main vrutra ne yuddh jeeta or vivaah kiya.
 ek pathar shilaalekh jo osiyaan main mila tha vah kuch or kahani batalaati hai. jain dharm ke ek acharya shrimad vijay ratnaprabhasuriji ne osiyaan kii yatra kii thiinke anusaar osiyaan kaa purv kaa naam upakeshpur tha or yahan chamunda mata kaa ek mandir bhi tha. inkaa maananaa tha ki chamunda maan logon se bhainson kii bali maangati thi. jain sadhu vijay ratnaprabhasuriji is pratha par pratibandh lagane kaa prayaas kar rahe the. inkaa maananaa tha ki chamunda mata kaa doosraa naam hi tha.
 aaj vartmaan samay main sachchiyaya matake mandir main mithai, nariyal, kumkum, kesar, dhup, chandan, laapasi ityadi kaa chadava kiya jaataa he. bali kaa kahin zikr bhi nahin hota he.
