 jaljivshaala yaa kritrimajalashay, pani se bhare bartan, yaa kaanch ke hauj ko kehete hai, jisamen jivit jalcharon yaa paudhon ko rakhaa jaataa he. ye shalaays mukhyat: machhliyon ko paalane or unke kauthuk dekhne dikhaane ke kaam main aati hai.
 machhliyon ke paale jane ke pramaan kam se kam 4,500 varsh purv tak ke praapt hue hai, jab sumer nivasee bhojan ke liye unhen haujon yaa pokharon main paalate the. kintu sambhav he ki yah pratha isse bhi purv prachlit rahi ho. bharat main machhliyon ko paalna sarvapratham kab aarambh hua yah kahana kathin he, kintu asian deshon main se china main, shungavansh ke rajyakaal main laal machhliyon kaa kauthum or sajaavat ke liye paalan prarambh hua. chiniyon ne chote baratanon main rakhane yogya tathaa sajaavat ke uparyukt machhliyon kii vishesh jatiyon kaa vikaas kiya. inhone uttam naslon ke chunaav se jin jatiyon kii machhliyon kaa sanvardhan kiya unheen se aaj kii sundartam paaltu machhliyaan praapt hui hai. roman logon main bhi paaltu machhliyaan rakhane kaa varnan he. ye machhliyaan haujon, yaa chote talabon, main paali jaati thim. shishe ke baratanon yaa shaalaaon main machhlee paalan kii pratha 200 varshon se adhik puraani nahin he.
 jaljivshalas do prakaar kii hoti hai : saarvajanik tathaa vyaktigat. bhinn bhinn deshon ke anek mukhya nagaron main saarvajanik jaljivshalas sthapit hai. neuyark, chicago, sinfrancisco, landan, berlin, ityadi nagaron main badi badi jaljivshalas hai. inase choti, kintu prasiddh, jaljivshalas madras, hawaii dweep, austrailia, dakshini africa tathaa sanyukt rajya ke washington, philadelphia, boston, baltimor ityadi nagaron main hai. ye jaljivshalas mukhyat: janshiksha tathaa manoranjan ke liye hai, kuch main thodaa bahut vaigyanik khoj kaa kaam bhi kiya jaataa he. ve jaljivshalas alag hai, jinka prayojan mukhyat: vaigyanik anusandhan he. ye saadhaarant: vishaal hoti hai. inke saath janata ke vinodarth choti jeevashalas bhi praaia: rahati hai. inamen pradhan sanyukt rajya ke masusats pradesh ke woods hole naamak sthaan main, england ke plimuth tathaa italy ke nepuls nagar main hai. dekhne kii suvidha main vichaar se jaljivshaalaon kii deevaaren kaach kii banaain jaati hai. bade jalaashyon kii deevaaren ek se dedh inch mote kaach kii hoti hai.
 saarvajanik jaljivshaalaon kii dekhbhaal ke liye jal ko aavashyak taap tathaa rasinic sanrachana ko banaae rakhana tathaa jalajeevon ke swasthya, bhojan, rog or parjiviyon se sambandhit samasyaon kaa niraakaran bhi aavashyak hota he. jahaan uchit prakaar kaa jal aavashyak pariman main sulabh hota he, vahaan mashiinon dwaara aavashyak jal kii purti saralata se hoti he. taaja jal nagarpalikaon ke jalaashyon se mil jaataa he, kintu is jal ke jeevanuon ko maarane ke liye prayukt chlorin gas ke avshesh ko pehle alag kar liya jaataa he, kyonki yah gas jalashay ke jeevon ko haani pahunchaati he. yadi jalashay ke liye samudri pani aavashyak he, to samudr ke aise sthaan se jal lete hai jahaan nadiyon se ai hui, yaa anya prakaar se giranevaali, gandagiyaan na hon. aise sthanon par bhi tufaanon ke kaaran jal upyog ke ayogy thahar saktaa he, isaliye anek jagahon par aisa prabandh rahata he ki hauj main ek baar bharaa hua jal pun: sanchaarit hota rahata he or maarg main uske chaanane or upyukt banane kii kriyaayein sampann ho jaati hai.
 is kaary ke liye jeevashaalaon se jal ek chhanane se hokar neeche sthit ek hauz main chalaa jaataa he. yahan iskaa rasinic shodhan tathaa tapaniyantran hota he. jal kaa taap niyamit banaae rakhane ke liye garam yaa thanda karne ke ushmasthaitik upkarnon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. neeche ke hauj se pump mashin jal ko uthaakar phir jeevashaala main pahunchaa detii hai. jeevon dwaara jal main se lee hui oxigen kii purti tathaa usamen chodi hui carbon diaksaide ke nikaas ke liye maarg main uchit sthanon par vayusanchaaran ke saadhan rahete hai. is prakaar kii badi sansthaaon main bhinn prakaar kii jalwoyu main paae janevaale jeevon ke liye ushn, samshitoshna tathaa sheethal, samudri jal ke bhinn bhinn jalashay hote hai. isi prakaar bhinn taap ke mridu jal ke jalashay aavashyak hai tathaa bhinn taap or bhinn kshareey yaa amleey jalon kii bhi aavashyaktaa hoti he. jal ke avaagaman ke liye dhaatu ke nalon ke sthaan par, jinka prabhaav vishaiylla ho saktaa he, kaanch ke yaa cement ke plasters kiye hue nal upyukt hote hai.
 jantuon ke sangrah main yah saavdhaanee atyaavashyak he ki pakadte samay unhen adhik chot na lage or parivahan ke samay upyukt jal tathaa khaadya unhen miltaa rahe. kuch khaadya saamaan to bazaaron main mil jate hai, kintu kuch khaadya jalashaala ke kaaryakartaaon ko dhoundkar ekatrit karnaa padataa he. parjiviyon tathaa rog or mahamariyon se raksha kar vishesh dhyaan dena chahie. jalajeevon ke rogon kii chikitsa kathin he, isaliye nivarak upaay hi adhik prabhaavshaalee siddh hue hai. chikitsa ke liy mukhyat: jeev ko aise vilyan main rakh dete hai jisamen usko koi haani na pahunche, kintu sankramak jeevanu mar jaaen. yadi jalashay ke jal ko thandha na hone diya jaae, to rogon or parjiviyon se vishesh ashanka nahin rahati.
 chote jalaashyon main machhliyon ke saath jaleey vanaspatiyon ko bhi rakhane ke kaaran, gharon main jaljivshaalaon ke prati aakarshan bad gaya he or ye lokapriy ho gai hai. anek jaleey jeev sthir jal main jeevanyaapan ke abhyast hai. isaliye is prakaar kii jeevashaalaon kaa rakrakhaav apekshaakrit saral hota he, yadhyapi inaki dekhbhaal ke siddhant mukhyat: ve hi hai jo saarvajanik badi jeevashaalaon ke sambandh main lagu hote hai.
 ek mithyaa vishwaas failaa hua he ki sthir jalawaali uparyukt jaljivshaalaon main upasthit vanaspatiyon se jal kaa oxikaran hota rahata he. vastav main baat iske vipareet he. vanaspatiyaan bhi raat main, yaa badaliwaale dinon main, jal se usee prakaar oxigen leti or carbon dioxide detii hai jaise jaleey jeev; kintu in jeevashaalaon main vanaspatiyon kii upasthiti se anya laabh hai. machhliyon tathaa anya jeevon ke sharir se jo mel ityadi nikalate hai ve vanaspatiyon ke liye khaad ke kaam aa jate hai or is tarah jal main gandagi nahin ekatrit hone paati. vanaspatiyon se jalashay kii sundarataa main bhi vriddhi hoti he.
 vanaspatiyon or jantuon dwaara jal se shoshit oxigen kaa pun:sthaapan tathaa inke dwaara jal main utpann carbon dioxide kaa nishkasana samuchit reeti se hona aavashyak he. yadi jalashay ke jal or vaayu kaa madhyasth str yathesht vistrut he, to yah kaary swayamev sampadit ho jaataa he. yadi aisa nahin he, to sookshm bulabulon ke rup main pump yaa anya kisi upaay se jal ke bheetar se vaayu kaa nishkasana karana aavashyak hota he. kisi bhi jalashay main yadi jeevon tathaa vanaspatiyon kaa pariman jalwaayu-madhyasth-str ke kshetrafal se santulit rakhaa jaay, to vayusancharan kii vishesh vyavastha kiye bina bhi kaam chal saktaa he.
 machhliyon tathaa anya jalajantuon ko paalane ke sivaay inaki santanotpatti kii ritiyon kaa adhyayan bhi aakarshak vishay he. in jeevon kii lagbhag 300 aisi jatiyaan hai jo jalaashyon main paali ja sakati hai. inamen se kuch kii prajnan reetiyaan vicitra prakaar kii hai. anek andey detii hai, jinako sene par bachche nikalate hai. anya jivit bacchon ko janm detii hai, anek bacchon kii badi dekhbhaal or saavdhaanee rakhti hai. syam desh kii ladaaku machhliyon kaa nar lasadaar phen kaa awaas bana letaa he. isamen maadaa dwaara die andey rakhkar vah unki raksha karata rahata he. siclidi jati kii machhliyaan apane andon or bacchon ko bhi suraksha ke liye apane munh main rakhe rahati hai or jitne dinon tak andon main se bachche nahin nikalate utane dinon tak bhojan nahin karti.
 jaljivshaala kii machhliyon ke bhojan kii samasya vishesh kathin nahin he. machhliyon ke saadhaaran bhojya padaarth or sookshm jalajeevon se inkaa nirvaah ho jaataa he. gharelu jeevashaalaon kii machhliyon ke liye dhan yaa bhujiyaa chaaval kaa lava bhi upyukt paaya gaya he. shesh bachaa bhojan jal ko ganda karata he. machhliyon ko atyalp aahaar kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. isaliye is bhul kii adhik sambhaavna he ki kam bhojan dene ke sthaan par aavashyaktaa se adhik bhojan diya jaay. yah dhyaan rakhana sadaiv aavashyak he ki jalaashyon main utana hi bhojan daalaa jaae jitna khap sake.
 jaljivshaalaon ke rakrakhaav sambandhi purvokt siddhant machhlee paalane kii sabhi ritiyon par lagu hote hai, chaahe sajaavat ke liye gharon main rakhi janevaali choti jeevashalas hon, yaa bagichon main banaae janevaale hauj hon, athwa bhojan ke liye paali janevaali machhliyon ke pokhare hon. lagbhag sabhi deshon kii sarkaron ne, saarvajanik jalaashyon main yathesht machhliyaan banaae rakhane ke liye, vishesh matsayshaalaaon main machhliyon ke rakhane, unke andon kaa sangrakshan tathaa bacchon ke paalane kaa prabandh kiya he. jahaan sambhav hota he vahaan adon ko machhliyon se alag rakhkar sene or bachche paidaa karne kaa bhi prabandh rahata he. is prakaar nadiyon yaa jalaashyon main chhodane ke liye choti yaa badi, jis prakaar kii bhi machhliyaan chahie, uplabdh ho jaati hai.
