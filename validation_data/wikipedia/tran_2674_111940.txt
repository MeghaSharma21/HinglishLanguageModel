 henry saptam
 sabhyataa ke ek str tak pahunche hue england ke pracheenatam nivasee keltic jati ke the jinamein pashchaat ke deshantaravasi brythan yaa britain kehalaae, jisase "britain" sangya nikali. keltic athwa uske purvi jatiyon ke aagaman ke koi likhit pramaan nahin milte. ireland kii dweep main, jo pehle irun or skoshia naam se vidit tha, ek or jati ke log, scots the. ye paanchavin shatabdi ke uttarardh main calladonia athwa uttari britain apane jaateey niyam, hastshilp, dhaatushastrastra, krishi, yudhkala tathaa dharm se parichit the. gaal pradesh ke kelti swajatiyon se tathaa greek se inke vyapaarik sambandh the. 330 i.pus. ke aasapaas panthiyas tathaa, do shatabdi uparaant, posidonius vyaparoddeshy se nikle greek vyaktiyon main se the.
 55 i.pus. main roman senaanee julius seizar akramanon ne britain ko ashaant kar diya. 43 i.pus. main samrat cladiyas ke shashan main britain par vijay kii niyamit yojana banaai gai tathaa aagaamee 40 varshon main scapula, palinius or agricola ityadi roman kshetrapon ke antargat use puraa kiya gaya. britain kaa brihat kshetra 410 i. tak roman praant rahaa tathaa is yug main is pradesh kii diksha roman samskruti main hui. sadakon kaa nirmaan hua. unase sambandhit nagaron kaa udai hua. roman vidhisanhita vahaan prachlit hui. khaanon kii khudaai shuru hui. niyam or vyavastha laai gai. britain ko anaaj kaa niryaatpradhan desh banane ke liye krishi ko mahatv mila or landinium pramukh vyapaarik nagar ban gaya. roman samrajya main, isai sabhyataa ke prasar ke kaaran, britain main bhi uske prachararth chauthi shatabdi ke prarambh main ek maarg dhunda gaya or kuch kaloparant iskaa paudha vahaan bhi lag gaya. britain main roman sabhyataa phir bhi kritrim or bahya hi rahi. janata usase prabhavit na ho saki. uske avshesh visheshat: vaastu se hi sambandhit rahe. paanchavin shatabdi ke aarambh main rome ko videshi akramanon ke viruddh ghar main sangharsh karnaa padaa or 410 i. main apni sena england se khinch leni padi.
 romanon ke chale jane par britain kuch samay ke liye barbar akramanon kaa lakshya bana. uttar se pict, pashchim se scot tathaa purv se samudri lutere sackson or jute aae. sackson tyutan jati ke the jisamen angal, jute or shuddh sackson bhi sammilit the. britain ne juuton kii sahaayta maangi. juuton ne 449 i. main britain main pravesh kar, picton ko parrast kar, kent pradesh main apni satta sthapit kii. iske uparaant sackson jatthon ne britnon ko jeet sasex, vesex or asex ke pradesh main prabhutv sthapit kar liya. ant main anglon ne uttar or madhya se desh par akraman kiya or angleey vyavastha sthapit kii. ye teenon vijeta jatiyaan saamanyat: english naam se prasiddh huin. anglosax vijay kii yah prakriya lagbhag 150 varshon tak chali jisamen adhikansh britnon kaa daman hua or ek nai sabhyataa aropit hui.
 anglosaxn vijayoparant saat rajyon kaa saptashasan centre, sasex, vesex, asex, narthmbria, poorviy angliya or marsia par sthapit hua. ye rajya satat parsparik yudhon main nirat rahe or teen rajya apni vijayon ke kaaran adhik shaktishaalee hue. ant main vesex ne sarvopari shakti arjeet kii. saptashasan ke pramukh raajaaon main kent ke ethelbert, narthmbria ke edwin, marsia ke peda tathaa vesex ke itani prasiddh hai. yahi vah samay he jab ogastin ke prayaas se england ne isai dharm kii diksha lee or ogastin cantarbari ke pratham archbishap niyukt hue. kent, narthmbria or marsia ne kram se nayaa dharm angikaar kiya. udhaar sent paatrik tathaa sent colambaa kramash: ireland or scotland main samaan kaary main nirat the. england ke is dharmaparivartan ne rashtriya ekat kaa maarg prashast kiya.
 pracheen 15 sackson raajaaon kii pankti kaa prarambh agbert se tathaa ant louhpurush edmund ke shashan se hota he. in do shatabdiyon main northmanon athwa denon ke akraman hue or isaki paraakaashtha alfred mahan ke shashan main hui jisane 878 i. main athenden ke yuddh kshetra main inako parrast kiya. alfred kaa shashan yuddh or shanti kii safaltaaon se ullekhneey he. usne vesex ko vyavasthit kiya, sainik sudhaar kiye, jalsena sthapit kii, niyamon main sanshodhan kiye or gyaan protsaahan diya. anglosaxn vrutant kaa sangrah isi ke shashan main hua. is yug kaa ek or prasiddh vyakti, cantarbari kaa archbishap, tansten hua, jo alfred ke uttaradhikariyon kii chhatrachhaaya main rashtranayak or dharmasudharak ke rup main vikhyath hua. sackson rajkul lagbhag chauthai shatabdi ke liye ethelrad kii adoordarshee niti ke kaaran sattahin kar diya gaya. antat: den apana nirankush rajtantr canute kii adhyakshata main sthapit karne main 1017 i. main safal hue.
 1017 se 1042 i. tak england teen den raajaaon dwaara shasit hua. canute, jisane 18 varsh shashan kiya, england, denmark tathaa norway kaa raja tha. shashan kaa prarambh barbarta se kar, usne england main niyamavyavastha pun: sthapit kii, denon or sthaneey janata ko samadrushti se dekha or rome kii tirthayatra kii, jahaan usne english yatriyon ko suvidhaayen dilaain. uske ayogy putron ke shashan main den samrajya kaa ant ho gaya.
 edward ke vyaktitv main vesex kaa punaruddhar hua. edward videshi prabhaavon kaa das ho gaya tha. vesex ke arl gadwin ke netritv main is prabhaav ke viruddh ek rashtriya andolan hua. edward kaa shashan usee andolan yaa sangharsh ke liye prasiddh he. uski mrityu par gadwin kaa putr herold shaasha chunaa gaya, kintu gaddi kaa daavedaar narmandi kaa duk wiliam ho gaya tha jo 1066 i. main hestings ke yudhakshetra main england par akraman karne ke uparaant, herold ko ukhaad phenk chukaa tha. sackson rajyatantr samaapt hua or wiliam english singhaasana par arudh hua.
 wiliam pratham kaa shasankaal punarnirmaan tathaa vyavasthanirat tha. usne apni sthiti nai sanmtniti se english or narman praja ke samaan reeti se dabaakar tathaa dharmik sudhaaron se sudrudh kar lee. lene frank kii popevirohi sahaayta se usne apni swadhinata sthapit kii. bhoomi kaa lekha, dumsde book, taiyaar kiya. uske putr wiliam dwitiya kaa shashan shathata or durvyavastha kaa parichaayak he. uske shasankaal kii pramukh ghatnaayen hai, cantarbari ke upar raja or insem kaa sangharsh tathaa pratham dharmayuddh jisamen uskaa bhai rubart yusanchalan ke liye narmandi ko girvee rakhkar sammilit hua tha. 1100 i. main vijeta kaa sabase chota betaa henry pratham gaddi par baitha or 1106 i. main narmandi ko, rubart ko haraakar pun: praapt kiya. uske prashaskeey sudhaar, jinamein kuria rejis yaa raja dwaara nyaayalay kii sthapana bhi sammilit he, use "nyaay kaa singh" kii padavi dilaane main sahaayak hue. henry kii putree matilda kaa vaivaahik sambandh aanju ke count jeophri plantagenet ke saath ho jane ke kaaran plantagenet vansh kii sthapana hui. aagaamee varshon main stillen ke shashan main matilda ke netritv main ek uttaradhikaar kaa yuddh chalata rahaa jab tak yah nirnay nahin ho gaya ki stillen ke uparaant matilda kaa putr navayuvak henry gaddi kaa adhikari hoga.
 henry dwitiya kaa shashan english itihaas main ghor garbhasthiti main tha. iske shashan kii visheshtaon main pradhan thim england or scotland ke sambandhon main samipya, rajkeey vyavastha kaa excekar or nyaay par aadhaarit drudhikaran, curia rejis kaa udai, saamaanya english niyam kaa aavirbhaav tathaa swytt shashan evam gyaan kii paramparaon kaa vikaas hua. uske clanrendon vidhaan ne raja or carch ke sambandhon kaa nirdhaaran kiya. henry tathaa cantarbari ke archbishap tamas baket main charchaniti par paraspar sangharsh tathaa baket ke vadh ne is charchaniti ko asafal kar diya or carch viruddh raja kaa paksh kshatigrast ho gaya. henry kaa putr richard, jiskaa shashan tritiy dharmayuddh ke sanchaalan tathaa saladin ke viruddh philistin kii uski vijayon ke liye prasiddh he, sadaiv hi anupasthit shaasha rahaa. uskaa shasankaal robinhood ke kaaryon se sambandhit he. uski mrityu ke uparaant uskaa bhai jaan gaddi par baitha, jiskaa shashan nrushans atyaachaar tathaa vishwasghaat kaa pratik he. france ke philip dwitiya se jhagadkar narmandi tathaa uskaa satat adhikaar usne kho diya or pope se jhagadkar use ghor lajja kaa saamana karnaa padaa. uske bairanon se sangharsh kaa ant english swadhinata kii neenv mahaan paripatr par hastakshar ke saath hua.
 henry tritiy ke deergh shashan ko simon di maantfort ke netritv main bairanon kii ashaanti tathaa 1258 kii oxford kii dhaaraon dwaara raja par laade gaye niyantran kaa saamana karnaa padaa. iske uparaant raja or simon ke netritv main sarvapriya dal ke bich grihayuddh chhidaa jisamen henry kii haar hui. yah shashan angreji sansthaaon ke vikaas ke liye prasiddh he. 1265 i. main maantford ne parliament main nagaron or baron ke pratinidhi aamantrit kar house ov kaamans kaa shilanyas kiya. edward pratham kii adhyakshata main wales kii vijay purna kii gai. iskaa shashan, angreji kanoon, nyaay or sena main sudhaar tathaa 1295 kii model parlament ke dwaara parlament ko rashtriya sanstha bana dene ke praytn ke liye mahatvapurn he. apriy tathaa shithil edward dwitiya kii mrityu par uskaa putr edward tritiy, jiskaa shashan ghatnapurna tha, gaddi par baitha. scotland se hue ek yuddh ke uparaant england or france ke bich shatvarshiya yuddh kaa sutarapaat hua jo 1453 i. tak paanch angrej shaskaon ko veikshipt kiye hue tha. uske shashan kii duusari ghatnaayen, parlament kaa do sadanon main vibhaajan, 1348 kii "kaali mrityu" tathaa vikliff ke upadesh aadi hai. vikliff ne bible kaa angreji main anuwaad kar sudhaar andolan kaa abhas de diya tha. richard dwitiya ke shashan main krishak vidroh ke rup main saamaajik kranti kii pratham pidaa kii anubhuti england ne kii or angreji sahitya ke aarambhyita chasar ne cantarbari tells likhi. plantagenet shashan kii pramukh safaltaayen parlament kaa vikaas, saadhaaran janata kaa vidroh, carch adhikaar kaa patan tathaa rashtriya bhaavana kaa udai he.
 lankaster vansh ke teenon henriyon kaa shashan 1399 i. se 1461 i. tak aantarik drushti se, keval loladon athwa vikliff ke anuyaayiyon ke daman ko chhod, koi ghatnatmak mahatv nahin rakhata. bahya drushti se henry pancham ke shashan main shatvarshiya yuddh kii punrarirtti, agin court kii 1415 kii vijay, rogen kaa bandi hona tathaa 1420 kii tris kii sandhi sahaayak hui. henry pasht ke shashan main shatvarshiya yuddh safaltaapurvak chalata rahaa, jab tak france ko krishakkumari us arq kii jone ke vyaktitv main tranakarta nahin mila, jiske joshilay netritv ke saamne angrej hatprabh ho gaye or 1453 i. main ek kaile ko chhod apane saare french pradesh ganvaa baithe. kintu is shashan main grihayuddh--gulabon kaa yuddh - hua jo shashan satta ke hastantaran ke liye lankaster tathaa yarkvansh main ladaa gaya. pakshon kaa netritv kramash: henry shashth tathaa richard ne kiya. antim vijayon ne rajmukut yark vansh ke edward ko diya jisane sansad kii sweekruti se 1461 i. main edward chaturth ke naam se rajarohan kiya. 1485 i. main yarkvansheey samant rishmand ke arl henry ne vasaworth ke yuddh main richard ko parrast kar henry saptam ke naam se, yarkvansheey rajkumaari elizabeth kaa byaah, england ko rajmukut le tudervansh kii sthapana kii.
 lankaster yug kii kuch ugantarkari ghatnaayen thim : sansadeey shaktiyon kaa vikaas, loksabhaa kii swatantry vijay, gulabon ke yudhon ke saamanti gharaanon ke vidhwans ke saath rashtriya bhaavana ko protsaahan tathaa rajasatta kii vruddhi, pope ke adhikaron kaa kramik hraas or caxton ke chhapekhane ke avishkaar se janit sahitya main badhati hui anurakti.
 yadhyapi tuder yug kaa aavirbhaav madhyayug kaa ant or aadhunik yug kaa aarambh he, phir bhi kai drushtiyon se madhyayugin pravritiyon ke vistaar ko hi siddh karata he. saath hi yah angreji itihaas ke mahaan parivartanon evam rachanaaon kaa yug tha, jab england ne vah sthiti grahan kii jo aagaamee itihaas main purvawat bani rahi. nae gyaan, bhugolic khojon, avishkaron, nutan rashtrawaad, sudhaar andolan tathaa saamaajik shaktiyon ne england ke swaroop main purnat: parivartan kar diya. henry saptam nutan rajtantr tathaa chhalpurna nirankushta kaa vidhaataa tha1 yah rajshakti kisi aupchaarik vaidhanik parivartan ke kaaran nahin, janata ke vishwaas, samay kii aavashyaktaaon tathaa raajaaon kii doordarshita ke parinamswaroop paidaa hui. tuder shaskaon ne samanvaadi satta ko dabaaya tathaa saarvajanaki sweekruti par aadhaarit samantasatta ke bhagnavshesh par drudh rajtantr sthapit kiya. tuder shaskaon ne ek sahaayak sansad ke sahyog se, jo rajechha kaa saadhan ban gai thi, shashan kiya. kintu sansad kaa adhikaar siddhantat: bhi samaapt nahin kiya gaya; varan sansad ke kaaryon ko protsaahan diya gaya jiske falswaroop yug ke ant tak sansadeey shaktiyon kii vruddhi hui. raajaaon kii lipsaa ne unhen aarthik drushti se swadhin kar diya tha.
 dharmik vyavastha in shaskaon kii mahaan safalataa thi. henry ashtum ke netritv main rome se jo sambandhviched ek vidhanmala ke dwaara hua, vah edward shashth ke shashan main bhi chalaa. yadhyapi kuch samay ke liye meri tuder ke shashan main vah vyavastha bhang hui thi, phir bhi elizabeth pratham ke shashan main uski purnataa kii ore pragati hui or anglican dharmavyavasthaaon kii sthapana hui. tuder shaskaon kii vaideshik niti, keval elizabeth ke yug ko chhod, jab shaasha ko pratirodh andolan ke anuyaayiyon ke viruddh sangharsh tathaa meri stuart kii faansee ke falswaroop spain se yuddh karnaa padataa tha, adhiktar shanti or england ko sudrudh karne main lagi thi. is niti kii ek abhivyakti rajvansheey vivaahon main hui. inke shaskaon ke drudh shashan main ireland kaa vighatan kar scotland ko pehle vaivaahik, phir dharmik bandhan main england se baandhakar britain kii ekta ko kriyaatmak sangya di gai.
 yah yug, jaan tathaa cabet kii bhugolic khojons, chancelar, vilgabi, frabisher, drek tathaa hakins ke vyapaarik margsthaapan, chhapaakhana, barood or qutubanuma ke avishkaar, vyapaarik kapaniyon kii rachana tathaa amareeki pramukh sthal par varjeenia aise upniveshon kii sthapana aadi ke liye mahatvapurn he. britain kii navikkala kii sarvocchata bhi tabhi pratishthit hui jisase vaanijya or krishi kaa vikaas hua. vyapaarik parivartanon ke madhya varg ko janm diya jo saamaajik adhinimyan kii aavashyaktaa kaa sanketak siddh hua. tuder shaasha ek aise swytt shashan ke rachayita the jo 19veen shatabdi tak prachlit rahaa. nirdhanon ko niyamit dhang se labhanvit karne kaa praytn 1601 ke nirdhan kanoon se hua. sukh or sabhyataa kaa bhautik str bhi unchaa uthaa. navjagriti ko majboot aadhaar mila or buddhi evam samskruti ke kshetra main iskaa pramaan mila. elizabeth ke shashan main sahitya ko bada protsaahan mila. tab naatakon kii parinti shakespeare tathaa marlo ne, kavita kaa vikaas spencer ne or nutan gadhy hookar tathaa bekan ne kiya.
 praarambhik stuart shaasha, grihayuddh, rajtantr kaa pun: sthapana tathaa kranti-1603 i. main james pratham ke rajarohan se england or scotland ke rajmukut ek ho gaye tathaa igland main vaideshik scot vansh kii sthapana prarambh hui. tuder nirankush vyavastha tathaa sansad se saamanjasy kii aavashyaktaa ke samaapt ho jane se england kii bahya or aantarik sthiti main ek nae yug kaa aavirbhaav hua. stuart shaasha vikasman raashtra kii shaktiyon se sangharsh kar bethe jiske parinaam grihayuddh, ganatantreey anubhav, rajtantr kaa pun:sthaapan tathaa krantikari vyavastha hue. raashtra kaa vikaas, raajaaon kaa charitra, stuart shaskaon ko daivi adhikarajnya raajnneeti main roodhiwaadee aasthaa tathaa ugr pureetanvaad ityadi kaa samuhik parinaam hua raja or sansad ke bich ek mahaan vaidhanik sangharsh. yah sangharsh james pratham tathaa charles pratham ke shashan kii pradhan ghatana he. raja ke visheshadhikaron kii prishthbhumi se utpann is sangharsh ke pradhan paksh dharm, arth, tathaa vaideshik niti the. 1628 i. main loksabhaa apane adhikaron kaa paripatr praapt karne main safal hui. kintu charles phir swechhapurna shashan par drudh ho gaya or sansad ke deergh adhiveshan ke uparaant ghatnachakron ne raja tathaa sansand ke dalon ke bich grihayuddh ko drutagami kar diya. 1648 i. tak raja ke pakshpaati ukhaad phenke gaye tathaa duusare varsh charles par abhiyog lagaakar use faansee de di gai.
 ganatantreey vishkanbhak main england ko ganatantr ghoshit kiya gaya or oliver kramwel ne mahaan sangrakshakapad se 1658 tak shashan kiya. aantarik drushti se yah yug sainik shashansthapana, ghor puritanvaadee prayog tathaa kai vaidhanik yojanaon ke liye ullekhneey he. kramwel kii vaideshik niti ke parinamswaroop dach or spain se yuddh hue tathaa england ko jal or sthal donon yudhon main yash mila. uskaa pradhan uddeshy british vyapaar tathaa puritain mat kii vruddhi karnaa tha. use england, scotland tathaa ireland kii ekta ke praytn main safalataa mili. kintu aantarik shashan main janatantr ko samaapt kar dene ke kaaran rajtantr phir se sthapit karne ke paksh main ek rashtriya pratikriya hui or kramwel kii mrityu ke uparaant uske putr richard ke shasankaal main saare desh par araajaktaa chaa gai. parinamswaroop 1660 i. main stuart rajtantr pun: sthapit hua.
 1660 i. kii vyavastha ne rajtantr tathaa parlament donon ko pun: sthapit kiya. charles dwitiya ke shashan ne clanrendon samhita ke antargat amlin dharmavyavastha sthapit kii, panratu charles dwitiya ne catholicon ko bhi dharmik sahishnutaa deni chaahi. bahishkaar-niyam janya sangharsh ne england main do dal, kramash: petishnar tathaa abhorer, paidaa kiye jo aage chalakar hwig or tori kehalaae. uske shashan kii visheshta vaidhanik pragati tathaa netik hinata main he. 1665 i. main taaoon kaa prakope hua tathaa 1666 main bheeshan agnikand. apni vaideshik niti kaa aarambh charles dwitiya ne france se maitreepurn vyavhaar, spain se shatruta tathaa dachon se yuddh kiya. uske shashan main raja or parlament kaa sangharsh phir apane praarambhik bindu par pahunchaa. usne catholic mat ke prati sahishnutaa, sthaayee sena tathaa french mantri par aadhaarit stuart nirankushta ko punerjeevit karne kaa praytn kiya. uskaa roman mat kaa saarvajanik pratipaadan, rajshakti kaa prayog, dharm-swatantry-ghoshnaa kaa prakaashan tathaa isi se mishrit, uske putr ho jane ke kaaran, catholic mat ke bhavi sunahare avasar, samuhik rup se 1688 i. kii tathakthith gauravshaalee kranti main parilakshith hue. parinamat: wiliam tritiy evam meri kaa rajtilak hua.
 wiliam tritiy or meri ke sammilit tathaa wiliam tritiy ke akele shashan main 1688 kii kranti dwaara arjeet safaltaaon kaa samyak pratipaadan hua. 1689 kaa adhikaron kaa prastaav tathaa uske uparaant 1702 i. ke vyavastha kanoon ne angreji swadhinata ke kshetra ko or bhi vyapak kar diya. tab bhoomi main sansadeey sarkaar ke beej daale gaye, dharmik sahishnutaa tathaa press swatantry praapt hua or aarthik sudhaaron ko karyanvit kiya gaya. vaideshik kshetra main pramukh ghatnaayen lui chaturdash ke viruddh english uttaradhikaar kaa yuddh tathaa spain ke uttaradhikaar ke prashn ko saral kar dene ke uddeshy se kii gai vibhaajansandhiyaan thim, jinhone england ko france se dwitiya yuddh karne ke liye baadhya kiya. wiliam ke uparaant rani en ke shashan main marlbaron kii vijayon ke kaaran prasiddh spain ke uttaradhikaar kaa yuddh tathaa 1713 kii utract kii sandhi hui. desh kii pramukh ghatnaayen raajnneetik dalgat sarkaar kii rachana tathaa 1707 main ekta kanoon ke dwaara england or scotland kaa ek raashtra main vilyan he.
 stuart kaaleen england kii visheshta vyapaarik prasar, west indies tathaa uttari america ke upniveshikaran or bharat tathaa america main vyapaarik kendron kii sthapana thi. vyapaar se dhan main vruddhi hui or samudr main dach or francisiyon ko parrast kar britain jal kaa swami ban gaya. isi kaal hui england ke bank kii sthapana vishesh mahatv rakhti he. saanskrithik or bauddhik unnati bhi paryaapt maatra main hui. vikhyath vyaktiyon main angreji kranti tathaa grihayuddh ke lekhak clanrenden, kavita main jaan milton, mahaan alankarik lekhakon main jaan banyan, vyangyalekhakon main jaan dryden, darshanikon main jaan laac tathaa ganitagyon evam bhautikee darshanikon main ijak newton aadi ullekhneey hai.
