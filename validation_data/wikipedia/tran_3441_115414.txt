 arminia pashchim asia or europe ke kakeshas kshetra main sthit ek pahari desh he jo chaaron taraf zamin se ghira he. 1990 ke purv yah soviet sangh kaa ek ang tha jo ek rajya ke rup main tha. soviet sangh main ek janakranti evam rajyon ke aazaadee ke sangharsh ke baad arminia ko 23 agast 1990 ko swatantraaa pradaan kar di gai, parantu iske sthapana kii ghoshnaa 21 sitambar, 1991 ko hui evam ise antarrashtriya manyata 25 disambar ko mili. isaki rajdhani erevan he. 
 armenian mul kii lipi aramike ek samay bharat se lekar bumadhya sagar ke bich prayukt hoti thi. purvi roman samrajya or faras tathaa arab donon kshetron ke bich avasthit hone ke kaaran madhya kaal se yah videshi prabhaav or yuddh kii bhoomi rahaa he jahaan islam or isiyat ke kai aarambhik yuddh lade gaye the. armenia pracheen aitihaasik saanskrithik dharohar vaala desh he. armenia ke raja ne chauthi shatabdi main hi isai dharm grahan kar liya tha. is prakaar armenia rajya isai dharm grahan karne vaala pratham rajya he. desh main armenian apostalik carch sabase bada dharm he. iske alaava yahan isaiyon, musalamaanon or anya sampradaayon kaa chota samudaay he.
 armenitha kaa kul kshetrafal 29,800 ki.meÂ² he jiskaa 4.71% jaleey kshetra he. anumaanatah yahan kii jansankhya 32,31,900 he evam varg kimi ghanatv 101 vyakti he. isaki seemayen turki, georgia, ajarbaijan or iran se lagi hui hai. aaj yahan 97.9 pratishat se adhik arminian jaateey samudaay ke alaava 1.3% yazidi, 0.5% roosi or anya alpsankhyak nivas karte hai. yahaan kii jansankhya kaa 10.6% bhaag antarrashtriya garibi rekha se neeche nivas karata he. armenia 40 se adhik antarrashtriya sanghathanon kaa sadsy he. isamen sanyukt raashtra, europe parishad, asian vikaas bank, swatantra deshon kaa rashtrakul, vishwa vyapaar sanghathan evam gut nirpeksh sanghathan aadi pramukh hai.
 armenian mul ke log apane ko hayak kaa vanshaj maanate hai jo nuh kaa par-parpota tha. kuch isaiyon kii manyata he ki noah or uskaa parivaar yaheen aakar bas gaya tha. arminia kaa armenian bhashaa main naam hayastan he jiskaa arth haayak kii jameen he. haayak noh ke par-parpote kaa naam tha. 
 islam, isai or yahudee dharm kii ubhay manyataon ke anusaar puranic mahapralay kii baadh se bachaane vaale noah kaa naav yaravan kii pahadiyon ke paas aakar ruk gaya tha. armenian apane ko nohe ke parpote ke pote hayak kaa vanshaj maanate hai. kaansya yug main hitti tathaa mitnni jaise samrajyon kii bhoomi rahaa he. louh kaal main arame ke uratu samrajya ne sabhi shaktiyon ko ek kiya or usee ke naam par is kshetra kaa naam armenia padaa. 
 itihaas ke pannon par arminia kaa aakaar kai baar badla he. 80 i.pus. main armenia rajshahi ke antargat vartmaan turki kaa kuch bhu-bhaag, syria, lebanon, iran, iraq, azarbaijan or vartmaan arminia ke bhu-bhaag sammilit the. roman kaal main armenia faras or rome ke bich banta rahaa. isai dharm kaa prachaar europe or khud armenia main isi samay hua. san 591 main bizentainon ne parsiyon ko haraa diya par 645 main ve khud dakshin main shaktishaalee ho rahe muslim arabon se haar gaye. iske baad yahan islam ke bhi prachaar hua. iran ke safavi vansh ke samay yah chaar baar istambul ke osmani turkon or isiahaan ke shiya safavi shaskaon ke bich hastantarit hota rahaa. 1920 se lekar 1991 tak arminia ek samyavaadee desh tha. yah soviet sangh kaa ek sadsy tha. aaj arminia kii turki or azarbaijan se lagati seemaa sangharsh kii vajah se band rahati hai. nagorno-karabakh par aadhipaty ko lekar 1992 main armenia or azarbaijan ke bich ladaai hui thi jo 1994 tak chali thi. aaj is jameen par arminia kaa adhikaar he lekin ajarbaijan abhi bhi jameen par apana adhikaar bataata he.
 
 armenia das praanton main banta hua he. prayek praant kaa mukhya karypalak armenia sarkaar dwaara niyukt kiya jaataa he. inamen yeravaan kon rajdhani shahar  hone se vishisht darja mila he. yeravaan kaa mukhya karypalak mahayor hota he, evam raashtrapati dwaara niyukt kiya jaataa he. harek praant main swa-shasit samudaay hote hai. varsh 2007 ke aankadon ke anusaar armenia main 915 samudaay the, jinamein se 49 shahari evam 866 gramin hai. rajdhani yeravaan shahari samudaay he, jo 12 ardh-swytt jilon main bhi banta hua he.
