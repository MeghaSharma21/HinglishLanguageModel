 darshan ranganathan bharat ke ek carbonic rasayanajna the. unhone jaev-jaivik rasayan vigyaan main kaam ke kiya tha, jisamen "protein tah main agrani kaam shaamil he."" unhen "supermulekulular assembliyon, aanvik dizine, mahatvapurn jaivik prakriyaaon ke rasinic simulation, kaaryaatmak hybrid peptids ke sanshleshan or nanotub ke sanshleshan main unke kaam ke liye bhi manyata mili.
 darshan ranganathan kaa janm 4 june, 1941 ko dilli main vidyavati marken or shanti swaroop se hua tha. vah dilli main shikshit hui or piechdi praapt kii 1967 main dilli vishvavidhyalay se rasayan vigyaan main. pehle unhone ek vyaakhyaata ke rup main kaam kiya or phir vah miranda college, dilli main rasayan vigyaan vibhaag ke pramukh bane. 
 1970 main, unhone bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan, kanpur main shodh shuru kiya. unhone fellowship ke aadhaar par iaiti kanpur main apana shodh jari rakhaa. alikhit niyamon ne use sankaay main shaamil hone se roka kyonki uske pati pehle se hi sadsy the. vah neshnal academy of sciensej ke phelo the.
