 vaanijyawad 16veen se 18veen shadi main europe main prachlit ek aarthik siddhanth tathaa vyavhaar kaa naam he jiske antargat rajya kii shakti badhaane ke uddeshy se raashtra kii arthvyavasthaaon kaa sarkaron dwaara niyantran ko protsaahan mila. 
 vyapaarik kranti ne ek naveen aarthik vichaardhaara ko janm diya. iskaa prarambh solahavin sadi main hua. is naveen aarthik vichaardhaara ko vaanijyawad, vanikwaad yaa vyapaarvaad kaha gaya he. france main is vichaardhaara ko colbertwaad or germany main kemralism kaha gaya. 1776 i. main prasiddh arthshaastri adam smith ne bhi apane granth ‘da velth of neshns' main iskaa vivechan kiya he.
 vaanijyawad se abhipraay us aarthik vichaardhaara se he jo pashchimi europe ke deshon main visheshkar france, england or germany main solahavi or satrahavin sadi main prasaarit hui thi or atharahavin sadi ke madhya tak iskaa khub vikaas hua. vaanijyawad kii dharna antarrashtriya vyapaar or usase praapt dhan se sambandhit he. is vaanijyawad ke siddhant ke anusaar krishi or uske utpaadan kii kuch seemaa tak hi vruddhi kar sakate hai. krishi aarthik drushti se kuch seemaa ke baad anutpaadak bhi ho sakati he, par udyogon, vyavsaayon or vaanijya vyapaar kii vruddhi or vistaar kii koi seemaa nahin he. audyogikaran se or vyapaar kii niyamit vruddhi se desh sona-chaandi praapt kar samrudh or shaktishaalee hoga. yah vaanijyawad kaa mul siddhant he. vaanijyawad main vyapaaree varg, vyavasthit, sunishchit or niyamit vaanijya-vyapaar or sone-chaandi kii prapti or sanghathan par adhik bal diya gaya. ‘‘adhik swarna praapt kar adhik balashaali bano’’ yah vaanijyawad kaa naaraa tha. adhik dhan sangrah se desh kii aarthik shakti or sampannataa badhati he. isse raajnneetik lakshya saralata se praapt kiye ja sakate hai. aantarik shaanti or bahya akramanon se suraksha praapt ho sakati he, isliye shaasha, raajnneetijna, vichaarak, arthshaastri, prashaasak or vyapaaree varg ne vaanijyawad kaa samarthan kiya. 
  sone or chaandi kaa sanchay - vyapaar-vaanijya se dhan kii vruddhi hogi or yah dhan sone, chaandi, heere, jawaharaat, bahumoolya ratna ke rup main praapt kar unka sangrah karnaa chahie. ye sab na to nashwan hai or na parivartansheel. ve har samay or har avasar par prachur sampatti hi rahete hai. swarna, chaandi or ratnon ke bhandar rajshakti ke pratik hai. is badhate hue dhan se yuddh saamagree praapt kar desh main aantarik shanti or bahya suraksha praapt kii ja sakati he. is prakaar vaanijyawad surakshatmak he. 
  antarrashtriya vyapaar - antarrashtriya vyapaar vaanijyawad kaa aadhaar he. desh main audyogikaran kar desh ke badhate hue utpaadan kii vastuon kaa anya deshon ko niryaat karnaa. aavashyak hua to rajya ke kuch vishisht udyogon ko sangrakshan dena chahie or videshi vyapaar ko protsaahit karnaa chahie. antarrajjiy vyapaar se dhan prapti yudhon main vijay kaa aadhaar he. 
  anukool or santulit vyapaar - vyapaar main paryaapt dhan praapt karne ke liye videshon ko atyaadhik maatra main vyapaarik maal beche, par videshon se apane desh main nunatam maatra main maal mangaye. iskaa arth yah he ki desh nunatam aayaat karen or adhiktam niryaat kare. isse desh ko niryaat karne main bahumoolya dhaatuen-swarna or chaandi-praapt hogi or nunatam aayaat karne se videshon ko bahut kam dhan baahar bhejnaa padegaa. is siddhant ko anukool or santulit vyapaar kehete hai. aayaat kiye hue maal or vastuon kii keemten niryaat kiye gayi maal or vastuon kii keemton se adhik nahin hona chahie. aayaat or niryaat main anukool santulan hona chahie. is pranali ko apnaane se desh main adhik utpaadan hoga, adhik mudra prachlan main hogi, poonjeewaad kii protsaahan milegaa or desh aarthik drushti se balashaali hoga. 
  audyogic pratibandh or vyapaarik niyantran - desh main udyog-vyavsaayon ko rajkeey protsaahan dekar unamem adhikadhik vruddhi karne se kahin atyaadhik utpaadan nahin ho jaae. atyaadhik utpaadan se anek haniyaan hoti hai, jaise berogari main vruddhi, maal ke uthaav kaa abhaav, bhaavon kaa girnaa, arth-vyavastha kaa gadabadana. isliye utpaadan ko rajya kanoonon se niyantrit karnaa padataa he. niryaat ko badhaava dene ke liye rajya ko vishesh protsaahan dena padataa he. isi prakaar aayaat par kar ke rup main niyantran lagaana padate hai, jisase ki maal atyaadhik aayaat nahin ho jaae. kinhin vishesh companiyon ko hi aayaat karne kii suvidhaayen di jaati hai. ise audyogic pratibandh or vyapaarik niyantran kehete hai. 
  naveen vyapaarik mandiyan evam upnivesh - desh se baahar bheji jane vaali taiyaar vastuon kii khapat ke liye videshon main vyapaarik mandiyon ko praapt karnaa or vahaan se desh ke udyog-vyavsaayon ke liye kaccha maal praapt karnaa. aisi vyapaarik mandiyaan praapt karne ke liye shaktishaalee rajyon ne videshon main apane upnivesh basaaye jahaan rajya main bani hui vastuon ko saralata se laabhprad daron par bechaa ja saktaa tha. inhen upniveshon ke bazaaron se kaccha maal bhi saralata se saste daamon par kharid kar desh main aayaat kiya ja saktaa tha. saste daamon par kaccha maal uplabdh ho jane se desh ke kal-kaarkaanon or udyogon main kam laagat par adhik utpaadan hoga. isse desh ko adhik aarthik laabh hoga. is prakaar vaanijyawad ne upniveshwaad or samrajyavad kaa maarg prashast kiya. 
  samudri yatraaen or bhugolic khojein - columbus, vascodigama, amerigo, magellan, jaan kebaat jaise saahsee navikon ne jokhimabhari samudri yatraayen karke anek naye deshon kii khoj kii. vahaan dhire-dhire nai vastiyaan basaai gayin. europe ke pashchimi deshon ne visheshkar spain, portugal, holland, france or england ne naye khoje hue deshon main apane-apane upnivesh or vyapaarik nagar sthapit kiye. in upniveshon se chamada, loha, rui oon aadi kaccha maal praapt kar apane desh main inase naveen vastuen nirmit kar upniveshon ko niryaat ke rup main bheji or badale main vahaan se prachur maatra main swarna or chaandi praapt kii. isse poonji kaa sanchay hua, aayaat-niryaat badha or desh samrudh hua. 
  punarjagran kaa prabhaav - punarjagran ne europe main naveen vaigyanik drushtikon ke saath-saath maanavjivan ke prati adhik abhiruchi or aakankshaayen utpann kii. maanav jivan ko adhik ruchikar or sukh-suvidha sampann banane kii pravriti ko protsaahan diya gaya. isse bhautikwaad kii vruddhi hui. bhautik sukh-suvidhaon kii purti ke liye paryaapt dhan kii maang badi or yah badhata hua dhan vaanijya-vyapaar or udyog-dhandhon se hi praapt ho saktaa tha. isse vaanijyawad ko protsaahan mila. 
  mudra prachlan or banking pranali - vibhinn vyavsaayon, udyog-dhandhon or vaanijya-vyapaar bad jane se vyavsaay or vyapaar pranaaliyon main sanshodhan, sudhaar or parivartan hue. vaigyanik anveshanon ke aadhaaron par udyog-dhandhon main adhikadhik utpaadan or vyapaar main vastuon kaa adhikadhik kray-vikray hone laga. isse mudra prachlan badha or aadhunik banking pranali kaa prarambh or vikaas hua. bainkon ne apane jamaa dhan ko anya vyapaariyon, vyavsaayiyon or udyogpatiyon ko uski saakh par udhaar diya or unke vyapaarik vastuon ke kray-vikray sambandhi bhugtan ko saralata se kiya. isse vaanijyawad ko adhikadhik protsaahan mila. dhire-dhire adhikadhik poonji ko vyapaariyon ko uplabdh karaane hetu joint stock companiyaan sthapit kii gayin or unka vikaas kiya gaya. in joint stock companiyon or bainkon main anek logon kii bachat kaa dhan sanchit ho rahaa tha. is dhan ko bade-bade udyogon or videshi vyapaar main lagaaya gaya. vishaal paimaane par bade kaarkaane sthapit or viksit hue. isse vishaal paimaane par vastuon kaa utpaadan badha, badi maatra main vastuon kaa vitaran or kray-vikray hone legaa. isse deshi or videshi vyapaar tathaa vaanijyawad kaa ek naveen yug prarambh hua. 
  navdith rashtra rajyon dwaara protsaahan or sangrakshan - europe main pandrahavin or solahavin sadi main rashtriya rajyon kaa udai or vikaas hua. in balashaali rashtriya raajaaon ne desh main aantarik shanti sthapit kii or bahari akramanon se desh ko suraksha pradaan kii. suraksha or shanti ke vaataavaran main udyog-dhandhe or deshi-videshi vyapaar badha or rashtriya raajaaon ne sone-chaandi ke aayaat ko protsaahit kiya. isi bich vyapaariyon se kar ke rup main paryaapt dhan praapt ho jane se rashtriya raajaaon ne yuddh kiye or apane rajya kii simaaon kaa vistaar kiya tathaa anya mahadwipon main apane naye upnivesh sthapit kiye. unhone udyogpatiyon or vyapaariyon ko protsaahit kiya ki ve upniveshon se vyapaar karke apni dhan-sampatti badhaawein. faltah uttarottar badhata hua vyapaar or desh kii aarthik samruddhi rashtriya rajya kii shakti ban gayi. in paristhitiyon main vaanijyawad or poonjeewaad khub fale-foole. 
 lagbhag 250 varshon tak vaanijyawad kii vichaardhaara kaa bahulya europe main rahaa. vaanijyawad kii vichaardhaara ne europe ke rashtron kii aarthik niti ko dhaala. europe main antarrashtriya vyapaar kaa prarambh vaanijyawad se hota he. aarthik laabh ke liye vibhinn rashtron ne santulit aayaat-niryaat kii niti apanaayi. vaanijyawad ke kaaran naveen udyogon ke maal ko adhikadhik niryaat kar videshon se bade paimaane par sona-chaandi or dhan praapt kiya gaya. is siddhant ko apanaaya gaya ki vastuon kaa adhikadhik niryaat karnaa or aayaat kam karnaa, jisase desh adhik samrudh ho jaae. videshi maal kii kharid or aayaat ko nirutsahit kiya gaya. swadeshi udyogon ko protsaahit karne hetu utpaadan main adhikadhik vruddhi kii gai. kaccha maal praapt karne or bane hue maal kii bikri or khapat ke liye naveen upniveshon kii sthapana kii gai. isse aupniveshik samrajya bane. vaanijyawad kii nitiyaan or siddhant apnaane se europe main england, france or germany jaise mahaan shaktishaalee rajyon kaa nirmaan ho sakaa. sheeghra hi inkaa samrajya europe ke baahar mahadwipon main fail gaya. 
  poonjeewaad - vaanijyawad ne udyog-dhandhon ke prasar se poonjeevaad ko janm diya. is poonjeewaad se europea samaaj main do virgon kaa udai hua- pratham poonjipatiyon or udyogpatiyon kaa varg jisane utpaadan ke sadhano par apana ekadhikaar sthapit kar liya, or dwitiya sarvhaara varg jiske paas swayan ke swaamitv ke utpaadan ke saadhan nahin hone se apane shram ko sast daamon par bechnaa padataa tha. isse kalantar main poonjipati or sarvhaara varg main kadaa sangharsh chhid gaya, jisase vidroh or krantiyaan hui tathaa aarthik vyavastha dagamagaa gayi. 
  sankeern rashtriyata - vaanijyawad ne ek raashtra ko mahatv dekar uski samruddhi ke liye duusare anya rashtron ke shoshan kaa maarg prashast kiya. ek raashtra adhik shashakt or sampann ho gaya or anya chote-chote desh shoshit hone se or garib ho gayi. is prakaar sankeern rashtriyata kaa maarg prashast hua. 
  antararashtreey vyapaarik or aupniveshik spardhaa - vaanijyawad ne vibhinn deshon main madhur maitreepurn antarrashtriya sambandhon ke sthaan par antarrashtriya vyapaarik or aupniveshik pratispardhaa ko janm diya, isse vidhwanskaari yuddh hue. 
  sone-chaandi ke sanchay kii nirarthakta - vaanijyawad ne sona-chaandi praapt kar uske sanchay par adhik mahatv diya. faltah jis varg ke paas swarna or chaandi sanchit hote gayi vah apaar dhan sampatti ke aadhaar par aniatik, vilaasi or bhrasht ho gaya. samaaj main naitik mulya samaapt ho gaye. udyog dhandhon ke vikaas hone par yah tathy saamne aayaa ki kisi desh main sone-chaandi ke bhandar kii apeksha loha, ispaat, koyala, khanij tel aadi adhik moolyawaan he. inke samuchit dohan se raashtra adhik samrudh or shaktishaalee hoga. is siddhant ne sone-chaandi ke bhandar ko nirarthak kar diya. 
  krishi kii upeksha - vaanijyawad ke samarthakon ne udyog-dhandhon or vyavsaayon ke adhiktam vikaas par bal diya. isse krishi kaa kshetra aviksit or pichhadaa rah gaya. kisi bhi desh kii aarthik samruddhi ke liye krishi or udyog-dhandhon kaa santulit vikaas hona chahie. 
  lok kalyaan kaa abhaav - vaanijyawad ne raajnneetik kshetra main rajya or shaasha or aarthik kshetra main udyog-dhandhon or vyapaar ko adhik mahatv diya. sarvhaara varg yaa daridr janata yaa aarthik rup se pichde varg ke hit main, lok kalyaan main koi abhiruchi nahin lee, iske liye koi yojana yaa siddhant nahin the. vaanijyawad main garibon, shilpiyon, shramikon kaa shoshan hua. 
  rajasatta or rajbhakti main vruddhi - vaanijyawad ke samarthakon, vyapaariyon or udyogpatiyon ne shaktishaalee rajya kaa samarthan kiya kyonki unke hit sanvardhan ke liye shashakt raja hi aantarik shanti or bahya suraksha pradaan kar saktaa tha. kalantar main desh main dhan kii prachurtaa or samruddhi se balashaali raja nirankush swechhachari shaasha ho gayi. raajaaon or shaskaon ne apni shaktiyon adhikaron kaa durupyog kiya. kalantar main unki nirankushta ke viruddh vidroh hue. uparokt kaarnon se vaanijyawad ke viruddh teevr ansatosh failane laga or 19veen sadi main parivartit paristhitiyon main iske siddhanton kaa virodh hua. inhen kaarnon se vaanijyawad kaa hraas ho gaya.
