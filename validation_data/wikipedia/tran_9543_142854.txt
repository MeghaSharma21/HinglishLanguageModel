 parth chatarji sabaltern adhyayan or uttar aupniveshik skulon se sambandhit ek bhartiya vidwaan he. inkaa janm 1947 main kalakatta main hua. yah ek bahu-vishyak vidwaan he jinka vishesh jor raajnneeti vigyaan, nrivigyaan or itihaas par he. shiksha kshetra main unke yogadan ke liye varsh 2009 main phukuoka asian samskruti puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. 
 yah raajnneetik vigyaan ke ek mananeey praadhyaapak hone ke saath "centre for studies in soshal science", kalakatta ke nideshak bhi rah chuke hai. vartmaan main colombia vishvavidhyalay main nrivigyaan or dakshin asian adhyayan ke praadhyaapak he. yah sabaltern adhyayan samuhik ke ek sansthaapak sadsy he.
