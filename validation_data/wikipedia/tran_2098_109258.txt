 shubaman gil ek bhartiya cricket khilaadi he farvari 2017 main inhone england ke khilaaf bhartiya under-19 team kii jeet kaa hissaa the. inhone 25 farvari 2017 ko 2016-17 main vijay hajaare trophy main punjab ke liye apni list e cricket kii shuruyaat kii thi. inhone 2017-18 main ranjai trophy main punjab ke liye apana pratham shrenee cricket match baad main usee maheene, inhone apane duusare pratham shrenee match main apana pehla shatak banaaya. disambar 2017 main, inhen 2018 isisi under-19 cricket vishwa cap ke liye bhartiya under-19 cricket team ke upakptain ke rup main namit kiya gaya tha.
 shubaman gil ko 2018 indian premier lig kii nilaamee main kolkata nite rides ne 1 karod 80 lakh rupee main kharida tha.
