 akaash ajukational services limited samooh ke medical santhaan, akaash iaiti-jeii or aakasha jiskaa gathan kiya gaya tha 2008 main.
 akaash pradaan karata he peshewar koching ke liye medical or ejineering pravesh pariksha bharat main he. yah ek vaarshik chaatr ke aadhaar 80,000 se adhik chaatro or 100 se adhik adhyayan centre bharat bhar main. yah pradaan karata he niyamit rup se kaksha kaarykram, duurastha shiksha or i-larning kaarykram. yah pradaan karata he lambii avadhi ke paathyakram ke saath, alpakaalik paathyakram, pariksha-shrinkhalaa paathyakram, or krash course he.
 medical wing ke Aakash gaya tha, jo pehle bhaag gaya tha, shuru kar diya dwaara je. si. choudhary, vanaspati vigyaan shikshak, 1988 main. ke rup main janaa jaataa Aakash santhaan, yah coach ke chaatro ke grad main gyarahavin or barahavin main pravesh ke liye medical collejon bharat menhai. 
 ejineering wing, akaash iaiti-jeii, main shuru kiya gaya tha 2007 ke liye koching uplabdh karaane ke chaatro ke liye kaksha gyarahavin or barahavin ke liye ejineering pravesh pariksha, vishesh rup se sanyukt pravesh parikshahai. 
 ke Aakash neenv brands, 2009 main sthapit, coach kakshaon main chaatro ko aathvin ke liye eks ke liye, unhen taiyaar karne ke liye board pariksha or junior str kii pratiyogi parikshaon jaise rashtriya pratibha khoj pariksha or pariksha dwaara yojit science olympiad foundation.
 ke akaash neshnal tallent hunt pariksha prativarsh yojit kiya jaataa he, jiske madhyam se chaatravrutti or shulk chhot se sammanit kar rahe hai karne ke liye meritorius chaatro ko shaamil hone ke ek koching kaarykram he. 2015 main, yah se adhik praapt 177,000 aavedakon. aajkal, yah yojit kiya jaataa he, do baar ek maheene. 
 Aakash pradaan karata he i-larning kaarykram ke madhyam se Aakash iTutor, ek goli-aadhaarit anuprayog, or Aakash tivi tution dit koching kaarykram, ke saath ek gathbandhan main bharat ke dit seva pradaata bharti airtel's new itivi seva — iExam ke liye — ek koching sevaaon ke madhyam se airtel digital tivi. 2012 main, vyapaar maanak he ki dhyaan diya akaash gaya tha "teji" ke bare main apni online prasad he. 
 akaash main apni shakhaon or matadhikaar kendron main uttar or purv bharat ke kuch bhaag pashchim bharat, or shuru karne ke liye apni shakhaon kaa vistaar dakshin bharat main isse pehle 2011 shaikshanik satr. ke kaaran NEET or jeii main kiye jane ke liye sabhi bharat aadhaarit kaaman entrens test ke liye medical or ejineering ummidavaaron shuruyaat 2013, akaash kaa vistaar kiya gaya he apni upasthiti main dakshin bharat or pashchim bharat main he. 
 ke madhyam se apni sahaayak company Aerita Cineworks, akaash main pravesh kiya, film ke utpaadan, vyapaar, may 2015 main debuting ke saath film .
