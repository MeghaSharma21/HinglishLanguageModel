 montana sanyukt rajya ke pashchimi kshetra main sthit rajya he. rajya kaa naam spanish shabd montana jiskaa arth parvat he, se liya gaya he. montana kii teen canadai praanton: british colombia, alberta, or saskechevan ke saath 545 mile kii seemaa he. iske purv main north dekota or south dekota, dakshin main vioming, pashchim or dakshin-pashchim main ydaho sthit he. montana america ke rajyon main aakaar ke hisaab se chauthaa sthaan he, lekin aabaadi main vah 48waan sthaan rakhata he. 
 montana kaa purvi hissaa 1803 main america ne france se kharida tha. pashchimi hissaa britain ke saath 1846 main kii gai sandhi se america ke haath aayaa. 1889 main 41wein rajya ke rup main ise sangh main shaamil kiya gaya. montana kii anumaanit jansankhya 2015 main 10,32,949 he. 94% janata angrezi bhashaa bolti he jo rajya kii adhikarik bhashaa bhi he.
