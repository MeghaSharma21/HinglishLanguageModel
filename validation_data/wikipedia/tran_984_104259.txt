 maniar singh hindi ke kavi he.
 maniyarsingh janm varanasi main sanwat 1807 vikrami ke lagbhag hua. inke pita kaa naam shyamsingh tha. "hanumat chhabisi" naamak rachana se gyaat hota he ki inhone kuch samay baliya main bhi bitaayaa tha. ramchandra pandit inke pramukh ashrayadata or krishnalal inke kavyaguru the. rachanaaon main kavi ne kahin-kahin "yaar" upnaam kaa bhi prayog kiya he.
