 janm chinh twachaa kii asaamanyataayen hoti hai jo bachche ke janm se maujuud hoti hai. 
 janm chinh sharir ke kisi bhi ang par sambhav he. jaise:
 bharat main aisi kai lokapriy manyataayen hai ki janm chihn se jivan kaa bhavishya tay hota he. yah bhi mana jaataa he ki kuch vishash hatheli main hone vaale chinh vah janm se hi lekar aate hai lakshmi kaa aashirwad or bhagya kaa saath.
