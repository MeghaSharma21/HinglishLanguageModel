 
 james, viscount severn vesex ke arl or countes ke chota or eklauta putr he. vah maharani elizabeth dwitiya kaa sabase chota pot he. james kaa janm 17 disambar 2007 main formali park aspataal, sari, england main hua tha. unka baptism sanskaar 19 april 2008 ko windsar kasal, barkshaayar main kiya gaya tha. apane janm ke samay wo apni daadi, rani elizabeth dwitiya ke baad british singhasana par uttaradhikaar ke anukram main aathven sthaan par the; cambridge ke rajkumaar jorge or rajkumaari sharlat ke janm ke baad, varttman samay main wo dasven sthaan par hai. wo apni badi bahan or mata-pita ke saath bagashot park, sari main rahete hai.
