 raja hindustani 1996 main bani dharmesh darshan dwaara nirdeshit hindi bhashaa kii premakahani film he. aamir khan or karishma kapur dwaara pramukh bhoomika nibhaai gai hai. 15 november 1996 ko jari yah film 1965 kii jab jab phool khile kii rimek he, jisamen shashi kapur or nanda kalakaar hai. film kaa sangeet nadeem-shravan dwaara diya gaya tha, jisamen samir ke bol the. jari hone ke baad yah varsh kii sabase zyada kamai karne vaali film bani. isane saat star screen puraskaar sahit paanch filmphere puraskaar jite the.
 raja hindustani ek yuva vyakti he jo taxi chaalak / paryatak guide ke rup main kaam karata he. aarti sahagal ek amir ladki he jo apane ateet se judane kii koshish kar rahi he.
 shri sahagal apni putree, aarti or uski sauteli maan shalini ke saath ek bhavy mahal ghar main rahete hai. aarti apni mrit maa kii yaadon ko khojane ke liye paalankhet naam ke ek chote pahari shahar main chuttee ke liye jane kaa faisla karti he. aagaman par, use pataa chalata he ki uske paas hawaii adde se paalankhet tak koi parivahan nahin he. isliye vah iklaute uplabdh driver, raja hindustani kii sevaayen leti he. paalankhet main aarti or raja rahane ke dauraan ghul mil jate hai or aakhir main pyaar main pad jate hai. ek din aarti ke pita paalankhet main aate hai or usko unke saath ghar chalane kii kehete he. aarti apane pita ko raja ke bare main bataati hai, lekin shri sahagal rishte se inkaar kar dete hai. aarti raja ko chunati he, lekin apane pita se aashirwad maangati he. uske pita apana aashirwad diye bina mumbai ke liye ravaanaa ho jate hai.
 kuch samay baad, shri sahagal apni beti ko maaf kar dete hai or paalankhet main jate hai. vahaan vah apni beti or damaad ko ek nayaa ghar dete hai. raja ghar main rahane se inkaar kar detaa he kyonki vah ise uphaar ke rup main nahin dekhtaa he, balki daan ke rup main dekhtaa he. kyonki vah garib he or shri sahagal samrudh he. shalini, uske bhai swaraj, or unke bhattije jai shri sahagal kii sampattiyon kaa puraa niyantran paane ke liye is sangharsh kaa phayda uthaane kii koshish karte hai.
 sangeet nadeem-shravan dwaara rachit kiya gaya tha. samir dawaara bol diye gaye the. 1990 ke dashak main aashiqui or dil to pagal he ke baad yah teesraa sabase acha bikne vaala album tha.
 karishma kapur ke kirdaar ke liye pehle juhi chawla ko prastaav diya gaya tha lekin unhone manaa kar diya. puja bhatta or aishwarya raay par bhi manthan kiya gaya tha.
