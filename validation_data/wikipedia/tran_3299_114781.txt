    yadi kisi sthir thos vastu par koi duusari thos vastu is tarah se rakhi jaati he kii donon samtal prishto ek-dusre ko spost karte he, to is dasha main duusari vastu ko pehali vastu par khisakane ke liye bal lagaana padataa he l is bal kaa man ek seemaa se kam hone par duusari vastu pehali vastu par nahin khisak sakati he l is kathan se yah spasht he kii donon vastue ke sampark taal ek bal gati ke vipareet disha main utpan ho jaataa he jo duusari vastu kii gati main virodh karata he l is virdohi bal ko ‘gharshan’ kehete he l
 gharshan ek bal he jo do talon ke bich saapekshik sparshi gati kaa virodh karata he. gharshan bal kaa maan donon talon ke bich abhilamb bal par nirbhar karata he.
 gharshan ke do prakaar hai: sthitik or gatij. sthitik gharshan do pindon ke sampark-prishth kii samaantar disha main lagata he, lekin gatij gharshan gati kii disha par nirbhar nahi karata.
 saamanyat: koi sataha purnataya chikani nahin hoti, apitu usamen atyalp pariman ke uthaav or gadde hote hai. inako achche sookshmdarshi dwaara hi dekha ja saktaa he. at: jab aisi do satahen ek duusare ko sparsh karti hai, to ek sataha ke uthaav duusari sataha ke gaddhon main fas jate hai. is avasthaa main ek sataha ko duusari sataha par khiskaane ke liye bal lagane par sataha kii banaavat main vikrut utpann ho jaati he. isi ke anuroop padaartho kii pratyasthata ke kaaran prayukt bal kii viruddh disha main pratibal kaary karata he, jise gharshanbal kehete hai. 
 hamaare dainik jivan main gharshan kaa bahut mahatvapurn sthaan he. prithvi kii sataha par chalanevaale prayek vaahan kii gati sataha tathaa vaahan ke aadhaar ke bich gharshanbal dwaara hi sambhav he. at: gharshan gati baadhak tathaa sadhak donon hi he. dharuk or snehakon ke vyavhaar main bhi gharshan kaa pramukh sthaan he.

 visthaapan se purv gharshanbal prayukt bal ke baraabar hota he, jise sthitik gharshan kehete he. visthaapan ke liye prayukt bal kam se kam itane pariman kaa hona chahie ki vikruti charam pratyasthata se adhik ho. visthaapan ke liye aavashyak is nunatam bal ke pariman ko charam gharshanbal kehete hai.
