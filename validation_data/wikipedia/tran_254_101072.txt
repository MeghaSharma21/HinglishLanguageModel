 1582gregory kaaldarshak kaa julian kaaldarshak kaa somvaar ko prarambh hone vaala ek aam varsh tha, jo aam yug or ano domini padnaam kaa 1582 varsh, 2 sahasrabdi kaa 582 varsh, 16 shatabdi kaa 82 varsh, or 1580 dashak kaa 3  varsh tha. 1582 ke shuruyaat par gregory kaaldarshak julian kaaldarshak se 10 din aage tha, jo pehle isai rashtron main saarvbhaumik rup se sweekrut kaaldarshak rahaa tha. haalanki, is varsh ne gregory kaaldarshak switch ke prarambh ko dekha, jab Inter gravissimas ke rup main vikhyath papele bul ne gregory kaaldarshak kaa armbh kiya, jo shuruyaat se hi spain, portugal, polish-lithuanian kaamanwealth or vartmaan samay ke italy ke adhikansh hisse ne apanaaya hua tha. in deshon main, guuruuvaar, oktober 4 tak varsh saadhaaran rup se chalata rahaa. haalanki, un deshon main aglaa din shuukravaar, oktober 15 ban gaya ; france se iskaa anusaran do maheenon baad kiya, taaki ravivaar, disambar 9 ke baad sidhaa somvaar, disambar 20 aa jaaen. kuch desh dashakon tak julian kaaldarshak kaa prayog karte rahen, to kuch kai shatabdiyon tak.
