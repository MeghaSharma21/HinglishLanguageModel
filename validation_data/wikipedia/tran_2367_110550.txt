 jyamiti main, kisi tribhuj kaa antarvrutt vah bada se bada vrutt he jo us tribhuj ke andar banaaya ja saktaa he. vastutah, antakavrutt teenon bhujaaon ko sparsh karata he. iskaa kendra 'antakkendra' kehalaata he. 
 kisi tribhuj ke teen bahirvrutt hote hai. ye teenon vrutt us tribhuj ke baahar hote hai. inamen se prayek vrutt, tribhuj kii kisi ek bhujaa ko tathaa shesh do bhujaaon ko aage badhaane se bani do rekhaon ko sparsh karata he.
