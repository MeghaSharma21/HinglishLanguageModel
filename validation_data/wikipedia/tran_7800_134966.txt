 kookar kaas yaa kookar khaansee yaa kaali khaansee jeevanu kaa sankraman hota he jo ki aarambh main naak or galaa ko prabhavit karata he. yah praayah 2 varsh se kam aayu ke bacchon kii shwasan pranali ko prabhavit karata he. is beemaari kaa namakaran is aadhaar par kiya gaya he ki is beemaari se peedit vyakti saans lete samay bhunkane jaisi aawaaj karata he. yah bordatella partusia kahalaane vaale jeevanu ke kaaran hota he. yah jeevanu vyaktiyon ke bich shwasan kriya se nishkasit jeevanu se failati he. yah tab hota he jab sankraman yukt vyakti khansate yaa cheenkte hai. yah sankraman yukt vyaktiyon ke shaareerik dravon se sampark hone se bhi failata he jaise naak kaa pani girnaa. 
 ye lakshan lagbhag 6 saptaah tak rahete hai or ye 3 charnon main vibhajit hote hai -
 in lakshanon main chheenkna, ankhon se pani aana, naak bahna, bhukh kam hona, urja kaa hraas hona or raat ke samay khansna shaamil he.
 in lakshanon main lagaataar khansate rahanaa or iske baad bhunkane jaisi aawaaj aana tab jabaki biimaar vyakti saans lene kaa prayaas karata he, aadi shaamil he.
 iske antargat sudhaar kii prakriya aati he jabaki khansna na to lagaataar hota he or na gambhir. yah str 4 saptaah se praayah aarambh hota he.
 yah cooker khaansee kam aayu ke rogiyon ko hoti he jinaki khaansee 2 saptaah tak rahati he.
 kukur khaansee bacchon main hone vaali ek sankramak tathaa khatarnaak beemaari he. mukhyat: shwasan tantra ko prabhavit karti he jo viksit or vikasshil donon prakaar ke deshon ke liye atyant chintaa kaa vishay he. bharat jaise vikasshil desh main prayek ek lakh kii aabaadi par 587 bachche prayek varsh is beemaari se grasit hote hai. isse bacchon main mrityudar 4.15 pratishat he jo ki kukur khaansee ke rogiyon kaa 10 pratishat, ek varsh ke bheetar marane vaale bacchon kaa aadha bhaag he.
 yah beemaari bordtal partusis naamak sookshmjivi se hoti he. sankraman kii saarvadhik bimaariyaan paanch varsh se kam umr main hoti hai joki nar bacchon kii apeksha maadaa bacchon main adhik hoti he. chah maheene se kam umr ke shishuon main is beemaari se mrityu-dar adhik hoti he, haalanki yah beemaari saal ke kisi bhi maah main ho sakati he, kintu jaada tathaa varsha kii ritu main isaki sambhaavna sarvaadhik hoti he. ab prashn uthta he ki yah beemaari ek bachche se duusare bachche main kis prakaar sankramit hoti he ? is beemaari se grasit bacchon kii naak bahati he, cheenk aati he or vah khaansata he. naak bahane ke kram main jo taral padaarth nikalataa he, isi ke dwaara beemaari failati he. khaansee yaa cheenk ke dauraan naak, munh tathaa saans chhodane ke kram main jeevanu aasapaas tathaa vaataavaran main fail jaataa he, jo duusare bacchon main sankramit hone kaa kaaran banataa he. yah beemaari mukhyat: bacchon kii praarambhik avasthaa main, jab unke mata-pita ko unki beemaari kaa pataa nahin chalata, khel ke maidan main, ek bichhaavan par sone or ek hi bartan main khaane-pine ke kram main failati he.
 aisi baat nahin he ki rog ke keetanuon ke sankraman ke turant baad beemaari ho jaati he. rogaanuon ke akraman ke pashchaat lakshan dikhaai padane main ek se do saptaah kaa samay lag jaataa he. sarvapratham jeevanu shwasan tantra kii bhitari sataha ke sampark main aakar sataha par chipak jaataa he, jahaan uchit madhyam paakar vruddhi karne lagata he. uske baad sataha par ghaav banataa he. falswaroop sthaan phool jaataa he. phir twachaa par necrosis kii kriya hoti he. necrosis ke kaaran anya bacteria akraman kar twachaa main teji se vruddhi karnaa shuru kar dete he.
 itani prakriya puri hone main ek-do saptaah kaa samay lag jaataa he. uske baad rog apana lakshan dikhaanaa shuru karata he, jo teen bhaagon main prakat hota he. shuru main bachche ke khaansee hoti he tathaa naak se pani bahata he. phir dhire-dhire khaansee se bachche ko vishesh pareshaani nahin hoti, kintu dhire-dhire isaki gati badhati he kyonki shwasan-tantra ko prabhavit karne kii prakriya lagaataar badhati jaati he. jaise-jaise shwasan-tantra prabhavit hota he khaansee or khansane kii gati badhati jaati he or ek samay aisa aata he ki khaansee kii vajah se rogi raat ko so nahin paata he. pehle jo keval jaagritavastha main khaansee hoti thi ab vah neend main bhi hone lagati he, jisase baccha khansate-khansate uth jaataa he.
 yah sthiti do se chaar saptaah tak bani rahati he. uske baad apana ugr or bhayankar rup dhaaran kar leti he. ab pehle kii apeksha or jaldi-jaldi khaansee kaa daura padane lagata he. is dauraan ek aisa sthiti aa jaati he ki bachche ko saans lene tak main kathinaayee hone lagati he. uske baad saans lene ke kram main ek vishesh prakaar kii aawaaj nikalne lagati he, jaise-kisi kute ke rone kii aawaaj. yah aawaaj shwasan-tantra main sankraman tathaa glatis ke khulne kii vajah se hoti he, kintu vah hoop-hoop aawaaj hameshaa nahin hoti he. uske baad khaansee ke saath gadhe rang kaa balgam nikalataa he. ek baat or ki jab khaansee apana vibhats rup dikhaa rahi hoti he to thik uske baad ulti hoti he or khaaya-piyaa sab baahar nikal jaataa he. khaansee ke dauraan jeebh kaa baar-baar munh se baahar aane ke kram main us par daant padane se ghaav ho jane kii sambhaavna atyadhik bad jaati he. baar-baar khansane kii vajah se cheharaa laal ho jaataa he. baccha chintit rahane lagata he. kai baar to atyadhik udasin apane aap main bhounchakka, kinkartavyavimudh kii sthiti main kuch samaj nahin paata ki aakhir mujhe koun-si sajaa di ja rahi he. maansik tanaav kii vajah se puure sharir se pasinaa chhootane lagata he, bhojan se virakti hone lagati he. vah bhojan karnaa nahin chahta.
 sabhi shishuon ke liye cooker khaansee kaa tikaa lagavaana aavashyak he. yah tikaakaran praayah ditipi sanyukt rup main diptheria, tetanus or cooker khaansee diya jaataa he. pehle vaala sankraman or tikaakaran jindagibhar asankrameekaran kii guarantee nahin detaa he. tathaapi 6 varsh kii aayu ke baad vardhak tikaakaran kii sifarish nahin kii jaati he jab tak ki sankraman na hua ho.
 is beemaari ke ilaaj ke liye antibiotics kaa prayog kiya jaataa he jo iske daure kii bhayaanakta ko kam kar detaa he. yadi rog kii pahchaan praarambhik avasthaa main ho jaae to antibiotics kaa prayog kaaphi laabhadaayak siddh ho saktaa he. irithromicine yaa ampisilin kaa prayog saat se das dinon tak kiya jaataa he. aajkal chlormphenicol kaa vyavhaar atyadhik kiya jaataa he kyonki yah dawa atyadhik sasti he. ghabrahat dur karne ke liye bhi inaki goliyaan di ja sakati hai. shwasan-naliyon kii sikudan ko kam karne ke liye bronkodailator diya jaataa he. kaph syrup bhi kaph ko nikaalane ke liye diya jaataa he.
