 akhaa bhagat gujarati kavi the jinka samay 1591-1656 i. mana jaataa he. ye ahmedabad ke nivasee the or baad main vahiin kii taksaal main mukhya adhikari ho gaye the. sansar se man ke virakt hone par ghar dwaar chhodkar ye tirthayatra ke liye nikle or guru kii khoj karte hue kaashi pahunche. brahmagyaan praapt kar punah ahmedabad aae.
 inhone panchikaran, guru shishy samvaad, anubhav bindu, chitt vichaar samvaad, aadi granthon kii rachana kii he. mithyaachaar, dambh, duraagrah, saamaajik durgunon aadi par bhi inhone kathor prahaar kiya he.
 akhaa bhagat 17ve shatabdi main jetalpur se ahmedabad aae the. unka sunar kaa vyavsaay tha tathaa nivas khadiya main dasaini-ni-pole main tha. aaj us jagah ko akhaa no orado yaa akhaa kaa cameraa kaha jaataa he.
