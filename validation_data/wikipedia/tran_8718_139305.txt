 osh kirgizstan ke dakshin-purv main sthit us desh kaa ek oblast he. is praant kii rajdhani kaa naam bhi osh shahar hi he. is praant kii china ke shinjiyang praant, tajikistan ke kuhistoni-badakhshaan swashasit praant or ganatantr-adheen zilon or uzbekistan ke andithan praant or faraghna praant se seemayen lagati hai.
 osh praant ke adhiktar log iske uttari bhaag ke maidani ilaake main rahete hai jo faraghna vaadi ke chor par he. dakshin main bhoomi pahari hokar alaay parvaton main parivartit ho jaati he. phir alaay vaadi aati he or uske baad alaay-paar parvat shrunkhlaa is praant or tajikistan ke bich kii sarhad par sthit hai. praant ke purv main kaara dariyaa bahata he jo uttarpashchim main narin nadi main milakar sir dariyaa banataa he.
 kirgizstan ke aadhe se zyaada uzbek log osh praant main basate hai or ve prantiy aabaadi ek lagbhag ek-tihaai hai. san 2009 kii janaganana ke anusaar praant ke samudaayon kii aabaadi is prakaar thi -
 alaay vaadi kii sari-mogol basti main ek kirghiz parivaar
 osh prantiy vishvavidhyalay
