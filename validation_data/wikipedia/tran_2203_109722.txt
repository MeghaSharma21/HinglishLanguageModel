 surya kendriya siddhant athwa surya kendriyata, ek khagoleey model he jisamen prithvi or grah soramandal ke centre main ek apekshaakrit sthir surya ke chaaron ore ghoomte he. yah shabd pracheen unani bhashaa se aayaa he . aitihaasik rup se, surya kendriyata ne bhukendriyata kaa virodh kiya tha jisane prithvi ko centre par rakhaa tha. yah dharna ki prithvi surya ke chaaron ore gumti he, samos ke aristerakas dwaara teesri shatabdi ipu ke uttarardh main prastaavit kiya gaya tha. kintu aristerakas ke surya kendriyata ne thode samay ke liye dhyaan aakarshith kiya jab tak kopernicus punerjeevit hua or iskaa savistaar hua. tathaapi, lucio ruso kaa tark he ki yah helenistic yug ke vaigyanik kaaryon ke nuksaan se utpann ek bhraamak dharna he. apratyaksh pramaan kaa prayog kar ve tark dete hai ki ek surya kendriya vichaar ko hipparkas ke gurutvkarshan par kaary main samajhaayaa gaya tha.
