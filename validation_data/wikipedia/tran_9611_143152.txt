 shamin mannaan ek bhartiya abhinayta hai. vah kai tivi dharawahikon main he sanskaar - dharohar apano kii, bhoomi kii pramukh bhoomika main tivi par sijan 1 kii prashansa or manyata praapt hui. samim ko sarvshereshth bhartiya fresh new phase ke liye bhartiya tele award main namankit kiya gaya tha. film ‘lav shagun’ ke saath bollywood main debyue kar chukin abhinetri shamin mannaan. unka janm 11 june ko dibrugarh assam main hua tha. unke pita muslim hai, jabaki unki maa hindu he. unki ek choti bahan tamanna mannaan he. disambar 2017 main shamin ne atul kumaar se vivaah kiya, jo ek anemator ke rup main kaam karata he.
