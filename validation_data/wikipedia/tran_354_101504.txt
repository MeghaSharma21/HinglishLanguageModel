 nepal ken adhikansh kshetra me hindi sthaneey bhashaa kii tarah boli jaati he or bahusankhya log hindi bol yaa samaj sakate hai. iske baavjood hindi kaa vikaas yahan maatr ek bolchaal kii bhashaa ke rup main hi ho rahaa he. gyaan-vigyaan, sahitya, samskruti or kala kii samrudh bhashaa ke rup main jo sammaan use milanaa chahie tha vah nahin mil paaya he. in paristhitiyo ko dekhte hue, hindi, bhojpuri tathaa sanskrit ke manishi evam lekhak pan. deep narayan mishra ji ne visan 2050 main nepal hindi sahitya parishd kaa gathan kiya tathaa yah sanstha satat rup se hindi ke vikaas ke liye kaam kar rahi he. is sanstha kaa aupchaarik panjikaran visan 2058 saal baishakh 2 gate ke din sampann hua.
 nepal kii adhikansh bhashaayen devanagari lipi kaa hi prayog karti he. is naate sabhi bhashaaon ke bich sumdhur sambandh-sethu banane kii aavashyaktaa he. sabhi bhashaayen paraspar sahyog se hi viksit ho sakati hai. hindi apni bhashik visheshtaon, saralata, sahajata, shabdic udaartaa or saahitiyik samruddhi ke kaaran saare vishwa me nirantar lokapriy ho rahi he. hindi kii is vyapaktaa or vishalata ke peeche vishvabhar me faile hindi bhashion kaa mahatvapurn yogadan he. vishwa kaa shaayad hi koi desh ho, jahaan koi hindi bhashi yaa hindi premi na ho. hindi sahi myne me vishwa bhashaa banane kii ore agrasar he. lekin yah yatra chunautiyon se bhari he. maujuuda chunautiyon kaa vyaavhaarik samaadhaan dhundne tathaa samay saapeksh gatishiltaa evam vikaas ke liye nepal hindi sahitya parishad kritasankalp he.
