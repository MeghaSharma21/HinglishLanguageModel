 gaay ek mahatvapurn paaltu jaanavar he jo sansar main praaia: sarvatra pai jaati he. isse uttam kism kaa doodh praapt hota he. hindu, gaay ko 'mata' kehete hai. iske bachade bade hokar gaadi khinchate hai evam kheton kii jutai karte hai. 
 bharat main vaidic kaal se hi gaay kaa vishesh mahatva rahaa he. aarambh main aadan pradaan evam vinimay aadi ke madhyam ke rup main gaay upyog hota tha or manushya kii samruddhi kii gananaa uski gosankhya se kii jaati thi. hindu dharmik drushti se bhi gaay pavitra maani jaati rahi he tathaa uski hatya mahapatak paapon main kii jaati he. 
 bharat main gaay kii 30 naslein pai jaati hai. red sindhi, sahivaal, gir, devni, tharaparkar aadi naslein bharat main dudharu gaayon kii pramukh naslein hai. 
 lokopayogi drushti main bhartiya gaay ko teen virgon main vibhajit kiya ja saktaa he. pehle varg main ve gaayen aati hai jo doodh to khub detii hai, lekin unki punsantan akarmanya at: krishi main anupayogi hoti he. is prakaar kii gaayen dugdhpradhan ekangi nasl kii hai. duusari gaayen ve hai jo doodh kam detii hai kintu unke bachade krishi or gaadi khinchne ke kaam aate hai. inhen vatsapradhan ekangi nasl kehete hai. kuch gaayen doodh bhi prachur detii hai or unke bachade bhi karmath hote hai. aisi gaayon ko sarvangi nasl kii gaay kehete hai. bharat kii gozatiyaan nimnlikhit hai:
 saiwal gaay kaa mul sthaan pakistan main he. in gaayon kaa sir chaudaa ubhara hua, sing choti or moti, tathaa maathaa majhola hota he. bharat main ye rajasthan ke bikaner,sriganganagar, punjab main maantagumari jila or raavi nadi ke aasapaas lailpur, lodhran, ganjeevar aadi sthanon main pai jaati he. ye bharat main kahin bhi rah sakati hai. ek baar byaane par ye 10 maheene tak doodh detii rahati hai. doodh kaa pariman prati din 10-20 litre pratidin hota he. inke doodh main makkhan kaa ansh paryaapt hota he.
 inkaa mukhya sthaan sindh kaa kohistaan kshetra he. bilochistan kaa kelsbela ilaaka bhi inke liye prasiddh he. in gaayon kaa varna badami yaa gehuna, sharir lanbaa or chamada motaa hota he. ye duusari jalwaayu main bhi rah sakati hai tathaa inamen rogon se ladane kii adbhut shakti hoti he. santanotpatti ke baad ye 300 din ke bheetar kam se kam 2000 litre doodh detii hai.
 kuccha kii choti khadi se dakshin-purv kaa bhubhag, arthath sindh ke dakshin-pashchim se ahmedabad or radhanpura tak kaa pradesh, kankrej gaayon kaa moolsthaan he. vaise ye kathiyawad, badoda or surat main bhi milti hai. ye sarvangi jati kii gaae hai or inaki maang videshon main bhi he. inkaa rang rupahalaa bhura, lohia bhura yaa kaala hota he. taangon main kaale chihn tathaa khuron ke upree bhaag kaale hote hai. ye sir uthaakar lambey or sam kadam rakhti hai. chalate samay taangon ko chhodkar shesh sharir niskriy prathit hota he jisase inaki chaal atpati maaloom padati he.
 ye gaayen dudharu nahin hotin. inkaa rang khaaki hota he tathaa gardan kuch kaali hoti he. avasthaa badhane par rang safed ho jaataa he. ye gwalior ke aas-paas pai jaati hai.
 inkaa prapthisthaan jodhpur ke aas-paas kaa pradesh he. ye gaayen bhi vishesh dudharu nahin hotin, kintu byaane ke baad bahut dinon tak thodaa-thodaa doodh detii rahati hai.
 ye gaayen dudharu hoti hai. inkaa rang khaaki, bhura, yaa safed hota he. kuccha, jaisalmer, jodhpur, bikaner or sindh kaa dakshinpashchimi registan inkaa prapthisthaan he. inaki khuraaq kam hoti he. iskaa doodh 10 se 16 litre pratidin tak hota he.
 pilibhit, puuranpur tehsil or khiri inkaa prapthisthaan he. inkaa munh sankaraa or sing sidhi tathaa lambii hoti he. singon kii labaai 12-18 inch hoti he. inaki punch lambii hoti he. ye swabhaav se krodhi hoti he or doodh kam detii hai.
 naadee nadi kaa tatavarti pradesh inkaa prapthisthaan he. jowar inkaa priy bhojan he. naadee ghaas or uski roti banaakar bhi inhen khilaai jaati he. ye gaayen doodh khub detii hai.
 punjab ke deraagajikhan jile main pai jaati hai. ye doodh kam detii hai.
 doodh saadhaaran maatra main detii he. prapthisthaan satapura kii tarai, wardha, chhindwara, nagpur, siwani tathaa bahiyar he. inkaa rang safed or kad majhola hota he. ye kaan uthaakar chalati hai.
 ye 8-12 litre doodh pratidin detii hai. gaayon kaa rang safed, motiyaa yaa halkaa bhura hota hai. ye unche kad or gathile badan kii hoti hai tathaa sir uthaakar chalati hai. inkaa prapthisthaan rohtak, hisar, sirsa, karnal, gudgaanv or jind he. bharat kii paanch sabase shreshth naslo main haryaanavi nasl aati he. yah adbhut he.
 ye gaayen dudharu, sundar or manthargamini hoti hai. prapthisthaan tamilnadu, andhra pradesh, guntur, nilor, bapttala tathaa sadanpalli he. ye chaara kam khaati hai.
 is gaay kaa mul mul sthaan rajasthan main bikaner, sriganganagar hai. ye laal-safed chakte vaali,kaale-safed,laal, bhuri,kaali, aadi kai rangon kii hoti he. ye khaati kam or doodh khub detii hai. ye pratidin kaa 10 se 20 litre tak doodh detii he. is par pashu vishvavidhyalay bikaner rajasthan main research bhi kaaphi hua he. isaki sabase badi khaasiyat, ye apane aap ko bharat ke kisi bhi kone main dhaal leti he.
 gir- ye pratidin 50-80 litre doodh detii hai. inkaa moolsthaan kathiyawad kaa gir jangal he.rajasthan me randa va ajmeri ke naam se janaa jaataa he
 devni - dakshin andhra pradesh or hinsol main pai jaati hai. ye doodh khub detii he.
 nimadi - narmada nadi kii ghati inkaa prapthisthaan he. ye gaayen dudharu hoti hai.
 amritmahal, hallikar, bargur, baalambaadi naslein mysore kii vatsapradhan, ekangi gaayen hai. kangaayam or krishnavalli doodh denevaali hai.
