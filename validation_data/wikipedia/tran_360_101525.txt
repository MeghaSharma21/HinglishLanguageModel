 vran yaa ulcer sharirprishth par sankraman dwaara utpann hota he. is sankraman ke jeevvish sthanik upakalaa ko nasht kar dete hai. nasht hui upakalaa ke upar mrit koshikaaye evam pus sanchit ho jaataa he. mrit koshikaon tathaa pus ke hat jane par nasht hui upakalaa ke sthaan par dhire dhire kanikamay utak aane lagate hai. is prakaar kii vikshati ko vran kehete hai. duusare shabdon main sankramanoparant upakalaa utak kii koshikeey mrityu ko vran kehete he.
 kisi bhi prishth ke upar, athwa parshv main, yadi koi shodhyukt parigalit bhaag ho gaya he, to vahaan vran utpann ho jayega. sheeghra bhar janevaale vran ko sudamya vran kehete hai. kabhi-kabhi koi vran sheeghra nahin bharta. aisa vran dudamya ho jaataa he, iskaa kaaran yah he ki usamen yaa to jeevanuon dwaara sankraman hota rahata he, yaa vranvale bhaag main rakt parisancharan uchit rup se nahin ho paata. vran, prishth par kii ek koshika ke baad duusari koshika ke nasht hone par, banataa he. 
 nimnlikhit teen aise sthaan he jahaan par vran praaia: ho jate hai :
  mukh, amashay athwa antra - inamen vikruti dwaara utpann shothyukt parigalan hota he 
  nimna shakhaon ke adhstvak - inamen vriddhavastha main rakt parisancharan ke uchit rup main na hone ke kaaran shoth utpann ho jaataa he, jisase parigalan hona prarambh ho jaataa he.
  garbhashay-greeva 
 vran kaa jivan nimnlikhit teen pravasthaon main vibhajit he :-
  vistaar, parivart tathaa sudhaar 
 vistaar kii pravastha main vran kaa tal srav evam galit padaarthon se dhankaa rahata he. vran ke parisar teevr hote hai tathaa isamen se pusmukt srav nikalnaa rahata he.
 parivart pravastha main vran kaa bharna prarambh hone lagata he. iske tal kaa bhaag saaf hone lagata he. tal main kanikamay utak banane prarambh ho jate hai or aapas main judane ke kaaran sampurna tal inamen dhank jaataa he.
 sudhaar kii pravastha main kanikamay reshedaar tantu utak main, parivartit ho jate hai. kanikamay utakon kaa adhik bananaa bhi uchit nahin he. yadi kisi vran main kanikamay utak adhik ban gaye hon, to unako khurch dena chahie athwa silver nitrate jaise kisi costic padaarth se jala dena chahie.
 vran nimnlikhit teen prakaar ke hote hai :
  vishishti, vishistataahin tathaa durdamy .
 iske hone kaa kaaran kshat kaa sankraman he. yah kshat abhighaat, athwa kinhin uttejk padaarthon, ke kaaran ho jaataa he. sthanik kshobh, jaisaa dantavran main, athwa rakt-parisancharan-baadha, jaisaa sphit shiraaon main, iske utpann karne main praathmik kaaran hai. poshanj vran vahika prerak niyantran ke anauchity se sambandhit he. aswasthavastha main yah vran ke bharne main baadhak he.
 ye kuch vishisht rogon ke sookshm jeevon ke sankraman ke kaaran utpann hote hai. ye rog he : yakshma, sifalis aadi. in vranon kii chikitsa karte samay sthanik chikitsa ke atirikt vishisht rog kii chikitsa bhi kerani hoti he.
 yah kisi sankraman kii shothaj pratikriya ke falswaroop utpann nahin hota, apitu durdamy arbud dwaara utakon ko nasht karne ke kaaran hota he. iske dwaara utpann vran ke parisar arbud main hi villin ho jate hai. yah vran atishighrata se badhata he. durdamy arbud hai : 
 utakon kii jeevanshkti bighad jaati he, jiske falswaroop sankraman bhi apana prabhaav daalata rahata he. yahi kaaran he ki vran ke ropan main adhik samay lag jaataa he nimna avasthaa main vran ke sheeghra nahin bharte :
  tantrika kshat ,
  sphit shiraaon ke kaaran rudhir sankulata evam kuposhan tathaa
  utakon main sanvardhan madhyam kii adhik maatra main upasthit, arthath madhumeh main sharkara kaa hona aadi.
