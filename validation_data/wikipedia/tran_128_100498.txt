 formula van, jise formula 1 yaa F1 ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he or jise aaj ke daur main aadhikarik taur par FIA formula van world championships ke rup main sanderbhit kiya jaataa he, federation international di l'automobile dwaara sweekrut auto racing kaa ucchtam varg he. is naam main nihit "formula" shabd niyamon ke ek set ko sanderbhit karata he jiskaa sabhi pratibhaagiyon ke kaaron ko paalan karnaa chahie. F1 satr main daud kii ek shrinkhalaa hoti he jise grands pricks ke rup main janaa jaataa he or prayojan-nirmit parikrama sthalon or kuch had tak, purv saarvjaanik sadakon or shahar kii band sadakon main yojit hota he. do vaarshik world championshipss kaa nirdhaaran karne ke liye prayek daud ke parinaamon ko sanyukt kiya jaataa he, jisamen se ek championships driveron ke liye or ek nirmaataaon ke liye hota he jiske saath main daud driver, nirmaataa dal, trek adhikari, aayojak or ve parikrama sthal bhi shaamil hote hai jo vaid super licenson ke aayojakon ke rup main hone ke liye aavashyak he jo FIA dwaara jari kiya jane vaala ucchtam varg racing license he.
 ek formula ke aadhaar par chakkar lagane vaale ingine se yukt 360 kimi/ghanta tak kii ucch gati kaa formula van car res kii seemaa 18,000 rpm thi. in kaaron main kuch konon par 5 g kii adhikadhik khichaav kii kshamta he. karon kaa pradarshan electronics, vayugatiki, nilamban or pahiyon par bahut zyada nirbhar he. formula ne khel ke itihaas ke madhyam se kai vikaas or parivartan dekha he.
 europe, formula van kaa paranfrinaat centre he jahaan sabhi timen aadhaarit hai or jahaan aadhe se zyada res hote hai. haalanki, haal ke varshon main khel ke kshetra main kaaphi vistaar hua he or grands pricks kaa aayojan duniya bhar main hota he. asia or sudur purv ke reson ke paksh main europe or americas main pratiyogitaaon main kami ai he—2009 main satraa reson main se aath reson kaa aayojan europe ke baahar hua tha.
 formula van ek vishaal television kaarykram he jiske kul vaishvik darshakon kii sankhya 6000 lakh prati res he. formula van group, vanijyik adhikaron kaa kanoonee dharak he. duniya ke sabase mahange khel ke rup main iskaa aarthik prabhaav mahatvapurn he or isaki vittiya evam raajnneetik ladaaiyon ko vyapak sthaan pradaan kiya jaataa he. iskaa ucch profil or isaki lokapriyata ise ek spasht kray-vikray vaataavaran banaata he, jiske parinamswaroop aayojakon kaa bahut zyada nivesh hota he jo nirmaataaon ke bahut adhik bajet kaa rup dhaaran kar letaa he. haalanki, zyaadaatar 2000 ke baad se, kharchon main hameshaa vruddhi hote rahane ke kaaran kai tiimon, jisame car banaanewaalon ke liye kaam karne vaale team or motor vaahan udyog se bahut kam samarthan paane vaale team shaamil hai, kaa diwaala nikal gaya he yaa un companiyon dwaara kharid liya gaya he jo khel ke bheetar ek team kii sthapana karnaa chaahate hai; in kharidariyon par bhi formula van kaa asar padataa he jo pratibhaagi tiimon kii sankhya ko simit kar detaa he.
 formula van shrinkhalaa kii shuruyaat 1920 or 1930 ke dashak ke european grand pricks motor racing se hui he. "formula" niyamon kaa ek set hai jise sabhi pratibhaagiyon or kaaron ko jarur puraa karnaa chahie. formula van 1946 main dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke baad sweekrut ek nayaa formula tha jiske saath us varsh yojit hone vaale pehle gaiyr-championships reson kaa astitv tha. kai grand pricks racing sanghathanon ne yuddh se pehle ek world championships ke liye kai niyamon kaa bandobast kiya tha, lekin sangharsh ke dauraan racing ke nilamban ke kaaran world drivers championships ko 1947 tak aupchaarik rup pradaan nahin kiya gaya. pratham world championships res kaa aayojan 1950 main united kingdom ke silverstone main kiya gaya. uske peeche-peeche 1958 main nirmaataaon ke liye ek championships kaa aayojan kiya gaya. 1960 or 1970 ke dashak main dakshin africa or UK main rashtriya championships kaa astitv tha. gaiyr-championships formula van pratiyogitaaon kaa aayojan kai saalon tak kiya gaya lekin pratiyogita ke kharch main lagaataar vruddhi hone ke kaaran inamen se antim pratiyogita kaa aayojan 1983 main kiya gaya.
 khel ke kitaab, formula van, sanket karata he ki iskaa iraadaa FIA ke racing formulon main se sabase unnat or sabase pratispardhi formula banane kaa he.
 1950 main italvi giusape forina ne apane alpha romeo main badi mushkil se apane argentini team-saathi juaan minial phangiyo ko haraakar pehla formula van world championships jeet liya. haalanki phangiyo ne 1951, 1954, 1955, 1956 or 1957 main is kitaab ko hasil kiya, lekin do baar champion rah chuke pheraari ke alberto askari ne unki is jeet kii daur ko avaruddh kar diya. haalanki britain ke sterling moss niyamit rup se pratispardhaa karne main sakasham the, lekin ve world championships jeetne main kabhi sakasham nahin hue or ab unhen vyapak rup se kabhi kitaab nahin jeetne vaala mahaan driver mana jaataa he. haalanki, phangiyo ko formula van ke pehle dashak tak is championships par haaavee rahane ke liye yaad kiya jaataa he or lambe samay se unhen formula van kaa "grand master" mana jaataa he.
 is avadhi par road kar nirmaataaon—alpha romeo, pheraari, marcedes benz or maazerati —dwaara sanchalit tiimon ne vishesh dhyaan dilaayaa. pehle satron kaa sanchaalan yuddh se pehle ke kaaron, jaise- alfaa kaa 158, kaa istemaal karke kiya gaya. in kaaron main ingine saamne ke taraf hote the, tair patale hote the or inke ingine 1.5 litre vaali ati-aaweshit yaa 4.5 litre vaali saamanytah chooshit ingine hote the. uplabdh formula van kaaron kii kami se sambandhit chintaaon ke kaaran apekshaakrit chote or kam shaktishaalee kaaron ke liye formula tu ke viniyamon ke tahat 1952 or 1953 main world championships kaa sanchaalan kiya gaya. jab 2.5 litre kii seemaa vaale injanon se yukt ek nae formula van ko 1954 main world championships ke liye behaal kiya gaya, tab marcedes-benz ne unnat W196 prastut kiya jisamen desmodromik valve or eendhan antalakshepan ke saath-saath sanlagn suvyavasthit dhaancha jaise navachaar shaamil the. 1955 ke le mans aapadaa ke prakat hone ke samay sabhi motorsport se team kii vaapasi se pehle marcedes driveron ne do varshon tak championships hasil kii.
 1950 ke dashak main pehla pramukh takneekee vikaas hua, arthath kupar ne madhya-ingine yukt kaaron ko phir se prastut kiya, jiskaa udbhav company ke formula 3 kii safal diginon se hua tha. 1959, 1960 or 1966 main world champion rah chuke australia ke jaik brabaham ne jald hi nae dizin kii shreshthata sabit kii. 1961 tak sabhi niyamit pratiyogiyon ne madhya-ingine yukt kaaron ko apana liya tha.
 pehle british world champion mike hothorn the jinhone 1958 main kitaab hasil karne ke liye pheraari kaa upyog kiya. haalanki, jab colin chapman ne F1 main ek chasis diziner or baad main team lotus ke sansthaapak ke rup main pravesh kiya, british racing green ne agle dashak tak kshetra main apana prabhutv sthapit karne safalataa praapt kii. brabaham, gym clark, jaiki stewart, jon surtis, graham hill or deni hulm ke bich british tiimon or kaamanwealth driveron ne 1962 or 1973 ke bich baarah world championships hasil kiye.
 1962 main, lotus ne paaramparik spes frem dizin ke bajaay ek aluminium shit monocoq yukt ek car prastut kiya. yah madhya-injayukt kaaron ke armbh ke baad se ab tak kii sabase badi takneekee safalataa sabit hui. 1968 main, lotus ne apane kaaron par imparial tobaco kaa chitra ankit kiya or is prakaar khel ke liye praayojan kaa armbh kar diya.
 vayugatikeey nimnabal ne dhire-dhire 1960 ke dashak ke antim daur main ayarofoil kii maujuudagi se car kii dizin main mahatv hasil kar liya. 1970 ke antim daur main, lotus ne bhu-prabhaav vayugatiki prastut kiya jisane bahut zyada nimnabal pradaan kiya or cornring gati main kaaphi vruddhi kii main istemaal kiya gaya tha). trek ke liye kaaron par dabaav daalane vaale vayugatikeey bal bahut adhik the, ek nirantar sawari kii oonchai ko barkaraar rakhane ke liye atyant kathor spring kii jarurat thi, nilamban ko lagbhag thos avasthaa main chhod diya gaya tha, sadak ke dharaatal main aniyamittaaon se car or driver kii thodi si bhi suraksha ke liye puri tarah se tiron par hi nirbhar tha.
 1970 ke dashak kii shuruyaat main, barney iklestone ne formula van ke vanijyik adhikaron ke prabandhan kii punahvyavastha kii; khel ko aaj ke is rup main arabon dollar ke vyavsaay ke rup main parinat karne kaa shrey unheen ko diya jaataa he. jab iklestone ne 1971 brabaham team ko kharid liya tab unhen formula van constructors association main ek seat mil gaya or 1978 main iske adhyaksh ban gaye. pehle parikrama sthal ke maalik team ke aay ko niyantrit karte the or ek duusare ke saath vyaktigat rup se baatcheet karte the, lekin iklestone ne FOCA ke madhyam se "ek jhund ke rup main shikaar" karne ke liye tiimon ko raji kar liya. unhone parikrama sthal ke maalikon ke saamne formula van kii peshkash ek aise package ke rup main kii jise ve le yaa chhod sakate the. is package ke badale main lagbhag sabhi ko trekside vigyaapan kaa aatmasamarpan karne kii aavashyaktaa he.
 1979 main federation international du sport automobile ke gathan ne FISA-FOCA yuddh kii shuruyaat kii jiske dauraan FISA or iske adhyaksh jin-mairi belaster kaa television rajasav or takneekee viniyamon ko lekar FOCA se baar-baar takaraav hua. da gargian ne FOCA ke bare main kaha ki iklestone or maiks mosle "ne ek bahu dirghakaalik drushtikon apanaate hue ek chhapamar yuddh kii shuruyaat karne ke liye iskaa istemaal kiya." FOCA ne ek pratidwandvi shrinkhalaa kii sthapana karne kii dhamkee di, grand pricks kaa bahishkaar kiya or FISA ne reson se apni sweekruti waapas le lee. iskaa parinaam 1981 ke concord agreement ke rup main saamne aayaa jisane takneekee sthirataa kii guarantee di kyonki tiimon ko nae viniyamon kii uchit soochna deni di. haalanki FISA ne TV rajasav ke apane adhikaar kii maang kii, lekin isane un adhikaron kaa prashasan FOCA ke haathon main saunp diya.
 FISA ne 1983 main bhu-prabhaav vayugatiki par pratibandh laga diya. tab tak, tathaapi, terbocharged ingine, jise 1977 main reynolt kaa maargdarshan praapt hua tha, 700 brake ashwashakti se adhik utpaadan kar rahaa tha or iskaa pratispardhi hona aavashyak tha. 1986 tak ek BMW terbocharged ingine ne 5.5 baar ke dabaav kaa ek flash pathan hasil kiya jiske italian grand pricks ke liye aharta praapt karne main 1,300 brake ashwashakti se adhik hone kaa anumaan tha. agle varsh res trim kaa dabaav 1,100 brake ashwashakti ke aasapaas pahunch gaya jiskaa badha hua dabaav keval 4.0 baar tak hi simit tha. ye car ab tak ke sabase shaktishaalee open-hvil circuit racing car the. ingine kii shakti ke utpaada or is tarah se isaki gati ko kam karne ke liye FIA ne 1984 main eendhan tanks kii kshamta ko simit kar diya or 1989 main puri tarah se terbocharged ingine par pratibandh lagane se pehle 1988 main bade hue dabaav ko bhi simit kar diya.
 electronic driver sahaayta ke vikaas kii shuruyaat 1980 ke dashak main hui. lotus ne sakriy nilamban kii ek pranali viksit kerani shuru kii jo sabase pehle 1982 main F1 ke lotus 91 or lotus aspirit road car par dikhaai diya. 1987 tak, is pranali ko nipun bana diya gaya tha or us varsh monaco grand pricks main yrton senna kii jeet ke liye ise chalaaya gaya tha. 1990 ke dashak main shuru main, anya tiimon ne ise apanaaya or arddh swachaalit gearbox evam karshan niyantran ek prakritik pragati the. driver kii dakshata se kahin zyada daud pratiyogitaaon ke parinaam kaa nirdhaaran praudyogiki kar rahi thi, is shikayaton ke kaaran FIA ne 1994 ke liye aisi sahaaytaaon par pratibandh laga diya. iske parinamswaroop electronic sahaaytaaon par pehle nirbhar rahane vaali kaaren bahut "jatkedaar" ban gai thi or unhen chalaane main mushkilon kaa saamana karnaa pad rahaa tha ) or kai paryvekshakon ko laga ki driver kii sahaaytaaon par lage pratibandh keval naam bhar the kyonki ve "prabhavi dhang se police ke liye mushkil sabit hue hai".
 tiimon ne 1992 main duusare or 1997 main tisare concord agreement par hastakshar kiya jisaki avadhi 2007 ke antim din ko samaapt ho gai.
 trek par, 1980 or 1990 ke dashak main maclaren or williams tiimon kaa prabhutv tha or saath main brabaham bhi 1980 ke dashak ke praarambhik bhaag main pratispardhaa karte rahe hai or nelson picket ke saath do drivers championshipss main jeet darj kii. porsh, honda or marcedes-benz ke sanchaalan main maclaren ne us avadhi main solah championshipss main jeet darj kii jabaki williams ne bhi solah khitaabon ko jeetne ke liye ford, honda or reynolt ke injanon kaa istemaal kiya. racing lemonds yrton senna or alain prost ke bich kii pratidwandvita 1988 main F1 kaa centre bindu ban gaya or 1993 ke ant main prost ke nivrutt hone tak chalata rahaa. 1994 ke san marino grand pricks main williams ke prost kii lead drive se aage nikalne kii hode main kukhyat mode temburelo ke prasthaan sthal kii diwaar se durghatnagrast hone ke baad sannaa kii maut ho gai. FIA ne us saptahanth ke baad se khel ke suraksha maanakon main sudhaar laane ke liye kaam kiya jis saptahanth ke dauraan rolland retznburger ko bhi shanivaar kii aharta praapt karne kii pratiyogita main apane jivan se haath dhonaa padaa. tab se formula van car ke pahiye ke trek par kisi bhi driver kii maut nahin hui he, haalanki do trek marshalon ko apane jivan se haath dhonaa padaa he jisamen se ek kii maut 2000 ke italian grand pricks main or duusare kii maut 2001 ke australian grand pricks main hui thi.
 yrton senna or rolland retznburger kii maut ke baad se FIA ne suraksha ko niyam main parivartan karne vaale ek kaaran ke rup main istemaal kiya he jise anyathaa, concord agreement ke tahat, sabhi tiimon kii sahamati praapt honi thi — jisamen se sabase khaas 1998 ke liye shuru kiye gaye parivartan the. is tathakthith 'sankeern trek' yug ke parinamswaroop aise kaaron kaa nirmaan hua jiske pichley tair pehle se chote the, samgr trek pehle se adhik sankeern ho gaye the or yantric pakad ko kam karne ke liye 'khanchedaar' tiron kaa prayog shuru ho gaya. aage or peeche ke tiron main chaar khaanche hote the — haalanki pehle varsh ke shuru main aage ke tiron main teen khaanche hote the — jo tair kii sampoorn paridhi ke madhyam se daudte the. modane kii gati ko kam karnaa or tair evam trek ke bich ek chota sa contact ptch ko lagu karke varsha ke halaaton kii tarah kii racing paidaa karnaa hi iskaa uddeshy tha. FIA ke anusaar yah sab driver kii dakshata ko protsaahit karne or ek behatar tamaasha pradaan karne ke liye tha.
 iske mili-jule parinaam mili hai kyonki yantric pakad ke abhaav ke parinamswaroop adhik saral dizinron ne vayugatikeey pakad ke saath ghate ko khatm karne kaa prayaas kiya he — jiske liye unhone dainon, vayugatikeey upkarnon, aadi ke madhyam se tiron par adhik bal lagaaya he — jiske badale main kam prabhavi parinaam mila he kyonki ye upkaran car ke peeche ke raste ko 'ganda' banane kii koshish karte hai or anya kaaron ko bahut najadik se peechha karne se rokte hai kyonki 'saafe' hawa par unki nirbharta car ko trek par rukne ke liye vivash kar detii he. khanchedaar tiron kaa bhi shuru main durbhagyapurna dushprinaam tha kyonki ve ek adhik kathin ghere se nirmit the or gruve treed blox ko pakadaney ke liye sakasham hona tha jiske parinamswaroop vayugatikeey pakad ke vifal hone ke samay pradarshneey durghatnaayen hoti thi kyonki adhik kathin ghere trek ko acchhee tarah se nahin pakad pate the.
 maclaren, williams, reynolt ) or pheraari ke driveron, jinhen "big four" kii oopaadhi di gai, ne 1984 se 2008 tak ke prayek world championships main jeet hasil kii he. 1990 ke dashak ke praudyogikeey unnati ke kaaran, formula van main pratispardhaa kii laagat main naatakeey rup se vruddhi hui. is vardhit vittiya boj or saath main chaaron tiimon ke prabhutv ke kaaran garib swatantra tiimon ko na keval pratispardhi bane rahane ke liye, balki vyavsaay main bhi apana astitv banaae rakhane ke liye sangharsh karnaa padaa. vittiya pareshaaniyon ne kai tiimon ko peeche hatane par majaboor kar diya. 1990 ke baad se, atthaees team formula van se baahar ho gaye hai. isane jordan ke purv maalik edi jordan ko yah kehane ke liye prerit kar diya he ki pratispardhi privacrs ke din khatm ho chuke hai.
 maaikel shoomakar or pheraari ne 1999 or 2004 ke bich abhootpurv dhang se lagaataar paanch drivers or chah constructors championshipss jeet lee. shoomakar ne kai nae record banaae, jinamein grand pricks kii jeet, ek satr main hasil kii gai jeet or sabase adhik drivers championshipss ke record shaamil the. shoomakar ke championships ke kram kaa ant 25 sitambar 2005 ko hua jab reynolt ke driver furnando alonso us samay ke formula van ke sabase yuva champion bane. 2006 main, reynolt or alonso ne donon kitaab phir se hasil kar liya. formula van main solah varsh ke baad 2006 ke ant main shoomakar sevanivrutt hue lekin 2010 ke satr ke liye sevanivritti ke baad bhi upasthit hue or navgathit marcedes GP ke liye racing kii.
 is avadhi ke dauraan championships ke niyamon main FIA dwaara kai baar parivartan kiye gaye jiskaa uddeshy on-trek ection main sudhaar or laagat ko kam karnaa tha. team ke adeshon, jo 1950 main championships ke shuru hone ke baad se vaid tha, par kai ghatnaon ke baad 2002 main pratibandh laga diya gaya jisamen tiimon ne khuleam res ke parinaamon main herpher kiya tha, nakaaratmak prachaaron ko janm diya tha, jisamen se 2002 ke australian grand pricks main pheraari dwaara kiya gaya herpher yaa nakaaratmak prachaar kaaphi mashhoor tha. anya parivartanon main aharta praapt karne kaa formet, ank scoring pranali, takneekee vinium or ve niyam shaamil the jo yah nirdisht karte the ki injanon or tiron ko kab tak chalanaa chahie. michelin or brigestone aapurtikartaaon ke bich ke 'tair yuddh' main kai baar giravat dekhi gai lekin indianapolis main 2005 ke united stats grand pricks main das main se saat tiimon ne res main bhaag nahin liya jab unke michelin tiron ko upyog ke liye asurakshit mana gaya. 2006 ke dauraan, maiks mosle ne formula van ke liye ek 'harith' bhavishya kaa ullekh kiya jisamen urja kaa kushal upyog ek mahatvapurn kaarak ban jayega. or tair yuddh samaapt ho gaya kyonki brigestone 2007 ke satr ke liye formula van kaa ekmaatr tair aapurtikarta ban gaya.
 1983 ke baad se formula van par williams, maclaren or beneton jaisi visheshgy res tiimon kaa prabhutv kaayam rahaa jinhone marcedes benz, honda, reynolt or ford jaise bade car nirmaataaon dwaara aapurti kii gai injanon kaa upyog kiya tha. 1985 ke ant main alpha romeo or reynolt ke prasthaan ke baad se pehali baar ford ke bade paimaane par asafal jaguaar team ke nirmaan ke saath 2000 main shuru hone vaale nae nirmaataaon ke malikana vaale tiimon ne formula van main pravesh kiya. 2006 tak nirmaataaon ke tiimon–reynolt, BMW, toyota, honda or pheraari –ne constructors championships main pratham chah sthanon main se paanch sthaan praapt karke championships par apana prabhutv sthapit kar liya. ekmaatr apwaad maclaren tha jis par kuch had tak marcedes-benz kaa swaamitv tha. grand pricks menuphacturers association ke madhyam se unhone formula van ke vanijyik laabh ke ek bahut bade hisse or khel ko chalaane main ek bahut bade samjhaute par baatcheet kii.
 2008 or 2009 main honda, BMW or toyota sabhi aarthik mandi ko dosha dete hue ek varsh ke bheetar hi formula 1 kii racing se peeche hat gaye. isse khel par se nirmaataaon kaa prabhutv samaapt ho gaya. 2010 ke satr main marcedes benz ne bron GP kharidne ke baad ek nirmaataa ke rup main phir se khel main pravesh kiya or maclaren ke saath 15 satron ke baad isse alag ho gaya. isse khel main ekmaatr car nirmaataaon ke rup main marcedes, reynolt or pheraari hi rah jate hai. AT&T williams ne 2009 ke antim samay main cosworth ke saath apane nae ingine saude kii pushti kii jo nae tiimon USF1, virgin racing, hispania racing F1 or navgathit lotus F1 team kii bhi aapurti karegaa. car nirmaataaon ke prasthaan ne apane-apane deshon kaa pratinidhitiv karne vaale tiimon kaa bhi maarg prashast kar diya he jinamein se kuch ko apane-apane desh kii sarkaron kaa vittiya samarthan praapt he or inamen se kuch aise bhi hai jo 1930 ke dashak ke baad se dikhaai nahin die the. in "rashtriyata kii tiimon" main force india, USF1 or lotus shaamil hai jo kramshah bharat, sanyukt rajya america or malaysia kaa pratinidhitiv kar rahe hai.
 formula van ke niyantran kii ladaai federation international du sport automobile, jo kabhi FIA kaa ek swytt upasmiti tha or FOCA ke bich ladi gai.
 vivaad kii shuruyaat ke kai kaaran hai or ho saktaa he ki antarnihit kaarnon main se kai kaaran itihaas main kho chuke ho. tiimon kaa vichaar tha ki bade or behatar vittposhit tiimon ke khilaaf pratispardhaa karne ke unke adhikaron or kshamta par pramukh nirmaataaon kii taraf niyantrak sanghathan ke bhaag ke purvaagrah kaa nakaaratmak prabhaav pad rahaa tha.
 iske alaava, ladaai khel ke vanijyik pahaluon or takneekee viniyamon ke ird-gird gumti rahi or jiskaa FOCA ke vichaar se ajnaulanghan kii prakruti se adhik ajnaulanghan karne vaale kii prakruti ke anusaar lacheela hone kii pravriti thi.
 maheenon baad yuddh kaa ant 1982 ke san marino grand pricks ke ek FOCA bahishkaar ke rup main hua. siddhant kii drushti se sabhi FOCA tiimon ko viniyamon or vittiya kshatipurti kii handlling kii ekajutata or shikaayat ke ek chihn ke rup main grand pricks kaa bahishkaar karnaa tha . karm kii drushti se FOCA kii kai tiimon ne "praayojak dayitvon" kaa hawala dete hue bahishkaar se samarthan waapas le liya. inamen se ullekhneey tyrel or tollman team the.

 is niyam ke kuch apwaad bhi hai, jaise - 1993 or 1994 main, jab maujuuda world drivers champion formula van main pratispardhaa nahin kar rahe the. is maamle main pichley varsh ke champion ke team ke driveron ko number 0 or 2 die jate hai. number 13 ko 1976 ke baad se istemaal nahin kiya gaya he, jiske pehle vyaktigat res aayojakon ke vivekadhikaar par kabhi-kabhi ise pradaan kiya gaya. 1996 se pehle, keval world championships jeetne vaale driver or uski team aam taur par pichley champion ke saath numberon main pher-badal karte the-shesh ke paas purv varshon ke unke apane number hote the kyonki unhen mul rup se 1974 ke satr ke armbh main nirdhaarit kiya gaya tha. kai varshon tak, udhaaharan ke taur par, pheraari ke paas number 27 or 28 the chaahe world championships main unki samaapti sthiti jo bhi rahi ho.
 jochen rindt hi ekmaatr aise maranoparant world champion hai jinke kul ankon ko 1970 ke italian grand pricks main unke ghaatak durghatana ke baavjood jirnoddhar ke daur se nahin guzarana padaa.
 maaikel shoomakar sarvaadhik saat drivers championships jeetne kaa record banaae hue hai. 
 F1 ke adhikansh driver cart racing pratiyogitaaon se shuruyaat karte hai or uske baad formula 3 ke liye formula ford or formula reynolt jaisi paaramparik europea ekal siton vaali shrinkhalaaon or ant main GP2 shrinkhalaaon ke madhyam se saamne aate hai. GP2 kii shuruyaat 2005 main formula 3000 kii jagah hui thi jisane khud hi F1 main antim pramukh "staping stone" ke rup main formula tu kii jagah lee thi. is str ke adhikansh champion F1 kii oopaadhi praapt karte hai lekin 2006 ke GP2 champion louis hamiltan 2008 main formula van driver kaa kitaab jeetne vaale pehle F2, F3000 yaa GP2 champion bane. formula van main pravesh karne se pehle driveron ko is str par pratiyogita karne kii jarurat nahin he. british F3 ne kai F1 driveron kii aapurti kii he or saath main aise champiyanon kii bhi aapurti kii hai, jinamein nigel mancel, yrton senna or mika hakkinen shaamil hai, jo us shrinkhalaa se sidhe formula van main ja chuke hai. shaayad hi kisi driver ko usase bhi neeche ke str se liya ja saktaa he jaisaa ki 2007 ke world champion kimi racconen ke maamle main hua tha jo formula reynolt se sidhe F1 main chale gaye the.
 american championships car racing ne mishrit parinaamon ke saath formula van grid main bhi yogadan diya he. CART champiens mario andreti or jeqas villenuv F1 world champiens bane. anya CART yaa champkaar champiyanon, jaise maaikel andreti or christiano da matta, ne F1 ke kisi bhi res main jeet hasil nahin kii. anya driveron ne F1 ke liye alag-alag raste apanaae hai; daiman hill ne motorbikon kii racing kii or maaikel shoomakar ne sports kaaron kii racing kii, haalanki unhone aisa junior single seater rankon ke madhyam se aage badhane ke baad kiya. haalanki racing ke liye driver ke paas ek FIA super license hona bahut jaruri he-jo yah sunishchit karata he ki driver ke paas apekshit kaushal hai or isliye vah dusron ke liye khatra nahin banegaa. kuch driveron ke paas yah license nahin hua karata jab ve pehali baar ek F1 team ke liye hastakshar karte; keval 23 car racing kaa shrey paane ke baavjood racconen ko license mil gaya tha.
 adhikansh F1 driver apane 30 ke madhya se ant tak ritair ho jate hai; haalanki, kai driver anushaasan ke tahat racing jari rakhte hai jisaki maang shaareerik drushti se bahut kam hoti he. DTM naamak german touring car championships ek lokapriy shrenee he jisamen purv driver, jaise - do baar F1 champion rah chuke mika hakkinen, ralf shoomakar or jin alesi, shaamil hote hai or kuch F1 driver america main racing karne chale gaye hai—nigel mancel or emmersan fittipaldy ne 1993 ke CART kitaab ke liye dwandv yuddh kiya, juaan pablo montoya, nelson picket junior or scot speed NASCAR ke liye nikal chuke hai. kuch driveron, jaise - vitantonio liuzi, narayan kartikeyan or jose varstapen, ne A1 grand pricks main racing karnaa jari rakhaa or kuch, jaise - gerhard burgers or alain prost, team ke maalikon ke rup main F1 main vaapasi kii. 2008 main apane udghaatan satr ke baad se superlig formula ne sebstian baurdes, antonio pizonia or jeeorgio pantano jaise purv-formula van driveron ko aakarshith kiya he. purv formula van driveron ke liye ek shrinkhalaa, jise grand pricks masters kehete hai, 2005 or 2006 main kuch samay ke liye chalaa. anya driver tivi kawarej ke pandit ban gaye hai, jaise - ITV ke liye martin brundal, globo ke liye luciano burty, italvi rashtriya network RAI ke liye jin alesi or BBC ke liye david coltahard. anya, jaise - daiman hill or jaiki stewart, apane-apane deshon main motorsport main sakriy bhoomikaayen nibhaate hai.
 ek satr main yojit hone vaale grands pricks kii sankhya main saal dar saal antar hota he. udghaatan 1950 world championships satr main keval saat res shaamil hote the; saal dar saal suchi kaa aakaar lagbhag teen guna ho gaya he. haalanki 1980 ke dashak se reson kii sankhya solah yaa satraa par rukii rahi, lekin yah sankhya 2005 main badhkar unniis ho gai.
 mul saat reson main se chah res europe main hote the; 1950 main world championships kii tarah ginati kii jane vaali ekmaatr gaiyr-europea res indianapolis 500 thi jise F1 kii tiimon kii bhagidaari ke abhaav or anya reson se alag vishistataaon vaale karon kii aavashyaktaa ke kaaran baad main united stats grand pricks dwaara pratisthapit kar diya gaya. F1 championships dhire-dhire anya gaiyr europea deshon main bhi fail gaya. argentina ne 1953 main pratham south american grand pricks kii mejabani kii or morocco ne 1958 main pratham african world championships res kii mejabani kii. uske baad asia or oshinia ne bhi mejabani kii. maujuuda unniis res europe, asia, oshinia, uttari america or dakshin america jaise mahadwipon main fail gaye hai.
 paaramparik rup se prayek raashtra ne ek-ek grand pricks kii mejabani kii he jiske saath us desh kaa naam sanlagn he. yadi ek desh ek varsh main ekaadhik grand pricks kii mejabani karata he to unhen alag-alag naam praapt hota he. udhaaharan ke liye, ek europea desh, jisane do grand pricks kii mejabani kii he, ke duusare grand pricks ko european grand pricks ke rup main janaa jaataa he, jabaki italy ke duusare grand pricks kaa namakaran sameepvarti san marinon ganatantr ke naam par kiya gaya. isi prakaar, jab 1994/1995 main japan main do reson ko soochibaddh kiya gaya, tab duusari pratiyogita ko pacific grand pricks ke naam se janaa gaya. 1982 main sanyukt rajya america ne teen grand pricks kii mejabani kii.
 grands pricks, jinamein se kuch kaa aayojan formula van world championships se pehle hua tha, har saal hameshaa ek hi circuit par yojit nahin hote hai. udhaaharan ke liye, british grand pricks, haalanki 1950 se har saal iskaa aayojan hota rahaa he, 1963 se 1986 tak brands hech or silverstone main baari-baari se yojit hua. prayek satr main shaamil kiya jane vaala ekmaatr anya res italian grand pricks he. world championships pratiyogita kaa aayojan vishesh rup se monza main hua he jiskaa keval ek apwaad he: 1980 main iskaa aayojan imola main hua tha jo 2006 tak san marino grand pricks kaa mejabaan tha.
 grand pricks kii suchi main nae reson main se ek, jo bahrain main yojit hua tha, ucch takanic ke uddeshy se banaae gaye registani trek ke saath madhya purv main formula van ke pratham prayaas kaa pratinidhitiv karata he. bahrain grand pricks or china evam turki main yojit hone vaale anya nae res, formula van grand pricks frenchise kii vruddhi or vikaas ke nae avasar pradaan karte hai jabaki nae-nae centre bhi puri duniya main anya formula van racing sthanon kii seemaa main vruddhi karte hai. nae reson kii suchi ke liye jagah banane ke uddeshy se europe or americas main puraane yaa kam safal pratiyogitaaon, jaise - argentina, austria, mexico, france, san marino or sanyukt rajya america kii pratiyogitaayen, ko suchi se baahar kar diya gaya he.
 suchi main bilkul haal hi main shaamil kii gai pratiyogitaaon main vailenshia street circuit kaa bhi naam he jo 2008 main european grand pricks kaa mejabaan bana or jisane spain ko do grand pricks die. sitambar 2008 main, singapore grand pricks, jisane formula van main yojit hone vaale ab tak ke sabase pehle raatri res kii mejabani kii, ek baar main hi yojit hone ke uddeshy se khel ke mukhya centre europea darshakon ke liye bahut anukool tha. suchi main sabase haal main shaamil abu dhabi grand pricks he, jisane 2009 ke satr ke antim res kii mejabani kii, jisane din se lekar raat tak hone vaale pehle res kaa gaurav praapt kiya. aane vaale samay main suchi main shaamil hone vaale soochibaddh nae circuiton main korean grand pricks, jo pehali baar oktober 2010 main yojit hoga or indian grand pricks, jo 2011 main bharat ke dilli main yojit hoga, kaa naam shaamil he.
 ek vishisht circuit main aam taur par sidhe sadak kaa ek failaav shaamil hota he jis par armbh grid sthit hota he. pit len, jahaan driver res ke dauraan eendhan or tiron ke liye rukte hai or jahaan team res se pehle kaaron par kaam karte hai, aam taur par armbh grid se aage sthit hota he. circuit ke shesh hisse kaa leout vyapak rup se bhinn hota he, haalanki zyaadaatar maamalon main circuit ek ghadi kii disha main chalata he. ghadi kii vipareet disha main jane vaali kuch circuiton kii vajah se driver ko gardan kii samasya kaa saamana karnaa pad saktaa he kyonki F1 kaaron dwaara bahut zyada parshv bal utpann hota he jo driver ke siron ko saamaanya sthiti se vipareet disha main khinchne lagate hai.
 aajkal istemaal ho rahi circuiton main se adhikansh circuiton ko vishesh rup se pratiyogita ke liye banaaya gaya he. maujuuda sadak circuit monaco, melborn, vailenshia or singapore hai, haalanki anya shahari sthanon par racing hoti rahati hai or aise reson ke prastaavon par aksar vichaar-vimarsh kiya jaataa he–abhi haal hi main landan or paris main aisa hua tha. kai anya circuiton ko bhi puri tarah se yaa aanshik rup se saarvjaanik raaston par lagaaya jaataa he, jaise - spa-francorchamps. monaco res kaa glamer or itihaas circuiton ke abhi bhi istemaal hote rahane ke praathmik kaaran hai kyonki aisa mana jaataa he ki anya trekon par lagaae gaye sakht suraksha jaruraton ko puraa nahin kiya gaya he. teen baar world champion rah chuke nelson picket ne monaco main racing kii vyakhya bade shandaar dhang se nimna rup main kii "yah ek tarah se apane living rup main chaaron taraf cycle kii sawari karne jaisaa tha".
 driveron kii suraksha ko dhyaan main rakhte hue banaae jane vaale circuit dizin ab din par din parishkrut hote ja rahe hai jiskaa ek jeeta jaagta udhaaharan naveen bahrain international circuit he jise 2004 main shaamil kiya gaya or jise harman tilk ne dizin kiya tha jo ek tarah se F1 ke adhikansh nae circuiton kii tarah hi tha. F1 ke nae circuiton main se kai, khaas taur par tilk dwaara dizin kii gaye circuit, kii aalochana main kaha gaya he ki isamen spa-francorchamps or imola jaisi utkrushtataa ke "pravaah" kaa abhaav he. germany main hokenheim circuit ke liye, udhaaharan ke liye, grandestands ke liye adhik kshamta pradaan karte hue or bahut zyada lambi or khatarnaak sidhe raste ko khatm karte hue, unhone phir se jo dizin taiyaar kiya tha us par kai logon ne naaraazgi jataai he, ve tark dete hai ki hokenheim circuit kii sthiti kaa bhaag lambaa tha jo sidhe kaale junglee bhaagon main chalaa gaya tha. ye navnirmit circuit, haalanki, aam taur par aadhunik formula van ke suraksha maanakon ko puraane circuiton kii apeksha adhik kushaltapurvak puraa karne main sakasham hai.
 F1 kii suchi main abhi haal hi main vailenshia, singapore or abu dhabi ko shaamil kiya gaya he. 2011 main pehali baar bharat main ek formula 1 grand pricks kaa aayojan kiya jayega.
 keval ek res ke liye kam se kam 5000 aagantukon ke thaharne ke liye hotel ke kamaron kii jarurat padati he. 
 formula van ke aadhunik kaaron ke ingine bich main hote hai, cockpit khule hote hai, khulaa pahiyaa hota he or baithne ke liye isamen keval ek hi seat hota he. iske chasis zyaadaatar carbon fibre mishran se bane hote hai jo ise halkaa lekin bahut kathor or majboot banaata he. ingine, taral padaarth or driver sahit puure car kaa vajan keval 620 kilo hota he—yah viniyamon dwaara nirdhaarit kam se kam vajan he. kaaron kii banaavat aam taur par kam se kam kii tulane main bhi halaki hoti he or isliye ve kam se kam vajan kaa boj uthaane main sakasham hote hai. res team is boj ko chasis ke ekadam neeche rakhkar iskaa laabh uthaate hai jisase sanchaalan or vajan hastantaran main sudhaar karne ke liye jitna sambhav ho utana neeche gurutvkarshan kaa centre sthit ho jaataa he.
 formula van ke kaaron kii modane kii gati ko zyaadaatar vayugatikeey nimnabal dwaara nirdhaarit kiya jaataa he jise ve utpann karte hai jo car ko trek par dhakelta he. ise vaahan ke aage or peeche kii taraf sthit "dainon" dwaara or car ke sapaat tal ke tahat nimna daab dwaara nirmit bhuprabhav dwaara pradaan kiya jaataa he. kaaron kii vayugatikeey dizin car ke upar, neeche or uske aasapaas hawa ke pravaah ko bahut kareeb se niyantrit karne ke liye dizin kii gai asankhya chote-chote dainon, "barge board" or terning vence vaale sport kaaron ke pradarshan or maujuuda peedhee ko simit karne ke liye bahut zyada baadhya hote hai.
 kaaron ko modane kii gati ko niyantrit karne vaala anya pramukh kaarak tiron kaa dizin he. adhikansh anya circuit racing shrinkhalaaon kii tarah 1998 se 2008 tak formula van ke tair "chikne" nahin hote the. iske bajaay, prayek tair ke sataha par chaar bade-bade paridhiy khaanche hote the jinhen kaaron ko modane kii gati ko simit karne ke liye dizin kiya gaya tha. 2009 ke satr main formula van main chikne tiron kii vaapasi hui. nilamban chaaron taraf se dabl vishabon yaa multilink hota he jiske saath chasis par pusharod dwaara chaalit spring or damper hote hai. iskaa ekmaatr apwaad 2009 ke vinirdesan red bul racing car main dekhne ko miltaa he jo peeche kii taraf pulrod kaa istemaal karata he, jo 20 se bhi adhik varshon main aisa karne vaala pehla car he.
 kam vajan or adhik gharshan pradarshan ke liye carbon-carbon discs brake kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he. ye bahut ucch stareey braking pradarshan pradaan karte hai or ye aam taur par aise tatv hai jo formula ke nae driveron se bahut zyada pratikriya utpann karte hai.
 injanon ko aakankshit V8 ke liye aam taur par 2.4 litre hona chahie or saath main unke dizin or samagriyon par kai anya avrodh bhi hone chahie jinhen istemaal kiya ja saktaa he. ingine saarvajanik rup se uplabdh petrol kii tarah bahut baariki se dikhne vaale bina sisa vaale eendhan par chalata he. tel, jo ingine ko chikna rakhte hai or use jarurat se zyada garm hone se bachaate hai, bahut kuch jal ke gadhepan kii tarah hi hota he. 2006 kii peedi kaa ingine 20,000 RPM tak chalaa or 780 brake ashwashakti tak utpaadan kiya. 2007 ke liye injanon ko 19,000 RPM ke liye pratibandhit kiya gaya or saath main ise kuch simit vikaas kshetron ke liye anumati di gai or uske baad 2006 ke ant se ingine ke vinirdesan kaa istemaal band ho gaya. 2009 ke formula van ke satr ke liye injanon ko ab phir se 18,000 RPM ke liye pratibandhit kiya gaya he.
 maujuuda viniyamon ke tahat vibhinn prakaar kii kai praudyogikiyon par pratibandh laga diya gaya he jisamen sakriy nilamban, bhuprabhav or terbocharger shaamil hai. iske baavjood aaj kii peedi kii kaaren kuch circuiton par 350 kimi/ghanta kii gati tak pahunch sakati hai. 2006 main mozev registan main ek ranve par kam se kam nimnabal ke saath daudti hui ek honda formula van car ne 415 kimi/ghanta kii ek sheersh gati hasil kii. honda ke anusaar, car ne FIA formula van viniyamon kaa puri tarah se paalan kiya. vayugatiki par simaaon ke saath bhi, 160 kimi/ghanta par vayugatikeey rup se pravaahit nimnabal car ke vajan ke baraabar hota he or baar-baar doharaaya jane vaale daawey ki formula van kaaren "oonchai par chalaane" par kaaphi nimnabal utpann karti hai, jabaki siddhant main sambhav he, ko kabhi bhi pareekshan main nahin daalaa gaya he. car ke vajan se 2.5 guna nimnabal ko punav gati par praapt kiya ja saktaa he. nimnabal kaa matlab he ki kaaren mode ke samay gurutvkarshan bal se 3.5 guna pariman vaala ek parshv bal praapt kar sakati hai. natijatan, mode ke samay 20 kilon ke vajan ke baraabar ke ek bal ke saath driver kaa sir agal-bagal khinchaa jaataa he. itnaa adhik parshv bal shwans lene main kathinaayee paidaa karne ke liye kaaphi he or islliye res ko puraa karne ke liye lagane vaale ek se do ghante tak apane dhyaan ko banaae rakhane ke liye driver ko sarvocch ekaagratha or fitness kii jarurat he. pheraari anzo kii tarah kaa ucch-pradarshan dene vaala road car keval 1g hi praapt karata he.
 2010 ke anusaar prayek team ke paas kisi bhi samay istemaal karne ke liye do se adhik car uplabdh nahin ho saktaa he. prayek driver ek satr ke dauraan aath se adhik ingine kaa istemaal nahin kar saktaa he; yadi aath se adhik ingine kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he, to vah pratiyogita ke armbh sthal ke das sthanon ko chhod detaa he jahaan ek atirikt ingine kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he. prayek driver chaar lagaataar pratiyogitaaon ke liye ek se adhik gearbox kaa istemaal nahin kar saktaa he; prayek anirdhaarit gearbox parivartan ke liye driver ko grid par paanch sthaan ko chhod dena padataa he jab tak vah team ke niyantran se baahar ke kaarnon kii vajah se pichley res ko puraa karne main vifal nahin ho jaataa.
 formula 1 isamen shaamil bahut se pakshon ke liye phydemand he—tivi channel reson kaa prasaaran karke laabh kamaate hai or tiimon ko prasaaran ke adhikaron kii bikri or apane kaaron par praayojakon ke logo se milane vaale dhan kaa ek hissaa mil jaataa he.
 ek brands nayaa sthaayee circuit, jaisaa ki china ke shanghai main he, kaa nirmaan karne kaa kharch lakhon-karodon dollar tak ja saktaa he, jabaki ek saarvajanik sadak, jaise - albert park, ko ek asthaayee circuit main badalane kaa kharch bahut kam hota he. haalanki sthaayee circuit nijee reson or anya reson, jaise - motogypi, ke liye trek ko patte par dekar saal bhar rajasav utpann kar saktaa he. shanghai circuit kaa nirmaan karne main $300 million se adhik dhan kharch hua tha. iske maalik 2014 tak isse hanirahit vyapaar karne kii ummid kar rahe hai. istambul park circuit ko banane main $150 million kharch hua.
 sabhi circuit laabh hasil nahin karte hai—udhaaharan ke liye, albert park ko 2007 main $32 million kaa nuksaan uthaanaa padaa.
 march 2007 main F1 racing ne formula van tiimon dwaara kiye gaye kharch ke apane vaarshik anumaan ko prakashit kiya. 2006 main sabhi gyaarah tiimon ke kul kharch ke 2.9 billian US dollar hone kaa anumaan tha. yah anumaan nimna prakaar se tut gaya; toyota $418.5 million, pheraari $406.5 million, maclaren $402 million, honda $380.5 million, BMW sobar $355 million, reynolt $324 million, red bul $252 million, williams $195.5 million, midland F1/spiker-MF1 $120 million, toro roso $75 million or super aguri $57 million.
 team dar team kharch main kaaphi antar hota he. anumaan he ki honda, toyota, maclaren-marcedes or pheraari ne 2006 main injanon par lagbhag $200 million kharch kiya, reynolt ne lagbhag $125 million kharch kiya or cosworth ke 2006 ke V8 ke vikaas main $15 million lag gaya. 2006 ke satr, jis par ye aankade aadhaarit hai, ke vipareet 2007 ke khel viniyamon ne ingine vikaas se sambandhit sabhi pradarshan par pratibandh laga diya.
 formula van ko 2000 ke dashak ke shuru main kathin paristhiti se guzarana padaa. maaikel shoomakar or scudyria pheraari ke prabhutv ke kaaran dekhne vaale logon kii sankhya main kami ai or prashansakon ne apni anichhaa jaahir kii. 2005 ke baad se vibhinn satron ke kaaran dekhne vaale logon ke aankade kii haalat main sudhaar ke kuch sanket mil rahe hai. pheraari or shoomakar ke panchavarshiya prabhutv kaa ant 2005 main hua jab reynolt formula van kaa sheersh team ban gaya or saath main fernando alonso nae world champion bane. uske baad se khel kii ruchi kaa ek punarutthan hua he, khaas taur par alonso kii mathrubhumi spain or louis hamiltan evam jension batan kii mathrubhumi united kingdom main. 2006 main, baees tiimon ne 2008 ke satr ke liye uplabdh antim barahawein team sthal ke liye aavedan kiya. ant main is sthal ko purv B.A.R. or beneton team principal david richards ke prodrive sanghathan ko pradaan kiya gaya, lekin yah team navambar 2007 main 2008 ke satr se baahar nikal gaya.
 formula van racing ke uttarottar badhate kharch se nipatane ke liye niyam banane or khaas taur par 1994 rolland retznburger or yrton senna kii maut ke pariprekshya main khel ko yathasambhav surakshit rakhane kii sthiti ko sunishchit karne kaa daayitv FIA par he. is uddeshy se FIA ne niyamon main kai parivartan kiye hai, jisamen nae tair pratibandh, multi-res ingine or nimnbal main katautee shaamil he. paaramparik rup se niyam-parivartan ko lekar hone vaale sabhi vichaar-vimarshon main suraksha or laagat sarvopari rahaa he. abhi haal hi main FIA ne apni praathamiktaaon main dakshata ko shaamil kiya he. aajkal FIA or nirmaataa 2011 ke satr ke liye jaev-eendhan injanon or punaryoji braking ko shaamil karne par vichaar-vimarsh kar rahe hai. purv FIA adhyaksh maiks mosle kaa maananaa he ki F1 ko motor vaahan udyog main takneekee drushti se praasangik bane rahane ke saath janata ko F1 praudyogiki ke prati utsahit banaae rakhane ke liye dakshata par avashya dhyaan dena chahie.
 ek world championships ke rup main khel kii bhoomika ko sahi arth pradaan karne kii ichha se FOM adhyaksh barney iklestone ne nae deshon main kai grand pricks kaa armbh or aayojan kiya he or nae bhavi reson par vichaar-vimarsh karnaa chaalu rakhaa he. prithvi ke globe ke nae kshetron main khel kaa teevr vistaar bhi kuch sawaal chhod jaataa he ki koun-koun se reson main katautee hogi.
 formula van ko duniya bhar ke lagbhag prayek desh or kshetra main live yaa der se tepe par dekha ja saktaa he or yah duniya ke tivi darshakon ke ek bahut bade hisse ko aakarshith karata he. 2008 ke satr ne prayek res ke liye duniya bhar se 600 million darshakon ko aakarshith kiya. yah ek vishaal tivi kaarykram he; 2001 ke satr ke liye sanchayi tivi darshakon kii sankhya 54 billian tha jiskaa prasaaran do sau deshon main kiya gaya tha.
 2000 ke dashak ke armbh ke dauraan, formula van group ne ise ek korporate pahchaan pradaan karne ke prayaas main iske liye asankhya trademark, ek aadhikarik logo or ek aadhikarik website kaa nirmaan kiya. iklestone ne ek digital television package ke saath prayog kiya jise 1967 ke german grand pricks main pehle GP rangeen TV prasaaran ke tees saal baad german digital television seva "DF1" ke sahyog se 1996 ke german grand pricks main shuru kiya gaya. is seva ne darshak ko kai samakaleen sambharan pradaan kiya jise paaramparik kawarej ke liye istemaal kiye jane vaale upkarnon se alag camaron, takneekee upkaran or karmchaariyon kii sahaayta se nirmit kiya gaya. ise saal dar saal kai deshon main shuru kiya gaya lekin vittiya kaarnon kii vajah se 2002 ke satr ke baad band kar diya gaya.
 tivi stesan vahi sab grahan karte hai jise "world feed" ke naam se janaa jaataa he, chaahe vah FOM dwaara nirmit ho yaa kabhi-kabhi "mejabaan prasarak" dwaara. "premier" hi ekmaatr aisa stesan tha jo vastav main isse alag tha—yah ek german channel he jo sabhi satron ko live or sanvadatmak rup main pesh karata he or saath main jisamen onboard channel kii suvidha hoti he. yah seva 2002 ke ant tak adhik vyapak rup se europe bhar main uplabdh tha, jab digital sanvadatmak sevaaon ke ek sampoorn alag sambharan kii laagat haalanki bahut adhik thi. united kingdom main ski digital ke madhyam se shuru kiye gaye "F1 digital +" channel ke vifal hone kii vajah se yah bade bhaag main tha. darshakon ke liye keemten itani zyada thi ki ve qualifing or res donon ko khud ITV par muft dekhne ke bare main soch-vichaar karne lage.

BBC, digital sthaliy platformon ke "red batan" sanvadatmak sevaaon par ek post-res program kaa bhi prasaaran karata he jise "F1 forum" kehete hai.
 barney iklestone ne ghoshnaa kii thi ki F1, 2007 ke satr ke ant ke nikat HD formet apana legaa. uske baad 2008 ke armbh main kii gai ek ghoshnaa main daawa kiya gaya tha ki BBC, ITV se adhikaar grahan kar 2009 se paanch varshon tak F1 kaa prasaaran karegaa jo 1997 se iskaa prasaaran kar rahaa tha. haalanki, 31 disambar 2008 ko BBC sport ke diarector roger mose ne ghoshnaa kii ki FA kaa prasaaran BBC HD par nahin kiya jayega kyonki "koi bhi HD world feed uplabdh nahin he".

 aajkal "formula van res" or "world championships res" shabd kaaphi prabhavi rup se ek samaan arth pradaan karte hai; 1984 ke baad se prayek formula van res kii ginati world championships ke rup main kii gai he or prayek world championships res kaa aayojan formula van ke viniyamon ke anusaar kiya gaya he. lekin donon shabd antauparivartaneey nahin he. gaur karen ki:
 yah antar us samay sabase adhik praasangik hota he jab kariyar saransh or "abhi tak kii soochiyon" par vichaar kiya jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye, formula van driveron kii suchi main clement biyondatty ke naam ke aage 1 res dikhaaya gaya he. biyondatty ne vastav main 1950 main chaar formula van reson main pratiyogita kii, lekin inamen se keval ek kii ginati world championships ke liye kii gai. isi tarah, kai indi 500 vijetaon ne takneekee rup se apana pehla world championships res jeeta, haalanki adhikansh darj pustikaon main ise najrandaz kiya gaya or iske bajaay keval niyamit pratibhaagiyon ko hi darj kiya gaya.
