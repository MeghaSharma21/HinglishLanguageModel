 gimmigela chuli yaa judawe himalay kaa ek parvat he jo kanchanajangha parvat se lagbhag 4.2 uttar-uttarpurv kii duuri par nepal kii bharat ke sikkim rajya kii seemaa par sthit he. gimmigela ke do shikhar hai - mukhya 7,350 meter unchaa gimmigela chuli 1 kahalaane vaala shikhar bharat-nepal seemaa par he or chota 7,005 meter unchaa gimmigela chuli 2 kahalaane vaala shikhar puri tarah bharat main he. yah donon shikhar ek saath dikhne se hi is parvat kaa naam "judawe" bhi pad gaya he.
 parvatiy soochiyon main 500 meter se kam kii sthalakritik udagrata rakhane vaale shikhar swatantra parvat nahin maane jate balki aasapaas ke anya parvaton ke hi shikhar maane jate hai . 7,350 meter unche gimmigela kaa parvatiy shikhar yadi akelaa kahin khadaa hota to vah vishwa kaa 78vaan sarvocch parvat hota). lekin kyonki gimmigela kii swatantra sthalakritik udagrata keval 432 meter he, jo ki 500 meter se kam he, isliye ise kanchanajangha kii hi ek sahaayak chotii mana jaataa he.
