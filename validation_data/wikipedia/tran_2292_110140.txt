 sthai khaataa sankhya yaa pan puure bharat main das ankiy varnatmak sankhya he jo iskar vibhaag dwaara leminated card ke rup main jari kiya jaataa he. yah pataa yaa sthaan parivartan ke saath nahin badalata he jahaan aap kaa nirdhaaran kiya ja rahaa he. pan sambandhi soochna praapt karne ke liye iskar vibhaag ne nimnlikhit ko praadhikrut kiya he :- 
 iskar adhinium main yah vyavastha kii gai he ki prayek vyakti jisaki kul aay us adhiktam raashi se adhik he, jis par kar nahin lagata yaa prayek vyakti jo kisi vyapaar karata yaa vyavsaay main laga he, jisaki kul aay, laabh yaa sakal prapti pehle kisi bhi vigat varsh main 5 lakh rupye se adhik hui he yaa koi vyakti jise aay vivarani file karne kii aavashyaktaa he, vah pan ke liye aavedan karegaa. iske atirikt koi vyakti jo uparyukt sharten puri nahin karata, vah bhi pan aabantan ke liye aavedan kar saktaa he. dinaank 1.6.2000 se kendriya sarkaar adhisuchana dwaara kisi varg yaa varg ke vyaktiyon ko vinirdisht karegi ayatak or niryaatak sahit chaahe unke dwaara koi kar kaa bhugtan kiya jaataa he yaa anyathaa, vah vyakti bhi nirdhaaran adhikari ke paas pan aabantan ke liye aavedan kar saktaa he. dinaank 1.4.2006 se vyakti jo laabhon ke liye vivarani file karne kaa uttaradaayi he uske liye bhi pan abantit kiya gaya he. use doosraa pan praapt karne kii aavashyaktaa nahin he.
 chunki kisi vittiya varsh kii aay par baad ke varsh main kar lagaaya jaataa he jo nirdhaaran varsh kaha jaataa he isliye sambandhit nirdhaaran varsh ke 30 june ke pehle pan ke liye aavedan kiya jaae.
 pan ke aabantan ke liye aavedan prapatr 49 k main karnaa he. is prapatr ko bharte samay nimnlikhit baton par dhyaan diya janaa he:-
 nimnlikhit parivartanon kii soochna nirdhaaran adhikari ko di jaae
 nai shrunkhlaa ke tahat nae pan ke liye aavedan nimnlikhit paristhitiyon main kiya ja saktaa he
