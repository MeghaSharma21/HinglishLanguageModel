 awaji dweep, seto antardeshiy samudr ke purvi bhaag main honshu or shikoku ke dwipon ke bich, japan ke higo praant kaa ek dweep hai. dweep kaa kshetrafal 592.17 varg kilometre hai, or antardeshiy samudr ke dwipon main se sabase bada hai.
 un donon dwipon ke bich ek avaagaman ke rup main, awaji kaa mul arth "awa kii sadak" he, narutho jalasanyogi main sthit yah dweep pehle shikoku praant main tha, jo ab tokushima praant kaa hissaa hai.
 shinto main srijan ke myth ke anusaar, kaami isaangi or izanaimi se se paidaa hui joyshima dwipon main awaji sabase pehla tha. awaji main 7veen or 19veen shatabdi ke bich ek praant kaa gathan hua, awaji praant, jo naankido kaa ek hissaa tha. aaj dweep main teen nagarpalikaayen hai: awaji, sumoto or minamiavaji
 500 varsh purana ek paaramparik-kathputli nrity, awaji ningo-joruri yaa ningo-joruri, kaa dweep ke dakshini hissen minamiyavaji ke awaji ningo-joruri haul main rojaanaa kai manchan prastut hota hai, aaj yah dweep kaa hissaa hai, or japan kaa ek amoort saanskrithik viraasat hai. awaji kii kathputliyaan kai lokapriy paramparaagat naatakon kaa pradarshan karti hai lekin unamem dharmik anushthanon ki chhavi zarur dikhaai detii hai.
 1830 ke dashak main, sthaneey kumhaaron ne minpei kaa utpaadan shuru kiya, jo ab awaji ware ke naam se janaa jaataa he, or jise minpei ware bhi kaha jaataa hai.
 tadaao ando ne dweep par kai nirmaan kaary karawaaye, unamem se hopuku-ji jalmandir or awaji yyumbutai, hai, donon hi hyogo main sthit hai. 1995 main, yah dweep, cobe main aae bukamp kaa centre tha, jisase 5,502 logon maut ho gai thi.
 yah dweep, akashi jalasanyogi dwaara honshu or narutho jalasanyogi dwaara shikoku ko alag karata hai. 5 april, 1998 se, yah vishwa ke sabase lambey nilamban pul akashi kico brij dwaara honshu ke cobe shahar se judaa hua he. cobe awaji narutho drutamarg, banane ke baad se yah dweep honshu or shikoku ke bich mukhya purvi bhoomi link rahaa hai.
