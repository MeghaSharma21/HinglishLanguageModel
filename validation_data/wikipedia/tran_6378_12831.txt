 
 Self Portrait, 1985
 anil karanjay ek purna bhartiya kalakaar the. purv bengal main janm lenevaale karanjay ne banaras main shiksha praapt kii, jahaan unka parivaar 1947 main bhartiya upmahadweep ke bantwaare ke baad bas gaya. ek chote bachche ke rup main ve mitti ke saath khilne, khilone or tir banane main kaaphi samay bitaate the. unhone bahut jaldi hi jaanwaron or paudhon kaa chitra bananaa yaa jis bhi vastu ne unhen prerit kiya uskaa chitra bananaa bhi shuru kar diya. 1956 main unhone bengal skool ke ek guru or nepali mul ke karanman singh kii adhyakshata vaale bhartiya kala kendra kaa purnakalik chaatr banane ke liye skool chhod diya. is shikshak ne anil ko vyapak rup se prayog karne or har samskruti kii kala kaa adhyayanke liye protsaahit kiya. anil 1960 tak yahaan rahe, is dauraan ve niyamit rup se pradarshan or anya chaatro kaa adhyaapan karte rahe. isi avadhi ke dauraan unhone bharat kala bhawan main banaras ke maharaj ke antim darbari chitrakaar kii dekharekh main laghu chitrakala kaa abhyaas kiya. unhone clay modelling or dhaatu kii dhalaai seekhney ke liye banaras politechnic main bhi daakhila liya.
 1960 ke krantikari ke dauraan, anil bhartiya or antarrashtriya raajnneetik-saanskrithik andolan main sabase aage the. 1962 main, karunanidhan mukhopadhyaay ke saath unhone sanyukt kalakaaron kii sthapana kii. unke studio ke naam 'shaitan kii kaaryshaala' ne puure bharat or videshon ke kalakaaron, lekhakon, kaviyon or sangeetkaaron ko aakarshith kiya. samooh ne ek na chalane vaali chaay kii dukaan, pardise cafe main, banaras kii pehali art gailaree kii sthapana kii, jahaan is jeevant shahar ke sabase rangeen vyaktitv ke kuch logon kaa aksar aana-janaa hota tha. anil or samooh ke anya log is dauraan ek samudaay main rahete the or anek deshon ke 'anveshakon' se vichaar-vimarsh or anubhav kaa vinimay kiya.
 anil karanjay kaviyon ke ek prasiddh kattarpanthi bengali samooh bhukhi peedi ke ek bahut sakriy sadsy rahe, jise bhukhwaadi হাংরি আন্দোলন andolan ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he. jab alan ginsburg or peter orlevoski ne apane bharat pravaas ke dauraan bhukhwaadiyon se baatcheet kii us samay anil beet janareshan se jude the. bhukhwaadi patna, kolkata or banaras main aadhaarit the or unhone nepal main avant garde ke mahatvapurn samparkon ke saath bhi jaalsaji kii. anil ne bhukhwaadi prakashnon ke liye anek chitra banaae. unhone poster or kavitaaon kaa bhi yogadan diya. or ve bharat main laghu patrika andolan ke ek sansthaapak the. 1969 main, ve nai dilli chale gaye jahaan unhone dilli shilpi chakra main ek 'laghu patrika pradarshnee' yojit kii or usamen bhaag liya.
 70 ke dashak ke purvarddh main anil karanjay ne apni takneekee paripakvata or apane swapn sareekhe, aksar bhayanak, gusse bhari kalpana ke saath bhartiya kala halkon main ek baari prabhaav choda. apane adhikansh praarambhik chitron main, unhone ajeeb paridrishyon -dhamkee or aarope; prakritik roopon - chattaanon, badal, jaanwaron or ped se ubharne vaali vikrut maanav roopon kaa srijan kiya - jo jan chetana ke liye ek vaahan ban gaye, jo sadiyon ke daman ke khilaaf apni urja ko mukt kar rahi he. apane dilli jane ke baad, itihaas ke atyaachaar ke liye ek roopak kii tarah, unke vikrut maanav roopon ko aksar khandhar ke saath ekikrut kiya gaya. lekin vyangyatmak hasya kaa ek tatv, inamen se adhikansh canvaason main anupasthit nahin he. iske alaava, anil kii kalpana kabhi-kabhi ek kaavyatmak romansawad, lagbhag komal abhivyakti se sampark karti he. yah unke baad ke adhikansh kaamon main or adhik spasht hua jinamein paridrishy pramukh mul bhav kaa kaam karata he or maanav upasthiti kaa keval sanket hota he, aksar ek rahasyamay gantavy ke liye jate raste yaa sidhiyon dwaara. unke paridrishy main puraani deevaaron, dwaar yaa murtikla yukt khambhon ke madhyam se bhutiya fusfusaahat bhi goonjati he.
 1972 main unhone ek rashtriya puraskaar jeeta, lekin iskaa unke jivan par bahut maamuli prabhaav padaa. ve vishisht rup se sthapana-virdohi kalakaar the or vah apane baaki jivan main bhi aise hi rahenge. teevr samuhik bhaavana or 1960 ke dashak kii uplabdhiyon ne anil par gaharaa prabhaav daalaa tha. uske baad se ve aksar rachanaatmak rup se alag mahasoos rahane lage. ek raajnneetik saanskrithik kaaryakarta ke rup main ve hameshaa patibaddh rahenge or vah bhukhi peedi ke malay raay choudhary, subimal basaa, samir roychoudhary, tridiv mitra or us yug ke anya duusare lekhakon se rishta banaae rakhenge. lekin ek kalakaar ke rup main ve khud ko apane samakalinon se ek antar par pate rahe. samay ke saath, unka vichaar abhivyakti ke us tareeke ke prati adhik jhukaav rahaa jise kala pratishthanon or bharat ke ek vaishvik arthvyavastha ban jane se upje nae samrudh kala sangraahakon dwaara fishan ke baahar mana jaataa he. yah vishesh rup se unke antim dashak kaa sach bana jab shuddh paridrishy unke sanchaar ke siddhant kaa vaahan ban gaya. haalanki abhi tak unke akele paridrishyon se log kabhi bhi bahut dur nahin rahe hai. vastav main, unke ped or anya prakritik tatv ajeeb maanav ishaaron dwaara alankrita hai. kabhi-kabhi puri prakruti ek chhipe dushman ke khilaaf shadyantr karti prathit hoti he, jo ek paryaavaran kaaryakarta ke rup main anil kii gahari chintaa ko darshaati he.
 lekin yah keval unke vishay nahin he jinhone unke liye kalakaaron kii sansaarik safalataa yaa vifaltaa kaa faisla karanevaale shaktishaalee samoohon main upeksha or apmaan arjeet kiya. yatharthwaad ne bhi isamen apni bhoomika nibhaai jo unke baad ke kaaryon kii visheshta thi. sataha par ye lagbhag shastreey dikhaai dete hai, phir bhi unki saraahna karanevaalon ke liye abhi tak, ve ek ucch yatharthwaad kii abhivyakti kar rahe hai jo hamaare puure samakaleen kaal main pratidhwanit he. khud anil ne apane kaam kii tulana ek 'jaadui yatharthwaad' ke saath kii he. jaisaa ki unhone apane par bani ek film main, da nechar of art, main kaha he: "mere chitra ek sapana hai, prakruti kaa ek sapana." chitron kii bhaavnaatmak saamagree sarvocch mahatv kii thi. isamen anil bhartiya shastreey sangeet, vishesh rup se raag ke bare main, ke apane vishaal gyaan se chitra banaate hai jisamen ek rachana ek samay yaa ek mausam kii manodasha yaa bhaavana ko vyakt karti he. kala main, iske samatuly ko ras kaha jaataa he, vastutah ras yaa saar, ek saundarya drushtikon he jise anil ne kalathit or saarvbhaumik samajha or apane chitron main uski vyakhya kii maang kii.
 apane paripakv charan main, kala ke bare main anil ke darshan main ek pramukh parivartan aayaa. jahaan uske aarambhik kaaryon ko vaicharik rup main takaraav ke taur par varnit kiya ja saktaa he, unke baad ke kaam kii kalpana or nishpaadan darshak ko raahat dene ke liye kii gayi. ve khud ko, ek doctor ke sadrush, ek kushal peshewar ke rup main dekhte the. jaisaa ki unhone kai avasaron par kaha jisamen vah film bhi shaamil he: "aaj ke kalakaar kii bhoomika hamaare samaaj dwaara die gaye ghaavon ko bharne kii he."
 apane puure karier ke dauraan, anil ne vibhinn madhyamon, vishesh rup se taileey rangon se kaam kiya tha jise vah atyadhik utkrisht maanate the. lekin antim varshon main unhone apane anek behtareen chitron ko sabhi sookhe pastalon ke upar pastel creyon se puraa kiya. yah ek aisa madhyam he jisamen unhone shaayad apane samay ke kisi bhi anya bhartiya kalakaar se adhik maharath or anusandhanatmakataa dikhaai he. apane antim dashakon main, anil bhi ek safal chitrakaar ban gaye. unhone astitv ke ek saadhan ke rup main anek chitra banaae, lekin is style main unka sabase acha kaam zyaadaatar un logon par he jo unke kareeb hai. ateet ke ustaadon kii parampara kaa anusaran karte hue, unhone anek aatm-chitra banaae. inamen se kuch unke sabase gahan abhivyakti vaale kaaryon main shaamil hai.
