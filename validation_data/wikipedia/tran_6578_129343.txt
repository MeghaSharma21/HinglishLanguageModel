 vimiya vishleshan ek sankaalpanik aujaar he jo bhautikee, rasayan, praudyogiki, ganit evam sankhyikee main prayukt hota he. yah vahaan upyogi hota he jahaan kai tarah kii bhautik rashiyaan kisi ghatana ke parinaam ke liye jimmedaar hon. bhautikavid aksar iskaa upyog kisi sameekaran aadi ki vaidhata kii jaanch ke liye karte rahete hai. duusari taraf iskaa upyog jatil bhautik sthitiyon se sambandhit charon ko aapas main sameekaran dwaara jodne ke liye kiya jaataa he. vimiya vishleshan kii vidhi se praapt in sambhavit samikarnon ko prayog dwaara jaanchaa jaataa he, yaa anya siddhanton ke prakaash main dekha jaataa he. buckingham kaa pai prameya, vimiya vishleshan kaa aadhaar he.
 newton dwaara likhit pustak 'princepia' main vimayein tathaa vimiya vishleshan 'saadrishy kaa siddhant' naam se varnit hai. is vishay ko badhaane main jin logon ne yogadan diya he, ve hai : i. buckingham, lard rali or pi. dablyoo. brijman . prarambh main vimiya vishleshan yantriki kii samasyaon main prayukt kiya gaya, kintu aajkal yah sabhi prakaar kii bhautikee evam ejiniyari kii samasyaon main prayukt hone laga he. vimiya vishleshan kaa maan uski is kshamta main he ki bhautikavigyaani or ejeeniyar ke pratidin kii saidhantik evam praayogic samasyaon ke samaadhaan main yah sahaayak hota he.
