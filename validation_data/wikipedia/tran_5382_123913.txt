 prahlaad ramsharan morishas ke hindi sahityakar-itihaaskaar hai. ve tribhaashi patrika 'indradhanush’ ke pradhan sampadak hai.
 hindi main ab tak 40 pustaken likh chuke hai. apane shodh-patron tathaa granthon ke dwaara unhone morishas ke itihaas ko nae aayam die hai. unki pustak 'morishas kii lokkathaayen’ kaa german tathaa marathi main anuwaad ho chukaa he. 'morishas ke aadi kavy kaanan’, 'morishas ke madhyakaalin kavy prasoon’ tathaa 'morishas kaa itihaas’ unki charchit pustaken hai. unhone francisi tathaa angrezi bhashaaon main bhi lagbhag 30 pustaken likhi hai. apane purakhon ke desh bharat se unhen vishesh lagaav he.
