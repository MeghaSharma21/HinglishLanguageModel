 khagoleey yantriki main akashiya pindon kii gatiyon ke ganitiya siddhanton kaa vivechan kiya jaataa he. newton dwaara princepia main upasthaapit gurutvkarshan niyam tathaa teen gatinium khagoleey yantriki ke mul aadhaar hai. is prakaar isamen vichaarneey samasya dwitiya varna ke saamaanya avakal samikarnon ke ek varg ke hal karne tak simit ho jaati he.
 17veen shatabdi ke prarambh main johain kepler ne grahgati ke teen prasiddh anubhutimulak niyamon kaa nirmaan kiya, jinke saath uskaa naam judaa he. ye niyam newton ke guru tvkarshan tathaa gati ke teen aadharbhoot niyamon ke do kaayon par prayog ke upafal hai tathaa is prakaar ye newton kii prakkalpana ko pusht karte hai. newton ke teen gatinium sadaa ek jadta pranali ke sandarbh main hai, jiskaa praaia: paryaapt sookshmata ke saath akashganga ke saapeksh sthir pranali se ekatm sthapit kiya ja saktaa he. do kaayon ke prashnon ko teen kaayon ke prashnon tak tathaa vyapak rup main ‘na’ kaayon ke prashnon tak vistrut karne main bahut kathinaayee upasthit hoti he. do kaayon ke prashnon ke vipareet ‘na’ kaayon ke prashn, yadi na do se adhik ho to, hal nahin hote. saura parivaar, jisamen surya tathaa navagraha hai or adhikansh grah upgrahonvaale hai, ek bahukayik prashn prastut karata he. isi prakaar surya, prithvi tathaa chandramaa kii sanhati teen kaayon ke prashn kaa udhaaharan he.
 khagoleey yantriki sambandhi niyamanirmaan ke praarambhik dinon main hi ganitagya jyotishiyon kaa dhyaan teen kaayon ke prashn kii ore gaya tha. is prashn ke hal ke liye bijaganitiya prakruti se das gyaat anukal apekshit hai. is prashn kaa sameekaran 18 varnon kii sanhati kaa he, jise joseph lui lagranj ne das anuklon kii sahaayta, patvilopan tathaa kalvilopan ke chah varnon ke sameekaran main simit kar diya tha. paru sha dasha main isse adhik laghav sambhav nahin tha. aisi dasha main, jisamen ek kaay kaa dravyaman atyalp maan liya jaay or vah aise do dravyamanon ke kshetra main gatisheel ho jo vrittakar kakshaon main bhraman karte hon, samasya simit ho jaati he or iskaa hal saral he. vyapak rup main teen kaayon ke prashn kaa hal mil saktaa he, jise sansruth ghaat shreniyon main vyakt kiya ja saktaa he. is vidhi kaa ke. epa. sundaman ne prayog kiya tha. ‘na’ kaayon ke prashn main grahon ke paraspar aakarshan kii tulana main surya kaa aakarshan adhik hota he. iske kaaran uttarottar asannikaran kii vidhi kaa prayog kiya ja saktaa he. anya grahon kii upasthiti ke kaaran grahakakshaon ke dirghavrittakar main honevaale vichlan kshobh kehalaate hai. lagranj ne grahon ke kshobhon kii gananaa ke liye ek vidhi nikaali thi. dirghavrittakar kaksha main chah sthiraank hote hai, jinhen avyav kehete hai. kshubdh kaksha main chah avyavon ko kaal kaa falan mana ja saktaa he. lagranj kii vidhi se in falnon ke avkaljon ke liye vaishleshik vyanjak aa jate hai, jinke anukulan ke liye uttarotar asannikaran kii vidhi kaa prayog karnaa padataa he. chah avyavon ke antim rup main aavartak pad or kaal ke anupaati pad arthath tathakthith dirghakaalik pad rahete hai. kshobh ke prashn ko hal karne kii duusari vidhi yah he ki sidhe niamakon main hi kshobhon ko nikaal liya jaay. is prachaar kii vidhiyon kaa laplas tathaa newcomb ne prayog kiya tha.
 nepchun kaa avishkaar grahgati ke siddhant kii mahatvuparn safalataa he. je. si. adams tathaa bi. je. ja. leverior ne uranus grah kii gati ke kshobhon kaa vichaar karte samay siddhant rup se isaki satta tathaa aakash main isaki sthiti kii bhavishyavaanee kii thi.
 chandramaa tathaa vyapak rup main upgrahon kii gati grahon kii gati se bhinn he. inamen pehali gati pichhali se bahut drut he. at: jis prakaar grahon ke siddhant main kaal kshobh ke padon ke gunak rup main aata he, vaisaa nahin hone diya ja saktaa. isaliye aise siddhant ke nirmaan kii aavashyaktaa he jo is dosha se rahit ho. upgrahon kii gati ke vivechan ke liye chandramaa kaa siddhant sarvottam he. yah praytn kiya gaya he ki chandramaa ke siddhanton main prayukt adhik shuddh vidhiyon kaa grahgati ke prashnon main prayog kiya ja sake. newton kaa guru tvkarshan niyam dravyakanon ke liye vihit he. khagoleey yantriki kii samasyaon main akashiya pindon ko saamanyat: bindudravya maan se vyakt kiya jaataa he. saant kaay, jinka dravyaman goleey samiti se banta he, ek duusare ko is prakaar aakarshith karte hai maanon tulymaan ke dravyakan centre main nihit hon. kintu akashiya pind golakaar nahin hai. duuri badhane se golakaar na hone ke prabhaav kaa dosha is prakaar kam ho jaataa he ki paryaapt duuri par sthit do kaayon kii dasha main golakar na hone kaa prabhaav mahatvuparn nahin hota. yadi do kaay paraspar nikat hon, jaise shani tathaa uskaa sabase bhitari upgrah hai, to iskaa prabhaav kaaphi drishy hota hai.
 yah acchhee tarah gyaat ho chukaa he ki newton kaa vishwavyaapee gurutvkarshan niyam tathaa teen gatinium asann rup main shuddh hai. shuddh gatinium to saapekshavaad hi prastut karata he, tathaapi jyotish kii adhikansh samasyaon main aapeksh shodhan ati nune hote hai. budh ke ravinich kii gati main aapeksh prabhaav kaaphi drishy hota he or ise vedh dwaara bhi pusht kiya ja chukaa he. khagoleey yantriki main praaia: apanaai janevaali vidhi yah he ki pehle newton ke siddhanton se gananaa kar lee jaati he tathaa baad main aapeksh prabhaavon ke liye upyukt shodhan kar diya jaataa he.
