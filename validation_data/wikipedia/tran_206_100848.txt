 ural nadi, uraal nadi yaa yaayak nadi rus or kazakhstan se bahane vaali ek nadi he. yah ural pahaado ke dakshini bhaag main utpann hoti he or 1,511 kimi ke lambe safar ke baad caspian sagar main mil jaati he. volga nadi or danyube nadi ke baad yah europe kii teesri sabase lambi nadi he. caspian sagar ke liye yah volga nadi ke baad doosraa sabase mukhya jalstrot he.
 'yaayak' shabd main 'ji' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'jha' se kaafii bhinn he. iskaa uccharan angrezi ke 'televithan' shabd or hindi ke andaha shabd ke 'ji' se miltaa he.
 ural nadi ural parvaton ke kruglaya pahaad kii dhalaan par shuru hoti he jo rajnatik rup se rus ke bashkoratustan praant main sthit he. yahan isaki chaudaai 60-80 meter he. phir yah neeche yike daldali kshetra main aa jaati he jahaan isaki chaudaai badhkar 5 kimi ho jaati he. phir yah rus ke maagnithogorsk shahar se guzarati he jo chelyabinsk oblast main he. iske kinare ab patthariile or gahare ho jate hai. phir yah orenburg oblast ke orsk shahar se guzarati hui achaanak pashchim kaa rukh karti he or gooserlinsk pahaado main ek tang ghati se nikalati he jiske baad yah kazakhstan ke ural shahar se nikalati he. iske baad yah sidhaa dakshin kii ore chalati hui pashchimi kazakhstan praant or atirau praant se guzarati he. ab isaki dhar bantakar bahut si upadharaayen or jhilein banaati he, jinamein yike dhara or zolotoy dhara pramukh hai. in donon se yahan ek vishaal ardra ilaaka banataa he. isamen bahut se pakshi, jaanavar, machhliyaan or vanaspati panpate hai. caspian sagar main ural nadi kii delta kaa ek ajeeb-garib lambi oongali jaisaa aakaar he.
