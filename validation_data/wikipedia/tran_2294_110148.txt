 joseph fritzl kaa maamla april 2008 main prakaash main aayaa. elizabeth fritzl jo aaropee joseph fritzl kii putree he ne apane pita joseph fritzl par ye aarope lagaaya ki usne use unke ke hi ghar ke tahkhaane main 24 varshon tak kaid main rakhaa or use shaareerik, maansik or yon utpedan kiya. uske pita dwaara uske saath banaae gayi aniatik sambandho ke chalate uski 7 santaanen hui or ek garbhpaat.
 teen bacchon ko unki maan ke saath hi kaid main rakhaa gaya: putree carustin, 19; or do putr stifan, 18 or felics, 5. ek anya putr maaikel kii janm ke teen din baad hi saans kii pareshaani ke kaaran mrityu ho gai. anya teen bacchon kaa paalan fritzl or uski patni rosemeri dwaara kiya gaya. in teen bacchon ke sambandh main usne ye bahaanaa banaaya kii ye teenon use sadak par pade hue mili.
 24 agast 1984 ke din elizabeth ke pita ne use ek darvaaze ko tahkhaane main rakhane ke liye sahaayta ke liye bulaayaa or ether se use behosh karke ek chupe hue chote tahkhaane main band kar diya or 26 april 2008 ke apane rihaai ke din tak wo vahin rahi.
 elizabeth ke yun achaanak se gaayab ho jane par uski maan ne police main report darj karwaai, lekin joseph fritzl ne ek managadnt kahani banaai kii wo apni ichha se ghar chhod kar bhaagi he or usne bronau naamak kasbe main kisi dharmik sampradaay kii sadsyta le lee he. iske liye usne apni putree dwaara likhe gaye patr ko bhi police ko dikhaaya or phir ye maamla band kar diya gaya. in 24 varshon kii kaid ke dauraan kisi ko bhi tahkhaane main aane kii anumati nahin thi or uski patni kaafii tak lekar neeche nahin aa sakati thi. maamle kii sunwaayee ke dauraan fritzl kii saali ne bataaya kii wo pratidin praatah 9 baje tahkhaane main chalaa jaaya karata tha or kabhi-kabhi der raat tak vahin rukaa karata tha. unke ghar main 12 varshon tak rahe ek kiraayadaar ne bataaya kii kabhi-kabhi tahkhaane se jor-jor kii awaazen aati thi or poochne par fritzl un awaazon ko mashin dbaara kaam karne se aa raheen awaajen bataata tha.
