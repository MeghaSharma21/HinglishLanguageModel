 dargah ek sufi-islamic punyasthaan hota he jise aksar kisi pratishthit sufi sant yaa darvesh kii kabr par banaaya jaataa he. sthaneey muslim, tirthayatra ke uddeshy se kisi dargah kii yatra karte hai or is yatra ko ziyaarat kaha jaataa he. kisi dargah ke saath aksar sufi baithken or vishram grih bane hote hai jinhen khanqaah kaha jaataa he. iske atirikt aamtaur par ek masjid, baithak, islamic dharmik vidhyalay, shikshakon or karmchaariyon ke liye ghar, aspataal, saamudayik bhawan aadi bhi kisi dargah parisar kaa hissaa hote hai.
