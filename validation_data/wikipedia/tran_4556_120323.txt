 dabar india limited bharat main swasthya, vyaktigat dhyaan evam bhojya utpaadon ke kshetra main nivesh karne vaali chauthi sabase badi company he. dabar kaa chyavanprash evam hajamola bahut hi lokapriy he.
 1884 main burman parivaar ne jab ek choti ayurvedic dawa company ke rup main shuruyaat kii thi to 125 saal baad yah number van company ban jaaegi kisi ne sochaa nahin hoga. kolkata ke burman parivaar kii company dabar india limited ne ab puri duniya main apana paracham laharaa diya he. ayurvedic va prakritik chikitsa ke kshetra main 125 saal kii ho gai is company kaa ab koi sani nahin he. aaj vah desh kaa herbal or natural product kii sabase badi peshewar company ban gai he.
 dabar india ke 250 se adhik utpaadon kii bazaar main tuuti bol rahi he. dawaai se lekar food tak main har jagah dabar maujuud dikhataa he. dabar ke utpaada puri duniya ke 60 se adhik deshon main uplabdh hai. sirf videsh main hi iskaa kaarobaar 500 karod rupye kaa he.
 cambridge vishvavidhyalay se emb kii degree hasil kar dabar india limited kii kaman sanbhaalane vaale company ke upaadhyaksh amit burman ek payalat bhi hai. unhen hawa main udana bahut acha lagata he. agar yah kahen ki amit burman kii udne kii isi lalak ne dabar india limited ko ayurveda kii bulandiyon par pahunchaa diya he to atishyokti nahin hogi. kehete hai amit burman company ke 'payalat' hai to isaki udne kii seemaa akaash hi he.
 patanjali ayurveda kii babbti chunauti se nipatane ke liye ab dabar company apni nai ranneeti taiyaar kar rahi he, jiske saath hi company apane ayurvedic utpaadon main aadhunik samay ke mutabik badlaav kar bazaar main apane nae utpaada utaarne kii taiyaari main he. dabar shuruuaat main mahilaon ke healthcare se jude productus ko aadhunik formet main laaegaa or iske baad baby segment main apane productus lonch karegaa.
