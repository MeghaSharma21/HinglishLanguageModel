 sheet ritu athwa shishir ritu varsh kii ek ritu he, jisamen vaataavaran kaa taapmaana praayah nimna rahata he. 
 saal kii anya pramukh ritu hai - grishm ritu, varsha ritu, vasant ritu.sheet ritu, bharat main yah navambar se farvari tak hoti he. anya deshon main yah alag samayon par ho sakati he.
 
 varsha ritu kii samaapti ke pashchaat sharad ritu kaa aagaman hota he . panchvati main shriram-lakshmana se shardaagam kaa varnan karte hue kehete hai:
 varsha vigat sarad ritu ai . lachhiman dekhahu param suhai .. foole kaas sakal mahi chai . janu barasaa krit pragat budhaai ..
 asina or karthik sharad ritu ke do maas hote hai . is ritu main surya pingal or ushn hota he . akaash nirmal or kahin-kahin shwet varna megh yukt hota he . sarovar kamalon sahit hanson se shobhyman hote hai . sukhi bhoomi chitiyon se bhar jaati he .
 varsha kaal main bhoomisth jal main anek prakaar ke khanij padaarth mil jate hai . mel, mutr, keet, krimi unka mel-mutr sab kuch jal main aakar mil jaataa he . ise nirvish karne ke liye surya kii jeevanu naashak prakhar kirane, chandramaa kii amritamay kirane or hawa aavashyak he tathaa yah sab sharad ritu main praapt hoti he .
 sharad ritu main raaten thandi or suhaavnee ho jaati he . van kumud or malti ke phulon se sushobhit hote hai . anginat taron kii chamak or chandramaa kii chandani se raatri kaa andhkaar dur ho jaataa he . sansar aisa lagata he . maanon doodh ke sagar main snaan kar rahaa ho .
 sharad ritu ke suhaavne mausam main aisa koi sarovar nahin he jis main sundar camel na hon, aisa koi pankaj nahin he jis par bhramar nahin baitha ho, aisa koi bhunra nahin he jo gunj na rahaa ho . aisi koi bhanbhanaahat or pakshiyon kaa colorav nahin jo man na har rahaa ho . kehane kaa taatpary yah he ki sharad ritu main camel khile hue he, kamalon par baithe hue bhauron kii raseeli gunj manushyon ke chitt ko chura rahi hai .
