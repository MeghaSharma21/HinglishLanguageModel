 dwitiya francisi samrajya france main, duusare ganraajya or tisaren ganraajya ke bich, 1852 se 1870 tak napolian tritiy kaa shahi bonapartist shashan tha.
 2 disambar 1851 ko louis-napolian bonapart, jise ganraajya kaa raashtrapati chunaa gaya tha, ne takhtapalat kar neshnal assembly ko bhang kar diya or france kaa ekmaatr shaasha ban gaya, usne saarvbhaumik matadhikaar ko phir se sthapit kiya, jise pehle vidhansabha dwaara samaapt kar diya gaya. unke faisle or 10 saal ke liye apane janadesh ko logon kaa samarthan praapt tha, jise maheene bhar baad main karaaye gayi ek janamat-sangrah se pushti hoti thi, jisamen 92 pratishat log unke samarthan main the.
 ek nayaa samvidhan janvari 1952 main adhiniyamit kiya gaya, jisamen louis-nepolian ko raashtrapati ke rup main 10 saal kaa kaarykaal or uske haathon main lagbhag sabhi shaktiyaan kendrit kar di gai. haalanki, vah keval ek sattavadi raashtrapati hone ke saath santusht nahin the, jaise hi unhone kanoon ke nae dastavej par hastakshar kiye, unhone samrajya ko behaal karne kii baat kahi. samrajya ke punahsthaapan ke aadhikarik taur par prerit anurodhon ke jawaab main, seenet ne november main ek doosraa janamat sangrah karaayaa gaya, jise 97 pratishat samarthan praapt hua. disambar 1851 ke janamat sangrah jeet ke baad, samrajya ko aupchaarik rup se 2 disambar 1852 ko phir se sthapit kiya gaya, or rajkumaar-raashtrapati, "napolian tritiy, francisi samraat" ban gaye the. haalanki samvidhan ke dwaara unhone pehle hi sari shakti apane haathon main le lee thi. maatr "raashtrapati" shabd "samraat" shabd se badal diya gaya, or yah pad ab anuvanshik ban gaya. lokapriy janamat sangrah bonapartism kaa ek vishisht lakshan ban gaya, jise aage chal kar charles di galle ne apane liye upyog kiya tha.
 1860 ke dashak ke dauraan, padosi prashia ke udai ne france kii neshnal assembly ke bheetar kaaphi asahajata paidaa hui. 15 july ko, spain ke singhaasana par honzolarn ke ummidavaari ko lekar france ne prashia ke khilaaf yuddh kii ghoshnaa kar di, haalanki frans ne iske bahaane, france main badhati asthirataa or madhya europe main prashia ke vistaar ko rokane ke liye yuddh kii ghoshnaa kii thi. july or agast 1870 ke dauraan, imperial francisi sena ko kai paraajay kaa saamana karnaa padaa, jo jaakar sedan ke yuddh main samaapt hua. sedan main, bachi hui francisi sena or napolian tritiy ne 2 sitambar ko prashia ke samaksh aatmasamarpan kar diya. 4 sitambar ko jab yah samachar paris pahunchi, usee din updravkaari bheed ne paris ke neshnal assembly par hamalaa bol diya. agle din republican dipti leon jubeta ne samrajya ke patan or tisare ganraajya kii sthapana kii ghoshnaa kar di. maharani igune france chhod kar great britain chali gai, iske saath hi samrajya aadhikarik rup se samaapt ho gaya.
