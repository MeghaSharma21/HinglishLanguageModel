 machhindra kambli, marathi rangamanch ke ek vayovruddh kalakaar the. inkaa janm malvan main hua tha. kambli ko unke shandaar hasya naatakon ke liye janaa jaataa he saath hi marathi rangamanch par malvani boli ko manyata dilaane kaa shrey bhi inhen hi jaataa he. unhone kai marathi/malvani naatakon kaa nirdesan kiya tathaa unamem abhinay bhi kiya. 55 varsh kii aayu main 30 sitambar 2007 ko unka nidhan ho gaya.
 unke kaamon main shaamil he: vaai varsha panchavan, maajha pati chhatrapati.
