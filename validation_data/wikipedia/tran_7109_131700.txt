 podbharti ek hindi podpatrika he. iske rachayita evam sanchaalak debashish chakravarti tathaa shashi singh hai. isamen hindi podcast vivaran sahit prakashit hote hai. podbharti april 2007 main prarambh hui. isamen takanalogy, internet par bhartiya bhashaaon se sambandhit takanic va jaankaree tathaa samayik vishyon or manoranjan se sambandhit vividh samachar vichaar prastut kiye jate hai. 2008 main podbharti ko pratishthit tata nen hotest starop tathaa manthan puraskaar ke liye namankit kiya gaya.
