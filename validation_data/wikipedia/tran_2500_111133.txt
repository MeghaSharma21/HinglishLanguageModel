 akhil vishwa gayatri parivaar pandit shriram sharma acharya dwaara sthapit hindu sudhaar aandolan he. isaki kaary pranali or uddeshy arya samaaj se milte-julate hai. isaki sthapana 1950 ke dashak main hui thi. is sanstha ne vichaar kranti abhiyaan, pragna abhiyaan aadi chalaaye jiskaa uddeshy janamanas main vaicharik parivartan laakar samaaj kaa utthan karnaa he. ham badalenge, yug badalegaa; manushya bhatka hua devtaa he aadi inke pramukh udghosh he. is sanghathan kaa kaary gayatri mantra kii mul bhaavana ke anusaar he.
