 morishas ganraajya, africa mahadweep ke tat ke dakshinpurv main lagbhag 900 kilometre kii duuri par hind mahaasaagar main or madagascar ke purv main sthit ek dweepiya desh he. morishas dweep ke atirikt is ganraajya me, saint brendan, rodrigaz or agaalega dweep bhi shaamil hai. dakshinpaschim main 200 kilometre par sthit francisi reunion dweep or 570 kilometre uttar purv main sthit rodrigaz dweep ke saath morishas muskarene dweep samooh kaa hissaa he. marishas kii samskruti, mishrit samskruti he, jiskaa kaaran pehle iskaa france ke aadhin hona tathaa baad main british swaamitv main aana he. morishas dweep vilupt ho chuke dodo pakshi ke antim or ekmaatr ghar ke rup main bhi vikhyath he.
 morishas ke sabase puraane abhilekh lagbhag 10 veen shatabdi kii shuruyaat ke hai jo dravid or austroneshi navikon ke sandanrbh se aate he. portugali navikon pehle pahal yahan 1507 main aaye or unhone is nirjan dweep par ek yatra adda sthapit kiya or phir is dweep ko chhod kar chale gayi. san 1598 main holland ke teen pot jo masala dweep kii yatra par nikle the ek chakravaat ke dauraan rasta bhatak kar yahan pahunch gayi. unhone is dweep kaa naam apane nasao ke yuvaraj morris ke sammaan main morishas rakh diya. san1638 main, dach logon ne yahan pehali sthaayee basti basaai. chakravaaton vaali kathor jalwaayu paristhitiyon or basti ko hone vaale lagaataar nuksaan ke kaaran dacho ne kuch dashakon baad is dweep ko chhod diya. france, jiskaa pehle se hi iske padosi oil borbon dweep par niyantran tha ne san 1715 main morishas par kabza kar liya or iskaa naam badalakar – oil de france rakh diya. france ke shashan me, yah dweep ek samrudh arthvyavastha ke rup main viksit hua jo chini utpaadan par aadhaarit thi. yah aarthik parivartan governor francoes maahe de lebordonis ke dwaara shuru kiya gaya tha.
 britain ke saath apane kai sainya sangharshon ke dauraan, france ne gaiyrkanoonee ghoshit jaldasyuon "corcerse" ko sharan di, jo aksar british jahajo, jin par moolyawaan vyapaar kaa maal ladaa hota tha ko unki bharat or britain ke madhya hone vaali yatraaon ke dauraan lut lete the. san 1803-1815 ke dauraan hue nepolian yudhon main british is dweep kaa niyantran paane main safal ho gayi. grand port kii ladaai jeetne ke baavjood, jo ki nepolian kii britishon par ekmaatr samudri vijay thi, francisi, teen maheene baad, kep mailaheurow main britain se haar gayi. unhonen aupchaarik rup se 3 disambar 1810 ko kuch sharton ke saath samarpan kar diya, ye sharten thim, ki dweep par francisi bhashaa kaa prayog jari rahegaa or aapraadhik maamalon main nagarikon par france kaa kanoon lagu hoga. british shashan ke antargat, is dweep kaa naam badalakar waapas morishas kar diya gaya.
 san 1965 main, britain ne chhagos dweepasmooh ko morishas se alag kar diya. unhone aansa british hind mahaasaagar kshetra sthapit karne ke liye kiya jisase ve samrik mahatv ke dwipon kaa prayog sanyukt rajya america ke saath raksha sahyog ke vibhinn prayojanon ke liye kar saken. haalaanki morishas kii tatkaaleen sarkaar unke is kadam se sahamat thi par baad kii sarkaron ne unke is kadam ko antarrashtriya kanoon ke tahat avaidh bataaya he or in dweep samoohon par apana adhikaar jataayaa he. unke is daawey ko, sanyukt raashtra dwaara bhi manyata di gayi he.
 morishas ne 1968 main swatantraaa praapt kii or desh rashtramandal ke tahat, san1992 main ek ganatantr bana. morishas ek sthir loktantr he jahaan niyamit rup se swatantra chunaav hote hai or maanavadhikaron ke maamle main bhi desh kii chhavi acchhee he, iske chalate yahan kaaphi videshi nivesh hua he or yah desh africa main sabase adhik prati vyakti aay vaale deshon main se ek he.
 morishas ek sansadeey loktantr he jisaki sanrachana britain kii sansadeey pranali par aadhaarit he. rajya kaa pramukh raashtrapati hota he jiskaa kaarykaal paanch varsh kaa hota he or uskaa chunaav rashtriya sabha, morishas kii ekasadaneey sansad karti he. rashtriya sabha ke 62 sadsy janata dwaara chune jate hai jabaki chaar se aath sadasyon kii niyukti chunaav main haare "shreshth parajeet" ummidavaaron ke bich se jaateey alpsankhyakon kaa pratinidhitiv karne ke liye tab kii jaati he jab in samudaayon ko chunaav se uchit pratinidhitiv naa mila ho. pradhanmantri or mantri parishad sarkaar kaa netritv karte hai. sarkaar paanch saal ke aadhaar par nirvaachit hoti he. sabase haal ke aam chunaav 3 july 2005 main mukhya bhoomi ke sabhi 20 nirvachan kshetron ke saath hi rodrigaz dweep ke nirvachan kshetra main bhi karaaye gayi the. antarrashtriya maamalon main, morishas hind mahaasaagar ayog, dakshini africa vikaas samudaay, rashtramandal or laa francophoni kaa hissaa he. san 2006 main, morishas ko portugali bhashaai deshon ke samudaay kaa ek prekshak sadsy banane ko kaha gaya jisase yah un deshon ke or kareeb ho sake. morishas kii koi sena nahin he, lekin iske paas ek tatarakshak bal tathaa police or suraksha bal hai.
 morishas dweep nau jilon main vibhajit he:
 morishas nimna kshetron par bhi adhikaar jataata he:
 morishas mascarene dweep samooh kaa hissaa he. is dweepasmooh kii shrinkhalaa un ant:samudri jwalamukheey visphoton ke kaaran bani he jo ab sakriy nahin hai. yah jwalamukheey visphot africa plate ke reunion taptbindu ke upar sarakane ke kaaran hue the. morishas dweep ek kendriya pathaar ke chaaron ore bana he, jisaki ucchtam chotii piton de laa petit riviere noere 828 meter unchi he or iske dakshin main sthit he. pathaar ke aasapaas, mul gart phir bhi pahaado se alag dikhaai padataa he.
 sthaneey jalwaayu ushnkaribandheey he, jo dakshinpurv kii hawaaon dwaara sanshodhit hoti he. yahan may se november tak shushk sardiyon padatin hai or navambar se may kaa mausam garm, adra or geeli garmi kaa hota he. virdohi-chakravaat desh ko may se sitambar ke dauraan prabhavit karte he. chakravaaton kaa samay november-april hota he. hollanda or dina pichley do chakravaat hai jinhone dweep ko sabase zyada prabhavit kiya he.
 uttarpashchim main sthit port lui is dweep kii rajdhani or sabase bada shahar he. anya mahatvapurn shaharon main curapipe, vakoas, finics, kuarte borness, rose hill or biyu-basin shaamil he.
 yah dweep apni prakritik sundarataa ke liye janaa jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye, lekhak mark twain, ne apane nijee yatra vrutant ‘folowing da equeater’ main likhaa he ki " morishas ke dekh kar aapako vichaar aata he pehle morishas bana or phir swarg or swarg, morishas kii ek nakal maatr he.”
 san 1968 main aazaadee ke baad se, marishas ek nimna aay vaali, krishi utpaada aadhaarit arthvyavastha se viksit hokar ek vividhtapurna madhyam aay vaali arthvyavastha main parivartit ho gaya he jisame teji se badhata audyogic, vittiya or paryaton kshetra shaamil he. adhikansh avadhi main vaarshik vruddhi dar, 5 % se 6 % ke bich darj kii gai he. yah dar badhati jivan pratyaasha, ghatati shishu mrityu dar or buniyadi dhaanche main sudhaar se parilakshith hoti he.
 san 2005 main, anumaanit 10155 amareeki dollars, krayshakti samta, ke saath morishas africa main prati vyakti sakal gharelu utpaada ke hisaab se saatven sthaan par he, isse aage hai reunion, seshals, gabon, botswana, bumadhya rekhiy gini or libya .
 arthvyavastha mukhyatah ganna bagan, paryaton, kapadaa or seva kshetra par nirbhar he, lekin anya kshetron main bhi teji se vikaas ho rahaa hai. morishas, libya or seshals keval teen aanse africa desh hai jinka darza " maanav vikaas suchakank” ke hisaab se ‘ucch’ he. 
 ganna 90% krishi yogya bhoomi par ugaaya jaataa he or jisase kul niryaat aay kaa 25% praapt hota he. lekin 1999 main pade bhayankar sookhe se ganne kii fasal buree tarah kshatigrast hui thi. sarkaar kii vikaas yojanayen videshi nivesh par aadhaarit he. marishas ne 9000 se adhik apatati sansthaaon ko aakarshith kiya he jinka uddeshy bharat or dakshin africa se vyapaar karnaa he jabaki akele banking kshetra main nivesh 1 arab dollar se adhik pahunch gaya he. disambar 2004 main berogari kii dar 7.6 % thi. france desh kaa sabase bada vyapaarik saajheedaar he jiskaa is desh ke saath na sirf nikat sambandh he, balki wo ise vibhinn roopon main takneekee sahaayta pradaan karata he.
 sthaneey nivasion ko kam keemton par aayaat karne kii suvidha dene or adhik paryatakon jo philhal dubai or singapore jate hai, ko aakarshith ke liye morishas agle chaar saal main ek shulk mukt dweep banane kii disha main prayasarat he. kai utpaadon par duty samaapt kar diya gaya he or 1850 se adhik utpaadon par jinamein kapade, bhojan, gahane, chhayankan upkaran, shravy drishy upkaran or prakaash vyavastha ke upkaran shaamil he par shulk gata diya gaya he. iske atirikt, nae vyavsaayik avasaron ko aakarshith karne ke uddeshy se aarthik sudhaaron ko bhi lagu kiya gaya he. haal hi main, 2007-2008 ke bajet main vitt mantri ram seetanan ne company kar ko gata kar 15 % kar diya he. british america investment company morishas main marcides banj, pijo, mitsubishi or saab kaaron kii bikri kaa pratinidhitiv karti he.
 e di bi network kii yojana puure morishas main logon ko wireles internet pahunchaane kii he, abhi tak yah pahunch dweep ke 60% kshetra or 70% jansankhya ke paas he. bharat ke kul 10.98 arab amareeki dollars ke pratyaksh videshi nivesh main morishas kaa sthaan pehla he. sheersh 2000 or janvari 2005 ke bich hone vaala morishas kaa pratyaksh videshi nivesh mukhyat: bijli ke upkaran, doorsanchaar, eendhan, cement or jipsom utpaada tathaa seva kshetra jaise vibhinn kshetron main aakarshith he.
 morishas kaa samaaj vibhinn jaateey samoohon ke logon se mil kar bana he. ganraajya kii adhikansh janata hai bharat, africa, france, britain, australia aadi ke nivaasiyon ke vanshaj hai. bahut se log mishrit jaateey mul ke he
 australia samaaj mishrit britain janata aadhaar vanshaj hai.
 morishas kii aadhikarik bhashaa angreji he, isliye sarkaar kaa sara prashasnic kaamkaaj angreji main hota he. shiksha pranali main angreji ke saath francisi kaa bhi istemaal kiya jaataa he. francisi bhashaa media kii mukhya bhashaa he, chahen prasaaran ho yaa mudran. iske alaava vyapaar or udyog jagat ke maamalon main bhi mukhyatah francisi hi prayog main aati he. sabase vyapak rup se yahan morician creyol bhashaa boli jaati he. hindi bhi ek bade varg dwaara boli va samajhi jaati he. 
 morishas main vibhinn dharmon ke log rahete he jiname pramukh hai hindu dharm, isai dharm or islam . ek bady sankhya naastik logon kii bhi he.
 morishas ko iske swaadisht khaane se bhi janaa jaataa he, jo bhartiya, chini, creyol or european khaano kaa mishran he.is dweep par ram kaa utpaadan bade paimaane par hota he. 1638 main dach logon ne morishas ko sabase pehle ganna se parichit karaayaa. dach ganne kii kheti mukhyatah arak ke utpaadan ke liye karte the. lekin france or britain ke shashan ke dauraan yahan ganne kii kheti ko bade paimaane par kiya gaya jisane is dweep ke aarthik vikaas main kaaphi yogadan diya. piyare charles francoez harel pehla vyakti tha jisane 1850 main morishas main ram ke sthaneey aswan kaa prastaav kiya.
 segaa sthaneey lok sangeet he. segaa mulat: africa sangeet he jisame paramparaagat vaadyo kaa upyog hota he jaise rawaane jise bakrii kii twachaa se banaaya jaataa he. aamtaur par segaa main gulaami ke dinon kii yaatnaaon kaa varnan hota he saath hi in geeton main aajkal ke daur main ashweton kii saamaajik samasyaon ko bhi uthaaya jaataa he. aamtaur par purushon vaadya bajaate hai or mahilayen saath main nrity karti hai. tateey kshetra ke hotalon main ye sho niyamit rup se yojit kiye jate he.
 1847 main morishas dak tikat jari karne vaala paanchvaan desh bana. yahan se do prakaar ke dak tikat jari kiye gayi jinhen tab morishas "daakghar" tikat ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. ek tikat ek "laal penny" or duusari " do nile pence" mulya varg kii thi or aaj yah tikat shaayad duniya kii sabase prasiddh or moolyawaan tikaten he.
 morishas kii jab isaki khoj hui thi, tab yah dweep ek agyaat pakshi prajati kaa ghar tha jise, portugaliyon ne dodo kah kar pukaara kyoki yah bahut aklmand nahin lagate the. lekin, 1681 tak sabhi dodo pakshiyon ko basane waalon or unke paaltu jaanwaron ne maar diya. ek vaikalpik siddhant bataata he ki basane waalon ke saath aaye junglee siiron ne dhimi gati se prajnan karne vaale dodo ke ghonsle ujaad diye. phir bhi, dodo aaj morishas kaa rashtriya pratik chinh ban gaya he.
