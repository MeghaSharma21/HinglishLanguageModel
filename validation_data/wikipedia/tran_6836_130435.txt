 bal gangadhar jambhekar, marathi patrakarita ke agradoot the. unhone 'darpan' naamak pratham marathi patrika armbh kii. unhone itihaas or ganit se sambandhit vishyon par anek pustaken likhin. royal aciatic socity tathaa jiographical socity main padhe gaye shilalekhon tathaa tamrapatron se sambandhit unke niabandh atyant mahatvapurn hai. shilalekhon kii khoj ke silsile main jab ve kanakeshwar gaye the, vahi unhen lu lag gai. isi main unka dehavsaan hua. sachche arth main unhone apane karm main apane jivan kaa samarpan kiya tha. grahan se sambandhit vaastaviktaa apane bhashaanon main prakat karne tathaa shripad sheshadri naamak brahman ko isai dharm se pun: hindoodharm main lene ke kaaran ve jatibahishkrut kar die gaye the. maharashtra ke ve samajasudharak the.
 bal gandhadhar jambhekar kaa janm rajapur jile ke ponworle gaanv main hua tha. unke pita achche vaidic the. adhyapakon main baapu chhatre tathaa baapu shastri shukla the.
 daadoba pandurang ne apni aatmakatha main unki adbhut smaranshakti ke sambandh main ek prasang kaa ullekh kiya he :
 ek baar unhone do gore sipaahiyon ko ladate hue dekha. adaalat main unako gavaah ke rup main upasthit hona padaa. yadhyapi unhen us samay tak angreji nahin aati thi, unhone keval apni smaranshakti se unke sambhaashan ko tathyat: uddhrut kiya. pro arlibar se unhone ganit shaastra kaa gyaan praapt kiya. sa. 1820 main adhyayan kii samaapti ke baad ve elfinston college main apane guru ke sahaayak ke rup main ganit ke adhyaaapak niyukt hue. 1832 main ve akkalkot ke rajkumaar ke angreji ke adhyaaapak ke rup main bhi rahe. isi varsh bhaau mahajan ke sahyog se unhone "darpan" naamak angreji marathi saptahik chalaaya. isamen ve angreji vibhaag main likhte the. ve anek bhashaaon ke pandit the. marathi or sanskrit ke atirikt latin, greek, english, french, farsi, arabi, hindi, bengali, gujarati tathaa kannada bhashaayen unhen aati thim. unki yah bahumukhi yogyata dekhakar sarkaar ne "justise ov di pis" ke pad par unki niyukti kii . is naate ve hoicourt main grand jeuri kaa kaam karte the. 1842 se 1844 tak ageucational inspector tathaa training college ke principal ke rup main bhi rahe. 1840 main "digdarshan" naam kii ek masik patrika bhi unhone shuru kii. isamen ve shastreey vishyon par niabandh likhte the.
