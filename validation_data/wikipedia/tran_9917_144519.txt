 bair 04 leverkusen bhi bair leverkusen ke rup main janaa, leverkusen yaa bas bair, leverkusen, north rin westephelia main sthit ek german footbal club he. yah TSV bair 04 leverkusen, jiske sadsy bhi athletics, jimnastic, basketball or RTHC bair leverkusen sahit anya khelon main bhaag lene ke ek sports club kii sabase prasiddh purv vibhaag he. 1999 main footbal vibhaag club se baahar rakhaa gaya he or bair 04 liverkusen jiemb bulaayaa gaya tha. club kaarkaane main shramikon ke liye pharma diggaj bair kii madad se 1904 main gathan kiya gaya tha.
 bair leverkusen bundeslig, german footbal lig pranali ke sheersh str main khelte hai. liverkusen ek DFB-pocal or ek yuiefee europa lig jeet chuke hai. 1958 ke baad se, bair leverkusen ke stadium bay arena he.
