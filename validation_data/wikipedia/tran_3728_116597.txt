 morse 19 shatabdi ke madhya main ek catholic virdohi or ek apravas virdohi aandolan ke netaa the.
 1836 main, apravas virdohi netivist party kii ore se neuyork ke meyar ke pad par khadey hue or keval 1496 vot paakar haar gaye.
 jab morse rome gaye, tab unhone pope ke saamne apni topi utaarne se inkaar kar diya.aisa dekhakar ek krodhit swiss rakshak ne jhapat kar unke sir se topi utaar di.
 morse ne catholic sansthaanon ke viruddh protestants ko ekajute karne ke liye kaary kiya, ve chaahate the ki catholic saarvajanik karyaalayon main kaam karne se inkaar kar den or unhone catholic deshon se apravas ko simit karne ke liye apravas ke kanoonon main parivartan ko badhaava diya.
 is vishay par unhone likhaa, "hamen jahaaj main hone vaale us risaav ko sabase pehle rokna hoga, jisamen se hokar ganda pani jahaaj main aa rahaa he, yah hamen dubo degaa."
 
 morse ne neuyark obasarwar ko anek patr likhe jisamen logon ko catholic dhamkiyon kaa saamana karne ke liye utsahit kiya gaya. 
 apane Conspiracy Against the Liberties of the United States main unhone likhaa: "america protestant, azadlog nishchit rup se videshi paakhand ke bare main jaanate hai.ve dekhenge ki ab popwaad hameshaa kii tarah, ek ansi paddati he jisamen rajanithik shadyantra or nirankushta he or dharm ke pavitra naam par akraman ko rokane kaa aadhaar he. ve sachai se bahut zyada prabhavit honge, popwaad ek dharmik or rajnatik pranali he; arthath yah anya sabhi jati roopon se alag he, desh main dharm ke anya sabhi roopon se alag he. 
 morse ne 29 sitambar 1819 ko koncord, new hampshire main lucresia pikkering vaakar se vivaah kiya. vah 7 farvari 1825 ko, apane chauthe bachche ke janm ke kuch hi samay baad mar gayin.. unki duusari patni sara elizabeth greeswold thim. unka vivaah 10 agast 1848 ko utikaa, neuyork main hua or unke chaar bachche the.
