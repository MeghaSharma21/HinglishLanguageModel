 aitihad airways emirate ke baad sanyukt arab emirate kii duusari sabase badi airline evam dhvaj vaahak he. iskaa mukhyalay abu dhabi antarrashtriya hawaii adde ke nikat khalipha city, abu dhabi main he. july 2003 main abu dhabi ke emirate ke shahi farmaan dwaara sthapit, is airline ne november 2003 main udaan bharna prarambh kiya.
 yah airlines middle east, africa, europe, asia, australia evam america ke 120 yatri evam malwahak gantavyon par har hafte 1100 udaane bharti he. 2012 main aitihaad ne 10.3 lakh yatriyon kaa parivahan kiya, jo pichley saal se 23% kii adhik thi. aitihaad airways madhya purv main teesri sabase badi airline he evam dubai sthit emirate airline ke baad sanyukt arab emirate main duusari sabase badi airline seva he. 
 yah airlines apane pramukh kshetra yatri parivahan ke alaava aitihaad holidays evam aitihaad cargo bhi sanchalit karti he. 2015 main isane “aitihaad airways parteners” ke naam se khud kaa airline gathbandhan kii sthapana kii. alitaliye, jet airways, air berlin, niki, air srbija, air seychelles or aitihaad kshetreey ne isamen pramukh bhagidaari nibhaakar hawaii yatriyon ko zyada vikalp pradaan kiya. 
 july 2003 main sanyukt arab emirate ke dhvaj vaahak ke rup main, aitihaad airways shaikh khalipha bin jaayad al nahayan dwaara jari kiye gaye royal decri dwaara sthapit kiya gaya tha. yah AED500lakh kii shuruaati chuktaa puunji ke saath shuru kiya gaya tha. 5 november 2003 ko al an kii aupchaarik udaan ke saath isane udaan seva pranrabh kiya. 12 november 2003 ko beirut kii udaan bharkar isane vanijyik parichaalan kii shuruyaat kiya. 
 farvari 2013 se, airline duniya bhar ke 86 gantavyon ke liye yatri or cargo seva evam apane abu dhabi main sthit sanchaalan centre chalaa rahi he. 
 2013 ke dubai airsho main, aitihaad ne swiss airline company darwin airline main 33.3% hissedari kharidne kii ghoshnaa kii. darwin march 2014 se “aitihaad kshetreey” ke rup main ribranded kii gayi thi. 
 aitihaad kaa mukhyalay abu dhabi international airport ke nikat khalipha city, abu dhabi, main he. 
 “equity allins” aitihaad airways ke oparation ke stambhon main se ek he jisame shaamil he.
 vartmaan:
 30 july 2007 main aitihaad airways kii harley queens rugby club or harlequins rugby lig club mukhya praayojak banane kii ghoshnaa kii gai thi.
 18 disambar 2007 main aitihaad ne ghoshnaa kii yas island main hone vaale 2009 abu dhabi grand pricks kaa pramukh praayojak hoga.
 oktober 2008 main, yah ghoshnaa kii gai thi aitihaad melborn main docklands stadium kaa praayojak hoga.1 march 2009 se yah prabhavi ho gaya evam iskaa naam aitihaad stadium ho gaya.
