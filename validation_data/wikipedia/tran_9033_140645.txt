 bharat kai sau bhashaaon kaa ghar he. zyaadaatar bhartiya indo-aryan parivaar kii bhashaa bolte hai jo indo-european kii hi ek shakha he. dravid, austroastrictic munda or sino-tibetan bhi boli jaati hai iske atirikt himalay kii kuch bhashaayen abhi bhi vargeekrut nahin hai. es ai el ethnolog kii suchi anusaar anusaar bharat main 415 jivit bhashaayen hai.
 bharat kaa samvidhan 23 bhashaaon ko sanvaidhanik rup se pahachaanata hai. hindi or bhartiya angreji, kendriya sarkaar dwaara rajbhasha ke rup main prayog kii jaati hai. rajya sarkarein unki aapni bhashaa kaa rajbhasha ke rup main prayog kar sakati hai.
 bharat ke uttari bhaag main hindi sabase adhik boli jaati he. bhartiya janaganana anusaar hindi main anek hindi bhashaayen sammilit hai. 2001 ke bhartiya janaganana anusaar bharat kii 45% aabaadi hindi jaanati he or 25% logon kii yah maatrubhaasha he.2001 kii janaganana main bhartiya angreji 226,449 bharatiyon kii maatrubhaasha record kii gayi he. va keval 14,135 ne sanskrit ko apni maatrubhaasha bataaya. bhili bhashaa sabase badi bhashaa he jo anusuchit nahin he.
 bharat kaa greenberg diversity index 0.914 he arthath agar desh main aise hi kinhi do logon ko chun liya jaye to 91.4% chance he ki donon kii matra bhashaa alag ho.
  pehali bhashaa ke rup main vaktaaon kii sankhya anusaar kram main. "deshi bhashaaon" kaa kul pratishat 127% he 1991 main bharat kii jansankhya main dwibhashavad19.4% or tribhashavad 7.2% paaya gaya.
 2001 kii janaganana main 29 bhashaaon ke 10 lakh se zyaada bolne vaale the. "bold" main ankit bhashaayen anusuchit bhashaayen hai. keval sanskrit hi aisi anusuchit bhashaa he jiske 10 lakh se kam bolne vaale hai.
 
 * manipur ke senapati zile ke paomata, maao-maram or purul up-vibhaagon kii sankhya, 2001 main janaganana radd ho jane ke kaaran nahin jodi gayi.
 2001 kii janaganana anusaaraprayek bhashaa kii ek se adhik maatrubhaashaen hai. bolne waalon kii sankhya in matra bhashaaon ke liye uplabdh hai or ve bhi unki sambandhit bhashaa ko bolne waalon kii sankhya main shaamil kiye gaye hai. nimna talika 10 lakh se adhik vaktaaon vaali matra bhashaaon ko soochibaddh karti he.
