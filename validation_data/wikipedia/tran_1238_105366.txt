 
 saola, jise siola, vu kwang bail, spindalhorn or anya naamon se bhi janaa jaataa he, ek sankatagrast standhari govenshi praani he. jeevvaigyanik drushti se yah gaay, bakrii or hiran se sambandhit he. saola keval laos or vietnam kii annami pahadiyon main hi paaya jaataa he or vishwa ke sabase durlabh mahapraniyon main se ek he. yah jin kshetron main rahata he vah durgam wanon se ghire hai or isaki sankhya bahut hi kam he. is jeevvaigyanik jati kii pahchaan keval san 1992 main hi ek shav kaa adhyayan kar ke hui thi. apni junglee avasthaa main isaki pehali tasviir keval 1999 main hi jaakar kheenchna sambhav hua tha.
