 harcourt butler pravidhik vishvavidhyalay kanpur uttar pradesh sarkaar kaa teesraa pravidhik vishvavidhyalay he. iske purv iskaa naam tha 'harcourt butler takneekee santhaan kanpur'. vishvavidhyalay banane ke purv harcourt butler takneekee santhaan kanpur 25 sitambar 1921 se 30 agast 2016 tak uttar pradesh sarkaar kaa sarvotkrisht takneekee santhaan rahaa.
 yah bharat ke sabase puraane praudyogiki sansthaanon main ek he. ise 2016 main vishvavidhyalay kaa darja mila he. is santhaan ko ISO 9001:2000 praaanpatr praapt he. yah santhaan ejiniyari vishyon main snaatak, parasnatak or shodh digriyan dene ke atirikt bijness administration or computor anuprayog main master degree pradaan karata he.
 uttar pradesh ke sarkari gajet main dinaank 7 april 2016 ko uttar pradesh sarkaar prakashit adhisuchana main harcourt butler takneekee santhaan kanpur ko harcourt butler pravidhik vishvavidhyalay adhishuchet kiya gaya saath main yah bhi kaha gaya kiya santhaan awasaiya hoga. 1 sitambar 2016 se vishvavidhyalay ne kaary karnaa prarambh kar diya. 
 vishvavidhyalay main kaaryakari parishad main 14 sadsy honge.
