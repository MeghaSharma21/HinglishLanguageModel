  nehru report bharat ke liye prastaavit nae adhirajya ke samvidhan kii ruparekha thi. agast, 1928ko jari yah rapat britani sarkaar ke bharatiyon ke ek samvidhan banane ke ayogy bataane kii chunauti kaa bhartiya rashtriya congress ke netritv main diya gaya shashakt pratyuttar tha. motilal nehru ke netritv main gathit is praaroop nirmaatri samiti main 2 musalmaan sahit 9 sadsy the. jawaharlal nehru iske sachiv the. report ne suzav diya ki shararthbhari sampradayik chunaav paddati tyaag di jaay taaki uske sthaan par alpsankhyakon ke liye unki jansankhya ke aadhaar par sthaan arakshit kar diya jaae. isane samast bharat ke liye ek ikai vaala samvidhan prastut kiya jiske dwaara bharat ko centre tathaa praanton main purna pradeshik swaittata mili. britani sarkaar ne ise atyadhik pragatisheel kahkar maanane se inkaar kar diya tha.
