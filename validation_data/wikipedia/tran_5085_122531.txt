 nallaari kiran kumaar reddy ek bhartiya raajnneetik netaa he or andhrapradesh ke vartmaan kertekar mukhyamantri he. ye chaar baar andhrapradesh vidhaan sabha ke liye chune gaye. farvari 2014 main telugana rajya ke ghoshna ke baad inhonen congress se istepha de diya.
 kiran kumaar reddy ke mata pita sarojmma or en amarnath reddy he. amarnath reddy bhi congress party ke sadsy rahe he or pivi narsinharav ke kaarykaal me kyabeanet mantri rah chuke he. amarnath reddy indira gandhi evam pivi narsinha raav ke atyant nikat vishwasi maane jate he. inkaa parivaar chitoor jila ke kalikiri ke nikat nagaripalle gaanv se he. kiran kii padhai hyderabad public skool se hui thi or preuniwarsity seyint jans junior kalege hyderabad se. nizaam kalege se bi.kaam kii or osmania uniwarsity ke vakaalat kalege se elelbi kii. ye hyderabad ke under-22 sout jone uniwarsitis or osmania uniwarsity ke cricket team captain rah chuke he. inhone kuch din ke liye channai sthit bicent thisafical skool me bhi vidyadhyayan kiya.
 kiran reddy pehali baar 1989 may unke pita ke mrityu ke baad, andhrapradesh vidhaan sabha ke liye chune gaye. 1989, 1999 or 2004 me vayalpadu se jite. 1994 me jab congress chitoor jile me nammaatr na rahaa, ye haar gaye. 2009 me pileru se chunaav jeeta. ye vaies rajasekharreddy ke anuyaayi maane jate he. 2004 se lekar 5 varsho ke liye ye chif vip rahe. uske baad ye vidhansabha ke sabhapati bane.
 andhrapradesh assembly ke terahave satr ke sabhapati ke rup me kiran reddy june 2009 me chune gaye. inke chunaav kaa samardhan vaies rajasekharreddy, praja rajyam party ke chiranjeevi, eiemaiem ke net akbaruddin ovaisi or asaduddin ovaisi ne kii.
