 avadhi hindi kshetra kii ek upbhasha he. yah uttar pradesh main "avadh kshetra", elahabaad, kaushambi, ambedkar nagar, gonda, baharich, shravasti tathaa fatehpur main bhi boli jaati he. iske atirikt isaki ek shakha baghelkhand main bagheli naam se prachlit he. 'avadh' shabd kii vyutpatti "ayodhya" se he. is naam kaa ek suba ke rajyakaal main tha. tulsidas ne apane "maanas" main ayodhya ko 'avadhpuri' kaha he. isi kshetra kaa purana naam 'kosal' bhi tha jisaki mahatta pracheen kaal se chali aa rahi he.
 bhashaa shastri dow sar "george abraham grearsan" ke bhashaa surveykshan ke anusaar avadhi bolne waalon kii kul aabaadi 1615458 thi jo san 1971 kii janaganana main 28399552 ho gai. maujuuda samay main shodhakartaaon kaa anumaan he ki 6 karod se zyada log avadhi bolte hai. uttar pradesh ke 19 jilon- sultanpur, amethi, barabanki, pratapgarh, elahabaad, kaushambi, fatehpur, raibareli, unnav, lakhanu, hardoi, sitapur, lakhimpur khiri, baharich, shravasti, balrampur, gonda, faijabad va ambedkar nagar main puri tarah se yah boli jaati he. jabaki 6 jilon- jonpur, mirzapur, kanpur, shahjahanpur, basti or banda ke kuch kshetron main iskaa prayog hota he. bihar ke 2 jilon ke saath padosi desh nepal ke 8 jilon main yah prachlit he. isi prakaar duniya ke anya deshon- morishas, trinidad evam tubago, fiji, guyana, surinam sahit austrailia, newjiland va holland main bhi lakhon kii sankhya main avadhi bolne vaale log hai.
 gathan kii drushti se hindi kshetra kii upbhaashaon ko do virgon-pashchimi or purvi main vibhajit kiya jaataa he. avadhi purvi ke antargat he. purvi kii duusari upbhasha chhattisgadhi he. avadhi ko kabhi-kabhi baisawadi bhi kehete hai. parantu baisawadi avadhi kii ek boli maatr he jo unnav, lakhanu, raibareli or fatehpur jile ke kuch bhaagon main boli jaati he.
 tulsidas krit ramcharitamanas evam malik mohammad jaaysi krit padmavat sahit kai pramukh granth isi boli kii dein he. iskaa kendra ayodhya he. ayodhya lakhanu se 120 kimi kii duuri par purv main he. sooryakanth treepathi 'niraala', pan mahavir prasad dvivedi, acharya ramachandra shukla, raamamanohar lohia, kunwar narayan kii yah janmabhumi he. umrao jaan, acharya narendra dev or ram prakaash dvivedi kii karmbhumi bhi yahi he. ramai kaaka kii lokwani bhi isi bhashaa main gunjarit hui. hindi ke reetikalin kavi dwijadev ke vanshaj ayodhya kaa rajpariwaar he. isi parivaar kii ek kanya kaa vivaah dakshin korea ke rajgaraane main arsa pehle hua tha. hindi ke varisht aalochak vishwanath treepathi ne avadhi bhashaa or vyakaran par mahatvapurn pustak likhi he. phahyan ne bhi apane vivaran main ayodhya kaa zikr kiya he. aaj kii avadhi pravaas or samskruthikaran ke chalate khadi boli or angreji ke prabhaav main aa rahi he.
 avadhi ke pashchim main pashchimi varg kii bundeli or braj kaa, dakshin main chhattisgadhi kaa or purv main bhojpuri boli kaa kshetra he. iske uttar main nepal kii tarai he jisamen tha degree aadi adivaasiyon kii bastiyaan hai jinaki bhashaa avadhi se bilkul alag he.
 hindi khadiboli se avadhi kii vibhinnata mukhya rup se vyakarnatmak he. isamen karta kaarak ke parasarg "ne" kaa nitant abhaav he. anya parasargon ke praaia: do rup milte hai- hrasv or deergh. .
 sangyaaon kii khadiboli kii tarah do vibhaktiyaan hoti hai- vikari or avikari. avikari vibhakti main sangya kaa mul rup rahata he or vikari main bahuvachan ke liye "na" pratyay jod diya jaataa he . karta or karm ke avikari rup main vyanjananth sangyaaon ke ant main kuch boliyon main ek hrasv "u" kii shruti hoti he . kintu nishchay hi yah purna swar nahin he or bhashavigyaani ise fusfusaahat ke swar-hrasv "i" or hrasv "e" milte hai.
 sangyaaon ke bahudhaa do rup, hrasv or deergh milte hai. inke atirikt avadhi kshetra ke purvi bhaag main ek or rup-dirghatar miltaa he . avadhi main kahin-kahin khadiboli kaa hrasv rup bilkul lupt ho gaya he; yatha billi, dibbi aadi rup nahin milte belia, dibia aadi hi prachlit hai.
 sarvnaam main khadiboli or braj ke "meraa tera" or "mero tero" rup ke liye avadhi main "mor tor" rup hai. inke atirikt purvi avadhi main pashchimi avadhi ke "so" "jo" "ko" ke samanaantar "se" "je" "ke" rup praapt hai.
 kriya main bhavishyakaal roopon kii prakriya khadiboli se bilkul bhinn he. khadiboli main praaia: pracheen vartmaan ke tadbhav roopon main- ga-gi-ge jodakar rup banaae jate hai. braj main bhavishyat ke rup pracheen bhavishyatkaal ke roopon par aadhaarit hai. . avadhi main praaia: bhavishyat ke rup tavyat pratyyant pracheen roopon par ashrith hai . avadhi kii pashchimi boliyon main keval uttampurush bahuvachan ke rup tavyatant roopon par nirbhar hai. shesh braj kii tarah pracheen bhavishyat par. kintu madhyavarti or purvi boliyon main kramash: tavyatant roopon kii prachurtaa badhati gai he. kriyaarthak sangya ke liye khadiboli main "naa" pratyay he or braj main "no" . parantu avadhi main iske liye "ba" pratyay he . avadhi main nishtha ekvachan ke rup kaa "vaa" main ant hota he . bhojpuri main iske sthaan par "l" main ant honevaale rup milte hai . avadhi kaa ek mukhya bhedak lakshan he anypurush ekvachan kii sakarmak kriya ke bhootkaal kaa rup . ya-"si" main ant honevaale rup avadhi ko chhodkar anyatra nahin milte. avadhi kii sahaayak kriya main rup "ha", "ah" or "baati" par aadhaarit hai.
 upar likhe lakshanon ke anusaar avadhi kii boliyon ke teen varg maane gaye hai : pashchimi, madhyavarti or purvi. pashchimi boli par nikatataa ke kaaran braj kaa or purvi par bhojpuri kaa prabhaav he. inke atirikt bagheli boli kaa apana alag astitv he.
 vikaas kii drushti se avadhi kaa sthaan braj, kannauji or bhojpuri ke bich main padataa he. braj kii vyutpatti nishchay hi shauraseni se tathaa bhojpuri kii magadhi praakrit se hui he. avadhi kii sthiti in donon ke bich main hone ke kaaran iskaa ardhamagadhi se nikalnaa maananaa uchit hoga. khed he ki ardhamagadhi kaa hamen jo pracheenatam rup miltaa he vah paanchavin shatabdi isvi kaa he or usase avadhi ke rup nikaalane main kathinaayee hoti he. pali bhashaa main bahudhaa aise rup milte hai jinase avadhi ke roopon kaa vikaas siddh kiya ja saktaa he. sambhavat: ye rup pracheen ardhamagadhi ke rahe honge.
 pracheen avadhi sahitya kii do shakhaayen hai : ek bhakthikavy or duusari premakhyaan kavy. bhakthikavy main goswami tulsidas kaa "ramcharitamanas" avadhi sahitya kii pramukh kriti he. isaki bhashaa sanskrit shabdaawalee se bhari he. "ramcharitamanas" ke atirikt tulsidas ne anya kai granth avadhi main likhe hai. isi bhakthi sahitya ke antargat laldaas kaa "avadhbilas" aata he. isaki rachana sanwat 1700 main hui. inke atirikt kai or bhakt kaviyon ne rambhakti vishyak granth likhe.
 sant kaviyon main baba malukadas bhi avadhi kshetra ke the. inaki bani kaa adhikansh avadhi main he. inke shishy baba mathuradas kii bani bhi adhiktar avadhi main he. baba dharanidas yadhyapi chhapra jile ke the tathaapi unki bani avadhi main prakashit hui. kai anya sant kaviyon ne bhi apane upadesh ke liye avadhi ko apanaaya he.
 premakhyaan kavy main sarvaprasiddh granth malik mohammad jaaysi rachit "padmavat" he jisaki rachana "ramcharitamanas" se 34 varsh purv hui. dohe chaupai kaa jo kram "padmavat" main he praaia: vahi "maanas" main miltaa he. premakhyaan kavy main musalmaan lekhakon ne sufi mat kaa rahasy prakat kiya he. is kavy kii parampara kai sau varshon tak chalati rahi. manzan kii "madhumalati", usmaan kii "chitrawali", alam kii "madhavanal kaamkandala", nurmuhammad kii "indravati" or shaikh nisaar kii "yusuf julekha" isi parampara kii rachanaayen hai. shabdaawalee kii drushti se ye rachanaayen hindu kaviyon ke granthon se is baat main bhinn hai ki isamen sanskrit ke tatsam shabdon kii utani prachurtaa nahin he.
 pracheen avadhi sahitya main adhiktar rachanaayen deshprem, samajasudhar aadi vishyon par or mukhya rup se vyangyatmak hai. kaviyon main pratapanarayan mishra, balbhadra dikshit "padhis", vanshidhar shukla, chandrabhushan dvivedi "ramai kaaka", guru prasad singh "mrigesh" or shardaprasad "bhushundi" vishesh ullekhneey hai.
 prabandh kii parampara main "ramcharitamanas" ke dhang kaa ek mahatvapurn aadhunik granth dwarikaprasad mishra kaa "krishnayan" he. isaki bhashaa or style "maanas" ke hi samaan he or granthkaar ne krishnacharit praaia: usee tanmayata or vistaar se likhaa he jis tanmayata or vistaar se tulsidas ne ramcharita ankit kiya he. mishra ji ne is granth kii rachana dwaara yah siddh kar diya he ki prabandh ke liye avadhi kii prakruti aaj bhi waisi hi upadey he jaisi tulsidas ke samay main thi.
 avadhi lok sahitya kii ek samrudh parampara he. avadhi lok sahitya par kai shodh hue hai. inamen kuch pramukh hai- avadhi lok sahitya -da sarojini rohtagi
