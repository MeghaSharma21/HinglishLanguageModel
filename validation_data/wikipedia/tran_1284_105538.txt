 nakki jhil mount abu kaa ek sundar paryaton sthal he. mithe pani kii yah jhil, jo rajasthan kii sabase unchi jhil hai sardiyon main aksar jam jaati he. kaha jaataa he ki ek hindu devtaa ne apane nakhunon se khodakar yah jhil banaai thi. isiilie ise nakki naam se janaa jaataa he. jhil se chaaron ore ke pahadiyon kaa drishy atyant sundar dikhataa he. is jhil main naukayan kaa bhi anand liya ja saktaa he. nakki jhil ke dakshin-pashchim main sthit suryaast bindu se duubate hue surya ke saundarya ko dekha ja saktaa he. yahan se dur tak faile hare bhare maidaanon ke drishy aankhon ko shanti pahunchaate hai. suryaast ke samay aasmaan ke badalte rangon kii chata dekhne saikadon paryatak yahan aate hai. prakritik saundarya kaa naiysargik anand denevaali yah jhil chaaron oly parvat shrunkhalaaon se ghiri he. yahan ke pahari tapu bade aakarshak hai. yahan karthik poornima ko log snaan kar dharm laabh uthaate hai. jhil main ek tapu ko 70 ashwashakti se chalit vibhinn rangon main jal fawwara lagaakar aakarshak banaaya gaya he jisaki dharaayen 80 foot kii oonchaai tak jaati hai. jhil main nauka vihaar kii bhi vyavastha he. jhil ke kinare ek sundar bagichaa he, jahaan shaam ke samay ghoomane or naukayan ke liye paryatakon kaa hujum umad padataa he. paas hi main bani dukaanon se rajasthani shilp kaa saamaan kharida ja saktaa he. yahan sangemarmar pathar se bani murtiyon or sooti kota saadiyaan kaaphi lokapriy he. yahan kii dukaanon se chaandi ke aabhushanon kii khariddaari bhi kii ja sakati he.
