 mohammad khaatami iran ke paanchaven raashtrapati the. unka 29 sitambar 1943 ko iran ke kendriya yajd praant main hua tha. ve sudhaarwadi netaa the jinhone 1997 ke chunaav ke liye mahilaon tathaa yuvakon ko or azaadi dene ke naam par vot liya tha. unka siddhant islamic ganatantr tha jisamen islamic vidhaanon ke tahat swatantraaa kii baat kahi gai thi.
