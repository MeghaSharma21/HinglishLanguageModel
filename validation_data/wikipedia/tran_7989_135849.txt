 paristhitik jal vigyaan ";ὕδωρ, hyder, "pani" or-λογία -lojia) pani or paristhitiki ke bich kii anyonyakriya kaa adhyayan karne vaala antarvishyak kshetra he. yah anyonyakriya nadiyon or jhiilon jaise jalpind ke bheetar yaa bhoomi, van, registan or anya sthaliy paaritantron main ghatit ho sakati he. vashpotsargan or sanyantr pani kaa upyog, jeevashm kaa apane jaleey paryaavaran se anukulan, dhara pravaah or pranali par vanaspati ke prabhaav tathaa paristhitik prakriyaaon or jaleey chakra ke bich pratikriya, ye paristhitik jal vigyaan ke anusandhan ke kshetra hai.
 jaleey chakra prithvi kii sataha ke upar or neeche jal kii satat gatividhi ko darshata he. anek binduon par yah pravaah paristhitiki pranaaliyon se badal jaataa he. vaataavaran ko adhiktar jal pravaah paudhon ke vashpotsargan se praapt hota he. vanaspati aavaran bhoomi kii sataha ke upar se behate jal ko prabhavit karata he jabaki nadi kii vahikaayen unke bheetar kii vanaspati se aakaar praapt karte hai. 
 paristhitik jal vaigyanik sthaliy or jaleey pranaaliyon donon kaa adhyayan karte hai. sthaliy paaritantron main, vanaspati kii anyonyakriya, bhoomi kii sataha, vados kshetra or bhoojal mukhya aakarshan bindu hai. jaleey paaritantron main is baat par bal diya jaataa he kis tarah jal rasayan, bhu-akruti vigyaan or jal vigyaan unki sanrachana or kaary ko prabhavit karte hai.
 paristhitik jal vigyaan ke siddhant teen anukramik ghatakon main vyakt kiye gaye hai:
 paristhitiki tantra sevaaon main vruddhi ho. 
 pareekshan yogya anumaan ke rup main unki abhivyakti ko dekha ja saktaa he:
 paristhitik jal vigyaan main ek moulik sankalpana yah he ki paudhe kaa sharir vigyaan sidhe pani kii uplabdhta se judaa hua he. jahaan paryaapt jal hota he jaise ki varshaavan main, vahaan paudhon kii vruddhi poshak tatwon kii uplabdhta par adhik nirbhar hoti he. haalanki, africa charaagaah jaise arddh-shushk kshetron main vanaspati kaa prakaar or vitaran sidhe hi pani kii us maatra se sambandhit hota he jo paudhe mitti se nikaal sakate hai. jab paryaapt mitti pani uplabdh nahin hota to jal-balit sthiti utpann hoti he. pani ke dabaav adheen paudhon main anek pratikriyaaon ke madhyam se vashpotsargan or prakaash sanshleshan donon kaa kshay hota he saath hi sarandhra bhi band hote hai. canopi jal pravaah or carbon dioxide ke pravaah main kami kaa aasapaas kii jalwaayu or mausam par prabhaav pad saktaa he. 
 aam taur par vados kshetra yaa zamin ke neeche mitti ke asantrupt bhaag main maujuud pani kii maatra kaa varnan karne ke liye mitti kii nami shabd kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. chunki mahatvapurn jaivik prakriyaaon ke liye paudhe is pani par nirbhar karte hai, mitti kii nami paristhitik jal vigyaan ke adhyayan kaa abhinn ang he. mitti kii nami ko aam taur par jaleey tatv,   θ  {\displaystyle \theta } yaa santrupti,   S  {\displaystyle S} ke rup main varnit kiya jaataa he. ye shabd   θ = n S  {\displaystyle \theta =nS} sameekaran ke madhyam se sanradhrata,   n  {\displaystyle n} se sambandhit hai. samay ke saath mitti kii nami main aae parivartan ko mitti kii nami kii gatiki ke rup main janaa jaataa he.
 
 paristhitik jal vigyaan sambandhi siddhant samayik or sthanik sambandhon ko bhi mahatv detaa he. samyaanusaar paaritantr ke vikaas main vishesh rup se varshan ghatnaon main, jal vigyaan ek mahatvapurn kaarak ho saktaa he. udhaaharan ke liye, bumadhya paridrishy main shushk garmi or barsaati sardiyaan hoti hai. agar vanaspati garmi ke mausam main utpann hoti he to aksar pani ke tanaav kaa anubhav hota he bhale hi saal bhar main kul varsha thik maatra main hui ho. in kshetron main paaritantr is prakaar viksit hue hai taaki sardiyon main jab jal sulabh hota he tab adhik pani kii maang karne vaale ghaas ko or garmiyon main jab jal kii kami hoti he tab sookhaa-anukulit pedo ko sahaara mili. 
 paristhitik jal vigyaan kaa sambandh paudhon ke sthanik vitaran ke peeche jaleey karkon se bhi he. paudhon main ishtatam antar or sthanik sanghathan kam se kam aanshik rup se jal kii uplabdhta se nirdhaarit hota he. acchhee tarah jal-palit kshetron kii tulana main mitti main alp nami vaale paaritantron main ped dur dur sthit hote hai.
 ek paridrishy main ek bindu par jal kaa santulan paristhitik jal vigyaan kaa moulik sameekaran he. jal santulan darshata he ki mitti main pravesh karne vaale pani kii maatra mitti se nikalne vaale evam mitti main sangrahit pani kii maatra main aae parivartan ke baraabar honi chahie. jal santulan ke mukhya chaar ghatak hai: varshan kaa mitti main pravesh, vaashpan-utsarjan, paudhon kii pahunch se pare mitti kii gaharaai main pani kaa risaav or zaminki sataha se pani kaa apvaah. nimna sameekaran dwaara iskaa varnan kiya gaya he:

 mul kshetra main nihit pani kii kul maatra sameekaran kii baayin ore ingit he. vanaspati ke liye sulabh is pani kii maatra mitti kii sanradhrata   n  {\displaystyle n} isaki santrupti se gunan or paudhe ke mul kii gaharaai ke samaan he. vibhedak sameekaran   d s  / d t  {\displaystyle ds/dt} se pataa chalata he ki kis prakaar samay biit jane ke baad mitti kii santrupti badalati he. daayin ore varsha kii dar, avarodhan, apvaah, vaashpan-utsarjan or risaav die gaye hai. ye prati din millimetre main die gaye hai . apvaah, vashpikaran or risaav sabhi ek nishchit samay par mitti kii santrupti par atyaadhik nirbhar karte hai.
 sameekaran ko hal karne ke liye, mitti kii nami ke kaary ke rup main vaashpan-utsarjan kii dar gyaat honi chahie. aam taur par ise varnan karne vaale namoonay ke anusaar ek nishchit santrupti ke baad vashpikaran uplabdh dhoop jaise jalwaayu ke karkon par hi nirbhar hoga. is bindu ke neeche pahunchane par mitti kii nami vaashpan-utsarjan ko niyantrit karti he or yah kam hone lagati he jab tak ki mitti us bindu tak na pahunch jaae jahaan vanaspati ke liye or pani nikaalnaa sukar na ho. mitti ke is str ko aam taur par "sthaayee shithilan bindu" kaha jaataa he. yah shabd bhraamak he kyonki kai padap prajatiyaan vastav main "shithil" nahin hoti hai.
