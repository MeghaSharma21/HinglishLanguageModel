 goekaturk madhya asia or uttarpurvi asia main ek madhyakaalin kanaabadosh qabeelon kaa parisangh tha jinhone 552 isvi se 744 isvi tak apana goekaturk kagaanath naam kaa samrajya chalaaya. inhonen yahan par apane se pehle sattadhari ju-jaan kagaanath ko hataakar resham maarg par chal rahe vyapaar ko apane niyantran main kar liya. 'goek' kaa arth turki bhashaa main 'akaash' hota he or 'goekaturk' kaa matlab 'aasamaani turk' he. yah itihaas kaa pehla samrajya tha jisane apane aap ko 'turk' bulaayaa. is se pehle 'turk' yaa 'turuk' nipun lohaar maane jate the lekin raja-maharaja nahin.
 goekaturk samrajya kii sthapana boomin kagaan ne kii thi or iske adheen madhya asia ke stapi kshetra kaa bahut se qabeele sangathit ho gaye. lekin chauthe kagaan ke baad yah samrajya do hisson - purvi kagaanath or pashchimi kagaanath - main bant gaya. or aage chalakar kuch qabeelon ne iske goekturkon ke khilaaf vidroh kara or unka raaj khatm hone ke saath-saath 744 isvi main uigur kagaanath sab se shaktishaalee turk samrajya ke rup main ubhari.
