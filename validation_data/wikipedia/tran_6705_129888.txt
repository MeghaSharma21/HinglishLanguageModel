 islamic dharamshastra main imaan islamic dharamshastra ke aadhyatmik pahaluon main ek aastik vishwaas ko darshata he. isaki sabase saral paribhaasha vishwaas ke chah lekhon main vishwaas he, jise "arkan al-imaan" ke naam se janaa jaataa he. iskaa arth islam ke chah vishwaas ke vishyon main aasthaa rakhana he.
 imaan shabd quran or haddees-e-jibril donon main chitrit kiya gaya he. quran ke mutabik, imaan ko dharmik karmon ke saath hona chahie or swarg main pravesh ke liye donon ek saath jaruri hai. jibril kii haddees main, islamic dharm ke ayaamon kaa nirmaan yah teen karte hai "islam" "ihasaan" or "imaan".
 islam ke bheetar or baahar, dharm main vishwaas or hetu kii charchaa hoti he, or donon ke saapeksh mahatv ko bhulaayaa nahin jaasakata. kai vidwaanon kaa tark he ki ek hi strot or jagah se vishwaas or hetu donon utpann hote hai isliye donon ko saamanjasypurna hona chahie. 
 arabi main, imaan, uccharan kaa arth he " vishwaas ". yah kriya sangya he, آمن, matlab kisi chiz main vishvas rakhana. 
 haddees main mohammad sahib ne "imaan kaa matlab - dil main gyaan, jeebh par awaz, or haath pair main gatividhi" ke rup main paribhaashit kiya. vishwaas ek bharosa he sachai par jo vaastavik he. jab logon ko vishwaas hota he, to ve khud ko us satya se jod dete hai. sachai jaananaa hi kaaphi nahin he, dil use maananaa bhi chahie, jeebh dwaara vyakt kii jaani chahie jo buddhi kii abhivyakti he or ant main unke aatmavishvaas ko pratibimbit karne ke liye us kaa amal karnaa bhi zaruri he. 
 hamiduddin faraahi, apane tafseer main imaan ke arth kii vyakhya karte hue, likhaa he:
 vishwaas aam taur par vishwaas ke chah lekhon kaa upyog karke ullikhit he:
 inamen se pehle quran or mohammad dwaara pehle paanch kaa ullekh kiya gaya he, jabaki allaah main vishwaas kii ek siddhant shaamil he - bhagwaan dwaara aadeshit bhagya ke achche or bure - ne sabhi chah ko nimnlikhit tareeke se sanderbhit kiya he gabrial ke haddees main:
 mohammad se bhi ek or samaan varnan he:
 " ebn abbaas ne varnan kiya ki malak jibril ne ek baar paigambar se poocha: "mujhe bataao imaan kya he?" paigambar ne uttar diya: "imaan he vishvas karnaa allaah par, nyaay ke din par, uske malaika par, kitaaben or paigambaron, or mrityu ke baad jivan main vishwaas karnaa he, or swarg or narak main vishwaas karnaa or mizaan kii sthapana par jo hamaare karm ko tolane ke liye he, or taqadir main vishwaas karnaa, iske achche or bure par. jibril ne phir un se poocha: "yadi main yah sab karata hum to main imaan ke saath rahoonga?" paigambar ne kaha: " jab aapane yah sab kiya he, to aap imaan main honge. " 
 yah bhi mana jaataa he ki aavashyak imaan main pehle 3 chize hoti hai . 
 quran main, 10 gunon main se ek gun imaan he jo kisi ko allaah kii dayaa or inaam praptakarta banane ke kaaran miltaa he. quran kahata he ki vishwaas allaah ke samaran ke saath bad saktaa he. quran yah bhi kahata he ki is duniya main momin ke liye ati priy chiz vishwaas ke ilaava kuch bhi nahin hona chahie. 
 mohammad ne kaha he ki; usne vishwaas kii mithaas praapt kii jo allaah ko ishwar islam ko dharm or mohammad ko paigambar ke rup main sweekaar kiya. unhone yah bhi kaha ki jab tak vah apane bacchon, mata-pita or rishtedaaron se zyada paigambar se pyaar nahin karata tab tak koi bhi sachcha aastik nahin ho saktaa. ek or udhaaharan main, unhone tippanee kii he ki yah allaah or mohammad ke saath prem karne ke baad hi ek vyakti vishwaas ke asli swaad se avgat ho saktaa he. 
 quran ke ek mufassiron amin ahsaan islahi ne is prem kii prakruti arth ko spasht kiya he: 
 ..prem keval kisi kii patni, bacchon or anya rishtedaaron ke liye swaabhaavik rup se bhavuk pyaar kaa sanket nahin detaa he, balki yah kuch drushtikon or buddhi or siddhanton ke aadhaar par prem ko bhi sanderbhit karata he. is prem ke kaaran hi ek vyakti, jivan ke har kshetra main, is drushtikon or siddhant ko praathamikta detaa he. agar vyakti apanai patni, bacchon or rishtedaaron kii maang is drushtikon kii maangon se juujhati he, to vah bina kisi hichkhichahat ke is kaa paalan karata he, isi imaani prem ke kaaran uski patni or bacchon kii ichchhaaon or unke parivaar or kabeeley kii maangon ko kam kar detaa he.
 islahi or maududi donon ne anumaan lagaaya he ki adhyay 14 main ek achche shabd or bure shabd kii quran kii tulana vastav main vishwaas or avishvaas kii tulana he. is prakaar, quran prabhavi rup se ek ped par vishwaas kii tulana kar rahaa he jisaki jaden mitti main gahari hai or akaash kii vishalata main shakhaaen faili hui hai. 
 imaan mohammad dwaara allaah se kii gai ek prarthana kaa vishay bhi he:
 he allaah! maine apane aap ko thukraayaa he or khud ko aap ke hawaale saunp diya he, or main ne aapaki badhaai pahchaan kar pehle aapke sharan main aagaya hoon, aap se bhaag kar kahin bhi sharan milane vaala nahin he. agar kahin hai to aapke saath he. prabhu! maine aapaki pustak main vishwaas kiya he jise aapane prakat kiya he or paigambar main vishwaas kiya he jise aapane paigambaron ke rup main bheja he. 
 imam al-behaqi apni kitaab shub al-imaan main sankalit kiya he ki vishwaas kii 77 shakhaaen he. isamen, vah imaan ke aavashyak gunon ko samajhaate huve kurani aaayaaton or nabi kii hadison kaa ullekh kiya he, jo vishvas ko darshaate hai. 
 nimnlikhit haddees mohammad sahib par aadhaarit he:
 abu huraira ne suna he ki paigambar ne kaha tha: "imaan main 70 se adhik shakhaaen hai. in shakhaon main sabase utkrisht" laa elaha illallaah " padhanaa he, or sabase choti shakha raste main padhi baadha ko dur karnaa he. or haya imaan kii ek mahatvapurn shakha he." 
 islam main, yah aavashyak he ki vishwaas or karmon ke bich sadbhaav or samanvay maujuud ho. faraahi ne is pahalu ko apane tafaseer main nimnlikhit tareeke se samajhaayaa he: 
 vishwaas ke baad quran main dharmik karmon kaa ullekh kiya gaya he ... vishwaas ke maamle main, isaki vyakhya kii aavashyaktaa spasht he: vishwaas kii jagah dil or buddhi he. buddhi or dil ke maamalon main, na keval ek vyakti dusron ko dhokhaa de saktaa he balki kabhi-kabhi vah khud dhokhe main rah saktaa he. vah khud ko ek mumin maanata he jabaki vastav main vah nahin he. is kaaran se, iske liye do sakshya kii aavashyaktaa hoti he: ek vyakti ke shabd or vyakti ke karm. chunki shabd asaty ho sakate hai, isliye ek vyakti jo keval shabdon ke madhyam se vishwaas kaa daawa karata he use muman ke rup main nahin mana jaataa he or ise aavashyak samajha jaataa tha ki ek vyakti ke karm bhi uske vishwaas kii gavaahi den. is prakaar quran ne kaha : he aap jeebh se vishwaas karte hai! apane karmon ke madhyam se vishwaas karo. 
 islam main tark or vishwaas ke bich sambandh sadiyon se faili ek jatil bahes he. ismail raji al-faaruqi is vishay par bataate hai:
 gaiyr-musalmaan islam ke siddhanton kaa virodh kar sakate hai. haalanki, unhen pataa hona chahie ki islam apane siddhanton ko vishesh rup se vishpasht rup se nahin pesh karata he, jo vishwaas karte hai yaa vishwaas karnaa chaahate hai vah kar sakate hai. islam is ko tarksangat rup se, tarksangat rup se pesh karata karata he. yah hamaare paas taarkik or susangat tarkon ke saath aavashyak aadhaaron ke saath pesh karata he. vyaktigat swaad, yaa vyaktiparak anubhav ke saapeksh aadhaar par asahmat hona hamaare liye vaid nahin he.
