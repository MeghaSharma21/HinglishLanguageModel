 alladin ek madhya purvi pari katha he. yah da book of van thoznd and van nit kii kai kathaon main se ek he va sabase adhik vikhyath he haalaanki ise sangrah main 18ve shatabdi main antony galand naamak ek francisi ne samavisht kiya tha.
 alladin chini shahar main rahane vaala ek aam garib ladkaa he. ek din magareb se aayaa ek jaadugar swayan ko uske gujarey hue pita mustafaa darzi kaa bhai bataa kar apan saath niyukt kar letaa he jis kaaran uski maan ko yah prathit hota he kii vah aage chalakar ek bahut amir vyapaaree banegaa. parantu jaadugar kaa asli makasad alladin ko bahala-phusalaakar usase jaadui chiraag hasil karnaa he jo ek jaadui gupha main maujuud he. jaadugar alladin ko dhokaa dene kii koshish karata he jisase alladin gupha main funs jaataa he. acchhee kismat ke chalate uske paas us samay ek jaadui anguthi hoti he jo use jaadugar ne suraksha k liye di thi. jab hataash hokar alladin apane haath ghisata he tab anjaane main usase anguthi bhi ghis jaati he or ek jinn prakat hota he jo use apni maan ke paas ghar pahunchaa detaa he. jab alladin kii maan bete ne laayaa hua chiraag saaf karne kii koshish karti he tab ek doosraa shaktishaalee jinn prakat hota he jo us chiraag ke maalik kaa gulam hota he.
 chiraag se nikle jinn kii sahaayta se alladin behad aamir va shaktishaalee ban jaataa he or rajkumaari badr-ul-budur se nikaah kar letaa he. hindi main badr-ul-budur kaa arth he, punam kii chaandon kii punam. 
 jinn alladin ke liye ek mahal kaa nirmaan karata he jo shahenshaah ke mahal se bhi kai guna zyaada bhavy hota he.
 ek din jaadugar puraane chiraagon ke badale nae chiraag dene ke bahaane alladin kii bivi se jaadui chiraag hasil kar letaa he. vah jin ko hukum detaa he ki mahal ko uske saare sajo saaman sahat apane ghar magareb pahunchaa de. kismat se alladin ke paas tab bhi jaadui anguthi hoti he jisase vah chote jinn ko bula letaa he. haalaanki chota jinn chiraag ke jinn dwaara kiya gaya jaadu ulat nahin saktaa par vah alladin ko magareb pahunchaa detaa he jahaan alladin ladaai main jaadugar ko maar kar chiraag ko punah hasil kar letaa he va mahal or rajkumaari ko punah apni jagah le aata he.
 jaadugar kaa shaktishaalee chota bhai alladin ko maar kar apane bhai kaa badla lene kii koshish karata he or ek boodhi aurat kaa vesh dhar letaa he jo apni rahasyamai jaadui shaktiyon ke liye vikhyath he. badr-ul-budur uske is jhaanse main funs jaati he ayyaar budhiya ko mahal main rahane kii anumati de detii he. chiraag kaa jinn alladin ko is bare main aagaah kar detaa he or alladin baharuupiye ko maar detaa he. sabhi log aage chalakar ek khushhaal zindagi jite he or bhavishya main alladin shahenshaah ban jaataa he.
 sabhi roopantaran asli kahani se kahin na kahin mail khate he. chini ghatnaon ko adhikansh samay arabian prishthbhumi main badal diya jaataa he
