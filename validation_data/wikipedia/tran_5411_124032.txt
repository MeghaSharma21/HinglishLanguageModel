 baghdad vishvavidhyalay iraq kii rajdhani baghdad main sthit vishvavidhyalay kaa iraq main ek mahatvapurn vishvavidhyalay he.
 college of islamic sciensej kaa daawa he ki yah 1067 isvi main abu-hanipha ke rup main shuru hua tha. haalanki, college of lau, aadhunik sansthaanon ke sabase pehle jo baghdad vishvavidhyalay ke pehle ghatak collejon banane ke liye the, kii sthapana 1908 main hui. 1921 main college of ejineering kii sthapana kii gai; ucch shikshak prashikshan college or 1923 main loar college of ajukeshan, 1927 main medicine kaa college, or 1936 main farmesi kalege tha. 1942 main, ladakiyon ke liye pehali ucch sanstha queen aalia college kii sthapana hui thi. 1943 main, aage ke nae collejon ke prastaav pesh hue, jisase college of arts or college of scienson 1949 main sthapana hui, or 1950 main abu ghareeb college of agriculture sthapana kii gai.
 1922 main, raja ne bab al-muajam main ek vishvavidhyalay ke sanghathan ke liye ek yojana shuru kii thi, lekin pravesh ke liye aparyaapt chaatr yogya the. baharhaal, thiological college ke nirmaan ke saath vishvavidhyalay kii rachana par ek shuruyaat kii gai thi. janvari 1925 main, haalanki, ejineering skool ko thiological college bhawan ke khaali upree manzil main sthanantarit kar diya gaya tha. 1935 main, monro kamishan ne tark diya tha ki iraq ek vishvavidhyalay ke liye taiyaar nahin tha, or 1945 tak ek vishvavidhyalay sthapit karne kaa aglaa prayaas shuru nahin hua. tab 1947 main ek varisht sadsy ne 'morgan report' iraq sarkaar ke liye taiyaar kiya tha british council ke karmchaaree 1948 main, haalanki, british council ke prastaavon ko mantralay dwaara taiyaar kii gai ek yojana ke paksh main khaarij kar diya gaya tha, lekin koi karrwai nahin hui. may 1953 main, british council ne british vishvavidhyalay ke prophessoron ke ek samooh dwaara baghdad kaa ek or daura ek baar phir se ek vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana ke liye protsaahit karne ke liye pryojit kiya.
