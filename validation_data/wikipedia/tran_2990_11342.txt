 nyaayalayik vigyaan vah vaigyanik anushaasan he jisame bhautik sakshya kii jaanch, manyata, nishchayeekaran or moolyankan kiya jaataa he. yah saamaanya vigyaan ke niyam evam sidhaant ko kanoon ke prayog ke upyukt banane kaa vigyaan he. aparaadh anusandhan ke kshetra main iskaa bahut mahatv he.
 nyaayalayik vigyaan ke niyam evam sidhaant nimna he:-
 sampark main aane par do vastuo main aadan-pradaan hota he! iske anusaar jab bhi koi apradhi kisi bhi vastu, kisi peedit manushya yaa aas paas ke sampark main aayega to sadev apane chinh chhodke jayega or aas paas ke sthal se kuch chinh le jaataa he! in chinhon kii jaanch or pahchaan karke sandigdh apradhi kaa pataa lagaaya ja saktaa he! yah sidhaant sabase pehle france ke vaigyanik sar admond locard ne diya tha tabhi is sidhaant ko "locard principle of exange ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he|
 yah niyam yah darshata he kii samay ke saath-saath har vastu main parivartan hote he. yah niyam nyaayalayik vigyaan main bahut mahatv rakhata he kyunki apradhi ho yaa aparaadh sthal yaa phir aas paas kii vastue, samay ke saath shigrha hi parivartit ho jaati he|
 "keval ek jaisi vastuo kii tulana kii ja sakati he",arthath kisi bhi vastu kii kisi or vastu se tulana kerani ho to ek jaisaa dikhne vaala arthath dekhne par ek saamaan lagane vaali vastu se hi kii jaaegi.
 is sidhaant kaa matlab yah he kii pareekshan or jaanch ke liye bheja gaya namoona jitna acha hota he, utane hi achche uske parinaam aate he! isliye ghatana sthal par paae gaye bhutik saakshyo kaa achche se sangrahan or paking kii jani chahie taaki vah jaanch or parikshan hone tak karaab na hoe|
 prayek prakaar kii pahchaan, nishchit v anishchit, hameshaa sambhaavna ke siddhant dwaara hi kii jaati he|
 is siddhant ke anusaar paristhtijanak sakshya kisi kii bhi maukhik gavaahi se adhik mahatvapurn hote he. paristhtijanak saksho ko jhuthlaayaa nahin ja saktaa he. yah siddhant is chintan par aadhaarit he ki vyakti jhooth bol saktaa he kintu tathy nahin.
