 
 kuldeep pavaar bhartiya cinema ke marathi bhashaa ke abhinayta the. wo mul rup se maharashtra ke kolhapur ke rahane vaale the. unhen unke hasya vinod or kuch nakaaratmak kirdaaron ke liye janaa jaataa he. unhone gupachup-gupachup, shaapit, are sansar sansar or vajeer jaisi kai prasiddh marathi filmon main abhinay kiya. televijan kii duniya main unako dharawahik paramveer se prasiddhi mili. 24 march 2014 ko gurde kii vifaltaa ke kaaran mumbai ke koclaben ambani astapatal main nidhan ho gaya. unke nidhan ke samay unke parivaar main unki patni nilima or do bachche hai.
