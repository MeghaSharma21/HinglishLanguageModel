 ballab rasik gaudeey sampradaay ke bhakt kavi hai.gadadhar bhatta ke vanshajon ke anusaar vallabh rasik,gadadhar bhatta ke dwitiya putr the. inke bade bhai kaa naam rasikottans tha. bhatta ji apane donon putron ko swayan shiksha or diksha di. is aadhaar par vallabh rasik kaa kavitaakaal vi 0 san 0 1650 ke aas paas sweekaar kiya ja saktaa he. kyonki gadadhar bhatta kaa samay vi 0 san 0 1600 se kuch purv kaa anumaanit kiya gaya he. bhatta ji ke putr hone ke kaaran ye bhi dakshinatya brahman hai. kintu in sabhi tathyon ~~ samay,vansh sampradaay aadi ke vishay main vallabh rasik ne swayan kuch nahin likhaa. apni vaani main to mahaprabhu chaitanya athwa shatgoswamiyon kii unhone vandana bhi nahin kii.
 vallabh rasik ke vichaar main sansar ke sabhi naate jhuthe hai. atah in sambandhon to todakar hamen radha-krishna yugalvar se hi apana sambandh chhodana chahie. ye upasya-yugal ras ke sagar hai isliye inase naata ho jane par bhakt bhi sadaa ras-sindhu main magn hokar anand praapt karte hai. unki rup maadhuri apurv he jise dekhakar upaask ke netra kabhi trupt nahin hote. isiilie unhone kaha he ~~ 
 vallabh rasik ne prem kshetra main radha ko bhi ucch sthaan diya he. 
 radha-krishna kii vividh madhur-lilaon kaa dhyaan karnaa hi vallabh rasik kii upasna he radha-krishna kaa yah prem janit he. atah isamen aadi ant nahin he. vah nijee sukh kaamana se rahit he. isiilie use ujjwal kaha gaya he:
 vallabh rasik ne radha-krishna kii sanyog or viyog suchak donon prakaar kii lilaon kaa varnan apni vaani main kiya he. inamen viyog kii apeksha sanyog parak lila ke padon,savaiyon kii sankhya adhik he. sanyog kii lilaayen sabhi prakaar kii hai ~~ jhulan, raas,hori, dhyut-krida, rathyatra ,jal-krida ,surat,vihaar aadi. in sabhi kaa varnan kavi ne bahut sundar dang se kiya he. jhulan ke is varnan main bhakt kii bhavukta ,alankaar tathaa lakshanik prayog ke kaaran kavi kii kavy-pratibha lakshith hoti he :
