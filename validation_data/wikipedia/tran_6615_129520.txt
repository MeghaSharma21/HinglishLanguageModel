 wintage car ko aam taur par ek aisi car ke rup main paribhaashit kiya jaataa he jiskaa nirmaan 1919 ke armbh se 1930 ke ant ke bich kiya gaya he; is avadhi ko "wintage yug" ke naam se janaa jaataa he. wintage yug kii armbh tithi ko lekar koi vivaad nahin he - pratham vishwa yuddh kaa ant ko isaki shuruyaat ke rup main mana jaataa he - lekin antim tithi ko lekar vivaad thodaa zyada he. british paribhaasha ke anusaar isaki antim tithi 1930 he or ise vyapak rup se sweekaar kiya jaataa he, jabaki kuch america strot isaki antim tithi 1925 maanate hai, kyonki clasik car club of america kii paribhaasha ke anusaar yah clasik car se pehle kii avadhi he. anya log clasik avadhi ko wintage avadhi ke overlap ke rup main dekhte hai, jiskaa mukhya kaaran yah he ki wintage naam main is avadhi main nirmit sabhi vaahan shaamil hai, jabaki aadhikarik clasik paribhaasha main aisa nahin he, jisamen keval us avadhi ke hai-and vaahan hi shaamil hai. kuch log dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke armbh ko wintage avadhi kii antim tithi maanate hai.
 motor vaahan kii duniya main wintage yug ek parivartan kaal tha. car kii shuruyaat 1919 main ek durlabh vastu ke rup main hui or 1930 main apane ant tak pahonchte-pahonchte yah kaaphi aam hone lagi thi. vastav main is avadhi ke ant tak hue automobile utpaadan ko 1950 ke dashak tak punah hasil nahin kiya ja sakaa tha. bich ke varshon main zyaadaatar audyogic rajyon ne rashtravyapee sadak pranaaliyon kaa nirmaan kiya, jiske parinamswaroop is avadhi ke antim daur main kachi sadakon par vaahan chalaane kii kshamta, automotive dizin ke liye sabase badi baadha ke rup main nahin rah gai thi.
 is avadhi ke dauraan kaaren adhik vyavhaarik, suvidhajanak or aaramdaayak ho gayi. car ke bheetar radio ke saath-saath car heating ko bhi pesh kiya gaya. ek aam foot pedal se chaar-pahie vaali braking ke saath-saath hydralikali ecchuated brikon ke istemaal ko bhi shuru kiya. power stearing bhi isi yug kii dein thi. wintage yug ke antim daur main eendhan kii octen reating kii pranali kii shuruyaat hui, jisase eendhanon main tulana karne main madad mili. 1923 main indi 500 main gasoline editive ithyle kaa pehali baar istemaal hua, jiske parinamswarup 50 se 80 ke dashak tak octen main teji ai. sanyukt rajya america main drive-in restaraan ke saath-saath upnagareey shopping centaron or motalon kii shuruyaat hui.
 general motors ke alfred pi. slown or harley arl or walter pi. chrisler ne ghode se tulana karte hue upabhokta ke jivan main automobile kii bhoomika ko keval upyogvaadi mulya se adhik jataanevaale vigyaapan ko poonjikrut kiya. 1929 main share bazaar main aane vaali giravat ke kaaran automotive karmchaariyon kii chantni aarambh kii gayi or kai nai companiyaan diwalia ho gayi, lekin phir bhi 1929 or 1930 main do million se zyada kaaron kaa nirmaan hua tha. "acchhee sadakon ke janak" ke naam se jane jane vaale horashio arle ne sarkaar ko 1902 main ek antarraji rajmarg pranali kaa nirmaan karne kaa prastaav diya tha or 1909 main datroit main woodward avenue par duniya kii pehali ek mile lambii kancreet sadak kaa nirmaan kiya.
 1916 kaa sangheey sahaayta sadak adhinium pehla sangheey rajmarg adhinium tha. yuddh or dhan kii kami kii vajah se is adhinium ke sakaaratmak parinaam main baadha utpann ho gai. 1921 ke sangheey sahaayta rajmarg adhinium ne sadakon ke nirmaan ke liye rajyon ko ek 50/50 milan kosh kii shuruyaat kii or iske parinamswaroop nai or behatar sadakon kaa nirmaan kiya gaya. is avadhi ke saath-saath kaaron ko samaaj ke anukool banane ke dauraan, behatar sadakon kaa nirmaan kiya gaya or samaaj ne bhi kaaron ko sweekaar karnaa shuru kar diya. dwite di. isenhavar ne 1919 main atiprachaarit transcontinental motor convoy main bhaag liya or adhyaksh banane ke baad unke anubhav ne 1956 ke sangheey sahaayta rajmarg adhinium ko prerit kiya, jisamen 41,000 mile lanbaa rajmarg shaamil tha.
 pratham vishwa yuddh ke ant main 20 pratishat mudrasphiti dar, 7 pratishat fed chhot rin dar or 11.7 pratishat berogari dar ke saath "1920-21 kii mandi" dekhi gai or kai companiyaan diwalia ho gayi or motor vaahan udyog ko baari nuksaan uthaanaa padaa.
 1920 se 1929 tak kai naatakeey parivartan hue. general motors ko vittiya sankat ne gher liya or 1923 main alfred slown ke adhyaksh banane tak yahi haal rahaa. hadsan ne 1919 main asex kaa nirmaan kiya jisane 1925 tak kul bikri kii drushti se company ko ford or shevarlyt ke baad tisare sthaan par pahunchaa diya. ford ek nae plant kaa nirmaan karne or share ko waapas kharidne kii prakriya main laga tha or 1927 main usne model e ke saath model tii ko pratisthapit karne ke liye 18 maheenon kii ek touling-ap prakriya shuru kii. 1921 main maxwell vifal ho gaya or general motors ke walter pi. chrisler ko ise phir se sangathit karne kaa kaam saunpa gaya or 1925 main chrisler korporation kaa gathan kiya gaya. kuch samay ke liye ford ke baahar hone kii vajah se chrisler ko 1927 main kam keemat vaale plimouth kaa nirmaan or vipanan karne main madad mili or 1928 main doj bros kaa aagaman hua, jiske parinamswaroop "da big thri" kaa nirmaan hua, jise vartmaan main "datroit thri" ke naam se janaa jaataa he. is samay ke dauraan britain ke paas teen ke bajaay chah pramukh nirmaataa the: morris, austin, standard, roots, ford of britain or woxol. kuch anya auto nirmaataa bhi the jo 1920-1921 kii mandi ko to jhel gaye lekin mahamandi ke dauraan vifal ho gaye. chote automobile nirmaataaon main franklin kaa naam ullekhneey tha jisane 1919 se 1930 tak wintage yug ke dauraan ucch gunvattta vaali lakjri kaaron kaa nirmaan kiya.
 antic automobile or armbh se madhya yug kii clasik kaaron main ve suraksha suvidhaayen uplabdh nahin hai jo aadhunik kaaron main saamaanya rup se pai jaati hai. seat belt jaisi sabase buniyadi suraksha suvidhaayen saath ke dashak se dikhaai deni shuru huin or 1968 main sangheey kaanoon dwaara anivaary bana di gayin. kaanoon dwaara anivaary ghoshit nai suraksha suvidhaayen kanoon ke prabhavi hone ke baad nirmit vaahanon par lagu hoti hai, isliye kisi bhi nae kaanoon ke banane ke saath uske pehle ke vaahanon ko purana ghoshit kar diya jaataa he. clasik car club of america ke illinois chapter 'des plance' ke adhyaksh chak conrod ne bataaya, "70 saal puraani lakdi ke tukade se khud ko baandhna kisi samasya kaa samaadhaan nahin he" .
 ausat logon ke liye car kaa sangrahan ek shauk he. ek vyakti main aam taur par kisi khaas vaahan yaa aise vaahan ke itihaas ke prati aakarshan hota he isliye vah is tarah ke kisi khaas vaahan yaa model ko paanaa chahta he. kifayati daam par koi antic car dhoondhna koi mushkil kaam nahin he, lekin halaat yaa vaanchit antim parinaam ke aadhaar par apekshaakrit mahangaa sabit ho saktaa he. kisi vaahan par kam kaam kii jarurat ucch keemat ke samaan he. zyada kaam kii jarurat kaa matlab sasti aarambhik laagat he, lekin dirghakaal main or zyada laagat or vyakti kaa marammat sambandhi anubhav ek mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaataa he.
 kamedian or car sangrah main dilchaspee rakhane vaale je leno ne bataaya, "agar aap sangrah karen to koi bhi car ek sangraahak car ho sakati he." ek nivesh ke rup main antic car kaa sangrahan phydemand sabit ho saktaa he, lekin zyaadaatar gambhir nivesh sangraahak durlabh yaa aakarshak kaaron kaa sangrah karnaa pasand karte hai or mul asanshodhit kaaron kii keemat adhik sthir hoti he. ek nivesh ke rup main sangrah karne ke liye utsaahi sangrahan se pare visheshgyata kii jarurat padati he or gunvattta kaa maanak kaaphi ooncha hone ke saath-saath iske liye nivesh sangrakshan jaise bhandaaran or rakrakhaav kii jarurat padati he. alpakaaleen nivesh sangraahak ko bazaar mulya vaale aise vaahan kaa pataa lagane main sakasham hona chahie jiske mulya main nikat bhavishya main vruddhi hone kii ummid ho. dirghakaalin nivesh sangraahak ko alpakaaleen mulya vaale kisi vaahan main bahut kam ruchi hogi jo kuch saalon main pratyaashit mulya vruddhi par poonji lagaana chahta he or isliye vaahan main kuch aise anternihit mulyon kaa hona jaruri he jo alpakaaleen or dirghakaalin donon tarah ke anya niveshkon yaa sangraahakon ke liye aam ho.
 durlabhta kii drushti se antic vaahanon kaa mulya kaaphi adhik hota he, jo aam taur par utpaadan yaa nirmaan ke samay inaki sankhya kii kami ke liye kuch kaarnon kaa parinaam tha. kuch vishisht varshon or model kii kaaron kii ati lokapriyata kii vajah se unka bazaar kaaphi garma gaya jiske kaaran unki mul avasthaa kaa naash ho gaya. anya modelon kaa utpaadan itani adhik sankhya main hua tha ki unki keemat main abhi tak koi vruddhi nahin hui he. bazaar kaa chalan wintage car kii keemat kaa ek mahatvapurn hissaa he. bahutayat maatra main nirmit or "lagbhag" mul swaroop or ekadam thik-thaak avasthaa vaali model e ko $20,000.00 main karidaa ja saktaa he. ek niveshak ke rup main ek sangraahak ko sambhavit bazaar kii malumat hoti he or use yah vishwaas hota he ki bhavi bazaar se nivesh kaa pratifal praapt hoga. kai sangraahakon kii pravriti bhi kuch aise vaahanon kii taraf hoti he yaa ve kuch aise vaahanon ko pasand karte hai jo unki visheshgyata ko darshata ho.
 sangraahakon or niveshkon ke liye vaahan kii haalat yaa dasha kii reating bahut myne rakhti he or inke liye do pranaaliyon - shrenee or ank pranali kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he. shrenee pranali main chah shreniyan hoti hai jinka istemaal vaahan kii haalat ko darja dene ke liye kiya jaataa he. ank pranaaliyon ke tahat 40 se 100 tak ank pradaan kiya jaataa he jo shrenee pranali ke samaan hote hai or 40 se neeche anya haalat yaa dashaon kii teen shreniyan hoti hai. tulana karne ke kaary ko aasaan banane ke liye donon pranaaliyon ko ek saath soochibaddh kiya gaya he:
