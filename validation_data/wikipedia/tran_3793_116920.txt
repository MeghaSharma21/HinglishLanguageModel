 keet vigyaan  praani vigyaan kii ek shakha he jisame keedo kaa vaigyanik adhyayan kiya jaataa he. ateet main, shabd "keet" adhik aspasht tha or aitihaasik rup se keetvigyaan kii paribhaasha main aarthropod, philaa or sthaliy jeevon kaa adhyayan jaise ki arachaids, myripod, gandav, bhoomi ghonghe or slog bhi isamen shaamil the. jantu vigyaan ke antargat vargeekrut anya kshetron kii tarah, keet vigyaan ek texon-aadhaarit shrenee he; vaigyanik adhyayan ke kisi bhi rup main ,jisamen keet se sambandhit visheshtaon par dhyaan kendrit kiya jaataa he, paribhaasha ke anusaar, keet vigyaan - antomology he. isliye aanvik anuvanshiki, vyavhaar, biomacenix, biochemestry, systmatics, physiology, deveopment biology, paristhitiki, ichology or peliantology ke rup main vibhinn vishyon kaa mishran hai.1.3 million se zyada jeev prajatiyon main do-tihaai se adhik to keet prajati ke praani hi he or prithvi par manushyon or jivan ke anya roopon ke saath 400 million varsh pehle se sampark hai.
 keetvigyaan pragaitihaasik kaal se lagbhag sabhi maanav samskritiyon main nihit he, mukhyatah krishi ke sandarbh main, lekin vaigyanik adhyayan keval 16 veen shatabdi main hi ek vigyaan ke rup main shuru hua. 'wiliam kirbi ' ko keetvigyaan ke pita ke rup main janaa jaataa he 'wiliam spence' ke sahyog se unhone ek keetvigyaan vishwakosh prakashit kiya. keet vigyaan parichay ,vishay ke moolbhoot paath ke rup main mana jaataa he. unhone 1833 main landan main royal antomological sosayadi kii sthapana kii. keetvigyaan 1 9veen or 20 veen shatabdi main teji se viksit hua or charles darwin, jin henry faibar, vladimir naabokove, karl won frishch or do baar pulitzer puraskaar vijeta io wilson ,sahit badi sankhya main logon ne ullekhneey aankadon ke saath adhyayan kiya.
 1994 main 'antomological socity of america' ne 'da associat certyphoid antamologist ' naamak keet niyantran udyog ke liye ek nayaa vyavsaayik pramann kaarykram launch kiya. ek "kitnashak adhyayankarta " ke rup main uteern hone ke liye ek vyakti ko saamaanya rup se ucch degree kii aavashyaktaa hoti he, saath hi adhikansh atakavigyaani apni piechdi is vishay main karte hai. paaramparik arthon main ve sahi marmajna nahin hai, jabaki asii pramanikaran praapt karne vaale vyaktiyon ko asii, ameor antomologist, associat kitnashak yaa -adhik saamaanya taur par associat-certyphoid atogologists ke rup main sanderbhit kiya ja saktaa he.
 kai vigyaani keede ke ek parivaar ke visheshgy hote hai or inamen se kai up-prajatiyo ko apane swayan ke anaupchaarik naam dete hai, aamtaur par ye naam unke samooh ke vaigyanik naam se liye jate hai: 
 kii tarah anya vaigyanik visheshta, entomologists kii ek sankhya he, sthaneey, rashtriya or antarrashtriya sanghathanon. vahaan bhi kar rahe hai kai sanghathanon main visheshgyata vishisht subareas.
 chaynit sangrahalayon kii ek suchi jahaan bahut bada keet sangrah he.
