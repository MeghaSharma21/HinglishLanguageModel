 bharat or pakistan ke bich pratham yuddh san 1947 main hua tha. yah kashmir ko lekar hua tha jo 1947-48 ke dauraan chalaa.
 isa purv teesri shatabdi main samraat ashok ne kashmir main buddha dharm kaa prasar kiya. baad main kanishka ne isaki jaden or gahari kin. chhathi shatabdi ke aarambh main kashmir par hunon kaa adhikaar ho gaya. yadhyapi san 530 main ghati phir swatantra ho gai lekin iske turant baad is par ujjainsamrajya kaa niyantran ho gaya. vikramaditya rajvansh ke patan ke pashchaat kashmir par sthaneey shaasha raaj karne lage. vahaan hindu or buddha samskritiyon kaa mishrit rup viksit hua. kashmir ke hindu raajaaon main lalitaditya sabase prasiddh raja hue jinka rajya purv main bengal tak, dakshin main konkan, uttar-pashchim main turkistan, or uttar-purv main tibet tak failaa tha. lalitaditya ne anek bhavy bhawanon kaa nirmaan kiya.bhartiya jammu or kashmir ke teen mukhya anchal hai : jammu, kashmir auraladdakh . greeshmakaleen rajdhani srinagar he or sheetakaaleen rajdhani jammu-tavi. kashmir pradesh ko ‘duniya kaa swarg’ mana gaya he. adhikansh rajya himalay parvat se dhakaa hua he. mukhya nadiyaan hai sindhu, jhelum or chenab. yahan kai kuubasurat jhilein hai: dal, wooler or nagin.
 jammu kaa ullekh mahabharat main bhi miltaa he. haal main akhnur se praapt hadappa kaaleen avasheshon tathaa maury, kushaan or gupt kaal kii kalakritiyon se jammu ke pracheen swaroop par nayaa prakaash padaa he. jammu 22 pahari riyasaton main banta hua tha. dogra shaasha raja maldev ne kai kshetron ko jitkar apane vishaal rajya kii sthapana kii. san 1733 se 1782 tak raja ranjit dev ne jammu par shashan kiya kintu unke uttaradhikari durbal the, isliye maharaja ranjit singh ne jammu kopunjab main mila liya. baad main unhone dogra shahi khandaan ke vanshaj raja gulab singh ko jammu rajya saunp diya. 1819 main yah punjab ke sikh shashan ke antargat aayaa or 1846 main dogra rajvansh ke adheen ho gaya. gulab singh ranjit singh ke governron main sabase shaktishaalee ban gaye or lagbhag samooche jammu kshetra ko unhone apane rajya main mila liya.
 kashmir main islam
 kashmir main islam kaa aagaman 13 veen or 14veen shatabdi main hua. muslim shaskaon main jain-ul-abdin sabase prasiddh shaasha hue, jo kashmir main us samay satta main aae, jab taataron ke hamale ke baad hindu raja singhdev bhaag gaye. baad main chak shaskaon ne jain-ul-aavdin ke putr haidarshah kii sena ko khaded diya or san 1586 tak kashmir par raaj kiya. san 1586 main akbar ne kashmir ko jeet liya. san 1752 main kashmir tatkaaleen kamazor mogul shaasha ke haath se nikalakar afghanistan ke ahamed shah abdali ke haathon main chalaa gaya. 67 saal tak pathaanon ne kashmir ghati par shashan kiya.
 1947 main angrejo ke bharat chhodane ke pehle or baad main jammu evam kashmir kii riyasat par naye bane dono rashtron main se ek main vilay kaa baari dabaav tha. bharat ke batwaare par hue samjhaute ke dastavej ke anusaar riyasto ke raajaaon ko dono main se ek raashtra ko chunane kaa adhikaar tha parantu kashmir ke maharaja hari sih apni riyasat ko swatantra rakhana chaahate the or unhone kisi bhi raashtra se judane se bachanaa chaha. angrejo ke bharat chhodane ke baad riyasat par riyasat par pakistani sainiko or pashtuno ke kabeelai ladaako ne hamalaa kar diya.
 is bhay se ki riyasat kii fuj inkaa saamana nahin kar paayegi maharaja ne bharat se sainik sahaayta maangi. bharat ne sainik sahaayta ke evaj main kashmir ke bharat main vilay kii shart rakh di. maharaja ke hami bharne par bharat ne is vilay ko manyata de di or riyasat ko jammu kashmir ke naam se nayaa rajya bana diya. bhartiya sena kii tukadiyaan turant rajya kii raksha ke liye tainaat kar di gayi. kintu is vilay kii vaidhata par pakistan asahmat tha. chunki jati aadhaarit aankade uplabdh nahin the isaliye maharaj ke bharat se vilay ke peeche kya kaaran the yah tay paanaa kathin tha.
 pakistan kii yah daleel thi ki maharaja ko bhartiya sena bulaane kaa adhikaar nahin tha kyonki angrejo ke aane ke pehle kashmir ke maharaja kaa koi pad nahin tha or yah pad keval angrejo kii niyukti thi. isaliye pakistan ne yuddh karne kaa nirnay liya par uske sena pramukh duglas greysi ne is aashay ke pakistani pradhanmantri kaa aadesh maanane se inkaar kar diya. unka tark yah tha ki kashmir par kabja kar rahi bhartiya senaayen british rajasatta kaa pratinidhitiv kar rahi hai atah vah usase yuddh nahin kar sakate. haalanki baad main pakistan ne senaayen bhej di par tab tak bharat kareeb kareeb do tihaayi kashmir par kabja kar chukaa tha
 yah yuddh purv jammu or kashmir riyasat kii simaaon ke bheetar bhartiya sena ardhsainik bal or purv jammu or kashmir riyasat kii senaaon or pakistani sena ardhsainik bal or pakistan ke uttar pashchim seemaant rajya ke kabeelai ladaako jo khud ko aaazaad kashmir kii sena ke naam se pukaarte the ke bich ladaa gaya tha. prarambh main purv jammu or kashmir riyasat kii sena aaazaad kashmir ke kabeelai ladaako ke shruwati hamalo ke liye taiyaar nahin thi use keval seemaa kii rakhwali ke liye bahut kam sankhya main tainaat kiya gaya tha isaliye unki raksha pranali akraman ke saamne turant dhah gayi or unki kuch tukadiya dushmano se ja mili.
 aaazaad kashmir ke kabeelai ladaake shuruwati aasaan safaltaaon ke baad lutpaat main vyast hry gayi or unhone aage bad aasaani se kabje main aa sakane vaale naye ilaako par hamalaa karne main der kar di or maharaja kae bharat main vilay main sahamati dete hi bhartiya sena ko vimaano kii madad se sainik pahuchane kaa mauka de diya. 1947 ke ant tak kashmir main kabja karne ke pakistani abhiyaan kii hawa nikal gayi. keval himalay ke upari hisso main aaazaad kashmir naam kii pakistani sena ko kuch safaltaayen mili par aakhir main unhe leh ke bahari hisse se june unniis sau adtalis main waapas khaded diya gaya. puure 1948 ke dauraan dono paksho ke bich anek choti ladaaiyaan hui par kisi ko bhi koi mahtvapurn samrik safalata nahin mili or dhire dhire ek seemaa jise aaj niyantran rekha ke naam se janaa jaataa he sthapit ho gai. 31 disambar 1948 main aupchaarik yuddh viraam kii ghoshnaa ho gayi.
 is yuddh ko das bhaago main bata ja saktaa he. yah bhaag is prakaar se hai.
 shuruwati hamale kaa mukhya uddeshy kashmir ghati or iske pramukh shahar srinagar ke niyantran ko apane haath main lenaa tha. jammu or kashmir riyasat kii greeshmakaleen rajdhani srinagar or sheetakaaleen rajdhani jammu thi. mujjafarabad or domel main tainaat riyasat kii sena turant hi aaazaad kashmir sena naam kii pakistani sena se haar gayi or srinagar kaa rasta khul gaya tha. riyasat kii sena ke punah sangathit hone ke pehle srinagar par kabja karne ke bajaay aaazaad kashmir sena seemaant shahro par kabja karne or uske nivaasiyon se lutpaat evam anya atyaachaar karne main jute gayi. poonch kii ghati main riyasat kii sena peeche hat kar shahro main kendrit ho gayin or unako kai mahino ke baad bhartiya sena gheraa bandi se mukt karaayaa.
 jammu or kashmir riyasat ke bharat se vilay ke baad bharat ne vimaan ke dwaara sainik or upkaran srinagar pahunchaaye. vahaan pahunch kar unhone riyasat kii sena ko majboot kiya or srinagar ke chaaro ore rk suraksha gheraa banaaya or aaazaad kashmir sena ko haraa diya. is suraksha ghere main bhartiya sena ke bakhtarband vahno ke dwaara virodhiyo ko peeche se gherna bhi shaamil tha. harkar peeche hatati hui pakistani sena kaa baraamula or uri tak peechha karke in dono shahro ko mukt kara liya gaya haalanki poonch ghati main pakistani sena ke dwaara shahro kii gheraa bandi jari rahi.
 gilgit main aaazaad kashmir kii kabeelai sena main gilgit rajya ke ardh sainik bal shaamil ho gayi or chitral ke mehtar jagirdaar kii sena bhi apane jagirdaar ke pakistan main vilay kii ghoshnaa ke baad usame shaamil ho gayi
 bhartiya sena ne aaazaad kashmir kii sena kaa uri or baraamula par kabje ke baad peechha karnaa band kar diya or ek sahaayta tukdi ko dakshin disha main poonch kii gheraa bandi todane ke prayaas main bheja. haalanki sahaayta tukdi poonch pahuch gayi par vah gheraabandi nahin tod paayi or vah bhi funs gayi. ek duusari sahaayta tukdi kotli tak pahuch gayi par use apana kotli kii morchabandi ko chhodkar peeche hatana padaa isi bich mirpur par aaazaad kashmir kii sena kaa kabja ho gaya.
 pakistan/aaazaad kashmir kii sena ne jhanger par kabja kar liya tatpashchaat usne naushera par nakaam hamalaa kiya. pakistan/aaazaad kashmir kii sena kii duusari tukdeeyon ne lagaataar uri par naakaam hamale kiye. duusari ore bharat ne ek chote se akraman se chhmb par kabja bana liya. is samay tak bhartiya sena ke paas atirikt sainya bal uplabdh ho gayi aise main niyantran rekha par sthitiyaan sthir hone lagi.
 bhartiya sena balon ne jhanger or rajori par pratiakraman kar ke unhe kabje main le liya. kashmir ghati main aaazaad kashmir sena ne uri ke suraksha tantra par akraman jari rakhaa. aaazaad kashmir sena ne uttar main scardu kii gheraa bandi kar di.
 aaazaad kashmir kii sena ke anek pratiakramano ke baavjood bharatiyo ne jhanger par niyantran banaaye rakhaa haalanki ab aaazaad kashmir kii sena ko niyamit pakistani sainiko kii madad adhikadhik milane lagi thi. kashmir ghati main bharatiyo ne akraman kar tidhwal par kabja kar liya. unche himalay ke kshetron main aaazaad kashmir kii sena ko acchhee badhat mil rahi thi. unhone tukadiyo kii ghuspaith kar ke kargil par gheraabandi kar di tathaa scardu kii madad ke liye ja rahe bhartiya sainya daston ko haraa diya. 
 bhartiya sena balo ne kashmir ghati main hamalaa jari rakhaa or uttar kii ore aage batch kar keran or gurants par kabja kar liya. unhone tidhwal par kiye gayi ek pratiakarman ko waapas khaded diya. poonch ghati main poonch main fasee bhartiya tukdi gheraabandi todakar kuch samay ke liye bahari duniya se waapas jud gayi. lambey samay se fasee kashmir riyasat kii tukdi gilgit scout se scardu kii raksha karne main ab tak safal thi is liye pakistani sena leh kii ore nahin batch paa rahi thi. agast main chitral kii sena ne mata-ul-mulk ke netrutv main scardu par hamalaa kar diya or topakhane kii madad se scardu par kabja kar liya. isse gilgit scout ladakh kii ore aage jane kaa mauka mil gaya.
 is samay ke dauraan niyantran rekha sthapit hone lagi thi or dono paksho main apane niyantran vaale kshetron kii raksha kaa zyada mahatv tha banisbat kii hamalaa karne ke. is dauraan keval ek mahatvapurn abhiyaan chalaaya gaya yah tha abhiyaan dak jo ki bhartiya balo dwaara draas ke kabje ke liye tha is dauraan poonch par gheraabandi jari rahi.
 ab bhartiya sena sabhi kshetron par pakistani sena or usase samarthit aaazaad kashmir sena par baari hone lagi thi. poonch ko ek saal lambii gheraabandi se aaazaad kara liya gaya tha or gilgit scout jo ki ab tak acchhee kamyaabi hasil kar rahi thi use use aakhirkaar haraakar uskaa peechha karte hue bhartiya sena ne kargil ko aaazaad kara liya par aage hamalaa karne ke liye bhartiya sena ko rasad kii aapurti kii samasya aa sakati thi atah unhe ruknaa padaa jojila darre ko tanks kii madad se kabje main le liya gaya pakistani sena tanks kii apeksha nahin kar rahi thi or unke turant paanv ukhd gayi. tanks kaa istemaal burma yuddh se mili anubhav ke kaaran hi sambhav ho paaya tha. is darre par kabje ke baad draas par aasaani se kabja ho gaya.
 ladaai ke is daur main pahunchane par bhartiya pradhanmantri ne maamle ko sanyukt raashtra mahasabha main le ja kar unke dwaara maamle kaa samaadhaan karwaane kaa man bana liya. 31 disambar 1948 ko sanyukt raashtra ke dwaara yuddh viraam kii ghoshnaa kii gai. yuddh viraam hone se kuch dinon pehle pakistani sena ne ek pratiakraman karke uri or poonch ke bich ke raste par kabja karke dono ke bich sadak sampark tod diya. ek lambey molbhav ke baad dono paksh yudhviram par raji ho gayi. is yudhviram kii sharten agast 13, 1948 ko sanyukt raashtra ne apanaaya. isame pakistan ko apane niyamit or aniyamit sanico ko puri tarah se hataane or bharat ko rajya main kanoon vyavastha lagu karne ke liye aavashyak sainik rakhane kaa prastaav tha. is shart ke puraa hone par janamat sangrah karke rajya ke bhavishya or malikana haq tay karne kaa nirdhaaran hota. is yuddh main dono paksho ke lagbhag 15-15 sau sainik maare jane kaa anumaan he. or is yuddh ke baad bharat kaa riyasat ke saath pratishat or pakistan kaa 40 pratishat bhubhag par kabja rahaa.
 is yuddh ke do charano main bakhtarband vaahan or halke tanko kaa prayog atyant mahatvapurn tha in dono main hi bahut hi kam sankhya main inkaa prayog hua tha. ye charan the
 yah ghatnaayen yah bataati hai ki asambhavit jagaho par bakhtarband vahno ke hamale kaa dushman par maansik dabaav padataa he. aisa bhi ho saktaa he ki hamalaavaron ne tagerodhi hathiyaaro kaa prayog nahin kiya shaayad unhone aavashyak na jaanakar unhe peeche hi chhod diya. bakhtarband vahno ke prayog kii safalataa ne bharat kii yuddh niti par gahari chhap chodi china ke saath yuddh ke vakt bharatiyo ne badi mehnat se durgam ilaako main bakhtarband vahno kaa prayog kiya kintu us yuddh main bakhtar band vahno ko apekshit safalataa nahin mili.
 seemaa rekha main aaye parivartano kaa yadi adhyayan kiya jaay to kaaphi rochak tathy ubhar kar aate hai. ek baar senaaon kaa jamaavada puraa hone ke baad niyantran rekha main badlaav behad dhimaa ho gaya or vijay keval un ilaakon tak simit ho gayi jiname sainik ghanatv kam tha jaise kii uttari himalay ke unche ilaake jiname shruwaat main aaazaad kashmir kii sena ko safaltaaen mili thi.1948 main pakistan ne kashmir par akraman karke, uske ek tihaai bhaag par adhikaar kar liya. abhi tak yah ek tihaai bhaag pakistan ke paas he.
 jammu or kashmir riyasat kii senaayen is yuddh ke prarambh main faili huin or choti sankhya main keval aatankwaadi hamalo se nipatane ke liye tainaat thim. jisase ve paaramparik sainik hamale ke saamne nishphal sabit hui. is ranneeti ko bharat ne purvi pakistan main bharat pakistan ke bich tisare yuddh main safalataa purvak prayog kiya.
