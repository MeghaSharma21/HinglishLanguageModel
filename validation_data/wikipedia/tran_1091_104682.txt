 arrenius swante august arrenius sweden ke prasiddh rasayanajna the. ve mul rup se ek bhautikashastri the, lekin unhen aksar ek rasayanajna ke rup main sanderbhit kiya jaataa he. unhen bhautik rasayan vigyaan ke sansthaapakon main se ek maan jaataa he. unhen 1903 main rasayan shaastra main nobel puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya or ve 1905 main nobel santhaan ke nideshak bane, jahaan vah apni mrityu tak bane rahe.
 inaki shiksha apasaala, stokhome tathaa riga main hui thi. inaki buddhi bahut hi prakhar tathaa kalpanaashakti tikshn thi. keval 24 varsh kii aayu main hi inhone vaidyut viyojan kaa siddhant upasthit kiya. apasaala vishvavidhyalay main inaki doctorat kii thisis kaa yahi vishay tha. is naveen siddhant kii kadi aalochana hui tathaa us samay ke bade-bade vaigyanikon ne, jaise lard kelvin ityadi ne, iskaa bahut virodh kiya. isi samay ek duusare vaigyanik waant hof ne patale ghol ke niyamon kaa adhyayan kar gas ke niyamon se uski samanta par jor diya. is khoj se tathaa ostwalt ke samarthan se apni nikali hui patrika 'citshrift foor phisikalice kemi' main arrenius kaa lekh prakashit kiya or apane bhaashanon tathaa lekhon main bhi is siddhant kaa samarthan kiya. ant main is siddhant ko vaigyanik manyata praapt hui.
 san 1891 main lekturer tathaa 1895 main professor ke pad par, stokhome main, arrenius kii niyukti hui. 1902 main unhen davy medal tathaa 1903 main nobel puraskaar mila. 1905 se mrityu paryant ve stokhome main nobel institute ke director rahe. baad main unhone duusare vishyon par bhi apane vichaar prakat kiye. ye vichaar unki pustak 'varlds in da mekking tathaa 'life on da univers' main vyakt hai. ye multah bhautikavid the kintu inhen praayah rasayanajna hi kaha jaataa he. bhautik rasayan kii sthapana kaa shrey inako hi he. 1903 main inhen rasayan vigyaan kaa nobel puraskaar diya gaya or is prakaar ve swweden ke pratham nobel vijeta bhi hai.
