 turki uresia main sthit ek desh he. isaki rajdhani ankara he. isaki mukhya- or rajbhasha turki bhashaa he. ye duniya kaa akelaa muslim bahumat vaala desh he jo ki dharmanirpeksh he. ye ek loktantrik ganraajya he. iske asian hisse ko anatolia or europea hisse ko thress kehete hai.
 sthiti : 39 degree uttari akshansh tathaa 36 degree purvi deshaantar. iskaa kuch bhaag europe main tathaa adhikansh bhaag asia main padataa he at: ise europe evam asia ke bich kaa 'pul' kaha jaataa he. igian sagar ke patale jalkhand ke bich main aa jane se is pul ke do bhaag ho jate hai, jinhen saadharantaya europea turki tathaa asian turki kehete hai. turki ke ye donon bhaag bosporas ke jaldmarumadhya, maarmara sagar tathaa dardnelj dwaara ek duusare se alag hote hai.
 turki ganatantr kaa kul kshetrafal 2,96,185 varg mile he jisamen europea turki kaa kshetrafal 9,068 varg mile tathaa asian turki kaa kshetrafal 2,87,117 varg mile he. iske antargat 451 daldali sthal tathaa 3,256 khaare pani kii jhilein hai. purv main rus or iran, dakshin kii ore iraq, syria tathaa bumadhyasaagar, pashchim main greece or bulgaria or uttar main kalasagar isaki raajnneetik seemaa nirdhaarit karte hai.
 europea turki - tribhujaakar prayadweepi pradesh he jiskaa sheershak purv main bosporas ke muhane par he. uske uttar tathaa dakshin donon ore parvatashreniyan faili hui hai. madhya main nichalaa maidan miltaa he jisamen hokar maritsa or ergin nadiyaan bahati hai. isi bhaag se hokar istasmul kaa sambandh pashchimi deshon se he.
 asian turki - isko ham teen prakritik bhaagon main vibhajit kar sakate hai: 1. uttar main kaala sagar ke tat par ponts parvat, 2. madhya main enatolia ore aarmenia ke nichle bhaag, 3. dakshin main toras evam antitoras parvat jo bumadhyasaagar ke tat tak vistrut hai.
 donon samudron ke tat par maidan kii patali pattiyaan milti hai. pashchim main igian tathaa maarmara sagaron ke tat par apekshaakrit kam unchi pahadiyaan milti hai, jisase madhya ke pathaar tak avaagaman sugam ho jaataa he. uttar se dakshin kii ore aane par kaala sagar ke tat par sankaraa maidan miltaa he jisase ek se lekar do mile tak unchi ponts parvatashreniyan ekaek uthati hui drushtigochar hoti hai. in parvatashreniyon ko paar karne par enatolia kaa vistrut pathaar miltaa he. iske dakshin toras kii unchi parvatashreniyan faili hui he or dakshin jane par bumadhyasagariya tat kaa nichalaa maidan miltaa he. anatolia pathaar main turki kaa ek tihaai bhaag sammilit he.
 jalwaayu ke vichaar se turki do pramukh bhaagon main vibhajit kiya ja saktaa he. 
 1. maidani bhaag jahaan kii jalwaayu bumadhyasagariya he or jahaan jaade main kareeb 20"" varsha hoti he, 
 2. ardhashushk pathari bhaag jisaki adhiktam varsh kaa ausat 10"" he. 
 samudratati bhaagon kii jalwaayu greece se milti julati he jahaan garmi praaia: shushk rahati he or sheetkaal main varsha hoti he. jaade ke dinon main is kshetra main sheetalhari bhi chalati he kaala sagar ke tat par sabase adhik varsha hoti he. purv kii ore to kareeb 100"" varsha hoti he. at: oonchaai ke anusaar vibhinn vanaspatiyaan milti hai. nichle maidani bhaag main praaia: chote chote ped tathaa jhaadiyaan milti he, pathari dhaalon par sheet katibandheey parnpaati van tathaa 6,000 foot kii oonchaai tak conifaras van tathaa or oonchaai par ghaas ke maidan milte hai.
 enatolia ke pathari bhaag kii jalwaayu dakshin purvi rus kii jalwaayu se milti julati he jahaan jaade main uttar purvi sheethal hawaayen chalati hai, jinase taap kabhi kabhi shunya ansh centegrade tak pahunch jaataa he. garmi main adhik garmi padati he tathaa kabhi kabhi aandhi bhi aati he. varsha 10"" se kam hoti he. jaade main lagbhag teen maheenon tak barf padati rahati he. falswaroop yah pedo se rahit shushk ghaas kaa maidan he. aarmenia kaa pahari bhaag or bhi thanda he jahaan jaade kii ritu chah mahine kii hoti he. isiilie log is bhaag ko "turki kaa sivyria" kehete hai.
 turki ke adhikansh bhaag main mitti kii gaharaai kam milti he. mitti kaa adhik bhaag katkar nikal gaya he. ghaas ke maidaanon main adhik charaagaahi ke kaaran mitti kaa kataav adhik hua he. kuch sthalon par jangalon ke cat jane se bhi mitti kaa kataav adhik hua he.
 asian turki kaa mukhya udhyam krishi evam charaagaahi hai. gehun mukhya fasal he, jo krishya bhoomi ke 45 pratishat bhaag main upjaaya jaataa he. iske aadhe bhaag main jau upjaaya jaataa he. teen pratishat bhaag main kapaas tathaa ek pratishat bhaag main tambaakoo kii kheti hoti he.
 yahan se niryaat honevaali vastuon main gehun, oon, tambaakoo, anjir tathaa kishmish mukhya hai.
 yahan ke mukhya khanij padaarth koyala, lignite, loha, tanba, mangneej, sisa, jasta, krom tathaa emry hai; kintu lignite, loha tathaa krom kaa atyadhik utpaadan hota he.
 turki ko bhugolic drushti se paanch mukhya bhaagon main vibhajit kiya ja saktaa he: 
 pashchim main dardnelj or purv se bansaporas ke jaldmarumadhya ke bich main sthit maarmara samudr europe or asia ke bich kii seemaa nirdhaarit karata he. uttar kii ore europe main thrais kaa maidan tathaa dakshin kii ore troy, varasa or vithunia ke maidan milte hai. vaarshik varsh 120"" ke nikat he. gehun, jau, jayee, jaitoon, angoor tathaa tambaakoo upjaai jaati he. jaitoon is desh ke liye bahut mahatvapurn vastu he. iskaa upyog makkhan ke abhaav main hota he. is kshetra main 750 foot se nichle sthalon main krishi mukhya udhyam he. kheti karne karaane kaa dhang bada purana he, bhartiya deshi hal kii bhanti ke hal kaa upyog hota he, par lohe ke hal kaa upyog badhata ja rahaa he.
 istambul turki kaa sabase bada nagar he. yah bosporas jaldmarumadhya ke dakshin ore ek pahari par sthit he. yahan kii "golden horn" naamak lambii khadi ke kaaran yah acha bandargah bhi ho gaya he. jaldmarumadhya kahin bhi paanch mile se adhik chaudaa nahin he, kuch sthal par isaki chaudaai aadha mile hi he.
 yahan kaa samudratat kam samtal he or parvatiy bhaag dakshin kii ore ekaek upar uthte hai. at: yahan bandargahon kaa nirmaan bahut kam ho paaya he. bastiyaan isi sankare bhaag tak hi simit hai. varsha kii adhikta ke kaaran junglee kshetra adhik hai, jinamein chestnut vriksh mukhya hai. lakdi kaa kaam yahan kaa mukhya udhyam he. tambaakoo duusari niryaat kii vastu he. dakshin kii ore ponts kaa pahari bhaag purv se pashchim kii ore failaa hua he. iskaa adhiktar bhaag junglee tathaa ghaas kaa maidan he. bhitari bhaag main jahaan kii oonchaai 3,000 foot se lekar 6,000 foot tak he, ardhamrusthaleey vaataavaran miltaa he. yahan jansankhya bhi viral he. purv kii ore kuch koyala kshetra he, jiskaa prati varsh utpaadan 30 lakh metric ton he.
 turki kaa purvi tathaa pashchimi nichalaa maidan krishi ke vichaar se adhik mahatvapurn he. bumadhyasagariya jalwaayu hone ke kaaran yahan teen se lekar chah maheene tak garmi kii shushk ritu hoti he or jaade main kareeb 20"" varsha hoti hai. igian sagar ke tat par bastiyaan badi ghani hai. anek dhansi hui ghaatiyon main mitti kaa purnat: athwa aanshik jamaav ho gaya he jisase kheti ke liye upjau maidan nirmit ho gaye hai. yahan ke teen maidani bhaag adhik mahatvapurn hai. pratham pashchim main izmir ke peeche, madhya main antalaya ke aasapaas panphilian kaa maidan tathaa uttar-purv ken kaane par adaaga ke paas silician kaa maidan he. yaheen par ek behad darre se hokar baghdad railway jaati he.
 gehun tathaa jau mukhya fasalein hai. kapaas har ek maidani bhaag main hoti he, visheshkar silician ke maidan main. izmir ke aasapaas angoor, kichamish, jaitoon, anjir tathaa opium adhik hoti he. toras parvatamalas jangalon se dhaki hai parantu vriksh 8,500 foot kii oonchaai tak hi simit hai. pahaado par barf adhik padati he.
 isaki oonchaai pashchim main 2,000 foot tak or purv main 4,000 foot tak he. yah 10,000 foot se bhi adhik unche toras or ponts parvatamalaon se ghira hua he. turki kaa bhitari bhaag unche basin kii bhanti he. jahaan tahaan unche pahaad bhi milte hai. adhikansh bhaag kaa jalpravaah bheetar kii ore he or nadiyaan yaa to jhiilon main girti hai athwa namakeen nichle daldalon main lupt ho jaati hai. parantu kuch nadiyaan parvaton ko katati or guphaayen nirmit karti hui bahati hai. chaaron taru unche parvaton se ghire rahane ke kaaran yah bhaag jaade tathaa garmi donon mausamon main bhapabhari hawaaon se saadharantaya vanchit rahane ke kaaran shushk rahata he, vaarshik varsh kaa ausat lagbhag 10"" he. jaade main bhoomi kabhi kabhi barf se dhak jaati he. garmi main garam tathaa tej hawaayen chalati hai. at: shushk pradeshon main mitti hat gai he ore marusthaleey kankadon kaa ubhaar ho gaya he. is ardhashushk ghaas ke maidaanon main bhed bakriyaan paali jaati hai. doodh dahi kaa samavesh yahan ke bhojan main adhik hota he. oon tathaa oonee kambal taiyaar karnaa mukhya pesha he. angora se unka niryaat hota he. garmi ke dinon main gaderiye apane jaanwaron ke saath pahaado par chale jate hai or anya mausam main maidani bhaag main utar aate hai. jahaan sinchai ke saadhan uplabdh hai vahaan gehun kii kheti hoti he. is pathaar main turki kii ek tihaai jansankhya rahati he. enatolia kaa yah pathari bhaag turk  kaa mukhya sthaan he. turki kii rajdhani ankara isi bhaag main sthit he.
 turki ke purv main ponts tathaa toras parvatashreniyan milakar aarmenia ke pahari bhaag kaa nirmaan karti hai. yaheen se dajala or faraat nadiyaan nikalati hai. uttar or dakshin kii ore se pahari shreniyan madhya ke pathaar ko gherti hai. dakshin kii ore toras kii ksheni kurdistan tathaa uttar kii ore ponts kii shrenee karawag, is bhaag ko ghere hue hai. jwalamukhi parvat tathaa laba ke vistaar se dharaatal or bhi unchaa nicha ho gaya he. oonchaai ke kaaran yah bhaag adhik thanda rahata he or bahut se daren varsh main kareeb aath maheene tak barf se dhake rahete hai. purv kii ore aradaar jwalamukhi parvat turki, iran or rus kii seemaa par sthit he. krishi kii apeksha charaagaahi is kshetra main adhik mahatvapurn he.
 turki ke adhikansh manushya turk jati ke hai. inke atirikt cokesian, aarminian, georgian, kurd, arab or turkman jatiyaan bhi turki main pai jaati hai. 
 turki ek aisa kshetra he jahaan maanav-jatiyon ke sthaanantaran tathaa sangharsh kii pranmukhataa rahi he. isa se purv unani jatiyon kaa basaav or phir sthaanantaran tathaa isa ke baad san 800-1400 tak turk jati kaa pradurbhaav is bhugolic kshetra kii itihaas kii pramukh likhit ghatana he.
 homer ke odissi main varnit try kii ladaai turki ke pashchimi tat par troy ke vaasiyon tathaa unani dwipon par based samrajyon ke bich hui thi. iskaa kaal san 1200 isa purv ke aasapaas mana jaataa he. iske purv yahi tat hitti tathaa finic samrajyon kaa bhi sthaan rahaa tha. san 530 isapurv main iran ke farsi samrajya ke antargat aayaa jo kai saalon tak unaniyon dwaara sangharsh ke kaaran greek or irani samrajyon main banta rahaa. san 330 isapurv main sikandar jab purv kii ore vijay abhiyaan par niklaa to yah pradesh bhi unani samrajya ke andar aa gaya. san 24 ke baad se yah roman samrajya kaa ang bana rahaa. saasaani or baad main turk jatiyon ke sanghathanon ne purvi roman samrajya par hamalaa kar iske kai kshetron par adhikaar bana liya. turk log dhire-dhire nauveen sadi main is kshetra main basate gaye. saasaani samrajya ke ant ke baad purvi tathaa dakshini turki main islam kaa prachaar hua. ouguj, saljuq or osmani turk sunni islam main parivartit ho gaye.
 osmani turkon ne roman rajdhani istambul par bhi adhikaar karne ke kai prayaas kiye. san 1453 main ve istambul ko sthai rup se vijayi karne main kaamyaab rahe. iske baad istambul ek majboot turk samrajya kaa kendra bana jo solahavin sadi main hungary se lekar arab deshon tak fail gaya. is samrajya ne europe tathaa iran ke safaviyon se kai ladaayeeyaan ladi. irani shaskaon ne barud kaa prayog turkon se hi siikhaa - jiske baad babar jaise aakrantha bharat par barudi topon se lash sena lekar akraman kar sakaa. osmani shaskaon ne solahavin sadi main makka or madina par bhi adhikaar kar liya jisaki vajah se wo islam ke caliph bhi ban gaye. lekin uttar or purv ke roosi tathaa dakshin-purv ke safavi shaskaon se unki ladaai hoti rahi. irani shaskaon ko to unhone uttar main badhane se roak diya par roosiyon ke saath kreemia ke yuddh main main unhen haar kaa munh dekhnaa padaa.
 pratham vishwayuddh main osmani turk germanon ke saath the. germanon kii haar or arab main angrejon dwaara khaded die jane ke baad osmani samrajya kaa ant ho gaya. iske baad mustafaa kamaal pasha ne loktantr kaa prachaar or dharmanirpeksh pranali kii vakaalat kii jiske falswaroop turki ek dharmanirpeksh desh bana. arabi lipi ko tyaag kar europea roman aadhaarit lipi ko apanaaya gaya or shashan ko dharm se alag kiya gaya.
 turki kii rajdhani or duusare sabase bade shahar istambul main he. sabase bada or sabase adhik bheed shahar istambul jo duniya ke do alag-alag mahadwipon par bhoomi he, main keval shahar he. teesraa sabase bada shahar istambul ke tateey shahar he jo desh ke mukhya bandargah he. turki main 81 praanton main baanta gaya he. prayek praant apane khud ke chote sarkaar he, lekin ve keval choti-choti baton ke bare main nirnay lene ke liye kar sakate hai: ankara main sarkaar mahatvapurn muddon kaa faisla kiya. praanton 7 kshetron main hai. prayek praant jilon main baanta gaya he. vahaan 923 jilon ko puri tarah se kar rahe hai.
 turki ko 81 rajyon main baanta gaya he. inako vyavastha or khaaskar janaganana main sahuliyat ke liye 7 pradeshon main baanta gaya he. haalanki in pradeshon kaa prashasnic taur par koi mahatv nahin he.
