 leeds united leeds, west yorkshaire main ek angrezi footbal club he. club leeds city epasi ko khatm karne kaa paalan 1919 main gathan kiya gaya tha footbal lig dwaara or unke alland road stadium main padbhaar sambhaal liya.
 leeds united teen first divijan lig khitaab, ek egge cap or ek lig cap jeet liya he. sammaan ke bahumat ke 1960 ke dashak or 1970 ke dashak main don revie ke prabandhan ke tahat jite the. club manchester united ke saath bahut bhayankar pratidwandvita he, donon tiimon ke bich match aksar hinsak ho gaye hai. leeds united safed vardi main khelte hai. club billaa white neuyork ke gulab or "lufc" suvidhaayen.
