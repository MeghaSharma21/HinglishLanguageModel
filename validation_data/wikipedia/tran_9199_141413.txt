 dakshin asian khel dwi-vaarshik bahu-krida pratiyogita he, jisamen dakshin asian khilaadi pratibhaagi hote hai. in khelon kaa shaasi nikaay dakshin asian khel parishad he, jisaki sthapana 1983 main hui thi. vartmaan main saig ke aath sadsy hai afghanistan, nepal, pakistan, bangladesh, bharat, bhutan, maldive or srilanka.
 pratham saif khel 1984 main kathmandu, nepal main yojit hue the or tabase ye khel prati do varshon ke interval par yojit hote hai, keval kuch avasaron ko chhodkar. 2004 main dakshin asian khel parishad kii 32veen baithak main yah nirnay liya gaya kii in khelon kaa naam dakshin asian sangh khelon se badalakar dakshin asian khel kar diya jaae kyonki adhikariyon kaa maananaa tha kii sangh shabd pratiyogita par kam bal de rahaa he or bheed aakarshith karne main baadhak ban rahaa he. is khelon ko bahudhaa dakshin asia olympic khelon ke roopaantar ke rup main badhachadha kar prastut kiya jaataa he.
