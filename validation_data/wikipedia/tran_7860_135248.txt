 achtitipi kuki ek prakaar kaa jalsthal dwaara bachi hui smriti hoti he, jo website ko aapke bare main jaankaree detii he. yah website dwaara aapke computor par daalaa jaataa he or yah apane samay se swatah hi hat jaataa he yaa aap bhi is jaankaree ko hataa sakate he.
 iskaa upyog pehle keval website sanchaar ke liye june 1994 se shuru kiya gaya. iskaa upyog us samay mukhya rup se website dwaara kisi vastu ko bechney ke liye kiya jaataa tha. baad main iskaa upyog website main khaataa kholane, aapaki jaankaree rakhane, aadi kaaryon main kiya jane laga.teesri website dwaara sanchit kuki ek prakaar kaa upyogkarta ko jaanane ke liye kiya jane laga tha. iske liye kai prakaar ke upkaran he, jisase yah kuki aapke computor main sanchit nahin ho pate.
 yah kuki kisi bhi website par jane se vah website ek prakaar kaa kuki aapke computor par daalata he, jab tak aap us website ko khole rahete ho tab tak vah jaankaree vahiin rahati he. jaise hi aap use hataa dete ho, vah apane aap hi hat jaati he.
 yah ek prakaar kaa sthaayee kuki hota he, jo aapke computor band karne ke baad bhi yaa kai varsho tak bhi rah saktaa he. is kuki ko samanytah ek varsh yaa usase adhik main rakhaa jaataa he. jab bhi aap us website par jate ho kuki ke saath to yah jaankaree aapke dwaara us website main chale jaataa he.
 yah us website ke dwaara daalaa jaataa he, jo 'https' main khulata he. yah kuki keval https ke dwaara us website ko kholane se hi yah jaankaree us website ko mil sakati he. yah jaankaree gopit hoti he or surakshit maani jaati he..
 yah keval http main hi khole gaye website dwaara aapke computor main daalaa jaataa he. saath hi yah keval http main hi kaary karata he. yadi aap surakshit website https se vahi website kholenge to yah jaankaree website ko nahin mil paaegi arthath yah kaary nahin karegaa.
 iskaa upyog mukhya rup se upyogkarta ko yaad rakhane ke liye kiya jaataa he. kisi bhi prakaar ke website se yadi koi vastu ko kharida jaataa he to yah upyogkarta ke computor par jaankaree daal detaa he or usee ke dwaara khaataa kaa bhi upyog kiya jaataa he. ek website ke panne se duusare panne par jane se yah hame yaad rakhata he or suvidha uplabdh karata he.
 isse aapke gopneeyata ko kai baar khatra hota he. kyonki iske dwaara yah pataa lagaaya ja saktaa he kii aap kab kis website ko dekhe. yadi aap kahin koi apni jaankaree jaise naam, pataa yaa koi anya jaankaree daalate hai, to kai baar kuch website use apane paas rakh lete hai. yah jaankaree vah kisi vigyaapan company aadi ko bech dete hai or vah company aapako vigyaapan aadi ke dwaara pareshaan karti hai. isse surakshit rahane ke liye kai upkaran bhi uplabdh hai.
 kai website gopneeyata or niyam va sharte jaise panne bana kar bataati he kii vah kisi ko bhi aapaki jaankaree nahin bechegi or vah jaankaree surakshit he.
