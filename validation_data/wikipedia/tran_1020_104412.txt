 pulakit samraat ek bhartiya abhinayta hai, jo 2006 ke dharawahik kyonki saas bhi kabhi bahu thi main lakshya viraani ke apane kirdaar ke kaaran jane jate hai. inhone filmon main abhinay kii shuruyaat bittu bos naamak film se kii iske pashchaat 2013 main phukare naamak film kii thi.
 yah pehle dilli main rahete the or 2005 ko yah dilli se mumbai aae or kishor namith kapur se abhinay siikhaa. yah 3 navambar 2014 ko goa main shweta rohira ke saath shaadi kii. yah shweta se 2010 main mumbai main mili the.
 pulakit 2005 main mumbai main aae or iske pashchaat vah 2006 main kyonki saas bhi kabhi bahu thi naamak dharawahik main lakshya viraani kaa kirdaar nibhaayaa or isi ke dauraan unhen dard ke kaaran 2007 main is dharawahik ko chhodana padaa. vah ilaaj hetu dubai gaye or aane ke baad unhen pasandida nayaa sadsy star parivaar or sarvshereshth nayaa cheharaa bhartiya tele puraskaar mila.
