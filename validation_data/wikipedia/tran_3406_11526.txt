 bharat kaa yojana ayog, bharat sarkaar kii ek sanstha thi jiskaa pramukh kaary panchavarshiya yojanayen bananaa tha.pradhanmantri narendra modi ne 2014 main apane pehle swatantra diwas ke bhaashan main yah kaha ki unka iraadaa yojana kamishan ko bhang karnaa he. 2014 main is sanstha kaa naam badalakar niti ayog kiya gaya. bharat main yojana ayog ke sambandh main koi sanvaidhanik praavdhaan nahin he. 15 march 1950 ko kendriya mantrimandal dwaara paarit prastaav ke dwaara yojana kii sthapana kii gai thi. yojana ayog kaa adhyaksh pradhanmantri hota he. 17 agast 2014 ko yojana ayog khatm kar diya gaya or iske jagah par niti ayog kaa gathan hua. niti ayog bharat sarkaar kii ek think tanks he. yojana ayog kaa head quarter yojana bhawan ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. yah nai dilli main he.
 iske atirikt iske anya kaary hai: -
 2014 main tatkaaleen pradhanmantri narendra modi ne ise bhang karne kii ghoshnaa kar di tathaa 2015 janvari main iske sthaan par niti ayog ke gathan kiya gaya.
 bharat main british raaj ke antargat sabase pehle 1930 main, buniyadi aarthik yojanayen banane kaa kaam shuru hua. bharat kii aupniveshik sarkaar ne aupchaarik rup se ek kaary yojana board kaa gathan bhi kiya jisane 1944 se 1946 tak kaary kiya. nijee udyogpatiyon or arthshaastriyon ne 1944 main kam se kam teen vikaas yojanayen banaain.
 swatantraaa ke baad bharat ne yojana banane kaa ek aupchaarik model apanaaya or iske tahat yojana ayog, jo sidhe bharat ke pradhanmantri ko report karata tha, kaa gathan 15 march 1950 ko pradhanmantri jawaharlal nehru kii adhyakshata main kiya gaya.
 pratham panchavarshiya yojana 1951 main shuru kii gayi or iske baad 1965 tak do or panchavarshiya yojanayen banaai gayin, 1965 ke baad pakistan se yuddh ke kaaran sangharsh yojana banane kaa kaam main vyavdhaan padaa. do saal ke lagaataar sookhe, mudra kaa avamulyan, keemton main saamaanya vruddhi or sansadhano ke ksharan ke kaaran yojana prakriya baadhit hui or 1966 or 1969 ke bich teen vaarshik yojanaon ke baad, chauthi panchavarshiya yojana ko 1969 main shuru kiya gaya.
 centre main teji se badalte raajnneetik paridrishy ke chalate aathvin yojana 1990 main shuru nahin kii ja saki or varsh 1990-91 or 1991-92 ko vaarshik yojanayen mana gaya. sanrachanaatmak samaayojan kii nitiyon kii shuruuaat ke baad, aakhirkaar 1992 main aathvin yojana ko shuru kiya gaya.
 pehle aath yojanaon main mukhya jor teji se bad rahe saarvajanik kshetra par tha or is dauraan aadharbhoot or baari udyog main baari nivesh kiya gaya, lekin 1997 kii nauveen yojana kii shuruuaat ke baad se, saarvajanik kshetra par jor thodaa kam tha,
 ayog ke sangyojan main apni sthapana ke baad se kaaphi parivartan aayaa he. pradhanmantri ke paden adhyaksh hone ke saath, samiti main ek namjad upaadhyaksh bhi hota he, jiskaa rank ek cabinet mantri ke baraabar hota he. manmohan singh nit congress sarkaar ke dauraan 2014 main shri montek singh ahaluwalia ayog ke antim upaadhyaksh the. iske baad kii narendra modi sarkaar ne is ayog ko udaarikaran ke nae daur main apraasangik maanate hue ise samaapt kar diya.
 kuch mahatvapurn vibhaagon ke cabinet mantri ayog ke asthaayee sadsy hote he, jabaki sthaayee sadasyon main arthshaastra, udyog, vigyaan evam saamaanya prashasan jaise vibhinn kshetron ke visheshgy shaamil hote hai.
 ayog apane vibhinn prabhaagon ke madhyam se kaary karata he, jinke teen prakaar hai:
 ayog ke visheshgyon main adhiktar arthshaastri hote hai, yah is ayog ko bhartiya aarthik seva kaa sabase bada niyokta bana detaa he.
