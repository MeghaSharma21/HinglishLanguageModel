 
 loha yaa loh aavart sarini ke aathven samooh kaa pehla tatv he. dharti ke garbh main or baahar milakar yah sarvaadhik prapya tatv he . dharti ke garbh main yah chauthaa sabase adhik paaya jane vaala tatv he. iske chaar sthaayee samasthanik milte hai, jinaki dravyaman sankhya 54, 56, 57 or 58 he. loh ke chaar radioactive samasthanik bhi gyaat hai, jo kritrim reeti se banaae gaye hai.
 
 
 lohe kaa latin naam :- pheras
 louh dhaatu kaa puraatan kaal se manushyon ko gyaan he. bharat ke logon ko isa se 300-400 varsh purv loh ke upyog gyaat the. tamilnadu rajya ke tinnaveli janapad main, karnataka ke brahmagiri tathaa takshashila main puraatatv kaal ke lohe ke hathiyaar aadi praapt hue hai, jo lagbhag 400 varsh isvi ke purv ke gyaat hote hai. kapilwastu, buddhagaya aadi main aaj se 1,500 varsh pehle bhi log lohe ke udyog main nipun the, kyonki in sthanon main louh dhaatukarm ke anek chihr aaj bhi praapt hai. dilli kii qutubameenar ke saamne lohe kaa vishaal stambh chauthi shatabdi main pushkarna, rajasthan ke raja chandravarman, ke kaal main bana tha. yah bharat ke utkrisht dhaatushilp kaa jwalanth udhaaharan he. is stambh kii lambaai 24 foot or anumaanit bhaar 6 ton se adhik he. iske lohe ke vishleshan se gyaat hua he ki isamen 99.72 pratishat loha he. chauthi shatabdi kii dhaatukarmakala kaa anumaan isi se ho saktaa he ki 15 shatabdiyon se yah stambh vaayu or varsha ke bich aprabhavit khadaa he. aashcharya kii baat to yah he ki itnaa lanbaa chaudaa stambh kis prakaar banaaya gaya, kyonki aaj bhi itnaa vishaal dand bananaa kathin kaary he.
 bharat main ispaat udyog kii parampara bhi bahut pracheen he. aitihaasik lekhon se gyaat hota he ki isa se 5 shatabdi purv bharat kii ispaat kii talavaarein iran aadi deshon main bahut vikhyath thim. bharat se loha or ispaat aaj se 2,000 varsh purv europe tathaa abissinia main bheja jaataa tha. samrat ashok ke kaal main ispaat ke upkaran anek vishesh kaaryon main prayukt hote the. iraniyon tathaa arabon ne ispaat par pani chadhaane kii kala ko bharat se hi siikhaa. charak ke samay main lohe kaa aushadhi ke rup main bhi upyog hota tha. us samay do prakaar ke lohe kaa varnan aayaa he : kaly or tikshaise, arthath louh churna tathaa morcha . morache kaa prayog raktakshinata ke upachaar main hota tha. ashm kasis yaa pheras sulphate tathaa makshik kaa upyog anek rogon main bataaya gaya he.
 loh kaa upyog kuch anya deshon main bhi pracheen kaal se gyaat he. pracheen misr, aisyria, yunaan tathaa rome main log lohe kaa upyog karte the. europe kii sarvapratham vaat bhatthi san 1350 main germany main bani thi. atharahavin shatabdi main coke kaa upyog prarambh hone se lohe ke udyog main bahut vruddhi hui. unnisvin shatabdi main ispaat banane kii do mukhya vidhiyaan, besimar tathaa cemens martin prakram, nikaali gai.
 prithvi kaa crode loh dhaatu kaa bana he, parantu upree sataha par duusare tatwon dwaara abhikriya ke falswaroop loh ke yogic hi milte hai. prithvi kii upree sataha par loh ke yogic prachur maatra main upasthit hai. inaki maatra anya tatwon kii tulana main chauthe sthaan par he. loh do mukhya roopon main paaya jaataa he : magnetic, lo3ou4 . or hematite lo2 ou3 . magnetic kaala crystalia khanij padaarth he, jisamen teevr chumbakeey gun hote hai. hematite praaia: jal dwaara hydrate lymonite, lo2 ou3. ha2 ou banane ke kaaran crystalia rup main kam miltaa he. shuddh hematite ke crystal gahare bhure, yaa kaale rang ke hote hai. jinamein laal dhaariyaan padi rahati hai. isamen nirbal chumbakeey gun hote hai. praaia: loh ke ayask main yah donon rup vibhinn maatra main vartmaan rahete he. kuch ayskon main pheras carbenit bhi upasthit rahata he. kabhi-kabhi loh makshik ke rup main bhi paaya jaataa he, jo pheras sulphide he. isko jalaane par phero ferick yaa ferick oxide, bachataa he tathaa sulfur dioxide mukt ho jaataa he.
 bharat main jharkhand, chhattisgarh, urisa tathaa mysore main lohe kii mukhya khaane he.
 lohe ke ayask lagbhag sab kshetron main paae jate hai. britain main yarkshir or uttari midland main, germany ke uttari samudri kinare par tathaa sweden main lohe ke uttam ayask he. uttari america ke superior jhil ke kshetra par america kaa vishaal ispaat udyog nirbhar karata he.
 loh ayask ko sarvapratham bhunkar jal vaashp aadi dur karte hai tathaa carbonate evam sulphide kaa oxikaran kar dete hai. is ayask kaa apchayan coke dwaara ek bhatthi main karte hai, jise vaatya bhatthi kehete hai. ayask ko calcium carbonate athwa magnesium carbonate, silica tathaa coke ke saath milakar, bhatthi ke upree chhidr se, bhatthi main pravesh karte hai. neeche ke chhidron se garam vaayu ko upar kii ore pravaahit kiya jaataa he. andar kii prakriya dwaara gas baahar nikalati he or drav loh tathaa dhaatumal jamaa ho jate hai, jinhen samay samay par nikaala ja saktaa he. bhatthi main honevaali mukhya prakriyaayein nimna samikarnon dwaara pradarshit kii ja sakati hai :

 loha mukhyat: do or teen sanyojaktaa ke yogic banaata he. do sanyojaktaa ke pheras,, aayan kaa vilyan halke hare rang kaa he. vaayu ke oxigen dwaara uskaa oxikaran ho jaataa he. teen sanyojaktaa ke ferick,, aayan kaa amleey vilyan pile rang kaa rahata he. pheras tathaa ferick donon aayan anek jatil yogic banaate hai. inke atirikt inke anek chilette yogic bhi gyaat hai.
 loh ke chaar sanyojaktaa ke parafariete, or chah sanyojaktaa ke feret, yogic bhi gyaat hai. ye kshareey avasthaa main prabal oxikarakon dwaara banate hai. ye asthaayee yogic hai or bahut kam maatra main banaae ja sakate hai.
