 khaasi ek janajati he jo bharat ke meghalaya, assam tathaa bangladesh ke kuch kshetron main nivas karte hai. ye khaasi tathaa jayantiya kii pahadiyon main rahanevaali ek maatrikulmulak janajati he. inkaa rang kaala mishrit pilaa, naak chapati, munh chaudaa tathaa sughad hota he. ye log hrishtpusht or swabhaavat: parishramee hote he. stri tathaa purush donon sir par bade bade bal rakhte hai, nirdhan log sir mundwa lete hai.
 khaasiyon kii visheshta unka matrumulak parivaar he. vivaah hone par pati sasuraal main rahata he. paramparanusaar purush kii vivahpurv kamai par maatrupariwaar kaa or vivahottar kamai par patniparivaar kaa adhikaar hota he. vanshavali nari se chalati he or sampatti kii swamini bhi vahi he. sanyukt parivaar kii sangrakshika kanishtha putree hoti he. ab kuch khaasiye shilong aadi main sanyukt parivaar se alag vyapaar, naukari aadi krushitar vritti bhi karne lage hai. paramparaagat paariwarik jaaydaad bechnaa nishiddh he. vivaah ke liye koi vishesh rasm nahin he. ladki or mata pita kii sahmati hone par yuvak sasuraal main aana janaa shuru kar detaa he or santan hote hi vah sthaayee rup se vahi rahane lagata he. sambandhviched bhi aksar saraltapurvak hote rahete hai. santan par pita kaa koi adhikaar nahin hota.
 khaasiyon main ishwar kii kalpana hote hue bhi keval upadevataaon kii puja hoti he. kuch khaasiyon ne kaali or mahadev jaise hindu devdeviyon ko apana liya he. rog hone par ye log oshadhi kaa upyog na kar sambandhit devtaa ko bali dwaara prasann karte hai. shav kaa dah kiya jaataa he or mrityu ke turant baad kaag kii or kabhi kabhi bail yaa gaay kii bhi bali di jaati he. mrityuparant maheenon tak karmkand kaa silsilaa chalata rahata he or ant main avashshti asthiyon ko parivaar-samadhishaala main rakhte samay bail kii bali di jaati he or is avasar par teen chaar din tak nritygan tathaa daavatein hoti hai. khaasiyon kaa vishwaas he ki jinka antyeshti sanskaar vidhiwat sampann hota he unki atmaaen ishwar ke udyaan main nivas karti hai, anyathaa pashu-pakshi banakar prithvi par gumti hai.
 khaasia khetihar hai or dhan ke atirikt naarangi, paan tathaa supaari kaa utpaadan karte hai. ye log kapadaa bunana bilkul nahin jaanate or etatsambandhi aavashyaktaa baahar se puri karte he.
 khaasia anekanek shakhaon main vibhakt hai. khaasi, sinteng, waar or linggam, unki chaar mukhya shakhaayen hai. inke bich paraspar vivahasambandh hota he. keval apane kul yaa kabeeley main vivahasambandh nishiddh he.
 prayek kabeeley main rajvansh, purohit, mantri tathaa jan saamaanya ye chaar shreniyan hai. kintu waar shakha main vishisht saamaajik shreniyan nahin hai. kabeeley ke sardaar yaa mantri sambandhit vishisht shrenee ke sadsy hi ban sakate hai. ek kabeeley main stri hi sarvocch shaasha hoti he or vah apane putr athwa bhaanje ko lingdoh banaakar uske dwaara shashan karti he.
 anek khaasiyon ne pichley dedh-do sau varsh main isai tathaa hindu dharm sweekaar kar liya he, phir bhi vibhinn mataavlambee ek hi parivaar ke sadsy hai. shilong khaasiyon ke kshetra main sthit he; falat: khaasiyon par bahari samskruti tathaa aadhunik sabhyataa kaa baraabar prabhaav pad rahaa hai. ab anek khaasiye vyapaar tathaa naukari or kuch pard likhakar adhyapaki evam vakaalat jaise peshey bhi karne lage hai.
