 1991-92 ke mausam main dakshin africa cricket team ne bharat kaa daura kiya. yah daura mahatvapurn tha kyonki yah dakshin africa kii pehali aadhikarik antarrashtriya cricket shrinkhalaa he kyonki 1970 main rangabhed niti ke chalate khel se nilamban ke baad se unka nilamban tha.
 is daure main bhartiya rashtriya team ke khilaaf teen ek divasiy antarrashtriya vanade maichon kii shrinkhalaa shaamil thi or dakshin africa dwaara kheli gai pehali aadhikarik ekadivasiy shrinkhalaa thi. bharat ne shrinkhalaa 2-1 se jiti, or shrinkhalaa ke purush sanjay manjrekar or dakshin africa ke kepler vesals the.
