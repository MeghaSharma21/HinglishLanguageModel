 anjanhari yaa 'bilani' yaa 'guaheri' rog ankhon kii upree yaa nichali parat par dane ke rup main halke laal rang main ubharta he. vaise to yah koi rog nahin he kintu is rog ke hone par rogi ko bahut pareshaani hoti he.
 bilani rog sankraman ke kaaran failata he. isamen sankramit hone vaale palkon ke baalo me choti si tel sravak granthiyaan hoti he. bilani rog main aankh main khujali va jalan hoti he jo baad main mawaad bhare daanon kaa rup le leti he.
 bilani rog main ubhare hue daanon kii sinkai kerani chahie taaki vah foot jaae. footne ke baad inhe garm pani main kapadaa bhigokar saaf kar lenaa chahie.
