 suvarnarekha yaa swarnarekha bharat ke jharkhand pradesh main bahane vaali ek pahari nadi he.
 yah ranchi nagar se 16 kilometre dakshin-pashchim main sthit nagadi gaanv main rani chuaan naamak sthaan se nikalati he or uttar purv kii ore badhati hui mukhya pathaar ko chhodkar prapaat ke rup main girti he. is prapaat ko hundru jalprapaat kehete hai. prapaat ke rup main girane ke baad nadi kaa bahaav purv kii ore ho jaataa he or manbhoom jile ke teen sangam binduon ke aage yah dakshin purv kii ore mudakar singhbhum main bahati hui uttar pashchim se midanapur jile main pravishti hoti he. is jile ke pashchimi bhubhag ke jangalon main bahati hui baleshwar jile main pahunchati he. yah purv pashchim kii ore tedi-medhi bahati hui baleshwar naamak sthaan par bengal kii khadi main girti he. is nadi kii kul lambaai 474 kilometre he or lagbhag 28928 varg kilometre kaa jal nikaas iske dwaara hota he. isaki pramukh sahaayak nadiyaan kanchi evam karkari hai. bharat kaa prasiddh evam pehla lohe tathaa ispaat kaa kaarkaana iske kinare sthapit hua. kaarkaane ke sansthaapak jamshed ji tata ke naam par basaa yahan kaa nagar jamshedpur yaa tatanagar kaha jaataa he. apane muhane se upar kii ore yah 16 mile tak deshi naavon ke liye naugamya he.
