 ek prakaar ke gorakhpanthi sadhu jinke sir par jataa or sharir par bhasma evam gerua vastra ho tathaa jo oon kii seli baandhte hon jisamen praaia: ghunghuru athwa ghanti lagi ho. bhiksha maangate samay ye log bahudhaa dariyaan khappar failaakar 'alakh alakh' pukaara karte hai or ek dwaar par adhik nahin ada karte .
 bharat ke pashchimottar pradeshon, visheshkar bikaner tathaa ambala jile ke ek prakaar ke sadhu jo apane ko alakhnami, alkhadhari yaa alakhgir kaha karte hai or kisi lalbeg kaa anuyaayi bhi batalaate hai jise ve shiv kaa avatar maanate hai. ye adhiktar dhedh jati ke hote hai, murtipuja main vishwaas nahin karte ore alakh agochar tatv kaa dhyaan karte hai. inke liye drishyman sansar ke atirikt paralok jaisaa koi sthaan nahin he or yaheen rahakar ye ahimsa, paropakaraadi kaa jeevanyaapan karnaa shreyaskar maanate hai. inke aadambaraheen jivan main oonch-neech kaa samjik bhed nahin he or na puja kii koi vistrut, vyavasthit vidhi hi he. ye topi ore mote kapade dhaaran karte hai or ek duusare se milane par 'alakh kaho' kaha karte hai tathaa vishuddh yogiyon ke rup main samaadrut hote hai.
 19veen shatabdi ke ek sadhu jo ayodhya, nepal or himalay kii taraaiyon main copine baandhe tathaa chimata liye bhraman karte ore bich-bich main akaash kii ore dekhakar chillaate hue 'alakhya alakhya' kehete rahete the. inhen 'alakhya swami' bhi kaha jaataa tha or ye ant tak katak ke nikatavarti parvatiy kumbhavartri jatiyon main dharmprachaarkaswaroop prasiddh the.
