 hun sen ek cambodian rajaneta hai, jo ki cambodia ke pradhanmantri tathaa cambodian piples party ke adhyaksh hai. ve 1985 se cambodia ke pradhanmantri hai, jiske kaaran ve vishwa ke sabase lambe kaarykaal tak pad par rahane vaale pradhanmantri hai, tathaa sabase lambe kaarykaal tak pad par rahane vaale sarkaar pramukh main se hai. ve 1979 se 1986 tathaa 1987 se 1990 tak desh ke videsh mantri bhi rahe hai. unki puri sammanarth oopaadhi samdech akka moha sena pedai techo hun sen he. janm ke samay unka naam hun bunal tha, khmer rouge se judane ke duusare hi varsh unhone apana naam badal kar hun sen kar liya.
 hun sen janvari 1985 main pehali baar pradhanmantri pad par asin hue, jab ek-daleey rashtriya asembli ne unhen chaan si kaa uttaradhikari ghoshit kiya tha. disambar 1984 main chaan si kii mrityu ke pashchaat unke pradhanmantri banane kaa rasta saafe ho gaya. unhone yah pad 1993 tak sanyukt raashtra samarthit chunaav hone tak sambhala, jisame kisi ko bahumat nahin mila. phunkinpek se aam sahmati ke pashchaat sen nordam ranariddh ke saath dwitiya pradhanmantri bane or 1997 ke takhtapalat tak is pad par rahe. 1998 ke chunaavon main unhone apni party sipipi ko vijay to dilaai magar phunkinpek ke saath gathbandhan sarkaar banani padi. hun sen tabase chah baar lagaataar pradhanmantri kaa kaarykaal sambhaal rahe hai. june 2015 main cheya sim kii mrityu ke pashchaat ve sipipi ke adhyaksh chune gayi. 2018 main vah bina vipaksh ke hue chunaav main ve chhathi baar pradhanmantri chune gayi. 
 sen kaa janm compong cham main ek kisaan parivaar main hua tha. vah apane pita hun neang kii kul chah santaanon main tisare the. unke pita compong cham kii ek wat main bhikshu the. baad main unhone desh ko francisiyon se aazaadee dilwane hetu ve swatantraaa aandolan main kud pade tathaa hun sen kii maan di yon se 1940 ke dashak main vivaah kiya. hun neang ke paitruk purvaj chini-cambodian mul ke bahut amir tathaa bade bhoomihaar the. hun neang ko bhi apni paitruk sampatti kaa kaaphi hissaa mila or ve apni jindagi kaaphi aaraam se vyateet kar rahe the, magar apharan cond ke kaaran unhen apni kaafii sampattiyaan bechni padi. sen ne apane parivaar ko 13 varsh kii hi avasthaa main nampenh sthit math vidhyalay main daakhila lene hetu chhod diya tha. jab lone nol ne 1970 main sihanuk se raktavihin takhtapalat ke madhyam se satta hathiyaa lee, tab ve khmer rouge se jud gayi. iske do varsh pashchaat hi unhone apana naam badal kar hun sen rakh liya. 
 unhone hun manet se vivaah kiya he jinase unhen chah santaanen kamsot, hun manet, mana, hun manith, hun maani, mali tathaa maalis hai. hun manet bristol vishvavidhyalay se arthshaastra main piechdi hai. sen ke kaarykaal ke dauraan unke sabhi putron ne badi bhoomika nibhaai he. unke bade bhai hun neng compong cham ke rajyapal rah chuke hai tathaa vartmaan main saansad hai.
 haalaanki hun sen kaa aadhikarik janmdin 4 april 1951 he, magar vaastavik 5 april 1952 he. apni maatrubhaasha khmer ke atirikt ve vietnami bhashaa bhi dharapravaah bol sakate hai. hun sen thodi bahut angreji bhi bol sakate hai magar saamanytah ve angreji media ke saamne khmer bolte hai jo interprator ke madhyam se anuvaadit hota he.
 bachapan main murga churaane ke falswaroop murge ne unki aankh main choch ghusaa di, is kaaran unki ek aankh nahin he.
