 manju qamaradullhi ek urdu naatakkaar, natya patakathakaar or shaayar the. unka janm 1908 main channapatna,karnataka main hua or unhone hyderabad deccan main apane karier kii shuruyaat kii. unhone saamaajik or aitihaasik mahatv ke vibhinn naatakon ko urdu main likhaa. unke naatakon main se adhikansh, rabindra bharti main manchan kiye gaye, jo hyderabad main kala pradarshan kaa pramukh centre he.hyderabad deccan ke saatven nizaam ke shasankaal ke dauraan unhone vibhinn naatakon kaa manchan unke atithi ke rup main kiya or sammanit bhi hue.
