 subhadra mahabharat ke pramukh nayak bhagwaan shrikrishna kii bahin thim,jo vasudev kii kanya or arjuna kii patni thim.krishna ke suzav par subhadra kaa vivaah arjuna se hua tha, abhimanyu inkaa hi putr tha. balram inkaa vivaah duryodhana se karnaa chaahate the, kintu krishna ke protsaahan se arjuna inhen dwarka se bhaga laae. subhadra ke putr abhimanyu mahabharat ke prasiddh yoddha the. puri, urisa main 'jagannatha kii yatra' main balram tathaa subhadra donon kii murtiyan bhagwaan shrikrishna ke saath-saath hi rahati hai.
