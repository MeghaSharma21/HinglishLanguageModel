 baba kanshiram ) bharat ke swatantraaa senaanee tathaa krantikari sahityakar the. unhone kavy se saamaajik, dharmik, raajnneetik, aarthik va saanskrithik shoshan ke khilaaf aawaaj uthaai thi. unhen 'pahari gandhi' ke naam se janaa jaataa he. unhone jansadharan kii bhashaa main chetana kaa sandesh diya. baba kanshi ram kii rachanaaon main janajagran kii pramukh dhaaraon ke atirikt chuaachhut unmoolan, harijan prem, dharm ke prati aasthaa, vishwabandhutav va maanav dharm ke darshan hote hai. baba kanshi ram ne angrej shaskaon ke viruddh vidroh ke geeton ke saath aam janata ke dukh dard ko bhi kavitaaon main vyakt kiya gaya.
 baba kanshiram kaa janm himachal pradesh main hua tha. saat varsh kii aayu main unka vivaah saraswati devi se hua. kintu unhone shiksha nahin chodi or apane gaanvan main hi apni puri shiksha lee. kuch hi dinon baad unke mata-pita kaa bhi dehant ho gaya. iske baad kaam kii talash main ve lahore aa gaye. yaheen unki bhent lala lajpat raay, lala hardayal, sardaar ajit singh, tathaa maulavee barktulla jaise krantikariyon se hui.
