 nirdeshaank: 24°35′N 80°50′E / 24.58°N 80.83°E / 24.58; 80.83chitrakoot dham mandakini nadi ke kinare par basaa bharat ke sabase pracheen tirthasthalon main ek he. uttar pradesh main 38.2 varg kilometre ke kshetra main failaa shant or sundar chitrakoot prakruti or ishwar kii anupam dein he. chaaron ore se vindhya parvat shrinkhalaaon or wanon se ghire chitrakoot ko anek aashcharyo kii pahari kaha jaataa he. mandakini nadi ke kinar bane anek ghat or mandir main puure saal shraddhaluon kaa aana-janaa laga rahata he.
 mana jaataa he ki bhagwaan ram ne sita or lakshmana ke saath apane vanvaas ke chaudaa varsho main gyaarah varsh chitrakoot main hi bitaye the. isi sthaan par rishi atri or sati ansuia ne dhyaan lagaaya tha. brahma, vishnu or mahesh ne chitrakoot main hi sati ansuia ke ghar janm liya tha.
 is pavitra parvat kaa kaaphi dharmik mahatv he. shraddhalu kamadgiri parvat kii 5 kilometre kii parikrama kar apni manokamanaayen purna hone kii kaamana karte hai. jangalon se ghire is parvat ke tal par anek mandir bane hue hai. chitrakoot ke lokapriy kamtanath or bharat milaap mandir bhi yaheen sthit he.
 ram ghat vah ghat he jahaan prabhu ram nity snaan kiya karte the l isi ghat par ram bharat milaap mandir he or isi ghat par goswami tulsidas ji kii pratimaa bhi he l mandakini nadi ke tat par bane ramghat main anek dharmik kriyalap chalate rahete hai. ghat main gerua vastra dhaaran kiye sadhu-santon ko bhajan or kirthan karte dekh bahut acha mahasoos hota he. shaam ko hone vaali yahaan kii aarti man ko kaaphi sukun pahunchaati he.
 ramghat se 2 kilometre kii duuri par mandakini nadi ke kinar janaki kund sthit he. janak putree hone ke kaaran sita ko janaki kaha jaataa tha. mana jaataa he ki janaki yahaan snaan karti thim. janaki kund ke sameep hi ram janaki raghuveer mandir or sankat mochan mandir he. 
 janaki kund se kuch duuri par mandakini nadi ke kinar hi yah shila sthit he. mana jaataa he ki is shila par sita ke pairon ke nishaan mudrit hai. kaha jaataa he ki jab vah is shila par khadi thim to jayant ne kaak rup dhaaran kar unhen chonch maari thi. is shila par ram or sita baithkar chitrakoot kii sundarata nihaarte the.
 sphatik shila se lagbhag 4 kilometre kii duuri par ghaney wanon se ghira yah ekaant ashram sthit he. is ashram main atri muni, anusuia, dattatreyay or durvasa muni kii pratimaa sthapit hai.
 nagar se 18 kilometre kii duuri par gupt godavari sthit hai. yahaan do gufaaen hai. ek gupha chaudee or oonchi he. pravesh dwaar sankara hone ke kaaran isamen aasaani se nahin ghusaa ja saktaa. gupha ke ant main ek chota talab he jise godavari nadi kaha jaataa he. duusari gupha lambii or sankari he jisase hameshaa pani bahata rahata he. kaha jaataa he ki is gupha ke ant main ram or lakshmana ne darbar lagaaya tha.
 pahari ke shikhar par sthit hanuman dhara main hanuman kii ek vishaal murti he. murti ke saamne talab main jharne se pani girtaa he. kaha jaataa he ki yah dhara shriram ne lanka dahan se aae hanuman ke aaraam ke liye banavaai thi. pahari ke shikhar par hi 'sita rasoi' he. yahaan se chitrakoot kaa sundar drushya dekha ja saktaa he.
 kaha jaataa he ki bhagwaan ram ke rajbhishek ke liye bharat ne bharat kii sabhi nadiyon se jal ekatrit kar yahaan rakhaa tha. atri muni ke paraamarsh par bharat ne jal ek coop main rakh diya tha. isi coop ko bharat coop ke naam se janaa jaataa he. bhagwaan ram ko samarpit yahaan ek mandir bhi he.
 chitrakoot kaa najdiki vimaansthal elahabaad he. khajuraho chitrakoot se 185 kilometre dur he. chitrakoot main bhi hawaii pattee banakar taiyaar he lekin yahaan se udaanen abhi shuru nahin hui hai. lakhanu or elahabaad hawaii addon se bhi chitrakoot pahunchaa ja saktaa he. elahabaad or lakhanu se bas or trainen lagaataar uplabdh hai.
 chitrakoot se 8 kilometre kii dur karvi nikatatam railway stesan he. elahabaad, jabalpur,banda, dilli, jhaansi, haavda, agra,mathura, lakhanu, kanpur,gwalior,jhaansi,raipur, katni, mughalsarai,varanasi banda aadi shaharon se yahaan ke liye railgadiyaan chalati hai. iske alaava shivarampur railway stesan par utakar bhi basen or tu whealer liye ja sakate hai. shivarampur railway stesan kii chitrakoot se duuri 4 kilometre he.
 chitrakoot ke liye elahabaad,banda, jhaansi, mahoba, kanpur, chhatarpur,satna, faijabad, lakhanu, maihar aadi shaharon se niyamit bas sevaayen hai. dilli se bhi chitrakoot ke liye bas seva uplabdh he. shivarampur se bhi basen or tu whealer uplabdh hai yahaan se chitrakoot kii duuri 4 kilometre he.
