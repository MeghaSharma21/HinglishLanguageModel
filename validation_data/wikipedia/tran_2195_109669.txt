 vikaswad kii dharna he ki samay ke saath jeevon main kramik-parivartan hote hai. is siddhanth ke vikaas kaa lambaa itihaas he. 18veen shatee tak pashchimi jeevvaigyanik chintan main yah vishwaas jad jamaaye tha ki prayek jeev main kuch vilakshan gun hai jo badale nahin ja sakate. ise ishensielism kaha jaataa he. punarjagran kaal main yah dharna badalane lagi.
 19veen shatee ke armbh main lamark ne apana vikaswad kaa siddhanth diya jo kram-vikaas se sambandhit pratham purnat: nirmit vaigyanik siddhanth tha.
