 purvi gangavansh bhartiya upmahadweep kaa ek hindu rajvansh tha. unhone kaling ko rajdhani banaaya. unke rajya ke antargat vartmaan samay kaa sampoorn urisa to tha hi, iske saath hi pashchim bengal, andhra pradesh tathaa chhattisgarh ke bhi kuch bhaag the.inkaa shashan 11veen shatabdi se 15veen shatabdi tak rahaa. unki rajdhani kaa naam 'kalinganagar' tha jo vartmaan samay main andhra pradesh ke shrikakulam jila kaa shrimukhalingam he. pehle yah urisa ke ganjam jile main tha. purvi gangavansh ke shaasha konark ke surya mandir ke nirmaan ke liye prasiddh hai.
