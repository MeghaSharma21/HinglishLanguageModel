 hindi upanyaas kaa armbh shrinivasadas ke "parikshaguru' se mana jaataa he. hindi ke aarmabhik upanyaas adhiktar aiyaari or tilsmi kism ke the. anudit upanyaason main pehla saamaajik upanyaas bhaaratendu harishchandra kaa "purnaprakash' or chandraprabha naamak marathi upanyaas kaa anuwaad tha. armbh main hindi main kai upanyaas banglaa, marathi aadi se anuvaadit kiye gaye.
 hindi main saamaajik upanyaason kaa aadhunik arth main sutarapaat premchand se hua. premchand pehle urdu main likhte the, baad main hindi kii ore mude. aapke "sevasadan', "rangabhumi', "kayakalp', "gaban', "nirmala', "godaan', aadi prasiddh upanyaas hai, jinamein gramin vaataavaran kaa uttam chithran he. charitrachitran main premchand gandhi ji ke "hridayaparivartan' ke siddhant ko maanate the. baad main unki rujhaan samaajwaad kii ore bhi hui, aisa jaan padataa he. kul milakar unke upanyaas hindi main aadhunik saamaajik sudhaarwadi vichaardhaara kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai.
 jaishankar prasad ke "kankal' or "titali' upanyaason main bhinn prakaar ke samaajon kaa chithran he, parantu style adhik kaavyatmak he. premchand kii hi style main, unke anukaran se vishvambharnath sharma kowshik, sudarshan, pratapanarayan shrivastav, bhagwatiprasad vaajapeyi aadi anek lekhakon ne saamaajik upanyaas likhe, jinamein ek prakaar kaa aadarshonmukh yatharthwaad adhik tha. parantu pandeya bechan sharma "ugr', rishabhcharan jain, chaturasen shastri aadi ne faransisi dhang kaa yatharthwaad or prakrutvaad apanaaya or samaaj kii buraaiyon kaa dambhasphot kiya. is shelli ke upanyaaskaaron main sabase safal rahe "chitralekha' ke lekhak bhagwaticharan verma, jinke "tede medhe raste' or "bhuule bisare chitra' bahut prasiddh hai. upendranath ashk kii "girti deevaaren' kaa bhi is samaaj kii buraaiyon ke chitranwali rachanaaon main mahatvapurn sthaan he. amritlal nagar kii "buund or samudr' isi yathartwaadee style main aage badhkar aanchalikta milaanevaala ek shreshth upanyaas he. siyaaramsharan gupt kii nari' kii apni alag visheshta he.
 manovaigyanik upanyaas janendrakumar se shuru hue. "parakh', "sunita', "kalyani' aadi se bhi adhik aap ke "tyaagptr' ne hindi main bada mahatvapurn yogadan diya. janendra ji darshanik shabdaawalee main adhik ulajh gaye. manovishleshan main sa. hi. vatsyayan "agyeya' ne apane "shekhar : ek jeevani', "nadi ke dweep', "apane apane ajanabi' main uttarottar gaharaai or sookshmata upanyaaskala main dikhaai. is style main likhanewaali bahut kam milte hai. saamaajik vikrutiyon par ilachandra joshi ke "sanyasi', "pret or chaaya', "jahaaj kaa panchi' aadi main acha prakaash daalaa gaya he. is style ke upanyaaskaaron main dharmaveer bharti kaa "suraj kaa saatvaan ghoda' or naresh mehta kaa "vah pathbandhu tha' uttam uplabdhiyaan hai.
 aitihaasik upanyaason main hajariprasad dvivedi kaa "banbhatta kii aatmakatha' ek bahut manoranjak kathaprayog he jisamen pracheen kaal ke bharat ko moort kiya gaya he. vrundavanlaal verma ke "maharani lakshmi bai', "mriganayani' aadi main aitihaasikta to bahut he, rochakata bhi he, parantu kavyamyata dvivedi ji jaisi nahin he. rahul sankrutyaayan, rangey raghav aadi ne bhi kuch sansmarneey aitihaasik upanyaas die hai.
 yathartwaadee style saamaajik yatharthwaad kii ore mudi or "divya' or "jhootha sach' ke lekhak bhootpurv krantikari yashpal or "balchanamaa' ke lekhak nagarjun is dhara ke uttam pratinidhi hai. kahin kahin inaki rachanaaon main prachaar kaa aagrah bad gaya he. hindi kii naveenatam vidha aanchalik upanyaason kii he, jo shuru hoti he fanishwarnath "renu' ke "mailaa anchal' se or usamen ab kai lekhak haath aajamaa rahe hai, jaise rajendra yadav, mohan rakesh, shailesh matiani, rajendra avasthi, manahar chouhan, shivani ityadi.
 hindi ke moulik kathasahitya kaa armbh isha allaah khan kii "rani ketki kii kahani" se hota he. bhartiya vaataavaran main nirmit is katha main loukik parampara ke spasht tatv dikhaai dete hai. khan sahab ke pashchaat pan. baalkrishna bhatta ne "nutan brahmacharee" or "sau ajaan or ek sujan" naamak upanyaason kaa nirmaan kiya. in upanyaason kaa vishay samajasudhar he.
 bhaaratendu tathaa unke sahyogiyon ne raajnneetijna yaa samajasudharak ke rup main likhaa. bawoo devakinandan sarvapratham aise upanyaslekhak the jinhone vishuddh upanyaslekhak ke rup main likhaa. unhone kahani kehane ke liye hi kahani kahi. vah apane yug ke ghaat pratighaat se prabhavit the. hindi upanyaas ke kshetra main khatri ji ne jo parampara sthapit kii vah ekadam nai thi. premchand ne bhaaratendu dwaara sthapit parampara main ek nai kadi jodi. iske vipareet babu devakinandan khatri ne ek nai parampara sthapit kii. ghatnaon ke aadhaar par unhone kahaniyon kii ek aisi shrunkhlaa jodi jo kahin tuutati najar nahin aati. khatri ji kii kahani kehane kii kshamta ko ham ishankrut "rani ketki kii kahani' ke saath saraltapurvak sambhddha kar sakate hai.
 vastav main kathasahitya ke itihaas main khatri ji kii "chandrakanta' kaa pravesh ek mahatvapurn ghatana he. yah hindi kaa pratham moulik upanyaas he. khatri ji ke upanyaas sahitya main bhartiya samskruti kii spasht chhap dekhne ko milti he. maryaada aapke upanyaason kaa praan he.
 upanyaas sahitya kii vikaasyatra main pan. kishorilaal goswami ke mahatvapurn hastakshar hai. yah upanyaason kii disha main ghar karke baith gaye. aadhunik jivan kii vishmataaon ke chitra aapke jasusi upanyaason main paae jate hai. goswami ji ke upanyaas sahitya main vaasana kaa jhina paradaa praaia: sabhi kahin padaa hua he.
 jasusi upanyaslekhakon main babu gopalram gahamri kaa naam mahatvapurn he. gahamri ji ne apane upanyaason kaa nirmaan swayan anubhav kii hui ghatnaon ke aadhaar par kiya he, isliye kathavstu par pramanikta kii chhap he. kathavstu hatya yaa laash ke paae jane ke vishyon se sambandhit he. janajivan se sampark hone ke kaaran upanyaason kii bhashaa main gramin prayog praaia: milte hai.
 hindi ke aarmabhik upanyaslekhakon main babu harikrishna johar kaa tilsmi tathaa jasusi upanyaas lekhakon main mahatvapurn sthaan he. tilsmi upanyaason kii disha main johar ne babu devakinandan khatri dwaara sthapit upanyasparampara ko viksit karne main mahatvapurn yoga diya he. aadhunik jivan kii vishmaaon evam sabhy samaaj ke yatharth jivan kaa pradarshan karne ke liye hi babu harikrishna johar ne jasuri upanyaason kaa nirmaan kiya he. "kaala baagh' or "gavaah gaayab' aapke is disha main mahatvapurn upanyaas hai..
 hindi ke aarmabhik upanyaason kaa nirmaan loksahitya kii aadharshila par hua. kautuhal or jigyaasa ke bhav ne ise viksit kiya. aadhunik jivan kii vishmataaon ne jasusi upanyaason kii katha ko jivan ke yatharth main pravesh karaayaa. asaty par satya kii sadaiv hi vijay hoti he yah siddhant bhartiya samskruti kaa centrebindu he. hindi ke aarmabhik upanyaason main yah pravriti mul rup se pai jaati he.
