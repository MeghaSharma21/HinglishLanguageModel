 khazar madhyakaalin uresia kii ek turki jati thi jinka vishaal samrajya aadhunik rus ke europea hisse, pashchimi kazakhstan, purvi ukraine, azarbejaan, uttari cocks, jorgia, kremia or uttarpurvi turki par vistrut tha. inaki rajdhani volga nadi ke kinare basaa atil shahar tha. khazar logon kii kagaanath san 448 se 1048 tak chali. isamen kai dharmon ke log or, turki jatiyon ke alaava, urali, slavi or anya jatiyaan bhi rahati thim. khazar kagaanath resham maarg par ek mahatvapurn padaav tha or dakshin-pashchimi asia ko uttari europe se jodne vaali ek mukhya kadi thi.
 madhyakaal main khazar shashan ne yahudee dharm ko apana liya tha, yadhyapi stapi ke anya turki jatiyon ne sunni islam apanaaya tha.
 bahut se vidwaanon kaa maananaa he ki 'khazar' shabd turki bhashaaon ke 'geser' yaa 'keser' shabd se aayaa he or iskaa matlab 'kanaabadosh' yaa 'ghummakad' hota he. 'khazar' shabd main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
