 vayuyan ke nodak, jinhen airscru bhi kehete hai, hawaii jahaazon par lage aise pankhenuma yantra hote hai jinase vimaanon ke injanon dwaara utpann ghurnan kaa prayog hawa ko peeche kii ore phenkakar vimaan ko aage kii taraf dhakelne ke liye kiya jaataa he. beete dinon main hawaii nodakon ko lakdi se banaaya jaataa tha or baad main inhen dhaatu se banaaya jane laga. aadhunik yug main yah mishrit padaarthon se banate hai. nodakon ke pankhons kaa rujhaav ek mahatvapurn pahalu hota he or hawa usee ke anusaar vimaan ko aage kii taraf dhakelti he. achche nodakon ke pankhe mude hue hote hai, kyonki sarvaadhik dhakka praapt karne ke liye yah aavashyak he ki hawa-bahaav kii disha ke saath unka kone nodak ke centre main alag or pankhons ke ant main alag ho. vaigyanik adhyayan kii drushti se nodakon ke upar se hote vaayu pravaah or vimaan ke pankh ke upar se hote pravaah main bahut saamantaayein hai.
