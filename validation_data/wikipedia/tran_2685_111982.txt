 ek dhruviya kaksha, vah kaksha he jisamen ek kritrim upgrah kisi pind kii prayek parikrama par ek puure chakkar main uske donon dhruvon ke upar yaa lagbhag upar se gujarata he | is kaaran bumadhyarekha se iskaa jhukaav 90 degree hota he | saamanytah yah pind prithvi jaisaa yaa sambhawatah surya ke jaisaa hota he | dhruviya bhu-samakalik kaksha ke vishesh maamle ko chhodkar, dhruviya kaksha main upgrah apane prayek chakkar main bumadhyarekha ke upar ek bhinn deshaantar par se gujarata he | 
 dhruviya kakshaon kaa upyog aksar prithvi ke maanchitran, prithvi prekshan or tohi upgrahon ke saath hi saath kuch mausam upgrahon ke liye bhi liye kiya jaataa he | iridium upgrah samooh bhi doorsanchaar sevaayen uplabdh karaane ke liye ek dhruviya kaksha kaa upyog karata he | is kaksha ke liye nuksaan yah he ki prithvi kii sataha par koi ek sthaan aisa nahin he jahaan se is dhruviya kaksha ke upgrah se lagaataar sampark kiya ja saktaa ho | 
 nikat-dhruviya kaksha upgrahon ke liye ek surya-samakalik kaksha kaa chayan karnaa mahaj saamaanya baat he: jiskaa arth he ki ek ke baad ek kakshiya guzaaren ke din par ek hi sthaneey samay hota he | vayumandaleey taapmaana ke sudur samvedan jaise anuprayogon ke liye yah vishesh rup se mahatvapurn ho saktaa he | parikrama karte hue upgrah dwaara sthaneey samay ke saath saamanjasy aavashyak he jisamen samay ke saath parivartan ho saktaa he | kisi die gaye guzaaren par ek hi sthaneey samay rakhane ke liye, kaksha ke liye yah vaanchhneeya he ki vah jitani sambhav ho choti se choti ho, kehane kaa taatpary he jitani sambhav ho sake nimna ho | haalanki, kuch sau kilometre kii bahut nimna kakshaon main teji se vayumandal se avrodh ke kaaran kshay hoga | prayog main laai jane vaali saamaanya unchai lagbhag 1000 kimi he, yah lagbhag 100 minuton kii ek kakshiya avadhi nirmit karti he |surya ke taraf kii aadhi kaksha main to keval 50 minute lagate hai, is dauraan din ke sthaneey samay main bahut bhinnata nahin hoti he | 
 jaise jaise prithvi varsh ke dauraan surya ke chaaron ore gumti jaati he vaise vaise surya-samakalik kaksha banaae rakhane ke liye, upgrah kii kaksha ko thik usee samaan dar par ayan karnaa chahie | sidhe dhruvon ke upar se guzarane vaale upgrahon ke liye, yah nahin hoga | lekin prithvi ke bumadhyarekhiy ubhaar ke kaaran, ek maamuli kone par jhuki kaksha balaghurna ke adheen aynansh kaa kaaran banati he, yah pataa chalaa he ki dhruv se lagbhag 8 degree kaa ek kone ek 100 minute kii kaksha main vaanchit aynansh nirmit karata he |
