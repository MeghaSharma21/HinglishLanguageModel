 seetaram agrahari 'prakaash' nepal ke kavi, sahityakar evam prakhyat patrkaar hai. ve nepal ke sabase puraane samachar patr gorkhaptr ke pradhan sanmpadak hai.
 inkaa janm san 1957 main nepal ke duhabi, sunasri main hua. aapaki mata kii naam swargeey ganpati devi or pita kaa naam swargeey lakhanram agrahari hai. inhone raajnneeti vigyaan main em. e., hindi main em. e. or bi.e. kiya he. aapane indian institude of maas communication, nai dilli se diploma in jarnalism kiya he or thomson foundation, landan se patrakarita sambandhit shikshan praapt kiya hai.
 main hi kosi, main hi kashimain kawa kailash remain sijiad, sindhu, nile, mississipicyon man main avishvaas re!
 jinda he isliye hai jinda aadmi kii tarahamurda hote to kab ke mar gaye hotemere bijmon ne jhele he jamaane ke zakhmavarana ham to kab ke ujaad gaye hote
 seetaram agrahari lagbhag teen dashak se lekhan va patrakarita se jude hai. inaki hajaaro lekh, rachanaaen vibhinn pratishthit patr patrikao main prakashit ho chuke he. varsh 1977 main seetaram prakaash kii pehali katha sangrah 'kathadweep' prakashit hua. inkaa kavy sangrah 'jiye swabhimaan bhi' farvari 1996 main nepal-bharat saanskruthik samaaj, kathmandu dwaara prakashit hua. april 2011 main seetaram agrahari kii duusari kavita sangrah 'tumhiin se kahata hun' prakashit hua, jiske liye inako rajarshi janak pratibha puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. inhone spain, italy, singapore, china, malaysia, hadkad va anya kai deshon kaa bhraman kiya hai.
 nepal ke sabase puraani samachar patr 'gorkhaptr dainik' kaa pradhan sampadak hone kaa gaurav praapt karne vaale seetaram agrahari patrakarita se pichley tees saal se jude he. nepali, hindi, matholi or bhojpuri main inke lekh, rachanaae akasar prakashit hoti rahati he. wein yuva manch va manoram apsara ke purv pradhan sampadak or nepal patrkaar mahasang, gorkhaptr shakha ke purv adhyaksh rah chuke hai. agrahari nepal khelkud patrkaar manch ke sansthaapak sanyojak or sports jarnalist forum ke sansthaapak adhyaksh hai.
