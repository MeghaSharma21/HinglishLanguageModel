 ogustein lui kosi france ke ganitagya the. ve ganitiya vishleshan ke agradoot the. iske alaava unhone anant shreniyon ke abhisar/apsaar, avakal sameekaran, saaranik, prayikta evam ganitiya bhautikee main bhi ullekhneey dogadan diya. ve france kii vigyaan academy ke sadsy tathaa 'echol polytechnic' ke professor bhi the. kaushi ke naam par jitne pramayon evam sankalpanaaon kaa namakaran hua he, utana kisi or ganitagya ke naam par nahin. unhone apane jeevankaal main 800 shodhpatr tathaa paanch pathyapustaken likhi. 
 inkaa janm 21 agast 1789 i. ko paris main hua. 1810 i. main ve ekol se ejeeniyar banakar shairbur chale gaye; vahaan laplas kii mechaanik selest or lagranj kii fonkyonjanalithik kaa adhyayan karte rahe. teen varsh pashchaat swasth ke kaaran ye paris laute or laplas or lagranj ke aagrah par ejiniyari tyaag ganit ko apanaaya.23 march 1857 i. ko unka dehanth hua.
