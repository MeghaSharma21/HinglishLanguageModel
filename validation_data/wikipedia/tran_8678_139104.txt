 
 scandium ek rasinic tatv he. yah aavart sarini ke di-khand kaa sadsy he or apane shuddh rup main shwet-chaandi jaisaa rang rakhata he. aitihaasik najariye se ise lanthanide samooh or yttrium ke saath ise durlabh mrudaa tatv samooh main shaamil kiya gaya he. scandium kii khoj san 1879 main scandinavia main kuch khanijon main kii gai thi or ise kshetra se iskaa naam padaa. zamin main yah aksar durlabh mrudaa khanijon main or uranium ke saath rasinic yogicon main miltaa he. yah itnaa durlabh he ki iskaa kul vishvabhar kaa vaarshik vyapaar keval 50 kilogram he.
