 malotsarg pranali ek niskriy jaivik pranali he jo jeevon ke bheetar se atirikt, anaavashyak yaa khatarnaak padaarthon ko hataati he, taaki jeev ke bheetar homeyostasis ko banaae rakhane main madad mil sake or sharir ke nuksaan ko roka ja sake. yah chayapachay ke apshisht utpaadon or saath hi saath anya taral or gasiy apshisht ke unmoolan ke liye jimmedaar he. chunki adhikansh swasth rup se kaary karne vaale ang chayapachay sambandhi or anya apshisht utpaadit karte hai, sampurna jeev is pranali ke kaary karne par nirbhar karata he; haalanki, keval ve ang jo vishesh rup se utsarjan prakriya ke liye hote hai unhen malotsarg pranali kaa ek hissaa mana jaataa he.
 chunki isamen kai aise kaary shaamil hai jo ek duusare se keval upree taur par sambandhit hai, iskaa upyog aamtaur par sharir rachana yaa prakaarya ke or adhik aupchaarik vargeekaran main nahin kiya jaataa he.
 jeev ke chayapachay or taral vifaille apshisht ko or saath hi atirikt jal ko nikaalata he.
 prayek gurde ke bheetar anumaanit das lakh sookshm nefron hote hai. khun kaa chanan in kshetron ke bheetar hi hota he. prayek nefron main vahikaaon kaa ek guchha hota he jise glomerules kehete hai. ek cap ke aakaar kii thili prayek glomerules ko ghere rahati he jise bomens capsule kehete hai. jo rakt, glomerules ke madhyam se behate hai ve bahut dabaav main hote hai. isi vajah se bomens capsule main glomerules, pani, glucose or uria pravesh kar jaati he. rakt main safed rakt koshikaayen, laal rakt koshikaayen or protein rahete hai. jaise-jaise rakt vahikaaon ke madhyam se rakt kaa bahaav jari rahata he, vah gurde kii choti nalee ke aasapaas lipat jaataa he. is dauraan, punah avchushan hota he. glucose or rasayan, jaise potacium, sodium, hydrogen magnesium or calcium rakt main punah avchooshit ho jate hai. nispandan ke dauraan hataaya gaya lagbhag puraa pani punah avchushan charan ke dauraan rakt main waapas laut aata he. gurde hamaare sharir main taral padaarth kii maatra ko niyantrit karte hai. ab nefron main keval apshisht batch jaataa he. is apshisht ko mutr kaha jaataa he isamen uria, pani or acarbonic lavan hote hai. shuddh rakt un nason main chalaa jaataa he jo rakt ko gurde se waapas dil main lekar jate hai.
 paribhaasha ke anusaar utsarjan niskriy hota he or gurdon dwaara filter kiye hue chayapachay apshisht se nipatata he. haalanki paseene main chayapachay apshisht ke kuch ansh hote hai, pasinaa, srav kii ek sakriy prakriya hoti he naa ki utsarjan kii, vishesh rup se taapmaana niyantran or pheromone jari karne ke liye. isliye, malotsarg pranali ke ek bhaag ke rup main sabase anukool parivesh main isaki bhoomika nunatam he. vishesh rup se, twachaa ek drav apshisht kaa srav karti he jise pasinaa kehete hai.
 jeevon ke fefde or gil, shwasan ke niyamit hisse ke rup main lagaataar rakt se gasiy apshisht ko nikaalte rahete hai.
 yah ridh vaale praaniyon kii malotsarg pranali main praathmik ang he. gurde, ridd kii haddi ke donon ore peeth ke nichle hisse ke paas sthit hote hai. ve mukhya rup se rakt ko saafe karne ke liye jimmedaar hai jisamen shaamil he chayapachay se nitrogen apshisht, lavan or anya atirikt khanijon or atirikt pani.
 jeev shouch ke dauraan guda ke madhyam se pachan nalee se, thos, ardh thos yaa taral apshisht padaarth nikaalte hai. brihadantra kii deevaaron par peshi sankuchan kii lehren jinhen kramakunchan kehete hai, mel ko pachan nalee ke madhyam se malashay kii ore dhakelta he. apachya bhojan bhi is maarg se nikal saktaa he; is prakriya ko ijeshan kaha jaataa he.
 maanav sharir rachana vigyaan main, mutranli mansapeshiyon se bani vahiniyaan hoti hai jo gurdon se mutr ko mutrashay main dhakelti hai. vayaskon main, mutranliyaan aamtaur par 25-30 semi lambi hoti he. manushyon main, mutranliyaan prayek gurde ke madhyavarti kshetra par vrikk shroni se nikalati hai or aage ja kar soas major pasli ke saamne mutrashay kii ore utar jaati he. mutranliyaan, shronifalak dhamniyon ke vibhaajan ke paas pedu seemaa ko paar karti he. yah "pelviureteric jankshan" gurde kii pattharii sthirikaran ke liye ek aam sthaan he . mutranliyaan, pedu kii parshv deevaaron par peeche or nichle kii ore se guzarati he. iske baad ve peeche kii ore se, vesicoyuteric jankshan par mutrashay main pravesh karne ke liye upree madhya main vakr ho jaati hai, yah mutrashay kii diwaar ke bheetar kuch centimetre tak rahati he. mutr ke parshv bahaav ko uretarvasical valve naamak vaalvon dwaara roka jaataa he. mahilaon main, mutranliyaan mutrashay ke raste main mesommetrium main se paar hoti hai.
 mutrashay ek aisa ang he jo mutr ko nikaalane se pehle gurdon dwaara utsarjit mutr ko ikattha karata he. mutrashay, ek khokhla, peshiyukt or prasarya ang hota he jo pedu kii jameen par sthit hota he. mutr, mutrvahini ke madhyam se mutrashay main pravesh karti he or mutrmarg ke raste se nikal jaati he.
 garbh kii drushti se, mutrashay, sinus mutrajananangi se vyutpann he or yah armbh main aparaposhika ke saath nirantar rahati he. purushon main, mutrashay kaa aadhaar, malashay or jaghan sahavardhan ke bich sthit he. yah prostate ke aage rahata he or roctovasical dwaara malashay se alag kiya hua he. mahilaon main, mutrashay or garbhashay ke neeche or yoni ke aage sthit he. yah garbhashay se vesicouterine ekskaveshan ke dwaara alag kiya hua he. shishuon or bacchon main, mutrashay khaali hone par bhi pet main hota he.
 sharir rachana main, mutrmarg ek nalee he jo mutrashay ko sharir ke bahari bhaag ke saath jodta he. donon hi lingon main mutrmarg kaa ek utsarjan kaary hota he or vah he mutr ko baahar nikaalnaa or saath hi purushon main iskaa ek prajnan kaary bhi he, jo he yon gatividhi ke dauraan veery ke liye ek maarg ke rup main.
 bahya mutrmarg dabaanewaala yantra ek dharidaar mansapeshi hoti he jo mutr par svaichhik niyantran ko sambhav banaata he.
 sabase pehle, rakt abhivahi dhamni ke madhyam se glomerules naamak koshikaon, se boman capsule main pahunchataa he. boman capsule rakt ko uske mukhya samagriyon - bhojan or apshisht se nichod kar alag karata he. is nichodan prakriya ke baad, rakt apni aavashyaktaa ke anusaar bhojan ke poshak tatwon ko lene ke liye phir se waapas aata he. apshisht tab ekatra karne vaali vahini, vrikk pedu or mutranli main chale jate hai jinhen phir sharir se nikaal diya jayega.
