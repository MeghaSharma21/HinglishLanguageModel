 molska yaa churnaprawaar prajatiyon kii sankhya main aksherucay kii duusari sabase badi jati he. 80,000 jivit prajatiyaan hai or 35,000 jeevashm prajatiyaan maujuud hai. kathin khol kii maujuudagi ke kaaran sangrakshan kaa mauka bad jaataa he. ve awwalan dwideshiy samamit hai.is sangh ke adhikansh jantu vibhinn roopon ke samudri praani hote hai, par kuch taaje pani or sthal par bhi paae jate hai. inkaa sharir komal or praaia: aakaarahin hota he. ve koi vibhaajan nahin dikhaate or dwipaksheeya samamiti kuch main kho jaataa he. sharir ek purvakaal sir, ek prishthiya aant kubad, rengne burowing yaa tairaaki ke liye sanshodhit ek udar peshi pair he. sharir ek calcium yukt khol sravit karata he jo ek maansal viraasat he chaaron ore yah aantarik ho saktaa he, haalanki khol kam yaa anupasthit he, aamtaur par bahari he. jati aam taur par 9 yaa 10 vargeekaran virgon main baanta gaya he, jinamein se do puri tarah se vilupt hai. molsk kaa vaigyanik adhyayan 'maalakology' kaha jaataa he.ye pravar main band rahete hai. saadharantaya straav dwaara kade kavach kaa nirmaan karte hai. kavach kai prakaar ke hote hai. kavach ke teen str hote hai. patala bahyastar calosium carbonate kaa bana hota he or madhyastar tathaa sabase nichalaster mukta sip kaa bana hota he. molsk kii mukhya visheshta yah he ki kai kaaryon ke liye ek hi ang kaa istemaal hota he. udhaaharan ke liye, dil or gurde prajnan pranali he, saath hi sanchaar or mel tyaagane pranaaliyon ke mahatvapurn hisse hai bivalves main, gahare naale donon "saans" or utsarjan or prajnan ke liye mahatvapurn he jo viraasat guha, main ek pani kii vartmaan utpaadan. prajnan main, molsk anya prajnan saathi ko samyojit karne ke liye ling badal sakate hai. ye squid or octopoda se milte julate hai par unase kai lakshanon main bhinn hote hai. inamen khandibhavan nahin hota.
 molsk kii prajatiyon main rahane vaale varnit anumaan 50,000 se adhiktam 120,000 prajatiyon kaa he. david nichol ne varsh 1969 main unhone lagbhag 107,000 main 12,000 taaja pani ke gastropod or 35,000 sthaliy kaa sambhavit anumaan lagaaya tha.
 kyonki molsk ke bich shaareerik vividhta kii bahut badi seemaa hone ke kaaran kai pathyapustakon main inhe archi-molsk, kaalpanik samanyikrut molsk, yaa kaalpanik paitruk molsk kaha jaataa he. iske kai visheshtaayen anya vividhta vaale jeevon main bhi paye jate he.
 samanyikrut molsk dwipaksheeya hote he. inke upree taraf ek kavach bhi hota he, jo inhe viraasat main mila he. isi ke saath-saath kai bina peshi ke ang sharir ke angon main shaamil ho gaye.
 yah mul rup se aise sthaan par rahanaa pasand karte he, jahaan unhe prayaapt sthaan or samudr kaa taaja pani mil sake. parantu inke samooh ke kaaran yah bhinn-bhinn sthano main rahete he.
 yah unako viraasat main mila he. is prakaar ke khol mukhya rup se anregonite ke bane hote he. yah jab andey dete he to kelsite kaa upyog karte he. 
 inamen neeche kii or peshi vaale pair hote he, jo anek paristhitiyon main bhinn-bhinn kaaryon ko karne ke kaam aate he. yah kisi prakaar ke chot lagane par shleshm ko ek snehak ke rup main sravit karata he, jisase kisi bhi prakaar kii chot thik ho sake. iske pair kisi kathor jagah par chipak kar use aage badhane main sahaayta karte he. yah urdhwadhar mansapeshiyon ke dwaara puri tarah se khol ke andar aa jaataa he.
