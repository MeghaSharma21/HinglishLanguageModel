 ‘sevasadan’ ke uparaant san 1920 main premchand kaa yah upanyaas prashit hua . kintu ‘sevasadan’ ke bhaanti is upanyaas main bhav kii garima or vaicharik spashtataa nahi thi . upanyaas kii adhikansh katha main kramshah kritrimata badhati chali gayi he or kalpana kii atishyata ne mul kathanak ko hi gadabed kar diya he . nisandeh hi ‘vardaan’ premchand kii ek durbal kriti he .
