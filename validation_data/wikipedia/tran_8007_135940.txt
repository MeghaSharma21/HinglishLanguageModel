 bhartiya rashtriya congress swatantra bharat kaa pramukh raajnneetik dal he or is kii sthapana swatantraaa se purv 1885 main hui thi. bhartiya rashtriya congress ke adhyaksh dal ke chune hue pramukh hote he jo aam janata ke saath dal ke rishte ko prabandhit karne ke liye jimmedaar hote he. 
 bhartiya rashtriya congress kii sthapana 1885 main hui or iskaa pehla sammelan bombay ke gokuldaas tejpal sanskrit college main hua jahaan desh bhar se 72 pratinidhione bhaag liya tha. is sammelan ke adhyaksh the umesh chandra banerjee. 1888 main chauthe satr main jorge ul pehle british adhyaksh bane. 1917 ke 33wein sammelan main ani bessent pehali mahila adhyaksh bani. unke baad keval anya chaar mahilaon ne yah pad sambhaala he; sarojini naidu, neli sengupt, indira gandhi or sonia gandhi.swatantraaa ke purv jawaharlal nehru chaar baar adhyaksh rah kar ye adhiktam kaarykaal rahaa; 1929, 1930, 1936 or 1937 main. swatantraaa ke baad or ab tak sonia gandhi ne 19 saalon tak padbhaar sambhaal he jo kii sabase adhiktam he.
