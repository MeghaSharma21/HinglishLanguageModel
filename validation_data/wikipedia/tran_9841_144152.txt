 shilong chhavnee bhartiya rajya ke meghalaya main east khaasi hills jile main ek chhavnee kasbaa he.
 2001 kii bharat kii janaganana ke anusaar, shilong chhavnee kii jansankhya 12,385 thi, jisamen purushon kaa bhaag 57% or mahilaon kii jansankhya kaa 43% he . shilong chhavnee kii ausat sakshartaa dar 74% he, jo rashtriya ausat 59.5% kii tulana main bahut adhik he. isamen purush sakshartaa 78% he, or mahila sakshartaa 69% he. shilong chhavnee kii 12% jansankhya 6 varsh se kam aayu kii hai .
