 pandubbi main prayukt honevaale paridarshi kii nalee lagbhag 40 foot lambii hoti he. yah jangrodhi ispaat yaa kaanse kii banaai jaati he. is nalee ke nichle sire kaa vyas chah inch tathaa upree sheersh kaa vyas do inch hota he. yah nalee pandubbi ke pete main sthit khokhli nalee main upar neeche kii ja sakati he kaam na rahane par yah is khokhli nalee main khadi rahati he. paridarshi ko upar neeche, yaa daayen baayein, ghumaane ke liye vidyutchhakti kaa upyog kiya jaataa he, jiskaa niyantran prekshak karata he.
 paridarshi kii nalee main do paravartak prism nalee kii diwaar se 45rou kaa kone banaate hue ek duusare ke samaantar lage rahete hai. in prismon main se ek nalee ke sheersh bhaag main tathaa doosraa tal bhaag main hota he. in donon prismon ke madhya main do khagoleey doordarshak pranaliyaan sthit rahati hai. sheersh prism ke saamne dahini ore kaanch yaa lens laga rahata he. yah kaach yaa lens drishy se aanevaali prakaash kii kshaitij kirnon ko sheersh prism main paravartit karata he or vah prism in kirnon ko udhrvadhar kirnon main parivartit kar detaa he. ye udhrvadhar kirane tal prism dwaara pun: kshaitij kirnon main parivartit hokar jaati he or prekshak netrika ke dwaara samudr tal par sthit vastuon ke bimb ko dekhtaa he. khagoleey doordarshak pranaaliyon main se uuparavaali pranali bimb ko nunikrut kar detii he or nichevali bimb ko chah guna bada kar detii he. praaia: aavardhan kshamta 1.5 se 6 tak hoti he. jab aavardhan-kshamta 1.5 hoti he tab drushtikshetra kaa paraas 32 degree se 40 degree tak hota he. ucch aavardhan kshamta kaa upyog lakshya ke vistaar ko adhik spasht karata he, kintu drushtikshetra kaa paraas 8 degree se 10degree tak ghat jaataa he. paridarshi ke tal bhaag main lagi muuthon main se ek ko ghumaane par-aavardhan kaa parivartan prabhavit hota he. duusari mooth ko ghumaane par drushtirekha kshitij ke neeche 10degree tak avanat ho jaati he. sheersh ke prism ko nat karne par drushtirekha kshitij se 45 degree upar uth jaati he.
 sheersh prism ke saamne dahini ore lage hue lens par telemetre scale bana rahata he, jisamen udhrvadhar evam kshaitij rekhaayen ek duusare ko katati hui bani rahati hai. ye rekhaayen tal vaale prism par paravartit hoti he or netrika ke dwaara dikhaai padati hai. jab pandubbi main baitha prekshak kisi zahaaj ke bimb ko paridarshi dwaara dekhtaa he tab vah paridarshi ko upar neeche, daayen baayein, us samay tak karata rahata he jab tak jahaaj kaa bimb telemetre scale kii rekhaon ke madhya main nahin aa jaataa. ye rekhaayen anshon ko vyakt karti hai. prekshak jahaaj ke bimb se anumaan dwaara jahaaj kaa vistaar nishchit karata he or isaki sahaayta se pandubbi se jahaaj kii duuri kaa pariklan kar letaa he. duuri kaa thik thik pariklan karne ke liye paridarshi main stadimeter laga rahata he.
