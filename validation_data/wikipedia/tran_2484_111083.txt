 bhuvaigyanik yug prithvi ke prakritik bhuvaigyanik itihaas kaa ek bhaag hota he. bhuvaigyanikon ne is itihaas ko chaar ionon main vibhajit kara he, jo sabhi aadhe arab varsh yaa us se adhik lambe hai. yah eon swayan mahakalpon main vibhajit hai, jo aage kalpon main bante hue hai. yah kalp swayan yugon main bante hai, jo aage kaalon main bante hai.
 prithvi ke vartmaan eon kaa naam drishyajivi eon he. ise teen mahakalpon main vibhajit kara gaya he, jinamein sammilit yug nimna vibhaagon main diye gayi hai. bhuvaigyanikon ko prithvi ke bhinn samayon kaa anumaan dharti kii sataha se neeche kii ore jaakar miltaa he, isliye vartmaan se sameepi samayon ko bhuvigyaan main aksar "upree" or dur ke samayon ko "nichalaa" kaha jaataa he.
