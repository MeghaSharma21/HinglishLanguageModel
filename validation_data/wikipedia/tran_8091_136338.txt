 
 manohar parrikar (janm 13 disambar, 1955, vartmaan main goa ke mukhyamantri he tathaa bharat ke raksha mantri rah chuke he. ve uttar pradesh se rajya sabha saansad the. unhone san 1978 me ai.ai.tii. mumbai se snaatak kii pariksha uteern kari. bharat ke kisi rajya ke mukhyamantri banane vaale vah pehle vyakti hai jinhone ai.ai.tii. se snaatak kiya. unhen san 2001 main ai.ai.tii. mumbai dwaara vishisht bhootpurv chaatr kii oopaadhi bhi pradaan kii gayi.
 bhartiya janata party se goa ke mukhyamantri banane vaale vah pehle netaa hai. 1994 main unhen goa kii dwitiya vyavasthapika ke liye chaynit kiya gaya tha. june 1999 se navambar 1999 tak vah virdohi party ke netaa rahe. 24 october 2000 ko vah goa ke mukhyamantri bane kintu unki sarkaar 27 farvari 2002 tak hi chal pai. june 2002 main vah punah sabha ke sadsy bane tathaa june 5, 2002 ko punah goa ke mukhyamantri pad ke liye chaynit hue. 
 13 march 2017 ko bhaajapa netaa manohar parrikar ne goa ke mukhyamantri ke rup main shapath lee,rajbhavan main yojit samaaroh main rajyapal mridula sinha ne unhen pad evam gopneeyata kii shapath dilaai.parrikar ne goa main chote dalon or nirdaleey vidhayakon ke samarthan se sarkaar banaai he or chauthi baar mukhyamantri pad kii shapath lee. 
 bi.je.pi. ko goa kii satta main laane kaa shrey unako hi jaataa he. iske atirikt bhartiya antarrashtriya film mahotsav ko akele goa laane kaa tathaa kisi bhi anya sarkaar se kam samay me ek antarrashtriya str kii moolbhoot sanrachana khadi karne kaa shrey bhi unhi ko jaataa he. kai samaaj sudhaar yojanaon jaise dayanand saamaajik suraksha yojana jo ki vruddh nagarikon ko aarthik sahaayta pradaan karti he, sibraj yojana, si.em. rojgaar yojana ityadi main bhi unka pramukh yogadan rahaa he. unhen kai pratishthit pratibhaaon jaise dow anupam saraaf tathaa aar. si. sinha ityadi ko sarkaar main salaahkaar ke taur par shaamil karne kaa shrey bhi jaataa he. planning kamishan of india tathaa india today ke dwaara kiy gaye surveykshan ke anusaar unke kaarykaal main goa lagaataar teen saal tak bharat kaa sarvshereshth shasit pradesh rahaa. kaaryshil tathaa siddhantvaadi shri parikar ko goa main mi. clin ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
 janvari 29, 2005 ko 4 bi.je.pi. netaaon ke istipha dene ke kaaran unki sarkaar alpamat main aa gayi. shri parikar ne daawa kiya ki vah apana bahumat sabit karenge tathaa farvari 2005 main aisa hua bhi. kintu baad me kisi karanvash unhen apana pad khona padaa. lagaataar vivaadon ke pashchaat march 2005 main goa main raashtrapati shashan lagu kar diya gaya kintu june 2005 main virdohi netaa pratap singh rane goa ke mukhyamantri bana diye gayi.
