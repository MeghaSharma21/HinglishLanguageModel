 kavitawali goswami tulsidas dwaara rachit kavy he.
 kavitawali main shri ramachandra ke itihaas kaa varnan kavitt, chaupai, savaiya aadi chhandon main kii gai he. ramcharitamanas ke jaise hi kavitawali main bhi saat kand hai. ye chand brajabhasha main likhe gayi hai or inaki rachana praaia: usee paripati par kii gayi he jis paripati par reetikaal kaa adhiktar reeti-mukt kavy likhaa gaya.
 'kavitawali' kaa kavy-shilp muktak kavy kaa he. uktiyon kii vilakshantaa, anupraason kii chata, laypurna shabdon kii sthapana katha bhaag ke chhndon main darshneey he. aage reeti kaal main yah kavy style bahut lokapriy hui or is prakaar tulsidas is kavy style ke pratham kaviyon main se gyaat hote hai phir bhi unki 'kavitawali' ke chhndon main puri praudhata dikhaai padati he.
