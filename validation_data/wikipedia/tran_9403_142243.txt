 mohammad hamid ansaari, bharat ke uparashtrapati the. ve bhartiya alpsankhyak ayog ke bhootpurv adhyaksh bhi hai. ve ek shikshavid, tathaa pramukh rajaneta hai, evam aligarh muslim vishvavidhyalay ke upkulapati bhi rah chuke hai. ve 10 agast 2007 ko bharat ke 13wein uparashtrapati chune gayi.
 shri ansaari kaa janm pashchim bengal kii rajdhani kolkata main 1 april 1937 ko hua tha. unki shiksha-diksha saint edwards hai-skool shimla, saint jevirs mahavidhyalay kolkata or aligarh muslim vishvavidhyalay main hui.
 shri ansaari ne apane karier kii shuruyaat bhartiya videsh seva ke ek naukarshah ke rup main 1961 main kii thi jab unhen sanyukt raashtra sangh main bharat kaa sthaayee pratinidhi niyukt kiya gaya tha. ve austrailia main bharat ke ucchayukta bhi rahe. baad main unhone afghanistan, sanyukt arab emirate, tathaa iran main bharat ke rajdoot ke taur par bhi kaam kiy. 1984 main unhen padmashri puraskaar se bhi sammanit kiya gaya.. ve may san 2000 se march 2004 tak aligarh muslim vishvavidhyalay ke upkulapati bhi rahe. unhen san guzarat dangon ke peediton ko muaavja dilaane or sadbhaavna ke liye unki bhoomika ke liye bhi saraahaa jaataa he.
 nahin balki duusare alpsankhyak jaise isaai, sikh, parsi aadi ke liye bhi sakriy rahe. unhone parmparik dharmik shiksha ke sthaan par aadhunik shiksha par jor diya. ve aadhunikaran or udaarvaad ke samarthak rahe.
 2007 ke uparashtrapati chunaav main mohammad hamid ansaari ko jeet hasil hui. 2012 main unke kaarykaal ko paanch saal ke badha diya gaya. 2017 ke agast maah kii 10 taarikh ko unka kaarykaal samaapt ho chukaa he tathaa yah daayitv ke liye unki jagah shri maan em. venkaiya naidu sanbhaalenge.
 hamid ansaari ke bharat main uparashtrapati kaal ke dauraan kuch vivaad bhi unase jude hai. bharat main united state of america ke tatkaaleen raashtrapati baraak obama kii upasthiti main ganatantr diwas manaaya gaya tha. obama ke saath hamid ansaari ke dwaara bharat ke rashtradhvaj ko salaami na dene kii tasviir viral hui thi or media main bhi is mudde par kaaphi charchaa hui thi. bharat ke soochna evam prasaaran mantralay ne uparashtrapati ke upar sangeen aarope evam tippaneeyon ke kaaran aakrosh vyakt karke kahan ki, 'uparashtrapati kaarykram ke dauraan saavdhaan mudra main khadey najar aae. uparashtrapati par salaami nahin dene or rashtradhvaj kaa apmaan karne ke channel ke aarope kii manshaa uparashtrapati par akshep lagane kii jaan padati he, kyonki aisa daawa bilkul jhooth, gumraahpurn, galat or aruchikar he.' channel ko is vishay main chetavanee bhi di gayi thi. bharat ke uparashtrapati kaa pad tyaagate samay hamid ansaari ne nivedan diya ki, 'bharat main muslim surakshit nahin hai.' is vidhaan ke upar logon ne anek pratikiaayein vyakt kii thi.
