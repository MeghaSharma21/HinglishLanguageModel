 sukarno ne bhi sucmaano, indonesian swatantraaa andolan ke netaa or indonesia ke pehle raashtrapati, jinhone desh kii mul sansadeey pranali ko ek sattadhari "gided demokrasi" ke paksh main dabaa diya or sena ke netaaon ke khilaaf communiston ko santulan banane kaa prayaas kiya. suharto ke tahat sena ne unhen 1966 main pesh kiya tha. sukarno ne tulunggang gaanv main apane dada daadi ke saath apane bachapan ke lambey samay tak bitaye the, jahaan vah gramin gramin java ke jeevwad or rahasyavaad ke sampark main tha. vahaan vah rastang ke aaajeevan bhakt ban gaye, kathputli chaaya hindu mahakavyon ke aadhaar par naatakon ke rup main anemated or ek guru kathputli ne sunai, jo ek puri raat ke madhyam se mantrmugdh shrota rakh saktaa tha. 15 saal kii umr ke rup main sukarno ko suraabaia main madhyamik vidhyalay or umr said tajochrominito, ek pramukh naagarik or dharmik aabaadi ke ghar main nivas karne ke liye bheja gaya tha. tsochrominato ne use ek poshit paalak putr or aashray ke rup main ilaaj kiya, apni aage kii shiksha kaa vittaposhan kiya, or ant main 20 saal kii umr main unki apni 16 varshiya beti, city utari se shaadi kar lee.
 ek chaatr ke rup main, sukarna ne mukhya rup se bhashaaon main shreshthata praapt kii unhone javanese, sudani, balinis or aadhunik indonesian main maharath hasil kii, jo vastav main, unhone banane ke liye bahut kuch kiya. unhone arabi bhi adhigrahan kiya, jo ek muslim ke rup main unhone quran ke adhyayan se siikhaa; dach, unki shiksha kii bhashaa; german; french; angrezi; or, baad main, japani tsochromaminoto ke ghar main vah ubharate hue netaaon se milakar aayaa jo teji se chaude hue rashtriya raajnneetik spectrum ko failaane lage, saamanti rajkumariyon se lekar bhagoda communist shadyantrkariyon tak. tajochromaminoto janak ke udaar sankeernata, jis tarah se romantic or rudhiwaad ke rahasyavaad, ne sukarno ke man or vyaktitv par avishwasaneey rup se ankit kiya. baad main vah raashtra banane ke liye ek vir naatakeey rup main vyavhaar karne ke liye tha, jisamen behosh purush or vichaaron kaa sangharsh saraasar kavy jaadu ke madhyam se mil saktaa he.
