 gunda 1998 main bani hindi bhashaa nirdeshak kanti shah kii aparaadh-drama aadhaarit film hai, film kii mukhya bhumikaon main mithun chakravarti, mukesh rishi or shakti kapur evam kai sah-abhinayta sammilit hai. film nirmaataa main anil singh, patakatha-lekhan main bashir babbar evam sangeet nirdeshak hai anand raaj anand. 
 'gunda' ek eemaandaar aadmi or kai gang-sarganaon ke hinsak muthbhed or uske aapasi takaraav par aadhaarit kahani hai, jo vah aage chalakar inase hinsaatmak pratishodh letaa he jab yuvavastha main uske pita, bahan or preyasi kii hatya ho jaati hai. 
 film kii shuruyaat bhrasht rajaneta kafanchor or lambu aataa kii us saude se hoti he, jahaan lambu ko bulla ko maarane kaa kaam miltaa he. lambu is jang kii elaan bulla ke aadmi ko markar aagaaz karata he, to vahiin bulla usee ke bhai kundan kii hatya karata he. lambu aavesh main apane bhai kaa badla bulla kii bahan kaa balaatkaar kar uski bhi hatya karata he. bulla aakhirkaar jang ko viraam lagane ke liye lambu aataa ko khatm karata he or anderworld kaa avijit sargana hone kaa elaan karata he. bhrasht rajaneta bacchubhai bhigona kii sharan main aakar bulla ko vah kafanchor-netaa ko maarane kaa jimmaa saunpta he, jise vah anjaam dene ke liye apane daahine haath kaala shetty ko kahata he. shetty tab kafanchor-netaa ko police bal ke saamne hi maar detaa he par police koi pratikriya nahin karti. tab shankar naam kaa yuvak uske raah kaa roda banataa he or use police ke haathon giraftaar karaata he. shankar apana jeevanyaapan bandargah or kabhi-kabhaar hawaondde main kuli mazdooree kar kamata he jahaan vah apni saadhaaran si jindagi apane pulicekarmi pita, bahan gita or ek paaltu bandar tinchu ke saath vyateet karata he. shankar kii ganga naam kii preyasi rahati jo uske saath kisi bhi tarah vivaah ko aatur rahati he lekin apni jimmedaari ko nibhaane ke sankalp main shankar usase byaah ko inkaar karata he. 
 vahiin shipyard par bulla dwaara sanchalit ek kushti pratiyogita kaa aayojan hota he jahaan bulla apane pahalwaan ko haraane bataur inam rakhata he. shankar ko kisi puraskaar yaa rutaba hasil karne kii laalasaa nahin rahati lekin apane kuli saathiyon ke jor dene or bahan kii shaadi kaa aashwaasan milane par mukaable ko raji ho jaataa he or pahalwaan ko haraakar hi puraskaar kii raashi jeetta he. lekin bulla kii dushmani uski tab chingaari pakadati he jab shankar ke pita bulla ke bheje gunde dukandaaron se jabaran haftaa vasuuli karne ke virodh karne par unke haathon pitayee khate he. shankar vahaan pahunch un gundo ko maar bhagaata he. 
 apane aadamiyon kii pitayee se baukhalaayaa bulla tab shankar kii bahan gita ko shikaar banane apane vafaadaar nate ko bhejtaa he, or nata usase chhedchhad karata he jiske virodh main gulshan uski pitayee karata he. gita uski dilari se prabhavit ho prem kar baithti he or donon shaadi kar lete hai. lekin use pataa nahin hota ki gulshan bulla kii hi shadyantr kaa hi hissaa he, jahaan wo gita ko chutia ke haathon uski yonapurti ke liye chhod jaataa he. bulla use vitamin-sex kii goli khilaataa he jiske uttejit hokar chutia gita kaa balaatkaar kar baad main maar daalata he or shav ko jangal main dafna detaa he. shankar kaa paaltu bandar, tinchu yah dekh letaa he or shankar ko bulaakar bulla ke bangale tak pahunchaata he. bahan kii vibhats hatya ujagar hone par shankar chutia kaa peechha kar bulla kii dahalij tak pahunchataa he. or vahiin maujud bulla, pote, ibu hatela or inspector kaale kaa saamana hone par unki maut kii chetavanee dekar apane raste laut jaataa he. 
 shankar ke pita apane beti gita kii maut ke dukh se pagal ho jate he or apane afasar inspector kaale ko gangster bulla kii tarafdaari ko lekar apni barbaadi ke liye dhamkaate hai. kaale se gutthamguthti kii asafal koshish main budha pita uske haathon galaa ghontkar maaraa jaataa he. vahiin shankar tab bulla or uske tarafdaaron ko agle das din baad ek-ek ko maarane kii kasam letaa he. raste main shankar ek anjaan laavaaris bacchee ko paakar use apni god letaa he. 
 shankar gusse kii jhonk main bulla ke aadamiyon ko maarane nikal padataa hai, jisaki shuruyaat vah gulshan kii hatya se karata he. phir bulla ke chamache ibu hatela ko ek ladki kii balaatkaar ke prayaas main uskaa sar kaat daalata he. phir apane agle makasad bhrashtaneta bacchubhai ko maarane kii asafal koshish main uske sniper dwaara shankar pakadaa diya jaataa he or hatya ke aarope main use umrakaid kii sajaa di jaati he par usee raat shankar jail se faraar ho jaataa he or kaale ke ghar aa dhamkata he. shankar ke raah main prashikshit kungphu hatyare vyaadhyaan daalate he jo kai chaaku, svachlit hathiyaaron or hathgolon se las hai. par shankar unako bhi datta batalaate hue kaale ko maar daalata he. 
 shankar ab bulla ke aadmi chikna ko maarane jaataa he jo uske liye gaanv kii ladakiyon ko agava kar veshyavriti ke liye shahar le jaataa hai. par chikna kii hatya se purv shankar ko yah maaloom ho jaataa he ki raste main mili wo laavaaris navjaat bhi bulla kii avaidh santan he. shankar kaa aglaa nishaanaa pote, chutia or bulla ko bane hi thi wo pehle hi shankar kii preyasi ganga kaa apharan kar maar daalate hai. pote gussaye shankar ke haathon maaraa jaataa he phir vah chutia kaa peechha kar use dhar-dabochata he, maut ke bhay se chutia bataata he ki vah to napunsak he or sari musiibat bulla kii di hui us vitamin-sex kii goli kaa asar tha jiske ugr unmaad main usne gita kaa berahami se balaatkaar kar diya. chutia ke itnaa kehete hi shankar uskaa guptang-bhang kar kahata he ki vah isi sajaa ke laayak he. 
 antim charam ke drishy main shankar or bulla kaa aamanaa-saamana usee shipyard-hawaondde vaale kamplex main hota he. par bulla apane darjan bhar hatyare rickshaw chaalkon kii bheed main gher use maarane kii koshish karata he, lekin shankar tab apni car kii dikky se raaket graned lanchar nikaal un sabhi hatyare rikhshachalkon kaa uda daalata he. lekin sthiti tab palatati he jab bulla shankar kii god lee hui bacchee ko uski beti samaj apni dhaal banaata he, lekin wo ye sunakar hairaan ho jaataa he wo bacchee uske hi kukarmon kaa nateeja he. isse pehle bulla uski jaan letaa shankar kaa paaltu tinchu bandar use bachaa letaa he. vahiin hawaondde main maujud kaala shetty helicopter ke jariye bulla ko bachaane pahunchataa he, shankar unako khadedate hue neeche giraakar kaala shetty or bulla kii pitayee kar maar daalata he. 
 film kaa sangeet nirdesan anand raaj anand ne compoj kiya he.

 3."aaj paraai hokar bahan jaae" - shabbir kumaar
 4. "kholi mere dil kii khaali he tu" - punam bhaatia, abhijit
 5. "teri ankhon kaa chal gaya jaadu" - kavita krishnamurti, kumaar shanu
 film main bole dwiarthi samvaad or mahilaon par apmaan va hinsak krity jaise drishyon par kai mahila sansthaaon ne jamkar film kii kadi aalochana kii.
