 madhyapradesh shashan ne adivasee lok or parmparik kalaon main utkrushtataa or shreshth uplabdhi ko sammanit karne or in kalaon main rashtriya mandand viksit karne kii drushti se tulsi sammaan ke naam se ek lakh rupee kaa ek vaarshik puraskaar sthapit kiya he. yah sammaan teen varsh main do baar pradarshnkaree kalaon or ek baar rupankar kalaon ke kshetra main diya jaataa he.
 tulsi sammaan kaa nikash asaadhaaran srujanaatmakataa, utkrushtataa or deergh sadhana ke nirapavaad sarvocch mandand rakhe gayi hai. chayan kii nishchit prakriya se yah spasht he ki kala ke vivaadit rashtriya mandand viksit karne ke is vinadha praytn main sabhi staron par visheshgyon kii hissedari he or is baat kaa puraa khyaal rakhaa gaya he ki jahaan ek ore kalatmak uplabdhiyon ke bare main ek tarah kaa vyapak mat sangrah sandarbh ke liye uplabdh rahe, vahiin sammaan se vibhooshit kiye jane vaale kalakaar yaa mandali kaa chayan asandigdha nishtha or vivek vaale visheshgy puri nishpakshta, vastuparakata or nirbhayata ke saath aise mandandon ke aadhaar par karen jo uttaradaayi jivan drushti, gambhir kalanushasan or soundaryabodh par ashrith hai.
 tulsi sammaan kii chayan prakriya ke anusaar samskruti vibhaag, varsh-vishesh ke liye nirdhaarit kalanushasan ke kalakaaron, visheshgyon, rasikon or sanghathanon aadi se apane rachanaatmak vaishishty, gyaan or sansakti kaa laabh lete hue is sammaan ke liye upyukt kalakaaron athwa mandaliyon ke naamon kii anushansa karne kaa anurodh karata he. ye anushansaayen sankalit karke visheshgyon kii chayan samiti ke saamne antim nirnay ke liye rakhi jaati hai. is samiti main rashtriya khyaati ke kalakaar or visheshgy shaamil hote hai. chayan samiti ko yah bhi swatantraaa he ki yadi koi naam chhot gaya ho to use apni taraf se jod len. rajya shashan ne chayan samiti ko anushansa ko apane liye bandhankaree mana he.
