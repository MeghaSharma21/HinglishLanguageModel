 besanagar madhya bharat kaa ek pracheen nagar he, jise paali buddha granthon main vessagar tathaa sanskrit sahitya main vidisha ke naam se pukaara gaya he. bhilsa railway stesan se pashchim kii taraf kareeb 2 mile kii duuri par sthit, yah sthaan puratatvettaon kii saanskrithik bhoomi kahi ja sakati he. yah vetravati or base nadi se ghira hua he tathaa shesh do taraf kii bhoomi par pracheer banaakar nagar ko ek kile kaa rup de diya tha.
 pracheen kaal kaa vaibhav sampann yah nagar, ab tuuti- phooti murtiyan va kalayukta bhawanon kaa khandhar maatr rah gaya he. yahan paye jane vaale bhagnavshesh isa purv teesri shatabdi se 11 veen shatabdi tak kii kahani kehete hai. ab bhi is sthaan par hi ek taraf "base' naamak gram bachaa hua he.
 besanagar kaa raajnneetik mahatv mauryaal main ashok ke samay se badha. shunga- kaal main yah bahut hi prasiddh dharmik centre tha. yah sthaan hindu va buddha donon ke liye mahatvapurn the. guptkaal tak isaki samruddhi kaayam rahi. uske pashchaat iske kritrim itihaas kaa spasht sakshya nahin miltaa. besanagar main bhagwaan vasudev ke sammaan main takshashila ke raja antialkids ke rajdoot heleodoras ne lagbhag 135 i. pus. main ek garudadhvaj sthapit karaayaa gaya tha.10 veen shatabdi main nadi ke duusari taraf sthit "bhilsa' astitv main aa chukaa tha. besanagar ke aitihaasik mahatv ko dekhte hue puraatatv vibhaag dwaara utkhanan kaary kiye gayi. utkhanan main mili kuch avshesh isvi kaal ke prarambh ke saamaajik jivan par prakaash daalate hai. puraanon ke anusaar yahan pushyamitra shunga ne yajna kiya tha. khudaai main bade- bade yagyon kii kaarysthali ke vishesh chinh mili hai. viddhanon dwaara shaastrarth kiye jane vaale kaksh evam bhojanshala kaa pramaan miltaa he. iske alaava vibhinn kaaryon se sambhddha kai tarah kii mudraayen tathaa mitti kii pakaai hui vastuon se unke jivan kii jhaanki pratibimbit hoti he.
 kai saahitiyik tathaa puratathvik pramaan, is sthaan kaa yawanon se sambandh ko spasht karte hai. abhilekhon main mila shabd "dwimitriy' ko sikandar kaaleen unani shaasha ke rup main mana jaataa he. khaam baba kaa nirmaan bhi yavan rajdoot anklitas ne hi karavaaya tha. kaha jaataa he ki varttman durjanpura vahi sthaan he, jahaan yawanon kaa vaas hua karata he. pehle yah dumitpura ke naam se janaa jaataa tha.
 yah unani rajdoot tha.isane bhagwat dharm se prabhavit hokar bidisha me ""garuda dhvaj"" kii sthapana kii.
