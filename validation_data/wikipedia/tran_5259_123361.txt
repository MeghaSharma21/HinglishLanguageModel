  riski bijness  1983 main pradarshit america hasya drama film he. mukhya kirdaar main tom cruge ar rebeka de morne hai. isi film ne tam cruge ko staradam se rubaru karaayaa.
 joe gudson ek hai skool vidhyaarthee he jo apane amir mata-pita ke saath chicago main rahata he. uske pita chaahate hai ki wo princeton vishvavidhyalay main padhe,isliye joe kii maan usase kahati he ki wo saakshatkarta, bill radarford, ko uske future interprijej ke bare main bataaye.
 film kaa sangeet tengarine dream ne diya he.
