 banu kinanah ek arabi qabeela he. parmparik qahtaani-adanani arab shreneekaran ke nazariye se banu kinanah adanani qabeele ke mudar qabeele kii sabase badi shakha he. is qabeele ke log adhiktar saudi arab ke hijaaz or tihamah kshetron main basate hai.
 banu kinanah ke log apane ko kinanah ke vanshaj maanate hai. kinanah ilyaas ke pote the, jinhen angrezi main ilijah kehete hai or jo islamic manyata main ek pigambar the. inke kuraish qabeele se sambandh rahe hai or unke saath yah itihaas main mitrapaksh main rahe hai.
