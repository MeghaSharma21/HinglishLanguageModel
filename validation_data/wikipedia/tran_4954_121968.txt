 baluchistan ke rajyapal, baluchistan, pakistan kii prantiy sarkaar ke pramukh rajya saunpa he. rajyapal kii niyukti raashtrapati pakistan, pradhanmantri pakistan kii paraamarsh par karte hai or saamanytah yah ek aupchaarik pad hota he arthath uske paas adhik sanvaidhanik adhikaar nahin hote hai. haalanki itihaas main kai baar aise avasar aae hai jab prantiy governron ko atirikt va purna adhikaar miltaa rahaa he, visheshkar un maamalon main jab prantiy vidhayika bhang kar di gai ho, tab prashasnic adhikaar sidhe rajyapal ke adhikarashetra ke antargat aa jate hai.
 baluchistan main governor kaa pad ek july 1970 ko yahya khan kii sainya sarkaar main gathan kiya gaya. julfikar ali bhutto kii pradhanmantritv ke dinon main rajyapal ke pad ko do baar samaapt kar diya gaya tha, pehali baar 1973 main duusari baar 1974 se 1976 tak. general zia ul haq ke sainya shashan ke dinon main general rahim deen khan baluchistan ke rajyapal ke pad par lambey samay tak rahe. yahaan tak â€‹â€‹ki parvej musharraf kii sainya sarkaar ke dinon main bhi 1999 se 2002 tak yahaan rajyapal shashan rahaa.
