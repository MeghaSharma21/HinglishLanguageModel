 uniphid modelling lenguej software ejineering kshetra kii ek manakikrut saamaanya-uddeshy modelling bhashaa he. maanak, vastu prabandhan samooh dwaara rachaa gaya or isi ke dwaara sambhala jaataa he.
 yumail software vriddhikar pranali ke drishy model banane ke liye chitramay sanketan takanic kaa ek set sammilit karata he.
 uniphid modelling lenguej, vikaas ke antargat ek vastu-abhivinyast software gahan pranali kii shilpakruti ko ullikhit, roopaantar, nirmaan or dastauez karne ke liye upyog kii jaati he. yumail ek pranali kii vaastushilpeey ruparekha ko dekhne ke liye ek maanak tarika pradaan karata he, jisamen nimnlikhit tatv shaamil hai:
 yumail data modelling, vyapaar modelling, vastu modelling or ghatak modelling se behtareen takneekon kaa sammishran karata he. software vikaas ke jivan chakra main or vibhinn karyanvayan praudyogikiyon ke paar, yah sabhi prakriyaaon ke saath istemaal kiya ja saktaa he. yumail ne booch vidhi, vastu modelling takanic {OMT) or {0}vastu-abhivinyast software ejineering ko ek saamaanya, ekal or adhik prayog karne yogya modelling bhashaa ke rup main jod kar, unke ankan kaa sanshleshan kiya he. yumail kaa uddeshy he ek maanak modelling bhashaa bananaa jo samavarti or vitaran pranali ko model kar sake. yumail ek vaastavik udyog maanak he or vastu prabandhan samooh ke tattwavdhan main viksit ho rahi he. OMG ne shuru main vastu-abhivinyast tareekon par jaankaree maangi jo ek kathor software modelling bhashaa bana sakati. kai udyog ke netaaon ne yumail maanak banane main madad karne ke liye aprihaas jawaab diya he.
 yumail model, OMG dwaara samarthit, QVT-jaisi roopaantar bhashaaon ke madhyam se swatah anya prativednon main badal sakate hai. yumail vitanya he or anukulan ke liye nimnlikhit tantra pesh karata he: khaakaa or chhavi. profile dwaara vistaar ke shabdaarth vigyaan kaa yumail 2.0 pramukh sanshodhan ke saath sudhaar kiya gaya he.
 varsh 1994 main general elactric se james roombog kii sevaayen kiraaye par lene ke uparaant reshnal software corporation aaj ke do sarvaadhik lokapriy vastu unmukh modelling tareekon kaa strot ban gaya : roombog kaa o em tii, jo ki object oriented analysis hetu behatar tha, tathaa grady booch kaa booch tarika, jo ki object oriented dizin hetu behatar tha. unke prayaason main unhen sheeghra hi object oriented software ejineering ke srujak iver jaikbasan se sahaayta praapt hui. company objectory e bi ke reshnal dwaara adhigrahan ke uparaant jaikbasan ne varsh 1995 main reshnal main kaarybhaar grahan kiya. teenon kaarypaddatividon ko samuhik rup se thri emigos ke naam se janaa jaataa tha, kyonki ve kaary paddati vyavhaar ke bare main aapas main praayah tark karte rahane ke liye jane jate the.
 varsh 1996 main reshnal ne yah paaya ki modelling bhashaaon kii bahutayat object praudyogiki ko apnaane kii dar ko dhimaa kar rahi thi, atah ek ekikrut tareeke se kaary ko punah vyavasthit karte huye unhone thri emigos ko ek non properitary uniphoid modelling laguez ke vikaas kaa kaary saunpa. pratispardhi objact praudyogiki companiyon ke pratinidhiyon se o o pi es el e '96 ke dauraan paraamarsh kiya gaya; unhone gradi booch ke booch tareeke kii ankan paddati, jisamen cloud rupi pratikon kaa prayog kiya gaya tha, ke sthaan par shreniyon ko darshane ke liye boxes kaa chayan kiya.
 thri emigos" ke takneekee netritv main uniphoid modelling laguage vargikarnon ko purna karne tathaa ise o em ji aar epa pi ke ek pratiuttar ke rup main prastaavit karne ke liye varsh 1996 main you.em.el. parteners naamak ek antarrashtriya sangh kaa gathan kiya gaya. you em el parteners kaa you em el 1.0 vargeekaran masauda janvari 1997 main o em ji ko prastaavit kiya gaya. isi maah you em el parteners ne kris cobryan kii adhyakshata evam aid ykholt ke prashasan main vargikarnon ke simanticas ko antim rup dene tathaa ise anya maanakeey prayaason ke saath ekikrut karne ke liye ek simantics task force kaa gathan kiya. is kaary ke parinaam, you em el 1.1, ko agast 1997 main o em ji ko prastut kiya gaya evam o em ji dwaara ise navambar 1997 main apanaaya gaya.te
 ek modelling sanketan ke rup main, OMT sanketan kaa prabhaav haaavee he . haalanki booch "cloud" ankan hataa diya gaya tha, booch ke kam-str dizin vrittant ko nirdisht karne kii kshamta kaa aalingan kiya gaya. objectari se upyog maamla ankan or booch se ghatak ankan ko anya sanket paddatiyon ke saath sanghatit kiya gaya, lekin shabdaarth vigyaan ekikaran yumail 1.1 main apekshaakrit kamjor tha or yumail 2.0 pramukh sanshodhan tay karne tak asal main nishchit nahin kiya gaya.
 kai anya OO tareekon se bhi avadharanaon ko yumail ke saath dhili tarah se sanghatit kiya gaya is uddeshy ke saath kii yumail sabhi OO tareekon kaa samarthan karegaa. jaise hi samooh ne vaastavik samay pranali domen main vyapak kawarej sunishchit karne kii koshish kii, kai anya logon ne bhi isamen yogadan diya, jinaki pahunch ne, tony vassarmen or pitor pirchaar ke "object-oriented strecchard dizin " sanketan, re bahar ke "system disine vid ada", archi bovan ke upyog maamle or samay vishleshan, paul ward ke data vishleshan or david harel ke "statchart" sahit aaj ke kai model ko jhalak di. parinamswaroop, yumail ejineering samasyaon kii kai kismon main upyogi he, ekal prakriya, ekal upyogkarta prayojyata se samavarti, vitaran vyavastha, jo yumail ko samrudh banaate hai par bada bhi.
 uniphid modelling bhashaa ek antarrashtriya maanak he:
 yumail 1.1 ke baad se yumail mahatvapoorntaa se paripakv hua he. kai chote sanshodhano ne yumail ke pratham sanskaran kii kamiyaan or bag thik kiye, jiske baad yumail 2.0 pramukh sanshodhan hua jo 2005 main OMG dwaara sweekaar kiya gaya.
 yumail 2.x vinirdesan ke chaar bhaag hai:
 in maanakon ke vartmaan sanskaran yumail paramdhaanchaa sanskaran 2.2, yumail buniyadi dhaancha sanskaran 2.2, OCL sanskaran 2.0 or yumail aarekh aadan-pradaan sanskaran 1.0 kaa paalan karte hai.
 haalanki kai yumail upkaran yumail 2.x kii nai suvidhaon main se kuch kaa samarthan karte hai, OMG unki vishistataaon ke saath saamaanya rup se pareekshan anupaalan karne ke liye koi pareekshan sthaan uplabdh nahin karwaata.
 yumail swayan main ek parivriddhi tarika nahin he, tathaapi, ise apane samay ke pramukh vastu-uddeshy software vikaas ke tareekon ke saath anukool hone ke liye banaaya gaya tha . jab se yumail viksit hua he, in main se kuch tareekon ko nae ankan kaa laabh lene ke liye phir se dhaala gaya he or nae tareeke yumail ke aadhaar par nirmit kiye gaye hai. sabase acha gyaat he ibiem reshnal uniphid process . kai anya yumail-aadhaarit vidhiyaan hai jaise, sankshepan tarika, gatisheel system vikaas tarika or anya jo adhik vishisht samaadhaan, yaa vibhinn uddeshy prapti uplabdh karwaane ke liye dizine kiye gaye hai.
 ek pranali ke chitra ke set or yumail model ke bich antar karnaa bahut mahatvapurn he. ek aarekh ek system ke model kaa aanshik chitramay pratinidhitiv he. model main ek 'shabdaarth vigyaan pasch samtal" bhi hota hai - pralekhan jaise likhe upyog maamle jo model tatwon or chitra ko karmashakti dete hai.
 yumail chitra ek model pranali ke do alag vichaaron kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai.
 XMI vinimay praaroop kaa upyog karne se yumail upkarnon ke bich yumail model vinimay kiye ja sakate he.
 yumail 2.2 main 14 prakaar ke chitra hai jinhen do shreniyon main vibhajit kiya gaya he. saat aarekh prakaar sanrachanaatmak soochna darshaate hai or anya saat saamaanya prakaar kaa vyavhaar darshaate hai, jinamein se chaar baatcheet ke vibhinn pahaluon ko darshaate hai. yah chitra taratamya rup se vargeekrut kiye ja sakate he jaisaa nimna shrenee ke chitra main dikhaaya gaya he:
 yumail, yumail tatv prakaar ko ek nishchit aarekh prakaar tak simit nahin karata. aamtaur par, har yumail tatv lagbhag sabhi prakaar ke chitra par prakat ho saktaa he, yah lacheelaapan yumail 2.0 main aanshik rup se pratibandhit kiya gaya he. yumail profile atirikt aarekh prakaar ko paribhaashit yaa atirikt ankan ke saath maujuuda chitra kaa vistaar kar sakate hai.
 ejineering drawing, kii parampara ko dhyaan main rakhte hue, upyog, baadha, yaa manshaa spasht karne ke liye yumail aarekh main ek tippanee yaa lekh kii anumati he.

 varg rekhachitr

 chunki sanrachana chitra ek pranali kii sanrachana kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai, unhen software pranali ke sthapaty kaa dastavejikaran banane ke liye bade paimaane par istemaal kiya jaataa he.
 vyavhaar chitra is baat par jor daalata he ki model kiye jane vaali pranali main kya hona chahie:
 yumail gatividhi aarekh
 rajya mashin aarekh
 yuj cese aarekh
 chunki vyavhaar chitra ek pranali ke vyavhaar kaa varnan karte hai, unhen bade paimaane par software pranali kii kaaryakshmata kaa varnan karne ke liye istemaal kiya jaataa he.
 sampark chitra, vyavhaar chitra kaa ek subset, jo model kiye jane vaali pranali kii chizon ke bich data or niyantran pravaah karne par jor detaa he:
 sanchaar aarekh

 protocol rajya mashin ek rajya mashin kaa up-sanskaran he. yah ek network sanchaar protocol ko model karne ke liye istemaal kiya ja saktaa he.
 object menagement samooh ne uniphid modling lenguej ko paribhaashit karne ke liye, meta-object fecility naamak meta modling sthapaty viksit kiya he. meta-vastu suvidha model-chaalit ejineering ke liye ek maanak he, jise ek chaar parat vaastukala ke rup main dizin kiya gaya he, jaisaa dahini ore di gai chhavi main dikhaaya gaya he. yah sheersh str par, M3 parat naamak, ek meta-meta model uplabdh karaata he. yah em3 bhashaa, meta-object fecility dwaara em2 model naamak metamodel banane ke liye prayukt kii ja rahi he. ek parat 2 meta-object suvidha modal kaa sabase pramukh udhaaharan yuamel he, vah model jo khud yumail kaa varnan he. yah em2 model em1 parat ke tatwon kaa varnan karata he or is prakaar em 1 model kaa. yah, udhaaharan ke liye, yumail main likhe gaye model honge. aakhri parat M0-parat yaa data parat he. yah asli duniya kii vastuon kaa varnan karne ke liye upyog kii jaati he.
 em 3 model ke pare meta-vastu suvidha, COBRA interphes, jo un parichaalanon kaa varnan karte hai, use paribhaashit karte hue metamodel or model ko banane env unka herpher karne kaa varnan karti he. menta-vastu suvidha em3-model or yumail sanrachana model ke bich kii samanta ke kaaran meta-vastu suvidha meta modles aamtaur par yumail varg chitra ke rup main model kiye jate hai. XMI meta-vastu suvidha kaa ek samarthan maanak he, jo em3-, em2- yaa em1- parat par modal ke liye ek XML-aadhaarit nivimay swaroop paribhaashit karata he.
 haalanki yumail ek vyapak rup se maanya or istemaal kiya gaya modelling maanak he, uski aksar nimnlikhit ke liye aalochana kii gai he:
 bartrend meyar kii "yumail: da positive spin", or "yujiz and abyuziz of da steriotip mekensm in yumail 1.x and 2.0" main bryan henderson-sellers sahit, modelling visheshgyon ne yumail kii teevr aalochana lipibaddh kii he.
