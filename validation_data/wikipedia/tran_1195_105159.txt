 bharat kaa mantrimandal bharat sarkaar kaa samuhik nirnay lene vaali parishad hoti he. isamen pradhanmantri or anya sabhi cabinet mantri shaamil hote hai.
 sabhi mantri nai dilli sthit sambandhit kendriya mantralayon main baithte hai. bharat ke samvidhan ke anusaar sabhi mantri aavashyak rup se saansad hone chahie.
 bharat main mantriyon kii teen shreniyan hoti hai:
 yah suchi bharat sarkaar ke vartmaan mantriyon kii he:
