 sant gora kumbhaar  bhakthi andolan or maharashtra ke warkari sampradaay se jude ek hindu sant the.
 gora kumbhaar or anya santon ne abhang ke saikadon geeton ko bhi likhaa or gaaya. warkari sampradaay kaa mukhya siddhant kirthan kaa dainik jap tha.
 gora kumbhaar ko paaramparik rup se satyapuri gaanv main rahane vaala mana jaataa he, jise vartmaan main maharashtra rajya ke osmanabad jile main gorba ter ke rup main janaa jaataa he. mana jaataa he ki vah namdev ke samakaleen the. vah 1267 or 1317 ke bich jiye. uske naam par ek chota sa mandir gaanv main banaaya gaya tha or jise unke bhakton pujas karne aate he.
