 pashupatinath mandir nepal kii rajdhani kathmandu se teen kilometre uttar-pashchim main baagmati nadi ke kinare devpatan gaanv main sthit ek hindu mandir he. nepal ke ek dharmanirpeksh raashtra banane se pehle yah mandir rashtriya devtaa, bhagwaan pashupatinath kaa mukhya nivas mana jaataa tha. yah mandir unesco vishwa saanskrithik viraasat sthal kii suchi main soochibaddh he.pashupatinath main aasthaa rakhane waalon ko mandir parisar main pravesh karne kii anumati he. gaiyr hindu aagantukon ko ise baahar se baagmati nadi ke duusare kinare se dekhne kii anumati he. yah mandir nepal main shiv kaa sabase pavitra mandir mana jaataa he. 15 veen shatabdi ke raja pratap mall se shuru hui parampara he ki mandir main chaar pujari or ek mukhya pujari dakshin bharat ke brahmanon main se rakhe jate hai. pashupatinath main shivaratri kaa parv vishesh mahatv ke saath manaaya jaataa he. 
 
 kinvadantiyon ke anusaar is mandir kaa nirmaan somdev rajvansh ke pashupreksh ne teesri sadi isa purv main karaayaa tha kintu uplabdh aitihaasik dastauez 13veen shatabdi ke hi hai. is mandir kii kai naklon kaa bhi nirmaan hua he jinamein bhaktpur, lalitpur or banaras shaamil hai. mul mandir kai baar nasht hua. ise vartmaan swaroop naresh bhupalendra malla ne 1697 main pradaan kiya.
 nepal mahatmaya or himavatkhand par aadhaarit sthaneey kinvadanti ke anusaar bhagwaan shiv ek baar varanasi ke anya devataaon ko chhodkar baagmati nadi ke kinare sthit mrigasthali chale gaye, jo baagmati nadi ke duusare kinare par jangal main he. bhagwaan shiv vahaan par chinkaare kaa rup dhaaran kar nidra main chale gaye. jab devataaon ne unhen khoja or unhen varanasi waapas laane kaa prayaas kiya to unhone nadi ke duusare kinare par chalaang laga di. is dauraan unka sing chaar tukdon main tut gaya. iske baad bhagwaan pashupati chaturmukh ling ke rup main prakat hue.
 bharat ke uttrakhand rajya main sthit prasiddh kedarnath mandir kii kinvadanti ke anusaar pandavon ko swargprayan ke samay bhainse ke swaroop main shiv ke darshan hue the jo baad main dharti main samaa gaye lekin bhim ne unki punch pakad lee thi. aise main us sthaan par sthapit unka swaroop kedarnath kahalaaya, tathaa jahaan par dharti se baahar unka mukh prakat hua, vah pashupatinath kahalaaya.
