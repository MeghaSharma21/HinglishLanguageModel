 shaitani dharm kai ardh-dharmik sampradaayon kaa naam he jo ek achche ishwar ke bajaay dusht shaitan ko maanate hai. inamen se kuch shaitan ko ek devtaa maanakar uski sahi main puja karte hai, par kuch anya naastik hote hai or "shaitan" shabd kaa arth is duniya kii kaam-vaasana or laalach ke liye lagaate hai. ye shaitanvaadee asal main paramparaagat dharmon kii khilli udaane ke liye hi dushtataa ke pratik kii puja karte hai. inaki puja paddatiyon main zyaadaatar pashubali, narbali, hatya or aatmahatya jaisi gandi chizein hoti hai. paramparaagat shaitanvaadee kaala jaadu bhi karte hai.
