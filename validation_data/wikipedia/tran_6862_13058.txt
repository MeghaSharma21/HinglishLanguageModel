 masini kaaryon ke sandarbh main, pehle se banaae gayi kisi chhidr ko adhik chaudaa karne kii kriya ko vedhan kehete hai. vedhan kaa upyog adhik shuddhta ke saath kisi diye hue vyas kaa chhed nirmit karne ke liye kiya jaataa he. iskaa upyog kramash: badalata hua vyas vaala chhed karne ke liye bhi kiya jaataa he.
