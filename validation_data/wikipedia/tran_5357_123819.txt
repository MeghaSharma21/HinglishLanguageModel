 hazaron saalon se kutton kaa chaynaatmak taur par prajnan kiya jaataa rahaa he. kabhi ek hi vansh ke kutton ke antahprajnan dwaara to kabhi bahut alag nasl ke kutton ke mishran dwaara. yah prakriya aaj bhi jari he. is prakriya ke parinamswaroop sankar beej kii tarah vividh prakaar ke kute paidaa ho rahe hai masalan chihuahua se lekar great den tak. dekha jaye to prithvi par kutta hi ek aisa jaanavar he, jiske rup-rang main vyapak bhinnata paayi jaati he. 
 yah sab kutton ke diene main manushya jati kii apeksha lagbhag dugune chromosome hone kii vajah se hua he. yah kutton kaa diene hi he jo unke shaareerik gathan or rup-rang kii atyadhik vistrut bhinnata ko anumat karata he.
 naslon kaa aam taur par vargeekaran jis vishesh jati se vah nasl viksit kii gayi he usee ke aadhaar par kiya jaataa he. kutton kii mul naslein is prakaar hai: 
 iske alaava kutton ke kai anya prakaar or upaprakaar bhi hai. or vistrut va sampurna suchi ke liye angreji vikipedia par kutton ke prakaaron kii suchi or nasl samooh kii suchi dekhen. 
 inaki anya shreniyon main shaamil hai:
 nimnlikhit suchi main "nasl" kii ek prashast vyakhya prayukt he. yahan soochibaddh naslein, panjibaddh naslon ke rup main lambe itihaas vaali parmparik naslein, apane swayan kii panjiyan vaali durlabh naslein, yaa abhi bhi vikasadhin nai naslein bhi ho sakati hai. is suchi main kute ko sabase adhik prayukt naam ke saath keval ek hi baar shaamil kiya he.
