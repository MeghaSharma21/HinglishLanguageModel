 bahukoshikeey jeev vah jeev hote hai jinamein ek se adhik koshikaaye hon. bahukoshikeey jeev banane ke liye in koshikaon ko ek-duusare ko pahachaanakar jud jane kii zaroorat hoti he. bina sookshmbin ke dikh sakane vaale lagbhag sabhi jeev bahukoshikeey hote hai or keval lagbhag ek darjan hi eckoshikeey jeev hai jo bina sookshmbin ke dikh saken. aise bhi kuch jeev hai, jaise ki dicteostelium jo alag-alag paristhitiyon main kabhi eckoshikeey or kabhi bahukoshikeey hote hai.
