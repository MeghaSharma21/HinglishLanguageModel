 bharat ke dakshini bhaag ko dakshin bharat bhi kehete hai. apni samskruti, itihaas tathaa prajateey mul kii bhinnata ke kaaran yah shesh bharat se alag pahchaan bana chukaa he. halanki itnaa bhinn hokar bhi yah bharat kii vividhta kaa ek angamaatr he.
 dakshin bhartiya log mukhyatah dravid bhashaa jaise telugu ,tamil, kannada or malayalam bolte hai or mukhyatah dravid mul ke hai.
 carbon dating paddati se yah pataa chalaa he ki is kshetra main isa purv 8000 se maanav basaav rahaa he. lagbhag 1000 isa purv se louh yug kaa sutarapaat hua. malabar or tamil log sangam pracheen kaal main yunaan or rome se vyapaar kiya karte the. ve rome, yunaan, china, arab, yahudee aadi logon ke sampark main the. pracheen dakshin bharat main vibhinn samayon tathaa kshetron main vibhinn shaskaon tathaa rajwanshon ne raaj kiya. satvaahan, cher, chol, pandya, chalukya, pallav, hoysal, rashtrakut aadi aise hi kuch rajvansh hai. madhyakaalin yug ke aarambhik madhya main kshetra muslim shashan tathaa prabhaav ke adheen rahaa. sabase pehle tughlqon ne dakshin main apana prabhaav badhaayaa. allaudin khilji ne yun to madurai tak apana sainik abhiyaan chalaaya tha par uski mrityu ke baad uskaa samrajya tik nahin sakaa. san 1323 main yahan turkon dwaara muslim bahamani sultanate kii sthapana hui. iske kuch saalon baad hindu vijaynagar samrajya kii sthapana hui. in donon main satta ke liye sangharsh hota rahaa. san 1565 main vijaynagar kaa patan ho gaya. bahamani sultanate ke patan ke kaaran 5 nae samrajya bane - bijapur tathaa golkonda sabase shaktishaalee the. aurangzeb ne satrahavin sadi ke ant main dakkan main apana prabhutv jamaa liya par isi samay shivaji ke netritv main maraathon kaa udai ho rahaa tha. maraathon kaa shashan attharahavin sadi ke uttarardh tak rahaa jiske baad mysore tathaa anya sthaneey shaskaon kaa udai hua. par iske 50 varshon ke bheetar puure dakshin bharat par angrezon kaa adhikaar ho gaya. 1947 main swarajya aayaa.
