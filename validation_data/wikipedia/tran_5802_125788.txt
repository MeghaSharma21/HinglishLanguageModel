 henry ford america main ford motor company ke sansthaapak the. ve aadhunik yug kii baari maatra main utpaadan ke liye prayukt asembli line ke janak the. haalanki ford ne asembli line kaa avishkaar nahin kiya, lekin ford ne pehali automobile nirmit or viksit kii jise kai madhyam varg ke america bardaasht kar sakate the. unhone "model tii" naamak gaadi nikaali jisane yatayaat evam america udyog main kranti laa di. ve mahaan avishkaarak bhi the. unhen america ke 161 patent praapt hue the. ford company ke maalik ke rup main ve sansar ke sabase dhani evam vikhyath vyaktiyon main se the. unhone apni adhikansh sampatti ford foundation ke naam kar diya or aisi vyavastha bana di ki vah sthaayee rup se unke hi parivaar ke niyantran main bani rahe.
 henry ford kaa janm mishigan rajya ke dearborne naamak nagar main hua tha. inke pita irelandwasi the, kintu apane mata-pita tathaa anya sambandhiyon ke saath america aakar dearborne ke aas paas san 1847 main bas gaye or kheti karne lage. hener ford ne 15 varsh kii umr tak skool main shiksha pai or ve khet par bhi kaam karte rahe, kintu inhen aarambh se hi sab prakaar ke yantron ke prati kutuhal or aakarshan rahaa. pita ke manaa karne par bhi raat main gupt rup se ye padosiyon tathaa anya logon kii ghadiyaan yaa anya yantra laakar muft marammat karne main lage rahete the.
 16 varsh kii umr main ye ghar chhodkar ditroit chale gaye. yahan kai kaarkaanon main kaam karke inhone yantric vidya kaa gyaan praapt kiya. san 1886 main ye ghar waapas aae, pita kii di hui 80 acre bhoomi par bas gaye or vahiin mashin marammat karne kaa ek kaarkaana kholaa. san 1887 main inkaa vivaah hua tathaa isi varsh inhone gas engine or kheton par baari kaam karanevaali mashin banane kii ek yojana banaai, kintu yantron kii ore vishesh aakarshan ke kaaran ye ghar par na tik sake or phir ditroit chale aae.
 san 1890 main inhone ditroit edison electric company main kaam karnaa aarambh kiya or san 1893 main petrol se chalanevaali pehali gaadi banaai, jisamen chaar ashwashakti tak utpann hoti thi or jisaki gati 25 mile prati ghanta thi. san 1893 main inhone duusari gaadi banani prarambh kii tathaa san 1899 main electric company kii naukari chhodkar ditroit automobile company kii sthapana kii. phir is company ko chhodkar ye daud main bhaag lenevaali gaadiyaan banane lage. in gadiyon ne kai daudon main safalataa pai, jisase inkaa bada naam hua. is prasiddhi ke kaaran ye san 1903 main ford motor company sthapit karne main safal hue.
 pratham varsh main ford motor company ne do silinder tathaa aath ashwashaktivaali 1,708 gaadiyaan banaain. inaki bikri se company ko shat pratishat laabh hua. duusare varsh 5,000 gaadiyaan bikin. ford is company ke adhyaksh ho gaye or ant main anya hissedaaron ko hataakar apane ekmaatr putr, adsell bryant ford, ke sahit sapurn company ke maalik ho gaye. inkaa uddeshy halaki, tivrgami, drudh kintu, sasti motor gadiyon kaa nirmaan karnaa tha. isamen safalataa praapt karne ke liye inhone mashin ke angon ke maanakeekaran, pragami sangyojan, vyapak bikri tathaa unchi mazdooree dene ke siddhanton ko apanaaya. inhone kheti ke liye tractor bhi banaae. san 1924 tak inaki company ne 20 lakh gaadiyaan, trek or tractor banaae the, kintu san 1931 tak inke sab kaarkaanon main nirmit gadiyon kii sankhya do karod tak pahunch gai.
 ford main adarshavadita tathaa kattarpan kaa vicitra sanmishran tha. ye punjotpaadan ke pakshpaati the, kintu inkaa yah bhi vichaar tha ki udyog ko is prakaar vikendrit karnaa chahie ki kheti ke saath saath kaarkaanon kaa kaam bhi chale. ye unchi mazdooree dene ke paksh main the, kintu majdoor sanghon ke ghor virdohi the; yahan tak ki apane kaarkaanon main sanghon ko panpane na dene ke vichar se ye bhediyon tathaa sashastr police se kaam lete the. shanti ke ye kattar pakshpaati the, kintu naatziyon kii bhanti ye yahudee virdohi the. bainkon or mahajanon se bhi inaki nahin patati thi. pratham vishwayuddh ke samay inhone kuch prabhaavshaalee logon ko ekatrit kar ""auskar dwitiya"" naamak shanti pot par europe kii yatra is vishwaas se kii ki yah abhiyaan yuddh band karaane main samarth hoga. yah sab hote hue bhi dehati jivan ke prati pakshpaat tathaa america kii vigat ritiyon tathaa smritichihnon ke prati atoot shraddha rakhane ke karan inhone badi lokapriyata praapt kii thi.
 inaki gananaa sansar ke sarvapradhan dhanapatiyon main thi. inhone dearborne main ek audyogic sangrahalay tathaa edison institute ov technology kii sthapana kii. mrityu ke purv inhone apni sampatti kaa adhikansh apane naam par sthapit janhitaishi sanstha ko de diya. yah sanstha sansar kii lokopkaarak sansthaaon main sabase dhani he. san 1947 main inaki mrityu hui. apni mrityu se do varsh purv hi inhone apane pote, henry ford dwitiya, ko company kaa adhyaksh bana diya tha.
