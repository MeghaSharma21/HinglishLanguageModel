 panj nadi yaa panj nadi madhya asia main sthit ek nadi he jo aamu dariyaa kii ek upanadi bhi he. yah nadi 1,125 kimi lambi he. afghanistan or tajikistan kii antarrashtriya seemaa kaa ek bada hissaa isi nadi isi nadi ko mana jaataa he. panj nadi purvi afghanistan main qila-e-panjeh gaanv ke paas vaakhaan nadi or pamir nadi ke vilay se banati he. phir yah pashchim chalati he or aage chalakar vaksh nadi se mil jaati he, jiske baad inhen amu dariyaa ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
