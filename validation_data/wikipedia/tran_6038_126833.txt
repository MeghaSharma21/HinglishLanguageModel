 kryptogam un padpon ko kehete hai jinamein prajnan bijaanuon kii sahaayta se hota he. greek bhashaa main 'kryptose' kaa arth 'gupt' tathaa 'gamin' kaa arth 'vivaah karnaa' he. arthath kryptogam padpon main beej utpann nahin hota.
 kryptogam ko kabhi-kabhi 'thalophita', 'nimna padam', tathaa 'bijaanu padap' aadi anya naamon se bhi janaa jaataa he. kryptogam samooh pushpodbhid samooh ke 'vilom samooh' he. kryptogam main shaiwal, likene, moss or fern aate hai.
 ek samay kryptogam ko padap jagat main ek samooh ke rup main manyata thi. karl linius ne padpon ke apane vargeekaran main sampoorn padap jagat ko 24 virgon main baanta tha jisamen se ek 'kryptogamia' tha jisko punah chaar ganon main vibhajit kiya gaya tha - shaiwal, massi yaa bryophite, philices yaa fern tathaa kavak.
 aadhunik vargiki ne kryptogam ko padap jagat main sthaan nahin diya he kyonki samajha gaya ki kryptogam kai asangat samooho kaa jamghat maatr he. vartmaan vargiki ke anusaar kryptogam main aane vaale keval kuch padpon ko hi padap jagat main sthaan diya gaya he, sabhi ko nahin. visheshtah kavak ko ek alag jagat ke rup main sweekaar kiya gaya he or unhen padpon ke bajaay jantuon kaa nikat sambandhi mana jaataa he. isi prakaar kuch shaiwalon ko ab jeevanuon kaa sambandhi maan liya gaya he.
 shaiwal to parnaharithyukt kryptogam hai. jo kryptogam parnaharith rahit hai, un virgon ke naam hai : jeevanu athwa bacteria, vishaanu yaa virus or kavak yaa fungi hai.
 bacteria - isko shysomicophita yaa shysomicota samooh main rakhaa jaataa he. ye atyant sookshm, lagbhag chah yaa saat micron tak lambey hote hai. ye manushyon ke liye hitkar or ahitkar donon prakaar ke hote hai.
 virus - virus bacterion se bahut chote hote hai. sookshm se sookshm sookshmdarshiyon se bhi ye dekhe nahin ja sakate. inaki chaaya kaa adhyayan electron sookshmdarshiyon dwaara kiya jaataa he, jo inke aakaar ko 5,000 guna badha dete hai. ye bhi paudhon, jantuon tathaa manushyon main naanaa prakaar kii bimaariyaan paidaa karte hai.
 mixomicites - yah bhi ek chota sa parnaharith rahit samooh he. inke sharir ke chaaron ore diwaar praaia: nahin ke baraabar hi hoti he. ye liblibe hote hai. inke kuch udhaaharan dicteostelium, plasmodium ityadi hai.
  kavak - yah parnrahit rahit padap kaa sarvapramukh gana he, jiske teen mukhya upagan hai : 
 in upaganon ko aajkal or unchaa sthaan diya jane laga he. phicomicitiwwaale fafoond ke bich kii diwaar nahin hoti or ye sinositic kehalaate hai. ascomicites bhi chote fafoond hai. yeast se davaaiyaan banati hai or sharaab banane main iskaa yoga atyaavashyak he.
 yah ek bada, lagbhag 16,00 jatiyon kaa gana he. ye ped kii chaal athwa chattan par adhik ugte hai tathaa shaiwal or kavak donon se milakar banate hai. inke teen rup hai :
 parnil jo ham sookshmdarshi main dekh sakate he.
