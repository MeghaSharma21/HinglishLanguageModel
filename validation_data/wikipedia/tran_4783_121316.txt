 shweta treepathi ek bhartiya abhinetri he. unhone kai doordarshan shrinkhalaa or filmon me kaam kiya he. vah digni channel par 'kya mast he life' siries main zeenia khan kii bhoomika ke kaaran sabase jaada jaani jaati he. vah tata ski downlod or mcdonalds or haal hi main tata tii ke liye vigyaapan main dikhaai di he.
 treepathi kaa janm 6 july, 1985 ko dilli main hua tha. mumbai ke sthanantarit hone se pehle vah kuch samay ke liye andaman or nicobar dweep samooh main rahete the. unhone deepies aarake puram, nai dilli main apni praathmik shiksha hasil kii or nift, dilli se snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii.
