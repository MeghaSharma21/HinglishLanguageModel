 bantiy mianchiya praant dakshinpurvi asia ke cambodia desh kaa ek praant he. yah desh ke pashchimottar kone main sthit he or pashchim main padosi thailand desh ke sa kaio or bureeram praanton se seemaa rakhata he.
 "bantiy mianchiya" kaa arth "vijay kaa qila" hota he. "chiya" shabd khmer bhashaa main sanskrit ke "jai" shabd kaa rup he or "bantiy" khmer main "qile" kaa arth rakhata he.
