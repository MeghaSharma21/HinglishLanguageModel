 bhartiya janata party ke sansadeey adhyaksh narendra modi kaa 26 may 2014 se bharat ke 15wein pradhanantri kaa kaarykaal armbh hua. modi ke saath 45 anya mantriyon ne bhi samaaroh main pad or gopneeyata kii shapath grahan kii. modi sahit kul 46 main se 36 mantriyon ne hindi main shapath lee jabaki anya 10 ne angrezi main shapath grahan kii.
 16 may 2014 ko aam chunaavon ke parinaam ghoshit hone ke baad 20 may ko modi bharat ke raashtrapati pranab mukherjee se mili. mukherjee ne modi ko agali sarkaar banane ke liye aamantrit kiya. bhaajapa ne is chunaav main 282 siten jiti hai jabaki unke gathbandhan rashtriya janatantric gathbandhan ne kul 336 lok sabha siton par jeet darj kii. yah 1984 ke chunaav ke baad ab tak kaa sabase adhik bahumat he jo kisi ek party athwa dal ko mila ho. 1984 main bhartiya rashtriya congress vijayi rahi thi. tab modi ne 26 may 2014 ko shaam 06:00 baje shapath grahan karne kii ghoshnaa kii.
 shapath grahan samaaroh dilli sthit raashtrapati bhawan praangan main hua. isse pehle keval purv pradhanmantri chandrasekhar or atal bihari vaajapeyi ne shapath grahan kii. darbar haul ek anya sambhavit sthaan tha lekin isaki darshak kshamta 500 tak simit hone ke kaaran asweekrut kar diya gaya. bhaajapa ne sanket diya ki samaaroh khule maidan par kii ja sakati he. isse pehle narendra modi gujarat ke mukhyamantri pad kii shapath bhi khule maidan main le chuke hai.
 atithi suchi main vibhinn rajyon or raajnneetik partiyon ke adhyaksh sahit saarc deshon ke rashtradhyakshon ko aamantrit kiya gaya he. atah is ghatana ko vrahat "pramukh rajanyik ghatana" ke rup main bhi dekha ja rahaa he.
 saarc deshon ke pramukhon ne samaaroh main bhaag liya.
 srilanka or pakistan ne bhartiya machhuvaaron ko reeha kiya. modi ne shapath grahan samaaroh main aamantrit deshon ke is kadam kaa swaagat kiya.
 is samaaroh main bharat ke sabhi rajyon ke mukhyamantriyon ko aamantrit kiya gaya tha. inamen se karnataka ke mukhyamantri, siddharmaiya or kerala ke mukhyamantri, umman chandi ne bhaag lene se manaa kar diya. bhaajapa or congress ke baad sabase adhik siton par vijay praapt karne vaali tamilnadu kii mukhyamantri jayalalita ne samaaroh main bhaag na lene kaa nirnay liya jabaki pashchim bengal ke mukhyamantri mamata banerjee ne apni jagah mukul roy or amit mitra ko bhejne kaa nirnay liya.
 madhya pradesh ke mukhyamantri, shivaraj singh chouhan ne apane purna mantrimandal ke saath vaayu-yaan kiraaye par liya jisase samaaroh main upasthit hokar usee raatri vaapasi kar saken.
 anya aamantrit hastiyon main anupam kher, madhur bhandarkar, vivek oberoi, rajnikant or amitabh bachchan shaamil hai.
 vadodara ke ek chaay vikreta kiran mahida jinhone modi kii ummidavaari prastaavit kii thi ko bhi samaaroh main aamantrit kiya gaya. inke alaava modi kii maan hiraben or anya teen bhai bhi samaaroh main upasthit rahenge.
 iske atirikt udyogajagat ke log sapariwaar mukesh ambani unke bhai anil ambani, kumaar mangalam birla or gautam adaani bhi samaaroh main upasthit rahe.
