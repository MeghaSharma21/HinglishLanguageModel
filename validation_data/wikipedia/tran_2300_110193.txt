release kiya gaya tha jise get e grip or nine livs ke dauron par kiye gaye pradarshnon kii samagriyon se taiyaar kiya gaya tha. release hone ke turant baad hi is elbum ko platinum kii pramanikta mil gai. band ne 1999 main nine livs or "ai dont waant tu miss e thing" ekal ko protsaahit karne ke liye prakat rup se apane na khatm hone vaale vishwa daure ko jari rakhaa.
 1999 main, arosmith ko digni hollywood studios ke walt digni world ke rock 'en' roller coster staring arosmith naamak sawari main ke digniland paris main) dikhaaya gaya jahaan unhone sawari kaa soundtrack or vishay-geet pradaan kiya. 9 sitambar 1999 ko, steven tyler evam jo peri, ran-di.em.si. ke saath phir se ek ho gaye or amtivi video music awards main "walk dis ve" ke ek sahyogaatmak live pradarshan ke liye kid rock bhi unke saath shaamil hue, jo girls of samar tour kaa ek agradoot tha. band ne japan ke ek sankshipt daure ke saath nai sahasrabdi kaa jashn manaaya, or charlie'sa anjals naamak 2000 kii film main "anjals ai" naamak gane kaa yogadan bhi kiya. 2000 ke antim daur main, unhone apane agle album par kaam karnaa shuru kiya.
 ==="just push play", "o, ye!" or rockismas maximus === band ne janvari 2001 main 'en seenk, britaini spirs, meri je. blij or neli jaise pop kalakaaron ke saath super boul XXXV ke ardhkalik kaarykram main pradarshan karke apane agle dashak main pravesh kiya. sabhi kalakaaron ne "walk dis ve" ke ek pradarshan ke ant main arosmith kaa saath diya.
 march 2001 main band ne just push play naamak apana 13waan studio album release kiya, jisane turant platinum kii pramanikta hasil kar lee, jisaki kamyaabi kaa mukhya aadhaar "jaded" naamak ek top 10 ekal or dose vigyapanon main dikhaaya gaya sheershak geet tha. march 2001 ke antim daur main, unke album ke release hone ke turant baad unhen rock and role haul of fame main shaamil kiya gaya. arosmith, haul of fame main shaamil hone vaala ekmaatr band he jiskaa ek geet chart main bana hua he. baad main usee varsh, band ne 9/11 peediton or unke pariwaron ke liye washington di.si. main United We Stand: What More Can I Give laabhakaari sangeet kaarykram ke hisse ke rup main pradarshan kiya. apane just push play tour ke hisse ke rup main, usee raat ek kaarykram ke liye band ne indianapolis ke liye waapas udaan bhari.
 band ne just push play tour ke samaapan or saath main viech1 par bihaind da music naamak apane vishesh kaarykram ke kuch anshon kii recording se 2002 kaa shuruyaat kiya, jisane na keval band ke itihaas ko, balki band ke maujuuda gatividhiyon or dauron ko bhi lipibaddh kiya. yah vishesh kaarykram, do ghanton tak chalane vaale kuch bihaind da music main se ek tha. 27 june ko band ne bi'za ke saath ek sahyog live kaa pradarshan kiya. jab fifa ne arosmith ko kaarykram ke liye pradarshan karne kii peshkash kii, to band ne bi'za ke saath stage par jane kii shart ke saath is peshkash ko sweekaar kiya. july 2002 main, arosmith ne o, ye! ultimate arosmith hits naamak ek sankalan release kiya jo unki jivan-vritti kaal ke albamon kaa do-discon vaala ek album samooh tha jisamen "girls of samar" naamak ek nayaa ekal shaamil tha or iske baad band ne kid rock or ran-di.em.si. ke udghaatan ke saath girls of samar tour kii shuruyaat kii. o, ye! ko uske baad se do baar platinaam kii pramanikta di ja chuki he. amtivi ne 2002 main apane amtiviican se arosmith ko sammanit kiya. pradarshnon main pink kaa cover geet "jeni haiz got e gan" bhi shaamil tha. shakira ne "dud " kaa, kid rock ne "mamaa kin" or "last child" kaa, train ne "dream on" kaa or papa roach ne "sweet imoshan" kaa pradarshan kiya. iske alaava, prashansa patron main aashcharya atithi metalika ke saath-saath jenet jackson, limp bijkit gayak fred darst, elicia silverstone or mila kunis bhi shaamil the. 2003 main, arosmith ne apane bluez album ke release kii taiyaari main kis ke saath rockismas maximus tour kii sah-sheershata kii. unhone rugrats go wild ke liye "lizard lav" naamak ek gane kaa bhi pradarshan kiya.
 arosmith ke honking on bobo naamak chirkaalik-vachanabaddh bluez album ko 2004 main release kiya gaya. yah band ke mul main ek vaapasi thi, jiske tahat unhone live satron main album kii recording kii, purv nirmaataa jaik duglas ke saath kaam kiya or apane bluez-rock ko majboot kiya. iske baad unhone disambar 2004 main you gota move naamak ek live dividi ko release kiya jise honking on bobo tour ke pradarshnon se liya gaya tha. "dream on" ko bhi 2004 main buik ke ek vigyaapan abhiyaan main shaamil kiya gaya tha, jiskaa lakshya marq kaa vah bazaar tha jisaki rachana ab zyaadaatar un logon se hui he jo is gane ke pehali baar chart main aane ke samay kishor the.
 2005 main steven tyler ko bi kool naamak film main dekha gaya. jo peri ne usee varsh apane swa-shirshit ekaaki album ko release kiya. 2006 gremi awards main, unhen "marsi" geet ke liye best rock instrumental paraformens ke liye manonith kiya gaya, lekin les paul se haar gaye. oktober 2005 main arosmith ne rocking da joint naamak ek sidi/dividi ko release kiya. band ne america ke sabase bade bazaaron main rangamanchon ke ek patjhad/sheetakaaleen daure ke liye leni crevitz ke saath 30 oktober ko rocking da joint tour ke liye sadakon par dhooma machaaya. band ne vasantkaal main chip trick ke saath daura karne kaa yojana banaaya or america main dwitiyak bazaaron main dhooma machaaya. haalanki, daure ke is padaav ke lagbhag sabhi kaaryakramon ko radd kar diya gaya. in kaaryakramon ko shuru main ek-ek karke 22 march 2006 tak radd kiya jaataa rahaa, jab ghoshnaa kii gai ki mukhya gayak steven tyler ko gale kii surgery karwaane kii jarurat thi or uske baad daure ke shesh kaaryakramon ko bhi radd kar diya gaya.
 2006 ke sashastr bal diwas ke avasar par arosmith ne ek nae album kii recording kii shuruyaat kii. tyler or peri ne 2006 main asplaned main apane vaarshik 4 july ke sangeet kaarykram ke liye boston pops orcestra ke saath pradarshan kiya, unka yah pradarshan ek mile kaa pathar tha kyonki steven tyler ke gale kii surgery ke baad se unka pehla pramukh kaarykram yaa pradarshan tha. isi samay ke aasapaas, band ne yah bhi ghoshnaa kii ki ve 2006 ke antim daur main motli kru ke saath root of ol ival tour kii shuruyaat karenge. 24 agast 2006 ko ghoshnaa kii gai ki tom hamiltan apane gale ke cancer kaa ilaaj karvaa rahe the. puri tarah se swasthya laabh praapt karne ke uddeshy se, phir se swasth hone tak ve root of ol ival tour ke adhikansh kaaryakramon main sammilit nahin hue. purv jo peri project basavadak david hal ne hamiltan kii vaapasi tak unki jagah pradarshan kiya tha. 5 sitambar 2006 ko, arosmith ne ohio ke columbus main motli kru ke saath root of ol ival tour kii shuruyaat kii. is sah-sheershata vaale daure ne donon band ko 24 navambar se uttar america kii rangbhumiyon main apana johar dikhaane kaa avasar pradaan kiya. uske baad, kuch gine-chune rangbhumiyon main kaaryakramon kaa aayojan kiya gaya jisamen se kuch kaarykram unhone moteli kru ke saath kiya tha. is daure kaa ant 17 disambar ko hua.
 17 oktober 2006 ko devil haiz got e new disgise - da veri best of arosmith naamak ek sankalan album release kiya gaya. is album main pichley hit gaanon ke saath do nae gaanon - "devil haiz got e new disgise" or "sadona sunraise", ko bhi shaamil kiya gaya tha, jo is album ke liye phir se record kiye gaye puraane outtek the. "devil haiz got e new disgise" ne menstream rock treks chart par #15 par pahunchane main kamyaabi hasil kii. is album ko soni ke saath arosmith ke anubandh ko puraa karne or band ke nae studio album ke release hone tak prashansakon kaa manoranjan karne ke iraade se banaaya gaya tha.
 2007 ke armbh main, band ne ek nae world tour kaa elaan kiya jo uttar america yaa japan ke baahar kiye jane vaale kaaryakramon ko shaamil karne vaala lagbhag ek dashak tak chalane vaala unka pehla daura tha. band ne apane europea daure ko protsaahit karne ke liye farvari 2007 main landan ke hard rock cafe main pradarshan kiya jisamen hard rock cafe dwaara pryojit hide park colling samaaroh ke hisse ke rup main hide park main ek raat kaa kaarykram bhi shaamil tha. vasantkaal main, band ne latin america kaa daura kiya jahaan unke kaarykram ko dekhne ke liye darshakon kii bheed ne stadium kii sari tikaten pehle se hi kharid lee thi. garmiyon main, band ne europe kaa daura kiya or vahaan kai pramukh rock samaaroh main pradarshan kiya or ve kuch aise deshon main bhi gaye jahaan unhone pehle kabhi pradarshan nahin kiya tha. iske atirikt, band ne pehali baar sanyukt arab emirate or bharat jaise asian deshon main bhi apana kaarykram prastut kiya. band ne july ke ant main california or canada main kuch gine-chune kaaryakramon main bhi pradarshan kiya. isi maheene kii ek taarikh, 21 july ko prince edward island main yojit ek sangeet kaarykram us kshetra ke itihaas kaa sabase bada sangeet kaarykram tha. sitambar main, band ne uttarpurvi uttar america ke pramukh bazaaron main aath kaaryakramon main pradarshan kiya. in kaaryakramon kaa udghaatan joan jet dwaara kiya gaya. band ne hawaii main bhi ek nijee gig kaa pradarshan kiya. moui main kiye jane vaale ek saarvajanik kaarykram ko kuch sahay-sahakaar sambandhi kaarnon se radd kar diya gaya, jisane band ke khilaaf ek varg karwai muqadame ko prerit kiya tha. april 2009 main, arosmith ne 20 oktober 2009 ko yojit hone vaale punarnirdhaarit moui kaarykram ke ek muft tikat ke saath radd kiye gaye kaarykram ke sabhi tikat khariddaaron kii kshatipurti karne or saath main kaarykram se sambandhit apane jeb se na kiye gaye sabhi kharch raashi ko waapas karne par sahmati vyakt kii.
 1 navambar 2007 ko band ne soni ke saath kiye gaye apane maujuuda anubandh ke antim studio album par kaam karnaa shuru kiya. mana jaataa he ki yah album pichley albamon se nikaale gaye or phir se record kiye gaye gaanon ke saath-saath nai or utkrisht samagriyon kaa ek mishran hoga. ek saakshaatkaar main, guitaravadak jo peri ne is raaj par se paradaa uthaaya ki ek nae album kaa nirmaan karne ko release kiya gaya tha jisamen unke sabase lokapriy gaanon main se kai gane shaamil hai. steven tyler ne 4 sitambar 2008 ko viech1 clasik radio par elaan kiya ki band ke 15wein studio album ko puraa karne ke liye arosmith sitambar 2008 ke ant main studio main pravesh karne kaa man bana liya he. yah 2001 ke just push play ke baad se mul samagriyon se banane vaala band kaa pehla album hoga. tyler ne yah bhi pushti kii ki band ne apane nae sheershakaheen album ke samarthan main june 2009 main ek nae america daure ko shuru karne kii yojana banaai he. is daure se pehle 1 farvari 2009 ko venezuela main ek sangeet kaarykram main pradarshan karnaa tha. haalanki, 15 janvari ko tyler ne kaha ki guitaravadak jo peri ke ghutanon main duusari baar chot lagane kii vajah se band is gig main apana "is baat kaa ehsaas hua ki garmiyon main sadakon par dhooma machaane se pehle is album kaa kaam puraa hone kaa koi mauka dikhaai nahin de rahaa tha." is daure main daure ke adhikansh kaaryakramon ke kaarykram bhi prastut kiya gaya.
 is daure ko june se sitambar 2009 tak puure uttar america main band ko le jane ke liye shuru kiya gaya tha. is daure ke pehle saat kaaryakramon ke dauraan band ke 1975 ke toys in da atic naamak album ke lagbhag sabhi gaanon kaa pradarshan kiya gaya or 1976 ke deep cat "yugma" par jo peri ke gaae gaye pramukh gaanon kaa bhi pradarshan kiya gaya tha. haalanki is daure ke dauraan kai swasthya samasyaon kii bharmaar ho gai thi. guitaravadak bread whitford ko apane car se nikalate samay sir par chot lag jane ke baad sir kii surgery se ubarne ke liye unhen daure ke pehle saat kaaryakramon main baithna padaa tha. 28 june 2009 ko connectikat ke ankaisvil main mohegan san arina main band ke daure ke saatven kaarykram main pramukh gayak steven tyler kii pair main chot lag gai jisaki vajah se agle saat kaaryakramon ko sthagit karnaa padaa. jaise hi band ne 15 july ko apane daure ko shuru kiya, whitford samooh main waapas laut aae lekin tom hamiltan ko gaiyr-aakraamak surgery se ubarne ke liye daure se prasthaan karnaa padaa tha. 5 agast 2009 ko south dakota ke stergis main ek sangeet kaarykram ke much se girane ke baad tyler ko turant aspataal le jaaya gaya. suraksha karmiyon ne unki madad kii or unhen manch ke peeche le gaye or uske baad guitaravadak jo peri ne darshakon ko kaarykram samaapt hone kii baat bataai. tyler ko rapid city riginal hospital le jaaya gaya jahaan unke sir or gardan kii chot ke saath-saath toote hue kandhe kaa bhi ilaaj kiya gaya. tyler kii choton ko dekhte hue band ko pashchimi canada main paanch kaaryakramon ko sthagit karne par baadhya hona padaa. 14 agast 2009 ko arosmith ne ghoshnaa kii ki tyler kii choton kii vajah se unhone jedjed top ke saath apane america daure ke shesh kaaryakramon ko radd karne kaa faisla kiya he.
 daure ke bich main, peri ne haev guitar, vil travel naamak apane paanchaven ekaaki album kaa kaam puraa kiya or dramvadak joy krimar ne hit hard naamak apni aatmajivani release kii. peri ke ekaaki album ko 6 oktober 2009 ko release kiya gaya.

 tyler 2009 ke ant main ek yojanabaddh dakshin america daure se nikal gaye or aisa laga jaise unka iraadaa apane ekaaki pariyojnaon main lage rahane kaa tha jisamen daz da noiz in maai head bodar you? naamak unki aatmajivani bhi shaamil thi. tyler ne clasik rock magazine ko bataaya, "mujhe nahin pataa ki main kya kar rahaa hum, lekin yah nishchit rup se kuch steven tyler banane ja rahaa he: apane khud ke brands - brands tyler par kaam kar rahaa hum." is bich, guitaravadak jo peri ne 2009 ke ant main sanyukt rajya america or 2010 ke armbh main japan or britain kaa daura kiya.
 navambar 2009 main, jo peri ne kaha ki tyler, band ke sampark main nahin the or ve erosemyth ko chhodane ke kagaar par ho sakate the. peri ne kaha ki samooh ke baaki sadsy "saath main kaam karne ke liye ek nae gayak kii talash kar rahe the." yah bhi khabar mili thi ki steven tyler kii jagah leni crevitz ko rakhane ke liye unhen prastaavit kiya gaya tha jise unhone us samay thukraa diya tha.

 20 janvari 2010 ko peri ne is baat kii pushti kii ki band, tyler kii jagah ek nae gayak ke liye audishan karne vaale the. peri ne kaha ki tyler ke pairon kii surgery unhen dedh saal tak "tasviir se baahar le" jayega or is bich, band ke baaki sadsy pradarshan jari rakhana chaahate the. peri ne yah bhi kaha ki yadi tyler chahen to band bhavishya main unke saath kaam karte rahane kaa icchhuk hoga. jawaab main, tyler ke vakil ne band or iske prabandhak ko ek "viraam evam virat" patr bheja or iske alaava donon ke khilaaf kanoonee karrwai karne kii dhamkee di yadi band ne tyler kii jagah kisi or ko rakhane kaa prayaas band nahin kiya.
 15 farvari 2010 ko, yah ghoshnaa kii gai ki arosmith june 2010 main england ke donnington park main downlod festival kii sheershata karne vaale hai. samaaroh protsaahak endi koping ne kaarykram ke pramukh kalakaar ke rup main steven tyler kii pushti kii he. yah bhi ghoshnaa kii gai thi ki 13 june ke kaarykram se pehle band solvesborg main 10 june ko sweden rock festival main ek upasthiti denge. 24 farvari ko band ne apane aagaamee cocked, locd, ready tu tour ke kaaryakramon ke pehle khep kii ghoshnaa kii. is daure main may ke liye anusuchit dakshin or madhya america main saat kaarykram shaamil hai jiske baad june or july ke liye anusuchit europe main gyaarah kaarykram honge. band is daure main pehali baar colombia, peru or greece kaa daura karenge. band ko uttar america main july ke ant main, agast main or sitambar ke armbh main 19 kaaryakramon main pradarshan karne ke liye bhi anusuchit kiya gaya he. daure ke hisse ke rup main, band boston ke phenve park main apane saathi bostenvaasiyon, je. gills band, ke saath kaarykram prastut karenge.
 
 
 
 arosmith ne ''billboard'' hot 100 ke top 40 main ikkis gane die hai.
 
 
 
 sangeet kii recording or pradarshan ke alaava, arosmith ne filmon, television, video game or sangeet video main bhi bhaag liya he. 1978 main, band ne sargent papers lonly hearts club band naamak film main "future vilan band" ke rup main abhinay kiya. baad main, 1980 ke dashak ke ant main or 1990 ke dashak main jab band ne khud ko phir se ek nae rup main prastut kiya, to arosmith ne atirikt prastutiyaan di, jisamen 1990 main saturde nite live par "vence world" kaa sketch, 1991 main da simpsans kaa "fleming mu'sa" episod or 1993 main vence world 2 naamak film bhi shaamil tha.
 band, 1994 main revolution eks, 1995 main quest for fame, or june 2008 main Guitar Hero: Aerosmith sahit kai video game kaa vishay rahaa he. band ne 30 se adhik pramukh sangeet video kaa nirmaan or saat home video yaa dividi bhi release kiya he.
 
 
 
 1970 ke dashak main arosmith kii lokapriyata or safalataa ke baavjood, 1980 ke dashak ke ant main or 1990 ke dashak main unki vaapasi tak unhone puraskaar or pramukh manyata hasil nahin kiya tha. 1987 main, arosmith ne ran-di.em.si. ke saath "walk dis ve" kii rimix ke liye sarvshereshth rap - ekal kii shrenee main sol train music award hasil kiya. 1990 main, arosmith ne kanth sangeet ke saath ek yugal yaa samooh dwaara sarvshereshth rock pradarshan kii shrenee main apana pehla gremi award hasil kiya or uske baad "jeni haiz got e gan", "living on da age", "krezi" or "pink" ke liye isi tarah ke kul chaar puraskaar hasil kiye . us shrenee main jite gaye puraskaron kii sankhya ke maamle main arosmith keval you2 ke baad duusare sthaan par he.
 iske alaava, arosmith ke sangeet video ne 1990 ke dashak bhar main anek puraskaar jite. arosmith ko ab tak aise das puraskaron ke saath amtivi video music awards main ab tak ke chauthe safal kalakaar kaa sthaan mila he. arosmith, sarvshereshth rock video or darshakon kii pasand kii shreniyon main ab tak kaa agrani band bhi he. arosmith ne video of da yar, best group video or best video from e film shreniyon main se prayek main ek-ek puraskaar hasil kiya he. jin video ke liye arosmith ne viemez jeeta he, unke naam hai - "jeni haiz got e gan", "da odar said", "living on da age", "kring", "folling in lav ", "pink" or "ai dont waant tu miss e thing".
 apane kariyar kii avadhi main, arosmith ne saat american music award, chaar billboard music award, do piples chwis award, solah boston music award or kai anya puraskaar evam sammaan sangrah kiye hai. arosmith ko mili ucch prashansa main se kuch main - 1990 main hollywoods rock walk main pravesh, 13 april 1993 ko tatkaaleen governor wiliam veld dwaara masachusets rajya main "arosmith diwas" kii ghoshnaa, 2001 main rock and role haul of fame main pravesh, or 2002 main amtiviican award se sammanit hona shaamil tha.
 praudyogiki or video game ke kshetron main, arosmith ne kai uplabdhiyaan hasil kii he. 1994 main, arosmith ne internet par "head first" naamak geet release kiya, jise online uplabdh sabase pehla puri lambaai vaala vanijyik utpaada mana jaataa he. 2008 main, arosmith, aisa pehla kalakaar bana jiskaa Guitar Hero: Aerosmith ke saath apane aasapaas aadhaarit guitar hero naamak ek sampoorn video game he.
 arosmith ne kai chart or album bikri uplabdhiyaan bhi hasil kii he jinamein - nau sadasyon kii ek samooh ke liye menstream rock treks chart par dwitiya sarvocch asankhya number ek ekal, "ai dont waant tu miss e thing" ke saath ek rock samooh dwaara ''billboard'' hot 100 par ekmaatr number ek armbh, or ek america samooh dwaara sarvaadhik swarna or bahu-platinum album shaamil hai. recording industry association of america se, arosmith ne ek hirak album or chaar swarna ekal ke alaava 25 swarna, 18 platinum or 12 bahu-platinum album praaanpatr praapt kiya he. duniya bhar main 150 million or sanyukt rajya america main 66.5 million elbamon kii bikri ke saath, arosmith sabase zyada bikne vaale america rock band he.
