 tripitak
 tripitak buddha dharm kaa pramukh granth he jise sabhi buddha sampradaay maanate he. yah buddha dharm ke pracheenatam granth he jisamen bhagwaan buddha ke upadesh sangrahit he. yah granth pali bhashaa main likhaa gaya he or vibhinn bhashaaon main anuvaadit he. is granth main bhagwaan buddha dwaara buddhatva praapt karne ke samay se mahaparinirvana tak die hue pravachanon ko sangrahit kiya gaya he. tripitak kaa rachanakaal yaa nirmaankaal isa purv 100 se isa purv 500 he.
 kii shrenee kaa hissaa
 tripitak ko teen bhaagon main vibhajit kiya gaya he, vinayapitak, suttapitak or abhidhamm pitak. iskaa vistaar is prakaar he- tripitak main 17 grantho kaa samavesh he.
 buddha-parampara ke anusaar tripitak teen sangitiyon se sthir hua. kaha jaataa he ki buddha ke parinirvana ke baad subhadr naamak bhikshu ne apane saathiyon se kaha- ‘‘aavuso, shok mat karo, rudan mat karo ! ham logon ko mahashraman se chhutkaaraa mil gaya he. ve hameshaa kehete rahete the- ‘yah karo, yah mat karo’. lekin ab ham jo chahenge karenge. jo nahin chahenge, nahin karenge.’’
 subhadr bhikshu ke ye vachan sunakar mahakashyap sthavir ko bhay hua ki kahin saddharm kaa naash na ho jaae. atev unhone vinay or dharm ke sansthaapan ke liye rajgrih main 500 bhikshuon kii ek sangeeti bulawaayi. buddha bhikshuon kii duusari sangeeti buddha-parinirvana ke 100 varsh baad vaishali main bulaai gayi. buddha-parinirvana ke 236 varsh baad patliputra main samraat ashok ke samay tiss moggaliputt ne teesri sangeeti bulaayi, jisamen therwaad kaa uddhaar kiya gaya.
 vartmaan tripitak vahi tripitak he jo sinhal ke raja vattagamani ke samay pratham shatabdi ke antim rup se sthir hua mana jaataa he.
 buddha tripitak anek drushtiyon se bahut mahatva kaa he. isamen buddhakalin bharat kii raajnneeti, arthniti, saamaajik vyavastha, shilpakala, sangeet, vastra-aabhushan, vesh-bhusha, reeti-rivaaj tathaa aitihaasik, bhugolic, vyapaarik aadi anek mahatvapurn vishyon kaa vistaar se pratipaadan he. udhaaharan ke liye, vinayapitak main buddha bhikshu-bhikshuniyon ke aachaar-vyavhaar sambandhi niyamon kaa vistrut varnan he. ‘mahavagg’ main tatkaaleen juute, asana, sawari, oshadhi, vastra, chhatari, pankhe aadi kaa ullekh he. ‘chullavagg’ main bhikshuniyon kii pravrajya aadi kaa varnan he. yahan bhikshuon ke liye jo shalaka-grahan kii paddati bataai gayi he. vah tatkaaleen lichhavi ganatantr ke ‘vot’ lene ke rivaaj kii nakal he. us samay ke ganatantr shashan main koi prastaav pesh karne ke baad, prastaav ko duhraate hue uske vishay main teen baar tak bolne kaa avasar diya jaataa tha. tab kahin jaakar nirnay sunaaya jaataa tha. yahi paddati bhikshu sangh main sweekaar kii gayi thi.
 ‘sutarapitak’ ke antargat dirghanikaay main pooranayssop, makali gosaal, ajit kesakambal, pakudh kacchayan, niganth natputt or sanjay weltthiputt naamak chah yashasvi tirthakaron kaa mat-pratipaadan, lichchhaviyon kii gana-vyavastha, ahinsamay yajna, jaat-paant kaa khandan aadi anek mahatvapurn vishyon kaa ullekh he. ‘majjhimanikaay’ main buddha kii charika, natputt-mat-khandan, anaatmavaad, varna-vyavastha-virodh, mansabhakshan-vichaar aadi vishyon kaa pratipaadan he. ‘sanyuttanikaay’ main kosal ke raja pasendi or magadh ke raja ajatshatru ke yuddh kaa varnan he.
 ‘anguttaranikaay’ main solah janapad aadi kaa ullekh he. ‘khuddakanikaay’ ke antargat ‘dhammapad’ or suttanipaat’ bahut pracheen maane jate hai jinka buddha sahitya main unchaa sthaan he. ‘suttanipaat’ main sachcha brahman koun he ? vaastavik maance-tyaag kise kehete hai ? aadi vishyon kaa maarmik varnan he. ‘thergatha’ or ‘therigatha’ main anek bhikshu-bhikshuniyon kii jeevancharya di gai he, jinhone bade-bade pralobhanon ko jitkar nirvana padavi paayi.
 jaatakon main buddha ke purvabhavon kii kathaayen hai, jinke anek drishy sanchi, bharhut aadi ke stupon par ankit hai. ye 150 isvi san purv ke aasapaas ke maane jate hai. ye kathaayen vishwa-sahitya kii drushti se bahut mahatva kii hai or vishwa ke praayah harek kone main pahaunchi hai.
 ‘abhidhammpitak’ main bauddhadharm main maanya padaarth or unke bhed-prabhedon kaa vistaar se varnan he. bauddhadharm ke itihaas kii drushti se yah mahatva kaa granth he. isaki rachana samrat ashok ke samay tissamoggaliputt ne kii thi.
 buddha vidwaanon ne ukt tripitak kii anek vyakhyaayein aadi likhi hai, jinhen atthkatha ke naam se janaa jaataa he. atthakathaayen bhi pali bhashaa main hai. kehete hai jab mahendra sthavir buddha shashan kii sthapana karne ke liye sinhal gayi to ve tripitak ke saath-saath unki atthakathaayen bhi lete gayi. tatpashchaat acharya buddhaghosh ne isvi san kii 5veen shatabdi main in sinhal atthakathaon kaa pali main roopaantar kiya. atthakathaayen ye hai-
 in sab atthakathaon kaa praneta praayah buddhaghosh ko mana jaataa he. iske atirikt ‘khuddakanikaay’ ke granthon par bhi bhinn-bhinn atthakathaayen hai, jinamein jataka-atthkatha or dhammapad-atthkatha vishesh rup se ullekhneey hai. jataka main keval buddha bhagwaan ke purvajanm se sambandh rakhanevaali gaathaayen hai, jo bina jataka-atthkatha ke samaj main nahin aa saktin. ye sab atthakathaayen bharat kii pracheen samskruti kaa bhandar hai jinamein itihaas kii vipul saamagree bhari padi he.
