 phukuoka ne japani bhashaa main anekon pustaken, vaigyanik shodhpatr evam anya sahitya likhaa. ve 1970 ke baad se hi televijan par bhi aate rahete the. ve prakruti ke siddhanton ke pakshdhar the.
