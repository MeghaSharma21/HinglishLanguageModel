 khagolashastra main kaala bouna yaa blike dwarf ek bache-kuche taare ko bolaa jaataa he jo bahut ghanaa ho or jis se raushnee or garmi na aa rahi ho yaa bahut kii kam maatra main aa rahi ho. vaigyanikon kaa maananaa he ke safed boune taare arabon saalon main thande padakar kaale boune ban jate hai.
 mana jaataa he ke jin taron main itnaa dravyaman nahin hota ke ve aage chalakar apana indhan khatm ho jane par neutron tara ban saken, ve saare safed boune ban jate hai. is nazariye se akashganga ke 97% taron ke bhagya main safed bouna ban janaa hi likhaa he. safed bunon kii raushnee badi madhyam hoti he. vaqt ke saath-saath safed boune thande padate jate hai or vaigyanikon kii soch he ke arabon saal main ant main jaakar ve bina kisi raushnee or garmi ke kaale boune ban jate hai. kyonki hamaara brahmand keval 13.7 arab saal purana he isliye abhi itnaa samay hi nahin guzara ke koi bhi safed bouna puri tarah thanda padakar kaala bouna ban sake. is vajah se aaj tak khagolashastriyon ko kabhi bhi koi kaala bouna nahin mila he.
