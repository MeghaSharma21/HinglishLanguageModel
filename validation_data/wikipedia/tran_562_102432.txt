 ashar terry remand 4 jo apane naam ashar se vikhyath he, ek america gayak, geetkaar, nartak va abhinayta he. unhen prasiddhi 1990 main apane duusare album maai ve kii release ke saath mili jisame unka pehla billboard hot 100 kaa pratham kramank kaa geet "naaise and slo" shaamil tha. album ko 6 baar kaa platinum praaanpatr diya gaya he. iske baad ke album 8701 ne billboard hot 100 ke pratham kramank ke geet "yu remind me" or "yu got it bed" die. is album ko 4 baar platinum praaanpatr mila he. 
 ashar kaa 2004 kaa album kanfeshans diamand praaanpatr kaa hakdaar rahaa or isaki america main 1 karod pratiyaan biki. kanfeshans ke naam pehle saptaah main kisi aar or bi kalakaar ke sarvaadhik bikri hone kaa kirtiman he. isamen chaar billboard ke pratham kramank ke geet shaamil the: "yaah!", "bern"m "kanfeshans part II" or "maai bu". ashar ke 2008 ke album hair ai stand kii vishvabhar main 50 lakh pratiyaan biki or iskaa geet "lav in club" billboard hot 100 ke pratham kramank par rahaa.
