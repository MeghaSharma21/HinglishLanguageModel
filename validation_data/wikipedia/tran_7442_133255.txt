 uttar pradesh ke gadhwal anchal main srinagar main baikunth chaturdashi kaa mela prativarsh laga karata he. vibhinn parvon kii bhaanti vaikuntha chaturdashi varmbhar main padane vaala hindu samaaj kaa mahatvapurn parv he. saamanytah deepawali tithi se 14 ve din baad aane vaale saal kaa yah parv dharmik mahatv kaa he. is avasar par vibhinn shivalayon main puja/archana sadhana kaa vishesh mahatv he. gadhwal janapad ke prasiddh shivalayon srinagar main kamaleshwar tathaa thalisain main binsar shivalay main is parv par adhikadhik sankhya main shrriddhalu darshan hetu aate hai tathaa is parv ko aradhana va manokamana purti kaa mukhya parv maanate hai. srinagar sthit kamaleshwar mandir puranic mandiron main se he. isaki atishay dharmik mahatta he, kivdanti he ki yah sthaan devataaon kii nagari bhi rahi he. is shivalay main bhagwaan vishnu ne tapasya kar sudarshan-chakra praapt kiya to shri ram ne ravan vadh ke uparant brahm hatya ke paap se mukti hetu kaamana arpan kar shiv ji ko prasann kiya va papamukt hue.
 vaikuntha chaturdashi katha kaa ek varnan is prakaar he-diya tha chakra shukta karthik ko, kari jab bhav se pujashri chodash shukla karthik ko, diya jab darshan shiv girija.tabhi se shukla chaudash kaa hua vikhyath yah mela.putr vardaan shankar de, katkar paap kaa jhela.spasht he ki is sthaan kii pracheen mahatta ke kaaran karthik maas shukla paksh kii chaudavin tithi ko bhagwaan vishnu ko sudarshan chakra prapti kaa parv mana gaya he. ise uplabdhi kaa pratik maanakar aaj bhi shrriddhaalu putr prapti kii kaamana se prativarsh is parv par raatri main sadhana karne hetu mandir main aate hai. to anek shrriddhalu darshan va moksh ke bhav se is mandir main aate hai. jisase uttrakhand ke gadhwal kshetra main yah mela ek visisth dharmik meley kaa rup le chukaa he.
 vaikuntha chaturdashi meley ke mahatv ke saath-saath kamaleshwar mandir ke mahatv kaa aitihaasik prishthbhumi ko bhi samajhnaa aavashyak he. srinagar jo pracheen kaal main shri kshetra kehalaata tha. treta yug main ravan vadhakar ramachandra ji dwaara yahan par 108 camel pratidin ek maah tak bhagwaan shiv kaa arpan kiya jane kaa varnan miltaa he.prativarsh karthik maas kii tripurotsav poornamasi ko jab vishnu bhagwaan ne sahas camel pushpon se archanaakar shiv ko prasann kar sudarshan chakra praapt kiya tha, us aadhaar par vaikuntha chaturdashi parv par putr prapti kii kaamana hetu dampatti raatri ko haath main deepak dhaaran kar bhagwaan shankar ko fal prapti hetu prasann karte hai.
 vaikuntha chaturdashi mela vartmaan main ek parv va puja aradhana tak simit nahin he. srinagar kii babbti aabaadi va gadhwal ke is shahar kii kendriya bhugolic sthiti va is shahar ke shaikshanik kendra hone ke kaaran ek vyapak dharmik, saanskrithik aayojan kaa swaroop le chukaa he. prativarsh nagarpalika parishad srinagar dwaara vaikuntha chaturdashi parv se lagbhag 5-6 din tak vyapak saanskrithik kaaryakramon, khelkud pratiyogitaaon va sthaneey samskruti par aadhaarit saanskrithik kaaryakramon kaa aayojan kiya jaataa he. in kaaryakramon ko or vyapak swaroop pradaan kar tathaa inkaa prachaar prasar kar kamaleshwar mandir se sambandhit puranic/ dharmik manyataon ko ujagar kar un par aadhaarit kaarykram taiyaar kar is avasar par paryatakon ko bhi aakarshith kiya ja saktaa he.
 srinagar shahar jo ki rishikesh se 107 ki.me.paudi se 29 ki.me.kotdwar se 135 ki.me.duuri par he va badri-kedaar yatra maarg par sthit he paryatakon ke avaagaman ke drushtikon se upyukt sthal he. is aayojan ko vyavasthit kar va vyapak swaroop dekar paryatakon ke upyog hetu prachaarit kiya ja saktaa he.
 srinagar rashtriya rajmarg par avasthit he tathaa chaar dham yatra maarg par padataa he evam rajya ke anya mukhya shaharon se sadak maarg se juda he. bas, taxi tathaa anya sthaneey yatayaat kii suvidhaayen uplabdh he.
