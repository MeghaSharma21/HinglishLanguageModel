 duusari ore, gopi police he jo sidhe goli bhi maar nahin saktaa he. unke chehre ke alaava, vah or kishan ke paas kuch bhi saamaanya nahin he. lekin jab kishan gopi ke bare main jaanata he, to vah apane laabh ke liye gopi kii pahchaan kaa upyog karne kaa faisla karata he. iske parinamswaroop gopi achaanak prachaarit or prasiddh ho jaataa he.
