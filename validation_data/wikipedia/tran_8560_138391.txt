 yah iran kaa pramukh pathaar hai. 
 isaki oonchaai pashchim main 2,000 foot tak or purv main 4,000 foot tak he. yah 10,000 foot se bhi adhik unche toras or ponts parvatamalaon se ghira hua he. turki kaa bhitari bhaag unche basin kii bhanti he. jahaan tahaan unche pahaad bhi milte hai. adhikansh bhaag kaa jalpravaah bheetar kii ore he or nadiyaan yaa to jhiilon main girti hai athwa namakeen nichle daldalon main lupt ho jaati hai. parantu kuch nadiyaan parvaton ko katati or guphaayen nirmit karti hui bahati hai. chaaron taru unche parvaton se ghire rahane ke kaaran yah bhaag jaade tathaa garmi donon mausamon main bhapabhari hawaaon se saadharantaya vanchit rahane ke kaaran shushk rahata he, vaarshik varsh kaa ausat lagbhag 10"" he. jaade main bhoomi kabhi kabhi barf se dhak jaati he. garmi main garam tathaa tej hawaayen chalati hai. at: shushk pradeshon main mitti hat gai he ore marusthaleey kankadon kaa ubhaar ho gaya he. is ardhashushk ghaas ke maidaanon main bhed bakriyaan paali jaati hai. doodh dahi kaa samavesh yahan ke bhojan main adhik hota he. oon tathaa oonee kambal taiyaar karnaa mukhya pesha he. angora se unka niryaat hota he. garmi ke dinon main gaderiye apane jaanwaron ke saath pahaado par chale jate hai or anya mausam main maidani bhaag main utar aate hai. jahaan sinchai ke saadhan uplabdh hai vahaan gehun kii kheti hoti he. is pathaar main turki kii ek tihaai jansankhya rahati he. enatolia kaa yah pathari bhaag turk  kaa mukhya sthaan he. turki kii rajdhani ankara isi bhaag main sthit he.
