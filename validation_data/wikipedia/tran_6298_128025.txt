 sagamental odontomexilleri displesia daanton kii vah samasya he jisame daanton main dard nahi hota apitu daanton kaa aakaar bad jaataa he saadhaaran aakaar se. yah samasya aksar sidhe jabade main pai gai he or iske hone kaa kaaran agyaat he. is samasya kaa prabhaav daanton par dekha jaataa he, asann komal utak par or jabado par. sagamental odontomexilleri displesia main kabhi-kabhi janm se hi premoller daant anupasthit hote he or kabhi-kabhi primary daanton kaa aakar saadhaaran daanton se chota hota he. daanton kii yah samasya bachapan main hi pataa lag jaati he. isamen daanton ke segments atyadhik bad jate he haddiyon ke badhane ke kaaran. or yah 9 saal kii umr main iskaa nidaan kiya jaataa he.
