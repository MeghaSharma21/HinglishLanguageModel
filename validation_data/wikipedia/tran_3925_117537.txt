 vartmaan yug vyapaar or vaanijya kaa yug he. is yug main pratidin anekon vyapaarik anubandh kiye jate hai. vastu vikray anubandh ko kanoon main ek vishesh prakaar kaa anubandh mana jaataa he. 1930 se purv vastu-vikray se sambandhit niyam bhartiya anubandh adhinium 1872 kii dhara 76 se 123 main shaamil the. ye vyavasthaaen vyapaarik paristhitiyon ke parivartan ke kaaran paryaapt nahin the. atah bhartiya sansad ne vastu-vikray anubandh kii pechidagi or mahatv ko dekhte hue san 1930 main vastu-vikray se sambandhit praavdhaanon ko bhartiya anubandh adhinium se samaapt karke ek pruthak vastu-vikray adhinium banaaya. yah adhinium bhartiya vastu-vikray adhinium 1930 tha.
