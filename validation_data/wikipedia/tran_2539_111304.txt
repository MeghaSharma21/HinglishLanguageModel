 cricket vishwa cap trophy
 isisi cricket vishwa cap ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket kii antarrashtriya championships he. toornament khel ke shaasi nikaay antarrashtriya cricket parishad dwaara har chaar saal yojit kiya jaataa he. toornament duniya main sabase zyada dekhi gayi khel spardhaon main se ek he, yah keval fifa vishwa cap or olympic peeche he.
 pehali baar vishwa cap 1975 main england main yojit kiya gaya tha, pehle teen vishwa cap england main mejabani kiye gaye the. lekin 1987 toornament ke baad se vishwa cap har chaar saal duusare desh main yojit kiya jaataa he. sabase haal hi toornament 2015 main yojit kii gai thi, yah toornament australia or newzealand ne mejabani kii thi or ise australia ne jeeta.
