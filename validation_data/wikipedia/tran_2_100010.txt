 prithvinaryan yaa pruthbinaryan madhya nepal main ek nagarpalika he jo 1996 main mukhya gorkha shahar se alag banaaya gaya. is nagar palika kaa naam raja prithvinarayan shah ke naam par rakhaa gaya he, jo gorkha jila main janmen thea jinhone nepal kaa ekikaran kiya or aadhunik nepal ko sthapit kiya.
 raja kaa purana mahal, gorakhnath or kalika yahan ke mukhya aakarshan hai. neechala mahal or ek aadhunik park yahan kaa anya mukhya aakarshan he.
 yah jagah ganesh trekking rute kaa bhi shuruaati jagah he. gorkha aspataal or aanp pipal aspataal or drabya shah or gorkha multiple campus yahan ke janata ke seva main virajman hai.
 varsh 2009 prithvinarayan nagarpalika kaa naam badal kar gorkha nagarpalika kar diya gaya.
