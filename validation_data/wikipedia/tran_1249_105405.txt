 antarbijanu farmicutis sangh kii kuch bacteria jatiyon dwaara banaaya jane vaala ek kathor va nishkray dhaancha hota he. haalanki iskaa naam or rup bijaanu va beej se miltaa he, yah prajnan se asambandhit he. yah kisi bacteria koshika kaa pratikul paristhitiyon main swayan ko sikodkar va apni adhikansh jeev-prakriyaaon ko bahut dhimaa kar ke yaa rokkar banaaya gaya rup hota he. is rup main bacteria bahut lambe kaal tak antarbijanu bana rah saktaa he or paristhitiyaan sudharne par punah sakriya ho jaataa he. yah antarbijanu avasthaa aksar poshak tatwon, jal yaa aahaar ke abhaav main dekhi jaati he.
 antarbijanu rup main bacteria kabhi-kabhi shatabdiyon tak bachaa rah saktaa he. kai vaigyanikon ne 10,000 varshon se adhik aayu ke antarbijanuon ke punah sakriya ho jane kii khabar di he or dasiyon lakh varsh se nishkray antarbijanu ke sakriya ho jane ke daawey bhi mili hai. ek samachar ke anusaar namak crystalon main mili basilas marismortui ke 25 karod varsh puraane antarbijanu mili hai jo abhi bhi jivit hai. adhikansh bacteria swayan ko antarbijanu banane main sakasham nahin hai. basilas aise bacteria kaa udhaaharan he jisamen yah kshamta he. antarbijanuon kaa adhyayan karne par inamen bacteria kaa dillane, ribosom or dipicolinics amla miltaa he. vaigyanikon kaa maananaa he ki dipicolinics amla bacteria ko nishkray avasthaa main rakhane ke liye sahaayak he or antarbijanuon main lagbhag 10% bhaar isi amla kaa hota he.
