 chatushfalkeey sankhya athwa trikoniya pyramid sankhya chitra sankhya he jo tribhujaakar aadhaar or teen anya falkon ko jodne par banane vaali chatushfalki akruti pyramid ko nirupit karti he. nvIM chatushfalkeey sankhya, pratham n trikon sankhyaon ke yoga ke baraabar hoti he.
 pratham 10 chatushfalkeey sankhyayen nimna hai:
 n-veen chatushfalkeey sankhya kaa sutara n ke tisare badate kramgunit ko 3 ke kramgunit se bhajit karke praapt kiya jaataa he:
 chatushfalkeey sankhya ko dwipad gunanko ke rup main bhi nirupit kiya ja saktaa he:
 atah chatushfalkeey sankhyayen pascal tribhuj ke baayen athwa daayen se chaturth sthaan par praapt hoti hai.
 chatushfalkeey sankhya kaa jyamitiya nirmaan golon ko jodakar kiya ja saktaa he. udhaaharan ke liye paanchavin chatushfalkeey sankhya kaa jyamitiya nirmaan 35 billiard gendon se kiya ja saktaa he. iske liye 15 billiard gendon ko ek tribhujaakar akruti main rakhkar unpar 10 or genden rakhi ja sakati hai. inke upar 6 or gend rakhkar, uske upar 3 tathaa sabake upar ek gend rakhane par chatushfalkeey sanrachana kaa nirmaan hota he.
 jab n–koti ke chatushfalak kaa nirmaan Tn golon se kiya ek ikai ke rup main kiya jaataa he to yah dekha ja saktaa he ki iske dwaara gheraa jane vaala sthaan isi tarah se n ≤ 4 se praapt chatushfalak ke ghanatv se praapt kiya ja saktaa he.
