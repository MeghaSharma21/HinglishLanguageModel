 guntur andhra pradesh praant kaa ek shahar he. andhra pradesh ke uttar purvi bhaag main krishna nadi delta main sthit he guntur. vijaywada-chennai trunk road par sthit guntur kii sthapana francisi shaskaon ne aathvin shatabdi ke madhya main kii thi. kareeb 10 shatabdiyon tak unhone yahaan raaj kiya. baad main 1788 main ise british sagrajya main mila diya gaya. guntur buddha dharm kaa pramukh centre rahaa he. prakruti ne apni khubsuurati uchen pahaado, haribhari ghaatiyon, kalakal bahati nadiyon or manamohak taton ke rup main yahaan bikheri he. aaj guntur apane dharmik or aitihaasik sthalon tathaa chatapate achaar ke liye duniya bhar main janaa jaataa he.
 guntur nagar kii sthapana 18veen shatabdi ke madhya main francoisiyon dwaara kii gai thi, lekin 1788 main angreji kaa is par sthaayee rup se adhikaar ho gaya. 1866 main yahan nagarpalika kaa gathan kiya gaya.
 railway jankshan or vyapaarik centre guntur kii arthvyavastha patson, tambaakoo or chaaval kii kheti par nirbhar he. guntur main ek krishi shodh centre bhi he.
 guntur ke aasapaas ke kshetron main jowar, mirch, moongfalee or tambaakoo kii kheti bhi hoti he.
 jinamein baptlaal ejineering college, ke. el. college of ejineering, aar. ve. aar. college of ejineering, vigyaan ejineering college, em. bi. tii. es. rajkeey politechnic, rajkeey mahila politechnic, guntur medical college, mahatma gandhi college for post graduate cources or andhra vishvavidhyalay se sambhddha kai mahavidhyalay hai. nikat hi 12veen shatabdi kaa bharanprath pahari durg sthit he.
 guntur zile kaa kshetrafal 11,377 varg kimi he or purv tathaa uttar main yah krishna nadi se ghira he |
 guntur se 49 kilometre dur bapatla kaa bhavnarayanswami mandir bhagwaan bhavnarayan ko samarpit he. samay bitne ke saath ab inhen baptla ke naam se janaa jaataa he. yah mandir guntur jile kaa sabase pracheen or sabase pramukh mandir he. itihaas or shilp kii drushti se mandir kaa bahut mahatv he.
 amravati guntur se 35 kilometre dur uttar-pashchim main krishna nadi ke kinare sthit he. yahaan puure varsh shraddhaluon kaa aana-janaa laga rahata he isliye yahaan paryatakon ke liye suvidhaon kii acchhee vyavastha he. yahaan bhagwaan shiv ke pramukh mandiron main se ek amareshwar he jahaan shivaratri ke avasar par bhakton kii baari bheed hoti he. amravati main vishwaprasiddh buddha stupa bhi he jahaan bhagwaan buddha ke jivan se sambandhit chitron ko dekha ja saktaa he.
 kotppa konda narsaraopet se 13 kilometre dakshin pashchim main sthit he. yahaan mukhya rup se rutrikoteshwar swami kii puja kii aati he jinka mandir pahaad kii chotii par sthit he. ab rajya sarkaar is sthaan ko paryaton or dharmik centre ke rup main viksit karne kaa prayaas kar rahi he. iske liye yahaan paryaton suvidhaayen badaye jane kii vyavastha kii ja rahi he. 
 mangalagiri vijaywada-chennai trunk road par sthit he. pragaitihaasik kaal se hi yah sthaan bahut prasiddh rahaa he. mangalagiri parvat par bhgvaan lakshmi narsimha swami kaa mandir he isliye ise bahut hi pavitra parvat mana jaataa he. mana jaataa he ki jo jal bhakt prabhu ko chadhaate hai unamem se aadha bhagwaan pi lete hai or baaki aadha bhakt prasad ke rup main le jate hai. lakshmi narsimha swami ko pankala narsimha swami yaa pankala swami bhi kaha jaataa he.
 guntur se 5 kilometre dur nallapadu yaa nasinghpuram kaa naam yahaan pahari par sthit narsinghswami mandir ke kaaran padaa he. is mandir ke alaava bhi yahaan kai pracheen mandir bhi hai. yahaan ke agasleshwaraswami mandir ke bare main kaha jaataa he ki yah kai shatabdi purana he. is mandir main susajjit dhvajastanbham, paanch naagon kii ukeri gai pratimaayein, shiv ji or brahmaramb, unki patni kii pratimaayein tathaa shankaracharya mandir darshneey hai.
 gutur se 11 kilometre dur pondugla main bahut saare mandir hai. inamen sabase pramukh mandir he gantala ramalingeshwara swami mandir. is mandir ke stambhon main paali main likhe shilalekhon ko dekha ja saktaa he. paas hi dandivgu nadi ke kinare sthit ayegaripalam gaanv main bhi ek mandir he jahaan sanskrit main likhe do shilaalekh mili hai. ise sangrakshit smarak ghoshit kiya gaya he.
 nagarjunasagar baandh ni:andeh bharat kii shaan he. yah pathar se bana duniya kaa sabase ooncha baandh he. is baandh kaa pani naalgonda, prakassam, khammam or guntur jaise andhra pradesh ke anek jilon main sinchai ke kaam aata he. 
 3568 garv kilometre kshetra main failaa yah abhyaarany bharat main sabase bada tiger rigerv mana jaataa he. iske alaava yahaan phulon va vanaspatiyon kii anek prajatiyaan bhi pai jaati hai. abhyaranya ke saath hi nagarjunasagar baandh he. yahaan kii gahari ghaatiyon kii sundarataa dekhte hi banati he.
 najdiki hawaii adda gannavaram he.
 najdiki railway stesan guntur or vijaywada hai jo sabhi pramukh shaharon se judaa hua he.
 bas sevaayen guntur ko jile ke andar va baahar ke pramukh sthanon se jodatii hai jinamein rajya mukhyalay bhi shaamil hai.
 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar kshetra kii jansankhya 5,14,707 he or zila kul jansankhya 44,05,521 he.
