 qasida shaayari kaa vah rup he jisamen kisi kii prashansa kii jaae. kuch shaayar apane behtareen qaseedon ke liye vikhyath hue hai, jaise ki mirza sauda. qaside likhane kaa rivaaj arab samskruti se aayaa he, jahaan yah islam ke aane se bahut purv se likhe ja rahe hai.
 'qasida' main 'qa' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'k' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'qeemat' or 'qareeb' ke 'qa' se miltaa he. yah ek arabi-mul kaa shabd he or 'qasaada' shabd se liya gaya he jiskaa matlab 'dhyey yaa niyat rakhana' he.
 qaseedon main har sher kaa doosraa misra ek hi radif or kaafiye main hota he. qaside do prakaar ke hote hai. ek vah, jisamen kavi prarambh se hi prashansa karne lagata he or doosraa vah jisamen prarambh main ek tarah kii bhoomika di jaati he or kavi or baton ke alaava vasant, bahaar, darshan, jyotish aadi ke vishay main kuch kahata he. in praarambhik varnanon ko "tashbeeb" kehete hai. tashbeeb ke baad kavi prashansa karne kii ore apane sheron ko modta he. is mod ko gurez kehete he. iskaa varnan bahut mushkil samajha jaataa he or isi ke dwaara shaayar ke kamaal kaa anumaan hota he. acchhee gurez vah he jisamen kavi 'tashbeeb' se 'tarif' par is tarah aa jaae ki padhne waalon ko yah pataa hi na chale ki prashansa kaa vishay thuns-thanskar laayaa gaya he. qaside ke tisare ang 'madah' ke baad chauthaa ang 'dua' hota he, jisamen kavi 'mamduh' ke liye shubhakamanaayen karata hua usase kuch yachana karata he. isi ke baad qasida samaapt ho jaataa he.
 lakhanu ke prasiddh shaayar 'nasikh' dwaara likhe kisi ghode par ek qaside kaa ansh is prakaar he -
