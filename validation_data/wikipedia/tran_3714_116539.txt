 adhiktar mubir aantarik mubir hote hai, jo company ke saathi karmchaaree yaa ucch adhikari ke durvyavhaar kii soochna dete hai. ek aantarik mubir se sambandhit sabase dilachasp sawaal yah he ki kyon or kin paristhitiyon main log avaidh kaary ko tatkaal rokte hai or anyathaa aise vyavhaar ko asweekaar kar isaki soochna dete hai. kuch kaarnon se isprakaar kii dharna ban gai he ki sanghathan ke andar asweekrut vyavhaar ke sambandh main adhiktar logon kii karrwai karne kii sambhaavna hoti he, agar aisi shikaayat pranali hai jo sanghathan kii yojana or niyantran kii ajna kaa vikalp hi pradaan nahin karti balki vyaktiyon ko kai vikalpon se chayan karne k saath purna gopneeyata sahit ek vikalp pradaan karti he. 
 bahari mubir jabaki, bahari vyaktiyon or sansthaaon ke galat aacharan ke bare main soochna dete hai. in maamalon main, praapt jaankaree kii gambhirtha or prakruti par nirbhar karte hue mubir durvyavhaar kii soochna vakeelon, media, kanoon pravartan yaa prahari agenciyon athwa anya sthaneey, rajya yaa sangheey agenciyon ko dete hai. kuch maamalon main, bahari mubir ko vittiya inaam dekar protsaahit kiya jaataa he.
 zyaadaatar america sangheey mubir vidhiyon ke tahat, ek mubir ke aadesh maane jane ke taur par sangheey karmchaaree ko yah vishwaas karnaa hoga ki unke niyokta ne kuch kanoon, niyam yaa vinium kaa ulanghan kiya he, kanoonee taur par sangrakshit baat par kanoonee kaarywahi pramanit yaa shuru karnaa; yaa asweekaar ke liye kanoon kaa ulanghan karnaa hoga. 
 kai maamalon main nirdisht kiya gaya he ki mukhabiri vishesh vishay par adhinium ke tahat surakshit he, america adaalaton ne aise mubir ko pratishodh se surakshit rakhaa he. haalanki, nikatataa se vibhajit america kaa supreme court kaa faisla, garsatti ve.sabllos tha ki pehla sanshodhan se mukt bhaashan sarkari karmchaariyon ke liye guarantee he ki karmchaariyon ke kartavya ke daayre ke bheetar kiye gaye khulaase kii raksha nahin hogi.
 mukhabiri ke bare main vichaar vyapak rup se bhinn hai. mubir ko aamtaur par saarvajanik hiton or santhnik javabdehi ke liye shahid hone vaale ke rup main dekha jaataa he; duusare unhen 'gapshap kii kahaniyon' yaa 'chugalkhor' ke rup main, jo keval prasiddhi kaa peechha or vyaktigat mahima batorne vaala hota he. kuch shikshavidon ke vichaaron se mubir ko kam se kam khandit pariklpana kaa hakdaar hona chahie taaki naitik siddhanton ko lagu karne ke prayaas main unhen dikkaton kaa saamana na ho or shashan pranali main mubir kaa adhik sammaan kiya janaa chahie agar naitik gunon main yah majboot shaikshanik aadhaar ho. 
 yah sambhav he ki kai log pratishodh lene kii bhaavana se hi nahin balki bahari or kaam par rishton ko khone kii vajah se darkar bhi mukhabiri se dur rahete hai. 
 kyonki zyaadaatar maamalon kii ruparekha nimna shrenee kii hoti he or saamanytah inhen media kaa dhyaan bahut kam yaa nahin ke baraabar praapt hota he or mubir kaa mahatvapurn kaam durvyavhaar kii soochna dena he jo ek prakaar se khatre kaa kaam he or aamtaur par jisamen mahima or prasiddhi kii maang kii dharna bahut kam hoti he. 
 hvisalbloar kaa utpeedan duniya ke kai hisson main ek gambhir mudda ban gaya he. haalanki kanoon ke tahat mubir niyokta ke pratishodh se sangrakshit hai, phir bhi kai maamle hai jahaan mubir ko sajaa ke taur par aksar, samaapan, nilamban, padavnati, vetan kaa rukna or/yaa vahaan ke anya karmchaariyon dwaara durvyavhaar miltaa he. udhaaharan ke liye, sanyukt rajya america main, adhiktar hvisalbloar sangrakshan kanoon simit he, agar mubir par pratishodh sabit ho jaataa he to nuksaan kii kshatipurti ke liye "mek hole" upachaar yaa rojgaar pradaan karnaa padataa he. haalanki, kai hvisalbloar ne soochna di he ki nigamon yaa sarkari agenciyon main kadachaar ke abhiyog main vyapak rup se shoot da massenger kii maansikta apanaayi gayi he or kuch maamalon main mubir dwaara aarope lagaae jane par pratishodh kii bhaavana se aapraadhik abhiyojan kiye gaye hai. 
 ek pratikriya ke rup main kai nijee sanghathanon ne mubir ke samarthan ke liye kanoonee bachaav nidhi yaa sahaayta samooh kaa gathan kiya; iske do aise udhaaharan hai naishanal hvisalbloar centre america main or public consarn ait work euke main. paristhitiyon par nirbhar karte hue mubir ke liye yah koi asaamaanya baat nahin he ki unhen apane sah kaaryakarta se bahishkrut hona padataa he, bhavishya ke sambhavit niyoktaaon dwaara bhedbhaav kaa ravaiya yaa yahaan tak ki sanghathan se nishkasit bhi kar diya jaataa he. yah sanghathan ke nirdesh par mubir ke sanghathan se nikaale jane par mabing ke rup main sanderbhit kiya jaataa he. yah kaarysthal main daraane-dhamkaane kaa charam rup he jisamen samooh lakshith vyakti ke khilaaf ho.
 sanyukt rajya america main, kanoonee suraksha mukhabiri ke vishay-vastu ke anusaar alag alag hote hai or kabhi kabhi rajya main jahaan yah maamla uthaaya jaataa he. 2002 sarbenes-oxle adhinium ko paarit karte samay seenet nyaaypalika samiti ne paaya ki hvisalbloar kii suraksha alag-alag rajya kii vidhiyon "pabandakaari or anischitataaon" par nirbhar karti he. phir bhi, sangheey or rajya kanoon un karmchaariyon kii raksha karte hai jo ulanghan kii taraf dhyaan khinchate hai, pravartan kaarywahi ke saath madad karte hai yaa avaidh nirdesh maanane se inkaar karte hai. 
 sabase pehla america kanoon vishesh rup se mubir kii raksha ke liye apanaaya gaya wo 1912 kaa loyad laa follette adhinium tha. isaki guarantee he ki sangheey karmchaariyon ke adhikaar kii jaankaree sanyukt rajya congress detii he. karmchaaree kii suraksha ke liye pehla america paryaavaran sangrakshan kanoon jal pradooshan niyantran adhinium 1972 main shaamil kiya gaya, jo svachh jal adhinium bhi kaha jaataa he. isi tarah kii suraksha surakshit peyajal adhinium sangheey sansaadhan sangrakshan ne 1974 main shamil kiya gaya, sansaadhan sangrakshan or vasuuli adhinium jise, vishakt padaarth niyantran adhinium, 1974 kaa urja punargathan adhinium, vyapak paryaavaran pratikriya, kshatipurti or adhinium daayitv  or svachh vaayu adhinium 1990 . isi prakaar kii karmchaaree suraksha osha ke madhyam se lagu trek chaalkon kii raksha ke liebhutal parivahan sahaayta adhinium, pipeline suraksha sudhaar adhinium 2002, vendale ech. ford vimaanan nivesh or sudhaar adhinium 21 veen sadi ke liye or sarbenes-oxle adhinium, 30 july 2002 par adhiniyamit. 
 kanoon kii pabandakaari kaa matlab yah he ki badale kii karrwai ke shikaar ke mudde par kanoon ko satark hone kii or uchit shikayaton ke liye samay seemaa or iske arth ko nirdhaarit karne kii jarurat he. kuch samay seemaa 10 dinon se bhi kam he . prashasan vyavsaayik suraksha or swasthya ke liye paryaavaran mubir ke ek likhit shikaayat karne ke liye yah 30 dinon kaa he. sangheey bhedbhaav, pratishodh yaa naagarik adhikaron ke kanoon ke anya ulanghan kii shikaayat karmchaaree 45 dinon ke andar unki agency ke samaan rojgaar avasar adhikari ko ek likhit shikaayat kar sakate hai. airline karmchaariyon or corporate dhokhadhadi ke liye mubir 90 dinon ke andar osha main apni shikaayat darj kar sakate hai. parmaanu hvisalbloar or trek driver 180 dinon ke andar osha main apni shikaayat darj kar sakate hai. thos gatividhiyon or anya peediton ke pratishodh ke khilaaf sangh ke aayojan main sudhaar ke liye kaam karne kii sthiti main rashtriya shram sambandh board main apni shikaayat darj karne kii avadhi chah maheene tak he. nijee kshetra ke karmchaariyon ko 180 dinon se 300 dinon ke andar sangheey samaan rojgaar avasar ayog shikaayat kar sakate hai jati, ling, rashtriya daawa yaa dharm ke aadhaar par mul bhedbhaav ke liye shikaayat kar sakate hai. jo nunatam mazdooree praapt karne ke liye yaa samyoparant kaarykaal ke liye javaabi karrwai do yaa teen saal ke andar ek civil mukadmaa darj kar sakate hai, nirbhar karata he ki chaahe to adaalat is ulanghan ko suvichaarit maan le. 
 jo log sangheey sarkaar ke khilaaf jhuthe daawey kii soochna dete hai or parinamswaroop pratikul rojgaar ravaiya bhugatna padataa he ve america jhootha daawa adhinium ke tahat upachaar ke antargat chah saal ke andar shikaayat kar sakate hai. kui taam, praavdhaan ke tahat "mul strot" kii soochna ke liye sarkaar aparaadhiyon se thik kitna pratishat vasuul karsakati he haalanki, "mul strot" ko sabase pehle sangheey civil main shikaayat darj karnaa hoga dhokhadhadi se hathiyaae gaye sangheey dhan kii vasuuli ke liye or is dhokhe ke daawey ke prachaar se bachanaa hoga jabatak america ke nyaay vibhagamen is daawey par chalane vaale mukademe kaa faisla na aa jaae. aise kui taam mukademe muhar ke tahat daayar kiya janaa chahie, vishesh prakriyaaon kaa upyog karnaa hoga taaki isaki jaankaree tabatak saarvajanik na ho jabatak ki sangheey sarkaar is par apana pratyaksh nirnay na de de . 
 sarkari karmchaariyon, mubir suraksha adhinium or koi dar nahin adhinium se laabh ho saktaa he . sangheey suraksha ko or adhik badha diya jaataa he jahaan vishesh suzav karyaalay hvisalbloar ke maamalon kaa anumodan karti hai.
