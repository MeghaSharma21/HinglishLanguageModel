 uttrakhand, uttar bharat main sthit ek rajya he. 2000 or 2006 ke bich yah uttaranchal ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. 9 november 2000 ko uttrakhand bharat ganraajya ke 27 wein rajya ke rup main astitv main aayaa. rajya kaa nirmaan kai varshon ke aandolan ke pashchaat hua. is rajya main vaidic samskruti ke kuch ati mahatvapurn tirthasthan hai. rajya kii seemayen uttar main tibet or purv main nepal se lagi hai tathaa pashchim main himachal pradesh or dakshin main uttar pradesh iske padosi hai. paaramparik hindu granthon or pracheen sahitya main is kshetra kaa ullekh uttrakhand ke rup main kiya gaya he. hindi or sanskrit main uttrakhand kaa arth uttari kshetra yaa bhaag hota he.
 janvari 2007 main sthaneey logon kii bhaawanaaon ko dhyaan main rakhte hue rajya kaa naam aadhikarik taur par uttaranchal se badalakar uttrakhand kar diya gaya. dehradun, uttrakhand kii antarim rajdhani hone ke saath is kshetra main sabase bada nagar he. garasain naamak ek chote se kasbe ko isaki bhugolic sthiti ko dekhte hue bhavishya kii rajdhani ke rup main prastaavit kiya gaya he kintu vivaadon or sansadhano ke abhaav ke chalate abhi bhi dehradun asthai rajdhani bana hua he. rajya kaa ucch nyaayalay nainital main he.
 rajya sarkaar ne haal hi main hastshilp or hathkargha udyogon ko badhaava dene ke liye kuch pahal kii hai. saath hi badhate paryaton vyapaar tathaa ucch takneekee vaale udyogon ko protsaahan dene ke liye aakarshak kar yojanayen prastut kii hai. rajya main kuch vivaadaaspad kintu vrahat baandh pariyojnaayen bhi hai jinaki puure desh main praayah aalochana kii jaati rahi he, jaise ki bhagirathi-bhilangana nadiyon par banane vaali tehri band pariyojna. is pariyojna kii kalpana 1953 main kii gai thi or yah antat 2007 main banakar taiyaar hua. uttrakhand, chipko andolan ke janmasthan ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he.
