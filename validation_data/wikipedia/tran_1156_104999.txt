 bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan, dilli bharat main vigyaan, ejiniyari evam praudyogiki me ucchttar anusandhan evam vikaas ke utkrisht kendra ke rup me nirmit chha: praudyogiki sthanon main se ek he isaki sthapana san 1961 me ejiniyari maahavidhyalay ko rup me hui thi. 1963 main ise eashtriya mahatv kaa santhaan ghoshit kiya gaya tathaa iskaa naam "bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan dilli" rakhaa gaya tab ise vishvavidhyalay ke samaan str praapt hua jisamen apni shaikshik nitiyaan nirdhaarit karne, apni parikshaayen swayan yojit karne tathaa apni oopaadhiyaan pradaan karne kii shakti nihit thi. santhaan kaa shilanyas mahamahim rajkumaar philip duk of edinbar ne janvari 1959 ko apane kar kamalon se kiya tha tatkaaleen vaigyanik anusandhan evam saanskrithik maamalon ke mantri professor humayun kabir ne 21 agast,1961 ko santhaan kaa udghaatan kiya santhaan kaa aupcharik udghaatan 2 march 1968 ko bharat ke tatkaaleen rashtapati dow zakir hossain ne kiya.
