 
 Sphaeria sinensis Berk. Cordyceps sinensis Sacc. 
 yarch gumba ek ayurvedic jadi-buti he jise angreji main Cordyceps Sinensis kaha jaataa he. yarch gumba nepali naam he jo samudr tal se 3800 metre upar nepal or tibet ke himalay par paaya jaataa he. yah ek High Value Traditional Medicine he.yarch gumba ek film kit or ek prakaar ke paudhe ke pratikriya ke dwaara sardiyon ke dinon main banataa he. yah bhure rang kaa hota he tathaa yah 2 eench lambaa hota he tathaa iskaa swaad meetha hota he.
 sabase pramukh baat yah he ki iskaa prabhaav naakaaratmak nahi hota koi bhi byakti iskaa istemaal kar saktaa he. iske istemaal se sex karne ki ichha utpann hoti he bina kisi nuksaan ke.
 yah anya bhi kai tarah ke bimario ke dawa ke liye istemaal hota he. jaise fefde kaa ilaj, tonifies kidney, empotence dieses aadi main upyog hota he. yah samaypurv budhapa ko bhi roktaa he or maanav pratirakshak kshamta ko badhaata he.
