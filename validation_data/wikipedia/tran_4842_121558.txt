 nibandh gadhy lekhan kii ek vidha he. lekin is shabd kaa prayog kisi vishay kii taarkik or bauddhik vivechna karne vaale lekhon ke liye bhi kiya jaataa he. niabandh ke paryaay rup main sandarbh, rachana or prastaav kaa bhi ullekh kiya jaataa he. lekin saahitiyik aalochana main sarvaadhik prachlit shabd niabandh hi he. ise angreji ke compozishan or esse ke arth main grahan kiya jaataa he. acharya hajariprasad dvivedi ke anusaar sanskrit main bhi niabandh kaa sahitya he. pracheen sanskrit sahitya ke un nibandhon main dharamshastreey siddhanton kii taarkik vyakhya kii jaati thi. unamem vyaktitv kii visheshta nahin hoti thi. kintu vartmaan kaal ke niabandh sanskrit ke nibandhon se thik ulate hai. unamem vyaktitv yaa vaiyaktikta kaa gun sarvapradhan he.
 itihaas-bodh parampara kii roodhiyon se manushya ke vyaktitv ko mukt karata he. niabandh kii vidha kaa sambandh isi itihaas-bodh se he. yahi kaaran he ki niabandh kii pradhan visheshta vyaktitv kaa prakaashan he.
 niabandh kii sabase acchhee paribhaasha he-
 is paribhaasha main ativyapti dosha he. lekin niabandh kaa rup sahitya kii anya vidhaon kii apeksha itnaa swatantra he ki uski sateek paribhaasha karnaa atyant kathin he.
 sari duniya kii bhashaaon main niabandh ko sahitya kii srujanaatmak vidha ke rup main manyata aadhunik yug main hi mili he. aadhunik yug main hi madhyayugin dharmik, saamaajik roodhiyon se mukti kaa dwaar dikhaai padaa he. is mukti se niabandh kaa gaharaa sambandh he.
 hajariprasad dvivedi ke anusaar-
 is prakaar niabandh main nibandhkaar kii svachhndatha kaa vishesh mahatva he.
 acharya ramchandra shukla ne likhaa he:
 iskaa taatpary yah he ki niabandh main kinhin aise thos rachana-niyamon or tatwon kaa nirdesh nahin diya ja saktaa jinka paalan karnaa nibandhkaar ke liye aavashyak he. aisa kaha jaataa he ki niabandh ek aisi kalakriti he jiske niyam lekhak dwaara hi avishkrut hote hai. niabandh main sahaj, saral or aadambarhin dhang se vyaktitv kii abhivyakti hoti he.
 'hindi sahitya kosh' ke anusaar:
 is prakaar niabandh ke do vishesh gun hai-
 niabandh kaa aarambh kaise ho, bich main kya ho or ant kis prakaar kiya jaae, aise kisi nirdesh or niyam ko maanane ke liye nibandhkaar baadhya nahin he. lekin iskaa arth yah nahin he ki niabandh ek uchhrunkhal rachana he or nibandhkaar ek uchhrunkhal vyakti. nibandhkaar apni prerana or vishay vastu kii sambhaavnaaon ke anusaar apane vyaktitv kaa prakaashan or rachana kaa sanghathan karata he. isi kaaran niabandh main style kaa vishesh mahatva he.
 hindi sahitya ke aadhunik yug main bharatendu or unke sahyogiyon se niabandh likhane kii parampara kaa aarambh hota he. niabandh hi nahin, gadhy kii kai vidhaon kaa prachlan bharatendu se hota he. yah is baat kaa pramaan he ki gadhy or uski vidhaayen aadhunik manushya ke swadhin vyaktitv ke adhik anukool hai. mote rup main swadhinata aadhunik manushya kaa kendriya bhav he. is bhav ke kaaran parampara kii roodhiyaan dikhaai padati hai. samayik paristhitiyon kaa dabaav anubhav hota he. bhavishya kii sambhaavnaayen khulti jaan padati hai. isi ko itihaas-bodh kaha jaataa he. bharatendu yug kaa sahitya is itihaas-bodh ke kaaran aadhunik mana jaataa he.
