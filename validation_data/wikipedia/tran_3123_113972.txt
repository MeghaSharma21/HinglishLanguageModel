 mewarudaisingh dwitiya ek bhartiya shaasha tha.
 udaisingh kii sabase pasandita patni rani dhirabai bhatiyani chaahati thi ki udaisingh ke nidhan ke tatpashchaat jagamaal ko uttaradhikaar banaaya jaae ,is kaaran udaisingh ne jagamaal ko apana uttaradhikaar banane ke liye taiyaar ho gaye the or unki mrityu ke baad 1572 main maharana pratap kii jagah jagamaal ko mewar samrajya kaa shaasha niyukt kiya gaya tha. jaise hi jagamaal shaasha bane vaise hi inhonen akbar se haath mila liya tha is kaaran akbar ne khush hokar jagamaal ko ajmer kii jaagir unako saunp di thi is kaaran jo log maharana pratap ke saathi the ve log chaahate the ki pratap raaj kare isliye jagamaal ko shaasha ke pad se hataa diya or mewar ratna maharana pratap ko gaddi par baitha diya tha. ek samay kii baat he jab sirohi ke shaasha ne jagamaal par hamalaa bol diya tha jisamen 1574 isvi main nidhan ho gaya tha. lekin kuch sroton se pataa chalata he ki jagamaal kaa nidhan 1574 ke bajaay 1583 main hua tha.
