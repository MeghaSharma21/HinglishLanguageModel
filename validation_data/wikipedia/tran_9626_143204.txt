 rajkumaari sophia dilip singh- england main auraton ke adhikaron ke liye sangharsh karne vaale nari sanagathanon kii suprasiddh karkun thi. inke pita maharaja dilip singh shere punjab ke naam se jane jate maharaja ranjit singh ke putr the, jise punjab ko angrez rajya main shaamil karne ke baad desh niklaa de kar england bhej diya gaya tha. jahaan usne karsachiinti se prabhavit hokar is ko apana liya. . sophia kii maan maharani bamba mivallar thi. uski dharmamata maharani victoria thi. sophia ek nariwadi thi or hepanpaton court mahal ke ek ghar main rahati thi jo maharani victoria ne use lihaaz main de rakhaa tha. uski chaar bahane, or chaar bhai the.
