 aniac, electronik newmerical integrator and computer kaa sankshipt rup, ek pehla aam-uddeshy electronic computer tha. yah ek purna-turing vaala, digital computer tha jise sanganana kii sampoorn samasyaon ke samaadhaan ke liye punah programit kiya ja saktaa tha. ENIAC ko sanyukt rajya america ke belistic anusandhan prayogshaala ke liye topakhane kii firing talikaon kii gananaa karne ke liye dizine kiya gaya tha, lekin iskaa pehla istemaal hydrogen bam kii gananaa ke liye kiya gaya.jab 1946 main, ENIAC kii ghoshnaa kii gai tab vah press main "vishalkay mastishk" ke naam se surkiyon main rahaa. isane vidyut-yantric mashiinon ke mukaable gati ko ek hazaar guna badha diya, computing shakti main yah ek aisi chalaang thi jisaki tulana koi bhi ekal mashin aaj tak nahin kar pai he. is ganitiya shakti ne, saamaanya-prayojan programing ke saath yugmit hokar, vaigyanikon or udyogpatiyon ko utsahit kiya. anveshakon ne in nae vichaaron ke prasar ko badhaava diya jiske tahat unhone computer vaastukala par vyaakhyaan kii ek shrinkhalaa kaa shikshan dena prarambh kiya.
 ENIAC kaa dizin or nirmaan, sanyukt rajya america kii sena dwaara dwitiya vishwayuddh ke dauraan vitt poshit kiya gaya. 5 june 1943 ko, nirmaan theke par hastakshar kiya gaya or pennsylvania vishvavidhyalay ke moore skool of electrical ejineering main agle maheene se code naam "project PX" ke antargat is computer par gupt rup se kaary shuru kiya gaya. taiyaar mashin kaa anaavaran 14 farvari 1946 ko, pennsylvania vishvavidhyalay main kiya gaya tha, isko banane main lagbhag 500,000 dollar kii laagat ai. ise aupchaarik rup se july 1946 main america sena aayudh corporation dwaara sweekaar kiya gaya. 9 navambar 1946 ko ENIAC ko behatar banane or uski memri ko unnat karne ke uddeshy se band kar diya gaya or 1947 main aberdeen proving ground, maryland main sthanantarit kar diya gaya. jahaan use 29 july 1947 ko punah chaalit kiya gaya or tab se lekar 2 oktober 1955 ke saayam 11:45 baje tak vah satat sanchaalan main rahaa.
 ENIAC kii kalpana or dizine pencylvania vishvavidhyalay ke jon mauchli or je. presspar ekirt dwaara kii gai. vikaas main sahaayta karne vaale dizin ejiniyaron kii team main shaamil the robert epa. shaw, chuan chu, thomas kit sharpalace, arthur burks, harry haski, jaik davis or iredell echus junior
 ENIAC ek modular computer tha, jisamen vibhinn kaaryon ke liye vyaktigat panel shaamil the. in modulon main se bees acumulator the, jo naa keval jod-ghataav karte the balki ek das ankon vaali dashamalav sankhya ko memri main rakhte the. sankhyaon ko in ikaayiyon ke bich kai saamaanya uddeshy bason, yaa trey par paar karavaaya jaataa tha. apni ucch gati ko praapt karne ke liye, panelon ko sankhya bhejnaa or praapt karnaa, gananaa karnaa, jawaab ko rakshit karnaa or agali kriya ko shuru karnaa hota tha or yah sab vah bina kisi chalyman bhaag ke karata tha. uske failaav kii kshamta uski bahumukhi pratibha kii kunjee thi; vah ek gananaa parinaam ke chihnon par nirbhar vibhinn kaaryon ko shuru karata tha.
 gati ke alaava, ENIAC kii sabase ullekhneey baat thi uskaa aakaar or jatilta. ENIAC main shaamil the 17,468 vaikum tub, 7200 crystal diod, 1500 riley, 70,000 racister, 10,000 kapesiter or lagbhag 5 million haath se jode hue jod. iskaa vajan 30 short ton tha, lagbhag 8.5 bata 3 bata 80 feet kaa tha, 680 varg feet jagah letaa tha or 150 kilowat bijli kii khapat karata tha. ek IBM card reader se input sambhav tha or output ke liye ek IBM card panch kaa prayog kiya jaataa tha. in cardon ko ek IBM lekhankan mashin kii sahaayta se ofline sthiti main mudrit output utpaadit karne ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he, jiskaa ek udhaaharan he IBM 405.
 ENIAC, ankon kaa bhandaaran karne ke liye das-sthetiya ring ganak kaa prayog karata tha; prayek ank 36 nirvaat tubon kaa istemaal karata tha, jinamein se 10 dohare tryod hote the jo ring ganak ke flip flop ko banaate the. ankaganit ko ring ganak dwaara pulse ko "ginate" hue puraa kiya jaataa tha or yadi pulse "lipat jaataa" tha to kairi pulse ko utpann karata tha, iske peeche vichaar yah tha ki yantric ading mashin ke ank chakra ke prachaalan ko electronics main hasil kiya ja sake. ENIAC main das-ank chihnit acumulator the jo das ke poorak pradarshan kaa prayog karte the or prayek second ek strot or unamem se kisi ke bhi bich 5,000 saamaanya jod yaa ghataav kar saktaa tha. kai acumulatoron ko ek saath chalaane ke liye jodna sambhav ho gaya tha, isliye kaary kii tivratam gati sambhawatah samanaantar kaary ke kaaran bahut adhik thi.
 ek acumulator ke kairi ko duusare acumulator main taar kii sahaayta se dohari parishuddh, ankaganit kaa pradarshan karne ke liye le janaa sambhav tha, lekin acumulator kairi circuit kaa samay nirdhaaran ucchtar parishuddhta ke liye teen yaa adhik taron ko jode jane par pabandi lagaataa tha. ENIAC ne acumulatoron main se chaar kaa istemaal kiya, jo ek vishesh multiplir ikai dwaara niyantrit the, taaki vah prati second 385 tak gunan kaary kar sake. ENIAC ne acumulatoron main se paanch kaa bhi upyog kiya, jo ek vishesh divyder/ank-mulak ikai dwaara niyantrit kiya jaataa tha, taaki prati second chalis bhaag yaa teen varg-mul kaary kiye ja sake.
 ENIAC main anya nau ikai inishiating unit, cycling unit, master programmer, reader, printer, kanstant transmeter, or teen fungshan tebles .
 rojaaz or hashagen dwaara die gaye sandarbh, kaary ke samay ke vishay main or adhik vivaran dete hai, jo upar die gaye vivaranon se kuch alag hai. mul mashin chakra 200 microsecond, yaa 10-ank sankhya par kaary karne ke liye 5,000 chakra prati second tha. inamen se ek chakra main, ENIAC ek register par ek sankhya likh saktaa tha, ek register se ek sankhya pard saktaa tha, yaa do sankhyaon ko jod/gata saktaa tha. 10-ankiy sankhya kaa gunan ek d -ank sankhya d +4 chakra letaa tha, aise hi ek 10 dwaara 10-ank kaa gunan 14 chakra, yaa 2800 microsecond-357 prati second kaa ek dar letaa he. yadi sankhyaon main se ek 10 se kam ank ho, to kaary teji se hota tha. bhagaganit or varg mul ne 13 chakra liya, jisamen d parinaam main ankon kii sankhya he. isliye ek bhagaganit yaa ank mul 143 chakra tak, yaa 28,600 microsecond-35 prati second kii dar letaa tha. yadi parinaam main das ankon se kam ho, to ve teji se praapt hote the.
 ENIAC un dinon ke aam ashtadhari radio tubon kaa istemaal karte the; dashamalav acumulator 6SN7, flip-flop se bane hote the, jabaki 6L7s, 6SJ7s, 6SA7s or 6AC7s kaa istemaal tark kaaryon main kiya jaataa tha. kai 6L6s or 6V6s ne line driver ke rup main kaary kiya taaki palson ko rack asembli ke bich ke cabalon ke madhyam se paar kiya ja sake.
 kuch electronics visheshgyon kaa purvanumaan tha ki tub aksar kharaab hogi jiske kaaran mashin kabhi upyogi nahin hogi. yah purvanumaan aanshik rup se sahi niklaa: lagbhag prayek din kai tub jal jate the, jiske falswaroop vah aadhe samay kaary karne yogya nahin rahata tha. vishesh ucch-vishwasaneeyata vaale tub 1948 tak uplabdh nahin the. haalanki, in kharabiyon main se adhikansh warm-ap or kool-down samay ke dauraan hue, jab tub heater or cathod sabase adhik thermal tanaav main rahete the. mashin ko kabhi band naa karne kii saadhaaran yukti ke saath ejiniyaron ne ENIAC ke tubon main kharabi ke dar ko gata kar ek tub prati do din kii dar par laa diya jo adhik sweekaarya tha. 1989 main ekirt ke saath ek saakshaatkaar ke anusaar lagaataar kharaab hote tub kii kahaniyaan zyaadaatar ek myth thi: "lagbhag har do din main ek tub kharaab hoti thi or ham 15 minute main samasya ko pahchaan lete the."1954 main, bina kisi kharabi ke prachaalan kaa lagaataar sabase lanbaa samay 116 ghante tha .
 chunki belistic anusandhan prayogshaala is teen varshiya pariyojna ke dauraan ek varsh ENIAC kaa praayojak rahaa ek ganitagya jon won neuman, jo los ellamos main ek hydrogen bam par kaam kar rahe the, ko is computer ke bare main pataa chal gaya. tatpashchaat los ellamos, ENIAC ke saath itnaa jud gaya ki usapar chalaaya gaya pehla samasya parikshan, topakhane kii talikaon ke bajaae hydrogen bam ke liye gananaa thi. is test ke liye input/output ek million card tha.
 ENIAC ko prachaalan ke jatil anukramon ke liye programit kiya ja saktaa tha, jisamen pash, shakhaaen or upanemakaayen shaamil ho sakate the. ek samasya ko lene or mashin par maanchitran kaa kaary jatil tha or aamtaur par isamen kai saptaah lag jate the. program ke kagaz par banaaye jane ke baad, switch or taron ke jodtod ke dwaara us program ko ENIAC ke "andar" daalane main kuch din or lagate the. iske baad satyaapan or doshamarjan kii ek avadhi chali, jisamen saath thi mashin ko "single step" karne kii kshamta.
 un chah mahilaon ko 1997 main vimen in technology international haul of fame main shaamil kiya gaya jinhonen ENIAC kii adhikansh programing kii. 1946 main ve ek duusare ko jis naam se bulaati thim, vah tha ke mcnlati, betty jenings, betty sneader, marlin vesco, fran bilas or ruth licharman. jenifer es. lite ke niabandh "wain computer var vimen" main ENIAC main mahilaon kii bhoomika or saath hi computer vigyaan ke itihaas main mahilaon kii bhoomika kaa aitihaasik lop yaa mahatv kam hone ko darj kiya gaya or uskaa varnan kiya gaya he.
 ENIAC apane prakaar kii ek maatr dizin thi or ise kabhi doharaaya nahin gaya. 1943 main iske dizin par lagi pratibandh kaa matlab tha ki computer ke dizin main kuch nawacharon kaa abhaav jo jald hi acchhee tarah se viksit ho gaya, vishesh rup se ek program ko sangrahit karne kii asamarthta. ekirt or mauchli ne ek nae dizin par kaam shuru kiya, jo aage chal kar EDVAC, ke naam se janaa gaya, jo adhik saral hone ke saath hi adhik shaktishaalee bhi hoga. vishesh rup se, 1944 main ekirt ne ek memri ikai ke vishay main apana vivaran likhaa jo data or program dono ko dhaaran kar saktaa tha. jon won neuman, jo EDVAC par moore skool ke liye paraamarsh kar rahe the, moore skool ke baithkon main bhaag liya jisamen sangrahit program avadharana ko savistaar bataaya gaya or unhone noton kaa ek adhuraa set likhaa jise ek aantarik gyaapan ke rup main prayog karne ke uddeshy se banaaya gaya tha or jisamen baithak main viksit hue vichaaron kaa varnan, vistaaran or nirupan ek aupchaarik taarkik bhashaa main kiya gaya. harman goldstine ne first draft kii pratiyon ko sarkari or shaikshik sansthaaon main vitarit kiya, jiske kaaran EDSAC or SEAC jaise nai peedi ke electronic sanganana mashiinon ke nirmaan main vyapak dilchaspee utpann hui.
 1948 se ENIAC main kai sudhaar bhi kiye gaye, jisamen ek pracheen read-only sangrahit programing tantra shaamil tha jiske liye program ROM ko fungashan tebles ke rup main prayog kiya gaya, yah vichaar BLR ke dow richard clipinger dwaara swatantra rup se prastaavit or ENIAC ke patent main shaamil kiya gaya. clipinger ne anudesh ke set ko lagu karne par won neuman ke saath salaah kii. clipinger ne ek 3-adress vaastukala ke vishay main sochaa, jabaki won neuman ne 1-adress vaastukala kaa prastaav rakhaa kyonki vah lagu karne ke liye aasaan tha. ek acumulator ke teen ankon kaa program ganak ke rup main prayog kiya jaataa tha, ek anya acumulator kaa prayog mukhya acumulator ke rup hota tha, ek tisare acumulator ko kaary talika se data padhane ke liye ek pataa suchak ke rup main prayog kiya jaataa tha or anya acumulatoron main se adhikansh data memri ke liye prayog kiya jaataa tha. ENIAC ke liye sangrahit program kii programing betty jenings, clipinger or adel goldstine dwaara kii gai thi. 16 sitambar 1948 ko, ise ek sangrahit-program computer ke rup main pradarshit kiya gaya, jisamen adel goldstine dwaara jon won neuman ke liye program chalaaya gaya. is sanshodhan ne ENIAC kii gati ko chah ke ganak se kam kar diya or samanaantar gananaa kii kshamta ko samaapt kar diya, lekin chunki isane punah programing ke samay ko dinon se gata kar ghanton main simit kar diya, iske kharaab pradarshan ko uchit mana gaya. vishleshanon ne yah dikhaaya kii abhikalan kii electronic gati or input/output ke vidyut-yantriki gati ke bich bhed ke kaaran, vaastavik-duniya kii lagbhag koi bhi samasya purnatah I/O aadhaarit thi, yahaan tak ki mul mashin kii samanantarata kaa bina upyog kiye or unamem se adhikansh is sanshodhan dwaara gati main kami ke baad bhi I/O aadhaarit hi honge. 1952 ke purvardh main, ek ucch-gati shiftor ko joda gaya, jisane sthanantaran ke gati ko paanch ke ganak se sudhara. july 1953 main, ek 100 shabd vistaar kor memri, dwiadhari kodit dashamalav, atirikt-3 sankhya prasatutikaran kaa upyog karke pranali main joda gaya. is vistaar memri kaa samarthan karne ke liye, ENIAC ko kuch kaary talika chayankarta, memri adress chayankarta or pulse ko aakaar dene vaale circuit se las kiya gaya or teen nae adeshon ko programing vyavastha main joda gaya.
 19veen sadi se yantric or bijli gananaa mashine aane lagi thi, lekin 1930 or 1940 ke dashak, aadhunik computer yug kii shuruyaat maane jate hai.
 ABC, ENIAC or colosus, sabhi thermionic valve kaa istemaal karte the. ENIAC ke registeron ne, Z3 yaa atanasof-beri computer kii tarah dwiadhari ankaganitiya ke bajaay dashamalav ankaganit pradarshit kiya.
 1948 tak, ENIAC ko punah program karne ke liye, colosus kii tarah punah viring kii aavashyaktaa hoti thi. program or data ke liye sanyukt memory ke saath sangrahit-program computer ke vichaar kii kalpana, ENIAC ke vikaas ke dauraan kii gai thi, lekin ise us samay lagu nahin kiya gaya kyonki dwitiya vishwa yuddh kii praathamiktaaon ke liye mashin kaa jaldi puraa kiya janaa aavashyak tha or yah mahasoos kiya gaya ki memory or program ke liye 20 bhandaaran sthaan bahut chota hoga.
 Z3 or colosus ko dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke dauraan ek duusare se or ABC or ENIAC se swatantra rup se viksit kiya gaya. Z3, 1943 main, berlin ke ellide bambaari ke dauraan nasht ho gaya. colosus mashine britain ke yuddh ke prayaason kaa ek hissaa rahi or gopneeyata banaae rakhane ke liye 1945 main nasht kar di gai. unke astitv kii jaankaree 1970 ke dashak main aam hui, haalanki unki kshamtaaon kaa gyaan unke britain ke karmchaariyon or aamantrit americanyon ke bich main rahaa. ABC ko iova state vishvavidhyalay dwaara dhwast kar diya gaya, jon atanasof ke america nausena main bhautikee anusandhan karne ke liye washington D.C main bulaaye jane ke baad aisa kiya gaya. iske vipareet, ENIAC ko 1946 main press ke samaksh pareekshan ke liye rakhaa gaya, "or isane duniya kii kalpana par kabja kar liya". isliye computing ke puraane itihaas apane kawarej or is avadhi ke vishleshan main vyapak nahin bhi ho sakate the.
 kai kaarnon se, ENIAC ke liye 1964 main die gaye U.S patent 3,120,606 ko, honeywel banaam speri rend, ke maamle main landmark fedral adaalat ke 1973 ke faisle main amaanya kar diya, iske tahat electronic digital computer ko saarvajanik adhikaar kshetra main rakhaa gaya or atanasof ko pratham electronic digital computer ke avishkaarak ke rup main kanoonee pahchaan di gai.
