 jain dharm main dashalakshan parv kaa bahut mahatv he.jain dharm ke das lakshan hote he. kehete he jo in das lakshanon kaa acchhee tarah se paalan kar le use is sansar se mukti mil sakati he. par sansaarik jivan kaa nirvaah karne main har samay in niyamon kaa paalan karnaa mushkil ho jaataa he or bahut shubh or ashubh karmon kaa bandh ho jaataa he. in karmo kaa prakshalan karne ke liye shravak uttam kshamaa aadi dharmon kaa paalan karte he. jain dharm ke das lakshan is prakaar he:- 1) uttam kshamaa, 2) uttam mardav, 3) uttam arjav, 4) uttam shouch, 5) uttam satya, 6) uttam sanyam, 7) uttam tap,8) uttam tyaag, 9) uttam akinchanya, 10) uttam brahmacharya.
 in das lakshanon kaa paalan karne hetu jain dharm main saal main teen baar dasalakshan parv manaaya jaataa he. 
 bhadra maheene main aane vaale dashalakshan parv ko logo dwaara zyada dhoomdhaam se manaaya jaataa he. in das dinon main shravak apni shakti anusaar vrath upwas aadi karte he. zyada se zyada samay bhagwan kii puja archana main vyateet kiya jaataa he.
