 
 chinkaara dakshin asia main paaya jane vaala ek prakaar kaa gazal he. yah bharat, bangladesh ke ghaas ke maidaanon or marubhumi main tathaa iran or pakistan ke kuch ilaakon main paaya jaataa he. isaki oonchaai kandhe tak 65 se.me. hoti he or vazan 23 ki. tak hota he. garmiyon main isaki khaal kaa rang laal-bhura hota he or pet tathaa anduruni taangon kaa rang halkaa bhura liye huye safed hota he. sardiyon main yah rang or gaharaa ho jaataa he. iske chehre ke kinaaron main aankh ke kinare se nathunon tak ek kaali dhari hoti he jiske kinare main safed dhari hoti he. sing 39 se.me. tak lambe ho sakate hai. yah sharmila praani he or insaani aabaadi se bachate rahata he. bina pani ke yah lambe samay tak rah saktaa he. haalanki yah ekaaki praani he lekin kabhi-kabhi yah 1-4 praaniyon ke jhund main paye ja sakate hai.
