 prativartan kaa saamaanya arth he - kisi vastu yaa kaary kaa baar-baar usee rup main doharaaya janaa. anekon vidhaon main is shabd kaa prayog hota he or unamem iske bhinn-bhinn arth or paribhaashaayen hai. udhaaharan ke liye, ganit evam computer vigyaan main jab kisi falan kii paribhaasha main usee falan kaa upyog ho to ise prativartan kaha jaataa he. prativartan kaa sarvaadhik upyog ganit main hi hota he. ganit tathaa tathaa sanganak vigyaan ke atirikt bhashavigyaan, tarkshastra, darshanashastra, jeevigyaan, tathaa kala main bhi vividh roopon main prativartan dekha ja saktaa he.
