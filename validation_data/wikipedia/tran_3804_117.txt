 sar charles entrobus  saint vincent or grenadins ke ek rajaneta hai. unhen 1 june 1996 se 3 june 2002 ke bich, saint vincent or grenadins kii rani, elizabeth dwitiya dwaara, saint vincent or grenadins ke governor-general yani maharajyapal ke pad par niyukt kiya gaya tha. is kaal ke dauraan ve maharani ke pratinidhi ke rup main, unki anupasthiti ke dauraan shaasha ke kartavyon kaa nirvaah karte the. karyaalay ke bich hi unki mrityu ho gai thi.
