 keraly jivan kii chhavi yahan manaaye janevaale utsavon main dikhaai detii he. kerala main anek utsav manaaye jate hai jo saamaajik mail-milaap or aadan-pradaan kii drushti se mahatvapurn hai. keraly kalaon kaa vikaas yahan manaaye janevaale utsavon par aadhaarit he. in utsavon main kai kaa sambandh devalayon se he, arthath ye dharmashrith hai to anya kai utsav dharmanirpeksh hai. olym kerala kaa rajyotsav he. yahan manaaye jane vaale pramukh hindu tyohaar hai - vishu, navaratri, deepawali, shivaratri, tiruvatira aadi. musalmaan ramadan, bakreed, muharam, milad-e-sharif aadi manaate hai to isai christmas, easter aadi. iske atirikt hindu, muslim or isaayiyon ke devalayon main bhi vibhinn prakaar ke utsav bhi manaaye jate hai.
