 
 chini pangolin, jiskaa vaigyanik naam manis pentadactaela he, pangolin kii ek jeevvaigyanik jati he jo uttari bharat, nepal, bhutan, bangladesh, burma, uttari hindchin, taiwan or dakshini chin main paaya jaataa he. yah pangolin kii aath jatiyon main se ek he or ati-sankatagrast mana jaataa he. china va purvi asia main iske maance ko khaane se sambandhit kai andhvishwaas hai ki us se kai rog thik hote hai haalaaki chikitsakon ne is manyata ko saraasar jhooth paaya he. antarrashtriya prakruti sangrakshan sangh ke anusaar pichle 15 saalon main isaki sankhya main jabardast giravat hui he.
