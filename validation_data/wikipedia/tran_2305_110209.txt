 sarveshwar dayaal saxena multah kavi evam sahityakar the, par jab unhone dinmaan kaa kaarybhaar sambhala tab samakaleen patrakarita ke samaksh upasthit chunautiyon ko samajha or saamaajik chetana jagaane main apana anukaraniya yogadan diya. sarveshwar maanate the ki jis desh ke paas samrudh bal sahitya nahin he, uskaa bhavishya ujjwal nahin rah saktaa. sarveshwar kii yah agragami soch unhen ek bal patrika ke sampadak ke naate pratishthit or sammanit karti he.
 janm:
 15 sitambar 1927 ko basti main vishweshwar dayaal ke ghar. 
 shiksha:
 elahabaad se unhone bie or san 1949 main eme kii pariksha uteern kii.
  kaaryakshetra
 1949 main prayag main unhen agi aphis main pramukh dispachar ke pad par kaary mil gaya. yahan ve 1955 tak rahe.
 tatpashchaat aal india radio ke sahaayak sampadak pad par aapaki niyukti ho gai. is pad par ve dilli main ve 1960 tak rahe.
 san 1960 ke baad ve dilli se lakhanu radio stesan aa gaye. 1964 main lakhanu radio kii naukari ke baad ve kuch samay bhopal evam radio main bhi karyrat rahe.
 san 1964 main jab dinmaan patrika kaa prakaashan aarambh hua to varisht patrkaar evam sahityakar sachchidanand hiranand vatsyayan 'agyeya' ke aagrah par ve pad se tyaagptr dekar dilli aa gaye or dinmaan se jud gaye. 1982 main pramukh bal patrika paraag ke sampadak bane. november 1982 main paraag kaa sampadan sanbhaalane ke baad ve mrityuparyant usase jude rahe.
 nidhan23 sitambar 1983 ko nai dilli main unka nidhan ho gaya.
