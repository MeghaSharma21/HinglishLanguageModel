 
 judith batler ek amareeki darshanik or nariwadi vichaarak hai, jinke kaary kaa prabhaav raajnneetik darshan, achaarshastra, tathaa nariwadi, quiyar, evam saahitiyik siddhanton par hua he. san 1993 se judith batler, uniwarsity of california, barkle main karyrat hai jahaan ve ab department of compartive litarechar and da program of critical thiyari main maxine aliot professor hai. european grajuet skool main ve hana arindt chair par padast hai.
 academik rup se batler ko unki pustakon- gender trubal: feminisam and da sabavarshan of aaidentity tathaa bodys dat matar: on da diskarsive limits of sex ke liye janaa jaataa hai. ye pustaken gender/ ling siddhanton par prashn uthaane evam gender parformetivity kii avadharana main prabhaavshaalee rahi hai. unhone sakriy rup se samalangik adhikaron ke andolan kaa samarthan kiya he.batler ne saath hi israeli raajnneeti or israel-philistin sangharsh par uske prabhaav kii aalochana kii he. batler kaa yah kahana he kii israel ko sabhi yahudiyon kaa pratinidhi maananaa uchit nahi he.
