 unhone Ukhadlo kii sthapana 16 saal kii umr main kari thi. Ukhadlo udhyamiyon, niveshkon, gativardhak or incubetaron ke liye ek online saamaajik netwarking manch he. Ukhadlo sambhavit sah-sansthaapakon ko joden or upyogkartaaon ko sahyog se abhipraay karne kaa mauka detaa hai. hindustan times or da iconomic times ne Ukhadlo ke bare main lekh likhe hai . agarwal ne mumbai or pune main TEDx kaaryakramon main or Brainstorm namak ek kyrkram main Ukhadlo ke saath apni yatra ke bare main bhaashan diya tha. 
 oberoi antarrashtriya skool, mumbai evam UWC mahindra united world college, pune main TEDxYouth@OIS evam TEDxUWCMC - agarwal ne do TEDx kayrakramon main apni or apni companiyon ke baaren main baat kii thi. agarwal ke TEDxYouth @ OIS bhaashan ko YouTube pe 22,000 se adhik baar dekha gaya he. agarwal ne bombai scottish skool main branstorm namak ek kaarykram main bhi bhaashan diya tha. 
 agarwal ne ClassFever kii sthapana 17 saal kii aayu main kari thi, jab unhen 10 veen kaksha ke baad vidhyalay vartanee karne main pareshaani kaa saamana karnaa padaa. ClassFever mul rup se ek skool samiksha ekatrikaran manch tha lekin jald hi ek khule strot par aadhaarit shrenee-kaaran manch main parivartit ho gaya. ClassFever big data or artifical intelligence ke madhyam se ye kaary sampurna karnaa hai. ClassFever vidyalayon ke shrenee karne hetu bees karod gadnaayen karata hai or jis aadhaar par ye shreniyan karata hai, unhen saarvajanik rup main jari karata hai. agarwal ne vidhyalay ke avkaash main apane doston or sahpaathiyon ko ekatrit karke ek AlphaTeam namak team taiyaar kari. ClassFever apni vidhyalay kii shreniyon ko ek agyaat antaraal ke paad tazaa karata hai. ClassFever ranking praaanpatr se alag skulon ko paraamarsh sevaaon kii peshkash se rajasav utpann karata he. agarwal ne donon TEDx kaaryakramon main ClassFever ke bare main baat kii thi. ClassFever acchhee tarah se yahuu or mid de sahit media dwaara praapt kiya gaya he, or yah acchhee tarah se sthaan diya he..
