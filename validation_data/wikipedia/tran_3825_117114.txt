 purushottam agarwal hindi ke ek pramukh aalochak, kavi, chintak or kathakaar hai.
 inkaa janm 25 agast 1955 ko gwalior, madhya pradesh main hua. inhone 1974 main maharani lakshmi bai college, gwalior, madhya pradesh se snaatak kii degree praapt kii. 1977 main jivaji vishvavidhyalay, gwalior, madhya pradesh se raajnneeti vigyaan main snatakottar karne ke baad bhartiya bhashaa centre, bhashaa, sahitya evam samskruti adhyayan santhaan, jawaharlal nehru vishvavidhyalay, nai dilli se hindi main em.e. or em.phil kii oopaadhiyaan praapt kin. agarwal ko 1985 main jawaharlal nehru vishvavidhyalay, nayi dilli ne hindi ke prasidd aalochak namvar singh ke nirdesan main 'kabir kii bhakthi kaa saamaajik arth' vishay par piech.di kii oopaadhi pradaan kii.
 1982-90 tak dilli vishvavidhyalay ke ramajas college main adhyaapan ke baad purushottam agarwal kii niyukti usee bhartiya bhashaa centre main esoceat profesar ke rup main hui jahaan se inhone ucch shiksha praapt kii thi. 2003 main ye yaheen par professor or centre ke adhyaksh bane. pratibhashali praadhyaapak ke rup main ve vidhyaarthiyon main bahut lokapriy rahe.2005 se 2007 tak rashtriya shaikshik anusandhan parishad kii hindi paathyakram nirmaan samiti ke pramukh salaahkaar bhi rahe. 
 purushottam agarwal cambridge vishvavidhyalay, united kingdom ke faikalti of oriental studies main british academi visiting professor rahe. 2002 main cambridge vishvavidhyalay ke hi volfasan college ke phelo ke rup main kaary kiya. cambridge vishvavidhyalay ke dakshin asian adhyayan centre, cambridge vishvavidhyalay main ‘vartmaan bhartiya raajnneeti main asmita vimarsh’ vishay par do sangoshtiyon kaa sanchaalan kiya. may se july, 2002 ke bich ve neshnal college of mexico, mexico city main visiting profesar rahe. bhartiya itihaas or samskruti ke vibhinn vishyon par chaar sangoshtiyon kaa sanchaalan kiya. november-disambar, 2004 ke dauraan america kii apni academik yatra main inhone colombia vishvavidhyalay, neuyark; emory vishvavidhyalay, atlanta; rice vishvavidhyalay, hoston main vibhinn vishyon par bhaashan die. american academy of religen ke nimantran par atlanta main kabir kii bhakthi samvednaa par international associyeshan of historiuns of religens kii vaarshik sabha main niabandh paath. emory vishvavidhyalay ke hell institute ne inke sammaan main lunch par ek sabha kaa aayojan kiya. is sabha main purushottam agarwal ne chunaav ke baad ke bhartiya paridrishy par charchaa kii.iske atirikt hidelburg, francfort, bainkak, landan, paris, colombo, erevan, astrakhaan kii academik, vyaakhyaan or shodh yatraayen kin.
 2 july 2007 ko bharat sarkaar ne purushottam agarwal ko sangh lok seva ayog kaa sadsy manonith kiya. tab se lekar ab tak ve is pad par bane hue hai.
 is dauraan inaki chhavi ek lok buddhijivi kii bani. saamaajik, raajnneetik or saanskrithik vishyon par tii.ve. par bahason se ve deshbhar main lokapriy hue. aaj ek lok buddhijivi ke rup main professor agarwal kaa mahatva nirvivaad he.
 agarwal ne kai pustakon kaa lekhan kiya he, jinamein 'samskruti: varchasv or pratirodh', 'teesraa rukh','vichaar kaa anant', 'shivdaan singh chouhan', 'nij brahm vichaar', 'kabir: saakhi or sabad', 'majbootee kaa naam mahatma gandhi' 'akath kahani prem kii: kabir kii kavita or unka samay' pramukh hai. pro agarwal kii prasiddhi kaa aadhaar he: 'akath kahani prem kii: kabir kii kavita or unka samay'. is pustak ne agarwal ko duniyabar main kabir ke marmbhedi aalochak ke rup main prasiddh kar diya. kabir par anginat pustaken or lekh likhe gaye hai lekin aisa mana jaataa he ki hajariprasad dvivedi kii pustak 'kabir' ke baad agarwal kii yah pustak kabir ko nae dhang se samajhane main sabase adhik sahaayak sabit hoti he. purushottam agarwal kii pahchaan bhaktikaal, khaastaur par kabir ke marmajna aalochak kii he. shaayad isiilie raajakmal prakaashan ne unhen sampoorn 'bhakthi shrinkhalaa' kaa sampadak niyukt kiya he.
 purushottam agarwal kii chhavi ek chintak or aalochak ke rup main he lekin idhar unhone moolyawaan rachanaatmak lekhan bhi kiya he. 2012 main unka yatra-vrittant 'hindi sarai: astrakhaan vaaya erevan' prakashit hua. aajkal ve ek upanyaas par kaam kar rahe hai. inaki ek kahani - 'cheng chui' 2012 main pragatisheel basudha main, 'chauraahey par putala' or 'pairghanti' 2013 nayaa gyanoday va 'shabdankan' main prakashit hui . ek film sameekshak or stanbhakaar ke rup main bhi purushottam agarwal kaa kaam mahatvapurn he. naatak or script lekhan, vruttchitr nirmaan or film samiksha main bhi gahari dilchaspee.
 1. 'hindi nai chaal main dhali' kaa sampadan.
 2. 1983-84 ke bich unhone saahityetar vishyon kii charchit patrika 'jigyaasa' kaa sampadan or prakaashan kiya.
 3. vartmaan main raajakmal prakaashan kii bhakthi shrinkhalaa ke sampadak hai. iske tahat abhi tak david loranjan kii pustak nirgun santon ke swapn or dow raman sinha kii pustak tulsidas : sangeet, chitra..... kaa vistrut bhoomikaaon ke saath sampadan kar chuke hai.
 1. apni aalochana pustak "teesraa rukh" ke liye 1996 main devi shankar avasthi sammaan se sammanit.
 2. apni duusari aalochana pustak "samskruti: varchasv or pratirodh" ke liye 1997 main madhya pradesh sahitya parishad ke mukutadhar pandeya sammaan se sammanit.
 3. "akath kahani prem kii : kabir kii kavita or unka samay" ke liye 2009 main raajakmal prakaashan kaa 1 lakh rupee kaa pratham raajakmal kriti sammaan -kabir, hajariprasad dvivedi puraskaar.""
