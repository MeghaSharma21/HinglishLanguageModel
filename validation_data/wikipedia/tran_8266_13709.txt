 yamuna nadi ke tat par sthit agra shahar aitihaasik smarakon ke liye prasiddh he. san 1506 main sikandar lodhi ne mughalon kaa rajya sthapit kiya tha. mughalon ke shashan ke dauraan khubasurat smarak sthapit kiye gaye the jinhen dekhne aaj bhi paryatak agra aate hai.
 agra main hastshilp bahut hi prasiddh he. yahan sangemarmar ke pattharon par sundar akritiyaan ukeri gai hai. gahane, tohfe evam anya chizon ko surakshit rakhane ke liye kuch vishesh prakaar ke box banaae jate hai. jardoji se banaae gaye kapade paryatakon ke madhya bahut hi lokapriy hai. sadar bazaar, kinari bazaar, raja-kii-mandi main vibhinn prakaar kii aakarshak or manohari chijen dekhi ja sakati hai.
 mithaiyon main agra ke pethe evam gajak bahut hi prasiddh hai. til evam gud se banaai gai mithaiyaan bahut hi swaadisht hoti hai.
 
 vishwa ke saat ajoobon main tajmahal ek anokhaa sthaan rakhata he. yah khubasurat mahal pyaar kaa pratik mana jaataa he. tajmahal ko dilli ke samraat shahjahan ne maharani mumtaj mahal ke liye nirmit kiya tha. har shuukravaar ko tajmahal ko band rakhaa jaataa he.
 samraat akbar chaahate the ki fatehpur sikri agra kii rajdhani kahalaai jaae. manyata he ki sufi sant shaikh saleem chishti kaa gharaana yahan par hi tha. dewan-e-aam, dewan-e-khaas evam pachisi darbar ke adbhut pracheen vaastushilp ko dekhne hajaaron paryatak aate hai. inaki khubsuurati un dinon ke shilp kalakaaron kii nipunata or kushalta ko darshaati he.
 vishwa ke saanskrithik dharohar sthal yah kila bahut hi majboot evam khubasurat najar aata he. yah aisa sthal he jahaan par mughal samarajy ke log rahaa karte the. is kile main shah jahaan ke putr aurangzeb ne unhen kaid karke rakhaa gaya tha.
 
 san 1648 main shahjahan kii beti joharaa begum ne prasiddh sufi sant shaikh saleem chishti kii smriti main jama masjid kaa nirmaan karaayaa tha.
 
 agra main paaramparik bhojan ke liye anek bhojanalay sthit hai. sthaneey nivaasiyon dwaara banaae gaye swaadisht vyanjan anek paryatakon ko aakarshith karte hai.
 paryatakon ke swaagat ke liye vibhinn hotelein sthit hai. inamen lok nrity, kathputli kaa khel evam parampara ke anusaar bhojan parosa jaataa he.
 agra desh kaa mahatvapurn railway stesan he. yahan dilli, varanasi aadi shaharon se aasaani se pahunchaa ja saktaa he.
 yahan se rashtriya rajmarg kramank 3 agra-mumbai prarambh hota he. dilli, fatehpur sikri, jaipur, mathura se sadak maarg se yahan pahunch sakate hai.
 agra se 7 kilometre kii duuri par hawaii adda sthit he. yah desh ke anek pramukh shaharon se judaa hua he.
