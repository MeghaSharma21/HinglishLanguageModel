 pashchim bengal vidhaan sabha bhartiya rajya pashchim bengal kaa ekasadaneey vidhaan bhawan he. yah kolkata ke bibidi baag main sthit he. vidhaan sabha ke sadsy sidhe janata dwaara chune jate hai. vidhansabha main 295 sadsy he jiname 294 sidhe janata ke dwaara tathaa ek sadsy anglo indian samudaay kaa namankit kiya jaataa he. iskaa kaarykaal 5 varsh kaa he.
