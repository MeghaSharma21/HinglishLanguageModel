 maazaa ek coca-cola kaa ek utpaada he jo madhya purv africa, purvi europe tathaa asia main bahut lokapriy he. ise pehali baar 1977 main banaaya gaya tha. maazaa ne ananas tathaa naarangi ke branch bhi khole lekin itane kaamyaab nahin ho sake.
 maazaa ke kai vigyaapan television par dikhaae ja rahe. bharat main "har mausam aam" ke naam se vigyaapan dikhaae jate hai.
