 mahiraa hafiz khan ek pakistani abhinetri hai. yah mukhya rup se humsafar naamak dharawahik ke kaaran jaani jaati hai.
 unke ek chote bhai hasan khan hai jo peshey ke patrkaar hai. khan ne 2006 main los angeles main pati ali aaskaari se mulaakaat kii thi. kuch media reporton kaa kahana he ki vah sindu tivi studio main usase mili. unhone shaadi main ek shaadishuda shaadi ke baavjood ek paaramparik islamic vivaah samaaroh main 2007 main ali asakari se shaadi kii. usne 2009 main ek ladke, ajalan ko janm diya. danpati 2015 main talakshuda.
