 ek vyast jahajrani maarg ke rup main, english channel ko vishakt cargo or tel ke risaav sambandhi durghatnaon ke baad paryaavarniya samasyaon kaa saamana karnaa padataa he. vastav main britain kii 40% se adhik durghatnaayen channel main yaa iske bahut nikat pradooshan ke khatre paidaa karti hai. inamen se sarvaadhik kukhyat durghatnaon main se ek emessi napoli kii thi, jisamen lagbhag 1700 ton kaa khatarnaak cargo vivaadit rup se ek sangrakshit world heritage sait, lime kii khadi ke samudratati maarg main samaa gaya tha. yah jahaaj kshatigrast ho gaya tha or portland jane ke raste main tha, jabaki isse kahin najadik ke bandargah uplabdh the.

 dover bandargah ke saath kathinaaiyon kii vajah se june 1843 main dakshin purv railway company ne kailis-dover ke ek vikalp ke rup main bolon-sur-mar-pholcastone maarg viksit kiya tha. pehali nauka captain heward ke cmand ke tahat paar hui thi. 
 mountbeton class hovercraft ) ne agast 1968 main vanijyik seva main pravesh kiya or shuruyaat main dover or bolon ke bich sanchalit kii gayi, lekin baad main yah nauka ramsget se kailis ke maarg main bhi chalaai gayi. dover se bolon tak kii yatra kaa samay lagbhag 35 minute kaa tha jisamen teji ke samay main chah khep prati din kii yatra hoti thi. ek vanijyik car-vaahak hovercraft dwaara english channel kii sabase tej crossing 22 minute kii thi, jise rajkumaari eni dwaara 14 sitambar 1995 ko 10:00 baje subah kii seva ke liye emsiech esaaar-en4 emke3 se darj kiya gaya tha.
 naav dwaara channel ko paar karne vaale sabase kam umr ke darj navikon main 18 july 2010 ko 7 se 16 varsh kii aayu ke aath bacchon kii ek team thi. unhone apni ek haath se chalaai jane vaali ares terra dingiyon ko khete hue dover se bolon kii 27 mile kii duuri 5 ghanton main puri kii or dover tatarakshak radar dwaara unki nigaraanee kii gayi jisane raste kaa ek record rakhaa. ye bachche britain ke teen klbon ke the: kaisal cove essi, dabchiks essi or dons essi.

 tairaaki sanghathan sies and pief or siese ne tairaakon ko unki mahangi payalat naukaaon ko simit karne kii safaltaapurvak pairvi kii he. is raajnneetik pairvi kaa parinaam is dastauez main ullikhit he. is pairvi ke prayaas ke baavjood tairaak is dastavej main se yah note karenge ki "haalanki, asaadhaaran maamalon main francisi samudri adhikariyon dwaara trephic sepperation skim ke antargat francisi kshetreey samudr ko paar karne ke liye aparamparaagat nauka ke istemaal kaa adhikaar pradaan kiya ja saktaa he jab ye naukaaen british samudratat se chalengin, is shart par kii iske adhikaar kaa anurodh unke paas british samudri adhikariyon kii raay ke saath bheja jaae". isliye channel main tairaaki ke samay ek gaiyr-siese yaa sies and pief payalat nauka kiraye par lenaa sambhav he.

 16 sitambar 1965 ko do amphykaron ne dover se kailis tak english channel ko safaltaapurvak paar kiya tha. ek car main chaalaak dal ke rup main do sena ke adhikari, captain mike beli aariemai or captain peter tapenden maujuud the. duusari car main tim dil-rasel or sargent jo minto areessi chaalaak dal ke rup main the. is crossing main 7 ghante or 20 minute kaa samay laga. channel ke madhya main sharten force 5 tak kii thi. kaaron ko us saal ke francfort motor sho main le jaaya gaya, jahaan unhen pradarshnee ke liye rakhaa gaya tha.
