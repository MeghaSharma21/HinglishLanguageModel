 kisi chaalak ke bheetar parivarti chumbakeey kshetra hone par usamen vidyut dhara utpann hoti he use bhanvar dhara kehete hai. dhara kii ye bhavaren chumbakeey kshetra paidaa karti hai or yah chumbakeey baahar se aropit chumbakeey kshetra ke parivartan kaa virodh karata he. bhanvar dhaaraon se utpann chumbakeey kshetra aakarshan, pratikarshan, ooshman aadi prabhaav utpann karata he. baahar se aropit chumbakeey kshetra jitna hi teevr hoga or uske parivartan kii gati jitani adhik hogi or padaarth kii vidyut chaalakata jitani adhik hogi, utani hi adhik maatra main bhanvar dharaayen utpann hongi tathaa unke kaaran utpann chumbakeey kshetra kaa maan bhi utana hi adhik hoga.
 parinamitra, vidyut janitr evam vidyut motaron ke kor main bhanvar dhaaraon ke kaaran urja kii haani hoti he or iske kaaran crode garm hoti he. kor main bhanvardhaara haani kam karne ke liye crode ko pattyit banaaya jaataa he, arthath patali-patali pattiyon ko milakar kor banaai jaati he, na ki ek thos kor se. 
 bhanvar dhaaraon se haani v unhen kam karne ke upaay-:bhanvar dhaaraon ke kaaran jo ushmiya urja utpann hoti he,vah vidyut urja kaa hi parivartit rup he.dianemo ke armechar,transformar tathaa prerana kundali main lohe kii crod prayukt kii jaati he. crode main bhanvar dharaayen banane ke kaaran ye bahut garm ho jaati he. jiske kaaran vidyut urja kaa ooshmaa ke rup main hraas hota he. urja hraas ko kam karne ke liye crode yaa frem ke naram lohe ke ek akele tukade ke rup main nahin lete,balki naram lohe ke kai patalon ko varnish dwaara jodakar banaate hai. is prakaar kii crode ko patlit crode kehete hai. faltah ooshmaa ke rup main hone vaala urja hraas kam ho jaataa he.
 bhanvar dhaaraon ke upyog:bhanvar dhaaraon kaa upyog uske ushmiya prabhaav avarohak ke liye kiya jaataa he.iske mukhya anuprayog nimnlikhit hai.:-1) chal kundali dharamapi ko dolan-ruddha banane main :-dharamapi kii kundalee yadi taanbe ke vidyutarodhi taar ko aluminium ke frem par lapetakar banaayi jaae,tab kundali main dhara pravaahit karne par kundali main vikshep hoga. saath hi kundali ke frem main bhanvar dharaayen utpann hogi jo kundali ko adhiktam vikshep kii sthiti main laakar sheeghra sthir kar degi.
 2) preran bhatti:-bhanvar dhaaraon se utpann ushmiya urja kaa upyog preran bhatti main kiya jaataa he. dhaatu ko kuch ucch aavruti kii laratyavarti dhara kii kundalee ke bich rakhaa jaataa he. teevr parivartan sheel chumbakeey kshetra ke kaaran prabal bhanvar dhaaraon se dhaatu kaa top bahut bad jaataa he. isi siddhant par preran bhatti banaai gayi he. inamen khaanaa pakaane se lekar dhaatu pighalaane tak kaa bhi kaary kiya jaataa he.s
