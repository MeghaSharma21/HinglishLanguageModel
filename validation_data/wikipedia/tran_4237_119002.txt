  udupi rajagopalacharya ananthamurti  samakaleen kannada sahityakar, aalochak or shikshavid hai. inhen kannada sahitya ke navya andolan kaa praneta mana jaataa he. inaki sabase prasiddh rachana sanskaar he. gyanpith puraskaar paane vaale aath kannada sahityakaron main ve chathe hai. unhone mahatma gandhi vishvavidhyalay tiranantpuram or kendriya vishvavidhyalay gulberga ke kulapati ke rup main bhi kaam kiya tha. sahitya evam shiksha]] ke kshetra main ullekhneey yogadan ke liye san 1998 main 
 unka janm shimoga jile ke tirthahalli tallka ke melige main hua. unki shiksha durvasapura ke paaramparik sanskrit paathshaala main shuru hui. iske baad kii shiksha tirthahalli or mysore main hui. mysore vishvavidhyalay se parasnatak kii oopaadhi hasil karne ke baad aage ke adhyayan ke liye ve rashtramandal chaatravrutti lekar england chale gaye. unhone 1966 i. main birmingham vishvavidhyalay se "1930 main raajnneeti or sahitya" sheershak shodhagranth likhakar shodh oopaadhi hasil kii. unka vivaah 1956 i. main istr anantmurti ke saath hua jinase unki mulaakaat 1954 i. main hui thi. unki do santane hai putree anuraadhaa or putr sharat. ve 1980 ke dashak main mahatma gandhi vishvavidhyalay tiruvanantpuram ke kulapati rahe. iske baad unhone kendriya vishvavidhyalay gulberga ke kulapati ke rup main bhi apni sevaayen di. 22 agast 2014 ko 81 varsh kii avasthaa main unka lambii beemaari ke baad bangalore main nidhan ho gaya.
 unki soch samaajawaadee or udaaravaadi thi. unhone aam logon ko sahitya main mahatv diya. saamaajik kuritiyon par unhone jamkar prahaar kiya.
