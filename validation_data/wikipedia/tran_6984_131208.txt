 kalyanpur ek chota gaanv he or yah bharat ke bihar rajya ke saaran jila ke sonpur prakhand main sthit he. yah 43 meter kii oonchai par he kalyanpur gaanv sonpur ke up-jila mukhyalay se 8 kimi dur or chhapra jila mukhyalay se 47 kimi dur sthit he. 2009 ke aankadon ke mutabik, kalyanpur gaanv kalyanpur gaanv kaa gram panchayat he. kalyanpur gaanv main gulriya chowk ke nikat kaa chota bazaar he. sonpur sabase nazadiiki shahar he jo kalyanpur se lagbhag 8 kimi dur he.
 kalyanpur gaanv sonpur shahar se kareeb 8 kimi dur sthit he. yah 100 hectare kaa kul bhugolic kshetra he or 43 meter kii oonchai par. is gaanv main adhikansh kshetra chaaval or gehun ke saath mukhya bhoomi ke rup main krishi bhoomi he. vaarshik varsha main badlaav ke kaaran, sthaneey kisaan sinchai ke liye mausami barish par nirbhar nahin kar sakate hai or iske bajaay sinchai ke liye baramasi kuon ko anaarit karnaa padataa he. or gaanv krishi or anya uddeshy ke liye gandak nadi ke paas he.
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar, kalyanpur kii jansankhya 3,608 thi. purushon kii aabaadi kaa 52% or mahilaon main 48% he. kalyanpur main ausat sakshartaa dar 70% he, jo rashtriya ausat 59.5% se adhik he: purush sakshartaa 74% he or mahila sakshartaa 62% he. kalyanpur main, aabaadi kaa 4.5% 5 saal se kam umr kaa he. kalyanpur gaanv main 597 ghar hai. kalyanpur ke paas nikatatam gaanv, shikarpur gaanv he.
 sarkaar dwaara vikaas ke prayaason ke kaaran kalyanpur main achche sadak sanchaar hai. gaanv ke nikatatam shahar or railway stesan, gaanv se lagbhag 8 kilometre, sonpur main he.
 kalyanpur gaanv main ek vyapak bijli aapurti he, lekin niyamit bijli aapurti nahin he ki sthaneey log nikaay ke prayaason main saura panelon ko sadak prakaash vyavastha ke liye bijli pradaan karne ke liye kiya karte he.
