 2014 isisi vishwa cap kwalifire 2015 isisi vishwa cap ke liye antim yogyata tay karne ke liye ek cricket toornament tha. sheersh do tiimon ko vishwa cap ireland main shaamil hone ke liye or pehali baar afghanistan kwalifai jo pehle se hi 2011-13 isisi vishwa cricket lig championships ke madhyam se yogya or unke vanade sthiti ko banaae rakhaa he. vishwa cap kwalifire 2009-14 vishwa cricket lig ke antim ghatana thi. yah 13 janvari 2014 se 1 farvari 2014 karne ke liye newjiland main manchan kiya gaya tha, ke baad scotland ise host karne ke liye sahi tyaag kar diya.
 toornament unke tisare vishwa cap ke liye yogyata or unke duusare vishwa cap ke liye unki vanade sthiti or sanyukt arab emirate yogyata ko banaae rakhana he or vanade kaa darja paane scotland ko dekha. vishwa cap, honkong or papua new gini ke liye yogyata nahin hone ke baavjood kramash: tisare or chauthe sthanon main toornament khatm karke pehali baar vanade kaa darja praapt kii.
 toornament bhi pramukh sahyogi deshon netherland, kenya or canada vishwa cap ke liye kwalifai or 2018 tak apane vanade kaa darja kho karne main vifal dekha haalanki netherland 2014 isisi vishwa twanti 20 ke bajaay scotland ke liye kwalifai kiya tha.
 isisi ke anusaar, "das tiimon ke liye samaan rup se do samoohon main sheersh teen pakshon ke saath prayek samooh ke super sicks charan ke liye pragati se vibhajit ho gaya tha. super sicks charan se do sheersh timen hi final main pahunchane nahin hoga, lekin yah bhi 14 team teli puraa karne ke liye isisi cricket vishwa cap 2015 ke liye arhata praapt karenge. sheersh chaar team vanade kaa darja mil jayega jahaan ke rup main paanchaven, chathe, saatven or aathven sthaan par tiimon rahegaa yaa divijan do main chalaa ja"
 toornament 2011-13 isisi vishwa cricket lig championships ke samaapan ke baad. is toornament, ireland or afghanistan se sheersh do timen 2015 vishwa cap ke liye kwalifai kiya, shesh chah tiimon ke vishwa cap kwalifire main pravesh karne ke saath. ve isisi vishwa cricket lig divijan do 2011 or se isisi vishwa cricket lig divijan teen 2013 sheersh do tiimon se tisare or chauthe sthaan par kabiz tiimon se jude hue the.
 toornament sthanapann teen match referi the or toornament ke dauraan sabhi main, vahaan 14 ampiron jo kartavya hota he, isisi ampiron ke emirates elite panel ke marice irasamas sahit the, jabaki shesh 13 pratinidhiyon isisi ampiron ke emirates antarrashtriya panel or isisi se the associats or sahyogi ampiron ke panel.
