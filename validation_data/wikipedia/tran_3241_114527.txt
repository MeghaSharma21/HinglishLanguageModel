 bihar sangrahalay patna main nirmit ek aadhunik kala sangrahalay he. yah agast 2015 main aanshik rup se kholaa gaya tha. agast 2015 main 'bacchon ke sangrahalay', mukhya pravesh kshetra or ek abhivinyas theatre saarvajanik rup se keval ek hi hissaa khol diya gaya tha. baad main, oktober 2017 main shesh ddeerghaaon ko bhi kholaa gaya. yahaan patna sangrahalay se 100 se adhik kalakritiyon ko sthanantarit kiya gaya tha.
 yah bihar rajya ke liye ek itihaas sangrahalay ke rup main yojana banaai gai, or oktober 2013 main beli road, patna, bihar, bharat main anumaanit bajet 498 karod ke saath nirmaan shuru kiya. sangrahalay ko is kshetra ke hajaaron varsh ke itihaas ko dhyaan main laane, vishwa ke chaaron ore se sthaneey nivaasiyon or aagantukon ko prerak banane ke liye bihar kii samrudh viraasat, aitihaasik sthalon or saanskrithik aakarshanon kaa pataa lagane kii yojana banaai gai thi.
 ek 25,000 varg meter kaa sangrahalay or bazaar avadharana "ji + 1" sanrachana ke saath or "ji + 4" kaa ek bahut hi chota bhaag ke saath viksit kiya janaa tha. yah 5.6-hectare main faile chah manjila imaarat he, jisamen 24,000 varg meter kaa nirmaan kshetra he.
 japan ke maaki and associats ne mumbai ke opolis architects ke saath bihar sangrahalay ke vaastushilp dizin kaa vikaas kiya. is prastaav main chaar pramukh pradarshnee ddeerghaaon, shaikshik, lobby, prashasnic or sthaayee gailaree shaamil thi
 pariyojna ke liye salaahkaar niyukt karne ke liye sarkaar ne july 2011 main canada aadhaarit paraamarsh company lord saanskrithik sansadhano ke saath samjhauta gyaapan par hastakshar kiye the. janvari 2012 main, rajya ke cabinet ne japan-aadhaarit maaki and associats or uske bhartiya partner opolis, mumbai ko pariyojna ke praathmik salaahkaar vaastukar ke rup main chayan ko manjuuri de di.
 prasiddh film nirmaataa bobi bedi kii company, callidoscope india privet limited ne sangrahalay ke liye audiosizual or filmaayi gai saamagree kaa nirmaan kiya he.
 oktober 2017 main, bharat ke pradhan mantri narendra modi bihar ke mukhyamantri nitish kumaar ke saath bihar sangrahalay kaa daura kiya. 2300 varshiya didarganj yakshi bihar sangrahalay main bhi hai. pracheen bharat yug se 1764 tak kalakritiyon ko bihar sangrahalay main rakhaa gaya he or 1764 ke baad ke avyav patna sangrahalay main rakhe jate hai.
