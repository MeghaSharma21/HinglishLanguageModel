 hastakshar jaalsaji ek gambhir aparaadh he. kisi vyakti dwaara kisi bhi dastavej par anya vyakti ke jaali hastakshar karnaa, hastakshar jaalsaji kehalaati he. kai hastakshar jaalsaaj to itane anbhavi hote he ki unka aparaadh sabit karnaa mushkil ho jaataa he kintu phir bhi hamaare nyaayalik anbhavi unki jaalsaji sabit karne main sakasham hote hai.
 hastakshar jaalsaji main kai prakaar ke tareeke istemaal kiye jate he. jinka vivaran is prakaar he -
 hastakshar karne ke tareeke se vyakti kaa pataa lagaaya ja saktaa he, kyonki har vyakti ke apane hastakshar kii kuch visheshtaayen hotin hai. unka adhyayan kar ke pataa lagaaya ja saktaa he jaise ki;
