 bharat sarkaar ne desh bhar main 18 biosphere bhandar sthapit kiye hai. ye biosphere bhandar bhugolic rup se jeev jantuon ke prakritik bhu-bhaag kii raksha karte hai or akasar aarthik upyogon ke liye sthapit bafer jonon ke saath ek yaa zyada rashtriya udyaan or abhyaarany ko sangrakshit rakhane kaa kaam karte hai. sangrakshan na keval sangrakshit kshetra ke vanaspatiyon or jeevon ke liye diya jaataa he, balki in kshetron main rahane vaale maanav samudaayon ko bhi diya jaataa he.
 atharah jeevamandal bhandar main se das unesco ke maanav or sangrakshit jaevmandal kaarykram suchi ke aadhaar par sangrakshit jaevmandalon ke vishwa network kaa ek hissaa hai.
 
 2009 main, bharat ne himachal pradesh ke sheet-registan ko biosphior rigerv ke rup main namit kiya. 20 sitambar, 2010 ko, paryaavaran or van mantralay ne shashchalam pahadiyon ko 17 veen jeevamandal aarakshan ke rup main namit kiya. panna ko 25 agast, 2011 ko 18 wein sthaan par namit kiya.
 van or paryaavaran mantralay dwaara chaynit biosphere rigerv ke liye sambhavit citon kii suchi nimnlikhit he.
