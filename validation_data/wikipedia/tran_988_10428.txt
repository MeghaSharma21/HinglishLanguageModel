 
 billi ek maansaahaari standhari jaanavar he. isaki sunne or sunghane kii shakti prakhar he or yah kam roshni, yahan tak ki raat main bhi dekh sakati hai. lagbhag 9500 varshon se billi manushya ke saathi ke rup main he. prakritik rup se inkaa jeevankaal lagbhag 15 varshon kaa hota he.
 billiyon kaa majboot or lacheela sharir, tej abhikriyaayein, sankuchan yogya panje or chote shikaar ko maarane ke liye roopantarit daant hote hai. billi guptangon kii sandhyaadaar or maansaahaar jagat main khaas jagah hoti he. billiyaan, maanav kaanon ke liye bahut hi madhyam or bahut hi teevr awaazon, jaise ki chuhon vagairah kii, ko aaraam se sun saktin hai. ye qareeb-qareeb ghaney andhero main bhi dekh saktin hai. baaqi stanadhariyon kii tarah inaki rang drushti manushya se kaafii kamazor he jabaki inaki soonghane kii taaqat or zyaada majboot hoti he.
 ekaant main shikari karne vaali jaanavar hone ke baavjood, billiyaan saamaajik jaanavar bhi hai. inke aapasi sanchaar kaa saadhan alag alag tarahon kii awaazen, rasinic tatv or billi vishesh shaareerik bhashaa ke prakaar hai.
 kyonki billiyaan pracheen misr main poojaneey jaanavar thim, ye vahaan hi paaltu banaain gayi thim, lekin paaltu banane ke kuch sanket pehle ghate "navpashan yug" main bhi milte hai.
 2007 kii ek anuvaanshik jaanch padtaal ne kulasa kiya he ki sabhi paaltu billiyaan madhya-purv ilaake kii paanch maadaa africa junglee billiyon ke vansh main se ain hai. billiyaan duniya kii sab se mashhoor paaltu jaanavar hai or jahaan bhi insaan rahete hai, vahaan hi pain jaatin hai.
 paaltu billi or uski sab se qareebi junglee purvaj, donon hi dvigunasutrak jaanavar hai jinamein 38 gunasutra or lagbhag 20,000 jin hote hai. billiyon ke takreeban 250 anuvanshik rog pahachaane ja chuke hai jinamein se kaafii maanviya janmajaat gadabadon ke samaan hai. is standhari jeevon kii chayapachay kii samanta ko nazar rakhte hue billiyon ke kai rog un anuvanshik pareekshanon ke saath jaanche jate hai jo shuruyaat main manushya ke liye banaae gaye the or isi tarah manushya ke kai rogon kii jaanch padtaal ke liye billiyon ko jeevanamoonon ke taur par istemaal kiya jaataa he.
 europe or uttari america main billiyaan aam paaltu jaanavar hai or duniya bhar main inaki kul ginati 50 karod se zyaada he. billi paalan zyaadaatar striyon ke saath joda jaataa he, lekin 2007 kii gassop vot ne sanket diya he ki mard or aurat kii billi rakhane kii sambhaavnaayen ek jaisi he.
 sabhi gyaat itihaas main, billiyon ko paalane kaa maqasad yah tha ki ve chuuhe khaati hai or is tarah anaaj ko is nuqasaan se surakshit rakhaa ja saktaa he. pracheen misri log billiyon kii puja bhi karte the or unki laashon ko surakshit karte the taaki unhen hameshaa ke liye mukti mil sake. pracheen misri devi baast billi kaa rup dhaaran karti thi.:220 
 haalanki islam main koi bhi jaanavar pavitra nahin maane jate hai, lekin aksar billiyon ko musalamaanon dwaara sammanit kiya jaataa he.
 aaj ke daur main billiyaan paaltu jaanwaron ke taur par paali jaati hai, iske alaava bhi billi insaani aabaadiyon main pai jaati he. viksit deshon main billiyon ko paaltu jaanwaron ke taur par paalane kaa rivaaj bahut zyaada he or unke liye vishesh khuraaq taiyaar karne ke kaarobaar ko ek bada udyog kii haisiyat praapt he.
 maanav gharon main paayi jane vaali billi ko marwari bhashaa main 'minaki' tathaa junglee billi ko 'balaara' kaha jaataa hai. iskaa angreji main prachlit naam 'pusi ket' bhi he.
