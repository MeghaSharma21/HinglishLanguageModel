 shyamanand krishnabhakt sant the. 
 inkaa janm caitra shu. 15, san. 1590 ko urisa ke dharenda gram main hua. pita kaa naam krishnamandal tathaa mata kaa doorika daasi tha. janm ke ananthar yah suvarnarekha nadi ke tatasth ambuya gram main aa based. yaheen inhone shiksha praapt kii. inkaa naam dukhi krishnadas tha tathaa ye sadgop the. srihridayanand se diksha lekar yatra karte hue ye vrundavan pahunche. yahan shri jeev goswami ke yahan adhyayan karte hue 28 varsh rahe. goswami ji ne inhen diksha dekar shyamanand naam rakhaa. shri radhika ji kii bhi inpar yaheen krupa hui. vrundavan main prastut hue goswami granthon ko bengal main prachararth bhejne ke liye shri jeev goswami ne shri nivasacharya, narottam thakur tathaa shyamanand ko niyat kiya kyonki ye teenon sahpaathee the. pratham do bengal main tathaa tritiy ne urisa main shri gaurang ke mat kaa prachaar kiya.
