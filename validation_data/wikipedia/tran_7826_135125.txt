 dow ashok tanvar ek bhartiya raajnneetijna, bhartiya yuva congress or enesayuai ke purv raashtrapati or vartmaan main sirsa se saansad hai.
 bhartiya yuva congress ke raashtrapati jaise pratishthit pad ko paane vaale ve sabase choti umr ke vyakti hai.unka sammaan naa sirf isliye hai ki ve jawaharlal nehru network ke sadsy bhi hai .
 unka janm dilbagh singh or krishna rathi ke ghar hua. ve itihaas se sambandhit padhai karne ke liye jawahar laal nehru centre vishvavidhyalay gaye or vahaan unhone em.e., em.phil.or piech.di. main kiya.
 drushok tanvar ne apane karier kii shuruyaat jeenyu main enesayuai ke ek kaaryakarta ke rup main kii.
 tanvar congress party ke ubharate hue yuva netaaon main se ek hai. ve rahul gandhi, ke bahut kareeb hai, jo kii yuva congress ke prabharee hai.tanvar kii khaasiyat yah he ki unka jeenyu se gaharaa sambandh us samay he jab, congress ke chaatr sanghathan, bhartiya rashtriya chaatr sangh, kii mushkil se parisar main maujuudagi he. jab bhi unhone jeenyu main adhyaksh pad ke liye chunaav ladaa he. .tanvar ne enesayuai ke sachiv kaa padbhaar 1999 main sambhaala or ve 2003 main iske adhyaksh bane. 2003 main unke padbhaar sanbhaalane ke baad dilli vishvavidhyalay chaatr sangh ke chunaavon main do baar lagaataar jeet or vampanthi dalon ke varchasv vaale jeenyu main, enesayuai ke pradarshan main ullekhneey sudhaar ke baad, tanvar ne apane adhyaksh hone ke kaarykaal ke dauraan enesayuai main adhik anushaasan baratane qi jarurat par jor diya he.
 bhartiya yuva congress ke raashtrapati ke rup main, "aivysi" ko majboot karne ke liye zyada dhyaan sabhagaaron, seminaron or kaaryshaalaaon par diya gaya he. janata kii samasyaon ko ujagar karne ke liye nukkad naatak or saamaajik kaam par adhik jor diya gaya. ashok tanvar ke netritv main, saamaajik muddon ko ujagar karne vaale kaarykram desh bhar main chalaaye gaye. unke kaarykaal main aam aadmi kaa sipaahi kii avadharana lagu kii gayi. yuvaaon ko shashakt banane ke kaaryakramon ko vishesh mahatv diya gaya he or rahul ji ke vijan ko in kaaryakramon ke madhyam se aakaar diya gaya he. bhartiya yuvak congress ne unke netritv main bahut zyada pragati kii he.
 unke kaarykaal ke dauraan lagu kiye gaye kaaryakramon kaa ek vishesh pahalu yah he ki un kaaryakramon ko vikendrikrut tareeke se lagu kiya gaya, jisamen block, jila or rajya stareey yuva congress ko shaamil kiya gaya. unhone sanghathan main pratibha par vishesh dhyaan dene kii koshish kii he. sanghathanaatmak chunaav karaane kii yojana main yah prayaas parilakshith hota he. ab, chunaav punjab or gujarat main ho chuke hai, jahaan sabhi tabake ke logon ne badi sankhya main yuva congress se jud kar use majboot banane kaa prayaas kiya he or is prakaar se congress party kaa aadhaar sabhi rajyon main majboot kiya he.
 congress party ke dwaara unhen 2009 ke lok sabha chunaav sirsa haryana se ladane ke liye namankit kiya gaya tha or yah seat unhone apane nikatatam pratidwandvi ai en el di ke dosita ram ko 35499 maton ke antar se haraa kar jiti. yah jeet kisi bhi anya ummidavaar kii jeet se zyada mahatvapurn thi kyonki sirsa haryana ke mahatvapurn netaa omprakash chautaala kaa grih jila he or ashok tanvar ko is nirvachan kshetra main yah chunaav jeetne ke liye ek maheene se bhi kam samay mila tha. is jeet ke saath, ashok tanvar kii khyaati rashtriya netaa ke rup main fail gayi kyonki ve sabase yuva ummidavaaron main se ek the jinhen congress mahasachiv rahul gandhi ne 2009 ke lok sabha chunaav ladane ke liye swayan chunaa tha.
 june 2005 main, ashok tanvar kaa vivaah avantika maakan se hua, jo kii lalit maakan kii beti hai or bhootpurv bharat ke raashtrapati, dow shankar dayaal sharma unke naanaa hai. unke ek putr or ek putree he.
