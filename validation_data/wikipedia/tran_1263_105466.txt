 wo teri bhabhi he pagale ek bhartiya hindi dharawahik he. jiskaa prasaaran sab tivi par 18 janvari 2016 se somvaar se shuukravaar raat 10:30 baje hota he. iskaa nirmaan vipul shah ne kiya he. isamen mukhya kirdaar main ali asagar, krishna gaukani or athar habib hai.
 isamen do ladke ek hi ladki se pyaar karte rahete hai. nathu nakaabandi usase milane ke liye aspataal aata he, jahaan use pataa chalata he ki diya ko gunde badmaashon se sakht nafrat he. ye baat jaan kar nathu ek nakli marij ke rup main vahaan chale jaataa he. doosraa premi ranvir jab us ladki se milane aspataal aata he to use bhi pataa chalata he ki diya amir logon se nafrat karti he. iske baad ranvir nakli doctor ban kar aspataal main naukari ke liye aa jaataa he. vahiin nathu ek anokhi beemaari ke saath vahaan aata he, jise vahaan kaa koi bhi doctor samaj nahin paata he. tabhi ranvir bhi vahaan naukari ke liye aata he or vah nathu ko dekh kar bataata he ki use ek nai beemaari he. iske baad nathu or ranvir donon aspataal main alag alag jhooth bol kar aa jate hai.
 lekin kuch hi dinon ke baad un donon ko ek duusare kii sachai kaa pataa chal jaataa he or ve donon is baat ko diya ko bataane kii koshish karte hai or ant main yah faisla karte hai ki koi bhi is raaj ko diya ko nahin batayega. iske baad ve ek duusare ko "wo teri bhabhi he pagale" kehete hai.
