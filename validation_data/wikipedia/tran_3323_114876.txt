 vishwa swasthya sanghathan kii paribhaasha ke anusaar, hinsa "swayan ke viruddh, kisi anya vyakti yaa kisi samooh yaa samudaay ke viruddh shaareerik bal yaa shakti kaa sabhipraay upyog he, chaahe dhamkeeswaroop yaa vaastavik, jiskaa parinaam yaa ucch sambhaavna he ki jiskaa parinaam chot, mrityu, manovaigyanik nuksaan, durbalata, yaa kuvikas ke rup main hota hai", haalanki sanghathan yah sweekaar karata he ki isaki paribhaasha main "shakti kaa upyog" shaamil karnaa shabd kii paaramparik samaj ko badhaata he. is paribhaasha main kriya ko hi karne kii sabhipraayta shaamil he, chaahe usase kuch bhi parinaam utpann ho. haalanki, aam taur par, jo kuch bhi hanikaarak yaa kshatikaarak tareeke se uttejit kiya jaataa he, vah hinsa ke rup main varnit kiya ja saktaa he, bhale hi vah hinsa ke matlab se nahin ho .
