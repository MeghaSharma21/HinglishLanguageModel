 helabidu karnataka rajya ke hasan jila main sthit he. ise purv main dorasamudr yaa dwarasamudr bhi kaha jaataa tha. yah hoysaal rajvansh kii rajdhani rahaa he. is kaaran yah hoysaal sthapatya kala kaa adbhut kendra he, jiske mukhya namoonay hai hoysaleshwar or kedareshwar mandir. vartmaan helabidu kaa arth he nasht shahar. is shahar ko bahamani samrajya dwaara do baar nasht kiya gaya tha. helibid ko bhartiya mandir or shilp kala kaa darshan karaane vaale sthaan ke rup main janaa jaataa he. belur ke sang judwa nagar kahi jane vaali yah jagah teen shatabdiyon tak hoysal vansh kaa garh tha. belur-helibid kii sthapana jan anuyaayi nrupa kama ne kii thi. lekin ise vaastavik prasiddhi hoyasla vansh ke shashan kaal main bane mandiron ke liye mili. hoyasla shaasha kala or shilp ke sangrakshak the. belur or helibid main inhone bhavy mandiron kaa nirmaan karaayaa jo aaj bhi usee shaan se khadey hai.
