 gaanpaty hinduon kaa vah sampradaay he jo ganpati ko sagun brahm ke rup main maanata evam puujata he.
 mantra -  gan ganapatye namah
 ganapaty sampradaay ke log gudh hindu sampradaay ke sadsy he jo gajanan ganesh ko param devtaa maanakar unki puja karte hai.
 lagbhag 10veen shatabadi main yah sampradaay apane charam par tha or isane sabhi mahatvapurn kaaryon or dharmik anushthanon ke prarambh main santusht kiye jane vaale mahatvapurn devtaa ke rup main ganesh kii sthapana kii.is sampradaay ne ganesh ko samarpit mandir banawaye, jinamein sabase bada, tiruchirapalli main chattaanon ko katkar banaaya gaya mandir ucchi-pillaiyar kovil he.ganesh kii puja unki murti ke samaksh dhyaan ke dwaara or bina dhyaan kiye fal-phool arpit karke, donon tareeke se kii jaati he.is sampradaay ke sadsy maathe par gol laal tikaa lagaate hai or kandhon par hathi kaa sir or daant kaa chihn ankit karavaate hai.
