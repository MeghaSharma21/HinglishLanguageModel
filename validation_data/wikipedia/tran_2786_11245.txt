 tripitak
 khuddakanikaay buddha granth tripitak kaa suttapitak kaa paanchava nikaay he. isamen dhammapad, udaan, itiduttak, suttanipaat, ther-theri gaatha, jataka aadi solah granth sangrahit he. inamen se kuch main buddha ke pramanik vachanon kaa sangrah hai. yah chote sutron kaa sankalan he. 
 is granth main kul 18 sheershak hai jinamein se 15 pramukh sheershak hai.-
