 englistaan main naatyashaalaaon kaa 18veen shatabdi main bahut parivartan hua. isi yug main david garik hua jisane apane abhinay se landan ke saare darshakon ko mohit kar liya tha. iske samay druryllen main naatyashaala thi hin, ek or naatyashaala gurmance fields main ban gai thi. yahan david garik ne shakespeare ke "richard tritiy" lear, hamelet" ityadi kaa abhinay is swaabhaaviktaa se kiya ki log dang rah gaye. is kaal main naatakkaar shakespeare ke samakaksh nahin pahunch paae parantu natyakala kaa puraa vikaas garik tathaa kenvel ne kiya. ye achche prabandhak bhi the.
 america main sabase pratham naatak new mexico ke rio grand main san 1598 main khelaa gaya. yah naatak kaptel faro kaa likhaa hua tha tathaa spain kii bhashaa main tha. 1606 i. main mark lecorbon kaa mask port royal akadia main khelaa gaya. varjeeniyaan ke purvin kinare par 1665 i. main pehla angreji naatak khelaa gaya parantu aisa samajha jaataa he ki 1732 i. main pehali naatyashaala neuyark main bani or 6 disambar ko isamen pehla naatak "da recruiting afisar" khelaa gaya. varjeenia main chalrs tathaa meri stag ke praytn se 1716 i. main naatyashaala bani. iske parde tathaa duusare upkaran englistaan se mangavaaye gaye. philadelphia main 1749 i. main naatyashaala kaa bananaa aarambh hua. neuyark ke naso sadak par par jo naatyashaala bani usamen shakespeare tathaa anya angreji naatakakaron ke naatakon kaa abhinay 1750 i. main prarambh hua. is kaal tak naatak ke parde, jo englistaan se mangaae gaye, puraane pad gaye the. mombatti se naatyashaala ko aalokit kiya jaataa tha. america kii kranti ke pashchaat neuyark main jon street naatyashaala bani, philadelphia main southwark main memriland main east balteemor sadak par, tathaa chalrston main dak street naatyashaalaays baniin. is kaal ke naatakon main vishesh rup se englistaan ke prati vidroh kii bhaavana jaagrath karne kaa praytn drushtigochar hota he.
 amareeki lekhak kaa likhaa hua sarvapratham naatak thomas gadfre kaa di prince aav parthia mana jaataa he. yon is kaal kaa royal tailor kaa likhaa hua di contrast, jo 1787 i. main khelaa gaya, bahut prasiddh hua. iske pashchaat america kii apni style chal padi or apane dhang kii naatyashaalaays bhi banane lageen tathaa europe kaa prabhaav kam hota chalaa gaya. 19veen shatabdi main france main ek naye yug kaa anrabh hua. dela crua hugo, laa martin, duma beljaa ityadi ne nai prerana di. paramparaagat paddati ke sthaan par nae prayogon ko apnaanaa prarambh hua, naatyashaalaaon ke aakaar main bhi ab parivartan hua tathaa saajasja bhi badali. naatyashaalaays ab bheetar se praaia: gol aakaar kii banane lageen. rangamanch ke saamne tathaa uske teenon ore harmikaaye rdaankon ke baithne ke hetu baniin. rangamanch par vaastaviktaa laane ke hetu bhawanon ke bhaagon ko dikhaaya jane laga. isi kaal main vigyaan ne tathaa darwin ke vikaswad ne jivan kii manyataon main bahut her pher kar diya tha. iske prabhaav se naatyashaalaaon main kal purjon ke sahare druy jaldi jaldi badale jane lage.
 19veen shatabdi ke uttarardh main emil jola tathaa alphans dode ne vaastavik jivan ko rangamanch par pradarshit karne kaa praytn kiya tathaa apane kaal kii jivan kii samasyaon kaa digrdaan karana prarambh kiya. andra antvaan ne is prayog ko or aage badhaayaa. ab saadhaaran janata ke jivan kaa bhi prardaan rangamanch par prarambh hua tathaa uski jhopadi, uski tuuti kursi tathaa mej bhi saji sajaai naatyashaalaaon main dikhaai jane lagi.
 is yug kaa sabase prabhavashali naatakkaar ibson hua. isane saare sabhy sansar ke naatakon ke likhane kii paddati ko hi badal diya. isane samaaj kii kritrim manyataon tathaa dhong kii aisi hansee udayi ki sari janata stabdh rah gai. iske saamaajik, raajnneetik tathaa dharmik jivan ke nagn chithran ne logon ko apane siddhanton ke vishay main sochane ko vivaa kar diya. di unjes fovend yaa di lig aav youth, at duckegem yaa e dals houd ityadi ne badi prasiddhi paai. ibson ke likhe naatakon kaa sudur japan tak main prachaar hua.

 ek prakaar kii duusari naatakashala, jo ab europe main banane lagi he, khule maidan kii naatakashala he. isamen rangamanch ke atirikt prekshagrih dhaluaan prithvi par rahata he. is sthaan par log motaron par baithkar rangamuch par khele ja rahe naatak kaa anand doorabeen se lete hai. motaren sheet ushn tapayantra se susajjit hone ke kaaran sukhadai rahati he. ab naatakashala rajyon dwaara banaai jane lagi hai jinamein naatak mandaliyaan apane naatak prastut karti hai. naatashalas saarvajanik banane lagi hai. kahin par inhen nagarpalika ne banwaaya he, kahin par rajya sarkaar ne.
 is prakaar europe kii naatashaalaon ke vikaas kii kahani mistra ke mandiron se prarambh hokar saarvajanik kshetron tak pahunchati he. aaj kaa yug hi saarvajanik udyogon kaa he. janata kaa europe main rajya he. jansadharan ke dhan se naatakashaalaon kaa nirmaan swaabhaavik he. inamen vishesh rup se janata kii suvidhaon kaa dhyaan rakhaa jaataa he. prekshagrih, rangamanch sabhi isi drushtikon se banaae jate hai. shitoshna yantra ke dwaara naatyashaala ke taapmaana par niyantran rahata he. bijli ke dhvaniprasaran yantron dwaara paatro ke cathopakathan rdaakon ko saamaanya rup se sunai dete hai. rdaakon ke liye anya suvidhaayen bhi prastut rahati hai.
