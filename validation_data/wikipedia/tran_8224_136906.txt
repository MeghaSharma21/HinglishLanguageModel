 pantnagar bharat ke uttrakhand rajya ke udham singh nagar janapad main sthit ek nagar he. govind ballab pant krishi evam praudyogiki vishvavidhyalay tathaa pantnagar vimaanashetra yahaan hi sthit hai. uttar pradesh ke pratham mukhyamantri, govind ballab pant ke naam par hi is nagar kaa naam pantnagar rakhaa gaya he.
 himalay kii talahati ke paas sthit yah kshetra 1960 se pehle ghanaa jangal hua karata tha, jiskaa upyog uttar pradesh sarkaar dwaara 1947 ke vibhaajan ke baad pashchimi pakistan se aaye hindu, sikh or anya pravaasiyon ke punarvaas main kiya ja rahaa tha. 1954 main, uttar pradesh sarkaar ne bhartiya krishi anusandhan parishad ke tatkaaleen uparashtrapati, dow ke aar daamale kii adhyakshata main ek bhartiya-america team ko aamantrit kiya taaki nainital janapad ke tarai kshetra main ek gramin vishvavidhyalay ke liye sambhavit sthal ko chunaa jaae. iske baad, bharat sarkaar, takneekee sahyog mishan or kuch america bhoomi anudaan vishvavidhyalayon ke bich ek anubandh par bharat main krishi shiksha ko badhaava dene ke liye hastakshar kiye gaye. 17 november, 1860 ko bharat ke pehle pradhan mantri jawaharlal nehru ne vishvavidhyalay ko samarpit kiya tha. is vishvavidhyalay ke aas paas dhire dhire log basane lage, or kuch varshon ke baad ise pant nagar kaha jane laga.
 pantnagar main sthit pantnagar vimaanashetra sampoorn kumaun kshetra kaa ekmaatr kaaryaatmak hawaii adda he. pantnagar se air india kii dainik udaanen dilli tak uplabdh hai. iske atirikt pantnagar rail tathaa sadak maarg se bhi haldwani-lalkuan tathaa kichha-bareli ityadi nagaron se judaa he.
