garmi evam varsha ke kaaran ghaney van paye jate hai. bade-bade vrikshon ke bich chote vriksh, lataayen tathaa jhaadiyaan paayi jaati hai. vriksh paas-paas ugte hai. vrikshon kii upree tahaniyaan is prakaar fail jaati hai ki surya kaa prakaash bhoomi par nahin pahunch paata or andhkaar chaaya rahata he. varsh bhar varsha hone ke kaaran vriksh kisi khaash samay main apni pattiyaan nahin giraate, atah in vrikshon ko sadabahaar vriksh kaha jaataa he. in vrikshon kii pattiyaan chaudee hoti hai, atah in wanon ko chaudee pattee vaale sadabahaar van kaha jaataa he. in wanon ke pramukh vrikshon main mahogany, rabad, tad, aabnoos, gataparch, bans, cinchona or rojud hai. in wanon main vibhinn prakaar ke bandar, hathi, dariyaan ghoda, chimpanji, gorilla, chita, bhainsa, saanp, ajgar aadi junglee jaanavar paae jate hai. yahan tisitisi naamak makkhi pai jaati he. mendrill naamak vishalkay bandar hote hai. yahan par okapi naamak bhure rang kaa jeev paaya jaataa he jiske pair par safed dhari paayi jaati he. yah ghode kii tarah dikhalaai padataa he, par yah jiraph jati kaa hota he. nadiyon main magar paye jate hai. yahan par chamikale rang kii vibhinn prakar kii chidiyaan paayi jaati hai.bumadhyarekhiy wanon ke donon ore, jahaan varsha kaaphi kam hoti he, ped nahin ug sakate. vahaan mukhyatah lambi-lambi ghaasein ugti hai. in ushn katibandheey ghaas ke maidaanon ko savana kehete hai. bich-bich main kahin-kahin patton se rahit kuch ped bhi paae jate hai. yah pradesh vibhinn prakaar ke ghaas khaane vaale pashuon kaa ghar he. in pashuon main hiran, barasingha, zebra, jirraf tathaa hathi mukhya hai. jirraf vishwa kaa sabase unchaa jaanavar mana jaataa he. kuch jantushastriyon kaa maananaa he ki jirraf kabhi sote nahin hai. yahan kuch hinsak pashu bhi rahete hai jo in ghaas khaane vaale pashuon kaa shikaar karte hai. inamen sher, chita evam gidad mukhya hai. dakshini africa ke sher bahut lambe hote hai. chita vishwa main sabase tej dodane vaala jaanavar he. savana ghaas ke maidan ke donon ore jahaan kii jalwaayu atyant garm va shushk he, vahaan ushn marusthaleey vanaspati pai jaati he. yahan keval cantili jhaadiyaan hin ugti hai. marudyanon main khajur ke ped paae jate hai. yahan kaa mukhya pashu oont he jise marusthal kaa jahaaj kehete hai. yahan bade aakaar tathaa ghode ke samaan tej daudane vaale shuturmurg naamak pakshi paae jate hai. africa ke uttari evam dakshini-pashchimi tateey kshetron par keval jaade main hi varsha hoti he phir bhi yahan chaudee pattee vaale sadabahaar vriksh paae jate hai. kyonki yahan ke vriksh apane aapako vaataavaran ke anusaar badal lete hai. in vrikshon kii jade lambi, pattiyaan choti tathaa chhaalen moti hoti hai. in visheshtaon ke kaaran ye vriksh garmiyon main bhi hare-bhare rahete hai. yahan jaitun, cark, larel evam ok ke vriksh paae jate hai. yahan kii jalwaayu falon kii kheti ke liye kaaphi upyukt he. yahan neembu, santaraa, angoor, seve, anjir aadi ras vaale falon kii kheti kii jaati he. yahan junglee pashuon kaa abhaav he. praayah paaltu pashu hi paae jate hai. africa ke dakshini-purvi bhaagon main bhi varsha kii kami ke kaaran shushk ghaas ke maidan paae jate hai. yahan par ugane vaali ghaasein choti-choti mulaayam tathaa guchchhedaar hoti hai. in ghaas ke maidaanon ko veld kehete hai. ghaas ke in maidaanon main vrikshon kaa ekadam abhaav hota he. is bhaag main pashuchaaran kaa kaary kiya jaataa he. africa ke dakshini evam purvi bhaagon main, jahaan adhik oonchaai ke kaaran himpat hota he, nukili pattee vaale sadabahaar van paae jate hai. madagascar dweep par bobaab naamak ek vicitra vriksh paaya jaataa hajo jameen ke neeche se nami khinchakar apane andar jal kaa sanchay karata he.
