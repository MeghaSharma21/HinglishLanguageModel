 
 samudri makadi, jo jeevvaigyanik vargeekaran main pichnogonida naamak varg he, samudr main rahane vaale aarthropod praaniyon kaa ek samooh he. isaki lagbhag 1300 jeevvaigyanik jatiyaan gyaat hai. yah saarvatrik rup se lagbhag har sagar va mahaasaagar main milte hai haalanki inhen vishesh rup se baari maatra main bumadhya sagar, caribbian sagar, atlantic mahaasaagar or arctic mahaasaagar main milte hai. inkaa aakaar vividh he or isamen 1 millimetre se lekar gahare pani main basane vaali 90 centimetre se bhi badi jatiyaan milti hai. adhiktar jatiyon kaa aakaar chota hota he or ve kam gaharaai vaale pani main rahati hai. apane naam ke baavjood yah vaastavik makadi se bhinn he or na hi yah aracnid kii shrenee main aati he, haalanki kelicerata sangh hone ke kaaran yah anya aarthropodon kii tulana main makadiyon se adhik qareebi sambandh rakhti hai.
