 vrindavanlaal verma hindi naatakkaar tathaa upanyaaskaar the. hindi upanyaas ke vikaas main unka yogadan mahatvapurn he. unhone ek taraf premchand kii saamaajik parampara ko aage badhaayaa he to duusari taraf hindi main aitihaasik upanyaas kii dhara ko utkarsh tak pahunchaaya he. itihaas, kala, puraatatv, manovigyaan, murtikla or chitrakala main bhi inaki vishesh ruchi rahi. 'apni kahani' main aapane apane sanghariumay jivan kii gaatha kahi he. 
 vrundavanlaal verma kaa janm 9 janvari 1889 i0 ko uttar pradesh ke jhansi jile ke mauranipur ke theth ruudhivaadee kysth parivaar main hua tha. pita kaa naam ayodhya prasad tha. praarambhik shiksha bhinn-bhinn sthanon par hui. bi.e. paas karne ke baad inhone kanoon kii pariksha paas kii or jhansi main vakaalat karne lage. 1909 i0 main 'senapati oodal' naamak naatak chapa jise tatkaaleen sarkaar ne jabt kar liya. 1920 i0 tak choti choti kahaniyaan likhte rahe. 1927 i0 main 'garh kundar' do maheene main likhaa. 1930 i0 main 'virata kii padmini' likhaa. victoria kalege gwalior se snaatak tak kii padhai karne ke liye ye agra aaye or agra kalege se kanoon kii padhai puri karne ke baad bundelkhand main vakaalat karne lage. inhe bachapan se hi bundelkhand kii aitihaasik viraasat main ruchi thi. jab ye unniis saal ke kishor the to inhone apni pehali rachana ‘mahatma buddha kaa jivan charit’ likh daali thi. unke likhe naatak ‘senapati oodal’ main abhivyakt vidrohi tewaron ko dekhte huye tatkaaleen angraji sarkaar ne isi pratibandhit kar diya tha. 
 ye prem ko jivan kaa sabase aavashyak ang maanane ke saath junun kii seemaa tak saamaajik kaary karne vaale sadhak bhi the. inhone vakaalat vyavsaay ke madhyam se kamaayi samast puunji samaaj ke kamjor varg ke nagarikon ko purnvasit karne ke kaary main laga di. 
 inhone mukhya rup se aitihaasik prishthbhumi par aadhaarit upanyaas, naatak, lekh aadi gadhy rachanaayen likhi hai saath hi kuch niabandh evam ladhukthayen bhi likhi hai. 
 vrundavanlaal verma aitihaasik kahanikar evam lekhak ke rup main prasiddh hai. bundelkhand kaa madhyakaalin itihaas inke katha kaa mukhya aadhaar he. ghatana kii vichitrataa, prakruti-chithran or maanav-prakruti ke ye safal chitere the. puranic tathaa aitihaasik kathaon ke prati bachapan se hi inaki ruchi thi. apni saahitiyik sevaaon ke liye vrundavanlaal verma ko agra vishvavidhyalay dwaara di.lit. kii oopaadhi se sammanit kiya gaya. 
 
 inke utkrisht saahitiyik kaary ke liye hindi sahitya sammelan tathaa agra vishvavidhyalay ne inhe kramshah ‘sahitya vachaspati’ tathaa ‘maanad doctrate’ kii oopaadhi se vibhooshit kiya. bharat sarkaar dwaara unhen padmabhushan se alankrita kiya gaya. bharat sarkaar ne inke upanyaas ‘jhaansi kii rani’ ko puruskrit bhi kiya he.
 antarrashtriya jagat main inke lekhan kaary ko saraahaa gaya he jiske liye inhe ‘soviet land nehru puraskaar’ praapt hua he. san 1969 main inhone bhautik sansar se vidaa avashya le lee parantu apni rachanaaon ke madhyam se prem ore itihaas ko purnsrujit karne vaale is yaksha sadhak ko hindi pathak rah rah kar yaad karte rahete hai tabhi to inaki mrityu ke 28 varsho ke baad inke sammaan main bharat sarkaar ne dinaank 9 janvari 1997 ko ek dak tikat jari kiya. 
 inke dwaara likhit saamaajik upanyaas ‘sangam’ or ‘lagaan’ par aadhaarit hindi filmein bhi bani he jo anya kai bhashaaon main anudit huyi hai.
