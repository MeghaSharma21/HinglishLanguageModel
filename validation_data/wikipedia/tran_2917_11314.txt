 suresh salil hindi ke samarth kavi, aalochak or saahitiyik itihaas ke gahan adhyeta hai. ganesh shankar vidhyaarthee ke krititv or vyaktitv par anek prakashnon ke atirikt ek kaviti sangrah khule main khadey hokar prakashit hua he. bacchon va kishoron ke liye bhi inhone kai kitaaben prakashit kii hai.
