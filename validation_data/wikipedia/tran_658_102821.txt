  andher nagari prasiddh hindi sahityakar bhaaratendu harishchandra kaa sarvaadhik lokapriya naatak he. 6ankon ke is naatak main vivekahin or nirankush shashan vyavastha par karaara vyangy karte hue use apane hi karmon dwaara nasht hote dikhaaya gaya he. bhaaratendu ne isaki rachana banaras ke hindu neshnal theatre ke liye ek hin din kii thi
 .
 yah naatak 6 ankon main vibhakt he. isamen ank ke bajaay drishy shabd kaa prayog kiya gaya he. pehle drishy main mahant apane do chelon ke saath dikhaai padate hai jo apane shishyon govardhan das or narayan das ko paas ke shahar main bhiksha maangane bhejte hai. ve govardhan das ko lobh ke bure parinaam ke prati sachet karte hai. duusare drishy main shahar ke bazaar kaa drishy he jahaan sabakuch take ser bik rahaa he. govardhan das bazaar kii yah kaphaiyat dekhakar anandit hota he or saat paise main dhai ser mithai lekar apane guru ke paas laut jaataa he.tisare drishy main mahant ke paas donon shishy lautate hai. narayan das kuch nahin laata he jabaki gobardhan das dhai ser mithai lekar aata he. mahant shahar main guni or avaguni ko ek hi bhav milane kii khabar sunakar sachet ho jate hai or apane shishyon ko turant hi shahar chhodane ko kehete hai. ve kehete hai-"set set sab ek se, jahaan kapur kapaas.aise desh kudes main, kabahoon na kijai baas.."narayan das unki baat maan letaa he jabaki govardhan das saste swaadisht bhojan ke laalach main vahiin rah jane kaa faisla karata he.chauthe drishy main andher nagari ke chaupat raja ke darbar or nyaay kaa chithran he. sharaab main duba raja fariyadi ke bakrii dabane kii shikaayat par baniyaa se shuru hokar karigar, chuunevaale, bhishti, kasaai or gadriya se hote hue kotwaal tak ja pahunchataa he or use faansee kii sajaa suna detaa he. paanchaven drishy main mithai khate or prasann hote mote ho gaye govardhan das ko chaar sipaahi pakadakar faansee dene ke liye le jate hai. ve use bataate hai ki bakrii mari isliye nyaay kii khatir kisi ko to phaansi par jarur chadhaaya janaa chahie. jab duble kotwaal ke gale se faansee kaa fandaa bada niklaa to raja ne kisi mote ko phaansi dene kaa hukum de diya.chathe drishy main shamshaan main govardhan das ko phaansi dene kii taiyaari puri ho gayi he. tabhi uske guru mahant ji aakar uske kaan main kuch mantra dete hai. iske baad guru shishy donon phaansi par chadhane kii utaavali dikhaate hai. raja yah sunakar ki is shubh saiyat main phaansi chadhane vaala sidhaa baikunth jayega swayan ko hi phaansi par chadhaane kii ajna detaa he. is tarah anyaayi or murkh raja swatah hi nasht ho jaataa he.
