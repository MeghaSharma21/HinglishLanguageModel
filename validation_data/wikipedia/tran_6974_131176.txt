 yamala pagalaa divaana 2 sangeet sivaan dwaara nirdeshit or 2011 main pradarshit film yamala pagalaa divaana kii uttar-kritti he. yah 2013 main pradarshit bollywood kii ection film he. film main dharmendra, sani deol or bobi deol ne abhinay kiya he. yah film sani sounds privet limited ke binar tale bani he.
 film main dharam ke do putr hai- paramveer or gajodhar . jyeshth putr paramveer landan main acchhee zindagi guzar basar kar rahaa he, vahiin kanisht putr gajodhar or dharam varanasi main thagi kaa dhandha karte hai. wo united kingdom se aae sar yograj khanna se milte hai, unhen lagata he ki wo ek karodpati aadmi hai. dharam, yograj ke qareeb jane ke liye ek tarkeeb bhidata he or gajodhar ko uski beti se nazadiiki badhaane ko kahata he. gajodhar apana naam badalakar prem rakh letaa he or yograj kii beti suman se romance karata he. isi saazish ko anjaam dene ke liye dharam or gajodhar landan bhi pahunch jate hai. jahaan wo paramveer se milte hai. tab paramveer ko lagata he ki uskaa bhai or pita zaraa bhi nahin badale hai or ab bhi logon ko thagte rahete hai. jaldi hi dharam or gajodhar ko jhatka lagata he jab khanna unhen bataata he ki suman unki god lee hui beti he or uski asal beti reet he. ab dharam, gajodhar ko reet ke qareeb jane ko kahata he lekin reet ko paramveer pasand aa jaataa he. is tarah film kii kahani aage babbti he.
