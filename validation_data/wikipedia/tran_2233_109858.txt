 palamira ek pracheen samiti nagar he jo ki vartmaan main homes praant, syria main sthit he. puratathvik surveykshanon main yah nagar navpashan kaal kaa bataaya gaya he or nagar kaa pehla dastavej dwitiya sahasrabdi ke purvarddh main miltaa he. roman samrajya se purv palamira nagar bahut se samrajyon ke utthan-patan kaa sakshi rahaa he.
 palamira shahar kii khubsuurati dekhakar ise registan kaa venice kaha gaya. yani jaise pani ke bichobich based venice kii khubsuurati kii koi misaal nahin usee tarah syriake palamira shahar jaisaa bhi dharti par koi doosraa shahar nahin. syria kaa palamira shahar aaj se lagbhag do hajaar saal pehle basaaya gaya tha. do hajaar saal pehle palamira shahar kii khubsuurati ko jis tarah se nikharaa gaya wo apane aap main hairaan kar dene vaali baat he. ye wo shahar he jisaki imaaraton par greek, roman or persian sabhyataa kaa prabhaav saaf dikhataa he. isi kaaran unesco ne palamira ko vishwa dharohar kaa darja de rakhaa he. ye shahar kabhi roman samrajya jitna shaktisaali tha, jahaan samskruti or vyapaar kaa anokhaa sangam hota tha. vishwa bhar ke vyapaaree apana saamaan lekar palamira pahunchate the. yani jis kaal main vishwa kii kai sari sabhyataaen abhi viksit bhi nahin ho pai thim, us vakt palamira ke jariye syria ke vyapaaree puure vyapaar main apana vyapaar failaa chuke the. hajaaron saal pehle yahaan banaaya gaya ye roman theatre or stadium is baat kii tasdik karte hai ki itihaas main palamir shahar kaa muqabla karne vaala koi doosraa shahar nahin tha.
 aantki sanghathan iesas ne may 2015 main is shahar par kabja kar liya tha usne is shahar ke aitihaasik sthalo ko nasht kar diya tha jiske baad syrian sena ne palamira ko swatantra karaayaa. aies ne palamira ke aitihaasik sthalo kaa istemaal logo ko maut kii sajaa dene ke liye kiya tha.
