 mohan dayaram bhavanani kaa janm 1903 main avibhajit bharat ke sindh praant ke hyderabad nagar main hua tha. vibhaajan ke baad ve bharat aa gaye the. ve hindi filmon ke nirmaataa, nirdeshak or lekhak ke rup main jane jate hai. unhone 1925 main apni pehali film veerbala bataur nirdeshak banaai. isamen pramukh bhoomika un dinon kii navdith abhinetri sulochana ne nibhaai thi. sulochana kii bhi yah pehali film thi. 1934 main unhone premchand jaise prasiddh hindi lekhak ke saath majdoor naamak film banaai. biswein dashak se pachaasawein dashak tak vistrut kaarykaal main lagbhag 25 filmon ke nirmaataa mohan bhavanani kii antim film 1957 main bani himalayan tepastry thi. yah film angrezi main bani ek vruttchitr thi. 30 disambar 1962 ko mumbai main unka dehanth ho gaya.
