 assam ke lakhimpur jile main honevaali is hatya ke maamle ko pehle ek balaatkaar kaa maamla samajha gaya tha. is maamle main ek 31 varshiya mahila sangeeta devi jo ek vaivaahit nari thi or apni ladki ko skool se laane ke liye auto-rickshaw main pravesh kar chuki thi, aparaadh kaa shikaar bani. vaahan main savaar chaalak or uske saathi ne maukey kaa galat faaydaa uthaakar use maar petakar chalate hue auto-rickshaw se baahar phenkaa tha. zamin par girte hi mahila kaa swargvaas ho gaya tha.
 is ghatana ke virodh main akhil assam vidhyaarthee sangh or mahila sanghathanon ne hazaaron kii sankhya main vyapak pradarshan kiye the.
 bitoni sharma, jo us mahila kaa pati or sthaneey bhartiya janata party ke yuva morcha ke netaa the, ne balatkaar kii baat ko bilkul hi khaarij kar diya. police ke anusaar peedit mahila sahaara chit fnd se judi hui thi. tahakikat ke anusaar sangeeta devi ke avaidh sambandh raju chetia naamak vyakti ke saath the or yahi uski hatya kaa kaaran bana.
