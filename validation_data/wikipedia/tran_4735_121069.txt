 farakka baandh bharat ke pashchim bengal praant main sthit ganga nadi par bana ek baandh he. yah baandh bangladesh kii seemaa se maatr 10 kilo meter kii duuri par sthit he. is baandh ko 1974-75 main hindustan construction company ne banaaya tha. is baandh kaa nirmaan kolkata bandargah ko gaad se mukt karaane ke liye kiya gaya tha jo kii 1950 se 1960 tak is bandargah kii pramukh samasya thi. kolkata hughali nadi par sthit ek pramukh bandargah he. greeshm ritu main hughali nadi ke bahaav ko nirantar banaaye rakhane ke liye ganga nadi kii ke pani ke ek bade hisse ko farakka baandh ke dwaara hughali nadi main mode diya jaataa he. is pani ke vitaran ke kaaran bangladesh evam bharat main lanbaa vivaad chalaa. ganga nadi ke pravaah kii kami ke kaaran bangladesh jane vaale pani kii lavanta bad jaati thi or machhli paalan, peyajal, swasth or naukayan prabhavit ho jaataa tha. mitti main nami kii kami ke chalate bangladesh ke ek bade kshetra kii bhumi banjar ho gayi thi. is vivaad ko sulajhane ke liye dono sarkaro ne aapas main samjhauta karte hue farakka jal sandhi kii ruparekha rakhi.
 tehri band tehri vikaas pariyojna kaa ek praathmik baandh he jo kii uttarakhand praant ke tehri main sthit he. yah baandh ganga nadi kii pramukh saahyogi nadi bhagirathi par banaaya gaya he. tehari baandh kii uchaai 261 meter he jo ise vishwa kaa paanchava sabase ooncha baandh banaati he. is baandh se 2400 mega wat vidyut utpaadan, 270,000 hectares kshetra kii sinchaai or pratidin 102.20 karod litre peyajal dilli, uttar pradesh evam uttaranchal ko uplabdh karana prastaavit he.
 bhimgoda baandh haridwar main sthit he. is baandh ko san 1840 main angrejo ne ganga nadi ke pani ko vibhajit kar upari ganga nahar main modane ke liye banwaaya tha. yah baandh ganga ke jal maarg ke liye bahut hi ghaatak siddh hua. baandh ke nirmaan ke purv east india company ke jal pot tehri jaise uchai par sthit shaharon tak aa sakate the.
