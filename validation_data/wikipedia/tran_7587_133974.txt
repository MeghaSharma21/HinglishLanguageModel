 aqsu vibhaag china ke shinjiyang praant kaa ek prashasnic vibhaag he. is vibhaag kaa kul kshetrafal 1,32,500 varg kimi he. san 2003 kii janaganana main is vibhaag kii aabaadi 21.9 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. aqsu vibhaag kii rajdhani aqsu shahar he.
 'aqsu' shabd kaa arth 'safed pani' he. sthaneey uigur bhashaa or us se milti turki bhashaaon main main 'aq' kaa matlab 'safed' hota he or 'su' kaa arth 'pani' hota he. 'aqsu' main ] par zaraa dhyaan den - yah 'k' se miltaa-julata lekin thodaa sa bhinn he.
 aqsu vibhaag tarim droni ke pashchimottar bhaag main tiyaan shaan parvaton kii dakshini dhalaanon par sthit he. is vibhaag kaa dakshini hissaa taklamkaan registan main aata he or yahan par krishi unhi ilaaqon main sambhav he jahaan se tarim nadi or usamen milane vaali aqsu nadi or muzaat nadi milti hai.
 2003 main aqsu vibhaag main rahane vaale 21.9 lakh logon main se 71.93% uigur jati ke, 26.62% haan chini jati ke or baaqi anya jatiyon ke the.
