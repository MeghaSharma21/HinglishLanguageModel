 field marshall archibald percial vevell, pehle arl vavel, jisibi, jisiesai, jisiaii, siemji, emasi, keestije, pisi, british sena ke ek varisht adhikari or bharat ke visarai the. unhone duusare boar yuddh, bazaar ghati abhiyaan or mahaan yuddh main seva di thi, jiske dauraan ve yaperes kii duusari ladaai main ghayal ho gaye. unhone dwitiya vishwa yuddh main, shuruyaat main commander-in-chif middle east ke rup main bhi seva di, jis bhoomika main unhone british senaaon ko disambar 1940 ke oparation compass ke dauraan pashchimi misr or purvi libya main italians par jeet hasil kii, keval april 1941 main german pashchimi registan main sena dwaara parajeet hue. unhone 1941 se june 1943 tak bharat ke commander-in-chif ke rup main kaary kiya or phir farvari 1947 main sevanivritti tak bharat ke visarai ke rup main kaary kiya.
