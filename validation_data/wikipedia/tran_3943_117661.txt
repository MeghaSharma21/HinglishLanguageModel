 mall pracheen bharat ke 16 mahajanapadon main se ek tha. iskaa ullekh angutar nikaay main aayaa he. 'mall' naam 'mall rajvansh' ke naam par he jo is mahajanapad kii us samay shaasha thi. 
 mallon kii do shakhaayen thim. ek kii rajdhani kushinaara thi jo vartmaan kushinagar he tathaa duusare kii rajdhani paava thi jo vartmaan fajilnagar he.
