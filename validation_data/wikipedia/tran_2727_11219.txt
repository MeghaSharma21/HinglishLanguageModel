 kii shrenee kaa hissaa
 buddha dharm main, bodhisattva satva ke liye prabud ko kehete hai. parmparik rup se mahaan dayaa se prerit, bodhichitt janit, sabhi samvedansheel praaniyon ke laabh ke liye sahaj ichha se buddhatv praapt karne vaale ko bodhisattva mana jaataa he. tibeti buddha dharm ke anusaar, bodhisattva maanav dwaara jivan main praapt karne yogya chaar utkrishth avasthaaon main se ek he.
 bodhisattva shabd kaa upyog samay ke saath viksit hua. pracheen bhartiya buddha dharm ke anusaar, udhaaharan ke liye gautam buddha ke purv jivan ko vishisht rup se pradarshit karne ke liye kaam main liya jaataa tha.
 das parmitaaon kaa purna paalan karne vaala bodhisattva kehalaata he. bodhisattva jab das balon yaa bhoomiyon ko praapt kar lete hai tab " gautam buddha " kehalaate hai, buddha bananaa hi bodhisattva ke jivan kii paraakaashtha he. is pahchaan ko bodhi naam diya gaya he. kaha jaataa he ki buddha shakyamuni keval ek buddha hai - unke pehle bahut saare the or bhavishya main or honge. unka kahana tha ki koi bhi buddha ban saktaa he agar vah das parmitaaon kaa purna paalan karte hue bodhisattva praapt kare or bodhisattva ke baad das balon yaa bhoomiyon ko praapt kare. buddha dharm kaa antim lakshya he sampoorn maanav samaaj se dukh kaa ant. "main keval ek hi padaarth sikhaata hoon - dukh he, dukh kaa kaaran he, dukh kaa nirodh he, or dukh ke nirodh kaa maarg he" . buddha dharm ke anuyaayi ashtangik maarg par chalakar na ke anusaar jikar agyanta or dukh se mukti or nirvana paane kii koshish karte hai.
 asia ke kuch buddha rashtron main neeche ati sammanneey kuch bodhisattva he. buddha rashtron main chaar bodhisattva sitaron kii tarah chamakate he.
 1) bodhisattva kwan om : ye buddha kii dayaa darshaati he or unke haath main jalpaatr he.
 2) bodhisattva padmapani : ye buddha kii karunaa darshaate hai or unke haath main camel kaa phul he.
 3) bodhisattva vajrapani : ye buddha kii shakti darshaate hai or unke haath main vajra he.
