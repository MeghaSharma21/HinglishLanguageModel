 chatagaanv yaa chatgaanv, bangladesh kaa ek pramukh bandargah or doosraa sabase bada shahar he. chatgaanv ek gahare pani kaa prakritik bandargah he. bengal kii khadi ke purvi tat or karmphuli nadi ke muhane par sthit yah shahar, desh ke dakshini vibhaag main padataa he. 2011 main isaki anumaanit jansankhya 65 lakh se adhik thi. dakshin asia main yah vaanijya, udyog or jahajrani kaa ek pramukh centre he. yah duniya ke sabase teji se viksit hote mahanagaron main se bhi ek he.
 sadiyon se chatagaanv ke is pracheen prakritik bandargah ne bengal or bengal kii khadi ke pramukh vyapaarik kendron ke liye ek pravesh dwaar kaa kaary kiya he. is bandargah ne madhya purv, dakshin purv asia, europe or china ke vyapaariyon ko aakarshith kiya he. arab anveshak ebn-batuta, venice ke vyapaaree nicolo de' konti or chini admiral jheng hi samet kai aitihaasik yatri is bandargah se hokar gujarey hai. 16 veen sadi ke portugali samrajya main ise porto grandey di bengala ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. british east india company ne bengal ke antim swatantra nawab ko haraane ke baad 1760 main bandargah kaa niyantran praapt kiya. 19 veen sadi main british raaj ke tahat, aadhunik chatagaanv bandargah ke vikaas ke liye assam-bengal railway kaa nirmaan kiya gaya. dwitiya vishwa yuddh main burma abhiyaan ke dauraan yah mitra deshon kii sena ke liye ek mahatvapurn aadhaar bana. 1947 main british bharat ke vibhaajan ke baad, chatagaanv purvi pakistan kaa hissaa ban gaya. 1971 main bangladesh mukti yuddh kii shuruyaat main bangladesh kii swatantraaa kii ghoshnaa chatagaanv se kii gayi.
