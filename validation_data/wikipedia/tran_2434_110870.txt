 spain or france kii seemaa par pirini parvaton kaa naqsha
 pirini parvat shrunkhlaa dkshinpashchimi europe main spain or france kii seemaa par sthit ek parvatamala he. yah iberia prayadweep ko europe ke mahadweep ke anya bhaagon se alag karti he. pirini pahaado kii panktiyaan purvottar main biske khadi se lekar dakshinpurv main bumadhya sagar tak 491 kimi chalati hai. andora kaa nanha sa desh inhi parvaton main sthit he.
 madhya pirineeyon main
 aaneto parvat, pirineeyon kaa sabase unchaa shikhar
 spain main tena ghati
 aragon main jalprapaat
