 kris sanders
 wiliam davis dean deblois 
 haau tu train yor dragon 2010 main bani computer-anemated 3di film he jiskaa nirmaan dreamworks anemeshan dwaara kiya gaya he. yah 2003 main chhapi isi naam kii pustak par aadhaarit he. film kaa nirdesan kris sanders or dean deblois dwaara kiya gaya he. isamen je baruchel, jerard butler, craig fergyusan, america farera, jonah hill, tii.je miller, christin vig or christopher minutes-plasi kii awaz shaamil he.
 film kii kahani mruthak viking vishwa main shuru hoti he jahaan ek nau jawan viking hicap ek paaramparik dragon shikari bananaa chahta he. aakhir main ek dragon ko pakadaney ke baad vah use maarane kii jagah usase dosti kar letaa he.
 film ko 26 march 2010 ko sakaaratmak samiksha ke saath release kiya gaya tha. film ne vishvabhar $500 million kaa vyavsaay kiya or yah dreamworks kii america va canada main paanchavi sarvaadhik safal film ban gai . ise 83ve academy puraskaar samaaroh main sarvshereshth anemated film, sarvshereshth dhwani ke liye namakan praapt hua tha.
 2014 main, ek agali kadi jari kii gai he. haau tu train yor dragon 2.
