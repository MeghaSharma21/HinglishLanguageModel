 arjuna rampal angrezi: Arjun Rampal,, ek bhartiya abhinayta hai, jo bollywood kii filmon main dikhaai dete hai, saath hi ve ek model hai.
 unki pehali film - moksh 2001 main pradarshit hui, jise ashok mehta ne nirdeshit kiya gaya tha. baharhaal, yah film unki duusari film pyaar, ishq or mohabbat ke baad jari kii gai thi, jisamen unhone sunil shetty or aafatab shivadaasaani ke saath kaam kiya tha. haalanki donon filmon ne box ofis par acha pradarshan nahin dikhaaya, par aalochakon ne donon hi filmon main unke abhinay kii saraahna kii. 2002 main unhone antarrashtriya bhartiya film academy se fes of da year puraskaar jeeta.
 apni pehali film ke baad se hi, unhone lagaataar ankhen, dil he tumhaara, yaqiin tathaa ek ajanabi jaisi filmon main kaam kiya. adhikansh filmon main ve sahaayak bhoomikaaon main nazar aae or ye vyavsaayik taur par safal nahin raheen.
 unhone shahrukh khan, rani mukherjee, saif ali khan, priti zinta or priyanka chopra jaise sitaron ke saath mil kar temptesan 2004 main bhaag liya.
 2006 main unhone bahu kalakaaron vaali film kabhi alavida naa kahana main atithi bhoomika nibhaai or amitabh bachchan abhinit 1978 kii film don ke rimek don - da chess bigins agen main ek sahaayak bhoomika nibhaai. yahaan rampal, mul sanskaran main praan dwaara abhinit jasjit kii bhoomika main nazar aae.
 iske baad unhone film ai si you ke nirmaan or usamen abhinay kaa nirnay liya, jo 29 disambar 2006 ko pradarshit hui. arjuna ne apane swayan kii nirmaan company chessing ganesha films ke zarie iskaa nirmaan kiya. unki patni meher jesia sah-nirmaataa thim. film main rampal, vipasha agarwal, sonaali kulkarni or boman irani ne abhinay kiya. bollywood abhinayta shahrukh khan or rithik roshan ne is film main choti bhoomikaayen nibhaain.ai si you ko puri tarah landan main filmaaya gaya. film ko acchhee samikshaayen nahin milin or yah box offis par vifal rahi. lekin unhone faraah khan ke om shanti om film main khalanaayak kii bhoomika nibhaane ke liye sakaaratmak samikshaayen praapt kin, jo bollywood ke itihaas main sabase badi hit filmon main se ek thi.
 2007 main arjuna ne rituparano ghosh kii angrezi-bhashaa kii kalatmak film main amitabh bachchan or priti zinta ke saath da last lier main abhinay kiya. yah film, toronto antarrashtriya film samaaroh main pehali baar pradarshit hui or anya kai film samarohon main bhi iskaa upyukt swaagat hua. bharat ke theatron main uske release hone par rampal ne apane abhinay ke liye anukool samikshaayen praapt kin. 2008 main rampal ne rock on!! main abhinay kiya, jisamen unhone band ke pradhan gitarist kii bhoomika nibhaai, jo vyapak rup se saraahi gai or unhone filmafere sarvshereshth sahaayak abhinayta puraskaar sahit kai puraskaar jite. baharhaal, arjuna kaa pradarshan saraahneey rahaa tha.
 arjuna rampal ne purv miss india or super model meher jesia se shaadi kii or unki do betiyaan, mahika, 17 janvari 2002 ko or mira june 2005 main paidaa huin.
