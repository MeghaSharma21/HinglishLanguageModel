 varsh 2013-2014 kaa aam bajet vitt mantri pi. chidambaram dwaara 28 farvari 2013 ko prastut kiya gaya tha.
 oktober main desh kaa pehla mahila bank khulegaa. is bank kaa sanchaalan puri tarah se mahilaayein hi karengi. dalit ladakiyon ko chaatravrutti jari kii jaaegi. chidambaram ne kaha ki sarkaar mahila suraksha ke liye patibaddh he. mahilaon ke liye nirbhaya fund sharu hoga jisamen 1,000 karod aavantit karne kaa prastaav he. mahilaon ke vikaas ke liye kul 97,000 karod aavantit kiye gaye hai.
 gramin awasaiya pariyojna ke liye 600 karod kaa praavdhaan kiya gaya he. gramin bajet main 45 fisadi kaa izapha kiya gaya he. 50,000 karod ke infrastracchar bond jari honge. infrastracchar main 47 fisadi vikaas nijee kshetra dwaara kiya jaayegaa. bajet 2013-14 main khaadya suraksha ke liye 10,000 karod or nivesh bhatte ke liye 100 karod kaa prastaav kiya gaya he.
 kelkar samiti kii siforeeshon ko anshat: amal kiya gaya he. agle saal vittiya ghate kaa lakshya 3.3 fisadi or rajkoshiya ghaataa 4.8 fisadi rakhaa gaya he.
