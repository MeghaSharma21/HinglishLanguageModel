 zeme bhashaayen purvottar bharat ke manipur rajya ke pashchimottaree bhaag main naga samudaay kii ek shakha dwaara boli jane vaali bhashaaon kaa ek samooh he. yah tibeti-burmi bhashaa parivaar kii ek shakha he lekin us vishaal bhashaa-parivaar ke antargat iskaa aage kaa shreneekaran abhi agyaat he. in bhashaaon kaa anya naga bhashaaon ke saath kaa bhi sambandh agyaat he.
