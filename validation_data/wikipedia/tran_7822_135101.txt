 james jozaf "gym" parcens ek america televijan va film abhinayta he. ve sibies ke dharawahik da big baing thiari main sheldon kupar ke paatr ke liye mashuhar he or unke abhinay ko kaarykram kii safalataa kaa mahatvapurn ang mana jaataa he. unhen apane abhinay ke liye kai puraskaron se sammanit kiya gaya he jiname televijan critics association puraskaar, naishanal association of brodcasters televigence chairarmens puraskaar, ek hasya shrunkhlaa main mukhya abhinayta ke rup main behtareen abhinay ke liye do lagaataar emi puraskaar va sarvshereshth abhinayta ek hasya televijan shrunkhlaa main kaa golden globe puraskaar shaamil he.
 parcens kaa janm hoston. texas main do bacchon main bade bete ke rup main hua tha. chah varsh kii umr main vidhyalay ke da elifunts child naatak main cola-cola pakshi kaa paatr nibhaane ke baad parcens ne nirnay liye kii ve ek abhinayta banenge. yuva parcens thrij company, family ties or da kosbi sho jaise dharawahikon se kaafii prabhavit hue. unhone clen ok hai skul, spring, texas main padhai kii. 
 college se upadhi uteern karne ke baad parcens hoston vishvavidhyalay main daakhil hue. vahaan unhone 3 varsh ke samay main 17 naatakon main abhinay kiya. ve infrnal bridagroom productions kii sthapana karne waalo main se ek the or ve stages repartori theatre main bhi hameshaa hissaa lete rahe. parcens ne san diyaago vishvavidhyalay main 1999 main daakhila liya. be un saat kshatron main se ek the jinhen khaas do-varshiya paaramparik theatre ke course ke liye chunaa gaya jo old globe theatre ke saath sikhaaya jaataa he. 2001 main upadhi grahan karne ke baad ve new york chale aae.
