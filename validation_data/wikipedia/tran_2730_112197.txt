 purvanchal uttar-madhya bharat kaa ek bhugolic kshetra he jo uttar pradesh ke purvi chor par sthit he. yah uttar main nepal, purv main bihar, dakshin me madhya pradesh ke baghelkhand kshetra or pashchim me uttar pradesh ke avadh kshetra dwaara ghira he. ise ek alag rajya banane ke liye lambey samay raajnneetik maang uthati rahi he. vartmaan main is kshetra kaa pratinidhitiv uttar pradesh vidhansabha main 117 vidhayakon dwaara hota he to vahiin is kshetra se 23 loksabhaa sadsy chune jate hai.
 purvanchal ke mukhyatah teen bhaag hai- pashchim main purvi avadhi kshetra, purv main pashchimi-bhojpuri kshetra or uttar main nepal kshetra. yah bhartiya-ganga maidan par sthit he or pashchimi bihar ke saath yah duniya main sabase adhik ghani aabaadi vaala kshetra he. uttar pradesh ke aasapaas ke jilon kii tulana main mitti kii samrudh gunvattta or ucch kentua ghanatv ke kaaran krishi ke liye anukool he. bhojpuri kshetra main pramukh bhashaa yaa boli he. haalaanki is kshetra main hindi or bhojpuri ke alaava avadhi tathaa baghelkhandi pashchimi or dakshini kshetron main boli jaati hai..
 1991 main uttar pradesh kii sarkaar ne purvanchal vikaas nidhi kii sthapana kii jiskaa uddeshy tha kshetreey vikaas pariyojnaon ke liye dhan ikattha karnaa jisase bhavishya main santulit vikaas ke jariye sthaneey jaruraton ko puraa karte hue kshetreey asamantaaon kaa nivaaran ho sake. 
 purvanchal uttar pradesh ke sabase pichde kshetron me se ek ke he. isaki vajah jati aadhaarit raajnneeti tathaa ek vishaal jansankhya he. purvanchal ke pramukh muddon me buniyadi suvidhaon kii kami, uchit gramin shiksha or rojgaar kaa abhaav, kanoon vyavastha ko chihnit kiya gaya he. purvanchal saanskrithik rup se samrudh kshetra he. 
 1857 ke pratham swadhinata sangram ke nayak mangal pandey isi kshetra ke baliya ke mul nivasee the. 
 sant ravidaas nagar, bhadohi or mirzapur asia main kaaleen nirmaan main pramukh khilaadi rahe hai. elahabaad iskaa pramukh or bada shahar he. jila varanasi bhartiya paryaton or vishesh rup se sadi ke nirmaan kaa centre he. sonbhadra purvanchal kaa ek jila, 7000MW bijli kaa utpaadan karata he, jo uttar pradesh ke rajya main kul bijli utpaadan ke lagbhag aadha he or bharat k sabase bada or keval chuunaa pathar kii pramukh khadaan he. varanasi or kushinagar kul uttar pradesh main aane ke paryatakon ke 65% se adhik logo ko aakarshith karata he. mirzapur or sonbhadra prakritik sansadhano ke saath bahut samrudh hai. sab ke baavjood purvanchal abhi bhi rajya main sabase pichde kshetro me se ek he. jiskaa mukhya kaaran rajya sarkaar or kendra sarkaar dwaara uchit dhyaan kii kami he. gorakhpur ko purvanchal kii rajdhani kaha jaataa he.
 mayawati, mulaayam or amar singh jaise rajaneta uttar pradesh se apane dhoort raajnneetik laabh ke liye jati or dharmik sameekaran ke aadhaar par is kshetra ko utkeern karne ke liye prayaas kar rahe hai. purvanchal kshetra sangathit aparaadh or bhrashtachar ke centre rup main apamaanit he, iski vajah aam janata ke liye paryaapt shiksha or rojgaar ke avasaron ke abhaav he. bharat ke kisi bhi kshetra ke logon se adhik shoshan britishon ne is kshetra ke logon kaa kiya kyonki is kshetra ke logon majboot rashtravaadi hai or deshbhakti kii bhaavana thi or british raaj ke khilaaf kadaa virodh kiya.
 elahabaad pramukh shahar or jila, yah maujuuda samay main upi kii nyaayik rajdhani he.elahabaad hoicourt yaheen par hai. upi lokseva ayog kaa daftar yahaan hai. sangam nagari kii naam se prasiddh he. har 12 varsh main yahaan duniya kaa sabase bada kumbh mela lagata he. elahabaad purvanchal kaa ekmaatr aisa jila he jiskaa lagbhag aadha hissaa teen tahseelein bundelkhand main aati hai. meja, koranv or baaraa tehsil bundelkhand main aati he. handiya tehsil purvanchal main aati he. handiya main do vidhansabha siten hai, 258 handiya auki 259 pratappur jo ki bhadohi loksabhaa main aati hai.
 varanasi shahar uttari bharat kii madhya ganga ghati main, bhartiya rajya uttar pradesh ke purvi chor par ganga nadi ke baayin ore ke vakrakaar tat par sthit he. yahaan varanasi jile kaa mukhyalay bhi sthit he. varanasi shahari kshetra â€” saat shahari up-ikaayiyon kaa samooh he or ye 112.26 varg kimi ke kshetra failaa hua he. shahari kshetra kaa vistaar - evam - ke bich he. uttari bharat ke gangey maidan main based is kshetra kii bhoomi puraane samay main ganga nadi main aati raheen baadh ke kaaran upatyaka rahi he.varanasi main vibhinn kutir udyog karyrat hai, jinamein banarsi reshmi sari, kapadaa udyog, kaaleen udyog evam hastshilp pramukh hai. banarsi paan vishwaprasiddh he or iske saath hi yahaan kaa kalakand bhi mashhoor he. varanasi main bal-shramikon kaa kaam joro par he. banarsi resham vishwa bhar main apni maheenta evam mulaaympan ke liye prasiddh he. banarsi reshmi saadiyon par baarik dizine or zari kaa kaam chaar chand lagaate hai or sari kii shobhaa badhaate hai. is kaaran hi ye saadiyaan varshon se sabhi paaramparik utsavon evam vivaah aadi samarohon main pahani jaati rahi hai. kuch samay purv tak zari main shuddh sone kaa kaam hua karata tha.varanasi ke ucchtar madhyamik vidhyalay indian certificate of sacendary ajukeshan, kendriya madhyamik shiksha board yaa uttar pradesh madhyamik shiksha parishad se sahabaddh hai.
 jonpur jila varanasi prabhag ke uttar-pashchim bhaag main sthit he. isaki bhumikshetra 24.240N se 26.120N akshansh or 82.70E or 83.50E deshaantar ke bich faili hui he. gomti or sai mukhya nadiyaan hai. inke alaava, varun, pili or mayur aadi choti nadiyaan hai. mitti mukhya rup se retili, chikani balui he. jonpur aksar baadh kii aapadaa se prabhavit rahata he. jonpur jile me khanijon kii kami he. jile kaa bhugolic kshetrafal 4038 kimi2 he.jonpur jila kii vaastavik jansankhya 4,476,072 he. jiname 2,217,635 purush tathaa 2,258,437 mahilaayein he.jile kaa aarthik vikaas mukhya rup se krishi par nirbhar he. is kaa mukhya kaaran jile main baari udyog kaa abhaav he. kai udyog varanasi-jonpur rajmarg ke saath aa rahe hai.
 bhadohi, uttar pradesh kaa ek jila he. yah kaaleen nirmaan ke liye prasiddh he. uttar pradesh ke purvanchal kshetra ke pramukh janapad varanasi se 1996 main bana bhadohi jila aam jan ke dwaara 'kaaleen nagari' naam se janaa jaataa he. elahabaad, jonpur, varanasi, mirjapur kii simaaon ko sparsh karata yah jila apane kaaleen udyog ke kaaran vishwa main atyant prasiddh he. bharat ke bhugolic maanchitr par yah jila madhya ganga ghati main 25.09 akshansh uttari se 25.32 uttari akshansh tak tathaa 82.45 deshaantar purvi tak failaa he. 1056 varg kimi kshetrafal vaale is jile kii jansankhya 10,77630 he. gyanpur, aurai, bhadohi teen tehsil mukhyalayon ke adheen digh, amoli, suriyaavaan, gyanpur aurai or bhadohi vikaas khand karyalay he. elahabaad kii 2 vidhansabha siten handiya or pratappur ke saath milakar sansadeey kshetra banane vaale is janapad me 3 vidhaan sabha kshetra gyanpur aurai or bhadohi hai. is janapad kaa mukhya vyavsaay kaaleen he.
 mirzapur uttar pradesh rajya kaa ek shahar he. yah mirzapur jila kaa mukhyalay he. paryaton kii drushti se mirzapur kaaphi mahatvapurn jila mana jaataa he. yahaan kii prakritik sundarataa or dharmik vaataavaran barabas logon kaa dhyaan apni ore khinchati he. mirzapur sthit vindhyaachal dham bharat ke pramukh tirthasthalon main se ek he. iske atirikt, yah jila sita kund, laal bhairav mandir, moti talab, tanda jalprapaat, vindham jharnaa, tarakeshwar mahadev, maha trikon, shiv poor, chunaar kila, gurudwara guru da baagh or rameshwar aadi ke liye prasiddh he. mirzapur varanasi jile ke uttar, sonbhadra jile ke dakshin or elahabaad jile ke pashchim se ghira hua he.
 mirzapur kii sthiti 25.15, 82.58 par he. yahaan kii ausat oonchai he 80 meter .
 purvanchal bhashaa sangam
 
 gajipur, purvanchal kaa purana jila evam nagar he. iskaa pracheen naam gadhipuri tha jo ki lagbhag san 1330 main syed masood gaazi naamak ek mulslim shaasha dwaara badal diya gaya. gajipur, angrejon dwaara 1820 main sthapit, vishwa main sabase bade opium ke kaarkaane ke liye prakhyat he. yahan hathkargha tathaa itr udyog bhi he. british bharat ke governor general lord cornwalis kii mrityu yaheen hui thi tathaa ve yaheen dafan hai. shahar uttar pradesh-bihar seemaa ke bahut najadik sthit he. yahan kii sthaneey bhashaa bhojpuri he. yah pavitra shahar banaras ke 70 kii me purv main sthit he 
 gorakhpur, purvanchal kaa pramukh jila evam nagar he. yah gorakhpur jila, or gorakhpur kamishnari tathaa purvottar railway kaa prashasnic mukhyalay he. yah nagar gita press evam gorakhnath mandir ke liye vishesh rup se prasiddh he. yah mahaan sant paramhans yoganand kaa bhi janm sthaan he. shahar main bhi kai aitihaasik buddha sthal he. dinadayal upaadhyaay gorakhpur vishvavidhyalay, gorakhpur purvachal kaa prasiddh vishvavidhyalay he.
 kushinagar bhartiya rajya uttar pradesh kaa ek jila evam ek ek chota sa kasbaa he. is janapad kaa mukhyalay kushinagar se koi 15 kimi dur paderuna main sthit he. kushinagar rashtriya rajmarg 28 par gorakhpur se koi 50 kimi purab main sthit he. mahatma buddha kaa nirvana yaheen hua tha. yahan anek sundar buddha mandir hai. is kaaran se yah ek antararashtriya paryaton sthal bhi he jahaan mukhyat: vishwa bhar ke buddha tirthayatri bhraman ke liye aate hai. kushinagar kasbe ke or purab badhane par lagbhag 20 kimi baad bihar rajya armbh ho jaataa he.
 devaria bharat ke uttar pradesh praant kaa ek shahar evam jila mukhyalay he.devaria janapad main mukhya rup se hindi bhashaa boli jaati he. devaria janapad kii kul jansankhya kii lagbhag 96 pratishat janata hindi, lagbhag 3 pratishat janata urdu or ek pratishat janata ke baatcheet kaa madhyam anya bhashaayen hai. boli kii baat karen to gramin janata ke saath-saath adhikansh shahari janata bhi prem kii boli bhojpuri bolti he. kul jansankhya kii drushti se is janapad main lagbhag churaasi pratishat hindu, lagbhag pandrah pratishat muslim or ek pratishat anya dharm ko maanane vaale hai. is janapad kii janata aapas main prem-bhav se rahete hue sabake dukh-sukh main sahabhaagi banati he. yaa yun kahen "devaria janapad rupi upavan ko hindu, muslim, sikh, isaai, buddha aadi pushp apni sugandh se mahakaate hai or ye sugandh aapas main milakar puure bharat ko gamkaati he."
 azamgarh uttar pradesh ke azamgarh jile kaa mukhyalay he. is shahar kii sthapana lagbhag 1665 i. main vikramjit ke putr ajam khan ne karwaai thi. shahar kii purv disha par tamasa nadi ke tat par ajam khan ne ek kile kaa nirmaan karavaaya tha.
 mau uttar pradesh ke mau jila kaa mukhyalay he. iskaa purv naam maunath bhanjan ) tha. avantikapuri, govind sahib, dattatreya, dohari ghat, durvasa, mehnagar, mubarakapur, maharajaganj, nizamabad or azamgarh mau ke pramukh sthalon main se he. yah jila lakhanu ke dakshin-purv se 282 kilometre or azamgarh ke purv se 56 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. yah shahar tamasa nadi ke kinare basaa he. tamasa nadi shahar ke bich se nikalati/guzarati he. bhamu jile ke itihaas ko lekar kai bhram he. saamanyat: yah mana jaataa he ki mau shabd turkish shabd se liya gaya he jiskaa arth garh, pandav or chhavnee hota he. vastut: is jagah ke itihaas ke bare main koi aitihaasik pramaan uplabdh nahin he. mana jaataa he prasiddh shaasha sher shah suri ke shashan kaal ke dauraan is kshetra main kai aarthik vikaas karwaae gaye. vahiin military base or shahi masjid ke nirmaan main kaaphi sankhya main shramik or karigar mughal sainikon ke saath yahaan aae the. swatantraaa aandolan ke samay main bhi mau kii mahatvapurn bhoomika rahi
 bharat-nepal seemaa ke sameep sthit maharajaganj uttar pradesh rajya kaa ek jila he. iskaa jila mukhyalay maharajaganj shahar me sthit he. pehle is jagah ko karapath ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. yah jila nepal ke dakshin, gorakhpur jile ke uttar, kushinagar jile ke pashchim or siddharth nagar va sant kabir nagar jile ke purv me sthiti he. aitihaasik drushti se bhi yah kaaphi mahatvapurn sthal he.
 yah bharat ke uttar pradesh praant kaa ek shahar or basti jila kaa mukhyalay he. aitihaasik drushti se bhi yah sthaan kaaphi mahatvapurn mana jaataa he. basti jila sant kabir nagar jile ke pashchim main or gonda ke purv main sthit he. kshetrafal kii drushti se bhi yah uttar pradesh kaa saatvaan bada jila he. pracheen samay main basti ko 'kaushal' ke naam se janaa jaataa tha.

 baliya purvanchal kaa pramukh jila evam nagar he. shahar kii purvi seemaa ganga or ghaghara ke sangam main nihit he. baliya, gajipur se 76 kilometre or varanasi se 150 kilometre sthit he.purvanchal ke anya jilon kii bhaanti bhojpuri yahan ke logon kii mukhya boli he.
 bharat ke swatantrata sangram main baliya baagi baliya ke rup main apana mahatvapurn yogadan ke liye janaa jaataa he. 1942 ke bharat chodo aandolan ke samay baliya ne ek choti avadhi ke liye british shashan ke jila sarkaar kaa takhta palat kiya or chittu pandey ke adheen ek swatantra prashasan sthapitkiya.
 ek vaarshik meley ke dadri mela, ek maidan par shahar kii purvi seemaa par ganga or sarayu nadiyon ke sangam par manaaya jaataa he. mau, azamgarh, devaria,gorakhpur,gajipur or varanasi ke rup main paas ke jilon ke saath niyamit sampark main rail or sadak ke madhyam se maujuud he.

 sonbhadra bhartiya rajya uttar pradesh kaa ek jila he. jile kaa mukhyalay rabartsganj he.swatantraaa milane ke lagbhag 10 varshon tak yah kshetra alag-thalag tha tathaa yahaan yatayaat yaa sanchaar ke koi saadhan nahin the. pahadiyon main chuunaa pathar tathaa koyala milane ke saath tathaa kshetra main pani kii bahutayat hone ke kaaran yah audyogic swarg ban gaya. yahaan par desh kii sabase badi cement factriyaan, bijli ghar, aluminium evam rasinic ikaiyaan sthit hai. saath hi kai sari sahaayak ikaiyaan evam asangathit utpaadan kendra, vishesh rup se stone krashar ikaiyaan, bhi sthapit hui hai.
