 bharat main hindi sab se adhik boli jane vaali bhashaa he. hindi bhashaa kaa janm bharat main hua tha. hindi ko bharat kii rajbhasha ke rup main 14 sitambar san 1949 ko sweekaar kiya gaya. iske baad samvidhan main rajbhasha ke sambandh main dhara 343 se 352 tak kii vyavastha kii gayi. isaki smriti ko taaja rakhane ke liye 14 sitambar kaa din prativarsh hindi diwas ke rup main manaaya jaataa he. 
 
 har varsh 14 sitambar ke din ko hindi diwas ke rup main manaaya jaataa he. is din bharat main hindi bhashaa main achche se kaary karne vaale 13 lekhakon ko rajbhasha gaurav puraskaar or 39 santhaan yaa vibhaag ko rajbhasha kirti puraskaar diya jaataa he.
