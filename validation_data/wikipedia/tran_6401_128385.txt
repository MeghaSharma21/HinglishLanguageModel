 manushyon kii anuvanshiki main matruvansh samooh bil6 yaa mitocandria-diene hapalogrup L6 ek matruvansh samooh he. is matruvansh samooh ke sadsy striyon or purushon kii taadaad zyaada nahin he, lekin ve yaman or ithiopia ke kuch samudaayon main milte hai. vaigyanikon ke mutabiq jis stri se is matruvansh kii shuruyaat hui vah purvi africa main aaj se lagbhag 81,500 se 129,800 saal pehle rahati thi.
 angrezi main "vansh samooh" ko "hapalogrup", "pitruvansh samooh" ko "waahe chromosome hapalogrup" or "matruvansh samooh" ko "amtidiene hapalogrup" kehete hai.
