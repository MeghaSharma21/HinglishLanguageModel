 yah shahar hameshaa shaktishaalee baadshahon ke bahan liye aakarshan kaa centre sabit hua he. daulatabad kii samrik sthiti bahut hi mahatvapurn thi. yah uttar or dakshin bharat ke madhya main padataa tha. yahaan se puure bharat par shashan kiya ja saktaa tha. isi karanvash badshaah mohammad bin tughlaq ne ise apni rajdhani banaaya tha. usne dilli kii samast janata ko daulatabad chalane kaa aadesh diya tha. lekin vahaan kii kharaab sthiti tathaa aam logon kii takalifon ke kaaran use kuch varshon baad rajdhani pun: dilli lani padi.bahan
 devgiri dakshin bharat kaa prasiddh aitihaasik nagar jo aajkal daulatabad ke naam se pukaara jaataa he. maharashtra ke aurangabad jile main 20 degree uttar akshansh tathaa 75 degree purv deshaantar main sthit he. bhilam naamak raja ne ise 11veen sadi main basaaya tha or usee kaal se do sau varshon tak hindu shaskaon ne devgiri par shashan kiya. 14veen sadi se yah nagar musalamaanon ke adhikaar main chalaa aayaa. devgiri ke sameep aurangzeb ke marane par yah jila aurangabad kaha jane laga.
 mathura ke yadav kul se devgiri ke hindu shaasha sambandh jodate hai jis kaaran yahan kaa rajvansh 'yadav' kahalaaya. hemadri rachit 'bratkhand' main tathaa abhilekhon ke aadhaar par drudhprahaar devgiri ke yadav vansh kaa pratham aitihaasik purush mana jaataa he. bhilam shaktishaalee naresh tha jisane hoysal, chol tathaa chalukya rajyon par safal akraman kiya tha. uske uttaradhikari singhn ne ise samrajya kaa rup de diya. yuddh ke falswaroop devgiri rajya khandesh se anantpur tak tathaa pashchimi ghat se hyderabad tak vistrut ho gaya.
 13veen sadi ke devgiri naresh krishna kaa naam anek lekhon main miltaa he. isane vansh kii pratishtha kii abhivruddhi kii. krishna ke putr ramchandra ke shashan main khilji vansh ke sultan allaudin ne devgiri par chadhaai kii thi. allaudin yahan se asankhya dhan lootakar le gaya or uske senapati kafoor ramchandra ko bandi bana liya. kuch samay pashchat ramchandra mukt kar diya gaya. yahi kaaran tha ki devgiri ke raaj ne telangana ke yuddh main kafoor ko hathiyaaron kii madad di thi. shakanrdev ne singhaasana par baithne ke baad musalamaanon se shatruta badha lee jiskaa fal yah hua ki shankardev ko haraakar kafoor ne devgiri par adhikaar kar liya.
 devgiri kaa naam mohammad tughlaq ke saath bhi sambadhit he. usne rajdhani dilli se hataakar devgiri main sthapit kii or se phir dilli waapas kiya tha. devgiri kaa durg aaj bhi dakshin bharat main prasiddh he or sthaneey kshatriya durg main sthapit devipratimaa kii puja puraane hi utsaah se karte hai.
 daulatabad kila'-ek upekshit kila ! na shodhakartaaon kii nazar padati he na hi ise sangrakshit rakhane ke prayaapt upaay kiye ja rahe hai. kamjor deevaaren gir rahi hai..ek pracheen dharohar bharat khota ja rahaa he. itihaas gavaah he -yahi ek maatr ek aisa kila he jise kabhi koi jeet nahin sakaa. 
 kai raajaaon kii tarah akbar mahaan ne bhi is par chaar baar chadhai kii thi magar safal nahin hua. is ke avijit hone main is kii saranchana ko shrey jaataa he. yah kila maharashtra rajya main aurangabad se 13 kilometre par pahari kii chotii par sthit he.
