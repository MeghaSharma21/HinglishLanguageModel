 maranottar stambhan, ek shaishnik stambhan he or jise takneekee bhashaa main prayapism kehete hai, aksar un purushon ke shav main dekhne main aata he, jinaki mrityu prandand, vishesh rup se faansee ke kaaran hui ho.
 maranottar stambhan hone kaa pramukh kaaran gale par fanda kasne ke kaaran anumastishk par padaa dabaav he. merurajju choton ko prayapism ke saath jodakar dekha jaataa he. jivit rogiyon main prayapism kaa kaaran anumastishk yaa merurajju par lagi chot se jodakar dekha jaataa he.aisa vishwaas kiya jaataa he ki, faansee se mrityu, chaahe wo nishpaadan dwaara hui ho yaa aatmahatya dwaara, purushon or mahilaon donon ke jananaangon ko prabhavit karti he.
 mahilaon main, ostak kii gatakan ke kaaran yoni se raktasrav ho saktaa he. purushon main, shishn ke kam yaa adhik stambhan ke saath shleshm, mutr, yaa prostatic taral kaa risaav hona ek nirantar ghatane vaali ghatana he, jisaki sambhaavna har teen main se ek vyakti ko hoti he. mrityu ke anya kaarnon se bhi ye prabhaav dekha ja saktaa he jaise mastishk, rakt vahikaaon main ghaatak goli laganaa, vishaaktata ke dwaara or nyaayalayik rup se kahen to shavpariksha ke dauraan prayapism is baat kaa sanket he kii mrityu teevr or hinsak dhang se hui thi.
