 swargranthi1 yaa swaryantr manushyon or anya standhari2 jeevon ke gale main maujuud ek granthi3 hoti he jis ke prayog se yah jeev bhinn prakaar kii dhvaniyon main bol pate hai. swargranthi ke andar bahut se swar-rajju4 hote hai. jab in swar-rajjuon ke upar se hawa kaa tej bahaav hota he tab inaki kanpakanpi se alag-alag dhvaniyon kii awaz paidaa hoti he, thik usee tarah jaise kisi sitar kii taron ke kampan se vibhinn suron kaa sangeet paidaa hota he.
