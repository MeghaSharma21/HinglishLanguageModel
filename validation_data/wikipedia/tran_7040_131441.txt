 mahananda vanyajeev abhyaarany tista or mahananda nadiyon ke bich, himalay kii talahati par sthit he. yah pashchim bengal ke darjeeling jile main sthit he. yah darjeeling vanyajeev vibhaajan ke antargat aata he or yahan silligudi se 30 minute main pahunchaa ja saktaa he. abhayaarany 159 varg kimi arakshit jangal main failaa hua he or 1955 main ek khel abhayaarany ke rup main shuru kiya gaya tha. 1959 main, ise mukhya rup se gaur or bengal baagh kii raksha ke liye ek abhayaarany kaa darja mila, jo ki vilupt hone kaa khatra tha.
