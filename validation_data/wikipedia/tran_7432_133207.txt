 jejakabhukti ke raja yaa jitauti guptkaal kaa ek prasiddh rajya tha jo yamuna or narmada nadi nadiyon ke bich main sthit he. ise ab bundelkhand kehete hai. is par chandel raajaaon kaa shashan tha. yah ab aanshik rup se uttar pradesh main tathaa aanshik rup se madhyapradesh main padataa he.
 jitauti vir-prasvini bhoomi rahi he. isane chandelon or banafar sardaar aalha oodal ko hi nahin, swatantray-premi chhatrasaal aadi ko bhi janm dekar bharat kaa mastak unnat kiya he. khajuraho kii anupam lalitkala or sthapaty bhi isi kii upaj hai.
 kaha jaataa he ki chandel raja jaishakti jejac ke naam se hi bundelkhand kaa naam jejabhukti yaa jitauti padaa he. chandel aarambh main pratihaaron ke samant rahe honge. harsh chandel ke samay main, jo is vansh kaa pehla pratapi raja tha, chandelon ne kshitipal ko apane rajsinghaasana par pun: pratishthit kiya. kshitipal kannauj ke raja mahipal kaa doosraa naam ho saktaa he. harsh ke putr yashoverma ne chediyon or pratihaaron ko haraaya or kalinger par adhikaar kar liya. uskaa putr dhang or bhi pratapi or chirjive siddh hua. rajya ke aarambhkaal main bhilsa, gwalior or kalinger ke durg uske adhikaar main the or padosi rajya usase bhaybheet the. iske baad rajya kii seemayen or bhi badi hongi. dhang ke putr gand ne sambhavat: anandapal shahi ko mahmud gaznavi ke viruddh sahaayta di. san 1018 main jab mahmud ne kannauj ke raja rajyapal pratihaar ko apni adheenta maanane ke liye vivash kiya to vidyadhar chandel ne rajyapal ko samrajya ke liye ayogy samaj kar yuddh main parrast kiya or uske putr trilochanpal ko kannauj kii gaddi par bithaaya. san 1020 ke lagbhag mahmud or vidyadhar main muthbhed hui. mahamood ko kalinger ke ghere main vifaltaa hui.
 vidyadhar kii mrityu ke baad kuch samay ke liye jitauti rajya kii shakti ksheen hui. kintu kirtiverma ne chediyon ko haraakar phir ise samunnat kiya. isi ke samay krishna mishra ne prabodhachandroday naatak kii rachana kii. keetiverma ke pautra madanvarma ne bhi acchhee khyaati praapt kii. madanvarma kaa pautra suprasiddh paramardi yaa parmaal tha. san 1182 main prithviraj tritiy ne iske rajya ke kuch bhaag ko lutaa. san 1202 main kutbuddin aibak ne kalinger par adhikaar kar liya kintu isse jitauti ke chandel rajya kii samaapti na hui. paramardi ke putr trailoky verma ne kalinger waapas le liya or 16veen shatabdi tak vah chandelon ke haath main rahaa.
