 olympic khel vartmaan kii pratiyogitaaon main agrani khel pratiyogita he jisame hazaron ethelite kai prakaar ke khelon main bhaag lete hai. olympic kii sheetakaaleen evam greeshmakaleen pratiyogitaaon main 200 se zyada desh pratibhaagi ke rup main shaamil hote hai. olympic khel prayek chaar varsh ke antaraal se yojit kiye jate hai. olympic khelon kaa aayojan anterrashtriya olympic samiti karti he.
 olympic khelon kaa itihaas bahut purana he. pracheen olympic khelon kaa aayojan 1200 saal purv yoddha-khilaadiyon ke bich hua tha. puraane samay main shantipurna samay antaraal ke dauraan yoddhaon ke bich pratispardhaa ke saath khelon kaa vikaas hua. shuruaati daur main daud, mukkebaaji, kushti or rathon kii daud sainik prashikshan kaa hissaa hua karte the. inamen se sabase behatar pradarshan karne vaale yoddha pratispardhi ko khelon main apana damakham dikhaane kaa mauka miltaa tha .
 pracheen kaal main yah greece yani yunaan kii rajdhani athens main 1896 main yojit kiya gaya tha. olympia parvat par khele jane ke kaaran iskaa naam olympic padaa. olympic main rajyon or shaharon ke khilaadi bhaag lete the. isaki lokapriyata kaa andaja is baat se lagaaya ja saktaa he ki olympic khelon ke dauraan shaharon or rajyon ke bich ladaai tak sthagit kar die jate the. is khelon main ladaai or ghudsawaari kaaphi lokapriy khel the. lekin uske baad bhi saalon tak olympic andolan kaa swaroop nahin le paaya. tamaam suvidhaon kii kami, aayojan kii mejabani kii samasya or khilaadiyon kii kam bhagidaari-in sabhi samasyaon ke baavjood dhire-dhire olympic apane maqasad main kaamayaab hota gaya. pracheen olympic kii shuruyaat 776 bisi main hui maani jaati he.
 pracheen olympic main baxing, kushti, ghudsawaari ke khel khele jate the. khel ke vijeta ko kavita or murtiyon ke jariye prashansit kiya jaataa tha. har chaar saal par hone vaale olympic khel ke varsh ko olympiad ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa tha. olympic khel anterrashtriya str par yojit hone vaali bahu-khel pratiyogita he. in khelome bharat gold medl praapt kar chukaa he.ek anya dantakatha ke anusaar harculis ne jeus ke sammaan main olympic stadium banwaaya gaya. chhathvin or paanchavin shatabdi main olympic khelon kii lokapriyata charam par pahunch gai thi. lekin baad main roman samrajya kii badhati shakti se greece khaasaa prabhavit hua or dhire-dhire olympic khelon kaa mahatv girane laga.
 393 isvi ke aasapaas olympic khel greece yani yunaan main band ho gaya. 1896 ke baad varsh 1900 main paris ko olympic kii mejabani kaa intazaar nahin karnaa padaa or sanskaran lokapriy nahin ho sake kyonki is dauraan bhavy aayojanon kii kami rahi. 2008 main china kii rajdhani beijing olympic main ab tak kaa sabase acha aayojan mana gaya he. pandrah din tak chale olympic khelon ke dauraan china ne naa sirf apni shandaar mezbaani se logon kaa dil jeeta balki sabase zyaada swarna padak jeet kar bhi itihaas rachaa. bharat ne bhi olympic ke itihaas main pehali baar 1928 main swarna padak jeeta or use pehali baar ek saath teen padak bhi mili. vishwa ke pracheenatam antarrashtriya khel samaaroh olympic kaa aayojan 2016 kaa brazil ke shahar rio di generio main 5 agast se 21 tak chalaa ! . is baar ke rio di generio olympic main 26 khelon main 204 deshon ke lagbhag 10500 khilaadeeyon ne bhaag liya tha. is baar bharat ne olympic main rajat, kaansya padak jeeta tha.
