 bhoomihaar brahman ke kuch mulon ke logo kaa bhoomihaar brahman rup main sangathit hone kii ek suchi yahan di ja rahi he :
 1. kanyakubj shakha se :- donwar ,sakarwaar,kinvar, tatiha, nanhuliya, vanshavaar ke tiwari, kudhania, dasikar, aadi.
 2. saraupari shakha se : – gautam, kolha, nainijor ke tiwari, pusarod khiri se aaye parashar gotri pandey, mujffarpur main mathurapur ke garg
 3. maithil shakha se : – maithil shakha se bihar main basane vaale kai mul ke bhoomihaar brahman aaye hai.iname savarna gotri bemuvaar or shandilya gotri dighavay – dighvait or dighvay sandalpur pramukh he.
 4. mahiyaalo se : – mahiyaalo kii bali shakha ke parashar gotri brahman pandit jagannath dikshit chhapra main ekasaar sthaan par bas gaye. ekasaar main pratham vaas karne se vaishali, mujffarpur, channpur, samastipur, chhapra, parasgarh, sursand, gauraiya kothi, gamirar, behalalpur, aadi gaanv main based hue parashar gotri eksariya mul ke bhoomihaar brahman ho gaye.
 5. chitpaavan se : – nyaay bhatta naamak chitapaavan brahman sapariwaar shraadh hetu gaya kabhi purv kaal main aaye the.ayaachak brahman hone se inhone apni potee kaa vivaah magadh ke ikkil pargane main vatsa gotri donwar ke putr udai bhaan pandey se kar diya or bhoomihaar brahman ho gaye.patna daltonganj road par dharhara,bharatpur aadi kai gaanv main tathaa dumka, bhojpur, rohtas ke kai gaanv main ye chitapaavan mul ke kaundilya gotri atharv bhoomihaar brahman rahete hai.!
