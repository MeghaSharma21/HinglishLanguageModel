 lollywood pakistan ke lahore sthit pakistani film udyog kaa anaadhikrut naam he. sanith film ko kaha jaataa he. is main lavali - pakistan ke ahm shahar lahore se liya gaya he.
 bharat main cinema kaa armbh 7 july 1896 ko hua jab lumiye-bandhuon kii cinematography main mumbai ke western hottle main chah laghu filmein pradarshit kii gai. videshi nirmaataaon dwaara pradarshit filmein dekhne ke baad kuch bharatiyon main bhi filmon ke prati utsaah jaagaa or 1910 ke dashak main pundalik naamak bhartiya film pradarshit hui. baad main pradarshit hui marathi film raja harishchandra ko bharat kii sarvapratham purna lambaai kii film mana jaataa he. 1928 main lahore main film nirmaan kaa kaary armbh hua jo bharat ke vibhaajan ke baad pakistani film udyog ke rup main viksit hua.
