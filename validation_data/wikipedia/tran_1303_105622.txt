 maanav kaamuktha vah tarika he jisase log kaamuk rup se apane aap ko anubhav or abhivyakt karte hai. isamen jaivik, kaamuk, shaareerik, bhaavnaatmak, saamaajik yaa aadhyatmik bhavanaayen or vyavhaar shaamil hai. chunki yah vyapak shabd he, jo samay ke saath badla hua he, isaki koi ek sateek paribhaasha nahin he. kaamuktha ke jaivik or shaareerik pahalu maanav prajnan kaaryon se mukhya rup se sambandhit hai, jisamen maanav kaamuk anukriya chakra shaamil he. kisi kaa yon abhivinyas us vyakti ke duusare vyakti ke liye yon ruchi or aakarshan ko prabhavit kar saktaa hai.
 kaamuktha ke shaareerik or bhaavnaatmak pahaluon main vyaktiyon ke bich bandhan hota hai jo gahari bhaawanaaon yaa prem, vishwaas or dekhbhaal kii shaareerik abhivyaktiyon ke madhyam se vyakt kiya jaataa he. saamaajik pahaluon ko kisi ke kaamuktha par maanav samaaj ke prabhaav se nipatna padataa he, jabaki aadhyatm kaa sandarbh ek vyakti ke duusare ke saath aadhyatmik sambandh se he. kaamuktha jivan ke saanskrithik, raajnneetik, kanoonee, darshanik, naitik, nitishastreey or dharmik pahaluon ko prabhavit karti he or unase prabhavit bhi hoti hai.
 yon gatividhi main ruchi aamtaur par bad jaati he jab koi vyakti yauvanarmbh tak pahunchataa he. kisi vyakti ke yon abhivinyas or yon vyavhaar kii utpatti par raay bhinn hai. kuch log tark dete hai ki kaamuktha anuvanshiki dwaara nirdhaarit hoti he, jabaki dusron kaa maananaa he ki yah paryaavaran dwaara dhaali jaati he, yaa ye donon kaarak vyaktigat yon abhivinyas banane ke liye paraspar kriya karte hai. yah prakruti banaam poshan vivaad se sambandhit hai.
