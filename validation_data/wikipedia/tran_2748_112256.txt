 udai pratap college varanasi shahar main sthit ujisi kaa ek swytt mahavidhyalay he. is college kii sthapana 1909 main rajarshi udai pratap singh ne kii thi. aarambh main ise ek hai skool ke rup main chalaaya gaya tha, kintu 1949 se ise snatakottar mahavidhyalay banaaya gya or kollege digriyan bhi dene laga. yahan kala, manviki, vigyaan evam prabandhan se sambandhit vishay or paathyakram uplabdh hai.
