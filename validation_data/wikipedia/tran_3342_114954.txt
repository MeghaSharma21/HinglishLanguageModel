 elahabaad main sangam ke nikat sthit is kile ko mughal samraat akbar ne 1583 i. main banwaaya tha. vartmaan main is kile kaa kuch hi bhaag paryatakon ke liye khulaa rahata he. baaki hisse kaa prayog bhartiya sena karti he. is kile main teen badi gailaree hai jahaan par oonchi minaaren hai. sailaaniyon ko ashok stambh, saraswati coop or jodhabai mahal dekhne kii ijaajat he. iske alaava yahaan akshay vat ke naam se mashhoor bargad kaa ek purana ped or patalpur mandir bhi he.
 elahabaad kile kii sthapana mughal badshaah akbar ne kii thi. haalanki is par itihaaskaaron main matbhed he. samakaleen lekhak abdul kaadir badayunni ne 'muntakhwul-tavaarikh' main likhaa he ki kile kii neenv san 1583 main daali gai thi. nadi kii kataan se yahaan kii bhugolic sthiti sthir na hone se iskaa nakshaa aniyamit dhang se taiyaar kiya gaya tha. ve likhte hai ki aniyamit nakshe par kile kaa nirmaan karana hi isaki visheshta he. kile kaa kul kshetrafal tees hajaar varg foot he. iske nirmaan main kul laagat chah karod, 17 lakh, 20 hajaar 214 rupee aayi thi.
 akbar ne is kile kaa nirmaan mughalkaal main purvi bharat se afgaan vidroh ko khatm karne ke liye kiya tha. 
 san 1773 main angrej is kile main aae or san 1775 main bengal ke nawab shujauddula ke haath 50 lakh rupee main bech diya. san 1798 main nawab shaajat ali or angrejon main ek sandhi ke baad kila phir angrejon ke kabje main aa gaya. aazaadee ke baad bharat sarkaar kaa kile par adhikaar hua. kile main parsi bhashaa main ek shilaalekh bhi he, jisamen kile kii neenv padane kaa varsh 1583 diya he. 
 kile main ek janaani mahal he, jise jahangir mahal bhi kehete hai. mughal shaskaon ne kile main bade pherbadal karaaye or phir angrejon ne bhi ise apane makul banane ke liye kaaphi tod-phod kii. isse kile ko kaaphi kshati pahunchi. 
 kai shaskaon or angrejon ko surakshit rakhane tathaa aaj bhi desh kii sena ko sharan dene vaale is kile kii avasthaa ab jarjar ho gai he. isaki deevaaron par bargad, neem, pipal aadi ne jaden jamaa lee hai. ghaas-foons or jhaadiyaan bhi fail gai hai. deevaaron par nikle ped puranic akshayavat kii jadon kii upaj hai. inamen kuch ped aise bhi hai, jo pakshiyon ke kaaran daraaron main jam gaye hai. jald in pedo ko nahin kaata gaya to ye kile kii deevaaron ke liye khatra ban jaaenge. chunki kila is samay sena ke kabje main he, isliye uski ijaajat ke bagaiyr puraatatv vibhaag saundaryikaran yaa marammat bhi nahin kara saktaa he. 
 sab aria commander brigadier aarake bhutani kaa kahana he ki deevaaron par uge ped kaate ja rahe hai. isse saath hi chemical treetment se un pedo kaa samul bhi naash kiya ja rahaa he. yah kaary pichley chah maheenon se chal rahaa he. deevaaron kii bhi marammat karai ja rahi he or unhen bhi rasinic upachaar diya ja rahaa he. sangrakshan kaary kile ke un kshetron main karaayaa ja rahaa he, jahaan aam shahriyon kaa janaa manaa he.
