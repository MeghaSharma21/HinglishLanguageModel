 romanian yaa daako-romanian ek romance bhashaa he, jise duniyabar main, visheshtaur se romania or moldova main, kariban dhai karod log bolte hai. ise romania, moldova ganraajya or srbija ke vojvudina swytt praant main aadhikarik darja praapt he. moldova main ise rajnatik kaarnon se limba moldavenska kaha jaataa he. 
 romanian bhashi puri duniya main, khaastaur se italy, spain, rus, ukraine, israel, portugal, united kingdom, sanyukt rajya america, canada, france or germany main faili hue hai.
