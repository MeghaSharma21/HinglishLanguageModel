 17 navambar 1965 ko unesco ne 8 sitambar ko anterrashtriya sakshartaa diwas ghoshit kiya. isko pehali baar 1966 main manaaya gaya. iskaa uddeshy vyaktigat, saamudayik or saamaajik rup se sakshartaa ke mahatv par prakaash daalana he. yah utsav duniyabar main manaaya jaataa he.
 lagbhag 77.5 karod yuva sakshartaa kii kami se prabhavit hai; arthath paanch main se ek yuva abhi tak sakshar nahin he or inamen se do tihaayi mahilayen hai. 6.7 karod bachche vidyalayon tak nahin pahonchte or bahut bacchon main niyamitata kaa abhaav he athwa bich main chhod dete hai.
