 nagarjunakonda andhra pradesh rajya ke nalgonda zile main sthit ek aitihaasik nagar he. hyderabad se 100 mile dakshin-purv kii ore sthit nagarjunakonda ek pracheen sthaan he.yah buddha mahayan ke prasiddh acharya nagarjun i. ke naam par prasiddh he.pratham shatabdi main yahan satvaahan nareshon kaa rajya tha.'haal' naamak satvaahan raja ne nagarjun ke liye shri parvat shikhar par ek vihaar banwaaya tha.yah sthaan buddha dharm kii mahayan shakha kaa bhi kaafii samay tak prachaar kendra rahaa.satvahanon ke pashchaat ikshvaku nareshon ne yahan rajya kiya.nagarjunakonda ikshvaku raajaaon ke samay ek sundar nagar tha.krishna nadi ke tat par sthit tathaa chaturdik parvat maalaon se parivritt yah nagar prakritik soundarya se samanvit hone ke saath hi durbhedya durg kii bhanti surakshit bhi tha.yahan se nau buddha stupon ke avshesh lagbhag 50 varsh purv utkhanit kiye gayi the.ye is nagar ke pracheen gaurav evam aishwarya ke sakshi hai.utkhanan main praapt yahan ke avasheshon main ek stupa, do chaityas or ek vihaar hai.stupa ke nikat buddha ke jivan ke drishyon ko vyakt karne vaale chooney ke pathar ke tukade mili hai.hindu dharm ke punarutthan se nagargunakonda kaa mahatva ghatane laga.nagarjunakonda se praapt abhilekhon se yah gyaat hota he, ki pehali shatabdi i. main bharat kaa china, unani jagat tathaa lanka se sambandh sthapit tha.nagarjunakonda ke ek abhilekh se sthaviron ke sanghon kaa gyaan hota he, jinhone kashmir, gandhar, china, kiraat, tosali, yavan, tamraparni dwipon main buddha dharm failaayaa tha.
