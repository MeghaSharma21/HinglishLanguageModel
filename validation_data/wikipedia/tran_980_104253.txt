  
 resalmenia pe-par-vyuu par aadhaarit ek peshewar kushti aayojan he, jo prayek varsh march ke ant yaa april ke shuru main world raceling entertenment  ke dwaara yojit kii jaati he. yah WWE kaa pramukh aayojan mana jaataa he, chunki yah vishwa main sabase adhik safal tathaa lambe samay tak chalane vaala peshewar kushti aayojan he. resalmenia kaa upnaam "da grandadi of dem aal", "da grandest stage of dame aal" or "da shokes of da emortal" he. sabase pehle iskaa aayojan 1985 main kiya gaya tha or uske baad se hi 2010 tak 26 shrinkhalaaon kaa aayojan kiya ja chukaa he jiname se resalmenia XXVII kaa aayojan 2011 main kiya janaa he.
 WWE ke maalik vins macmahon dwaara pariklpit, resalmenia kii vyapak safalataa ne peshewar kushti ke udyog ko badalane main sahaayta kii he tathaa WWE ko vishwa main sabase adhik safalataa se prachaarit kiya he. resalmenia ne andertekar, halk hogan, brett heart, shaun maaikel, steve austin, da rock or tripal ech jaise pahalawaanon ko prasiddhi dilaane main sahaayta kii he. anek hastiyon jaise mohammad ali, mistar tii, elis kupar, laurence tailor, pamela anderson, mike tyson, donald trump, floid mewedar, snup dog, reven-simon, kim cardasian, mikki rorke, jeni mekakarthi or anyon ne isamen bhaag liya yaa aayojan main vishesh pradarshan kiya. WWE ke tivi sho resalmenia ko charmotkarsh tak pahunchane ke liye banaaye jate hai jisame company ke championships khitaabon ke saath saath vishesh or naatakeey maichon kaa pradarshan kiya jaataa he. resalmenia match main or vishesh rup se mukhya spardhaa main bhaag lenaa kai pahalawaanon or prashansakon dwaara peshewar kushti main sabase badi uplabdhi or safalataa ke pratik ke rup main mana jaataa he. 
 resalmenia media, saamaan tathaa sho kii sahaayta se WWE kii vishwavyaapee vanijyik safalataa ko badhaata he. abhi tak hue sabhi aayojanon ke tikat kam avadhi ke andar hi bik gayi tathaa haal ke sanskaran ke tikat bikri shuru hone ke kuch hi minuton ke bheetar bik gayi. pehla resalmenia neuyork shahar ke medisson squaire garden main yojit kiya gaya tha. 10 wein or 20 wein sanskaran kaa aayojan bhi vahaan hua tha. datroit main yojit resalmenia III 93,000 se adhik prashansakon kii upasthiti ke saath vishwa main sabase adhik upasthiti vaala indoor khel aayojan tha. do ke alaava sabhi sanskarnon kii mejabani america shaharon main kii gayi he - do kaa aayojan toronto, canada main hua he - lekin sho ko puri duniya main pe-par-vyuu ke madhyam se pradarshit kiya gaya.
 jabaki adhikansh resalmenia ne pramukh shaharon ke khel akhaadon main jagah bana lee he, sabase adhik upasthiti vaale aayojanon main shaamil hai - pontiak main resalmenia III, toronto main resalmenia VI, indianapolis main resalmenia VIII, huston main resalmenia X7, toronto main resalmenia X8, sietal main resalmenia XIX, datroit main resalmenia 23, orlando main resalmenia XXIV huston main resalmenia XXV or glandale main resalmenia XXVI .
 resalmenia ke mukhya aakarshan WWE championships or haul hi main world haiviveet championships ke pramukh mukaable hai. 2007 se 2009 tak, ECW ke frenchizi ne bhi resalmenia main bhaag liya tha. kuch chune hue championships khitaab ke liye bhi mukaable hote hai, jabaki kai naatakeey match vyaktigat jhagadon par aadhaarit bhi hote hai.
 1993 ke baad se vaarshik royal rambal match vijeta ne nishchit rup se us varsh kaa WWE championships match jeeta he. 2002 main world haiviveet championships ke nirmaan ke baad vijeta ke paas world haiviveet championships match ke chayan kaa vikalp praapt he. 2007 main ECW, brands ke aagaman ke pashchaat vijeta ke paas uparokt kisi bhi resalmenia maichon ke alaava ECW world championships ke match kaa chayan karne kaa vikalp bhi joda gaya tha, farvari 2010 main ECW brands kii samaapti ke pashchaat yah khatm ho gaya he.
 2005 ke baad se is spardhaa ko mani in da bank leader match kii vajah se bhi janaa jaataa he jisame chah se das khilaadi bhaag lete hai. match kaa vijeta ek anubandh praapt karata he jo uski pasand ke samay or sthaan par WWE ke do vishwa khitaabon ke match ke liye guarantee detaa he. anubandh ek varsh se le kar agle resalmenia varsh tak vaid hota he. 
 pehle chah resalmenia main se paanch main gorilla monsoonaur jessi venchura ne mukhya camentator ke rup main kaary kiya, jabaki bobi hinan or dusron ne atithi kii bhoomika nibhaai. resalmenia VII or VIII, ke liye monsoon or hinan mukhya camentator the. 1990 ke dashak ke madhya se ant tak, team vins mekamahon, gym ross or jeri 'da king' lavlar se mil kar bani thi. 2002-03 main brands alag hone ke baad se ro brands ke maichon kii camentry ross or lavlar dwaara; smacdown maichon kii maaikel kol, tez, jon "bradshow" lefield or jonathan kochamen or ECW joi styles or tez dwaara kii jaati he. WWE brands vistaar ke baad resalmenia XXV main pehle teen sadasyeey inter brands camentry team kaa istemaal kiya gaya tha or isamen jerry lavlar, gym ross or maaikel kol shaamil the. agle varsh XXVI resalmenia main gym ross kaa sthaan mat striker ne le liya. harvard finkel ne ring udghoshak ke rup main lambe samay tak seva pradaan kii he or prayek spardhaa main dikhaai diya he, lekin WWE brands ke vistaar ke baad lilian garsia, justin robrts or tony chimel ne bhi yah bhoomika nibhaai he.
 world raceling federation ne 31 march 1985 main neuyork shahar ke medisson squaire garden par pehle resalmenia kaa aayojan kiya. mukhya muqabla ek tag team match tha jisame WWF champion halk hogan or mistar tii ke saath jimi snuka, raudi pyper va paul orndorf kii team ke khilaaf the jinke saath kaubvaay bob orton the. spardhaa kii vittiya or mahatvapurn safalataa ne company ko sanyukt rajya main sabase safal prachaarak ke rup main badal diya or yah pratidwandi jaise neshnal raceling alins or american raceling association se upar pahunch gayi. agle varsh resalmenia 2 yojit kiya gaya or desh bhar main teen sthanon par yojit hua. uniondel main neuyork main nasaau waiteran memorial kolligium rosement, illinois main rosemont horaiden or los angeles, california main los angeles memorial sports erina, prayek main kai maichon ke pradarshan ke baad mukhya spardhaa huyi jisame WWF champion halk hogan ne chunauti dene vaale king kong bandi ko ek steel kej match main haraa diya.
 resalmeniya III main 93173 prashansakon kii upasthiti ne vishwa record banaaya, joki us samay peshewar kushti ke itihaas main sabase adhik bhugtan karne vaale darshak bhi the. spardhaa ko 1980 ke dashak main kushti ke aakarshan ko shikhar par pahunchane kaa kaaran mana jaataa he. silverdom main har seat bhari ho, yah sunishchit karne ke liye, world raceling foundation ne pe-par-vyuu suvidha ko spardhaa se hataa diya, jiske kaaran prashansakon ke liye spardhaa ko dekhne kaa ekmaatr tarika isamen upasthit hona tha. mukhya spardhaa main WWF champianship ke bachaav main hogan kaa andre da joint se muqabla tha. hogan dwaara andre da joint ke sharir ko patakana peshewar kushti ke itihaas main sabase prasiddh kshanon main se ek he. WWF intercantinental champion randi sevej or riki steambot ke bich hue match ko bhi atyadhik lokapriyata or prashansa hasil hui.
 sanyukt rajya ke baahar hone vaala pehla aayojan resalmenia VI tha. yah canada main toronto, ontario ke scydom main yojit kiya gaya tha. spardhaa ke pramukh match main da ultimate variyar ne halk hogan ko haraa kar WWF championships kaa kitaab jeeta. resalmenia VII kaa aayojan multah los angeles memorial kaalizium main kiya janaa tha, kintu khadi yuddh ke suraksha kaarnon ke chalate aayojan saamne kii ore sthit los angeles memorial sports erina main kiya gaya. spardhaa main WWF championships ke liye halk hogan kaa saamana sargent sloter se hua, jabaki andertekar ne jimi snuka ko haraa kar resalmenia main apni shuruyaat kii. tab se, apane sabhi resalmenia maichon main andertekar aparajit rahaa he.
 resalmenia IX outdoor jagah par hone vaala pehla resalmenia aayojan tha. aayojan ke 10wein sanskaran main, resalmenia X ne medisson squaire garden main apni vaapasi kii. spardhaa ke dauraan oven heart ne apane bade bhai brett ko haraaya, WWF intercantinental championships ke liye ek leader match bhi prasiddhi mili jisame reiser reman ne shaun maaikel ko haraaya. resalmenia XII main WWF championships jeetne ke liye maaikel ne 60 minute aayran main match main brett heart ko haraaya. is match ko aayojan ke itihaas main sarvottam maichon main se ek mana jaataa he. 
 resalmenia XIV main stone cold steve austin shaun maaikel ko haraa kar nayaa WWF champion bana, ek aisa match jisame mike tyson ne vishesh rup se uksaane kaa kaam kiya. yadhyapi tyson ne maaikel tathaa uski sthir di-janareshan eks ke saath gathbandhan kiya tha, baad main khulaasa hua ki vah austin ke saath mila hua tha chunki usne nijee taur par pinphal kii ginati kii tathaa austin ko vijeta ghoshit kiya. is mukaable ko "da atitude ira" kii shuruyaat karne ke liye janaa jaataa he. iske ek varsh baad resalmenia XV main austin ne da rock ko haraa kar punah WWF championships ko hasil kiya. spardhaa ke dauraan resalmenia main da atitude ira ke do sabase pramukh or lokapriy sitaron kii pratidwandvita ke kaaran kai muthabheden huin.
 resalmenia 2000 main WWF tag team championships ke liye pehali baar tikdi leader match hua jisamehardi boij, da dadli boij tathaa age va chrishchiyan shaamil the. mukhya spardhaa main WWF champion tripal ech ne steffni mekamahon ke saath mil kar teenon chalangers : da rock va vins mekamahon, big sho va shen mekamahon or mik foli va linda mekamahon ke khilaaf safaltaapurvak apane kitaab kii raksha kii. 
 resalmenia X-7 main, stone cold steve austin ne da rock ko haraaya or punah WWF championships par kabza kiya. spardhaa ke dauraan vins or shen mekamahon kii street fit bhi dikhaai gayi, jabaki age or chrishchiyan ne duusare teble, leader or chairs match main hardi boij va dadli boij ke khilaaf WWF tag team championships main jeet darj kii. aayojan 1990 ke dashak main kushti ke aakarshan ke shikhar par tha, jiske saath da atitude ira kii samaapti hui. company ke pratidwandvi world championships raceling tathaa mande nit vars ke vighatan ke baad hone vaali yah pehali resalmenia pratiyogita bhi thi. resalmenia X8 WWF ke binar tale hone vaala antim resalmenia tha or isamen tripal ech ne kris jerrico ko haraa kar andisputed championships jiti. steve austin ne nWo ke scot haul ke saath kevin naish ko haraaya jabaki rock or andertekar ne kramshah halk hogan tathaa rik flair ko haraaya, jiname donon ne WCW ke saath apane algaav ke kaaran company main phir se vaapasi kii. resalmenia XIX main steve austin kaa ab tak kaa antim match dikhaaya gaya jisame unhonen resalmenia main teesri baar da rock kaa saamana kiya, jiske saath unke lambe samay se chale aa rahe jhagade kaa ant hua. halk hogan ne vins mekamahon ko haraaya or shaun maaikel ne paanch varshon ke baad apane pehle resalmenia match ko khelte hue kris jerrico ko haraaya. tripal ech ne bukar tii ke khilaaf pehali baar vishwa haiviveet championships kaa bachaav kiya, jabaki broke lesner ne court angel ko haraa kar WWE championships jiti. 
 world raceling antertenment ne resalmenia XX ke saath medisson squaire garden main resalmenia ke 20wein sanskaran kaa jashn manaaya. aayojan main andertekar dwaara ken ko haraane ke liye apane "deadman" ke vyaktitv ke saath vaapisi kii, edi gurero or kris benoit dwaara kramshah world haiviveet championships or WWE championships jiti gayi. aayojan main da rock or mik foli banaam evolution ke batista, rendi orton or rik flair tathaa broke lesner or bill goldvarg ke bich match main atithi referi ke rup main steve austin ko dikhaaya gaya. aayojan main da rock kaa ab tak ke aakhri match ke saath saath WWE ke saath lesner or goldberg kaa aakhri match dikhaaya gaya. resalmenia se pehle kii raat main yojit vaarshik indection sho ke dauraan vaarshik WWE haul of fem ko punah shuru kiya gaya.
 resalmenia 21 main, mani in da bank leader match kii shuruyaat sicks main leader match ke rup main kii gayi jisame ring ke upar ek suutakes latakaaya gaya jisame ek kantrect tha jiske anusaar jeetne vaale ro pratibhaagi ko uski pasand ke anusaar ek varsh se agle resalmenia tak kisi bhi sthaan or samay par world tital kitaab match main bhaag lene kii guarantee thi. pramukh spardhaon main, WWE championships or world haiviveet championships par kramshah jon seenaa or batista ne kabza kiya jinhonen apane maichon main kramshah jon "bradshow" lefield or tripal ech ko haraaya. aayojan main ek saal ke lambey antaraal ke baad stone cold steve austin kii bhi vaapasi vishesh rup se pradarshit he, jabaki ek prashansit match main court angel ne shaun maaikel ko haraaya. resalmenia 22 main ek sicks main interpromotional match ke rup main da mani in da bank leader match bhi dikhaaya gaya jahaan vijeta apni pasand ke anusaar brands kii parvaah kiye bagaiyr koi bhi world tital match praapt kar sakate the. da mani in da bank leader match seedhi match resalmenia 23 main ek interpromotional at main match ke rup main punah yojit kiya gaya jisamen ro, ECW or smacdown ke sitaron ne bhaag liya. jon seenaa ne resalmenia 22 or 23 donon main apana WWE championships kitaab barkaraar rakhaa jabaki isi aayojan main re mistirio or andertekar ne kramshah world haiviveet championships jiti. donald trump kaa pratinidhitiv karte hue ECW world champion bobi leshley ne umaga ko haraaya jo vins mekamahon kaa pratinidhitiv kar rahaa tha, ek aisa match jise "arbapatiyon kii ladaai" kaa naam diya gaya or resalmenia 23 main stone cold steve austin dwaara manamaane dhang se sanchalit kiya gaya.
 resalmenia XXIV main, shaun maaikel ne rik flair ko ek prashansit match main haraaya, jabaki mani in da bank leader match ko interpromotional match ke rup main dikhaaya gaya jisame ro, ECW or smack down ke saat pratibhaagi the. resalmenia aayojan main pehali baar ECW championships kaa kitaab bachaate hue record 8 second main ken naye ECW champion ke rup main ubhara, jabaki rendi orton ne WWE championships ko barkaraar rakhaa or andertekar ne age ko haraa kar lagaataar dusre saal world haiviveet championships jiti. ek aisa muqabla jise media main atyadhik kawarej mili, main boxing world champion floid mewedar junior ne da big sho ko haraaya. yah aayojan outdoor sthal ke rup main doosraa resalmenia tha. resalmenia XXV main kris jerrico ne WWE haul of da fame rody pyper, jimi snuka or ricki steambot ko ek aise match main haraaya jisame rik flair or abhinayta miki rurk dikhaai die. resalmenia X8 main pehali baar WWE intercantinental championships kaa bachaav hua jab re mistirio ne jon "bradsha" lefield ko haraaya. world haiviveet championships ke liye jon seenaa ne age or da big sho ko haraaya, jabaki tripal ech ne rendi orton ke khilaaf WWE championships kaa kitaab barkaraar rakhaa.
 resalmenia XXVI main, shaun maaikel kaa peshewar kushti karier samaapt ho gaya jab usne pichley varsh ke muqablon ke baad ek prashansit ri-match main da andertekar kaa saamana kiya. andertekar kii jeet se unka resalmenia jeet/haar kaa record 18-0 tak pahunch gaya. aayojan main jon seenaa ne WWE championships jiti or kris jerrico ne age ke khilaaf world haiviveet championships kaa kitaab barkaraar rakhaa. montrial scrujob spardhaa ke 12 se bhi adhik saalon ke baad brett heart kii vaapasi ke baad, brett heart ne vins mekamahon ko no holds board match main haraaya jisame heart raceling femily ke sadsy upasthit the.
 varshon se, resalmenia ne kai staron par celibrity ko dikhaaya he.
 pehle resalmenia kii mukhya spardhaa pramukh hastiyon se ati padi thi. ring udghoshak purv yenkis menager billy martin the, liberace timekeeper the or vishesh rup se uksaane ke liye mohammad ali the. iske alaava, tivi sho da e team ke mistar tii ne pramukh muqablon main tag team sahyogi halk hogan ke saath bhaag liya.
 resalmenia XIV main mike tyson vishesh atithi enforcer ke rup main shaun maaikel or steve austin ke bich hone vaale WWF championships match ke bich dikhaai die. tyson ne teen tak ginati gini or austin ko kitaab dete hue samaapti kii. 
 kuch mashhoor hastiyon ne ring main pahalawaanon kaa saath diya he jaise syndi lupar, ozi osborn, aais tii, elice kupar, pamela anderson tathaa jeni mekkaarthi . resalmenia 23, main donald trump ne apni paanchavin resalmenia upasthiti darj karte hue umaga ke khilaaf bobi leshley kaa prabandhan kiya jisame haarane vaale menager ko apana sar mundvana tha.
 aayojan main sangeet kaa sidhaa prasaaran bhi hota he. re charles, eritha franklin, gledis nite, robert gaulet, vili nelson, reba mcentry, little richard, da DX band, boij II main, ashaanti, boij choir of harlem, micel williams, jon legend, nichol sherzinger or funtasia barino ne sho se pehle apni apni baar "america da beautiphul" gaaya he or drowning pool dwaara.
 kai avasaron par prasiddh hastiyaan swayan bhi match main bhaag leti hai. resalmenia 2 ke teen pramukh muqablon main se ek 20 main battle royal pitting tha, jisame kai NFL sitare kushti ke sitaron ke saamne the, jisame antat andre da joint ne jeet praapt kii. laurence tailor ko ekal match main bam bam bigelo kaa saamana karnaa padaa or baaju ke duusari rassi se baahar latakane ke baad jeeta. mistar tii ne do match khele, pehle resalmenia main halk hogan ke saath team bana kar paul orndrof or rody piper kaa saamana kiya jiske baad resalmenia 2 main rody piper ke khilaaf boxing match khelaa. mistar tii ne donon match jite jisame doosraa ayogyata ke aadhaar par tha. peshewar boxr butterbin ko bart gan dwaara aal boxing match ke liye resalmenia XV main chunauti di gayi thi. butterbin ne gan ko 30 second main nokout dwaara haraa diya. resalmenia 21 main big sho ne sumo kushti champion acebono kaa ek sumo mukaable main saamana kiya. resalmenia XXIV main big sho peshewar veltervet boxr floid "mani" memvedar se bhi ladaa. 
 resalmenia XIV, XV or 2000, main peet roj ken se ek jhagade main shaamil ho gaya jiske ant main antat roj ken ke haathon tombastone pyledriver yaa chokaslam dwaara haar gaya. san diego chikan kaa bhi istemaal is jhagade main kiya gaya tha.
 resalmenia main apane pradarshan ke ek bhaag ke rup main peet rose or wiliam "refricrator" peri ko celibrity wing ke tahat WWE haul of fame main shaamil kiya gaya.
 resalmenia XXV main, miki rork ne kris jerrico ke WWE ke mahaarathiyon ke khilaaf handicap alimination match ko ek darshak ke rup main dekha. jerrico dwaara teen mahaarathiyon ko haraane ke baad, rork ko jerrico ne ring main bulaayaa. rork ne ulte haath ke mukke se jabade par waar kar ke jerrico ko gira diya. aayojan main kid rock dwaara ek 10 minute kaa mini concert bhi tha jisane apane geeton kii shrankhla pesh kii.
