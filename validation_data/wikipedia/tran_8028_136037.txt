 vachaspati mishra bharat ke darshanik the jinhone advait veydant kaa bhaamati naamak sampradaay sthapit kiya. vachaspati mishra ne navy-nyaay darshan par aarmabhik kaary bhi kiya jise mithila ke 13ve shatee ke gangesh upaadhyaay ne aage badhaayaa.
 vachaspati mishra pratham mithila ke brahman the jo bharat or nepal seemaa ke nikat madhubani ke paas andhrathadhi gaanv ke nivasee the. inhone vaisheshik darshan ke atirikt anya sabhi paancho aastik darshanon par tikaa likhi he. unke jivan kaa vrittant bahut kuch nasht ho chukaa he. aisa mana jaataa he ki unki ek kriti kaa naam unki patni bhaamati ke naam par rakhaa he.
 vachaspati mishra pratham ne us samay kii hinduon ke lagbhag sabhi pramukh darshanik sampradaayon kii pramukh kritiyon par bhashya likhen hai. iske atirikt tatvabindu naamak ek mul granth bhi likhaa he jo bhashya nahin he.
