 shatobriaan france ke lekhak, rajaneta, itihaaskaar tathaa rajanyik the. ve francisi sahitya main romantisism ke sansthaapak maane jate hai. 
 shatobriaan kaa janm 'se maalon' main ek pracheen kuleen parivaar main hua tha. aap apane saral kintu udhaas pita, khinn aswasth mata, lucil naamak dharmik kintu snayudurbal bahan, bretan ke vanya drishy tathaa samudr se prabhavit hue. santapt yuvavastha; nirvaasan evam nirdhantaa main england main pravaas; america, jerusalem, misr tathaa spain kii yatraayen, france main saahitiyik evam raajnneetik jivan tathaa avkashgrahan aapke jivan ke pramukh paksh hai. aapaki mitrataa fauntan tathaa juber naamak lekhakon or maadaam rekamiyar tathaa maadaam da boma naamak saamaajik mahilaon se thi. aapaki pustak 'l jeni du christianism' sandhi-diwas 1802 ke suavasar par prakashit hokar france main catholic mat kii pun:sthapana main sahaayak hui. aapaki pustika 'da bunaparth e de burbaan' france main mitrarashtron ke pravesh ke din prakashit hui. aapane napolian kii adheenta main tathaa burbaan parivaar main kai padon par kaary kiya; kintu apni darpapurna swatantra prakruti ke kaaran aapako inhen tyaagna padaa. san 1811 main aap academi ke sadsy chune gaye. san 1830 main aapane raajnneeti se avkaash grahan kiya.
 aapaki pustaken aapke vyaktitv kaa pratibimb hai. 'esi surle revolushian' tarun-paanditya-purna granth he. 'l jenidu christianism' naamak pustak main aapaki aarambhik naastikta ke praayshchitt, isai mat ke samarthan, saundarya siddhant evam naveen samalochana kaa mishran he. 'atila' or 'rane' nae yug ke do upanyaas hai. 'atila' romentic paddati kaa ek videshi upanyaas he. aapaki sarvotkrisht kriti 'rane' main ek khinn, parishrant evam viplavakaari romentic vir kaa chithran he. yah shiller or vaayran ke 'child herold' ke bich kii kadi he. 'le marthir' main prakrutipujak aadarshon kii ucchata dikhaai gai he. yah ek gadyatmak mahakavy he; kintu aapaki pratibha adhiktar itihasonmukhi he. aapane agrenji sahitya par ek niabandh, yatravarnan, 'laa vi da raanse' tathaa aitihaasik granth likhe; or 'paradise lost' kaa anuwaad kiya. bhavy chithran evam upakthaaon se paripurna aapkaa sarvshereshth granth 'memvaar dutra tomb' kaa ek praytn tha.
 shatobriaan vichaarak nahin the, varan bhavy varnanon ke liye prasiddh ek kalakaar the. aapaki ahanmanyta sabhi rachanaaon main parilakshith hoti he. aapane buddhivaadee yug main ant tathaa romentic yug ke anrabh kii ghoshnaa kii. inke romentisism ke mukhya tatv he :- prakruti evam aatma kii puja, pragitatmakataa, bhavukta ityadi. inhone aise gadhy kii rachana kii jisamen keval spashtataa evam yatharthata ke sthaan par komalta evam lacheelaapan he. shatobriaan kaa drushtikon saundarya pradhan tha. aapane kavita, upanyaas, itihaas tathaa samalochna ke kshetron main french sahitya ko prabhavit kiya.
