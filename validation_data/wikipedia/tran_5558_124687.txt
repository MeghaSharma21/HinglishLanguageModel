 alagadha bharat ke pashchim bengal rajya ke kalimpong zile main kalimpong nagar se 15 kilometre purv sthit ek basti he. yah 1,780 meter kii oonchaai par he or san 2001 main isaki jansankhya lagbhag 3,000 thi. alagadha ke sameep hi damsang gadhi naamak ek qila he jiskaa nirmaan san 1690 main lepcha raja shri gyabo achook ne kiya tha. har varsh 20 disambar ko aasapaas ke kshetron se lepcha samudaay ke log unka janmparv manaane aate hai.
