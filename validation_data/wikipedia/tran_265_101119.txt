 kadlor bharat kaa ek nagar he jo tamilnadu rajya ke kadlur jile kaa mukhyalay he. yah nagar madras nagar se 190 kimi tathaa pandicherry se 19 kimi kii duuri par madras-trichanapalli sadak par sthit he. 
 yahan kii jalwaayu acchhee he. yah aasapaas ke jilon kaa swasthyavardhak centre he. ponaire tathaa gadilam nadiyaan is nagar se bahati hui samudr main girti hai. iskaa naam sambhavat: 'kudal-ur' kaa vikrut rup he, jiskaa arth do nadiyon kaa sangam he. 1884 i. main baadh kaa pani nagar ke bich se bahane laga tha. yahan se ganna or telhan baahar bheja jaataa he. yah nagar sant david ke kile ke liye prakhyat he jo khandhar ke rup main gadilam nadi ke kinare sthit he. is kile kaa nirmaan ek hindu vyapaaree ne karaayaa tha. san 1677 i. main yah shivaji ke haath main chalaa aayaa. tab se iskaa naam 'sant david kaa kila' ho gaya. san 1756 i. main robert clive yahan kaa garvanar niyukt kiya gaya. 1758 i. main francisiyon ne isko apane adhikaar main kar liya. 1785 i. main yah pun: angrejon ke haath main chalaa aayaa. baftaa kii bunai yahan kaa mukhya udyog he. jail ke kaidi dari, gamche tathaa anya sooti kapade bunate hai. yahan do mahavidhyalay hai.
