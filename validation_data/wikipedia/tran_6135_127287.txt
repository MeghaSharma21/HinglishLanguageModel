 
 
 edinburgh kasal athwa edinburgh kaa kila, scotland ke edinburgh shahar ke madhya main kasal rock naamak pahari par sthit ek giridurg he. aitihaasik taur par, is kile ke sthaan par kamasekam 12veen sadi se ek shahi kile ke hone ke pramaan hai. yah kila san 1633 tak shahi nivas ke rup main satyapit rahaa tha. 15veen sadi se, is kile kii rihaishi mahatv ghatane lagi or 17veen sadi se ise mukhyatah ek sainya thikaane ke rup main istemaal kiya jane laga. 19veen sadi ke baad se is kile ke aitihaasik mahatv ke maddenzar ise scotland ke mahatvapurn rashtriya dharohar kr rup main dekha jane laga, or tatpashchaat is kile par anek punahsthaapan pariyojnaayen chalaai gayin. scotian rajshahi ke sabase ahm gadhon main se ek hone ke kaaran, is kile kaa anek aitihaasik yudhon or muthbhedon main ahm bhoomika rahi he, jiname scottish swatantraaa yuddh or 1745 kii jaikobiyai jaagran shaamil hai. is kile par kiye gaye shodhon se pataa chalata he kii is kile ke 1100 varshon ke itihaas main is par kamasekam 26 muhaasare lagaaye gaye the. aaj yah kila scotland kaa sabase lokapriy paryaton sthal he. 2013 main is kile ko dekhne ke liye kul 14 lakh aagatuk aaye the. is kile ko edinburgh antarrashtriya mahotsav ke ageemburg military tatu ke kaarykram sthal ke rup main upyog kiya jaataa he, jisame prayek varsh edinburgh, euke tathaa videshon se bhi lakhon log aate hai. isaki aitihaarik mahatta or lokapriyata ke maddenzar is kile ko kai myanon main edinburgh shahar tathaa puure scotland kaa pratik bhi mana jaataa he. is kile ke pratibimb ko edimbirag nagar parishad tathaa edinburgh vishvavidhyalay ke kulankon main bhi dekha ja saktaa he.
 kile ke gine-chune bhawan hai jo 16veen sadi se purvatith hai, kyonki laing ke muhasire ke samay topon kii golabari dwaara is kile ke adhikansh imaaraton ko dhwast kar diya gaya tha. is sandarbh main sabase vishisht apavaadatmak bhawan saint margrets chapel he, joki 12veen saad se aabaad he, or ise aksar edinburgh kii sabase puraatan sanrachana mana jaataa he. iske alaava shahi mahal or uskaa "great haul", donon hin us avrodh se purvatith hai, haalanki, great haul ke interiars mul madhya-victorian kaal se kaafii parivartit ho chuke hai. iske alaava yah kila, scotland kii rashtriya yuddh smriti, rashtriya yuddh sangrahalay tathaa scotian rajparidhaanon kii bhi mezbaani karata he. aaj bhi is kile ke kuch hisson main british sena kii upasthiti he, parantu ab sena kii upasthiti keval prashasnic or paaramparik he.
