 hindu dharmagranthon main asur ve log hai jo 'sur' se sangharsh karte hai. dharmagranthon main unhen shaktishaalee, atimanviya, ardhdevon ke rup main chitrit kiya gaya he. ye achche or bure donon gunon vaale hai. achche gunon vaale asuron ko 'aditya' tathaa bure gunon se yukt asuron ko 'daanav' kaha gaya he. 
 'asur' shabd kaa prayog rugveda main lagbhag 105 baar hua he. usamen 90 sthanon par iskaa prayog 'shobhan' arth main kiya gaya he or keval 15 sthalon par yah 'devataaon ke shatru' kaa vaachak he. 'asur' kaa vyutpattilabhya arth he- pranavant, pranashakti sampann or is prakaar yah vaidic devon ke ek saamaanya visheshan ke rup main vyavahrut kiya gaya he. visheshat: yah shabd indra, mitra tathaa varun ke saath prayukt hokar unki ek vishisht shakti kaa dyotak he. indra ke to yah vaiyaktik bal kaa suchak he, parantu varun ke saath prayukt hokar yah unke naitik bal athwa shashanbal kaa spashtat: sanket karata he. asur shabd isi udaatt arth main parsiyon ke pradhan devtaa 'ahurmajd' ke naam se vidyaman he. yah shabd us yug kii smriti dilaataa he jab vaidic aaryon tathaa iraniyon ke purvaj ek hi sthaan par nivas kar ek hi devtaa kii upasna main nirat the. ananthar aaryon kii in donon shakhaon main kisi agyaat virodh ke kaaran foot padi gai. falat: vaidic aaryon ne 'na sur: asur:' yah naveen vyutpatti maanakar asur kaa prayog daityon ke liye karnaa aarambh kiya or udhar iraniyon ne bhi dev shabd kaa apane dharm ke daanvon ke liye prayog karnaa shuru kiya. falat: vaidic 'vrutraghn' avastaa main 'verthroghn' ke rup main ek vishisht daitya kaa vaachak ban gaya tathaa iraniyon kaa 'asur' shabd pipru aadi devvirohi daanvon ke liye rugveda main prayukt hua jinhen indra ne apane vajra se maar daalaa tha. . 
 shatapath brahman kii manyata he ki asur devdrushti se apbhrasht bhashaa kaa prayog karte hai . patanjali ne apane 'mahabhashya' ke paspashahnik main shatapath ke is vaakya ko udhrut kiya he. shabar swami ne 'pick', 'nem', 'tamras' aadi shabdon ko asuri bhashaa kaa shabd mana he. aaryon ke aath vivaahon main 'aasur vivaah' kaa sambandh asuron se mana jaataa he. puraanon tathaa avaantar sahitya main 'asur' ek swar se daityon kaa hi vaachak mana gaya he.
