 nepolian kii samrajyavaadee aakanksha tathaa mahadwipiya vyavastha ke prashn par rus ke saath uske sambandh punah bighad gaye. falswaroop 1812 i. main nepolian ne rus par akraman kar diya. france main ise 'roosi abhiyaan' kaha jaataa he jabaki rus main ise '1812 kaa deshbhaktipurna yuddh'.
 nepolian kii sena ne nomen nadi paar kar rus kii seemaa main pravesh kiya. roosi senaaon ne fasalon, bhandaron ko nasht karte hue peeche hatane tathaa chhapamar hamale karte rahane kii niti apanaai. anek kathinaayeeyon kaa saamana karte hue kisi tarah jab nepolian masco pahucha to usne puure shahar ko viraan paaya. vastav main abhi tak francisi sena apana kharch parajeet pradeshon se nikaalati thi kintu is yuddh paddati or ranneeti se yah sambhav nahin ho sakaa. nepolian ko aashaa thi ki jaar aatmasamarpan kar degaa parantu jaar aatmasamarpan ke bajaay siberia chalaa gaya. nepolian mascon main lagbhag do maheene tak shanti prastaav kii pratiksha main rahaa, tabhi uske sainikon ke liye khaadya saamagree kaa sankat paidaa ho gaya tathaa mahamari fail gai. atah nepolian ko laachaar hokar moscow se lautaanaa padaa. sena ko bhukh, thand or roosiyon ne pareshaan kiya. is tarah jab nepolian ne rus kii seemaa chodi tab vah apane 6 lakh main se 5 lakh sainik kho chukaa tha. is tarah se yah abhiyaan nirarthak siddh hua. isaki asafalataa kaa kaaran nepolian kii hathdharmita or roosi sainikon kii peeche hatane kii kootneeti ko na samaj paanaa tha.
