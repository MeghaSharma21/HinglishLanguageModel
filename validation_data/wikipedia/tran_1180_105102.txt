 bharti antarctic main bharat dwaara sthapit anusandhan centre kaa naam he. yah antarctic main bharat dwaara sthapit teesraa anusandhan centre he. iske purv dakshin gangotri or maitri anusandhan centre sthapit kiye ja chuke hai. vartmaan barf main daw jane ke kaaran dakshin gangotri ko supplai base ke rup main prayukt ho rahaa he. jab ki maitri anusandhan centre chaalu he. maitri or bharti anusandhan centre sthaayee rup se kaam kar rahe hai.
 purvi antarctic kii larseman hills or penisulas hills ke bich patharilii bhoomi par sthit he.yah sthal prakritik rup se ek grenite pahari he. jo samudr tal se lagbhag 90 meter unchaa tower jaisaa dikhaai padataa he. yah anusandhan centre maitri anusandhan kendra se lagbhag 3,000 kilometre dur sthit he. australia, asia, africa, dakshin america or bharat ek saath milakar is super-mahadweep kaa ek hissaa ban gaye hai. bharat ke purvi tat ke gondvanaland se antartica ke tat se tulana kii ja sakati he. 
 is anusandhan centre kii sthapana 18/03/2012 main hui thi.

 antartica main bharat kii nadiyon ke basin kii chattaanon or khanij padaarthon se larsman pahari kii chattaanon or khanij padaarthon kaa tulanaatmak adhyayan. dhruviya vigyaan or krayosephere, main anusandhan gatividhiyaan or himalay ke glaciaron se tulanaatmak adhyayan shaamil.
