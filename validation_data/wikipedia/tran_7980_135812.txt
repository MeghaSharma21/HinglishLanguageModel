 new taipe shahar, taiwan kaa ek vishesh nagar palika or sabase adhik aabaadi vaala shahar he. uttari taiwan main sthit is shahar main, dweep kii uttari tat rekha kaa ek bada hissaa shaamil he or taipe basin se ghira hua he, jo ise kaooshung ke peeche kshetra dwaara duusari sabase badi vishesh nagar palika banaata he. new taipei shahar purvottar se kelang, dakshin purv main yilan county or dakshin-pashchim main taoyuan se ghira hua he. yah puri tarah se taipe se ghira hua he. bankiyo jila isaki nagarpalika seat or sabase bada vanijyik kshetra he. 2010 tak, kshetra jo vartmaan main vartmaan taipe shahar se mail khaataa he use taipe county ke naam se janaa jaataa tha.
 2010 main shahar ke rup main padonnati se pehle new taipe shahar ko taipe county ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. county kii aabaadi taipe shahar se aage nikalne ke baad, yah nirnay liya gaya ki county ko shahar kii sthiti main padonnat kiya janaa chahie lekin iskaa naam badalakar "taipe shahar" nahin kiya ja saktaa tha.
 naye astitv kaa naam pehali baar angreji main pinyin romanikaran ke madhyam se sinbei ke rup main prastut kiya gaya tha, lekin shahar ke pehle meyar chunaav ke donon ummidavaaron ne is naam kaa virodh kiya. natijatan, saarvajanik raay kaa hawala dete hue, nirvaachit mahayor, erik chu ne aantarik mantralay se angreji main "new taipe shahar" ke rup main naam dene ke liye anurodh kiya, jise sweekruti mil gai. yah pratipaadan 31 disambar 2010 ko aadhikarik ban gaya.
 puratathvik abhilekh bataate hai ki new taipei shahar niolithik kaal ke baad se basaa hua he. bali jile main khudaai ke dauraan mili kalakriti, 7000 se 4700 isa purv tak ke he. new taipei shahar ke aas-paas kaa kshetra, ketaglan maidani adivaasiyon kaa nivas sthal tha, or saboot bataate hai ki atoyal prajati wooli jile main rahati thi. 1620 ke shuruuaat se yahan mukhya bhoomi china ke logon kaa pravaas armbh hua, or sthaneey janajatiyon ko pahaad kshetron main dhakel diya gaya. varshon se, kai adivasee aam jansankhya main aatmsaat ho gaye hai.
 taiwan main ching rajvansh ke shashan ke dauraan, haan chini log 1694 main new taipei shahar ke rup main namit kshetra main basane lage or mukhya bhoomi china se apravasiyon kii sankhya main or vruddhi ho gai. dashakon ke vikaas or samruddhi ke baad, tamsui 1850 tak ek antarrashtriya vanijyik bandargah ban gaya tha. is kshetra main british vaanijya duutavas or dukaan sthapit kiye gaye the, jisase sthaneey chaay vyapaar ko badhaava dene main madad mili, jiske parinamswaroop europe main bade paimaane par chaay ke pattee ke niryaat hone lage. 1875 main, shen baosen ne taipe praant kii sthapana kii maang kii. fuziyaan-taiwan-praant kii ghoshnaa 1887 main hui thi or vartmaan main new taipe shahar kshetra ko taipe prifecture ke adhikaar kshetra main rakhaa gaya tha.
 1895 main, taiwan, ching samrajya se nikal kar japani samrajya ke haath main aa gaya. japani shashan ke dauraan, new taipe shahar kshetra, taihoku praant ke antargat aadhunik taipe, kelang or yilan county ke saath-saath prashasit kiya jaataa tha. kelang mountain main sone or anya khanij kii khoj kii gai, jo is kshetra main khanan udyog ko kaaphi badhaava mila tha. oktober 1896 main, japani sarkaar ne kelang mountain ke aasapaas khanan kshetra ko do jilon main vibhajit kiya: ek purvi jila, jise kinkseki ke rup main namit kiya gaya tha, or pashchimi jila, jise kuphun ke rup main namit kiya gaya tha. donon jilon ab ruifang jile ke kuch hisson hai. is kshetra main khanan karne ke liye sthaneey taiwan khanan companiyon kii bajaay japani companiyon ko khanan adhikaar pradaan kiye gayi the.
 oktober 1945 main japan dwaara taiwan ko chini ganraajya ko saupane ke baad, usee varsh 25 disambar se, vartmaan new taipe shahar kshetra ko taiwan praant ke taipe county naamak ek county ke rup main prashasit kiya jane laga, jisamen benchiao shahar county seat ke rup main tha. july 1949 main, taipe county kaa aakaar kam ho gaya tha jab bitau or shilin township ko nav nirmit kaioshan prashasnic beuro ke adhikaar kshetra main rakh diya gaya, jise baad main yangmingshan prashasnic beuro kaa naam diya gaya. 1 july, 1968 ko, taipe county kaa aakaar 205.16 kimi2 tak kam kar diya gaya tha jab jingmei, mujha, nangong or nehu township, bitau or shilin ke saath, taipe shahar main vilay kar die gaye.
 baad main county main das county niyantrit shahar ; chaar shahari township ; or pandrah gramin township jod diye gayi. ise aage 1,017 gaanvon or 21,683 padoson main baanta gaya tha. agast 1992 main, neigau or dacking stream main taipei shahar or taiwan praant ke bich seemaa rekha ke samaayojan ke kaaran, taipe county kaa kshetra 0.03 kimi2 se kam ho gaya tha. 25 disambar 2010 ko, taipe county ko ek vishesh nagar palika main unnat kiya gaya, jisamen new taipe shahar main 29 jilon main benchiao jila nagarpalika seat ke rup main tha.
 new taipe shahar, taiwan dweep ke uttari sire par sthit he. isamen parvat, pahadiyaan, maidani ilaakon or ghati samet ek vividh sansthiti ke saath ek vishaal kshetra shaamil he. uttari bhaag main sthit 120 kimi lambii samudr tat, khubasurat tatarekha or samudr taton se paripurna he. tamsui nadi new taipei shahar ke madhyam se bahane vaali mukhya nadi he. anya badi sahaayak nadiyon main sindain, keelung or dahan nadiyaan hai, jinamein se kuch sahaayak nadiyon ke kinare park kaa nirmaan kiya gaya he. shahar main sabase oonchi chotii mount jhuji he, jo 1,094 meter lambii he or sanjhi jile main sthit he.
 shahar main jalwaayu ardra monsoon ke saath ek ardra ushnkaribandheey ke rup main varnit kiya ja saktaa he jahaan paryaapt varsha puure varsh samaan rup se vitarit hoti he. mausam main taapmaana bhinnataayen dekhne ko milti hai, haalanki taapmaana aamtaur par puure saal garm rahata he, haalanki sardiyon ke maheenon main thandi hawa ke jhapeton se taapmaana kabhi-kabhi 10 degree celsius tak neeche gir jaataa he. janvari aamtaur par sabase acha maheenaa or july aamtaur par sabase garm hota he.
 new taipei shahar, china ganraajya kii centre sarkaar ke antargat ek vishesh nagarpalika he. new taipe shahari sarkaar kii adhyakshata, nirvaachit mahayor karata he or iskaa mukhyalay benchiao jile main new taipe city haul main he. new taipe shahar ke pehle or vartmaan mahayor erik chu he.
 new taipe shahar, 28 jilon or 1 parvatiy swaayat jila ko niyantrit karata he. up-shahar, 1,017 gaanv se milakar bane hai, jo aage 21,683 pados main vibhajit hai. nagarpalika seat benchiao jile main sthit he. 
 rajdhani shahar taipe se isaki nikatataa ke kaaran centre sarkaar kii kai agenciyaan new taipei shahar main sthit hai.
 new taipe shahar kii anumaanit jansankhya lagbhag 39 lakh he. new taipei shahar ke 80% se adhik nivasee pehle 10 jilon main rahete the jo purv main county niyantrit shahar the, jo ki kshetra kaa ek chatha hissaa he. 28.80% nivasee taipe shahar se is kshetra main aa gayi gaye he. new taipei shahar main rahane vaali lagbhag 70% aabaadi taiwan ke vibhinn hisson se aate he, or shahar main rahane vaale logon main lagbhag 73,000 videshi hai, taiwan main videshi nivasee aabaadi ke maamle main new taipe shahar teesri sabase badi nagar palika he.
 yah shahar 952 panjikrut mandiron or 160 buddha-taowadi mandiron or 3,000 se adhik taowadi mandiron sahit 120 charchon kaa ghar he. is shahar main paanch pramukh buddha math bhi hai, jaise ki jinshaan jile main dharm drum mountain or gonglio jile main ling-jiu mountain math. ausatan, shahar ke chaaron ore prayek varg kilometre main do puja sthal sthit hai. sijhi jila or sencia jile main panjikrut mandiron kii sabase adhik sankhya he, jabaki wooli gilen main sabase kam he. yonge jile main new taipei shahar vishwa dharm sangrahalay sthit he.
 apane samrik sthaan ke kaaran, taipe ke baad new taipe shahar vyapaar udyog kaa doosraa pramukh shahar he, 250,000 se adhik nijee swaamitv vaali companiyon or 20,000 kaarkaanon main $1.8 trillian kii kul puunji ke saath lagbhag paanch audyogic park faile hue hai. kai ucch praudyogiki udyog, seva udyog or paryaton udyog bhi hai, jo taiwan ke sakal gharelu utpaada main ek badi maatra main yogadan karte hai. shahar ke paanch pramukh udyogon main soochna praudyogiki, doorsanchaar, digital saamagree, jaev praudyogiki or parishuddhta upkaran hai. shahar iti utpaada utpaadan maatra ke maamle main vaishvik bazaar main sheersh teen shaharon main se ek he, madraboard, notebook, alsidi monitor or siarti monitor jaise utpaadon ke vaishvik bazaar main yah 50% se adhik hissedari rakhata he.
 iske alaava new taipe shahar kai saanskrithik or rachanaatmak udyogon se bharaa hua he, jaise yingaj jile main mitti ke baratan, tamasui jile main liuli udyog, zinzuang jile main dhol udyog, sanksia jile main rongan udyog, ruifang jile main nobel dhaatu prasanskaran udyog, hawa main udne vaali kandil udyog pingzi jila main aadi. taiwan main film udyogon ke vikaas ke liye taiwan film samskruti centre kaa nirmaan singjhuan jile main kiya janaa he. gyaan udyog park ko digital saamagree companiyon ke clustering or vistaar ko protsaahit karne ke liye usee jile main bhi nirmaan karne kii yojana banaai gai he jisase shahar ko abhaasi digital manoranjan park main badalane main madad milegi.
 bali jile main sthit taipe bandargah main 80,000 ton vajan vaale container jahaajon ke avaagaman or salanaa 2 lakh se adhik tiiu parivahan karne kii kshamta he. tamsui jile main tamasui machuaare kaa ghat machhlee pakadaney kii naukaaon ke saath-saath darshneey sthalon kii yatra or avkaash ke liye mukhya bandargah ke rup main kaary karata he.
 new taipe shahar main shiksha, new taipe shahar sarkaar ke shiksha vibhaag dwaara shasit kiya jaataa he. shahar kii aabaadi behad shikshit he, 38% se adhik log ucch shikshit he. 1914 main sthapit rashtriya taiwan pustkaalay, taiwan kaa sabase purana saarvajanik pustkaalay he, yah jhonge jile main sthit he.
 new taipe shahar main paryatakon ke liye aitihaasik, prakritik or saanskrithik aakarshan kii ek vistrut shrinkhalaa he. shahar main paryaton se sambandhit udyog new taipe shahari sarkaar ke paryaton or yatra vibhaag dwaara prashasit hote hai.
 aitihaasik aakarshanon main bittauzia linehouse, chin paao san, fort santo domingo, hob kila, igon shrin, tamasui old street, lin family haveli or garden, fuguijia linehouse, kep san diego linehouse or tsasui main ching rajvansh avshesh or jiuphen ke puraane khanan kasbon shaamil hai.
 sabase prasiddh mandir sensia jile kaa jushi mandir he. new taipe shahar niyamit rup se 5000 se adhik vaarshik kala, sangeet or saanskrithik tyohaaron kaa aayojan karata he, jaise gonglio jile main hohyaan rock festival, pingzi jile main yojit laalaten mahotsav aadi.

 yah kshetra benchiao stesan ke madhyam se taiwan hai speed rail se judaa hua he, jo taiwan railway prashasan or taipe metro ke saath ek intermodal stesan he.
 tiaare kii yilan line gongliyao, shuangzi or ruifang ke madhyam se chalati he. pashchimi rekha sijhi, benchiao, shuling or ying ke madhyam se chalaati he. pingksi line pingzi ko ruifang se jodatii he.
 taipei metro, tsamui main tamasui line, yonge or jhonge main jhongahe line, sanchong main luzu line or jhulau, zinzuang main zinzuang line, sanchong, or taishan, zindian main zindiyan line, or banan line ke madhyam se kshetra main seva pradaan karti he.
 taiwan taoyuan antarrashtriya hawaii adde ke pravesh emarti system line dwaara sanchong, zinzuang, taishan or linko ko joda gaya he.
 new taipe shahar main ek prasiddh taipe pul he, jo tamsui nadi par bana hua he or taipe ko new taipe shahar se jodta he. new taipe brij ek anya prasiddh pul he.
 hawaii yatayaat, padosi shahar taoyuan main sthit taiwan taoyuan antarrashtriya hawaii adde or taipe ke sanshan hawaii adde dwaara smpann hota he.
