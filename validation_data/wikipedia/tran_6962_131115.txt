 manipal praudyogiki santhaan manipal vishvavidhyalay se sambhddha mahavidhyalay he. isamen ejiniyari or praudyogiki se sambandhit vishyon kii shiksha di jaati he. isamen 16 shaikshanik vibhaag hai. yah santhaan purvasnatak, snaatak tathaa shodh digriyan pradaan karata he. isaki sthapana 1957 main hui thi. yah santhaan karnataka ke manipal main sthit he tathaa bharat ke sabase puraane swavittaposhit mahavidhyalayon main se ek he.
