 00chrm.jpgpaMdiwa sunderlaal sharma, chhattisgarh main jan jaagran tathaa saamaajik kranti ke agradoot the. ve kavi, saamaajik kaaryakarta, samajasevak, itihaaskaar, swatantraaa-sangram senaanee tathaa bahumukhi pratibha ke dhani the. unhen 'chhattisgarh kaa gandhi' kaha jaataa he. unke sammaan main unke naam par pandit sundarlal sharma mukt vishvavidhyalay, chhattisgarh kii sthapana kii gai he.
 21 disambar 1881 ko rajim ke nikat mahanadi ke tat par based gram chandrasur main aapkaa janm hua.aapke pita kaa naam jaglaal tiwari tha or aapaki mata kaa naam devamati tha. aapaki schuli shiksha praathmik str tak hui or aage ghar par hi swadhyay se aapane sanskrit, bangala, oriya bhashaaen sikh leen. kishoravastha se aap kavitaayen, lekh tathaa naatak likhane lage. kuritiyon ko mitaane ke liye shiksha ke prachaar-prasar ko aavashyak samajhate the. aap hindi bhashaa ke saath chhattisgadhi ko bhi mahatv dete the. aapane hindi tathaa chhattisgadhi main lagbhag 18 granthon kii rachana kii, jisamen chhattisgadhi daan-lila charchit kriti he. inhen chha.ga. kaa pratham swapndrushtaa vah sankalpana car kaha jaataa he. aapane chha.ga. main dularava patrika or hindi main krishna janmasthan patrika likhaa. 
 19 veen sadi ke antim charan main desh main rajnatik or saanskrithik chetana kii lehren uth rahi thi. samaaj sudhaarkon, chintakon tathaa deshbhakton ne parivartan ke is daur main samaaj ko nayi soch or disha di. chhattisgarh main aapane saamaajik chetana kaa swar ghar-ghar pahunchane main avismaraneey kaary kiya. aap rashtriya krishak andolan, madhnishedh, adivasee andolan, swadeshi andolan 
 jude or swatantraaa ke yagyavedi par apana sarvasv arpit kar diya.
 chhattisgarh ke gramin anchalon main vyaapt rudhivadita, andhvishwaas, asprishyata tathaa kuritiyon ko dur karne ke liye aapane athak prayaas kiya. aapke harijanoddhar kaary kii prashansa mahatma gandhi ne mukt kanth se karte hue, is kaary main aapako guru mana tha. 1920 main dhamtari ke paas condel nahar satyagrah aapke netritv main safal rahaa. aapke prayaason se hi mahatma gandhi 20 disambar 1920 ko pehali baar raipur aae.
 asahyog andolan ke dauraan chhattisgarh se jail jane vaale vyaktiyon main aap pramukh the. jivan-paryant sada jivan, ucch vichaar ke adarsh kaa paalan karte rahe. samaaj seva main rat parishram ke kaaran sharir ksheen ho gaya or 28 disambar 1940 ko aapkaa nidhan hua. chhattisgarh shashan ne unki smriti main sahitya/aanchilek sahitya ke liye pan. sundarlal sharma sammaan sthapit kiya he.
