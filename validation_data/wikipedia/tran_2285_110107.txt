 aberdeen lambey samay se apane 45 parkon or bagichon ke liye prasiddh rahaa he. saath hi, shahar bhar main phulon kii bahaar ke liye bhi yah mashhoor he. shahar main bees lakh gulab, ek crores das lakh dafodills or tees lakh crocus ke paudhe hai. shahar ne royal horticulture sosayadi kaa britain in bloom 'sarvshereshth shahar' award das baar jeeta he, samgr scotland main bloom pratiyogita bees baar or 1968 se harek saal bada shahar shrenee kaa puraskaar jeeta he. nau saal tak lagaataar jeetne ke baad, duusare shaharon ko mauka dene ke liye, aberdeen ko britain in bloom pratiyogita se pratibandhit kar diya gaya. is shahar ne 2006 main scotland in bloom "sarvshereshth shahar" puraskaar ke saath-saath international cities in bloom award bhi jeeta. upnagar dise ne bhi small touns puraskaar jeeta.
 di nadi ke uttari tat par 1899 main duthi park kholaa gaya tha. ise 1881 main sushri elizabeth chrombi duthi ne uphaar main diya tha or unheen ke naam par iskaa naam rakhaa gaya. iskaa bahut bada bagichaa he, ek gulab kaa tila, nauka vihaar sarovar, bandstand or khilne kaa maidan he. saath hi, europe kaa doosraa sabase bada sanlagn david velch winter garden bhi he. shahar se baahar sthit vanachhadit havelhed park bada or vanachhadit he, wanon main saiyr karne waalon, prakrutivaadiyon or picnic ke shaukeenon ke bich yah park lokapriy he. yahaan footbal maidan, do golf course, ek pich or put course or ek ghudsawaari skool bhi hai.
 britain in bloom pratiyogitaaon main aberdeen kii safalataa kaa shrey aksar jaunston gardence ko diya jaataa he, jo shahar ke pashchimi chor par ek hectare kshetra main failaa ek chota-sa park he, jisamen vibhinn prakaar ke anek phool tathaa paudhe lage he, jinaki khubsuurati mashhoor he. 2002 main ise british dweepasmooh main sarvshereshth bageeche kaa khitaab mila.
 kabhi ek nijee ghar ke maidan par bana sitan park saint machar girjaghar ke maidan ke chor par sthit he. giraje ke raste ko anek prakaar ke paudhon se ek aupchaarik style main lokapriy dhang se sajaayaa gaya he. tulanaatmak shaileyon ke saath is park main anya anek kshetra bhi shaamil hai.
 union terrace garden 1879 main kholaa gaya, jo shahar ke centre main sthit he. aberdeen ke centre main union street ko teen taraf se gherte hue yah 2.5 acre kshetra main failaa hua he. park denbern ghati main ek prakritik golakaar rangabhumi kaa nirmaan karata he or yah shahar ke centre main shanti va sukun kaa ek nachlistaan he. is bageeche ke sthaan par ek teen manzili kancreet or ispaat kii imaarat khadi karne ke haal kaa prastaav atyadhik vivaadaaspad sabit hua, jisamen ek vyavsaayik centre bhi banane kaa prastaav shaamil tha.
 ek-duusare ke aasapaas victoria park or westeberne park donon 26 acre kshetra main faile hue hai. victoria park 1871 main kholaa gaya tha. vanaspati-rakshagrih baithne ke kaam aata he or chaudaa prakaar ke grenite se ek fawwara banaaya gaya he, jise grenite polishron tathaa aberdeen ke pramukh nirmaataaon ne janata ko bhent main diya he. uske uttar kii ore westeberne park he jise 1901 main kholaa gaya. ghaas ke bade maidan bhi hai, jinka vyapak rup se khel ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he. indoor or outdoor court vaale bade tennis centre hai, bacchon kaa ek cycle rasta, khel kshetra or ek ghaas bouls lon bhi he.
 deside ve aberdeen kaa ek lokapriy gasht maarg he ise aksar cycle chaalkon or paidal chaalkon dwaara ek khushnuma gasht va saiyr-sapate ke liye istemaal kiya jaataa he. iskaa rasta duthi park se petercolor tak puraane rail line ke kinare se hokar jaataa he.
 formartine and buchan ve bhi ek lokapriy guest maarg he joki puraane formrtine or bachan railway ke maarg se hota hua jaataa he kaaphi kuch dyside ve kii tarah hi iskaa rasta purv railway line ke us se hisse par se gujarata hua jaataa he jahaan par kabhi railway trek hua karata tha. ek hisse par iskaa rasta naishanal cycle network ke raste ke samanaantar bhi gujarata he.
 inke alaava tirbager woods main bhi aise kai raste hai jinhen shahar ke liye istemaal kiya jaataa he. kai aise raaston par jagah-jagah murtiyon bhi dekhi ja sakati he jinhen jagah kii sundarataa nikhaarane ke liye lagaaya gaya he.
