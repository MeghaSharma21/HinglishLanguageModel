 allaah baks sumro, britishkaleen bharat ke jameendaar, sarkari thekedaar, swatantraaa sangram senaanee evam rajaneta the. vah sindh ke sabase shreshth rajya-pramukhon main gine jate hai. unhen shahid ke rup main yaad kiya jaataa he. ve sindh praant ke do baar mukhyamantri or ithtehad party ke sakriy raajnneetik kaaryakarta the. unhone yah pehali baar is pad ko 23 march 1938 se 18 april 1940 tak, jabaki duusari dhara 7 march 1941 ke baad se 14 oktober 1942 tak sambhala tha.
