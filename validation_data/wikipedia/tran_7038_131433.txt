 pariklan vigyaan ) teji se badhata hua anek vidhaon vaala kshetra he jo aadhunik yug main uplabdh unnat pariklan kshamtaaon kaa upyog karke jatil samasyaon ko samajhati tathaa unka samaadhaan karti he. pariklan vigyaan anek vidhaon main failaa hua he kintu iske antargat mukhyatah prakritik pranaaliyon ke modelon kaa vikaas tathaa simulation kiya jaataa he.
