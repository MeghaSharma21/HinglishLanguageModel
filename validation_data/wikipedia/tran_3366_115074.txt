 vishnupurana ke anusaar prithvi kaa varnan is prakaar he.yah varnan shriparashar ji ne shri maitrey rishi se kiya tha. unke anusaar iskaa varnan sau varshon main bhi nahin ho saktaa he. yah keval ati sankshep varnan he.
 yah prithvi saat dwipon main banty hui he. ve dweep es prakaar se hai:-
 ye saaton dweep chaaron ore se kramshah khaare pani, ichuras, madiraa, ghrit, dadhi, dugdh or mithe jal ke saat samudron se ghire hai.ye sabhi dweep ek ke baad ek duusare ko ghere hue bane hai or inhen ghere hue saaton samudr hai. jambudweep in sab ke madhya main sthit he.
 is dweep ke swaami bhavy veeravar the. inke saat putron : jalad, kumaar, sukumaar, marichak, kusumod, maudaaki or mahadrum ke naam sangyanusaar hi iske saat bhaagon ke naam hai. maththe kaa sagar apane se duune vistaar vaale shaak dweep se chaaron ore se ghira hua he. yahaan bhi saat parvat, saat mukhya nadiyaan or saat hi varsh hai.
 udayachal, jalaadhar, raivatak, shyaam, astachal, ambikeya or atisuramya giriraaj kesari naamak saat parvat hai.
 sumumari, kumari, nalini, dhenuka, ichu, venuka or gabhasti naamak saat nadiyaan hai.
 jalad, kumaar, sukumaar, marichak, kusumod, maudaaki or mahadrum.yahaan vang, magadh, maanas or mangad naamak chaar varna hai.
 yahaan ati mahaan shaak vriksh he, jiske vaayu ke sparsh karne se hriday main param ahlad utpann hota he. vastutah shaak aaj ke paalak sam tha.yah dweep apane hi baraabar ke dugdh se bhare samudr se chaaron ore se ghira hua he.yah sagar apane se dugune vistaar vaale pushkar dweep se ghira he.
 bhavishyapurana aadi puraano main ek katha aati he jo mahabharat kaal se sambandhit he. usamen bataaya gaya he ki jab bhagwaan krishna ke putr ko kusht rog kaa srap mila tab bhagwaan nen shakadweep se attharah suryaansh divya brahmanon ko sapariwaar jambudweep ke bharatkhand main apane rajya dwarika le aae jinhone samb kii chikitsa kii. tatpashchaat ve brahman magadh aa gaye. vah prakand sanskrit ke gyani the tathaa chikitsak bhi the. aaj unheen attharah pariwaron ke vanshaj ko shakadwipiya brahman kehete hai.brahmanon kaa bada dal inhe brahman nahi sweekaar karata or hin drushti se dekhtaa he .
