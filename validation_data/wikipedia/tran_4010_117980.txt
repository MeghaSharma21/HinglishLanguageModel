 paanchajnya bhagwaan vishnu kaa shankh he. vishnu ke avatar shrikrishna paanchajnya naamak ek shankh rakhte the aisa varnan mamahabharat main praapt hota he. shrimadbhagavadgita jo ki mahabharat kaa ang he us main vasudev dwaara kurukshetra yuddh ke dauraan iskaa istemaal kiya janaa bataaya gaya he.
 bhagwat puraana ke anusaar sandipani rishi ke ashram main krishna kii shiksha puri hone par unhen guru dakshina lene kaa aagrah kiya. tab rishi ne kaha samudr main duube huye mere putr ko le aao. shri krishna dwaara samudr tat par jaakar shankhaasur ko maarane par uskaa khol shesh rah gaya tha. usee se shankh kii utpatti hui. shaayad usee shankh kaa naam paanchajnya tha.
 paanchajnya vibhinn arthon main prayukt hota he. iske kuch arth is prakaar hai:
 1. shrikrishna ke shankh kaa naam
 2. rashtriya swayamsevak sangh kaa rashtravaadi saptahik hindi patr : paanchajnya 
 3. paanch sarvaadhik pracheen kshatriya jatiyaan
