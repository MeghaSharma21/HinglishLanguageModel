 choumohalla pales hyderabad rajya ke nizaam kaa mahal he. iskaa nirmaan varsh 1869 main 5 wein nizaam afazal ud-daula ke shasankaal ke dauraan hua tha. yah 45 acre ke kshetra main failata he. aaj kii tarik main yah mahal 7 wein nizaam ke pehle pote - mukaram jaah kii sampatti he.
 yah mahal kaa sabase purana hissaa he, or isamen chaar mahal : afjal mahal, mahatab mahal, tehniyat mahal or aftab mahal hai.
 is bhaag main baaraa imam he -jisamen camerun kaa ek prasidd lanbaa galiyara he .
 yun kahen ke yah choumahalla pales kaa dil he. yah hyderaabadi logon main ek ucch sammaan main yojit kiya jaataa he, kyonki yah asaf jaahi rajvansh kaa singhaasana tha isamen prasiddh clock tower, council haul or roshan bangla shaamil hai.
