 kaushitaki brahmanopanishad kaushitaki upanishad kaa puraa naam he. yah ek rigvedic upanishad he.kaushitaki upanishad rugveda ke kaushitaki brahman kaa ansh he. isamen kul chaar adhyay hai. is upanishad main jeevatma or brahmalok, pranopasna, agnihotra, vividh upasnaayen, praantatv kii mahima tathaa surya, chandra, vidyut megh, akaash, vaayu, agni, jal, darpan or pratidhwani main vidyaman chaitanya tatv kii upasna par prakaash daalaa gaya he. ant main 'aatmatatva' ke swaroop or uski upasna se praapt fal par vichaar kiya gaya he. 
 pratham adhyay main gautam rishi evam garg rishi ke praputra chitra ke sanvaadon dwaara 'brahmagyaan' ke liye kiye jane vaale agnihotra tathaa uski falshruti par prakaash daalaa gaya he. jab mrityu ke uparant sadhak brahmalok pahunchataa hai, to uskaa saamana apsaraon or ek vicitra panlag par baithe brahmaji se hota he. vah unase baaten karata he. is vaartalap ko 'paryank-vidya' bhi kehete hai. garg rishi ke praputra, maharishi chitra, yajna karne kaa nishchay karke arun ke putr mahatma uddalak ko pradhan ruthvik ke rup main aamantrit karte hai, parantu muni uddalak swayan na jaakar apane putr shwethaketu ko yajna sampann karaane ke liye bhej dete hai. shwethaketu apane pita kii aagyaanusaar vahaan pahunchakar ek oonche asana par virajman hote hai. tab chitra usase prashn karata he-'he gautamakumar! is lok main koi aisa aavarnayukt sthaan he, jahaan tum mujhe le jaakar rakh sakate ho yaa phir usamen bhi koi aisa sarvatha pruthak or vilakshan aavaran se shunya pad he, jisko jaanakar tum usee lok main mujhe pratishthit kar sakate ho?'
 aisa kahkar shwethaketu yajna kaa asana chhodkar chale gayi or apane pita se prashn kiya-'he pitashri! maharishi chitra ne jo prashn mujhase kiya he, uskaa uttar main kaise duun?' shwethaketu ne chitra kaa prashn apane pita ke saamne doharaa diya. tab uddalak rishi ne kaha-'putr! main bhi iskaa uttar nahin jaanata. ham donon maharishi chitra kii yajyashala main chalakar va iskaa adhyayan karke hi is vidya ko praapt karenge.'tadnantar donon pita-putr prasiddh aaruni muni ke haath se samidha grahan karke jigyaasu-bhav se maharishi chitra ke paas gayi or kaha ki ve vidya-prapti hetu unke paas aaye hai. chitra ne kaha-'he gautam! aap brahmanon main ati poojaneey hai or brahmavidya ke adhikari he; kyonki mere paas aate hue aapke man main apni shreshthata kaa tanik-bhi abhimaan nahin he. main nishchay hi aapako iskaa bodh karaungaa.'maharishi chitra ne kaha-'he vipravar! jo vyakti agnihotradi satkaryon kaa anushthan karne vaale hai, ve sabhi is lok se chandralok, arthath swarglok kii ore gaman karte hai, lekin jo vyakti swargeey sukh ke prati aasakt hokar chandralok sweekaar kar letaa he, uske sabhi punya nasht ho jate hai or vah pun: is dharti par waapas aa girtaa he, arthath uskaa phir se punarjanm ho jaat he. use moksh nahin mil paata.'maharishi chitra ke aisa kehane par gautam muni ne phir poocha-'he bhagwan! mujhe bataayen ki main koun hoon? mujhe is bhavasaagar se paar hone kaa vah upaay bataayen, jisase main samast bhav-bandhanon se mukt ho sakuun?'
 maharishi chitra ne kaha-'he gautam! devyan maarg se jane vaala sadhak kramash: agnilok, vayulok, suryalok, varunalok, indralok va prajapatilok aadi chah lokon main se hota hua 'brahmalok' main pravesh kar paata he. 'brahmalok' ke pravesh dwaar par 'ar' naam kaa ek bada jalashay he. yah jalashay kaam, krodh, moh aadi shatruon dwaara nirtit he. yahaan pal-bhar kaa bhi ahankaar or kaam, krodh, lobh aadi kaa bandhan, sadhak ke sabhi punyon ko nasht kar daalata he. 'isht' kii prapti main yah jalashay sabase badi baadha he, parantu jo ise paar kar letaa he, vah phir se paavan viraja nadi ke kinare pahunch jaataa he. uskaa samast shram or vriddhavastha, viraja nadi ke darshan maatr se hi dur ho jate hai. usamen aage 'ilya' naamak vriksh aata he. yaheen par anek devataaon ke sundar upawanon, udyanon, baawali, coop, sarovar, nadi or jalashay aadi se yukt nagar he, jo viraja nadi or ardhachandrakaar parcote se ghira he. ye sabhi man ko baar-baar mohane ke liye saamne aate hai, kintu jo sadhak inamen lipt na hokar aage bad jaataa he, use saamne hi brahma ji kaa ek vishaal devalayas dikhaai padataa he. is devalayas kaa naam 'aparajita' he. surya kii prakhar rashmiyon se yukt hone ke kaaran ise vijit karnaa atyant kathin he. isaki raksha megh, yajna se uplakshith vaayu tathaa akaash-swaroop indra evam prajapati dwaara kii jaati he, parantu jo unhen parajeet kar letaa he, vah brahmalok main pravesh kar jaataa he. vahaan ek vishaal vaibhav-sampann sabha-mandap ke madhya 'vichkshna' naamak vedi par sarvashaktimaan pranaswaroop brahma ji ek ati sundar singhaasana par virajman dikhaai padate hai. vishwa janani amba or ambavayavi naamak apsaraayein unki seva main rat hai, jo brahmavetta sadhak kaa swaagat karti hai or use alankrita karke brahma ji ke sammukh upasthit karti hai.'is upanishad main ek atyant sundar roopak baandhakar devyan maarg se brahmalok tak pahunchane kaa maarg dikhaaya gaya he. yaheen par rishi kahata he ki rath main baithkar yatra karne vaala purush, jis prakaar rath ke pahiyon ko daudte hue to dekhtaa he, parantu vah pahiyon kii gati ke bhoomi se hone vaale sanyog ko nahin dekh paata. isi prakaar brahmalok kii yatra karne vaala sadhak rath par baithkar din or raat ko to dekhtaa he, kaal kii gati ko bhi dekhtaa he, paap-punya ko bhi dekhtaa he, parantu vah unamem lipt nahin hota. vah unase dur rahakar hi 'brahm' ko praapt karata he. kaam, krodh, lobh, moh or ahankaar uske maarg ke baadhak nahin banataa. maharishi chitra aage bataate hai-'he gautam! brahmavetta ban sadhak apni sampoorn indriyon main brahmagandh, brahmaras, brahmatej, brahmayash tathaa brahmanad kaa anubhav karata he. tab brahma ji us brahmagyani se prashn karte hai-'tum koun ho?' us samay brahma ji dwaara puche gayi prashnon kaa uttar brahmagyani ko is prakaar dena chahie—
 is prakaar brahma ji ke sabhi prashnon kaa uttar dene ke uparant swayan brahma ji sadhak se kehete hai ki 'jal' aadi prasiddh paanch mahabhoot mere sthaan hai. at: yah meraa lok bhi jal aadi tatva dwaara pradhan he. tum mujhase abhinn mere upaask ho. at: yah tumhaara bhi lok he.'is prakaar vah sadhak brahma kii 'jiti' or 'vyashti', donon ko praapt kar letaa he.'maharishi chitra ne is prakaar 'brahmagyaan' kaa upadesh dekar gautam rishi ko abhibhoot kiya. rishi gautam apane putr shwethaketu ke saath maharishi chitra ko pranaam karke waapas laut gayi.
 is adhyay main 'prantatva' kii upasna or 'brahmavidya' ke vyaavhaarik paksh par prakaash daalaa gaya he. iske atirikt apane paapon ko nasht karne ke liye 'suryopasna' putr kii kushal-mangal kaamana or suraksha ke liye, 'chandropasna,' achche swasthya ke liye 'somopasna,'moksh-prapti ke liye 'pranompasna' tathaa putr ko apane sampoorn jivan kaa daayitv-bhaar saunpte samay 'sampradaan karm' kaa bada hi sangopang varnan kiya he. prantatva kii upasnis adhyay main 'praan' ko hi 'brahm' kaa rup mana he. praan kii kalpana raja ke rup main kii gayi he. 'man' uskaa doot he, 'vaani' uski rani he, 'chakshu' uski suraksha karne vaale mantri hai, 'karnendriya' sandesh grahan karne vaale dwarpal hai. praan ke aate hi samast indriyon kii seva praan-rupi raja ko swat: hi praapt ho jaati he. suprasiddh mahatma shushkabhingaar 'praan' ko hi brahm kaa rup sweekaar karte hai. at: praan kii upasna hi isht siddhiyon ko dene vaali he. jivan main shreshthata, sukh-samruddhi, yash, tejaswita tathaa gyaan pranopasna dwaara ho saktaa he. suryopasnakaushitaki rishi ne apane anubhav se suryopasna teen baar-praat:kaal, madhyahnkaal or sanykaal- karne kii baat kahi he. unhone kaha he ki praat:kaal yagyopavit ko savy bhav se baayen kandhe par rakhkar aachman karen. phir jalpaatr ko teen baar shuddh jal se bharkar, udai hote hue surya ko arghya pradaan karen or is mantra kaa uccharan karen—' virgosi papmanan me vriddhi.' is prakaar madhyahnkaal main, bhagwaan bhaskar ko samaran karen or is mantra kaa uccharan karen-' udvargosi papmanan main sanvruddhi.' isi prakaar sanykaal main, ast hote hue surya kii upasna karen or is mantra kaa uccharan karen-	' sanwargosi papmanan me sanvruddhi.' is prakaar suryopasna karne se manushya ke din-raat ke saare paapon kaa shaman ho jaataa he, paap karm na karne kii shakti utpann ho jaati he. chandropasnamavasya main, jab surya pashchim bhaag main sthit ho tathaa chandramaa sushumna naamak rashmi main chandramaa sthit ho, tab is vidhi se chandropasna kerani chahie. arghya dene vaale paatr main do hari durva ke ankur bhi avashya rakh len. tab arghya dete hue is mantra kaa uccharan karen-'yatte susiman hridayamadhi chandramsishritan tenamritatvasyeshane maahan pautramadyan rudam.' is prakaar kii prarthana se upaask putr shok se bachaa rahata he tathaa putr na hone kii sthiti main putr-ratna praapt kar letaa he. somopasanarishi ne somopasna ko swasth sharir kaa kaaran mana he. vah som se prarthana karata he-'he stri-rupi som! tum purush-rupi surya ke prakaash se vikaas ko praapt ho. tum sabhi ore se ann kii prapti main sahaayak bano. he som! tum saumy gunon se yukt ho. tumhaara divya ras surya ke tej ko praapt karke purush maatr ke liye atyant hitkaari ho jaataa he. tum is divya ras kaa seven karne vaale purushon ko pushti do or unke sabhi shatruon kaa paraabhav karaane main puri tarah se sahaayak bano. he som! tum aagney tej se prasannataa ko praapt karte hue, amritv kii prapti main sahyog pradaan karo or swayan apane yash ko swarglok main sthir karo. he som! main tumhaari hi gati kaa anugaman karte hue apni dahini bhujaa ko baar-baar ghumata hum.' rishi ne som ko paanch mukh vaala prajapati kaha he. uskaa ek mukh 'brahman' he, doosraa mukh 'kshatriya' hai, teesraa mukh 'baj' pakshi he, chauthaa mukh 'agni' he. or paanchvaan mukh tum 'swayan' ho. is prakaar som kii prarthana karne ke uparant garbhaadhan ke liye tatpar stri ke paas baithne se pehle uske hriday kaa sparsh karen or is mantra kaa paath karen—'yatte sumime hridaye hitmant: prajapathu manyehan maa tadvidwansan ten maahan pautramadhan rudam.' is prakaar kii gayi prarthana se sadhak ko kabhi putr shok nahi jhelana padataa. rishi kaa sanket us mata kii ore he, jo apana doodh apni santaan ko pilaati he. uske doodh main somras-jaisi rakshaatmak shakti vidyaman hoti he. usee se balak swasth rahata he. mata kaa doodh balak ke liye naiysargik prakriya he. prakruti kaa virodh anekanek bhayanak parinaamon kaa kaaran ban jaataa he. moksh hetu pranopasanamoksh ke liye prantatva kii upasna ke sandarbh main, rishi ne ek sundar roopak dwaara praanon ke mahatv ko darshaya he. ek samay vaani aadi samast devtaa ahankaar ke vasheebhoot hokar apni-apni mahatt siddh karne ke liye paraspar vivaad karne lage. praan ke saath sabhi ne sharir se bahirgaman kar diya. unke nikal jane se sharir kashtha kii bhaanti chetana-rahit hokar so gaya. 'vaani' ne apana varchasv siddh karne ke liye sharir main akele hi pravesh kiya. vaani ke pravesh karte hi sharir vaani se bolne laga, lekin vah apane sthaan se uth nahin sakaa. iske baad 'netrendriya' devtaa ne sharir main pravesh kiya. tab vah vaani se bolne or netron se dekhne laga, parantu is baar bhi vah uth nahin sakaa. phir 'shrotendiy' devtaa ne sharir main pravesh kiya. vah sunne laga, bolne bhi laga or dekhne bhi laga. lekin is baar bhi vah uth nahin sakaa, nishcesht hi padaa rahaa. tatpashchaat us sharir main 'man' ne pravesh kiya. man ke dwaara vah sochane yogya to ho gaya, par is baar bhi vah uth nahin sakaa. tab sabase ant main 'praan' ne us sharir main pravesh kiya. us prantatva ke pravesh karte hi vah sharir uthakar baith gaya. isse praanon kaa mahatva sarvopari siddh ho gayaan sabhi anya indriyon ne praan main hi, moksh aadi sadhana kii shakti ko sweekaar kiya. unhone janaa ki praanon ke dwaara hi amritv gun ko praapt kiya ja saktaa he or yah sharir upar uthakar swarglok kii ore ja saktaa he. praanon ke dwaara hi, sharir kii samast chetnaayen or indriyaan kaary karti hai. at: praanon kii upasna dwaara hi brahm se sanyog kaa kaaran banataa he. praanon ke dwaara hi vishisht gyaan-swaroop parabrahm ko praapt kiya ja saktaa he. pita dwaara sampradaan-karm karnaa is adhyay main pita dwaara apane putr ko uttaradhikari banane kii prakriya kaa sangopang ullekh kiya gaya he. rishi kaa kahana he ki apana antim samay aayaa jaanakar, pita ko apane sampoorn uttardayitvon se mukt ho janaa chahie or jo kuch bhi uske paas he, use apane putr ko saunp dena chahie. putr ko bhi apane pita dwaara chode gayi daayitv ko saharsha sweekaar kar lenaa chahie. pita ko chahie ki shubhr vastra dhaaran karke apane putr ko apane paas bulaaye or usase kahe-'he putr! main tumhen apni vaak shakti, apane praan, apane netra, apane kaan, apane rasaswadan, apane samast shreshth karm, apane sukh-du:kha, apni maithunjnya shakti tathaa rati-sukh, apni gatishiltaa, apni samast icchhaaen, apni buddhi, apana yash, brahmatej ou apana shreshth swasthya tathaa ann ko pachaane kii shakti aadi sabhi sadgun pradaan karata hum yaa tumhaare bheetar pratishthit karata hoon.' pita dwaara aisa kehane par putr vinamrata se unhen sweekaar kare or apane baayen kandhe kii ore drushti karke haath se oot karke kahe-'pitashri! aap apni icchaanusaar kaamanayukta swarg ko tathaa vahaan ke samast bhogon ko praapt karen.' iske uparant, yadi pita nirog ho, to vah apane putr ko ghar kaa swami banaakar athwa maanakar uske saath nivas kare yaa phir sabhi kuch tyagkar va ghar chhodkar sanyas kaa jivan bitaaye. aisa pita uchit samay ke aane par divyalok ko gaman karne vaala hota he. vastav main uttaradhikaar kaa sahi niyam yahi he. 
 is adhyay main rishi ne praan, pragna or indriyon kaa vishad varnan karte hue unke parsparik sambandhon kaa vishleshan kiya he. iske liye samvaad-style kaa sahaara liya gaya he. yahaan indra or divodas raja ke putr pratardan ke parsparik sanvaadon dwaara praan ko pragna kaa swaroop bataaya he or kaha he ki is pragna se hi lokhit sambhav he. 
 ek baar devasur sangram main, devaataaon kii sahaayta ke liye raja divodas ke putr pratardan swarglok gayi the. vahaan unki adwitiya yudhkala evam shaury se prasann hokar indra ne unhen vardaan dena chaha tha. is par pratardan ne kaha-'he devraj! jis shreshth var ko aap maanav-jati ke liye param kalyanyukt maanate hon, vaisaa hi koi shreshth var aap swayan hi mujhe pradaan karen.'pratardan kii baat sunakar devraj indra ne kaha-'rajan! is sansar main koi bhi aisa vyakti nahin he, jo dusron ke sukh ke liye var maangata ho. aap koi aisa var maangen, jo aapke apane liye ho?'is par pratardan ne koi bhi var grahan karne se inkaar kar diya. raja pratardan ko satya kaa arudh dekhakar devraj indra ne kaha-'he rajan! tum mujhe hi, mere yatharth rup ko jaano? yahi maanav-jati ke liye shreshth vardaan he.'devraj ke is kathan main pratardan ne pahachaanaa ki indra ne na jane kitne pathbhrasht asuron or rishi-muniyon ko dand diya, par ahankarviheen or nishkaami hone ke kaaran indra kaa bal bhi baankaa nahin hua. tab usne samajha ki nishkaam karm karte hue or ahankaar kaa sarvatha tyaag karte hue, jo jivan jiya jaataa he, vah maanav-kalyaan ke liye hi hota he. at: lok-kalyankari vyakti ke liye nirabhimani or nishkaam karmi hona param aavashyak he. aisa vyakti hi indra, arthath pragna-rupi praan-tatva ke yatharth swaroop ko praptaj kar saktaa he or bade-se-bada paapakarm bhi use prabhavit nahin kar saktaa. yadi daivik pravritiyon kii raksha ke liye kisi aattaayi vyakti kaa vadh bhi karnaa pade, to uskaa bhi paap nahin lagata. 
 devraj indra ne pratardan ko upadesh dete hue kaha ki jo bhi mere satya swaroop ko pahchaan jaataa he, use kabhi paap nahin lagata; kyonki, 'main swayan hi pragna-swaroop praan hum.' indra aage kahata he ki us praan tathaa shreshth gyaan se yukt aatmaswaroop ke rup main prakhyat, mujh indra kii, tum aayu ou amrit-rup se upasna karo. vah spasht karata he ki aayu hi praan he, praan hi aayu he or praan hi amritatatv he. is sharir main jab tak praan he, tabhi tak aayu he. is prantatva ke dwaara hi sadhak duusare lok main jaakar amritatatva ke vishesh sukh ko praapt karata he. jo vyakti 'aayu' or 'amrit' ke rup main indra kii upasna karata he, vah is lok main purnayu ko praapt karata he or swarg ke sukhon kaa paan karata he. sharir main praan kaa mahatva hi sarvopari he. yadi praan ke rahete sharir kaa koi ang nasht ho jaye, to bhi sharir jivit rahata he, parantu praan ke na rahane par, ek kshan bhi sharir kaa jivit rahanaa asambhav he. kriya shakti kaa bodh karaane vaala praan hi he. praan hi gyaan main pravriti hone kii shakti detaa he. ise hi 'pragna-swaroop praan' athwa 'aatma' kaha gaya he. at: jivan ke utthan ke liye pragna-swaroop is praan kii hi upasna kerani chahie. sharir main pragna or praan, donon saath-saath nivas karte hai tathaa jeevatma ke saath milakar ek saath hi is sharir ko chhodte hai. pranamya parabrahm kaa yahi darshan he or yahi vigyaan he. isi kii upasna jan-kalyaan kaa mukhya strot he. 
 sushupt avasthaa main, jab manushya soya hua hota he, tab sabhi indriyaan or gyaan, praan main samahit ho jate hai. lekin jab vah jaagta he, tab sabhi prakaar kaa gyaan, praan se nikalakar apni-apni indriyon main samaa jaataa he or sushupt indriyaan sajag ho jaati hai. ise upanishad main maranasann vyakti ke udhaaharan se samajhaayaa he. ek vyakti, jo maranasann avasthaa main he, uski samast chetana shakti indriyon kaa saath chhodkar praan main samaa jaati he. us samay vah na to sunata he, na dekhtaa he, na bolta he, ne haath-pair hilaa paata he, na uskaa mastishk hi kaam karata he. yahi sthiti dhyansth yogi kii hoti he. parantu jab vah maranasann vyakti rogon se chhutkaaraa paakar pun: jivit ho uthta he athwa vah dhyansth yogi phir se sahaj jivan main laut aata he, tab unki samast indriyaan apane-apane swabhaav ke anusaar phir se sajag ho uthati hai. man ke dwaara nirdeshit hone lagati hai. praan hi sarvapratham 'man' ko jaagrath karata he. vahi sampoorn indriyon ko niyantrit or aniyantrit karata he. man kaa sidhaa sambandh 'pragna' se he or pragna kaa praan se. is prakaar praan, pragna or indriyon kaa anyonyashrith sambandh he. praantatv se hi pragna he or pragna se hi samast indriyon ko sanchaalan hota he. sharir se unmukt hone vaala 'praan' jab utkraman karata he, tab vah us samay in sabhi indriyon-sahit hi utkraman karata he. vaani sabhi naamon kaa, naak sabhi gandhon kaa, chakshu sabhi roopon kaa, kaan sabhi dhvaniyon kaa or man sabhi dhyansth vishyon kaa parityag kar detaa he. at: sharir main praan hi vah pragna he or pragna hi vah praan he, jo ek saath is sharir main nivas karte hai. sharir kii samast indriyon kaa parichaalan inhen ke dwaara hota he. pragna ke dwaara hi vah samast indriyon ke swabhaav ke anuroop anubhuti ko praapt karata he. pragna ke abhaav main sabhi kaa astitv naganya ho jaataa he. praan athwa pragna ke bina kisi bhi rup, vishay athwa indriya kii siddhi nahin ho sakati. jis prakaar rath kii nemi aron ke or are rath kii naabhi ke ashrith hote hai, usee prakaar ke samast indriyaan, pragna ke dwaara hi vidyaman hai. yah pragna athwa praan hi paramatma kaa anandaswarup or ajar-amar rup he. yah na to achche kaam se vruddhi paata he ou na bure kaary se sankuchit hota he. yah samdarshi he. yah samast lokon kaa adhipati he or sabakaa swami he. yah praan hi hamaara aatma he. ise jaanakar hi paramatma kii upasthiti ko anubhav kiya ja saktaa he. 
 is adhyay main gargy naamak brahman rishi or kaashi ke vidwaan raja ajatshatru ke madhya hue vaartalap ko aadhaar banaakar samvaad-style main brahmand kii vividh shaktiyon kaa ullekh kiya gaya he tathaa 'aatma' ko hi 'brahm' sweekaar kiya he. 
 garg gotra main utpann balaaka rishi ke putr kaa naam gargy rishi ke naam se prasiddh tha. ve chaaron vedon ke prakand vidwaan the. ushinar pradesh unka nivasasthaan tha. ve praaia: desh ke vibhinn pradeshon main bhraman karte rahete the. kabhi matsay desh main, to kabhi kuru-paanchal main rahane pahunch jate the. ek baar ve ghoomte-ghoomte kaashi ke vidwaan raja ajatshatru ke dabaar main pahunche. vahaan ve atyant ahankaarpurn vaani main raja se bole-'he rajan! main aapako 'brahmatatv' kaa upadesh duungaa.' gargy rishi ke aisa kehane par kaashi naresh ajatshatru ne kaha-'he vipravar! aapke is upadesh ke liye main aapako ek sahastra uttam kul vaali guaen pradaan karuunga. mujhe to prasannataa he ki ab tak brahmavidya ke shrota or dani ke rup main mithila naresh janak kaa hi naam tha. aaj aapane hamaare paas aakar videh raaj janak kii bhaanti hamaara gaurav badhaayaa he.' 'brahmatatva' kaa upadesh dete hue rishi gargy or ajatshatru ke madhya is saprkaar vaartalap hua-rishi gargy—'he rajan! is suryamandal main jo antaryami parameshwar sthit he, main brahmabuddhi se usee kii sadhana karata hum.'ajatshatru—'he brahman! aisa nahin he. yah shwet vastradhari surya to sabhi se mahaan he. yah sabase ucch sthiti main kendrit, sabakaa sheesh he. jo manushya is viraat purush kii is prakaar se aradhana karata he, vah sabase ucch sthiti main pahunchataa he.'rishi gargy-'he rajan! chandra mandal main jo yah antaryami viraat purush he, main use brahmroop main maanakar uski upasna karata hum.'ajatshatru-'he vipravar! aisa nahin he. yah to som raja he or ann kii aatma bhi yahi he. main isi prakaar isaki upasna karata hum. jo bhi is prakaar isaki upasna karata he, vah nishciya hi ann kii aatma ho jaati he tathaa dhan dhaany se bhar jaataa he.'rishi gargy-'he rajan! vidyut mandal ke antargat yah jo antaryami viraat parabrahm he, main usee kaa upaask hum.'ajatshatru-'he vipravar! aisa nahin he. main is vidyut ko 'prakaash kii aatma' maanakar isaki upasna karata hum. jo bhi is prakaar isaki upasna karata he, vah swayan prakaash-swaroop 'aatma' ho jaataa he.'rishi gargy-' he rajan! megh mandal main garjana ke rup main vidyaman us antaryami ishwar ko main saakshaat brahm maanata hum.'ajatshatru-'he vipravar! aisa na kahen. main shabd kii aatma samajhakar hi is shreshth tatva kii upasna karata hum. jo is prakaar isaki upasna karata he, vah swayan hi shabd kii aatma ke rup main parinat ho jaataa he.'rishi gargy ne phir kaha-'he rajan! akaash mandal main pratishthit parabrahm parameshwar kii main avinashi brahm ke rup main upasna karata hum.'ajatshatru ne uttar diya-'he vipravar! main is vishay main kuch nahin kahana chahta. yah to purna, pravriti-rahit brahm, sabhi se vishaal he. avashya hi main isi rup main isaki upasna karata hum. jo aise divya brahm kii upasna karata he, vah samast praaniyon main nirvikaar ho jaataa he. samay se purv uski mrityu nahin hoti.'isi prakaar vaarta ko aage badhaate hue gargy rishi ne vayumandal main sthit viraat purush ko brahm kaha, par ajatshatru ne use indra ke shreshth aishwarya se yukt vaikuntha kaha, jahaan kunthaayen nasht ho jaati hai ou vah sadaiv aparajit rahata he. uske baad rishi ne agni mandal main sthit viraat purush ko, jalmandal main upasthit viraat purush ko, darpan main pratibimbit viraat purush ko, pratidhwani main sthit dhwani ko, gatisheel viraat purush ke peeche uthne vaale dhvanyatmak shabd ko, sharirdhari kii chaaya ko, sharir main sthit viraat purush ko, pragna se yukt praan-swaroop aatma ko, daahine netra main sthit viraat purush ko, baayen netra main sthit viraat purush ko avinashi brahm ke rup main upasthit karke, uski upasna karne kii baat kahi. parantu ajatshatru ne kramash: sabhi ko nakaarte hue agni ko dusron kaa prahaar sahan karne vaala 'vishasahi,' jal main sthit brahm ko namdhari jeevatma, pratibimb ko praturup, pratidhwani ko gati kaa abhavayukt, dhvanyatmak shabd ko 'pranaswaroop', sharir kii chaaya ko mrityu-rup, sharir main sthit viraat purush ko prajapati kaa swaroop, pragna se yukt aatma ko yama kaa swaroop, daahine netra main sthit purush ko naam, agni or jyoti kii aatma, baayen netra main sthit purush ko satya, vidyut or tej kaa aatma bataaya or usee rup main unki upasna karne kii baat kahi. ajatshatru kii baat sunakar gargy rishi maun ho gayi or unhone ajatshatru ko apana guru sweekaar kar liya, parantu kshatriya hone ke kaaran ajatshatru ne brahman gargy ko shishy bananaa sweekaar nahin kiya. haa, ekaant main unhen unhen brahm kaa gyaan avashya karaayaa. ve unhen lekar ek sote hue vyakti ke paas gayi or use anek naamon se pukaar kar jagaane kaa praytn kiya, parantu jab vah nahin jaagaa, to ajatshatru ne chadi se chot markar use uthaaya. uske jaagane par ajatshatru ne rishi gargy se kaha-'he vipravar! yah vyakti is prakaar achet hokar kahaan sota tha or kis pradesh main yah tha or ab jaagane ke baad yah kahaan aa gaya he?' rishi gargy is rahasy ko nahin samaj sake, to raja ajatshatru ne pun: kaha-'rishiver! yah purush jahaan sota tha, vah sthaan praan-rupi hridayakamal he. vahaan 'hita' naam kii prasiddh naadee he. uske dwaara hriday kaa vistaar sampoorn naadiyon tak he, jo bal ke hajaarwein bhaag se bhi sookshm he. in naadiyon main purush sote samay sthit rahata he. is soye hue purush ke hriday main sthit 'praan' kaa sabhi indriyon se sambhaav he. yah praan hi 'aatma' kaa khol he. yah pragnavan aatma isi khol main sookshm rup se usee prakaar virajman rahati he, jaise lakdi kii myaan main talwaar rahati he. purush ke jaagane par yah 'aatma' sampoorn 'prantatva' ko sakriy kar detii he. tab us 'sakshi-rup aatma' kaa sabhi indriyaan anumat sevak kii bhaanti anusaran karti hai.'isi 'aatma' ko jaanane vaala gyani, indra kii bhaanti apane sabhi shatru asuron athwa paapon ko nasht karke triloki kaa sarvshereshth pad praapt karata he. at: 'aatma' ko jaanane ke liye ahankaar kaa tyaag param aavashyak he. tabhi vah apane paapon kon nasht kar saktaa he.
