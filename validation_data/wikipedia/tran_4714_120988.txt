 bowing 777, bowing commercial airplance dwaara nirmit lambi-shrinkhalaa, chaudee-sanrachana or judawaan-ingine vaala ek jet airliner he. yah vishwa kaa sabase bada twinjet he jise aam taur par "tripal seven" ke rup main sanderbhit kiya jaataa he. vimaan main 300 se adhik yatriyon ke baithne kii suvidha he or model ke aadhaar par iske udaan kii seemaa 5,235 se 9,380 samudri mile he. isaki vishisht suvidhaon main kisi bhi vimaan ke sabase adhik vyas vaale terbophan ingine, har mukhya landing gear main chah-chah pahie, ek vrittakar vimaanakbanounuprastha-kaat or blade ke aakaar vaala shankvakar poonch shaamil he. aath pramukh airlinon ke paraamarsh par viksit kiye gaye is 777 ko puraani chaudee sanrachana vaale airlineron ko pratisthapit karne or 767 evam 747 kii kshamta ke antar ko samaapt karne ke liye taiyaar kiya gaya tha. pehla fli-bai-vir airliner hone ke naate bowing kaa niyantran computer kii madhyasthata ke madhyam se kiya jaataa he; yah sampoorn rup se computer dwaara dizin kiya jane vaala pehla vanijyik vimaan bhi he.
 777 ko do vimaanakbandh lambaaiyon main prastut kiya gaya he. mul 777-200 model ne 1995 main sabase pehali seva pradaan kii jiske baad 1997 main isaki vistrut shrinkhalaa 777-200iaar kaa aagaman hua; isaki agali shrinkhalaa 777-300 ne 1998 main seva pradaan karnaa shuru kiya jo 33.3 feet adhik lambaa he. adhik lambi shrinkhalaa 777-300iaar or 777-200elaar bhinnarupon ne kramshah 2004 or 2006 main apni seva pradaan karnaa shuru kiya jabaki ek malwahi sanskaran, 777epa, ne 2008 main apni pehali seva pradaan kii. donon adhik lambi shrinkhalaa vaale sanskarnon or malwahi sanskaran main general electric jeei90 injanon ke saath-saath vistrut or raced wingtips bhi lage hue hai. anya modelon main yaa to jeei90, prat and whitney PW4000, yaa rolls-royas trent 800 ingine laga hua he. 777-200elaar ko duniya kii sabase lambi shrinkhalaa vaala airliner kaa darja mila he or isane ek bina phir se eendhan bhare hue vanijyik vimaan dwaara sabase zyada dur udaan bharne kaa kirtiman sthapit kiya he or saath main isane duniya bhar ke aadhe se zyada raaston main udne kii kshamta kaa pradarshan bhi kiya he.
 united airlines ne sabase pehle 777 ko 1995 main vanijyik airline seva main shaamil kiya. july 2010 tak, 59 graahakon ne sabhi prakaar ke 1,143 vimaanon kaa order diya he jisamen se 881 kaa vitaran kar diya gaya he. duniya bhar main istemaal kiya jane vaala sabase aam bhinnrup 777-200iaar he jiske 415 vimaanon kaa vitaran kar diya gaya he or emirate 86 vimaanon ke saath sabase zyada 777 vimaan samoohon kaa sanchaalan karata he. October 2009 ke anusaar, is airliner ko ek baar vimaan-kshati durghatana se guzarana padaa he jisamen kisi bhi yatri kii maut nahin hui thi jiskaa shrey trent 800 ingine ke ek eendhan ghatak ko diya jaataa he.
 2000 ke dashak ke shuru se ant tak, 777 apane nirmaataa ke sabase zyada bikne vaale modelon main se ek ke rup main ubhara he. eendhan kii badhati laagat kii vajah se airlinon ne anya chaudee sanrachana vaale jeton kii tulana main ek eendhanksham vikalp ke rup main is tarah ke vimaanon ko apanaaya he or lambi duuri vaale paar-mahasagariya margon ke liye uttarottar is tarah ke vimaanon kaa istemaal kiya he. pratyaksh vipanan pratiyogiyon main airbas e330-300 or e340 shaamil he or saath main aagaamee e350 eksdbljubi or bowing 787 abhi vikasadhin he.
 1970 ke dashak main shuru main, bowing 747, mcdonal duglas disi-10 or lochid el-1011 trister seva main pravesh karne vaale pehali peedi ke chaudee sanrachana vaale yatri airliner bane. 1978 main bowing ne teen nae modelon par se pardaa uthaaya: sammanneey 727 ko pratisthapit karne vaala judawaan ingine vaala 757, airbas e300 ko chunauti dene vaala judawaan ingine vaala 767 or disi-10 or el-1011 ke saath pratispardhaa karne vaala ek triget 777 concept. madhyam aakaar vaale 757 or 767 ko aanshik rup se 1980 ke dashak ke vistrut-shrinkhalaa judawaan-ingine sanchaalanaatmak pradarshan maanak ) ke vinium shaasi paar-mahasagariya twinjet sanchalanon kii vajah se vipanan safalataa hasil hui. in viniyamon ke anusaar judawaan ingine vaale airlineron ko apatkaaleen dikparivartaneey hawaii addon se teen ghante kii duuri tak mahaasaagar ko paar karne kii anumati thi. atops ke niyamon ke tahat, airlinon ne lambi duuri vaale un samudr-paar raaston par 767 kaa sanchaalan karnaa shuru kiya jise bade airlineron kii kshamta kii jarurat nahin thi. triget 777 ko baad main 757 or 767 bhinnarupon ke samarthan vaale vipanan adhyayanon ke baad tyaag diya gaya. 767-300iaar or 747-400 ke bich kii utpaada shrinkhalaa main aakaar or seemaa ke antar ke saath bowing ko chhod diya gaya.
 1980 ke dashak ke antim daur tak, disi-10 or el-1011 model sevanivritti ke kagaar par pahunch rahe the or nirmaataaon ko inaki jagah anya modelon kaa dizin viksit karne ke liye utsahit kar rahe the. mcdonal duglas emdi-11 par kaam kar rahe the jo ki disi-10 kaa ek vistrut or unnat sanskaran tha jabaki airbas e330 or e340 ko viksit kar rahe the. 1986 main bowing ne disi-10 jaisi pehali peedi kii chaudee sanrachanaon vaale airlineron ke pratisthaapan vipanan kaa lakshya lekar or company kii linep main maujuuda 767 or 747 modelon kii purti ke liye asthaayee taur par 767-eks naamak ek abhivardhit 767 ke prastaavon par se pardaa uthaaya. aarambhik prastaav main winglates ke saath-saath, maujuuda 767 kii tulana main adhik lambe vimaanakbandh or bade daine shaamil the. parvarti yojanaon main vimaanakbandh anuprastha-kaat kaa vistaar kar diya gaya lekin maujuuda 767 flit deck, naak or anya tatwon ko yathaavat rakhaa gaya. 
 airline graahak 767-eks ke prastaavon se naakhush the or iske bajaay ek adhik chaude vimaanakbandh anuprastha-kaat, puri tarah se lacheela aatanrik vinyas, antaramahadwipiya-seemaa kshamta se kam or kisi bhi 767 vistaar kii tulana main kam sanchaalanaatmak laagat vaale airliner chaahate the. airline yojanakaaron kii bade vimaan kii jaruratein uttarottar vishisht ho gai thi jisane vimaan nirmaataaon kii pratiyogita ko or badha diya. 1988 tak bowing ne mahasoos kiya ki iskaa ekmaatr jawaab ek nayaa dizin tha jisane 777 twinjet kaa rup le liya. company ne ateetkaaleen dizin kii safaltaaon, prastaavit ingine ke vikaason or kam laagat laabhon ke aadhaar par judawaan-ingine vinyas kaa vikalp chunaa. 8 disambar 1989 ko bowing ne airlinon ko 777 kii peshkash kerani shuru kar di.
 bowing ke nae twinjet ke dizin kaa charan company ke pichley vanijyik jetalineron se alag tha. pehali baar, airliner ke vikaas main ol nippon airways, american airlines, british airways, cathe pacific, delta air lines, japan airlines, kwants or united airlines naamak aath pramukh airlinon ne bhaag liya tha. yah ek udyog prasthaan abhyaas tha jahaan nirmaataaon ne mote taur par nunatam graahak input vaale vimaan kaa dizin taiyaar kiya tha. dizin prakriya main apana yogadan dene vaale aath airlinon ko bowing ke bheetar "varking tugedar" samooh ke rup main janaa jane laga. janvari 1990 main hui is samooh kii pehali baithak main, airlinon ko 23 pannon vaali ek prashnawali vitarit kii gai jahaan prayek se yahi poocha gaya ki nae dizin main kya-kya shaamil karnaa chaahate hai. march 1990 tak, bowing or airlinon ne ek buniyadi dizin vinyas kaa faisla liya: kaaphi had tak 747 kii tarah kaa ek cabin anuprastha-kaat, 325 yatriyon ko le jane kii kshamta, lacheela aantarik bhaag, ek kaanch kaa cockpit, fli-bai-vir niyantran or e330 or emdi-11 kii tulana main 10 pratishat behatar seat-mile yaa yatri-vahan laagat. bowing ne antim sangyojan sthal ke rup main 747 nirmaan centre washington sthit apane evarett factory kaa chayan bhi kiya. 
 14 oktober 1990 ko united airlines 777 kaa aarambhik graahak bana jab isane 34 atirikt vimaanon ke vikalp ke saath 11 billian america dollar mulya vaale 34 prat and whitney sanchalit vimaan kaa order diya. vikaas charan ke dauraan hi united ne bhi apane puraane ho chale disi-10 ko pratisthapit karne kaa kaarykram banaaya. united chahta tha ki nae vimaan main teen alag-alag margon: chicago se hawaii, chicago se europe or denwar, ek garm or ooncha hawaii adda, se bina ruke hawaii tak udne kii kshamta ho. atops pramanikaran bhi united ke liye ek praathamikta thi jiske tahat united ke hawaii margon kaa samudr-paar vaala hissaa diya gaya. janvari 1993 main, united ke developaron kii ek team ne evarett factory main anya airline kii tiimon or bowing ke dizinron ke dal main shaamil hua. adhiktam 40 sadasyeey 240 dizin tiimon ne vyaktigat vimaan ghatakon ke dizin ko lekar lagbhag 1,500 muddon ko sambodhit kiya. cathe pacific ke liye anukool banane ke liye vimaanakbandh ke vyas main vruddhi kii gai, ol nippon airways ke liye aadharbhoot model ko or lambaa kiya gaya or british airways ke input ke falswaroop antarnimit pareekshan or aatanrik lacheelaapan shaamil kiya gaya or saath hi saath mul vimaan ke liye ucchtar sanchaalaneey vajan vikalpon ko shaamil kiya gaya.
 777 aisa pehla vanijyik vimaan tha jise puri tarah se computer par dizin kiya gaya tha. prayek dizin drawing ko triayamicad software system par banaaya gaya tha jisekaitiya  ke naam se janaa jaataa he, jiskaa strot dusult systames or ibiem tha. isse baadhaon kii jaanch karne ke liye or hajaaron purjon ke uchit samaayojan ko satyapit karne ke liye banaavati dhang se ek abhaasi vimaan ko sanyojit karne kaa avasar praapt hua jisase mahange punarkaarya kii jarurat kam ho gai. bowing ne bade paimaane par sahyogaatmak ejineering dizin samikshaon, utpaadan drushtaanton or ejineering ke baahar cad data ke anya upyogon main sahaayta karne ke liye apni khud kii ucch pradarshan vaali maanasadarshan pranali, fli thru, ko viksit kiya jise baad main iviti  naam diya gaya. bowing shuru main katiya kii kshamtaaon ke prati aashwast nahin the or unhone iske parinaamon ko satyapit karne ke liye ikse naak vaale bhaag ke ek bhautik nakli sanrachana kaa nirmaan kiya. yah pareekshan itnaa safal rahaa ki atirikt nakli sanrachanaon ko radd kar diya gaya.
 utpaadan prakriya main ek bowing jetaliner ke liye ek bemisaal darje ke vaishvik upanubandhikaran ke saath paryaapt antarrashtriya saamagree shaamil thi jise sirf parvarti 787 dwaara atikaramit kiya gaya. antarrashtriya yogadanakartaaon menmitsubishi heavi industries or kawasaki heavi industries, fuji heavi industries limited, hoker de havilaland  or airospes technologies of australia shaamil the. japani airospes thekedaaron kaa netritv karne vaale japan aircraft deveopment korporation or bowing ke bich hue ke samjhaute ke tahat sampoorn vikaas kaarykram ke 20 pratishat ke liye parvarti jokhim kii bhagidaari karne vaale bhagidaaron kaa aagaman hua. praarambhik 777-200 model kaa shubharambh teen nirmaataaon, general electric, prat and whitney or rolls-royas ke pranodan vikalpon ke saath kiya gaya jisase airlinon ko pratispardhi companiyon ke apni pasand ke ingine mil gaye. prayek nirmaataa duniya ke sabase bade twinjet ko shakti sampann banane ke liye ucchtar thrust class or 77,000 pound-bal main ek ingine kaa vikaas karne ke liye raji the.
 apane nae airliner ke utpaadan ko anukool banane ke liye bowing ne do nae asembli linon arthath sangyojan vithiyon ke liye jagah uplabdh karaane ke liye lagbhag 1.5 billian america dollar kii laagat par evarett factory ke aakaar ko doguna kar diya. utpaadan ke nae tareekon kaa vikaas kiya gaya or saath main ek ghumaane vaali mashin ko bhi viksit kiya gaya jo vimaanakbandh ke upsanyojanon ko 180 degree tak ghuma saktaa tha jisase karmchaariyon ko sanrachana ke upree anubhaagon tak pahunchane main aasaani hoti thi. pehle vimaan kaa pramukh sangyojan kaary 4 janvari 1993 ko shuru hua. utpaadan kaary ke armbh tak is kaarykram ko 118 form order mil chuke the or saath main 10 airlinon se or 95 order milane kaa vikalp khulaa tha. is kaarykram main lagbhag bowing kii taraf se 4 billian america dollar se bhi zyada or aapurtikartaaon kii taraf se alag se lagbhag 2 billian america dollar kaa nivesh kiya gaya tha.
 9 april 1994 ko pratham 777, line number dablyooe001, ko 100,000 aamantrit mehmaanon ke liye jagah kii vyavastha karne vaale din ke dauraan yojit 15 samarohon kii ek shrinkhalaa main chaalu kiya gaya. pehali udaan 12 june 1994 ko mukhya pareekshan vimaanchaalak jon i. casshman kii kaman ke tahat bhari gai thi. yah 11 maheene kii ek udaan pareekshan kaarykram ke shuruyaat kii nishaani thi jiskaa makasad pichley kisi bhi bowing model ke kaarykram kii tulana main adhik vyapak hona tha. general electric, prat and whitney or rolls-royas injanon vaale nau vimaanon ke udaan kaa pareekshan california ke edwards air force base ke registani hawaii adde se laakar alaska ke kaaphi thande ilaakon main kiya gaya. atops kii jaruraton ko puraa karne ke liye 180 minute vaale ekal ingine vaale aath pareekshan udaanon kaa pradarshan kiya gaya. pratham nirmit vimaan kaa istemaal 1994 se 1996 tak bowing ke gaiyr-vinaashaatmak pareekshabhiyaan main kiya gaya or -200iaar or -300 kaaryakramon ke liye data uplabdh karaayaa gaya. udaan pareekshan ke safal samaapan par 777 ko 19 april 1995 ko sangheey uddyan prashasan ) or sanyukt uddyan praadhikaran ) dwaara samakalik udan yogyata pramanikaran se sammanit kiya gaya.
 bowing ne 15 may 1995 ko united airlines ko pratham 777 kaa vitaran kiya. pha ne 30 may 1995 ko prat and whitney peedblyoo4084 ingine yukt vimaan ke liye 180 minute vaala atops nikasi se sammanit kiya or is tarah yah seva main apane pravesh ke samay hi ek atops-180 kaa darja paane vaala pehla airliner ban gaya. iske baad oktober main 207 minute vaale adhik lambe atops nikasi ko manjuuri di gai. pehali vanijyik udaan 7 june 1995 ko landan ke hithro hawaii adde se disi ke washington ke paas dales antarrashtriya hawaii adde tak bhari gai.
 12 navambar 1995 ko bowing ne british airways ko general electric jeei90-77bi injanon vaale pehle model kaa vitaran kiya jiske paanch din baad us vimaan ko seva ke liye prayog main laayaa gaya. aarambhik seva par gear box bering aavaran ke muddon kaa asar padaa jisaki vajah se 1997 main airline ko paar-atlantic seva se apane 777 ke udaan ko asthaayee taur par band karnaa padaa. british airways kaa vimaan us saal baad main phir se puri tarah se apni puri seva pradaan karne laga or baad main general electric ne ingine unnayan kii ghoshnaa kii.
 pratham rolls-royas trent 877 sanchalit vimaan kaa vitaran 31 march 1996 ko thai airways international ko kiya gaya, jab shuru main airliner ke liye viksit kiye gaye teen bijligharon ko shuru karne kaa kaam puraa ho gaya. prayek ingine-vimaan sangyojan ne seva main pravesh ke samay se hi atops-180 pramanikaran ko surakshit kar liya tha. june 1997 tak 777 ke liye 25 airlinon kii taraf se 323 order mil chuke the jisamen santusht shuruuaati graahak bhi shaamil the jinhone atirikt vimaan kaa order diya tha. sanchaalan pradarshan data se lambi duuri vaale paar-mahasagariya margon par twinjet kii anukool kshamtaaon kaa pramaan mil gaya jiske falswaroop in vamaanon kii atirikt bikri hone lagi. 1998 tak ravaanagi vishwasaneeyata ke aankade takneekee muddon kii vajah se bina kisi deree ke udaan bharne kii 99.96 pratishat dar tak pahunch gai thi or vimaanon ke udaan bharne ke ghanton kii babbti sankhya 900,000 tak pahunch gai thi.
 aarambhik model ke baad, bowing ne 777-200iaar ko viksit kiya jo adhik duuri tay karne vaala or adhik maal vahan kii kshamta rakhane vaala vardhit sakal vajan vaala bhinnrup tha. -200iaar ne 7 oktober 1996 ko apni pehali udaan bhari, 17 janvari 1997 ko pha or ja pramanikaran praapt kiya, or 9 farvari 1997 ko british airways ke saath apni seva prarambh kii. adhik se adhik lambi duuri tay karke is bhinnrup vimaan ne 2000 ke dashak ke sampoorn aarambhik daur main sabase zyada order paane vaale sanskaran vimaan kaa darja praapt kar liya. 2 april 1997 ko malaysia airlines ke "super ranger" naamak ek -200iaar ne 21 ghante 23 minute main sietal ke bowing field se purv kii taraf kuala lampur tak kul 10,823 samudri mile duuri tak udaan bharkar ek airliner ke mahaan vrutt "landing rahit duuri" kaa record tod diya.
 -200iaar ke aagaman ke baad, bowing ne airliner ke ek vistrut sanskaran par apana dhyaan dena shuru kar diya. 16 oktober 1977 ko 777-300 ne apni pehali udaan bhari. 242.4 feet lambaa -300 ab tak nirmit sabase lambaa airliner tha or isaki samgr kshamta maanak lambaai vaale model kii tulana main 20 pratishat adhik thi. -300 ko 4 may 1998 ko ek hi samay pha or ja dwaara tip pramanikaran se sammanit kiya gaya or 27 may 1998 ko shuruyaat graahak cathe pacific ke saath seva main pravesh kiya.
 vikaas kaarykram ke armbh se bowing ne ati lambi seemaa vaale bhinnarupon ko banane par vichaar kiya tha. aarambhik yojanaayen 777-100eks ke ek prastaav par kendrit tha jo -200 kaa ek sankshipt sanskaran tha jiskaa vajan kam or udaan kshamta adhik thi jo 747espi kii tarah tha. haalanki, -100X kii yatri vahan kshamta -200 se kam thi jabaki sanchaalan laagat ek samaan thi jiske falswaroop prati yatri adhik laagat kaa anumaan tha. 1990 ke dashak ke antim daur tak, dizin yojanaon ko badalakar maujuuda modelon ke adhik lambi duuri vaale sanskaran ke liye banaaya jane laga. 100,000 pound-bal vaale adhik shaktishaalee ingine or ucchtar thrust class kii jarurat thi jiske falswaroop bowing or ingine nirmaataaon ke bich sakriy rup se vichaar-vimarsh hone laga. general electric ne jeei90-115bi ingine ko viksit karne kaa prastaav diya jabaki rolls-royas ne trent 8104 ingine ko viksit karne kaa prastaav diya. 1999 main, pratidwandvi prastaavon ko thukrakar bowing ne general electric ke saath ek samjhaute kii ghoshnaa kii. general electric ke saath samjhaute ke bhaag ke rup main, bowing is baat par sahamat the ki jeei90 ingine 777 ke nae sanskarnon ke liye prastaavit ekmaatr bijlighar honge.
 
 29 farvari 2000 ko bowing apane agali peedi vaale twinjet kaarykram kaa shubharambh kiya jise shuru main 777-eks kaha jaataa tha or airlinon ko peshkash jari karnaa shuru kar diya. lambi-seemaa vaale modelon ke vikaas kii gati airline udyog ke ghate kii vajah se dhimi pad gai jo 2000 ke dashak ke aarambhik daur tak kaayam rahi. is kaarykram se ubhar kar saamne aane vaale pehle model, 777-300iaar, kaa shubharambh air frans kii taraf se das vimaanon ke ek order ke saath-saath atirikt patibaddhtaon ke saath hua. 24 farvari 2003 ko -300iaar ne apni pehali udaan bhari or pha or iyasa  kaa uttaradhikari) ne 16 march 2004 ko is model ko pramanikrut kiya. air frans ko pehla vitaran 29 april 2004 ko kiya gaya. -300iaar, jisamen -200iaar kii seemaa ke saath -300 kii atirikt kshamta ko shaamil kiya gaya tha, 2000 ke dashak ke antim daur main sabase zyada bikne vaala 777 bhinn rup bana, or airlinon ne tulaneey chaar ingine vaale modelon kii jagah kam sanchaalanaatmak laagton kii vajah se twinjeton kaa order dene lage. 
 agali peedi vaale kaarykram se utpann hone vaale duusare model, 777-200elaar, kaa aagaman 15 farvari 2005 ko hua jisane 8 march 2005 ko apni pehali udaan bhari. -200elaar ko 2 farvari 2006 ko pha or iyasa donon kii pramanikta praapt hui or pakistan international airlines ko is model kaa pehla vitaran 26 farvari 2006 ko kiya gaya. 10 navambar 2005 ko pratham -200elaar ne honkong se purv kii taraf landan tak 11,664 samudri mile duuri tak udaan bharkar ek yatri airliner kii sabase lambi aviraam udaan kaa ek record kaayam kiya. 22 ghante 42 minute kii is udaan ne -200elaar kii maanak dizin seemaa ko peeche chhod diya or ginis world records main apni jagah bana lee. 
 agali peedi vaale malwahi model, 777epa, kaa aagaman 23 may 2008 ko hua. -200elaar ke sanrachanaatmak dizin or ingine vinirdeshnon ke saath-saath -300iaar ke eendhan tinkon vaale 777epa ne 14 july 2008 ko apni pehali udaan bhari. 6 farvari 2009 ko is malwahi ko pha or iyasa kaa pramanikaran praapt hua or 19 farvari 2009 ko shuruuaati graahak air frans ko iskaa pehla vitaran kiya gaya.
 prarambh main, 747 ke baad bowing ke sabase phydemand jetaliner ke rup main 777 ne uske baad se company ke sabase laabhprad model kaa sthaan hasil kar liya he. 2000 main, is kaarykram kii bikri se hone vaali bowing kii kar purv kamai lagbhag 400 million america dollar thi jo 747 kii kamai se 50 million america dollar adhik thi. 2004 tak, is airliner se bowing ke vanijyik hawaii jahaazon ke prabhag ke liye chaudee sanrachana kaa thok rajasav shaamil tha. 2007 main, agali peedi ke 777 modelon ke order kii sankhya 350 vimaanon tak pahunch gai or us varsh navambar main bowing ne ghoshnaa kii ki sabhi utpaadan slot 2012 ke liye bech die gaye hai. is kaarykram ke baaki 356 orderon kaa mulya 2008 kii suchi keemton ke anusaar 95 billian america dollar tha. 
 2000 ke dashak ke antim daur main, 777 ne airbas ke yojanabaddh e350 eksdbljubi or atarik rup se 787 ke prastaavit bhinn roopon kii vardhit pratispardhaa kii sambhaavna kaa saamana kiya jinamein se donon airlineron main eendhan kshamta main sudhaar laane kaa vaada kiya gaya tha. udyog kii reporton ke anusaar, is vimaan ko ant main ek nae utpaada parivaar, bowing yalostone 3, dwaara pratisthapit kiya ja saktaa he jisamen 787 kii praudyogikiyon kaa istemaal kiya jayega.
 bowing ne 777 dizin ke saath kai unnat praudyogikiyon kaa armbh kiya jisamen sampoorn digital fli-bai-vir niyantran, sampoorn software-vinyas yogya viviki, honeywel alsidi glass cockpit flit disple, or ek vanijyik airliner par pehali baar ek fibre optic viviki network kaa upyog shaamil tha. bowing ne radd bowing 7je7 kshetreey jet par kiye gaye kaam kaa istemaal kiya jisamen chaynit praudyogikiyon ke samaan sanskarnon kaa istemaal kiya gaya tha. 2003 main, bowing ne cockpit electronic flit bag computer disple ke vikalp kii peshkash kerani shuru kar di.
 pratham fli-bai-vir vanijyik vimaan ke rup main 777 kaa dizin banaate samay bowing ne kai fli-bai-vir ladaaku vimaan or kai airbas airlineron main istemaal kiye jane vaale sidstic niyantrakon ko badalane ke bajaay paaramparik niyantran jodiyon ko banaae rakhane kaa faisla kiya. paaramparik yoke or radar niyantranon ke saath-saath cockpit main ek saral leout shaamil he jo bowing ke pichley modelon kii samanta ko kaayam rakhata he. fli-bai-vir pranali main udaan aavaran suraksha bhi shaamil he jo sanchaalaneey maapdandon ke ek computer-pariklit fremawork ke bheetar vimaan chaalkon kaa maarg nirdesan karne vaali ek pranali he or baadhaon or bahut zyada tanaavpurn yuddhayaason kii rokthaam karata he. kaman sanbhaalane vaale vimaan chaalak dwaara jarurat mahasoos karne par is pranali kii avhelnaa bhi kii ja sakati he.
 777 ke dainon kii dizin ek supercritical airfoil dizin kii tarah hoti he jo 31.6 degree par waapas gum jaati he or maa 0.83 par paribhraman ke liye anukool he . dainon ko pichley airlineron kii tulana main adhik motaa or adhik failaa hua banaaya gaya he jiske parinamswaroop adhik se adhik pelod or seemaa kshamta aa gai he, udaan pradarshan main sudhaar hua he or adhik oonchai par udne kii kshamta praapt hui he. vimaan kaa shubharambh karte samay airlinon kii pasand ke liye iske folding wingtips kaa prastaav rakhaa gaya tha jo chote vimaan kii suvidha ke anusaar banaae gaye phatakon kaa istemaal kar sakate the lekin kisi bhi airline ne is vikalp ko nahin karidaa.
 airfrem main mishrit samagriyon kaa upyog kiya gaya he jiskaa vajan mul sanrachanaatmak vajan kaa nau pratishat he. mishrit samagriyon se cabin kaa farsh or radar banaaya gaya he. mukhya vimaanakbandh anuprastha kaat ek blade kii akruti vaale shankvakar poonch main vrittakar or peeche kii taraf shundakaar he jiske saath port kii taraf sahaayak bijli kii ikai he. is vimaan main sabase bada landing gear or sabase bade tair bhi lagaae gaye hai jiskaa istemaal isse pehle ab tak kisi vanijyik jetaliner main nahin hua he. 777-300iaar ke chah pahiyon vaale mukhya landing gear kaa prayek tair 59,490 pound boj vahan kar saktaa he jo 747-400 jaise anya chaudee sanrachana vaale vimaanon se adhik baari he. is vimaan main teen anaavashyak dravchaalit pranaliyaan hai jisamen se landing ke liye ek pranali kii jarurat he. ek ram air tarabine, ek chota sikudne yogya ingine jo apatkaaleen bijli pradaan kar saktaa he, ko bhi daine ke mul fering main lagaaya gaya he.
 bowing signechar interior ke naam se mashhoor 777 ke interior yaa aatanrik sancharana main ghumaavdaar panel, bade-bade upari dibbe or apratyaksh prakaash vyavastha shaamil he. baithne ke vikalpon main pratham darje main agal-bagal chah siton se lekar nichle darje main das siton kii shrenee shaamil he. 15-inch bai 10-inch par, 787 tak ke kisi bhi vartmaan vanijyik airliner kii tulana main sabase badi khidkiyaan lagi hui thi. cabin main bhi "namyata kshetra" shaamil he jisamen aatanrik sthal main har taraf aprihaary rup se jal, vidyut, hawa or anya hook-ap kii sahi vyavastha he jisase airline aasaani se cabin kii vyavastha ko samyojit karte samay siton, gali or shauchalayon ko turant sthanantarit kar sakate hai. kai vimaanon main gaiyr-airline upyog ke liye viipi interiaron kaa bhi intjaam kiya gaya he.
 2003 main bowing ne 777 par ek vikalp ke rup main upree chaalak dal vishram sthal kaa bhi intjaam kar diya. mukhya cabin ke upar sthit or sidhiyon ke madhyam se jude aage ke udaan chaalak dal vishram sthal main baithne ke liye do seat or sone ke liye do bistar kii vyavastha he jabaki peeche ke cabin chaalak dal vishram sthal main sone ke liye kai bistaron kaa intjaam he. uske baad se signechar interior ko bowing ke anya chaudee sanrachana or sankadi sanrachana vaale vimaanon ke liye naukulit kiya gaya he jisamen 737enji, 747-400, 757-300 or nutan 767 model shaamil hai. 747-8 or 767-400iaar main bhi 777 ke adhik badi or adhik gol khidakiyon ko shaamil kiya gaya he.
 
 bowing apane 777 modelon ko paribhaashit karne ke liye do visheshtaon, vimaanakbandh kii lambaai or seemaa, kaa istemaal karata he. vimaanakbandh kii lambaai kaa vahan kiye jane vaale yatriyon kii sankhya or maal kii maatra par asar padataa he; 777-200 or vyutpaadon kaa aakaar buniyadi aakaar he or 1998 main vimaan ko 777-300 main vistrut kiya gaya. seemaa ke sandarbh main, dizin mandandon ke aadhaar par vimaan ko teen kshetron main vargeekrut kiya gaya he; inhen shuru main nimna rup main paribhaashit kiya jaataa tha:
 vibhinn bhinn roopon kaa zikr karte samay bowing or airline aksar model number or bhinn rup nirdharak ko ek sankshipt rup main vyakt karte hai. antarrashtriya nagar uddyan sanghathan vimaan tip nirdharak pranali ek purvavarti nirmaataa akshar jodate hai. kshamta sankhya ke baad, padanaamon ko seemaa pahachanakarta ke saath joda yaa nahin joda ja saktaa he . ye sanketan vimaan minial yaa airline samay saraniyon main mil sakate hai.
 777-200 praarambhik e-bazaar model tha. pratham -200 kaa vitaran 15 may 1995 ko united airlines ko kiya gaya tha. adhiktam seemaa 5,235 samudri mile vaale -200 kaa mukhya uddeshy america gharelu airline sanchaalkon par tha. july 2010 tak das vibhinn -200 graahakon ko 88 or saath main airline seva main 62 vimaanon kaa vitaran kar diya gaya he. airbas kaa pratispardhi vimaan e330-300 he.
 777-200iaar, -200 kaa bi-bazaar sanskaran, ko iske vardhit sakal vajan ke liye vastav main 777-200aijeedblyoo ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. -200iaar main -200 kii atirikt eendhan kshamta or ek vardhit adhiktam techof vajan ) kii suvidha he. antarrashtriya airlinon kii sanchaalaneey paar-atlantic margon ke uddeshy par -200iaar kii adhiktam seemaa 7,700 samudri mile he. purvabhimukh mahaan vrutt "landing rahit duuri" tay karne ke record ko todane ke alaava, -200iaar dwaara prashanth mahaasaagar par 17 march 2003 ko 255 yatriyon kaa vahan karne vaali ek united airlines udaan par sabase lambe atops-sambandhit apatkaaleen udaan pathantaran kaa record bhi kaayam he. 
 pratham -200iaar kaa vitaran 6 farvari 1997 ko british airways ko kiya gaya tha. july 2010 tak, 33 vibhinn graahakon ko 415 -200iaar kaa vitaran kiya gaya tha, jisase ise ab tak ke sabase zyada paimaane par nirmit hone vaale twinjet ke bhinn rup kaa darja mil gaya tha. july 2010 tak airline seva main 434 vimaan the. airbas kaa pratispardhi vimaan e340-300 he.
 vistrut 777-300 ko 747-100 or 747-200 ke liye ek e-bazaar pratisthaapan ke rup main dizin kiya gaya tha. puraane 747 ke mukaable vistrut bhinn rup main tulaneey yatri kshamta or seemaa he or ise ek tihaai kam eendhan jalaane ke liye dizin kiya gaya he or iskaa rakrakhaav laagat 40 pratishat kam he. -300 main besaline -200 par ek 33.3 feet vimaanakbandh failaav shaamil he jisase ek single class ucch-ghanatv vaale vinyas main adhik se adhik 550 yatriyon ke baithne kii suvidha uplabdh hui he jo ki bahut zyada traffic vaale japani margon ke liye apanaai gai vyavastha he. vimaan kii lambaai kii vajah se hawaii jahaaj ko jameen par chalaane ke dauraan vimaan chaalak kii sahaayta ke liye -300 main ek telscid or bhuyukti camere lagaae gaye hai. adhiktam seemaa 6,015 samudri mile he jisase puraane 747 dwaara pehle udaae jane vaale mukhya margon main -300 ko sanchalit karne kii suvidha praapt hui he.
 pratham -300 kaa vitaran 21 may 1998 ko cathe pacific ko kiya gaya tha. aath vibhinn -300 graahakon ko 60 vimaanon kaa vitaran kar diya gaya he or july 2010 sabhi vimaan airline seva main shaamil the. haalanki, 2004 main adhik lambi seemaa vaale -300iaar ke aagaman ke baad sabhi sanchaalkon ne -300 model ke iaar sanskaran kaa chayan kiya he. -300 kaa koi pratyaksh airbas pratidwandvi nahin he lekin e340-600 ko pratiyogita main prastut kiya jaataa he. 
 777-200elaar, si-bazaar model, duniya kaa sabase lambi seemaa vaala vanijyik airliner bana jab isane 2006 main seva main pravesh kiya. bowing ne is vimaan kaa naam worldliner rakhaa, duniya ke lagbhag kisi bhi do hawaii addon ko jodne kii isaki kshamta par vishesh prakaash daalaa haalanki yah abhi bhi atops ke pratibandhon ke adheen he. isane ek vanijyik airliner dwaara sabase lambi aviraam udaan bharne kaa vishwa record kaayam kiya he or isaki adhiktam seemaa 9,380 samudri mile he. -200elaar kaa uddeshy ati lambi duuri vaale maarg jaise los angeles se singapore yaa dalas se tokio tak udaan bharne kaa tha. 
 -300iaar ke saath-saath viksit hone vaale -200elaar main ek vardhit emtiodblyoo or pichley cargo hold main teen vaikalpik sahaayak eendhan tankiyaan shaamil he. anya nai suvidhaon main raced wingtips, phir se dizin kiye gaye mukhya landing gear or atirikt sanrachanaatmak sudrudheekaran shaamil he. -300iaar or 777epa kii tarah -200elaar main 12.8 feet lambe wingtip ekstansion lagaae gaye hai. pratham -200elaar kaa vitaran 26 farvari 2006 ko pakistan international airlines ko kiya gaya tha. july 2010 tak, chah vibhinn -200elaar graahakon ko 43 vimaanon kaa vitaran kiya ja chukaa he or saath main 17 orderon ko puraa nahin kiya gaya he. airbas kaa nikatatam pratispardhi vimaan e340-500echjidblyoo he.
 777-300iaar -300 kaa bi-bazaar sanskaran he. isamen raced or vistaarit wingtips, ek nayaa mukhya landing gear, majboot noz gear or atirikt eendhan kii tankiyon kii suvidha uplabdh he. -300iaar main ek majboot vimaanakbandh, daine, ampenej yaa jahaaj kaa pichhlaa bhaag or ingine sanlagnak bhi hai. maanak jeei90-115bi terbophan duniya kaa sabase shaktishaalee sevarat jet ingine he jiskaa adhiktam thrust 115,300 pound-bal he. isaki adhiktam seemaa 7,930 samudri mile he, jo ek ucchtar emtiodblyoo ke saath-saath vardhit eendhan kshamta kii vajah se sambhav hua he. yatriyon or maal se bhare -300 kii tulana main -300iaar lagbhag 34 pratishat zyada dur ud saktaa he. udaan pareekshan ke baad, ingine ke karyanvayan, danna or vajan sanshodhan se eendhan kii khapat main 1.4 pratishat kii kami dikhaai di. 
 pratham -300iaar kaa vitaran 29 april 2004 ko air frans ko kiya gaya tha. -300iaar ko sabase zyada bikne vaale 777 bhinn rup kaa darja praapt he jisane july 2010 main -200iaar ko peeche chhod diya, or is model ke shubharambh ke baad se yah pratidwandvi e340 ko peeche chhod twinjet kii bikriyon kaa ek praathmik chaalak bana hua he. keval do injanon ke istemaal se e340-600 par -300iaar ke liye lagbhag 8 se 9 pratshat kaa vishisht sanchaalaneey laagat laabh praapt hua he or saath hi saath 747-400 par 20 pratishat kaa eendhan khapat laabh bhi hasil hua he. kai airlinon ne eendhan kii badhati keemton ke bich 747-400 kii jagah -300iaar ko apanaaya he. july 2010 tak 21 vibhinn graahakon ko 246 -300iaar kaa vitaran kiya gaya he saath hi 186 orderon ko puraa nahin kiya gaya he. july 2010 tak sanchaalkon ke paas 237 sevarat vimaan the. -300iaar kaa pratyaksh airbas pratiyogi e340-600echjidblyoo he.
 777 malwahi twinjet kaa sampoorn cargo sanskaran he. isamen -200elaar or -300iaar kii suvidhaon ko shaamil kiya gaya he, pehle vaale vimaan kaa airfrem or ingine kaa istemaal kiya gaya he or baad vaale vimaan kii eendhan kshamta ko shaamil kiya gaya he. adhiktam 226,000 pound pelod ke saath isaki cargo kshamta 747-200epa kii 243,000 pound ke samaan he. is malwahi kii adhiktam pelod seemaa 4,885 samudri mile he haalanki maal kaa vajan kam hone se isaki seemaa ke adhik hone kii sambhaavna he. chunki maujuuda malwahiyon kii tulana main is vimaan ke sanchaalaneey arthvyavastha main sudhaar hua he, isliye airlinon ne 747-200epa or emdi-11epa sahit puraane malwahiyon kii jagah 777epa kaa istemaal karne kaa man bana liya he.
 pratham 777epa kaa vitaran 19 farvari 2009 ko air frans ko kiya gaya tha. july 2010 tak saat alag alag graahakon ko 29 malwahiyon kaa vitaran kiya gaya tha or saath main 44 orderon ko puraa nahin kiya gaya tha. july 2010 tak 23 malwahiyon kaa sanchaalan ho rahaa tha.
 kasee-777, 777 kaa ek prastaavit tanker sanskaran he. sitambar 2006 main, bowing ne saarvajanik rup se ghoshnaa kii ki yadi united stats air force arthath sanyukt rajya america vaayu sena ) ko kasee-767 se zyada bade tanker kii jarurat ho to vah kasee-777 kaa utpaadan karegaa. 777 tanker main adhik maal or karmiyon ko le jane kii bhi kshamta hogi. april 2007 main, bowing ne badale main uaseef ke kasee-eks pratiyogita ke liye apane kasee-767 unnat tanker kii peshkash kii.
 adhikansh 777 praapt karne vaale graahakon main lefasi, emirate, singapore airlines or united airlines kaa naam shaamil he. july 2010 tak airline seva main kul 858 vimaan sevarat the, udhaaharan ke liye, emirate, singapore airlines, air frans, united airlines, american airlines, british airways, ol nippon airways, japan airlines, cathe pacific, korean air, saudi arbian airlines, thai airways international, continental airlines, keelem royal dach airlines or is tarah ke kuch vimaanon vaale anya sanchaalak.
 june 2010 ke ant tak kaa vivaran. 20 july 2010 ko apadated. 
 may 2010 tak 777 ko saat ghatnaon kaa saamana karnaa padaa he jisamen se ek durghatana main vimaan kaa kaaphi nuksaan hua tha jisamen kisi bhi yatri yaa chaalak dal main se kisi kii bhi maut nahin hui thi. twinjet kii maujuudagi vaali ekmaatr ghaatak durghatana 5 sitambar 2001 ko denwar international airport par eendhan bharne ke dauraan lagi ek aag kii vajah se hui thi jisamen ek jamini karmchaaree buree tarah se jal gaya tha. british airways dwaara sanchalit is vimaan ke daine is aag main jhulas gaye the jise marammat karke waapas seva ke liye taiyaar kar diya gaya.
 is tarah ke vimaan kii ekmaatr vimaan haani 17 janvari 2008 ko hui thi jab british airways flit 38, ek rolls-royas trent 898 ingine vaala 777-200iaar jo beijing se landan kii udaan par tha, utarate samay hithro hawaii adde ke ranve se lagbhag 1,000 feet kii duuri par durghatnagrast ho gaya or ranve ke pravesh dwaar kii taraf phisalata chalaa gaya. isamen 47 log ghayal hue the lekin kisi kii maut nahin hui thi. is durghatana main landing gear, daine kii jad or ingine kshatigrast ho gaye the or vimaan nasht ho gaya tha. chaanbin se pataa chalaa kii is durghatana ke liye eendhan pranali ke barf ke tukade jimmedaar the jisaki vajah se eendhan-tel garmi excanger ) avaruddha ho gaya tha. hawaii durghatana kii chaanbin karne waalon ne trent 800 shrinkhalaa ke ingine ke is purje ko phir se dizin karne ke liye kaha or nirmaataa rolls-royas ne march 2009 main kaha ki iskaa nayaa poorja a year ke anusaar ke bheetar taiyaar ho janaa chahie.
 2008 main trent 895 injanon vaale thrust ke do anya maamuli kshanik haniyaan hui thim. naishanal transportesan sefti board arthath rashtriya parivahan suraksha mandal ) ke jaanchakartaaon ne yah nishkarsh nikaala ki thik bie38 kii tarah eendhan-tel garmi excanger ke eendhan main barf ke avarodhan kii vajah se bijli kii haani hui thi. haalanki vimaan chaalkon ke aasapaas kii sanrachana ab bhi maujuud he, antiesbi ne garmi excanger ko phir se dijain karne kaa bhi anurodh kiya.
