 haveli zila azaad kashmir kaa ek zila he. paak-adhikrut kashmir do bhaagon main vibhajit he: azaad kashmir or gilgit-blatistan. haveli zila inamen se azaad kashmir naamak hisse main aata he. iskaa pramukh nagar kotli he. bharat ise apana bhaag maanata he. san 2009 tak haveli zila baag zile kaa hissaa hua karata tha lekin us varsh ise vibhajit kar ke alag zila bana diya gaya.
