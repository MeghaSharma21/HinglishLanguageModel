 sidhon kaa sambandh buddha dharm kii bajrayani shakha se he. ye bharat ke purvi bhaag main sakriy the. inaki sankhya 84 maani jaati he jinamein sarhappa, shavrappa, luippa, dombhippa, kukkurippa aadi mukhya hai. sarhappa pratham siddh kavi the. inhone jatvaad or vahyacharon par prahaar kiya. dehwaad kaa mahima mandan kiya or sahaj sadhana par bal diya. ye mahasukhwad dwaara ishwartv kii prapti par bal dete hai.
 buddha dharm ke vajrayan tatv kaa prachaar karne ke liye jo sahitya desh bhashaa main likhaa gaya vahi siddh sahitya kehalaata he. yah sahitya bihar se lekar assam tak failaa tha. rahul sankrutyaayan ne 84 sidhon ke naamon kaa ullekh kiya he jinamein siddh 'sarahapa' se yah sahitya armbh hota he. bihar ke nalanda viapeeth inke mukhya adde maane jate hai. baad main yah 'bhot' desh chale gaye. inaki rachanaaon kaa ek sangrah mahamahopadhyaay harprasad shastri ne bangla bhashaa main 'bauddhagan-o-doha' ke naam se nikaala. sidhon kii bhashaa main 'ulatbasi' style kaa purv rup dekhne ko miltaa he. hajaari prasad dvivedi ne siddh sahitya kii prashansa karte hue likhaa he ki, "jo janata taatkaalik nareshon kii swechhacharita, paraajay trast hokar niraashaa ke gart main giri hui thi, unke liye in sidhon kii vaani ne sanjivani kaa kaary kiya. sadhana avasthaa se nikali sidhon kii vaani 'chariya geet / charyagit' kehalaati he.
