 manushyon kii anuvanshiki main matruvansh samooh bil5 yaa mitocandria-diene hapalogrup L5 ek matruvansh samooh he. is matruvansh samooh ke sadsy striyon or purushon kii taadaad zyaada nahin he, lekin ve purvi or madhya africa ke kuch samudaayon main milte hai. pigmi janajati kii ambuti shakha ke 15% log iske vanshaj hai or tanzania ke sandave samudaay ke main bhi kam taadaad main iske vanshaj milte hai. africa se baahar bahut kam taadaad main iske vanshaj soudi arab main bhi paae gaye hai. vaigyanikon ke mutabiq jis stri se is matruvansh kii shuruyaat hui vah purvi africa main aaj se lagbhag 114,200 se 126,200 saal pehle rahati thi.
 angrezi main "vansh samooh" ko "hapalogrup", "pitruvansh samooh" ko "waahe chromosome hapalogrup" or "matruvansh samooh" ko "amtidiene hapalogrup" kehete hai.
