 jalashirsh uccharit/ˌhaɪdrɵˈsɛfələs/, "mastishk main jame pani" ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he, jo ek chikitsakeey sthiti he jisamen mastishk ke nilay yaa cotaron main mastishcameru drav siesuef kaa asaamaanya jamaav ho jaataa he. yah khopadi ke andar atyadhik dimagi dabaav or sir ke kramik badhane, anthan or maansik viklaangata kaa kaaran ban saktaa he. jalashirsh maut kaa bhi kaaran ban saktaa he. iskaa naam unani shabd yopo - "pani" or κέφαλος "sir" se vyutpann he.
 khopadi ke andar ke bade hue dabaav ke lakshanon ke saath sir dard, ulti, michali, akshibimbshof paapiledema tandra yaa coma bhi shaamil ho sakate hai. khopadi ke andar ke varddhit dabaav kaa parinaam jivan ghaatak mastishk nalee sampeedan sahit ankush sambandhi anakal yaa anumastishkeey tonsil herniation ho saktaa he. khopadi ke andar ke varddhit dabaav par anya abhivyaktiyon ke vistrut vivaran ke liye:
 chaal asthirataa, mutr asanyam or manobhransh da trid saamaanya dabaav jalashirsh enpiech kaa ek apekshaakrit alag vishisht prakatikaran swarup he. kendriya snaivik kami bhi ho sakati he, jaise apvartanee tantrika pakshaaghaat or urdhwadhar takataki pakshaaghaat .
 lakshan rukaawat ke kaaran par nirbhar karte hai, jaise vyakti kii aayu or sujan dwaara mastishk ke utak kii kshatigrastata ke anupaat par.
 jalashirsh grast shishuon ke kendriya tantrika tantra main siesuef drav bad jaataa he, jo brahmarandhra ke ubhaar kaa kaaran banataa he or sir ko ummid se adhik bada kar detaa he. praarambhik lakshanon main ye bhi shaamil ho sakate hai:
 bade bacchon main utpann hone vaale lakshanon main ye bhi shaamil ho sakate hai:
 jalashirsh grast khopadiyon ke sandarbh isa purv 2500 se 500 isvi tak ke pracheen misr ke chikitsa sahitya main paye ja sakate hai. i.pus.chauthi shatabdi main pracheen unani chikitsak hippocrates dwaara jalashirsh ko or adhik spasht rup se varnit kiya gaya tha, jabaki baad main duusari i.sadi main roman chikitsak gallen dwaara ek or adhik sateek varnan diya gaya. arab sarjan abu al-kasim al-zaharvi dwaara al-tasrif main pehla naidanik vivaran or jalashirsh ke liye shalopchaarak prakriya astitv main ai, jisane jalashirsh grast bacchon main satahi antahkapaly drav kii nikasi kaa spasht rup se varnan kiya he. usne tantrikashlyak rogon ke apane adhyay main iskaa varnan kiya he, usne shaishavkaleen jalashirsh kaa varnan karte hue ise yantric sampeedan kii vajah se honevaala bataaya he. vah kahata he :
 20 veen sadi main, parshvapathon or anya tantrikashlyak upachaar ke tareeke viksit hone tak yah ek duhsadhay sthiti bani rahi. yah ek kam prasiddh chikitsa sthiti he, jalashirsh ke upachaar ko viksit karne ke liye apekshaakrit kam anusandhan kiye gaye hai or aaj tak is avasthaa ke liye koi ilaaj vidyaman nahin he.
 jalashirsh bal or vayask donon rogiyon ko prabhavit karata he. enech website ke anusaar anumaanatah 700,000 bachche or vayask jalashirsh ke saath ji rahe hai. 
 bal jalashirsh prayek 500 jivit janmon main ek ko prabhavit karata he, jo saamaanya vikaas viklaangata ko janmajaat vikruti yaa bahrepan se adhik aam bana rahaa he. yah sanyukt rajya america main bacchon ke liye mastishk shalya chikitsa ke pramukh kaarnon main se ek he. is sthiti ke 180 se adhik alag-alag kaaran hai, yah samay se pehle janm ke saath jude mastishk raktasrav ke sabase aam adhigrihith kaarnon main se ek he. bal jalashirsh ek anuvanshik sthiti bhi ho sakati he or yah mukhya rup se purushon ko prabhavit karata he. jalashirsh kaa pataa janm ke purv ultrasound pareekshanon se lagaaya ja saktaa he.
 pramastishkeey parshvapath jalashirsh ke liye sabase adhik prachlit upachaar vidhiyon main se ek he, jo 1960 main pehali baar viksit kii gayi thi. parshvapath ko tantrika shalyachikitsa ke madhyam se rogi ke mastishk main pratyaropit kiya janaa chahie, jo swayan mastishk kii kshati kaa kaaran ban sakane vaali ek prakriya he. ek anumaan ke anusaar sabhi parshvapathon main se 50% do varshon ke bheetar asafal ho jate hai tathaa parshvapathon ko badalane ke liye phir se shalyachikitsa kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. pichley 25 varshon main, jalashirsh ke saath judi mrityu dar 54% se 5% kam huyi he or bauddhik viklaangata kii ghatnaon main 62% se 30% tak kii kami ai he.
 jalashirsh mastishcameru drav main sankraman kaa bhi kaaran banataa he. yah janm ke dauraan maan ke sankraman kaa bhi parinaam ho saktaa he.
 jalashirsh aamtaur par nilay yaa mastishk ke upar kii antariksh avajaaltanika main mastishcameru drav ke bahirvah main rukaawat kii vajah se hota he. jis vyakti main jalashirsh nahin hota, uske nilay or merudand main mastishcameru drav mastishk ke madhyam se nirantar sancharit hota rahata he tathaa lagaataar parisancharan pranali main nishkasit hota rahata he. vaikalpik rup se, yah haalat drav padaarth kii saamaanya nikasi ko avaruddh karne vaali janmajaat vikruti yaa sir par lage aghaat yaa sankraman se utpann siesuef drav ke adhik utpaadan kaa parinaam bhi ho sakati he.
 jame drav kii vajah se mastishk kaa sampeedan antat akshep or maansik mandatha kaa kaaran ho saktaa he. ye lakshan vayaskon main jaldi prakat hote hai, jinaki khopadi ab bheetar taral padaarth kii maatra ko samyojit karne ke liye badhane main sakasham nahin hoti. jalashirsh yukt bhrunon, shishuon or chote bacchon main chehre ko chhodkar ek asaamaanya rup se badi khopadi hoti he, kyonki vyaktigat khopadi kii haddiyon par drav kaa dabaav - jise abhi bhi fus hona hota he- unke jodon ke binduon ko baahar kii taraf ubharta he. shishuon main ek anya chikitsakeey lakshan kii visheshta iris ke upar ankhon ke safed bhaag ko dikhaati neeche kii or lagi takataki he, maano baccha khud apni palkon kii jaanch karne kii koshish kar rahaa he.
 varddhit antahkapaly dabaav mastishk ke sampeedan ke kaaran ho saktaa he, jo mastishk kii kshati or anya jatiltaaon kii ore agrasar karata he. prabhavit vyaktiyon kii sthitiyaan vyapak rup se badalati rahati hai. jin bacchon ko jalashirsh ho chukaa he unamem bahut chote nilay ho sakate hai or "saamaanya maamle" ke rup main prastut kiye ja sakate hai.
 yadi pramastishkeey jalsetu ke chauthe nilay kaa randhra avaruddh he to mastishcameru drav siesuef nilay ke bheetar sanchit ho saktaa he. is sthiti ko aantarik jalashirsh kehete hai or iske parinaam se siesuef dabaav main vruddhi hoti he. jab saamaanya rup se mastishk main iske nikaas kii anumati dene vaala maarg avaruddh ho gaya ho tab bhi siesuef kaa utpaadan jari rahata he. natijatan, mastishk ke andar ban rahaa drav tantrika utak par dabaav daalata he or nilay ko failaa detaa he. tantrika utak kaa sampeedan aamtaur par aparivartaneey mastishk kshati kaa kaaran banataa he. yadi jalashirsh hone ke samay khopadi kii haddiyaan asthikrut nahin hoti hai to iskaa dabaav sir ko gambhir rup se badha detaa he. janm ke samay pramastishk jalsetu avaruddh ho saktaa he yaa mastishk-tane main kisi tumor ke bad jane kii vajah se baad ke jivan main bhi avaruddh ho saktaa he.
 ucch aantarik dabaav ko samaapt karne ke liye mastishk nilyon or udar guha ke bich ek jal nikasi tub lagaakar safaltaapurvak aantarik jalashirsh kaa ilaaj kiya ja saktaa he. in parshvapathon ke dwaara mastishk main sankraman hone ke kuch khatre rahete hai tathaa us vyakti ke bade hone ke saath-saath in parshvapathon ko avashya badal dena chahie. ek avajaaltanika raktasrav parisancharan main siesuef kii vaapasi ko baadhit kar kar sakati he. agar siesuef antariksh avajaaltanika main jamaa ho jaataa he, to is sthiti ko bahari jalashirsh kaha jaataa he. is haalat main, mastishk par bahya dabaav lagu hota he, jo tantrika uttakon ko sampidit karata he or mastishk ke nuksaan kaa kaaran ban saktaa he. is prakaar mastishk ke utakon ko aage or nuksaan pahunchataa he tathaa jiske parinamswaroop unhen necrotigeshan kii ore le jaataa he.
 jalashirsh mastishcameru drav siesuef ke ksheen pravaah, punauvasoshana yaa siesuef ke atyadhik utpaadan kii vajah se ho saktaa he. 
 iske antarnihit tantra ke aadhaar par jalashirsh ko sanchari or gaiyr sanchari main vargeekrut kiya ja saktaa he. donon hi rup janmajaat yaa adhigrihith ho sakate hai.
 sanchari jalashirsh ko gaiyr-pratirodhi jalashirsh ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he, yah nilyon or antariksh avajaaltanika ke bich siesaf pravaah main rukaawat kii anupasthiti main vikrut mastishcameru drav ke pun:shoshan kii vajah se hota he. yah anumaan lagaaya gaya he ki aisa mastishkavarak jhilli ke grenulation ki kaaryaatmak vikruti ki vajah se hota he, jo superior sagital sinus ke saath sthit hote hai evam or shiraparak pranali main mastishcameru drav ke waapas pun:shoshan kaa sthaan he. sanchari jalashirsh, avajanalatika/antarnilai sanvahan raktasrav, mastishkavaranshoth, shiyaari vikruti or mastishkavarak jhilli grenulation kii janmajaat anupasthiti sahit vibhinn tantrikatmak sthitiyon kaa parinaam ho saktaa he. sankramak, sujan yaa rakthasravi ghatnaon ke baad antariksh avajaaltanika ke daag or tantushoth bhi siesaf ke punah shoshan ko roak sakate hai or visteern nilayi failaav kaa kaaran ban sakate hai.
 gaiyr-sanchari jalashirsh yaa pratirodhi jalashirsh siesaf- pravaah main rukaawat kii vajah se hota he or antat antariksh avajaaltanika main siesaf- ke pravaah ko roktaa he samaypurv shishuon main antarnilai sanvahan sancha raktasrav, 2) sankraman, 3) dwitiya prakaar kii arnold-shiyari kami, 4) jalsetu margrodh or sankeernata tathaa dendi-vaakar vikruti shaamil hai. 
 jalashirsh yukt navjaaton or nanhen shishuon main sir kii paridhi teji se badhati he or jaldi hi 97 pratishatak ko paar kar jaati he. chunki khopadi kii haddiyaan abhi tak dridhtaa se ek saath judi nahin hotin isliye marij ke sahi sthiti main hone par bhi ubhaar, agravarti kathorta or kapaal ke prishth main riktata vidyaman ho sakati he. 
 shishu main chidchidapan, kam aahaar lenaa or lagaataar ulti jaise lakshan dikhaai de sakate hai. jalashirsh kii pragati ke saath akarmanyata aati he or shishu apane aasapaas main ruchi kii kami darshata he. baad main, upree palken mukar jaati hai or ankhe neeche kii ore mud jaati hai sankraman, mastishkaavaran shoth, mastishk ke tumor sir ke aghat antahkapaly raktasrav se adhigrihith hoti he or aamtaur par bahut dard hota he.
 kyonki, jalashirsh mastishk, vichaar or vyavhaar ko haani pahunchaa saktaa he tathaa iskaa pratikul prabhaav pad saktaa he. alp-aavadhik smriti haani sahit adhigam viklaangata shabd jalashirsh ke marijon main aam he, jo pradarshan bauddhik str kii apeksha maukhik bauddhik str main behatar pradarshan karne ke liye jane jate hai, jise mastishk kii tantrika kshati ke vitaran ko darshane vaala samajha jaataa tha. haalanki alag-alag vyaktiyon main jalashirsh kii gambhirtha main ullekhneey bhinnata pai jaati he or kuch ausat tathaa kuch ausat se upar bauddhik str ke hote hai. jalashirsh yukt kisi vyakti ko samanvay kii samasyaon ke saath, gati or drushti kii samasyaayein ho sakati hai yaa bedaulata ho sakati hai. ve ausat bacchon kii apeksha jaldi tarunai tak pahunch sakate hai . chaar main se ek main mirgee viksit ho sakati he.
 jalashirsh kaa upachaar shalya chikitsa he. isamen pravaah kii rukawat/arconoidal grenuleshnon kii vikruti ke liye ek upmarg bananaa or adhik drav ko sharir ke anya vivaron main nikalne ke liye, jahaan use punarshoshit kar liya jaataa he, pramastishk nilyon main ek nilayi nalika lagaana shaamil he. adhikansh parshvapath drav ko udaravan gahvar main nikaal dete hai lekin vaikalpik sthanon main dahinaa alind fusfus guha or pittashay kii thili shaamil hai ridh ke katiprak rikt sthaan main bhi ek parshvapath pranali sthapit kii ja sakati he or siesuef ko udaravan gahvar lumber-peritoneal shant kii ore punah nirdeshit kiya ja saktaa he. pratirodhi jalashirsh ke chaynit rogiyon ke liye ek vikalp upachaar endoscopy third ventriculostomy  he, jisamen shalya chikitsa dwaara tisare nilay kii sataha main banaaya gaya dwaar siesuef ke pravaah ko sidhe aadhariya kunda main bhej detaa he, is prakaar jalsetu sankuchan main utpann kisi bhi avrodh ko dur karata he. vyakti kii shaareerik rachana par aadhaarit hone kii vajah se yah upyukt ho bhi saktaa he or nahin bhi.
 sambhav jatiltaaon ke udaahranon main parshvapath vikruti, parshvapath vifaltaa or parshvapath sankraman shaamil hai. haalanki, aamtaur par ek parshvapath acchhee tarah kaam karata he, lekin agar yah alag, avaruddh, sankramit ho jaye, yaa samay seemaa paar kar le to yah kaam karnaa band kar detaa he. yadi aisa hota he to mastishcameru drav phir se jamaa hone lagegaa or anek shaareerik lakshan tathaa kuch daure jaise atyant gambhir lakshan viksit hone lagenge. parshvapath kii vifaltaa kii dar apekshaakrit ucch he or rogiyon ke liye unke jivan main parshvapathon ke kai sanshodhan asaamaanya nahin hai.
 mastishcameru drav ke jamaa hone kaa nidaan jatil he or iske liye visheshgy kii dakshata kii aavashyaktaa hoti he.
 jab siesuef kaa nishkasana ranjit snayujaal main iske utpaadan kii apeksha adhik teji se hone lage to susti, gambhir sir dard, chidchidapan, prakaash samvedansheeltaa shravan-sambandhi ati samvedansheeltaa, michali, ulti, chakkar aana, sir kaa chakkar, ardh-shirih pidaa daure, vyaktitv main badlaav, haath yaa pair main kamajori, 12}bhengapan or duhari drushti - jab marij sidhaa khadaa ho to aadi anya jatiltaayen bhi paidaa ho sakati hai. yadi marij let jaataa he to aamtaur par alp samay ke andar hi ye lakshan gaayab ho jate hai. ek siti scan nilyon ke aakaar main hue kisi parivartan ko dikhaa bhi saktaa he or nahin bhi, khaskar yadi marij kaa daraar yukt nilyon kaa itihaas rahaa ho. atyadhik nikasi ke nidaan main kathinaayee is jatilta ke upachaar ko, vishesh rup se rogiyon or unke pariwaron ke liye, niraashajanak bana sakati he.
 paaramparik dard nivarak aushadhiy upachaar kaa pratirodh bhi parshvapath kii adhik nikasi yaa vifaltaa kaa ek sanket ho saktaa he. vishesh jatilta kaa nidaan aamtaur par lakshanon ke prakat hone ke samay par nirbhar karata he - jaise ki, kya ye lakshan tab utpann hote hai jab marij sidhe khadey hone kii sthiti main hota he yaa adhomukh sthiti main hota he, jab sir mote taur par pairon ke samaan str par hon.
 chunki parshvapath pranaaliyon kii laagat vikasshil deshon main aam logon kii pahunch se baahar he, isliye jalashirsh se grasit adhiktar log parshvapath kii prakriya kaa ek baar bhi prayog kiye bina hi maare jate hai. parshvapath kii pranaaliyon kii punrariti kii dar or bhi buree he jo parshvapath kii laagat ko kai guda badha detii he. is bindu par vichaar karte hue dow banzmin si. varf ne parshvapath badalane kii vibhinn prakriyaaon kaa adhyayan kiya or adhiktar vikasshil deshon main kam laagat kii parshvapath badalane kii pranaaliyon kii tulana kar unki bhoomika par prakaash daalaa. is adhyayan ko general of neurosurgery:peediatrix ke may 2005 ke sanskaran main prakashit kiya gaya he. yah viksit deshon main prayog kii jane vaali parshvapath pranaaliyon se chhabda pranali kii tulana ke bare main he. yah adhyayan uganda main kiya gaya tha or dwimeruta evam jalashirsh ke antarrashtriya sangh dwaara parshvapathon kaa daan kiya gaya.
 ateet main jalashirsh yukt ek 44 varshiya francisi vyakti se sambandhit ek dilachasp maamla tha, jiske mastishk main mastishcameru drav ke jamaa hone ke kaaran uskaa mastishk sakuchit hokar ek vaastavik mastishk utak kii ek patali shit kii tulana main kuch hi bada rah gaya tha. vah aadmi, jiske sir main drav ke nishkasana ke liye parshvapath daalaa gaya tha, apane baayen pair main halki kamajori kaa anubhav karne ke baad ek aspataal main gaya tha.
 july 2007 main fox samachar ne marsile main hospital di laa timone ke dolainel fivillette ko yah kehete hue uddhrut kiya tha: "tasviren bahut hi asaamaanya thim. ..mastishk lagbhag anupasthit tha." jab doctoron ne us vyakti kii chikitsa kaa itihaas janaa tab unhone ek abhikalan tomography scan or chumbakeey anunaad imaging scan kiya tathaa khopadi main parshvik nilyon ke "vyapak parivardhan" ko dekhakar chakit rah gaye. budhimatta pareekshanon ne darshaya ki us vyakti ke paas 100 ke ausat score se neeche 75 kaa bauddhik str tha ise "seemaayi bauddhik kaary" mana gaya - jo aadhikarik taur par maansik rup se viklang maane jane ke bilkul agle str par he.
 ullekhneey rup se, vah aadmi shaadishuda or do bacchon kaa pita tha tathaa ek janasevak ke rup main kaam kar chukaa tha, mastishk ke utakon kii kam maatra or bade hue nilyon ke baavjood kam se kam satahi taur par vah ek saamaanya jivan vyateet kar rahaa tha. ek bal chikitsa mastishk dosha visheshgy dow maiks muinke ne rashtriya maanav jinom research institute main tippanee kii, "mujhe yah baat aaj tak ascharyajanak lagati he ki aapako jo kuch jivan ke saath sangat nahin lagata he, uske saath mastishk kaise samjhauta kar saktaa he. "yadi kaaphi samay se, shaayad dashakon se, bahut dhire dhire kuch hota he to mastishk ke vibhinn bhaag us kaary ko apane jimme le lete hai jo saamaanya rup se us hisse dwaara kiya jaataa he jise kinare kar diya gaya ho."
