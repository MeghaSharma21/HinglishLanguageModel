 smitha rajan kerala ke mohiniattam nrity kalakaar or mahaan bhartiya shastreey nartak padam shri kalamandam krishnan nair or kalamandalam kalyanickutti ammaa kii yugal kii potee hai. unka janm 1969 main hua tha, unki maa shridevi rajan ek prasiddh mohiniattam guru or smith kii shikshak hai. unke pita divangat tii aar raajppan the.
 smita rajan ne kochi se tripunithura main apane naanaa ke nivas par nrity main apana prashikshan shuru kiya tha. guru kala vijayan ne unhen bharatnatyam main prashikshan dena shuru kiya or smith ne 4 varsh kii umr main bharatnatyam main unke arnagram prarambh kiya. unki maa, guru shridevi rajan, ne mohiniattam main apana pehla paath sikhaaya or smith ne 6 varsh kii aayu main mohiniattem main unke arnagram kaa pradarshan kiya.vah 12 saal kii umr main peshewar nartki ban gai or 1980 main mohiniattem ko lokapriy banane ke dauraan apni bhavy maa ke saath duniya bhar main or videshon main kai jagahon main ghumi. vah 1979 se 1992 tak kerala kallaayam ke pramukh kalakaar the. unhone apni maa, daadi or unki maasi ko mohiniattem ko kalakaaron ko kai maheenon tak padhaane main madad kii he. aajkal smitha rajan apane parivaar ke saath saint louis, mizori main rahati he or santhaan nridhikta "nrity kaa mandir" chalaa rahi he.
