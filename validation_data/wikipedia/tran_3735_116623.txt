 oki dweepasmooh ) shimane dweepasmooh ke ang hai jo japan ke adhikaar main hai. inamen ek bada dweep he jise 'dogo' kehete hai tathaa teen chote-chote dweep, chiburishima, nishinoshima or naakanoshima hai jinhen samuhik rup se 'dogin' kaha jaataa he. 
 inkaa kul kshetrafal 346.1 varg kilometre he. kul tateey lambaai 130 mile he. dogo dweep kaa pramukh nagar sago he jo shimane dweep ke sakaai bandargah se 40 mile dur he. 'oki-no-shima' kaa arth he 'dur ke dweep'. inaki japani itihaas main bada mahatvapurn sthaan rahaa he.
