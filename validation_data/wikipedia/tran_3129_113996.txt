 nauka asana:is asana kii antim avasthaa main hamaare sharir kii akruti nauka samaan dikhaai detii he, isi kaaran ise naukasana kehete he. is asana kii ginati peeth ke bal letkar kiye jane vaale asana main maani jaati he.
 pet ke bal let jaaen! jaise dandavat pranaam karte hai! 
 isi mudra me donon hatheliyaan paraspar judy rakh kar donon bahen tathaa sir va donon pairo ko ek saath upar kii or uthaanaa he 
 jitna der ruk saken ruken! 
 isse pachan kriya, choti-badi aant main laabh miltaa he. anguthe se anguliyon tak khinchaav hone ke kaaran shuddh rakt teevr gati se pravaahit hota he, jisase kaaya nirogi bani rahati he. hernia rog main bhi yah asana laabhadaayak mana gaya he. nidra adhik aati ho to use niyantrit karne me ye nauka asana sahaayak he!
 note:- vishesh laabh hetu - asana karte samay apana gurumantra kaa jap karen! yadi durbhaagya se gurumantra nahin mila he to  namah shivaay mantra kaa hi jap karen! athwa jo bhi mantra man ko bhaataa ho, asana ke saath - saath usika jap karen!
