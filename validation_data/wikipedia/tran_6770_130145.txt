 asother kasbaa asother janapad fatehpur mukhyalay se 30 kilometre or neshnal haive thariyaanw se 12 kilometre ki duuri par yamuna nadi main kinare par fatehpur janapad ke mukhyalay ke dakshini seemaa par sthit he. 11ve shatabdi ke madhya raja bhagwant raay khinchi ji ne ise basaaya tha. guru dronacharya ke putr ashwasthama ne mahabharat kaal main bramhastra paane ke liye yaheen par aakar tapasya kii thi, tabhi se is basti kaa naam asufal ho gaya jo kaalaantar main asother ke naam se jane janaa laga . khichi vansh ke raajaaon main raja bhagwant raay ne ashwasthama kaa mandir banwaaya . aisi manyata he ki ashwasthama aaj bhi ajar - amar he or apni taposthali va mote mahadev mandir main aaj bhi puja archana karne aate hai . tabhi to samooche kshetra ko ashwasthama kaa mandir aasthaa or vishwaas main samete hue he . kehete hai ki mahabharat kaal main pandav agyatavaas ke dauraan yahan tike the. janapad fatehpur ke mukhya paryatak sthalon ke rup main vikhyath pandavkaleen pracheen mandir baba ashwasthama dham, siddh peeth mote mahadev mandir asother ke dakshin main sthit he.
