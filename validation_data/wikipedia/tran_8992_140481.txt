 rashtriya takneekee santhaan, srinagar, kashmir kii sthapana 1960 main kii gai thi tathaa 7 agast 2003 ko ise rashtriya praudyogiki santhaan kaa darja diya gaya tha. santhaan civil ejineering, vidyut ejineering, electranik tathaa sanchaar ejineering, maikenical ejineering, rasayan tathaa dhaatukarmee ejineering, vishyon main avar snaatak pathyakram tathaa sanchaar evam soochna praudyogiki or maikenical system dijin main emai pathyakram sanchalit karata he. santhaan sabhi vigyaan vibhaagon tathaa kuch ejineering vibhaagon hetu em.phil tathaa pi.ech-di. kaarykram bhi pradaan karata he. pustkaalay tathaa prashasan sahit vibhinn vibhaagon ke bich network sthapit kiya gaya he.
