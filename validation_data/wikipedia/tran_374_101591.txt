 maamluk samrajya ke bich misr tathaa pashchimi arab par shashan karne vaala ek samrajya tha. terahavin sadi ke uttarardh main inaki ladaai mongolon se hui thi jisamen ve vijayi rahe the. iske alawa dharmayudhon main bhi imaki bhoomika rahi thi. mongolon dwaara baghdad ke luute jane ke baad kahiraa main inkaa ek caliph bhi hota tha. 
 yah samrajya ek samay ghulaam ke rup main rahe logon dwaara shasit tha - ye ghulaam mukhaytah turk hote the jinhen maamluk kaha jaataa tha. uttari bharat main bhi lagbhag isi samay ghulaam vansh sthapit hua tha jinke shaasha turk nasl ke ghulaam the.
 naveen sadi se hi abbasi kilaafat main turk ghulaamon kaa chalan tha. caspian sagar ke pashchim tathaa purv donon jagahon par turk rahete the. purv ke turk irani raajaaon dwaara khride jate the jabaki pashchimi turk syrian or arab amiron dwaara. inke kaam raajaaon kii raksha karne se lekar sainya netritv tathaa gharelu kaamon tak ke hote the. ye apni seva se prasann karke azaadi paa sakate the or apane swami dwaara chune gaye turk ghulaam ladakiyon se shaadi kar sakate the. kai jagahon par inkaa vandhyakaran bhi kar diya jaataa tha jisase ye parivaar bana paane main asamarth the. inako gilmaan yaa maamluk kehete the. caliph al mutsim ne inaki kshamta ko dekhakar inako bahut saraahaa or
