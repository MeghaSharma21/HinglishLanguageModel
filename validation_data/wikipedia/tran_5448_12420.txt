 saamuraai yaa samurai madhyakaalin saamanti japan ke nimna stareey sainik sargana ko kaha jaataa tha. is shabd kaa prayog baad main saamaanya sainik ke liye kiya jane laga. kai europea shaktiyon ne bhi saamuraai vastron main ladayeeyaa ladi. pashchimi sabhyataa main yadhyapi saamuraai kaa prayog kam hi rahaa.
 yah shabd pracheen japani bhashaa se aayaa he jiskaa shabdic arth hota he - seva karnaa.
