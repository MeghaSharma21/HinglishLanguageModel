 anuvanshiki or rog main bahudhaa koi na koi sambandh rahata he. anek rog dooshit vaataavaran tathaa paristhatiyon se utpann hote hai, kintu anek rog aise bhi hote hai jinka kaaran mata-pita se janmana praapt koi dosha hota he. e rog anuvanshik rog kehalaate hai. kuch aise rog bhi hai jo anuvanshiki tathaa vaataavaran donon ke prabhaavon ke falswaroop utpann hote hai.
 jeevon main nar ke shukranu tathaa stri kii andkoshika ke sanyog se santan kii utpatti hoti he. shukranu tathaa andkoshika donon main cendrakasutra rahete hai. in cendrakasutron main sthit jin ke swabhavanusaar santan ke maansik tathaa shaareerik gun or dosha nishchit hote hai. jin main se ek yaa kuch ke doshatpadak hone ke kaaran santan main ve hi dosha utpann ho jate hai. kuch dosha main se koi rog utpann nahin hota, keval santan kaa shaareerik sanghathan aisa hota he ki usamen vishesh prakaar ke rog sheeghra utpann hote hai. isliye yah nishchit jaananaa ki rog kaa kaaran anuvanshikta he yaa pratikul vaataavaran, sarvada saadhy nahin he. anuvanshik rogon kii sahi gananaa main anya kathinaaiyaan bhi hai. udaahranarth; bahut se janmajaat rog adhik aayu ho jane par hi prakat hote hai. duusari ore, kuch anuvanshik doshayukt bachche janm lete hi mar jate hai.
 tirodhayak rogakaarak jin ke upasthit rahane par inke prabhaav se rog prayek peedi main prakat hota he, kintu tirohit jin ke kaaran honevaale rog vansh kii kisi santan main anayas utpann ho jate hai, jaisaa mendel ke anuvanshikta vishyak niyamon se spasht he. kuch rog ladakiyon se kahin adhik sankhya main ladakon main paae jate hai.
 anuvanshik rogon ke anek udhaaharan die ja sakate hai. inamen se kuch nimnlikhit hai :
 tirodhayak jin ke dosha se motiyobind, ati nikatadrushti, glocoma, dirghadrushti ityadi rog hote hai. tirohit jin ke kaaran vivarnataa, astigmatism, keratoconus, ityadi rog utpann hote hai. lingagrathit jin janit chakshurogon main, jo purushon main adhik hote hai, varnandhta, dinaandhta, rataundhi ityadi rog hai.
 inamen ek sau se adhik anuvanshik rogon kii gananaa kii gai he. inamen psorisis, ikthiasis, keratosis ityadi pramukh hai.
 adhikangulata, yuktangulata, kai prakaar kabonapan, asthiyon kaa uchit reeti se na viksit hona, janm se hi nitambasthi kaa ukhda rahanaa ityadi
 hemophilia, vishesh prakaar kii raktaheenata ityadi.
 madhumeh, gathiya, chehre kaa vikrut tathaa bhayaavah ho janaa ityadi.
 sanak, mirgee, alpbuddhita ityadi kaa bhi kaaran anuvanshikta ho sakati hai. vividh rog, jaise bahraapan, goongaapan, kataa hont, videern taalu aadi bhi anuvanshikta se prabhavit hote hai. iske sivaay anuvanshikta ghegha, ucch raktpach karkat ityadi rogon kii ore jhukaav utpann kar detii he.
