 sarovigyaan nadiyon, jhiilon, talabon, jalprapaaton, ardrabhumion, pani ke chashamon or bhumigat anya aise jal-samoohon kaa adhyayan he jo samudr se alag hai. yah shabd 'sarovar' se liya gaya he lekin dhyaan den ki yah keval sarovaron hi kaa adhyayan nahin he. vigyaan kii is shakha main in jalsamoohon ko jeevugyanik, rasinic, bhautik or bhuvaigyanik drushti se samajha jaataa he.
