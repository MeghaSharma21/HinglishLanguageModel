 ankitha gaba ek internet udhyami or "soshal samosa" kii sah-sansthaapak hai. 
 ankitha mumbai kii rahane vaali hai, unhone wilson college se bijness management main snaatak kiya. 2010 main unhone weylingkar institut of management se embiai puri kii. 
 ankitha ne 2007 main piaar udyog main apana vyavsaayik karier shuru kiya, hastiyon or filmon ke liye jansampark kaa prabandhan kiya. june 2008 main, unhone bharat kii pehali soshal media agenciyon main se ek, superchuha, se apana pehla kaam shuru kiya. ankitha saamaajik media club ke mumbai adhyay ke gathan main ek sarjak kaaryakrtta rahe hai, soshal media club ek antarrashtriya enjio he jo duniyabar ke soshal media visheshgyon ko jodne ke liye kaam karata he. 2011 main, usne bharat main soshal media paridrishy ke bare main vichaaron, vichaaron or sanvaadon ke liye gyaan bhandar kaa ek pramukh strot soshalsmosa dot com kii sthapana kii. yah portal nijee niveshkon ko may 2015 main 2-3 karod ke liye bech diya gaya tha.
