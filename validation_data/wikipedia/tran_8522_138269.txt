 maance, maans yaa gosht un jeev utakon or mukhyatah mansapeshiyon ko kehete hai, jinka seven bhojan ke rup main kiya jaataa he. maance shabd, ko aam bolchaal or vyavsaayik vargeekaran ke aadhaar par bhoomi par rahane vaale pashuon ke gosht ke sandarbh me prayog kiya jaataa he, jaleey matsya jeevon kaa maance machhlee kii shrenee main jabaki anya samudri jeev jaise ki krastasian, molsk aadi samudri bhojan kii shrenee main aate hai. vaise yah vargeekaran purnat: lagu nahin hota or kai samudri standhari jeevon ko kabhi maance to kabhi machhlee mana jaataa he. poshaahar kii drushti se maanav ke bhojan main maance, protein, vasa or khanijon kaa ek aam or acha strot he. sabhi pashuon or paudhon se praapt bhojanon main, maance ko sabase uttam ke saath sabase adhik vivaadaaspad khaadya padaarth bhi mana jaataa he. jahaan poshan kii drushti se maance ek poshaahar hai vahiin vibhinn dharm iske seven se apane anuyaayiyon ko dur rahane kii salaah dete hai. jo pashu purna rup se apane bhojan ke liye maance par nirbhar hote hai unhen maansaahaari kaha jaataa he, iske vipareet, vanaspati khaane vaale pashuon ko shaakaahaari kehete hai. jo paudhe chote jeevon or keede kaa seven karte hai mansabhakshi padap kehalaate he.
