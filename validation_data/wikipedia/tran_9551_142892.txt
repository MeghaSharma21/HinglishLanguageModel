 bhautik saakshyon ke parikshan evam prativedan ko jo vyakti nyaayalay ke samaksh tathyon ko lekar jaataa he, us mukhya vyakti ko visheshgy kehete hai. visheshgy pramanit kiye gaye sabhi parikshanon ko achche tareeqe se jaankaree ekatrit karne ke alaava sambhandit sahitya main apane se varisht sahyogiyon kii sahaayta lekar bhi report taiyaar kar saktaa he. visheshgy apni shaishnik yogyata, pareekshan kaa prakaar evam avadhi, sambandhi vishay main parikshan kaary kaa anubhav aadi ke bal par nyaayalay main apni yogyata siddh karata he.
 visheshgy jahaan matvpurn sabooton ko unke mahatv ke anusaar jamaa karte hai, vahiin ghatana sthal par hi praarambhik pareekshan karke anaavashyak vaastuo ko ekatrut karte hai. visheshgy aatmahatya, hatya, or durghatana kii sambhaavna vaale vyaktiyon kii pahchaan karte hue nyaay kii disha bhi tay kr sakate hai. ghatana sthal par paae gaye oongali ke nishaan, padchinh, tair chinh aadi kaa sangrakshan karte hai or shav parikshan ke dauraan chikitsak ko ghatana sthal evam shav ke bare main upyogi jaankaree bhi pradaan karte hai. visheshgy apradhi ke kaary-style yani uske aane jane or hatya main hathyaar istemaal karne ke tareeqe ko bhi bataane main sahaayak hote hai.
