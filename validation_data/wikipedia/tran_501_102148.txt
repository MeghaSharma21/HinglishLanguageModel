 ramnarayan upaadhyaay hindi ke prasiddh lekhak hai. unka janm 2 may 1918 ko kaalmukhi, madhya pradesh ke khandwa main hua or shiksha gaanv main hui. aapane gandhi-jivan se lekhan kii prerana lekar lekhan armbh kiya.	
 bhashaa style- sahaj, saral prabhaavshaalee gadhy. inke vyangyon ke bare main agyeya ji ne likhaa he- madhur vyangy jo jaadon ke ghaam jaisaa snigdh, apane man se bhar jaae or sadha hua sookshm sparsh jo bhav ko jagaae par chankane na de.
 prakashit kritiyaan-vyangy, lalit nibandh, roopak, reportaj, laghu kathaayen, sansmaran aadi vidhaon main 30 se adhik pustaken prakashit.ham to babul tere baag kii chidiya nimad kaa saanskrithik adhyayan	bakshishnama, dhundle kaanch kii diwaar, naak kaa sawaal, muskaraati filein, ganwain man or gaanv kii yaad, doosraa suraj aadi vyangy sankalan hai.janam-janam ke phere mrug ke chhaune jinaki chaaya bhi sukhkar he tathaa jinhen bhul na sakaa kathaon kii antarktha, chitthee, maamuli aadmi aadi prasiddh pustaken hai.
