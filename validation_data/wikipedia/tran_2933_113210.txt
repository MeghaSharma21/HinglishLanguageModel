 jeremi bentham  england kaa nyayavid, darshanik tathaa vidhik va saamaajik sudhaarak tha. vah upyogetavaad kaa kattar samarthak tha. vah prakritik vidhi tathaa prakritik adhikaar ke siddhanton kaa kattar virdohi tha. 
 san 1776 main uski 'shashan par sfoot vichaar' sheershak pustak prakashit hui. isamen usne yah mat vyakt kiya ki kisi bhi kanoon kii upyogita kii kasuti yah he ki jin logon se uskaa sambandh ho, unke anand, hit or sukh kii adhik se adhik vruddhi vah kare. uski duusari pustak 'aachaar or vidhaan ke siddhant' 1789 main nikali jisamen uske upyogetavaad kaa saar marm sannihit he. usne is baat par bal diya ki 'adhiktam vyaktiyon kaa adhiktam sukh' hi prayek vidhaan kaa lakshya hona chahie. 'upyogita' kaa siddhant vah arthshaastra main bhi lagu karnaa chahta tha. uskaa vichaar tha ki prayek vyakti ko, kisi bhi tarah ke pratibandh ke bina, apana hit sampann karne kii swatantraaa rahani chahie. soodakhori ke samarthan main usne ek pustak 'dephence ov yuzari' san 1787 main likhi thi. usne garibon sambandhi kanoon main sudhaar karne ke liye jo suzav die, unheen ke aadhaar par san 1834 main usamen kai sanshodhan kiye gaye. parliament main sudhaar karaane ke sambandh main bhi usne ek pustak likhi thi . isamen usne suzav diya tha ki matdaan kaa adhikaar prayek vayask vyakti ko milanaa chahie or chunaav prati varsh kiya janaa chahie. usne bandigruhon ke sudhaar par bhi bal diya. or 1811 main 'dand or puraskaar' sheershak ek pustak likhi.
