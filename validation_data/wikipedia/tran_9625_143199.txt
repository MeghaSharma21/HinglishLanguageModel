 uzen austria kaa field marshall or mahan raajnneetijna tha.
 uzen kaa janm paris main hua tha. vah savaay ke uzen morris kaa paanchavaan ladkaa tha. france ke rajya darbar main apni mata ke virodh ke kaaran usne austria kii sena main naukari kar lee. 1683 main vienna ke ghere or turk virdohi abhiyaan main usko bahut khyaati mili. austria kii sena ke senapati ke rup main 1697 main usne turkon ko buree tarah parajeet kiya. 1701 or 1702 main italy ke abhiyaan ke baad spain ke uttaradhikaar yuddh main usne mitra senaaon kaa sanyukt senapatitv kiya. 3 agast 1704 ko turin yuddh main uski sainya shakti kaa adbhut parichay mila. 1716 or 1717 ke turk virdohi abhiyaan main bhi use bahut badi safalataa mili. baad main apni pratibha ke bal par hi vah samrat charles shashth kaa raajnneetik salaahkaar bana.
 uzen ek mahan yoddha or ucch koti kaa sainik abhiyaan sanchaalak tha. vah kala kaa sangrakshak or vidwaanon kaa mitra tha. 21 april 1736 ko avivaahit avasthaa main uski mrityu vienna main hui.
