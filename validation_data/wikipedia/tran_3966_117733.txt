 nayi kavita bhartiya swatantraaa ke baad likhi gayi un kavitaaon ko kaha gaya, jinamein paramparaagat kavita se aage naye bhavabodhon kii abhivyakti ke saath hi naye mulyon or naye shilp-vidhaan kaa anveshan kiya gaya. yah bhi kaha ja saktaa he ki prayogwaad ke baad hindi kavita kii jo naveen dhara viksit hui, vah nai kavita he. nayi kavita naam azaadi ke baad likhi gai un kavitaaon ke liye rutes ho gaya, jo apni vastu-chhavi or rup-chhavi donon main pragativaad or prayogwaad kaa vikaas hokar bhi vishisht he.
 nayi kavita-andolan kaa aarambh elahabaad kii saahitiyik sanstha parimal ke kavi lekhakon- jagadish gupt, ramswarup chaturvedi or vijayadevanrayan saahi ke sampadan main 1954 main prakashit "nayi kavita" patrika se mana jaataa he. isse pehle agyeya ke sampadan main prakashit kavy-sangrah 'doosraa saptak' kii bhoomika tathaa usamen shaamil kuch kaviyon ke vaktavyon main apni kaviton ke liye 'nayi kavita' shabd ko sweekaar kiya gaya tha.
 nayi kavita ke liye jagat-jivan se sambandhit koi bhi sthiti, sambandh, bhav yaa vichaar kathy ke rup main tyajya nahin he. janm se lekar maran tak aaj ke maanav-jivan kaa jin sthitiyon, paristhitiyon, sambandhon, bhaavon, vichaaron or kaaryon se saahchary hota he, unhen nayi kavita ne abhivyakt kiya he. nae kavi ne kisi bhi kathy ko tyajya nahin samajha he. kathy ke prati nayi kavita main swanubhuti kaa aagrah he. nayaa kavi apane kathy ko usee rup main prastut karnaa chahta he, jis rup main use vah anubhoot karata he.
 nayi kavita vaad-mukti kii kavita he. isse pehle ke kavi bhi praayah kisi na kisi vaad kaa sahaara avashya lete the. or yadi kavi vaad kii parvaah na karen, kintu aalochak to uski rachana main kavy se pehle vaad khojataa tha-vaad se kavy kii parakh hoti thi. kintu nayi kavita kii sthiti bhinn he. nayaa kavi kisi bhi siddhant, matvaad, sampradaay yaa drushti ke aagrah kii kattarta main fasane ko taiyaar nahin. sankshep main, nayi kavita koi vaad nahin he, jo apane kathy or drushti main simit ho. kathy kii vyapaktaa or sristi kii unmuktata nai kavita kii sabase badi visheshta he.
 nai kavita parampara ko nahin maanati. manushyon main vaiyaktik bhinnata hoti he, usamen achchhiyaan bhi hai or buraayeeyaan bhi. nai kavita ke kavi ko manushya in sabhi roopon main pyaara he. uskaa uddeshy manushya kii samagrata kaa chithran he. nai kavita jivan ke prati aasthaa kii rakhti he. aaj kii kshanavaadi or laghumanavvaadi drushti jivan-mulyon ke prati sweekaaratmak drushti he. nai kavita main jivan kaa purna sweekaar karke use bhogane kii laalasaa he. jivan kii ek-ek anubhutiyon ko, vyathaa ko, sukh ko, satya maanakar jivan ko saghan rup se sweekaar karnaa kshamon ko satya maananaa he.
 nai kavita ne jivan ko na to ekangi rup main dekha na keval mahat rup main, usne jivan ko jivan ke rup main dekha. isamen koi seemaa nirdhaarit nahin kii. jaise- dukh sabko maanjata he or, chaahe swayan sabko mukti dena na jane, kintu-jisko maanjata he, unhen yah sikh detaa he ki, sabko mukt rakhen. 
 nai kavita main do tatv pramukh hai- anubhuti kii sachai or buddhimulak yathartwaadee drushti. vah anubhuti kshan kii ho yaa ek samooche kaal kii, kisi saamaanya vyakti kii ho yaa vishisht purush kii, aashaa kii ho yaa niraashaa kii, apni sachai main kavita ke liye or jivan ke liye bhi amuly he. nai kavita main buddhivaad naveen yathartwaadee drushti ke rup main bhi he or naveen jivan-chetana kii pahchaan ke rup main bhi. yahi kaaran he ki tatasth prayogshiltaa nai kavita ke kathy or style-donon kii visheshta he. nayaa kavi apane kathy ke prati tatasth vritti rakhata he, kyonki uskaa praytn vaadon se mukt rahane kaa rahata he. is visheshta ke kaaran nai kavita main kathyon kii koi ek paridhi nahin he. isamen to kathy se kathy kii nai paraten ughadati aati hai. kabhi-kabhi vah apane hi kathanon kaa khandan bhi kar detaa he- eemaandaaree ke kaaran. nayaa kavi duubakar bhogata he, kintu bhogate hue dub nahin jaataa.
 nai kavita jivan ke ek-ek kshan ko satya maanati he or us satya ko puri hardikta or puri chetana se bhogane kaa samarthan karti he. anubhuti kii sachai, jitna vah le paata he, utana hi uske kavy ke liye satya he. nai kavita anubhutipurna gahare kshanon, prasangon, vyapaar yaa kisi bhi satya ko uski aantarik maarmikata ke saath pakad lenaa chaahati he. is prakaar jivan ke saamaanya se saamaanya deekhanevaale vyapaar yaa prasang nai kavita main nayaa arth paa jate hai. nai kavita main kshanon kii anubhutiyon ko lekar bahut-si marmasparshi kavitaayen likhi gai hai. jo aakaar main choti hoti hai kintu prabhaav main atyant teevr. nai kavita parampara ko nahin maanati. in kaviyon ne paramparawaadi jadta kaa virodh kiya he. pragatisheel kaviyon ne parampara kii jadta kaa virodh isliye kiya he ki vah logon ko shoshan kaa shikaar banaati he or duniya ke mazdooron tathaa daliton ko ek jhande ke neeche ekatra hone main baadha daalati he. nayaa kavi uskaa virodh isliye karata he ki uske kaaran maanav-vivek kunthit ho jaataa he.
 nai kavita saamaajik yatharth tathaa usamen vyakti kii bhoomika ko parakhane kaa prayaas karti he. iske kaaran hi nai kavita kaa saamaajik yatharth se gaharaa sambandh he. parantu nai kavita kii yathartwaadee drushti kaalpanik yaa adarshavaadi maanavvaad se santrusht na hokar jivan kaa mulya, uskaa saundarya, uskaa prakaash jivan main hi khojati he. nai kavita dvivedi kaaleen kavita, chhayavaad yaa pragativaad kii tarah apane bane-banaaye moolyalaadi nuskhe pesh nahin karti, balki vah to use jivan kii sachhee vyathaa ke bheetar paanaa chaahati he. isliye nai kavita main vyangy ke rup main kahin puraane mulyon kii asweekruti he, to kahin dard kii sachai ke bheetar se ugte hue nae mulyon kii sambhaavna ke prati aasthaa. nai kavita ne dharm, darshan, niti, aachaar sabhi prakaar ke mulyon ko chunauti di he. nai kavita kaa swar apane parivesh kii jivananubhuti se phuta he.
 nai kavita main shahari jivan or gramin-jivan-donon pariveshon ko lekar likhanewaale kavi hai. agyeya ne donon par likhaa he. jabaki baalkrishna raav, shamsher bahadur singh, girijakumar maathur, kunvaranarayan singh, dharmaveer bharti, prabhakar machave, vijayadevnarayan saahi, raghuveer sahay aadi kavi kii samvednaayen or anubhutiyaan shahari parivesh kii hai to duusari ore bhavaniprasad mishra, kedarnath singh, shambhunath singh, thakurprasad singh, nagarjun, kedarnath agarwal aadi aise kavi hai jo multah gaanv kii anubhutiyaan or samvednaa se jude hai. inke atirikt usamen jahaan ghutan, vyarthta, ub, paraajay, hin-bhav, aakrosh hai, vahiin atmpeedan parak bhavanaayen bhi hai. nai kavita kaa parivesh apane yahan kaa jivan he. kintu us par akshep he ki usamen atirikt anasthaa, niraashaa, vyaktivaadee kuntha or marandharmita he jo pashchim kii nakal se paidaa hui he.
 nai kavita main pidaa or niraashaa ko kahin-kahin jivan kaa ek paksh na maanakar samgr jivan-satya maan liya gaya he. vahaan pidaa jivan kii sarjanaatmak shakti na banakar use gatiheen karanevaali baadha batch gai he. lok-sampukti nai kavita kii ek khaas visheshta he. vah sahaj lok-jivan ke kareeb pahunchne kaa praytn kar rahi he. bhavani prasad mishra kii kavitaaon kii samiksha karte hue professor mahavir saran jain kaa kathan he ki "hindi kii nai kavita par sabase bada akshep yah he ki usamen atirikt anasthaa, niraashaa, vishaad, hataasha, kuntha or marandharmita he. usko padhane ke baad jeene kii lalak samaapt ho jaati he, vyakti hatotsahit ho jaataa he, man nirashaavadi or maranasann ho jaataa he. yah ki nai kavita ne pidaa, vednaa, shok or niraashaa ko hi jivan kaa satya maan liya he.
 nai kavita bharat kii jameen se prerana praapt nahin karti. iske vipareet yah pashchim kii nakal se paidaa hui he. bhavani prasad mishra kii kavitaayen in saare aaropon ko dhwast kar detii hai. mishra ji gandhivaadi he. gandhi kii desh-bhakthi manzil nahin he. gandhi ji kii desh-bhakthi vishwa ke jeev maatr ke prati prem or uski seva karne ke liye unki jivan yatra kaa ek padaav he. unke vichaar main kahin bhi lesh maatr bhi niraashaa kaa bhav nahin he. usamen aashaa, vishwaas or aasthaa kii jyoti aalokit he. isi alok ke kaaran gandhi ji ne dakshin-africa or bharat main jo jan-aandolan chalaae unhone sampoorn samaaj main nai jagriti, nai chetana or nayaa sankalp bhar diya. unke jivan darshan se vishaad, niraashaa or maran-dharmita nahin apitu iske sarvatha vipareet nai aashaa, nai aasthaa or nai umang paidaa hoti he. usase satya, ahimsa evam prem kii triveni pravaahit hoti he. . bhavani prasad mishra kii kavitaayen isi kaaran samaaj main jo vipann hai, laachaar hai, thake hue hai, dharashayi hai un sabko sahaara dene ke liye prerit karti hai, unako uthaane ke liye protsaahit karti hai".
 nai kavita ne lok-jivan kii anubhuti, saundarya-bodh, prakrutti or uske prashnon ko ek sahaj or udaar maanviya bhoomi par grahan kiya. saath hi saath lok-jivan ke bimbon, pratikon, shabdon or upmaanon ko lok-jivan ke bich se chunakar usne apane ko atyadhik samvednapurna or sajiv banaaya. kavita ke upree aayojan nai kavita vahan nahin kar sakati. vah apni antarlay, bimbaatmakataa, naveen pratik-yojana, naye visheshnon ke prayog, naveen upmaan main kavita ke shilp kii maanya dharanaon se baaki alag he.
 nai kavita kii bhashaa kisi ek paddati main bandhakar nahin chalati. shashakt abhivyakti ke liye bolchaal kii bhashaa kaa prayog isamen adhik hua he. nai kavita main keval sanskrit shabdon ko hi aadhaar nahin banaaya he, balki vibhinn bhashaaon ke prachlit shabdon ko sweekaar kiya gaya he. nae shabd bhi bana liye gayi hai. toye, bhabhake, khinchaa, siti, thithuran, thasakna, chidchidi, thunth, viras, siraaya, phunagiana â€“ jaise anek shabd nai kavita main dhadlle se prayukt hue hai. jisase isaki bhashaa main ek khulaapan or taazgi dikhaai detii he. isaki bhashaa main lok-bhashaa ke tatv bhi samahit hai.
 nai kavita main pratikon kii adhikta he. jaise- saanp tum sabhy to hue nahin, na honge, nagar main basna bhi tumhen nahin aayaa. ek baat poonchhu? uttar doge! phir kaise siikhaa dansana? vish kahan paaya? nai kavita main bimb bhi vipul maatra main uplabdh he. nai kavita kii vividh rachanaaon main shabd, arth, takneekee, mukt aasang, divaswapn, saahchary, puranic, prakruti sambandhi kavy bimb nirmitt kiye gayi hai. jaise- saamne mere sardi main bore ko odhakar, koi ek apane, haath pair samete, kaanp rahaa, hill rahaa,-vah mar jaayegaa. 
 nai kavita main chand ko keval ghor asweekruti hi mili ho- yah baat nahin, balki is kshetra main vividh prayog bhi kiye gayi hai. naye kaviyon main kisi bhi madhyam yaa shilp ke prati na to raag he or na viraag. gatishalata ke prabhaav ke liye sangeet kii lay ko tyagkar nai kavita dhwani-parivartan kii ore badhati gai he. ek varnya vishay yaa bhav ke sahare uskaa sangopang vivaran prastut karte hue lambii kavita yaa puri kavita likhakar use kavy-niabandh banane kii puraani style nai kavita ne tyaag di he. nai kavita ke kaviyon ne lambii kavitaayen bhi likhi hai. kintu ve puraane prabandh kavy ke samaanaantar nahin he. nai kavita kaa prayek kavi apni nijee vishistataa rakhata he. nae kaviyon ke liye pradhan he sampreshan, na ki sampreshan kaa madhyam. is prakaar ham dekhte hai ki nai kavita kathy or shilp-donon hi drushtiyon se mahatvapurn uplabdhi he.
