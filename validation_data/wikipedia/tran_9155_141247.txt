 bakaayan neem kii jati kaa ek ped he. bharat main praayah sabhi praanton main paaya jaataa he. neem se chota evam achirsthai hota he. iske pattey kadave neem ke samaan tathaa aakaar main kuch bade hote hai. bakin kii lakdi imarti kaamon ke liye bahut upyogi hota he. yah chhayaadaar ped hota he. iske fal bhi kadave neem ke fal ke samaan hote hai.
 isko mahanim bhi kehete hai. yah ek aushadhiy ped he. ayurveda main bakaayan kaa bahut mahatva he.
 isaki pattiyaan neem kii pattiyon ke samaan tathaa kuch badi or durgandhyukt hoti he.
 gucchhon main neem ke phool ke aakaar vaale kuch neelaapan liye laliyukt hote hai. 
 jhumakon main neem ke fal ke aakaar vaale gol hote hai.
