 asi/disi ek australian rock band he jise 1973 main malcom or angus yang bhaaiyon ne banaaya tha or jo ant tak mukhya sadsy bane rahe. mukhyatah hard rock shrenee ke is band ko heavi metal shrenee kaa racheta bhi kaha jaataa he parantu ve swayan ko hameshaa keval "rock and role" shrenee kaa band hi kehete aae he. aaj kii taarikh tak yah abtak ke sarvaadhik kamai vaale bandon main se ek he. 17 farvari 1975 main apana pehla album hai voltage release karne se purv asi/disi ke sadasyon main kaafii badlaav kiya gaya. 1977 tak sadsyta sthir rahi par ant main baas vadak mark ivance ko cliff williams se badal diya gaya or album powerij release kiya gaya. album hive tu hell kii recording ke kuch maah pashchyaat hi mukhya gayak or geetkaar bone scot sharaab ke baari nashe ke chalate 19 farvari 1980 ko chal based. samooh ne is ghatana ke baad swany ko band karne kaa vichaar kiya parantu scot ke mata-pita ne unhen nae gayak ko shaamil kar aage badhane kaa suzav diya. purv jordi band ke gayak bryan johnson ko scot kii jagah shaamil kar liya gaya. us varsh band ne apana sarvaadhik bikri vaala va abtak kaa kisi kalakaar dwaara tisra sarvaadhik bikri vaala album back in blike release kiya.
 band kaa aglaa album for doj about tu rock vi selut yu tha jo unka pehla album tha jo america main pratham kramank par pahunchne main safal rahaa.
