 naagarikta ek vishesh saamaajik, rajnatik, rashtriya, yaa maanav sansaadhan samudaay kaa ek naagarik hone kii avasthaa he. 
 saamaajik anubandh ke siddhant ke tahat naagarikta kii avasthaa main adhikaar or uttardaayitv donon shaamil hote hai. "sakriy naagarikta" kaa darshan arthath nagarikon ko sabhi nagarikon ke jivan main sudhaar karne ke liye aarthik sahabhaagita, saarvajanik, swayamsevi kaary or isi prakaar ke prayaason ke madhyam se apane samudaay ko behatar banane kii disha main kaary karnaa chahie. is disha main, kuch deshon main skool naagarikta shiksha uplabdh karte hai. varjeenia lieri ke dwaara naagarikta ko "adhikaron ke ek samucchy-ke rup main paribhaashit kiya gaya he- unke anusaar naagarikta kii avasthaa main praathmik rup se saamudayik jivan main rajnatik bhagidaari, matdaan kaa adhikaar, samudaay se vishesh sangrakshan praapt karne kaa adhikaar or daayitv shaamil hai. 
 sabhi nagarikon kaa nagari samooh kehalaata he.
 aam taur par naagarikta ko ek vyakti or ek vishesh desh ke bich sambandh ke rup main dekha jaataa he.
 pracheen greece main, mukhya rajnatik ikai shahar-rajya hota tha or naagarik vishesh shahar-rajya ke sadsy hote the. pichley paanch sau varshon main, raashtra-rajya ke vikaas ke saath, naagarikta ko ek vishesh raashtra kii sadsyta ke rup main janaa jane laga he. kuch had tak, kuch sansthaaen rashtriya simaaon ko paar kar jaati hai, jaise vyaper sanghathan, gaiyr-sarkari sanghathan or multi-neshnal karporation or kabhi kabhi shabd "vishwa kaa naagarik" un logon ke liye prayukt kiya jaataa he jo kisi vishesh raashtra ke saath kam sambandh rakhte hai, iske bajaay unamem puri duniya ke saath sambandhit hone kii bhaavana adhik hoti he.
 aadhunik samay main, naagarikta kii niti zeus sengunis  or zeus soli  rashtron ke bich vibhajit he. ek zeus sengunis niti jaateeyata yaa vansh ke aadhaar par naagarikta detii he or yah europe main prachlit ek raashtra rajya kii avadharana se sambandhit he. ek zeus soli niti har us vyakti ko naagarikta detii he jo rajya ke praant vishesh main paidaa hua he, is niti kaa anusaran sanyukt rajya sahit america ke kai deshon main kiya jaataa he. kai deshon main sthaneey raashtra kii ek sankar janm adhikaar aavashyaktaa or kam se kam ek abhibhavak kii naagarikta kii aavashyaktaa hoti he.
 aamtaur par sameekaran ke madhyam se yaa ek aise vyakti se vivaah karke bhi kisi desh kii naagarikta praapt kii ja sakati he jo us desh kaa naagarik he ). 
 haal hi ke varshon main, kuch antar sarkari sanghathanon ne naagarikta se sambandhit shabdaawalee or avadharana ko antarrashtriya str tak vistrut kiya he, jahaan yah nagarikon ke sanyukt avyav deshon kii samagrata par lagu hoti he. is str par naagarikta ek dwitiyak avadharana he, jahaan adhikaar rashtriya naagarikta se vyutpann hote hai. 
 "rashtramandal naagarikta" kii avadharana tab se astitv main he jab se rashtramandal rashtron kii sthapana hui he. jaisaa ki europea sangh ke maamle main hota he, koi vyakti rashtramandal naagarikta ko tabhi praapt kar saktaa he agar vah rashtramandal sadsy rajya kaa naagarik ho. is prakaar kii naagarikta kuch rashtramandal deshon main kuch visheshadhikaar uplabdh karaati he:
 haalanki ireland ne 1949 main rashtramandal ko chhod diya, aksar iske saath aisa vyavhaar kiya jaataa he, jaise ki yah rashtramandal kaa sadsy ho. kanoonee dastavejon main 'rashtramandal or ireland ganraajya' ke liye sandarbh diya jaataa he or iske nagarikon ko vishesh rup se sanyukt raashtra main videshi nagarikon ke rup main vargeekrut nahin kiya jaataa he.
 1921 main nishtha ke sandarbh main paribhaashit kiye jane par canada rashtriyata ke siddhant se alag ho gaya. 1935 main irish mukt rajya ne pehali baar apni naagarikta ko shuru kiya . canada kaa naagarikta adhinium jo 1 janvari 1947 ko astitv main aayaa, canada kii ek alag naagarikta uplabdh karaata he, yah un nagarikon ko swatah hi praapt ho jaati he jo canada main paidaa hue hai or saath hi isamen aisi paristhitiyon ko bhi paribhaashit kiya jaataa he jinke tahat koi vyakti samikrut naagarik ban saktaa he. rashtramandal naagarikta kii avadharana ko 1948 main british rashtriyata adhinium 1948 main shuru kiya gaya. anya dominian ne newzealand, british rashtriyata or newzealand naagarikta adhinium 1948 main is siddhant ko apanaaya. naagarikta ne nishtha ko pratisthapit kar diya he, jo ek pratikatmak parivartan kii tulana main kuch adhik he. 
 naagarikta adhiktar raashtra rajya kii sadsyta se sambandhit hoti he, parantu is shabd ko ek up rashtriya str bhi lagu kiya ja saktaa he. up rashtriya sansthaaen residency yaa anyathaa, aavashyaktaaon ko adhyaropit kar sakati hai, jo nagarikon ko us sanstha kii raajnneeti main hissaa lene kii anumati detii he, yaa is sanstha kii sarkaar ke dwaara uplabdh karaaye gaye phaaydon kaa anand uthaane kaa mauka pradaan karti he. lekin aise maamalon main, ye paatr kabhi kabhi praasangik rajya, praant yaa kshetra ke "naagarik" maane jate hai. iskaa ek udhaaharan bataata he ki swiss naagarikta kaa mul aadhaar kaise kisi vyaktigat commune kii naagarikta he, jisase ek kenton yaa confederation kii naagarikta praapt hoti he. ek anya udhaaharan he ek aland jahaan naagarik finland, hembigdasraat main vishesh prantiy naagarikta kaa laabh uthaate hai.
 sanyukt rajya america main ek dohari naagarikta pranali he jisamen vyakti apane nivas rajya ke saath sanyukt rajya kaa bhi naagarik he. rajya ke samvidhan sanyukt rajya ke samvidhan ke tahat die jane vaale adhikaron se pare or inase badhkar adhikaar pradaan kar sakate hai or saath hi karadhaan ke samprabhu adhikaar or sainya seva sahit apane dayitvon ko bhi adhyaropit kar sakate hai .

 sangh kii naagarikta iske dwaara sthapit he. har vyakti jiske paas sadsy rajya kii rashtriyata he, vah sangh kaa naagarik hoga. sangh kii naagarikta poorak hogi or rashtriya naagarikta ko pratisthapit nahin karegi. 
 sanshodhit europea sangh sandhi europea sangh ke nagarikon ke vishisht nunatam adhikaron ko sthapit karti he. sanshodhit yaropee sangh sandhi kaa anuchhed 12 sandhi ke daayre ke bheetar gaiyr bhedbhaav ke saamaanya adhikaar kii guarantee detaa he. anuchhed 18 sadsy rajyon main mukt aandolan or awaas kaa ek simit adhikaar detaa he, bajaay iske ki europea sangh ke naagarik rashtriya hai. anuchhed 18-21 lekh or 225 vishisht rajnatik adhikaar uplabdh karte hai. 
 sangh ke nagarikon ke paas kisi bhi sadsy rajya main aarthik gatividhi main hissaa lene kaa vyapak adhikaar bhi hota he, jo sangh naagarikta main shaamil hone ko purvadinaankit karata he.
 naagarikta kii avadharana pehle kanoonon se utpann hui. 
 pehle prakaar kii naagarikta is baat par aadhaarit thi ki log polis ke chote paimaane ke carbonic samudaayon main, kis tareeke se pracheen unani kaal main rahete the. un dinon main naagarikta ko ek saarvajanik maamle ke rup main nahin dekha jaataa tha, yah ek vyakti ke nijee jivan se alag hoti thi. naagarikta ke daayitv polis ke dainik jivan ke saath gaharaai se jude hue the. sahi myne main manushya hone ke liye, vyakti ko samudaay kaa ek sakriy naagarik bananaa padataa tha, jise arastu ne is tarah se abhivyakt kiya tha: "samudaay ke maamalon ke sanchaalan main hissaa na lene kaa arth he ki yaa to aap jaanavar hai yaa devtaa!" naagarikta kaa yah rup samudaay ke prati nagarikon ke dayitvon par aadhaarit tha, iske bajaay samudaay main nagarikon ko die jane vaale adhikaar adhik myne nahin rakhte the. yah ek samasya nahin thi kyonki ve sabhi log dridhtaa se polis ke saath jude the; unka apana bhagya or samudaay kaa bhagya ek duusare se prabalata se sambandhit the. iske alaava, polis ke naagarik dayitvon ko dharmik hone kaa avasar maanate the, yah unke liye sammaan or aadar kaa ek strot hota tha. athens main, shaasha or shasit donon prakaar ke naagarik the, mahatvapurn rajnatik or nyaayik karyaalayon ko ghumaya jaataa tha or sabhi nagarikon ko rajnatik sabha main bolne or matdaan dene kaa adhikaar hota tha.
 haalanki, polis kii naagarikta kaa ek mahatvapurn pahalu vishistataa thi. pracheen greece or rome tathaa madhyakaalin shaharon main naagarikta, jo polis kii naagarikta kaa anusaran karti thi, vah vishisht thi or asamanta kii sthiti ko vyapak rup se sweekrut kiya jaataa tha. nagarikon kaa str gaiyr nagarikon kii tulana main kaaphi ucch tha. gaiyr nagarikon main mahilaayein, gulam or 'barberian' shaamil the. udhaaharan ke liye, mahilaon ko rajnatik bhagidaari ke liye asamarth or tarkahin mana jaataa tha . koi vyakti naagarik ho saktaa he yaa nahin ise nirdhaarit karne ke tareeke sampatti, rajnatik sahabhaagita, yaa viraasat par aadhaarit ho sakate hai. 
 roman samrajya main, polis naagarikta main parivartan aayaa: naagarikta chote paimaane ke samudaay se purna samrajya main vistrut ho gayi. rome ke logon ne mahasoos kiya ki puure samrajya se logon ko naagarikta dena vijay praapt roman shaasi kshetron ko vaidyata detaa he. roman yug main naagarikta ab kisi raajnneetik agency kaa darja nahin thi; yah kam hokar ab ek nyaayik suraksha or kanoon or niyam kii abhivyakti main badal gayi thi. ).
 europea madhya yugon ke dauraan, naagarikta aamtaur par shaharon se sambandhit hoti thi, dekhen barghar, great barghar or borgeoisi . abhijaat varg ke paas aam logon kii tulana main adhik visheshadhikaar hote the, parantu francisi kranti or anya krantiyon ne in visheshadhikaron ko radd kar diya or naagarik banaaye.

 raashtrapati kii manjuuri or sanyukt rajya congress adhinium ke dwaara, maanad naagarikta keval saat vyaktiyon ko di gayi he. 
 canada kii maanad naagarikta ke liye sansad ke sarvasammati se anumodan kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. gine chune log jinhen canada kii maanad naagarikta di gayi he, ve hai 1985 main raul valenberg posthumosli, 2001 main nelson mandela, 14 wein dalai lamas, 2006 main tengin gyatso, 2007 main ong sen su kyi or 2009 main prince kareem aagaa khan. 
 2002 main dakshin korea ne dach footbal coach goose hidink ko maanad naagarikta di jinhone safaltaapurvak or apratyaashit rup se rashtriya team ko 2002 fifa vishwa cap main pahunchaa diya. 2006 main ek blike korean america footbal khiladi hiness ward ko bhi maanad naagarikta se sammanit kiya gaya, kyonki unhone half-koriens ke khilaaf korea main bhedbhaav ko kam karne kaa prayaas kiya tha.

 germany main maanad naagarikta shaharon, kasbon or kabhi kabhi sangheey rajyon ke dwaara pradaan kii jaati he. maanad naagarikta vyakti kii mrityu ke saath samaapt ho jaati he, yaa asaadhaaran maamalon main, ise shahar, kasbe yaa rajya kii sansad yaa parishad ke dwaara waapas le liya jaataa he. yuddh ke aparaadhiyon ke maamle main, aise sabhi sammaan 12 oktober 1946 ko "germany kii mitra niyantran parishad ke anuchhed VIII, khand II, akshar i" ke dwaara le liye gaye. kuch maamalon main, maanad naagarikta ko 1989/90 ko GDR ke patan ke baad purv GDR sadasyon jaise arich honekar se le liya gaya.
 ireland main, "maanad naagarikta" vastav main ek purna kanoonee naagarikta he jisamen ireland main rahane or matdaan karne kaa adhikaar shaamil hota he. 
 cuba ke samvidhan ke adhyay II anuchhed 29 paragraph 'e) ke anusaar janm se cuba ke ve naagarik videshi hai, jinhone apane asaadhaaran gunon ke se cuba ke sangharsh main jeet hasil kii, unhen janas ke dwaara cuba ke naagarik mana jaataa he.che gwera ko cuba kranti main bhaag lene ke liye fidel kastro ke dwaara cuba ke maanad naagarik kaa sammaan diya gaya, baad main gwera ne unhen prakhyat vidaai bhi di. 
 aitihaasik drushti se adhikansh rajya naagarikta ko apni aabaadi tak hi simit rakahate hai, iske dwaara ve naagarik varg ko rajnatik adhikaar dete hai, jinhen aabaadi ke anya virgon se behatar mana jaataa he, lekin ve ek duusare ke saamaan hote hai. simit naagarikta kaa ek udhaaharan athens he jahaan gulam, mahilaayein or awasi videshiyon ko rajnatik adhikaron se vanchit rakhaa jaataa he. roman ganraajya ek anya udhaaharan prastut karata he or haal hi main, polish-lithuanian rashtramandal ke abhijaat varg main kuch aisi hi visheshtaayen paayi gayi.
