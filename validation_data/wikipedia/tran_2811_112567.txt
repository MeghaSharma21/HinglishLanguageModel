 jeevadhariyon ke sharir main samay nirdhaaran kii ek samunnat vyavastha hoti he jise ham jaivik ghadi yaa kehete hai.
 manushya main jaivik ghadi kaa mul sthaan hamaara mastishk he. hamaare mastishk main karodo koshikaayen hoti he jinhe ham neuron kehete hai. ye koshikaayen puure sharir kii gatividhiyon ko niyantrit evam nirdhaarit karti he. ek koshikaon se anya koshikaon ko soochna kaa aadan-pradaan vidhut spandon dwaara diya jaataa he. ham raat ko samay vishesh par sone jate hai tathaa subah swat jaag jate hai. aakhir ham kaise jaan jate hai ki subah ho chuki he. koun hamen jagaa detaa he. ham nidra main rahete hai kintu hamaara mastishk tab bhi sakriy rahata he. ausatan ham ek minute main 15-18 baar saans lete hai tathaa hamaara hriday 72 baar dhadkta he. aakhir yah kahaan se sanchalit hota he. 
 jaivik ghadi se vyakti ke sochane, samajhane kii dasha evam disha, tark-vitark, nirnay kshamta ev vyavhaar par prabhaav padataa he. kai dinon se na so paae yaa anayamit dincharya vaale vyakti, chidchide swabhaav ke ho jate hai. kisi baat ko tatkaal yaad nahin kar pate tathaa kisi baat par dhyaan kendrit nahin kar pate. shift main duty karne waalo kii ye aam shikayate hai. aisa unki jaivik ghadi main aae vyavdhaan ke kaaran hota he. hamaari jaivik ghadi din-raat hone ke saath hi bahaari sthitiyon se samanjit hoti he. raatri hone par vah hamen sone ke liye prerit karti he tathaa hamaare samvedan env gyanedriyon ko dhire-dhire sust kar aaraam kii sthiti main laati he jisase ham so saken. subah punah hame jagaa bhi detii he. jaivik ghadi main aayaa vyavdhaan dhire-dhire dur ho jaataa he tathaa bahya sthitiyon se vah set ho jaati he. din evam raat ke anusaar hamaare sharir kaa taapmaana bhi nishchit praaroop ke anusaar badalata rahata he.
