 narvar durg madhya pradesh ke shivpuri jile ke narvar main vindhya varvatmala kii ek pahari par sthit he. isaki oonchaai bhustar se lagbhag 500 feet he or yah lagbhag 8 varg kimi ke kshetra main failaa hua he. kaha jaataa he ki 10veen shatabdi main jab kushwaha rajputon ne narvar par adhikaar kiya to is durg kaa nirmaan kiya. kushwahon ke baad yahaan parihaar or fir tomars rajputon kaa aadhipaty rahaa or antatah yah 16veen shatabdi main mughalon ke adheen aa gaya. kaalaantar main 19veen shatabdi ke armbh main yahaan maratha sardaar sindhiya ne adhikaar kiya. 
 is durg ke purvi ore purv se pashchim disha kii ore lambavat ek doosraa pahaad he, jise hajira pahaad kehete he, kyonki iske pashchimi bhaag ke shikhar par do kalatmak hajira nirmit hai. durg ke dakshin–pashchim evam uttar main lagi hui sindh evam purv se ahir nadi ne chaaro ore se prakritik suraksha pradaan kar di he. narvar durg main anek hindu mandir nirmit he.
 narvar kaa kila bundelkhand main pehle evam madhya bharat ke gwalior kile ke baad duusare number kaa he. narvar durg naag raajaaon kii rajdhani tha. yahaan 9 naag raajaaon ne raaj kiya. ye raja is prakaar se the:
 inke baad samudr gupt ne naag raajaaon par akraman kiya evam is vansh ko samaapt kar kile ko adheen kiya. is bare main elahabaad stambh main ullekh milte hai. 
 mahabharat kaaleen narvar kaa ullekh shri harsh rachit naishidhiyacharitam main nishadh nagar yaa nishadh desh ke naam se vistaarpurvak miltaa he. prasiddh sanskrit sookhti - narvar ke raja nal dwaara apni patni damyanti ko kahi gayi. narvar kaa kila bhartiya kilon kii shaan or atyant pracheen va sabase surakshit kila mana jaat he. videshi yatri tiffinthalar ne narvar kile kii suraksha ke bare main varnan karte hue likhaa he ki - "narvar kile ko jeet paanaa behad dushkar he. suraksha ke drushti se iskaa koi jawaab nahin". is shahar kaa aitihaasik mahatv bhi rahaa he or ise 12veen shatabdi tak nalapura ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. is mahal kaa naam raja nal ke naam par rakhaa gaya he jinke or damayanti kii premgaathaayen mahakavy mahabharat main padhane ko milti hai.
