 pragalan ek prakaar kii nishkarshan dhaatukarmiki he. ayask se dhaatu banane ke liye mukhyatah iskaa upyog kiya jaataa he. chaandi, loha, tanba aadi is vidhi se nirmit hote hai. pragalan kii takanic aaj se koi aath hajaar varsh purv se upyog main laayi ja rahi he. phir bhi pragalan ko ek ucch takneekee uplabdhi mana jaataa he. pragalan kii prakriya main bhoonna, apchayan tathaa gandagee ko flux ke rup main dhaatu se alag karnaa shaamil he.
 ayask ke pragalan ke falswaroop dhaatu praapt hoti he vah tatva ke rup main hoti he yaa dhaatu ke saral yogic ke rup main. praayah ayask ko kisi oxykaarak yaa kisi apachayak kii upasthiti main galnaank se adhik taap tak garam kiya jaataa he. anumaan kiya jaataa he ki sabase pehle taanbe kaa pragalan 5000 isapurv kiya gaya tha. uske baad tin, sisa or chaandi kaa pragalan hua. chunki pragalan ke liye ucch taap kii aavashyaktaa hoti he, iske liye bhattiyon main tej vaayu kaa jhonka bheja jaataa he. indhan eke rup main charcol kaa upyog kiya jaataa tha jo 18veen shatabdi main coke ke aane tak jari tha. aaj vatya bhatthi is kaam ko atyant dakshatapurvak karti he.
 alag-alag dhaatuon ke liye pragalan kii prakriya main bahut kuch bhinnata hoti he.
