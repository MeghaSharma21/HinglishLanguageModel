 brahman  yah varna vyavastha kaa varna he. aitihaasik rup hindu varna vyavastha main chaar varna hote hai. brahman, kshatriya, vaisya tathaa shudra . 
 yask muni kii nirukt ke anusaar - brahm janaati brahman: -- brahman vah he jo brahm ko jaanata he. atah brahman kaa arth he - "ishwar kaa gyaata".
 yadhyapi bhartiya jansankhya main brahmanon kaa pratishat kam he, tathaapi dharm, sanskti, kala tathaa shiksha ke kshetra main inkaa yogadan aparimit he.
 brahman samaaj kaa itihaas bahut pracheen bharat ke vaidic dharm se aarambh hota he. bharat kaa mukhya aadhaar hi brahmano se shuru hota he. brahman naram vyavhaar ke hote he | brahman vyavhaar kaa mukhya strot veda hai. brahman samay ke anusaar apane aap ko badalane main sakshayam hote he. brahmano kaa bharat kii azaadi main bhi bahut yogadan rahaa he jo itihaas main gadhaa gaya he. brahmano ke sabhi sampradaay vedon se prerana lete hai. paaramparik taur par yah vishwaas he ki veda apaurushey tathaa anadi hai, balki anadi satya kaa prakatya he jinaki vaidhata shaashvat he. vedon ko shruti mana jaataa he .
 dharmik va sanskatik ritiyon evam vyavhaar main vivadhtaaon ke kaaran or vibhinn vaidic vidyalayon ke unke sambandh ke chalate, acharya hin brahman hai | sutara kaal main pratishthit vidwaanon ke netatv main, ek hi veda kii vibhinn naamon kii pruthak-pruthak shakhaaen banane lageen. in pratishthit rishiyon kii shikshaaon ko sutara kaha jaataa he. prayek veda kaa apana sutara he. saamaajik, naitik tathaa shastranukool niyamon vaale sutron ko dharm sutara kehete hai, aanushthanik waalon ko shrout sutara tathaa gharelu vidhishastron kii vyakhya karne waalon ko gah sutara kaha jaataa he. sutara saamanytah padya yaa mishrit gadhy-padya main likhe hue hai.
 brahman shastragyon main pramukh hai agniras, apstambh, atri, bahaspati, boudhayan, daksh, gautam, vatsa,harith, katyayan, likhit, parashar, samavart, shankh, shattap, ushanas, vashishtha, vishnu, vyas, yajyavalky tathaa yama. ye ikkis rishi smatiyon ke rachayita the. smatiyon main sabase pracheen hai apstambh, boudhayan, gautam tathaa vashishthi.
 brahman kaa nirdhaaran mata-pita kii jaati ke aadhaar par hi hone laga he. skandapurana main shodashopchaar pujan ke antargat ashtum upachaar main brahma dwaara naarad ko yagyopavit ke aadhyatmik arth main bataaya gaya he, 
 atah aadhyatmik drushti se yagyopavit ke bina janm se brahman bhi shudra ke samaan hi hota he.
 brahman maance sharaab kaa seven jo dharm ke viruddh ho wo kaam nahin karte hai. brahman sanathan dharm ke niyamon kaa paalan karte hai. jaise vedon kaa ajnapaalan, yah vishwaas ki moksh tathaa antim satya kii prapti ke anek madhyam hai, yah ki ishwar ek he kintu unke gungan tathaa pujan hetu anginat naam tathaa swaroop hai jinka kaaran he hamaare anubhav, sanskti tathaa bhashaaon main vividhtaayen. brahman sarvejnasukhino bhavantu evam vasudaiv kutumbakam main vishwaas rakhte hai. saamanyat: brahman keval shaakaahaari hote hai .
 hindu brahman apni dharanaon se adhik dharmacharan ko mahatv dete hai. yah dharmik panthon kii visheshta he. dharmacharan main mukhyatah he yajna karnaa. dincharya is prakaar he - snaan, sandhyaavandanam, jap, upasna, tathaa agnihotra. antim do yajna ab keval kuch hi pariwaron main hote hai. brahmacharee agnihotra yajna ke sthaan par agnikaryam karte hai. anya reetiyaan hai amaavasya tarpan tathaa shraadh.
