 peri mahanadi kii sahaayak nadi he. peri gariyaaband tehsil kii bindranvagarh jameendaari main sthit bhatrugarh pahari se nikalati he. uske baad uttar-purv disha kii ore kareeb 96 kimi bahati hui rajim kshetra main mahanadi se milti he. peri nadi dhamtari or rajim ko vibhajit karti he. peri nadi ke tat par sthit he rajeevalochan mandir. rajim main mahanadi or sondhur nadiyon kaa triveni sangam-sthal bhi he. isaki lambaai 90 kilometre he tathaa pravaah kshetra 3,000 varg meter he.
