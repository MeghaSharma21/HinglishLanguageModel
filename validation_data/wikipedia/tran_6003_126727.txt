 dow laalamani mishra, em0 mus0, bi0 mus0, dean va pramukh, sangeet evam lalit kala sankaay, banaras hindu vishvavidhyalay, varanasi bhartiya sagit jagat ke aise manishi the jo apni kala ke samaan hi apni vidwata ke liye jane jate the.
 mata ke sangeet shikshak ko apni pratibha se prabhavit kar balak laalamani kii sangeet yatra armbh hui. pandit shankar bhatta or munshi bhrigunath laal se dhrupad or dhamaar kii paramparaagat shiksha hui. rampur seni gharaana ke ustad vazeer khan ke shagird ustad menhadi hossain khan se unhone khyal gayan siikhaa. dhruvpad, bhajan or tabalaa kii shiksha swami pramodanand ji se praapt hui. ustad amir ali khan ke dekh rekh me anek sangeet vaadyon jaise sitar, surbahaar, sarod, santoor, jalatarang, vaayalin, tabalaa aadi main maharath hasil kii. jab vah keval baarah varsh ke the, unhen kalakatta kii shahanshahi ricarding company main sahaayak sangeet nirdeshak ke pad par niyukti mili. agle do varshon main unhone kai filmon main sangeet diya. ustad amir ali khan kaa saannnidhya or sangeet nirdesan—inhin donon ke sanyog se orcastration ke prati unamem rujhaan paidaa hua.
 pita ke swargvaas ke baad san 1940 main ve kanpur laut aae. apni naiysargik pratibha se prerit wo baalkon ko sangeet sikhaane ke naye raste dhund rahe the; vah bhi tab jab parmparik samaaj main kuleen vyakti sangeet ko hey drushti se dekhte the. ek ek kar unhonen kai bal sangeet vidhyalay sthapit kiye; vidhyaarthee kii zarurat ke mutabiq aupchaarik, anaupchaarik paathyakramon main parivartan kiya; vaadya vrund samiti kii sthapana kii. kshetra ke prasiddh santhaan bhartiya sangeet parishad kaa gathan kiya tathaa pehla ucch shikshan kaa aadhaar gandhi sangeet mahavidhyalay armbh kiya. sangeet ke har aayam se parichit unhone style, shikshan paddati, vaadya-rup, vaadan swaroop -- sabhi par kaary kiya jisase unhen sabhi ore se aadar or sammaan mila.
 prakhyat nrity guru pandit udai shankar ne apni nrity mandli main unhen sangeet nirdeshak ke pad par aamantran diya jise laalamani ji ne saharsha sweekaar kiya. mandali kii abhinav nrity prastution tathaa puranic evam aadhunik vishyon par aadhaarit baile, opera aadi ke liye unhone manohari sangeet rachanaayen kii. san 1951 se 1955 tak bharat ke kai nagaron se hota hua udai shankar ji kaa trup srilanka, england, france, belgium, america, canada kaa bhraman karata rahaa. is prayogdharmi nrity mandli ke liye unka bahu vaadya paarangat hona tathaa orcestra main ruchi rakhana faldaayi siddh hua.
 unke liye bhi iskaa anubhav arthpurna rahaa. swadesh lautate hi unhone mira opera kii rachana kii jiskaa pratham manchan san 1956 main kanpur main kiya gaya. bhagwaan krishna kii murti main mira kaa villin ho janaa darshakon ko stambhit kar gaya.
 bharat waapas pahunchne par bhartiya shastreey sangeet ke sarvaadhik khyath sangeet santhaan, "akhil bhartiya gandharv mandal mahavidyalay", bombay kaa register niyukt kiya gaya. kintu apane nagarvasiyon kaa anurodh na taal paane ke kaaran san 1956 main hi unhen kanpur laut kar swayam dwaara sthapit 'gandhi sangeet mahavidhyalay' kaa praachaarya pad sambhaalanaa padaa.
 isi dauraan san 1950 main kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay, varanasi main pandit onkaar nath thakur ke praachaaryatv main sangeet evam lalit kala mahavidhyalay kii sthapana ho chuki thi. san 55-56 tak praarambhik samasyaon se ubar yah vikaas ke liye taiyaar tha. is kii punarsanrachana ke liye tatkaaleen kulapati dow sar si pi ramasvami kii prerana se yojana banaayi gayi. pandit onkaar nath thakur ke aagrah par main laalamani mishra teesri baar kanpur chhodane ko baadhya hue or san 1957 main varanasi pahunch mahavidhyalay main reader kaa padbhaar grahan kiya.
 pandit onkaar nath thakur or dow bi aar devdhar dwaara sthapit is yuva santhaan ko pallavit karne kii chunauti ko dow laalamani mishra ne atyant sahajata se sweekaara or iskaa kushalta se nirvahan kiya. unke nirdesan main kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay kaa sangeet evam lalit kala mahavidhyalay bhartiya shastreey sangeet shikshan kaa adwitiya santhaan ban gaya. saamaanya paathyakramon ke saath saath pradarshan evan rachanakarm par kendrit diploma se lekar snaatak, snatakottar tathaa doctoral oopaadhi -- di mus -- kaa anoothaa kaarykram armbh kiya gaya jisamen vibhinn deshon ke chaatro ne pravesh liya or aaj yahan ke purv chaatr vishwa bhar main sangeet sansthaanon main sheersh padon par karyrat hai. pen vishvavidhyalay, philadelphia ne dow mishra ko visiting professor ke rup main aamantrit kiya jahaan ve 1969 se 1978 tak jate rahe. unke gyaan, shikshan kii sarasata or sanvedan sheel vyavhar ke kaaran duniya bhar se chaatr unke paas aate rahe. swayan udai shankar ji ne apane putr anand shankar ko sitar vaadan tathaa vaadya vrund kii baarikiyaan samajhane unke paas bheja.
 bahumukhi pratibha ke dhani dow mishra ne patiala ke ustad abdul azeez khan ko sun gupt rup se vicitra veena hetu vaadan takanic viksit karne ke saath saath bhartiya sangeet vaadyon ke itihaas tathaa vikascram par anusandhan kiya. bhartiya gyanpith, nai dilli ne ise pustak rup main san 1973 main bhartiya sangeet vaadya sheershak se prakashit kiya. dow mishra ne is pustak main bhartiya sangeet vaadyon ke udbhav ko tarkpurna aadhaar se bataate hue un se jude anek bhramon kaa nivaaran kiya. aaj tak yah pustak vaadyon kii pahchaan, vargeekaran tathaa unke antarsambandh ko samajhane kaa pramukh strot he. iske alaava bhi unhone anek pustaken likhin. chaar yugma granthon main se ek, tantri naad san 1979 main prakashit hua; doosraa tataninaad sheeghra prakaashya he. 1979 main unke dehavsaan uparaant unke putr dow gopal shankar mishra ne pita kii hast-likhit pandulipiyon par kaary armbh kiya kintu pita kii smriti main madhukali bhopal dwaara yojit kaarykram kii purv sandhyaa par 13 agast 1999 ko hriday gati ruk jane se unka bhi nidhan ho gaya. unki putree dow raagini trivedi ne pita dwaara likhe lekhon kaa sampaadan kiya sangeet or samaaj, jo bhopal ke madhukali prakaashan dwaara san 2000 main prakashit hui. bal evan kishoron hetu likhi gayi anek pustaken jaise sangeet sarita, tabalaa vigyaan aadi dow mishra ke jeevankaal main hi prakashit ho chukin thim. aprakashit pandulipiyon par ab unki putree kaary kar rahi hai.
 vaidic sangeet par shodh karte hue unhone samik swar vyavastha kaa rahasy suljhaayaa. samaveda ke in praapt swaron ko sangrakshit karne ke liye unhone raag sameshwari kaa nirmaan kiya. bharat muni dwaara vidhaan kii gayi baees shrution ko maanav itihaas main pehali baar dow mishra dwaara nirmit vaadya yantra shruti-veena par ek saath sunanaa sambhav hua. iske nirmaan tathaa upyog kii vidhi “shruti veena” main di gayi he jise narendra printing work, varanasi dwaara 11 farvari 1964 ko prakashit kiya gaya. sameshwari ke atirikt unhone kai raagon kii rachana kii jaise shyaam bihag, jog todi, madhukali, madhu-bhairav, baleshwari aadi. dow mishra ne purna rup se tantri vaadyon ke liye nihit vaadan style kii rachana kii jise mishrabani ke naam se janaa jaataa he. is hetu unhone saikadon raagon main hazaron bandishon kii rachana kii jinka sankalan unki pustakon ke atirikt unke shishyon kii pustakon, sangeet patrikaon main miltaa he.san 1996 main unesco dwaara music ov laalamani mishra ke sheershak se unke vicitra veena vaadan kaa compact discs jari kiya gaya.
