 vikram samvath hindu panchang main samay gananaa kii pranali kaa naam he. yah samvath 57 i.pus. armbh hoti he. iskaa praneta bharat kii bhoomiverma vikramaditya ko mana jaataa he. is tathyako gujarat ke khojakarta pan.bhagavanlal indraji ne bhi sahi tehraaya he. koi koi kehete hai ki i sanwat bharatvarsh ke samraat vikramaditya ne shuru kiya tha lekin samay ke gananaa main wo sahi nahin rahata. kyonki magadhake samraat chandragupta dwitiya ne ujjayani or ayodhya par vijay praapt kiya or khud ko 'vikramaditya' samratka oopaadhi di. usase pehle kahin or kabhi bhi vikramaditya kaa ullekh nahin he.
 baarah maheene kaa ek varsh or saat din kaa ek saptaah rakhane kaa prachlan vikram samvath se hi shuru hua. maheene kaa hisaab surya va chandramaa kii gati par rakhaa jaataa he. yah baarah rashiyaan baarah saura maas hai. jis din surya jis raashi main pravesh karata he usee din kii sankranti hoti he. poornima ke din, chandramaa jis nakshatra main hota he. usee aadhaar par maheenon kaa namakaran hua he. chandra varsh saura varsh se 11 din 3 ghati 48 pal chota he. isiilie har 3 varsh main isamen 1 maheenaa jod diya jaataa he.
 ye samvath nepal kaadhikarik sanwat he. aaj bhi nepal main yahi samvath rashtriya sanwat he.
