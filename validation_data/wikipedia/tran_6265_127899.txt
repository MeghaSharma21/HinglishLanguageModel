 jinprabh suri isvi san kii 14veen shatabdi main ek asaadhaaran pratibhashali jain acharya ho gaye hai. mughal badshaah akbar ke darbar main jo sthaan jagadguru heeravijay suri ko praapt tha, vahi sthaan tughlaq sultan mohammadshah ke darbar main jinprabh suri kaa tha.
 jinprabh suri laghu khartar gachh ke pravartak jinsingh suri ke pradhan shishy the. sanskrit, praakrit or apbhransh main vividh vishyon par inhone mahatvapurn granth likhe hai. pratidin kisi abhinav kavy kii rachana karne ke pashchaat hi aahargrahan karne kaa unka niyam tha. unke jeevankaal main baghela vansh kaa ant, gujarat kaa musalmaan baadshahon ke adhikaar main chalaa janaa, dilli main mughal sultanate kaa aarambh aadi anek romaanchakaari aitihaasik ghatnaayen ghati thim.
 jinprabh suri ko bhraman kaa bahut shauk tha tathaa gujarat, rajasthan, malwa, madhyapradesh, baraar, dakshin, karnataka, telugana, bihar, avadh, uttar pradesh or punjab aadi sthanon kii yatraayen inhone kii thim. apane vividh tirthakaal main is bhraman kaa vistrut vrutant jinprabh suri sankalit kiya he. is vrittant ke anusaar vikram sanwat 125 main sultan allaudin ke chote bhai ullu khan ne dilli se gujarat par akraman kiya. us samay chittod ke naresh summersingh ne ullu khan ko dand dekar mewar kii raksha kii. vividh tirthakalp main banaras ke manikaninkaghat tathaa devvaranasi, rajdhani varanasi, madan varanasi or vijayavaranasi kaa ullekh he. rajdhani varanasi main yavan rahete the. aajkal kaa madanpura hi madan varanasi ho saktaa he.
