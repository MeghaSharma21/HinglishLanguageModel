 paak sena pakistan kii thalsena he. iskaa kaam pakistan kii sarhadon kii raksha karnaa he. isamen kul 5,00,000 reserve sipaahi hai. yah british bhartiya sena se astitv main aayaa jiskaa bharat ke vibhaajan ke baad astitv samaapt kar diya tha. pakistani sena agast 1947 main sthapit kii gayi. international institute for stratagic studies ke mutabik, isame 2015 main lagbhag 620,000 sakriy karmchaaree the. pakistan main, svaichhik sainya seva ke liye 16-23 varsh aayu he; pakistan raashtra ke samvidhan ke mutabik sainikon ko 18 saal umr se kam par yuddh ke liye tainaat nahin kiya ja saktaa he.
 praathmik uddeshy or iskaa sanvaidhanik mishan yah he ki rashtriya suraksha or pakistan kii rashtriya ekta ko bahari akraman yaa yuddh ke khatre ke viruddh bachaav ke liye sunishchit karnaa he. rashtriya aapadaaon or apatkaal kii ghatnaon ke dauraan, yah ghar par maanviya bachaav kaaryon kaa sanchaalan karti he or sanyukt raashtra dwaara anivaary shanti mishan main bhaag leti he, sabase ullekhneey rup se 1993 main somalia main funs gaye america sainikon or 1992-95 main bosnian yuddh ke bachaav main isane pramukh bhoomika nibhaai.
