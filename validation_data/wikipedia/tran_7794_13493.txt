 khategaanv madhya pradesh ke devas jile ke aakhri chor par sthit pramukh vyapaarik centre he. purv main iskaa naam hariganj tha, jo maratha holkar rajgaraane ke nemawar jile se sambhddha tha. khategaanv indore baitool rashtriya rajmarg sankhya 59 e par sthit he, jo sidhaa nagpur se indore ko amravati or jalgaanv hote hue jodta he. iske alaava paas hi sthir sandalpur, jo isi rashtriya rajmarg par sthit he, khategaanv ko do anya alag margon se jodta he; ek rehati ke raste bhopal kii ore, to doosraa rehati ke hi raste salkanpur, budhani hote hue hoshangabad ko jodta he, jo sandalpur se jabalpur tak rajmarg sankhya 22 kehalaata he. hoshangabad se hi yah maarg jabalpur kii ore jaataa he or khategaanv ko jabalpur se jodta he, duusari ore khategaanv se ek maarg satvas hokar sidhe donon ore nimad kshetra se jud jaataa he ek satvas se punasa hokar khandwa to doosraa kaantaphod, punjapura, udainagar, katkut hote hue badvaah sanaavad ke raste khargon kii ore jaataa he, mukhya rup se indore kii ore jane vaala maarg adhiktam vyast he kyonki indore se kai anya margon dwaara khategaanv jud jaataa he ! khategaanv upasambaag evam tehsil ke saath hi vikaas khand bhi he jo jila mukhyalay se 130 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he yahi isaki ajeeb vajah he ki jila mukhyalay se itnaa dur or aakhri chor par sthit hone ke baad bhi itnaa vikasshil hote hue bhi swayan jila nahin he! khategaanv vidisha loksabhaa ke antargat aane vaale vidhansabha kshetra kramank 173 he, yanha se sushamaa swaraj loksabhaa saansad or ashish sharma kshetreey vidhayak hai, avibhajit madhyapradesh ke samay evam varsh 2003 tak yah bhopal sansadeey kshetra main shaamil hua karata tha parantu naveen parisemen me ise vidisha main shaamil kar diya gaya jo naa sirf dur he balki kisi bhi aadhaar par mail nahin khaataa! khategaanv varsh 1952 se hi vidhansabha nirvachan kshetra rahaa he
