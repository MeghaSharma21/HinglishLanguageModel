 nongthimmaai bhartiya rajya ke meghalaya ke east khaasi hills jile main ek janaganana shahar he.
 2001 kii bharat kii janaganana ke anusaar, nongthimmaai kii kul jansankhya 34,209 thi, jisamen purushon kaa 50% evam shesh 50% bhaag mahila kaa tha. yahaan kiausat sakshartaa dar 82% he jo rashtriya ausat 59.5% kii tulana main adhik he. purush sakshartaa 84% thi or mahila sakshartaa 80% he . 11% jansankhya 6 varsh kii kii aayu se neeche kii he.
 nongthimmaai main hi prasiddh purvottar parvatiy vishvavidhyalay parisar bhi sthit he. haalanki nehu kaa nayaa parisar mawalai main sthanantarit hokar chaalu ho gaya he, fir bhi kuch vibhaag abhi bhi yaheen se karyrat hai.
 is chote se gaanv kaa naam khaasi bhashaa ke do shabdon se niklaa he: "nong" arthath "gaanv" or "thimmaai" jiskaa arth he "nayaa", to is shabad kaa puraa arth he "nayaa gaanv".
