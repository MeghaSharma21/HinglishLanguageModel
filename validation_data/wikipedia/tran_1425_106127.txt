 sikandariyaa, misr kaa doosraa sabase bada shahar he. yahan kii jansankhya 41 lakh he or yah desh kaa sabase bada samudri bandargah he jahaan misr kaa lagbhag 80% aayaat or niryaat kaary sampann hota he. sikandariyaa ek mahatvapurn paryatak sthal bhi he. sikandariyaa, uttar-madhya misr main bumadhya sagar ke tat ke kinare lagbhag 32 kimi dur tak failaa hua he. yah bibliotheka alexandrina kaa ghar he. ek anya shahar, suez, se apane prakritik gas or tel kii pipelinon kii vajah se yah misr kaa ek mahatvapurn audyogic centre ban gaya he.
 pracheen kaal main, sikandariyaa duniya ke sabase prasiddh shaharon main se ek tha. isaki sthapana sikandar mahaan ne lagbhag 331 i.pus. main ek chote-se feronic shahar ke aasapaas kii thi. phustaat main ek nai rajdhani kii sthapana hone ke samay 641 i. main muslim misr vijay tak lagbhag ek hajaar saal tak yah misr kii rajdhani rahaa. sikandariyaa apane prakaash stambh, pracheen duniya ke saat ajoobon main se ek; apane pustkaalay ; or catacombs of kom al shokapha, madhya yug ke saat ajoobon main se ek, ke liye kaaphi mashhoor tha. sikandariyaa ke bandargah main 1994 main chaalu hone vaale avirat samudri puraatatv se sikandar ke aagaman se pehle or tolemi vansh ke kaal ke sikandariyaa ke rahasyon par se pardaa uth rahaa he.
 19veen sadi ke antim daur se yah antarrashtriya jahajrani udyog kaa ek pramukh centre or duniya ke sabase mahatvapurn vyapaarik kendron main se ek ban gaya kyonki isaki vajah se bumadhya sagar or laal sagar ke darmyaan aasaan jamini sambandh se or misr ke kapaas ke laabhprad vyapaar se kaaphi phayda hota tha.
 alexandria kii sthapana sikandar mahaan ne april 331 i.pus. main Ἀλεξάνδρεια ) ke rup main kii thi. is pariyojna ke liye sikandar kaa mukhya vaastukar dinocrates tha. alexandria kii sthapana misr main naukratis ko ek unanivadik centre ke rup main adhikramit karne or ise yunaan evam samrudh nil ghati ke bich kii kadi banane ke iraade se kiya gaya tha. samudr ke tatavarti kshetra main pehle se hi racottice naam kaa ek misri shahar maujuud tha jise baad main ise misri bhashaa ) main alexandria naam diya gaya. misri shahari quarter ke rup main iskaa astitv bana rahaa. sthapana ke kuch maheenon ke baad sikandar ne misr se purv kii ore prasthaan kiya or phir kabhi unhone apane shahar main vaapasi nahin kii. sikandar ke chale jane ke baad unke visarai, cleomeness, ne vistaar jari rakhaa. sikandar ke anya uttaradhikariyon ke saath sangharsh ke baad, unke general tolemi ne alexandria main sikandar ke shav ko laane main kamyaabi hasil kii.
 haalanki cleomeness mukhya rup se alexandria ke satat vikaas ko dekhne kaa prabharee tha, lagata he ki heptastadian or mukhyabhumi ke quarter mukhya rup se tolemi ke hi krity the. barbaad tair ke vyapaar ko viraasat main praapt karke or europe or arbian or indian east ke darmyaan ek nayaa vanijyik kendra banakar is shahar ne ek peedi se bhi kam avadhi main karthej kii tulana main adhik vikaas kiya. ek sadi main, alexandria duniya kaa sabase bada shahar ban gaya tha or agali kuch shatabdiyon main yah keval rome ke baad duusare sthaan par tha. yah misr kaa pramukh unani shahar ban gaya jahaan kai shaharon se aane vaale or kai prishthbhumiyon vaale unaniyon kaa ek asaadhaaran mishran dekhne ko miltaa tha.
 alexandria, unanivad kaa keval ek kendra hi nahin tha balki yah duniya ke sabase bade yahudee samudaay kaa ghar bhi tha. septuagint, hebrew bible kaa ek unani anuwaad, kii rachana vahiin kii gai thi. aarambhik tolemiyon ne ise krambaddh kiya or iske sangrahalay ke vikaas ko pramukh unanivadik shikshan centre main parinat kiya lekin unhone apni jansankhya kii teen pramukh jatiyon: unani, yahudee or misri, ke antar ko banaae rakhane main kaaphi saavdhaanee barti. is vibhaajan se baad main kaaphi ashaanti paidaa hui, jo 221 se 204 i.pus. tak shashan karne vaale tolemi philopator kii adheenta main apane aap saafe dikhaai dene laga. 144 se 116 i.pus. tak shashan karne vaale tolemi ashtum fiscan ke shashan kaal ko parishkaran or grih yuddh dwaara chihnit kiya jaataa tha.
 tolemi alexander kii ichha ke anusaar, lekin keval ek sau saal se bhi zyada samay tak roman prabhaav ke adheen rahane ke baad, 80 i.pus. main yah shahar aupchaarik rup se roman adhikaar main chalaa gaya. tolemi terahaven evam unke salaahkaaron or prasiddh rani cleopetra saptam ke bich hone vaale gharelu grih yuddh main ek roman hastkshep ke dauraan 47 i.pus. main julius seizar ne is par kabza kar liya. is par ant main octavian, bhavi samraat augustus, ne 1 agast 30 i.pus. kabza kiya jinaki jeet kii yaad main baad main aathven maheene ke naam ko badalakar agast kar diya gaya.
 isa ke janm ke 115 varsh baad, unani-yahudee grih yudhon ke dauraan alexandria kaa vishaal khand nasht ho gaya jisane hadrian or unke vaastukar, decriyanas, ko iskaa punarnirmaan karne kaa avasar pradaan kiya. 215 main samraat karakalla ne is shahar kaa daura kiya or vahaan ke nivaasiyon dwaara un par kuch apmanjanak vyangy kiye jane kii vajah se unhone achaanak apane sainikon ko un sabhi navajavaanon ko maut ke ghat utaar dene kaa aadesh de diya jo hathiyaar chalaane main sakasham the. 21 july 365 ko ek tsunami kii vajah se alexandria tabah ho gaya, is ghatana ke do sau saal baad aaj bhi is ghatana ko prati varsh "santras diwas" ke rup main yaad kiya jaataa he. chauthi sadi ke antim daur main, butaparaston par nav isai romanon dwaara kiya jane vaala utpeedan nai oonchaaiyon ko chhu rahaa tha. 391 main, samraat theodosius pratham ke aadesh par patriark theophilis ne alexandria ke sabhi butaparast mandiron ko nasht kar diya. paanchavin sadi main bruchium or yahudee quarter sunsaan ho gaye the. mukhyabhumi par, lag rahaa tha maano jindagi serapium or sizarium ke ird-gird kendrit ho gai thi jinamein se donon baad main isai carch ban gaye. tathaapi, faros or heptastadium quarteron main adhik sankhya main rahane vaale logon kii jansankhya jyon-ki-tyon bani rahi or unhen akshunn chhod diya gaya tha.
 619 main alexandria sasanid farsiyon ke haathon main chalaa gaya. haalanki bigentini samraat heraclius ne 629 main ise phir se praapt kar liya tha, lekin 641 main general amar ebn al-as kii adheenta main arbiyon ne chaudaa maheenon kii gheraabandi ke baad is par kabza kar liya.
 1798 main nepolian ke misr ke abhiyaan kii sainya karravaayiyon main alexandria pramukh rup se prakaash main aayaa. francisi sainikon ne 2 july 1798 ko shahar main tahalaka machaa diya or 1801 main ek british abhiyaan ke aagaman tak yah unke haathon main rahaa. 21 march 1801 ko alexandria kii ladaai main angrejon ne francisiyon par kaaphi had tak vijay praapt kar lee thi jiske baad unhone shahar kii gheraabandi kar di jo 2 sitambar 1801 ko unke haathon main aa gaya. mohammad ali, misr kaa turk rajyapal, ne 1810 ke aasapaas alexandria ke punarnirmaan or punarvikas kaa kaam shuru kiya or 1850 tak is shahar ne kuch had tak apni puraani garima ko phir se praapt kar liya. july 1882 main british nausainya balon ne is shahar par bambaari kii or is par kabza kar liya. july 1954 main yah shahar ek israeli bambaari abhiyaan kaa nishaanaa bana jo baad main lavon afaire ke naam se mashhoor hua. keval kuch hi maheene baad, alexandria ke mansheya squaire main jamaal abdul naser kii hatya kaa ek asafal prayaas kiya gaya.
 alexandria kii sabase mahatvapurn ladaaiyon or gheraabandiyon main shaamil hai:
 alexandria main shushk jalwaayu ) pai jaati he lekin bumadhya sagariya kshetron se hokar bahane vaali prabal uttari hawa is shahar ko registani bhitari pradesh se ek alag jalwaayu pradaan karti he. shahar kii jalwaayu main bumadhyasagariya ) visheshtaayen dekhne ko milti hai, arthath halke, parivartaneey varsha sheet kaal or garmi, shushk greeshm kaal jo kabhi-kabhi bahut zyada ardra ho saktaa he; janvari or farvari kaa maheenaa sabase thanda rahata he jiskaa dainik taapmaana kii adhiktam seemaa aam taur par 12 se 18 °se. he.12 se 18 °se. jaade ke maheenon main alexandria main tej aandhi, barish or kabhi-kabhi ole bhi padate hai. july or agast ke maheenon main saal kii sabase zyada garmi or sabase zyada nami hoti he jiskaa ausat dainik adhiktam taapmaana 30 °se. hota he.
 unani alexandria ko teen kshetron main vibhajit kiya gaya tha:
 panktibaddh stanbhashrenee yukt or kathith rup se lagbhag 60 meter chaudee do mukhya sadaken shahar ke bich main ek duusare se hokar guzarati thim jo us sthal ke nikat tha jahaan sikandar ke sema  kaa sthit tha. yah sthal nebi dainiel kii vartmaan masjid ke kaaphi nikat sthit he; or saath main yah mahaan east-west "canopic" sadak kii pankti ke bhi kaaphi nikat he jo aadhunik baulaward di roset se sirf thodaa sa hataa hua he. roseta get ke paas iske feetpath or nahar ke nishaan paae gaye hai lekin sadakon or naharon ke avasheshon kaa anaavaran purvi durgon ke baahar german utkhankon dwaara 1899 main hua tha jo pracheen shahar kshetra ke bheetar kaaphi acchhee sthiti main tha.
 alexandria main mul rup se faros ke dweep se thodaa adhik kshetra shaamil tha jise lagbhag ek mile lambe ek tile ke dwaara mukhya bhoomi se judaa hua tha or jise heptastadian . iskaa antim sira vartmaan grand squaire ke sheersh sthal kii bhoomi se sata hua tha jahaan "moon get" sthit tha. ve sab ab us sthal or gaad par bane aadhunik "raas al-tin" quarter ke bich sthit he jo dhire-dhire failata gaya or is tile kaa namonishaan mitaa diya. "raas al-tin" un sabhi kaa pratinidhitiv karata he jo faros ke dweep par shesh rah gaye hai jo samudr ke prabhaav se samaapt hone vaale vaastavik prakashastambh kaa sthal he. tile ke purv disha main great harbar sthit tha jo ab ek khuli khadi he; pashchim main unostose bandargah or uskaa aatanrik basin kibotose sthit tha jo ab kaaphi vistrut hokar aadhunik bandargah sthal kaa rup dhaaran kar chukaa he.
 strabo kaal kii pramukh imaaraton kaa vivaran usee kram main neeche prastut kiya gaya he jis kram main unhen great harbar main pravesh karne vaale jahaaj se dekha ja saktaa tha.
 mukhya bhoomi par sthit kuch anya saarvajanik imaaraton ke naam gyaat he lekin unki vaastavik avasthiti ke bare main bahut kam jaankaree uplabdh he. haalanki, unamem se koi bhi imaarat faros dweep ke purvi kshetra main sthit imaaraton kii tarah prasiddh nahin he. vahaan, duniya ke saat ajoobon main se ek, da great linehouse, kii sthiti kaa pataa chalaa jo 138 meter ooncha hone kii vajah se kaaphi mashhoor tha. tolemi pratham ne is pariyojna ko shuru kiya tha or tolemi dwitiya ne ise puraa kiya tha jiske nirmaan main kul 800 tallent kharch hua tha. ise puraa hone main 12 saal lag gaye or isane duniya ke sabhi parvarti prakashastambhon ke ek prototip ke rup main apane seva pradaan kii. sheersh par sthit ek bhatthi se prakaash utpann hota tha or iskaa tower zyaadaatar chuunaa-pathar ek thos blokon se nirmit tha. faros prakashastambh 14veen sadi main aae ek bukamp main nasht ho gaya tha, great pyramid of giza ke baad sabase zyada dinon tak bachaa rahane vaala yah doosraa pracheen ajooba tha. faros main tile ke sheersh sthal par hefastus kaa ek mandir bhi tha.
 pehali sadi main, alexandria kii jansankhya main bahut badi sankhya main mukt daason, mahilaon, bacchon or daason ke alaava, 180,000 se adhik vyask purush naagarik shaamil the. 500,000 se lekar 1,000,000 se bhi adhik kii anumaanit kul jansankhya ke aadhaar par yah audyogic kranti se pehle nirmit us vakt tak ke sabase bade shaharon main se ek tha or aisa sabase bada purv-audyogic shahar tha jo ek shahi rajdhani nahin tha.
 pracheen kaal main alexandria main lagaataar yuddh hote rahane kii vajah se vartmaan kaal main is pracheen shahar kaa bahut kam hissaa bachaa rah gaya he. bukamp avatalan kii vajah se adhikansh shahi or naagarik quarter bandargah-sthal ke neeche dab chuke hai or shesh quarteron kaa nirmaan aadhunik kaal main hua he.
 alexandria kaa "pompi'sa pillar", ek roman vijay stambh, sabase zyada prasiddh pracheen smarakon main se ek he jo aaj bhi vahaan maujuud he. yah alexandria ke pracheen ecropolis — shahar ke arabi kabristaan ke nikat sthit ek maamuli pahari — par sthit he or yah vastav main ek mandir kii stanbhashrenee kaa hissaa tha. aadharatal sahit isaki oonchai 30 meter he; iskaa shaft policd laal grenite kaa bana he, jiske aadhaar kaa vyas 2.7 meter he jo upar kii taraf kam hote-hote sheersh par keval 2.4 meter rah jaataa he. grenite ke keval ek hi tukade se bane is shaft kii oonchai 88 feet he. yah 132 ghan meter yaa lagbhag 396 ton hoga. pompi ke khambhe ko unheen tareekon kaa istemaal karke khadaa kiya gaya hoga jin tareekon kaa istemaal pracheen chatushkonik stambhon ko khadaa karne main kiya gaya tha. romanon ke paas crinen thi lekin unamem is tarah ke baari-bharkam khand ko uthaane laayak majbootee nahin thi. roger hopkins or mark lerner ne chatushkonik stambhon ko khadaa karne ke liye kai prayog kiye jinamein se unhone 1999 main 25 ton vaale ek chatushkonik stambh ko khadaa karne ke prayogon main safalataa praapt kii. iske baad chote-chote chatushkonik stambhon ko sidhaa khadaa karne ke liye do prayog kiye gaye or 25 ton vaale ek chatushkonik stambh ko khadaa karne ke do asafal prayaas kiye gaye. butaparasti ke sarvanaash ke sandarbh main ek bishap dwaara aadesh jari kiye jane par chauthi sadi main is sanrachana ko lut liya gaya or dhwast kar diya gaya. "pompi'sa pillar" ek mithyaa naam he kyonki pompi ke saath iskaa koi lenaa-dena nahin he, jise 293 main shaayad domitius domitiyanas ke vidroh kii yaad main, diocalatian ke liye banwaaya gaya tha. khud ecropolis ke neeche serapium ke bhumigat avshesh dabe pade hai, jahaan serapis devtaa ke gudh tathyon ko adhiniyamit kiya gaya tha or jiske naqkashidaar deevaariya sthalon ne manyatanusaar pracheen pustkaalay ke liye bahutayat bhandar sthal pradaan kiya he.
 alexandria bhugarbha kabristaan, jise kom al-shokapha ke naam se janaa jaataa he, is khambhe se dakshin pashchim disha main kuch duuri par sthit he, jisamen ek bahu-stareey bhulbhulaiya he, jahaan tak ek vishaal ghumaavdaar seedhi ke madhyam se pahunchaa ja saktaa he, jahaan murtiklatmak khambhon, murtiyon or anya samadharmi romano-misri dharmik pratikon, kabr sthalon or pathar ke tabuton se susajjit darjanon kakshon ke saath-saath roman-style ke aadhaar par nirmit ek vishaal pritibhoj kaksh bhi he jahaan mritak ke rishtedaaron dwaara mritak kii yaad main bhojan kaarykram kaa aayojan kiya jaataa tha. 1800 ke dashak main sanyogvash bhugarbha kabristaan kii khoj hone tak naagarik ise kaaphi lambe samay se ise bhulaae hue the.
 alexandria ke vartmaan main chal rahi sabase vyapak pracheen khudaai ko kom al-dikka ke naam se janaa jaataa he or isse is pracheen shahar ke susanrakshit naatyashaala kaa pataa chalaa he or iske romankaaleen snaan kunda ke avshesh bhi mili hai.
 alexandria kii pracheenkaaleen vastuon kaa pataa lagane ke liye lagaataar prayaas kiye gaye hai. sthaneey puratetvik samaaj or kai logon, vishesh rup se is shahar ko apane desh ke itihaas kaa ek gaurav maanakar garv karne vaale unaniyon ne, is kaary main apana protsaahan or sahyog pradaan kiya he.
 sangrahalay ke purv or vartmaan nideshkon ko samay-samay par avasar milane par vyavasthit bhang se khudaai kaary karne kii kshamta pradaan kii jaati he; di. ji. hogarth ne 1895 main socity for da pramotion of helenic studies or egypt exploration fund kii taraf se jaanch ke taur par kuch shodh kiye; or ek german abhiyaan par do saal tak kaam hota rahaa. lekin alexandria main asafal utkhanak ko do mushkilon: khudaai ke liye jagah kii kami or kuch ruchipurna kshetron ke kaary-sthal kaa pani ke neeche hona, kaa saamana karnaa padaa.
 ekadam se pracheen shahar ke upar vartmaan vishaal or vikasshil aadhunik shahar ke sthit hone kii vajah se, keval atyadhik kharch ke alaava, khudaai karne laayak koi paryaapt jagah milanaa asambhav he. isa ke janm ke pashchaat chauthi sadi main cleopetra saptam ke shahi quarter bhukampon or jwariya laharon main jalmagn ho gaye jisase ye dhire-dhire dhansane lage. is jalmagn bhaag kaa khulaasa 1992 main hua jisamen unanivadik shahar ke mahal ke quarter sahit kai sabase dilachasp bhaag shaamil the or francisi jalmagn puratatvavid frank godio or unke dal ke log abhi bhi bade paimaane par isaki chaanbin kar rahe hai. isamen sizarian kaa ek ullekhneey sir baraamad hua. kuch vivaadon ke baavjood inhen paryatakon ke liye kholaa ja rahaa he. adhikansh khule sthaan uttar-purv or dakshin-pashchim kii taraf sthit nichali jameen he jahaan roman str se neeche janaa vyavhaarik rup se asambhav he.
 "pompi ke khambhe" ke pados main sangrahalay ke swargavaasi nideshak, dow ji. boti, ne sabase mahatvapurn kamyaabi hasil kii jahaan kaaphi pariman main khuli jameen uplabdh he. yahaan ek bade bhawan yaa bhaavon ke samooh kii upsanrachanaayen ujagar hui hai jo shaayad serapium kaa hissaa he. paas main, vishaal bhugarbha kabristaan or columberiyaa kholaa gaya he jo mandir ke bahari hisse ho sakate hai. inamen ek bahut ullekhneey gumbaj shaamil he jis par kautuhal paidaa karne vaali chitrankit nakkashi kii gai thi jise ab kritrim rup se prakashit kiya gaya he or aagtukon ke liye khol diya gaya he.
 anusandhanon main praapt vastuon ko sangrahalay main rakhaa gaya he jisamen se sabase ullekhneey vastu besalt se bana ek vishaal bail he jo shaayad kabhi serapium main ek upasna yogya vastu thi. anya bhugarbha kabristaan or kabron ko kom al-shokapha or raas al-tin main khol diya gaya he.
 german khudaai dal ko shahar ke uttar-purv main ek tolemic stanbhashrenee or sadakon ke avshesh, lekin bahut kam, mili. hogarth ne kom al-dikka ke tile ke andar sthit ek vishaal eent sanrachana ke hisse kaa pataa lagaaya jo penium, mausoliya, yaa ek roman kile kaa hissaa ho saktaa he.
 nai abari kaa nirmaan karne ke liye patriarkal carch ke avasheshon kaa talkarshan hua; or aadhunik imaaraton kii neenv rakhane ke dauraan shaayad hi kabhi kisi pracheenkaaleen vastu kaa pataa na lagata ho. isamen koi andeh nahin he ki jameen ke bheetar kaaphi dhan dabaa padaa he; lekin har koshish karne ke baavjood puravettaon ko alexandria main sangrahalay or "pompi ke khambhe" ke pados ke baahar zyada kuch dekhne ko nahin mila he. haalanki, deshi-kabr luteron, su-gotakhoron, nikarshakon or isi tarah ke anya madhyamon se samay-samay par moolyawaan vastuon kaa pataa chalaa he jinamein se adhikansh ko nijee sangrah main rakhaa gaya he.

 rome ke baad alexandria ko duniya main isai dharm kaa ek pramukh centre mana jaataa tha. alexandria ke pope, dwitiya amang ikvals the, jo rome ke bishap ke baad duusare shreshth vyakti the, jo 430 tak roman samrajya kii rajdhani thi. alexandria ke carch ke adhikaar kshetra main sampoorn africa mahadweep shaamil tha. isa ke janm ke 451 varsh baad chalceden parishad kii tarah alexandria ke carch ko miaphisites or melkits main vibhajit kar diya gaya. miaphisites ne jis carch kaa nirmaan kiya use aaj alexandria ke coptic roodhiwaadee carch ke naam se janaa jaataa he. melkits ne jis carch kaa nirmaan kiya use aaj alexandria ke unani roodhiwaadee carch ke naam se janaa jaataa he. 19veen sadi main, catholic or protestant mishnariyon ne roodhiwaadee charchon ke anuyaayiyon main se kuch ke dharmon ko apane-apane dharmon main tabdeel kar diya.
 aaj, pope of coptic roodhiwaadee carch kaa kulapati centre, ramleh main sthit saint mark cathedral he. alexandria ke sabase mahatvapurn coptic roodhiwaadee charchon main cleopetra kaa pope siril first carch, sporting kaa saint georges carch, sidi bisher kaa saint mark and pope peter first carch, asaphra kaa saint mairi carch, gianaklis kaa saint mairi carch, fleming kaa saint minaa carch, mandara kaa saint minaa carch or ibrahimia kaa saint tekal hemint carch shaamil hai.
 alexandria ke sabase mahatvapurn unani roodhiwaadee carch, saint anargiri carch, carch of da enanciation, saint anthony carch, arkengels gabriyal and maaikel carch, saint catherine carch, mansheya kaa cathedral of da dormishan, carch of da dormishan, prophet elizah carch, saint georges carch, ibrahimia kaa carch of da imaculett concepshan, fleming kaa saint joseph carch, saint joseph of eremathia carch, ramleh kaa saint mark and saint nectarius chapel, saint nicholas carch, saint parskavi carch, ramleh kaa saint sawa cathedral or saint theodor chapel hai. unani roodhiwaadee carch se mail khaane vaale dharmik sampradaay main alexandria ke roosi roodhiwaadee saint alexander nevsky carch kaa pramukh sthaan he jo shahar main rahane vaale roosi bolne vaale samudaay ko apni seva pradaan karata he.
 latin catholic sanskaron kaa paalan karne vaale charchon main mansheya kaa saint catherine carch or cleopetra kaa carch of da jesuits shaamil he.
 college saint mark ke hisse ke rup main praapt, shatbi kaa saint mark carch bahuaiami he or yahaan par latin catholic, coptic catholic or coptic roodhiwaadee sanskaron ke anusaar puja-prarthana kiya jaataa he.
 alexandria ke adhikansh naagarik islam dharm kaa paalan karte hai. alexandria kaa sabase prasiddh masjid, enfooshi kaa abu al-abbaas al-mursi masjid he. shahar ke anya ullekhneey masjidon main somuha kaa ali ebn abi talib masjid, bilal masjid, mandara kaa al-gami al-bahaari, somuha kaa hatem masjid, sidi bisher kaa hoda al-islam masjid, haadaraa kaa al-movasah masjid, miami kaa shark al-madina masjid, mustafa kamel kaa al-shohada masjid, qaid ibrahim masjid, zizinia kaa yehia masjid, sidi gabar kaa sidi gabar masjid or sultan masjid shaamil hai.

 alexandria main asankhya ucch shiksha santhaan hai. alexandria vishvavidhyalay ek saarvjaanik vishvavidhyalay he jo misari ucch shiksha pranali kaa anusaran karata he. iske kai faceltiyon, khaas taur par ejineering ke faceltiyon, kaa antarrashtriya str par kaaphi naam he. iske alaava, arab vigyaan evam praudyogiki or samudri parivahan academy ek ardh-nijee shikshan santhaan he jo ucch vidhyalay ke saath-saath purvasnatak str ke chaatro ke liye bhi paathyakramon kii peshkash karata he. uniwarsity senghar ek nijee francisi vishvavidhyalay he jo manviki, raajnneeti or antarrashtriya sambandhon ke adhyaapan par dhyaan kendrit karata he or jo mukhya rup se africa mahadweep ke chaatro ko apana lakshya banaata he. alexandria ke anya ucch shikshan sansthaanon main alexandria institute of technology ) or faros uniwarsity in alexandria shaamil hai.
 alexandria ke videshi shikshan sansthaanon kaa ek bahut lanbaa itihaas he. pehle videshi vidyalayon kaa samaykaal 19veen sadi kaa aarambhik daur he, jab francisi mishnariyon ne misravasiyon ko shikshit karne ke liye francisi dharmarth vidyalayon kii sthapana karnaa shuru kiya tha. aaj, alexandria main catholic mishnariyon dwaara chalaae jane vaale sabase mahatvapurn francisi vidyalayon main college de laa mer di diu, college notray dame di sayan, college saint mark, icholes des soyurs franciscans, ichole jerard, ichole saint-vincent di paul, ichole saint joseph, ichole sante catherine or institution sante jiyaan-antide shaamil hai. francisi dharmik sansthaaon kii sthapana ke pratikriyaswaroop, ek dharmanirpeksh mishan ne lisi al-horreya kii sthapana kii, jisane shuru main ek francisi shikshan pranali kaa anusaran kiya lekin vartmaan main yah misr kii sarkaar dwaara chalaaya jane vaala ek saarvjaanik vidhyalay he. alexandria main puri tarah francisi shiksha pranali kaa anusaran karne vaala ekmaatr vidhyalay ichole champolian he. yahaan aam taur par alexandria ke francisi visheshgyon or rajanyikon ke bachche hi aksar aate hai.
 alexandria main angreji vidyalayon kii sankhya bahut kam he or francisi vidyalayon kii tulana main inaki sthapana abhi haal hi main hui he. is shahar ke sabase mahatvapurn angreji bhashi vidyalayon main alexandria american skool, british skool of alexandria, egyptian american skool, modern american skool, secred heart girls skool ), shatz american skool, victoria college, al manaar language skool of girls ), comeya language skool ), al nasr boij skool ) or al nasr girls college ) shaamil hai. inamen se adhikansh vidyalayon ko naser yug ke dauraan rashtriyakrut kiya gaya he or vartmaan main ye misr ke shiksha mantralay dwaara sanchalit misri saarvjaanik vidhyalay ban gaye hai.
