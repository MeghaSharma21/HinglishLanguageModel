 sandip singh ek maidani hockey khilaadi or bhartiya hockey team ke purv captain hai. bhartiya hockey ke draig flikar sandip ko penalty carnor visheshgy ke rup me janaa jaataa he. hockey main vishesh uplabdhiyon ke liye bharat sarkaar ne sandip ko 2010 main arjuna puraskaar se sammanit kiya. vartmaan me haryana police vibhaag main dspi rank par karyrat hain. 
 film nirmaataa shaad ali ne sandip singh ke jivan par suurama naam kii ek film banaai he. dilajit dosanjh ne film main sandip singh kii bhoomika nibhaai he. ise 13 july 2018 ko jari kiya gaya tha. film main taapasi pannu or angad bedi bhi hai. 
 sandip singh haryana main kurukshetra jile ke shahbaad shahar se hai. sandip ke bade bhai bikramjit singh bhi hockey khelte he, bikramjit indian oil ke liye khelte hai.
 sandip singh ne antarashtriya khel safar 2004 main yojit kualalumpur main ajalan shah cap se shuru kiya. 2009 main bhartiya hockey team kaa captain niyukt kiya gaya, sandip kii captaini main bharat ne ajalan shah cap 2009 kaa khitaab jeeta. ye bharat kii sultan ajalan shah hockey main 13 saal baad pehali jeet thi.
 sandip hero hockey india lig ke pehle sanskaran main 11 gol karke us sanskaran main sarvaadhik gol karne vaale khiladi ban gaye the.
