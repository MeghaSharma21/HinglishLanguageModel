 krishnakanth malviya hindi patrkaar the. ve mahaamanaa madanamohan malviya ke bade bhai jaikrishna malviya ke dwitiya putr the. 
 1904 i0 main elahabaad vishvavidhyalay se bi0e0 kiya. 1910 i0 main 'abhyuday' ke sampadak hue or 1911 i0 main masik 'maryaada' kaa bhi sampadan shuru kiya jisamen sahityak, raajnneetik or saamaajik vishyon par vichaarpurn lekh prakashit hote the.san 1916-18 varsh kii avadhi me abhyuday ke sampadak pad se alag rahe par 1918 se 1930 i0 tak phir 'abhyuday' ke sampadak rahe. maryaada kaa sampadan 1922 tak kiya. satyagrah andolanon ke sambandh main teen baar jail gaye. lagbhag 12 varsh tak kendriya essembly ke sadsy rahe. 
 aapke vichaaron par lala lajpat raay or mahatma gandhi kaa prabhaav adhik tha. falat: vidhwaa vivaah, achhutodwar aurash paitrik sampati main ladakiyon ko bhi hissaa milane kaa samarthan kiya. pehle vishwa mahayuddh ke samay 'sansar sankat' stambh ke antargat antarrashtriya raajnneeti kaa vishleshan hindi patrakarita ko aapaki vishesh dein thi.
