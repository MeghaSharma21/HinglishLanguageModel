 max liberman german chitrakaar or khudaai kala kaa karigar tha.
 max liberman berlin ke ek yahudee banker ke ghar janma tha. pehle usne berlin vishvavidhyalay se kanoon evam darshan kii shiksha lee. kintu baad main 1869 main vimar main tathaa 1872 main paris main chitrakala kii shiksha lee. usne 1876-77 main nederlands main bhi chitrakala kii shiksha lee. 
 liberman berlin main hi rahata tha. stepphed kaa vah shishy tha. vimar ke skool ov art main 1868 i. tak usne adhyayan kiya. san 1873 se 1878 main usne prabhaavvaadi drushti apni kritiyon main apanaai thi. iske banaae gaye wills ke gramin drishyon, holland ke kheton or germany ke kaarkaanon ke drishy ke chitron par je. israels ke chitron kaa prabhaav lagata he. 'san bunakar', 'stri or bakriyaan', 'vruddh kaa ashrayasthaan' aadi kritiyaan prasiddh he. iske kuch chitra berlin neshnal art gailaree main rakhe hai.
