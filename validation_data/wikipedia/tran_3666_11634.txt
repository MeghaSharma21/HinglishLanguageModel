 jahaan ek yaa anek varnon kii krmanusaar aavriti keval ek baar ho arthath ek yaa anek varnon kaa prayog keval do baar ho, vahaan chhekanupras hota he. chhekanupras kaa ek udhaaharan drashtavy he—
 dekhu duru vah kunj kutir main baitho palotat radhika payan.main manohar bain bajai susajai tan sohat pit pata he.uparyukt pehali pankti main 'da' or 'k' kaa tathaa duusari pankti main 'm' or 'ba' kaa prayog do baar hua he.
 varnon kii aavriti ko anupras kehete hai. udhaaharan -
 chaaru chandra kii chanchal kirane,
 khel raheen thim jal-thal main.
 svachh chandani bichhi hui thi,
 avani or ambaratal main.
 anupras ke prakaar
 chhekanuprasː jab varnon kii aavriti ek se adhik baar hoti he to vah chhekanupras kehalaata he. udhaaharan -
 mud mangalamay sant samaju. jo jag jangam tirthraju.
 vrutyanuprasː jab ek hi varna kii aavriti anek baar hoti he to vrutyanupras hota he. udhaaharan -
 kaam koh kalimal karigan ke.
 latanuprasː jab ek shabd yaa vaakyakhand kii aavriti hoti he to latanupras hota he. udhaaharan -
 vahi manushya he, jo manushya ke liye mare.
 antyanuprasː jab ant main tuk miltaa ho to antyanupras hota he. udhaaharan -
 maangi naav na kevatu aana. kahahi tumhaar maramu main janaa.
 shrutyanuprasː jab ek hi varg ke varnon kii aavriti hoti he to shrutyanupras hota he. udhaaharan -
 dinanth tha the dinnath duubate, sadhenu aate grih gwaal bal the.
