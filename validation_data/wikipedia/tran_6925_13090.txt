 loha 1997 main bani nirdeshak kanti shah kii ection-drama aadhaarit hindi bhashaa kii film he. film kii mukhya bhoomikaaon main dharmendra, mithun chakravarti, shakti kapur, mohan joshi or kiran kumaar ne abhinay kiya he. nirdeshak kanti shah ne isi film ke mukhya abhinayta mithun evam anya charitra kalakaaron ke saath laghu bajet kii gunda film banaai jo sameekshak rup se nakaaratmak rahi lekin vishesh darshakon kii pasand se sankshipt rup main safal ise kalt film ghoshit kiya gaya.
