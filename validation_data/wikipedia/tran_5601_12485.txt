 lahore zila, pakistan ke punjab praant kaa ek jila. yah mukhyatah ek nagariya janapad he, or aitihaasik lahore shahar tathaa uske upnagareey kshetron ko gherta he. yah kshetra aitihaasik tathaa saanskrithik drushtikon se, keval punjab or pakistan hi nahin balki puure bharatvarsh main mahatvapurn sthaan rakhti he. yahan bole jane vaali pramukh bhashaa punjabi he, jabaki urdu praayah har jagah samajhi jaati he. saath hi angrezi bhi adhikansh logon dwaara samajhi jaati he. prabhukh prashasnic bhashaayen urdu or angrezi he.
 yah purnatah ek shahari ilaaka he, atah prashasan hetu is 9 "nagaron" main baanta gaya he, jinhen or bhi chote union parishadon main vibhajit kiya gaya he. jiname se kai ilaake lahore shahar main aitihaasik mahatv rakhte hai, jabaki kai aadhunik kshetra hai.
