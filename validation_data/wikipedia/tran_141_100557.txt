 khmer lipi cambodia kii khmer bhashaa ko likhane main prayukt hone vaali lipi he. aisa vishwaas kiya jaataa he ki khmer lipi kaa vikaas bharat kii pallav lipi se hua.
 khmer lipi vishwa kii sabase vrihad varnmaala he . isamen 33 byanjan, 23 swar tathaa 12 swatantra swar hai. jo 23 mukhya byanjan hai unako do samoohon main baanta ja saktaa he, pratham shrenee tathaa dwitiya shrenee iske alaava iske prayek byanjan ke do rup hai - pehla, saamaanya rup or doosraa, byanjan ke neeche vaala rup. iske alaava iske sarvaadhik prayukt 10 byanjanon kaa ek 'aupchaarik rup' bhi he jo sheershak, vigyaapan, mandir kii deevaaron aadi par likhane ke liye prayukt hota he. is prakaar kul 101 alag-alag pratik hai.
