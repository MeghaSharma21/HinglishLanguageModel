 rajanyik mishan se taatpary kisi desh yaa antarrashtriya antar-sarkari sanstha ke logon ke us samooh se he jo kisi duusare desh yaa antarrashtriya antar-sarkari sanstha main rahete hue aadhikarik taur par apane desh kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai. isko 'sthai mishan' bhi kehete hai.
 14 march 1975 main viana kanvension, jo saarvbhaumik swaroop ke anterrashtriya sanghathanon main rajyon ke pratinidhitiv se sambandhit thi, ne sthaayee mishan ko prabhavit kiya. is kanvension ke anuchhed 1 ke anusaar, sthaayee mishan kaa arth ek aise mishan se he jo sthaayee swaroop kaa hota he. aise rajya kaa pratinidhitiv karne ke liye sanghathan ko bheja jaataa he jo ki intaraashtriya sanghathan kaa sadsy rajya he.
