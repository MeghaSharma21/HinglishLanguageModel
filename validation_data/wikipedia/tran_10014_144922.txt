 north carolina sanyukt rajya america ke dakshini kshetra main ek rajya he. rajya kii seemaa dakshin main south carolina or georgia se, pashchim main tennesi se, uttar main varjeenia, or purv main atlantic mahaasaagar se lagati he. 18veen shatabdi main north carolina carolina naam ke upnivesh kaa hissaa tha jo mul teraa upnivesh main se ek thi. 1861 main america grihayuddh ke samay rajya parisangheey rajya america main jane vaala aakhri rajya bana tha. 
 sabhi 50 rajyon main north carolina kaa kshetra ke maamle main 28waan sthaan he. 2016 ke anumaan ke mutabik rajya kii jansankhya 1,01,46,788 hai jisase uskaa sthaan 9waan hua. angreji adhikarik bhashaa he jo 90% janata maatrubhaasha ke rup main bolti hai.
