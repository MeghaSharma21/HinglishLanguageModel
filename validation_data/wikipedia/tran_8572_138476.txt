 bhartiya nausena academy eheemala , bhartiya nausena kaa ek atyaadhunik prashikshan centre he jise eneviasi bhi kaha jaataa he. yah dakshin bharat ke kerala rajya ke kannur jile main sthit he. deeli kii bhavy pahadiyon, kavvayi ke nirmal apravahee jal or vishalkay arab sagar ke bich sthit eneviasi, apane manoram or shant maahaul ke kaaran prashikshan hetu ek adarsh sthaan he. yahaan bhartiya nausena or bhartiya tatarakshak bal main shaamil sabhi adhikariyon ko vibhinn paathyakramon ke tahat buniyadi prashikshan diya jaataa he. iene ke prashasnic or anya kaaryon ke liye mukhyatah ienes jaimorin jahaaj kaa prayog kiya jaataa he.
 yah academy ek antareep ke lagbhag 2,500 acre hisse par faili he, jo ki pracheen mushika raajaaon kii rajdhani thi. pracheen yug main yah kerala kaa ek samrudhshali bandargah or 11veen sadi main chola-chera yuddh ke dauraan yah ek pramukh yuddh kshetra rahaa tha. aisa mana jaataa he ki bhagwaan buddha ne eheemala kaa daura kiya tha.
 mana jaataa he ki eheemala naam saat pahadiyon se prerit he jo is ilaake ke kshitij par haaavee hai. yah ilaaka elimalai ke naam se janaa jaataa he jiskaa matlab 'rat hill' yaa chuha pahari he .ek or manyata yah he ki yah naam 'ejhil malai' se praapt kiya gaya he jiskaa matlab saundarya kii dharti he . lokkathaon ke mutabik saat pahadiyaan 'rishabadri' parvat kaa hissaa hai jo us vakt dharti par gir gaya tha jab ramayana kii puranic ladaai ke dauraan lakshmana gambhir rup se ghayal hokar moorchhit pade the or unhen bachaane ke liye bhagwaan hanuman mrit sanjivani or anya jadi-bootiyon ke saath is pahari ko lekar lanka ja rahe the. ye baat isse bhi siddh hoti he ki ye ilaaka kai tarah kii aushadhiy jadi-bootiyon se bharaa padaa he.
 nausena academy ke aadhaar depo jahaaj ienes jaimorin kaa namakaran kosicode ke jaimorin logon ke naam par kiya gaya he, jinke paas prasiddh kunjali marakkar ke netritv main yupoton kaa ek shaktishaalee bedaa tha. nausena ne kunjali marakkar kii yaad main watakara ke nikat sthit iringel main unke paitruk ghar main ek smarak kaa nirmaan bhi karavaaya he.
 1950 ke dashak kii shuruyaat main rashtriya raksha academy yaa naishanal dephens acedemi kii sthapana se pehle 'niyamit' pravishti vaale cadeton ko united kingdom main royal navy ke saath chaar saal ke prashikshan se guzarana padataa tha or ve up-leftinent ke taur par bharat waapas lautate the. 1960 ke dashak kii shuruyaat main ye mahasoos kiya gaya ki rashtriya raksha academy se baahar nikalne vaale cadet badhati hui bhartiya nausena kii jaruraton ko puraa nahin kar paaenge or isi vajah se cochin main ek academy sthapit karne kaa faisla kiya gaya. tadnusaar janvari, 1969 main cochin main ek nausena academy kii shuruyaat kii gayi jise antat 1986 main ienes mandovi main sthanantarit kar diya gaya. lagaataar bad rahe prashikshan ke dabaav kaa saamana karne ke liye ek nai sthaayee nausena academy kii manjuuri praapt karne kaa faisla kiya gaya tha. isi tarah, tatarakshakon ko bhi nausena ke saath prashikshit kiya jaataa tha.
 nai nausena academy kii vaicharik aavashyaktaaen 100 acre kshetrafal vaali ek jagah 'sabase mahatvapurn' aavashyaktaa thi, jo simenship or waterman ship prashikshan ke liye samudr yaa jhil ke aasapaas hone ke saath-saath railway stesan ke bhi nikat ho lekin phir bhi shahar se dur sthit ho. saath hi, ek 'vaanchit' aavashyaktaa yah bhi thi ki sthaan nausena bandargah ke nikat ho or vahaan kaa jalwaayu bhi pranaposhak tathaa sam ho.
 eheemala kaa chayan nai nausena academy ke liye jin sthanon par vichaar kiya gaya unamem wellington ke paas nilgiri pahadiyon main aruvankadu or pune-kolhapur road par sthit paayakara baandh jhil, lloyds baandh, bangalore ke paas hesarget, saurashtra samudratat par sthit porbandar, madras ke paas chingalput or kerala samudr tat par eheemala shaamil the. 1979 main sarkaar ne ek sthaayee nausena academy kii jarurat ko sweekaar kar liya. kerala sarkaar ne nausena ko uttari kerala ke kannur ke uttar main eheemala main 960 acre jameen dene kii peshkash kii. vahaan sabhi jaruri moolbhoot suvidhaayen, jaise peyajal, nirmaan ke liye pani, bijli, sadak or pul, kavvayi kaa apravahee pani, kataav rokane ke liye ek samudri-diwaar kaa nirmaan, paiyyanoor ke paas najdiki railway stesan kaa vistaar ityadi kerala sarkaar dwaara nausena ko muft main muhaiyaa karaae jaaenge. 1982 main sarkaar ne eheemala kii jagah ko sweekaar kar liya or kerala sarkaar ko jameen adhigrihith karne or visthapiton ke punarvaas ke liye madhyam avadhi kaa aasaan rin muhaiyaa karaayaa.
 puranic sambandh arab sagar se sate deeli parvat ke saath eheemala kii sthalakriti hi kuch aisi he ki pracheen kaal se sthaneey log iske bare main vibhinn kiwanditiyaan gadhane ke liye prerit hote rahe hai. inamen sabase lokapriy ramayana kii parampara se sambandhit he. ram or ravan ke bich ladaai ke ek charan main, bhai lakshmana samet ram kii sena ke kai log maare ja chuke the. pareshaan ram ne vanar sena ke sabase varisht jambavan se paraamarsh kiya. tab sharir se tir nikaalane, ghaavon ko thik karne, sharir ke kate hue hisse kii silaai karne or aakhir main mritkon ko jivit karne ke liye himalay se chaar aushadhiy jadi-booti, shalya karani, vishalee karani, sandhan karani or mrit sanjivani mangane kaa tay kiya gaya. in jadi-bootiyon ko laane kaa jimmaa hanuman ko diya gaya or ve himalay ke liye nikal pade. hanuman himalay to pahunch gaye lekin ve un ayurvedic jadi-bootiyon ko pahchaan nahin sake. isliye unhone ek bada kadam uthaaya – unhone puraa kaa puraa rishabadri parvat hi ukhaad liya or use lekar waapas ud chale. dakshin kii ore lautate samay is parvat kaa ek tukdaa samudr ke paas gir gaya or vahi agimala he. sthaneey log maanate hai ki eheemala main aaj bhi ve durlabh ayurvedic jadi-bootiyaan maujuud hai.
 academy kaa chaalu hona is academy kaa shilanyash 17 janvari 1987 ko purv pradhanmantri rajeev gandhi dwaara kiya gaya tha. academy ke aadhaar depo ienes jaimorin ko kerala ke mukhyamantri oman chandi dwaara 6 april 2005 ko shuru kiya gaya tha, is tarah eheemala nausena academy pariyojna kaa pratham charan puraa hua. shuru main is base ne academy ke nirmaan or iske prashikshan or prashasan ke sabhi pahaluon ke kaaryon main madad kiya. iske shilanyas ke kareeb 22 saal baad eheemala main bhartiya nausena academy udghaatan ke liye taiyaar thi. 8 janvari 2009 ko pradhanmantri manmohan singh ne is nausena academy kaa udghaatan kiya tha. raksha mantri e ke antni bhi is maukey par maujuud the, unhone 17 janvari 1987 ko tatkaaleen pradhanmantri rajeev gandhi ko shilanyas karte hue bhi dekha tha.

  rail – rajdhani express ko chhodkar konkan railway root par chalane vaali sabhi trainen, sampark kranti express, jodhpur express or jaipur maaru sagar express paiyyanoor main rukati he. kuch maamalon main uparokt trainen paiyyanoor ko chhodkar paas ke stesan kannur main rukati he.

  hawaii maarg – paiyyanoor main hawaii adda nahin he. yahaan se sabase najadik hawaii adda mangalore main sthit he jo bhartiya nausena academy se 150 kilometre dur sthit he. indian airlines or jet airways kii mangalore se aane or jane kii sevaayen sanchalit karte hai. anya hawaii adda kosicode main sthit he jo paiyyanoor se 145 kilometre dur he. mangalore or kosicode donon hi paiyyanoor se rail or sadak maarg bahut acchhee tarah se judaa hua he. haalanki mattanur main ek nayaa hawaii adda prastaavit he .
