 guangdong janavaadee ganraajya china kaa ek praant he. yah dakshini china sagar ke tat par sthit he. janvari 2005 main isaki jansankhya 10,43,03,132 anumaamit kii gai thi or yah china kaa sab se adhik aabaadi vaala praant he. iskaa kshetrafal 1,77,900 varg kimi he. guangzou shahar, jise puraane jamaane main canton bulaayaa jaataa tha, is praant kii rajdhani he. prasiddh audyogic shahar shanjan bhi isi praant main sthit he.
 aitirasic rup se yah praant pashchimi duniya kii nazar main bahut mahatvapurn he. solahavin or satrahavin sadi me portugali log china main yaheen par apana mahatvapurn upnivesh banaae the. unhone hi inkaa naam bolchaal kaa naam cantone- Canton rakhaa tha jo guangdong kaa sarlikrut rup tha. yaheen par baad main afim yuddh hue or british logon ne hongkong par 150 saalon tak raaj kiya. china kii do pramukh bhashaa - uttari boli or dakshini boli - main dakshini kaa naam isi shahar par padaa he - kentonies bhashaa. uttar kii pramukh bhashaa ko mandarin kehete hai. or, bharat main prasiddh cantonies chikan jaise vyanjanon ko isi jagah ke naam par rakhaa gaya he.
