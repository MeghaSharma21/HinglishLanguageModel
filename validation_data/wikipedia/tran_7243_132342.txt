 judaai 1997 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. iskaa nirdesan raaj kanvar dwaara kiya gaya or mukhya kalakaar anil kapur, shri devi or urmila matondkar hai. kadar khan, farida zalaal, johny liver, paresh rawal, upasna singh or said jafari sahaayak bhoomika main shaamil hai; jabaki punam dhillon ne ek vishesh upasthiti darj kii.
 yah film 1994 kii telugu film shubhalaagaman kii rimek thi. yah shri devi kii antim film thi jab tak ki unhone 2012 main inglish winglish se vaapasi nahin kii. relies hone par, film 1997 kii saatvin sabase zyada kamai karne vaali bhartiya film banane vaali ek pramukh vyavsaayik safalataa thi.
 jab kaajal peshey se abhiyantaa raaj se shaadi karti he, to vah or uske pita, swachaalit rup se maanate hai ki vah amir he. yahan tak ​​ki do bacchon ke janm or vivaah ke kai saalon main bhi vah nahin badalati. iskaa ek udhaaharan tab hota he jab kaajal apane puraani saheli nisha se milti he or kahati he uskaa pati ek bada bijness main he, uske parivaar ke paas kai car hai or ek bade bangale main rahete hai, lekin nisha, jo vastav main he amir sach jaan jaati he. jab jaahnvi, raaj ke maalik saahani kii bhatiji videsh se aati hai, vah hawaii adde par raaj se milti he or maanati he ki vah car chaalak he. uske baad vah use uske saamaan ke saath sadak par fanse hue chhod detaa he, ye parvaah kiye bina ki uske maalik kya kahenge. jahnavi samay par acchhee tarah se karyaalay pahaunchi or raaj ke ravaiye kii prashansa kii. jab vah jaanati he ki vah vivaahit vyakti he, to vah tab bhi raaj se shaadi karne ke liye atal he.
 vah pehali baar ek dukaan par kaajal se milti he, jahaan ve donon raaj ke liye casette player kharidne jate hai. parivaar jahnavi kaa diya hua tepe sunata he jisamen wo apana pyaar kabool karti he. raaj se shaadi karne ke prayaas main, jahnavi shahar ke ganesh mandir ke andar laalchee kaajal se milti he. vah raaj ke vivaah ke badale main do karod rupee kii peshkash karti he. tab kaajal, ise dhan or aasaan jivan paane kaa avasar maanate hue, achaanak uske prastaav ko sweekaar karti he. kaajal ne samjhaute ke liye raaj ko majaboor kar diya or hindu vivaah adhinium kaa anupaalan karne ke liye raaj or jahnavi ko vivaahit karakar khud raaj ko talaak de diya; yah sochakar ki vah or jahnavi khushi se saath rahenge or raaj ko saajha karenge. uske baad vah sauda main mili dhan kaa upyog ek vishaal haveli or kai car kharidne ke liye karti he. vah jo bangala kharidti he vah nisha kaa nikalataa he. tab kaajal ne use taana maar ke kaha ki usne apane sapanom ko puraa kar liya he, jabaki nisha ke sapane ab sabakuch khone ke baad tut gaye hai. nisha ne kaajal ko yaad dilaayaa ki usne apana ghar or sampatti ko apane biimaar pati ke ilaaj ke liye bech diya, jabaki kaajal ne apane pati ko dhan ke liye bech diya he; or kuch had tak, kaajal usase se bhi garib he. kaajal ne khud ko prabhavayukt vyakti main dhaala or isse apane parivaar kii upeksha kar rahi. raaj shuruyaat main kaajal dwaara khaarij kiya gaya mahasoos karata he or jahnavi ke kareeb nahin gaya. lekin bacchon or raaj ko jahnavi ke saath saathi miltaa he, jo unhen pyaar or sneh ke saath dekhati he. vah khud ko badalati he or ek saamaanya bhartiya gruhinee ban jaati he.
 ab kaajal ko pataa chalata he ki vah apane parivaar se kitni dur chali gai he. vah apni shaadi kii saalagirah bhul jaati he or apni beti ke janmdin ke liye janmdin kii party rakhti he, yah mahasoos kiye bina ki raaj kabhi bhi bhavy jeevanstyli ke liye aakarshith nahin hua he or is tarah use jahnavi kii ore dhakel rahi he. kaajal sudharne kii koshish karti he. jab kuch bhi kaam nahin karata, tab kaajal jahnavi ko thappad maarthi he or use apane pati ko chura lene kaa aarope lagaati he. tab vah jahnavi ko ghar se baahar phenkane kii koshish karti he, lekin iskaa koi phayda nahin hua, kyonki raaj ne jahnavi ke saath jane kaa faisla kiya. bachche bhi jahnavi or unke pita ke saath rahane kaa faisla karte hai. duusari ore kaajal, apane parivaar ko chhodkar chale jane se vyakul ho jaati he. apana sabhi dhan wo daan karti he. jab vah jaanati he ki raaj or bachche america ja rahe hai, to vah hawaii adde par jaati he. vah unhen jane ke liye taiyaar paati he. lekin aakhri minute main, jahnavi raaj ko bataaya ki vah akele america ke liye ja rahi he, lekin vah sachmuch akeli nahin he. vah raaj ke bachche kii ummid kar rahi he. jahnavi sweekaar karti he ki yadhyapi kaajal ne apane pati ko bech diya tha, lekin vah hi thi jisane use kharidne kii peshkash kii or is tarah use bhi apni dendariyon kaa bhugtan karnaa chahie. to, film kaajal ke saath apane parivaar ko waapas aane or jahnavi ke hameshaa ke liye neuyork jane ke saath samaapt hoti he.
