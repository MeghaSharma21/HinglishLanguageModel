 rachanaatmakatavaad antarrashtriya sambandhon ke adhyayan kaa teesraa drushtikon he. adarshavaad or yatharthwaad ke mukaable yah nazariyaa vishwa-raajnneeti kii jaanch saamaajik dharaatal par karne kii tajaviz karata he. rachanaatmakataavaadiyon kaa daawa he ki antarrashtriya sambandhon kii sahi samaj keval bhautik simaaon ke daayre main hone vaali tarksangat anyonyakriyaaon se hi nahin hasil kii ja sakati or na hi unhen sansthagat simaaon ke bheetar parakhane se kaam chal saktaa he . rachanaatmakataavaadiyon ke anusaar samprabhu rajyon ke bich banane vaale sambandh keval unke sthir rashtriya hiton par hi aadhaarit nahin hote. unhen lambi avadhi ke dauraan bani asmitaaon ke prabhaav main uthaaye jane vaale kadamon ko praroopon ke taur par dekh kar samajhnaa padataa he.  rachanaatmakataavaadi apana dhyaan antarrashtriya samudaay ke str par maujuud sansthaaon par kendrit karte hue antarrashtriya kanoon, rajnay or samprabhutaa ko ahmiyat dete hai. shashan-vyavasthaayen bhi unke liye mahatvapurn hai, kyonki ve bhi viniyamkaari or vidheyak sanrachanaayen paidaa karti hai. unke haathon ek saamaajik jagat rachaa jaataa he jisaki madad se rajya ke vyavhaar or aacharan taatparyon kaa andaaza lagaaya ja saktaa he. 
 rachanaatmakatavaad kaa siddhant bhautik ayaamon kii upeksha nahin karata, par vah unke saath-saath antarrashtriya vyavastha ko samajhane ke liye saamaajik ayaamon ko bhi jod detaa he. iske tahat jab sansthaaon kii charchaa kii jaati he to uskaa matlab keval santhnik dhaanchaa hi nahin hota. sanstha kaa taatpary he asmitaaon or hiton kii ek sthir sanrachana. ek aisi sanrachana jisamen sajhi samaj, sajhi apekshaayen or saamaajik gyaan antarnihit he. rachanaatmakataavaadi sansthaaon ko multah ek sangyanatmak astitv kii tarah dekhte hai jinhen apane karttaaon ke vichaaron kii simaaon ke daayre main hi rahanaa padataa he. sansthagat sanrachanaon ke maanakeey prabhaav ke saath- saath rachanaatmakatavaad badalte hue maanakon or asmitaaon va hiton ke bich bane sutron kii jaanch karata he. chunki rajya or anya karttaaon kii gatividhiyon ke kaaran sansthaaon main lagaataar parivartan hota rahata he, isliye rachanaatmakatavaad sansthaaon or karttaaon ko paraspar anukulan karte rahane vaale astitvon kii tarah dekhtaa he. 
 is vivaran se lag saktaa he ki ek saidhantik ravaiye ke taur par rachanaatmakatavaad par amal karnaa kuch mushkil hi hoga. darasal, rajyon ke vyavhaar kaa andaaza lagane ke liye kisi ek khaas saamaajik sanrachana ko chunane kii sifaarish karne ke bajaay yah drushtikon kahata he ki kisi ek paristhiti main maujuud sanrachana kii jaanch-padtaal karke uske vishisht daayre main rajya ke vyavhaar kaa anumaan lagaaya ja saktaa he. agar anumaan galat sabit hota he to iskaa matlab yah hua ki us sanrachana ko thik se samajha nahin gaya he yaa vah achaanak badal gayi he. masalan, agar yathartwaadee kehete hai ki antarrashtriya sambandhon main araajaktaa vyaapt he, to rachanaatmakataavaadi kahenge ki unki yah dharna hobbs dwaara pratipaadit ‘prakrut avasthaa’ ke aaine main antarrashtriya samudaay ko dekhne se upji he. is prakrut avasthaa kaa nishkarsh saamaajik sambandhon kii ek vishisht sanrachana se nikaala gaya he jo ab badal chuki he. 
 sanrachnawadiyon kii manyata ke anusaar antarrashtriya sansthaayen viniman bhi karti hai or vidheyak bhoomika bhi nibhaati hai. vinimankaaree bhoomika kaa arth he kuch khaas tarah ke vyavhaaron or aacharnon ko maanya yaa pratibandhit karnaa. vidheyak bhoomika kaa matlab he kisi vyavhaar yaa aacharan ko paribhaashit karte hue use ek nishchit taatpary se sampann karnaa. rajya unki nigaah main ek nagomik asmita he. vah apane buniyadi lakshyon kaa nirdhaaran karata he. par in lakshyon kii prapti saamaajik asmitaaon par nirbhar he; arthath rajya khud ko antarrashtriya samaaj ke sandarbh main jis tarah samajhegaa, usee tarah uski saamaajik asmitaayen banengi. isi tarah kii asmitaaon ke aadhaar par rajya kaa rashtriya hit sanrachit hoga. 
 sanrachnawadi yathartwaadiyon dwaara araajaktaa ko diye jane vaale mahatva ko puri tarah nazarandaaz nahin karte, par unka kahana he ki araajaktaa apane aap main ahm nahin ho sakati. sambandhon kii araajaktaa do mitra rajyon ko bich bhi ho sakati he or do shatru rajyon ke bich bhi. donon kaa matlab alag-alag hoga. is lihaaz se araajaktaa nahin, balki uske prabhaav main ban sakane vaale saamaajik sambandhon kii kismein hi mahatvapurn ho sakati hai. in kism-kism ke saamaajik sambandhon ke mutabik hi rajya apane hiton ko paribhaashit karte hai. masalan, sheet-yuddh ke dauraan america or soviet sangh ke bich kaa rishta bhi ek saamaajik sambandh tha jiske tahat ve donon ek-duusare ko shatru kii tarah dekhte the or usee ke mutabik unke rashtriya hiton kii sanrachana hoti thi. jab unhone ek-duusare ko us saamaajik sambandh ke aaine main dekhnaa band kar diya, yaa us tarah ke saamaajik sambandh kii paristhitiyaan hi nahin rah gayin to uske parinamswaroop sheet-yuddh khatm ho gaya. 
 spasht he ki rachanaatmakatavaad ek vyaakhyaatmak siddhant ke taur par apni upyogita bahut spasht nahin kar paaya he. iske baavjood use ek saidhantik dhaanchaa pesh karne main kamyaabi avashya mili he. rachanaatmakatavaad ke prachlit hone ke peeche yathartwaadee or adarshavaadi drushtikonon kii aparyaapttaon kii bhoomika bhi he.
