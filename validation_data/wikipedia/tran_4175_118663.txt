 2010 asian khel ek bahu-khel pratiyogita thi jo china ke guanganou shahar main 12 navambar se 27 navambar 2010, ke bich yojit kii gai thi. 1990 main beijing ke uparant guanganou asian khelon kii mezbaani karne vaala doosraa chini shahar tha. khelon main 45 asian rashtriya olympic samitiyon se chaynit 9,704 athleton ne kul 476 pratispardhaon main vibhajit 42 khelon main bhaag liya. anterrashtriya olympic samiti ne janvari 2010 main kuwait kii rashtriya olympic samiti ko raajnneetik hastkshep ke kaaran nilambit kar diya tha, parinamswaroop kuwaiti khiladiyon ne in khelon main olympic dhvaj tale bhaag liya tha. 
 pantis rashtriya olympic samitiyon ke athleton ne khelon main padak jite or iname se untiis ne kam se kam ek swarna padak jeeta. macau or bangladesh ne in khelon main kramshah vushu or cricket main asian khelon ke apane pratham swarna padak jite the. iran, bharat, afghanistan, bangladesh, chini taipe, honkong, indonesia, jordan, kirgizstan, macau, malaysia, myanmar, uttar korea, oman or pakistan ne 2006 asian khelon kii tulana main saamaanya padak talika main apni sthiti main sudhaar kiya. mezbaan desh china ne lagaataar aathvin baar asian khelon kii padak talika main sarvocch sthaan arjeet kiya. chini athleton ne sabhi padak shreniyon main sarvaadhik padak hasil kiye, jisamen unhone 199 swarna, 119 rajat va 98 kaansya padak jite. china ne samgr taur par bhi sabase adhik padak jite . dakshin korea ne kul 232 padakon ke saath padak talika main dwitiya sthaan hasil kiya. japan 48 swarnon va kul 216 padakon ke saath tisare sthaan par rahaa.
 is talika main ranking antarrashtriya olympic samiti dwaara prakashit padak talikaon kii parampara ke saath sangat he. praathmik rup se talika kisi raashtra ke athleton dwaara jite gayi swarna padakon kii sankhya ke anusaar kramit he . iske pashchaat jite gayi rajat padakon ko tathaa phir kaansya padakon kii sankhya ko mahatv diya gaya he. agar phir bhi rashtron ne ek hi sthaan arjeet kiya he, to unhen ek hi ranking di gai he or unhen unke iosi code ke varnanukram anusaar soochibaddh kiya gaya he. 
 in khelon main kul 1,577 padak vitarit kiye gayi. kaansya padakon kii kul sankhya swarna yaa rajat padakon kii kul sankhya se adhik he kyonki 15 khelon kii prayek pratispardhaa main do padak vitarit kiye gayi the: badminton, mukkebaazi, kyuu sports, talwarbazi, judo, kabddi, karate, sepaktakara, soft tennis, squash, teble tennis, taikwando, tennis, kushti or vushu . padakon kii shreniyon main vividhta kaa kaaran kuch pratispardhaon main hue taai bhi hai. purushon kii kalatmak jimnastics kii bhootal spardhaa main swarna padak ke liye taai hua tha or koi bhi jimnast rajat se sammanit nahin kiya gaya. purushon kii 200 meter breststroke tairaaki, athletics main purushon ke pole walt or bowling ke purush yugal main bhi rajat padak ke liye taai tha tathaa in pratispardhaon main koi kaansya padak nahin diya gaya. canoeing main purushon kii ke1 1000 meter, athletics main mahilaon kii unchi kud main taai va purushon kii unchi kud main teen athleto ke taai kaa matlab tha ki in pratispardhaon main ekaadhik kaansya padak vitarit kiye gayi. 
 asian olympic parishad ne 19 navambar 2010 ko ghoshnaa kii thi ki uzbek judoka shokir muminove dope main fel ho gaye hai tathaa unase unka purushon kii 81 kigra pratispardhaa main jeeta gaya rajat padak chin liya gaya he. muminove ke mutr ke namoonay main pratibandhit padaarth mithailhekjamine paaya gaya tha. iske pashchaat kaansya padak vijeta japan ke maashahiro taakamatsu va kazakhstaan ke islam bojbaev ko rajat padak ke liye padonnat kar diya gaya tha.
 24 janvari 2011 ko osie ne katari athlete ahamed mohammad dheeb ko ayogy ghoshit karte hue unase unka chakkaa-phenk main jeeta gaya rajat padak chin liya. dheeb kaa bahirzaat testosterone chayapachayon kaa pareekshan sakaaratmak aayaa tha. kaansya padak vijeta mohammad samimi ko rajat va bharat ke chauthe sthaan par rahe vikaas gauda ko kaansya ke liye padonnat kar diya gaya.
