  .carlov kii hatya us samay kari gai jab vah ek kala pradarshnee ke udghaatan samaaroh ko sambodhit kar rahe the.
 hamalavar pistaul laharaate hue turki bhashaa main chillaaya, "ham aleppo main marate to tum yahaan maro. aleppo or syria main jo kar rahe ho usko bhuulo mat agar syria or aleppo main hamaare log surakshit nahin to aap surakshit nahin rahenge." iske baad hamalavar ne hawa main bandook chalaai, jisase vahaan sthit log bhaag gayi, or phir karlev ko goli maar di. rusi rajdoot kii hatya karne ke baad hamalavar vahiin khadaa rahaa, or turki ke pulicekarmiyo ne use maar giraaya. hamale ke baad ghayal rajdoot ko aspatal pahunchaaya gaya, parantu unako bachaa nahin sake.
