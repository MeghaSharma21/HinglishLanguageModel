 ganit main kshetra aisa samucchy hota he jisamen jod, ghataana, guna or vibhaajan paribhaashit hon or vahiin arth rakhen jo ve parimey sankhyaon or vaastavik sankhyaon ke liye rakhte hai. kshetron kaa bijganit, sankhya siddhanth or ganit kii kai shakhaon main mahatv he. parimey sankhyaon kaa kshetra or vaastavik sankhyaon kaa kshetra sabase adhik prayog hone vaale kshetra hai, haalanki sammishra sankhyaon kaa kshetra ganit ke alaava vigyaan va abhiyantriki main bhi prayog hota he.
