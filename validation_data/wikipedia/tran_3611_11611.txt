 pavitra bandhan ek bhartiya hindi dharawahik he. jiskaa prasaaran doordarshan par 9 sitambar 2013 se prarambh hua he. yah somvaar se shuukravaar raat 8:30-9:00 tak prasaarit hota he. 
 yah doordarshan neshnal ke pramukh prime time dharawahikon main se ek he.iskaa nirmaan ekta kapur or shobhaa kapur ne kiya he.
 yah kahani do logon kii he, jo ek duusare se majaburi main shaadi kar lete hai or baad main unhe ek duusare se pyaar ho jaataa he.
