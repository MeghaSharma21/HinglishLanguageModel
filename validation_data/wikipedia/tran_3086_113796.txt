 salaar de uuni, jise salaar de tunupa bhi kaha jaataa he, duniya kaa sabase bada namak kaa maidan he. 10,582 varg kimi ke kshetrafal vaala yah namak kaa maidan bolivia ke potosi va oruro vibhaagon main sthit he. yah 3,656 meter kii oonchaai par andy parvat shrunkhlaa ke chor par sthit he.
 salaar de uuni kuch pragaitihaasik jhiilon main badlaav ke kaaran ban gaya tha. vartmaan kaal main yah kai meter moti namak kii parat vaala maidan he jiske neeche ek atyant khaare pani kaa sarovar he. is nichle sarovar main lithium kaa ghanatv bahut he or anumaan lagaaya gaya he ki vishwa kaa 30% se 70% praapt kiya ja sakane vaala lithium isi sthaan par he. anumaan lagaaya gaya he ki salaar de uuni main 10 arab ton namak jamaa hua he.
