 hemanth joshi jansanchaar or patrakarita ke profesar hai. francisi kaviyon ke anuvadak.
 janm- 27 march 1954. nainital . shiksha:jawaharlal nehru vishvavidhyalay se bhashaa vigyaan main shodh oopaadhi evam francisi bhashaa evam sahitya main snaatak. 18 varsh tak bhartiya jansanchaar santhaan main patrakarita ke prashikshan/adhyaapan ke baad do varshon ke liye jamiya millia islamia ke hindi vibhaag main patrakarita kaa prashikshan . samprati bhartiya jan sanchaar santhaan main adhyaapan. bharat sarkaar ke soochna praudyogiki mantralay ke i-prashasan main sthaniyakaran kii upasmiti ke sadsy. jansanchaar shodh ke antarrashtriya sangh kii samiti ke sadsy. iske alaava anek antarrashtriya shaikshik sanghon ke sakriy sadsy. hungary, france, singapore, finland or sweden kii yatraayen.
 kavitaayen, vyangy or kahaniyaan anek patr-patrikaon main prakashit.francisi ke anek kaviyon ke anuwaad. patrakarita kii bhashaa par hindustan dainik ke liye 2 varshon tak niyamit stambh "vagarth" aurpatrkarita kii shiksha se sambandhit lekh anek pustakon main prakashit.
