 parmian kalp prithvi ke bhuvaigyanik itihaas main ek kalp tha, jo aaj se 29.8 karod varsh pehle armbh hua or 25.217 karod varsh pehle ant hua. yah purajivi mahakalp kaa antim kalp tha. is se pehle purajivi mahakalp kaa silurian kalp aayaa tha. parmian kalp ke ant ke saath-saath purajivi mahakalp kaa bhi ant hua or madhyajivi mahakalp ke pehle kalp, tritisik kalp, kaa armbh hua. parmian kalp ke ant main parmian-tritisik vilupti ghatana hui jisamen prithvi kii bahut-si jatiyaan vilupt ho gai.
