 dravon evam thoson main ghuli hui gaison ko nikaalane ko vigasan yaa gas nishkasana kehete hai. vishesh rup se jal yaa jaleey vilyanon se gaison ko nishkasit karne kii aavashyaktaa padati he. thoson se gas nikaalane kii bahut si vidhiyaan hai. 
 gaison ko thos athwa dravon se nishkashit karne ke kai kaaran hai. rasayan shastri aise vilaayakon se gas nishkasit karte hai jinase banane vaale yogic vaayu yaa oxygen ke prati samvedansheel hon . drav ko thanda karke jamaane par usamen gas ke avaanchit bulbule ban sakate hai yadi drav main gas ghuli ho.
