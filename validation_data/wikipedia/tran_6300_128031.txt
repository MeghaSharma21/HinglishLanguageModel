 puja dhingra ek bhartiya pastery shef or vyavsaayee he. vah bharat main pehali baar mkarun store sahit, mumbai main macrun bekri chen lee 15 patiseri ke maalik hai. 
 dhingra gastronomi main ruchi rakhte ek parivaar main paidaa hui thi. uske pita or uske bade bhai donon restaraan maalik hai. javaani main hi dhingra ne apni maa se pastery pakaane kii kala sikh lee. usne pehle 2004 main mumbai main kanoon kaa adhyayan kiya or phir bhinn kariyar aajamaane ke liye switzraland ke le buveret main seizar ritz skool main aatithy or prabandhan paathyakram karne ke liye chali gai. teen saal baad, usne paris main le cordan blue main prashikshan shuru kiya. vahaan usne piyare hemeri ke petisris main se ek main ek makaaka dekha. apana course puraa karne ke baad, dhingra mumbai laut ai or march 2010 main apana pehla store kholaa.2016 main dhingra ne dakshin mumbai main le15 cafe naamak ek nayaa store kholte hughapane udhyam kaa vistaar kiya. jabaki francisi mahilaon ke naam par hastakshar desrt pradarshit kiye jaaenge, cafe ke menu main saral, zaekedaar bhojan mukhya course vyanjan shaamil hai. 
 jab usne 2010 main apana mumbai kaarobaar kholaa, to uske paas keval do karmchaariyon kaa staff tha. oktober 2014 tak, unke paas 42 karmchaaree the. uski mahatvakanksha puure bharat main kuch 20 pastery pratishthanon ko kholana he.
 vah ek sarvshereshth bikri pakkala pustak ke lekhak bhi hai.
