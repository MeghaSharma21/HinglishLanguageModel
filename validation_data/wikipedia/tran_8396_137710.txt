 kurukshetra jila bharat ke uttari rajya haryana ke 21 jilon main se ek he. kurukshetra kasbaa hinduon kaa ek paavan sthaan, jile kaa prashasnic mukhyalay he. jile kaa kul kshetrafal 1682.53 varg kimi he. jile kii kul jansankhya 964231 he. yah jila ambala sambhaag kaa ek bhaag he.
 jile kaa naam kurukshetra ke pracheen kshetra ke naam se vyatpann hua, jiskaa shabdic arth kuruon kii bhoomi he. yah mana jaataa he ki puraanon or mahabharat main varnit kurukshetra yuddh yahan hua tha or krishna ne yuddh se pehle yuddh ke maidan main arjuna ko shrimadbhagavadgita kaa vyaakhyaan kiya tha.
 jile kaa nirmaan 1973 main us samay ke karnal jila jile se alag karke kiya gaya. baad main jile ke kuch bhaagon kaa kaithal or yamunanagar jilon ke nirmaan ke samay sthanantarit kar diya.
 kurukshetra jile main do up-sambhaagon kaa samavesh he: thanesar or pihova. thanesar up-sambhaag main main do tahsilen, thanesar or shahbaad or do up-tahsil, ladwa or babin hai. pihova up-khand main pihova tahsil or ismailabad up-tahsil shaamil hai. is jile ke mahatvapurn nagar kurukshetra, thanesar or pihova hai. punjab seemaa par sthit hone ke kaaran yahan badi sankhya main sikh aabaadi bhi he.
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar kurukshetra jile kii kul jansankhya 964,231 thi jo lagbhag fiji ganraajya ke samaan athwa sanyukt rajya america ke montana praant ke tulya he. is prakaar yah jila bharat main aabaadi main 452wein sthaan par he. jile kaa jansankhya ghanatv 630 prayek varg kilometre main nivasee he. 2001-2011 ke dashak main isaki jansankhya vruddhi dar 16.81% rahi. kurukshetra main linganupaat prati 1000 purushon par 889	mahilaayein, or sakshartaa dar 76.7% he.
 kurukshetra vishvavidhyalay kii 1956 main kurukshetra ke ekaatmak awasaiya vishvavidhyalay ke rup main sthapana hui. kurukshetra ke kshetreey abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay jise baad main iskaa naam rashtriya praudyogiki santhaan rakhaa gaya.
