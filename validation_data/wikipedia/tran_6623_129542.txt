 is shrunkhlaa kii chauthi film golmaal agen, diwaali ke avasar par 20 oktober 2017 ko pradarshit hui.
 golmaal ab bollywood main paanchavi sabase zyada kamai vaali film shrunkhlaa he.
 siaidi ​​999 shrunkhlaa, sibiai shrunkhlaa, major mahadevan shrunkhlaa or raaj shrunkhlaa ke baad yah paanchavi bhartiya film frenchise jisaki chaar filme ai he.

 lakshmana mahavidhyalay kaa ek chaatr he or hostel main rahata he jahaan uske saath teen dost - gopal, madhav, or lucky avaidh rup se rahete he. apane lender vasuuli bhai se bachane ke liye ye andhe somanath or uski andhi bivi ko jhooth bolkar unke pote samir banakar rahane lagate he. isi bich un logon kii jindagi me niraali aati he. or ve use rijhaane ke kai paitare chalate he. bubbly bhai kii bhi najar us ghar main rahati he or vah kai logo ko us ghar main bhejtaa he. is bich kai ghatnakram ke baad film naatakeey ant kii ore agrasar hota he.
 gopal, madhav, lucky, lakshmana or lakshmana anaath hai, jo uti ke seth jamanadas anathalay se paley-bade hote hai. lekin gopal or madhav ke bich ladaai ke chalate ye log anathalay chhod kar chahale jate he. kai saalon baad jamanadas kii maut ke baad ve sab waapas is anathalay main pahunchate hai. jahaan unki mulaakaat khusi or ana mathew se hoti he. vahaan unhen pataa chalata he ki vaasu reddy naam kaa builder or uskaa saathi nikhil us ashram or uske saath lage karnal chouhan ke bhukhand ko hathiyanna chaahate hai. baad main unhen pataa chalata he ki khusi kii maut ho chuki he or vah keval unheen ko dikhti he. uski hatya nikhil ne kii hoti he. or ve log anna mathew kii sahaayta se khusi ko nyaay dilaate he.
