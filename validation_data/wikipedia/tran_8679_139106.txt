 1,22,978 lekhon main khojein
 kashipur bharat ke uttrakhand rajya ke udham singh nagar janapad main sthit ek mahatvapurn puranic evam audyogic shahar he. varsh 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar is nagar kii kul jansankhya 1,21,623 he, jabaki kashipur tehsil kii kul jansankhya 2,83,136 he. is prakaar, jansankhya kii drushti se kashipur kumaun main teesraa or uttrakhand main chatha sabase bada nagar he. udham singh nagar janapad ke pashchimi bhaag main sthit yah nagar bharat kii rajdhani, nai dilli se lagbhag 240 kilometre dur uttar-purv main, or uttrakhand kii antarim rajdhani, dehradun se lagbhag 200 kilometre dakshin-purv main sthit he. 
 amer durg bharat ke rajasthan rajya kii rajdhani jaipur ke amer kshetra main ek oonchi pahari par sthit ek parvatiy durg he. yah jaipur nagar kaa pradhan paryatak aakarshan he. amer kaa kasbaa mul rup se sthaneey meenaon dwaara basaaya gaya tha, jis par kalantar main kushwaha rajput maan singh pratham ne raaj kiya va is durg kaa nirmaan karavaaya. yah durg va mahal apane kalatmak vishuddh hindu vaastu style ke ghatakon ke liye bhi janaa jaataa he. durg kii vishaal prachiron, dwaaron kii shrunkhalaaon evam pathar ke bane raaston se bharaa ye durg pahari ke thik neeche bane maawatha sarovar ko dekhtaa hua prathit hota he. laal balua pathar evam sangmarmar se nirmit yah aakarshak evam bhavy durg pahari ke chaar staron par bana hua he, jisamen se prayek main vishaal praangan hai. isamen dewan-e-aam arthath jan saadhaaran kaa praangan, dewan-e-khaas arthath vishisht praangan, sheesh mahal yaa jai mandir evam sukh nivas aadi bhaag hai.
