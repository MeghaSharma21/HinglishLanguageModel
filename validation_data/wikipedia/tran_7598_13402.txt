 maanavendranath raay bharat ke swatantraaa-sangram ke rashtravaadi krantikari tathaa vishwaprasiddh raajnneetik siddhantkaar the. unka mul naam 'narendranath bhattacharya' tha. ve mexico or bharat donon ke hi communist partiyon ke sansthaapak the. ve communist international kii congress ke pratinidhimandal main bhi sammilit the.
 manvendranath kaa janm kolkata ke nikat ek gaanv main hua tha.. aapkaa mul naam narendranath bhattacharya tha, jise baad main badalakar aapane maanavendra raay rakhaa. tatkaaleen bengal main rashtriya swatantrata aandolan kii lahar chal rahi thi, aise samay main raajnneetik bodh hona swaabhaavik tha. is prakaar praarambhik avasthaa main hi ve rashtravaadi vichaaron ke sampark main aae. raay ke jeevani lekhak ‘munshi or dikshit’ ke anusaar, ‘‘raay kaa jivan swami vivekananda, swami ramtirth or swami dayanand se prabhavit rahaa. in santon or sudhaarkon ke atirikt unke jivan par vipin chandra paal or vinayak damodar saavarkar kaa amit prabhaav padaa. shikshan ke aarambhik kaal main hi aap krantikari andolan main ruchi lene lage the. yahi kaaran he ki aap metric pariksha uteern karne ke purv hi krantikari andolan main kud pade. 
 police aapaki talash kar hi rahi thi ki aap dakshin-purvi asia kii ore nikal gaye. java sumatra se america pahunch gaye or vahaan aatankwaadi gatividhi kaa tyaag kar marxvaadi vichaardhaara ke samarthak ban gaye. unke vichaaron kii yatra kaa armbh america main marxvaadi vichaardhaara se hua kyonki us samay ve lenin ke vichaaron se prabhavit the. mexicon kii kranti main aapane aitihaasik yogadan kiya, jisase aapaki prasiddhi antarrrashtriya str par ho gai. aapke kaaryon se prabhavit hokar third international main aapako aamantrit kiya gaya tha or unhen uske adhyakshamandal main sthaan diya gaya. 1921 main ve masco ke praachya vishvavidhyalay ke adhyaksh niyukt kiye gaye. 1922 se 1928 ke bich unhone kai patron kaa sampadan kiya, jinamein 'vaanaguard' or 'masses' mukhya the. san 1927 i. main chini kranti ke samay aapako vahaan bheja gaya kintu aapke swatantra vichaaron se vahaan ke netaa sahamat na ho sake or matbhed utpann ho gaya. roosi netaa isapar aapse cruddh ho gaye or stallin ke raajnneetik coup kaa aapako shikaar bananaa padaa. videshon main aapaki hatya kaa kuchakr chalaa. germany main aapako vish dene kii cheshtaa kii gai par saubhaahgya se aap batch gaye.
 idhar desh main aapaki krantikari gatividhi ke kaaran aapaki anupasthiti main kanpur shadyantr kaa mukadmaa chalaaya gaya. british sarkaar ke guptchar aap par kadi najar rakhe hue the, phir bhi 1930 main aap gupt rup se bharat lautne main safal ho gaye. mumbai aakar aap doctor mahmud ke naam se raajnneetik gatividhi main bhaag lene lage. 1931 main aap giraftaar kar liye gaye. chah varshon tak karawas jivan bitaane par 20 navambar 1936 ko aap jail se mukt kiye gaye. congress kii nitiyon se aapkaa matbhed ho gaya tha. aapane radical democratic party kii sthapana kii thi. sakriy raajnneeti se avkaash grahan kar aap jivan ke antim dinon main dehradun main rahane lage or yaheen 25 janvari 1954 ko aapkaa nidhan hua.
 manvendra nath raay kaa aadhunik bhartiya raajnneetik chintan main vishisht sthaan he. raay kaa raajnneetik chintan lambi vaicharik yatra kaa parinaam he. ve kisi vichaardhaara se bandhe hue nahin rahe.unhone vichaaron kii bhautikwaadi aadhaar bhoomi or maanav ke astitv ke naitik prayojanon ke madhya samanvay karnaa aavashyak samajha. jahaan unhone poonjeewaadi vyavastha kii katu aalochana kii, vahinmarxvad kii aalochana main bhi peeche nahin rahe. raay ne sampoorn maanviya darshan kii khoj ke praytn ke kram main yah nishkarsh nikaala ki vishwa kii prachlit aarthik or raajnneetik pranaliyaan maanav ke samgr kalyaan ko sunishchit nahin karatin. poonjeewaad, marxvad, gandhivaad tathaa anya vichaardhaaon main unhone aise tatwon ko dhund nikaala jo kisi na kisi rup main maanav kii satta, swatantraaa, tathaa swaittata par pratibandh lagaate hai.
 raay ne apane naveen maanavvaadi darshan se maanav ko swayan apana kendra bataakar maanav kii swatantraaa evam uske vyaktitv kii garima kaa prabal samarthan kiya he. vastutah beesvin sadi main fasivadi tathaa samyavaadee samagratavaadi rajya-vyavasthaaon ne vyakti kii swatantrata evam vyaktitv kaa daman kiya or udaaravaadi loktantr main maanav-kalyaan ke naam par nirantar badhati kendrikaran kii pravriti se saavdhaan kiya. raay ne vyakti kii swatantrata evam vyaktitv kii garima ke paksh main jo ugr bauddhik vichaar diye hai, unka aadhunik yug ke liye vishisht mahatv he.
