 deraa gaazi khan nagar sindh nadi se 11 kimi0 purv disha main tathaa multan nagar se 77.5 kimi0 pashchim, dakshin-pashchim main sthit he. nagar kii sthapana ek baluchi sardaar ke putr gaji khan ne 15 veen shatabdi ke antim kaal main kii thi. nagar kaa adhikansh san 1908-09 main sindh nadi kii baadh se vah gaya tha or usee ke sameep vartmaan nagar sthapit hua. yah is kshetra kii krishigat upaj kaa vyapaarik centre he. yahan rui saaf karne tathaa khaadya saamagree taiyaar karne ke kaary or gharelu udyog mahatvapurn hai. yahan ek kala vidhyalay he. nagarikon main adhikansh baluchi musalmaan hai.
 haalanki yah pakistani punjab kaa hissaa he lekin bhugolic rup se pakistan kaa kendriya jila he. yahan se kaibar pakhtuunakva, sindh or baluchistan sate hue hai.
