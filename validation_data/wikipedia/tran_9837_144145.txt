 da pink panther kamedi filmon kii ek shrinkhalaa he jisaki shuruyaat 1963 main isi naam kii film ke relies ke saath hui, shrankhla kii filmon main ek francisi police jasoos jacks clouso ko pesh kiya gaya he jo hameshaa kuch gadabad karata rahata he. yah bhoomika peter sellers dwaara armbh kii gayi thi or unase sabase gaharaai se judi hui thi. adhiktar filmon kaa nirdesan or sah lekhan blake edwards ne henry mansini ke saath milakar kiya tha, jinhone ullekhneey sangeet kii rachana kii thi.
 is shrinkhalaa kii adhiktar filmon ke sheershakon main upyog hone ke baavjood 'pink panther' clouso kaa charitra nahin he, balki vah isi naam kaa ek bada or moolyawaan kaalpanik gulabi hira he jo shrinkhalaa kii pehali film kaa "mike gaffin" he. chauthi film da ritern of da pink panther ke sheershak main is vaakyansh kaa phir se prayog kiya gaya, jisamen kahani kaa centre heere kii chori tha or das saalon ke antar ke baad is film main phir se us bhoomika main sellers kii vaapasi hui thi, jisase charitra or heere ke bich kuch bhram paidaa hua. is shrinkhalaa main baad kii sabhi filmon main is vaakyansh kaa prayog kiya gaya he, yahaan tak ki jab kahani main heere kaa koi zikr bhi nahin tha tab bhi .
 is shrinkhalaa kii pehali film ke shuruaati anukram main ek anemation tha jise depeti-freleng enterprises ne banaaya tha or vishay sangeet henry mansini ne taiyaar kiya tha, isamen pink panther ke charitra ko darshaya tha. hole prat dwaara taiyaar kiya gaya yah charitra baad main anemated cartoon kii apni khud kii shrinkhalaa kaa vishay ban gaya tha, jise vyapak safalataa us vakt mili jab shanivaar kii subah ise da pink panther sho ke taur par pradarshit kiya gaya. a shot in da dark or inspector clouso ko chhodkar shrinkhalaa kii sabhi filmon main is charitra ko pradarshit kiya gaya.
 haalanki haal kii do pink panther filmon main steve martin ko pramukh bhoomika di gayi thi, jabaki shrinkhalaa kii adhiktar filmon main inspector clouso ke rup main peter sellers najar aate hai or inkaa nirdesan or sah-lekhan blake edwards dwaara kiya gaya tha. lokapriy jaaz-aadhaarit theme sangeet henry mansini dwaara rachaa gaya tha. prasiddh drishyon ke alaava, pehali film main vishay aksar anischittapurna anukram ke saath aata he or baad kii filmon main charitra kaa upyog kiya gaya he.
 pehali film ke liye mansini ke anya vishyon main italvi-bhashaa kii megleo stasera naamak set-pis bhi shaamil he jiskaa makasad yuva abhinetri fran jeffries ko saamne laana tha. film kii sangeet prishthbhumi main kai baar vaadya sanskaran ke kuch bhaag kaa prayog kiya gaya. anya kshetron main piano sangeet "shades of senet", ek "haunki tonk" bhi shaamil he jo rome kii sadakon par peechha karne vaale film ke charam drishy ke dauraan chalata he. soundtrack album main baaki adhikansh geet 1960 ke shuruaati dinon ke orcestra jaij ke hisse the jo us yug kii style se mail khate the. haalanki mukhya vishay kii vividhtaon ko pink panther shrinkhalaa kii kai pravishtiyon ke saath hi cartoon shrinkhalaa ke saath bhi doharaaya gaya lekin mansini ne a shot in da dark ke liye ek alag dhun taiyaar kii jise baad main anemated spin-of shrinkhalaa da inspector main istemaal kiya gaya tha.
 sheershak kaa pink panther ek hira he jisamen ek dosha he jisaki vajah se uski chhavi ek "uchhalte hue tendue" kii prathit hoti he, jise roshni main khaas tarah se pakadaney par dekha ja saktaa he. ise pehali film ke shuruyaat main samajhaayaa gaya he or camere ko heere par zum kiya jaataa he taaki uski dhundhlee kami ko dikhaaya ja sake, jo panther par kendrit kiya jaataa he taaki shuruaati prasiddh anukram ko shuru kiya ja sake. pehali film kaa kathanak is heere kii chori par aadhaarit he. is shrinkhalaa kii paanch anya filmon main heere kii vaapasi hui . "pink panther" naam inspector clouso ke saath usee tarah jud gaya jis tarah film ke sheershakon main dow frenkstin kii rachana ke sandarbh main frenkstin kaa upyog kiya gaya yaa jasusi filmon kii ek shrinkhalaa main da thin main kaa prayog kiya gaya.
 a shot in da dark, vah film he jisamen mul rup se inspector clouso ko pradarshit nahin karnaa tha or is shrinkhalaa kii do filmon main se pehali film he jiske shuruaati or aakhri credit main na to heere ko or na hi anemated pink panther ko shaamil kiya gaya. leonard maltin sahit kai aalochakon ne is film ko shrinkhalaa main sabase acchhee film mana he.
 pink panther kii mul film main puraa dhyaan kukhyat hira chor charles littan kii bhoomika nibhaa rahe david niven par kendrit tha, jiskaa upnaam "da phentom" tha or jisane apane maalik se pink panther churaane kii yojana banaai thi. inspector clouso kaa charitra litan ke aksham pratidwandi ke rup main sahaayak bhoomika nibhaataa he or film ko dohari pattee kaa hasya pradaan kar raahat detaa he anyathaa yah bhi edwards kii theth filmon kii tarah ek halki-phulki or aparaadh naatak par aadhaarit usee style kii film hoti. clouso kii lokapriyata ne unhen baad kii pink panther filmon main mukhya paatr banane kaa mauka diya jo or adhik saral dohare hasya yukt thim.

 itisi mul rup se inspector clouso televijan shrinkhalaa bananaa chaahati thi lekin blake edwards ne utpaadan company ko pehle feechar film banane or baad main film ke safal sabit hone par shrinkhalaa banane ke liye raji kar liya. film ummiido se behatar pradarshan karte hue 1975 kii sabase adhik laabh arjeet karne vaali film bani. sanyukt kalakaaron ne jaldi se itisi ke nivesh kharid liye or turant agali feechar film par kaam karnaa shuru kar diya.
 haalanki adhikarik taur par, prashansakon dwaara 1968 kii film inspector clouso ko pink panther "canan" kaa hissaa nahin mana jaataa he kyonki isamen sellers yaa edwards nahin hai. jab peter sellers ne 1975 main ritern main phir se bhoomika sweekaar kii tab orchins ke pradarshan or saajasja ke kuch tatwon ko barkaraar rakhaa gaya. atakalon ke baavjood, alan arqin trial of da pink panther main dikhaai nahin diye.
 pink panther kii duusari shrinkhalaa ko shuru karne vaali film da pink panther main inspector clouso kii bhoomika steve martin ne nibhaai, iskaa nirdesan shaun levi ne or nirmaan robert simonds ne kiya he, ise farvari 2006 main release kiya gaya tha. yah columbia pikchars dwaara release kii jane vaali pehali panther film he, jiskaa sah-nirmaan metro-goldvin-meyar ke saath milakar kiya gaya tha. yah vartmaan samay par aadhaarit he or alag mukhya paatro kaa parichay karaati he, isliye yah ek alag nirantarata se sambandhit he. isaki agali kadi da pink panther 2 main martin ne bhi abhinay kiya he, jo 2009 main pradarshit hui or vartmaan samay main vah nirmaataa robert simonds ke saath milakar da pink panther 3 banane main lage hue hai.
 jaak clouso ek kaankl anaadi pulicekarmi or jasoos he. vah bevajaah french uccharan ke saath ootpataang angreji main baat karata he, jabaki anya paatr apane uccharan main angreji bolte hai. clouso ke uccharan pehali film main kam spasht hai, lekin a shot in da dark ke baad se yah atiranjit uccharan majaak kaa hissaa ban gaya. yah suzav diya gaya ki aksham police kaa francisi ke rup main chitrit kiya janaa francisi police yaa puri francisi aabaadi ke bare main british roodhiwaadita par aadhaarit he.
 clouso kii bhoomika nibhaa rahe abhinayta peter sellers ne tippanee kii he ki unki raay main clouso jaanata tha ki vah ek vidooshak tha, lekin clouso main apana astitv banane kaa avishwasaneey kaushal he. aamtaur par keval kismat yaa uskaa bhaddaapan hi use bachaate hai. pehali film main rome ke ek fawwaare ke ird-gird haasyaaspad tareeke se kaaron kaa peechha kiye jane kii vajah se sari gaadiyaan aapas main joardaar tareeke se takaraayi, jiske parinaam swaroop chor pakade jate hai . is koshish main puri shrinkhalaa main baar-baar clouso sidhiyon se neeche ludhakte hue, pool or jharne main girte hue; aag or aapadaaon kaa kaaran banate hue or yahaan tak ki bam ke saath udate hue dikhaaye gayi hai. da pink panther strix agen main duniya bhar ke hatyaron ko clouso ko maarane ke liye bheja jaataa he, aise main vah juute kaa feeta baandhne ke liye neeche jhukta he, gir jaataa he, aadi, vah bhi thik sahi samay par jab yah sunishchit ho jaataa he ki hatyare ek duusare ko khatm kar den. trail of da pink panther main ek flashbek main ham dekhte hai ki clouso dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke samay german kabje ke khilaaf francisi pratirodh andolan main bhi ladata he, lekin phir se flash back main doharaaya jaataa he ki apni aakshamata ke kaaran clouso shaayad kisi bhi sthiti main batch saktaa he.
 inspector clouso ek deshbhakt francisi he isliye aatmasweekrut rup se uskaa desh uski sarvocch praathamikta he. jabase sar charles litan dwaara use chalaa gaya tha tab se vah moh grastata main pad jaataa he . vah baar-baar transvestite logon se is had tak uljhan main pad jaataa he ki vah unhen "mahoday yaa mahodaya" ke rup main sambodhit karata he.
 yah bhoomika mukhya rup se peter sellers dwaara nibhaai gayi he, lekin alan arqin ne bhi kii bhoomika kii he, trial of da pink panther ke flash back ke naveen sanskarnon main dainiel peakock or lakka mejzophonti ne kaam kiya he, roger moore ne or steve martin ne 2006 kii pink panther film main or baad main 2009 main isaki agali kadi main kaam kiya he.
 clouso ke varisht, charles drephus ko a shot in da dark main filmaaya gaya jisamen vah kamishnar ke pad par he. vah drephus kii gadabad se itnaa vyakul rahata he ki durghatanavash apane seene main chitthee kholane vaali churi ghonpakar khud ko ghayal kar letaa he or antat khatarnaak pagal tehraaya jaataa he. da ritern of da pink panther main drephus ko ayukta kii jagah chif inspector ke pad par dikhaaya gaya he. pehle kii tarah is film ke aakhir main bhi vah pagal ho jaataa he or ant main ek gaddidaar cell main band drephus ko apane pairon kii ungaliyon main pakade hue kreyan rang se diwaar par "clouso ko maar daalo" likhte hue dikhaaya jaataa he. a shot in da dark kii tarah shuru main drephus kai prakaar kii vyaktigat choton se ksht paata he durghatanavash apane chikitsak kii hatya kar detaa he or ek sniper ke rifl se clouso ko maarane kii koshish karata he. da pink panther strix agen main puri tarah thik hone ke baad drephus ek paagalakhaane se reeha hone vaala hota he, lekin clouso ke pahunchane ke 5 minute ke andar vah waapas anek prakaar kii choton ko sahan karata he or phir se jaanaleva paagalpan kii purvavastha main laut jaataa he. drephus paagalakhaane se bhaag nikalataa he or ek vaigyanik kaa apharan kar use ek naashak kiran banane par majaboor karata he, baad main is upkaran kaa prayog baaki duniya ko clouso kii hatya ke prayaas ke liye blackmel karne ke liye kiya jaataa he. strix agen ke ant main drephus vighatan karne ke liye prakat hota he, lekin baad main or bina kisi vivaran ke revange of da pink pantha ke shuru hone par vah phir se aspataal main paaya jaataa he or clouso ko galati se mrit ghoshit kar die jane par use phir se chif inspector ke rup main pad par vaapasi kii anumati di jaati he. harbart lom prasiddhi sahit apane paatr ko ek spasht khinchaav detaa he, jo tab ghatit hota he jab vah vishesh rup se tanaav main hota he or clouso kii hatya kii sajeesh karne ke samay bachche kii tarah kilkaari bharta he. 
 san of da pink panther main, drephus banakar clouso kii tarah hi uske masakhre bete jaik gambrelli se nipatata he. clouso ke saath apane sambandhon kii tulana main, drephus gambrelli ke prati adhik udaar he. film ke ant main, drephus clouso kii purv premika, maaria gambrali se shaadi kar letaa he. shaadi ke resepshan main, drephus ko yah jaanakar jhatka lagata he ki asal main clouso or maaria ke judwa bachche the: jaik or jacleen gambrali.
