 bahalul khan lodhi dilli ke lodhi vansh kaa pratham sultan bana, jab pichley saiyad vansh ke antim shaasha padchyut hua. bahalul ek afgaan vyapaariyon ke parivaar main paidaa hua tha. baad main yah ek prasidd yoddha hokar punjab kaa rajyapal bana. isane dilli sultanate april 19,1451 ko grahan kiya.
 bahalul khan ne vidrohiyon ko shant karne hetu bahut prayaas kiye or apane rajya kii simaaon ko gwalior, jonpur or upree uttar pradesh tak failaayaa. san 1486 main, apane putr, barbak shah ko jonpur kaa rajyapal niyukt kiya. isse kuch samay baad uttaradhikaar kii samasya khadi hui, jo ki iske duusare putr sikandar lodhi ne daawa kar khadi kii. sikandar ko uttaradhikari swayan bahalul ne hi banaaya tha. baad main bahalul kii mrityoparant vahi sultan bana san 1489 main.
