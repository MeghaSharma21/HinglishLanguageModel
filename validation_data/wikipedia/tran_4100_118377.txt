 yahan bharat main vishvavidhyalayon kii suchi di gai he. bharat main saarvajanik or nijee, donon vishvavidhyalay hai jinamein se kai bharat sarkaar or rajya sarkaar dwaara samarthit hai. inke alaava nijee vishvavidhyalay bhi maujuud hai, jo vibhinn nikaayon or samitiyon dwaara samarthit hai. sheersh dakshin asian vishvavidhyalayon ke tahat soochibaddh vishvavidhyalayon main se adhikansh bharat main sthit hai.
