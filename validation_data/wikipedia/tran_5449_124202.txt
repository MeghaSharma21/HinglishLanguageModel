 kanya kumari tamilnadu praant ke sudur dakshin tat par basaa ek shahar he. yah hind mahaasaagar, bengal kii khadi tathaa arab sagar kaa sangam sthal he, jahaan bhinn sagar apane vibhinn rango se manoram chata bikherte hai. bharat ke sabase dakshin chor par basaa kanyakumari varsho se kala, samskruti, sabhyataa kaa pratik rahaa he. bharat ke paryatak sthal ke rup main bhi is sthaan kaa apana hi mahatch he. dur-dur faile samudr ke vishaal laharon ke bich yahaan kaa suryoday or suryaast kaa najaara behad aakarshak lagata hai. samudr bich par faile rang birangi ret isaki sundarataa main chaar chand laga detaa he.
 kanyakumari dakshin bharat ke mahaan shaskaon chol, cher, pandya ke adheen rahaa he. yahaan ke smarakon par in shaskaon kii chhap spasht dikhaai detii he. is jagah kaa naam kanyakumari padane ke peeche ek puranic katha prachlit he. kaha jaataa he ki bhagwaan shiv ne asur banasuran ko vardaan diya tha ki kunvaari kanya ke alaava kisi ke haathon uskaa vadh nahin hoga. pracheen kaal main bharat par shashan karne vaale raja bharat ko aath putree or ek putr tha. bharat ne apana samrajya ko nau baraabar hisson main baantkar apni santanon ko de diya. dakshin kaa hissaa uski putree kumari ko mila. kumari ko shakti devi kaa avatar mana jaataa tha. kumari ne dakshin bharat ke is hisse par kushaltapurvak shashan kiya. uski ichha thi ki vah shiv se vivaah karen. iske liye vah unki puja karti thi. shiv vivaah ke liye raji bhi ho gaye the or vivaah kii taiyaariyaan hone lageen thi. lekin naarad muni chaahate the ki banasuran kaa kumari ke haathon vadh ho jaae. is kaaran shiv or devi kumari kaa vivaah nahin ho paaya. is bich banasuran ko jab kumari kii sundarataa ke bare main pataa chalaa to usne kumari ke samaksh shaadi kaa prastaav rakhaa. kumari ne kaha ki yadi vah use yuddh main haraa degaa to vah usase vivaah kar legi. donon ke bich yuddh hua or banasuran ko mrityu kii prapti hui. kumari kii yaad main hi dakshin bharat ke is sthaan ko kanyakumari kaha jaataa he. mana jaataa he ki shiv or kumari ke vivaah kii taiyaari kaa saamaan aage chalakar rang birangi ret main parivartit ho gaya. 
 
 sagar ke muhane ke dai or sthit yah ek chota sa mandir he jo parvati ko samarpit he. mandir teenon samudron ke sangam sthal par bana hua he. yahaan sagar kii laharon kii aawaaj swarg ke sangeet kii bhaanti sunai detii he. bhaktagan mandir main pravesh karne se pehle triveni sangam main dubkee lagaate hai jo mandir ke bai ore 500 meter kii duuri par he. mandir kaa purvi pravesh dwaar ko hameshaa band karke rakhaa jaataa he kyonki mandir main sthapit devi ke aabhushan kii roshni se samudri jahaaj ise linehouse samajhane kii bhul kar baithte he or jahaaj ko kinare karne ke chakkar main durghatnagrast ho jate he.
 
 yah smarak rashtrapita mahatma gandhi ko samarpit he. yahi par mahatma gandhi kii chita kii raakh rakhi hui he. is smarak kii sthapana 1956 main hui thi. mahatma gandhi 1937 main yahaan aae the. unki mrityu ke baad 1948 main kanyakumari main hi unki asthiyaan visarjeet kii gai thi. smarak ko is prakaar dizin kiya gaya he ki mahatma gandhi ke janm diwas par surya kii pratham kirane us sthaan par padati hai jahaan mahatma kii raakh rakhi hui he. 
 tirukkurul kii rachana karne vaale amar tamil kavi tiruvalluvar kii yah pratimaa paryatakon ko bahut lubhaati he. 38 feet oonche aadhaar par bani yah pratimaa 95 feet kii he. is pratimaa kii kul unchai 133 feet he or iskaa vajan 2000 ton he. is pratimaa ko banane main kul 1283 pathar ke tukdon kaa upyog kiya gaya tha.
 samudr main bane is sthaan par badi sankhya main paryatak aate he. is pavitra sthaan ko vivekanand rock memorial cameti ne 1970 main swami vivekanand ke prati sammaan prakat karne ke liye banwaaya tha. isi sthaan par swami vivekanand gahan dhyaan lagaaya tha. is sthaan ko shripad paraai ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. pracheen manyataon ke anusaar is sthaan par kanyakumari ne bhi tapasya kii thi. kaha jaataa he ki yahaan kumari devi ke pairon ke nishaan mudrit hai. is smarak ke vivekanand mandapam or shripad mandapam naamak do pramukh hisse hai.
 yah chota-sa gaanv kanyakumari se lagbhag 12 kimi dur sthit he. yahaan kaa thanumalaine mandir kaaphi prasiddh he. mandir main sthapit hanuman kii chah meter kii unchi murti kaaphi aakarshak he. mandir ke mukhya garbhagrih main brahma, vishnu or mahesh joki is brahmad ke rachayita samajhe jate he unki murti sthapit he. yahaan nauveen shatabdi ke pracheen abhilekh bhi paae gaye hai.
 kanyakumari se 20 kimi dur nagarkol kaa nagaraj mandir naag dev ko samarpit he. yahaan bhagwaan vishnu or shiv ke do anya mandir bhi hai. mandir kaa mukhya dwaar china kii buddha vihaar kii kaarigari kii yaad dilaataa he.
 padamanabhapuram mahal kii vishaal haweliyaan traavncor ke raja dwaara banwaaya hai. ye haweliyaan apni sundarataa or bhavyata ke liye jaani jaati hai. kanyakumari se inaki duuri 45 kimi he. yah mahal kerala sarkaar ke puraatatv vibhaag ke adheen hai.
 yah jharnaa 167 meter oonchi he. is jharne ke jal ko aushadhiy gunon se yukt mana jaataa he. yah kanyakumari se 137 kimi duuri par sthit he.
 85 kimi dur sthit tiruchendur ke khubasurat mandir bhagwaan subramanyam ko samarpit hai. bengal kii khadi ke tat par sthit is mandir ko bhagwaan subramanyam ke 6 nivaason main se ek mana jaataa he.

 kanyakumari chennai sahit bharat ke pramukh shaharon se rail maarg dwaara judaa hua he. chennai se kanyakumari express dwaara yahaan jaaya ja saktaa he.
 bas dwaara kanyakumari jane ke liye trichi, madurai, chennai, tirnantpuram or tiruchendur se niyamit bas sevaayen hai. tamilnad paryaton vibhaag kanyakumari ke liye single de bas tour kii vyavastha bhi karata he.
 samudr ke kinare hone ke kaaran kanyakumari main saal bhar manoram vaataavaran rahata he. yun to puraa saal kanyakumari jane laayak hota he lekin phir bhi paryaton ke lihaaj se oktober se march kii avadhi sabase behatar maani jaati he.
 paryatak aksar yahaan se vibhinn rangon ke ret ke packet kharidaten hai. yahaan se sip or shankh ke alaava pravaali shailabhitti ke tukdon kii khariddaari kii ja sakati he. saath hi kerala style kii nariyal jataaen or lakdi kii handicraft bhi kharidi ja sakati hai.
