 sanrachanaatmak samavyawata ) vah samavyawata he jisamen do yaa do se adhik anuon, kaa anusutra to samaan hota he kintu unke parmaanu aapas main alag-alag kram main aabandhit hote hai. sanrachanaatmak samavyawata, trivimsamavayawata se alag prakaar kii samavyawata he. 
 sanrachanaatmak samavyawata 3 prakaar kii hoti he- kankali, sthitik, tathaa kaaryaatmak samavyav .
