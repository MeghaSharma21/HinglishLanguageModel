 badan mamorial carch bharat ke uttrakhand rajya ke almoda nagar main sthit ek methodist girijaghar he. 2 march 1897 ko yah banakar taiyaar hua tha. yah girjaghar roman english style kaa bana hua he. isaki visheshta yah he ki france main srujit gothic style ke is carch kaa nirmaan puri tarah sthaneey pattharon se kiya gaya he. yah girijaghar elaar saah maarg main jagnath ke sameep malla kasoon main sthit he. yahaan se nagar ke chaaron ore kaa drishy dikhaai detaa he. carch bhawan kareeb 30 meter ooncha tathaa 40 meter lanbaa he. is girjaghar main 300 se adhik log ek saath baithkar prarthana kar sakate hai. girjaghar parisar kareeb chah naalee bhoomi main failaa hua he.
