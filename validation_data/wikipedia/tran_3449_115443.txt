 gaiyr rajya abhinayta aisi santhaayen athwa nikaay hote he jo antarrashtriya sambandhon main hissaa lekar unamem adhiniuman karte hai. ye aise sanghathan hote hai jo kisi prakirya ko prabhavit karne or usamen parivartan karne ke liye paryaapt shakti rakhte hai, haalaan ki ve kisi rajya kii sthapit sanstha se sambandh nahin rakhte hai.
