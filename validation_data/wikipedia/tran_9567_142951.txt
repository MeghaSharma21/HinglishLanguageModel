 manushyon kii anuvanshiki main matruvansh samooh bil2 yaa mitocandria-diene hapalogrup L2 ek matruvansh samooh he. is matruvansh kii upashakhaayen africa ke pashchimi, uttari ore purvi hisson main kai sthanon par ore kai samudaayon main milti hai. chaad desh ke lagbhag 38% log iske vanshaj hai, lekin yah bahut se or deshon main bhi miltaa he. anumaan he ke jis nari se yah matruvansh shuru hua vah aaj se lagbhag 68,100 se 111,100 varsh pehle jivit thi. kuch vaigyanikon kaa maananaa he ke vah purvi africa kii nivasee thi, lekin is ko lekar vaigyanikon main kaafii aapasi matbhed he.
 angrezi main "vansh samooh" ko "hapalogrup", "pitruvansh samooh" ko "waahe chromosome hapalogrup" or "matruvansh samooh" ko "amtidiene hapalogrup" kehete hai.
