 zahir khan  ek bhartiya cricket khilaadi hai. ve mukhyatah unki gendbaazi ke liye jane jate hai. daayen haath se ballebaazi or baayein haath se madhyam tej gati kii gendbaazi karte hai.
 ek baayen haath ke tej gendbaaj jahir ne donon tareeke se gend ko swing kii kshamta ke liye janaa jaataa he or ek ballebaaj ke rup main bhi sarvaadhik test ran kaa record se khul nahin 11. jaldi -2000 ke zyada ke liye bhartiya tej akraman kaa netritv karne ke baad, 2003 main choton pankh kaatna aavarti or 2004 use jabaran baahar team ke or ek varsh ke liye lautne ke baad, vah phir der se 2005 main choda gaya tha. gharelu circuit main shashakt abhinay dekha he use apane pramukh tej gendbaaj ke rup main team ko yaad kiya.
