 gitanjalishri hindi kaa jaani maani kathakaar or upanyaaskaar hai. uttar-pradesh ke mainpuri nagar main janmi gitanjali kii praarambhik shiksha uttar pradesh ke vibhinn shaharon main hui. baad main unhone dilli ke ladi shriram college se snaatak or jawaharlal nehru vishvavidhyalay se itihaas main em.e. kiya. maharaj sayaji raav vivi, vadodara se premchand or uttar bharat ke aupniveshik shikshit varg vishay par shodh kii oopaadhi praapt kii. kuch dinon tak jamiya millia islamia vivi main adhyaapan ke baad surat ke centre for soshal studies main post-dow taral research ke liye gayi. vahiin rahete hue unhone kahaniyaan likhni shuru kin. 
 unki pehali kahani belptr 1987 main hans main prakashit hui thi. iske baad unki do or kahaniyaan ek ke baad ek 'hans` main chhapin. ab tak unki 'maai`, 'hamaara shahar us baras`, 'tirohit` ; 'anugunj` or 'vairaygy` kritiyaan prakashit ho chuki hai. 'maai` kaa angreji roopantaran haal hi main prakashit hua tha, jo 'crossward award` ke liye namit antim chaar kitabon main shaamil tha. apane lekhan main vaicharik rup se spasht or praudh abhivyikti ke jariye unhone ek vishisht sthaan banaaya he.
 dilli kii hindi academy ne unhen 2000-2001 ke sahityakar sammaan se alankrita kiya he.1995 main unhen apane kahani sangrah anugoonj ke liye euke katha sammaan se sammanit kiya gaya.
