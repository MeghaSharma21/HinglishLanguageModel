 anuprayukt nitishastra nijee or saarvajanik jivan ke vishisht muddon kaa, naitik drushtikon se, darshanik parikshan hai, jo naitik jajment ke maamle hai. atah, ye, rosemara ke jivan main alag-alag kshetron main naitik rup se sahi kaary-maarg pahchaanane hetu darshanik paddatiyon kaa upyog karne ke prayaas hai. udaahranarth, jeev vigyaan - jaise ki yuthnesia, durlabh swasthya sansadhano kaa aavanton, athwa sanshodhan main maanvi bhrun kaa istemaal - in main vidhik muddon ke liye sahi drushtikon ko pahchaanane se jaivnitishastra samudaay kii chintaa hai.
