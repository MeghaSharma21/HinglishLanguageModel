 "aniatik" shabd aam taur par vyaktiyon yaa kaaryon kaa varnan karne ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he. ek vyapak arth main yah samooh yaa korporate nikaayon, vishwaason, dharmon, or kala ke kaaryon ke liye lagu kiya ja saktaa he. kuch krity aniatik he ki kehane ke liye hai ki kuch naitik kanoonon, mandandon yaa maanakon kaa ulanghan karti he kehane ke liye hai.
 kaayarta or aturata saahas se sambandhit hai arestu, kuch punya ke sambandh main jyadatiyon yaa ghate ke rup main kai dosha dekha. kuch vyavhaar or karrwai - jaise irshyaa, hatya, or chori - vah ]. arestu, aachaar  pi. 102
