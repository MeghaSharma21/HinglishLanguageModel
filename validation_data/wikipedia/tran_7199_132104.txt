 nirdeshaank: 30°02′51″N 31°14′18″E / 30.04750°N 31.23833°E / 30.04750; 31.23833kahiraa nagar kshetra yaa downtaun cairo kahiraa, misr main sthit he. yah shahar kaa mukhya vyapaarik kshetra va zila he.
 misr ke shaasha ismil pasha ne ranneetik sooyeeboojh ke saath jazira dweep ke saamne nil nadi ke purvi kinaaron ko dobaaraa paane or nil nadi par paris jaisaa shahar banane kaa faisla liya. nadi kaa nichalaa kshetra jo uske talabon, daldal, geeli mitti or lakhon logon ke nivas vaale prakritik jaldhara ke tateey kshetra ne ise bahut hi ummiido vaali faldaayi naagarik abhiyantriki pariyojna bana diya. 1874 main jab kaaphi had tak dhaanchaagat nirmaan puraa ho gaya tab ismil pasha ne bhawanon ke rakh rakhaav, saaj sajja va nirmaan aadi ke liye bahut bady raashi kharch karne kii shart rakhi jisase yahan sirf vishisht, dhanwaan or prabhaavshaalee vyakti hi rah sakate the. isaki vajah se shruwati varshon main isane bahut saare dhanadhyon ko aakarshit kiya. isaki vajah se yah kshetra bahut mahatvapurn hua, iskaa or aasapaas ke kshetron kaa teji se vikaas hua or yahan vyavsaayik gatividhiyaan batch gai. yahan ke margon or uske nae bhawanon kii banaavat ek nae antarrashtriya str kaa shahari janapad banane kii kavayad kaa hissaa the jiskaa uddesy tamaam nai videshi upakramon ko misr kii samrudh aitihaasik islamic dharohar se jodna tha.
 nagar ke sabase prasiddh pratik chinhon main 29 talaat herb maarg sthit 1908 main nirmit cafe richi restraan he. pichley ek shatabdi main yahan misr kii bahut sari mahatvapurn ghatnaayen huin. jaise yaheen par misr ke raja faruk ne apni duusari patni nariman sadek ko dekha tha, 1919 main misr ke pradhanmantri usoof vahabaa ko maarane kaa prayaas karne vaale aparaadhiyon ne unka yaheen par intajaar kiya tha, or yaheen par 1919 kii kranti ke asankhya sipahsalaar iske tahkhaanon main milte the, yojanaayen banaate the or andolan se judy parchiyaan chhapate the.baad main cafe richi budhimaan logon ke liye milane julne va vaartalap kii jagah bana. 
 talaat herb maarg par sthit aais kriim kii yah dukaan shahar kii behad prasiddh va puraani dukaanon main se he. iske maalik italy ke prasiddh groppi parivaar se taalluk rakhte hai.
