 bankimachandra chattopadhyaay  bengali ke prakhyat upanyaaskaar, kavi, gadhykar or patrkaar the. bharat ke rashtriya geet 'vande maataram' unki hi rachana he jo bhartiya swatantraaa sangram ke kaal main krantikariyon kaa preranarot ban gaya tha. rabindranath thakur ke purvavarti bangla sahityakaron main unka anyatam sthaan he. 
 aadhunik yug main bangala sahitya kaa utthan unnisvin sadi ke madhya se shuru hua. isamen raja rammohan raay, ishwar chandra vidyasagar, pyarichand mitra, maaikel madhusudan dutt, bankim chandra chattopadhyaay, rabindranath thakur ne agrani bhoomika nibhaai. iske pehle bengal ke sahityakar bangala kii jagah sanskrit yaa angreji main likhnaa pasand karte the. bangala sahitya main janamanas tak paith banane waalon me shaayad bankim chandra chattopadhyaay pehle sahityakar the.
 bankim chandra chattopadhyaay kaa janm uttari choubees pargana ke kanthalpada, naihati main ek paramparaagat or samrudh bengali parivaar main hua tha. unki shiksha hughali college or presidency college, kolkata main hui. 1857 main unhone bie paas kiya or 1869 main kaanoon kii degree hasil kii. iske baad unhone sarkari naukari kar lee or 1891 main sarkari seva se ritair hue. unka nidhan 8 april 1894 main hua. presidency kalege se bi. e. kii oopaadhi lenevaale ye pehle bhartiya the. shikshasmapti ke turant baad dipti magistrate pad par inaki niyukti ho gai. kuch kaal tak bengal sarkaar ke sachiv pad par bhi rahe. rhibahadur or si. ai. i. kii oopaadhiyaan pain.
 bankimchandra chatarji kii pahchaan bangla kavi, upanyaaskaar, lekhak or patrkaar ke rup main he. unki pratham prakashit rachana rajmohans wif thi. isaki rachana angreji main kii gai thi. unki pehali prakashit bangla kriti 'durgeshnandini' march 1865 main chhapi thi. yah ek romani rachana he. unki agali rachana kaa naam kapalkundla he. ise unki sabase adhik romani rachanaaon main se ek mana jaataa he. unhone 1872 main masik patrika bangdarshan kaa bhi prakaashan kiya. apni is patrika main unhone vishavriksh upanyaas kaa kramik rup se prakaashan kiya. krishnakanter vil main chatarji ne angreji shaskaon par tikhaa vyangy kiya he.
 aanandamath raajnneetik upanyaas he. is upanyaas main uttar bengal main 1773 ke sanyasi vidroh kaa varnan kiya gaya he. is pustak main deshbhakti kii bhaavana he. chatarji kaa antim upanyaas seetaram he. isamen muslim satta ke prati ek hindu shaasha kaa virodh darshaya gaya he. 
 unke anya upanyaason main durgeshnandini, mrinalini, indira, radharani, krishnakanter daftar, devi choudharani or mochiram gaurer jeevancharit shaamil he. unki kavitaayen lalita o maanas naamak sangrah main prakashit hui. unhone dharm, saamaajik or samasamayik muddon par aadhaarit kai niabandh bhi likhe.
 bankimchandra ke upanyaason kaa bharat kii lagbhag sabhi bhashaaon main anuwaad kiya gaya. bangla main sirf bankim or sharathchandra chattopadhyaay ko yah gaurav hasil he ki unki rachanaaen hindi sahit sabhi bhartiya bhashaaon main aaj bhi chaav se padhi jaati he. lokapriyata ke maamle main bankim or sharad or rabindra nath thakur se bhi aage hai. bankim bahumukhi pratibha vaale rachanaakaar the. unke katha sahitya ke adhiktar paatr shahari madhyam varg ke log hai. inke paatr aadhunik jivan kii traasadiyon or pracheen kaal kii paramparaon se judi dikkaton se saath saath jujhate hai. yah samasya bharat bhar ke kisi bhi praant ke shahari madhyam varg ke samaksh aati he. lihaaja madhyam varg kaa pathak bankim ke upanyaason main apni chhavi dekhtaa he.
