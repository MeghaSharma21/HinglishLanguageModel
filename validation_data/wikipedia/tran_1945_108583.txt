 june 2014 main centre sarkaar ne aviation companiyon ko chandigarh airport se international flits kii anumati pradaan kar di he. 11 sitambar 2015 ko is orport kaa sanchaalan ek antarrashtriya orport ke rup main armbh hua. iskaa udghaatan bharat ke pradhanmantri narendra modi ne kiya.
