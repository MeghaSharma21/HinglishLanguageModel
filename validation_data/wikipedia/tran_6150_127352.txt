 shandilya naam gotrasuchee main he, at: puranadi main shandilya naam se jo kathaayen milti hai, ve sab ek vyakti kii nahin ho saktin. chhandogya or brihadaaranyak upanishad main shandilya kaa prasang he. pancharaatr kii parampara main shandilya acharya pramanik purush maane jate hai. shandilyasanhita prachlit he; shandilya bhakthisutra bhi prachlit he. isi prakaar shandilyopanishad naam kaa ek granth bhi he, jo bahut pracheen gyaat nahin hota.
 yudhisthir kii sabha main vidyaman rishiyon main shandilya kaa naam he. raja sumantu ne inako prachur daan diya tha, yah anushaasan parv se janaa jaataa he. anushaasan 65.19 se janaa jaataa he ki isi rishi ne baiylgaadee ke daan ko shreshth daan kaha tha.
 shandilya naamak acharya anya shastron main bhi smrit hue hai. hemaadri ke lakshanprakash main shandilya ko ayurvedacharya kaha gaya he. vibhinn vyaakhyaan granthon se pataa chalata he ki inke naam se ek grihyasutra evam ek smritigranth bhi tha.
