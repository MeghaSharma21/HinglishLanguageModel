 kai bhashaaon main bahuvachan, vyakaranik shrenee kii sankhya ke mulyon main se ek he. sangyaaon kaa bahuvachan aamtaur par sangya dwaara pratinidhitiv kii gai difolt maatra jo aam taur par ek hota he, ke alaava maatra ko ingit karata he. aamtaur par, isliye, bahuvachan kaa upyog do yaa do se adhik chizon ko darshane ke liye kiya jaataa he. udhaaharan swaroop hindi main bahuvachan gaadiyaan jiske anuroop ekvachan gaadi he.
