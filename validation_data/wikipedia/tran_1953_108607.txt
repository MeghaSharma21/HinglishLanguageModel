 muchkund ikshvaku naresh mandhata ke putr the. inhone apane baahubal kii pariksha ke liye alakaapati kuber par akraman kiya tha. puranic kathaon ke anusaar daityon se bahut samay tak inhone devataaon kii raksha kii thi. nivrutt hone par inhone nidra kaa tathaa nidra se jagaanevaale vyakti ko bhasma hone kaa vardaan maanga tha. shrikrishna kaa peechha karte samay kaalyavan inhen jagaakar bhasma ho gaya tha.
