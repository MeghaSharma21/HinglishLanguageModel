 baudhon kaa sanskrit bhashaa main charitapradhan sahitya avdaan sahitya kehalaata he. "avdaan" kaa amarkosh ke anusaar arth he - pracheen charit, puraatan vrutt . "avdaan" se taatpary un pracheen kathaon se he jinke dwaara kisi vyakti kii gungarima tathaa shlaghneey charitra kaa parichay miltaa he. kalidas ne isi arth main "avdaan" shabd kaa prayog kiya he . buddha sahitya main isi arth main "jataka" shabd bhi bahush: prachlit he, parantu avdaan jataka se katipay vishyon main bhinn he. "jataka" bhagwaan buddha kii purvajanm kii kathaon se sarvatha sambhddha hote hai jinamein buddha hi purvajanm main pradhan paatr ke rup main chitrit kiye gaye rahete hai. "avdaan" main yah baat nahin pai jaati. avdaan praaia: buddhopas vyaktivishesh adarsh charit hota he. baudhon ne jansadharan main apane dharm ke tatwon ke prachaar ke nimitt subodh sanskrit gadhy-padya main is sundar sahitya kii rachana kii he.
 i "avdaanshtak" he jo das virgon main vibhakt he tathaa prayek varg main das-das kathaayen hai. in kathaon kaa rup theravaadi he. mahayan dharm ke vishisht lakshanon kaa yahan vishesh abhaav drushtigochar hota he. yahan bodhisattva sampradaay kii baaten bahut kam hai. buddha kii upasna par aagrah karnaa hi in kathaon kaa uddeshy he. in kathaon kaa vargeekaran ek siddhant ke aadhaar par kiya gaya he. pratham varg kii kathaon main buddha kii upasna karne se vibhinn dasha ke manushyon ke jivan main chamatkaar utpann hota he tathaa ve agle janm main buddhatv pate vivaran. avdaanshtak kaa chini bhashaa main anuwaad tritiy shatabdi ke purvardh main hua tha. falat: iskaa samay dwitiya shatabdi mana jaataa he.
 mahayani siddhanton par ashrith kathankon kaa rochak varnan is lokapriy granth kaa pradhan uddeshy he. iskaa 34vaan prakaran "mahayanasutra" ke naam se abhihit kiya gaya he. yah ullekh granth ke moulik siddhanton kii disha pradarshit karne main upyogi mana ja saktaa he. divyavdaan, avdaanshtak ke kathanak tathaa kavyastylee se visheshat: prabhavit hua he. isaki aadhi kathaayen vinayapitak se or baaki sutralankar se sangruhit kii gai hai. samgr granth kaa to nahin, parantu katipay kathaon kaa anuwaad chini bhashaa main tritiy shatak main kiya gaya tha. shunga vansh ke raja pushyamitra tak kaa ullekh yahan uplabdh hota he. falat: iske katipay anshon kaa rachanakaal dwitiya shatabdi maananaa uchit hoga, parantu samgr granth kaa bhi nirmaankaal tritiy shatabdi ke baad nahin he.
 divyavdaan ke hi katipay avdaan maharaj priyadarshi ashok se sambhddha hone ke karna "ashokadaan" ke naam se pukaare jate hai. in kathaon kaa, jo aitihaasik drushti se nitant mahatvapurn hai, centrebindu priyadarshi ashok hi hai jinke vyaktigat gharelu jivan, dharmik nishtha tathaa dharmprachaar ke adamya utsaah kii jaankaree ke liye ye kathaayen abhipret hai. is avdaan main do kathaayen apni rochakata ke kaaran vishesh mahatv rakhti hai. ashok ke putr kunal kii karun katha baudhdhyug kii romaanchak kathaon main badi prakhyat he. buddha kaa rup dhaaran kar maar kaa acharya upagupt se shiksha ke liye prarthana karnaa bhi bada hi rochak aakhyaan he, naatak ke samaan hridayavarjak he.
 kalantar main avdaanshtak kii kathaon kaa hi shlokabaddh sankshipt rup anek granthon main miltaa he. "avdaanshtak" ke upar ashrith granthon main kalpadrumavadanamala pracheenatam prathit hot he. isaki pratham tathaa avdaanshtak kii antim katha ek hi he. acharya upagupt ne in kathaon ko ashok ke upadesh ke liye kaha he. yahan avdaanshtak ke prayek varg kii pratham tathaa dwitiya kathaon kaa hi shabdaantar se varnan he. ratnavadaanamala main isi prakaar prayek varg kii teesri or chauthi kathaon kaa sankshep he. ashokadaanamala, dwavinshatyavadaan, bhadrakalpavdaan, vratavdanamala, vicitrakarnikavdaan tathaa sumogadhavdaan is sahitya ke anya granth hai. kashmiri kavi kshemendra rachit tathaa unke putr somendra dwaara sampoorit avadaanakalpalata is sahitya kaa sachmuch ek bahumoolya ratna he jisaki abha tibeti anuwaad main bhi kisi prakaar feeki nahin hone pai he.
