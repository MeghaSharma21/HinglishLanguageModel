 prithvi shaw ek bhartiya cricket khilaadi or bhartiya under-19 cricket team ke captain hai jo mumbai main madhya aay samooh cricket club ke liye khelte hai or rizvi springfield hai skool ke captain bhi hai. november 2013 main unhone 1901 ke baad kisi bhi sangathit rup se kisi bhi ballebaaj ke ore se elite divijan match main 546 ran banaaye the, lekin baad main inkaa record pranav dhanavaade ne tod diya hai. inhone apni captaini main bhartiya under-19 cricket team ko 2017-18 under-19 cricket vishwa cap jitaaya he. prithvi shaw ko westeindies ke khilaaf do test maichon kii siries main mauka diya gaya or pehle hi match kii pehali pari main 99 gendon par apana shatak puraa kiya or bhartiya team ke 15wein aise ballebaaj bane jinhone padaarpan test main shatak banaaya. inhone 134 ranon kii pari kheli or main of da match kaa puraskaar bhi diya gaya.
 ye ek daaen haath ke ballebaaj or daaen haath ke of spin gendbaaj hai, jinaki kshamta ek olerounder kii bhi maan sakate hai, is prakaar inaki sachin tendulkar ke saath lagaataar tulana kii he. shaw ko ab purv rup se antarrashtriya cricket ke liye puraa darja diya gaya he. 
 shaw ne esji ke saath 36 lakh rupee kaa sauda arjeet kiya he, jo ki sunil gavaskar, rahul dravid or virendra sahvaag jaise diggajon ne bhi samarthan kiya he. inaki captaini main bhartiya under-19 cricket team ne 2018 under-19 cricket vishwa cap jeeta he or saath hi ye sabase kam umr ke vishwa cap jitaane vaale captain bhi ban gaye he.
 gharelu cricket main inhone pratham shrenee cricket main mumbai ke liye inhone pehali baar koi match saal 2017 main 1 se 5 janvari ko rajkot main khelaa tha jisamen inke saamne tamilnadu kii team thi. is mukaable main unhone pehali pari main 4 ran banaaye to duusari pari main 120 ranon kii pari kheli thi.
 shaw ne list e cricket kii shuruyaat bhi usee saal vijay hajaare trophy main 25 farvari ko gujarat ke khilaaf kii thi lekin pehle match main inhone mahaj 10 ran hi banaae the.
 inhone apane list e cricket main lestershire ke khilaaf match main acchhee ballebaaji karte hue apana pehla shatak banaaya. is match main bharat 'e' team ne jahaan 458 ran banaaye to prithvi shaw ne 132 ranon kii pari kheli.
 prithvi shaw ko 2018 indian premier lig kii nilaamee main dilli dairedevills frenchiji ne 1 karod 20 lakh main kharida tha or inhone apane duusare hi match main sanyukt rup se sabase kam 18 saal 169 dinon kii aayu main ipel main ardhashatak banaaya he. isse pehle itani hi aayu main sanju samson ne ardhashatak banaaya tha.
