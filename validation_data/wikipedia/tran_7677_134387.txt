 
 kutu ek prakaar kaa paudha he jisaki bahut si naslein hai, jinamein se zyaadaatar junglee hai. iske beej ko piskar ek aataa banaaya jaataa he jiske bhartiya or anya kshetron ke khaanon main kai istemaal hai. misaal ke taur par avadhi khaane main kutu parantha banaaya jaataa he. haalaanki zyaadaatar aate anaajon ke banate hai or anaaj adhiktar ghaas ke parivaar ke hi paudhe hote hai, kutu naa to asli anaaj he or naa hi vanaspati parivaar main ghaas parivaar kaa sadsy he.
 uttar bharat main navaratri main hindu anuyaayi aksar kutu ke aate kii bani chizein khate hai, jaise kii kutu kii puri or kutu ke pakora. punjab main kutu ko 'okhla' bolaa jaataa he or iske aate kaa kaafii istemaal kiya jaataa he.
 kutu ko angrezi main bakawheat bolte hai. punjabi main ise 'okhla' bolte hai.
