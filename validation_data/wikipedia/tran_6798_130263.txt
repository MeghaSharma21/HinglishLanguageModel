 rajendra pratap singh moti singh ke naam se lokapriy, uttar pradesh se bhartiya janata party ke vidhayak hai or vartmaan main yogi adityanath kii uttar pradesh sarkaar main cabinet mantri hai.aap chaar baar se pratapgarh jile ke pattee vidhansabha kaa pratinidhitiv karte aa rahe hai. 
 inkaa janm 20 oktober 1954 ko pratapgarh jile main hua. inke pita kaa naam bharat singh gandhi or maa kaa naam kaushalya devi tha. inhone elahabaad vishvavidhyalay se vigyaan vishyon main snaatak kii degree or elahabaad vishvavidhyalay se kaanoon kii shiksha grahan kii. ve peshey se vyapaaree hai.

 bhaajapa se lagaataar teen baar vidhayak rah chuke moti singh, bhaajapa kii tikat par 1996 main upi ke pattee vidhansabha se chunaav jitkar pehali baar vidhayak bane the. phir bhaajapa ke tikat par ve 2002 main pun: isi kshetra se vidhayak bane. 2003 main ve upi kii bhaajapa sarkaar main krishi rajya mantri rahe. 2007 ke vidhansabha chunaav main bhaajapa ke tikat par chunaav jitkar ve teesri baar pun: vidhayak bane. saal 2012 ke vidhansabha chunaav main jeet ke baad ve haar gaye the. 2012 chunaav main haarane ke baad ve hoicourt pahunch gaye the. ullekhneey he ki 2012 ke pratapgarh kii pattee vidhansabha ke chunaav main samaajawaadee party ke ram singh ne bhaajapa ke moti singh ko shikast di thi. ram singh ko 61434 vot mili the. unhone moti singh ko 156 vot se haraaya tha. moti singh ko 61278 vot mili the. is parinaam ko moti singh ne chunauti di thi. elahabaad hoicourt kii lakhanu bench ne pratapgarh main samaajawaadee party ke vidhayak kii vidhansabha sadsyta ko rad kar diya tha. iske baad hoicourt ke faisle main ve pun: vidhayak ho gaye the, lekin is faisle ke khilaaf sapa ke vidhayak ramsingh supreme court chale gaye.
 varsh 2017 ke vidhansabha chunaav parinaam phir se unke paksh main rahaa. unhen kul 74428 mat mili or unke nikatatam pratidwandi sapa ke ram singh ko kul 73051 mat mili. varsh-2017 ke yogi adityanath kii sarkaar main unhone cabinet mantri kii shapath lee.
