 bharat main chini patrakarita do samachaarpatron par aadhaarit he. ye donon samachaarpatr kolkata shahar se nikalate hai or unhen is shahar kii chini bhashaai alpsankhyak padhate he. in akhabaaron ke naam jaise ki angreji main bataae jate hai, di overseas chaayineez commers of india or di chaayineez jarnal hai. in akhabaaron ke prakaashan main aadhunik takanic ke bajaay puraane taur-tareekon kaa prayog jaataa he. visheshkar sulekh or chayai main puraane tareeke prachlit hai. unke alaava samachar bhi naveenatam ke bajaay kam se kam ek din pehle kii prastut kii jaati he. in akhabaaron ke kharidaaron kii sankhya 1,200 se adhik nahin he. inamen adhikansh kharidaar kolkata main hi hai. lekin akhabaaron kii kuch pratiyaan mumbai or chennai pahunchaai jaati hai.
 yah akhbaar 1935 se chap rahaa he.
 yah akhbaar tulanaatmak rup se aadhunik he. iskaa prakaashan 1978 se jari he.
