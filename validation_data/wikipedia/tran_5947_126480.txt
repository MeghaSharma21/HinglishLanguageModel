 
 
 diavi, "dayanand anglo vaidic" kaa sankshipt rup he. diavi nijee kshetra main bharat kaa sabase badi shikshan santhaan he. diavi vaidic aadhyatmik upadeshta swami dayanand saraswati ke vichaaron par aadhaarit he. diavi schuli shiksha se lekar vishvavidhyalay str tak kii shiksha detaa he.
 shaayad yah sansar main kuch hi shikshan sansthaanon main he jahaan sanchaalkon main kisi tarah ke ahan kaa takaraav nahin he. pichley oktober man unhone jalandhar main diavi vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana kii. apane 125 varsh ke itihaas main iske 715 santhaan hai jo ki prabandh samiti ke adhyaksh shripunmasuri ke netritv main kaary kar rahe hai. nihsandeh isaayiyon ne kisi bhi duusare samudaay se zyada skool or kalege sthpit kiye hai lekin unamem se zyada videshi mishnariyon kii koshishon ke kaaran he. bhartiya isaayiyon ne is disha main bahut kam kaam kiya hai.
 diavi kii sthapana lala hansraj ne san 1886 main lahore main kii thi. diavi ke antargat aaj 667 shikshan sansthaaen hai, jinamein 461 hiskool hai. jisaki vaarshik bajet kareeb 2 arab he. in skulon kaa sanchaalan dayanand anglo-vaidic college trust or prabandhan socity ke dwaara kiya jaataa he. diavi desh main sabase zyada sankhya main sibiesai board ko chaatr detaa he.
