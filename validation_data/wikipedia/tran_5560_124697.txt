 chatar manzil lakhanu kaa ek aitihaasik bhawan he. iske nirmaan kaa prarambh nawab ghaziuddin haidar ne kiya or unki mrityu ke baad unke uttaradhikari nawab nasiruddin haidar ne isko puraa karavaaya. is dumanzili imaarat kaa mukhya kaksh dumanzili oonchaai kaa he or uske upar ek vishaal sunahari chhatari he jo dur se dekhi ja sakati he. is chhatari ke kaaran hi is bhawan kaa naam chatar manzil padaa he. 
 aajkal isamen kendriya aushadhi anusandhan santhaan kaa karyaalay he.
