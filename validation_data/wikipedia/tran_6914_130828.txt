 shalab ek kit ko kehete he or shalab tidde ko bhi. is asana main sharir kii akruti kuch isi tarah kii ho jaati he isiilie ise shalbhasana kehete he. ek pair ko upar uthaane se is asana ko ardh-shalbhasana kehete he.
 is asana kii ginati bhi pet ke bal letkar kiye jane vaale asana main kii jaati he. pet ke bal letkar sabase pehle thodi ko bhoomi par tikas. phir donon haathon ko janghaaon ke neeche dabaaen. tab shwaas andar lekar ek pair ko upar uthaayen. pair ko or upar uthaane ke liye haathon kii hatheliyon se janghaaon ko dabaaen.waapas aane ke liye dhire-dheer pair ko bhoomi par le aae. phir haathon ko janghaaon ke neeche se nikaalte hue makrasana kii sthiti main let jaaen.
 ghutane se pair nahin mudana chahie. thodi bhoomi par tiki rahe. 10 se 30 second tak is sthiti main rahen. jinhen merudand, pairon yaa janghaaon main koi gambhir samasya ho vah yoga chikitsak se salaah lekar hi yah asana karen.
 merudand ke neeche vaale bhaag main hone vaale sabhi rogo ko dur karata he. qamar dard evam ciatic dard ke liye vishesh laabhprad he.
