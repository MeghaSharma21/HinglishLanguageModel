 coin oprated babies un shishuon ko kaha jaataa he jinhen saarvajanik lokaron main chhod diya jaataa he, jo zyaada-tar japan or china main hua karata he. iske peeche ek dhaaran yah he ki aise lokaron ko vahaan ke sevak/ padaadhikari khate rahete hai or un bacchon ko jald hi dhund liya jayega; parantu aisa na hone ke kaaran aise kai bachche mrit ho chuke hai. 1980 se 1990 ke bich 191 aise maamle saamne aae hai jinamein ye bachche murda paae gaye, jo is arse kii kal bal hatya ke maamalon kii 6% taadaad he. haal ke kuch saalon main osaka kii sarkaar ne ek gut niyukt kiya he jiskaa lakshya shoshit or andekhaapan ke shikaar bacchon ko khoj nikaalnaa or unki suraksha kaa prabandh karnaa. 1993 main in logon ke liye ek nirdeshika prakashit kii jisamen bal shoshan se jojane ke bare main likhaa tha. isamen samasya yah he ki japani prashasan ab bhi shoshan se sambandhit puraane niyamon kaa istimaal karti he. aadhunik niyamon kii tayyari ke prayaas bhi jari hai.
