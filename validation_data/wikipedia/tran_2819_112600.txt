 asif ali zaradaari ek pakistani raajnneetijna or pakistan pipuls party ke sah adhyaksh hai. ve 2008 se 2013 tak pakistan ke 11wein raashtrapati ke rup main padasth rahe. wo pakistan ke pratham loktantrik rup se nirvaachit raashtrapati hai jinhone apana paanch varsh kaa kaarykaal purna karke anya nirvaachit rup se chune hue raashtrapati ko kaarybhaar saunpa.. ve sindhi hai jinka mul rup se baloch jatiyata ke ek jameendaar janajati se hai. 1987 main benazir bhutto se shaadi ke baad unhen bahut shoharat mili or wo pakistan ke pratham purush banane kaa saubhaahgya praapt kar sake. 1993 se 1996 ke madhya duusare bhutto sarkaar ke kaarykaal main sangheey nivesh mantri, khufia beuro ke mahanideshak or sangheey jaanch agency ke pramukh sahit unhone vibhinn cabinet padon kaa kaarybhaar sambhaala.
 unhen 1996 ke ant main bhutto sarkaar ke girane ke baad bhrashtachar ke aaropon main giraftaar kiya gaya.
