 uttrakhand rajya ke kumaun kshetra main holi kaa tyohaar ek alag tarah se manaaya jaataa he, jise kumaunni holi kehete hai. kumaunni holi kaa apana aitihaasik or saanskrithik mahatva he. yah kumaunni logon ke liye sabase mahatvapurn tyohaaron main se ek he, kyonki yah keval buraai par achchhai kii jeet kaa hi nahin, balki pahari sardiyon ke ant kaa or nae buaai ke mausam kii shuruyaat kaa bhi pratik he, jo is uttar bhartiya krishi samudaay ke liye bahut mahatv rakhata he. 
 holi kaa tyohaar kumaun main basant panchami ke din shuru ho jaataa he. kumaunni holi ke teen praaroop hai; baithki holi, khadi holi or mahila holi. is holi main sirf abir-gulaal kaa tikaa hi nahin hota, varan baithki holi or khadi holi gayan kii shastreey parampara bhi shaamil hoti he. basant panchami ke din se hi holyar prayek shaam ghar-ghar jaakar holi gaate hai, or yah utsav lagbhag 2 maheenon tak chalata he.
 kumaunni holi kii utpatti, vishesh rup se baithki holi kii sangeet parampara kii shuruyaat to 15veen shatabdi main champavat ke chand raajaaon ke mahal main, or iske aas-paas sthit kaali-kumaun, sui or gumdesh kshetron main maani jaati he. baad main chand rajvansh ke prasar ke saath hi yah sampoorn kumaun kshetra tak faili. saanskrithik nagari almoda main to is tyohaar par holi gane ke liye dur dur se gayak aate the.
 baithki holi parmparik rup se kumaun ke bade nagaron main hi manaai jaati rahi he. baithki holi basant panchami ke din se shuru ho jaati he, or is main holi par aadhaarit geet ghar kii baithak main raag raganiyon ke saath hormonium or table par gaae jate hai. in geeton main mirabai se lekar nazir or bahadur shah zafar kii rachanaayen sunne ko milti hai. ye baithken aashirwad ke saath sampurna hoti hai jisamen mubarak ho manjari phulon bhari... yaa aisi holi khele janaab ali...jaisi thumariyaan gaai jaati hai. kumaun ke prasidd janakavi girish girda ne baithki holi ke saamaajik shastreey sandarbhon ke bare main gaharaai se adhyayan kiya he, or is par islamic samskruti or urdu kaa asar bhi mana he.
 khadi holi baithki holi ke kuch dinon baad shuru hoti he. iskaa prasar kumaun ke gramin kshetron main adhik dekhne ko miltaa he. khadi holi main gaanv ke log nukili topi, kurtaa or chudeedaar pyjama pahan kar ek jagah ekatrit hokar holi geet gaate hai, or saath saath hi dhol-damau tathaa hudke kii dhunon par naachte bhi hai. khadi holi ke geet, baithki ke mukaable shastreey geeton par kam hi aadhaarit hote hai, tathaa purnatah kumaunni bhashaa main hote hai. holi gane vaale log, jinhen holyar kehete hai, baari baari gaanv ke prayek vyakti ke ghar jaakar holi gaate hai, or uski samruddhi kii kaamana karte hai.
 mahila holi main prayek shaam baithki holi jaisi hi baithken lagati hai, parantu inamen keval mahilaayein hi bhaag leti hai. iske geet bhi pramukhtah mahilaon par hi aadhaarit hote hai.
 holikaa dahan ke liye kumaun main chardi se 15 din pehle hi chid kii lakadiyon se holikaa kaa nirmaan kiya jaataa he, jise chid bandhan kehete hai. prayek gaanv apane apane chid kii suraksha main lag jate hai, kyonki pratidwandvi gaanv ke log dusron kii chid churaane kii koshish karte hai. holi se ek raat pehle chid ko jalaayaa jaataa he, jise chid dahan kaha jaataa he. chid dahan prahlaad kii hiranyakashiphu ke vichaaron par jeet kaa pratik mana jaataa he.
 chardi ke din log ek duusare par rang, abir-gulaal ityadi phenkate hai. aitihaasik taur par is kshetra main charad se holi manaai jaati thi, jis kaaran ise chardi kaha jaataa tha. charad banane ke liye tesu ke phulon ko dhoop main sukhaakar pani main ghola jaataa tha, jisase naarangi-laal rang kaa ghol banataa tha, jo charad kehalaata tha.
