 gumaan mishra sanskrit or hindi bhashaa tathaa sahityashastra ke pandit the.
 ye sandi ke nivasee the or san0 1806 vi0 main vartmaan the. apana parichay dete hue kavi ne swayan likhaa he ki vah mishra brahman or sabasukh mishra kaa shishy he. kuch samay tak ye dilli main mohammadshah samrat ke yahan raja jugulkishor bhatta ke paas rahe. phir pihaani ke muhmadi mahraaj akbar ali khan ke yahan gaye the. unheen kii prerana se inhone harshkrit sanskrit granth 'naishadh' ko 'kavyakalanidhi' naam se hindi main bhashantarit kiya. iskaa bhashaantaran kaal san0 1805 vi0 he. is anuwaad kaa prakaashan shri venkateshwar press se ho gaya he jo kaaphi ashuddh he. khoj reporto main iske atirikt inaki do or kritiyaan kahi gai hai- alankaar darpan or gulaal chandroday. inamen pratham kaa nirmaankaal san0 1818 or duusare kaa san0 1819 vi0 he. 'alankaardarpan' kaa varnyavishay alankaaron kaa varnan karnaa. sh 'gulalachandroday' kii rachana bisawaan ke talukedaar ke aashray main hui thi. 'naishadh' ke anuwaad ko kavi ne naanaa chhandon ke karke safal banane kii cheshtaa kii hai, kintu usamen use purna safalataa nahin mil saki he. kavy chamatkaar kii ore kavi kaa swaabhaavik rujhaan tha, yah is anuwaad se spasht gyaat hota he. kavi kii rachanaaon se uski kavy-kala-marmagrata tathaa uske abhivyanjan kaushal kaa acha parichay miltaa he.
