 prashnopenishad atharvavedic shakha ke antargat ek upanishad he. yah upanishad sanskrit bhashaa main likhit he. iske rachiyata vaidic kaal ke rishiyon ko mana jaataa he parantu mukhyat vedavyas ji ko kai upanishdon kaa lekhak mana jaataa he. sa upanishad ke pravaktaa acharya pippalad the jo kadachit pipal ke gode khaakar jite the. 
 upanishdon ke rachanakaal ke sambandh main vidwaanon kaa ek mat nahin he. kuch upanishdon ko vedon kii mul samhitaon kaa ansh mana gaya he. ye sarvaadhik pracheen hai. kuch upanishad ‘brahman’ or ‘aaranyak’ granthon ke ansh maane gayi hai. inkaa rachanakaal samhitaon ke baad kaa he. upanishdon ke kaal ke vishay me nishchit mat nahi he samaanyat upanishdo kaa kaal rachanakaal 3000 isa purv se 500 isa purv mana gaya he. upanishdon ke kaal-nirnay ke liye nimna mukhya tathyon ko aadhaar mana gaya he— 
 nimna vidwaanon dwaara vibhinn upanishdon kaa rachana kaal nimna kram main mana gaya he-
 sukesha, satyakam, sauryayani gargy, kausalya, bhargav or kabandhi, in chah brahmajigyaasuon ne inase brahmanirupan kii abhyarthna karne ke uparaant use hridyangam karne kii paatrata ke liye acharya ke aadesh par varsh paryant brahmacharyapurvak tapasya karke pruthak-pruthak ek ek prashn kiya. pippalad ke savistaar uttaron ke sahit in chah prashnon ke naam kaa yah upanishad kaa poorak batalaaya jaataa he. iske pratham teen prashn aparaa vidya vishyak tathaa shesh para vidya sambandhi hai.
 pratham prashn prajapati ke rathi or praan kii ore unase sristi kii utpatti batalaakar acharya ne dwitiya prashn main praan ke swaroop kaa nirupan kiya he or samajhaayaa he ki vah sthool deh kaa prakaashak dharayita evam sab indriyon se shreshth he. tisare prashn main praan kii utpatti tathaa sthiti kaa nirupan karke pippalad ne kaha he ki marankaal main manushya kaa jaisaa sankalp hota he uske anusaar praan hi use vibhinn lokon main le jaataa he.
 chauthe prashn main pippalad ne yah nirdesh kiya he ki swapnavastha main shrotradi indriyon ke man me lay ho jane par praan jaagrath rahata he tathaa sushupti avasthaa main man kaa aatma main lay ho jaataa he. vahi drashtaa, shrota, manta, vigyaata ityadi he jo akshar brahm kaa sopaadhik swaroop he. iskaa gyaan hone par manushya swayan sarvajna, sarvaswaroop, param akshar ho jaataa he.
 paanchave prashn main onkaar main brahm kii ekanishtha upasna ke rahasy main batalaaya gaya he ki uski prayek maatra kii upasna sadgati pradaayini he evam sapurn  kaa ekanishtha upaask cachul nirmukt sarp kii tarah paapon se niyukt hokar ant main paratpar purush kaa saakshaatkaar karata he.
 antim chathe prashn main acharya pippalad ne dikhaaya he ki isi sharir ke hriday pundarikansh main solahakalatmak purush kaa vaas he. brahm kii ichha, evam usee se praan, usase shraddha, akaash, vaay, tej, jal, pruthivi, indriyaan, man or ann, ann se veery, tap, mantra, karm, lok or naam utpann hue hai ja uski solah kalaayen or sopaadhik swaroop hai. sachcha brahrum nirvishesh, advay or vishuddh he. nadiyaan samudr main milakar jaise apane naam rup kaa usee main lay kar detii hai purush bhi brahm ke sachche swaroop ko pahachaanakar namarupatmak in kalaon se mukt hokar nishkal tathaa amar ho jaataa he. is nirupan kii vyakhya main shankaracharya ne apane bhashya main vinashwad, shoonyavad, nyaay sankhya evam laukayitkon kaa yathasthaan vishad khandan kiya he.
 prashnopanishad par sarvapradhan shankarabhashya atirikt madhyabhashya tathaa ramanujacharya jayatirthacharya ev anya aacharyo kii tikaayen athwa bhashya prachlit he.
