 shah shuja durrani afghanistan kaa amir tha jo dost mohammad khan se harkar kashmir main rahaa tha. pratham angl afakhaan yuddh ke baad angrezon ne use afghanistan kaa shaasha phir se banaaya lekin 2 saal ke bheetar uski kabul main hatya kar di gai. iske baad durrani vansh kaa patan ho gaya tha.
 use sikh maharaja ranjit singh ne ek akraman main kashmir se chhudvaaya tha or apni rihaai ke badale main usne ranjit singh ko prasiddh kohinoor hira diya tha.
