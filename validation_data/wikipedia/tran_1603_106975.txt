 ambikapur nagar se 12 kilometre kii duri par darima hawaii adda hai. darima hawaii adda ke paas bade - bade pattharon kaa samuh he. in pattharon ko kisi thos cheez se thokane par awaaje aati he. sarvaadhik aashcharya kii baat yah he ki ye awaaje vibhinn dhaatuo kii aati he. iname se kisi - kisi pathar khule bartan ko thokane ke samaan aawaaj aati he. is pattharon main baithkar yaa letkar bajaane se bhi iske aawaaj main koi antar nahin padataa he. ek hi pathar ke do tukde alag-alag aawaaj paidaa karte hai. is vilakshantaa ke kaaran is pattharon ko anchal ke log thinthini pathar kehete hai.
