 butler sanyukt rajya america main indiana rajya ke dekalb county main sthit ek shahar he. 2010 main is shahar kii jansankhya 2684 thi. butler 1856 me goonthkar gaya tha, jab rail ko badhaayaa gaya tha. yah shahar david butler, ek agrani ke liye namit kiya gaya tha. 2015 main anumaanit jansankhya 2701 he.
 butler 1866 me ek nagar ke rup me shaamil kiya gaya tha or 1903 main ek shahar ke rup main. butler ke aitihaasik jila 2001 main aitihaasik sthanon ke rashtriya register main joda diya tha. 2010 kii jansankhya ke anusaar, butler 2.09 varg mile ke kul kshetra he. shahar ke sarkaar main ek mahayor or ek nagar parishad hai.shahar ke mahayor ron walater he. puure shahar ke vot main se chune gaye he. nagar parishad main paanch sadasyon hote hai jinamein se chaar alag-alag jilon se chune gaye hai or ek sadasya bade jile se chunaa gaya he. 
 butler bulatin disambar 2005 main kepisi media group ink dwaara kharida gaya tha. jo shelton ne samachapatr ko 1976 main stapit kiya gaya tha. iske sampadak jeff jones 1985 main se saptahik ke liye kaam kiya he. butler ke shahar main bahut skulon hen. shahar nivaasiyon yahon ke butler praathmik, eastsayd junior-senior hai skool or butler vishvavidhyalay jate he. butler ke shahar butler public librari dwaara kaary kiya he. pustkaalay agast 2009 main ek vistaar or naveekaran pariyojna ke puraa ho gaya tha or lagbhag apane aakaar ko doguna hai. vistaar ek bacchon ke naye vibhaag or kahani samay kamare, ek nayaa samudaay kamare, kishor jone or vanshavali kaksh shaamil the.
 2010 ke janaganana se hamen pataa chalata ki shahar ke janaskia 2684 ta 951 gharon main, or 668 pariwaron ko shahar main rah rahe the.shahar main ausat umr 33.7 saal thi. butler ke parivaar kaa ausat aakaar 2.71 tha or parivaar kaa ausat aakaar 3.17 tha. uske samay kshetra EST he.
