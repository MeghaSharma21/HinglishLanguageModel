  manch prabandhan aayojan or ek naatakeey utpaadan samanvay kii pratha he. yah utpaadan ke aayojan or vibhinn karmiyon . manch prabandhan kii ek up anushaasan he. manch prabandhakon utpaadan vyavasthit karne main madad karne ke liye ek manch prabandhak kii pustak kaa upyog kar saktaa he.
 manch prabandhan kaa mul zarutat he ki voh baki sab kuch dhyaan me rakh sakta he. kab purvaabhyas karne aana he, kab baaki saar kaam karn he, har chheez k dhyaan rakhmana padt he. yh manch shilp kaa ek hissaa he. apne manch prabandhan ke kitab ke maddad se insaan sab sambhal sakta he.
 16 chenturya me yah logon ne sgahuru kiy tha. logon ko bahut kaam karn padt tha. har chheez sambhaln padt tha or isi karan unko bahut mushkil haoti thi.isi kaaran se logon ne manch prabandhan shuru kiya.
 manch prabandhak ko achche plener or organiser hona padt he.haalanki kai mashhoor logon ne manch prabandhakon ke saath kaam ho saktaa he, kaam hi nahin glamras, aksar lambey samay tak or kaaphi ubaau, dohraye kaaryon ke saath-saath shaareerik rup se maang kii ja rahi shaamil he. reharsal avadhi ke dauraan manch prabandhan dal kalatmak prakriya ke bich purvaabhyas kamare main vikaas ke rup main kaary karata he or logon ko shaareerik rup se nirmaan, silaai, sangyojan or he ki purvaabhyas kamare ke baahar utpaadan kar rahi he. manch prabandhan par pratikul utpaadan ko prabhavit karne se kuch ko rokane ke liye kar rahe hai. vistaar or acha sanchaar kaushal ke liye yahan dhyaan jaruri he.
 manch prabandhan me alag alag jaghao k alag alag kaam hot he. kuch jaghao me to manch prabandhak ke saath saath up manch prabandhak or sahaayak manch prabandhak bhi hote he. 
 up manch prabandhak abhinaytaon sanket detaa he or aam taur par takneekee chaalak dal ke sadasyon kyuu jayega or kabhi kabhi, kalakaaron or nirdeshak manch prabandhak ke aadesh kaa paalan karte hue. up manch prabandhak, jabaki takneekee samasyaon reharsal ke dauraan sulajhaa rahe hai dhaaran karne ke liye abhinayta kahata he, or nirdhaarit karata he jahaan script. up manch prabandhak rukaa ko punah aarambh karne main kuch cinemagharon main ek alag sthiti he, jabaki anya logon main up manch prabandhak kii jimmedaariyon manch prabandhak yaa sahaayak manch prabandhak dwaara grahan kiya ja saktaa he. 
 up manch prabandhak jimmedaariyon, jo manch prabandhak dwaara aavantit kar rahe hai vividh he. unhone kaha ki lag rahaa he or reharsal ke dauraan rangamanch kii saamagree or sho ke ran banaae rakhane main madad karata he. unhone upasthiti yaa anumaan darshakon aakaar le saktaa he, manch ke peeche takanicianon prabandhan kar sakate hai, chaalak dal, kalakaaron or prabandhan ke bich ek sampark ke rup main kaary kar sakate hai, or kuch sanketon kah sakate hai. aisi avasthaa or coffee yaa chaay pak ke rup main sansaarik kaaryon sahaayak manch prabandhak ke liye gir saktaa he. to manch prabandhak apane yaa apane kartavyon kaa paalan karne main asamarth he, sahaayak prabandhak stage main bharne ke liye sakasham hona chahie.
