 marex rog ek vishaanu janit rog he. is rog kaa naam pashu chikitsak joseph marek par rakhaa gaya he. yahr rog, galid herpus virus naamak vishaanu se utpann hota he. rog, tii cell limphoma kii upasthiti se jane jate he. lasikakoshika tantrikaon or angon main pravesh karte he. emdivi se sambandhit virus saumy prathit hote hai or vaxin upabhedon ke rup main istemaal kiya ja saktaa he. is vishaanu se sankramit pakshi virus ke vaahak ho sakate hai. navjaat chuja kuch hafton ke liye matra pratipindon dwaara sangrakshit hai. sankraman ke baad, sookshm ghaav ek yaa do saptaah ke baad maujuud hote hai. sakal ghaav teen se chaar saptaah ke baad maujuud hai. yah vishaanu pankh ke rome se failata he or saans dwaara preshit hota he. clasical marek kii beemaari yaa neurolemophotomatis ek yaa adhik angon ke asamamit pakshaaghaat kaa kaaran banataa he.
