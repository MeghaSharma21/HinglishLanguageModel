 kiyomiju-deraa aadhikarik taur par otawa-saan kyomiju-deraa, purvi kioto main sthit ek swatantra buddha mandir hai.yah mandir pracheen kioto ke aitihaasik smarakon kaa hissaa hai, or unesco vishwa dharohar sthal main se ek hai.
 is sthaan ko, yasuggi shimena main sthit kyomiju-deraa ke saath bhramit na ho, jo pashchimi japan ke madhyam se chauguko 33 kaanon tirthayatra ke 33-mandir maarg kaa hissaa hai, or naa hi buddha pujari nicharen ke saath jude kiyomimi-deraa mandir ke saath.
 kiyomiju-deraa ko praarambhik hen kaal main sthapit kiya gaya tha. mandir 778 main sakaanui no tamuramaaro dwaara sthapit kiya gaya tha, or isaki vartmaan imaarat 1633 main, tokagawa imetsu ke aadesh par banaae gaye. puure dhaanche main ek bhi keel kaa istemaal nahin kiya gaya hai. iskaa namakaran, parisar ke bheetar pani ke jharne se se liya gaya he, joki nazadiiki pahadiyon main chalaa jaataa hai. kiyomiju kaa arth saaf pani, yaa shuddh pani hota hai.
 yah mul rup se naaraa kaal se judi puraane or prabhaavshaalee hoso sampradaay se sambadhdit tha. haalanki, 1965 main is sambaddhta ko tod diya or iske vartmaan sangrakshak apane aap ko "kitavoso" sampradaay ke sadsy kehane lage.
 mukhya haul main ek bada baramada hai, jo oonche khambhe par khadaa hua hai, jo dhalaan se baahar nikal imaarat ko pakade rakhaa hai, yahan se shahar kaa khubasurat drishy nazar aata hai. badi sankhya main aane vaale tirthayatriyon ko samyojit karne ke liye eado kaal ke dauraan kai lokapriy sthalon main bade baramada or mukhya haul kaa nirmaan kiya gaya tha.
 angreji ke ek lokapriy abhivyakti "dubkee lene ke liye" ke hi samakaksh yahan japani abhivyakti "kyomizu ke manch se chalaang" prashikad hai. joki eado-kaal kii ek parampara ko sanderbhit karata hai, jisamen koi bhi vyakti yahan se 13-meter neeche kudata hai, or batch jaataa hai, to uski ek ichha kii purti kar di jaaegi. eado kaal ke dauraan, yahan se 234 chalaangen darj kii gayi, or unamem se, 85.4% log batch gaye. ab is abhyaas parampara ko nishiddh kar diya gaya hai.
 mukhya haul ke neeche otova jharnaa he, jahaan jal, teen naalee ke madhyam se ek talab main aakar girte hai. koi bhi aagatuk iskaa pani pi saktaa hai, mana jaataa he ki isamen manokamana purna karne ki shakti hoti hai.
