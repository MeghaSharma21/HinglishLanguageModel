 dhaka-istambul fret coridor ek antarrashtriya rail maarg pariyojna he. yah rail maarg pariyojna bangladesh,bharat,pakistan ,iran or turki ko jodatii he.bharat kaa purvi dadicated fret coridor is coridor kaa ek hissaa he. 
 yah prastaavit pariyojna 6,000 kilometre duuri ke antaraal main faili hui he. is ekikrut maal haada railway network se nimna desh judenge. 
 is pariyojna kaa code he :ITI-DKD-Y
 yah mana jaataa he ki asia prashanth kshetra ke vyapaar or aarthik vikaas ke liye dhaka se ek trans container maal train bangladesh, bhutan, bharat, nepal, pakistan, afghanistan, iran or turki main istambul tak pahunchegi. haalaanki bhutan or afghanistan railway maarg se jude nahin hai. phir bhi bhutan ko sadak maarg se kolkata ke liye joda gaya he or container ke dwaara railway stesan queta se sadak maarg se afghanistan main pahunchaaya ja saktaa he. nepal kaa dakshini shahar birganj kolkata se bhi is galiyare se judaa hua he. is prakaar is coridor se apratyaksh rup se bhutan ,nepal or afghanistan labhanvit honge.
