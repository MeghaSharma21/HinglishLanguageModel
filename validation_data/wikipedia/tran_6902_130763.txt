 pratham vishwa yuddh kii jisame angrejo kii ore se jodhpur riyasat kii sena ne bhi hissaa liya. itihaas main is ladaai ko hypha kii ladaai ke naam se janaa jaataa he.hypha kii ladaai 23 sitambar 1918 ko ladi gayi. is ladaai main raajaputaane kii sena kaa netritv jodhpur riyasat ke senapati dalpat singh ne kiya. angrejo ne jodhpur riyasat kii sena ko hypha par kabja karne ke aadesh die gaye. aadesh milte hi jodhpur riyasat ke senapati dalpat singh ne apni sena ko dushman par tut padane ke liye nirdesh diya. jiske baad yah rajasthani ranbankur kii sena dushman ko khatm karne or hypha par kabja karne ke liye aage kii ore badi. lekin tabhi angrejo ko yah maaloom chalaa kii dushman ke paas bandooke or mashin gan he jabaki jodhpur riyasat kii sena ghodo par talwaar or bhaalo se ladane vaali thi. isi vajah se angrejo ne jodhpur riyasat kii sena waapas lautne ke aadesh diya. lekin jodhpur riyasat ke senapati dalpat singh ne kaha kii hamaare yahan waapas lautne kaa koi rivaaj nahin he. ham ranbankure jo ran bhoomi main utarane ke baad yaa to jeet hasil karte he yaa phir veergati ko praapt ho jate he. duusari ore yah sena ko dushman par vijay praapt karne ke liye bandooke, topon or mashin gan ke saamne apane chaati adaakar apni paramparaagat yuddh style se badi bahaduree ke lard rahi thi. is ladaai main jodhpur kii sena ke kareeb no sau sainik veergati ko praapt hue. yuddh kaa parinaam ne ek amar itihaas likh daalaa. jo aaj tak pure vishwa main kahi nahin dekhne ko mila tha. kuki yah yuddh duniya ke maatr aisa yuddh tha jo kii talavaaro or banduukon ke bich hua. lekin antat : vijayshri rathodo mili or unhone hypha par kabja kar liya or chaar sau saal puraane otoman samrajya kaa ant ho gaya. ranbankaa rathodo kii is bahaduree ke prabhavit hokar bharat main british sena ke commander-in-cheef ne flag-staff house ke naam se apane liye ek rihaaysi bhawan kaa nirmaan karavaaya. bhawan ek chauraahey se laga hua bana he, is chauraahey ke madhya main gol chakkar ke bichon bich ek stambh ke kinare teen dishaao main munh kiye hue teen sainikon kii murtiyan lagi hui hai. jo kii ranbankaa rathodo kii bahaduree ko yaadgaar banane ke liye banaai gai.
