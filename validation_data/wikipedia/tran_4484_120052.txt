 rupahala 
 rupahalaa ek dhatvik chaaya rang he, jo ki saleti ke nikatatam he.
 drishy anubhuti praayah dhaatu rajat jaisi hoti he, uski dhatvik abha main. yah saral thos rang dwaara pradarshit nahin kiya ja saktaa he. kyonki isaki chamak dhaatu kii ujjwal chamak se hoti he, jo ki bhinn konon par bhinnta se badalati he. isi prakaar aisa koi tarika abhi tak nahin he, jisase ki dhatvik yaa pratideept rangon ko computer par pradarshit kiya ja sake. faltah kalaatmakataa hetu kagaz ityadi par chamakta jhilamilaataa rang prayukt hota he. matt saleti jo yahan dhabbe main dikhaaya he, use rupahalaa to nahin kaha ja saktaa, parantu isse behatar koi vikalp abhi tak nahin he. yaa phir ise chhayit rup main dikhaaya jaye. 
 daanye main web rang rupahala pradarshit he.
