 vander vuman ek kaalpanik super hero he jo disi comics dwaara prakashit america comic pustakon main pradarshit hoti he. justise lig kii ek sansthaapak sadsy hone ke saath saath vah ek devi tathaa amejan ke logon kii rajdoot hai. yah charitra oktober 1941 main ol star comics main pehali baar pradarshit hua tha. apane mathrubhumi, themiskira main unka aadhikarik sheershak "themiskira kii rajkumaari dyna, hipopulita kii beti" he. haalanki baahar samaaj main unhen apni naagarik pahchaan dyna prince ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. inhen "amezzing amejan", "da spirit of trath", "themiskira'sa champion", "da god killer" or "da godes of lav and war" ityadi naamon se bhi janaa jaataa rahaa he.
