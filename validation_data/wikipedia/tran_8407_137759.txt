 dirghakaalik smriti ek prakaar kii smriti he jo lambe samay tak smriti ko saheje rakhti he. yadi aap kuch varsho yaa maheenon kii koi bhi smriti yaad rakhte hai yaa koi badi jaankaree to vah sabhi dirghakaalik smriti main aati he. yah lambe samay tak anginat jaankaree yaad rakh sakati he.
 ek anusandhan main paaya gaya ki dirghakaalik smriti ko yaad rakhane ke liye dimag us jaankaree ko kutabandhan kar yaad rakhata he. yah dirghakaalik smriti main jane se purv sakriy smriti main jaataa he. ek anusandhan main yah pataa chalaa ki koi bhi vastu yaa jaankaree jo ham dekhte hai, vah hamaare dimag main jaldi se yaad ho jaataa he. iskaa arth yah he ki yadi hamaara sakriy smriti jitna adhik jaankaree grahan karegaa utana ham adhik gati se kuch bhi sikh sakenge..
 nidra kaa kaary dirghakaalik smriti main bahut bada hota he. iske dwaara hi nae smriti ke liye sthaan banataa he.
