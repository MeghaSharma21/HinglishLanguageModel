 philipinoz ke nagar dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh main samvidhan dwaara paribhaashit shahar hote hai jinako kanoonee rup se teen shreniyon main vargeekrut kar ke unhen sthaneey sarkarein di jaati hai.
 inamen kam-se-kam 2 lakh log rahete hai, jisaki pushti rashtriya sankhyikee karyaalay ko kerani hoti he. is darje ke liye nagar kii kul aay 5 karod pasos honi aavashyak he. puure desh main 2016 main 33 ati-nagarikrut shahar the, jinamein se 16 metro manila kshetra main the.
 yah ve nagar hai jo ati-nagarikrut swatantra shahar kaa darja praapt nahin kar sakate. inhen us praant kaa hissaa mana jaataa he jisamen ve bhugolic rup se sthit hai. agar ve kinhin do praanton kii seemaa par sthit hai to us praant kaa bhaag maane jate hai jisaki yah kabhi nagarpalika hua karte the.
 yah aise shahar hai jo ati-nagarikrut swatantra shahar ke str par to nahin hai lekin jinhen philippini sansad ne apane aasapaas ke praant se swatantra astitv pradaan kar diya he. is shaharon ke nivasee apni praant kii sarkaar chunane ke liye matdaan nahin kar sakati kyonki in nagaron kii apni bhinn sarkarein hoti hai. philipinoz main 2016 tak aise keval 5 shahar kotabato, dagupan, naga, ormoke or santiago the.
