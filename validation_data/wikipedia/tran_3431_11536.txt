 tattwarthsutra, jain acharya umaswami dwaara rachit ek jain granth he. ise 'tattwarth-adhigam-sutara' tathaa 'moksh-shaastra' bhi kehete hai. sanskrit bhashaa main likhaa gaya yah pratham jain granth mana jaataa he. isamen das adhyay tathaa 350 sutara hai. umaswami sabhi jain matavalambiyon dwaara maanya hai. unka jeevankaal dwitiya shatabdi he. acharya poojyapad dwaara virachit sarvarthsiddhi tattwarthsutra par likhi gayi ek pramukh tikaa he.
 tattwarth sutara ke das adhyay is prakaar he :-
