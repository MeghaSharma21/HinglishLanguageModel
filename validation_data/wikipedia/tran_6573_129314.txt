 bhartiya ayurvigyaan parishad kii sthapana, bhartiya ayurvigyaan parishad adhinium, 1933 ke antargat 1934 main kii gai thi. iskaa mukhya kaary chikitsa ke kshetra main ucch shiksha hetu tathaa bharat va videshon kii chikitsa yogyata kii manyata ke liye samaan maanakon ko sthapit karnaa tha, ab ise nirast kar diya gaya he. 
 bharat main swatantrata ke pashchaat medical collejon kii sankhya main nirantar vruddhi hoti rahi he. isliye yah mahasoos kiya gaya ki desh main chikitsa shiksha main teji se ho rahe vikaas or pragati ke kaaran utpann hui chunautiyon se nipatane ke liye bhartiya ayurvigyaan parishad adhinium ke praavdhaan paryaapt nahin the. parinamswaroop, 1956 main puraane adhinium ko nirast kar diya gaya or ek nayaa adhinium banaaya gaya. is adhinium ko aage bhi 1964, 1993 or 2001 main sanshodhit kiya gaya.
