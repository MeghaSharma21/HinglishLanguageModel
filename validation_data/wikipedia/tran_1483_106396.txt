 jalaur rajasthan rajya kaa ek etihasik shahar he. yah shahar prachinkaal main 'jaballipur' ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. jalaur jila mukhyalay yahan sthit he. luni nadi kii upanadi sukari ke dakshin main sthit jalaur rajasthan kaa aitihaasik jila he. pahalen bahut badi riyasaton me ack thi. jalaur riyasat, chittorgarh riyasat ke baad me apana sthaan rakhti thi. pachchimi rajasthan me pramukh riyasat thi.
 prachinkaal main ise jabalipur or suvarngiri ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa tha. 12veen shatabdi main yah chouhan gurjar kii rajdhani tha. vartmaan main yah jila barmer, sirohi, paali or gujarat ke banaaskantha jile se ghira hua he. gurjar shaskaon ke haath se guzarane ke kaaran yahaan ke smarakon par unka prabhaav dekha ja saktaa he. jalaur kaa pramukh aakarshan jalaur kila he lekin iske alaava bhi yahaan anek darshneey sthal hai.
