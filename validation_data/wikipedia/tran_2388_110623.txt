 tii ke rajeev kumaar tiruvananthapuram se malayalam cinema main rashtriya puraskaar vijeta nirdeshak hai. rajeev kumaar ne 2003 se 2006 tak kerala rajya chalchitr academy ke adhyaksh pad par karyrat rahe hai jo 2002 se vahaan upaadhyaksh the. unhen kerala rajya film puraskaron ke liye jury chayarmen ke liye bhi namit kiya ja chukaa he.
 rajeev kumaar kaa janm kottayam jila, kerala main thajtaoopurkal parivaar main hua. unki praarambhik shiksha amti seminary hai skool, kottayam or esadivi skool, alappuzha me purna hui. uniwarsity college trivendrum se unhone snaatak kii padhai purna kii.
