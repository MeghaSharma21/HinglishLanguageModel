 mekong vishwa kii pramukh nadiyon main se he jo tibet se shuru hokar china ke yunan praant, myanmar, thailand, laos tathaa cambodia se hokar bahati he. lambaai ke anusaar yah vishwa kii 13veen sabase badi nadi he or pravaah ke aaytan dar ke anusaar 10veen. bahaav main aniyatta tathaa jalprapaaton ke kaaran se iskaa adhikansh bhaag naukaaon ke liye agamya he.
 ise tibet me dza-chu kaha jaataa he. chini bhashaa me meigoga he yaa lanchaag jongan, thai me mai cham thogan, lao bhashaa main menum khogan, kambuja bhashaa me mechogank or vietnam kii bhashaa me kuu log jogan ke naam se yah parichit he.
