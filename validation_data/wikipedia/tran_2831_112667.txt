 times squaire, neuyork shahar ke midtown manhaton ilaake main avasthit ek mahatvapurn sadak chauraahaa he, joki neuyork kaa ek atiprasiddh, vanijyik, manoranjan evam paryaton kendra bhi he. yah chauraahaa, manhaton ke broadway or seventh avenue ke pratichhedan se bani he, or uttar se dakshin, 47tha street se 42da street ke bich avasthit he. beete varshon main yah sthaan neuyork shahar ke ek mahatvapurn saanskrithik kendra ban kar ubhara he, tathaa ise neuyork evam sanyukt rajya ke saanskrithik pratik ke rup main bhi dekha jaataa he. bilbordon or prachaar bordon se din-raat alokik rahane tathaa nirantar gatividhiyon kaa kendra bane rahane ke kaaran, america main ise "duniya kaa chauraahaa", "vishwa kaa hriday" or "centre of da univers" jaise naamon se pukaara jaataa he. yah neuyork ke manoranjan kaa kendra kahe jane vaale broadway theatre district kaa bhi kendra, atah ise vaishvik manoranjan udyog kaa bhi mahatvapurn kendra he. tathaa times squaire duniya ke vyastatam nagareey padayatra margon main se ek bhi he, evam vishwa ke prasiddhtam paryatak aakarshanon main se ek he, jahaan prativarsh, ek anumaanit 5 karod paryatak aate hai. neuyork paryaton ke aankadon ke anusaar, pratidin times squaire se takreeban 330000 log guzarate hai, jabaki kuch vishesh dinon main yahan se guzarane vaale padayatriyon kii sankhya 460000 tak bhi pahunch sakati he. yah chauraahaa neuyork mahanagar ke nagar chowk ke saamaan haisiyat rakhti he.
 is chauraahey or maarg-milan kendra ko purvatah longekar squaire kaha jaataa tha, or 1904 main iske naam ko badalakar times squaire kar diya gaya jab, neuyork times ke apana mukhyalay, is chauraahey kone par navnirmit times building main punahsthapit kar diya. jise punahaavaran ke baad van times squaire kaha jaataa he, or prativarsh yojit kiye jane vaale vishwa prasiddh new yar evs ball drops ke aayojan kaa aayojan sthal hota he. yah ek aisi parampara he, jo neuyork times ne 31 disambar 1907 main shuru kii thi, or vah aaj tak chalati aa rahi he. is kaarykram kaa anubhav karne hetu prativarsh 31 disambar kii raat ko times squaire main 10 lakh se bhi adhik aaagantuk aate hai.
 bhale hi is chauraahey kaa naam "times squaire" yani "times chowk" he, parantu is "chowk" kaa aakaar chaukor yaa vargakar nahin he. yah chauraahaa, 45tha street par, broadway or seventh avenue sadakon ke pratichhedan se bani he, joki latin akshar oaks ke tarah ek duusare ko 45tha street par kaate hai. tathaa uttar main 47tha street se or dakshin main 42da street se yah oaks-crossing ghira hua he, jiske kaaran yah chauraahaa do samshikhariya trikonon se bana he, or kisi damru ke aakaar kaa dikhta he. broadway, neuyork shahar ke kshaitijic or lambavat grid pattern ko tede angel par katata hua guzarataa he, jiske kaaran do samshikhariya trikonon vaali yah damru-aakaar chauraahaa banataa he, jinke shikhar 45tha street par, broadway or seventh avenue sadakon ke pratichhedan par avasthit he.
 haalaanki times squaire ke dakshini trikon kaa koi vishesh naam nahin he, parantu uttari trikon ko fadar dafi squaire kaha jaataa he. ise san 1937 main amareeki 69wein infentry regiment ke chaplen francis dafi ko samarpit kiya gaya tha. fadar dafi squaire main unka smritismaarak tathaa jorge ium kohan kii ek pratimaa maujuud he. tathaa yahan, theatre deveopment fund dwaara sanchalit ek laal rang ke trikoniya aakaar kaa seedhidar natya manch bhi he, jise saamaanya dinon main logon dwaara, baithne, baat karne or tasviren khinchne ke liye upyog kiya jaataa he.
