 
 sarnath main ashok ne jo stambh banwaaya tha uske sheersh bhaag ko singhchaturmukh kehete hai. is murti main chaar bhartiya singh peeth-se-peeth sataye khadey hai. ashok stambh ab bhi apane mul sthaan par sthit he kintu uskaa yah sheersh-bhaag sarnath ke sangrahalay main rakhaa hua he. yah singhchaturmukhstambhashirsh hi bharat ke rashtriya chihn ke rup main sweekaar kiya gaya he. iske aadhaar ke madhyabhaag main ashok chakra ko bharat ke rashtriya dhvaj main bich kii safed pattee main rakhaa gaya he.
