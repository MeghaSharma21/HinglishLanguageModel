 josip broz tito ek yugoslav krantikari or rajaneta the. ve yugoslavia communist party ke mahasachiv the, jinhone dwitiya vishwayudgh ke dauraan yugoslav pratirodh andolan kaa netritv kiya. yuddh ke baad ve samaajawaadee sangheey ganraajya yugoslavia ke pradhanmantri or baad main raashtrapati bane. 1943 se lekar 1980 main maut tak ve yugoslav sena, yugoslav pipuls sena ke sarvocch commander ke rup main marshall of yugoslavia ke pad par asin rahe.
 tito " duusare yugoslavia" ke rup main dwitiya vishwa yuddh se lekar 1991 tak barkaraar ek samaajawaadee mahasang ke mukhya vaastukar the. kominform ke sansthaapakon main se ek hone ke baavjood ve soviet aadhipaty kii upeksha karne vaale pehle or iklaute kominform sadsy the. samaajwaad ke swatantra raah ke pairokaar hone ke saath-saath ve gut nirpeksh andolan ke mukhya sansthaapakon or pratham mahasachiv the. is tarah se unhone shityudh ke dauraan do virdohi block ke bich gut nirpeksh kii niti kaa samarthan kiya.
