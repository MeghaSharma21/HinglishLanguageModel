 kanu sanyaal bharat main naksalwadi andolan ke janak kahe jane hai. darjeeling jile ke karsiyang main janmen kanu sanyaal apane paanch bhai bahanon main sabase chote hai. pita anand govind sanyaal karsiyang ke court main padasth the. kanu sanyaal ne karsiyang ke hi emai skool se 1946 main metric kii apni padhai puri kii. baad main inter kii padhai ke liye unhone jalpaigudi college main daakhila liya lekin padhai bich main hi chhod di. uske baad unhen darjeeling ke hi kalingpong court main rajasav clerk kii naukari mili. kuch hi dinon baad bengal ke mukhyamantri vidhaan chandra raay ko kaala jhanda dikhaane ke aarope main unhen giraftaar kar liya gaya. jail main rahete hue unki mulaakaat chaaru majumdaar se hui. jab kanu sanyaal jail se baahar aae to unhone purnakalik kaaryakarta ke bataur bhartiya communist party kii sadsyta lee. 1964 main party tuutane ke baad unhone makapa ke saath rahanaa pasand kiya. 1967 main kanu sanyaal ne darjeeling ke naksalbadi main sashastr andolan kii aguwai kii. apane jivan ke lagbhag 14 saal kanu sanyaal ne jail main gujaarey. in dinon ve bhakpa maale ke mahasachiv ke bataur sakriy hai or naksalbadi se lage hue hathighisa gaanv main rahete hai.
 23 march 2010 ko kanu sanyaal kaa nidhan hua. aisa mana jaataa he ki unhone aatmahatya kii he.
