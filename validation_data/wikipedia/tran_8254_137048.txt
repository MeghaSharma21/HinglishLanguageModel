 chitral yaa chhetrar pashchimottaree pakistan ke kaibar-pakhtuunakva praant ke chitral zile kii rajdhani or sabase bada shahar he. yah hindu kush parvatamala ke sabase unche pahaad tirich mir ke charnon main kunar nadi ke pashchimi kinare par basaa hua he. chitral zile kii kul 3 lakh logon kii aabaadi main se lagbhag 2 lakh chitral shahar main rahate hai. shahar jis vaadi main sthit he vah 3,700 foot kii oonchaai par sthit he.
 'chitral' khovaar bhashaa ke 'chhetrar' shabd se aayaa he. kyonki khovaar hind-arya bhashaa-parivaar kii ek dardi bhashaa he, isliye 'chhetrar' ke sajaateey shabd hindi main bhi milte hai, jaise ki 'kshetra', 'chhetra' or 'khet'.
