 satyagrah sadan, yaa satyagrah house, jise aamtaur par gandhi house ke rup main janaa jaataa he, johansburg main sthit sangrahalay or atithi grih he. ghar mahatma gandhi kaa tha: un kaa yah 1908 se 1909 ke bich nivas sthaan va kaarysthal tha. yah johansburg kii aitihaasik viraasat ke hisse ke rup main panjikrut he. satyagrah kaa arth sachai kaa aagrah karnaa hota he. ghar gandhi or khud ke liye vaastukar harman calenbaak dwaara dizine kiya gaya tha.
 gandhi ne 1893 se 1914 ke bich apane jivan ke 21 varsh dakshin africa main vyateet kiye the, haalanki is avadhi main ve bharat or united kingdom kaa daura bhi kiya karte the. aisa mana jaataa he ki gandhi ko nasleey bhedbhaav ke bare main sabase pehle vahaan gyaan hua, jab unhen pitermaritzberg railway stesan se keval goron ke liye arakshit dibbe se yatra karne ke kaaran giraftaar kar liya tha.
