 falasi bharat ke uttrakhand rajya ke rudraprayag jile main sthit ek gaanv he. yahan par bhagwaan tungnath kaa pracheen mandir he.
 mandir main bhagwaan shiv kaa aaparup ling he. iske atirikt bhagwati chandika evam ganesh ji hai. is mandir main shiv kii pradhanta he, jabaki tungnath ke ek anya mandir jo ki satera main he usamen shakti kii pradhanta he. mandir main saamaanya puja paas ke hi falasi gaanv ke log karte hai. 
 mandir pracheen dakshin style kaa bana he. yadhyapi baad main iskaa punuruddhar kiya gaya lekin pracheen style kii sundarata ko kaayam rakhane kaa praytn kiya gaya. mandir main kuch puraani mandir kii deevaaron par bani murtiyon ke avshesh hai jinase pataa chalata he ki puraane samay main mandir kii deevaaron par dakshin style main sundar murtiyan bani rahi hongi.
 mandir kaa swaamitv paas ke rajput thokadaaron ke paas hota he.
 mandir main lagbhag 12 saalon baad bhagwati chandika kii banyath hoti he. yatra ke pehle devi ke pratik barma thanguru kii praan pratishtha kii jaati he. yatra ke dauraan barma thanguru ko mandir ke kshetra main aane vaale sabhi gaanvon main ghar-ghar evam sabhi devsthanon par le jaaya jaataa he. chaar rajput jati ke purush maheenon tak kathin evam pavitra jivan style kaa nirvaah karte huye barma thanguru ko le jate hai. saath main ganesh devtaa kii doli jaati he jise brahman le jaataa he. iske atirikt bhutyor devtaa saath jaataa he.
 yatra kii samaapti par yajna hota he. mandir ke upar kuch seedhinuma khet hai jinamein se ek main yajna kund banaaya jaataa he. yajna main karmkand gram benji ke brahmanon dwaara karavaaya jaataa he jabaki gram maykoti ke brahmanon ke paas acharya pad hota he. yajna kii samaapti par barma thanguru ko prithvi ke neeche padhara diya jaataa he. saath hi barma thanguru ko le jane vaale ervaalon kii ek jhonpadi hoti he jise jala diya jaataa he, iske peeche yah manyata he ki bhagwati ko kai maheene saath rahane se ervaalon ke prati moh ho jaataa he or vah unhen apane saath le janaa chaahati he, isaliye bhagwati ko yah jataane ke liye ki ab unka sambandh samaapt ho chukaa he unki jhopadi ko jala diya jaataa he.
 rishikesh se motormarg dwaara bas athwa taxi se rudraprayag, phir rudraprayag se chopta kasbe tak pahunchein. chopta se ek paidal maarg neeche falasi gaanv kii taraf jaataa he jahaan se mandir tak 15-20 minute kaa rasta he.
