 muzataag ata yaa muzatagaata tibet ke pathaar ke uttari chor kii pahadiyon kaa doosraa sabase unchaa parvat he. isaki oonchaai 7,546 meter he or uigur bhashaa main iske naam 'muz', 'taag' or 'ata' jodakar bana he, yani iskaa puraa matlab 'pita barf-parvat' he. muzataag ata ko kabhi-kabhi kunlun shaan parvat-shrunkhlaa kaa hissaa mana jaataa he haalanki bhugolic drushti se yah pamir parvaton ke zyaada sameep he. apni pashchimi mukh kii aasaan dhalaan or shinjiyang ke shushk vaataavaran kii vajah se yah vishwa ke 7,000 meter se unche pahaado main chadhane main sabase aasaan waalon main se ek mana jaataa he. yah kongur taag se zaraa dakshin main he, jo kunlun shaan kaa sabase unchaa pahaad he. kyonki kabhi-kabhi yah vivaad kaa vishay hota he ki kongur taag or muzataag ata kunlun shrunkhlaa main gine jane chahie ki nahin isliye aksar 7,167 meter unchaa kunlun devi parvat ko hi kunlun shaan kaa sabase ooncha parvat kaha jaataa he.
 'muzataag ata' ke naam main 'ga' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'ga' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'galati' or 'garib' shabdon ke 'ga' se miltaa he.
