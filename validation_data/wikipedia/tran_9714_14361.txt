 bhagabhadra shunga rajvansh ke ek raja the. unhone 110 isa purv ke lagbhag uttar kendriya or purvi bharat main shashan kiya. yadhyapi shunga kii rajdhani patliputra thi, unhen vidisha main adaalat nirmaan ke liye bhi janaa jaataa he. shunga rajvansh ne 112 varshon tak shashan kiya or unamem se 9wein raja bhaga ko vidisha ke bhadra ke rup main janaa jaataa he.
