 plasma globe athwa plasma deep saamanytah vibhinn utkrisht gaison se bharaa hua kaanch kaa gola he jiske kedra par ucch voltata ke electrode lage huye hai. plasma tantu aantarik electrode se bahari kuchaalak kaanch tak vistaarit hota he jisase vibhinn rango ke sthaayee prakaash poonj dekhne ko milte hai. plasma globe 1980 ke dashak main vicitra vastu ke rup main lokapriy huye the.
 plasma deep kaa avishkaar nicola testla ne kiya tha. unhone ucch voltata se sambaddh ghatnaon ke adhyayan ke liye kaanch kii khaali nalee main ucch aavriti dhara ke saath prayog armbh kiya tha jisamen iskaa avishkaar kiya. lekin iskaa aadhunik sanskaran pehali baar bill parker ne banaaya. testla ne apane is avishkaar ko akriy gas anaveshan nalee naam diya.
