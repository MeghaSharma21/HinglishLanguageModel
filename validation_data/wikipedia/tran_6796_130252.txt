 ramadaa asia-prashanth, ramadaa antarrashtriya hotel shrinkhalaa kii kshetreey shakha he jo windham hotel samooh dwaara sanchalit hota hai. windham hotel samooh vishwa bhar main sampatti prabandhan sevaayen pradaan karata he.
 ramadaa asia prashanth network samooh kii shuruyaat, guangazu, china main varsh 1991 ek hotel ke saath shuru hua jiskaa naam ramadaa parl guangazu tha. tab se yah samooh 12 deshon main 79 hotel khol chukaa hai. agle kuch varshon ke bheetar asia-prashanth kshetra ke liye isane kai hotel kholane kii yojana banaai he.
 asia prashanth kshetra ke ramadaa hotel vyaktigat swaamitv main hai rahe hai or inkaa lakshya madhya bazaar ke avkaash or vyapaar yatri hai. yah ek purna seva hotel hai jahaan onsite khaadya or peya dukaanon, vyapaar suvidhaon, or swasthya suvidha uplabdh hai.
 is kshetra main ramadaa asia-prashanth sampatti main shaamil hai:
 australia

 guam
 haggkog
 india
 indonesia
 japan
 korea
 malaysia
 new calladonia
 pakistan
 shri lanka
 thailand
 windham hotel samooh ne haal hi main singapore ke hotel or awaas bazaar main pravesh karne kii ghoshnaa kii is sampati ke khulne kii ummid 2014 main khulne kii ummid hai 391-room vaala ramadaa singapore, balestir park kshetra main sthit he. yah singapore main pehali ramadaa antarrashtriya sampatti hogi.
 farvari 2010 main, windham hotel samooh ne china main apane vistaar kii ghoshnaa chaar atirikt hotel ke vikaas ke saath kii is tarah china main ramadaa kii kul 39 sampattiyaan ho jaayengi.
 is samooh kaa bharat main bhi bharat main apni upasthiti kaa vistaar karne kii yojana he philhal desh main iske kul 14 sampattiyaan hai.
