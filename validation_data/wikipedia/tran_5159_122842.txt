 buddhadev bhattacharya bhartiya raajnneetijna tathaa marxvaadi communist party ke paulitte beuro sadsy hai. wo 2000 se 2011 tak pashchim bengal ke mukhyamantri rahe. wo jadhavpur vidhansabha kshetra se 14 may 2011 tak vidhayak rahe. 24 varshon tak vidhayak rahane ke baad wo apni hi sarkaar ke purv mukhya sachiv manish gupta se 16,684 maton se parajeet huye. wo apane hi vidhansabha kshetra se haarane vaale pashchim bengal ke duusare mukhyamantri hai. unase pehle praphul chandra sen 1967 main apane hi nirvachan kshetra se haare the.
