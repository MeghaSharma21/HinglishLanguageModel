 soni arikson, mobile phone banane ke liye japani upabhokta electronics company soni corporation or swedish doorsanchaar company arikson dwaara ek sanyukt udhyam he, jo 1 oktober 2001 ko sthapit hua tha. is udhyam ke liye diya gaya kaaran he, sanchaar ke kshetra main arikson ke praudyogikeey naayakatv kaa soni kii upabhokta electronics nipunata ke saath sangyojan karnaa. donon companiyon ne apane mobile phone banane band kar die hai.
 company kaa vaishvik prabandhan landan, united kingdom main hamerasmith main sthit he or isaki anusandhan evam vikaas timen sweden, japan, china, germany, sanyukt rajya america, bharat or united kingdom main hai. 2009 tak nokia, samsung or alji ke baad yah duniya kii chauthi-sabase badi mobile phone nirmaataa ban gai. soni ke lokapriy walkmen or sibar-shot shrinkhalaa ke anukulan ke shubharambh ke kaaran utpaadon kii bikri vyapak rup se bad gai.
 yadhyapi haal main, soni arikson majboot sanvruddhi kaa anand uthaa rahaa he, iskaa dakshin korian pratidwandvi alji electronics kyuu1 2008 main isse aage batch gaya tha kyonki company ke munaafe main 43% tak kii baari giravat ai thi or vah â‚¬ 133 million, par pahunch gaya tha, bikri 8% tak giri or bazaar bhaag 9.4% se 7.9% tak gir gaya tha, 2008 main handset bazaar ke 10% tak badhane kii ummid thi, magar anukool paristhitiyon ke baavjood bhi aisa hua. soni arikson ne june 2008 main ek or munafa chetavanee kii ghoshnaa kii or dekha ki kyuu2 2008 main asal munaafe main 97% kii giravat ai he, ghoshnaa kii gai ki vah 2,000 naukariyon main katautee karegaa, jiske parinamswaroop vyapak shankaa paidaa ho gai ki apane sangharsh kar rahe pratidwandvi motorola ke saath soni arikson bhi avnati kii kagaar par he. kyuu3 main munaafe kaaphi hadd tak samaan str par rahe, lekin november or disambar main nae modelon ke jari hone ke saath, jaise C905 puure britain main sarvshereshth vikritaon main se ek rahaa, munaafe main badhotari dekhi gai.
 18 july 2008 se, soni arikson ke paas duniya bhar main lagbhag 9400 karmchaaree tathaa 2500 thekedaar hai. bart nordberg company ke adhyaksh hai. anders runivad corporate prabandhakarta upaadhyaksh hai.
 sanyukt rajya america main, arikson ne nabbe ke dashak ke aarambh main general electric ke saath bhagidaari kii, yah mukhya rup se america upasthiti or brands pahchaan sthapit karne ke liye kii gai.
 arikson ne apane phonon ke liye ek hi strot se chips praapt karne kaa nirnay liya - new mexico main ek philips ) suvidha. march 2000 main, philips ) kaarkaane main aag lagane se roganuhin karne kii suvidha dooshit ho gai. philips ne arikson or nokia  ko aashwaasan diya ki utpaadan main ek saptaah se zyaada kii deree nahin hogi. jab yah spasht hua ki utpaadan vastav main maheenon ke liye jokhim main rahegaa, tab arikson ko ek gambhir abhaav kaa saamana karnaa padaa. nokia ne abhi tak vaikalpik sutron se bhaag praapt karne shuru kar die the, lekin arikson kii sthiti zyaada kharaab thi kyonki vartmaan modelon kaa utpaadan or nae modelon kaa armbh ruk gaya tha.
 arikson, jo dashakon se celular phone bazaar main tha or duniya kaa number 3 celular telephone handset nirmaataa tha, baari ghate ke saath sangharsh kar rahaa tha. yah mukhya rup se is aag ke kaaran hua or is vajah se bhi kyonki yah nokia kii tarah saste phonon kaa utpaadan karne main asamarth tha. ghate main katautee karne ke liye, isane asian companiyon ko utpaadan theke par dene par vichaar vimarsh kiya, jo kam laagat main handset kaa utpaadan kar sakati thim.
 anumaan lagaaya jane laga ki arikson sambhawatah apana mobile phone vibhaag bech rahaa he, lekin company ke adhyaksh ne kaha ki ve aisa kuch bhi karne kii yojana nahin bana rahe. "mobile phone vastav main arikson ke liye ek crod kaarobaar he. ham itane safal nahin ho pate yadi hamaare paas phone nahin hote", unhone kaha.
 soni duniya bhar ke cell phone bazaar main ek atyalp khilaadi tha, jiskaa bhaag 2000 main 1 pratishat se bhi kam tha. is kshetra main ghate ke baavjood, vah is par or dhyaan kendrit karnaa chahta tha. april 2001 main, soni ne pushti kii ki vah arikson ke saath handset vyapaar main ek sambhav sahyog ke liye vichaar vinimay kar rahaa tha. yah november 2000 main cemens or toshiba ke 3 ji mobile network ke liye handset par ek saath kaam karne kii yojana kii ghoshnaa, jo 2001 main radd kar di gai, ke jald baad kiya gaya.
 agast 2001 tak donon companiyon ne april main ghoshit kiye gaye vilay kii sharton ko antim rup diya tha. company main 3,500 karmchaariyon kaa praarambhik kaarybal rakhane kaa nishchay kiya gaya.
 apane sabase pehle saal main phaljanak hone ke uddeshy ke baavjood, asal main arikson kaa bazaar bhaag gir gaya or agast 2002 main, arikson ne kaha ki yadi vyapaar niraashajanak rahaa to vah soni ke saath apni saajhedaari ko ant kar degaa or mobile phone banane band kar degaa, tabhi soni ne kaha ki vah is sanyukt udhyam ke prati purnataya patibaddh tha or ise safal bananaa chahta tha. haalanki, janvari 2003 main, donon companiyon ne kaha ki ve nuksaan ko kaabo main karne ke prayaas main sanyukt udhyam main adhik dhan lagaayenge.
 soni arikson kii ranneeti aise nae model jari karnaa thi, jo digital photography or saath hi anya multimedia kshamtaaon jaise video clip downlod karne va dekhne or vyaktigat jaankaree prabandhan kshamtaaon main bhi main sakasham hon. is uddeshy se, uski kai nae model jari kiye jinamein sannihit digital camera or rangeen screen thi, jo us samay kii adbhut vasteen thim. ucch-str P800, jisamen ek sannihit camera or pi di e visheshtaon jaisi suvidhaayen thim, safal rahaa or isaki kismat palatney main madadgaar bhi. sanyukt udhyam ne, haalanki, apni falti-phoolati bikri ke baavjood bhi bade nuksaan karnaa jari rakhaa. apane pehle saal se 2002 tak munafa banane kii nirdhaarit tithi 2003 tak sthagit kar di gai or phir 2003 kii duusari chamahi tak.
 yah multimedia handset ke ucch vikreta banane ke apane mishan main vifal rahaa or paanchaven sthaan par aayaa tathaa 2003 main sangharsh karata rahaa.
 june 2002 main, soni arikson ne kaha ki vah america bazaar ke liye si di em e cellphone bananaa band kar denge or ek pramukh praudyogiki ke rup main ji es em par dhyaan kendrit karenge. unhone sanyukt rajya america or germany main anusandhan or vikaas main rojgaar bhi gata die. oktober 2003 main, unhone apana pehla timahi munafa darj kiya magar chetavanee di ki phonon or pratidwandviyon ke girte daam blike main rahanaa mushkil kar denge. soni arikson kii bahaali kaa shrey T610 model kii safalataa ko diya gaya he.
 apane P800 phone kii safalataa ke baad, soni arikson ne oktober 2003 main las vegas or beijing ke samakaleen kaaryakramon main P900 pesh kiya. yah apane purvavarti se zyaada chota, tej, saral or adhik lacheela bataaya gaya.
 march 2004 main, arikson ne kaha ki vah apane pratidwandvi nokia ko simbian, ek udyog sangh jo smart phonon ke liye oparating software banaata he, par niyantran praapt karne se rokenga.
 2004 main, soni arikson kaa bazaar bhaag pehali timahi main 5.6 pratishat se duusari timahi main 7 pratishat tak badha. july 2004 main, soni arikson ne samaklit thamb-board, vyapak i-mail samarthan, charguna mamori or behatar screen ke saath P910 komunicator udghaatit kiya.
 farvari 2005 main, soni arikson ke adhyaksh milz flint ne 3 ji es em world congress par ghoshnaa kii ki agle maheene soni arikson ek mobile phone/digital music player anaavruth karenge. yah walkmen phone kahalaaya jayega or AAC tathaa empi 3 jaise music fil formet bajaaega.
 1 march 2005 ko, soni arikson ne 2 megapixel camera yukt K750i pesh kiya or saath hi uskaa platform sangi W800i, shuruuaati walkmen phonon main se ek jisamen 30 ghante ke plabac kii kshamta thi or do halake-str ke phone.
 2007 main unke pehle 5-megapixel camera phone, soni arikson K850i kii ghoshnaa kii gai or uske baad 2008 main soni arikson C905 kii, jo duniya kaa pehla 8-megapixel phone tha. 28 may 2009 ko mobile world congress 2009 par soni arikson ne setio naamak pehla 12 megapixel phone udghaatit kiya.
 1 may 2005 ko, soni arikson ne ek saude ke tahat dablyu tii e daure ke liye vaishvik sheershak praayojak banane kii sahamati di, yah sauda 6 varshon ke samay main 88 million amareeki dollars kaa tha. mahilaon ke peshewar tennis circuit kaa nayaa naam soni arikson dablyoo tii e tour rakhaa gaya. ek hi maheene baad 7 june ko, isane west indies ke ballebaaz kris gail or ramanaresh sarwan ke praayojan kii ghoshnaa kii.
 oktober 2005 main, soni arikson ne you ai kyuu 3 par aadhaarit pehla mobile phone P990 prastut kiya.
 2 janvari 2007 ko, stoffhome main soni arikson ne ghoshnaa kii ki vah apane kuch mobile phonon kaa utpaadan bharat main karavaaenge. unhone ghoshnaa kii ki unke do outsorcing bhagidaar flextronics tathaa phoxcon 2009 tak prati varsh 10 million cellphone kaa utpaadan karenge. chennai main, bharat ke sanchaar mantri dayaanidhi maaraan ke saath hui ek press confrence main siio milz flint ne ghoshnaa kii ki bharat duniya ke sabase tezi se badhane vaale bazaaron main se ek tha or soni arikson ke liye ek pradhanya bazaar tha jisamen ji es em mobile telephone ke 105 million upyogkarta the.
 2 farvari 2007 ko, soni arikson ne simbian limited se ek swedish software company, you ai kyuu technology adhigrahan kii. milz flint ne ghoshnaa kii ki you ai kyuu ek swadhin company rahegi.
