 america sahitya‘nathniel havthorne’ america laghu kahani lekhak or romance upanyaaskaar the. ve 4 july 1804, salem, masachusets me paidaa hue the. america sahitya main sabase badi katha lekhakon main se ek, 'd scarlett letter' or 'da house off da seven gables' ke liye janaa jaataa he.
 havthorne ke purvajon 17 veen sadi se salem main rahete the. unke pehle america purvaj, wiliam havthorne ek magistrate the, jisane ek quekar mahila ko sajaa sunai thi. unhone kaha ki ek "shuddh" aprabhavit dharmik puja ke rup main apane puure josh or vakaalat ke saath, naitiktavaadi kattarpathiyon ke ek kattar rakshak ke rup main kaam kiya tha. ek saral, lagbhag gambhir, jivan kii vidha ko kathor paalan kiya tha. havthorne 18 veen sadi ke dauraan apane parivaar kii samruddhi or prasiddhi main kami kii aashcharya karne lag tha, jabaki anya salem pariwaron aakarshak shipping vyapaar se amir ban rahe the, is adhinium ke liye teen me se ek ke rup main wiliam ke bete jon salem jaadu tone pareekshan kii bhoomika ke liye ek pratikaar nahin ho saktaa tha . nathniel ke pita-ek jahaaj ke captain the. unke mrityujahaj ke yatrao ke dauraan ho gai. vah saadhan ke bina apane yuva vidhwaa ko chhod di apane do ladakiyon or yuva nathaniel jo char saal ke hoge. vah apane samrudh bhaaiyon, mannigs ke saath chale gaye. nathniel salem main unke ghar main badi hui thi or remand, main main apni kishoravastha bitaai, cebgo jhil ke tat par. ve brownshvik main ke bovdoin college se shikshak laker chaar saal baad 1825 main salem par laut aae. havthorne ek yuvak ke rup main khud ko alag nahin kiya tha. iske bajaay, vah lagbhag ek darjan se adhik varshon padhane or upanyaas lekhan kii kala main maharath hasil karne kii koshish kar bitaayaa.
 havthorne ko khud kii aawaaj, style, or vishyon dhire-dhire se prapt hui. unhone kai tarah ke prabhaavshaalee or vishisht kahaniyaan prakashit kiye, jese; 'da hallove off d thri hills'or 'an old vomons tale'. 1832 me, 'maai kinsman, major molenieuksh' or 'roger malvins burial' unki sabase badi do kahaniyon or behtareen bhashaa me likhe the. 'yang gudman brown' shaayad jaadu tone kii sabase badi kahani kabhi likhi hoge jo 1835 main dikhaai diya gaya tha. is kahani se havthorne kaa pedaesh jagah salem gaav ke prathaon ko mukhya aakarshan kii he jaha jaadu tone kiya jane kii baat hoti thi. unki badhati safalataa use ek chhoti si prasiddhi le aayaa. baad me apane chachaa ke upar nirbhar karne ke liye taiyaar nahin the, vah krishi sahakaari brooke khet main ek nivasee boston custom house,1841 main chah maheene ke liye diya gaya tha jo vest raokshburya, maas me tha jaha use naukari karne ke liye diya gaya tha. jab apni pehali hastakshar kiye kitaab, do baar kaha kisse jo 1837 main prakashit kiya gaya tha. unke kaam se unhe santushtidaayak manyata le aai lekin koi bharosemand aay nahi le aayaa tha. kintu sal 1842 tak, tathaapi, havthorne ke lekhan use sophia pibody se shaadi karne ke liye anumati dene layak ek paryaapt aay le aayaa tha. danpati conkord main old manse kiraye par liya or khushi se teen saal beataye jo baad main apane niabandh 'da old manse' ke naam par record banaaya tha.
 kuch ese pramukh saamaajik vichaarkon bhi upasthiti the, jaise 'osulf valdo amerson','henry thoreu'or 'bronson alkott'. ese vichaarkon ne concord gaav ko 'transsendentalism'ke darshan kaa centre bana diya tha jinamein aadmi ko bhautikwaadi duniya paar karne ke liye protsaahit kiye gaye he jaha anubhav, tathyon or brahmad ke sarvyaapee bhaavana or maanav swatantraaa ke liye yogyata ke prati sachet ho jate hai.
 unke upanyaso me se,pramukh kaam "da scarlet letter', 'da house off da seven gables', 'da marble phun' the. havthorne american katha lekhakon ke bich ucch rank main kam se kam teen baton kaa parinaam he. sabase pehle, vah farm ke ek prabhaavshaalee architekchar bhaavana ke saath ek kushal shilpkaar tha. unki mahanata ke liye ek doosraa kaaran uski naitik antardrushti he. unhe naitik eemaandaaree kii naitiktavaadi parampara viraasat se mila he. unke shreshthata ke liye ek teesraa kaaran roopak or pratikon ke bare main he jo prabhutv he.
 havthorne ke kaam pratikatmak romance he ki aparaadh kii saarvbhaumikta ko maan liya gaya he or jatiltaaon or aadmi kii pasand kii aspashtataa kii padtaal kii he. unhone america upanyaas main sabase zyada tikaoo parampara shuru kiya he. unki sabase badi choti kahaniyon or scarlett letter ke naitik antardrushti shaayad hi kabhi kisi america lekhak dwaara barabari kii gaharaai se chihnit kiye hoge.
