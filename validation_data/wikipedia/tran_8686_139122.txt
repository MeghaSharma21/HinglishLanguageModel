 rashtriya harith praadhikaran adhinium, 2010 dwaara bharat main ek rashtriya harith praadhikaran kii sthapana kii gai he. 18 oktober 2010 ko is adhinium ke tahat paryaavaran se sambandhit kanoonee adhikaron ke pravartan evam vyaktiyon or sampatti ke nuksaan ke liye sahaayta or kshatipurti dene yaa usase sambandhit yaa usase jude maamalon sahit, paryaavaran sangrakshan evam wanon tathaa anya prakritik sansadhano ke sangrakshan se sambandhit maamalon ke prabhavi or twarit niptaare ke liye rashtriya harith adhikaran kii sthapana kii gayi. yah ek vishisht nikaay he jo bahu-anushaasanamak samasyaon vaale paryaavarniya vivaadon ko sanbhaalane ke liye aavashyak visheshgyata dwaara susajjit he. yah praadhikaran, civil prakriya samhita, 1908 ke antargat nirdhaarit prakriya dwaara baadhya nahin hoga, balki naiysargik nyaay ke siddhanton dwaara nirdeshit kiya jayega.
 paryaavaran sambandhi maamalon main adhikaran kaa samarpit kshetradhikaar teevr paryaavarniya nyaay pradaan karegaa tathaa ucch nyaayalayon main mukademebaazi ke bhaar ko kam karne main sahaayta karegaa. adhikaran ko aavednon yaa apilon ke praapt hone ke 6 maheene ke andar unke niptaan kaa prayaas karne kaa kaary saunpa gaya he. shuruuaat main engiti ko paanch baithak sthalon par sthapit kiya janaa he or yah swayan ko adhik pahunchayogy banane ke liye circuit vyavastha kaa anupaalan karegaa. adhikaran kii baithak kaa mukhya sthaan nai dilli hoga or saath hi bhopal, pune, kolkata tathaa chennai adhikaran kii baithkon ke anya chaar sthal honge.
