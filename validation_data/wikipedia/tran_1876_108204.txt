 sundarmurti swamigal nayannar aathvi sadi ke tamilnadu ke ek nainmar sant the. ve bhagwaan shiv ke bhakt the or chaar tamil samya achaaryo me se ek he.
 unka janm thirunavlur naam ke gaanv me hua. unke bachapan kaa naam "nambi aruraar" tha. unke pita kaa naam "sadaiyar" or maan kaa naam "isagnani" tha. ve brahman the.
 thirunavlur ke raja narsingh munaiyar ne nambi ko god lenaa chaha. sadaiyar anasakti se paripurna the, unhone apana betaa khushipurvak raja ko de diya. or nambi raajputr ke rup me paley bade.
 dantakatha ke anusaar jab sundaraar kii shaadi thi tab shankar bhagwaan vahaan aae. ve ek sadhu ke rup me the. unke paas ek parchaa tha, jiske anusaar sundaraar ke dada ne vachan diya tha ki unke aane vaali peedhee unke anuyaayi rahege or unki seva karge. sundaraar ne ise apni niyati samajha or sadhu ke saath tamilnadu kaa bhraman kiya, vahaan ke mandiro kii saiyr kii. thanjavur ke tiruvarur gram pahunchne par unhe paravaai naam kii ladkee se pahchaan hui or baad me unhone unase shaadi kii.
 dantakatha ke anusaar unhone thiruvarur me sab 63 nainmar ke naam kaa keerthan kiya. in kirtanon ko tamilnadu me tirutondar tokai ke naam se janaa gaya. unka tamilnadu bhraman jari rahaa, unhone bhagwaan shankar ke bhajan likhe or kai chamatkaaro ko unke saath joda jaataa he.
 unki khyaati kerala ke raja cheraman parumal tak bhi pahunchi. raja parumal tiruvarur ko aae or sundaraar se mili. raja or sundaraar kii dosti hui or un dono ne saath-saath tirtho kii yatra kii.
 thode saal baad sundaraar thak gaye or shankar bhagwaan kii stuti kii. unhone puchha "is janam se aap hame moksh de". shankar bhagwaan ne unako ek hathi diya. sundaraar ne shankar bhagwaan se parumal ko bhi swarg aane kii anumati maangi. sundarar, unka hathi, parumal or unka ghoda swarglok ko gaye, 825 saal me.
