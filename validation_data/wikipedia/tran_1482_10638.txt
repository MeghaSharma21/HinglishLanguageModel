 ahmedabad gujarat pradesh kaa sabase bada shahar he. bharatvarsh main yah nagar kaa saatven sthaan par he. ikkyavan lakh kii jansankhya vaala ye shahar, sabarmati nadi ke kinare basaa hua he. 1970 main gandhinagar main rajdhani sthanantarit hone se pehle ahmedabad hi gujarat kii rajdhani hua karata tha. ahmedabad ko karnavati ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. is shahaar kii buniyaad san 1411 main daali gayi thi. shahar kaa naam sultan ahamed shah par padaa tha.
 ahmedabad kaa naam sultan ahamed shah ke naam par rakhaa gaya he. sultan ahamed shah ne is shahar kii sthapana 1411 isvi main kii thi. is shahar ko bharat kaa menchester bhi kaha jaataa he. vartmaan samay main, ahmedabad ko bharat ke gujarat praant ke ek pramukh audyogic shahar ke rup main janaa jaataa he.
 aitihaasik taur par, bhartiya swatantraaa sangharsh ke dauraan ahmedabad pramukh shivi aadhaar rahaa he. isi shahar main mahatma gandhi ne sabarmati ashram kii sthapana kii or swatantraaa sangharsh se juden anek aandolan kii shuruyaat bhi yahi se hui thi. ahmedabad bunai ke liye bhi kaaphi prasiddh he. iske saath hi yah shahar vyapaar or vaanijya kendra ke rup main bahut adhik viksit ho rahaa he. angrezi hukumat ke dauraan, is jagah ko fauzi taur par istamaal kiya jaataa tha. ahmedabad is pradesh kaa sabase pramukh shahar he.
 ahmedabad shahar kaa naam sultan ahamed shah ke naam par rakhaa gaya he. san 1411 i. main gujarat ke tatkaaleen shaasha sultan ahamed shah ne pracheen hindu shahar asaval ke nikat ahmedabad kii sthapana kii thi. ahmedabad ko bharat kaa menchester bhi kaha jaataa he. vartmaan samay main ahmedabad ko bharat ke gujarat praant kii rajdhani hone ke saath saath ahmedabad ko ek pramukh audyogic shahar ke rup main janaa jaataa he. aitihaasik taur par, ahmedabad bhartiya swatantraaa sangharsh ke dauraan pramukh shivi aadhaar rahaa he. mahatma gandhi ne sabarmati ashram kii sthapana ahmedabad main kii or swatantraaa sangharsh se juden anek aandolan kii shuruyaat bhi yaheen se hui thi. oon kii bunai ke liye bhi ahmedabad kaafii prasiddh he. iske saath hi ahmedabad shahar vyapaar or vaanijya kendra ke rup main bahut adhik viksit ho rahaa he.
 pashchim bharat main basaa ye shahar, samudr se 174 foot kii oonchai par sthit he. shahar main do jhilein hai - kankaria or vastrapur talab. sabarmati nadi ke garmi ke mausam main suukh jane ke kaaran, nadi kii jagah safed mitti rah jaati he. barish ke mahinon ke alaava puure saal garmi kaa maahaul rahata he. sabase ucch taapmaana 47 degree tak pahanchataa he or kam se kam 5 degree thand ke samay.
 ahmedabad "nagarpalika nigam" is shahar ke dekh rekh kaa kaam sambhalata he or kuch bhaag auda sambhalata he.
 is jhil kaa nirmaan qutub-ud-deen ne 1451 isvi main karavaaya tha. aaj ke samay main ahmedabad ke nivaasiyon ke bich yah jagah sabase adhik prasiddh he. is jhil ke chaaron ore bahut hi khubasurat bagichaa he. jhil ke maghy main bahut hi sundar dweep mahal he. jahaan mughal kaal ke dauraan noorjahan or jahangir aksar ghoomane jaaya karte the. aaj kankaria jhil ahmedabad me ghumne laayak mahatvapurn jagah he. jo aaj kankaria lekaarrant ke naam se prasidh he .
 sajaavat ke saath jatil nakkashi is mandir kii pramukh visheshta he. is mandir kaa nirmaan safed sangemarmar par kiya gaya he. hathisingh jain mandir ahmedabad ke pramukh jain mandiron main se ek he. is mandir kaa nirmaan 19 veen shatabdi main richajan marchent ne kiya tha. is mandir ko unhone jainon ke 15 wein guru dharmnath ko samarpit kiya tha.
 jama masjid kaa nirmaan 1423 isvi main kiya gaya. pashchim bharat main sthit yah behad hi khubasurat masjid he. yah masjid behtareen kaarigari kaa acha udhaaharan prastut karata he.
 ek anya khubasurat masjid jo rani sipari ke naam se jaani jaati he. iskaa nirmaan mahmud shah begda kii rani ne 1514 isvi main karavaaya tha. rani kii mrityu hone ke baad unke shav ko yaheen par dafnaaya gaya tha.
 is ashram kii sthapana mahatma gandhi ne 1915 isvi main kii thi. yaheen se gandhi ji ne dandi yatra kii shuruuaat kii thi. iske alaava yahaan pramukh bhartiya swatantraaa andolanon kii neenv bhi rakhi gai. aaj sabarmati ashram sabarmati riverfrant park ke saamne he .
 is sangrahalay main puraane or aadhunik dhang kii bunai kii kaarigari pradarshit kii gai he. iske alaava yahaan kuch puraani bunai mashin bhi rakhi gai he. is sangrahalay main sangrahit saamaan 17veen shatabdi se bhi pehle ke hai. iske atirikt yahaan bunai se sambandhit ek pustkaalay bhi maujuud he. 
 yahaan jane ke liye sabase uttam samay oktober se farbari tak kaa he. iske alaava nau dinon tak chalane vaale navaratri utsav main bhi jaaya ja saktaa he.
 yahaan sardaar vallabhbhai patel airport he. yah pramukh bhartiya shaharon ke saath saath videshon jaise, colombo, mashkat, landan or neuyark ko bhi jodta he. 
 ahmedabad stesan desh ke lagbhag sabhi pramukh steshnon se sidhe taur par juda hua he.
 ahmedabad kii duuri mumbai se lagbhag 545 kilometre tathaa dilli se 873 kilometre he. yahaan mumbai se bas dwaara bhi jaaya ja saktaa he.
 ahmedabad kii lagbhag aadhi aabaadi sooti vastra udyog tathaa anya laghu udhyamon par ashrith he.
 ahmedabad main gujarat vishvavidhyalay or lalbhai dalpatbhai bharat vidya shodh santhaan hai.
 ahmedabad main ghoomane laayak kai sthal hai. inamen se kuch aadhunik hai tathaa kuch puranic bhi hai.
