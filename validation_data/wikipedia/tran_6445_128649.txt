 pihova haryana ke kurukshetra jile kaa ek nagar he. iskaa purana naam 'prithoodak' he. yah ek prasiddh tirth he.
 mahabharat main kaha gaya he ki
 kurukshetra pavitra mana gaya he or saraswati kurukshetra se bhi pavitra he. saraswati tirth atyant pavitra he, kintu prithoodak inamen sabase adhik paavan va pavitra he.
 mahabharat, vaaman puraana, skanda puraana, markendeya puraana aadi anek puraanon evam dharmagranthon ke anusaar is tirth kaa mahatv isliye zyada ho jaataa he ki puranic vyakhyanon ke anusaar is tirth kii rachana prajapati brahma ne prithvi, jal, vaayu va akaash ke saath sristi ke armbh main kii thi. 'prithudak' shabd kii utpatti kaa sambandh maharaja pruthu se rahaa he. is jagah pruthu ne apane pita kii mrityu ke baad unka kriyakarm evam shraadh kiya. arthath jahaan pruthu ne apane pita ko udak yani jal diya. pruthu va udak ke jod se yah tirth prithoodak kahalaaya.
 vaaman puraana ke anusaar ganga ke tat par rahane vaale rushangu naamak rishi ne apana ant samay jaanakar mukti kii ichha se ganga ko chhodkar prithudak main jane ke liye apane putron se aagrah kiya tha. kyonki uskaa kalyaan ganga dwaar par sambhav nahin tha. padmapurana ke anusaar jo vyakti saraswati ke uttari tat par prithudak main jap karata hua apane sharir kaa tyaag karata he, vah ni:andeh amarta ko praapt karata he.
