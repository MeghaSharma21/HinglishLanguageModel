 hens chrishchiyan anderson denmark ke ek prasiddh lekhak evam kavi the. ve bacchon kii kahaniyon ke liye prasiddh hai. apane jivan main vishwa bhar ke bacchon kaa manoranjan karne ke liye ve prasiddh the. unki kavitaayen evam kahaniyaan vishwa kii 150 se adhik bhashaaon main anudit hui hai. inaki kritiyon ko lekar chalchitr, naatak, evam anemated filmein bani hai. 
 hanse krishichayan andersan kaa janm 2 april 1805 ko odens main hua. apane bachapan main hi unhone kathputliyon ke liye ek naatak kii rachana kar apni bhavi kalpanaashakti kaa parichay diya. yah chote hi the jab inke nirdhan pita kii mrityu ho gai. tatpashchaat ye opera main gayak banane kii ichha se copenhagen aae. inhone is samay bure din bhi dekhe, parantu kuch gayak mitron kii sahaayta se kaam chalata rahaa. gayak banane kii abhilaashaa chhodkar inhone royal theatre main nrity siikhnaa aarambh kiya. royal theatre ke nirdeshak shri colin ne denmark naresh se inaki prashansa kii or kuch varsho ke liye unhone inaki shiksha kaa bhaar sabhaalaa. 
 san 1829 main inhen foderise" naamak pustak ke prakaashan ke falswaroop pratham safalataa praapt hui. 1833 main denmark naresh ne inako kuch dhan bhramanarth diya, jisase inkaa anubhav badha. 1835 main inaki katha "improwizator" ko bahut safalataa mili. is samay inhone "fayari tels" likhnaa aarambh kiya, jinke dwaara ye vishwavikhyath hue. inhone kai naatak bhi likhe. 1872 main ek durghatana ne inhen kisi yogya na rahane diya or 4 agast 1875 ko inaki mrityu ho gai. 
 vishwa ke bal sahitya or scadinavia ke sahitya main inkaa sarvapratham sthaan he. vishwa kii lagbhag sabhi bhashaaon main inaki vikhyath kritiyon kaa anuwaad ho chukaa he. 
 inaki mukhya kritiyaan nimnlikhit hai : "foderise", "rambles" ; "di improwiser" ; "fayari tels" ; "e pikchar book vidout pikchars" ; "e poets bazaar" ; "da du baroneses" ; "in sweden" ; "aatmakatha", "tu bi or note tubi" or "in spain" .
