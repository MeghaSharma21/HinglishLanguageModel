 dewan-e-aam agra kile main sthit mughal badshaah kaa pramukh sabhaagaar tha. icemen mayur singhaasana yaa takhte touse sthapit tha is sabhaagaar kaa prayog aam janata se baat karne or unki faryaad sunne ke liye hota tha. 
 is dhaanchen kaa nirmaan mul rup se lakdi se kiya gaya tha lekin baad main shahjahan se use vartmaan rup pradaan kiya. isapar shahjahan style ke prabhaav ko spasht rup se dekha ja saktaa he jo sangemarmar par kii gai phulon kii nakkashi se pataa chalati he. is kamare main raja aam janata kii fariyaad sunate the or adhikariyon se milte the. dewan-e-aam se ek rasta nagina masjid or mahila bazaar kii ore jaataa he jahaan keval mahilaayein hi mughal auraton ko saamaan bech sakati thim.
