 madhya bhartiya arya parivaar kii bhashaa ardhamagadhi sanskrit or aadhunik bhartiya bhashaaon ke bich kii ek mahatvapurn kadi he. yah pracheen kaal main magadh kii saahitiyik evam bolchaal kii bhashaa thi. jain dharm ke 24wein tirthankar mahavir swami ne isi bhashaa main apane dharmopadesh kiye the. aage chalakar mahavir ke shishyon ne bhi mahavir ke upadeshon kaa sangrah ardhamagadhi main kiya jo aagam naam se prasiddh hue.
 hemchandra acharya ne ardhamagadhi ko 'aarsh praakrit' kaha he. ardhamagadhi shabd kaa kai tarah se arth kiya jaataa he :
 is bhashaa main vipul jain evam loukik sahitya kii rachana kii gai. jain dharm ke 24wein tirthankar mahavir swami ne isi bhashaa main apane dharmopadesh kiye the. aage chalakar mahavir ke shishyon ne ardhamagadhi main mahavir ke upadeshon kaa sangrah kiya jo aagam naam se prasiddh hue. samay-samay par jain aagmon kii teen vaachanaayen huin. antim vaachana mahaviranirvana ke 1,000 varsh baad, isvi san kii chhathi shatabdi ke aarambh main, devardhigani kshamaakshaman ke adhinaayakatv main valbhi main hui jab jain aagam vartmaan rup main lipibaddh kiye gaye. isi bich jain aagmon main bhashaa or vishay kii drushti se anek parivartan hue. in parivartanon ke hone par bhi aacharang, sutarakrutang, uttaradhyayan, dashaivakalik aadi jain aagam paryaapt pracheen or mahatvapurn hai. ye aagam shwetambar jain parampara dwaara hi maanya hai, digambar jainon ke anusaar ye lupt ho gaye hai.
 aagmon ke uttarkalin jain sahitya kii bhashaa ko ardhamagadhi na kahkar praakrit kaha gaya he. isse yahi siddh hota he ki us samay magadh ke baahar bhi jain dharm kaa prachaar ho gaya tha.
