 da fayari queen 16veen shatabdi ke prasiddh angreji kavi edmund spencer kii sarvottam rachana he. is granth ke pranayan main unka uddeshy roopak ke madhyam se arestu dwaara varnit 12 naitik gunon kii mahatta par prakaash daalana tha. puri pustak 12 sargon main hoti, lekin ve keval chah sarg hi puraa kar paae. jin naitik gunon kii in chah sargon main charchaa he ve kramash: is prakaar hai - dharmikata, sanyam, satitv yaa pavitrataa, mitrataa, nyaay or vinamrata. 7wein sarg ke bhi kuch ansh milte hai, jisamen dridhtaa kii mahatta par prakaash padataa he.
 spencer kii kalpana main pustak kii yojana is prakaar thi - parilok kii rani gloriana prati varsh apane darbar main ek utsav karti he jisamen rani kii sahaayta ke aakaankshi utpeedit jeev tathaa aise logon kii sahaayta karne ke icchhuk ek saath ekatra hote hai. yah utsav saadharantaya 12 din chalata he. prayek ko kisi dukhi praani kii sahaayta ke liye kaha jaataa he or is kaary main use bahut si kathinaaiyaan jhelni padati hai or saahasik kaary karne padate hai. 'fayari queen' ke chha: sargon main di hui roopak kahaniyaan gloriana ke darbar ke ek aise hi utsav se sambandhit hai.
 spencer ne 'feiri queen' kii rachana ireland main prarambh kii or iske pratham teen sarg san 1590 main england main prakashit hue. unka mantavya rupakon ke sahare vyapak sansar tathaa prayek manushya ke hriday main chal rahe sat pravritiyon or kupravritiyon ke bich ke sangharsh ko pradarshit karnaa tha. jaisaa ki unhone sar walter raile ke naam apane patr main ghoshit kiya, is pustak kaa uddeshy pathakon ko naitikta evam sadacharan main shikshit karnaa tha.
 lekin 'fayari queen' main roopak kaa sahaara tatkaaleen raajnneeti tathaa shashan se sambandhit vyaktiyon kii charchaa ke liye bhi liya gaya he. paridesh kii rani gloriana ke naam par kavi maharani elizaveth kii prashasti gaata he. isi prakaar fayari queen ke anya paatr bhi tatkaaleen raajnneetik jivan main pramukh vyaktiyon ke pratik hai.
