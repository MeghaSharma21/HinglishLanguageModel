 dhyaan yoga 
 
 shreebhagwatistotram shakti roopen devi bhagwati durga kii stuti main kaha gaya stotra he. iske anek uwach hai, jo ki nimnlikhit hai. yah ek dharmik paath he jisaki rachana vyas muni ne kii thi. aisa mana jaataa he ki jo pavitra bhav se niyampurvak is vyasakrut stotra kaa paath karata he athwa shuddh bhav se ghar par hi paath karata he, uske upar bhagwati sadaa hi prasann rahati hai. 
 jai bhagavti devi namo varade, jai papavinashini bahufalde.jai shumbhanishumbhkapaldhare, pranmaami tu devi narartihare.1.
 jai chandradivakarnetradhare, jai pavkabhooshitavaktravare.jai bhairavadehnilinpare, jai andhakadaityavishoshakare.2.
 jai mahishavimardini shulkare, jai loksamastakpapahare.jai devi pitamavishnunate, jai bhaskarashakrashirovanate.3.
 jai shanmukhsayudhishnute, jai sagargamini shambhunute.jai dukhadaridravinashkare, jai putrakalatravivriddhikarey.4.
 jai devi samastashariradhare, jai nakavidarshini dukhahare.jai vyaadhivinashini moksh kare, jai vaachhitdayini siddhivare.5.
 etadvyaskrutan stotran ya: pathenniytah shuchih.gruhe vaa shuddhbhaven prita bhagwati sadaa.6.
 iti vyasakrutan shreebhagwatistotran sampoornam
