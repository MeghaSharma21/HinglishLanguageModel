 udaisagar jhil udaipur kii paanch pramukh jhiilon main se ek he. yah jhil purv udaipur se lagbhag 13 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he.iskaa nirmaan udaisingh dwitiya ne 1565 isvi main karavaaya tha. jo ki lagbhag 4 kilometre chaudee or 9 meter gahari he. 
 1559 isvi main mewar ke maharana udaisingh dwitiya ne pani kii aapurti ko acchhee karne ke liye sochaa tha or phir baad main udaisagar jhil kaa nirmaan karavaaya tha. is jhil kaa nirmaan isliye bhi karavaaya tha taaki samrajya tak pani aasaani se pahunch sake. yah jhil bahari band se viksit he. yah band jo 479 kilometre lambi he.
