 khortha ek bhashaa he jo bharat ke jharkhand pradesh ke kuch bhaagon or bangladesh ke kuch bhaagon main boli jaati he.
 khortha bhashaa jharkhand ke do pramandalon ke adhikansh kii maatrubhaasha hone ke saath-saath jharkhand ke choubees jilon me se pandrah jilon kii punatah yaa anshatah sampark bhashaa he.
 khortha ke aadi kavi shrinivas panuri kii 92 veen jayanti 2012 main par mandakini ucch vidhyalay bada jamua ke maidan main shradhanjali samaaroh sah kavi sammelan kaa aayojan kiya gaya. mukhya atithi bhu-rajasav mantri mathura prasad mahato ne kaha ki khortha bhashaa ko dwitiya rajbhasha kaa darja to diya gaya, lekin isko dharaatal par pahunchane ke liye bal varg se pathan-pathan kii jarurat he.
