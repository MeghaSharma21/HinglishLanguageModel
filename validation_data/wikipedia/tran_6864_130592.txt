 hindu puranic kathaon ke anusaar, chandra vansh hindu dharm ke kshatriya yaa yoddha-shaasha varg ke chaar pramukh vanshon main se ek he. sambandhit puranic kathaon ke anusaar yah  vansh 'chandra' yaa chandramaa se niklaa tha. 
 "mahabharat" ke anusaar, is rajvansh ke prajnankarta ila prayag ke shaasha the, jabaki unke putr shashibindu bahali desh main shashan karte the.
 mahaan rishi vishwamitra, kanyakubj rajvansh ke raja gadhi ke putr the jo ki chandravanshi raja puru yaa pururava ke putr amavasu ke vanshaj the
 ila ke vanshaj,chandravanshi yaa aila kehalaae jo ki pracheen bharat kaa ek rajvansh tha jisaki neenv budh ke putr puru yaa pururava ne rakhi thi.
