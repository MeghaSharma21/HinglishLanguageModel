 sarvapratham africa unity sanghathan dwaara manaaya jane vaala yah diwas 16 june 1976 ko dakshin africa ke toyota main behatar shiksha ke adhaikaar ke liye chaatro dwaara kiye gaye pradarshan kii smriti main manaaya jaataa he. iske dwaara africa baalkon kii shiksha kii aavashyaktaaon ke bare main jaagarukta laane kii koshish kii jaati he.
