 khel patrakarita lekhan kaa ek rup he jo khel ke vishay or ghatnaon par report karata he. yah khel ke ghatnaon or athleton ke bare main likhane kaa abhyaas or vyavsaay he. khel patrakarita media sanghathan ke mukhya virgon main se ek he. kuch samay pehle, is rup ke patrakarita ko bahut adhik mahatv nahin diya gaya tha, lekin ab isaki gati lagaataar bad rahi he.
