 jorasi, almoda jile ke salt va dvaarahaat vidhansabhaon main sthit parvatamala he. yah parvatamala ramganga va vinod nadiyon ke bich sthit romantic parvat shrinkhalaa he. yah lagbhag 6500 foot kii unchi ek manamohak parvatamala he. is parvat shrinkhalaa ko 'jorasi kshetra' ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. 
 uttrakhand main ramganga or vinod nadiyon se ghiri yah parvat maala apane aap main kisi aashcharya se kam nahin he, jo ram ganga or vinod nadi ke sangam kedaar se shuru hokar dudhatoli kii sabase oonchi chotii tak pahuchati he. is parvat shrinkhalaa main mukhyatah jorasi, gairkhet, ghanyaal pokhar, asur gadi, danu thaan, chaubatiya, gadhwal bunga, jadapani, durga devi, kaliya linguda, nagachaula khaal or phulang parvat ko jodta hua dudhatoli ke ghanghor jangalon main samahit ho jaataa he. isaki sabase badi visheshta yah he ki yah uttarakhand ke teen jillon almoda, chamoli or paudi kii sanyukt seemaa par basaa he. oonchai par stith hone ke kaaran yahaan par pani kii baari kami he, jo yahan se palaayan kaa mukhya kaaran bhi he. apaar kshamtaaon se bhare is kshetra main aaj keval kuch vruddh log hi gaanv main rah gaye hai.

 adarsh rajkeey inter kalege, jorasi kii sthapana san 1959 main kii gayi thi. yah vidhyalay uttrakhaden main sabase puraane vidyalayon main gina jaataa he syalde va chaukhutiyaa block main sabase pehle science said bhi isi vidhyalay main shuru kiya gaya tha. is samay main vidhyalay main lagbhag 465 chaatr va chatraayen apni shiksha grahan kar rahe hai. adhyapakon kii baari kami hone kii bajah se yah vidhyalay abhi badahaali kii maar jhel rah he.
