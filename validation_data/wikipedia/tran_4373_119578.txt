 viksit deshon dwaara vikasshil deshon ke aantarik maamalon main pratyaksh yaa paroksh rup se kiye jane vaale hastkshep ko nav-upniveshwaad kaha jaataa he. navaupaniveshwaad kii dharna ke maanane waalon kaa sochna he ki purv main upniveshi shaktiyon ne jo aarthik dhaancha bana rakhaa tha unka ab bhi un upniveshon par niyantran karne main istemaal kiya ja rahaa he. 
 europe ke deshon ne ek lambe samay tak asia or africa ke deshon par apana samrajyavaadee jaal phenkar unka raajnneetik va aarthik shoshan kiya lekin un deshon main ubharne vaale swatantrata aandolanon ne samrajyavaadee deshon ke manasuubon par pani pher diya. dhire-dhire asia or africa ke desh ek-ek karke samrajyavaadee chungal se mukti paane lage. jab samrajyavaadee shaktiyon ko apane din ladate najar aae to unhone aupniveshik shoshan ke nae nae tareeke talashne shuru kar die. unhone is prakriya main un deshon par apana varchasv banaae rakhane ke liye aarthik samrajyavad kaa sahaara liya. swatantra hone ke baad navdith raashtra is sthiti main nahi rahe ki ve apana swatantra aarthik vikaas kar saken. unke aarthik vikaas main sahaayta ke naam par viksit samrajyavaadee deshon ne dollar kii kootneeti kaa prayog karke unki arthvyavasthaaon par apana niyantran sthapit kar liya or dhire-dhire ve nae samrajyavad ke jaal main is kadar funs gaye ki aaj tak bhi ve viksit deshon ke hi paradheen hai. is vyavastha ko nav-upniveshwaad ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
 yah ek nai avadharana he. iskaa prayog nae prakaar ke samrajyavad ke liye kiya jaataa he. ise 'dollar samrajyavad', 'aarthik samrajyavad', 'nayaa-samrajyavad' aadi naamon se bhi janaa jaataa he. dwitiya vishwayuddh ke baad adhiktar paradheen raashtra raajnneetik rup se to swatantra ho gaye lekin phir bhi apratyaksh rup se kisi na kisi tarah samrajyavad kaa shikaar bane rahe. unki yah sthiti aadhunik samrajyavad yaa nav-upniveshwaad kehalaati he. yah ek aisi vyavastha he jo ek shaktishaalee viksit raashtra yaa apekshaakrit kam shaktishaalee raashtra kaa sambandh ek aarthik yaa upgrah upnivesh ko darshaati he. bahurashtriya nigam iske pramukh saadhan hai. inkaa uddeshy aarthik rup se paradheen yaa upgrah upnivesh kaa adhik se adhik shoshan karke us par apana varchasv banaae rakhana he.
 raashtrapati sucarno ne bandung sammelan main 1955 main kaha tha ki raashtra ke andar chote se videshi samudaay dwaara aarthik niyantran, bauddhik niyantran tathaa vaastavik bhautik niyantran ke rup main yah ek nae libaas main upniveshwaad he. uskaa spasht ishaara bahurashtriya nigamon kii badhati bhoomika kii taraf tha. aaj viksit desh apana samrajyavaadee shikanja kasne ke liye inkaa sahaara le rahe hai. inke dwaara nirdhan deshon ke kachche maal par niyantran puunji niryaat va aasaan vinimay daron ke naam par dimak kii tarah ghuskar kiya ja rahaa he taaki ve desh kachche maal kii purti tathaa taiyaar maal kii bikri kaa kendra ban jaaen.
 is prakaar kaha ja saktaa he ki nav-upniveshwaad samrajyavaadee niyantran kii aisi prakriya he jiskaa prayog viksit raashtra navdith alp viksit rashtron kaa aarthik shoshan karne ke liye karte hai. ye raashtra raajnneetik rup se to swatantra hote hai, lekin aarthik sahaayta, sainik sahaayta, shastron kii sahaayta, takneekee gyaan, utpaadan ke kshetra main viksit deshon ke upar hi ashrith hote hai. inaki viksit deshon par nirbharta itani adhik bad jaati he ki ye apratyaksh rup se raajnneetik rup se bhi swatantra nahin rah jate.
 argenski ne upniveshwaad ke teen roopon - raajnneetik upniveshwaad, aarthik drushti se paradheen desh tathaa pichhalggu desh kaa varnan kiya he. aadhunik yug main raajnneetik drushti se to sabhi desh swatantra ho chuke hai. isliye iskaa koi mahatv nahin rah gaya he. vah antim do ko hi nav-upniveshwaad ke antargat shaamil karata he.
 ye desh raajnneetik rup se to swatantra hote hai lekin aarthik sahaayta ke liye viksit rashtron kii ore dekhte hai. in deshon main aarthik pichhadaapan paaya jaataa he. logon kii nirdhantaa va asthir raajnneetik vyavasthaaen iske suchak hai. logon kii vyaktigat aay va rashtriya aay naam maatr kii hoti he. asia va africa ke navdith swatantra raashtra is shrenee main shaamil hue. lambe samay tak aarthik vikaas ke liye unhen videshi sahaayta par nirbhar hue. unamem se adhiktar aaj bhi aarthik rup se paradheen rashtron kii shrenee main aate hai. viksit deshon ne in deshon main adhik se adhik puunji lagaakar inamen apni utpaadan ikaiyaan sthapit kar rakhi hai. kai deshon main to yah nivesh 80 pratishat tak he. ye desh parashrith rashtron ke raajnneetik jivan ko bhi prabhavit karne kii kshamta rakhte hai. aise deshon ko aarthik parashrith upnivesh kaha jaataa he. america dwaara pakistan, thailand, ghana aadi asia va africa ke deshon main dollar samrajyavad kii niti kaa prasar is prakaar ke upniveshwaad kaa hi ek hissaa he.
 yah ek aisa raashtra hota he jo aupchaarik rup se to swatantra hota he lekin raajnneetik va aarthik drushti se kisi videshi shakti ke adheen hota he. ve desh swatantra videsh niti kaa paalan karne main sakasham nahin hote. dwitiya vishwayuddh ke baad anek deshon main soviet sangh ke niyantran vaali samyavaadee sarkarein sthapit hui. ve sabhi desh pichhalggu desh yaa upgrah upnivesh kii shrenee main aate the. aaj afghanistan main america samarthit sarkaar he. isliye afghanistan america kaa pichhalggu desh he. aavashyaktaa padane par pichhalggu deshon ko anek aakaaon dwaara sainik sahaayta bhi uplabdh karai jaati he. dwitiya vishwayuddh ke baad donon mahashaktiyon ne apane pichhalggu deshon kii har prakaar se madad kii thi. aarthik rup se parashrith deshon kii tulana main in upniveshon kii sthiti adhik kharaab hoti he.
 dwitiya vishwayuddh tak pahunchate-pahunchate samrajyavaadee shaktiyaan itnaa adhik kamjor ho gai ki ve paradheen rashtron par apana samrajyavaadee niyantran banaae rakhane main ayogy siddh hone lage. saath main paradheen deshon main nirantar ubhar rahi raajnneetik chetana ke parinamswaroop ubhare swatantrata aandolan ne bhi samrajyavaadee shaktiyon ko bahut haani pahunchaai. do vishwa yudhon ne samrajyavaadee deshon kii arthvyavasthaaon ko baari nuksaan pahunchaaya. ve ab is sthiti main nahin rahe ki aandolankaaree takaton se loha le saken. isliye unhone apana samrajyavaadee niyantran dhilaa kar diya or upniveshi shashan kaa ant hone laga. lekin iske baad ek nae prakaar ke samrajyavad kaa janm hua jise nav-upniveshwaad yaa aarthik samrajyavad ke naam se janaa jaataa he. yah viksit samrajyavaadee deshon dwaara apni khandit arthvyavasthaaon ko patari par laane ke liye kiye gaye prayaason kaa mahatvapurn hissaa tha. samrajyavaadee takatein chaahati thi ki aarthik niyantran dwaara ve navdith swatantra rashtron ke aarthik jivan par apana niyantran ladakar unase pehle jaisaa hi laabh uthaa sakati hai. iske janm ke nimnlikhit kaaran hai-
 dhire-dhire aupniveshik shoshan ke shikaar deshon main raajnneetik chetana kaa udai hua. isse vahaan par swatantrata aandolan kaa teji se vikaas hone laga. bharat jaise rashtriya mukti aandolan ne aupniveshik takaton ko yah ahsaas kara diya ki ab unka niyantran zyada din tak nahin tik saktaa. sanyukt raashtra sangh ke charter main aatmanirnay ke adhikaar ko sweekruti mil chuki thi. isliye samay kii najukta ko dekhakar unhen apni upniveshon ko swatantra karnaa padaa. parantu un deshon kii raajnneetik asthirataa ne in deshon ko apana aarthik niyantran sthapit karne kaa mauka de diya. isse purana upniveshwaad nae rup main parivartit ho gaya.
 do vishwa yudhon ne europe kii samrajyavaadee takaton ko bhayankar haani pahunchaai. ab upniveshon main shashan chalaanaa unke samarthy se baahar ho gaya. lagaataar uth rahe mukti andolanon ne bhi bade-bade samrajyon ko nasht kar diya. bharat main england ko jo haani uthaani padi, usase bhaybheet hokar angrejon ne yaha par apana aupniveshik shashan jari rakhane main asamarthta jaahir kii. navdith prabhusatta sampann rajyon ne antarrashtriya sambandhon ko prabhavit karnaa shuru kar diya. 1949 main china ek samyavaadee shakti ke rup main ubhara. ab vahaan par samrajyavaadee takaton kaa tikna mushkil ho gaya. maao ne sabhi samrajyavaadee takaton ko bhayankar parinaam kii chetavanee de daali. lekin sabhi navdith raashtra china va bharat jaise shaktishaalee nahin the. adhiktar deshon main apane aarthik vikaas kaa samarthy nahin tha. isliye unhone apane puraane sahukaaron par hi nirbharta kii ichha vyakt kii. sabhi samrajyavaadee takatein aarthik sahaayta ke naam par apane adheen rahe deshon main hastkshep ke prayaas karne lagi. isse vahaan nav-upniveshwaad kaa janm hua.
 sabhi navdith swatantra rashtron apane aarthik vikaas ke liye videshi aarthik madad kii aavashyaktaa anubhav hui. lambe samay aupniveshik shashan ke shikaar rah chuke adhiktar deshon ke aarthik sadhano kaa dohan samrajyavaadee desh kar chuke the. in navdith rashtron ke paas apane aarthik vikaas ke liye na to paryaapt puunji thi or na hi takneekee gyaan, apane aarthik vikaas ke lakshya ko praapt karne ke liye in deshon ko puraani upniveshi takaton se karj lene par majaboor kar diya. isse in deshon main puraani upniveshi takatein phir se apana jaal bichaane main kaamyaab hui or nae-upniveshwaad kaa janm hua.

 dwitiya vishwayuddh ke baad america or soviet sangh main sheet-yuddh armbh ho gaya. donon mahashaktiyon ne apni apni sthiti majboot karne ke liye nae rashtron ko apane guton main shaamil karne kaa nirnay kiya. videshi sahaayta, shastr purti, bahurashtriya nigamon par niyantran aadi sadhano dwaara in mahashaktiyon ne navdith swatantra rashtron ko aarthik rup se apane niyantran main le liya. isse upgrahi rajyon kii sankhya main vruddhi hone lagi. unki sabhi aarthik nitiyaan ek-ek karke nav-upniveshwaad ko majboot aadhaar pradaan karne ke liye hi thi. aaj bhi america jaise viksit raashtra bahurashtriya nigamon ke madhyam se apane nav-upniveshvaadi niyantran ko sudrudh bana rahe hai.
 dhani va shaktishaalee samrajyavaadee raashtra apane aarthik hiton ko puraa karne ke liye navdith swatantra rashtron main apane niyantran ke alag alag tareeke apanaate hai. unka mukhya dhyey aviksit deshon par apana nayaa samrajyavaadee niyantran sthapit karnaa he. iske liye unke dwaara apanaae gaye saadhan nimnlikhit hai-
 sabhi dhani deshon dwaara vishwa ke sabhi bhaagon main aarthik tathaa audyogic ikaiyon ko niyantrit karne ke liye bahurashtriya nigamon kaa jaal bichaaya gaya he. apni aarthik shakti va kaaryakshetra main vrihata ke kaaran ye apane kshetradhikaar main aane vaale sabhi deshon main apni manamaani karne lagate hai. inkaa uddeshy adhik se adhik laabh kamanaa hota he. vikasshil deshon main ye bahurashtriya companiyaan pratyaksh puunji nivesh karke poonjivadi deshon ke liye kaccha maal tathaa praathmik utpaadan kii purti kii garanti dete hai. ye sarvaadhik nivesh praathmik utpaadan tathaa kaccha maal ke kshetra main karte hai. ye jivan main moolbhoot aavashyaktaaon kii vastuon ke nivesh main sarvaadhik munafa dekhte hai. vikasshil deshon main saste shram, kachche maal tathaa shoshan kii tivrata ke kaaran puunji nivesh se adhik munafa hota he. 1996 main bharat main 741 videshi companiyon ne nivesh kar rakhaa tha. bharat jaise vikasshil desh main bhi in companiyon ko kai guna laabh praapt hota he. apni puunji kii suraksha ke liye ye nigam apane niyantran vaale deshon ke aantarik maamalon main bhi hastkshep karte hai. ye companiyaan videshon main antarrashtriya puunji vyapaar, vaanijya tathaa utpaadan va vitaran par apana ekadhikaar karne ke liye kaary kar rahe hai. asia, africa tathaa latin america ke deshon par inkaa puraa niyantran he. IBM, GEC tathaa Standard Oil jaisi companiyaan in deshon se kaaphi munafa uthaa rahi hai. apane munaafe kii dar ko adhik se adhik oonche str par le jane ke liye ye apane adheen rashtron ke raajnneetik hastkshep karne se bhi nahin chookte. is prakaar bahurashtriya nigam nav-upniveshwaad ke sabase adhik shaktishaalee saadhan hai or inhen sabhi viksit rashtron kaa puraa samarthan praapt hota he.
 navdith swatantra raashtra apane aarthik vikaas ke liye purna rup se videshi sahaayta va samrajyavaadee karj par hi nirbhar hai. apane karj kii aad main tathaa videshi sahaayta ke naam par viksit raashtra in deshon ko manamaani sharten maanane ke liye baadhya kar dete hai. inhen karj lete samay anek kathor sharten bhi manani padati he. in deshon kaa karja din-pratidin badhata hi ja rahaa he. niryaat se praapt aay kaa adhiktar hissaa samrajyavaadee karj kaa byaaj chukaane main lag jaataa he or bhugtan santulan kaa ghaataa nirantar badhata hi jaataa he. unhen is karj kii raashi kaa prayog bahurashtriya companiyon se hi saamaan kharidne ke liye karnaa padataa he. unhen vyapaar avarodhon ko samaapt karne kii sharten bhi manani padati he. sabhi sharten puunji nivesh karne vaale deshon ke hiton kii poshak hoti hai. videshi sahaayta praapt karne vaale desh ko sadaiv niveshak yaa sahaayta pradaan karne vaale desh ke hiton main vruddhi karne ke liye apni arthvyavastha main moolbhoot parivartan karne padate hai. dhire-dhire samrajyavaadee karj or videshi sahaayta par vikasshil deshon kii nirbharta badhati hi jaati he. alp viksit yaa pichde hue deshon kii haalat to vikasshil deshon se bhi badtar hoti he. is prakaar kaha ja saktaa he ki videshi sahaayta tathaa samrajyavaadee karj kii hode main viksit desh nav-upniveshwaad kaa hi poshan kar rahe hai.
 aaj ke aanvik yug main prayek raashtra apane ko sainik va samrik drushti se surakshit dekhnaa chahta he. iske liye unhen viksit deshon se shastr kharidne padate hai. aarthik rup se kamjor hone ke kaaran ye desh kai baar karja bhi lete hai. aaj vikasshil deshon kii rashtriya aay kaa adhiktar hissaa hathiyaar kharidne par hi kharch ho rahaa he. mahashaktiyon dwaara kiya jane vaala shakti pradarshan shastr pratispardhaa ko badhaava detaa he. 1979 main mahashaktiyon kaa aapasi takaraav videshon main pahunchakar paryaapt laabh dilaae. in mahashaktiyon dwaara dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke baad kiye gaye shakti pradarshan inke shastr udyog main ai mandi ko kam karne main sahaayak siddh hue. aaj tritiy duniya ke adhiktar desh america, france, britain tathaa soviet sangh se hi hathiyaar kharidte hai. apni aupniveshik sthiti ko majboot banane ke liye samrajyavaadee deshon ne teesri duniya ke deshon main apane sainik adde khol rakhe hai. hathiyaaron kii purti ke rup main ye desh nav-upniveshwaad ko hi badhaava de rahe hai.
 aaj vishwa bank, antarrashtriya mudra kosh jaisi antarrashtriya sansthaaen antarrashtriya arthvyavastha kaa sanchaalan kar rahi hai. ye sansthaaen rin dete samay kathor sharten lagaakar nav-upniveshwaad kaa hi poshan karti hai. deshon ko diya jane vaala rin hameshaa raajnneetik pratibandhon par aadhaarit hota he. vah hameshaa viksit rashtron ke hiton kaa hi poshak hota he. isiilie tritiy vishwa ke desh nai antarrashtriya arthvyavastha kii purajor maang karte rahete hai. unka aarope he ki vishwa kii aarthik sansthaaen samrajyavaadee hiton ko poshit karne vaali he. ye nirantar pichde hue yaa vikasshil deshon ke aarthik va raajnneetik hiton par kutharaghaat karti hai. is tarah ye upniveshwaad ke nae rup ko badhaava de rahi hai.
 samrajyavaadee shaktiyaan aarthik sahaayta dekar pichde rashtron ko nirantar kamjor karti rahati he. vahaan ke aarthik va raajnneetik jivan main samrajyavaadee yaa niveshkarta desh kaa puraa hastkshep bad jaataa he. videshi takat ashrith desh ke vyapaar par apana purna niyantran rakhti he to unka niyantran dhire-dhire sthaayee rup praapt kar letaa he or yah nirbharta nirantar badhati hi rahati he or vah ant main upgrahi rajya kaa rup le letaa he. ab samrajyavaadee takat aarthik kshetra kii bajaay raajnneetik kshetra kii taraf apana dhyaan laga leti he. isse upgrahi raashtra ko apane aakaa kii har baat maanane ke liye baadhya hona padataa he. 1990 se pehle soviet sangh ne is prakaar kii niti kaa khulkar prayog kiya tha. iske peeche nav-upniveshwaad ke prasar kii hi bhaavana vidyaman rahati he.
 is prakaar kaha ja saktaa he ki aaj bhi nav-upniveshwaad ke rup main viksit raashtra alpaviksit tathaa vikasshil deshon par apana varchasv banaae hue hai. dakshin ke garib desh nirantar nav-upniveshwaad ke shoshan kaa shikaar ho rahe hai. uttar ke viksit desh bahurashtriya nigamon tathaa antarrashtriya aarthik sansthaaon ke madhyam se in deshon par apni pakad majboot banaae hue hai. bahurashtriya companiyaan vikasshil va pichde deshon main dimak kii tarah ghus rahi hai. adhiktar deshon main ye raajnneetik vyavhaar ko bhi prabhavit karne main samarth he. apane hiton kii purti ke liye viksit raashtra saanskrithik samrajyavad kaa bhi sahaara le rahe hai. shityudh kii samaapti ke baad sanyukt rajya america vishwa main apane aarthik yaa dollar samrajyavad kaa teji se vikaas kar rahaa he. vah aarthik jivan ke saath-saath vikasshil va pichde tritiy vishwa ke deshon ke raajnneetik kriya-kalapon main bhi hastkshep karne laga he. yadi uske ubharate nav-upniveshwaad ko na roka gaya to samast vishwa ke liye nayaa khatra paidaa ho jayega. aaj tritiy vishwa ke deshon ko ekajute hokar nav-upniveshwaad kii jaden ukhaadne kii jarurat he taaki nai antarrashtriya arthvyavastha kii sthapana ho sake.
