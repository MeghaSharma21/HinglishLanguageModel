 gandhakuti buddha, kevali yaa bhagwaan ke viraajane kaa sthaan. 
 moh kaa kshay hone se kaivalya kii prapti hoti he. tirthakar kevali ke liye indra vishaal jangan sabha kaa nirmaan karata he. samavasaran ke centre main ucch sthaan par bhagwaan ke liye kuti hoti he. isamen sadaiv malaychandan, kalagaru aadi jalte rahete hai atev ise gandhakuti kehete hai. saadhaaran kevliyon ke liye keval gandhakuti banati he. samavasaran ke pratik jain mandiron main gandhakuti ke sthaan par garbhagrih hota he tathaa murtiyan isi main rahati hai. mahatma buddha ke baithne ke sthaan ko bhi divyavdaan aadi main 'gandhakuti' naam se hi abhihit kiya gaya he. trilokprajripti main gandhakuti kaa varnan he. rishabhdev kii gandhakuti kii lambaai, chaudaai or oonchai kramash: 600, 600 or 900 dand thi. iske baad neminath paryant teenon main 25, 25 or saadhe 37 dand ghatate gaye. parswanath kii gandhakuti saadhe 62 lan., chau. or saadhe 93 unchi thi. mahavir swami kii 50, 50 or 75 dand, lambii chaudee unchi thi. sarnath main buddha kii bhi gandh kuti athwa mulgandhakuti thi.
