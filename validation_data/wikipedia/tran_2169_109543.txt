 hindi sahitya ke itihaas main lagbhag 8veen shatabdi se lekar 14veen shatabdi ke madhya tak ke kaal ko adikaal kaha jaataa he. is yug ko yah naam dow hajaari prasad dvivedi se mila he. acharya ramchandra shukla ne 'virgatha kaal' tathaa vishwanath prasad mishra ne ise 'virkaal' naam diya he. is kaal kii samay ke aadhaar par sahitya kaa itihaas likhane vaale mishra bandhuon ne iskaa naam praarambhik kaal kiya or acharya mahavir prasad dvivedi ne bijwapan kaal. dow ramkumar verma ne is kaal kii pramukh pravritiyon ke aadhaar par isko chaaran-kaal kaha he or rahul sankrutyaayan ne siddh-samant kaal.
 is samay kaa sahitya mukhyatah chaar roopon main miltaa he :
 yah sahitya us samay likhaa gaya jab hindi apbhransh se aadhunik hindi kii ore viksit ho rahi thi. buddha dharm kii vajrayan shakha ke anuyaayi us samay siddh kehalaate the. inaki sankhya churaasi maani gai he. sarahapa pratham siddh maane gaye hai. iske atirikt shabarapa, luipa, dombhipa, kanhpa, kukkuripa aadi siddh sahitya ke pramukh kavi he. ye kavi apni vaani kaa prachaar jan bhashaa main karte the. unki sahajia pravriti manushya kii swaabhaavik vritti ko centre main rakhkar nirdhaarit hui thi. is pravriti ne ek prakaar kii svachhndatha ko janm diya jisaki pratikriya main nath sampradaay shuru hua. nath-sadhu hathyog par vishesh bal dete the. ve yoga margi the. ve nirgun niraakaar ishwar ko maanate the. tathakthith nichi jatiyon ke logon main se kai pahunche hue siddh evam nath hue hai. nath-sampradaay main gorakhnath sabase mahatvapurn the. aapaki kai rachanaaen praapt hoti hai. iske atirikt chauranginath, gopichand, bharthri aadi nath panth ke pramukh kavi he. is samay kii rachanaaen saadharantah dohon athwa padon main praapt hoti hai, kabhi-kabhi chaupai kaa bhi prayog miltaa he. parvarti sant-sahitya par sidhdon or visheshkar naathon kaa gaharaa prabhaav padaa he.
 apbhransh kii jain-sahitya parampara hindi main bhi viksit hui he. bade-bade prabandhkavyon ke uparaant laghu khand-kavy tathaa muktak rachanaaen bhi jain-sahitya ke antargat aati hai. swayambhu kaa paum-chariu vastav main ram-katha hi he. swayambhu, pushpadant, dhanpal aadi us samay ke prakhyat kavi hai. gujarat ke prasiddh jainacharya hemchandra bhi lagbhag isi samay ke hai. jainon kaa sambandh rajasthan tathaa gujarat se vishesh rahaa he, isiilie anek jain kaviyon kii bhashaa pracheen rajasthani rahi he, jisase arvachin rajasthani evam gujarati kaa vikaas hua he. suriyon ke likhe ram-granth bhi isi bhashaa main uplabdh hai.
 iske antargat chaaran ke uparaant brahmabhatt or anya bandijan kavi bhi aate hai. saurashtra, gujarat or pashchimi rajasthan main chaarnon kaa, tathaa braj-pradesh, dilli tathaa purvi rajasthan main bhatton kaa praadhanya rahaa tha. chaarnon kii bhashaa saadharantah rajasthani rahi he or bhatton kii braj. in bhashaaon ko dingal or pingal naam bhi mili hai. ye kavi praayah raajaaon ke darbaron main rahakar unki prashasti kiya karte the. apane ashrayadata raajaaon kii atiranjit prashansa karte the. shringaar or vir unke mukhya ras the. is samay kii prakhyat rachanaaon main chandbardaai krit prithviraj raso, dalpati krit khumaan-raso, narpathi-naalh krit bisaldev raso, jaganic krit aalh khand aadi mukhya hai. inamen sarvaadhik mahatvapurn prithviraj raso he. in sab granthon ke bare main aaj yah siddh hua he ki unke kai ansh kshepak hai.
 khadi boli ke aadi-kavi amir khusro isi samay hue he. khusro kii paheliyaan or mukariyaan prakhyat hai. maithil-kokil vidyapati bhi isi samay ke antargat hue hai. vidyapati ke madhur padon ke kaaran inhen 'abhinav jayadev' bhi kaha jaataa he. maithili or avahtt main bhi inaki rachanaaen milti hai. inaki padaavalee kaa mukhya ras shringaar mana gaya he. abdul rahamaan krit 'sandesh raasak' bhi isi samay kii ek sundar rachana he. is chote se prem-sandesh-kavy kii bhashaa apbhransh se atyadhik prabhavit hone se kuch vidwaan isko hindi kii rachana na maanakar apbhransh kii rachana maanate hai.
 ashrayadaataaon kii atiranjit prashansaaen, yudhon kaa sundar varnan, shringaar-mishrit viraras kaa aalekhan vagairah is sahitya kii pramukh visheshtaayen hai. islam kaa bharat main pravesh ho chukaa tha. deshi rajavaade paraspar kalah main vyast the. sab ek saath milakar musalamaanon ke saath ladane ke liye taiyaar nahin the. parinaam yah hua ki alag-alag sabko haraakar musalmaan yaheen sthir ho gaye. dilli kii gaddi unhone praapt kar lee or kramshah unke rajya kaa vistaar badhane laga. tatkaaleen kavita par is sthiti kaa prabhaav dekha ja saktaa he.
 hindi kaa pratham kavi koun he, is par mataiky nahin he. vibhinn itihaaskaaron ke anusaar hindi kaa pehla kavi nimnlikhit hai-
