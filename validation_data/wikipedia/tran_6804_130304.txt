 
 curry pattey kaa ped ; anya naam: bergera koenijee,, chalkas koenijee ) ushnkaribandheey tathaa up-ushnkaribandheey pradeshon main paaya jane vaala rutasy parivaar kaa ek ped he, jo multah bharat kaa deshaj he. akasar rasedaar vyanjanon main istemaal hone vaale iske patton ko "curry patta" kehete hai. kuch log ise "mithi neem kii pattiyaan" bhi kehete hai. iske tamil naam kaa arth he, 'wo pattiyaan jinka istemaal rasedaar vyanjanon main hota he'. kannada bhashaa main iskaa shabdaarth nikalataa he - "kaala neem", kyonki isaki pattiyaan dekhne main kadave neem kii pattiyon se milti-julati hai. lekin is curry pattey ke ped kaa neem ke ped se koi sambandh nahin he. asal main curry patta, tej patta yaa tulsi ke patton, jo bumadhyasaagar main milanevaali khushboodaar pattiyaan hai, se bahut alag he.
 yah ped chota hota he jisaki unchai 2-4 meter hoti he or jiske tane kaa vyas 40 sein.me. tak hota he. isaki pattiyaan nukili hotin hai, har tahani main 11-21 patteedaar kamaniyaan hoti hai or har kamaani 2-4 sein.me. lambi va 1-2 sein.me. chaudee hoti he. ye pattiyaan bahut hi khushboodaar hotin hai. iske phool chote-chote, safed rang ke or khushboodaar hote hai. iske chote-chote, chamkile kaale rang ke fal to khaae ja sakate hai, lekin inke beej zahreele hote hai.
 is prajati ko vanaspatijna johaan kanig kaa naam diya gaya he.
 bundelkhand praant main yah patta curry yaa kari naamak vyanjan banane main bhi upyukt hota he.
 isaki pattiyon ko pis kar chatnee bhi taiyaar kii jaati he.
 dakshin bharat va pashchimi-tat ke rajyon or shri lanka ke vyanjanon ke chhaunk main, khaskar rasedaar vyanjanon main, bilkul tej patton kii tarah, isaki pattiyon kaa upyog bahut mahatva rakhata he. saadharantaya ise pakaane kii vidhi kii shuruyaat main kate pyaaz ke saath bhunaa jaataa he. iskaa upyog thoran, vada, rasm or curry banane main bhi kiya jaataa he. isaki taji pattiyaan na to khule main or na hi frij main zyaada dinon tak tazaa rahati hai. vaise ye pattiyaan sukhi hui bhi milti hai par unamem khushboo bilkul nahin ke baraabar hoti he.
 muraayaa koenijee kii pattiyon kaa ayurvedic chikitsa main jadi-booti ke rup main bhi istemaal kiya jaataa he. inke aushadhiy gunon main anti-diabetec, antioxident, antimicrobial, anti-inflemetary, hepatoprotective, anti-hypercholesterolemic ityadi shaamil hai.curry patta lambe or swasth baalo ke liye bhi bahut laabhakaari mana jaataa he.
 haalanki curry pattey kaa sabase adhik upyog rasedaar vyanjanon main hota he, par inke alaava bhi anya kai vyanjanon main masaale ke saath iskaa istemaal kiya jaataa he.
 paudhe ugane ke liye taaze bijon ko bona chahie, sookhe yaa murajhaaye falon main ankur-kshamta nahin hoti. fal ko yaa to sampoorn rup se gamale ke mishran main gaad dijiye or use geela nahin balki sirf nam banaae rakhiye.
