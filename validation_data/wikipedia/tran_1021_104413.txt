 bhartiya rashtriya congress ne bharat kii swatantraaa kii ghoshnaa 26 janvari 1930 ko kii. isi din purna aazaadee yani british shashan se alag swytt shashan kii ladaai ke liye prastaav paarit kiya gaya. congress adhyaksh jawaharlal nehru ne 31 disambar 1929 ko lahore main bharat kaa aadhikarik jhanda fahraaya. congress ne bharat kii janata se chhbbees janvari ko swatantraaa diwas ke rup main manaane kii apil kii. deshbhar main congress ke kaaryakartaaon, rashtravaadiyon or aamalogon ne rashtradhvaj fahraaya.
