 bharat main maut kii sazaa kuch gambhir aparaadhon ke liye di jaati he. bharat ke ucchtam nyaayalay ne 1995 ke baad 5 ghatnaon main maut kii sazaa di he jabaki 1991 se ab tak isaki kul sankhya 26 he.
 mithu banaam punjab rajya maamle main ucchtam nyaayalay ne bhartiya dand samhita kii dhara 303 ke tahat aaajeevan karawas kii sazaa kaat rahe kisi vyakti ko aavashyak rup se maut kii sazaa dene ko gaiyrkanoonee mana he. bharat main 1947 main swatantraaa ke baad maut kii sajaa praapt logon kii sankhya vivaadit he; adhikarik sarkari aankadon ke anusaar swatantraaa ke baad ab tak keval 52 logon ko phaansi kii sajaa di gayi he. yadhyapi pipuls union for civil liberties ke ek shodh ke anusaar yah sankhya bahut adhik he or iske anusaar keval 1953 se 1963 ke dashak main hi yah sankhya 1,422 he. neshnal lau uniwarsity, dilli ke ek shodh ke anusaar bharat main varsh 2000 se ab tak nichali adaalaton dwaara kul 1,617 kaidiyon ko maut kii sazaa sunai ja chuki he, jinamein se keval 71 maamalon main mrityu dand kii pushti keval 71 maamalon main hui. neshnal lau uniwarsity dilli ke anusaar 1947 se ab tak bharat main kul 755 logon ko mrityu dand diya ja chukaa he. neshnal lau uniwarsity, dilli kii jaanch main paaya gaya ki 1947 se swatantraaa ke baad ke baad uplabdh aparaadhiyon kii suchi main 1,414 kaidiyon ko phaansi par latakaana paaya gaya. bhartiya vidhi ayog kii ek rapat ke anusaar 1953 se 1963 tak mrityu dand ko praapt logon kii sankhya 1,410 he.
 disambar 2007 main, bharat ne maut kii sajaa par roak ke sanyukt raashtra mahasabha sankalp ke viruddh matdaan kiya tha. navambar 2012 main, maut kii sajaa ko pratibandhit karne ke liye rakhe gayi sanyukt raashtra mahasabha ke masaude ke viruddh matdaan karte huye apane faisle ko barkaraar rakhaa.
 31 agast 2015 ko, bharat ke vidhi ayog ne sarkaar ko ek prativedan saunpa jisamen deshdroh athwa aatanki aparaadhon ke atirikt anya aparaadhon ke liye maut kii sajaa ko samaapt karne kii sanstuti kii. is prativedan main maut kii sajaa ko samaapt karne ke liye vibhinn karkon ko uddhrut kiya gaya he jisamen 140 anya deshon main isaki samaapt kaa bhi ullekh he. yah ek trutipurna tathaa ekapaksheeya soch vaala prativedan tha tathaa isamen aparaadhiyon main bhay paidaa karne vaale kisi bhi prabhaav kaa ullekh nahin tha.
 aupniveshik bharat main, bhartiya dand samhita, 1860 ke anusaar maut kii sajaa ko nirdhaarit kiya gaya tha, jo vibhinn sangeen aparaadhon ke liye nirdhaarit thi. yah 1947 main desh kii swatantraaa ke baad bhi jari rahi. swatantra bharat main pehali maut kii sajaa 15 navambar 1949 ko mahatma gandhi kii hatya maamle main nathuram godse or narayan apte ko di gayi.
 bhartiya samvidhan ke anuchhed 21 ke anusaar vidhi dwaara nirdhaarit prakriya ke atirikt kisi bhi vyakti ko uske jivan se vanchit nahin kiya ja saktaa.
 bachan singh banaam punjab rajya main bharat ke ucchtam nyaayalay kii sanvaidhanik peeth ke faisle ke anusaar bharat main viral se virale maamalon main hi maut kii sajaa di ja sakati he. yah nirnay jagmohan singh banaam uttar pradesh rajya or uske baad rajendra prasad banaam uttar pradesh rajya purv nirnyon ke ullekh ke saath hua. bharat ke ucchtam nyaayalay ne rekhaankit kiya ki maut kii sajaa "virale se virale maamalon" main hi deni chahie. jabaki iske saath yah bhi kaha ki sammaan ke liye hatya "virale se virale" shrenee main hi aati he, nyaayalay ne isaki sanstuti kii ki "sammaan ke liye hatya" ke maamalon main maut kii sajaa badhaayaa ja saktaa he. ucchtam nyaayalay ne un police adhikariyon ke liye bhi maut kii sajaa sunai jo farji muthbhed ke maamalon main barbarta failaate hai.
 vikram singh or mrityu-dand kii sajaa praapt anya vyakti ne 2013 main bhartiya panel samhita ke anuchhed 364e kii sanvaidhanik vaidhata par sawaal uthaate huye apil daayar kii.
 bhartiya dand samhita ke saath-saath bhartiya sansad dwaara kanoonon kii nayi shrunkhlaa adhiniyamit kii gayi jinamein maut kii sajaa kaa praavdhaan he.
 hatya
 iske liye uksaana chaalu karanethva ladaai karnaa.
 ek satr nyaayalay dwaara maut kii sajaa dene ke baad, ucch nyaayalay dwaara isaki antim pushti honi chahie. pushti ke baad, apradhi ko sarvocch nyaayalay se apil karne kaa vikalp hota he. agar yah sambhav nahin he, yaa yadi sarvocch nyaayalay yachika ko sunakar yaa apil ko asweekaar kar detaa he, to apradhi vyakti bharat ke raashtrapati or rajya ke rajyapal ko dayaa yachika prastut kar saktaa he. 
 aitihaasik drushti se raashtrapati or rajyapal ko yah adhikar bharat sarkaar adhinium, 1935 se aate he. par bharat ke samvidhan ke khand 72 ke tahat hi in adhikaron kaa istemaal kiya ja saktaa he.
