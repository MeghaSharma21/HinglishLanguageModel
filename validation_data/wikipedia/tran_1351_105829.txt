 yah ruparekha main badlaav kaa ijaajat detaa he. iske alaava yah 8 shrenee main iske adhikaar diye gaye hai. isamen anuprayog dwaara apane aap kisi ko koi adhikaar dene jaise suvidha nahin he. jab anuprayog ko kisi suvidha kaa upyog karnaa hoga jaise camere aadi kaa to use pehle aapako ek or anumati deni hogi. jabaki yah suvidha pehle ke sanchaalan pranali ke sanskaran main pehle sthapit karne ke samay hoti thi. jisamen jis anuprayog ko chahen use us tarah kii anumati de sakate hai or jab chahen tab us anumati ko hataa sakate hai. lekin ab aapako kaary ke samay hi anumati deni hogi.
 is tarah kii anumati kii suvidha keval nai banaai gai anuprayog main hi milegi. puraane vaale anuprayog ke liye usee tarah shuruuaat main hi anumati dene kii aavashyaktaa he.
