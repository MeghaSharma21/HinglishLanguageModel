 bannu pakistan ke kaibar-pakhtuunakva praant kaa ek zila he. yah karak zile ke dakshin main, lakki maravat zile ke uttar main or uttar wazeeristaan naamak qabaili kshetra ke purv main sthit he. is zile ke mukhya shahar kaa naam bhi bannu he. yahan bahut-si shushk pahadiyaan hai haalanki vaise is zile main bahut hariyaali dikhaai detii he or yahan kii dharti bahut upjau he. british raaj ke jamaane main yahan ke qudarti soundarya se prabhavit hokar bannu kii 'swarg' se tulana bhi kii jaati thi.
 bannu zile main san 1998 main 6,77,346 logon ki aabaadi thi. iskaa kshetrafal qareeb 1,227 varg kimi he. yahan pani mukhya taur par wazeeristaan kii pahadiyon se utpann hone vaali kurram nadi or gambila nadi se aata he. bannu pahari ilaaka he jiske bich main 100 kimi lambi or 60 kimi chaudee bannu vaadi vistrut he. kurram nadi zile ke pashchimottar kone se zile main daakhil hokar pehle to dakshin-purv or phir mudakar sidhaa dakshin kii ore chalakar lakki maravat zile main nikal jaati he. tochi nadi kurram se 6 kimi dakshin par zile main aati he or phir vah bhi kurram ke baraabar chalakar lakki maravat main chali jaati he, jahaan kurram main mil jaati he. in donon nadiyon ke bich kaa doaab hi bannu zile kaa sab se upjau kshetra he.
 sanskrit ke prasiddh vaiyaakaran panini ne sab se pehle chauthi shatabdi isapurv main bannu kaa zikr kiya tha or uskaa pracheen naam 'varnu' bataaya tha. saatvin sadi isvi main bhartiya upmahadweep aaye chini dharmayatri hwain tsang ne bhi bannu kaa daura kiya or vartmaan afghanistan main sthit gazani kii nagari tak gaye. parsi dharmagranth avesta main bhi bannu ko 'varan' ke naam se bulaayaa gaya or kaha gaya kii ahur mazd dwaara duniya main banaae gaye 16 sarvotam jagahon main se yah ek he. itihaaskaaron ko bannu ke akara naamak kshetra main maujuud tilon main atipracheen sindhu ghati sabhyataa ke avshesh mili hai or madhya asia se aaye bahut se hamalaavaron dwaara chode gaye tarah-tarah ke chihn bhi praapt hue hai.
 jab punjab se sikh samrajya failaa to bannu zila bhi uskaa bhaag ban gaya, haalanki yah ek pashtun ilaaka he. jab angrezon ne punjab ko british raaj kaa hissaa banaaya, to bannu bhi usamen shaamil kiya gaya. yahan ke faujee addon se sena kii tukadiyaan aksar tochi ghati or wazeeristaan ke qabaili kshetron main samay-samay par qaabu paane ke liye bheji jaati thim. british jamaane main hi fuj ke prayog ke liye deraa gaazi khan se bannu tak ek sadak taiyaar kii gai.
