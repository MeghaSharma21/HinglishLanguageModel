 ”’rani”’ rajasthan rajya ke paali jile kii desuri tehsil kaa ek kasbaa he.
 vartmaan main yahaan nagarpalika adhyaksh ghisulal choudhary he, jo ki agast 2015 main janata dwaara nirvaachit he. 2012 se rani panchayat samiti kshetra ko tehsil bana diya gaya hai vartmaan main shri vishanaraam devada tehseeldaar ke pad par karyrat he.rajya sarkaar ke adeshon ke anusaar rani kaa upkhand ghoshit kar diya gaya hai. rani panchayat samiti kshetra main kul 29 gram panchayatein hai.rani main shri parswanath jain vidhyalay varkana 1927 se shiksha ke kshetra main kaayr kar rahaa hai vartmaan main fatechand ranavat bijova adhyaksh hai
