 gol gumbaz yaa gol gumbad, farsi گل گنبذ bijapur ke sultan mohammad adil shah kaa makbara he or bijapur, karnataka main sthit he. isko farsi vaastukar dabul ke yakuut ne 1656 i0 main nirmaan karavaaya tha. haalanki mul rup main saadhaaran nirmaan hone par bhi apni sthapaty visheshtaon ke kaaran dackhin vaastukala kaa vijay stambh mana jaataa he. 
 isaki sanrachana ke mul main 47.5 meter kii bhujaaon vaala ek ghan he, jiske uprastha 44 me bahari vyas vaala ek vishaal gumbad he. do samaan.   kone pa ghoomte hue chaturbhujon se banane vaale ek-duusare ko katate hue aath mehrabon se guntha hua gumbadeey tribhuj-kone banataa he jo is gumbad ko uthaaye hue hai. is ghan ke chaaron konon par gumbadnuma chhatari se dhanke hue ashtakoniya sapt-taleey attalikaayen yaa minaaren bani hai. inke andar seedhiyaan bhi hai. in prayek minaaron ke upree tal bade gumbad ko gherte hue galiyare main khulata he. makbare ke mukhya haul ke bheetar chaaron ore sidhiyon se ghira hua ek chaukor chabutara he. is chabutare ke madhya ek kabr kaa pathar he, jiske neeche isaki asal kabr bani he. adil shahi vansh ke makbaron main ye is prakaar kaa ekmaatr udhaaharan he. uttari ore ke madhya main, ek vrahat ardh-ashtakoniya aakaar baahar ko nikalataa he. 1,700 me2, kshetrafal vaala yah makbara vishwa kaa sabase bada ekal-kaksh or bina kisi madhya aadhaar vaala nirmaan he.
 makbare ke gumbad ke aantarik paridhi par ek golakaar galiyara bana hua he, jise angrezon ne "whisparing gailaree" arthath fusfusaane vaala galiyara naam diya he. is galiyare ke nirmaan main prayukt dhwani-vigyaan ke vaastu main sammilan ke kaaran yahaan dhime se fusfusaaya hua ek shabd bhi iske vyas ke thik duusari ore ekadam spasht sunai detaa he.
