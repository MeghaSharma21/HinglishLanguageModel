 kurmali bhashaa jharkhand kii ek pramukh bhashaa he. yah ek antar-prantiy bhashaa he. iskaa vistaar kshetra "udishya shikhar, nagpur, aadha-aadhi khadgpur" lokokti se gyaat hota he. kurmali ke kshetra rajanithik maanchitr dwaara parisimit nahin kiya ja saktaa. yah keval chotanagpur main hi nahin, balki urisa main kyonjhar, boni, bamda, mayurganj, sundargarh, pashchim bengal ke antargat purulia, midanapur, bankura, malda, dinajpur ke simavarti ilaakon, jo bihar se sate hai, evam chotanagpur ke ranchi, hazaribagh, giridih, dhanbad, singhbhum, evam, bihar ke bhagalpur ilaake va santhalpargane main bhi boli jaati he. yah bhashaa keval kurmio tak hi simit nahin, balki inke saath nivas karne vaale anya jatiyon ke bhav-vinium kaa bhi saadhan he. yah mukhyatah devanagari lipi main likhi jaati he, parantu iske sahitya bangla or oriya main bhi uplabdh he.
 kurmali bhashaa kaa namakaran chotanagpur kii anya bhashaaon- mundaari, santali, ho, khadiya kii bhaatin kurmi jaati ke naam par hua he. yah ‘kurm’ shabd main ‘aali’ pratyay lagaakar bana he. kurmali bangla, odissi or asamiya kii bhaanti magadhi apbhransh prasut he or inhi kii tarah ek nishchit bhashaa he. bihari varg kii bhashaaon se iskaa prayaapt samya parilakshith hota he. iname anek samaantaaon ke baavjood kurmali kii apni nijee visheshtayen hai jo anya magadhi-prasut bhashaao se pruthak karti he. kurmali kii dhvanigat visheshtayen, muhaavarey, lokoktiyaan, sur, taal, lay, vividh geet iske parthaky ke aadharbhoot kaaran hai. isamen lok-geeton, lok-kathaon, lokoktiyon or paheliyon kii sankhya bahul hai. kurmali loksahitya main lokgeeton ke baad lok-kathaon, paheliyon, or lokoktiyon kaa sthaan he. in lokgeeton, lok-kathaon evam lokoktiyon main kurmali jivan kii chhap spasht parilakshith hoti he. in vidhaon kii vividh rachanaaon se gyaat hota he ki kurmali sahitya jitna samridh he, bhav- soundarya kii drushti se bhi utana hi utkrisht.
 adhyayan-adhyaapan kii drushti se kurmali loksahitya ko teen kaalon main vibhajit kiya ja saktaa he. 
 iske antargat kurmali ke lok-geeton- dhap, biha, damakach, sarhul, natua, vandana, karam, edheia, udhwa, kuanrijupan, dhabaka, maharaai, ityadi lok-kathaye, lokoktiyaan, or paheliyaan hai, jinke rachayita agyaat hai.
 madhyakaal main kurmali ke janakaviyon main vinand singh, gaurangiya, barajuram, narottama, pitambar, dina, udai, khep, bogha, ramkrisht, trilokya nath mandal, handiram, dima, jagaa, haldhara ko rakhaa gaya he.
 is kaal ke prasiddh kaviyon main sristidhar, mahipal, bhavaprita, maheem, shrikant, kashinath, haraadhan, lakhikant, keshavachandra, maansingh, anant keshariar, sunil, jagannatha, rameshwar ityadi hai. chunki kurmali ek antarprantiy bhashaa he, atah aadhunik kaal main devanagari, banglaa, or oriya lipi main sahitya kaa bharpoor vikaas hua he.
 yon to kurmali shisht sahitya kaa prarambh kurmali ke madhya kaal se man jaataa he, parantu iskaa utkrisht kaal san 1900 i. se hi he. sarvapratham grearsan mahoday ne san 1903 i. main “bharat kaa bhashaa surveykshan” main kurmali bhashaa ke vyakaranik visheshtaon kaa vistrut evam vaigyanik vivechan prastut kiya. san 1921 main swa. niranjan mahato ne geet-sangrah “nethapala”, san 1970 main shri budhu mahato ne “karam-geet” prakashit karai. san 1953 main shri rajendra prasad mahato ne swarchit pustak “kapila mangal” prakashit karai. isi varsh shri chakravarti devaki nandan prasad kii do pustaken “na dhare aankhi jal aankhi pate” tathaa “kete akuha bhashaa, kete uttala aasa” prakashit huin. san 1956 ke aas-paas swa. raja upendra nath singh ne vinandan aadi kaviyon vinandan singh or gaurangiya ke geeton kaa sangrah kar “aadi jhumar sangeet” or or “taal-manjari” naamak do pustaken prakashit karaayi. san 1958 main da. vishwanath prasad evam shri sudhakar jha shastri ne “linguistic survey of manabhoon and dhalbhum” main kurmali bhashaa ke geeton ke saath-saath vyakaranik visheshtaon par prakaash daalaa. iske purv san 1957-58 main apane laghu shodh prabandh ke liye “chotanagpur ke kurmali lokgeeton kaa adhyayan” main lagbhag 300 geeton kaa sankalan prastut kiya. phir inhone hi san 1962 main “kurmali boli: ek bhashaa tathvik adhyayan” namak shodh-patr likhakar pi.ech.di. kii oopaadhi lee. san 1975 i. ke baad se abtak anekanek gadhya-padya kavy-grantho kaa prakaashan ho chukaa he. 1982 i. ke pashchaat kurmali kurmali sahitya vishay-vastu kii drushti se ek nayaa mode le rahaa he. jise ham kurmali ke aadhunik sahitya hi kah sakate hai. kurmali lok sahitya ke sangrah or aadhunik vyakhya ke sambandh main kai lekhak or kaviyon ne mahatvapurn yogadan diya he jiname sarvashree ech. en.singh, shashibhushan mahato, da. parameshwari pra. mahato, vrindavan mahato, da.santosh kumari jain, da. nandakishor singh, shyaam sundar mahato, aadi pramukh hai.
 saadharantah kurmali sahitya ko do bhaagon main vibhakt kiya ja saktaa he:- kurmali lok sahitya or kurmali shisht sahitya.
 lok sahitya main un saahitiyon ko rakhte hai, jinke udgam, kaal-nirdhaaran, yaa lekhak sambandhi koi nishchit anumaan lagaana sambhav nahin he. yah sahitya un kurmi sabhyataa ke us viksit paramparaagat gyaan or sahitya kaa sankalan hota he jo kii peedi-dar-peedi sadiyon se lok-katha, lok-geet, aadi main abtak kurmali-bhashi logo ko viraasat main mili he. kurmali lok-sahitya kii jo samagriyan milti hai, unhen ham mote taur par paanch virgon main rakh sakate hai:- 
 kurmali main lokgeeton kii sankhya bahul he. aaj bhi ye geet lok-mukh main jivit he. geet atyant saras or marmasparshi he. kurmali geeton kii parampara ati pracheen he. koi bhi anushthan geet evam nrity ke bina sampann nahin hota. adhikansh geet nritygit hai. raag ke dwaara hi geeton ke parthaky or vaishishty ko samajha ja saktaa he. kurmali jivan ke harek pahalu, vividh drushtikon or bahuaiami vichaar-dhaaraon ko kurmali loakgeet sansparsh karata he. kurmali lok-geeton kii pramukh visheshta yah he ki adhikansh geet prashnottar ke rup main hai. chand-vidhaan ke niyam se purnatah mukt he. iske apane chand hai jo gey hai. geeton ke le dwaara hi shailigat tatv ko pahachaanaa ja saktaa he. kahin-kahin swatah alankaaro kaa prayog hua he. kurmali lokgeeton ko kai virgon main vargeekrut kiya ja saktaa he. jaise sanskaar-geet, ritu-geet, devi-devataaon ke geet, shram-geet, khel-geet, jaati-geet, prabandh geet aadi. kurmali ke pramukh loakgeet hai: udhwa, dhap, biha, damakach, sarhul, natua, diddhara, java-karam, edheia, dabaka, bandana, kuwanri- jhupaan aadi. saadharantah kurmali ke lok-geeton main prakruti kaa chithran prayaapt maatra main miltaa he. karam geeton main ankuroday, punarvivaah, krishi-karm, hasya-vyangy, jivan-yaapan kii paddhiti kaa ullekh miltaa he. vivaah geeton main kurmali samskruti, saamaajik vyavhaar, khan-paan, dainik karm, rahan-sahan kii chhap parilakshith hoti he. kurmali samaaj main kanya kaa mahatvapurn sthaan he. jis prakaar van kii shobhaa pushp he, usee prakaar ghar kii shobhaa kanya hoti he. kurmali ke kuwanri jhupaan geeton main jaadu-tona, tantra-mantra evam andh-vishwaas kaa varnan miltaa he. diddhara, dhap aadi geeton main ucch koti kaa darshanik bhav jhalkata he. vahiin damakach geeton main bhav-saundarya ke saath-saath hasya kaa put bhi miltaa he.
 kurmali main lok-kathaon kaa vishaal bhandar miltaa he. saadharantah lok-kathao kaa mukhya uddeshy manoranjan karnaa hi rahaa he, parantu ye ek purna viksit bhashaa se kam shikshaprad, darshanik bhav se ot-prot hai. log raat ko ghar main khaliyaan ke kumba yaa dhandhaura ke chaaro ore jaade kii lambi raat kaatane ke liye kahaniyaan kehete hai. kurmali lok-kathao main alokik or asambhav batoo kii bharmaar rahati he. ek ullekhneey baat yah he ki jo lok-kathayen america, europe evam hamaare desh ke anya lok-bhashaao main prachlit he, thodaa-bahut antar ke saath kurmali main bhi uplabdh he. kurmali ke lok-kathaon ko mukhya rup se teen virgon main baanta ja saktaa he: 1.	dharmik lok-kathaayen- yatha ‘karma-dharma’, teen thakurine, aar-ni-raham, aadi. 2.	shiksha-prad lok-kathaayen- yatha buddhik-daam, ricus-aar-amari, garkhiya-aar-rajkumaari, atukaara-raja, moonga-moti, karam-kapad, aadi. 3.	manoranjan pradhan lok-kathaayen- yatha bouna, baadaa-siyaar, puitu-boodhaa, siarek-chouchhaali, benia-aar-charvaaha aadi
 is prakaar ham is nishkarsh main aate hai ki kurmali lokgeeton main jivan ke vibhinn pahaluon kaa yatharth or sajiv chithran hua he.
 hamaare desh main lok-natya kii parampara bahut pracheen he. yadhyapi lok-natyo main naatak ke sabhi tatv nahin milte he, phir bhi naatak ke kuch na kuch tatv to avashya hi milte hai. vastutah saahitiyik naatak ke niv ye lok-natya hi hai, isamen koi andeh nahin kurmali ke pramukh lok-natya hai:-i.	chau nrityaaii.	natua nritydhiii.	damakach auraiv.	machhaani.
 i.	 chau nrity:- is nrity main nartak apane munh par mukhauta dhaaran karata he or apane ang-pratyang ke haav-bhav dwaara bhaavo ko abhivyakt karata he. katha-wastu ke rup main pehle geet gaya jaataa he. geet ke dwaara hi drishy bhi badla jaataa he. yah mausami nrity he. parantu, apni prachur lokapriyata ke kaaran ab yah nrity deshi akhaade se nikalakar antarrashtriya rangamanch par aa gaya he.
 ii.	natua nrity:- yah kurmali kaa bahut purana nrity he. geeton ke dwara kathavstu kaa parichay diya jaataa. yah vir ras kaa nrity he. nartak apane sharir ko rangeen feeton se susajjit karata he or apane hatho main dhaal or talwaar lekar nrity karata he. uski bhangima yuddh kii stithi kii hoti he. is nrity ko dekh koi aparichit darshak rajasthani rajputon ke nrityon se tulana kar saktaa he.
 iii.	damakach:- yah vivaah ke avasar par hone vaala nrity he. jab var vivaah karne ke liye chalaa jaataa he to striyaan mardon ke poshaak pahanakar nrity karti hai. kabhi-kabhi ve vivaah kaa abhinay bhi karti to kabhi-kabhi kisi anya kathavstu ko lekar abhinay karti hai. geet prashnottar ke rup main hote hai, jise ham cathope-kathan style kah sakate hai.
 iv machhaani:- lok nrity main stri-purush donon hi bhaag lete hai. geeton ke dwaara ek dusre ko prashn karte hai. geet hi isaki kathavstu he. bich-bich main gadhya-style kaa prayog hota he. is nrity main drishy bhi badalte hai. kathavstu vyangyatmak style main hoti he.
 kisi bhi bhashaa kaa sahitya us samaaj kaa darpan hota he. lok-kahavatein or lok-lokoktiyaan us samaaj, jati ke aachaar-vichaar, reeti-rivaaj, jivan-darshan, haas-parihaas, jivan-paddati, vyavsaay aadi kii abhivyakti va digdarshit hoti he. jharkhand ke kurmiyon kaa mukhya pesha krishi he. kurmali-lokoktiyon main krishi kaa sambandh spasht jhalkata he. in lokoktiyon main mukhyatah acchhee fasal hone ke sanket, vinaash ke kaaran, avasar tathaa stithi kaa digdarshan hota he. in sab ke atirikt isamen hasya, vyangy, lalkaar, chetavanee, soochna, sukhtiyon ke dwaara gudh baton kii shiksha di jaati he. kurmali lokoktiyon main aachaar-vichaar, reeti-rivaaj, aarthik, dharmik, rajanithik, saanskrithik jivan kii abhivyanjana milti he.

 kurmali lok-bhashaa main paheliyon kaa prachur bhandar he. gramin janata krishi kaary se mukt hokar saayam-kaal main gaanv ke kisi saamudayik jagah par ikaitha hote hai, bujurg apane gyaan or anubhav ko baantate hai. saath hi baccho, va navayuvakon se vibhinn prakaar kii paheliyaan puch kar ek-duusare kaa manoranjan karte hai. isse na keval logon kaa manoranjan yavan gyaan-vardhan hota he balki bacchon ke tark-kshamta ke saath swasth maansik vikaas hota he. kurmali bhashaa ke kuch pramukh paheliyaan or unka hindi anuwaad:-1.	dungri uparen pilei gachh bin bataanse hilei gachh - .2.	lack-lack dandi chak-chak paat, khaitake madhuras ulgeit ke kapaas - .3.	uluk gadaa dhuluk chaanpe, kaath khaike sindur haage- .4.	sir re sitaka bhuiyen pataka - .5.	duudaku upar bhootku nachei - .6.	dubi-dubi jaai puunchhe chaara khai - 
 madhya-kaal se shisht sahitya kaa pradurbhav mana ja saktaa he. san 1485 i. ke aas-paas chaitanya mahaprabhu ke padaarpan jharkhand pradesh main hone -se vaishnav-bhaavana kaa prachaar-prasar hua. tatkaaleen kaviyon ne ramayana or mahabharat se katha-vastu lekar geeton kii rachana kii. ram-krishna ko apana nayak maanakar unki lilaon ko lalit-padon main rachakar geeton kaa rup diya.
 madhya kaal ke kurmali lokkaviyon main baraju ram, narottama, gaurangiya, duryodhana, pitambar, dina, ramkrishna, vinand singh aadi pramukh the. is yug ke kaviyon kii rachanaaon main alankaar, tuk or chand kaa bhi sachet prayog hua he.
