 ashwashakti ) shakti kii maapan ikai he. yah ek gaiyr-SI ikai he. ashwashakti ke kai maanak hai or kai prakaar bhi hai.
 'horsepower' shabd kaa sabase pehle upyog 18veen shatabdi ke antim kaal main scotland ke ejeeniyar james wat ne kiya. usne vaashp injanon se milane vaali shakti kii tulana ashv kii khinchne kii shakti se kii. baad main horsepower shabd kaa upyog pistonyukt anya injanon tathaa terbine, vidyut motaron, evam anya mashiinon kii shakti ke liye bhi kiya jane laga. kintu is shabd kii paribhaasha bhinn-bhinn kshetron main bhinn-bhinn thi. vartmaan samay main adhikansh deshon main shakti ke maapan ke liye wat naamak es ai maatrak kaa prayog kiya jaataa he.
