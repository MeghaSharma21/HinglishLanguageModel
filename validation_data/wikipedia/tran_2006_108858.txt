 lalit tripura sundari devisda saubhagyawatinam
 maharani lalit tripura sundariko tasvir
 maharani lalit tripura sundari vaa lalita kumari thapa nepalke raja ran bahadur shaahake choti or baadamein pramukh patni thi.  unako nepali sahityake pratham mahila srashtaake rupame mana jaataa hai. unhone sanskritse “rajdharm” ko anuwaad kiya tha or kachach kavitaae likhi thi. isliye wo nepalke pratham mahila sahityakar maani jaati hai. unke preranaase sautela putr nepalke raja girvana yuddh vikram shah or naati rajendra vikram shahne bhi 3 pustak likhe the. unke pustak rajdharmako bhashaa or shailike liye itihasvidane utkrisht mana hai. 
 unke janm pita kaaji nainsingh thapa or mata rankumari pandey ko vikram samvat 1850 main hua tha. unke pardada sardaar virbhadra thapake gorkha pipalthok ke sthani bagaale thapa gharanake hai. unke bhaai mathaverasingh thapa baadamein nepalke pratham pradhanmantri and commander-in-chiff ban gaye.
