 bateshwar uttar pradesh agra jile main sthit ek gaanv he. ise 'bhadaavar ke kaashi' ke naam se janaa jaataa he. treta yug main yah 'shur sanpur' ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. bateshwar ke ghat main prasiddh ghat aaj bhi 'kansh kagaar' ke naam se janaa jaataa he. bhadaavar ke bhadauria maharaajaaon ne yahan 108 mandiron kaa nirmaan kar is pavitra sthaan ko tirth main badal diya.
 atharahavin sadi main aurangzeb kii mandiron ko dhvansh kar ke islamic rajya sthapit karne ke prayaas kaa dirghakaalik prabhaav hindu pahchaan kii bhaavana badhaata ja rahaa tha. bateshwar shahar ke nirmaan main hindu bhakthi ke mahatv kaa ek shandaar udhaaharan he. bhadaavar ke raja dwaara 1720 or 1740 ke bich kaa 108 mandiron kaa nirmaan karaayaa gaya tha I
