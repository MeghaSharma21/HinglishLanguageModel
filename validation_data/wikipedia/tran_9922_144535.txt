 sanhmaarg bharat kaa hindi bhashaa kaa pramukh samachar patr he jo varanasi, kolkata, patna, ranchi evam bhubaneshwar se prakashit hota he. iske sansthaapak swami karpatri ji the. nandananand saraswati iske sampadak the. yah akhil bhartiya raamarajy parishad ke mukhpatr ke rup main shuru hua tha.
 sanhmaarg kalakatta se 18 april 1948 ko prakashit hua. prabud varg kaa aakarshan is patr ke prati adhik rahaa. visheshat: jab yah patr pan anant prasad mishra ke sampaadan main niklaa tha, uskaa raajnneetik hi nahin saahitiyik str bhi unnat hua. `sanhmaarg' ke vartmaan sampadak hai-ram avatar gupt. pehle iskaa prakaashan swami karpatri ji ke aashirwad se varanasi se hua tha or baad main kalakatta se sanskaran niklaa. aajkal kalakatta sanskan hi mukhya patr ban gaya he.
