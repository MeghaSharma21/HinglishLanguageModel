 funas oken, 1816 lophotus fisher, 1813 mecrobates bilberg, 1828 satyras lasan, 1840
 orangutan asia main vartmaan yug main paaya jane vaala akelaa manavnuma he. yah sabhi vrikshavasi jaanwaron main aakaar main sabase bade hai or inaki bhujaayen sabhi anya mahakapiyon se lambi hoti hai. yah kaafii budhimaan hote hai or bhinn prakaar ke auzaar istemaal karte hai. har raat ko yah dandiyon or patton se apane liye ek shayya-si bhi taiyaar karte hai. jahaan anya mahakapiyon ke bal kaaki yaa kaale hote hai, vahaan orangutanon ke bal laal-se rang ke hote hai.
 orangutan indonisia or malayasia ke jangalon main paae jate hai. inaki do upjatiyaan hai - bornian orangutan or sumatraai orangutan . sumatraai orangutan ab bahut kam bache hai or inaki nasl lupt hone kaa bada khatara he. orangutan kii or bhi jatiyaan hua karti thim lekin ve ab khatm ho chuki hai, jin main se ek shivapithekas naamak nasl bharat kii shivalik pahadiyon main vaas karti thi. 
 orangutan shabd malay bhashaa se aayaa he or iskaa arth he vanamaanush .
 orangutan chidyagharon main 60 saal tak jite hai or mana jaataa he ke jangalon main swatantra rup se yah lagbhag 35 varshon tak jite honge, haalanki iskaa pakkaa anumaan abhi tak nahin lagaaya gaya he.
