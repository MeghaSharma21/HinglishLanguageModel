  multan zila, pakistan ke punjab praant kaa ek zila he. iskaa prashasnic mukhyalay, multan shahar he. is zile kaa kul kshetrafal 3,720 he, tathaa varsh 1998 kii janaganana ke anusaar, isaki kul jansankhya 3,116,851 thi. yahan bole jane vaali pramukh bhashaa punjabi he, jabaki urdu praayah har jagah samajhi jaati he. saath hi angrezi bhi adhikansh shahari kendron main samajhi jaati he. prabhukh prashasnic bhashaayen urdu or angrezi he.
