 parijaat ek nepali lekhika thim. unka asli naam vishnu kumari wiba tha. srijan ke dauraan ve apane naam ke saath upnaam ke rup main parijaat kaa prayog kiya karte the. dhire-dhire unka yah naam nepali sahitya main mile kaa pathar banataa chalaa gaya. unki rachana siris ko phool sarvaadhik charchit rachanaaon main se ek he, jo bharat, nepal sahit kuch angreji bhashi deshon main kuch collejon ke sahitya ke paathyakram main roopantarit kiya gaya he.
 parijaat darjeeling ke pahari stesan, jo apni chaay baganon ke liye vikhyath he, main 1937 main paidaa huyi thim. jab vah kaaphi choti thi tab unki maan amrit moktan kaa dehavsaan ho gaya. aisi paristhiti main parijaat kii parvarish unke naanaa dow ke.en.wiba dwaara kii gayi, jo ek manovaigyanik the.
 jahaan parijaat kaa janm hua vah shahar darjeeling us samay nepali bhashaa, samskruti or sahitya kaa ek pramukh centre tha. haalanki aaj bhi darjeeling nepali logon kaa nivas he or nepali bhashaa, samskruti tathaa sahitya ke ek pramukh centre ke rup main janaa jaataa he. nepal ke saath ek kareebi rishta saajha karne ke saath-saath yah shahar sahitya ke vikaas main bhi ek prabhaavshaalee bhoomika nibhaai he. yahi kaaran he, ki tamaam jatiltaaon ke vabjood parijaat kaa bachapan nepal or nepali sahitya se judaa rahaa or ek shashakt lekhika ke rup main unhen apni pahchaan banane main unki madad karata rahaa. 
 parijaat ne darjeeling main apni schuli shiksha kaa hissaa puraa karne ke baad 1954 main kathmandu, nepal chali aayi, jahaan padma kanya skool main unhone apni schuli shiksha puri kii or arts main snaatak kii degree lee. 26 saal kii umr main, jaldi hi shaareerik rogon se peedit hone ke kaaran ve aage kii shiksha praapt nahin kar sakin. unke lakwagrast ho jane ke kaaran unki dekharekh lambey samay tak unki bahan ne kiya.
 parijaat kii pehali kavita 1959 main, dharth dwaara prakashit kiya gaya tha. aakanksha, parijaat kii kavita or bisalu vartmaan sahit unke teen kavita sangrah prakashit he. unki pehali laghu kahani maile najanamaeko choro thi. ve nepal main ek shashakt upanyaaskaar ke rup main jaani jaati he. unhone das upanyaas likhe, jinaame se siris ko phool sarvaadhik lokapriy rachanaaon main se ek he. unhen 1965 main isi upanyaas ke liye madan puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha. iske alaava unhen sarvshereshth pandulipi puraskaar, gandaki basunbdhara puraskaar aadi se bhi alankrita kiya gaya. siris ko phool unke dwaara nepali sahitya ke mahatvapurn srijan main se ek mana jaataa he.
 ve tribhuvan vishvavidhyalay ke nirvaachit sadsy or ralafaa sahitya andolan kaa ek hissaa thi. unhone pragatisheel esael lekhan sangh kii sthapana main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai or akhil nepali mahila manch, bandi sahaayta niyog or nepal maanav adhikaar sanghathan ke liye bhi kaam kiya. ve avivaahit raheen or shaareerik viklaangata ke vabjood srujandharmita ko lagaataar jari rakhaa. 1993 main unki mrityu ho gai.
