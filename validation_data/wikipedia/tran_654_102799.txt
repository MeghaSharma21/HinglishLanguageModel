 belingshousan sagar antarctic prayadweep ke pashchimi tat par 57°18' pashchim or 102°20' pashchim rekhansho ke bich sthit sagar he. yah akexzander dweep se pashchim main, tharston dweep ke flaaing fish antareep se purv or peter pratham dweep se dakshin main sthit he. belingshousan sagar se dakshin main pashchim-se-purv ats tat, bryan tat or inglish tat sthit hai, jo pashchimi antarctic k bhaag hai. pashchim main yah amandsen sagar se miltaa he.
 aaj se 21.5 varsh purv, atinutan yug main, ek kshudragrah belingshousan sagar main aa gira. is kshudragrah ko ab 'altanin kshudragrah' kehete hai.
