 abhiyantriki, bhautikee or rasayaniki main parivahan parighatna kinhi do yaa do se adhik bhautik tantron ke bich main dravyaman, urja, aavesh, samveg yaa koniya samveg ke aadan-pradaan ko kehete hai. dravyaman, taap, urja or samveg ke ganitiya vishleshan main bahut-si samantaayein hoti hai jinka parivahan parighatnaon ke adhyayan main vyavasthit rup se laabh uthaaya jaataa he.
