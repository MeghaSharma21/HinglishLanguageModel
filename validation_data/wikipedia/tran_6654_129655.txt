 bhaati athwa "bhatti" bharat or pakistan ke rajput kabeeley he .bhaati rajput chandravanshi mul ke hone kaa daawa karata he.
 bhaati kabeeley dwaara kabhi-kabhi apane puraane naam "yadavapati", jo krishna or yadu yaa yadav se unke vansh ko darshaate the,kaa bhi prayog kiya jaataa he .
 12 veen sadi main bhaati rajput ne jaisalmer par shashan kiya. ye log oont savaar, yoddhaon or maveshi chori or shikaar ke shaukeen the. registan main gahare sthit hone ke kaaran, jaisalmer bharat main muslim vistaar ke dauraan sidhe muslim akraman se batch gaya tha lekin 
 kuch bhaati khanaabadosh maveshi rakhane vaale the. 1857 ke vidroh se pehle ke kuch varshon main, in samoohon ne british east india company dwaara kiye gaye faislon ke kaaran apni jameen kho di thi, jo ki jat kisaanon ko charaai vaale bhoomi ko purv main dilli or haryana kshetron main bhaatiyon dwaara aavruth karti thi.
 rajasthan ke bhaati rajput main se kuch ,un samudaayon main shaamil the jo 1883-1998 ke bich kanya shishu htya karte the. dipalpur main hindu bhaati rajput sattarudh parivaar kii ek rajkumaari ne ek muslim shaasha saller rajab se shaadi kar lee thi or firoz shah tughlaq ko janm diya. yah bharat main turki muslim shashan kii avadhi ke dauraan antar-dharmik shahi vivaah sahyog ke kai udaahranon main se ek tha. rajput bhaati rajkumariyon kaa bhi jodhpur ke shahi parivaar main vivaah hua tha.
 aadhunik pakistan ke kuch hisson main, khaskar uttari or madhya punjab main, nimna jati ke dom ab bhi khud ko 'bhatti ' kehete hai.
