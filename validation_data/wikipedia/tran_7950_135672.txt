 hind-yavan rajya bhartiya upmahadweep ke pashchimottaree kshetra main sthit 200 isapurv se 10 isvi tak ke kaal main unani mul ke raajaaon dwaara prashasit rajya the. is dauraan yahan 30 se bhi adhik hind-yavan raja rahe jo aapas main bhi ladaa karte the. in rajyon kaa silsilaa tab armbh hua jab bactria ke dimitrius pratham naamak yavan raja ne 180 ipu main hindu-kush parvat shrunkhlaa paar karke pashchimottar bhartiya kshetron par dhaava bol diya. apane kaal main in shaskaon ne bhashaa, veshbhusha, chihnon, shashan pranali or rahan-sahan main unani-bhartiya samskritiyon main gaharaa mishran kiya or bahut se hindu or buddha dharm ke tatwon ko apanaaya. hind-yawanon kaa raaj shaq logon ke akramanon se ant hua, haalanki 10 i ke baad bhi ikki-dukki jagahon par kuch der tak unani samudaay apni pahchaan banaae hue the. samay ke saath unka bhartiya samaaj main vilay ho gaya.
