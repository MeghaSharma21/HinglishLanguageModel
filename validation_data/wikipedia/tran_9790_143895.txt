 pheri chumbakatv bhautikee main pherite evam anya aise hi padaarthon main paaya jane waal ek prakaar kaa chumbakatv he. yah sthool rup se louhachumbakatv jaisaa hi hota he parantu sookshmat: pratilohachumbakatv se miltaa-julata he kyonki isamen padosi yanon ke chumbakeey aghurnon kii pravriti anudish ke bajaay pratidish sanrekhan kii hoti he. kintu phir bhi pratiloh chumbakatv ke vipareet isamen alag-alag sabhi chumbakeey aghurna ek samaan nahin hote. or sabhi prachakranon kaa yogafal shunya nahin hota jiske kaaran bahut adhik maatra main parinami swat: chumbakan bana rah saktaa he.
