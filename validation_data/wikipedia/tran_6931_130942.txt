 hindu avibhakt kutumb hindu vivaah adhinium se sambandhit ek vidhik shabd he. iske sthaan par 'hindu avibhajit parivaar' yaa 'hindu sanyukt parivaar' bhi prayukt hota he. bhartiya vidhik tantra ke vikaas ke kaaran ab striyon ko bhi hindu avibhakt kutumb ke antargat sampatti ke ansh kaa adhikaar praapt he.
 sanyukt hindu parivaar vyavsaay ek aisa vyavsaay he jiskaa swaamitv ek sanyukt hindu parivaar ke sadasyon ke paas hota he. sanghathan kaa yah swaroop hindu adhinium ke antargat kaary karata he tathaa uttaradhikaar adhinium se niyantrit hota he. 
 sanyukt hindu parivaar vyavsaayik sanghathan kaa aisa swaroop he jisamen parivaar ke paas purvajon kii kuch vyavsaayik sampatti hoti he. sampatti main hissaa keval purush sadasyon kaa hota he. kisi sadsy ko purvajon kii is sampatti main se hissaa apane pita, dada tathaa pardada se miltaa he. atah teen aanukramik peediyaan ek saath viraasat main sampatti praapt kar sakati hai. sanyukt hindu parivaar vyavsaay ko keval purush sadsy chalaate hai jo ki vyavsaay ke sahabhaagi kehalaate hai. aayu main sabase bade sadsy ko karta kehete hai.
