 swarna hode yaa swarna daud us samay ko kehete he jab kisi sthaan par sone kii khoj ke baad us sthaan par badi sankhya main log or karmi pahunchne lagate hai. 19veen sadi ke dauraan australia, brazil, canada, dakshin africa or sanyukt rajya america main is prakaar kii swarna hode lagi thi, jabaki anya sthanon par kuch choti swarna hode hui thi.
 swarna hode ke saath aamtaur par "sabhi ke liye mukt" aay kii gatishiltaa jaisi bhaavana judi hoti he, jisamen koi bhi vyakti prachur rup se kshambhar main dhani ho saktaa he. aitihaasik rup se swarna hode kaa vahi arth he jo is pad kaa he lekin aaj ke samay main yah pad kisi bhi us poonjeewaadi gatividhi ko darshane ke liye hota he jisamen pratibhaagi kisi pratyaksh spasht rup se laabhprad bazaar ke liye hode karte hai.
 swarna hodo ne nae kshetron main baahar se aae logon ke avasthaapan main bahut sahaayak bhoomika nibhaai or uttar america or australian samskritiyon ko bahut seemaa tak paribhaashit kiya. aise samay main, jab kii mudra sone par aadhaarit thi, roman samrajya main nae-nae khananit sone ne swarna khaadaanon se kahin adhik dur tak aarthik sahaayta pradaan kii.
 saamudayik or laghu khanan ke anusaar vartmaan main vishvabhar main lagbhag 1.3 karod se 3 karod tak laghu khanankarta hai. lagbhag 10 karod log paroksh yaa aparoksh rup se laghu khanan par ashrith hai. congou loktantrik ganraajya main 8 se 15 lakh tak dastkaar khananak hai, sierra leone main 3.5 se 6.5 lakh or ghana main 1.5 se 2.5 lakh tak dastkaar khananak hai. iske atirikt shesh africa main bhi dasiyon lakh khananak hai.
