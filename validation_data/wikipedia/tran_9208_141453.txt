 nayantara kerala se bhartiya abhinetri he jo dakshin bharat kii filmon main dikhti he. unhonen 2003 kii malayalam film manasinakaade se apane abhinay karier kii shuruyaat kii or vismayathumbathu ke karne ke baad tamil cinema or telugu cinema main chali gai.
 unhonen ayya ke saath 2005 main tamil cinema main apni shuruyaat kii or uske baad telugu film lakshmi, jiske baad unhonen kai vyavsaayik rup se safal tamil or telugu filmon main mahila netritv kaa kirdaar nibhaayaa. jisase unhonen khud ko tamil or telugu cinema main sabase zyaada maang vaali abhinetriyon ke rup main sthapit kiya. 2010 main unhonen film super ke madhyam se kannada film main shuruyaat kii. shri ram rajyam main sita ke chithran ke liye unhonen sarvshereshth telugu abhinetri kaa film fere puraskaar or sarvshereshth abhinetri ke liye nandi puraskaar arjeet kiya. 2011 main unhonen chennai main isai dharm ko chhodkar arya samaaj mandir main hindu dharm apana liya or unka manchi naam nayantara unka aadhikarik naam ban gaya.
