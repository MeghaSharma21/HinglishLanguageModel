 hai phong yaa hefong, vietnam ke uttari bhaag main doosraa sabase bada or ek pramukh audyogic shahar he. hai phong, vietnam ke uttari tat main praudyogiki, arthvyavastha, samskruti, chikitsa, shiksha, vigyaan or vyapaar kaa centre bhi he. iske alaava, yah shahari aabaadi ke maamle main vietnam kaa teesraa sabase bada shahar he. 
 hai phong shahar ek bandargah praant ke rup main francisi aupniveshik samrajya ke dauraan 1887 main sthapit hua tha. 1888 main, french tisare ganraajya ke adhyaksh saadi carnet ne hai phong shahar ke sthapana kii ghoshnaa kii thi. 1954 se 1975 tak hai phong uttar vietnam ke sabase mahatvapurn samudri shahar ke rup main tha or yah 1976 se hanoi or ho chi minh shahar kii tarah pratyaksh-niyantrit nagar palikaon main se ek ban gaya.
 hefong ek tateey shahar he, jo kim nadi ke muhane par sthit he. yah vietnam ke uttar-purvi tateey ilaake main, hanoi se 120 kimi purv main sthit he. kem nadi par bana binh brij, shahar ko thu guyen jile ke saath jodta he. 
 hefong main ek ardra up-ushnkaribandheey jalwaayu he, jisamen garm, ardra greeshmakaal or garm, shushk sardiyaan hoti he. shahar main barish april se oktober tak hoti he; shahar kii vaarshik varsha kaa lagbhag 90% aam taur par in maheenon main hi girtaa he. shahar ke sardiyon or garmiyon ke bich ke taapmaana main kaaphi antar hota he.
 hai phong, vishesh rup se uttar or pure vietnam kaa ek pramukh aarthik centre he. francisi shashan ke dauraan, hai phong, sigon or hanoi ke baraabar pehle-str kaa shahar tha. aaj, hefong vietnam ke sabase mahatvapurn aarthik kendron main se ek he. 2009 main, hai phong kaa rajya bajet rajasav 34,000 arab rahaa tha. 2011 main, shahar main bajet rajasav 47,725 arab tak pahunch gaya, 2010 kii tulana main 19% kii vruddhi hui. 2015 main, shahar kii kul rajasav 5688 arab tak pahunch gaya. sarkaar kii yojana he ki 2020 tak, hefong kaa rajasav 80,000 arab se adhik ho jaaegi or gharelu rajasav 20,000 arab tak pahunch jayega. hefong kaa duniya bhar ke 40 se adhik deshon or kshetron ke saath vyapaarik sambandh he.
 food processing, halke udyogon or baari udyogon sahit hefong main kai mahatvapurn udyog kshetra he. pramukh utpaadon main machhlee sos, bear, cigarette, textile, paper, plastic pipe, cement, loha, pharmaceuticals, electric prashansakon, motorbikes, steel pipe or jahaajon or outsorcing software kriyanvayan shaamil hai. in udyogon main se adhikansh main 2000 or 2007 ke bich kaaphi vrudhdi rahi hai. cigarette or dawa udyog ko chod kar, jahaz nirmaan, steel pipe, plastic pipe or vastra udyogon main sabase tej vruddhi hui hai.
 ek udyogic shahar ke rup main isaki sthiti ke baavjood, hefong kshetra kaa lagbhag ek tihaai yaa 52,300 hectare kshetra krishi ke liye upyog kiya jaataa he. chaaval sabase mahatvapurn fasal he, jiskaa lagbhag 80% krishi bhoomi main utpaadan hota he, 2007 main iskaa 463,100 ton kaa utpaadan hua tha. anya krishi utpaadon main makka, chini or moongfalee shaamil hai.
