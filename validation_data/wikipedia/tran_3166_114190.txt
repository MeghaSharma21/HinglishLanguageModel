 slav log yaa slavi log purvi europe, dakshini europe or uttar asia main basane vaali ek maanav jati he. yah or inke purvaj slavi bhashaayen bolte the, jo hind-europea bhashaa-parivaar kii ek upashakha he. slavi logon ke naam aksar 'ek' yaa 'ich' kii dhwani main khatm hote hai. roosi, polish, bulgarian, sarbi, check, slovenian, belarusi, ukraini or slovakian log slavi logon ke kuch samudaay hai. europe kii aabaadi ke ek-tihaai log slavi jatiyon main se ek ke sadsy hai.
