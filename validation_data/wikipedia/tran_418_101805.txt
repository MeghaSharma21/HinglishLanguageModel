 upaskar avataran pranali glide path – antarrashtriya doorsanchaar union ke itiu radio vinium – main paribhaasha ke anusaar " instrument landing pranali main sannihit urdhwadhar maargdarshan jo avataran ke samay vimaan ko apane ishtatam avataran path se urdhwadhar vichlan ingit karata he."prayek radio stesan yaa aisi pranali ko, vah jis seva ko sthaayee yaa asthaayee rup se pradaan kar rahaa he, usee ke anusaar vargeekrut kiya jaataa he. 
 
 glide slop stesan udanpattee ke tachadown kshetra ke nearesth ek antina sarini kaa upyog karata he. GS sanket upaskar ke vahika aavitti par loklaizar kii hi bhaanti bheja jaataa he. glide slop sanket kaa kendra lagbhag 3° kshaitij par preshan karne hetu vyavasthit kiya jaataa he. isaki kiran 1.4° deep hoti he.
 payalat vimaan ko is prakaar niyantrit karata he ki uskaa vimaan sthapit suchak kendra main hi rahe, jisase vimaan glide path kaa anusaran karte hue kisi bhi vyavdhaan aadi se upar rahete hue udanpattee par ekadam tachadown bindu par pahunchnaa sunishchit ho paye.
 les ke kul 40 uplabdh channels main se kisi ek par do aavritiyon kaa preshan kiya jaataa he. inamen se ek ko 90 hertz par evam duusari ko 150 hertz par aavriti modulan kiya jaataa he. inako sah-sthapit sangraahakon kii shrenee se preshit kiya jaataa he. prayek sangraahak ek sankeern kiran preshit karata he.
 lokliser evam glide slop kii vaahak aavritiyon ke jode is prakaar se nirdhaarit kar bane hue hote hai, ki vimaan sthapit nauvahan radio graahak lokliser aavriti chayan karne par swatah hi glideslope aavatti bhi chun letaa he. lokliser aavriti 110 megahertz or glideslope aavriti 330 megahertz ke range main hoti hai.lokliser vaahak aavritiyaan 108.10 - 111.95 megahertz . les aavritiyaan tacan channels ke 18X se 56X tak ke sam channels par dekhen.
