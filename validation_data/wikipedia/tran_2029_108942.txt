 darbhanga raaj bihar ke mithila kshetra main lagbhag 6,200 kilometre ke daayre main tha. iskaa mukhyalay darbhanga shahar tha. is raaj kii sthapana maithil brahman jameendaaron ne 16veen sadi kii shuruyaat main kii thi. british raaj ke dauraan tatkaaleen bengal ke 18 cirkil ke 4,495 gaanv darbhanga naresh ke shashan main the. raaj ke shashan-prashasan ko dekhne ke liye lagbhag 7,500 adhikari behaal the. bharat ke rajavaadon main darbhanga raaj kaa apana khaas hi sthaan rahaa he.
 darbhanga-maharaj khandwal kul ke the jiske shashan-sansthaapak mahesh thakur the. unki apni vidwata, unke shishy raghunandan kii vidwata tathaa maharaja maansingh ke sahyog se akbar dwaara unhen rajya kii prapti hui thi.
 1.mahesh thakur - 1556-1569 i. tak. inaki rajdhani vartmaan madhubani jile ke bhaur gram main thi, jo madhubani se kareeb 10 mile purab lohat chini mil ke paas he.
 2.gopal thakur - 1569-1581 tak. inke kaashi-vaas le lene ke kaaran inke anuj parmanand thakur gaddi par baithe.
 3.parmanand thakur - 
 4.shubhankar thakur - inhone apni rajdhani ko madhubani ke nikat bhauaara main sthaanantarit kiya.
 5.purushottam thakur - - 1617-1641 tak.
 6.sundar thakur - 1641-1668 tak.
 7.mahinath thakur - 1668-1690 tak. ye parakramee yoddha the. inhone mithila kii pracheen rajdhani simraon pargane ke adhishwar sugaon-naresh gajsingh par akraman kar haraaya tha.
 8.narpathi thakur - 1690-1700 tak.
 9.raja raghav singh - 1700-1739 tak. inhone apane priy khawas viru kurmi ko kosi anchal kii vyavastha saunp di thi. shashan-mad main usne apane is maharaj ke prati hi vidroh kar diya. maharaj ne veertapurvak vidroh kaa shaman kiya tathaa nepal tarai ke panchamahal pargane ke updravi raja bhupasingh ko bhi ran main maar daalaa. inke hi kul ke ek kumaar ekanath thakur ke dweshvash ubhaadne se bengal-bihar ke nawab alivardi khan inhen sapariwaar bandi banaakar patna le gaya tathaa baad main bhadrapada shukla chaturthi ko kar nirdhaarit kar mukt kar diya. mana gaya he ki isi kaaran mithila main yah tithi parv-tithi ban gayi or is tithi ko kalankit hone ke baavjood chandramaa kii puja hone lagi. mithila ke atirikt bharat main anyatra kahin chauth-chandra nahin manaaya jaataa he. yadi kahin he to mithilavasi hi vahaan rahane ke kaaran manaate hai.
 10.raja visun singh - 1739-1743 tak.
 11.raja narendra singh - 1743-1770 tak. inke dwaara nishchit samay par rajasav nahin chukaane ke kaaran alivardi khan ne pehle patna ke soobedaar ramnarayan se akraman karavaaya. yah yuddh rampattee se chalakar gangaduaar ghat hote hue jhanjharpur ke paas kandarpi ghat ke paas hua tha. baad main nawab kii sena ne bhi akraman kiya. tab narahan rajya ke dronvar brahman-vanshaj raja ajit narayan ne maharaja kaa saath diya tha tathaa lomaharshak yuddh kiya tha. in yudhon main maharaj vijayi hue par akraman phir hue.
 12.rani padmavati - 1770-1778 tak.
 13.raja pratap singh - 1778-1785 tak. inhone apni rajdhani ko bhauaara se jhanjharpur main sthaanantarit kiya.
 14.raja madhav singh - 1785-1807 tak. inhone apni rajdhani jhanjharpur se hataakar darbhanga main sthapit kii. lard cornwalis ne inke shasankaal main jameen kii damami bandobasti karavaayi thi.
 15.maharaja chhatra singh - 1807-1839 tak. inhone 1814-15 ke nepal-yuddh main angrejon kii sahaayta kii thi. hestings ne inhen 'maharaja' kii oopaadhi di thi.
 16.maharaja rudra singh - 1839-1850 tak.
 17.maharaja maheshwar singh - 1850-1860 tak. inaki mrityu ke pashchaat kumaar lakshmishwar singh ke avyask hone ke kaaran darbhanga raaj ko court of wards ke tahat le liya gaya. jab kumaar lakshmishwar singh balig hue tab apane paitruk singhaasana par asin hue.
 18.maharaja lakshmishwar singh - 1880-1898 tak. ye kaaphi udaar, lok-hitaishi, vidya evam kalaon ke premi evam prashray daata the. rameshwar singh inke anuj the.
 19.maharajaadhiraj rameshwar singh - 1898-1929 tak. inhen british sarkaar kii ore se 'maharajaadhiraj' kaa virud diya gaya tathaa or bhi anek oopaadhiyaan milin. apane agraj kii bhanti ye bhi vidwaanon ke sangrakshak, kalaon ke poshak evam nirmaan-priy ati udaar narendra the. inhone bharat ke anek nagaron main apane bhawan banawaaye tathaa anek mandiron kaa nirmaan karavaaya. vartmaan madhubani jile ke rajnagar main inhone vishaal evam bhavy rajaprasaad tathaa anek mandiron kaa nirmaan karavaaya tha. yahan kaa sabase bhavy bhawan 1926 i. main banakar taiyaar hua tha, jiske architech da. em.e.korni the. ye apni rajdhani darbhanga se rajnagar laana chaahate the lekin kuch kaarnon se aisa na ho sakaa, jisamen kamala nadi main bheeshan baadh se kataai bhi ek mukhya kaaran tha. june 1929 main inaki mrityu ho gayi. ye bhagwati ke param bhakt evam tantra-vidya ke gyaata the.
 20. maharajaadhiraj kameshwar singh - pita ke nidhan ke baad ye gaddi par baithe. inhone apane bhai raja bahadur vishweshwar singh ko apane poojya pita dwaara nirmit rajnagar kaa vishaal evam darshneey rajaprasaad dekar us anchal kaa rajya-bhaar saunpa tha. 1934 ke bheeshan bhucamp main apane nirmaan kaa ek dashak bhi puraa hote na hote rajnagar ke ve adbhut nakkashidaar vaibhavshaalee bhawan kshatigrast ho gayi.
 maharajaadhiraj kameshwar singh ke samay main hi bharat swatantra hua or jameendaari pratha samaapt hui. deshi riyasaton kaa astitv samaapt ho gaya. maharajaadhiraj kameshwar singh ko santan na hone se unke bhattije kumaar jiveshwar singh sampatti ke adhikari hue.*
 darbhanga naresh kameshwar singh gautam apni shaan-shaukat ke liye puri duniya main vikhyath the. angrej shaskaon ne inhen 'maharajaadhiraj' kii oopaadhi di thi. raaj darbhanga ne nae jamaane ke rang ko bhaanp kar kai companiyon kii shuruyaat kii thi. nil ke vyavsaay ke alaava maharajaadhiraj ne [chini mil, kaagaj mil aadi khole. isse bahuton ko rojgaar mila or raaj sirf kisaanon se khiraj kii vasuuli par hi aadhaarit nahin rahaa. aay ke naye strot bane. isse spasht hota he ki darbhanga naresh aadhunik soch ke vyakti the. patrakarita ke kshetra main darbhanga maharaj ne mahatvapurn kaam kiya. unhone 'newspaper and publication privet limited' kii sthapana kii or kai akhbaar va patrikaon kaa prakaashan shuru kiya. angreji main 'da indian nesan', hindi main 'aryaavart' or maithili main 'mithila mihir' saptahik magazine kaa prakaashan kiya. ek jamaana tha jab bihar main aryaavart sabase lokapriy akhbaar tha.
 darbhanga maharaj sangeet or anya lalit kalaon ke bahut bade sangrakshak the. 18veen sadi se hi darbhanga hindustani shastreey sangeet kaa bada centre ban gaya tha. ustad bismilla khan, gauhar jaan, pandit ramchatur mallik, pandit rameshwar pathak or pandit siyaaraam tiwari darbhanga raaj se jude vikhyath sangitajna the. ustad bismilla khan to kai varshon tak darbar main sangitajna rahe. kehete hai ki unka bachapan darbhanga main hi beetaa tha. gauhar jaan ne saal 1887 main pehali baar darbhanga naresh ke saamne prastuti di thi. phir vah darbar se jud gayi. darbhanga raaj ne gwalior ke muraad ali khan kaa bahut sahyog kiya. ve apane samay ke mashhoor sarodavadak the. maharaja lakshmishwar singh swayan ek sitarvadak the. dhrupad ko lekar darbhanga raaj main naye prayog hue. dhrupad ke kshetra main darbhanga gharaana kaa aaj alag sthaan he. maharaj kameshwar singh ke chote bhai raja vishweshwar singh prasiddh film abhinayta or gayak kundanlal sahagal ke mitra the. jab donon darbhanga ke bela pales main milte the to baatcheet, ghazal or thumari kaa daur chalata tha. darbhanga raaj kaa apana phani orchestra or police band tha.
 khelon ke kshetra main darbhanga raaj kaa mahatvapurn yogadan rahaa. swatantraapurv bihar main laheria sarai main darbhanga maharaj ne pehla polo maidan banwaaya tha. raja vishweshwar singh ol india footbal federation ke sansthaapak sadsy main the. darbhanga nareshon ne kai khelon ko protsaahan diya.
 shiksha ke kshetra main darbhanga raaj kaa yogadan athulaneey he. darbhanga nareshon ne banaras hindu vishvavidhyalay, kalakatta vishvavidhyalay, elahabaad vishvavidhyalay, patna vishvavidhyalay, kameshwar singh sanskrit vishvavidhyalay, darbhanga medical college evam hospital, lalitnarayan mithila vishvavidhyalay, aligarh muslim vishvavidhyalay or kai sansthaanon ko kaaphi daan diya. maharaja rameshwar singh bahadur pandit madanamohan malviya ke bahut bade samarthak the or unhone banaras hindu vishvavidhyalay ko 5,000,000 rupee kosh ke liye die the. maharaja rameshwar singh ne patna sthit darbhanga house patna vishvavidhyalay ko de diya tha. san 1920 main unhone patna medical college hospital ke liye 500,000 rupee dene vaale sabase bade daandaata the. unhone anand baag pales or usase lage anya mahal kameshwar singh sanskrit vishvavidhyalay ko de die. kalakatta vishvavidhyalay ke granthalay ke liye bhi unhone kaaphi dhan diya. lalitnarayan mithila vishvavidhyalay ko raaj darbhanga se 70,935 kitaaben milin.
 iske alaava, darbhanga nareshon ne schuli shiksha ke kshetra main kaaphi yogadan kiya. maharaja lakshmeshwar singh bahadur bhartiya rashtriya congress ke sansthaapak sadsy the. angrejon se mitraapurna sambandh hone ke baavjood ve congress kii kaaphi aarthik madad karte the. dow. rajendra prasad, abul kalam aaazaad, subhash chandra bose or mahatma gandhi se unke ghanishtha sambandh the. san 1892 main congress elahabaad main adhiveshan karnaa chaahati thi, par angrej shaskaon ne kisi saarvajanik sthal par aisa karne kii ijaajat nahin di. yah jaankaree milane par darbhanga maharaja ne vahaan ek mahal hi kharid liya. usee mahal ke ground par congress kaa adhiveshan hua.
 maharaja ne vah kila congress ko hi de diya. maharaja kameshwar singh gautam ne bhi rashtriya andolan main kaaphi yogadan diya. mahatma gandhi unhen apane putr ke samaan maanate the.
