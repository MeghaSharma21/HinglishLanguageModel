 tarashankar bandhopadhyaay ek bangla sahityakar hai. inhen ganadevataa ke liye 1966 main gyanpith puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha. tarashankar bandhopadhyaay ko sahitya evam shiksha kshetra main bharat sarkaar dwaara san 1969 main padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. ye pashchim bengal se hai. inke dwaara rachit ek upanyaas aarogy niketan ke liye unhen san 1956 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
