 yah raja vrishaparva kii putree thi. vrishaparva ke guru shukracharya kii putree devayani uski sakhi thi. ek baar krodh se usne devayani ko peeta or kuen main daal diya. devayani ko yayati ne kuen se baahar nikaala. yayati ke chale jane par devayani usee sthaan par khadi rahi. putree ko khojate hue shukracharya vahaan aae. kintu devayani sharmishtha dwaara kiye gaye apmaan ke kaaran jane ko raji na hui. duhkhi shukracharya bhi nagar chhodane ko taiyaar ho gaye. jab vrishaparva ko yah gyaat hua to usne bahut anunay-vinay kiya. ant main shukracharya is baat par ruke ki sharmishtha devayani ke vivaah main daasi rup main bhent kii jaaegi. vrishaparva sahamat ho gaye or sharmishtha yayati ke yahan daasi banakar gai. sharmishtha se yayati ko teen putr hue.
