 chennai kaa ek mahatvapurn aakarshan he saint jorge fort. ise san 1640 main east india company ke francins de ne banaaya tha. yah kila east india company kaa vyapaarik centre tha. 150 varshon tak yah yudhon or shadyantron kaa centre bana rahaa. is kile main puraani sainik chhavnee, adhikariyon ke makaan, saint meri girjaghar evam robert clive kaa ghar he. yah carch angarejon dwaara bharat main banwaaya gaya sabase purana carch mana jaataa he.
