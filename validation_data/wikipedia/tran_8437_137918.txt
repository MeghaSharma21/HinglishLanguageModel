 vestminster pranali, shashan kii ek loktantric sansadeey pranali he, joki saikadon varshon ke kaal main, sanyukt adhirajya main viksit hui thi. is vyavastha kaa naam, landan ke pales of westominster se aata he, joki british sansad kaa sabhasthal he. vartmaan samay main, vishwa ke anya kai deshon main is pranali par aadhaarit yaa isse prabhavit shashan-vyavasthaayen sthapit hai. britain or rashtramandal prajabhumiyon ke alaava, aisi vyavasthaaon ko visheshtah purv british upniveshon ke shashan-vyavastha main dekha ja saktaa he.
 westminster pranali kii sarkarein, vishesh taur par rashtramandal deshon main dekha ja saktaa he. isaki shuruyaat, sabase pehle canada praant main hui thi, or tatpashchaat australia ne bhi apni sarkaar ko is hi pranali ke aadhaar par sthapit kiya. aaj ke samay, vishwa bhar main kul 33 deshon main is pranali par aadhaarit yaa isse prabhavit shashan-vyavasthaayen hai. ek samay aisa bhi tha jab tamaam rashtramandal yaa purv-rashtramandal desh or uske uparashtriya ikaayiyon main westominster pranali kii sarkarein thim. baad main, anya kai deshon ne apni shashan pranali ko badal liya.
