 naanaa fadanvis 'balaji janardan bhanu' urf 'naanaa fadanvis' kaa janm satara main hua tha. yah chatur raajnneetijna tathaa deshbhakt tha or isamen vyaavhaarik gyaan tathaa vaastaviktaa kii chetana jaagrit thi. isamen teen gun the - swamibhakti, swabhimaan, tathaa swadeshbhimaan. iske jivan kii yahi kasuti he. iske saamne teen samasyaein thim; peshwa pad ko sthir rakhana, maratha sangh ko banaae rakhana, tathaa videshiyon se maratha rajya kii raksha karnaa. kushal raajnneetijna naanaa fadanvis par maratha-sangh kii niti ke sanchaalan kaa bhaar 1774 se aayaa. maratha sangh ke ghatak sindhiya, holkar, gaekwad tathaa bhosle bhi the. inamen paraspar dwesh or katutaa thi or paksh or upapaksh the jinaki prerana se anek shadyantr, viplav, vishwasghaat aadi ghrunit kaary karwaae ja rahe the. at: naanaa fadanvis ko badi doordarshita evam kootneetijyata se sangh kaa kaary chalaanaa padaa or rajya ke andar suvyavastha laane kaa bhaar bhi usapar aa padaa. sabhi sardaar peshwa ko apana netaa maanate the. parantu panipat kii teesri ladaai ke baad peshwa kaa niyantran atyant shithil hone laga tha. saath hi peshwa ke uttaradhikaar ke sambandh main matbhed or aapasi shadyantr bhi chal rahe the. at: naanaa ko do samasyaon kaa hal ek saath hi karnaa tha. at: apane swabhimaan kii raksha karte hue naanaa ne peshwa kii, tathaa maratharajya kii raksha kii, tathaa maratha sangh ko vichhin hone se bachaaya. angrej apane samrajya ko badhanaa chaahate the parantu naanaa ne varshanuvarsh yuddh karke maraathon ke shatru angrejon se peshwa kaa sashti ke atirikt pooraarajya jeet liya. is prakaar naanaa ne angrejon kii badhati hui shakti ko roka or sangh ko vicchhinn hone se bachaaya. parantu naanaa kii mrityu ke baad hi maratha sangh kaa ant hua. lard weljali ke naanaa ke sambandh ke shabd smaraneey hai. naanaa fadanvis keval ek yogya mantri hi nahin tha, kintu vah eemaandaar tathaa ucch aadarshon se prerit tha. usne apana puraa jivan rajya ke kalyaan ke liye tathaa apane adhikariyon kii kalyankamana ke liye utsarg kiya.
