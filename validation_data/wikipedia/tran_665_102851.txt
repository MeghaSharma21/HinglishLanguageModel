 rasayan vigyaan evam nirmaan me vidyut apghaton us prakriya ko kehete hai jiske dwaara kisi rasinic yogic main vidyut-dhara pravaahit karke uske rasinic bandhon ko ko todaa jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye jal main vidyut dhara pravaahit karne par jal, hydrogen evam oxigen main vighatit ho jaataa he jise jal kaa vidyut apghaton kehete hai. vidyut apghaton ke bahut se upyog hai. ayskon ko prasanskaarit karke unamem nihit rasinic tatv ko shuddh karnaa evam use alag karnaa iskaa sabase mahatvapurn audyogic evam vyavsaayik upyog he. 
 jab kisi oinic padaarth se jab vidyut dhara pravaahit karte hai to vidyutagron ke upar rasinic abhikriya hoti he or isse naye padaarth banate hai. 
 uparokt avyavon kii bhoomika is prakaar he-
 vidyutagra, vidyut ke chaalak hone chaiye. dhaatu, grafite or ardhchaalak padaarthon ke electrode bahuta prayog main laaye jate hai. electrode ke padaarth kaa chunaav in baton se prabhavit hota he- 
 jal kaa vidyut apghaton karne ke liye disht dhara kaa koi strot aavashyak hota he, jaise bydaree yaa disi janitr yaa ractifire aadi. pani ke vidyut apghaton se hydrogen or oxygen praapt hote hai. pani se hydrogen kii prapti ise mahatvapurn banaati he.
 hydrogen bahut upyogi he. iskaa upyog anterdahan injanon main indhan ke rup main kiya ja saktaa he athwa ise indhan cell main upyog karke usase vidyut motaren chalaane ke kaam main laayaa ja saktaa he.
