 nirdeshaank: 29°09′N 77°37′E / 29.15°N 77.62°E / 29.15; 77.62saradhana pashchimi uttar pradesh main meerut jila main ek kasbaa he. ye dilli ke uttar-purv main 85 kimi meerut shahar se 13 ki.me dur sthit he. saradhana kapadaa bazaar va girijaghar ke liye prasiddh he. meerut se 22 kilometre duuri par 'saradhana kasbaa' bhi aitihaasik or pracheen dharohar kaa gavaah he.
 13 veen shatabdi main saradhana ke 'raja sarakat' dwaara yah sthal sthapit kiya gaya.yahan roman catholic carch kaa nirmaan 1822 i. main karaayaa gaya.iskaa dizin tab italy ke vaastuvid anthani regleni dwaara banaaya gaya.carch ke nirmaan main sangemarmar va anya keemtee pattharon kaa istemaal kiya gaya.isi janapad main meerut mahanagar se 23 kilometre duuri par 'parakshitgarh' kasbe ko arjuna ke pautra, vir abhimanyu ke putr raja parakshit kii rajdhani hone kaa gaurav praapt he.
 yahan begum samaru mahal, roman catholic carch, jama masjid va digambar jain mandir darshneey sthal he.
