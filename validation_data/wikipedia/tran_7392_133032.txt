 vijay shendge - marathi kavi
 vijay shendge is namase marathi vaachak evam marathi sahitya vishwa thikse parichit nahin he. magar unki kavitaaye acchhee ki hoti he. prem kavitaaka unka akalan jitna acha he utanaahi saamaajik vishayonka. ' gharat ', ' vaajat panjan, vaajat kakan ' or ' cheharyamdhala chandra ' yah unki prakashit saahityakritiya. ' rimjhim paoos ' is shirshak se ve blog bhi likhte hai. ' re ghanaa ' or ' kadhi watate ' is namase unki rachana par adhrith sangeet kaarykram bhi chalaaye jate hai. kavita ke saath saath ve kahania bhi likhte hai. unke ' byshi ' is kahaani ko dakshin maharashtra sahitya parishad kaa pratham puraskaar praapt he.
