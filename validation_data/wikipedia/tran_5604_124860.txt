 sikh dharmapar ek shrenee kaa bhaag
 maharashtra ke nanded main sthit sikh panth ke 10wein guru gobindsingh ke pavitra sthaan jise sikh panth ke paanch takhat sahiban main se ek shiromani takhat sachkhand shrihajur abachalanagar sahib kaha jaataa he.
 shriguru gobindsingh ji ke alokik jivan ke antim kshanon se sambandhit yah pavitra sthaan sikh panth ke paanch takhat sahiban main se ek shiromani takhat he. jisaki prasiddhi bharat main hi nahin balki duniya bhar main he.
 jab shriguru gobindsingh ji ke mata-pita or chaaron bete desh ke liye shahid ho gaye, tab ve sansar kaa bhala karte hue godavari nadi ke kinare based nagar nanded pahunche. nanded main guruji ne leelaayen rachi. yahan aapane gurudwara nagina ghat se tir chalaakar apane satgaruon ke samay kaa tap-sthaan pragat kiya. vah tir ek masjid main ja laga, jahaan guruji ne dhai haath jameen khudwakar satyugai asana, karmandal, khadaau or maala nikaali or vah sthaan pragat kiya. badale main us jameen ke maalik muslim ko us sthaan par sone kii moharen bichhaakar di gai.
 is sthaan ke pragat hone par yahan guruji roj nai-nai leelaayen karne lage. subah-shaam dewan sajane lage. chaaron taraf aanandamayi raunak bad gai. kuch hi samay baad suba sarhad ke nawab vajeer khan ke bheje kaatilon ke hamale ke baad aapane sachkhand gaman kii taiyaari kii to ati vyakul sangat ke poochne par aapane farmaaya ki ham aap logon ko dhur kii bani 'shabad' guru ke hawaale kar chale hai, jisase aapako har samay aadhyatmik aguwai kii bakshish hoti rahegi.
 vikrami samvath 1765 karthik sudi dooj ke din aapane paanch paise or nariyal shriguru granth sahib ji ke aage rakhkar maathaa tekkar shraddha sahit parikrama kii or is paavan din samooh sikh sangat ko sahib shriguru granth sahib ji se jodakar or yug-yugon tak atal guruta gaddi arpan kii. is tarah shriguru granth sahib ko guru gaddi dekar dewan main baithi sangat ko farmaaya :
 aagia bhai akaal kii tavi chalaao panth..sab sikhan ko hukum he guru manio granth..guru granth ji manio pragat guraan kii deh..jo prabh ko milbo chahai khoj shabd main leh..
 iske baad guru sahib ne sarvatra khalsa sikh sangat ko vachan diya ki yugon yug kii is paavan pavitra hui dharti kaa naam shriabachalanagar hua. is tarah jagat tamaasha dekhne ke baad 'vicitra naatak' khelte hue samvath 1765 karthik sudi panchami ke din aap param purakh paramatma main abhed ho gaye.
 yah gurudwara sachkhand sahib ji se thodi duuri par godavari ke kinare gurudwara nagina ghat ke sameep sushobhit he. aurangzeb kii maut ke uparaant uske shahjadon ke bich dilli ke takht ke upar baithne ke liye aapas main yuddh chhid gaya. shahjada bahadur shah ne shri guru gobind singh ji ko is yuddh main sikh fouj ke saath uski madad karne ke liye vinati kii. us ne guru ji ke saath vaayada kiya ki yuddh jeet jane par jab bah dilli ke singhaasana par baithegaa, to aurangzeb ke samay main punjab main bekasoor sikhon ke upar atyaachaar karne vaale doshi hakimon ko guru ji ke hawaale kar degaa. guru ji kii yuddh main kii gai madad se bahadur shah kii jeet hui tathaa vah hindustan ke shahenshaah ke rup main dilli ke takht par virajman hua. unhi dinon dakshin bharat main baghawat ho gai. usne baghawat ko kuchalane ke liye shahi fouj ko lekar dakshin kii ore kooch kiya tathaa guru ji ko bhi saath chalane ke liye vinati kii. takht hasil kar lene ke baad uski niyat main khot aa gai thi tathaa vah doshiyon ko guru ji ke supurd karne se taal-matol karne lag padaa tha..
 guru ji ne is mudde ko hal karne hetu uske saath hi dakshin bharat kii ore prasthaan kiya. padaav karte-karte bahadur shah tathaa guru ji jab nanded pahunche the to us vakt guru ji kii sena ke liye isi sthaan par lungar taiyaar kiya jaataa tha tathaa phujon ko khilaayaa jaataa tha jahaan ab gurudwara lungar sahib sushobhit he.kha jagat main lungar kii pratha shri guru nanak dev ji ke samay main hi aarambh ho gai thi. dur-dur se yaa aas-paas se guru ji ke upadesh sunne ke liye tathaa unke darshan ke liye aane vaali sangat ke liye lungar shuru kiya gaya tha. iske alaava guru kaa lungar garibon tathaa zaruratmand logon ke liye bhi khulaa tha. brahmanon kii paidaa kii gai jati-varna kii baant ko sikh dharm main pravesh karne se rokane hetu guru ji ne hukum diya tha ki guru ghar main aane vaale sabhi log bina kisi bhed-bhav ke ekasaath pangat main baithkar lungar chhaken. shri guru angad dev ji ke samay unki supatni mata khivi ji lungar kaa prabandh swayan dekhati thim.
 pratidin praat: do baje sameep sthit godavari nadi se jal kii gaagar bharkar sachkhand main laai jaati he. sukhmani sahib ji ke paath kii samaapti ke pashchaat guru granth sahib kaa prakaash kiya jaataa he. aradaas ke pashchaat sampurna diwas gurudwara paath or kirthan se goonjata rahata he. sandhya main rahiraas sahib kaa paath or aarti ke baad guru gobindsingh, maharaja ranjitsingh or akali phulasingh ke pramukh shastron ke darshan karwaae jate hai.
 haal hi main 30 oktober 2008 ko guru granth sahib ke prakaash ke 300 varsh purna hone ke uplakshya main guruta gaddi diwas manaaya gaya. paanch divasiy is samaaroh main desh or videsh se lakhon kii sankhya main sikh sangat, sant or vidwaan shaamil hue. yahan sabhi guru parab ke saath hi dashahra, deepawali or hola mohalla bade dhoomdhaam se manaaya jaataa he.
 nanded main rashtriya vimaanatal he. jahaan se sachkhand kii duuri maatr 5 kilometre duuri par sthit he.
 maharashtra ke aurangabad se nanded kareeb 300 kilometre duuri par sthit he. sabhi pramukh shahro se sarkari va nijee vaahanon ke jariye nanded aasaani se pahunchaa ja saktaa he.
 nanded sabhi pramukh railmarg se judaa hua he. amritsar se nanded ke liye vishesh rail suvidha uplabdh he.
