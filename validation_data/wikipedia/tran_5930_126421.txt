 dakshin africa kii cricket team ne 2005-06 ke sijan main cricket maichon ke liye bharat kaa daura kiya. sabhi match ek divasiy match the, jisamen paanch ek divasiy antarrashtriya or hyderabad se team ke khilaaf ek tour match tha. donon paksh shrinkhalaa jeet se baahar aa rahe the, bharat ne srilanka ko ghar main 6-1 se haraaya jabaki dakshin africa ne newjiland par 4-0 se jeet hasil kii thi. shrinkhalaa se pehle, bibisi sport website kaa ek purvavlokan tha jisane tark diya ki bharat or dakshin africa vishwa champions kii raksha ke rup main "australia ke mukut ke liye adhik gambhir chunauti" the, or yah ki "kuch gambhirtha se achche cricket ke liye ho saktaa he" 
 yah ek kareebi shrinkhalaa thi - kisi bhi dar par dakshin africa ne shrinkhalaa main do baar lead par kabja kar liya tha, lekin vah nahin roak sake or eme e chidambaram stadium main tisare ekadivasiy match main barish hui, shrinkhalaa 2-2 se bani thi. main of da series puraskaar bhi dakshin africa ke greem smith or bharat ke yuvaraj singh ke bich saajha kiya gaya, jinhone 209 ranon ke saath apni team kii ballebaaji ausat main sheersh par rahaa. gendbaaji kii ore se, shaun polock ne saat wicket or nau madan overon ke saath yogadan diya, lekin antim oriai main 5.5 ek over ke liye gaya, jab bharat 50 over main jeet ke liye 222 ran bana.
