 ritesh deshmukh ek bhartiya film abhinayta he jo hindi or marathi bhashaa main filmon main abhinay karte he. inkaa janm 17 disambar 1978 ko maharashtra rajya ke latur zile main maharashtra ke purv mukhyamantri vilasarav deshmukh ke ghar par hua tha jabaki inaki mata kaa naam vaishali deshmukh he or bhai kaa naam amit deshmukh he jo congress ke ek sadsy he. ritesh deshmukh ne apane filmi karier kii shuruyaat 2003 main tuje meri kasam naamak film se kii thi inke baad inhone kai bollywood kii hindi filmon main abhinay kiya jabaki ye marathi filmon main bhi kaary kiya karte he. abhinayta ritesh deshmukh ne ek varsh main sabase zyada saal 2007 main 5 filmon main kiya tha.
