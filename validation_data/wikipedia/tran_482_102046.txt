 sukhoi/echeel paanchavi peedi ke ladaaku vimaan yaa persepective multi-role fiter or Perspective Multi-role Fighter) bharat or rus dwaara viksit paanchavin peedi kaa ladaaku vimaan he. yah roosi sukhoi esayu-57 kii ek vyutpann pariyojna he jise roosi vaayu sena ke liye viksit kiya ja rahaa he. exfee bhartiya sanskaran ke liye kaha jaataa he jabaki sanyukt pariyojna ko ab persepective multi-role fiter kaha jaataa he.
 purna exfee main sukhoi esayu-57 ke kul 43 sudhaar shaamil honge, jisamen stealth, superkruj, unnat sensor, netwarking or ladaaku avionics shaamil hai. exfee ke do alag-alag prototip viksit kiye jaaenge, ek rus dwaara or bharat dwaara. bhartiya sanskaran me payalat or sah-payalat/hathiyaar system oparator ke liye do seat hogi.
 brahmos pariyojna kii safalataa ke baad, rus or bharat ne 2007 kii shuruyaat main ek paanchaven janareshan ladaaku vimaan program kaa adhyayan or vikaas karne ke liye sahmati vyakt kii. 27 oktober 2007 ko asia times ke saath ek saakshaatkaar main sukhoi ke nideshak mikhail pogosyan ne kaha: "ham 50-50 anupaat main funding, ejineering or bauddhik sampada ko saajha karenge."
 11 sitambar 2010 ko, yah bataaya gaya tha ki bharat or rus praarambhik dizin anubandh par sahamat hue the. or ise cabinet kii manjuuri ko bheja jayega. sanyukt vikaas saude main prayek desh 6 billian dollar kaa nivesh karegaa or exfee ladaaku vimaan kaa vikaas karne main 8-10 saal lagenge. disambar 2010 main, hindustan aironautics limited or roosi companiyon rosoboron export or sukhoi ke bich bharat-rus ladaaku vimaan ke praarambhik dizin ke liye ek samjhauta gyaapan par hastakshar hua tha.
 2 sitambar, 2017 ko, bhartiya vaayu sena ne rakrakhaav kaaryakramon kii maang or pariyojna ke saath aage badhane ke liye anichhaa jataai. jisame mukhya kaarnon ke rup main ucch rakrakhaav kii laagat kaa ullekh kiya. bhartiya vaayu sena ne bataaya ki exfee ladaaku vimaan ke rakrakhaav me bahut zyada laagat aane vaali he. bharat kaa roosi ladaaku vimaanon ke saath ek lanbaa anubhav he kyukki bharat lagbhag 200 sukhoi esayu-30 sanchalit karata hai.. paanch bhartiya sukhoi esayu-30 pichley paanch varshon main durghatnagrast ho gaye hai, jis par bharat ne bearing vifaltaaon ko kaaran bataaya. bharat china ke changdu je-20 stealth ladaaku vimaan ke baraabar vaali kshamta ke liye apana paanchavin peedi ke ladaaku kaa utpaadan karnaa pasand kar rahaa he.
 inamen se adhikansh aankade sukhoi tii-50 prototip ke liye hote hai. echeel exfee ke bare me abhi zyada jaankaree nahin he.
