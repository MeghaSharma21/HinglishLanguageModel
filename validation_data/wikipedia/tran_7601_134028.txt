 digansh yaa azimuth kisi goleey nirdeshaank pranali main ek vishesh kone ke maap kaa naam he.
 udhaaharan ke liye, agar dharti par khadey kisi darshak ke liye kisi taare kaa digansh maapna ho to khagoleey nirdeshaank paddati ke anusaar yah kiya ja saktaa he. isamen darshak ko khagoleey gole ke uttari dhruv se jodne vaali kaalpanik rekha maanak disha hoti he. darshak kaa chaaron ore kaa kshitij ek samtal hota he, jisamen yah maanak disha kii rekha bhi vidyaman hoti he. darshak ko taare se jodne vaali ek anya rekha ko lamb ke prayog se is samtal par utaara jane se darshak or samtal par us taare ke neeche vaale bindu ke bich ek duusari rekha banati he. is rekha or maanak disha kii rakhaa ke bich kaa kone hi digansh kehalaata he.
 nauvahan, khagolashastra, abhiyantriki or sainya kshetra main missile ityadi kii udaan nirdhaarit karne ke liye digansh kaa bahut prayog hota he. digansh ek kone he isliye iskaa maap aamtaur par degree main kiya jaataa he.
