 miki runi ek america film abhinayta or manoranjanakrtta the jo apane purna jeevankaal main filmon, television or rangamanch main vyateet kiya.
 unhone juvenil academy award, academy maanad puraskaar, emi or do golden globe puraskaron sahit vibhinn puraskaar praapt kiye. ek bal kalakaar ke rup main kaary karte hue yuvavastha main hi endi hardi kaa kirdaar nibhaane ke liye superstar ban chuke the. unhone apane jivan kaa sabase lambaa samay abhinayta ke rup main bitaayaa. unhone 1920 ke dashak se 2010 ke dashak tak ke 10 daskon ke 92 varsh sakriy rup se film nirmaan main vyateet kiye.
 april 2014 main unke nidhan tak runi mook filmon main kaam karne vaale antim sitaron main se ek the. 1930-1940 ke dashak kii vibhinn filmon ke kalakaaron main se wo ekmaatr jivit sadsy the.
 runi kaa janm joseph ul junior ke rup main neuyork nagar ke brukaleen main hua. unke pita jau ul glassgo, scotland se tathaa unki mata neli dablyoo cansus city, mizori se thim.
