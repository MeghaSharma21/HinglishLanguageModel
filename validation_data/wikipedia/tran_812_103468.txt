 ekrilamide yaa 'acril amide' ek rasinic yogic he jiskaa rasinic sutara C3H5NO. iskaa IUPAC naam prop-2-enamide he. yah gandhhin crystalia padaarth he jo jal, athenul, ether or chloroform main ghulansheel he.
 ekrilamide tamaam tarah ke bhojanon main unke pakaae jane ke dauraan prakritik rup se utpann hota he jisamen coffee, chocolate, baadaam, french friese, crackers, aalu ke chips, anaaj, bread or yahaan tak ki kuch fal or subjiyan bhi shaamil hai. ekrilamide bhojan main tab se maujuud he jab se insaanon ne bhojan pakaana shuru kiya, kintu april 2002 tak, jab swedish vaigyanikon ke ek samooh ne shodh prastut kiya jisamen kuch backed evam frid foods main yogic ke darshneey staron kaa pataa lagaaya gaya tha, bhojan main iske hone kii jaankaree nahin thi. swedish adhyayan se purv, ekrilamide ke liye bhojan kaa vishleshan nahin kiya gaya kyonki ise na to sanghatak ke rup main prayog kiya jaataa tha or na hi bhojan ke avyav ke rup main isaki jaankaree thi.
