 samanta kii pratimaa yaa dow babasaheb ambedkar smarak, mumbai' 106.68 meter unchaa maharashtra sarkaar dwaara prastaavit bharat ke pratham kanoon mantri tathaa bhartiya samvidhan ke pita dow bhimrao ambedkar ji kaa smarak he. bharat ke pradhanmantri shri narendra modi ne oktober 2015 ko dow bhimrao ambedkar ji kii is vishalkay murti evam vishwa-smarak ke nirmaan kaa mumbai main shilanyas kiya. indu mil kii 12 ekad bhoomi par yah bhavy smarak banegaa. samanta kaa yah smarak dow ambedkar ke samadhi sthal chaityas bhoomi ke kareeb he.
 dow bhimrao ambedkar ji ne karodo deshavaasiyon ke samanta ke puure jivan bhar sangharsh kiya, isliye bhimrao ko samanta kaa pratik kaha jaataa he.
 ambedkar smarak mumbai par 18 agast 2012 main bharat ke bhootpurv pradhanmantri dow manmohan singh ne ghoshnaa kii thi. yah dadar main puraane indu mills kii jameen par sthit kiya ja rahaa he.
 11 oktober 2015, smarak kii aadharshila bharat ke pradhanmantri narendra modi dwaara rakhi gai thi. smarak kaa kaam november 2015 main shuru kiya gaya tha.
 sanrachana ke mukhya pravesh dwaar escees maarg ke saath sate Cadell sadak madhyamik pahunch bindu ke rup se hoga. smarak bheed ke aasaan andolan ke liye chaityas bhoomi ke saath joda jayega. smarak bhartiya rupaya 425 karod 12 acre main tatkaaleen indu mil kii jameen kharch honge. iskaa mukhya aakarshan ek talab ke chaaron ore ek 25,000 varg foot stupa hoga. 24 pathar pasaliyon ke saath ek gumbad kii tarah sanrachana bhukhand ke madhya main kiya jayega. vahaan ek ashok chakra smarak sanrachana ko cover kiya jayega. vahaan bhi ek prastaavit 39,622 varg foot interactive ambedkar ke jivan ke kai pahaluon ko pradarshit sangrahalay hoga. smarak 400 vaahanon ke aasapaas park karne ke liye ek suvidha ke liye hoga.
 bhartiya rupaya 110.95 par sabase mahangi hissaa hoga karod . stupa 40 meter oonchai 80 meter kii paridhi vyas kaa ho jayega ek dharidaar chat is prakaar buddha chaityas jaisi kerani hogi. ek aath stareey kaansya chandwa gumbad ke pair main ek camel talab ke saath 2,400 varg meter kaa ek nirmit kshetra main stupa ke sheersh par buddha kii aath guna path kaa pratinidhitiv.
 vahaan bhi 13,000 logon ke baithne kii kshamta ke saath smarak par vipashyana haul kaa prastaav he.
 sangharsh kii gailaree bhi vahaan ho jayega ek pustkaalay main 50,000 varg foot bhar main faile ke saath jivan or dow bi aar ambedkar ke kaam ke saath jude mahatvapurn ghatnaon ko chitrit karne ke liye.
 smarak kii purv aadharshila ghatana kii bichaane ke baad, dow. ambedkar ke parivaar or maharashtra ke ambedkarwaadi netaa memorial dizin se santusht nahin the. jabaki dusron ne sochaa kii yah memorial dow bhimrao ambedkar kaa kad karaara nahin tha kuch dizin ke vyaavhaarik vivaran par chintaa thi. prakaash ambedkar smarak ke liye dabaaya he karne ke liye ek 'think tanks sanstha ", duniya bhar main vidwaanon ke liye antarrashtriya maanakon ke ek bauddhik centre bauddhik gatividhi ke liye aane ke rup main dow. bhimrao ambedkar ek bauddhik vishalata he jinhone kai kshetron alag kai myanon main yogadan diya tha. aanandaraj ambedkar ne bhi rajya sarkaar ko apane dizin ke rup main dow. bhimrao ambedkar, samanta kii pratimaa ke ek 360 foot oonchi pratimaa ko shaamil nahin kiya gaya tha jinka ek praaroop kaa suzav diya tha. praaroop sahi ab or adhik ek smarak kii tulana main jahaan jogging 'park kii tarah lag rahaa he. netaaon to mumbai ke kshitij ke ek prabhaavshaalee hisse ke rup main karne ke liye or pehali baat yah he ki mumbai main utarane aagantukon ke aane par unki aankhon karnaa hona chahie.
 mumbai, dadar sthit indu mil kii jameen par banane vaale dow. bhimrao ambedkar ke bhavy antarrashtri smarak ke nae praaroop ko agast 2016 ko mukhyamantri devendra fadanvis dwaara manjuuri de di gai he. yah manjuuri rajya ke saamaajik nyaay mantri rajkumaar badole kii adhyakshata main banaai gai ek sadasyeey samiti ne di he. is nae praaroop ke mutabik indu mil kii jameen par jo ambedkar smarak viksit kiya jayega, usamen dow. bhimrao ambedkar kii 350 foot oonchi pratimaa sthapit kii janaa prastaavit he. pehle jo praaroop banaaya gaya tha usamen keval 80 foot oonchi pratimaa kaa hi prastaav tha. lekin ab america ke stachu of liberty se bhi oonchi pratimaa sthapit hogi. yah pratimaa sansad bhawan parisar main sthapit dow. ambedkar pratimaa jaisi hi hogi or smarak ko sanchi ke vishwa prasiddh buddha stupa kii tarah banaaya jayega. iske alaava yahaan banane vaali art gailaree main dow. bhimrao ambedkar ke jivan par aadhaarit kalakritiyaan bhi sthapit kii jaaengi. 2014 main pradhanmantri narendra modi ke haathon smarak ke liye bhumipujan kiya gaya tha. lekin kuch sanghathanon ne smarak ke praaroop ko lekar aitraaj jataayaa tha. republican sena ke aanandaraj ambedkar sahit kuch sanghathanon ne maang kii thi ki bhimrao kaa smarak america ke stachu of liberty se ooncha ho. or is sandarbh main faisla lene ke liye badole kii adhyakshata main cameti banaai gai thi.
