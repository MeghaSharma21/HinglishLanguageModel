 junapani ke shilavart bharat ke maharashtra rajya ke nagpur nagar ke paas sthit junapani shahar main mahapashan shilavart hai jo yahan pragaitihaasik kaal main banaae gaye the. yahan pattharon ke aise 300 chakra mili hai. san 1879 main itihaaskaar je much rivat-karnac ne yahan khudaai karke inaki khoj kii thi. in shilavarton main lohe ke kanjar, kulhaade, fawde, kangan, challe, taraashne ke paine auzaar or chimate mili hai. yahan laal or kaali matti ke bartanon ke tukade bhi mili hai jinamein se kuch par kaale rang se dizin bane hue hai. kuch jagahon par log bhi dafan hai or vahaan kearn bane hue hai.
 itihaaskaaron ke anusaar yah shilavart shaayad 1000 isapurv kaal main bane hon. kuch pattharon par pyaalon ke chihn hai or unhen dishaao ke anusaar khadaa kiya gaya he jis se yah sambhaavna banati he ki inkaa prayog khagol-jigyaasa se sambandhit rahaa ho. haalanki bahut se shilavart pashaan yug main bane hote hai, apni nirmaan aayu ke mutabiq yah shilavart louh yug main bane the.
