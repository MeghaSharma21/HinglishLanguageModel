 yapaniy pashchimi karnataka main ek jain sampradaay tha jo ki ab vilupt ho chukaa he. unke bare main pehla varnan 475-490 i main palasika ke, kadamb ke raja mrigsavarman ke shilalekhon main miltaa he, jinhone jain mandir ke liye daan diya tha or yapaniyon, nirgrathiyon tathaa kurchakon ko anudaan diya.
 shaq 1316 kaa antim shilaalekh jisamen yapaniyon ke bare main varnan he, dakshin pashchim karnataka ke tuluv ilaake main mila he. 
 darshan-sara ke anusaar ve shevatambar sampradaay kii ek shakha the. haalaanki shwetambar lekhakon dwaara unako digambar ke taur par dekha gaya he. yapaniy sadhu nagn rahete the lekin saath hi saath kuch shwetambar drushtikon ko bhi anusaran karte the. unke paas shwetambar ritiyon kii apni khud kii vyakhya thi.
 malaygir ne apane granth nandisutra main likhaa he ki mahaan vyakarnacharya shkatayan, jo ki rashtrakut ke raja amoghvarsh nripatung ke sam-samayik the, ek yapniya the.
 yapaniy duusari shatabdi main apane prabhutv kii ore agrasar hue or dakkan kii ore prasthaan ke baad digambaron or shwetambaron ke saath vilay ke pashchaat unka patan ho gaya.
