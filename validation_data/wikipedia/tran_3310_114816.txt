 labhaansh niti se aashay labhaansh vitarit karne ke siddhanton va yojanaon se hota he. labhaansh niti kaa arth sanchaalkon ke us nirnay se he jiske dwaara ve yah tay karte hai ki laabh kaa kitna bhaag labhaansh ke rup main vitarit kiya jaay or kitna pratidhaarit kiya jaay.
 ek vyavsaayik sanstha kaa uddeshy laabh kamanaa he. yah apane aap main ek mahatvapurn vittiya nirnay he ki arjeet laabh kaa prayog kis prakaar kiya jaay. mukhya prashn yah uthta he ki laabh kaa purna upbhog swaamiyon dwaara kiya jaae yaa use vyavsaay main hi pratidhaarit karke punarviniyog kiya jaye. ekal vyapaaree kii dasha main is prakaar ke nirnay lene main koi bhi samasya khadi nahin hoti he. isi prakaar saajhedaari sanstha kii dasha main saajhedaari sanlekh main spasht rup se ullekh kiya gaya he ki laabh ko saajhedaari/swaamiyon main kis prakaar vitarit kiya jayega. haan, company sanghathan swaroop kii dasha main yah nirnay kuch jatil avashya prathit hota he. company adhinium me spasht ullekh kiya gaya he ki ek company ko apane laabh kaa kitna pratishat anshadhariyon ke bich main vitarit karnaa evam kitne pratishat pratidhaarit karnaa he jisase company apni bhavishya kii yojana nirdhaarit kar sake.
 company ek 'kritrim vyakti' he or vyavsaay ke anshadhari adhik faile hue hote hai. laabh ke prayog sambandhi nirnay kaa bhaar kuch vyaktiyon ke samooh par hi hota he jinhen sanchaalak mandal kehete hai. anya sanghathan swaroopon kii bhonti company ke shuddh laabh ke bantwaare kii samasya bhi yaa to laabh ko vyavsaay main hi pratidhaarit karne kii yaa anshadhariyon ko labhaansh ke rup main baantne kii hoti he. anshadhariyon main vibhajya laabh ke vitaran sambandhi nirnay mahatvapurn hota he. is sambandh main liye gayi nirnay kaa matlab anshadhariyon ko adhik aay, kam aay athwa kuch aay nahin ho saktaa he. vidyaman anshadhariyon ke ruukh ko prabhavit karne ke saath-saath labhaansh dene ke nirnay kaa prabhaav bhavi anshadhariyon, skandh vinimay va vittiya sansthaaon ke ruukh va vyavhaar par bhi pad saktaa he, kyonki labhaansh kaa sambandh company ke mulya se hota he jo company ke anshon ke bazaar mulya ko prabhavit karata he. 
 labhaansh ke rup main laabh kaa vitaran vivaad kaa vishay ban saktaa he kyonki vibhinn pakshon jaise sanchaalak, karmchaaree, anshadhari, rinpatradhari, rin pradaan karne vaali sanstha aadi kaa hit takaraav kaa hota he. jahaan koi paksh niyamit aay ke paksh main hota he, to koi puunji vruddhi yaa puunjigat laabh main ruchi rakhata he is prakaar labhaansh niti kaa nirmaan karnaa ek jatil nirnay he. anek baton kaa saavdhaneepurvak manan karnaa padataa he parantu yah baat tay he ki koi tadarth kadam uthaane ke bajaay labhaansh ke sambandh main ek yathochit dirghakaalin niti kaa paalan karnaa chahie. 
 vittiya prabandh kaa mukhya uddeshy sanstha ke bazaar mulya kaa adhikeekaran hota he. sanstha ke sam anshon kaa bazaar mulya is niti se prabhavit hota he ki shuddh laabh athwa aadhiky ko labhaansh bhugtan or punarviniyojan ke bich kis prakaar aavantit kiya jaataa he. prabandhakon ke saamne yah vikalp nahin hota he ki labhaansh baante yaa na baante. haan, yah prashn avashya hota he ki kitna labhaansh baante? iskaa uttar labhaansh niti se miltaa he. labhaansh niti kaa arth labhaansh vitarit karne ke siddhanton va yojana se hota he. weston evam brigam ne likhaa he, 
 anshadhariyon main labhaansh ke rup main arjan ke vitaran ke sambandh main prabandh dwaara nirmit niti ko hi labhaansh niti kehete hai. keval ek vishesh satr main dey labhaansh se hi iskaa sambandh nahin hota he balki kai varshon tak apanaae jane vaale kadamon se bhi yah sambandh rakhata he. 
 labhaansh niti nirmaan karne se purv nimnlikhit prashnon ke uttar dhundne honge -
 laabh kaa vah bhaag, jise vibhajya laabh kehete hai, jo company ke sadasyon ko praapt hota he, labhaansh kehalaata he. vibhajya laabh se aashay company ke un laabhon se he jo vaidhanik taur par company ke anshadhariyon ke bich baante ja sakate hai. company adhinium, 1974 sanshodhit 1975 ke anusaar vibhajya laabh kaa abhipraay company ke shuddh laabh kaa 1/3 bhaag yaa company ke sam anshon ke ankit mulya par bhugtan yogya 12 pratishat labhaansh kii raashi va purvadhikaar anshon par dey labhaansh kii raashi se hota he. is aashay ke liye shuddh laabh vah laabh hota he, jo company adhinium, 1956 kii dhara 394 ke praavdhaanon ke anusaar ho or usamen se iskar kii raashi gata di gayi ho tathaa company adhinium kii dhara 205 ke anusaar hraas gata diya gaya ho.
 labhaansh vastutah kul arjeet aay main se samast vyayon ko ghataane ke baad va vishesh prakaar ke koshon va karon ke liye praavdhaan karne ke baad bache aadhiky kaa hi ek bhaag hota he. is aadhiky par sam anshadhariyon kaa hi adhikaar hota he. haan purvadhikaar anshon kii dasha main unhen is aadhiky par sam anshadhariyon kii tulana main praathamikta praapt hoti he. yadhyapi sam anshadhariyon kaa is aadhiky par puraa adhikaar hota he phir bhi kanoonee taur par anshadhari iske purna vitaran ke liye yaa tatkaal vitaran ke liye company ko baadhya nahin kar sakate hai.
 labhaansh niti ko nirdhaarit karne vaale tatv nimnlikhit hai :-
 labhaansh kaa vitaran company ke vartmaan va gat varsh ke laabhon main se hi kiya ja saktaa he isliye labhaansh niti ko prabhavit karne vaala sabase pramukh tatv company kaa laabh hota he. company kaa laabh, labhaansh kii ucchtam seemaa nirdhaarit karne main sahaayak hota he. isi prakaar sthaayee rup se laabh arjeet karne vaali companiyaan un companiyon kii apeksha jinke laabh main ucchavachan hota rahata he laabh kaa adhik bhaag labhaansh ke rup main vitarit karti hai.
 yadi company ke paas labhaansh kii ghoshnaa ke samay paryaapt nakad uplabdh ho to nakad labhaansh kaa bhugtan uchit rahegaa. udhaar sharton par vikray karne vaali sasthaaon main taralata sthiti kamjor hone par nakad labhaansh na dekar bonus anshon kaa nigarman kiya jaataa he isliye company kii taralata sthiti bhi labhaansh nirnay ko prabhavit karti he.
 vigat varsh kii tulana main achaanak labhaansh dar ekadam badha di jaae to bazaar main satte kii pravriti penpegi. yathasambhav labhaansh dar sthir rakhane kaa prayaas company prabandh dwaara kiya jaataa he. isliye labhaansh ghoshit karte samay sanchaalkon ko vigat varshon kii labhaansh daron ko aadhaar bananaa chaiye. 
 anshadhari jab ansh kray karte hai to ve labhaansh ke bare main kuch apekshaayen bana lete hai, unka aadar sanchaalkon ko karnaa chahie. vaidhanik drushti se bhi sanchaalak company kii aay ko vibhinn uddeshyon ke liye upyog main laane ke liye swatantra hote hai par anshadhariyon ke vichaaron va aashaaon ko ve bhula nahin sakate, varanaa bhavishya main atirikt puunji ekatra karne main baari kathinaayee aa sakati he.
 puraani companiyaan udaar labhaansh niti apana sakati hai jabaki nav sthapit companiyon ko apni aay kaa adhikansh bhaag vikaas kaaryon main lagaana aavashyak hota he. atah ve anshadhariyon ko kam dar se labhaansh dekar shesh aay kaa pratidhaaran kar liya karti he.
 ucchavachanon arthath teji-mandi ke saath-saath labhaansh niti bhi parivartit ho jaati he. mandi ke samay laabh kii maatra ghat jaati he faltah labhaansh dar main kami karne ke liye company baadhya ho jaati he. agar company ne labhaansh samanikaran kosh paryaapt maatra main rakhaa ho to yen-ken prakaren labhaansh kii uchit dar banaae rakhane main safal ho jaati hai. teji ke samay to laabh badhane se company jagat main labhaansh dar badhaane kii hode si lag jaati he.
 sarkaar kii nitiyon main samay samay par parivartan hote rahete hai jisase company ke laabhon main kami va vruddhi hona avashyambhavi ho jaataa he. sarkaar kii audyogic shram evam prashulk nitiyon kaa labhaansh niti par prabhaav padataa he. udhaaharan ke liye udaarikaran ke vartmaan daur main company kshetra ke niyantran va shulkon main kami ho rahi he. faltah laabh va labhaansh par dhanatmak prabhaav pad rahaa he.
 kabhi-kabhi sarkaar puunji nirmaan kii gati teevr karne ke uddeshy se laabhon ko adhikansh bhaag ko punarviniojit karne vaali companiyon ko aay kar main suvidhaayen detii he. isi prakaar adhik labhaansh vitarit karne vaali sansthaaon par atirikt kar lagaakar laabhon ke punarviniyog ko protsaahit kiya jaataa he. isliye labhaansh niti par kar niti kaa prabhaav padataa he.
 kisi bhi loktantric desh main janamat tathaa saarvajanik pratikriyaaon kaa vyapak prabhaav labhaansh niti par padataa he. adhik labhaansh dene vaali companiyaan kii janata dwaara aalochana kii jaati he. shramik apane vetan or bonus vruddhi kii or upabhokta vastu mulya main kami kii maang karne lagate hai jisase company ko kathor labhaansh niti apnaani padati he.
