 maithil brahmanon kaa naam mithila ke naam par padaahai. mithila ke brahmano ko maithil brahman kaha jaataa he. mithila pracheen kaal main bharat kaa ek rajya tha| mithila vartmaan main ek saanskrithik kshetra he jisame bihar ke tirhut, darbhanga, munger, kosi, purniya or bhagalpur ke aadhunik pramandalon ke saath saath nepal or jharkhand ke kuch bhaag bhishamil hai. janakapur, darbhanga or madhubani maithil brahmano kaa pramukh saanskrit kenndra he. maithil brahman bihar, nepal, braj uttar pradesh va jharkhand ke devgarh main adhik hai. panch goud brahmano ke antargat maithil brahman, kanyakubj brahman, saraswat brahman, goud brahman, utkal brahman hai |
 brajasth maithil brahman ve brahman hai jo gayasuddin tululaq se lekar akbar ke shashan kaal tak tirhut se bharat kii tatkaaleen rajdhani agra main based tathaa samyoparant kendriya braj ke teen jilo me auranjeb ke kushasan se pratadit hokar bas gayi. braj me paye jane vaale maithil brahman usee samay se braj me pravaas kar rahe hai. jo ki mithila ke ganmaanya vidwano dwaara shodhoparant brajasth maithil brahmano ke naam se gyaat hue. ye brahman braj ke agra, aligad, mathura or hathras me pramukh rup se rahete hai. yaha se bhi pravasit hokar yah dilli, ajmer, jaipur, jodhpur, bikaner, badaudara, dahaud, lakhnanau, kanpur aadi sthano par rah rahe hai. mogul shaasha aurangzeb ne mithila sahit sampoorn bharat par apane shashan kaal main atyaachaar kiya iske atyaachaar se peedit hokar bahut se mithilavasi brahman mithila se palaayan kar anya pradeshon main bas gaye brij pradesh main rahane vaale maithilon va mithilavasi maithilon kaa avaagaman bhi band ho gaya aisa 1658 i0 se lekar 1857 kii kranti tak chalata rahaa . 1857 i0 ke baad bhartiya samaaj sudhaarkon ne ek aaazaad bharat kaa sapana dekha tha mithila ke brahman samaaj bhi aaazaad bharat kaa sapana dekhne lage 'swami brahmanand saraswati ne thik isi samay jaati utthan kii aawaaj ko buland kiya unhone jaati utthan ke liye sampoorn bharat main based mithila vaasiyon se sampark kiya jiskaa parinaam yah hua kii aurangzeb ke samay main mithilavasi or pravasi maithil brahmanon ke jo sambandh tut gaye the vah phir se chaalu ho gaye unhi ke prayaason se aligarh ke maithil brahmanon kaa mithila janaa or mithilavaasiyon kaa aligarh aana sambhav hua isi samay swami ji ne mithila se lautakar aligarh main maithil siddhant sabha kaa aayojan kiya siddhant sabha ke kaaryakartaaon or mithilavasi runa jha dwaara 1882 se 1886 ke bich patr vyavhaar armbh hua.
 maithil brahmano main panji vyavastha he jo maithil brahmano or maithil kaaystho ki vanshavali likhit rup se rakhti he aaj kal yah pratha samaapt ho rahi he.
