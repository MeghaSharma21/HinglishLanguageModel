 punjab krishi vishvavidhyalay ) ludhiana main sthit punjab sarkaar kaa krishi vishvavidhyalay he. isaki sthapana 1962 main kii gayi thi. yah govind ballab pant krishi evam praudyogiki vishvavidhyalay, pantnagar ke baad bharat main doosraa sabase purana krishi vishvavidhyalay he. 1960 ke dashak main bharat kii harith kranti main is vishvavidhyalay kii ahm bhoomika thi. 
 san 2005 main iskaa vibhaajan karke guru angad dev pashuchikitsa evam pashuvigyaan mahavidhyalay nirmit kiya gaya.
