 da fox and da hound 1981 kii ek america anemated film he jiskaa nirmaan walt dijani productions dwaara kiya gaya tha. july 10, 1981 ko jari kii gai yah film walt dijani kii anemated clasix shrinkhalaaon kii 24veen anemated film he. film kii kahani do mitron, ek laal lomady tod or ek shikari kute copper ke ird-gird ghumti he. release ke vakt yah sabase mahangi anemated film thi.
 jab ek choti laal lomadee anaath ho jaati he tab big mamaa, buumar or dinki vidhwaa twid dwaara use god lene kaa intazaam karte hai. twid uskaa naam tod rakhti he kyonki isse use apane chote bachche kii yaad aati he. duusari ore twid kaa padosi amos slade copper naam kaa ek shikari kute kaa pilla laata he or uski apane shikari kute chif se milwataa he. tod or copper ghul mil jate he or hameshaa ghanisht mitra bane rahane kii kasam khate hai. slade copper ke baar baar khilne ke liye bhaagane ke chalate pareshaan ho jaataa he or use sazaa detaa he. duusari ore copper ke saath uske ghar main khelte vakt tod galati se chif ko uthaa detaa he. slade or chif uskaa peechha karte he parantu twid unhe roak kar use bachaa leti he. donon main kaha suni ke baad slade use aagaah karata he kii yadi tod dobaaraa uske khet main ghusaa to vah use maar degaa. jab shikaar kaa mausam aa jaataa he tab slade apane kutton ko lekar jangal main nikal jaataa he. duusari ore big mamaa tod ko samajhati he kii uski or copper kii dosti sahi nahin he kyonki ve donon naiysargik dushman he.
 maheene guzar jate he or tod or copper bade ho jate hai. jis raat copper waapas aata he us raat tod chhipake use milane aata he. copper use samajhaata he kii vah abhi bhi tod kii dosti kii kadra karata he parantu ab vah ek shikari kutta ban chukaa he jiske chalate chizein ab badal gayi he. tabhi chif uth jaataa he or slade ko aagaah kar detaa he or peechha karne par copper tod ko pakad letaa he. parantu copper tod ko bhaagane detaa he or chif va slade ko bhatakane ke liye chale jaataa he. chif phir bhi ek rail kii patari par peechha jari rakhata he jahaan rail se takaraa kar vah ghayal ho jaataa he. copper or slade tod ko is baat ke liye doshi maanate he or badla lene kii kasam khate hai. twid ko ahsaas ho jaataa he kii uskaa paaltu jaanavar uske saath surakshit nahin he or vah use shikaar ke liye surakshit jagah main chod detii he. big mamaa use ek duusari lomadee viksi se milwati he or tabhi slade or copper bhi suraksha kshetra main ghus jate he or donon lomadiyon kaa peechha karte hai. peechha karte vakt slade or copper par ek bhaaluu halma kar detaa he. slade apane hi jaal main funs jaataa he or uski banduk uske haath se dur gir jaati he. copper bhaaluu se ladane kii koshish karata he parantu uske saamne vah tik nahin paata. tod bhi bhaaluu se ladane lagata he or antat donon ek jharne se niche gir jate hai. copper tod drushtikon ke rup main vah jab prakat hota he slade neeche jhil main nihit he, lomadee par aag karne ke liye taiyaar he. copper tod ke saamne apane sharir daalane ke or dur le jane ke manaa kar diya. slade copper ke saath apni bandook or pattiyon ko kam karti he, lekin isse pehle do purv virdohi vidaai se pehle pichley ek muskaan saajha. ghar main, twid nurson waapas swasthya ke liye, jabaki kutton baaki slade. copper, aaraam se pehle, muskaan ke rup main vah din yaad he jab vah tod ke saath dost ban gaye. ek pahari viksi par tod milti he ke rup main vah copper or twid ke gharon par neeche dikhataa he.
