 palpa jila nepal ke pashchimanchal vikaas kshetra ke lumbini anchal main sthit ek atyaadhik urvar evam ghanaa vasti sameta huwa jila hai. is jila kaa kshetrafal 1373 berg kimi or jansankhya karive 3 lakh hai. is jila ke purv main navalparasi, uttar main gulmi, syanja or tanhun pashchim me argakhanchi or gulmi dakshin main navalparasi or rupandehi jilaayein hai. ye jila 27°34’ se 27°57’ uttari akshansh or 83°15’ se 84°22’ purvi deshaantaramen avasthit hai.
