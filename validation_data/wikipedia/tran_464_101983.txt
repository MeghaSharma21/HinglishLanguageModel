 singapore yani singhon kaa poor. yani ise singhon kaa shahar kaha jaataa he. yahan par kai dharmon main vishwaas rakhane vaale, vibhinn deshon kii samskruti, itihaas tathaa bhashaa ke log ekajute hokar rahete hai. mukhya rup se yahan chini tathaa angreji donon bhashaayen prachlit hai. aakaar main mumbai se thode chote is desh main basane vaali kareeb 35 lakh kii aabaadi main chini, malay va 8 pratishat bhartiya log rahete hai.
 dakshin-purv asia main, nicobar dweep samooh se lagbhag 1500 kimi dur ek chota, sundar va viksit desh singapore pichley bees varshon se paryaton va vyapaar ke ek pramukh centre ke rup main ubhara he. aadhunik singapore kii sthapana san 1819 main sar stemford restles ne kii, jinhen east india company ke adhikari ke rup main, dilli sthit tatkaaleen waisarai dwaara, company kaa vyapaar badhaane hetu singapore bheja gaya tha. aaj bhi singapore dollar va saint ke sikkon par aadhunik naam singapore va purana naam singapora ankit rahata he. san 1965 main malaysia se alag hokar nae singapore raashtra kaa udai hua. kinvadanti he ki chaudaavin shatabdi main sumatra dweep kaa ek hindu rajkumaar jab shikaar hetu singapore dweep par gaya to vahaan jangal main singhon ko dekhakar usne ukt dweep kaa namakaran singapora arthath singhon kaa dweep kar diya.
 singapore vishwa kii 9veen tathaa asia kii chauthi sabase badi arthvyavastha he. arthshaastriyon ne singapore ko 'aadhunik chamatkaar' kii sangya di he. yahan ke saare prakritik sansaadhan yahan ke nivasee hi hai. yahan pani malaysia se, doodh, fal va subjiyan newjiland va australia se, daal, chaaval va anya dainik upyog kii vastuen thailand, indonesia aadi se aayaat kii jaati hai.
 1970 ke dashak se yahan ke vyavsaay ne jor pakadana shuru kiya jisane puri duniya kaa dhyaan khinchaa. aaj mukhya vyavsaayon main electronic, rasayan or sevakshetra kii company jaise hotel, kollcenter, baking, outsorcing ityadi pramukh hai. yah videshi nivesh ke liye kaafii aakarshak rahaa he or haal hi main yahan kii companiyon ne videshon main acha nivesh kiya he.
