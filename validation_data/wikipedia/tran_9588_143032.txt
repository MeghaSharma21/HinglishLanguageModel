 kalazar dhire-dhire viksit hone vaala ek deshi rog he jo ek kosiya paraseete yaa jinas lisnamania se hota he. kalazar ke baad darmal lisnamaniosis ek aisi sthiti he jab lisnamania twachaa koshaanuon main jate hai or vahaan rahete hue viksit hote hai. yah darmal licion ke rup main taiyaar hote hai. kai kalazar main kuch varshon ke upachaar ke baad pi ke di en prakat hote hai.
 saamaanya bolchaal ki bhashaa main ise baaluu makkhi yaa sand fli kaha jaataa he.
