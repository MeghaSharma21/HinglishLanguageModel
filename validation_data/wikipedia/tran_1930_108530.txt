 citigroup ink. neuyork shahar main aadhaarit ek pramukh america vittiya seva company he. citigroup kaa gathan, itihaas kaa sabase bada vilay tha jo vishaal banking company citicorp or vittiya samooh trevellersagroup ke sangyojan se 7 april 1998 ko falit hua.
 citigroup ink. duniya kii sabase badi vittiya seva network he, jo lagbhag 16,000 karyaalayon ke saath duniya bhar main 140 deshon main faili hui he. is company ke duniya bhar main lagbhag 260,000 karmchaaree hai or isamen 140 se adhik deshon main 200 million se adhik graahakon kaa khaataa he. yah US tregeri pratibhutiyon main ek praathmik dealer he.
 2008 ke vittiya sankat ke dauraan citigroup ko baari nuksaan sahanaa padaa or november 2008 main america sarkaar dwaara bade paimaane par die gaye ek belout dwaara uskaa bachaav hua.iske sabase bade shairdharakon main middle east or singapore ke dhan shaamil hai. 27 farvari 2009 main, citigroup ne ghoshnaa kii ki sanyukt rajya sarkaar is company main 36% share legi jiske tahat 25 billian dollar kii apatkaaleen sahaayta aam sheyaron main tabdeel hogi; yah hissaa gata kar 27% kar diya gaya jab citigroup ne apane aam sheyaron main se 21 billian share or equity bech die joki america itihaas main sabase badi ekal share bikri thi or jo ek maah pehle bank of america ke dwaara beche gaye 19 billian dollar ke shayr ko bhi paar kar gai.
 citigroup, sanyukt rajya america ke chaar bade bainkon main se ek he, jinamein shaamil he bank of america, jepi morgan chess or wales phargo.
 citigroup kaa gathan 8 oktober 1998 ko citicorp or trevelors group ke bich hue 140 billian dollar ke vilay ke pashchaat hua, jiskaa uddeshy vishwa kii sabase badi vittiya seva sanghathan bananaa tha. is prakaar, company kaa itihaas, kai companiyon ke kaamkaaj main vibhajit he jo samay-samay par yaa to citicorp ke saath milti gai, joki 100 se bhi adhik deshon main sanchaalan karti ek bahurashtriya banking nigam he; yaa trevelors group main shaamil ho gai, jiske vyavsaay main credit sevaayen, upabhokta vitt, dallali or beemaa shaamil hai. isliye company ke itihaas kii gananaa nimnlikhit companiyon kii sansthapana kii taarikh se shuru hoti he: 1812 main, city bank of neuyork ; 1870 main, bank handlovi; 1873 main smith barni, 1873 main banamex; 1910 main solomon bros.
 iske itihaas kii shuruyaat city bank of neuyork se hui, jo neuyork rajya dwaara 16 june 1812 ko 2 million dollar ke saath shaspatrit kiya gaya. neuyork ke vyapaariyon ke samooh ke liye kaary karte hue vyapaar ke liye is bank ko usee varsh 14 sitambar ko kholaa gaya or samul osgud ko company ke pratham adhyaksh ke rup main chunaa gaya. 1865 main company ke naam ko badal kar di naishanal city bank of neuyark kar diya gaya, aisa uske nae america rashtriya banking pranali se judane ke baad or 1865 tak sabase bade america bank banane ke baad kiya gaya. yah 1913 main fedral rigerv bank of neuyark ke liye pehla yogadan karta bana or agle varsh isane ek U.S. bank kii pehali videshi shakha kaa udghaatan bunus yrs main kiya, haalanki bank unnisvin sadi ke madhya ke baad se krishi arthvyavastha main sakriy rahaa, udhaaharan ke liye cuba ke chini udyog. 1918 main U.S. overseas bank antarrashtriya banking corporation kii kharid ne ise 1 billian dollar sampatti ko paar karne vaali pehali america bank banane main madad kii or 1929 main yah vishwa kii sabase badi vanijyik bank ban gai. jaise-jaise yah viksit hota gaya, yah bank vittiya sevaaon main ek agraniy prarvatak ban gaya, iske saath hi yah bachat ; asurakshit vyaktigat rin ; graahak jaanch khaaton or parcramy jamaa pramaan patr par yogic byaaj kii peshkash karne vaala pehla pramukh amareeki bank ban gaya.
 1955 main is bank ne apana naam badal kar di first naishanal city bank of neuyork rakh liya, jo 1962 main company ke 150 veen varshganth par chota karke first naishanal city bank kar diya gaya. is company ne vyavasthit rup se patte or credit card kshetron main pravesh kiya or iske dwaara landan main shuru kiya gaya USD jamaa praaanpatr 1888 se bazaar main sabase pehla nayaa parcramy likhat ke rup main chihnit hua. bank ne 1967 main apni first naishanal city charge sarvice credit card kii shuruyaat kii - jo aam taur par "avarithing card" ke naam se janaa jaataa tha or jo aage chal kar mastercard bana.
 1976 main, CEO walter bi. riston ke netritv main first naishanal city bank kaa citibank, N.A. ke naam se punah namakaran kiya gaya . iske kuch hi samay baad, bank ne citicard kii shuruyaat kii, jisne 24-ghante ATM ke upyog kaa bidaa uthaaya. jaise-jaise bank kaa vistaaran hota gaya, 1981 main naar warren-kairline springs credit card company ko kharid liya gaya. 1984 main jon es. read ko CEO bana diya gaya or city landan main sthit CHAPS samashodhan grih kaa sansthaapak sadsy bana. unke netritv main, aane vaale 14 varshon ne citibank ko sanyukt rajya kaa sabase bada bank banate dekha, jo vishwa main credit card or charge card kaa sabase bada pradaata bana or apni vaishvik pahunch ko 90 se bhi adhik deshon main vistrut kiya.
 trevelors group, vilay ke samay, vittiya masalon kaa ek vividh samooh tha, jo CEO sandy veil ke netritv main ek saath milaayi gayi. isaki jaden commercial credit main thi, jo control data corporation kii ek sahaayak company thi or jise 1986 main veil dwaara usee varsh ke purvardh main company kii bandor sanbhaalane ke pashchaat, nijee bana diya gaya. do varsh baad, veil ne priamerica ke adhikansh sheyaron par aadhipaty hasil kar liya - jo ek samooh company thi or jisane pehle se hi jivan beemaa company e el williams or saath hi stock dalal smith barne ko apane saath mila liya tha. nai company ne priamerica naam ko dhaaran kiya or ek aisi "cross-sailing" ranneeti ko apanaaya jiske tahat janak company ke bheetar ke sansthaaon main se prayek ne ek duusare kii sevaaon ko bechney kaa laksh tay kiya. uski gaiyr vittiya vyapaaron ko band kar diya gaya.
 1992 sitambar main, travelers insurance, jo bure achal sampatti nivesh kaa saamana kar rahaa tha or andrew tufaan ke baad nirantar hote mahatvapurn nuksaanon ke chalate disambar 1993 main parimerica ke saath ek ranneetik gathbandhan kiya jisane tahat yah ek ekal company me ekikrut ho gai. is adhigrahan ke saath hi, yah samooh travelers ink. ban gaya. iske vyapaar main sampatti or hataahat or jivan or varshikiyaan jokhim ankan kshamtaayen bhi jodi gayi. is bich, travelers kaa vishisht laal chaataa logo ko, jo saude main adhigrahit kiya gaya tha, nae naam vaale sanghathan ke daayre me aane vaale sabhi vyapaaron me lagu kiya gaya. is avadhi ke dauraan, travelers ne sharesan leman kaa adhigrahan kiya jo ek khudara dallali or aasti prabandhan form tha jiskaa netritv veil ne 1985 tak kiya or use smith barne ke saath vilay kar diya.

 6 april 1998 ko, citicorp or travelers group ke vilay kii ghoshnaa ko saarvajanik kiya gaya, jiske tahat lagbhag 700 billian dollar sampatti vaali ek 140 billian dollar kii form banaai gai. is saude ne travelers ko citicorp ke khudara graahakon ke liye mutual fund or beemaa kaa vipanan karne main sakasham bana diya, jabaki banking prabhaagon ko niveshkon or beemaa kharidaaron ke vistaarit graahak aadhaar main abhigam pradaan kiya.
 ek vilay ke rup main prastut kiye jane ke baavjood, yah sauda vastav main ek share swap ke jaisaa tha, jiske tahat travelers group ne citicorp ke sampurna sheyaron ko 70 billian dollar main kharid liya or prayek citicorp ke share ke badale city group ke 2.5 nae share jari kiye. is tantra ke madhyam se, prayek company ke maujuuda shairdharak nai company aadhe bhaag ke maalik ban gaye. jahaan ek ore nai company apane naam main citicorp ke "city" brand ko banaae rakhi thi, vahiin duusari ore usne travelers ke vishisht "laal chaate" ko nae corporate logo ke rup main apanaaya, jo 2007 tak istemaal kiya gaya.
