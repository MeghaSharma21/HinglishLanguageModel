 radhamohan gokul hindi sahityakar the. inkaa janm 1865 main uttar pradesh ke gopalganj naamak sthaan main hua tha. 3 sitambar 1935 ko pechish ke kaaran unki maut ho gai. unki smriti ko saheje rakhane ke liye akhil bhartiya hindi sahitya sammelan, prayag ne swatantraaa-sangram or samaaj-sudhaar par utkrishth pustak likhane vaale lekhakon ko "radhamohan gokulji puraskaar" dene kii ghoshnaa kii.
