 stantman 1994 main bani hindi bhashaa kii ection film he. isamen jaiki shroff or zeba bakhtiyaar pramukh bhoomika main hai. 
 amir rina apane bhai ke dost, pune sthit bajrang tiwari jo motorbike-stantman he ke saath pyaar main padati he. uski vidhwaa maa ko wo pasand nahin he. jab rina ne usase shaadi karne kaa jor diya, to uski maa ne vijay or bajrang se apane pati ke jaise apane jivan ko khatre main na daalane ke liye baat karne kaa prayaas kiya. bajrang ke pita bhi isi khatarnaak peshey se jivan kho chuke the. vah bajrang ko uske saath rahane kii peshkash karti he, lekin usne manaa kar diya. vivaah hota he or rina apane pati ke saath jhopadiyon main rahane ke liye chali jaati he. iske turant baad vah ek beti guddi ko janm detii he. vijay ke maare jane ke baad unka jivan bikhar gaya or saboot bajrang kii ore ingit karte hai. rina use chhod detii he or apni maa ke saath aage rahati he or chaahati he ki guddi bhi uske saath rahen. baad main vah paati he ki bajrang guddi ko lekar faraar ho gaya he.
 sabhi geet samir dwaara likhit; sara sangeet nadeem-shravan dwaara rachit.
