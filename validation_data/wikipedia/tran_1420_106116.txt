 bobsley ek sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main ek ghatana he jahaan ek do yaa chaar vyakti team ek vishesh rup se dizin kiye gaye barf ke trek kii taraf chalati he, jisaki jeet vaali team sabase tej samay ke saath maarg ko puraa karti he. 1924 main chemonix, france main pehali baar sheetakaaleen khelon ke baad is ghatana ko vishesh rup se squa vaili main 1960 ke khelon ke apwaad ke saath dikhaaya gaya, jab aayojan samiti ne kharch kam karne ke liye ek trek banane kaa faisla nahin kiya. is apwaad ke alaava, chaar-purush pratiyogita har game par pratispardhaa kar rahi he . do-purush ghatana ko 1932 games main pesh kiya gaya tha or 2002 ke sheetakaaleen olympic main pehali baar ek mahila pratiyogita hui thi.
 kam se kam ek swarna padak or ek kaansya padak jeetne vaale athlete neeche soochibaddh hai.
