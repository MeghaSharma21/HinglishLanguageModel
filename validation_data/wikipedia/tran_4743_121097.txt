 chitrapur saraswat brahman hindu dharm ke atyadhik tyoharon ko manaaten hai. ve tyoharon ko hindu panchang ke anusaar manaate hai. ve har tyohaar ke anusaar vishisht rup se bhojan ko taiyaar karte hai. ve har ek tyohaar ko alag andaaz main manaate hai.
 panchang ke anusaar nayaa saal kaa shuruwat caitra maah se hota he. usee maah main gudi padva manaaya jaataa hai.gudi padva ko ugadi/yugadi/panavaar puja jaise naamon se bhi janaa jaataa hai. is din kaa prarambh chitrapur saraswath brahman ghar main mangal murthi yani bhagwaan ganpati or apane-apane ghar ke kul-devtaa ko samaran karte hue, ek shubh varsh kaa aagaman, puja-paath se karte hai. naye kapadon ko pahante hai. ghar ke striyaan vishesh bhojan taiyaar karte hai jaise kheer, madgane-jo channa daal, kaashew, chaaval, nariyal kaa doodh, gud se banaaya gaya padaarth he ; upakari jo tindora or kaashew se banaaya gaya padaarth he or sukke jo navalkol yaa aalu se banaaya jaat he.shakkar athwa gud or ghi main bhuna hua neem kaa ek churnit mishran ko aarti ke baad bhagwaan ko arpit karte hue dusron ko baanta jaataa he jo bimariyon ko simit rakhane main madad karata he or tattviktaa ke anusaar jivan main khushiyaan or vipatti ke palon ko saamana karne ke liye taiyaar karata he.chitrapur saraswath brahman shaam hone par chitrapur math ko jate he, vahaan ke ek pujari panchang se us nayaa varsh kaa barsaat, laabh, aadi or raashi ke anusaar varsh kaa bhavishya ko ghoshan karte he. puja ke baad prasad ke rup main gud, kali mirch or ilaychi se banaaya hua panak ko logon ko baanta jaataa he. uske baad shri bhavanishankar ko puja karte hai. uske baad logon ko panavaar khilaai jaati hai. panavaar, kerala ke tyohaar main bane jane vaale olym sadhya bhojan ke samajaat he jisamen kaju, penas, tarbooz; mung daal, channa daal ke kosambari; shakarkand, kaju, yaa channa kaa upakari; kaccha aam ki chatnee, aadi ko patrawali yaa kele ke patton par paroste hai.
 ghar ke suvasini striyaan or balikaay is din par swarna gauri devi ko puja-paath arpit karte hai. 5 nariyal har suvasini ke liye or 2 nariyal balikaay ke liye tokari main alankrita karke rakhaa jaataa he. un nariyal kaa upyog kiya jaataa he jinka shendi na ho yaa nikaal diya gaya ho. nariyal ko saaf karke, haldi pani main dubokar, dhokar un par chaak ke dwaara rekhaayen khinchi jaataa he. nariyal ke do aankhon par kaala kaajal kaa tilak or teesri aankh par sindur kii tilak lagaai jaati he. eekh, supaari, paan, kele, bhingaar, kakadi, bhindi, aadi suvasini ke tokari main jalte hue tel ke diye, vain dori, kale kangan, kandhi, chota kaajal k dibba or kunkum aadi ke saath rakhte he.teen nariyalon ko vishesh rup se dhoi jaati he, unako haldi ke pani main dhokar, kaajal or sindur kaa samast tilak lagaai jaati he. ek bhagwaan shiv or dusraa shri ganesh ke pratik hote he. yah sab kaary ghar ke suvasini striyaan karte he jab vain taiyaar karte he. swarna gauri ke prati naivedhy karne ke liye varai ke yaa gehun kaa aataa ke patholiyaan, kakadi kaa kheer, kakadi kaa kocholi, hari patteydaar subjiyon kaa paanch prakaar ke upakari namak ke bina taiyaar kiya jaataa he.gauri puja tathaa naivedhy ke baad vain ko suvasiniyon main baanta jaataa he.
 is din bhagwaan ganesh kaa mitti kaa murti ko baahar se laayaa jaataa he yaa ghar main jo murti puja ke liye rakhaa hua he usee kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. khotte, madgane, chakli, gud ke laddu, karanjya, modak, panchakhaadya aadi ko bhagwaan ganesh ko naivedhy kaa peshkash kii jaati he.aalu kaa sukha bhaaji, kakadi k kocholi, patrode, patrode bhaaji, karela or ambadi kaa ghashshi, karela ke pakoda, aadi ko taiyaar kiya jaataa he. ant main shri ganesh ji ke murti ko modak kaa aarti kiya jaataa he.is murti ko visarjan ke samay fal chadaaya jaataa he, visarjan aarti ke baad dhooma-dham se murti kaa visarjan kiya jaataa he.
 deepawali chaar deen kaa tyohaar he :
 is shubh din par sona, chaandi or dhan kii puja kiya jaataa he. sandhyakaal ko saaf kiye gaye bartan main pani ko bharaa jaataa he . un bartan ko gendaa-jhandu phool or toran ko baandhakar alankrita kiya jaataa he. is din par mitha poha ko baanta jaataa he, agarbattiyon ko jalaayaa jaataa he or aarti kiya jaataa he.
 pau fatne se pehle tel snaan kiya jaataa he. nahaane ke vakt nirkasur ke jaise rakt ko anukaran karne ke liye tel main kumkum ko milakar maathe par lagaai jaati he. nahaane ke baad careet naamak tarkari ko pairon ke neeche pis diya jaataa he or uske beej ko muh main daali jaati he. baad main poha ke saath gud ko milakar naashtaa kiya jaataa he. saamaanya rup se diwali ke samay anras, boondi laddu, chinada aadi jaise khaas aahaar ko bhi banaai jaati hai. madhyahn-bhojan ke liye kuch mithai ko bhi banaai jaati he. shaam hoti hi ghar ke pravesh-dwaar or snaan-ghar ke dwaar par tel ke diye ko deepotsva manaane ke liye kam se kam teen din ke liye jalaai jaati he.
 amavasya ke din par ghar main kalash sthaapan karke lakshmi mata ki puja kii jaati he or naivedhy bhi chadhaaya jaati he. shaam ko paanch yaa saath suvasiniyon ko haldi-kumkum diya jaataa he. usee din kalash kaa visarjan kiya jaataa he or usee nariyal kaa meetha banaai jaati he.
 karthik shudhd pratipada ke diwas par gaayon ko go-puja kii jaati he or bali chakravarti evam bhagwaan shri krishna ko bhi puja arpit kii jaati he.khopra, gud or poha tathaa kelon ko ghar ke gau ko arpit kiya jaataa he. gau ko phulon ki maala se puja hone samay yaa puja ke baad alankrita kii jaati he.
 is shubh din par bahane apane bhaaiyon ko bhojan ke liye nimantran dete he or unke priy khaadya ko taiyaar karte he. hamaare samskruti ke anusaar tel snaan ke baad, bahane apane bhaaiyon kaa aarti karte he and badale main is avasar par bahanon ko unke bhaiyon se uphaar milti he.
