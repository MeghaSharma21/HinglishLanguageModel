 bangladesh ke swatantraaa diwas, 26 march ko manaaya jaataa he. is din bangladesh main rashtriya avkaash hota he. is avasar par bangladesh main swatantraaa diwas parade, raajnneetik bhaashanon, melon, sangeet samaaroh ke saath bangladesh kii paramparaon par aadhaarit utsav manaaya jaataa he. tivi or radio steshnon main vishesh kaaryakramon or deshbhakti ke geeton kaa prasaaran kiya jaataa he. aam taur par is din subah main yojit samaaroh ke dauraan ekathis topon kii salaami di jaati he. mukhya sadakon ko rashtriya dhvaj se sajaayaa jaataa he. vibhinn raajnneetik dal or saamaajik sanghathanon ke dwaara ek uchit tareeke se swatantrata diwas samaaroh dhaka ke aasapaas tathaa rashtriya smarakon par yojit kiya jaataa he. 
 bangbandhu ke naam se vikhyath shaikh mujiburrahman ke dwaara 25 march 1971 kii aadhi raat ke baad pakistan se apane desh kii aazaadee kii ghoshnaa kii gai, uske baad unhen pakistani sena dwaara giraftaar kar liya gaya. 26 march 1971 ko bangladesh kii swatantraaa kii ghoshnaa ke saath hi mukti yuddh kii shuruyaat ho gai thi. ant main jeet 16 disambar ko ek hi varsh main hasil kiya gaya tha, jo vijay diwas ke rup main manaaya jaataa he. shaikh mujiburrahman ne pakistan ke khilaaf sashastr sangram kii aguwai karte hue bangladesh ko mukti dilaai. ve bangladesh ke pratham raashtrapati bane or baad main pradhanmantri bhi bane.
 26 march 1971 main bangladesh kii swatantraaa kii ghoshnaa kii he or mukti yuddh shuru kar diya gaya tha. east bengal ke logon ke sabhi virgon ke mukti ke liye pakistani sena ke shaskaon ke nirantar utpeedan se bachaane ke bangladesh yuddh main bharat ne saath diya. swatantraaa hetu yuddh ke nau maheene main maanav jivan ke maamle main pakistani sena ko 3 million kii haani is grihayuddh ke dauraan hui thi. ant main jeet 16 disambar ko ek hi varsh main hasil kiya gaya tha, jo vijay diwas ke rup main manaaya jaataa he.
 bangladesh ke sansthaapak hone ke naate shaikh mujiburrahman ko bangladesh kaa janak kaha jaataa he. ve avami lig ke adhyaksh the. pakistan ke khilaaf sashastr sangram kii aguwai karte hue bangladesh ko mukti dilaai. ve bangladesh ke pratham raashtrapati bane or baad main pradhanmantri bhi bane. ve 'shaikh mujeeb' ke naam se bhi prasiddh the. unhen 'bangbandhu' kii padavi se sammanit kiya gaya tha, kintu 15 agast 1975 ko sainik takhtapalat ke dwaara unki hatya kar di gayi.
 swatantraaa puraskaar bangladesh kaa sarvocch naagarik puraskaar he. yah bangladesh ke swatantrata diwas kii purv sandhya main yojit ek samaaroh main bangladesh kii sarkaar dwaara pradaan kiya jaataa he. yah vaarshik puraskaar, 1977 main shuru kiya gaya tha, jo prativarsh mukti yuddh, bhashaa andolan, shiksha, sahitya, patrakarita, saarvajanik seva, vigyaan praudyogiki, chikitsa vigyaan, saamaajik vigyaan, sangeet, khel or khel, lalit kala, gramin, vikaas or kuch anya kshetron main ullekhneey yogadan hetu diya jaataa he.
