 shaanti bhushan
 shaanti bhushan bharat ke bhootpurv vidhimantri evam sarvocch nyaayalay main varisht adhivaktaa hai. ve morarji desai sarkaar main vidhi, nyaay evam company kaary mantri the. san 2009 main indian express dwaara unhen vishwa ke sabase shaktishaalee bhartiya logon kii suchi main 74wein sthaan par rakhaa gaya tha. ve bharat main bhrashtachar ke viruddh sangharsh karne waalon main agrani hai.
 shaanti bhushan congress party or baad main janata party ke ek sakriy sadsy the. ve 14 july 1977 se 2 april 1980 tak rajya sabha ke sadsy rahe. ve morarji desai kii sarkaar main 1977 se sarkaar ke patan tak vidhi, nyaay evam company kaary mantri rahe. san 1980 main ve bhartiya janata party main shaamil ho gayi. parantu 1986 main jab bhartiya janata party ne ek chunaav yachika par unki salaah nahin maani to unhone bhaajapa se tyaagptr de diya.
 tatkaaleen vidhi mantri ke rup main, unhone 1977 main lokapal bill prastut kiya tha jo morarji desai kii sarkaar gir jane ke kaaran paarit nahin ho sakaa. samprati ve april 2011 main gathit jan lokapal vidheyak kii sanyukt samiti ke sah-adhyaksh hai.
