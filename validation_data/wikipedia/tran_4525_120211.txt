 vishwa swasthya sanghathan  vishwa ke deshon ke swasthya sambandhi samasyaon par aapasi sahyog evam maanak viksit karne kii sanstha he. vishwa swasthya sanghathan ke 193 sadsy desh tathaa do sambhddha sadsy hai. yah sanyukt raashtra sangh kii ek anushangik ikai he. is sanstha kii sthapana 7 april 1948 ko kii gayi thi. iskaa uddeshy sansar ke logo ke swasthya kaa str unchaa karnaa he. dablyuechao kaa mukhyalay switsraland ke geneva shahar main sthit he. ithiopia ke doctor tadros aderenom gibreyesus vishwa swasthya sanghathan ke nae mahanideshak nirvaachit hue hai.
 wo doctor margaret chaiyn kaa sthaan lenge jo paanch-paanch saal ke do kaarykaal yani das varshon tak kaam karne ke baad is pad se ritair ho rahi hai.. 
 bharat bhi vishwa swasthya sanghathan kaa ek sadsy desh he or iskaa bhartiya mukhyalay bharat kii rajdhani dilli main sthit he.
