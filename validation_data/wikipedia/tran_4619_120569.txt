 jannisari yaa yanichari kuleen paidal sena ikaiyaan thim jo osmani sultan ke gharelu sipaahi, angrakshak or europe main sabase pehali aadhunik paidal sena thi. sultan orkhan ne sarvapratham iskaa sanghathan 1330 main kiya tha. muraad pratham ne isaki unnati kii or 1362 main iske sipaahiyon kii sankhya 10,000 ho gai. yah sena, apane rankaushal or veertapurna dakshata ke liye prasiddh he. sipaahiyon kaa yah daawa tha ki ve yuddh se kabhi vichlit nahin hue. yah turki kii bahut badi shakti thi. vaitanaik sthaayee sipaahiyon kii sankhya ek samay 60,000 ke lagbhag thi. baad main yah sankhya ghataakar 25,000 kar di gai. inke rahane ke liye qustutuniya or anya shaharon main barrack bane hue the. asthaayee sipaahiyon kii sankhya 3,00,000 se 4,00,000 tak rahati thi. ye sipaahi rajya ke sabhi shahraron main bikhare hue the or shanti ke samay police kaa kaary karte the. sultan kii angarakshata main rahanevaale jannisari dhire-dhire inte ugr ho gaye ki ve kabhi kabhi vidroh bhi karne lage. lekin in vidrohon kaa daman bhi kiya jaataa rahaa. 1826 main jannisari sipaahiyon ne nai rashtriya sena kii sthapana ke prastaav par vidroh kar diya. isapar mahmud dwitiya ne pradhan jannisari senapati kii sahaayta lekar inhen buree tarah parajeet kiya or unki barraken jala deen. usee samay ek shahi ghoshnaa ke anusaar yah sena samaapt kar di gai. uske lagbhag 15,000 sipaahiyon ko mrityudand diya gaya or 20,00 desh se nikaal die gaye.
