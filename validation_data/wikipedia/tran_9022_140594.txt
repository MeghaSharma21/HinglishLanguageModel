 dwikpaati, yaa patalaklomi aksheruki tathaa jaleey praani hai. yah molska sangh kaa ek varg he. ise lamelebrankiata, dwikpaati, yaa pelecepoda bhi kehete hai. chunki inke paad chapate hone ke bajaay navatlit adhariya hote hai, isliye ye 'pelacepoda' kehalaate hai. is varg ke praaniyon main sir nahin hota, at: yah varg molska ke anya virgon se bhinn he. inamen labial sparshakon ke dwaara sir kaa pratinidhitiv hota he. ye dwiparshv samamit praani he. inke sabhi ansh jode main athwa madhyasth hote hai. lamelebrankia sthanbaddh praani hai. kuch dwikpaati chattaanon se baddh rahete hai, jab ki anya dhaage sadrush pulindia se jameen se sanlagn rahete hai. is pulindia ko sutaraguchh kehete hai. yah sutaraguchh paad kii ek guhika se sravit hota he. adhikansh dwikapaatiyon ke paad bill banane, yaa gaman ke liye vyavahrut hote hai. kuch dwikpaati apane kavchon ko ekaek band kar, pani ko baahar nikaalane ke dwaara tairte hai.
 lamelebrankia ke 100 se adhik kul evam 7,000 spishij gyaat hai.
 inke kavak main do praroopik, samaan kapaat hote hai. donon kapaat ek pratyasth snaayu ke dwaara jude rahete hai. yadi snaayu aantarik hote hai, to ye racilium kehalaate hai. ye snaayu kapaaton ko alag rakhte hai, jabaki do abhivartanee peshiyaan kavchon kaa band rakhane kaa prayaas karti hai. kavach ke aantar prishthiya bhaag, yaa hinjupatt main hinj daant hote hai, jo antarkilit hote hai. daanton kaa saadhaaran rup anek samaan daanton kaa bahudanti hinj he. kuch vibhedit daanton kaa ucchtam vikaas hua hai. anek dwikapaatiyon main adhar or parshvik upant ke sookshm danta dwaara kapaaton kaa thik-thik band hona sahaapit hota he.
 praavaar ke srav ke kavach kaa nirmaan hota he. praavaar sampurna sharir ko dhank letaa he. isaki dayee evam bain do paaliyaan hoti hai. ye paaliyaan praavaar peshiyon, yaa vartual peshiyon ke dwaara kapaaton se judi rahati hai. kavach kaa praavaar kshatchahn sanlgani rekha ko prakat karata he. praavaar rekha ke ant main anuprastha abhivartanee peshiyaan hoti hai. praavaar pali ke swatantra adhar, seemaant do, teen yaa chaar chhidrak chhodte hue, anshat: jude rahete hai.
 apwaahi tathaa antarvahi dhaaraon ke liye pashchchhidrak hote he. in do chhidrakon par praavaar praaia: do peshiy tub ke rup main badha rahata he. uuparavaala tub apwaahi yaa gudanal tathaa nichevala tub antarvahi yaa chlomanal hota he. tisare chhidr se paad kaa bahirbedhan hota he. praavaar guhika main do mukhya dharaayen hoti he. antarvahi chhidrak se munh ko dhankanevaale lavial sparshakon tathaa gilon kii ore ek dhara pashchat: disht hoti he. duusari dhara ulti disha main apwaahi naal kii ore disht hoti he. baaluu, yaa bajri main gade rahanevaale pinna or solen main apwaahi dharaayen pakshmabhikamay naal dwaara jaati hai. praavaar kii kor par praaia: granthiyaan, sparshak, varnak chakatta tathaa ankhen hoti hai.
 praaia: lamelebrankia ke gil, yaa chlom, kankat chlom kehalaate hai, kyonki ab inkaa mukhya kaary shwasan nahin he. shwasan mukhyat: praavaar se hota he. ye pakshmabhiki gati ke dwaara antarvahi chhidrak se ek dhara utpann karte hai, jo, sookshm jeevon ko bhojan ke liye chhantakar labial sparshak par pahunchaa detii he. labial sparshak muhan ke oshth, yaa yugmit paliyukt prakshepan he. do gilon main se prayek main ek kendriya aksh hota he, jisamen tantuon kii do shreniyan hoti hai, jinhen ardhaklom, kehete hai. protobrank, dwikapaatiyon main tantu saadhaaran patticas dand hote hai, philibrank gilon main tantu samaantar dand hote hai, jo antarkilit pakshmabhiki taff dwaara jude rahete hai tathaa eulamelibrake gilon main dand sanvahani sandhiyon dwaara jude rahete hai.
