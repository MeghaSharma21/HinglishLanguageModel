 dwarka prasad sharma hindi ke sahityakar the. inhen hindi gadhy ke vikaas kaal ke aarambhik lekhakon main gina jaataa he. chaturvediji ko hindi sahitya jagat main mahatvapurn yogadan hetu "sharma" kii oopaadhi se sammanit kiya gaya.
 unka janm uttar pradesh ke itawa main samvath 1877 main hua tha. ve baad main elahabaad main aakar bas gaye the. ye pehle sarkari naukari main the parantu warren hestings kaa jivan charitra likhane ke kaaran naukari se alag kar die gaye or sahitya kii ore pravruth hue. chaturvediji ne "raghavendra" masik patr bhi nikal tha.
 inhone kai mahatvapurn koshon sahit 150 se adhik pustaken likhi jisamen se pramukh hai 
 inke dwaara likhi bal sahityamala bhi bahut lokapriy hui jinamein 20 se adhik pustaken hai. chaturvedi ji kai varshon tak hindi sahitya sammelan ke prabandh mantri bhi rahe.
