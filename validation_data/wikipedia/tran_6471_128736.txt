 prashulk yaa tarriff, antarrashtriya vyapaar main lagaae jane vaala kar he jo aamtaur par 5% tak hote hai lekin kisi bhi desh dwaara gharelu udyogon athwa utpaadon ke sangrakshan ke liye ise badhaayaa ja saktaa he. saamanytah prashulk aaayaatit vastuon par lagaaya jaataa he.
 tarriff arthath prashulk, aayaat yaa niryaat par lagaaya jane vaala tex arthath kar he.
 tarriff, aam taur par sangrakshanvaad, deshon ke bich hone vaale vyapaaron ko simit karne vaali aarthik niti, se judaa hua he. raajnneetik kaarnon se, tarriff, aam taur par aaayaatit maal par lagaaya jaataa he, haalanki niryaatit maal par bhi tarriff lagaaya ja saktaa he.
 aajkal kii tulana main, ateet main, tarriff se sarkari rajasav kaa ek bahut bada hissaa aata tha.
 jab maal se bharaa jahaaj kisi seemaa paar yaa bandargah par pahunchataa he, to seemaa shulk adhikari samagriyon kaa nirikshan karte hai or un samagriyon par tarriff formula arthath prashulk sutara ke anusaar ek tarah kaa kar lagaaya jaataa he. chunki maal tab tak aage nahin bad saktaa he jab tak duty arthath shulk kaa bhugtan nahin kar diya jaataa, isliye yah sangrah kiya jane vaala sabase aasaan shulk he or iske sangrah kii laagat bhi kam he. tarriff se bachane kii koshish karne vaale vyapaariyon ko smagaller arthath taskaron ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
 tarriff ke vibhinn prakaar hai:
 ve anuchit sthanantaran keemat kii samasya kaa bhi saamana karte hai jahaan koi company vyapaar kiye jane vaale maal ke mulya kii ghoshnaa karata he jo bazaar keemat se alag hota he jiskaa uddeshy kul shesh karon main katautee karnaa hota he.
 20veen sadi main, teriffon kaa nirdhaaran, sarkaar yaa sthaneey adhikari se praapt jaankaree ke aadhaar par yaa udyog sanrachana adhyayan kii swaprerana se kisi tarriff kamishan dwaara kiya jaataa he.
 aadhunik samay main kar, tarriff or vyapaar sambandhi niyamon ko aam taur par audyogic niti, nivesh niti or krishigat niti par unke aam prabhaav kii vajah se ek saath sthapit yaa nirdhaarit kiya jaataa he. vyapaarik gut, sambhddha deshon kaa ek samooh he jo ek duusare ke saath kiye jane vaale vyapaar par lagaae jane vaale teriffon or anya baadhaon yaa shulkon ko kam karne yaa khatm karne ke liye or sambhawatah us gut ke baahar ke kisi desh se aaayaatit maal par sangrakshnatmak tarriff lagane ke liye sahamat hote hai. seemaa shulk sangh kaa ek aam bahari tarriff hota he or, sahmati sutara ke anusaar, isamen bhaag lene vaale desh is seemaa shulk sangh main pravesh karne vaale maalon par lagaae jane vaale teriffon se praapt rajasav ko aapas main baant lete hai.
 agar kisi desh ke pramukh udyog videshi pratispardhaa main haar jate hai to kaam or kar rajasav kii haani se us desh kii arthvyavastha gambhir rup se vikrut ho sakati he or garibi bad sakati he. agar kisi desh ke jeevanyaapan kaa maanak yaa audyogic niyamon kaaphi ooncha ho to gharelu udyog chote deshon ke saath samjhauta kiye bina unke saath asanrakshit vyapaar ko jivit rakh sakate hai; is samjhaute main neeche kii ek vaishvik daud shaamil he. sangrakshnatmak teriffon kaa istemaal aitihaasik drushti se is sambhaavna ke khilaaf ek upaay ke rup main kiya jaataa he. haalanki, sangrakshnatmak teriffon se nuksaan bhi hota he. sabase ullekhneey he ki ve sthaneey pratispardhaa ko kamazor banaakar tarriff ke adheen maal kii keemat ko rokte hai jisase us maal ke upabhoktaaon yaa nirmaataaon ko nuksaan uthaanaa padataa he jo kuch alag utpann karne ke liye us maal kaa istemaal karte hai: udhaaharan ke taur par, khaadya par lagane vaale tarriff se garibi bad sakati he, jabaki steel yaa ispaat par lagane vaale tarriff se automobile nirmaataa kam pratispardhi ho sakate hai. ve ulti pratikriya bhi kar sakate hai agar tarriff se nuksaan hone vaale vyapaar ko karne vaale desh khud par hi tarriff lagaate hai jiske parinamswaroop vyapaar yuddh shuru ho jaataa he or, mukt vyapaar siddhantkaron ke anusaar, donon pakshon ko nuksaan uthaanaa padataa he. 
 navparmparik aarthik siddhanton ke anusaar, tarriff, vyaktigat swatantraaa or mukt bazaar ke kanoonon vaala ek hanikaarak hastkshep he. unka vishwaas he ki sthaneey maangon dwaara aksham kiye gaye kisi udyog kaa kritrim rup se rakrakhaav karnaa upabhoktaaon ke prati anuchit or aam taur par kisi desh ke liye nuksaandaayak he or ve yah bhi maanate hai ki iskaa patan hone kii ijaajat dena behatar he. sabhi teriffon ke vipareet paksh, mukt vyapaar siddhant kaa hissaa he; vishwa vyapaar sanghathan kaa uddeshy teriffon ko kam karnaa or teriffon ko lagu karte samay alag-alag deshon ke bich ke bhedbhaav se deshon ko dur rakhana he.
 nimnlikhit graph main ham gharelu arthvyavastha par aayaat tarriff ke prabhaav ko dekhte hai. bina vyapaar vaale kisi band arthvyavastha main, hamen maang or aapurti vakrarekha ke kataan bindu par santulan dikhaai degaa jisase keemat main 70 dollar kaa laabh or Y* kaa ek utpaada praapt hota he. is maamle main, upabhokta kaa adhishesh A, B or K binduon ke bheetar ke kshetra ke baraabar hoga, jabaki nirmaataa ke adhishesh ko A, B or L kshetra ke rup main dikhaaya gaya he. is model main mukt antarrashtriya vyapaar ko shaamil karte samay, ham ek nai aapurti vakrarekha kii sthapana karte hai jise SW ke rup main suchit kiya jaataa he. is vakrarekha se ek purvanumaan miltaa he ki maal yaa seva kii antarrashtriya aapurti puri tarah se namya he or yah bhi ki duniya main bataai gai keemat par ek najdiki anant maatra main utpaadan ho saktaa he. jaahir he, vaastavik duniya kii paristhitiyon main, yah kuch had tak avyavhaarik yaa avastavik he, lekin is tarah ke purvanumaan lagane se model ke parinaam par kisi saamagree kaa prabhaav padane kii sambhaavna nahin rahati he. is maamle main maal kii antarrashtriya keemat 50 dollar he .
 uparokt model keval charam sthiti main hi puri tarah se sateek he jahaan koi bhi upabhoktanirmaataa samooh se sambandhit nahin he or utpaada kii laagat, unke vetan kaa ek ansh he. agar iske bajaay, ham vipareet charam sthiti ko lete hai or maan lete hai ki sabhi upabhokta nirmaataa samooh se sambandhit hai or yah bhi maan lete hai ki unki ekmaatr kharidari shakti vetan se sambandhit he jo utpaadan ke rup main praapt hota he or utpaada kii laagat unka puraa vetan he, to graph ekadam alag dikhaai detaa he. teriffon ke bina, keval vishwa keemat par utpaada kaa nirmaan karne main samarth utpaadakon yaa nirmaataaon /upabhoktaaon ke paas hi utana paisa hoga ki use us keemat par kharid sake. chote exl trikon ko kharidne main abhi bhi samarth upabhoktaaon ke ek samaan chote darpan chhavi trikon se milan kiya jayega. tarriff ke saath, ek bada sidiel trikon or uskaa darpan batch paaegaa.
 is baat par bhi dhyaan den ki teriffon ke saath yaa teriffon ke bina, gharelu ke upar aaayaatit maal kharidne kaa koi protsaahan nahin miltaa he,kyonki prayek kii keemat ek samaan he. keval rin ke madhyam se, parisampattiyon ko bechakar, yaa nai tarah kii gharelu utpaadan se nae vetan ke madhyam se uplabdh kharidne kii shakti main pherbadal karke aaayaatit maal ko karidaa jayega. yaa, jaahir he, agar isaki keemat keval mazdooree yaa vetan kaa ek ansh ho.
 asli duniya main, chunki adhik aaayaatit maal gharelu maal kii jagah lete hai, isliye ve uplabdh gharelu vetan ke ek bahut bade ansh kaa upbhog karte hai, jisase graph model ke is drushtikon kii disha main badhata he. agar utpaadan ke nae tareeke samay par na mili, to desh diwalia ho jayega or aatanrik raajnneetik dabaav ke falswaroop rin kaa bhugtan nahin ho paaegaa, bahut zyada tarriff lagegaa, yaa usase bhi badtar haalat ho jaaegi.
 teriffon kii sthapana is prakriya ko dhimaa kar detii he, jisase nae tarah ke utpaadan ko viksit hone ke liye adhik samay mil jaataa he, lekin yah un udyogon ko bhi majboot bana detaa he jo shaayad kabhi pratispardhi keemat ko phir se na paa sake.
 tarriff ko ek swatantra raashtra kii sthapana karne ke liye ek raajnneetik upkaran ke rup main istemaal kiya jaataa he; udhaaharan ke liye, sanyukt rajya america kaa tarriff act of 1789 arthath prashulk adhinium 1789, jis par vishesh rup se 4 july ko hastakshar kiya gaya tha, ko akhbaaron ne "second dikaleyareshan of independence" arthath 'swatantraaa kii duusari ghoshnaa' kaha kyonki iskaa makasad saarvbhaumik or swatantra sanyukt rajya america ke raajnneetik lakshya kii prapti ke liye ek aarthik saadhan bananaa tha.
 aadhunik samay main, teriffon kaa raajnneetik prabhaav ek sakaaratmak or nakaaratmak arth main dekha jaataa he. 2002 united stats steel tarriff arthath 2002 kaa sanyukt rajya america ispaat prashulk ne teen varshon kii ek avadhi tak vibhinn prakaar ke aaayaatit steel utpaadon par 30% kaa tarriff lagaaya. america steel utpaadakon ne tarriff kaa samarthan kiya, lekin kaato institute ne is kadam kii aalochana kii. 
 tarriff kabhi-kabhi kisi chunaav se pehle ek raajnneetik mudde ke rup main ubhar saktaa he. 2007 australian sangheey chunaav kii taiyaari main, australian lebar party ne ghoshnaa kii ki nirvaachit hone par yah australian car teriffon kii samiksha karegi. libral party ne isi tarah kii patibaddhta kii, jabaki swatantra praarthi nik jenophon ne "atyaavashyaktaa kii ek baat" ke rup main tarriff-aadhaarit vidhaan kii sthapana karne ke apane iraade kii ghoshnaa kii.
