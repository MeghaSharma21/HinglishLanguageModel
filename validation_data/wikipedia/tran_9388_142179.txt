 rivaayati taur par pita kaa bacchon ke prati suraksha, sahaayta or zimmedaari vaala ravaiya hota he. pita sirf jaivik hi nahin balki paalanhaar pita bhi ho saktaa he. maanavvigyaani morris godalior ke mutabiq maanviya pita dwaara dhaaran kii gai pitru-bhoomika, maanviya samaaj or unke sabase nazadiiki jaivik rishtedaar—chimpanzi or bonobo— main ek aalochanatmak farq he kyonki yah jaanavar apane pitru-sambandh se anjaan hote hai.
