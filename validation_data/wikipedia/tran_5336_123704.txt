 forencik khagol vigyaan khagol vigyaan ke upyog, akashiya pindon ke vaigyanik adhyayan, pichley akashiya nakshatron kaa nirdhaaran karne ke liye he. yah nyaayalayik vigyaan ke kshetra main istemaal kiya jaataa he jaise kii aitihaasik samasyay kala ke itihaas main adhik aam taur par muddon ko hal karne or vishesh rup se.
 vistaar karne par, visheshan "nyaayalayik" ateet kii ghatnaon ke kisi bhi vistrut vishleshan ke liye istemaal kiya jaataa he, ki kya kanoonee prashnon se sambandhit he yaa nahin, or itnaa adhik aam taur par ateet akashiya nakshatron ke nirdhaaran ab teji se "forencik khagol vigyaan" ke rup main janaa jaataa he. khagol vigyaan ke tareeke kabhi kabhi jagah or kala, yaa ghatana he, ki yah prerit kii ek vishesh kaary ke nirmaan ke samay ko nirdhaarit karne ke liye upyogi hote hai. 
 is tarah ke tareekon udhaaharan ke liye chandramaa badhati saath taarikh vincent vaan gaag kii painting shaam landscape karne ke liye istemaal kiya gaya he.
 khagoleey gyaan kuch kanoonee prashnon ko hal karne main madad kar saktaa hai.din ho yaa raat ke kuch samay main akaash main surya yaa chandramaa ke sthaan ke bare main sawaal kanoonee rup se praasangik ho saktaa he, tarik jis din yah tasviir banaai gai hogi.
