 
 iridium ek rasinic tatva he. strip of cosmos
 iridium dhaatuon ke platinum samooh kaa ek sadsy hai. sabase pehle tenna ne 1804 main osmieredium naamak mishran se isko praapt kiya. yah bahut hi kathor dhaatu he, lagbhag 2,450 degree centegrade par pighalati he or iskaa aapekshik ghanatv 22.4 he. iskaa vishisht vidyutiya pratirodh 4.9 he jo platinum kaa lagbhag aadha he. isse taar, chadar ityadi bananaa bada hi kathin he. rasinic abhikriya main yah dhaatuon main sabase adhik akriyasheel he, yahan tak ki amlaraj bhi saadhaaran taap par isapar kriya karne main asafal rahata he.
 iridium fountain pen kii nibon kii nonk, aabhushan, chumbakeey sampark sthapit karanevaale yantra, poli sui tathaa bahut hi baarik fus taar banane main kaam aata he.
 iridium bahut se yogic banaata he, jinamein 1, 2, 3, 4 tathaa 6 tak sanyojaktaa hoti he. isamen jatil yogic banane kii bhi pravriti pai jaati he. yah duusari dhaatuon se milakar, visheshkar platinum ke saath, badi sugamawa se mishradhatu banaata he. ye mishradhatuen badi kathor hoti hai
