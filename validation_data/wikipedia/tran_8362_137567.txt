 bhairon ghati dharali se 16 kilometre tathaa gangotri se 9 kilometrdur uttarakhand rajya main sthit he. 
 bhairon ghati, jadh jahnavi ganga tathaa bhagirathi ke sangam par sthit he. yahaan tej bahaav se bhagirathi gahari ghaatiyon main bahati he, jisaki aawaaj kaanon main garjati he. varsh 1985 se pehle jab sansar ke sarvocch jadhaganga par jhula pul sahit gangotri tak motor gadiyon ke liye sadak kaa nirmaan nahin hua tha, tirthayatri lanka se bhairon ghati tak ghaney deodaaron ke bich paidal aate the or phir gangotri jate the. bhairon ghati himalay kaa ek manoram darshan karaata he, jahaan se aap bhrigu parvat shrinkhalaa, sudarshan, matra tathaa chidvasa chotiyon ke darshan kar sakate hai.
 raja vilson dwaara nirmit jahnavi nadi par ek rassi-pul hua karata tha jo vishwa kaa sarvocch jhula-pul tha, jisapar se aap bahut neeche nadi ko bhramit karne vaala drishy nihaar sakate the. ab yahaan do kagaaron se latakate hue kuch rassiyon ke tukade hi bache hai.parantu i.tii. atkinson ne varsh 1882 ke apane da himalayan gajetir main bataaya he ki yahaan ek jhula-pul tha, jise “vanadhikari shri. o. kellaghan dwaara jadhaganga par ek halke lohe ke pul kaa nirmaan kar badal diya gaya.” us 380 feet lambey tathaa 3 feet chaude pul ko tirthayatri rengte hue paar karte the.
 jahnavi ke strot kaa pratham khojakartta hagasan bhairon ghati ke prabhaavshaalee saundarya ko dekhtaa rah gaya! vishaal chattaanon, khadi deevaaren, oonche deodaar ke ped tathaa kolahali bhagirathi sabon ko niharta rahaa. usne is jagah ko “sabase bhayanak tathaa daraavnee jagah bataaya he, jiske upar ek bada chattan aage tak badha hua he.”
 prasiddh german parvatarohi henrik herier bhairon ghati se jahnavi ke kinare-kinare tibet gaya tha. tibet main vah dalai lamas kaa shikshak ban gaya tathaa usne apni kriti ‘tibet main saat varsh’ main apane anubhavon ko bataaya.
 himalayan gajetir main udadhrut frager ke anusaar pul paar karne tathaa deodaar ke ghaney jangalon se guzarane ke baad aap “ek chote mandir bhairon ke samtal safed bhawan pahunchate hai, jise amar singh gorkhali ke aadesh par banaaya gaya tathaa jise sadak kii marammat tathaa gangotri kii puja ke liye sthaan nirmit karne ke liye dhan diya.”
 gangotri mandir tak pahunchane se pehle is pracheen bhairav nath mandir kaa darshan avashya karnaa chaiye.
