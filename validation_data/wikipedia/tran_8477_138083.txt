 america adhikariyon ne dawaaon ke pareekshan ke naam par guatemala main kaidiyon se haivaaniyat bharaa vyavhaar kiya. 1940 ke dashak main hue in aparaadhon ke maamle main chaukaanewaali baaten saamne aa rahi hai.
  raashtrapati baraak obama dwaara niyukt ayog ko is baat ke saboot mili hai ki america sarkaar ke shodhakartaaon ne 1940 ke dashak main guatemala ke kaidiyon par shaareerikor maansik prayog kiye. america paise se hui us research main logon se insaan kii tarah vyavhaar nahin kiya gaya. peediton ko yah bhi nahin bataaya gaya ki un par ek researchki ja rahi he. research yues public health sarvice ne karwaai. 15353781,00.html
 somvaar ko ayog kii report jari hui. ayog kii pramukh emi gutman kahati hai, "jo log isamen shaamil the ve ise ati gopneey rakhana chaahate the. ve jaanate the ki agar yah batfaili to janata unki aalochana karegi." gutman pensiolvenia uniwarsity kii adhyaksh hai.
 mana ja rahaa he ki jaanch report se dawa udyog ke liye banaae gaye niyamon par asar padegaa. report ke baad marijon par nai dawa ke pareekshan se sambandhit kanoon badal sakate hai.philhal america dawa nirmaataa duusare deshon main dawaaon kaa pareekshan karte hai.
 ayog kii report saamne aane ke baad guatemala ne tikhi pratikriya di he. guatemala ne research ko maanavtaa ke khilaaf aparaadh bataate hue antarrashtriya adaalat main janeki baat kahi he. peedit bhi america sarkaar ke khilaaf mukademe daayar kar rahe hai. guatemala apni taraf se bhi jaanch kar rahaa he.image/0, 6450304_1,00 popup_lupe/0, 15353781_ind_1,00
 kaise hui research
 1946 main america public health adhikari doctor jon katler ne america or guatemala kii kai agenciyon kii madad se research shuru kii. research ke dauraan kareeb 1,300 logonmen vibhinn bimaariyaan sankramit kii gayi. inamen se ek sex sambandh banane se failane vaali beemaari sifalis bhi thi.
 jaanch main pataa chalaa ki researchron ne kaidiyon or maansik rogiyon ko yonkarmiyon se sambandh banane ke liye majaboor kiya. iske chalate kai logon ko sifalis ho gaya. peeditonke jananaangon or sharir ke kai hisson main funfood lag gaya. jananaangon se sifalis sharir ke bhitari hisson main pahunchaa. kai rogiyon ko lakva maar gaya, kuch andhe ho gaye auraaise rogiyon kii jaan bhi chali gai.
 in marijon par ilaaj ke naam par bhi kai prayog kiye gaye. sifalis failaane ke baad rogiyon ko antibiotic pencylin ke inkjeshan lagaae gaye. ayog kii nazar main ek aisa maamlasamne aayaa he jab maansik rup se biimaar ek mahila ko sifalis se sankramit kiya gaya. phir doctor katler ne uskaa adhyayan kiya gaya. vah maut ke muhane par khadi thi lekinctler ko apane prayog kii padi rahi. sifalis ke baad mahila astidi se sankramit kiya gaya. aakhirkaar us mahila ne dam tod diya. is baat ke likhit saboot hai ki doctorkatler is dauraan mahila par nazar rakhe hue the.image/0, 6130845_1,00 popup_lupe/0, 15353781_ind_2,00
 nakel kasne kii taiyaari
 ayog kii report ke baad america main dawa companiyon ke liye nae niyam banaae jane kii maang tej hoti ja rahi he. velesle college kii professor sujan reverbai kahati hai,"unhone sochaa ki ham bimariyon ke khilaaf yuddh kar rahe hai or yuddh main jawan maare jate hai. yah kah dena bahut aasaan he ki bhavishya main ham aisa nahin karenge. lekinhamen vaaki is bare main sochane kii jarurat he. ham abhi jo kar rahe hai or 20 saal baad daraavna lagane lagegaa."
 maamle ke khulaase main ahm bhoomika nibhaane vaali reverbai kaa kahana he ki 1972 tak america rajya albama main ashwet mahilaon par sifalis kaa prayog chalata rahaa. doctor katalraki 2003 main maut ho gai. katler ne apane is krity ke liye kabhi maafee nahin maangi. haal ke barason main is maamle ko lekar kai thos baaten saamne ai hai. yahi vajah he ki pichlesaal america sarkaar ko guatemala ke logon se maafee maangani padi. 15353781,00.html
