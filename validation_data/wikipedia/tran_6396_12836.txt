 yantra shabd bhartiya sahitya main upkaran, mashin yaa yukti ke arth main aayaa he. jyotish, rasshastra, ayurveda, ganit aadi main is shabd kaa prayog hua he. brahmagupta dwaara rachit brahmasphutsiddhanth main 'yantradhyay' naamak 22waan adhyay he.

 tra  vastav main ek rahasyamay akruti he, jo mukhya rup se bhartiya dharmon kii tantric paramparaon se sambandhit he. unka upyog mandiron yaa gharon main devataaon kii puja tathaa dhyaan main sahaayta ke liye kiya jaataa he. hindu jyotish or tantric granthon ke aadhaar par unka upyog kaalpanik gupt shaktiyon dwaara die jane vaale laabhon ke liye kiya jaataa he. mukhya rup se unke saundarya or samamit gunon ke kaaran, inhen mandir main farsh kii sajaavat ke liye bhi upyog kiya jaataa he. vishisht yantra paramparaagat rup se vishisht devataaon se sambandhit hote hai.
 bharat main yantra kaa varnan ab tak 11,000-10,000 varsh isapurv mana jaataa he. son nadi ghati main upree-purapashan sandarbh main paaya gaya bagore pashaan, sharma ji dwaara sabase purana udhaaharan mana jaataa he, jo pashaan kii khudaai main shaamil the. tribhuj ke aakaar kaa pathar, jiske ek taraf trikoniya nakkashi kii gai he, use geru se ranga gaya tha, jise puja se sambandhit sthal mana jaataa tha. yah paaya gaya he ki us samay kshetra main deviyon kii puja-archana vartmaan samay ke samaan tareeke se kii jaati thi.kenoir, jo utkhanan main bhi shaamil the,ise shakti ke saath judaa hua mana he.
 yantra aamtaur par ek vishesh devtaa se jude hote hai or unka upyog vishisht laabhon ke liye upyog kiya jaataa hai, jaise: dhyaan ke liye; hanikaarak prabhaav se suraksha ke liye; vishesh shaktiyon kaa vikaas karne ke liye; dhan yaa safalataa ko praapt karne ke liye ityadi. aksar ghar par yaa mandiron main dainik anushthanik puja main unka istemaal kiya jaataa he, or kabhi-kabhi taabeej ke rup main pahana jaataa he.dhyaan main sahaayta ke rup main, yantra devtaa kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai jiskaa uddeshy dhyaan he. ye yantra kendriya-bindu, arthath bindu se nikalate hai. yantra main saamaanya rup se centre se sankendrit rup se failati hui anek jyamitiya akritiyaan, jaise trikon, vrutt, shatbhuj, ashtbhuj or camel kii pratikatmak pankhudiyaan shaamil hoti hai. baahar hisse main aksar chaar mukhya dishaao kaa pratinidhitiv karne vaala varg shaamil hota he, jinamein se prayek ek darwaja-yukt hota hai. yantra kaa ek lokapriy rup shri chakra, yaa shri yantra he, jo tripura sundari ke rup main apane devi kaa pratinidhitiv karata he. shri chakra main shiv kaa pratinidhitiv bhi shaamil he, or iskaa nirmaan brahmad ke saath upyogkarta kii apni ekta ke saath-saath srijan or astitv kii samagrata dikhaane ke liye kiya gaya he.
 ek yantra main jyamitiya akritiyaan, chhaviyaan, or likhit mantra shaamil hote hai. trikon or shatbhuj saamaanya hai, jaise vrutt or 4 se 1000 pankhudiyon ke camel saamaanya hai. shiv or shakti yantra main aksar ek trishul kaa shul shaamil hota he.

 paaramparik yantra main rangon kaa upyog puri tarah se pratikatmak he, na ki keval sajaavati yaa kalatmak pradarshan ke liye rangon kaa upyog kiya gaya hai. prayek rang kaa upyog chetana ke vichaaron or aantarik avasthaaon ko darshane ke liye kiya jaataa he. safed/laal/ kaala sabase mahatvapurn rang sanyojanon main se ek he, jo teen gunon yaa prakruti ke gana kaa pratinidhitiv karata he.
 paaramparik yantron ke centre-bindu main ek bindu yaa point hota he, jo yantra se jude mukhya devtaa kaa pratinidhitiv karata he. devtaa ke paricharon ko aksar centre ke chaaron ore jyamitiya bhaagon main darshaya jaataa he. ek yantra main bindu ko ek dot yaa chote vrutt dwaara pratinidhitiv kiya ja saktaa he, yaa yah adrishy rah saktaa he. yah us bindu kaa pratinidhitiv karata he jahaan se sabhi srijan utpann hote hai. kabhi-kabhi, ling bhairavi yantra ke maamle main, bindu ko ling ke rup main prastut kiya ja saktaa he.
