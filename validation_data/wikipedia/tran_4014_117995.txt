 incheyan anterrashtriya vimaanashetra   dakshin korea kaa sabase bada vimaanashetra, rashtriya rajdhani kshetra ko seva dene vaala pradhan vimaanashetra evam vishwa ke sabase bade evam vyastatam vimaanakshetron main se ek he. aviraam saat varshon tak ise airports counsil international dwaara vishwa kaa sarvshereshth vimaanashetra ghoshit kiya gaya tha. vimaanashetra main ek golf-course, spa, nijee shayan kaksh, him-skating rink, casino, aankatik udyaan evam korean samskruti kaa ek sangrahalay bhi bane hai. yahaan prasthaan prakriya main maatr 16 minute evam aagaman prakriya main maatr 12 minute kaa samay lagata he, jo vaishvik maanakon kramshah 60 evam 45 minute se kahin behatar he.
 vimaanashetra da.korea kii rajdhani evam sabase bade shahar tseol se 48 kimi pashchim disha main sthit he. yah eshiana airlines, jeju air, korean air, evam polar air cargo ke liye pradhan hub he. purvi asia main anterrashtriya parivahan evam maal yatayaat ke liye yah vimaanashetra hub kaa kaary karata he. yah vimaanashetra vartmaan main yatri sankhya kii drushti se asia kaa 8waan vyastatam, cargo yatayaat kii drushti se vishwa kaa chauthaa vyastatam, evam 2010 main anterrashtriya yatri parivahan kii drushti se vishwa kaa 8vaan vyastatam vimaanashetra he. varsh 2011 main, 35,062,376 yatriyon ne iskaa prayog kiya.
 vimaanashetra 2001 ke aarambh main puraane jimpo anterrashtriya vimaanashetra ke sthaan par aarambh hua. ab yah adhikansh anterrashtriya vimaanashetra gantavyon ko seva dene ke saath-saath hi china, japan evam taiwan ke liye vaikalpik vimaanashetra kaa kaary bhi karata he.
