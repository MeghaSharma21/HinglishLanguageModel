 
 jarmenium ek rasinic tatv he. iskaa sthaan aavart sarini main usee varg main he, jisamen sis or tin hai. iskaa avishkaar 1886 i. si. vincler ne kiya tha. iskaa sanket Ge, parmanusankhya 32 or parmaanu bhaar 72.6 he. yah tatv badi alp maatra main prithvi par paaya jaataa he. saadhaarant: yah jaste ke khanijon ke saath mila hua miltaa he. khanijon ko jalaane par jo raakh batch jaati he usamen 0.25, pratisht jarmenium oxide rahata he. isko pahase vashpashil tetracloride main parinat karte hai. tetracloride kaa prabhaajak aswan rake anya dhaatuon se yah pruthak kiya jaataa he. iske oxide ko aluminium yaa carbon yaa hydrogen dwaara apachayit karne se dhaatu praapt hoti he.
 jarmenium kuch bhuraapan liye shwet rang kii dhaatu he. isaki banaavat manibhiya hoti he. yah ati bhangur hota he. iskaa vishisht gurutv 20 degree sein. par 5.35 or galnaank 958.5 degree sein. he. oxigen main garam karne se oxide banataa he. iskaa varnheen tetracloride drav, tetrabromite rangahin or tetrodide naarangi rang kaa thos hota he, jo kramash: 26.8 degree or 144 degree sein. par pighalta he.
 silicon tathaa tin ke aisa jarmenium carbonic yogic, hydride aadi banataa he. hydride ke kyurosanjat bhi banate hai. jarmenium ke hydrochlorisanjat drav or thos hote hai. kaanch main silica kaa sthaan jab jarmenium oxide letaa he tab kaanch kaa vartanaank bahut bad jaataa he. raktakshinata main jarmenium yogicon ke prayog kaa suzav miltaa he. anya kai yogicon ke nirmaan main bhi jarmenium or tin ke bich samanta dekhi jaati he.
 varsh 1869 main jab mendleve ne 'rasinic tatwon kaa aavarti gun' naam se shodhpatr prakashit kiya to unhone bahut se agyaat tatwon kii bhavishyavaanee kii thi jisamen jarmenium bhi tha. unhone iske liye carbon samooh main silicon or tin ke bich main khaali sthaan chhod rakhaa tha or isko ikosicon naam diya tha. 
 1885 main sexoni ke paas ek khan main ek nayaa khanij mila. climens vincler ne isaki pariksha kii or 1886 main isse ek nayaa tatva nikaala jo antimony se miltaa-julata tha. baad main isi ko 'jarmenium' naam diya gaya.
 aajkal jarmenium ke sampoorn upyog kaa lagbhag 35% fibre-optic pranaaliyon main hota he; 30% infrared prakaashiki main ; 15% bahulkikaran ke liye utprerak ke liye; tathaa 15% electronic or saura-vidyut anuprayogon main kiya jaataa he.
