 nik junior ek america digital cabel or satellite tivi channel he, jo vycom ke vycom media networks kii ikai, nicelodian samooh dwaara chalaaya ja rahaa he. is channel kaa mukhyalay new york shahar main he. ye channel chah varsh se kam umr ke bacchon ko dhyaan main rakh kar apana kaarykram chalaati he. in kaaryakramon main nae kaaryakramon ke alaava kuch pehle se de chuke yaa kisi anya jagah par chal rahe kaarykram bhi hote hai. nicelodian ke kaaran kabhi kabhi nik junior ko bhram dur karne ke liye "da nik junior channel" naam dikhaanaa padataa he. khaas kar aise samay pe, jab nik main bhi chote bacchon ke kaarykram dete rahete hai.
