 avidya, vidya kaa vilom shabd he. bhartiya dharmon main 'avidya' kaa arth he- agyaan, galat dharna. hindu granthon main 'avidya' kaa baar-baar prayog hua he. 
 brahm gyaan se itar gyaan ko bhi avidya kaha gaya he. aajkal jise vigyaan kaha jaataa he, use bhi avidya kaha gaya he. aisa nahin he ki bhartiya darshan main sarvatra avidya ko hin hi bataaya gaya hao. kahin-kahin use vidya kaa poorak bhi mana gaya he. udhaaharan ke liye, ishopanishad main uplabdh ek mantra kaa antim vaakyansh nimnlikhit he :
 iskaa shabdic arth yah he- jo vidya or avidya-in donon ko hi ek saath jaanata he, vah avidya se mrityu ko paar karke vidya se amritatva praapt kar letaa he.
