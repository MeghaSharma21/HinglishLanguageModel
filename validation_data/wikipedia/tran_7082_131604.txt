 herschel vikram patel ek bhartiya pratham shrenee cricket ke khilaadi hai jo mukhya rup se tej gendbaaji karte he. daahine haath ke tej gendbaaj, herschel patel ne 2008-09 under-19 vinu makad trophy main 11 kii prabhaavshaalee ausat se 23 wicket liye the. baad main unhone 2009-10 main gujarat ke liye apane ek divasiy cricket kii shuruyaat kii thi.
 ye 2012 se 2017 tak indian premier lig main ye viraat kohli kii captaini vaali royal chalangers bangalore team main khel rahe the lekin 2018 indian premier lig main inhen dilli dairedevills ne apni team main shaamil kiya tha.
