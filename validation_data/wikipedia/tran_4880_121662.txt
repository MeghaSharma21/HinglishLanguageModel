 dharmasamraat swami karpatri bharat ke ek mahaan sant, swatantraaa sangram senaanee evam rajaneta the. unka mul naam hari narayan ojha tha. ve hindu dasnami parampara ke sanyasi the. diksha ke uparant unka naam " hariharanand saraswati" tha kintu ve "karpatri" naam se hi prasiddh the kyonki ve apane anjuli kaa upyog khaane ke bartan kii tarah karte the. unhone akhil bhartiya ram rajya parishad naamak rajnatik dal bhi banaaya tha. dharamshastron main inaki adwitiya evam athulaneey vidwata ko dekhte hue inhen 'dharmasamraat' kii oopaadhi pradaan kii gai.
 jeevani -
 swami shri kaa janm sanwat 1964 vikrami main shravan maas, shukla paksh, dwitiya ko gram bhatni, zila pratapgarh uttar pradesh main sanathan dharmi sarauparin brahman swa. shri raamanidhi ojha evam paramdharmik susanskrita swa. shrimati shivarani ji ke aangan main hua. bachapan main unka naam 'hari narayan' rakhaa gaya. swami shri 8-9 varsh kii aayu se hi satya kii khoj hetu ghar se palaayan karte rahe. vastutah 9 varsh kii aayu main saubhagyawati kumari mahadevi ji ke saath vivaah sampann hone ke pashchaat 16 varsh kii alpaayu main grihatyag kar diya. usee varsh jyotirmath ke shankaracharya swami shri brahmanand saraswati ji maharaj se naishthik brahmacharee kii diksha lee. hari narayan se ' harihar chaitanya ' bane. ve swami brahmanand saraswati ke shishy the. swami ji kii samaran shakti 'photographic' thi, yah itani teevr thi ki ek baar koi cheez pard lene ke varshon baad bhi bataa dete the ki ye amuk pustak ke amuk prishth par amuk rup main likhaa hua he. unhone tark dhurandhar madan mohan malviya ji evam swami dayanand saraswati ji ko bhi parrast kiya tha, unhen saraswati ji kaa vardaan tha ki swapn main bhi unki vaani shaastra viruddh nahin hogi. unke liye shaastra sammat bhagavan kaa swaroop hi grahya tha.swami ji vaishnav maton ke viruddh nahin the, balki jab kaashi ke vidwatparishad ne bhagavatpurana ke navam dasham skandh ko ashleel evam kshepak kahkar nikaalane kaa nirnay kar liya tab karpatriji ne puure do maah paryant bhagwat kii adbhut vyakhya prastut kar siddh kar diya ki vah to bhagwat kii aatma hi he, haan jab kuch vaishnavon ne karpatriji ke liye apane granthon main katu shabdon kaa prayog kiye tab unke shastrarth maharathi shishyon ne uskaa usee bhashaa main uttar diya. isse unhen vaishnav virdohi samajhane kii bhul kabhi nahin kerani chahie. aaj jo raamarajy sambandhi vichaar gandhi darshan tak main dikhaai dete hai, dharm sangh, raamarajy parishad, ramamandir adolan, dharm saapeksh rajya, aadi sabhi ke mul main swami ji hi hai. santon ke vaicharik virodhon ke saath hi unamem aapasi prem bhi he hamen use nahin bhoolanaa chahie.
 shiksha-
 naishthik bramhchary shri jivan dutt maharaj ji se sanskrit adhyayan shaddarshanacharya pandit swami shri vishweshwarashram ji maharaj se vyakaran shaastra, darshan shaastra, bhagwat, nyaayashastra, vedant adhyayan, shri achutmuni ji maharaj se adhyayan grahan kiya. 
 tapasvi jivan-
 17 varsh kii aayu se himalay gaman prarambh kar akhand sadhana, aatmadarshan, dharm seva kaa sankalp liya. kaashi dham main shikhasutra parityag ke baad vidwat, sanyas praapt kiya. ek dhai gaz kapadaa evam do langoti maatr rakhkar bhayankar shitoshna varsha kaa sahan karnaa inkaa 18 varsh kii aayu main hi swabhaav ban gaya tha. trikaal snaan, dhyaan, bhajan, pujan, to chalata hi tha. vidyadhyayan kii gati itani teevr thi ki sampurna varsh kaa paathyakram ghanton or dinon main hridyangam kar lete. gangatat par foons kii jhonpadi main ekaaki nivas, gharon main bhikshagrahan kerani, choubees ghanton main ek baar. bhoomishyan, niravana charan yatra. gangatat nakhar main prayek pratipada ko dhoop main ek lakdi kii kil gad kar ek taang se khadey hokar tapasya rat rahete. choubees ghante vyateet hone par jab surya kii dhoop se keel kii chaaya usee sthaan par padati, jahaan 24 ghante purv thi, tab duusare pair kaa asana badalte. aisi kathor sadhana or gharon main bhiksha ke kaaran "karpatri" kehalaae. shri vidya main dikshit hone par dharmasamraat kaa naam shodashanand nath padaa.
 dand grahan-
 24 varsh kii aayu main param tapasvi 1008 shri swami brahmanand saraswati ji maharaj se vidhivat dand grahan kar "abhinavshankar" ke rup main prakatya hua. ek sundar ashram kii sanrachana kar purna rup se sanyasi ban kar "paramhans paribrajakacharya 1008 shri swami hariharaanand saraswati shri karpatri ji maharaj" kehalaae. 
 akhil bhartiya ram rajya parishad-
 akhil bhartiya ram rajya parishad bharat kii ek paramparawaadi hindu party thi. isaki sthapana swami karpatri ne san 1948 main kii thi. is dal ne san 1952 ke pratham loksabhaa chunaav main 3 siten praapt kii thi. san 1952, 1957 evam 1962 ke vidhansabha chunaavon main hindi kshetron main is dal ne darjanon siten hasil kii thi. 
 goraksha aandolan - 
 indira gandhi ke liye us samay chunaav jeetna bahut mushkil tha, karpatri ji maharaj ke aashirwad se indira gandhi chunaav jiti . indira gandhi ne unase vaada kiya tha chunaav jeetne ke baad gaay ke saare katl khaane band ho jaayegein . jo angrejo ke samay se chal rahe hai .lekin indira gandhi musalamaanon or communiston ke dawaab main aakar apane vaade se mukar gayi thi .jab tatkaaleen pradhanmantri ne santon is maang ko thukraa diya, jisame savidhan main sanshodhan karke desh main gau vansh kii hatya par paabandi lagane kii maang kii gayi thi ,to santon ne 7 navambar 1966 ko sansad bhawan ke saamne dharna shuru kar diya, hindu panchang ke anusaar us din vikrami samvath 2012 karthik shukla kii ashtami thi, jise "gopashtami" bhi kaha jaataa he .is dharne main bharat sadhu-samaaj, sanathan dharm, jain dharm aadi sabhi bhartiya dharmik samudaayon ne isamen bad-chadhakar bhaag liya. is aandolan main chaaron shankaracharya tathaa swami karpatri ji bhi jute the. jain muni sushilkumaar ji tathaa sarvadeshik sabha ke pradhan lala ramgopal shalwale or hindu mahasabha ke pradhan pro ramsingh ji bhi bahut sakriy the. shri sant prabhudutt brahmacharee tathaa puri ke jagadguru shankaracharya shri swami niranjandev tirth tathaa mahatma ramachandra vir ke aamaran anshan ne aandolan main praan phoonk diye the.lekin indira gandhi ne un nihate or shant santon par police ke dwaara goli chalava di, jis se kai sadhu maare gaye . is htya cond se kshubdh hokar tatkaaleen grahamantri ” gulzari laal nanda ” ne apana tyaag patr de diya, or is cond ke liye khud ko sarkaar ko jimmedaar bataaya tha .lekin sant " ram chandra vir " anshan par date rahe jo 166 dinon ke baad unki maut ke baad hi samaapt hua tha . ram chandra vir ke is adwitiya or itane lambe anshan ne duniya ke sabhi ricard tod die he . yah duniya kii pehali ansi ghatana thi jisame ek hindu sant ne gau mata kii raksha ke liye 166 dinon tak bhukhe rah kar apana balidaan diya tha .
 brahmaleen-
 magh shukla chaturdashi samvath 2038 ko kedarghat varanasi main swechha se unke panch praan mahapraan main villin ho gaye. unke nirdeshaanusaar unke nashwar parthiv sharir kaa kedarghat sthit shri ganga maharani ko paavan god main jal samadhi di gai|
 unhone varanasi main "dharmasangh" kii sthapana kii. unka adhikansh jivan varanasi main hi beetaa. ve advait darshan ke anuyaayi evam shikshak the. san 1948 main unhone akhil bhartiya ram rajya parishad kii sthapana kii jo paramparawaadi hindu vichaaron kaa rajnatik dal he.
 aapane hindu dharm kii bahut seva kii. aapane anek adbhut granth likhe jaise :- vedarth parijaat, ramayana mimasaa, vichaar piyush, marxvad or raamarajy aadi. aapke grantho main bhartiya parampara kaa bada hi adbhut v pramanik anubhav praapt hota he. aap ne sadaiv hi vishuddh bhartiya darshan ko badi dridhtaa se prastut kiya he.
