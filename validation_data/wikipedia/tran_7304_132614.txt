 "titaram" uttar bhartiya rajya haryana ke kaithal jile main sthit ek gaanv he. yah gaanv rashtriya rajmarg 65 or rajya rajmarg 11-e ke mahatvapurn jankshan par kaithal se maatr 8 ki.me. duuri par sthit he. is bindu ko titaram moff ke rup main janaa jaataa he. yah mana jaataa he ki pracheen bhartiya dharmik granthon taitreey samhita or taittiriopanishad kii rachana isi gaanv ke aas-paas hui thi.2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar titaram gaanv kii aabaadi 4309 he jo 752 pariwaron main rahete hai.inamen se 2341 purush jabaki 1968 mahilaayein hai.is gaanv kii sakshartaa dar lagbhag 70% he.purush sakshartaa 79.35% he, jabaki mahila sakshartaa dar 57.14% he.panchayat raaj adhinium tathaa bharat ke samvidhan ke anusaar yah gaanv sarpanch dwaara prashasit kiya jaataa he.is gaanv kii aabaadi main mukhyat: kundu gotra ke jat hai. chamaar, kumhaar or lohaar aadi kuch anya jatiyaan is gaanv main hai.pehle yahaan kii adhikansh aabaadi krishi dwaara jeevanyaapan karti thi lekin ab kaithal shahar ke najadik hone tathaa ucch shiksha tak pahunch ke kaaran naukari or chote paimaane par vyapaar ke avasaron main vibhinn anya vikalp talash rahi he.ek jawahar navoday vidhyalay gaanv main 1987 main sthapit kiya gaya tha jo kaithal-jindan rajyamarg par sthit he.iske atirikt yahan ek sarkari hai skool or 2 sarkari praathmik vidhyalay ladakon or ladakiyon ke liye alag-alag se he.chandana, pyoda, devban or harsola aadi anya aasapaas ke gaanv hai.
