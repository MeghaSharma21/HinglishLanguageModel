 2015 isisi vishwa twanti-20 kwalifire, 2016 twanti-20 vishwa cap ke liye, 6 se 26 july 2015 ke liye yojit kiya gaya tha. toornament se donon ireland or scotland yojit kiya gaya. 51 maichon main pehle 16 deshon ke bich 72 maichon main se 14 deshon ke bich khelaa gaya, neeche. toornament isisi twanti-20 vishwa cap kwalifire shrinkhalaa kaa hissaa banaaya he, sheersh chah tiimon 2016 isisi vishwa tinti 20 toornament ke kwalifing daur main aage ja rahaa he.
 maiches jahaan donon tiimon ke tii20i kaa darja diya he ek twanti-20 antarrashtriya match ke rup main darj kiya gaya. is sthiti ke saath is toornament main tiimon scotland, ireland, netherland, afghanistan, sanyukt arab emirate, honkong, nepal or papua new gini kar rahe hai. tii20i maichon jo sthiti ke bina ek yaa do tiimon main shaamil ek tii-20 match ke rup main darj kiya gaya.
 scotland group bi kii sheersh khatm karke 2016 isisi vishwa twanti-20 toornament ke liye kwalifai karne vaali pehali team thi sah mejabaan ireland unhen group e kii sheersh khatm karke shaamil ho gaye kwalifire maichon ke madhyam se do samooh ke vijetaon main shaamil hone netherland the, afghanistan honkong, or oman. yah pehali baar he ki oman ke ek pramukh isisi ghatana ke liye or namibia ke upar unki jeet ke saath yogya tha, ve tii20i kaa darja praapt kii. sanyukt arab emirate or nepal, jinhone 2014 isisi vishwa twanti 20 main apane karier kii shuruyaat yogya nahin tha banaaya he, haalanki sanyukt arab emirate 2016 asia cap ke liye pehali baar ek twanti-20 praaroop main khelaa gaya tha main khelnaa tha.
 final ke baad ek gend barish ke kaaran gendbaaji kii ja rahi bina chhod diya gaya tha scotland or holland trophy saajha kii he. netherland keval sahyogi raashtra ateet 2014 isisi vishwa tinti 20 toornament main group charan ke liye agrim ke the.
    2016 isisi vishwa tinti 20 or isisi twanti-20 vishwa cap kwalifire 2019 ke liye kwalifai kiya.
