 nikaah pakistani urdu dharawahik he, jiskaa prasaaran 4 janvari 2015 se 14 june 2015 tak kul 23 din hua. iskaa nirdesan ahamed bhatti ne or lekhan kaa kaary nasir hossain ne kiya he.
 yah kahani aesha or rohail ke madhya hi gumti rahati he. vah donon bachapan main ek duusare ke kaaphi kareeb rahete hai. rohail kaa yah sapana rahata he ki vah baahar jaakar apni baaki kii padhai kare. uskaa sapana sach bhi ho jaataa he. vahiin samay ke saath uske pasand main bhi badlaav aa jaataa he. uski mulaaqaat vahaan jahara se hoti he vah use pasand karne lagata he or shaadi bhi kar letaa he. lekin vah apane parivaar ko nahin bataata kyonki use yah dar lagata he ki kahin uskaa parivaar use apnaane se inkaar na kar de.
