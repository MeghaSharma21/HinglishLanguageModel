 rajdhani vah nagarpalika hoti he, jise kisi desh, pradesh, praant yaa anya prashasnic ikai athwa kshetra main sarkaar kii gaddi hone kaa praathmik darja hasil hota he. rajdhani misaali taur par ek shahar hota he, jahaan sambandhit sarkaar ke daphtar or sammelan -tikaane sthit hote hai or aam taur par apane kaanoon yaa samvidhan dwaara nirdhaarit hoti he.
 shabd rajdhani sanskrit se aayaa he. rajdhani aam taur par sanghatak kshetra kaa sab se bada sahar hota he parantu lazimi taur par nahin.
 bahut saare suubon kii do se zyaada rajdhaniyaan hai or kuch aise bhi hai jin kii koi rajdhani nahin he.
