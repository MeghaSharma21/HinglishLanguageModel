 mirabai krishna-bhakthi shakha kii pramukh kavyitri hai. unki kavitaaon main stri paradheenata ke prati ek gahari tis he, jo bhakthi ke rang main rang kar or gahari ho gayi he. mira bai ne krishna-bhakthi ke sfoot padon kii rachana kii he.
 mirabai kaa janm sanwat 1504 vikrami main medta main duuda ji ke chauthe putr ratan singh ke ghar hua.  vistaar se jaankaree ke liye mira charit. ye bachapan se hi krishnabhakti main ruchi lene lagi thim.mira kaa janm rathore rajput parivaar main hue v unka vivaah mewar ke sisodiya raaj parivaar main hua. udaipur ke maharana kunwar bhojraj inke pati the jo mewar ke maharana sanga ke putr the. vivaah ke kuch samay baad hi unke pati kaa dehant ho gaya. pati kii mrityu ke baad unhen pati ke saath sati karne kaa prayaas kiya gaya, kintu mira iske liye taiyaar nahin huin. ve sansar kii ore se virakt ho gayin or sadhu-santon kii sangati main harikirthan karte hue apana samay vyateet karne lageen. pati ke paralokavas ke baad inaki bhakthi din-pratidin badhati gai. ye mandiron main jaakar vahaan maujuud krishnabhakton ke saamne krishnaji kii murti ke aage naachati rahati thim. mirabai kaa krishnabhakti main naachna or gaanaa raaj parivaar ko acha nahin laga. unhone kai baar mirabai ko vish dekar maarane kii koshish kii. ghar waalon ke is prakaar ke vyavhaar se pareshaan hokar vah dwarka or vrundavan gayi. vah jahaan jaati thim, vahaan logon kaa sammaan miltaa tha. log unhe devi ke jaisaa pyaar or sammaan dete the.mira kaa samay bahut badi rajnatik uthal puthal kaa samay rahaa he. babar kaa hindustan par hamalaa or prasiddh khanvaa kii ladaai jo kii babar or rana sangram singh ke bich hui, jisamen rana sanga kii paraajay hui or bharat main mughalon kaa adhipaty shuru hua. hindutv ke patan or avsaan ke din armbh hue. desh main rajnatik asthirataa paidaa hui jisamen dharm or samskruti kii raksha ek bahut badi chunauti thi. is sabhi paristithiyon ke bich mira kaa rahasyavaad or bhakthi kii nirgun mishrit sagun paddatti savarmaanya bani.
 dwarka main samvath 1627 wo bhagwaan krishna kii murti main samaa gayi.
