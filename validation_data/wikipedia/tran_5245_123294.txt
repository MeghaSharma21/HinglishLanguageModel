 1990 ke baad se bharat me kaaphi saare udyogon kaa tezi se vikaas hua he. zyaadaatar logon ke najariye se 1990 se abak kiye gayi aarthik sudhaar. aarthik sudhaaron ne hi bharat ko duniya main ek shashakt aarthik desh kaa darza dilaayaa. vibhinn udyogon kaa bharat me vikaas abhootpurv he.
 software ke kshetra me bharat me '70 ke dashak se vikaas shuru hua. ye voh samay tha jab infosis, wipro, satyam, aadi bhartiya companiyon kii sthapana hui. '80 ke dashak me bharat ke tatkaaleen pradhanmantri shri rajeev gandhi ne bharat me computer ke prayog ko pehali baar sarkari rup se protsaahan diya. haalaaki us samay software udyog ne zyaada tarakkee nahin kii par ye is udyog kii neenv thi. 1990 ke baad bharat me software or hardware pe lagane vaale shulk or kar. karon ko kam karnaa shuru kar diya. desh bhar me software parks banaaye gayi. bangalore me bahut sari software companiyon ne tarakkee kii. dhire dhire bharat kaa software niryaat badhta chalaa gaya. bhartiya software companiyo ne vishwa bhar me kaaphi naam kamaaya. bahut saare kaulejon se nikle hue chhatron ko in companiyon ne naukari di. shuru me bharat vishwa ko sasta software dene ke liye mashhuur hua. dhire dhire antar-rashtreey pratispardhaa ke chalate in companiyon kii gunvatta me bhi sudhaar aayaa or inhe pahle se bhi zyaada bijnes milane laga. galat nahin hoga agar ye kaha jaye ki software udyog hi vah uddyog he jisaki safalta baad me duusare udyogon me bhi dikhne lagi. '90 ke dashak me bharat me bahut sari multi-naishanal companiyon ne bhi apane daftar shuru kiye. jaise ibiem, intel, microsoft, sap, orkl. or bangalore or phir saraa bharat, ek software kendra ke rup me janaa jane laga.
 software ke baad aglaa udyog jisane bharat ke madhyam vargeey yuva varg ko pahchaan dilaayi. is udyog kii shuruyaat '90 ke dashak me hui.
 pehle bharat me sirf sarkaari bank hote the. state bank or uski duusari bainkon kaa varchasv hota tha. ye bank kaaphi iniafishiant hote the. inme babugiri or laal fitaashahee k bolbala tha. nayi takneekon se, jaise computer, atm, internet, se inkaa koi vaasta nahin tha. us samay bank upbhogataaon kaa khaataa khol ke unpe ehsaan karte the. bainko se rin lenaa kaaphi mushkil hota tha. rin lene jaise saadhaaran kaamon ke liye rishwat dena aam baat thi.
 1990 ke baad nijee kshetron ke bank jaise isiaisiai bank, achdiafasi bank, utiai bank, aaye. baad me kayi antar-rashtreey bainko ko bhi bazaar me khuli arthvyavastha ke antargat aane kaa mauka mila. state bank or kayi anya bainkon kaa anshik nijeekaran hua or inke share share bazaar me utaare gayi. bazaar me is tarah kii pratispardhaa ke kayi fayade hue. upbhogataaon ko behtar seva milane lagi. babugiri or laal fitaashahee se sarkari bainkon ko nijaat mili. rishwat ke kaarobaar kaa sarkari bainkon se boria bistar gol ho gaya. '00 ke dashak me or bhi kayi parivartan hue. bhartiya resarv bank ne apni zimmedaariyon ko thik se nibhaana shuru kiya. ab bazarwaad ke chalate byaaj dar. byaaj daren ghatane lagi. grih rin kii byaaj dar jo '90 ke shuruaati saalon me 17%-20% tak hua karti thi, vah ghat ke 2005 me 7.5-8% pe aa gayi. haalaaki 2006 me ye phir se batch ke 8.5-9% ke kareeb aagayi he. byaaj dar. byaaj daron main aayi giravat or bainkon me pardarshita ke badhne se logo ko apni zaruuraton ke liye aasaani se or bina rishwat diye rin milane laga. isse kaphi saare naye udyog-dhandon ko badhaava mila. achal sampatti ke bazaar me jam ke oochaal aayaa. 1990 ke pehle jahaan ghar khareednaa boodhaape kaa kaam hota tha vahi ab itna aasaan ho gaya ki bahut log 25-30 saal kii umr me hi ghar kharidne lage.
 ritel udyog ko '00 ke dashak se badhaava milno shuru hua. abhi ye udyog praarambhik avasthaa me he. par yahan tarakkee ke nishaan put ke paanv paalane me hi dikh rahe he. is udyog ne 2004-2006 ke dauraan hazaron logo ke liye rozgaar ke dwaar khol diye hai. bade shaharon me olerady kayi mal khul chuke he. foodworld, mande tu sande, big bazaar, pantalun, vivex, pai elactricals, showers stop, izone jaise bade ritel stop bharat ke pramukh shahron me aam ho gayi hai. vishaal megamart jaise ritel dukaane manzole shaharon me batch rahe he. haal hi me reliance ne bade paimaane pe ritel me utarane kaa elaan kar ke desh bhar me sexdon store kholane kaa man bana liya he.is udyog me kaaphi utsaah dikhaayi detaa he. agar aise hi chalaa to sheeghra hi ye udyog bahut tarakkee kar legaa.
 90 ke shuruaati varsho tak is udyog me sarkari companiyon - indian airlins or air india kaa ekadhikaar tha. phir maidan me aaye sahaara or jait. sarkari ekadhikaar tutaa or bharat me pahli baar is udyog me koi pratispardhaa dekhne ko mili. ye voh samay tha jab ki vimaan me udan bharna rahisi kaa pratik or madhyam varg kaa sapana tha. '00 ke dashak me deccan airuez, spice jait, kingfisher airlines, go airuez, indigo, jaisi kayi companiyaan shuru hui. duusare udyogo kii tarah yahan bhi pratispardhaa ke badhane se kiraaye me baari giravat aayi. bangalore se dilli kaa kiraaya jahaan 2001 me 9000 rupee se le ke 13,000 rupee tak hota tha, vahi 2006 me sasti vimaan sevaaon me ye 3000 rupee rah gaya. is udyog me bahut saare naye rozgaar bane. 4 saal me vimaan yatra karne valon kii sankhya is kadar batch gayi ki hawaiaddon pe jagah kii kami pads gayi. aajkal bangalore or hyderabad samet kayi duusare shaharon me naye hawaii addon kaa nirmaan chal rahaa he.
