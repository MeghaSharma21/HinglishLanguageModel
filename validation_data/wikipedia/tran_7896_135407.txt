 alakh kaa arth he, jo dikhaai na pade, adrishy, apratyaksh. udhaaharan- 'alakh na lakhiya jaai' . agochar, indriatit, paramatma kaa ek visheshan. 'alakh arup avaran so karata ' - jaaysi.
 'alkhadriba' vah sthaan jahaan par sant dadudayaal apane anuyaayiyon ke saath baithkar aadhyatmik charchaa kiya karte the.
 'alakh niranjan' paramatma kaa ek naam jo uske shoonyavat adrishy rahane ke kaaran padaa.
 'alakhwala' jogiyon kaa ek upsampradaay.
 alakh shabd se sambandhit kuch or sampradaay bhi hai, yatha 'alkhadhari' bharat ke pashchimotar pradeshon kaa ek sampradaay, jiske anuyaayi alakh agochar tatv kaa dhyaan karte hai. 'alakhnami' sampradaay.
