 sloth dakshin va madhya america main paaya jane vaala madhyam aakaar kaa ek shaakaahaari standhari praani he. yah dhimi gati se hilne ke liye prasiddh he or lok-kalpana main ise ek bahut aalsee jaanavar samajha jaataa he. sloth kii chah jatiyaan hai jo do jeevvaigyanik kulon main vibhajit hai: do-oongali vaale megalonicidaye or teen-oongali vaale bradepodidaye . aadhunik sloth jangal ke vrikshon main hi rahete hai haalanki ateet main inaki samudr main tair sakane vaali jaleey or hathi-jaise bade aakaar kii dharti par ghoomane vaali jatiyaan bhi thim jo hazaron yaa lakhon varsh purv vilupt ho chuki hai.
 sloth pilosa naamak jeevvaigyanik gana main shaamil hai jisamen cheenteekhor bhi aate hai, yani slothon or cheenteeshoron kaa anuvanshik sambandh he.
