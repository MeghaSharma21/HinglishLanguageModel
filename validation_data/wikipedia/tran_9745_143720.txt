 
 kshetri yaa chhetri nepal ke pahari kshetron main rahane vaale yoddha varna ke mul nivasee hai, inhen pahari raajput, khas raajput, nepali yaa gorkhali kshatriya bhi kahan jaataa he. ye ek hind-arya bhashik jati hai. kshetri yaa chhetri yaa kshathareey sab kshatriya ke apbhransh hai or ye hindu varna vyavastha ke antargat kshatriya varna main aate hai. ye log mul rup se sainik, raja or prashasnic kshetra main kaaphi aage hai. ye bahun or khas dalit ke jaise khas samudaay ke ek vibhaajan hai.
 kshetri nepal ke kul jansankhya main sarvaadhik 16.6% hai. is jati ko nepal main sattaruq mana jaataa he. in logon kii utpatti ke bare main vibhinn itihaaskaar or khojakarta jesai ki dorbahadur visht or suryamani adhikari, aadi ke anusaar nepal ke pashchimi pahari ilaaka karnali pradesh main hua tha. is jati ke purvaj purvi irani bhashik khas jati hai jo bahlik-gandhar kshetramen paae jate the. aaj ye nepal ke sabhi kshetron or bharat ke kuch kshetron main bhi paae jate hai. ye log purnat: hindu hote hai or sthani mashto devtaa kii pujas karte hai. is pujas ko masht pujas yaa devaali kehete hai. in kaa maatrubhaasha nepali bhashaa he or ye indo-european bhashaa parivaar ke sadsy hai. 
 nepal ke khas rajvansh, khaptad rajvansh, sinja rajvansh, thapa vansh, basnet, kunwar or pande vansh, darbaria samuh or nepal ke pichley samay ke croor shaasha rana vansh bhi kshetri jati main aate hai. kshetri adhiktar nepal sarkaar or nepali sena ke ucch pad par karyrat paae jate hai. in ke liye bharatiya sena main ek sainik dasta 9veen gorkha regiment arakshit he.
 kshetri kshatriya kii nepali bolichali ke shabd hai. kshetri sanskrit shabd kshatriya ke sidhe aprabhansh hai. inco khasia bhi kahan jaataa he.
