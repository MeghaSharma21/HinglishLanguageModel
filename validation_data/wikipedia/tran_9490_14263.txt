 kaarbhailic zila china ke shinjiyang praant ke kashgar vibhaag main sthit ek zila he. iskaa kshetrafal 28,600 varg kimi he or san 2002 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 3,70,000 anumaanit kii gai thi. tibet-shinjiyang rajmarg yaheen se shuru hokar tibet kii ore jaataa he.
 dhyaan dijiye ki 'kaarbhailic' naam uigur bhashaa se liya gaya he. isamen 'qa' kaa uccharan 'k' se zaraa bhinn he or usee tarah 'ga' kaa uccharan bhi 'ga' se zaraa bhinn he.
