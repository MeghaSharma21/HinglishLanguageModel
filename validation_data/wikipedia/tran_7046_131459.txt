 kampilya ek puranic nagar he jo mahabharat ke samay panchal janapad kii rajdhani thi jiske raja drupad the. 
 iske naam kaa sarvapratham ullekh yajurveda kii taittareey samhita main 'compila' rup main miltaa he. bahut sambhav he, puraanon main varnit panchal naresh bhrimyashv ke putr kapil yaa kampilya ke naam par hi is nagari kaa namakaran hua ho. mahabharat kaal se pehle panchal janapad ganga ke donon ore vistrut tha. uttar-panchal kii rajdhani ahimhatra or dakshin panchal kii kampilya thi. dakshin panchal ke sarvapratham raja ajamidh kaa puraanon main ullekh he. isi vansh main prasiddh raja nip or brahmadatt hue the. mahabharat ke samay dronacharya ne panchal naresh drupad ko parajeet kar usase uttar-panchal kaa pradesh chin liya tha. is prasang ke varnan main mahabharat main kampilya ko dakshin panchal kii rajdhani bataaya gaya he. us samay dakshin panchal kaa vistaar ganga ke dakshin tat se chambal nadi tak tha. brahmadatt jataka main bhi dakshin panchal kaa naam compilratth yaa kampilya raashtra he. buddha sahitya main kampilya kaa buddha ke jeevancharit ke sambandh main varnan miltaa he. kinvadanti ke anusaar isi sthaan par buddha ne kuch ascharyajanak kaary kiye the, jaise swarg main jaakar apni mata ko upadesh dene ke pashchaat vah isi sthaan par utre the. chini yatri yuvan chwang ne bhi saatvin sadi i. main is nagar ko apni yatra ke prasang main dekha tha. 
 vartmaan compila main ek ati pracheen dhuh aaj bhi raja drupad kaa kot kehalaata he evam budhi ganga ke tat par draupadikund he jisase, mahabharat kii katha ke anusaar, draupadi or dhridhyumna kaa janm hua tha. kunda se bade pariman kii, sambhavat: mauryalin, inten nikali hai. compila ke mandiron se anek pracheen murtiyan praapt hui hai. compila bauddhadharm ke samaan jaindharm kaa bhi kuch dinon tak centre rah chuki he, jaisaa yahan se praapt tirthankarasn kii anek pratimaaon tathaa jain abhilekhon se suchit hota he. kampilya ke 'compilnagar', 'compillanagar' or 'compila' naam sahitya main uplabdh hai. iskaa apbhransh rup kampil bhi miltaa he. kampilya nagari pracheen kaal main kaashi, ujjayini aadi kii bhanti hi prasiddh thi or pracheen sahitya main ise anek kathaon kii ghatnasthali banaaya gaya he, jaise mahabharat, shantiparv main raja brahmadatt or poojani chidiya kii katha ko kampilya main hi ghatit kaha gaya he.
 pracheen kinvadanti ke anusaar prasiddh bhartiya jyotishacharya barahamihir kaa janm kampilya main hi hua tha.
