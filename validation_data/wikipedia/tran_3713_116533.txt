 raktaadhan rakt yaa rakt-aadhaarit utpaadon ko ek vyakti se duusare vyakti ke parisancharan tantra main sthanantarit karne kii prakriya he. kuch sthitiyon main, jaise ki chot lagane ke kaaran atyadhik raktasrav hone par, raktaadhan jivan-rakshi ho saktaa he, yaa shalya-chikitsa ke dauraan hone vaale rakt kii haani kii aapurti karne ke liye iskaa upyog kiya ja saktaa he. raktaadhan kaa upyog rakt sambandhi beemaari ke dwaara utpann gambhir raktaheenata yaa bimbaanu-alpata kaa upachaar karne ke liye bhi kiya ja saktaa he. adhiraktasrav yaa ardhchandrakaar laal raktakoshika sambandhi beemaari se prabhavit vyakti ke liye bahudhaa raktaadhan kii aavashyaktaa ho sakati he. praarambhik raktaadhanon main sampurna rakt kaa upyog hota tha, lekin aadhunik chikitsa pranali aam taur par keval rakt ke avyavon kaa upyog karti hai.

 vastav main, vladimir lenin kii mrityu ke baad, mrit bolshevik netaa ko punerjeevit karne ke uddeshy se, bogdanove ko lenin ke mastishk ke adhyayan kaa kaary saunpa gaya. apane ek prayog ke parinamswaroop bogdanove kii 1928 main us samay mrityu ho gai jab ek aadhaan main malaria evam tapedic se peedit ek chaatr kaa rakt uske sharir main daal diya gaya. kuch vidwaanon ne anumaan lagaaya ki unki mrityu ek aatmahatya ho sakati he, jabaki anya ise rakt samooh kii asangati ko iskaa kaaran bataate hai jise us samay purna rup se nahin samajha gaya.

 jamaa kiye gaye rakt ke surakshit rakhe jane kii avadhi main vistaar karne vaala ek skandanrodhi parirakshak, sipidie-1 tha, jisaki shuruuaat 1979 main kii gai, jisane rakt kii aapurti main vruddhi kii or blad bainkon ke bich sansadhano ke aadan-pradaan ko sahaj banaaya.

 aretch samooh kaa pramukh mahatv bhrun or navjaat shishu ke raktalayi rog main isaki bhoomika he. jab ek aretch negative maa ek positive bhrun dhaaran karti he, to vah aretch pratijan ke viruddh pratirakshit ho sakati he. yah aamtaur par garbhavastha ke dauraan mahatvapurn nahin hota he, lekin baad ke garbhadharanon main vah aretch pratijan ke prati ek pratiraksha pratikriya viksit kar sakati he. maa kii pratiraksha pranali aparaa ke dwaara shishu kii laal koshikaon par hamalaa kar sakati he. echduaen ke halke maamle viklaangata utpann kar sakate hai, lekin kuch gambhir maamle ghaatak ho sakate hai. aretch-di echduaen main sabase adhik saamaanya rup se shaamil laal koshika vaala pratijan he, lekin laal koshika vaale anya pratijan bhi yah sthiti paidaa kar sakate hai. sune hue rakt prakaaron main "positive" yaa "negative" jaise ki "o positive" aretch-di pratijan hota he.
 anek sankramak rog daata se praptakarta ko sanchaarit ho sakate hai.
 aadhaan ke madhyam se sanchaarit hone vaale rogon main shaamil hai:
 jab kisi vyakti ke aadhaan kii jarurat kaa purvanumaan kiya jaataa he, jaisaa ki nirdhaarit shalya-chikitsa main hota he, to beemaari ke sanchaaran se raksha karne or rakt prakaar kii sangatta kii samasya dur karne ke liye ke liye usee jeev se sambandhit daan kaa upyog kiya ja saktaa he. echivi ke aarambhik varshon ke dauraan praptakarta ko gyaat daataaon se daan ek aam baat thi. is prakaar ke daan abhi bhi vikasshil deshon main aam baat hai.
 sangrah karne ke baad daan main die gaye rakt kaa aamtaur par prasanskaran kiya jaataa he, jisase ki ise vishisht rogi jansankhya main upyog ke liye upyukt banaaya ja sake. udaahranon main shaamil hai:
 bal chikitsa vaale rogiyon main rakt aadhaan kii suraksha sunishchit karne ke liye, aspataal sankraman se bachane ke liye atirikt ehtiyaat barat rahe hai or ve vishesh rup se jaanch kii gai un ikaiyon kaa upyog karnaa pasand karte hai jo cytomegalovyrus ke liye negative ghoshit kiya ja chukaa he. adhikansh dishanirdesh navjaaton yaa kam vajan vaale shishuon ke liye, jinamein pratiraksha pranali purna rup se viksit nahin hui he, sirf shwet rakt koshika kii kami vaale ghatakon kii nahin balki siemavi-negative rakt ghatakon ke upyog kii salaah dete hai. ye vishisht aavashyaktaaen un rakt daataaon par atirikt pratibandh lagaati hai jo navjaat upyog ke liye daan kar sakate hai.
 aam taur par navjaat aadhaan nimnaankit do shreniyon main se kisi ek shrenee main padate hai:
 jaanch ke liye tip or screen shabdon kaa upyog kiya jaataa he jo rakt samooh ko nirdhaarit karata he evam jo samaan prajati ke vyaktiyon ke bahya utak ke viruddh swaabhaavik rup se utpann hone vaale rog-pratikaarakon kii jaanch karata he. ise puraa karne main lagbhag 45 minute lagate hai. marij kii purv ke pahchaan kiye gaye rog-pratikaarak ko jaanane ke liye rakt bank ke praudyogvigya marij kii vishesh aavashyaktaaon evam itihaas kii bhi jaanch karte hai.
 ek positive jaanch ek rog-pratikaarak ke panel/anveshan ko nyaaysangat tehraata he. ek rog-pratikaarak panel main daataaon ke rakt se vanijyik rup se taiyaar kiya gaya samooh O laal koshika vaale nilamban hote hai jinhen aam taur saamana kiye gaye evam naidanik rup se mahatvapurn rup jo samaan prajati ke vyaktiyon ke bahya utak ke viruddh swaabhaavik rup se utpann hone vaale rog-pratikaarakon ke prati samalakshani banaaya jaataa he. daata koshikaon main samayugmaji, vishamyugmaji vyanjak yaa vibhinn pratijanon ke koi bhi vyanjak nahin hote hai. pareekshan kiye jane vaale sabhi daata koshikaon ke samalakshaniyon ko ek chart main darshaya gaya he. vruddhi paddati kaa upyog kar rogi ke serum kii vibhinn daata koshikaon ke viruddh jaanch kii jaati he, jaise jail yaa elses. daata koshikaon ke viruddh marij ke serum kii pratikriyaaon ke aadhaar par, ek yaa adhik rog-pratikaarakon kii upasthiti kii pushti karne ke liye ek paddati ubhar kar saamne aegi. sabhi rog-pratikaarak naidanik rup se mahatvapurn nahin hote hai.ek baar marij ke andar naidanik rup se mahatvapurn rog-pratikaarak viksit ho gaya he to yah aavashyak he ki marij pratijan negative samalakshani laal rakt koshikaayen praapt kare jisase ki bhavishya main aadhaan sambandhi pratikriyaayein nahin ho. rog-pratikaarak sambandhi jaanch ke ek hisse ke rup main ek pratyaksh globulingrodhi pareekshan bhi kiya jaataa he.
 ek baar tip or prakaar screen puraa ho jane par, sambhavit daata ikaiyon ko marij ke rakt samooh ke saath sangatta, vishesh aavashyaktaaon evam pratijan negative ke aadhaar par chunaa jaayegaa. yadi koi bhi rog-pratikaarak upasthit nahin rahata he yaa uski sambhaavna nahin hoti he, to tatkaal spin yaa siesi vidhi kaa istemaal kiya ja saktaa he.
 tatkaal spin vidhi main, ek parakh nalee main daata koshikaon ke 3-5% vaale nilamban kii ek boond ke viruddh marij ke do boond serum kii jaanch kii jaati he or ek serophyug main ghumaya jaataa he. parakh-nalee main rakt kaa ekatrikaran yaa rakt sanlayan ek sakaaratmak pratikriya he or ikai nahin chadhaai jaani chahie.
 yadi koi rog-pratikaarak sandigdh he, to sambhavit daata ikaayiyon ko samalakshani banaakar sangat pratijan ke liye unki jaanch kii jaani chahie. tab pratikriyasheeltaa main vruddhi karne evam jaanch ko padhane main saral banane ke liye pratijan negative ikaiyon kii jaanch marij ke plasma ke viruddh 37 degree celsius par globulingrodhi/apratyaksh crossmatch takanic kaa prayog kar kii jaati he.
 yadi koi samay nahin ho to rakt ko "bina cross-milan kiya hua rakt" kaha jaataa he. bina cross-milan kiya hua rakt O positive yaa O negative hota he. aamtaur par O negative kaa upyog bacchon or prasav umr kii mahilaon ke liye kiya jaataa he. prayogshaala ke liye yah behatar he ki in maamalon main ek purv-aadhaan namoona praapt kiya jaae jisase ki marij ke vaastavik rakt samooh kaa nirdhaaran karne or samaan prajati ke vyaktiyon ke bahya utak ke viruddh swaabhaavik rup se utpann hone vaale rog-pratikaarakon kii jaanch karne ke liye tip evam screen kii prakriya apanaayi ja sakati he.
 raktaadhan ko unke strot ke aadhaar par do mukhya prakaaron main baanta ja saktaa he:
 jeevanu sambandhit vruddhi ko rokane ke liye koshikeey chayapachay ko dhimaa karne ke liye daata ke rakt kii ikaiyon ko prashitit rakhaa janaa chahie. ikai ko niyantrit bhandaaran se baahar le jane ke 30 minute ke bheetar aadhaan shuru karnaa chahie.
 rakt keval antahshirtmak rup se hi diya ja saktaa he. isliye ek upyukt kshamta vaali praveshni daalane kii aavashyaktaa hoti he.
 aadhaan sambandhi pratikriyaaon ke jokhimon ko kam se kam karne ke liye rakt dene ke purv, marij ke vyaktigat vivaranon kaa aadhaan kiye jane vaale rakt ke saath milan kiya jaataa he. lipikeey truti aadhaan pratikriyaaon kaa ek mahatvapurn strot he or shayya parshv main hone vaali milan prakriya main atirek kaa nirmaan karne ke prayaas kiye gaye hai.
 aam taur par 4 ghante kii avadhi tak rakt kii ek ikai di jaati he. raktaadhiky sambandhi hriday kii vifaltaa kii jokhim vaale marijon main, kai chikitsak taral padaarth ke adhibhaar ko rokane ke liye mutrasravvardhak aushadhi dete hai, jis sthiti ko aadhaan sambandhi parisanchari adhibhaar yaa tiesio kaha jaataa he. aadhaan sambandhi anya prakaar kii pratikriyaaon ko rokane ke liye kabhi-kabhi aadhaan ke purv acitaminophen or/yaa ek histamins ke prabhaav ko nishphal karne vaali aushadhi jaise ki disphenhydramin diye jate hai.
 sabase aam taur par rakt daan sampurna rakt ke rup main nas main nalshalaka lagaakar evam ise gurutv ke madhyam se ek plastic ke bag main sangrahit kiya jaataa he. tab sangrahit rakt ko ghatakon main vibhajit kiya jaataa he taaki iskaa sarvshereshth upyog kiya ja sake. laal rakt koshika, plasma or platelet ke atirikt, parinami rakt ghatak utpaadon main albumin protein, thakke banane vaale kaarak kaa sandran, ghulansheel padaarth ke thanda hone par bane avakshep, fibrinogen sandran, evam immunoglobulin bhi shaamil hote hai. laal koshikaon, plasma or platelets kaa bhi ek adhik jatil prakriya afferosis ke dwaara vyaktigat rup se daan diya ja saktaa he.
 viksit deshon main, aamtaur par daan praptakarta ke liye anaam hote hai, lekin kisi rakt bank main utpaada hameshaa daan ke sampurna chakra, pareekshan, ghatakon main algaav, bhandaaran, evam praptakarta ko die jane ke madhyam se vyaktigat rup se pataa lagane yogya hote hai. yah kisi sandigdh aadhaan sambandhit rog ke sanchaaran yaa aadhaan pratikriya ke prabandhan evam jaanch ko sakasham banaata he. vikasshil deshon main daata kabhi-kabhi vishesh rup se praptakarta ke dwaara yaa praptakarta ke liye, aamtaur par parivaar kaa koi sadsy, niyukt hota he or aadhaan se thik pehle daan kiya jaataa he.
 raktaadhan praapt karne se jude hue jokhim hote hai or inhen anumaanit laabh ke viruddh santulit kiya janaa chahie. raktaadhan kii sabase aam pratikul pratikriya jwariy gaiyr-raktasalayi pratikriya he, jisamen fever hota he jo swayan thik ho jaataa he or vah koi sthaayee samasyaayein yaa anushanbee-prabhaav utpann nahin karata he.
 raktasalayi pratikriyaaon main shaamil hai thand laganaa, sir dard, peeth main dard, saans phoolnaa, shyavata, seene main dard, hridkshiprata evam nimnaraktchaap.
 rakt utpaada shaayad hi jeevanu se dooshit ho sakate hai; 2002 se, 50,000 platelet aadhanon main se 1, evam 50,000 rakt koshika aadhanon main se 1 vyakti main gambhir jeevanu sankraman evam raktputita kaa jokhim anumaanit he.
 is baat kaa jokhim he ki die gaye raktaadhan se iske praptakarta main vishaanu sankraman sanchaarit hoga. 2006 se, sanyukt rajya america main rakt aadhaan ke madhyam se hepatitis bi hone kaa jokhim 250,000 chadhaayi gai ikaayiyon main 1 he or raktaadhan ke madhyam se sanyukt rajya america main echivi yaa hepatitis si hone kaa jokhim 2,000,000 chadhaayi gai ikaayiyon main 1 he. aadhaan sanchaarit rogon ke duusari evam teesri peedi ke jaanchon ke aagaman ke purv pehle ye jokhim adhik bade the. sadi 2000 ke aarambh main neucleic amla jaanch yaa "eneti" ke kriyanvayan ne jokhimon ko or adhik badha diya he, evam yah pushti kii he ki viksit vishwa main rakt aadhaan dwaara vishaanu sankraman atyant durlabh he.
 aadhaan-sambandhi acute phenfada sambandhi jakhm rakt aadhaan se sambandhit teji se babbti hui maanya pratikul ghatana he. tiareelai teevr shwasan sambandhi ksht vaala sahalakshan he, jo aksar fever, gaiyr-hriday janit phuffusiu shof, evam nimnaraktchaap se judaa hota he, jo 2000 aadhanon main se 1 main utpann ho saktaa he. lakshan halke se pranaghatak ho sakate hai, lekin adhikansh marij 96 ghante ke bheetar purna rup se change ho jate hai, evam is sthiti se hone vaali mrityu dar 10% se kam he. haalanki tiareelai ke kaaran bahut spasht nahin hai, yah nirantar echele rodhi antibody ke saath judaa rahaa he. kyonki echele rodhi garbhavastha ke saath gahare rup se jude hue hai, kai aadhaan sanghathanon ne aadhaan ke liye keval purushon se plasma kaa istemaal karne kaa faisla kiya he.
 rakt aadhaan praapt karne se jude hue anya jokhimon main shaamil hai maatra adhibhaar, louh adhibhaar, aadhaan se jude nirop-banaam-parposhi rog, tivrograhita sambandhi pratikriyaayein, evam acute raktasalayi pratikriyaayein .
 kya aadhaan sambandhi jokhimein bhandaaran samay ke dwaara bad jaati he, is baat kii chintaaen bhi ubhar ke saamne aa rahi hai, haalanki rakt yug ke mahatva ke sambandh main abhi tak aam sahmati nahin ho pai he. sambandhit rup se, kuch atisamvedansheel rogi samoohon jaise ki gambhir rup se biimaar vyakti ke liye aadhanon kii anishchit evam asangat kshamta ke sambandh main prashn uthaaye gaye hai, phir bhi adhyayan nirantar umr ko ekmaatr nirnaayak kaarak nahin maanate hai. is samay 17 billian dollar main anumaanit, aksar-apratyaashit aadhaan prabhaavaheenata se nipatane main lagi laagat kray, pareekshan/upachaar karne, evam raktaadhan karne kii sanyukt laagton se bahut adhik he. 
 copenhegan vishvavidhyalay main kaam kar rahe vaigyanikon ne april 2007 main patrika prakruti jaev praudyogiki main anzimon kii khoj karne kaa pataa lagaaya, jo sambhawatah samooh e, bi or abi se rakt ko o samooh main parivartit karne main sakasham banaata he. ye anzime rakt ke aretch samooh ko prabhavit nahin karte hai.
 raktaadhan ke sambandh main aapattiyaan vyaktigat, chikitsa, yaa dharmik kaarnon se utpann ho sakati he. udhaaharan ke liye, jehova ke gavaah mukhya rup se dharmik kaarnon se rakt ke aadhaan kaa virodh karte hai - ve maanate hai ki rakt pavitra he, haalanki unhone aadhaan se judi hui sambhavit jatiltaaon par prakaash daalaa he.
 pashuchikitsak bhi anya jaanwaron main aadhaan kaa prayog karte hai. ek sangat milan sunishchit karne ke liye vibhinn prajatiyon ko pareekshan ke vibhinn staron kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. udhaaharan ke liye, billi ke 3 gyaat prakaar hote hai, maveshi ke 11, kutton ke 12, suar ke 16 evam ghodon ke 34 rakt prakaar hote hai. haalanki, kai prajatiyon main pratham aadhaan ke purv paar milan kii aavashyaktaa nahin hoti he, kyonki gaiyr aatm koshika kii sataha ke pratijan ke viruddh rog-pratikaarak moolbhoot rup se abhivyakt nahin hote hai - arthath chadhaaye gaye rakt ke viruddh ek pratirakshi pratikriya apnaane ke liye jaanavar ko samvedansheel bananaa chahie.
 antar prajateey raktaadhan kii durlabh or prayogtmak karypranali vishmanirodh kaa ek rup he.
 2009 se, manushya ke liye koi bhi vyapak rup se istemaal kiye jane vaale oxigen-vaahak rakt sthanapann nahin hai, haalanki, gaiyr-rakt maatra vistaarak evam anya rakt-bachat takanic vyapak rup se uplabdh hai. ye sahaayak chikitsak evam shalya-chikitsak rog sanchaaran evam or pratiraksha daman ke jokhim se bachate hai, chronic rakt daata kii kami kii charchaa karte hai or jehova ke gavaahon evam anya logon kii chintaaon par dhyaan dete hai, jinaki aadhanit ko praapt karne main dharmik aapattiyaan hai.
 vartmaan main rakt ke kai sthanapann naidanik moolyankan ke charan main he. rakt ke ek upyukt vikalp kii talash karne ke adhikansh prayaason ne abhi tak koshika-rahit haemoglobin ke samaadhaan ke tareekon par dhyaan kendrit kiya he. rakt ke sthanapann aadhanon ko apatkaaleen aushadhi main or aspataal-purv iemes dekhbhaal main adhik saralata se uplabdh kara sakate hai. safal hone par, rakt ke aise sthanapann kai jiwanon ko bachaa sakate hai, vishesh rup se aghat hone par jahaan atyadhik raktasrav hota he. hemopyor, haemoglobin aadhaarit chikitsa dakshin africa main upyog ke liye maanya he.
