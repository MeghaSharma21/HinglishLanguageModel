 rin sanchayan kii adhikta ko aarthik samasyaon ko badtar banane hetu doshi tehraaya gaya he. udhaaharan ke liye atyadhik avanaman rin ke prarambh se purv/jidipi anupaat bahut adhik tha. aarthik agent baari rini the. bhavishya kii vapasiyon par atyadhik pratyaasha ke samakaksh, yah atyadhik rin, share bazaar main parisampatti bulabulon ke saath rahaa. jab pratyaasha main sudhaar kiya gaya, mudra apsfiti tathaa saakh charamrahat prarambh hui. mudra apsfiti ne prabhavi rup se rin ko adhik manhagaa kar diya tathaa, jaisaa ki fisher ne spasht kiya he, isse mudra apsfiti punah prabhavi hui kyonki apane rin str ko kam karne ke liye, aarthik agenton ne unke upbhog tathaa nivesh ko kam kar diya. maang kii kami ne vyapaar gatividhiyon ko kam kar diya evam or berogari paidaa kii. adhik pratyaksh arthon main mudra apsfiti ke kaaran badi hui rin laagat tathaa ghati hui maang, donon ke kaaran adhik diwaliyapan bhi paidaa hua.
 kuch sanghathanon ke liye udhari tathaa punarbhugtan vyavasthaaon ke vaikalpik prakaaron main pravesh karnaa sambhav hota he jisase diwaliyapan nahin hoga. udhaaharan ke liye companiyaan kai baar rinon ko unke dwaara udhaar lee gai equity main badal detii he. is maamle main, rindaata ko rin ke samakaksh kuch tathaa rini ke dividend evam capital gen ke rup main byaaj ko praapt karne kii aashaa rahati he. "punarbhugtan", atev, rini dwaara kii gayi aay ke anupatik hota he evam isaliye apane aap main diwaliyapan kaa kaaran nahin banataa. is tarah se ek baar rin ke parivartan ho jane ke baad ise rin nahin kaha jaayegaa.
 kuch vyakti vyaktigat, paariwarik, saamaajik, corporate tathaa shaskeey str par rin ke viruddh ek instrument tathaa sanstha ke rup main tark karte hai. islam main byaaj ke saath udhaar dena aaj bhi varjit he jabaki catholic carch ne ise 1882 ke baad se hi anumati di he, tathaa tora ne kaha he ki sabhi rinon ko prayek saat saalon tathaa prayek pachaas saalon main mitaa dena chaiye.
 yadi samay ke saath rin kaa punarbhugtan nahin kiya gaya to byaaj ke saath yah badhata jaayegaa. is prabhaav ko soodakhori kaha jaataa he, jabaki anya sandarbho main soodakhori shabd ko sweekrut jokhim ke liye vaazib laabh kii adhikta main, keval byaaj kii atyadhik dar ke rup main sanderbhit kiya jaataa he.
 anterrashtriya kanoonee vichaar main, ghrunit rin vah rin hota he jo shashan tantra dwaara un uddeshyon ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he jo rajya hit main na ho. is tarah ke rinon ko is siddhant ke dwaara us shashantantr dwaara liye gayi vyaktigat rinon ke rup main samajha jaataa he tathaa na ki rajya ke rin ke rup main.
 ucch byaaj daron ke saath kisi arth vyavastha main, equity nivesh par adhik lacheele dividand kii tulana main kisi vyapaar ke liye adhik manhage honge. kisi sangharshrat vyapaar ke liye equity nivesh ke madhyam se vitt pradaan karnaa adhik aasaan hota he kyonki kathin paristhiti main dividand kaa bhugtan rokna sambhav hota he.
 vaishvik rin kharid saal dar saal 4.3 % badi he jo 2004 ke dauraan $ 5.19 trilean tak pahunchi he. yah apeksha kii jaati he ki aane vaale varsho main yah or badhegi yadi vishwavyaapee lakhon logon main kharch karne kii aadat aisi hi bani rahi, jaisi aaj he.
