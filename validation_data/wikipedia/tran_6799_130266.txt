 bathinda punjab kaa ek bahut hi purana or mahatvapurn shahar he. yah malwa ilaake main sthit he. bathinda ke hi jangalon main kaha jaataa he ki guru govind singh ji ne chumkka naaman takaton ko lalakaara tha or un se lade the. bathinda kaa aazaadee kii ladai main bhi mahatvapurn yogadan tha. is main ek khaas kila he 'kila mubarak'.
 bathinda bahut teji se industries se bhar rahaa he. haal hi main bane palaaton main thermal power palant, fortliger faikatary or ek bady oyal rifinari hai. bathinda north bharat kii sabase bady anaaj ke bajaaron main se he or bathinda ke aas paas ke ilaake angoor kii kheti main batch rahe hai. bathinda ek bahut bada rail junkashan bhi he. papasi yahaan upjau anaj ko paroses karti he.
 ye mana jaataa he ki raao bhatti main bathinda shahar ko lakhi jangal main teesri sadi main sthapit kiya tha. phir is shahar ko baraaron nen hadap liya tha. bal raao bhatti nen phir ise 965 main hasil kiya or tab iskaa naam bathinda padaa . yah raja jaipal kii rajdhani bhi rahi he.
 1004 main ghazani ke mahmud nen iskaa kila chin liya. phir muhamad ghori nen hamalaa kiya or bathinda kaa kila chin liya. phir prithvi raaj chouhan nen 13 maheenon ke baad tagdi ladai ke pashchaat ise phir se jeeta.
 razia sultan, bharat kii pehali mahila shaasha bathinda main aprill 1240 main kaid kii gai thi. use altuniya kii koshishon dwaara augast main vahaan se chudaa liya gaya. razia or altuniya kii shaadi hui lekin ve kaithal ke paas choron dwaara 13 okatubar ko maare gayi.
 siddhu baraaron ko lodi ke shashan main bathinda se nikaal diya gaya tha lekin babur ke shashan main vaapis sthaan de diya gaya tha. kuch saalon baad, rup chand naam ke sikhkh punjab ke itihaas main aaye. rup chand ji ke ladke phool nen lungar kii pratha chalaai or 1654 ke aas paas ek kila banaaya.
 yah it kaa bana sabase purana or ooncha smarak he. iskaa itihaas thodaa adbhut he. raja binpal jo ki bhaati rajput the, is kile kaa nirmaan lagbhag 1800 saal pehle karavaaya tha. isi kile main pehali mahila shasika razia sultan ko 1239 isvin main kaid kar liya gaya tha. razia sultan ko uske garvanar altuniya ne kaid kiya tha. dasven sikh guru, guru govind singh is kile main 1705 ke june maah main aae the or is jagah kii salaamati or khushhaali ke liye prarthana kii thi.
 patiala rajya ke maharaja aalaa singh ne is kile ko 1754 main apni adheen kar liya tha. or is kile kaa naam govindaghar kar diya gaya. lekin jald hi is jagah ko bakramghar ke naam se bulaane jane laga. is kile ke sabase upar gurudware kaa nirmaan karavaaya gaya he. is gurudware kaa nirmaan patiala ke maharaja karam singh ne karavaaya tha.
 bahia kile kaa nirmaan 1930 main mukhya kile ke saamne kiya gaya tha. is kile kaa nirmaan es. balwant singh siddhu ne karavaaya tha. iske alaava yahaan patiala rajya ke maharaja bhoopendra singh kii sena kaa sthaneey karyaalay tha. lekin ab yah chaar sitara hotel main badal chukaa he.
 bathinda jhil paryatakon kii pasandida jagahon main se ek he jahaan paryatak boating or water scooter kaa mazaa lene aate hai. yahan ke kashmiri shikaras paryatakon ke mukhya aakarshan kaa centre he. is jhil kii hariyaali yahan par aane vaale sailaaneeyon kaa man moh leti he.
 bathinda main desh ke sabase achche shopping complex hai, jaise mittal mal, city centre mal, penninsula mal or city walk mal hai jahaan sthaneey logon ke alaava paryatak bhi ghoomte hai. in mal main ek hi chat ke neeche prasiddh brandon kii dukaanon ke saath food restaurant bhi hai.
 yah bhatinda se 15 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. is jangal main purana gurudwara he. is gurudware main guru nanak dev ji ne shri japuji sahib ke ek lakh pavitra margo kaa varnan kiya tha. iske baad se hi is jagah ko lakhi jangal ke naam se janaa jaataa he. sikhon ke dasven guru, guru govind singh bhi is par ghoomane ke liye aae the.
 is bageeche main gulabon kii kai kismein hai. yah jagah shahar se kaaphi najadik he. kaaphi sankhya main log yahaan par aate hai. yah bagichaa das acre tak failaa hua he. yah jagah thermal plant ke kaaphi kareeb he. yahaan gulabon kii kai kismein dekhi ja sakati he. yah jagah picnic spot ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he. 
 kendriya vidhyalay sankhya 4, bhatinda kaint 1985 main sthapit kiya gaya tha. yah or shiksha kaa madhyam ke rup main angreji or hindi ke saath ek sah - shiksha vidhyalay he or kendriya madhyamik shiksha board, nai dilli se sambhddha he. bhatinda kaint main bare main 7 km main choudhary maarg ke paas sthit he. bathinda ke railway stesan / bas stand se is vidhyalay ke 45 shikshakon kii ek samarpit team kii dekhbhaal ke antargat 850 chaatro or samarthan prashasnic karmchaariyon kii jaruraton ko puraa karata he.
 ju van vibhaag dwaara chalaai jane vaali paudhon kii nursery he. yah jagah cantonment se takreeban das kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. yah jagah picnic spot ke rup main bhi jaani jaati he. yahaan ek chota sa chidyaghar he.
 yah ek aitihaasik jagah he. is jagah kaa sambandh sikhon ke itihaas se judaa hua he. talwandi tehsil, damdama sahib ke naam se bhi prasiddh he. bhatinda ke dakshin disha se is sthaan kii duuri 18 kilometre he. yah prasiddh gurudwara paanch takhton main se ek he. har saal baisakhi ke avasar par yahaan bahut bade meley kaa aayojan kiya jaataa he. guru govind sahib yahaan par nau maheene or nau dinon tak rahe the.
 urja ke morche par vikat sthiti kaa saamana kar rahaa desh main rifinary kachche tel kii kul khapat main 80 pratishat aayaat hota he, aise main kachche tel kii oonchi keemton se aayaat kharch par baari dabaav he.
 mesar khaanaa mandir bhatinda se 29 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. har saal, yahaan par do meley lagate hai. is meley main jwaala ji ke darshan ke liye har saal lakhon kii sankhya main bhaktagan yahaan aate hai.
