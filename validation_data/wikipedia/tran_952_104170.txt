 july 23, 1983 ko srilanka main tamilon ke viruddh sinhalon dwaara kiye gaye dangon kaa naam 'kaali july' yaa 'blike july' he. isamen 400 se lekar 3000 kii maut kaa anumaan he. bahut se tamilon ko maut ke ghat utaar diya gaya. hajaaron gharon ko tabah kar diya gaya. is kaaran saikadon tamilon ne srilanka chhod diya or videshi sharan kii maang kii. altitii dwaara kiye gaye akraman main srilanka ke 43 sainikon ke maare jane ke baad ye dange shuru hue the.
 black july srilanka main tamil ugravadiyon evam srilanka sarkaar ke madhya grihayuddh kaa kaaran bana. srilanka ke tamil logon ke liye yah dukhad samaran kaa din ban gaya he.
 british sarkaar ne bahut srilankai tamilon ko mishanri dwaara sthapit shiksha suvidhaon evam 'baanto or raaj karo' ke pratyay kaa upyog karke alpsankhyak tamilon ko sarkaar main taratamyaheen shakti pradhan kiya. tamilon ko civil sevaaon evam anya padon ke liye chunaa gaya. jab shri lanka ko 1948 main swatantrata praapt hui, bahusankhyak sarkari pad tamilon ke haathon main the, jab ki unki jansankhya alp thi. nirvaachit netaaon ne sinhalon ko niyantran main rakhane kaa yah ek chaal samajha, tathaa is sthiti ko badalane ki koshish shuru ho gai. san 1956 ke keval singhaal niyam ke anusaar tamil evam angraji par pratibandh laga diya gaya. tamilon ke is pratyay ke virodh karne ke kaaran sinhal bhadak gayi tathaa 1958 main bhayanak dange shuru ho gayi. 1960 ke dashak, virodh or in par sarkari nirodh badhate bair kaa kaaran bana. 1971 main, maanakeekaran niyam ke kaaran shatruta batch gai. united neshnal party main 1977 ke chunavon main vijay ke kaaran do samudaay phir bhid gayi. 1981 main jafana ke prasiddh pustkaalay ko jala kar barabaad kar diya gaya. jafana ke prasiddh pustkaalay main tamil ugravadi sanghatan ke baithak hothen thea. 1983 tak babbti tamil ugravadiyon evam sarkaar ke bich muthbhed ke ghatnaayen hue or donon or logon ke laapata hone evam yatnaaen dene ke aarope lagaaye gayi.
 blike july ke ghatnaon kaa aarambh tab hua jab el.tii.tii.i sanghatan ke sadsy july 23 ke siankal jafana ke samip srilanka ke faujee kafile par akraman kiya. sinhala rakshakbal par yah naveenatam akraman tha. kafile ke pramukh ek jeep tha jiske neeche ek bam visphot ke kaaran do sainik ghayal hue. saathiyon ke madad karne ke kaaran jab sainik kafile ke gadiyon se utar rahe the, tamil tagaron ne ghaat lagaana shuru kar diya barood evam goliyon ke jariye. ladai main ek afsar evam 12 sainik turant maare gayi or do buree tarah se ghayal ho gayi, jis kaaran 15 maare gayi tigron ke alaava . kittu, ek tamil tigar ne is hamale ke sanchaalak hon swikar liya.
 ghaat kii samachar ko dabaane ke liye singhaala sarkaar ne 15 sainikon ke laashon ko colombo ke kanate shmashaan main chupchaap antim sanskaar karaayaa gaya,, taaki tamilon ke khilaaf hamalen na ho.. asal main maare gaye sainikon ke antim sanskaar gaanv main kiya jaaya karata tha. july 15 ke din jab antim sanskaar kiya ja rahaa tha, tab sinhalon kii ek dal vahaan pahunch kar faile gaye ghaat kii samachar par coup prakat kar rahe the dangen shuru ho gaye the or sinhalon ke dal nirdosh tamil nagarikon pe lut maar armbh kar diya tha.anderworld ke apradhi dal bhi shaamil hogaye. chunaav matadaata panjikaran soochiyon ke sahaayta se singhaala dalon ne keval tamil gharon evam dukaanon par var karnaa shuru kar diya, is bich rakshak baalo ko deree se tainaat kiya. isi kaaran dangon main shaamil dalon ko sarkari madad milane kaa anumaan lagaaya gaya .bhay biit tamil praja colombo sheher chhodane lage. bahut saare singhaala evam mussalman tamilon kii sahaayta karne aage bade.tamilon ne mandir, masjid, sarkari bhawanon or sinhal gharon main sharan liye.
 24 ke sandhyakaal par shri lanka kii sarkaar ne karfu kii ghoshnaa kiya, lekin rakshak baalo ne karfu laago karne se saafe inkaar kar rahe the yaa tho aksham paae gaye the TSrI lanka kii sena police kii sahaayta karne bulaayaa gaya. iske vipareet agle din hinsa jari thi.shri lanka ke anya kshetron main bhi hinsa armbh ho chukaa tha, adhiktam tamil bahusankhyak kshetra jaise candy, jahaan karfu siankal ke 6 par lagu tha, matle, navalapitiya, badulla nuvaraelia. vaahanon ko jalaayaa gaya or andar logon ko bahaar khinch kar maaraa yaa maar diya gaya..
 dangon main bhayanak sanghatan main se ek velikada jail narsanhaar main hua tha jo velikada ke ucch suraksha kargar july 25 ke din hua tha. 37 tamil sandigdh algaavvaadiyon ko singhaala jail bandhiyon ne lathi or chaaku kaa upyog karke maut ke ghat utar die. singhaala jail adhikariyon pe aarope hai ki chaabiyon de di gayi lekin aagaamee anveekshan main yah apramanith ghoshit kiya gaya . july 28 main dubaara dangon main 15 khaidi maare gaye
 unke bahusankhyak kshetron main tamilon ke viruddh hinsa ke kaaran july 26 tak karfu ko pure desh main ghoshit kiya gaya. lekin police evam sena tainaat hone ke kaaran 26 ke sandhyakaal tak hinsa kam hoti gayi or dangaiyon ke viruddh karrwai lene lage. jafana ke ghaatak hamale main maare gaye sainikon kaa antim sanskaar raat ke kurfu ke samay kiya gaya.. agle din colombo ke din ke samay kurfu ko uthaa liya gaya lekin tamil tiger ke hamalon ke dar se donon samudaayon ke bich mutbhed abhi jari thea..29 july ko dangon ke ek dal ne hinsa shuru kii lekin police ki firing main 15 dangayen maare gaye 24 ghanton kaa kurfu lagaaya gaya or rakshak baalo ne sheher ko phir se shantipurvak banane main safal rahe
 saanchaa:adhik jaankaree ke liesrilanka main tamilon or sinhalon ke bich tanaav in dangon ke purv upasthit tha or tamil ugravadi dalon kii sthapana ke baad sinhalon ke tamil-viruddh bhavanaaen bad gayi. haalanki yah sinhalai sainikon ke maare jane or gupt dafan kii khabar se swabhaavik rup se bhadak gaye the, baad main tamil-virdohi sanghon ke ikattha hokar or bhadakaayaa gaya. dangon ke shruwaat main police or fouj ne dangon ko rokhane kii koi koshish nahin kii thi. lekin july 26 tak police or fouj ne paristhitiyaan sanbhaalane kii koshishen shuru kar di or adhiktar hinsa rukh gayi. sarkaar ne nishedhagna ko lagu kar ke hinsa ko srilanka ke or jagahon tak badhane se roak di. july 29 main ek ghathane main police 15 sinhalai luteron ko goliyon se waar kar ke maar diya.
 haalaanki dangon ko naa rokane ke liye tamil raajnneetigyon ne sarkaar ko doshi tehraaya, sarkaar ke mutabik dangai dalon ko rokane ke liye bahut mahatvapurn kadam uthaaye gaye thea. dangon kii shruwaat ke baad turant karfu kii ghoshnaa kii gayi thi. sarkaar ke mutabik, dangai dal acchhee tarah se yojit thea or rail, imaaraten or basen pehle shikaar thea. us samay ke pradhanmantri ransinha premdasa ne tamilon aashray or bhojan kaa aayojan ke liye ek kamity kii sthapana kiya tha. yah aashray paanch skool bhawanon or ek hawaii adde main sthit tha. jab sharnaarthiyon kii sankhya 20000 se 50000 bad gayi, tab bharat sarkaar ne jahaajon dwaara madad bheji.
 dangai dalon ne pehle sarkari sampatti par waar kiya. bhoot or bhavishya ke kayi avasaron kii tarah borella kanatta ke kabristaan main ikkattha hue thea jahaan shri lanka ke sainikon ke antim sanskaar hone ko tha. ve sarkaar se apni naaraazgi jataa rahe thea. uske baad tamil jaati ke khilaaf badhak uth gaye.
 lut-maar or fasaad kaa aayojan acchhee tarah se kii gayi thi. colombo ke sadakon pe tamilon ke liye talash ho rahi thi. tamil nagarikon ko pahchaanane ke baad unhen, gadee samet, zindaa jala diya tha.
 dangai bheed ne bason ko bhi naa choda. tamilon ko baahar dhakel kar unhen chaaku, lathi or aag se waar kar ke maar diya. ek norway ke paryatak ne sinhalon ke ek dal ko tamil yatriyon ke ek minibas ko jalaate hue dekha tha.
 sinhalon ne shri lanka ke anya sheheron main, jaise anuradhapura, kandi, hattan, nuvaara elia, badulla, ginigathena, aadi, tamilon pe waar karnaa shuru kar diya.
 hataahaton kii sankhya ke anumaan bhinn hai . shri lanka kii sarkaar ke anusaar 250 tamil nagarikon kii hatya kii gayi, kintu gaiyr-sarkari sansthaaon ke anusaar 400 or 3000 ke bich kaa anumaan ho saktaa he, jiname shri lanka sthaneey tamil or bharat se based hue tamil bhi maujuud hai. akele velikade jail katleam main hi 53 aatankwaad ke sandigdhon kii maut hui.. ant main shri lanka sarkaar ne hataahaton kii sankhya 300 laga di.
 18,000 se zyada ghar or bahut saare vyapaaree pratishthan barbaad ho gaye or lakhon tamil europe, america, australia or canada ke liye desh chhod di. bahut saare tamil nau jawan el.tii.tii.i main barti ho gaye.
 raashtrapati ke or se ek mukadmaa jari kiya gaya tha or baad ke pipalas allins sarkaar ne hataahaton or barbaad hue gharon kii sankhya tay kar ke yah elaan kiya muaavja bharna chahie. lekin aaj tak yah bharpai kabhi di nahin gayi or kisi bhi tarah kaa mukadmaa shuru nahin hua.
 yah taarikh duniya ke sabhi tamil logon ke liye samaran kaa vishay ban gaya he. canada ke tamil congress ne 2009 ke 23 july ke din ikkattha hokar canada ke sarkaar ke sharanden kii or ahsaan vyakt kiya tha. is tarah ke ghatnaaye switzaraland, norge, denmark, germany, frans, britain, australia or new zealand main ghatit hui.
