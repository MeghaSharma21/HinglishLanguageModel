 agni-6 ek antaramahadwipiya belistic missile he. jise bhartiya sashastr balon ke istemaal ke liye raksha anusandhan evam vikaas sanghathan dwaara viksit kii ja rahi he.
 agni 6 ek chaar-charan vaali antaramahadwipiya belistic missile he. isak dizin charan ke puraa hone ke baad ab yah hardware ke vikaas ke charan main he. agni-6 main kai swatantra rup se lakshith kiye jane vaale punah pravesh vaahan or saath hi manuverable punah pravesh vaahan kii bhi sambhaavna he. or ye manuverable ranneetik hathiyaar agni 6 ko vistaarit seemaa denge jo vartmaan main gupt he. yah apane purvavarti agni 5 se lambi hogi or 2017 tak is missile kii udaan kii ummid he. bharat sarkaar ne abhi tak pariyojna ko manjuuri nahin di he. haalanki diardio ne sabhi gananaa puri kar lee he or ejineering kaa kaam shuru kar diya he.
 yah agni mixilon main naveenatam or sabase unnat sanskaran hoga. sutron ke mutabik, agni 6 missile main 10 emiarvi hathiyaar le jane kii kshamta ho sakati he. or 8,000 kimi se lekar 12,000 kimi kii ho sakati he. haalanki diardio ne missile kii seemaa kii pushti karne se inkaar kar diya he. diardio ke ek varisht vaigyanik ne yah kehete hue uddhrut kiya ki nai peedi agni-6 missile behad sahaj or aasaani se parivahan yogya hogi or aasaani se tainaat kii ja sakegi. isamen pandubbi or bhoomi aadhaarit laancharon se laanch hone kii kshamta hogi.
 2009 tak, yah bataaya gaya tha ki bharat sarkaar 10,000 kimi yaa isse adhik seemaa vaali antaramahadwipiya belistic missile ke vikaas par vichaar nahin kar rahi he. ek lambii duuri antaramahadwipiya belistic missile ke liye ek kaarykram kii atakaley 2011 main phir shuru ho gai. kuch reporton main daawa kiya gaya he ki antaramahadwipiya belistic missile kaa naam pehle se hi "surya" or naam code agni-6 he.
 anya reporton se bataaya diya gaya ki nai dilli antaramahadwipiya belistic kii aavashyaktaa ke liye gambhir nahin he. bharat sarkaar se anumati ke baad hi diardio bharat ke antaramahadwipiya belistic missile ko viksit karne ke liye ek pariyojna shuru kar saktaa he. chunki bharat 27 june 2016 tak missile praudyogiki niyantran vyavastha kaa hastaksharkarta nahin tha. to bhartiya missile kaarykram antaramahadwipiya belistic missile kshamta ke vikaas ke liye kisi bhi sandhi patibaddhta dwaara simit nahin tha. kuch media reporton ne kabhi-kabhi bataaya tha ki bharat emticiar ke liye ek gaiyr-hastaksharkarta hone ke kaaran 5000 kilometre kii seemaa se pare mixilon ke vikaas par ek svaichhik adhisthgan he. missile praudyogiki niyantran vyavastha 5,000 kimi se pare mixilon ke vikaas se bharat ko nahin roktaa. emticiar ek alag rajya ke liye missile praudyogiki ke hastantaran ko roktaa he jisase missile praudyogiki prasar naa bade. yah sandhi praudyogiki ke hastantaran yaa ghatak ke sambandh main emticiar hastaksharkartaaon or gaiyr-emticiar hastaksharkartaaon ke bich antar nahin karata he. missile praudyogiki niyantran vyavastha sandhi kaa hastaksharkarta desh kisi dusre desh ko missile praudyogiki nahin de saktaa he. haalanki, ek kanoonee tark he ki kisi missile praudyogiki niyantran vyavastha sandhi kaa hastaksharkarta desh dusre hastaksharkarta desh ko missile praudyogiki sthanantarit karne se ise prasar nahin mana jaataa he.
 june 2011 main, pehali baar bhartiya vayusena pramukh marshall pradeep vasant nike ne tatkaal pados se pare bharat kii parmaanu kshamta ko badhaane ke paksh main tark diya. nik, jo chif of staff cameti kaa bhi pramukh the. unhone kaha ki "bharat ko 10,000 kimi yaa usase adhik kii simaaon ke liye ek isibiem kaarykram shuru karnaa chahie. kshetreey sandarbh se baahar todna mahatvapurn he kyonki desh ke prabhaav kaa kshetra badhata he. kisi bhi desh par hamaare paas koi kshetreey dizin nahin he. lekin bharat ko apane kshetra apana prabhaav kshamta kii aavashyaktaa he."
 oktober 2011 main, da pyniyar dwaara prakashit ek report ne isibiem ke liye "sadhak praudyogiki" ko swatantra rup se viksit karne kii diardio kii kshamta par andeh vyakt kiya, sadhak praudyogiki se hi missile ko 10,000 kilometre se adhik duuri tak pahucha sakate hai. isi report ne bharat ko "sadhak praudyogiki" main sahaayat pradaan karne kii rus kii ichha par jor diya. is report kii pramanikta kam se kam ek videshi akhbaar dwaara vivaadit he, jisamen counter-daawey kiye gayi ki rus kii bhagidaari sambhawatah nahin he, kyonki yadi roosi bhagidaari ke bare main report sach he, to rus ne missile praudyogiki niyantran vyavastha kaa ulanghan kiya he. jo rus itani aasaani se nahin kar saktaa he. vah missile praudyogiki niyantran vyavastha kaa ek sadsy desh he. andeh ke jawaab main, ek sheersh diardio vaigyanik ne kaha ki bharat main isibiem viksit karne ke liye aavashyak sabhi upkaran or takanic hai.
 20 june 2011 ko, bhartiya raksha samachar ne10,000 kimi isibiem ke bare main bharat gambhir naamak ek lekh prakashit kiya jisamen kaha gaya he ki bharat apni ranneetik mixilon kii pahunch ko badhaane ke liye gambhirtha se vichaar kar rahaa he or raksha mantralay raksha mahasang ke prastaav par vichaar kar rahaa he ki intercantinental belistic missile 10,000 kilometre dur lakshya ko maarane main sakasham honi chahie. ek isibiem kaa nirmaan antarrashtriya str par asar kar saktaa he isliye prastaav kaa antim nirnay suraksha par cabinet samiti dwaara liya jayega.
 april 2012 main, agni 5 ke safal prakshepan ke baad, saraswat ne khulaasa kiya ki bharat ke paas agni kaarykram ko band karne kii koi yojana nahin he or agni missile kii shrinkhalaa main or adhik mixilo kaa nirmaan ho saktaa hai.
 may 2012 main, reporton ne agni shrinkhalaa main ek or isibiem agni 6 missile ke vikaas kii pushti kii. yah kathith tha ki missile 2014 main viksit kii jaaegi or yah 8,000 kimi se lekar 10,000 kilometre tak se bhi lambii range kii ko sakati he. agni 6 agni-5 kii tulana main aakarshak hogi or kam se kam 10 parmaanu hathiyaar le jane main sakasham, ek hi samay main kai lakshya ko lakshith karne main sakasham hogi. janvari 2013 main, diardio ke pramukh ve ke saraswavat ne kaha ki agni 5 ke vikaas ke baad, diardio agni 6 kaa vikaas karegi, jisamen kai swatantra lakshyikaran yogya pun: pravesh vaahan kshamta hogi. unhone kaha ki missile kaa dizin puraa ho chukaa he or ab diardio hardware charan par kaary kar rahi he. agni 6, vikaas ke tahat nai bahu-lakshith missile, 'bal gunak' ke rup main kaary karegi, raksha anusandhan evam vikaas sanghathan ke pramukh ve.ke. saraswati ne 9 farvari 2013 ko kaha tha.
 missile kaa pandubbi launch belistic missile sanskaran bhartiya nausena ke arihant varg kii pandubbiyon main upyog hoga. diardio ne 2012 main khulaasa kiya ki vah agni-6 missile ke ek anya sanskaran ko viksit karne kii prakriya main bhi he. yah 6,000 kilometre kii adhiktam seemaa or teen ton ke pelod le jane vaali ek pandubbi-launch thos eendhan missile hogi.
