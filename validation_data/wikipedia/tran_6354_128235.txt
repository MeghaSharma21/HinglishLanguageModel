 tad kaa tel, nariyal tel or tad kii gari kaa tel tad ke pedo ke fal se nikaale gayi khaane yogya vanaspati tel hai. tad kaa tel aayal palm aleis guininsis ke fal kii lugdi se nikaala jaataa he; tad kii gari kaa tel aayal palm ke fal kii gari se nikaala jaataa he or nariyal kaa tel nariyal kii gari se praapt kiya jaataa he. tad ke tel kaa rang swaabhaavik rup se laal hota he, kyonki isamen beta caroten kaa ek ucch pariman shaamil rahata he.
 tad kaa tel, tad kii gari kaa tel or nariyal tel kuch atyadhik santrupt vanaspati vasaaon main se teen hai. tad kaa tel kamare ke taapmaana par ardh thos rahata he. tad ke tel main kai santrupt or asantrupt vasaaen glissril lurat, miristate, pamitate, stearet, olet linoliet ke rup main shaamil hai. tad kii gari kaa tel or nariyal tel, tad ke tel se adhik ucch santrupt tel hai. sabhi vanaspati telon kii tarah tad ke tel main cholestrol nahin hota, haalanki santrupt vasa ke seven se ldl or echdiel cholestrol donon bad jate hai.
 tad kaa tel africa, dakshinpurv asia or brazil ke kuch bhaagon kii ushnkaribandheey pattee main khaanaa pakaane kaa ek aam upadan he. isaki kam laagat or talane main upyog ke samay parishkrut utpaada kii ucch jarnkari sthirataa duniya ke anya bhaagon main vyavsaayik bhojan udyog main iske badhate upyog ko protsaahit kar rahi he.

 1990 ke dashak ke madhya ke baad se, tad ke laal tel ko khaanaa pakaane ke tel ke rup main upyog ke liye thanda-dabaaya or bottleband kiya gaya he tathaa mayonez or salad ke tel main bhi mishrit kiya gaya he. vishisht swasthya upyog ke khaadya padaarthon or budhaape ko rokanevaali prashaadhan samagriyon main bhi tad ke laal tel ke tocotreynolon or carotenon jaise oxicrnarodhakon kaa prayog ho rahaa he.

 2009 ke ek adhyayan main vaigyanikon ne spain main tad ke laal tel, jaitoon or bahaasantrupt telon main aalu ko gaharaa talane ke samay acrolin utsarjan kii dar kaa pareekshan kiya. unhone bahaasantrupt telon main acrolin utsarjan kii dar ko ooncha paaya. vaigyanikon ne tad ke laal tel ko "ekal asantrupt" ke rup main varnit kiya.yah tale hue francisi aalu ko ek aakarshak rang detaa he.
 palm tel utpaada pisai or parishkrut karne kii prakriyaaon ke dwaara banaae jate hai: thos or taral bhaagon ko praapt karne ke liye pehle vibhaajan ke saath ravaakaran or pruthakkaran kii prakriya kaa upyog kiya jaataa he.iske baad pighalaane or gond hataane se ashuddhiyaan dur ho jaati hai. tab tel ko filter or prakshalit kiya jaataa he. aglaa bhautik shodhan, gandh or rang ko hataata he or ise vasa amlon se mukt kar parishkrut, prakshalit, nirgandhikrut tad ke tel yaa arabidipio utpaadit karata he, jo sabun, kapade dhone kaa powder tathaa vyaktigat dekhbhaal ke utpaadon ko banane main ek mahatvapurn kachche maal ke taur par istemaal kiya jaataa he. arabidipio vishwa ke panya bazaaron main biknevaala buniyadi tel utpaada he, haalanki kai companiyaan ise aage khaanaa banane ke tel yaa anya utpaadon ke liye palm olin main vibhajit karti hai.
 jaleey vishleshan dwaara yaa sabunikaran kii buniyadi sharton ke tahat tel or vasa ke vibhaajan se glisreen ke saath vasa amlon kaa ek gaun-utpaada praapt hota he. vibhajit vasa amla C4 se lekar C18 tak kaa ek mishran hote hai, jo tel/vasa ke prakaar par nirbhar karata he.

 aaiie ne bhavishyavaanee kii he ki asian deshon main jaev-eendhan kaa prayog maamuli rahegaa. lekin tad ke tel kii ek pramukh utpaadak, malaysian sarkaar jaev-eendhan tel ke feedstock ke utpaadan ko protsaahit kar rahi he or tad ke tel ke jaev-dijal sanyantron kaa nirmaan kar rahi he. gharelu taur par, malaysia 2008 tak dijal se jaev- eendhan main badalane ke liye taiyaari kar rahaa he, jisamen is badlaav ko anivaary banane ke liye kanoon kaa masauda taiyaar karnaa bhi shaamil he.

 khaadya banaam eendhan bahes badhane ke saath-saath, anusandhan kachare se baio dijal utpaadan kii ore mud rahaa he. ek anumaan ke anusaar malaysia main prati varsh talane ke telon ke rup main prayukt 50,000 ton vanaspati tel or pashu vasa donon kaa, bina kachare kaa upachaar kiye niptaan kar diya jaataa he. 2006 ke ek adhyayan main shodhakartaaon ne talane main prayukt tel main purv-upachaar ke baad silica jail paaya, jo sodium hydroxide ke prayog se utprerak pratikriya dwaara mithail aster main roopantaran ke liye ek upyukt feedstock he. utpaadit mithail aster main aise eendhan gun hai, jinaki tulana petroleum dijal se kii ja sakati he or asanshodhit dijal injanon main iskaa istemaal kiya ja saktaa he.
 malaysian vigyaan vishvavidhyalay main vaigyanikon dwaara 2009 ke ek adhyayan kaa nishkarsh he ki tad kaa tel anya vanaspati telon kii tulana main khaadya tel kaa ek swasth strot he or saath hi itani maatra main uplabdh he ki jaev-dijal kii vaishvik maang ko puraa kar sakane main samarth he. tad ke vriksharopan or tad ke tel kii khapat ne khaadya banaam eendhan bahes main gatirodh utpann kar diya he, kyonki isamen ek saath donon maangon ko puraa karne kii kshamta he. ek british vaigyanik ne anumaan lagaaya he ki 2050 tak, khaadya telon ke liye vaishvik maang shaayad 240 lakh ton ke aasapaas hogi, jo lagbhag 2008 kii khapat ke doguni hogi. atirikt tel kaa zyaadaatar bhaag tad kaa tel hoga, jisaki utpaadan laagat pramukh telon main sabase kam he, lekin sambhawatah soyabean tel ke utpaadan main bhi vruddhi hogi. agar ausat paidaavaar main vruddhi pichley kram se hi jari rahi to ek atirikt tad ke pedo, kii aavashyaktaa ho sakati he. jangal kii keemat par aisa karne kii jarurat nahin he, maanavkrut charaagaah 2050 main khaadya uddeshyon ke liye aavashyak samast tel kii aapurti kar sakate hai.

 sanyukt rajya america ke janhit main vigyaan centre ne kaha he ki tad kaa tel, jo santruptata main ucch or asantruptata main kam he, dil kii beemaari ko badhaava detaa he. siespiai report ne 1970 ke anusandhan or ko metastadies ko uddhrut kiya he. siespiai ne yah bhi kaha ki rashtriya hriday, fefde or rakt santhaan, vishwa swasthya sanghathan or anya swasthya adhikariyon ne tad ke tel kii khapat ko kam kar dene kaa aagrah kiya he. hu kahata he ki pamitic amla kaa seven hradvahini rogon ke vikaas main yogadan karne ke liye ek jokhim he. costa rika main 2005 ke anusandhan ne tad ke gaiyr hydrogenikrut asantrupt tel ke seven kaa suzav diya he.1993 main, malaysia ke chikitsa anusandhan santhaan ke hradvahini rog ikai hradvahini, madhumeh or poshan centre ke vibhaagadhyaksh dotoni enji cock vaai ne dikhaaya ki santrupt vasa kii pitt-sandrav kaa prabhaav isaki 2-es.en.sthiti ke pariman dwaara prabhavit hota he. tad ke tel main ucch pamitic amla hone ke baavjood, es.en. 2 sthiti main iskaa keval 13-14% hi vidyaman rahata he.
 hu kii 2002 kii masauda report par pratikriya karte hue vistar santhaan, philadelphia ke dow david kritchevsaki ne ek email main is baat se inkaar kiya he ki us samay, vahaan tad ke tel ke seven ko atherosclerosis kaa kaaran dikhaane vaala koi aankada maujuud tha.
 haalanki, rashtriya swasthya santhaan or usd krishi anusandhan seva dwaara samarthit 2006 ke ek adhyayan ne nishkarsh nikaala he ki aanshik rup se hydrogenit vasa yukt hone kii vajah se tad kaa tel khaadya udyog main ek surakshit vikalp nahin he, kyonki tad kaa tel ldl cholestrol or apollipoprotein bi kii rakt sandratha main vaisaa hi pratikul parivartan karata he jaisaa trans vasa se hota he.
 sabhi santrupt vasaaen samaan rup se pitt-sandravik nahin hai. adhyayanon se sanket mila he ki nariyal ke tel, deyari evam pashu vasa jaise santrupt vasaaon ke sroton kii tulana main palm olen kaa seven rakt cholestrol ko kam karata he.
 1996 main, masachusets vishvavidhyalay ke dow baker ne jor dekar kaha ki tryasilglisrols kii es.en.-1 or-3 sthiti main santrupt vasa apni dhimi avasorakata kii vajah se alag-alag upapachayee namoonay darshaati he. santrupt vasa yukt aahaar vasaaon, mukhya rup se es.en.-1 or -3 sthitiyon vaali main un vasaaon se bahut bhinn jaivik parinaam hote hai, jinamein santrupt vasa es.en.-2 kii sthiti main hoti he (jaise, dugdh vasa or charbi. vasa poshan adhyayanon or vishisht khaadya utpaadon ke utpaadan main stereovishisht vasa amlon ke sthanon kaa antar bhi ek mahatvapurn vichaar bindu hona chahie. 
 varsh 2004 kii ek samiksha main kramshah california vishvavidhyalay, davis or switzraland ke nestle research centre ke dow german or dow dilard ne vishisht santrupt vasaaon ke parihrid dhamni rog main yogadan or swasthya par anya prabhaav main prayek vishisht santrupt vasa amla kii bhoomika se sambandhit anusandhan main yah nishkarsh nikaala ki inase praapt parinaam aise nahin hai, jinke aadhaar par har vyakti ke aahaar se santrupt vasa ko dur karne kii vaishvik sifarish kii ja sake, kyonki paryaapt avadhi ke kam vasa vaale aahaar yaa kam santrupt vasa vaale aahaar par koi bhi aniyamit niyantrit pareekshan nahin kiya gaya he. is jaankaree kaa abhaav he ki kam santrupt vasa kaa seven hanikaarak swasthya parinaam ke jokhim ke bina kaise kiya ja saktaa he. bhavishya ke adhyayanon main santrupt vasa amlon ke seven ke alag-alag prabhaav ke sandarbh main vibhinn prakaar ki jivan shaileyon or vyaktigat anuvanshik prishthbhumi ko dhyaan main rakhana hoga.
