 khoja::khoja gotra ke jat mukhyat: rajasthan main paae jate he.rajasthan ke madhya bhaag main khoja adhik jansankhya main paae jate he.khoja nagaur main kherwad, guda bhagavaandas, aadi main mukhyat: paae jate he.jaise kherwad main nagaur ke mukhya gharaane ke khoja gotra ke jat paae jate he. mukhya taur par yahaan dharmaraam chodhri F/O harkaram choudhary aadi khoja gotra ke jat mukhya hai.
 anya:yahaan par ham kuch itihaasakaron ke khoja gotra ke jaaton kaa anya rajasthan main varnan bataa rahe hai.kyonki nagaur kaa ham pehle bataa chuke hai.
 is gotra ke jat marwar, ajmer merwada or jhuujhavati main paye jate hai. yah naam kis kaaran padaa, yah to maaloom nahin ho sakaa, kintu gyarahavin shatabdi main inkaa rajya tonk main tha yah pataa lag gaya he. ‘tarikhar rajgan hind’ ke lekhak maulavee hakim najmulaganikhan ne tonk rajya ke varnan main likhaa he - "shahar tonk lambaai main uttar 26 akshansh 10 deshaantar or chaudaai main pachhim 45 akshansh 57 deshaantar par dehali se mau jane vaali sadak se chipta hua he, dehali se dakshin pachhim main 218 mile mau se uttar main 289 mile faasale par banaas nadi ke kinare par avasthit he. yahaan yah nadi praayah do feet pani kii gaharaai se bahati he. shahar ke chaaron ore diwaar he or usamen kaccha kila he. ek itihaas main likhaa he ki khoja ramsingh ne kisi yuddh ke baad dehali se aakar sanwat 1300 vikrami miti magh sudi teras ko is sthaan par nagar aabaad kiya. us nagar kaa naam tonkara rakhaa tha. yah aabaadi ab tak kot ke naam se mashhoor he. arse ke baad maah sudi panchami sanwat 1337 ko allaudin khilji ne madhaupur or chittod fatah kiye, tab is gaanv kii dubaara aabaadi hui. ‘vakya raajapootaana’ main isi bhaanti likhaa hua he. kintu isamen shankaa yah he ki ‘silsilaa talumulk’ ke lekhaanusaar allaudin khilji san 1295 i. main shaasha hua or san 1316 i. main mar gaya. is hisaab main uskaa shashan-kaal sanwat 1352 se 1372 ke bich main yaa isse ekaadh saal aage-peeche qaraar paata he. san 1806 i. main tonk amirkhaan ke kabje main aayaa. usne shahar se ek mile dakshin main apane nivas ke liye raaj-bhawan or daftar banaaye. isse maaloom hota he ki raja ramsingh ke vanshajon ne tonk par san 1003 se san 1337 athwa 1352 tak raaj kiya. khilji athwa anya kisi bhi musalmaan sardaar ne unka garh tahas-nahas kar diya. tab phir se dubaara basaaya gaya.
