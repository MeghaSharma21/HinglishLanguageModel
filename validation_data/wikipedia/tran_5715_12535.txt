 rankoji mandir, an: Renkōji Temple) japan ke tokio main sthit ek buddha mandir he. 1594 main sthapit yah mandir buddha sthapaty kala kaa darshneey sthal he. ek manyata ke anusaar bhartiya swatantrata sangram ke agrim senaanee subhash chandra bose kii asthiyaan yahan aaj bhi surakshit rakhi hui hai.darasal 18 sitambar 1945 ko unki asthiyaan is mandir main rakhi gayin thim. parantu praapt dastavezon ke anusaar netaji kii mrityu ek maah purv 18 agast 1945 ko hi taihoku ke sainik aspataal main raatri 21.00 baje ho gayi thi. japan ke log yahan prati varsh 18 agast ko netaji subhaashachandr bose kaa balidaan diwas manaate hai.

 germany main apane pati professor phalf ke saath rah rahi subhaashachandr bose kii ekmaatr putree anita bose phalf ke anusaar unke pita kii mrityu taipenh main hi hui thi or rankoji mandir main rakhi asthiyaan unke pita kii hi hai. justise mukherjee report par vidwaanon kaa yah kahana he ki subhash kii niti dushman ke dushman ko dost banane kii thi. iske liye ve bhootpurv british khufia afasar ke us kathan kaa hawala dete hai jisamen usne sweekaar kiya tha ki yadi use subhash jinda haath lag jaataa to vah use maut kii sajaa dilaa kar hi chaiyn letaa. aisi haalat main sambhav he ki unki mrityu vimaan durghatana main hi hui ho. duusari ore bharat main kuch logon kaa aisa maananaa he ki faijabad main subhaashachandr bose vesh badalakar gumnami baba ke rup main rahe or vahiin unki mrityu hui. san 1945 ke baad subhaashachandr bose ke rus main hone kii khabar par bhi prashnchinh lagaate hue bharat sarkaar se un sabhi gopneey filon ko saarvajanik karne kii maang kii gayi jo joseph stallin kii putree swetlana ne grih mantralay ko saunpi thim.

 hindu dharm main koi vyakti jab marataa he to dah sanskaar karne ke baad uski asthiyon ko kisi pavitra nadi yaa ganga sagar main visarjeet karne kii parampara he. iske saath yah manyata bhi he ki yadi koi hindu kisi aise sthaan par mare jahaan aas paas koi bhartiya nadi na ho to uski asthiyon ko kisi potli main baandhakar yaa to pipal ke vriksh kii kisi shakha par lataka diya jaataa he yaa phir paas ke hi kisi mandir main rakh diya jaataa he. uske baad uske parivaar ke log un asthiyon ko apni suvidhanusaar ganga yaa anya kisi pavitra nadi main pravaahit kar dete hai. parantu netaji ke asthi-kalash ke saath aisa bhi nahin hua. unki mrityu vimaan durghatana main hui athwa nahin hui is par vivaad paidaa kar diya gaya. bharat san 1947 main azaad ho gaya. tab se lekar aaj tak is vivaad kii jaanch ke liye teen-teen ayog baitaaye gayi. pehle shahanwaaz ayog, phir khosla ayog or sabase ant main mukherjee ayog ne isaki jaanch kii parantu parinaam kuch nahin niklaa. 8 navambar 2005 ko justise mukherjee ayog ne apni report sarkaar ko saunpi. 17 may 2006 ko sansad main is par khub bahes hui or bataaya gaya ki netaji kii maut vimaan durghatana main nahin hui. saath hi yah bhi kaha gaya ki rankoji mandir main rakhi jin asthiyon kaa sambandh subhaashachandr bose se batalaaya jaataa he ve unki nahin hai. baad main bharat sarkaar ne mukherjee ayog kii vah report khaarij kar di.
