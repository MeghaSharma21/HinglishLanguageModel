 vartmaan main bhartiya cricket team janvari or farvari 2018 main dakshin africa kaa daura kar rahi he jisamen teen test, chah ek divasiy antarrashtriya or teen twanti-20 antarrashtriya maichon kaa aayojan kiya gaya he. janvari, 2017 main, cricket dakshin africa ne bataaya ki yah daura laagat ke kaaran srilanka kii anusuchit yatra kii jagah legaa or samaybaddh nirdhaaran bheed.
 agast 2017 main, yah ghoshnaa kii gai ki kingsmed main paaramparik boxing de test nahin hoga, kyonki srilanka ke khilaaf bharat kii gharelu shrinkhalaa 24 disambar 2017 tak samaapt nahin hogi. agle maheene yah ghoshnaa kii gai ki yatra 5 janvari 2018 ko shuru hone kii sambhaavna he. siese ne sitambar 2017 main kaarykram kii pushti kii. test maichon se pehle, bharat bolend park, paral main do divasiy warm-ap match main khelenge. haalanki, yah radd kar diya gaya tha, kyonki bharat ne match ke bajaay prashikshan satr karne kaa vikalp chunaa tha.
