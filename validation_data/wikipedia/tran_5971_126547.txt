 jain mat main raja ram ke liye 'padam' shabd kaa prayog hota he, isliye swayambhu kii ramayana ko 'padam charit' kaha gaya. isaki rachana chah varsh teen maas gyaarah din main puri hui. mularoop se is ramayana main kul 92 sarg the, jinamein swayambhu ke putr tribhuvan ne apni ore se 16 sarg or jode. goswami tulsidas ke 'ramcharita maanas' par mahakavi swayambhu rachit 'paum chariu' kaa prabhaav spasht dikhalaai padataa he.
