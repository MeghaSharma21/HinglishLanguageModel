 chakamak bacchon par kendrit hindi kii ek bal patrika he. he to yah bacchon ke liye, lekin bado ko bhi pasand aati he. 
 kai varsh tak 'hoshangabad vigyaan shikshan kaarykram chalaakar deshbhar main vigyaan shikshan va navachaar ke kshetra main prasiddh hui sanstha 'eklavya' masik 'chakamak ' patrika prakashit kar rahi he. ab tak 'chakamak ' ke 343 ank prakashit ho chuke hai. kehane ko to yah aathvin kaksha tak ke bacchon kii patrika he par isaki rochakata ke kaaran bade bhi ise chaav se padhate hai. isamen kahaniyaan, kavitaayen, lekh, chitra paheli, bacchon kii rachanaayen khaas andaaj main hoti hai. 
 bal sahitya ke naam par ab tak ham upadeshaatmak, raja-raniyon or jaanwaron kii kahaniyaan padhane ke abhyast ho chuke hai. 'chakamak ' bal sahitya kaa yah myth todatii he. bacchon kaa jivan bhi, jivan kaa mahatvapurn bhaag he, isamen sukh-dukh, chal-kapat, yojanaayen or samaj kaa talmail he. bal avasthaa main bhaawanaaon kaa jatil rup paaya jaataa he. ek baat jo bachche ke jivan ko bado se alag karti he vah he uskaa samaaj, parivesh va prakruti ke prati vaigyanik drushtikon jo har bachche main hota he. isiilie to baccha chizon, naitik upadeshon, ghatnaon ko ult-palat kar dekhtaa he or kisi bhi baat ko vaise param satya kii tarah sweekaar nahin karata jaise bade aamtaur par kar lete hai. 'chakamak ' main aisi hi saamagree hoti he jo bal man kii chanchalta ko pakadaney kaa prayaas karti he. 
 geetkaar gulzaar jaise bade naam 'chakamak 'ke saath jude hue hai.
