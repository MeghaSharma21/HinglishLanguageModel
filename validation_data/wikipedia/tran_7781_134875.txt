 blostium ek chota kila va louh dhaatukarm kaa centre tha jo roman praant britania superior main sthit tha. britania superior roman britain kaa hissaa tha. nivartmaan samay main isaki sthiti dakshin-purvi wales ke ek paramparaagat county nagar monmouth main he. ek sthaneey bank main lagi pattica kile ke yahan hone kii jaankaree va uski sthiti kaa vivaran detii he.
 aisa suzav diya jaataa he ki 'blostium' naam greek bhashaa ke βλαστος se utpatti hua he, jisak arth hota he 'shakha'. aisa ab samaanya taur par sweekaar kiya jaataa he ki nero yaa purv-flavian rajvansh kaa sainya kila monmouth main banaaya gaya tha, shaayad 55 i se pehle, jo wales main ise sabase pehla roman kila banaata he. aisa mana jaataa he ki ek samay blostium main lagbhag 2,000 sainik rahaa karte the, parantu baad main yah bas ek chota kila hi ban ke rah gaya.
