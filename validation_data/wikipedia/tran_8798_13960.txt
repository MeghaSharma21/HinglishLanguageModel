 shrimadbhagavadgita, brahmasutra tathaa upanishdon ko milakar prasthaantrayee kaha jaataa he.
 pracheen kaal main bharatvarsh main jab koi guru athwa acharya apane mat kaa pratipaadan evam uski pratishtha karnaa chahta tha to uske liye sarvapratham vah in teenon par bhashya likhata tha. aadi shankaracharya, ramanuj aadi bade-bade guruon ne aisa kar ke hi apane mat kaa pratipaadan kiya.
