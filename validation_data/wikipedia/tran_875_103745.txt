 jibraltar rashtriya footbal team british pravasi shasit pradesh jibraltar kaa footbal kii pratispardhaao main pratinidhitiv karti he. jibraltar footbal sangh team va is se jude sabhi kaaryo ko niyantrit karata he. yah fifa yaa yuiefee kaa purna sadsy nahin he isliye team vishwa cap yaa europian championships main bhaag lene ke liye yogya nahin he. jibraltar ne yuiefee kii sadsyta ke liye aavedan kiya tha parantu ise 2007 main yuiefee board ne thukraa diya. yah vivaad court of arbritessan for saport tak chalaa gaya tha jiske pashchaat yuiefee kii kaaryakarini samiti ne jibraltar ko asthaayee sadsy kaa darja pradaan kar diya.
 jibralterian rashtriya footbal kaa itihaas shaayad tab se shuru hota he jab rashtriya team april 1923 main spain ke daure par sevilla footbal club ke saath dostana match khilne gai thi. team ne vahaan do match khele jinamein ise haar kaa saamana karnaa padaa. team ne 1949 main vishwa prasiddh spani footbal club real madrid ke khilaaf khele gaye match main club ko barabari par hi roak diya tha.
 team ne apana pehla intaraashtriya match 1993 main dweep khelon ke dauraan khelaa. jibraltar apane sabhi match haar gaya or puure tournament main team ne keval ek hi gol kiya or team antim sthaan par rahi.
 1995 main hue dweep khelon main team ne acha pradarshan kiya. in khelon kaa mejabaan jibraltar hi tha. apane pehle match ko greenland ke viruddh khone ke pashchaat bhi jibraltar ne tournament main vaapasi kari or itihaas main apni pehali pratispardhaatmak jeet oil of main ke viruddh darj kii. angelse ke viruddh apni duusari jeet ke pashchaat jibraltar ne apane samooh main doosraa sthaan sunishchit kar liya; angelse ne bhi do match hi jite the parantu gol antar ke kaaran jibraltar semifinal ke liye arhata praapt karne main safal rahaa. semifinal main team ne jersey ko 1–0 se haraaya or tournament ke final main jagah banaai. final main team kaa muqabla oil of white se hua jahaan use semifinal ke scor se hi haar kaa saamana karnaa padaa or team ko khelon kaa rajat padak mila.
 1997 ke dweep khelon ke sanskaran main team ne samooh str ke do match jite or do match haare or use shethland ke viruddh plaof khelnaa padaa jisamen use haar kaa saamana karnaa padaa or jibraltar nau deshon kii is pratispardhaa main chathe sthaan par rahaa. 1999 ke inhi khelon main team ne ore adhik bura pradarshan kiya or gyarahawein sthaan se santosh karnaa padaa.
 2001 main parinaamon main kuch sudhaar aae or team paanchave sthaan par rahi or 2003 main team ne apni itihaas kii sabase badi jeet darj kari jab usne saarc ko 19–0 se haraaya. aage badhate hue team ne greenland or orchni ko haraaya or antat baarah deshon ke bich chatha sthaan praapt kara. in choti safaltaaon ke pashchaat bhi jibraltar ne 2005 ke tournament main hissaa nahin liya.
 2007 main jibraltar ne pehali baar dweep khelon kaa swarna padak jeeta jab usne final main rods kii team ko 4–0 se haraaya.
 nivartmaan samay main jibralterian rashtriya team kii kit german khel company adidas taiyaar karti he. yah puri safed he jisamen kinaaron kii taraf laal rang kii rekhaayen hai jo jibralterian rashtriya dhvaj kaa pratinidhitiv karti hai.
