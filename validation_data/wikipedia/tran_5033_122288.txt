 rashtrakut dakshin bharat, madhya bharat or uttari bharat ke bade bhubhag par rajya karne vaala rajvansh tha. 
 inkaa shasankaal lagbhag chhathi se terahavin shatabdi ke madhya tha. is kaal main unhone paraspar ghanishtha parantu swatantra jatiyon ke rup main rajya kiya, unke gyaat pracheenatam shilalekhon main saatvin shatabdi kaa 'rashtrakut' tamrapatr mukya he, jisame ullikhit he kii, 'malwa praant' ke manpur main unka samrajya tha, isi kaal kii anya 'rashtrakut' jatiyon main 'achalpur', ke shaasha tathaa 'kannauj' ke shaasha bhi shaamil the. inke moolsthaan tathaa mul ke bare main kai bhrantiyaan prachlit he. elichpur main shashan karne vaale 'rashtrakut' 'badami chalukyon' ke upnivesh ke rup main sthapit hue the lekin 'dantidurg' ke netritv main unhone chalukya shaasha 'kirtivarman dwitiya' ko vahaan se ukhaad phenkaa tathaa aadhunik 'karnataka' praant ke 'gulburg' ko apana mukhya sthaan banaaya. yah jati baad main 'manyakhet ke rashtrakooton ' ke naam se vikhyath ho gai, jo 'dakshin bharat' main 753 isvi main satta main ai, isi samay par bengal kaa 'paal samrajya' evam 'gujarat ke pratihaar samrajya' 'bhartiya upmahadweep' ke purv or uttarpashchim bhubhag par teji se satta main aa rahe the. 
 aathvin se dasvin shatabdi ke madhya ke kaal main ganga ke upjau maidani bhaag par sthit 'kannauj rajya' par niyantran hetu ek tridaleey sangharsh chal rahaa tha, us vakt 'kannauj' 'uttar bharat' kii mukhya satta ke rup main sthapit tha. prayek samrajya us par niyantran karnaa chaah rahaa tha. 'manyakhet ke rashtrakooton' kii satta ke ucchtam shikhar par unka samrajya uttaradisha main 'ganga' or 'yamuna nadi' par sthit doaab se lekar dakshin main kanyakumari tak tha. yah unke raajnneetik vistaar, vaastukala uplabdhiyon or saahitiyik yogadan kaa kaal tha. is rajvansh ke praarambhik shaasha hindu dharm ke anuyaayi the, parantu baad main yah rajvansh jain dharm ke prabhaav main aa gaya tha.
 ye sambhavat: mul rup se dravid kisaan the, jo lattapur ke shahi parivaar ke the. ye kannada bhashaa bolte the, lekin unhen uttar-dakkani bhashaa kii jaankaree bhi thi. apane shatru chalukya vansh ko parajeet karne vaale rashtrakut vansh ke shashan kaal main hi dakkan samrajya bharat kii duusari badi raajnneetik ikai ban gaya, jo malwa se kanchi tak failaa hua tha. is kaal main rashtrakooton ke mahatv kaa is tathy se pataa chalata he ki ek muslim yatri ne yahan ke raja ko duniya ke chaar mahaan shaskaon main se ek bataaya .
 kai rashtrakut raja adhyayan or kala ke prati samarpit the. duusare raja, krishna pratham ne ellora main chattan ko katkar kailash mandir banwaaya. is rajvansh kaa prasiddh shaasha amoghvarsh pratham ne, jinhone lagbhag 814 se 878 tak shashan kiya, sabase puraani gyaat kannada kavita kavirajmarg ke kuch khandon kii rachana kii thi. unke shashan kaal main jain ganitagyon or vidwaanon ne 'kannada' va 'sanskrit' bhashaaon ke sahitya main mahatvapurn yogadan diya. uni vaastukala 'dravinan style' main aaj bhi mile kaa pathar maani jaati he, jiskaa ek prasiddh udhaaharan 'ellora' kaa 'kailashnath mandir' he. anya mahatvapurn yogadanon main 'maharashtra' main sthit 'elephanta gufaaon' kii murtikla tathaa 'karnataka' ke 'patadkkal' main sthit 'kaashi vishwanath' or 'jain mandir' aadi aate hai, yahi nahin yah sabhi 'unesco' kii world heritage sait main bhi shaamil hai.
 is vansh ke kai raja yuddh kala main paarangat the. dhruv pratham ne gangavaadi ke gangavansh ke raajaaon ko parajeet kiya, kanchi ke pallvon se loha liya or bengal ke raja tathaa kananoj par daawa karne vaale pratihaar shaasha ko parajeet kiya. krishna dwitiya ne, jo 878 main singhaasana par baithe, phir se gujarat par kabja kar liya, jo amoghvarsh pratham ke haathon se chhin gaya tha. lekin ve vengi par phir se adhikaar karne main asafal rahe. unke pautra indra tritiy ne, jo 914 main sattarudh huye. kannauj par kabja karke rashtrakut shakti ko apane charam par pahunchaa diya. krishna tritiy ne uttar ke apane abhiyaanon se iskaa or vistaar kiya tathaa kanchi or tamil adhikaar vaale maidani kshetra par kabja kar liya.
 khottim amoghvarsh chaturth apni rajdhani kii raksha main vifal rahe or unke patan ne is vansh par se logon kaa vishwaas uthaa diya. samraat bhagkar pashchimi ghaaton main chale gaye, jahaan unka vansh saahsee gang or kadamb vanshon ke sahyog se tab tak gumnaam jivan vyateet karata rahaa, jabatak tel pratham chalukya ne lagbhag 975 main satta sangharsh main vijay nahin praapt kar lee.
