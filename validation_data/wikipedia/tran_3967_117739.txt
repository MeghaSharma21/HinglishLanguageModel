 khalil jibran  ek lebanani-america kalakaar, kavi tathaa neuyork pen lig ke lekhak the. unhen apane chintan ke kaaran samakaleen padariyon or adhikari varg kaa kopbhaajan hona padaa or jati se bahishkrut karke desh nikaala tak de diya gaya tha. aadhunik arabi sahitya main jibran khalil 'jibran' ke naam se prasiddh hai, kintu angreji main vah apana naam khalil jwran likhte the or isi naam se ve adhik prasiddh bhi hue.
 khalil jibran 6 janvari 1883 ko lebanon ke 'bathri' nagar main ek sampann parivaar main paidaa hue. 12 varsh kii aayu main hi mata-pita ke saath belgium, france, america aadi deshon main bhraman karte hue 1912 main america ke neuyork main sthaayee rup se rahane lage the.
 boston nagar main unhone baalkon ke ek public skool main dhaai varsh tak shiksha praapt kii. taduparant ek raatri ke skool main varsh bhar padhate rahe. phir vah lebanon main 'madrastul hikmat' naamak ek ucch koti ke vidhyalay main shiksha praapt karne ke liye chale gaye. vahaan shiksha praapt karke vah syria tathaa lebanon main aitihaasik sthanon kii saiyr karte hue 1902 i. main lebanon se waapas chale gaye. vah apane parivaaravaalon se bada prem karte the. isi kaaran 1902 i. main apni bahin, 1903 i. main apane bhai tathaa teen maas uparaant hi apni maan ke rswagvaas se unhen bada shok hua. in paariwarik du:khon kii anubhuti tathaa apane mitbhaashi swabhaav ke kaaran ve apane vichaaron ke jagat main hi vicharan karte rahete the. chitrakala se unhen badi ruchi thi. jab bachche unhen baton main lagaana chaahate, ve aisi adbhut baaten ched dete ki ve yah samajhane par vivash ho jate ki koi bada hi vicitra balak he. 1908 i. main unhone paris kii fine arts academy main murtikla kii shiksha praapt kii. paris se lautakar ve neuyark main nivas karne lage kintu ve har varsh apane parivaaravaalon ke paas kuch samay vyateet karne ke liye boston jaaya karte the. vahiin ve shantipurvak chitrakala main apana samay vyateet karte.
 unke jivan kii kathinaaiyon kii chhap unki kritiyon main bhi vartmaan he jinamein unhone praaia: apane prakritik evam saamaajik vaataavaran kaa chithran kiya he. aadhunik arabi sahitya main unhen prem kaa sandeshvahak mana jaataa he. angreji main anudit unki kritiyaan badi prasiddh ho chuki he.
 ve apane vichaar jo ucch koti ke subhashit yaa kahawat rup main hote the, unhen kaagaj ke tukdon, theatre ke kaarykram ke kaagajon, cigarette kii dibbiyon ke gatton tathaa fate hue liphaafon par likhakar rakh dete the. unki secretary shrimati barbara yang ko unhen ikatthi kar prakashit karwaane kaa shrey jaataa he. unhen har baat yaa kuch kehane ke purv ek yaa do vaakya sutara rup main sookhti kehane kii aadat thi.
 unamem adbhut kalpana-shakti thi. ve apane vichaaron ke kaaran kaviver rabindranath tagore ke samakaksh hi sthapit hote the. unki rachanaaen 22 se adhik bhashaaon main desh-videsh main tathaa hindi, gujarati, marathi, urdu main anuvaadit ho chuki hai. inamen urdu tathaa marathi main sabase adhik anuwaad praapt hote hai. unke chitron kii pradarshnee bhi kai deshon main lagaai gai, jisaki sabhi ne muktakanth se prashansa kii. ve isa ke anuyaayi hokar bhi padariyon or andhvishwaas ke kattar virdohi rahe. desh se nishkasana ke baad bhi apni deshbhakti ke kaaran apane desh hetu satat likhte rahe. 48 varsh kii aayu main car durghatana main gambhir rup se ghayal hokar 10 april 1931 ko unka neuyork main hi dehanth ho gaya. unke nidhan ke baad hajaaron log unke antim darshanon ko aate rahe. baad main unhen apni janmabhumi ke girjaghar main dafnaaya gaya.
