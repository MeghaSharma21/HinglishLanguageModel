 cavin red australia ke nivartmaan pradhanmantri hai. ve sangheey australian shram dal ke netaa bhi hai. unhonen 2007 kaa sangheey chunaav jeeta or australiya ke pradhanmantri bane. julia gellard ke lebar party ke netaa pad se apdasth hone ke ek din baad kevin red ko ek baar pun: australiya ke pradhanmantri pad kii shapath dilaai gayi. varsh 2013 ke sangheey chunaaon main haar mili.
 sansad main chune jane se purv ve ek rajanyik or sarkari karmchaaree the. red ne 1998 main sansad kii seat jiti thi. sangheey australian lebar party ne disambar 2006 main unhen vipaksh kaa netaa chunaa. 2007 kaa sangheey chunaav unhone kaary samjhauton or bhumandaleey ooshmeekaran ke kaaran jeeta tha. pradhanmantri ke rup main unhone pehle hi kioto sandhi par hastakshar kar diye the or ab ve iraq yuddh se australian sainikon ko baahar nikaalenge.
