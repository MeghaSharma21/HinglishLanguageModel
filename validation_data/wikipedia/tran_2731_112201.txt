 hit dwand aise nyaay-viruddh yaa anauchity kii paristhiti hoti he jisamen koi vyakti yaa sanghathan ek se adhik hiton yaa dhyeyon ko praapt karne main lipt ho jinamein aapasi takaraav he. bhrashtachar aksar hit dwand kii paristhitiyon main janm letaa he, jab koi sarkaar yaa sanstha kisi vyakti ko kisi samuhik hit-prapti ke kaaryon main nirnay lene kaa adhikaar de or vah vyakti in adhikaron kaa prayog kisi vyaktigat hit-siddhi ke liye kare.
