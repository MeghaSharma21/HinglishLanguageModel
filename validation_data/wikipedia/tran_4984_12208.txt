 kisi tarah kii shiksha athwa prashikshan ke liye nirdhaarit vishyon, upavishyon evam sambandhit saamagree kii vyavasthit evam sarrup main prastuti hi paathyavivaran kehalaata he. paathyavivaran praayah kisi shiksha parishad dwaara nirdhaarit kii jaati he yaa kisi praadhyaapak dwaara banaayi jaati he jo us vishay ke shikshan kii gunvattta ke liye uttaradaayi hota he. samuchit paathyavivaran kaa shiksha main bahut mahatvapurn sthaan he.
 paathyavivaran yah sunishchit karata he ki shikshak or chaatr yah acchhee tarah samaj len ki kya or kitna padhaanaa/padhanaa he, us saamagree ko padhane kaa uddeshy kya he, kaksha main vidhyaarthee se kya apekshaayen hai aadi. isse vidhyaarthee ko ek disha milti he or rasta bhi dikhaai padataa he.
