 gothic vaastukala vaastukala kii ek style he jo europe main uttar madhyakaal main prachlit thi. gothic style 'romanesco vaastukala' se janmi. yah style france main 12veen shatee main janmi tathaa 16veen shatee tak prachlit rahi. us kaal main ise 'francisi karm' kaha jaataa tha. 'gothic' shabd kaa prayog punarjagran ke baad ke kaal main prachlit hua. gothic style ke baad punaruddhar vaastukala kaa janm hua. is style kii mukhya visheshtaayen hai- bindumay chaap, rib kiya hua gumbad, ardhachaap vapra . europe ke anek mahaan girijagharon evam anya isai dharmik bhawan isi style main bane hai.
