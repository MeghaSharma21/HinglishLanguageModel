 alpine dashsebrake kutto kii saint haund tarah kii ek choti nasal he jinka janam austria main hua. alpine dashsebrake ko ghyal hue shikaar yaa jaanavar jaise kii bhaaluu - lomady aadi kaa pataa lagane va peechha karne ke liye viksit kiya gaya. yah nasal kisi chiz kaa peechha karne main maahir he chaahe phir kitna hi samay kyon naa ho gaya ho.
 iskaa vajan 15 se 18 killo ke kareeb hota he va uchai 13 se 16 ich.
