 minar-e-jam yaa jam kii minar pashchimi afghanistan ke gor praant ke sheharak zile main hari nadi ke kinare khadi ek prasiddh eenton kii bani minar he. yah 65 meter unchi minar dilli ke qutub minar ke baad duniya kii duusari sabase unchi minar he, haalanki qutub minar vastav main isi minar se prerit hokar banwaaya gaya tha. minar-e-jam jam nadi or hari nadi ke sangam ke paas he or chaaron taraf se 2,400 meter unche pahunchne vaale pahaado se ghiri hui he. san 1190 ke dashak main bani is minar par eent, gach plasters or tilein lagi hui hai jinpar quran kii aayatein or aakarshak lakeeren va akritiyaan bani hui hai.
 mana jaataa he ki minar-e-jam gori rajvansh kii greeshmakaleen rajdhani firuzakuh ke paas banavaai gai thi. isapar arabi bhashaa main bani likhaeeyon par lagi tarikh thik se padhi nahin ja sakati. yaa to yah san 1193-94 kaa zikr kar rahi he, yaa phir san 1174-75 kaa. isliye yaa to yah goriyon ke sultan giyasauddin kii dilli main san 1192 main gazanaviyon par hui jeet kaa smarak thi yaa phir san 1173 main gazani main guzâ€‹ turkon par hui jeet kaa. 12veen or 13veen sadi main gori rajvansh kaa samrajya purvi iran se lekar uttar bharat main dilli tak failaa hua tha.
 gori rajvansh kaa bakhaan karne vaale madhyakaalin lekhak juljani ke anusaar yah minar yahi par sthit jumma masjid ke saath judi thi lekin nadi main achaanak sailaab aane se masjid bah gai. itihaaskaaron ne yahan chaanbin karke paaya he ki masjid ke saath ek bada aangan sata hua he jo sambhav he usee masjid kaa hissaa rahaa ho. 1202 main giyasauddin ke dehanth ke baad goriyon kaa samrajya dhalane laga or vah apni zaminein khwaaresm samrajya ko khone lage. juljani kaa kahana he ki 1222 main mongol akraman ne phiruzakuh shahar ko khatm kar diya.
 afghanistan se baahar kii duniya ko is minar kii zyaada khabar nahin thi lekin 1886 main bharat ke british raaj se jude afasar thomas holdic yahan pahunche or unhone iskaa zikr apni rapat main kiya. 20veen sadi main kai itihaaskaaron ne aakar iskaa adhyayan kiya.
 hari nadi or jam nadi ke paas hone se iske neeche kii zamin main pani chootaa he jis se yah minar zaraa tedi hone lagi he. ise sthir karne kaa kaam jari he. is kshetra main zalazale bhi aate hai or vah bhi ek khatara hai. samay ke saath-saath luteron or baimaan itihaaskaaron ne bhi iske aasapaas bina anumati ke khudaai karke nuksaan pahunchaaya he.
