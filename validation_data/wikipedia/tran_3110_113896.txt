 chandausi uttar pradesh ke sambhal jile kaa ek shahar he. isko puraane samay main chandasi nagari ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
 chandausi dilli se lagbhag 140 kimi purv main tathaa muradabad nagar se 40 kimi dakshin main sthit prasiddh vyapaarik mandi he. aligarh, khayr, meerut, bareli, nainital or saharanpur ke bich main sthit hone ke kaaran is mandi kaa kendriya mahatv he. sadakon or relon kaa prasiddh junkashan he. yahan bharat kaa prasiddh terning kalege hai jo railway stesan ke nikat sthit he. gehun, chaaval, makka, sarson, jau tath namak kaa vyapaar hota he. chandausi kaa ghi shuddhta ke liye uttari bharat main prasiddh he. kapaas se vinola nikaalane kii mashine bhi yahan hai. yahan se kapaas, san, patua, chini or pathar baahar bheja jaataa he. iske sameep jalavidyut centre he.yahan ganesh chouth kaa mela lagata he or ye mumbai ke baad yahan lagata he. bharat main chandausi ke ganesh mela kaa dwitiya sthaan he. yahaan mint aayal kaa bada kaam hota he .
