 bhavali se bhimtal kii ore kuch dur chalane par baayin taraf kaa rasta ramgarh kii ore mud jaataa he. yah maarg sundar he. is bhuvali - mukteshwar motor-maarg kehete hai. kuch hi der main 2300 meter kii oonchaai vaale 'gaagar' naamak sthaan par jab yatrigan pahonchte hai to unhen himalay ke divya darshan hote hai. 'gaagar' naamak parvat kshetra main 'gargacharya' ne tapasya kii thi, isiilie is sthaan kaa naam 'gargacharya' se apbhransh hokar 'gaagar' ho gaya. 'gaagar' kii is chotii par gargeshwar mahadev kaa ek purana mandir he. 'shivaratri' ke din yahan par shivbhakton kaa ek vishaal mela lagata he.
 'gaagar' se malla ramgarh keval 3 kimi kii duuri par samudratal se 1789 meter kii oonchaai par sthit he. nainital se keval 25 kimi kii duuri par ramgarh ke falon kaa yah anokhaa kshetra basaa hua he. kumaun kshetra main sabase adhik falon kaa utpaadan bhuvali - ramgarh ke aasapaas ke kshetron main hota he. is kshetra main anek prakaar ke fal paye jate hai. barf padane ke baad sabase pehle green sweet seb or sabase baad main pakane vaala haraa pichola seb hota he. iske alaava is kshetra main delicious, golden king, pheni or jonathan jati ke shreshth varg ke seb bhi hote hai. adu yahan kaa sarvottam fal he. totaapari, hilderli or gaula ki kaa adu yahan bahut paidaa kiya jaataa he. isi tarah khumanion kii bhi mokapark va gaula ki behatar bhang se paidaa kii jaati he. pulam to yahan kaa vishesh fal ho gaya he. green goj jati ke pulam yahan bahut paidaa kiya jate hai.
 ramgarh, jahaan apane falon ke liye vikhyath he, vahaan yah apane naiysargik soundarya ke liye bhi prasiddh he. himalay kaa viraat soundarya yahaan se saaf-saaf dikhaai detaa he. ramgarh kii parvat chotii par jo bangala he, usee main ek baar vishwakavi rabindra nath tagore aakar tehre the. unhone yahan se jo himalay kaa drishy deka to mugdh ho gaye or kai dinon tak himalay ke darshan isi sthaan par baithkar karte rahe. unki yaad main bangala aaj bhi 'tagore top' ke naam se janaa jaataa he. bharat ke pratham pradhanmantri pandit jawaharlal nehru ko bhi ramgarh bahut pasand tha. kehete hai acharya narendradev ne bi apane 'buddha darshan' naamak vikhyath granth ko antim rup yaheen aakar diya tha. sahityakaron ko yah sthaan sadaiv apni ore aakarshith karata rahaa he. swa. mahadevi verma, jo aadhunik hindi sahitya kii mira kehalaati hai, ko to ramgarh bhaayaa ki ve sadaiv greeshm ritu main yaheen aakar rahati thim. unhone apana ek chota sa makaan bhi yahan banavaa liya tha. aaj bhi yah bhawan ramgarh-mukteshwar motor maarg ke baayin ore bas stesan ke peeche vaali pahari par vrikshon ke bich dekha ja saktaa he. jivan ke antim dinon main ve pahaad par nahin aa sakati thim. at: unhone mrityu se kuch pehle is makaan ko bechaa tha. parantu unki aatma sadaiv is anchal main aane ke liye sadaiv tatpar rahati thi. aise hi anek gyaat or agyaat sahitya - premi hai, jinhen ramgarh pyaara laga tha or bahut se aise prakruti - premi hai jo bina naam bataae or bina apana parichay die bhi in pahadiyon main vichram karte rahete hai.
