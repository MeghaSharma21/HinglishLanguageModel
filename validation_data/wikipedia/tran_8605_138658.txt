 jorge farnandis bhartiya rajaneta hai. ve shramik sanghathan ke bhootpurv netaa, tathaa patrkaar the. ve rajyasabha or loksabhaa ke sadsy rah chuke hai. unhone samta party kii sthapana kii. ve bharat ke kendriya mantrimandal main rakshaamantri, sanchaarmantri, udyogmantri, railmantri aadi ke rup main kaary kar chuke hai. aajkal unka swasthya thik nahin he or ve ekaaki jivan ji rahe hai.
 chaudaavin loksabhaa main ve mujaffarpur se janata dal ke tikat par saansad chune gaye. ve 1998 se 2004 tak kii rashtriya janatantric gathbandhan kii kendriya sarkaar main raksha mantri the.
 1977 main, apatkaal hataa die jane ke baad, furnandis ne anupasthiti main bihar main mujffarpur seat jiti or unhen industries ke kendriya mantri niyukt kiya gaya. kendriya mantri ke rup main apane kaarykaal ke dauraan, unhone nivesh ke ulanghan ke kaaran, america bahurashtriya companiyon ibiem or coca-cola ko desh chhodane kaa aadesh diya. vah 1989 se 1990 tak rail mantri ke rup main apane kaarykaal ke dauraan konkan railway pariyojna ke peeche prerana shakti thim. vah rashtriya janatantric gathbandhan sarkaar main raksha mantri the, jab kargil yuddh bharat or pakistan or bharat ne pokharan main parmaanu pareekshan kiye ek anbhavi samaajawaadee, furnandis ko baraak missile ghotaale or tahalaka maamle sahit kai vivaadon se dar lagata he. jorge furnandis ne 1967 se 2004 tak 9 loksabhaa chunaav jite.
