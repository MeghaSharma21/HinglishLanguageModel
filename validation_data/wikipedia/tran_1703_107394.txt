 sardaar chutkule athwa sardarji chutkule sikhon par aadhaarit jaateey vyangy hote hai jinamein aksar saradaaron ko alpbuddhi, angrezi na jaanane vaale yaa shabdon ke galat matlab samajhane vaale bataaya jaataa he. bharat or pakistan main ye chutkule bahut lokapriy hai.
 sardaar chutaklon main sardaar ko mukhya rup se bhole, anaadi, buddhi se kamjor, ashuddh or angrezi bhashaa main kamjor dikhaaya jaataa he. adhiktar sardaar chutkule anya jaateey chutkulon athwa rudhi chutkulon kaa rupantaran hote hai. kuch main sardarji kaa mazaakia rup main chithran athwa anyalogon dwaara sardarji ko rudhiwaadi tarike se prastut kiya jaataa he.
 santa singh or banta singh, sardaar chutkulon main vishesh paatr ke rup main prasiddh hai. shodharthi jawaharlal handu ke anusaar sardaar chutkule sikhon ke rudhiwaad or unke angrezi bhashaa or samaj ke sthaan par shaareerik yogyata ko adhik mahatva dena he. wo saath main likhte hai ki "mere anusaar, sampoorn rup se sikh samudaay ki ‘safalataa kii kahani’ gaiyr-sikh samudaayon, khaskar bharat ke hinduon main vyagrata kii gahari jadon kaa rup he. .... sikh kaaphi samrudh or safal log hai. .... isse hinduon ke ahankaar ko thes pahaunchi hogi or isse vyagrata utpann hui hogi jisane vibhinn prakaar ke rudh-prarupi or chutkula chakra main parinat hui." saumen sen ke anusaar chutkule bhartiya gaiyr-sikh abhijaat varg kii chintaa ko paravartit karti he, jo udhyami sikhon ke vikaas or pratispardhaa ke kaaran asuraksha mahasoos karte hai.
