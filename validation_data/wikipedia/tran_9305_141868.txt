 jamshedpur jiskaa doosraa naam tatanagar bhi he, bharat ke jharkhand rajya kaa ek shahar he. yah jharkhand ke dakshini hisse main sthit purvi singhbhum jile kaa hissaa he. jamshedpur kii sthapana ko parsi vyavsaayee jamshedji nausherwan ji tata ke naam se joda jaataa he. 1907 main tata aayran and steel company kii sthapana se is shahar kii buniyaad padi. isse pehle yah sakachi naamak ek adivasee gaanv hua karata tha. yahan kii mitti kaali hone ke kaaran yahan pehla railway-stesan kalimati ke naam se bana jise baad main badalakar tatanagar kar diya gaya. khanij padaarthon kii prachur maatra main uplabdhta or kharkai tathaa suvarnarekha nadi ke aasaani se uplabdh pani, tathaa kolkata se najdiki ke kaaran yahan aaj ke aadhunik shahar kaa pehla beej boyaa gaya. 
 jamshedpur aaj bharat ke sabase pragatisheel audyogic nagaron main se ek he. tata gharaane kii kai companiyon ke utpaadan ikai jaise tisco, tata motors, tiscon, tinpalet, timkan, tub divijan, ityadi yahan karyrat he.
 jamshedpur sahi myne main ek atyaadhunik audyogic nagari he.yahan ke kuch pramukh kaarkaane hai:
 aadhunik steel and power limited, kohinur steel and power limited, jemipol, en em el aadi. sakachi yahan kaa ek pramukh vyapaarik kendra he.
 jamshedpur sadak or rail-maarg dwaara puure desh se judaa hua he. howrah mumbai rail maarg par sthit hone ke kaaran tatanagar dakshinpurv railway ke atyant vyast steshnon main se gina jaataa he. rashtriya rajmarge 33 yahan se hokar guzarati he. nagar ke uttar purvi hisse main ek sonari hawaii adda he jo vayudoot kii sevaaon se judaa he. shahar kii zyaadaatar sadakon kaa rakrakhaav tata parivaar ke dwaara hone kii vajah se yahan kii sadake jharkhand main anya shaharon kii apeksha kaaphi acchhee hai.
 jamshedpur ke pramukh shiksha evam shodh santhaan: 
 lauhnagari ke rup main vikhyath jamshedpur keval jharkhand main nahin, balki puure vishwa patal par charchit he. ise tatanagar ke bhi naam se janaa jaataa he. paryaton kii drushti se tatanagar kaa mahatv antarashtriya str par bhi he. ise haal main hi international clin city' ke award se nawaja gaya he. log puure vishwa se is lauhnagari ko dekhne aate he. tisco, telko jaise antarrashtiya str ke kaarkaane ke alaava dimna lake, jubilee park, dalma pahaad, hudco lake, modi park, keenan stadium aadi aise jagah he jahaan paryatak gum sakate he. 
 yah park tata steel ne apane 50 varsh puure karne ke uparant banavaa kar yahaan ke nivaasiyon ko tohfe swarup bhent kii thi. 225 acre bhoomi main faile is park kaa udghaatan 1958 i. main us samay ke tatkaaleen pradhanmantri jawahar laal nehru ne kiya tha. vrundavan garden ke tarj par bane is park main gulab ke lagbhag ek hajaar kism ke paudhen lage hue hai. is park main ek children park bhi he. haal main hi yahaan ek amujament park kaa nirmaan kiya he. amusement park main anek kism ke jhule lage huue he. harek saal 3 march ko jamshedji nausarvanji tata kii yaad main puure park ko bijli ke rangeen balvon ke dwaara bade bhavy tareeke se sajaayaa jaataa he. is din puure vishwa se hajaaron kii sankhya main log yahaan is kaarykram main shariq hone aate he. yah kaarykram teen dinon tak chalata he. is park ke ek hisse main chota sa chideaghar bhi he. 
 purv main ise jubilee lake ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. 40 acre main faile is jhil ko vishesh taur par boating ke liye banaaya gaya he. is jhil ke bichombich ek iland kaa nirmaan kiya gaya he jo ki isaki sundarata main chaar chand lagaataa he. iske saath hi paryatak boating ke dauraan is island kaa istemaal aaraam farmaane ke liye bhi karte he. 
 3000 feet kii oonchai par sthit tathaa 193 varg kilometre main faile is abhyaarany kaa udghaatan swargeey sanjay gandhi ne kiya tha. yahaan par junglee jaanwaron ko najadik se dekhne ke liye anek jagah vishesh rup se banaae gaye he jahaan se paryatak aasaani se junglee jaanavar jaise hathi, harin, teduna, bagh aadi ko dekh sakate he. iske alaava durlabh van sampada yahaan dekha ja saktaa he..raat ko dalma pahari kii chotii se tatanagar kaa najaara bilkul akaash main timatimaate taaren ke samaan prathit hota he. yahaan par paryatakon ke thaharne ke liye tatasteel tathaa van vibhaag dwaara guest house kaa bhi nirmaan kiya gaya he. yahaan par ek gupha main bhagwaan shiv kaa prakritik mandir he. jinhen shraddha se log dalma baba kehete hai..inhen jamshedpur kaa sangrakshak devtaa bhi kaha jaataa he.. sawan ke dinon main tathaa shivaratri ke din in mandiron ko bhavy tareeke se sajaa kar yahaan par puja archana kii jaati he. dalma pahari haathiyon kii prakritik ashrayasthali he..prashasnic drushtikon se dekhen to jharkhand ke purvi singhbhum, sarayakela-kharasaavaan se lekar pashchim bengal ke purulia jile ke belpahadi tak iskaa dayera failaa he..dalma pahari main kai adivasee gaanv hai..
 yah jamshedpur shahar se 13 kimi kii duuri par sthit he. dalma pahari kii talahati main bane is kritrim jhil ko dekhne saalon bhar paryatak aate rahete he. disambar-janvari main maheene main paryatak yahaan vishesh taur picnic manaane aate he. is jhil kaa nirmaan tata steel ne jal sangrakshan ke liye tathaa yahaan ke nivaasiyon ke liye karavaaya tha.
 puure jharkhand main keval keenan stadium hi antarrashtiya str kaa cricket ground he. shahar ke thik bich main sthit is maidan par ab tak kai antarrashtiya cricket match kaa aayojan ho chukaa he. mohali cricket ground ke baad ise sabase sundar cricket ground samajha jaataa he. 
 hudco jhil jamshedpur main chota govindpur or telko colony ke bich sthit tata motors dwaara nirmit ek kritrim jhil he. company dwaara is kshetra ko ek picnic spot ke rup main viksit kiya gaya he.
