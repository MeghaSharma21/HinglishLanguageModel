 atyadhik atmachetana​ swayan ke bare main atyadhik chetana ko kehete hai. yah saadhaaran aatmbodh or atmachetana se bhinn dasha he. isamen dusron dwaara saadhaaran str se adhik parakhe jane yaa dekhe jane kii apriy bhaavana ho sakati he. manovigyaan main atyadhik atmachetana ko kabhi-kabhi sankoch, shame yaa sanvibhram kii sthitiyon se sambandhit paaya jaataa he. is se vyathit log aksar lagaataar yah anumaan lagane main jute rahete hai ki duusare unhen dekhakar yaa sunakar unke bare main kya soch rahe hai or apane prakat rup par zaroorat se adhik dhyaan dete hai.
 kuch manovaigyanikon ke anusaar atyadhik atmachetana​ aksar kishoravastha main shuru hoti he or isamen peedit vyakti ko lagata he ki anya log use dekh rahe hai jabaki vastav main usapar logon kaa dhyaan kam hota he yaa bilkul hi nahin hota. in 'kaalpanik darshakon' ke bare main sochane se vyakti khulkar apane vyaktitv ke anusaar kaam nahin kar paata. vah apni bolchaal or anya vyavhaaron par anuchit lagaam laga letaa he.
