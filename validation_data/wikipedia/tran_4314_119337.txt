 kotvan mathura jile, uttar pradesh, bharat kaa ek gaanv he. yah hindumat kii manyata ke anusaar vah sthaan he jahaan krishna ne apana bachapan bitaayaa or braj bhoomi ke mukhya sthanon main se ek he. yah gaanv agra se 100 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he.
 "kotvan" roopa ke bete kattarmal or daahagaanv se banataa he jise uske ek or bhai momel ne viksit kiya tha. unke pita dujana ke mul nivasee the jo dilli ke nikat he.

 kotvan uttar pradesh ke mathura jile ke nandagaanv block kaa ek gaanv he. yah agra khand main aata he. yah mathura jila mukhyalay se 55 kilometre uttar par sthit he. yah nandagaanv se 10 kilometre dur he. rajya rajdhani lakhanu se yah 429 kilometre dur he.
 nabipur, hataanaa, lalpur, kharout or baaten khurd aasapaas ke gaanv hai. kotvan ke purv main chhatablock he, hodal block uttar main he, hasanpur block uttar main he, punaahana block pashchim main he.
 hodal, palwaal, vrundavan or mathura aasapaas ke shahar hai.
 yah sthaan mathura jile or bharatpur jile kii seemaa main he. bharatpur jile kii kaman is jagah ke pashchim main he. yah rajasthan ke rajya kii seemaa ke nikat he.
 kotvan kii sthaneey bhashaa hindi he. gaanv kii kul janaskhya 5193 thi or gharon kii sankhya 802 he. mahilaon kii janaskhya 47.2% he. gaanv kii sakshartaa dar 58.0% he or mahila sakshartaa dar 21.8% he.
 kotvan ko hindu dharm kii vaishnav parampara ke liye ek pavitra sthal mana gaya he. ise braj or sheetalkund kii puja se sambandhit bataaya gaya he jo krishna se jude hai. 
 radha krishna ke lakhon shraddhalu in tirthasthalon kaa har varsh daura karte hai or kai tyoharon main bhaag lete hai. krishna-bhakthi kaa ek kendra kaafii samay se brijabhoomi rahi he.
 kotvan kaa ateet hindu sabhyataa or itihaas se judaa he or yah sadiyon se ek mahatvapurn hindu tirthasthal he. 
 kotvan mathura jile ke nandagaanv block main sthit he. yah maharaj seetaram ke adheen tha jo ek mahaan senapati or bharatpur rajya ke revary pargana ke zameendaar the. unka bharatpur ke rajpariwaar ke saath sambandh tha.
