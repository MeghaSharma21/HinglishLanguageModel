 yuya ozeki kaa janm 5 march 1996 ko hua. wo japan ke film abhinayta hai. unhen baccha-bhoot shrunkhlaa kii ju-on filmon main toshiyo ke abhinay ke liye janaa jaataa he.
 unhone maatr saat varsh kii aayu main 2003 main chalchitr video ju-on main toshiro kii bhoomika ko doharaaya. jisase taakashi matsuyama or takoka fuji abhinaytaon ko kaary main bahut sahaayta mili. unhone apana abhinay da graj or da graj 2 naamak america punarnirmaanon main bhi kiya. unhone ek pralekhi dividi main kaha ki yah bahut bhaybheet karne vaala tha kyonki billi ke saath apane drishyon ko filmaana bahut hi mushkil tha.
 unki sauteli bahan port orchard, washington, sanyukt rajya america main rahati hai atah ve aksar washington or japan ke bich yatra karte rahe hai.
