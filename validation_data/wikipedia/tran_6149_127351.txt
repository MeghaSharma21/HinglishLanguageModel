 luluser yaa lalusar pakistan ke uttari bhaag main kaibar-pakhtuunakva praant ke mansehara zile kii kaayaan ghati main sthit ek parvatiy jhil or parvaton kaa samooh he. 3,410 meter kii oonchaai par sthit yah jhil kunhaar nadi kaa mul strot he. yah ek bada paryatak-aakarshan he.
 1857 kaa pratham bhartiya swatantraaa sangram ke kaal main 55vaan bengal deshiy paidal dasta kaibar-pakhtuunakva kshetra main tainaat tha. vidroh failane par us kaa bada bhaag usamen shaamil ho gaya or british afasaron ko maar daalaa. jab angrez vidroh dabaane main safal hue to vidrohiyon kaa antim gut luluser ke paas hi giraftaar kara gaya.
