 chandani chowk dilli kaa sabase purana evam sabase vyast kshetra he. yah puraani dilli ke sabase vyast bazaaron main se ek he. chandani chowk puraani dilli railway stesan ke najadik sthit he. laal kila smarak bazaar ke bheetar sthit he. yah 17 veen shatabdi main bharat ke mughal samraat shahjahan dwaara banaaya gaya tha, or iskaa dizin unki beti jahanara dwaara taiyaar kiya gaya tha. chand kii roshni ko pratibimbit karne ke liye bazaar ko naharon dwaara vibhajit kiya gaya tha or yah bharat ke sabase bade thok bazaaron main se ek bana hua he.
 chandani chowk puraani dilli ke madhya main laal kile ke lahori get se shuru hokar fatehpuri masjid tak vistrut he. isi bazaar ke naam par aas paas ke kshetra ko bhi chandani chowk kaha jaataa he. ek nahar kisi samay main sadak ke bich main bahati thi, or chowk ke talab main jal bharaa karti thi. aarambhik kal main ise teen khandon main baanta gaya tha:
 bazaar kaa itihaas rajdhani shahjahanabad kii sthapana se shuru hota he, jab samraat shahjahan ne apni nai rajdhani ke bagal main yamuna nadi ke tat par laal kile kii sthapana kii thi.
 chandani chowk ko 1650 isvi main shahjahan kii putree, rajkumaari jahanara begum ne dizin kiya tha. 1,560 dukaanon vaala yah bazaar mul rup se 40 gaj chaudaa or 1,520 gaj lambaa tha. bazaar akruti main chaukor tha, tathaa iske centre main ek taal upasthit tha, jo chandani raat main chamakta tha, or isi kaaran bazaar kaa naam chandani chowk padaa tha. sabhi dukaanon ko us samay aadhe chandramaa ke aakaar ke pattern main banaaya gaya tha, jo ab vilupt ho gaya tha. yah bazaar apane chaandi ke vyapaariyon ke liye prasiddh tha, jis kaaran ise "silver street" ke naam se bhi pahachaanaa gaya he.
 chandani chowk ek samay main bharat kaa sabase bada bazaar tha. mughal shahi juluus chandani chowk se guzarate the. 1903 main dilli darbar ke aayojan ke samay is parampara ko punarsthapit kiya gaya tha. 1863 main british sarkaar dwaara chowk ke paas dilli toun haul banaaya gaya tha. chowk ke talab ko 1950 ke dashak tak ek ghantaghar se pratisthapit kar diya gaya tha. isi kaaran bazaar kaa centre abhi bhi ghantaghar ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
 chandani chowk ko kai filmon main puraani dilli kii abhinn pahchaan ke rup main darshaya gaya he. 2001 kii bollywood film kabhi khushi kabhi gam main chandani chowk ko pramukhataa se darshaya gaya tha; yahaan film kii paatr anjali or unki bahan puja rahete the. 2008 main anil kapur, anuraag sinha, shefali chaaya or aditi sharma abhinit bollywood film blike and white chandani chowk main set he. iske baad agle hi varsh aayi do anya filmein bhi chandani chowk ke aasapaas kendrit thi; akshay kumaar, deepika padukon, mithun chakravarti or ranvir shouri abhinit chandani chowk tu china, or abhishek bachchan, sonam kapur, wahidaa rahamaan, om puri, atul kulkarni or divya datta abhinit dilli - 6. 2017 se sab tivi par prasaarit ho rahaa tivi kaarykram jijaji chat par hai bhi chandani chowk main hi set he.
