 17veen shatabdi ke manavedan naamak samutiri raja dwaara prastut drishyakala krishnanattem kehalaati he. manavedan ne shrikrishna katha ko aadhaar banaakar sanskrit main 'krishngiti' naamak drishykavy kii rachana kii thi. yah katha abhinay kii drushti se bhi shreshth thi. iske antargat avataram, kaaliy mardanam, raasakreedaa, kansavdham, swayamvaram, banyudhm, vividhvadham, swargarohanam naamak kathaayen aati hai, jinaki prastuti aath dinon tak chalati he. 
 krishnanattem main nritt pradhan abhinay kaa mahatv he. krishnanattem main lok nrity parampara kaa prabhaav lakshith hota he. gayak shloke or pad gaate hai or abhinayta unki taal - lay par nrity ko pradhanta dete hue abhinay karte hai. is kii prastuti main topi maddal, shuddh maddal, idkka aadi vaadyantron kaa prayog hota he. topi maddal satvik veshon kii prastuti ke liye tathaa shuddh maddal asuru veshon kii prastuti ke liye prayukt kiye jate hai. chempata, chempa, atanta, panchari aadi taalon par kiya janevaala nrity 'krishnanattem' kii visheshta he. 
 kaha jaataa hai ki kathakali ke prastutakarta kottarakkara tampuran ne bhi krishnanattem se prerana lee thi. kinvadnti he ki jab tampuran apane yahan krishnanattem sangh ko amantrit kiya to samutiri ne asahmati prakat kii. isi se naaraaz hokar kottarakkara tampuran ne ramnattem likhaa. krishnanattem kaa adhik prachaar nahin he. guuruvayur mandir main bhakt manauti ke rup main krishnanattem kaa aayojan karavaate hai. isi kaaran yah kala aaj bhi jivit he. aisa vishwaas he vishesh din kii katha manauti ke rup main prastut karaane se abhisht siddhi hoti he. putr prapti keliye 'avatar', vivaah sampann hone keliye 'swayamvar', striyon ke aishwarya keliye 'raasakreedaa', shatrusanhaar keliye 'kansavadh', daridray mukti keliye 'vivid vadh', sarpkope shamit karne keliye 'kaliumrdan', mangalakaarya prapti ke liye 'banyuddh' aadi prastut karawaaye jate hai. aisa niyam he ki 'swargarohan' akele nahin kiya janaa chahie. uske saath 'avatar' ko bhi prastut karwaanaa chahie. 'swargarohan' katha kii prastuti se kisi vishesh prayojan ke siddh hone kaa koi nirdesh nahin miltaa he.
