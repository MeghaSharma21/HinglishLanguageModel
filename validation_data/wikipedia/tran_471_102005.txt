 hundai motor company, ek dakshin korian bahurashtriya motor vaahan nirmaataa company he, jiskaa mukhyalay tseol, dakshin korea main he. company kii sthapana 1967 main hui thi or isaki 32.8% swaamitv vaali sahaayak company kia motors or isaki 100% swaamitv vaali sahaayak sahaayak company jenesis motors ko mila kar, hundai motor samooh sthapit kii gai thi. yah duniya main teesraa sabase bada vaahan nirmaataa company he.
 hundai, ulsaan, dakshin korea main duniya kii sabase badi ekikrut motarvahan nirmaan kaarkaana sanchalit karti he, jisaki vaarshik utpaadan kshamta 1.6 lakh ikai he. company duniyabar main kareeb 75,000 logon ko rojgaar detii he. hundai apane vaahanon ko 193 deshon main 5000 dilarship or showrooms ke madhyam se bechati he.
 hundai motor india limited bharat main vartmaan main doosraa sabase bada vaahan niryaatak he. yah bharat ko choti kaaron ke liye vaishvik vinirmaan kaa kendra bana rahi he.
 hundai bharat main kai utpaada bechati he, sabase lokapriy sentro jing, ai10, hundai ion or ai20 hai. 3 sitambar 2013 ko, hundai ne apane sabase safal car kaa petrol or dijal sanskarnon main nayaa utpaada grand ai10 jari kiya. iske anya utpaada main getz, accent, elantra, duusari peedi kii varanaa, santa fe or sonata transform shaamil hai. hundai kaa bharat ke tamilnadu rajya main shreeperambdur main do vinirmaan sanyantr sthit hai. donon sanyantron kii sanyukt vaarshik utpaadan kshamta 600,000 ikaayiyon kii he. varsh 2007 main, hundai ne hyderabad main apni arenddy suvidha ki shuruuaat kii, jisamen desh ke vibhinn hisson ke kareeb 450 ejeeniyar karyrat he.
 june 2017 main, bhartiya pratispardhaa ayog ne kaaron ke liye chhot pradaan karne ke sambandh main anuchit vyapaarik vyavhaaron ke liye 87 karod kaa company par jurmaana lagaaya.
