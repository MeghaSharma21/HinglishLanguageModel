 
 daali shahar dakshin-pashchimi china ke unnan praant ke daali bai swashasit vibhaag kii rajdhani he or ise china kii prashasnic pranali main ek 'prantiy-str ke nagar' kaa darja mila hua he.
 yah aitihaasik shahar bai logon ke pracheen naanjhaao rajya kii rajdhani bhi hua karata tha jo is kshetra main 8veen or 9veen sadiyon main apane charam par tha. iske baad 937 se 1252 isvi tak yah daali rajya kii rajdhani bhi rahaa. 1252 main kublaai khan kii phujon ne isapar hamalaa kar ke ise dhwast diya to shahar ke nivasee ise chhodkar bhaag gaye. 1382 main ming rajvansh kaal main ise dobaaraa sthapit kiya gaya. dakshini china ke is ilaake main bahut musalmaan based hue the jinhonen 1856-1863 ke kaal main panthai baghaavat naamak abhiyaan main china ke ching rajvansh se alag hokar swatantra hone kii asafal koshish kii thi. aadhunik kaal main 1925 main yahan ek 7.0 richter kii shakti kaa bukamp aayaa jis se is shahar ko kaafii kshati pahunchi.
 daali main sangemarmar kii khaane hai or yah china-bhar main itnaa mashhoor he ki sangemarmar ko chini bhashaa main 'daali-pathar' kaha jaataa he. yah shahar unnan kii rajdhani kunming se 40 minute kii udaan dur he. is shahar ke do hisse hai - purana daali or nayaa daali. purana daali ming rajvansh kaal main banaaya gaya tha. daali kaa ilaaka ek upjau pathaar par sthit he jiske pashchim main changshan shrunkhlaa ke pahaad sthit hai or purv main arhai jhil . yah kshetra parmparik rup se yi logon or bai logon kii mathrubhumi he. daali kaa mausam suhaavna he. shahar kaa taapmaana garmiyon main dopaher ko 25°centegrade tak chadh jaataa he or sardiyon main raat ko 2°centegrade tak gir jaataa he. adhiktar barish garmiyon main padati he.
