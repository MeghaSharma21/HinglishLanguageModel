 rajput - panchayat main is jati kii sankhya jhadakund gaanv main pai jaati he ye log bhi vartmaan main mehnat mazdooree karke apana jivan nirvaah kar rahe he. is jati ke log bhagwaan ram ko sarvaadhik maanate he vahiin sabhi devi devataaon ko bhi puujate he. 
 shiksha ke kshetra main jhonpada panchayat dhire dhire pragatisheel he. vartmaan main is panchayat kaa shiksha str 60.34 pratishat ke lagbhag he. panchayat kaa sabase bada vidhyalay jhonpada gaanv main sthit he jo 12veen tak he. jhonpada panchayat main 12 veen tak kaa vidhyalay he. 12veen kaksha ke baad vidhyaarthee savai madhopur, chouth kaa barwada main padhane ke liye jate hai. gaanv main shiksha ke kshetra main sabase pichhadaa hua gaanv naharighurd evam jhadakund he. gaanv main sabase pehle meth metics vishay main padhai karne vaale vyakti shri ramdhan minaa ai.tii.ai.instractor hai. gaanv ke sabase pehle sarkari karmchaariyon main motilal ji meena evam bhajanlaal ji meena kaa naam miltaa he. sirohi gaanv main paanchavin kaksha ke baad vidhyaarthee jhonpada gaanv main adhyayan hetu aate he. gram panchayat jhonpada main nimna vidhyalay karyrat he :- • rajkeey ucch madhyamik vidhyalay, jhonpada • rajkeey ucch praathmik vidhyalay, bagina • rajkeey praathmik vidhyalay, sirohi • rajkeey praathmik vidhyalay, nahari khurd&jhadakund • rajkeey praathmik sanskrit vidhyalay, jhonpada
  - gram panchayat main do upaswasthya kendra he jo panchayat ke jhonpada evam bagina gaanv main sthit he. - jhonpada gram panchayat ke jhonpada, bagina, naharighurd evam jhadakund gaanv chouth kaa barwada police stesan ke antargat aate he vahiin panchayat kaa sirohi gaanv navnirmit surwal police stesan ke antargat aata he. vaise dekha jaae to chouth kaa barwada police stesan hi gaanv kaa sabase najdiki thana he or sirohi gaanv ke liye surwal thana kaa safar dugana he. - gram panchayat jhonpada main teen anganbari kendra karyrat he inamen se do anganbari kendra jhonpada gaanv main or ek anganbari kendra bagina gaanv main sthit he. - jhonpada panchayat ke sarpanch chunaavon kii matganana jhonpada gaanv main hi kii jaati he evam bagina,naharighurd, sirohi evam jhadakund gaanvon kii voting bhi jhonpada gaanv main hi kii jaati he. chaaron gaanvon kaa poling booth jhonpada gaanv main sthit he. - gram panchayat kii sabhi vidyalayon kaa nodel kendra jhonpada gaanv kii rajkeey ucch madhyamik vidhyalay ko banaaya gaya he. - gram panchayat main paanch saamudayik bhawan bane hue he in main teen saamudayik bhawan jhonpada gaanv main, ek bagina gaanv main or ek saamudayik bhawan sirohi gaanv main sthit he.
 gram jhonpada jila mukhyalay savai madhopur se 32 kilometre pashchim disha kii ore padataa he gram jhonpada ke liye savai madhopur jile se post offis ke saamne se sidhe bas suvidha uplabdh he jila mukhyalay savai madhopur se bas nimna gaanvon main hoti hui jaati he savai madhopur, chakachinpura, karmonda, surwal, sinoli, bandhaa, bhagavatgarh, sirohi evam jhonpada. savai madhopur se surwal tak bas mega haive par chalati he baad main surwal se jhonpada ke liye gramin rod ja rahaa he !gram jhonpada tehsil mukhyalay chouth kaa barwada se 12 kilometre uttar disha kii ore padataa he ! chouth kaa barwada se bhaidola gaanv hote hue jagamonda hokar pakke sadak maarg se jhonpada gaanv pahunchaa ja saktaa he. jaipur se jhonpada gaanv kii duuri 103 kilometre he.
