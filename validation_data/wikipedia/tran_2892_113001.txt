 bhartiya ispaat praadhikaran ) bharat kii sarvaadhik ispaat utpaadan karne vaali company he. yah purnatah ekikrut lohe or ispaat kaa saamaan taiyaar karti he. company main gharelu nirmaan, ejiniyari, bijli, railway, motargadi or suraksha udyogon tathaa niryaat bazaar main bikri ke liye mul tathaa vishesh, donon tarah ke ispaat taiyaar kiye jate hai. yah bharat sarkaar kii purna-swaamitv praadhikaran he.
 yah vyapaar ke hisaab se desh main saarvajanik kshetra kii sabase badi 10 companiyon main se ek he. cell anek prakaar ke ispaat ke saamaan kaa utpaadan or unki bikri karti he. inamen hot tathaa cold rold shiten or coil, jasta chadhi shit, vaidyut shit, sanrachanaayen, railway utpaada, plate baar or rod, steneles steel tathaa anya mishra dhaatu ispaat shaamil hai. cell apane paanch ekikrut ispaat kaarkaanon or teen vishesh ispaat kaarkaanon main lohe or ispaat kaa utpaadan karti he. ye kaarkaane desh ke purvi or kendriya kshetra main sthit hai tathaa inke paas hi kachche maal ke gharelu strot uplabdh hai. in sroton main company kii louh ayask, chuunaa-pathar or dolomite khaane shaamil hai. company ko bharat kaa doosraa sabase bada louh ayask utpaadak hone kaa shrey bhi praapt he. iske paas desh main doosraa sabase bada khaanon kaa jaal he. company ke paas apane louh ayask, chuunaa-pathar or dolomite khaane hai jo ispaat nirmaan ke liye mahatvapurn kachche maal hai. isse company ko pratiyogita main laabh mil rahaa he.
 cell kaa anterrashtriya vyapaar divijan iesao 9001: 2000 se pramanit he. iskaa karyaalay nai dilli main he or yah cell ke paanch ekikrut ispaat kaarkaanon se mridul ispaat utpaadon tathaa kachche lohe kaa niryaat karata he.
 cell kaa armbh bharat kii swatantrata ke saath hua tha. swatantrata milane ke pashchaat raashtra nirmaataaon ne desh ke teevr audyogikeekaran ke liye aadharbhoot suvidhaayen jutaney kii pariklpana kii. ispaat kshetra ko aarthik vikaas kaa saadhan mana gaya. in kaarnon ke chalate 19 janvari, 1954 ko hindustan steel pra.li. kii sthapana kii gai.
 armbh main hindustan steel ko rourkela main lagaae ja rahe ek ispaat kaarkaane kaa prabandh karne ke liye gathit kiya gaya tha. bhilai or durgapur ispaat kaarkaanon ke liye praathmik kaary lohe or ispaat mantralay ne kiya tha. april 1957 main in do ispaat kaarkaanon kaa niyantran va kaary kii dekharekh bhi hindustan steel ko saunp diya gaya. hindustan steel kaa panjikrut karyaalay armbh main nai dilli main tha. 1956 main ise kalakatta or 1959 main ranchi le jaaya gaya.
 bhilai or rourkela ispaat kaarkaanon kii das lakh ton kshamta kaa charan disambar, 1961 main puraa kiya gaya. durgapur ispaat kaarkaane kii das lakh ton kshamta kaa charan wheel evam eksal sanyantr ke chaalu hone ke baad janvari, 1962 main puraa hua. iske saath hi hindustan steel limited kii kaccha ispaat utpaadan kshamta 1 lakh 58 hajaar ton se badhkar 16 lakh ton ho gai. bokaro ispaat kaarkaane ke nirmaan or parichaalan ke liye janvari, 1964 main bokaro steel limited ke naam se ek nai company kaa nigaman kiya gaya. bhilai ispaat kaarkaane kaa doosraa charan vir rod mil chaalu hone ke saath hi sitambar, 1967 main puraa kiya gaya. rourkela kii 18 lakh ton kshamta kii antim unit-tendom mil, farvari, 1968 main chaalu hui tathaa durgapur ispaat kaarkaane kaa 16 lakh ton kshamta kaa charan steel melting shaap main bhatti chaalu hone ke baad agast, 1969 main puraa kiya gaya. bhilai main 25 lakh ton, rourkela main 18 lakh ton tathaa durgapur main 16 lakh ton ke charan puure hone ke saath hi hindustan steel limited kii kul kaccha ispaat utpaadan kshamta 1968-69 main badhkar 37 lakh ton or 1972-73 main 40 lakh ton ho gai.
 ispaat tathaa khan mantralay ne udyog ke prabandhan ke liye ek nayaa model taiyaar karne ke vaaste nitigat vaktavy taiyaar kiya. 2 disambar 1972 ko yah niti vaktavy sansad main pesh kiya gaya. iske aadhaar par kachche maal or utpaadan kaa kaary ek hi ke adheen laane ke liye dharak company ke siddhanth ko aadhaar banaaya gaya. parinamswaroop, steel athurity of india limited kaa gathan kiya gaya. 24 janvari 1973 ko nigamit is company kii adhikrut puunji 20 arab ru. thi tathaa ise bhilai, bokaro, durgapur, rourkela or burnpur main paanch ekikrut ispaat kaarkaane tathaa durgapur sthit mishra ispaat kaarkaana or selem ispaat kaarkaane ke liye uttaradaayi banaaya gaya. 1978 main cell kaa punargathan kiya gaya or ise ek parichaalan company banaaya gaya.
 apane gathan ke baad se hi cell desh main audyogic vikaas ke liye ek sudrudh aadhaar taiyaar karne main sahaayak siddh hui he. iske atirikt isane takneekee tathaa prabandhkeey visheshgyata ke vikaas main bhi mahatvapurn yoga diya he. cell ne upbhog karne vaale udyogon ko nirantar kaccha maal uplabdh kara kar aarthik vikaas kii anek prakriyaayein prarambh kii hai.
 ek sammanit vishwa stareey pratishthan banane ke saath-saath bhartiya ispaat vyavsaay main gunvattta, utpaadakta, laabhpradata or upabhokta santushti ke kshetra main agrani rahanaa.
 steel athurity of india limited or neshnal thermal power karporation limited ke bich 50:50 kaa yah sanyukt udhyam rourkela, durgapur or bhilai nijee bijligharon kaa prabandhan karata he. isaki sanyukt kshamta 314 megawatt kii he. isane 500 megawatt kii atirikt kshamta kaa bhilai main ek bijlighar sthapit kiya he. pehali unit se bijli kaa utpaadan apairal, 2009 main shuru ho gaya or duusari unit se 2009 tak vanijyik utpaadan shuru hone kii aashaa he.
 cell or damodar vaili karporshan ke is 50:50 sanyukt udhyam kii sthapana janvari, 2002 main hui thi. yah bokaro ispaat kaarkaane main 302 megawatt vidyut utpaadan or 1880 ton prati ghante steam utpaadan suvidhaon kaa prabandhan kar rahaa he. bipiessiel ne bokaro main 2Ã—250 megawatt kshamta kaa koyala aadhaarit taap bijlighar sthapit karne kaa prastaav kiya he. iske atirikt bokaro main 9wein boyler or 36 megawatt back pressure terbo jenerator pariyojna kii sthapana kaa kaary kiya ja rahaa he.
 cell or tata steel ke 50:50 aadhaar par sthapit yah sanyukt udhyam ispaat main i-commers gatividhiyon or use jude kshetron ko badhaava detaa he. company dwaara uplabdh karai jane vaali nai suvidhaon main i-parisampatti-bikri, aayojan tathaa sammelan, koyala bikri or logisticks, prakaashan ityadi shaamil hai.
 cell ne bmdablyoo industries limited ke saath 40:60 aadhaar par bokaro main sarvice centre sthapit karne ke liye sanyukt udhyam kaa gathan kiya he. iskaa uddeshy ispaat main upabhokta jaruraton ke anuroop sateek utpaadon ke liye gunvattta main vruddhi karnaa he.
 cell ne macers jaiprakash associats limited ke saath slag par aadhaarit 22 lakh ton kshamta kaa ek cement kaarkaana bhilai main sthapit karne ke liye ek sanyukt udhyam banaaya he. yah company march, 2010 se bhilai main cement kaa utpaadan prarambh kar degi. satna main clinkar kaa utpaadan 2009 main shuru ho jayega.
 cell ne macers jaiprakash associats limited ke saath bokaro main slag par aadhaarit 21 lakh ton kshamta kaa ek cement kaarkaana sthapit karne ke liye ek sanyukt udhyam banaaya he. aashaa he ki nirmaan kaary 2009 main or cement kaa utpaadan july, 2011 tak prarambh ho jayega.
 cell ne mangneej ore limited ke saath nandini/bhilai main 1 lakh ton kshamta kaa phero mangneej or silico mangneej kaarkaana sthapit karne ke liye ek sanyukt udhyam banaaya hai.
 cell ne koyale ke block/khaanon ke adhikaran tathaa vikaas ke liye tata steel ke saath ek sanyukt udhyam company banaai he. sanyukt udhyam company koking koyale kii supplai praapt karne ke liye nae swadeshi avasaron kii talash main he.
 koking koyale ke kshetra main saarvajanik kshetra ke ispaat udhyamon ko aatmanirbhar banane ke uddeshy se ek sanyukt udhyam banaaya gaya he. is udhyam main cell, rashtriya ispaat nigam limited, kol india limited, antipisi limited or enemdisi limited shaamil hai. company australia, mozambik tathaa anya chune hue deshon main uplabdh koyale ke gunon kii jaanch kar rahi he.
