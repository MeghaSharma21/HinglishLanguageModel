 bhartiya parampara main siddhanth kaa arth 'parampara' yaa 'darshan' se he. bhartiya darshan main kisi sampradaay ke sthapit evam sweekrut vichaar darshan kehalaate hai.
 siddhanth, 'siddhi kaa ant' he. yah vah dharna he jise siddh karne ke liye, jo kuch hamen karnaa tha vah ho chukaa he, or ab sthir mat apnaane kaa samay aa gaya he. dharm, vigyaan, darshan, niti, raajnneeti sabhi siddhant kii apeksha karte hai.
 dharm ke sambandh main ham samajhate hai ki buddhi, ab aage aa nahin sakati; shankaa kaa sthaan vishwaas ko lenaa chahie. vigyaan main samajhate hai ki jo khoj ho chuki he, vah vartmaan sthiti main paryaapt he. ise aage chalaane kii aavashyaktaa nahin. pratishtha kii avasthaa ko ham peeche chhod aae hai, or siddh niyam ke avishkaar kii sambhaavna dikhaai nahin detii. darshan kaa kaam samast anubhav ko gathit karnaa he; darshanik siddhant samgr kaa samaadhaan he. anubhav se pare, iskaa aadhaar koi satta he yaa nahin? yadi he, to vah chetan ke avchetan, ek he yaa anek? aise prashn darshanik vivechan ke vishay hai.
 vigyaan or darshan main gyaan pradhan he, iskaa prayojan satta ke swaroop kaa jaananaa he. niti or raajnneeti main karm pradhan he. inkaa lakshya shubh yaa bhadra kaa utpann karnaa he. in donon main siddhant aisi manyata he jise vyavhaar kaa aadhaar bananaa chahie.
 dharm ke sambandh main teen pramukh manyataayen hai- ishwar kaa astitv, swadhinata, amartv. kaant ke anusaar buddhi kaa kaam prakatano kii duniyaan main simit he, yah in manyataon ko siddh nahin kar sakati, na hi inkaa khandan kar sakati he. krity buddhi inaki maang karti he; inhen niti main nihit samajhakar sweekaar karnaa chahie.
 vigyaan kaa kaam 'kya', 'kaise', 'kyon'- in teen prashnon kaa uttar dena he. tisare prashn kaa uttar tathyon kaa anusandhan he or yah badalata rahata he. darshan anubhav kaa samaadhaan he. anubhav kaa strot kya he? anubhavvaad ke anusaar sara gyaan baahar se praapt hota he, buddhivaad ke anusaar yah andar se nikalataa he, aalochanawad ke anusaar gyaan saamagree praapt hoti he, isaki akruti man kii dein he.
 niti main pramukh prashn 'ni:shreyas kaa swaroop' he. naitik vivaad bahut kuch bhoga ke sambandh main he. bhogavaadi sukh kii anubhuti ko jivan kaa lakshya samajhate hai; duusari ore kath upanishad ke anusaar shrey or prey do sarvatha bhinn vastuen hai.
 raajnneeti raashtra kii samuhik niti he. niti or raajnneeti donon kaa lakshya maanav kaa kalyaan he; niti bataati he ki iske liye samuhik ytn ko kya rup dhaaran karnaa chahie. ek vichaar ke anusaar maanav jati kaa itihaas swadhinata sangram kii katha he, or raashtra kaa lakshya yahi hona chahie ki vyakti ko jitani swadhinata di ja sake, di jaae. yah prajatantr kaa mat he. iske vipareet ek-duusare vichaar ke anusaar saamaajik jivan kii sabase badi kharabi vyaktiyon main sthiti kaa antar he; is bhed ko samaapt karnaa raashtra kaa lakshya he. kathinaayee yah he ki swadhinata or barabari donon ek saath nahin chalatin. sansar kaa vartmaan khinchaav in donon kaa sangram hi he.
