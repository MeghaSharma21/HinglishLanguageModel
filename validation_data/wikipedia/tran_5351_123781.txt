 kulottung chol pratham dakshin bharat ke chol rajya kaa prakhyat shaasha tha. yah vengi ke chalukyanaresh rajraj narendra kaa putr tha or iskaa naam rajendra tha. iskaa vivaah cholvansh kii rajkumaari madhurantka se hua tha jo virarajendra kii bhatiji thi. yah vengi rajya kaa vaid adhikari tha kintu paariwarik vaimnasy ke kaaran virarajendra ne rajendra ke chachaa vijayaditya ko adheenta sweekaar karne kii shart par rajya praapt karne main sahaayta kii. is prakaar yah vengi kaa apana paitrik rajya praapt na kar sakaa. kintu kuch varshon baad virarajendra kaa uttaradhikari or putr adhirajendra ek janavidroh main maaraa gaya tab chalukya rajendra ne chol rajya ko hathiyaa liya or kulottung ke naam se iskaa shaasha bana. tab isane apane paitruk rajya vengi se vijayaditya ko nikaal baahar kiya or apane putron ko vahaan kaa shaasha banaakar bheja.
 kulottung kii gananaa chol ke mahaan nareshon main kii jaati he. abhilekhon or anushrutiyon main uskaa ullekh sangamatvirtt ke rup main hua he. uske shasankaal kaa adhikansh bhaag adbhut safalataa or samruddhi kaa tha. uski niti thi anaavashyak yuddh na kiya jaay or unase bachaa jaae. parinamswaroop srilanka ko chhodkar chol samrajya ke saare pradesh 1115 i. tak uske adheen bane rahe. use mukhya rup se virarajendra ke damaad kalyani ke chalukya naresh vikramaditya se nirantar sangharsh karnaa padaa. iske kaaran uske antim dinon main chol rajya kii sthiti kaaphi dayaneey ho gai or vah tamil desh or telegu ke kuch bhaagon main hi simat kar rah gaya.
