 paavan dham paavan dham : jaipur se pehle viratnagar naamak ek qasbaa aata he. yah vahi sthaan he jahaan pandvo ne agyatavaas ke dauraan apana jivan vyateet kiya tha. yaheen panchkhand parvat par bharat varsh kaa sabase anokhaa or ekmaatr hanuman mandir he jahaan hanuman ji kii bina bandar or puch vaali murti sthapit he. iskaa naam vajrang mandir he or isaki sthapana amar hutatma gobhakt, mahaan swatantraaa senaanee, yashashvi lekhak, hindu sant, hindu netaa shriman mahatma ramachandra vir ji ne kii thi. 
 == vajrang prabhu kaa bhimgiri parvat par avataran == samarth guru ramdaas maharaj kii pavitra padukaao ko guru maanakar divya diksha grahan karne vaale shriman mahatma ramachandra vir maharaj ne bhaktavatsal bhagwaan shri vajrang dev hanuman prabhu kii prerana se, apni janmabhumi viratnagar ke pavitra bhimgiri parvat ke panchkhand shikhar par unke shri vigrah kii pratishthapana kii to na keval papaacharo se suukh gaye bhimlata kund ke pashaani tal se jaldhara foot padi, varan mahapratapi shri bhimsen maharaj kaa parvat parampavan tirthdham ban gaya. satya to yah he kii apane anuj pavanputra kunti nandan bhimsen ke priy parvat kaa uddhaar karne ke liye pawan saputra anjanaanandan shri vajrang dev hanuman mahaprabhu bhimgiri parvat ke shikhar par aakar virajman hue. vishwa main yahi ekmaatr tirth he jahaan donon pawan suputron kii puja hoti he, samvath 1993 vikrami main parvat sikhar par laghu rup main viraaje bhagwaan shri vajrang dev kaa vikrami samvath 2001 main ek sau man ke vishaal shrivigrah main parvatarohan aisa avishwasaneey, vismayjanak, chamatkaarpurn prasang he jo swayan mahaprabhu kii ichha or inke laadle bhakt mahatma ramachandra vir maharaj ke mahaan sankalp ke bina sambhav nahin tha. mahatma ramachandra vir ji ne apane jivan kaal main hi apane yashasvi putr acharya dharmendra ji ko paavan dham viraat nagar kaa san 1978 main panchkhand pithadishvar niyukt kar diya tha. vajrang mandir se jab pahaad par se niche aate he to waha par bhim talab sthit he. 
  yah bhim ke pair ke panje ke aakar kaa he. agyatvaas ke dauraan pandav viratnagar main maharaj viraat ke yahan ruke the.
