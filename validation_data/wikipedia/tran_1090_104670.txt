 sanskaar bharti, rashtriya swayamsevak sangh kii ek anusaangik sanstha he. isaki sthapana lalit kala ke kshetra main rashtriya chetana laane kaa uddeshy saamne rakhkar kii gayi thi. isaki prishthbhumi main bhaurav devaras, haribhaau vakankar, nanaaji deshmukh, madhavrao devle or yogendra ji jaise manishiyon kaa chintan tathaa athak parishram tha. 1954 se sanskaar bharti kii pariklpana viksit hoti gayi or 1981 main lakhanu main isaki bidhivat sthapana hui. 1988 main phalgun shukla paksh kii ekadshi ko mirjapur ikai kaa gathan kiya gaya. sa kala yaa vimuktaye arthath "kala vah he jo buraaiyon ke bandhan katkar mukti pradaan karti he" ke ghosh-vaakya ke saath aaj deshbhar main sanskaar bharti kii 1200 se adhik ikaiyaan kaary kar rahi hai.
 samaaj ke vibhinn virgon main kala ke dwaara rashtrabhakti evam yogya sanskaar jagaane, vibhinn kalaon kaa prashikshan va navdith kalakaaron ko protsaahan dekar inke madhyam se saanskrithik pradooshan rokane ke uddeshy se sanskaar bharti kaary kar rahi he. 1990 se sanskaar bharti ke vaarshik adhiveshan kala sadhak sangam ke rup main yojit kiye jate hai jinamein sangeet, naatak, chitrakala, kavy, sahitya or nrity vidhaon se jude deshbhar ke sthapit va navdith kalakaar apni kala kaa pradarshan karte hai.
 bhartiya samskruti ke utkrisht mulyon kii pratishtha karne kii drushti se rashtriya geet pratiyogita, krishna rup-sajja pratiyogita, rashtrabhaavna jagaane vaale nukkad naatak, nrity, rangoli, menhadi, chitrakala, kavy-yatra, sthaan-sthaan par rashtriya kavi sammelan aadi bahuvidh kaaryakramon kaa aayojan sanskaar bharti dwaara kiya jaataa he. sanskaar bharti prativarsh rashtriya swayamsevak sangh dwaara manaaye jane vaale chah utsavon ko bhi manaati he.deshbhar main sanskaar bharti kii 1200 se adhik ikaiyaan kaary kar rahi hai.

 nav sanvatsar swaagat – is din brahmaji ne sristi nirmaan kaa muhoort kiya. shriram kaa rajbhishek va samraat shantivaahan kaa shakon par vijay kaa yah din. atah yah vijay diwas bhi he. aaj prakruti kaa saudarya rup parivartan kaa din, desh kii asmita, sthirataa va swatv nirmaan main aatmasakti kaa jaagran he. srijan kii kala kaa dharm he. atah ‘sanskaar bharti’ main is diwas kii mahatvapurn bhoomika he.
 gurupurnima – bharat main guru parampara sabhi kshetron main he. gyaan-vigyaan-aadhyatm, kala main nataraj satya hi shiv or shiv hi sundar kaa darshan dy tandav vinaash ke liye or lasya sthai bhav main srijan va param shanti kaa dyotak he.hamaara nataraj. guru pujan kalakaar ke liye shreykar he. anu main nartan or shiv kaa nartan donon ek hi he. jinamein taal, gati or sthirataa bhi he. shiv kala jagat main guru kii mahati pradhanta suspasht karne waal yah apana pramukh utsav he. sangeet, nrity, natya ke aadi kala guru nataraj shiv, pratham kavy praneta balmiki evam gyaan vigyaan ke rachayita vedavyas kii parampara aadi kala guruon ko sammaan pradaan karnaa he.
 shrikrishna janmashtami – shrikrishna 16 kalaon ke gyaata he, purna purush hai. unhen natvarlaal bhi kaha gaya he. raasalila, garba, murali vaadan yah sabhi samaaj jaagran or samaaj sanghathan ke liye kiya. samaaj ke chote se chote ghatak ko joda. shrikrishna shikshak or sanghathan ke drushti se adarsh hai. unke vividh roopon kaa darshan, pradarshan, bal, gita darshan yah vishuddh adbhut udhaaharan he.anek roopon ke darshan ke baad bhi tatv rup, sthai bhav rakhaa. kala se jivan main shuddhta laane kaa maarg unke jivan se miltaa he. ishwartv kaa saakshaatkaar tanman kii vishuddhta kaa yah parv dyotak he. samaaj jivan main kalakaar tathaa kaaryakarta se yahi apekshit he.
 deepawali parivaar mela – saamaajik sthirataa main paariwarik yogadan kaa sabase bada mahatv he. kalakaar va kaaryak kaa jivan paariwarik bhav sthir ho, yah atyant aavashyak he. hamaari paddati bhi sabhi ko saath lekar chalane kii paariwarik paddati he. andhkaar se prakaash, agyaan se gyaan, kutumb vyavastha kaa saamanjasy, khushiyon kaa samuhik aadan-pradaan kaa deepawali parv ek pratik he. samanjasy, saahchary, ekatm darshan yah paavan sandesh ‘sanskaar-bharti’ parivaar main bhi vishist bhoomika kaa parichaayak he.
 bharat mata pujan – ‘sanskaar bharti’ ne is utsav ko aadarsho se joda he. bharat mata ke chitra darshan ko viraat rup main saamne laana. sab devon se badi he hamaari bharat maan. kalakaar – kaaryakarta kaa jivan adarsh he hamaari ‘bharat maan’. aaj ke din samvidhan kaa nirmaan hua, isamen swatv kaa bodh hota he. apana dharm apni bhashaa, chinh, pratik, vidhaan, samvidhan or apni asmita kaa rashtravyapee prabodh. yah ‘vande maataram’ main bhi bankimachandra ji ne kaha he. bharat maan saakshaat durga – dashapraharandharini, kamala kamaldal viharini yah shuddhta kaa pratik tathaa vaani vidyaadaayini he. yah saraswati kaa saakshaat rupadarshan he. atah kalakaar ke liye maan bharti kaa pujan sabhi devon kaa sujan he.
 bharatmuni smriti diwas – kalakshetra ke praneta bharmuni ke smriti kaa yah aayojan he. natya shaastra aadigranth he. natyakala lok dharm ke paripaalan ke liye patibaddh he. yah vyavsaay nahin samaaj prabodhan kaa mahatvapurn anushthan he kalaon main samanvay, sahitya, kala, sangeet, vaastu, rang tathaa prakruti kaa mul shrot bhi natya kala main nihit he. natya yah jivan kaa vishuddh pratibimb he. bharatmuni ne kaha, “brahmaji ke charnon main sutara banaaye gayi. shiv ji ne unhen abhinit kiya. maine lipibaddh kiya. meraa apana kuch nahin”. yah darshan bharatmuni ne diya. jo guru ke prati ek adarsh kalakaar se apekshit he. yah kalakaar – kaaryakarta kaa paavan poojya parv he.
