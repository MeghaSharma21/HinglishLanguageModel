 
 Quisqualis indica L.
 madhumalati ek vanaspati he jo kshup yaa lata ke rup main hoti he. yah caprifoliasi vansh main aati he. isaki lagbhag 180 prajatiyaan gyaat hai. inamen se lagbhag 100 prajatiyaan china main, 20 bharat main, 20 europe main, tathaa 20 uttari america main paayi jaati hai. isaki sabase prasiddh prajatiyaan hai- lonicera japonica, lonicera periclimenum, tathaa lonicera semparvirence . madhumalika kii kuch prajatiyon ke pushpon kii ore gunjan pakshi bahut aakarshith hote hai. 
 iskaa naam 'madhumalati' isaliye sarthak he kyonki iske pushpon se khaadya madhu nikaala ja saktaa he. asia ke alaava vishwa ke kai anya bhaagon main yah soundarya hetu ugaayi gayi yaa swatah faili hui jhaadi ke rup main bhi paayi jaati he.
