 jodhpur riyasat marwar kshetra main 1250 se 1949 tak chali riyasat thi. isaki rajdhani varsh 1950 se jodhpur nagar main rahi.
 lagbhag 90,554 kimi2 kshetrafal ke saath, jodhpur riyasat raajapootaana kii sabase badi riyasat thi. iske antim shaasha ne iske bharat main vilay par 1 navambar 1956 ko hastakshar kiye.
 shaskaon kii bhartiya saamanti rajya ke jodhpur ke the, ek pracheen rajvansh kii sthapana 8 veen sadi main. haalanki, is rajvansh kii kismat dwaara kiye gaye the, raav jodha, pehle ke shaskaon ke rathod rajvansh main jodhpur main 1459.
 rajya ne chandrasen rathod kii mrityu ke pashchaat, akbar ke rajya ke dauraan mughal samrajya kii sahaayta kii. 17veen sadi ke uttararddh main aurangzeb ke samrajya main sakht niyantraran ke baavjood, rathod parivaar kii is kshetra main ardh-swaittata jari rahi. 1830 ke dashak tak rajya par koi britani prabhaav nahin tha lekin iske pashchaat maan singh ke samay rajya sahaayak gathbandhan kaa hissaa bana or marwar ke raja deshi riyasat ke rup main shashan karte rahe.
 varsh 1947 main bharat kii aazaadee ke samay jodhpur rajya ke antim shaasha maharaja hanvant singh ne bharat main vilay ke prastaav par hastakshar karne main deree kar di. yahan tak kii wo alparup se pakistan main vilay kaa sanket de chuke the, chunki jodhpur kii seemaa pakistan se lagati he or mohammad ali jinna ne vyaktigat rup se unhen pakistan ke bandargah kaam main lene ke liye vishwaas dilaayaa tha. antatah wo apane rajya kaa bharat adhirajya main vilay ke liye sahamat ho gayi lekin antim samay ke naatakeey ghatnakram se pehle nahin.
