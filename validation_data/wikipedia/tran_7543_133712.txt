 arthshaastra, upyogita kii vidya he. upyogita ke antargat logon kii praathamikta, vastuon se milane vaali santushti, vastuon ko logon dwaara pradutt mahatv aadi aate hai. icchaayen anant hai or iske saath yah bhi satya he ki ek hi vastu kii adhikadhik maatra milane par logon ko usase kramash: nune-se-newnatar laabh yaa santushti milti he.
