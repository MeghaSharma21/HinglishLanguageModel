 nariwad rajnatik andolan kaa ek saamaajik siddhant he jo striyon ke anubhavon se janit he. haalaaki mul rup se yah saamaajik sambandho se anuprerit he lekin kai strivadi vidwaan kaa mukhya jor langik asamanta or auraton ke adhikaar ityadi par zyada bal dete hai. 
 nariwadi siddhanto kaa uddeshy langik asamanta kii prakruti evam kaarnon ko samajhnaa tathaa iske falswaroop paidaa hone vaale langik bhedbhaav kii raajnneeti or shakti santulan ke siddhanto par iske asar kii vyakhya karnaa he. stri vimarsh sambandhi rajnatik prachaaron kaa jor prajnan sambandhi adhikaar, gharelu hinsa, maatrutiv avkaash, samaan vetan sambandhi adhikaar, yon utpeedan, bhedbhaav evam yon hinsaper rahata he. 
 strivadi vimarsh sambandhi adarsh kaa mul kathy yahi rahata he ki kanoonee adhikaron kaa aadhaar ling na bane. 
 aadhunik strivadi vimarsh kii mukhya aalochana hameshaa se yahi rahi he ki iske siddhant evam darshan mukhya rup se pashchimi mulyon evam darshan par aadhaarit rahe hai. haalaaki jamini str par strivadi vimarsh har desh evam bhugolic simaaon me apane stra par sakriy rahati hai or har kshetra ke strivadi vimarsh kii apni khaas samasyaayen hoti hai.
