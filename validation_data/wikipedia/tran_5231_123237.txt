 netravigyaan, chikitsavigyaan kaa vah ang he jo aankh kii rachana, karypranali, uski bimariyon tathaa chikitsa se sambadhit he. netrachikitsa, chikitsa vyavsaay kaa ek pradhan mahatvapurn ang samajha janaa chahie. netra jivan ke liye anivaary to nahin, kintu iske bina maanav sharir ke astitv kaa mulya kuch nahin rahata. aise ang kii jivan paryant raksha kaa prabandh rakhana rogi, uske parichaayak evam chikitsak kaa punit kartavya hona chahie.
 yah bahut hi purana vigyaan he, jiskaa varnan atharvaveda main bhi miltaa he. sushrutsamhita, sanskrit bhashaa kii anupam kriti he, jisamen aankh kii bimariyon tathaa uni chikitsa kaa sabase praarambhik vivaran miltaa he. sushrut, ayurveda shaastra ke pratham shalyachikitsak the, jinhone vivaranpurvak or purnat: aankh kii utpatti, rachana, karypranali, bimariyon tathaa unki chikitsa ke vishay main likhaa he, yah netravigyaan ke lekh "sushrutsamhita" ke "uttaratantra" ke 1-19 tak adhyay main sammilit he. isamen palken kajanktiva, swalera, cornea lens or kalapani ityadi kaa vivaran miltaa he. motiyobind kaa sabase pehle aaparishan karne kaa shrey shalya chikitsak sushrut ko praapt he.
 netra sharir kii paanch vishesh indriyon main se ek indriya he, jiske do bhaag hote hai :
 1. sahaayak ang, jo aankh ke gole ko sahaayta dete or surakshit rakhte hai.
 2. dekhne kaa ang, jisamen ve bhaag hote hai jinamein dekhne kii shakti hoti he.
 aankh kaa vishesh kaary dekhne kaa he. iske liye yah aavashyak he ki aankh kii golakaar akruti sthaayee rup se bani rahe tathaa netra ke ve sab aantarik ang jinase kirane guzarati hai, paardarshak bane rahen. netrod tathaa vitrius golakaar akruti ko sthaayee rup main rakhte hai. cornea, netrod, lens tathaa vitrius netra ke ve aantarik ang hai jinase kirane guzarati hai or drushtipatal par kisi vastu kii chaaya banane main sahaayta karti hai. aankh ke is kaary ko samajhane ke liye, isaki tulana camere se kii ja sakati he. jis prakaar mamera chaaron ore se kaala hota he tathaa chitra khinchate samay kaale kapade se dhank liya jaataa he usee prakaar aankh ke drushti patal ke chaaron ore ek kaala pardaa hota he, jise pigment apithilium kehete hai. camere ke plate par achche photo banane ke liye yah aavashyak he ki camere ko focus kiya jaae, parantu aankh main yah kaary swayan hi lens dwaara hota rahata he, jo apni golai ko gata badhaakar kirnon ko drushtipatal par focus karne main sahaayta karata he. yadi ham chahen to camere ke chhed ko chota bada karke roshni kii maatra ko kam athwa adhik kar sakate hai. aankh main yah kaam putali dwaara kiya jaataa he, jo apane aap sikudakar choti athwa badi hoti rahati he.
 drushtipatal par jo chaaya banati he vah drushti-tantrika se hoti hui mastishk main drushticendre par jaakar banati he. yadi kisi prakaar kirane drushtipatal par ikatthi na hokar uske saamne athwa uske peeche ikatthi hon to isko vartandosh kehete hai, jo chashma lagane se thik ho jaati he. yadi kirane drushtipatal ke peeche ikatthi hon, to uttal lens lagaana chahie, jisase kirane or aage hi drushtipatal par ikatthi ho jaaen. isi prakaar yadi ye drushtipatal ke saamne ikatthi hon to avatal lens lagaana chahie, kirane or duuri par ictthi hokar chaaya drushtipatal par bana saken.
