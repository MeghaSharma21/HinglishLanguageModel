 indira gandhi nahar rajasthan kii pramukh nahar hai. iskaa purana naam "rajasthan nahar" tha. yah rajasthan pradesh ke uttar-pashchim bhaag main bahati he. 
 rajasthan kii mahatvakankshi indira gandhi nahar pariyojna se marusthaleey kshetra main chamatkaarik badlaav aa rahaa he or isse marubhumi main sinchai ke saath hi peyajal or audyogic kaaryo ke liebhi pani milane laga he.
 nahar nirmaan se purv logon ko kai mile dur se pine kaa pani laana padataa tha. lekin ab pariyojna ke antargat baarah sau kusek pani keval peyajal udyog, sena evam urja pariyojnaon ke liye arakshit kiya gaya he. visheshtaur se churu, sriganganagar, hanumangarh, bikaner, jaisalmer, barmer or nagaur jaise registani jilon ke nivaasiyon ko is pariyojna se peyajal suvidha uplabdh karaane ke prayaas jari hai.
 rajasthan nahar satluj or vyas nadiyon ke sangam par nirmit harike baandh se nikaali gai he. yah nahar punjab va rajasthan ko pani kii aapurti karti he.punjab main is nahar kii lambaai 132 kilometre he or vahaan ise rajasthan feader ke naam se janaa jaataa he. isse is kshetra main sinchai nahin hoti he balki peyajal kii uplabdhi hoti he. rajasthan main is nahar kii lambaai 470 kilometre he. rajasthan main is nahar ko raaj canal bhi kehete hai.rajasthan nahar isaki mukhya shakha yaa main canal 256 kilometre lambii he jabaki vitarikaayen 5606 kilometre or iskaa sinchit kshetra 19.63 lakh hectare aanka gaya he. isaki menfider 204 kilometre lambii he jiskaa 35 kilometre hissaa rajasthan va 169 kilometre hissaa punjab va haryana main he.yah nahar rajasthan kii ek pramukh nahar he.
 is nahar kaa udghaatan 31 march 1958 ko hua jabaki do november 1984 ko iskaa naam indira gandhi nahar pariyojna kar diya gaya.
