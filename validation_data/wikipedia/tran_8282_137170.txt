 sanskrit bharti ek saanskrithik sanstha he jo sanskrit ko punah bolchaal kii bhashaa banane main sanlagn he. chamu krishna shastri ne samast vishwa main sanskritbhasha ko punerjeevit karne ke liye is sanstha sthapana kii. 
 sanskrit bharat kii ati pracheen bhashaa he kintu durbhaagya se aadhunik kaal main isaki upeksha kii ja rahi he. swa. babasahab apte sanskrit ke param agrahi the or swayamsevakon ko sanskrit seekhney tathaa iskaa prachaar karne ke liye prerit karte the. is kaaran anek swayamsevak is disha main karyrat hue or prantiya tathaa sthaneey str par bharat sanskrit parishad, swadhyay mandalm, vishwa sanskrit pratishthan ityadi anek kaary prarambh kiye gaye. farvari, 1996 main sanskrit ke kshetra main kaary karne vaale desh ke sabhi kaaryakarta dilli main ekatrit hue jahaan uparokt sansthaaon kaa vilay karke akhil bhartiya str par "sanskrit bharti' kii sthapana hui. 
 sanskrit bharti kaa mukhya uddeshy he - sanskrit kaa prachaar-prasar karnaa or sanskrit-sambhaashan sikhaakar ise phir se vyaavhaarik bhashaa bananaa. jab logon ke man main sanskrit ke prati prem jaagega to samskruti ke prati bhi swaabhaavik prem utpann hoga.
 sanskrit bharti dwaara "sanskrit-sambhaashan shivi" yojit kiye jate hai. inamen das din tak pratidin do ghante ke prashikshan dwaara balak-balikaayen sanskrit main sambhaashan karne yogya ho jate hai. sanskrit sambhaashan sikhaane ke liye sanskrit ke aacharyon ko prashikshan diya jaataa he. ab tak aise hajaaron aacharyon dwaara lakhon logon ko sanskrit-sambhaashan sikhaaya ja chukaa he. patrachaar dwaara sanskrit adhyayan kii yojana chaar bhashaaon main prarambh ho chuki he or hajaaron log iskaa laabh le chuke hai. desh ke sabhi praanton main sanskrit bharti kaa kaary prarambh ho chukaa he or lagbhag 300 purnakalik kaaryakarta isamen karyrat hai.
 sanskrit bharti dwaara kuch anya prayaas bhi kiye ja rahe hai jinamein pramukh hai- "sanskrit parivaar yojana" or "sanskrit gram yojana'. "sanskrit balkendram"' yojana ke dwaara bacchon ko khelon ke madhyam se sanskrit va samskruti kaa gyaan diya jaataa he. sanskrit bharti ke prayaason se karnataka ke mattuur jile ke ek gram main sanskrit aam bol-chaal kii bhashaa ban gai he.
