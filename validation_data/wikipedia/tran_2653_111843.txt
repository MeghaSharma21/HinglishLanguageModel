 tripura vishvavidhyalay bharat ke tripura rajya main sthit ek kendriya vishvavidhyalay he. isaki sthapana 2 oktober 1987 ko hui thi.
 yah agartala se 10 kimi dakshin main suryamaninagar main he. vishvavidhyalay kaa nirmaan kaary teji se hua tathaa rajya vidhansabha ne san 1987 main tripura vishvavidhyalay adhinium paarit kiya. 2 oktober mahatma gandhi kii janm punyatithi par tripura vishvavidhyalay aupchaarik rup se sthapit hua. 2006 ke sansad adhinium dwaara 2 july 2007 ko vishvavidhyalay ko kendriya vishvavidhyalay main parivartit kar diya gaya.
 vartmaan main tripura vishvavidhyalay 32 vishyon main snatakottar paathyakram uplabdh karaata he. iske antargat vibhinn vibhaag, nideshaalay or kendra hai jo jinamein 38 snaatak paathyakram uplabdh karaae jate hai. rajya main sabhi 24 saamaanya or takneekee college tripura vishvavidhyalay se sambhddha he jo ab rajya main ucch shiksha ke mukhya kendra hai tathaa sabhi kshetron main ise ucch sthaan praapt he.
 tripura kaa pratham uchy shikshan santhaan, em.bi.bi. mahavidhyalay tha jo kalakatta vishvavidhyalay se sambhddha hokar san 1947 se kaary karnaa shuru kiya. parantu haal ke vibhaajan ke parinamswarup tripura kii jansankhya main achaanak bahut adhik vikaas dekha gaya or is kaaran bahut badi sankhya main chaatr ucch shiksha praapt karne ke aakankshi hue.
 em.bi.bi .mahavidhyalay kaa prarambh san 1950 main kailashshahar main ramakrishna college kii pratisthapana ke dwaara hua tha jo ki vartmaan main uttar tripura jile kaa mukhya karyaalay he. baad main 1964 main belonia college dakshini- tripura kii sthapana ke saath-saath teen varsho main hi san 1967 main ram thakur college agartala kii sthapana dwaara mahavidhyalay sthapana ke kaary ko puraa kiya gaya. antat is rajya ke chaatro ko ek baat ne stabdh kar diya ki ye sabhi mahavidhyalay kalakatta vishvavidhyalay se sambhddha hone ke liye baadhya the or tripura main vishvavidhyalayan shiksha kii sambhaavna kaa astitv hi nahin tha.
 pichhali shatabdi ke antim saath ke dashak se tripura main hue chaatr aandolan atalneey rup se ucch shiksha kii suvidhaon ke prasar or tripura ke apane vishvavidhyalay ke nirmaan kii maang par kendrit the is badhati hui maangon ke pratikriyasvarup kalakatta vishvavidhyalay ke adhikari sahamat hue ki maharaja vir vikram mahavidhyalay main ganit, arthshaastra or itihaas ke snatakottar kakshaen lee jaaen or vishvavidhyalay anudaan ayog ne san 1976 main aupchaarik rup se kalakatta vishvavidhyalay ke santhaan kalakatta vishvavidhyalay snatakottar centre ko manyata pradaan kii. is ghatana ne rajya ke logon ko ucch shiksha kii jigyaasa hetu tathaa alag vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana karne ke liye prerit kiya.
 tripura ke apane vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana karne main bhi nau saal kaa atirikt samay liya gaya. vah ati-saubhagyapapurna diwas may 18, 1985 tha jab tatkaaleen shiksha mantri or lokapriy adivasee netaa dasharath deb ne 75 acre main faili bhoomi tripura vishvavidhyalay kii neenv rakhi.
