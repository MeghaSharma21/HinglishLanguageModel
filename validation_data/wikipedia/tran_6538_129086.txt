 kalabaddh loop aisa paripath hota he jisase utpann sanket kii kala ek input sanket kii kala ke samaan hoti he. ye kai tarah ke hote hai kintu vishesh baat he ki isamen ek negative feedback loop hota he jo input or output signal ke kala ko samaan rakhata he. ek saamaanya kalabaddh loop ke mukhya avyav saamne ke chitra main dikhaae gayi hai .
