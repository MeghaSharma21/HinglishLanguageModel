 moothli rajasthan ke barmer jile kii sivaana tehsil main basaa ek gaanv he.
 moothli rajasthan ke barmer jile kii sivaana tehsil main balotra-jalor rajmarg par basaa hua he. sivaana se 17 kimi or balotra se 18 kimi va brahmaji mandir se maatr 5 ki kii duuri par he. yahaan se rajyamarg 38 va rashtri rajmarg 325 bhi gujarata he.
 gaanv kii kul jansankhya 2976 he jisamen 1612 purush or 1364 mahilayen he.
 kaha jaataa he ki iskaa gauravamayi itihaas rahaa he. iskaa andaja gaanv main pravesh karte hi talab ke kinare bani chhatriyon se lag jaataa he. yahan ke kai vir yoddhaon ne gau, brahman va mathrubhumi ke liye balidaan kiye he. jo junjar bhomiaji va mamaji ke naam se puuje jate he. moothli bhaayalon ke mukhya 12 gaanvon main se ek he. rajputon main bhaayalon ke alaava dhandhal rathod, mahecha rathod, dhavechaa rathod, chouhan or parmaron ke bhi kaaphi ghar hai. inke alaava raajpurohito ke bhi kaaphi ghar he. suthar, nai, devasi, jain, bhil, meghavaal or harijanon ke log bhi rahete he yanha par. shiksha ke kshetra main yanha par kul milakar 5 vidhyalay he jiname ek nijee va chaar sarkari he. badi skool 12veen tak he jisame kariban 800 vidhyaarthee padhate he. khel jagat main bhi yanha kii acchhee pahchaan he. kai yuvak rajya stareey khelo main bhaag le chuke he. khilne ke liye cricket, wollyboul or basketbaal ke maidan bhi he. jiname hai skool ke mangalaram ji adhyaaapak kaa acha yogadan rahaa he. rajnetik drushti main 2015 se pehle moothli or thaapan kii milakar gram panchayat thi lekin 2015 ke baad moothli kii alag se panchayat bani he jiskaa pehla sarpanch chunnilaal bhil bana. yanha par ek shiv mandir he janha par shri khetram ji ne apane jivan kaa kuch samay bitaayaa tha unke samay kaa ek dhunaa abhi bhi vidyaman he. yanha par har tyohaar acchhee tarah se va miljul kar manaaya jaataa he jisame yanha ke kuch yuvako kii acchhee bhagidaari rahati he. vyapaarik drushti se bhi yah gaanv sudrudh he lagbhag choti badi vastu yanhi mil jaati he. yanha ke kai log pradesho main vyapaar karte he.
