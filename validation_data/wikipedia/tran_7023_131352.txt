 hindi katha main apni alag or deshaj chhavi banaae or bachaae rakhane vaale charchit lekhak bhagavaandas morwal ke is naye upanyaas ke kendra main he - mana guru or maan nalinya kii santan kanjar or uskaa jivan. kanjar yani kaananchar arthath jangal main ghoomane vaala. apane lok-vishwaason va lokacharon kii dhuree par apni asmita or apane astitv ke liye sangharsh karti ek vimukt janajati. 
 raajakmal se prakashit is upanyaas main kahani he conjaron kii - ek aisi ghumantu jati jo samooche dakshin-pashchimi asia main kamobesh faili hui he. kahani gumti he kamala sadan naam ke ek makaan ke ird-gird, jo parivaar kii mukhiya kamala buaa ke naam par rakhaa gaya he or jahaan rukmini, santo, pinki aadi tamaam mahilaon kaa jamghat he. ye sabhi paaramparik yonkarmi hai or jis kanjar samaaj se ve aati hai, vahaan is peshey ko lambey samay se manyata mili hui he. samooche paridrishy main sirf ek purush paatr he jise sabhi baidji kah kar pukaarte hai. yah paatr vaise to duusari jati kaa he, lekin ghar-parivaar ke maamalon main ise salaahkaar or hitaishi kii bhoomika main dikhaaya gaya he. yaheen se shuru hoti he stri vimarsh ke nimnavargeey prasangon kii daastaan - jahaan stri kii deh ko purush samaaj ke khilaaf lekhak ne ek aujaar or hathiyaar ke rup main sthapit kiya he.
 gajuki or kamala sadan ke bahaane yah katha aise durdamy samaaj kii katha he jisamen ek taraf kamala buaa, sushila, maaya, rukmini, vandana, punam hai; to duusari taraf hai santo or anita bhabhi. 'buaa' yani kathith sabhy samaaj ke bar-aks puure parivaar kii sarvesarva, yaa kahie pitrusattatmak vyavastha main chupake se send lagaate matrusattatmak varchasv kaa paryaay or 'ganga nahaane' kaa supaatr. jabaki 'bhabhi' hone kaa matlab he gharon kii charadivari main ghutane ko vivash ek doum darje kaa sadsy. ek aisa sadsy jo parivaar kaa hote hue bhi uskaa nahin he.
 ret bhartiya samaaj ke un ankahe, ansulajhe antarvirodhon va vaton kii katha he, jo ghanashyam 'krishna' urf vaidyaji kii 'kute fail' cycle ke kariyar par baith gajuki nadi ke bihadon se hote hue aage badhati he. yah safaltaaon ke shikhar par virajati rukmini kanjar kaa aisa lomaharshak aakhyaan he jo abhi tak itihaas ke pannon main ret se ata hua tha. jaraayam-pesha or kathith sabhy samaaj ke madhya gadi yon-shuchitaon kaa atikaraman karata, apni gahari taral sanlagnta, sookshm samvednaatmak rachana-kaushal tathaa gazab kii qissagoi se labarez lekhak kaa yah nayaa shaahakaar, hindi main stri-vimarsh ke choukhaton va hadon ko todta hua is vimarsh ke ek naye adhyay kii shuruyaat karata he. is upanyaas ko 2009 main you ke katha sammaan dwaara sammanit kiya gaya he.
