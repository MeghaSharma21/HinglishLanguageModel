 didihat uttrakhand rajya ke pithauragarh janapad main sthit ek nagar he. yah kumaun mandal main aata he, or didihat tehsil kaa mukhyalay he. 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar didihat kii jansankhya 6,522 he, or yah uttrakhand kii rajdhani dehradun se 520 kimi kii duuri par sthit he. didihat naam do kumaunni shabdon, 'dandi' or 'haat' se judakar bana he, jinka arth kramshah 'choti pahari' or 'bazaar' hota he. didihat 'kailash maansarovar tirthayatra' ke maarg par padataa he.
 vartmaan didihat nagar aitihaasik sira rajya kii rajdhani, sirkot ke sameep basaa he. didihat nagar ke pashchim main sthit digtad ke paas ek parvat chotii par sirkot kila tha, jo sira ke mall raajaaon kii rajdhani hua karata tha. raja hari mall ke samay tak yah kshetra nepal ke doti samrajya ke adheen tha. baad main 1581 isvi main chand vansh ke shaasha rudra chand ne mall raajaaon ko parajeet kar is kshetra par niyantran praapt kiya. abhi bhi pracheen kile or mandiron ke kuch avshesh didihat main upasthit hai.
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar, didihat nagar panchayat kii jansankhya 6,522 thi. didihat main sakshartaa dar 91.03% he; 95.20% purush or 86.44% mahilaain sakshar hai. nagar kaa ling anupaat 889 mahilaain prati 1000 purush he. didihat ke lagbhag sabhi nivasee kumaunni mul ke ​hai. kul jansankhya ke lagbhag 20.55% log anusuchit jatiyon se he, or unki sankhya 1,340 se adhik he. iske alaava, shahar main 649 log anusuchit janajatiyon ke hai. didihat 'raji' janajati kaa mul sthaan bhi he.:424 nagar main lagbhag 1,400 log jhuggiyon main rahete hai. 2001 main didihat kii jansankhya 4,805, or 1991 main 3,514 thi.
 varsh 1962 main pehali baar didihat ko jila ghoshit karne ko lekar sangharsh samiti kaa gathan kiya gaya tha. 1962 se 1992 tak didihat kii janata sangharsh karti rahi. varsh 1994 main tatkaaleen mukhyamantri mulaayam singh yadav ne didihat jile ko lekar dikshit ayog kaa gathan kiya. ek saal main ayog ne apni report main didihat ko jile ke liye sabhi maanak puraa karte hue iske gathan kii sanstuti sarkaar se kii thi, lekin us samay yah jila raajnneeti kii bhent chadh gaya.
 2000 main uttarakhand gathan ke saath hi choti prashasnic ikaiyon ko banane kii ghoshnaa bhaajapa evam congress dwaara kii gai. varsh 2000 se 2005 tak didihat kii janata ne jile kii gathan kii aas uttarakhand sarkaron se lagaae rakhi, lekin jila na banane par varsh 2005 main 36 din kaa aamaran anshan didihat ke ramlila maidan main kiya gaya. us samay tatkaaleen mukhyamantri endi tiwari ne andolan ko khatm karne ke liye sheeghra hi didihat ko jila ghoshit karne kii baat kahi, lekani jila ghoshit nahin ho paaya.
 15 agast 2011 ko tatkaaleen mukhyamantri ramesh pokharial nishank ne didihat, ranikhet, yamunotri, kotdwar ko jila banane kii ghoshnaa kii. 8 disambar 2011 ko shasnadesh jari kiya gaya, lekin gajet notification na hone ke kaaran nae jile astitv main nahin aa paae. harish ravat ke kaarykaal main 4 janvari 2017 jila gathan ke liye ek hajaar karod kaa carpus fund sthapit kiya, lekin congress ke shasankaal main bhi jile nahin bane.
