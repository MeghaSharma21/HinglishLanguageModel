 maharaja express 2010 se bhartiya rail dwaara chalaai jane vaali ek vilasita train he. yah indian railway cataring and tourizam korporation dwaara ek sanyukt udhyam he. sabhi samaveshi laagat ko milakar iskaa har tikat $800 prati vyakti prati din se lekar $2,500 prati vyakti prati din he.
 maharaja express kii chaar yatra kaarykram hai jo zyaadaatar dilli se shuruuaat karke agra tak jaati he. phir yah bharat kii anya hisson main bhi jaati he. vahaan par pracheen smarakon, viraasat sthal, aadi ke darshan kiya jaataa hai.
 is train main 23 dibbe hai, jo bharat kii maharaajaaon kii vyaktigat jivan kii yaad dilaati hai. is train main do atyadhik sundar bhojanalay, ek avlokan lounj, ek yaadgaar vastuon kii dukaan or rahane ke liye 43 shandaar cabin hai.
 indian railway cataring and tourizam korporation or cox kings india limited, duniya ke kuch puraani yatra companiyon main se ek ne maharaja express ko chalaane ke liye ek sanyukt udhyam par hastakshar kiya.
 maharaja express bharat kii sabase keemtee vilasita train he. 2010 main antarrashtriya railway yatriyon kii sosayadi dwaara train par awaas or bhojan kii suvidha, seva or band gaadi bhraman kaarykram ke liye prashansa kii or 2011 main ise vishwa kii sabase vilasita vaali 25 trainon kii suchi main namit kiya gaya.
