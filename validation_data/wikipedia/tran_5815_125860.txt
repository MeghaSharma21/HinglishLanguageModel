 smiling buddha yaa muskuraate buddha bharat dwaara kiye gaye pratham safal parmaanu pareekshan kaa kootnasm he. yah pareekshan jise 18 may 1974 ko pokharan main sena ke sthal pe jise pokharan test range kehete he vah varisht sena ke afasaron kii nigaraanee main kiya gaya. 
 pokharan -1 is maamle main bhi mahatvapurn he ki yah sanyukt raashtra ke paanch sthaayee sadsy deshon ke alaava kisi anya desh dwaara kiya gaya pehla parmaanu hathiyaar kaa pareekshan tha. adhikarik rup se bhartiya videsh mantralay ne ise shantipurna parmaanu bam visphot bataaya, lekin vaastavik rup se yah twarit parmaanu kaarykram tha.
 bharat ne homi jahangir bhabha ke netritv main san 1944 main " tata moolbhoot anusandhan santhaan " main apane parmaanu kaarykram kii shuruyaat kii. bhautikashastri raja rammana ne parmaanu astron ke nirmaan sambandhi kaarykram main apana sakriy yogadan diya, unhone parmaanu hathiyaaron kii vaigyanik takanic ko adhikadhik unnat kiya or baad main pokharan -1 parmaanu kaarykram ke liye gathit pehali vagyaniko kii toli ke mukhiya bane.
 sun 1947 main bharat ke angrejo se azaad hone ke baad tatkaaleen pradhanmantri jawaharlal nehru ne parmaanu kaarykram kii jimmedaari homi je bhabha ke kaabil hatho main saupin. 1948 main paas hue "parmaanu urja act " kaa mukhya uddeshy iskaa shantipurna vikaas karnaa tha. bharat shuruyaat se hi parmaanu aprasar sandhi ke paksh main tha lekin us par hastakshar nahin kar sakaa.
 san 1954me bhabha ne parmaanu kaarykram ko shastr nirmaan kii taraf modaa tathaa do mahatvapurn buniyadi dhancho par dhyaan diya. pehla tromabe parmaanu urja centre kii sthapana tathaa doosraa ek sarkari sachivalay, parmaanu urja vibhaag . iske pehle sachiv bhabha  rahe jinke netritv main 1958 tak urja chhetra main kaaphi teji se kaary hua. 1959 ke aate aate DAE koraksha bajet kaa ek tihaai bhaag sweekrut ho gaya tha. san 1954 main bharat ne sanyukt raashtra tathaa canada ke saath maukhik rup se parmaanu urja ke shantipurna upyog par sahamati kar lee thi. sanyukt raashtra or canada trombe main sarkas riector ke nirmaan main sahyog dene par raji bhi ho gaye, sarkas riector ek prakaar se bharat or sanyukt raashtra ke madhya parmaanu aadan pradaan kii shuruyaat thi. sarkas riector puri tarah se shantipurna upyog ke liye bana tha tathaa iskaa uddeshy plutonium yukti ko viksit karnaa tha. jiske kaaran nehru ne canada seppermanu eendhan lene se manaa kar diya tathaa swadeshi nabhikeey eendhan chakra viksit karne kii ore bal diya.
 july 1958 main nehru ne "finics pariyojna " jiskaa lakshya saal bhar main 20 ton nabhikeey eendhan banane kaa tha kii shuruyaat kii jo kii sarkas riector kii aavashyak kshamta ke anusaar eendhan pradaan kar sake. ek america company vitro international dwaara banaai gayi yah ikai purex  prakriya kaa upyog karti thi. is ikai kaa nirmaan kaary trombe main 27 march 1961 main chaalu hua tathaa 1964 ke madhya tak yah ban ke taiyaar ho gaya tha.
 nabhikeey pariyojna apni purti kii ore aage bad rahi thi, tabhi 1960 main nehru ne ise nirmaan kii ore modane kii sochi tathaa bharat main bhi nabhikeey bijli ghar kii apni pariklpana ko moort rup dete hue america company vestinouse electric ko bharat kaa pehla nabhikeey bijli ghar, tarapur main banane kaa jimmaa saupa. kenneth nicols joki america sena main abhiyantaa the ne nehru se mulaakaat kii, yahi wo vakt tha jab nehru ne bhabha se nabhikeey hathiyaaron ko banane main lagane vaale vakt ke bare main puchha tab bhabha ne 1 varsh kaa anumaanit samay maanga. 
 1962 ke aate aate parmaanu kaarykram dhimi raftaar se hi sahi lekin chal rahaa tha. nehru bhi bharat-china yuddh ke chalate jisame bharat ne china se apni jameen khoyi thi, parmaanu kaarykram se vichlit ho gaye the. nehru ne soviet sangh se madad kii apil kii lekin usne cubai missile sankat ke chalate madad karne main apni asamarthta jaahir kii. tab bharat is nishkarsh par panhucha kii soviet sangh vishwaas yogya saathi nahin he, tab nehru ne drudhsankalpit hoke kisi or ke bharose baithne ke bajaay khud ko parmaanu shakti sampann desh banane kaa nishchay kiya jiskaa khaakaa san 1965 main bhabha ko diya gaya jinhone raja ramanna ke adheen tathaa unki mrityu ke baad parmaanu hathiyaar kaarykram ko aage badhaaya.
 bhabha ab aakraamak tareeke se parmaanu hathiyaaron ke liye pairvi kar rahe the or bhartiya radio par kai bhaashan die.1964 main, bhabha ne bhartiya radio ke madhyam se bhartiya janata ko bataaya ki "is tarah ke parmaanu hathiyaar ullekhneey saste hote hai" or america parmaanu pareekshan kaarykram kii kifayati laagat kaa hawala dete hue apane tark kaa samarthan kiya.parmaanu kaarykram aanshik rup se dhimaa ho gaya jab laal bahadur shastri pradhanmantri bane.1965 main, pradhanmantri shastri ji ko pakistan ke saath ek or yuddh kaa saamana karnaa padaa.shastri ji ne ,parmaanu kaarykram ke pramukh ke rup main bhautik vigyaani vikram sarabhai ko niyukt kiya lekin kyonki unke gandhivadi vishwaason kii kaaran sarabhai ne sainya vikaas ke bajaay shantipurna uddeshyon kii ore kaarykram kaa nirdesh kiya. 1967 main, indira gandhi bharat kii pradhanmantri bani or parmaanu kaarykram par kaam nae utsaah ke saath phir se shuru ho gaya.homi sethna, ek rasinic ejeeniyar ne, plutonium grad hathiyaar ke vikaas main ek mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai. jabaki ramanna ne puure parmaanu divaais kaa dizine or nirmaan karavaaya|pehle parmaanu bam pariyojna main ,apni samvedansheeltaa kii vajah se, 75 se adhik vaigyanikon nen kaam nahin kiya. parmaanu hathiyaar kaarykram ab urinium ke bajaae plutonium ke utpaadan kii disha main nirdeshit kiya gaya tha.
 1968-69 main, pi.ke. ingar teen sahyogiyon ke saath soviet sangh kaa daura kiya or masco, rus main parmaanu anusandhan suvidhaon kaa daura kiya.apni yatra ke dauraan ingar plutonium fuel pulsed riector bahut se prabhavit hue|bharat lautne par, ingar ne plutonium riektaron kaa ,janvari 1969 main, bhartiya raajnneetik netritv dwaara anumodith vikaas ke bare main nirdhaarit kiya tha. gupt plutonium sanyantr poornima ke rup main janaa jaataa tha,or nirmaan kaa kaary march 1969 main shuru hua.sanyantr ke netritv main ingar, ramanna, homi sethna or sarabhai shaamil the.
