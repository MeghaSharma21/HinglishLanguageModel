 siwan yaa sivaan bharat ganraajya ke bihar praant main saaran pramandal ke antargat ek jila tathaa shahar he. yah bihar ke uttar pashchimi chor par uttar pradesh kaa simavarti jila he. jila mukhyalay sivaan shahar daha nadi ke kinare basaa he. iske uttar tathaa purv main kramash: bihar kaa gopalganj tathaa saaran jila tathaa dakshin evam pashchim main kramash: uttar pradesh kaa devaria or baliya jila he. bharat ke pratham raashtrapati da rajendra prasad tathaa kai agrani swatantraaa senaniyon kii janmabhumi evam karmasthali ke liye sivaan ko janaa jaataa he.
 sivaan kaa namaakaran madhyakaal main yahan ke raja 'shiv maan' ke naam par hua he. unke purvaj babar ke yahan aane tak shashan kar rahe the. iskaa ek anumandal maharajaganj he jiskaa naam is kshetra par raaj kar rahe maharaja kaa ghar yaa kila sthit hone ke chalate padaa he. isi jila ke ek samant ali baksh ke naam par ise aliganj bhi pukaara jaataa tha. 
 paanchavi sadi isapurv main sivaan kii bhoomi kosal mahajanapad kaa ang tha. kosal rajya ke uttar main nepal, dakshin main sarpika nadi, purab main gandak nadi tathaa pashchim main paanchal pradesh tha. iske antargat aaj ke uttar pradesh kaa faijabad, gonda, basti, gorakhpur tathaa devaria jila ke atirikt bihar kaa saaran kshetra aata he. aathvin sadi main yahan banaras ke shaskaon kaa aadhipaty tha. 15 veen sadi main sikandar lodi ne yahan apana aadhipaty sthapit kiya. babar ne apane bihar abhiyaan ke samay siswan ke najadik ghaghara nadi paar kii thi. baad main yah mughal shashan kaa hissaa ho gaya. akbar ke shasankaal par likhe gaye aainaa-e-akabari ke vivaran anusaar kar sangrah ke liye banaae gaye 6 sarkaron main saaran vittiya kshetra ek tha or iske antargat vartmaan bihar ke hisse aate the. 17veen sadi main vyapaar ke uddeshy se yahan dach aae lekin baxur yuddh main vijay ke baad san 1765 main angrejon ko yahan kaa divaani adhikaar mil gaya. 1829 main jab patna ko pramandal banaaya gaya tab saaran or champaran ko ek jila banaakar saath rakhaa gaya. 1908 main tirhut pramandal banane par saaran ko iske saath kar iske antargat gopalganj, sivaan tathaa saaran anumandal banaae gaye. 1857 kii kranti se lekar aazaadee milane tak sivaan ke nirbhik or jujharu logon ne angreji sarkaar ke khilaaf apni ladaai ladi. 1920 main asahyog aandolan ke samay sivaan ke braj kishor prasad ne pardaa pratha ke virodh main aandolan chalaaya tha. 1937 se 1938 ke bich hindi ke murdhanya vidwaan rahul sankrutyaayan ne kisaan aandolan kii neenv sivaan main rakhi thi. swatantraaa kii ladaai main yahan ke majaharul haq, rajendra prasad, mahendra prasad,ramdaas pandeya foolena prasad jaise mahaan senaniyon nen samooche desh main bihar kaa naam unchaa kiya he. swatantraaa pashchaat 1981 main saaran ko pramandal kaa darja mila. june 1970 main bihar main trivedi eward lagu hone par sivaan ke kshetron main parivartan kiye gaye. san 1885 main ghaghara nadi ke bahaav sthiti ke anusaar lagbhag 13000 acre bhoomi uttar pradesh ko sthaanantarit kar diya gaya jabaki 6600 acre jameen sivaan ko mila. 1972 main sivaan ko jila bana diya gaya.yaahan ek gaavan he, hasanpura.
 sivaan jila uttari ganga ke maidan main sthit ek samtal bhu-bhaag he. jile kaa vistaar 25053' se 260 23' uttari akshans tathaa 840 1' se 840 47' purvi deshaantar ke bich he. nadiyon ke dwaara jamaa kii gayi mittiyon kii gaharaai 5000 feet tak he. maidani bhaag kaa dhaal uttar-pashchim se dakshin-purv kii ore he. nichle maidan main jalajmav kaa kai kshetra he jo chaur kehalaata he. yahan se kai choti nadi yaa 'sota' bhi nikalate hai. mukhya nadi ghaghara he jiske kinare daraara nirmit hua he. is khaas bhugolic banaavat main baaluu kii moti parat par mritrika or silt kii patali parat paayi jaati he. sivaan kii mitti khadar evam bangar ke bich kii he. khadar mitti ko yahan domat tathaa bangar ko balsundari kaha jaataa he. balsundari mitti main kankar kii maatra paayi jaati he. kai jagahon par gandhakyukt mitti milti he jahaan se kabhi saltapeter nikaal jaataa tha. angreji shashan main yah ek udyog hua karata tha lekin ab yah gaayab ho chukaa he.
 sivaan main uttar pradesh or pashchim bengal ke bich kii jalwaayu paayi jaati he. march se may ke bich yahan chalanevaali pachua pawanon ke chalate mausam shushk hota he lekin kai baar shaam main chalanevaali purvaai hawa ardrata le aati he jisase uttar pradesh se chalane vaali dhulbhari aandhi ko viraam lag jaataa he. garmiyon main taapmaana 40 degree celsius tak pahunch jaataa he or lu kaa chalanaa in dinon aam he. jaadon kaa mausam suhaavna hota he kintu kai baar sheet lahar kaa prakope kshtdaayi ho jaataa he. july-agast main honevaali monsooni varsha ke alaave pashchimi avdaab se jaade main bhi baaris kaa hona saamaanya baat he. ausat vaarshik varsha 120 centimetre hoti he.
 yahan kii fasalon main dhan, gehun, eekh, makka, arhar aadi pramukh hai. iske alaava is jile main kuch jagahon par phulon or subjiyon kii bhi kheti rojgarparak krishi ke taur par kii jaati he. krishi aadhaarit is jile main kutir udyogon ke alaava ganna mills, plastic factory, soot factriyaan aadi kaaphi sankhya main thi, jinamein se ab adhikansh band he. ganna mil band he
 sivaan jile kaa adhikansh janajivan krishi kendrit/aadhaarit he. iske alaava yah puure purvanchal main arab deshon kii rojgaar ke liye palaayan karne vaale mazdooron kii sankhya main bhi sambhawatah awwal sthaan par he. arab deshon se parijanon dwaara bheje gaye paison se yahan ke jan-jivan apane aarthik sthitiyon main jeeta he. 
 sivaan jile se vartmaan main do rashtriya rajmarg tathaa do rajkeey rajmarg guzarati hai. rashtriya rajmarg 85 chhapra se sivaan hote hue gopalganj jaati he. chhapra se shuru honevaali rashtriya rajmarg 101 jile ko mohammadpur ko jodatii he. sivaan jile main rajkeey rajmarg sankhya 47, 73 evam 89 kii kul lambaai 125 kilometre he. saarvajanik yatayaat mukhyatah nijee bason, autoricshaw or nijee vaahanon par ashrith he. gopalganj, chhapra tathaa patna se yahan aane ke liye sadak maarg sabase upyukt he. sivaan jile kii mahatvapurn sadakon main sivaan-mairava-guthni, sivaan-chhapra, sivaan-sarfara, sivaan-raghunathpur, sivaan-sisvaan, sivaan-maharajaganj, sivaan-badali, sivaan-mirganj, bhantapokhar-jiradei, mairava- darauli, mairava-punak tathaa darauli, raghunathpur hote hue guthni- chhapra shaamil he. 
 dilli-hajipur-guwahati railmarg par sivaan ek mahatvapurn jankshan he. yah purv madhya railway ke sonpur mandal main padataa he. jile main 45 kilometre lambii railmarg mairavaan, jiradei, pachrukhi, daraundha hokar guzarati he. daraundha se maharajaganj ke bich ek loop-line bhi maujuud he, jiskaa vistaar masarakh tak prastaavit he. sivaan se dilli, mumbai, ahmedabad, amritsar, kolkata or guwahati jaise mahatvapurn shaharon ke liye sidhi trainen uplabdh he.
 najdiki hawaii adda rajya kii rajdhani patna main he. jaiprakash narayan antarrashtriya hawaii kshetra se dilli, kolkata, ranchi aadi shaharon ke liye indian, spice jet, kingfisher, jetlite, indigo aadi vimansevaayen uplabdh hai. sivaan se chhapra pahunchakar rashtriya rajmarg 19 dwaara 140 kilometre dur patna hawaii adda jaaya jaataa he.
