 maldive main raashtrapati chunaav 23 sitambar 2018 ko sampann hue, jisame progressive party of maldive kii taraf se vartmaan raashtrapati abdulla yamin or vipaksh ke sanyukt ummidwar tathaa maldivian democratic party ke ibrahim mohammad solih ke madhya muqabla tha. 
 chunaav parinaamon main solih kii ascharyajanak jeet hui or ve 58.3% mat paakar maldive ke raashtrapati bane. ve apana karyaalay 17 navambar 2018 ko grahan karenge. solih desh ke tisare loktantric rup se nirvaachit raashtrapati hai. unase pehle 2008 main mohammad nashid momun abdul gayyum ko haraakar loktantric rup se raashtrapati bane the.
 maldive ke raashtrapati kaa chunaav dwi charniya paddati se hota he; agar chunaavon main do se zyada ummidwar ho tathaa kisi ko pratham charan main 50% se adhik mat na mili to dwitiya charan sampann hota he.
 in chunaavon main do ummidwar the. vartmaan raashtrapati abdulla yamin sattarudh progressive party of maldive ke ummidwar the jabaki vipaksh ke sanyukt ummidwar ibrahim mohammad solih the jo ki vipakshi dal maldivian democratic party se the.
 padasth raashtrapati abdulla yamin ne chunaavon main aarthik vikaas tathaa islamickaran ko banaaya tha, or unhone vipakshi dalon par aarope lagaaya ki ve isai padariyon dwaara samarthit hai. unki sarkaar china sarkaar kii kareebi maani jaati he kyonki unhone china sarkaar ke saath mukt vyapaar anubandh par hastakshar kiya tha tathaa vinirmaan pariyojnaon ke liye chini dhan udhaar liya tha jabaki vipaksh bharat se rishte sudhaarney kii maang kar rahaa tha.
 chunaavon se purv yamin ne sabhi nagarikon ke liye ghar ke saath hi yatayaat va bill par lagane vaale dandon ko khatm karne kaa vaada kiya tha. is kram main saikadon bandiyon ko bhi mukt kiya gaya. 
 chunaavon se purv yamin ne apane samarthak ahamed shareef ko mukhya chunaav ayukta bana diya, jisase chunaavon main maton ke saath chhedchhad hone kii sambhaavna utpann ho gayi. antararashtriya paryvekshakon ke chunaav nigaraanee par roak laga diya gaya tathaa videshi media par bhi kathor pratibandh lagaaye gayi.
 police ne chunaav ke ek din purv hi democratic party ke karyaalay par chapa maaraa tathaa daawa kiya ki ve "paise baantkar mat kharidne" ke maamle kii jaanch karne aaye hai. america tathaa britani sarkaar ne is chhapemari kii ninda kii.
 chunaav ke din matdaan kendron par baari bheed ke kaaran matdaan ko teen ghante ke liye badha diya gaya..
