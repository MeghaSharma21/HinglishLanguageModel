 ikia prabhaav ek sangyanatmak purvaagrah he. jisamen upabhokta utpaadon par ek adhiktar ucch mulya rakhte hai jinako unhone aanshik rup se banaaya he. yah naam swedish nirmaataa or furniture retailor ikia se niklaa he, joki kai furniture utpaada bechata he.
 ikia prabhaav par prayogtmak parinaam pehle harvard bijness skool ke maaikel ai norton, yel vishvavidhyalay ke danniel mokone, or 2011 main duk vishvavidhyalay ke daan arili dwaara prakashit kiye gaye the. 
 unke prayogon ne darshaya ki aatm assembly kaa upabhoktaaon dwaara utpaadon ke mulyankan par kya asar padataa he. 
 atirikt shodh se pataa chalaa he ki shram ucch moolyankan karne ke liye hota he jab keval shram faldaayak he. pratibhaagiyon ne apane swayan ke parishram kii aavashyaktaa hoti he agar ek kaary ko puraa karne main vifal rahaa he, to ikia prabhaav vyast he. 
 ikia prabhaav dub laagat prabhaav main yogadan karne ke liye samajha jaataa he. yah prabhaav "yahan nahin avishkaar" syndrome se bhi sambandhit he.
