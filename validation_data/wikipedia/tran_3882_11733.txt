 akhil bhartiya ayurvigyaan santhaan yaa ames saarvajanik ayurvigyaan mahavidhyalay kaa samooh he. is samooh main nai dilli sthit bharat kaa sabase purana utkrisht ames santhaan he. isaki aadharshila 1952 main rakhi gayi or iskaa srijan 1956 main sansad ke ek adhinium ke madhyam se ek swytt santhaan ke rup main swasthya dekhbhaal ke sabhi pakshon main utkrushtataa ko poshan dene ke kendra ke rup main kaary karne hetu kiya gaya. ames chowk dilli ke ring road par padane vaala chauraahaa he, ise aravind maarg katata he.
 san 2015 main pradhanmantri narendra modi ne isi shrankhla main 6 anya ames santhaan puure bharat main sthapit kiye gayi. taaki dur daraaj ke logon ko behatar ilaaj kii suvidhaayen pai ja sake! 2022 tak har rajya main ek AIIMS kholane kaa vichaar he. 
 akhil bhartiya ayurvigyaan santhaan kii sthapana sabhi shakhaon main snaatak or snatakottar chikitsa shiksha main adhyaapan ke pattern viksit karne ke uddeshy se rashtriya mahatv ke ek santhaan ke rup main kii gai thi, taaki bharat main chikitsa shiksha ke ucch maanak pradarshit kiye ja saken tathaa swasthya gatividhi kii sabhi mahatvapurn shakhaon main carmikon ke prashikshan hetu ucchtam str kii shaikshik suvidhaayen ek hi sthaan par laane or snatakottar chikitsa shiksha main aatmanirbharta pai ja sake.
 santhaan main adhyaapan, anusandhan or rogiyon kii dekhbhaal ke liye vyapak suvidhaayen hai. ames dwaara snaatak or snatakottar donon hi staron par chikitsa tathaa paramedical paathyakramon main adhyaapan kaarykram chalaae jate hai or yah chaatro ko apni hi degree detaa he. yahaan 42 vishyon main adhyaapan or anusandhan yojit kiye jate hai. ames main ek nursing mahavidhyalay bhi chalaaya jaataa he or yahaan bi. essi. nursing post pramaan patr degree ke liye chaatro ko prashikshan bhi diya jaataa he.
 ames dwaara haryana ke vallabh garh main vyapak gramin swasthya dekhbhaal kendra main 60 bistaron vaale aspataal kaa bhi prabandhan kiya ja rahaa he or yahaan saamudayik upachaar ke liye kendra ke madhyam se lagbhag 2.5 lakh aabaadi ko swasthya suvidhaayen di jaati hai.
