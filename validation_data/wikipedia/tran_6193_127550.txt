 tilak kaa arth he bharat main puja ke baad maathe par lagaaya janevaala nishaan. 
 shastranusaar yadi dwij tilak nahin lagaate hai to unhen "chandal" kehete hai. tilak hamensha donon bhauhon ke bich "aajnachakra" par bhrukuti par kiya jaataa he.ise chetana centre bhi kehete hai.

 chandan hameshaa parvat ke nok kaa, nadi tat kii mitti kaa, punya tirth kaa, sindhu nadi ke tat kaa, chinti kii baanbi va tulsi ke mul kii mitti kaa yaa chandan vahi uttam chandan he. tilak hamensha chandan yaa kumkum kaa hi karnaa chahie. kumkum haldi se bana ho to uttam hota hai.
