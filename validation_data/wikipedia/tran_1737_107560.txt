 ooshmaa pump yaa 'hit pump' aisi yukti he jo kisi ooshmaastrot se lekar ooshmaanimjjak ko taap pravanta ke ulti disha main ooshmaa sthaanantarit karti he. arthath ooshmaapump sahaj ooshmaa-pravaah ke vipareet disha main ooshmaa pravaahit karaate hai. compressor se chalane vaale vaataanukoolan or freezer ooshmaa-pump ke aam udhaaharan hai. kintu vishesh rup se gharon/kakshon kii hawa ko garm/thanda karne main prayukt yuktiyon ko hi 'ooshmaa pump' kaha jaataa he.
