 durud shareef yaa salwat yaa as-salaatu alan-nabi ek vishesh arabi vaakyansh hai, jisamen islam ke aakhri paigambar mohammad sallallahu alaihi vasallam or ahal al-bait par abhivadan bheja jaataa hai | paigambar mohammad sahab kaa ullekh karte samay, muslim logon dwaara durud shareef kaa uchaaran kara jaataa hai | sankhyatmak rup se durud shareef kii taadaad hajaaron yaa lakhon main hai, parantu pratek durud shareef kaa mul arth mohammad or unke parivaar ke liye allaah taaala se aashirwad or dua maangana hai |
 salwat, salaat kaa bahuvachan rup hai| mukhya rup se salaat main teen mul arabi shabd he- "saad, laam, vaav" | durud kaa arth prathana, bandagi,nivedan,vinati,prashansa or stuti hai | 
 arabi bhashavidon kaa maananaa hai ki salwat kaa arth upyog karne vaale or kiske liye iskaa upyog kiya gaya, us par nirbhar karata hai | 
 
 allaah quran ke surah al-ehzab: 56 main musalamaanon ko mohammad par durud bhejne kaa nirdesh detaa he | iskaa upadesh kuch is prakaar he:
 إِنَّ اللَّهَ وَمَلَائِكَتَهُ يُصَلُّونَ عَلَى النَّبِيِّ يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آَمَنُوا صَلُّوا عَلَيْهِ وَسَلِّمُوا تَسْلِيمًا 
 anuwaad: beshak allaah or is ke farishte nabi par darud bhejte hai. e imaan waalo! tum bhi un par darud bhejo or koob salaam bheja karo--al-koran surat allaahaz ab:5
 iskaa angreji anuwaad nimnlikhit he: 
 durud shareef padhane ke liye kuch khaas vakt behtareen maane jate he | 
 nimnlikhit dinon main durud padhnaa sabase acha mana jaataa he:
 hazarat os kii rivaayat : main ne allaah ke rasul ko yah kehete suna : "sab dino main juma kaa din bahatar he, isi din aadam alai paidaa kiye gayi, isi din unki aatma nikaali gai, isi din sur fooka jayega, isi din udhane kaa hukum hoga, at: is din mujh par mehnat se durud sharif bheja karo" | 
 اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَعَلَى آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ كَمَا صَلَّيْتَ عَلَى إِبْرَاهِيمَ وَعَلَى آلِ إِبْرَاهِيمَ إِنَّكَ حَمِيدٌ مَجِيدٌ اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ، وَعَلَى آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ كَمَا بَارَكْتَ عَلَى إِبْرَاهِيمَ وَعَلَى آلِ إِبْرَاهِيمَ إِنَّكَ حَمِيدٌ مَجِيدٌ
 allahummaa salli alaa muhammadin va alaa aali muhammadin kama sallaita alaa ibrahim va alaa aali ibrahim innak hamidum majid, allahumm barik alaa muhammadin va alaa aali muhammadin kama barakta alaa ibrahim va alaa aali ibrahim innak hamidum majid.
