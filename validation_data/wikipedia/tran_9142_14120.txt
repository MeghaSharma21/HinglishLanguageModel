 bhagavat mudit kaa samay sanwat 1630 tathaa san. 1720 vi. ke madhya main tha. yah agra main shuji ke dewan the or vahaan se virakt hokar vrundavan main aa based the. inhen hit sampradaay ke bhakton kaa bhi satsang praapt tha or inhone is sampradaay ke 35 bhakton kaa charitra rasik ananymaal main granthit kiya he. prabodhanand saraswati ke anek vrundavan shatakon main se ek kaa inhone padyanuwaad kiya he, jo sanwat 1707 kii rachana he. inke do sau saat sfoot pad ab tak mili hai. yah bhi chaitanya sampradaay ke radharamani vaishnav the.
 inke pita madhav mudit chaitanya sampradaay ke bhakt sukavi tathaa agra ke nivasee the.
