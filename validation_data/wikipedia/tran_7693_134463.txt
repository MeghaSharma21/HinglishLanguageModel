 1900 main romania ne pehali baar olympic khelon main bhaag liya, ek ekal pratibhaagi ke saath. romania ke liye rashtriya olympic samiti romanian olympic or khel samiti he, or ise 1914 main banaaya or manyata praapt thi. raashtra ne pehali baar 1924 main khelon main pratispardhaa karne ke liye ek team bheji, or uske baad se keval do sanskarnon ko greeshmakaleen olympic khelon or sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main se keval yaad kiya. visheshkar, 1984 greeshmakaleen olympic main bhaag lene ke liye romania akelaa purvi block raashtra tha, jise anya deshon ne bahishkaar kiya tha. yah romania kaa sabase safal olympic khelon bhi tha: unhone 20 swarna padak or kul 53 padak jite.
 jimnastics romania kaa sheersh padak banane vaala khel he. romania ne rashtron kii duusari sabase badi kul padak jeeta he, jinhone kabhi bhi khelon kii mejabani nahin kii he.
