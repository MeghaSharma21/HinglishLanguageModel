 baaraan jila pashchim bhartiya rajya rajasthan kaa ek jila he. iskaa jila mukhyalay baaraan 3386306
 he. sanderbhika rajasthan sujas san 1948 main sanyukt rajasthan ke nirmaan ke samay bhi baaraan ek jila tha. 31 march 1949 ko rajasthan kaa punarnirmaan hua or baaraan jila mukhyalay ko kota jile kaa upkhand mukhyalay banaaya gaya. 10 april 1991 ko purv kota jile se baaraan jile kaa nirmaan kiya gaya. "'varah nagari" baaraa chodahavi-pandrahavin shatabdi main solanki raajputon ke adheen tha. us samay iske antargat 12 gaanv aate the, isliye yah nagar baaraa kahalaaya. baaraa samudr tal se 262 meter kii unchaai par kalisindh, parvati va parvan nadiyon ke bich sthit ha.baaraa jila chha: upkhandon - baaraa, mangrol, ataru, kishanganj, shahabaad evam chabada tathaa aath tahseelon- anta, baaraa, mangrol, ataru, kishanganj, shahabaad chhabada evam chhipabdod main vibhajit ha.
