  ke rajya or sangh kshetra
 bharat untiis rajyon or saat kendra shasit pradeshon kaa ek sangh he. san 2009 main, lagbhag 1.15 arab kii jansankhya ke saath bharat vishwa kaa doosraa sarvaadhik jansankhya vaala desh he. bharat ke paas vishwa kii kul bhushetra kaa 2.4% bhaag he, lekin yah vishwa kii 17% jansankhya kaa nivas sthaan he. ganga ke maidani kshetra vishwa ke sabase vishaal upjau failaavon main se ek hai or yah vishwa ke sabase saghan based kshetron main se ek he. dakkan ke pathaar ke purvi or pashchimi tateey kshetra bhi vishwa ke sabase saghan kshetron main hai. pashchimi rajasthan main sthit thar marusthal vishwa ke sabase saghan marusthalon main se ek he. uttar or uttar-purv main himalay parvat shrinkhalaaon main based rajyon main thande shushk marusthal or upjau ghaatiyaan hai. kathin sanrachana ke kaaran in rajyon main desh ke anya bhaagon kii tulana main jansankhya ghanatv kam he.
 british india main pratham janaganana 1872 main ki gai thi. 1947 main swatantraaa ke baad, 1951 se janaganana prati das varsh ke antaraal par kii jaati he. grih mantralay ke adheen register general or janaganana ayukta dwaara janaganana kii jaati he.
 janaganana ke naveenatam aankade varsh 2001 kii janaganana ke hai. 1991–2001 ke dashak ke dauraan bharat kii jansankhya vruddhi dar kam hokar 2.14% se 1.93% ho gai he.dashakeey janaganana ke anusaar sarvaadhik vruddhi dar 64.53% nagaland main darj kii gai. iske baad hai rashtriya rajdhani kshetra dilli, chandigarh or sikkim . kerala kii vruddhi dar sabase kam 9.43% rahi.
 bharat main kul basasit graamon kii sankhya he 5,93,731 or kul jansankhya kaa 72.2 pratishat bhaag inhen gramin kshetron main nivas karata he. inamen se 1,45,000 graamon main jansankhya aakaar 500–999 vyaktiyon kaa he; 1,30,000 graamon main jansankhya aakaar 1000–1999 he or 1,28,000 graamon main 200–499. 3,961 gram aise hai jinaki jansankhya 10,000 yaa adhik he. bharat kii 27.8 pratishat jansankhya 5,100 se adhik kasbon or lagbhag 380 nagareey kshetron main rahati he. 1991-2001 ke dashak ke dauraan pramukh mahanagaron kii ore hue palaayan ke kaaran mahanagareey jansankhya main bahut vruddhi hui he. pichley ek dashak ke dauraan pravaasiyon ke nival antim nivas ke aadhaar par, maharashtra me sarvaadhik apravasana hua, phir rashtriya rajdhani kshetra dilli, gujarat or haryana . uttar pradesh or bihar main sarvaadhik antarraji utpravas hua. paanch rajyon uttar pradesh, maharashtra, bihar, pashchim bengal or andhra pradesh main milakar bharat kii lagbhag aadhi jansankhya nivas karti he.yadhyapi linganupaat ke rashtriya ausat 1991 main 927 se badhkar 2001 main 933 hua he, lekin 2001 kii janaganana yah darshaati he kii bal linganupaat main teevr giravat darj kii gai he. desh ke unnat rajyon jaise haryana, punjab, himachal pradesh or gujarat main bal linganupaat main teevr giravat darj kii gai he. rashtriya bal linganupaat 1991 ke 945 se girakar 2001 main 927 rah gaya he.
 bharat kaa kul bhugolic kshetrafal 32,87,240 varg kimi he. jansankhya ghanatv nikatatam purnank ke liye poornit he.
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar, bharat kii kul jansankhya 1,21,01,93,422 he.
