 chhattisgarh main bahut se tyohaar, parv va utsav manaae jate hai.
 jaateey utsaah kii abhivyakti kaa ek or umdaa madhyam he, chhattisgarh ke apane teej-tyohaar hai. hinduon ke tyohaar hi praayah maanate hai. alabatta kuchhek tyohaar zarur aise hote hai jo khaas mahatv liye rahete hai. inhen main phaguna kii masti main duba holi vishesh tyohaar he. holi devaar main kaaphi umang-hadddang ke sang manati he. is din samoochaa kunba mahuye kii madamasti main mast ho jaataa he. maandar, dhol manjire ke sang geet bhi gaaye jate he. holi par kisi chinhit sthaan par ekatra hone kaa chalan he. is roj shubh muhurt dekhakar baigaa anushthan karnaa he or uski anumati ke uparaant pratikatmak holi jalaai jaati he. vruddh-jawan or baccha mandali bhi madiraa pikar lot-pot hoti he. 
 devaron main pora kaaphi mahatv he. albala tija nahin maanate. saamanytah bahan ko bhai jis tarah apane ghar laate hai us parampara kii bajaay bahan sasuraal main rahakar hi tija maanati he. vahiin vrath-upwas aadi hota he. lekin vastradi uphaar swadwap dene kaa koi chalan nahin he. pora main kumhaaron se mitti kii kuch vastuein kharidkar uski puja ke baad bali di jaati he. bhado ke shukla paksh main thakur dev ko bhi ye log badi aasthaa se puujate hai or bali ke baad prasad bantata he.
 devaron main sakat kaa atyadhik mahatvapurn parv he. sakat main mahilayen apane mata-pita ke ghar aati he. upwas rakhaa jaataa he. samuhik bhoj se upwas todaa jaataa he. parijan vastra, shringaar samagriyaan apni kanya ko dete hai.
 hareli yadhyapi khetihar-samaaj kaa parv he phir bhi iske duusare swarup yani tantra mantra vaale hisse ko devaron kaa varg maanata he. jis tarah chhattisgarh ke graamyanchalon main buree-balaaon ko baahar hi rakhane neem kii pattiyon ko lavay kii tarah istemaal karte he. usee tarah devaar bhi neem kii dangaalon kaa sahaara lete he. suar deraa ke baahar neem kii pattiyaan khonsi jaati hai. apane sangithik upkaran ko bhi hareli par puujate hai. lekin vyapak taur par hareli kaa utsav nahin manata.
 devaron kii pramanik pahchaan unka saanskrithik gyaan hai. jan-saamaanya main bhi unke isi rup kii sarvaadhik khyaati hai. inhen pratishtha dilwane main gayan, vaadan evam nrity par inkaa achook adhika mana gaya hai. is janm-jaat or asaadhaaran kala-gyaan ke chalate har hameshaa se devaar jeevant bane hue hai. jivan ke prayek pal main geet nrity kii khanak deevaaron kaa jaateey gun hai. inaki isi visheshta ke darshan rosemara kii dincharya main siankal ke samay main deraa main aasaani se kar sakate hai. jivicoparjan kaa ek thos madhyam to yah hai hi, vaadya, gayan evam nartan in teen binduon ke sahare bhi inaki visheshtayen samajhi ja sakati he. sangitak bhed ko aadhaar maane to raipuriha or ratanpuriha devaron kii alag-alag pahchaan hai. jo inhen samajhane main bhi sahaayak banate hai.
