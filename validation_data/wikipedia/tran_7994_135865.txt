  crasnodar kroy cocks ke pahari kshetra main sthit rus kaa ek sangheey khand he, jo roosi prashasan pranali main 'kroy' kaa darja rakhata he. isaki rajdhani or prashasnic centre crasnodar naam kaa shahar he. iskaa kshetrafal 76,000 varg kimi he or san 2010 main isaki jansankhya 52,25,826 thi.
 crasnodar kroy cocks kshetra main he or isamen cocks parvat shrunkhlaa se uttar kaa kuch bhaag or in parvaton kii mahakocus shrunkhlaa kaa kuch ansh aata he. is kroy ke pashchim main azov sagar he, jiske paar ukraine sthit he. isaki anya seemayen rus ke rostov oblast, stavropol kroy or karaachaae-charakassia ilaaqon se or georgia se algaavvaad kar rahe abkhaazia ganatantr se lagati hai. rus kaa ek or rajya he, adigey ganatantr, jo puri tarah se crasnodar kroy se ghira hua he.
 kuban nadi crasnodar kroy ko do alag hisson main baantati he. dakshini bhaag main mausam bumadhya sagar ke tatavarti ilaaqon jaisaa he - garmiyon main adhik garmi nahin padati or sardiyon main thand to hoti he lekin bhayankar thand nahin padati. uttari bhaag kaa mausam stapi ke kshetron jaisaa he - jahaan garmi bhi bahut hoti he or sardiyon main baari barfabaari ke saath-saath taapmaana bahut hi neeche jaataa he.
 is kshetra kii adhiktar aabaadi kuban nadi or uski naharon ke ird-gird rahati he. crasnodar kroy roosi-mul ke kazakh yaa kosa logon kaa ilaaka he or is kshetra ke 85% se adhik log roosi hai. yahan charakas log or arminian log bhi rahete hai.
