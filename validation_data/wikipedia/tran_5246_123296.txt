 sarjana bi. ai. tii., sindri se prakashit hone vaali vaarshik santhaan patrika he. iskaa madhyam hindi or angareji donon he. yah mukhya rup se sahitya ko samarpit he. is patrika kaa armbh saamanantar ke naam se hui kalantar main iskaa naam sarjana ho gaya. iske mukhya sangrakshak nideshak, bi. ai. tii., sindri shri es.ke.singh. hai. iske vartmaan sanyojak doe. ke. es. choudhary hai. iske mukhya praadhyaapak shri ravi shankar prasad hai. iske vartmaan mukhya sampadak shri manish vatsa hai.
 iske sadasyon kaa chayan bi. ai. tii., sindri ke pratham semester ke chaatro ke bich se hota he. sarjana main chayan hetu rachanashiltaa, sahaj saahitiyik pratibha kii parakh kii jaati he. iske liye ek likhit pariksha hindi athwa angreji main lee jaati he. tatpashchaat antarviksha kaa ek satr hota he. inkaa chayan sarjana main patrkaar ke rup main prarambh hota he.
 
 sarjana vitan sarjana ke purvavarti sadasyon dwaara sanchalit mukhya maargdarshi sanstha he. tathaapi isamen sarjana ke antim varsh ke chaatr bhi shaamil hai. iskaa vaarshik aayojan sarjana visamvaad he. iskaa aayojan ek saahitiyik mahotsav ki tarah hota he.
