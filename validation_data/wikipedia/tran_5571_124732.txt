 tungatamapi tungta maapan ke liye prayog me aanevaala ek yantra he.
 sabase saadhaaran prakaar kaa yantra chinhit, shushk dabmapi hota he, jisamen maapak kaa ankan is prakaar hota he ki nirdeshsuchika vibhinn sthanon kii daab ke antar evam yantra main daab parivartan ko swat: oonchaai ke rup main vyakt kar sake. taap tathaa sthaneey vaayu-daab-janit trutiyon ko shuddh kar is yantra kan dwaara vayuyan aadi kii oonchaai gyaat kii ja sakati hai. saath hi iske dwaara avarohan adde kii bhi oonchaai gyaat kii ja sakati he. duusare prakaar ke yantra ko bhupradesh nirvadhika suchak kehete hai. jisamen prayukt radio laharon kii oochaal dwaara dhwani evam pratidhwani main laganevaali kalavidhi se vibhinn sthanon kii oonchaai gyaat kii ja sakati hai.
