 pakhtunistan yaa pathanistan, shabd "pathan" or "stan" ke sangyojan he. iskaa arth sansar kaa vah aitihaasik kshetra jisamen pathan logon kii jansankhya sarvaadhik he or jahaan pashto bhashaa boli jaati he. pakhtunistan kaa adhik hissaa afghanistan or pakistan kii bhoomi main he lekin bharat or iran kaa bhi chota sa hissaa shaamil he.
 pakhtunistan shabd kaa upyog angrezon ne tab kiya jab vah bharat par shashan kar rahe the, british raaj ne pashchimi-uttari kshetron ko pakhtunistan kaha karte the or yahi kaaran se is naam sampurna bhartiya upmahadweep main prasiddh hua or phir is kshetra ko pakhtunistan kaha gaya he.
