 abdur rajjak ek bangladeshi cricketer he, jo vartmaan main test cricket kheltaa he or khel ke simit praroopon se sevanivrutt hota he. desh dwaara utpaadit sabase achche gendbaajon main se ek mana jaataa he, rajjak oriai main 200 wicket lene vaale pehle bangladeshi hai. saqlan mushtak ne hat-trick lene ke baad vah pehle baayen haath ke spinner or duusare spinner bhi hai. vah twanti-20 antarrashtriya main bangladesh kii pehali topi he.
 unhone 2001/02 sijan main khulena divijan ke liye bangladeshi gharelu str par apni pratham shrenee kii shuruyaat kii. ek lambey baayen haath ke roodhiwaadee spin gendbaaj hone ke liye sarvshereshth jane ke liye, unhone apane pehle satr main rashtriya vibhaajan sheershak main apane vibhaajan kaa maargdarshan karne main madad kii. vahaan se unhen bangladesh e ke liye chunaa gaya tha, jo ki zimbabwe e ke khilaaf acha pradarshan kar rahaa tha, jisamen dhaka main ek khel main 17 ran dekar 7 wicket shaamil the.
 haalanki unhone sirf nau test khele hai, lekin vah vanade international main or adhik safal rahe hai or praaroop main bangladesh ke agrani wicket lene vaale hai.
 janvari 2018 main, vah pratham shrenee cricket main 500 wicket lene vaale pehle bangladesh khilaadi bane. chaar saal baad, unhone srilanka ke khilaaf 8 farvari 2018 ko test team main vaapasi kii.
