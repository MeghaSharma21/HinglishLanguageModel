 kaanke jharkhand ke ranchi jile kaa ek kasbaa he. yahan par ek vishaal jalashay he jise 'kanke baandh' kehete hai. isi se ranchi shahar ko jal aapurti kii jaati he. yahan kaa kendriya manashchikitsa santhaan prasiddh he. iske atirikt yahan birsa krishi vishvavidhyalay, koyala prabandhan santhaan, pashuchikitsa santhaan evam kuch anya santhaan sthit hai. ranchi railway stesan se kanke dam kii duuri paanch kilometre he.
