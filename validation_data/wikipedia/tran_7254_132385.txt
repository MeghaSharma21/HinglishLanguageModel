 kosi pariyojna bharat kii ek pramukh nadi ghati pariyojna he.
 jab saptakoshi nadeeki barh bihar rajyamen tawaahi machaana suru kiya tab se angrej shaskaon ne hi kosi tatabandh nirmaan kar barh niyantran kii adhyayan suru kiya tha. "1779 aasapaas major je. renal ne, san 1863 maa james fergusan or uski baad egge siliofield ne kosi bainki adhyayan kii. jab 1869-70 kii barh biharki purniamey tandav rachaa tab angrej kosi tatabandh banaakar baadhi niyantran kii nishkarshme pahunche the."
 britisane nepalki udaipur jilaaki barah kshetramen band nirmaan kii prastaav laayaa. nepalki rana pradhanmantri birasamsher barahakshetrase 5-6 kilometre niche chatraamen band nirmaanke liye raji hue. britis raaj khatam honeke baad main swatantra bharat sarkaar is prastaavko or ghaneebhoot rupamen uthaaya. vibhinn charanki chhalfal or adhyayan ho ke beepi koirala pradhanmantri hote vakt kosi or gandak nadeemen band nirmaanki samjhaute hue. nepal bharat simavarti hanumananagaramen san 1965 ko kosi band nirmaan sampann huwa.
 6 april 1947 ko nirmali me baadh peediton kaa sammelan hua tha. jisame lagbhag 60 hajaar log sammilit hue the. is sammelan me rajendra prasad, shrikrishna singh, gulzaarilal nanda, lalit narayan, harilal aadi desh ke bade netaa shaamil hue the. yojana mantri si ech bhabha ne kosi par barah me band ke nirmaan kii ghoshnaa kii. isse 12 lakh hectares sichaai hogi ttatha 3300 megawatt bijli banegaa. 1954 me aupchaarik rup se kosi pariyojna kaa nirmaan kaa kaam shuru kar diya. us samay mana gaya tha ki 15 varsh main baadh kii samasya par kaabo paa liya jaayegaa.
