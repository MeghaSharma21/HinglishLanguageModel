 mehsana ke shankheshwar main sthit yah jain tirthasthal 125 feet oonchi padamavati devi kii aakarshak pratimaa ke liye prasiddh he. padamavati devi kii murti ke alaava saraswati devi, mahalakshmi devi, nakoda bharavji or manibhadraveer kii pratimaayein bhi yahaan dekhi ja sakati hai. is tirthasthal main yatriyon ke thaharne kii vyavastha he. is tirth ke nikat hi 108 bhakthivihar parswanath, shri agam mandir tirth, bhaktaamar mandir or gurumandir bhi dekhe ja sakate hai.
