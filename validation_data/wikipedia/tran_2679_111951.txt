 yahan bharat main paayi jane vaali pakshiyon kii suchi di gayi he. bharat main pakshiyon kii koi 1301 prajatiyaan hai jinamein se 42 keval bharat main paayi jaati hai; 1 maanav dwaara viksit he tathaa 26 durlabh prajatiyaan hai. mor bharat kaa rashtriya pakshi he.
 suchi main pakshiyon ke paye jane kaa vivaran is prakaar he:-
 isaki vishwa main 21 jatiyaan hai or bharat main 1 pai jaati he.
 inaki vishwa main 156 jatiyaan/prajatiyaan maujuud hai tathaa bharat main 46 pai jaati hai.
