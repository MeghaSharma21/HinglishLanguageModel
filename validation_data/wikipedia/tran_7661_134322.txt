 gleez 581si ek super prithvi he or yah apane saura mandal se pare he. yah laal boune taare gleez 581 kaa chakkar lagaataa rahata he. esa mana gaya he ki taare ko gherne vaale kshetra kaa taapmaana rahan yogya ho sktaa he or pani kii upasthiti kii bhi sambhaavna he.
