 dictaphone ek america company thi, jo shrutlekh mashin kaa nirmaan karti thi - ye dhwani recording upkaran the jinka sabase aam istemaal bhaashan ko record karnaa tha jise baad main chalaaya jaataa tha yaa mudrit kiya jaataa tha. "dictaphone" naam ek trademark he, lekin kuch sthanon main aise sabhi upkarnon ko sanderbhit karne ke liye iskaa prayog aam he or ise ek jatigat utpaada trademark ke rup main istemaal kiya jaataa he. vartmaan main dictaphone, boston aadhaarit nuance communications kii ek shakha he.
 dictaphone kaa shuruuaati vikaas, volta leboratory main hua, isaki sthapana 1881 main washington, di.si. main alexander graham bel dwaara kii gai thi. jab prayogshaala dwaara dhwani recording kaa avishkaar paryaapt viksit ho gaya tab bel or unke saathiyon ne volta graphophone company kaa nirmaan kiya jiskaa baad main america graphophone company ke saath vilay hua or baad main colombia records ke rup main viksit hui. 
 1907 main colombia graphophone company dwaara "dictaphone" naam ko trademark banaaya gaya, jo ki jaldi hi is prakaar ke upkarnon ke nirmaataaon main agrani nirmaataa ban gai. isamen dhwani recording ke liye mom silendaron ke upyog ko banaae rakhaa gaya tha. ve sangeet recording ke liye unki jagah discs technology ko pasand karne lage. si. king woodbrij ke netritv main 1923 main dictaphone ek alag company ke rup main sthapit hui.
 dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke ant tak mom silendar recording kaa istemaal kiya jaataa tha lekin 1947 main dictaphone ne dictabelt praudyogiki kii shuruuaat kii, jisamen mom silendar ke bajaae ek plastic kii belt main yantric nalee hoti thi. baad main ise chumbakeey dhwani recording ke dwaara parivartit kiya gaya, jise 1979 tak bechaa gaya tha. chumbakeey tepe ricorder kii shuruuaat sattar ke dashak ke ant main hui, shuruuaat main "si" str ke casette kaa upyog kiya gaya . iske baad jaldi hi mini casette ricorder, micro casette ke upyog ko jari kiya gaya. pehali baar japan main jevisi dwaara is upkaran kaa nirmaan kiya gaya lekin iskaa dizin or vikaas dictaphone dwaara kiya gaya tha. casette kaa aakaar mahatvapurn tha kyonki yah nirmaataa ko portable ricorder ke aakaar ko kam karne ke liye sakasham banaata tha, jo ki lokapriy ho rahaa tha. baad main dictaphone ne 1985 main jari hue pico casette ko jevisi ke saath milakar viksit kiya. yah chota tha, lekin phir bhi isamen acha recording samay or gun tha.
 dictaphone ne chumbakeey tepe kaa istemaal karte hue "antaheen loop" recording ko bhi viksit kiya. ise sattar ke dashak ke madhya main "thut tanks" ke rup main pesh kiya tha. iske kai sanskarnon ko jari kiya gaya or yah lokapriy vikalp ban gaya vishesh kar swasthya dekhbhaal ke peshey ke bheetar kyonki shrutlekh jahaan pahunch saktaa tha vahaan recording media nahin pahunch sakati thi, yahi kaaran tha ki yah aise parivesh ke liye adarsh tha jo ki pradooshan yaa sankraman ko prerit kar saktaa he. is pranali kaa istemaal typing pool ke bheetar kiya ja saktaa he or ek sanskaran prayek typist ke liye samay kii gananaa karata tha or shrutlekh ke agle tukade ko tivratam samay vaale typist ko aavantit kiya jaataa tha.
 dictaphone multi channel ricorder main pramukh the. inkaa istemaal apatkaaleen sevaaon main apaat telephone kol ko or baad ke radio vaartalap ko record karne ke liye bade paimaane par kiya jaataa tha. assi ke dashak main jaise-jaise vittiya bazaar mukt hota gaya in ricarderon kaa istemaal vittiya udyog main saudebaaji vaale kamare ke vaartalap ko record karne ke liye kiya gaya. yah recording reel se reel tepe par hoti thi or use praapt kiya ja saktaa he or taarikh or samay ke anusaar chalaaya ja saktaa he. assi ke dashak ke uttarardh main digital recording kii peshkash, reel se reel tepe ke ek vikalp ke rup kii gai or jaldi hi media main lokapriy ho gaya.
 1979 main, dictaphone ko pitni boes dwaara kharida gaya, lekin ek purna swaamitv vaali lekin ek swytt sahaayak ke rup main rakhaa gaya. is avadhi ke dauraan dual disple verde procesar company ko kharida gaya jo udyog main pramukh company vaing kii ek pratiyogi thi. pisi, emes dos or verde processing packages jaise verde parfect ke aagaman se samarpit verde procesar or shakha ko band hote paaya gaya.
 1995 main pitni bowes ne kathith taur par $462 million main nivesh samooh estonington parteners of connectikat ko dictaphone bech diya.
 baad ke varshon ke dauraan, dictaphone ne kai shreniyon ke utpaadon kii bikri kii, jisamen dhwani pahchaan or voicemel software shaamil he.
 2000 main, us samay kii agrani belgian dhwani pahchaan or anuwaad company lernout and hospi dwaara kareeb 1 arab dollar main ise adhigrahit kiya gaya tha. lernout and hospi ne dictaphone ke pratilekhan pranali ke saath voes ricognation pranali ke liye voes ricognation pradaan kiya.
 kharid ke turant baad, america companiyon ke badhate swaamitv ke chalate - dictaphone ko kharidne ke baad, ek maheene ke bheetar hi lernout and hospi ne america main apane pramukh vois ricognation pratidwandvi dregon systams ko kharid liya, jo ki pehle nuans communications ink. dwaara adhigrahit tha - esaisi ne lernout and hospi ke vitt se sambandhit sawaalon ko uthaaya, jisamen purv asia udhyam se iske darj aay ko kendra main rakhaa gaya tha jisase yah prathit hota tha ki is samay ke dauraan yah safalataa kii bulandiyon par tha. baad main company or uske sabhi sahaayak diwalia ho gaye .
 2002 ke shuru main, dictaphone, adhyay 11 se ek nijee sanstha ke rup main ubhara jiske chairman or siio rob shejar the, jabaki shesh sampatti tukdon main bant gai or scansoft ke saath vyaktigat rup se bech di gai, jise vartmaan main nuans communications ink ke rup main janaa jaataa he, isane america main dregon system or voes ricognation anusandhan karmi jaise vyavsaay ko adhigrahit kiya.

 june 2005 main, dictaphone ne apane communications recording solushans ko $38.5 million main naaise system ko bech diya, jise udyog main bada sauda mana jaataa he. iske baad se hi simit safalataa ke saath swasthya udyog ke liye spich ricoganishan main apane lakshyon par dhyaan kendrit karata he, mukhya rup se apni sthapit brands ke naam ko or ooncha karne par dhyaan detaa he.
 sitambar 2005 main, dictaphone ne america se baahar IVS vyapaar ko ek swiss company ko bechaa, dictaphone IVS agi, udorf, switzraland, jisane sammilit spich ricognation or workelo menagement ke saath pehla hardware swatantra shrutlekh prabandhan software samaadhaan ko viksit kiya. switzraland main ispich agi ne purv ke kellison agi ke kriyalap or utpaadon ko hasil kar liya.
 2006 ke farvari or march main, shesh dictaphone ko $357 million main nuans communications ko bechaa gaya jise pehle scansoft kaha jaataa tha, ek swatantra company ke rup main kuch hi samay bitaane ke baad ise 2002 main shuru kiya gaya or lernout or hospi ko bechney ke chalate prabhavi rup se kaaryakramon ke chakra ko samaapt karte hue 2000 main shuru kiya gaya .
 march 2007 main, nuans ne focus informatics ko adhigrahit kiya or kuch shuruuaati safalataa ke saath healthcare transcripshans main or vistaar karne ke liye dictaphone divijan ke saath ise joda.
