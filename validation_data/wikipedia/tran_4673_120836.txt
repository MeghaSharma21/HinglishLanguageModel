 jain dharm main moksh kaa arth he pudgal karmon se mukti. jain darshan ke anusaar moksh praapt karne ke baad jeev janm maran ke chakra se nikal jaataa he or lok ke agrabhag siddhashila main virajman ho jaati he. sabhi karmon kaa naash karne ke baad moksh kii prapti hoti hai. moksh ke uparaant aatma apane shuddh swaroop main aa jaati he. aisi aatma ko siddh kehete he. moksh prapti har jeev ke liye ucchtam lakshya mana gaya he. samyak darshan, samyak gyaan, samyak charitra se ise praapt kiya ja saktaa he. jain dharm ko mokshamarg bhi kaha jaataa he.

 nirvana yani samast karmon se mukti. jab sadhu ghatiya karm arthath jo aatma ke gunon kaa naash karte he, aise karmon kaa naash karte he, to keval gyaan prakat hota he. or jab kevali bhagwaan bache hue aghatiyaa karmon kaa naash karte he or sansar sagar se mukt ho jate he to vah nirvana kehalaata he. moksh or nirvana shabd jain granthon main ek duusare kii jagah prayog kiye gaye he. nirvana ke uparaant arihant siddh ban jate he. jain granthon ke anusaar tirthankar bhagwaan ke paanch kalyanak devon dwaara manaae jate he, inamen antim nirvana kalyanak hota he.
