 vasudev sharan agarwal bharat ke itihaas, samskruti, kala evam sahitya ke vidwaan the. ve sahitya academy dwaara puraskrit hindi gadhykar hai.
 hindi gadhy ke lokavishrut rachanaakaar vasudev sharan agarwal ji kaa janm uttar pradesh ke meerut janapad main sthit kheda naamak gram main 6 agast,1904 i. ko hua tha. mata kaa nivas lakhanu main hone ke kaaran inkaa bachapan yaheen vyateet hua. mata-pita kii chaatra-chaaya main rahakar apni shiksha bhi apane yaheen praapt kii lakhanu vishvavidhyalay se 1929 main em•e• karne ke pashchaat 1940 tak mathura puraatatv sangrahalay ke adhyaksh rahe. 1941 main pi-ech•di• tathaa 1946 main di•lit• kii oopaadhi praapt kii. 1946 se 1951 tak central asian anticuities museum ke suparintendent or bhartiya puraatatv vibhaag ke adhyaksh rahe. san 1951 main kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay ke kalege of indolaji main professor kaa pad sushobhit kiya. san 1952 main lakhanu vishvavidhyalay main radhakumud mukherjee vyaakhyananidhi kii ore se vyaakhyaata niyukt hue. vyaakhyanmala 'panini' par yojit kii gayi thi. iske atirikt bhartiya mudraparishad, bhartiya sangrahalay parishad, ol india oriental congress, fine art section aadi sansthaaon ke sabhapati bhi rahe.
 vasudevasharan agarwal ke krititv evam tajjanit yash kaa amar aadhaar unke dwaara sanskrit evam hindi ke anek granthon kaa kiya hua saanskrithik adhyayan evam vyakhya he. sanskrit main kalidas evam banbhatta ke granthon se lekar puraana evam mahabharat tak tathaa hindi main vidyapati ke avahatth kavy se lekar jaaysi ke avadhi bhashaa ke amar mahakavy 'padmavat' tak vishaal evam bahuaiami granthratn unke avagaahan ke vishay rahe hai. "paninikalin bharatvarsh" naamak unki kriti bharatvidya kaa anupam granth he. isamen unhone panini ke ashtaadhi ke madhyam se bharat kii samskruti evam jeevandarshan par prakaash daalaa he. unhone bhashaa evam sahitya ke sahare bharat kaa pun: anusandhan kiya he or usamen vaigyanik evam tarkpurna vidhi kaa prayog kiya he. yah granth vishvakosiya swaroop kaa ho gaya he or anukramanika ke sahare kosiya rup main uskaa adhyayan sulabh bhi he or uttam bhi.
 unki chaynit pratinidhi rachanaaon ko padhane ke liye sahitya academy dilli se prakashit uttam brihat sanchayan he vasudevasharan agarwal rachana sanchayan.
