 khaadya tel yaa paak tel, vanaspatiyon se praapt vasa kaa parishuddh rup hote hai. yah saamaanya taapmaana par taral avasthaa main rahete hai. . 
 khaadya telon ke udhaaharan hai: sarson kaa tel, jaitoon kaa tel, tad kaa tel, soyabean tel, canola tel, kaddu ke beej kaa tel, makka kaa tel, surajamukhi tel, moongfalee tel, til kaa tel, chaaval kii bhusi kaa tel aadi. inke alaava or bhi bahut se vanaspati telon kaa prayog khaanaa pakaane main kiya jaataa he.
 jab ham khaas taur par "vanaspati tel" kii baat karte hai tab hamaara abhipraay kisi khaas tel yaa telon ke mishran se taiyaar kisi vishisht tel se hota he. khaadya telon main taaja jadi booti, lahasun, mirch aadi jaise khaadya padaarthon ko milakar unka khaas swaad or khushboo pradaan kii ja sakati he. haalanki yadi in khushboodaar telon kaa bhandaaran ek lambey samay ke liye karnaa ho to unamem jeevanu punap sakate hai.
