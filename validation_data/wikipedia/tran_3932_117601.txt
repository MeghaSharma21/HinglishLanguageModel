 manilla devi mandir bharat ke uttrakhand rajya ke almoda zile ke shalt kshetra main sthit he. yah sthaan ramnagar se lagbhag 70 kimi kii duuri par sthit he. yahan tak bas yaa nijee vaahan dwaara pahunchaa ja saktaa he. yah is kshetra kaa ek prasidd mandir he or har varsh yahan dur-dur se log devi ke darshanon ke liye aate hai. yah mandir katyuri logo kii paariwarik devi manilla kaa mandir he.
 mandir do bhaagon main banta hua he jise sthaneey bhashaa main mel manilla or tal manilla kehete he. pracheen mandir to tal manila main hi sthit he lekin mel manilla mandir ke peeche kii kahani jo sthaneey logo ke bich bahut prachlit he wo ye hai ki "ek baar kuch chor manilla devi kii murti ko churaana chaahate the. lekin jab wo murti uthaa rahe the to uthaa na sake or tab ve murti kaa ek haath hi ukhaad kar le gaye, lekin us haath ko bhi ve adhik dur naa lejaa sake." or tabase vahi par mata kaa ek or mandir sthapit kar diya gaya jise aaj mel manilla ke naam se log jaanate he.
