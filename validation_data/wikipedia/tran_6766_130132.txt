 bandra-varli samudrasetu 8-len kaa, taar-samarthit kankreet se nirmit pul he. yah bandra ko mumbai ke pashchimi or dakshini upnagaron se jodta he or yah pashchimi-dweep mahamarg pranali kaa pratham charan he. 16 arab rupee kii maharashtra rajya sadak vikaas nigam kii is pariyojna ke is charan ko hindustan construction company dwaara puraa kiya gaya he. is pul kaa udghaatan 30 june, 2009 ko sanyukt pragatisheel gathbandhan pramukh shrimati sonia gandhi dwaara kiya gaya lekin jan saadhaaran ke liye ise 1 july, 2009 ko madhya-raatri se kholaa gaya. saadhe paanch kilometre lambey is pul ke banane se bandra or varli ke bich yatra main lagane vaala samay 45 minute se ghatakar maatr 6-8 minute rah gaya he. is pul kii yojana 1980 ke dashak main banaayi gai thi, kintu yah yatharth rup main ab jaakar purna hua he.
 yah sethu mumbai or bharat main apane prakaar kaa pratham pul he. is sethu-pariyojna kii kul laagat 16.50 arab ru he.is pul kii keval prakaash-vyavastha karne ke liye hi 9 karod ru kaa vyay kiya gaya he. iske kul nirmaan main 38,000 ki.me ispaat rassiyaan, 5,75,000 ton kankreet or 6,000 shramik lage hai. is sethu main lagane vaale ispaat ke khaas taron ko china se mangaya gaya tha. jang se bachaane ke liye in taron par khaas tarah kaa paint lagane ke saath plastic ke aavaran bhi chadaaye gaye hai. ab taiyaar hone par is pul se guzarane par yatriyon ko chungi kar dena tay hua he. yah chungi kiraaya prati vaahan 40-50 ru tak hoga. is pul kii kul 7 ki.me ke yatra-samay main lagbhag 1 ghante kii bachat or kai sau karod vaahan sanchaalan vyay evam eendhan kii bhi katautee hogi. is bachat ko dekhte hue isaki chungi naganya he. pratidin lagbhag sava lakh vaahan is pul par se guzrenge.
