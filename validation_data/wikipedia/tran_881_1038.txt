 
 rajiba bai begum 
 raaj singh pratham mewar ke shishodiya rajvansh ke shaasha the. ve jagat singh pratham ke putr the. unhone aurangzeb ke anekon baar virodh kiya.
 rajnagar ke raja maharana raaj singh ji kaa janm 24 sitambar 1629 ko hua. unke pita maharana jagat singh ji or maa maharani madetaniji thim. maatr 23 varsh kii choti umr main unka rajbhishek hua tha. ve na keval ek kalapremi, jan jan ke chahete, vir or dani purush the balki ve dharmanishth, prajapalak or bahut kushal shashan sanchaalan bhi the.
 unke rajyakaal ke samay logon ko unki daanveerta ke bare main jaanane kaa mauka mila. unhone kai baar sone chaandi, anmol dhaatuen, ratnadi ke tuladaan karawaaye or yogya logon ko sammanit kiya. rajsamand jhil ke kinare nauchoki par bade-bade pachaas prastar patton par utkeern raaj prashasti shilaalekh banawaaye jo aaj bhi nauchoki par dekhe ja sakate hai. inke alaava unhonen anek baag bageeche, fawwaare, mandir, baavadiyaan, rajaprasaad, ddhaar or sarovar aadi bhi banawaaye jinamein se kuch kaalaantar main nasht ho gayi. unka sabase bada kaary rajsamand jhil par paal baandhna or kalapurna nauchoki kaa nirmaan kaha ja saktaa he.
 ve ek mahaan ishwar bhakt bhi the. dwarikadhish ji or shrinath ji ke mewar main aagaman ke samay swayan palki ko unhone kandha diya or swaagat kiya tha. unhone bahut se logon ko apane shashan kaal main aashray diya, unhe duusare akramanakariyon se bachaaya va sammanpurvak jeene kaa avasar diya. unhone ek rajput rajkumaari charumati ke satitv kii bhi raksha kii.
 unhone orangzeb ko bhi jajia kar hataane or nirparaadh bholi janata ko pareshaan naa karne ke bare main patr bhej daalaa. kaha jaataa he ki us samay orangzeb kii shakti apane charam par thi, par prajapalak raja rajasihji ne is baat kii koi parvaah nahin kii.
 rana raaj singh sthapaty kala ke bahut premi the. kushal shilpkaar, kavi, sahityakar or dastkaar unke shashan ke dauraan hameshaa uchit sammaan pate rahe. vir yoddhaon va yogya saamanto ko ve khud sammanit karte the.
