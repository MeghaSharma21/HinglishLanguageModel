 raajaaon ke maranoparant unki yaad main sthapaty kii drushti se vishisht smarak banaaye gaye, jinhen chhatariyaan tathaa deval ke naam se janaa jaataa he. chhatariyaan zyaadaatar bhartiya rajya rajasthan main dekhne ko milti he. jisamen jaipur kii gator, jodhpur kii jaswant thada ,kota kaa chhatra vilaas baag, jaisalmer kaa bada baag to is drushti se bahut hi mahatvapurn he. jaisalmer ke pile pathar se bani chhatariyaan sundar he. chhatriyon main hindu or muslim donon shaileyon kaa mishran paaya jaataa he. sabase neeche chaukor athwa aath konon kaa chabutara banaaya jaataa he. in chabutaron ke upar doosraa gol chabutara banaaya jaataa he.
 84 khambhon kii chhatari - shatrusaal kaa smarak bundi shaasha - shahjahan kaa samakaleen. bundi ke devpura gaanv ke paas raav raja aniruddh singh ke dhaibai deva kii smriti main banaai gayi. teen manjila chhatari jisamen 84 bhavy stambh hai. 
 bundi se lagbhag 4-5 kilometre dur sthit kesarbagh main bundi shaskaon tathaa rajpariwaron kii 66 chhatariyaan hai. inamen sabase pracheen maharaj kumaar dadu kii he tathaa sabase naveen maharaja raja vishnu singh kii chhatari he. in chhatriyon par raajaaon ke saath hui sati hui raniyon kii murtiyan bhi utkeern he. 
 inaki chhatari rajasthan ke alvar ke bala durg ke neeche sagar ke dakshin kinare hindu sthapaty kala kii is 80 khambhon kii chhatari kaa nirmaan maharaja bakhtarasingh kii musee rani kii smriti main maharaja vinay singh ke kaal main hua tha. safed sangemarmar evam laal pathar se nirmit is chhatari kii upree manzil par ramayana or mahabharat ke bhitti chitra bhi bane hue hai.
 ye chhatariyaan bhi rajasthan ke alvar zile main sthit he. yahan sthit mishraji kii chhatari vishesh rup se prasiddh he jiskaa nirmaan lagbhag 1432 isvi main kiya gaya tha. is chhatari ke guband ko aath samakon khadey khambhon par sthapit kiya gaya he. is chhatari kii visheshta bhitti chithran kaa bemisaal alankaran he. 
 mondor ke paas panchkunda ke nikat sthit ye chhatariyaan bhavyatapurna nakkashi kii kul 42 chhatariyaan he jinamein rani surya kanvar kii 32 stambhon vaali chhatari sabase badi or bhavy he. iske alaava yahan maharaja maansingh kii maharani bhatiyani rani kii chhatari unche chabutare par bani hui he.
 mondor jodhpur main panchkunda ke nikat laal balui pathar kii chhatari ,mehraangadh durg ke tantric anushthan main jis brahman ne atmbalidaan diya ,uski he. yah shilp kala or sthapaty kala kaa adbhut najaara he. 
 jodhpur se paanch kilometre dur rishi shreshth kaag bhushundi kii tapobhumi ,jahaan bhagwati ganga bhi rishi ke tap se prakat hui thi, yahan lagbhag 150 chhatariyaan bani hui hai. kaaga main sheetala devi kaa mandir bhi he jiskaa nirmaan jodhpur ke naresh vinayasingh ne karavaaya tha. is mandir ke paas dewan dipachand kii chhatari sthit he. kaaga kii chhatriyon ke paas maharaja jaswant singh ke kaal main bak naamak jaadugar dwaara ek din main lagaaya gaya anaar kaa bagichaa he.
 kaaga kii chhatriyon ke madhya pradhanmantri kii chhatari he. yah chhatari 18 stambhon par laal pattharon se banaai hui he. maharaj jaswant singh kii praan raksha hetu unke pradhanmantri rajsingh kumpavat ne atmbalidaan kiya tha. isi kii smriti main is chhatari kaa nirmaan maharaja jaswant singh ne karavaaya tha.
