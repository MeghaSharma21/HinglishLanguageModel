 guna bharat ke madhya pradesh praant kaa ek shahar he. yah shahar madhya pradesh ke uttar main sthit he. 35 kilometre dur rajasthan seemaa he, parvati nadi madhya pradesh or rajasthan ko alag karti he. guna malwa kaa pravesh dwaar kaha jaataa he or gwalior sambhag main aata he. guna shahar 77' deshaantar tathaa 25' akshansh tathaa rashtriya rajmarg 3 par sthit he. kota or binaa shahar se rail maarg dwaara bhi yahan pahunchaa ja saktaa he. shahar main mukhyatah hindu, muslim tathaa jain samudaay ke log nivas karte hai.. kheti yahan kaa mukhya kaary he. guna pracheen avanti kingdom chand pradyot mahesena dwaara sthapit kaa hissaa tha. baad main shishusangh avant kaa rajya he, jo magadh ke badhate samrajya ke guna shaamil joda.18 veen sadi main, guna maratha netaa ramoji raav sindhiya ne vijay praapt kii, or sheeghra hi bharat kii aazaadee ke baad jab tak gwalior ke rajya kaa hissaa bana rahaa tha. guna rajya ke isagarh jile ke hisse ke rup main dilaai. 1897 main bhartiya railway midland ek rail maarg guna se guzar nirmaan kiya.bharat kii swatantraaa prapti, guna apane 16 jilon main se ek ke rup main 28 may 1948 ko madhya bharat ke nae rajya kaa hissaa ban gaya. 1 november, 1956 ko madhya bharat madhya pradesh rajya main vilay kar diya gaya tha.guna ke paas bajrangarh naamak aitihaasik sthaan he. yahon pracheen kila he. kile main bhagwaan shri ram janaki evam shri hanuman ji ke etehasik prasiddh mandir he jo sabhi kii aasthaa ke kendra hai yahan mahavir bhagwaan kii pracheen murti he. swatantraaa se pehle guna gwalior raaj gharaane kaa hissaa tha. jis par sindhiya vansh kaa adhikaar tha. kul kshetrafal 6484.63 varg kimi tathaa jansankhya 838026 he.
