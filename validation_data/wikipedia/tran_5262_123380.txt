 sanaa said bhartiya abhinetri or model he, jo bollywood filmon main dikhaai detii he. vah sabase pehle kuch kuch hota he main ek bal kalakaar ke rup main dikhaai deen or har dil jo pyaar karegaa or badal main aisa karnaa unhone jari rakhaa. vah televijan kaaryakramon main bhi dikhaai deen jaise babul kaa angan chute naa or lo ho gai puja is ghar kii . 2012 main, sanaa ne karan johar kii student of da year main sahaayak bhoomika main ek vayask ke rup main apane film kariyar kii shuruyaat kii. film box offis vyavsaayik safalataa ke rup main ubhararee thi. vah jhalak dikhalaa ja, nach baliye or feare factor: khataron ke khilaadi sahit kai reality kaaryakramon main dikhaai di he.
 vah gambhir or vyavsaayik rup se safal filmon kuch kuch hota he, badal or har dil jo pyaar karga main ek bal kalakaar ke rup main dikhaai deen. said ne star plus par prasaarit prasiddh bacchon ke kaarykram fox kids kii bhi mejabani kii jisamen unhone chatur chachee kii bhoomika nibhaai. vah soni tivi par babul kaa angan chutti naa or esabi tivi par lo ho gei puja iz ghar kii television sho main bhi dikhaai deen.
 sanaa nrity reality sho main chathe sthaan par rahi or naachne main unke urja ke str kii saraahna kii gai. jhalak dikhalaa ja. 
 2012 main, said ne karan johar ke chaatr of da year main mukhya bhoomika siddharth malhotra, varun dhawan or aalia bhatta ke saath ek sahaayak bhoomika main apni pehali film banaai. unki choti bhoomika ke baavjood unhen apane bal sitara smriti ke kaaran dekha gaya tha. bollywood hangamaa ke film aalochak taran adarsh ne likhaa, "sanaa saaed glamras dikhataa he or acha karata he". film aalochak komal nahata ne tippanee kii, "sanaa said keval tanya ke rup main apane sharir ko jhukaa or fatkaarta he." film ko desh bhar main 1400 se adhik screenon main 1 9 oktober 2012 ko release kiya gaya tha or aalochakon or achche box offis sangrahon se mishrit samikshaon ke liye sakaaratmak hasil kiya gaya tha. Boxofficeindia ne film ko teen hafton ke baad ek semihite ke rup main ghoshit kiya. usne naach bali 7 main 5 wein sthaan par rakhaa.
