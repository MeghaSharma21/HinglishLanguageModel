 
 palash ek vriksh he jiske phool bahut hi aakarshak hote hai. iske aakarshak phoolo ke kaaran ise "jangal kii aag" bhi kaha jaataa he. pracheen kaal se hi holi ke rang iske phoolo se taiyaar kiye jate rahe he. bharat bhar me ise janaa jaataa he. ek "lata palash" bhi hota he. lata palash do prakaar kaa hota he. ek to laal pushpo vaala or doosraa safed pushpo vaala. laal phoolo vaale palash kaa vaigyanik naam butia monosperma he. safed pushpo vaale lata palash ko aushadhiy drushtikon se adhik upyogi mana jaataa he. vaigyanik dastavejo me dono hi prakaar ke lata palash kaa varnan miltaa he. safed phoolo vaale lata palash kaa vaigyanik naam butia parviflora he jabaki laal phoolo vaale ko butia superba kaha jaataa he. ek pile pushpon vaala palash bhi hota he.

 palas ke pattey praayah pattal or done aadi ke banane ke kaam aate hai. rajasthan or bengal main inase tambaakoo kii bidiyaan bhi banaate hai. phool or beej oshadhirup main vyavahrut hote hai. vies main pet ke keede maarane kaa gun vishesh rup se he. phool ko ubaalane se ek prakaar kaa lalaai liye hue pilaa ranga bhi nikalataa he jiskaa khaskar holi ke avasar par vyavhaar kiya jaataa he. fali kii bukani kar lene se vah bhi abir kaa kaam detii he. chaal se ek prakaar kaa resha nikalataa he jisko jahaaj ke pataron kii daraaron main bharkar bheetar pani aane kii roak kii jaati he. jad kii chaal se jo resha nikalataa he uski rassiyaan bati jaati hai. dari or kaagaj bhi isse banaaya jaataa he. isaki patali daaliyon ko ubalkar ek prakaar kaa kattha taiyaar kiya jaataa he jo kuch ghatiya hota he or bengal main adhik khaaya jaataa he. moti daaliyon or tanon ko jalaakar koyala taiyaar karte hai. chaal par bachne lagane se ek prakaar kaa gond bhi nikalataa he jisko 'chuniyaan gond' yaa palas kaa gond kehete hai. vaidyak main iske phool ko swaadu, kadava, garam, kasaiyla, vatavardhak sheetaj, charpara, malrodhak trisha, dah, pitt kaph, rudhiravikaar, kusht or mutrakruchh kaa naashak; fal ko ruukhaa, halakaa garam, paak main charpara, kaph, vaat, udrarog, krimi, kusht, gulm, prameh, bavasir or shul kaa naashak; beej ko stigdh, charpara garam, kaph or krimi kaa naashak or gond ko malrodhak, grahani, mukherog, khaansi or paseene ko dur karanevaala likhaa he.
 yah vriksh hinduon ke pavitra maane hue vrikshon main se hai. iskaa ullekh vedon tak main miltaa he. shrotrasutron main kai yajyapatro ke isi kii lakdi se banane kii vidhi he. grihvasutra ke anusaar upanayan ke samay main brahmankumar ko isi kii lakdi kaa dand grahan karne kii vidhi he. vasant main iskaa patraheen par laal phulon se ladaa hua vriksh atyant netrasukhad hota he. sanskrit or hindi ke kaviyon ne is samay ke iske saundarya par kitni hi uttam uttam kalpanaayen kii hai. iskaa phool atyant sundar to hota he par usamen gandh nahin hote. is visheshta par bhi bahut si uktiyaan kahi gai hai.
