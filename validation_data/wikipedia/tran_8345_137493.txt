 rupasahi reetikaal ke reetigranthkaar kavi hai.
 ye bagmahal panna ke rahanevaale or jati ke guniyaar kysth the. inke pita kaa naam kamalnain tathaa pitamah kaa shivaram tha. inke ashrayadata the chhatrasaalavansheey maharaj hindoopati singh . kavi ne inhen ke prityrth apane prasiddh granth 'rupavilas' kii rachana kii, jisaki samaapti 4 sitambar san 1756 i. ko hui thi. 
 nagariprachaarini sabha, kaashi ke yaajik sangrahalay main isaki ek hastlikhit prati vartmaan he. puure granth main 900 dohe hai or vah 14 'vilaason' main vibhakt he. yah reetigranth kavy ke sarvang par vichaar karata he- kavyalakshan, chand, nau ras, nayak nayika, alankaar or shadritu, aadi aadi. alankaaron ke varnan main kavi ne, 'bhashaabhushan' kii paripati ko grahan kar lakshan or udhaaharan ek hi dohe main die he. duusari mahatvapurn baat yah he ki inhone anek kavyavrittiyon ko vibhinn rason kaa samarvaay mana he. is prakaar, kavi kaa krititv jitna kavyashastreey vivechan kii drushti se mahatv kaa he utana kavitv ke vichaar se nahin.
