 formik amla ek carbonic yogic he. yah laal chintiyon, shahad kii mackhiyon, bichchhu tathaa barron ke dankon main paaya jaataa he. in keedon ke kaatane yaa dank maarane par thodaa amla sharir main pravisht ho jaataa he, jisase vah sthaan phool jaataa he or dard karne lagata he. pehle pahal laal chintyon ko pani ke saath garam karke, unka sat khinchne par usamen farmik amla mila paaya gaya. isiilie amla kaa naam 'formik' padaa.
 iskaa upyog rabad jamaane, rangaai, chamada kamai tathaa carbonic sanshleshan main hota he.
 yah ekaksharaki vasa amlon kii shrenee kaa pratham sadsy he. duusare vasa-amlon ke vipareet formik amla tathaa foment tej apchaayak hote hai or apchayan gun main ye eldihide ke samaan hote hai. yah rajat lavenon ko rajat main, fehling vilyan ko laal kuprus oxide main tathaa maracurik chloride ko mercury main apachayit kar detaa he. iskaa sutara HCOOH he. ise methil alcodaul yaa formaldihide ke upchayan dwaara, oxylik amla ko shighrata se garam karke athwa oxylik amla ko glisreen ke saath 100 se 110 degree sein. tak garam karke praapt kiya jaataa he.
 ajal farmik amla banane ke liye, lead yaa tamra foment ke upar 130 degree sein. par hydrogen sulphide pravaahit kiya jaataa he. sandra formik amla ko sodium formet ke 90% formik amla main bane vilyan ko sandra salphurik amla ke saath asut karke banaaya jaataa he. yah teevr gandhvala rangahin drav he. yah kisi bhi anupaat main pani, alcohul tathaa ether main mishry he. iskaa kvathanank 100.8 degree sein. he. twachaa par girane par bahut jalan hoti he or fafole ban jate hai.
