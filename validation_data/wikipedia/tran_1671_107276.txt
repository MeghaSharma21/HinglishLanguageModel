 saamanyat: audyogic vaastu ke antargat aisi imaaraten tathaa kaarkaane aate hai jahaan vastuon kaa praarambhik nirmaan, utpaadan, sangrah or kray-vikray hota he. aisi imaarte hai : kal-kaarkaane, mil, vidyutsakti centre, tailashodhan centre, pradarshan kaksh, annsangraahak or godaam ityadi. mulat: in imaaraton kaa nirmaan vyaavhaarik dhang par hona chahie, arthath inkaa dhaanchaa aisa ho jisase kam se kam kharch se, sthaan, saamagree or dhan kaa apvyay bachaate tathaa kaarykushalta ko akshunn rakhte hue ye us vishisht uddeshy ko siddh kar saken jiske liye inkaa nirmaan kiya jaataa he. ye imaaraten or kaarkaane jin logon ke upyog main aate hai unhen paryaapt suraksha or adhik sukh suvidha praapt ho sake, iskaa puraa dhyaan rakhana aavashyak hota he. aakaar prakaar main bhi in imaaraton ko susantulit, manoram or bhavy hona chahie.
 aarambh main bharat main audyogic imaaraten mukhyat: shateer, eent or pattharon se banati thim or ekamanzili hi hoti thim. shaharon main, jahaan bhoomi kaa mulya apekshaakrit bahut adhik hota tha, ye imaaraten dumanjili banati thim. teen yaa isse adhik manjilonvali imaaraten to bahut hi kam thim. lambii dharanon ke na mil sakane ke kaaran chat ke neeche paas-paas khambhe rakhane padate the jisase imaarat ke bheetar kaa ek bada bhaag kisi kaam main na aa paata tha. aage chalakar jab loha sulabh hone par khambhe ispaat ke hi dharan, kainchiyan or khambhe banaae jane lage jisase khambhe dur-dur rakhe ja saken or kaam ke liye kaarkaane ke bheetar adhik sthaan milane laga. saath hi ispaat ke payon par khadey kiye gaye kai manzil ke bhawanon kaa nirmaan bhi sambhav ho sakaa.
 prabalit cement kancreet, acchhee jati ke ispaat or aluminium kii mishra dhaatuon ke vikaas se audyogic imaaraton kii dizine, nirmaan or saaj sajja main acchhee pragati hui. telephone, lift tathaa swachaalit sanvahan se is pragati main or tivrata ai.
 audyogic imaaraton ke nirmaan ke liye upyukt sthaan kaa chunaav karte samay nimnlikhit baton par dhyaan dena aavashyak hai : vidyutsakti or jal sasta or paryaapt maatra main mil sake. aavashyak maatra or santoshjanak rup main shram sulabh ho. kachche maal or aavashyak upkaran ko uchit vyay or suvidhajanak reeti se praapt karne tathaa prastut maal ko baahar bhejne ke liye samudr yaa nausanvahan yogya nadi, rail line or pakki sadak ho. vyavsaayjanya raddi saamaanon ke uchit vikray kii suvidha ho. bhoomi bhavannirmaan yogya ho or pados aisa ho jisase bhavishya main udyog kaa kam kharch se suvidhajanak evam santoshjanak rup se vistaar sambhav ho sake. yudhkalin bambaari jaise jokhimon se bachane ke liye yathasambhav janakeern env samrik mahatv ke kshetron ko nahin chunanaa chahie.
 sthaan kii aavashyaktaa par saavdhaanee se vichaar karnaa chahie. vibhinn ekakon kii rachana badi satarkta se kerani chahie jisase dainik kaarysanchalan main shakti kaa apvyay na ho or na sthaan, saamagree, shram yaa dhan kii barabaadi ho. aayojan saral hona chahie jisase kam se kam kharch main pratishthan main kaary karanevaalon kii kaaryakshmata adhik se adhik badhaai ja sake or unhen adhiktam sukh suvidha praapt ho sake. jalwaayu kii sthiti, vayupravah kii disha, varsha kii maatra aadi par bhi uchit dhyaan dena aavashyak he. imaaraten ekamanzili hon yaa kai manjilon kii, yah udyogvishesh kii apni aavashyaktaaon, bhoomi ke aapekshik mulya, bhoomi kii sthiti tathaa kshetrafal aadi par nirbhar he. kai manjilonvali imaaraton main agni ke niyantran ke liye swachaalit vyavastha honi chahie jisase bime kaa kharch kam ho. agnikand or sankat ke samay nikal bhaagane kaa bhi uchit prabandh aavashyak he. lift or swachaalit sopanon kii vyavastha bhi ho sake to acha he.
 yah dhyaan rakhana aavashyak he ki prayek vibhaag kaa vistaar samay aane par uchit reeti or kam vyay se kiya ja sake or isse utpaadan main koi hraas na ho. pratishthan ke vistaar ke anuroop jalpan evam bhojangrih, vishramkaksh, shauchalay, bahumoolya vastuon ko rakhane ke liye surakshit sthaan, chikitsaalay evam kridaangan aadi kalyankari suvidhaayen bhi nitant apekshit hai. vaastu ko prabhaavshaalee banane ke liye bhawan ke aakaar prakaar, banaavat, saushthav or samyak anupaat kaa dhyaan rakhana chahie. karmchaariyon kii manodasha or maansik vrittiyon par rangon ke aayojan kaa bada prabhaav padataa he, jisase antat: utpaadan ke pariman or achchhai donon prabhavit hote hai. pratishthan kii bhitari diwaalon kii rangaai halke rangon se yaa safed honi chahie. imaaraton main roshni kii paryaapt vyavastha honi chahie jisase nirantar ekarup prakaash mil sake, kintu chakachaundh na utpann ho. prakritik prakaash kaa adhiktam laabh uthaanaa chahie. raat ke samay kritrim prakaash ke rup main bikharakar aayaa bijli kaa shwet prakaash apekshit hota he. praaia: vidyunnalikaay sarvaadhik suvidhajanak hoti hai. iske liye prakritik or kritrim donon prakaar kii vyavasthaayen kii ja sakati hai. tambaakoo, aushadh or vastrodyog jaise pratishthanon main, jahaan taap evam ardrata kaa niyantran or dhulikanon kaa dur rakhana bahut aavashyak hota he, vaayu anukulan kii bhi vyavastha kerani padati he. audyogic imaaraton kaa nirmaan agnisah hona chahie.
 kuch deshon main kaarkaanon kii vruddhi itani adhik hui he ki shaharon main unka bananaa asambhav ho gaya he. isliye bade kaarkaane shahar se dur banaae jate hai or paas main hi kaaryakartaaon ke liye grih, paathshaala, udyaan, aspataal, bazaar, cinema aadi sabhi vishesh rup se banaae jate hai. is prakaar prayek kaarkaana ek chota sa nagar hi ho jaataa he.
 karyaalayon ke liye bhawan bhi audyogic vaastu ke antargat gine jate hai. videshon main kuch itane bade karyaalay hai ki ve tees manzilen yaa isse bhi unche banaae gaye hai. ispaat ke dhaanche ke avishkaar ke pehle aise unche karyaalayon ke nimnatam khand main jagah bilkul nahin bachati thi, kyonki aavashyak dridhtaa ke liye deevaaren badi moti banani padati thim. udhaahrant:, 348 foot unche ek karyaalay ke nimnatam khand kii deevaaren 20 foot moti thim. san 1884 main pehali baar aisa bhavana bana jisamen ispaat kaa kankal tha or sab chhaton or saamaan kaa boj isi kankal par tikaa tha. isamen deevaaren bahut patali thim or unka bhi bhaar kankal par hi sanbhala hua tha. peeche ispaat ke gardaron ko lavangit karne ke badale welding jodne kaa upyog hone laga. tab vaanchit dridhtaa ke liye bahut halke kankalon kaa hi prayog hone laga or bahut unche bhawan banane lage. parantu bahut unche bhawanon main itane alevetaron kii aavashyaktaa padane lagi ki bahut sa upyogi sthaan unheen main lag jaataa tha. ab swayanchal elistaron ke prayog se is samasya kaa bhi hal nikal aayaa he.
 bhawanon ko agnisah banane ke liye aavashyak he ki ispaat ke garder aadi cement-kancreet main dabe rahen, anyathaa bhawan ke bheetar rakhe samaan ke jalane par ve tapt hokar naram pad jate hai or bhawan gir padataa he.
 prakaash adhik aa sake, is abhipraay se kabhi-kabhi kaanch kii eenton se diwaar bana di jaati he. yadi aisa na bhi kiya jaae to kaanch lagi badi khidakiyon se kaam liya jaataa he. kankalayukt bhawanon main deevaaron par to koi boj rahata nahin, isliye unako praaia: kaanch se hi bharna sambhav hota he. videshon main bahut se kaarkaanon main diwaar kaa 90 pratishat kaanch hota he; parantu bharat main dhoop se bhi bachanaa rahata he; isliye itnaa kaanch nahin lagaaya ja saktaa. kankalayukt bhawanon main khambhon ke bich 30 foot v 60 foot kaa sthaan sugamawa se rakhaa ja saktaa he. hawaii jahaaj ke kaarkaanon main isse bhi bade chauke rakhe jate hai. dwitiya vishwayuddh ke baad england main bane ek kaarkaane main 358 foot v 420 foot chauke hai. aise bhawanon par pade garder sade nahin, kanchi yaa pulon par prayukt kankalmay garder kii tarah yaa mehrab hote hai.
 pichley vishwayuddh main isaki bhi aavashyaktaa padi ki audyogic bhawan shighrata se bane. tab aisi nirmaan reetiyaan nikaali gayi ki varshon kaa kaam saptahon main hone laga. safalataa pramanik naap ke avyavon or byaaron se mili. udhaahrant: sab kaarkaanon main vishisht naapon ke kaksh banate the or daravaaje, khidkiyaan aadi vishesh naapon ke or vishesh melon ke hi lagaae jate the.
