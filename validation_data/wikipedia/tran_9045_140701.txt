 dashakumarcharit, dandi dwaara pranit sanskrit gadhykavy he. isamen dash kumaron kaa charit varnit hone ke kaaran iskaa naam "dashakumarcharit" he. 
 dandi dwaara rachit "avantisundari katha" naamak gadhykavy kii khoj se dashakumarcharit kii samasya or bhi jatil ban gai he. ye vikalp upasthit hote hai ki kya dashakumarcharit or avantisundari katha donon ke rachayita dandi ek hi hai athwa bhinn-bhinn hai; or yadi in donon ke lekhak ek hi dandi maan liye jaaen, to kya ye donon gadhykavy ek duusare ke poorak hai athwa do swatantra gadhykavy hai. kuch vidvan is paksh main hai ki avantisundari katha hi mul dashakumarcharit kaa khoyaa hua bhaag he or donon milakar ek hi gadhykavy hai. vidwaanon kaa doosraa varg is vichaar se bilkul sahamat nahin he.
 dashakumarcharit ke hastlikhit tathaa prakashit sanskarnon main praaia: teen bhaag hai-
 inamen se granth kaa madhya bhaag hi, jo aath uchhvaason main vibhakt he, dandi kii moulik kriti mana jaataa he, parantu kyonki inamen rajvahan kii asampurn katha evam saat mitron kii kathaayen hai at: vah asampurn he. shesh bhaag arthath purvapithika or uttarpithika anya lekhakon kii rachanaayen hai jo kalantar main mul granth ke aadi or ant main kramash: jod di gai he. vidwaanon kii dharna he ki dandi ne pehle avashya purna granth kii rachana kii hogi, kintu baad main karanvash ve bhaag nasht ho gaye. dandi ke mul granth ke aath uchhvaason main keval aath kumaron kii katha aati he. kintu purvapithika main di gai do kumaron kii katha milakar das kumaron kii sankhya puri ho jaati he. isi prakaar mul granth ke aathven uchhvas main varnit apurna vishrut charit ko uttarpithika main puraa kiya gaya he.
 bhoj ne apane saraswati kanthabharan main purvapithika kaa stuti shloke uddhrut kiya he. at: yah bhaag gyarahavin shatabdi ri. purv hi jud chukaa hoga. uttarpithika ke kari sanskaran hai- ek chakrapani kaa, doosraa narayan kaa, or kuch bhaag padmanabh ke. kyonki mukhya katha main ullikhit baton se purvapithika evam uttarpithika main kari sthanon par virodh paaya he at: purvapithika tathaa uttarpithika ko pashchaadvarti siddh karnaa sugam he. udhaahrant: mukhya bhaag main arthpal tathaa pramati kampal ke putr hai jinaki maataaon ke naam kramash: kantimati tathaa taravali he, parantu purv-peethika main arthpal, taravali kaa putr he or pramati, sumati naamak mantri kaa. isi prakaar mukhya bhaag main vishrut ke pitamah kaa naam sindhudutt he parantu purvapithika main unka naam padmodbhav he. punasch mukhya bhaag main rajkumaar rajkumaari ke anuj kii sahaayta se ant:poor main pravesh paata he parantu purvapithika main ek sahakaari vidyadhar kii sahaayta se. isi prakaar granth main anek virodh evam vipratipattiyaan vidhman hai.
 mul dashakumarcharit ke pratham uchhvas kaa aarambh ekaek mukhya nayak rajkumaar rajvahan ko ek bandi ke rup main prastut karte hue hoti he. baad main champa ke abhiyaan main rajvahan ko uske bhuule bhatke sabhi saathi mil jate hai jo shesh saat uchchhavaason main apni-apni romaanchakaari evam kutuhljanak ghatnaon ko rajvahan se kehete hai. duusare uchanvaas main uphaar verma kii katha aati he jo atyant vistrut ev vinodpurn hai. marichi naam ke rishi tathaa vaastu-paal naam ke shreshthi kaa pravanchanaa bahut rochak he. nayak ke dyutshala main anubhav tathaa gharon main send lagaana aadi hasyapurna ghatnaayen hai. tritiy uchhvas se lekar shashth uchhvas tak kramash: uphaar verma, arthpal, pramati tathaa mitragupta kii kathaayen hai. tisare uchhavas main uphaar verma kii katha he. usne rajypahari ke man par adhikaar karke apni adbhut soundarya dene kii shakti ke vishay main sujhaayaa. is prakaar soundaryashakti ke bahaane se unki hatya kar di tathaa apane pita ke khoe hue rajya ko waapas liya. chaturth uchhavas main batalaaya gaya he ki arthpal ne apane pita ke khoe mantripad tathaa manikarnika naamak rajkumaari ko praapt kiya. pancham uchhavas main pramati shravasti kii rajkumaari kaa panigrahan karata he. yuvaraj kii yatra ke varnan main graamy tathaa naagarik jivan kaa anek prakaar kaa ullekh praapt hota he. pashtha uchhavas main mitragupta ke dwaara khunh desh kii rajkumaari kii prapti kaa varnan he. isamen dandi ne samudr par kiye gayi saahason kaa varnan kiya he. saptam uchhavas main mantragupta kii katha he. is uchhvas kii yah visheshta ullekhneey he ki isamen kathavaktaa kaa oshth uski preyasi dwaara kaate jane ke kaaran oshth se uccharyman pavarg ke varnon kaa prayog nahin hua he. ashtum uchhavas main vishrut ke saahasik kaaryo kaa varnan he jo vidarbha desh ke rajkumaar ko uskaa khoyaa hua rajya dilaataa he. isamen raajnneetishastra par parishkrut vyang he. anand evam sugam jivan kaa shashakt pratipaadan trutipurna hetu par adhrith he. ekaek prarambh ke samaan yahan kriti ekaek samaapt ho jaati he.
 sanskrit sahitya main dashakumarcharit kaa anupam sthaan he. vastut: yah sanskrit gadhykavy aadhunik upanyaason ke bahut nikat he. yah tatkaaleen samaaj kaa sarvangin yatharth chitra upasthit karata he. dashakumarcharit dhoortta se purna kavy kaha jaataa he. dyutkrida, send lagaana, chaalaaki, dhoortta, pravanchanaa, hinsa, hatya, jaalsaji, apharan evam avaidh prem ek-ek athwa samuhik rup main sab kathaon main milte hai. lekhak kaa samaaj ke prati vyavhaar ativ sopalambh he. brahran, sanyasi, raja, rajkumariyaan evam pavitra saadhuon kaa uphaas kiya gaya he. devataaon ko bhi nahin choda gaya. parantu iskaa uddeshy aniatikta kaa samarthan karnaa nahin he. logon kii mithyaa manyataon kaa vishleshan karke samaaj ke sammukh prastut karnaa hi inkaa mukhya uddeshy he.
 mukhya kathavstu ko avaantar kathaon ke saath badi kushalta se piroyaa gaya he. katha kaa pravaah jo aadi se ant tak abadhgati se chalata he, rochak evam kutuhalavardhak he. uplabdh pracheen sanskrit gadhykavyon main dandi kaa dashakumarcharit hi ek aisa gadhykavy he, jo saral evam swaabhaavik gadhy style main likhaa gaya he.
 dandi kaa charitra-chithran bahut prabhaavshaalee he. unhone marichi rishi, ganika kammanjari, tathaa dhatri shringaalika tathaa sipaahiyon ke afasar kantak ke jivan-chitra pradarshit kiye hai. kavi dwaara prastut manoranjan sthitiyon main hasya evam vyangy abhivyaapt hai. nissandeh dandi kaa bhashaa par adhikaar he parantu vah badha chadhaakar vastuon kaa varnan nahin karte or apni kavyakala kii praudhata kaa sookshm drushti se pradarshan kar pathak ke hriday par prabhaav daalate hai. sanskrit aalochak unke utkrisht shabd soundarya kii saraahna karte hai. vidwaanon main yah prasiddh ukti prachlit he 'dandinah padlaalityam' or iske dandi adhikari bhi hai. anupras ke lalit prayog se dandi manoranjak dhwani prabhaav utpann karte hai. ve lambe samaason kaa prayog karte hai parantu arth kii vyakti main aspashtataa nahin aane dete. unke varnan prabhaavshaalee evam hridaygrahi hote hai parantu ve pathak ke hriday ko aakarshith kiye rakhte hai kyonki unke alankaaron ke prayog evam varnaneey vishay main ek kalatmak santulan rahata he. dandi ke padlaalitya ke udhaaharan rup prastut hai-
 athwa
 is prakaar ke udhaaharan praaia: prachurya se milte hai or 'dandinah padlaalityam' kii ukti ko siddh karte hai. dandi kaa padlaalitya gaurav kaa vishay he. unki bhashaa main sukumaartha, parishkar, pranjalata, prasad or maadhurya gun praapt hote hai. dashakumarcharit padhane par pathak ko yah anubhuti hoti he ki vah kuch saras rachana kaa rasaswad kar rahaa he. jivan kii anubhutiyaan aankhon ke samaksh utar aati hai or vah kavi ko apana ek atarang mitra sa anubhav karata he. dandi ke gagadhy main na to subandhu ke tulya 'pratyakshrashlesh' kii yojana he or na baan ke sadrush 'sarasasyarvarnapad' kii kritrimata. usamen dainik vyavhaar kii pravaahashil bhashaa he. chote-chote pad or vaakya navashishuon ke samaan krida karte hue drushtigochar hote hai or ve sahasaa hriday ko aakrisht kar lete hai. kathaon or upanyaason main prayukt manish style kaa isamen antarbhaav dikhta he. bhashaa kii sarasata, madhurata or sahaj sundarata neeras main bhi sarasata kaa abhas kar detii he.
