 
 yangiyer madhya asia ke uzbekistan desh ke purvi bhaag main sthit sirdariyaa praant kaa ek shahar he. is shahar kii sthapana san 1957 main kii gai thi jab soviet sangh ne golodanaya stapi ke shushk registani kshetra ko naharon ke dwaara seench kar upjau banaaya tha.
 uzbek bhashaa main 'yangi yer' kaa matlab 'nai bhoomi' hota he.
 yangiyer main kuch udyog hai jinamein nirmaan saamagree, pipen or daamer banane ke kaarakaane shaamil hai. yahan taashkant krishi or sinchai abhiyantriki santhaan kii ek shakha he. iske alaava yahan civil abhiyantriki kaa ek college he. golodanaya stapi ko krishi ke liye anukool banane ke maha-abhiyaan ke smarak ke rup main shahar main ek sangrahalay bhi he.
