 bharat ke madhyapradesh main bundelkhand kii bhu dharaa par janme taaran panth ke sansthaapak acharya taaran taran dev the.
 acharya taaran taran dev kaa janm pushpavati naamak nagari main vi.san.1505 aghan sudi saptami din guuruuvaar ko hua. inaki mata veerashri devi va pita gadhashah the. inhen 11 varsh kii aayu main samyak darshan, 21varsh kii aayu main bramhchary vrath,30varsh kii aayu main saptam pratimaa or saath varsh kii aayu main muni pad grahan kiya. inaki samadhi nisaiji malhargarh main jyeshtha vadi chat vi.san.1572 ko hui. inke jivan main do upsarg hue pehla nadi main dubaaya gaya wo doosraa jehar kaa pyaalaa pilaayaa.
 inhone paanch maton main chaudaa granthon kii rachana kari.
 vichaar mat main acharya taaran taran dev ji ne shri malarohan,panditpuja,kamalbattisi ji granthadhiraj kii rachana kii jisamen mukhya rup se samyak darshan samyak gyaan va samyak chaaritra kaa varnan kiya he.
 isamen poojya gurudev ne shravakachar ji granthadhiraj kii rachana kii jisamen mukhya rup se shravak ke aacharan kaa varnan kiya he.
 isamen poojya swami ji ne upadesh shuddh saar,tribhangisar ji or nyanasamucchy granthadhiraj kii rachana kii.
 isamen poojya shri guru maharaj ne choubeesthana ji evam shri bhaykhipanik mamalpahud ji granthadhiraj kii rachana kii. mamalpahud ji main shri taaran taran mandlacharya maharaj ne 3200 gaathaayen tathaa 150 se adhik phoolanaaon kii rachana kii.
 isamen acharya taaran taran dev ji ne chhadmasthwani,nammala,shatikavishesh,sunnaswabhaav,siddhiswabhaav ji granthadhiraj kii rachana kii.
