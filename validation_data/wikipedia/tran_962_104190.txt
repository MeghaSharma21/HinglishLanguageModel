 bartholom henry alan ek kaalpanik superhiro he jo disi comics dwaara prakashit comics main dikhaai detaa he. yah charitra pehali baar shokes #4 main dikhaai diya tha, or ise lekhak robert kanighar or pencillar carmen infantino dwaara banaaya gaya tha. bairi alan flash naamak ek charitra kaa naveekaran he, jo 1940 ke dashak kii comic kitabon main je gairik ke rup main dikhaai diya tha. bairi alan kaa naam tok sho host bairi grey or steve alan ko jodne se bana he.
 uski mukhya shakti atimanviya gati he; anya sabhi prabhaav aanvik kampan kii gati ko niyantrit karne kii uski kshamta se hi sambhav ho pate hai, jinamein vastuon ke aar-paar nikalne kii kshamta bhi shaamil he. flash ke taur par vah gharshan or hawa pratirodh karne ke liye ek alag laal rang kii poshaak pahentaa he, jise vah paaramparik rup se ek anguthi ke andar sangrahit karke rakhata he.
 bairi alan kii clasik kahaniyon ne hi disi comics kii multivers kii avadharana pesh kii, or is avadharana ne kai varshon main disi ke vibhinn ribooton main ek bada hissaa nibhaayaa. flash paramparaagat rup se hi disi kii pramukh riboot kahaniyon main hameshaa mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaataa rahaa he, or 1985 kii chrysis on infinit arths crossover shrunkhlaa main, multivers ko bachaane ke liye bairi ne apane praan tyaag diye, jiske baad agle 23 saalon tak disi linep se is charitra ko hataa diya gaya tha. niyamit comics main uski vaapasi 2008 main grant morrison kii final chrysis crossover kahani, or jeff johns kii flash: riberth simit shrinkhalaa se hui. tab se usne blicust nite, flashpoint, kanvargence, or disi riberth samet kai crossover kahaniyon main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai he.
 comics ke baahar anya media main bhi yah charitra vibhinn roopantaron main dikhaai diya he. abhinayta jon wesli ship ne 1990 kii sibies televijan shrinkhalaa main bairi alan kii bhoomika nibhaai thi, jabaki grant gastin vartmaan main 2014 kii seedblyoo televijan shrinkhalaa main yah kirdaar nibhaa rahe hai. alan tudic, jorge eeds, james arnald tailor, talisen jaaf, dwite shaltz, maaikel rosenbam, nil patric haris, justin chembers, christopher goraham, josh kiton, adam davin or anya kai abhinaytaon/gayakon ne anemeshan roopantaron main charitra kii awaz pradaan kii he. disi ekstended univers main abhinayta ajra miller bairi kii bhoomika nibhaa rahe hai, miller pehali baar 2016 kii bitman banaam superman main bairi ke rup main dikhe the, or phir unhone suicide squad, or justise lig main yah kirdaar nibhaayaa. bairi alan ke upar disiiu ke antargat flashpoint naamak ek ekal film nirmaanadhin he.
