 saral angrezi angrezi bhashaa kaa sarlikrut rup he. ye bhi roman lipi main likhi jaati he lekin isamen kathin shabd nahin hote -- volchaal ke shabd zyaada hote hai. ye khaas taur par bacchon or aadhe-padhe-likhe logon ke liye banaayi gayi he. isaki ssailing angrezi kii saamaanya ssailing hi hoti he.
