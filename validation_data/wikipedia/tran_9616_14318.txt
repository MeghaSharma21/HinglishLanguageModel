 bhartiya cricket team ne 24 november 2014 se 10 janvari 2015 tak australia kaa daura kiya. daure main do tour maichon or chaar test match shaamil the. pehla test 4 disambar ko brisben main shuru hua tha, lekin philip huge kii maut ke kaaran ise sthagit kar diya gaya tha. iske bajaay, edilade ne 9 disambar se pehle test kii mejabani kii or bristben ne 17 disambar se duusare test kii mejabani kii. tisare test main drow ke baad bhartiya captain emes dhoni ne test cricket se tatkaal prabhaav se unki sevanivritti kii ghoshnaa kii. sidney main antim test main drow ke baad australia ne shrinkhalaa 2-0 se jiti. test match ke baad, australia or bharat ne england cricket team ke saath carlton mid trikoniya series main bhaag liya.
