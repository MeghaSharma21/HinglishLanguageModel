 gatishiltaa jeevigyaan main urja kaa prayog karke swayan ko jagah-se-jagah hilaa paane kii kshamta ko kehete hai. zyaadaatar jaanavar gatisheel hote hai haalanki yah shabd eckoshikeey or saral bahukoshikeey jeevon ke liye bhi prayog hota he. kabhi-kabhi is shabd ko sharir ke andar khaanaa or anya chizon ko sthaan-se-sthaan hilaa paane kii kshamta ke liye bhi istemaal karte hai.
