 amir i milat al haj hafiz pir said jamaat ali shah sahib mohammad alipir sharif jile, sialkot, pakistan main paidaa hue the, dakshin bharat ke paaramparik barelvi muslimon ke ek nakshabandi sufi sant or samuhik netaa bhi the.vah sunni barelvi sanghathan ol india sunni sammelan ke adhyaksh the or shahid ganj masjid main mukhya netaa the. sunni sufi ke charampanthiyon ke bich apni khwahishon ke madhyam se unhone pakistan andolan ko apana samarthan diya or isliye pakistan andolan ke ek pramukh dharmik netaa ban gayi.
