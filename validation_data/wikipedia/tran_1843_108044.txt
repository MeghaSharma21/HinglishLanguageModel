 bangladesh ke samvidhan, bangladesh kii sarvocch nyaayi samhita he. ise 4 november 1972 ko paarit kiya gaya tha. isamen kul 11 bhaag, 153 anuchhed or 27 anusuchiyaan hai. is samvidhan main sanshodhan kaa bhi praavdhaan bhi diya gaya he. samvidhan kaa bhaag 10- anuchhed 142, samvidhan main sanshodhano se sambandhit vishay ko vistrut rup se ankit karata he. samvidhan ke praavdhaanon ke anusaar samvidhan main sanshodhan ke liye rashtriya sansad ke sadsysamooh ke do tihaai sankhya ke sakaaratmak mat kii aavashyak bataai gai he. arthath, samvidhan main sanshodhan tabhi laayaa ja saktaa he jab rashtriya sansad kii do tihaai bahumat iske paksh main apana mat de. 2016 kii sthiti anusaar bangladesh ke samvidhan main kul 16 baar sanshodhan kiye gaye hai.
