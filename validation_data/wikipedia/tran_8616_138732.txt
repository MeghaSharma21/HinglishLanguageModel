  raajnneeti praveshdwar
 pakistan kii nyaaypalika, ek shreneebaddh pranali he jisamen adaalaton ke do virgon he: shreshthatar nyaaypalika or adhinsth nyaaypalika. shreshthatar nyaaypalika, "supreme court ne pakistan ke", "sangheey sharia court" or "paanch ucch nyaayalayon" se bana he, jiske sheersh par "supreme court" virajman he. iske alaava, prayek chaar praanton evam islamabad rajdhani kshetra ke liye ek ucch nyaayalay he. pakistan kaa samvidhan, nyaaypalika par samvidhan kii raksha, sangrakshan va bachaav kaa daayitv saunpta he. naa ucchtam nyaayalay, naa hin, ucch nyaayalay, janajaatiy kshetron ke sambandh main adhikarita kaa prayog kar sakate hai, sivaay anyathaa yadi pradaan kii jaay to. aaazaad kashmir or gilgit-baltistan ke vivaadit kshetron ke liye alag nyaayik pranali he.
 adhinsth nyaaypalika main, civil or aapraadhik janapadiya nyaayalay va anya anek vishesh adaalatein shaamil hai, jo, banking, beemaa, seemaa shulk va utpaada shulk, taskari, drugs, aatankwaad, karadhaan, paryaavaran, upabhokta sangrakshan, or bhrashtachar sambandhit maamalon main adhikarita kaa prayog karti hai. aapraadhik adaalaton ko dand prakriya samhita, 1898 ke tahat banaaya gaya tha or civil adaalatein, pashchimi pakistan civil nyaayalay adhyadesh, 1964 dwaara sthapit kiye gaye the. saath hi, rajasav adaalatein bhi hai, jo ki pashchimi pakistan bhu-rajasav adhinium, 1967 ke tahat kaam kar rahe hai. iske alaava, sarkaar, vishisht maamalon main vishisht adhikaar karyanvit karne hetu prashasnic adaalaton or adhikranon kii sthapana kar sakati he.
 pakistan kii sarvocch nyaayalay, islamic ganraajya pakistan kii sarvocch adaalat he or pakistan kii nyaayik vyavastha kaa sheersh hissaa he or pakistani nyaayik kram kaa shikhar bindu he. pakistan kii sarvocch nyaayalay, pakistan kanoonee or sanvaidhanik maamalon main faisla karne vaali antim madhyasth bhi he. sarvocch nyaayalay kaa sthaayee karyaalay pakistan kii rajdhani islamabad main sthit he, jabaki is adaalat kii kai up-shakhaaen, pakistan ke mahatvapurn shaharon main kaaryshil hai jahaan maamalon kii sunwaayee kii jaati he. sarvocch nyaayalay, pakistan ko kai sanvaidhanik va nyaayik vikalp praapt hote hai, jinaki vyakhya pakistan ke samvidhan main kii gai he. desh main kai sainya sarkaron or asanvaidhanik taanaashaahee sarkaron ke kaarykaal main bhi sarvocch nyaayalay ne swayan ko sthapit kar rakhaa he. saath hi, is adaalat ne sainya shakti par ek vaastavik nireekshak ke rup main swayan ko sthapit kiya he or kai avasaron main sarkaron kii nigaraanee kii he.
 is adaalat ke paas, sabhi ucch nyaayalayon or sangheey adaalat ke upar apiliy adhikaar he. iske alaava yah kuch prakaar ke maamalon par mul adhikaar bhi rakhata he. supreme court ek mukhya nyaayadhish or ek nirdhaarit sankhya ke varisht nyaayadhishon dwaara nirmit hota he, jo pradhanmantri se paraamarsh ke baad raashtrapati dwaara namit kiya jaataa he. ek baar niyukt nyaayadhish ko, ek nirdisht avadhi ko puraa karne or uske baad hi ritair hone kii ummid kii jaati he, jab tak ki ve duraachaar ke kaaran sarvocch nyaayik parishad dwaara nilambit nahin kiye jate hai.
 sangheey sharian nyaayalay yaa vafaaqi sharai adaalat, pakistan kii ek nyaayik santhaan he, jiska kaary yah jaanch va nirdhaarit karnaa he kii desh ke kanoon, sharia kaa paalan karte hai yaa nahin. is nikaay main kul aath musalmaan nyaayadhish hoti hai jisamen mukhya nyaayadhish bhi shaamil hote hai. yah sabhi nyaayadhish, pakistan ke raashtrapati kii manjuuri se niyukt kiye jate hai jinka pakistan kii ucchtam nyaayalay yaa kisi bhi prantiy nyaayalay ke sevanivrutt yaa sevarat nyaayadhish main se chunaa janaa aavashyak he. sangheey sharia adaalat ke maujuuda mukhya nyaayadhish nyaayamurti riyaj ahamed khan hai.
 sangheey sharia nyaayalay ke 8 nyaayadhish main se 3 nyaayadhishon kaa ulema hona aavashyak he, jiskaa kaa adhyayan islamia or vyavastha kaa alam hoga jinhen islamic kanoonon or niyamon main atyadhik moolyankan kaushal praapt ho. is adaalat ke sabhi nyaayadhish 3 saal kii avadhi ke liye tainaat kiye jate hai. kisi bhi nyaayadhish kii dur tainaati pakistan ke raashtrapati ke vivek par badhaayaa ja saktaa he.
 pakistan main paanch ucch nyaayalay yaa aalaa adaalatein hai, jinamein se chaar prayek praant ke mukhyalay main sthit hai. pakistan ne paanchaven nyaayalay sangheey rajdhani kshetra ke liye paarit kiya he jo islamabad main sthit he. paanchaven nyaayalay kii yojana lahore prantiy nyaayalay ne roak laga di thi, or is faisle ko pakistan kii nyaayalay ne 24 disambar 2007 par roak laga di. 
 nimna talika main saare ucch nyaayalay va unki avasthiti di gai he:
 pakistan kii jila adaalatein, pakistan main jila str par gatisheel hai or adaalatein prantiy nyaayalayon ke adheen sanvaidhanik sthiti main kaary nishpadit karte hai. jila adaalatein, pakistan ke sabhi praanton ke har jile main sthapit kii gai hai or yah divaani or aapraadhik maamalon kii sunwaayee ke adhikaar kshetra shaamil hoti hai. prayek jile ke mukhya karyaalayon main, jila adaalaton ke tahat kai saare atirikt jila or satr nyaayadhish kii tainaati bhi hoti he ki jila adaalaton main maamalon kii sunwaayee karte hai. jila or satr mansanin ko jile bhar main sambal car or nyaayik adhikaar hote hai. jila adaalaton main satr adaalat aamtaur par aparaadh jaise hatya, vyabhichaar, chori, chori aadi ke maamalon kii sunwaayee karti he. iske alaava maamuli prakruti ke divaani maamalon kii sunwaayee kaa adhikaar kshetra bhi adaalat ko praapt hota he. prashasnic sevaaon kii behatar aapurti ke liye ab har kasbe or shahar main jila adaalaton ke adheen ek atirikt jila or satr nyaayadhish tainaat kiya gaya he, jo ki har tarah se aavantit gaye kshetra main divaani or aapraadhik maamalon kii sunwaayee ke liye jimmedaar hota he. is adaalatein jab aapraadhik maamalon kii sunwaayee karti hai to yah satr adaalat jabaki divaani maamalon kii sunwaayee ke dauraan jila adaalat kehalaati he. mukademebaaji ke dauraan mahatvapurn maamalon ko sirf jila or satr nyaayadhish kii adaalat main pesh kiya jaataa he. jila or satr nyaayadhish, har jile main ek maamle main jila nyaayadhish ucch mana jaataa he. mukademebaaji ke dauraan agar abhiyogi ke kisi bhi paksh ko jila adaalaton ke faisle par aapatti ho, ve prantiy nyaayalay main sthapit ek aplate board main yachika daayar kar saktaa he, jiskaa uddeshy janata ko paardarshee sabase nyaay kii aapurti he.
 aise kai vishesh adhikranon pakistan main hai; jinamein:
 upabhokta nyaayalayon ke alaava, uparaant ullekith lagbhag sabhi adaalaton ke nyaayadhish, jila evam satr nyaayadhish yaa baraabar yogyata ke hote hai.iske alaava, vahaan rajasav adaalatein bhi maujuud hai jo pashchimi pakistan bhu-rajasav adhinium, 1967 ke tahat kaam karti hai.rajasav nyaayalayon ko, rajasav mandal ke rup main vargeekrut kiya ja saktaa he. inamen ayukta, colector evam pehle or duusare darje ke sahaayak colector jaise adhikari hote hai. prantiy sarkaar, jo prashasnic niyantran rakhane ke liye jimmedaar he, aise adhikariyon kii niyukti karti he.
 pashchimi pakistan parivaar nyaayalay adhinium 1964 paariwarik nyaayalayon ke kshetradhikaar ko niyantrit karata he. in adaalaton vyaktigat sthiti se sambandhit maamalon par anany kshetradhikaar he. parivaar nyaayalay se apil ucch nyaayalay se jhooth bolte hai. in adaalaton main, parivaar nyaayalay main ek jila nyaayadhish, atirikt jila nyaayadhish, yaa ek sarkari adhikari-adhishuchet kii adhyakshata he. har shahar or shahar yaa tehsil ke ek parivaar adaalat he. kuch kshetron main, jahaan yah keval parivaar nyaayalay main he lekin adhikansh kshetron main civil jaj nyaayalayon parivaar nyaayalay ke nyaayadhishon kii shaktiyaan di gai hai. parivaar nyaayalay adhinium, 1964 ke praavdhaanon ke si.pi.si. kii dhara 17 ke anusaar or kaanoon-e-shahadat aadesh parivaar nyaayalay or ek hi khatm karne ke liye lagu farm yaa maamle kaa faisla karne ke liye teji se, thik se apni prakriya ko niyantrit karne ke liye anumati di jaati he nahin kar rahe hai or mahila ke sarvottam hit or suvidha main vaadiyon.
 pakistan ke supreme court main ek mukhya or 16 anya niyukt nyaayadhishon ke hote hai. nyaayadhish ke rup main anubhav ke 5 saal tak yaa vakil ke rup main 15 varshon ke anubhav waal kisi vyakti ko hi sarvocch nyaayalay ke nyaayadhish ke pad ke liye aavedan karne kaa adhikaar he. pakistan ke raashtrapati vyaktiyon ko apane vivek or kanoon ke vibhinn kshetron main anubhav ke aadhaar par supreme court dwaara kii gai sifarish ke bich se nyaayalay ke nyaayadhishon kii niyukti karte hai. supreme court kii siforishein ko raashtrapati par badhyakaari he. abhyasatah, ek niyam ke rup main, supreme court ke sabase varisht nyaayadhish ko mukhya nyaayadhish niyukt kiya jaataa he. 
 prayek nyaayadhish 65 saal kii umr tak pad dhaaran kar sakate hai, jis bich ve jaldi hi istipha dwaara yaa samvidhan ke praavdhaanon ke anusaar pad se hataaya ja saktaa he. arthath, shaareerik yaa maansik aakshamata yaa duraachaar - jisaki vaidhata sarvocch nyaayik parishad dwaara nirdhaarit kii jaati he - ke kaaran koi bhi nyaayadhish keval samvidhan dwaara pradaan kiye gaye praavdhaanon ke aadhaar par pad se kaarykaal purna hone se purv hi hataaya ja saktaa he.
 ucch nyaayalayon ko niyuktiyon kii prakriya, ucchtam nyaayalayon main niyuktiyon ke samaan he. 18 wein samvidhan sanshodhan karne se pehle, ucch nyaayalay niyuktiyaan supreme court kii niyukti kii tarah hi aalochanaon kaa saamana karnaa padaa. vartmaan niyuktiyon supreme court main niyukti ke rup main ek hi tareeke se kiya jaataa he. lekin bajaay 4 ucchtam nyaayalay ke nyaayadhishon, 4 sabase varisht ucch nyaayalay ke nyaayadhishon, prantiy kanoon mantri or prantiy baar council ke ek sadsy, nyaayik ayog main baithne ke ucch nyaayalay ke nyaayadhishon kii niyukti ke bare main faisla karte hai.
 civil jaj san nyaayik magistrate ko bhi, lok seva ayog ke prastaav ke aadhaar par, prantiy ucch nyaayalay dwaara niyukt kiya jaataa he.yah ayog, prativarsh, khule pratispardhaa pariksha yojit karti he, jinamein vibhinn sambandhit vishyon par prashn hote hai. is pad ke liye mul yogyata elelbi kii degree evam 2 varsha kii sambandhit vakaalat kaa anubhav hoti he. likathin pariksha main uteern pratyaashee ko maansik pariksha evam saakshaatkaar se guzaarana padataa he, jiske pashchaat, parinaam ke anusaar, ayog dwaara ucch nyaayalay ko sifaarish bheji jaati he.
 atirikt zila evam satr nyaayadhish ko prantiy ucch nyaayalay dwaara, vakeelon va adhinsth nyaayadhishon ke puure samooh main se chun kar niyukt kiya jaataa he. is pad par niyukti ke liye sakasham hone ke liye, ek vakil ke paas kam-se-kam 10 vaarsh kaa achche naam ke saath anubhav hona chahie, tathaa use ucch nyaayalay dwaara yojit ek pariksha main bhi uteern hona hota he. adhinsth nyaayadhishon ko bhi varishthata ke aadhaar par varisht civil jaj se padonnat kiya jaataa he.
