 nirdeshaank: 20°49′00″N 82°32′00″E / 20.8167°N 82.5333°E / 20.8167; 82.5333nuapada bharat ke orisa praant kaa ek shahar he. yah nuapada jila mukhyalay he. pashchimi orisa kaa nuapada jila madhya pradesh ke raipur or orisa ke bargarh, balangir va kalahandi jilon se ghira hua he. 3407.05 varg kilometre main failaa yah jila 1993 tak kalahandi kaa hissaa tha, lekin prashasnic suvidha ke lihaaj se ise kalahandi se alag ek nae jile ke rup main gathit kar diya gaya. patora jogeshwar mandir, rajeev udyaan, patalganga, yogimath, budhikomana, khariyar, gaudhas jalpratap aadi yahaan ke pramukh paryaton sthal hai.
 yah pashchimi orisa or chhattisgarh ke lokapriy shiv peethon main ek he. maraguda ghati ke maraguda gaanv main sthit is mandir main 6 feet ooncha shivlinga sthapit he. iske nikat hi ram mandir bhi bana hua he. 40 feet oonchi hanuman kii murti yahaan kaa mukhya aakarshan he.
 hinduon kaa yah pavitra tirthasthan khariyaar se 41 kilometre or boden se 6 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. mana jaataa he ki jo vyakti iske garm jal main snaan karata he, use mukti milti he. patalganga main snaan karne ko pavitra ganga nadi main snaan karne ke baraabar mana jaataa he. kaha jaataa he ki jab bhagwaan ram apane vanvaas ke dauraan is kshetra se guzar rahe to sita ko pyaas lagi he. sita kii pyaas bujhane ke liye ram ne dharti par baan chalaaya or patalganga kii utpatti hui.
 yogimath lokapriy pragaitihaasik kaaleen gupha he. is gupha main purapashan kaal ke anek chitra pattharon par bane hue hai. yahaan bani sand kii akruti kaaphi aakarshak he. krishi or pashuon ke chitra aaj bhi us kaal ke jivan ankhon ke saamne upasthit kar dete hai.
 budhikomana khariyaar se 73 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. is sthaan kii lokapriyata yahaan bane bhagwaan pataleshwar shiv ke mandir ke kaaran he. trirath style main bane is mandir ke nirmaan main eenton kaa prayog kiya gaya he. vartmaan main yah mandir kshatigrast avasthaa main he.
 khariyaar nagar ke bichon bich bana pracheen dadhibaman mandir bana hua he. is mandir ko sthaneey log badagudi naam se bhi pukaarte hai. iske alaava bhagwaan jagannatha mandir, hanuman mandir, devi lakshmi mandir or raktambari mandir yahaan ke anya lokapriy aakarshan hai. khariyaar main american evenjalical mishan kii gatividhiyon hoti rahati hai.
 nuapada se 30 kilometre dur sthit is jalprapaat kii kul oonchai 30 meter he. yah jharnaa garmiyon ke dinon main suukh jaataa he. iske nikat hi bhagwaan shiv kaa mandir dekha ja saktaa he. baisakhi parv ke maukey par yahaan dur-dur se bhakton kaa aagaman hota he.
 600 varg kilometre main failaa yah abhayaarany nuapada jile main chhattisgarh kii seemaa ke nikat sthit he. ise barasinha kaa adarsh sthaan mana jaataa he. saath hi tigar, tendue, hena, barking dear, chital, gaur, sloth bear or pakshiyon kii vividh prajatiyaan dekhi ja sakati hai.
 nuapada kaa najdiki airport raipur vimaanashetra main he. yah airport 120 kilometre kii duuri par he or desh ke anek bade shaharon se vayumarg dwaara judaa he.
 nuapada road railway stesan yahaan kaa kareebi railway stesan he, jo nuapada nagar se 3 kilometre dur he. yah railway stesan dakshin purv railway ke visoka patanam-raipur rail line par sthit he.
 rashtriya rajmarg 353 or rajya rajmarg 3 nuapada ko anya shaharon se jodta he. rajya parivahan kii anek basen nuapada ke liye chalati rahati hai.
