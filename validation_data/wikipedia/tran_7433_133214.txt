 sant harmano pedro  yaa saint jozaf di betncourt yi gonzalas ke sant peter ek spani mul ke isai dharmprachaarak or mishanri the jinaki karmbhumi guatemala thi. ve order of aavar ladi of bethelem ke sansthaapak the. tathaa ve na sirf canary dweepasmooh ke pehle mulnivaasee sant the, balki unhen guatemala or puure madhya america kaa pehla sant mana jaataa he.
 tenreef ek saadhaaran parivaar main janme the. apni praathmik vyavsaayik yuvavastha main inhone padri ke rup main dharm ke kshetra main kaam kiya. 23 varsh kii aayu main, inhone isai dharm ke sandesh pravaah ke lakshya se ek dharmprachaarak ke rup main apane desh ko chhod diya or do ​​saal baad guatemala aa pahunche, jo us samay spani samrajya kaa hissaa tha. guatemala main apane nivas ke dauraan vahaan inhone garib, biimaar, anaath or peedit or shoshit logon ke kalyaan ke liye atyant kaam kiya, jiske kaaran isiyat ke madhya america main pravaah ko atyant samarthan mila. inke dwaara kiye gaye maanvi kaaryon ko sadiyon baad madar terresa dwaara kiye gaye maanvi kaaryon ke samanaantar mana gaya he. 25 april 1667 ko 41 varsh kii aayu main inaki achaanak mrityu ho gai. mrityu ke kareeb 350 varsh baad, 2002 main pope jon paul dwitiya ne inhen roman catholic carch kaa ek sant ghoshit kar diya. ve canary dweepasmooh, guatemala or madhya america ke pehle sant the.
 inaki pratimaaon main inhen aksar, peeth par ek bada choga daale, ek haath main canary dweep ke charavaahon dwaara prayog kii jane vaali lathi pakade dikhaaya jaataa he. inaki daavat ko prativarsh 24 april ko manaaya jaataa he.
