 subhash chandra bharat ke ek udhyami, media swami tathaa abhiprerak vaktaa hai. ve bharat ke sabase vishaal tivi channel samooh zee media tathaa assel samooh ke adhyaksh hai jisane bhartiya upgrah televijan prasaaran main kranti kaa sutarapaat kiya. unke dwaara 1992 main sthapit ji tivi bharat main pehla cabel tivi tha. aaj soni evam star-plus aadi ke saath takkar kar rahaa he. emmi puraskaar se navaaje ja chuke chandra kii aatmakatha kaa vimochan pradhanmantri narendra modi ne dilli main 20 janvari 2016 ko hue ek kaarykram main kiya. 11 june 2016 ko ve haryana se rajyasabha sadsy chune gayi.
