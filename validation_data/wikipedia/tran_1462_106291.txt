 asperanto kii pragati chaahane vaale ham log yah ishtihaar sab sarkaron anterrashtriya sansthaaon or sajjanon ke naam de rahe hai; yahan diye gaye uddeshyon kii ore drudh nishchay se kaam karne kii ghoshnaa kar rahe hai; or sab sansthaaon or logon ko hamaare prayaas main jutane kaa nimantran de rahe hai.
 asperanto jiskaa aagaaz 1887 main anterrashtriya sampreshan ke liye ek sahaayak bhashaa ke rup main hua tha or jo jaldi hi apane aap main ek jiti jaagti zabaan ban gai -- pichley ek shatabdi se logon ko bhashaa or samskruti kii deevaaron ko paar karaane kaa kaam kar rahi he. jin uddeshyon se asperanto bolne vaale prerit hote aae hai, wo uddeshy aaj bhi utane hi mahatvapurn or sarthak hai. naa duniya bhar main kuch hi rashtriya bhashaaon ke istemaal hone se, naa sampreshan takneekon main pragati se, naa hi bhashaa sikhaane ke nae taur-tareeqon se, yah nimnlikhit mul yathart ho paayenge jinhen ham sachhee or sadhak bhashaa pranali ke liye anivaary maanate hai.
 loktantraisi sanchaar-pranali jo kisi ek ko khaas faaydaa pradaan karte hue auron se yah chaahe ki ve saalon bhar kaa prayaas karen or wo bhi ek maamuli yogyata praapt karne ke liye, aisi pranali buniyadi taur par alukatantric he. yadhyapi asperanto or zabaanon kii tarah hi, dosha-hin nahin he, par vishwavyapak samaan sampreshan ke liye, asperanto baaki bhashaaon se kahin behatar he.ham maanate hai ki bhashaa asamta sampreshan asamta ko har str par paidaa karti he, anterrashtriya str par bhi. hamaara aandolan loktantric sampreshan kaa aandolan he.
 vishwavyaapee vidyahar jaateey bhashaa kisi naa kisi samskruti or desh se mili judi hui he. misaal ke taur pe, angrezi sikhta chaatr duniya ke angrezi bolne vaale deshon ke bare main sikhta he -- khaas kar, america or england ke bare main. asperanto seekhney vaala chaatr ek aisi duniya ke bare main sikhta he jisame seemayen nahin hai, jahaan har desh ghar he.hamaara mat he ki har bhashaa main di gayi vidya kisi naa kisi drushtikon se judi hui he. hamaara aandolan vishwavyaapee vidya kaa aandolan he.
 prabhaavasheel vidya
 videshi bhashaa seekhney waalon main kuch pratishat hi videshi bhashaa main dharapravaah hasil kar pate hai. asperanto main dharapravaah ghar baith kar padhai se bhi mumkin he. kai shodh-patron main sabit kiya gaya he ki asperanto seekhney se anya bhashaaon kaa siikhnaa aasaan ho jaataa he. yah bhi anushansit kiya gaya he ki bhashaa-chetana ke paathyakramon kii buniyaad main hi asperanto sammilit hona chahie.
 hamaara mat he ki jin vidhyaarthiyon ko duusari bhashaa seekhney se faaydaa ho saktaa he, un vidhyaarthiyon keliye videshi bhashaayen seekhney kii kathinaaiyaan hameshaa ek diwaar ban kar khadi rahengi. hamaara aandolan prabhaavasheel bhashaagrahan kaa aandolan he.
 bahubhashyata
 asperanto samudaay un chune-gine vishwavyaapee samudaayon main se he jinka har sadsy dubhashi yaa bahubhashi he. is samudaay ke har sadsy ne kam se kam ek videshi bhashaa ko bolchaal-str tak seekhney kaa prayaas kiya he. kai baar is kaaran anek bhashaaon kaa gyaan or anek bhashaaon ke prati prem paidaa hua he -- niji maansik-kshitijon main badhautri hui he.
 ham maanate hai ki har insaan ko, chaahe wo choti zabaan bolne vaala ho, yaa badi, ek duusari zabaan ucch-str tak seekhney kaa mauka milanaa chahie. hamaara aandolan vah mauka har ek ko detaa he.
 bhashaa-adhikaar
 bhashaaon kii takaton kaa asmaan vitaran, duniya main zyaadaatar logon main bhashaa asuraksha paidaa karti he, yaa phir yah asamanta khule aam bhashaa atyaachaar kaa rup leti he. asperanto samudaay kaa har sadsy, chaahe wo taqatvar zabaan kaa bolne vaala ho, yaa balhin, ek samaan str par har duusare sadsy se miltaa he, samjhauta karne ke liye tayyar. bhashaa adhikaron or zimmevaariyon kaa yah samtulan, anya bhashaa asamantaaon or sangharaanon kii kasuti he.
 bhashaa jo bhi ho, bartaav ek hi hoga -- ham maanate hai ki kai anterrashtriya samjhauton main vyakt kiya gaya yah siddhant, bhashaaon kii takat main asamantaaon ke kaaran nasarthak ban rahaa he. hamaara aandolan bhashaa-adhikaron kaa aandolan he.
 bhashaa vibhinnata
 sarkarein duniya kii vishaal bhashaa vibhinnata ko sampreshan or tarakkee ke raste main ek diwaar maanati hai. magar asperanto samudaay bhashaa vibhinnata ko ek atyavashyak or nirantar dhan ke rup main dekhati he. is kaaran, har bhashaa, har prakaar ke jeev-jantu kii tarah, sahaayta or suraksha ke laayak he.
 hamaara mat he ki sampreshan or vikaas kii nitiyaan jo sab zabaanon ke sammaan or sahaayta par aadhaarit nahin he, vah nitiyaan duniya ke adhiktar bhashaaon ke liye sazaa-e-maut sabit hongi. hamaara aandolan bhashaa vibhinnata kaa aandolan he.
 maanav bandhanmukti
 ham maanate hai ki keval jaateey bhashaaon par aadhaarit rahanaa abhivyakti, sampreshan or sanghathan kii swatantraaa main avashya hi baadhaayen paidaa karti he. hamaara aandolan maanav bandhanmukti kaa aandolan he.
