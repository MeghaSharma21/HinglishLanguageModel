 shilaras ek atyadhik upyogi padaarth hai, jiskaa upyog denic jivan main bahut adhik hota hai. shilaras vastav main udprangaron kaa mishran hota he. iskaa nirmaan bhi koyale kii tarah vanaspatiyon ke prithvi ke neeche dabane tathaa kalantar main unke upar ucch daab tathaa taap ke aapatan ke kaaran hua. prakritik rup se paae jane vaale shilaras ko aprishkrut tel kehete hai jo kaale rang kaa gadha drav hota he. iske prabhaji aswan se kerosine, petrol, deazal, prakritik gas, vaseline,tarkol lubricant tel ityadi praapt hote hai.
 darasal jab tel ke bhandar prithvi par kahin dhunde jate hai, tab yah gadhe kaale rang kaa hota he. jise crude oil kaha jaataa he or isamen udprangaron kii bahulata hoti he. udprangaron kii khaasiyat yah hoti he ki inamen maujuud hydrogen or prangaar ke anu ek duusare se vibhinn shrinkhalaaon main bandhe hote hai. ye shrinkhlaayein tarah-tarah kii hoti hai. yahi shrinkhlaayein vibhinn prakaar ke tel utpaadon kaa strot hoti hai. inaki sabase choti shrinkhalaa mithen naamak product kaa aadhaar banati he. inamen lambii shrinkhalaaon vaale udprangaron thos jaise ki mom yaa tar naamak utpaada kaa nirmaan karte hai.
 jab prithvi se tel khod kar nikaala jaataa he us vakt aprishkrut tel thos rup main hota he. isse tel ke vibhinn rup paane ke liye aprishkrut tel main maujuud udprangaar ke vibhinn chen ko alag karnaa padataa he. udprangaar ke vibhinn chenon ko alag karne kii prakriya rasinic krans jodne udprangaar kehalaati he. jise ham shodhan prakriya ke naam se jaanate hai. yah shodhan prakriya shodhan kaarkaane main hoti he. ek tarah se yah shodhan behad aasaan bhi hota he or mushkil bhi. yah saral tab hota he jab crude oil main paae jane vaale udprangaron ke bare main pataa ho or mushkil tab jab isaki jaankaree nahin hoti he. darasal har prakaar ke udprangaron kaa kvathanank ke, alag-alag hota he is tarah aswan kii prakriya se unhen aasaani se alag kiya ja saktaa he. tel shodhak kaarkaana kii puri prakriya main yah ek mahatvapurn charan hota he.
 darasal aprishkrut tel ko alag-alag taapmaana par garm karke vaashp ekatrit karke tathaa use dobaaraa sanghanit karke udprangaar kii alag-alag chen nikaal lee jaati hai. tel shodhak kaarkaana main shodhan kaa yah sabase saamaanya or purana tarika he. ubalte taapmaana kaa upyog karne vaali is vidhi ko prabhaji aswan kehete hai. aswan kaa ek tarika yah bhi hota he ki udprangaar kii ek lambii chen ko jaise kaa taisa nikaal lene ke bajaae use choti-choti chens main todakar nikaal liya jaataa he. is prakriya ko rasinic prasanskaran kehete hai. to bachche ab aap samaj gaye honge ki petrol or carosine ke alaava duusare eendhan kaise banate hai. is sari prakriya main tel shodhak kaarkaana kii ahm bhoomika ho
