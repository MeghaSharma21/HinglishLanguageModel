 sansaarik prem or desh-prem premchand kii pehali prakashit kahani he. iskaa prakaashan kanpur se nikalne vaali urdu patrika zamana ke april 1908 ke ank main hua tha. aam taur se premchand ke pehle kahani sangrah sozewatan main prakashit duniya kaa sabase anmol ratan ko unki pehali kahani mana jaataa rahaa he. dow camel kishor goyanka ke anusaar sozewatan kaa prakaashan june 1908 main hua tha jabaki sansaarik prem or deshprem april 1908 main prakashit ho chuki thi. yah kahani italy ke mashhoor rashtravaadi magini or uski premika megdalin ke jivan ko aadhaar banaakar likhi gai he. yah desh kii aazaadee ke liye aamaran sangharsh karne kii bhaavana ko abhivyakt karti he. yah vyaktigat prem par rashtraprem ko mahatta pradaan karne vaali kahani he. 
 1 landan ke badchalni ke akhaade main italy kaa namvar deshpremi magini desh ke atyaachaari shashan or gulaami par chintit he. uske saath nirvasit dost italy ke amir kaa betaa rafeti apana nayaa kot christmas kii chintaa kiye bagaiyr girvee rakhkar kal shaam se na khaae jojeff ke liye biscuit or cigarette laata he. magini ne uski bimaari ke samay doctor kii fees ke liye premika megdalin kii anguthi bech daali thi.2 christmas ke din sara landan khush he magar magini or rafeti andhere kamare main maayoos baithe hai. magini ne khaanaa nahin khaaya he or subah se cigarette bhi nahin mili he. tabhi megdalin ke khat main christmas kaa uphaar baalo kaa ek guchha aayaa. switsraland ke ek rais kii anindy sundari beti vahaan magini ke bitaye kuch dinon main usase parichit hui or phir prem karne lagi thi. kintu magini ne swayan ko desh par nyochaavar kar diya tha. isliye usne megdalin kii khud ko swikaarne kii prarthana asweekaar kar di thi or deshhit kii chintaa karte hue maaraa-maaraa phirane laga tha. magini ne jabaabi patr main khud ko magadonil ke liye anupyukt mana or use bahan kehete hue tohfe ke liye shukr adaa kiya evam uske swasthya kii dua kii.3 iske bahut din baad italy main janata ke rajya kaa elaan kiya gaya or magini sahit teen vyakti rajya kii dekhbhaal ke liye niyukt hue. magar france kii jyadatiyon or pidmant ke badshaah kii dagaabaaziyon ke kaaran janata kaa rajya bikhar gaya or magini rome kii galiyon kii khaakh chaanane laga. uske doston rafeti, rasarino, palino, barnabas aadi ne maukaparasti dikhaai or usase dagaa kii. ve magadonil se bhi magini kaa bura kehete rahe magar usne yakeen nahin kiya. magini ke khud ko kamjor kehane ne use magadonil ke dil main or pratishthit kar diya. magar yah hikmat magini ne hi kii thi. taaki magadonil uske dil se apana khyal nikaal de. asar ulataa hua or magadonil rome ke ek sarai main aa thahari. magini ko vah dur se prasann dekhati magar jab usapar asafalataaon kaa waar hua to vah use aa mili.6 magini rome se landan jaakar bahut din rahaa or phir sicily ke aakrosh kii khabar sunakar vidroh kaa netritv karne ke liye sicily ja pahunchaa. magar vahaan vidroh kaa daman ho chukaa tha. shahi fouj ne use jahaaj se utarate hi giraftaar kar liya or phir mar jane ke bhay se buudhe magini ko reeha kar diya. magini ne janata main chetana failaane ke uddeshy se sweetsraland jaakar akhbaar nikaalnaa shuru kiya. ek saal baad hi alps parvat kii talahati main swasth laabh ke liye landan jate samay nimonia se uski mrityu ho gai. magdonil ne uske kabr par phool chadaaye or uske naam se ashram kholkar mufalis auraton or garib ladakiyon kii seva suru kii. uskaa ashram kisi bhi navagantuk ke liye apane ghar jaisaa tha. teen saal baad uski mrityu ho gai or uski vasiyat ke anusaar use usee ashram main dafna diya gaya.
