 dilli kii dilli metro pranali main 21 metro stesan hai, jo ki dilashaad garden se rithala tak jaati he. iske dwaara tay kii gai kul duuri he 25.15 ki.me. yah line adhiktar prachaalan main he. yah adhikansh line uparigami he. yah line uttar, uttar-purv or uttar-pashchim or pashchim dilli ko jodatii he.yah line dilli main pehali line thi, jo janata ke liye aarambh hui.
 yalo line kaa udghaatan disambar, 2004 main hua tha.
 rithala se barwaala, jo ki purv phase-1 ke liye yojanabaddh tha, phase-3 main joda gaya he, va 2015 tak puraa hoga. phase-3 main 9.5 kimi vistaar niojit he, jo dilashaad garden purv se gajiabad bas adde tak hoga.
