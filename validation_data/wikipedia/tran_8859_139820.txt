 prem bandhan 1979 main ramanand sagar dwaara nirdeshit hindi film he. iske pramukh kalakaar he rajesh khanna, rekha va mausami chatarji or sahaayak kalakaar helen, bhagwaan, casto mukherjee, e ke hangal va lalita pavaar he. 
 yah katha rajesh khanna dwaara abhinit paatr kaa he jo apni smaranshakti khoye ek machhuari se vivaah karata he or shahar main premika uski pratiksha karti he. 
 mahua naamak adivasee apane pita ke saath ek machuaare gaanv main rahati he. ek din gaanv kaa pujari ek aadmi ko paae, use khilaae pilaae, mahua ke pita ko uske dekh-rekh karne kahata he. use apni sud na hone ke kaaran use kishan naam die gaanv main rakhte hai. kuch din baad mahua use prem kar vivaah karti he. kuch maheene baad kishan shahar jaakar nahin lautataa| chintit mahua apane pita va police ke saath shahar jaae use dhundti he. uske na milane se niraash hue ve gaanv lautate hai. kuch din baad chintit mahua kishan ko dhundne nikal padati he. raste main use ek doctor miltaa he jo uske bataaye hue surat vaale aadmi kaa chikitsa kiya he or us aadmi kaa bombay vaala pataa detaa he. mahua bombay ke us pate par pahunchakar paati he ki kishan use pahchaanane se inkaar kar apneko ek dhanik mohan khanna bataata he. kuch hi dinon main vah ek dhani ladki minaa mehara se mangani karne vaala he. kishan mahua se prem bandhan jodna yaa mohan kaa minaa se, isi baat ko katha main aage darshaya gaya he.
