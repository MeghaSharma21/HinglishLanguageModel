 farvari 2018 israel-syria tanaav 10 farvari, 2018 ko ek irani drone israel ke hawaii kshetra main pravesh karne kii soochna mili, jise isroyal vaayu sena ne mar giraaya. pratishodh main syrian anti-air dephence system ne israel ke epa16 ladaaku vimaan ko maar giraaya. jisamen israeli ladaku vimaan nasht ho gaya tha lekin payalat dal ko surakshit bachaa liya gaya.. jet vimaan ke girane ke 2 ghante baad isroyal ne syria ke andar atirikt lakshya hamale kiye..ullekhneey he ki syria main iran or charampanthi sanghathan hizbullah raashtrapati bashar al-asad ke samarthan main lard rahe hai. ye donon hi israel ke khilaaf hai. isliye seemaa par takaraav hota rahata he.
 10 farvari ko 4:30 baje, ek israeli eech -64 apache helicopter uttari shahari beth sheen ke nikat ek irani drone ko maar giraaya. irani drone ko syria main ek base se utarana padaa tha..
 israeli ilaake main irani yuavi drone ke vaayu seemaa ulanghan ko dekhte hue, 8 vimaanon sahit isroyal vayusena ne drone par hamalaa kiya, is hamale ke pratikriya swarup syria ke vaayu raksha pranali ne israeli jet vimaanon par golibari karne ke baad ek israeli epa -16 ladaaku vimaan ko maar giraaya. vimaan hifa ke purv harduf ke kibbutz ke paas gira or vimaan ko chalaane vaale do payalat ghayal hue, jinamein se ek gambhir rup se ghayal ho gaya.
