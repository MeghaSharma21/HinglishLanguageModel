 
 potacium ek rasinic tatv he. iskaa pratik 'K' he. yah aarvat sarini ke pratham mukhya samooh kaa tatv he. iske do sthir samasthanik gyaat hai. ek asthir samasthanik prakruti main nune maatra main paaya jaataa he. inke atirikt teen anya samasthanik kritrim rup se nirmit hue hai.
 potacium ke yogic puraatan kaal se gyaat hai. charakasamhita main bhasma se kshar banane kii vidhi kaa varnan aayaa he. chini turkistan me sthit buddhamandir main ek chikitsa granth kii 1890 i. main prapti hui. is granth main yavakshar kaa varnan aayaa he. uparyukt baton se gyaat hota he ki pautacium kshaaron kaa upyog puraatan kaal main oshadhi tathaa rasinic kriyaaon main hota tha.
 potacium tatv kaa pruthakkaran 1807 i. main sar hanferi davy ne potacium hydroxide ke vidyudvishlaishan dwaara kiya. potash shabd ke aadhaar par davy ne is tatv kaa naam potacium rakhaa.
 potacium atyant sakriy tatv hone ke kaaran yogic avasthaa main hi paaya jaataa he. iske yogic prithvi main samuchit maatra main faile hai. anek chattaanon main iske jatil silicket upasthit rahete hai. bhuparpati main 2.6 pratishat potacium rahata he. samudr main is tatv ke yogicon kaa prachur pariman he, parantu pratishat maatra main kam hone ke kaaran abhi uskaa adhik upyog nahin ho paaya. germany main stresfurt pradesh main iske bahut samrudh strot hai, jinamein potacium chloride yaa silwite badi maatra main magnesium or calcium lavenon ke saath mishrit dasha main paaya jaataa he. sanyukt rajya america, ke callophania pradesh main potacium ke lavan paae jate hai. bharat main shora, kenite or langbinite potacium ke mukhya prapya yogic he. ye sambar jhil main samuchit maatra main milte hai.
 potacium dhaatu kaa nirmaan davy kii vidhi par nirbhar he. isamen vishuddh potacium hydroxide athwa costic potash kaa sangalit avasthaa main vidyudvishleshan karne par, potacium louh ke rin vidyudagr par jamaa ho jaataa he. costic potash ke sthaan par potacium chloride or potacium fluoride kaa sanmishran bhi liya jaataa he.
 potacium nilimaa liye chamakdaar shwet dhaatu he, jo 00 sein. taap par kathor or bhangur he, parantu saadhaaran taap par itnaa komal hota he ki chaaku se kaata ja saktaa he. iske kuch bhautik gun nimnaankit hai : sanket K, parmanusankhya 19, parmanubhaar 39.102, galnaank 62.50 sein., kvathanank 7600 sein. ghanatv 0.865, parmanuvyas 4.76 angstrum, vidyutpratirohakata 6.15 microoham-menmi. tathaa ynikaran vibhav 4.329 evo. he.
 potacium vaayu main sheeghra malin ho jaataa he or garm karne par pidyalkar jalataa he. jal main daalane par visphot ke saath kriya karke bangnee jwaala ke saath jalataa he. is prakriya main hydrogen mukt hota he, jo ucch taap ke kaaran vaayu ke sampark main jalane lagata he. potacium ke vaashp ke kaaran jwaala rang bangnee ho jaataa he. potacium ko tel athwa niskriy vaataavaran main surakshit rakhte hai.
 sodium kii bhanti potacium bhi drav ammonia main ghulkar nila vilyan detaa he or potashmide banataa he. 2000 sein. se 4000 sein. taap par potacium hydrogen ko apane andar adhidhaarit kar letaa he. aisa anumaan he ki is dasha main ek yogic banataa he.
 potacium ekasanyoji yogic banaata he. potacium parmaanu main chauthe kaksh main keval ek electron hota he. yadi yah electron parmaanu se nikal jaay, to uski electron rachana aagran gas ke samaan rah jaaygi, jo sthir hoti he. is kaaran prayek kriya main potacium kii is electron ko pradaan karne kii pravriti rahati he.
 potacium ke nishchit rup se teen oxide, potacium oxide, potacium dioxide or potacium paroxide, gyaat hai. yadi pautacium nitrid ko potacium dhaatu ke saath vaayu kii anupasthiti main garm kiya jaay, to potacium oxide praapt hoga, jo matamaila bhangur padaarth he. yah bada sakriy oxide he or teevr gati se jal se abhikriya kar potacium hydroxide banaata he.
 yadi potacium ko adhik oxigen ke vaataavaran main jalaayaa jaay, to naarangi rang kaa thos potacium daoxide banataa he. yah atyant sakriy yogic he or jal se kriya kar oxigen tathaa hydrogen paroxide donon mukt karata he. yah anek tatwon kaa oxikaran teevr gati se karata he.
 yadi potacium ko oxigen kii pariganit maatra main garm kiya jaay, to potacium paroxide praapt hoga, jo shwet rang kaa thos padaarth he. yah jal se prakriya kar keval hydrogen par oxide mukt karata he.
 yah potacium chloride vilyan ke vaidyut vighatan se banataa he. idas kriya ko kesanar-calenar vidhi kehete hai. yah shwet, apaardarshee, thos padaarth he or garam karne par sheeghra hi gal jaataa he. yah atyant jalpriya tathaa atyant jal viley he. amlon se kriya kar yah viley lavan banaata he.
 fluorine, chlorine, bromine tathaa iodine ke saath potacium ke yogic gyaat hai. costic potash par hydrophloric amla kii kriya se potacium fluoride, ek saamaanya fluoride, doosraa amleey fluoride banate hai. potacium fluoride namakeen swadwala ardratagrahi thos padaarth he.
 potacium chloride germany ke stresfurt pradesh main bahut maatra main uplabdh he. magnesium or calcium yogicon ke saath mishrit avasthaa main yah praaia: miltaa he. yadi iske vilyan main hydrogen chloride gas pravaahit kii jaay, to shuddh potacium chloride kaa avakshep praapt hoga or anya yogic vilyit avasthaa main rahenge. potacium chloride urvarak tathaa anya potacium lavenon ke banane ke udyog main badi maatra main kaam aata he.
 potacium bromide or iodide costic potash par kramash: bromine or iodine kii abhikriya dwaara praapt ho sakate hai. ye donon lavan chloride se samanta rakhte hai, parantu jal main usase adhik viley hai. potacium bromide kaa photography udyog main upyog hota he. donon yogic oshadhi ke rup main tathaa rasinic prayogshaalaon kii anek kriyaaon main kaam aate hai.
 gandhak ke saath potacium sulphide or iskaa jal ke 5 anuon se sanyukt ho crystal banataa he. iske atirikt costic potash ke vilyan main hydrogen sulphide pravaahit karne se potacium hydrosulphide banataa he. costic potash vilyan main salfar diaksaide pravaahit karne par par potacium sulphite banegaa.
 potacium sulphate stresfurt ke khanijon main uplabdh he. yah anek rasinic udyogon main upjaat ke rup main bhi miltaa he. iskaa amleey rup potacium hydrogen sulphate he. potacium sulphate kaa upyog oshadhiyon, urvarak or fitkaree ke banane main praaia: hota he.
 kemiagar ise saltapeter ke naam se sambodhit karte the. prakruti main carbonic padaartho ke kshay dwaara yah banataa rahata he. bihar main noni mitti se yah nikaala jaataa tha. rajasthan main sambhars jhil ke pradesh main bhi uplabdh he.
 ise potash bhi kehete hai. bahut kaal tak yah kashtharakh se praapt kiya jaataa tha, jisko sanskrit granthon main yavakshar kaha gaya he. aajkal leblank vidhi se yah taiyaar hota he. yah jal main bahut viley he or kshar gun pradhan he. yah kathor kaanch, potash sabun or costic potash banane main kaam aata he. iske vilyan main carbon dioxide pravaahit karne se potacium bicarbonate banataa he.
 potacium chloropletinet kii vileyata bahut kam he. iskaa pilaa avakshep potacium vishleshan main kaam aata he. iske atirikt potacium cobalteenitrite, K3, Co6] bhi aviley he, jo potacium or cobalt ke vishleshan main prayukt hota he.
 potacium chloride kaa prayog saadhaaran namak ke sthaan par kiya jaataa he.
