 samudr tal se 1525 meter kii oonchai par basaa madikeri karnataka ke kodagu jile kaa mukhyalay he. madikeri ko dakshin kaa scotland kaha jaataa he. yahaan kii dhundhlee pahadiyaan, hare van, coffee ke bagaan or prakruti ke khubasurat drishy madikeri ko avismaraneey paryaton sthal banaate hai. madikeri or uske aasapaas bahut se aitihaasik or dharmik sthal bhi hai. yah mysore se 125 kilometre dur pashchim main sthit he or coffee ke udyanon ke liye bhi bahut prasiddh he.
 1600 isvi ke baad lingayat raajaaon ne kurg main raaj kiya or madikeri ko rajdhani banaaya. madikeri main unhone mitti kaa kila bhi banwaaya. 1785 main tipu sultan kii sena ne is samrajya par kabja karke yahaan apana adhikaar jamaa liya. chaar varsh baad kurg ne angrejon kii madad se aazaadee hasil kii or raja vir rajendra ne punarniman kaary kiya. 1834 i. main angrejon ne is sthaan par apana adhikaar kar liya or yahaan ke antim shaasha par mukadmaa chalaakar use jail main daal diya.
 madikeri kii sthiti 12°25′N 75°44′E / 12.42°N 75.73°E / 12.42; 75.73 par he. yahaan kii ausat oonchai he 1062 meter .
 yah khubasurat jalprapaat madikeri se lagbhag 5 kilometre kii duuri par he. ek nijee coffee bagan ke bheetar yah jharnaa sthit he. paryatak badi sankhya main is sthaan par aate hai. monsoon ke dinon main yahaan kii sundarataa dekhte hi banati he.
 prarambh main yah kila mitti kaa bana tha. tipu sultan ke iskaa purnirmaan karke isamen pattharon kaa istemaal kiya. tipu sultan ke 18veen shatabdi main yahaan kuch samay ke liye shashan kiya tha. kile ke andar lingayat shaskaon kaa mahal he.
 yah vah sthaan he jahaan se kodagu ke raja dhalate surya ko dekhte the. yahaan se dur-dur faile har dhan ke kheton, ghaatiyon, bhure-nile ghaaton ke drishy dekhe ja sakate hai. raja kii seat ke saath hi chotii mariammaa naamak ek pracheen mandir he. 
 madikeri se 40 kilometre dur bhagmandal ko dakshin kaashi bhi kaha jaataa he. jab se bhagand maharishi ne yahaan shivlinga sthapit karavaaya ise bhagmandal ke naam se janaa jaataa he. taalkaveri yahaan se 8 kilometre dur he. bhagmandal ke sameep hi mahavishnu, subramanyam or ganpati mandir bhi hai.
 agar aap vanya jeevon se rubaru hona chaahate he to madikeni se 93 kilometre dur nagarhole rashtriya park zarur jaaen. yahaan hiran, bishan, hathi, junglee bhaaluu, bhediye ke alaava bandar kii vibhinn prajatiyon or vishaal tigaron ko dekha ja saktaa he. 284 varg kilometre kshetra main failaa yah park ushnkaribandheey patjhad wanon se ghira hua he.
 bhagwaan shiv or vishnu ko samarpit yah mandir kodagu raja lingraj ne 1820 main banwaaya tha. yah mandir masjidaakaar style main banwaaya gaya he. mandir main ek kendriya gumbad or chaar minaaren hai jo basav arthath pavitra belon se ghiri harui hai. mandir ke saamne ek tanks bana he jo mandir ko or aakarshak banaata he.
 madikeri se 91 kilometre dur irappu tirth sthal ke rup main prasiddh he jiskaa sambandh ramayana ke nayak ram se he. ramtirth nadi ke kinare par hi bhagwaan shiv kaa mandir he. shivaratri ke maukey par hajaaron tirth yatri is nadi main dubkee lagaate hai.
 madikeri se 48 kilometre dur taalkaveri kaveri nadi kii udgam sthali he isliye dharmik drushti se iskaa bahut mahatv he. yah brahmagiri pahaad ke dhalaan par sthit he. samudr tal se is sthaan kii oonchai 4500 feet he.
 madikeri se lagbhag 36 kilometre dur harangi apane tree houseon ke liye lokapriy he. uttari kurg ke kushalnagar ke sameep sthit harangi baandh ek darshneey picnic sthal he. kaveri nadi par bana yah baandh 2775 feet lanbaa or 174 feet ooncha he. 
 kodagu kii sabase oonchi pahari ke tal par bana nalknad mahal ateet kii yaad dilaataa ek khubasurat paryaton sthal he. madikeri se 45 kilometre dur yah mahal 1792 main doda virraj dwaara banwaaya gaya tha. do khand kaa yah mahal aakarshak chitrakaari or vaastuakari se sabko mohit kar detaa he.
 mangalore vimaanashetra madikeri kaa najdiki gharelu airport he jo 135 kilometre dur he. yahaan se bangalore ke liye sidhi flite he.
 madikeri kaa najdiki rail mukhyalay mysore he. mysore bharat ke pramukh shaharon se train ke madhyam se judaa hua he.
 madikeri behatar sadak network se karnataka ke pramukh shaharon se judaa he. rajya parivahan nigam kii bason se madikeri pahunchaa ja saktaa he.
