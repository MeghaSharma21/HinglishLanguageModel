 raavuri bharadwaj gyanpith puraskaar vijeta telugu upanyaaskaar, laghu-katha lekhak, kavi evam sameekshak the. unhone 37 laghu kathaayen, satraa upanyaas, chaar naatak evam paanch radio roopaantaran likhe.
 unhen 2012 main gyanpith puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. inke dwaara rachit ek rekhachitr jivan samarum ke liye unhen san 1983 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
 raavuri ne 18 oktober 2013 ko hyderabad ke banjaaraa hills aspataal kii dekharekh main antim saanse lee.
