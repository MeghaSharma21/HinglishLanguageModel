 climent richard atli british raajnneetijna, lebar party ke netaa, tathaa 1945 se 1951 tak united kingdom ke pradhanmantri the. unke hi pradhanmantritvakaal main bharat ko swatantraaa mili. unke kaal main hi euke kalyankari rajya bana jiske antargat anya baton ke alaava vahaan nickshulk saarvajanik swasthya-seva armbh hui. ve euke ke sarvottam pradhanmantri maane jate hai tathaa vahaan ke sabase lokapriy pradhanmantriyon main unki ginati hoti he.
 unka janm 3 janvari, 1883 ko landan ke putani main hua tha. unke pita ek dhanwaan vakil the.1905 main unhone oxford vishvavidhyalay se baristry paas kii kintu vakaalat kii jagah vah saamaajik kaary karne lage. do saal baad vah samaajawaadee ho gaye or 'indapendent lebar party' ke sadsy ban gaye. pehle mahayuddh main unhone france or nikat purv ke deshon main major kii haisiyat se ladaaiyaan ladiin. 1922 main atali parliament ke sadsy chune gaye or jab 1931 main majdoor dal kii sarkaar bani tab vah yuddh ke liye upasachiv niyukt hue. 1931 ke chunaav ke baad vah majdoor dal ke pehle upanetaa, phir netaa, chune gaye. dwitiya vishwayuddh ke samay churchill ke mantrimandal main bhi vah mantri the or churchill ke baad vah swayan england ke pradhan mantri hue. 1945 main bharat ko pehle aupniveshik phir 1947 main purna swarajya unheen ke tatwadhan main mila. 1950 main vah phir nae chunaav ke baad pradhanmantri hue. us chunaav main udaar or anudaar dalon ke upar majdoor dal kaa bas thodaa hi bahumat tha. kuch kaal baad jab majdoor dal kaa mantrimandal hataa tab major atali bhi sarkaar se alag ho gaye. san 1967 i. main inaki mrityu ho gai.
