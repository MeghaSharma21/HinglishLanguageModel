 mujhase shaadi karogi 2004 kii david dhawan dwaara nirdeshit hindi bhashaa kii hasya premakahani film he. is film main salmaan khan, akshay kumaar or priyanka chopra mukhya kirdaar nibhaate hai. yah akshay or priyanka kii andaaz ke baad ek saath duusari film he. sahaayak kalakaaron main amrish puri, kadar khan, satish shah or raajapaal yadav shaamil hai. kai bhartiya cricket team ke khilaadi ne vishesh upasthiti darj kii.
 is film ko ko sakaaratmak samiksha kalakaaron ke pradarshan, sangeet, chhayankan, kala disha, paridhaan or style ke liye praapt hui. 30 july 2004 ko jari yah film ek badi vyavsaayik safalataa thi, jo saal kii teesri sabase zyada kamai karne vaali bhartiya film ban gai. isko filmafere puraskaar or antarrashtriya bhartiya film academy puraskaar main kramshah teen or das namakan praapt hue the. 
 samir tunukmizaaj he or krodh prabandhan ke muddon se ghira hua he. jab vah apni premika roma par hamalaa karne kii koshish karne vaale gundon ke ek samooh ko martha he, to vah uske gusse ke kaaran usase rishta tod detii he. uski kundali ke anusaar, samir pyaar main durbhagyapurna he. isi tarah ke muddon se bachane ke liye, vah goa main ek lifeguard ban jaataa he.
 train main samir suraj prakaash se miltaa he, jo use lagata he ki chor he. samir luutane se bachane ke liye puri raat jaagta rahata he. suraj agali subah stesan par samir ko yah sweekaar karata he ki wo apane saamaan kii raksha karwaane ke liye naatak kar rahaa tha. samir jaanata he ki suraj uske resort main ek suraksha guard he or use teen maheene tak raat kii guard duty par rakhata he.
 samir navdith fishan diziner rani se miltaa hai or uske saath pyaar karne lagata he. phir sani ke rup main pareshaani aati he. vah rani se miltaa he or wo bhi uske saath pyaar karne lagata he or samir kii galatiyon par phayda uthaata he.
