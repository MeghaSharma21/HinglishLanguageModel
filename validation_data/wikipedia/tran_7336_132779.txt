 nepal cricket sangh nepalmein cricket khel ke aadhikarik parichaalak nikaay hai. iske vartmaan mukhyalay kathmandu, nepal main rahaa hai. san 1988 se nepal cricket sangh antarrashtriya cricket parishadamein nepal ke pratinidhi hone ke saath hi associat sadsy bhi hai. yah asian cricket parishad ke bhi sadsy rahaa hai. san 2012 main sangh ne apana brands ambesder mahendra singh dhoni ko chayan kiya hai.
