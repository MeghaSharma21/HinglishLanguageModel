 rashtriya prodyogiki santhaan, goa goa main sthit rashtriya mahatv kaa santhaan he. yah bharat ke prashiddh rashtriya praudyogiki sansthaanon main se ek he. ise 'enaiti goa' ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. is santhaan main shikshan kaary san 2010 se prarambh hua tha. purnakalik campus kaa nirmaan 300 acre main shuru ho chukaa he. iske liye paisa maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantralay detaa he.
