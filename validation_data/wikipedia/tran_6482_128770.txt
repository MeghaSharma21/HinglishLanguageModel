 2016-17 zimbabwe trikoniya shrunkhlaa vartmaan main november 2016 main zimbabwe main yojit kiya ja rahaa ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket toornament he. yah zimbabwe, srilanka or westeindies kii rashtriya pratinidhi cricket tiimon ke bich ek trikoniya shrunkhlaa he. srilankai team mul rup se do test, teen vanade or ek tii 20 ke liye zimbabwe daure ke liye nirdhaarit kiya gaya. haalaanki, vanade or tii 20 is trikoniya siries kii jagah thi.
 diares praudyogiki zimbabwe main ek simit overon kii shrunkhlaa main pehali baar ke liye istemaal kiya jayega. yah srilanka ko turant trikoniya siries ke pehle ke khilaaf zimbabwe kii shrunkhlaa main duusare test main iske upyog ke baad.
 shen daurich or miguel kamins ko marlon sampuls or alzarari joseph ke sthaan par westeindies ke team main joda gaya tha. derren braavo westeindies ke team se hataa diya gaya tha ke baad unhone twitor par ek tippanee post kiya he ki westeindies cricket board "anuchit or asweekaarya" samajha. vah jesan mohammad dwaara badal diya gaya tha. sunil narayan vyaktigat kaarnon kii vajah team main chhod diya or devendra bishu dwaara badal diya gaya tha.
