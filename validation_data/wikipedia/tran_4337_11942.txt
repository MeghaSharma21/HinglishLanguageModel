 shakatayan naam ke do vyakti hue hai, ek vaidic kaal ke antim charan ke vaiyaakaran, tathaa duusare 9veen shatabdi ke amoghvarsh nripatung ke shasankaal ke vaiyaakaran.
 vaidic kaal ke antim charan ke shakatayan, sanskrit vyakaran ke rachayita he hai. unki kritiyaan ab uplabdh nahin hai kintu yaksha, panini evam anya sanskrit vaiyakarnon ne unke vichaaron kaa sandarbh diya he. 
 shakatayan kaa vichaar tha ki sabhi sangya shabd antat: kisi na kisi dhaatu se vyutpann hai. sanskrit vyakaran main yah prakriya krit-pratyay ke rup main upasthit he. panini ne is mat ko sweekaar kiya kintu is vishay main koi aagrah nahin rakhaa or yah bhi kaha ki bahut se shabd aise bhi hai jo lok kii bolchaal main aa gaye hai or unase dhaatu pratyay kii pakad nahin kii ja sakati. shakatayan dwaara rachit vyakaran shaastra 'lakshan shaastra' ho saktaa he, jisamen unhone bhi chetan or achetan nirmaan main vyakaran ling nirdhaaran kii prakriya kaa varnan kiya tha.
