 gaayab hone vaale sahyatri kii kahani ek shahari kinvadanti he, jisamen vaahan se yatra kar rahe logon kii yaa to kisi sawari karne ke icchhuk se mulaakaat hoti he yaa phir ve uske saath yatra karte hai. baad main yah vyakti bina kisi soochna ke or akasar chalate vaahan se, gaayab ho jaataa he. lupt hone vaale sahyatriyon ke bare main jaankaree sadiyon se uplabdh he or yah kahani duniya bhar main vividh roopon main prachlit he. is kahani kii lokapriyata or bane rahane kii kshamta iske samakaleen lokapriy samskruti main failane main sahaayak rahi he.
 is shabd ke bare main janata kii jaankaree 1981 main jain herold brunwand kii pustak da venishing hichhiker ke prakaashan ke saath vishaal paimaane par badi, jisase shahari kinvadantiyon ke vishay main saarvajanik jaagrukta utpann hui.
 theth aadhunik gaayab hone vaala sahyatri ekmaatr sawari ke saath raat main yatra kar rahi kisi car kii roshni main dikhaai dene vaala koi vyakti hota he.
 yah vyakti lift maangane ke anurodh karne vaale ke samaan rukh apanaataa he. kaarchaalak ruk jaataa he or us vyakti ko car main chadhane detaa he. yatra phir shuru hoti he, vah bhi kabhi-kabhi purna chuppi ke saath or aage kisi bindu par vah yatri vaahan ke gatisheel hone kii sthiti main hi gaayab hota lagata he. kai maamalon main, vaahan ke us muft savaar ke gantavy sthaan tak pahunchate hi vah sahyatri lupt ho jaataa he.
 uparokt vivaran ke ek aam prakaar main gaayab hone vaala sahyatri, jo car main koi vastu chhod jaataa he yaa jaade se bachane ke bahaane koi vastra udhaar letaa he, saamaanya yatri kii tarah hi vidaa lete dekha jaataa he, shaamil he. gaayab hone vaala sahyatri jate samay kisi tarah kii soochna bhi chhod ja saktaa he jisase vaahan chaalak ko baad main usase sampark karne kaa kathith rup se protsaahan miltaa he.
 is tarah kii kinvadantiyon main, udhaar liya gaya paridhaan aksar baad main kisi sthaneey kabristaan main kisi kabr ke pathar par lipta paaya jaataa he. is or 'praapt jaankaree' ke udhaaharan main, sachai se anbhigya vaahanchaalak baad main kisi mrit vyakti ke parivaar se sampark karata he or paata he ki uske saath yatra karne vaale vyakti kaa huliya us parivaar ke kisi apratyaashit tareeke se main maare gaye kisi sadsy ke huliye se mail khaataa he or uskaa lop hone vaale sahyatri se saakshaatkaar uski mrityu kii barsi ke din hua tha.
 gaayab ho jane muft ke savaaron kii sabhi khabaren kathith rup se aavarti bhuton se sambandhit nahin hoti hai. hawaii main prachlit ek prakaar kii katha main devi pele shaamil he, jo sadakon par gupt rup se yatra karti he or yatriyon ko puraskrit karti he. 
 anya prakaaron main bhavishyavaanee karne vaale lop hone vaale sahyatri shaamil hai.
 gaayab ho jane savaaron kii kahani kaa pehla samuchit adhyayan 1942-3 main america lokkthakaron, richard beardsale or rojali hanki ne kiya tha, jinhone jitne sambhav ho sake utane vrittanton kaa sangrah kiya or unka vishleshan karne kaa prayaas kiya.
 beardsale-hanki surveykshan ne saare america bhar se gaayab hone vaale sahyatriyon se saakshaatkaar ke 79 likhit byore praapt kiye.
 unhone bataaya: "chaar spasht rup se bhinn sanskaran paae gaye, jo vikaas or saar main spasht bhinnataaon ke kaaran alag pahachaane ja sakate the."
 inkaa varnan nimna prakaar se kiya gaya he:
 beardsale or hanki ek ghatana main vishesh rup se ruchi rakhte the: jisamen gaayab ho jane vaala sahyatri kii aage chalakar swargeey mata cabrini, sthaneey secred heart anathalay kii sansthaapak, jinhen unke kaary ko liye bhavi sant kii oopaadhi di gai thi, ke rup main pahchaan kii gai. lekhakon kaa maananaa tha ki yah sanskaran 'bi' ke sanskaran 'di' main badalane kii jhalak dene vaala maamla tha.
 beardsale or hanki ne nishkarsh nikaala ki sanskaran 'e' kahani ke mul rup ke sabase kareeb tha, jisamen kinvadanti ke sabhi aavashyak tatv the. unka maananaa tha ki sanskaran 'bi' or 'di', sthaniyakrut vividhtaayen thim, jabaki 'si' sambhavat: ek alag bhoot kii kahani ke rup main shuru hua jo kisi avasthaa par mul gaayab hone vaale sahyatri kii kahani main sammishrit ho gaya.
 unke nishkarshon main se ek nishchit rup se gaayab ho jane vaale sahyatri kii kahaniyon kii nirantarata main parilakshith lagata he: adhikansh maamalon main, sahyatri koi mahila or lift-daata purush hota he. beardsale or hanki ke namoonon main 47 yuva mahila pret, 14 budhi auraton ke pret, tathaa 14 kisi anishchit prakaar ke pret the.
 arnest dablyoo boman ke england or uttari america kii lokkathaon ke prakaar- or anukalpon ke suchakank dwaara banaai gai buniyadi gaayab hone vaale sahyatri kii ruparekha is prakaar he:
 boman kii vargeekaran pranali main is buniyadi kahani ko i 332.3.3.1 kii ruparekha kii shrenee pradaan kiya gaya he.
 upashreniyon main shaamil hai: 
 yahaan, yah ghatana dharmik muthbhedon main ghulmil jaati he or aglaa va antim lupt hone vaale sahyatriyon kaa vargeekaran- i 332.3.3.2 - un daivi vyaktiyon ke liye he jo sahyatriyon ke rup main raste par prakat ho jate hai. saint christopher kii katha ko inamen se ek mana jaataa he or dharmadoot philip kii kahani jisamen use ithiopian se raste main milane ke baad parameshwar dwaara vaahan main le jaaya jaataa he, kii vyakhya bhi isi prakaar se kii jaati he.
 gaayab ho jane sahyatriyon kii ghatnaon ne 1970 or 1980 ke dashak ke prarambh main ek nishchit rup se bhavishyasuchak rup le liya.
