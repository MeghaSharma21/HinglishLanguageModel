 vaastukala main dwaar yaa daravaaza ek aisa hill sakane vaala dhaancha hota he jis se kisi kamare, bhawan yaa anya sthaan ke pravesh-sthal ko kholaa or band kiya ja sake. aksar yah ek chapata fatta hota he jo apane kulabe par gum saktaa he. jab daravaaza khulaa hota he to us se baahar kii hawa, roshni or awaazen andar pravesh karti hai. dwaaron ko band karne ke liye unpar aksar taalon, zanjiron yaa kundion kaa bandobast kiya jaataa he. aane jane kii suvidha yaa roak ke liye lagaae gaye lakdi, dhaatu yaa pathar ke ek tukade, yaa jode hue kai tukdon, ke pallon ko dwarkapaat, kapaat yaa kivaad kehete hai.
 dwaar kaa saadhaaran rup ytaakar chhidr kaa hota he, kintu aayat kaa upree bhaag gol yaa lambii mehrab vaala, yaa anya kisi rup kaa bhi ho saktaa he. eent, yaa pathar kii chiniwale bhawanon ke dwaaron main choukhat lagi hoti he, jisamen upar kii ore lakdi kaa jota hota he. lakdi ke makaanon main jote kii kshaitij lakdi main chulein banaakar agal-bagal kii khadi lakadiyon ke bich laga dete hai or khadi lakadiyaan upar chat tak chali jaati hai. gufabhawanon, arthath pathar yaa shila katkar banaae hue bhawanon main alag se chaukhate kii aavashyaktaa nahin hoti, kintu bahudhaa aise dwaaron ke chaturdik sajaavat ke liye rekhaayen yaa anya abhikalp utkeern kar die jate hai.
 vibhinn deshon main bhinn-bhinn samayon par prachlit vaastukala ke anusaar dwaaron ke upree bhaag kaa rup badalata rahaa he. pracheen bhawanon ke avasheshon main aise sabhi roopon ke dwaar milte hai. pracheen misr main parde kii diwaar main bane dwaar diwaar se bhi unche banate the, taaki jhande yaa dharmik kaaryein se sambadhit anya lambii vastuen bheetar le jane main suvidha ho. baajuon ke pathar upar kii ore thodaa thodaa aage badhaakar rakhe jaaya karte the. is prakaar dwaar kaa tatkaaleen rup adhuri chotii kaa sa hota tha. pracheen itruria tathaa greece main bhi dwaar bahudhaa chotii par chote or neeche bade banaae jate the.
 europe main roman kaal ke pashchaat, romanesco tathaa gothic vaastukala ke kaal tak, girjagharon ityadi main aise dwaar banaae jate the jinaki akruti diwaar main kram se ek ke baad ek khode hue, anek aalon ke samaan hoti thi. dwaar ke upar ke torana bhi isi prakaar kram se kaate jate the. dwaar ke chhidr kii kshaitij chotii par diwaar kaa ek khadaa bhaag chuta rahata tha.
 muslim deshon main dwaaron kaa bada mahatv he. diwaar kii puri oonchaai bhar main banaae hue torana ke upree bhaag main jhaad latakaae hue rahete hai, yaa keval ek unchi nokdaar mehrab rahati he or neeche ke bhaag main praveshdwar hota he. china, japan or bharat main dwaaron kii banaavat praaia: sidhi hi hoti he.
 ati pracheen kaal ke adivasee bhi vartmaan adivaasiyon kii bhanti kivaad kaa kaam vriksh kii daaliyon se bane tattar yaa chamade, chataayee, taat, yaa kisi prakaar ke parade se lete the. aavashyaktaa na rahane par chataayee ityadi lapetakar band di jaati thi. misr ke tai naamak makbare kii deevaaron par banaae chitron main dwaaron par latakati huin aisi chataaiyaan chitrit hai. aadhunik bhawanon main bhi dwaaron par latakate parde anshat: asthaayee kivaadon kaa kaam dete hai.
 drudh padaarthon se bane kivaad kaa prayog bhi pracheen he. ye kivaad praaia: kisi bhi kaath ke mote, baari takhton ke bane hote the. kivaad kii ek bagal main upar or neeche kii ore chulein yaa keeliyaan nikali rahati thim. ye chulein dwaar ke upar or neeche kii ore bane hue gaddhon main baitha di jaati thim. inhen chulon par ghumaakar kivaad kholaa yaa band kiya jaataa tha. yadi dwaar kam chaudaa hota tha to ek palle kaa, nahin to do palle ke kivaad lagaae jate the. is prakaar ke kivaad bharat ke puraane bhawanon main or abhi bhi dehaton main sarvatra paae jate hai.
 jin deshon main ardra jalwaayu ke kaaran lakdi ke takhte ke bane pallon ke tede ho jane kii ashanka rahati he, vahaan lakdi ke khadey yaa bade kai tukade jodakar, yaa chaukhate main jadkar, kivaad banaae jate hai. kuch deshon main, jaise syria, pelestin, mesopotamia tathaa bharat main lakdi ke kivaadon par dhaatu kii chaddar madhne kii paripati he. lakdi ke kivaadon ko sudrudh karne ke liye unpar lohe, kaanse yaa pital ke band jade jate hai. kilon ke kapaaton par inke sivaay nukile barche yaa kaante jade jate the, taaki hathi ke dhakke se bhi ve kapaat tode na ja saken.
 jin deshon main kaath durlabh he, vahaan pathar ke dilledaar kapaat banaae jate the. isa purv jwalamukhi visphot main dhwast ponpiayin nagar ke avasheshon main sangemarmar ke tathaa syria main chauthi se chhathi shatabdi ke pathar ke anek pracheen kapaat mili hai.
 dwarkapaat praaia: lakdi ke dilledaar hote the. inaki banaavat bahut kuch aadhunik dwaaron si hi hoti thi. kabhi-kabhi kapaat do, teen yaa chaar pallon ke hote the or ye palle aapas main kabjon se jude rahete the. europe main 12veen shatabdi se kapaaton ko chul par ghoomane vaala na banaakar kabjon se lagane kii pratha chali. lohe ke ye kabje phool pattiyon ke aakaar ke bane hote the or inke kapaaton ko sudrudh karne ke saath-saath sajaane kaa avasar bhi miltaa tha.
 muslim deshon ke kapaaton main praaia: taron sadrush, shatkoniya yaa anya jatil akritiyon ke dille hote hai. adhik sajaavat ke liye dhaatu kii chaddaron main vividh akritiyaan katkar lakdi ke kapaaton par jad di jaati hai. china, japan tathaa bharat main bhi iske udhaaharan milte hai. kabhi kabhi kapaat ke neeche vaale dille thos hote hai or upree dillaa alankrut jaali kaa. japan main praaia: sarakane vaale kivaad hote hai, jo deevaaron ke praturup prathit hote hai.
 aajkal bhi dilledaar kivaad banate hai, kintu samtal kivaadon kaa chalan bad rahaa he. agni se bachaav ke liye dhaatu kaa bhi prayog hota he. aise kivaad do prakaar ke hote hai : ek main bhitari bhaag to lakdi kaa hi hota he kintu upar dhaatu kii chaddar chadhaakar jhaal di jaati he, yaa anya prakaar se jod di jaati he. duusare main dhaatu ke dhaanche par chaddaren jad di jaati hai. vishesh prakaar ke kaam ke liye vishesh prakaar ke daravaaje banaae jate hai.
 kapaaton main upyog ke liye aajkal anek prakaar ke taale, kabje, chulein, aadi banate hai tathaa judanaron main bahut yantric vikaas hua he.
