 democratic party america ke pramukh do rajanithik dalo main se ek hai. yah dal aamtaur par badi sarkaar, samaaj ko niyantrit karne main, samaaj ke bheetar or mukhya rup se nimna virgon ke logon ko laabh or sevaayen muhaiyaa karaane ke liye sarkaar kii badi bhoomika ke paksh main hai.
 aadhunik democratic party 1828 ke aasapaas sthapit kii gayi thi, party ne america ko 15 raashtrapati diye hai. sabase pehle 1829-1837 main seva vaale andryu jaikson the. america ke haal hi kaa raashtrapati baraak obama jo 2009 se karyrat he isi party se he.
