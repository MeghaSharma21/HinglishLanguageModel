 bhupen khakhar samakaleen bhartiya kala jagat ke ek bade kalakaar the. vadodara hi unki kaarybhumi thi or unke kaary ko antarashtriya str par khyaati mili thi. 
 shri khakhar ek swa-prashikshit kalakaar the or ek chitrakaar ke rup me unhone apana karier kaafii der se shuru kiya tha. unka kaary rupakaatmak charitra vaala tha or iskaa sambandh maanviya sharir or isaki pahchaan se tha. ek swa-ghoshit samalangik vyakti ke rup me ling kii paribhaasha or iske pahchaan se judi samasyaayen unki kala kii pradhan vishay vastu thim. unke chitron me aamtaur par bhartiya mithkon or mithkeey prakaran vaale chitra shaamil hua karte the. 
 bhupen khakhar kaa janm mumbai me hua tha jahaan unhone khetvaadi kshetra me apane abhibhavakon or teen bhai-bahano ke saath apana bachapan guzaaraa. vah chaar bacchon me sabase chote the or unke pita parmanand, jo peshey se ek abhiyantaa the, mumbai ke mattunga ilaake me sthit vi.je.tii. ai. naam ke santhaan me bahya pareekshak ke rup me karyrat the. parmanand ko bahut zyaada sharaab pine kii lat thi or jab bhupen chaar saal ke the tabhi unke pita kaa dehanth ho gaya. unki mata mahalakshmi ek aam gruhani thi or unhone puri aashaa ke saath apni sari poonji apni choti santan ke bhavishya ko savaarane me laga di. 
 khakhar parivaar paramparaagat rup se kalakaaron kaa parivaar tha jo mul rup se tatkaaleen portugali upnivesh diu ke rahane vaale the. ghar par ve praayah gujarati, marathi or hindi me baat karte the par unhe angrezi kaa khaas gyaan nahi tha. 
 bhupen apane parivaar ke pehle vyakti the jinhone bombai vishvavidhyalay se arthshaastra or raajnneeti vigyaan jaise vishesh vishyon ke saath snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii. apane parivaar ke dawaab ke kaaran unhone baad me vaanijya snaatak kii shiksha bhi grahan kii or sanadi lekhaakaar kii yogyata bhi praapt kii. 
 shri khakhar ne kai varshon tak bharat paarikh angsosiats ke saath vadodara me saajhedaari me lekhaakar kaa kaam kiya or saath hi khaali samay me apni kalatmak abhiruchio ko bhi jari rakhaa. ve kuch hi samay me bhartiya mithkon or sahitya ke jaankaar ban gayi or unhe drishy kala kaa bhi acha khaasaa gyaan ho gaya tha. 
 1958 me unki bhent yuva gujarati kavi or chitrakaar gulam mohammad shaikh se hui or unhone shri khakhar kii kala ke kshetra me supt abhiruchi ko protsaahit kiya tathaa unhen vadodara me sthapit hue naye faklti of fine arts me aane kaa nimantran bhi diya. 
 shri khakhar ke taileey chitra aksar kaafii vivarnaatmak hote the or uname atmavarnan kaa put hota tha. unke sarvapratham pradarshit chitron me lokapriy pustakon se kaate gayi devataaon ke chitra shaamil the jinhe darpan par chipka kar, bhittichitaron or sankethik chinhon ke rup me pradarshit kiya gaya tha. 
 unhone 1965 se hi apane chitro kaa ekal pradarshan aarambh kar diya tha. haalaanki ve ek swaprashikshit kalakaar the phir bhi jald hin unki kalakirtiyan aakarshan kaa kend ban gayin or unhe aalochakon kii saraahna bhi milane lagi. 1980 ke dasak tak shri khakhar ke chitron ke ekal pradarshan london, berlin, amsterdam or tokyo jaise jagahon par hone lage the. 
 saal 2000 me ko shri khakhar da royal palace of amsterdam me prince clos aword se sammanit kiya gaya. unke anya sammaanon me da asian counsil'sa star foundation dwaara 1986 me di gayi fellowship or bharat sarkaar dwaara 1984 me diya gaya padma shri sammaan shaamil hai. 
 unke chitra british muzium, da tet gallery, london, da muzium of modern art, new york aadi me pradarshit kiye gayi hai.
