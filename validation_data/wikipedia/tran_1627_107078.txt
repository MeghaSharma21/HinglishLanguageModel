 vera hingorani ek bhartiya stri rog visheshgy, prasuti, chikitsa lekhak hai. vah praadhyaapak or pramukh he ol india institute of medical sciensej main stri rog or prasuti vibhaag kii.
 vera hingorani kaa janm 23 disambar, 1924 ko bharat main bubek shahar main hua tha. unhone 1947 main ladi harding medical college se chikitsa main snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii or stri rog or prasuti main masters kii degree paprapt kii. pher uske baad hi unhone ol india institute of medical sciensej main shaamil ho gai 1959 main. hingorani oparation aashaa chalati he, ek gaiyr sarkari sanghathan he jo bharat se tapedic ke unmoolan ke liye kaam kar rahaa he, unki prabandhan team ke sadsy ke rup main shaamil he.
