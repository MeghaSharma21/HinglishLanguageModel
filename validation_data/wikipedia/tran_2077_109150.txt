 masaba gupta ek bhartiya faishan diziner hai. unhone kai bhartiya abhinetriyon ke liye apni sevaayen pradaan kii hai.
 masaba mashhoor cricket khilaadi sar vivian richards or bhartiya film abhinetri nina gupta kii santan he. unka bachapan mumbai or westeindies main gujara he or unhone shrimati nathibai thakarase mahila vishvavidhyalay se faishan dizin kii shiksha grahan kii he.
 unhone film produsar madhu mantena se sagaai kii he.
