 goldilox or teen bhaaluu ek prasiddh angrezi pari katha he. kathan ke rup main yah kahani lambe arase se britain main chali aa rahi thi, lekin likhit rup main ise sabase pehle british lekhak robert soudi ne gumnaam rup se san 1837 main apane ek lekh-sangrah main chhapavaya. angrezi bhashaa kii yah sab se lokapriy kathaon main se he or pashchimi samskruti main iske tatwon kaa kai sandarbhon main prayog hota he.
 is kahani ke mul dhaanche main kisi van main teen bhaaluu manushyon kii tarah ek ghar main rahete hai. unamem se ek bhaaluu bahut bada he, ek bhaaluu bahut chota he or ek bhaaluu bich ke aakaar kaa he. ve apane ghar ko bahut svachh or saafe rakhte hai. ek goldilox naam kii choti-si ladki us van main gumti hui bhukhi or thakee-haari us ghar par pahunchati he. us samay teenon bhaaluu ghar par nahin hai.
 andar aakar use rasoi main mez ke irdagird teen kursiyaan or taaze-bane daliye se bhari teen badi katoriyaan milti hai. jab vah baithne lagati he to pehali kursi bahut badi hoti he, duusari bahut choti hoti he, lekin teesri madhyam aakaar kii or uske liye bilkul thik hoti he. jab vah dalia khaane lagati he to ek katori kaa dalia adhik garam hota he, duusari kaa adhik thanda hota he, lekin tisare kaa bilkul thik taapmaana kaa hota he. vah dalia kha leti he. ghar main gumti vah bhaaluon ke shayankaksh main daakhil hoti he, jisamen teen bistar hai. ek bistar uske liye bahut bada hota he, doosraa bahut chota hota he, lekin teesraa bilkul thik aakaar kaa hota he. vah usapar so jaati he.
 itane main teenon bhaaluu ghar aa jate hai. pehle to vah apane mez kii teetar-bitar haalat dekhakar hairaan hote hai. phir madhya-aakaar kaa bhaaluu dekhtaa he ki koi uskaa sara dalia kha chukaa he or shor machaata he. phir kamare main aakar ve dekhaten hai ki bistar sab gadabad kiye gaye hai. bich kaa bhaaluu goldilox ko apane bistar main sota paata he or phir shor machaata he. goldilox shor sunakar ghabaraakar uthati he or bhaaluon ko dekhakar ek khidki se baahar kudakar bhaag jaati he.
 anya varnanon main goldilox sab se chote bhaaluu kii kursi or bistar par baithte or letate hue unhen tod detii he, jis se chillane vaala bhaaluu sab se chota vaala hota he. kabhi-kabhi in teen bhaaluon ko pita bhaaluu, mata bhaaluu or baccha bhaaluu ke paatro main darshaya jaataa he. kuch roopon main goldilox bhaagane kii bajaae kuch dinon tak bhaaluon kaa mehmaan banakar rahati he.
 is kahani main goldilox kai dafaa do charam kii chizon ko aazamaane ke baad unke bich kii chiz ko uchit paati he - bich ke aakaar kii kursi, bich ke taapmaana kaa dalia or bich ke aakaar kaa bistar. is tatv kii ore pashchimi kalaon, lekhon or vaigyanik rachanaaon main bahut se ullekh milte hai or "goldilox" ek prakaar kaa suutaravakya ban chukaa he:
