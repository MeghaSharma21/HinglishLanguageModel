 2003-04 tivies cap ek trikoniya vanade antarrashtriya cricket toornament tha, jise bharat, newzealand or australia ne khele. yah toornament 23 oktober 2003 se 18 november 2003 tak bharat main yojit kiya gaya tha, or ek round robin charan shaamil tha, jisamen prayek desh ne teenon main se prayek ko teen baar khel diya tha. round robin charan ke ant main sheersh do tiimon ne shrinkhalaa ke antim match khele.
