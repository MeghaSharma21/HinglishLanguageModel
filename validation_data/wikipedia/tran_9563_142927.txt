 nandikeshwarkashika 27 padon se yukt darshan evam vyakaran kaa ek granth he. iske rachayita nandi yaa nandikeshwar hai. is granth main shaiva advait darshan kaa varnan he, saath hi yah maheshwar sutron kii vyakhya ke rup main he. upamanyu rishi ne is par 'tatvavimarshini' naamak tikaa rachi he.
 nandikeshwar ko panini, thirumul, patanjali, vyaghrapad, tathaa shivayogamuni kaa guru mana jaataa he. 
 nrittavsaane natarajarajo nanaad dhakkaan navpanchvaaram.
 uddhartukamah sanakadisiddhantadvimarshe shivasutrajalam. 1.
 atra sarvatra sutreshu antyavarnachaturdasham.
 dhatvarthan samupadishta paninyadishtsiddhye. 2.
 
 acaro brahmarupah syannirgunah sarvavastushu.
 chitkalamin samashritya jagadrup unishwarah. 3.
 akar sarvavarnagryah prakaashah parameshwarah.
 aadyamantyen sanyogadahamityev jaayate. 4.
 sarvam paraatmakan purvan jeeptimatramidan jagat.
 jyapterbabhuv pashyanti madhyamaa vaak tatah smruta. 5.
 vaktre vishuddhchakrakhye vaikhari sa mata tatah.
 sristyavirbhavamasadya madhyamaa vaak samaa mata. 6.
 akaaran sannidhikruty jagataan kaarantwatah.
 ikaara sarvavarnanaa shaktitvaata kaarann gatam. 7.
 jagat srashtumbhudichha yada hyasittadabhawat.
 kaambijmiti prahurmunyo vedapargah. 8.
 acaro jeeptimaatram syadikarashchitkala mata.
 ukaaro vishnurityahurvyapakatvanmaheshwarah. 9.
 
 rik sarveshwaro maayaan manovritimadarshayt.
 tamev vrittimashritya jagadrupamajijana. 10.
 vrittivrittimatoratra bhedalesho na vidyate.
 chandrachandricaoryadvad yatha vagarthyorapi. 11.
 swechhaya swasy chichchhaktau vishvamunmilayatyasau.
 varnanaa madhyaman clibmrivarnadvayan viduh. 12.
 
 eon mayeshwaratmaikivigyaanan sarvavastushu.
 sakshithvaata sarvabhootanaan sa ek iti nischitam. 13.
 
 aiuch brahmaswarupah san jagat swantargatan tatah.
 ichgiya vistaram karttumavirasinmahamunih. 14.
 
 bhootpanchakmetasmadhyavaranmaheshwarat.
 vyomvayvambuvahnyakhyabhutanyasit sa ev hi. 15.
 hakarad vyomsangran c yakaradvayuruchyate.
 rakaradvahnistoyan tu vakaraditi saiv vaak. 16.
 
 aadharabhutan bhootanamannadinaan c karanm.
 annadretastato jeevah kaarantvallaniritam. 17.
 
 shabdasparshau ruparasagandhasch vimajnanam.
 vyomadinaan guna hyete jaaniath sarvavastushu. 18.
 
 vakpani c jhabhayasidviradrupachidatmanah.
 sarvajantushu vigyeyan sthavradu na vidyate.
 vargana turyavarna ye karmendriyamya hi te. 19.
 
 ghandhash sarvabhootanaan padpayu upsthakah.
 karmendriyagana hyete jaataa hi parmarthatah. 20.
 
 shroatratvannayanghranjihvadhindriyapanchakam.
 sarveshamapi jantunamiritan jabgadadash. 21.
 
 pranadipanchakan chaiv mano buddhirhankrutih.
 babhuv kaarantven khafchhathathachatatva. 22.
 
 vargdwitiyavarnotthah pranadyah panch vyvah.
 madhyavargatrayajjata antahkarnavrittayah. 23.
 
 prakrutin purushanchaev sarveshamev sammtam.
 sambhutamiti vigyeyan kapay syaditi nischitam. 24.
 
 sattvam rajastam iti gunana trityan pura.
 samashritya mahadevah shashasar krideti prabhuh. 25.
 shakardrajasodbhutih shakaarattamasodbhavah.
 sakaaratsaitvasambhutiriti trigunasambhavah. 26.
 
 tattwathitah paran sakshi sarvanugrahavigrahah.
 ahmaatma paro hal syamiti shambhustirodadhe. 27.
