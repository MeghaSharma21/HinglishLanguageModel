 basant or sharad kaal pracheen china ke purvi jhou rajvansh kaal ke pehle bhaag ko kehete hai, jo 771 isapurv se 476 isapurv tak chalaa, haalaanki kabhi-kabhi 403 isapurv ko is kaal kaa ant mana jaataa he. is kaal se sambandhit chini sabhyataa kaa kshetra hwang nadi ghati ke maidan main, shandong prayadweep main or inke kuch nazadiiki ilaaqon main sthit tha. is kaal kaa 'basant or sharad' vaala naam 'basant or sharad ke vrutant' naamak aitihaasik granth se aata he, jisamen lu naam ke rajya kii daastaan darj he, jo prasiddh dharmik darshanik kanceyushious kaa ghar bhi tha.
 is kaal main jhou vansh ke samraaton kii shakti bas naam-maatr kii rah gai. unka keval apni rajdhani, luo yi, par niyantran tha. jhou kaal kii shuruyaat main rajgaraane ke bhai-bandhuon or senapatiyon ko alag-alag rajya de die gaye the jinako samraat ke adheen rakhaa gaya tha. yah isliye kiya gaya tha kyonki jhou apane se pehle aane vaale shang rajvansh se jeet to gaye the lekin unhen itane bade samrajya par niyantran rakhane main kathinaayee aa rahi thi. shuru main yah riyasaton kii vyavastha chali lekin jhou sarkaar kamazor padane par yah sab apane alag-alag rajkul or desh chalane lage. in riyasaton main se 12 sabase badi riyasaton ke sardaar samay-samay par milakar nitiyaan tay karne lage. kabhi-kabhi kisi ek netaa ko sabhi riyasaton kii mili-juli fuj kaa senapati bhi ghoshit kar diya jaataa tha. samay ke saath-saath in rajyon main jhadpen badhin or ye ek dusre kii dharti hadapney lage. chhathi shatabdi isapurv tak sabase chote rajya gaayab ho chuke the or ginati ke kuch bade rajyon kaa china par bolbalaa tha. kuch dakshini rajyon ne to khullam-khulla jhou samrajya se apni swatantraaa ghoshit kar di, jis se anya rajyon ne unamem se kuch ke viruddh abhiyaan chalaaye.
 in rajyon main aapas main jhagade to chal hi rahe the, lekin inke andar bhi satta ke liye kheenchaatani jari thi. 'jin' naamak rajya main chah zamindaaree pariwaron main aapasi yuddh hue. chi rajya main chen parivaar ne apane sabhi dushmano ko maar daalaa. jab in rajyon main andruni grih-yuddh samaapt hue or shaasha parivaar apane shatruon kaa khaatmaa kar ke spasht rup se ubhar aaye phir unki shaktiyaan rajyon ke bich kii ladaaiyon main lagani armbh hui. 403 isapurv main 'jin' rajya ke teen sarvocch pariwaron ne us rajya kaa vibhaajan kiya or yahi varsh 'basant or sharad kaal' kaa ant or 'jhagadate rajyon ke kaal' kaa armbh mana jaataa he.
