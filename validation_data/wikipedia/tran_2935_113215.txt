 tufaan meranti haal hi main darj kiye sabase majboot ushnkaribandheey chakravaati tufaan he. taiwan main pichley 59 saalon main yah ab tak kaa sabase tej tufaan rahaa he. isaki raftaar 370 kilometre prati ghante maapi gai he. iske kaaran paanch lakh se adhik parivaar prabhavit hue or 2.6 lakh se adhik gharon kii bijli vyavastha puri tarah thap ho gai. iske kaaran china ke 6 rajyon main chetavanee jari kii gayi he. isse dakshin china ke tateey ilaakon main 44 feet tak oonchi lehren uth sakati hai. iske kaaran philippines, taiwan or china main 30 logon kii maut ho chuki he or kai log ghayal ho chuke hai.
 8 sitambar ko sanyukt tufaan chetavanee kendra ne pashchimi prashanth mahaasaagar ke guam main 155 kilometre kshetra main ek ushnkaribandheey chakravaat kii chetavanee jari kii, iske anusaar yah bahut teji se majboot ho rahaa tha. 18:00 utisi ko japan ke mausam vibhaag ne is pranali ko avsaad ke rup main vargeekrut kiya. uske agle din sanyukt tufaan chetavanee kendra ne ise ushnkaribandheey avsaad ke rup main vargeekrut kiya. us samay yah bahut dhire-dhire pashchim-uttar-pashchim se aage badhate hue, yah uttar or dakshin pashchim kii ore bad rahaa tha. vahaan ise asaamaanya rup se garm pani kaa strot mila jo iske liye urja ke strot ke rup main kaary karne laga. 
 japan ke mausam vibhaag ke anusaar 10 sitambar ko 6:00 utisi main yah tufaan avsaad se aage bad kar ushnkaribandheey tufaan meranti ban gaya.
 meranti naamak tufaan philippines ke batanes naamak sthaan par bahut tej tha. yah jab itabaayat naamak dweep se 14 sitambar ko gujara to sara sanchaar vyavastha thap ho gaya tha. 17 sitambar ko pataa chalaa kii iske kaaran 292 ghar puri tarah se tabaaho ho gaye or 932 gharon ko kshati pahunchi he. kul kshati lagbhag das lakh dollar ke aas paas hui or iske kaaran das hajaar se adhik log prabhavit hue.
 taiwan main pichley 59 varshon main pehali baar 370 kilometre prati ghante kii raftaar se tufaan aayaa tha. iske kaaran paanch lakh log prabhavit hue. jisamen se ab tak do ki maut ho gai he. do lakh saath hajaar gharon main vidyut vyavastha thap ho gai or 130 makaane dhwast ho gaye. iske kaaran 370 udaanon ko bhi radd karnaa padaa.
 china main tufaan ke aane se pehle mausam vibhaag ne china ke chah rajyon main chetavanee jari kar di thi. isamen kaha gaya tha ki samudr ke tateey ilaakon main 44 feet tak oonchi lahare uth sakati hai. is tufaan ke kaaran china main paanch lakh gharon kii bijli aapurti puri tarah se thap ho gai he. isse bees se adhik rail or udaane radd ho gai he.
 china main is tufaan ke kaaran sabase adhik tabaahi fujjan or zhejiyang naamak praanton main hui. fujjan main is tufaan ke kaaran 18 logon kii maut ho gai or 11 anya laapata ho gaye. iske tej vaayu or baadh ke kaaran 20 karod dollar kaa nuksaan hua. yongchun shahar main baadh ke kaaran 871 saal purana pul tut gaya. zhejiyang main baadh ke kaaran 10 logon kii maut ho gai or 4 laapata ho gaye. lagbhag 902 ghar pani main dub gaye or 15 lakh se bhi adhik log iske kaaran prabhavit hue.
