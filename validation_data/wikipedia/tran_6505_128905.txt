 isisi mahila cricket vishwa cap ke sabase puraane or sabase pratishthit antarrashtriya mahila cricket toornament he. yah khel ek divasiy antarrashtriya praaroop ke liye vishwa championships ke rup main kaary karata he.
 mahila vishwa cap vartmaan main antarrashtriya cricket parishad dwaara yojit kiya jaataa he. 2005 tak, jab do sanghathanon kaa vilay kar diya, yah ek alag nikaay dwaara prashasit kiya gaya tha, antarrashtriya mahila cricket parishad . pehali baar vishwa cap 1973 main england main yojit kiya gaya tha, do saal udghaatan purushon ke toornament se pehle. ghatana ke praarambhik varshon ke vitt poshan main pareshaani, jiskaa arth yah kai tiimon kaa nimantran pratispardhaa karne ke liye asweekaar karne ke liye kiya tha or toornament ke bich ap karne ke liye chah saal ke antaraal kii vajah se chihnit kiya gaya. haalanki, 2005 ke baad se vishwa cap niyamit rup se chaar saal ke antaraal par kii mejabani kii he.
 das vishwa cap kii taarikh se paanch alag-alag deshon main yojit kiya gaya he karne ke liye khelaa jaataa he, bharat ghatana main teen baar mejabani hone ke saath. tiimon kii sankhya 2000 ghatana ke baad se aath nirdhaarit kiya gaya he, ke saath 1997 main purvavarti toornament ek ricard gyaarah tiimon dwaara ladaa gaya he. australia ab tak ke sabase safal team se kar rahe hai, chah khitaab jite or keval do avasaron par final banane main asafal rahi he. england or newjiland hi anya tiimon ke liye he, jabaki bharat or westeindies ke prayek jeetne ke liye par jane ke bina tak pahunch chuke ek avasar par antim, ghatana jeeta he.
 mahila antarrashtriya cricket pehle, 1934 main khelaa gaya tha jab england se ek party ke liye australia or newjiland kaa daura kiya. pehle test match disambar 1934 ko 28-31 khelaa gaya tha, or england ne jiti. newjiland ke khilaaf pehle test main agle varsh jaldi peechha kiya. in teen jatiyon hi test 1960 main jab dakshin africa se khelaa england ke khilaaf maichon main se ek number ke liye jab tak mahila cricket tiimon main khel rahe hai bane rahe. simit overon ke cricket pehle 1962 main england main pratham shrenee tiimon dwaara khelaa gaya tha. nau saal baad, pehali baar antarrashtriya match ek din purushon kii cricket main khelaa gaya tha, jab england melborn cricket maidan par australia par le liya.
 vaarta mahila cricket, jaik heward ke netritv ke liye ek vishwa cap dhaaran ke bare main 1971 main shuru hua. dakshin africa, unke rangabhed kanoonon ke liye duniya ke dabaav main pratiyogita main bhaag lene ke liye aamantrit nahin kiya gaya. anya do test khilne vaale deshon ke donon, australia or newjiland ke liye aamantrit kiya gaya. hayward pehle england mahilaon dwaara west indies ke daure kaa aayojan kiya tha, or yah is kshetra he ki anya do pratispardhi deshon se taiyaar kiya gaya tha; jamaica or trinidad evam tobago. number banane ke liye, england bhi ek "yuva england team" ko maidan main utaara he, or ek "antarrashtriya ilevan" ko bhi shaamil kiya gaya tha. paanch dakshin africa team ke liye muaavje ke liye aamantrit nahin kiya ja rahaa karne kaa ek saadhan ke rup main antarrashtriya ekadash ke liye khilne ke liye aamantrit kiya gaya tha, lekin baad main in nimantran waapas le liya gaya.
  udghaatan toornament june or july 1973 main england main sthanon kii ek kism main yojit kiya gaya tha, pehle purushon kii cricket vishwa cap do saal pehle khelaa tha. pratiyogita ek round robin toornament ke rup main khelaa gaya tha, or pichley anusuchit match australia ke khilaaf england tha. australia ek ekaant bindu se talika agrani khel main chalaa gaya: ve chaar maichon main jeet darj kii thi or ek ko chhod diya tha. england ne chaar maichon main jeet hasil kii thi, lekin ve newjiland ke liye kho diya tha. natijatan, match bhi pratiyogita ke liye ek vaastavik antim rup main kaary kiya. england toornament jeetne ke liye 92 ran se match main yojit, birmingham main jeet hasil kii. england toornament jeetne ke liye 92 ran se match edbeston main yojit kiya he, birmingham jeeta.
