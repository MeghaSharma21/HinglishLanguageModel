 upale yaa conde yaa goitha ko gaay yaa bhens ke gobar ko haath se aakaar dekar or phir dhoop main sukhaakar taiyaar kiya jaataa he. geele gobar ko aam taur par gramin mahilaayein haath se aakaar detii hai or yah prakriya upale pathana kehalaati he. sookhne ke pashchaat inkaa bhandaaran ek gobar or bhuse se bani kothri jise bitura kehete hai main kiya jaataa he. inkaa upyog gramin kshetron main khaanaa pakaane main prayukt hone vaale eendhan ke rup main lakadiyon ke saath yaa bina lakadiyon ke kiya jaataa he.
