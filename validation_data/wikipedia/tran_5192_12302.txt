 mairi laine bose, ek amareeki ganitagya or bhautik vigyaan kii professor thi. jinhen "mathematical methodes in da physical sciensej" ke lekhak ke rup main janaa jaataa hai, jon ki abhi bhi snaatak kii kakshaon main vyapak rup se prayog kii jaati hai. unhone uniwarsity of washington se ganit main 1938 main bechalar va 1940 main master degree hasil kii. masachusets institute of technology se bhautik vigyaan main piechdi kii degree hasil kii. unhone chicago main depaul uniwarsity main tees saal tak bhautikee padhaayi, or 1987 main sevanivrutt ho kar washington chali gayi. depaul vishvavidhyalay main apane samay se pehle, unhone duk vishvavidhyalay ke ganit vibhaag main ek prashikshak ke rup main kaary kiya. 2005 main 88 varsh kii aayu main bose ne apni pustak kaa teesraa sanskaran niklaa. mairi bose kaa vivaah ganitagya ralf pi bose se hua.
