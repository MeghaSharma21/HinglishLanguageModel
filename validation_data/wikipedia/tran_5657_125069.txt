 state bank of mysore bhartiya state bank kaa ek sahyogi bank he.
 state bank of mysore bharat main ek rashtriyakrut bank tha, jiskaa mukhyalay bengalooru main tha. yah state bank of india ke paanch sahyogi bainkon main se ek tha. state bank of mysore kii sthapana 1913 main maharaj krishna raja vaadiyar chaturth ke sangrakshan main bank of mysore limited ne banking samiti ke mahaan ejeeniyar-stateman kii adhyakshata main, bharat ratna sar em. vishwavaray ke rup main kii thi. 1953 ke dauraan, "mysore bank" ko sarkari vyavsaay or tregeri parichaalan karne ke liye bhartiya rigerv bank ke agent ke rup main niyukt kiya gaya tha or march 1960 main, yah state bank of indiyake tahat bhartiya state bank kii sahaayak company ban gai thi. adhinium 1959. ab bank state bank samooh ke adheen ek associat bank he or state bank of india main 92.33 pretisht share hai. bank ke sheyaron ko bengalooru, chennai or mumbai stock exenjon main soochibaddh kiya gaya he.
