 paramparaagat rup se vaidic swaraghaat ke teen vibhaag kiye jate hai- udaatt, anudaatt or swarit. 
 vedon main mantron kii 20 hajaar richaayen hai. in sabhi ko vibhinn chhandon main vibhakt kiya gaya he. koun kis prakaar gaaya jaayegaa, iskaa rishiganon dwaara usee samay nirdhaaran ho gaya he, jab inkaa srijan hua he. inhen layaaddh gaaya janaa chahie. motaa vibhaajan to udaatt, anudaatt swarit ke kram main hua he. mantron ke neeche, upar aadi tedi lakeeren jo lagaai jaati hai, unamem uccharan ke sanket hai. lekin jab inhen swar samet gaanaa ho to unke sargam, samaveda main die gayi hai. vahaan ankon ke chinh hai. vaidic sargam kaa sanket 1, 2, 3, 4 aadi ankon main aksharon ke upar diya jaataa he. 
 ek chand ko anek dhvaniyon main gaaya ja saktaa he. in dhwani bhinnataaon main sargam ke atirikt aadi-tedi lakiron main sanket dhwani bana dete hai. is prakaar veda mantron main jo richa samgaan ke rup main gaayi jaati he, tab unke upar ank sanket laga dete hai. samgaan kii yahi parampara he. kuch din purna samgaan kii anekon shakhaayen thi, par ab ve lupt ho gai. jo bachi hai maatr ve hi uplabdh hai.
