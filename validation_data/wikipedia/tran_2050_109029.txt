 sparsh aspataal yaa sparsh hospital kii sthapana 2006 main dow sharan shivaraj patil ne kii. yah narayan health city kaa ek ang he. narayan health city vishwa-vikhyath hriday sarjan dow devi shetthi kii pariklpana kaa saakaar rup he. sparsh aspataal kii sthapana kam kharch main sabhi logon ko vishwastareey swasthya suvidha dene kii shart par kii gai. sparsh aspataal sparsh foundation ke antargat aata he jo narayan health city kaa alaabhakaari ang he.
 sparsh aspataal ekaek media kii surkhiyon main us vakt aayaa jab usne november 2007 main bihar kii aath haath-pair vaali do varshiya ladki lakshmi tatamaa kaa safal oparation kiya or use saamaanya jivan jeene laayak banaaya. lakshmi ke maa-baap saamaanya majdoor the or is jatil oparation kaa kharch uthaane main asamarth the. aise main sparsh aspataal ke doctoron ne sattaaise ghante tak chale safal oparation ko muft main karke maanavtaa kii misaal kaayam kii.
 sparsh aspataal 19 se 25 oktober 2009 tak tees vishwavikhyath doctoron kii team ke saath 200 bacchon kaa muft main jatil oparation karne ja rahaa he.
