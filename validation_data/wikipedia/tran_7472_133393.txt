 
 
 
 parmaanu aprasar sandhi ko enpiti ke naam se janaa jaataa he. iskaa uddeshy vishwa bhar main parmaanu hathiyaaron ke prasar ko rokane ke saath-saath parmaanu pareekshan par ankush lagaana he. 1 july 1968 se is samjhaute par hastakshar hona shuru hua. abhi is sandhi par hastakshar kar chuke deshon kii sankhya 190 he. jisamen paanch ke paas anvik hathiyaar hai. ye desh hai- america, britain, france, rus or china. sirf paanch samprabhutaa sampann desh iske sadsy nahin hai. ye hai- bharat, isroyal, pakistan da.sudan or uttari korea. enpiti ke tahat bharat ko parmaanu sampann desh kii manyata nahin di gai he. jo iske dohare maapdand ko pradarshit karti he. is sandhi kaa prastaav ireland ne rakhaa tha or sabase pehle hastakshar karne vaala raashtra he finland. is sandhi ke tahat parmaanu shakti sampann raashtra use hi mana gaya he jisane 1 janvari 1967 se pehle parmaanu hathiyaaron kaa nirmaan or pareekshan kar liya ho. is aadhaar par hi bharat ko yah darja antarrashtriya str par nahin praapt he. kyonki bharat ne pehla parmaanu pareekshan 1974 main kiya tha. uttari korea ne is sandhi par hastakshar kiye, iskaa ulanghan kiya or phir isse baahar aa gaya.
