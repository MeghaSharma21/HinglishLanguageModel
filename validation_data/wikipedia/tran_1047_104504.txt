 purvi bharat 
 
 purvi bharat, main bharat ke purv ke kshetra aate hai. inamen pashchim bengal, urisa, bihar evam jharkhand rajya shaamil hai. yahaan boli jane vaali pramukh bhashaaon main hindi, bangla, oriya, urdu tathaa maithili aati hai. yahaan ke bade shaharon main kolkata, bhubaneshwar, patna, kataak, ranchi, rourkela hai.
