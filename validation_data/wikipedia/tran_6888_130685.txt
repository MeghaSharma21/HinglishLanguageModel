 punargamanwaad ek darshanik drushtikon he jisase yatharthy kii vivechna bhartiya parampara main hoti he. punargaman kaa arth vidhaan athwa vyavastha ke rup kaa vibhinn sthanon par utthan. iskaa utgam veda ke mahavakya yad pinde tad brahmande he. ek hi samay puure brahmand ko ishwar kaa rup or ishwar kaa aatma se sameekaran is mat se anuroopya hai. par iskaa pramaan samakaleen vigyaan main bhi miltaa he.
 yah virodhabhas he or anokhi baat he ki ise darshan kaa navyottar vishay athwa vaidic vichaar kaa prabandh mana ja saktaa he. subhash kaak ne is darshanik mat par kai kritiyaan likhin hai.
 is vichaardhaara se ek pracheen vaidic jyotish kaa gyaan hua he, jisase bharat kii samskruti, vigyaan or kalkram par nayaa prakaash padataa he. inamen se sabase rochak 108 ank, jo bhartiya samskruti main bahut aata he, kii vyakhya he. pramukh devi-devataaon ke 108 naam hai, japamala main 108 dane, 108 dham hai, aadi. subhash kaak ke shodh ne dikhaaya he ki vaidic kaal main yah gyaan tha ki surya or chandramaa pruthivi se lagbhag 108 niji vyas ke guna dur hai. aadhunik jyotish ne to yah bhi dikhaaya he ki surya kaa vyas pruthivi ke vyas se lagbhag 108 guna he.
