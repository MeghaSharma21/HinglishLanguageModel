 prashantak vah narcotic aushadhi he jo kendriya tantrika tantra par avsaadak prabhaav utpann karte hai. yah narcotic dawaai agar kam maatra main lee jaati he to yah mansapeshiyon ko vishram or chintaa mukt karti he. kintu agar yah aushadhi zyada maatra main lee gai to yah brahm utpann karti he, gahari neend jaise lakshan utpann karti he. in prashantak kaa upyog behoshee ke liye kiya jaataa he or lutpaat ke liye bhi inkaa upyog kiya jaataa he. prashantak ke udaahran he: banjodaijapin, diagepam, nitaronjepalm, chloropromegen, mithaqualin, mendrex aadi.
