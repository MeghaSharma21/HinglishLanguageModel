 america baptist interneshnal ministries ek antarrashtriya protestant isai mishanri samaaj he. is socity kii sthapana 1814 main sanyukt rajya america main hui thi. yah america baptist carch sanyukt rajya america se sambaddh kanstituint board he.
 is socity kii sthapana 1814 main pehali traivarshik sammelan main hui thi jahaan aarmabhik rup se burma kii do america mishnariyon, adoniram judson or ain haseltine judson dwaara pryojit kii gayi thi. yah sanghathan uttari america main aadhaarit sabase puraane baptist mishanri sanghathan he. yah socity apane kaaryakrttao vishwa ke vibhinn alag alag deshon main prachaar prasar karne hetu bhejne ke kaary main lambe samay tak sanlagn rahi. inhen ne china ke king rajvansh ke kaarykaal ke antim daur main vahaan bhi kaaryakrtta bheje the. 2009 ke aankado ke anusaar vishwa ke 76 deshon main is socity kii mishnariyaan he .
