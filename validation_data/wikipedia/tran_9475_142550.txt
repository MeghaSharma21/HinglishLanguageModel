 bhagwaan dada hindi filmon ke ek hasya abhinayta the.
 bhagwaan dada 1913 main bhagwaan Abhaji Palav ke rup amravati, maharashtra main main paidaa hua tha. vah ek kapadaa mil majdoor kaa betaa tha, lekin filmon ke saath junun savaar tha. vah ek majdoor ke rup main kaam kiya, lekin filmon kaa sapana dekha tha. vah mook filmon main bit bhoomikaaon ke saath uskaa tod mil gaya or studio ke saath puri tarah se shaamil ho gaya. unhone film banane or kam bajet filmon ru. ke liye 65,000 banane ke liye istemaal ek manch par siikhaa he.
