 asamiya bhashaa unnati saadhini sabha asmi bhashaa kii ek sanstha thi jisaki sthapana 25 agast 1888 ko hui thi. yah assam sahitya sabha kii purvavarti sanstha thi. iske pratham mahasachiv shriram sharma bordoloi the. is sanstha ke nirmaan kaa uddeshy asamiya bhashaa evam sahitya kii unnati ke liye praytn karnaa tha. isi sanstha ke prayaason ke falswaroop us samay kii asamiya boli apane vartmaan swaroop ko praapt ho paayi.
