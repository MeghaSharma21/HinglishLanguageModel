 bihar kii rajdhani patna ke dakshinpurv main lagbhag 100 kilometre dur sthit bodhgaya gaya jile se sata ek chota shahar he. bodhgaya main bodhi ped ke neeche tapasya kar rahe bhagwaan gautam buddha ko gyaan kii prapti hui thi. tabhi se yah sthal buddha dharm ke anuyaayiyon ke liye atyant mahatvapurn he. varsh 2002 main unesco dwaara is shahar ko vishwa viraasat sthal ghoshit kiya gaya.
 kareeb 500 ipu. main gautam buddha phalgu nadi ke tat par pahunche or bodhi ped ke neeche tapasya karne baithe. teen din or raat ke tapasya ke baad unhen gyaan kii prapti hui, jiske baad se ve buddha ke naam se jane gaye. iske baad unhone vahaan 7 hafte alag alag jagahon par dhyaan karte hue bitaayaa or phir sarnath ja kar dharm kaa prachaar shuru kiya. buddha ke anuyaayion ne baad main us jagah par janaa shuru kiya jahaan buddha ne baishakh maheene main purnima ke din gyaan kii prapti kii thi. dhire dhire ye jagah bodhgaya ke naam se janaa gaya or ye din buddha purnima ke naam se janaa gaya.
 lagbhag 528 i pus. ke baishakh maheene main kapilwastu ke rajkumaar gautam ne satya kii khoj main ghar tyaag diya. gautam gyaan kii khoj main niranjana nadi ke tat par based ek chote se gaanv uruvela aa gaye. vah isi gaanv main ek pipal ke ped ke neeche dhyaan sadhana karne lage. ek din vah dhyaan main leen the ki gaanv kii hi ek ladki sujatha unke liye ek katoraa kheer tathaa shahad lekar ai. is bhojan ko karne ke baad gautam pun: dhyaan main leen ho gaye. iske kuch dinon baad hi unke agyaan kaa badal chat gaya or unhen gyaan kii praapt hui. ab vah rajkumaar siddharth yaa tapasvi gautam nahin the balki buddha the. buddha jise sari duniya ko gyaan pradaan karnaa tha. gyaan praapt ke baad ve agle saat saptaah tak uruvela ke najadik hi rahe or chintan manan kiya. iske baad buddha varanasi ke nikat sarnath gaye jahaan unhone apane gyaan praapt kii ghoshnaa kii. buddha kuch maheene baad uruvela laut gaye. yahaan unke paanch mitra apane anuyaayiyon ke saath unase milane aae or unase dikshit hone kii prarthana kii. in logon ko dikshit karne ke baad buddha rajgir chale gaye. iske buddha ke uruvela waapas lautne kaa koi pramaan nahin miltaa he. duusari shatabdi isa purv ke baad uruvela kaa naam itihaas ke pannon main kho jaataa he. iske baad yah gaanv sambodhi, vaijarasana yaa mahabodhi naamon se janaa jane laga. bodhgaya shabd kaa ullekh 18 veen shatabdi se milane lagata he.
 vishwaas kiya jaataa he ki mahabodhi mandir main sthapit buddha kii murtti sambandh swayan buddha se he. kaha jaataa he ki jab is mandir kaa nirmaan kiya ja rahaa tha to isamen buddha kii ek murtti sthapit karne kaa bhi nirnay liya gaya tha. lekin lambey samay tak kisi aise shilpkaar ko khoja nahin ja sakaa jo buddha kii aakarshak murtti bana sake. sahasaa ek din ek vyakt aayaa or use murtti banane kii ichha jaahir kii. lekin iske liye usne kuch shartten bhi rakhin. uski shartt thi ki use pathar kaa ek stambh tathaa ek lamp diya jaae. uski ek or shartt yah bhi thi iske liye use chha: maheene kaa samay diya jaae tathaa samay se pehle koi mandir kaa darwaja na khole. sabhi shartten maan lee gai lekin vyagr gaanvvaasiyon ne tay samay se chaar din pehle hi mandir ke daravaaje ko khol diya. mandir ke andar ek bahut hi sundar murtti thi jiskaa har ang aakarshak tha sivaay chaati ke. murtti kaa chaati vaala bhaag abhi purna rup se taraasha nahin gaya tha. kuch samay baad ek buddha bhikshu mandir ke andar rahane laga. ek baar buddha uske sapane main aae or bole ki unhone hi murtti kaa nirmaan kiya tha. buddha kii yah murtti buddha jagat main sarvaadhik pratishtha praapt murtti he. nalanda or vikramshila ke mandiron main bhi isi murtti kii pratikruti ko sthapit kiya gaya he.
 bihar main ise mandiron ke shahar ke naam se janaa jaataa he. is gaanv ke logon kii kismat us din badal gai jis din ek rajkumaar ne satya kii khoj ke liye apane rajasihasan ko thukraa diya. buddha ke gyaan kii yah bhoomi aaj baudhon ke sabase bade tirthasthal ke rup main prasiddh he. aaj vishwa ke har dharm ke log yahaan ghoomane aate hai. pakshiyon kii chahchahaat ke bich buddham-sharanam-gachhami kii halki dhwani anokhi shanti pradaan karti he. yahaan kaa sabase prasiddh mandir mahabodhi mandir' he. vibhinn dharm tathaa sampradaay ke vyakt is mandir main aadhyatmik shanti kii talash main aate hai. is mandir ko unesco ne 2002 main world heritage site ghoshit kiya tha.buddha ke gyaan prapti ke 250 saal baad raja ashok bodhgaya gaye. mana jaataa he ki unhone mahabodhi mandir kaa nirmaan karaayaa. kuch itihaaskaaron kaa maananaa he ki pahli shatabdi main is mandir kaa nirmaan karaayaa gaya yaa uski marammat karai gai.
 hindustan main buddha dharm ke patan ke saath saath is mandir ko log bhul gaye the or ye mandir dhul or mitti main dab gaya tha. 19veen sadi main Sir Alexander Cunningham ne is mandir kii marammat karai. 1883 main unhone is jagah kii khudaai kii or kaaphi marammat ke baad bodhgaya ko apane puraane shandaar avasthaa main laayaa gaya.
 bodh-gaya ghoomane ke liye do din kaa samay paryaapt he. agar aap ek raat vahaan rukte hai to ek din kaa samay bhi praryaapt he. bodh-gaya ke paas hi ek shahar he gaya. yahaan bhi kuch pavitra mandir hai jise zarur dekhnaa chahie. in donon gaya ko dekhne ke liye kam se kam ek-ek din kaa samay dena chahie. ek atirikt ek din nalanda or rajgir ko dekhne ke liye rakhana chahie. bodhgaya ghoomane kaa sabase badhia samay buddha jayanti he jise rajkumaar siddharth ke janmadiwas ke rup main manaaya jaataa he. is samay yahaan hotalon main cameraa milanaa kaaphi mushkil hota he. is dauraan mahabodhi mandir ko hajaaron candilon kii sahaayta se sajaayaa jaataa he. yah drishy dekhnaa apane aap main anokhaa anubhav hota he. is drishy kii yaaden aapke jehan main hameshaa basii rahati he. bodhgaya main hi magadh vishvavidhyalay kaa campus he. iske najadik ek sainik chhavnee he tathaa kareeb saat kilometre dur intaraashtriya airport he jahaan se thailand, srilanka burma ityadi ke liye niyamit saptahik udaanen uplabdh hai.
 yah mandir mukhya mandir ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. is mandir kii banaavat samraat ashok dwaara sthapit stupa ke samaan he. is mandir main buddha kii ek bahut badi murtti sthapit he. yah murtti padmasana kii mudra main he. yahaan yah anushruti prachilat he ki yah murtti usee jagah sthapit he jahaan buddha ko gyaan nirvana praapt hua tha. mandir ke chaaron ore pathar kii nakkashidaar railing bani hui he. ye railing hi bodhgaya main praapt sabase purana avshesh he. is mandir parisar ke dakshin-purv disha main prakritik drishyon se samrudh ek park he jahaan buddha bhikshu dhyaan sadhana karte hai. aam log is park main mandir prashasan kii anumati lekar hi pravesh kar sakate hai.
 is mandir parisar main un saat sthanon ko bhi chinhit kiya gaya he jahaan buddha ne gyaan praapt ke baad saat saptaah vyateet kiya tha. jataka kathaon main ullekith bodhi vriksh bhi yahaan he. yah ek vishaal pipal kaa vriksh he jo mukhya mandir ke peeche sthit he. kaha jaataa buddha ko isi vriksh ke neeche gyaan praapt hua tha. vartmaan main jo bodhi vriksh vah us bodhi vriksh kii paanchavin peedhee he. mandir samooh main subah ke samay ghanton kii aawaaj man ko ek ajeeb si shanti pradaan karti he.
 mukhya mandir ke peeche buddha kii laal balua pathar kii 7 feet oonchi ek murtti he. yah murtti vijarasana mudra main he. is murtti ke chaaron ore vibhinn rangon ke pataake lage hue hai jo is murtti ko ek vishisht aakarshan pradaan karte hai. kaha jaataa he ki teesri shatabdi isa purv main isi sthaan par samraat ashok ne hiron se bana rajasihansan lagavaaya tha or ise prithvi kaa naabhi centre kaha tha. is murtti kii aage bhure balua pathar par buddha ke vishaal padchinh bane hue hai. buddha ke in padchinhon ko dharmachakr prarvatan kaa pratik mana jaataa he.
 buddha ne gyaan praapt ke baad doosraa saptaah isi bodhi vriksh ke aage khadaa avasthaa main bitaayaa tha. yahaan par buddha kii is avasthaa main ek murtti bani hui he. is murtti ko animesh lochan kaha jaataa he. mukhya mandir ke uttar purv main animesh lochan chaityas bana hua he.
 mukhya mandir kaa uttari bhaag chankamana naam se janaa jaataa he. isi sthaan par buddha ne gyaan praapt ke baad teesraa saptaah vyateet kiya tha. ab yahaan par kaale pathar kaa camel kaa phool bana hua he jo buddha kaa pratik mana jaataa he.
 mahabodhi mandir ke uttar pashcham bhaag main ek chhatvihin bhagnavshesh he jo ratnaghara ke naam se janaa jaataa he. isi sthaan par buddha ne gyaan praapt ke baad chauthaa saptaah vyateet kiya tha. dantakthaon ke anusaar buddha yahaan gahan dhyaan main leen the ki unke sharir se prakaash kii ek kiran nikali. prakaash kii inhen rangon kaa upyog vibhinn deshon dwaara yahaan lage apane pataake main kiya he.
 mana jaataa he ki buddha ne mukhya mandir ke uttari daravaaje se thodi dur par sthit ajapala-nigrodha vriksh ke neeche gyaan praapt ke baad paanchavaa saptaah vyateet kiya tha. buddha ne chatha saptaah mahabodhi mandir ke daayin ore sthit moochalinda kshil ke najadik vyateet kiya tha. yah kshil chaaron taraf se vrikshon se ghira hua he. is kshil ke madhya main buddha kii murtti sthapit he. is murtti main ek vishaal saanp buddha kii raksha kar rahaa he. is murtti ke sambandh main ek dantakatha prachlit he. is katha ke anusaar buddha prarthana main itane tallin the ki unhen aandhi aane kaa dhyaan nahin rahaa. buddha jab musaladhar barish main funs gaye to sanpon kaa raja moochalinda apane nivas se baahar aayaa or buddha kii raksha kii.
 is mandir parisar ke dakshin-purv main rajayatnaa vriksh he. buddha ne gyaan praapt ke baad apana santava saptaah isi vriksh ke neeche vyateet kiya tha. yaheen buddha do burmi vyapaariyon se mili the. in vyapaariyon ne buddha se aashray kii prarthana kii. in prarthana ke rup main buddhaman sharanam gachhami kaa uccharan kiya. isi ke baad se yah prarthana prasiddh ho gai.
