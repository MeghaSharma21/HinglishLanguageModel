 prayavarti dhara vah dhara he jo kisi vidyut paripath main apni disha badalati rahati hai. iske vipareet disht dhara samay ke saath apni disha nahin badalati. bharat main gharon main prayukt prayavarti dhara kii aavriti 50 hertz hoti he arthath yah ek second main pachaas baar apni disha badalati he.
 prayavarti dhara yaa prayavarti vibhav kaa pariman samay ke saath badalata rahata he or vah shunya par pahunchakar vipareet chinh kaa bhi ho jaataa he. vibhav yaa dhara ke pariman main samay ke saath yah parivartan kai tarah se sambhav he. udhaaharan ke liye yah sin-aakaar ho saktaa he, tribhujaakar ho saktaa he, vargakar ho saktaa he aadi. inamen sin-aakaar kaa vibhav yaa dhara kaa sarvaadhik upyog kiya jaataa he. aajkal duniya ke lagbhag sabhi deshon main bijli kaa utpaadan evam vitaran praayah prayavarti dhara ke rup main hi kiya jaataa he, na ki disht-dhara ke rup main. iskaa pramukh kaaran he ki asi kaa utpaadan aasaan he; iske pariman ko bina kathinaayee ke transafarmar kii sahaayta se kam yaa adhik kiya ja saktaa he ; tarah-tarah kii tri-feji motaron kii sahaayta se isko yantric urja main badla ja saktaa he. iske alaava shravy aavriti, radio aavriti, drishy aavriti aadi bhi prayavarti dhara ke hi rup hai.
