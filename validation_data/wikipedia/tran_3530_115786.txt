 pashtunvaali yaa pakhtunvaali dakshin asia ke pashtun samudaay kii samskruti kii alikhit maryaada parampara he. iske kuch tatv uttar bharat or pakistan kii izzat maryaada se milte-julate hai. ise 'pashtuni tareeqa' yaa 'pathan tareeqa' bhi kaha ja saktaa he. haalanki pashtun log vartmaan kaal main muslim hai, lekin pashtunvaali kii jade islam se bahut pehle shuru hui maani jaati hai, yani yah ek islam-purv parampara he. pashtunon kii sohabat main rahane vaale bahut se gair-pashtun log bhi aksar pashtunvaali kaa paalan karte hai.
 pashtunvaali maryaada ke nau niyam hote hai:
 pashtunavali Pashtunwali pashtunavali yaa pakhtunavali kaa matlab hota he, pakhtun jeevanstyli. yah ek islam ke aane se pehle kii jeevanstyli yaa mazahab he. yah paramparaagat jeevanstyli he. yah pakhtun yaa pathaanon kii naitikta yaa acharsanhita bhi he. iske mukhya siddhanth nau hai. inkaa palaan pakhunon athwa pathaanon dwaara pichley 5 hazaar saalon se kiya ja rahaa he. inkaa islam ke saath koi matbhed nahin he, ev yah sabhi islam main shaamil hai.beshumaar jananath kii nematen pakhto ke maadyam se pusunon par nazil hoti hai:- gani khan pakhtunavali kaa paalan afghanistan, bharat va pakistan samet sari duniya ke pathaanon dwaara kiya jaataa he. kuch gair pathan log bhi pathaanon ke prbhaav main aakar pakhtunavali kaa paalan karte hai. yah ek aachaar samhita he jo vyaktigat jivan ke saath saath saamaajik jivan kaa bhi maargdarshan karti he. pashtunavali bahumat pathaanon dwaara paalan kii jane vaali aachaar samhita he. yah kehane main koi harz nahin he ki pathan jab tak pakhtunavali kaa paalan karte rahe tab tak unhone duniya par raaj kiya va kabhi bhi kisi se parajeet nahin hue. bharat main jab unhone pakhtunavali kaa paalan karnaa band kar diya tab ve jat maraathon, mughalo angrezon sabhi se parajeet hote gayi, zaleel hue, va mufalisi ne unhen aa gheraa. pehle bataaya ja chukaa he ki pakhtun bani israel se hai. yah khuda kii chuni hui qaom he. pakhtunavali ke siddhanth musa alaihissalam kii tauraat par hi aadhaarit hai. isamen vah ahad shaamil he jo khuda ne bani israel se baandha tha. jab bani israel apana vaada puraa karenge to khuda bhi apana vaada puraa karegaa jo usne bani israel se kiya tha. jaisaa ki quran shareef main bhi aayaa he ki i bani israel tum apana vah vaada puraa karo jo tumne mujh se kiya tha, taaki main apana vah vaada puraa karun jo maine tum se kiya tha. pakhtunon ne apana paanch hazaar saal ke itihaas kaa bhi record surakshit rakhaa hua he. pakhtunavali aatm sammaan, azaadi, nyaay, mehmaannawaazi, pyaar, maaf kar dena, badla lenaa, sahansheeltaa aadi ko badhaava dene vaali aachaar samhita he. yah sab kuch sabake liye he, jisamen ajanabi va paradesi bhi shaamil hai chaahe unki jati, dharm, vishwaas, rashtriyata kuch bhi ho. pashtunavali kaa paalan karnaa vaa isaki hifaazat karnaa har ek pathan kii vyaktigat zimmedaari he. mukhya siddhantapaktunavali ke kuch mukhya siddhanth is prakaar hai.1.	melmestiyansnelmstia – dayaa, meharbaani va mehmaannawaazi sabhi paradesiyon va ajanabiyon par dikhaaya janaa. isamen dharm, jati, mulvansh rashtriyata aadi ke aadhaar par koi bhedbhaav naa karnaa. yah sab bagair kisi inaam yaa badale main kuch milegaa aisi ummid ke kiya janaa chaiye. pashunon kii mehmaan nawazi duniya bhar main mashhoor he. 2.	nanavatai:- Nanawatai – agar koi apane dushmano se bachaane ke liye madad va sangrakshan maange to unhen sangrakshan diya janaa chaiye. har qeemat par unhen sangrakshan diya jaataa he. un logon ko bhi jo kaanoon se bhaag rahe hon us samay takh sangrakshan diya janaa chaiye jab tak ki vastusthiti saafe nahin ho jaati. jab parajeet paksh vijeta ke paas jaataa he, tab bhi unhen sangrakshan diya jaataa he. kai maamalon main aatm samarpan karke apane dushman ke hi ghar main sharan lee jaati he.3.	badal – koon ke badale koon, aankh ke badale aankh, daant ke badale daant. aisa patit hota he ki yah maaf kar dene, dayaa karne ke siddhanth ke vipareet he. lekin aisa samaj kam hone ke kaaran galat fahemiyaan paidaa hoti hai. maafi ke liye zaruri he, ki galati karne vaala aafi maange. bagaiyr maange kisi maafi? yah sabhi prakaar ke gunahon va aparaadhon par lagu he. chaahe galat kaam kal kiya gaya tha, chaahe ek hazaar saal pehle kiya gaya ho. agar galati karne vaala zindaa naa ho to uske nazadiiki koon ke rishte vaale se badla liya jayega. 4.	tureh Tureh –ek pathan ne apni zamin, sampatti, parivaar striyaan aadi kii hifaazat bahaduree se ladaai lard kar kerani chaiye. yah pashtun stri purush donon hi kii zimmedaari he. iske liye usne maut ko bhi yadi gale lagane kii zarurat pade to maut ko gale laga lenaa chaiye. 5.	sabaat Sabat –har ek ne apane parivaar, doston, qabeele, aadi ke prati vafaadaar hona chaiye. sabaat yaa vafadaari behad zaruri he va ek pathan kabhi bhi bewafaa nahin ho saktaa. 6.	eemaandaaree Imandari – ek pakhtun ne hameshaa acchhee baaten sochna chaiye, acchhee baaten bolnaa chaiye, achche kaam karnaa chaiye. pakhtun ne sabhi cheezon kaa sammaan karnaa chaiye jisamen log, jaanavar va pryaavaran bhi shaamil he. inako nasht karnaa pakhtunavali ke kilaaf he.7.	isteqaamat Isteqamat – khuda par vishwaas karnaa. pashto main jise khuda, arabi main allaah va hindi main vagvaan kaha jaataa he. isi ko angrezi main gaad kehete hai. aisa vishwaas karnaa ki khuda ek he, usee ne sari duniya kii sabhi chizein banaai hai. yah yahaan hazaar saal purana pakhtunavali kaa siddhanth islam ke tauhid ke samakaksh he.8.	 gairat:- Ghayrat – pakhtun ne hameshaa apana maanviya garima banaaye va bachaaye rakhana chaiye. pakhtun samaaj main garima bahut mahatvapurn he. unhone apni va anya logon kii bhi garima ko banaaye va bachaaye rakhana chaiye. unhone khud apana bhi sammaan karnaa chaiye va dusron kaa bhi sammaan karnaa chaiye namoos Namus – ek pathan ne pathan striyon va ladakiyon ke sammaan kii raksha har qeemat par kerani chaiye. anya mahatvapurn siddhanth:- 	azaadi:- shaareerik, maansik, dharmik ruhani, raajnneetik va aarthik azaadi, us samay tak jab tak ki yah dusron ko nuksaan naa pahunchane lage. 	nyaay va maaf karnaa:- agar koi jaanboojh kar galat kaam kare va aapane agar nyaay kii maang nahin kii naa hi galati karne vaale ne maafi maangi to koon ke badale koon aankh ke badale aankh dant ke badale dant ke anusaar badla jab tak naa liya jaye, pathan par yah ek qarza rahata he. yahaan tak ki yah us par ek bandhan he ki use aisa karnaa hi hoga. chaahe vah pathan stri ho yaa pathan purush. 	vaade puure karnaa:- ek asli pathan kabhi bhi apane vaade se mukaregaa nahin.	ekta va barabari:- chaahe ve koi bhi bhashaa bolte hon, chaahe kisi bhi qabeele ke hon, chaahe garib hon yaa amir, chaahe kitna hi rupaya unke paas ho, pakhtunavali sari duniya ke pakhtunon yaa pathaanon ko ek sutara ben baandhti he. har insaan baraabar he, yah pashtunavali kaa mul siddhanth tha.	sune jane kaa adhikaar:- chaahe ve koi bhi bhashaa bolte hon, chaahe kisi bhi qabeele ke hon, chaahe garib hon yaa amir, chaahe kitna hi rupaya unke paas ho har ek ko yah adhikaar praapt he ki uski baat samaaj main va jirga main suni jaye. 	parivaar va vishwaas:- yah maananaa ki har ek pakhtun stri va purush anya pakhtunon kaa bhai va bahan he, chaahe pakhtun 1 hazaar qabeelon main hi bante kyon naa hon. pakhtun ek parivaar he, unbhein anya pakhtun pariwaron ke striyon, betiyon, bde buzurgon, mata- pita, beton, va patiyon kaa khyaal rakhana chaiye. 	sahyog:- garib va kamazoron kii madad kii jaani chaiye vah bhi is prakaar se ki kisi ko maaloom bhi naa pade. 	ilm yaa gyaan praapt karnaa:- pakhtun ne zindagi, itihaas, vigyaan, sabhyataa samskruti aadi ke bare main lagaataar apana gyaan badhaate rahane kii koshish karte rahanaa chaiye. pakhtun ne apana dimaag hameshaa naye vichaaron ke liye khulaa rakhana chaiye. 	buraai ke kilaaf lado:- achchhai va buraai ke bich ek lagaataar jang jari he, pakhtun ne jahaan kahin bhi vah buraai dekhe to uske kilaaf ladanaa chaiye. yah uskaa farz he. hevaad:- Hewad –pakhtun ne apane pakhtun desh se pyaar karnaa chaiye. ise sudhaarney va muqammal banane kii koshish karte rahanaa chaiye. pakhtun sabhyataa va samskruti kii raksha karnaa chaiye. kisi bhi prakaar ke videshi hamale kii sthiti main pakhtun ne apane desh pakhtuunakva kii hifaazat karnaa chaiye. desh kii hifaazat se taatpary sabhyataa samskruti, paramparaayen, jivan mulya aadi kii hifaazat karne se he. 	dod pasbani:- pakhtun par yah bandhankaree he ki vah pakhtun sabhyataa va saskruti kii hifaazat kare. pashtunavali yah salaah detii he ki isko safaltaapurvak karne ke liye pakhtun ne pashto zabaan kabhi nahin chhodana chaiye. pashto pakhtun sabhyataa va samskruti ko bachaane kaa mukhya strot he.
