 chikitsashastra ke kshetra main naanaa prakaar ke rogon ke vibhinn kaarnon ko vaigyanik vivechan hetuki athwa hetuvigyaan kaha jaataa he. 
 manushya ko apni raksha, vruddhi tathaa vikaas ke liye virdohi paristhiti se niratar sangharsh karnaa padataa he. pratikul paristhiti se nirantar sangharsh karnaa padataa he. pratikul paristhiti ko anukool banane kii cheshtaa main yadi manushya deh vifal hone lagati he, to vah swayan apane ang pratyangon kii raksha hetu unki rachana tathaa kriya main aavashyak her-pher kar virdohi paristhiti se yathasambhav saamanjasy sthapit karne kaa prayaas karti he, kintu jab paristhiti kii vikatataa deh kii sahan athwa samanjan shakti se adhik prabal, yaa vegavati ho jaati he to prasthaapit saamanjasy main ulat pher ho jaataa he, jiske falswaroop virdohi paristhiti kaa dushprabhav deh ke utakon ko vikrut kar peedaakaari lakshanon se yukt rog vishesh ko prakat karata he.
 manushya kaa jivan sabhi prakaar kii anukool, pratikul or pavirthansheel paristhitiyon kaa saamana karne ke kaaran atyat jatil ho gaya he. isliye rogon ke vibhinn kaarnon kii vaigyanik reeti se chaanbin kar kis rogvishesh kaa pradhan kaaran tathaa uske sahaayak gaun kaarnon kaa pataa lagaana ek gorakhdhandha he.
 rog hetuvigyaan kii drushti se praaia: sabhi vanshaagat arjeet rogon kaa sthool vargeekaran is prakaar sambhav he :
 rog ke kaaran bhi kai prakaar ke hote hai. purvapravartak athwa purvavarti kaaran swayan rogajanak na hote hue bhi manushya kii deh kii rogapratirohak shakti ko kam kar use samvedansheel yaa grahanshil, bana dete hai. taatkaalik athwa uttejk kaaran rog ke prakope ko sahasaa bhadkaa dete hai. paraseete sookshm jeevanu sharir main pravesh kar badhate hai or vishesh prakaar kaa jeevvish utpann kar swayan aakraamak hokar rog ke karta hote hai. sabhi prakaar ke kaarnon kii shrunkhlaa kaa tathaa unke paraspar sambandh or apekshaakrit balaabal ke adhyayan dwaara vaataavaran kii rogesahayak pravritiyon ko vash main kar anek rogon kaa safal niyantran yaa unmoolan sambhav ho sakaa he. ab keval rog kaa hi adhyayan nahin kiya jaataa, apitu samgr vaataavaran se sanyukt manushya kaa man shaareerik adhyayan karne kii ore pravriti he. is kaaran samajamoolak chikitsavigyaan ke siddhantanusaar rog kii karanmala kii vibhinn ladiyon kaa gyaan manushya ke sharir, man tathaa samaajagat dosha ke adhyayan se praapt hota he or unke dur karne ke prayaas se hi rog ke nirodhan main sahaayta milti he.
 dedh-do sau varsh purv sansar main jeevanujnya sankramak rogon kaa praadhanya itnaa adhik tha ki anya kayik athwa kriyaagat rogon kii ore vaastavik dhyaan akrusht nahin ho sakaa. viksit or samrudh deshon main sankramak rogon kaa safal niyantran ho jane par, anya rogon kii rokthaam kaa prayaas santoshpurn dhang se ho rahaa he or ab vriddhavastha ke rogon kii samasya par vishesh dhyaan diya ja rahaa he. jivan kii visham jatilta ke kaaran maansik rogon, audyogikaran ke kaaran vyavsaayik rogon or mashiinon dwaara durghatnaon kii sankhya apekshaakrit bad rahi he, jinpar dhyaan dena aavashyak ho gaya he. bharat main sankramak rogon kii kami avashya hui he, kintu unka puraa niyantran nahin ho paaya he. yahan keet, vaayu, jal tathaa bhojan dwaara prasaarit sankramak rogon kii pradhanta ke saath kuposhanjanit vikaar or balrogon kaa bahulya he.
 rog or maadhy main bhed he. donon hi aswasthkar hai. maadhy kii dasha main deh kii swaabhaavik kriyaaon main aisi baadha pad jaati he jisase manushya apane jivan kii aavashyaktaaon kii purti karne main asamarth sa ho jaataa he. yah deh kaa kriyaagat dosha he. parantu rogavastha main sharir kii avyavasthit kriya ke dushprabhavon, athwa ghaatak padaarthon or durghatnaon, ke kaaran deh main asaadhaaran rup se kayik rog ho jaataa he. is kaaran rog or maadhy main keval aanshik sambandh he. rog se maadhy hona aavashyak nahin or maadhy bina rog ke bhi sambhav he. sharir kriyavigyaan, rasayan-vigyaan tathaa praani-vigyaan maadhy ke samast jatil kaarnon ko prakat karne main asamarth hai. yah ab spasht ho gaya he ki keval kriyaagat dosha se utpann maadhy hi nahin, apitu deh ke anek kayik rogon kaa mul saamaajik, paariwarik athwa audyogic dussamanjasy, aarthik arakshita, yaa aahaar sambandhi hinata dwaara sinchit hota rahata he. isi kaaran manushya ke swasthya, sukh suvidha or dakshata ke liye kriyaagat dosha, kayik rog, maansik asthirataa or saamaajik avyavashtha kaa vigyanmoolak anubhavsiddh upachaar hona chahie. sabhi praaniyon kii sabhi prakaar kii purna dekhbhaal se hi rog ke gunfit kaarnon ko dur kiya ja saktaa he. adhure athwa ekang upaay vyarth hi hai.
