 avishkaar hindi kii ek vigyaan patrika he. yah neshnal research devlepment corporation, nai dilli dwaara prakashit hoti he. iskaa mukhya uddeshy soochna kaa prasar karnaa or nai praudyogiki, khoj, naveentaaon, bauddhik sampada adhikarase jude muddon aadi ke bare main aam janata ke bich jaagrukta paidaa karnaa he or chaatro, vaigyanikon, takanicianon, ubharate udhyamiyon aadi ke bich khoj, naveenta tathaa udhyamita kii bhaavana kaa vikaas karnaa he. hindi main vigyaan patrika avishkaar kaa prakaashan 1971 main shuru hua tha. avishkaar janhit ke chaalu muddon ke saath vigyaan, praudyogiki, khoj, nawacharon or bauddhik sampada adhikaron se sambandhit rashtriya mahatva ke muddon par kendrit rahata he.
 sampurna praasangik aalekhon ke alaava patrika main bhinn niyamit kollam bhi rahete hai – vividha, vaigyanikon kaa bachapan, kuch rochak kuch prerak prasang, vigyaan sahitya charchaa, bauddhik sampada kii baaten, avishkaar or navachaar, khel-khel main vigyaan, samachariki or enardisi kii praudyogiki – aapke liye. vividha kollam main bhinn esendty vishyon par chote feechar prakashit hote hai.
 vaigyanikon kaa bachapan main vaigyanikon, avishkaarakon, nayaa karne waalo aadi ke bachapan par aalekh hote hai taaki un paristhitiyon ke bare main bataaya ja sake jahaan se ve aage bade or apni safalataa tak pahunche. is patrika main vaigyanikon, avishkaarakon, nayaa karne waalo aadi kii jeevani bhi prakashit kii gai he. kuch rochak, kuch prerak main dilachasp or prerak kathaayen kahi gai hai. yah bhi vaigyanikon, avishkaarakon, nayaa karne waalo aadi par kendrit he. vigyaan sahitya charchaa kollam main vigyaan va takanalaji par ai nai pustak kii samiksha chhapati he. bauddhik sampada kii baaten main bauddhik sampada adhikaron par vibhinn vishyon par charchaa hoti he.
 'khel khel main vigyaan' schuli bacchon kaa ek kollam he. isamen vigyaan ke buniyadi siddhant samajhaae jate hai. 'samachariki' main vigyaan va takanalaji ke kshetra main pragati se sambandhtit khabare prakashit hoti hai.
 enardisi kii 'praudyogiki aapke liye' udhyamiyon ko praudyogiki ke bare main adhyatan jaankaree detii he jo enardisi ke paas vyapareekaran ke liye uplabdh he.
