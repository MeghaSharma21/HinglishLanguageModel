 patna yaa patliputra bharat ke bihar rajya kii rajdhani evam sabase bada nagar he. patna kaa pracheen naam patliputra tha. aadhunik patna duniya ke gine-chune un vishesh pracheen nagaron main se ek he jo ati pracheen kaal se aaj tak aabaad he.
 apane aap main is shahar kaa aitihaasik mahatv he. isa purv megasthnij ne apane bharat bhraman ke pashchaat likhi apni pustak indica main is nagar kaa ullekh kiya he. palibothra jo ganga or arennovas ke sangam par basaa tha. us pustak ke aakalnon ke hisaab se pracheen patna 9 mile lambaa tathaa 1.75 mile chaudaa tha.
 patna bihar rajya kii rajdhani he or ganga nadi ke dakshini kinare par avasthit he. jahaan par ganga ghaghara, son or gandak jaisi sahaayak nadiyon se milti he. 
 solah lakh se bhi adhik aabaadi vaala yah shahar, lagbhag 15 kimi lambaa or 7 kimi chaudaa he.

 patna naam patandevi se prachlit hua he. ek anya mat ke anusaar yah naam sanskrit ke pattan se aayaa he jiskaa arth bandargah hota he. mauryaal ke unani itihaaskaar megasthnij ne is shahar ko palibothara tathaa chiniyatri phahiyaan ne palinphu ke naam se sambodhit kiya he. yah aitihaasik nagar pichhali do sahastrabdiyon main kai naam paa chukaa he - patligram, patliputra, pushppur, kusumpur, azimabad or patna. aisa samajha jaataa he ki vartmaan naam shershah suri ke samay se prachlit hua.
 mukhya lekh: patna kaa itihaas
 pracheen patna son or ganga nadi ke sangam par sthit tha. son nadi aaj se do hajaar varsh purv agamkuna se aage ganga main milti thi. patligram main gulab kaaphi maatra main upjaaya jaataa tha. gulab ke phool se tarah-tarah ke itr, dawa aadi banaakar unka vyapaar kiya jaataa tha isliye iskaa naam patligram ho gaya. lokkathaon ke anusaar, raja patrak ko patna kaa janak kaha jaataa he. usne apni rani patli ke liye jaadu se is nagar kaa nirmaan kiya. isi kaaran nagar kaa naam patligram padaa. patliputra naam bhi isi ke kaaran padaa. sanskrit main putr kaa arth betaa tathaa gram kaa arth gaanv hota he.
 puratathvik anusandhano ke anusaar patna kaa likhit itihaas 490 isa purv se hota he jab haryak vansh ke mahaan shaasha ajatshatru ne apni rajdhani rajgrih yaa rajgir se badalakar yahan sthapit kii. yah sthaan vaishali ke lichchhaviyon se sangharsh main upyukt hone ke kaaran rajgrih kii apeksha samrik drushti se adhik mahatvapurn tha kyonki yah yuddh anek maah tak chalane vaala ek bhayaavah yuddh tha. usne ganga ke kinare samrik rup se mahatvapurn yah sthaan chunaa or apana durg sthapit kar liya. us samay se is nagar kaa itihaas lagaataar badalata rahaa he. 2500 varshon se adhik purana shahar hone kaa gaurav duniya ke bahut kam nagaron ko hasil he. buddha dharm ke pravartak gautam buddha apane antim dinon main yahan se hokar gujarey the. unki yah bhavishyavaanee thi ki nagar kaa bhavishya ujjwal hoga, baadh yaa aag ke kaaran nagar ko khatra bana rahegaa. aage chal kar ke mahaan nand shaskaon ke kaal main iskaa or bhi vikaas hua evam unke baad aane vaale shaskaon yathamaury samrajya ke utkarsh ke baad patliputra bhartiya upmahadweep main satta kaa kendra ban gaya. chandragupta maury kaa samrajya bengal kii khadi se afghanistan tak fail gaya tha. maury kaal ke aarambh main patliputra ke adhikansh rajmahal lakadiyon se bane the, par samraat ashok ne nagar ko shilaon kii sanrachana main tabdeel kiya. china ke phahiyaan ne, jo ki san 399-414 tak bharat yatra par tha, apane yatra-vrutant main yahan ke shail sanrachanaon kaa jivant varnan kiya he.
 megasthaneez, jo ki ek unani itihaaskaar or chandragupta maury ke darbar main unani shaasha silukas ke ek rajdoot ke naate aayaa tha, ne patliputra nagar kaa pratham likhit vivaran diya he usne apni pustak main is shahar ke vishay main evam yahaan ke logon ke bare main bhi vishad vivaran diya he jo aaj bhi bhartiya itihaas ke chaatro ke liye sandarbh ke rup main kaam aata he. sheeghra hi patliputra gyaan kaa bhi ek kendra ban gaya. baad main, gyaan kii khoj main kai chini yatri yahan aae or unhone bhi yahaan ke bare main apane yatra-vrutanton main bahut kuch likhaa he. mauryon ke pashchaat kanv evam shungo sahit anek shaasha aaye lekin is nagar kaa mahatv kam nahin hua.
 iske pashchaat nagar par gupt vansh sahit kai rajwanshon kaa raaj rahaa. in raajaaon ne yaheen se bhartiya upmahadweep par shashan kiya. gupt vansh ke shasankaal ko pracheen bharat kaa swarna yug kaha jaataa he. par lagaataar hone vaale huno ke akraman evam gupt samrajya ke patan ke baad is nagar ko vah gaurav nahin mil paaya jo ek samay maury vansh yaa gupt vansh ke samay praapt tha.
 gupt samrajya ke patan ke baad patna kaa bhavishya kaaphi anishchit rahaa. 12 veen sadi main baktiyaar khilji ne bihar par apana adhipaty jamaa liya or kai aadhyatmik pratishthanon ko dhwast kar daalaa. is samay ke baad patna desh kaa saanskrithik or rajnatik kendra nahin rahaa.
 mughalkaal main dilli ke sattadhariyon ne apana niyantran yahan banaae rakhaa. is kaal main sabase utkrishth samay tab aayaa jab shershah suri ne nagar ko punerjeevit karne kii koshish kii. usne ganga ke tir par ek kila banane kii sochi. uskaa banaaya koi durg to abhi nahin he, par afakhaan style main bana ek masjid abhi bhi he.
 mughal badshaah akbar kii sena 1574 isvi main afagaan sargana daud khan ko kuchalane patna aayaa. akbar ke rajya sachiv evam aaine-akabari ke lekhak abul fajal ne is jagah ko kaagaj, pathar tathaa shishe kaa sampann audyogic kendra ke rup main varnit kiya he. patna rice ke naam se europe main prasiddh chaaval ke vibhinn naslon kii gunvattta kaa ullekh bhi in vivaranon main miltaa he.
 mughal badshaah aurangzeb ne apane priy pote mohammad azeem ke anurodh par 1704 main, shahar kaa naam azimabad kar diya, par is kalkhand main naam ke atirikt patna main kuch vishesh badlaav nahin aayaa. azeem us samay patna kaa soobedaar tha.
 mughal samrajya ke patan ke saath hi patna bengal ke nababon ke shasnadhin ho gaya jinhone is kshetra par baari kar lagaaya par ise vanijyik kendra bane rahane kii chhot di. 17veen shatabdi main patna antarrashtriya vyapaar kaa kendra ban gaya. angrezon ne 1620 main resham tathaa kelico ke vyapaar ke liye yahan factory kholi. jald hi yah sault peter ke vyapaar kaa kendra ban gaya jiske kaaran french or dach log se pratispardhaa tej hui. baxur ke nirnaayak yuddh ke baad nagar east india company ke adheen chalaa gaya or vaanijya kaa kendra bana rahaa.
 isvi san 1912 main bengal vibhaajan ke baad, patna urisa tathaa bihar kii rajdhani bana. ai eff munning ne patna ke prashasnic bhawanon kaa nirmaan kiya. sangrahalay, ucch nyaayalay, vidhansabha bhawan ityadi banane kaa shrey unheen ko jaataa he. kuch logon kaa maananaa he ki patna ke nae bhawanon ke nirmaan main hasil hui maharath dilli ke shasnic kshetra ke nirmaan main bahut kaam ai. san 1935 main urisa bihar se alag kar ek rajya bana diya gaya. patna rajya kii rajdhani bana rahaa.
 bhartiya swatantraaa sangram main nagar ne apni mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai. nil kii kheti ke liye 1917 main champaran aandolan tathaa 1942 kaa bharat chodo aandolan ke samay patna kii bhoomika ullekhneey rahi he. aazaadee ke baad patna bihar kii rajdhani bana rahaa. san 2000 main jharkhand rajya ke alag hone ke baad patna bihar kii rajdhani purvavat bana rahaa.
 patna ganga ke dakshini tat par sthit he. ganga nadi nagar ke saath ek lambi tat rekha banaati he. patna kaa vistaar uttar-dakshin kii apeksha purv-pashchim main bahut adhik he. nagar teen ore se ganga, son nadi or punpun nadi nadiyon se ghira he. nagar se thik uttar hajipur ke paas gandak nadi bhi ganga main aa milti he. haal ke dinon main patna shahar kaa vistaar pashchim kii ore adhik hua he or yah danapur se ja mila he.
 mahatma gandhi sethu jo ki patna se hajipur ko jodne ko liye ganga nadi par uttar-dakshin kii disha main bana ek pul he, duniya kaa sabase lambaa sadak pul he. do len vaale is prabalit kancreet pul kii lambaai 5575 meter he. ganga par bana digha-sonpur rail-sah-sadak pul patna or sonpur ko jodta he.

 mukhya lekh dekhen: patna main dashahra
 dashahra main saanskrithik kaaryakramon kii lambi par ksheen hoti parampara he. is parampara kii shuruyaat varsh 1944 main madhya patna ke govind mitra road muhalle se hui thi. dhurandhar sangitagnon ke saath-saath bade qavvaal or mukesh yaa talat mahmud jaise gayak bhi yahan se judate chale gaye.
 1950 se lekar 1980 tak to yahi lagata rahaa ki desh ke sheershasth sangeetkaaron kaa tirth-sa ban gaya he patna. divi paluskar, onkaar nath thakur, bhimsen joshi, ali akbar khan, nikhil banerjee, vinayak raav patavardhan, pandit jasraj, kumaar gandharva, biji jog, ahamed jaan thirakwa, birju maharaj, sitara devi, kishan maharaj, gudai maharaj, bismilla khan, hariprasad chaurasia, sivakumar sharma ... badi lambii suchi he. pandit ravishankar or ustad amir khan ko chhodkar baaqi praayah sabhi nami sangitajna un dinon patna ke dashahra sangeet samarohon kii shobhaa ban chuke the.
 60 varsh pehle patna ke dashahra or sangeet kaa jo sambandh sutara qaayam hua tha vah 80 ke dashak main aakar tut-bikhar gaya. usee parampara ko phir se jodne kii ek tathakthith sarkari koshish varsh 2006 ke dashahra ke mauqe par hui lekin naakaam rahi.
 janata kaa mukhya bhojan bhaat-daal-roti-tarkari-achaar he. sarson kaa tel parmparik rup se khaanaa taiyaar karne main prayukt hota he. khichdi, joki chaaval tathaa daalon se saath kuch masaalon ko milakar pakaaya jaataa he, bhi bhojya vyanjanon main kaaphi lokapriy he. khichdi, praayah shanivaar ko, dahi, papad, ghi, achaar tathaa chokha ke saath-saath parosa jaataa he. bihar ke khaadya padaarthon main litti evam bangan evam tamaato va aalu ke saath mila kar banaaya gaya chokha bahut hi pramukh he. vibhinn prakaar ke sattuon kaa prayog is sthaan kii visheshta he.
 patna ko kendriya bihar ke mishthannon tathaa mithe pakwaanon ke liye bhi janaa jaataa he. inamen khaja, maave kaa laddu,motichur ke laddu, kaala jamun, kesaria peda, paraval kii mithai, khubi kii laai or chanaa marki evam thekua aadi kaa naam liya ja saktaa he. in pakwaano kaa mul inke sambandhit shahar hai jo ki patna ke nikat hai, jaise ki silao kaa khaja, barh kaa maave kaa laai,maner kaa laddu, vikram kaa kaala jamun, gaya kaa kesaria peda, bakhtiyarpur kaa khubi kii laai kaa chanaa marki, bihiya kii puri ityadi ullekhneey he. halavaiyon ke vanshaj, patna ke nagareey kshetra main badi sankhya main bas gaye is kaaran se yahaan nagar main hi achche pakwaan tathaa mithaiyaan uplabdh ho jate hai. bengali mithaiyon se, joki praayah chaashni main duube rahete hai, bhinn yahaan ke pakwaan praayah sookhe rahete hai.

 rail parivahan 
 bhartiya rail ke nakshe par patna ek mahatvapurn jankshan he. bhartiya rail dwaara rashtriya rajdhani dilli ke atirikt yahan se mumbai, chennai, kolkata, ahmedabad, jammu, amritsar, guwahati tathaa anya mahatvapurn shaharon ke liye sidhi trainen uplabdh he. patna desh ke anya sabhi mahatvapurn shaharon se railmarg dwaara judaa he. patna se jane vaale railway maarg hai- patna-mokama, patna-mughalsarai' tathaa patna-gaya. yah purv railway ke dilli-haavda mukhya maarg par sthit he.
