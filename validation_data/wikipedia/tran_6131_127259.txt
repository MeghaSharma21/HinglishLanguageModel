 raav maldev jodhpur ke shaasha raav ganga rathod ke putr the. inkaa janm visan 1568 posh badi 1 ko hua tha. pita kii mrityu ke pashchaat visan 1588 main sojat main marwar kii gaddi par baithe. us samay inkaa shashan keval sojat or jodhpur ke parganon par hi tha. jaitaaran, pokaran, falaudi, barmer, kotda, khed, maheva, sivana, medta aadi ke sardaar aavashkatanusaar jodhpur naresh ko keval sainik sahaayta diya karte the. parantu anya sab prakaar se ve apane-apane adhikrut pradeshon ke swatantra shaasha the.marwar ke itihaas main raav maldev kaa rajyakaal marwar kaa "shaury yug" kehalaata he. raav maldev apane yug kaa mahan yoddha, mahan vijeta or vishaal samrajya kaa swami tha. usne apane baahubal se ek vishaal samrajya sthapit kiya. maldev ne sivana jaitamalot rathodon se, chauhaton, parkar or khabad parmaron se, raipur or bhadrajun sidhalon se, jalaur bihari pathaanon se, malani mahechon se, medta viramdev se, nagaur, sobhar, deedwanaa musalamaanon se, ajmer saanchor chauhanon se chin kar jodhpur-marwar rajya main milaayaa. is prakaar raav maldev ne visan 1600 tak apane samrajya kaa atyadhik vistaar kar liya. uttar main bikaner va hisar, purv main bayana va dhaulpur tak evam dakshin-pashchim main raghanpur va khabad tak uski rajya seemaa pahunch gai thi. pashchim main bhaatiyon ke pradesh ko uski seemayen chhu rahi thi. itnaa vishaal samrajya na to raav maldev se purv or na hi uske baad hi kisi rajput shaasha ne sthapit kiya.
 jaisalmer ke lunkaran kii putree umade kaa vivaah 1536 i. main jodhpur ke raav maldev ke saath hua, vivaah ke uparaant raav maldev va umade main anban ho jane ke kaaran rajasthan ke itihaas main rani umade ko ruthi rani ke naam se janaa jaataa he, rani ne apana sampoorn samay taragarh durg main vyateet kar diya | 
 raav maldev vir the lekin ve ziddee bhi the. zyaadaatar kshetra inhone apane hi samanton se chhina tha. visan 1591 main medta par akraman kar medta raav viramdev se chin liya. us samay ajmer par raav viramdev kaa adhikaar tha, vahaan bhi raav maldev ne adhikaar kar liya. viramdev idhar-udhar bhatakane ke baad dilli shershah suri ke paas chalaa gaya. visan 1568 main raav maldev ne bikaner par hamalaa kiya. yuddh main raav jaitasi bikaner kaam aae. bikaner par maldev kaa adhikaar ho gaya. raav jaitasi kaa putr kalyanmal or bhim bhi dilli shershah ke paas chale gaye. raav viramdev or bhim donon milakar shershah ko maldev ke viruddh bhadakaane lage.
 chausa ke yuddh main humayun shershah se parrast ho gaya tha, tab vah marwar main bhi kuch samay rahaa tha. viramdev or raav kalyanmal apana khoyaa hua rajya praapt karne kii aashaa se shershah se sahaayta praapt karne kaa anurodh karne lage. is prakaar apane swajaateey bandhuon ke rajya chin kar raav maldev ne apane patan ke beej boye. veram va kalyanmal ke uksaane par shershah suri apni 80000 sainikon kii ek vishaal sena ke saath maldev par akraman kerane chalaa. maldev mahan vijeta tha parantu usamen kootnithik chal-bal or aitihaasik dur drushti kaa abhaav tha. shershah main kootnithik chal-bal or samrik kaushal adhik tha.
 donon senaaon ne sumail-girari main aamane-saamne padaav daalaa. raav maldev bhi apni vishaal sena ke saath tha. rajputon ke shaury kii gaathaon se or apane sammukh maldev kaa vishaal sainya samooh dekhakar shershah ne bhaybheet hokar lautne kaa nishchay kiya. rojaanaa donon senaaon main chhut-put ladaai hoti, jisamen shatruon kaa nuksaan adhik hota. isi samay medta ke viramdev ne chhalkapat kii niti apanaai. maldev ke saradaaron main firoz shahi moharen bhijwai or nai dhaalon ke andar shershah ke farmaanon ko silava diya tha. unamem likhaa tha ki sardaar raav maldev ko bandi banaakar shershah ko saunpe denge. ye jaali patr chaalaaki se raav maldev ke paas pahunchaa die the. isse raav maldev ko apane saradaaron par sandeh ho gaya. yadhyapi saradaaron ne har tarah se apane swami kii shankaa kaa samaadhaan kurne kii cheshtaa kii, tathaapi unka sandeh nivrutt na ho sakaa or vah raatri main yuddh shivi se waapas laut gaya. marwar kii sena bikhar gai. yah dekh jaita, kumpa aadi saradaaron ne peeche hatane se inkaar kar diya. yah dekh jaita, kumpa ne raatri main apane 12000 savaaron ke saath samar ke liye prayaag kiya. andhkaar kii vajah se bahut se sainik rasta bhatak gaye. subah jaldi hi shershah kii sena par achaanak dhaava bol diya, us samay inke saath keval 6000 sainik hi the. saare-ke-saare rajput apane desh or maan kii raksha ke liye sammukh ran se jujhakar kar mar mite. is yuddh main rathod jaita, kumpa panchayan karamsot, sonagar akhairaj aadi marwar ke pramukh vir kaam aae yuddh main vijay shershah kii hui. jab yah samvaad shershah ko mila to usne kaha “khuda kaa shukr he ki kisi tarah fatah hasil ho gai, varanaa maine ek mutti bajre ke liye hindustan kii badshahath hi kho di hoti.’ yah raktranjit yuddh visan1600 posh sud 11 ko samaapt hua.
 shershah kaa jodhpur par shashan ho gaya. raav maldev piplod ke pahaado main chalaa gaya. shershah suri kii mrityu ho jane ke kaaran raav maldev ne visan 1603 main jodhpur par dubaara adhikaar kar liya. dhire-dhire punah marwar ke kshetron ko dubaara jeet liya gaya. raav jaimal se dubaara madta chin liya tha. visan 1619 karthik sudi 12 ko raav maldev kaa swargvaas hua. inhone apane rajyakaal main kul 52 yuddh kiye. ek samay inkaa chote bade 58 parganon par adhikaar rahaa. farsi itihaas lekhakon ne raav maldev ko 'hindustan kaa sarvaadhik samarth raja' kaha he.
