 kongka darra yaa kongka laa bharat ke jammu va kashmir rajya ke laddakh kshetra main sthit ek pahari darra he. yah himalay kii chang-chemno shrunkhlaa main sthit he. bharat ke anusaar yah purnat: bharat kii bhoomi par he lekin china-dwaara niyantrit aksai chin kshetra or laddakh ke anya bhaag ke bich main china-bharat vaastavik niyantran rekha par hone ke naate yah in do deshon ke niyantrit kshetron ke bich aata he. prashasnic rup se darre-paar kaa kshetra chini sarkaar ne shinjiyang praant ke khotaan vibhaag me vilay kiya hua he.
 dhyaan den ki 'laa' shabd tibeti bhashaa main 'darre' kaa arth rakhata he. qaayade se is darre ko 'kongka darra' yaa 'kongka laa' kahana chaiye. ise 'kongka laa darra' kahana 'darre' shabd ko dohraane jaisaa he.
 oktober 1959 main, 1962 ke bharat-china yuddh se kuch varsh purv, ek bhartiya police daste par kongka laa kshetra main maujuud chini sainikon ne hamalaa bol diya jisamen kai bhartiya pulisavaalon kii mrityu ho gai. bhartiya hawaale se yah kshetra bharat kii seemaa ke lagbhag 64 kimi bheetar tha or is se bharat-chini tanaav main bahut badhautari hui.
