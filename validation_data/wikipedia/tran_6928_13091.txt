 hartles shekhar suman dwaara nirdeshit ek hindi film he. isamen adhyayan suman, ariana ayam, shekhar suman, dipti naval, om puri or madan puri ne abhinay kiya he. yah film 7 farvari 2014 ko jari kii gai. film chikitsakeey paddati anaesthesia awarenes par kendrit he. sangyaaharan jaagrukta ek prakaar kii sthiti he jisamen bimaar apane sharir ke angon ko idhar-udhar nahin kar saktaa lekin apane aas-paas kii ghatnaon ko samaj saktaa/sakati he.
 aditya ek bijnesaman he. uski maan gayatri use behad pyaar karti he. aditya kaa dil transplant hona he, par apni maa ke baar-baar kehane par bhi vah is transplantesan ke liye raji nahin hota. aditya apane bijness ke silsile main dubai jaataa he, jahaan uski mulaakaat ek hotel kii resepshanist riya se hoti he. donon ke bich nazdikiyaan badhane lagati hai, lekin ek din achaanak aditya kii tabiyat kharaab ho jaati he. use turant heart transplant karana he. iske liye vah apane nami sajarn dost kii madad letaa he, lekin tabhi achaanak kuch aisa hota he ki aditya kii jindagi main bhuuchaal aa jaataa he. baadame use pataa chalata he ki ye usee ke dost sarjan kii kartut he jo vah inshurans kii rakam ke liye kar rahaa hota he. usame riyabhi shaamil hoti he.
