  shiv kumaar bhartiya film abhinayta the jo brajawood ke janak the. unhone ne hi brajabhasha kii sarvapratham film braj bhoomi banaai thi. iske alaava unhone lalluram bhi banaai thi.shiv kumaar kaa janm bambirpur gram, main hua tha. unka hindi chalchitr jagat main pravesh nirdeshak kishor sahu kii film punam kii raat se hua.
 2 sitambar 2018 ko kumaar kaa aligarh ke ek nijee aspataal main nidhan ho gaya.
 chalchitr jagat ke alaava chote parade par inke yogadan main mashaal, tara, peediyaan, aadi hai.
