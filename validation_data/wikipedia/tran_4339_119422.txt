  jalpaiguni bhartiya rajya pashchim bengal kaa ek prashaskeey jila he. yah pashchim bengal ke uttar main sthit he or is jile ke antargat jaladapada rashtriya uddhaa aata he. newjalpaigudi uttar bengal kii sabase pramukh railway stesan he va uttar purvi seemaant railway kii ek mandleey railway stesan he jo isi jile ke adheen aata he.bharat kii kuch pramukh railgadiyaan yahaan se hokar hi assam ke liye jaati he. kuch pramukh railgadiyaan is prakaar haverajdhani express shatabdi express kamrup express kanchanajangha express kanchanakanya express darjeeling mail ityadi.
