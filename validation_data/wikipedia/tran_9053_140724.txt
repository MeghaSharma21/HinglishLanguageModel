 bhartiya rupaya bharat kii rashtriya mudra he. iskaa bazaar niamak or jarikarta bhartiya reserve bank he. naye pratik chihn ke aane se pehle rupee ko hindi main darshane ke liye 'ru' or angreji main Re., Rs. or Rp. kaa prayog kiya jaataa tha.
 aadhunik bhartiya rupee ko 100 paise main vibhajit kiya gaya he. sikkon kaa mulya 5, 10, 20, 25 or 50 paise he or 1, 2, 5 or 10 rupee bhi. banknote 5, 10, 20, 50, 100, 200, 500, 1000 or 2000 ke mulya par hai.
 bharat ke adhikansh bhaagon main rupee ko in naamon se janaa jaataa he: hindi main rupaya, gujarati main rupio, telugu, tulu bhashaa or kannada main roopaai, tamil main rubaai, malayalam main roopa, marathi main rupee yaa sanskrit se nikle anya shabd jaise roopyakam, roopyakan ityadi. sanskrit main roupya kaa arth hota he chaandi; roopyakan kaa arth hota he chaandi kaa sikaa. haalanki pashchim bengal, tripura, mizoram, urisa or assam main rupee ko aadhikarik rup se sanskrit ke tanak naam se janaa jaataa he. isliye rupee ko bengali main taka, asamiya main toka or oriya main tanka ke naam se janaa jaataa he or roman akshar 'T' se bhartiya bankenoton main darshaya jaataa he.
 bhartiya mudra ke liye ek aadhikarik pratik-chihn dinaank 15 july, 2010 ko chun liya gaya he jise iaiti, guwahati ke professor di. udai kumaar ne dizine kiya he. america dollar, british pound, japani yen or europea sangh ke euro ke baad rupaya paanchavi aisi mudra ban gaya he, jise uske pratik-chihn se pahachaanaa jayega. iske liye ek rashtriya pratiyogita yojit kii gai thi. iske antargat sarkaar ko teen hazaar se adhik aavedan praapt hue the. rigerv bank ke dipti governor kii adhyakshata main gathit ek ucchstareey samiti ne bhartiya samskruti or bhartiya bhashaaon ke saath hi aadhunik yug ke behatar saamanjasy vaale is pratik ko antim taur par chayan karne kii sifarish kii thi. ise unicode maanak main shaamil karne hetu aavedan kar diya gaya he. is chihn ko unicode main U+20A8 par sthaan milegaa, jo pehle hi rupee ke Rs jaise dikhne vaale chihn ke liye aavantit he. philhal is chihn ko computer par mudrit karne ke liye kuch non-unicode font banaaye gayi hai.
 rupaya shabd kaa udgam sanskrit ke shabd rup yaa rupyah main nihit he, jiskaa arth chaandi hota he or roopyakam kaa arth chaandi kaa sikaa he. 
 rupaya shabd kaa prayog sarvapratham sher shah suri ne bharat me apane shashan 1540-1545 ke dauraan kiya tha. sher shah suri ne apane shashan kaal main jo rupaya chalaaya vah ek chaandi kaa sikaa tha jiskaa bhaar 178 grain ke lagbhag tha. usne tambe kaa sikaa jise daam tathaa sone kaa sikaa jise mohar kaha jaataa tha, ko bhi chalaaya. 
 kalantar main mughal shashan ke dauraan puure upmahadweep main maudrik pranali ko sudrudh karne ke liye teenon dhaatuon ke sikkon kaa maanakeekaran kiya gaya. 
 sher shah suri ke shasankaal ke dauraan armbh kiya gaya 'rupaya' aaj tak prachlan main he. bharat main british raaj ke dauraan bhi yah prachlan main rahaa, is dauraan iskaa bhaar 11.66 gram tha or iske bhaar kaa 91.7 pratishat tak shuddh chaandi thi. 19veen shatabdi ke ant main rupaya prathagat british mudra vinimay dar, ke anusaar ek shilling or chaar pence ke baraabar tha vahiin yah ek pound sterling kaa 1/15 bhaag tha. 
 19veen sadi me jab duniya main sabase shashakt arthvyavasthaaen swarna maanak par aadhaarit thim tab chaandi se bane rupee ke mulya main bheeshan giravat ai. sanyukt rajya america or vibhinn europea upniveshon main vishaal maatra main chaandi ke strot milane ke parinamswaroop chaandi kaa mulya sone ke apeksha bahut gir gaya. achaanak bharat kii maanak mudra se ab baahar kii duniya se adhik kharid nahin kii ja sakati thi. is ghatana ko 'rupee kii giravat' ke rup main janaa jaataa he. 
 pehle rupaya 16 aane yaa 64 paise yaa 192 pai main baanta jaataa tha. rupee kaa dashamalvikaran 1957 main bharat me, 1869 main ceylone main or 1961 main pakistan main hua. is prakaar bhartiya rupaya 100 paise main vibhajit ho gaya. bharat main paise ko pehle nayaa paisa naam se janaa jaataa tha. bharat main bhartiya rigerv bank dwaara mudra jari kii jaati he, jabaki pakistan main yah state bank aaf pakistan ke dwaara niyantrit hota he.

 mahatma gandhi ke chitra, rigerv bank kii muhar, guarantee or vachan up-vaakya, baanyi ore ashok stambh pratik, bhartiya rigerv bank ke governor ke hastakshar utkeern rup se mudrit arthath ubhare mudran vaale hote hai jinhen 20 rupe, 50 rupe, 100 rupe 500 rupe or 1000 rupe ke noton main chhukar mahasoos kiya ja saktaa he.
 10/-rupe ke noton ko chhodkar sabhi noton ke watermark vindo kii baanyi ore utkeern rup main ek vishesh vishistataa daali gai he. yah vishistataa vibhinn moolyavirgo ke liye vibhinn aakaaron he or moolyavarg kii pahchaan karne main drishtiheen logon kii sahaayta karata he.
 noton ke sankhya patal ko pratideept syaahi main mudrit kiya gaya he. noton main prakaashiya tantu hai. donon tab dekhe ja sakate hai jab note ko para-bangnee lamp ke saamne laayaa jaataa he.
 yah november 2000 main lagu sanshodhit rang yojana ke saath 500 rupe or 1000 rupe ke noton main shaamil kii gai ek nai suraksha visheshta he. kramshah 1000 rupe or 500 rupe ke aamukh par sankhya 1000 or 500 ko prakaash main parivartaneey syaahi arthath rang badalane vaali syaahi main mudrit kiya gaya he. sankhya 1000 or 500 kaa rang note ko sidhaa dekhne par haraa dikhaai detaa he lekin ise tirachha karne par rang badalakar nila ho jaataa he.
 watermark ke sameep urdhvadhar pattee ke madhya main note ke saamne or prishth bhaag donon ore mudrit ek pushpit swaroop ko donon taraf thik-thik ankit kiya gaya he. is swaroop ko prakaash main dekhne par ek hi pushpit swaroop dikhaai padataa he.
 jaali noton kaa mudran or parichaalan bhartiya dand samhita kii dhaaraon 489e se 489i ke antargat aparaadh he tathaa kisi vidhik nyaayalay dwaara aarthik dand athwa karawas athwa donon ke rup main dandaneey he.
