 samasutri koshika vibhaajan yaa samasutran saadhaaran koshika vibhaajan he. isaki sampoorn prakriya do charnon main puri hoti he, pratham charan main koshika ke kendrak kaa vibhaajan hota he. is prakriya ko kendrak-vibhaajan kehete hai. vibhaajan ke dwitiya charan main koshika-dravya kaa vibhaajan hota he. is prakriya ko koshika-dravya vibhaajan kehete hai. vibhaajan ke ant main maatrukoshika, putree-koshika main badal jaati he.
 koshika ke prayek vibhaajan ke purv uske cendrak kaa vibhaajan hota he. centrekavibhaajan reeti ke anusaar honevaali sutathy ghatana he, jise kai avasthaaon main vibhajit kiya ja saktaa he. ye avasthaaen nimnlikhit hai : 
  purvavastha, 
  madhyavastha, 
  pashchaavastha tathaa 
  antyavastha 
 isamen cendrak ke bheetar patale patale sutara dikhaai padate hai, jinako cendrakasutra kehete hai. ye cendrakasutra kramash: sarpelikaran ke kaaran chote or mote ho jate hai. madhyavastha aate samay tak ye purvavastha kii apeksha kai gune chote or mote ho jate hai. madhyavastha aane tak koshika ke bheetar kuch or mahatvapurn parivartan hote hai. cendrak kaa aavaran nasht ho jaataa he or uski jagah ek tarkuvat upkaran utpann hota he. adhikansh praaniyon kii un koshikaon main, jinamein vibhaajan kii kshamta bani rahati he, ek vishesh upkaran hota he jis centrosom kehete hai or jiske madhya main ek kanika hoti hai, jise tarakendre kehete hai.
 purvavastha main hi tarakendre kaa vibhaajan ho jaataa he or ek se do tarakendre ek duusare ko pratikarshith karte hai. iske kaaran ye ek duusare se dur hote jate hai or centrosom do bhaagon main vibhajit ho jaataa he. donon centrosom ek duusare se adhik se adhik duuri par vyasabhimukh sthapit ho jate hai. prayek centrosom ke chaaron ore koshikadravya kii patali patali rekhaayen ban jaati hai, jinako tarakiran kehete hai. donon ore se tarakirane aakar centrekavaran par aghat karti hai. isak samay tak purvavastha apni parisamapti tak pahunch jati he or, jaisaa upar kaha ja chukaa he, centrekavaran nasht ho jaataa he. ab ek centrosom se lekar duusare tak tarku kaa prasar hota he. centrosom or uski tarakiran ko centre kehete hai. tarku do prakaar ke tarkutantuon kaa bana hota he. ek to ve tantu hote hai jo ek centre se duusare centre tak faile hote hai or jinako satat tantu kehete hai. duusare ve tantu hote hai, jinka ek sira kisi cendrakasutra se sata hota he or doosraa donon main se kisi ek centre se.
 madhyavastha par cendrakasutra tarku kii madhyarekha ke samtal par ekatrit ho jate hai. is samtal ko madhyavastha falak kehete hai. madhyavastha main prayek cendrakasutra avibhajit hi prathit hota he parantu isamen andeh nahin ki is avasthaa ke bahut pehle se hi prayek cendrakasutra do bhaagon main vibhajit rahata he. vastut: vibhaajan kii kriya ke purv hi antaraal avasthaa main cendrak main prayek cendrakasutra apane sadrush ek doosraa pratiwalit bana letaa he or ye donon sutara ek duusare ke itane sameep hote hai ki dekhne main ek hi gyaat hote hai. prayek cendrakasutra main ek vishesh sthaan hota he jahaan tarku kaa cendrakasutreey tantu judaa hota he! isko sentonmir kehete hai. kisi kisi jantu main cendrakasutra madhyavastha falak ke bahya bhaag main hi paae jate hai, parantu anya jantuon main bahya bhaag or aantarik bhaag donon main paae jate hai. 
 pashchaavastha main prayek cendrakasutra ke donon bhaag ek duusare se pruthak hone lagate hai or is avasthaa ke ant kaal tak abhimukhakendre tak pahunch jate hai.
 iske pashchaat antyavastha aarambh hoti he. is avasthaa main cendrakasutron ke donon samoohon or kendron ke chaaron ore kendravaran utapann hote hai. is prakaar ek cendrak se do cendrak utpann hote hai.
 koshika vibhaajan kii is avasthaa main koshikadravya ke vibhaajan se ek matra koshika se do santati koshikaon kaa nirmaan ho jaataa he. jantuon me koshikadravya kaa vibhaajan plasma jhilli main khaanch ke dwaara tathaa padpo main fregamoplast ke dwaara hota he.
 pranijivan tathaa praniprajnan ke hetu cendrak sutron kaa bada mahatv hai, kyonki ye anuvanshik padaarth se bane hote hai. bhinn bhinn jati ke jantuon kii koshikaon main cendrakasutra bhinn bhinn sankhyaon main paae jate hai, parantu kisi bhi ek jati ke liye cendrakasutron kii sankhya niyat hoti he or saadhaaran avasthaa main is sankhya main koi vibhinnata nahin hoti, jaise manushya ke sharir kii prayek koshika main 46 cendrakasutra hote hai.
