 naga parvat shrunkhlaa, jinhen naga pahadiyaan bhi kehete hai, bharat or burma myanmar kii seemaa par lagane vaali parvat shrunkhlaa he. isaki oonchaai lagbhag 3,825 meter he. yah jatil pahaad pranali kaa ek bhaag he jisamen se kuch bhaag bhartiya rajya nagaland main tathaa burma main aate hai.
 britani rajya ke dauraan inamen se adhiktar pahari kshetra naga hills jile ke antargata aata thi.
 shabd "naga", naga logon ke liye kaam main liya jaataa he jise burmi bhashaa main "naga" athwa "naakaa" kaha jaataa he or iskaa arth "chhede huye kaan vaale logon" ke saath joda jaataa he.
 britani bharat main naga parvat shrunkhlaa kaa ek bhaag 1866 main ek gilen main kar diya gaya. naga hills jile kii simaayen dhire-dhire badhati chali gayi or isamen or adhik naga prajatiyon ko shaamil kiya gaya jaise ayos athav ao naga, semas or konyak . san 1912 main is jile ko assam praant kaa jila ghoshit kar diya gaya. bharat ke vibhaajan ke samay iskaa twainsang jile main vilay kar diya gaya jisase 1963 main nagaland rajya nirmit kiya gaya.
