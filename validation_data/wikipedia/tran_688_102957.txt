 rambha yaa crobaar ek haath kaa auzaar he jo dhansi hui keel aadi nikaalane ke kaam aata he. rambha majboot lohe kaa banahua hota he. lakdi ke baxon main lagi keelon ko nikaalkar us bakse ko dhahaane ke liye evam anya isi tarah ke kaaryon ke liye iskaa upyog hota he. isamen kisi dhaatu kii ek chad ke sire par vakrakaar sira hota he jo keel ke sir ko pakadaney ke hisaab se banaaya gaya hota he.
 angreji main ise crobaar shaayad isaliye kaha jaataa he kyonki iskaa aakaar kauwe ke pair yaa chonch se miltaa-julata he.
