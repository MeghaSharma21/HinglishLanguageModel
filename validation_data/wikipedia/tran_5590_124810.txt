 roganuo or sookshmaanuon ko maar daalane ke liye nissankramakaon kaa prayog hota he. isse sankramak rogon ko rokane main sahaayta milti he. inhen '	visankramak', 'visankrami' yaa 'sankramanhari' bhi kehete hai. 
 sarvotkrisht nissankramak to surya kii kirane he, jinka vyavhaar pracheen kaal se iske liye hota aa rahaa he. ab kuch anya kritrim padaarth bhi prayukt hote hai. aise padaatho ko teen virgo me vibhakt kiya ja saktaa he.
 pehle varg ke padaarthon main formeldihide, salfuras amla, kapur or kuch vashpashil tel aate hai. fomaldyhide sarvotkrisht nissankramak hai, yah manushya ke liye vishaiylla nahin hota, yadhyapi aankhon or gale ke liye kshobhakari hota he. yah rogaanuon ko badi jaldi nasht kar detaa he. yadhyapi pidak jantuo ke vinaash ke liye yah utana prabhavakaari nahin he jitna salfuras amla. 
 duusare varg ke padaarthon me anek oxykaarak, jaise potash paramanganet, lavan, chuunaa, soda or potash ke chloride, sulphate yaa sulphite tathaa aluminium or jaste ke chloride ityadi, alakatre ke utpaada phenol, krisol criosot salicylic amla aadi aate he.
 tisare varg ke sadhano main ushma or sheet he. sheet saadharantaya prapya nahin hai. ushma saralata se prapya he. ooshmaa se pahanane ke kapado, shayyaon tathaa soot ke anya vastro kaa nissankraman hota he. ushma dabaav vaali bhaap se praapt hoti he. iske liye bhaap kaa taap kuch samay ke liye lagbhag 250Â° sein. rahanaa aavashyak hota he.
