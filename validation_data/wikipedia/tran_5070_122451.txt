 redcliff rekha 17 agast 1947 ko bharat vibhaajan ke baad bharat or pakistan ke bich seemaa ban gai. sar siril redcliff kii adhyakshata main seemaa ayog dwaara rekha kaa nirdhaaran kiya gaya, jo 88 karod logon ke bich 175,000 varg mile kshetra ko nyyochit rup se vibhajit karne ke liye adhikrut the.
 15 july 1947 ko, british sansad ke bhartiya swatantraaa adhinium 1947 ne nirdhaarit kiya ki bharat main british raaj bas ek maheene baad 15 agast 1947 ko samaapt hoga. usamen bharat kaa do prabhusatta-sampann swatantra upnivesh ke rup main vibhaajan bhi niyat kiya gaya: bharat sangh or british bharat main musalamaanon kii mathrubhumi ke rup main swatantra upnivesh pakistan.
 pakistan musalamaanon kii mathrubhumi ke rup main abhipret tha, jabaki bharat hindu bahumat ke saath dharmanirpeksh tha. uttar main musalmaan bahul kshetra pakistan banane vaale the. baluchistan ke praant or sindh puri tarah pakistan ko saunp die gaye. baharhaal, do praanton main ekasmaan bahumat nahin tha - uttar purv main bengal or uttar pashchim main punjab) . punjab kaa pashchimi bhaag pashchim pakistan kaa ansh bana or purvi bhaag bharat kaa punjab rajya ban gaya. isi prakaar bengal kaa vibhaajan purvi bengal or pashchimi bengal main kiya gaya. azaadi ke baad, uttar-pashchim seemaant praant ne janamat sangrah ke zarie pakistan main shaamil hone kaa chayan kiya.
 punjab kii jansankhya kaa vitaran kuch aisa tha jisamen aisi koi rekha nahin thi jise karine se hindu, musalmaan or sikhon main vibhajit kiya ja sake. isi tarah, koi rekha jinna ke netritv main muslim lig ko, evam nehru tathaa patel ke netritv main bhartiya rashtriya congress or saath hi saath british ko santusht nahin kar saki. iske alaava, kisi bhi vibhaajan ke liye "sadak or rail sanchaar, sinchai yojanaayen, vidyut pranaliyaan or jot kshetron ko kaatna" aprihaary tha. tathaapi, ek bhalee prakaar khinchi gai rekha se kisaanon ko apane kheton se alag karne ke maamalon ko kam kiya ja saktaa tha or lakhon logon ko sthanantarit karne se bachaaya ja saktaa tha.
 jaisaa ki ghatit hua "samgr up-mahadweep main, kuch 14 karod logon ne apane ghar chhod die or har sambhav saadhan dwaara - hawaii, rail or sadakon ke madhyam se, kaaron or lauriyon main, bason or baiylgaadiyon main, par adhikanshtah paidal - apane jaise logon ke paas sharan paane ke liye nikal pade". unamem se kai logon ko virodhiyon ne maar daalaa, kuch logon kii bhukh yaa thakaan ke kaaran mrityu ho gai, jabaki anya log "pechish, haija or har jagah kuposhit sharnaarthiyon ko peedit karne vaale sabhi anya rogon se" prabhavit hue. marane waalon kii sankhya 200,000 or do karod ke bich hone kaa anumaan he, jahaan lagbhag ek karod hataahat hone ke bare main aam sahmati bani thi.
 farvari 1947 main vysarai ke rup main lord louis mountbayton ke pratisthaapan se pehle bharat ke vysarai lord vevell dwaara pehle se hi ek kachi seemaa rekha khinchi gai thi. sahi taur par yah nirdhaarit karne ke liye ki kis desh ko koun se pradesh abantit kiye jaaen, june 1947 main, britain ne do seemaa aayogon kii adhyakshata ke liye - ek bengal or ek punjab ke liye - sar siril redcliff ko niyukt kiya.
 ayog ko nirdesh die gaye ki "musalamaanon or gair musalamaanon ke bahumat vaale samipsth kshetron kii jaanch ke aadhaar par, punjab ke do hisson kii simaaon kii hadabandi karen. aisa karte samay, ve anya karkon ko bhi hisaab main len." anya kaarak aparibhaashit the, jisane redcliff ko chhot di, lekin inamen "prakritik simaaen, sanchaar, jalpravaah or sinchai pranaaliyon" ke bare main faisale or saath hi saamaajik or raajnneetik vichaar shaamil the. prayek ayog main chaar pratinidhi the - 2 bhartiya rashtriya congress se or 2 muslim lig se. donon pakshon ke hiton ke bich gatirodh ko dekhte hue or unke vidweshpurn sambandhon ke kaaran, antim nirnay anivarytah redcliff kaa tha.
 8 july ko bharat main pahunchane ke baad, redcliff ko seemaa ke bare main faisalaa karne ke liye sirf 5 saptaah die gaye. jald hi unhone apane college ke bhootpurv chaatr mountbeton ke saath mulaaqaat kii or ayog ke sadasyon, visheshtah congress se nehru or muslim lig ke adhyaksh jinna se milane ke liye lahore or kalakatta kii yatra kii. unhone nirdhaarit kam samay ke liye aapatti jataai, lekin sabhi dalon kaa yah aagrah tha ki 15 agast ko bharat se british vaapasi ke saath hi rekha khinch di jaae. mountbeton ne sheeghra samay-seemaa kii shart par vysarai kaa pad sweekaar kiya tha. vaapasi se thik kuch din pehle hi nirnay sampann hua, lekin raajnneetik chalbaasiyon ke kaaran, bharat or pakistan ko azaadi manzur karne ke do din baad, yani 17 agast tak ise prakashit nahin kiya gaya.
 vyavsaay se vaqeel rahane vaale, redcliff or anya ayuktaon ko paas sabhi chamak-damak thi lekin is vishesh kaary ke liye aavashyak gyaan nadaarad tha. unke paas seemaa khinchne ke liye zaruri susthaapit prakriya or jaankaree suchit karne ke liye koi salaahkaar nahin the. or naa hi surveykshan or kshetreey jaankaree ekatrit karne kaa samay tha. deree se bachane ke liye, sanyukt raashtra jaise kuch visheshgy or salaahkaaron kii jaanboojh kar anupasthiti thi. britain kii nai lebar sarkaar "jo yudghakaalin baari karz main dubi hui thi, bas apane teji se asthir hote samrajya ko or adhik bardaasht nahin kar paa rahi thi." "bahari pratibhaagiyon — udhaaharan ke liye, sanyukt raashtra — kii anupasthiti ne bhi british sarkaar dwaara laaj bachaane kii apni tatpar ichha ko santusht kiya ki use apane hi samrajya main—shashan chalaane—yaa shashan samaapt karne ke liye bahya madad chahie."
 bhartiya rashtriya congress or muslim lig ke netaaon ko diya gaya baraabar kaa pratinidhitiv gatirodh paidaa karne ke bajaay, santulan pradaan karata hua prathit hua. rishte itane vivaadaaspad ho gaye the ki nyaayadhish "mushkil se ek duusare kii baat ko sahan kar pate the" or kaarysoochiyon main itnaa antar tha ki charchaa kaa koi matlab nahin rah jaataa. yahaan tak ki "kuch hafte pehle lahore ke sikh nyaayadhish kii patni or do bacchon kii rawalpindi main musalamaanon dwaara hatya kar di gai thi."
 vastutah, seemaa-rekha ke galat ore main hinduon or musalamaanon kii sankhya ko kam karnaa hi santulan kii ekmaatr chintaa nahin thi. punjab seemaa ayog ko sikh samudaay ke kshetreey ghar ke bich se rekha khinchni thi. lord isle british ke prati udhaas the ki samudaay kaa adhik lihaaj na kiya gaya, jo unke shabdon main pratham vishwa yuddh ke dauraan samrajya kii seva main "bhartiya sena main kai hazaar shandaar rangaruton ko bhartee kiya tha." baharhaal, sikh kisi bhi aise samaadhaan ke prati apane virodh main ugr the jo unke samudaay ko muslim shasit rajya main rakhe. iske alaava, kai logon ne apane swayan ke samprabhu rajya kaa aagrah kiya, jis par koi sahamat nahin hota.
 sabase ant main, aise samudaay the jinka koi pratinidhitiv nahin tha. bengal seemaa ayog ke pratinidhiyon ko mukhyatah is sawaal par chintaa thi ki kalakatta kise milegaa. bengal main chatgaanv pahari ilaaqon ke buddha janajatiyon kaa koi aadhikarik pratinidhitiv nahin tha or vibhaajan ke do dinon baad tak apni sthiti se jujhane ke liye koi jaankaree unke paas nahin thi.
 paristhiti ko duhsadhay or atyaavashyak maanate hue, redcliff ne sabhi mushkil nirnay swayan liye. yah shuruuaat se hi asambhav tha, lekin lagata he redcliff ko khud par koi andeh nahin tha or halaaton ko badalane ke liye koi aadhikarik shikaayat yaa prastaav nahin uthaaya.
 apni niyukti se pehle, redcliff ne kabhi bharat kaa daura nahin kiya tha or vahaan kisi ko nahin jaanate the. british or jhagadne vaale rajanetaaon ke liye, yah kami faaydemand nazar ai; britain ke atirikt, unhen kisi bhi dal ke prati nishpaksh mana gaya. keval unke nijee sachiv, christopher bumont, punjab ke prashasan or jivan se parichit the. nishpakshta ke pradarshan ko banaae rakhane ke liye, redcliff ne vysarai mountbeton se bhi apni duuri banaae rakhi.
 gyaan kii koi maatra aisi rekha banane main safal nahin ho saki jo takaraav hone se bachaae; pehle se hi, "punjab or bengal ke sampradayik dangon ne british kii twarit or sammanjanak vaapasi kii ummid ko feekaa kar diya tha". "dakshin asia ke kai upniveshottar vikaron ke beej, pratyaksh or paroksh british shashan kii do sadiyon main, bahut pehle hi boe gaye the, lekin, jaisaa ki har pustak ne darshaya he, vibhaajan kii jatil traasadee kaa koi bhi ansh aprihaary nahin tha."
 agar ayog or adhik saavdhaan hota, vibhaajan main baari bhuulon se bachaa ja saktaa tha. udhaaharan ke liye, aisi kai ghatnaayen hai jahaan seemaa-rekha is prakaar khinchi gai ki gaanv kaa kuch bhaag bharat main or kuch bhaag pakistan main rah gaya. chunki redcliff ke paas sirf ek maheene kaa samay tha, unhone gaanvon kii simaaon ke maamle main saavdhaanee baratane ko nazarandaaz kiya. unki seemaa ghani aabaadi vaale do pradeshon ke bich se jane ke bajaay, thik unke bichon bich se hokar guzari. aise kai udhaaharan hai jahaan vibhaajan-rekha ek ghar ke bich se guzari jiske kuch kamare ek desh main or duusare anya desh main rah gaye.
 redcliff ne aise laaparvaahee se kiye gaye vibhaajan ko ghisi-pity baat se jaays tehraaya ki bhale hi ve kuch bhi karte, logon ko to bhugatna hi padataa. is auchity ke peeche kii soch kabhi prakat nahin hogi kyonki redcliff ne "apane bharat chhodane se pehle sabhi kaagazat nasht kar die". ve swatantraaa diwas par hi chale gaye, isse pehle ki seemaa adhinirnay vitarit kiye jate. apni hi sweekaarokti main, redcliff bhartiya jalwaayu ke prati apane swasthya kii kami or bharat chhodane kii utsukata se vishesh taur par prabhavit the.
 karyanvayan seemaa-rekha khinchne kii prakriya se kuch kam tej nahin thi. 17 agast ko redcliff adhinirnay prakashit hone se pehle, bhartiya or pakistani pratinidhiyon ko 16 agast 1947 shaam 5:00 baje, pratiyon ke adhyayan ke liye do ghanton kaa samay diya gaya.
 vivaadon or deree se bachane ke liye vibhaajan gupt rup se kiya gaya tha. antim adhinirnay 9 agast or 12 agast ko taiyaar the, lekin vibhaajan ke do din baad tak prakashit nahin kiye gaye.
 read ke anusaar, kuch paristhitijanya saboot maujuud hai ki nehru or patel ko punjab adhinirnay kii vishaywastu ke bare main agast 9 yaa 10 ko, sambhawatah mountbeton ke madhyam se yaa redcliff ke bhartiya sahaayak sachiv ke zarie gopneey rup se jaankaree di gai thi. yah kaise ghatit hua bina iskaa lihaaj kiye, adhinirnay main satluj nahar ke mukhya purvi bhaag ko pakistan ke bajaay bharat ke adhikaar-kshetra main daalane ke liye parivartan kiya gaya. is kshetra main paanch lakh kii sanyukt jansankhya ke saath do muslim bahul tahseelein thim. is parivartan ke liye do spasht kaaran the: is kshetra main sena ke hathiyaaron kaa depo maujuud tha or isamen bharat ke hisse main aane vaale rajasi pradesh bikaner ko sinchit karne vaali nahar kaa nadishirsh tha.
 isi tarah, yah gyaat nahin he ki kaise redcliff ko chatgaanv pahari ilaaqon ko pakistan ko saunpane ke liye raji kiya gaya. yah patel or nehru ke liye ek jhatka tha jo yah maan rahe the ki ye kshetra bharat ko saunp die jaaenge kyonki vahaan basane vaale 98% log gair muslim the. isi tarah, bengal main muslim bahul jile murshidaabad or malda ke nirnay ko bhi gupt rakhaa gaya jahaan ke nivaasiyon ne 17 agast 1947 ko adhinirnay ke saarvajanik prakaashan tak pakistan kaa jhanda fahraaya.
 ye nirnay kaise liye gaye is baat kii sachai kabhi gyaat nahin hogi, kyonki redcliff ne saare abhilekh nasht kar die or mountbeton ne spasht rup se kisi vishesh jaankaree yaa pakshpaat se inkaar kar diya.
 vibhaajan ke baad bharat or pakistan kii nai naveli sarkaron par seemaa lagu karne kii sari jimmedaari chhod di gai. agast main lahore kaa daura karne ke baad, vysarai mountbeton ne jaldi se lahore ke aas-paas shanti banaae rakhane ke liye punjab seemaa bal kii vyavastha kii, lekin hazaron logon kii hatya ko rokane ke liye 50,000 log kaafii nahin the, jinamein 77% gramin kshetra se the. kshetra ke aakaar ko dekhte hue, 1 se kam prati varg mile sainik bal kaa hisaab baithta tha. yah shaharon kii suraksha ke liye paryaapt nahin tha or yahi baat aage pakistan kahalaane vaale pradeshon main apane gharon ko chhod kar jane vaale hazaron-lakhon sharnaarthiyon ke kaaravaan ke liye sateek baithti he.
 bharat or pakistan, donon hi, khinchi gai seemaa ke galat ore rahane vaale gaanvon main vidroh kaa samarthan karke samjhaute kaa ulanghan karne ke anicchhuk the, kyonki isse antarrashtriya manch par shame se munh chhipaana padataa or british yaa sanyukt raashtra ke hastkshep kii aavashyaktaa paidaa ho sakati thi. antat, seemaa sangharshon kii vajah se 1947, 1965 or 1971 main teen yuddh hue, saath hi may 1998 main dohare parmaanu hathiyaaron ke pareekshan or 1999 kaa kargil sangharsh saamne aayaa.
 redcliff rekha ke sambandh main do pramukh vivaad maujuud the, chatgaanv pahari ilaake or gurdaspur jila. bengal ke malda, khulanaa or murshidaabad tathaa assam ke up-prabhag karimganj ke bare main kuch chhitput vivaad saamne aae.
 chatgaanv pahari ilaakon main 97% tak bahusankhyak gair-musalamaanon kii aabaadi thi, lekin yah pakistan ko saunp diya gaya. chatgaanv hill trects piples association ne bengal seemaa ayog ke samaksh yachika daayar kii, kyonki chatgaanv main adhikash gair musalmaan based the, ve bharat main rahanaa chaahate the. kyonki unka kisi ne aadhikarik pratinidhitiv nahin kiya tha, is maamle par koi aadhikarik charchaa nahin hui or bharat kii ore kai logon kaa maananaa tha ki CHT bharat ko saunp diya jayega.
 15 agast 1947 ko, kai janajatiyon ko pataa nahin tha ki unka sambandh seemaa ke kis ore ke desh se hai. 17 agast ko redcliff adhinirnay ke prakaashan ke baad CHT ko pakistan main daal diya gaya. pakistan ke hisse main chatgaanv pahari ilaako ko daalane kaa tark yah tha ki ve bharat ke liye durgam the or chatgaanv ko kuch ubhaypratirodhi kshetra, pramukh shahar or bandargah uplabdh karaane ke liye aisa kiya gaya; yah bhi tark diya gaya tha ki keval pahunch chatgaanv ke madhyam se tha.
 "do din baad, CHTPA ne adhinirnay kaa paalan na karne kaa sankalp kiya or bhartiya jhanda fahraaya gaya. pakistani sena is virodh se nipati lekin samasya kaa samaadhaan abhi tak nahin hua he."
 up-prabhag shakargarh ke sivaay, gurdaspur jila bharat ko diya gaya. gurdaspur jile main aitihaasik islamic shahar devband, barelvi or ahmadiya samudaay ke saanskrithik centre shaamil the. gurdaspur jile main musalamaanon kaa maamuli bahumat tha kyonki ahmadiya samudaay kii ginati musalamaanon ke rup main kii jaati thi, bhale hi musalmaan maulaviyon ne unhen gair-musalmaan ghoshit kiya tha; is kshetra main badi sankhya main ahmadiya ekatrit ho gaye the kyonki unka dharmik centre kaadiyaan gurdaspur jile main tha. jile main samagratah musalmaan or gair musalamaanon kaa anupaat kramshah 50.6 or 49.4 tha; shakargarh or gurdaspur prayek tehsil main 51% bahumat tha; batala tehsil main 53% bahumat tha; pathankot tehsil main 67% gair musalamaanon kaa bahumat tha. ant main, keval shakargarh tehsil pakistan ko saunpa gaya, jisase shesh jile main gair musalamaanon kaa maamuli bahumat tha.
 yah anumaan lagaaya gaya ki use lord mountbeton ke aagrah par saunpa gaya tha, taaki agar kashmir rajya ke shaasha bhartiya sangh main ekikrut karne kaa faisalaa lete hai, to vah bharat ke liye sameep sulabh ho jayega. maamla chaahe jo bhi ho, pathankot tehsil bharat ko chalaa jaataa or vah purvi punjab ke hoshiyarpur or kangra jilon ke aas-paas ke ilaaqon ke saath sidhe rail maarg se judaa hua tha. ek or baat yah thi ki batala or gurdaspur sikhon ke pavitra shahar amritsar ke liye pratirohak pradaan karte, jo anyathaa pakistan ke muslim ilaake se ghira hota. antim baat mountbeton or anya logon dwaara seemaa ayog ke saamne daleel ke rup main prastut kii gai thi ki agar raavi nadi ke purvi kshetra ko ek khand ke rup main mana jaataa he, jisamen amritsar or gurdaspur jile kaa adhikansh bhaag shaamil he, to isamen gair musalmaan maamuli taur par bahusankhyak honge. iske alaava, aisa karke, bahusankhyak sikh janasamudaay purvi punjab ke hisse main aa jayega; jabaki iske vipareet karne se, ek maamuli bahumat pakistan main chhot jayega or isse sikh sharnaarthiyon kii sankhya bad jaaegi. yah sikhon ko shant karne kaa ek prayaas tha, kyonki unhone pashchimi punjab main kai pramukh ilaaqon ko kho diya tha. badale main redcliff dwaara pakistan ko firozpur or zeera tahseelon ko saunpane kaa prayaas kiya gaya tha. iskaa bikaner ke maharaja dwaara virodh kiya gaya kyonki sutluj or beas ke sangam par harike nadishirsh ferozepur main sthit tha, jahaan se ek nahar kaa udgam hua tha, jo ki unke registan rajya ke liye pani kaa ekmaatr strot tha. unhone jab mountbeton ko dhamkee di ki ve apana rajya pakistan ko saunp denge yadi firozpur pashchim punjab ko diya gaya, to uske baad hi antim kshan main adhinirnay badal diya gaya or puraa firozpur bharat ke saunp diya gaya.
 redcliff dwaara kiye gaye nirnyon main ek or vivaadit nirnay bengal ke malda jile kaa vibhaajan tha. jile main samagratah maamuli muslim bahumat tha, lekin ise vibhajit kiya gaya or malda shahar sahit, iskaa adhikansh hissaa bharat ko mila. 15 agast 1947 ke baad jila 3-4 dinon ke liye purvi pakistan prashasan ke tahat bana rahaa. yah keval jab adhinirnay ko saarvajanik taur par prakat kiya gaya tab malda main pakistani jhande ko hataa kar bhartiya dhvaj fahraaya gaya.
 52% ke maamuli hindu bahumat vaala samgr khulanaa jila, bharat ko milane vaale 70% musalmaan bahusankhyak ke zyaada chote murshidaabad jile ke badale mempurv pakistan ko de diya gaya. 
 janamat sangrah ke anusaar assam kaa silahat jila pakistan main shaamil ho gaya. tathaapi, muslim-bahul up-vibhaag karimganj ko silahat se kaat diya gaya or bharat ko diya gaya. yatha 2001 bhartiya janaganana ke anusaar, karimganj main 52.3% musalamaanon kii bahulata he.
