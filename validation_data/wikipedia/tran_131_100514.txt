 parmaanu urja shikshan sanstha, bharat sarkaar ke parmaanu urja vibhaag kaa swytt nikaay he jo parmaanu urja vibhaag evam uske adheen sanghatak ikaayiyon main karyrat karmchaariyon ke santanon ko barahavin kaksha tak shiksha detaa he. parmaanu urja kendriya vidhyalay tathaa kanishtha mahavidhyalay bharat bhar main faile hue hai. ye vidhyalay kendriya madhyamik shiksha board tathaa anya rajya ke bordon se sambaddh hai. parmaanu urja shikshan sanstha ke tahat is samay 16 vibhinn kendron par 30 vidhyalay / kanishtha mahavidhyalay hai, jinamein lagbhag 28000 vidhyaarthee, 1547 shikshak or 300 gaiyr shikshak karmchaaree karyrat hai. 
 parmaanu urja shikshan sanstha kii sthapana varsh 1969 main kii gai thi. iskaa mukhyalay mumbai main he.
