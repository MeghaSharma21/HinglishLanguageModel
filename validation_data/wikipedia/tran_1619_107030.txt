 saiddhanthik bhautikee main utkram samasya durbal nabhikeey va gurutv balon ke madhya vishaal bhinnata he. bhautik vaigyanik abhi tak isko samajha paane main asamarth hai, jaise - durbal bal, gurutv se 1032 guna prabal kyon hai.
 utkram samasya saamanytah tab aati he jab kuch longrajian ke moolbhoot prachal praayogic rup se maape gayi prachalon se behad alag hon.
 kan bhautikee main sabase mahatvapurn utkram samasya kaa prashn yah he ki durbal bal, gurutvkarshan se 1032 guna prabal kyon he. donon bal prakruti ke niyatanko par aadhaarit hai, durbal bal ke liye fermi niyatank tathaa gurutvkarshan bal ke liye gurutvkarshak sthiraank.
