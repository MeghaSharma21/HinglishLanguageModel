 phiet  ek vaahan nirmaataa, ingine nirmaataa, ek vittiya or audyogic samooh he jo uttari italy ke turine main sthit he. samooh kii sthapana 1899 main niveshkon ke ek samooh dwaara kii gayi jisame jiovani agnelli bhi shaamil the. phiet ne tanks or vimaan kaa nirmaan bhi kiya he.
 phiet kii kaaron kaa utpaadan duniya bhar main hota he or samooh kaa italy ke baahar sabase bada kaarkaana brazil main sthit he jo in kaaron kaa sabase bada upabhokta desh bhi he. iske kaarkaane argentina or poland main bhi hai. phiet kaa apane utpaadon kii licesing anya deshon main karaane kaa ek lanbaa itihaas he bhale hi in deshon kii sthaneey rajnatik yaa saanskrithik sthitiyaan kuch bhi rahi hon. iske sanyukt udhyam france, turki, misr, dakshin africa, bharat or china main karyrat hai.
 jiovani agnelli ke pautra jiani agnelli 1966 se unki mrityu 24 janvari 2003 tak phiet samooh ke adhyaksh rahe. haalaanki 1996 se wo sirf "maanad" adhyaksh the or vaastavik adhyaksh seesaare romiti the. unke hatane ke baad, paolo fresco ko adhyaksh or paolo cantarela ko siio banaaya gaya. 2002 se 2004 ke bich ambarto agnelli ne samooh ke adhyaksh ke rup main kaary kiya. 28 may 2004 ko ambarto agnelli kii mrityu ke baad, luka kordero di montegemelo ko adhyaksh or agnelli, ke vaaris jon alkan ko 28 varsh kii umr main upaadhyaksh pad par namit kiya gaya. aajkal samooh ke siio sarjio marchione he jinhone 1 june 2004 ko kaarybhaar sambhala tha.
