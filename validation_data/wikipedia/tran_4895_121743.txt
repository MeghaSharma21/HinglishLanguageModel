 nirdeshaank: 25°20′N 84°01′E / 25.34°N 84.01°E / 25.34; 84.01baxur bharat ke bihar praant kaa shahar he.
 bharat ke purvi pradesh bihar ke pashchim bhaag main ganga nadi ke tat par sthit ek aitihaasik shahar he. yahan kii arth-vyavastha mukhya rup se khetibari par aadhaarit he. yah shahar mukhyatah dharmik sthal ke naam se janaa jaataa he. pracheen kaal main iskaa naam 'vyaghrasar' tha. kyonki us samay yahan par baaghon kaa nivas hua karata tha tathaa ek bahut bada sarovar bhi tha jiske parinamswarup is jagah kaa naam vyaghrasar padaa.
 baxur patna se lagbhag 75 mile pashchim or mughalsarai se 60 mile purv main purvi railway line ke kinare sthit he. yah ek vyapaarik nagar bhi he. yahan bihar kaa ek pramukh karagrih hai jisamen apradhi log kapadaa aadi bunate or anya udyogon main lage rahete hai. suprasiddh baxur kii ladaai shujauddula or kasim ali khan kii tathaa angrej major munro kii senaaon ke bich yahan hi 1764 i main ladi gai thi jisamen angrejon kii vijay hui. is yuddh main shujauddula or kasim ali khan ke lagbhag 2,000 sainik dub gaye yaa maare the. karthik poornima ko yahan bada mela lagata he, jisamen lakhon vyakti ikatthe hote hai.
 baxur main guru vishwamitra kaa ashram tha. yaheen par ram or lakshmana kaa praarambhik shikshan-prashikshan hua. prasiddh tadka rakshasi kaa vadh ram dwaara yaheen par kiya gaya tha. 1764 i kaa 'baxur kaa yuddh' bhi itihaas prasiddh he. isi naam kaa ek zila shahbaad kaa anumandal he. baxur ke yuddh ke parinamswaroop nichle bengal kaa antim rup se british adhigrahan ho gaya. manyata he ki ek mahaan pavitra sthal ke rup main pehle iskaa mul naam 'vedagarbha' tha. kaha jaataa he ki vaidic mantron ke bahut se rachayita is nagar main rahete the. iskaa sambandh bhagwaan ram ke praarambhik jivan se bhi joda jaataa he.
 mauryaal kii anek sundar laghu murtiyan baxur utkhanan main praapt hui thim jo ab patna sangrahalay main surakshit hai.
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar baxur zile kii kul jansankhya lagbhag 1,707,643 he.
 baxur kii ladimir kasim ne avadh ke nawab se sahaayta kii yachana kii, nawab shujaudaula is samay sabase shakti shaali tha. marathe panipat kii teesri ladaai se ubar nahin paae the, mogul samraat tak uske yahan sharnaarthi tha, use ahamed shah abdali kii mitrataa praapt thijanvari 1764 main mir kasim us se mila usne dhan tathaa bihar ke pradesh ke badale uski sahaayta kharid lee. shah alam bhi unke saath ho liya. kintu tino ek duusare par shaq karte the.
