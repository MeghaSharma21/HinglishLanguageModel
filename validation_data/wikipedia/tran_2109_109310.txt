 nai dilli main hauj khaas kshetra main sthit he bhagwaan jagannatha ji kaa ek bhavy mandir. 
 yah mandir tyagaraj nagar main sthit he. iske nikatavarti kshetron main aate hai:
 is mandir kaa vaastu ekadam oriya mandir style kaa he. usapar chamakta hua ujala shwet varna isaki abha ko chaar chand lagaataa he. is mandir kii deevaaron par anekon devi devataaon kii murtiyon baniin huin hai. yah mandir mul jagannatha puri ke mandir kii ek choti prati lagata he.
 mukhya mandir main bhagwaan jagannatha, balbhadra or subhadra kii murtiyon sthapit hai. ye murtiyon garbhagrih main sthitahai. garbh grih ke upar torana par navagrahaon kii murtiyon sthit hai. 
 mukhya mandir ke thik saamne hi garud stambh sthitahai, jis kly upar vishnu vaahan garud asin hai.
 mukhya mandir ke saamne baanin ore hanuman ji kaa mandir he.
 mukhya mandir kii parikrama ke dai ore surya mandir sthit he.
 mukhya mandir kly aage hi dai ore shivalay bana he.
 vikimapia par is mandir kii sthiti dekhne hetu yahaan clic karen.
