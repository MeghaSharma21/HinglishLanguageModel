 england ke padington main 19 farvari 1963 ko janme sil henry olucangun olumide adiola semual ko saamanytah keval "sil " ke naam se janaa jaataa he. ve nyeejirian or brazilian mul ke english sol tathaa aar&bi gayak-geetkaar hai. unke naam olucangun kaa arth he "god ij victorius " . sil ko unke kai antarrashtriya hits ke liye janaa jaataa he. unka vivaah ek german model hedi klum se hua he.
 ve nigerian or brazilian mata-pita ke bete hai. unke mata-pita unka paalan-poshan karne main asamarth the, isaliye unhen god liya gaya. landan ke bhitari hisse main westminster ke ek jile padington main unka paalan-poshan hua. unhone architekchar main do varshiya diploma praapt kii or landan kshetra main vibhinn naukariyaan kin.
 yadhyapi kaaphi samay se unke chehre ke nishaanon ke kaaran ko lekar afvaahen failati rahi hai, kintu ye nishaan adivaasiyon ke godane kii rasm yaa kutton dwaara kaate jane kaa parinaam nahin hai. sil kaa rup-rang 'discoid lupas erithemetosus' naamak avasthaa ke lakshanon kaa adarsh udhaaharan he. sil ne saakshatkaaron main yah bataaya he ki ve in lakshanon se kishoravastha main peedit hue the. yah vah avasthaa he jab pratirakshak koshikaayen sharir ke vibhinn utakon par hamalaa karti hai. dlai aamtaur par yuva logon ko prabhavit karata he. twachaa main, visheshkar sharir ke khule bhaagon main, teevr sujan viksit hoti he. or yadi dhoop se bachaav or sujan-rodhi dawaaon ke saath tatkaal upachaar shuru na kiya jaye to iske parinamswaroop twachaa par dhabbe ho sakate hai. is beemaari ne sil ke na keval gaalon par nishaan utpann kiye the, balki unki khopadi bhi ullekhneey rup se prabhavit hui jiske parinamswaroop unke bal jhad gayi the. unki avasthaa main kaaphi varshon se sudhaar dekhne ko mila he.
 1980 ke dashak main kuch samay tak sthaneey club or baar main gane ke baad ve ek british fank band push main shaamil ho gayi or uske saath japan kaa daura kiya. asia main group se alag hone se pehle kuch samay ke liye ve thailand main ek bluez band main shaamil hue or baad main apane dam par sampurna bharat kii yatra kii. dost julian banstor, jo tab ek model the, ke sofe par sote hue sil england laut gayi. ve kabhi kabhi us se poochhate "kya main acha gaata hoon?"to unhen praayah jawaab miltaa ki adhikansh vartmaan kalakaaron kii tulana main unhone behatar gaaya. unhen pehla avasar tab mila jab ve nirmaataa edamski se mili. unhen 1990 kaa ek super hit geet "da killer" gane ko diya gaya. 
 sil ko sakaaratmak aalochanayen milin. ekal "kreji", "future lav paradise" or unki apni prastuti "killer" ne chart par acha pradarshan kiya. vishesh rup se "kreji" 1991 main ek antarrashtriya hit banakar billboard mujeec chart ke saatven sthaan par tathaa britain main duusare sthaan par pahunchaa.
 april 1992 main sil ne rock band queen ke jivit sadasyon ke saath fredy marakari tribut cancert main pradarshan kiya. sil ne apane hi gaaye hue 1986 ke hit geet "hu vaants tu liv forever" par pradarshan kiya or "ve aar da champions" ke ol star samaapan gayan ke shesh acts main shaamil ho gayi.
 unka doosraa album bhi unke hi naam "sil " sheershak se 1994 main jari kiya gaya tha. is safal album main ekal geet "prere for da daaing" tathaa "newborn frend" shaamil the, jise baad main varsh ke album ke liye ek gremi namakan praapt hua. "prere for da daaing" sanyukt rajya america main pop hit hua or billboard charts par 21wein sthaan par pahunchaa. tisare ekal geet "kis from roj" kaa pradarshan shuru main saamaanya rahaa, kintu baad main jab ise "bitman forever " ke soundtrack ke saath rimix kiya gaya to ise bahut vyapak lokapriyata mili. 1996 main "kis from e roj" ne varsh ke record tathaa varsh ke geet ke liye gremi puraskaar jeeta or america ke bazaar main sil kaa sabase acha pradarshan vaala ekal ban gaya .
 1998 main apane pichley album kii relies ke baad, sil ne "human bing" relies kiya. yah album us samay kaa he jab unke jivan main kaaphi uthal-puthal chal rahi thi; nirmaataa horn se ve alag hue tathaa baad main sulah ho gayi or saath hi jedtiti records se bhi ve alag hue or 1997 main warner bros records ke saath unka anubandh hua. record kii relies par isaki kaaphi aalochana hui. haalaanki, isaki relies kii taarikh ke do maheene baad hi aariee dwaara ise gold pramanikaran diya gaya. is album main teen ekal geet the "human bing", "letest craij" or "lost maai pheth".
 2001 main prashansak "tugetherland " naamak naye album ke aagaman kii pratiksha kar rahe the. haalaanki, nirmaan-pashchaat kii lambii avadhi ke baad album ko radd kar diya gaya. iskaa aadhikarik kaaran yah tha ki sil ke anusaar yah vaanchit str kaa nahin tha, haalaanki duusari report iske vipareet yah kahati he ki nirmaataaon ne yah anubhav kiya ki yah album vanijyik rup se safal nahin hoga, atah company dwaara ise asweekaar kar diya gaya. phir bhi, is album se ek ekal geet jari kiya gaya tha. "dis kud bi haven" america main jari kiya gaya tha or da family main ke soundtrack main ise shaamil kiya gaya. disambar 2006 ke baad sil ne sanket diya he unki yojana ki "tugetherland " se geeton ko ek-ek kar alag karke unhen strimming downlod ke liye uplabdh karaayaa jaye.is bich, 2002 main sil ne sah-geetkaar ke rup main hit ekal "maai vijan" likhaa tathaa use aawaaj bhi di. usee avadhi ke dauraan unhone french iken mylin farmar ke saath ek safal yugal geet "les mots" bhi record kiya.
 2003 main sil ne apana chauthaa album bhi, australia ko chhodkar jahaan yah "sil IV " ke naam se jari hua, apane hi naam se "sil" jari kiya. haalaanki, isaki bikri pehle do album kii tarah nahin hui, phir bhi is album kii relies ne sil ko sanyukt rajya america tathaa mahadwipiya europe main logon kii najaron main waapas laa diya. is album main ekal geet "vatting for you", "get it tugedar" or "loves divine" shaamil the .
 2004 main bekaarak/david ke clasik geet "walk on by" tathaa eco and banimen ke geet "lips like sugar" ke cover sahit "sil: best 1991 -2004" sheershak se sabase bade hit geeton ke ek album ko jari kiya gaya. album kaa ek aisa bhi sanskaran uplabdh tha jisamen sil ke kuch hit geeton ke dhvanik sanskarnon kii ek atirikt sidi ko shaamil kiya gaya tha.
 2004 main sil ne paris, france ke olympia theatre main sho ke pradarshan bhi kiye. 6 july 2004 ke sho ko record kiya gaya or lagbhag ek varsh baad use "live in paris " sheershak ke saath sidi/dividi package ke rup main relies kiya gaya.
 june 2005 main sil ne ek vishesh concert ko record kiya jise baad main 2006 main "van nite tu remember " sheershak se sidi / dividi sangyojan ke rup main relies kiya gaya. dividi main live pradarshan ke atirikt ek vishesh vruttchitr "da mekking of" bhi shaamil he. daseldorf, germany kii ek aitihaasik steel mil altes caselhouse main record kiye gayi is pradarshan main, sil ke dwaara vishisht shrotaaon ke liye, pehle german main phir angreji main gaaye gayi, brahm ke geet "lulaabi " ke vishesh sanskaran ko shaamil kiya gaya he. pehle kii recording main sil kaa band gane main unka saath detaa tha, lekin is baar ek purna orcestra tathaa 52 sangeetkaaron kii gayak mandali ne unka saath diya.
 "system " ko 12 navambar 2007 ko britain main tathaa 13 navambar 2007 ko america main jari kiya gaya. sil album kaa varnan karte hue kehete hai ki yah adhik nrityonmukh he, jahiraa taur par yah unke pehle album ke mul kii ore vaapasi he. "wedding de" sheershak vaale trek par, sil ne apni model patni hidi klum ke saath ek yugal geet gaaya he. album kaa pehla ekal geet "ameging" 25 sitambar 2007 ko relies kiya gaya or ise 2007 ke 50wein vaarshik grami puraskaar main "sarvshereshth pop gayan pradarshan" ke liye namankit kiya gaya. 
 2007 main sil ne royal vairieti kaarykram main "ameging" tathaa betals ke "luci in da sky vid diamonds" kaa pradarshan kiya".
 sil ne disambar main '2007 victoria'ja sikret fishan sho' main bhi "ameging" kaa pradarshan kiya tathaa saath hi saath yugal geet "wedding de" kaa apni patni ke saath pradarshan kiya. 2007 victoria'ja sikret fishan sho main pradarshan karne vaale anya kalakaaron main 'spice girls' or 'wiliam of blike id pij' shaamil hai. unhone american idal sijan 7 ke samaapan kaarykram main tisare sthaan ke pratiyogi saaysha markaado ke saath gaaya.
 sil kaa chatha studio album "sol " 11 navambar 2008ko sanyukt rajya america main tathaa 3 navambar 2008 ko antarrashtriya str par relies kiya gaya. is album main david foster dwaara nirmit gyaarah sol clasik geet hai.pratham ekal geet, sam cook ke cover "e change ij gonna kam" kaa video unke aadhikarik utyub prishth par uplabdh he. iske alaava ve 'dushland susht den superstar' par sheersh 9 pratiyogiyon kaa maargdarshan karenge tathaa album se apane ek geet par pradarshan bhi karenge.
 sil ne germany ke tivi sho 'dusland susht den superstar' ke pratibhaagiyon ko farvari / march, 2009 main prashikshan diya. 
 4 disambar 2009 ko "hits " sheershak se ek sankalan album jari kiya gaya tha jisamen do naye geet "ai em yor main" tathaa "thank you" shaamil the.
 sil kii aadhikarik website se praapt jaankaree ke anusaar ve vartmaan main apane saatven studio album "kamitment " kii recording kar rahe hai. yah album 28 sitambar 2010 ko america main tathaa 20 sitambar ko antarrashtriya str par relies kiya jaayegaa.
 sil kii german model hidi klum se mulaakaat sabase pehle 2003 kii garmiyon main hui - kuch hi samay baad hidi ne apni garbhavastha kii or phir italvi reno formula van team ke nideshak flavio brietor ke saath sambandh samaapt karne kii ghoshnaa kii.
 sil ne 23 disambar 2004 ko vislar, british colombia main ek glacier par 14000 foot kii oonchaai par unke dwaara banaaye gaye ek iglu main klum ke saamne vivaah kaa prastaav rakhaa. 10 may 2005 ko mexico ke costa kairess main sil ke ghar ke paas, samudr tat par is jode ne vivaah kiya. sil ke kareebi mitra robin doharti ne varapaksh ke mukhiya kii bhoomika nibhaai.
 har saal klum or sil apni shaadi kii saalagirah par samay nikaal kar, apane kareebi doston or parivaar ke saath apni shaadi kii kasmon ko taaja karte hai. is kasmon ke navinikaran ke bare main sil kehete hai-
 "har saal, hidi or main dobaaraa shaadi karte hai. yah ek badi party hoti he, lekin ek ghante ke liye, ham apane apane dam par ek nijee samudr tat par jate hai. ham bacchon ke saath vahaan baithte hai or jaise hi surya ast hota he, ham ek duusare ke liye kasame padhate hai. hamaare liye yah ek bahut hi vishesh kshan he. "
 klum ne 5 may 2004 ko neuyork shahar main, sil kii upasthiti main brietor kii putree, helen leni bushovan klum ko janm diya. klum ke anusaar brietor kaa leni ke jivan main koi dakhal nahin he or klum ne jor dekar kaha he ki "sil" leni ke pita hai. keval sil hi uske pita hai or unhone leni se duuri banaaye rakhane or uske jivan main rukaawat nahin daalane ke liye brietor kii prashansa kii he. 2009 ke ant main, sil ne aadhikarik taur par leni ko apanaaya liya or uskaa antim naam badalakar samuel ho gaya. is jode ke teen bachche hai- henry gunthar adiola dashtu samuel, johaan riley phyodor taiwo samuel or lu sulola samuel 2009).
 11 june 2009 ko, apane nae album "sol" ke saath daure ke dauraan sil ne bataaya, "meri shaadi ko chhod kar kisi anya baat ke liye patrikaon or samachar patron main aana acha lagata he." unhone kaha ki unki shaadi unka dhyaan to hataa sakati he, lekin sangeet banane ke unke shauk, jise ve prem karte hai, main koi kami nahin laa sakati.
 shaadi ke paanch saal baad, oktober 5, 2009 ko hidi klum ne apana naam badal kar hidi samuel karne ke liye yachika daayar kii.
 sil or unki patni hidi klum ne june 2010 main ghoshnaa kii ki sil ke hi ek geet ke naam par, "loves divine " sheershak se jeevankaal par ek reality siries banane ja rahe hai.
