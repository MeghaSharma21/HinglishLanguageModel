 jihad ek arabi shabd he jiskaa shabdic matlab vishesh rup se prashansaneey uddeshy ke saath prayaas karnaa yaa sangharsh karnaa he. iske islamic sandarbh main arth ke bahut se rang hai, jaise ki kisi ke buraai jhukaav ke khilaaf sangharsh, avishvaasiyon ko badalane kaa prayaas, yaa samaaj ke naitik bharose kii ore se prayaas,   islamic vidwaanon ne aamtaur par rakshaatmak yuddh ke saath sainya jihad ko samanta pradaan kii he.  sufi or dharmik mandal main, aadhyatmik or naitik jihad ko paaramparik rup se adhik jihad ke naam par bal diya gaya he.  is shabd ne aatankwaadi samoohon dwaara apane upyog ke dwaara haal ke dashakon main atirikt dhyaan aakarshith kiya he.
 islam main isaki badi ahmiyat he. do tarah ke jehad bataae gaye hai. ek he jehad al akbar yani bada jehad or doosraa he jehad al asgar yani chota jehad.
 jihad shabd aksar quran main sainya arthon ke bina dikhaai detaa he, aksar muhaavareydaar abhivyakti "ishwar ke maarg main prayaas kar rahaa he".  shastreey yug ke islamic nyayavidon or anya ulema ne mukhya rup se ek sainya arth main jihad kii daayitv ko samaj liya tha. unhone jihad se sambandhit niyamon kaa ek vistrut set viksit kiya, jisamen un logon ko nuksaan pahunchane ke pratibandh shaamil hai, jo ladaai main shaamil nahin hai.  aadhunik yug main, jihad kii dharna ne apni nyaayik praasangikta ko kho diya he or iske bajaay ek vaicharik or raajnneetik pravachan ko janm diya he. jabaki aadhunik islamic vidwaanon ne jihad kii rakshaatmak or gaiyr-sainya pahaluon par bal diya he,
 jehad al akbar ahimsaatmak sangharsh he sabase acha jihad dandakari sultan ke saamne nyaay kaa shabd he- ebn nuhas dwaara uddhrut kiya gaya or ebn habban dwaara sunai1. swayan ke bheetar maujuud sabhi buraaiyon ke khilaaf ladane kaa prayaas or samaaj main prakat hone vaali aisi buraaiyon ke viruddh ladane kaa prayaas .
 2. nasleey bhed-bhav ke viruddh ladanaa or auraton ke adhikaar ke liye prayaas karnaa 
 3. ek behatar chaatr bananaa, ek behatar saathi bananaa, ek behatar vyavsaayee sahyogi bananaa or in sabase upar apane krodh ko kaabo main rakhana 
 jehad al asgar kaa uddeshy islam ke sangrakshan ke liye sangharsh karnaa hota he. jab islam ke anupaalan kii azaadi na di jaae, usamen rukaawat daali jaae, yaa kisi muslim desh par hamalaa ho, musalamaanon kaa shoshan kiya jaae, unpar atyaachaar kiya jaae to usko rokane kii koshish karnaa or uske liye balidaan dena jehad al asgar he.
