 mikhail veller kaa janm 1948 main ek faujee afasar ke parivaar main hua. unka bachapan siberia or sudur-parv kii faujee chhavniyon main guzara. unhone 1972 main leningrad rajkeey vishvidhyalay ke bhashaa-sankaay se em.e. kiya. or uske baad, jaisaa ki khud unka kahana he, unhone puure desh main yayavari kii. taiga main van kaate. uttari dhruv ke us paar ke kshetra main toondra ke ilaake main shikari rahe. altaay parvatamala ke kshetra main charvaaha rahe. phir patrkaar rahe, adhyaaapak rahe... or is tarah mikhail veller ne kul milakar kareeb 30 peshey badale.
 pehali baar mikhail veller kii kavitaayen 1965 main belarus main ek pradeshik samachar patr main prakashit huin. unka pehla katha-sangrah 'khachu bitch dwornicum' 1983 main estonia main prakashit hua, jisane apni bhashaa kii sahajata or kahaniyon ke vishyon kii aakasmikta ke kaaran aalochakon or pathakon kaa dhyaan aakarshith kiya. iske baad 1988 main unka katha-sangrah 'rajabivachel serdets' ouk 1989 main 'tekhanalogia rasskaaza' naamak sangrah prakashit hue. phir 1991 main prakashit 'major zvyagin kaa romaanch' us varsh kii bestceller kitaab rahi.
 iske baad 1993 main chhapi 'nevski maarg kii dantakathaayen' naamak mixil veller kii kitaab ko aalochakon ne sabase manoranjak pustak mana. upanyasika 'nojhik ceryojhi dawlatva' par saahitiyik duniya main kalah or hangaamaa hua. 'piza kaa daudak' raashtrapati putin ke satta main aane se pehle kii desh kii sthiti or loktantr kii paraajay ke bare main he. 'aakhri mahaan mauka' pichley varshon kii raajnneeti ke bare main unki hit kitaab rahi he. mikhail veller rus kii saahitiyik patrikaon main niyamit rup se chhapate hai.
