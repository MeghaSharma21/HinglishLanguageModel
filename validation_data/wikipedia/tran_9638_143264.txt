 mahant avaidhyanath bharat ke rajaneta tathaa gorakhnath mandir ke bhootpurv peethadhishwar the. ve gorakhpur loksabhaa se chauthi loksabhaa ke liye hindu mahasabha se sarvapratham nirvaachit hue the. iske baad nauveen, dasvin tathaa gyarahavin loksabhaa ke liye bhi nirvaachit hue.
 mahant avedyanath kaa janm 28 may 1921 ko mahant avedyanath ji kaa janm gram kandi, jila paudi gadhwal, uttrakhand main shri raay singh bisht ke ghar hua tha. aapke bachapan kaa naam shri kripal singh bisht tha or kalantar main shri avaidhyanath banakar bharat ke rajaneta tathaa guru gorakhnath mandir ke peethadhishwar the ke rup main prasidd hue. shri avaidhyanath ji ne hindu dharm kii aadhyatmik sadhana ke saath "saamaajik hindu" sadhana ko bhi aage badhaaya or saamaajik janajagran ko adhik mahatvapurn maanakar hindu dharm ke soshal ejineerig par bal diya | shri yogi adityanath ji ke hindu yuva vahini jaise yuva sanghathan kii prerana bhi kahin na kahin isi sosal ejineerig kii prerana rahi thi |
 himalay or kailash maansarovar kii yatra or sadhana se shaiva dharm se gahare prabhavit shri mahanth ji pehali baar 1940 main apni bengal yatra ke dauraan menman singh main shri nivruti nath ji ke madhyam se shri digvijay nath ji se mili | 8 farvari 1942 ko aap gorakshpith ke uttaradhikari ban gaye | or is tarah maatr 23 saal kii avasthaa main shri kripal singh bisht se avaidhyanath banakar vishwamanch par ek daidivyman akshay prakaash punj ke rup main sadaiv ke liye amar ho gayi |
 aajanm vivaadon se dur rahane vaale, virakt sanyasi, sajjan, saral or sumdhur or mitbhaashi vyaktitv ke dhani shri avaidhyanath ji ne shri ramajanm bhoomi aandolan ko maatr gati hi nahin diya apitu ek sangrakshak kii bhaanti har tarah se rakshit or poshit kiya | 
 rajnatik jivan 
 dakshin bharat ke ramnathpuram or minakshipuram main anusuchit jati ke logon ke samuhik dharmaataran kii ghatana se khaase aahat hote hue maharaj ji ne raajnneeti main padaarpan kiya. is ghatana kaa vistaar uttar bharat main na ho, iske liye mahatvapurn kadam uthaaye gaye or raajnneeti main rahakar mataantaran kaa dhruvikaran karne ke kutil prayaason ko asafal kiya |
 aapane 1962, 1967, 1974 va 1977 main uttar pradesh vidhansabha main maniram seat kaa pratinidhitiv kiya or 1970, 1989, 1991 or 1996 main gorakhpur se loksabhaa sadsy rahe. 34 varshon tak hindu mahasabha or bhartiya janata party se jede rahakar hindutv ko bhartiya raajnneeti main gati dene vaale or saamaajik hiton kii raksha karne vaale shri avaidhyanath ji ne swayan ko avasarwaad or padbhaar se swayan ko dur rakhaa or is tarah unhone rajyog main bhi hathyog kaa prayog bakhubi kiya | kitne pad swayan maharaj ji ke charnon main aakar swayan sushobhit hote the or ashish lete the | 
 saamaajik samarasata ke agradoot
 yoga va darshan ke marmajna mahantji ke raajnneeti main aane kaa makasad hindu samaaj kii kuritiyon ko dur karnaa or ram mandir andolan ko gati dena rahaa he. hindu dharm main oonch-neech dur karne ke liye unhone lagaataar sahabhoj ke aayojan kiye. iske liye unhone banaras main santon ke saath domraja ke ghar jaakar bhojan kiya or samaaj kii ekajutata kaa sandesh diya.
 hindu samaaj kii ekta hi unke pravachan ke centre main hoti thi. vah mulat: itihaas or ramcharitamanas kaa sahaara lete the. shriram kaa shabari, jataayu, nishadaraaj va girijanon se vyavhaar kaa udhaaharan dekar dalit-garib logon ko gale lagane kii prerana dete rahe. 
 gorakshpith par guru gorakhanathji ke pratinidhi ke rup main sammanit sant ko mahant kii oopaadhi se alankrita kiya jaataa he. vartmaan samay main mahant shri avedyanath ji maharaj goraksh peethadhishwar ke pad par asin the. is mandir ke pratham mahant varadanathji maharaj rahe hai jo guru gorakhnath ji ke shishy the.
 saraswati mata ke kripapaatr
 mahant avedyanath ne varanasi va haridwar main sanskrit kaa adhyayan kiya tha. vah sanskrit se shastri the. maharana pratap shiksha parishad se judi shaikshanik sansthaaon ke adhyaksh ke alaava ve masik patrika yogavani ke sampadak rahe. yoga va darshan par lagaataar likhaa.gorakshpith se judi chikitsa, shiksha or saamaajik kshetron main kaam kar rahi kareeb teen darjan sansthaaon ke adhyaksh evam prabandhak the. maharana pratap shiksha parishad se judi shaikshanik sansthaaon ke adhyaksh ke alaava ve masik patrika yogavani ke sampadak rahe. yoga va darshan par aajanm likhte rahe |
 mahant avedyanath ne gorakhpur ke vartmaan saansad yogi adityanath ko gorakshpith kaa na sirf uttaradhikari banaaya balki unhone yogi adityanath ko 1998 main sabase kam umr kaa saansad banane kaa gaurav pradaan kiya. baad main yogi adityanath ne hindu yuva vahini kaa gathan kiya jo hindu yuvaaon ko dharmik banane ke liye prerana detii he.
 maharajaganj ke chowk bazaar sthit gorakshpithadhishwar mahant avedyanath mahavidhyalay inhi ke naam par rakhaa gaya hai.
 mahanth avedyanath kaa nidhan 12 sitambar 2014 ko ho gaya. brahmaleen mahant avaidhyanath ko nath parampara ke anusaar samadhi di gayi| police kii tukdi ne guard of anar diya tathaa maatami dhun bajaayi | padhyamasana kii mudra main unhen samadhi kii pashchim disha main bani samadhi grih main rakhaa gaya| samadhi main kaccha rot, veda, janeu, adhari, khappar, jholii, chini, chaaval, ghi tathaa nariyal par deep rakhaa gaya. dhoop, ghi, lobaan va kapur se antim puja ke baad gorakshpithadhishwar mahant adityanath ne samadhi gayatri ke saath sabase pehle mitti daalkar antim vidaai di | ve kaaphi samay se biimaar 97 varshiya mahant ek maah se gudgaanv ke medanta aspataal main bhartee the. shuukravaar shaam haalat bigadane par unhen vishesh vimaan se gorakhpur laayaa gaya, jahaan unhone sharir choda.
