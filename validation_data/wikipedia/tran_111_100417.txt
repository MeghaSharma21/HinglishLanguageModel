 ejineering projects limited, baari udyog vibhaag ke prashasnic niyantran ke adheen saarvajanik kshetra kii udhyam he or turnaki pariyojna prabandhan or pariyojna niryaat ke kshetra main maarg-darshak rahi he. apane 41wein vashon se adhik ke kaarykaal main ipiai ne vividh kshetron main visheshgyata hasil kii he. jinamein nimnlikhit shaamil hai -
 company dwaara pradutt sevaaon main vividh visheshgyata kaary-kalap sammilit hai, jaise kih-
 ipiai ne bharatvarsh main 5608.03 karod ru. mulya kii 475 se adhik pariyojnaon tathaa bharat se baahar 782 million amareeki dollar mulya kii 30 pariyojnaon ko safalataa purvak puraa kiya he. varsh 2010-11 kii avadhi main ipiai ne 1103.69 karod ru. kaa kaarobaar kiya or 1411.48 karod ru. mulya kii pariyojnaayen praapt kin.
 ipiai ne pichley kuch vashon ke dauraan samjhauta gyaapan lakshyon kii prapti ke sambandh main apane kaary nishpaadan ke liye or pariyojna kaary nishpaadan ke liye bhi kai paritoshik praapt kiye hai.
 ipiai ek aadhunik vichaardhaara or pragatisheel drushtikon vaali company he or apane saamaajik dayitvon ke prati bhi purnatah jaagru k he. yah desh main pehali un kuch companiyon main se ek he jinhen unnt koti kaa iesao 9001:2000 pramann pradaan kiya gaya he. is unnat koti pramann main purv iesao 9001:1994 pramann ke samaan hi ipiai ke prachaalan ke samast kshetra aate hai, jabaki anya companiyon ke sambandh main keval vishesh kshetra hi iske antargat aate hai. ise ab iesao-14001 ke liye praaanpatr pradaan kiya gaya he. iske saath hi ipiai bharat varsh kii pratham aisi nirmaan or theka companio me se ek ho gai he jinhe yah dono praaanpatr mili hai.
