 vishnu sahasranam bhagwaan vishnu ke hajaar naamon se yukt ek pramukh stotra he. yah hindu dharm main sabase pavitra tathaa prachlit stotron main se ek he. mahabharat main uplabdh vishnu sahasranam iskaa sabase lokapriy sanskaran he. padam puraana va matsay puraana main iskaa ek or sanskaran uplabdh he. prayek naam vishnu ke anginat gunon main se kuch gunon ko suchit karata he. kai hindu pariwaron me pujan ke samay iskaa pathan karte he. iske sunne yaa pathan se manushya kii manokamanaayen purna hone kii manyata he.
 anushaasanparv ke 149 wein adhyay ke anusaar, kurukshetra me baanon kii shayya par lete pitamah bhishma ne yudhisthir ko iskaa upadesh diya tha.
 kurukshetra yuddh me hui vinaash dekh dharmraj yudhisthir vichlit hue. baanon kii shayya par lete bhishma bhi apane praan tyaagane kii pratiksha karte the. samay uchit paakar bhagwaan vedavyas or shrikrishna ne yudhisthir ko samaakalin sarvocch gyani bhishma se dharm va niti ke vishay me upadesh lene kii prerana di. apane sabhi bhai samet krishna ko saath liye yudhisthir pitamah bhishma ke paas pahunch kar unka naman kiya or dharm va niti ke vishay me vichaar karte kiye prashn va bhishma kaa vivaran sahit uttar hi yah sahasranam stotra he.
 is stotra ke teen pramukh bhaagon ko nimna bhaagon me diya gaya he.
 isame sarvapratham ganesh, vishvaksen, vedavyas tathaa vishnu kaa naman kiya jaataa he.
 iske baad yudhisthir ke prashn he: 
 arth : 
 iske uttar main bhishmaji ne kaha,"jagat ke prabhu, devon ke dev, anant va purushottam vishnu ke sahasranam ke japane se, achal bhakthi se, stuti se, aradhana se, dhyaan se, naman se manushya ko sansar ke bandhan se mukti milti he. yahi sarvottam dharm he."
 iske baad rishi, devadi sankalp tathaa paramatma kaa dhyaan kiya jaataa he.
