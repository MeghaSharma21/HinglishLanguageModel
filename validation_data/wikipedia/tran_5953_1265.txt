 
 Pongamia glabra Vent.Millettia pinnata L.Derris indica Bennet
 karanj naam se praaia: teen vanaspati jatiyon kaa bodh hota he jinamein do vriksh jatiyaan or teesri lata sadrush faili hui gulm jati he. inkaa parichay nimnaankit he :
 karanj naam se jaani jane vaali pratham vriksh jati ko sanskrit vaanmay main naktmaal, karanjika tathaa vrikshakranj aadi or lokbhashaon main didhori, doharkaranj athwa kanjhi aadi naam die gaye hai. iskaa vaigyanik naam pongamia glabra he, jo leguminosi kul evam pepileonacy upkul main samavisht he. yahi sambhavat: vaastavik karanj he.
 yadhyapi paristhiti ke anusaar isaki oonchaai aadi main bhinnata hoti he, parantu vibhinn paristhitiyon main ugane kii isamen adbhut kshamta hoti he. iske vriksh adhiktar nadi-naalon ke kinare swat: ug aate hai, athwa saghan chhayaadaar hone ke kaaran sadakon ke kinare lagaae jate hai.
 iske patr, pakshawat sanyukt, assam pakshawat or patrak gahare hare, chamkile or praaia: 2-5 inch lambey hote hai. pushp dekhne main moti sadrush, gulabi or aasamaani chaaya liye hue shwet varna ke hote hai. fali kathor evam mote chhilake kii, ek bijwali, chipti or tedi nokwali hoti he. pushpit hone par iske moti tulya pushp raatri main vriksh ke neeche girakar bahut sundar maaloom hote hai. 'karanj' evam 'naktmaal' sangyaaon kii sarthakata or kaavyon main prakrutivarnan ke prasang main inkaa ullekh isi kaaran hota he.
 ayurvedic chikitsa main mukhyat: iske beej or bijtel kaa prachur upyog batalaaya gaya he. inkaa adhik upyog vranshodhak evam vranropak, krimighn, ushnviry tathaa charmrogaghn rup main kiya jaataa he.
 bhinn jati evam kul kaa hone par bhi chirbilva naam-rup-gun teenon baton main naktmaal se bahut kuch miltaa julata he. yah almesi kul kaa holoptelia integripholia naamak jati kaa vriksh he, jise chirbilva, karanjak vriksh yaa vriddhakaranj tathaa udkiry or lokbhashaon main chilbil, paapadi, kanju tathaa kanjhi aadi naam die gaye hai.
 iske vriksh praaia: bahut unche or mote hote hai or nadi naalon ke sannikat adhik paae jate hai. chaal dhoosar varna kii or pattiyaan praaia: akhand or lambaagra hoti hai. taaji chaal or kashtha se tathaa masalane par pattiyon se teevr durgandh aati he. jaadon main pattey gir jane par nangi shakhaon par sookshm harith pushpon ke guchchhe nikalate hai or greeshm main bahut halake, patale chipte tathaa sapaksh vrittakar falon ke guchchhe ban jate hai, jo sookhne par vaayu dwaara prasaarit hote hai. dwikhandit pankh ke bich main ek beej band rahata he jise nikaalkar gramin baachche chirunji kii bhanti khate hai. bijon se tel bhi nikaala ja saktaa he. pratham shrenee ke karanj ke sadrush iske patr, beej tathaa bijtel chikitsopayogi maane jate hai, kintu aajkal inhen prayog main nahin laayaa jaataa. shoth, vran tathaa charmarogon main iskaa upyog gramin chikitsa main paaya jaataa he.
 yah ek kantedaaralata sadrush failaa hua gulm he jise vitapakaranj, contakiranj, prakiry or lokbhasha ke kanja, sagargota tathaa nata karanj kehete hai. iskaa ek naam 'fiver nat' bhi he. aadhunik granthkaaron ne ise hi ayurvedic sahitya kaa 'purti k' evam 'putikranj' bhi likhaa he. kintu karanj ke sabhi bhedon main newnaadhik puti hone ke kaaran kisi vargvishesh ko hi putikranj kahana sangat nahin prathit hota.
 katakaranj leguminosi kul evam sezalapiniapadi upkul kaa sezalapinia christa naam kaa gulm he, jisaki kantedaar shakhaayen lata ke samaan failati he. kaante drudhmulak, sidhe athwa patradand par praaia: tede hote hai. pattiyaan dwipakshwat or patrak lagbhag ek inch tak bade hote hai. halake pile pushpon kii manjariyaan naktmaal ke falon ke aakaar kii hoti hai, kintu fal kaanton se dhake rahete hai or unamem drudh kavachavaale tathaa dhoomravarn ke praaia: do-do beej hote hai. beej, bijtel evam pattee kaa chikitsa main adhik upyog hota he. katakaranj uttam jverghn, katu, paushtik, shothghn or krimighn dravya he or sutikatovar, sheetajvar, yakrut evam pleeha ke rog tathaa kupachan main iske pattey kaa ras, yaa bijachurna kaa upyog hota he. yadhyapi nighantuon main karanj ke teen bhed bataae gaye hai, tathaapi chikitsagranthon main anek baar 'karanjadway' kaa ek saath upyog batalaaya gaya he. karanjadway se yahan kin-kin bhedon kaa grahan hona chahie, iskaa nirnay prasang tathaa vyaktigat gunon ke anusaar kiya ja saktaa he.
 karanj ke vriksh bahut bade-bade hote hai. jo adhiktar wanon main hote hai. pattey paakar patton ke samaan gol hote hai. or upar ke bhaag main chamakdaar hote hai.
 iske phool aasamaani rang ke hote hai. or fal bhi nile-nile jhumkedaar hote hai. patton main durgandh aati he.
 karanj pachne main charpari, netra hitkaari, garam, kaduvi, kastyli tathaa udaavart, vaat yoni rog, vaat gulm, arsh, vran, kandu, kaph, vish, kusht, pitt, krimi, charmrog, udrarog, prameh, pleeha ko dur karti he. karanj ke fal - garam, halke tathaa shirorog, vaat, kaph, krimi, kusht, arsh or prameh ko nasht karte hai.
 pachne main charpare, garam, bhedak, pittajnak, halke tathaa vaat, kaph, arsh, krimi, ghaav tathaa shoth rog naashak he. phool - ooshna, veery tathaa tridosh naashak he.
 ras evam pachne main charpare, agnideepak, pachak, vaat, kaph, arsh, kusht, krimi, vish, shoth rog ko nasht karata he.
 tikshn, garam, kriminashak, raktpittakaarak, tathaa netra rog, vaat peeda, kusht, kandu, vran tathaa khujali ko nasht karata he. iske lep se twachaa vikaar dur hote hai ghrit karanj - charpara garm tathaa vran, vaat, sarv prakaar ke twachaa rog, arsh rog, tathaa kusht rog ko nasht karata he.
