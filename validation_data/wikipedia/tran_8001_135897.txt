 1958 main nirmit bengalooru kaa vidhaan saudh, karnataka sarkaar ke sachivalay or rajya kii vidhaan sabha ke kaarysthal ke rup main upyog main laayaa jaataa he. bengalooru grenite se nav-dravid style main bani yah imaarat bhartiya sthapaty kala kaa shandaar udhaaharan he. ise ravivaar raatri ke samay kritrim prakaash main dekhnaa sukhad anubhav he.
