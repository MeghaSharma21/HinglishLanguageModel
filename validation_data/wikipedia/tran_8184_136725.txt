 vigyaan main aisi samasyaon ko pratilom samasya kehete hai jo kuch prekshanon kii sahaayta se unako utpann karne vaale kaarnon kii gananaa karatin hai. udhaaharan ke liye dharti ke gurutveey kshetra ke aankadon ke aadhaar par dharti ke andar vibhinn binduon par ghanatv nikaalnaa ek pratilom samasya he.
 inakon pratilom samasya isaliye kehete hai ki yah parinaamon se shuru hotin hai or 'ulta' chalakar upyukt kaaran kii khoj karatin hai jinase aise parinaam praapt ho sakate hai. atah yah anulom samasya kaa ulta he jo kaaran se shuru hokar parinaam kii gananaa karti he.
 pratilom samasya kii gananaa, ganit or vigyaan kii kuch sarvaadhik mahatvapurn samasyaon main hoti he. ye isaliye mahatvapurn hai kyonki ve un prachalon ke bare main bataane kaa prayaas karatin hai jinako sidhe mapa nahin ja saktaa. inkaa upyog prakaashiki, radar, dhvaniki, sanchaar siddhanth, sanket prasanskaran, medical imaging, computer drushti, bhubhautikee, samudravigyaan, khagoliki, sudur samvedan, mashin larning, prakritik bhashaa prasanskaran, avinashi pareekshan tathaa anya anekanek kshetron main kiya jaataa he.
