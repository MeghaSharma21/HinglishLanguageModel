 rogan josh bhed ke maance kaa ek kashmiri vyanjan he jo kashmiri khaane kii ek khaasiyat maani jaati he.
 farsi main 'rogan' kaa matlab 'tel' or 'josh' kaa matlab 'utsaah' yaa 'garmi' hota he. garm tel main banaae jane ke kaaran is pakwaan kaa naam 'rogan josh' padaa he. anya sroton ke anusaar yah naam rajasthan or kashmir main ugaae jane vaale 'ratanjot' naamak paudhe se aayaa he jiskaa prayog bhi is vyanjan main hota he. 'rogan' main 'ga' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'ga' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'galati' or 'garib' shabdon ke 'ga' se miltaa he.
 rogan josh main bina haddi vaali maance kii botiyon kaa prayog hota he. ise pyaaz, dahi, lahasun, adarak, laung, ilaychi or kabhi-kabhi kesar ke saath bani tari main pakaaya jaataa he. saath hi isamen badi maatra main sukhi laal kashmiri mirch daali jaati he, jinamein se beej nikaalkar unka tikhaapan kam kar diya gaya hota he. rogan josh kaa laal rang inhi mirchiyon se aata he. paaramparik rup se isamen ratanjot naamak ek paudhe kaa bhi prayog hota he.
