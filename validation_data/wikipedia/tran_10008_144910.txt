 america footbaal gyaarah khilaadiyon kii do tiimon ke bich khelaa jane vaala ek lokapriy khel he jisamen har team kaa uddeshy he kii vah gend ko duusari team ke "and zone" main pahunchaakar ank batore. 
 jis team ke paas gend par qabza hota he, vah gend ke saath daudkar yaa apane saathiyon main aapas main gend phenkakar use virdohi team ke oundzon tak badhaane kii koshish karti he. agar us team kaa koi khilaadi gend pakade hue gol kii lakeer ko paar karke virdohi oundzon main pahunch jaataa he, yaa oundzon ke andar khadaa hua apane saathi dwaara phenki gai gend ko safaltaapurvak pakad letaa he yaa phir maidan se gol kii lakeer ke peeche bane do khambon ke bich se gend ko laat markar pahunchaa detaa he to uski team ko ank milte hai. virdohi team kaa kaam he ki takkar markar, giraakar, yaa bich main aakar phenki gai gend ko pakadakar kisi tarah gend par qabza kare yaa dushman team ko apni team ke oundzon kii taraf badhane se roke.
 america footbaal adhiktar sanyukt rajya america or canada main kheli jaati he. iske khel ko vyavsaayik str par niyantrit karne vaali santhaan kaa naam "naishanal footbaal lig" he, jise chote rup main chhanalfail kaha jaataa he. america main is khel ke do mukhya rup hai, college footbaal or vyavsaayik footbaal, jinke niyam ek-dusre se thode alag hai. skulon kii ucch kakshaon main bhi footbaal kheli jaati he or uske niyam bhi thode bhinn hote hai.
 khel kaa maidan 100 gaz lambaa or 53.3 gaz chaudaa hota he. maidan kii lambaai ke donon taraf ek gol kii lakeer hoti he, jiske aage das-das gaz ke oundzon hote hai, yani kul milakar maidan kii lambaai 120 gaz hoti he. maidan main har paanch gaz ke faasale par ek yardline naam kii lakeer hoti he. har oundzon ke akhir main zamin se 10 foot kii oonchaai par do khambe hote hai jinhen "goalpost" kaha jaataa he.
 kisi bhi samay har team maidan main apane 11 khilaadi rakh sakati he, lekin tiimon ko adhikaar hota he ki khel main kisi bhi antaraal ke samay apana ek yaa sabhi khilaadi badal de. isliye footbaal ek visheshgy khilaadiyon kaa khel he. kisi bhi team main vastav main bahut se khilaadi hote hai, jinhen teen prakaaron main baanta jaataa he: akramak khilaadi, rakshak khilaadi or vishesh khilaadi . kuch khilaadi to tiimon main sirf isliye rakhe jaate hai ki agar unke hi hunar vaale kisi khilaadi ko chot lag jaae to unhen unke sthaan par daalaa ja sake. zyaadaatar muqaabalon main kisi bhi team ke kai khilaadiyon ko maidan main utarane kaa mauqaa hi nahin miltaa.
 jis team ke paas gend par qabza ho, use "aakraamak" bulaayaa jaataa he or jis ke paas na ho use "rakshak" bulaayaa jaataa he. akramak team ke sabase mahatvapurn khilaadi ko quarterbaque kehete hai. jab akramak or rakshakon main bhidnt shuru karti he to isi kaa kaam he ki tezi se maidan kaa muina karke faisalaa kare ki akramak team kaa koun-sa khilaadi gend ko rakshak team ke oundzon kii taraf badhaane ke liye sabase acchhee sthiti main maujuud he. phir quarterbaque use gend fank detaa he, yaa khud hi bhagkar gend aage badhaane kii koshish karata he.
 khel kaa puraa samay ek ghanta hota he, jise chaar 15 minute ki chauthaiyon main baanta jaataa he. duusari chauthai ke baad ek 12 minute kaa viraam hota he. khelte hue kai baar gend ruk jaati he or tab khel kii ghadi bhi roak di jaati he. is se 15 minute kii chauthai vastav main 45 minute se bhi adhik le sakati he or puraa ek ghante kaa khel vastav main teen ghante se bhi zyaada chal saktaa he. jab khel television par prasaarit hota he to in antaraalon main prasarankarta ishtehaar dikhaate hai.
 muqaabale kii shuruyaat main sikaa uchhaala jaataa he or ek team ko aakraamak team ghoshit kar ke unhen gend de di jaati he. ab 50 gaz kii lakeer par donon timen ek dusre se bhidane ke liye aamane-saamne lakeeren banaakar khadi ho jaati hai. aakraamak team kaa quarterbaque apni team ke peeche khadaa hota he. maahaul thik aisa hota he ki do gut jang ke liye taiyaar ho rahe hon.
 khel shuru hota he jab gend aakraamak quarterbaque ke haathon main pahunchati he. foran rakshak team ke khilaadi us tak pahunch kar use dhane kii ziddo-jahad main lag jate hai. kuch aakraamak khilaadi unase bhidakar unhen rokane kii koshish karte hai, jabaki baaqi akramak khilaadi virdohi ke kshetra main daakhil hokar apane aap ko gend aage badhaane ke liye acchhee sthiti main daalane kii koshish karte hai. quarterbaque ke paas kuch hi kshan hote hai jisamen use faisalaa karnaa hota he: kis saathi ko gend phenken yaa phir khud rasta dhundta hua aage bhaage. kuch hi kshanon main uski raksha karne vaale ginati ke akramak khilaadiyon ko unase jujhane vaale rakshak khilaadi patakekar yaa dhakelkar quarterbaque tak pahunch jate hai. agar gend quarterbaque ke haath main ho or use daboch diya jaae to ise "sac" kehete hai or kisi bhi quarterbaque ke liye yah bahut sharmnaak mana jaataa he. adhik baar sac hone kaa matlab he kii quarterbaque jhadpon ke bich soch nahin paaya: vah kamazor he or aakraamakon kaa netritv karne ke qaabil nahin he.
 chaahe quarterbaque gend kisi ko phenke, chaahe khud use lekar bade, aam taur se rakshak thodi hi duuri par gendwale aakraamak khilaadi ko dha hi lete hai or gend ruk jaati he. is sthiti ko down kehete hai. aakraamakon kaa kaam he kii 4 dounon main gend ko kam-se-kam 10 gaz badhayen. agar ve aisa nahin kar pate to rakshakon ko gend par qabza de diya jaataa he or rakshak ab aakraamak banate hai or aakraamak rakshak. gend kaa qabza tab bhi badal jaataa he agar kisi aakraamak ke haath se khelte hue gend chhot jaae or koi rakshak us hilti hue gend ko pakad le yaa phir quarterbaque kisi anya aakraamak khilaadi ko gend phenke or koi rakshak bich main aakar use pakad le . fambal yaa interspshan kii sthiti main jis rakshak ne gend pakad liya he vah virdohi ke oundzon kii taraf foran bhaagna shuru kar saktaa he or tab tak bhaag saktaa he jab tak use giraakar daboch na liye jaae, maidan se baahar na dhakel diya jaae, yaa vah oundzon main pahunchakar apane team ke liye ank hi na bana le.
 jab gend ruukti he to yah bade dhyaan se mapa jaataa he kii 100 gaz ke maidan main yah kaunse gaz par he. aglaa khel maidan ke bich main usee gaz par shuru kiya jaataa he. is jagah ko "bhidane kii lakeer" kaha jaataa he. jaisaa kii kaha gaya he, aakraamak team kaa yahi maqasad he ki chaar yaa us se kam dounon main gend 10 gaz aage bade. farz kijiye ke ve do hi dounon main gend ko 10 gaz aage badha len. to kaha jaataa he ki ve phir se pehle down par hai or unhen ab is nai scrimmege lakeer se phir se chaar dounon main gend ko 10 gaz badhanaa he. yah silsilaa tab tak chalata he jab tak aakraamak rakshakon ke oundzon tak na pahunch jaaen yaa galati karke virdohi ko gend kaa qabza na de den.
 qabza badalane par jo khel main rukaav hota he, usamen khilaadi bhi badal die jate hai. bahut se aakraamak khilaadi daudane main or phenki gai gend pakadaney main nipun hote hai or aksar unke sharir halake hote hai. bahut se rakshak bhimkaay hote hai or agar ve daboch len yaa patake den to zyaadaatar aakraamak-vishesh khilaadiyon ke liye uthaanaa mushkil ho jaataa he. acha aakraamak vahi he jise quarterbaque dwaara phenki gai gend ko bina galati ke pakadana aata ho or jo bhaagate hue rakshakon ko chakma dete hue dushman ke kshetra main dur tak gend badha sake. kuch aakraamakon kaa kaam hota he ki ve rakshakon se bhiden taaki anya aakraamak gend aage le ja saken. inkaa naam hi "oinsive tekal" hota he.
