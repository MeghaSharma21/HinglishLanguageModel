 samantfal sutt dighanikaay ke 34 sutton main se doosraa he. iskaa shabdic arth he- shraman kaa jivan jeene kaa fal.
 is sutt main bimbisar ke putr va uttaradhikari ajatshatru dwaara vibhinn shramanon se puche gayi ek prashn vishesh ke unke anusaar diye gayi uttar hai. vah prashn tha- "kya saman kaa jivan jeene se koi aisa fal hota he, jo abhi or yaheen dikhaai de ?" yah prashn usne nimnlikhit chah samanon se poocha, jo sabhi vibhinn maton ko maanane vaale the-
 inamen se prayek ne apane-apane mat ke anusaar uttar diya, jinase ajatshatru sarvatha asantusht rahaa. ant main usne yah prashn gautam buddha se poocha jinke uttar se use santushti hui.
