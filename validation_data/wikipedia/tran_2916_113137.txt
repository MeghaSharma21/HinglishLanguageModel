 rog kaa aarambh dhire dhire hota he. thakaavat or pairon main kamajori, chalane main ladkadaahat, pairon main dard, shoonyata, chunachunanaahat, poshanheenata ke chihn, peshiyon kaa sankuchan, pair yaa kalai kaa lakva tathaa maansik lakshan prakat ho sakate hai. rogi apahiz ho jaataa he. bhinn kaarnon se utpann tantrikashoth ke lakshan bhi bhinn hote hai. udhaaharan ke liye sankhiya ke vish ke prabhaav se pairon kii kamajori, sis ke vish ke prabhaav se anguliyon or kalaaiyaan kaa lakva, madhumeh main pairon main durbalata tathaa poshan vran, diphthyria main komal taalu kaa lakva, sharaab ke vifaille prabhaav se pairon kaa sunn hona, haath pair kii anthan, hilne dulne main dard, sparshavednaa aadi lakshan hote hai.
 yah kathin rog he. kaaran gyaat hone par uski tadnusaar chikitsa kii jaati he. kshtdaayak lakshanon kii shanti kaa upaay kiya jaataa he, yatha senk, halki maalish, vidyuttejan, pidanashak aushadhi aadi. vitamin bi 1 kaa upyag laabhadaayak ho saktaa he. peshiyon kaa sankuchan rokna chahie.
