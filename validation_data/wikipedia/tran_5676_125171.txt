   manipadme hum ek sanskrit mantra he jiskaa sambandh awalokiteshwar se he. yah tibeti buddha dharm kaa mul mantra he.
 yah praayah pattharon par likhaa jaataa he yaa phir kaagaj par likh kar puujachakra main laga diya jaataa he. manyata he ki is pahiye ko jitani baar ghumaya jaayegaa yah utani baar mantra bolne ke baraabar fal degaa.
