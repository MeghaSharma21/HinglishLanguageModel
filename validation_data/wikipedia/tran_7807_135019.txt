 adleen virginia vulf  20veen sadi kii ek pratibhashali angrej sahityakar or nibandhkaar thim. e room of vans on kii lekhika virginia woolf prasiddh lekhika, aalochak or parvatarohi pita sar stephen or maa julia stephen kii beti thim. unka janm 1882 main landan main hua tha. buddhijiviyon kii awajahi unke ghar main hoti rahati thi. jaahir he virginia kaa bhi rujhaan aarambh se hi likhane-padhane kii ore rahaa. virginia kii adhiktar smritiyaan cornwall kii hai, jahaan vah akasar garmeeyon kii chhuttiyaan bitaane jaati thim. inhen smritiyon kii dein thi unki pramukh rachana - tu da linehouse. jab vah keval 13 varsh kii thim, tab unki maa kaa aakasmik nidhan ho gaya. iske do varsh baad apni bahan va 1904 main pita ko bhi unhone kho diya. yah unka avsaad bharaa daur tha. iske baad aaajeevan avsaad ke daure unhen gherte rahe. iske baad bhi unhone kai mahatvapurn kritiyon kii rachana kii. shaareerik rup se bahut durbal hone ke kaaran unki padhai-likhaai ghar par hi hui. baad main unhone adhyaapan kaary aarambh kiya. 30 varsh kii aayu main unhone loyonard vulf se vivaah kiya. unhone diary, jivaniyaan, upanyaas, aalochana sabhi likhe. lekin unki priy vishaywastu stri vimarsh hi thi. isi kaa parinaam tha, unki mahatvapurn pustak e room of vans on. 
 1940 main dwitiya vishwayuddh main naziyon ke hamale ke dauraan woolf dampati bahut pareshaan rahaa karte the, kyonki unke pati leonard yahudee the, jinase naji ghrinaa karte the. isi varsh bambaari main unka press nasht ho gaya. avsaad kii sthiti main unhone 28 march 1941 main nadi main chalaang laga di or aatmahatya kar apana jivan samaapt kar liya. google ne unke 136wein janmdin ko ek doodle ke saath manaaya.
