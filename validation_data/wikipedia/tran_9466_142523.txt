 
 wilis tower sanyukt rajya america ke chicago illinois shahar main sthit ek gaganchumbi imaarat he. is imaarat ke nirmaan ke liye agast 1970 main sears, roibuk and company ko niyukt kiya gaya. is bhawan kaa nirmaan kaary 1974 main purna hua. yah tower uttari america kii sabase oonchi imaarat hone ke saath-saath 1974 se 1996 ke bich se duniya kii sabase oonchi imaarat thi. is 108 manzili imaarat kii oonchai aadhaar se lekar chat tak 442 meter he.
 is tower ke nirmaan se pehle vishwa vyapaar centre duniya kii sabase oonchi thi. 1996 main kualalampur, malaysia main sthit petronas twin towers duniya kii sabase oonchi imaarat ban gai. imaarat ke shikhar par lage do televijan antina kii badaulat ciers tower abhi bhi jameen se lekar shikhar tak duniya kii sabase oonchi imaarat he. jameen se lekar antina tak kii oonchai 527 meter he.
 khabar ke anusaar, insurance sevaaon ke liye jane jaani vaali landan kii company wilis group ne varsh 2009 ke shuru main tower ke namakaran kaa adhikaar sears prabandhan se jeet liya tha. tower kii mukhya kirayedar sears roibuk and company thi, jo varsh 1992 main yahaan se chali gai thi. vartmaan main is imaarat kaa malikana haq american landmark praparties of scoki ke paas he. wilis group kii yojana yahaan 50 manzili hotel kholane kii he.
