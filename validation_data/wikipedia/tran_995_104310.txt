 abhiyantriki evam praudyogiki santhaan ), madhya pradesh ke pramukh abhiyantriki mahavidhyalayo me se ek he. isaki sthapana san 1996 main akhil bhartiya takneekee shiksha parishad evam vishvavidhyalay anudaan ayog ke dishanirdeshaanusaar hui thi. yah swashaasi santhaan he aiiti bhartiya takneekee shiksha socity, nai dilli ke ek sansthagat sadsy he. aiiti kaa parisar khandwa road par rashtriya soyabean anusandhan centre ke saamne sthit he
 sthapana ke baad aiiti kaa vistaar drut gati se hua he. aiiti ki gananaa madhya bharat main takneekee shiksha pradaan karne vaale sarvouttam kendron main kii jaati he. san 2002 se aiiti me do snatakottar kaarykram shuru kiye gayi, vartmaan main yahan teen snatakottar kaarykram evam chaar visheshgy kaarykram yojit kiye ja rahe he.
