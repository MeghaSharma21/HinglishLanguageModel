 karnam malleshwari bharat kii mahila bharottolak hai. ve olympic khelon main kaansya padak jeetne vaali pratham mahila khilaadi hai.
 karnam malleshwari kaa janm 1 june,1975 ko shrikakulam, andhra pradesh main hua tha. unhone apane kariyar kii shuruyaat junior vetalifting championships se kii, jahaan unhone number ek paaydaan par kabja kiya. 1992 ke asian championships main malleshwari ne 3 rajat padak jite. vaise to unhone vishwa championships main 3 kaansya padak par kabja kiya he, kintu unki ko sabase badi kamyaabi 2000 ke sidney olympic main mili, jahaan unhone kaansya par kabja kiya or isi padak ke saath ve olympic main padak jeetne vaali pehali bhartiya mahila bani.
 malleshwari ne 1997 main shaadi kii or pratispardhi vajan uthaane se alp viraam hetu yamunanagar, haryana chali ai. 1998 main bainkak asian khelon main bhaag lene ke lee jab lauti to rajat padak jeeta. haalanki, ve 1999 ke athens vishwa bharottolan championships main padak jeetne main asamarth rahi, kintu 2000 ke liye bharat kii olympic team main vivaad ke baavjood unhone kaansya padak par kabja jamaaya.
