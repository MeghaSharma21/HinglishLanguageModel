 ekal swaamitvekal swaamitv ek prakaar kaa vyavsaayik sanghathan he. iskaa arth he- 'ek vyakti kaa swaamitv'. arthath ek hi vyakti vyavsaay kaa swami hota he. is prakaar ekal swaamitv vah vyapaar sanghathan he, jisamen ek hi vyakti swami hota he or vyavsaay se sambandhit sabhi kaaryakalapon kaa prabandhan or niyantran usee ke haath main hota he. ekal vyavsaay ke swami or sanchaalak ‘ekal swami’ yaa ‘ekal vyavsaayee’ kehalaate hai. ekal vyavsaayee apane vyavsaay se sambandhit sabhi sansadhano ko jutakar unhen yojanabaddh dhang se vyavasthit karata hai tathaa laabh kamaane ke ekmaatr uddeshy se sari gatividhiyon kaa sanchaalan karata he. ise - je. el. hanson is tarah paribhaashit kiya he ekal vyapaaree vyavsaay ek asi vyavsaayik ikai he jisamen ek hi vyakti puunji lagaataa he, udhyam kaa jokhim uthaata he va prabandhan karata hai.. 
 ekal swaamitv vyavsaay swaroop kii nimnlikhit visheshtaayen hai -
 bharat main ekal swaamitv bahut prachlit he. iske nimnlikhit laabh hai :
 upar bataae gaye gunon ke kaaran ek vyakti dwaara sanchalit vyavsaay sanghathan vyavsaay kaa sabase acha swaroop he. lekin anya sanghathanon ke samaan isaki kuch simaaen bhi hai.
