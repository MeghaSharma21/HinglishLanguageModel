 dilip buvaa mumbai kaa ek kukhyat apradhi tha jo daud ibrahim ke di company ke liye kaam karata tha. mumbai police ne ise 1991 lokhandwala complex golibari main iske anya apradhi saathiyon ke saath maar giraaya. shoot out at lokhandwala inke aparaadhik jivan par aadhaarit hai, jisame tushaar kapur dilip buvaa kii bhoomika main hai.
 maaya dolas bas laaparvaah or abhadra boli vaala vyakti tha. dilip buvaa nirdayee tha. dolas ke vipareet kuch bhi buvaa ke dhyaan ko hataa nahin saktaa tha jab uski ungliyaan triggar par hoti thi. vah chote rajan kaa paramarshdaata tha. samachar madhyamon main uski golibari kii khabaren dolas ko mazaa de rahi thi. “yah baat sachai se kaafii dur he. dolas parisar ke andar se yun hi gaali-galoch die ja rahaa tha jabaki bava baahar bhid rahaa tha. main maaya dolas ke bare main uski khyaati ke sivaa kuch bhi nahin jaanata. buvaa kuch or hi tha. vah daud ibrahim kaa bahtareen nishanebaaz tha. aaj tak vah apana koi sani nahin rakhata.” 
 
  – aafatab ahamed khan, shoot out at lokhandwala ke samay ke prabharee aatangvadavirohi daste ke prabharee adhikari
