 wasp  maarval comics dwaara prakashit america comic pustakon main dikhaai dene vaali ek kaalpanik charitra he. stan lee, arni heart or jaik kirbi dwaara nirmit yah charitra sarvapratham tels tu esthonish #44 main dikhaai di. wasp hank pim kii patni he, tathaa avangers kii ek sansthaapak sadsy he. vah pim dwaara nirmit wasp suut kii sahaayta se aakaar main choti hokar udne ke aktirikt baio-energy blasts kaa upyog bhi karti he.
 may 2011 main, wasp ne ijien ke "top 100 comic book hiroj of ol time" kii suchi main 99 waan sthaan praapt kiya, or phir 2012 main use "top 50 avengers" kii suchi main 26 wein sthaan par rakhaa gaya tha. comics byrs guide kii "100 sexest vimen in comics" suchi main yah charitra 94 wein sthaan par tha. 2013 main maarval.com ne wasp ko paanchavin sarvshereshth avanger mana tha.
 jenet vine dian kaa charitra 2015 kii film ant-main main ek chemiyo upasthiti main dikhaa tha, jiske baad 2018 ke iske sikval aint-main and da wasp main is charitra kii mukhya bhoomika rahi, jise abhinetri mishael pefifer ne nibhaayaa tha.
