 swatantra olympic khilaadi olympic khelon main bhaag lene vaale wo khilaadi hai jo kisi bhi desh kii ore se nahin khelte balki swatantra rup se in khelon main bhaag lete hai. aisa kai kaarnon se ho saktaa he jaise kisi desh kii rashtriya olympic samiti, anterrashtriya olympic samiti dwaara manyata praapt naa hon yaa khelon ke dauraan uski manyata radd kar di gai ho yaa phir kisi anya kaaran se kisi desh ke khilaadi apane rashtriya dhvaj tale khelon main bhagidaari naa kar saken.
 in swatantra olympic khilaadiyon kii team kaa namakaran or rashtriya code nirdhaaran samanurup nahin rahaa he.
