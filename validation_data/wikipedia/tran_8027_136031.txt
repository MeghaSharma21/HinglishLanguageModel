 rasinic vishalkan ek takanic he jisase twachaa ke rang-rup ko behatar banaaya jaataa he. iskaa upyog praayah chehre kii twachaa ko sundar banane main kiya jaataa he. iske liye praayah ek rasinic vilyan kaa upyog kiya jaataa he jo mrit twachaa ko hataane main madad karti he. iske uparant viksit nayi twachaa pehle se adhik chikani hoti he.
 aajkal bazaar main kai tarah ke rasinic pil uplabdh hai, iname se glicolic acid pil sabase kaam hanikaarak evam sabase zyada lokapriy hai. iskaa mukhya kaaran hai glicolic acid kaa prakritik hona hai. yah mukhyat ganne main paaya jaataa hai evam yah alpha hydroxi acid samooh kaa pratinidhitiv karata hai. glicolic acid seb ke mask main bhi paaya jaataa he. yah alpha hydroxi acid samooh kaa pramukh avyay hai.
 glicolic acid pil kaa saundarya prashaadhan main bahut adhik upyog kiya jaataa hai. yah twachaa ke upree sataha main jami dhul evam gandagi ko saafe karata hai. prakritik tatwon se bane hone ke kaaran isaki lokapriyata kaaphi adhik hai. haalanki ab kritim taur par bhi inkaa nirmaan hone laga hai. glicolic acid kaa upyog kai tarah ke utpaadon main hota hai iname phase clinzars, serms, moischarigers evam aankhon kaa kriim shaamil he.
 glicolic acid kaa ek pramukh rup se kiya jane vaala upyog he glicolic fatial. yah ek prakaar kaa twachaa dekhbhaal upachaar he, jahaan alpha hydroxi acid ko twachaa par lagaaya jaataa he taaki gandigi or tel ko hataaya ja sake or line, jhurriyaan or yahaan tak ki bahya twachaa tone ko kam kiya ja sake. inhe chehre par lagaakar kuch der chhod dete hai evam kitni der tak chehre par laga kar rakhana hai yah utpaada main glicolic acid pil kii maatra nirbhar karata hai. yah twachaa ke bhitari hisson par pahunchakar twachaa ko santulit karata hai.
 iske upyog se twachaa kaa kam nuksaan hota hai, inke upyog kaa twachaa par turant hi laabhadaayak asar dikhne lagata hai. yadpi is prashaadhan ke kai saare faayde hai par inke upyog main saavdhaanee baratna zaruri he anyathaa kai nuksaan bhi ho sakate hai. atah jahaan tak sambhav hofetial ko unhi logon dwaara karwaanaa chahie jo prashikshit peshewar hote he.
