 dina shihabi philistini mul ke ek saudi arab kii abhinetri, sanyukt rajya main kaam kar rahi he. wo 2011 se lekar 2016 tak kuch short or drama filmon main najar aa chuki hai.
 dina shihabi kaa janm riyaad, saudi arab main hua tha, or saudi arab main uthaaya gaya tha, beyrut or dubai. unhone 6 saal kii umr main nrity kakshaen shuru kii thi, or 13 saal kii umr main unhone dubai community theatre or arts centre chaliv studio main ek prasiddh dance teacher sharmila kaamate se nrity sabak lenaa shuru kiya, jise united kii "daancing rani" kaha jaataa he. arab emirate. vah apni aadhunik peshewar nrity team kaa sadsy ban gai. 
 dubai main unhone al mavkeb skool, emirate international skool or dubai american academy main bhaag liya. shihabi ne kai skool naatakon main pradarshan kiya, or ek abhinay karier kaa peechha karne ke liye unke shikshak, nancy mock, dwaara protsaahit kiya gaya. 
 18 saal kii umr main, apane pita kii anumati ke saath, dina neuyork shahar main chali gai or abhinay main karier kaa peechha karnaa shuru kar diya; yah 2010 main uske liye ek purnakalik pesha ban gaya. 2011 main, vah kala ke liye NYU Tisch skool main Juilliard or snaatak abhinay kaarykram donon main sweekaar kiya gaya tha. haalanki, dina ne 4 saal kii bachaler kii degree main bhaag nahin lee thi, lekin enayuyu ne bachaler kii degree kii aavashyaktaa ko maaf kiya or 2014 main unhone mene se snaatak kiya. vah juliard or envaiyu snaatak abhinay kaaryakramon donon ke liye sweekaar karne vaali pehali madhya purv kii mahila he. 
 shihabi kii mul arabi boli laventin arabi kaa ek rup he.
