 mahilaon kii big bash lig australian mahilaon kii gharelu twanti-20 cricket pratiyogita he.
 WNCL australian mahila twanti -20 cap he, jo 2014-15 ke mausam ke madhyam se varsh 2007-08 ke satr WBBL se bhaag gaya badal diya. pratiyogita aath shahar aadhaarit frenchiji, purushon kii big bash lig main frenchiji ko hoobahoo branded kii suvidha he. pratiyogita kaa udghaatan 2015-16 sijan ke dauraan maichon main se ek number network ten, free-tu-air network dwaara prasaarit kiya gaya. namakaran adhikaar WBBL ke liye praayojak rebel sport he. maujuuda champion sidney thunder hai.
 jaldi 2014 main, ek antarrashtriya mahila tii-20 pratiyogita ke gathan, indian premier lig kii frenchiji model ke aasapaas aadhaarit kii ghoshnaa kii thi. purv australian cricketer lisa sthalekar or australian vyavsaayee shaun martin ke netritv main yah prastaav kiya gaya tha ki chah tiimon, singapore main sthit, sabhi nijee swaamitv main kiya jayega, or khilaadiyon ko mausam ke anusaar $US40,000 se adhik kamai hai.
 avadharana ke liye sheersh mahila khilaadiyon se majboot samarthan tha, or samarthan, antarrashtriya cricket parishad se maang kii thi, jabaki purv antarrashtriya cricketer jyoff lawson or clive loyad jahaan sanghathan ke board par.
 avadharana june ke shuru main ek jhatka he, pesh kiya gaya tha jab england or wales cricket board ne ghoshnaa kii ki ve kendriya anubandhit khilaadiyon angreji reeha karne ke liye manaa kar diya. ek hi samay main, cricket australia yah bhi toornament ke liye apane khilaadiyon ko relies karne ke liye manaa kar diya hoga kii ghoshnaa kii. donon sanghathanon chintaa yah he ki toornament ke kendra main ek rashtriya cricket board dwaara chalaaya nahin kiya ja rahaa tha, lekin ek nijee company vyakt kiya.
 mahilaon kii big bash lig kii sthapana se pehle, cricket australia ke ek rashtriya twanti-20 pratiyogita, australian mahila twanti-20 cap kaa aayojan kiya. toornament ke antim twanti-20 big bash or big bash lig ke saath ek dabl header ke rup main khelaa ja rahaa he ke saath, mahila rashtriya cricket lig, australian rashtriya mahila simit overon kii pratiyogita ke saath sangyojan ke rup main bhaag gaya.
 pratiyogita kuch pradarshnee khel ke saath 2007-2008 or 2008-2009 main yojit kiya ja rahaa, 2014-2015 ke mausam ke liye 2009-2010 ke mausam se bhaag gaya. pratiyogita 2014-2015 ke mausam ke baad ghaav tha mahilaon kii big bash lig ke liye rasta banane ke liye.
 australia ke purv rashtriya captain belinda clark ek mahila bibiel ke liye 19 janvari 2014 kii yojana par pataa chalaa apni praarambhik avasthaa main tha, lekin yah bahut jald hi ek vaastaviktaa ban saktaa he. ve bibiel 03 ke mausam main televijan reating main baari vruddhi or mahila cricket kii lokapriyata main vruddhi ke liye yah vichaar kar rahe hai.
 par 19 farvari 2015, cricket australia ne ghoshnaa kii ki ek mahila big bash lig vartmaan purushon kii pratiyogita ke liye gathbandhan dalon ke saath, mausam 2015/2016 main shuru hogi. tiimon ke naam or maujuuda purushon kii bibiel tiimon ke rang kaa hissaa he, jiskaa arth he ki vahaan sidney or melborn main se prayek se do timen hongi jayega, jabaki brisben, perth, edilade or hobart main se prayek se ek team. praaroop or toornament ke drow abhi tak saamne ujagar ho rahe hai.
 cricket australia ke siio james sadarland ek media vijyapti main kaha, "ham mahilaon ke khel ke premium ke rup main tii-20 praaroop ko dekhte hai or WBBL ek romaanchak avadharana he ki sanvardhan or mahila cricket ke jokhim main vruddhi hogi". sadarland ne yah bhi kaha, "hamaari maujuuda mahila gharelu pratiyogitaaon yakeenan, duniya main sabase majboot hai sheersh sthaan par raheen mahila team, kaamanwealth bank dakshini sitare, ki ke liye ek vasietnama ke nirantar safalataa ke saath". cricket australia ke kaaryakari mike makenon ne kaha, "hamaara lakshya cricket ke liye ki kya pratibhaagiyon yaa prashansakon ke rup main desh bhar main mahilaon or ladakiyon ke liye chunaav kaa khel ban gaya, dekhne ke liye hai." 13 oktober 2015, australia ke abhijaat varg ke saath shaamil ho gaye, australia main cricket ke vikaas kii disha main $20 million pratigya karne, jamini str cricket madad karne ke liye purv khilaadiyon kaa samarthan or mahila cricketron ke liye aage ke avasaron kaa vikaas cricketron ke 100 par.
 pratiyogita main ek hi aath shahar aadhaarit frenchiji big bash lig main he ki suvidha he. prayek rajya kii rajdhani se ek team suvidhaon ke saath sidney or melborn do kii visheshta he.
