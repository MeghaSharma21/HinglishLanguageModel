 namada ek prakaar kaa kapadaa he jo oon, bal, vanaspatik reshon tathaa par se banaaya jaataa he. yah kampanarodhi, ooshmaa pruthgnyaasak tathaa dhvanishamak hota he. iskaa upyog rail or jahaaj kii chat banane, shishe or sangemarmar kii vastuon kii packing, dhaatuon par polish karne, vaadyayantron, mudran, motaron, juuton, hat tathaa kot main hota he. upyogitanusaar isaki bunaavat main antar ho saktaa he.
 namada mukhyat: do prakaar kaa hota he : 
 pratham prakaar ke namade main ucch namada gunwale oon kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. halkaa namada keval ek parat kaa tathaa baari namada kai paraton ke ek duusare par bunakar banaaya jaataa he. reshevaale namade kii bhi aadharbhoot vastu oon he. isamen oon lambaai main chota or ucch namada gunavala hota he. reshon ko oon ke saath milakar is prakaar kaa namada banaaya jaataa he. kuch vishesh prakaar kaa namada reshon kii kai paraton ko sares se chipkaakar bhi banaaya jaataa he.
