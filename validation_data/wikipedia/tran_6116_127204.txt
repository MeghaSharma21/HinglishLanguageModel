 radha  80 or 90 ke dashak ke shuruuaat kii ek lokapriy bhartiya abhinetri hai. vah ek dashak se adhik samay tak tamil or telugu donon film udyog main lokapriy thim. unhone dakshin bhartiya cinema ke ucch shrenee ke nayakon jaise camel hasan, rajnikant, sathyaraj, prabhu, vijayakanth se lekar chiranjeevi, karthik, suresh, mohan, mohanlal, krishna, venkatesh aadi ke saath kai filmon main kaam kiya he. unhone 5 bhartiya bhashaaon kii 200 se adhik filmon main abhinay kiya he.
 radha kerala ke trivendrum jile ke kallara se taalluk rakhti hai. unhone ek safal hotel vyavsaayee rajasekaran nair se shaadi kii or unki do betiyaan hai. apni shaadi ke baad vah mumbai main bas gai or apane pati ke saath restaraan kaarobaar main he. vah apni badi beti ke saath shastreey nrity sikhti he. unhone haal hi main prasiddh chidambaram natarajar mandir main pradarshan kiya he. unki bahan ambika bhi ek abhinetri he. unki beti karthika nair ne josh film main naag chaitanya ke vipareet abhinetri kii bhoomika ke saath apane karier kii shuruyaat kii or ab vah makarmanju naam kii ek malayalam film va ayaan ke saath keo naamak tamil film kar rahi hai jiske nirdeshak anand hai.
 radha ko nideshak bharthiraja ke dwaara dhundha gaya tha or kishor yuva kalakaaron ke rup main pradarshit kiya gaya tha or 1981 main navagantuk karthik ke vipareet alagal oivathilai main bhoomika di gayi thim.
 radha ne apana karier bharthiraja kii damdaar hit 'alagal oivathilai' ke saath shuru kiya. vah garv ke saath kahati he ki unhone bharti raja kii teen lagaataar filmon alagal oivathilai, tik tik tik or katahal ovium main kaam kiya tha. karier kii shuruuaat ke saath, jab tak unhone swayan filmon ko chhodane kaa nirnay nahin liya, tab tak vah safal hi raheen.
 gopurangal saivathillaai ne uski abhinay pratibha ko sabit kar diya. unhone mohan kii duusari patni kii ek shaktishaalee bhoomika nibhaai or film main rashtriya puraskaar vijeta suhasini ke samakaksh abhinay kiya. radha ne camel hasan abhinit, bade bajet kii film tik tik tik main ek choti si bhoomika adaa kii. haalanki tik tik tik main radha kii bhoomika choti thi, lekin isse radha ko phayda hua or vah bade bajet kii filmon main aane lageen, is film ne unke karier main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai. iske baad unhone camel hasan abhinit kai filmon main kaam kiya jaise oru cathiin diary, thungathey thambi thungathey, cathal parisu, jappanil kalyanaraman. unki kareebi dost or guru abhinetri suhasini maniratnam ne unke mechap main sudhaar laane main unki sahaayta kii or jab bhi un donon ne saath main kai telugu/tamil filmon main kaam kiya to vah swayan hi radha ke bal banaati thi.
 unke jivan kaa pramukh mode unke guru bharthiraja se mila. radha ko shivaji ganeshan ke vipareet film main bhoomika di gayi. muthal mariyaathi varsh 1985 main jari hui. muthal mariyaathi ke baad unako nyaay tarasu jaisi filmon main chunauteepurn bhoomikaayen nibhaane kaa avasar mila.
 us samay unki bahan ambika bhi bahut prasiddh abhinetri thim. unhone ankeyo ketta kural or velli rojaa jaisi kuch filmon main ek saath kaam kiya.
 unhone apni pehali telugu film mistar.vijay main sobhan babu ke saath abhinay kiya. unhone tollywood filmon main zyada kaam kiya. yamudiki mogudu, state roudi aadi ullekhneey telugu filmein thim.
 radha ne anekon kannada filmon main bhi kaam kiya he. unhone anbhavi kannada abhinayta vishnuvardhan or abhinetri lakshmi ke saath saubhaahgya lakshmi main abhinay kiya, jo hindi film maang baro sajnaa kii rimek thi, usamen radha ne abhinetri rekha kii bhoomika kii he. hindi film maang baro sajnaa, ek telugu film kii rimek thi. yahaan tak ki unhone ravichandranke saath bhi filmein kii he. 1991 main, unhone ek kannada film ranchandi kii thi jisame vah sah abhinayta sharat babu, ramesh bhatta, mukhyamantri chardru, sudhir or anya ke saath mukhya bhoomika main thi. unhone is film main ek police adhikari kii bhoomika kii thi, sharat babu unke pati kii bhoomika main the or mukhyamantri chardru pramukh khalanaayak the, unhone is film main kai stant or ladaai ke drishy bhi kiye the. kannada film ke darshak aaj tak is film ko yaad karte hai. yah film kannada udyog main ek badi hit film rahi thi.
