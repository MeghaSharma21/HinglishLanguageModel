 janakapur nepal kaa prasiddh dharmik sthal he ye nagar pracheen kaal main mithila kii rajdhani mana jaataa he. yahan par prasiddh raja janak the. jo sita mata ke pita the. yah shahar bhagwaan ram kii sasuraal ke rup main vikhyath he.sandarbh truti: tag ke liye samaapti tag nahin mila yatha : janakapuradakshinanshe saptkosh-vyatikramen. mahagrame gahasch janakasy vai..
 janakapur kii khyaati kaise badi or use raja janak kii rajdhani log kaise samajhane lage, iske sanvandh main ek anushruti prachlit he. kaha jaataa he ki pavitra janak vansh kaa karaal janak ke samay main naitik adhya:patan ho gaya. kautilya ne prasangvash apnirthshaastra main likhaa he ki karaal janak ne kamandh hokar brahman kanya kaa abhigaman kiya. isi kaaran vah vandhu-bandhavon ke saath maaraa gaya. ashwaghosh ne bhi apane granth buddha charitra main isaki pushti kii he. karaal janak ke bad ke pashchaat janak vansh main jo log batch gaye, ve nikatwarti tarai ke jangalon main ja chupe. jahaan ve log chhipe the, vah sthaan janak ke vanshajon ke rahane ke kaaran janakapur kahalaane laga.
 ramayana katha ke anusaar janak raajaaon main sabase prasiddh sirdhvaj janak hue. yah bahut hi vidwaan evam dharmik vichaaron vaale the. shiv ke prati inaki agaadh shraddha thi. inaki bhakthi se prasann hokar bhagwaan shankar ne inhen apana dhanush pradaan kiya tha. yah dhanush atyant baari tha. janak kii putree sita dharmaparayan thi vah niyamit rup se puja sthal kii saaf-safai swayan karti thi. ek din kii baat he janak ji jab puja karne aae to unhone dekha ki shiv kaa dhanush ek haath main liye hue sita puja sthal kii safai kar rahi hai. is drishy ko dekhakar janak ji aashcharya chakit rah gayi ki is atyant baari dhanush ko ek sukumaari ne kaise uthaa liya. isi samay janak ji ne tay kar liya ki sita kaa pati vahi hoga jo shiv ke dwaara diye gayi is dhanush par pratyancha chadhaane main safal hoga.
 apni pratigya ke anusaar raja janak ne dhanush-yajna kaa aayojan kiya. is yajna se sampurna sansar ke raja, maharaja, rajkumaar tathaa vir purushon ko aamantrit kiya gaya. samaaroh main ayodhya ke raja dasharath ke putr ramchandra or lakshmana apane guru vishwamitra ke saath upasthit the. jab dhanush par pratyancha chadhaane kii baari ai to vahaan upasthit kisi bhi vyakti se pratyancha to dur dhanush hilaa tak nahin. is sthiti ko dekh raja janak ko apane-aap par bada kshobh hua. goswami tulsidas ne ramcharita maanas main likhaa he-
 "ab ani kou maakhai bhat maani, vir viheen mahi main jaani
 tajahu aas nij-nij grih jaahu, likhaa na vidhi vaidehi bibausukritu jaai jau pun pahiharoon, kuunri kuari rahau kaa karuun
 jo tanteuun binu bhat bhuvibhai, tau panu kari hoteuun na hansaai"
 raja janak ke is vachan ko sunakar lakshmana ke aagrah or guru kii ajna par ramchandra ne jyon hi dhanush par pratyancha chadhaai tyon hi dhanush teen tukdon main vibhakt ho gaya. baad main ayodhya se baraat aakar ramchandra or janak nandini janaki kaa vivaah magh sheersh shukla panchami ko janakapuri main sampann hua. kehete hai ki kalantar main treta yugakalin janakapur kaa lop ho gaya. kareeb saadhe teen sau varsh purv mahatma surakishor das ne janaki ke janmasthal kaa pataa lagaaya or murti sthapana kar puja prarambh kii. tatpashchaat aadhunik janakapur viksit hua.
 naulakha mandir: janakapur main ram-janaki ke kai mandir hai. inamen sabase bhavy mandir kaa nirmaan bharat ke tikamgarh kii maharani vrishabhanu kumari ne karavaaya. putr prapti kii kaamana se maharani vrishabhanu kumari ne ayodhya main 'kanak bhawan mandir' kaa nirmaan karavaaya parantu putr praapt na hone par guru kii ajna se putr prapti ke liye janakapuri main 1896 i. main janaki mandir kaa nirmaan karavaaya. mandir nirmaan prarambh ke 1 varsh ke andar hi vrishabhanu kumari ko putr praapt hua. janaki mandir ke nirmaan hetu nau lakh rupye kaa sankalp kiya gaya tha. falswaroop use 'naulakha mandir' bhi kehete hai. parantu iske nirmaan main 18 lakh rupaya kharch hua. janaki mandir ke nirmaan kaal main hi vrishabhanu kumari ke nidhanoparant unki bahan narendra kumari ne mandir kaa nirmaan kaary puraa karavaaya. baad main vrishabhanukumaari ke pati ne narendra kumari se vivaah kar liya. janaki mandir kaa nirmaan 12 varshon main hua lekin isamen murti sthapana 1814 main hi kar di gai or puja prarambh ho gai. janaki mandir ko daan main bahut-si bhoomi di gai he jo isaki aamadani kaa pramukh strot he. janaki mandir parisar ke bheetar pramukh mandir ke peeche janaki mandir uttar kii ore 'akhand kirthan bhawan' he jisamen 1961 i. se lagaataar seetaram naam kaa kirthan ho rahaa he. janaki mandir ke bahari parisar main lakshnan mandir he jiskaa nirmaan janaki mandir ke nirmaan se pehle bataaya jaataa he. parisar ke bheetar hi ram janaki vivaah mandap he. mandap ke khambhon or duusari jagahon ko milakar kul 108 pratimaaen hai.
 vivaah mandap : is mandap main vivaah panchami ke din puri reeti-rivaaj se ram-janaki kaa vivaah kiya jaataa he. janakapuri se 14 kilometre 'uttar dhanusha' naamak sthaan he. bataaya jaataa he ki ramchandra ji ne isi jagah par dhanush todaa tha. pathar ke tukade ko avshesh kaha jaataa he. puure varmbhar khaaskar 'vivaah panchami' ke avasar par tirthayatriyon kaa tantaa laga rahata he. nepal ke mul nivaasiyon ke saath hi apane desh ke bihar, uttar pradesh, madhya pradesh, pashchim bengal, rajasthan tathaa maharashtra rajya ke anginat shraddhalu nazar aate hai. janakapur main kai anya mandir or talab hai. prayek talab ke saath alag-alag kahaniyaan hai. 'vihaar kunda' naam ke talab ke paas 30-40 mandir hai. yahan ek sanskrit vidhyalay tathaa vishvavidhyalay bhi he. vidhyalay main chaatro ko rahane tathaa bhojan kii nickshulk vyavastha he. yah vidhyalay 'gyanakup' ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
 mandap ke chaaron ore chaar chote-chote ‘kohbar’ hai jinamein sita-ram, mandavi-bharat, urmila-lakshmana evam shrutikeerti-shatrughra kii murtiyon hai. ram-mandir ke vishay main janashruti he ki anek dinon tak surakishoradasji ne jab ek gaay ko vahaan doodh bahaate dekha to khudaai karaayi jisamen shriram kii murti mili. vahaan ek kutiyaa banaakar uskaa prabhaar ek sanyasi ko saunpa, isliye adyaparyant unke ram mandir ke mahant sanyasi hi hote hai jabaki vahaan ke anya mandiron ke vairaagi hai.
 iske atirikt janakapur main anek kunda hai yatha- ratna sagar, anuraag sarovar, sitakund ityadi. unamem sarvapramukh he pratham arthath ratnasagar jo janaki mandir se kareeb 9 kilometre dur ‘dhanukha’ main sthit he. vahiin shriram ne dhanush-bhang kiya tha. kaha jaataa he ki vahaan prayek pachchis-tees varshon par dhanush kii ek vishaal akruti banati he jo aath-das dinon tak dikhaai detii he.
 mandir se kuch dur ‘doodhmati’ nadi ke bare main kaha jaataa he ki juti hui bhoomi ke kunda se utpann shishu sita ko doodh pilaane ke uddeshy se kamdhenu ne jo dhara bahaayi, usne ukt nadi kaa rup dhaaran kar liya.
 nepal kii rajdhani kathmandu se 400 kilometre dakshin purab main basaa he. janakapur se kareeb 14 kilometre uttar ke baad pahaad shuru ho jaataa he. nepal kii rail seva kaa ekmaatr centre janakapur he. yahan nepal kaa rashtriya hawaii adda bhi he. janakapur jane ke liye bihar rajya se teen raste hai. pehla rail maarg jayanagar se he, doosraa sitamarhi jile ke bhiththamod se bas dwaara he, teesraa maarg madhubani jile ke umagaanu se bas dwaara he. bihar ke darbhanga, madhubani evam sitamarhi jila se is sthaan par sadak maarg se pahunchnaa aasaan he. bihar kii rajdhani patna se iskaa sitamarhi hote huye sidhaa sadak sampark he. patna se isaki duuri 140 kii me he.
 yahan se kathmandu jane ke liye hawaii jahaaz bhi uplabdh hai. yatriyon ke thaharne hetu yahan hotel evam dharmashalaon kaa uchit prabandh he. yahan ke reeti-rivaaj bihar rajya ke mithlanchal jaise hi hai. vaise pracheen mithila kii rajdhani mana jaataa he yah shahar. bhartiya paryatak ke saath hi anya desh ke paryatak bhi kaaphi sankhya main yahan aate hai.
