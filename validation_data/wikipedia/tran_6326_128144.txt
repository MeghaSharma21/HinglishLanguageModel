 kailash chandra meghavaal ek bhartiya rajaneta hai. vartmaan main ve rajasthan vidhansabha ke adhyaksh hai. ve purv main kendriya saamaajik nyaay evam adhikarita rajya mantri rah chuke hai. or ve bhartiya janata party ke purv main rashtriya upaadhyaksh rah chuke he. vartmaan main bhilwara jile ke shahpura vidhansabha kshetra ke vidhayak hai. purv main ve tonk-savai madhopur lok sabha nirvachan kshetra se saansad the. ve rajasthan sarkaar main anek baar mantri pad par rah chuke hai..
 meghavaal kaa janm 22 march 1934 ko udaipur jile ke maajawada gaanv main hua tha. unki praarambhik shiksha udaipur main hui. unhone udaipur ke mohanlal sukhadiya vishvavidhyalayake mahraana bhoopal college se snaatak kiya or yaheen se vidhi snaatak or snatakottar bhi.
