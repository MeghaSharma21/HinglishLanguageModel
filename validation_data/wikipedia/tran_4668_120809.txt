 kanpur main kai darshneey sthal hai. vaise to yah shahar audyogic nagar he, kintu aitihaasik or paryaton sthal bhi yahaan hai.inamen hai: nanarav park, chidyaghar, radha-krishna mandir, sanadharm mandir, kaanch kaa mandir, shri hanuman mandir panaki, siddhanath mandir, jajmau anandeshwar mandir paramat, jageshwar mandir chidyaghar ke paas, siddheshwar mandir choubepur ke paas, bithur saani mandir, mandhana takneekee evam shaikshik santhaan, shri chhatrapati saahuji maharaj vishvavidhyalay, bhartiya takneekee santhaan, harcourt butler takneekee santhaan, chandrasekhar aaazaad krishi vishvavidhyalay kanpur, ganga bairaj ityadi.
 jajmau ko pracheen kaal main siddhapuri naam se janaa jaataa tha. yah sthaan puranic kaal ke raja yayati ke adheen tha. vartmaan main yahaan siddhanath or siddh devi kaa mandir he. saath hi jajmau lokapriy sufi sant makhdoom shah alaul haq ke makbare ke liye bhi prasiddh he. is makbare ko 1358 i. main firoz shah tughlaq ne banwaaya tha. 1679 main kulich khan kii dwaara banavaai gai masjid bhi yahaan kaa mukhya aakarshan he. 1957 se 58 ke bich yahaan khudaai kii gai thi jisamen anek pracheen vasteen praapt hui thi.
 yah mandir je. ke. mandir ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. behad khubsuurati se bana yah mandir je. ke. trust dwaara banwaaya gaya tha. pracheen or aadhunik style se nirmit yah mandir kanpur aane vaale deshi-videshi paryatakon ke aakarshan kaa kendra rahata he. yah mandir mul rup se shriradhakrishna ko samarpit he. iske alaava shri lakshminarayan, shri ardhanarishwar, narmadeshwar or shri hanuman ko bhi yah mandir samarpit he.
 vartmaan main yah mandir paryatakon ke aakarshan kaa kendra ban gaya he. yah khubasurat nakkasidaar mandir kamala tower ke vipareet maheshwari mohaal main sthit he. mandir main tamrachini or kaanch kii sundar sajaavat kii gai he. 
 kamala retreat agriculture college ke pashchim main sthit he. is khubasurat sampada par singhania parivaar kaa adhikaar he. yahaan ek swimming pool bana hua he, jahaan kritrim lehren utpann kii jaati he. yahaan ek park or nahar he. jahaan chidyaghar ke samanaantar boating kii suvidha he. kamala retreat main ek sangrahalay bhi bana hua he jisamen bahut si aitihaasik or puratathvik vastuon kaa sangrah dekha ja saktaa he. yahaan jane ke liye dipti general manager kii anumati lenaa anivaary he.
 phool baag ko ganesh udyaan ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. is udyaan ke madhya main ganesh shankar vidhyaarthee kaa ek mamorial bana hua he. pratham vishwayuddh ke baad yahaan otharapedic rihebilitation hospital banaaya gaya tha. yah park shahar ke bichon bich mal road par bana he.
 1971 main khulaa yah chidyaghar desh ke sarvottam chidyagharon main ek he. kuch samay picnit ke taur par bitaane or jeev-jantuon ko dekhne ke liye yah chidyaghar ek behtareen jagah he.
 1875 main bana yah carch lombardic gothic style main bana hua he. yah carch un angrejon ko samarpit he jinaki 1857 ke vidroh main mrityu ho gai thi. east bengal railway ke vaastukar walter grenville ne is carch kaa dizin taiyaar kiya tha.
 naanaa raav park phool baag se pashchim main sthit he. 1857 main is park main bibighar tha. aazaadee ke baad park kaa naam badalakar naanaa raav park rakh diya gaya.
