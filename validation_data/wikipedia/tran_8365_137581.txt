 hajaari prasad dvivedi hindi ke moulik nibandhkaar, utkrisht samalochak evam saanskrithik vichaardhaara ke pramukh upanyaaskaar the.
 acharya hajaari prasad dvivedi kaa janm shravan shukla ekadshi sanwat 1964 tadnusaar 19 agast 1907 i0 ko uttar pradesh ke baliya jile ke 'aarat dube kaa chhapra', ojhawaliya naamak gaanv main hua tha. inke pita kaa naam shri anmol dvivedi or mata kaa naam shrimati jyotishmati tha. inkaa parivaar jyotish vidya ke liye prasiddh tha. inke pita pan anmol dvivedi sanskrit ke prakand pandit the. dvivedi ji ke bachapan kaa naam vaidyanath dvivedi tha.
 dvivedi ji kii praarambhik shiksha gaanv ke skool main hi hui. unhone 1920 main vasariapur ke middle skool se pratham shrenee main middle kii pariksha uteern kii. iske baad unhone gaanv ke nikat hi parashar brahmacharya ashram main sanskrit kaa adhyayan prarambh kiya. san 1923 main ve vidyadhyayan ke liye kaashi aaye. vahaan ranvir sanskrit paathshaala, kamachha se praveshika pariksha pratham shrenee main pratham sthaan ke saath uteern kii. 1927 main kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay se hiskool kii pariksha uteern kii. isi varsh bhagwati devi se unka vivaah sampann hua. 1929 main unhone intermediat or sanskrit sahitya main shastri kii pariksha uteern kii. 1930 main jyotish vishay main acharya kii oopaadhi praapt kii. shastri tathaa acharya donon hi parikshaon main unhen pratham shrenee praapt hui. 8 navambar 1930 se dvivediji ne shanti niketan main hindi kaa adhyaapan prarambh kiya. vahaan gurudev rabindranath thakur tathaa acharya kshitimohan sen ke prabhaav se sahitya kaa gahan adhyayan kiya tathaa apana swatantra lekhan bhi vyavasthit rup se aarambh kiya. bees varshon tak shantiniketan main adhyaapan ke uparant dvivediji ne july 1950 main kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay ke hindi vibhaag main professor or adhyaksh ke rup main kaarybhaar grahan kiya. 1957 main raashtrapati dwaara 'padmabhushan' kii oopaadhi se sammanit kiye gayi.
 pratidwandviyon ke virodh ke chalate may 1960 main dvivediji kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay se nishkasit kar diye gayi. july 1960 se punjab vishvavidhyalay, chandigarh main hindi vibhaag ke professor or adhyaksh rahe. oktober 1967 main punah kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay main hindi vibhaagadhyaksh hokar laute. march 1968 main vishvavidhyalay ke rector pad par unki niyukti hui or 25 farvari 1970 ko is pad se mukt hue. kuch samay ke liye 'hindi kaa aitihaasik vyakaran' yojana ke nideshak bhi bane. kaalaantar main uttar pradesh hindi granth academy ke adhyaksh tathaa 1972 se aaajeevan uttar pradesh hindi santhaan, lakhanu ke upaadhyaksh pad par rahe. 1973 main 'alok parv' nibandh sangrah ke liye unhen 'sahitya academy puraskaar' se sammanit kiya gaya. 4 farvari 1979 ko pakshaaghaat ke shikaar hue or 19 may 1979 ko brain tumor se dilli main unka nidhan ho gaya.
 dvivedi ji kaa vyaktitv bada prabhaavshaalee or unka swabhaav bada saral or udaar tha. ve hindi, angrezi, sanskrit or bangla bhashaaon ke vidwaan the. bhaktikalin sahitya kaa unhen acha gyaan tha. lakhanu vishvavidhyalay ne unhen di.lit. kii oopaadhi dekar unka vishesh sammaan kiya tha.
 dvivedi ji kii pramukh rachanaayen nimnlikhit hai 
 agast 1981 i0 main acharya dvivedi kii uplabdh sampoorn rachanaaon kaa sankalan 11 khandon main hajariprasad dvivedi granthavali ke naam se prakashit hua. yah pratham sanskaran 2 varsh se bhi kam samay main samaapt ho gaya. dwitiya sanshodhit parivardhit sanskaran 1998 i0 main prakashit hua.
 acharya dvivedi ne hindi bhashaa ke aitihaasik vyakaran ke kshetra main bhi kaam kiya tha. unhone 'hindi bhashaa kaa vrihat aitihaasik vyakaran' ke naam se chaar khandon main vishaal vyakaran granth kii rachana kii thi. isaki pandulipi banaras hindu vishvavidhyalay ke hindi vibhaag ko saunpi gayi thi, parantu lambey samay tak vahaan se iskaa prakaashan nahin hua or antat vahaan se pandulipiyaan hi gaayab ho gayin. dvivedi ji ke putr mukunda dvivedi ko ukt vrihat granth ke pratham khand kii pratikapi mili or san 2011 i0 main is vishaal granth kaa pehla khand hindi bhashaa kaa vrihat aitihaasik vyakaran ke naam se prakashit hua. isi granth ko yathavat granthavali ke 12wein khand ke rup main bhi sammilit karke ab 12 khandon main 'hajariprasad dvivedi granthavali' kaa prakaashan ho rahaa he.
 dvivedi ji ke nibandhon ke vishay bhartiya samskruti, itihaas, jyotish, sahitya vividh dharmon or sampradaayon kaa vivechan aadi he. vargeekaran kii drushti se dvivedi ji ke niabandh do bhaagon main vibhajit kiye ja sakate hai - vichaaratmak or aalochanatmak.vichaaratmak nibandhon kii do shreniyan hai. pratham shrenee ke nibandhon main darshanik tatwon kii pradhanta rahati he. dwitiya shrenee ke niabandh saamaajik jivan sambandhi hote hai.aalochanatmak niabandh bhi do shreniyon main baanten ja sakate hai. pratham shrenee main aise niabandh hai jinamein sahitya ke vibhinn angon kaa shastreey drushti se vivechan kiya gaya he or dwitiya shrenee main ve niabandh aate hai jinamein sahityakaron kii kritiyon par aalochanatmak drushti se vichaar hua he. dvivedi ji ke in nibandhon main vichaaron kii gahanta, nirikshan kii naveenta or vishleshan kii sookshmata rahati he.
 dvivedi ji kii bhashaa parimarjeet khadi boli he. unhone bhav or vishay ke anusaar bhashaa kaa chaynit prayog kiya he. unki bhashaa ke do rup dikhalaai padate hai - pranjal vyaavhaarik bhashaa, sanskritnishtha shastreey bhashaa. pratham rup dvivedi ji ke saamaanya nibandhon main miltaa he. is prakaar kii bhashaa main urdu or angrezi ke shabdon kaa bhi samavesh hua he. dwitiya style upanyaason or saidhantik aalochana ke kram main parilakshith hoti he. dvivedi ji kii vishay pratipaadan kii style adhyapakeey he. shastreey bhashaa rachane ke dauraan bhi pravaah khandit nahin hota.
 dvivedi ji kii rachanaaon main unki style ke nimnlikhit rup milte hai -
  gaveshnatmak style dvivedi ji ke vichaaratmak tathaa aalochanatmak niabandh is style main likhe gaye hai. yah style dvivedi ji kii pratinidhi style he. is style kii bhashaa sanskrit pradhan or adhik pranjal he. vaakya kuch bade-bade hai. is style kaa ek udhaaharan dekhie - lok or shaastra kaa samanvay, grahastha or vairaygy kaa samanvay, bhakthi or gyaan kaa samanvay, bhashaa or samskruti kaa samanvay,nirgun or sagun kaa samanvay, katha or tatv gyaan kaa samanvay, brahman or chandal kaa samanvay, paanditya or apaanditya kaa samanvay, ramcharita maanas shuru se aakhir tak samanvay kaa kavy he.
  varnanaatmak shailidvivedi ji kii varnanaatmak style atyant swaabhaavik evam rochak he. is style main hindi ke shabdon kii pradhanta he, saath hi sanskrit ke tatsam or urdu ke prachlit shabdon kaa bhi prayog hua he. vaakya apekshaakrit bade hai.
  vyangyatmak shailidvivedi ji ke nibandhon main vyangyatmak style kaa bahut hi safal or sundar prayog hua he. is style main bhashaa chalati hui tathaa urdu, farsi aadi ke shabdon kaa prayog miltaa he.
  vyas shailidvivedi ji ne jahaan apane vishay ko vistaarpurvak samajhaayaa he, vahaan unhone vyas style ko apanaaya he. is style ke antargat ve vishay kaa pratipaadan vyaakhyaatmak dhang se karte hai or ant main uskaa saar de dete hai.
 dvivedi ji kaa hindi niabandh or aalochanatmak kshetra main mahatvapurn sthaan he. ve ucch koti ke nibandhkaar or safal aalochak hai. unhone sur, kabir, tulsi aadi par jo vidvattapurna aalochanaayen likhi hai, ve hindi main pehle nahin likhi gayi. unka niabandh-sahitya hindi kii sthai nidhi he. unki samast kritiyon par unke gahan vichaaron or moulik chintan kii chhap he. vishwa-bharti aadi ke dwaara dvivedi ji ne sampadan ke kshetra main paryaapt safalataa praapt kii he.
 acharya dvivedi ji ke sahitya main maanavtaa kaa parishilan sarvatra dikhaai detaa he. unke niabandh tathaa upanyaason main yah drushti vishesh rup se prathit hoti he.
  hajaari prasad dvivedi ko sahitya evam shiksha ke kshetra main san 1957 main padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya.
