 myitcheena, jise sthaneey jingpo bhashaa main myitkeena kehete hai, burma ke uttar main sthit kachin rajya kii rajdhani he. yah burma kii rashtriya rajdhani yangon se 1,480 kimi dur or mandle se 785 kimi dur, iravati nadi ke pashchimi kinare par sthit he. yahan burma kaa uttartam rail stesan bhi sthit he.
 myitcheena main jingpo samudaay kii bahusankhya he, jo kachin log bhi kehalaate hai. inke alaava yahan bahut se bamar log bhi rahete hai. myitcheena main kuch bhartiya, nepali or chini log bhi based hue hai. adhiktar log therwaad buddha mat or isai mat ke anuyaayi hai, lekin kuch sarvatmavaad, hindu dharm va islam ke anuyaayi bhi myitcheena main rahete hai.
