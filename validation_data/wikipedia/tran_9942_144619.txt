 banarsi sari ek vishesh prakaar kii sari he jise vivaah aadi shubh avasaron par hindu striyaan dhaaran karti hai. uttar pradesh ke chandauli, banaras, jonpur], azamgarh, mirzapur or sant ravidasanagar jile main banarsi saadiyaan banaai jaati hai. iskaa kaccha maal banaras se aata he. pehle banaras kii arthvyavastha kaa mukhya stambh banarsi sari kaa kaam tha par ab yah chintajanak sthiti main he. 
 resham kii saadiyon par banaras main bunai ke sang zari ke dizin milakar bunane se taiyaar hone vaali sundar reshmi sari ko banarsi reshmi sari kehete hai. ye paaramparik kaam sadiyon se chalaa aa rahaa he or vishwaprasiddh he. kabhi isamen shuddh sone kii zari kaa prayog hota tha, kintu badhati keemat ko dekhte hue nakli chamakdaar zari kaa kaam bhi joro par chaalu he. inamen anek prakaar ke namoonay banaaye jate hai. inhen 'motiff' kehete hai. bahut tarah ke motifon kaa prachlan chal padaa he, parantu kuch pramukh paramparaagat motiff jo aaj bhi apni banarsi pahchaan banaae hue hai, jaise booti, bootaa, konia, bel, jaal or janglaa, jhaalar aadi.
 banarsi sari kaa mukhya centre banaras he. banarsi sari mubarakapur, mau, khayrabad main bhi banaai jaati hai. yah mana ja saktaa he ki yah vastra kala bharat main mughal baadshaahon ke aagaman ke saath hi ai. pataka, sherwani, pagadi, safa, dupattey, bed-shit, masand aadi ke banane ke liye is kala kaa prayog kiya jaataa tha. chunki bharat main saadiyon kaa prachlan adhik tha iraan, iraq, bukhaara sharif aadi se aae hathkargha ke kaarigaron dwaara vibhinn prakaar ke dizinon ko saadiyon main daalaa jaataa tha yatha bel, booti, anchal evam konia aadi. us samay main resham evam zari ke dhaagon kaa prayog kiya jaataa tha. taane main katan or bane main paat bana prayog kiye jate the jis ke parinaam swaroop vastra ati mulaayam va gafadaar banate the. purv main nakshaa, jaala se saadiyaan banaai jaati thim. uske baad daabi tathaa jecard kaa prayog hone laga jo ki parampara se hatakar mana ja saktaa he or ab yah power-loom ke rup main viksit hui maani ja sakati he.banarsi sari banane vaale zyaadaatar karigar musalmaan - ansari hote hai. kavi kabir bhi bunakar the. is sari ke khariddaar gujarati, marwari, rajput or jimmedaar gharaanon ke log hote hai. pracheen samay se hi banarsi saadiyon kaa prayog visheshtaur se vivaah samarohon main dulhan va navvivaahita striyon dwaara prayog kiya jaataa tha or aaj tak yah parampara chalati aa rahi he.
 booti chote-chote tasveeron kii akruti liye hue hota he. iske alag-alag pattern do yaa teen rango ke dhaage kii sahaayta se banaaye jate hai or yadi paanch rang ke dhaagon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he to ise pachranga kaha jaataa he. yah banarsi sari ke liye pramukh aavashyak tathaa mahatvapurn diginon main se ek he. isse sari kii jameen yaa mukhya bhaag ko susajjit kiya jaataa he. pehle rang ko 'hunar kaa rang ' kaha jaataa he. jo saamanytah gold yaa silver dhaage ko ek extra bharani se banaaya jaataa he jiske liye sirki kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. haalaanki aajkal iske liye reshmi dhaagon kaa bhi prayog kiya jaataa he jise minaa kaha jaataa he jo reshmi dhaage se hi banataa he. saamanytah mine kaa rang hunar ke rang kaa hone chahie.
 jab booti kii akruti ko bada kar diya jaataa he to is badi hui akruti ko bootaa kaha jaataa he. chote bade ped-paudhe jiske saath choti-choti pattiyaan tathaa phool lage hon isi akruti ko boote se ubhaara jaataa he. yah ped paudhe bhi ho sakate hai or kuch phool bhi ho sakate hai. gold, silver yaa reshmi dhaage yaa inke mishran se bootaa kadha jaataa he. rangon kaa chayan dizine tathaa aavashyaktaa ke anusaar kiya jaataa he. bootaa sari ke border, pallu tathaa anchal main kadha jaataa he jabaki broked ke angan main ise kadha jaataa he. kabhi-kabhi sari ke kinare main ek khaas prakaar ke boote ko kadha jaataa he, jise yahan ke log apni bhashaa main konia kehete hai.
 jab ek khaas prakaar kii ke boote ko banarsi vastron ke kone main kadha jaataa he to use konia kehete hai. dizine akruti ko is tarah se banaaya jaataa he jisase ve kone ke aakaar main aasaani se aa saken tathaa boote se vastra or acchhee tarah se alankrita ho sake. jin vastron main swarna tathaa chaandi ke dhaagon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he unheen main konia ko banaaya jaataa he. saamanytah konia kaadhane ke riye reshmi dhaagon kaa prayog nahin kiya jaataa he kyonki reshmi dhaagon se shuddh phool pattiyon kaa dizin sahi bhang se nahin ubhar paata. pallu dizine ke baad, kone se konia banaaya jaataa he jo ki praaia: aam ke aakaar kaa rahata he jise bananaa bahut kathin hota he kyonki ek saath isamen teen jaalon se bunai kii jaati he. yah ek parmparik kala he. 
 yah ek aari yaa dharidaar phool pattiyon yaa jyamitiya dhang se sajaae gaye dizine hote hai. inhen kshaitij, aade yaa tede mede tareeke se bataaya jaataa he, jisase ek bhaag ko duusare bhaag se alag kiya ja sake. kabhi-kabhi bootiyon ko is tarah se sajaayaa jaataa he ki ve pattee kaa rup le len. bhinn-bhinn jagahon par alag-alag tareeke ke bel boote banaae jate hai. bel sadi ke kinare par lagati he or chaar angul main naapi jaati he. isse ghunght kaa naap tay hota he. bel main phool-pattiyon ke atirikt vibhinn pashu-pakshiyon va maanav akritiyon ke motiff bhi banaae jate hai.
 jaal, jaise naam se hi spasht hota he jaal ke akruti liye hue hote hai. jaal ek prakaar kaa pattern/bandish he, jiske bheetar booti banaai jaati he tathaa ise jaal- janglaa kehete hai. janglaa dizine prakritik tatwon se kaaphi prabhavit he. janglaa katan or taana kaa plane vastra he. taana-bana katan kaa rahata he or dizine ke liye sunahari yaa chaandi kii zari kaa prayog hota he jisamen samast phool, pattey, jaanavar, pakshi ityadi bane hote hai. janglaa, jaal se kaaphi miltaa julata hota he. yadi jungle main minaakaari kerani ho to alag alag rangon ke resham ke dhaagon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. isi prakaar bel janglaa bhi banaaya jaataa he.
 border ke turant baad jahaan kapade kaa mukhya bhaag jise angana kaha jaataa he kii shuruyaat hoti he vahaan ek khaas dizine vastra ko or adhik alankrita karne ke liye diya jaataa he, jise jhaalar kaha jaataa he. saamanytah yah border ke dizine se rang tathaa material main miltaa hota he. jhaalar main tota, mor, paan, kairi, tin patiyaa, paanch patiyaa motive dizine banaae jate hai. jhaalar bel ke aakhir main saadiyon ke alaava jardoji kaa kaam bhi yahan chaalu ho chukaa he. ye banaras kii apni alag parampara nahin he parantu ab logon ne banarsi sari main bhi jardoji kaa kaam karnaa prarambh kar diya he. banaras main banarsi sari tathaa vastra ke alaava anya sari, suut ke kapade, dress materials, pardaa, kushan cover aadi main bhi adhikanshtah muslim sampradaay ke log lage hue hai. jardoji bahut hi kalatmak tathaa dhairya kaa kaam he. isaki baariki ko samajhane ke liye yah jaruri he ki ise kam umr se hi siikhaa jaae. faltah jardoji ke peshey main bachche tathaa auraten bhi lage hue hai.
