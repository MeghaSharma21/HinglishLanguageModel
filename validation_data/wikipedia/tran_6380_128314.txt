 indonesia main 4thi se 15veen sadi ke dauraan bane hindu or buddha mandiron or anya punyasthanon ko java bhashaa main chandi kaha jaataa he. iske atirikt pracheen gaiyr-dharmik nirmaan jaise dwaar, nagareey khandhar or snaan sthanon ko bhi "chandi" kaha jaataa he. yah shabd 'chandika'  yaa pali shabd 'chedi' kaa apbhransh lagata he.
 'chandi' shabd saamanytah bhartiya mandiron kii tarah ekkaksheeya sanrachanaon ke liye prayukt kiya jaataa he, jinamein dwarmandap or iske upar pyramida sheersh ho.
