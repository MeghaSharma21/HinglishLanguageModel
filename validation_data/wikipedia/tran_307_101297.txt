 tromes uttari norge main ek county he. isaki simaaen uttar-purv main finmark county or dakshin-pashchim nordland county se lagati hai. sweden kaa norbaboton lain dakshin main, or isse aage dakshin purv main finland kaa lapland praant sthit he. iske pashchim main norwegian sagar he. 1866 main sthapit yah county sampurna rup se, arctic vrut ke uttar main sthit he. tromes county nagar palika, trams ke logon dwaara chune gaye county kaa shaasi nikaay hai, jabaki tromes county governor raja or norge kii sarkaar kaa pratinidhi he. 2014 main county kii aabaadi 161,771 thi.
