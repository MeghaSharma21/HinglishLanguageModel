 nagar style uttar bhartiya hindu sthapaty kala kii teen main se ek style he. is 
 style kaa prasar himalay se lekar vindhyas parvat maala tak dekha ja saktaa he. vaastushastra ke anusaar nagar style ke mandiron kii pahchaan aadhaar se lekar sarvocch ansh tak iskaa chatushkon hona he. viksit nagar mandir main garbhagrih, uske samaksh kramshah interval, mandap tathaa ardhmandap praapt hote hai. ek hi aksh par ek duusare se sanlagn in bhaagon kaa nirmaan kiya jaataa he.
 'nagar' shabd nagar se bana he. sarvapratham nagar main nimarn hone ke kaaran inhe nagar kii sangya pradaan kii gai. shilpashaastra ke anusaar nagar mandiron ke aath pramukh ang he - 
 nagar style kaa kshetra uttar bharat main narmada nadi ke uttari kshetra tak he. parantu yah kahin-kahin apni simaaon se aage bhi vistaarit ho gayi he. nagar style ke mandiron main yojana tathaa oonchai ko maapdand rakhaa gaya he. nagar vaastukala main vargakar yojana ke aarambh hote hi donon konon par kuch ubhara hua bhaag prakat ho jaataa he jise 'ast' kehete hai. isamen chandi samtal chat se uthati hui shikha kii pradhanta pai jaati he. yah shikha kala uttar bharat main saatvin shatabdi ke pashchaat viksit hui arthath parmaar shaskaon ne vaastukala ke kshetra main nagar style ko pradhanta dete hue is kshetra main nagar style ke mandir banawaaye.
