 nandavanshi shabd nand yaa nandagop naamak prasiddh puranic charitra ke vanshajon kaa parichaayak he. harivansh puraana ke anusaar - nand "paavan gwaal" naam se paribhaashit gopaalak yaa gop jati ke mukhiya the. vasudev navjaat krishna ke janm kii raat ko hi paalan poshan hetu nand ko saunp kar aae the.
 nandavanshi ahir swayan ko nand kaa vanshaj maanate hai. nandavanshi ahir rajasthan main jaipur main paye jate hai.. ve 
 mandla ke kaunra va jabalpur ke kamariya ahir bhi swayan ko nandavanshi samooh kaa maanate hai.shodh adhyayan ke anusaar nandavanshi va yaduwanshi oopaadhiyaan paraspar samanaarthi hai, kyonki uplabdh sroton main yadu naresh vasudev va raja nand rishte main chachere bhai the arthath ek hi vansh ke the. nand kii krishna ke alaava apni koi santan nahin thi atah nand ke vansh kaa uttaradhikaar bhi swatah hi yadu rajkumaar shrikrishna ko unka dattak putr hone ke naate praapt hua.

 ke.em. munshi sahit itihasvidon ke ek samooh kaa mat he ki gurjar jati pracheen jati ahir athwa yadav kii vanshdhar he. gurjaron kaa ek samudaay aaj bhi nandavanshi hone kaa daawa karata he..
