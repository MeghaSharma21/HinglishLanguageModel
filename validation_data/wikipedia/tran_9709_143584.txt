 casini-hoygens ek antariksh yaan mishan he jo san 2004 se hamaare saura mandal ke duusare sabase bade grah shani or uske prakritik upgrahon kaa adhyayan kar rahaa he. yah 1997 main prithvi se roket ke zariye choda gaya or is mishan main shani ke ird-gard kaksha main ghoomane vaala ek kritrim upgrah or shani ke sabase bade chandramaa titan par utarana vaala ek yaan shaamil the. titan par utarane vaale yaan kaa naam 'hoygens' tha or 2005 main vah mukhya casini yaan se alag hokar us upgrah par utraa. casini san 2015 tak shani kii parikrama karke uski tasviren letaa rahegaa or usapar jaankaree ekatrik kar ke prithvi kii ore prasaarit karata rahegaa. 2015 main use shani ke bhayankar vayumandal main giraakar dhwast kaa diya jayega.
