 jain dharm main tirthankar un 24 vyaktiyon ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he, jo swayan tap ke madhyam se atmagyaan praapt karte he. jo sansar sagar se paar lagane vaale tirth kii rachana karte he, vah tirthankar kehalaate hai. tirthankar vah vyakti hai jinhonen puri tarah se krodh, abhimaan, chal, ichha, aadi par vijay praapt kii ho). tirthankar ko is naam se kaha jaataa he kyonki ve "tirth", ek jain samudaay ke sansthaapak hai, jo "payab" ke rup main "maanav ksht kii nadi" ko paar karaata he.
 atmagyaan praapt karne ke baad tirthankar dusron ko aatmakalyaan ke path kaa upadesh dete he. jain siddhanton kaa nirmaan tirthankar ke dharmik shikshan se hua he. sabhi tirthankarasn kii aantarik gyaan sahi he or har sambandh main samaan he, kyonki ek tirthankar kii shikshaayen kisi duusare kii virodhabhas me nahin he. lekin us avadhi ke manushyon kii pavitrataa or aadhyatmik unnati ke anusaar vistaar ke str me vibhinnata he. jitani aadhyatmik unnati or man kii pavitrataa he, utani hi vistaar kii aavashyaktaa kam he.
 apane maanav jivan kii ant-avadhi main ek tirthankar mukti ko praapt karte he, jo anant janm or mrityu ke chakra ko samaapt karata he.
 jain dharm ke anusaar samay kaa aadi yaa ant nahin he. vah ek gaadi ke pahie ke samaan chalata he. hamaare vartmaan yug ke pehle anant sankhya me samay chakra hue he or is yug ke baad bhi anant sankhya me samay chakra hongen. ikkisvi sadi ke aarambh main, ham vartmaan ardh chakra ke paanchaven daur main lagbhag 2,530 wein varsh main hai.
 brahmad ke is bhaag main samay ke prayek ardh chakra main choubees tirthankar janm lete hai. vartmaan main avasarpini ardh chakra main, pehle tirthankar rishabhdev arabon varsh pehle rahe or tisare yug kii samaapti kii ore moksh prapti kii. choubeeswein or antim tirthankar mahavir swami the, jinka astitv ek aitihaasik tathy sweekaar kar liya gaya he. 
 hamaare bhaag vaale brahmad main agle tirthankar kaa janm samay ke agle ardh chakra ke tisare yug ke aarambh main, lagbhag 82,500 varsh main hoga.
 jaise tirthankar atmagyaan ke liye hamen nirdeshit karte hai, jain mandiron main unki murtiyon kii puja atmagyaan praapt karne ke icchhuk jainiyon dwaara kii jaati he. tirthankar ishwar yaa devtaa nahin hai. jain dharm ek nirmaataa ke rup main ishwar ke astitv par vishwaas nahin karata, balki yah maanata he kii praaniyon ke rup main devtaa, manushyon se shreshth he lekin, phir bhi, puri tarah se prabud nahin he.
 sabase mul jain mantra, namokar mantra main panch parameshthiyon main sarvapratham arihanton ko namaskara kiya gaya he. siddh paramatma hai lekin arihant bhagwaan lok ke param upakaarak hai, isliye inhen sarvottam kaha gaya he. ek main ek hi arihant janm lete hai. jain aagmon ko arhat dwaara bhashit kaha gaya he. arihant kevali or sarvajna hote hai. adhatiya karmon kaa naash hone par keval gyaan dwaara ve samast padaarthon ko jaanate hai isliye unhen 'kevali' kaha he. sarvajna bhi use hi kehete hai.
 jis karm ke bandha se tirthankar pad kii prapti hoti he, use namakarm kehete he. jain granthon ke anusaar nimnlikhit solah bhavanaaen tirthankar namakarm ke asrav kaa kaaran he:-
 murtiyan, chhaviyaan, aadi sahit, vibhinn roopon ke chithran main, tirthankarasn kaa pratinidhitiv sadaa unke pair saamne paar asin, ek pair kii ungaliyon dusre ke ghutane ke paas or dahinaa haath bain ke upar god main hota he. keval do kaa pratinidhitiv alag bhang se he: parswanath, tebiswein, jinke upar sanpon kii chhatari he or suparshvanath, saatven, jinka chithran digambar saanp-chhatriyon ke ek chote samooh ke saath karte hai.
 digambaron kaa abhyavedan bilkul nagn he, jabaki shwetambaron kaa vaastrit evam mukut or aabhushanon ke saath sajit he. apane parichar yaksha or yakshini, saath hi unke singhaasana ke takiye par naqkashidaar unase sambandhit chihn ke dwaara ve abhyavedan main ek duusare se or bhi vishisht hai.
 22 tirthankarasn kaa janm ikshvaku vansh main, hua. jain granthon ke anusaar isaki sthapana rishabhdev ne kii thi. munisuvrat, beesvin or neminath, bisawein, harivansh ke kahe jate the.
 rishabh purimtal, neminath girnaar or mahavir rujukula nadi ke tat ke nikat par kevali bane . bees tirthankarasn ne samet shikhar par nirvanu yaa moksh praapt kiya. haalanki rishabhdev, ne himalay ke kailash parvat par nirvana praapt kiya; vasupujya kaa dehanth uttar bengal main champapuri main hua; neminath girnaar parvat par or mahavir, antim, pavapur se.
 kaha jaataa he ki ikkis tirthakaron ko kyotsarg mudra main moksh praapt hua; rishabh, nemi or mahavir ko padmasana par.
 prayek tirthankar kaa sankshep varnan neeche diya gaya hai:
 ye sab tirthankar kshatriya kul main utpann maane jate hai. inamen muniswrut or nemi ne harivansh main tathaa baaki 22 tirthankarasn ne ikshvakuvansh main janm dhaaran kiya. rishabh, nemi or mahavir ko chhod baaki ne sanmedshikhar parvat par nirvana paaya. in tirthankarasn ke bich lakhon-karodon varshon kaa antar bataaya gaya he. udhaaharan ke liye, 20wein tirthankar nemi or 23wein tirthankar parswanath ke bich 84,000 varsh kaa antar tha. parswanath or mahavir ke bich kul 250 varsh kaa antar bataaya gaya he. tirthankarasn kii aayu or unke sharir kii oonchaai bhi aparimit maani gai hai. udhaaharan ke liye, rishabh kii aayu 84 lakh varsh or unke sharir kii oonchaai 500 dhanush thi; nemi kii aayu 1000 varsh or oonchaai 10 dhanush thi. lekin parshv kii aayu 100 varsh or oonchaai 9 haath tathaa mahavir kii aayu 72 varsh or oonchaai keval 7 haath bataai gai he. 
 rishabh ayodhya ke nivasee the or kailash parvat par unhone nirvana praapt kiya. bharat or bahubali naam ke unke do putr tathaa brahmi or sundari naam kii do putriyaan thim. rishabh ne bharat ko shilpakala, bahubali ko chitrakala, brahmi ko aksharlipi tathaa sundari ko ganit kii shiksha di. jain parampara ke anusaar rishabh ne hi sarvapratham aag jalaana, kheti karnaa, bartan bananaa or kapade bunane aadi kii shiksha apni praja ko di. unhen pratham raja, pratham jin, pratham kevali, pratham tirthankar or pratham dharmachakravarti kaha gaya he. bhagwatpurana main rishabhdev kaa ullekh miltaa he. bengal main rishabh kii upasna kii jaati he or unhen avadhoot param tyaagi yogi mana gaya he. nath sampradaay main adinath ko aadiguru sweekaar kiya gaya he. 
 malli kaa janm mithila main hua tha. shwetambar sampradaay main malli ko stritirthankar sweekaar kiya gaya he. lekin digambar, sampradaay main streemukti kaa nishedh he. 
 nami kii gananaa karkandu, durmukh or nagnjit naam ke prayek buddha ke saath kii gai he. kuch log mahabharat ke rajarshi janak ko hi nami maanate hai jinhen jataka granthon main mahajanak kaha gaya he. ramayana or puraanon main nami ko mithila ke rajvansh kaa sansthaapak bataaya gaya he.
 nemi samudravijay ke putr the jinka janm suryapur main hua tha. vasudev ke putr krishna ke ve chachere bhai the. ugrasen kii kanya or kansa kii bahan rajimati ke saath unka vivaah honevaala tha. lekin vivaah ke liye baraat lekar jate hue unhone baratiyon ke bhojanarth ekatrit pashuon kii chitkaar suni or ve turant waapas laut gaye. girnaar parvat par unhone tap kiya or vahiin se nirvana paaya.
 parswanath ke chaaturyam dharm kaa ullekh baudhon ke tripitakon main miltaa he or pracheen jain granthon se maaloom hota he ki mahavir ke mata pita parshv dharm ke anuyaayi the, isaliye parshv ko aitihaasik purush mana jaataa he. bhadrabahu ke kalpasutra ke anusaar inke sangh main hajaaron shishy the .
 mahavir buddha ke samakaleen the. buddha tripitakon main unhen 'nirgrath gyatruputra' naam se ullikhit kiya gaya he. inkaa janm kshatriyakund gram main hua tha or 72 varsh kii umr main pavapuri main karthik krishna 14 ko unka nirvana hua. jainon kii prachlit manyata ke anusaar yah samay vikram sanwat se 470 varsh purv or shaq sanwat se 605 varsh purv baithta he. shwetanvar sampradaay ke anusaar mahavir pehle devananda brahmani ke garbh main avatarit hue, lekin indra dwaara garbh kaa parivartan kar die jane par ve trishla kshatraani kii kokh se utpann hue. unke vivaah ke sambandh main bhi shwetambar or digambar parampara main matbhed paaya jaataa he .
 avasarpinikaal ke samaapt hone par utsarpinikaal aarambh hoga. usamen bhi 24 tirthankar honge. magadh ke raja shrenik, krishna or mankhali goshaal aadi is kaal main tirthakar pad dhaaran karenge. 
 jain tirthankarasn kii bhanti buddha dharm main bhi 24 yaa 25 budhon kaa ullekh miltaa he.
