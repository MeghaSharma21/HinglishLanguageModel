 wain aarekh vah aarekh hai jo samucchyon ke parimit sangrahon ke bich sabhi parikaalpanik rup se sambhav taarkik sambandhon ko darshaate hai. wain aarekh kaa avishkaar 1880 ke aasapaas jon wain dwaara kiya gaya tha. inkaa kai kshetron main upyog kiya jaataa he, jaise samucchya siddhant, prayikta, tark, sankhyikee or computer vigyaan.
 udhaaharan ke liye, teen samucchy A, B or C diye hon, to unka wain aarekh neeche darshaya gaya he-
 wain aarekh
 isi prakaar, greek varnmaala, latin varnmaala, or roosi varnmaala ko teen samucchy mana jaay, to unka wain aarekh saamne darshaya gaya he. isamen dekh sakate hai ki kuch varna jo teenon varnmaalaon main hai, ve teenon vritton ke sarvanishtha kshetra main dikhaaye gayi hai. 
 wain aarekh dwaara samucchyon kaa aapasi sambandh dikhaaya ja saktaa he tathaa usamen samucchyon kaa pratichchhed, sangh tathaa viyojan dikhaa sakate hai.
