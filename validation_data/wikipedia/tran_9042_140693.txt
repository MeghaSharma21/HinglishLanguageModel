 pindari dakshin bharat ke yupriy pathan savaar the. unki utpatti tathaa namakaran vivaadaaspad he. ve bade karmath, saahsee tathaa vafaadaar the. tattu unki sawari thi. talwaar or bhaale unke astr the. ve dalon main vibhakt the or prayek dal main saadhaarant: do se teen hajaar tak savaar hote the. yogyatam vyakti dal kaa sardaar chunaa jaataa tha. uski ajna sarvamaanya hoti thi. pindariyon main dharmik sankeernata na thi. 18veen shatabdi main paasi jati bhi unke sainik dalon main shaamil the. unki striyon kaa rahan-sahan hindu striyon jaisaa tha. unamem-devi devataaon kii puja prachlit thi.
 maraathon kii asthaayee sena main unka mahatvapurn sthaan tha. pindari sardaar nasru ne mughalon ke viruddh shivaji kii sahaayta kii. punapa ne unke uttaradhikariyon kaa saath diya. gajiuddin ne bajirao pratham ko uske uttari abhiyaanon main sahyog diya. chingodi tathaa hul ke netritv main 15 hajaar pindariyon ne panipat ke yuddh main bhaag liya. ant main ve malwa main bas gaye or sindhiyaashahee tathaa holkarshahi pindari kehalaae. hi degree or burran unke sardaar the. baad main chitu, kareem khan, dostamohammad or vasilmohammad sindhiya kii pindari sena ke prasiddh sardaar hue tathaa kaadir khan, tukku khan, sahib khan or shaikh dulla holkar kii sena main rahe.
 pindari savaaron kii kul sankhya lagbhag 50,000 thi. yuddh main luutmaar or vidhwans ke kaary unheen ko saunpe jate the. lut kaa kuch bhaag unhen bhi miltaa tha. shaantikaal main ve khetibari tathaa vyapaar karte the. gujaarey ke liye unhen karmukt bhoomi tathaa tattu ke liye bhatta miltaa tha.
 maratha shaskaon ke saath velesli kii sahaayak sandhiyon ke falswaroop pindariyon ke liye unki sena main sthaan na rahaa. isliye ve dhan lekar anya rajyon kaa sainik sahaayta dene lage tathaa avyavashtha se laabh uthaakar luutmaar se dhan kamaane lage. sambhav he unheen ke bhay se kuch deshi rajyon ne sahaayak sandhiyaan sweekaar kii hon.
 san 1807 tak pindariyon ke dhaave yamuna or narmada ke bich tak simit rahe. tatpashchaat unhone mirzapur se madras tak or urisa se rajasthan tathaa gujarat tak apana kaaryakshetra vistrut kar diya. 1812 main unhone bundelkhand par, 1815 main nizaam ke rajya se madras tak tathaa 1816 main uttari sarkaron ke ilaakon par bhanyakar dhaave kiye. isse shanti evam suraksha jaati rahi tathaa pindariyon kii gananaa luteron main hone lagi.
 is gambhir sthiti se mukti paane ke uddeshy se lard hestings ne 1817 main maratha sangh ko nasht karne ke purv kootneeti dwaara pindari saradaaron main foot daal di tathaa sandhiyon dwaara deshi rajyon se unke viruddh sahaayta lee. phir apane or hisslap ke netritv main 120,000 sainikon tathaa 300 topon sahit unke ilaakon ko gherkar unhen nasht kar diya. hajaaron pindari maare gaye, bandi bane yaa jangalon main chale gaye. chitu ko asorgarh ke jangal main chitah ne kha daalaa. vasil mohammad ne karagaar main aatmahatya kar lee. kareem khan ko gorakhpur jile main ganeshpur kii jaagir di gai. is prakaar pindariyon ke sanghathan tut gaye or ve teetar bitar ho gaye.
