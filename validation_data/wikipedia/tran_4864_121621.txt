 peter heden dinklej ek america abhinayta or film nirmaataa he. unhone paanchavin shrenee main da velvaton rabit se ek bachche ke rup main abhinay karnaa shuru kiya. dinklej ne bennington college main abhinay kaa adhyayan kiya, jisamen kai shaukia str prastutiyon the. unki kariyar kii pehali film living in oblivian main thi or unki safalataa kii shuruyaat kamedi drama da stesan agent ke saath hui thi. unhine baad main kamedi elf, aparaadh film me gilti, superhiro kamedi anderdog, kaalpanik film da chronicals of nornia: prince caspian, super hero film eks-main : days of future past, or prashansit blike kamedi film third billboard outside abing, mizori kii.
 2011 ke baad se, dinklej ne tiorian lenistar ko echbio shrinkhalaa singhaasana ke khel main chitrit kiya he, jiske liye unhone 2011 or 2016 main naatak series main utkrisht sahaayak abhinayta ke liye emi ko praapt kiya, saath hi saath 2011 se 2016 tak lagaataar emi namakan praapt kiya tathaa ve 2012 main sarvshereshth sahaayak abhinayta - series, misriries yaa television film ke liye golden globe se puraskrit hue. dinklej televijan par sabase zyada kamaane karne vaale kalakaaron main se ek he.
 
 peter heden dinklej kaa janm 11 june 1969 ko neujersey ke morristown main, ek beemaa celsman jon karl dinklej or ek praathmik vidhyalay ke sangeet shikshak dyna dinklej, ke ghar main hua. unhen janm se hi acondroplasia tha jo ki bounepan kaa ek saamaanya rup he. dinklej apane mata-pita or bade bhai, jonathan ke saath brukside, new jersey main apane parivaar ke ekmaatr bouna ke rup main bade hue. vah german or irish mul ke hai. ek bachche ke rup main, dinklej or unke bhai ne apane pados main logon ke liye kathputli sangeet prastut kiye. dinklej ne apane vaayalin vadak bhai ko "parivaar kaa asli kalakaar" bataaya he, yah kehete hue ki vaayalin ke liye unke bhai kaa junun hi ekmaatr kaaran tha jo unhen abhinay karne se roktaa tha.
 danklaj ke paanchavin shrenee ke utpaadan da velvaten rabit main unki pehali naatakeey safalataa thi. mukhya kirdaar main vah naatak ke darshakon kii pratikriya se khush the. dinkjej ne ladakon ke liye ek catholic praarambhik skool 'delberton skool' main shiksha grahan kii, jahaan unhone apana abhinay viksit kiya. 1984 main, danklaj ko america naatakkaar sam shepard dwaara likhe gaye naatak 'tru west' se abhinay main bhavishya banane ke liye prerana mili. 
 dinkjej ne bennington college se padhai kii, jahaan unhone naatak kii degree ke liye adhyayan kiya or 1991 main snaatak hone se pehle kai prastutiyon main bhi dikhaai die. iske baad vah ek theatre company banane ke liye apane dost ian bel ke saath neuyork shahar main chale gaye. kiraye kaa bhugtan na karne par, ve apane apartment se baahar nikal gaye. danklaj ne ek purnakalik abhinayta ke rup main kaam karne se pehle chah saal ke liye data processing company main kaam kiya.
 dinklej ne shuru main ek abhinayta ke rup main kaam ko khojane ke liye sangharsh kiya, aanshik rup se iskaa kaaran yah bhi tha ke unhone un bhoomikaaon se inkaar kar diya jo aam taur par unki sthiti vaale abhinaytaon ko peshkash kii jaati hai, jaise "kalpit boune yaa lepracouns". unhone kam bajet vaale swatantra kamedi-drama 'living in oblivian' main apane filmi safar kii shuruyaat kii, jahaan unhone steve busemi ke saath pradarshan kiya. yah film neuyork shahar ke madhya main ek kam bajet vaali swatantra film kii shooting kar rahe nirdeshak, chaalak dal or abhinaytaon kii kahani kahati he. dinklej kii bhoomika ek niraash boune abhinayta kii thi, jo apni ghisi-pity bhoomikaaon ke bare main shikaayat karte hai. film aalochakon dwaara pasand ki gayi. agle saal vah raper tupake shakur abhinit aparaadh naatak 'bullate' main ek imaarat prabandhak ke rup main dikhaai diye. 'living in oblivian' main unke achche pradarshan ke baad bhi, koi bhi dinklej kaa pratinidhi banane ko taiyaar nahi hua. busemi dwaara nirdeshak elexander rockwel ko sifarish ke baad, dinklej ko kamedi film '13 moons' main liya gaya. baad main jab theatre website ke liye saakshaatkaar diya, to unase poocha gaya ki unki adarsh bhoomika kya thi, or unhone kaha "romantic mukhya paatr" jise ladki milti he.
