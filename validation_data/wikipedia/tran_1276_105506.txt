 rail mantralay, bharat main relon ke nirmaan or parivahan ko niyantrit karne vaala bharat sarkaar kaa ek mahatvapurn mantralay he. bhartiya rail kaa prabhaar isi mantralay ke paas he. piyush goyal bharat ke vartmaan rail mantri hai.
 ek kebinet or do rajya mantriyon ko milakar railway ke paas kul teen mantri hai. bhartiya rail kaa sheersh nikaay railway board, rail mantralay ke aadhin aata he. railway board, ek adhyaksh or kai "railway board ke sadsy" dwaara gathit hota he. kai nideshaalay railway board ke aadhin aate hai.rail mantralay kaa mukhyalay nai dilli main sthit rail bhawan he.
 aitihaasik kaarnon se prayek varsh bharat kaa kendriya rail mantri, bharat ke aam bajet se alag, rail bajet prastut karata he. is pratha kii shuruyaat 1924 main hui thi jis samay desh ke kul bajet kaa 70% rail bajet hota tha. is prakaar rail bajet ko aam bajet se alag kar bajet kii prayek praathamiktaaon par samuchit dhyaan diya ja saktaa tha. aaj bharat kaa rail bajet bharat ke rashtriya bajet kaa lagbhag 15% he. rail bajet ke dwaara nai rail sevaaon, kiraayon or shulkon main parivartan aadi kii ghoshnaa kii jaati he. prayek varsh, railway bajet dekhne ke liye uplabdh karaayaa jaataa he.
