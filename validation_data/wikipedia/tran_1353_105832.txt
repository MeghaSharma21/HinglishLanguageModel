 pravasi pakshi ko sthaneey bhashaa main kurjaan kehete hai. kurjaan adhiktar bikaner sambhag or jodhpur sambhag ke gaanvon main talabon par pani pine ke liye aati hai. ye pakshi siberia se iran, afghanistan aadi deshon se hote hue bharat main prativarsh aate hai. chhapar kaa talab or ghana pakshi vihaar bharatpur main ye aana adhik pasand karte hai.
