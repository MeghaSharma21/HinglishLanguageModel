 parmaar yaa panvar vansh madhyakaalin bharat kaa ek agnivanshi rajput rajvansh tha. is rajvansh kaa adhikaar dhar or ujjayini rajyon tak tha.or puure madhyapradesh kshetra main unka samrajya tha. jab daityon kaa atyaachaar badh gaya tab chaar rishione abu parvat par ek yajna kiya us agnikund main se chaar agnivanshi kshatriya nikle. parmaar ,chouhan ,solki ,or pratihaar.unheen ke naam par unka vansh chalaa.rishi vashishtha kii aahuti dene se ek vir purush niklaa. jo agnikundse nikalate samay maar-maar ki trad karata niklaa or agnikund se baahar aate hi par naamak rakshas kaa ant kar diya. esa dekhakar rishi vashishtha ne prasann hokar us vir purush ko parmaar naam se sambodhit kiya. usi ke vansh par parmaar vansh chalaa. parmaar arth hota he par -yani shatru.maar kaa arth hota he-maarna = yani shatru ko maar giraane vaala parmaar
 abu chandravati or malwa ujjain ,dhar parmaar rajvansh kaa pramukh rajya kshetra rahaa he. ye 8veen shatabdi se 14veen shatabdi tak shashan karte rahe.
 【parmaar vansh me do mahaan samraat hue:】
 parmaar panvaar ek rajvansh kaa naam he, jo madhyayug ke praarambhik kaal main mahatvapurn hua. chaaran kathaon main iskaa ullekh rajput jati ke ek gotra rup main miltaa he. parmaar sindhuraj ke darbari kavi padmagupta parimal ne apni pustak 'navsahsaankacharit' main ek katha kaa varnan kiya he. rishi vashishtha ne rishi vishwamitra ke viruddh yuddh main sahaayta praapt karne ke liye bhabu parvat ke agnikund se ek vir purush kaa nirmaan kiya jinke purvaj agnivansh ke kshatriya the.. is vir purush kaa naam parmaar rakhaa gaya, jo is vansh kaa sansthaapak hua or usee ke naam par vansh kaa naam padaa. parmaar ke abhilekhon main baad ko bhi is kahani kaa punarullekh hua he. isse kuch log yon samajhane lage ki parmaron kaa mul nivasasthaan abu parvat par tha, jahaan se ve pados ke deshon main ja jaakar bas gaye. kintu is vansh ke ek pracheen abhilekh se yah pataa chalata he ki parmaar dakshin ke rashtrakooton ke uttaradhikari the.
 parmaar parivaar kii mukhya shakha aathvin shatabdi ke praarambhik kaal se malwa main dhara ko rajdhani banaakar rajya karti thi or iskaa pracheenatam gyaat sadsy upendra krishnaraj tha. is vansh ke praarambhik shaasha dakshin ke rashtrakooton ke samant the. rashtrakooton ke patan ke baad simpak dwitiya ke netritv main yah parivaar swatantra ho gaya. sipaak dwitiya kaa putr vaakpati munj, jo 10veen shatabdi ke antim chaturthaansh main hua, apane parivaar kii mahanata kaa sansthaapak tha. usne keval apni sthiti hi sudrudh nahin kii varan dakshin raajapootaana kaa bhi ek bhaag jeet liya or vahaan mahatvapurn padon par apane vansh ke rajkumaron ko niyukt kar diya. uskaa bhatija bhoj, jisane san 1000 se 1055 tak rajya kiya or jo sarvatomukhi pratibha kaa shaasha tha, madhyugin sarvshereshth shaskaon main gina jaataa tha. bhoj ne apane samay ke chaulubhy, chandel, kaalchuri or chalukya ityadi sabhi shaktishaalee rajyon se yuddh kiya. bahut badi sankhya main vidvan iske darbar main dayapurna aashray paakar rahete the. vah swayan bhi mahan lekhak tha or isane vibhinn vishyon par anek pustaken likhi thim, aisa mana jaataa he. usne apane rajya ke vibhinn bhaagon main badi sankhya main mandir banawaye.
 raja bhoj kii mrityu ke pashchaat cholukya karn or karnaton ne maalav ko jeet liya, kintu bhoj ke ek sambandhi udayaditya ne shatruon ko buree tarah parajeet karke apana prabhutv pun: sthapit karne main safalataa praapt kii. udayaditya ne madhyapradesh ke udaipur naamak sthaan main nilkanth shiv ke vishaal mandir kaa nirmaan karaayaa tha. udayaditya kaa putr jagaddev bahut pratishthit samrat tha. vah mrityu ke bahut kaal baad tak pashchimi bharat ke logon main apni gauravpurn uplabdhiyon ke liy prasiddh rahaa. maalav main parmaar vansh ke ant allaudin khilji dwaara 1305 i. main kar diya gaya.
 parmaar vansh kii ek shakha abu parvat par chandravati ko rajdhani banaakar, 10veen shatabdi ke ant main 13veen shatabdi ke ant tak rajya karti rahi. is vansh kii duusari shakha vagad or duungarpur riyasaton main utthtuk bansawada rajya main varttman arthuna kii rajdhani par 10veen shatabdi ke madhyakaal se 12veen shatabdi ke madhyakaal tak shashan karti rahi. vansh kii do shakhaayen or gyaat hai. ek ne jalor main, duusari ne binamaal main 10veen shatabdi ke antim bhaag se 12veen shatabdi ke antim bhaag tak rajya kiya. vartmaan main parmaar vansh kii ek shakha ujjain ke gaanv nandvasla,khatakhedi tathaa narsinghgarh evam indaur ke gaanv benganda main nivas karte hai.dharavia parmaar talavali main bhi nivas karte hai.11ve se 17 ve shatabdi tak panvaaro kaa pradeshaantar satapura or vidarbha main hua, jaha bhoyar panvar or powar kii shakha nivas karti hai jo ki raja bhoj ko apana aradhya maanate hai jo dhar panvaar vansh kii shakha hai,  kalika mata ke bhakt hone ke kaaran ye parmaar kalauta ke naam se bhi jane jate hai.dharavia bhojavansh ke parmaar main ek shakha ke dhar hi kii sardarpur tehsil main parmaar rahete he. inke ishtdev shri hanuman ji tathaa kuladevi maan kalikahai|ye apane yahan paidaa hone vaale har ladke kaa mundan rajasthan ke paali jila ke boosi main sthit shri hanuman ji ke mandir main karte hai. inaki teen shakha or he;ek boosi gaanv main,ek malpuria rajasthan main tathaa ek nimuch main nivasarath he.
