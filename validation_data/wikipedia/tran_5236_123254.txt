 ravi treepathi/ravi ke treepathi  ek prakhyat bhartiya parshvagayak, sangeetkaar, sangeet nirdeshak, abhinayta va televijan mezbaan hai. inhen soni antertenment televijan ke riyality sho indian idal se khyaati mili. unhone doordarshan ke close-ap-parformar jaise kaarykram kii mezbaani kii hai.
 ravi treepathi kaa janm uttar pradesh main 4 farvari, san 1978 ko hua. inke pita shrikrishna treepathi, pratapgarh jila ke lalganj tehsil main bhoomi evam jal sangrakshan vibhaag]main adhikari rahen he or maan shrimati sumitra treepathi ek gruhani hai. treepathi ne gayan kii shuruyaat chaar varsh kii aayu se kii. ve patiala gharaane se aate hai, or belha pratapgarh ke paitrik nivasee hai.
 ravi lakhanu vishvavidhyalay sein snaatak hai. kuch varsh tak bhatkhede sangeet vishvavidhyalay se sangeet kii shiksha praapt kii. baad main ve prasiddh sangeetkaar suresh wadkar ke sangeet ke gun siikhe.
 varsh 1992 main ravi mumbai aae jahaan par unki mulaakaat mahaan sangeetkaar rabindra jain se hui, uske baad vah safalataa ke mukaam par pahunchaa to mumbai kii kai nami-giraami sakhsiyat se mulaakaat hui, or ravi indian idal ke saath hi kai serialon va rang manchon ke madhyam se desh ke kone-kone main apane awaajon kaa jaadu bikher diya. varsh 2006 main indian idal ke duusare sijan main top finlist me jagah banane ke baad ravi ne doordarshan ke sangeet riyality sho close-ap-parformar kii mezbaani kii.
 akshay kumaar va deepika padukon starer film chandani chowk tu china se bollywood main filmi karier shuruuaat hui, jisame ravi ne bappi lahari ke saath 'india se aayaa tera dost' gaanaa gaaya. ravi ke debyute elbum 'bante' ko bhi sangeet premiyo dwaara kaaphi saraahna mili. times mujeec dwaara prastut elbum bate, jise jagajeet singh, suresh wadkar, rabindra jain, nida fazli kii upastithi main gayak sonu nigam dwaara mumbai main launch kiya gaya.
 treepathi ne soni tivi, imagin, star plus, ji tivi, i tivi, evam life oke jaise kai tivi vahikaaon ke liye sangeet kiya. in vahikaaon me aane vaali serials ke liye becoground geet va serials ke tital geet bhi gaayen.
 varsh 2009 main ravi treepathi ne asian khel ke countdn sho main hollywood star jaiki chaiyn ke saath parform kar china main bharat kaa pratinidhitiv kiya. saath hi asian khel ke samaapan samaaroh me bhi unhen aamantrit kiya gaya, jahaan unhone auskar vijeta e. aar. rahamaan kaa jai ho gaanaa gaaya. 
 jab main gaanaa ga rahaa tha, to jaiki chen bhi stage par aakar bade utsaah se uchhal kud machaane lage. unhone meri peeth thapthapai or kaha ki tum bahut acha gaate ho.
 ravi varsh 2016 kii aagaamee bollywood red signal main sangeet de rahe he, jisame kailash kher ne bhi aawaaj diya he. bataur sangeet nirdeshak yah ravi kii pehali bollywood film hai.
 varsh 2016 main ravi nirdeshak ravi kashyap kii bhojpuri film bhauji ai hamaar angana main bataur abhinayta debyue kar rahe he. is film main ravi mukhya kirdaar nibhaa rahe he. saath hi abhinetri anjana singh or abhinayta vikram singh bhi mukhya kirdaar main he.
