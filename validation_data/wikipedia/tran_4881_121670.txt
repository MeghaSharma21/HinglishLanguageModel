 hindi gadhy ke udbhav ko lekar vidwaanon main matbhed he. kuch vidwaan hindi gadhy kii shuruuaat 19veen sadi se hi maanate hai jabaki kuch anya hindi gadhy kii parampara ko 11veen-12veen sadi tak le jate hai. aadhunik kaal se purv hindi gadhy kii nimna paramparaayen milti hai-
 hindi main gadhy kaa vikaas 19veen shatabdi ke aasapaas hua. is vikaas main kalakatta ke fort wiliam college kii mahatvapurn bhoomika rahi. is college ke do vidwaanon lallulaal ji tathaa sadal mishra ne gilakraist ke nirdesan main kramshah premsagar tathaa nasiketopakhyaan naamak pustaken taiyaar kin. isi samay sadasukhlal ne sukhsagar tathaa munshi insha allaa khan ne 'rani ketki kii kahani' kii rachana kii in sabhi granthon kii bhashaa main us samay prayog main aanevaali khadi boli ko sthaan mila. ye sabhi kritiyaan san 1803 main rachi gayi thim.
 aadhunik khadi boli ke gadhy ke vikaas main vibhinn dharmon kii parichayatmak pustakon kaa khub sahyog rahaa jisamen isai dharm kaa bhi yogadan rahaa. bengal ke raja ram mohan raay ne 1815 isvi main vedant sutara kaa hindi anuwaad prakashit karavaaya. iske baad unhone 1829 main bangadoot naamak patr hindi main nikaala. iske pehle hi 1826 main kanpur ke pan jugal kishor ne hindi kaa pehla samachar patr udantamartand kalakatta se nikaala. isi samay gujarati bhashi arya samaaj sansthaapak swami dayanand saraswati ne apana prasiddh granth satyarth prakaash hindi main likhaa.
 bhaaratendu harishchandra ko hindi-sahitya ke aadhunik yug kaa pratinidhi mana jaataa he. unhone kavivachan sudhaa, harishchandr magazine or harishchandra patrika nikaali. saath hi anek naatakon kii rachana kii. unke prasikad naatak hai- chandravali, bharat durdasha, andher nagari. ye naatak rangamanch par bhi bahut lokapriy hue. is kaal main niabandh naatak upanyaas tathaa kahaniyon kii rachana hui. is kaal ke lekhakon main baalkrishna bhatta, pratap narayan mishra, radha charan goswami, upaadhyaay badrinath choudhary premaghan, lala shrinivas das, babu devaki nandan khatri or kishori laal goswami aadi ullekhneey hai. inamen se adhikansh lekhak hone ke saath saath patrkaar bhi the.
 shrinivasadas ke upanyaas parikshaguru ko hindi kaa pehla upanyaas kaha jaataa he. kuch vidwaan shraddharam phullauri ke upanyaas bhagyawati ko hindi kaa pehla upanyaas maanate hai. babu devakinandan khatri kaa chandrakanta tathaa chandrakanta santati aadi is yug ke pramukh upanyaas hai. ye upanyaas itane lokapriy hue ki inako padhne ke liye bahut se ahindi bhashiyon ne hindi sikhi. is yug kii kahaniyon main shivaprasad sitare hind kii raja bhoj kaa sapana mahatvapurn he.
 baldev agrahari kii san 1887 me prakashit natya pustak 'sulochana sati' main sulochana kii katha ke saath aadhunik katha ko bhi sthaan diya gaya hai, jisame sampadako or desh sudharko par vyangy kiya gaya hai. kai naatako main mukhya kathanak hi yatharth chithran prastut karte hai. baldev agrahari kii sulochana sati main bhinnatukant chand kaa aagrah bhi dikhaai detaa hai.
 pandit mahavir prasad dvivedi ke naam par hi is yug kaa naam dvivedi yug rakhaa gaya. san 1903 isvi main dvivedi ji ne saraswati patrika ke sampadan kaa bhaar sambhala. unhone khadi boli gadhy ke swaroop ko sthir kiya or patrika ke madhyam se rachanakaaron ke ek bade samudaay ko khadi boli main likhane ko prerit kiya. is kaal main niabandh, upanyaas, kahani, naatak evam samalochna kaa acha vikaas hua.
 is yug ke nibandhkaron main mahavir prasad dvivedi, madhav prasad mishra, shyaam sundar das, chandradhar sharma guleri, bal mukand gupt or adhyaaapak purna singh aadi ullekhneey hai. inke niabandh gambhir, lalit evam vichaaratmak hai. kishorilaal goswami or babu gopal ram gahamri ke upanyaason main manoranjan or ghatnaon kii rochakata he.
 hindi kahani kaa vaastavik vikaas dvivedi yug se hi shuru hua. kishori laal goswami kii indumati kahani ko kuch vidwaan hindi kii pehali kahani maanate hai. anya kahaniyon main bang mahila kii dulaai vaali, shukla ji kii gyaarah varsh kaa samay, prasad ji kii gram or chandradhar sharma guleri kii usne kaha tha mahatvapurn hai. samalochna ke kshetra main padmasingh sharma ullekhneey hai. hariudh, shivanandan sahay tathaa raay deviprasad purna dwaara kuch naatak likhe gaye.is yug ne kai sampadakon janm diya. pandit ishwari prasad sharma ne aadha darjan se adhik patron kaa sampaadan kiya. shiv pujan sahay unke yogya shishyon main shumaar hue. is yug main hindi aalochana ko ek disha mili. is yug ne hindi ke vikaas kii neenv rakhi. yah kai myanon me nai manyataon kii sthapana karne vaala yug rahaa.
 gadhy ke vikaas main is yug kaa vishesh mahatva he. pan ramchandra shukla ne niabandh, hindi sahitya ke itihaas or samalochna ke kshetra main gambhir lekhan kiya. unhone manovikaron par hindi main pehali baar niabandh lekhan kiya. sahitya samiksha se sambandhit nibandhon kii bhi rachana kii. unke nibandhon main bhav or vichaar arthath buddhi or hriday donon kaa samanvay he. hindi shabdhsagar kii bhoomika ke rup main likhaa gaya unka itihaas aaj bhi apni sarthakata banaae hue he. jaaysi, tulsidas or surdaas par likhi gayi unki aalochanaon ne bhavi aalochakon kaa maargdarshan kiya. is kaal ke anya nibandhkaron main jainendra kumaar jain, siyaaramsharan gupt, padumalal punnalaal bakshi or jaishankar prasad aadi ullekhneey hai.
 katha sahitya ke kshetra main premchand ne kranti hi kar daali. ab katha sahitya keval manoranjan, kautuhal or niti kaa vishay hi nahin rahaa balki sidhe jivan kii samasyaon se jud gaya.unhone seva sadan, rangabhumi, nirmala, gaban evam godaan aadi upanyaason kii rachana kii. unki teen sau se adhik kahaniyaan maansarovar ke aath bhaagon main tathaa guptadhan ke do bhaagon main sangrahit hai. pus kii raat, kafan, shatranj ke khiladi, panch parameshwar, namak kaa daroga tathaa idgah aadi unki kahaniyaan khub lokapriy hui. iskaal ke anya kathakaron main vishwambhar sharma kowshik, vrundavanlaal verma, rahul sankrutyaayan, pandeya bechan sharma ugr, upendranath ashk, jaishankar prasad, bhagwaticharan verma aadi ke naam ullekhneey hai.
 naatak ke kshetra main jaishankar prasad kaa vishesh sthaan he. inke chandragupta, skandagupt, dhruvaswamini jaise aitihaasik naatakon main itihaas or kalpana tathaa bhartiya or paashchaatya naatay padyatiyon kaa samanvay hua he. lakshminarayan mishra, harikrishna premi, jagadishchandra maathur aadi is kaal ke ullekhneey naatakkaar hai.
 is kaal main gadhy kaa chahunmukhi vikaas hua. pan hajaari prasad dvivedi, janendra kumaar, agyeya, yashpal, nandadulaare vaajapeyi, dow nagendra, ramavriksh bennipuri tathaa dow ramvilas sharma aadi ne vichaaratmak nibandhon kii rachana kii he. hajaari prasad dvivedi, vidyanivas mishra, kuber nath raay, kanhaiyalaal mishra prabhakar, viveki raay, ne lalit nibandhon kii rachana kii he. harishankar parasai, sharad joshi, shrilaal shukla, raveendranath tyaagi, tathaa ke pi saxena, ke vyangy aaj ke jivan kii vidrupataaon ke uddhatan main safal hue hai. jainendra, agyeya, yashpal, ilachandra joshi, amritlal nagar, rangey raghav or bhagwati charan verma ne ullekhneey upanyaason kii rachana kii. nagarjun, fanishwar nath renu, amritraay, tathaa raahi maasoom raja ne lokapriy aanchalik upanyaas likhe hai. mohan rakesh, rajendra yadav, mannu bhandari, kamaleshwar, bhishma saahani, bhairav prasad gupt, aadi ne aadhunik bhav bodh vaale anek upanyaason or kahaniyon kii rachana kii he. amarkant, nirmal verma tathaa gyanaranjan aadi bhi nae katha sahitya ke mahatvapurn stambh hai.
 prasadottar naatakon ke kshetra main lakshminarayan laal, lakshmikant verma, mohan rakesh tathaa kamaleshwar ke naam ullekhneey hai. kanhaiyalaal mishra prabhakar, ramavriksh bennipuri tathaa banarsidas chaturvedi aadi ne sansmaran rekhachitr va jeevani aadi kii rachana kii he. shukla ji ke baad pan hajaari prasad dvivedi, nand dulaare vaajapeyi, nagendra, ramvilas sharma tathaa namvar singh ne hindi samalochna ko samrudh kiya. aaj gadhy kii anek nayi vidhaon jaise yatra vrittant, reportaj, radio roopak, aalekh aadi main vipul sahitya kii rachana ho rahi he or gadhy kii vidhaaen ek duusare se mil rahi hai.poiets is most history of hindi
