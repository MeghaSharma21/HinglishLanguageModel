 dalma abhayaarany jharkhand ke jamshedpur, ranchi or pashchim bengal ke purulia ke bich basaa purvi bharat kaa ek pramukh vanya jeev abhayaarany he. is abhayaarany ko khaas taur par haathiyon ke sangrakshan ke liye chunaa gaya he.
  jharkhand se sambandhit yah lekh apni praarambhik avasthaa main he, yani ki ek aadhaar he. aap ise badhaakar vikipedia kii madad kar sakate he.
