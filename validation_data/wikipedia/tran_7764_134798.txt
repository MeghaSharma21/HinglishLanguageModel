 sapane saajan ke laurence disuza dwaara nirdeshit 1992 kii hindi film he. isamen mukhya kirdaar karishma kapur, rahul roy or jaiki shroff ne nibhaae hai. saajan kii safalataa ke baad lorence kii ye film badi ummiido ke saath relies hui thi. lekin yah ausat pradarshan hi kar paayi. iske sabhi gaanen hit sabit hue or aaj bhi lokapriy hai. 
 abhinayta jaiki shroff kaa car chaalak kaam nahin kar paata he to uskaa betaa deepak wo kaam sambhalata he. jab deepak ek sangeet kaarykram main jaiki shroff ke saath hota he to deepak jyoti se miltaa he. deepak or jyoti pyaar main pad jate hai.
 jyoti kii maa shalini chaahati hai ki vah amir aadmi ke bete gulu se shaadi kare. lekin jyoti gulu ko pasand nahin karti he. shalini apni beti kii pasand se sahamat ho jaati he or use deepak se shaadi karne detii he. lekin jyoti ko pataa chalata he ki deepak ek dhani vyakti nahin he jo wo hone kaa naatak kar rahaa tha.
 
 sabhi nadeem-shravan dwaara sangitabaddh.
