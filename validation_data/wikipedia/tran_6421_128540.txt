 kyoga jhil africa ke uganda desh main sthit ek bade aakaar kii lekin kam gaharaai vaali jhil he. 1,720 varg kimi ke kshetrafal vaali yah jhil 1,034 meter kii oonchaai par sthit he. nil nadi kii victoria nil naamak upree shakha victoria jhil se nikalati he or kyoga jhil se guzarati hui albert jhil tak jaati he. kyoga jhil vaise to purvi africa daraar main sthit he or mahaan africa jhiilon ke kshetramandal main hi padati he, lekin iskaa aakaar itnaa vishaal nahin he ki ise swayan mahaan africa jhiilon main se ek gina jaae.
