 ecuador, aadhikarik taur par ecuador ganraajya, dakshin america main sthit ek pratinidhi loktantric ganraajya he. desh ke uttar main colombia, purv or dakshin main peru or pashchim kii ore prashanth mahaasaagar sthit he. yah ek dakshin america main un do deshon main se he, jisaki simaaen brazil ke saath nahin milti he. desh ke hisse main mukhya bhoomi ke pashchim main prashanth mahaasaagar main sthit galapogos dweep bhi aate hai. bumadhya rekha, jiske aadhaar par desh kaa naam rakhaa gaya he, ecuador ko do bhaagon main vibhajit karti he. isaki rajdhani quito or sabase bada shahar guayakil he.
 1533 main spain dwaara vijay praapt karne se pehle tak ecuador uttari inkaa samrajya kaa bhaag tha. san 1563 main kuito spani samrajya kaa ek centre bana or 1717 main new granada ke viceroyalty kaa bhaag bana. viceroyalty ke kshetron jaise new granada, venezuela or kuito ne 1819 or 1822 ke bich apni-apni swatantraaaon kii ghoshnaa kii or gran colombia ke naam se ek mahasang banaaya.1830 main jab quito mahasang se vilag ho gaya to iskaa naam "bumadhya rekhiy ganraajya" rakh diya gaya. san 1904 se 1942 ke madhya padosi deshon se sangharshon ke kaaran ecuador ko apana bahut sa bhubhag khona padaa. 1995 main peru ke saath seemaa vivaad ke kaaran jo yuddh dadhak rahaa tha vah 1999 main sulajhaa liya gaya. yadhyapi san 2004 main ecuador naagarik shashan ke 25 varsh puure kar rahaa tha, lekin ye puraa daur rajnatik uthal puthal bharaa hi rahaa tha. quito main hue virodh pradarshnon ke kaaran ecuador main pichhali teen loktantric sarkaron ko kaarykaal puraa hone se purv hi apdast hona padaa. 2007 main nae samvidhan kii ruparekha taiyaar karne ke liye samvidhan sabha kaa chunaav kiya gaya he or swatantrata milane ke baad se ye ecuador kaa 20 waan samvidhan he.
 ecuador main gailapagos dweepasmooh sahit 283,561 kimi2 kaa kul kshetrafal he. inamen se 276,841 kimi2 bhoomi or 6,720 kimi2 pani he. ecuador dakshin america main urugve, surinam, guyana or french guyana se bada he.
 ecuador prashanth mahaasaagar se pashchim main sthit akshansh 2°en or 5°es ke bich sthit he, or isamen 2,337 kimi tat rekha he. isamen 2,010 kimi bhoomi simaaen hai, uttar main 590 kimi seemaa or purv main peru or dakshin main 1,420 kimi seemaa he. yah bumadhyasagariya desh he jo bumadhya rekha par sthit he
 desh chaar mukhya bhugolic kshetron laa costa, laa sierra, laa amezaniya or laa regian insular main bata hua hai.ecuador kii rajdhani quito he, jo sierra kshetra main pichencha praant main he. guayas praant main iskaa sabase bada shahar gavkil he. quito ke dakshin main kotoxi, duniya ke sabase sakriy sakriy jwalamukhi main se ek sthit he. mount chimborazo ke sheersh par, grah ke andakaar aakaar ke kaaran ecuador kaa sabase ooncha pahaad prithvi ke centre se prithvi ke centre se sabase dur bindu he.
 jalwaayu main badi vividhta he, jo mukhya rup se oonchai se nirdhaarit hoti he. pahaad ghaatiyon main yah salanaa kam he, tateey kshetron main ek ardra ushnkaribandheey jalwaayu or nichle ilaakon main varshaavan he. prashanth tateey kshetra main ek ushnkaribandheey jalwaayu he jahaan bheeshan barsaat hoti he. andian pahari kshetron main jalwaayu samshitoshna or apekshaakrit shushk he, or pahaado ke purvi hisse main amejan basin anya varshaavan kshetron kaa vaataavaran saajha karata he.
 bumadhya rekha par sthit hone kii vajah se, ecuador ek varsh ke dauraan surya kaa prakaash ghanton main thodi bhinnata kaa anubhav karata he. suryoday or suryaast donon din do chah baje ghanton main hote hai.
 ecuador 24 praanton main baanta gaya he, prayek praant apni prashasnic rajdhani ke saath:-
 ecuador paryaapt rup se apane tel sansadhano par nirbhar he, jisaki desh ko niryaat se hone vaali kamai main aadhe se adhik bhagidaari he or saarvajanik kshetra kaa ek chauthai rajasav isi se praapt hota he.1999-2000 main ecuador ko gahan aarthik sankat kaa saamana karnaa padaa jisase desh ke sakal gharelu utpaada main 6% kii kami ai or saath hi garibi rekha se neeche rahane waalon kii sankhya main bhi vruddhi hui. banking kshetra bhi dharashayi ho gaya or us varsh ecuador apane bahya rin ke bhugtan main bhi chook gaya. 2000 main rashtriya congress dbaara bahut se dhanchaagat sudhaaron ko sweekruti di gai jisamen america dollar ko kanoonee nivida ke rup main apanaae jane kaa bhi praavdhaan tha. dollarikaran ke kaaran arthvyastha ko sudridhtaa mili or aage aane vaale varshon main phir se vikaas ko gati mili jiskaa shrey unche tel mulyon, wipreshan or apaaramparik niryaaton main hui vruddhi ko jaataa he.
 2002-06 kii avadhi main arthvyastha 5.5% kii dar se badi, jo pichley 25 varshon main sabase unchi panch varshiya dar thi. 2006 main garibi dar main bhi giravat hui lekin phir bhi ye 38% tak bani rahi. 2006 main sarkaar dwaara videshi tel companiyon par apratyaashit kar laga diya gaya jisase america ke saath hone vaali mukt vyapaar vaarta nilambit ho gayi. in upayon ke chalate varsh 2007 main tel utpaadan main bhi kami ai. raashtrapati rafel kauria dwaara rin difolt kaa bhay dikhaaya gaya or us bhay ko dhyaan main rakhte hue, bhay se nibatane ke liye disambar 2008 main kuch vyavsaayik band dayitvon se mukh mode liya. unhone nijee tel companiyon par bhi ek ucch apratyaashit rajasav kar laga diya or unke saath kiye hue anubandhon par punah vaarta armbh kari taaki kar ke ahakt prabhaavon ko dur kiya ja sake. isse aarthik anischitata utpann hui; nijee nivesh main giravat ai or aarthik vikaas dhimaa hua he.
 columbia main jari sanyojit avaidh maadak padaarthon kii taskari colombia se lagati hui chhidril seemaa dwaara ecuador main bhi hoti he, ecuador kii saajha seemaa hajaaron colombian naagarik bhi apane desh main hinsa se bachane ke liye ecuador main pravesh karte hai.
