 datia mahal, jise bir singh mahal yaa beerasingh dev mahal bhi kaha jaataa he, madhya pradesh ke gwalior shahar se lagbhag 75 kimi dur sthit he. yah mahal 7 manjila he. vartmaan main yahan par shahi parivaar kaa koi sadsy nahin rahata he. bundelkhand main datia samrajya ke sansthaapak - maharaj beerasingh deo ne desh bhar main aise 52 smarak banawaaye the. datia ke mahal yaa satkhanda mahal ko datia mahal bhi kaha jaataa he, saath ise hi puraana mahal yaa "purana mahal" bhi ise kehete he. itihaaskaar abdul hamid lahori, shahjahan ke saath 19 november 1635 is shahar main aae the. yah mahal lagbhag 80 meter lanbaa or bahut hi vyapak he. yah bahut hi sundar or majboot mehalon me se ek mana jaataa he.
 yah mahal ke nirmaan main nau saal or 35 lakh rupee kii laagat ai thi. yah datia shahar ke pashchimi kinare par ek alag chattan par sthit he. yah rajput vaastukala ke saath mughal vaastukala kaa pratinidhitiv karti he. yah raja birising dev dwaara nirmit sabhi 52 mehalon main se sabase bada or sabase prasiddh he or ise aasaani se dur se bhi dekha ja saktaa he.
