 gurdeh bhartiya rajya rajasthan ke karauli jile kii mandaryal tehsil main ek madhyam aakaar kaa gaanv or gram panchayat he. gurdeh kii kul jansankhya 1,245 he. yah gaanv bhankari se kuch duuri par dholpur seemaa kii ore sthit he.
 2011 ke janaganana aankadon ke anusaar, gurdeh kii kul jansankhya 1,245 he jisamen 679 purush evam 566 striyaan shaamil hai.gaanv main 0-6 varsh aayu varg kii jansankhya 217 he jo kul jansankhya kaa 17.43% he. ausat linganupaat 834 jo rajasthan ke ausat linganupaat 928 kii tulana main kam he jabaki shishu linganupaat 736 he or yah bhi rajya ke ausat 888 se kam hi he.
 gurdeh main sakshartaa kii sthiti rajasthan ke ausat se behatar he, varsh 2011 main yah 66.54% thi jabaki rajasthan kaa ausat 66.11% tha. isamen purushon main sakshartaa dar 86.46% evam stri sakshartaa 43.25% darj kii gayi.
