 indira nagar bharat ke lakhanu shahar ke vyastatam ilaakon main gina jaataa he. yah uttar pradesh awaas board dwaara viksit asia kii sabase badi basti he.shuru main indira nagar chaar block main vibhajit kiya gaya tha, lekin ab kai kshetron main vistaar hota ja rahaa he. bhootnath bazaar is ilaake kaa mukhya bazaar he. yaheen par prasiddh bhootnath mandir, avasthit ha. pramukh bazaaron main lekharaj bazaar, amrapali bazaar, sahaara shopping centre, munshi puliya, minaa bazaar, nagina plaza aadi ullekhneey he. khaane pine ke kendron ke alaava, is ilaake main bank shakhaon, atithi grihon, banquet haul or aspataalon, dawa kii dukaanon, nirmaan saamagree kii dukaanon, skulon or vibhinn sarkari karyaalayon kii ek badi sankhya vidyaman he. indira nagar main acchhee tarah se viksit kai jogging park he. aurbindo park or swarna jayanti awaas vikaas dwaara viksit parkon ko paryaay ke rup main janaa jaataa he.
 kukarail foresti ek mashhur picnic sthal he. yahaan ghadiyaalon or kachhuon kaa ek abhayaarany he. yah lakhanu ring maarg par indira nagar ke nikat sthit he. yaheen par maanas vihaar socity dwaara nirmit utkrisht coloniyaan jase mayur vihaar,mayur udyaan, mayur racidensi, mayur anchlev bhi mojood han.is ilaake main sabhi sadakon ko durust kiya ja rahaa he. sabhi coloniyaan bahut acchhee tarah se i rickshaw or auto rickshaw dwaara judy hui hai. nae shopping mal main yahan spencers, big bazaar, izi de or sabakaa bazaar jasi badi dukaane bhi hai. gajipur main police stesan sthit ha.
