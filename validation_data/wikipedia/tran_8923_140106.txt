 sukhdev singh babbar  1980 main bharat ke punjab rajya main sakriy babbar khalsa international kaa netaa tha. jiskaa praathmik uddeshy sikhon ke liye swatantra rajya kaa nirmaan tha, jise khalistaan kaha jaataa tha.. usne babbar khalsa international kii sthapana kii or 1992 main jab tak unki mrityu ho gai tab tak 14 saal tak lagaataar bikeai ko chalaaya.
 da tribune ke anusaar, nirnakiri-sikh sangharsh kaa din wo hi din tha. jis din unki shaadi tay hui. is din, unhone pratigya kii ki ve nirankarees par badla lege. usne sikhon ke liye bharat se alag rajya khalistaan banane ke uddeshy se talabindar singh parmaar ke saath babbar khalsa international kii sthapana kii. bikeai kaa pehla unit 1981 main canada main sthapit kiya gaya tha. yah sanghathan sanyukt rajya america, canada, britain, germany, france, belgium, norge, switsraland or pakistan main maujuud tha. babbar khalsa international uske maargdarshan main khalistaan andolan main ek pramukh bhagidaar ban gaya or bhartiya suraksha balon ke khilaaf saikadon aapareshnon main bhaag liya or kai bhartiya rajyon main sakriy rahaa.
 neuyork times ne sukhdev singh babbar ko babbar khalsa international ke adhyaksh ke rup main bataaya. khalistaan andolan ke dauraan, babbar khalsa international ke pramukh sukhdev singh babbar kaa naam, police ke dimag main aatki ke rup main tha.
 9 agast 1992 ko uski mrityu ho gai. jab baari sashastr pulicekarmiyon ne agast kii shuruyaat main patiala ke bheed bhare shahar main aaramdaayak vila par hamalaa kiya or use pakad liya. da neuyork times ke mutabik uski mrityu kii paristhitiyaan vivaadit hai. shuru main, police ne kaha ki shahar ke baahar ek bandook kii ladaai main use maar diya gaya tha. uske baad, unhone kaha ki vah nihathe tha or usne synide capsule ko khaakar aatmahatya kii. ek teesri report ne kaha ki uske kabje ke baad use police ne goli maar di thi.
