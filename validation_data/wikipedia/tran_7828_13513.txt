 unhen 12 agast 1765 ko robert clive ke madhyasthata ke madhyam se naththranagar, or suraah of arcot ke nib subah or samraat shah alam dwitiya dwaara umadat ul-umr ke khitaab main niyukt kiya gaya tha.

 umdaat ul-umr ne british east india company kii maangon kaa joardaar virodh kiya. umadaat ul-umr, haalanki, jald hi baad main, company dwaara uddhrut kii mrityu ho gai. umdaat ul-umr ke domen kaa british adhigrahan unke bhattije or uttaradhikari azeem-ud-daula ke shasankaal ke dauraan hua tha. jaise hi azim-ud-daula singhaasana par chadh gaye, 31 july 1801 ko unhen karnataka ke naagarik or nagarpalika prashasan ko british east india company ko saunpane vaali karnataka sandhi par hastakshar karne ke liye majaboor kiya gaya. is dastavej ne pradaan kiya ki azim-ud-daula ne apni sabhi bhoomiyon ko british shashan main shaamil kiya, jisamen paalegaron ke kshetra bhi shaamil the.
