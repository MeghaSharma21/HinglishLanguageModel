 taravadi haryana kaa ek shahar he jo ki rashtriya rajmarg sankhya-1 par kurushetra va karnal ke madhya sthit he.
 yahaan par basmati chawlon kii kheti kii jaati he. in chawlon kaa niryaat videshon main kiya jaataa he.
 taravadi karnal kii uttar disha main sthit aitihaasik shahar he. itihaas main yah nagar tarin kaa yuddh athwa taravadi kaa yuddh ke kaaran prasiddh he, jo ki yudhon kii ek aisi shrunkhlaa thi, jisane puure uttar bharat ko muslim niyantran ke liye khol diya.
 yahaan par aurangzeb ke putr ajam kaa janm hua tha. ajam ke naam par iskaa naam aajamabad rakhaa gaya tha. baad main yah aajamabad se taravadi ho gaya. aurangzeb ne iske chaaron taraf diwaar banavaai thi or charadivari ke andar talab or masjid kaa nirmaan bhi karaayaa tha. yah talab or masjid bahut khubasurat he. ise dekhne ke liye paryatak pratidin yahaan aate hai.
 rashtriya rajmarg par sthit nikatatam nagar -karnal, kurushetra. rashtriya rajmarg sankhya-1.
