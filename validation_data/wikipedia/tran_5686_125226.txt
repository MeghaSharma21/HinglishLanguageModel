 raay bahadur sar vangal tiruvenkatachari krishnamachari bhartiya naagarik sevak or prashaasak the. wo 1927 se 1944 tak badoda ke dewan or 1946 se 1949 tak jaipur rajya ke pradhanmantri rahe. iske atirikt san 1961 se 1964 tak rajya sabha sadsy rahe. bharat vibhaajan ke uparant unhen harendra kumaar mukhopadhyaay ke saath bhartiya samvidhan sabha kaa upaadhyaksh chunaa gaya tha.
