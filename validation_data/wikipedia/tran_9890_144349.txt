 dwinetri, field glass athwa dwinetri doordarshee samaan athwa darpan samamiti vaale doordarshee-yugma he, jo saath-saath lage hote hai tathaa ek disha main dekhne ke liye parishuddhta se lagaae jate hai. ek sadhran dwinetri doordarshee, gallileo kism ke do doordarshiyon kaa yugma hota he. dwinetri kaa upyog parthiv vastuon ke dekhne main hota he, isliye yah aavashyak ho jaataa he ki is prakaar ke dwinetri main vastu kaa sidhaa pratibimb bane. galileyon kism ke doordarshee sidhaa pratibimb banaate hai. isliye saadhaaran dwinetri doordarshee ke nirmaan main isi prakaar ke doordarshee kaa upyog hota he. saadhaaran dwinetri doordarshee ko natya doorabeen kehete hai.
 telescope ke vipareet doorabeen upyogkarta ko tri-aiami chhavi prastut karaati he: apekshaakrit nazadik kii vastuon ko dekhte samay darshak kii donon ankhon ke liye thode se alag drushtikon se chhaviyaan prastut hoti hai jo ki mil kar gaharaai kaa prabhaav prastut karti hai. monocular telescope ke vipareet isamen bhram se bachane ke liye ek aankh ko band athwa dhakne kii aavashyaktaa nahin padati he. donon ankhon ke prayog se drushtisambandhi tivrata kaaphi bad jaati he or aisa kaaphi dur kii vastuon ke liye bhi hota he jahaan gaharaai kaa abhas spasht nahin hota.
 sabase pehla dwinetri doordarshee san 1608 main leparhe dwaara taiyaar kiya gaya. yah upkaranika do samaantar aksh ke doordarshiyon kaa yugma thi.
 gallileo kism ke doordarshee main do mukhya dosha hote hai :
 1. iskaa drushtikshetra vistrut nahin hota tathaa
 2. isaki aavardhan kshamta adhik nahin hoti.
 kepler kism ke doordarshiyon ko khagoli doordarshee kehete hai. drushtikshetra ke vistaar or aavardhakata kii drushti se kepler doordarshee gallileo doordarshee se acha hota he, kintu kepler doordarshee kaa upyog dwinetri doordarshee banane main isliye nahin ho saktaa tha ki usamen pratibimb ulataa banataa he. poro ne ek aise triparshv sangyojan kaa nirmaan kiya jo kepler doordarshee main bane hue ulte pratibimb ko kshaitij or urdhvadhar donon dishaao main sidhaa karke dikhaa saktaa he. dwinetri upkarnikaon ke vikaas main poro kaa ukt avishkaar bada mahatvapurn he. poro ke triparshv sangyojan main do samakon triparshvi ko is prakaar joda jaataa he ki unke karn prishth ek duusare ke sampark main rahete hai or unke purna paravartan prishth paraspar samakonik hote hai. poro ke baad aibe ne triparshv yogicon ke prashn par vishesh rup se vichaar kiya. aadhunik triparshv dwinetri ke vikaas par aibe ke anusandhanon kaa vishesh prabhaav padaa he.
 yah kepler kism ke do doordarshiyon kaa yugma hota he, jisamen doordarshee ke abhidrishyak dwaara bane hue ulte pratibimb kodo samakon samdvibahu triparshv sidhaa kar dete hai. ye triparshv doordarshee ke abhidrishyak or upanetra ke bich main sthit rahete hai. triparshvi dwaara jis prakaar kshaitij or urdhvadhar dishaao main pratibimb ko sidhaa kiya jaataa he, vah chitra 1. main bataaya gaya he. vastu se ai hui prakashakiran abhidrishyak le1 main se guzarane ke baad pratham triparshv par takaraati he. is triparshv kii vartak kor urdhvadhar hoti hai, jisase vah pratibimb ko kshaitij dharaatal main sidhaa kar detii he. pratham triparshv se nikalne ke baad prakashakirane duusare triparshv par girti hai, jisaki vartak kor kshaitij sthiti main rahati he. isse urdhvadhar dharaatal main pratibimb sidhaa ho jaataa he. duusare triparshv se nikalne ke baad prakashakirane upanetra le2 main pravesh karti he. upanetra dwaara is sidhe pratibimb kaa aavardhan hota he. triparshvon ke kaaran doordarshee kii nalika kii lambaai paryaapt kam ho jaati he.
 yah do sookshmdarshiyon kaa yugma hota he jisamen triparshwo kii sahaayta se pratibimb ko sidhaa kiya jaataa he.
 isaki visheshta yah hoti he ki isse vastuon ke thospan kaa anubhav hota he. do nearesth vastuon kii duuri athwa gaharaai kaa anubhav netra or vastuon ke antar par nirbhar karata he. donon netron ke retinaon par bane hue pratibimbon main kuch antar hota he or isi bhinnata ke kaaran gaharaai yaa thospan kaa anubhav hota he. trivimadrushti kaa paraas 1,250 se 1,600 gaj tak hota he.
 saadharantaya trivimdarshi main ek hi vastu ke do photo is prakaar rakhe jate hai ki unase padaarth ke thospan kaa anubhav hone lagata he. aakhon ke bich kii duuri jitani hoti he utani hi duuri par sthit do lenson se vastu ke do photo le liye jate hai. ab in donon photon ko is tarah vyavasthit kiya jaataa he ki dahini aankh keval dahini ore ke lens se lee hui photo ko hi dekh sake or duusari photo ko na dekh sake. isi vyavastha ko trivimdarshi kehete hai.
 aisa lagata he ki 17veen sadi main telescope ke avishkaar ke saath hi aise do telescope ko saath laga kar unase praapt hone vaali bainocular drushti ke laabhon ko khoja jane laga. sabase shuruaati doorabine gallilian prakaashiki kaa prayog karti thim; ve ek uttal objective lens tathaa avatal ipis lens kaa prayog karti thim. gallilian dizin kaa yah phayda he ki praapt chhavi sidhi hoti he parantu drishy kshetra sankara hota he tathaa iskaa aavardhan bahut adhik nahin hota he. is prakaar ke nirmaan kaa prayog abhi bhi kaaphi saste modelon main tathaa opera glasson va theatre glasson main kiya jaataa he.
 keplarian prakaashiki kaa prayog karte hue ek behatar chhavi or ucch aavardhan praapt kiya ja saktaa he, isamen objective lens se praapt chhavi ko dhanatmak ipis lens se dekha jaataa he. is vinyas kaa ek nuksaan yah he ki praapt chhavi ulti hoti he. in kamiyon ko dur karne ke kai tareeke hai.
 poro prism doorabeenon kaa naam italy ke prakaashiki vidvan ignazio poro ke naam par rakhaa gaya he jinhone is chhavi sudhaar pranali ko 1854 main patent karavaaya or baad main karl zius jaise nirmaataaon dwaara 1890 ke dashak main isamen sudhaar kiya gaya. is prakaar kii doorabeen jed ke aakaar ke dohare poro prism vaale vinyas kaa prayog chhavi ko sidhaa karne main karti hai. is vishistataa ke prayog se chaudee doorabeenon ko aise objective lens ke saath bana paanaa sambhav hota he jo kaaphi dur hote hai kintu ipis dwaara samyojit hote hai. poro prism dizin kaa ek or laabh yah he ki inamen prakaash path chota ho jaataa he jiske kaaran doorabeen kii vaastavik lambaai objective lens kii focal lambaai se choti ho jaati he tathaa donon objective ke bich kii badi hui duuri ke kaaran gaharaai kaa behatar ehsaas hota he.
 esile victor emil dobres dwaara 1870 main di gayi dizine ke parinamswarup ruf prism aadhaarit doorabine aayin. adhikansh ruf prism aadhaarit doorabine yaa to ebbe-konig prism athwa shmit-pechan prism kaa prayog chhavi ko sidhaa karne tathaa prakaash path ko chota karne ke liye prayog karti hai. inamen aise objective lens hote hai jo ipis kii lagbhag sidh main hote hai.
 ruf prism dizine ki sahaayta se aise upkarnon kaa nirmaan ho paata he jo patale tathaa poro prism kii tulana main chote hote hai. yahaan chhavi kii chamak main bhi antar aata he. poro prism aadhaarit doorabeenon main samaan aavardhan, vastu aakaar tathaa samak prakaashiki gunvattta vaali ruf-prism aadhaarit doorabeenon ke mukaable adhik chamak vaali chhavi praapt hoti he, aisa isliye hota he kyonki ruf prism dizine main rupahali sataha kaa prayog kiya jaataa he jo ki prakaash ke sancharan ko 12% se 15% tak kam kar detii he. ruf prism dizine vaali doorabeenon ko apane prakashkeey tatwon ke sarekhan main adhik sookshmata kii bhi aavashyaktaa hoti he. aise main inaki keemat bad jaati he kyonki unki dizine unhen factory main hi kuch nishchit ghatakon ke saath ucch colimation par set kiye jane par aadhaarit hoti he. poro prism vaali doorabeenon main unke prism ko kabhi kabhi punahrekhit karnaa hota he jisase ki colimation ko punarprapt kiya ja sake. sthir rekhan vaali ruf dizine kaa arth yah hua ki unhen aamtaur par dobaaraa kabhi punah colimation kii aavashyaktaa nahin padegi.
 doordarshee kii dizine aamtaur par vishisht anuprayog ke liye kiya jaataa he. ye vibhinn dizin kuch vishisht optical maapdand utpann karte hai ye maapdand hai:
 ipis kii focal lambaai ko objective kii focal lambaai se vibhajit karne par praapt anupaat doorabeen kii rekhiy aavardhan shakti kehalaata he . udhaaharan ke liye aavardhan shakti 7 ke gunak main hone kaa arth yah hota he maano dekhne vaala vyakti dekhi ja rahi vastu ko 7 guna nikat se dekh rahaa ho. aavardhan kii maatra isapar nirbhar karti he ki doorabeen kis anuprayog ke liye banaayi gayi he. chunki haath main pakad kar prayog kii jane vaali doorabeenon main aavardhan kam hota he atah ve kampan ke prati kam samvedansheel hoti hai. adhik aavardhan hone se drishy kshetra chota ho jaataa he.
 objective lens kaa vyas yah nirdhaarit karata he ki ek chhavi banane ke liye kitna prakaash ekatrit kiya ja saktaa he. yah sankhya sidhe pradarshan ko prabhavit karti he. jab aavardhan or gunvattta baraabar hoti hai, doorabeen kii duusari sankhya jitani badi hoti he, praapt chhavi tani hi ujjwal tathaa spasht hoti he. is prakaar ek 8X40 dwaara praapt chhaviyaan 8X25 kii tulana main adhik ujjwal tathaa spasht hongi, haalanki donon hi kisi chhavi ko aath guna hi bada karengi. 8x40 main saamne ke bade lens prakaash ke bade punj utpann karenge jo ipis main jaayenge. is prakaar 8x40 se dekhnaa ek 8x25 kii tulana main adhik aaramdaayak hoga. yah aamtaur par millimetre main vyakt kiya jaataa he. prathagat rup se doorabeenon kaa shreneekaran aavardhan X objective ke vyas ke rup main kiya jaataa he, udhaaharan ke liye 7X50 .
 kisi doorabeen kaa drishy kshetra uske optical dizine dwaara nirdhaarit hota he. ise rekhiy maan se vyakt kiya jaataa he, jaise 1000 gaz se dekhne par kitne feet chaudaai dikhaai degi, athwa kitne degree kaa koniya maan dekha ja saktaa he.
 objective dwaara praapt prakaash ko doorabeen ek punj ke rup main kendrit karti hai, exit pupil jiskaa vyas objective ke vyas ko aavardhan shakti se vibhajit karke praapt kiya jaataa he. sabase acchhee prakaash grahyata tathaa ujjwal chhavi ke liye exit pupil kaa vyas ek puri tarah faili maanviya aankh kii putali ke aakaar kaa hona chahie jo lagbhag 7 mimi hota he tathaa aayu ke saath kam hoti jaati he. agar doorabeen se nikalne vaala prakaash shanku us aankh kii putali ke aakaar se bada hoga jisame ise pravesh karnaa he to, putali ke aakaar ke atirikt ajo bhi prakaash he vah vyarth ho jaayegaa arthath uske madhyam se netron ko koi soochna nahin milegi. din ke samay aankh kii putali lagbhag 3 mimi fail jaati he jo ki 7X21 doorabeen ke exit pupil ke baraabar hoti he. adhik bade 7x50 doorabeen putali se zyada bade prakaash shanku kaa nirmaan karte hai, jo ki din ke samay vyarth ho jaati he. isliye ek bade upkaran ko saath lekar chalanaa vishesh upyogi nahin lagata. haalanki, ek bada exit pupil se netra ko us sthaan par rakhana aasaan ho jaataa he jahaan se ise prakaash mil sake: prakaash shanku main kahin bhi aankh lagaayi ja sakati he. aankh lagane kii yah aasaani chhavi ko aspasht hone se bachaati he, jo ki prakaash kaa maarg aanshik rup se avaruddh hone par kaalepan athwa dhundhalepan ke rup main ho saktaa he. or, iskaa yah matlab bhi he ki chhavi ko jaldi se dhundha ja saktaa he jo pakshiyon yaa pashuon ke adhik gatisheel khelon ko dekhne ke dauraan mahatvapurn he. sankeern exit pupil vaali doorabine shighrata se thakaa sakati hai kyanki sahi chhavi praapt karne ke liye upkaran ko thik ankhon ke saamne hona chahie. ant main, kai log apni doorabeenon kaa prayog shaam ke samay, dhundhalake ke samay, tathaa raat main karte hai jab unki putaliyaan badi hoti hai. is prakaar din ke samay kaa exit pupil hameshaa sabase upyogi maanak nahin hota he. suvidha, upyog main aasaani or anuprayogon main vibhinnata ke liye badi doorabine jinamein bade exit pupil hon, santusht karne vaale vikalp hote hai, haalanki din main unki kshamta kaa puri tarah prayog nahin ho paata.
 ai relif pichley ipis lens se eczit pupil athwa netra bindu ke bich kii duuri ko kaha jaataa he. yah vah duuri he jo dekhne vaale ko ipis se apni aankh ke bich rakhni hoti he jisase use ek spasht chhavi praapt ho sake. ipis kii focal lambaai jitani badi hogi, ai relif utani hi adhik hogi. doorabeenon main ai relif kuch millimetre se lekar 2.5 centimetre athwa usase bhi adhik ho sakati he. chashma pahanane waalon ke liye ai relif vishesh rup se mahatvapurn hoti he. chashma pahanane vaale vyakti kii aankh aamtaur se doorabeen ke ipis se dur hoti he jisaki vajah se yah aavashyak ho jaataa he ki doorabeen kii aai relif adhik ho jisase ki vah vyakti puraa drishy kshetra dekh sake. choti ai relif vaali doorabeenon ke prayog main vahaan adhik dikkat ho sakati he jahaan unhen sthirataa pakadaney main mushkil ho. 
 nikat focus duuri vah kam se kam duuri he jahaan doorabeen focus kar sakati hai. yah duuri 1 meter se 30 meter tak ho sakati he or yah doorabeen ke dizine par nirbhar karti he.
 chunki doorabeenon kaa prayog jin vastuon ke dekhne main kiya jaataa he ve kisi nishchit duuri par nahin hoti hai, atah unamem focus karne kii vyavastha hona aavashyak he jisase drushti tathaa objective lenson kii duuri ko badla ja sake. paramparaagat rup se, focus karne ke liye do alag alag vyavasthaaon kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he. jin doorabeenon main "swatantra focus" kii vyavastha hoti he, unamem do alag-alag telescopon ko swatantra rup se unke ipis se focus kiya jaataa he. paaramparik rup se baari field prayog, udhaaharan ke liye sainya anuprayogon ke liye, swatantra focus vaali doorabeenon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. chunki saamaanya prayogkarta doorabeenon ke donon tubon ko ek hi samaayojan se focus karnaa adhik suvidhajanak pate hai, ek duusare prakaar kii doorabine "kendriya focus" kaa prayog karti hai jinamein ek kendriya focus karne vaala pahiyaa laga hota he jisase donon tubon ko ek saath samyojit kiya ja saktaa he. iske atirikt, donon ipis bhi darshak kii ankhon ke antar kii kshatipurti karne ke liye samyojit kiye ja sakate hai . ipis ko uske mount main ghuma kar samyojit kiye jane se hone vaale focal parivartan ko apvartan shakti kii prachlit ikai diopter main mapa jaataa he isi liye kai baar samaayojan kiye ja sakane vaale ipis ko bhi "diopter" hi kaha jaataa he. kisi vyakti vishesh ke liye yah samaayojan kiye jane ke baad alag alag duriyon par sthit vastuon ko dekhne ke liye focus karne vaale pahiye kii sahaayta se donon tubon ko focus kiya jaataa he or ipis ko dobaaraa samyojit nahin karnaa padataa he. adhikansh aadhunik doorabeenon main ek kabjedaar banaavat kii sahaayta se donon alag alag telescopon ke bich kii duuri ko bhi parivartit kiya ja saktaa he jisase vibhinn logon kii donon aakhon ke bich kii duuri ke anusaar inhen set kiya ja saktaa he. adhikansh vayaskon kii donon putliyon kii bich kii duuri  ke anukool bani hoti he.
 kai doorabine "focus free" athwa "phixd focus" vaali hotin hai, jinamein focus karne kii koi yantrawali nahin hoti he. unka dizine sthir drishy kshetra ke liye hota he jo apekshaakrit nikat kii vastuon se lekar anant tak hota he jisamen ek badi hyperfocal duuri hoti he. in diginon ko samjhautapurna dizine mana jaataa he, isamen suvidha to he, parantu ve vasteen jo inaki dizine ke aadhaar par inke paraas ke bahaar hoti hai, ve thik se nahin dikhaai padatin.
 kuch doorabine samaayojan yogya aavardhan pradaan karatin hai, inhen zum bainocular kehete hai, inkaa uddeshy prayogkarta ko ek hi doorabeen rakh kar usase "zum" liver kaa prayog karte hue vibhinn aavardhan str praapt karnaa hota he. aisa karne ke liye lenson ke samaayojan kii ek jatil shrinkhalaa kii aavashyaktaa hoti he, jaisaa ki zum camera lenson main kiya jaataa he. ye dijin samjhauta athwa hatkandaa samajhe jate hai kyonki ye doorabeen main vishaal aakaar, jatilta tathaa najukta ko badha detii hai. jatil prakaashiya path ke kaaran drishy kshetra kam ho jaataa he tathaa ucch zum ke str par chamak kam ho jaati he. is prakaar ke modelon main zum kii puri paraas main, donon ankhon ke liye baraabar aavardhan rakhana aavashyak hota he tathaa ankhon ko tanaav va thakaan se bachaane ke liye samantaran bhi rakhana hota he.
 doorabeenon main image sthirikaran takanic kaa prayog karte hue chhavi ko hilne se bachaaya tathaa ucch aavardhan kaa prayog kiya ja saktaa he. ve kal-purze jo chhavi kaa sthaan badalte hai ve urja se chalane vaale jiroscope athwa aisi yantriki se dridhtaapurvak sthir rakhe jate hai jo jiroscope yaa sthayitv jaanchane vaale yantro kii sahaayta se sanchalit ho, kai baar unhen aise lagaaya jaataa he ki ve kampan gati ke prabhaav ke vipareet kriya karte hai. sthirikaran ko prayogkarta kii ichha ke anukool shuru yaa band kiya ja saktaa he. in takneekon se doorabeen ko 20X tak ke aavardhan ke saath haath main pakde ja sakane yogya banaaya ja saktaa he, saath hi kam shakti vaale upkarnon main chhavi sthirikaran kii gunvattta ko bhi badhaaya ja saktaa he. yahaan par kuch kamiyaan bhi hai: sthirikaran kaa prayog na karne vaali shreshth doorabeenon ko tripad par lagane ke baad, unase praapt tasviir kahin behatar hoti he, saath hi sthirikaran vaali doorabine apni samatuly sthirikaran kaa prayog na karne vaali doorabeenon se mahangi tathaa baari hoti hai.
 acchhee tarah se samaantarit doorabeen ko, maanav ankhon se dekhe or maanav mastishk dwaara sansaadhit kiye jane par, ek ekal, spashtatya tri-aiami chhavi dikhni chahie or aisa nahin laganaa chahie ki vah thode se alag drushtikon se do alag-alag tasviren dekhi ja rahi he. is anukulan ke abhaav main, bhrampurna asuvidha tathaa drushti sambandhi thakaan ho sakati he, parantu drushti kshetra phir bhi sampoorn dekha ja saktaa he. doorabeen se dikhne vaale cinemai drishy jisamen do aanshik rup se aachhadit aath kii sankhya ke gole jaise dikhte hai, aisa vaastaviktaa main nahin hota he.
 prijmon ko thoda hilaa dula kar galat sanrekhan ko aksar doorabeenon ko khole bina pech ghuma kar thik kiya jaataa he athwa aisa objective kii sthiti badal karke bhi karte hai, jiske liye objective kii sataha ke baahar vikendrit chudiyaan hoti hai. aamtaur par sanrekhan kisi peshewar dwaara kiya jaataa he haalanki sanrekhan trutiyon ko pahchaanane tathaa unhen sanrekhit karne ke liye nirdesh internet par bhi mil jate hai.
 kisi saadhaaran doorabeen main 6 se 10 prakaashiya tatva prayukt hote hai jinka vishisht prayog hota he, tathaa kaanch se hawa ke sampark vaali satahen 16 tak ho sakati hai, isliye doorabeen nirmaataaon ko takneekee kaarnon se vibhinn prakaar kii optical coating kaa prayog karnaa hota he jisase upkaran se praapt chhavi kii gunvattta badhaai ja sake.
 pratek sataha par paravartan ke kaaran hone vaali prakaash kii haani ko anti-recolactive coating se kam kiya jaataa he. anti-recolactive coating ke madhyam se doorabeen ke andar "lupt" prakaash, jo ki praapt chhavi ko dhundhala bana saktaa he, ko kam kiya jaataa he. 8x40 kii doorabeen jisamen acchhee optical coating kaa prayog hua ho, bina coating vaali 8x50 doorabeen se behatar tathaa ujjwal chhavi prastut kar sakati he. ek paaramparik lens coating saamagree magnesium fluoride he jo paravartan ko 5% se 1% tak kam kar detaa he. aadhunik lens coating jatil bahu-paraton se milakar banati he or keval 0.25% yaa usase kam pratibimbit karti he, saath hi adhiktam chamak or prakritik rangon ke saath ek acchhee chhavi detii he.
 ruf prism vaali doorabeenon main prakaash do raaston main vibhajit ho jaataa he jo ruf prism kii peeth ke kisi bhi taraf se paravartit hota he. aadha prakaash ruf kii sataha 1 se sataha 2 par paravartit hota he. prakaash kaa bachaa hua aadha hissaa sataha 2 se sataha 1 par paravartit hota he. is kaaran prakaash aanshik rup se polaraise ho jaataa he . iske aage hone vaale parvartanon se iskaa polaraise sadish badalata he parantu yah prayek path main phokalt pendulum ke sadrush badalata he. alag alag pathon par ja rahaa prakaash jab miltaa he, tab prayek path ke polaraise sadish mail nahin khate hai. polaraise sadishon ke bich ke kone ko fez shift yaa jyamitiya fez athwa beri fez kaha jaataa he. bhinn jyamitiya fez vaale alag pathon ke takaraane par chhavi main ghatati-badhati tivrata kaa vitaran praapt hota he, jisase praapt hone vaala contrast va rizolution poro prism pranali ke mukaable kam ho jaataa he. ruf prism kii ruf sataha par in avaanchit hastkshep prabhaav ko kam karne hetu kuch vishesh dividyutiya coating padaarthon kaa vaashp nirupan kiya jaataa he, in padaarthon ko fez-sudhaar coating athwa pi-coating kehete hai. yah coating do pathon ke bich jyamitiya fez main antar ko thik karke donon pathon se aa rahe prakaash ko ek hi fez main parivartit karti he jisase takaraav ke kaaran chhavi karaab nahin hone paati he.
 shmit-pechan ruf prism athwa ebbe-konig ruf prism kaa prayog kar rahi doorabine fez coating se vishesh rup se labhanvit hoti hai. poro prism aadhaarit doorabine alag alag path se aa rahi prakaash kirnon ko punah ekatrit nahin karatin atah inhen fez coating se koi vishesh laabh nahin hota.
 shmit-pechan ruf prism kaa prayog kar rahi doorabeenon main ruf prism kii satahon ko darpan coating kiya jaataa he taaki uski kisi ek sataha par pad rahaa prakaash yadi seemaant kone se kam par pade to uskaa sampoorn aatanrik paravartan na ho jaye. darpan coating ke bina adhikansh prakaash kii kshati ho jaaegi. shmit-pechan ruf prism main aluminium darpan coating athwa rajat darpan coating kaa prayog hota he.

 poro prism tathaa ruf prism vaali doorabine jo ebbe-konig ruf prism kaa prayog karti hai, inako darpan coating kaa prayog nahin karnaa padataa kyonki ye prism 100% paravartaneeyata sampoorn aatanrik paravartan se praapt kar lete hai.

 doorabeen ke sainya upyog kaa ek lanbaa itihaas he. 19veen sadi ke ant tak gallilian dizin vyapak rup se prayog main tha tathaa uske pashchaat poro prism dizine kaa prayog kiya jane laga. saamaanya sainya prayog ke liye banaayin gayi doorabine apni asainya samakakshon ke mukaable kahin adhik majboot banaayin jaati hai. sainya upyog main najuk kendriya focus ke sthaan par swatantra focus kaa prayog kiya jaataa he, isse doorabeen ko vaataavaran ke lihaaz se abhedya banane main aasaani hoti he tathaa prabhaavshaalee vaataavaran anukulan pradaan kiya ja saktaa he. sainya doorabeenon ke prijmon par aluminium kii atirikt coating kii jaati he jisase ki bhig jane par bhi unki parvartaneeyata kam naa ho. doorabeen kaa ek or prayukt rup "trench doorabeen" hota he jo darasal doorabeen va periscope kaa mishran hota he, iskaa prayog topakhane kaa pataa lagane main kiya jaataa he, iskaa prayog karte samay sainik apana sar munder ke neech rakhte hue dekh saktaa he, jisase use suraksha praapt hoti he.
