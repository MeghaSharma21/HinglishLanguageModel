  
 putempuvelil chandrika tulsi, 31 agast 1991 ko palakkad main janme, kerala kii ek bhartiya badminton khilaadi he. 2010 dakshin asian khelon main unhone mahila yugal main rajat padak or bhartiya team ke saath swarna padak jeeta. 2012 main vah bharat international badminton championships main safal rahi thi. unhone telugana ke khilaaf badminton ekal main 34 wein rashtriya khelon 2015 main swarna padak jeeta. april 2016 main, vah unex sunraise 80 wein rashtriya badminton championships ekal khitaab jeetin.
