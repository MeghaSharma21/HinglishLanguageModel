 golestan iran ke 31 praanton main se ek he. yah desh ke uttar purv main tathaa caspian sagar ke dakshin main sthit he. isaki rajdhani gorgan he.
 golestan 1997 main mazandaraan praant se nikaalkar banaaya gaya tha. isaki jansankhya 16 lakh tathaa kshetrafal 20,380 varg kilometre he. is praant kii badi bastiyaan is prakaar hai: gorgan, gonbad kawoos, bandar torkman, bandar gaz, aliyabad-e katul, cord kuy, fenderesk tathaa minu dasht. aaj kaa gorgan 1937 tak esterba yaa astarabad kaha jaataa tha.
