 devendra kumaar nim ek rajaneta or uttar pradesh kii 17veen vidhaan sabha ke sadsy hai. ye uttar pradesh ke saharanpur jile ke rampur maniharan vidhaan sabha kshetra kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai.
 devendra kumaar nim ne uttar pradesh vidhansabha chunaav, 2017 main bhartiya janata party ke pratyaashee ke rup main chunaav ladaa or apane nikatatam pratidwandvi bahujan samaaj party ke pratyaashee rabindra kumaar molhu ko 595 maton se parajeet kar jeet darj kii.
