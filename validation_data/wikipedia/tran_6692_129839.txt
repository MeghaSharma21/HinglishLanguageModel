 selina jetali, ek bhartiya abhinetri or purv sundari he. unhen 2001 main femina miss india kaa taj pahanaaya gaya tha.
 jetali kabul, afghanistanmen ek punjabi bhartiya pita or afgaani maa ke yahaan janmin. unke pita, ve.ke. jetali, bhartiya sena ke ek sevanivrutt karnal he, or unki maa, mita, ek afagaan hindu, bhartiya sena main ek nurse thi, saath hi saath afagaan purv sundari bhi. unka ek bhai he, jo bhartiya sena main sevarat he.
 selina, lakhanu sahitkai nagaron main pali badhin, jahaan unhone nagar ke maunteshari skool main adhyayan kiya. unhone baharaampur, urisa main bhi adhyayan kiya jab unke pita ko vahaan tainaat kiya gaya tha. unhone kolkata, bharat main apana sabase adhik samay bitaayaa he. vah dharm se ek hindu hai. unhone kaha he ki jab vah choti thim, vah sena main bhartee hona chaahati thim, yaa to ek chikitsak yaa ek payalat ke rup main. unhone indira gandhi naishanal open uniwarsity se vaanijya main snaatak kii degree praapt kii.
 jetali miss univars 2001 ke final main pratiyogi thim. femina miss india univers ke sheershak ke alaava, selina ne miss margo khubasurat twachaa, india times surbers chwis or MTV kaa most wanted award jeeta.
 2003 main unhone film udyog main apni shuruyaat firoz khan kii film jaanashin ke saath kii. 2007 main jetali newjiland gayi apni pehali antarrashtriya film lav haij no language, ek romantic kamedi kii shooting ke liye. saath hi vahaan kisi ne chori se unako nishaanaa banaaya jo bharat se hi unka peechha kar rahaa tha. kaha gaya ki jetali ko is anubhav se maansik aghat pahunchaa tha.
 jetali ko quest ouf sheherezade main faras kii ek rajkumaari kii bhoomika nibhaane ke liye shaun conary or orlando bluemake saath anubandhit kiya gaya he. film arbian nits par aadhaarit he or ken khan dwaara nirdeshit he. yah selina kaa hollywood main pehla upkram hoga. vah misr paryaton or di'damas kii brands ambesder bhi hai.
 jetali LGBT samudaay ke adhikaar or samanta kii samarthak hai or bharat main samalangik adhikaar andolan kaa bhi samarthan karti hai.
