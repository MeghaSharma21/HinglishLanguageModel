 dakshin asian kshetreey sahyog sanghathan mahasachiv, saarc sachivalay kaa pramukh he, jiskaa kathmandu, nepal main mukhyalay he. saarc aath dakshin asian sadsy deshon, afghanistan, bangladesh, bhutan, bharat, maldive, nepal, pakistan or srilanka ke bich ek aarthik or bhu raajnneetik sangh he. sadsy-rajyon ke mantriyon kii parishad dwaara chunaav se teen saal kii avadhi ke liye mahasachiv niyukt kiya jaataa he. 1987 ko bangladeshi rajanyik abul ahsaan ne apane pehle mahasachiv ke rup main kathmandu main saarc sachivalay kii sthapana kii thi or nepal ke raja birendra bir bikram shah ne udghaatan kiya tha. isaki rachana ke baad se, iske sadsy deshon ne kul 13 teraa sachivon ko rup main yogadan diya he. pakistan ke rajanyik amjad hossain bi siyaal 1 march, 2017 ko prabhaar sanbhaalane vaale maujuuda mahasachiv hai.
