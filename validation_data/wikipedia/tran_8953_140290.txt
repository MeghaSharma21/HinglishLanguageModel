 chumbakeey lolak vah dolak he jisamen dhaage se lataka dhaatu kaa lolak kai chumbakon ke chumbakeey kshetra tathaa gurutveey kshetra ke adheen gati karata he. lolak ke dhaage kii lambaai itani rakhi jaati he ki vah kisi bhi chumbak ko na chhu sake. 
 isamen lolak kii gati gurutveey kshetra tathaa chumbakon ke chumbakeey kshetra - donon se nirdhaarit hota he. yadhyapi is prayog main lolak kii gati 'aniyamit' dikhti he phir bhi yah 'diterministic' he. yah gati aarmabhik sthitiyon par nirbhar karti he. is gati ko 'diterministic keos' kaha gaya he.
