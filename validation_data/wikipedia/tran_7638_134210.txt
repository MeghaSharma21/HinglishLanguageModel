 grami puraskaar
 grami award, yaa grami, da recording academy dwaara sammanit kiya gaya sammaan mukhya rup se angreji-bhashaa sangeet udyog main utkrisht uplabdhiyon ko pahchaanane ke liye he. vaarshik prastuti samaaroh main pramukh kalakaaron ke pradarshan or un puraskaron kii prastuti hoti he, jinamein ek adhik lokapriy ruchi he. yah sangeet udyog ko emi puraskaar, tony awards or academy puraskaar jaise anya pradarshan puraskaron ke rup main manyata detaa he. pehla gremi award samaaroh 4 may, 1 9 5 9 ko yojit kiya gaya tha, jo 1 9 58 ke liye kalakaaron dwaara sangeet kii uplabdhiyon kaa sammaan or sammaan karne ke liye yojit kiya gaya tha. 2011 ke samaaroh ke baad, academy ne 2012 ke liye kai grami award shreniyon ko bhang kar diya. 59 veen gremi awards, sarvshereshth kaa sammaan oktober 2015 se sitambar 2016 tak uplabdhiyaan, 12 farvari, 2017 ko los angeles ke stepuls centre main yojit kii gayi.
 itihaas
 1 9 50 ke dashak main hollywood walk of fame project main graamiya kaa mul tha.  walk of fame cameti ke liye chune gaye ricarding adhikariyon ne ek mahatvapurn recording udyog kii suchi taiyaar karne main kaam kiya, jo walk of fame star ke liye arhata praapt kar sakate the, unhen ehsaas hua ki bahut se log the jo apane vyavsaay main netaa the jo kabhi ek star nahin kamaate the hollywood bulward par sangeet ke adhikariyon ne auskar or emiz ke samaan unke udyog dwaara die gaye ek puraskaar kaa nirmaan karke ise sudhaarney kaa nirnay liya. yah neshnal academy of recording arts and sciensej kii shuruyaat thi is tarah ke ek puraskaar kaa nirmaan karne kaa nirnay lene ke baad, yah abhi bhi ek sawaal tha ki use kya karnaa he; ek kaam karne vaala sheershak edi tha, jo ki phonograph ke avishkaarak thomas edison kaa sammaan karata tha. aakhirkaar ve puraskaar ke liye emile berlinner, gramophone ke avishkaar kaa naam istemaal karne par based hue the, jinhen pehali baar 1 9 58 main diya gaya tha.  
 pehla puraskaar samaaroh 4 may 1 9 5 9 ko bevarli hills california main bevarli hilton hotel or neuyork shahar main park sheraton hotel or 28 graamiyon ke saath do sthanon par ek saath yojit kiya gaya. ek varsh main 100 se adhik samay tak pahunchane vaale shreniyon main jode gaye or hataae gaye varshon ke saath puraskaron kii sankhya badi or bad gai. 1 9 5 9 main yojit duusare grami awards, television ke liye pehla samaaroh tha, lekin 1 9 71 main 13 veen vaarshik grami award tak samaaroh yojit nahin kiya gaya tha
 gramophone trophy
 sone kii chadhaav vaali traphiyaan, prayek ko sone kaa pani chadha hua gramophone dikhaati he, jo rideway, colorado main billings artwork ke haath main ikatthe hote hai. 1 99 0 main, mul grami dizine ko nayaa rup diya gaya, trophy ko bada or shandaar banaate hue, ek majboot mishra dhaatu ko kam kshati ke liye paaramparik naram netritv main badal diya gaya. billams ne ek jasta mishra dhaatu kaa namakaran kiya jo ki gramium he, jo trademark he. praptakarta ke naam par traphiyaan puraskaar ghoshit karne ke baad tak uplabdh nahin hai, isliye prayek varsh prasaaran ke liye "stant" traphiyaan pun: upyog kii jaati hai. 
 farvari 200 9 tak, 7,578 gremi traphiyaan pradaan kii gayi
 shreniyan
 "general field" chaar puraskaar hai jinhen style dwaara pratibandhit nahin kiya gaya he. kalakaar kii tulana main yadi album kaa album kalakaar or purna album kii production team ko sammanit kiya jaataa he. kalakaar ke alaava anya agar kisi ekal geet ke produsar or production team ko varsh kaa record diya jaataa he. ek geet ke lekhak / sangeetkaar ko varsh kaa geet diya jaataa he best new artist ko ek aashajanak safalataa kalakaar se sammanit kiya gaya he, jo paatrata varsh ke dauraan relies karata he, pehali recording jo us kalakaar kii saarvajanik pahchaan sthapit karti he . in sabhi chaar puraskaron ko jeetne vaale keval do kalakaar christopher cross hai, jinhone 1 9 80 main chaaron baar jeeta tha or adele ne 2009 main sarvshereshth new artist puraskaar or 2012 or 2017 main anya teenon ko jeeta tha. anya puraskaar vishisht shaileyon main pradarshan or utpaadan ke liye die gaye hai, saath hi artwork or video jaise anya yogadanon ke liye bhi. sangeet udyog main lambey samay tak chalane vaale yogadanon ke liye vishesh puraskaar die jate hai. badi sankhya main puraskaar shreniyon ke kaaran, or vibhinn kalakaaron dwaara kai pradarshnon kii suvidha dene kii ichha, keval sabase lokapriy byaaj vaale log - aam taur par lagbhag 10 se 12, jinamein chaar saamaanya kshetra shreniyan or sabase lokapriy sangeet shaileyon main ek yaa do shreniyan - television puraskaar samaaroh main sidhe prastut kii jaati hai. grami awards ke prasaaran ke pehle dopaher main kai anya grami traphiyaan purv-prasaaran 'premier samaaroh' main prastut kii jaati hai.
 2012 shrenee punargathan
 6 april, 2011 ko, da recording academy ne 2012 ke liye kai gremi award shreniyon kaa ek baari overhaal ghoshit kiya. shreniyon kii sankhya 109 se ghataakar 78 kar di gai. sabase mahatvapurn parivartan purush or mahila ekal kalakaaron ke bich or vibhinn style kshetron main sahyog or jodi / samoohon ke bich antar ko samaapt karnaa tha. iske alaava, sahaayak ekal kalakaaron ke liye kai shreniyan band thim in shreniyon main recording ab sarvshereshth ekal pradarshnon ke liye saamaanya shreniyon ke antargat aati hai
 rock field main hard rock or metal albamon ke liye alag-alag shreniyan sanyukt thim or ek rengne vaali pravishtiyon kii sankhya ke kaaran best rock instrumental pradarshan shrenee samaapt ho gai thi.
 aar and bi main, sarvshereshth samakaleen aar and bi album or anya arendaby albamon ke bich kaa antar samaapt ho gaya he. ve ab ek, saamaanya sarvshereshth aar and bi album shrenee main suvidha dete hai.
 rap main, sarvottam rap ekal kalakaar or sarvshereshth rap jodi yaa samooh kii shreniyan nae best rap pradarshan varg main villin ho gai hai.
 sabase unmoolan jad varg main hui. 2011 tak or isamen shaamil the, hawaii kshetreey sangeet, mul america sangeet or zeedeko / kejun sangeet jaise vibhinn kshetreey america sangeet praroopon ke liye alag-alag shreniyan thim in shreniyon ke liye lagaataar kam sankhya main pravishtiyon ke kaaran, da recording academy ne in sabhi sangeet vividhtaon ko nae sarvshereshth kshetreey roots sangeet album main shaamil karne kaa faisla kiya, jisamen polka shaamil he, jisamen 2009 main apni alag shrenee kho gai thi. 
 samaan style ke kshetra main, pravishtiyon kii sankhya ke kaaran, paaramparik or samakaleen bluez shreniyon or paaramparik or samakaleen lok shreniyon main se prayek ko ek prati style main samekit kiya gaya or samakaleen lok or america or samakaleen or paaramparik bluez. vishwa sangeet style kshetra main, paaramparik or samakaleen shreniyon ko bhi vilay kar diya gaya.
 shastreey style ke kshetra main, iske mukhya shrenee best clasical album ko band kar diya gaya kyonki is shrenee main adhikansh praptakartaaon ne ek hi elbum ke liye anya shastreey shreniyon main se ek bhi jeeta tha. shastreey recording ab varsh shrenee ke mukhya album ke liye paatr hai.
 alag-alag shreniyon kii prakruti ko behatar dhang se pradarshit karne ke liye kuch chote naam parivartan bhi the. yah recording academy dwaara nirdhaarit kiya gaya tha ki susamachar style ke kshetra main "susamachar" shabd paaramparik aatma susamachar kii chhaviyon or dhvaniyon ko aachhadit karata he or vartmaan samakaleen isai sangeet ko chhod detaa he. isliye, style kshetra or kuch shreniyon kaa naam badalakar engil / samakaleen isai sangeet kaha jaataa he. 
 2012 ke baad se, shreniyon or style kshetron kii suchi main kiye gaye samaayojan main bahut kam samaayojan kiya gaya he. shreniyon kii sankhya 2012 main 78 se badhkar 2017 main 84 ho gai he.
 pravesh prakriya or namankit vyaktiyon kaa chayan
 neshnal academy of recording arts and sciences or naras ke vyaktigat sadsy ke saath panjikrut media companiyaan recording ke liye vichaar kar sakati hai. pravishtiyaan online kii jaati hai or kaam kii ek bhautik pratilipi NARAS ko bheji jaati he. ek baar kaary darj ho jane ke baad, recording udyog se 150 se adhik visheshgyon ko shaamil karne ke liye satron kii samiksha kii jaati he, yah nirdhaarit karne ke liye ki kya kaam sahi shrenee main darj kiya gaya he yaa nahin.
 paatr pravishtiyon kii parinami suchiyaan, matdaan ke sadasyon ko parichaalit kii jaati hai, jinamein se prayek saamaanya kshetron or nau se zyada main namankit karne ke liye vot kar sakate hai 30 anya kshetron main se apane mataptr par prayek shrenee main sabase adhik vot arjeet karne vaale paanch recording namankit vyakti ban jate hai, jabaki kuch shreniyon main samiksha samitiyaan hoti hai jo antim 5 ummidavaaron ko nirdhaarit karti hai. yadi namakan prakriya main ek taai he to paanch se adhik namankit vyakti ho sakate hai
 jabaki academy of moshan pikchar arts and sciensej ke sadasyon ko aam taur par screening karne ke liye aamantrit kiya jaataa he yaa unhen auskar ke liye namankit filmon ke dividi bheje jate hai, naras sadasyon ko namankit recording praapt nahin hote hai. iske bajaay, ve ek nijee online shravan samaaroh tak pahunch praapt karte hai
 antim voting
 namakan ke baad nirdhaarit kiya gaya he, antim voting mataptr naras matdaan karne vaale sadasyon ko bheje jate hai, jo phir saamaanya kshetron main or 30 kshetron main nau se zyada nahin vot sakate. sadasyon ko protsaahit kiya jaataa he, lekin aavashyak nahin he, keval visheshgyata ke apane kshetron main vot karne ke liye. pramukh swatantra lekhankan form delite toch tehmatsu dwaara gupt rup se matdaan kiya jaataa he. voton ke sarini ke baad vijetaon ko grami awards main ghoshit kiya gaya he. ek shrenee main sarvaadhik voton ke saath recording jeet jaati he or ek taai ho sakati he . vijeta ko gremi awards ke saath prastut kiya jaataa he or jo log jeet nahin karte unhen unke namakan ke liye ek padak diya jaataa he. donon matdaan dauron main academy ke sadasyon ko keval gunvattta ke aadhaar par vot dena hota he, or bikri, chart pradarshan, vyaktigat dosti, kshetreey praathamikta yaa company vafadari se prabhavit nahin hona chahie. uphaaron kii sweekruti nishiddh he sadasyon ko ek aise tareeke se vot dene kaa aagrah kiya jaataa he jo academy or unke sadsy samudaay kii akhandta ko surakshit rakhata he. haalanki panjikrut media companiyaan pravishtiyaan jamaa kar sakati hai, haalanki ve matdaan ke kisi bhi daur main vot nahin de sakate hai. 59 veen vaarshik grami awards ke liye paatrata avadhi 1 oktober 2015 se 30 sitambar, 2016 tak he
 sthaan
 1 9 71 se pehle, grami award samaaroh usee din alag-alag sthanon par yojit kiye gaye the. mul rup se neuyork shahar or los angeles mejabaan shaharon the chicago 1 9 62 main ek mejabaan shahar main shaamil hua, or tab 1 9 65 main naishaville chauthe sthaan par rahaa. 1 9 71 samaaroh, los angeles main hollywood paladium main yojit kiya gaya tha, ek sthaan par jagah lene vaale pehle the. samaaroh ko neuyork shahar main medisson squaire garden ke falit forum main le jaaya gaya, or uske baad naishaville ke tennesi theatre ko agle do varshon main sthanantarit kiya gaya. phir 1 9 74 se 2003 tak, neuyork shahar or los angeles ke vibhinn sthanon main graamiyaan yojit kii gayi. ullekhneey sthanon main neuyork shahar ke medisson squaire garden or radio city music haul shaamil hai; or los angeles ke shroin auditorium, stepuls centre or hollywood paladium. 2004 main, stepuls centre puraskaar samarohon kaa sthaayee ghar ban gaya. grami awards ke itihaas ko sangrakshit karne ke liye laa live main stepuls centre se sadak ke paar grami sangrahalay banaaya gaya tha. sangrahalay kii sadakon par footpath par ambeded kaansya discs, hollywood walk of fame ke samaan he, prayek varsh ke sheersh vijetaon, record kaa varsh, sarvshereshth nai kalakaar, varsh kaa album, or varsh kaa geet sammanit kiya jaataa he. puraskaar samaaroh los angeles kings, los angels lekars or los angeles katarani ko sadak ke khel kii ek vistaarit lambaai khilne ke liye majaboor karata he.

 tivi prasaaran or reating

 grami or record bikri
 jab television graamiya 1 9 75 main prasiddh ho gaye, to gremi awards vijetaon or uske baad ke record bikri ke bich ek rishta shuru hua. billboard patrika ke kai lekh ek grami-behatar record bikri jeetne ke vanijyik prabhaav kaa sanchaar karte hai. haalanki, 1 9 84 ke baad tak yah nahin tha ki gremi ke praptakartaaon ke record bikri main paryaapt vruddhi dikhaate hai. yah bade paimaane par naras or neshnal association of record marchendisers dwaara kiye gaye ek samjhaute ke kaaran tha. is samjhaute ke antargat "record label, stikar, poster or anya bindu-kii-kharid vaali saamagree ko 'gremi nomini' yaa 'gremi awards vijeta' pradaan karte hai, jo khudara vikritaon ke vipanan prabhaav ko sudhaarney ke liye istemaal kar sakate hai."
