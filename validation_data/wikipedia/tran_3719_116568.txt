 ural, pashchimi rus kii ek parvat shrinkhalaa he jo uttar se dakshin kii ore tak vistrut he. yah bhugolic rup se asia or europe ko alag karti he. isse kai nadiyaan nikalati he. pramukh nadi kama caspian sagar main apana jal visarjeet karti he.
 yah parvat shrunkhlaa uttar main arctic mahaasaagar se dakshin main caspian sagar tak faili hui he, or europe ko asia mahadweep se alag karti he. is parvat shrunkhlaa kaa utthan kai yugon main hua he. shrunkhalaaon kaa vistaar uttar se pashchim tathaa uttar se purv kii ore he or sarvaadhik oonchaai dakshini bhaag main pai jaati he. is parvat kii sanyukt banaavat isaki bhoumiki dashaon se spasht parilakshith hoti he. 
 ural parvat shrunkhlaa ko teen bhaagon main baanta jaataa he:
 yah kaara kii khadi se prarambh hokar dakshin-pashchim main 64 ansh uttari akshansh tak faili he. isamen kai spasht shrankhlaayen pai jaati hai. yah parvatakshetra dakshin-purv kii ore chattani, oobad-khabad tathaa adhik dhaluvaan he tathaa europea rus ke daldalon kii ore kam dhaalu he. isaki sarvocch chotiyaan khard yaves 3,715 foot tathaa paiyer 4,764 foot unchi hai. mukhya shrunkhlaa ke pashchimi bhaag main paratdaar chattaane pai jaati hai. dakshini bhaag main ural kii sarvocch chotiyaan sabaliya 5,402, foot tathaa muraai chakhl, 5,545 foot hai. parvatiy dhaalon par ghaney jangal paae jate hai. dakshini bhaag main 2400 foot kii oonchaai tak vanaspati pai jaati he lekin uttari bhaag main arctic vrutt ke paas parvat ke paad pravesh tak hi vanaspati simit he. 65 ansh uttari akshansh ke lagbhag vaneey vanaspati lupt ho jaati he. 64 ansh uttari akshansh se 41 ansh uttari akshansh ke madhya ek pathari kshetra he, jahaan jal vibhaajak uttar-pashchimi disha main failaa he. yahan vistrut, sam tathaa daldali ghaatiyaan hai. chotiyon kii ausat oonchaai 3,000 foot he. yung tump shikhar 4,170 foot unchaa he. issh kshetra main bastiyon kaa abhaav he.
 isaki chaudaai lagbhag 125 kimi he. yahan lohe, taanbe or sone kii khaane hai. madhya ural kii seemaa uttar main denezkin kamen se nirdhaarit hoti he. nimna pathari bhaag se siberia ke liye sadak jaati he. jalavibhaajak 1245 foot kii oonchaai par paaya jaataa he. madhya urail ghaney jangalon se aachhadit he. ghaatiyon main tathaa nimna dhaalon par upjau mitti evam ghani gramin basti pai jaati he.
 yah uttar-purv tathaa dakshin-pashchim main vistrut teen samaantar shrunkhalaaon main vibhakt he. mukhya urail parvat kii shrunkhlaa 2,200 foot se 2,800 foot unchi he. mand dhaalon par adhiktar jangal hai tathaa nimna bhaagon main charaagaah paae jate hai. dakshin kii ore lagbhag 1,500 foot unchaa pathari kshetra he jisamen nadiyon kii gahari ghaatiyaan pai jaati hai. yah kshetra bolga tak failaa he. ural parvat ek modadaar parvat he jisamen tritiy yug kii chattaane pai jaati hai. yah pashchim main silyurian, divoni, karvoni, parmian tathaa triasik kalp kii paraton dwaara dhankaa hua he. isamen kai samaantar mode paae jate hai.
