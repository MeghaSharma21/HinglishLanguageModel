 halabja iraqi kurdistan main ek shahar he, jo iraq kii rajdhani baghdad se 240 kimi purvottar or irani seemaa se 14 kimi duuri par sthit he.yah shahar iran-iraq seemaa main faile bade heverman kshetra rup main janaa jaataa he. halabja purvottar main havaraaman or shrarway range se kshetra se hua he, dakshin main balambo kshetra or pashchim main sarvaan nadi he. halabja shahar main taur par kurd log kurdi boli bolte hai, lekin aasapaas ke gaanvon ke kuch nivasee hevami boli bolte hai.
 halabja kaa ek lanbaa itihaas he, jaisaa ki bakar awa jaise paas ke puratathvik sthalon main khudaai se sabit hua he. kabristaan main ahamed mukhtaar jaaf, tayyar bag jaaf or aadila khanim jaise kai aitihaasik logon ke kabre shaamil hai. agast 2009 main, shahar ke ababil jile main teen 17 veen shatabdi ke makbare kii khoj kii gai thi..
 isse pataa chalata he ki shahar kuch sroton dwaara sanketit kuch had tak purana he, jiskaa daawa he ki yah 1850 ke bare main turk samrajya dwaara banaaya gaya tha. haalanki, aadhunik vikaas 20 veen shatabdi kii shuruyaat se he. daakghar 1924 main kholaa gaya or pehla skool agle varsh kholaa gaya. cusri pasha or hamid bag bazaar 1932 main banaae gaye the. bijli 1940 tak shahar tak nahin pahunch pai thi..
 20 veen shatabdi kii shuruyaat main, halabja main kai british sainik tainaat the. pratham vishwa yuddh ke dauraan, sthaneey mahila adela khanam ne kai british sainikon ko bachaaya tha, jiske parinamswaroop angrejon ne unhen bahadur kii rajkumaari khan bahadur sheershak se sammanit kiya tha..

 iran dwaara samarthit kurd peshmarga gorilla ne iran-iraq yuddh ke antim charan main halabja ko mukt kiya. 11:00 baje, 16 march, 1988 ko paaramparik topakhane ke hamalon ke do dinon ke baad, iraqi vimaanon ne shahar par gas kanchi chodi.. shahar or aasapaas ke jile par bam, topakhane kii aag or rasinic hathiyaaron ke saath hamalaa kiya gaya tha, jinamein se aakhri sabase vinaashkari sabit hua. rasinic hamale ke tatkaal parinaam ke rup main kam se kam 5,000 log maare gaye or anumaan lagaaya gaya he ki 7,000 log ghayal ho gaye the or logo ko lambii avadhi kii beemaari kaa saamana karnaa padaa tha. halabja shahar par hamale ke adhikansh peedit kurd naagarik the...
 aisa mana jaataa he ki hamale main tabun, sarin or viex, saath hi sarson gas bhi shaamil thi. haalanki, purv varisht siie vishleshak stephen si. peletier ke anusaar, iraq main hamale main istemaal hone vaale tantrika agent ke paas nahin tha, lekin sarson gas thi jiskaa upyog iran-iraq yuddh main kiya gaya tha. kabhi-kabhi yah suzav diya jaataa he. ki in rasinic hathiyaaron main synide bhi shaamil kiya gaya tha, haalanki is daawey ko andeh main daal diya gaya tha, kyonki synide ashuddh tabun kaa prakritik upaj he.. halabja par hamalaa kukhyat anfal abhiyaan ke bich hua tha, jisamen saddaam hossain ne iran-iraq yuddh ke dauraan kurd vidrohiyon ko hinsak failane kaa dabaa kiya diya tha. lekin hamale kaa mul kaaran tha iran-iraq yuddh ke dauraan iraqi kurd log kaa iran kaa samarthan kar rahe the.
 yuddh khatm hone se pehle shahar ko puri tarah se nasht kar diya tha.. march 2010 main, iraqi ucch aapraadhik nyaayalay ne halabja narsanhaar ko ek narsanhaar aparaadh ke rup main manyata di; kurdistaan kshetreey sarkaar ne nirnay kaa swaagat kiya.].
 halabja main bumadhya jalwaayu jalwaayu he jo garmi main garm or thand main adhik sardi hoti he.
