 swaziland, aadhikarik naam kingdom of swaziland, dakshini africa main sthit ek samprabhu desh he. purv kii ore mozambik, va uttar, pashchim tathaa dakshin kii ore dakshin africa is sthal-ruddha desh ke padosi hai.
 swaziland africa ke sabase chote deshon main se ek he — isaki adhiktam lambaai 200 ki. me. va adhiktam chaudaai 130 ki. me. he. chote aakaar ke baavjood is desh kii jalwaayu va sthalakriti vividh he — kahin sheethal va parvatamay pathaar, to kahin ushn va shushk maidan | jansankhya mukhyatah nrijaateey swaaziyon kii he, jinaki maatrubhaasha swazi he. unhone is samrajya kii sthapana guane tritiy ke netritv main attharahavin shatabdi ke madhya main kii; vartmaan simaaon kaa nirdhaaran varsh 1881 main kiya gaya. 1903 se 1967 tak swaziland ek britani sangrakshit rajya tha. 6 sitambar 1968 ko is desh ne punah swatantraaa praapt kii.
 swaziland ek purna raaj-tantra he, jiske vartmaan shaasha raja mswati tritiy hai. ve rashtradhyaksh bhi hai, tathaa desh ke pradhanmantri sahit sansad ke donon sadanon ke kai pratinidhiyon kii niyukti bhi karte hai. sansad ke nichle sadan main bahumat nirdhaarit karne ke liye har 5 varshon main chunaav yojit hote hai. desh kaa vartmaan samvidhan 2005 main apanaaya gaya.
 swaziland ek laghu arthvyavastha kaa vikasshil desh he. prati vyakti sakal gharelu utpaada 7917 dollar hone ke kaaran, swaziland ko antarrashtriya mudra kosh dwaara nimnatar-madhyam aay vaale desh ke rup main vargeekrut kiya gaya he. is desh kaa mukhya sthaneey vyapaarik saajhedaar dakshin africa he. swaziland kii mudra, lilangini, dakshin africa kii mudra rand ke anusaar aanki jaati he. iske pramukh gair-africa vyapaarik saajhedaar sanyukt rajya america evam europea sangh hai. desh ke rojgaar kaa adhikansh bhaag krishi evam vinirmaan ke kshetron se aata he. swaziland dakshin africa vikaas samudaay, africa sangh tathaa rashtramandal deshon kaa sadsy he.
 swazi aabaadi ghor swasthya samasyaon se jujh rahi he— aids or tapedic jaisi bimaariyaan gambhir chunautiyaan hai. varsh 2013 ke prakkalan anusaar swaziland main anumaanit jivan pratyaasha 50 saal he. swaziland kii janata kaaphi yuva he; aabaadi kii madhyika aayu 20.5 varsh he tathaa jansankhya kaa 37.4 pratishat 14 varsh yaa usase kaam aayu kaa he.
 swaziland apni samskruti ke liye prasiddh he. agast/sitambar main yojit umhalanga samaaroh, or disambar/janvari main yojit inkwala rajkeey sanskaar, raashtra ke sabase mahatvapurn samaaroh hai.
