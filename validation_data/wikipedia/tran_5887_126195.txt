 prativarsh 25 disambar ko tulsi pujan diwas manaaya jaataa he. tulsi sampoorn dharaa ke liye vardaan he, atyant upyogi aushadhi he, maatr itnaa hi nahin, yah to maanav jivan ke liye amrit he! yah keval sharir swasthya kii drushti se hi nahin, apitu dharmik, aadhyatmik, paryaavarniya evam vaigyanik aadi vibhinn drushtiyon se bhi bahut mahatvapurn he.
 tulsi uttam avsaadarodhak evam utsaah, sphurti, sattviktaa vardhak hone se yah parv manaanaa vardaantulya sabit hota he. dhanurmaas main sabhi sakaam karm varjit hote hai parantu bhagwatpritirth karm vishesh faldaayi va prasannataa dene vaale hote hai. 25 disambar dhanurmaas ke bich kaa samay hota he. tulsi pujan diwas bharat main shraddha purvak manaaya jaataa he.is pahal ko sant shri aashaaraamji baapu ne shuru kii.
 25 disambar ko subah snanadi ke baad ghar ke svachh sthaan par tulsi ke gamale ko jameen se kuch unche sthaan par rakhen. usamen yah mantra bolte hue jal chadhaayein-
 mahaprasadjanani sarv saubhagyawardhini.
 aadhi vyaadhi haraa nityam tulsi tvan namostute..
 phir tulasyai namah mantra bolte hue tilak karen, akshat va pushp arpit karen tathaa kuch prasad chadhaayein. deepak jalaakar aarti karen or tulsi ji kii 7,11, 21, 51 yaa 111 parikrama karen. us shuddh vaataavaran main shant ho ke bhagwatprarthana evam bhagavannam yaa gurumantra kaa jap karen. tulsi ke paas baithkar pranayam karne se bal, buddhi or oj kii vruddhi hoti he.
 tulsi pattey daalkar prasad vitarit karen. tulsi ke sameep raatri 12 baje tak jaagran kar bhajan, kirthan, satsang-shravan va jap karke bhagavad-vishranti paayen. tulsi namashtak kaa paath bhi punyakaarak he. tulsi pujan apane ghar yaa tulsi van main athwa yatha anukool kisi bhi pavitra sthaan par kar sakate hai.
 tulsi namashtak:
 vrundan vrindaavaneen vishwapavaneen vishwapujitam.
 pushpasaraan nandineen c tulsin krishnajivanim..
 etannamashtakan chaitastotran namarthsanyutam.
 yah pathettan c sampoojya soshvamedhafalan labhet..
 bhagwaan narayan devarshi naradji se kehete hai- "vrinda, vrundaavanee, vishwapavani, vishwapujita, pushpasara, nandini, tulsi or krishnajivani – ye tulsi devi ke aath naam hai. yah sarthak namavali stotra ke rup main parinat he. jo purush tulsi kii puja karke is namashtak kaa paath karata he, use ashwamedh yajna kaa fal praapt hota he." 
 25 disambar se pehle tulsi kaa paudha har ghar main pahunche taaki har koi is punya-swasthya pradaayak, dhan-dhaany-saubhaahgya vardhak, hriday main bhagavadbhakti utpann karne vaale pujan kaa laabh le saken. yah lokhitkaari daivi kaary khub vyapak ho or samast vishvamaanav isse labhanvit ho is uddeshy se shri yoga vedant seva samiti evam mahila utthan mandal dwaara "tulsi pujan diwas" evam "ghar-ghar tulsi lagaao abhiyaan" shuru kiya gaya he. shri yoga vedant seva samiti har varsh ghar ghar tulsi lagavane ke kaarykram chalaata he or iske aushadhiy gunon se parichit karwaata he.
 ham 1 din main lagbhag 1.5 kilo bhojan karte hai, 2 se 3 litre pani pite hai lekin 21 hajaar 600 shwaas lete hai. usamen 11 hajaar litre hawa lete chhodte hai, jisase hamen lagbhag 10 kilo bhojan kaa bal miltaa he. ab vah vaayu jitani gandi hoti he, utana hi logon kaa swasthya or bhojan kharaab ho jaataa he. tulsi vaayu ko shuddh karti he.
 nilgiri ke vriksh vaayu ko ganda karte hai, jeevanishkthi harte hai. iske vipareet tulsi, pipal ke ped jeevanishkthi vipul pramaan main dete hai. atah tulsi, pipal, neem tathaa aanwale ke vriksh dil kholkar lagane chahie. ye vriksh lagane se aapke dwaara pranimaatr kii badi seva hogi. khud vriksh lagaana or dusron ko prerit karnaa bhi ek seva he. rashtriya kartavya he paryaavaran ke liye ped lagaana. ped hamaare swasthya ke liye or paryaavaran ke liye vardaan hai, aashirwad hai.
 poojaneey vrikshon main tulsi kaa bada mahatvapurn sthaan he. tulsi kaa pujan, darshan, seven va ropan aadhidaivik, aadhibhautik or aadhyatmik – teenon prakaar ke tapon kaa naash kar sukh-samruddhi dene vaala he.
 bhagwaan shiv kehete hai- "tulsi sabhi kaamanaayon ko purna karne vaali he." 
 apane hit saadhan kii ichha se dandakaarany main va rakshason kaa vadh karne ke uddeshy se sarayu tat par bhagwaan shri ram ji ne evam gomti tat par va vrundavan main bhagwaan shrikrishna ne tulsi lagaayi thi. ashok vatika main sita ji ne raamji kii prapti ke liye tulsi ji kaa maanas pujan dhyaan kiya tha. himalay parvat par parvati ji ne shankar ji kii prapti ke liye tulsi kaa vriksh lagaaya tha. 
 bhagwaan vishnu kii koi bhi puja bina tulsi ke purna nahin maani jaati. hamaare rishi-muni apane aasapaas tulsi kaa paudha lagaate va tulsiyukt jal kaa aachman lete the.
 'padam puraana' ke anusaar 'jinke darshanamaatr se karodon godaan kaa fal hota he, un tulsi kaa pujan or vandan kyon na karen !' 
 takneekee visheshgy shri ke.em.jain ne ek vishesh yantra ke madhyam se pareekshan karke yah nishkarsh nikaala ki 'yadi koi vyakti tulsi ke paudhe yaa deshi gaay kii nau baar parikrama kare to uske aabhamandal ke prabhaav kshetra main teen meter kii aashcharyakaarak badhotaree hoti he.' aabhamandal kaa dayera jitna adhik hoga, vyakti utana hi adhik kaaryaksham, maansik rup se kshamtawan va swasth hoga.
 yaa drushtaa nikhilaghasanghashamani sprushtaa vapushpawani.
 roganambhivandita nirasani siktantaktrasini..
 pratyaasttividhaayini bhagawatah krishnasya sanropita.
 nyasta tachcharane vimuktifaladaa tasyai tulasyai namah..
 jo darshan karne par saare paap-samudaay kaa naash kar detii he, sparsh karne par sharir ko pavitra banaati he, pranaam karne par rogon kaa nivaaran karti he, jal se seenchne par yamraj ko bhi bhay pahunchaati he, aropit karne par bhagwaan shrikrishna ke sameep le jaati he or bhagwaan ke charnon main chadhaane par moksharupi fal pradaan karti he, us tulsi devi ko namaskara he. 
 garud puraana main aata he ki tulsi kaa paudha lagane, paalan karne, seenchne tathaa dhyaan, sparsh or gungan karne se manushyon ke purv janmarjeet paap jalakar vinasht ho jate hai.
 brahmavaivart puraana main aata he ki mrityu ke samay jo tulsi pattey sahit jal kaa paan karata he, vah sampoorn paapon se mukt hokar vishnulok main jaataa he.
 skanda puraana ke anusaara jis ghar main tulsi kaa baghichaa hota he, usamen yamadut pravesh nahin karte.
 basi phool or basi jal puja ke liye varjit hai parantu tulsidal or gangaajal basi hone par bhi varjit nahin hai. 
 ghar main lagaayi hui tulsi manushyon ke liye kalyankarini, dhan putr pradaan karne vaali, punyadayini tathaa haribhakti dene vaali hoti he. praatavkaal tulsi kaa darshan karne se suvarna daan kaa fal praapt hota he. 
 apane ghar se dakshin kii ore tulsi-vriksh kaa ropan nahin karnaa chahie, anyathaa yama-yatnaa bhogani padati he. 
 tulsi kii upasthitimaatr se halake spandanon, nakaaratmak shaktiyon evam dusht vichaaron se raksha hoti he.
 ishan kone main tulsi kaa paudha lagane tathaa puja ke sthaan par gangaajal rakhane se barkat hoti he.
 tulsi ko roj jal chadhaane tathaa gaay ke ghi kaa deepak jalaane se ghar main sa kha-samruddhi badhati he.
 jo daridray mitaanaa va sukh-smapda paanaa chahta he, use tulsi pujan diwas ke avasar par shuddh bhav va bhakthi se tulsi ke paudhe kii 108 parikrama kerani chahie.
