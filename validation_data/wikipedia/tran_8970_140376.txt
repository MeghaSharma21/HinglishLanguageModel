 nigade praant main demirkajik shikhar
 toros parvat yaa toras parvat dakshini turki main sthit ek parvatamala he jo us desh ke bumadhya sagar se lage tatavarti ilaake ko kendriya anatolia ke pathari kshetra se alag karti he. yah parvatamala pashchim main evirdir jhil se lekar purv main furaat nadi or dajala nadi ke aarmabhik bhaag tak ek muda hua maarg tay karti he. iskaa sabase unchaa shikhar 3,756 meter lambaa demirkajik parvat he.
