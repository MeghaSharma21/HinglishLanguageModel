 do bhai 1947 main jari bollywood kii munshi dil dwaara nirdeshit hindi film he. isamen ulhas, nur jehan, kamini kaushal, deepak mukherjee, ramayana tiwari, rajan haksar or paro mukhya abhinay bhoomika main hai. yah 1947 tak sabase adhik kamai karne vaali film thi.
 film main meraa sundar sapana biit gaya or yaad karoge gita dutt ne gaaye hai jinka sangeet sachin dev burman ne taiyaar kiya or bol raja mehadi ali khan ne diye.
