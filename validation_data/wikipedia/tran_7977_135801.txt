 . iti shri rudrashtakam sampoornam .
 he mokshaswaroop, vibhu, brahm or vedaswarup, ishan disha ke ishwar va sabake swami shri shiv ji! main aapako namaskara karata hoon. nijaswaroop main sthit, gunon se rahit, bhed rahit, ichha rahit, chetan akasharup evam akaash ko hi vastra rup main dhaaran karne vaale digambar aapako bhajata hoon..1.
 niraakaar, onkaar ke mul, turiya, vaani, gyaan va indriyon se pare, kailashpati, vikaraal, mahakaal ke bhi kaal, krupaalu, gunon ke dham, sansar ke pare aap parameshwar ko main namaskara karata hoon..2.
 jo himachal samaan gauravarna va gambheer hai, jinke sharir main karodon kaamdevon kii jyoti evam shobhaa he,jinke sar par sundar nadi ganga ji virajman hai, jinke lalaat par dwitiya kaa chandramaa or gale main sarp sushobhit he..3.
 jinke kaanon main kundal hill rahe hai, sundar bhrikuti va vishaal netra hai, jo prasanmukh, nilkanth va dayaalu hai, singhcharm dhaaran kiye va mundamal pahane hai, unke sabake pyaare, un sab ke nath shri shankar ko main bhajata hoon..4.
 prachand, shreshth, tejasvi, parameshwar, akhand, ajanma, karodon suryon ke samaan prakaash vaale, teenon prakaar ke shulon ko nirmul karne vaale, haath main trishul dhaaran kiye, prem ke dwaara praapt hone vaale bhavani ke pati shri shankar ko main bhajata hoon..5.
 kalaon se pare, kalyanswaroop, kalp kaa ant karne vaale, sajjanon ko sadaa anand dene vaale, tripur ke shatru sachchinanandaman, moh ko harne vaale, prasann hon, prasann hon..6.
 he parvati ke pati, jabatak manushya aapke charan kamalon ko nahin bhajte, tab tak unhen na to islok main na paralok main sukh shaanti milti he or na hi tapon kaa naash hota he. at: he samast jeevon ke andar nivas karne vaale prabho, prasann hoiye..7.
 main na to jap jaanata hoon, na tap or na hi puja. he prabho, main to sadaa sarvada aapako hi naman karata hoon. he prabho, budhapa va janm du:khon se jalaaye hue mujh dukhi kii dukhon se raksha karen. he ishwar, main aapako namaskara karata hoon..8.
 bhagwaan rudra kaa yah ashtak un shankar ji kii stuti ke liye he. jo manushya ise premaswaroop padhate hai, shrishankar un se prasann hote hai.
