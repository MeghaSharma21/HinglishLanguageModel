 raashtrapati shashan bharat main shashan ke sandarbh main us samay prayog kiya jane vaala ek paribhaashik shabd he, jab kisi rajya sarkaar ko bhang yaa nilambit kar diya jaataa he or rajya pratyaksh sangheey shashan ke adheen aa jaataa he. bharat ke samvidhan kaa anuchhed-356, centre kii sangheey sarkaar ko rajya main sanvaidhanik tantra kii vifaltaa yaa samvidhan ke spasht ulanghan kii dasha main us rajya sarkaar ko barkhaast kar us rajya main raashtrapati shashan lagu karne kaa adhikaar detaa he. raashtrapati shashan us sthiti main bhi lagu hota he, jab rajya vidhansabha main kisi bhi dal yaa gathbandhan ko spasht bahumat nahin ho.
 sattarudh party yaa kendriya sarkaar kii salaah par, rajyapal apane vivek par sadan ko bhang kar sakate hai, yadi sadan main kisi party yaa gathbandhan ke paas spasht bahumat naa ho. rajyapal sadan ko chah maheene kii avadhi ke liye â€˜nilambit avasthaa' main rakh sakate hai. chah maheene ke baad, yadi phir koi spasht bahumat praapt naa ho to us dasha main pun: chunaav yojit kiye jate hai.
 ise raashtrapati shashan isliye kaha jaataa he kyonki, iske dwaara rajya kaa niyantran bajaay ek nirvaachit mukhyamantri ke, sidhe bharat ke raashtrapati ke adheen aa jaataa he, lekin prashasnic drushti se rajya ke rajyapal ko kendriya sarkaar dwaara kaaryakari adhikaar pradaan kiye jate hai. prashasan main madad karne ke liye rajyapal aam taur par salaahkaaron kii niyukti karata he, jo aam taur par sevanivrutt civil sevak hote hai. aamtaur par is sthiti main rajya main centre main sattarudh party kii nitiyon kaa anusaran hota he.
 jammu or kashmir main raashtrapati shashan ko rajyapal shashan kaha jaataa he.
 anuchhed 356, centre sarkaar ko kisi rajya sarkaar ko barkhaast karne or raashtrapati shashan lagane kii anumati us avasthaa main detaa he, jab rajya kaa sanvaidhanik tantra puri tarah vifal ho gaya ho.
 yah anuchhed ek saadhan he jo centre sarkaar ko kisi naagarik ashaanti kii dasha main kisi rajya sarkaar par apana adhikaar sthapit karne main sakasham banaata he . raashtrapati shashan ke aalochakon kaa tark he ki adhiktar samay, ise rajya main rajnatik virodhiyon kii sarkaar ko barkhaast karne ke liye ek bahaane ke taur par istemaal kiya jaataa he, isliye ise kuch logon ke dwaara ise sangheey rajya vyavastha ke liye ek khatre ke rup main dekha jaataa he. 1950 main bhartiya samvidhan ke lagu hone ke baad se kendra sarkaar dwaara iskaa prayog 100 se bhi adhik baar kiya gaya he.
 anuchhed ko pehali baar 31 july 1957 ko vimochan samaaram ke dauraan loktantric tareeke se chuni gayi kerala kii communist sarkaar barkhaast karne ke liye kiya gaya tha. babari masjid vidhwans ke baad uttar pradesh kii bhaajapa kii rajya sarkaar ko bhi barkhaast kiya gaya tha.
 anuchhed 355 centre sarkaar adhikrut karata he taaki wo kisi bahari akraman yaa aantarik ashaanti kii dasha main rajya kii suraksha sunishchit kar sake or prayek rajya kaa shashan samvidhan ke praavdhaanon ke anusaar chalata rahe.
 is anuchhed kaa istemaal tab kiya gaya jab bhaajapa shasit rajyon main girijagharon par hamale ho rahe the. tab ke sansadeey kaary mantri vylar ravi ne anuchhed 355 main sanshodhan kar, rajya ke kuch bhaagon yaa rajya ke kuch khaas kshetron ko centre dwaara niyantrit karne kaa suzav diya tha.
