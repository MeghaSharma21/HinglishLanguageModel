 vijaydaan detha jinhen bijji ke naam se bhi janaa tha rajasthan ke vikhyath lekhak or padmashri puraskaar se sammanit vyakti the. unhen sahitya academy puraskaar or sahitya chudamani puraskaar jaise vibhinn anya puraskaron se bhi samaanit kiya ja chukaa tha. 10 navambar 2013 ko 87 varsh kii aayu main unka nidhan ho gaya.
 vijaydaan detha chaaran jati se the. unke pita sabaldaan detha or dada jugtidaan detha bhi rajasthan ke jane-maane kaviyon main se the. detha ne apane pita or do bhaaiyon ko ek pushtaini dushmani main maatr chaar varsh kii aayu main kho diya. 
 apni matra bhashaa rajasthani ke ke samaadar ke liye 'bijji' ne kabhi anya kisi bhashaa main nahin likhaa, unka adhiktar kaary unke ek putr kailash kabir dwaara hindi main anuvaadit kiya.
