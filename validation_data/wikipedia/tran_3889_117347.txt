 bhoomi kii urvartaa ko tikaoo banaae rakhte hue satat fasal utpaadan ke liye krishi vaigyanikon ne prakrutipradutt jeevanuon ko pahachaanakar unase bibhinn prakaar ke paryaavaran hitaishi urvarak taiyaar kiye hai jinhe ham jaev urvarak yaa 'jeevanu khaad' kehete he. duusare shabdon main ham kah sakate he kii jaev urvarak jivit urvarak he jiname sookshmjeev vidyaman hote he. 
 fasalon main jaev urvarkon istemaal karne se vayumandal main upasthit natrajan paudho ko sugamawa se uplabdh hoti he tathaa bhoomi main pehle se maujuud aghulansheel phosphorus aadi poshak tatv ghulansheel avasthaa main parivartit hokar paudhon ko aasaani se uplabdh hote hai. chunki jeevanu prakritik hai, isliye inke prayog se bhoomi kii urvara shakti badhati he or paryaavaran par vipareet asar nahin padataa. jaev urvarak rasinic urvarkon ke poorak he, vikalp katai nahin he. rasinic urvarkon ke poorak ke rup main jaev urvarkon kaa prayog karne se ham behatar parinaam praapt kar sakate he.
 vastav main jaev urvarak vishesh sookshmjivon evam kisi nami dharak padaarth ke mishran hai. vishesh sookshm jeevon kii nirdhaarit maatra ko kisi nami dharak dhuleey padaarth main milakar jaev urvarak taiyaar kiye jate hai. yah praayah 'culture' ke naam se bazaar main uplabdh he. vastav main jaev urvarak ek prakritik utpaada he. inkaa upyog vibhinn fasalon main natrajan evam sfoor kii aanshik purti hetu kiya ja saktaa he. inke upyog kaa bhoomi par koi dushprabhav nahin padataa he balki ye bhoomi ke bhautik va jaivik gunon main sudhaar kar uski urvara shakti ko badhaane main sahaayak hote hai. jaivik kheti main jaev urvarkon kii mahatvapurn bhoomika hoti he.
 jeevanu khaad kaa prabhaav dhire-dhire hota he. hamaare khet kii ek gram mitti main lagbhag do-teen arab sookshm jeevanu paye jate hai jisamen mukhyatah bacteriya, fafoond, kavak, protojoa aadi hote hai jo mitti kii urvara shakti badhaane va fasalotpaadan kii vruddhi main anek kaary karte hai.
