 bhartiya nyaaypalika aam kanoon par aadhaarit pranali he. yah pranali angrejon ne aupniveshik shashan ke samay banaai thi. is pranali ko 'aam kanoon vyavastha' ke naam se janaa jaataa he jisamen nyaayadhish apane faislon, adeshon or nirnyon se kanoon kaa vikaas karte hai. bharat main kai str ke tathaa vibhinn prakaar ke nyaayalay hai. bharat kaa sheersh nyaayalay nai dilli sthit sarvocch nyaayalay he or uske neeche vibhinn rajyon main ucch nyaayalay hai. ucch nyaayalay ke neeche jila nyaayalay or uske adhinsth nyaayalay hai jinhen 'nichali adaalat' kaha jaataa he. 
 bharat me chaar mahanagaron main alag alag ucchtam nyaayalay banane par vichaar kiya ja rahaa he kyonki dilli desh ke anek bhugolic bhaag se bahut dur he tathaa ucchtam nyaayalay main kaary kaa bhaar zyada he
 bharat kii swatantra nyaaypalika kaa sheersh sarvocch nyaayalay he, jiskaa pradhan pradhan nyaayadhish hota he. sarvocch nyaayalay ko apane naye maamalon tathaa ucch nyaayalayon ke vivaadon, dono ko dekhne kaa adhikaar he. bharat main 24 ucch nyaayalay hai, jinke adhikaar or uttardaayitv sarvocch nyaayalay kii apeksha simit hai. nyaaypalika or vyavasthapika ke paraspar matbhed yaa vivaad kaa sulah raashtrapati karata he.
