 anuppur, bhartiya rajya madhya pradesh kaa ek jila he. jo madhya pradesh ko chhattisgarh rajya se milaataa he. yah jila se 15 agast 2003 ko shadol jile se alag hua tha.anuppur railway stesan madhya pradesh kaa antim jankshan he, iske baad jaitahari, venkatnagar stesan chote stesan jo madhya pradesh main hai. venkatnagar kasbaa madhya pradesh evam chhattisgarh dono rajya ke madhya basaa hua he.
 anuppur son nadi ke paas basaa hua he. iske aasapaas ke sthaan main koyale ki bahut khadaane he. yahan se 07 kimi dur chachaai naam k sthaan he jaha amarkantak taap vidhut grah sthit he.
 anuppur jile kii jansankhya 7,49,237 he. jansankhya ghanatv 200 vyakit prati varg kimi or linganupaat 976 he.
 amarkantak, chachaai, jaitahari, rajendrogram, kotama, kelhari prasiddh sthal he. amarkantak narmada nadi va son nadi kaa udgam tathaa prakritik darshanik sthaan he.
 chachaai main amarkanta taap vidhut grih he tathajaitahari main moger vier thermal power plant viksit ho rahaa he, jahaan 2520 megawatt bijli utpaadan hoga.
 anuppur railway stesan se 3 train root jaati hai.
 1. bilaspur 2. ambikapur, chiramiri 3. katni 
 vayumarg - nikatatam airport jabalpur he jo 245 kimi dur sthit he.
 railmarg - anuppur katni evam bilaspur railway stesan ke madhya mahatvapurn railway jankshan he.
 sadakmarg - anuppur sadakmarg dwaara bilaspur shadol evam kotama se judaa hua he.
