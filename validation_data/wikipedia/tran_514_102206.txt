 ben laughlin ek australian cricket khilaadi he. inhone lambey samay se khelte hue bhi rashtriya team main achche se jagah nahin bana paae or may 2018 tak mahaj 5 ek divasiy antarrashtriya match or 3 tinti-tinti maichon main hi mauka mil paaya.
 inhone apane antarrashtriya cricket karier kii shuruyaat saal 2009 main 3 april ko dakshin africa cricket team ke khilaaf vanade match khelkar kii thi or aakhri vanade match 3 may 2009 ko pakistan cricket team ke khilaaf khelaa tha.
 vahiin inhone apana pehla tinti-tinti muqabla 29 march ko dakshin africa ke khilaaf khelaa tha or antim baar inhen 28 janvari 2013 ko srilanka cricket team ke khilaaf khilne kaa mauka mila tha. 2018 indian premier lig kii nilaamee main inhen rajasthan royles kii team ne kharida tha.
