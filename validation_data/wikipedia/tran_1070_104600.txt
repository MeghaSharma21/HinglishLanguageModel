 48 varshiya bhartiya mahila vaigyaniktacy thomas ko 1988 se agni prakshepastra kaarykram se judane ke baad se hi agniputree tacy thomas ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. unki anek uplabdhiyon main agni-2, agni-3 or agni-4 prakshepastra kii mukhya team kaa hissaa bananaa or safal prasheekshan he. unhone purv raashtrapati abdul kalam ko apana prerana strot mana he.
 thomas bhartiya sena main mahilaon ko yuddh kii bhoomika die jane kaa bhi samarthan karti hai va kahati hai ki yadi vah itani tatparta se sena main bhoomika nibhaa rahi hai to vah yuddh kshetra main bhi bhoomika nibhaa sakati hai.
 thomas agni-5 pariyojna kii aguai kar rahi hai or unke saath paanch anya mahila vaigyanik bhi kaam kar rahi hai. diardio main prakshepastra pariyojna se jude 250 vaigyanikon main 20 mahila vaigyanik hai. thomas 2008 main agni pranali kii pariyojna nideshak baniin. usee samay unhen agni-2 kaa netritv karne kii jimmedaari di gai thi. varsh 2009 main unhen agni-4 kii pariyojna nideshak banaaya gaya. aage kii yojana ke bare main unhone kaha, dil tham kar agni-5 kii pratiksha kiijie. unke nirdesan main farvari 2012 main agni prakshepastra ke safal prakshepan ke saath hi bharat antaramahadwipiya belistic prakshepastron kaa vikaas karne main sakasham america, rus or china jaise deshon kii shrenee main shaamil ho chukaa he.
 laal bahadur shastri rashtriya puraskaar nai dilli ke laal bahadur shastri prabandhan santhaan dwaara lok prashasan, shiksha or prabandhan kshetra main kisi vyakti dwaara kiye gaye yogadan ke liye pradaan kiya jaataa he. 2012 main tacy thomas ko laal bahadur shastri rashtriya puraskaar ke liye chayan kiya gaya tha. yah puraskaar unhen raashtrapati dwaara pradaan kiya gaya tha.
