 monmouth toun eggsi  etehasik shahar monmouth main aadhaarit ek welsh footbal club he. team vartmaan samay main welsh footbal lig divijan do main khelti he. club gwant county egge kii sadsy he. club ne 8 agast 2011 ko internet udhyam fivepoundfootballclub.com se anubandh kara tha jiske tahat club kaa swaamitv paanch varshon kii avadhi main fivepoundfootballclub.com ke paas aa jayega. sitambar 2011 ko club ne swayan ko community interest company susthit kar liya, jo ki wales main apni tarah kii pehali ghatana thi. club kaa naaraa "kingfisher ol da ve!" he.
 club kii aadhikarik nirmaan tithi spasht nahin he, parantu is baat ke pramaan hai ki ek 'sangh club' varsh 1906 ke samay sakriy tha. saptahik samachaarpatr monmouthshire bikan ke anusaar monmouth main kaaphi samay se footbal khelaa jaataa rahaa he or shaayad 1873 se. monmouth or clearval eggsi 1925/26 kii district lig ke sanyukt vijeta the.
 club ne 1940 kii britain kii ladaai kii purv sandhya main monmouthshire senior cap main monmouth sportsground main yojit hue match main lavals athlettic ko 3–2 se haraakar sadarn lig west kaa kitaab jeeta. lagbhag 20 varsh pashchaat 1958 main kingfishras ne pontlanfreth main safan forest ko 3–0 se haraakar monmouthshire ameor cap jeeta. agle satr main bhi monmouthshire ameor cap pe club kaa kabja rahaa or iske saath-saath club ne apana doosraa monmouthshire senior cap bhi jeeta.
 club sirf ekbar, 1965 main, hi footbal association chalange cap main jagah bana paaya tha, jisamen use praarambhik daur main hi haar kaa saamana karnaa padaa. club ne 1984, 1986 or 1996 main gwant ameor cap jeeta.
