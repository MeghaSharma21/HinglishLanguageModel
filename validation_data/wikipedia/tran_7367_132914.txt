 hindi sahitya ke itihaas ke pratham kaal kaa namakaran vidwaanon ne is prakaar kiya he-
 acharya ramchandra shukla ne is kaal kaa naam virgatha kaal rakhaa he. is namakaran kaa aadhaar spasht karte hue ve likhte hai- ...adikaal kii is deergh parampara ke bich pratham dedh-sau varsh ke bheetar to rachana kii kisi vishesh pravriti kaa nishchay nahin hota-dharm, niti, shringaar, vir sab prakaar kii rachanaayen dohon main milti he. is anirdisht lok pravriti ke uparaant jab se musalamaanon kii chadhaaiyon kaa aarambh hota he tab se ham hindi sahitya kii pravriti ek vishesh rup main bandhati hui pate hai. rajashrith kavi apane ashrayadata raajaaon ke parakrampurna chariton yaa gaathaon kaa varnan karte the. yahi prabandh parampara raso ke naam se paayi jaati he, jise lakshya karke is kaal ko hamane virgatha kaal kaha he. iske sandarbh main ve teen kaaran bataate hai-
 dow grearsan ne hindi sahitya ke itihaas ke pratham kaal ko charankaal naam diya he. par is naam ke paksh main ve koi thos tark nahin de paye hai. unhone hindi sahitya ke itihaas kaa prarambh 643 i. se maani he kintu us samay kii kisi chaaran rachana yaa pravriti kaa ullekh unhone nahin kiya he. vastutah is prakaar kii rachanaayen 1000 i.sa. tak milti hi nahin hai. is liye dogrimmersan dwaara diya gaya naam yogya nahin he.
 mishrabandhuon ne i.sa. 643 se 1387 tak ke kaal ko praarambhik kaal kaha he. yah ek saamaanya naam he or isamen kisi pravriti ko aadhaar nahin banaaya gaya he. yah naam bhi vidwaanon ko sweekaarya nahin he.
 doramkumar verma- inhone hindi sahitya ke praarambhik kaal ko charankaal naam diya he. is namakaran ke bare main unka kahana he ki is kaal ke sabhi kavi chaaran the, is tathy se inkaar nahin kiya ja saktaa. kyonki sabhi kavi raajaaon ke darbar-aashray main rahanevaale, unke yashogaan karanevaale the. unke dwaara rachaa gaya sahitya chaarani kehalaata he. kintu vidwaanon kaa maananaa he ki jin rachanaaon kaa ullekh verma ji ne kiya he unamem anek rachanaayen sandigdh hai. kuch to aadhunik kaal kii bhi hai. is kaaran doverma dwaara diya gaya charankaal naam vidwaanon ko maanya nahin he.
 rahul sankrutyaayan- unhone 8veen se 13 veen shatabdi tak ke kaal ko siddh-sanmat yug kii rachanaayen mana he. unke matanusaar us samay ke kavy main do pravritiyon kii pramukhataa milti he- 1.sidhon kii vaani- iske antargat buddha tathaa nath-sidhon kii tathaa jainmuniyon kii upadeshmulak tathaa hathyog kii kriya kaa vistaar se prachaar karanevaali rahasyamoolak rachanaayen aati hai. 2.saamanton kii struti- iske antargat chaaran kaviyon ke charit kavy aate hai, jinamein kaviyon ne apane aashray daata raja evam saamanton kii struti ke liye yuddh, vivaah aadi ke prasangon kaa badha-chadhaakar varnan kiya he. in granthon main virtv kaa naveen swar mukherit hua he. rahul ji kaa yah mat bhi vidwaanon dwaara maanya nahin he. kyonki is namakaran se loukik ras kaa ullekh karanevaali kisi vishesh rachana kaa pramaan nahin miltaa. nathpanthi tathaa hathyogi kaviyon tathaa khusro aadi kii kavy-pravritiyon kaa is naam main samavesh nahin hota he.hu
 acharya mahavir prasad dvivedi- unhone hindi sahitya ke pratham kaal kaa naam beej-bapan kaal rakhaa. unka yah naam yogya nahin he kyonki saahitiyik pravritiyon kii drushti se yah kaal adikaal nahin he. yah kaal to purvavarti parinishthit apbhransh kii saahitiyik pravritiyon kaa vikaas he.
 acharya hajaari prasad dvivedi- inhone hindi sahitya ke itihaas ke praarambhik kaal ko adikaal naam diya he. vidwaan bhi is naam ko adhik upyukt maanate hai. is sandarbh main unhone likhaa he- vastutah hindi kaa aadi kaal shabd ek prakaar kii bhraamak dharna kii sristi karata he or shrota ke chitt main yah bhav paidaa karata he ki yah kaal koi aadim, manobhavapann, paramparawinirmukt, kavy-rudhiyon se achoote sahitya kaa kaal he. yah thik vahiin he. yah kaal bahut adhik parampara-premi, rudhigrast, sajag or sachet kaviyon kaa kaal he. adikaal naam hi adhik yogya he kyonki sahitya kii drushti se yah kaal apbhransh kaal kaa vikaas hi he, par bhashaa kii drushti se yah parinishthit apbhransh se aage badi hui bhashaa kii soochna detaa he. acharya hajariprasad dvivedi ne hindi sahitya ke adikaal ke lakshan-nirupan ke liye nimnlikhit pustaken aadharbhoot bataayi hai- 
 is prakaar hindi sahitya ke itihaas ke pratham kaal ke namakaran ke rup main adikaal naam hi yogya va sarthak he, kyonki is naam se us vyapak pushasbhumi kaa bodh hota he, jis par parvarti sahitya khadaa he. bhashaa kii drushti se is kaal ke sahitya main hindi ke praarambhik rup kaa pataa chalata he to bhav kii drushti se bhaktikaal se lekar aadhunik kaal tak kii sabhi pramukh pravritiyon ke aadim beej isamen khoje ja sakate hai. is kaal kii rachana-shaileyon ke mukhya rup iske baad ke kaalon main milte hai. adikaal kii aadhyatmik, shringaarik tathaa veerta kii pravritiyon kaa hi viksit rup parvarti sahitya main miltaa he. is kaaran adikaal naam hi adhik upyukt tathaa vyapak naam he.
