  kurnul bhartiya rajya andhra pradesh kaa ek jila he. kurnul tungabhadra or handri nadi ke dakshini kinare par sthit andhra pradesh kaa ek pramukh jila he. 12veen shatabdi main oddar jab alampur kaa nirmaan karne ke liye pattharon katate the to yahaan aakar unako phinishing dete the. 1953 se 1956 tak kurnul andhrapradesh rajya kii rajdhani bhi rahaa. iske baad hi hyderabad yahaan kii rajdhani bani. aaj bhi yahaan vijaynagar raajaaon ke shahi mahal ke avshesh dekh ja sakate hai jo 14veen se 16veen shatabdi ke bich bane hai. parsi or arabi shilaalekh bhi yahaan dekhne ko milte hai jisase yahaan ke mahatv kaa pataa chalata he.
 yahaan par ek kila he jo ek samay main vijaynagar raajaaon kaa garh tha. iske avshesh aaj bhi grenite kii paanch pahadiyon main dekhe ja sakate hai. inamen se do pahariyaan 800 feet oonchi hai. aadoni main sthit jama masjid muslim vaastukala kaa sundar udhaaharan he.
 yah ek pramukh dharmik centre he. yah hinduon kii aasthaa kaa pratik he vishesh rup se vaishnav sampradaay ke logon ke liye yah sthaan bahut mahatvapurn he. yahaan kaa mandir do bhaagon main banta he- nichalaa ahobilam or upree ahobilam. upree ahobilam pathaar par samudr tal se 2800 feet kii oonchai par sthit he.
 madhavaran gaanv kurnul kaa ek anya pramukh paryatak sthal he. yahaan par ek pracheen dwaar ke avshesh dekhe ja sakate hai. ise dekhakar mumbai ke getwe of india kaa abhas hota he.
 sapt nandi sangam ke naam se prasiddh sangameshwar kurnul se 55 kilometre dur he. yah bhavanasi or krishna nadiyon kaa pavitra sangam sthal he. is khubasurat sthaan par lakdi ke lingam kaa ek mandir bhi he. mahashivaratri ke avasar par hajaaron shraddhalu yahaan shivaji kii puja archana karne aate hai. 
 shrisalam nallamalai pahadiyon par sthit ghanaa jangal he. yah dakshin bharat ke sabase pracheen or pavitra sthalon main se ek he. sabase pramukh mandir krishna nadi ke dakshini tat par rishabhagiri pahari par he. anek puraanon main ise shri giri, shriparvat, rudra parvat or sheshachalam ke naam se bhi pukaara gaya he.
 nallamalai pahari ke purv main sthit yah sthaan apni prakritik khubsuurati or mahanandishwar ke mandir ke liye mashhoor he. is mandir ke aasapaas anek mandir hai jo paryatakon ko aakarshith karte hai. farvari-march main mandir main vaarshik utsav manaaya jaataa he.
 in sthanon ke alaava bhi kuch or jagahen hai jo darshneey hai jaise abdul vahib kaa makbara, 1618 main bana gopal raju kaa makbara, peta anjaneyaswami kaa mandir, venugopalswami mandir or birla mandir aadi.
 najdiki hawaii adda hyderabad yahaan se 219 kilometre dur he.
 kurnul main sikanderabad-bangaleru railway line kaa railway stepshan he.
 anantpur, chitoor or hyderabad se yahaan ke liye bas seva uplabdh he.
