 dharti par sthit sagaron ke jal-str kaa saamaanya-str se upar uthanaa jowar tathaa neeche girnaa bhaata kehalaata he. jowar-bhaata kii ghatana keval sagar par hi lagu nahin hoti balki un sabhi chizon par lagu hotin hai jin par samay evam sthaan ke saath parivartansheel gurutv bal lagata he. 
 prithvi, chandramaa or surya kii parsparik gurutvkarshan shakti kii kriyasheeltaa hi jowar-bhaata kii utpatti kaa pramukh kaaran hai.
 - jab surya, prithvi tathaa chandramaa ek sidh main hote hai.
 - jab surya, prithvi tathaa chandramaa samakonik avasthaa main hote hai.
