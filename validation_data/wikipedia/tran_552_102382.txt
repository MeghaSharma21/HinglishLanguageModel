 sangeet naatak academy bharat sarkaar dwaara sthapit bharat kii sangeet evam naatak kii rashtriya str kii sabase badi academy he. iskaa mukhyalay dilli main he.
 bharat sarkaar ke shiksha mantralay ne ek sansadeey prastaav dwaara ek swytt sanstha ke rup main sangeet naatak academy kii sthapana karne kaa nirnay kiya. tadnusaar 1953 main academy kii sthapana hui. 1961 main academy bhang kar di gai or iskaa nae rup main sanghathan kiya gaya. 1860 ke societes registration ke adheen yah sanstha panjikrut ho gai. isaki nai parishad or kaaryakarini samiti kaa gathan kiya gaya. academy ab isi rup main kaary kar rahi he.
 sangeet naatak academy kii sthapana sangeet, naatak or nrity kalaon ko protsaahan dene tathaa unke vikaas or unnati ke liye vividh prakaar ke kaaryakramon kaa sanchaalan karne ke uddeshy se kii gayi thi. sangeet naatak academy apane mul uddeshy kii purti ke liye desh bhar main sangeet, nrity or naatak kii sansthaaon ko unki vibhinn kaaryayojanaon ke liye anudaan detii he, surveykshan or anusandhan kaary ko protsaahan detii he. sangeet, nrity or naatak ke prashikshan ke liye sansthaaon ko vaarshik sahaayta detii he. vichaargoshtiyon or samarohon kaa sanghathan karti he tathaa in vishyon se sambandhit pustakon ke prakaashan ke liye aarthik sahaayta detii he.
 sangeet naatak academy kii ek mahaparishad hoti he jisamen 48 sadsy hote hai. inamen se 5 sadsy bharat sarkaar dwaara manonith hote hai - ek shiksha mantralay kaa pratinidhi, ek soochna evam prasaaran mantralay kaa pratinidhi, bharat sarkaar dwaara niyukt vitt salaahkaar, prayek rajya sarkaar ke 1-1 manonith sadsy or lalit kala academy va sahitya academy ke 2-2 pratinidhi ke hote hai. is prakaar manonith ye 28 sadsy ek baithak main 20 or sadasyon kaa chunaav karte hai. ye vyakti sangeet, nrity or naatak ke kshetra main vikhyath kalakaar or vidvan hote hai. inkaa chayan is prakaar se kiya jaataa he ki sangeet or nrity kii vibhinn paddatiyon or shaileyon tathaa vibhinn kshetron kaa pratinidhitiv ho sake. is prakaar gathit mahaparishad kaaryakarini kaa chunaav karti he jisamen 15 sadsy hote hai. sabhapati kaa manonayan shiksha mantralay kii sifarish par raashtrapati dwaara kiya jaataa he. upasabhapati kaa chunaav mahaparishad karti he. sachiv kaa pad vaitanaik hota he or sachiv kii niyukti kaaryakarini karti he.
 kaaryakarini kaary ke sanchaalan ke liye anya samitiyon kaa gathan karti he, jaise vitt samiti, anudaan samiti, prakaashan samiti aadi. academy ke samvidhan ke adheen sabhi adhikaar sabhapati ko praapt hote hai. mahaparishad, kaaryakarini tathaa sabhapati kaa kaarykaal paanch varsh hota he.
 academy ke sabase pehle sabhapati shri pi.ve. rajmannar the. duusare sabhapati mysore ke maharaja shri jaychamraj vadyar the.
 academy kaa in kalaon ke abhilekhan kaa ek vyapak kaarykram he jiske adheen paaramparik sangeet or nrity tathaa naatak ke vividh roopon or shaileyon kii filmein banaai jaati hai, photograph liye jate hai or unka sangeet tapericard kiya jaataa he. academy sangeet, nrity or naatak ke kaarykram bhi prastut karti he. academy sangeet, nrity or naatak ke kaarykram bhi he jiske adheen in vishyon kii vishisht pustaken prakashit kii jaati he. academy angreji main ek tremasik patrika "sangeet naatak" kaa prakaashan karti he.
 academy prativarsh sangeet or nrity tathaa naatak ke kshetra main vishisht kalakaaron ko puraskrit karti he. puraskaron kaa nirnay academy mahaparishad karti he. puraskaron kaa nirnay academy mahaparishad karti he. puraskaar samaaroh main puraskaravitaran raashtrapati dwaara hota he. sangeet nrity or naatak ke kshetra main academy prativarsh kuch ratnasadasyon kaa chunaav karti he.
