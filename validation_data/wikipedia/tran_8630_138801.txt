 mahaan al-nuri masjid mosul, iraq main ek masjid thi. isaki prasiddhi isaki jhuki hui minar se thi, jisane shahar ko iskaa upnaam "kubada" diya tha. mana jaataa he ki is masjid ko pehle pahal 12veen shatabdi ke ant main banaaya gaya tha, haalanki iske baad iskaa kai baar punarnirmaan kiya gaya. apane 850 varshon ke itihaas main is masjid ne shatru senaaon ke bahut saare akraman jhele lekin antat: 21 june 2017 ko iraq or levant ke islamic raashtra ne mosul kii ladaai ke dauraan ise nasht kar diya.
 iraqi sainikon ne iesas ko is masjid ke vinaash ke liye jimmedaar tehraaya he, kyonki usne ise apane haath se jane dene ke bajaay barbaad karne kaa kaam kiya. is masjid kaa iesas or uske netaa abu bakr al-bagdadi ke liye ek pratikatmak mahatva tha, kyonki 2014 main aatankwaadiyon ne iskaa istemaal apane "khalipha" ko ghoshit karne ke liye kiya tha. june 2014 main aatankiyon ke iraq or syria main aage badhane or ek vishaal ilaake ko apane kabze main lene ke baad se iesas kaa kaala dhvaj is masjid kii 45 meter minar oonchi se fahraa rahaa tha, or unhone daawa kiya tha ki ab unka dhvaj kabhi bhi neeche nahin utrega. aadhikarik dastavejon or sthaneey muslim chashmadidon ke vipareet, iesas ne ise nasht karne kaa aarope lagaaya sanyukt rajya america par lagaaya he, haalanki iesas kaa yah daawa sabit nahin hua.
 iraqi pradhan mantri haidar al-abaadi ne kaha ki iesas dwaara masjid kaa vinaash kiya janaa unki "haar kii ghoshnaa" he, or "al-habda minar or al-nuri masjid ko visphot se udaana iesas dwaara haar kii aadhikarik sweekaarokti he."
