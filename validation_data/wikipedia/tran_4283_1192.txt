 gangotri rashtriya udyaan uttarkashi, uttarakhand, bharat main sthit ek rashtriya udyaan he. is rashtriya udyaan kaa kshetrafal 1553 varg kimi he. yah udyaan shankudhari wanon kii sundarata or himandon kii duniya kii bhavyata ko hare-bhare ghaas ke maidaanon ke saath sanyukt rup se pradaan karata he. 
 april se oktober ke maheene main, rashtriya udyaan main paryaton apane charam par hota he. nikatatam railway stesan or hawaii adda dehradun he. nikatatam railway stesan 210 kilometre kii duuri par he, jabaki nikatatam hawaii adda rashtriya udyaan se 220 kilometre kii duuri par he. iske alaava harshil nikatatam shahar he.
 gangotri rashtriya udyaan bharat ke uttarakhand rajya main uttarkashi jile main bhagirathi nadi ke upree jalgrahan kshetra main sthit he. udyaan kii purvottar seemaa tibet ke saath antarrashtriya seemaa se lagi huyi he. iskaa kul kshetrafal 1553 varg kimi he or samudr sataha se ausatan oonchaai 7083 me. he. barf se dhaki pahaad or himand is udyaan ke vistrut bhaag main faile huye hai. ganga nadi kaa udgam sthaan gomukh bhi isi udyaan ke andar hi sthit he. gangotri himand, jisapar udyaan kaa naam diya gaya he, hinduon ke pavitra sthalon main se ek he. udyaan kshetra govind rashtriya udyaan or kedarnath vanyajeev abhayaarany ke bich parsparik nirantarata ke rup main he. unchi parvatashrankhlaayen, gahare darre or dhalava chattaane, chattani nukile himand or sankari ghaatiyon is kshetra kii visheshtaayen hai. oonchaai main baari vibhinnata ke kaaran yahan uposhnkaribandheey parivesh se lekar alpine charaagaah tak dekhne ko milte hai.
 yah udyaan pashuon kii 15 prajatiyon or pakshiyon kii 150 prajatiyon kaa ghar he. paryatakon ko yahan him tendue, bhure bhaaluu, kasturi mrug, taahar, baagh tathaa himalay kshetra main paye jane vaale kai pakshi bhi dikh sakate hai.
