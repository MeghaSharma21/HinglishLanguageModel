 belivere mahal  ek gothic style main nirmit mahal he jo ki pashchimottar palma se 3 ki0me0 kii doori ke ek choti pahari par he. yah pahari myorka naam ke dweep par he, jo ki spain ke belierik dwipon ke samooh main se ek dweep he. yah myorka ke shaasha james dwitiya ne chaudahavi sadi main banaaya tha. yah europe main gine-chune paripatr mehalon main se he. atharahavin sadi se lekar beesvin sadi ke madhya tak ke use sainya karagaar ke rup main upyog kiya gaya. ab yah mahal asainik niyantran main he. yah mahal is dweep ke paryaton aakarshanon main mukhya he or shahar ke itihaas sangrahalay kendra kaa sthaan he.
 mahal kii yojana or iske saath-saath isse se jude golaaidaar toweron ke bare main ek prachlit anumaan jisaki akasar shikshavid charchaa karte hai vah yah he ki upree parisar pashchimi tat ke herideon se prabhavit hokar banaaya gaya he kyonki vah bhi usee prakaar se golaaidaar, uskaa bhi ek mukhya kendrit lambaa tower he or teen chote-chote tower iske atirikt donon hi sthanon samaan rup se paae jate hai. yah teen chote-chote tower mukhya tower se lage hue hai or mukhya tower ek pul se judaa he jo ki aasapaas ke ilaake ko ghere hue he. 
 is qilabandi ke mukhya bhaag kaa nirmaan pere selva naam ke us samay ke prasiddh architect ne kiya tha. pere selva ke laa almudaina ke raaj mahal ke nirmaan main bhi ullekhneey bhoomika nibhaai thi. yah kaary usne sainkadon mazaduron kii sahaayta se 1300 or 1311 ke bich usne shaasha james dwitiya or mayorka ke liye kiya tha. nirmaan ke liye usee pahari hi se bada pathar liya gaya tha jisase ek daraad pad gai thi. mahal ke nirmaan ke saath hi hathiyaar, gola-barood jo ki balakani main rakhaa jaataa tha. samay ke badlaav ke saath-saath is jagah hathiyaaron ke bhandar ke rup main istemaal samaapt gaya jabaki iske kuch nishaan peeche chhut gaye.
 yah mahal mukhya rup se mayorka ke raajaaon ke nivas sthaan ke taur kaary de rahaa tha jab bhi ve madhya europe main rahane nahin rah rahe the. iske pashchaat ke daur main shaayad hi kabhi kisi ke nivas ke rup main 17 veen sadi tak prayog main laayaa gaya jabaki kabhi-kabhaar vysarai ke nivas liye istemaal kiya gaya tha. is parisar ko ek yuddh-durg ke taur par nuksaan uthaanaa padaa he or safaltaapurvak madhya yug ke dauraan do gheraav se juj chukaa he; unamem se pehla 1343 main mayorka ke peter chauthe kaa mayorka pradeshon ko aragon ke mukut ko punah sammilit karne kaa abhiyaan or phir pun: 1391 ek yahudee-virdohi sami vidroh shaamil hai. mahal keval ek baar iske itihaas main dushman haathon main 1521 main mayorka ke bhrata vidroh ke dauraan parajeet kiya gaya tha.
 mahal ko aamtaur se ek lord warden dwaara sanchalit kiya gaya tha. 1408 main aragon ke raja martin pratham ne belivere kii aadhipaty ko yishu ke nasarat-vaale charterhouse ko validemossa main diya tha. antat raja ne aadhipaty yaa belivere mahal nahin diya.
 ek sanlagn jagah hone ke kaaran 14 veen sadi ke ant ke baad se yah ek jail ke rup main istemaal kiya jaataa rahaa he. sabase pehle rani mayorka vayolant, unke bachche james or isabela or inke saath-saath mayorka ke james tritiy ko 1349 main lutsamyor kii yuddh main unki mrityu ke pashchaat unke samarthakon ko rakhaa gaya tha. spain ke uttaradhikaar kii yuddh ke dauraan is vishaal mahal ko philip da anyu ke samarthakon ko rakhane ke liye istemaal kiya gaya tha. boorbon vijay yahan mauleton  ko qaid karne ke liye istemaal kiya gaya tha. spani aazaadee ke yuddh ke dauraan ise pakade gaye kai kaidiyon rakhane ke liye istemaal liya gaya. bellan ladaai main raajnneetik kaidiyon ko yahan rakhaa gaya. baad ke daur main yahan pramukh qaidiyon main mantri gaspar melchor de jovellanos  the jinhone pehali baar mahal kaa varnan kiya or pehali blueprint ke chitra tayyar kiye the. mahal par phir se ek raajnneetik jail ke or par 19 veen sadi ke dauraan, spani singhaasana ke liye sangharsh ke kaaran habsburg dhokhebaaz ke kai mahatvapurn samarthakon ko avarodhit karne ke liye istemaal kiya tha. 20 veen sadi ke dauraan katalani netaaon yahan rakhaa gaya tha jinamein elexander jome, sansad sadsy jinone mahal ko shahar ke liye pehali baar jeeta tha, or emily darder, jo shahar ke meyar the, donon ko baad main jala diya gaya.
 malorka ke james dwitiya ke shahi adaalat ke liye mukhya uddeshy se banaae jane ke kaaran, isaki sanrachana rakshaatmak tatwon ke saath ek mahal kii jaruraton ko jodatii he. isaki sanrachana main sabase ullekhneey visheshta spain main adwitiya iskaa gol aakaar he. iske aasapaas kii diwaar or bhitari khuli jagah donon hi ek samaan aakaar rakhte hai or isliye teen chote toweron or pramukh burj yahan ghere hue hai. ek khai mahal or uske pramukh burj aasapaas ke pai jaati he.
 paripatr bhitari khuli jagah par prakaash daalaa janaa chahie. iske neeche ek talab he jo ingit karata he uske neeche ek panmahal hi centre kii khuli jagah ke aasapaas ek do manjila imaarat ke rup main sanrachit he. sabhi apni nirbharta gothic arddh paripatr mehrab kii ek gailaree ke madhyam se is khuli jagah ke aage ghere hue he.
 1931 main, spain kii dwitiya ganraajya ne belivere mahal or aasapaas ke jangal ko palma ke shahar ko pradaan kiya. 1932 main yah ek sangrahalay ban gaya. yah shahar ke itihaas kaa sangrahalay banane ke liye 1976 main nae prayaas kiye the, jinhen kaafii naagarik samarthan praapt hua tha. mahal se lagakar nirmit parking sthal or sadak ke laabh ke kaaran vartmaan samay main yahan aagantukon kii ek badi sankhya dekhi ja sakati he. mukhya khule sthal par kai saarvajanik samaaroh jaise protocollari or saanskrithik kaary or sangeet ke rup main kai alag alag ayaamon kii jagah ban chuki he. samudr yaa shahar ke kisi bhi anya bindu se drishyata ke kaaran yah ek shahar kaa pratik ban gaya he.
 aasapaas ke jangal shahar ke ghudsavaar police ke astabal kaa kaam dete hai. yahan par saint alfonsus rodrigage ke liye samarpit ek chota girizaa ghar he jiskaa nirmaan 1879 or 1885 ke bich kiya gaya tha. 
 easter ravivaar ke baad ke ravivaar ko is jangal or mahal ke paas nagarikon kii baithak hoti he jo dyumenge del enjal manaate hai.
