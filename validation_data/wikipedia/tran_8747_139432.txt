 pamiri bhashaayen purvi irani bhashaa-parivaar kii ek upashakha hai jisaki sadsy bhashaayen pamir parvat kshetra main basane vaale bahut se samudaay bolte hai, visheshkar vah samudaay jo panj nadi or uski upanadiyon ke kinare vaas karte hai. ise bolne vaale ilaaqon main purvottaree afghanistan kaa badakhshaan praant or purvi tajikistan ke kuhistoni-badakhshaan swashasit praant hai. inke alaava ek saricoli naamak bhashaa afghanistan or china ke shinjiyang praant ke simavarti ilaaqon main boli jaati he. saricoli ko china main 'tajiki bhashaa' kaha jaataa he lekin dhyaan den ki yah tajikistan main boli jane vaali tajiki bhashaa se bilkul alag he.
 san 1990 main andaaza lagaaya gaya ki vishwa bhar main pamiri bhashaayen bolne waalon kii sankhya lagbhag ek lakh thi. kuch mukhya pamiri bhashaayen shughni, saricoli, yazgulaami, munji, ishkashimi, vaakhi, yidaga or vanji hai. vanji ke bare main bhashavaigyanikon kaa anumaan he ki yah vilupt ho chuki he kyonki ise bolne vaale ab tajiki bhashaa bolne lage hai.
