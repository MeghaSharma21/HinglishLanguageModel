 blike forest, dakshin-pashchim germany ke baden-woortemberg main sthit ek vanachhadit parvat shrinkhalaa he. isaki dakshini or pashchimi simaaon par rin ghati sthit he. 1493 meter kii oonchai ke saath feldberg iskaa sabase unchaa shikhar he. 200 km lambaai tathaa 60 km chaudaai ke saath yah kshetra lagbhag puri tarah se ytaakar he. isliye iskaa kshetrafal lagbhag 12,000 km² he. shwarjawald naam romanon dwaara diya gaya he jo vahaan sthit ghaney jangalon vaale parvat ko silva nigra arthath "blike forest" kehete the kyonki uske andar ke ghaney shankuvrikshon ke kaaran van main prakaash pravesh nahin kar paata tha.
 blike forest main nise tathaa grenit se nirmit kor ke upar sandstone kaa ek cover maujuud he. purv main iskaa tectonic vikaas nikat sthit vosges parvat ke samaan tha. baad main madhya iosin yug ke dauraan ek rifting avadhi ne is kshetra ko prabhavit kiya jisase rin graben kii utpatti hui. vurm himachhadan kii antim himachhadan avadhi ke dauraan blike forest barf se dhank gaya tha; mummelsi jaisi anek choti pahari jhilein is avadhi ke avshesh ke rup main aaj bhi maujuud hai.
 blike forest kii nadiyon main shaamil hai danube, anj, kinjig, murg, nagold, nekar, rench, tathaa vies. blike forest atlantic mahaasaagar drenez basin tathaa blike si drenez basin ke bich ke mahadwipiya vibhaajan kaa hissaa he.
 prashasnic rup se blike forest purna rup se baden-woortemberg rajya kaa hissaa he or isamen forjhim shahar ke saath-saath nimna jile bhi shaamil hai. uttar main: anj, rastat tathaa kol; madhya main: friudenstot, ortenocrants tathaa rautvil; dakshin main: amendingen, shwarnwald-baar, brego-hoshwarzwald, loraach tathaa valdoot.
 van main zyaadaatar chid or deodaar ke vriksh hote hai jinamein se kuch ko vanijyik monoculture main ugaaya jaataa he. anya vanachhadit kshetron ke samaan hi blike forest ke kuch kshetra bhi lakdi kii atyadhik kataai ke kaaran nasht ho chuke hai. lakdi kataai or bhoomi upyog main parivartanon ke kaaran yah van apane mul aakaar kaa ek ansh maatr bachaa he. storm lothar ne 1999 main pahaado kii chotiyon ke saikadon acre kshetra se vrikshon kii kataai kii thi. iske kaaran kuch unchi chotiyaan or manoram pahaad lagbhag nagn ho chuke hai jahaan keval kuch ghaas-phoos or chote deodaar vriksh hi bache hai.
 paryaton yahaan kaa mukhya udyog he. neeche varnit smarakon or shaharon ke alaava lambii duuri ke kai paidal raste bhi blike forest se ho kar guzarate hai, jinamein se kai ko pehali baar banaaya gaya he. europea lambii duuri kaa maarg i1, lambii duuri ke kuch sthaneey margon kaa anusaran karte hue blike forest se gujarata he. din main chalane ke liye upyukt kai chote raaston ke atirik vahaan mountain biking tathaa cross-country skiing ke raste bhi maujuud hai. in margon kii kul lambaai lagbhag 23,000 kilometre he jinaki dekharekh shwarnwaldverin naamak ek svaichhik sanstha dwaara kii jaati he; is sanstha main lagbhag 90,000 sadsy hai . 
 blike forest main kai puraane shahar maujuud hai. lokapriy paryaton sthalon main shaamil hai frieberg, kol, gengenbak, stoffen, shilt, haasala, tathaa eltonstic. anya lokapriy sthalon main shaamil hai feldberg, belchen, condel, tathaa shouinsaland jaise parvat; titisi tathaa shlukasi jhilein; ol cents jharnaa; triberg jharnaa jo sabase unchaa to nahin he par germany kaa sabase prasiddh jharnaa he; or vutaach nadi kii tang ghati.

 madhyayugin kaal main yahaan kaaphi sari khadaanen thim jiname main kai ko janata ke liye khol diya gaya he . is prakaar kii khadaanon ko kinjig ghati, sugental, muinster ghati or totamoos ke aasapaas dekha ja saktaa he.
