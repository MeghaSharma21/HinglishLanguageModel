 moulana abul kalam azaad yaa abul kalam gulam muhiyuddeen ek prasiddh bhartiya muslim vidwaan the. ve kavi, lekhak, patrkaar or bhartiya swatantraaa senaanee the. bharat kii aazaadee ke vaad ve ek mahatvapurn raajnneetik rahe. ve mahatma gandhi ke siddhanto kaa samarthan karte the. unhone hindu-muslim ekta ke liye kaary kiya, tathaa ve alag muslim raashtra ke siddhant kaa virodh karne vaale muslim netao main se the. khilaafat andolan main unki mahatvapurn bhoomika rahi. 1923 main ve bhartiya neshnal kagrence ke sabase kam umr ke president bane. ve 1940 or 1945 ke bich kagrence ke president rahe. aazaadee ke vaad ve bharat ke uttar pradesh rajya ke rampur jile se 1952 main saansad chune gaye or ve bharat ke pehle shiksha mantri bane.
 ve dharasana satyagrah ke ahm inkalabi the. ve 1940-45 ke beet bhartiya rashtriya congress ke adhyaksh rahe jis dauraan bharat chodo aandolan hua tha. congress ke anya pramukh netaaon kii tarah unhen bhi teen saal jail main bitaane pade the. swatantraaa ke baad ve bharat ke pehle shiksha mantri bane or vishvavidhyalay anudaan ayog kii sthapana main unke sabase avismaraneey kaaryon main se ek tha.
 moulana azaad afghaan ulimaon ke khandaan se taalluk rakhte the jo babar ke samay heraat se bharat aae the. unki maan arabi mul kii thim or unke pita mohammad khayruddin ek farsi the. mohammad khayruddin or unke parivaar ne bhartiya swatantraaa ke pehle aandolan ke samay 1857 main kalakatta chhod kar makka chale gaye. vahaan par mohammad kharuddin kii mulaakaat apni hone vaali patni se hui. mohammad khiruddin 1890 main bharat laut gaye. mauhammad khiruddin ko kalakatta main ek muslim vidwaan ke rup main khyaati mili. jab azaad maatr 11 saal ke the tab unki mata kaa dehanth ho gaya. unki aarambhik shiksha islamic taur tareekon se hui. ghar par yaa maszid main unhen unke pita tathaa baad main anya vidwaanon ne padhaayaa. islamic shiksha ke alaava unhen darshanashastra, itihaas tathaa ganit kii shiksha bhi anya guruon se mili. azaad ne urdu, farsi, hindi, arabi tathaa angreji bhashaaon main maharath hasil kii. solah saal unhen wo sabhi shiksha mil gai thim jo aamtaur par 25 saal main mila karti thi.
 teraa saal kii aayu main unka vivaah zulaikha begham se ho gaya. ve devbandi vichaardhaara ke kareeb the or unhone quran ke anya bhavarupon par lekh bhi likhe. azaad ne angrezi samarpit swadhyay se sikhi or paashchaatya darshan ko bahut padhaa. unhen muslim parmparik shiksha ko raas nahin ai or ve aadhunik shikshavaadi sar syed ahamed khan ke vichaaron se sahamat the.
 aaazaad angreji hukumat ke khilaaf the. unhenne angreji sarkaar ko aam aadmi ke shoshan ke liye jimmevaar tehraaya. unhone apane samay ke muslim netaaon kii bhi aalochana kii jo unke anusaar desh ke hit ke samaksh sampradayik hit ko tarzih de rahe the. anya muslim netaaon se alag unhone 1905 main bengal ke vibhaajan kaa virodh kiya or ol india muslim lig ke algaavvaadi vichaardhaara ko khaarij kar diya. unhone iran, iraq misr tathaa syria kii yatraaen kii. aaazaad ne krantikari gatividhiyon main bhaag lenaa aarambh kiya or unhen shri arbindo or shyamsunhar chakravarti jaise krantikariyon se samarthan mila.
 azaad kii shiksha unhe ek dafaatar bana sakati thi par raajnneeti ke prati unke jhukaav ne unhen patrkaar bana diya. unhone 1912 main ek urdu patrika al hilaal kaa sutarapaat kiya. unka udyeshy musalmaan yuvakon ko krantikari aandolanon ke prati utsahit karnaa or hindu-muslim ekta par bal dena tha. unhone congressi netaaon kaa vishwaas bengal, bihar tathaa bombai main krantikari gatividhiyon ke gupt aayojanon dwaara jeeta. unhen 1920 main ranchi main jail kii sajaa bhugatni padi.
 jail se nikalne ke baad ve jaliyanvaala baag hatyakand ke virdohi netaaon main se ek the. iske alaava ve khilaafat aandolan ke bhi pramukh the. khilaafat turki ke osmani samrajya kii pratham vishwayuddh main haarane par unpar lagaae harjane kaa virodh karata tha. us samay automan makka par kaabiz the or islam ke khaliph vahi the. iske kaaran vishvabhar ke muslimon main rosh tha or bharat main yah khilaafat andolan ke rup main ubhara jisamen osmanon ko haraane vaale mitra rashtron ke samrajya kaa virodh hua tha.
 gandhi ji ke asahyog aandolan main unhone sakriy rup se bhaag liya.
 swatantra bharat ke pehle shiksha mantri the. unhone gyaarah varshon tak raashtra kii shiksha niti kaa maargdarshan kiya. moulana azaad ko hi 'bhartiya pradyogiki santhaan' arthath 'ai.ai.tii.' or 'vishvavidhyalay anudaan ayog' kii sthapana kaa shrey he. unhone shiksha or samskruti ko vikisit karne ke liye utkrisht sansthaanon kii sthapana kii.
 kendriya salaahkaar shiksha board ke adhyaksh hone par sarkaar se centre or rajyon donon ke atirikt vishvavidhyalayon main sarabhoumik praathmik shiksha, 14 varsh tak kii aayu ke sabhi bacchon ke liye nickshulk or anivaary shiksha, kanyaon kii shiksha, vyavsaayik prashikshan, krishi shiksha or takneekee shiksha jaise sudhaaron kii vakaalat kii.
 unhe varsh 1992 main maranoparant bharat ratna se sammanit kiya gaya.
