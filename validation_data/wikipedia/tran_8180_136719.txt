 bharat main garbhpaat karwaane kii kanoonee ijazat garbhvatee hone ke baad 20 hafton ke andar kuch vishesh haalat main hi he.
 bharat ke kuch hisson main betiyaan kii apeksha ladakon ko tarjeeh di jaati he or isko dhyaan main rakhte ling-adhaarit garbhpaat karavaaya jaataa he. isko nishkarsh rup se bharat main gair-kudarti ling-anupaat kii sthiti ban gai he.
 nayi takanic aane ke saath garbhvatee hone se chaar maheene baad garbh main pal rahe bacchon kaa ling pataa kiya ja saktaa he. bharat ke kai hisson main ladakon ko ore zyaada tarjeeh di jaati he or ladakiyon ko pet main hi maar diya jaataa he.
