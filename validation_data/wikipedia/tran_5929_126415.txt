 baldev ek kasbaa he jo mathura jile main aata he. baldev ek aitihaasik evam dharmik paryaton sthal ke rup main prasiddh he. yaho par daujee maharaj kaa ek vishwa prasiddh mandir he. yah krishna ke bade bhai the. ek lambey samay se baldev pracheen bhartiya samskruti evam sabhyataa kaa centre rahaa he. bhartiya dharm, darshan kala evam sahitya ke nirmaan tathaa vikaas main baldev kaa mahatvapurn yogadan sadaa se rahaa he. sangeet ke acharya swami haridas ji jaisi mahaan atmaaon se is nagari kaa naam judaa hua he.
 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar, baldev kii 9,695 kii aabaadi thi. purushon kii aabaadi kaa 54% or mahilaon kii 46% he. baldev kii ausat sakshartaa dar 63% he, jo rashtriya ausat 59.5% se adhik he. jansankhya kaa 17% 6 saal se kam umr ke hai.
