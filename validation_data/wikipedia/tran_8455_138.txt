 rakaposhi, paak adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit shahar se 100 kilometre dur sthit nagar ghati main sthit ek 7788 meter unchaa parvat he. sthaneey bhashaa main 'rakaposhi' kaa arth he - barf se dhakaa hua. yah karakoram parvatashrenee main sthit he. yah vishwa kaa 27waan sarvocch parvat he. is par sabase pehle chadhane waalon main do british parvatarohi mike banks or tom pati hai jinhone 1958 main, is chotii par chadhane main safalataa pai thi. rakaposhi ko dumani bhi kehete hai.
