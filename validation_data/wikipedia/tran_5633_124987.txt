 bilhor,yah ek kasbaa he jo bharat main uttar pradesh praant ke kanpur jile kii pramukh nagarpalika he. bilhor kii jansankhya 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar 18,056 aanki gayi thi. yahaan mukhya rup se hindi bhashaa ke taur par istemaal kii jaati he. yah kshetra kannauj shahar ke kareeb hone ke kaaran yahan pe kuch lakshan kannauji bhashaa ke bhi milte hai.
 yahan ke kisaano kii mukhya fasal aalu he. kisaan ko aalu rakhane ke liye uchit sheet-grahon ki vyavastha he. is kasbe main bhartiya jivan beemaa nigam kaa karyaalay, police thana, bas stesan, railway stesan or tehsil bhi sthit he,
