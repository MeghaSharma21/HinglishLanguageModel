 upanishad kii drushti main aparaa vidya nimna shrenee kaa gyaan maani jaati hai. mundak upanishad ke anusaar vidya do prakaar kii hoti he-
  para vidya jiske dwaara avinashi brahmatatv kaa gyaan praapt hota he ,
  aparaa vidya ke antargat veda tathaa vedangon ke gyaan kii gananaa kii jaati he.
 upanishad kaa aagrah para vidya ke uparjan par hi he. rugveda aadi chaaron vedon tathaa shiksha, vyakaran aadi chhahon angon ke anushilan kaa fal kya he ? keval bahari, nashwar, vinashi vastuon kaa gyaan, jo aatmatatv kii jaankaree main kisi tarah sahaayak nahin hota. chhandogya upanishad main naarad-sanatkumar-samvaad main bhi isi parthaky kaa vishleshan prastut kiya gaya he. mantravid naarad sakal shastron main pandit hai, parantu aatmavid na hone se ve shokgrast hai. mantravidevasmi naatmavit... riti shok-maatmavit. at: upanishdon kaa spasht mantavya he ki aparaa vidya kaa abhyaas karnaa chahie jisase isi janm main, isi sharir se aatma kaa saakshaatkaar ho jaae . unani tatvajna bhi isi prakaar kaa bhed-'doksa' tathaa 'epistemi'- maanate the jinamein se pratham saadhaaran vichaar kaa tathaa dwitiya satya k sanketak mana jaataa tha.
