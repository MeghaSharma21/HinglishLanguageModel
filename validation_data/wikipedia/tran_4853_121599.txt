 kamov kee-50 "blike shark" ekal seat vaala roosi atake helicopter he jo ki kamov dizin beuro kii vishisht samakshiya roter pranali ke saath he. yah 1980 ke dashak main dizin kiya gaya tha or 1995 main roosi sena main seva ke liye apanaaya gaya tha. yah Arsenyev main pragati company dwaara nirmit he. yah ek baari sashastr scout helicopter ke rup main prayog kiya jaataa he. yah ek bachaav injection system ke saath duniya kaa pehla parichaalan helicopter he.
 1990 ke dashak ke aakhir main, kamov or isroyal airospes industries ne turki ke atake helicopter pratiyogita main pratispardhaa karne ke liye ek agranukram-seat cockpit sanskaran, kamov kee-50-2 "eradogan" viksit kiya. kamov ne ek or do seat vaale sanskaran kee-50 "magaramchh" ko bhi dizin kiya he.
 kee-50 vishisht "blike shark" poshaak ke saath 17 june 1998 ko ek durghatana main kho gaya tha. iske payalat major general boris vorobiove ko durghatana main maare gaye the. durghatana kaa kaaran "helicopter ke sah aksheeya roter blade me kharabi ko" jimmedaar tehraaya gaya tha.
 13 march 2012 ko Torzhok ke nikat ek prashikshan udaan ke dauraan ek kaa 52 durghatnagrast ho gaya, donon payalton kii hatya ek jaanch shuru kii gai thi.
 roosi mistral-varg dwidha atake jahaajon par bhavishya kii tainaati ke liye udaan pareekshanon ke dauraan 29 oktober 2013 ko dakshin-purv masco main ek kee-52 ke durghatnagrast ho gaya. praarambhik jaanch se sanket miltaa he ki durghatana kaa strot "iject tantra kii kharabi" kii vajah se tha jiske kaaran ise anaichhik rup se sakriy kiya gaya tha. apaat sthiti ke mantralay ne bataaya ki koi maut nahin hui or payalat vimaan se surakshit rup se baahar nikal gaye, lekin payalat ko "aghat kii choton" kaa saamana karnaa padaa.
