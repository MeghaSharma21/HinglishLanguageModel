 jorge simon om prasiddh bhautikashastri the. unhone hi vidyut chaalkon ke ek samanya gun kii khoj kii or 'om kaa niyam' pratipaadit kiya.
 vidyut chaalkon main pratirodh kii khoj karne vaale german vaigyanik george simon om kaa puraa jivan sangharsh bharaa rahaa. vidyut dhara parichaalan kii khoj karne vaale om professor bananaa chaahate the lekin tatkaaleen gatirodhon ne unhen yah sammaan praapt nahin karne diya. om ko professor kaa ohada maut se kuch varsh pehle mila. germany ke airlanjen main 16 march 1789 ko janme om ke pita ek anpadh talasaaj the lekin pita ne om or unke bhai bahan kii padhai main koi kami nahin hone di. ise vidambnaa hi kahenge ki padhane se adhik prayogon main ruchi rakhane vaale om ne vidyut paripath se jude ek aise gun kii khoj kii jisase aage chalakar bijli kii khapat kam karne or upyogi chaalkon kii talash kaa rasta tay hua. airlanjen vishvavidhyalay main unki kalege shiksha hui. usee samay italvi vaigyanik alesandro volta ne naye electro chemical cell kii khoj kii thi.
 om ne apane prayog main paaya ki diye gayi taapmaana par chaalak ke siron par pravaahit kiya gaya vibhavantar praapt hone vaali dhara ke samaanupati hota he. apane prayog se unhone paaya ki vibhav dhara or pratirodh ke bich moolbhoot sambandh hota he. pratirodh hi vah moolbhoot gun tha jisaki khoj om ne kii. om ne achche or upyogi vidyut paripathon ke liye pratirodh jaisaa ek moolbhoot gun khoja jo chaalak main nihit hota he. behatar parinaam ke liye achche chaalak kii jarurat pratirodh naamak isi gun se tay hoti he. om ne vidyut paripath vishleshan kii buniyaad daali. vah unke prerak pita hi the jinhone om ko is mukaam par pahunchaaya jisase vah duniya ko kuch de sakane ke kaabil bane. vishvavidhyalayan shiksha main dhyaan kendrit karne main vifal om ko vah sahi raste par laae.
 pita ne om ko sitambar 1806 main switsraland bheja. jis pratirodh ke bare main om ne apana niyam diya uske bare main 1827 main pehali baar 'da galvenic circuit investigated mathematicali' main unka shodh chapa. ise kaaphi der se varsh 1841 main landan sthit royal socity se manyata mili or unhen kople padak se sammanit kiya gaya. george simon om ne vaidyutchaalak bal ke vitaran kii khoj kii or pratirodh vidyutvahak bal or vidyut dhara ke pariman main ek nishchit sambandh sthapit kiya.
 prayog kii manyata ko lekar ashankit om ne ise siddhant rup main bhi taiyaar kiya. lekin hua vahi jiskaa dar tha. unhone dhara ke pratikul bidaa uthaaya tha. unhone bhautikee kaa siddhant us samay pesh kiya jab germany main bhautik vigyaan ko ganitiya drushtikon se manyata praapt nahin thi. yahi kaaran he ki khoj ko vah prasiddhi nahin mili jisaki vah hakdaar thi. circuit dizin karte samay pratirodh kii andekhi nahin kii ja sakati. bijli ke sugamawa se parichaalan main pratirodh kaa mahatv kaaphi he.royal socity kii manyata ne pratirodh kii khoj ko paras pathar sa sparsh diya. varsh 1854 main om kaa nidhan ho gaya. isse thik do saal pehle unhen 1852 main professor kii manyata di gayi jo unki dili khwaahish thi.
