 
 karakaara pakshiyon ke falconidaaye kul ke polyborinaaye upkul ke sadsy hote hai, haalanki kuch jeevvaigyanikon ke anusaar inkaa karakarinaaye naamak alag upkul hona chahie. inke saath van shyen bhi polyborinaaye upkul main sammilit hai or donon hi shikari pakshi hote hai. karakaara dakshin va madhya america ke nivasee he or inkaa uttari vistaar dakshini sanyukt rajya america tak he. yah falconidaaye kul kii duusari, falco, naamak shakha ke pakshiyon jitani tezi se nahin ud pate or na hi yah hawa main udati anya chidiyaan kaa shikaar karne main sakasham hai, isliye yah yah aksar murdaakhor, yani mrit jaanwaron kaa maans khate hue, bhi nazar aate hai.
