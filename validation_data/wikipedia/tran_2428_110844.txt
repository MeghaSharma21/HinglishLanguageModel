 dhyaan ke liye sukhasana mahatvapurn asana he. padmasana ke liye yah asana vikalp hai.
 sukhasana karne se sadhak yaa rogi kaa chitt shant hota he. padmasana ke liye yah asana vikalp hai. isse chitt ekagra hota he. chitt kii ekaagratha se dharna siddh hoti he.sukhasana se pairon kaa rakt-sanchaar kam ho jaataa he or atirikt rakt anya angon kii ore sanchaarit hone lagata he jisase unamem kriyasheeltaa badhati he. yah tanaav hataakar chitt ko ekagra kar sakaaratmak urja ko badhaata he. chaati or pair majboot banate hai. veery raksha main bhi madad milti he. 
 pairon main kisi bhi prakaar kaa atyadhik ksht ho to yah asana na karen. cytica athwa ridh ke nichle bhaag ke aasapaas kisi prakaar kaa dard ho yaa ghutane kii gambhir beemaari main iskaa abhyaas na karen.
