 petha uttar bharat kii ek paardarshee naram mithai he. aamtaur par ytaakar yaa belanaakaar akruti kaa yah vyanjan, ek vishesh sabji, safed lowki se banaaya jaataa he. agra nagar se sambandhit hone ke kaaran ise aksar agra kaa petha bhi kaha jaataa he.
 badhati maang or navachaar ke saath, mul rup main taiyaar pethe kii anek kismein viksit ho chuki hai. anek swaadisht prakaar uplabdh hai, jaise ki kesar petha, anguri petha aadi. saamagree ke aadhaar par kuch anya badlaav kiye jate hai, udhaahrantah nariyal ke saath ek mishrit, yaa kewda saar petha ko swaadisht va sugandhit karne ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he. 
 aisa kaha jaataa he ki pethe ne mughal samraat shahjahan kii rasoi main janm liya tha. 
 mul rup se agra main nirmit hone ke kaaran yah agra kaa petha ke naam se sthaneey mithai pramanit hota he.
