 shwet pradar yaa safed pani kaa yoni maarg se nikalnaa Leukorrhea kehalaata he. yah hameshaa rog kaa lakshan nahin hota.
 adhiktar mahilaayein is galat faimi main hoti he ki safed pani ke jane se sharir main kamajori aati he, chakkar aata he, badan main dard hota he. sharir se tejaswita chali jaati he aisi bharat ar pados ke desh ke kuch prantome galat manyata purvakaal se prachlit he.  
 safed pani kaa nikalnaa do pramukh kaarnonse hota he. 
 safed pani nikalnaa praaia: striyon main swaabhaavik rup se kuch maatra main hota he.
 visheshat: maahvaaree ke purv, maahvaaree ke baad, andotsarg ke samay ar kamechha uddipt hone par swaabhaavik he.
 iske liye koi upachaar ki aavashyaktaa nahin hoti| samupadesan, sahi jaankaree dena paryaapt he. 
 shwet pradar yaa leukoria yaa liquoria yaa "safed pani aana" strion kaa ek rog he jisamen stri-yoni se asaamaanya maatra main safed rang kaa gaadha or badboodaar pani nikalataa he or jiske kaaran ve bahut ksheen tathaa durbal ho jaati he. mahilaon main shwet pradar rog aam baat he. ye guptangon se pani jaisaa bahane vaala straav hota he. yah khud koi rog nahin hota parantu anya kai rogon ke kaaran hota he.
 shwet pradar vastav main ek beemaari nahin he balki kisi anya yonigat yaa garbhashaygat vyaadhi kaa lakshan he; yaa saamanytah prajnan angon main sujan kaa bodhak he.
 schaabhavik shwet pradar
 safed pani kaa nikalnaa nimnan paristhiti main swaabhaavik hota he:
 safed pani kaa aavirbhaav adhik maatra main kaam uttejnaa hone par hota he. yah pani chikanahat utpann karata he. kudarat ki yah vyavastha sambhog ke liye atyant upyogi he. yah safed pani jab bhi kaamuk uttajna man main ho tab tab nikalataa he chaahe aap vivaahit ho yaa avivaahit| iske nikalanese naa kamajori, naa dard, naa anya kisibhi prakaar kaa swasth par hanikaarak prabhaav hota he. kamichha hone par sahi maatra main yah uttapann naa ho to maithun dard daayak ho saktaa he. iskaa ilaaj karnaa padataa he.
 shwet pani masik srav ke kuch din pehle adhik maatra main hota he. bijotapatti ke samay estrogen ki maatra badne se safed pani zyada bah saktaa he. garbhavastha main bhi safed pani kaa nikalnaa adhik maatra main hota he. navjaat arbhak bacchee main bhi mata ke estrogen ki vajah se safed pani nikal saktaa he.
 atyadhik upwas, uttejk kalpanaaen, ashleel vaartalap, mukh maithun, sambhog main ulte asana kaa prayog karnaa, sambhog kaal main atyadhik gharshan yukt aghat, rogagrast purush ke saath sahavaas,do teen purushon se ekasaath atyaadhik sambhog karnaa,sahavaas ke baad yoni ko svachh jal se na dhonaa va vaise hi gande bane rahanaa aadi is rog ke pramukh kaaran banate hai. baar-baar garbhpaat karana bhi safed pani kaa ek pramukh kaaran he.safed pani kaa ek or kaaran protist hai jo ki ek sookshm jeevon kaa samooh he.
 iske liye sabase pehle jaruri he saaf-safai - yoni ko dhone ke liye sarvottam upaay fitkaree ke jal se dhonaa he; fitkaree ek shreshth jeevanu naashak sasti aushadhi he, sarvasulabh he. boric acid ke ghol kaa bhi prayog kara ja saktaa he or yadi andruni safaai ke liye pichakaari se dhonaa ho to ayurveda kii atyant prabhavakaari aushadhi “narayan tel” kaa prayog sarvottam hota he.
 yoni maarg se safed, chipchipa gadha srav hona aaj madhya umr kii mahilaon kii ek saamaanya samasya ho gai he. saamaanya bhashaa main ise safed pani janaa kehete hai. bhartiya mahilaon main yah aam samasya praayah bina chikitsa ke hi rah jaati he. sabase buree baat yah he ki ise mahilaayein atyant saamaanya rup se lekar dhyaan nahin detii, chupa leti hai shwet pradar main yoni kii deevaaron se yaa garbhashay greeva se shleshma kaa srav hota he, jisaki maatra, sthiti or samyavadhi alag-alag striyon main alag-alag hoti he. yadi srav zyada maatra main, pilaa, haraa, nila ho, khujali paidaa karne vaala ho to sthiti asaamaanya maani jaaegi. isse sharir kamjor hota he or kamajori se shwet pradar badhata he. iske prabhaav se haath-pairon main dard, qamar main dard, pindliyon main khinchaav, sharir baari rahanaa, chidchidapan rahata he. is rog main stri ke yoni maarg se safed, chipchipa, gadha, badboodaar srav hota he, ise vezinal discharge kehete hai. is rog ke kaarnon kii jaanch stri rog visheshgy, ladi doctor se kara lenaa chahie, taaki us kaaran ko dur kiya ja sake.
 greeva se utpann shleshma kaa bahaav yonic srav kehalaata he. agar srav kaa rang, gandh yaa gaadhaapan asaamaanya ho athwa maatra bahut adhik jaan pade to ho saktaa he ki rog ho. yonic srav saamaanya prakriya he jo ki masik chakra ke anuroop parivartit hoti rahati he. darasal yah srav yoni ko svachh tathaa snigdh rakhane kii prakritik prakriya he vahiin andotsarg ke dauraan yah srav isaliye bad jaataa he taaki andanu aasaani se tair sake. andotsarg ke pehle kaaphi maatra main shleshma banataa he. yah safed rang kaa chipchipa padaarth hota he. lekin kai paristhitiyon main jab iskaa rang badal jaataa he tathaa isse buree gandh aane lagati he to yah rog ke lakshan kaa rup le letaa he.
 safed yonic sravah masik chakra ke pehle or baad main patala or safed yonic srav saamaanya he. saamanytah safed yonic srav ke saath khujalaahat yaa chunamunaahat nahin hoti he. yadi iske saath khujali ho rahi he to yah khamir sankraman ko pradarshit karata he.saaf or failaa huah yah urvar shleshma he. iskaa aashay he ki aap andotsarg ke chakra main hai.saaf or pani jaisaah yah srav mahilaon main saamaanya taur par puure chakra ke dauraan alag-alag samay par hota rahata he. yah baari tab ho jaataa he jab vyaayaam yaa mehnat kaa kaam kiya jaataa he.
 pilaa yaa haraah yah srav saamaanya nahin mana jaataa he tathaa beemaari kaa lakshan he. yah yah darshata he ki yoni main yaa kahin teevr sankraman he. visheshkar jab yah paneer kii tarah or gandi badboo se yukt ho to turant chikitsak ke paas janaa chaiye. bhurah yah srav aksar maahvaaree ke baad dekh ne ko miltaa he. darasal yah “safai” kii swaabhaavik prakriya he. puraane rakt kaa rang bhura sa ho jaataa he saamaanya prakriya ke tahat shleshma ke saath baahar aata he.
 raktim dhabbe/bhura srav: yah srav andotsarg/madhya masik ke dauraan ho saktaa he. kai baar baar shuruuaati garbhavastha ke dauraan bhi yah srav dekhne ko miltaa he. is aadhaar par kai baar ise garbhadhaaran kaa sanket bhi mana jaataa he.
 saamaanya yonic srav kii maatra main nimnlikhit sthitiyon main vruddh ho sakati he- yonparak uttejnaa, bhavatmak dabaav or andotsarg 
 asaamaanya yonic srav ke ye kaaran ho sakate hai- yon sambandhon se hone vaala sankraman jinke sharir kii rodhkshmata kamjor hoti he yaa jinhen madhumeh kaa rog hota he unki yoni main saamanytah fungal yeast naamak sankramak rog ho saktaa he.
 yonic srav se bachane ke liye –
  jananendriya kshetra ko saaf or shushk rakhana jaruri he.
  yoni ko bahut bhigona nahin chahie bahut si mahilaayein sochati hai ki maahvaaree yaa sambhog ke baad yoni ko bharpoor bhigone se ve saaf mahasoos karengi vastutah isse yonic srav or bhi bighad jaataa he kyonki usase yoni par chaaye swasth bacteria mar jate hai jo ki vastutah use sankramak rogon se bachaate hai
  dabaav se bachein.
  yon sambandhon se lagane vaale rogon se bachane or unhen failane se rokane ke liye condom kaa istemaal avashya karnaa chahie.
  madhumeh kaa rog ho to rakt kii sharkara ko niyantran main rakhaana chahie.
