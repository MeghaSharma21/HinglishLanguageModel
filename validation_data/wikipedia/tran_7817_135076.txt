 union parishad, bangladesh main sthaneey prashasan ke hinatam graamy prashasan nikaay hai. prayek union 9 "vaardon" ke sammilan se banataa he, jiname saamanytah ek gram ko hi ek ward ke rup main ankit kiya jaataa he. prayek parishad 12 parshadon dwaara rachit hota he, jiname se 3 sadsy mahila hotin hai.yah parishad ek adhyaksh ko chunati he, jo is padisah kaa pramukh hota he. 
 prayek union kii bhugolic seemayen, jile ke upayukta nirdhaarit karte hai. yah nikaay sambandhit union main krishi, audyodic tathaa saamaajik vikaas ke kiye jimmedaar, pramukh nikaay he. in parishadon ko aamane kartavyon kii aapurti hetu vibhinn adhikaar tathaa vittiya strot pradaan kiye gaye hai.
