 cocks yaa kukasas europe or asia kii seemaa par sthit ek bhugolic or rajnatik kshetra he. is kshetra main cocks parvat shrunkhlaa bhi aati he, jisamen europe kaa sabase ooncha pahaad, albrus parvat shaamil he. cocks ke do mukhya khand bataaye jate hai: uttar cocks or dakshin cocks. uttar cocks main chechanya, ingushetia, dagistaan, aadigeya, kabaradino-balkaria, karaachaae-charakassia, uttar osetia, crasnodar kroy or stavropol kroy ke kshetra aate hai. dakshin cocks main arminia, azarbaijan or georgia aate hai, jisamen dakshin osetia, abakazia or nagorno-karabakh shaamil hai.
 turki, iran or rus se ghire is pradesh main in teenon deshon ke ghatnakramon kaa asar rahaa he. halaaki atharahavin sadi se pehle rus or aathvin sadi se pehle turkon kaa is pradesh main koi astitv yaa mahatv nahin tha. iran ke midiyon ne aathvin sadi isapurv main yahan apana sikaa jamaaya. midi halaaki ek bade rajya kii tarah nahin the or asyriaayiyon ke sahyogi the. isa ke purv 559 main jab midiyon ke sahyogi pars ke hakhaamaniyon ne midia or asyria donon par vijay hasil kar lee to uske baad yah pradesh bhi hakhaamani parsiyon ke haash aa gaya. san 95 isapurv main yahan ke armenianyon ke samrajya kaa udai hua or ek sthaneey shashan ne satta kii bagdor samhaali. dhire-dhire iskaa vistaar hua or ek samay yah misr tak fail gaya tha. dhyaan rahe ki armenian arya nasl ke log the lekin aaj yahan semetic tathaa turk nasl ke log bhi basate hai. iran main saasaniyon ke shashan or roman samrajya kii spardhaa main is pradesh ko donon ne kai baar apana ang banaaya. roman is ilaake ko hameshaa apana ang banane main asafal rahe. lekin isi samay yahan isai dharm kaa prachaar bhi hua. nauveen sadi main arabon ne akraman kiye par unka shashan sthapit hokar nahin rah sakaa. san 1045 main bijantinon ne arabon ko bhaga kar georgia main ek isai samrajya kii sthapana kii. terahavin sadi ke madhya main mongolon ke akraman kii vajah se yah samrajya bikhar gaya. osmani turkon or uske baad roosiyon or iraniyon ke bich bhi is pradesh kaa bantwaaraa hota rahaa. 
 uttari cocks ke kai pradesh rus ke ang hai or uttar kii ore rus hi failaa hua he. pashchim kii ore cocks kii seemayen krishna sagar or turki ko chooti hai. purv main caspian sagar cocks kii seemaa he or dakshin main isaki seemaa iran se milti he. cocks ke ilaake ko kabhi europe or kabhi asia main mana jaataa he. iske nichle ilaaqon ko kuch log madhya purv kaa ek dur-daraaj ang bhi samajhate hai. cocks kaa kshetra bahut had tak ek pahari ilaaka he or isaki alag-alag vaadiyon or bhaagon main alag-alag samskritiyaan, jatiyaan or bhashaayen yugon se punap raheen hai or ek-duusare se jujh rahi hai.
 yahan 6,400 bhinn nasalon ke ped-paudhe or 1,600 prakaar ke jaanavar aise hai jo isi ilaake main paae jate hai. yahan paae jane jaanwaron main tendua, bhura rich, bhediya, junglee bhaisa, caspian hangool, sunaharaa mahashyen or odhani kauwa charchit hai. cocks main 1,000 alag naslon kii makadiyaan bhi pai gayi hai. wanon ke nazariye se yahan kaa paryaavaran mishrit he - pahaado par ped hai lekin vriksh rekha ke upar kii zamin banjar or patharilii dikhti he. cocks ke pahaado se ovecharaka naam kii ek bhedon ko charaane main madad karne vaali kutton kii nasal aati he jo vishwa bhar main mashhoor he.
 cocks ke kshetra main lagbhag 50 bhinn-bhinn jatiyaan rahati hai. inaki bhashaayen bhi bhinn hai or yahan tak kii is ilaake main teen aise bhashaa parivaar milte hai jo puure vishwa main keval cocks main hi hai. tulana ke liye dhyaan rakhiye ke hindi jis hind europi bhashaa parivaar main he vah ek akelaa hi dasiyon hazaron mile ke rackbe main failaa hua he - bharat ke purvi assam rajya se lekar andh mahaasaagar ke iceland dweep tak. cocks kii uljhi anginat vaadiyon main yah alag-alag bhashaayen or jatiyaan basii hui hai. yahan kii do bhashaayen hindi or farsi kii tarah hind-europi parivaar kii hai - arminian bhashaa or ouseti bhashaa. yahan kii azeri bhashaa altaai bhashaa parivaar kii he, jisaki sadsy turki bhashaa bhi he. dharm ke nazariye se yahan ke log bhinn isaai or islamic samudaayon ke sadsy hai. yahan ke musalmaan zyaadaatar sunni mat ke hai, haalanki azarbaijan ke ilaake main kuch shiya bhi milte hai.
 itihaas main, cocks kii kuch jatiyon ko rang kaa bahut gora mana gaya he or angrezi main kabhi-kabhi shwetvarniya jatiyon ko "kocksi" yaa "cokesian" kaha jaataa he - haalanki vaigyanik drushti se kuch ashwet jatiyaan bhi isamen sammilit maane jate hai. madhyakaal main yahan ke bahut se stri-purush kuch misr jaise arab kshetron main jaakar bas gaye the or aksar vahaan par bhuri yaa neeli aankhon vaale gore rang ke logon ko kocksi logon kaa vanshaj mana jaataa he. yah goraapan khaaskar charakas logon ke bare main mashhoor he.
