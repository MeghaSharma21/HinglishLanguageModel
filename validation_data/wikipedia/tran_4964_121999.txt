 khadija aslan calili ek turki bhashaa kii abhinetri he jisane nuri bilge jaylaan kii 2008 film uch mamun main abhinay ke liye yaad kiya jaataa he. is film ke kaaran jaylaan ko 2008 kaan filmotsav main sarvshereshth nirdeshak puraskaar mila or khadija sarvshereshth abhinetri ke rup main namankit hui thi. 
 aslan ne adana swarna ball antarrashtriya filmotsav kii sarvshereshth abhinetri puraskaar ke liye 2011 main ek or abhinetri gorkem yeltan ke saath vujuud film main abhinay ke liye diya gaya.
