 nausena kisi desh kii sangathit samudri sena ko kehete hai. iske antargat ranpot, cruzar, vayuyanavahak, dhwansak, surangen bichaane tathaa unhen nasht karne aadi ke saadhan evam sainikon ke atirikt, samudratat par nirmaan, dekhbhaal, purti tathaa prashasan, kaman, aayojan or anusandhan santhaan bhi sammilit hai. is prakaar nausena sarkaar ke sidhe niyantran main sthit sainik sanghathan he, jiske prashasan ke liye sarkaar pruthak vibhaagon kii sthapana karti he.
 nausakti kaa uddeshy sainik or vaanijya kii drushti se samudri maarg kaa niyantran karnaa he. nausena aisa saadhan he jisase raashtra samudr ke upyog par apana adhikaar to rakhte hai, parantu shatru ko is upyog se vanchit rakhte hai. nausena main ab vayuyan or niyantrit prakshepyastron kaa samavesh ho gaya he, jinase shatru ke desh ke sudur aantarik bhubhag par bhi prahaar kiya ja saktaa he.
 pracheen samay main nausena kisi jati yaa nagar ke sashastr logon ke jhund ke rup main thi, jo badi-badi naukaaon yaa jahaajon main samudr maarg se duusare kii bhoomi par akraman karti thi. inhen jahaajon se vyapaar, machhlee pakadana, yuddh or samudri lutpaat tak kii jaati thi. yuddh ke nimitt vishesh prakaar ke jahaajon kaa nirmaan apwaad ke rup main hi hota tha.
 aadhunik nausena kaa janmasthan bumadhyasagariya desh hai. kreet ke raajaaon ne itihaas ke prabhaat kaal main hi samudri sena sangathit kar lee thi or falswaroop bumadhyasaagar unke niyantran main tha. aitihaasik pramaanon se gyaat hota he ki sarkaar dwaara samarthit nausena sabase pehle athens main thi. baad main vistaaravaadi rashtron main athens kii samudri shakti nasht kar di, jisase kuch varshon baad genoa, holland or germany main samudri shakti ke prabhaavshaalee prayog ke liye adde bane.
 uttari africa main phiniciyanon ke upnivesh karthej kaa adda samakaleen shaktishaalee rome ke liye bhi ajey tha. yudhon main rome ne apane shatru karthej se nausainik chaaturi sikh lee or is prakaar roman nausena kaa janm hua. un dinon kaa ekmaatr nausainik asr lakdi kaa duramus tha, jisamen loha jadaa hota tha or jise pani ke andar andar chalaakar shatru ke jahaaj ko nasht kiya jaataa tha. jab duramus chalaane kii sthiti nahin hoti thi tab jahaaj ke deck par se sashastr sainik ladate the.
 17veen, 18veen or 19veen shatabdiyon main nausena kaa vikaas mand gati se hota rahaa. jahaajon ko adhik se adhik bada banane kaa prayaas kiya gaya. 17veen shatabdi ke madhyakaal main jahaajon par 50-60 topein hoti thim. 18veen shatabdi bitate bitate yah sankhya lagbhag dooni ho gai or england, france, america, spain tathaa kuch anya deshon main nausena vibhaag kii sthapana ho gai.
 19veen shatabdi main nausainik shakti kii drushti se britain sabase prabal tha. isaki nausainik shakti france or rus kii sammilit shakti ke baraabar thi. grihayuddh ke samay america ne kavchit jahaajon kaa nirmaan kar liya, jisase kuch samay tak vah sabase prabal nausenawala raashtra rahaa.
 20veen shatabdi main anek deshon ne apni nausainik shakti badhaai, jinamein germany, japan or america pramukh the. pratham vishwayuddh main germany or britain main anek nausainik sangharsh hue. britain kii shakti ne germany ko bite ov hegololand tak simit rakhaa, kintu germany ne pandubbi kaa itnaa safal prayog kiya ki mitra rashtron kii sammilit shakti bhi lagbhag vyarth si ho gai. yuddh kii samaapti tak germany ke bede ne aatmasamarpan kar diya or kuch samay ke liye german nausakti niskriy ho gai.
 pratham vishwayuddh ke baad 20 varshon tak britain kii nausakti sabase prabal rahi. in varshon main japan, america or germany ne bhi ani shaktiyon kaa vikaas kiya. dwitiya vishwayuddh main jahaajon se yuddh kam hua. nausainik yuddh adhikansh vayuyanon or pandubbiyon se ladaa gaya. germany ne pandubbiyon ke dhuandhaar prayog se britain ko nicha dikhaane kii koshish kii, parantu mitra rashtron ke pandubbimaar sadhano ke kaaran aisa nahin ho sakaa. yuddh ke ant main germany, italy or japan naushaktiviheen ho gaye. france bhi bahut durbal ho gaya or america kii shakti britain se dwiguneet ho gai.
 san 1613 i. main east india company kii yudhkarini sena ke rup main indian mereen sangathit kii gai. 1685 i. main iskaa namakaran "bombai mereen" hua, jo 1830 i. tak chalaa. 8 sitambar 1934 i. ko bhartiya vidhanparishad ne bhartiya nausena anushaasan adhinium paarit kiya or royal indian navy kaa pradurbhaav hua. dwitiya vishwayuddh ke samay nausena kaa vistaar hua or adhikari tathaa sainikon kii sankhya 2,000 se badhkar 30,000 ho gai evam bede main aadhunik jahaajon kii sankhya badhane lagi.
 swatantraprapti ke samay bharat kii nausena naam maatr kii thi. vibhaajan kii sharton ke anusaar lagbhag ek tihaai sena pakistan ko chali gai. kuch atishay mahatv ke nausainik santhaan bhi pakistan ke ho gaye.
 bharat sarkaar ne nausena ke vistaar kii tatkaal yojana banaai or ek varsh bitne ke pehle hi great britain se 7, 030 ton kaa kruger "dilli" kharida. iske baad dhwansak "rajput", "rana", "ranjit", "godavari", "ganga" or "gomti" kharide gaye. iske baad aath hajaar ton kaa kruger kharida gaya. iskaa namakaran "mysore" hua.
 1964 i. tak baariya bede main vayuyanavahak, "vikrant", kruger "dilli" evam "mysore" do dhwansak squadran tathaa anek frigate scawadran the, jinamein kuch ati aadhunik pandubbinashak tathaa vayuyananaashak frigate sammilit kiye ja chuke the. "brahmaputra", "vyas", "betwa", "khukhri," "kripaan", "talwaar" tathaa "trishul" nae frigate hai, jinka nirmaan vishesh reeti se hua he. "kaveri", "krishna" or "tir" puraane frigate hai jinka upyog prashikshan dene main hota he. "konkan", "karwar", "kakinada" "kananoor", "kadlur", "basin" tathaa "bimlipttum" se sunrag hataanevaale teen squadran taiyaar kiye gaye hai.
 chote nausainik jahaajon ke navnirmaan kaa kaary prarambh ho chukaa he or teen sagarmukh pratiraksha naukaaye, "ajay", "akshay" tathaa "abhaya" or ek naubandh "dhruvak" taiyaar ho chuke hai. cochin, lonawala, tathaa jamnagar main bhartiya nausena ke prashikshan santhaan hai.
