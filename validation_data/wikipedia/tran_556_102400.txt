 bombai, baroda and central india railway dwaara san 1906 main shuru kii gayi flying rani express, bharat kii madhyam duuri kii tivratam railgadee he. is railgadee kaa sanchaalan maharashtra ke mumbai central or gujarat ke surat steshnon ke madhya, pashchim railway dwaara kiya jaataa he. yah railgadee mukhyat: dainik rup se daftar jane vaale karmchaariyon or kaarobaariyon ke bich bahut lokapriy he. is railgadee ko ek saptahanth vishesh ke taur par shuru kiya gaya tha lekin aaj iskaa sanchaalan dainik rup se kiya jaataa he. 
 yah railgadee 263 kilometre kii duuri tay karti he or maarg main padane vaale pramukh stesan hai; andheri, borivali, palghar, dahanu, vaapi or valsad. 
 saptahanth main saiyr sapate par jane ke shaukeen logon ke liye 1906 main, flying queen ke naam se is railgadee ko shuru kiya gaya tha. iske aath saal ke baad 24 april, 1914 ko pratham vishwa yuddh chhid jane ke kaaran iskaa parichaalan band kar diya gaya. railgadee ko 1 may, 1937 ko ek baar phir se flying rani ke naam se shuru kiya gaya lekin ek baar phir dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke kaaran iskaa parichaalan radd karnaa padaa. aazaadee ke baad bahut saalon se band padi is railgadee ko teesri baar phir se 1 november, 1950 ko shuru kiya gaya or tab se lekar ab tak iskaa parichaalan anwarat jari he.
