 bharatvarsh main shakon ke jo rajya sthapit hue unamem bhi kshatrapeey rajyavyavastha thi. bhartiya kshatrapon ke teen pramukh vansh or ek rajvansh tha-
 kapisha, pushppur tathaa abhisar ke kshatrapon kaa pataa vahaan se praapt abhilekhon se miltaa he. maanikyala abhilekh main granvryak ke putr kisi kshatrap kaa ullekh miltaa he. use kapishing kaa kshatrap bataaya jaataa he. 83wein varsh ke kabul sangrahalay abhilekh main pushppur ke tiravharn naamak ek kshatrap kaa ullekh he. abhisarprast se praapt ek taanbe kii anguthi ke aakaar kii mudra par kshatrap shivasen kaa naam praapt he.
 punjab ke kshatrap teen vanshon se sambandh rakhte hai -
  manigul tathaa uskaa putr jihonic - mudrashastriyon ne inhen ayas dwitiya kaa, pushkalavati par shashan karne vaala, kshatrap mana he. kintu takshashila se praapt rajatapatr abhilekh ke anusaar jihonic chukhs jile kaa kshatrap bataaya gaya he. inkaa uttaradhikari kushulakar kaha jaataa he.
  indravarman kaa vansh - is vansh main indravarman, uske putr aspavarman tathaa aspavarman ke bhattije sas aate he. aspavarman ne ayas dwitiya tathaa gudufar donon ke rajtv kaal main kshatrap kaa kaary kiya or sas ne gudufar tathaa uske uttaradhikari packorij ke rajyakaal main kshatrap kaa kaary kiya.
 is vansh main sabase pehla rajuvul athwa ranjuvul tha jisane sambhavat pehle sakal par bhi rajya kiya tha. stencono ne us vansh kaa is prakaar anumaan kiya he:
 rajuvul kaa naam abhilekhon tathaa mudraon main praapt hota he. mora crup abhilekh main use mahakshatrap kaha gaya he. kintu uski mudraon par praapt lekh main use rajaadhiraj kaha gaya he. rajuvul kaa uttaradhikari shuds tha. abhilekhon main bhi use mahakshatrap kaha gaya he. inke abhilekhon main diye gaye varshon ko kuch vidvan shaq or kuch vikram samvath main maanate hai. is matbhed ko mitaane kaa saadhan abhi tak praapt nahin ho sakaa he. kharoshth, cono ke anusaar, rajuvul kaa shvasur tathaa palit ke anusaar, dauhitr tha. ek anya mudra par kharoshtee lipi main ‘kshatrapas prakhar ostas artsputras’ likhaa hua miltaa he.
 in kshatrapon ke muldesh ke sambandh main vidwaanon main matbhed he. kabhi unhen palhaav, kabhi shaq desh se aayaa hua bataaya jaataa he. sambhavat ve shaq the. faras se hokar aane ke kaaran ve kshatrapeey shashan vyavastha se parichit or usase sambhddha ho gayi; inke atirikt hagaan or hagaamash naamak do kshatrapon kii bhi mudraayen mathura se praapt hui hai. jo rajuvul vansh ke pashchaat mathura ke shaasha anumaan kiye jate hai. kuch sikkon par donon ke sanyukt naam bhi milte hai. or kuch par keval hagaamash kaa hi. inke pashchaat mathura main do teen kshatrap or hue jinke naam bhartiya hai. kadachit is kaal tak in videshiyon ne purnroop se bhartiyata grahan kar lee thi.
 ujjain ke kshatrapon ko pashchimi bharat ke kshatrap ke naam se bhi pukaarte he. ye kshatrap do vanshon ke prathit hote hai. pehla vansh bhumak or nahpaan kaa tha tathaa doosraa chashton kaa.bhumak ke sikkon ke agr bhaag par baan, chakra or vajra ke chinh or prishth bhaag par bramhi va kharoshtee main 'kshahraat kshatrap bhumak' ankit lekh ke saath saath dharm chakra sahit sinhashirsh ankit miltaa he. bhumak ke uttaradhikari nahpaan kaa pataa uski rajat evam tamramudraon se hi nahin varan uske damaad ushavadaat ke abhilekhon se bhi lagaaya jaataa he. nahpaan ne pashchimi bharat ke kuch bhaag par bhi rajya kiya tha.nahpaan ne pashchimi bharat ke kuch bhaag par bhi rajya kiya tha.periplus se gyaat hota he ki nahpaan kii rajdhani minanagar thi. usne satvaahan ke samrajya kaa kuch bhaag bhi jeet liya tha. iske vansh ko shaharaat kehete he. shaharaat vansh ko rudradaaman pratham ne samaapt kiya. girnaar abhilekh main use khakhraat vasanivases karas kaha gaya he.
  ujjain main shashan karne vaale dwitiya vansh ke kshatrapon main kardamakavansheey chashton ke pita yasmotik kaa naam sarvapratham aata he. chashton kaa putr jaydaaman kshatrap tha kintu sambhavat vah pita ke jeevankaal main hi mar gaya or ujjain par chashton tathaa rudradaaman ne sammilit rup se shashan kiya. junagarh abhilekh main mahakshatrap rudradaaman ke sambandh main kaha gaya he ki usne mahakshatrap kii oopaadhi arjeet kii thi. prathit hota he ki uske vansh kii rajyashri sambhavat gautamiputra satkarni ne chin lee thi or rudradaaman ko mahakshatrap kii oopaadhi pun un pradeshon ko jitkar kerani arjeet kerani padi. junagarh abhilekh main uski vijayon tathaa uske vyaktitv kii prashasti he. rudradaaman pratham kaa uttaradhikari uskaa jyeshtha putr damaghasad hua. uske pashchaat damaghasad kaa putr jivadaman tathaa uskaa bhai rudrasingh pratham uttaradhikari hue. isi rudrasingh ke samay aabhiron ne pashchimi kshatrapon ke rajya kaa kuch bhaag hadap liya tha. rudrasingh pratham ke uttaradhikari uske teen putr rudrasen pratham, sanghdaaman tathaa damsen hue. tadnantar damsen ke teen putr yashodhaman, vijaysen tathaa daamajadashri mahakshatrap hue. daamajadashri kaa uttaradhikari uskaa bhatija rudrasen dwitiya hua. iske pashchaat uske putr vishwasingh tathaa bhartrudaman hue. bhartrudaman ke hi kaal se uskaa putr vishwasen uskaa kshatrap bana. bhartrudaman tathaa vishwasingh kaa sambandh mahakshatrap rudradaaman dwitiya se kya tha. yah nishchaypurvak nahin kaha ja saktaa. is vansh kaa antim kshatrap rudrasingh tritiy hua jisane lagbhag 388 i. tak shashan kiya. guptavansh ke chandragupta dwitiya ne ujjaini ke kshatrapon kaa ant kar unke samrajya ko apane samrajya main mila liya or unke sikkon ke anukaran par apane sikke prachlit kiye.
