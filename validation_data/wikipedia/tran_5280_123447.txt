 munishwar ne suryasiddhanth ke bhaganon ke aadhaar par san 1646 main 'siddhanth saarvbhaum' naamak jyotish siddhanth granth kii rachana kii. 'swashay prakaashini' naam se apane is granth kii tikaa bhi swayan unhone likhi. iske alaava unke dwaara rachit anya granth hai-
