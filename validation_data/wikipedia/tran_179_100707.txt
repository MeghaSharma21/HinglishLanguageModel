 kachche bhojya padaarthon ko khaane yogya banane kii kala ko pakkriya kehete hai. 
 bahut si vastuen kachi khai ja sakati hai or ve isi rup main khaane par vishesh laabhprad bhi hoti hai, parantu bahut si vastuen aisi hai jo kachi nahin khai ja sakati. kuswaad evam hanikaarak hona donon hi iske kaaran hai. kaccha aalu, jamikand, kelaa aadi, kuswaad hote hai. samast anaaj kaccha khaane par haani pahunchaate hai. shwetsar se yukt vastuen pakaakar khaane par hi laabhprad hoti hai. unka shwetsar pakakar hi supachya hota he. aise kachche bhojya padaarth do prakaar se khaane yogya banaae ja sakate hai, ek to pakaakar, duusare surakshit karke.
 kachche bhojya padaarthon ko agni ke sanyog se pakaakar khaane ke yogya banane kii yah prakriya he. bhojan pakaane ke kai tareeke hai. bhojan chiknayee, bhaap athwa pani ke madhyam se pakaaya jaataa he. sidhe agni ke sanyog se bhi pakaaya jaataa he. inhen ritiyon ke anuroop bhojan pakaane kii kai vidhiyaan hai.
 bhojya padaarth ko ghi athwa tel ke madhyam se senkane kii prakriya ko talanaa kehete hai. kadaahi athwa anya kisi chhichley bartan main ghi athwa tel chuulhe par chadha diya jaataa he. acchhee tarah garam ho jane par isamen vastuen daal di jaati hai. ghi athwa tel itnaa rahata he ki vastu usamen bhalee prakaar dubi rahe. is tarah puri, kachaudee, pakodee ityadi banaai jaati he. is prakriya main bhojan ke kuch tatwon kii haani bhi ho jaati he.
 bhojya padaarthon ko thodi chiknayee ke saath, athwa bina chiknayee ke, pakaane kii prakriya ko senkna kehete hai. roti or toast bina chiknayee ke senke jate hai. ye vastuen tave athwa toster kii sahaayta se taiyaar kii jaati hai. parathe, chile, dosa, omelette ityadi thodi chiknayee ke saath tave par seke jate hai.
 bhojya padaarthon ko agni ke sidhe sanyog se pakaane kii kriya ko bhoonna kehete hai. is prakaar mukhyatya chanaa, laai, matar ityadi, jise chabaina kehete hai, pakaae jate hai. hare chane, hare bhutte, aalu, shakarkand ityadi bhi isi prakaar taiyaar kiye jate hai. angaare, garam raakh athwa garam baaluu bhunne ke liye kaam main laae jate hai.
 khaulate pani main bhojya padaarth ko pakaane kii kriya ko ubaalana kehete hai. kisi bartan main pani daalkar chuulhe par chadha dete hai. usee main bhojya padaarth daal die jate hai. pani khulata he or vastu pakati he. is prakaar pake hue bhojya padaarthon ke poshak tatv bahut kam maatra main nasht hote hai. praaia: sabhi bhojya padaarth is prakaar pakaae ja sakate hai. kuch padaarth keval ubalkar khaae jate hai. paashchaatya paddati ke to adhikansh bhojya padaarth ubalkar khaae jate hai. sab prakaar kii saag subjiyan ubalkar khaane se adhik laabhadaayak hoti hai. pani itnaa hi hona chahie jisase ubalanevaali vastu dhank maatr jaay. khaulate pani main subjiyan daalkar pakaana adhik acha he. isse poshak tatv kam maatra main nasht hote hai.
 is prakriya se bhojya padaarth bhaap ke dwaara pakaae jate hai. pakaane ke liye kai prakaar ke upkaran bane hai. aadhuniktam upkaran pressure cooker kehalaate hai. ye ek prakaar ke dhakkandaar bhagaune se hote hai. pendon main kuch pani daalkar, dabbon main bharkar, jo kuch pakaana ho isamen jamaa dete hai or dhakkan band karke aag par chadha dete hai. isse pani garam hone par jo bhaap banati he vah andar hi rahati he or bhojya padaarth kuch hi minute main pak jate hai. daal, chaaval, chanaa ityadi main kuch pani daalana padataa he, parantu naram vastuen bina pani ke bhi gal jaati hai. ek sada cooker bhi hota he. kai dibbe bhojya padaarth sahit ek ke upar ek jamaakar kas die jate hai. phir saath main jo lambii baaltee si hoti he, usamen pani daalkar ye dabbe rakh die jate hai. neeche choti si sigdi hoti he, jisamen-koyale sulgaakar rakh dete hai. is cooker main bhojan do yaa teen ghante main taiyaar hota he. ek teesri prakriya or he. kisi chaude muhan ke banrtan main pani chadha dete hai. uske munh par kapadaa band dete hai, yaa jaali kii chalani ityadi rakh dete hai. kisi bhi prakaar se bhaap kaa prayog pakaane main kiya jaay, bhojan laabhprad hi hota he. is prakaar pakaae gaye bhojan ke poshak tatv bahut badi maatra main surakshit rahete hai.
 is prakriya se bhojya padaarth na to chiknayee ke madhyam se, na pani ke madhyam se or na bhaap ke madhyam se pakaae jate hai. ek tarah se yah kahana adhik upyukt hoga ki ye ushn vaayu main pakate hai. ek vishesh prakaar ke chuulhe haate hai, jinhen oven yaa tandoor kehete hai. inhen aag jalaakar khub garam kar lete hai. jo vastu pakaani hoti he use isamen band kar dete hai. vastuen upar neeche donon taraf se sik jaati hai. pav roti, biscuit, kek, pastery, nankhatai mukhyat: isi prakaar banaai jaati hai. oven yaa tandoor kai tarah ke hote hai, puraani paddati ke to lakdi ke koyale se garam kar liye jate hai. ab bijli dwaara garam kiye janevaale oven bhi ban gaye hai. ye bahut suvidhajanak hai.
 yah prakriya kuch kuch ubaalane kii prakriya se milti hai. isamen bhi madhyam jal rahata he. antar keval taap kaa hota he. kachche bhojya padaarth pani main daalkar chuulhe par chadha die jate hai aanch bahut kam rakhi jaati he, jisase pani khule nahin. dhime dhime mandi aanch par pakataa rahe. is prakaar pakaae bhojya padaarthon ke posh tatv ubaalane kii apeksha adhik surakshit rahete hai. samast prakaar ke soup isi prakaar banate hai. pakane ke liye prayukt pani bhi soup main kaam jaataa he, at: poshak tatv nasht nahin hote. kai prakaar kii tarkari milakar isi prakaar pakaai jaati he. kadachit isiilie is prakriya ko "stuing" kehete hai.
 bhojya padaarthon ko surakshit karne ke kai madhyam hai. pramukh chini, namak, amla, tel or soda criyosot hai.
 chini ke madhyam se jam, jelly, achaar or murabbe banaae jate hai. falon ko katkar chini ke saath pakaate hai. jab pakaate pakaate chaashni khub gadhi hokar ekares ho jaati he, tab utarkar use thanda kar lete hai or dibbabandi kar dete hai. isi prakaar jelly banati he. antar itnaa he ki isamen falon ko pakaakar nikaala ras kaam main laate hai or nibu kaa ras bhi milaate hai. mithe achaar bhi chini yaa gud ke madhyam se banaae jate hai.
 praaia: achaar hi namak ke dwaara surakshit kiye jate hai. nibu or aam ke achaar keval namak daalkar bhi banaae jate hai or maheenon chalate hai.
 praaia: do prakaar ke amla, nibu or sirka, bhojya padaarthon ko surakshit karne ke liye prayukt hote hai. in madhyamon se mukhya rup se achaar hi banaae jate hai. shikanjabin main nibu kaa ras or chini donon prayukt hote hai.
 tel ke madhyam se achaar banaae jate hai. tel main banaae achaar varsho chal sakate hai. aam, katahal, lahasoda, mirch ityadi sabake achaar tel se banate hai.
