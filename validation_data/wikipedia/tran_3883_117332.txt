 champa shashthee margshirsh maas kii shukla paksh kii shashthee tithi ko manaai jaati he. yah tyohaar bhagwaan shiv ke avatar khandoba yaa khanderao ko samarpit he. khandoba ko kisaanon, charavaahon or shikariyon aadi kaa mukhya devtaa mana jaataa he. yah tyohaar karnataka or maharashtra jaise rajyon kaa pramukh tyohaar he. manyata he ki champa shashthee vrath karne se jivan main khushiyaan bani rahati he. aisi manyata he ki yah vrath karne se pichley janm ke saare paap dhul jate hai or aage kaa jivan sukhmay ho jaataa he.
