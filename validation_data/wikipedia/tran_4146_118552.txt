 bhartiya darshan ke sandarbh main, pratyaksh, teen pramukh pramaanon main se ek he. yah pramaan sabase shreshth mana jaataa he. 'pratyaksh' kaa shabdic arth he- vah vastu jo aankhon ke saamne ho. yahan 'aankh' se taatpary sabhi indriyon se he. 
 pratyaksh kaa arth prati+aksh hota he. prati kaa arth 'saamne' or aksh kaa arth 'aankh', yani pratyaksh kaa arth 'aankh ke saamne' hota he. yaha aksh kaa arth sankuchit na lete hue vyapak arth paanch indriya ke saamne arthath pach indriyon ke samaksh hone vaale gyaan ko pratyaksh gyaan kehete hai. yah gyaan prapti kaa saadhan he. nyaay darshan ke anusaar pratyaksh kaa arth indriarthsannikarshajnya gyanam pratyakshm yah he. pratyaksh gyaan kii utpatti kuch is prakaar hoti he.
 inamen se aatma, man or indriya kaa sanyog rup jo gyaan kaa karan yaa pramaan he vahi pratyaksh he. vastu ke saath indriya-sanyog hone se jo uskaa gyaan hota he usee ko 'pratyaksh' kehete hai. 
 gautam ne nyayasutra main kaha he ki indriya ke dwaara kisi padaarth kaa jo gyaan hota he, vahi pratyaksh he. jaise, yadi hamen saamne aage jalati hui dikhaai de athwa ham uske taap kaa anubhav karen to yah is baat kaa pratyaksh pramaan he ki 'aag jal rahi he'. is gyaan main padaarth or indriya kaa pratyaksh sambandh hona chahie. yadi koi yah kahe ki 'vah kitaab puraani he' to yah pratyaksh pramaan nahin he; kyonki isamen jo gyaan hota he, vah keval shabdon ke dwaara hota he, padaarth ke dwaara nahin, isiliye yah shabdpraman ke antargat chalaa jaayga. par yadi vahi kitaab hamaare saamne aa jaay or maili kuchaili yaa fatty hui dikhaai de to hamen is baat kaa avashya pratyaksh gyaan ho jaayga ki 'yah kitaab puraani he'.
 pratyaksh gyaan kisi ke kahe hue shabdon dwaara nahin hota, isi se use 'avyapadeshy' kehete hai. pratyaksh ko avyabhichaari isaliye kehete hai ki uske dwaara jo vastu jaisi hoti he uskaa vaisaa hi gyaan hota he. kuch naiyayik is gyaan ke karan ko hi pramaan maanate hai. unke mat se 'pratyaksh pramaan' indriya he, indriya se utpann gyaan 'pratyaksh gyaan' he. par avyapadeshy pad se sutarakaar kaa abhipraay spasht he ki vastu kaa jo nirviklpak gyaan he vahi pratyaksh pramaan he. 
 naveen granthkaar donon maton ko milakar kehete hai ki pratyaksh gyaan ke karan teen hai-
 pehali avasthaa main jab keval indriya hi karan ho to uskaa fal vah pratyaksh gyaan hoga jo kisi padaarth ke pehle pahal saamne aane se hota he. jaise, vah saamne koi cheez dikhaai detii he. is gyaan ko 'nirviklpak gyaan' kehete hai. duusari avasthaa main yah jaan padataa he ki jo cheez saamne he, vah pustak he. yah 'saviklpak gyaan' hua. is gyaan kaa kaaran indriya kaa sambandh he. jab indriya ke sambandh se utpann gyaan karan hota he, tab yah gyaan ki yah kitaab acchhee he athwa buree he, pratyaksh gyaan hua.
