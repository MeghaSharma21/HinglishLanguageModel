  ravaani yaa ramani chandravansi kshatriya kii shakha he. yah bharat ke vibhinn praanton main vibhinn naamon se paayi jaati he.bihar or jharkhand main ravaani yaa ramani yaa revni ,uttar pradesh main thakur rajput ,rajasthan main ravat rajput or madhya pradesh main puru vanshi rajput tathaa kuch somvanshi rajput bhi kehete hai.ravaani jati ke kul devi zaraa devi he jinka anek naam he jaise banni ,bandi, van durga,arany devi parameshwari.
 is samaaj main manyata he ki magadh ke shaasha mahapadmanand urf dhananand ke atyaachaar or kahar se bhaage hue chandravanshi kshatriya ramani yaa ravaani kehalaae. ye swayan ko magadh samraat "mahraaj jaraasandh" ke vanshaj maanate hai.
