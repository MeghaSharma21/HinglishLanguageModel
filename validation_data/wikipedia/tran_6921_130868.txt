 da rising nepal nepal kaa ek prasiddh dainik angreji samachar patr he. yah samachar patr nepal kaa sabase purana dainik angreji samachar patr bhi he. yah patrika kaa prakaashan gorkhaptr santhaan karata he.

 yah samachar patr nepal ke rajdhani kathmadon ke new road se prakashit hota he.
