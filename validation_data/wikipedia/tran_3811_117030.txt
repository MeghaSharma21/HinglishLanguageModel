 karnal rejinald edward harry diar british sena kaa ek adhikari tha jise asthaayee brigadier general banaaya gaya tha jo punjab ke shahar amritsar main jaliyanvaala baag narsanhaar ke liye uttaradaayi tha. diar apana pad se hataa diya gaya tha, lekin vah britain main ek prasiddh nayak ban gaya, khaaskar british raaj se sambandhit logon ke darmyaan. kuch itihaaskaaron kaa yah maananaa he ki is durghatana british raaj ke ant tak ek nirnaayak qadam tha.
