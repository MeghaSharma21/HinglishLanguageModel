 bhartiya cricket team ne agast 1997 main srilanka kaa daura kiya, do test match main bhaag liya or teen ek divasiy antarrashtriya maichon main bhaag liya. pehle test match ke dauraan, srilanka ne 6 wicket liye 952 ran banaae, test cricket main sarvaadhik team kii kul he. is match main kai record banaae gaye, jisamen sanath jayasoorya or roshan mahanama kii duusari wicket ke liye sabase zyada bhagidaari shaamil he. test series ko bina kisi parinaam ke samaapt ho gaya, donon test maichon ne drow kiya.
 srilanka ne teenon ekadivasiy antarrashtriya maichon main jeet hasil kii, haalanki kharaab mausam ke kaaran tisare ko phir se khelnaa padaa. toornament ke dauraan sanath jayasoorya sabase ullekhneey khilaadi tha. unhen test series or ekadivasiy shrinkhalaa donon main shrinkhalaa ke khilaadi ke rup main chunaa gaya tha, or saath hi saath match ke do purush bhi jeet gaye. vah test siries ke dauraan sarvaadhik scoring ballebaaj the, or ekadivasiy shrinkhalaa main srilanka ke liye bhi sarvaadhik wicket le chuke the.
