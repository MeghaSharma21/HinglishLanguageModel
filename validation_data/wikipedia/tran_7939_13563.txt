 nishkasit grah yaa dusht grah jinhen laavaaris grah yaa anaath grah bhi kaha jaataa he, aise grah ke aakaar kii vastuen hoti hai jo apane saura mandal se baahar nikalakar ab kisi bhi taare yaa anya chiz ke gurutvkarshan se pare hai or ab sidhaa akashganga kii parikrama karti hai. kuch khagol-vaigyanikon ne anumaan lagaaya he ki budh se dogune aakaar ke itane nishkasit grah hai jitne brahmad main taaren he.
