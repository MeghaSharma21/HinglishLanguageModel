 sandhi shoth yani "jodon main dard" ke rogi ke ek yaa kai jodon main dard, akadan yaa sujan aa jaati he. is rog main jodon main ganthen ban jaati hai or shul chubhane jaisi pidaa hoti he, isliye is rog ko gathiya bhi kehete hai.
 sandhishoth sau se bhi adhik prakaar ke hote hai. asthisandhishoth inamen sabase vyapak he. anya prakaar ke sandhishoth hai - amavatik sandhishoth yaa 'rumeti sandhishoth', psoriasis sandhishoth .
 sandhishoth main rogi ko aakraant sandhi main asay pidaa hoti he, naadee kii gati teevr ho jaati he, fever hota he, veganusaar sandhishul main bhi parivartan hota rahata he. isaki ugravastha main rogi ek hi asana par sthit rahata he, sthanparivartan tathaa aakraant bhaag ko chune main bhi bahut ksht kaa anubhav hota he. yadi samayik upachaar na hua, to rogi khanj-lunj hokar rah jaataa he. sandhishoth praaia: un vyaktiyon main adhik hota he jinamein rogrodhi kshamta bahut kam hoti he. stri or purush donon ko hi samaan rup se yah rog aakraant karata he.
 sandhishoth do prakaar ke hote hai : 
 kisi bhi teevr sankraman ke samay yah shoth ho saktaa he. nimnlikhit prakaar ke sankramak sandhishoth adhik vyapak hai : 
 yah shoth praaia: sharir ke anek angon par hota he. pyria, jeern unduk shoth, jeern pittashay shoth, jeern vayukotor shoth, jeern tonsil shoth, jeern grasani shoth ityadi.
 sandhishoth ke kaarnon ko dur karne tathaa sandhi kii sthaneey avasthaa thik karne ke liye chikitsa kii jaati he. inke atirikt rogi ke liye purna shaareerik or maansik vishram, paushtik aahaar kaa seven, dhoop seven, halaki maalish tathaa bhautik chikitsa karnaa atyant aavashyak he.
 Ml* rakt pareekshan or eks-re kii sahaayta se sandhi shoth kii dekharekh kii ja sakati he.
 pairon kii haddion ko sahi sanrekhan main rakhane ke liye or un par padane vaale vajan ko kam karne ke liye majboot mansapeshiyon kii aavashyakta hoti he. majboot mansapeshiyaan vyaayaam ke dwaara banaai ja sakati he. saptaah main kam se kam 3 baar vyaayaam jaruri he.
