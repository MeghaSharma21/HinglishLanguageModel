 navchand jhil, jise chini bhashaa main uayachuaan kehete hai, china ke pashchimi gansu praant main doonhuang shahar se 6 kimi dakshin main registani ilaake main ek nachlistaan main ek nae chaand kii akruti vaali ek jhil he.
 1960 main is jhil kii ausat gaharaai 4 se 5 meter maapi gai thi or isaki sab se zyaada gaharaai 7.5 meter he. samay ke saath iskaa pani khatm hota ja rahaa he or 1990 ke dashak tak isaki ausat gaharaai sirf 0.9 meter or sarvaadhik gaharaai sirf 1.3 meter rah gai thi. yahan kii sthaneey sarkaar ne ise phir se bharne kii thaanee he or tab se isamen pani badha he. yahan sailaani aksar unton kii saiyr or jhil kaa nazaaraa karne aate hai. is jhil ke aasapaas unche ret ke tile hone ke baavjood bhi yahan hawaaon kaa rukh kuch aisa rahata he ki yah jhil ret se bharkar nasht nahin hoti.
