 pataal hindu dharm ke anusaar prithvi ke neeche hote hai. vishnu puraana ke anusaar saat prakaar ke pataal lok hote hai.
 yah samast bhumandal pachaas karod yojan vistaar vaala he. isaki oonchai sattar sahasr yojan he. iske neeche saat pataal nagariyaan hai.ye pataal lok is prakaar se hai:-
 sundar mehalon se yukt vahaan kii bhumiyaan shukla, krishna, arun or pit varna kii tathaa sharkramayi, style or suvarnmayi hai. vahaan daitya, daanav, yaksha or bade bade naagon kii jatiyaan vaas karatin hai.vahaan arunanayan himalay ke samaan ek hi parvat he.
