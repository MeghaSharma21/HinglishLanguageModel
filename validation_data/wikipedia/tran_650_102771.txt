 vasa arthath chiknayee sharir ko kriyasheel banaae rakhane main sahyog karti he. vasa sharir ke liye upyogi he, kintu isaki adhikta hanikaarak bhi ho sakati he. yah maance tathaa vanaspati samooh donon prakaar se praapt hoti he. isse sharir ko dainik kaaryon ke liye shakti praapt hoti he. isko shaktidaayak eendhan bhi kaha jaataa he. ek swasth vyakti ke liye 100 gram chiknayee kaa prayog karnaa aavashyak he. isko pachaane main sharir ko kaafii samay lagata he. yah sharir main protein kii aavashyaktaa ko kam karne ke liye aavashyak hoti he. vasa kaa sharir main atyadhik maatra main bad janaa uchit nahin hota. yah santulit aahaar dwaara aavashyak maatra main hi sharir ko uplabdh karai jaani chahie. adhik maatra jaanaleva bhi ho sakati he, yah dhyaan yogya he. yah amashay kii gatishiltaa main kami laa detii he tathaa bhukh kam kar detii he. isse amashay kii vruddhi hoti he. chiknayee kam ho jane se rogon kaa muqabla karne kii shakti kam ho jaati he. atyadhik vasa sidhe strot se hanikaarak he. isaki santulit maatra lenaa hi laabhadaayak he.
 khaadya padaarthon main kai prakaar kii vasa hoti he. inamen se pramukh teen prakaar kii hoti hai, santrupt, ekal asantrupt or bahu-asantrupt .
 santrupt vasa nuksaandeh ldl cholestrol badhaati he, ise simit maatra main hi lenaa chahie. makkhan, shuddh ghi, vanaspati ghi, nariyal or tad kaa tel santrupt vasa ke pramukh strot hai. thos najar aane vaale hydrogenated vanaspati ghi main trans-fat acid hote hai. ye bhi nuksaandeh hote hai. 
 asantrupt vasa cholestrol ke echdiel ansh badhaati he. yah simit maatra main thik kahi ja sakati he.praayah bhojan main ekal asantrupt vasa or bahu-asantrupt vasa samaan maatra main ho to thik rahata he. aldiel cholestrol ghataane hetu, santrupt vasa kam kar den or ekal asantrupt vasa badha den. ekal asantrupt vasa ke pramukh strot moongfalee, sarson or jaitoon ke tel hai, jabaki kardi, surajamukhi, soyabean or makai ke telon main bahaasantrupt vasa adhik hoti he. kuch pakwaan ek prakaar ke or kuch anya telon main banane chaiye. isse ekal asantrupt or bahu-asantrupt vasa donon kii purti hoti rahati he.
 din main kul 15-20 gram khaanaa pakaane kaa tel hi prayog karnaa chaiye. vasa kii shesh dainik jarurat anaaj, daalon or subjiyon se puri ho jaati he. baadaam, kaashew or moongfalee tathaa doodh, paneer or kriim main bhi vasa prachur maatra main hoti he. vasa vaale vanaspati tel main bane pakwaan bhi baar-baar garam kiye jaaen to nuksaandeh hote hai. acchhee sehat ke liye vyanjanon ko talen nahin, balki un racippi par jor den jinamein pakwaan steam, bek yaa gril karke banate hai.
