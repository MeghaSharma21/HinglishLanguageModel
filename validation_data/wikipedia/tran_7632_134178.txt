 idia celular bharat ke vibhinn rajyon main ek wireles telephone sevaaon ko sanchalit karne vaali ek company he. iskaa shubharambh 1995 main tata, aditya birla samooh or anti and tii ke bich ek sanyukt udhyam ke rup main hua tha jab "wings cellular" jo madhya pradesh, uttar pradesh pashchim or rajasthan main sakriy thi kaa vilay kar diya gaya.
 armbh main jiesem kshetra main yah bahut hi simit thi, lekin 2004 main escotel ke adhigrahan se idia vastav main ek akhil bhartiya seva pradaata ban gaya jisaki upasthiti maharashtra, goa, gujarat, andhra pradesh, madhya pradesh, chhattisgarh, uttar pradesh, haryana, kerala, rajasthan or dilli main thi.
 company ke outlet "Idea n' U" binar ke adheen hai. yah preeped graahakon ke liye lacheele tarriff kii yojana uplabdh karne vaali pratham company he. yah nagareey kshetron main jeepiares sevaayen bhi pradaan karti he.
 idia cellular ne "sarvshereshth billing or graahak seva samaadhaan" ke liye lagaataar 2 varshon tak jiesem association puraskaar jeeta.
