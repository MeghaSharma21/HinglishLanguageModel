 vishnudharmottar puraana ek uppurana he. isaki prakruti vishvakosiya he. kathaon ke atirikt isamen brahmand, bhugol, khagolashastra, jyotish, kaal-vibhaajan, kupit grahon evam nakshatron ko shant karnaa, prathaen, tapasya, vaishnavon ke kartavya, kanoon evam raajnneeti, yudhniti, maanav evam pashuon ke rogon kii chikitsa, khanpan, vyakaran, chand, shabdkosh, bhaashankala, naatak, nrity, sangeet or anekanek kalaon kii charchaa kii gayi he. yah vishnupurana kaa parishisht mana jaataa he. vrihaddharm puraana main di hui 18 puraanon kii suchi main vishnudharmottar puraana bhi he.
 vishnudharmottar puraana ke 'chitrasutra' naamak adhyay main chitrakala kaa mahatva in shabdon main bataaya gaya he-
 
 vartmaan samay main uplabdh granth main teen khand hai. pratham khand main 269 adhyay hai, dwitiya khand main 183 adhyay tathaa tritiy khand main 118 adhyay hai.
 vastav main vishnudharmotarpurana swayan ek vrihad puraana he. isamen lagbhag 16 hajaar shloke hai jinka sankalan 650 i. ke aas-paas hua. iske teen khand hai. pratham khand main 269 adhyay hai jinamein anya puraanon ke samaan sansar kii utpatti, bhugol sambandhi varnan, jyotish, raajaaon or rishiyon kii vanshawaliyaan aadi or shankaragita, pururava, urvarshi kii katha, shraadh, vrut aadi strot aadi vishay hai. 
 dwitiya khand ke 183 adhyyon main dharm, raajnneeti, ashram, jyotish kaa paitamah-siddhanth, aushadhi vigyaan aadi maanav ke nity prati ke jivan se sambandhit vishay hai.
 tritiy khand main 118 adhyay hai. isamen sanskrit or praakrit kaa vyakaran, shabdkosh, chhandashastra, kavyashastra, aadi saahitiyik vishay to hai hi nrity or sangeet aadi lalit kalaayen or vaastu jaisi lalit shilp-kalaon kaa bhi vistrut vivechan hua he. 
 itane vishyon kaa or itane sookshm rup se anya kisi puraana main varnan nahin hua he or isi drushti se vishnudharmotarpurana anya sabhi mahapuraanon or uppuraanon main sabase adhik mahatvapurn puraana he. visheshkar shilpashaastra ke shastreey adhyayan ke liye yah khand apurv he. lalit kalaon sambandhi jo saamagree isamen milti he isse pehle anyatra kahin uplabdh nahin hoti. isaki gananaa nissandeh pracheen bharat ke lalit kalaon par sarvageen or mahanatam shastron main kii ja sakati he. vaise visheshat: yah chitra-sutara he, kintu kuch vishay jaise maan-pramaan, rup or lakshan, ras, bhav, sthaan or kshay-vruddhi jyon ke tyon murti kala ke liye bhi lakshith hai. chitra or murti sambandhi ye lakshan isse purvavarti anya kisi granth main uplabdh nahin hote. kalaon kaa jo prayog or abhyaas bharat main ho rahaa tha uskaa sabase pehle shastrikaran vishnu-dharmottar-puraana main hi 650 i. ke aas-paas hua. lalit kalaon sambandhi vivechan prarambh karte hue shaastrakaar ne kalaon main moolbhoot darshan kii ore sanket kiya. donon lokon main sukh kii prapti ke liye devataaon kii puja kerani chaiye. sangeet or nrit-kalaayen dharm ke upyog main aayi or unka rup mulat: dharmik ban gaya. bharat main lalit kalaon kaa vikaas or paripakvikaran dharm kii chaaya main hi hua. bharat main dharm koi bahari aacharan nahin he, dharm jivan kaa abhinn bhaag he.
