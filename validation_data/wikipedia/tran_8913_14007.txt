 shlipad yaa filpaanv yaa 'hathipanv' ke rogi ke paanv phulakar hathi ke paanv ke samaan mote ho jate hai. parantu yah aavashyak nahin ki paanv hi sadaa foole; kabhi haath, kabhi andkosh, kabhi stan aadi vibhinn avyav bhi phool jate hai. rog ke bahut se maamalon main koi lakshan hota tathaapi, kuch maamalon main haathon, pairon yaa guptangon main kaaphi adhik sujan ho jaati he. twachaa bhi moti ho sakati he or dard ho saktaa he. sharir main parivartanon ke kaaran prabhavit vyakt ko saamaajik or aarthik samasyaayein ho sakati he. 
 sankramit machhar ke kaatane se iske keede failte he. jab manushya baccha hota he to aam taur par sankraman aarambh ho jaataa he. teen prakaar ke keede hote he jinke kaaran beemaari failati he: Wuchereria bancrofti, Brugia malayi, or Brugia timori. Wuchereria bancrofti yah sabase saamaanya he. yah keedaa lymphatic system ko nuksaan pahunchaata he. raat ke samay ekatrit kiye gaye khun ko, ek prakaar ke sookshmdarshi ke dwaara dekhne par is beemaari kaa pataa chalata he. khun ko thick smear ke rup main or Giemsa ke saath daag ke rup main hona chahie.. beemaari ke viruddh antibadiyon hetu khun kii jaanch bhi kii ja sakati he. yah shoth newnaadhik hota rahata he, parantu jab ye krimi andar hi andar mar jate hai, tab lasikavahiniyon kaa maarg sadaa ke liye band ho jaataa he or us sthaan kii twachaa moti tathaa kadi ho jaati he. lasika vahiniyon ke maarg band ho jane se yadi ang phool jaaen, to koi bhi aushadh aisi nahin he jo avaruddh lasikamarg ko khol sake. kabhi kabhi kisi kisi rogi main shalyakarm dwaara lasikavahini kaa nayaa maarg banaaya ja saktaa he. is rog ke samast lakshan filaria ke ugr prakope ke samaan hote hai.
 jis samooh main yah beemaari ho, us sampurna samooh kii upachaar ke dwaara vaarshik aadhaar par rokthaam karke beemaari se puri tarah chhutkaaraa paaya ja saktaa he. isamen lagbhag chha: varsh lag lagate he. prayog kii gai dawaaon main albendazole ke saath ivermectin yaa albendazole ke saath diethylcarbamazine shaamil he.. davaaiyaan bade keedon ko nahin maarthi parantu keedon ke swayan mar jane tak beemaari ko aage failane se rokatii he. machharon ke kaatane se bachaav ke prayaason ke saath saath machharon kii sankhya ko kam karne or bednet ke prayog kii sifarish bhi kii jaati he. 
 120 million se adhik vyakti lymphatic filariasis se sankramit he. 73 deshon main lagbhag 1.4 billian vyaktiyon par beemaari kaa khatra madra rahaa he. africa tathaa asia ke kshetra main yah aam hai. is beemaari ke kaaran, ek varsh main kai billian dollar kaa aarthik nuksaan hota he.
