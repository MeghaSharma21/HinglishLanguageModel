 bharat vanade cricketron kii suchi
 ek divasiy antarrashtriya simit overon ke cricket kaa ek rup he, jisamen prayek team 50 over ke liye katoraa or chamagadad se prayek ke liye mil rahaa he. vanade main do pratinidhi tiimon, prayek hone vanade sthiti, ke rup main antarrashtriya cricket parishad dwaara nirdhaarit ke bich ek antarrashtriya cricket match he. pehle vanade melbern cricket ground main 5 janvari 1971 ko australia or england ke bich khelaa gaya tha. bharat antarrashtriya cricket parishad ke ek purna sadsy he or ek sthaayee vanade kaa darja praapt he. 1974 main bharat ne apana pehla ekadivasiy match khelaa; uske baad se 210 khilaadiyon kii kul team kaa pratinidhitiv kiya he. 1974 ke baad se bharat 896 vanade khele hai, 451 jeet, 399 haar, 6 baraabar or 39 main koi parinaam main jiske parinamswaroop. â‰ƒbharat 1981 main 2-1 se 3 maichon kii siries main england ke khilaaf apni pehali shrinkhalaa jeet darj kii. bharat ne 1983 or 2011 main do baar cricket vishwa cap jeeta tha or 2003 main bharat varsh 2013 main hone vaali isisi champions trophy jeet lee upavijeta rahaa tha or isse pehle barish kii vajah se antim do baar puraa karne kaa prayaas baahar dhoyaa 2002 main srilanka ke saath ek baar saajha kiya tha. bharat ne newjiland ke khilaaf 2000 main upavijeta rahaa tha. bharat 1984, 1988, 1990, 1995 or 2010 main asia cap main paanch baar kii kul jeeta.
 sachin tendulkar 16 saal or 238 din kii umr main sabase kam umr ke navdith he or farrukh ejeeniyar 36 saal or 138 din kii umr main sabase purana navdith he. anil kumble ne apane naam karne ke liye 337 wicket ke saath sarvaadhik wicket lene vaale gendbaaj he, or sachin tendulkar 44.83 kii ausat se 452 pariyon main 18426 ran apane naam karne ke saath agrani ran ganak he. vartmaan main, tendulkar 463 ke saath vanade match kii sabase adhik sankhya khilne ke liye record rakhti he. unhone yah bhi match khitaab kii main kii adhiktam sankhya ke liye vishwa record rakhti he. vanade main 418/5 ucchtam ek pari main bharat dwaara banaaye gaye ranon he. bharat kii ek pari main sabase kam kul ballebaaji karte hue 54 ran he. vartmaan main, november 2014 main srilanka ke khilaaf 264 ke rohit sharma kaa score ek divasiy match main kisi bhi khilaadi dwaara banaaye gaye ranon kii sankhya sabase zyada he.

 suchi jis kram main prayek khilaadi apane pehle vanade topi jeeta main vyavastha kii he. jahaan ek se adhik khilaadi ek hi match main apni pehali vanade topi jeeta he, un khilaadiyon karier kii shuruyaat ke samay antim naam se varnanukram main soochibaddh hai. is talika main aankade keval vanade bharat ke liye match khele shaamil hai. sankhyikee 23 janvari 2016 ke rup main sahi kar rahe hai.
 23 khilaadiyon kii kul bhartiya vanade team ab tak kii captaini kii he or unke bich mahendra singh dhoni sabase zyada jeet dar he. kapil dev ek 56% jeet dar ke saath duusare number par aata he. mohammad ajaharuddin 174 maichon main bharat kii captaini kii. kapil dev ko vishwa cap jeet cricket ke liye, 1983 main team kii aguwai karne vaale pehle bhartiya captain the. mahendra singh dhoni ne 2011 main bharat ke duusare vishwa cap jeeta. mahendra singh dhoni 50 se adhik maichon ke liye kisi bhi team ke captaini ko pehali or ekmaatr wicketiper he. ajaharuddin, sachin tendulkar or dhoni keval captainon baraabar-vanade maichon ke saath kar rahe hai.
