 rashtriya rajdhani kshetra dilli ko kul nau jile main baanta hua he. harek jile kaa ek upayukta niyukt he or jile ke teen upjile hai. prayek up jile kaa ek up jiladhish niyukt he. sabhi upayukta mandaleey adhikari ke adheen hote hai. dilli kaa jila prashasan sabhi prakaar kii rajya evam kendriya nitiyon or kaa pravartan vibhaag hota he. yahi vibhinn anya sarkari kaaryakartruyon par aadhikarik niyantran rakhata he. nimna likhit dilli ke jilon or upjilon kii suchi he:-
 in jilon ke sivaay teen nagarpalikaayen bhi rashtriya rajdhani kshetra main astitv main hai: 
 rashtriya rajdhani kshetra kaa vistrut kshetra dekhati he.
 nai dilli jila kaa prashasan dekhati he.
 dakshin pashchim jila
