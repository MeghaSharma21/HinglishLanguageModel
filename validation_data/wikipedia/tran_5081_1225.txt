 anamika 1973 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he.
 devendra dutt naamak lekhak apane chachaa ke saath rahata he. ek raat jab vah apane chachaa va sachiv hanuman singh ke saath ghar aate chalati car se ek aurat ko girte dekh use apane ghar laata he. agali subah vah apane ko devendra patni kahati he. chachaa use anamika naam die uske thik hone tak ghar main rakhane kii devendra ko salaah dete hai. kuch hi dinon main devendra anamika se prem karne lagata he. jab vah uski pahchaan jaanane kii koshish karata he to uske kai rup dikhte hai, jaise archana naamak ek vivaahita, kanchan naamak veshya ityadi| jaise achaanak ai thi vaise hi ek din achaanak vah gharase nikal jaati he. kuch din baad ek party main uski anamika se mulaaqaat hoti he or vah devendra ko pahchaanane se nakaarti he. jaise vah uski pahchaan jaanane kaa praytn karata he vaise uskaa rahasy ulajhata jaataa he. aage kii kahani main dikhaaya gaya he kaise devendra anamika kaa vaastaviktaa pahchaan paata he.
