 shalve nrisih dev raay vijayanagara rajya ke samraat the. ve vijaynagar ke saluva vansh se the. madhava sant shreepadarai ke sangrakshak, unhone sanskrit ke kaam ramguruon ko likhaa. unhone kannada kavi ling kaa bhi sangrakshan kiya. 1452 me unhen mallikarjun raay ke shasankaal ke dauraan chandraragiri ke mahamandaleshwar kaa sheershak diya gaya tha. unke pita saluva gunda chandragari ke rajyapal the. veerapaksh raaya dwitiya kii mrityu ke baad or prun devaraay ke vijaynagar ke nae rajkumaar ke aagaman ke baad, samrajya upeksha or araajaktaa main funs gaya. saluva narshima ne samrajya kaa vistaar karne kii koshish kii, haalanki unhone lagaataar vidrohi saradaaron ke kaaran kathinaaiyon kaa saamana kiya tha. ve vijaynagar ke saluva mul rup se uttari karnataka ke kalchuris hai. rajvansh saluva jo aitihaasik parampara dwaara uttari karnataka ke kalyani kshetra ke mul nivasee dwaara banaaya gaya tha. gorantala shilaalekh pashchim kii chalukyon or karnataka ke kalchuris ke samay se is kshetra main apni utpatti kaa pataa lagaataa he. vah shabd "saluva" shaks likhane waalon ke liye janaa jaataa he kyonki shikaar main "hoque" kaa upyog kiya jaataa he ve baad main aadhunik andhra pradesh ke purvi tat main faile, shaayad pravaas ke dwaara yaa 14 veen shatabdi ke dauraan vijaynagar vijay ke dauraan. sabase pehle gyaat saluva se vijaynagar yug main shilaalekh ke sakshya the mangaledev, salwa narsingh dev raay ke mahaan dada the. mangaldev ne madurai ke sultanate ke khilaaf raja bukka raya ek kii jeet main ek mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai. unke vanshaj ne saluva rajvansh kii sthapana kii thi or dakshini bharat ke vijaynagar samrajya kii sattarudh rekhaon main se ek tha. teen raajaaon ne 1485 se 1505 tak shashan kiya, jiske baad tulva rajvansh ne singhaasana kaa daawa kiya. unhone vijaynagar ke saath lagbhag puure dakshin bharat par apni rajdhani kaa shashan kiya. virupaksh raaya dwitiya kii mrityu or is kshetra ke singhaasana ke liye pruda dev raay ke pravesh ke baad vijaynagar samrajya ne kaaphi upeksha or patan dekha.. urisa ke gajpati shaskaon ke saath saluva kii ladaai ke baad 1489 kii ladaai main hamalaa kiya gaya, parajeet or kaid kar liya gaya, iske baad se nakaaratmak parinaamon ke baad kiya gaya. haalanki, baad main unhen apane samrajya ke kile or kuch asann kshetron ko aatmasamarpan kar die jane ke baad unhen karawas se mukt kar diya gaya tha.
