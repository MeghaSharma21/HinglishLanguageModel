 rajindar sachhar dilli ucch nyaayalay ke purv mukhya nyaayadhish the. ve 6 agast, se 22 disambar, 1985 tak dilli ucch nyaayalay ke mukhya nyaayadhish rahe. sevanivritti ke baad vah pipuls union for civil liberties naamak ek pramukh maanavadhikaar samooh ke saath jud gaye the. ve bharat sarkaar dwaara varsh 2005 main bharat ke muslim samudaay kii saamaajik, aarthik va shaikshanik sthiti kaa akalan karne ke liye gathit ‘sachhar- samiti’ ke adhyaksh the.
