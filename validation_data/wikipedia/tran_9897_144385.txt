 sanyukt raashtra mahasabha, sanyukt raashtra ke chah angon main se ek hai or yaheen keval sarvangin sanstha he, jisamen sanyukt raashtra ke samast sadsy rashtron kaa sam pratinidhitiv he. mahasabha sanyukt raashtra ke ghoshnaptr ke antargat aanevaale samast vishyon par tathaa sanyukt raashtra ke vibhinn angon kii kaaryaparidhi main aanevaale prashnon par vichaar karti he or sadsy rashtron evam suraksha parishad se uchit abhistaav kar sakati he.
 yah sanyukt raashtra ke paanch mukhya angon main se ek he. is sabha kaa har varsh sab sadsy deshon ke pratinidhiyon ke saath sammelan hota he. in pratinidhiyon main se ek ko adhyaksh chunaa jaataa he. kyonki samaanya sabha vah akelaa mukhya ang he jisamen sab sadsy desh sammilit hote he, uske sammelan adhiktar vivaad ke manch hote he.
 mahasabha baqayada, vishesh or apaat adhiveshan main milti he. baqayada adhiveshan sitambar ke tisare mangalvaar ko yojit hoti he or disambar main thodi der ke liye rukati he. agle varsh, yadi aavashyaktaa pade, to mahasabha phir se yojit hoti he or yah adhiveshan sitambar main, duusare adhiveshan ke ek din pehle, samaapt hoti he. har adhiveshan ke teen maheene pehle sabha kaa adhyaksh chunaa jaataa he . aarambhik do saptahon ke liye, samaanya vivaad jari rahete hai, jisamen mahasachiv or adhyaksh ke baad har pratinidhi ko sabha ke saamne vyaakhyaan dene kaa avasar diya jaataa he.
 mahasabha ke vishesh adhiveshan suraksha parishad yaa sabha ke bahumat ke anurodh dwaara yojit kii ja sakati he.
 mahatvapurn prashnon ke nirnay do tihaai bahumat ke anusaar hote hai. baaki ke nirnay saadhaaran bahumat ke anusaar liye jate hai. har sadsy ko ek mat miltaa he.
 1980 ke aas-paas, mahasabha vikasshil rashtron or viksit rashtron ke bich ke vivaad kii jagah ban gai thi. sabha ke do tihaai se adhik sadsy vikasshil rashtron ke hai or isliye vikasit rashtron ke paas mahasabha main sankhya bal kii drushti se adhik shakti nahin he.
 mahasabha ke pramukh vichaarneey vishay he - ni:shastreekaran evam shastraniyantran ke siddhant or antarrashtriya shanti evam suraksha sambandhi prashn. mahasabha ko antarrashtriya sahyog kii vruddhi, antarrashtriya vidhi kaa vikkaas evam samhitakaran, maanavmaatr ke moulik adhikaar aadi vishyon par adhyayan kii vyavastha karke un par abhistaav karne kaa bhi adhikaar he. mahasabha suraksha parishad kaa dhyaan un sthitiyon kii ore aakrisht kar sakati he jinase shanti evam suraksha ko sankat kii ashanka he. uparyukt vishyon par mahasabha ke prastaav aadeshaatmak nahin he parantu apane naitik bal evam vishwa janamat ke nirdeshak hone ke naate unka vishesh mahatv he. iske atirikt mahasabha suraksha parishad ke asthaayee sadasyon or saamaajik aarthik parishad ev nyaasatv parishad ke sadasyon ko nirvaachit karti he or mahasachiv evam antarrashtriya nyaayalay ke nyaayadhish ke nirvachan main yoga detii he. rashtrasang ke sadasyon kaa pravesh or nishkasana bhi, suraksha parishad kii sanstuti par, mahasabha dwaara kiya jaataa he. mahasabha ke anya krityon main rashtrasang ke bajet kaa anumodan, nyaas vyavastha kaa paryvekshan or anya angon ke kaaryon kaa sangyojan ullekhneey he.
 mahasabha kaa niyamit adhiveshan prati varg sitambar maas se hota he parantu adhikansh sadasyon athwa suraksha parishad ke anurodh par, mahasachiv vishesh adhiveshan bula saktaa he. mahasabha prayek adhiveshan ke liye ek sabhapati or saat upasabhapati chunati he.
 mahasabha kaa adhikansh kaary nimna saat mukhya samitiyon main hota he jinamein prayek sadsy raashtra ke pratinidhi hote hai:
 mahasabha kii do prakriyaatmak samitiyaan bhi hai:
 sanyukt raashtra suraksha parishad ke sthaayee sadasyon ke nishedhadhikaar prayog se utpann rashtrasang kii akarmanyata ke nivaaran ke liye mahasabha ne 1940 main laghusabha naamak ek antarim samiti kii sthapana kii. mahasabha ke satravsaan main mahasabha kaa kaary laghusabha kar sakati he or mahasabha kaa adhiveshan bula sakati he. iske anusaar, suraksha parishad main shanti evam suraksha ke prashnon par mataiky na hone par, 24 ghante kii soochna par mahasabha kaa vishesh adhiveshan bulaayaa ja saktaa he jo samuhik upayon kaa abhistaav or sainik kaarywahi kaa nirdesh kar saktaa he.
 mahasabha dwaara suraksha parishd ke 10 asthaayee sadasyon tathaa saamaajik evam aarthik parishd ke 54 sadasyon evam nyaas parishd ke asthaayee sadasyon kaa nirvachan kiya jaataa he. antarrashtriya nyaayalay ke sadasyon kaa nirvachan karne kaa adhikaar saamaanya rup se mahasabhaevam suraksha parishd ko hi hai.
 mahasabha ne pichley varshon main vishwa kii vibhinn jatil samasyaon par vichaar kiya or korea, greece, pelestin, spain aadi ke prashn par uchit kaarywahi kii. 1959 main britain, france or israel dwaara suez par kiye gaye akraman ko rokane main mahasabha safal hui. mahasabha ko praapt safaltaaon evam asafalataaon ke aadhaar par iskaa moolyankan karnaa uchit na hoga. yadhyapi mahasabha ke nirnay sadasyon ke liye aadeshaatmak nahin he, tathaapi vishwa itihaas kii sarvaadhik pratinidhi sanstha hone ke naate antarrashtriya shanti evam sahyog kii sthapana ke liye uskaa mahatvapurn sthaan nirvivaad he.
