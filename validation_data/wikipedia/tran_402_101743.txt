 masjid al-haraam yaa masjid al-haram islam ke sabase pavitra sthal, kaaba, ko puri tarah se gherne vaali ek masjid he. yah saudi arab ke makka shahar main sthit he or duniya kii sabase badi masjid he. duniya bhar ke muslim namaaz padhate hue kaabe kii taraf mukh karte hai or har muslim par anivaary he ki, agar vah iskaa zariyaa rakhata ho, to jivan main kam-se kam ek baar yahan haj par aaye or kaaba kii tawaaf kare.
 agar andar-baahar kii namaaz padhane kii puri jagah ko dekha jaae to masjid ke vartmaan dhaanche kaa kshetrafal 3,56,800 meter2 he . isamen haj ke dauraan 40 lakh log aa sakate hai, jo manushyon kaa sabase bada vaarshik jamaavada he .
