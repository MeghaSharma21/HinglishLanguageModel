 kaayra adwani bhartiya abhinetri hai jo bollywood filmon main kaam karti hai. 
 kaayra ne 2014 kii hindi film fagali se abhinay kii duniya main padaarpan kiya. iske baad vah 2016 kii hindi byopic em.es. dhoni: da antold story, or phir 2018 kii telegu drama bharat ane nenu main dikhaai di. kaayra baad main netflix film last stories main dikhaai di. film main kaayra adwani kaa hastmaithun drishy, ek wibrator kaa upyog karke, mahilaon kii kaamuktha ke spasht chithran ke liye prashansa kii gai thi.
 kaayra adwani kaa janm 8 march 1992 ko vyavsaayee jagadeep adwani or adhyapika genevy jaafaree ke ghar main hua.
