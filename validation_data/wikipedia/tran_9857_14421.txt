 ek parvatamala yaa parvat shrunkhlaa pahaado kii ek shrunkhlaa hoti he jahaan ek parvat duusare se sata rahata he or in parvaton ko koi darra yaa ghati hi se duusare parvat se alag karti he. yah aavashyak nahin he ki ek hi parvat shrunkhlaa ke bheetar aane vaale har parvat kaa bhuvigyaan ek samaan hi ho par aksar aisa hi hota he or ek hi shrunkhlaa main pruthak utpatti ke parvat ho sakate hai, udhaahrant: jwalamukhi, protthana parvat yaa valit parvat ek hi shrunkhlaa main ho sakate hai jo vastutah alag-alag chattan se bane hote hai. himalay parvat shrunkhlaa main prithvi kii sataha par sthit duniya ke kuch sarvocch parvat shaamil hai or jisamen se ucchtam mount everest he. andies prithvi kii sataha par vishwa kii sabase lambii parvat shrunkhlaa he. sabase lambii parvat shrunkhlaa vastav main madhyahn atlantic parvatashrenee he, jiskaa failaav atlantic mahaasaagar ke tal par bich main he. arctic cordilera vishwa ke sabase uttar main sthit parvatamala he jisame purvi uttari america ke kuch unche parvat samahit hai.
