 padaarthon ke bhautik gunon ko praayah do bhaagon main vibhajit kiya jaataa he -
 ye padaarth ke ve gun hai jo is baat par nirbhar nahin karte nikaay kaa aakaar chhota he yaa bada. udhaaharan ke liye ghanatv ek avistartmak gun he kyonki kisi padaarth ke 1 gram ke tukade kaa ghanatv dekhen yaa 10 kigra ke tukade kaa - donon samaan honge. in gunon ko 'maatra-swatantra gundharm' bhi kehete hai.
 anya udhaaharan : taap, apvartanaank, kathorta
 padaarth ke vistartmak gun ve hai jo nikaay ke aakaar par nirbhar karte hai. udhaaharan ke liye dravyaman ek vistartmak gun he kyonki padaarth ke chote namoonay kaa dravyaman kam hoga or bade namoonay kaa adhik. in gunon ko 'maatraatmak gundharm' bhi kehete hai.
 anya udhaaharan : aaytan
