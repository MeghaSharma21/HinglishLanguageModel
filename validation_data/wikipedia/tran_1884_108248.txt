 volta nadi pashchimi africa main bahane vaali ek nadi he. yah burkina faso desh main armbh hoti he, jahaan isaki teen upanadiyaan - shwet volta, laal volta or kaali volta - alag-alag chalati hai. yah tino phir ghana desh main pravesh karti hai, jahaan pehle shwet or laal volta kaa sangam hota he or phir kaali volta is nadi main milti he. aage chalakar iskaa pani ek jhil banaata he, jiskaa naam volta jhil he. volta nadi par lage aakosombo baandh dwaara bani yah jhil vishwa kii sabase badi kritrim jhil he or iskaa kshetrafal lagbhag 8,502 varg kimi he . is baandh se jalavidyut banaai jaati he. jhil se dakshin main ubharkar yah gini kii khadi main vilay ho jaati he jo atlantic mahaasaagar kii ek khadi he.
