 saptam jayavarman khmer samrajya kaa ek pratapi raja tha jisane san 1181 se 1218 tak shashan kiya. usne 'mahaparamsaugat' naam dhaaran kiya tha. vah raja dwitiya dharnindravarman kaa putr tha. uski rani kaa naam jayrajdevi tha. pratham rani kii mrityu ke baad usne uski bahan indradevi se vivaah kiya. aisa mana jaataa he ki ye donon raniyaan uske liye mahaan prerana kii strot thim. dwitiya jayavarman ko itihaaskaar sabase shaktishaalee khmer samraat maanate hai.
