 
 himalayan monal  jise nepal or uttarakhand main daanphe ke naam se jaanate hai. yah pakshi himalay par paye jate hai. yah nepal kaa rashtriya pakshi or uttrakhand kaa "rajya pakshi" he.
 is pakshi ko pashchimottar himalay main munal, ghur munal, ratiyaa kawaan, ratanal, rataap, kashmir main sunal, himachal pradesh main nilguru yaa munal, uttar pradesh main datia, mishmi bhashaa main piya padir yaa daafe, lepcha bhashaa main fo daung, nepal main dangan, bhutan main bup tathaa sikkim main chamdong ke naamon se janaa jaataa he.
 in pakshiyon kaa awaas kshetra pakistan, bharat, nepal, bhutan, myanmar tathaa china main he. afghanistan main ab inke hone kii pushtataa nahin kii ja sakati. yah praayah 6,000 se 12,000 fit kii oonchaai main paye jate hai lekin inako 15,000 or 4,500 fit kii oonchaai main bhi dekha gaya he.
 is pakshi ke aakaar main kaafii vividhta dekhi gai he. nar kaa aakaar 26 se 29 inch, pankhons kaa failaav 34 se 37 inch tathaa punch kii lambaai 9.5 se 10.5 inch hoti he jabaki maadaa kii lambaai 24 se 26 inch, pankhons kaa failaav 34 se 36.5 inch or punch kii lambaai 9 se 10 inch hoti he.
 patjhad ke mausam main praayah yah keede or illiyaan khate hai jo inako sadi pattiyon ke neeche mil jaati hai. anya samay yah jaden, pattiyaan, jhaadiyon or ghaas kii koplein, shahbaloot ke fal, beej, bari ityadi khate hai. sardiyon ke mausam main yah gehun yaa dhan ke kheton main mil jate hai lekin dane khaane ke liye nahin balkee jaden or illiyon ke liye jinhen yah pairon se khodakar nikaalte hai. 
 may or june main inkaa prajnan kaal hota he or prajnan kshetra junglee ilaaka hota he. ek mausam main ek hi baar ande diye jate hai. ande sene main nar koi madad nahin karata he or maadaa akele hi ande seti he. ande praayah maadaa dwaara kiye gayi zamin ke gadde main, kisi shila kii oot ke neeche, kisi bade ped kii vishaal jadon main yaa vanaspatiyon ke jhund main diye jate hai lekin kabhi-kabhi ande dene ke sthaan main sukhi ghaas, giri pattiyaan yaa thodi kaai bicha di jaati he. praayah ek ghonsle main 4-5 ande hi dekhe ja sakate hai lekin kabhi-kabhi darjan bhar bhi dekhe gayi hai. ande 2.75 inch lambey tathaa 1.75 inch chaude hote hai. yah halke badami rang ke hote hai or inamen chocolati rang ke chite hote hai.
