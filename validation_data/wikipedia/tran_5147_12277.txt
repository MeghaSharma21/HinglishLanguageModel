 
 koozastan iran ke 31 praanton main se ek he jo desh ke dakshin-pashchim kii disha main iraq kii seemaa se laga he. iskaa itihaas ilam ke samrajya se shuru hota he jo isa ke 2400-640 saal pehle tha. yahan ke log arabi or farsi donon bolte hai or iske alaava baktiyaari tathaa luri bhashaa bhi boli jaati he. isaki rajdhani ahvaaz he.
 kuzastan ko huz logon kaa sthaan mana jaataa he jo prachinkaal se yahan rahete hai.
