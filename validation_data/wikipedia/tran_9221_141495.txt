 
 
 eni, princes royal, KG, KT, GCVO, GCStJ, QSO, GCL, CD  rani elizabeth dwi or rajkumaar philip, edinburgh ke duk kii duusari santan or ekmaatr putree he. apane janm ke samay ve uttaradhikaar ke anukram main tisare sthaan par thi, apni maan or bade bhai ke peeche, par ab ve barahawein sthaan par he.
 rajkumaari eni "princes royal" ke upadi kii saatvin dharak hai. unhen apane dankarm ke liye janaa jaataa he, or ve 200 se bhi adhik danakarmi sanghathanon kii pitrunamika hai. saath hi unhen apni ashvakrida pratibha ke liye bhi janaa jaataa he; unhone european aventing championships main do rajtapadak or ek swarnapadak jeet he. tathaa ve british shahi parivaar se olympic khelon main bhaag lene vaali pehali sadsy hai.
 rajkumaari eni kaa vivaah capten mark philips ke saath 1973 main hua tha. 1992 main unhone aapas main talaaq le liya. unke kul do santaanen or teen pote-potiyaan hai. 1992 main, talaaq ke kuch maheenon ke bheetar hi, eni ne vais-admiral sar timothy laurence se shaadi kar lee, jinase ve tab milin thi, jab wo unki maan ke ghudsal ke rup main 1986 se 1988 ke bich kaam karte the.
