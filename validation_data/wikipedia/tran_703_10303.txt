 harini ravi tamil filmon kii mashhoor parshvagayika hai. 
 yah bharat kii pramukh vaayalinavadak ve ve ravi or vishalan ravi kii beti he. or yah raghav krishna kii bahan he.
 harini ravi chennai main paidaa hui thi. or sar shivaswami kalalaya skool main padhaa. vah skool str par sangeet, khel, chitrakala, bhagavad gita pratiyogitaaon ke liye kai puraskaar or puraskaar jeeta he. harini ravi use bachapan se pita or maa ke saath karnataka sangeet kaa prashikshan shuru kiya. vah angreji or bhartiya bhashaaon main 2000 se adhik vigyaapan filmon ke liye aawaaj di he.
 vah ilaiyaraja, e.aar. rahamaan, vidya sagar imaan, ke bhagyaraj, dina, vijay antony, kavi priy tampi jaise pratishthit sangeet nirdeshkon ke liye kores or ekal geet gaaya he.
