 
 santara ek fal he. santare ko haath se chhilne ke baad peshiyon ko alag kar ke chuskar khaaya ja saktaa he. santare kaa ras nikaalkar piyaa ja saktaa he. santara thanda, tan or man ko prasannataa dene vaala he. upwas or sabhi rogon main naarangi di ja sakati he. jinaki pachan shakti kharaab ho, unako naarangi kaa ras teen gune pani main milakar dena chaiye. ek vyakti ko ek baar main ek yaa do naarangi lenaa paryaapt he. ek vyakti ko jitne vitamin ‘si’ kii aavashyaktaa hoti he, vah ek naarangi pratidin khate rahane se puri ho jaati he. khaansee-zukam hone par naarangi ke ras kaa ek gilaas nity pite rahane se laabh hoga. swaad ke liye namak yaa mishri daalkar pi sakate he.
 santara ek swasthyavardhak fal he. isamen prachur maatra main vitamin si hota he. loha or potacium bhi kaaphi hota he. santare kii sabase badi visheshta yah he ki isamen vidyaman fructose, dextros, khanij evam vitamin sharir main pahunchate hi urja dena prarambh kar dete hai. santare ke seven se sharir swasth rahata he, chusti-furti badhati he, twachaa main nikhaar aata he tathaa saundarya main vruddhi hoti he. prastut he iske kuch prayog-
 is tarah santara sehat ko hi nahin, khubsuurati ko bhi sanvarta he. hameshaa pake va mithe santare kaa hi seven karnaa chahie. garmiyon main santare kii fasal apane puure jor par hoti he.
 ek naarangi, vishesh rup se, mithi santare, neembu × Citrus sinensis or uske fal. santare kii kheti pracheen mul ke ek sankar, pomelo ke bich sambhawatah he or keenu . yah ek chota sa phool ke bare main 10 sadabahaar pattey, jo baari kii vyavastha, crenulate munafa or 4-10 semi lambey samay se ovate aakaar ke hote hai saath lanbaa me badhati vriksh he. naarangi fal ek hesperidium, beri kaa ek prakaar he.
 dakshin purv asia main utpann santare. Citrus sinensis kaa fal meetha naarangi kaha ki yah citrus kaa ped se alag, kadavi naarangi he. naam ke ant main apane antim rup se naarangi kaa ped, ke liye dravid or tamil shabd se praapt vikasshil madhyavarti anek bhashaaon ke madhyam se guzar jane ke baad laga he. sabhi cites pedo ekal jinas, citrus ke hai or kaaphi had tak rah interbreedable, vah he, vahaan keval ek "superspecies" jo grapefruits, neembu, nibu or santare shaamil he. phir bhi, naam jinas ke vibhinn sadasyon ko diya gaya he, santare aksar Citrus sinensis or citrus ped ke rup main bheja ja rahaa he. jinas citrus ke sabhi sadasyon kaa fal jamun mana jaataa he kyonki ve kai beej he, hai maansal or naram or ek hi andashay se nikaale jate hai. naarangi rang kaa beej ek ghayal karnaa kaha jaataa he. chhil ke andar se judi saamagree kii tarah safed dhaage kathith majja he.
 santare kaa ras bahut sphurti daayak hota he.
