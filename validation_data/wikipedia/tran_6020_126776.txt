 ek agrant lod yaa bikri charge vah kamishan he jo ek dalal ko mutual fund ke dwaara chukaaya jaataa he jab share khride jate hai, yah nivesh kiye gaye fund kii pratishtata ke rup main liya jaataa he. nivesh ke mulya ko lod kii maatr ke dwaara kam kiya gaya he. kuch fundon ke paas asthgit bikri prabharee yaa bak and lod hai. is prakaar ke fund main ek niveshak share kharidte samay koi bikri charge nahin detaa he, lekin share kitne samay ke liye rakhe jate hai iske aadhaar par unki vaapasi ke samay kamishan detaa he. ek anya vyutpann he sanrachana bhaar ke str par fund, jisamen fund ko kharidte samay koi bikri charge nahin diya jaataa he, lekin yadi khride gaye sheyaron ko ek saal ke andar bech diya jaae to bek and lod liya jaataa he. 
 lod fund vittiya madhyasthon jaise dalal, aarthik yojanakaar s or anya prakaar ke panjikrut pratinidhiyon ke madhyam se beche jate hai jo apni sevaao ke liye kamishan lete hai. front -and lod fundon ke share kai vibhinnataaon ke aadhaar par breakpoints  ke liye aavarti rup se yogya hote hai. inamen niveshkon yaa vibhinn paariwarik sadasyon ke dwaara rakhe gaye samaan fund parivaar ke khate bhi shaamil hote hai, yaa " aaj " ke kam kamishan ke liye ek di gayi samay avadhi main vaapasi par adhik fund kharidne ke liye vachanabaddhata hoti he.
 kai mutual fundon ko bina bikri mulya chukaaye karidaa ja saktaa he. ye no-lod fund kehalaate hai. khud fund company se uplabdh hone ke saath hi, dalal ke dwaara no lod fund ko ek sapaat vinimay shulk yaa shunya shulk ke liye kuch batte par bechaa ja saktaa he. 
 no-lod funds main suchakank fund or sakriy rup se prabandhit fund donon shaamil hote hai. no-lod suchakank fund bechney vaali sabase bade mutual fund parivaar hai Vanguard or fidelity, yadhyapi kai chote mutual fund parivaar hai jinke paas no-lod fund hai. kuch no-lod suchakank fundon main vyay anupaat 0.2 % prati varsh se kam hota he jabaki vishisht sakriyata se prabandhit fund kaa vyay anupaat lagbhag 1.5 % prati varsh he. lod funds main saamanyatya ucch vyay anupaat hote hai jab lod ke bare main vichaar kiya jaataa he. vyay kaa anupaat he pratyaashit vaarshik mulya se, fund ke share rakhane vaale niveshak ko.udhaaharan ke liye, 100000 dollars ke nivesh par 0.2 % kharch ke anupaat kaa taatpary he 200 dollar vaarshik vyay, jabaki 1.5 % kharch kaa anupaat, 1500 dollar salanaa kharch kaa kaaran hoga.ye vyay mutual fund ko kharidne ke liye kisi bhi bikri kamishan ko chukaane se pehle kiye jate hai. 
 anek keval shulk vittiya salaahkaar no-load funds jaise suchakank fundon kii salaah dete hai. yadi salaahkaar keval-shulk prakaar kaa nahin he lekin kamishan par kaary karata he, salahakaar ucch kamishan lod funds ko bechney main byaaj par matbhed rakh saktaa he.
