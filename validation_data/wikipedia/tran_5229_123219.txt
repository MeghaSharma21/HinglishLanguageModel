 tokyo khadi, japan ke dakshini kaanto kshetra main sthit ek khadi he, or tokyo, kanagava praant, or chiba praant ke taton main failaa hua hai. tokyo khadi uraaga channel dwaara prashanth mahaasaagar se judaa hua hai. iskaa purana naam eado khadi tha. tokyo khadi kshetra, japan main sabase adhik aabaadi vaala or sabase bada audyogic kshetra he.
 pracheen samay main, japan main tokyo khadi ko uchee-umi yaa "aantarik samudr" ke rup main jaanate the, ajuchi-momoyama kaal main is kshetra ko eado khadi ke rup main janaa jane laga, joki paas sthit eado shahar se liya gaya tha. 1868 main, shahi adaalat ko eado main laayaa gaya or shahar ko tokyo naam diya gaya, jiske kaaran khadi ko apana aadhunik tokyo khadi naam mila.
 tokyo khadi peri khojayatra kaa ek sthal tha, jisamen comodor mathew peri dwaara sanyukt rajya or japan ke bich 1853 se 1854 main do alag-alag yatraaen shaamil thim. peri ne apane chaar "blike ships" ke saath 8 july, 1853 ko eado khadi main pahuchaan or 1854 main tokugava shogunet ke saath sanyukt rajya or japan ke bich shanti or vyapaar sandhi kii vaarta shuru kii. camodor peri ke jahaajon main se ek kaa jhanda nausena academy sangrahalay se bhent kiya gaya tha or samaaroh main pradarshit kiya gaya tha.
 dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke ant main japan dwaara samarpan ke patr par 2 sitambar, 1945 ko tokyo khadi main khadey uassoes mizori par hastakshar kiye gaye.
 tokyo khadi, pramukh rup se kaanto ke maidan kshetra main aata hai. yah chiba praant ke bosco prayadweep dwaara purv main or pashchim main kanagava praant ke miura prayadweep se ghira hua hai. is prakaar yahan kaa kshetrafal lagbhag 922 varg kilometre hai. tokyo khadi ke tat main jal nikasi talachat ke pathaar ban jate he halanki yah teji se banate bigadate rahete hai. khadi ke kinare par sthit talachat ek chikani, satat tatarekha banaati hai. tokyo khadi kaa kshetra uraaga channel ke madhyam se samudr se judata hai, is prakaar iskaa kul kshetrafal bad kar 1,500 varg kilometre ho jaataa hai. 
 chiba praant ke kep phutsu or yokohama praant ke kep maanmuk ke bich kaa chhichhlaa khadi kshetra, nakaanos ke rup main janaa jaataa hai, or isaki gaharaai 20 meter hai. is kshetra ke uttar main khadi kii gaharaai 40 meter kii or pani ke neeche ek sapaat sthalakriti he. nakaanos ke dakshin main prashanth mahaasaagar kii ore gaharaai badhati jaati hai.
 tokyo khadi main kai nadiyaan aakar milti hai, or sabhi khadi ke aas-paas ke awasaiya or audyogic kshetron ko jal pradaan karti hai. unamem se tema nadi or sumida nadi, tokyo main khadi se milti hai.. eado nadi, tokyo or chiba praant ke bich tokyo khadi main milti he. vahiin obitsu nadi or yoro nadi, chiba praant main khadi se milti hai.
 tokyo khadi ekua-line pul-surang, kawasaki or kiseraju ko aapas main jodatii he; tokyo-vaan pheri bhi uruga channel kii ore kurama or kanaya ko aapas jodatii he.
 tokyo khadi, machhlee pakadaney ke udyog kaa ek aitihaasik centre rahaa hai, yah shelfish kaa ek strot or anya jaleey krishi kaa kendra bhi rahaa hai. 20veen shatabdi tak tokyo khadi kshetra ke audyogikaran ke saath hi in udyogon main kami aati gai, or dusre vishwa yuddh ke baad kehin or keyo audyogic kshetra ke nirmaan ke saath hi lagbhag puri tarah se samaapt ho gai.
 japan ke sabase mahatvapurn bandargah tokyo khadi main sthit hai. yokohama bandargah, chiba bandargah, tokyo bandargah, kawasaki bandargah, yokosuka bandargah, kisaraju bandargah, na keval japan ke balki asia-prashanth kshetra ke sabase vyast bandargah hai.yokosuka ke bandargah main japani samudri swa-raksha bal or sanyukt rajya nausena kii ek chaowkee sthit hai.
