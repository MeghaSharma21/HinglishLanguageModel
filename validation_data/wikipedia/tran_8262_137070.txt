 12 farvari 2013, uttar korian media ne ghoshnaa kii ki deepiarake ne bhumigat parmaanu pareekshan kiya he jo ki 7 saalon main teesraa tha. 5.1Mw kaa ek kampan jo parmaanu pareekshan kaa sanket tha chini bhucamp network centre, sanyukt rajya bhugarbha surveykshanaur vyapak parmaanu pareekshan pratibandh sandhi sanghathan ke liye praarambhik ayog ke dwaara mapa gaya. jawaab main japan ne 12 farvari ko sanyukt raashtra kii ek apatkaaleen baithak bulaai or dakshin korea ne apni sena ko sachet avasthaa main kar diya. yah pataa nahin chalaa ki vah visfot ek parmaanu pareekshan tha yaa koi saamaanya bam visfot jise parmaanu pareekshan kii tarah dikhaaya gaya; kyonki do dinon baad chini, japani or dakshin korian visheshgy kisi bhi tarah kaa vikiran nahin pakad paae. 
 12 farvari 2013 ko yonhap ke mutabik uttar korian sainya kaman ke ek pravaktaa ne bataaya ki unhone apana teesraa safal parmaanu pareekshan kiya he. uttar korea ne yah bhi kaha ki usne adhik parmaanu shakti sampann ek chote bam kaa bhi safal pareekshan kiya he. 
 uttar korea kii pareekshanon kii ghoshnaa se pehle hi sanyukt rajya bhugarbha surveykshan or vyapak parmaanu pareekshan pratibandh sandhi sanghathan ke liye praarambhik ayog ne uttar korea main pichley parmaanu pareekshanon kii jagah, kilju county ke paas bhukampi gatividhiyaan maap leen thim. pehle yah bhucamp 4.9 ke pariman kaa mapa gaya baad main usgs ne ise uttar korea main sungajibegam ke 24 kilometre purv-uttarpurv ke paas 5.1 pariman kaa bhucamp bataaya. yah kampan subah 11:57 baje KST yaa ko darz kiya gaya or usgs ke anusaar jameen main iskaa bhugarbheey centre sirf 1 kilometre andar tha. chini bhucamp network centre ne bhi is ghatana kii rapat dete hue pariman ko 4.9 bataaya. pareekshan ke kaaran utpann hui tarangon ko paas ke shahar hunchun or antu county ke nivaasiyon ke dwaara bhi mahasoos kiya gaya. pareekshan sthal ke 80 kimi pashchim main sthit ryangang praant ke hyesaan shahar ke ek nivasee ne rapat di ki kai paanch - saat manjila imaaraten bahut teji se hilne lagi thim jinaki vajah se deevaaron main daraaren pads gayi. is visfot ko sitibitio ke 94 bhucamp maapi kemdron dwaara pakadaa gaya. baad main sitibitio radionuclide network ne parmaanu vikiran utpann karne vaali gaison kii maujuudagi darz kii jo parmaanu pareekshan hone kaa sanket thim. 
 dakshin korian raksha mantralay ne ghatana se 6–7 kiloton ke visfot hone kaa anumaan lagaaya, jise baad main sitibitio ke ganan tareekon se naapane ke baad 6–9 kiloton bataaya gaya. korea ke bhuvigyaan or khanij strot santhaan ne utsarjan ko 7.7–7.8 kiloton kaa bataaya.
 kuch visheshgyon ne visfot ke utsarjan ko 15 kiloton kaa bhi bataaya chunki pareekshan sthal kaa bhuvigyaan thik se samajha nahin ja sakaa tha. roosi raksha mantralay ne ise 7 kiloton se zyada bataaya jabaki roosi vigyaan academy kii bhugarbha seva ne ise 5.4 kiloton bataaya. norsar ne teenon parmaanu pareekshan sthalon ke bhugarbheey aankanon kaa adhdhayan karne ke baad ise 10 kiloton kaa bataaya. haalanki germany kii sangheey bhuvigyaan or prakritik sansaadhan sanstha ne is utsarjan ko 40 kiloton kaa bataaya.
 19 june 2013 ko china kaa vigyaan or takanic vishvavidhyalay ne ek rapat jari karte hue bataaya ki usne pareekshan sthal kaa thik-thik pataa akshansh 41°17′26.88″, deshaantar 129°4′34.68″ par maatr 94 meter kii truti sambhaavna ke saath 12.2 kito, visfot ke 3.8 kito kii truti sambhaavna ke saath kiya he.
 pratikriya main japan ke pradhanmantri shinjo abe ne sanyukt raashtra suraksha parishad kii ek apaat baithak bulaai. yah apaat baithak 12 farvari 2013 ko hui. sitibitio ke kaaryakari sachiv tibor toth ne pareekshan sthal ko 2006 or 2009 ke parmaanu pareekshan sthalon ke paas hone kii soochna kii pushti kii. japan kii kyodo news ne samachar diya ki japan ke raksha mantralay ne apatkaaleen aadesh jari kar hawaii jahaajon ko radio vikiran ke parinaamon kaa pataa lagane ke liye bula liya he. japan kii sarkaar ne tokyo main ek rashtriya suraksha baithak kii. dakshin korian sena ne bhi apana satarkta str badhaa diya. aam taur par korian parmaanu pareekshanon par antarrashtriya pratikriyaayein nakaaratmak hi thim.
