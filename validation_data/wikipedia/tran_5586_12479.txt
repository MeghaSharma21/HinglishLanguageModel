 california sanyukt rajya america ke pashchimi tat par sthit ek rajya he. yah america kaa sabase adhik aabaadi or kshetrafal main alaska or texs ke pashchaat teesraa sabase bada rajya hai. california ke uttar main aurigan, or dakshin main mexico he. california kii rajdhani secramento he. sanyukt rajya america kii arthvyavastha main yah rajya 14 pratishat kii hissedari rakhata he. agar california ek swatantra desh hota to duniya main uskaa sakal gharelu utpaada 6waan sthaan par hota or jansankhya 35wein sthaan par hoti.
 angrezi rajya kii adhikarik bhashaa he jo lagbhag 57% pratishat janata maatrubhaasha ke rup main bolti he. spani 29% pratishat, chini 2% or tagalog bhashaa 2% dwaara boli jaati he. los angelis sabase bada shahar he. 
 california shabd kaa pehla arth baha california prayadweep tha. baad main isko california, newada, uta, erizona, new mexico, or vioming ko milakar sambodhit karne ke liye prayog kiya jane laga.
