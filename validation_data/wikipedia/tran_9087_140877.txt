 
 frexinas ornas fexinas kii ek prajati he jo dakshini europe tathaa dakshin-pashchim asia main paidaa hoti he.
 yah paudha dakshin europe kaa deshaj he or oshadhi ke liye italy or visheshkar sicili main ugaaya jaataa he. greeshm ritu ke aarambh main isamen shwet pushp ke guchchhe nikal aate hai. is paudhe se kshiri naamak oshadhi praapt kii jaati he. jab paudha lagbhag aath varsh kaa evam uske tane kaa vyas kam se kam teen inch kaa ho jaataa he, tab july yaa agast main bhoomi ke uuparavaale tane kii chaal main keval ek ore prati din dedh se do inch lambii ek anuprastha kaat lagaai jaati he. prayek katan ek duusare se praaia: ek athwa do inch upar lagati he. in katano main se sharkarayukta srav nikalataa he jisko tane par hi sookhne diya jaataa he. isi ko kshiri kehete hai. katano main lakdi aadi ke tukade khons dene se unpar kshiri jam jaati he jo sabase uttam hoti he. isko maina aa kanolo kehete hai.
 kshiri, jal evam alcohal main ghul jaati he or iske dwaara chamkile samachatubhuj stambh or soochiyon ke rup main praapt hoti he. kshiri main 60 se 90 pratishat manitol, frexin naamak pratideept glucoseida, sharkaraayen or shleshm or rezin ityadi paae jate hai. yah halaki rechak oshadhi he. mithi hone ke kaaran bacchon ko julab ke liye bhi di jaati he. isaki sabase adhik khapat dakshini america main hoti he. anya paudhon ke ras se bhi kai prakaar kii kshiriyaan banaai jaati he, parantu unamem manitol nahin hota.
