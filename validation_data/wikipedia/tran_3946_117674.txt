 ronen patric jon keating ek irish gayak-githlekhak, sangeetkaar or televijan-radio vyaktitv hai. keating ne apni shuruyaat kith daphi, miki graham, shen lynch or stephen gatle ke saath 1994 main boyzone ke mukhya gayak ke rup main kii. unka ekal karier 1999 main shuru hua, jiske dauraan unhone 7 studio elbum pesh kiye. keating par vishwa samudaay kaa dhyaan us vakt gaya jab unka ekal "when you se nathing at ol" geet, notting hill film main prastut hua jo vibhinn chart par number ek par pahunchaa. 
 keating ne dharmarth prachaarak ke rup main kaam kiya he khaskar mairi keating foundation ke liye. yah sanstha jiskaa naam unki swargavaasi mata ke naam par rakhaa gaya he stan cancer ke liye logon main jaagrukta badhaati he. 
 ronen keating kaa janm 3 march 1977 ko hua tha. paanch bacchon main sabase chote, keating northsaide of dublin or westomith main paley-bade. unke pita geri keating ek texi driver the; or unki maa mairi nai thim. keating kii ek bahan thi: linda or teen bhai the: kaayran, jerard or gairi.
 jab stan cancer se unki maa kii mrityu ho gai to unka paariwarik awaas, jise keating ne atharah varsh kii umr main kharida tha, bik gaya, jisase unke pita beghar ho gaye or parinamswaroop pita or putr ke bich jhagada shuru ho gaya. do saal baad, bina samvaad ke donon ke bich sulah ho gai. keating ek trek or field khilaadi the or unhone kai toornament main ireland kaa netritv kiya, jiske tahat unhone ol ireland under-13 200m khitaab jeeta. apane bhai-bahanon ke sanyukt rajya america main utpravas karne ke samay, keating ne ek juute kii dukaan main ek sahaayak ke rup main bhi kaam kiya he. keating, keltic epasi ke samarthak hai or keltic or ireland ke striker robi keen ke mitra hai.
 keating, 1993 main boyzone band main shaamil ho gaye. ronen ko kith daphi, richard rock, shen lynch or stephen gatle ke saath boyzone ke sadsy ke rup main chunaa gaya. rock ne baad main band chhod diya or baad main unki jagah miki graham ne lee. is samooh ne chaar mausamon ke liye aap "dwaara pichhlaa samooh or vibhinn klbon or pabon main pradarshan kiya jiske baad poligram ne 1994 main unke saath anubandh kiya or unhone da four sizan ke "varking maai ve back tu you" ke ek cover sanskaran ko jari kiya.. is samooh ne "lav me for a rizan" kaa bhi ek cover sanskaran jari kiya, jo kai chart par chotii par pahunchaa. 1998 main, unki maa kaa 54 varsh kii aayu main stan cancer se nidhan ho gaya, jiske falswaroop mairi keating foundation kii sthapana hui, jo stan cancer ke liye jaagrukta badhaane vaali ek dharmarth sanstha he. keating ne april, 1998 main yuvan kaunoli ke saath vivaah kiya, is dampatti kii teen santaanen hai: jaik, mairi or ali . is danpati ne ghoshnaa kii ki unki shaadi 20 may 2010 ko samaapt ho gai he. 1997-1999 se, boyzone ne 2000 main vikhandit hone se pehle 3 studio elbum or 1 sankalan album jari kiya.
 1999 main, jabaki ve abhi bhi boyzone ke sadsy the, keating ne moshan film notting hill ke liye "when you se nathing at ol" ke ek sanskaran ko record kiya. yah ekal britain main chart par number ek par chadha or jo ek safal ekal carrier main falit hua. keating ne 2000 main apana pehla ekal album ronen jari kiya jo UK elbum chart par number ek par gaya.
 safal shuruuaati album ronen ke baad keating ne apane ekal karier ko aage badhaayaa or tab se unhone chaar anya studio album jari kiye hai: destination, turn it on, 10 phers of hit or bring you home, jinamein se chaar UK album chart par number ek par pahunche. keating ne in albamon ke liye pramukh sitaron ke saath jugalbandi kii he jisamen shaamil hai alton jon, lulu, bi gis or lean rimes. 
 keating or paul brady ne 2001 ke hit ekal "da laung gudby" ko ek saath likhaa, yah geet keating ke prashansakon ke bich sabase adhik pasandida safal geeton main se ek he or sanyukt rajya america main brookes and dun ke liye ek bada hit tha. keating or brady ne ekal ke liye "biemai europea geet lekhan puraskaar" jeeta. 2003 main, keating ko rier of da ear namit kiya gaya - aisa puraskaar jise us pramukh hasti ko diya jaataa he jiskaa pasch ullekhneey hota he. oktober 2007 main, keating ko aise ekmaatr kalakaar ke rup main gineez book of world records main soochibaddh kiya gaya jiske geeton ne UK chart par sheersh 10 ekal main lagaataar 30 baar sthaan banaaya or unhone is maamle main elvis presli ko peeche chhod diya. keating kii sahabhaagita do vishwa daure, ek iver novello or biemai githlekhan puraskaar, ek aatmakatha, isai sahaayta ke liye ek rajdoot kii bhoomika or vishwa bhar main 22 milian album se adhik bechney main rahi he.
 keating ne kareeb 250,000 darshakon ke saamne, 2006 fifa vishwa cap ke udghaatan samaaroh par berlin, germany main brandenberg get par pradarshan kiya or cosmopolitan patrika ke 10 phers of neced centerfold ke liye nagn prastuti di. 9 may 2007 ko, keating nom penh, cambodia main kaarykram pesh karne vaale pehle sheersh bikri vaale antarrashtriya videshi kalakaar ban gaye. keating ne olympic stadium indor arena par bhi pradarshan kiya. keating ne new york ke medisson garden main alton jon ke saath yugalabandi kii or pope ke liye do baar gaaya he or princes trust ke 30veen varshganth par prince charles ke liye pradarshan kiya, jo tower of landan par sampann hua. unhone, crown princes victoria ke janmdin samaaroh main sweden ke shahi parivaar ke liye manch par pradarshan kiya or gotaborg main 19wein europea athletics championships ke udghaatan samaaroh main apni prastuti di.
 5 navambar 2007 ko, keating ne pushti kii ki boyzone bibisi ke vaarshik nidhiyan, children in nid par vishesh upasthiti ke liye dobaaraa ekajute hoga or safal geeton kii madli prastut karegaa, haalanki unhone kisi nae daure yaa nae album kii sambhaavna par abhi koi tippanee nahin kii he. jald hi, boyzone 29-date tour par nikal gaya. 29-date tour kai shaharon se guzara jaise O2 arena main cardiff, newcastle, liverpool, landan or wembali, menchester, birmingham, glassgo, aberdeen, shefield, newcastle, nottingham, edinburgh kaisal or dublin main RDS. stephen gatele jo keating ke ek abhinn mitra the, unka phuffusiya shof, yaa fefdon main taral bharne ke kaaran 10 oktober 2009 ko nidhan ho gaya. isse keating tut gaye. 2009 main keating ne niles brink dwaara gaae gaye "bilive agen" kaa sah-lekhan kiya, jisane danks melody grand pricks jeeta or moscow main eurovision sangeet pratiyogita 2009 main denmark kaa pratinidhitiv kiya jahaan isane dwitiya semifinal se final ke liye kwalifai kiya or 74 ankon ke saath 13wein sthaan par rahaa. ronen keating ke album saungs for maai madar kii safalataa ke baad, unhone winter saungs ke naam se ek anya album record kiya. saungs for maai madar ke nirmaataa steve lipson ke saath ek baar phir haath milaate hue keating ne ek saath gyaarah gane pesh kiye jo unhen sardiyon or christmas kii yaad dilaate the. winter saungs paaramparik or aadhunik utsav clasik kaa ek mishran he, jisamen simon and garphuncel, kaa joni mishael or sugarland jaise kalakaaron ke trek shaamil the. inke alawa, unhone maarmik christmas bhajan "silent nite" or "haev yoraself a meri little christmas" ko bhi shaamil kiya. is album main do bilkul nae gane bhi shaamil hai: its onli christmas or skirs .
 oktober 2005 main, chrishchiyan aid or 2004 main trade justise abhiyaan main shaamil hone ke fouran baad, keating rome chale gaye jahaan unako khaadya or krishi sanghathan ke madhyam se sanyukt raashtra kaa sadbhaavna rajdoot niyukt kiya gaya. 
 keating ne cancer research ke liye dhan jutaney ke liye 13 april 2008 ko 3hr 59min ke apane sarvshereshth nijee samay ke saath landan marathon main daud lagaai. unhone ireland kii lambaai ke baraabar bhi do baar pad yatra kii he or har baar mairi keating foundation ke liye dhan jutaya he, jo ab britain main cancer research se judaa he. prayek varsh yah sanstha dharmarth nrity or vibhinn kaaryakramon ko yojit karti he, taaki unki chal ikaayiyon ko sadak par banaae rakhane ke liye dhan jutaya ja sake. 
 2009 main, keating ne comic relif ke liye dhan jutaney kii khatir mount kilimanjaro par chadhaai kii; unke saath the gairi barlo, kris moyals, ben shefrd, kimbrali walsh, denis vine outen, phern coton, alesha dikson or cheril kol.
 june 2009 main, keating ko cancer research UK se ek vishesh uplabdhi puraskaar praapt hua - unhen 2006 se un logon ke saath unke kaaryon ke liye chunaa gaya tha. unhone, mairi keating charity ke saath milakar britain main dharmarth kaaryon ke liye Â£1.7 milian jutaya he. is paise kaa istemaal teen vishesh chal ikaayiyon ko aarthik sahaayta dene ke liye hota he jo puure britain bhar main jaagrukta badhaane kaa kaam karti hai. 
 oktober 2009 main, unhone apane band mitra stephen gatele kii asamayik mrityu ke kaaran chicago marathon se apana naam waapas le liya; ve is daud main apni maa kii smriti main daudane vaale the.
 kai varshon ke dauraan unhone smash hits ke sarvshereshth purush ekal kaa puraskaar jeeta he. princes trust ke liye pradarshan karne ke liye unhen landan ke hide park main ek ped diya gaya. unke paas, sarvshereshth bikri vaale irish kalakaar hone kaa world music award bhi he. 
 3 may 1997 ko, keating ne kairi krole ke saath eurovision geet pratiyogita prastut kii. unhone 1997 or 1999 main MTV europe music award prastut kiya or 2002 main mandi moore ke saath MTV asia award pastut kiya. unhone miss world 1998 kii bhi mejabani kii.
 20 april 2009 ko keating ne australia main keli gileas ke saath ek sah atithi ke rup main da morning sho kii mejabani kii, jabaki is sho ke niyamit purush mejabaan laire emder chuttee par the.iske alaava, ve bibisi radio 2 par steve rhite ke kaarykram sande lav saungs main niyamit pratisthaapak kaa kaam karte hai. ve vartmaan main magic 105.4 FM. par ravivaariya anurodh kaarykram prastut karte hai.
 jeremi clarkson 2009 dividi duel ke liye ek khand main, keating ko kachi sadak kii ek res main hissaa lene ke liye aamantrit kiya gaya taaki "kachi sadak ke sabase tej celibrity" kaa faisla ho sake, jisamen unke saath the samachar vaachak ket silverton, rugby khilaadi mat dowsan or clarkson. ve samgr rup se tisare sthaan par aae, kyonki unhen car main rakhe time bam ko hataane ke liye course ko puraa karne ke liye shesh bache 20 second se pehle hi majabuuran car chhodana padaa. 
 keating, 2010 main seven network par da X factor ke australian sanskaran par nirnaayak rahenge.
 keating ne haal hi main kaha he ki ve abhinay main khud ko aajmaana chaahate hai, lekin ek recording kalakaar ke rup main kaam band karne kaa unka iraadaa nahin he. haal hi main unhone aane vaali fantasy film "da hobit" main ek bhoomika ke liye audishan diya he. yah film jearar tokin ke isi naam ke upanyaas par aadhaarit he.
 jab keating ne ghoshnaa kii ki ve boyzone se ek avkaash lenaa chaahate hai to lui walsh ne unke karier ko prabandhit rakhana jari rakhaa. keating ne 2000 main "life iz a rolercoster" ke saath number ek hit hasil kiya jab unke album kii 4.4 milian pratiyaan bikin. keating or walsh ne baad main khud ko alag kar lene kaa faisla kiya jiske baad unke bich gahare matbhed ubhar aae. walsh ne press se kaha: " sarvaadhik pratibhashali nahin tha - vah ek mahaan gayak nahin he or uskaa koi vyaktitv nahin he." keating ne baad main closer patrika ko bataaya: "us aadmi ne mujhe bilkul barbaad karne kii koshish kii or agar vah sochataa he ki ham kabhi use gale laga kar sulah kar lenge to bhul jaae. pichley teen saal main meri usase koi baat nahin hui he or mujhe koi samasya hogi agar main phir usase kabhi naa miluun. vah ek acha vyakti nahin he."
 donon vyaktiyon ke bich vivaad jahiraa taur par march 2008 ko samaapt ho gaya or donon ne britain ke ek tivi kaarykram da X factor ke samooh shrenee main antim pratibhaagiyon ke chayan main madad kii. 
 keating ne 2008 ke uttarardh or 2009 ke purvardh main radiohed sahyogi thom yorke kii press main kai baar aalochana kii he or ek hotel main yorke dwaara kathith taur par nazarandaz kiye jane ke kaaran, keating ne unhen "naatakabaaz" or "bevakoof" qaraar diya. keating kii tippanee ke sheeghra baad, kanye west or mili sirus ne bhi yorke kii kathith ashishtataa ke bare main shikaayat kii. aaropon ke jawaab main, yorke ne radio head kii aadhikarik sait par un logon ke liye ek blog likhaa jo un logon main se nahin the "jinka apmaan maine bina kuch kiye kiya."
