 prabandh, madhyakaalin sanskrit sahitya kii ek vidha thi. inamen prasiddh vyaktiyon ke jivan ke ardh-aitihaasik vivaran hai. inaki rachana 13 shatabdi ke baad ke kaal main mukhyatah gujarat or malwa ke jain vidwaanon ne kiya. prabandhon kii bhashaa bolchaal kii sanskrit he jisamen sthaneey boliyon kaa bhi put he.
