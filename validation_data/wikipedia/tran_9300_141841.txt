 prakaash yashavanth ambedkar, balasahab ambedkar naam se lokapriy, ek bhartiya raajnneetijna, saamaajik kaaryakarta or vakil hai. ve bharip bahujan mahasang ke sansthaapak evam rashtriya adhyaksh hai. ve sansad ke donon sadan rajyasabha va loksabhaa ke saansad rah chuke hai. ve bharat ratna dow bhimrao ambedkar ke pautra hai.
 prakaash ambedkar kaa janm 10 may 1954 ko mumbai main hua tha. unke pita kaa naam yashavanth ambedkar or mata kaa naam mirabai ambedkar hai. prakaash ambedkar apane dada, bhartiya samvidhan ke janak ke rup main jane jate dow bhimrao ambedkar dwaara namit kiya gaya tha; prakaash ambedkar ne saint stenisalaos hai skool or siddharth college, mumbai bharat main shiksha lee. unhone 1982 ke baad se puure bharat main kai saamaajik, dharmik evam rajnatik andolanon kaa netritv kiya. unke chote bhai aanandaraj ambedkar republican sena ke netaa hai.
