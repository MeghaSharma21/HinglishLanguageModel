 china main kai aitihaasik rajvansh rahe hai. kabhi-kabhi inke varnanon main aisa prathit hota he ke china main ek rajvansh swayan hi samaapt ho gaya or nae rajvansh ne aage badhkar shashan kii bagdor sambhaal lee. vastav main aisa nahin tha. koi bhi rajvansh swechha se khatm nahin hua. aksar aisa hota tha ke nayaa rajvansh shuru to ho jaataa tha lekin vah kuch arase tak kam prabhaav rakhata tha or pehle se sthapit rajvansh se ladaaiyaan karata tha. aisa bhi hota tha ke koi parajeet rajvansh haarane ke baavjood kuch ilaaqon main prabhutv rakhata tha or china kaa singhaasana waapas chhinane kii koshish main juta rahata tha. 
 udahaaran ke liye san 1644 main manchu nasl vaale ching rajvansh ne beijing par qabza jamaa liya or china ko apane adheen kar liya. lekin ching rajvansh san 1636 main hi shuru ho chukaa tha or us se bhi pehle san 1616 main ek anya naam ke naam se astitv main aa chukaa tha. ming rajvansh beijing kii rajasatta se to 1644 main haath dho baitha, lekin unke vanshaj 1662 tak singhaasana par apana adhikaar jatalate rahe or use waapas lene ke prayaas karte rahe.
