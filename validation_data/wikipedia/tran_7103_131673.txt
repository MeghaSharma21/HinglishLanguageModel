 dindori yaa dindori bharat ke madhya pradesh rajya kaa ek jila hai.dindori jila madhya pradesh ke purvi bhaag main chhattisgarh rajya kii seemaa se laga hua hai. dindori jile kii seemaa purv main shadol, pashchim main mandla, uttar main umariya or dakshin main chhattisgarh rajya ke bilaspur se lagi hui he. yah jabalpur se 144 kimi kii duuri par he. dindori kii mandla se duuri 104 tathaa amarkanta kii duuri 88 kimi he. yah 81.34 degree deshaant tathaa 21.16 degree akshansh main sthit he. isaki samudr tal se oonchai 1100 me. he. yah maikaal parvat maala par he. dindori main kai aitihaasik sthaan hai. inamen lakshmana modev, kukramath, kalchuri kaali mandir aadi prasiddh hai. jila mukhyalay se kanha tiger rashtriya udyaan 140 kimi tathaa bandhavgarh rashtriya udyaan 140 kimi kii duuri par sthit he. is jile kaa gathan 25 may 1998 ko kiya gaya. isamen 927 gaanv shaamil hai. jile main 7 vikaskhand dindori, shahpura, mehandwani, amarpur, bajaang, karanjia or samanpur he. jile ke 927 gaanvon main se 899 gaanvon main baigaa janajati ke log nivas karte hai.
