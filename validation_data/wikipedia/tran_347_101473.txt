 tharu, nepal or bharat ke simavarti tarai kshetra main paayi jane vaali ek janajati he. nepal kii sakal jansankhya kaa lagbhag 6.6% log tharu hai. bharat main bihar ke champaran jile main or uttrakhand ke nainital or udham singh nagar main tharu paye jate hai. tharuon kaa mukhya nivas sthaan jalodh mitti vaala himalay kaa sampurna upaparvatiy bhaag tathaa uttar pradesh ke uttari jile vaala tarai pradesh he. ye hindu dharm maanate hai tathaa hinduon ke sabhi tyohaar manaate hai. 
 tharu shabd kii utpatti praaia: "tehre", "tarhuva", "thithurava" tathaa "athvaru" aadi shabdon main khoji gai he. ye swayan apane ko mulat: sisodiya vansheey rajput kehete hai. tharuon ke kuch vanshagat oopaadhiyaan hai: rana, kathariya, choudhary. kuch samay purv tak tharu apana vanshanukram mahilaon kii ore se khojate the. tharuon ke shaareerik lakshan prajateey mishran ke dyotak hai. inamen mongoleey tatwon kii pradhanta hote hue bhi anya bharatiyon se samya ke lakshan paae jate hai. akhet, machhlee maarna, pashupaalan evam krishi inke jeevanyaapan ke pramukh saadhan hai. tokari tathaa rassi bunana sahaayak dhandhon main hai.
 turnar ke mat se vigat tharu samaaj do ardhanshon main banta tha jinamein se prayek ke chah gotra hote the. donon ardhanshon main pehle to unche ardhanshon main neeche ardhansh kii kanya kaa vivaah sambhav tha par dhire-dhire donon ardhansh antarvivaahi ho gaye. "kaaj" or "dola" arthath vadhoomulya or kanyapharan paddati se vivaah ke sthaan par ab tharuon main bhi saanskarik vivaah hone lage hai. vidhwaa dwaara devar se yaa anya avivaahit purush se vivaah inke samaaj main maanya he. apane gotra main bhi yah vivaah kar lete hai. tharu sagaai ko "dikhnauri" tathaa gaune kii rasm ko "chaalaa" kehete hai. inamen naatedari kaa vyavhaar simaaon main baddh hota he. purush kaa saale-saaliyon se madhur sambandh hamen inke loksahitya main dekhne ko miltaa he. devar-bhabhi kaa swachand vyavhaar bhi inke yahan sweekrut he.
 tharu samaaj main stri ke vishisht pad kii ore praaia: sabhi nritatvavettaon kaa dhyaan gaya he. inamen stri ko sampatti par vishesh adhikaar hota he. dharmik anushthanon ke atirikt anya sabhi gharelu kaamkaajon ko tharu stri hi sambhalati he.
