 amravati express bharat sarkaar dwaara shuru kii gayi do train sevaaon kaa naam he jinke anukramank alag alag hai, donon kaa sanchaalan ekadam alag rup se hota he, inkaa kshetra bhi bahut alag he par donon kaa naam samaan he.
 is anukramank main nimna trainen aati hai-
 in trainon kii seva saptaah main teen baar pradaan kii jaati he arthath yah train donon hi ore se apane gantavy ke liye saptaah main teen baar nikalati he. in trainon kaa parichaalan dakshin madhya railway ke vejavada arthath vijaybada namak sthaan se goa tak andhra pradesh se hote hue jaati he.
 in do sevaaon ko bhi amravati express ke naam se hi janaa jaataa he-
 is train kii seva apane donon srotra stesan se apane gantavy ke liye saptaah main chaar baar pradaan kii jaati he. iskaa parichaalan vijaybada, guntakaal, hubli evam madagaanv se hote hue hota he. yah seva dakshini purvi railway ke khargpur naamak sthaan dwaara pradaan kii jaati he. iske aas paas ke kshetra guntur, narsarapet, kambam, giddaluru, nandyaal, mahanandi, guntukaal evam bellari aadi hai.
 amravati express machhilipatanam evam mormugo railway trek par chalati he. is seva kii shuruyaat sabase pehle san 1950 ke lagbhag guntur evam hubli ke bich meter gez par kii gayi thi. 1987-1990 ke bich main guntur â€“ hubli fast paisanger seva ko apgrade karke express seva main parivartit kar diya gaya tathaa iskaa naam amravati express kar diya gaya. san 1994 main is train kii seva ko badhaakar vijaybada tak kar diya gaya.
 san 2000 ke prarambh ke saath, jaise jaise gez parivartan hota gaya, in train sevaaon ko badhaakar londa, kesal rock tathaa baad main vasco da gamma tak kar diya gaya. baad main san 2000 ke madhya main train kaa sanchaalan ek dainik train ke rup main honen laga baad main yatriyon kii bahut hi karaab sthiti kii vajah se ise saptaah main do baar chalaaya jane laga. 2003 kii shuruyaat se ise badhaakar saptaah main teen baar kar diya gaya tathaa vijaybada evam hubli seva ko saptaah main bache hue chaar din chalaaya jane laga.
 san 2010 main in donon hi trainon ko alag alag naam de die gaye evam 12 farbari 2013 ko rail mantri malikarjun karge nen hubli tathaa vijaybada ke bich chalane vaali tridivsiy train ke sanchaalan diwason kii sankhya badhaakar ise dainik kar diya.
 is train kaa naam satvaahan rajya kii aitihaasik rajdhani amravati ke naam par rakhaa gaya he jo kii vartmaan samay main guntur jile main he. amravati ko apane stupon ke kaaran dakshin bharat ke sanchi ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he.
