 raasalila yaa krishnalila main yuva or balak krishna kii gatividhiyon kaa manchan hota he. krishna kii manamohak adaaon par gopiyaan yani brijabalaayein lattu thim. kanha kii murali kaa jaadu aisa tha ki gopiyaan apni sutabut ganvaa baithti thim. gopiyon ke madahosh hote hi shuru hoti thi kanha ke mitron kii sharaaraten. maakhan churaana, matki phodna, gopiyon ke vastra churaana, jaanwaron ko charne ke liye gaanv se dur-dur chhod kar aana hi pramukh sharaaraten thi, jin par puraa vrindavan mohit tha. janmashtami ke maukey par kanha kii in sari athkheliyon ko ek dhaage main pirokar yani unako naatakeey rup dekar raasalila yaa krishna lila kheli jaati he. isiilie janmashtami kii taiyaariyon main shrikrishna kii raasalila kaa anand mathura, vrundavan tak simit na rah kar puure desh main chaa jaataa he. jagah-jagah raasalilaon kaa manchan hota he, jinamein saje-dhaje shri krishna ko alag-alag rup rakhkar radha ke prati apane prem ko vyakt karte dikhaaya jaataa he. in raas-lilaon ko dekh darshakon ko aisa lagata he maano ve asaliyat main shrikrishna ke yug main pahunch gaye hon.
