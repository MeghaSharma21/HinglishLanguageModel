 
 ilaychi kaa seven aamtaur par mukhashuddhi ke liye athwa masaale ke rup main kiya jaataa he. yah do prakaar kii aati he- hari yaa choti ilaychi tathaa badi ilaychi. jahaan badi ilaychi vyanjanon ko lajij banane ke liye ek masaale ke rup main prayukt hoti he, vahiin hari ilaychi mithaiyon kii khushboo badhaati he. mehmaanon kii aavbhagat main bhi ilaychi kaa istemaal hota he. lekin isaki mahatta keval yaheen tak simit nahin he. yah aushadhiy gunon kii khan he. sanskrit main ise ela kaha jaataa he.
 choti ilaychi ko sanskrit main 'ela', 'tikshngandha' ityadi or latin main aleteria cardamomum kehete hai. bharat main iske bijon kaa upyog atithisatkar, mukhashuddhi tathaa pakwaanon ko sugandhit karne ke liye hota he. ye pachanavardhak tathaa ruchivardhak hote hai. ayurvedic matanusaar ilachai sheethal, tikshn, mukh ko shuddh karanevaali, pittajnak tathaa vaat, shwaas, khaansi, bavasir, kshay, vastirog, sujak, pathari, khujali, mutrakruchh tathaa hridayrog main laabhadaayak he.
 ilaychi ke bijon main ek prakaar kaa udansheel tel hota he.
 choti ilaychi kaa paudha sadaa haraa tathaa paanch foot se 10 foot tak unchaa hota he. iske pattey barche kii akruti ke tathaa do foot tak lambey hote hai. yah beej or jad donon se ugta he. teen chaar varsh main fasal taiyaar hoti he tathaa itane hi kaal tak isamen gucchhon ke rup main fal lagate hai. sookhe fal bazaar main 'choti ilaychi' ke naam se bikate hai. paudhe kaa jivakaal 10 se lekar 12 varsh tak kaa hota he. samudr kii hawa or chhayaadaar bhoomi iske liye aavashyak hai. iske beej chote or konedaar hote hai. mysore, mangalore, malabar tathaa shri lanka main ilaychi bahutayat se hoti he.
 ilaychi kii jo do pramukh prajatiyaan hai unka vitaran is prakaar he:-
 
 ilaychi kaa fal evam beej
 hari ilaychi kii fali evam beej
 hari ilaychi se bharaa martabaan
 
 
 ilaychi ke swasthya laabh
