 khokand madhya asia main purvi uzbekistan ke faraghna praant main sthit ek shahar he. yah faraghna vaadi ke dakshin-pashchimi chor par qaayam he. khokand ubekistan kii rajdhani taashkand se 228 kimi dakshin-purv main, andithan shahar se 115 kimi pashchim main or faraghna shahar se 88 kimi pashchim main sthit he. ise kabhi-kabhi 'hawaaon kaa shahar' yaa 'junglee boar kaa shahar' bhi bulaayaa jaataa he. khokand kuch bahut hi aitihaasik or mahatvapurn vyaper margon par sthit he. ek to yahan se pahaado se hota hua pashchimottar main taashkand ko jaataa he or doosraa pashchim main khujand kii ore. faraghna vaadi main yatayaat kaa yah ek mukhya padaav he. san 1999 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 1,92,500 anumaanit kii gai thi.
