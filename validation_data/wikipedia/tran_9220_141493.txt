 barguzin nadi rus ke siberia kshetra ke buratiya ganatantr kii ek pramukh nadi he. yah 480 kimi lambi nadi baykaal jhil ke barguzin naamak bhaag main khaali ho jaati he. selenga nadi or upree angaara nadi ke baad baykaal jhil main jal le jane vaali yah teesri sabase badi nadi he. iskaa jalsambhar kshetra 21,000 varg kimi he or baykaal se lekar us se 204 kimi upar tak is nadi par navi yatayaat chalata he. isaki mukhya upanadiyaan gagra, argada, inaa or ulyun nadiyaan hai.
