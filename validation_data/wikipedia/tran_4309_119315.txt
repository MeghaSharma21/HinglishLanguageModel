 18 june ko prati varsh goa kranti diwas ke rup main manaaya jaataa he. kyonki 18-06-1946 ko doctor ram manohar lohia ne goa ke logon ko portugaliyon ke kilaaf aawaaj uthaane ke liye prerit kiya. 18 june goa kii aazaadee kii ladai ke itihaas main swarna aksharon se likhaa gaya he. 18 june 1946 ko doctor ram manohar lohia ji ne goa ke logon ko ekajute hone or portugali shashan ke kilaaf ladane kaa sandesh diya tha. 18 june ko hui is kranti ke joshilay bhaashan ne aazaadee kii ladaai ko majboot kiya or aage badhaaya.
 goa kii mukti ke liye ek lambaa aandolan chalaa. antatah19 disambar 1961 ko bhartiya sena ne yahan akraman kar is kshetra ko portugali aadhipaty se mukt karavaaya or goa ko bharat main shaamil kar liya gaya.
