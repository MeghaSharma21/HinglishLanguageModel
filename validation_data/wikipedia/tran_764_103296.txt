 purananusaar dason dishaao kaa paalan karanevaala devtaa. yatha-purv ke indra, agnikon ke vahri, dakshin ke yama, nairitykon ke nairut, pashchim ke varuna, vaayu kone ke marut, uttar ke kuber, ishan kone ke ish, urdhv disha ke brahma or adho disha ke anant.
 dikpala kii sankhya 10 maani gai he. varah puraana ke anusaar inaki utpatti kii katha is prakaar he. jis samay brahma sristi karne ke vichaar main chintanarat the us samay unke kaan se das kanyaayein - 
 utpann huin jinamein mukhya 6 or 4 gaun thim. un logon ne brahma kaa naman kar unase rahane kaa sthaan or upyukt patiyon kii yachana kii. brahma ne kaha tum logon kaa jis ore jane kii ichha ho ja sakati ho. sheeghra hi tum logon ko anuroop pati bhi doongaa. iske anusaar un kanyaon ne ek ek disha kii ore prasthaan kiya. iske pashchaat brahma ne aath digpalon kii sristi kii or apni kanyaon ko bulaakar prayek lokapal ko ek ek kanya pradaan kar di. iske baad ve sabhi lokapal un kanyaon main dishaao ke saath apni dishaao main chale gaye. in digpalon ke naam puraanon main dishaao ke kram se nimnaankit he
