 poornima shreshth ek prasiddh bollywood parshv gayika he jinhone bhartiya sangeet udyogon main kai superhit gane gaae hai. unhen sushamaa shreshth naam se adhik janaa jaataa he. sushamaa ne ramesh sippi kii 1971 kii film andaaz main ek bal gayika ke rup main apana kariyar shuru kiya. us film main, unhone shankar jaykishan ke sangeet nirdesh main "he naa bolo bolo" geet mohammad rafee, suman kalyanpur or pratibha ke saath gaaya. unhen is samay zyaadaatar unke geeton "tera mujhase he pehle" aa gale lag ja se or "kya hua tera vaada" ham kisi se kam nahin se ke liye yaad kiya jaataa he. donon ke liye unhen filmafere sarvshereshth parshv gayika puraskaar main namankit kiya gaya. sangeetkaar jodi anand-milind 90 ke dashak kii shuruyaat main unki awaz ke bahut kaayal the or unhone unhen apane geet gane ke kai maukey die. poornima ne david dhawan kii filmon kii shrinkhalaa main kai lokapriy geet gaae hai, jaise ki kuli nan 1, judwa, hero nan 1 or bivi nan 1 . vah mul rup se nepal se hai.
