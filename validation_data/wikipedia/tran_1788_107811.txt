 check ganraajya ke prag skool of langwejez ke shaam ke samay padhaaye jane vaale paathyakram main hindi bhashaa bhi padhai jaati he. hindi bhashaa padhne is desh ke yuvakon kaa utsaah chaatro kii acchhee-khaasi sankhya se lagaaya ja saktaa he.
 duusare vishwa yuddh ke samay praha skool of kamrs kii ek shakha institute of di hindustani language thi. us samay lagbhag sau vidhyaarthee vahaan pard rahe the. profesar oktaar pertold jo kisi samay checkoslovakia ke mumbai main mahavanijyadoot rah chuke the, ne hindustani bhashaa ke liye ke safal paathya pustak likhi thi jisamen devanagari or nastalik dono lipiyaan chaatro ko padhai jaati thi.
 bharat kii swatantraaa ke pashchaat hindi bhashaa kii shiksha checkoslovakia main mukhya centre rahi, jabaki urdu iske saath ek atirikt aichhik vishay ke taur par padhai jane lagi. 1980 ke dashak main urdu shiksha ko hindi ke paathyakram se alag kiya gaya tha.
 check ganraajya ke charles vishvavidhyalay ke us vibhaag main jahaan hindi padhai ja rahi he, bhartiya duutavas kaa samarthan praapt he. is vibhaag ko samay-samay par hindi pustak or patrikas pahunchaai jaati hai.
 iske atirikt duutavas samay-samay par hindi bhashaa seekhney ke icchhuk chaatro ke bharat bhejne kaa prabandh karata he. 2015 main paradubitse vishvavidhyalay kii ek chaatra ne duutavas ke saujany se kendriya hindi santhaan, agra main avedaan bhej chuki hai. santhaan kii ore se is chaatra kaa vahaan par ek varsh ke course ke liye chayan kiya gaya tha. yah chaatra pehle se kuch hindi jaanati he, par vah bharat main rahakar or seekhney kii icchhuk thi.
