 varli kila mumbai main varli main british dwaara banaaya gaya tha. kaafii samay tak ise portugaliyon  nirmaan mana jaataa rahaa tha, parantu yah kila asal main british ne 1675 main banaaya tha. yah kila varli pahari par bana tha, mahim kii khadi par najar rakhane ke liye, jab mumbai shahar sirf saat dwipon se bana tha. yah dushman ke jahaajon or daakuon par najar rakhane ke liye banaaya tha.
