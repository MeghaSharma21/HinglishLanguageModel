 abdulla ebn al-jubayr yaa ebn jubayre ek arab sahabi the, or jinaki maa asamaa bin abi thi. vah islam ke paigambar hazarat mohammad sahaab kii patni bibi aaisha ke bhattije the. abd-allaah ebn al-jubayr hijra ke baad madina main paidaa hone vaala pehle muslim the.. hazarat al-jubayre ne khalipha kaa khitaab jeeta or sattarudh umayyad kilaafat ke khilaaf lagbhag ek dashak tak vidroh kiya. general al-hajaj ebn yusuf dwaara chah maheene kii gheraabandi ke baad 692 isvi main makka main hazarat ebn jubayra ko shahid diya gaya tha..
