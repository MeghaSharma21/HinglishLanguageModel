 pandit brijbhushan kabra bhartiya sangitajna hai. ye jodhpur ke ek prakhyat sangitajna parivaar main janmen the. inke pita ne prasiddh sitarvadak ali akbar khan ke pita master inayat khan se sitar siikhaa tha. shastreey sangeet main balykaal main ruchi naa hone par bhi inhen apane pita or bhrata se sangeet kaa saannnidhya mila. inke bhrata ali akbar khan ke shishy the, jinke sath pandit ji kai baar unke kaarykram dekhne gayi the. 
 inhone hindustani shastreey sangeet main hawian guitar kaa prayog aarambh kiya.
