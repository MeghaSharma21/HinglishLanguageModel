 hamun iran ke pathaar par mausami taur par chaand maheenon ke liye ban jane vaali jhiilon or daldalon ko kaha jaataa he. yah iran or afghanistan main or pakistan ke pashchimi balochistan praant main ban jate hai. yah hamun kshetreey jaanwaron, machhliyon or pakshiyon ke jeevankram main bahut hi mahatvapurn jagah rakhte hai. jab aam-taur par shushk registan main yah hamun banate hai to kuch arase ke liye vahaan ek nachlistaan kaa vaataavaran ban jaataa he.
