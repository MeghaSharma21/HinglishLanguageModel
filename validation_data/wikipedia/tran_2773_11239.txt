 jaivik nrivigyaan nrivigyaan kii pratham evam sarvapramukh shakha he. yah maanav kaa ek jaivik praani ke rup main     adhyayan karata he. yah maanav kii utpatti, udvikas evam vividh maanav samoohon ke bich maujuud prajateey vividhta kaa adhyayan karata he.
 maanav kii utpatti evam uske udvikas kaa adhyayan jeevashmiya saakshyon ke aadhaar par kiya jaataa he. iske saath hi primet ke maanviya gunon kaa pataa lagaakar maanav ke purvajon ke bare main anumaan lagaaya jaataa he. iske atirikt adikalin jalwaayu, paudhon evam jantuon ke adhyayan se praapt jaankaree se bhi maanav udvikas ke bare main jaanane kaa prayaas kiya jaataa he. maanav kii prajateey vividhta ke adhyayan ke liye maanavvigyaani maanav kii un shaareerik vishistataaon kaa adhyayan karte hai jo anuvanshik hote hai. yah adhyayan prajateey vargeekaran main sahaayak hote hai.
 is shakha kaa sambandh aadi maanvon or maanav ke purvajon kii bhautik yaa jaev visheshtaon tathaa maanav jaise anya jeevon, jaise chimpanji, gorilla or bandaron se samaantaaon se he. yah shakha vikaas shrunkhlaa ke jariye saamaajik reeti rivaajon ko samajhane kaa prayaas karti he. yah jatiyon ke bich bhautik antaron kii pahchaan karti he or is baat kaa bhi pataa lagaati he ki vibhinn prajatiyon ne kis tarah apane aap ko shaareerik rup se parivesh ke anuroop dhaala. isamen yah bhi adhyayan kiya jaataa he ki vibhinn pariveshon kaa unpar kya asar padaa.
 jaev yaa bhautik nritatv vigyaan kii anya up shakhaaen or vibhaag bhi hai jinamein or bhi adhik visheshgyata hasil kii ja sakati he. inamen aadi maanav jeev vigyaan, osteolaji, palioenthropolaji yani pura nritatv vigyaan or forencik anthropolaji.
 maanavvigyaan kii is shakha kaa vyaavhaarik jivan main upyog bad rahaa he, jaise- aapraadhik guttheon ko sulajhane main, saarvajanik swasthya ko sudhaarney main, pitrutv sambandhi vivaado ke samaadhaan main, sharir par feet aanevaale vastron va anya sajo-saamaan ke nirmaan main aadi.
