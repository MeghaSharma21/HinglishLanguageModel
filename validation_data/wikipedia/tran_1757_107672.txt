 parantha bhartiya roti kaa vishisht rup he. yah uttar bharat main jitna lokapriy he, lagbhag utana hi dakshin bharat main bhi he, bas mul fark ye hai, ki jahaan uttar main aate kaa banataa he, vahiin dakshin main maide kaa banataa he. pratidin ke uttari bhartiya upmahadwipiya naashte main sabase lokapriy padaarth agar koi he to vah parantha hi he. ise banane kii jitani vidhiyaan hai vaise hi hindi main iske kai rup prachlit hai jaise paratha, parautha, paravatha, parantha or paraanwatha. uttar se dakshin or purab se pashchim tak yah bhartiya rasoi kaa hissaa he or har subah tave par senke jate paranthe kii lubhaavni khushboo bhukh badha detii he. haa swasthya kii drushti se ye avashya vasa se bharpoor hone ke kaaran simit maatra main hi upbhog kiye jane chaiye.
 parantha lagbhag roti kii tarah hi banaaya jaataa he, fark sirf isaki sinkai kaa he. roti ko jahaan tave par senkane ke baad sidhe aanch par bhi phulayaa jaataa he vahiin parantha sirf tave par hi senka jaataa he. roti ko banane ke baad upar se shuddh ghi lagaaya ja saktaa he, vahiin paranthe ko tave par senkate samay hi ghi yaa tel laga kar senka jaataa he. bharwaan parantha banane ke liye aataa yaa maida mel kar uski loi bel kar usamen bharaavan bharen, phir use bel kar tave par senken. parantha shabd bana he upari+aavart se. upari yani upar kaa or aavart yani chaaron or ghumana. sirf tave par banaai jane vaali roti yaa paranthe ko senkane kii vidhi par gaur karen. ise samtap miltaa rahe iske liye ise upar se lagaataar ghuma-phiraa kar senka jaataa he. phulke kii tarah paranthe kii dono parten nahin phulatin balki sirf upree parat hi phoolati he. iskaa kram kuch yun rahaa upari+aavart > uparawata > paraanwatha > parantha. vaise sidhe shabdon main part vaala aataa kaa vyanjan = part+aataa= parata=parantha. bharat paryant or videshon main bhi ye bahut prachlit hai. dakshin bharat main kerala kaa parantha prasiddh he. isko vahaan protta kehete hai. isamen atyadhik chiknayee ke saath dheron parten hoti hai. paranthe ko bhartiya log malaysia or morishas tak le gayi, jahaan aaj ise farata or singapore main roti kanai yaa roti prata kehete hai. myanmar main ise palaata kehete hai. trinidad evam tobago main ye atyadhik patale or bahut bade hote hai or bassap-shat kehalaate hai.
 hindustani rasoi main bahut tarah ke paranthe banate hai. sabase aasaan to sada parantha hi hota he. sade paranthe ke bhi kai prakaar hote hai, jaise gol parantha, tikona parantha, chujor parantha. duparti parantha ya bahuparti parantha. sade ke baad bharwaan paranthe aate hai. inamen sarvaadhik lokapriy he aalu kaa parantha. bahut si gruhiniyan saal bhar mausami subjiyon or anya padaartho kii subjiyon se bharwaan paranthen banaati hai. rasoiyon kii prayogdharmita se paranthon kii vividhta lagaataar badhati hi rahi he. is prakaar se paranthon main mukhya prakaar hai:-
 dilli ke chandani chowk kshetra main mughal kaal se aabaad nanbaayiyon kaa ek bazaar ab paranthevaali gali ke naam se hi mashhoor ho gaya he. yahaan bahut prakaar ke paranthe aaj bhi milte hai. maharashtra main bharwaan paranthe kaa ek antaran he puran poli. yah meetha hota he.
