 tii0 es0 aliot 1948 ke nobel-puraskaar-vijeta tathaa aadhunik yug kii mahanatam angreji saahitiyik vibhootiyon main se the.
 26 varsh kii aayu main aap apni mathrubhumi america chhodkar england main bas gaye or 1927 main british naagarik ban gaye. aapane naatak, kavita or aalochana teenon kshetron main mahan khyaati praapt kii he tathaa aadhunik yug ke praaia: sabhi prasaddhi lekhakon ko prabhavit kiya he. ve swayan dan, ezara pound tathaa francisi pratikwaadi kavi loforz dwaara sabase adhik prabhavit hue hai.8
 yadhyapi aapkaa pehla kavyasangrah 'prafrook and adar abjaraveshans' 1917 main prakashit hua, tathaapi aapako vaastavik khyaati 'da westland' dwaara praapt hui. mukt chand main likhe tathaa vibhinn saahitiyik sandarbho evam uddharanon se purna is kavy main samaaj kii tatkaaleen paristhiti kaa atyant nairashyapurna chitra khinchaa gaya he. isamen kavi ne jaan boojhkar ankarshak evam kurup upmaanon kaa prayog kiya he jisase vah pathakon kii bhaavana ko thes pahunchaakar unhen samaaj kii vaastavik dasha kaa gyaan kara sake. uske mat main sansar ek 'marubhumi' he-aadhyatmik drushti se anurvar tathaa bhautik drushti se ast vyast. iske baad kii rachanaaon main hamen ek doosraa hi drushtikon miltaa he jo dharmikata kii bhaavana se purna he or jiskaa charam vikaas 'aish vencede' or 'for quartats' main hua.
 aalochana ke kshetra main aapkaa sabase mahatvapurn kaary 17veen shatabdi ke lekhakon, visheshkar dan tathaa dryden kii khoi hui pratishtha kaa pun: sansthaapan tathaa milton evam shelli kii bhartsana karnaa rahaa he. dante kii bhi aapane nai vyakhya kii hai. vaise to aapane kai sau aalochanaayen likhi hai, parantu 'da secred wood', 'da yus ov poetry and da yus ov critisism' tathaa 'aan poetry and poets' vishesh ullekhneey hai.
 aapane nimnlikhit paanch naatakon kii rachana kii he: 'murder in da kathedral', 'family reunion', 'da cocktail party', 'da kanfidenshal clock', 'da elder statsman' . ye sabhi padya main likhe gaye hai evam rangamachan par lokapriy hue hai. 'murder in da kathedral' par film bhi ban chuki he.
