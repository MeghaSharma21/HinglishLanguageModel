 prithvi par kam-se-kam 109 parvat hai jinki oonchaai samudratal se 7,200 meter se adhik he. inamen se adhikansh bhartiya upmahadweep or tibet kii seemaa par sthit hai, or kuch madhya asia main hai. is soochi main keval aise hi shikhar sammilit hai jinhen akelaa khadaa parvat mana ja saktaa he, yani ek hi parvat ke alag-alag shikharon ko nahin gina gaya he.
 is suchi ke adhikansh parvat himalay or karakoram shrunkhalaaon main hai, jo bhartiya upmahadweep se uttar main or tibet ke pathaar se dakshin va pashchim main sthit hai. vishwa ke sabhi 7,000 me parvat asiake madhya main sthit hai. yah sabhi ek aayat main hai jo pashchim main paak-afkhaan seemaa-sthit noshak parvat, uttar main kirgiz-shinjiyang seemaa-sthit jengish chokusu, purv main sichuaan-sthit minya konka or dakshin main bharat-nepal seemaa-sthit kaabru parvat se paribhaashit hota he.
 asia mahadweep se baahar sthit sabase unchaa parvat akonkagua he, jo dakshin america kii andes parvatamala main khadaa he or jise 500 me yaa 1,640 feet se adhik ubhare ugr shikharon kii ek aadhikarik suchi vishwa kaa 189vaan sabase unchaa shikhar bataati he .
 sarvocch parvaton ke sthaan neeche dikhaae asia ke chitron main dekhe ja sakate hai. sankhyaayein suchi main sthaan ankit karti hai. pathan-saralata ke liye, unche parvaton ke paas sthit unase nichle parvat nahin ankit kare gaye hai. sabase adhik parvat-ghanatv vaale kshetron ko alag dabbon main dikhaaya gaya he.
