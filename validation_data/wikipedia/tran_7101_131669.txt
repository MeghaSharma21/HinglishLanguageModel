 abkhajia aanshik rup se anterrashtriya pahchaan praapt vivaadit kshetra he jisaki ek swatantra sarkaar he. yah kshetra kaala sagar ke purvi tat or cocks ke dakshin pashchim main sthit he. yah georgia kaa ek hissaa tha jo ki ise aaj bhi swatantra desh kaa darja nahin detaa. 
 abkhaazia apane aap ko abkhaazia ganraajya athwa apsani ke rup main ek samprabhu rajya kaa darja detaa he. ise rus, nikaragua, venezuela or nauru ne purna samprabhu tathaa dakshin osetia ne aanshik samprabhu raashtra kaa evam transnistria or nagorno-karabakh ne agyaat rajya ke rup main rakhaa he.
