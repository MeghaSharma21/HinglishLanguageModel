 un ushma injanon ko vahirdahan ingine kehete hai jinke kaaryakari taral ko garam karne ke liye kisi vahya strot kaa sahaara liya jaataa he. yah taral vahirdahan ingine main failata he or uski mechanism se antarkriya karke gati evam upyogi kaary karata he. steam terbine bahirdahan ingine kaa ek acha udhaaharan he.
 samaan shakti vaala vahirdahan ingine praayah anterdahan ingine se adhik bada hota he. kintu chunki isamen dahan kaa kaary alag se swatantra rup se hota he, use adhik daksh banaaya ja saktaa he yaa kam hanikaarak gaise aadi paidaa karne ke hisaab se dizin kiya ja saktaa he.
