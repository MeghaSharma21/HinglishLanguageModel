 ireland cricket team sitambar 2016 main dakshin africa daura kar rahe hai. ve do ek divasiy antarrashtriya, dakshin africa or australia ke khilaaf ek-ek nibhaani hogi.
 abi di williars mul rup se ireland ke khilaaf khel ke liye captain ke rup main namit kiya gaya tha. haalanki, vah abhi bhi ek chot se ubar gaya tha taaki phalf du plasis ke captain ke rup main apni jagah le lee or reeli roso bhi team main shaamil kiya gaya. kris morris ghutane kii chot use do maheene ke liye baahar sattarudh saamana karnaa padaa. unhone dwaine pretoris dwaara badal diya gaya tha.
