 kadambi minakshi pallav itihaas par ek bhartiya itihaaskaar or visheshgy thi. vah madras vishvavidhyalay se doctoret kii degree praapt karne vaali pehali mahila baniin. minakshi kaa janm 12 sitambar 1905 ko madras main hua tha.
 minakshi ko praarambhik varshon se itihaas main ruchi thi or mannargudi, pudukkottai, villupuram or kanchipuram jaise aitihaasik sthalon kaa daura bhi kiya. 1929 main unhone madras ke mahila chrishchiyan collejasnatak se snaatak kii. vah madras chrishchiyan college main apni master degree chaahate thi lekin unki ummidavaari shuru main ek mahila hone ke aadhaar par khaarij kar di gaye. 1936 main unhone madras vishvavidhyalay se doctoret kii degree praapt kii.
 unka 1940 main nidhan ho gaya.
