 asmita dilli sthit rangamanch karmiyon kii ek natya sanstha he. asmita natya sanstha ne nukkad natako ke saath- saath do dashakon main 60 se adhik manch naatak kiye he. sanstha saamaajik-raajnneetik drushti se praasangik va samakaleen muddon par naatak karne ke liye patibaddh he. in dino aravind goud asmita theatre group ke nideshak ke rup me karyrat he.
 desh main agrani hindi theatre samooh asmita kii sthapana 1993 main hooi. yuvaao ko rangamanch me prashikshit karnaa or samakaleen muddon par darshakon ko jaagruk bananaa is natya sanstha kii sthapana kaa uddeshy he. pichley 15 varshon main asmita ne rangamanch kaaryshaalaaon kaa vibhinn sansthaanon, vishvavidhyalayon or schoolu me aayojan kiya he. jiname- india habitate centre, jawaharlal nehru vishvavidhyalay, lok kala manch, bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan ,akhil bhartiya ayurvigyaan santhaan, madar international skool, di pa sa skool, sahyadri skool, madar international skool, gargi kalege, hindu kalege, ladi shri ram kalege, kamala nehru kalege, aditi kalege, kirodi mel kalege or ai pi kalege aadi pramukh he.
 asmita natya sanstha ne bharat or videshon ke pramukh theatre mahotsavo main pradarshan kiya he. videshon main france, austrailia, rus, armenia or sanyukt arab imaraat ke saath neshnal skool of drama mahotsav ,sangeet naatak academy, sahitya kala parishad, darpana academy of arts, nandikaar, vivechena theatre mahotsav, old vald theatre mahotsav, times mahotsav, gajanan madhav 'muktibodh' natya mahotsav, world soshal forum or nehru centre mahotsav me bhaag liya he. 
 sanstha ne abhinay theatre, theatre world, british council, katha, association of british vidwaan ,2nd foundation, lakshya, baharoop, banjaaraa theatre, india international centre, prithvi theatre or gaiyr sarkari sanghathanon mobile cretch, ection aid, haq, deepalaya, henrich bal foundation, times foundation, aalo-aashaa kii ek kiran, teach india, asian saamaajik forum, youth rich, vishwa saamaajik manch jaise saamaajik sanghathanon ke saath vibhinn naatakon kaa bhi manchan kiya he. ekal pradarshan ke liye ek nai bhashaa kii khoj main asmita ne kaam kiya he.
