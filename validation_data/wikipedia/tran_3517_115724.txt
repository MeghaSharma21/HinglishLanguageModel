 tania sachdeva ek bhartiya shatranj khilaadi he, jinhone international master or mahila grandmaster ke FIDE kaa khitaab jeeta hua he. tania kaa janm 20 agast 1986 ko dilli main hua tha. vah ek shatranj prastota or vivarankaar he. tania sachdeva ne 6 saal kii umr main hi apni maan anju se shatranj kaa khel siikhnaa shhuru kar diya tha. unke mata-pita ne unhen vyavsaayik prashikshan pradaan kiya. or vah aath saal kii thi jab unhone apana pehla antarrashtriya khitaab hasil kiya. vah ke.si. joshi dwaara prashikshit kii gai thi or apane shuruaati varshon ke dauraan unhone ek bachche ke rup main kai kaarykram jite. 2002 main, unhone marwala main asian junior girls championships jiti. unhone dilli ke vasant vihaar ke modern skool se schuli shiksha praapt kii or shri venkateshwar college se snaatak kiya. 
 2005 main vah mahila grandmaster khitaab se sammanit hone vaali aathven bhartiya khilaadi bani. unhone 2006 or 2007 main bharat kii rashtriya mahila premier championships jiti. 2007 main unhone tehran main nau round main 6 ank ke saath mahila asian shatranj championships jiti. or unhen 2009 main pratishthit arjuna puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha. 2016 main, tania ne ricjevik open main sarvshereshth mahila kaa puraskaar jeeta or katalu main rashtramandal mahila champion kaa khitaab jeeta. unhone 2008 main mahilaon ke shatranj olympiad main bhartiya rashtriya team ke liye, 2009 or 2011 main mahilaon kii vishwa team shatranj championships, 2003 ke asian mahila shatranj championships, 2006 ke asian khelon main or 2009 ke asian indor khelon ke liye khel khele he. unhone stanbul main 2012 main mahila shatranj olympiad main board-3 ke liye vyaktigat kaansya padak jeeta, 2008, 2009, 2012 0r 2014 main chaar team rajat padak or mahilaon kii asian team championships main chaar padak jiname teen rajat or ek kaansya padak tha hasil kiye. tania ne shatranj ke liye ek fritttrriner stritji dividi pesh kii he or vah 2013 main chennai main megnas carlson or vishvanathan anand ke bich hui vishwa championships match ke liye aadhikarik comentary team kaa sadsy bhi rah chuki he.
