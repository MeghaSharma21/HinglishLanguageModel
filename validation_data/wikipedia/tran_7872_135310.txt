 torace jalsandhi australia or nayaa gini dweep ke bich kii jalsandhi he. yah apni nunatam bindu main 150 kimi kii chaudaai rakhti he. iske dakshin main australian mukhyabhumi ke queensland rajya kaa york antareep prayadweep he or uttar main papua new gini kaa pashchimi praant he. yah jaldandhi purv main sthit coral sagar ke pani ko pashchim main sthit arafura sagar se jodatii he. haalanki yah naav-yatayaat ke liye ek mahatvapurn jalmaarg he, iske adhiktar bhaag main gaharaai bahut hi kam he . itani kam gaharaai or jagah-jagah par shail-bhittiyon or dwipon ke bikhare hone se ise ek khataranaak maarg mana jaataa he.
 torace jalsandhi main kam-se-kam 274 dweep hai jinamein se 17 par sthai maanav aabaadi he. yahan ke log hazaron varshon se australian mukhyabhumi par australian adivaasiyon or nayaa gini ke malaneshian logon ke bich main vyapaar va saanskrithik sampark banane vaali ek mahatvapurn kadi the. aadhunik kaal main yahan ke dwipon main lagbhag 6,800 or australian mukhyabhumi par lagbhag 42,000 torace jalsandhi dweepavasi rahete hai.
