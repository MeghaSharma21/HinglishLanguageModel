 2017 antarrashtriya cricket sizan may 2017 se sitambar 2017 tak he. vartmaan main, is avadhi ke dauraan 13 test maichon, 52 ek divasiy antarrashtriya, 12 twanti-20 antarrashtriya, or 31 mahila vanade international ko khelaa jaayegaa. sijan kii shuruuaat test cricket ranking main bharat ke saath shuru hui, dakshin africa ne ekadivasiy ranking kii aguwai kii, newjiland ne twanti-20 ranking kii aguwai kii or australia kii mahilaon ne mahila ranking kii aguwai kii.
 yah satr england or ireland ke bich do maichon kii ekadivasiy shrinkhalaa se shuru hua, iske baad ireland main trikoniya ekadivasiy shrinkhalaa, or england or dakshin africa ke bich teen maichon kii ek divasiy series. in maichon ne isisi champions trophy kii taiyaari kii, jo june main england main yojit kii gai thi. yah pehla champions trophy tha jisamen westeindies ne 30 sitambar, 2015 ko cat of kii taarikh main ekadivasiy ranking main sheersh 8 ke baahar hone ke kaaran bhaag nahin liya tha. yah turant mahila cricket vishwa cap ke baad kiya gaya, jo england main bhi yojit kiya gaya tha. in do pramukh ghatnaon ke baad, antarrashtriya cricket main kai dwipaksheeya siries ke saath phir se shuru ho rahaa he jisamen afghanistan kaa pehla westeindies kaa pehla dwipaksheeya daura he. mahatvapurn shrinkhalaa main tulsi di olivera trophy ke 6 wein sanskaran or visden trophy ke 26 wein sanskaran shaamil hai, jinamein se baad main england main khelaa jane vaala pehla din / raat test match bhi shaamil he. pakistan ne july main bangladesh kaa daura kiya tha, lekin shrinkhalaa radd kar di gai thi. is sizan main zimbabwe kaa srilanka kaa pehla dwipaksheeya daura 15 saal main bhi shaamil tha, jisamen zimbabwe ne srilanka par apana pehla siries jeet darj kii thi.
 is sizan ke dauraan, 2019 isisi cricket vishwa cap kii yogyata prakriya ke kai mahatvapurn kadam honge. isisi ekadivasiy championships main pratispardhaa karne vaali 12 tiimon ke bich hone vaale oriai vishesh mahatv ke honge kyonki keval is sizan ke ant main sheersh 8 main sthaan par rahane vaale hi sidhe vishwa cap ke liye yogya honge. yah pichley vishwa cap se alag he kyonki purna sadasyon ko un toornamenton main swachaalit yogyata pradaan kii gai thi. neeche chaar main shaamil hone vaale log 2018 cricket vishwa cap qualifire main pratispardhaa karenge, jo bangladesh main yojit kiya jayega, or vishwa cricket lig championships main sheersh chaar tiimon or vishwa cricket lig divijan do kii sheersh do tiimon main shaamil ho jayega. is sijan ke dauraan vishwa cricket lig championships or intercantinental cap ke round 5 or 6 main match yojit hai. vishwa cricket lig divijan teen toornament is sijan main yojit kiya gaya, sheersh 2 tiimon, oman or canada ke saath, divijan 2 main padonnat kiya gaya.
