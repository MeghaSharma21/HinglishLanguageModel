  sanju yadav ek bhartiya footbal khilaadi hai jo bhartiya mahila lig main alakhpura ke liye forward kii bhoomika nibhaati hai or bharat kii mahila footbal team kaa pratinidhitiv karti hai.
 alakhpura ke gaanv main janmi, yadav ne apane parivaar ke liye chaatravrutti or dhan hasil karne ke liye das varsh kii aayu main footbal khelnaa shuru kiya tha. vah mukhya rup se apane gaanv or bharat rashtriya team se prashikshit thi. oktober 2016 main, sanju alakhpura paksh kaa hissaa tha jisane bhartiya mahila lig ke praarambhik daur main bhaag liya tha. 17 oktober 2016 ko usne bodiline ke khilaaf club ke liye ek hetric banaai. isse unki team ne 4-0 se jeet hasil kii. vah lig ke praarambhik daur charan main sheersh scorer ke rup main ubhar kar saamne ai thi. 11 baar neting karke or is prakaar unki team ko toornament ke antim daur ke yogya hone ke liye yogya banane main madad kari. mahilaon kii lig ke final round main ve apni team ko teen baar scoring ke saath semifinal main arhata praapt karne main madad kii.
  21 disambar 2016 ko, club or desh donon ke liye unke pradarshan ke baad, yadav ko efeef imarging player of da year ke rup main namit kiya gaya tha.
 farvari 2016 main, yadav ko 20- mahila bharat scwed ke hisse ke rup main chunaa gaya jo 2016 dakshin asian khelon main bhaag lenge. unhone apana pehla pradarshan kiya or 13 farvari 2016 ko bangladesh ke khilaaf antarrashtriya str par apana pehla gol daaga.74 wein minute main unka gol chauthaa tha kyonki bharat ne 5-1 se jeet hasil kii or swarna padak match main jagah bana lee. do din baad vah 69 wein minute main ek vikalp ke rup main ain, jab bharat ne toornament jeeta, shilong ke jawaharlal nehru stadium main nepal ko 4-0 se haraaya.
