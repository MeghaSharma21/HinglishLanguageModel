 sirium ek rasinic tatva he. yah viral mrudaa tatva kaa ek pramukh sadsy he. iskaa ; parmaanu sankhya 58 tathaa parmaanu bhaar 140.13 he. 
 
 sirium ke chloride ko sodium athwa meganecium ke saath garam karne athwa shuddh chloride ko pautacium or sodium chloride ke saath milakar vidyut apghaton dwaara praapt kiya ja saktaa he.
 sirium lohe jaisaa dikh padataa he. yah vidyut kaa kuchaalak he. yah vishesh kathor dhaatu nahin he or saralata se iske pattar banaae ja sakate hai.
 sirium par garam jal ke prabhaav se hydrogen nikalataa he. shuddh dhaatu par 260 degree sein. taap par hydrogen pravaahit karne se sirium trihydride or sirium diahydride kaa mishran praapt hota he. 210 degree sein. par chlorine badi tivrata se kriya kar ajal sirium trichloride banataa he. tanu athwa sandra hydrochloric amla se jaleey sirium chloride aasaani se banataa he. yah sulfur, silinium tathaa telurium se milakar dhaatu ke sulphide, selinide tathaa teluride banaata he. tanu salphurik amla kaa is par prabhaav padataa he, parantu sandra kaa koi prabhaav nahin padataa. nitric amla sirium oxide ko avakshipt kar detaa he. yah dhaatu nitrogen, phosphorus, arsenic antimony or carbon ke saath ati tapt karne par kramash: nitrite phosphid, arsinide tathaa carbide banati he.
 yah kai dhaatuon ke saath milakar mishra dhaatuen banaati hai. magnesium, jasta or aluminium ke saath anek mishra dhaatuen bani hai.
 sirium kii do sanyojaktaayen 3 tathaa 4 hai. iske do oxide, do hydraksaide Ce3 or Ce4 fluoride CaF3 chloride sulphide sulphate, carbonate, nitrate, phosphate aadi lavan banate hai.
 yah dhaatu kai dwilavan banaati he, jaise M2, Ce4 8H2O .
