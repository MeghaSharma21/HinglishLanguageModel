 unka janm maharashtra ke chiploon main 12 april 1943 ko ushaa or purushotham saathe ke ghar main hua. unka vivaah 29 janvari 1965 main indore ke jayant mahajan ke saath hua. unhone indaur ke devi ahilya vishvavidhyalay se snatakottar or elelbi kii shiksha praapt kii.
 sumitra mahajan ne 1989 ke aam chunaav main pehali baar loksabhaa chunaav main bhaag liya or unhone congress netaa or madhya pradesh ke purv mukhyamantri prakash chandra sethi ko haraaya. isse pehle indore vidhansabha nirvachan kshetra se wo lagaataar teen vidhaan sabha chunaav haar chuki thim. sumitra mahajan 2002 se 2004 tak kendriya mantrimandal main shaamil thim. unhen maanav sansaadhan, sanchaar tathaa petroleum mantralay kaa kaam diya gaya tha. wo pratham mahila hai jo kabhi loksabhaa chunaavon main parajeet nahin hui. wo aath baar loksabhaa chunaav jeetne vaali pratham mahila saansad hai.
 sumitra mahajan ne 5 june 2014 ko lok sabha adhyaksh ke liye namakan bharaa. 6 june 2014 ko wo nirvirodh loksabhaa adhyaksh chuni gai. yah pehali baar he ki bhartiya janata party kaa koi saansad lok sabha adhyaksh bana.
