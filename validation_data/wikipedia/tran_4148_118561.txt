  asthma  shwasan maarg kaa ek aam jeern sujan disease vaala rog he jise char va aavarti lakshanon, prativarti shwasan baadha or shwasni-aakarshase pahachaanaa jaataa he. aam lakshanon main ghargharaahat, khaansee, seene main jakadan or shwasan main samasyashamil hai.
 dama ko anuvaanshik or paryaavarniya karkon kaa sangyojan mana jaataa he.iskaa nidaan saamanyatya lakshanon ke praturup, samay ke saath upachaar ke prati pratikriya or spirometriper aadhaarit hota he. yah chikitsiya rup se lakshanon kii aavriti, ek second main balpurvak nickshwasan maatra or shikhar nirishwaas pravaah dar ke aadhaar par vargeekrut he.dame ko atopic yaa gaiyr-atopic kii tarah bhi vargeekrut kiya jaataa he jahaan par atopi ko tip 1 atisamvedansheeltaa pratikriyaaon ke vikaas kii ore pehle se anukulit rup main sandarbhit kiya gaya he.
 gambhir lakshanon kaa upachaar aam taur par ek antahshwasan vaali laghu avadhi me kaam kare vaali beta-2 agonist or maukhik corticostroed dwaara kiya jaataa he. prayek gambhir maamle main antahshira corticostroed, magnesium sulphate or aspataal main bhartee karnaa aavashyak ho saktaa he. lakshanon ko allergy karkon or takalif karkon jaise utprerakon se bachaav karke tathaa corticostroed ke upyog se roka ja saktaa he. yadi asthma lakshan aniyantrit rahete hai to lambii avadhi se sakriy hathi beta yaa lukotrin pratipakshi ko shwasan kiye jane vaale corticostroed ko upyog kiya ja saktaa he. 1970 ke baad se asthma ke lakshan mahatvapurn rup se bad gayi hai. 2011 tak, puure vishwa main 235-300 million log isse prabhavit the, jinamein lagbhag 2,50,000 mautein shaamil hai.
 asthma ko baar baar hone vaali ghargharaahat, saans lene main hone vaali takalif, seene main jakadan or khaansee se pahachaanaa jaataa he. khaansee ke kaaran fefde se kaff utpann ho saktaa he lekin isko baahar laana kaaphi kathin hota he. kisi daure se ubarne ke samay yah mawaad jaisaa lag saktaa he jo ki shwet rakt kanikaon ke ucch str ke kaaran hota he jinhen snophills kaha jaataa he. aamtaur par raat main or subah-subah yaa vyaayaam or thandi hawa kii pratikriya ke kaaran lakshan kaaphi kharaab hote hai. asthma se peedit kuch logon ko aamtaur par utprerakon kii pratikriya main shaayad hi kabhi lakshanon kaa anubhav ho, jabaki dusron main lakshan dikhte hai va bane rahete hai.
 asthma se peedit logon main kai sari anya swasthya sthitiyaan adhik baar hoti he jinamein:gastro-oesophagiel riflex rog, rhinocynucitis or obstructive sleep epaniyashamil hai. manovaigyanik vikaar bhi kaaphi aam hai jisamen se chintaa vikaar 16–52% logon main or manodasha vikaar 14–41% logon main hota he. haalanki yah abhi gyaat nahin he ki asthma, manouvaigyanik samasyaayein paidaa karata he yaa manouvaigyanik samasyaayein asthma kaa kaaran hoti hai.
 jatil tathaa aparyaapt rup se samajhi gayi paryaavarniya or jin sambandhi parsparik kriyaaon ke sangyojan se asthma hota he. ye kaarak isaki gambhirtha or upachaar ke prati pratikriya ko prabhavit karte hai. aisa vishwaas kiya jaataa he ki asthma kii dar main main haal main aayi vruddhi badalati epigenietic tathaa badalte paryaavaran ke kaaran ho rahi he.
 asthma ke vikaas tathaa vistaar se kai paryaavarniya kaarak jude huye hai jinamein allergy kaarak tatv, vaayu pradooshan tathaa anya paryaavarniya rasayan shaamil hai. garbhavastha ke dauraan dhumrapaan tathaa iske baad kiya gaya dhumrapaan asthma jaise lakshanon ke gambhir jokhim se judaa he. trephic pradooshan ke kaaran nimna vaayu gunvattta yaa ucch ozone str, asthma ke vikaas tathaa isaki badi hui gambhirtha se judaa he. ghar ke bheetar ke asthir carbonic yogicon asthma ke utprerak ho sakate hai; udhaaharan ke liye formeldihide anaavaran kaa isse ek sakaaratmak sambandh he. saath hi, pivisi main upasthit pethfalats bacchon tathaa vayaskon main hone vaale asthma se sambandhit hai isi tarah se andotoxin anaavaran bhi isse sambandhit he.
 asthma, ghar ke bheetar upasthit allergy karkon ke saath anaavaran se sambandhit he. ghar ke bheetar ke aam allergy karkon main dhul vaale ghun, cockroach, jaanwaron ke baalo kii roosi tathaa fafoond samil hai. dhul ke ghunon ko kam karne ke prayaas aprabhavi paye gayi hai. kuch prakaar ke virus janit shwasan sankraman asthma ke vikaas ke jokhim ko badha sakate hai vishesh rup se tab jabaki unako bachapan main shwasan sinsishayal virus tathaa rhinovyraske rup main hasil kiya gaya ho. haalanki kuch anya prakaar ke sankraman jokhim ko kam kar sakate hai.
 swatchta pariklpana ek siddhant he jo puri duniya main asthma kii badi dar ko gaiyr-sankramak bacteria tathaa virus se bachapan ke dauraan ghate huye anaavaran ke pratyaksh tathaa anjaane parinaam ke rup main samajhane kaa prayaas karata he.dharna yah he ki bacteria or virus ke prati anaavaran main kami kaa kaaran, kuch had tak aadhunik samaajon main badi hui swatchta or parivaar ke ghate aakaar hai.swatchta pariklpana kaa samarthan karne vaale saakshyon main gharelu va kheti sambandhi pashuon main asthma kii ghati daren shaamil hai.
 aarambhik jivan main antibiotic kaa upyog asthma ke vikaas se judaa hua he.saath hi, shalyakriya dwaara janm asthma ke bade huye jokhim se sambandhit he – yah badha hua jokhim swasth bacteria ke jhund kii kami ke kaaran hota he jise navjaat janm naal ke maarg ke madhyam se grahan karegaa. asthma tathaa samruddhi kii dar ke bich ke ek sambandh hota he.
 asthma ke liye paariwarik itihaas ek jokhim kaarak he jisamen vibhinn jin shaamil kiye gayi hai. yadi samaan judawaan main se ek prabhavit hota he to duusare ke prabhavit hone kii sambhaavna 25% tak hoti he. 2005 kii samaapti tak, 6 se adhik pruthak jansankhyaon main 25 jeens ko asthma se sambandhit paaya gaya he jinamein dusro ke saath GSTM1, IL10,CTLA-4, SPINK5,LTC4S, IL4R or ADAM33 shaamil hai. in jeens main se adhisankhya pratiraksha pranali yaa niyaman karne vaali sujan se sambandhit hai. atyadhik pratirupit adhyayanon se samarthit in jeens kii suchi main se bhi milane vaale parinaam sabhi parakshit jansankhyaon par ek rup nahin hai. 2006 main 100 se adhik jeens ko ek anuvaanshik sambandh adhyayan se sambandhit paaya gaya tha; or adhik jeens kaa milanaa jari he.
 kuch anuvaanshik bhinn rup keval tab asthma paidaa karte hai jab unako vishisht paryaavarniya anavaranon ke saath joda jaataa he. CD14 kshetra main single newcliotide polimorphism tathaa andotoxin iskaa ek vishisht udhaaharan he. andotoxin anaavaran kai paryaavarniya sroton se ho saktaa he jisamen dhumrapaan, kute or khet shaamil hai. isaliye asthma kaa jokhim vyakti kii anuvaanshikta or andotoxin anaavaran, dono ke madhyam se nirdhaarit kiya jaataa he.
 atopic esezma, allergic rinitis or asthma ke trikon ko etoppy kehete hai. asthma ke vikaas ke liye sabase majboot kaarak atopic rog kaa itihaas hota he jisamen esezma yaa he bukharpeedit logon ko asthma hone kii adhik dar hoti he. asthma swapratirakshi rog kurg-strans syndrome tathaa vasculitis se sambandhit rahaa he. kuch vishisht prakaar kii pitti se peedit logon main asthma ke lakshan dikh sakate hai.
 motaape or asthma hone ke jokhim ke bich ek sahasambandh hota he aajkal ye dono kaaphi bad gayi hai. iske liye kai kaaran bhoomika nibhaa sakate hai jinamein charbi ke ekatra hone ke kaaran shwasan kriya main kameen or yah tathy ki adepos utak sujan badhaane kii sthiti paidaa karte hai shaamil hai.
 beta avarohak dawaaen jaise ki propranolol un logon main asthma ko shuru kar saktaa he jo iske prati atisamvedansheel hai. haalankihruday sambandhi beta avarohak, halki yaa madhyam beemaari se peedit logon main surakshit dikhte hai. anya dawaaen jo samasyaayein paidaa kar sakati hai unamem ASA, NSAIDs or angiotencin- parivartak enzim avarohakashamil hai.
 kuch logon ko hafton yaa maheenon sthir asthma ho saktaa he or phir achaanak teevr asthma kii sthiti paidaa ho sakati he. bhinn-bhinn log bhinn karkon par alag-alag tarah se pratikriya karte hai. adhiktar logon main kai saare utprerakon se gambhir tivrata viksit ho sakati he.
 ve gharelu kaarak jo asthma kii gambhir tivrata ko badha sakate hai dhul, jaanavar roosi, cockroach allergy kaarak or fafundishamil hai.sugandhi, mahilaon va bacchon main gambhir dauron ke aam kaaran hai. upree shwasanmaarg ke virus janit tathaa bacteria janit sankraman rog kii sthiti ko or gambhir kar dete hai. maansik tanaav lakshanon ko or gambhir kar sakate hai – aisa mana jaataa he ki tanaav pratiraksha pranali main herpher karata he or is prakaar allergy or pareshaan karne vaale tatwon kii vayumarg kii phulav pratikriya ko badhaata he.
 asthma vayumarg ke gambhir phulav ke kaaran hota he jiske parinaam swaroop iske aasapaas kii chikani mansapeshiyon main sankuchan bad jaataa he. duusare karkon ke saath iske kaaran vayumarg ko simit karne ke jatke shuru hote hai or iske saath saans lene main takalif ke lakshanon kii shuruyaat ho jaati he. sankare hone kii prakriya upachaar dwaara yaa uske bina bhi prativarti ho sakati he.kabhi kabhaar vayumarg apane aap badal jate hai. vayumarg ke aam badlaavon main snophil main badhottaree tathaa lamina kii aantarik sataha kaa motaa hona shaamil he. samay bitne ke saath vayumarg kii chikani mansapeshiyaan, shleshm grathiyon kii sankhya vruddhi ke saath apana aakaar badha sakati hai. anya prakaar kii koshikaon main nimnlikhit shaamil hai: tii limphosites, macrophez auranutrophills. pratiraksha pranali ke anya ghatak bhi inamen shaamil ho sakate hai jaise cytokins, kemokins, histamine auralukotrinez tathaa anya.
 jabaki asthma ek jaani pahachaani paristhiti he, lekin phir bhi isaki koi vyapak sweekruti vaali koi paribhaasha nahin he. ise asthma ke liye vaishvik pahal dwaara nimna rup main nirdhaarit kiya gaya he "vayumarg kaa ek jeern phulav vikaar jisamen kai koshikaon tathaa koshikeey tatwon kii bhoomika hoti hai. yah jeern phulav vaayu maarg kii ati-pratikriya se sambandhit he jiske kaaran baar-baar ghargharaahat, shwasan main kami, seene main jakadan or raat main yaa bhor main khaansee hoti he. ye sthitiyaan aam taur par fefdon main vyapak lekin parivartansheel vaayu pravaah baadha se sambandhit he jo ki tatkaal yaa upachaar ke baad aksar saamaanya ho jaati hai".
 iskaa koi sateek pareekshan nahin he tathaa nidaan aam taur par lakshanon ke swaroop tathaa samay ke saath upachaar ke prati pratikriya par aadhaarit he. asthma ke nidaan kii shankaa tab kii jaani chaiye jab, nimnlikhit kaa itihaas ho: baar-baar ghargharaahat hona, khaansee yaa shwasan main takalif or in lakshan vyaayaam karne se or kharaab hote hon, virus sankraman, allergy yaa vaayu pradooshan.spirometry ko nidaan kii pushti ke liye upyog kiya jaataa he. chah saal se kam umr ke bacchon main nidaan kaaphi kathin ho jaataa he kyonki ve spirometry ke liye kaaphi chote hote hai.
 nidaan tathaa prabandhan ke liye spirometry kii anushansa kii jaati he. asthma ke liye yah akelaa sarvshereshth pareekshan he. yadi is takanic dwaara mapa gaya FEV1, salbutamol jaise bronchodailator ke diye jane se 12% tak behatar ho jaataa he to yah lakshan kaa samarthak mana jaataa he. haalanki yah unamem saamaanya ho saktaa he jinamein halke asthma kaa itihaas ho or vah vartmaan main pradarshit na hota ho.ekal shwasan phulav kshamta dwaara asthma va COPD main antar karne main sahaayta milti he. prayek ek yaa do saal main spirometry karnaa upyukt hota he jisase ki yah gyaat ho sake ki kisi vyakti kaa asthma kitne behatar tareeke se niyantrit hai.
 mithacolin chalange main pehle se samvedansheel logon main shwasan maarg sankuchan ke liye jimmedaar kisi tatv kii badhati matraaon kaa antahshwasan shaamil hota he. yadi rinatmak ho to iskaa arth he ki vyakti ko asthma nahin he; haalanki dhanatmak hona is rog ke liye vishisht nahin hota he.
 anya saakshyon main nimnlikhit shaamil hai: kam se kam do saptaah tak prati saptaah teen din main ≥20% kaa shikhar nirishwaas pravaah dar antar, solbutamol dwaara upachaar ke saath shikhar nirishwaas pravaah dar main ≥20% kaa sudhaar yaa kisi uttejk ke anaavaran ke kaaran shikhar pravaah main ≥20% kii kami. shikhar nirishwaas pravaah kaa pareekshan spirometry se adhik char he haalanki niyamit nidaan ke liye anushansit he. yah un logon ke dainik swa-nirikshan ke liye upyogi ho saktaa he jinako madhyam yaa gambhir rog ho saktaa he tathaa nai dawa kii jaanch kii prabhaavashiltaa ke liye bhi yah upyogi ho saktaa he.yah gambhir tivrataon vaale logon main upachaar ke maargdarshan main sahaayak ho saktaa he.
 lakshanon kii aavritiyon, prati second balpurvak nickshwasan maatra, tathaa shikhar nirishwaas pravaah dar dwaara asthma ko chikitsiya rup se vargeekrut kiya jaataa he. asthma ko atopic yaa gaiyr-atopic ke rup main bhi vargeekrut kiya ja saktaa he, jo is aadhaar par nirdhaarit hota he ki lakshan allergy dwaara upje hai yaa nahin . jabaki asthma ko gambhirtha ke aadhaar par vargeekrut kiya jaataa he vartmaan main is baat kii koi spasht vidhi nahin he jo is pranali se aage asthma ko vibhinn up samoohon main vargeekrut kar sake. un up samoohon kii pahchaan karne vaale tareekon kii khoj karnaa, jo vibhinn prakaar ke upachaaron ke saath sahi pratikriya karen, asthma shodh kaa vartmaan mahatvapurn lakshya he.
 haalanki asthma ek jeern avarohak sthiti he, phir bhi ise jeern pratirodhi fefde kii beemaari kaa hissaa nahin mana jaataa he kyonki yah shabdaawalee vishisht rup se rog ke sanyojanon se sandarbhit he jo ki aparivartaneey hote hai jaise ki shwasanalikavisphar,jeern shvasanishoth or vatasfiti. in rogon ke vipareet, asthma main vayumarg avrodh aam taur par parivartaneey hota he; haalanki yadi upachaar na kiya jaye to asthma ke kaaran fefdon main hone vaala jeern phulav aparivartaneey rup se pratirodhi ho jaataa he kyonki vayumarg usee ke anuroop dhal jaataa he. amphisema ke vipareet, asthma shwasanaliyon ko prabhavit karata he na ki vayukoshthika ko.
 jeern asthma phulav ko aam taur par ek “asthma daura” kaha jaataa he. hameshaa se pratishthit lakshanon main shwasan main kami, ghargharaahat or seene main jakadan shaamil he. jabaki ye asthma ke praathmik lakshan hai, kuch logon ko shuruyaat main khaansee hoti he or gambhir maamlo main vaayu gati itani adhik baadhit ho sakati he ki koi bhi ghargharaahat na sunai de.
 ek asthma daure ke samay dikhne vaale chihnon main shwasan kii sahaayak mansapeshiyon kaa upyog shaamil hai, paradoxical naadee-spand, tathaa seene kaa atirikt phulav bhi ho saktaa he. oxigen kii kami ke kaaran twachaa tathaa nakhunon main nila rang dikh saktaa he.
 ek halke ubhaar main shikhar nirishwaas pravaah dar is ≥200 L/min yaa sarvshereshth bhavishyavaanee kaa ≥50% hota he. madhyam ubhaar ko 80 or 200 L/min ke bich yaa sarvshereshth bhavishyavaanee kaa 25% or 50% jabaki gambhir ko ≤ 80 L/min yaa sarvshereshth bhavishyavaanee kaa ≤25% ke rup main nirdhaarit kiya jaataa he.
 jeern gambhir asthma, jise pehle asthematicas sthiti kaha jaataa tha, asthma kii ek aisi gambhir dasha hoti he jo bronchodailators tathaa corticostroed ke maanak upachaaron ke prati pratikriya nahin karte hai. maamalon main se aadhe sankramanon ke kaaran hote hai jo allergy, vaayu pradooshan yaa aparyaapt yaa anupyukt dawaaon ke upyog se paidaa hote hai.
 bhangur asthma, asthma kaa ek aisa prakaar he jisamen baar-baar va gambhir daure hote hai. bhangur asthma kaa tip 1 ek aisa rog he jisamen gambhir dawa upachaar ke baavjood vistrut shikhar pravaah parivartaneeyata hoti he. bhangur asthma kaa tip 2 ek suniyantrit prishthbhumi asthma hota he jisamen achaanak gambhir ubhaar hota he.
 vyaayaam ke kaaran, asthma se peedit tathaa gaiyr-peedit, dono logon main bronchocancetriction paidaa ho saktaa he. yah asthma se peedit adhikansh logon main tathaa gaiyr-peedit logon main 20% logon ko hota he. athleton main yah kuleen athleton main yah kaaphi aam taur par hota he, jisaki dar bobsley res waalon main 3% se lekar cycle dhavakon main 50% tathaa cross country skiing karne vaale athleton main 60% tak hota he. jabaki kinhi bhi jalwaayu paristhitiyon ho saktaa he phir bhi yah shushk va thandi jalwaayu paristhitiyon main adhik aam he. antahshwasan beta2-agonist, asthma se gaiyr-peedit athleton ke pradarshan par koi sudhaar nahin dikhataa he haalanki maukhik khuraaq sahanshakti or takat badha sakati he.
 kaarysthal anaavaran ke parinamswaroop hone vaala asthma ek aam taur par bataaya gaya peshewar rog he. haalanki bahut se maamle is prakaar se na to report kiye gayi hai or na hi darj kiye gayi hai. yah anumaanit he ki asthma ke 5-25% maamlen kaary se sambandhit hai. kuch sau vibhinn agenton ko iske sabase aam kaarnon ke rup main sanlipt paaya gaya he jinamein nimnlikhit shaamil hai: isosynet, anaaj yaa lakdi kaa buraada, colophoni, shouldering flux, letex, jaanavar oreldihids. samasyaon ke ucchtam jokhimon se jude rojgaar main nimnlikhit shaamil hai: ve jo paint spray karte hai, bekri vaale or jo khaadya prasanskaran se jude hai, norsen, rasyanon ke saath kaam karne vaale, jo jaanwaron ke saath kaam karte hai, welding karne vaale, bal lanvaarane vaale tathaa lakdi kaa kaam karne vaale.
 bahut si anya paristhitiyaan hai, jo asthma jaise lakshanon ko paidaa kar sakati hai. bacchon main, anya upree vayumarg rogon jaise ki, allergic rinitisathatha sinositis ko tathaa vayumarg pratirodh ke anya kaarnon main nimna shaamil hai: bahari tatwon kaa antahshwasan, trekial stenosis yaa laringontrekkiyomalasia, vascular ring, bade huye lynodes yaa gardan kaa maance. vayaskon main, COPD, sankulan janit hriday vifaltaa, shwasanpath jamaav saath hi ACE avarodhakon ke kaaran dawa-prerit khaansee par dhyaan diya janaa chaiye. dono prakaar kii jansankhyaon main swar rajju main shithilataa samaan rup se upasthit ho sakati he.
 jeern pratirodhi fefde kaa rog asthma ke saath maujuud rahata he tathaa jeern asthma kii jatiltaaon ke rup main ho saktaa hai. 65 kii umr ke baad pratirodhi vayumarg rog se peedit adhiktar logon ko asthma or COPD hoga. is setting main, COPD ko bade hue vayumarg, asamaanya rup se badi hui diwaar kii motaai tathaa shwasanaliyon main badi chikani mansapeshiyon dwaara vibhedit kiya ja saktaa he. haalanki COPD or asthma ke prabandhan siddhant ek jaise hone ke kaaran is str kii jaanch nahin kii jaati he: corticosteroed, lambey samay tak kaam karne vaale beta aginst or dhumrapaan samaapti. ye lakshanon main asthma ke kaaphi samaan hota he or yah cigarette ke dhune ke atirikt anaavaran se, adhik umr, bronchodailator dene kaa baad bhi lakshanon main kam parivartan tathaa atopi ke paariwarik itihaas kii ghati sambhaavnaaon se sambandhit he.
 asthma ke vikaas kii rokthaam ke upayon kii prabhaavashiltaa ke sakshya kamjor hai. kuch sakaaratmak dikhte hai jaise: garbh ke dauraan or prasav ke baad dono hi sthitiyon main dhune se anaavaran simit karnaa, stanpan or din kii dekhbhaal main badhat tathaa bada parivaar, lekin inamen se koi bhi saakshyon se itnaa adhik samarthit nahin he ki is prayojan ke liye uski anushansa kii jaye. paaltu jaanwaron ke saath pehle se anaavaran upyogi ho saktaa he. anya samayon par paaltu jaanavaro se anaavaran ke parinaam adhure hai or keval yah anushansit he ki yadi kisi vyakti ko kisi paaltu jaanavar se allergy he to us jaanavar ko ghar se hataa dena chaiye. garbhavastha yaa stanpan ke dauraan khanpan main pratibandhon ko prabhavi nahin paaya gaya he isaliye inaki anushansa nahin kii jaati he. samvedansheel logon ke ke liye gyaat yogicon ko kaarysthalon se kam karnaa yaa hataanaa prabhavi ho saktaa he.
 haalanki asthma kaa koi upachaar nahin he lekin aamtaur par lakshanon ko behatar kiya ja saktaa he. lakshanon kii agrasakriy rup se nigaraanee tathaa prabandhan ke liye ek vishisht tathaa anukulit yojana banaayi jaani chaiye. is yojana main allergy ke anaavaran ko karnaa, lakshanon kii gambhirtha kaa aankalan karne ke liye pareekshan tathaa dawaaon kaa upyog shaamil hona chaiye. upachaar yojana ko likhaa janaa janaa chaiye or lakshanon main parivartan ke aadhaar par upachaar main samaayojan kii salaah di jaani chaiye.
 asthma ke sabase prabhavi upachaar ke liye utprerakon kii pahchaan kii jaani chaiye jaise ki cigarette kaa dhuna, paaltu jaanavar yaa aspirin or unase anaavaran ko samaapt kiya janaa chaiye. yadi utprerakon se bachaav aparyaapt ho to dawa kaa upyog anushansit he. chikitsiya dawaaon kaa chunaav, anya chizon ke saath rog kii gambhirtha tathaa lakshanon kii aavriti par aadhaarit he. asthma ke liye vishisht dawaaon ko mote taur par teevr kriya tathaa deergh avadhi main kriya karne vaali shreniyon main vargeekrut kiya jaataa he.
 bronchodailators ko lakshanon main thodi hi avadhi main aaraam dene ke liye anushansit kiya jaataa he. dauron ke kabhi-kabhaar hone kii dasha main kisi anya dawa kii aavashyaktaa nahin padati he. yadi rog kii halki niyamit upasthiti ho to shwasan dwaara lee jane vaali corticosteroed kii halki khuraaq yaa vaikalpik rup se ek maukhik lukotrin antagonist yaa koi mast cell stablizer anushansit he. jinako dainik daure padate hai unke liye shwasan dwaara lee jane vaali corticosteroed kii behatar khuraaq kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. madhyam yaa gambhir tezi kii sthiti main, in upachaaron ke saath maukhik corticosteroed jodi jaati he.
 niyantran behatar karne tathaa dauron kii rokthaam ke liye utprerakon se bachaav mukhya ghatak he. sabase aam utprerakon main allergy, dhuna, vaayu pradooshan,gaiyr chaynaatmak beta blowers tathaa sulphite vaale khaadya shaamil hai. dhumrapaan karnaa or dwitiyak dhumrapaan corticosteroed jaisi dawaaon kii prabhaavashiltaa kam kar saktaa he. dhul kii ghun vaale niyantran upaay jinamein vaayu nisyandan, ghunon ko maarane vaale rasayan, chushan, gadde ke cover tathaa anya vidhiyon ke asthma lakshanon par koi prabhaav nahin the.
 asthma kaa upachaar karne vaali dawaaon ko do virgon main baanta gaya he: gambhir lakshanon kaa upachaar karne vaali teevr kriya dawaaen tathaa atirikt ubhaar ko rokane vaali deergh avadhi main kriya karne vaali dawaaen.
 dawaaon ko aam taur par asthma spesar yaa ek shushk powder inheler ke saath meter vaali khuraaq inheler ke sangyojan main diya jaataa he. spesar ek plastic kaa silendar hota he jo dawa ko vaayu ke saath milaataa he jisase ki yah aasaani ke saath dawa kii puri khuraaq ke rup main lee ja sake. nebuliser tathaa spesar un logon ke liye samaan rup se prabhavi hote hai jo halke se madhyam lakshanon se prabhavit hote hai lekin gambhir lakshanon vaali sthitiyon ke liye kisi prakaar ke antar ko nirdhaarit karne ke liye paryaapt sakshya uplabdh nahin hai.
 shwasan dwaara lee jane vaali corticosteroed ke deergh avadhi tak parmparik khuraaqon ke saath upyog main vipareet prabhaavon ke halke jokhimon kii upasthiti bani rahati he. jokhimon main motiyobind kaa vikaas hona or kad main halki kami shaamil he.
 jab asthma saamaanya upachaaron ke prati pratikriyaatmak nahin hota he to aakasmik prabandhan tathaa apratyaashit ubhaar kii rokthaam dono ke liye anya vikalp uplabdh hai. aakasmik prabandhan ke liye anya vikalpon main nimnlikhit shaamil hai:
 ve log jinamein shwasan ke madhyam se diye jane vaale corticosteroed or LABAs se asthma niyantrit nahin hota he bronchiel thermoplasty ek vikalp ho saktaa he. isamen bronacoscopy shrinkhalaaon ke dauraan, vayumarg diwaar par niyantrit tapiya urja pravaahit kii jaati he. jabaki yah pehle kuch maheenon main tivrata kii aavriti ko badha saktaa he lekin aisa lagata he ki baad kii dar main kami laata he. ek saal se adhik ke prabhaav agyaat hai.
 asthma se peedit bahut se log jaise jeern vikaar vaale log vaikalpik upachaaron kaa upyog karte hai; surveykshan darshaate hai ki lagbhag 50% log gairparmparik upachaar kaa upyog karte hai. aise adhikansh upachaaron kii prabhaavashiltaa ke samarthan main behad kam aankade uplabdh hai. vitamin si ke upyog kaa samarthan karne vaale sakshya bhi aparyaapt hai. acupanchar ko upachaar ke liye anushansit nahin kiya jaataa he kyonki iske upyog ke samarthan main aparyaapt sakshya uplabdh hai. air ynaiser bhi aise sakshya prastut nahin karte ki ve asthma ke lakshanon ko behatar karte hai yaa fefdon ke sankraman ko laabh pahunchaate hai; yah dhanatmak or rinatmak aayan jenerators par bhi samaan rup se lagu hota he.
 "minial upachaar" dwaara asthma ke upachaar ke samarthan main aparyaapt sakshya uplabdh hai, jinamein osteopathic, cyropractic, phisiotherapeutic or shwasaneey therapeutic yuktiyaan shaamil hai. ucch shwasan dar kaa niyantran karne ke liye beautico shwasan takanic dawa ke upyog main kami laa saktaa he haalanki iskaa fefdon ke prakaarya par koi prabhaav nahin padataa he. is prakaar ek visheshgy panel ko yah laga ki iske upyog ke liye paryaapt sakshya uplabdh nahin he.
 vishesh rup se halke rog se peedit bacchon main asthma ke rog kaa purvanumaan saamaanya rup se acha rahata he. behatar pahchaan tathaa dekhbhaal main sudhaar ke kaaran pichley kuch dashakon main mrityu dar main kami aayi he. vaishvik rup se 2004 main 19.4 million logon main isane madhyam yaa gambhir asamarthta paidaa kii thi . bachapan main pataa chale asthma ke aadhe maamle ek dashak ke baad nidaan nahin jari rahegaa. vayumarg kii sanrachana main punarrachana hote dekha gaya he lekin yah agyaat he ki ye laabhadaayak parivartanon kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai yaa hanikaarak parivartanon kaa.corticosteroed ke saath aarambhik upachaar, fefdon kii gatividhi main rokthaam yaa sudhaar karata dikhataa he.
 2011 main puri duniya main 235-300 million log asthma se peedit the, or lagbhag 2,50,000 log har saal is rog se marate hai. deshon ke bich isaki dar isaki upasthiti ke aadhaar par 1 se lekar 18% ke bich he. yah vikasshil deshon se adhik viksit deshon main aam he. is prakaar se isaki dar asia, purvi europe tathaa africa main kam dikhti he. viksit deshon main yah unamem adhik aam he jo aarthik rup se vanchit hai jabaki iske vipareet vikasshil deshon main yah samrudh logon main aam he. in bhinnataaon kaa kaaran acchhee tarah se gyaat nahin he. nimna yaa madhyam aay deshon main mrityu dar 80% tak hoti he.
 ladakiyon kii tulana main asthma, ladakon main dugani dar se aam he, lekin gambhir asthma dono main samaan rup se hota he. iske vipareet vayask mahilaon main asthma kii dar purushon se adhik hoti he tathaa yah bujurgon kii tulana main yuvaaon main adhik aam he.
 asathama kii vaishvik dar 1960 se 2008 ke bich mahatvapurn rup se badi thi 1970 se ise pramukh saarvajanik swasthya ke rup main manyata di gayi he. 1990 ke madhya se viksit deshon main asthma kii dar sthir ho gayi he or haal kii badhat mukhya rup se vikasshil duniya main aayi he. asthma america kii lagbhag 7% jansankhya tathaa united kingdom kii 5% jansankhya ko prabhavit karata he. canada, australia tathaa newzealand main yah pratishat lagbhag 14–15% he.
 asthma ko pracheen misr main pahachaanaa gaya tha or ise kaayphi naam ke ek sugandhitamishran ko pilaakar thik kiya jaataa tha. ise isa purv 450 main hippocrates dwaara aadhikarik rup se vishisht tarah kii shwasan sambandhi samasya ke rup main, greek shabd “panting” se namit kiya gaya tha jisane hamaare aadhunik naa kaa aadhaar prastut kiya. isa purv 200 main aisa vishwaas kiya jaataa tha ki yah aansik rup se hi sahi lekin bhaawanaaon se judaa rog he.

 tirchhe akshar
 dama ek gambhir beemaari he, jo shwaas nalikaaon ko prabhavit karti he. shwaas nalikaayen fefde se hawa ko andar-baahar karti hai. dama hone par in nalikaaon kii bhitari diwaar main sujan hota he. yah sujan nalikaaon ko behad samvedansheel bana detaa he or kisi bhi baichaiyn karanevaali cheez ke sparsh se yah tikhi pratikriya karata he. jab nalikaayen pratikriya karti hai, to unamem sankuchan hota he or us sthiti main fefde main hawa kii kam maatra jaati he. isse khaansee, naak bajnaa, chaati kaa kadaa hona, raat or subah main saans lene main takalif aadi jaise lakshan paidaa hote hai.
 dama ko thik nahin kiya ja saktaa, lekin is par niyantran paaya ja saktaa he, taaki dame se peedit vyakti saamaanya jivan vyateet kar sake. dame kaa daura padane se shwaas nalikaayen puri tarah band ho sakati hai, jisase sharir ke mahatvapurn angon ko oxygen kii aapurti band ho sakati he. yah chikitsakeey rup se apaat sthiti he. dame ke daure se marij kii maut bhi ho sakati he.
 dama yaa dama ek athwa ek se adhik padaarthon ke prati shaareerik pranali kii asweekruti he. iskaa arth he ki hamaare sharir kii pranali un vishesh padaarthon ko sahan nahin kar paati or jis rup main apni pratikriya yaa virodh prakat karti he, use allergy kehete hai. hamaari shwasan pranali jab kinhin allergence ke prati allergy prakat karti he to vah dama hota he. yah saans sambandhi rogon main sabase adhik kshtdaayi he. dama ke rogi ko saans phoolne yaa saans na aane ke daure baar-baar padate hai or un dauron ke bich vah akasar puri tarah saamaanya bhi ho jaataa he.
 asthma unani shabd he, jiskaa arth he - 'jaldi-jaldi saans lenaa' yaa 'saans lene ke liye jor lagaana'. jab kisi vyakti ko dama kaa daura padataa he to vah saamaanya saans ke liye bhi gahari-gahari yaa lambii-lambii saans letaa he; naak se lee gai saans kam padati he to munh kholkar saans letaa he. vastav main rogi ko saans lene kii bajaay saans baahar nikaalane main zyada kathinaayee hoti he, kyonki fefde ke bheetar kii choti-choti vaayu naliyaan jakad jaati hai or dooshit vaayu ko baahar nikaalane ke liye unhen jitna sikudna chahie utana ve nahin sikud paatin. parinamswaroop rogi ke fefde phool jate hai, kyonki rogi agali saans bheetar khinchne se pehle khinchi hui saans kii hawa ko thik se baahar nahin nikaal paata.
 dama yaa to dhire-dhire ubharta he athwa ekaek bhadakta he. jab dama yaa dama ekaek bhadakta he to usase pehle khaansi kaa daura hota he, kintu jab dama dhire-dhire ubharta he to usase pehle aamtaur par shwaas pranali main sankraman ho jaaya karata he. dama kaa daura jab tej hota he to dil kii dhadkan or saans lene kii raftaar donon bad jaati hai tathaa rogi baichaiyn va thakaa hua mahasoos karata he. use khaansi aa sakati he, seene main jakadan mahasoos ho sakati he, bahut adhik pasinaa aa saktaa he or ulti bhi ho sakati he. dame ke daure ke samay seene se aanevaali saany-saany kii aawaaj tang shwaas naliyon ke bheetar se hawa baahar nikalne ke kaaran aati he. dama ke sabhi rogiyon ko raat ke samay, khaskar sote hue, zyada kathinaayee mahasoos hoti he.
 
 dama kai kaarnon se ho saktaa he. anek logon main yah allergy mausam, khaadya padaarth, davaaiyaan itr, parafum jaisi khushboo or kuch anya prakaar ke padaarthon se ho saktaa hai; kuch log rui ke baarik reshe, aate kii dhul, kaagaj kii dhul, kuch phulon ke paraag, pashuon ke bal, faphund or cockroaj jaise keede ke prati allergit hote hai. jin khaadya padaarthon se aamtaur par allergy hoti he unamem gehun, aataa doodh, chocolate, beans kii faliyaan, aalu, boar or gaay kaa maance ityadi shaamil hai. kuch anya logon ke sharir kaa rasayan asaamaanya hota he, jisamen unke sharir ke anzime yaa fefdon ke bheetar mansapeshiyon kii doshapurn prakriya shaamil hoti he. anek baar dama allergic or gaiyr-allergywali sthitiyon ke mail se bhadakta he, jisamen bhaavnaatmak dabaav, vaayu pradooshan, vibhinn sankraman or anuvanshik kaaran shaamil hai. ek anumaan ke anusaar, jab mata-pita donon ko dama yaa he fever hota he to aise 75 se 100 pratishat mata-pita ke bacchon main bhi allergy kii sambhaavnaayen pai jaati hai.
 dame ke kuch kaaran is prakaar hai-
 dama ke prakritik upachaar main nimnlikhit upaay kiye ja sakate hai-
 susti yaa kamjor nirgaman angon ko bal pradaan karnaa. rogi padaarthon ko sharir se baahar nikaalane ke liye samuchit aahaar kaarykram apnaanaa. sharir kaa punarniman karnaa athwa sharir kii kamajori ko dur karnaa. yogasana or pranayam kaa abhyaas karnaa, taaki bhojan thik tarah se hajam hokar sharir ko lage. fefdon ko bal mili, unamem lacheelaapan aae, pachan kriya sudhare or tej ho tathaa shwaas pranali balashaali ho. rogi ko enema dekar uski aanton kii safai karnaa chahie, taaki unke bheetar visangati na punap sake. pet par geeli pattee rakhane se bina pache hue khaadya padaarth sad nahin paayenge or aanton kii kriya tej hone ke kaaran, jaldi hi pani main bhigaa kapadaa rakhane se fefdon kii jakadan kam hoti he or unhen bal miltaa he. rogi ko bhaap, snaan karakar uskaa pasinaa bahaya ja saktaa he. iske atirikt use garam pani main kulhon tak yaa paanv daalkar baithaaya ja saktaa he or dhoop snaan bhi karavaaya ja saktaa he. isse twachaa uttejit hogi or use bal milegaa tathaa fefdon kii jakadan dur hogi.
 apane sharir kii pranali ko poshak tatva pradaan karne ke liye or hanikaarak tatva baahar nikaalane ke liye rogi ko kuch din tak taaje falon kaa ras hi lenaa chahie or kuch nahin. is upachaar ke dauraan use taaja falon ke ek gilaas ras main utana hi pani milakar do-do ghante ke baad subah aath baje se shaam aath baje tak lenaa chahie. baad main dhire-dhire thos padaarth bhi shaamil kiye ja sakate hai. kintu rogi ko saamaanya kism kii bhojan sambandhi galatiyon se dur rahanaa chahie. yadi uske aahaar main carbohydrate chiknayee evam protein jaise tejaab banane vaale padaarth simit maatra main rahen or taaje fal, hari subjiyan tathaa ankurit chane jaise kshareey khaadya padaarth bharpoor maatra main rahen to sabase acha rahata he.
 chaaval, shakkar, til or dahi jaise kaph yaa balgam banane vaale padaarth tathaa tale hue evam garisht khaadya padaarth na hi khaae jaaen to acha he.
 dama ke rogiyon ko apni kshamta se kam hi khaanaa chahie. unhen dhire-dhire or apane bhojan ko chaba-chabaakar khaanaa chahie. unhen pratidin kam se kam aath se das gilaas pani pinaa chahie. bhojan ke saath pani yaa kisi tarah kaa taral padaarth lene se parhez karnaa chahie. tej masaale, mirch achaar bahut adhik chaay coffee ityadi se bhi dur rahanaa chahie.
 dama, visheshkar tej dame kaa daura, hajme ko kharaab karata he. aise maamalon main rogi par khaane ke liye jor mat dijie, aise maamalon main jab tak dame kaa daura dur na ho jaae tab tak rogi ko lagbhag upwas karne dijie. rogi har do ghante ke baad ek pyaalaa garam pani pi saktaa he. aise maamle main yadi rogi enema letaa he to use bahut phayda hota he.
