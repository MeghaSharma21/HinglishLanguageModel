 yuiefee europa lig /juːˈeɪfə jʊˈroʊpə ˈliːɡ/, yah pehle yuiefee cap /juːˈeɪfə ˈkʌp/ namit kiya gaya tha, ek vaarshik purushon ke footbal club pratiyogita he, yogya europea footbal club ke liye jo 1971 ke baad se yuiefee dwaara yojit kiya jaataa he. club apni rashtriya lig or cap pratiyogitaaon main unke pradarshan ke aadhaar par pratiyogita liye arhata praapt karte he.
 isse pehle yah yuiefee cap bulaayaa jaataa tha, praaroop main ek parivartan ke baad pratiyogita 2009-10 satr ke baad yuiefee europa lig ke rup main janaa gaya he. yuiefee footbal record prayojanon ke liye, yuiefee cap or yuiefee europa lig, ek hi pratiyogita maane jate he.
 1999 main, yuiefee cap vinurs cap ko samaapt kar diya gaya tha or yuiefee cap ke saath vilay kar diya gaya. 2004-05 pratiyogita ke liye ek group charan nokout charan se pehle joda gaya. 2009 punah branding me yuiefee intertoto cap ke saath ek vilay shaamil tha, ek bade hue pratiyogita praaroop kaa utpaadan hua, saath hi ek vistaarit group charan or paatrata maandand badal. yuiefee europa lig ke vijeta yuiefee super cap ke liye uteern hote he or 2015-2016 ke satr ke baad se pichley satr ke yuiefee europa lig ke vijetaon yuiefee champions lig ke liye arhata praapt karenge.
 khitaab 28 vibhinn klbon dwaara jeeta gaya he or jinamein se 12 ek baar se adhik khitaab jeeta he. sevilla 5 khitaab ke saath pratiyogita main sabase safal club hai. angrezi paksh manchester united maujuuda champion hai, ve 2017 final main ajaks ko 2–0 se haraa diya tha.
 yuiefee cap, inter shaharon fares cap ko pratisthapit, jo 1955 or 1971 ke bich khele gaye ek europea footbal pratiyogita thi. pratiyogita ke pehle saal ke dauraan 11 tiimon se badi or 1970-71 main khelaa gaya, antim toornament se 64 tiimon tak pahunch gayi. yah europea footbal drishy par itnaa mahatvapurn ban gaya tha kii ant main yah yuiefee dwaara liya gaya or agle satr main yuiefee cap ke rup me phir se shuru kiya gaya. yuiefee cap pehali baar 1971-72 satr main khelaa gaya, final angreji tiimon wolverhampton wanderrs banaam totenham hotspur ke bich tha, final hotspur jeeta tha. 
 pratiyogita paramparaagat gharelu lig ke upavijeta ke liye khuli thi, toornament cap vijeta cap ke saath vilay kar diya gaya the ke baad se gharelu cap pratiyogitaaon ke vijetaon ko bhi yuiefee cap main pravesh kiya gaya. 
 2009-10 satr ke baad se, pratiyogita yuiefee europa lig ke rup main janaa gaya he. isi samay, yuiede intertoto cap, yuiede tisare stareey pratiyogita, band kar di gai or nae europa lig main vilay kar diya.
 chaar tiimon ko ek hi satr main yuiefee cap or unke gharelu lig or cap pratiyogitaaon main jeeta he, 1982 main iefasi gotaborg, 2000 main galtasarai, 2003 or 2011 main porto or 2005 main sieskee masco. iske atirikt, totenham hotspur, borashia maunchengladbakh, iefasi gotaborg, ajaks, galtasarai or fenurd apane abhiyaan main ek bhi haar ke bina cap jeeta he.
 pratiyogita kaa gaan yohann zveig dwaara rachit he or 2009 ke shuru main paris opera dwaara darj kii gai thi. punah branding yuiefee cap pratiyogita ke liye theme pehla aadhikarik taur par 2009-10 satr ke group charan ke dra se pehle 28 agast 2009 ko greemaldi forum main anaavaran kiya gaya tha. gaan mejabaan stadium main har europa lig match se pehle bajaanaa janaa chahie.
 pratiyogita ke liye yogyata ke yuiefee gunank ke aadhaar par kiya jaataa he, adhik safal rashtron ke liye behatar pravesh dwaar daur diya jaataa he. vyavahaaratah, prayek sangh ek maanak teen sthaan he, sivaay
 aamtaur par, sthanon europe kii sheersh stareey lig or mukhya cap pratiyogitaaon ke vijetaon main vibhinn upavijeta sthanon main samaapt karne vaale tiimon ko sammanit kiya jaataa he. kuch deshon main madhyamik cap pratiyogitaaon he lekin vartmaan main apane madhyamik cap vijetaon ko ek yuiefee europa lig jagah anudaan keval england or france karte hai.
 adhik teen berth yuiefee fere play talika main ek nishchit str se upar samaapt karne vaale mahasanghon ko diya jaataa he. unki reating kam se kam 8.0 he to sheersh teen mahasanghon swatah ek fere play pravishti praapt karte hai. sthaan, ki desh ke sheersh lig kii fere play talika main sarvocch sthaan paane vaali team ko jaataa he jo europe ke liye yogya nahin he. 
 
 2009-10 satr se, pratiyogita kii chhavi ko badhaane ke liye ek prayaas main yuiefee europa lig ke rup main punah brands kiya gaya. saath hi pratiyogita kaa naam badla, ek atirikt 16 tiimon ko ab pratiyogita ke mukhya charan ke liye kwalifai hote he, group charan ke ab chaar tiimon main se 12 samoohon se milakar he, prayek samooh main sheersh do rakhaa tiimon ke agle daur ke liye pragati hote he. final may ke maheene main khelaa jaataa he, aam taur par buudhvaar ko or yuiefee champions lig ke final se kam se kam 10 dinon pehle. 
 toornament ke liye kwalifai karne ke liye ek or rasta he, gharelu cap pratiyogita ke vijeta pehle se hi yuiefee champions lig ke liye kwalifai kiya he, to gharelu cap pratiyogita main haari hui team yuiefee europa lig ke liye arhata praapt karti he.
 yuiefee champions lig ke prakaar, klbon dwaara praapt kii puraskaar raashi nirdhaarit bhugtan main vibhajit he, bhagidaari or parinaam ke aadhaar par or apane prasaaran ke adhikaar ke mulya pe.
 europa lig main group charan bhagidaari € 1.3 karod kii ek aadhaar shulk puraskaar he. samooh main ek jeet € 200,000 or ek drow € 100,000 kaa bhugtan karti he. iske alaava, prayek samooh ke vijeta € 400,000 or prayek upavijeta € 200,000 kamata he. naak out charan tak pahunchne ke atirikt bonus he. 
 pratiyogita ek vyapak televijan darshakon ko aakarshith karti he, na sirf europe main, bal kii duniya bhar main. yah toornament europe main doosraa sabase mahatvapurn club footbal toornament he. jisamen kabhi kabhi aamtaur par yuiefee champions lig main khilne vaale club bhi arhata hote he, jaise 2007-08 ke satr main bern munich, 2008-09 satr main milan, 2009-10 satr main liverpool, 2010-11 ke satr main manchester city, 2011-12 ke satr main manchester united or 2012-13 ke satr main chelsia kii tarah bade or lokapriy club, aise klbon baari maatra me televijan darshakon ko aakarshith karte he. tivi channel prasaaran ke liye, is tarah ke ucch profil ke maichon ke liye badi rakam kaa bhugtan karte he.
