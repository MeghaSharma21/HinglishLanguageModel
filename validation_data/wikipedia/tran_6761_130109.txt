 sunda jalsandhi indonesia ke sumatra dweep or java dweep ke bich kii jalsandhi he. yah java sagar ko hind mahaasaagar se jodatii he. iskaa naam pashchim java dweep ke sunda samudaay par padaa he. yah apane nunatam chaudaai par keval 24 kimi he or isamen kai chote tapu va dweep sthit hai. yah apane pashchimi chor par bahut gahari he lekin iske purvi ant par gaharaai keval 20 meter tak aa jaati he. is kam gaharaai or samudr sataha se neeche kai retile tilon kii upasthiti se yah badi naukaaon ke liye bahut khataranaak mana jaataa he. is kaaran se samudri jahaaz is jalsandhi kii bajaay malakka jalsandhi kaa maarg apanaate hai.
