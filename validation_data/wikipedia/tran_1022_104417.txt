 ram, viski, chuliya, mahua, brandi, jin, bear, handiya, aadi sabhi ek he kyonki sabamein alcohol hota he. haan, inamen alcohal kii maatra or nasha laane ki apekshit kshamta alag-alag jarur hoti he parantu sabhi ko ham 'sharaab' hi kehete he. kabhi-kabhi log hadiya yaa bear ko sharaab se alag samajhate hai jo ki bilkul galat he. donon main alcohol to hota hi he. 
 sharaab aksar hamaare samaaj main anand ke liye pi jaati he. zyaadaatar shuruuaat doston ke prabhaav yaa dabaav ke kaaran hota he or baad main bhi kai anya kaarnon se log iskaa seven jari rakhte he. jaise- boriyat mitaane ke liye, khushi manaane ke liye, avsaad main, chintaa main, teevr krodh yaa aaveg aane par, atmavishwaas laane ke liye yaa mood banane ke liye aadi. iske atirikt sharaab ke seven ko kai samaaj main dharmik va anya saamaajik anushthanon se bhi joda jaataa he. parantu koi bhi samaaj yaa dharm iske durupyog kii sweekruti nahin detaa he.
 sharaab ke lagaataar seven se kuch vishesh lakshan dikhaai dene lagate hai, jiske aadhaar par bhi iske aadi hone ko pahachaanaa ja saktaa he. jaise- 
 jab log sharaab kaa seven jari rakhte he to dhire-dhire aisi aadat ban jaati he ki use chhod paanaa mushkil ho jaataa he. vah vyakti ke jivan kaa abhinn ang ban jaataa he. chhodane kii koshish karne par naanaa prakaar ke shaareerik va maansik pareshaaniyaan hoti he or vyakti iskaa lagaataar seven karne ke liye baadhya ho jaataa he. 
 jab vyakti lagaataar sharaab pine lagata he tab samasyaon kii nirantar badhati hui sthitiyaan aati he tathaa shaareerik samasyaayen vishesh rup se pet kii bimaariyaan, yakrut kii beemaari visheshtah serosis, snaayu tantra kii kamajoriyaan, cancer aadi. yadi aadaton kaa shikanja bahut majboot ho chukaa he to aap apane aap se chaar sawaal puuchhen- 
 yadi inamen se do sawaal ke bhi uttar haan main he to aap sharaab ke aadi ho chuke he or aapako turant chikitsa vyavastha karwaani chahie varanaa aapkaa jivan kharaab ho saktaa he. 
 sharaab sharir ke lagbhag sabhi ango par apana bura prabhaav chodta he ar sharir kaa shaayad hi koi ang iske dushprabhav se vanchit rahata he. sharaab se pet sambandhi bimariyaan jaise- apach, pet ke dhav, yakrut kii beemaari jaise-serosis, liver kaa puri tarah se kshatigrast hona, snaayu tantra kii kamajoriyaan, hriday sambandhi rog visheshtah raktchaap, yadast kii beemaari, cancer aadi. is tarah se ham dekhte he ki sharir to kharaab hota hi he, mastisk kii koshikaaye bhi marane lagati he. maansik rog utpann hote he tathaa vyakti main parivartan aa jaataa he. 
 vyakti ke jivan par anya prabhaav sharaab vyakti ke jivan main kai staron par apana prabhaav daalati he. jaise-maansik str par uttejnaa, chidchidapan, udaasi, aadi. vyavsaayik kshetra main kaary dakshata or kshamta main giravat. saamaajik str par dhire-dhire samaajik gatividhiyon se vimukh hona or dusro kii najar main girnaa aadi. 
 sabase pehle sharaab pine vaale khud yah tay kare ki ab main sharaab nahi piunga to chikitsak inaki madad kar sakate he. dekha jaataa he ki parivaar vaale to unke ilaaj ke liye taiyaar rahete he kintu vyakti swayan ilaaj nahin karana chahta. aisi haalat main chikitsak kaa prayaas sarthak ho hi nahin saktaa. 
 manashchikitsa kendron main nasha vimukti kendra hote he jahaan di-toxification dwaara sharaab chhudaane tathaa uske uparaant motiveshan therapy, physiotherapy tathaa group therapy dwaara isse nijaat paane kii koshish kii jaati he. swayan vyakti ke prabal ichhashakti tathaa parivaar ke sahyog tathaa chikitsakon ke satat prayaas se safalataa purvak iskaa ilaaj sambhav he.
