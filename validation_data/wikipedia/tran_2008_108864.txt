 swaroop ke aadhaar par kavy ke do bhed hai - 
 shravy kavy- jis kavy kaa rasaswadan duusare se sunakar yaa swayan pard kar kiya jaataa he use shravy kavy kehete hai. jaise ramayana or mahabharat.shravy kavy ke bhi do bhed hote hai - 
 prabandh kavy-isamen koi pramukh katha kavy ke aadi se ant tak krambaddh rup main chalati he. katha kaa kram bich main kahin nahin tuutata or gaun kathaayen bich-bich main sahaayak ban kar aati hai. jaise ramcharita maanas.prabandh kavy ke do bhed hote hai -
 1- mahakavy isamen kisi aitihaasik yaa puranic mahapurush kii sampurna jivan katha kaa adyopant varnan hota he.2- khandakavy isamen kisi kii sampurna jeevanakatha kaa varnan na hokar keval jivan ke kisi ek hi bhaag kaa varnan hota he.
