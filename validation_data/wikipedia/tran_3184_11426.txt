 upabhokta jaagrukta ek upabhokta ke rup main apane adhikaron ke kisi vyakti, samaj uplabdh utpaadon or sevaaon ke vipanan kiya ja rahaa he or bechaa ke vishay main ek upabhokta ke rup main apane adhikaron ke kisi vyakti dwaara kiya ja rahaa he. is avadharana kii chaar shreniyon ke sahit suraksha, pasand, jaankaree, or suna jane kaa adhikaar shaamil he. upabhokta adhikaron kii ghoshnaa sabase pehle america main 1962 main sthapit kii gayi thi. upabhokta jaagrukta ke kaaryakarta ralf nader he. unhe upbhok tathaa andolan ke pita ke rup main sanderbhit kiya jaataa he. poonjeevaad or vaishvikaran ke is yug main, apane laabh ko adhiktam karnaa prayek nirmaataa kaa mukhya uddeshy he. har sambhav tarah se yah nirmaataa apane utpaadon kii bikri ko badhaane ke liye prayaas kar rahe hai. isliye, apane uddeshy kii purti ke liye ve upabhokta ke hit ko bhul jate he or apane udhaaharan ke liye zyada kiraaya, vajani, milavati or garib gunvattta kii vas tuon kii bikri, jhuthe vigyaapan aadi dekar upabhoktaaon ko gumraah karne ke tahat shoshan karte rehate he. is tarah ke dhokhe se khud ko bachaane ke liye, upabhokta ko chaukas rehane kii aavashyaktaa he. is tarah se, upabhokta jaagrukta kaa matlab he kii upabhokta apane adhikaron or kartavyon ke prati jaagrukta rakhte he.
 yah aksar dekha jaataa he ki ek upabhokta sahi maal or sevaayen praapt nahin karata he. vah ek bahut hi ucch mulya charge kiya jaataa he yaa milavati yaa kam gunvattta par saamaan ko kharidta he. isliye yah dhokhaa uske bare main pataa karne ke liye aavashyak he. nimnlikhit tathyon ko vargeekrut upabhoktaaon ko jaagruk banane kii jarurat he:
 bharat main upabhokta sangrakshan kii avadharana nai nahin he. upabhokta kii ruchi vyapaar or udyog, kam vajan or maap, milawat or in aparaadhon ke liye dand dwaara shoshan ke khilaaf kii raksha ke sandarbh kautilya ke ' arthshaastra ' me kiye gaye he. haalaanki, upabhoktaaon, ke hit kii raksha karne ke liye ek sangathit or vyavasthit andolan ek haal kii hi ghatana he.
 upabhoktaaon ko na sirf bikri or kharid vastuon kii he, lekin swasthya or suraksha pahaluon kii vanijyik pahaluon kaa pataa hona karnaa bhi he. khaadya suraksha ke in dinon upabhokta jaagrukta kaa ek mahatvapurn tatv ban gaya he. khaadya utpaadon ke maamle main, isaki gunvattta na keval apni poshan mulya par, lekin yah bhi maanav upbhog ke liye isaki suraksha par nirbhar karata he. yah sunishchit karen ki nirmaataaon or vikritaon ekarupata or keemten, stox or unke maal kii gunvattta main paardarshita kaa nirikshan karne ke liye majboot kanoonee upayon ke liye bulaayaa gaya he. 
 upabhokta sangrakshan adhinium, 1986 ke ek adhinium bharat kii sansad main 1986 adhiniyamit bharat main upabhoktaaon ke hiton kii raksha karne kaa he. yah upabhokta parishadon or anya adhikariyon ne upabhoktaaon ko vivaadon ke niptaan ke liye or shaq maamalon ke liye kii sthapana ke liye praavdhaan banaata he.
