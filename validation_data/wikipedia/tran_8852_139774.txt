 chingei-tibet railmarg chini janavaadee ganraajya main ek railmarg he jo chingei praant main sthit shining ko tibet kii rajdhani lhasa se jodta he. yah railmarg tibet ko shesh china se jodne vaala pehla railmarg he, kyonki tibet ke bahut oonchaai par sthit hone ke kaaran yahan kisi bhi railmarg kaa nirmaan nahin ho sakaa tha. beijing, chengdu, chongking, shining, shanghai or lanjhou se lhasa ke liye railgadiyon kii sevaayen uplabdh hai.
 is railmarg kii kul lambaai 1,956 kimi he. shining se golmud tak ke 815 kimi lambe railmarg kaa nirmaan 1984 tak puraa ho chukaa tha. shesh 1,142 kimi lambe bhaag, jo golmud se lhasa tak kaa he, kaa udghaatan 1 july 2006 ko party aam sachiv hu jintaao dwaara kiya gaya tha.yah railmarg tangula darre se hokar bhi gujarata he jo 5,072 meter kii oonchaai par vishwa kaa sabase unchaa railmarg he. tangula railway stesan jo 5,068 meter kii oonchaai par sthit he vishwa kaa sabase unchaa railway stesan he.
 1,338 meter lambi fenghuoshan surang 4,905 meter kii oonchaai par sthit he or vishwa kii sabase unchi rail surang he. 4,010 meter kii oonchaai par sthit guanjiao surang shining or golmud ke madhya sabase lambi surang he or 3,345 meter kii oonchaai par sthit yangbaging surang golmud or lhasa ke madhya sabase lambi surang he. golmud-lhasa anubhag kaa 960 kimi yaa lagbhag 80% bhaag 4,000 meter se adhik kii oonchaai par he. kul 675 pul jinaki lambaai 159.88 kimi he or 550 kimi railmarg sthai-tushaar bhoomi par bichaaya gaya he.
 is railmarg par golmud se lhasa tak ke stesan is prakaar hai:golmud - nanshankou - ganlong - nachitaai - yujhufeng - shiaonanchuan - vankun - budongkuan - chumaayerhe - vudaoliyang - zishuihe - jiankedong - riyakchi - tuotuohe - tongtiyanhe - yanshipping - bukiyange - tangula -jhajiazangbu - tuoju - amdo - kuonahu - divooma - gangziu - naaku - tuoru - gulu - damzung - voomatang - daakionguo - yangbajing - maziyang- lhasa pashchim - lhasa
 golmud se lhasa upbhaag par 45 stesan hai jinamein se 38 par koi karyrat nahin he or jinaki nigaraanee shining sthit niyantran kaksh se kii jaati he. teraa or stesan yojanadhin hai.
