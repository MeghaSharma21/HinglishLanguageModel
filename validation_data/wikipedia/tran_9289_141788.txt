 rashtriya dairy vikaas board, bhartiya sansad ke ek adhinium dwaara sthapit rashtriya mahatv kaa santhaan he. iskaa mukhyalay gujarat ke anand shahar main he tathaa kshetreey karyaalay desh ke vibhinn nagaron main faile hue hai. 
 rashtriya dairy vikaas board kii sthapana utpaadakon ke swaamitv or unke dwaara niyantrit sanghathanon ko protsaahit karne or unhen aarthik sahaayta dene ke uddeshy se kii gai thi. rashtriya dairy vikaas board ke kaarykram or gatividhiyon kaa uddeshy krishak sahakaari sansthaaon ko sudrudh karnaa tathaa un rashtriya nitiyon kaa samarthan karnaa he jo aisi sansthaaon ke vikaas keanukool hai.
