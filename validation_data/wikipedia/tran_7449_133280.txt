 garbhanaliyaan yaa dimbavahiniyaan garbhashay ke upree bhaag ke donon ore se nikalati he tathaa donon taraf kuulhe kii hadidyon tak jaati he. inaki lambaai lagbhag 10 semi or motaai lagbhag aadha semi tak lambi hoti he. donon ore iskaa aakaar ek keep kii tarah kaa hota he. is keep kaa antim chor lambi-lambi anguliyon kii taraf hota he jisko tantrikaayen kehete he. inkaa pramukh kaary dembugranthiyon se nikle andey ko gherkar use vahiniyon me bhejnaa hota he. yah naliyaan mansapeshiyon se bani, tathaa inke andar kii diwaar ek jhilli kii bani hoti he jisko mukas jhilli kehete he.
 dembugranthiyon se pakade andey, vahiniyon ke aage ke bhaag main jaakar rukte he. jahaan ye purush ke shukranu ke saath milakar ek naye jivan kaa nirmaan hota he. stri janan ang main is sanrachana ko jaigot kehete he. jaigot ke chaaron taraf ek khaas parat utpann hoti he.
