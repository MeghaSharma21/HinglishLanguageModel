 baalchikitsa yaa ballrog vigyaan chikitsa vigyaan kii vah shakha he jo shishuon, baalo evam kishoron ke rogon evam unki chiktsa se sambandhit he. aayu kii drushti se is shrenee main navjaat shishu se lekar 12 se 21 varsh ke kishor tak aa jate hain. is shrenee ke umr kii upari seemaa ek desh se duusare desh main alag-alag he.
 ballrog vigyaan yaa comarbhrity ko bhartiya chikitsak isa se 600 varsh purv ayurveda ke ashtaangon main ek mahatvapurn ang ke rup main maanate the. comarbhrity ke antargat prasutitantr, streerogvigyaan tathaa ballrog vigyaan aate the. is vaigyanik yug main vigyaan main krantikari pragati ke saath saath chikitsashastra ke gyanbhandar ke ativardhit hone se ye teenon shaastra pruthak-pruthak mahatvapurn ho gaye hai. comarbhrity vishay par swatantra aarsh granth keval kaashyapasanhita hi uplabdh hua he. is granth kaa pratisanskarta vruddhijeevak, jo comartantr kaa visheshgy mana jaataa tha, shalya visheshgy jeevak se nitant bhinn he. comarbhrity ke antargat kumaar kaa poshan, rakshan, uski paricharika yaa dhatri, dugdh yaa aahaar janya vikaar, shaareerik vikrutiyaan, grihjanya baadha evam aupsargik rog tathaa aguntak rogon kaa vivaran evam chikitsa varnit hai. isi ke antargat balswasthya kaa varnan uplabdh hota he.
 yadi aadhunik chikitsapaddati ke itihaas kaa avlokan kiya jaae, to gyaat hota he ki ballrog vigyaan naamak koi swatantra shaastra 19veen shatabdi ke ant tak nahin tha tathaa balak, yuvak kaa hi laghurup mana jaataa tha. sarvapratham 1899 i. main kings kalege chikitsaalay, landan, main ballrog visheshgy pruthak rakhaa gaya. is samay shishuon kii mrityu dar 20% se 40% tak pahunch chuki thi. 20veen shatabdi main krantikari anusandhanon, paryaapt adhyayan evam janaswasthya ke siddhanton kii sahaayta se shishu-mrityu-dar pehle se 10 pratishat kam hone lagi. iske pashchaat bhi vaigyanikon ko santosh nahin hua he or ve mrityu dar ko kam karne ke upayon ke anusandhan main lage hue hai. aadhunik chikitsak balak kii vruddhi evam vikaas ko ek yuva purush se bhinn maanate hai or kumaar ko sharirarachana vigyaan, sharirkriya vigyaan, maanas vigyaan evam rog kshamta ke drushtikon se yuva se bhinn maanate hai. balak kii sharirkriya main lagaataar parivartan hote rahete hai, jo uske swasthya ke liye atyant anukool evam aavashyak hai. iske saath-saath swasthya vigyaan, poshan vigyaan, rogakshmata vigyaan, bhrun vigyaan, sookshmjeev vigyaan, mahamari vigyaan evam swatchta vigyaan ke sambandh main ho rahe anusandhanon se chikitsakshetra main badi unnati hui hai. naveen aushadhiyon kii khoj se, nidaan ke tareekon main hue parivartanon se, raschikitsa tathaa kumaar shalyavigyaan ke dwaara vyaadhiyon par paryaapt vijay praapt kar lee gai he. in samast kaarnon se comarbhrity, yaa comartantr, aajkal ek vishesh vigyaan mana jane laga he.
 shishuon, baalkon or kumaron main jo rog utpann hote hai, unhen kaaran ke anusaar, athwa jis santhaan vishesh kaa aashray grahan kar utpann hote hai tadnusaar, vargeekrut kiya jaataa he. ye rog baalkon kii vruddhi par prabhaav daalate hai. at: un kaarnon kaa jo garbhaadhan se lekar purna abhivruddhi tak prabhaavasheel hote hai, adhyayan is shaastra ke antargat aata he; udaahranarth, anuvanshikta, garbhini rog evam poshan tathaa prasavajnya rog.
 balrogon kaa vargeekaran evam vivaran nimnlikhit he :
 uparyukt kaarnon se utpann honevaale mukhya rog haemophilia, garbhaj raktnal koshikaprasu rog, paariwarik saavadhik angaghaat tathaa mastishk vikaar evam allergy rog, jaise ekjima or shvasanigat shwaas rog aadi hai1
 balak mata ke garbh main rahete hue mata-pita ke rogon se grasit ho jaataa he, jaise firang. itani hi nahin, vyaadhiyon se garbh kii thik vruddhi nahin hoti or kuch vikrutiyaan paidaa ho jaati hai jaise : sahaj motiyobind, hatvikrut rachana tathaa viklaangata.
 shwasavarodh, mastishk raktasrav, mriduasthibhagn tathaa peshighaat prasavkaal main honevaale mukhya rog hai. ye rog prasavkaal main shishu ke liye ghaatak ho jate hai yaa avaruddh maansik vruddhi, mirgee tathaa mastishk ghaat aadi updravon ko paidaa karte hai.
 bacchon kii vruddhi ke liye evam swatchta ke liye poshak aahaar atyant aavashyak he. yah balak kii lambaai, aakaar, vajan tathaa vay par nirbhar karata he. poshak aahaar main protein, aavashyak emino acid, vasa, carbohydrate, vitamin, jal tathaa khanij dravya atyant aavashyak hai.
 protein kii kami se sharir kii vruddhi, rakt protein kaa nirmaan tathaa nai vastuon kaa nirmaan ruk jaataa he. carbohydrate kii kami se sharir main kaam karne kii shakti ghat jaati he. khanij dravyon kii kami se asthi kaa nirmaan, hormonon kaa nirmaan, anzimo kaa nirmaan, shareeravriddhi, raktaranjan tathaa anya rasinic kriyaayein avaruddh ho jaati hai. rakt tathaa sharir ke dravon kaa kshar-amla-santulan bigadane se atisar, vrikk rog, vaman rog vaman evam kamajori aadi rog hote hai. is prakaar vitamin e kii kami se tvak shushkta, ratryandhata hoti he. vitamin bi kii kami se kai rog hote hai. vitamini bi ke kai prabhed hai. thymin kii kami se beri beri rog, riboflavin kii kami se munh or aanton main vran tathaa nicotinic amla kii kami se raktavahiniyon ke rog hote hai. piridixine vaman roktaa he. calcium pantothenet kii kami se haath, pair main jalan hoti he tathaa niacin kii kami se pelegra rog hota he. vitamin di kii kami se ricket hota he. vitamin si kii kami se scurvy rog hota he. vitamin 'ke' kii kami se rakthasravi rog ho jaataa he. yadi bhojan main doodh, maance, andey, machhlee, falras, hari subjiyan tathaa lavan hon jalheenta na ho, to vitamin kii kami se honevaale rog nahin hote. jal paryaapt maatra main milane par tvak shushkta, pyaas, ant:sraavon kii utpatti main avrodh tathaa raktaparisancharan main baadha nahin ho paati.
 bacchon main kuch vaikarik jeevanu tathaa paraseete krimiyon ke kaaran bhi rog utpann hote hai, jinhen aupsargik rog kehete hai. ye rog nimnlikhit virgon main vibhajit kiye ja sakate hai :
 k. jeevanujnya rog, kha. vishaanujnya rog, ga. ricketsial rog, gha. micotic rog tathaa c. parajeevijnya rog.
 mukhyat: sankramak rogon main masoorika, karnpher, kukurakhaansi, rohinee, scarlett fever, shaishavik angaghaat, chechak, chikanpox, aankh dukhana, kaan bahna aadi aate hai. inamen kuch jeevanuon se tathaa kuch vishaanuon se utpann hote hai. titanas, neumonia tathaa kuch rogon ko, jaise diphtheria yaa rohinee, whooping kaph, small pox aadi ko tike dwaara roka ja saktaa he. in rogon kii chikitsa inke pratijeevvish, pratijaiviki, toxoids, manvik gamma globin aadi se kii jaati he. titanas, pratiserum se roka ja saktaa he.
 baalyavastha main shwasan santhaan main honevaale rog nimnlikhit hote hai : sardi-zukam, shaishavik vishaanuj neumonia, influenza tathaa atipical neumonia. ye sab rog vishesh virus se utpann hote hai. inke atirikt bacterial neumonia bhayanak ballrog he, parantu aadhunik sulpha oshadhiyon tathaa pratijaiviki se parajeet kar liya gaya he; baalkon main yakshma bhi hota he. yah bi.si.ji. ke tike evam achche poshan tathaa shuddh vaataavaran se roka ja saktaa he. streptomycin, parayemino salicylic amla, tathaa iso nicotinic acid hydreside se yakshma rog se mukt kiya ja saktaa he. in oshadhiyon ke saath saath calcium, vitamin di aadi bhi diya jaataa he.
 baalkon main sifalis rog na ho, iske liye balak utpann hone se pehle hi rogi mata ko penicillin paryaapt maatra main dekar is rog ko roka ja saktaa he.
 is prakaar bacchon main honevaale kuch or rog bhi hai, jinhen penicillin streptomycin, terramycin, chloromicitin, ke dwaara roka ja saktaa he. kuch rog, jaise mastishkvaran shoth, lasaparvashoth, strypto cokaay, meningokoky, neumocoky aadi, jeevanuon ke upsarg se hote hai. typhoid tathaa ganoria bhi chloromycin, penicillin, streptomycin aadi, se achche hote hai. malaria - quinine, pelydrine, nivaquin aadi se acha hota he.
 kuch rogon ko, jaise hriday kii raktavahiniyon ke or annavah strotas ke rogon ko, tathaa tantrika santhaan evam haath-pair ityadi kii sahaj vikrutiyon ko shalya chikitsa se thik kiya ja saktaa he.
 kumaron main rumatic fever bhi paaya jaataa he. iskaa thik kaaran abhi agyaat he, parantu ise salicyllette, e. si. tii. ech. or cortisone se thik kiya jaataa he.
 chikitsa jagat main hormone chikitsa dwaara endocrine granthij rogon kaa unmoolan kiya jaataa he. endocrine granthij rog nimnlikhit hai : dyabetis melaitus, ekaek honevaali sharkaraheenata, dyabetis insipids, perathyroidjanya titani, adrinaljanya rog, ati adrinaljanya rog, pituitary hinata janya rog, thairoid hinatajanya rog tathaa yon granthij rog.
 baalkon main maansik, bhavuktajnya tathaa saamaajik vishyak asantulit avasthaaon se honevaale rogon kaa mahatv daihik vyaadhiyon se kam nahin he. iske liye maansik swasthata or man:kayik chikitsa kii sahaayta dwaara baalkon ke maansik vikaas kii abhivruddhi kii ja sakati he. baalkon ke ghaatak rogon main titanas, diphtheria, yakshma, menengitis, anseflitis, neumonia, bal yakritshoth aadi hai.
