 maan nanda bhagwati mandir bharat ke uttarakhand rajya ke bageshwar jile main sthit pothing naamak gaanv main he. jila mukhyalay se mandir kii duuri lagbhag 30 ki0me0 he. yah mandir kshetravasiyon ko hi nahin balki dur-daraaj ke logon ko bhi apni bhavyata evam aasthaa se apni ore aakarshith karata he. yahan prativarsh bhadrapada maas yani agast-sitambar main maan nanda bhagwati kii puja kii jaati he. jisamen dur-dur se bhaktajan is puja main sammilit hone ke liye aate hai. jisamen vishaal jan-samudaay ekatrit hota he. apni bhavyata or vishalata ke kaaran ab is puja ne ek meley kaa rup le liya he jise pothing kaa mela naam se janaa jaataa he. yahan kaa mandir bhale hi naveen style main bana ho lekin yahan hamen pracheen kaal se sthapit mandir ke andar rakhe gaye shilaon ke darshan hote hai.
 pothing main maan nanda bhagwati kii puja kaa shubharambh shravan maheene ke 1 gatte yani harele ke din se hi ho jaataa he. is din kapkot ke uttaruda gaanv se bhagwati, latu, golu devataaon ke dungariyon or sthaneey nivaasiyon ke dwaara 'doodh kele' kaa ped laakar gaanv main hi nirdhaarit sthaan par ropa jaataa he. lagbhag 1 maheene tak is kadli vriksh ko gaay ke doodh dwaara sinchaa jaataa he. bhadrapada maheene kii saptami ko is kadli vriksh ko katkar iske stambhon se ashtami ke din maan kii pratimaa banaai jaati he.
 bhadrapada ashtami ko pothing main maan nanda ke darshanath ek vishaal janasamudaay umad padataa he. yahan maan nanda bhagwati kii puja ke samay jo pudiyaan banaai jaati hai, wo hameshaa hi aakarshan kaa centre rahati hai, ek puri kaa vajan lagbhag 250 gram se lekar 300 gram tak hota he. bhaktajan is puri ko hi prasad swaroop grahan karte hai.
 maan bhagwati kaa yah mandir pothing gaanv ke madhya main sthapit he. jahaan silang ke vrikshon ke phulon kii khushboo or hariyaali maanav man ko moh leti he. yahan par aakar bhaktajan sukun kaa anubhav karte hai or har varsh yahan maan ke darbar main aakar maan ke darshan karte hai. yahan kii mahima, bhavyata or logon kii maan ke prati aasthaa ko dekhte hue is kshetra ko paryaton se jodne kii koshish kii ja rahi he. yah sthal sadak maarg se acchhee tarah juda hua he. mandir main anek dharmashalaayein bani huyi hai or yahan rahane kii acchhee vyavastha he.
 is kshetra main paryaton kii apaar sambhaavnaaen hai. pothing se lagbhag 05 kilometre kii duuri par sukunda naamak taal he. is taal ko bhi uttarakhand paryaton vibhaag dwaara viksit karne kii koshish kii ja rahi he. bhagwati mandir pothing ke purv disha main chirpatkot kii pahari main shri guru gusaain devtaa kaa ek mandir he or pashchim main bhi pokhari naamak sthaan main yahi devtaa virajman hai. in pavitra dharmik sthalon ko yadi sarkaar kaa sahyog mila to avashya hi uttarakhand ke paryaton main kaaphi ijapha ho saktaa he.
