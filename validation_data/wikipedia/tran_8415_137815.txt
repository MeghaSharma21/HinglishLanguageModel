 koi bhi yukti yaa tantra jo anunaadi vyavhaar pradarshit karti ho, anunadak kehalaati he. iskaa arth he ki yah kuch vishesh aavritiyon par anya aavritiyon kii apeksha sahaj rup main adhik aayam ke saath dolan karti he. anunadak, yantric or vidyutchumbakeey dono tarah ke ho sakate hai. inkaa upyog kisi vishesh aavriti kaa sanket paidaa karnaa tathaa kisi mishrit sanket se kisi vishesh aavriti ko filter karnaa ho saktaa he.
 kotor anunadak shabd praayah vidyutchumbakeey anunadakon ke liye prayukt hota he. jabaki shravy tarangon ke anunaad ke liye liye prayukt khokhale paatro ko helmolj anunadak kehete hai.
