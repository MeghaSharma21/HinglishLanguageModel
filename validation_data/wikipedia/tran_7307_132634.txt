 daara tritiy yaa darius tritiy mahaan hakhaamani rajvansh kaa antim prasiddh raja hua. vah bahadur or doosht prakruti kaa suyogy vyakti tha. lekin use shanti se rajya karne or apni shaskeey yogyata dikhaane kaa avasar na mil sakaa. uske durbhaagya se mysidonia or yunaan kii rajshakti, sikandar ke netritv main bahut prabal ho chali thi. falat: daara tritiy parsi samrajya ke samast sadhano or shaktiyon ko batorkar bhi sikandar ko akramanon se apane samrajya ko bachaane main samarth na ho sakaa. 
 parsikon ko adbhut europea vijeta alexnder ne i.pus. 334 main granicus ke yuddh main parajeet kiya. duusare varsh i.pus. 333 main issus ke yuddh main usne daara ko buree tarah haraakar bhaagane par vivash kiya. iraniyon kii rajdhani percipolis jala di gai . is paraajay ne daara kii shakti kaa todakar parsik samrajya kii jadon ko puri tarah hilaa diya. daara ne apane samrajya ko bachaane ke liye sikandar se phir ek vishaal sena lekar arvela ke paas goggmela main yuddh kiya. is baar bhi vah buree tarah parajeet hua or use bhaag janaa padaa. bhagode daara ko ant main bactria ke kshatrap besus ne marava daalaa. mahaan parsik rajvansh ke antim mahaan raja kaa yah du:khad ant tha.
