 dhruviya jyoti, yaa merujyoti, vah romantic deeptimay chata he jo dhruvakshetron ke vayumandal ke upree bhaag main dikhaai padati he. uttari akshanshon kii dhruviya jyoti ko sumeru jyoti, yaa uttar dhruviya jyoti, tathaa dakshini akshanshon kii dhruviya jyoti ko kumeru jyoti, yaa dakshin dhruviya jyoti, kehete hai. pracheen romavaasiyon or unaniyon ko in ghatnaon kaa gyaan tha or unhone in drishyon kaa behad rochak or vistrut varnan kiya he. dakshin golardhwaalon ne kumeru jyoti kaa kuch spasht kaarnon se vaisaa vyapak or rochak varnan nahin kiya he, jaisaa uttari golardhavalon ne sumeru jyoti kaa kiya he. inkaa jo kuch varnan prapya he usase isamen koi andeh nahin rah jaataa ki donon ke vishisht lakshanon main samanta he.
 sumeru jyoti ke anek rup hote hai. stormar ne inkaa vargeekaran is prakaar kiya he :
  kiransanrachana pradarshit karanevaali jyoti - iske antargat corona kiran or tathakthith parichhad hai.
  kiransanrachana na pradarshit karanevaali jyoti - iske antargat samang chaap, samang patt or spandaman prishth hai. vegord ne jyoti ko shant or chal roopon main vargeekrut kiya he. 
 antarrashtriya bhuprishth tathaa bhubhautik sangh dwaara sweekrut pratikon ke saath vividh jyotiyon kaa sankshipt vivaran nimnlikhit he :
 1. samang chaap ech e - inaki seemayen paryaapt spasht hoti hai. ye akaash main kuch dishaao main faile hote hai or chaap kaa ucchtam bindu chumbakeey yamyottar par hota he. deept chaap upree bhaag haraa, madhya bhaag pilaa or nichalaa bhaag saamanyat: laal hota he.
 2. sakiran chaap aar e - inase kirane pahie ke aron ke samaan apsruth hoti hai.
 3. spandaman chaap pi e - ye spandit hokar or damakkar kuch hi second main lupt ho jate hai.
 4. kiran aar - ye akeli yaa jhund main badi raashi main prakat ho sakati hai. ye ekmaatr shant, yaa teji se chalanevali, ho sakati hai.
 5. parichhad di - ye bahut lambii kirnon se bane or parde sadrush hote hai. kabhi kabhi kirane chumbakeey balarekhaon kaa anusaran karti hui pankhe jaisi dikhaai padati hai.
 6. kirit yaa corona si - ye atyant ucch akshanshon par, jahaan chumbakeey balarekha, bhuprishth par praaia: abhilamb hoti hai, dikhaai padate hai. prekshak ke chumbakeey shirovindu ke paas hi akaash ke kisi nishchit vindu se kirit kiran kii dharaayen failati hai.
 7. samang patt ech bi or kiran sanrachana patt, aar bi - ye chaap kii disha main hi failte hai.
 8. spandaman pi es yaa spandaheen di es visarit deeptimay prishth - yah anishchit aakaar or spasht simaaon kaa deept megh sa dikhaai padataa he.
 9. durbal dipti ji - yah kshitij ke nikat chaap ke upree bhaag main ushakal ke samaan prathit hoti he.
 uparyukt varnit vividh prakaar kii jyotiyon main sanrachanayukta yaa sanrachanavihin chaap, patt or parichhad hi adhik saamaanya hai, jabaki spandaman prishth or kirane bahut kam dikhaai padati hai.
 chumbakeey nimna akshanshon se ucch akshanshon kii ore merujyoti kii aavriti kramash: badhati jaati he or dhruviya kshetron main sarvaadhik hoti he. oonchaai ke saath merujyoti ke vitaran ke adhyayan se gyaat hota he ki meru jyoti kii ghatana 90 or 130 kilometre ke bich hoti he. stormar ke anusaar merujyoti kii nimnaseema 80 kilometre kii oonchaai he. chaap, patt or parichhad kshaitij disha main chumbakeey yamyottar ke lagbhag samakon kii disha main failte hai or kirandharaen chumbakeey balarekhaon ke saath saath newnaadhik kshaitij dishaao main failati hai. vaastavik disha sthaan par nirbhar karti he. yah mahatv kii baat he ki kirandhara ke vikiranvindu kaa chumbakeey shirovindu se sampatan hota he. merujyotiya sakriyata main dainik or mausami parivartan dekhe jate hai. aadhi raat ke thik pehle spasht dainik pramukh ucchtam or praat: durbal ucchtam sakriyata hoti he. nimna akshanshon kii vaarshik aavriti main do ucchtam sakriyataayen hoti hai jinka vishuvon se sampatan hota he. jyon-jyon ham merujyotiya kshetra kii ore badhate hai ye ucchtam sakriyataayen ek duusare ke nikat aati jaati hai or ucchtam sakriyata madhya jaade main hoti he. merujyotiya sakriyata sugyaat 11 varshiya or sakriyata kaa anusaran karti he or jab bade-bade surya dhabbon ke samooh surya ke kendriya yamyottar ke nikat se guzarate hai, usee samay is ghatana ke hone kii pravriti hoti he. chumbakeey vikshobh ke samaan hi 27 dinon baad pun: merujyotiya sakriyata kii pravriti dekhi jaati he. curi evam edward ke anusaar nirmit chitra upar diya he, jis se merujyotiya, chumbakeey or bhaumdhaara sakriyataaon main samya kaa pataa chalata he. parthiv chumbakeey vikshobh or dhruviya prakaash donon hi drishy ghatnaon kaa mul teevr veg se aavisht surya kii kanikaon ko mana jaataa he. in drishy ghatnaon kii vyakhya ke liye anek siddhant pratipaadit hue hai, jinamein chapman or pheraaro kaa siddhant, jise baad main martin ne pallavit kiya, sarvaadhik santoshprad or mahatvapurn he.
