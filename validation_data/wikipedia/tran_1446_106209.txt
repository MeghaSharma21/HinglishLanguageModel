 "ke ke mohammad" ek prasiddh bhartiya puratatvavid he. ve bhartiya puraatatv surveykshan ke kshetreey nideshak the, or vartmaan main aagaa khan samskruti trust main puratathvik pariyojna nideshak ke rup main seva de rahe hai.
 ke.ke. mohammad kerala ke kalikat main ek madhyam vargeey parivaar main paidaa hue the. bearan kutti haji or maariyam kii paanch santanon main ve dusri santan he. sarkari ucch madhyamik vidhyalay, kodavali se apni padhai puri karne ke baad unhone aligarh muslim vishvavidhyalay se itihaas main apni master degree or snatakottar diploma in puraatatv  skul of puraatatv surveykshan, puratathvik surveykshan bharat, nai dilli, bharat se kiya.keke mohammad ne 29 july 1983 ko kalikat kii nivasee raabia se shaadi kii. unke do santaanen hai, jamshed or shaheen.
 
 ke ke mohammad ne chhattisgarh ke jagadalpur ke paas dantewada jile main barsuar or samlur mandiron ko sangrakshit kiya. yah kshetra is kshetra main naksal gatividhiyon ke garh ke rup main janaa jaataa he. 2003 main, ke ke mohammad naksal kaaryakartaaon ko samajhane main sakasham hue or unke sahyog ke saath, mandiron ko aaj ke vartmaan rajya main sangrakshit kar diya.
 bateshwar, muraina gwalior se 40 kimi dur sthit 200 pracheen shiv or vishnu mandiron kaa parisar he. khajuraho se 200 saal pehle gurjar-pratihaar rajvansh ke dauraan 9 wein or 11 veen shatabdi ke bich in mandiron kaa nirmaan hua tha. yah kshetra nirbhay singh gujjar or gadriya daakuon ke niyantran main tha. keke mohammad dakaiton ko samajhane main safal rahe taaki ve in mandiron ko punarsthapit kar saken. vah kshetra main apane kaarykaal ke dauraan 60 mandiron ko punarsthapit karne main sakasham hue . police dwaara dakaiton kaa safaya hone ke baad, is kshetra ko khanan mafia dwaara gher liya gaya.
 2016 main, ke.ke. mohammad kii malayalam bhashaa main aatmakatha "main ek bhartiya" naam se prakashit hui. pustak main is daawey ke kaaran logon kaa dhyaan aakarshith hua ki marxvaadi itihaaskaaron ne charampanthi muslim samoohon kaa samarthan kiya or ayodhya vivaad kaa ek sauhardpurn samaadhaan khojane ke prayaason ko patari se utaar diya. unke anusaar, ayodhya main puratathvik khudaai main spasht rup se masjid ke neeche ek mandir kii upasthiti ke nishaan mili the, lekin vampanthi itihaaskaaron ne inhen khaarij kar diya or elahabaad ucch nyaayalay ko bhi gumraah karne kii koshish kii.
