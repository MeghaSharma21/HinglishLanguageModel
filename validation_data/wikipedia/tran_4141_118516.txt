 hindu dharm main jivan ke 4 bhaag kiye gaye hai- brahmacharya, grihasth, vanprastha or sanyas. chauthaa bhaag sanyas kaa arth ek nyaasi yaa trustee kii tarah jivan vyateet karnaa hota he. is ashram kaa uddeshy moksh kii prapti he. manushya jivan ko 100 varshon kaa maanakar 75 varsh ke uparaant vyakti ko is tarah se jivan yaapan karnaa chahie.sanyas kaa arth sansaarik bandhanon se mukt hokar nishkaam bhav se prabhu kaa samaran nirantar karte rahanaa.shastron main sanyas ko jivan kii sarvocch avasthaa kehete hai. sanyasi is sansar main rahete hue nirlypt bane rahete hai, arthath brahm chintan main leen rahete hue bhi bhautik aavashyaktaaon ke prati udasin rahete hai.
