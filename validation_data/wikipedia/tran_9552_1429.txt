 philippines ne 1924 main olympic khelon main pratispardhaa karne ke liye athleates ko pehle bheja tha, jisase vah dakshin-purv asia main ek padak jeetne or jeetne vaala pehla desh bana. 1980 ke greeshmakaleen olympic ke america netritv main bahishkaar main bhaag lene ke alaava, raashtra ne tab tak har samar olympic khelon main bhaag liya he. philippines ne 1940 ke greeshmakaleen olympic main bhaag lene ke bare main bhi nirnay liya, isse pehle ki khelon ko antat dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke failane ke kaaran radd kar diya gaya. philipino athleton ne 1972 ke baad se chaar alag-alag avasaron par sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main bhi hissaa liya he.
 philippines ne 1972 main sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main bhaag liya, jab usne do alpine sciyaron ko bheja. singapore main 2010 main desh ne pehle greeshmakaleen yuva olympic khelon main bhi bhaag liya.
 philipino athleton ne kul 10 olympic padak jite, sheersh padak banane vaale khel ke rup main mukkebaaji ke saath. koi philipino athlete ne kabhi bhi swarna padak nahin jeeta he.
 philippines kii rashtriya olympic samiti philippine olympic samiti he, 1911 main sthapit or 1929 main antarrashtriya olympic samiti dwaara manyata praapt thi.
