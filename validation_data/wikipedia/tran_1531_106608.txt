 om prakaash jindal bharat ke ispaat udyog ke ek bade kaarobaari evam raajnneetijna the. ve jindal samooh ke sansthaapak the tathaa haryana rajya kii arthvyavastha main is audyogic parivaar kaa baari yogadan mana jaataa he. 
 om prakaash jindal haryana ke urja mantri rahe hai or 11veen loksabhaa ke sadsy bhi rahe. 
 main of steel ke alankaran se sushobhit om prakaash jindal takneekee evam ejineering kaaryon main atyadhik ruchi rakhte the. unhone takneekee evam ejineering kii koi vidhivat shiksha nahin lee thi lekin in vishyon ke prati gahare lagaav or gahan abhiruchi ne unhen ek safal udyogpati banaaya. ve sarvapratham kisaan the, phir saadhaaran vyapaaree, uske baad baaltee nirmaataa or ant main antarrashtriya khyaati ke udyogpati evam haryana ke lokapriy netaa hue.
 jindal udyog samooh ke sansthaapak om prakaash jindal kaa janm 7 agast 1930 main haryana rajya ke nalava gaanv main ek kisaan parivaar main hua. lekin unhen bachapan se hi mashiinon main ruchi thi. unhone kate or bekaar phenk die gaye pipon kaa vyapaar shuru kiya. vah is tarah ke pipe, assam ke bazaaron se nilaamee main kharidte the or unhen kalakatta main bechate the. kolkata ke paas main lilua naamak sthaan main pipe bend or socket banane kii ek factory lagaai. yah sabase pehali audyogic ikai thi, jise om prakaash or unke bhaaiyon ne 1952 main sthapit kii or iskaa naam jindal limited rakhaa gaya. tata or kaling ke baad, bharat main apni tarah kii teesri factory thi. is uplabdhi ke chalate om prakaash jindal pashchim bengal or purvi bharat main lokapriy ho gaye. yahaan kuch saalon tak kaam karne ke baad 1960 main apane paitruk jila hisar aa gaye or yahaan bhi udyog lagaaya. unhone 34 audyogic ikaiyaan sthapit kin. inamen se 30 bharat main, teen america main or ek indonesia main sthit hai. ab in ikaayiyon kaa sanchaalan unki patni savitri devi jindal or chaar putr prithvi raaj jindal, sajjan jindal, ratan jindal or naveen jindal karte hai. jindal udyog samooh kaa mukhya vyavsaay steel se sambandhit he, phir bhi use chaar virgon-pips, carbon steel, steneles steel, rail va urja main baanta ja saktaa he. apane pita kii hi tarah unke chaaron putr kaamyaab udyogpati maane jate hai or apni companiyon ko nai oonchaiyon le jate hue desh ke audyogic vikaas main yogadan de rahe hai. 
 san 1991 main pehali baar ve vidhayak chune gaye. san 1996 main congress kii tikat par kurukshetra se loksabhaa ke liye chune gaye. hisar se dobaaraa vidhayak banane ke saath hi om prakaash jindal ne pradesh kii raajnneeti main sakriy bhoomika nibhaai. isi dauraan apane chote putr naveen jindal ko 2004 main loksabhaa kaa chunaav kurukshetra se ladaaya, jisamen unhen vijay hasil hui. varsh 2005 main punah hisar se vidhayak ke liye khadey hue or jeet hasil hui. swargeey jindal ko urja mantri banaaya gaya. raajnneeti or janaseva karte hue ve lokapriyata ke shikhar par pahunche or is sansar se vidaa le lee.
