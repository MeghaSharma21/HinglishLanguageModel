 nirdeshaank: 28°47′N 76°08′E / 28.78°N 76.13°E / 28.78; 76.13bhivani bharat ke haryana rajya kaa ek jila he jiskaa mukhyalay bhivani he. kshetrafal kii drushti se yah haryana kaa sabase bada jila he. isaki sthapana 22 disambar, 1972 ko hui thi jab ise hisar se alag kar diya gaya tha. iske jila mukhyalay kaa naam bhi bhivani hi he. iskaa kshetrafal 5140 varg kimi he. 442 gaavon ko samete is jile kii jansankhya 1,425, 022 he jo jansankhya kii drushti se bhivani ko haryana main teesraa bada jila bana detaa he . jila mukhyalay bhivani bharat kii rajdhani dilli se 124 kilometre dur he.
 bhivani ke uttar main hisar, purv main rohtak, dakshin main mahendragarh, dakshin purv main rewadi tathaa pashim or dakshin pashchim main rajasthan he. ye haryana ke sabase neeche jal str ke jilon main aata he.
 bhivani jile kaa naam iske mukhyalay ke naam se liya gaya he. is sambandh main aitihaasik pariprekshya yah he ki ye rajput rani 'bhaani' ke naam se sambandhit he.
 bhivani ke ek gaanv, mitathal main kii gayi khudaai se praapt pramaan bataate hai ki yah sthaan hadappa samskruti ke samay se hi aabaad tha. bhivani ke sameep naurangabad gaanv main kii gayi khudaai ke dauraan praapt vasteen lagbhag dhai hazaar saal puraani hai. ine-e-akabari main bhivani shahar kaa zikr miltaa he.
 bhivani main paanch tehsil hai - bhivani, bavani kheda, tosham, loharu or sivaani.
 adhikansh logon kaa vyavsaay krishi he. yahan kii fasal bajra mukhya he. iske alaava jowar, gehun, ganna, sarson, dhan or chanaa he. bhivani ke dakshini ilaake thar registan ke sampark main aate hai. atah vahaan par baaluu mitti adhik he. atah vahaan par pani kii poorti bhumigat jal ko nikaal kar kii jaati he jahaan sookhe kuen do sau feet kii gaharaai tak jate hai jinamein pani kii motor sthaayee rup se rakh di jaati he. baaki sthanon par haryana sarkaar dwaara pradaan kii gayi nahar kii seva zamin ko upjau banaaye rakhane main madad karti he.
