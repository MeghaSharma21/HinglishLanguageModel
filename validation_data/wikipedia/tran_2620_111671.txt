 gorichen parvat chotii, bhartiya rajya arunachal pradesh main tawang or pashchim kameng jilon ke bich maujuud ek parvat chotii he. arunachal pradesh paryaton vibhaag ke anusaar kul 22,500 feet oonchaai ke saath yah purvi bharat or arunachal pradesh kii sabase unchi chotii he. vartmaan main yah gaiyr-pratibandhit ilaake main he or parvatarohan karne waalon ke liye khuli he tathaa pramukh paryatak aakarshan he. kuch anya sroton ke mutabiq gorichen samooh main kul chah chotiyaan hai.
 trekking or parvatarohan ke liye yahan sitambar-oktober sabase behtareen hota he jab monsoon kii barish band ho chuki hoti he or drishyata acchhee hoti he. anya sroton main, jahaan gorichen ko chah chotiyon ke samooh ke rup main paribhaashit kiya gaya he, "kong to" chotii, 7,042 meter, jise sthaneey rup se "sher kongdi" kehete hai, ko sabase unchaa bataaya gaya he or purvi bharat kii ekmaatr chotii bataaya jaataa he jo saat hajaar meter se adhik oonchaai vaali he.
