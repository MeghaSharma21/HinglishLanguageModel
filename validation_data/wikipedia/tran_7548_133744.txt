 vaidic dharmapar ek shrenee kaa bhaag
 moksh
 dhyaan yoga 
 
 vaiwaswath manu hindu dharm ke anusaar maanav jati ke praneta va pratham purush swyambhuv manu ke baad saatven manu the. harek manwantar main ek pratham purush hota he, jise manu kehete hai. vartmaan kaal main vawaswat manvantar chal rahaa he, jiske pratham purush vaiwaswath manu the, jinke naam par hi manvantar kaa bhi naam he.
 bhagwaan surya kaa vivaah vishwakarma kii putree sangya se hua. vivaah ke baad sangya ne vaiwaswath or yama naamak do putron or yamuna naamak ek putree ko janm diya. yahi vivaswan yani surya ke putr vaiwaswath manu kahalaaye.
 vaiwaswath manu ke netritv main trivishtap arthath tibet yaa devloak se pratham peedi ke maanvon kaa meru pradesh main avataran hua. ve dev swarg se athwa ambar se pavitra veda bhi saath laae the. isi se shruti or smriti kii parampara chalati rahi. vaiwaswath manu ke samay hi bhagwaan vishnu kaa matsay avatar hua. inaki shashan vyavastha main devon main paanch tarah ke vibhaajan the: dev, daanav, yaksha, kinnar or gandharva. inke ke das putr hue the. il, ikshvaku, kushanaam, arishth, dhrist, narishyant, karush, mahabali, sharyaati or prushadh putr the. isamen ikshvaku kul kaa hi mukhyatah vistaar hua. ikshvaku kul main kai mahaan pratapi raja, rishi, arihant or bhagwaan hue hai.
 vaiwaswath saatven manwantar kaa swami banakar manu pad par asin hue the. is manwantar main urjasvi naamak indra the. atri, vasishtha, kashyap, gautam, bharadwaj, vishwamitra or jamadagni- ye saaton is manwantar ke saptarshi the.
 veda, puraanon or anya dharmagranthon ke saath vaigyanik shodhon va adhyayanon se gyaat hota he ki manushya va anya jeev-jantuon kii vartmaan aadi sristi himalay ke aasapaas kii bhoomi par hui thi jisamen tibet kaa sarvadhik mahatva he. himalay ke paas hone ke kaaran purv main bharat varsh ko himavarsh bhi kaha jaataa tha. veda-puraanon main tibet ko trivishtap kaha gaya he. mahabharat ke mahaprasthaanik parv main swargarohan main spasht kiya gaya he ki tibet himalay ke us rajya ko pukaara jaataa tha jisamen nandankanan naamak devraj indra kaa desh tha. isse siddh hota he ki indra swarg main nahin dharti par hi himalay kshetra main rahete the.
 purv main yah dharti jal pralay ke kaaran jal se dhank gai thi. kailash, gori-shankar kii chotii tak pani chadh gaya tha. isse yah siddh hota he ki sampurna dharti hi jalmagn ho gai thi. kai maah tak vaiwaswath manu dwaara naav main hi gujaarne ke baad unki naav gauri-shankar ke shikhar se hote hue neeche utari. gori-shankar jise mount everest shikhar bhi kaha jaataa he, vishwa main sabase unchaa, barf se dhankaa hua or thos pahaad he. tibet main dhire-dhire jansankhya vruddhi or vaataavaran main teji se hote parivartan ke kaaran vaiwaswath manu kii santanon ne alag-alag bhoomi kii badhanaa aarambh kiya. vigyaan ke anusaar bhi pehle prithvi ke sabhi mahadweep ikatthe the. arthath america dweep idhar africa or udhar china tathaa rus se judaa hua tha. africa bharat se judaa hua tha. dharti kii ghurnan gati or bhu-garbheey parivartan ke kaaran dharti dwipon main bant gai.
 is judi hui dharti par hi himalay kii nimna shreniyon ko paar kar manu kii santaanen kam oonchaai vaale pahari vistaaron main basati gayi. phir jaise-jaise samudr kaa jal str ghatata gaya ve or bhi madhya bhaag main aate gaye. dakshin ke ilaake to jalpralay se jalmagn hi the. lekin bahut kaal ke baad dhire-dhire jaise-jaise samudr kaa jalastr gata manu kaa kul pashchimi, purvi or dakshini maidan or pahari pradeshon main fail gaye. jo himalay ke idhar failte gaye unhone hi akhand bharat kii sampoorn bhoomi ko brahmavart, brahmarshidesh, madhyadesh, aryaavart evam bharatvarsh aadi naam die. jo idhar aae ve sabhi manushya arya kahalaane lage. yahi log saath main veda lekar aae the. isi se yah dharna prachlit hui ki devbhumi se veda dharti par utre. swarg se ganga ko utaara gaya aadi anek dharanaayen.
 in aaryon ke hi kai samooh alag-alag jhundon main puri dharti par fail gaye or vahaan bas kar bhanti-bhanti ke dharm or samskruti aadi ko janm diya. manu kii santaanen hi arya-anarya main bantkar dharti par fail gayi. purv main yah sabhi dev-daanav kehalaati thim. is dharti par aaj jo bhi manushya hai ve sabhi vaiwaswath manu kii hi santaanen hai is vishay main vidwaanon main matbhed hai.
