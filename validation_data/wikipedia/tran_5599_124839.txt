 chirang jila assam ke 27 jilon main se ek he. bharat ke janaganana, 2011 ke anusaar uttar kachar jila ke baad yah assam kaa doosraa sabase kam aabaadi vaala jila he.
 yah rajya ke kshetra bodoland kshetreey parishad main ek apekshaakrit nayaa jila he. chirang jile ko bogigaanv jila se 2004 main alag kiya gaya."chirang" shabd gaaro bhashaa ke shabd se liya gaya he jahaan "chi" shabd kaa arth jal tathaa "rang" shabd kaa arth dhan he. yah shabd pados ke desh bhutan ke sirang jile kii ek pratilipi bhi ho saktaa he. 
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar chirang jile kii aabaadi 481,818 he, jo ki surinam desh ke lagbhag baraabar he. jansankhya ke yah aakade is jile ko bharat main 547waan sthaan dete he . is jile kaa ghanatv 244 prayek varg kilometre main nivasee he. 2001-2011 ke dashak main jansankhya vruddhi dar 11.26% rahi he. chirang kaa linganupaat	969 mahila prati 1000 purush he, or sakshartaa dar 64.71% he.
 maanas rashtriya udyaan isi jile main sthit he, jiskaa kshetrafal 500 kimi2 he. yah udyaan chirang ke saath chaar anya jilon main bhi failaa he.
