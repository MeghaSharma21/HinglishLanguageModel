 rashtriya praudyogiki santhaan ko pehle kshetreey enginring kalege ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. san 2002 main bharat sarkaar ke maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantralay ne in 17 engeniurang mahavidhyalayon kaa str badhaakar inkaa naam "rashtriya praudyogiki santhaan" kar diya. bharat main takneekee shiksha ke kshetra main shikshan ke str, vidhyaarthiyon kii gunvattta tathaa sthaapan kii drushti se bhartiya praudyogiki sansthaanon ke baad inkaa hi sthaan aata he. is samay en ai tii kii sankhya 30 he. prarambh main san 1959 or san 1965 ke bich chaudaa aar i si kii sthapana hui. vartmaan samay main 31 en ai tii nimnlikhit sthanon par sthit hai:
 sabhi kalejon main vibhinn takneekee shakhaon main snaatak, para-snaatak or doctorat str kii shiksha di jaati he. aajkal en ai tii hone ke baad in sansthaanon kaa sara kharch kendra sarkaar vahan karti he. in sansthaanon ke snaatak str ke paathyakramon main pravesh akhil bhartiya enginring pravesh pariksha ke madhyam se hoti he jisamen bahu-vikalpiya vastunishth prashn puche jate hai.
