 sudesh beri bhartiya televijan or film abhinayta hai. sudesh ne apana kariyar 1988 main film khataron ke khilaadi se armbh kiya. unhone vansh or yudhapath main mukhya kirdaar kaa abhinay kiya lekin donon hi filmein tikat khidki par pit gayi. unhone vibhinn bahu-sitar filmon main sahaayak abhinayta kaa abhinay bhi kiya jaise ghayal, border, rifujee, elosi kargil, tango charlie aadi. unhone shankar mondal dwaara nirdeshit rajesh khanna kii filmon vafaa or shaabaash! you can du it main bhi abhinay kiya.
