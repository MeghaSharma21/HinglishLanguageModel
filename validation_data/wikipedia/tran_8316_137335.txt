 samaara, 1935 se 1991 tak kuybishev ke rup main janaa jaataa tha, rus kaa chatha sabase bada shahar or samaara oblast kaa prashasnic centre he. yah volga ke purvi tat par volga or samaara nadiyon ke sangam par europea rus ke dakshini hisse main he jo shahar kii pashchimi seemaa ke rup main kaary karata he; nadi ke paar jhiguli parvat hai, jiske upar sthaneey bear kaa naam rakhaa gaya he. uttari seemaa sokolii parvatein or dakshin or purv main maidaanon se bani gai he. shahar main 1,164,685 kii aabaadi ke saath 46,597 hectare kaa kshetrafal shaamil he; samaara ke mahanagar kshetra, tolyatti or sijran kii jansankhya 3 million se adhik he. yah kaazan se 300 kilometre, oofa se 410 kilometre, saratov se 340 kilometre or kazakstan ke oral se 235 kilometre kii duri par sthit he.
 purv main ek band shahar, samaara ab europea rus main ek bada or mahatvapurn saamaajik, raajnneetik, aarthik, audyogic or saanskrithik centre he or may 2007 main isane europea sangh-rus shikhar sammelan kii mejabani kii thi. yahan garm garmeeyon or thande sardiyon kii visheshta dekhi ja sakati he. samaara ke nagarikon kaa jivan hameshaa volga nadi se aantarik rup se judaa hua he, jisane na keval kai shatabdiyon main rus ke mukhya vanijyik gahanta ke rup main kaary kiya he, balki isamen badi drishy apil bhi he. samaara ke nadi ke tat ko sthaneey nagarikon or paryatakon donon ke liye pasandida manoranjan sthanon main se ek mana jaataa he. soviet upanyaaskaar vasili aksenove ne samaara kaa daura karne ke baad, unhone tippanee kii: "mujhe yakeen nahin he ki pashchim main kahaan se ek lanbaa or sundar tatbandh mil saktaa he."
