 capatoun dakshin africa kii rajdhani he. kep toun dakshin africa kaa doosraa sabase zyada jansankhya vaala shahar he. yah western kep kii rajdhani he. yah dakshin africa kaa sansad bhawan bhi he. yah jagah bandargah, parvat or baag aadi ke liye prasiddh he. teble mauten, teble mountain neshnal park, teble mountain ropave, kep of good hope, chapmanas peak, signal hill, victoria and alfred waterfrant aadi yahaan ke pramukh darshneey sthal hai.
 hajaaron saal pehle kep toun aadim janajati khoi kaa nivas sthaan hua karata tha. 1652 i. main yahaan pehali baar europiyanon ne pravesh kiya tha. sarvapratham yahaan dach log aae. is jagah par 1652 i. main jaan vaan rivik ne ek vyapaar centre kii sthapana kii. dachon ke peeche-peeche german tathaa french aae. urapiyanon ke aane ke baad kep toun dhire-dhire basna shuru ho gaya. phir stallanbosch, parl tathaa kep wineland jaise upshahron kii sthapana hui. kep toun main hi desh kaa sansad bhawan bhi he. ab yah ek vishwastareey shahar he jo apane me pracheen sabhyataa-samskruti ko sanjoe hue he.
 teble mountain samtal sataha kii kam oonchai vaala ek pahaad he. yah pahaad 1086 meter ooncha he. yah kep toun ke pashchami bhaag main sthit he. is pahaad par se kep toun shahar kaa vihangam drishy dekha ja saktaa he. is pahaad kii chotii par jane ke liye ropave laga hua he. yah pahaad mukhya rup se teble mountain neshnal park kaa ek hissaa he. 
 is park ko pehle kep pennisula neshnal park ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. is park kii sthapana 29 may 1998 i. ko kii gai thi. is park kii sthapana kaa mukhya uddeshy teble mountain ke paas ke paryaavaran ko surakshit rakhana tha. visheshkar teble mountain ke jangalon main paae jane vaale fenvas naamak jantu ko surakshit rakhana. is park kii dekhbhaal dakshin africa neshnal park dwaara kii jaati he. is park ke paas paryaton kii drusht se do atyant mahatvapurn sthaan teble mountain or kep of good hope he.
 yah ropave teble mountain par jane ke liye banaaya gaya he. yah kep toun ke mahatvapurn paryaton sthalon main ek he. is ropave kaa nichalaa stesan trekslgar road se 302 meter kii oonchai par he. jabaki is ropave kaa upree stesan 1067 meter kii oonchai par he. upree stesan se kep toun shahar kaa adbhoot najaara dikhataa he. 
 kisi bhi desh main bahut kam aise sthaan hote hai, jinhone itihaas ko badal kar rakh diya ho. kep toun main sthit kep of good hope ek aisa hi sthaan he. 1488 i. main portugaliyon ne isi sthaan se africa mahadesh main kadam rakhaa tha. iske baad se hi africa kaa itihaas badal gaya. yah atlantic mahaasaagar ke paas sthit he. kep toun aane vaale paryatak is sthaan par ghoomane zarur aate hai.
 yah ek pahari he jo kep toun ke dakshin main 15 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. is pahari tak ek sadak jaati he, jise chapmanas peak drive ke naam se janaa jaataa he. 1915 se 1922 i. ke bich bana yah sadak apane samay main ejineering kaa adbhoot namoona he. yah ek khadi sadak he. yah maarg kathin hone ke kaaran yahaan saavdhaanee purvak gaadi chalaae. 
 yah ek sapaat sataha vaali pahari he, jo teble mountain ke najadik sthit he. is pahari ko da liance flank' ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. par ab yah naam aprachlit ho gaya he. yah nun gan ke liye prasiddh he.
 samudr tat par sthit hone ke kaaran kep toun main bahut saare bich hai. glen bich, campus be, cliffton, si point, sand ve, bluebergstrand aadi yahaan sthit mahatvapurn bich hai. in bichon par hameshaa paryatakon kii bheed lagi rahati he. yahaan par tairaaki kaa anand bhi uthaaya ja saktaa he.
 kep toun sthit victoria and alfred waterfrand paryatakon ke sabase pasandida sthalon main se he. yah ek aitihaasik bandargah he, jo abhi bhi kaam kar rahaa he. yah bandgah robin iceland or teble mountain ke bich sthit he. is bandgah ke aas-paas paryatakon ke manoranjan kaa bhi prabandh kiya gaya he. is bandargah kaa nirmaan 1860 i. main rani victoria ke putr prince alfred ne karavaaya tha.
 yah equirium victoria and alfred water park main sthit he. is equirium main saat gailariyaan hai. ise 13 navambar 1995 i. ko aam logon ke liye kholaa gaya tha.
 yah kep toun kaa mukhya bazaar he, jo kep toun ke city boul kshetra main he. yahaan theatre, bukstor, hotel, restaurant aadi kaaphi sankhya main he. 
 yah ek ministrail tyohaar he jo kep toun main prativarsh manaaya jaataa he. is tyohaar main 13,000 se upar ministrail apane chehre ko safed rang se range hue bhaag lete hai. ve vibhinn rang ke chaate or vaadyayantra liye hote hai. is tyohaar ko kep toun ministrail carnival ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. yah tyohaar har varsh 2 janvari ko manaaya jaataa he. 
 yah garden newland main sthit he. is garden main vibhinn prakaar ke paudhon kaa sundar sangrah he.ghoomane kaa samay: subah 8 baje se shaam 6 baje tak.
 yah iceland kep toun se kuch duuri par sthit he. isi sthaan par nelson mandela ko yaheen par najarband rakhaa gaya tha.
 kep toun main hi dakshin africa kaa sansad he. kep toun aane vaale paryatak is bhawan ko dekhne zarur aate hai. is bhawan main pravesh ni: shulk he.
 iske alaava bo-cap museum, distric sicks museum aadi bhi yahaan dekhne yogya he.
 kep toun main kep toun antarrashtriya hawaii adda he. yah dakshin africa kaa doosraa sabase bada hawaii adda he. yahaan duusare deshon se niyamit rup se hawaii jahaaj aate rahete hai.
