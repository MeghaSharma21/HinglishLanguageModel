 pankaj naram ek bhartiya ayurvedic chikitsak hai jinhonen televijan par apni upasthiti dwaara antarrashtriya pratishtha praapt kii. 
 unhone bombai vishvavidhyalay main adhyayan kiya or ayurvedic chikitsa main vibhinn oopaadhiyaan praapt kin tatpashchaat bhartiya chikitsa kii chikitsa parishad main ek chikitsak ke rup main panjikrut kiye gayi. iske baad unhone ayurveda ke chikitsak or aarogy sadhak baba ramdaas kii pracheen, kintu ucch upachaar paddatiyon, jaise naadee nidaan aadi kii shiksha praapt kii. 
 dow. naram 1987 se 2012 tak mumbai main ayushakti ayurveda ke nideshak rahe. ve paramparaagat aushadhiy utpaadon ke nirmaataa or pracheen yuva rahasy ke sansthaapak or nirdeshak hai. 
 unhone apane tivi kaarykram “yoga for you” ke dwaara antarrashtriya str par manyata praapt kii I is kaarykram ke 3000 se bhi adhik ansh, varsh 2008 se zee tivi stesan dwaara 169 deshon main prasaarit kiye gaye hai. varsh 2016 se ek anya kaarykram “ancient healing” colors tivi stesan dwaara prasaarit kiya gaya I donon hi kaaryakramon main dow. naram aahaar, jivan style or paaramparik gharelu upachaar ke aadhaar par swasthya sambandhi salaah pradaan karte hai.
 11 sitambar 2001 ko aatankwaadi hamalon ke baad, world tres centre par bachaav kaary ke dauraan dhuyen or vishakt gaison ke sampark main aane vaale bachaav karmiyon or sahaayakon kii swasthya kshati ke dauraan, dow. naram ne ayurvedic aushadhiyon or upachaaron dwaara unki sahaayta kii. iske saath hi is upachaar main prayog kii gai ayurvedic aushadhiyon par ek sanyukt swatantra vaigyanik adhyayan main in ayurvedic aushadhiyon kii prabhaavashiltaa kaa moolyankan bhi kiya gaya. 
 apane maanavtaavaadi kaaryon, visheshkar 11 sitambar 2001 ke hamalon main bachaav kaaryakartaaon kii sahaayta karne ke inke prayaason ke liye, dow. naram ko new jersey sansad dwaara “varsh ke maanavtaavaadi” puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya I 
 varsh 2017 se dow. naram steenbis uniwarsity berlin esaechabi ke “steenbis transfar institute” main swasthya kshamta or swasthya shiksha par adhyaapan kaary kar rahe hai.
