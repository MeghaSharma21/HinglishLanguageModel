 ke. je. yesudas ek prasiddh karnataka sangeet gayak he
 kattari joseph yesudas ko san 2002 main bharat sarkaar dwaara kala ke kshetra main padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. ye kerala se hai. unhen kul paanch baar sarvshereshth pashvagayak kii shrenee main sarhatariya film puraskaar se nawaja gaya he, joki is puraskaar kii itihaas main sabase adhik he.
