 kakuwala punjab ke sangrur zile ke sunaam block kaa ek gaanv he, jo sangrur-patraan rashtriya rajmarg 17 par sthit he. bhugolic sthiti mutabik iske uttar kii taraf dirhba or gaanv khetla, dakshin-pashchim kii taraf gaanv ladbanjaraa kal, dakshin-purv kii taraf patraan or gaanv dugal padate hai. iskaa naam kaku singh se aayaa he. isaki aabaadi lagbhag 1003 he or yahan 153 ke kareeb ghar hai.
 is gaanv main bacchon kaa ek praathmik paathshaala, ek gurudwara or ek nirankaari bhawan he.
