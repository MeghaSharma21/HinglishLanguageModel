 
 felinae pantherinaaye‎ †makairodontinaaye †proylurinaaye 
 felidaye billiyon or billi-numa maansaahaari jaanwaron kaa jeevvaigyanik kul he jisamen billi, singh, sher, chita or links jaise praani shaamil hai. vartmaan yug main is kul kii do shakhaayen astitv main hai: pantherinaaye‎ or felinae‎ . pantherinaaye‎ main baagh, singh, tendua or jaiguaar shaamil hai.felinae‎ main kugar, chita, links, oselot​ or saadhaaran gharelu billi aate hai.
