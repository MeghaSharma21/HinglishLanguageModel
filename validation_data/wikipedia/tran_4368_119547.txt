 lomadee ek jantu he.
 Phylum: Chordata
 Class: Mammalia
 Order: Carnivora
 Family: Canidae
 lomady saamanytah 2 se 3 varsh tak jivit rahati he aaharlomdy ke aahaar main rodent, keedon, krimi, fal, machhlee, pakshi, andey or anya sabhi prakaar ke chote jaanavar he. lomadee kii zarurat aam taur par har din 1 kilogram khadyann kii he.
