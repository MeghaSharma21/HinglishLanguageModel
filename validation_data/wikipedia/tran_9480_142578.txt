 jiyangnan yaa jiang naan china kaa ek bhugolic kshetra he jo yangtse nadi ke antim bhaag main nadi se dakshin main padataa he. isamen yangtse nadi kii delta kaa dakshini hissaa bhi aata he. is ilaake main adhiktar log vu chini bhashaayen bolte hai. haalanki jiyangnan kaa ilaaka chini ke kul kshetrafal kaa 5% hi he, pichley 1000 varshon se yah aarthik jivan main china ke sabase viksit kshetron main se ek rahaa he. pichley 15 varshon main yahan motor gadiyon, electronic or kapade banane ke kaarakaane bade paimaane par khadey hue hai or duniya bhar main niryaat kiya gaya bahut sa chini maal isi kshetra main bana hota he. bhugolic rup se yah nadi-jharanon kaa ilaaka he or samudr ke qareeb he. yahan bahut si jhilein or daldali kshetra hai. yahan kii hawa nam rahati he or sardiyon main barish kaa mausam hota he.
 chini bhashaa main yangtse nadi kaa naam 'chang jiyang' he or 'naan' shabd kaa arth 'dakshin' he. isliye 'yangtse se dakshin' ko 'jiyang naan' bulaayaa jaataa he.
