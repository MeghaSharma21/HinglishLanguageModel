  bagmara upjila, bangladesh kaa ek upazila he, joki bangladesh main tritiy str kaa prashasnic anchal hota he . yah rajshahi vibhaag ke rajshahi zile kaa ek upjila he, jisamen, zila sadar samet, kul 9 upazile hai. yah bangladesh kii rajdhani dhaka se purv kii disha main avasthit he.
 yahan kii aadhikarik str kii bhashaayen bangla or angrezi he. tathaa bangladesh ke kisi bhi anya kshetra kii tarah hi, yahan kii bhi pramukh maukhik bhashaa or maatrubhaasha bangla he. bengali ke alaava angrezi bhashaa bhi kai logon dwaara jaani or samajhi jaati he, jabaki saanskrithik or aitihaasik nikatataa tathaa bhashaai samanta ke kaaran, kai log simit maatra main hindustani bhi samajhane main sakasham hai. yahan kaa bahusankhyak dharm, islam he, jabaki pramukh alpsankhyak dharm, hindu dharm he. rajshahi vibhaag main, janasankhyikeek rup se, islam ke anuyai, aabaadi ke ausatan 88.42% he, jabaki shesh jansankhya pramukhtah hindu dharm kii anuyai he.
 bagmara upjila bangladesh ke purvi bhaag main, rajshahi vibhaag ke rajshahi jile main sthit he.
