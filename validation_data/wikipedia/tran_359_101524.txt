 2005 vimbledon tennis pratiyogita kaa aayojan ol england club main 26 june - 9 july ko hua. 
 purush ekal main pichley do baar ke vijeta switzaraland ke roger federr kaa muqabla america ke endi rodic se hua jisamen federr 6-2 7-6 6-4 se vijayi rahe. 
 mahila ekal main america kii venus williams ne yah khitaab belgium jeet liya. unhone final main apane hi desh kii lindse devonport ko 4-6 7-6 9-7 se haraaya.
