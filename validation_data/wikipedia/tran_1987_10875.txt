 kan-twarak asi mashin he jiske dwaara aaweshit kanon kii gatij urja badhai jaati hai. yah ek aisi yukti he, jo kisi aaweshit kan kaa veg badhaane ke kaam main aati hai. veg badhaane ke liye vaidyut kshetra kaa prayog kiya jaataa he, jabaki aaweshit kanon ko modane evam focus karne ke liye chumbakeey kshetra kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. twarit kiye jane vaale aaweshit kanon ke samooh yaa kiran-punj dhaatu yaa siremic ke ek pipe se hokar guzarati he, jisame nirvaat banaakar rakhana padataa he taaki aaweshit kan kisi anya anu se takaraakar nasht na ho jaayen. 
 tivi aadi main prayukt cathod kiran tub bhi ek ati saadhaaran kan-twarak hi he. jabaki large hadran colider vishwa kaa sabase vishaal or shaktishaalee kan twarak he.
 kan twarakon kaa mahatv itnaa he ki unhen 'anusandhan kaa yantra' kaha jaataa he.
 kan twarak mukhya rup se do prakaar ke hote hai:
 microtron, cychlotron, betatron, syncrotron aadi chakree twarak hai.
 ucch urja vaali aaweshit kiran punj kai kaaryon ke liye upyogi he. iskaa sabase mahatvapurn upyog parmaanu ke andar jhaankne or is baat kaa pataa lagane ke liye kiya jaataa he parmaanu kaa sabase moulik kan kya he.
 aaweshit kanon ko twarit karne ke liye unhen ek vaidyut kshetra se hokar guzaara jaataa he jisaki disha aavesh ke gati kii disha main hoti he.
 jab kisi q aavesh ko V volt vibhavaantar vaale do binduon ke bich se guzaara jaataa he to uski urja main qV jul kii vruddhi ho jaati he. udhaaharan ke liye kisi electron ko 1 volt vibhavaantar se hokar guzaara jaay to uski urja main 1 eV kii vruddhi ho jaati he. gyatavya he ki electron kaa aavesh e=1.6E-19 koolomb hota he.
 kan twarak ek bahut jatil tantra he jo kai tantron se milakar bana hota he. iske mukhya ghatak is prakaar hai:
 vishwa ke sabhi pramukh deshon main kan twarak banaaye gayi hai. is samay vishwa ke vibhinn deshon main vibhinn prakaar ke, vibhinn urja vaale evam vibhinn upyog ke liye nirmit kai hajaar kan twarak hai. geneva sthit sarn large hadran colider vishwa kaa sabase vishaal or shaktishaalee kan twarak he.
