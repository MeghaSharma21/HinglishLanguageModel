 'cold start' bharat kii sena dwaara viksit naveen sainya siddhanth he, jise bhartiya sena ne pakistan ke khilaaf sambhavit yuddh ko dhyaan main rakhkar viksit kiya he. 'cold start' siddhant ke anusaar aadesh milane ke 48 ghanton ke bheetar hamalaa shuru kiya ja saktaa he. itane kam samay main hamalaa karne se bhartiya sena pakistani sena ko aashcharyachakit kar degi. is paddati main bhartiya sena ke vibhinn hisson ko akraman ke liye ekikrut karne par jor diya gaya he. is tarah kaa abhiyaan punjab or rajasthan ke simavarti ilaakon main hoga. 
 cold start siddhant kaa ek uddeshy yuddh kii sthiti main pakistan ko parmaanu hamale se rokna he, kyonki use parmaanu hamale ke liye jaraa bhi samay nahin dena he. is yojana main mulat: teji se hamale par jor diya gaya he. bakhtarband vaahan or topakhana pakistan ke ilaake main kam-se-kam samay main pravesh karaayaa ja saktaa he. pakistan ke khilaaf bhartiya senaaon ko kuch hafton ke sthaan par keval kuch dinon main hi tainaat karne ke liye cold start siddhant banaaya gaya tha. iskaa pareekshan abhi yuddh main kiya janaa shesh he. iskaa uddeshy he ki tatkaal lamabandi or twarit hamale se pakistan ko aashcharyachakit kar dena.
