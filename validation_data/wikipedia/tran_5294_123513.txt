 history, jise purv main da history channel ke naam se janaa jaataa tha, ek antarrashtriya satlit or cabel tivi channel he jo blue collar americana, gupt rahasyon, sansanikhej khabaron vaale kaarykram, syudovaigyanik or apasaamaanya ghatnaon vaale kaaryakramon kaa prasaaran karata he. vyakhya or tippaniyan praayah itihaaskaaron, vidwaanon, lekhakon, gudh tathyon kaa adhyayan karne vaale vidwaanon, jyotishi or bible sambandhi vidwaanon dwaara di jaati hai- saath hi saath gavaahon or/yaa unke pariwaron evam anekon aitihaasik ghatnaon or vyaktiyon ke saath saakshaatkaar or ghatana kaa punah abhinay prastut kiya jaataa he.
 history channel kaa armbh 1 janvari 1995 ko kiya gaya tha. is channel ke swaamitv adhikaar e and i television networks ke paas hai, jo hurst karporation, dijani-abisi television group or enbisi universal, kaa sanyukt udhyam he, yah sanyukt rajya america, germany, britain, australia, new jiland, portugal, ireland, isroyal, spain, poland, italy, turki, da netherlands, belgium, romania or latin america main bhinn-bhinn roopon main kaary kar rahaa he. 21 navambar 2008 tak star tivi or eitian international ke madhya ek saude ke antargat dakshin asia main bhi iskaa network uplabdh tha. sanyukt rajya america main channel lagaataar prime time kii reating de rahaa tha jo swayan e and i network se tulaneey yaa ucch tha.
 16 farvari 2008 ko, pramukh america network par iske ek nae pratik chinh kaa padaarpan kiya gaya. is pratik chinh main inke trademark "H" akshar ko purvavat hi rakhaa gaya he, baayin ore kaa tribhuj vaala aakaar vigyapanon or kaarykram ke dauraan anemeshan or fli out ke liye play batan ke rup main kaary karata he. 20 march 2008 ko, da history channel ne apane naam main se "da" or "channel" ko hataa diya jisase yah sirf "history" rah gaya.
 iske kaarykram kaalon or vishyon kii vistrut shrinkhalaa ko aachhadit karte hai, jabaki inhen vishyon ko ek hi vishay-vastu aadhaarit saptahik kaarykram or rojaanaa prasaarit hone vaale lambe kaaryakramon ke rup main vyavasthit kiya jaataa he. yah channel 80 million se bhi zyada gharon main dekha jaataa he. iske vishyon main itihaas, madhyakaalin itihaas, 19veen, 20veen or 21veen shatabdiyaan, aadhunik praudyogiki, aitihaasik jivaniyaan, abhautik vishay or aapadaa paridrishy aadi shaamil hote hai; iname se adhikansh vruttchitr edward herrrman dwaara varnit hote hai. kai kaarykram samakaleen samskritiyon or praudyogikiyon kii bhootkaal se tulana karte hai, jabaki kuch kaarykram adhik gopneey vishyon par kendrit hote hai jaise kumantranaa siddhant, dharmik vivechna, uafo se sambandhit afvaahen yaa riyality television. da history channel ek samuhik kaarykram seve aavar history kaa bhi netritv karata he, jo itihaas or aitihaasik sthalon tathaa shilpakritiyon ke sangrakshan ke liye samarpit he, yah vichaardhaara main naishanal trust for historic priderveshan ke saamaan hi he kintu yah donon alag-alag hai.
 praayah uphaas main history channel kii ore "da hitler channel" ke naam se bhi sanket kiya jaataa tha, aisa iske dwaara vishwa yuddh II kii vyapak kawarez karne ke kaaran kaha jaataa tha. haal hi main, iske adhikansh sainya vishay-vastu aadhaarit kaarykram isaki up-nigamit sanstha main sthanantarit kar die gaye hai, jiskaa naam miletry history he or ab is network ke program itihaas ke vishyon kii vividh shrinkhalaa or pariklpit bhavishya sambandhi ghatnaon se sambandhit kaaryakramon kaa prasaaran karte hai.
 is sanyukt rajya aadhaarit network kii aalochana isliye bhi hui he ki yah apni adhikansh kawarez visheshtah pashchimi vishwa or sanyukt rajya america se sambandhit aitihaasik vishyon par hi karata he .
 sanyukt rajya aadhaarit iske network kii pracheen yaa madhyayugin kaal ke vipareet apekshaakrit haalia itihaas par hi kendrit hone ke kaaran bhi aalochana kii gayi he.
 stanley katnar ne bhi san 2003 main vivaadit shrinkhalaa da main hu kild kennedy ke prasaaran ke liye is network kii aalochana kii thi; katnar un teen itihaaskaaron main se ek the jinhen is vruttchitr kii samiksha ke liye bulaayaa gaya tha, jise channel ne apana manane se inkaar kar diya or phir iskaa prasaaran kabhi nahin hua. duusari ore, modern marvels jaise kaarykram apane dwaara prastut jaankaree kii vistrut soochna or uski manoranjak prastuti ke liye saraahe gaye hai. 
 is network kii kuch shrinkhlaayein, jaise aais road trekars, eks main or pon stars ne sanyukt rajya main record reating arjeet kii he, jabaki shrinkhalaa kii gaiyr-aitihaasik prakruti ke liye isaki aalochana bhi kii gayi he. aalochakon kaa yah tark he ki is prakaar ke kaaryakramon main aitihaasik mulya kaa sarvatha abhaav tha. 
 da history channel kii ek website www.history.com, bhi he jo anya kai visheshtaon ke saath-saath anekon sandesh manchon kaa kaary bhi dekhati he jisame history of chrischienity, WWII, da civil waar, oparation iraqi freedom or curent events aadi shaamil hai.
 history ko kai baar galati se, ek aisi hi swatantra swaamitv vaali, canada kii seva, history television samaj liya jaataa he. vastav main, vaakyansh "note avelable in canada" is channel ke shuruaati varshon main iske liye vastutah ek naaraa ban gaya tha, aisa isliye hua tha kyonki canadai system main aaayaatit america channelon ke vigyapanon main iskaa prayog kiya gaya tha, jaise i and i .
 da history channel ne bharat main apana prasaaran 2003 ke ant main shuru kar diya tha jisamen news corp'sa star iske bikri saajhedaar ke rup main the, 21 navambar 2008 tak iskaa prabandhan naishanal jeeographic dwaara kiya ja rahaa tha. 21 navambar 2008 ko da history channel india band ho gaya or iskaa sthaan fox history and intertenment ne le liya, jo bharat main uplabdh fox network kaa pehla channel tha. eitian or estro ol asia networks kaa ek sanyukt udhyam singapore, honkong, thailand, philipino, malaysia, indonesia, vietnam or bruneii main 2007 ke duusare yaa tisare timahi main da history channel kii shuruyaat karne vaala he or china or taiwan main isaki shuruyaat varsh ke ant tak hogi. kuch anya asian desh jaise ki israyal or japan main is network kaa apana sanskaran he. japan, dakshin korea or philipino ke baad, 1 sitambar 2008 ko singapore or honkong main history channel asia kii aadhikarik shuruyaat kar di gayi. 
 sitambar 1997 main ek scandinavian sanskaran kii shuruyaat kii gayi thi, jo pratidin ek analog vaiasat plateform se shuruyaat main 3 ghante or baad main 4 ghante prasaarit hota tha. shuruyaat main yah tivi1000 cinema ke saath samay kii saajhedaari karata tha, baad main ise sweden ke tivi8 channel main sthanantarit kar navambar 2004 tak iskaa prasaaran jari rakhaa gaya, navambar 2004 main vaiasat ne naurdic kshetra main apana swayan kaa history channel, vaiasat history, shuru kar diya or da history channel ko band kar diya. 1 farvari 2007 ko, da history channel denmark, naurve, finland or sweden main tab phir se chaalu kar diya gaya jab iskaa britain vaala sanskaran canal digital satlite plateform par ek swatantra channel ke rup main prasaarit hone laga.
 1 farvari 2007 ko history channel canal digital dit satlite package par naurve, sweden, denmark or fin land ke darshakon ke liye shuru kiya gaya. yah channel, history channel euke, jo i and i kaa biskibi ke saath sanyukt udhyam he, ke dwaara shuru kiya ja rahaa he. haalanki yah angreji main prasaarit hoga, kintu phir bhi yah channel euke sanskaran se alag samay par dikhaaya jaayegaa. up-sahaara africa or greece main pehle se hi is da history channel ke alag-alag sanskaran hai. da biography channel or krime and investigeshan network bhi naurdic bazaar main utaare jane vaale hai.
