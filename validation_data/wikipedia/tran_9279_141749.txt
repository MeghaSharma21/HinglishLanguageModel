 † upasthiti 
 ronaldo di asis morera, ko aam taur par ronaldinho yaa ronaldinho gasho, ke naam se janaa jaataa he, ve ek brazilian footbowler hai, jo italian syriye e said milan or brazil kii rashtriya team ke liye khelte hai. unhen vyapak rup se unki peedi ke sabase pratibhashali footbaal khilaadiyon main se ek mana jaataa he.
 ronaldinho, "chota ronaldo" kaa portugali rup, ko brazil main ronaldo se, jo brazil main pehle se hi ronaldinho ke naam se janaa jaataa he, alag karne ke liye "gasho" ke upnaam se bulaayaa jaataa he. lekin europe main ronaldo ne apni pahchaan apane pehle naam se hi banaai, jiske chalate ronaldinho ko apane gasho upnaam ke prayog kii bajaae keval ronaldinho naam rakhane kaa avasar mila.
 milan main shaamil hone se pehle, ve paris saint-german or FC barsilona ke liye khelte the, jahaan unhone 2006 ke champions lig ke rup main apni pehali jeet hasil kii. janvari 2007 main ve spanish naagarik ban gaye.
 ronaldinho kaa janm brazil kii rajdhani rio grand do sul ke shahar porto elegre main hua. unki maa donna miguelina, ek purv vikreta thi, jisane ek nurse banane ke liye padhai kii. unke pita, joao, pot kaarkaane main ek mazdoor the or sthaneey club esporte klube crugerio ke footbaal khilaadi the . ronaldinho jab aath saal ke the tab unke pita ko ghar ke swimming pool main ek ghaatak hriday aghat kaa saamana karnaa padaa tha. ronaldinho ke bade bhai, roberto ke gramio ke saath anubandh ke baad, puraa parivaar porto elegre ke ek samrudh ilaake guaruja main sthanantarit ho gaya, jo roberto ko club main bane rahane ke liye gramio dwaara ek uphaar tha. lekin antat chot ke kaaran roberto kaa kareer bahut jaldi hi samaapt ho gaya.
 ronaldinho kaa footbaal hunar bahut kam umr main hi khilanaa shuru ho gaya tha or unhe pehali baar ronaldinho kaa upnaam diya gaya, kyonki ve yuva club maichon main khilne vaale sabase kam umr or chote kad ke khilaadi the.unhone sabase pehle footsal or bich footbaal main apni ruchi ko jagaaya, jiskaa baad main footbal main suvyavasthit vistaar hua. teraa saal kii umr main unka pehali baar media se saamana hua, jab unki team ne sthaneey team par 23-0 se jeet darj kii, jisamen unhone saare 23 gol daage the. misr main varsh 1997 main yojit U-17 vishwa championships main ronaldinho ko pehali baar ubharate khilaadi ke rup main pahachaanaa gaya, jisamen unhone penalty kik ke rup main do gol daage.
 aajkal, unke bade bhai roberto, ronaldinho ke prabandhak ke rup main kaary karte hai, jabaki unki bahan deisi unke press samanvayak kaa kaam karti he. 25 farvari 2005 ko pehali baar ronaldinho pita bane, jab brazilian nartki janina mendes ne unke bete ko janm diya, jiskaa naam ronaldinho ke pita ke naam par joao rakhaa gaya.
 ronaldinho ne apane kareer kii shuruuaat gramio yuva team ke saath pramukh coach lium higgins ke prashikshan main kii. varsh 1998 main, kopa libertadores pratiyogita ke dauraan unhone apane varisht khilaadi ke rup main shuruuaat kii. 2001 main, arsenal ne ronaldinho ko anubandhit karne main ruchi dikhaai, lekin ve kaamyaab nahin hue, kyonki ronaldinho ko gaiyr EU khilaadi hone or kaaphi kam antarrashtriya match khilne ke kaaran work permit nahin mila. unhone scottish premier lig said saint miren kii ore se khilne par vichaar kiya, lekin brazil main ek farji passport maamle main unke shaamil hone ke kaaran aisa kabhi nahin hua. 2001 main, ronaldinho ne france ke paris saint-german ke saath €5.1 million ke antaran main paanch saal ke anubandh par hastakshar kiya.
 2001-02 satr ke dauraan, paris saint-german ke prabandhak louis furnandis ne daawa kiya ki ronaldinho footbaal kii bajaae paris ke raatri jivan par adhik kendrit the or shikaayat kii ki brazil main unhone apni chhuttiyaan kabhi nirdhaarit samay par khatm nahin kii. PSG main do varsh kii karyavadhi puraa karne se pehle hi 2003 main, ronaldinho ne PSG ke kisi bhi european pratiyogita ke liye arhata praapt na karne par, spasht kar diya ki ve club chhodana chaahate hai. 
 multah, FC barsilona ke adhyaksh joan laporta ne club main david bekham ko laane kaa vaada kiya tha, lekin unke real madrid main sthanantaran ho jane ke baad, barsilona, ronaldinho ko team main laane kii daud main shaamil ho gaya or nilaamee main manchester united se zyaada boli lagaakar ronaldinho ko €32,250,000 ke antaran main anubandhit kiya. unhone team main apana pehla match washington DC ke RFK stadium main milan ke khilaaf ek dostana match main khelaa or unki team ne 2-0 se jeet darj kii, jisamen ronaldinho ne ek gol daaga. abhiyaan ke pratham bhaag ke dauraan unke chotil ho jane ke kaaran ve ghar laut aae or barsilona ko lig main doosraa sthaan praapt karaane main madad kii.
 varsh 2004-05 main ronaldinho ne apana pehla lig khitaab jeeta or 20 disambar 2004 ko FIFA ke sarvshereshth khilaadi chune gaye. 2005 main, ronaldinho ne chelsia ke frank lampard or saathi khilaadi samuel ato ko maat dekar lagaataar duusari baar FIFA kaa sarvshereshth khilaadi kaa sammaan praapt kiya. 8 march 2005 ko, UEFA champions lig ke pehle nock-out charan main hi chelsia ne barsilona ko pratiyogita se baahar kar diya, haalanki 4-2 kii is paraajay main brazil kii taraf se donon hi gol ronaldinho ne daage.
 varsh 2008 main jab ronaldinho kaa club ke saath anubandh samaapt hone vaala tha, club ne ronaldinho ke samaksh 2014 tak anubandh ko badhaane kaa prastaav rakhaa, jiske tahat anubandh ke nau varshon main unhen kareeb £85 million diya jaataa, lekin ronaldinho ne ise thukraa diya. sitambar 2005 main unhone do saal ke ek aise vistaar par hastakshar kiya, jiske antargat ek nunatam shulk release shart shaamil tha, jo kisi club dwaara unke liye barsilona ko kam se kam £85 million kaa prastaav die jane par, unhen kisi bhi samay club chhodane kii anumati detaa. 
 2004-05 satr ke ant tak, ronaldinho ne dheron vyaktigat puraskaar batorne shuru kar die. sitambar 2005 main unhone pehali baar shuru kiye gaye FIFPro vishwa khilaadi kaa khitaab apane naam kiya, iske alaava 2005 main unhen FIFPro world XI main bhi shaamil kiya gaya or 2005 kaa european footbaal khilaadi ke khitaab se bhi nawaaza gaya. isi varsh, ronaldinho ne apane puraskaron ke sangrah main duusari baar FIFA vishwa khilaadi kaa khitaab joda, unhone 956 ankon ke saath yah khitaab apane naam kiya jo duusare sthaan par kaabiz frank lampard ke ankon se lagbhag teen guna se bhi adhik tha. 19 navambar ko,El Clásico main barsilona ne real madrid ko 3-0 se haraaya jisamen ronaldinho ne akele do gol daage. apane duusare gol ke saath jab unhone match ko samaapt kiya to madrid ke prashansakon ne khadey hokar ronaldinho ke liye taaliyaan bajaai.
 ronaldinho ko lagaataar teesri baar janvari 2006 main UEFA team ke liye chunaa gaya or 05-06 ke champions lig ke quarter final main barsilona ne SL benfika ko 2-0 se haraakar pratiyogita se baahar kar diya, is 2-0 kii gharelu jeet main ronaldinho ne ek gol daaga. semifinal main milan ko 1-0 se haraane ke baad, jisamen ludovik giuli dwaara lagaae gaye ekmaatr gol main ronaldinho ne uski madad kii, barsilona champions lig ke final main pahunchaa or 17 may 2006 ko barsilona ne arsenal ko 2-1 se haraakar jeet hasil kii. do hafte pehle hi, barsilona ne selta vigo ko 1-0 se haraakar duusari baar laa liga khitaab par kabza kiya tha, vahiin ronaldinho ko unke kareer main pehali baar dohari jeet praapt hui. is satr kii samaapti tak 26 gol laga kar ronaldinho ne apane kareer kaa behtareen pradarshan kiya or unhen 2005-06 champions lig ke sarvshereshth khilaadi ke khitaab se nawaaza gaya. 
 25 navambar 2006 ko ronaldinho ne apane lig kareer kaa 50waan gol villarrial ke khilaaf lagaaya, isi match main unhone doosraa gol overhead bicical kik ke dwaara maaraa. baad vaale gol ke bare main unhone samvaadadaataaon se kaha ki ve bachapan se hi is tarah ke gol maarane kaa swapn dekha karte the. 14 disambar ko, club world cap main barsilona ne mexico ke club america ko 4-0 se haraaya, jisamen ronaldinho ne ek gol kiya or anya do golon main madad kii, lekin final main brazilian club international ne barsilona ko 1-0 se maat di. tab bhi, is pratiyogita main ronaldinho ko bronz ball award se sammanit kiya gaya.
 agle hi din, 2006 ke FIFA sarvshereshth khilaadi kii daud main ronaldinho ko teesraa sthaan praapt hua or ve vishwa cap vijeta captain fibio kanavaro or zinedin ziden ke peeche rahe. janvari 2007 main UEFA varsh kii team ke liye ronaldinho ko lagaataar teesri baar chunaa gaya or unhen 290,000 se bhi adhik sankhya main mat praapt hue. chotil hone ke kaaran, 13 march ke ek charity match main ronaldinho khel nahin sake, yah zakhm unhen kai din pehle El Clásico main barsilona or real madrid ke bich hue match main laga tha, jo 3-3 ke score par drow ho gaya tha.
 3 farvari 2008 ko unhone apane kareer kaa 200waan lig match barsilona ke liye CA osasuna ke khilaaf khelaa. haalanki, varsh 2007-08 ke apane khel abhiyaan main ve zyaadaatar choton se grast rahe or 3 april ko daahine paanv main mansapeshi ke khichaanv ke kaaran samay se pehle hi apana satr samaapt kar diya. 19 may ko, laporta ne kaha ki ronaldinho ko "nai chunauti " kii aavashyaktaa he, unhone daawa kiya ki unhen ek nae club kii jarurat he jahaan ve apane kareer kii shuruuaat nae sire se kar saken. manchester city ke maalik thaksin shinawatra ne 6 june ko pushti kii ki ve unhen adhigruhit karne ke icchhuk the.
 28 june ko ronaldinho or barsilona team ke saathi khilaadi leonelle messi ne venezuela main naslwad ke virodh main ek pradarshnee match main antarrashtriya khilaadiyon se saji do tiimon kii captaini kii, jo 7-7 ke score se barabari par samaapt hua. barsilona ke khilaadi ke rup main ronaldinho kaa ye aakhri match tha jisamen unhone do gol kiye or anya do golon main madad kii. 
 july 2008 main ronaldinho ne menchester city ke £25.5 million kii peshkash ko thukaraate hue italian siriye e jayant milan main prati varsh kareeb £5.1 million anumaanit mulya ke teen saal ke anubandh ko sweekaar kiya, jisaki keemat kareeb £14.5 million thi. 10 nambar kii jersey par team ke hi khilaadi clarence sidorf ne pehle hi kabza kar liya tha, isiilie ronaldinho ne apane jersey ke liye 80 nambar ko chunaa kyonki 1980 unka janm varsh tha. 28 sitambar 2008 main milan kii taraf se ronaldinho ne darbi main apana pehla gol international ke khilaaf lagaaya, jisamen milan ne 1-0 kii jeet darj kii or 19 oktober 2008 main sampdoriya ke khilaaf 3-0 se jeet darj karnaa unka pehla bres tha. 6 navambar ko unhone UEFA cap ke group charan main SC braga ke khilaaf 93wein minute main gol lagaakar match vijeta bane.
 milan ke pehle satr main samast pratiyogitaaon main ronaldinho ne 32 prastutiyon main 10 gol maare. pehle satr kii acchhee shuruuaat ke baad, ronaldinho ko ek baar phir fitness ke saath sangharsh karnaa padaa or fitness kii samasya ke chalate ve kuch hi match khel paae, jiske kaaran milan ke liye pehla satr niraashajanak rahaa. 
 ronaldinho ke duusare satr kii shuruuaat utani thos nahin rahi, haalanki lagbhag sabhi maichon main ve maidan main utaare lekin sheeghra hi vishram karte nazar aate. kuch hi samay main unhone phir se apane puraane lay ko paa liya or yaqinan us satr ke milan ke sarvshereshth khilaadi bane. unhone apni bhoomika atekking midfilder se left winger main tabadil kar di: jo ek adhik parichit bhoomika thi. 8 anko ke saath ve abhi vartmaan main ceriye e main acist chart main sabase aage hai. 
 ronaldinho brazil ke un chand khilaadiyon main se hai jinhone har umr ke antarrashtriya str ke match khele hai. jab pehali baar 1997 main brazil team ne FIFA U-17 vishwa championships jeeta tha tab ronaldinho bhi us team kaa hissaa the, jiske pehle group maichon main austria ke khilaaf ronaldinho ne apana pehla gol panaalty ke rup main maaraa tha, jisamen brazil 7-0 vijayi hua. antim rup se is puri shrinkhalaa main ronaldinho ke khate main keval do gol aaye or chunki brazil ne do gol khate hue kul 21 gol maare, unhen branj ball puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. 
 antarrashtriya match khilne kii drushti se varsh 1999 ronaldinho ke liye kaaphi vyast varsh tha. varsh 1999 ke FIFA world youth championships main ronaldinho ne bhaag liya or brazil ke aakhri group match main unhone apana pehla gol maaraa. solah charan main, kroeshia par 4-0 se jeet darj karne ke dauraan unhone pehle haaf main do gol kiye or antim rup se teen gol hi lagaaya chunki kwaterfinal main hi urugve ne brazil ko haraakar shrinkhalaa se baahar kar diya tha. 1999 kopa america main shrinkhalaa ke shuru hone ke teen din pehle yani 26 june ko brazil ne latvia ko 3-0 se haraaya, jisamen ronaldinho ne brazil ke liye pehla cap hasil kiya or unhone brazil ke kopa america vijay abhiyaan ke dauraan ek gol daaga. kopa america ke nishkarsh ke ek saptaah ke baad, 1999 kanfederations cap ke liye ronaldinho ko aamantrit kiya gaya, jiske final ke alaava unhone sabhi maichon main gol daage, jisamen saudi arab par 8-2 kii jeet main ronaldinho kaa hetric gol bhi shaamil he. final match main ronaldinho ek gol bhi nahin maar sake, jisamen mexico ne brazil ko 4-3 se haraaya. ronaldinho ko is toornament ke sarvshereshth khilaadi ke taur par golden ball puraskaar or saath hi toornament main sabase adhik gol maarane ke liye golden boot kaa puraskaar diya gaya.
 2000 main, ronaldinho ne brazil U-23 team ke saath australia ke sidney main yojit samar olympic main bhaag liya. is varsh pehle ronaldinho ne brazil kii pre olympic toornament jeet main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai or saat maichon main unhone nau gol daage. tathaapi olympic ke kwaterfinal main brazil ko camerun ne haraaya, jisane baad main swarna padak jeeta. haalanki ronaldinho chaar baar gol maarane kii sthiti main dikhaai die lekin ek hi baar gol kar paae, jiske chalate kwaterfinal main camerun dwaara team ko haar kaa munh dekhnaa padaa. 
 varsh 2002 ke vishwa cap main ronaldinho ne pehali baar ronaldo or rivaldo ke saath bharosemand offensive tukdi ke rup main hissaa liya, jo varsh 1999 ke kopa america jeetne vaali team main shaamil the. unhone paanch maichon main keval do gol kiye. unhone apana pehla gol, group stage match main china ke khilaaf maaraa, jisamen brazil ne 4-0 se jeet darj kii. 21 june ko england ke khilaaf kwaterfinal main unka doosraa gol nirnaayak gol rahaa jisane match main jeet dilaai. 50wein minute main, ronaldinho ne 35 meter kii duuri se free kik-liya, jo england ke golkeeper david siman ko parrast karte hue gol ke rup main tabadil hua or brazil ko 2-1 se badhat mili. haalanki, saat minute ke baad hi england ke dephender danni mills ke saath foul karne ke liye ronaldinho ko baahar bhej diya gaya. unhen semifinal ke liye nilambit kar diya gaya, lekin final main unki vaapasi hui jisamen brazil ne germany ko 2-0 se haraate hue paanchavi baar vishwa cap par kabza kiya.
 ronaldinho kaa aglaa antarrashtriya toornament varsh 2003 kaa canfederations cap tha. baharhaal, is toornament ke dauraan ronaldinho ne koi gol nahin kiya or pratiyogita main brazil kaa pradarshan kaaphi kharaab rahaa, jo group stage main hi pratiyogita se baahar ho gaya. agle saal, brazil ke 2004 kopa america squad se ronaldinho ko baahar rakhaa gaya, chunki team ke coach carlos alberto pareira ne apane bade khilaadiyon ko aaraam dene or arakshit khilaadiyon kaa istemaal karne kaa faisla kiya. 
 2005 main yojit dwitiya canfederation cap main ronaldinho ne captaini kii or mukhya pratidwandvi argentina ke saath hue final main 4-1 se jeet darj kii, jisamen unhen main of da match ke khitaab se sammanit kiya gaya. ronaldinho ne is puri pratiyogita main teen gol kiye or nau gol ke saath toornament ke sarvakalik scorer ke rup main Cuauhtémoc Blanco ke saath barabari kii. 
 ronaldinho ne brazil ke 2006 vishwa cap ke sabhi paanch final maichon kii shuruuaat edriyano, ronaldo or kaaka ke saath offensive khilaadiyon ke "magic quartate " kaa hissaa ban kar kii. haalanki, in chaaron ne antim rup se puri pratiyogita main keval paanch gol hi lagaae or puri pratiyogita main brazil ne samgr rup se niraash kiya. ronaldinho ne is toornament main apane antarrashtriya kareer kaa sabase kharaab samuhik pradarshan kiya, isamen ve koi gol nahin kar paae lekin japan ke saath group stage match main gilberto ko gol maarane main keval ek sahaayta kii, jisamen brazil ne japan par 4-1 se jeet darj kii. ve is pratiyogita main beasar sabit hue or kwaterfinal main france ne brazil ko 1-0 se haraakar pratiyogia se baahar kar diya, is puure match main brazil ko gol maarane kaa keval ek hi mauka mila. khilaadiyon ke ghar waapas aane ke baad brazilian darshakon or media ke dwaara team kii katu aalochana kii gai. 3 july ko, pratiyogita se baahar hone ke do din baad, kuch updravi logon ne chepeko main ronaldinho kii shishe se bani kareeb 7.5-meter lambi pratimaa ko nasht kar diya. varsh 2004 main ronaldinho ke pehle FIFA world player of da year banane kii khushi main is murti ko sthapit kiya gaya tha.usee din, ronaldinho, edriyano ke saath barsilona laute or apane ghar par ek jashn kaa aayojan kiya, jo subah tadake tak nite club main jari rahaa. is baat se brazil ke kuch prashansakon kii aahat bhaawanaaon ko or adhik thes pahunchi, jinka maananaa tha ki in khilaadiyon ke prayaas ke abhaav ke kaaran unhen thaga gaya he.
 2005 ke canfederation cap final ke baad, 24 march 2007 main chile par 4-0 kii jeet main ronaldinho ne pehali baar do gol maare, jisane unki do saal se chali aa rahi bina gol kii avadhi ko samaapt kiya. thakaan kii vajah se pratiyogita se baahar jane kii anumati lene ke baad unhen 2007 kopa america ke liye aamantrit nahin kiya gaya. brazil ke ecuador par 5-0 kii jeet ke baad spain main deree se aane ke liye 18 oktober ko unhen vivaadit rup se bench par baitha diya gaya. jeet ke baad unhone or kai brazili khilaadiyon ne rio di janero ke ek mahange nite club main raat bhar party kii. ronaldinho vahaan se agali subah kareeb 11 baje nikle or media se bachane ke liye kathith rup se ek car ke trunk main gaye.
 7 july 2008, main 2008 samar olympic ke liye brazil team ke scowad main umradraj khilaadiyon ke rup main ronaldinho ko namit kiya gaya. club ke saath ronaldinho kii aagaamee champions lig kii sharton ke kaaran barsilona ne shuru main is kadam par pabandi laga di, lekin ronaldinho ke milan main sthaanantaran ke baad yah nirnay nirast kar diya gaya, jiske parinamswaroop unhen beijing jane kii anumati mili. brazil ke semifinal main argentina se haarane se pehle newjiland ke saath hue ek nirnaayak match main 5-0 kii jeet main ronaldinho ne keval do gol kiye. kaansya padak ke match main belgium ko 3-0 se haraane ke baad brazil ko antim rup se kaansya padak praapt hua.
 club AC milan ke liye kharaab pradarshan ke chalate ronaldinho ne brazil squad main apana sthaan kho diya he, jiske kaaran yah sandehaaspad he ki ve 2010 FIFA vishwa cap ke liye dakshin africa ke liye prasthaan karenge yaa nahin.
