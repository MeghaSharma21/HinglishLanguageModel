 colins 1819 main sthapit ek scotland kii prakaashak company he jo ab harper colins kaa ang he. isaki sthapana glassgo ke ek adhyaaapak wiliam colins ne kii thi. aarambh main ye dharmik or shaikshanik kitaaben nikaalati thim lekin 1950 main kai kahaniyon tathaa kalpana-smriti jaisi kitaaben nikaalkar prasiddh hui. 1990 main ise america ke harper and ro ne karidakar harper-colins banaaya. abhi ye duniya ke 6 sabase bade prakaashak samoohon main se ek he.
