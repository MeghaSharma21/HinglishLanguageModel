 pitruvansh samooh aar1e yaa hapalogrup R1a manushyon main waahe gun sutara kaa ek varg he, yani kii sabhi purush jinamein is vansh samooh ke chinh hai ek hi aitihaasik purush kii santan hai. anumaan lagaaya jaataa he ke yah purush aaj se 18,500 saal pehle jivit tha. is pitruvansh ke purush bharat, pakistan, madhya asia, rus, purvi europe or scandinavian deshon main paae jate hai.
