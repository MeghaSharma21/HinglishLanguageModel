 ek rishta 2001 kii sunil darshan dwaara nirdeshit or nirmit hindi bhashaa kii film he. film main amitabh bachchan, raakhi, akshay kumaar, juhi chawla, karishma kapur, mohanish bahal hai. yah amitabh or akshay kii ek saath pehali film thi. is film kii yojana main ek pita, unke bete or unki beti ke bare main ek kahani banane kii thi. haalanki jab tak film utpaadan charan main ai, tab tak chawla garbhvatee ho gayi. isliye unki bhoomika ko atithi bhoomika main badla gaya.
 vijay kapur ek safal vyavsaayee he. vah apni patni or unke chaar bacchon ke saath khushi se rahata he. ajay, unka ekmaatr betaa jo videsh main pard rahaa tha or vishvavidhyalay se snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kar chukaa he. usko soochna praudyogiki main academik degree mili or ab ghar waapas aa gaya.
 rajesh purohit parivaar main pravesh karata he or vijayi rup se vijay ko apni dakshata or uski sabase badi beti priti ko apane aakarshan ke saath prabhavit karata he. jald hi vijay priti or rajesh ke vivaah ko manjuuri de detaa he. is bich ajay, nisha se pyaar karata he or usase shaadi karne kii yojana banaata he.
 vijay pehle ajay ko paariwarik vyavsaay main anubhav hasil karne ke liye aashwast karata he. jabaki ajay apana khud kaa vyavsaay shuru karnaa chahta he. haalanki, vaicharik matbhed ke kaaran donon main matbhed hota he.
