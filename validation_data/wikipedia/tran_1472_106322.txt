 ve jeev jinhen manushya nangi ankhon se nahi dekh saktaa tathaa jinhen dekhne ke liye sookshmdarshi yantra kii aavashyaktaa padataa he, unhen sookshmjeev kehete hai. sookshmjaiviki main sookshmjivon kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. 
 sookshmjivon kaa sansar atyant vividhta se bahra hua he. sookshmjivon ke antargat sabhi jeevanu or orchia tathaa lagbhag sabhi protojoa ke alaava kuch kavak, shaiwal, or chakradhar aadi jeev aate hai. bahut se anya jeevon tathaa padpon ke shishu bhi sookshmjeev hi hote hai. kuch sookshmjeevigyaani vishaanuon ko bhi sookshmjeev ke andar rakhte hai kintu anya log inhen 'nirjeev' maanate hai.
 sookshmjeev sarvyaapee hote hai. yah mrudaa, jal, vaayu, hamaare sharir ke andar tathaa anya prakaar ke praaniyon tathaa padpon main paae jate hai. jahaan kisi prakaar jivan sambhav nahin he jaise gizar ke bheetar gaharaai tak, jahaan taap 100 degree celsius tak badha hua rahata he, mrudaa main gaharaai tak, barf kii parton ke kai meter neeche tathaa ucch amleey paryaavaran jaise sthanon par bhi paae jate hai. 
 jeevanu tathaa adhikansh kavakon ke samaan sookshmjiviyon ko poshak media par ugaaya ja saktaa he, taaki vruddhi kar yah colony kaa rup le len or inhen nagn netron se dekha ja sake. aise sanvardhanjan sookshmjiviyon par adhyayan ke dauraan kaaphi laabhadaayak hote hai.
 sookshmjeev prithvi par upasthit jivan ke bahut hi mahatvapurn ghatak hai. praayah sabhi jaanate hai ki sookshmjeev manushyon main bahut se rog utpann karte hai. ye pashuon tathaa padpon main bhi rog utpann karte hai, parantu iskaa yah arth nahin he ki sabhi sookshmjeev haniprad hai. 
 bahut se sookshmjeev manushyon ke liye atyant hi laabhprad hote hai. ham pratidin sookshmjivon tathaa sookshmjivon se vyutpann utpaadon kaa prayog karte hai. lactic acid bacteria doodh main vruddhi karte hai jisase vah dahi main badal jaataa he. bread banane main prayukt geele gunthe aate kaa kinvan secoromisij serivisi naamak yeast se kiya jaataa he. kuch pakwaan jaise idli tathaa dosa aise geele aate se banaae jate hai jinhen sookshmjiviyon dwaara kinvit kiya gaya hota he. jeevanu tathaa kavakon kaa prayog 'chiz' ko ek vishesh banaavat, swaad tathaa sungadh dene ke liye kiya jaataa he. sookshmjivon kaa prayog audyogic utpaada jaise lactic acid, acetic acid tathaa alcohol utpann karne main kiya jaataa he. pratijaivik jaise panicilin kaa utpaadan laabhprad sookshmjivon dwaara kiya jaataa he. pratijaivik sankramak rog jaise dipthiria, kaali khaansi, tathaa nimonia kii rokthaam main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaate hai. sau varshon se adhik ke samay se sookshmjivon kaa prayog wahiwamal ke upachaar ke liye kiya ja rahaa he. isse prakruti main jal ke punahchakran main bhi sahaayta milti he. mithanogen sanyantr apshisht ke apghaton ke dwaara methane utpann karte hai. gramin kshetron main sookshmjeev dwaara utpann byogas kaa upyog urja ke rup main kiya jaataa he. sookshmjivon kaa prayog jaivaniyantran vidhi dwaara haniprad pidakon ko maarane ke liye bhi kiya jaataa he. jaivaniyantran maapan se vifaille pidaknashiyon ke prayog main baari kami ai he. aaj samay kii maang he ki rasinic uvarrakon ke sthaan par jaev urvarkon kaa prayog kiya jaay. 
 udyogon main bhi sookshmjivon kaa prayog bahut se utpaadon ke sanshleshan main kiya jaataa he jo manushya ke liye kaaphi moolyawaan hote hai. maadak peya tathaa pratijaivik iske kuch udhaaharan hai. vyavsaayik paimaane par sookshmjiviyon ko paidaa karne ke liye bade bartan kii aavashyaktaa hoti he jise 'farmentre' yaa 'kinvak' kehete hai.
 sookshmjeev kaa prayog pracheen kaal se wine, bear, hniski, brandi yaa ram jaise peyon ke utpaadan main kiya jaataa aa rahaa he. vahi yeast sacaromisij seribisi bread banane tathaa maltikrut dhaanyon tathaa falon ke rason main aithanol utpann karne main prayog kiya jaataa he. kinvan tathaa vibhinn prakaar ke sansaadhan kachche padaarthon par nirbhar karti he : wine tathaa bear kaa utpaadan bina aswan ke kiya jaataa he jabaki hniski, brandi tathaa ram kinvit ras ke aswan dwaara taiyaar kiye jate hai.
 sookshmjivon dwaara pratijaivikon kaa utpaadan 20veen shatabdi kii atyant hi mahatvapurn khoj or maanav samaaj ke kalyaan ke liye ek bahut badi uplabdhi maani jaati he. pratijaivik ek prakaar ke rasaynic padaarth hai, jinka nirmaan kuch sookshmjiviyon dwaara hota he. yah anya sookshmjiviyon kii vruddhi ko mand kar sakate hai athwa unhen maar sakate hai. painicilin sabase pehla antibiotic tha. is antibiotic kaa prayog duusare vishwa yuddh main ghayal american sipaahiyon ke upachaar main vyapak rup se kiya gaya.
 panicilin ke baad anya sookshmjiviyon se anya antibioticon ko banaaya gaya. plig, kaali khaansi, dipthiria, kushtarog jaise bhayanak rog, jinase sansar main lakhon log mare hai, ke upachaar ke liye antibioticon ne hamaari kshamta main vruddhi kii ek shakti ke rup main aaye hai. aaj ham antibioticon se rahit sansar kii kalpana hi nahin kar sakate hai.
 carbonic amla, alcohol tathaa anzime aadi kuch vishesh prakaar ke rasayanon ke vyavsaayik tathaa audyogic utpaadan main sookshmjivon kaa bade paimaane par upyog kiya jaataa he. 
 amleey utpaadakon ke udhaaharan 
 aithanol ke bade paimaane par utpaadan ke liye yeast kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. lipej kaa prayog dhulaai main kapadon se tel ke dhabbe hataane main kiya jane laga he. ham bazaar se kharid kar fal-ras kii jo bottle laate hai uskaa ras ghar main bane ras kii tulana main adhik saaf dikhaai padataa he. pactinegij tathaa proteiagij ke prayog ke kaaran bottle vaala ras adhik svachh evam saaf hota he. streptocinage streptococus jeevanu dwaara utpann hota he jo anuvanshik ejineering dwaara roopantarit kiya jaataa he. iskaa prayog rogiyon ke rakt vahikaaon se thakka hataane ke liye kiya jaataa he. 
 anya jaev sakriy anu ‘cychlosporin-e’ he. jiskaa prayog ang pratiropan main pratiraksha nirodhak kaarak ke rup main rogiyon main kiya jaataa he. iskaa utpaadan tricoderma polospormanaamak kavak se kiya jaataa he. monoscus parpurius yeast se utpann is statin kaa vyapaarik str par prayog rakt-cholestrol ko kam karne vaale kaarak ke rup main kiya jaataa he. cholestrol ke sanshleshan ke liye uttaradaayi anzime spardhaa sandman kii tarah kriya karte hai.
 ham jaanate hai ki pratidin nagar evam shaharon se vyarth-jal kii ek bahut badi maatr janit hoti he. is vyarth jal kaa pramukh ghatak manushya kaa malmutra he. nagar ke is vyarth jal ko wahiwamal bhi kehete hai. isamen carbonic padaarthon kii badi maatr tathaa sookshmjeev paye jate hai jo adhikanshtah rogajanakeey hote hai. wahiwamal kii badi maatr athwa shahari vyarthjal kaa rojaanaa niptaan kaise kiya jaay? ise prakritik jal sroton jaise nadi, jharne main sidhe visarjeet nahin kiya ja saktaa he. visarjan se purv wahiwamal kaa upachaar wahiwamal sanyantr main kiya jaataa he taaki vah pradooshan mukt ho jaay. 
 sookshmjeev pratidin vishvabhar main vyarth jal ke lakhon-karodon galen pani ke upachaar main mahatvapurn bhoomika-nibhaate hai. sansar ke lagbhag-samast bhaagon main lagbhag sadiyon se isi kaary pranali kaa prayog kiya jaataa aa rahaa he. aaj ke din tak koi bhi manushya dwaara taiyaar kii gai praudyogiki wahit mel ke sookshmjivi upachaar ke saamne nahin tik pai.
 badhate hue shaharikaran ke kaaran pehle kii tulana main wahit mel kii ek bahut badi maatr utpann ho jaati he, atah wahiwamal kii itani badi maatr ko upachaarit karne main in upachaar sanyantren kii sankhya paryaapt nahin badhaai gai he. isliye apchaarit wahiwamal ko sidhe hi nadiyon main chhod diya jaataa he. parinamswaroop pradooshan or jal janit rogon kii sankhya bad rahi he.
 vyarth jal kaa upachaar parposhit sookshmjeev se kiya jaataa he jo wahit mel main prakritik rup se vaas karte hai. yah upachaar nimnlikhit do charnon main sampann kiya jaataa he-
 praathmik upachaar - upachaar ke is charan main wahit mel se bade-chote kanon ko nisyandan tathaa avsaadan dwaara bhautik rup se alag kar diya jaataa hai. inhen bhinn-bhinn charnon main alag kiya jaataa he. aarambh main tairte hue kooday-karkat ko anukramik nisyandan dwaara hataa diya jaataa he. iske baad shitabalukashm  ko avsaadan dwaara nishkasit kiya jaataa he. sabhi thos jo praathmik aanpak ke neeche baithe kan hai, vah or plavi bahirisrav kaa nirmaan karata he. bahirisrav ko praathmik nihsaadan tanks se dwitiyak upachaar ke liye le jaaya jaataa he.
 dwitiyak upachaar athwa jeev vigyaneey upachaar - praathmik bahirisrav ko bade vayuviya tinkon main se guzaara jaataa he jahaan yah lagaataar yantric rup se hilaayaa jaataa he or vaayu ko isamen pump kiya jaataa he. isse laabhadaayak vayuviya sookshmjiviyon kii prabal shashakt vruddhi urnak ke rup main hone lagati he. vruddhi ke dauraan yah sookshmjeev bahirisrav main upasthit carbonic padaarthon ke pramukh bhaagon kii khapat karata he. yah bahirisrav ke bi o di ko mahatvapurn rup se ghataane lagata he. bi o di oxigen kii us maatr ko sanderbhit karata he jo jeevanu dwaara ek litre pani main upasthit carbonic padaarthon kii khapat kar unhen oxikrut kar den. wahit mel kaa tab tak upachaar kiya jaataa he jab tak bi o di ghat na jaay. jal ke ek namoonay main sookshmjiviyon dwaara oxigen ke udgrahan kii dar kaa maapan bi o di pareekshan se kiya jaataa he. atah apratyaksh rup se jal main upasthit carbonic padaarthon kaa maapan hi bi o di he. jab vyarth-jal kaa bi o di adhik hoga, tab isaki pradooshan kshamta bhi adhik hogi.
 ek baar wahit mel athwa vyarth jal kaa bi o di paryaapt maatr main ghat jaay, tab bahirisrav ko nihsaadan tanks main bhejte hai jahaan jeevanu jhund use avsaad main parivartit karte hai. yah avsaad sakriyit aapank kehalaata he. sakriyit aapank ke chote se bhaag ko phir se peeche vayuviya tanks main pump karte hai. yah aapank niveshadravya kii tarah se kaary karata he. aapank kaa bachaa-khuchaa mukhya bhaag bade tanks main pump kiya jaataa he jise avayaviya aapank sampachitr kehete hai. yahan jeevanuon kii anya kismein jo avayuviya rup se vruddhi karti hai. ve aapank main upasthit jeevanuon tathaa kavakon kaa pachan kar leti hai. pachan ke dauraan jeevanu gaison kaa mishran jaise methane, hydrogen sulphide tathaa carbon diaksaide utpann karte hai. gaison kaa yah mishran biogas kehalaata he. chunki yah gas jvalansheel hoti hai, is kaaran inkaa prayog urja ke strot ke rup main kiya ja saktaa he.
 dwitiyak upachaar plant se bahirisrav saamanytah jal ke prakritik sroton jaise nadiyon, jharanon main chhod diya jaataa he.
 biogas kai gaison kaa mishran he tathaa sookshmjivi sakriyata dwaara utpann hoti he. ham jaanate hai ki vruddhi tathaa upaapchayan ke dauraan sookshmjeev vibhinn kismon ke gasiy utpaada utpann karte hai. jo gas utpann hoti he, vah in sookshmjiviyon dwaara khapat kiye gaye carbonic padaarthon par nirbhar karti he. 
 geele aate kaa kinvan, paneer nirmaan tathaa peyon kaa utpaadan main CO2 gas hi mukhya rup se utpann hoti he. yadhyapi kuch bacteria jo sailulogy padaarthon par avayuviya rup se ugte hai vah CO2 tathaa H2 ke saath-saath badi maatr main methane bhi utpann karte hai. samuhik rup se in jeevanuon ko mithanogen kehete hai. inamen saamaanya jeevanu mithanobacterium he. yah bacteria saamanytah avayuviya gadhe keechad main paaya jaataa he. yah jeevanu pashuon ke rumen main bhi paae jate hai. rumen main sailulogy padaarthon kii ek badi maatr uplabdh rahati he. rumen main yah jeevanu sailyulos ko todane main sahaayak hote hai or pashuon ke poshan main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaate hai.
 isi prakaar, yah jeevanu pashuon ke mel main prachur sankhya main paae jate hai. gobar main padpon ke sailulogy vyutpann prachur maatr main hote hai. atah inkaa prayog biogas ko paidaa karne main kiya jaataa he jise saamanytah 'gobar gas' bhi kehete hai. 
 biogas sanyantr ek tanks hota he jisamen apshisht evam gobar kii kardam bhari jaati he. kardam ke upar ek sachal dhakkan rakhaa jaataa he. sookshmjivi sakriyata ke kaaran tanks main gas banati he jisase dhakkan upar ko uthta he. biogas sanyantr main ek nikaas hota he jo ek pipe se judaa rahata he. isi pipe kii sahaayta se aas-paas ke gharon main biogas kii aapurti kii jaati he. upyog kii gai kardam duusare nikaas dwaar se baahar nikaal di jaati he. iskaa prayog urvarak ke rup main kiya jaataa he.
 padap rogon tathaa pidakon ke niyantran ke liye jeevvaigyanik vidhi kaa prayog hi jaev niyantran he. aadhunik samaaj main in samasyaon ke niyantran ke liye rasayanon, kitnasiyon tathaa pidaknashiyon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. kintu ye rasayan manushyon tathaa jeev-jantuon ke liye atyant vifaille tathaa hanikaarak hai. ye paryaavaran ko pradooshit karte tathaa falon, saag-subjiyon or fasalon par bhi hanikaarak prabhaav daalate hai. kharpatvaar nasiyon kaa prayog kharpatvaar ko hataane main kiya jaataa he. yah bhi hamaari mrudaa ko pradooshit karte hai.
 pidakon tathaa rogon kaa jaivaniyantran- krishi main, pidakon ke niyantran kii yah vidhi rasayanon ke prayog kii tulana main 'prakritik parbhakshan' par adhik nirbhar karti he. jaev vividhta hi swasthya kii kunjee he. bhudrishy par jitani adhik kismein hongi, vah utani hi adhik sthayitv pradaan karti hai. atah jaivik krishak ek tantra ko viksit karne ke liye kaary karte hai, jisamen keet unmilit na ho, ve iske bajaay unhen niyantraneey str par banaaye rakhane main vishwaas karte hai. jaev niyantran ek prakaar kaa 'samgr drushtikon' he, jisase karodon jeevon ke madhya parsparik kriyaaon ke jaal kii vyakhya kaa vikaas hota he. khet pranijat tathaa ped-paudhon kaa nirmaan karte hai. argenic krishak kaa drushtikon yah he ki pidakon kaa unmoolan keval asambhav hi nahin balki avaanchhneeya bhi he. in pidakon ke bina laabhprad parabhakshi tathaa paraseete keet jivit nahin rah paayenge jo pidakon par apane poshan athwa bhojan ke liye ashrith he. 
 aaj paryaavaran pradooshan chintaa kaa ek mukhya kaaran he. krishi utpaadon kii badhati maangon ko puraa karne ke liye rasaynic urvarkon kaa prayog is pradooshan kaa pramukh kaaran he. log ab samajhane lage hai ki rasaynic urvarkon ke adhikadhik prayog se kai samasyaayen judi hui hai. iske parinamswaroop jaivik kheti karne par tathaa jaev urvarkon ke prayog par bal diya ja rahaa he. haal hi main, bharat main jaev urvarkon kii ek badi sankhya bade paimaane par bazaar main uplabdh hone lagi he. kisaan apane kheton main lagaataar inkaa prayog kar rahe hai. isse mrudaa poshak kii bharpai tathaa rasayan urvarkon par ashritha bhi kam ho rahi he.
 jaev urvarak ek prakaar ke jeev hai jo mrudaa kii poshak gunvattta ko badhaate hai. inkaajai mukhya strot jeevanu, kavak tathaa cynobacteria hote hai. dwidaleey padpon kii jadon par sthit grathiyon kaa nirmaan rijobium ke sahjivi sambandh dwaara hota he. yah jeevanu vayumandaleey nitrogen ko sthirikrut kar ise carbonic rup main parivartit kar dete hai jisase padap iskaa prayog poshakon ke rup main karte hai. anya jeevanu mrudaa main muktavastha main rahete hai. yah bhi vayumandaleey nitrogen ko sthir kar sakate hai. is prakaar mrudaa main nitrogen avyav bad jate hai.
 kavak padpon ke saath sahjivi sambandh sthapit karte hai. glomas jinas ke bahut se sadsy micoraija banaate hai. is sangyojan main kavakeey sahjivi mrudaa se phosphorus kaa avasorana kar use padpon main bhej dete hai. aise sambandhon se yukt padap kai anya laabh jaise muulavatodh rogajanak ke prati pratirohakata, lavanta tathaa sookhe ke prati sahansheeltaa tathaa kulavriddhi tathaa vikaas pradarshit karte hai. 
 cynobacteria swaposhit sookshmjeev hai jo jaleey tathaa sthaliy vayumandal main vistrut rup se paae jate hai. inamen bahut se vayumandaleey nitrogen ko sthirikrut kar sakate hai, jaise- ainabina, nostok, osilatoria aadi. dhan ke khet main cynobacteria mahatvapurn jaev urvarak kii bhoomika nibhaate hai. nil harith shaiwal bhi mrudaa main carbonic padaarth badha dete hai jisase uski urvartaa bad jaati he.
