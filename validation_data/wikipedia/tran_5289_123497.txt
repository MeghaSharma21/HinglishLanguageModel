 bengal gajat bharat me prakashit hone vaala ek angreji bhashaa kaa pehla samachar patr tha. iske prakaashak james augustus hikki the. yah ek saptahik patr tha jo kolkata se san 1779 main armbh hua.
 hiki gajet ke prakaashan kaa ek kaaran bazaar ke liye suchanaaen uplabdh karana tha. yah maananaa ki vah angreji prashasan ke virodh ke liye nikaala gaya galat or bhraamak he. haa yah jarur he ki usamen angreji prashasan main vyaapt bhraptachar or rishwatakhori ke samachar pramukhataa se hote the.
