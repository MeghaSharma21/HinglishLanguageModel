 hambantota srilanka ke dakshini praant main sthit hambantota jile kaa mukhyalay he. yah pichhadaa hua kshetra 2004 main hind mahaasaagar main aayi tsunami se buree tarah se prabhavit hua tha. is samay is nagar main bahut si vikaas pariyojnaayen chal rahi hai jisame jisame bandargah tathaa antararashtriya vimaanashetra bhi shaamil hai. iske atarikt kuch anya pariyojnaayen jaise hambantota cricket stadium srilanka sarkaar ke hambantota ko rajdhani colombo ke baad doosraa sabase bada nagar banane kii disha main kaary kar rahe hai.
 ruhana samrajya kii sthapana ke pashchaat yahan par siam, china tathaa indonesia se bahut se paryatak va vyapaaree aate the tathaa apane jahaaj kaa lungar ek bandargah godaavaya, ambalantota main daalate the. in vyapaariyon kii badi naavon tathaa jahaajon ko "sampan" tathaa "tota" kehete the jiskaa arth bandargah yaa lungar daalane kaa sthaan hota he. is kaaran se is kshetra kaa naam "sampantota" pad gaya. kuch samay baad is kshetra kaa naam hambantota ho gaya. 
 hambantota srilanka ke parmparik dakshin kshetra main he jo ki ruhana naam se janaa jaataa tha. pracheen kaal main is kshetra main samrudh sabhyataa fali phuli thi. pracheen kaal se mili pramaan bataate hai ki yah kshetra upjau bhoomi tathaa ati vishaal sichaain jaal se paripurna tha. hambantota ko bahut se naamon jaise 'mahagama', 'ruhana' va 'dolos dahas rata' se bhi janaa jaataa tha.
 isa purv 200 ke aasapaas anuradhapura ke uttar madhya kshetra main anuradhapura samrajya phala phula. apane bhai va samraat devanampia tissa se vivaad ke baad raja mahanaga ne srilanka ke dakshin main ruhana samrajya kii sthapana kii. is kshetra ne srilankai baudhon ke samskruti vikaas main pramukh bhoomika nibhaai. hambantota ke hi nikat tissamaharama kaa buddha mandir jahaan bhagwaan buddha kaa pavitra daant rakhaa he.
 1801 se 1803 isvi ke kareeb angrejon ne yahan kii patharilii bhoomi par prakaash stambh ke saath martelo tower kaa nirmaan kiya jahaan se hambantota kaa samudr dikhataa he. is tower ke nirmaataa captain gopar the jinhone purv main bane dachon ke mitti ke kile ke sthaan par yah tower banwaaya.
 2 agast se 9 sitambar 1903 ke madhya candi yuddh ke dauraan silon netive infentry ke je prendergast hambantota main british sena ke netritv kar rahe the. unhone snow jahaaj minareva kii madad se akraman ko taal diya tathaa upnivesh kii safalapurvak raksha kii. yah ekmaatr ghatana thi jab kisi tower ke madhyam se kisi akraman ko taala gaya ho.
 virginia vulf ke pati leonard vulf hambantota ke 1908 se 1911 ke madhya aupniveshik prashaasak rahe hai.
 2004 main hind mahaasaagar main aayi tsunami ne hambantota nagar ko puri tarah se tahas-nahas kar diya. is aapadaa main 4500 se adhik log maare gayi.
 copen jalwaayu vargeekaran ke anusaar hambantota kii ushnkaribandheey savana jalwaayu he. yahan sahi arthon main shushk mausam nahin hota parantu phir bhi anya maheenon kii tulana main janvari se march tathaa june se agast tak apekshaakrit kam varsha hoti he. baari varsha oktober se navambar ke madhya hoti he. nagar main ausatan 1,050 millimetre vaarshik varsha hoti he. hambantota kaa taapmaana puure varsh bhar lagbhag saamaan rahata he jisaki seemaa 26.3 °se.  tathaa 28.1 °se.  ke madhya main rahati he.
 cement kii pisai va khaad kaa udyog lagaaya gaya he. hambantota kii mukhya visheshta yahan ke namak ke vishaal maidan hai. yah kasbe main badi maatra main namak kaa utpaadan hota he. pradhanmantri ranil vikramsinghe ne yahan par 15,000 acre ke vishesh aarthik kshetra banane kaa prastaav diya he. in aarthik kshetron main kaam karne vaale kamgaaron ko prakshikshan dene hetu 2017 main china kii madad se ek vyavsaayik prashikshan kendra kii sthapana bhi kii gayi he. pradhanmantri ne ek samjhaute ke antargat yahan ke bandargah ko chini vyapaariyon ko patte par de diya he, jisase hambantota china kii ati mahatvakankshi yojana "van belt van road" main shaamil ho gaya he.
 mataala rajpaksha antararashtriya vimaanashetra mataala main sthit he jo ki hambantota se 18 kimi uttar main sthit he. march 2013 se shuru hua yah vimaanashetra colombo ke bhandarnayake antararashtriya vimaanashetra ke baad doosraa antararashtriya vimaanashetra he.
 yahan par e2 rajmarg colombo ko hambantota se gall or matara ke raste jodta he.
 matara-katragama badi railway line pariyojna par 2006 se kaam chal rahaa he jisaki anumaanit laagat $91 million he.
