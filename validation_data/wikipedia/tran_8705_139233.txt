 vaksh nadi, jise suranob nadi or kizil suu bhi kaha jaataa he, madhya asia main sthit ek nadi he jo aamu dariyaa kii ek upanadi bhi he. yah tajikistan kii mukhya nadiyon main se ek he.
 'vaksh' or 'suranob' main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
 vaksh kirgizstan kii alaai vaadi se utpann hokar 262 kilometre pashchim kii ore chalati he or phir tajikistan main pravesh karti he. vahaan yah 524 kimi chalakar pamir parvat kshetra pahunchati he jahaan yah bahut pahari ilaaqon kii tang galiyon se hoti hui nikalati he. tajikistan kii bahut si himanion se bhi isko or jal miltaa he. yahan muksu nadi bhi isamen ja milti he. tajikistan main isko 'vaksh' naam us jagah ke baad diya jaataa he jahaan suranob nadi or obihingu nadi kaa sangam hota he. pamir kshetra se nikalakar yah dakshin-pashchimi tajikistan ke maidani ilaaqon se guzarati he or phir jaakar panj nadi main vilay ho jaati he. is vilay ke baad in donon nadiyon ke mili hui dharaa ko aamu dariyaa ke naam se pukaara jaataa he.
