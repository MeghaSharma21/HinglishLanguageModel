 priyanvada mohanti hejamaadi odissi kii ek bhartiya shastreey nartki, kala lekhika avan ek jeevigyaani or sambalpur vishvavidhyalay main purv vaais chancelar rah chuki hai. unka janm 18 navambar 1939 main hua, unhone master kii degree or uske baad doctoret kii degree main jeev vigyaan main kii. unhone odissi kam umr se hi siikhnaa shuru kardi or nai dilli main 1954 main inter uniwarsity yuva mahotsav main unke 'odissi' nrity ke pradarshan ne is nrity kala ko charles fabri, jo ki sabha main maujuud the, unke sahyog se anterrashtriya samudaay kaa dhyaan aakarshith kiya. 
 priyanvada bhartiya vigyaan academy kii ek khojkaar hai. unhone kai lekh or ek kitaab, odissi: ek bhartiya shastreey nrity rup, jisamen nrity ke itihaas or vikaas kaa byora diya he, likhe hai. 2013 main unhen ''odissi nrity sammaan'' se sammanit kiya gaya. vigyaan or praudyogiki ke kshetra main unke yogadan ke liye, bharat sarkaar ne unhen chauthe sarvocch naagarik puraskaar, padam shri se 1998 main sammanit kiya.
