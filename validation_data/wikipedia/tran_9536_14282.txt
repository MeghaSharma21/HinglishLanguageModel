 bhagwat ke anusaar ugrayudh nip kaa putr, lekin anya puraanon main ullekh he ki yah krit kaa putr tha. isane aath hajaar varsh tapasya kii thi or yama ne swayan ise tatvagyan sikhaaya tha. iske putr kaa naam kshemya tha. isane 101 neepon kaa naash kiya tha or bhallatputra janamejay ko bhi maaraa tha. shantnu kii mrityu ke baad isane satyavati kii maang kii thi jisase cruddh hokar bhishma ne iskaa vadh kar diya tha.
