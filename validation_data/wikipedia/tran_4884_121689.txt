 yusi broujar ek web broujar he. china main iskaa majboot yuzar base he or customij karne ke baad isaki bharat ke sthaneey bazaar main kaaphi vruddhi hui he. yah multah 2004 main laanch hua tha. us samay ye keval J2me ke liye uplabdh tha.par ye ab andride windos ioes blueberi par bhi uplabdh he. 2010 main isane apni pehali ap appal ap store par laanch kii thi.
 isapar ham computer tathaa mobile par internet dekh sakate hai. yusi broujar par internet dekhnaa bahut saral he. is broujar main ham anginat prishthon ko roak kar kabhi bhi dekh sakate hai.
 yusi web ye daawa karata he ki yusi web brouzar sabhi menstream oparating systams ke saath campatewal he.
