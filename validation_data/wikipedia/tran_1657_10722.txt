 bhugol vah shaastra he jiske dwaara prithvi ke upree swarup or uske prakritik vibhaagon kaa gyaan hota he. prakritik vigyaanon ke nishkarshon ke bich kaary-kaaran sambandh sthapit karte hue prithvital kii vibhinnataaon kaa maanviya drushtikon se adhyayan hi bhugol kaa saar tatv he. prithvi kii sataha par jo sthaan vishesh hai unki samtaon tathaa vishmataaon kaa kaaran or unka spashtikaran bhugol kaa nijee kshetra he. bhugol shabd do shabdon bhu yani prithvi or gol se milakar bana he.

 bhugol ek pracheenatam vigyaan he or isaki neenv praarambhik unani vidwaanon ke kaaryon main dikhaai padati he. bhugol shabd kaa pratham prayog unani vidwaan iretosthanij ne teesri shatabdi isa purv main kiya tha. bhugol vistrut paimaane par sabhi bhautik va maanviya tathyon kii antarkriyaaon or in antarkriyaaon se utpann sthalroopon kaa adhyayan karata he. yah bataata he ki kaise, kyon or kahan maanviya va prakritik kriyakalapon kaa udbhav hota he or kaise ye kriyalap ek duusare se antarsambandhit hai.
 bhugol kii adhyayan vidhi parivartit hoti rahi he. praarambhik vidwaan varnanaatmak bhugolvetta the. baad main, bhugol vishleshnatmak bhugol ke rup main viksit hua. aaj yah vishay na keval varnan karata he, balki vishleshan ke saath-saath bhavishyavaanee bhi karata he. 
 aarambhik pramaanon ke anusaar is samay ke vidwaan maanchitr nirmaan or khagoleey maapon dwaara prithvi ke bhautik tathyon ko samajhate the. bhugol main aarambhik vidwaan dene kaa shrey yunaan ko hi jaataa he, jisamen pramukh the homer, herrodotus, thels, arastu or iretosthanij.

 reter or hambolt kaa ullekh bahudhaa aadhunik bhugol ke sansthaapak ke rup main kiya jaataa he. saamanytah 19veen sadi ke uttarardh kaa kaal aadhunik bhugol kaa kaal mana jaataa he. vastutah retgel pratham aadhunik bhugolvetta the, jinhone chirsmmat bhugolvettaon dwaara sthapit neenv par aadhunik bhugol kii sanrachana kaa nirmaan kiya.
 dwitiya vishwayuddh ke baad bhugol kaa vikaas badi teevr gati se hua. hartshorn jaise america or europea bhugolvettaon ne is dauraan adhiktam yogadan diya. hartshorn ne bhugol ko ek aise vigyaan ke rup main paribhaashit kiya jo kshetreey vibhinnataaon kaa adhyayan karata he. vartmaan bhugolvetta pradeshik upaagam or krambaddh upaagam ko virodhabhasi kii jagah poorak upaagam ke rup main dekhte hai.
 bhugol ne aaj vigyaan kaa darja praapt kar liya he, jo prithvi tal par upasthit vividh prakritik or saanskrithik roopon kii vyakhya karata he. bhugol ek samgr or antarsambandhit kshetreey adhyayan he jo sthanik sanrachana main bhoot se bhavishya main hone vaale parivartan kaa adhyayan karata he. is tarah bhugol kaa kshetra vividh vishyon jaise sainya sevaaon, paryaavaran prabandhan, jal sansaadhan, aapadaa prabandhan, mausam vigyaan, niyojan or vividh saamaajik vigyaanon main he. iske alaava bhugolvetta dainik jivan se sambandhit ghatnaon jaise paryaton, sthaan parivartan, awaason tathaa swasthya sambandhi kriyakalapon main sahaayak ho saktaa he

 ek anya drushti se bhugol ke do pradhan ang he : shrunkhalabaddh bhugol tathaa pradeshik bhugol. prithvi ke kisi sthanvishesh par shrunkhalabaddh bhugol kii shakhaon ke samanvay ko kendrit karne kaa pratifal pradeshik bhugol he.

 aarthik bhugol-- isaki shakhaayen krishi, udyog, khanij, shakti tathaa bhandar bhugol or bhu upbhog, vyavsaayik, parivahan evam yatayaat bhugol hai. arthik sanrachana sambandhi yojana bhi bhugol kii shakha he.
 raajnneetik bhugol -- iske ang bhuraajnneetik shaastra, antarrashtriya, rashtriya, aupniveshik bhugol, sheet yuddh kaa bhugol, samrik evam sainik bhugol hai.
 aitihaasik bhugol --pracheen, madhyakaalin, aadhunik vaidic, puranic, engil sambandhi tathaa arabi bhugol bhi iske ang he.

 iske atirikt bhugol ke anya khand bhi viksit ho rahe hai jaise granth vigyaneey, darshanik, manovaigyanik, ganit shastreey, jyotish shastreey evam bhraman bhugol tathaa sthannamadhyayan hai.

 hetner or hartshorn par aadhaarit bhugol kii teen mukhya shakhaayen he : bhautik bhugol, maanav bhugol or pradeshik bhugol. bhautik bhugol main prakritik parighatnaon kaa ullekh hota he, jaise ki jalwaayu vigyaan, mrudaa or vanaspati. maanav bhugol bhootal or maanav samaaj ke sambandhon kaa varnan karata he. bhugol ek intaraa-anushaasanic vishay he.
 bhugol kaa ganit, prakritik vigyaanon or saamaajik vigyaanon ke saath ghanishtha sambandh he. jabaki anya vigyaan vishisht prakaar kii parighatnaon kaa hi varnan karte hai, bhugol vividh prakaar kii un parighatnaon kaa bhi adhyayan karata he, jinka adhyayan anyavigyaanon main bhi shaamil hota he. is prakaar bhugol ne swayan ko antarsambandhit vyavhaaron ke sanshleshit adhyayan ke rup main sthapit kiya he. 
 bhugol sthanon kaa vigyaan he. bhugol prakritik va saamaajik donon hi vigyaan he, joki maanav va paryaavaran donon kaa hi adhyayan karata he. yah bhautik va saanskrithik vishwa ko jodta he. bhautik bhugol prithvi kii vyavastha se utpann prakritik paryaavaranaka adhyayan karata he. maanav bhugol raajnneetik, aarthik, saamaajik, saanskrithik or janankikeey prakriyaaon se sambandhit he. yah sansadhano ke vividh prayogon se bhi sambandhit he. praarambhik bhugol sirf sthanon kaa varnan karata tha. haalaanki yah aaj bhi bhugol ke adhyayan main shaamil he parantu pichley kuch varshon main iske pratirupon ke varnan main parivartan hua he. bhugolic parighatnaon kaa varnan saamanytah do upagamon ke aadhaar par kiya jaataa he jaise pradeshik or krambaddh. pradeshik upaagam pradeshon ke nirmaan va visheshtaon kii vyakhya karata he. yah is baat kaa varnan karne kaa prayaas karata he ki koi kshetra kaise or kyon ek duusare se alag he. pradesh bhautik, saamaajik, aarthik, raajnneetik, janankikeey aadi ho saktaa he. krambaddh upaagam parighatnaon tathaa saamaanya bhugolic mahatvon ke dwaara sanchalit he. prayek parighatna kaa adhyayan kshetreey vibhinnataaon va duusare ke saath unke sambandhon kaa adhyayan bhugol aadhaar par kiya jaataa he.
 bhugol kii praayah sabhi shakhaon vishesh rup se bhautik bhugol kii shakhaon main tathyon ke vishleshan main ganitiya vidhiyon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. 
 khagol vigyaan main akashiya pindon kaa vaigyanik adhyayan kiya jaataa he. bhootal kii ghatnaon or tatwon par akashiya pindon - surya, chandramaa, dhoomaketuon aadi kaa pratyaksh prabhaav hota he. isiilie bhugol main soramandal, surya kaa apane abhaasi path par gaman, prithvi kii dainik or varshi gatiyon tathaa unke parinamswaroop hone vaale parivartanon - din-raat or ritu parivartan, chandra kalaon, surya grahan, chandragahan aadi kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. in tatwon or ghatnaon kaa moulik adhyayan khagol vigyaan main kiya jaataa he. is prakaar bhugol kaa khagol vigyaan se ghanisht sambandh parilakshith hota he.
 bhu-vigyaan prithvi ke sanghathan, sanrachana tathaa itihaas ke vaigyanik adhyayan san sambandhit he. iske antargat prithvi ke sanghathak padaarthon, bhootal par kriyasheel shaktiyon tathaa unase utpann sanrachanaon, bhupatal kii shailen kii sanrachana evam vitaran, prithvi ke bhu-vaigyanik kaalon aadi kaa adhyayan sammilit hota he. bhugol main sthalroopon ke vishleshan main bhuvigyaan ke siddhanton tathaa saakshyon kaa sahaara liya jaataa he. is prakaar bhautik bhugol visheshtah bhu-akruti vigyaan kaa bhu-vigyaan se atyant ghanisht sambandh he. 
 bhootal ko prabhavit karne vaale bhugolic karkon main jalwaayu sarvaadhik prabhaavshaalee or mahatvapurn kaarak he. mausam vigyaan vayumandal visheshtah usamen ghatit hone vaale bhautik prakramon tathaa usase sambaddh sthalmandal or jalmandal ke vividh prakramon kaa adhyayan karata he. iske antargat vayudab, taapmaana, pawan, ardrata, varshan, meghachhadan, surya prakaash aadi kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. mausam vigyaan ke in tatwon kaa vishleshan bhugol main bhi kiya jaataa he.
 jal vigyaan prithvi par sthit jal ke adhyayan se sambandhit he. iske saath hi isamen jal ke anveshan, prayog, niyantran or sangrakshan kaa adhyayan bhi samahit hota he. mahasagariya tatwon ke sthanik vitaran kaa adhyayan bhugol kii ek shakha samudr vigyaan main kiya jaataa he. samudr vigyaan main mahaasaagarayi jal ke taapmaana, ghanatv, lavanta, sagariya laharon, dhaaraon aadi kaa adhyayan sammilit hota he. maanav jivan main jalaashyon kaa mahatv sarvopari he kyonki pine se lekar pashuon ke pine, fasalon kii sinchai karne, kaarkaanon main jalaapurti, jalashakti, jal parivahan, matsyakhet aadi vibhinn roopon main jal aavashyak hi nahin anivaary hota he. is prakaar ham pate hai ki bhugol or jal vigyaan main atyant nikat kaa or ghanisht sambandh he. 
 mrudaa vigyaan mitti ke nirmaan, sanrachana or visheshta kaa vaigyanik adhyayan karata he. bhootal par mittiyon ke vitaran kaa adhyayan mrudaa bhugol  ke antargat kiya jaataa he. mitti kaa adhyayan krishi bhugol kaa bhi mahatvapurn vishay he. is prakaar bhugol kii ghanishtataa mrudaa vigyaan se bhi paayi jaati he. 
 ped-paudhon kaa maanav jivan se gaharaa sambandh he. vanaspati vigyaan padap jivan or uske sampurna vishwa roopon kaa vaigyanik adhyay karata he. prakritik vanaspatiyon ke sthanik vitaran or visheshtaon kaa vishleshan bhautik bhugol kii shakha jaev bhugol  or upashakha vanaspati yaa padap bhugol  main kii jaati he. 
 jantu vigyaan yaa praani samast prakaar ke praani jivan kii sanrachana, vargeekaran tathaa kaaryon kaa vaigyanik adhyayan he. manushya ke liye jantu jagat or pashu sansaadhan kaa atyadhik mahatv he. pashu manushya ke liye bahu-upyogi hai. manushya ko pashuon se anek upyogi padaarth yatha doodh, maance, oon, chamada aadi praapt hote hai or kuch pashuon kaa prayog saamaan dhone or parivahan yaa sawari karne ke liye bhi kiya jaataa he. jaev bhugol kii ek up-shakha he jantu bhugol  jisamen vibhinn praaniyon ke sthanik vitaran or visheshtaon kaa vishleshan kiya jaataa he. maanav jivan ke aarthik, saamaajik, saanskrithik sabhi kshetron main pashu jagat kaa mahatvapurn sthaan hone ke kaaran isko bhugolic adhyayanon main bhi vishisht sthaan praapt he. atah bhugol kaa jantu vigyaan se bhi ghanisht sambandh pramanit hota he. 
 bhojan, vastra or aashray manushya kii praathmik aavashyaktaaen hai jo kisi bhi desh, kaal yaa paristhiti main prayek vyakti ke liye anivaary hoti hai. iske saath hi maanav yaa maanav samooh kii anyaanya saamaajik, aarthik, raajnneetik aadi aavashyaktaaen bhi hoti hai jo bahut kuch arthvyavastha par aadhaarit hoti hai. arthvyavastha kaa adhyayan karnaa arthshaastra kaa mul vishay he. kisi sthaan, kshetra yaa janasamudaay ke samast jeevika sroton yaa aarthik sansadhano ke prabandh, sanghathan tathaa prashasan ko hi arthvyavastha kehete hai. vividh aarthik pakshon kaa sthanik adhyayan bhugol kii ek vishisht shakha aarthik bhugol main kiya jaataa he. 
 samajashaastra manushyon ke saamaajik jivan, vyavhaar tathaa saamaajik kriya kaa adhyayan he jisamen maanav samaaj kii utpatti, vikaas, sanrachana tathaa saamaajik sansthaaon kaa adhyayan sammilit hota he. samajashaastra maanav samaaj ke vikaas, pravriti tathaa niyamon kii vaigyanik vyakhya karata he. samast maanav samaaj anek virgon, samoohon tathaa samudaayon main vibhakt he jiske apane-apane reeti-rivaaj, prathaen, paramparaayen tathaa niyam hote hai jin par bhugolic paryaavaran kaa prabhaav nishchit rup se paaya jaataa he. atah samajashastreey adhyayanon main bhugolic gyaan aavashyak hota he. 
 maanav bhugol maanav sabhyataa ke itihaas tathaa maanav samaaj ke vikaas kaa adhyayan bhugolic pariprekshya main karata he. kisi bhi desh yaa pradesh ke itihaas par vahaan ke bhugolic paryaavaran tathaa paristhitiyon kaa gaharaa prabhaav paaya jaataa he. prakritik tathaa maanviya yaa saanskrithik tathy kaa vishleshan vikasaatmak drushtikon se karte hai tab manushya or prithvi ke parivartansheel sambandhon kaa spashtikaran ho jaataa he. kisi pradesh main jansankhya, krishi, pashupaalan, khanan, udyog dhandhon, parivahan ke sadhano, vyapaarik evam vanijki sansthaaon aadi ke aitihaasik vikaas kaa adhyayan maanav bhugol main kiya jaataa he jiske liye upyukt sakshya or pramaan itihaas se hi praapt hote hai.
 raajnneeti vigyaan ke adhyayan kaa kendra bindu ‘shashan vyavastha’ he. isamen vibhinan rashtron evam rajyon kii shashan pranaaliyon, sarkaron, antarrashtriya sambandhon aadi kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. raajnneetik bhugol maanav bhugol kii ek shakha he jisamen raajnneetik rup se sangathit kshetron kii seemaa, vistaar, unke vibhinan ghatakon, up-vibhaagon, shasit bhu-bhaagon, sansadhano, aantarik tathaa videshi raajnneetik sambandhon aadi kaa adhyayan sammilit hota he. maanav bhugol kii ek anya shakha bhuraajnneeti bhi he jiske antargat bhootal ke vibhinn pradeshon kii raajnneetik pranali vishesh rup se antarrashtiy raajnneeti par bhugolic karkon ke prabhaav kii vyakhya kii jaati he. in tathyon se spasht he ki bhugol or raajnneeti vigyaan paraspar ghanisht rup se sambandhit hai. 
 janankiki yaa janasankhyikee ke antargat jansankhya ke aakaar, sanrachana, vikaas aadi kaa parimanatmak adhyayan kiya jaataa he. isamen jansankhya sambandhi aankadon ke ekathran, vargeekaran, moolyankan, vishleshan tathaa prakshepan ke saath hi janankikeey pratirupon tathaa prakriyaaon kii bhi vyakhya kii jaati he. maanav bhugol or uski upashakha jansankhya bhugol main bhugolic paryaavaran ke sambandh main janankikeey prakramon tathaa pratirupon main paayi jane vaali kshetreey bhinnataaon kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. is prakaar vishay saadrishy ke kaaran bhugol or janankiki main ghanisht sambandh paaya jaataa he.
