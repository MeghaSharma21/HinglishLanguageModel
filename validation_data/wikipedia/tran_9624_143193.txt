 gond paudha kaa ek utsarji padaarth he jo koshika bhitti ke selulose ke apghaton ke falswaroop banataa he. sukhi avasthaa main yah ravaa ke rup main paaya jaataa he, kintu pani main daalane par yah phulakar chipchipa ban jaataa he. iskaa upyog kaagaj aadi vibhinn padaarthon ko chipkaane main hota he. akeshia senigal se hamen sabase acha gond miltaa he. babul, aam or neem aadi paudhon se bhi gond nikalataa he iskaa upyog dawa banane evam vibhinn udyog dhandhon main hota he.
