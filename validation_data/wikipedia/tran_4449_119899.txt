 mon blaan, alps kaa sabase unchaa parvat
 alps yaa alps madhya europe kii sabase badi parvatamala he. dakshin-pashchim se purvottar kii ore chalane vaali yah parvaton kii shrenee lagbhag 1,200 kimi lambi he or aath europea deshon se nikalati he. haalaanki cocks parvat is se adhik unche hai or ural parvat shrenee is se adhik duuri tak chalati he, lekin ve donon europe or asia ke bich hai. is karanvash alps europe ke sabase mahaan pahaad maane jate hai.
