 balukashm yaa balua pathar aisi drudh shila he jo mukhyatya baaluu ke kanon kaa dabaav paakar jam jane se banati he or kisi yojak padaarth se judi hoti he. baaluu ke samaan isaki rachana main bhi anek padaarth vibhinn maatra main ho sakate hai, kintu isamen adhikansh sphatik hi hota he. jis shila main baaluu ke bahut bade bade dane milte hai, use mishrapindashm or jisamen chote chote dane hote hai use balumay shail yaa mrinmay shail kehete hai.
 balua pathar main ve hi dhatvik tatv haate hai, jo baaluu main. sphatik kii bahutayat hoti he, jiske saath praaia: felsapaar tathaa kabhi-kabhi shwet abhrak bhi hota he. kabhi kabhi pathar kii vibhinn paraton ke bich main abhrak kii tah si jami hui maaloom padati he. khan se pathar nikaalane main is tah kaa mahatvapurn yogadan he. isi ke kaaran pathar kii patali paraten nikaali ja sakati hai, jo farsh banane ke kaam aati hai. yojak padaarth praaia: baarik calcidani silica hota he, kintu kabhi kabhi mul sphatik bhi yojak kaa kaam karata he. aisi dasha main shila sphatik jaisi taiyaar hoti he. kailcite, gloconite, louh oxide, carboneey padaarth or anya anek prakaar ke padaarth bhi jodne kaa kaam karte hai, tathaa apana apana vishisht rang pradaan karte hai, jaise gloconite vaali shilaayein hari or lohevaali laal, bhuri yaa dhoosar hoti hai. jab yojak padaarth chikani mitti hota he, tab shila praaia: shwet yaa dhoosar varna kii hoti he or atyant dridhtaa se jami hui hoti he.
 shuddh balua pathar main 99% tak silica ho saktaa he. mulaayam pathar piskar baaluu banane ke kaam aata he, kintu jo bahut dridhtaa se pathar jamaa hota he, uski inten bana lee jaati hai. yah bhatthiyon tathaa angithiyon main astr lagane ke kaam aati hai, kyonki silica atyant taapasah hota he. ganistar shila isi prakaar kii hoti he. atyant dridhtaapurvak jame, kuch kam shuddh pathar sil, batte or chakkiyaan banane ke kaam aate hai.
 balua pathar daanedaar or chhidral hota he, isliye isapar acchhee polish nahin kii ja sakati or na baarik kaam ho saktaa he, par moti gadhaai tathaa kataai saaf or sachhee ho sakati he. isliye imaaraton main iskaa bahuvidh upyog hota he. agre kaa laal pathar musalamaanon ke jamaane se hi mahatvapurn imaaraton main lagane ke liye dur dur tak bheja jaataa he. ab bhi sangeen chinai main safed or laal balua pathar hi mukhyatya prayukt hote hai. ye praaia: khaanon se khodakar or kabhi kabhi surang lagaakar nikaale jate hai. panna kaa safed pathar farshi chaukon ke rup main dur dur tak bheja jaataa he. iske 10, 10, 12, 12 foot tak ke chauke nikaale ja sakate hai. patale chauke chat par khapril kii bhanti chhaae jate hai. 10 se 12 foot paat tak kii chhaton main isaki dharane bhi rakhi jaati hai, kintu chhaton par is prakaar iskaa upyog, dhulaai manhagi hone ke kaaran, nearesth kshetron tak hi simit he. jahaan doosraa adhik kathor pathar suvidhapurvak nahin miltaa, vahaan sadakon ke liye or kancreet ke liye isaki gitti bhi banaai jaati he.
 chhidral hone se isaki paraton main bhumigat jal ekatra ho jaataa he, at: ye mahatvapurn jalstrot hoti hai.
