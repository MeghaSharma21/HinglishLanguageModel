 hansal mehta ek bhartiya film nirdeshak, lekhak, abhinayta or nirmaataa hai. mehta ne kariyar ke khaanaa khazaana shrinkhalaa ke saath televijan main apane kariyar kii shuruyaat kii or baad main ... jayate, dil pe mat le yaar or chal jaisi filme banaayin . vah shahid film ke liye sabase prasiddh hai, jiske liye unhone sarvshereshth nirdesan ke liye 2013 kaa rashtriya film puraskaar jeeta . iske baad unhone city lits, aligarh  or simran ko nirdeshit kiya.
 hansal mehta ek gujarati bhashi hai, jiskaa janm mumbai ke madhyavargeey parivaar main hua tha. 
 mehta ne tivi sho khaanaa khajaana ke saath nirdesan karke 1993 main apana kareer shuru kiya, is prakaar zee tivi par celibrity shef sanjeev kapur ke television karier kii shuruyaat kii. iske alaava, mehta ne amrutha, hive, yaaden, lakshya, niti or kai or televijan shrinkhalaaon kaa bhi nirdesan kiya.
 unhone ek feechar film nirdeshak ke rup main apni pehali shuruyaat ...jayate naamak film se kii thi jo hyderabad, bharat ke antarrashtriya film mahotsav main bhartiya panorama 1999-2000 kaa hissaa thi . yah baad main dil pe mat le yaar kaa nirdesan kiya. usee saal baad main chal, ek style vaali gangster film ai jise unki pichhali filmon kii tulana main adhik prashansa mili thi. 
 mehta vastav main rajkumaar raav ke saath apni film shahid ke liye jane jate he . shahid kaa 2012 main toronto antarrashtriya film mahotsav main vishwa str par pradarshan hua. tha, jiske baad duniya bhar main kai antarrashtriya film samarohon main iskaa pradarshan hua. baad main is film ko dizani-utivi ne adhigrahan kar liya or oktober 2013 main vanijyik taur par release kiya. ye ,maanavadhikaar vakil shahid aazmi ke jivan par bani film thi, jinaki 2010 main hatya kar di gai thi.  hansal ko sarvshereshth nirdesan ke liye 61 wein rashtriya film puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya jabaki rajkumaar raav ne shahid ke liye sarvshereshth abhinayta kaa 61 waan rashtriya film puraskaar jeeta .
 shahid kii safalataa ke baad, hansal mehta ne rajkumaar raav ke saath milakar fox star studio or mahesh bhatta ke liye prashansit city lits banane ke liye phir se kaam kiya. citilit ke baad, mehta ne, aligarh film ko nirdeshit kiya jo ek marathi professor or kavi par aadhaarit he jise samalangik hone ke liye apane vishvavidhyalay dwaara nilambit kiya gaya tha. yah film 20 veen busaan antarrashtriya film samaaroh kaa premier bieggai landan film festival dwaara kiya gaya. aligarh 17 veen jio maami mumbai film mahotsav main puraskrit pehali film thi. unke lambey samay sahyogi apurv asraani kii ek script ke aadhaar par aligarh ne mehta ke pasandida abhinayta manoj bajpeyi  ashish vidhyaarthee or rajkumaar raav ke saath purna kiya . aligarh ko iros international dwaara pesh kiya gaya or sah-utpaadan kiya gaya.
