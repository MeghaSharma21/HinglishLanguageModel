 bhartiya maanak samay  bharat kaa samay mandal he, ek utisi+5:30 samay ofaset ke saath main. bharat main divalok bachat samay yaa anya koi mausami samayog nahin he, yadhyapi dsti 1962 bharat-china yuddh, 1965 bharat-paak yuddh or 1971 bharat-paak yuddh main vyavhaar tha. samrik or vimaanan samay main, iasti kaa E* ke saath main namit hota he.
 
 1947 main aazaadee ke baad bharat sarkaar ne puure desh ke liye sarkari samay ke rup IST ko sthapit kar diya, haalaanki kolkata or mumbai 1955 tak apane sthaneey samay ko banaae rakhaa he. central vedhashala chennai se ek sthaan par le jaaya gaya tha, bhartiya samay uttar pradesh ke mirzapur jile se nirdhaarit hota he., isliye ki yah sambhav ke rup main UTC +5:30 ke kareeb hoga. 1962 ke bharat-china yuddh tathaa 1965 or 1971 ke bharat-pakistan yuddh main alp samay ke liye delite seaving time kaa upyog kiya gaya tha.
 desh kii purv-pashchim duuri lagbhag 2933 kilometre he jiske kaaran purv main suryoday or suryaast pashchim se 2 ghante jaldi hota he or isiilie uttar-purv rajyon ke logon ko unki ghadiyaan aage badhane kii aavashyaktaa hoti hai jisase suryoday ke uparaant urja kaa kshay na ho. iske kaaran aupchaarik vyavhaaron main anek ksht hote hai.
 1980 main sanshodhakon kii ek mandali ne bharat ko do athwa teen samay mandalon main vibhajit karne kaa suzav diya, parantu ye suzav hamen british sarkaar dwaara sthapit samay mandalon ko apnaane ke baraabar tha, isliye is suzav ko nakaara diya gaya. 2001 main vigyaan or praudyogiki mantralay ne 4 sadsy kii samiti sthapit kii jiskaa uddeshy samay mandal tathaa delite seaving ke vishyon ko jaanchna tha. samiti ke nishkarsh 2004 main sansad main vigyaan or praudyogiki mantri, shri kapil sibbal, dwaara prastut kiye gaye jisame kapil sibbal ne kaha ki "bharat ke samay mandalon ko vibhajit karne kii koi aavashyaktaa nahin he.
 2014 main, assam ke mukhyamantri shri tarun gogoi ne assam tathaa anya uttar-purv rajyon ke liye ek alag samay mandal kii maang kii he parantu is prastaav ko centre sarkaar dwaara koi manjuuri nahin mili he. 
 aupchaarik samay sanket nai dilli main sthit rashtriya bhautik prayogshaala ke samay evam aavriti maanak prayogshaala ke dwaara preshit kiye jate hai. ye sanket parmaanu ghadiyon par aadhaarit hai jo saarvbhaumik samanvit samay par aadhaarit hai. bhartiya maanak samay desh kaa maanak samay isliye hai kyonki ye bharat ke madhya se gujarata he. desh kaa thik samay akashwani or doordarshan ke dwaara logon tak pahunchaaya jaataa he.
