 bharat ke pehle or vishwa ke 138 wein antariksh yatri rakesh sharma kaa janm 13 janvari 1949 ko punjab ke patiala main hua tha.
 rakesh bachapan se hi vigyaan main kaaphi ruchi rakhte thea. bigadi chizon ko bananaa or electronic chizon par baariki se najar rakhana unki aadat thi. 
 rakesh jab bade hue to aasmaan main udate hawaii jahaaj ko tab tak dekha karte the jab tak vah unki ankho se ojhal naa ho jaae. jald hi rakesh ke man main aasmaan main udne kii tamanna jaag gai. phir kya, vah bas usee ore lag gaye or ek din wo kar dikhaaya jisase har bhartiya ko un par garv he.
 patiala ke ek hindu goud brahman parivaar main janmen rakesh ne hyderabad kii osmania uniwarsity se grajuation kiya.
 kismat ne liya you-turn1966 main endie paas kar indian air force cadet bane rakesh sharma ne 1970 main bhartiya vaayu sena ko jwine kar liya. phir yaheen se inaki kismat ne you-turn liya or rakesh ne kuch aansa kar dikhaaya ki aaj unke naam se har bhartiya kaa seenaa fakr se chaudaa ho jaataa he.
 maatr 21 saal kii umr main hi bhartiya vaayu sena main shaamil hone kaa baad rakesh kaa josh doogana ho gaya or wo ise barkaraar rakhte hue teji se aage badhate gaye.
 pakistan se yuddh ke baad charchaa main aae1971 ke bharat pakistan yuddh ke dauraan rakesh sharma ne apane vimaan "mig air croft" se mahatvapurn safalataa hasil kii. isi yuddh ke baad se rakesh sharma charchaa main aae or logon ne unki yogyata kii jamkar tarif kii. sharma ne dikhaa diya tha ki kathin paristhitiyon main bhi kis tarah shandaar kaam kiya ja saktaa he.
 aath din ke liye antariksh main rahe1984 main bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan or soviet sangh ke intercosmos kaarykram ke ek sanyukt antariksh abhiyaan ke antargat rakesh sharma aath din tak antariksh main rahe. ye us samay bhartiya vayusena ke squadran leader or vimaanchaalak the.
 2 april 1984 ko do anya soviet antarikshayatriyon ke saath soyuj tii-11 main rakesh sharma ko launch kiya gaya. is udaan main or salut 7 antariksh centre main unhone uttari bharat kii photography kii or gurutvakarshan-hin yogabhyas kiya.
 saare jahaan se acha hindustan hamarownki antariksh udaan ke dauraan bharat kii tatkalin pradhanmantri indira gandhi ne rakesh sharma se poocha ki upar se antariksh se bharat kaisaa dikhataa he. rakesh sharma ne uttar diya- "saare jahaan se acha".
 dharti se tatkaaleen pradhanmantri indira gandhi kaa yah sawaal or antariksh main roosi antariksh yaan se bharat ke antariksh yatri rakesh sharma ke is jawaab ne har hindustani ko romanchit kar diya tha. 
 ashok chakra se sammanitbharat sarkaar ne unhen ashok chakra se sammanit kiya. wing commander ke pad par seva-nivrutt hone par rakesh sharma ne hindustan aironaticks limited main pareekshan vimaanchaalak ke rup main kaam kiya.
 navambar 2006 main inhone bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan kii ek samiti main bhaag liya jisane ek nae bhartiya antariksh udaan kaarykram ko sweekruti di.
