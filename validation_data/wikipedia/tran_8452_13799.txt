 kilari anand paul bharat mul ke ek vivaadaaspad isai upadeshak he jo baad main swaabhaavik rup se ek deshikrut american ban gaye.. unki haal kii saarvajanik upasthiti florida ke padri terry jones ke saath dekhi gayi thi jahaan unhone 11 sitambar 2010 ko quran kii pratiyon ko jalaane main samarthan dene kii yojana banaai. 
 ve bharat ke, andhra pradesh rajya ke, chithtivalsa naamak ek chote se gaanv main 25 sitambar 1963, ko ek garib, hindu parivaar main paidaa hue. unka mul naam anand kilari tha. unke pita kaa naam bernbas or mata kaa naam santhosamma tha.
 unke mata pita 1966 main isai ban gaye. march 1971 main, paul jab aath saal ke the ek isai ban gaye. paul kaa daawa he ki gaiyr isaayiyon ke bich susamachar ke sahbhaajan ke liye unhone swaghoshit enjeelwaadi pita ke saath bharat ke saikadon gaanvon kii yatra kii he. paul ne daawa kiya ki 19 saal kii umr main hi unhen aisa laga ki dharmaseva hetu purohitai ke liye bulaava aa gaya he atah unhone purnakalik purohitai sweekaar kar lee. .
 bharat kii sarkaar main parivartan hetu madad dene ke liye anand sitambar 2007 main bharat laut aae. unka maananaa he ki chunki sonia gandhi ek isai hai or congress party tathaa bharat kii sansad main sattarudh gathbandhan sarkaar kii sarvesarva hai, atah unhen logon kii seva karne ke liye adhik se adhik samay tak sarkaar main bane rahanaa chahie. unhen aksar vivaadaaspad mana jaataa rahaa he or unke bhai sahit, unpar virodhiyon dwaara adharm karrm ke aarope lagaae jate rahe hai.
 unke sanghathan main dharmarth yogadan ke madhyam se, paul ke kai samarthakon ne 747SP vayuyan kharidne ke liye dhanrashi muhaiyaa kii jise paul ne "global pis ambasstors" naam se karwaai or isane "ekak vaishvik shanti" ke upnaam se udaan bhari. paul ne is vimaan kaa istemaal teesri duniya ke deshon ke mishan ke liye kiya, jisame aapadaa main ghire kshetron or un deshon ke jaruratmand logon ke liye sahaayta pradaan kii. chunki yah pehle se hi china airlines kaa vimaan tha, isliye iske saath apane aap main hi ek dilachasp itihaas tha. 19 farvari 1985 ko, taipe se los angeles ke liye udaan bharte hue yatriyon se bhare is vimaan kaa ek ingine vifal ho gaya, tathaa 41000 feet kii oonchai se aniyantrit avasthaa main 9500 feet neeche utar aayaa or prashanth mahaasaagar ke upar puri 360 degree kii sidh main hawa main ghota lagaate hue payalton ne waapas niyantran paa liya or vimaan ko jameen par surakshit utaara.
 2005 main, vittiya javabdehi ke liye evangajelical concil ne or vittiya javabdehi tathaa shaskeey maanakon ke nirvaah kii asafalataa ke kaaran paul ke sanghathan kii sadsyta samaapt kar di. unke mantralayon ke vittiya vivaran ministry watch dwaara soochibaddh kiye jate hai, jo mantralay kii reating ke liye swatantra strot he.
 paul kabhi kathith yuddh aapradhi liberia ke bhootpurv raashtrapati charles tailor ke ek vishwaasapaatr or salaahkaar rah chuke the.
 ke.e. paul saarvajanik rup se florida ke ek padri terry jones ke saath dikhaai die jisane neuyork main ground zero ke nikat ek masjid ke nirmaan ke khilaaf virodh pradarshan ke rup main quran kii pratiyaan jalaane kii yojana banaai thi. quran jalaane ke daawey ko aakraamak karm ke rup main sweekaar karte hue, donon ne ek saath pariklpit masjid ke imam ko quran jalaane kii yojana ke bare main 2 ghante kii notice di ki is sandarbh main vah unase sampark kare.
 2009-15 sitambar 2009 ko dilli ucch nyaayalay ne, wiesar, kendriya vitt mantri pranab mukherjee or purv mantri ke natwar singh ke khilaaf paul dwaara daayar yachika ko khaarij karte hue kaha ki kathith taur par 2007 main hi unka global pis mishan thap ho gaya, jiske parinamswaroop use kareeb 500 karod rupee se adhik kaa nuksaan hua tha. adaalat ko teen netaaon ke khilaaf koi saboot nahin mila.
