 cart racing yaa carting ek choti, khuli va chaar pahiye ke vaahanon ke open-wheel motorsport kaa ek sanskaran he jise uski dizin ke aadhaar par cart, go-cart, gearbox/ shiftor cart kaha jaataa he. ye aam taur par chote-neeche circuit par daudai jaati hai. carting ko saamanytah motor sport ke ucch or adhik mahange sanskarnon kii shuruyaat ke rup main mana jaataa he.
 cart kii gati vyapak rup se bhinn hoti he or kuch cart 160 mile prati ghanta se adhik gati tak pahunch sakati hai, jabaki gocart ko mukhyatah manoranjan park main aam janata ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he jisaki gati ko adhiktam 15 mile prati ghanta tak simit kiya ja saktaa he. 125 sisi 2 stroke ingine or chaalak sahit 150 kilogram ke ek samgr vajan ke saath ek keef1 cart kii adhiktam gati 85 mile prati ghanta hoti he. apane 125sisi shiftor cart evam lambey paripath par 115 mile prati ghanta kii adhiktam gati ke saath yah 0 se 60 mile prati ghanta kii raftaar praapt karne main 3 second se kuch hi adhik samay leti he.
 art angiles aamtaur par carting ke janak ke rup main sweekrut hai. ek anbhavi hot roder or kurticraft par ek res car nirmaataa ke rup main unhone 1956 main dakshini california main pehali cart banaayi. carting teji se anya deshon main fail gayi, or vartmaan main europe main iske badi sankhya main diwane hai.
 gocart menuphaccharing company naamak ek america company pehali cart nirmaataa company thi. makkaloch vah pehali company thi jisane 1959 main, cart ke liye ingine kaa utpaadan kiya. isaki pehla ingine makkaloch emasi -10, ek anukulit chensow 2 stroke ingine tha. baad main 1960 ke dashak main motorcycle ingine bhi cart upyog ke liye anukulit kiya gaya jab tak ki samarpit nirmaataaon, vishesh rup se italy ne is khel ke liye ingine kaa nirmaan karnaa shuru kiya.
 iske chasis ispaat tub ke bane hote hai. isamen koi nilamban yaa suspension nahin he isliye chasis ko suspension ke rup main kaary karne ke liye paryaapt lacheela evam mode par rasta dene va na tuutane ke liye paryaapt kadaa hona chahie. sanyukt rajya america main cart chasis ko 'open', 'kejd', 'street' yaa 'ofaset' ke rup main vargeekrut kiya gaya he. sabhi siaike - exe anumodith chasis 'street' or 'open' hai.
 chasis kii kathorta cart ko vibhinn paristhitiyon hetu vibhinn handlling visheshtaon se sakasham banaati he. aamtaur par, sookhe dashaon ke liye ek kathor chasis behatar hoti he, jabaki geeli yaa anya buree paripath paristhitiyon main adhik lacheelee chasis behatar kaam kar sakati he. sabase acchhee chasis main daud kii sthiti ke anusaar peeche, saamne evam bagal main stianning baar ko jode yaa hathaaye jane kii suvidha hoti he.
 peeche kii dhuree yaa eksal par lage discs brake se brake lagaaya jaataa he. front discs brake teji se lokapriy huye hai, lekin kuch varg isaki anumati nahin dete.
 peshewar rup se daudane vaali cart kaa vajan driver ke bina aamtaur par 165 se 175 pound hota he. daud gunvattta vaali cart chasis ke kai europea nirmaataaon main se avanti, tony cart, truly, biral, siarji, gilard, intripid, cosmik, genadry yaa egge cart aadi kuch prasiddh udhaaharan hai. immik or marge cart chasis nirmaan karne vaali america companiyaan hai.
 manoranjan park main prayog kii jane vaali go-cart ko 4 stroke ingine yaa bijli kii motaron se sanchalit kiya ja saktaa he, jabaki racing cart main chote 2 stroke yaa 4 stroke ingine kaa prayog hota he.
 cart main vibhedak yaa diferential nahin hota he. vibhedak kii kami se matlab he ki ek peeche ke tair carnring ke dauraan phisalana chahie; aisa chasis kii dizin se sambhav hota he ki andar ke peeche ke tair kone par cart ke mudane ke vakt thodaa se uthe. isse tair ko apni pakad ko dhili karne evam maidan se purnatah phisalane yaa uthne main madad milti he.
 ingine se ek shrinkhalaa yaa chen ke madhyam se pichhali dhuree yaa eksal tak shakti prasaarit hoti he. ingine or eksal sprokets dono hi hataane yogya hote hai ; ingine se sabase zyada shakti arjeet karne ke liye paripath kii sthitiyon ke anusaar unke anupaat ko anukulit kiya janaa hota he.
 shuruaati dinon main, cart main keval pratyaksh drive hote the, lekin is saidap se hone vaali asuvidha ke kaaran jald hi club str virgon ke liye centriphugal klach kii shuruyaat hui. shushk centriphugal yaa cendratyagi klchon kaa prayog ab kai shreniyon main kiya jaataa he or ab yah maanak ban gaya he kyonki sheersh antarrashtriya varg janvari 2007 se 125 sisi klach yukt ingine kaa prayog kar rahe hai.
 ek saamaanya car main istemaal hone vaale pahiyon or tiron kii tulana main pahie or tair adhik chote hote hai. iske rim magnesium mishra dhaatu yaa aluminium ke bane hote hai. chasis, ingine or motor seoup ke aadhaar par tair 2 ji se adhik bal kaa samarthan kar sakate hai.
 anya motor sport kii hi tarah, upyukt paripath dashaon ke liye cart tair kai prakaar ke hote hai.
 anya motor khel kii tarah cart racing ke liye kai data adhigrahan pranaliyaan viksit kii gayi hai. in pranaaliyon se chaalak ko stearing wheel par niyat disple dwaara kuch mandandon par najar rakhane kii suvidha hoti he, jaise - aar.pi.em., laps yaa chakkar kaa samay, laps kii sankhya, sarvshereshth lap, shitalk pranali kaa taapmaana or kai baar gati yaa shiftor cart hetu samaan gear aadi.
 inamen se kuch pranaliyaan sensoron ke madhyam se lap ko record karne, puure daud satr ke riple yaa/tathaa ek data vishleshan software se las vyaktigat computer par pratyaksh downlod karne main sakasham hai. adhik parishkrut pranali main parshv or anudairdhya twaran, avrodh sthiti, stearing pahiyaa sthiti evam brake dabaav aadi adhik jaankaree praapt karne kii suvidha hoti he.
 cart racing ko aamtaur par chaar pahiyon ke motorsport ke sabase saste rup ke rup main sweekaar kiya jaataa he. ek mukt samay gatividhi ke rup main, ise lagbhag kisi bhi vyakti ke dwaara sanchalit kiya ja saktaa he or apane aap main ek motorsport ke rup main yah exe dwaara viniyamit khelon main se ek he, jo 8 saal kii umr se adhik kisi bhi vyakti ke liye license racing kii anumati detaa he. 
 sanyukt rajya america main exe kii adhik bhagidaari nahin he, iske bajaay, aikeef, dablyukee, keearti jaise kai sanghathan racing ko viniyamit karte hai.
 britain main emesa carting ko viniyamit karti he. natska jaise kuch sanghathan emesa ke adhikaar ke tahat desh bhar main daud baithkon kaa aayojan karte hai.
 australia main, cart racing kaa prabandhan exe or sieemes kii ore se australian carting association dwaara kiya jaataa he. vahaan prayek varsh ek minial jari kiya jaataa he jisamen daud baithkon or driver dwaara anupaalan ke liye vibhinn niyamon or viniyamon kaa vivaran hota he.
 is khel main kai kism ke cart circuit kii anumati he yadhyapi europe main keval sajaateey patariyon par aadhikarik rup se daud kaa aayojan ho saktaa he.
 racing kakshaain 7 yaa 8 saal kii umr main shuru hoti hai or aam taur par 3 saal ke umr samoohon yaa vajan divijanon main chalati hai jab tak ki yah shrankhla ke aadhaar par 15 yaa 16 varsh kii umr tak "varisht" sthiti par nahin pahunch jaati.
 aamtaur par, racing swaroop nimna main se ek hote hai:
 sprint racing daayen va baaain mode ke saath chote sadak paathyakram jaisi samarpit cart paripath par yojit hoti he. patariyaan lambaai main 1/4 mile se lekar main 1 mile se adhik ho sakati hai.
 sprint praaroop laghu avadhi kii daud kii ek shrinkhalaa hoti he jisamen saamanytah kam sankhya main lap yaa chakkar hote hai, jiske baad final lap hota he evam vibhinn tareeke kii ank score gananaa ke saath spardhaa ke vijeta kaa antat nirdhaaran hota he. aamtaur par res kii avadhi 15 minute se adhik nahin hoti. yahaan gati tathaa safal paas dena adhik mahatvapurn he. saamanytah iske praaroop main teen kwalifing yaa arhak dauden tathaa trophy praapt karne ke liye final daud hoti he.
 carting vishwa championships sahit exe championships, isi praaroop main hoti hai.
 indurence dauden 30 minute se lekar 24 ghante yaa adhik samay kii ek vistaarit avadhi ke liye, ek yaa ek se adhik chaalkon ke liye hoti hai. saamanytah sthirataa, vishwasaneeyata or ranneeti aadi gati se adhik mahatvapurn hoti hai.
 sanyukt rajya america main dablyukee or aikeef dwaara adhiktar sweekrut "indeuro" naamak racing, aam taur par 30 minute yaa 45 minute tak chalati hai or bina kisi pit stop ke nirantar chalati hai. indeuro spardhaaain purna aakaar ke racing road circuit par yojit hoti hai jisaki lambaai 1.5 se 4 mile ke bich hoti he.
 automobile ke liye prasiddh 24 ghante kii le mens daud kii hi tarah cart ke liye bhi 24 ghante kii spardhaa kaa aayojan da cart circuit ellen prost, le mans france main hota he. yah daud 1986 se yojit ho rahi he or iske vijetaon main chaar baar ke vijeta car champion sebestian bordas shaamil hai.
 speedway racing, daamer yaa mitti kii saamanytah 1/6 mile se lekar 1/4 mile tak lambii andakaar patariyon par hoti he. patariyaan mukhya rup se do sidhi or chaar baayein ore mude kone vaali hoti hai, kuch patariyon samamit hoti hai or inkaa aakaar aksar andakaar yaa tri-andakaar hota he.
 'ofaset' cart chasis ko andakaar or tri andakaar patariyon par keval baayen mode kii daud pratiyogita ke liye sateek handlling or samaayojan ke liye viksit kiya gaya he.
 speedway cart daud trophy ke liye lambaai main 4 lap se lekar mukhya spardhaa ke liye 20 lap tak lambii hoti he.

 carting main kai alag varg yaa sutara hote hai.

 cart chaalkon ko suraksha ke liye samuchit upkaran pahanane kii aavashyaktaa he:
 pasli rakshak or gardan collar kii haalanki atyadhik sifarish kii jaati he parantu zyaadaatar deshon main yah vaikalpik he. uparyakt main se koi bhi agnirodhi saamagree kaa banaaya janaa aavashyak nahin he. motorcycling ke dauraan chaalkon dwaara istemaal kiye jane kii hi tarah supercart chaalkon ke liye chamade kaa chaudaa pahananaa jaruri he.
 cart racing ko aamtaur par chaalkon ko motor racing se parichit karaane ke liye ek kam laagat or apekshaakrit surakshit tareeke ke rup main prayog kiya jaataa he. kai logon ise yuva chaalkon ke saath sambhddha karte hai, lekin vayask bhi carting main bahut sakriy hai. kisi bhi gambhir resar ke karier main carting pehla kadam mana jaataa he. yah chaalkon ko twarit sajagata, sateek car niyantran or nirnay kaushal ke vikaas main madad ke dwaara ucch gati racing ke liye taiyaar kar sakati he. iske alaava, yah vibhinn maapdandon ke bare main jaagrukta laati he jinka prayog cart kii pratispardhaatmakataa main sudhaar karne kii koshish main kiya ja saktaa he jo ki motor racing ke anya roopon main maujuud hoti he.
 sabhi vartmaan or kai purv formula van chaalkon ne racing cart se shuruyaat kii he, inamen sabase pramukh hai vishwa champion maaikel shoomakar, yrton senna, ellen prost, fernando alonso, kimi riconen, jension batan, louis hamiltan or sebstian vetal. kai eneeskeeaar chaalkon ne bhi cart racing se shuruyaat kii he jinamein direll waltrip, lake speed, riki rud, juaan pablo montoya, tony stewart or jeff gordon shaamil hai. 
 agast 2009 main, pheraari ke saath formula van racing main sambhavit vaapasi kii pratyaasha main, formula van vishwa champion maaikel shoomakar ne lonato, italy main ek cart dwaara taiyaari kii thi. shoomakar ne las vegas main prayek varsh yojit hone vaali esakeuese super neshnal spardhaa main formula van chaalkon sibastian bumi evam nelson picket junior ke saath res kii thi.. hungarian grant pricks qualifire ke dauraan hui apni durghatana ke do maheene baad brazil main apni sthiti kaa pareekshan karne ke liye philip massa ne sitambar 2009 main carting kaa prayog kiya tha.
