 is moolbhoot siddhant kaa paalan karte hue prajapita brahmakumari ishwariya vishwa vidhyalay in dinon vishwa bhar main dharm ko nae mandandon par paribhaashit kar rahaa he. jivan kii daud-dhoop se thak chuke manushya aaj shanti kii talash main is sanstha kii ore pravrutt ho rahe hai.
 yah koi nayaa dharm nahin balki vishwa main vyaapt dharmon ke saar ko aatmsaat kar unhen maanav kalyaan kii disha main upyog karne vaali ek sanstha he. jisaki vishwa ke 137 deshon main 8,500 se adhik shakhaayen hai. in shakhaon main 10 lakh vidhyaarthee pratidin naitik or aadhyatmik shiksha grahan karte hai.
 is sanstha kii sthapana dada lekharaj ne kii, jinhen va aaj ham prajapita brahma ke naam se jaanate hai.
 dada lekharaj avibhajit bharat main hiron ke vyapaaree the. ve balykaal se hi dharmik pravriti ke the. 60 varsh kii aayu main unhen paramatma ke satyaswaroop ko pahchaanane kii divya anubhuti hui. unhen ishwar kii sarvocch satta ke prati khinchaav mahasoos hua. isi kaal main unhen jyoti swaroop niraakaar parampita shiv kaa saakshaatkaar hua. iske baad dhire-dhire unka man maanav kalyaan kii ore pravrutt hone laga.
 unhen sansaarik bandhanon se mukt hone or paramatma kaa maanavarupi madhyam banane kaa nirdesh praapt hua. usee kii prerana ke falswaroop san 1936 main unhone is viraat sanghathan kii choti-si buniyaad rakhi. san 1937 main aadhyatmik gyaan or rajyog kii shiksha anekon tak pahunchaane ke liye isane ek sanstha kaa rup dhaaran kiya.
 is sanstha kii sthapana ke liye dada lekharaj ne apana vishaal kaarobaar kalakatta main apane saajhedaar ko saunp diya. phir ve apane janmasthan hyderabad sindh main laut aae. yahan par unhone apni sari chal-achal sampatti is sanstha ke naam kar di. prarambh main is sanstha main keval mahilaayein hi thi.
 baad main dada lekharaj ko ‘prajapita brahma’ naam diya gaya. jo log aadhyatmik shanti ko paane ke liye ‘prajapita brahma’ dwaara uccharit siddhanto par chale, ve brahmakumar or brahmakumari kehalaae tathaa is shaikshanik sanstha ko ‘prajapita brahmakumari ishwariya vishwa vidhyalay’ naam diya gaya.
