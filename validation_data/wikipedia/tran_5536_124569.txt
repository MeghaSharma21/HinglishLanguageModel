 uigur purvi or madhya asia main basane vaale turki jati kii ek janajati he. vartmaan main uigur log adhiktar chini janavaadee ganraajya dwaara niyantrit couinjiyang uigur swytt pradesh naam ke rajya main basate hai. inamen se lagbhag 80 pratishat is kshetra ke dakshin-pashchim main sthit tarim ghati main rahete hai. uigur log uigur bhashaa bolte hai jo turki bhashaa parivaar kii ek boli he.
 aitihaasik rup se 14veen sadi main inhen hunaan praant ke ek vidroh ko dabaane ke liye ming raja dwaara bulaayaa gaya tha. kuch sainik vahiin bas gaye the jinhen shaasha ne jiaan kii oopaadhi di thi. aaj bhi kuch uigur hunan praant main rahete hai. ugur logon ko is naam se vishwayudhon ke bich kisi samay se bulaanaa shuru kiya gaya. isse pehle inako turki, muslim yaa sarat kehete the. or us samay uigur shabd kaa prayog kisi pracheen samrajya ke liye kiya jaataa tha, jo us samay khatm ho chukaa tha. 1921 main hue taashkand sammelan main inhen uigur sambodhan pradaan kiya gaya- jo usee kshetra ke puraane uigur kagaanath ke naam se liya gaya tha. anya pashchimi paryatakon ne us samay tak turki shabd kaa hi istemaal kiya.
