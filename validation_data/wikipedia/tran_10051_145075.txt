 11 farvari 2014 ko algerian airfors lokaheed si-130 harculas vimaan algiers se 500 kimi purv aum al-buaagi main durghatnagrast ho gaya. praarambhik khabaron main 100 yatriyon sahit chaalak dal ke sadsy savaar bataaye gaye the. vimaan main kuch sainik or unke parivaar savaar the. kanstantin or aum al-buaagi ke bich vimaan se sampark tut gaya. praathmik samaacharon ke anusaar kharaab mausam ko vimaan haadase kaa kaaran bataaya gaya he. lekin baad main praapt aadhikarik sutron ke anusaar bachaav karmiyon ne 76 shav praapt kiye or vimaan main savaar kul logon kii sankhya 78 bataai gai jinamein se ek surakshit rah paaya. kuch pratyekshadarshiyon ke anusaar durghatnagrast hone se pehle vimaan pahari se takaraayaa.
