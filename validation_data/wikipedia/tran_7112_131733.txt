 baaqi solahavin shatee ke ek prasiddh turk kavi the.
 iskaa puraa naam mahmud abdul baaqi tha or iskaa janmasthan kustutuniya he. yah daridr gharaane kaa vyakti tha kintu isko us samay ke prasiddh vidwaanon se shiksha grahan karne kaa avasar mila or turki ke ucch koti ke sahityakaron evam kaviyon kaa satsang bhi. 18-19 varsh hi kii avasthaa main istabol ke prasiddh kaviyon main isaki gananaa hone lagi. san 1555 i. main jab sultan suleman aazam iran kii chadhaai se laut aayaa, baaqi ne uske eshwarya par bada ullaspurn ek prashansaatmak kaseeda uske samaksh upasthit kiya. sultan ise sunakar itnaa prabhavit hua ki usne baaqi se apni kavitaaon par 'naziriye' likhane kaa aadesh diya. is prakaar isaki pahunch darbar tathaa ucch koti ke samaaj tak sahaj main ho gai. sultan kii is krupa se swayan iske mitragan bhi jalane lage parantu yah turki kaa sabase bada kavi mana jane laga or isaki prasiddhi badi shighrata se puure rajya hi main nahin, pratyut hindustan tak fail gai.
 sultan suleman kii vishesh krupa se baaqi ko uski nikat parshvavartita praapt ho gai thi. is kaaran sultan kii mrityu kaa isapar bada prabhaav padaa or isi prabhaav ke kaaran isane sultan kii smriti main ek marasia likhaa, jo isaki shreshth rachana maani jaati he. baaqi arabi tathaa farsi kaa bhi vidvan tha. isane arabi kii bahut si pustakon kaa turki main anuwaad bhi kiya he or farsi bhashaa main kavita bhi kii he. parantu isaki sarvaadhik janpriyata turki kii kavita hi ke kaaran hui he or isko us yug ke kaviyon kii pratham shrenee hi main sthaan nahin diya gaya he, pratyut turki ke gazal gayakon kaa sirtaj bhi kaha gaya he. gazalon ke sivaa iske qaside tathaa marasiye bhi kavyadrushti se purnataa tak pahunche hue hai. yadhyapi isane apane anek purvavartiyon kii kavita se laabh uthaaya he tathaapi apane vishisht vyaktitv ko bhi banaae rakhaa he.
