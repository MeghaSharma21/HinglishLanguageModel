 shringaar kaa bhav rati naamak sthaayee manobhaav se utpann hota he. is duniya main jo kuch bhi sud or deeptiman he use shringaar kaha jaataa he.
 yah bhav purush, stri or ujjwal yuvon se sambandhit he. iskaa matlab yah he ki ye purush or stri ke bich pyaar or uske parinamo se sambandh rakhata he. is avadhi dwaara ek bahut gahari samaj avgat ko karaayaa jaataa he, shringaar avadhi kaa matlab he saundarya. isliye shringaar lahari ko saundarya lahari bhi kaha jaataa he.pyaar manushya kaa saundarya he or yahi pyaar use ek alag pahchaan detii he or use sabhi kritiyon main sarvocch banaati he.is ras ke do kursiyaan he; arthath: sgh main pyaar or judaai main pyaar .
