 anvik kram-vikaas samay ke saath-saath anuon main hone vaale kram-vikaas ko kehate hai. jeev-vaigyanikon nen kuch anu kii shreniyon main yugon ke saath-saath aae parivartan kaa gaharaai se adhyayan kiya he, jaise kii deoxyribonucleic amla, ribos neucleic amla or bhinn prakaaron ke protein. in anuon main hue kram-vikaas kaa jeev-jantuon par bahut bada asar padataa he kyonki kisi jeev ke andar maujuud anuvanshiki anu hi yah tay karte hai ke jeev kii prakruti kya hogi. manushyon main bhi rang-rup, lambaai-nataapan, yahan tak ki rakt samooh bhi yahi anuvanshiki-sambandhit anu nirdhaarit karte hai.
 angrezi main "anvik kram-vikaas" ko "moulicular evolushan" kehete hai.
