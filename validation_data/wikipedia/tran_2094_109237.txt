 sahih muslim sunni islam main yah sunni musalamaanon sunni musalamaanon ke saath-saath jaidi shiya muslimon dwaara atyadhik prashansit he. or sahih al-bukaari ke baad doosraa sabase pramanik haddees sangrah mana jaataa he. yah muslim ebn al-hajjaj dwaara ekatra kiya gaya tha, jinhen imam muslim bhi kaha jaataa he. is tathy ke kaaran kabhi-kabhi isaki pramanikta par sawaal uthaaya gaya he ki yah islamic paigambar, mohammad ke 250 saal baad likhaa gaya tha. iske baavjood, sunni musalamaanon kaa maananaa ​​he ki yah vaastavik or pramanik he. sahih muslim, sahih al-bukaari ke saath sahihain ke rup main janaa jaataa he.
 sahih muslim ke sangrahakarta muslim ebn al-hajj kaa janm nishapur main 204 hijri main ek farsi parivaar main hua tha or 261 hijri main unki mrityu ho gai thi. unhone vyapak rup se iraq, arab prayadweep, syria or misr ke kshetron sahit ahadis ke sangrah ko ikattha karne ke liye vyapak rup se yatra kii.
 300,000 haddees main se unhone moolyankan kiya, kade sweekruti mandandon ke aadhaar par unke sangrah main shaamil karne ke liye lagbhag 4,000 hi nikaale gaye. unke sangrah main prayek report kii jaanch kii gai or ullekhakartaaon kii shrinkhalaa kii satyata ko dridhtaa se sthapit kiya gaya tha. sunni musalamaanon ko sahih al bukhari ke baad yah doosraa sabase pramanik haddees sangrah mana jaataa he. sahih muslim 43 kitabon main baanta gaya he, jisamen kul 9200 kathaayen hai. haalanki, yah jaananaa mahatvapurn he ki muslim ebn al-hajjaj ne kabhi bhi sabhi pramanik paramparaon ko ikattha karne kaa daawa nahin kiya kyonki unka lakshya keval paramparaon ko ikattha karnaa tha ki sabhi muslimon ko sateekta ke bare main sahamat hona chahie.
 munsiri ke anusaar, sahih muslim main kul 2,200 haddees hai. mohammad amin ke mutabik, 1,400 pramanik haddees hai jo anya pustakon main mukhya rup se chah pramukh haddees sangrahon main dekhe jaasakate hai.
 kai muslim is sangrah ko chah pramukh haddees sangrahon main se duusare sabase pramanik maanate hai,  jisamen keval sahih haddees hote hai. or is haddees ke sangrah ko sahih al bukhari ke saath saamaan mana jaataa he, donon ko do sahih kaha jaataa he. shiya muslim kuch kathaon kii sandigdh vishwasaneeyata ke kaaran apni kuch saamagree ko avishwasaneey rup se khaarij kar dete hai.
 pustak ke ucch str ke baavjood, or vidwaanon kii sarvasammati yah he ki sahih al bukhari ke baad haddees kii duusari sabase maanya vargeekrut pustak he, yah is baat par sahamat he ki iskaa matlab yah nahin he ki isamen har tatv satya he, anya haddees kii kitabon kii tulana main yah kitaab pramanik mani jaati he. praathmik rup se is kitaab ko sahih al bukhari ke saamaan mana jaataa he, is kaa yah bhi matlab nahin ki sahih al bukhari main sahih muslim se bhi zyaada pramanik haddees hai.
