 sugauli sandhi, east india company or nepal ke raja ke bich hui ek sandhi he, jise 1814-16 ke dauraan huye british-nepali yuddh ke baad astitv main laayaa gaya tha. is sandhi par 2 disambar 1815 ko hastakshrar kiye gayi or 4 march 1816 kaa iskaa anumodan kiya gaya. nepal kii ore se is par raaj guru gajraj mishra or company ore se leftinent karnal paris bradshow ne hastakshar kiye the. is sandhi ke anusaar nepal ke kuch hisson ko british bharat main shaamil karne, kathmandu main ek british pratinidhi kii niyukti or britain kii sainya seva main gorkhaon ko bhartee karne kii anumati di gayi thi, saath hi iske dwaara nepal ne apni kisi bhi seva main kisi america yaa europea karmchaaree ko niyukt karne kaa adhikaar bhi kho diya. 
 sandhi ke tahat, nepal ne apane niyantran vaale bhubhag kaa lagbhag ek tihaai hissaa ganvaa diya jisame nepal ke raja dwaara pichle 25 saal main jite gayi kshetra jaise ki purv main sikkim, pashchim main kumaun or gadhwal rajshahi or dakshin main tarai kaa adhiktar kshetra shaamil tha. tarai bhoomi kaa kuch hissaa 1816 main hi nepal ko lautaa diya gaya. 1860 main tarai bhoomi kaa ek bada hissaa nepal ko 1857 ke bhartiya vidroh ko dabaane main britishon kii sahaayta karne kii evaj main pun: lautaaya gaya.
 kathmandu main tainaat british pratinidhi mall yug ke baad nepal main rahane pehla pashchimi vyakti tha. . nepal main britishon ke pehle pratinidhi, edward gardenar ko kathmandu ke uttari hisse main tainaat kiya gaya tha or aaj yah sthaan lazimpaat kehalaata he or yahaan british or bhartiya duutavas sthit hai. disambar 1923 main sugauli sandhi ko adhikramit kar "satat shanti or maitri kii sandhi", main pronnat kiya gaya or british nivasee ke darje ko pratinidhi se badhaakar doot kaa kar diya gaya. 1950 main bharat or nepal ne ek nayi sandhi par do swatantra deshon ke rup main hastakshar kiye gaye jiskaa uddeshy donon deshon ke bich sambandhon ko ek nae sire se sthapit karnaa tha.
 do saal lambey chale british- nepali yuddh ko khatm karne ke liye 1816 main, east india company or nepal kii gorkha rajshahi ne is sandhi par hastakshar kiye the. is sandhi ke tahat, mithila kshetra kaa ek hissaa bharat se alag hokar nepal ke adhikaar kshetra main chalaa gaya. is bhaag ko nepal main, purvi tarai yaa mithila kaha jaataa he.
 sugauli sandhi ke astitv main aane tak, purv main darjeeling or tista, dakshin-pashchim main nainital or pashchim main kumaun rajshahi, gadhwal rajshahi or bashahar jaise kshetra nepal ke adheen aate the. in kshetron par nepal ne pichley lagbhag 25 varshon ke dauraan vijay praapt kii thi, jinhen sandhi ke baad bharat ko waapas lautaaya gaya.
 british-nepali yuddh ke baad, nepal sarkaar or east india company ke bich shanti or maitri kii ek sandhi par hastakshar kiye gayi. 2 disambar 1815 ko is sandhi par nepal sarkaar kii ore se raaj guru gajraj mishra jinke sahaayak chandra shekhar upaadhyaay the or company kii ore se leftinent karnal paris bradshow dwaara hastakshar kiye gayi. 4 march 1816 ko chandra shekhar upaadhyaay or general david octerloni dwaara makwanpur main sandhi kii hastakshrit pratiyon kaa aadan pradaan kiya gaya. sandhi kii sharten nimnlikhit thim: -
 iske baad disambar 1816 main ek uttargami samjhaute par sahmati bani jiske anusaar nepal ko mechi nadi ke purv or mahakali nadi ke pashchim ke bich kaa tarai kshetra waapas lautaa diya gaya. is samjhaute ke falswaroop do lakh rupye prativarsh kii kshatipurti raashi ke praavdhaan ko samaapt kar diya gaya. ek bhoomi surveykshan ke dwaara donon rashtron ke bich kii seemaa ko tay karne kaa prastaav bhi sweekaar kiya gaya tha.
