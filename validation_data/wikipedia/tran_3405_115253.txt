 harishankar parasaai hindi ke prasiddh lekhak or vyangakaar the. unka janm jamaani, hoshangabad, madhya pradesh main hua tha. ve hindi ke pehle rachanaakaar hai jinhone vyangy ko vidha kaa darja dilaayaa or use halke–phulke manoranjan kii paramparaagat paridhi se ubarkar samaaj ke vyapak prashnon se joda. unki vyangy rachanaayen hamaare man main gudagudi hi paidaa nahin karatin balki hamen un saamaajik vaastaviktaaon ke aamane–saamne khadaa karti he, jinase kisi bhi vyakti kaa alag rah paanaa lagbhag asambhav he. lagaataar khokhli hoti ja rahi hamaari saamaajik or rajnatik vyavastha main pisate madhyamvargeey man kii sachchaaiyon ko unhone bahut hi nikatataa se pakadaa he. saamaajik paakhand or roodhiwaadee jivan–mulyon kii khilli udaate hue unhone sadaiv vivek or vigyaan–sammat drushti ko sakaaratmak rup main prastut kiya he. unki bhashaa–style main khaas kism kaa apanaapa he, jisase pathak yah mahasoos karata he ki lekhak uske saamne hi baitha he.
 unhone nagpur vishvavidhyalay se hindi main em.e. kii oopaadhi praapt kii.
 18 varsh kii umr main van vibhaag main naukari. khandwa main 6 maheene adhyaapan. do varsh jabalpur main spes training kaliz main shikshan kii oopaadhi lee. 1942 se vahiin model hai skool main adhyaapan. 1952 main unhone sarkari naukari chodi. 1953 se 1957 tak privet skulon main naukari. 1957 main naukari chhodkar swatantra lekhan kii shuruuaat. jabalpur se 'vasudha' naam kii saahitiyik masiki nikaali, nai duniya main 'suno bhai saadho', nayi kahaniyon main 'paanchavaan kalam' or 'uljhi–uljhi' tathaa kalpana main 'or ant main' ityadi kahaniyaan, upanyaas evam nibandh–lekhan ke baavjood mukhyat: vyangyakaar ke rup main vikhyath.parasaai mukhyatah vyang -lekhak he, par unka vyang keval manorajan ke liye nahi he. unhone apane vyang ke dwaara baar-baar pathako kaa dhyaan vyakti or samaaj kii un kamajoriyon or visangatiyo kii ore aakrisht kiya he jo hamaare jivan ko dubhar bana rahi he. unhone saamaajik or raajnneetik jivan main vyaapt bhrashtachar evam shoshan par karaara vyang kiya he jo hindi vyang -sahitya main anoothaa he. parasaai ji apane lekhan ko ek saamaajik karm ke rup main paribhaashit karte he. unki manyata he ki saamaajik anubhav ke bina sachcha or vaastavik sahitya likhaa hi nahi ja saktaa.parasaai ji multah ek vyangakaar he. saamaajik visangatiyo ke prati gaharaa sarokaar rakhane vaala hi lekhak sachcha vyangakaar ho saktaa he. parasaai ji samayik samay kaa rachanaatmak upyog karte he. unka samoochaa sahitya vartmaan se muthbhed karata hua dikhaai detaa he. parasaai ji hindi sahitya main vyang vidha ko ek nai pahchaan di or use ek alag rup pradaan kiya, iske liye hindi sahitya unka hameshaa rini rahegaa.
 parasaai jabalpur va raipur se prakashit akhbaar deshbandhu main pathakon ke prashnon ke uttar dete the. unke kolum kaa naam tha - parsi se puuchhen. pehle pahal halke, ishkia or filmi sawaal puche jate the. dhire-dhire parasaai ji ne logon ko gambheer saamaajik-rajnatik prashnon kii ore pravrutt kiya. dayera antarrashtriya ho gaya. yah pahal logon ko shikshit karne ke liye thi. log unke sawaal-jawaab padhane ke liye akhbaar kaa intjaar karte the.
 viklang shraddha kaa daur ke liye sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit.
