 katchal bharat ke nicobar dweepasmooh main boli jane vaali ek bhashaa he jo austro-asian bhashaaon kii nicobari shakha kii sadsy he. iske maatrubhaashi katchal dweep par kendrit hai. iske padosi trinket dweep par isaki ek upbhasha boli jaati he. katchal bhashaa or trinket bhashaa ke bolne vaale ek-duusare ko samaj sakate hai lekin ve kisi bhi anya madhya nicobari bhashaa ko nahin samaj sakate. 2004 kii sunami ke baad trinket ke sabhi logon ko katchal or camorta dweep par laayaa gaya or anumaan he ki, jaise ve sthaneey aabaadi main ghulmil jate hai, trinket upbhasha vilupt ho jaayegi.
