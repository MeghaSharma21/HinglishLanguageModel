 madras vishvidhyalay bharat ke tamilnadu praant kii rajdhani chennai nagar main sthit he. yah dakshin bharat ke vishvavidhyalayon main sabase purana vishvavidhyalay he. 11 navambar 1839 main ek saarvajanik yachika dwaara madras vishvavidhyalay kaa prarambh hua. 1840 main george norton kii adhyakshata main vishvavidhyalay samiti kaa nirmaan hua or 5 sitambar 1857 se yah vishvavidhyalay prarambh hua.
