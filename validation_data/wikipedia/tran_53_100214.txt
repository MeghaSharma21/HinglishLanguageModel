 punaravartan main sambhavit upyog main aane vaali samagriyon ke apshisht kii rokthaam kar nae utpaadon main sansaadhit karne kii prakriya taaje kachche maalon ke upbhog ko kam karne ke liye, urja ke upyog ko ghataane ke liye vaayu-pradooshan ko kam karne ke liye tathaa jal pradooshan paaramparik apshisht ke niptaan kii aavashyaktaa ko kam karne ke liye, tathaa aprayukt vishuddh utpaada kii tulana main greenhouse gas ke utsarjan ko kam karne ke liye punaravartan main prayukt padaarthon ko nae utpaadon main prasanskaran kii prakriyaayein sammilit hai. punaravartan aadhunik apshisht ko kam karne main pramukh tathaa apshisht ko "kam karne, punah prayog karne, punaravartan karne" kii kram parampara kaa teesraa ghatak he.
 punaravartaneey padaarthon main kai kism ke kaanch, kaagaj, dhaatu, plastic, kapade, evam electronics shaamil hai. haalanki prabhaav main ek jaisaa hi lekin aamtaur par jaivik vikrutiyon ke apashesh se khaad banane athwa anya punah upyog main laane - jaise ki bhojan tathaa baag-bagichon ke kacharon ko punaravartan ke laayak nahin samajha jaataa he. punaravartaneey samagriyon ko yaa to kisi sangrah shaala main laayaa jaataa he athwa ucchhist sthaan se hi uthaa liya jaataa he, tab unhen nae padaarthon main utpaadan ke liye chantai, safai tathaa punarvinikaran kii jaati he.
 sahi myne main, padaarth ke punaravartan se usee saamagree kii taaja aapurti hogi, udaahranarth, istemaal main aa chukaa kagaz or adhik kagaz utpaadit karegaa, athwa istemaal main aa chukaa fom polistyrine se adhik polistyrine paidaa hoga. haalanki, yah kabhi-kabhaar yaa to kathin athwa kaaphi kharchila ho jaataa he, isiilie kai utpaadon athwa samagriyon ke punaravartan main anya samagriyon ke utpaadan main badale main unken hi apane hi punah upyog shaamil hai. punaravartan kaa ek or doosraa tarika mishra utpaadon se, bache hue maal ko yaa to unki nijee keemat ke kaaran, athwa unki jokhimi gunvattta ke kaaran phir se ubarkar vyavhaar yogya bananaa he.
 punaravartan kii prakriya main ai laagat ke kaaran aalochakon main nival aarthik or paryaavarniya laabhon ko lekar matbhed hai or unke suzav ke anusaar punaravartan ke prastaavak padaarthon ko or bhi badtar bana dete hai tathaa anubhodan evam pushtikaran ke pakshpatpurna purvagrah jhelana padataa he. visheshrup se, aalochakon kaa is maamle main tark he ki sangrahikaran evam dhulaai main lagane vaali laagat evam urja utpaadan ki prakriya main bachaai gai laagat or urja se ghat jaati or saath hi yah bhi ki punaravart ke udyog main utpann naukariyaan lakdi udyog, khadaan evam anya moulik utpaadanon se jude udyogon kii naukariyaan ko nikrusht sakjha jaati he; or samagriyon jaise ki kagaz kii lugdi aadi kaa punaravartak saamagree ke apakarshan se kuch hi baar pehle ho saktaa he jo or aage punaravartan ke liye baadhak hai. punaravartan ke prastaavkon ke aise prayek daawey main vivaad he or is sandarbh main donon hi pakshon se tark kii pramanikta ne lambe vivaad ko janm diya he.
 maanav sabhyataa ke itihaas main punaravartan ek aam dastur rahaa he, jiske 400 isapurv se plato jaise himachati rahen hai. ansi avadhiyon main jab sansadhano kaa abhaav rahaa he apshisht kooday karkaton ke pracheen puratathvik adhyayanon se pataa chalata he ki gharon ke kooday karkat kam hoten the — jisase yah pramanit hota he ki nae ke abhaav main zyada se zyada kooday-karkat ko punaravartit kar liya jaataa tha.
 aise bhi pramaan he ki praaq audyogic kaal main, europe main kaanse evam anya dhaatuon kii kataranon ko ekachitr kar phir se istemaal main laane ke liye galaaya jaataa tha. britain main kooda-karkat batorne waalon ko koyale kii aag se ikaitha kii gai raakh or dhul ko mul saamagree ke rup main istemaal kar it banane main punraritt kar liya jaataa tha. in punrariti ke ke prabhedon main pramukh parichaalak punraritt punanirvesh 
 yogya saamaan ke stock kii aarthik anukulata thi, na ki kachche suddh saamaan kii uplabdhta saath hi saath dhani aabaadi vaale ilaakon main kooda-karkat kii safai main kami bhi thi. varsh 1813 main, betli, yorkshir benjamin kanoon ne chithdon ko "mote kapadon" tathaa 'maango' uni vastron main badal dene kii prakriya viksit kii. is padaarth main punraritt reshon ke saath shuddh oon ko mishrit kar diya gaya. pashchimi yorkshaire ke shaharon maran mote khurdare kapadon ke udyog udaahranarth betli or dusbari, 19veen sadi se aarambh kar kam se kam pratham vishwa yuddh tak barkaraar rahe. 
 vishwayudhon ke samay sansadhano ke abhaav, evam aise hi vishwa-parivartaneey ghatnaon ne punaravartan ko protsaahit kiya. vishwayuddh dwittiya main shaamil harek desh main sarkari str par punaravart kaa vyapak rup se protsaahan hetu prachaar-prasar kiya gaya, nagarikon se desh bhakthi bhaavana pradarshan karne hetu yah nivedan kiya gaya ki ve dhaatuon or istemaal ho chuke vastron ko daan kar den. yuddh ke dauraan sansadhano ke sangrakshan hetu pariyojnaayen lagu kii gayi jo kuch deshon main jahaan prakritik sansadhano ke prachurya kaa abhaav tha jaise ki japan main, yuddh kii samaapti ke baad bhi jari rahi.
 urja ke laagat main vruddhi ke kaaran, punaravartan main sabase bada nivesh 1970 ke dashakon ke dauraan hua. aluminium ke punaravartan main shuddh utpaadan hetu keval 5 pratishat urja kii hi aavashyaktaa hoti hai; kaanch, kagaz tathaa dhaatuon main naatakeey rup se kam kintu bahut hi ullekhneey rup se urja kii bachat hoti he, jab punaravartit jame sansaadhan ke stock ko upyog main laayaa jaataa he.
 samooche sanyukt rajya main neujersey woudbari hi punaravartan ko adhideshadhin karne vaala ekmaatr pratham shahar tha. rose rowane ke netritv main 1970 ke aarambhik dashakon main, apshisht prabandhan kii gaadi ke peeche punaravartan trailor ko khinchne kaa khyaal aayaa taaki kachare tathaa jamane vaali punaravartaneey saamagree ko saath hi saath sangruhit bhi kiya ja sake. is padyati kaa anukaran duusare kasbon or shaharon ne bhi kiya or aaj sanyukt rajya main kai shahar punaravartan ke liye aavashyak saamagree juta lete hai.
 varsh 1987 main, mobro 4000 jalayaan kooda-karkat ke bhandar ko neuyork se dakshini kerolina dho kar le gaya; jahaan use lene se manaa kar diya gaya. tab ise beliz bhej diya gaya; vahaan bhi isi tarah ise lene se manaa kar diya gaya. jalayaan neuyork laut aayaa or kooda-karkat ke us ambar ko jalaakar bhasma kar diya gaya. is ghatana se apshisht ke nishpaadan tathaa punaravartan ko lekar media main garmagaram bahes chhid gai. yah aksar 1990 ke dashakon kii punaravartan ke "paagalpan" ko prajjwalit karne vaali ghatana ke rup main maani jaati he.
 punaravartan kii pariyojna ko kargar banane main, vyapak rup se, punaravartaneey padaarth kii satat aapurti kathin he. aisi aapurti kii sristi ke liye teen vaikalpik vidhaan prayog main laae jate hai: anivaary punaravartan sangrahan, paatr main jamaa karne kaa kanoonee aadesh, tathaa apshisht par pratibandh. anivaary sangrahan kanoonon ne saharon main paravartan ke uddeshyon ko aamtaur par nishchit tithi tak sahar ke kachare kaa nirdhaarit pratishat avashya hi visthapit kar die jane ko lakshya banaaya. tab shahar is lakshya ko puraa karne ke liye uttaradaayi ho gaye.
 sangraahak paatr main jamaa karne ke kanoon main kuch contenron ko lautaane ke evaj main kuch raashi lautne kii peshkash kii gai, khaastaur par kaanch, plastic or dhaatu. aise container main jab koi utpaada karidaa jaataa tha, ek alp atirikt shulk uske mulya par dhary hota tha. agar container ko sangrah-centre par lautaa diya jaataa tha to upabhokta dwaara yah atirikt shulk waapas maang liya ja saktaa tha. ye pariyojnaayen aksar 80% punaravartan ke parinaam ke dar se kaaphi safal raheen. aise parinaamon ke baavjood, sangrahan aanchalik sarkaron se hatakar udyog tathaa upabhoktaaon ke paas sthanantarit ho jane ke kaaran kuch kshetron main ansi yojanaon ko sashatt pratirodh kaa saamana karnaa padaa.
 punaravartaneey padaarthon kii aapurti main vruddhi kii teesri padyati kuch padaarthon ko apshisht ke rup main kooda-karkat main nishpaadan par pratibandh lagaana he, jinamein aksar istemaal ho chuke tel, puraani batariyaan, tyrs tathaa baag-bagichon ke kooday-karkat shaamil hai. pratibandhit utpaadon ke uchit nishpaadan ke liye vyavhaarya aarthik sthiti utpann karnaa hi is padyati kaa pramukh uddeshy he. is bare main saavdhaanee baratani chahie ki aisi paravartaneey sevaayen adhikadhik sankhya main maujuud na hon, athwa aise pratibandh gaiyrkanoonee tareeke se kooday-karkat kaa bahut bada ambaar na khadaa kar de. 
 punaravartateet padaarthon ke liye maang ko badhaane evam barkaraar rakhane ke liye bhi kanoon kaa prayog kiya gaya. aisi chaar kanoonee paddatiyaan maujuud hai: kam-se-kam punaravartit maatra ke adhidesh, upyogita kii daren, adhiprapti kii nitiyaan, punaravartit utpaada ke vargeekaran evam namakaran.
 kam se kam punaravartit maatra ke adhidesh tathaa upyogita kii daron ne utpaadakon ko unke prachaalan main paravartan ko shaamil karne ko sidhe baadhya kar diya. maatra ke adhideshon main yah saafe-saafe jaahir he ki nae utpaada ke kuch nishchit pratishat main punaravartit padaarth kaa hona aavashyak he. upyogita kii daren adhik lacheele vikalp hai; prachaalan ke kisi bhi bindu par athwa yahaan tak ki vipaneey rinon ke liye vinimay main paravartan ke lakshya kii prapti ke liye udyogon ko anubandh kii bhi anumati de di gai he. in donon hi paddatiyon ke virodhiyon kaa nishaanaa unli aavashyaktaaon ko baadha-chadhaakar pesh karne or yah daawa karne kii ki ve udyogon se unke jaruri lacheelepan ko jabardastee le lete hai.
 sarkaron ne unki apni cruy, kshamta kaa prayog paravartaneey mog ko badhaane ke liye jis jariye kiya he use "prapan prabandh" niti kehete hai. in nitiyon ko yaa to "dar kinar" kar diya gaya he, jo punraritt utpaadon main puri tarah kharch karne ke ek nishchit pariman par apni chhap chodta he, athwa "mulya kii praathamikta" kii pariklpanaaen karata he jo punraritt samanon ke kharid jane par bade bajet kaa praavdhaan karata he. atirikt adhinium vishisht maamalon ko hi apana lakshya nirdhaarit kar sakate hai: sanyukt rajya america main, udaahranarth, paryaavarniya sangrakshan agency ke tel, kagaz, tair tathaa punraritt athwa yathasambhav punah sanshodhit awaas main istemaal hue rodhan kii kharid par adhidesh jari karti he.
 antim rup se sarkari adhinium punraritt utpaada kii labling kii badhati hui bhaag ko lekar he. jab utpaadakon ko unki punraritt padaarthon ke utpaada ke parinaam ke saath packaging par labling upabhokta hi uchit samajhadaari vaala chunaav kar sakate hai. kray karne kii prachur kshamta vaale upabhokta tab adhik paryaavaranneey sachetan vikalp kaa chunaav kar sakate hai, utpaadakon ko unke utpaadon main punraritt padaarth ke pariman main vruddhi ke liye tatpar karte hai, tathaa paroksh rup se maang main vruddhi karte hai. paravartan ke pruthakkaran evam namakaran ke starikaran main bhi punaravartiya aapurti ke ghanaatmak prabhaav ho sakate hai agar labling main ve suchanaaen bhi shaamil ho ki kaise or kahaan utpaadon ko punaravartit kiya ja saktaa he.
 punaravartiya padaarthon ko aam apshisht kii bikhri hui silsilewar qatari se sangrah kar ekatrit karne kii kai alag-alag pranaliyaan lagu kii gai hai. ye pranaliyaan aam janata kii suvidha or sarkari aasaani evam kharch ke bich vyavsaayik pratibimb banaati hai. ekatrikaran kii teen pramukh shreniyan "kachra phenke jane vaale centre", punah kharide jane vaale centre evam niyantrit kshetra ke ekatrikaran hai.
 jahaan kachra giraae jate hai aise kendron main apshisht utpaadakon ko punaravartiya padaarthon ko kendriya ilaake main, yaa to sthapit yaa phir bhramyaman sangrahan stesan athwa khud punah parichaalit sanyatra tak dhokar le janaa padataa he. ekatrikaran ke sabase aasaan tareeke to ye maan liye gaye hai, lekin apekshit kaaryaklap se anbhigya or kam hone ke kaaran sahanaa padataa he. punah khride jane vaale centre punaravartaneey padaarthon kii kharid or safai ke maamle main chunki bhinn hai, atah ke nirantar aapurti evam istemaal ke liye spasht protsaahan pradaan karti he. tab sansadhano parant padaarth ko bechaa ja saktaa he, is aashaa ke saath ki isse laabh hoga. durbhagyavash sarkari aarthik sahaytaaen punah kharid jane vaale kendron ko upyogi laabhprad udyog banane ke liye sarkari aarthik sahaytaaen aavashyak hai, jaisaa ki united stats nesan solid vests management ke anusaar ek ton saamagree ko process karne main isaki laagat ausatan 50 america dollar ke aasapaas aati he, jise keval 30 america dollar par hi phir se bechaa ja saktaa he.
 pratibandhit kshetra se ekatrikaran main kai pranaliyaan hai jinamein sookshm antar visheshkar is baat ko lekar he ki punaravartiya padaarthon ko kahaan alag-alag kar saafe kiya gaya he. mishrit apshisht ke ekatrikaran kii pramukh shreniyan mishrit punaravartaneey tathaa strot se hi pruthakkaran he. apshisht ko ekatrit kar dhone vaali gaadi aamtaur par kooda-karkat uthaati he.
 is pratibimb ke ek chor par mishrit apshisht kaa ekatrikaran he, jisamen sabhi punaravartaneey padaarthon ko baaqi bache duusare kooda-karkat ke saath hi mishrit rup main hi ekatrit kiya jaataa he or tab baanchit padaarthon ke ek kendriya chhaantane vaale suvidhajanak sthaan par alag-alag kar saafe kar liya jaataa he. iske falswaroop ek badi maatra main punaravartaneey apshisht, khaskar kagaz itane adhik sad jate hai ki phir se sansaadhit ho hi nahin sakate, batch jate hai, lekin iske phaayde bhi hai; shahar ko punaravartanee padaarthon ko alag se ekatrit karne ke liye na to kuch bhugtan hi karnaa padataa he, nahin iske liye aam logon ko kuch sikhaane kii zarurat hi padati he. jo padaarth punaravartaneey hai unamem koi bhi parivartan lagu karnaa aasaan he kyonki chhaantane kii prakriya ek nirdhaarit kendriya sthal par hi hoti he.
 ek mishrit athwa ekal-dhara vaali pranali main, ekatrit kiye jane vaale sabhi punaravartaneey padaarthon ko ek saath mila diya jaataa he lekin duusare apshisht se alag hi rakhaa jaataa he. isse ekatrikaran ke uparant pariskrit kiye jane kii aavashyaktaa kaaphi kam to ho jaati he kintu aam logon ko yah sikhaane kii zarurat pad jaati he ki koun-koun se padaarth punaravartaneey he.
 duusari padyati strot se alag-alag kar dene kii he, jisamen ekatrikaran se purv hi prayek padaarth ko alag-alag kar saafe kar liya jaataa he. is padyati main ekatrikaran ke pashchaat sabase kam chhaantan kii zarurat padati he tathaa uttam punaravartaneey padaarthon kii utpatti hoti he, lekin prayek alag-alag chhante gaye padaarth ke ekatrikaran ke liye atirikt parichaalan laagat aati he. isamen vishad rup se logon ko prashikshit karne kii pariyojna kii bhi aavashyaktaa hoti he jiskaa safal hona avashya hi anivaary he agar punaravartaneey padaarth main milawat se bachanaa he to.
 strot se hi chhaantan kii padyati ko sarvaadhik praathamikta pradaan kii gai he kyonki mishrit ekatrikaran main alag karne main kaaphi laagat aati he. chhaantane or alag-alag karne kii takanic main vikaas ke kaaran, haalanki inamen kaaphi ullekhneey giravat aa gai he-kai kshetron main jahaan strot se algaav kii pariklpana viksit kii ja chuki he, mishrit ekatrikaran kii or mud gaye hai.
 ek baar jab mishrit paravartaneey padaarth ekatrit kar liye jate hai or kendriya ekatrikaran suvidha ko supurd kar die jate hai tab vibhinn prakaar ke padaarthon ko avashya hi alag-alag chhaant lenaa chahie. yah kai staron kii shrinkhalaa main kii jaati he, jisamen kai swachaalit prakriyaayein bhi shaamil hai jaise ki padaarth se bhari ladi trek kii ek ghante se bhi is samay main chantai kar lee ja sakati he. kuch sanyatra aise hai jo padaarthon ko swachaalit tareeke se alag-alag chhaant sakate hai, jinhen ekal-padyati kaa punaravartan kaha ja saktaa he. punaravartan dar main 30 pratishat kii vruddhi un kshetron main dekhi gai he jahaan ye sanyatra maujuud hai.
 prarambh main punaravartaneey padaarth ekatrikaran vaale vaahanon se alag kar liye jate hai or unhen vaahak patto par ek str main failaa kar rakh diya jaataa he. gatte ke bade-bade tukade or plastic ke theliyon ko haath se is charan main alag kar liya jaataa he kyonki ve baad main chalakar mashin ko jam kar sakate hai.
 baad main, swachalit mashin paravartaneey padaarthon ko vajan ke aadhaar par alag-alag kar detii he, jisamen baari vajani kaanch or dhaatu se halke vajan-vaale kagaz tathaa plastic alag kar liye jate hai. gatton ko mili-jule kaagajon se alag kar liya jaataa he or sabase adhik saamaanya prakaar ke plastic, PET or HDPE, ekatrit kiye jate hai. yah chantai aamtaur par, haath se hi kii jaati he, lekin kuch chantai kendron main ab yah swachaalit ho gai he: aatmsaat tarangadairya ke aadhaar par specteroscopy scinar yantra ke jariye vibhinn prakaar ke kagaz or plastic alag-alag kar liye jate hai or saath hi saath prayek padaarth ko tadnusaar alag-alag dishaao main ekatrikaran ke sahi channelon main bhej diya jaataa he.
 lohamay dhaatuon ko alag karne ke liye shaktishaalee chumbakon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he, jaisaa ki loha, isyat evam tin-ke pattar lage ispaat ke paatr . gaiyr lauhamay dhaatuon ko chumbakeey chakravaateey pravahon ke bal se baahar nikaal liya jaataa he jisase aluminium ke paatro ke charon or ghurni chumbakeey kshetra vaidhuthik tarang utprerit karte hai, jo badale main, patron ke bheetar chumbakeey chakrawathiya tarang paidaa karte hai. ye chumbakeey chakrawathiya tarang ek bade chumbakeey kshetra ke dwaara vikarshith ho jate hai or paatr baaki bache paravartiya pravaah se rikt ho jaataa he.
 ant main, kaanch ko bhi haath se hi bhura, kahuuruvaa, haraa athwa svachh paardarshee rangon main alag-alag chhaant diya jaataa he.
 paravartan kya aarthik drushti se prabhavi he yaa nahin, is bare main kuch virthak hai. nagarpalikaayen punaravartan kii pariyojna ko lagu karne main aksar rajkoshiya laabh dekhati hai, vyapak rup se jameen kii bharaai main kram laagat aane ke kaaran. technical uniwarsity of denmark ke dwaara kiye gaye ek adhyayan se pataa chalaa ki 83% maamalon main gharelu kooday-kacharon ko nipataane ke liye punaravartan hi sabase adhik prabhavi padyati he. haalanki, danish inviranmental assesament institute dwaara varsh 2004 main kiye akalan ke aadhaar par yah nishkarsh niklaa ki jalaakar bhasmeekaran kar dena hi peya paatro, yahaan tak ki aluminium tak ko nipta kar nast kar dene kaa sabase kargar tarika he.

 iske atirikt, kuch puraane akhbaar kabhi bhi punaravartan ke plant main nahin bheje jate, kyonki vibhinn prakaar ke gharelu or audyogic anuprayogon main athwa keval jameen kii bharaai ke hi kaam aakar samaapt ho jate hai. punaravartan kii dar ek desh se duusare desh main tathaa, deshon ke bheetar bhi, ek shahar se gramin kshetron main saath hi saath ek shahar se duusare shahar main bhi ghatati badhati rahati he. da american forest and paper association kaa anumaan he ki 72% se adhik akhabaari kagaz jo 2006 main uttari america main utpaadit hua use phir se upyog main laane ke liye yaa niryaat ke liye uddhaar kiya gaya jiskaa 58% punah prayog ke liye kagaz kii mil athwa paper board ke kaarkaane main bhej diya gaya, 16% kaa upyog lugdi kii milon main kiya gaya or baaki bachi maatra ko samudri raste se duusare deshon ko bhej diya gaya. uttari america kii kagaz milon athwa paper board kii milon dwaara punavywahht kaa pratishat AFPA ke anumaan se lagbhag ek tritiyansh akhabaari kagaz ke manufacchyar ke liye waapas jaataa he.punaravartan kii dar bhi samyaanusaar puraane akhabaaron ke liye bazaar dwaara die gaye mulya jo kaaphi asthir ho sakate hai, badalati rahati he. udhaaharan ke liye, haal-philhal ke varshon main china ke vibhinn prakaar ke kagaz or packaging ke utpaadan ke rup main ubharne ke saath-saath ullekhneey maatra main punaravartit fibre ko sanyukt rajya athwa anya kahin se bhi aaayaatit kar - puraane akhabaari kaagazon kii isaki maang ek samay kaaphi upar uth gai jisane vishvabhar main punaravartit fibre kii keemton par kaaphi asar daalaa. haalanki punarnavinikaran vaale fibre kii oonchi keemten jameen kii bharaai kii maatra ko kam karne ke lakshya kii disha main acchhee khabar ke rup main hai, ve punarnavinikaran kiye gaye fibre kaa upyog karne vaale kaagaj-milon ke munaafe ko prabhavit kar sakate hai.
 akhabaari kagaz ke milon dwaara laagat ke alaave fibre ke chayan main ek mahatvapurn soch-vichaar fibre ke chayan main akhbaar chhapane kii aadhunik mashiinon tathaa aadhunik samachar patr ke mudran press kii ucch gati ko lekar kiya jaataa he. sanyukt rajya main akhbaar chhapane kii aisi bhi mashine hai, jo 1400 meter prati minute kii gati tak pahunchakar parichaalit hoti hai, udyog samooh kii jaankaree vaali sanstha, RISI ink. ke anusaar, sansar kii naveenatam mashiinon main 1800 meter pratiminut kii gati kii oonchai bhi chhu sakati he. aadhunik akhbaar ke press prati ghante 90,000 pratiyaan chhap sakane kii gati tak parichaalit ho sakate hai, jabaki kuch to 1,00,000 pra.pra.dha kii gati tak bhi pahunch jaati hai.
 aisi oonchi gati-sampannataa ko sahane ke liye kagaz falak kii manufaccuring kii prakriya ke dauraan kagaz kii mashin par tathaa mudran ke dauraan press par donon hi sthitiyon main kshamta kii maang karata he. vishvabhar main ek badi sankhya main akhabaari kagaz kii milen 100% punaravartaneey fibre kaa istemaal karte hue vanijyik taur par sweekaarya gunvattta vaale akhabaari kagaz kaa utpaadan karti hai. haalanki, aisi milon ke oparator ko apshisht kii shuddhta ke bare main kafichaynatmak hona chahie, yah sunishchit karte hue ki ve kam-se-kam nunatam milawaton ko kaam main laga rahe hai tathaa jitani sambhav ho utane lambey akhabaari reshon ko shaamil kar rahe hai. vishuddh akhabaari kagaz lambey fibre vaale pedo jaise ki dawaadaaru, mokha or chid se banate hai, jabaki kuch kagaz or paper board ke utpaada chote fibre vaali sakht lakadiyon se utpaadit hote hai. akhabaari kagaz chhapane kii milen puraane akhabaaron, athwa puraane akhabaaron or puraani patrikaon ke mishran ko pasand karte hai na ki duusare grad ke kagaz kaa punaravartan ko. jaisaa ki sanyukt rajya america kii nagarpalikaon ne haal hi main "ekal stream" ke punaravartan kii ore kadam badhaayaa hai - anek prakaar apshisht utpaadon ko ek vaahan ke ek dabbe main ekatrit karte hue - milon ko baadhya kar diya gaya he ki ve svachh, uchit apshisht kii dhara ko hi lugdi banane ke uddeshyon kii prapti ke liye adhik raashi kharch karen.
 san 1996 main da neuyork times ke ek lekh main, jon tyrni ne yah daawa kiya ki sarkaar ne yah janadesh jari kiya ki punaravartan main bachaane kii tulana main sansadhano kaa kharch adhik hota he. lekh ke vishisht ansh is prakaar hai:
 tyrni ke lekh ko paryaavaran suraksha kosh se sanderbhit samalochna kii salaah mili, jisamen yah darshaya gaya ki "yah lekh evam chintansheel logon or salaahkaaron ke ek varg se praapt uddharanon evam soochnaaon par puri tarah nirbharsheel he jo paravartan ke bare shashakt vaicharik aapatti rakhte hai. san 2003 main, california ke santa clurita shahar $28 america dollar pratiton kachare se jameen kii bharaai ke liye bharpai kar rahaa tha. tab is shahar ne punaravartan kaarykram ke liye ek anivaary dyapar ke istemaal ko apanaaya jisase pratiton $1800 america dollar kii laagat ai. duk universiti ke raajnneeti vigyaan ke pradhan, maaikel munger ne san 2007 main apane ek lekh main likhaa "...naye saamaanon ke upyog karne kii tulana main agar punaravartan adhik kharchila he to, yah kisi bhi prakaar kargar ho hi nahin saktaa, koi bhi saaman sansaadhan he... yaa keval kachra hi he ise nirdhaarit karne kaa ek saadhaaran-sa pareekshan he... kisi saaman ke liye agar aapako koi bhugtan karata he, to yah sansaadhan he.. lekin agar koi saaman aapke paas se le jane ke liye aapako kisi ko bhugtan karnaa padataa he, to samaj lijie vah saamaan kachra he." cato institute main prakritik sansaadhan adhyayan ke nideshak, jeri tailor, ne da hartland institute ke liye san 2002 ke ek lekh main likhaa, "agar bazaar main nai utpaadit plastic ko vitarit karne ke liye laagat X aati he, udhaaharan ke liye, lekin phir se istemaal ki ja chuki plastic ko bazaar main vitarit karne ke liye 10X laagat aegi, isse ham yah niskarsh nikaal sakate hai ki vyavahht plastic ke punaravartan ke liye jin sansadhano kii zarurat hoti he ve toote-phute tukdon se plastic banane ke jaruri sansadhano kii tulana main das guna zyada durlabh hai or kyonki punaravartan ko sansadhano ke sangrakshan ke rup main maan liya gaya he, isliye un paristhitiyon punaravartan ke liye janadesh jari karnaa acha karne se adhik nuksaandeh ho saktaa he." san 2002 main, WNYC ne apni ek report main ullekh kiya ki neuyork shahar ke nivasee punaravartan ke liye kachare kaa 40% jo chhaant kar alag karte hai vah dar asal jameen kii bharaai main hi samaapt ho jaataa he.
 aisi kai alag-alag tarah kii saamagreeyan hai jo punaravartit ho sakati hai lekin har prakaar ke liye alag-alag takanic kii aavashyaktaa he.
 vidhwans ko dhaal dene vaale ilaakon se ekatrit kul thos samagriyon ko chur-chur kar pisane vaali mashin se guzaaraa jaataa he, jisamen aksar aisphult, eent, gandagi ke gubaar evam chattaane shaamil hoti hai. nae nirmaan kii pariyojnaon ke liye bajri ke rup main kancreet ke chote-chote tukdon kaa istemaal hota he. pise hue punaravartit kancreet kaa bhi istemaal ekadam se nai kancreet ke saath sookhaa sookhaa milakar bhi kiya ja saktaa he agar yah milavati se mukt he to. yah duusare chattaanon ke khod kar nikaale jane kii zarurat ko kam karata he, jisase badale main ped-paudhon or paryaavaran kii raksha hoti he.
 badariyon ke aakaar or prakaar main vyapak bhinnata unke punaravartan ko behad mushkil bana detii he: sabase pehle unhen ek prakaar main chhaant kar avashya alag-alag kar lenaa chahie or har prakaar ke liye punaravartan kii alag prakriya kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. iske atirikt, puraani badariyon main paara or cadmium hote hai, jo hanikaarak padaarth hai jinaki dekhbhaal rakrakhaav sambhaal kar kii jaani chahie. paryaavarniya kshati karne kii unki kshamta ke kaaran, kai anchalon main istemaal kii ja chuki badariyon ke uchit niptaan ke liye kanoonee niyamon kii aavashyaktaa he. durbhagyavash, is janadesh ko lagu kiya janaa kaaphi dushkar ho gaya he.
 sheesha-amla vaali batariyaan, jaise ki automobile main istemaal main aane vaali badariyon kii tarah, punaravartan karne main apekshaakrit aasaan hai evam kai anchalon main vikritaon ko istemaal main aa chuke utpaadon ko sweekaar karne ke maamle main kanoon kii zarurat hogi. sanyukt rajya america main, 80% tak punaravartit saamagree dhaaran karne vaali nai badariyon ki punaravartan dar 90% he.
 rasoighar, bagichaa, evam anya hare apshishton ko sadaakar upyogi khaad main punaravartit kiya ja saktaa he. yah prakriya prakritik vayujeevi bacteria ko apshisht vikhandit hone detii he jo mitti ki urvar ke upari sataha kaa nirmaan karti he. sadaakar khaad banane kaa kaam adhiktar gharelu paimaane par hi hota he, lekin iske saath hi saath nagarpalika dwaara bhi hare apshisht ke ekatrikaran kaa kaarykram bhi maujuud he. utpaadit upari urvar mitti ko bechakar in kaaryakramon ke poorak ke liye nidhiyan ho saktaa he.
 preshan athwa gamagaman ke jariye vastron kaa punaravartan kaaphi teji se lokapriy ho rahaa he. vastron ke gamagaman main, logon kaa ek samooh ek niyat sthaan par ekatrit, hokar aapas main vastron kaa aadan-pradaan karte hai. clothing swap inkaa jaisi sansthaaon main laavaaris kapadon ko sthaneey daan karne ke liye daan swaroop de diya jaataa he.
 bijli ke upkarnon ke pratyaksh niptaan-jaise ki computer or mobile phone- kuch andruni ghatakon ke vishakt hone ke kaaran - kai anchalon main pratibandhit kar die gaye hai. punaravartan kii prakriya dhaatuon, plastic tathaa circuit bords jo un upkarnon main nihit hai unhen swatah yantravat alag karti he. jab yah ek apshisht punaravartan sanyantr main bade paimaane par kar liya jaataa he ghatak ko uddhaar karne kaa kaary laagat-prabhavi tareeke se sampadit kiya ja saktaa he.
 loha or ispaat sansar ke sarvaadhik punaravartit padaarth hai, evam punaravartan kiye jane vaale saamaanon main sabase aasaan, kyoki chumbak kaa istemaal kar unhen apshisht ke dher se alag kiya ja saktaa he. ispaat ke kaarkaane se hokar punaravartan kii prakriya guzarati he: vaidhuthik bhatti main ko yaa to phir se galaa diya jaataa he athwa basic oxigen kii bhatti main kaa istemaal charge ke ek ansh ke rup main kar liya jaataa he. kisi bhi darje ke ispaat ucch koti ke naye dhaatu, main punaravartit kiya ja saktaa he, gunvattta ke lihaaj se oonche dar ne se neeche darje main jisaki padavnati nahin hoti he kyonki ispaat kaa baar-baar punaravartit kiya ja saktaa he. utpaadit kachche ispaat kaa 42% punaravartit padaarth hi to he.
 aluminium vyapak rup se punaravartit padaarthon main se sarvaadhik sakasham he. aluminium ko kasakar dabaakar chote-chote tukdon main pis liya jaataa he, athwa gaathon ke rup main kuchal diya jaataa he. in tukdon yaa gaathon ko pighale aluminium kaa utpaadan karne ke liye aluminium pighalaane vaali ek bhatti pighalaaya jaataa he. is charan tak punaravartit aluminium kii vishuddh aluminium kii alag se pahchaan nahin ho paati he or donon ke liye hi aage chalakar sansaadhan ek samaan hi hota he. is prakriya se dhaatu main koi parivartan nahin paidaa hota he; atah aluminium anischitkaal tak ke liye punaravartit ho saktaa he.
 nae aluminium ke sansaadhan karne kii tulana main aluminium ke punaravartan se 95% urja ke laagat kii bachat hoti he. yah keval is kaaran ki punaravartit lagbhag shuddh, aluminium ko pighalaane main 600 °C tapamatra kii aavashyaktaa hoti he, jabaki aluminium ke ayask se khanij aluminium ke nishkarshan ke liye 900 °C tapakram kii zarurat padati he. itane oonche taapmaana tak pahunchane ke liye adhik se adhik urja kii aavashyaktaa hoti he jisase aluminium ke punaravartan se oonche paryaavarniya laabh kii ore pahunchaa jaataa he. america prativarsh prachur pariman aluminium phenk dete hai taaki puure vanijyik hawaii bade kaa punanirmaan kar saken. iske alaava ek aluminium ke paatr ke punaravartan se bachaai gai urja ek television-set ko teen ghante tak chalaane ke liye kaaphi he.
 nukkadon par rakhe koodaydaanon se kaanch kii bottlen or martabaan ikatthe kar sangrah karne vaale trek tathaa bottle-bainkon main ekatrit kar liye jate hai jahaan kaanch ko rangon ke vargeekaran ke anusaar chhaant kar alag-alag kar liya jaataa he. toote-phute kaanch ke tukdon ko kaanch ke punaravartan sanyantr main le jaaya jaataa he jahaan isaki shuddhta par najar rakhi jaati he or milawat kii gandagiyon ko hataa diya jaataa he. kaanch ke tukdon ko kuchal diya jaataa he or kachche maal ke ek mishran ke saath milakar pighalaane vaali matti main daal diya jaataa he. tab ise yantric tareeke se hawa se phulaakar athwa pighla kar nae aakaar ke joro yaa botalon main gadha jaataa he. kaanch ke toote-phute tukdon kaa bhi istemaal samgr or glassfalt ke rup main nirmaan udyog main kiya jaataa he. glassfalt sadak par bichaaya jane vaala ek padaarth he jisamen punaravartit kaanch kaa 30% shaamil he. kaanch kaa punaravartan anischitkaal tak kiya ja saktaa he jaisaa ki jab iskaa punsansaadhan kiya jaataa he isaki sanrachana nast hokar vikrut nahin hoti.
 rang aksar sarkaar parichaalit gharelu khatarnaak apshisht kii suvidhaon se ikattha kiya jaataa he. vahaan se, ise paint punarchakran sanyantron main le jaaya jaataa he, jahaan gunvattta ke aadhaar par alag kiya jaataa he. rang ke upyog jinhen punah sansaadhit nahin kiya ja saktaa or phir se bechaa nahin ja saktaa punarchakran dwaara isaki bhinnataayen nirdhaarit hoti hai.
 kaagaj kaa punaravartan ise lugdi banaakar tathaa nai kataai vaali lakdi kii lugdi ke saath milakar kiya ja saktaa hai. jaisaa ki punaravartan kii prakriya kagaz ke reshon ko todatii jaati he, atah jitani baar kagaz kaa punaravartan hota ahi, isaki gunvattta main kami aati jaati he. iskaa matlab yah he ki yaa to oonche pratishat main nae reshon ko zarur joda janaa chahie yaa kaagaj ko nichi gunvattta vaale utpaadon main punaravartit kar dena chahie. koi lekhan yaa kaagaj par rang sarvapratham deinking ke dwaara hataa diya janaa chahie, jo philrs, mitti, tathaa reshon ke tukdon ko bhi hataa detaa he.
 aaj to lagbhag sabhi kaagaj punaravartit kiye ja sakate hai, kintu aise bhi prakaar ke kagaz he jinhen dusron kii tulana main punaravartit karnaa kathin he. kagaz jo plastic coted hote hai yaa aluminium foyal, tathaa aise kagaz jinpar mom kaa lepan hota he, pasted yaa gond lage hote hai unka aamtaur par punaravartan nahin hota he kyonki yah prakriya kaaphi mahangi he. uphaar-samagriyon ko lapetane vaale kagaz bhi apni karaab gunvattta ke kaaran punaravartit nahin ho sakate.
 kabhi kabhi punaravartak akhbaaron se chamkile aaveshan ko hataa dene ko kehete hai kyonki ve ek alag hi prakaar ke kagaz hai. chamkile aaveshanon par mitti kii ek baari parat hoti he jise kuch kagaz kii milen sweekaar nahin karti hai. punaravartit kagaz kii lugdise adhikansh mitti ko hataa diya jaataa he jiskaa zarur hi niptaan kar diya keechad ke rrope main kiya janaa chahie. agar parat chadhe kagaz mitti ke vajan ke kaaran 20% adhik he to chamakdaar kaagaj ke prati ton se 200 kilogram se bhi adhik keechad or 800 kilogram se bhi kam fibre paidaa hoga. 
 plastic kaa punaravartan scrap athwa apshisht plasticon koubaarane tathaa padaarth ko upyogi utpaadon main punarsansaadhit karne kii prakriya he. kaanch athwa dhaatu ke saamaanon se tulana kii jaay to plastic ko apurv chunautiyon kaa saamana karnaa padataa he. plastic ke prakaaron kii vishaal sankhya hone ke kaaran, prayek prakaar ke paas rezin kaa pahchaan code hota he or punaravartan se purv unhen avashya hi chhaant kar alag lenaa chahie. ho saktaa he yah mahangaa ho, kyonki vaidyuthik chumbakon kaa upyog kar dhaatuon ko jab alag-alag kiya ja saktaa he aisi koi 'aasaan chantai' vaali kshamta-sampann upaay yaa saadhan plastic ke liye uplabdh nahin he. iske atirikt, punaravartan ke liye botalon se labalon ko hataa di jane kii zarurat nahin hoti lekin dhakkan aksar alag tarah ke apravartaneey plastic se bane hote hai.
 vibhinn prakaar ke plastic ke saamaanon main padaarthon kii alag-alag pahchaan kar paane ke sahaytarth, chah aam prakaar ke punaravartaneey plastic rezins kii pahchaan ke liye 1 se 6 tak ke rezins aaidentification code numbers aavantit kiye gaye hai. prayek rezin code ko sammilit karte hue manakikrut pratik uplabdh hai.
 kapade yaa vastra ke punaravartan kaa khyaal aate hi yah avashya samaj lenaa chahie ki yah kis padaarth se bana hai. adhiktar kapade rui evam synthetic plastic ke sangyojan hai. kapade kii sanrachana iske ticoupan or punaravartan kii paddati ko prabhavit karegi.
 shramik ekachitr kapadon ko chhaantate or acchhee quality ke vastron tathaa juuton ko jinka dobaaraa istemaal ho saktaa yaa jo pahane ja sakate hai, unhen alag karte hai. aisi suvidhaon ko viksit deshon se vikasshil deshon tak yaa to daan kii khatir yaa sasti keemton par bech die jane ke liye muhaiyaa karne kii pravriti he. kai antarashtriya sanghathan istemaal kiye ja chuke vastron ko daan-swaroop ikaitha karte hai or unhen teesri duniya ke deshon ko daan main de dete he. punaravartan kii is aadat ko protsaahit kiya jaataa he kyonki yah avaanchit ko kam karne main madad detii he saath hi saath zarurat mandon ko vastra muhaiyaa karaati he.nast ho gaye vastron ko aage chalakar gradon main chhaant liya jaataa he jinase audyogic jhaadan-ponchhan ke kapade banaae jate hai tathaa kagaz ke vinirmaan main yaa fibre uddhaar ke liye upyogi saamagree main tathaa bharaai ke utpaadon ke upyog main laate hai. agar textile ke punarsansadhak sanyantr jile athwa sade-gale kapade pate hai, haalanki inhen phir bhi jameen kii bharaai main nipta diya jaataa he, kyonki chhaantan ikaayiyon main dhone or sukhaane kii suvidhaayen maujuud nahin hai.
 reshon ke punaruddhar kii milen, fibre ke prakaar or rang ke aadhaar par vastron ko chhaantati hai. rang kii chantai punaravartit vastron ko phir se rangane kii aavashyaktaa ko kam karti he. vastron ko "ghatiya" fibre main kasakar baandh diya jaataa he or duusare chaynit fibron ke saath ekasaath mila diya jaataa he punaravartit soote ke antim upyog ke iraade par nirbhar hokar. ekasaath mila hua yah mishran ghunakar saafe kar diya jaataa he tathaa bunai silaai ke liye katkar taiyaar kar diya jaataa. in fibron sankuchit kar gadde kaa utpaadan bhi kiya jaataa he. in kapadon ko gaddon main bharne vaale udyogon ke liye kasakar dabaakar bhej diya jaataa he jo gadiyon ke insulation main bharne vaali saamagree ke rup main chat ke felts, laudspikar ke cone, panelle kii lining tathaa furniture kii pading ke kaam main laayaa jaataa he.
 paryaavaran kii drushti se upyogi utpaada hone kii apni chhavi ke kaaran lakdi kaa punaravartan lokapriy ho gaya he, upabhoktaaon ke is amavishvaas ke saath ki punaravartit lakdi kii kharid se hari lakdi kii maang main kami aegi or antatogatva paryaavaran labhanvit hoga. greenpis bhi punaravartit lakdi ko paryaavaran ke liye mithravat upyogi utpaada kii drushti se dekhtaa he, unki apni websit par ise lakdi ke pasandida behatar strot ke rup main hawala dete hue. nirmaan ke utpaada ke utpaada ke rup main punaravartit lakdi kaa aagaman ubharate hue udyog tathaa jangal kii kataai evam lakdi milon ko badhaava dene tathaa paryaavaran ke prati mithravat sahyog ke abhyaas ko apnaane kii disha main mahatvapurn hai.
 lakdi kaa punaravartan ek aisa vishay he jisane haal ke varshon main hamaare jivan main sarvaadhik mahatvapurn bhoomika apana lee he.haalanki, samasya yah he ki, yadhyapi kai sthaneey adhikari punaravartan ke vichaar ko pasand to karte hin, lekin ve puri tarah iskaa samarthan nahin karte hai. anginat udaahranon main se ek jo samaacharon kii surivaryon main bana rahaa vah sachmuch lakdi ke punaravartan kii avadharana he jo shaharon main badhati ja rahi he. udhaaharan ke liye lakdi ke punaravartan, pedo evam anya sroton ke naam liye ja sakate hai.
