 fateh khaan, sultan firozshah tululaq kaa sabase bada putr tha.
 firozshah tululaq kii mrityu se purv hi fateh khaan kii mrityu ho gai thi.uskaa bada ladkaa gayasuddin tululaq dwitiya apane baba sultan firozshah tululaq kii mrityu ke pashchaat dilli kii gaddi par baitha.gaddi par baithne ke kuch hi maheenon use apdasth karke uski hatya kar di gai.fatehkhaan kaa doosraa ladkaa nasarat shah tululaq, tululaq vansh kaa upantim sultan tha.nasarat shah tululaq ne 1395 se 1399 i. tak shashan kiya.
