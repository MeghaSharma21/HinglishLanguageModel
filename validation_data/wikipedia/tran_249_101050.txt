 mizoram vishvavidhyalay bharat kaa ek kendriya vishvavidhyalay he. vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana 2 july 2001 ko sansadeey adhinium ke tahat kii gai jise 25 april 2000 ko adhishuchet kiya gaya. vishvavidhyalay ke antargat 7 sankaay evam 30 vibhaag hai. 27 mahavidhyalay bhi iske antargat angeebhoot kiye gayi hai.
