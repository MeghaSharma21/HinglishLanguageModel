 sanganak vigyaan main bakkas-naar praaroop formal bhashaaon ke cintax ko nirupit karne kii paddati he. ise jon bakcus evam peter naur ne viksit kiya tha. dhyatavya he ki yah praaroop panini ke vyakaran nirupan kii paddati se bahut kuch mail khaataa he. panini ke ashtaadhi main 'rikrshan', transformation, 'metaruls' evam anya prakaar kii aadhunik evam unnat formal takneekon kaa prayog hua he.
