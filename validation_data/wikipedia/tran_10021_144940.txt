 kedarnath mandir bharat ke uttrakhand rajya ke rudraprayag jile main sthit he. uttrakhand main himalay parvat kii god main kedarnath mandir baarah jyotirling main sammilit hone ke saath chaar dham or panch kedaar main se bhi ek he. yahan kii pratikul jalwaayu ke kaaran yah mandir april se november maah ke madhya hi darshan ke liye khulata he. pattharon se bane katyuri style se bane is mandir ke bare main kaha jaataa he ki iskaa nirmaan pandav vansh ke janamejay ne karaayaa tha. yahan sthit swayambhu shivlinga ati pracheen he. aadi shankaracharya ne is mandir kaa jirnoddhar karavaaya.
 june 2013 ke dauraan bharat ke uttrakhand or himachal pradesh rajyon main achaanak ai baadh or bhuskhalan ke kaaran kedarnath sabase adhik prabhavit kshetra rahaa. mandir kii deevaaren gir gai or baadh main bah gayi. is aitihaasik mandir kaa mukhya hissaa or sadiyon purana gumbad surakshit rahe lekin mandir kaa pravesh dwaar or uske aas-paas kaa ilaaka puri tarah tabah ho gaya.
 kedarnath kii badi mahima he. uttrakhand main badrinaath or kedarnath-ye do pradhan tirth hai, dono ke darshanon kaa bada hi mahatmaya he. kedarnath ke sambandh main likhaa he ki jo vyakti kedarnath ke darshan kiye bina badrinaath kii yatra karata he, uski yatra nishphal jaati he or kedarnaapath sahit nar-narayan-murti ke darshan kaa fal samast paapon ke naash purvak jivan mukti kii prapti batalaaya gaya he.
 is mandir kii aayu ke bare main koi aitihaasik pramaan nahin he, par ek hajaar varshon se kedarnath ek mahatvapurn tirthayatra rahaa he. rahul sankrutyaayan ke anusaar ye 12-13veen shatabdi kaa he. gwalior se mili ek raja bhoj stuti ke anusaar unka banavaay hua he jo 1076-99 kaal ke the. ek manyatanusaar vartmaan mandir 8veen shatabdi main aadi shankaracharya dwaara banwaaya gaya jo pandavon dwaara dwapar kaal main banaaye gayi pehle ke mandir kii bagal main he. mandir ke bade dhoosar rang kii sidhiyon par paali yaa brahmi lipi main kuch khuda he, jise spasht jaananaa mushkil he. phir bhi itihaaskaar dow shiv prasad dabraal maanate he ki shaiva log aadi shankaracharya se pehle se hi kedarnath jate rahe hai. 1882 ke itihaas ke anusaar saaf agrabhag ke saath mandir ek bhavy bhawan tha jiske donon ore pujan mudra main murtiyan hai. “peeche bhure pathar se nirmit ek tower he iske garbhagrih kii ataari par sone kaa mulammaa chadha he. mandir ke saamne tirthayatriyon ke awaas ke liye pandon ke pakke makaan he. jabaki puujaari yaa purohit bhawan ke dakshini ore rahete hai. shri trail ke anusaar vartmaan dhaancha haal hi nirmit he jabaki mul bhawan girakar nasht ho gayi.”kedarnath mandir rudraprayag jile main he uttarkashi jile main nahi
 yah mandir ek chah feet unche chaukor chabutare par bana hua he. mandir main mukhya bhaag mandap or garbhagrih ke chaaron ore pradakshina path he. baahar praangan main nandi bail vaahan ke rup main virajman hai. mandir kaa nirmaan kisne karaayaa, iskaa koi pramanik ullekh nahin miltaa he, lekin haan aisa bhi kaha jaataa he ki isaki sthapana aadi guru shankaracharya ne kii thi.
 mandir kii puja shri kedarnath dwadash jyotirligon main se ek mana jaataa he. praat:kaal main shiv-pind ko prakritik rup se snaan karakar us par ghi-lepan kiya jaataa he. tatpashchaat dhoop-deep jalaakar aarti utaari jaati he. is samay yatri-gana mandir main pravesh kar pujan kar sakate hai, lekin sandhya ke samay bhagwaan kaa shringaar kiya jaataa he. unhen vividh prakaar ke chittkarshak dhang se sajaayaa jaataa he. bhaktagan dur se keval iskaa darshan hi kar sakate hai. kedarnath ke pujari mysore ke jangam brahman hi hote hai.
 chauribaari himand ke kunda se nikalati mandakini nadi ke sameep, kedarnath parvat shikhar ke paad main, katyuri style dwaara nirmit, vishwa prasiddh kedarnath mandir kii oonchaai par avasthit he. ise 1000 varsh se bhi purv kaa nirmit mana jaataa he. janashruti he ki iskaa nirmaan pandavon yaa unke vanshaj janmejay dwaara karavaaya gaya tha. saath hi yah bhi prachlit he ki mandir kaa jirnoddhar jagadguru aadi shankaracharya ne karavaaya tha. mandir ke prishthbhag main shankaracharya ji kii samadhi he. rahul sankrutyaayan dwaara is mandir kaa nirmaankaal 10veen va 12veen shatabdi ke madhya bataaya gaya he. yah mandir vaastukala kaa adbhut va aakarshak namoona he. mandir ke garbh grih main nukili chattan bhagwaan shiv ke sadashiv rup main puji jaati he. kedarnath mandir ke kapaat mesh sankranti se pandrah din purv khulte hai or aghan sankranti ke nikat balraj kii raatri chaaron pahar kii puja or bhaiya dooj ke din, praatah chaar baje, shri kedaar ko ghrit camel va vastradi kii samadhi ke saath hi, kapaat band ho jate hai. kedarnath ke nikat hi gandhi sarovar va vasukital hai. kedarnath pahunchne ke liye, rudraprayag se guptkashi hokar, 20 kilometre aage gaurikund tak, motormarg se or 14 kilometre kii yatra, madhyam va teevr dhaal se hokar guzaranevaale, paidal maarg dwaara kerani padati he.
 mandir mandakini ke ghat par bana hua hai bheetar ghaare andhkaar rahata he or deepak ke sahare hi shankar ji ke darshan hote hai. shivlinga swayambhu he. sammukh kii ore yatri jal-pushpadi chadhaate hai or duusari ore bhagwaan ko ghrit arpit kar baanh bharkar milte hai, murti chaar haath lambi or dedh haath moti he. mandir ke jagmohan main draupadi sahit paanch pandavon kii vishaal murtiyan hai. mandir ke peeche kai kund hai, jinamein aachman tathaa tarpan kiya ja saktaa he.
 bhagwaan kii pujaaon ke kram main praat:kaalik puja, mahabhishek puja, abhishek, laghu rudrabhishek, shodashopchaar pujan, ashtopchaar pujan, sampoorn aarti, pandav puja, ganesh puja, shri bhairav puja, parvati ji kii puja, shiv sahastranam aadi pramukh hai. mandir-samiti dwaara kedarnath mandir main puja karaane hetu janata se jo dakshina liya jaataa he, usamen samiti samay-samay par parirvatan bhi karti he.
