 maanas rashtriya udyaan yaa maanas vanyajeev abhayaarany, assam, bharat main sthit ek rashtriya udyaan hai. yah abhayaarany unesco dwaara ghoshit ek prakritik vishwa dharohar sthal, baagh ke arakshit pariyojna, haathiyon ke arakshit kshetra ek arakshit jeevamandal hai. himalay kii talahati main sthit yah abhayaarany bhutan ke royal maanas neshnal park ke nikat he. yah park apane durlabh or luptpraaya sthanik vanyajeev ke liye janaa jaataa he jaise assam chat vaale kachhue, hepid khargosh, golden langur or paigi hogg. maanas junglee bhainson kii aabaadi ke liye prasiddh he. yahan ek sing kaa ganda or barasingha ke liye vishesh rup se paye jate he. yah bhutan kii tarai main bodo kshetreey parishad kii dekharekh main 950 varg kilometre se bhi bade ilaake main failaa hua he, jiske antargat 1973 main project tiger ke tahat sthapit 840.04 varg kilometre kaa ilaaka maanas vyaghr sangrakshit kshetra bhi aata he. 
 ise 1985 main vishwa dharohar sthal kaa darja diya gaya tha lekin assi ke dashak ke ant or nabbe ke dashak ke shuru main bodo vidrohi gatividhiyon ke kaaran is udyaan ko 1992 main vishwa dharohar sthal suchi se hataa liya gaya tha. june 2011 se yah punah unesco kii vishwa dharohar main shaamil kar liya gaya he.
 is park kaa naam maanas nadi par rakhaa gaya he, jise sarpon kii devi mansa ke naam par rakhaa gaya he. maanas nadi brahmaputra nadi kii ek pramukh sahaayak nadi he, jo is rashtriya udyaan ke mukhya centre se hokar guzarati he.
 1 oktober 1928 ko 360 kimi2 kshetra ke saath maanas neshnal park ko abhayaarany ghoshit kiya gaya tha. maanas baio reserve kii sthaphna 1973 main banaaya gaya tha. abhayaarany kii ghoshnaa se pehle yah ek arakshit van tha jise maanas aar.epa. or uttar kamrup aar.epa. ke naam se janaa jaataa tha iskaa prayog kooch bihar kaa shahi parivaar or gauripur ke raja shikaar ke liye arakshit rup main karte the. 1951 or 1955 main kshetra ko badhaakar 391 kimi2 kar diya gaya. yah unesco dwaara disambar 1985 main ek vishwa dharohar sthal ghoshit kiya gaya tha. 
 rashtriya udyaan ke mukhya bhaag main agrganj ek jangalo se ghira hua gaanv he. is gaanv ke alaava 56 se adhik gaanv park ko chaaron ore se ghere hue hai. 
 is park ko teen shreniyon main vibhajit kiya gaya he. isaki pashchimi seemaa panbaari, kendriya seemaa barpeta road ke paas bansabari main or pathasla ke nikat bhuiyapur main purvi seemaa sthit hai. ye shreniyan aapas main acchhee tarah se judi nahin hai.
 bhautik bhugol: maanas purvi himalay kii talahati main sthit he or ghaney jangalon se aachhadit he. maanas nadi park ke pashchim chor se bahati he or yah is abhayaarany kii mukhya nadi he. yah brahmaputra nadi kii sabase badi sahaayak nadi he or baad main do alag-alag nadiyon, beki or bhuladuba main bibhakt ho jaati he. maanas nadi bharat or bhutan ko vibhajit karne vaali ek antarrashtriya seemaa ke rup main bhi kaary karti he.
 maanas main do pramukh jaev vaataavaran maujuud hai:
 vanaspati: maanas ke monsoon ke jangalon main brahmaputra ghati ke ardh-sadabahaar jangal ke jaev kshetra main padate hai.  yah duniya ke sabase amir jaev vividhta vaale kshetron main se ek he.
 is abhayaarany main standhariyon kii 55 prajatiyaan, pakshiyon kii 380 prajatiyaan, sarisrupon kii 50 or ubhaychar kii 3 prajatiyaan pai jaati hai. in vanyajeevon main 21 standhari bharat ke shedul ek stanpayi hai or inamen se 31 khatre main bataai jaati hai.
 abhayaarany ke anya praaniyon main bhartiya hathi, bhartiya gande, gaura, asian jal bhens, barasinga, bhartiya baagh, bhartiya tenduon, asian swarna billi, dhol, topi vaale langur, swarna langur, sambar hiran or chital ityadi hai.
