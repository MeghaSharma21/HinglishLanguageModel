 vir chandra singh gadhwali ko bhartiya itihaas main peshawar cond ke nayak ke rup main yaad kiya jaataa he. 23 april 1930 ko hawaldaar major chandra singh gadhwali ke netritv main royal gadhwal rifles ke javaanon ne bharat kii aazaadee ke liye ladane vaale nihathe pathaanon par goli chalaane se manaa kar diya tha. bina goli chale, bina bam fate peshawar main itnaa bada dhamaakaa ho gaya ki ekaek angrej bhi hakke-bakke rah gayi, unhen apane pairon tale jameen khisakati hui si mahasoos hone lagi.
 chandra singh gadhwali kaa janm 25 disambar 1891 main hua tha. chandrasingh ke purvaj chouhan vansh ke the jo muradabad main rahete the par kaaphi samay pehle hi vah gadhwal kii rajdhani chandpurgarh main aakar bas gayi the or yahan ke thokadaaron kii seva karne lage the. chandra singh ke pita kaa naam jalouth singh bhandari tha. or vah ek anpadh kisaan the. isi kaaran chandra singh ko bhi wo shikshit nahin kar sake par chandra singh ne apni mehnat se hi padhanaa likhnaa sikh liya tha.
 3 sitambar 1914 ko chandra singh sena main bhartee hone ke liye lansdon pahunche or sena main bhartee ho gayi. yah pratham vishwayuddh kaa samay tha. 1 agast 1915 main chandrasingh ko anya gadhwali sainikon ke saath angrejon dwaara france bhej diya gaya. jahaan se ve 1 farvari 1916 ko waapas lansdon aa gayi. pratham vishwa yuddh ke dauraan hi 1917 main chandrasingh ne angrejon kii ore se mesopotamia ke yuddh main bhaag liya. jisamen angrejon kii jeet hui thi. 1918 main baghdad kii ladaai main bhi hissaa liya.
 pratham vishwa yuddh samaapt ho jane ke baad angrejo dwaara kai sainikon ko nikaalnaa shuru kar diya or jinhen yuddh ke samay tarakkee di gayi thi unke padon ko bhi kam kar diya gaya. isamen chandrasingh bhi the. inhen bhi hawaldaar se sainik bana diya gaya tha. jis kaaran inhone sena ko chhodane kaa man bana liya. par ucch adhikariyon dwaara inhen samajhaayaa gaya ki inaki tarakkee kaa khyal rakhaa jaayegaa or inhen kuch samay kaa avkaas bhi de diya. isi dauraan chandrasingh mahatma gandhi ke sampark main aaye.
 kuch samay pashchaat inhen inaki batallian samet 1920 main bajiristaan bheja gaya. jiske baad inaki punah tarakkee ho gayi. vahaan se waapas aane ke baad inkaa zyada samay arya samaaj ke kaaryakartaaon ke saath beetaa. or inke andar swadesh prem kaa jazbaa paidaa ho gaya. par angrejon ko yah raas nahin aayaa or unhone inhen khaybar darre ke paas bhej diya. is samay tak chandrasingh major hawaldaar ke pad ko paa chuke the.
 us samay peshawar main swatantraaa sangram kii lau puure jorshor ke saath jali hui thi. or angrej ise kuchalane kii puri koshish kar rahe the. isi kaam ke liye 23 april 1930 ko inhen peshawar bhej diya gaya. or hukum diye kii andolnarat janata par hamalaa kar den. par inhone nihatthi janata par goli chalaane se saaf manaa kar diya. isi ne peshawar cond main gadhwali batalian ko ek unchaa darja dilaayaa or isi ke baad se chandra singh ko chandrasingh gadhwali kaa naam mila or inako peshawar cond kaa nayak mana jane laga.
 angrejon kii ajna na maanane ke kaaran in sainikon par mukadmaa chalaa. gadhwali sainikon kii pairvi mukundi laal dwaara kii gayi jinhone athak prayaason ke baad inke mrityudand kii sajaa ko kaid kii sajaa main badal diya. is dauraan chandrasingh gadhwali kii sari sampatti zapt kar lee gai or inaki vardi ko inke sharir se kaat-kaat kar alag kar diya gaya.
 1930 main chandrasingh gadhwali ko 14 saal ke karawas ke liye abtabad kii jail main bhej diya gaya. jiske baad inhen alag-alag jelon main sthaanantarit kiya jaataa rahaa. par inaki sazaa kam ho gai or 11 saal ke karawas ke baad inhen 26 sitambar 1941 ko aaazaad kar diya. parantu inke main pravesh pratibandhit rahaa. jis kaaran inhen yahan-vahaan bhatakate rahanaa padaa or ant main ye wardha gandhi ji ke paas chale gayi. gandhi ji inke behad prabhavit rahe. 8 agast 1942 ke bharat chhodon andolan main inhone elahabaad main rahakar is andolan main sakriy bhagidaari nibhaai or phir se 3 teen saal ke liye giraftaar hue. 1945 main inhen aaazaad kar diya gaya.
 22 disambar 1946 main communiston ke sahyog ke kaaran chandrasingh phir se gadhwal main pravesh kar sake. 1957 main inhone communist ke ummidavaar ke rup main chunaav ladaa par usamen inhen safalataa nahin mili. 1 oktober 1979 ko chandrasingh gadhwali kaa lambi bimaari ke baad dehant ho gaya. 1994 main bharat sarkaar dwaara unke sammaan main ek dak tikat bhi jari kiya gaya. tathaa kai sadakon ke naam bhi inke naam par rakhe gayi.
