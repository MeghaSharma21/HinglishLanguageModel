 1960 main morocco ne pehali baar olympic khelon main bhaag liya, or 1980 ke greeshmakaleen olympic ke america netritv main bahishkaar main bhaag lene ke alaava, tab se har greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main pratispardhaa karne ke liye athleton ko bheja he. morocco ne 1976 ke khelon kaa bahishkaar bhi kiya, jisamen shuru main ek pratinidhimandal bheja gaya tha. aisa karte hue, morocco, dakshin africa kii ek rangabhed team ke khilaaf olympic ke liye koi ol blike rugby match ke baad newjiland kii bhagidaari ke virodh main zyaadaatar africa deshon dwaara khel ke bahishkaar main shaamil ho gaya. keval ek morocco ke pratinidhi ke paas apane desh kii vaapasi se pehle pratispardhaa karne kaa samay tha: abdaram nazeem ne mukkebaaji main purushon kii lite flavevett pratiyogita main hissaa liya, or apana pehla or ekmaatr match ganvaa diya.
 morocco ne 1968 ke baad se chah avasaron par sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main bhaag liya he.
 morocco ke athleton ne kul 22 padak, athletics main unniis or mukkebaaji main chaar jite hai. do suvarna padak or ek rajat padak ke saath hicham el guruj, or ek sone or ek rajat ke saath sad auta, morocco ke do padak vijeta hai.
 1959 main morocco ke liye rashtriya olympic samiti banaai gai thi.
