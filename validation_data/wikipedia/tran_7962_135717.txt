 vanar hindu gaatha ramayana main varnit manavnuma kapiyon kii ek jati thi jiske sadsy saahas, shakti, buddhi or jigyaasa ke gun rakhte the.
 vanar shabd do shabdon se milakar bana he - 1. van 2. nar.
 iskaa shabdic arth hota he - "van main rahane vaala nar". yani jo praani van main rahete hai unhen vanar kehete hai. isi tathy ke aadhaar par mahatma ramachandra vir ne "hanuman ji kii jati vanar bataayi he or isi aadhaar par rajasthan ke viratnagar main nar swarup main hanuman ji kii murti vajrang mandir me sthapit kii he. yah mandir puure bharatvarsh me hanuman ji ke anya mandiron se alag he.
 "vanar" shabd kaa arth kabhi-kabhi sidhaa "bandar" bhi nikaala jaataa he.
