 abhishek - rajtilak kaa snaan jo rajarohan ko vaid karata tha. puraane kaal main jab kisi ko raja banaaya jaataa tha to us ke sir par abhimantrit jal or aushadhiyon kii varsha kii jaati thi. is kriya ko hi abhishek kehete he. abhi upsarg or sinch dhaatu ki sandhi se abhishek shabd bana he. kalantar main rajbhishek rajtilak kaa paryaay ban gaya.
 atharvaveda main abhishek shabd kai sthalon par aayaa he or iskaa sanskaragat vivaran bhi vahaan uplabdh he. krishna yajurveda tathaa shrout sutron main ham praaia: sarvatra "abhishechneey" sangya kaa prayog pate hai jo vastut: rajasuya kaa hi ang tha, yadhyapi aitarey brahman ko yah mat sambhavat: sweekaar nahin. uske anusaar abhishek hi pradhan vishay he.
 aitarey brahman ne abhishek ke do prakaar batalaaye hai:
  punarabhishek ;
  andra mahabhishek .
 inamen se pratham kaa rajasuya se sambandh jaan padataa he, na ki yauvarajya athwa singhasanagrahan se. andra mahabhishek avashya indra ke rajbhishek se sambandhit he. ukt brahman granth main aise samraaton kii suchi bhi di hui he jinka abhishek vaidic niyam se hua tha. ye hai:
  janmejay parikshit, tur kaavshey dwaara abhishikt,
  sharyaat maanav, chyavan bhargav dwaara abhishikt,
  shatanik saatrajit, som shashman vaajaratnayan dwaara abhishikt,
  ambashthay, parvat or naarad dwaara abhishikt,
  yudhashrushthi ougrasainya, parvat or naarad dwaara abhishikt,
  vishwakarma chyavan, kashyap dwaara abhishikt,
  sudas packvan, vasishtha dwaara abhishikt,
  marutt avichhitt, sanwart angiras dwaara abhishikt,
  ang udmay aatreya,
  bharat daushyant, dirghatamas yayatey.
 nimnaankit raja keval saskaar ke gyaan se vijayi hue:
  durmukh paanchal, brihasukth se gyaan paakar,
  atyarati janantpi vasishtha sathhavy se gyaan paakar.
 in soochiyon ke atirikt kuch anya suchiyaan prasiddh paashchaatya tatvajna goldstukar ne di hai. aage chalakar mahabharat main yudhisthir ke do baar abhishikt hone kaa ullekh miltaa he, ek sabhaparv or doosraa shantiparv, 100,40 main.
 maury samrat ashok ke sambandh main ham yah jaanate hai ki use yauvarajya ke pashchaat chaar varsh abhishek kii pratiksha kerani padi thi or isi prakaar harsh shiladitya ko bhi, jaisaa "mahavansh" evam yuvan chwang ke "si-euki" naamak granthon se gyaat hota he. kalidas ne bhi raghuvansh ke dwitiya sarg main abhishek kaa nirdesh kiya he. aitihaasik vrittanton se gyaat hota he ki aage chalakar rajaschivon ke bhi abhishek hone lage the. harshcharit main "murdhabhishikta amatya rajaan:" is prakaar kaa sanket paaya jaataa he. aage chalakar anek aitihaasik samraaton ne praaia: vaidic vidhaan kaa aashray lekar abhishek kriya sampadit kii, kyonki uske bina samrat nahin mana jaataa tha. abhishek ke katipay anya saamaanya prayogon main pratimapratishtha ke avasar par uskaa aadhaan ek saadhaaran prakriya thi jo aajkal bhi hinduon main bharat evam nepal main prachlit he.
 ek vishisht arth main abhishek kaa prayog buddha "mahavastu" main hua he jahaan sadhana kii parinti das bhoomiyon main antim "abhishek bhoomi" main batalaai gai he.
 vaidic evam uttar vaidic sahitya main abhishek kaa jo vidhaan diya gaya he vah nimnlikhit he. praaia: abhishek ke samay, uske kuch pehle, athwa uske bich main sachivon kii niyukti hoti thi or isi prakaar anya rajratnon kaa nirvachan bhi sampann hota tha jinamein samraji, hasti, shwetvaji, shwetvrushabh mukhya the. upkarnon main shwetantra, shwetchaamar, asana, singhaasana, bhadrapeeth, parmasana, swarnavirachit evam ajinavruth tathaa mangalik dravyon main swarnapaatr, madhu, dugdh, dadhi, udumbardand evam anya vastuen rakhi jaati thim. bhartiya abhishekavidhaan main jis ucch koti ke mangalik dravya or upkaran prayukt hote the vaise pracheen isaayiyon athwa sami rajarohan kii kriyaaon main nahin hote the. is prasang main yah ullekhneey he ki abhishek ek siddhant prakriya ke rup main keval isi desh kii sthaayee sampatti he, anya deshon main is prakaar ke siddhant itane aspasht or uljhe hue hai ki unka nishchayatmak siddhant-swaroop nahin ban paaya he; yadhyapi shaktisadhana or aishwarya kii kaamana rakhanevaale sabhi samraaton ne kisi na kisi rup main snaan, vilepan ko pratik kaa rup dekar is sanskaar kaa aashray liya he.
