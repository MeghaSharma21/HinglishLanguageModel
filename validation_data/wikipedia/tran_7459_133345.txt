 lakshmi narayan treepathi ek tivi kalakaar, bharatnatyam nartika, or saamaajik kaaryakarta hai. vah ek hijdaa, hai jo hijdaa samaaj ke hit ke liye kaary karata hai. lakshmi bigabos sijan 5 kii pratibhaagi bhi rah chuki hai. tivi sho "sach kaa saamana", "das kaa dam" or "raaj pichley janam kaa" main bhi dekhi ja chuki hai.
