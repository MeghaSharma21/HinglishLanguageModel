 lathina pracheen roman samrajya or pracheen roman dharm kii rajbhasha thi. aaj ye ek mrit bhashaa he, lekin phir bhi roman catholic carch kii dharmabhasha or vatican city shahar kii rajbhasha he. ye ek shastreey bhashaa he, sanskrit kii hi tarah, jisase ye bahut zyaada mail khaati he. lathina hind-europea bhashaa-parivaar kii romance shakha main aati he. isi se francisi, italvi, spanish, romanian or portugali bhashaaon kaa udgam hua he . europe main isai dharm ke prabhutv kii vajah se lathina madhyayugin or purv-aadhunik kaalon main lagbhag saare europe kii antarrashtriya bhashaa thi, jisamen samast dharm, vigyaan, ucch sahitya, darshan or ganit kii kitaaben likhi jaati thim.
 latin shabd kii utpatti latium jile ke naam se hui. yah vah jila tha jahaan romulas ne 1000 i.pus. main rome nagar kii neenv rakhi thi.
 lathina ki lipi roman lipi he. asal main roman lipi lathina ke liye hi bani he, yani ki lathina kii apni lipi he. isaliye iskaa harek akshar lagbhag hameshaa ek hi uccharan detaa he . ati-pracheen roman lipi ye thi 
 swar ke upar samtal rekha kaa arth hota tha ki swar deergh he, par ise likhnaa zaruri nahin mana jaataa tha. baad main unani bhashaa ke udhaar ke shabd laane ke liye unani lipi se ye akshar liye gayi : K, Y ), Z . vyanjan ji ke liye V prayukt kiya jane laga or swar u ke liye U. iske bhi kuch baad J or W jude. chote aksharon ke rup madhyayug main aaye. pashchim or madhya europe kii sari bhashaaon ne likhaawat ke liye roman lipi apana lee.
 lathina kii adhikansh shabdaawalee sanskrit se milti he, kyonki dono bahin bhashaayen hai or ek hi mul aadim-hind-europea bhashaa se nikali hai. yahi sambandh iskaa unani bhashaa se bhi he, lekin pracheen unani bhashaa se isane kai shabd sidhe udhaar liye the. baad main angrezi ne laathini se baari maatra main shabd udhaar liye.
