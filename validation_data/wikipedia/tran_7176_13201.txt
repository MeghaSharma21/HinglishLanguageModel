 2016 greeshmakaleen olympic, aadhikarik taur par XXXI olympiad khel, vishwa kii sarvocch pramukh anterrashtriya bahu-khel pratiyogita kaa 31vaan sanskaran he jiskaa aayojan brazil ke rio di genero shahar main hua tha.is main america pehle paaydaan par rahaa tha.
 2016 ke greeshmakaleen olympic kaarykram main 306 ghatnaon ke 28 khel shaamil the. prayek anushaasan main ghatnaon kii sankhya koshthkon main dekha jaataa he
 sabhi 206 rashtriya olympic samitiyon ne kam se kam ek athlete ke liye yogyata praapt kii he. khel ke liye athleton ke yogya hone vaale pehle teen deshon main germany, great britain or netherland the, jo 2014 epaiai world equastrian games main team ke aayojan main padak jitkar team ke dressshoe ke liye chaar athleton main se prayek yogya the.
 mejabaan raashtra ke rup main, brazil ko kuch khelon ke liye swat: pravesh mila he jisamen sabhi cycle chaalan vishyon or vetalifting kaaryakramon ke liye chah sthanon shaamil hai. 2016 greeshmakaleen olympic pehla khel he jisamen kosovo or dakshin sudaan bhaag lene ke liye paatr hai. kai anti doping ulalanghanon ke liye balgerian or roosi bharottolak ko rio olympic se pratibandhit kar diya gaya tha.
 kuwait ko oktober 2015 main desh kii olympic samiti main sarkari hastkshep se paanch saal main duusari baar pratibandh laga diya gaya tha.
 yah vartmaan main usee din jari kii gai shedul par aadhaarit he jab tikat kii bikri shuru hui, 31 march 2015.
 sheersh das soochibaddh enosi gold medal kii sankhya ke anusaar neeche soochibaddh hai mejabaan raashtra brazil kul milakar 19 padak ke saath 13 wein sthaan par rahe.
 raashtra dwaara is talika ko krambaddh karne ke liye, kul padak ginati, yaa koi anya stambh, kollam sheershak ke bagal main sort karen iken par clic karen.
