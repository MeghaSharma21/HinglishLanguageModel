 england kaa bank england kaa kendriya bank he. anshadhariyon ke bank ke rup main isaki sthapana parliment ke ek vishisht kanoon dwaara san 1844 main hui thi. san 1946 main sarkaar ne ek kanoon dwaara iskaa rashtriyakaran kar diya.
 bank ke prabandhsanchalan ke liye ek prabandhkarini samiti he jise 'court' kehete hai. court main ek garvanar, ek dipti governor tathaa 16 sanchaalak hote hai. in sabaki niyukti england kii maharani dwaara kii jaati he. governor tathaa dipti governor kii kaarividhi paanch varsh or sanchaalkon kii karyavadhi chaar varsh hoti he par inhen pun: niyukt bhi kiya ja saktaa he. 'court' kii baithak prati saptaah saamanyat: guuruuvaar ko honi anivaary he or tabhi bank dar kii ghoshnaa kii jaati he.
 aantarik vyavastha ke liye bank kaa kaary anek vibhaagon main vibhakt he. prayek vibhaag kii vyavastha vibhaagadhyaksh ke atirikt prabandh sanchaalkon tathaa governor or dipti governor ke adheen hoti he. bank ke lagbhag 7,000 karmchaaree uski dainik kaarywahi sanbhaalate hai. nirikshan evam kynvin ke hetu bank main kai sthaayee samitiyaan hai jinamein se prayek ko bank kii kriyaaon kaa nitinirdhaaran sambandhi bhaar sanbhaalanaa padataa he. tregeri samiti sabase adhik mahatvapurn sthaayee samiti he jisamen governor, dipti governor tathaa 'court' dwaara nirvaachit paanch sanchaalak sadsy hote hai. bank kii kendriya sambandhi niti kaa nirdhaaran tregeri samiti kii sweekruti dwaara hi hota he.
 desh kaa kendriya bank hone ke kaaran, bank ov england sarkaar kaa banker, agent tathaa paramarshdaata he. sarkari kosh isi bank main jamaa rahata tathaa saarvajanik rin kii vyavastha bhi isi bank ke adheen he. desh main note jari karne kaa ekadhikaar bhi isi bank ko praapt he. bank ov england desh main 'bainkon ke bank' ke rup main bhi kaam karata he. desh ke anya bank apane apane lekhe bank ov england main kholte tathaa unamem nirdhaarit raashi jamaa karte hai jisase kendriya bank ko desh main pratyay niyantran kaa ek saadhan mil jaataa he or vah samay par in bainkon kii sahaayta bhi kar saktaa he. isi prakaar desh ke katautee grih, jo landan mudramandi kii apni visheshta he, isi bank main apane apane lekhe kholkar raashi jamaa rakhte or aavashyaktaanusaar rin lete hai. in katautee grihon ke liye bank va england 'antim rindaata' kaa kaam karata he. desh kii mudramandi ke saath sarkaar kaa sampark bank ov england ke madhyam dwaara hi bana rahata he. maudrik evam saakh sambandhi koi bhi sarkari niti evam nirnay isi bank ke madhyam dwaara desh ke bainkon tak pahunchataa he.
 anya deshon ke saath england kii sarkaar ke maudrik sambandhon ke sandarbh main bhi bank ov england kuch mahatvapurn yoga detaa he, jaise, vinimay samkaari lekhe kaa sanchaalan videshi vinimay kii vyavastha, sterling kshetreey tathaa anya deshon ke kendriya bainkon ke saath sampark rakhana tathaa antarrashtriya maudrik sansthaaon main england kaa pratinidhitiv karnaa. bank ov england apane desh kii maudrik pranali kaa nirmaataa, prabandhak evam sangrakshak he.
