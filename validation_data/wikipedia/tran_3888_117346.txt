 is pratiyogita pravesh pariksha kaa lokapriy naam e ai tripal i bhi he. iskaa aayojan central board of secondari ajuketion dwaara kiya jaataa he jo medical kalejon main pravesh ke liye e ai pi em tii kii pariksha bhi leti he.
 is pariksha main vastunishth prashn puche jate hai jo bahu-vikalpiya hote hai. is pariksha ke merit ke aadhaar par sabhi rashtriya takneekee santhaan kii snaatak paathyakram ke vibhinn shakhaon main pravesh diya jaataa he. is ke atirikt nimnlikhit anya takneekee sansthaaon ke snaatak stareey paathyakramon main prvesh bhi isi pariksha ke merit ke aadhaar par diya jaataa he:
 DA-IICT, DCE, NSIT, IIITs, SPA dilli tathaa birla praudyogiki santhaan, mesra
