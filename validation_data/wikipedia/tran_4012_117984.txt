 moksh
 dhyaan yoga 
 
 hindu dharm ke anusaar jahaan sati devi ke sharir ke ang gire, vahaan vahaan shakti peeth ban gayi. ye atyany paavan tirth kahalaaye. ye tirth puure bhartiya upmahadweep par faile hue hai.
 puraanon ke anusaar sati ke shav ke vibhinn angon se baawan shaktipeethon kaa nirmaan hua tha. iske peeche yah antarktha he ki daksh prajapati ne kankhal main 'brihaspati sarv' naamak yajna rachaaya. us yajna main brahma, vishnu, indra or anya devi-devataaon ko aamantrit kiya gaya, lekin jaan-boojhkar apane jamaata bhagwaan shankar ko nahin bulaayaa. shankaraji kii patni or daksh kii putree sati pita dwaara na bulaae jane par or shankaraji ke rokane par bhi yajna main bhaag lene gayi. yajna-sthal par sati ne apane pita daksh se shankar ji ko aamantrit na karne kaa kaaran poocha or pita se ugr virodh prakat kiya. is par daksh prajapati ne bhagwaan shankar ko apshabd kahe. is apmaan se peedit hui sati ne yajna-agni kunda main kudakar apni pranahuti de di. bhagwaan shankar ko jab is durghatana kaa pataa chalaa to krodh se unka teesraa netra khul gaya. bhagwaan shankar ke aadesh par unke ganon ke ugr coup se bhaybheet saare devtaa rishigan yajyasthal se bhaag gayi. bhagwaan shankar ne yajyakund se sati ke parthiv sharir ko nikaal kandhe par uthaa liya or duhkhi hue idhar-udhar ghoomane lage. tadnantar sampoorn vishwa ko pralay se bachaane ke liye jagat ke paalankartta bhagwaan vishnu ne chakra se sati ke sharir ko kaat diya. tadnantar ve tukade 52 jagahon par gire. ve 52 sthaan shaktipeeth kehalaae. sati ne duusare janm main himalayaputree parvati ke rup main shankar ji se vivaah kiya.

 puraana granthon, tantra sahitya evam tantra chudamani main jin baawan shaktipeethon kaa varnan miltaa he, ve nimnaankit hai. nimnlikhit suchi 'tantra chudamani' main varnit ikyavan shakti peethon kii he. baavanavaan shaktipeeth anya granthon ke aadhaar par he. in baawan shaktipeethon ke atirikt anekanek mandir desh-videsh main vidyaman hai. himachal-pradesh main nayana devi kaa peeth bhi vikhyath he. gupha main pratimaa sthit he. kaha jaataa he ki yah bhi shaktipeeth he or sati kaa ek nayan yahan gira tha. isi prakaar uttarakhand ke paryaton sthal musuri ke paas surkanda]] devi kaa mandir he. yah bhi shaktipeeth he. kaha jaataa he ki yahan par sati kaa sir dhad se alag hokar gira tha. mata sati ke ang bhoomi par girane kaa kaaran bhagwaan shri vishnu dwaara sudarshan chakra se sati mata ke samastang vichhedit karnaa tha.
 shaktipeethon kii sankhya ikyavan kahi gai he. ye bhartiya upmahadweep main vistrut hai. yahaan puri shaktipeethon kii suchi di gai he.
 in ikyavan shaktipeethon kaa vivaran 
 1. kirit shaktipeeth :kirit shaktipeeth, pashchim bengal ke hughali nadi ke tat lalbaag kot par sthit he. yahaan sati mata kaa kirit yani shirabhushan yaa mukut gira tha. yahaan kii shakti vimala athwa bhubaneshwari tathaa bhairav sanwart hai.
 2. katyayani shaktipithavrindavan, mathura ke bhooteshwar main sthit he katyayani vrindavan shaktipeeth jahaan sati kaa keshpash gira tha. yahaan kii shakti devi katyayani hai tathaa bhairav bhutesh he.
 3. karvir shaktipeethamaharashtra ke kolhapur main sthit he yah shaktipeeth, jahaan mata kaa trinetra gira tha. yahaan kii shakti mahisasuramdini tathaa bhairav krodhshish hai. yahaan mahalakshmi kaa nij nivas mana jaataa he.
 4. shri parvat shaktipithis shaktipeeth ko lekar vidwaanon main mataantar he kuch vidwaanon kaa maananaa he ki is peeth kaa mul sthal ladakh he, jabaki kuch kaa maananaa he ki yah assam ke silahat main he jahaan mata sati kaa dakshin talp yani canapati gira tha. yahaan kii shakti shri sundari evam bhairav sundaranand hai.
 5. vishalakshi shaktipithuttar pradesh, varanasi ke mirghat par sthit he shaktipeeth jahaan mata sati ke daahine kaan ke mani gire the. yahaan kii shakti vishalakshi tathaa bhairav kaal bhairav hai.
 6. godavari tat shaktipithandhrapradesh ke kabbur main godavari tat par sthit he yah shaktipeeth, jahaan mata kaa vamgand yani baayan kapol gira tha. yahaan kii shakti vishweshwari yaa rukmani tathaa bhairav dandpani hai.
 7. shuchindram shaktipithtamilnadu, kanyakumari ke trisagar sangam sthal par sthit he yah shuchi shaktipeeth, jahaan sati ke ufdhrvadant gire the. yahaan kii shakti savitri tathaa bhairav sthanu hai.
 40. yugadya shaktipeeth, kshirgram shaktipithapashchim bengal ke bardaman jile ke kshirgram main sthit he yugadya shaktipeeth, yahaan sati ke daahine charan kaa angootha gira tha. yahaan kii shakti jugadya or bhairav ksheer khandak he.
 41. viraat kaa ambika shaktipithrajasthan ke gulabi nagari jaipur ke vairatagram main sthit he viraat shaktipeeth, jahaan sati ke ‘daayen paanv kii ungliyaan’ giri thim.. yahaan kii shakti ambika tathaa bhairav amrit hai.
 42. kalighat shaktipithapashchim bengal, kolkata ke kalighat main kalimandir ke naam se prasidh yah shaktipeeth, jahaan mata ke daaen paanv kii angootha chhod 4 anya anguliyaan giri thim. yahaan kii shakti kalika tathaa bhairav nakulesh hai.
 43. maanas shaktipeethatibet ke maansarovar tat par sthit he maanas shaktipeeth, jahaan mata kaa dahinaa hatheli kaa nipaat hua tha. yahaan kii shakti kii daakshayani tathaa bhairav amar hai.
 44. lanka shaktipithsrilanka main sthit he lanka shaktipeeth, jahaan mata kaa noopur gira tha. yahaan kii shakti indrakshi tathaa bhairav rakshaseshwar hai. lekin, us sthaan gyaat nahin he ki srilanka ke kis sthaan par gire the.
 45. gandki shaktipithanepal main gandki nadi ke udgam par sthit he gandki shaktipeeth, jahaan sati ke dakshingand gira tha. yahaan shakti `gandki´ tathaa bhairav `chakrapani´ hai.
 46. guhyeshwari shaktipithanepal ke kathmandu main pashupatinath mandir ke paas hi sthit he guhyeshwari shaktipeeth he, jahaan mata sati ke donon janu gire the. yahaan kii shakti `mahamaya´ or bhairav `kapaal´ hai.
 47. hinglaj shaktipithpakistan ke bluechistan praant main sthit he mata hinglaj shaktipeeth, jahaan mata kaa brahmarandhra gira tha. yahaan kii shakti kottary or bhairav bhimlochan he.
 48. sugandh shaktipithbangladesh ke khulanaa main sugandh nadi ke tat par sthit he ugratara devi kaa shaktipeeth, jahaan mata kaa nasika gira tha. yahaan kii devi sunanda he tathaa bhairav trayambak hai.
 49. karatoyaghat shaktipithbangladesh bhavanipur ke begda main karatoya nadi ke tat par sthit he karatoyaghat shaktipeeth, jahaan mata kaa vam talp gira tha. yahaan devi aparnaa rup main tathaa shiv vaaman bhairav rup main vaas karte hai.
 50. chattal shaktipithbangladesh ke chatgaanv main sthit he chattal kaa bhavani shaktipeeth, jahaan mata kaa dahinaa bahu yani bhujaa gira tha. yahaan kii shakti bhavani tathaa bherav chandrasekhar hai.
 51. yashor shaktipithbangladesh ke jaisor khulanaa main sthit he mata kaa yashoreshwari shaktipeeth, jahaan mata kaa baayin hatheli gira tha. yahaan shakti yashoreshwari tathaa bhairav chandra hai.
