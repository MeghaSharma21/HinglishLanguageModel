 kiskindhakand valmiki krit ramayana or goswami tulsidas krit shri ram charit maanas kaa ek bhaag he.
 ram rishyamook parvat ke nikat aa gayi. us parvat par apane mantriyon sahit sugriv rahata tha. sugriv ne, is ashanka main ki kahin baali ne use maarane ke liye un donon veeron ko na bheja ho, hanuman ko ram or lakshmana ke vishay main jaankaree lene ke liye brahman ke rup main bheja. yah jaanane ke baad ki unhen baali ne nahin bheja he hanuman ne ram or sugriv main mitrataa karvaa di. sugriv ne ram ko santvana di ki janaki ji mil jaayengin or unhen khojane main vah sahaayta degaa saath hi apane bhai baali ke apane upar kiye gayi atyaachaar ke vishay main bataaya. ram ne baali kaa vadh kar ke sugriv ko kiskindha kaa rajya tathaa baali ke putr angad ko yuvaraj kaa pad de diya.
 rajya prapti ke baad sugriv vilaas main lipt ho gaya or varsha tathaa sharad ritu vyateet ho gai. ram ke naaraazgi par sugriv ne vaanron ko sita kii khoj ke liye bheja. sita kii khoj main gayi vaanron ko ek gupha main ek tapaswini ke darshan huye. tapaswini ne khoj dal ko yogashakti se samudratat par pahunchaa diya jahaan par unki bhent sampaati se hui. sampaati ne vaanron ko bataaya ki ravan ne sita ko lanka ashokavatika main rakhaa he. jambavant ne hanuman ko samudr langhane ke liye utsahit kiya.
