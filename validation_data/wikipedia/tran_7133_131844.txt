 vedarashi kii suraksha ke liye tathaa mantron kii pracheen parampara ko suvyavasthit banaae rakhane ke uddeshy se pracheen maharishiyon ne prayek vaidic samhita ke vividh vishyon kii krambaddh anukramani banaai he. sanskrit vaanmay ke sutarasahitya ke antargat chah vedangon ke atirikt anukramaniyon kaa bhi samavesh he.
 aisi anukramaniyan anek hai. inamen vaidic samhitaon ke sakal sookt, unamem prayukt pad, prayek mantra ke drashtaa rishi, prayek richa ke chand or devtaa krambaddh rup se anusuchit hai. sankalit vishay ke anusaar inaki pruthak pruthak sangyaayen hai- jaise 
 usee tarah mandalantanukram or devanukram bhi he. is prakaar kisi bhi vaidic mantra kaa rishi, chand yaa devtaa koun he athwa vah mantra kis mandal, anuwak yaa sookt kaa he, yah jaanane ke liye tatsambandhi anukramani kaa avlokan sahaayak hota he.
 vastut: uparokt anukramaniyan kosh kii bhanti vishayanusndhan main sahaayak hi na thim, apitu inkaa lakshya sookt evam anuvaakon ke yathavat swaroop tathaa mantron ke paath ko bhrasht na hone dene kaa apurv saadhan he. phir bhi kisi bhi ek mantra ke sambandh main uske chand, devtaa aadi ke gyaan ke liye anek anukramaniyan dekhni padati thim, kyonki tatsambandhi sakal gyatavya vishay ek jagah uplabdh na tha. 
 is kathinaayee ko dur karne kii drushti se maharishi katyayan ne ek aisi anukramani kii rachana kii jisamen samhita ke antargat samast mantron ke sambandh main sakal gyey vastu kii ekatra uplabdhi ho jaae. isamen prayek mantra kaa chand, devtaa, rishi, mandal, sookt, evam anuwaad kaa vivaran purna rup se ek hi sthaan par diya hua miltaa he. ise unhone sarvanukramani naam diya. katyayan pranit sarvanukramani kii sangya kaa nirvachan kiya he- sarvaggeyarth varnanaat sarvanukramnishbdan nirbuvanti vipaschit:. 
 katyayan ne ek sarvanukramani rugveda kii shakal evam vaashkal samhita kii banaai or duusari shukla yajurveda kii vaajasaneyi samhita kii. katyayan pranit sarvanukramani par "vedarthdipika" naamak ek sundar vyakhya shadgurushishy dwaara rachi gai jo atyant pramanik maani jaati he. vishay vishesh ko lekar shaunak dwaara pranit anya anukramaniyan padhyabaddh hai; katyayan kii donon hi sarvanukramaniyan gadyatmak hai or ve gadhy sutarastyli main nibaddh hai. sarvanukramani ke praneta katyayan vahi the jinhone panini kii ashtaadhi par vaarthik kii rachana kii. panini se parvarti evam mahabhashyakaar patanjali se purvavarti katyayan the. is sambandh main shadgurushishy likhte hai-
 sarvanukramani kaa rachanakaal sutarayug ke antim charan main hi mana ja saktaa he. sutarayug kaa kalnirnay paashchaatya itihaaskaaron ne isapurv 600 se 200 tak kaa sweekaar kiya he.
 rugveda sambandhi sarvanukramani sutara style main rachit ek bada granth he. mudrit rup main iskaa aayam lagbhag 46 prishth kaa he. iske pehle 12 adhyyon main prastavik charchaa he jinamein se 9 adhyyon main vaidic chhandon ke swaroop or rachanapaddati par parichayatmak niabandh he. sarvanukramani ke praneta katyayan ne grantharambh main "yathopadesh main rugveda kii richaon ke pratik aadi kii anukramani prastut karata hoon" aisi pratigya kii he. yathopadesh se yah sanket he ki yah rachana tatpurv shaunakpranit vividh chandobaddh ke aadhaar par kii gai he. kyonki sarvanukramani main katipay gadyansh vruttagandhi hai ore shaunakeey arshanukramani or brihaddevataa main prayukt katipay pad swarupatah parigrihit hai. katyayan pranit rugveda kii sarvanukramani kaa sampadan acharya macdonal ne kiya he jo oxuard se san 1886 isvi main prakashit hui. isamen anuvakanukramani tathaa shadgurushishy kaa bhashya bhi parishisht main mudrit he.
 katyayanpranit shuklayajurvedic sarvanukramani main keval paanch hi adhyay hai. pehle chaar adhyyon main yajush mantron ke drashtaa rishiyon, devataaon or chhandon kii namat: gananaa he. isaki ek or visheshta yah he ki samhitakaal se uttarvarti yug ke nae rishiyon ke bhi naam sangruhit hai jinamein katipay shatapath brahman se sambandh rakhanevaale bhi hai. iske antim adhyay main vaajasaneyi samhita ke mantron kaa sankshipt vivaran bhi diya he. shuklayajurvedic sarvanukramani kaa prakaashan webar dwaara sampadit yajurveda ke sanskaran main parishisht rup se sangruhit he, tathaa swatantra rup se yah granth sabhashya banaras sanskrit series ke antargat isvi san 1893-94 main sarvapratham prakashit hua miltaa he. granth kaa naam "katyayanpranit shukla yaju: sarvanukramasutra-yajnikanantadev krit bhashya sahit" diya he.
