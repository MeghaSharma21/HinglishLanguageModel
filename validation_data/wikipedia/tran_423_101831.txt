 nigamon kii credit reating kaa nirdhaaran karne vaali agenciyon main nimnlikhit sammilit hai: 
 credit reating kaa prayog niveshkon, rin nirgamit karne vaali sansthaaon, nivesh bank, dallalon-vyapaariyon or sarkaar dwaara kiya jaataa he. credit reating sansthaaen niveshkon ke liye nivesh vikalpon ke kshetra ko vistrut kar detii hai or saapeksh rin jokhim ko maapne kaa saral or swatantra tarika detii hai; isse aam taur par bazaar kii kushalta bad jaati he or udhaar lene waalon or udhaar dene waalon donon ke hi liye laagat ghat jaati he. iske parinaam swarup arthvyavastha main jokhim yukt puunji kii kul aapurti bad jaati he, jo shaktishaalee vikaas kii ore le jaati he. yah puunji bazaar ko us shrenee ke udhaar lene waalon ke liye bhi khol detii he jo aisa na hone par kul milakar puunji bazaar se baahar ho jate: jaise, choti sarkarein, haal main shuru hui companiyaan, aspataal or vishvavidhyalay ityadi.
 jarikarta, apni saakh yogyata or apane dwaara jari upkaran ke parinami mulya ke swatantra pramanikaran ke rup main in credit retings par nirbhar karte hai. adhiktar maamalon main, ek khaas band ke jari hone par uske safal hone ke liye uske paas ek pratishthit siaare dwaara kam se kam ek reating kaa hona zaruri he band market association dwaara kiye gaye adhyayan yah kehete hai ki kai sansthagat niveshak ab is baat ko prathimakata dete hai ki kisi bhi rin nirgaman ke liye kam se kam teen reating avashya hon.
 jarikarta, credit retings kaa kuch nishchit niojit vitt sambandhi lenden ke liye bhi prayog karte hai. udhaaharan ke liye, ek bahut hi ucch credit reating vaali company joki ek jokhim purna pariyojna ko shuru karne kii ichha rakhti he, vah kuch sampatti ke saath ek vaidhanik rup se alag sanstha kaa nirmaan kar sakati he jo anusandhan kaary kaa sanchaalan karegi or uske liye uttaradaayi hogi. iske baad yah "vishisht lakshya vaali sanstha" anusandhan kaary ke vitt poshan ke liye usase hone vaale sabhi sambhavit jokhim kaa uttardaayitv apane upar lekar apni swayan kii rin pratibhuti jari karegi. vishisht lakshya vaali sanstha kii reating sambhawatah bahut kam hogi or jarikarta ko jari kiye gaye band par adhik dar se pratilabh kaa bhugtan karnaa hoga. haalanki, is jokhim se mul company kii samgr credit reating kam nahin hogi kyonki vaidhanik rup se espei ek alag sanstha hogi. iske vipareet, nimna credit reating vaali ek company acchhee sharton par udhaar paa sakati he, basharte vah ek espei bana le or apni mahatvapurn sampatiyon ko us adhinsth company ko hastantarit kar ke sangrakshit rin pratibhutiyaan jari kare. is prakaar se, yadi uskaa udhyam asafal bhi ho jaataa he to udhaar dene waalon ke paas espei ke rup main strot uplabdh rahegaa. isse vah byaaj dar bhi kam ho jaaegi jiskaa espei ko rin ke bakaayaa ke rup main bhugtan karnaa hoga.
 ek hi jarikarta ke paas bhinn prakaar ke baandon ke liye bhinn credit reating ho sakati he. yah bhinnata band kii sanrachana ke kaaran hoti he, jaise ki vah kis prakaar sangrakshit he or vah kis seemaa tak anya rin ke adhinsth he. kai badi siaare sansthaaen "credit reating adwiseri services" kii suvidha detii hai, joki ek jarikarta ko aavashyak rup se is bare main salaah detii he ki vah apane band ke prastaav kii ruparekha kisi rakhe or espei sansthaaon ko yah salaah detii hai ki vah kis prakaar rin ke ek nishchit ansh ke liye aavashyak credit reating ko praapt karen. swaabhaavik rup se isse sambhavit hit sangharsh chaalu ho jaataa he kyonki yadi jarikarta band kii ruparekha ke sambandh main siaare kii salaah ko maanane ko taiyaar ho jaataa he to vah jarikarta ko aavashyak reating dene ke liye baadhya hoga. kuch siaare un rin prastaavon ko reating dene se inkaar karke is sangharsh se batch jaati hai, jinke liye unase salaah kii suvidha bhi lee gayi ho.
 niyantrankarta, niamak uddeshyon ke liye credit reating ke saath-saath permit reating kaa bhi prayog karte hai. udahaaran ke liye, banking niyantrankarta, jab unhen apni shuddh arakshit puunji kii gananaa kerani hoti he tab banking paryvekshan ke sambandh main besel cameti ke besel II samjhauton ke tahat bainkon ko kuch nishchit manyata praapt siaare, yaa "eksternal credit reating institutions"kehete hai) kii credit reating ke prayog kii anumati de saktaa he. sanyukt rajya main, da securities and exange kamishan aise hi uddeshyon ke liye investment bainkon or dalal-vyapaariyon ko "neshanali recognised statistical reating argenisations" kii credit reating kaa prayog karne kii anumati detaa he. iske peeche vichaar yah he ki yadi vittiya santhaan kaa adhiktam nivesh atyadhik nakadi or atyant "surakshit" pratibhutiyon ke rup main he to bank or anya vittiya sansthaanon ko bank ke adhikaran ke virodh main apane sansthaanon kii raksha karne ke liye utani hi arakshit puunji rakhane kii aavashyaktaa na pade.
 siaare kii reating kaa prayog anya vyavsaayik uddeshyon ke liye bhi hota he. udahaaran ke liye, yues esaisi, kuch nishchit band jarikartaaon ko band ke jarikaran ke dauraan ek laghukrit vivaran prapatr ke prayog kii anumati detaa he, basharte jarikarta purana ho, usne pehle bhi band jari kiye hon or yadi uski credit reating ek nishchit seemaa ke upar ho. esaisi ke niyamon ke anusaar yah bhi aavashyak he ki puunji bazaar kii nidhi insurance se rahit hote hai) main maatr vahi pratibhutiyaan hon jinaki enaresaro reating bahut ucch ho. isi prakaar, beemaa niyantrak beemaa companiyon dwaara rakhi gayi arakshit nidhi kii kaarysadhakata ke nirdhaaran ke liye credit reating kaa prayog karte hai.
 besel II or esaisi donon ke hi niyamon ke antargat, niamak uddeshyon ke liye kisi bhi siaare kii reating kaa prayog nahin kiya ja saktaa. balki, is sambandh main kai prakaar kii jaanch prakriyaayein astitv main hai. udahaaran ke liye, besel II nirdesh, nishchit maapdandon kaa ullekh karata hai, jin par bank niyantrakon ko kisi vishesh siaare dwaara pradutt reating ke prayog ko anumati dete samay dhyaan dena chahie. iname "nishpakshta," "swatantraaa," "paardarshita" or anya shaamil hai. tab hi se kai adhikaar kshetron ke banking niyantrak is vishay par apane swayan ke charchaa patr jari karne lage hai jiske dwaara ve is tathy ko or spasht rup se samajha rahe hai ki vyavhaar main in sharton kaa prayog kis prakaar hoga. 
 sanyukt rajya main, 1975 se enaresaro kii manyata esaisi karmchaaree dwaara bheje gaye ek "no ection latter" ke dwaara sweekaar kii jaati he. is paddati kaa anusaran karte hue, yadi ek siaare  niamak uddeshyon ke liye kisi vishesh siaare kii reating kaa prayog karne kaa icchhuk he to, esaisi ke karmchaaree yah tay karne ke liye bazaar kaa shodh karenge ki us siaare kii reating kaa prayog vyapak str par hota he yaa nahin or uski reating "vishwasaneey va pramanik" he yaa nahin. yadi esaisi ke karmchaaree yah tay karte hai ki aisa he, to ve siaare ko ek patr bhejenge jisame yah sanket diya jaayegaa ki yadi koi niyantrak sanstha siaare kii reating par vishwaas karne vaali he to esaisi karmchaaree us sanstha ke viruddh kisi bhi jabaran kriya kii anumati nahin degaa. ye "no ection" patr saarvajanik kiye ja sakate hai or vah sanstha jisane vastav main iske liye anurodh kiya tha, uske atirikt anya niamak sansthaaen bhi in par vishwaas kar sakati hai. esaisi ne is moolyankan ke dauraan apane dwaara prayog kiye gaye maapdandon ko or spasht rup se bataane kaa nishchay kar liya tha or march 2005 main usne isi ke liye e praposd regulation kaa prakaashan bhi karavaaya.
 29 sitambar 2006 ko, america ke raashtrapati jorge dablyoo. bush ne "credit reating riform act 2006" ke kaanoon par hastakshar bhi kiye. is kanoon ke anusaar yues security and exange kamishan yah spasht karne ke liye baadhya he ki enaresaro kii manyata kis prakaar sweekaar kii jaati he, yah "no ection letters" ko radd kar detaa he or enaresaro kii manyata ko kamishan kaa nirnay bana detaa he karmchaaree dwaara liye gaye nirnay ke) or enaresaro ko baadhya karata he ki vah esaisi dwaara panjikrut tathaa uske dwaara niyantrit bhi ho. es and pi ne is aadhaar par is adhinium kaa virodh kiya ki yah vaani kii swatantraaa ke kaanoon kaa ek avaidhanik ulanghan he. 2007 kii garmiyon main, esaisi ne adhinium ke karyanvan ke liye niyam jari kiye, jiske anusaar reating agenciyaan gaiyr-saarvajanik soochnaaon ke durupyog ko rokane kii nitiyaan banane, phaayde ke sangharsh ke prakatikaran or "galat aacharan" ke viruddh pratibandhon ke liye baadhya hai.
 puunji nirmaan main siaare kii bhoomika ko dekhete hue, kuch sarkaron ne kai prakaar kii niamak raahaton or protsaahanon ke saath apana gharelu reating agency vyapaar chaalu karne kaa prayaas kiya. haalanki, yah pratikul bhi ho saktaa he, yadi yah kam samarth agenciyon kii sahaayta ke dwaara or ucch gunvattta yukt vichaaron ke liye saadhan uplabdh karne vaali agenciyon ko dandit karne ke dwaara bazaar kii us kriyavali ko mand kar de jisase agenciyaan pratispardhaa karti hai.
 credit reating agenciyaan niojit vittiya lenden main bhi pramukh bhoomika nibhaa sakati hai. ek "vishisht" rin yaan band ke jarikaran se bhinn, jahaan udhaar lene vaala rin par ek nishchit pratifal dene kaa prastaav rakhata he, niojit vittiya lenden ko yaa to vibhinn lakshanon se yukt rinon kii ek shrankhla yaa phir ek hi prakaar ke anekon chote rinon ke rup main samajha ja saktaa he jo ek saath "gatthar" kii ek shrankhla ke rup main sankalit kar die gaye hai . credit reating praayah byaaj dar yaa ek vishesh ansh se sambandhit mulya ko nirdhaarit karti he, yah us samooh ke antargat aane vaale rin kii gunvattta yaan sampatti kii gunvattta par aadhaarit hota he.
 niojit vitt vyavastha main sanlagn companiyaan praayah alag-alag anshon kii sanrachana ke nirdhaaran ke liye credit reating agenciyon se salaah leti hai, jisase ki prayek ko icchit reating mil jaye. udahaaran ke liye, ek farm rin pratibhutiyon ko jari kar kaaphi maatra main puunji udhaar lene kii ichha rakh sakati he. haalanki, yah puunji itani badi he ki niveshak iske ekal nirgaman ke liye jis pratifal kii maang karenge, jo pratishedhaatmak hoga. iske badale main, yah teen alag band ko jari karne kaa nishchay karata he, jinaki teen bhinn credit reating hongi- e, bibibi or bibi . form yah aashaa karti he ki vah e-reating vaale band par jis prabhavi byaaj kaa bhugtan karti he, vah usase kaaphi kam hoga jiskaa bhugtan ise bibi-reating vaale band ke liye karnaa hoga, lekin yah bhi ki samgr rup se, ise ugaahi gayi kul puunji ke liye jis raashi kaa bhugtan karnaa aavashyak he vah us raashi se kam hi hogi jiskaa bhugtan ise ek hi band ke madhyam se ugaahi gayi puunji ke liye karnaa padataa. is prakaar ke lenden kii yojana banane ke baad, form kisi reating agency se yah salaah le sakati he ki prayek ansh ko kis prakaar vinyasit kiya jaye - duusare shabdon main, prayek ansh main rin kii suraksha ke liye kis prakaar kii sampatti kaa prayog kiya jaye- jisase ki vah ansh jari hone par icchit reating praapt kar sake.
 samaanaantar rin daayitv bazaar main vishaal ghaaton ke hone ke kaaran isaki aalochana kii gayi, yah ghate siaare sansthaaon dwaara sheersh reating die jane ke baavjood hue. udahaaran ke liye, credit suis group dwaara jari 340.7 million dollar mulya ke samanaantar rin daayitv  par hua ghaataa lagbhag 125 million dollar tak pahunch gaya, jabaki inhen standard and pors, mudies inwestors sarvice or fich group dwaara eee yaa eee reating di gayi thi.
