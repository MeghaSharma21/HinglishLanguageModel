 ramchandra vidyabagis  ek bhartiya bhashashastrajna or sanskrit vidwaan the. vah apane bangabhashabhaashan ke liye janaa jaataa he, pehla monolinguel bengali dictionari 1817 main prakashit hua. unhone raja rammohan roy dwaara sthapit vedant college main padhaayaa or baad main 1827-37 main sanskrit college main padhaayaa. kolkata main raja rammohan roy ke kaam se jude hue, vah 1828 main sthapit brahmo sabha ke pehle sachiv the or unhone 1843 main debendranath tagore or 21 anya yuvaaon ko brahmo samaaj main shuru kiya. raja rammohan roy england gaye, uske anuthe yug or bishnu chakravarti ke bhakthi gayan ne brahmo samaaj ke astitv main madad kii.
 vah nandkumar vidyalankar ke chote bhai the, ek bhatakati sadhu, jinhone apane chote dinon se raja rammohan ke parichit the. vah mahanivan tantra ke anusaar ek sachche ishwar kaa bhakt tha.
