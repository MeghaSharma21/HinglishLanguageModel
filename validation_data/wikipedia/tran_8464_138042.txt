 badrinaath prasad bharat ke suprasiddh ganitagya evam shikshashasthri the. unhe sahitya evam shiksha ke kshetra main san 1963 main padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. 
 badrinaath prasad kaa janm 12 janvari 1899 i. ko jila azamgarh ke muhammadabad gohna gram ke ek samrudh parivaar main hua tha. inaki padhai apane gram muhammadabad, siwan, patna or varanasi main hui. patna vishvavidhyalay se san 1919 main bi. es-si. uteern kar inhone kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay main em. es-si. kii oopaadhi praapt kii. liverpool vishvavidhyalay se 1931 i. main pi-ech. di. kii or 1932 i. main paris vishvavidhyalay se state di. es-si. kii oopaadhi praapt kii. liverpool or paris vishvavidhyalayon main suprasiddh ganitagyon ke adheen inhone adhyayan or anusandhan kaary sampann kiya tha. 
 ye hindu vishvavidhyalay main suprasiddh bhartiya ganitagya dow ganesh prasad ke priy shishyon main se the or unke adheen inhone vaastavik charavaale falnon ke siddhanton tathaa shreniyon, visheshtya phuryein shrenee, tathaa unase sambhddha anya shreniyon kii, aakalaniyata par gaveshna kii. england main apane ek professor ke saath abel aakalaniyata kii nirpeksh vidhi gyaat karne tathaa upyog karne kaa sammaan bantaane kaa shrey praapt kiya. do varsh tak hindu vishvavidhyalay main praadhyaapak rahane ke pashchaat ye july, 1924 i. main elahabaad vishvavidhyalay chale gaye, jahaan lekturer, reader, professor tathaa ganit vibhaag ke adhyaksh pad par rahe. bich main do varshon ke liye ye patna kalege main bhi ganit ke professor tathaa adhyaksh pad par chale gaye the. inhone bharat ke baahar anek deshon kii yatra kii thi. vigyaan ke naishanal institute tathaa naishanal acedemi ke ye puraane phelo the. indian mathematical sosayadi or vigyaan parishad ke adhyaksh the. bhartiya vigyaan congress ke puraane sadsy or utsaahi kaaryakarta the. 1945 i. main ganit tathaa sankhyikee anubhag kii adhyakshata bhi aapane kii thi. bhartiya vigyaan congress ke 53wein adhiveshan ke pradhan adhyaksh rahe. 
 bharat sarkaar ne inhen padmabhushan kii oopaadhi se 1963 i. main vibhooshit kiya tha or 1964 i. main sansad rajya sabha ke sadsy nirvaachit hue. 18 janvari 1966 i. ko hridaygati band ho jane se aapaki sahasaa mrityu ho gai.
