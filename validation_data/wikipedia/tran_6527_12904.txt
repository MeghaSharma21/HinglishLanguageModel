 bataam dakshinpurv asia ke indonesia desh kaa ek dweep he. yah sumatra dweep ke paas riyau dweepasmooh praant ke kshetra main sthit he. yah indonesia-singapore-malaysia mukt vyapaar kshetra kaa bhaag hone ke kaaran viksit or dhanwaan ho gaya he. bataam apane padosi bintaan dweep se pashchim main avasthit he. bataam riau dweepasmooh kaa sabase bada shahar bhi he or medaan or palembang ke baad sumatra main teesraa sabase adhik aabaadi vaala shahar he. bataam ke mahanagareey vistaar main kai bikhare dweep or tapu aate hai. yah pados ke galang or rempang dwipon se pul dwaara judaa hua he lekin in dwipon par bataam ke vipareet gramin vaataavaran va arthvyavastha he.
