 motilal banarsidas bharat kaa prasiddh prakaashan samooh he. yah sanskrit tathaa bharatvidya se sambandhit stareey pustakon ke prakaashan ke liye vikhyath he. iskaa armbh san 1903 se hua. dilli, mumbai, kolkata, chennai, bangaloru, patna, varanasi or pune main inkaa prakaashan-kaary sansthit he. ab tak is samooh ne 5000 se adhik pustakon kaa prakaashan kiya he.
