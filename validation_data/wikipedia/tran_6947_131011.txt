 dugdh-utpaada yaa deyari utpaada se abhipraay un khaadya vastuon se he jo doodh se banati hai. yah aam taur par ucch urja pradaan karne vaale khaadya padaarth hote hai. in utpaadon kaa utpaadan yaa prasanskaran karne vaale sanyantr ko deyari yaa dugdhashala kaha jaataa he. bharat main inke prasanskaran ke liye kaccha doodh aam taur gaay yaa bhainson se liya jaataa he, lekin yada kada anya stanadhariyon jaise bakrii, bhed, oont, jaleey bhens, yaks, yaa ghodon kaa doodh bhi kai deshon main prayukt hota he. dugdh-utpaada saamanytah europe, madhya purv or bhartiya bhojan kaa mukhya hissaa hai, jabaki purvi asian bhojan main inkaa prayog na ke baraabar hota he.
