 sankisa basantpur farrukhabad, farrukhabad, uttar pradesh sthit ek gaanv he.
 yahaan aane jane ke liye ek maatr saadhan he sadak maarg. yah sthaan shahar farrukhabad se lagbhag 45 kilometre dur he.
 yahaan buddha dharm kaa puratathvik mahatv kaa ek sthaan bhi he. duniya ke anek deshon ne yahaan par buddha mandir tathaa yatri niwasahagrih bhi bana rakhe hai. mul buddha sthal par suraksha kii drishti se boundary karvaa di gayi he tathaa kuch sainik bhi tainaat kar diye hai. yahaan buddha reeti se bana hua pakki eenton kaa ek chabutara he. paas main hi ek balua pathar kaa stambh he. stambh ke shirsh par hathi ban hua he. sambhavtah ise samraat ashok ne banwaaya. yah sthaan farrukhbad jile ke dakshin-pashchim kshetra par pasta he. yahaan varsh me ek baar buddha-poornima ke din anterrashtriya mela lagata he. logon kaa yah vishwaas he ki tathagat buddha apane jeevankaal main yahaan anek baar aaye.
