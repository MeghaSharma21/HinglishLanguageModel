 abu goraab misr main ek ilaaka he jo ki kahiraa ke dakshin main 15 kimi sthit he, sakkarah or al-jijah ke bich, abusir ke uttar main lagbhag 1 kimi uttar main, nile ke pashchimi tat par registan pathaar ke kinare. ilaake apane puratathvik sthalon ke liye sabase acchhee tarah se janaa jaataa he, jisamen puraane rajya kaal ke dauraan 25 veen shatabdi isa purv main nirmit donon sabase bade or sabase surakshit sangrakshit mandir, saath hi saath yujaracaf ke surya mandir, faro neurrere ini ke surya mandir shaamil hai. iske alaava, abu goraab bhi pehle rajvansh ke kabristaan kii sthal he.
