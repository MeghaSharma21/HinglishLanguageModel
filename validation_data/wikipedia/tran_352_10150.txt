 prekshan vidhi saamaajik vigyaan, manovigyaan evam sankhyikee aadi ke kshetra main upyog main laayi jane vaali vidhi he. isamen ek namoonay kaa adhyayan karke us par puri aabaadi ke vishay main nishkarsh nikaala jaataa he. is vidhi main liya gaya swatantra-char anusandhankarta ke niyantran main nahin hota. ise 'aprakshepi vidhi' bhi kehete hai. 
 aprakshepi vidhiyon ke do prakaar maane gayi hai - aatmanishth evam vastunishth .
