 lisutu yaa lesotho  ek landlock or dakshin africa ganatantr se chaaron taraf se ghira hua desh he. mahaj 30,000 varg kimi kshetrafal vaale is des kii anumaanit jansankhya 1,800,000 he. desh kii rajdhani or sabase bada shahar maseru he. yah rashtramandal kaa sadsy he. lesotho kaa arth mote taur par 'sesotho bolne vaale logon kaa desh" he. desh kii 40% jansankhya antarrashtriya garibi rekha se neeche 1.25 america dollar pratidin se kam aamadani main guzaara karti he.
 iske pashchim or uttar main orange free state, purv main netaal tathaa purvi greekvaland tathaa dakshin main kep provins hai. yahan kaa ausat taap lagabh 15.5 degree celsius tathaa jalwaayu shushk he. lagbhag 30 inch varsha haati he. yahan baantu jati ke basuto log rahete hai. isaki purvi seemaa par 10,000 foot unchaa 'drakhensburg' naamak pathaar he. orange pramukh nadi he. mukhyat: gehun, makka, soragham, jau, jayee, faliyon, matar evam subjiyon kii upaj he. ghode, bandar, bhed, bakre, khachchar aadi pramukh pashu hai. maseru yahan kii rajdhani tathaa railway stesan he.
