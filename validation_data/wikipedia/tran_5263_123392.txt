 hemachandracharyane yogashastra par bada hi mahatvapurn granth likhaa he. iski style patanjali ke yogasutra ke anusaar hi he. kintu vishay or varnan kram main moulikta evam bhinnata he.
 vishad tikaa sahit pratham chaar parichchhedon main jain darshan kaa vistrut or spasht varnan diya gya he. yogashastra niti vishyak upadeshaatmak kavy kii koti main aata he. yogashastra jain sampradaay kaa dharmik evam darshanik granth he. vah aadhyatmopanishad he.
 aachaar pradhan he tathaa dharm or darshan dono se prabhavit he. yoga shashtra ne nitikavyon yaa upadesh kaavyon kii prarampra ko samruv evam samunnat kiya he. yogashastra vishudv dharmik evam darshanik granth he. isamen 12 prakaash tathaa 1018 shloke he.
 iske antargat madiraa dosha, maance dosha, navaneet bhakshan dosha, madhu dosha, udumbar dosha, raatri bhojan dosha kaa varnan he.
 antim 12 wein prakaash ke prarambh main shrut samudr or guru ke mukhase jo kuch main ne janaa he uskaa varnan kar chukaa hu, ab nirmal anubhav sidv tatvako prakashit karata hu aisa nidesh kar ke veikshipt, yatayaat, in chit-bhendo ke swarupaka kathan karte bahiraatma, intaraatma or paramatma kaa swarup kaha gaya he.
 samvedan ke liye paatchjal yogasutra tathaa hemchandra yogashastra main paryaapt samya he. yoga se sharir or man shudv hota he. yoga kaa arth chitravritika nirodh. man ko sabal banane ke liye sharir sabal bananaa atyaavashyak he.
 yogasutra or yogashastra main atyant satvik aahaar kii upadeyata batalaakar abhakshya bhakshanaka nishedh kiya gaya he.
 acharya hemchandra sab se pratham 'namo ari hantann' se raag -dweshadi aantarik shatruon kaa naash karne vaale ko namaskara kaha he.
 yogasutra tathaa yogashastra paas-paas he.
 sansar ke sabhi vaad, sampradaay, mat, ddashtirag ke parinaam he. ddashtirag ke kaaran ashaanti or du:kha he. atah vishvashanti ke liye, ddashtirag uchhedan ke liye hemchandraka yogashastra aaj bhi atyant upadey granth he.
