 parenda maharashtra ke osmanabad zile main sthit ek nagar he.parenda main bahamani rajya ke prasiddh budhimaan mantri mahmud gavaan kaa banwaaya hua qila mukhya aitihaasik smarak he. isamen kai badi-badi topein rakhi hui hai.1605 i. main mughalon kaa ahmednagar par adhikaar hone ke pashchaat nizaamshaahi sultaanon ne apni rajdhani yahan banaai. tatpashchaat bijapur ke sultan adilash ne is par adhikaar kar liya.1630 i. main shahjahan ne parenda ko apane gher main liya lekin aurangzeb ne apni dakshin kii soobedaari ke samay is par purna rup se adhikaar kar liya.parenda kaa qila to acchhee dasha main he, kintu purana nagar ab khandhar ho gaya he. khandhiron kaa vistaar dekhte hue jaan padataa he ki pracheen samay main yah nagar kaafii lambaa-chaudaa rahaa hoga.
