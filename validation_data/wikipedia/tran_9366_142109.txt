 manushyon kii anuvanshiki main matruvansh samooh si yaa mitocandria-diene hapalogrup C ek matruvansh samooh he. yah matruvansh samooh sizad kii ek upashakha he. is matruvansh ke log zyaadaatar purvottar asia or america ke adivaasiyon main paae jate hai, lekin haal hi main yah pashchimi europe ke iceland raashtra main bhi paae gaaye hai. siberia kii chukchi janajati ke 11% log matruvansh samooh si ke vanshaj hote hai. vaigyanikon kii manyata he ke jis stri ke saath is matruvansh kii shuruyaat hui vah aaj se lagbhag 60,000 saal pehle madhya asia main caspian sagar or bekaal jhil ke darmiyaani kshetra main kahin rahati thi.
 dhyaan den ke kabhi-kabhi matruvanshon or pitruvanshon ke naam milte-julate hote hai, lekin yah keval ek ittefaq hi he - inkaa aapas main koi sambandh nahin he.
 angrezi main "vansh samooh" ko "hapalogrup", "pitruvansh samooh" ko "waahe chromosome hapalogrup" or "matruvansh samooh" ko "amtidiene hapalogrup" kehete hai.
