 jon trumbul  american chitrakaar the. unka janm kaanactikat rajya ke lebanon sthaan main 6 june 1756 main hua tha. unki mrityu neuyark, 10 navambar 1843 ko hui thi. harvard se snaatak hone ke pashchaat unhone america swatantray yuddh main bhaag liya tha. unhone 1977 tak sena main yojanaon ke nakshe banaae the. ve 1780 main landan gaye or benjamin west ke saath kaam kiya. thode din kaary karne ke pashchaat hi ve rajdroh ke abhiyog main giraftaar kar liye gaye. 1784 main unhone 'bunker kii pahari' or 'maantagomari' chitrit kiya. unhone 1875 main paris jaakar adhikariyon ke akritichitra chitrit kiye. jeffrsan kii sahaayta se unhone 'swatantraaa ke ghoshnaptr par hastakshar' naamak chitra shuru kiya. afasaron, senadhikariyon se sambandhit drishy ke chithran main ve patu the. anek vyakticitra bhi inaki kala ke uttam udhaaharan he.
