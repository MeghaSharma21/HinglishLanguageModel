 aashaaen foundation bharat kii ek gaiyr-sarkari sanstha he jo garib bacchon kii shiksha ke madhyam se sahaayta karti he. sanstha kii sthapana pehali janvari 2007 main hui thi parantu iskaa aupchaarik panchikaran 19 may 2007 ko hua tha. iske peeche 20-30 varsh ke ai tii peshey ke log hai. sanstha apane lakshyon kii prapti lakshya se judi pariyojnaon or samarohon ke madhyam se karti he.
 aashaaen foundation shiksha ke vyaktiyon, desh or sansar ke vikaas main mahatv ko maanati he. foundation kii sabhi gatividhiyaan shiksha ke maanakon garib bacchon main sudhaarney par kendrit karata he.
 haalaanki bharat main shiksha pranali ko kai chunautiyaan dekhi gai hai, foundation ne teen mukhya kshetra ko apni gatividhiyon or prayaason ke liye chunaa he.
 shiksha se achoote rahe bacchon ke liye sanstha bachche man ke sachche pariyojna chalaati he.
 schuli shiksha kaa vaataavaran sudhaarney ke liye sanstha pustak abhiyaan pariyojna chalaati he.
 shiksha main naveenatam takneekon or madhyamon ke liye sanstha shikshan abhiyaan pariyojna chalaati he.
