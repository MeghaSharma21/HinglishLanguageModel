 belvan : vrundavan ke yamuna paar sthit gram jahangirpur post - dangauli/ tehsil -maant jila - mathura rajya - uttar pradesh sthit lakshmi devi kii tapasthali he. yah sthaan atyant siddh he. yahaan lakshmi mata kaa bhavy mandir he. is sthaan par pus maah main chaaro ore lakshmi mata kii jai jayakaar kii gunj sunai dene lag jaati he. dur-daraaj ke asankhya shraddhalu yahaan apni sukh-samruddhi ke liye puja-archana karne aate hai. yahaan pus maah ke prayek guuruuvaar ko vishaal mela judta he. 
 pracheen kaal main is sthaan par bel ke vrikshon kii bharmaar thi. isi kaaran yah sthaan belvan ke naam se prakhyat hua. krishna-balram yahaan apane sakhaon ke saath gaayen charaane aayaa karte the. shrimadbhagwat main is sthaan kii mahatta kaa vishad varnan he. bhavishyotarpurana main isaki mahima kaa bakhaan karte hue likhaa he: 
  tap: siddhi pradaayavanamobilvavanayach. janardan namastubhyanvilveshynmostute.
 vrundavan kii ore unmukh krishna kii puja karti hui lakshmi kii murti vaali lakshmi mata mandir yahaan kaa sarv pramukh aakarshan he. is mandir ke sambandh main manyata he ki bhagavan shri krishna ne jab solah hajaar ek sau aath gopikaon ke saath divya maharasalila kii tab mata lakshmi devi ke hriday main bhi is lila ke darshan karne kii ichha hui or vah belvan ja pahunchi, parantu usamen gopikaon ke atirikt kisi anya vyakti kaa pravesh varjit tha. at: unhen lalita sakhi ne yah kah kar darshan karne se roak diya ki aapkaa aishwarya lila se sambandh he, jabaki vrundavan maadhuryamayilila kaa sthaan he. at:lakshmi mata vrundavan kii ore apana mukh karke bhagavan shrikrishna kii aradhana karne lag gayi. bhagavan shrikrishna jab maharasalila karke atyant thak gaye tab lakshmi mata ne apni sadi se agni prajvalit kar unke liye khichdi banaai. is khichdi ko khaakar bhagavan shrikrishna unase atyadhik prasann hue. lakshmi mata ne bhagavan shrikrishna se braj main rahane kii anumati maangi to unhone unhen saharsha anumati pradaan kar di. yah ghatana pus maah ke guuruuvaar kii he. baad main isi sthaan par lakshmi mata kaa bhavy mandir sthapit hua.
 khichdi yahan kaa sarvapramukh khaadya evam prasad he. yahan khichdi mahotsav yojit karne kii parampara he. yahaan ke lakshmi mata mandir main bhi khichdi se hi bhoga lagaaya jaataa he. yahaan pus maah main prayek guuruuvaar ko jagah-jagah asankhya bhattiyaan chalati hai. saath hi hajaaron bhakt-shraddhalu saare din khichdi ke bade-bade bhandare karte hai.
