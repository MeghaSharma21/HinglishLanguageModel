 munshi naval kishor angreji shashan ke samay ke bharat ke udhyami, patrkaar evam sahityakar the. inaki jindagi kaa safar kamyaabiyon kii daastaan hai. shiksha, sahitya se lekar udyog ke kshetra main unhone safalataa paayi or jo baat sabase ullekhneey thi vah yah ki unhone hameshaa maanav mulyon kaa sammaan kiya.
 munshi naval kishor kaa janm aligarh jile ke vistoi gram ek kuleen brahman parivaar main san 1836 ko hua tha. shuru se hi samachaarpatron or vyapaar main ruchi thi. kuch samay tak 'kohinoor' main kaam karne ke baad 1858 main 22 saal kii umr main lakhanu aa gaye. yahan aate hi unhone naval kishor press sthapit kiya. dekhte hi dekhte is press kii khyaati itani badi ki ise paris ke elpine press ke baad doosraa darja diya jane laga. sabhi majahab kii pustakon or ek se bad kar ek sahityakaron kii kritiyon ko is press ne chapa. kul prakaashan kaa 65 pratishat urdu, arabi or farsi tathaa shesh sanskrit, hindi, bengali, gurumukhi, marathi, pashto or angreji main he. puri duniya ke bade-bade pustakaalayon main unke yahaan kii kitaaben mil jaati he. japan main munshi naval kishor ke naam kaa pustkaalay he to germany ke hidilberg or america ke harvard vishavidhyalay main unki prakashit saamagree ke vishesh kaksh hai.
 udyog ke kshetra main bhi unka apana yogadan he. 1871 main unhone lakhanu main apper india coupon paper mil kii sthapana kii thi jo uttar bharat main kaagaj banane kaa pehla kaarkaana tha. shah iran ne 1888 main kalakatta main patrakaron se kaha ‘hindustan aane ke mere do makasad hai ek vysarai se milanaa or doosraa munshi naval kishor se’. kuch aise hi khayalaat ludhiana darbar main afghanistan ke shah abdul rahamaan ne 1885 main jaahir kiye the.
 bahuaiami vyaktitv or bahuaiami safaltaaon ko apane main samete munshi naval kishor ko kaal ke krur haathon ne 19 farvari 1895 ko sadaa ke liye samet liya.
