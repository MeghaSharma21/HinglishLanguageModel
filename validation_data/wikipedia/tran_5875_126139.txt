 nirdeshaank: 23°26′18″N 88°23′34″E / 23.4382755°N 88.3928686°E / 23.4382755; 88.3928686mayapur pashchim bengal ke nadiya jila main ganga nadi ke kinare, uske jalaangi nadi se sangam ke bindu par basaa hua ek chota sa shahar he. yah navadweep ke nikat he. yah kolkata se 130 kimi uttar main sthit he. yah hindu dharm ke gaudeey vaishnav sampradaay ke liye ati paavan sthal he. yahaan unke pravartak shri chaitanya mahaprabhu kaa janm hua tha. inhen shri krishna evam shri radha kaa avatar mana jaataa he. yahaan lakhon shraddhalu tirthayatri prayek varsh dashanon hetu aate hai. yahaan isckon samaaj kaa banwaaya ek mandir bhi he. ise isckon mandir, mayapur kehete hai. 
 mayapur apane shandaar mandiron ke liye puure vishwa main janaa jaataa he. in mandiron main bhagwaan shri krishnako samarpit iskan mandir pramukh he. mandiron ke alaava paryatak yahaan par saraswat advait math or chaitanya gaudia math kii yatra bhi kar sakate hai. holi ke dinon me mayapur kii chata dekhne laayak hoti he kyonki us samay yahaan par bhavy rathyatra yojit kii jaati he. yah rathyatra aapasi sauhaard or bhaichaare kaa pratik maani jaati he.
 Jai shri krishna
