 lakshan kaa arth he - 'pahchaan kaa chihn' yaa gundharm yaa prakruti. kisi padaarth kii vah visheshta jiske dwaara vah pahachaanaa jaay. ve gun aadi jo kisi padaarth main vishisht rup se hon or jinke dwaara sahaj main uskaa gyaan ho sake. jaise,—akaash ke lakshan se jaan padataa he ki aaj pani barasegaa.
 sharir main dikhaai padanevaale ve chihn aadi jo kisi rog ke suchak hon, bhi 'lakshan' kehalaate hai. jaise,—is rogi main kshay ke sabhi lakshan dikhaai dete hai.
 saamudrik ke anusaar sharir ke angon main milane vaale kuch vishesh chihn bhi lakshan kahe jate hai jo shubh yaa ashubh maane jate hai. jaise,—chakravarti or buddha ke lakshan ek se hote hai. lakshanon ko jaananevaala yaa shubh ashubh chihnon kaa gyaata lakshangya kehalaata he.
 kavy yaa sahitya ke lakshanon kaa vivechan karanevaala granth lakshan granth kehalaata he. duusare shabdon main, lakshan granth kaa arth saahitiyik samiksha kii pustak yaa 'samalochna shaastra' he.lakshan vayavhaar ke vishisht gun hai- jaise kuntha ke prati pratikriyaayein, samasyaon kaa samaadhaan karne kii pranaliyaan, aakraamak tathaa pratirakshak vyavhaar or dusron kii upasthiti main vyakti kaa bahirmukhi yaa antarmukhi vayavhaar aadi.
