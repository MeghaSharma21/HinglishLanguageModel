 sar winston scot  barbados ke ek rajaneta hai. unhen 18 may 1967 se 9 agast 1976 ke bich, barbados kii rani, elizabeth dwitiya dwaara, barbados ke governor-general yani maharajyapal ke pad par niyukt kiya gaya tha. is kaal ke dauraan ve maharani ke pratinidhi ke rup main, unki anupasthiti ke dauraan shaasha ke kartavyon kaa nirvaah karte the.
