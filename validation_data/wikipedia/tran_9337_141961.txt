 veda raahi hindi or dogri ke sahityakar tathaa film nirdeshak hai, jinhone swatantraaa senaanee or krantikari vinayak damodar saavarkar ke jivan par aadhaarit film vir-saavarkar banaai. veda raahi ne doordarshan dharawahik gul gulshan gulfam kaa bhi nirdesan kiya.
 veda raahi kaa janm 22 may 1933 ko jammu kashmir main hua tha. inke pita kaa naam lala mulkraj saraaf tha jo jammu se “ranbir” naam kaa samachar-patr nikalate the. raahi ji ko bachapan se hi likhane kaa shauk tha. unhone pehle urdu main likhnaa armbh kiya or phir hindi or dogri bhashaa main bhi likhane lage. unki ab tak kii kuch mashhoor kahaniyaan he - kaale hathe, aale, cross firing. unke pramukh upanyaason main jhaadu bedi te pattan, parade, tuuti hui door, garm june aadi. 
 veda raahi ne filmi sansar main kadam ramanand sagar ke kaaran rakhaa jinke saath jud rahakar unhone lagbhag 25 hindi filmon ke liye kahania, dialog or screen riting kii. inhone kai filmein kii jaise vir saavarkar, bezubaan, charas, sanyasi, be-imaan, mom kii gudiya, aap aaye bahaar ai, paraayaa dhan, pavitra paapi, 'yah raat phir na aegi' aadi. iske alaava unhone 9 filme or serial kaa nirdesan kiya ehsaas, rishte, zindagi, gul gulshan gulfam, naadaniyaan, kaali gata, prem parvat, daraar aadi. iske alaava inhone kaali gata naamak film nirmit bhi kii.
