 gardi samudaay tapi ke banks ke burhanpur se hyderabad or telugana kshetra tak faile deccan nivasee ek jati he jisamen bhil, laman, vanjaaraa, pardhi, mahadev coli, masana jogi sahit anya marathi kuch bhi shaamil hai. gardi samudaay kaa mukhya bhaag hyderabad or telugana kshetra ke nikatavarti maharashtra ke marathavada kshetra ke logon ko kaha jaataa he. unki kuch rasmen unke anusaar 250 varshon purv gardi samudaay main armbh hui. kuch pardhi samudaay kii jatiyaan vishesh rup se burhanpur kii takankar samudaay apane kaseedon or rasmon main suleman khaan gardi or ibrahim khaan gardi kii pujas kii jaati he. pardhi samudaay ne banduk, pistal or dynamite jaise hathiyaar ko kaam main lene ke liye vishesh kaushal bhi viksit kiya.
