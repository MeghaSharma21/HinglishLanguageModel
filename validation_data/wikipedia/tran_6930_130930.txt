 
 petha yaa kushmmand ), ek bel par lagane vaala fal he, jo sabji kii tarah khaaya jaataa he. yah halke hare varna kaa hota he or bahut bade aakaar kaa ho saktaa he. puraa pakane par yah satahi baalo ko chhodkar kuch shwet dhul bhari sataha kaa ho jaataa he. isaki kuch prajatiyaan 1-2 meter tak ke fal detii hai. isaki adhikansh kheti bharat sahit dakshini or dakshin-purvi asia main hoti he. isse bharat main ek mithai bhi banati he, jise petha hi kehete hai.
 kushmand yaa kushmand kaa fal petha, bhatua, konhdaa aadi naamon se bhi janaa jaataa he. iskaa latin naam 'beninkesa hispida' he. 
 yah lata varshiki, kathin shwet romon se aavruth 5-6 inch vyas ke patton vaali hoti he. pushp ke saath andakaar fal lagate hai. kaccha fal haraa, par pakane par shwet, brihadaakaar hota he. yah varsha ke prarambh main boyaa jaataa he. shishir main fal pakataa he. beej chipte hote hai. iske ek bhed ko kshetrakushmand, bhatua yaa konhdaa kehete hai, jo kachi avasthaa main haraa, par pakane par pilaa ho jaataa he.
 kushmand kheton main boyaa jaataa athwa chappar par lata ke rup main chadhaaya jaataa he. kushmand bharat main sarvatra upajataa he.
 ayurveda main yah laghu, snigdh, madhur, sheetavaary, baat, pitt, kshay, apsmaar, raktpitta or unmaad naashak, baldaayak, mutrajnak, nidrakar, trishnashamak or beej kriminashak aadi kaha gaya he. iske sabhi bhaag-fal, ras, beej, tvak patr, mul, danthal-tel oshadhiyon tathaa anya kaamon main prayukt hote hai.
 iske murabbe, paak, avaleh, thandhai, ghrit aadi banate hai. iske fal main jal ke atirikt starch, kshar tatv, protein, myosin sharkara, tikt raal aadi rahete hai.
 kushmand ke falon ke khaadya ansh ke vishleshan se praapt aankade is prakaar hai 
 kumhada ke bijon kaa upyog khaadya padaarthon ke rup main kiya jaataa he. iske taaje beej kriminashak hote hai. isliye iske bijon kaa upyog aushadhi ke rup main hota he.
 ise jharkhand/bihar main jeth maheenaa ke aarambh hone se pehle dhulaa hua urad daal, thodaa chanaa daal, garm masala, ke saath kaddukas kiya hua kushmand ko mila kar tej dhup main sukha kar adori banaaya jaataa he. jise salbhar dabbaband rakh kar log khate hai.
