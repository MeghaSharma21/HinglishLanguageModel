 panthi geet or is geet ke saath kiya jane vaala panthi nrity chhattisgarh main ke satnami jati ke logo ke dwaara ishwar kii stuti main or sant gurughasi baba ke jivan charitra kaa varnan kiya jaataa he. yah nirgun bhakthi dhara se prerit geet or nrity hai jisame guru ghasidas ke dwaara die gaye upadesh ko geet or nrity ke madhyam se manch par prastut kiya jaataa he.
 iske saath pramukh vaadya yantra ke rup main jhanjh manjira or maandar tathaa dholak kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. ise chhattisgarh kii khaas pahchaan ke rup main bhi dekha jaataa he.
 is nrity ke nartakon main swargeey devdas banjaare kaa naam ullekhneey he.
