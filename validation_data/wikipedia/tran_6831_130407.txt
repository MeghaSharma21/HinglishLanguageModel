 panj pyaare athwa paanch pyaare, sikh guru guru gobind singh dwaara 13 april 1699 ko anandpur sahib ke aitihaasik divaa main paanch logon bhai sahib singh, bhai dharam singh, bhai himmat singh, bhai mohakam singh or bhai dayaa singh ko diya gaya naam he. unhone kaalasaa panth ke kendra kii sthapana kii.
 aisa mana jaataa he ki jab mughal shasankaal ke dauraan jab badshaah aurangzeb kaa aatank badhata hi ja rahaa tha. us samay sikh dharm ke guru gobind singh ne baisakhi parv par anandpur sahib ke vishaal maidan main sikh samudaay ko aamantrit kiya. jahaan guruji ke liye ek takht bichaaya gaya or takht ke peeche ek tamboo lagaaya gaya. us samay guru gobind singh ke daayen haath main nangi talwaar chamak rahi thi. gobind singh nangi talwaar liye manch par pahunche or unhone elaan kiya- mujhe ek aadmi kaa sir chahie. kya aap main se koi apana sir de saktaa he? yah sunate hi vahaan maujuud sabhi sikh aashcharyachakit rah gaye or sannata chaa gaya. usee samay dayasingh naamak ek vyakti aage aayaa jo lahore nivasee tha or bolaa- aap meraa sir le sakate hai. gurudev use paas hi banaae gaye tamboo main le gaye. kuch der baad tamboo se khun kii dhara nikalati dikhaai di. tambu se nikalate khun ko dekhakar pandal main sannata chaa gaya. guru gobind singh tambu se baahar aae, nangi talwaar se taaja khun tapak rahaa tha. unhone phir elaan kiya- mujhe ek or sir chahie. meri talwaar abhi pyaasi he. is baar dharmadas aage aaye jo saharanpur ke jawada gaanv ke nivasee the. gurudev unhen bhi tamboo main le gaye or pehle kii tarah is baar bhi thodi der main khun kii dhara baahar nikalne lagi. baahar aakar gobind singh ne apni talwaar kii pyaas bujhane ke liye ek or vyakti ke sir kii maang kii. is baar jagannatha puri ke himmat raay khadey hue. guruji unhen bhi tamboo main le gaye or phir se tamboo se khun dhara baahar aane lagi. gurudev punah baahar aae or ek or sir kii maang kii tab dwarka ke yuvak mohakam chand aage aae. isi tarah paanchavi baar phir gurudev dwaara sir maangane par bidar nivasee sahib chand sir dene ke liye aage aaye. maidan main itane sikhon ke hone ke baad bhi vahaan sannata pasar gaya, sabhi ek-duusare kaa munh dekh rahe the. kisi ko kuch samaj nahin aa rahaa tha. tabhi tamboo se guru gobind singh kesaria bana pahane paanch sikh naujavaanon ke saath baahar aae. paanchon noujvaan vahiin the jinke sir kaatane ke liye gobind singh tamboo main le gaye the. gurudev or paanchon noujvaan manch par aae, gurudev takht par baith gaye. paanchon naujavaanon ne kahaan gurudev hamaare sir kaatane ke liye hamen tamboo main nahin le gaye the balki vah hamaari pariksha thi. tab gurudev ne vahaan upasthit sikhon se kaha aaj se ye paanchon mere panj pyaare hai. is tarah sikh dharm ko panjpyare mil gaye. jonhone baad main apni nishtha or samarpan bhav se khalsa panth kaa janm diya.
