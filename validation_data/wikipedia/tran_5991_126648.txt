 paribhaasha kisi bhi vishay yaa vastu yaa cheez kaa sankshipt or taarkik varnan he, jo vastuon ke moolbhoot vishisht gun yaa sankalpanaaon ke arth, antarvastu or simaaen bataata he. kisi shabd yaa vaakyansh yaa sanket kii vyakhya karne vaale gadyansh ko bhi paribhaasha kah sakate hai. udhaaharan ke liye, bhautikee main 'shakti' kii paribhaasha nimnlikhit prakaar se kii jaati he-
 nishkarsh yah he ki paribhaasha aisi honi chahie jo avyaapti or ativyapti dosha se mukt ho. arthath, sanderbhit vishay yaa vastu ke vaacharth kaa aisa lakshan batala sake jo prayek prasang ke liye to upyukt ho kintu, us vishay yaa vastu ke sivaay kisi anya vastu ke liye upyukt na ho.
