 nishacharata kuch jaanwaron kii raat ko sakriya rahane kii pravriti ko kehete hai. nishachari jeevon main ullu, chamagadad or rekun jaise praani shaamil hai. kuch nishachari jaanwaron main din or raat donon main saafe dekhne kii kshamta hoti he lekin kuch kii ankhen andhere main hi thik se kaam karti hai or din ke vaqt chaundhiya jaati hai. 
 pracheen kathaon main rakshason ko bhi raat main sakriya rahane vaale jeev bataaya jaataa tha, isliye kabhi-kabhi "nishachar" kaa arth "rakshas" nikaala jaataa tha, jaisaa kii maithilisharan gupt kii "panchvati" naam kii kavita main dekha ja saktaa he -
 "nishachari" ko angreji main "noctornal", farsi main "shabazi" or arabi main "al-lailai" kaha jaataa he.
