 pathar ke phool 1991 kii anant balani dwaara nirdeshit hindi bhashaa kii film he. salmaan khan isamen anderworld giroh se ladane vaale police adhikari kaa kirdaar nibhaate hai. ravina tandon pramukh mahila bhoomika nibhaati hai. jabaki vinod mehara, kiran kumaar, rima lagu or manohar singh sahaayak bhoomikaaon main shaamil hai. vinod mehara is film kii relies se pehle guzar gaye the. unki aawaaj sachin khedekar dwaara dab kii gai. 
 suraj inspector vijay verma or unki patni mira kaa putr he. ek din, suraj kiran naam kii ek ladki se miltaa he. sabhi ke liye agyaat, kiran anderworld giroh ke raja balraj khanna kii putree he. balraj ne apni sachai ko duniya or apni beti se ek rahasy rakhaa he. is bich vijay, balraj ke giroh kaa maamle saunpa gaya. ek hi college main shaamil hone se, suraj or kiran pyaar main padate hai. chunki vijay giroh main gaharaa or gaharaai se jaanch karata he, giroh ke sadsy, vishesh rup se goga, sakate main jate hai.
 balraj is tathy se pareshaan hai ki uski beti vijay ke bete ke pyaar main pad gai he. vijay ko bhi abhas ho jaataa he ki balraj giroh kaa netaa he. isliye, jab vijay apane bete or kiran ke bich sambandh ke bare main jaanata he, to vah unke pyaar ko roktaa he. is maamle main suraj ke uske pita ke saath matbhed he. jaise suraj apni maa ko apni bhavanaayen bataata he. mira bhi uske liye ek rahasy prakat karti he: suraj vijay kaa jaivik putr nahin he.
 mira use bataati he ki uskaa asli pita, uskaa pati inspector or vijay kaa mitra tha. mira suraj ke saath garbhvatee thi, jab suraj ke pita kii hatya hui thi. ramsingh gupti naamak ek gunde ne unki hatya kii thi. unki mrityu ke baad, vijay ne mira ko apane ghar le liya or baad main usase vivaah kiya jab logon ne mira ke charitra ke bare main baat karnaa shuru kar diya.
 sabhi ram lakshmana dwaara sangitabaddh.
 ravina tandon ne is film ke liye filmafere mahila pratham abhinay puraskaar ne arjeet kiya tha.
