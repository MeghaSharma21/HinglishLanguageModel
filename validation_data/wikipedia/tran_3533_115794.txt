 raahi maasoom raza kaa bahucharchit upanyaas “aadha gaanv” 1966 main prakashit hua jisase raahi kaa naam ucchkoti ke upanyaaskaaron main liya jane laga. yah upanyaas uttar pradesh ke ek nagar gajipur se lagbhag gyaarah mile dur based gaanv gangoli ke shiksha samaaj kii kahani kahata he. raahi nen swayan apane is upanyaas kaa uddeshy spasht karte hue kaha he ki “vah upanyaas vastav main meraa ek safar tha. main gajipur kii talash main niklaa hum lekin pehle main apni gangoli main thaharuunga. agar gangoli kii hakeekat pakad main aa gayi to main gajipur kaa apik likhane kaa saahas karuunga”.
