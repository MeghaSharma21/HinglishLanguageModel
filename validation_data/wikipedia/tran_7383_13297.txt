 
 parvatiy bans kaa teetar Mountain Bamboo Partridge teetar ke kul kaa ek pakshi he. 
 yah pakshi china, thailand, bangladesh, bharat, myanmar, laos tathaa vietnam main vidyaman he.
 yah pakshi bans ke jangalon main pani ke paas, unche ghaas ke maidaanon main tathaa un sthanon main jahaan jangal kaate ja chuke hai or bans ke jangal uga diye gayi hai, vahaan rahanaa pasand karata he. puraa din yah jhaad main chhipakar vyateet kar letaa he or subah tadake yaa der shaam ko hi bhojan kii talash main baahar nikalataa he. khatara hone par hi yah udata he or phir jaldi hi zamin par aakar chhip jaataa he.
 is pakshi ko kachar bhashaa main daobui laai, kuki bhashaa main vengte tathaa manipuri bhashaa main vakrek ke naamon se janaa jaataa he.
