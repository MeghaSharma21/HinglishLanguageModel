 kanika kapur bollywood kii ek naveenatam gayika hai. inkaa janm 23 march 1978 ko lakhanu, uttarapradesh main hua, vartmaan main yah landan kii nivasee hai. inkaa baby doll naamak gaanaa prasiddh hua. inhone apan kariyar hindi bhashi sangeet main 2012 main shuru kiya.
 kanika kapur punjabi tathaa sufi lokgeeton kii gayika hai. raagini ms 2 naamak film main inke geet baby doll ke baad inhone prasiddh praapt kii jo antarjaal par ati prasiddh hui. is gane main sanni leone he, tathaa is gane ne film release hone se pehle hi das lakh hits praapt kii. inhone apana pehla sangeet video 2012 main release kiya tha, jo diaar. jeus dwaara nirmit tha tathaa uskaa naam jugni ji tha, yah geet alaam lohaar ke gane jugni kaa hip-hop avataran tha jiske nirmaataa landan ke gayak tathaa sangitajna diaar. jeus ne nirmit kiya tha. baby doll main kanika ke saath teen sah-gayak bhi the jinka naam harmit singh, manameet singh tathaa anjaan bhattacharya hai tathaa yah teenon milakar meet brose anjaan kehalaate hai. 2015 main roy film main inkaa gayachittiyaan kalaaiyaan bhi kaafii mashhoor hua. phir kanika nen sanni leone kii film ek paheli lila ke liye "desi luk" gaanaa gaaya. 2016 main inhone hag me tathaa lav letters jaise lokapriy geet gaae.
 kanika kapur kaa janm bharat main hua tha tathaa ab vah landan kii nivasee hai. inkaa vivaah raaj chandok se hua tathaa teen bachche bhi hue parantu ab kanika talakshuda hai.diaar. jeus se inaki mulaakaat baby doll release se chha: saal pehle hui thi tathaa tabhi unhone nishchay kiya tha ki ve dono ek saath kaam karenge.
