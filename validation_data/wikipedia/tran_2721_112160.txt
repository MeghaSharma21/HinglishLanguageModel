 orissa ke log bahut miljulkar rahete hai. 
 oriya ke log ariyan samooh se aae the. odisha ke purvi tateey rajya main bahumat paae jate he. vishaal bahumat log hindu dharm ke he. jayaadaatar log suraj ke dharm me maanate he. yah jagah konark mandir ke liye janaa jaataa he. isaayiyon or musalamaanon kii jaati bahut kam he idhar. 
 aarambhik dinon main odiya ke logo ko kaling ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. mahabharat jaise pavitra granth me bhi yah baat dashaya gaya he. paali sahitya me unako odakas bulaayaa jaataa tha. 
 odiya samaaj me auraton ko bahut sammaan diya jaataa he. yahan adivasee bhi adhik maatra main paye jate hai.
 yah rajya apane hell mail or bhaichaare ke liye janaa jaataa he. yahan ke log ekajute hokar rahete hai. auraton ko bhi bahut izzat di jaati he.ve makar, dhanu, sarsavati puja, tirth yatra, rama navami, magh saptami, holi, shivaratri, rath yatra aadi manaate hai. pakhaala yahan kii prasiddh pakwaan me ek mana jaataa he. . yah pakwaan chaaval or pani se banaaya jaataa he. ghi ke chaaval bhi prasiddh hai. kanika, chaaval kii mithai he.
