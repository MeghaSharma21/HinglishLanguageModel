 azetek 14veen se 16veen shatabdi ke madhya mexico main ek samrajya tha. azetek logon kii rajdhani tenoctilan thi or wo vartmaan mexico main bas gaye the. azetek log nahuatl bhashaa bolte the. azetek logon kii samskruti ke kuch ang the maanav bali or mithkeey jeevon par vishwaas. iske atirikt, azetek logon kaa panchang bahut hi satik tha jisamen 365 din hote the. unka ek dharmik panchang bhi tha jo 260 kaa hota tha.
 azetek mul america log the jo 15veen or 16veen shatabdiyon ke madhya mexico main rahaa karte the. ve log swayan ko mexiki yaa nahua kehete the. azetek kii rajdhani thi, tenoctilan. yah un dwipon se bana tha jo ek jhil main the. us samay tenoctilan vishwa ke mahanatam nagaron main se ek tha.
 azetek bahut se devataaon main vishwaas rakhte the. quetzalcotl, huitjilopochtili or tezkatlipoka inamen pramukh the. kabhi-kabhaar azetek apane devataaon ko khush karne ke liye maanav bali bhi dete the. 1519 or 1521 ke madhya spani netaa hernan cortis ne azetek logon ko parrast kiya or unka samrajya le liya. kuch azetek cortis ke sainiko se ladanaa nahin chaahate the kyonki ve samajhate the kii ve devtaa hai.
 aaj bahut se mexico vaasiyon ke azetek or anya mul america purvaj hai. abhi bhi mexico ke log azetek chinhon kaa upyog karte he. mexico ke jhande main jo cactus par munh main saanp ko dabaae chil kaa chitra he, vah azetek kaa pratik he. mexico naam bhi ek azetek shabd he. 16veen shatabdi main spain dwaara parast hone se pehle azetek sabhyataa bahut fali-phuli.
 azetek log bahut se aise paudhe or sabziyaan khate the jo mexico main saralat se ugaae ja sakate the. makka unka mukhya aahaar tha, jise ve mez kehete the. ek anya aahaar tha squash.
