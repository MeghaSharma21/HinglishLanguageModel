 woxwagon ek germany kii car utpaadan company he. jiskaa mukhyalay volfsburg, germany main he. yah company 28 may 1937 ko yahan sthapit hui thi. is company ne varsh 1975 ko sabase adhik bikri karne vaali company ban gaya. yah ab vishwa main toyota ke baad duusari sabase badi company he.
 woxwagon vaastavik rup se 1937 main bana tha. 1930 ke samay germany main companyyaan mukhya rup se mahange car hi banaate the. us samay yah vaahan kisi madhyam vargeey germany logon ke kharidne se bahut dur tha. iske parinaam swaroop keval 50 main se keval 1 vyakti ke paas hi car hoti thi. is varg ke liye car banane main nayaa bazaar dekhne vaale log is pariyojna par kaary shuru kar diye the.
 lekin yah pariyojna nai nahin thi. isse pehle bela bareni ne bhi is tarah ke car kii ruparekha 1920 ke aasapaas banaai thi. isi ke saath hi joseph ganj ne bhi ek tarah kaa german woxwagon banaaya tha. iske liye hanomag naamak germany company iske liye 2/10 pies kaa komissabrot naamak ek chota engine banaaya. yah company is ke liye 1925 se 1928 tak yah kaary karti rahi.
