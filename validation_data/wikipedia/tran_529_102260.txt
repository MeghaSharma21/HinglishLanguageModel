 deenanath malhotra, amar shahid mahashya raajapaal ji ke putr the tathaa swatantraaa senaanee the.22 farvari, 1923 main lahore main janme deenanath ne hi bhartiya kapi rhite act kaa praaroop taiyaar kiya tha. ve federation publishars of india ke adhyaksh rahe. padmashri se sammanit shri malhotra ko unesco ne bhi sammanit kiya tha. vah ameritas, hind packet books ke bhi adhyaksh rahe. vah antarrashtriya str par vikasshil deshon ke nazariye se copirite ke muddon ke visheshgy the or unesco main visheshgyon ke saath baithkon main bhaag liya. unhen 2000 main bharat sarkaar dwaara padam shri se sammanit kiya gaya tha.deenanath malhotra ​​ne 1944 main punjab vishvavidhyalay lahore main masters main swarna padak jeeta tha. lekin unka padak desh ke bantwaare ke samay vahiin chhot gaya tha, jise bahut baad main unhen 2012 main pradaan kar pakistan ucchayog, nai dilli main sammanit kiya gaya tha.13 disambar 2017 ko inaki mrityu ho gai.
