 pakistani rashtriya cricket team march 2017 se may 2017 tak west indies kaa daura karne vaali he. daure main teen test match kii shrinkhalaa, teen ek divasiy antarrashtriya or chaar twanti-20 antarrashtriya shaamil hai. west indies cricket board ne florida main central broward kshetreey park main tii20i match khilne kii sambhaavna ko dekha, jaisaa ki unhone agast 2016 main bharat ke khilaaf kiya tha. haalanki, dablooisibi ne is daure ke liye carabian main sabhi judanar banaae rakhaa.
 janvari 2017 main, pakistan cricket board ne pakistan main do tii20i match khilne ke liye dablyooisibi ko ek prastaav diya tha. ve 18 or 19 march 2017 ko lahore main yojit kiye gaye the, florida ke lauderhil main do or tii20i khel rahe tiimon ke saath. haalanki, jab 12 janvari 2017 ko dablyooisibi ne daure ke judanar kii pushti kii thi, tab pakistan main match kaa koi zikr nahin hua tha. baad main usee din dablyooisibi ne pushti kii ki suraksha sambandhi chintaaon ke kaaran westeindies pakistan main nahin khelenge, lekin march 2017 main sanyukt rajya america main do tii20i match khilne kii koshish kar rahe the.
 mul rup se daure main do tii20i match honge, lekin march 2017 ke shuruuaati samay main do atirikt tii20i phiksture shaamil kiye gaye the. daure se pehle, misbah-ul-haq ne pushti kii ki vah pakistan test team ke captain ke rup main jari rahegaa, jo pisibi ne sweekaar kiya he. april 2017 main, misba ne ghoshnaa kii ki vah shrinkhalaa ke aakhir main antarrashtriya cricket se sanyas legaa. do din baad, younus khan ne bhi ghoshnaa kii ki shrinkhalaa ke samaapan ke baad vah test cricket se ritair ho jayega.
 pakistan ne twanti-20 shrinkhalaa 3-1 se jiti, or ekadivasiy shrinkhalaa 2-1 se jiti. unhone westeindies main test shrinkhalaa 2-1 se bhi jeet lee, unki pehali test series jeet thi.
