 dakshin bharat main apni tarah kaa pehla mudumalai vanyajeev abhayaarany kerala-karnataka seemaa par sthit he. 321 varg kilometre main faile is abhayaarany ke paas hi bandipur rashtriya udyaan he. in donon udyanon ko moyaar nadi alag karti he. mysore or uti ko jodne vaala rashtriya rajmarg is udyaan se hokar gujarata he.
 mudumalai main vanyajeevon kii anek prajatiyaan dekhne ko milti hai jaise langur, baagh, hathi, gaur or udne vaali gilahariyaan. iske alaava yahaan anek prakaar ke pakshi bhi dekhe ja sakate hai jaise malabar trogan, grey hornbill, crestid hoque eagle, crestid serpaint eagle aadi. farvari se june ke bich kaa samay yahaan aane ke liye sabase adhik upyukt he. yahaan par vanaspati or jantuon kii kuch durlabh prajatiyaan pai jaati hai or kai luptpraaya:jaanwaron bhi yahaan paae jate he. hathi, sambar, chital, hiran aasaani se dekhe ja sakate hai. jaanwaron ke alaava yahaan rangabirange pakshi bhi udate hue dikhaai dete hai. abhayaarany main hi bana theppakkadu hathi camp bacchon ko bahut lubhaata he.
