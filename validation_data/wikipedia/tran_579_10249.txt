 aara-chhapra sethu athwa vir kunwar singh sethu ganga par bana he. yah bhojpur jile ke babura or saaran jile ke doriganj ko jodta he. is pul ke ban jane ke baad aara se chhapra kii duuri 100 kimi kam ho gai. aara said main 16 kilometre va chhapra said main ek kilometre aprope road he. pul ke nirmaan par 860 karod kharch anumaanit he. varsh 2010 main 676 karod kaa laagat se is sethu kaa nirmaan shuru kiya gaya. enech 30 se shahar main aane vaali gadiyon bina patna pahunche hi uttar bihar pahunch jaaengi.
