 qalaat dakshini afghanistan ke zabul praant kii rajdhani he. yah shahar yatayaat ke nazariye se rajmarg dwaara purv main kandahaar or pashchim main gazani shaharon se judaa hua he. qalaat lagbhag 5,000 foot kii oonchai par sthit he.
 yahan 'gar bolan baba' naamak ek 730 meter gahari gufa he jiske istemaal dharmik prayogon ke liye kiya jaataa he. yahan 19veen sadi main bharat kii british sarkaar dwaara banwaaya gaya ek qila he.
