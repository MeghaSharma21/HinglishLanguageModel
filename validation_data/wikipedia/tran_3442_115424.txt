 nirdeshaank: 31°23′N 75°23′E / 31.38°N 75.38°E / 31.38; 75.38kapoorthala jalandhar shahar ke pashchim main sthit punjab kaa ek pramukh shahar he. yah kapoorthala jila kaa mukhyalay he. iskaa naam iske sansthaapak nawab kapur singh ke naam par padaa. baad main kapoorthala riyasat ke raja fateh singh aahalwalia kii shahi rajdhani thi. yah shahar apni khubasurat imaaraton or sadakon ke liye janaa jaataa he. ek samay main isaki safai ko dekhakar ise punjab kaa paris kaha jaataa tha. panch mandir, shalimaar baag, jagatjit singh kaa mahal yahaan kii kuch pramukh imaarte hai. maharaj jagatjit singh ne yahaan bahut si imaaraton kaa nirmaan karavaaya jo iske sunahare itihaas kii gavaahi dete hai.
 
 sainik skool kii imaarat pehle jagatjit mahal ke naam se jaani jaati thi. yahaan par kapoorthala riyasat ke purv maharaj, maharaj jagatjit singh rahete the. 200 acre main failaa yah mahal vaastushilp kaa khubasurat namoona he jo peles of varselas or fountainblu kii yaad dilaataa he. iskaa dizin french vaastukar em. markel ne banya tha. iskaa manamohak darbar haul bharat ke sabase khubasurat haul main se ek he. plaster of paris kii murtiyon or ceiling par bani chitrakaari francisi kala or vaastushilp kii visheshta ko darshaati hai. is mahal kaa nirmaan kaary 1900 main shuru hua tha or 1908 main puri tarah banakar taiyaar ho gaya tha.
 kapoorthala kii dharmik sahishnutaa kaa pratik moorish masjid kaa nirmaan francisi vaastukar monnaire em.mentics ne kiya tha. yah masjid morocco ke marakesh kii vishaal masjid kii tarj par banaaya gaya tha. iskaa nirmaan kapoorthala ke aakhri shaasha maharaja jagatjit singh ne karavaaya tha or ise puraa hone main 13 varsh kaa samay laga tha. masjid ke aantarik gumbad kii sajaavat lahore ke myo kala vidhyalay ke kalakaaron ne kii thi. yah masjid rashtriya smarak he jisaki dekharekh bhartiya puraatatv surveykshan vibhaag karata he. iskaa lakdi kaa ek model lahore sangrahalay ke pravesh dwaar par rakhaa gaya he. 
 jagatjit club shahar ke bich main sthit ek shandaar imaarat he. is imaarat kaa nirmaan vaastukala kii greek style main kiya gaya he. yah kuch-kuch athens ke ecropolis kii yaad dilaati he. apane banane ke samay se lekar aaj tak yah imaarat kai tarah se istemaal kii gai he. 19veen shatabdi kii shuruyaat main yahaan carch tha, 1940 main cinema haul bana or aaj is imaarat main sthaneey club he.
 prasiddh gurudwara ber sahib kapoorthala kii sultanpur lodhi tehsil main sthit he. sikh dharm ke anuyaayiyon ke liye is sthaan kaa mahatv bahut adhik he. yahi vah sthaan he jahaan sikhon ke pehle guru, guru nanak dev ne apni jindagi ke 14 saal bitaye or yahi par been naamak choti si nadi main snaan karte hue unhen divya gyaan praapt hua tha. is sthaan kaa naam ber ke ek ped ke naam par rakhaa gaya he jiske bare main mana jaataa he ki ise swayan guru nanak ji ne lagaaya tha or isi ke neeche sikh dhrm kaa pehla mul mantra diya tha.
 is mandir kaa nirmaan sardaar fateh singh ke shasankaal main hua tha. aitihaasik mahatv rakhane vaale is mandir main anek adbhut pratimaayein rakhi gai hai. yah bharat kaa doosraa aisa mandir he jahaan surya bhagwaan kii pratimaa par har subah suraj kii kirane sidhi padati hai. mandir parisar main or bhi anek mandir hai jo vibhinn devi-devataaon ko samarpit hai. is mandir ke bare main ek anya adbhut baat yah he ki yahaan ke mukhya rajat dwaar se ek bhakt sabhi murtiyon ko pranaam kar saktaa he. panj mandir main brahmaji kii durlabh pratimaa bhi dekhi ja sakati hai.
 railway coach kii badhati maang ko puraa karne ke liye punjab ke kapoorthala sthit rail coach factory ke liye 170 karod rupee nivesh vaali vistaar yojana taiyaar kii gai he.
 rail mantralay ne utpaadan kshamta ko 1,000 coach se badhaakar 1,500 coach prativarsh karne kii yojana banaai he. iske liye 92 karod rupee kaa nivesh kiya jayega. iske alaava 68 karod rupee ke nivesh se ek pahiyaa vinirmaan factory lagane kaa faisla bhi kiya gaya he. yah factory ek saal main 1,900 coach ke liye pahiyon kaa vinirmaan karegi. iske alaava digaayan main utkrushtataa hasil karne ke liye alag se 10 karod rupee nivesh kiye jaaenge.
