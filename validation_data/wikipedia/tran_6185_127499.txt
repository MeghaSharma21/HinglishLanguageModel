 mahamahopadhyaay pandit bapudev shastri kaashi sanskrit kalege ke jyotish ke mukhya adhyaaapak the. ve pratham brahman the jo bhartiya evam paashchaatya khagoliki dono ke professor bane. ye prayag tathaa kalakatta vishvavidhyalayon ke parishad tathaa ireland or great britain kii royal sosayadi ke sammanit sadsy the. inhen mahamahopadhyaay kii oopaadhi mili thi. 
 bapudev shastri kaa janm shaq san 1743 main maharashtra ke ahmednagar main godavari nadi ke kinare 'tonke' naamak gaanv main ek brahman parivaar main hua tha. unki aarmabhik shiksha nagpur ke marathi vidhyalay main hui jahaan unhone ankaganit evam bijganit kii shiksha pai. dhundiraj mishra se unhone bijganit tathaa leelavati granth sikhi. unki pratibha se prabhavit hokar british raajnneetik agent lanslot wilkinson ne sihor ke sanskrit vidhyalay main unka namakan kara diya jahaan unhone siddhantashiromani, yulidia jyamiti tathaa europea vigyaan kii shiksha pai. pandit sevaraam or swayan wilkinson unke shikshak the. san 1842 main shastri ji rajkeey sanskrit mahavidhyalay varanasi main niyukt hue jahaan ve rekhganit padhaane lage.
 unhone bhaskaracharya ke siddhantashiromani kaa anuwaad kiya or ise banaras main 1891 main prakashit kiya. wilkinson ke saath milakar unhone 'suryasiddhanth or ain ancient system of estronaumi' naamak granth rachaa jo suryasiddhanth kaa angreji anuwaad he. inkaa driksiddh panchang aaj bhi varanasey sanskrit vishvavidhyalay se prakashit hota he.
