 maapan ke sandarbh main maatrak yaa ikai kisi bhautik raashi kii ek nishchit maatra ko kehete hai jo paripati yaa/or niyam dwaara paribhaashik evam sweekrut kii gai ho tathaa jo us bhautik raashi ke maapan ke liye maanak ke rup main prayukt hoti ho. us bhautik raashi kii koi bhi anya maatra is 'ikai' ke ek gunak ke rup main vyakt kii jaati he. 
 udhaaharan ke liye lambaai ek bhautik raashi he. 'meter' lambaai kaa maatrak he jo ek nishchit purvanirdhaarit duuri ke baraabar hota he. jab ham kehete hai ki amuk duuri '47 meter' he to iskaa arth he ki ukt duuri 1 meter ke 47 guna he.
 pracheen kaal se hi maatrakon kii paribhaasha karnaa, un par sahmati karnaa, unka vyaavhaarik upyog karnaa aadi kii bahut mahatvapurn bhoomika rahi he. vibhinn sthanon evam kaalon main maatrakon kii vibhinn pranaliyaan hona ek saamaanya baat thi. kintu ab ek vaishvik maanak pranali astitv main he jise 'antararashtriya maatrak pranali' ) kehete hai.
