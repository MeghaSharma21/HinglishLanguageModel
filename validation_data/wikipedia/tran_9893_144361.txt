 sanrachanaatmak jeevigyaan aanvik jeevigyaan, jeevarsayan or jaivbhautikee kii ek shakha he jisamen protein jaise vrihadanuon ke dhaanchon kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. isamen in dhaanchon kii utpatti kaa kaarnon or dhaanchon main parivartan se un anuon kii kriyaaon par prabhaav kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. jeevigyaan main yah vrihadanu bahut mahatv rakhte hai kyonki koshikaon ke adhiktar kaary vahi karte hai or in kaaryon ke liye unka vishesh aakaaron va dhaanchon ko apana lenaa atyant zaruri hota he.
