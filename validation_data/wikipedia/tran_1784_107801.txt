 kris jordan  ek antarrashtriya str ke cricket khilaadi he jo england kii rashtriya cricket team ke liye khelte hai.
 kris jordan kaa janm to westeindies ke barbados shahar main hua tha lekin unhone westeindies ke bajaay england cricket team ke liye khelnaa pasand kiya.
 inhone apane ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket karier kii shuruyaat australia team ke khilaaf 16 sitambar 2013 ko kii thi jabaki test cricket karier kii shuruyaat 12 june 2014 ko srilanka cricket team ke khilaaf khelkar kii thi.
 jordan mukhya rup se daayen haath se tej gati se gendbaaji karte hai. jabaki daayen hi haath se ballebaaji bhi karte hai lekin mukhya rup se gendbaaj kii bhoomika nibhaate hai.
