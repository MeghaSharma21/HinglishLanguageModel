 naga bharat kii ek pramukh janajati hai. inkaa nivas kshetra bharat ke purvottar kshetra va myanmar ke pashchimottar kshetra main he. bharat main ye nagaland rajya main bahusankhyak he. 2012 main yahan par inaki sankhya 17 lakh darj kii gayi. iske alaava ye manipur, assam, arunachal pradesh main bhi inaki acchhee khaasi jansankhya he.
 vahi myanmar main ye janajati kuch kshetron main bahusankhyak he. vahaan par inke ilaakon ko swytt kshetra ghoshit kiya gaya he jahaan kaa prashasan ye khud hi sanbhaalate hai. in ilaakon ko naga swytt kshetra kaha jaataa he.
 british kaal se pehle tak inkaa bahari duniya se koi bhi sampark nahin tha. 1828 tak assam british shashan ke kabje main aa gaya. parantu naga janajati lagaataar assam ke simavarti kshetron main akraman karti rahati. 1845 main naga saradaaron ke saath angejon kaa samjhauta hua jiske tahat naga akraman band karne par raji ho gaye. lekin uske baad samjhaute kaa ulanghan hota rahaa.
 1851 main angejon ne is kshetra main apni sena tukdiyaan tainaat kar di. parantu 1878 main angami naagaon ne angreji campon par phir se chaape maare. iskaa jawaab angrejon ne drudhtapurvak diya or kai naga gaavon main aag laga di.
 19 ve shatabdi ke ant main yahan isai mishnariyaan ai or bahut se naagaon kaa dharm parivartan kiya. atah in mishnariyon ne inke rahan sahan ko prabhavit kiya. atah mishnariyon ne inke saamaajik or saanskrithik rup ko badal daalaa.
 aaj lagbhag 95% naga isai he. 
 angami japu phijo ne naagaon ke sangharsh ke liye naga naishanal council kaa gathan kiya jiskaa uddeshy alag naga rajya tha. june 1947 main naga naishanal council kaa angrajon se 9 sutri samjhauta hua parantu anekon naagaon ne iskaa virodh kiya.
 british raaj ke antim din 14 agast 1947 ko naga naishanal council ne nagaland kii swatantraaa ghoshit kar di. may 1951 main naneka ne daawa kiya ki alag nagaland ke liye uske dwaara karaaye gaye janamat - sangrah main 99% log uske saath he. par 1952 main bharat sarkaar ne uskaa daawa kaarij kar diya. naga ab bharat ke khilaaf gorilla movement chalaane lage. parantu bharat sarkaar ne uskaa vidroh dabaa diya. baad main phijo landan bhaag gaya or vahaan se yah abhiyaan apni mrityu tak chalaaya.
 inaki pramukh bhashaa kuki-chin-naga bhashaayen he. iske alaava inhone creole bhashaa kaa bhi vikaas kiya jiskaa ye prayog aapas main baatcheet karne main karte hai. 
 paaramparik rup se naga gaavon main rahete hai. inkaa mukhya dhandha shikaar he. ye junglee jaanwaron kaa shikaar karte hai. ye apane ghar baas se banaate hai. ye yoddha janajati he. inaki veerta ko hi dekh parakhkar inaki shaadi hoti he.
 striyon kii dasha bhi is jati kaaphi acchhee he. inhen purna swatantraaa praapt he.
 lagbhag 95% naga isai he. kuch naga samooh sarvatmavaadi bhi he.
