 itbayat dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh ke kagayan ghati prashasnic kshetra ke baatanes dweepasmooh va praant kaa sabase bada dweep he. yah philipinoz kii prashasan pranali main nagarpalika kaa darja rakhata he. itbayat philipinoz kii uttartam nagarpalika he or taiwan ke dakshini chor se keval 200 kimi kii samudri duuri rakhata he. sthaneey log itbayat bhashaa bolte hai.
