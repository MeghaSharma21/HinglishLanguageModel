 
 jazan praant yaa jizan praant, jise aupchaarik arabi main mintaqaah​ jazan kehete hai, saudi arab ke dakshinpaschim kone main laal sagar ke tat par sthit ek praant he. yah thik yaman se uttar main he or is praant kii us desh ke saath antarrashtriya seemaa bhi lagati he. jazan praant main laal sagar main sthit 100 se adhik dweep bhi aate hai, jinamein se farsaan dweep samooh saudi arab kaa sarvapratham sangrakshit kshetra he. yahan arabi ghazal or europe se sardiyon main aani vaali bahut si pakshiyon kii naslein milti hai. yah praant saudi arab kaa doosraa sabase chota praant he.
 jazan praant ke teen mukhya bhaag hai:
 is praant main do mrit jwalamukhi bhi hai.
 jazan ke unche ilaaqon main mausam aseer praant jaisaa he jabaki iske tatavarti ilaaqon main mausam bahut garm he. in tateey kshetron main july main taapmaana 40 °centegrade or janvari main 31 °centegrade tak jaataa he or pani sameep hone se hawa main bahut nami rahati he, jis se yahan kaa vaataavaran kaafii apriy mana jaataa he.
 jazan praant 14 zilon main banta hua he:
 uparalikhit kuch praanton ke naam main bindu-vaale 'ji' ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bindu-rahit 'tha' se zaraa alag he or phank you vaale 'ji' ke uccharan se miltaa julata he.
