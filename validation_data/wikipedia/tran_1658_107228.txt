 wiliam gilbert "dablyoo ji" gres angrej shaukia cricketer the jo is khel ke vikaas main ahm yogadan rakhte hai or jinhen vyapak rup se iske sabase bade khilaadi main se ek mana jaataa he. unhone 1865 se 1908 tak ricard 44 satr ke liye pratham shrenee cricket khelaa, jiske dauraan unhone england, glustershire, maricebon cricket club, or kai anya tiimon kii captaini kii.
 unke parivaar main cricket kaaphi prasiddh tha. 1880 ke ek test match main wo apane bade bhai i em gres or chote bhai fred gres ke saath khele. yah pehla maamla tha ki teen bhai test cricket main ek saath khele the. unka takneekee nayaapan or vishaal prabhaav ek sthaayee viraasat chhod gaya he. harfanmaulaa ke taur par vah ballebaaji, gendbaaji or kshetrarakshan ke sabhi aavashyak kaushal main utkrisht pradarshan karte the, lekin yah unki ballebaaji he jiske liye vah sabase prasiddh he. unhen aadhunik ballebaaji kaa avishkaarak mana jaataa he.
 inhone 1879 main chikitsak ke rup main arhata praapt kiya tha. unke chikitsa peshey kii vajah se, vah shaukia cricketer the. par ye darja nammaatr kaa tha, kaha jaataa he ki unhone cricket kii gatividhiyon se kisi bhi peshewar cricketer ke mukaable adhik paisa banaaya. apane 22 test match ke jivan kaal main gres ne 32.29 ke ausat se 1098 ran banaae jisamen unhone do shatak lagaae tathaa 26.22 ke ausat se kul 9 wicket liye. pratham shrenee cricket main gres ne 39.55 ke ausat se 54,896 ran banaae or 126 shatak lagaae. saath hi unhone 17.52 ke ausat se 2876 wicket bhi hasil kiye.
