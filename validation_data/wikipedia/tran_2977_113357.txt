 liyaoning sahaayta·soochna uttarpurvi janavaadee ganatantr china main sthit ek praant he. iskaa naam praant se hokar bahane vaali liyaao nadi ke naam ko 'ning' shabd ke saath jodakar banaaya gaya he. is praant kii sthapana san 1907 main 'fengatiyan' naam se kii gai thi lekin 1929 main naam badalakar 'liyaoning' kar diya gaya. liyaoning kii rajdhani shenyang shahar he.
