 neeper nadi europe kii ek pramukh nadi he, jo rus ke somolensk naamak sthaan ke paas se nikal kar rus, belarus or ukraine main bahane ke baad kaala sagar me girti he. yah belarus or ukraine kii sabase lambii nadi he or puure europe kii nadiyon main lambaai ke maamle main iskaa chauthaa sthaan he. 
 kul 2,145 kimi or 2,201 kimi ke bich lambaai vaali is nadi ke apvaah basin kaa aakaar 504,000 varg kilometre he.
 yah nadi, iske upar sthit jalavidyut kendron ke liye jaani jaati he. neeper nadi ek mahatvapurn jalmaarg bhi he. neeper-bag nahar dwaara yah europe ke anya nahari jalmargon se bhi judi he. jalmaarg ke rup main is nadi kaa mahatv belarus or ukraine ke liye he.
