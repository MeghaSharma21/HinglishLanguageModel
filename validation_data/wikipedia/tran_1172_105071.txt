 san 1939 i. main is sabha kii sthapana ke. vasudevan pille ne kii. 
 kerala rajya main rashtrabhasha hindi kaa prachaar-prasar karne ke liye hindi kii choti-badi sabhi parikshaon ko sanchalit karnaa, hindi kii pustaken evam patr-patrikas prakashit karnaa tathaa hindi naatakon ko abhinit karnaa is sabha ke mukhya uddeshy rahe hai. san 1948 i. se 'kerala hindi prachaar sabha' swatantra rup se apni parikshaayen hindi prathama, hindi pravesh, hindi bhushan or saahityacharya sanchalit karti he. hindi, malayalam or tamil bhashaaon main saamanjasy sthapana karne ke prayojan se 'rashtravani' naam kii ek tribhaasha saptahik patrika kaa prakaashan kiya gaya. iske atirikt 'kerala jyoti' naamak ek masik patrika kaa prakaashan bhi ho rahaa he. sabha bhawan main ek kendriya hindi mahavidhyalay kaary kar rahaa he. sabha kaa prakaashan vibhaag bhi sucharu rup se chal rahaa he.
