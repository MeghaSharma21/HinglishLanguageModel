 rashtriya koshika vigyaan kendra, pune pashu cell sanvardhan ke rashtriya aadhaan ke rup main sthapit kiya gaya tha. iske paas pashu utak sanvardhan main janashakti vikaas kaaryakramon kaa aayojan karne, shordhakattaaon or jaivaracinic vigyaan main sansthaanon ko avasanrachanaatmak suvidhaayen pradaan karne or sambandhit kshetron main state-aaf-di-art anusandhan va vikaas kaa aayojan karne kaa adhidesh bhi he.
 
 yah suvidha desh main kai anusandhan sansthaanon ko kai cell sanvardhanon or vibhinn cell linon ko pradaar kar rahi he. hibridomas sahit 25 vibhinn prajatiyon se praapt ekatrit sanvardhan kii sankhya 1127 tak batch gai he.
 
 vartmaan main santhaan nimnlikhit kshetron main moolbhoot anusandhan kaa kaary kar rahaa he. bonmaro ke liye cryo sangrakshan praudyogiki, jalan navy or vitiligo ke maamalon main pratyaropan ke liye jaev-equalant skin vyapaarik rup se mahatvapurn invartbreats or vartebrates se cell culture kaa vikaas, sateria, dijitata antigen ke aijiji4 antibades ke liye sthanik or gaiyr-sthanik sera, melanoma onkogenes kii pahchaan karnaa, ipithelial mesunchaymul transshin, maanav intastinan mesunchaumal koshikaon ke saath anvik or cellular interekashgn lipopali sachride par invitro adhyayan, hemojayan kaa adhyayan or feetsis par protein ultrishan kaa prabhaav jinaki maatra garstesan ke poorak samay lo protein dit par nirbhar hoti he.
 
 santhaan vaigyanik aadhaar ke srijan karne main laga hua he or fundamental anusandhan or tannijibal public upyogita ke liye praapt samaadhaanon kii khoj main apni gatividhiyaan kendrit kar rahaa he. vaigyanik samudaay ko apni sevaayen pradaan karke kendra ne moolbhoot anusandhana main badi acchhee pragati kii he.
