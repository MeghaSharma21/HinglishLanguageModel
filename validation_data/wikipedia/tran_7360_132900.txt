 
 28 april, 2007 ke anusarrot: 
 kapildev raamalaal nikhanj bharat ke purv cricket khilaadi hai. bharat ke sarvshereshth cricket khilaadiyon main unki gananaa hoti he. ve bhartiya cricket ke captain ke pad par rah chuke hai. 1983 ke cricket vishwacap main ve bhartiya cricket team ke captain the or unke netritv main team ne vishwacap jeetne kaa gaurav praapt kiya. ve visden dwaara varsh 2002 main 'sadi ke bhartiya cricketer' chune gayi. ve 10 maah ke liye bhartiya cricket team ke prashikshak bhi rahe the.
 kapildev kaa janm chandigarh main hua. unka vivaah romi bhaatia se san 1980 main hua. unki beti, amiya dev kaa janm 16 janvari, 1996 ko hua.
 Kapil Dev ka another name kya h
 unhone apane cricket karir kii shuruyaat 1975 main haryana kii ore se payaab ke virudh gharelu cricket se kari. ve ek ol rounder the joki daaye haath se ballebaazi ev tej gindbaazi bhi karte the. unka antar-rashtriya vyavsaay pakistan ke virudh faslabad main 16 oktober 1978 ko hua. halanki yah daura unke liye kuch acha na rahaa, parantu aane vaale samay main unhone apane unda pradarshan se bhartiya cricket team main apni jagah sunishchit kar lee. shri-lanka ke virudh 1982-83 main unhone apni captaini main pratham pravesh kiya. jab unhe vishwa cap kii captaini kaa avasar mila to ve ek ausat khiladi hi the, parantu apane aashcharya-janak pradarshn se tathaa apni team ke sahyog se bharat ko pehla vishwa cap jitayaa or rato-raat hi bhartiya itihaas kaa chamakta sitara ban gayi. mohammag azahruddin kii captaini main unhone 1992 ke vishwa cap main apana aakhri antar-rashtria khel khelaa. unhone apane cricket vyavsaay main ek divasiy cricket main 225 or test cricket main 131 match khele. ek divasiy cricket main unhone 23.79 kii ausat se 3783 ran banaaye tathaa test cricket main unhone 31.05 kii ausat se 5248 ran banaaye. gindbaazi karte hue unhone ek divasiy tathaa test cricket main 253 tathaa 434 wicket liye kramash:. 1983 ke vishwa cap main zimbabwe ke virudh unki 175 ran kii pari yaadgaar rahegi jiski badaulat bharat vah match jeeta. unhone ek divasiy cricket main 1 or test criket main 8 shatak lagaayi he.
 kapil dev ne 1994 main antar-rashtriya cricket ko alvida kah diya. 1999 main unhe bhartiya cricket team kaa coach chunaa gaya. unki avadhi ke dauraan bharat kaa pradarshan khaas na rahaa jisme ve keval ek test match jite or ausatrelia or south africa ke virudh do bady series haare. manoj prabhakar dwaara sattebaazi main fasaaye jane ke baad unhone apane coach ke pad ko tyaag diya. 2005 main unhone khushi naamak ek rashtriya sarkari sanghathan kii sthapna kii. abhi ve uske adhyaksh he. khushi dilli main kam visheshadhikrut baccho ke liye teen vidhyalay chalaati he. 24 sitambar 2008 ko unhone bhartiya pradeshik sena main bhaag liya or unhe leftinent karnal ke rup main chunaa gaya.

 2005 main kapil dev ne zikaum electronics main 5% daav liya. chandigarh ke capils ileven restaurant ke ve maalik he. unhone iqbaal, chaiyn khuli kii main khuli tathaa mujhase shaadi karogi jaisi filmo main chote kirdaar bhi nibhaae hai.
