 tapiya neutron riactor un riactaron ko kehete hai jinaki vikhandan shrunkhlaa abhikriya ke peeche tapiya yaa mand neutranon kaa haath hota he. yahan 'tapiya' kaa arth 'garam' nahin he balki iskaa arth yah he ki vah neutron aasapaas ke madhyam se tapiya samya main he. nabhikeey vikhandan ke turant baad jo neutron paidaa hote hai unki urja bahut adhik hoti hai or unhen 'drut neutron' kehete hai. ye neutron apane aasapaas se apratyasth sanghatt karke apni urja kaa kshay kar dete hai or tab inhen 'tapiya neutron' kaha jaataa he.
