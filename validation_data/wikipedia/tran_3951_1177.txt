 sandhipad praani jagat kaa sabase bada sangh he. prithvi par sandhipad kii lagbhag do tihaai jatiyaan hai, isamen keet bhi sammilit hai. inkaa sharir sir, vaksh or udar main banta rahata he. sharir ke chaaron ore ek khol jaisi rachana milti he. praayah sabhi khandon ke parshv kii ore ek sandhiyukt shakhang hote hai. sir par do sanyukt netra hote hai. ye jantu ekalingi hote hai or jal tathaa sthal donon sthanon par milte hai. tilchatta, machhar, makkhi, goger, jhinga, kekada aadi is sangh ke pramukh jantu hai.
