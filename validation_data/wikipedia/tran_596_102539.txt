 jaladapada rashtriya udyaan jise pehle jaladapada vanyajeev abhayaarany bhi kehete the, pashchim bengal ke alipuradwar up-mandal ke antargat jalpaigudi zile main sthit ek rashtriya udyaan he, jo ki torasa nadi ke kinare he. yah ilaaka vastutah purvi himalay kaa tarai kshetra he. is udyaan kii samudr sataha se ausatan oonchaai 61 me. he or yah 261.51 varg kilometre main failaa hua he. isamen vistrut ghaas ke maidan hai or yah naalon or vanyakshetron se bharaa hua he. san 1941 main ise rajya ke vanaspati tathaa jeevon ko bachaane ke liye abhayaarany ghoshit kiya gaya tha jiske antargat bhartiya ganda bhi tha or pashchim bengal main isaki sabase badi sankhya yaheen maujuud he.
