 bharat sevak samaaj bharat sarkaar ke yojana ayog dwaara pryojit vikaas sanstha he. isaki sthapana yojana ayog dwaara janasahyog praapt karne ke liye san 1951 main kii gai, rashtriya salaahkaar samiti kii siforeeshon ke anusaar 12 agast 1952 main kii gai thi.
 iske pramukh uddeshy ye hai : 
 18 varsh kaa har aisa vyakti iskaa sadsy ho saktaa he, jo saptaah main kam se kam do ghante swechha se sevakaarya ke liye de sake. jinhone apana puraa samay sanstha kii pravritiyon ke liye samarpit kar diya ho, ve iske aaajeevan sadsy kehalaate hai.
 aisi swechhasevi sansthaayen jo suchanaatmak yaa samaajakalyaan ke kaaryo main lagi hon, isaki sanstha sadsy ho sakati hai.
 aisa koi bhi vyakti, jo samaaj kaa saadhaaran sadsy ho or samaaj kii pravritiyon athwa aarthik rup main ni:swaarth sahyog detaa ho, iskaa sahaayak sadsy ho saktaa he. sadsyta ke sambandh main ek pratibandh yah he ki jo vyakti, hinsa main vishwaas karata ho yaa samaaj kaa upyog vyaktigat athwa raajnneetik kshetra main karata ho vah is sanstha kaa sadsy nahin ho saktaa.
 bharat sevak aise sadsy ho sakate hai, jinhen saadhaaran sadsy nishchit vyavastha ke anusaar chun lete hai.
 samaaj kii niti nirdhaarit karne kaa kaam bharat sevak sabha karti he. iske ek tihaai sadsy bharat sevak sangh dwaara, ek tihaai sadsy bharat sevak sangh ke sadasyon ke atirikt sabhapati dwaara manonith kiye jate hai. bharat sevak sangh ke sadasyon kaa chunaav bharat sevak karte hai. isn sangh kii baithak varsh main ek baar hoti he.
 samaaj ke din prati din ke kaaryo kaa sanchaalan kendriya pradhan mandal karata he. isamen nau sadsy hote hai, jinamein do sadsy samaaj ke trustrion dwaara manonith hote hai.
 isi tarah kendriya sanghathan ke antargat pradesh, rajya, jila, prakhand, nagar, gram tathaa muhallon main bhi shakhaon kaa sanghathan hota he.
 kaaryakshetra hri lokseva ke liye kaaryakartaaon kaa prashikshan, janajagran tathaa samaaj kalyaan sambandhi kaary, gandi bastiyon kaa sudhaar, parivaar niyojan aadi vividh kaary is sanstha ke kaaryakshetra ke antargat aate hai.
 lokkarya kaa kaaryakshetra janajagran kii prakriya puri hone par shuru hota he. janakalyaan ke vyapak kaaryakramon main janasahyog praapt karnaa hi iskaa mukhya uddeshy he. saare desh main samaaj ke sabhi vibhaagon ke sakriy kaaryakartaaon ke prashikshan ke liye is vibhaag dwaara do prashikshan shivi, ek dilli tathaa ek trivendrum main chalaae ja rahe hai. bharat sevak dal kaa prashikshan bhi isi vibhaag ke antargat hota he.
 janajagran ke kaary main vichaargoshtiyon kaa aayojan, yojana soochna kendron kaa sanchaalan, buletinon, brosharon tathaa choti pustikaon ke jariye yojana kaa prachaar karnaa or yojana-prachaar-saptahon kaa aayojan karnaa aadi kaam he.
 samaaj kalyaan ke kaaryakshetra main ranbaseron kaa sanchaalan, upnagar sudhaar kaarykram or mahila-bal-kalyaan ke kaarykram aate hai. naagarik kshetra main aavashyak vastuon ke mulyon kii vruddhi rokane kaa kaam bhi ab iske kaaryakshetra main aa gaya he.
 gandi bastiyon ke sudhaar ke kaaryakshetra main swatchta-safai-abhiyaan, naagarik niyamon kii shiksha ke sivaa sakshartaa kakshaayen tathaa mahila shilp kakshaayen chalaanaa aadi bhi hai.
 iskaa gathan san 1955 main is aadhaar par kiya gaya tha ki rashtriya dhan kii bachat kii ja sake or sarkari theke ke kaamon main jo der or andher hota he, use roka ja sake. kosi tatbandh, shahdara kaa jamuna band, chambal band, nagarjun sagar nahar, dilli kii antarrashtriya pradarshaniyon, ke anek mandalon kaa nirmaan, hawaii addon, sadakon tathaa bhawanon kaa nirmaan ab tak is vibhaag ne kiya he.
 yuvak evam shram shivi desh bhar main gram yuvakon or vidhyaarthiyon ke pakshik shivi lagaataa he or shivi main kiye gaye shramdan kaaryo kaa moolyankan karata he. is vibhaag main ab praathmik chikitsa, grih vigyaan, shaareerik prashikshan evam "adhik ann upjaao andolan" shaamil kiya ja chukaa he. parivaar niyojan bhi yuvak or shramshivi ke antargat he, par isaki apni alag kaaryakarini he. parivaar-niyojan-shiviron kaa mukhya sanchaalak bhi pradeshik shivi sanchaalak hi hota he.
 swasthya evam swatchta abhiyaan main prati varsh greeshmakaleen evam sharadkalin swasthya saptaah manaaya jaataa he. 2 oktober ko rashtriya swatchta diwas or prati maas ke antim ravivaar ko swatchta abhiyaan bhi kiya jaataa he.
 prashikshan shivi ke do centre hai - ek dilli ke sameep ashok bihar main or doosraa he kerala ke trivendrum nagar main. in shiviron main bharat sevak samaaj ke sabhi vibhaagon main kaam karanevaale tathaa anya swechhasevi sansthaaon ke kaaryakarta bhi prashikshit kiye jate hai.
 prakaashan vibhaag samaaj se sambandhit sahitya prakashit karata he. iske saath bharat sevak masik patr hindi tathaa angreji main prakashit karata he. iske saath bharat sevak masik patr hindi tathaa angreji main prakashit karata he. isaki ek kaarysmiti he, jisamen sabhapati, upasabhapati, mantri or kuch namjad sadsy hote hai. chah prantiy bhashaaon main bulatin nikaale jate hai.
 yogasana kaa kaary asana or pranyam kaa janata main vyapak prachaar karata he. isane 64 saral asana kaa chunaav kiya he, jinke prachaar ke liye san 1958 main ek a. bha. yogasana samiti bana di gai. desh ke praaia: sabhi bade shaharon main isaki kakshaayen lagati hai.
 san 1962 main iskaa gathan hua. desh ke kuch chune hue audyogic kshetron main 
  mulyon kii jaanch, 
  sahakaari upabhokta bhandaron kii sthapana, 
  vishuddh khaadya padaartho kaa utpaadan, 
  upabhoktaaon ko prashikshit kar unamem nirodh shakti paidaa karnaa, 
  mulya niyantran ke liye khudara thok vyapaariyon kaa sanghathan aadi kaary karne kii yojana he.
 chini akraman ke baad iskaa gathan hua he. sainik pariwaron ko sahaayta, janata ke naitik bal ko tikaae rakhana, pratiraksha ke liye nirmaan ikai kaa gathan, moolyavriddhi kii roak, bachat abhiyaan swechha-sevi-sansthaaon se sahyog aadi kaary hai, jinhen ab samaaj ke uparyukt vibhaagon main mila diya gaya he.
 san 1964 main sabase pratham dilli me isaki shakha khuli. logon main sadachaar nirmaan kar sarkari prashasan main vyaapt bhrashtachar ko mitaanaa hi iskaa mukhya uddeshy he.tatkaaleen bharat ke grah evam shram rojgaar /yojana mantri shradey shri gulzaarilal nanda ji ne bharat ke grah mantri ke rup main yah anuthi yojana desh main swachhik jaagrukta ko lekar armbh kii thi jiskaa uddeshy bhrshtachar ko jad se samaapt karne main rajnatik sadachaar kaa udai ho sake. bharat sarkaar kaa harkadam ve bharstachar nivaaran main shaman se chaahate the daman se nahi. yah pahal grahamantri nanda ne laal bahadur shastri ji ke pradhanmantri kaarykaal main safal kar dikhaai thi jisaki saraahna khud pradhanmantri ke rup main shastri ji ne kii thi. yah baat khud gulzaarilal nanda foundation ke chairmen krishnaraj arun ne bharat ratna nanda ke shishy country and politics patrika main varisht patrkaar sampadak ke rup main kahi he.
 iskaa muloddeshy yahi he ki iske madhyam se nishthavan, sevabhaavvaale or nisswarth aise samajasevak taiyaar kiye jaaen, jo apana sara jivan samajaseva main laga den or unke jivan kii paanchon aavashyaktaaon kii purti unheen ashramon ke madhyam se ho.
 vyas samaaj ke gathan kaa mukhya uddeshy katha kirtankaron ke madhyam se gaanv main janchetana laana or logon main charitranirmaan kii bhaavana bharna he. 1960 main prayag ke kumbh meley ke avasar par pehla, 1961-62 main bombai main doosraa or 1962-63 main haridwar main teesraa sammelan kiya gaya. haridwar main ek 40 din kaa prashikshan shivi bhi lagaaya gaya tha, jisamen 53 katha-kirthan-kaaron ko prashikshit kiya gaya.
 samaaj ke sakriy kaaryakartaaon kii sankhya 50,000 he, jinamein puraa samay denevaale kaaryakarta 2,000 hai, rajyon kii shakhaayen 20, jila shakhaayen 300, gram samitiyaan 3,800 hai. 1964 tak bharat sevak dal ke sadsy 30,000, prashikshit sadsy 12,000, gandi basti sudhaar centre 39, sampark kiye gaye parivaar aath lakh, samaaj kalyaan vistaar centre 27, labhanvit parivaar 13,500 tathaa shram seva shivi 9504 the. idhar in sansthaaon main or bhi vistaar hua he.
