 khumbu mount everest kii nepali disha par sthit purvottar nepal kaa ek kshetra he. yah solukhumbu jila kaa hissaa he, jo sagarmatha anchal ki antargat he. khumbu mukhya-khumbu ke teen upakshetra main se ek he vishesh rup se thulung or himalay kii sherpa niptaan kaa, anya do upakshetra main solu or baraag he. khumbu main namche bazaar shahar or gavonmen khumjub, pampochhe, forche ityadi shamil he. ek prasiddh buddha math tempochhe bhi khumbu kshetra main sthit he.
 khumbu kii oonchai, prithvi par sab se ooncha jagah 8,848 meter mount everest shikhar sammelan ke liye 3,300 meter se chalata he. khumbu kshetra donon sagarmaatha rashtriya udyaan or sagarmatha neshnal park bafer jone, lukalha or monjo ke bich main shaamil he. khumbu ek glacier 500,000 saal pehle kaa maananaa tha kii pichley mahaan himyug parinaam ho saktaa he. lonly planet ne duniya kii yatra karne main chatha sabase acha kshetra main khumbu kshetra sthaan diya gaya he.
