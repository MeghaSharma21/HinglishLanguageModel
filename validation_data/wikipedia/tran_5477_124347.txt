 that athwa thaat hindustani shastreey sangeet main raagon ke vibhaajan kii paddati he. saptak ke 12 swaron main se 7 krmanusaar mukhya swaron ke us samudaay ko thaat yaa that kehete hai jisase raag kii utpatti hoti he. that ko mail bhi kaha jaataa he. iskaa prachlan pan bhatkhede ji ne prarambh kiya. hindi main 'thaat' or marathi main ise 'that' kehete hai. unhone das thaton ke antargat prachlit sabhi raagon ko sammilit kiya. vartmaan samay main raag vargeekaran kii yahi paddati prachlit he. 
 that ke kuch lakshan maane gayi hai- 
 pan. bhatkhede ke dwaara vargeekrut das thaton ke naam is prakaar hai-
 that-raag paddati main swar-samya kaa bahut adhik dhyaan rakhaa gaya he. is paddati ke anusaar ek that ke antargat unheen raagon ko rakhaa gaya he jin ke swaron main adhik samanta he. udaahranarth--kalyaan that main madhyam teevr lagata he, isilye is that se jitne bhi raag utpann hon un sabhi main madhyam teevr avashya lagegaa. isi prakaar kaaphi that main gandhar or nishad swar komal lagate hai, isaliye is that ne jitne bhi raag utpann honge un sabhi main gandhar or nishad komal avashya lagenge.
