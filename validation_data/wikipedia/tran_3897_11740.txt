 bharat vikaas sangam ek sammelan he jisaki kalpana ke en govindacharya ne kii thi. iske sanyojak samajasevi evam rajyasabha ke saansad vasav raaj patil hai.
 shri govindacharya kaa maananaa he ki desh ke saamne aaj jo samasyaayein hai, unase nipatane ke liye bauddhik, rachanaatmak or andolanaatmak str par kaam karne kii jarurat he. rachanaatmak kaaryon ke liye bharat vikaas sangam kaa gathan kiya gaya. is sanghathan main raajnneetik kaaryakartaaon ko dur rakhane kii lagaataar koshish kii gai.
