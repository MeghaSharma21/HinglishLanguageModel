 pakistan ke cricket coach bob woolmer kaa janm bharat ke kanpur shahar main 14 may 1948 ko hua tha. daayen haath se ballebaazi or madhyam gati kii gendbaazi karne vaale robert andrew woolmer ne 1975 main england kii team se apane test cricket jivan kii shuruyaat kii thi.
 bab woolmer kaa nidhan 18 march 2007 ko west indies main hua.
 bob woolmar kaaphi vikhyath or shaayad sabase zyada prasiddh kirket coach the. is vazah se vah do tiimon, dakshin africa evam pakistan ke coach bane or do anya tiimon ne unko apana coach banane kii koshish kii. saath hi vah ek behtareen khilaadi bhi the jinako kairi paikar ne world series cricket ke liye sin kiya.
