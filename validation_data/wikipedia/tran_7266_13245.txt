 takneekee vastra un vastron ko kehete hai jinka nirmaan aise uddeshyon ke liye kiya jaataa he jinke nirmaan main soundarya gaun hota he or kaary-sampaadan mukhya mudda hota he. yah ek ubharta hua kshetra he. takneekee vastron kaa bahut se anya udyogon main upyog hota he.
 vaahanon main upyog main aane vaale vastra, chikitsa main upyog kiye jane vaale vastra, bhu-vasan, krishiwasan, suraksha vastra aadi takneekee vastra ke kuchh udhaaharan hai.
