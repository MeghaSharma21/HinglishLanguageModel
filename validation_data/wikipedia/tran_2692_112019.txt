 brihatsamhita varahamihir dwaara 6thi shatabdi sanskrit main rachit ek vishwakosh he jisamen maanav ruchi ke vividh vishyon par likhaa gaya he. isamen khagolashastra, grahon kii gati, grahan, varsha, badal, vaastushastra, fasalon kii vruddhi, itranirmaan, lagn, paariwarik sambandh, ratna, moti evam karmkaandon kaa varnan he. 
 vrihatsamhita main 106 adhyay hai. yah apane mahaan sankalan ke liye prasiddh he.
