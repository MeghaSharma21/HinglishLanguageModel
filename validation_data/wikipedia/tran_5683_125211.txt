 thakur ramsingh rashtriya swayamsevak sangh ke varisht prachaarak evam itihaas sankalan samiti ke sansthaapak sadsy the.
 akhil bhartiya itihaas-sankalan yojana ke sansthaapak-adhyaksh thakur ramsingh ji kaa janm 16 farvari 1915 ko himachal pradesh ke gram jhandvi, tehsil bhonraj, jila hamirpur main hua tha. aapke pita kaa naam shri thakur bhagasingh evam mata kaa naam shrimati nihatu devi tha. thakur ramsingh 5 bhai-bahan the. aapaki primary shiksha primary paathshaala main hui. san 1935 main unhone rajput hiskool, bhorwaha se pratham shrenee main matriculation kii pariksha uteern kii tathaa san 1938 main diavi kalege se intermediat evam 1940 main sanathan dharm kalege se bie kii pariksha uteern kii. san 1942 main unhone lahore vishvavidhyalay ke epasi kalez se snatakottar kii pariksha uteern kii.
 raajnneetivigyaan evam angreji ke inke gyaan kii prashansa sabhi praadhyaapak karte the. kalege main haaki-team ke pramukh the. ek aankh haaki-stick lagane se nasht ho gai thi.
 padhai puri karne ke baad san 1942 main thakur ji ne sangh ke prachaarak ke rup main swayan ko desh-seva main samarpit kar diya. pathan-pathan main atyadhik ruchi ke kaaran sangh-karyaalay main unhone ek pustkaalay kii sthapana kii jisamen unhone 5 hazaar pustakon kaa sangrah kiya. 
 san 1948 main gandhi-hatya ke baad sangh par pratibandh ke viruddh unhone yol camp jail main 42 din bhukh-hadtaal par rahe 1,400 swayamsevakon kaa netritv kiya. bhukh-hadtaal ke falswaroop punjab ke gopichand bhargav shashan kaa patan hokar bhimsen sachhar sarkaar bani. pratibandh samaapt hone ke baad puure desh main sangh kaa kaary punah khadaa hone laga. shri golwalkar ji ne thakur ramsingh ji ko assam-kshetra main sangh-kaary khadaa karne ke liye bheja. sitambar, 1949 se april, 1971 tak 22 varsh assam praant ke pratham praant-prachaarak ke rup main kaary karte hue thakurji ne sampoorn assam-kshetra ko sanghathan kii drushti se sudrudh kiya. assam main sabhi sansthaaon se sambandh sthapitkar sabko ekasutra main pirone kaa kaary unhone avishrant bhav se kiya. 
 san 1962 ke chini-akraman ke samay gaiyr-asmi log assam se bhagkar desh ke anya surakshit sthanon par ja rahe the. us samay thakur ji na keval date rahe, balki chiniyon ke assam par adhikaar kar lene kii sthiti main sangh-swayamsevakon ko lekar unase muqaabala karne kii, us samay ke varisht prachaarak ekanath ranade ke saath milakar, ranneeti banane main vyast the. usee varsh ek sadak-durghatana main unka dahinaa pair tut gaya, kintu apni adamya ichhashakti ke kaaran vah punah apane pair par khadey ho gayi tathaa phir usee tatparta se 1971 tak assam ke praant-prachaarak ke rup main kaary karte rahe. unhen asamiya bhashaa likhnaa, padhanaa or bolnaa acchhee tarah se aata tha. assam main apane snehpurn vyavhaar va sampark se 50 se adhik prachaarak nikaale.
 san 1971 main thakurji kii niyukti punjab ke sah-praant-prachaarak ke rup main hui jo uttar kshetra-prachaarak ke rup main 1989 tak chali. san 1990 ‘baba sahib apate smarak samiti’ evam 1994 main ‘akhil bhartiya itihaas-sankalan yojana’ kaa daayitv bhi thakur ramsingh ji ke paas aayaa. is daayitv kaa baad unhone kaa kashmir se lekar kanyakumari tak sampoorn desh kaa pravaas kiya tathaa anek itihaaskaaron ko sangathitkar bhartiya-itihaas main ek nayaa adhyay prarambh kar diya. adhik umr ho jane par san 2003 kii sangh kii kurukshetra-baithak main thakur ji ko sabhi dayitvon se mukt kiya gaya. lekin kaary karne kii utkar ichha or jeevanaparyant aaraam na karne kii dridhtaa kaa parinaam tha ki thakur ji nirantar kaary main lage rahe or akhil bhartiya itihaas-sankalan yojana, baba sahib apate smarak samiti kaa anwarat maargdarshan karte rahe. jivan ke antim varshon main thakur ji yojana ke shodh-prakalp ke rup main neri, himachalapradesh main ‘thakur jagadev chand shodh-santhaan’ ke nirmaan main sanlagn rahe. 95 varsh kii aayu main bhi aapane agle 5 varshon kii kaaryayojna bana rakhi thi. dinaank 06 sitambar 2010 ko unka swargvaas ho gaya.
