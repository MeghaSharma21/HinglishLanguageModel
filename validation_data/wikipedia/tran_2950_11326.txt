 aryabhatta ganit or jyotish donon vishyon ke achche acharya the. inkaa banaaya hua mahasiddhanth granth jyotish siddhant kaa acha granth he.
 inhone bhi apana samay kahin nahin likhaa he. doctor singh or dutt kaa mat he ki ye 950 i. ke lagbhag the, jo shakakaal 872 hota he. dikshit lagbhag 875 shaq kehete hai. aryabhatta dwitiya brahmagupta ke peeche hue he, kyonki brahmagupta ne aryabhatta kii jin baton kaa khandan kiya he ve aryabhattiya se milti hai, mahasidhant se nahin. mahasidhant se to prakat hota he ki brahmagupta ne aryabhatta kii jin-jin baton kaa khandan kiya he ve isamen sudhaar di gai hai. kuttak kii vidhi main bhi aryabhatta pratham, bhaskar pratham tathaa bahmagupta kii vidhiyon se kuch unnati dikhaai padati he. isliye isamen andeh nahin ki aryabhatta dwitiya brahmagupta ke baad hue hai.
 brahmagupta or lall ne ayanchalan ke sambandh main kaai charchaa nahin kii he, parantu aryabhatta dwitiya ne isapar bahut vichaar kiya he. apane granth madhyamaadhyay ke shloke 11-12 main inhone ayanbindu ko ek grah maanakar iske kalpabhagan kii sankhya 5,78,159 likhi he jisase ayanbindu kii vaarshik gati 173 vikala hoti he jo bahut hi ashuddh he. spashtadhikaar main spasht aynansh jaanane ke liye jo reeti bataai gai he usase prakat hota he ki inke anusaar aynansh 24 ansh se adhik nahin ho saktaa or ayan kii vaarshik gati bhi sadaa ek si nahin rahati. kabhi ghatate-ghatate shunya ho jaati he or kabhi badhate-badhate 173 vikala ho jaati he. isse siddh hota he ki aryabhatta dwitiya kaa samay vah tha jab ayangati ke sambandh main hamaare siddhanton ko koi nishchay nahin hua tha. manjul ke laghumanas main ayanchalan ke sambandh main spasht ullekh he, jiske anusaar ek kalp main ayanbhagan 1,99,669 hota he, jo varsh main 59.9 vikala hota he. manjul kaa samay 854 shaq yaa 932 isvi he, isliye aryabhatta kaa samay isse bhi kuch pehle hona chahie. isliye inkaa samay 800 shaq ke lagbhag hona chahie.
