 electronic sangrahit jaankaree, civil prakriya ke sangheey niyamon ke uddeshy ke liye banaaya jaankaree, chaalaaki se bheji, sangrahit he, or sabase acha digital rup main upyog kiya, computer hardware or software ke upyog kii aavashyaktaa hoti he..iesai ek kanoonee rup se paribhaashit vaakyansh ke rup main america sarkaar ko 2006 ke civil prakriya ke sangheey niyamon niyam he ki electronic sangrahit jaankaree ke liye rakrakhaav or khoj ke liye prakriyaaon elaan ke prayojanon ke liye chunaa gaya tha aavashyak ban gaya he.civil prakriya ke sangheey niyamon main "electronic sangrahit jaankaree" ke sandarbh kya civil mukademebaaji ke tathy khojane charan ke dauraan kii khoj kii ja sakati he ke liye ek vishaal drushtikon aahvaan.
 niyam 34 ke utpaadan ke liye or anurodh party yaa nirikshan karne ke liye apane pratinidhi, copy, pareekshan kii anumati he, yaa namoona jawaab party ke kabje, hiraasat, yaa niyantran main nimnlikhit madon kisi anya party anurodh karne ke liye ek civil mukadmaa main ek party ke liye sakasham banaata he:
 kisi bhi dastavej namit yaa electronic sangrahit jaankaree sahit lekhan, chitra, rekhankan, chart, photo, dhwani recording, chhaviyon, or anya data yaa data kisi bhi madhyam se jo jaankaree yaa to sidhe praapt kiya ja saktaa he yaa, yadi aavashyak main sankalan-sangrahit, anuwaad ke baad ek had tak sahaayak ke rup main jawaab party dwaara ... niyam 34 kaaphi vyapak computer aadhaarit soochna kii sabhi maujuuda prakaar ke cover ke liye hone kaa iraadaa he, or lacheela paryaapt bhavishya parivartan or vikaas dharna.
 
 deshi filein
 avadhi deshi filon upyogkarta banaaya dastavej he, jo microsoft offis yaa open ofis dastauez swaroopon ke saath hi computer par sangrahit anya filon main ho saktaa he ko sanderbhit karata he, lekin video nigaraanee ek computer kii hard drive par saheja footage shaamil ho sakate hai. is tarah ke blueprint yaa nakshe, digital photo, scan chhaviyon, sangrah filein, i-mail, or digital audio filein, anya data ke bich ke rup main computer aded dizin filein.
