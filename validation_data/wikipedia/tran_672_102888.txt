 marudevi, jinhen mata marudevi bhi kaha jaataa he, pratham jain tirthankar rishabhdev kii mata or nabhiraj kii rani thi. 
 tirthankar mata ke garbh main jeev ke aane ko garbh kalyanak ke rup main manaaya jaataa he. is samay, rani marudevi sapana dekha solah shubh sapane. raja nabhiraj samajhaayaa mahatv ke in sapanom ko subah main.
 ke baad in solah sapane main usne dekha ki ek bade, sundar bail pravesh kar uske khule munh main, sanket kii ek pavitra or asaadhaaran aatma main pravesh kar uske garbh he.
