 vidyut shakti ke audyogic str par utpaadan ke liye nirmit sanyantra ko bijli ghar yaa vidyut kendra kehete hai. ise 'power stesan', 'power plant' yaa 'power house' yaa 'janan kendra' bhi kehete hai. adhikansh bijligharon main ek yaa adhik vidyut janitr hote hai. vishwa main adhikansh bijli ghar vidyut utpann karne ke liye koyale, petroleum or prakritik gas jaise jeevashm eendhan jalaate hai. kuch anya parmaanu urja kaa upyog karte hai. lekin ab saura, pawan, lahar or jalavidyut jaise adhik 'saaf' akshay sroton kaa upyog badhata ja rahaa he.
