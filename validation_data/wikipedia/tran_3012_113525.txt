 rishabhdev uttar pashchim bharat kii rajasthan rajya ke udaipur jile dakshini bhaag main sthit shahar hai. 
 rishabhdev udaipur se 65 kilometre dur sthit he, or udaipur-ahmedabad sadak maarg ) par he. shahar kaa naam dhulev he, haalanki yah behatar ke rup se rishabhdev/keshariaji ke naam se janaa jaataa hai, yah ek prasiddh tirth sthal he. mukhya aakarshan rishabhdev mandir he, pratham jain tirthankar evam bhagwaan vishnu ke anthve avatar. sthaneey bhil bhi inaki puja karte hai. nagar kaa anya naam kesariyaji bhi hai kuki mandir main kesar se puja kii jaati hai.
 is mandir ko mewar ke chaar mukhya dharmik sansthaaon main ek mana jaataa hai, udaipur, ke chatur singh ji ke anusaar:
 bharat kii janaganana, 2001 ke anusaar nagar kii jansankhya 8023 thi. purush jansankhya 52%, or mahilaon kii 48%. nagar kii ausat sakshartaa dar 76%, jo rashtriya ausat 59.5% kii tulana main adhik he. purush sakshartaa 82% he, or mahila sakshartaa 70% he. nagar main, 14% jansankhya 6 varsh se kam umr kii hai. abhi ucch shiksha ke liye kai chaatr baahar ja rahe hai. 
 shahar ke aadhe nivasee jain hai, jisame 95% digambar he, anya brahman, suthar, lohaar, darji, mochi, kumhaar, kalaal, patel, sompura aadi hai. shwetambar jain bhi yahaan rahane vaale he. mandir ke aasapaas shwetambar jain or digambar jain, kai bhil or meena aasapaas ke gaanvon main rahete hai, or prarthana ke liye nity aate hai.
 hare rang ke sangemarmar ke liye bhi prasiddh he. masaro kii obari, oravas evam kagadar main 300 se adhik khaane hai, duniya bhar ke 90% pratishat hare rang ke sangemarmar utpaadit kiya jaataa hai. lagbhag 70% pratishat kii hare sangemarmar kaa niryaat america, sanyukt arab emirate, canada or kai anya deshon main kiya jaataa he. 
 
 mukhya mandir main bhagwaan rishabhdev kii kaale pathar se khudi padmasana mudra main 3.5 feet unchi murti he.
 bhagwaan ram kaa mandir bhi is nagar kaa mukhya aakarshan hai.
 gajmandir ek jain shwetambar mandir, jiskaa kaa nirmaan 2011 kiya gaya, jo kii kika bhai dharmashala ke rup main acchhee tarah se janaa jaataa he.
