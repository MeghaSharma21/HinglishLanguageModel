 makintosh yaa mak vyaktigat computaron kii ek shrenee kaa naam he jisamen appal incorporation naamak company dwaara kai tarah ke pisi kaa dizin, vikaas or vipanan kiya gaya. makintosh 24 janvari san 1984 ko sabase pehle bazaar main utaara gaya tha. makintosh hi vyapaarik rup se safal pehla pisi tha jisamen 'command line interphes' ke bajaay moose or graphical yujar interphes uplabdh karaayaa gaya tha.
 unniis sau assi ke uttarardh main company ne bazaar banaaya kintu nabbe ke dashak main isamen kramash: kami aa gayi kyonki pisi-bazaar kaa ruukh ibiem sangat pisi kii taraf chal padaa jo emes-dos yaa microsoft vindos par chalate the. baad main san 1998 main imak laakar appal ne apana bazaar phir badhaayaa.
