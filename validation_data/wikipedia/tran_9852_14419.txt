 apamarg ek aushadhiy vanaspati he. iskaa vaigyanik naam 'achiranthis aspera' he. hindi main ise 'chirchita', 'latajira', 'chirchira ' aadi naamon se janaa jaataa he.
 apamarg ek sarvavidit kshupajaateey aushadhi he. varsha ke saath hi yah ankurit hoti he, ritu ke ant tak badhati he tathaa sheet ritu main pushp falon se shobhit hoti he. greeshm ritu kii garmi main paripakv hokar falon ke saath hi kshup bhi shushk ho jaataa he. iske pushp hari gulabi abha yukt tathaa beej chaaval sadrush hote hai, jinhen tandul kehete hai.
 sharad ritu ke ant main panchang kaa sangrah karke chaaya main sukhaakar band paatro main rakhte hai. beej tathaa mul ke paudhe ke sookhne par sangrahit karte hai. inhen ek varsh tak prayukt kiya ja saktaa he.
 ise vajra danti bhi kehete hai. isaki jad se datun karne se daanton kii jaden majboot or daant moti kii tarah chamakate hai. 
 bichchhu ke kaatane par ek katori main 50 gram lahi ke tel ko ubaalo or us ubalte hue tel main latajira ke paudhe ko ukhaad kar or uskaa ras nichod kar daalo isse jo vaashp nikle usamen bichhuse kate hue bhaag kii sikai karo. sheeghra laabh hoga.
