 ashv sanchaalan do shabdon se milakar bana he. ashv va sanchaalan. is mudra main jis prakaar ghode ko daudaya jaataa he us mudra main sharir ko rakhkar yoga kaa abhyaas kiya jaataa he. 
 ashv sanchaalan yoga se janghaon main sthit tanaav dur hota he. yah mudra merudand ko sidhaa banane rakhane ke liye kargar hota he. yah chaati or hips ke liye acha vyaayaam hota he. isse shwasan kriya acchhee rahati he. is asana ke abhyaas se janghaon or sharir main lacheelaapan aata he jisase peeche kii ore jhukkar kiya jin yoga mudraon kaa abhyaas kiya jaataa he unhen abhyaas karte samay vishesh pareshaani nahin hoti he. isi prakaar baithkar jin asana kaa abhyaas kiya jaataa he unke liye bhi yah yoga bahut hi laabhakaari hota he. 
 is asana ke abhyaas ke samay qamar jhuke nahin iske liye merudand sidhaa or lambavat rakhana chahie. abhyaas ke samay shwasan ke saath dhad or merudand upar kii ore ho iskaa khyaal rakhana chahie or prashwaas ke saath qamar neeche kii ore ho iskaa dhyaan rakhana chahie. is mudra kaa abhyaas karte samay chahen to ghutanon ke neeche kambal yaa tauliya rakh sakate hai isse aapke ghutane relax rahenge. 
 jab ghutanon main athwa hips main kisi prakaar kii pareshaani ho us samay is madra kaa abhyaas nahin karnaa chahie.
