 antoni gaudi i cornet spani katalonyai vaastukar the. gaudi reus ke nivasee the va kala or sahitya andolan catalan modrnizme ke kalpit sardaar the. gaudi kaa kaary jo ki mukhya rup se barsilona main sankendrit he unki atyadhik vyaktigat or vishisht style ko darshata he. inkaa sabase utkrisht kaary sagrada familia naam kaa roman catholic girjaghar he jo ki apni apurna sthiti main bhi unesco vishwa viraasat sthal he.
 gaudi kaa zyaadaatar kaary unke jivan ke teen bade junun: vaastushastra, prakruti or dharm dwaara chihnit kiya gaya. ve apni rachanaaon ke har vistaar kaa adhyayan karte the, apni vaastukala main kaarigari kii un shreniyon ko ekikrut karte the jin main ve kushal the: chini mitti, daag kaanch, gadha luhakaarya forging or badhaigiri. inhone vaastukala sambandhit maal ke istemaal ke liye nai takanic viksit kii thim, jin main trainkadis bhi shaamil he jo bekaar chini mitti ke tukade se bana mosek hota he.
 kuch varsho ke pashchaat nav-gothic kala or praachya takneeko ke prabhaav main gaudi modrnizme andolan ke hissaa ban gaye, jo ki unnisvin sadi ke ant or beesvin sadi ke shuruyaat main apane shikhar par pahunch rahaa thi. inke kaary ne mukhyadhara modrnizme ko alag aayam tak pahunchaaya jis main prakruti se prerit jaivik style sammilit thi. gaudi shaayad hi kabhi apane kaary kii vistrut yojana taiyaar karte the, iske bajaay ve teen-aiami paimaane kaa model kaa nirmaan karnaa pasand karte the jise apni avadharana ke saath-saath dhaalte rahete the.
 gaudi ke kaary ko intaraashtriya apil praapt he tathaa kai adhyayan inaki vaastukala ko samajhane ke liye samarpit kiye gaye hai. vartmaan main inke kaary ke vaastukaron or aam janata ke bich samaan rup se prashansak milte hai. inaki utkrisht kriti, abhi bhi apurna sagrada familia, katalonya ke sabase prasiddh or bade paryatakon ke aakarshan ke kendron main se ek he. 1984 se 2005 ke bich unesco ne inke saat kaaryo ko vishwa viraasat sthal ghoshit kiya. gaudi kii roman catholic aasthaa jivan ke saath-saath or adhik ghanishtha hoti chali gai or dharmik chitra inke kaary main vyaapt hai. iske kaaran inhen 'bhagwaan kaa vaastukar' kaa upnaam mila or inke biatfakeshan kii maang uthne lagi.
 gaudi kaa janm 25 june 1852 ko spain ke katalonya praant ke ridoms yaa reus nagar main ek samaanya parivaar main hua tha. inke pita tamrakaar the. vaastukala main apni shuruuaati dilchaspee ke pashchaat ye 1869/70 main barsilona padhane chale gaye, jo us samay katalonya kaa raajnneetik or bauddhik kendra hone ke alawa spain kaa sabase aadhunik shahar tha. inhone kuch samay sena main bhi vyateet kiya jiske kaaran inaki shiksha main bhi vighn padaa.
 gaudi ne apane vyavsaay ke liye apana sampoorn jivan samrpit kar diya tha va jivan bhar kunvaare rahe. inke nijee jivan ke bare main jitani jaankaree he uske anusaar ye keval mataro coparative kii hosepha moreu naam kii ek adhyapika ke prati aakarshith hue the, parantu duusari taraf se inhen sakaaratmak pratikriya nahin mili. tatpashchaat gaudi ne apane catholic vishwaas dwaara pradaan kii gai gahan aadhyatmik shanti main sharan lee. inkaa vyaktitv aksar ekantapriya or apriy darshaya gaya he, ek karkash pratikriyaaon or abhimaani bhav vaala vyakti. haalanki jo inke kareeb the unhone inkaa varnan milansaar or vinamr vyakti ke rup main kiya, jo baat karne main khushnuma or mitron ke prati vafaadaar tha.
 nordic	 mukhaakriti, sunahare bal or neeli aankhon vaala gaudi kaa vyaktigat swaroop samay ke saath-saath moulik rup se badalata gaya. yuva avasthaa main ye apane vyavhaar, vastra or rup ke upar vishesh dhyaan dete the jisamen ye mahanga suut pahante, acchhee tarah se bal or dadhi taiyaar karte, ruchikar swaad vaala bhojan khate, rangamanch or opera kaa lagaataar daura karte tathaa ghoda gaadi main apane pariyojna sthalon kaa daura karte the. vruddh avasthaa main gaudi mitvyayita se khaaya karte, puraane or fate hue suut pahante or is had tak apane swaroop kii upeksha karne lage the ki kai baar unhen duusare log bhikhaari tak samaj baithte the, jaisaa inaki durghatana ke dauraan bhi hua or shaayad inkaa rup hi inaki mrityu kaa kaaran bana.
 7 june 1926 ko gaudi apni saint phelip neri girjaghar tak kii dainik saiyr par the, jahaan ve niyamit rup se prarthana or sweekaarokti kiya karte the. raste main inaki ek chalati hui tram se takkar ho gai or ye behosh ho gaye. pahchaan dastavejon ke abhaav or daridr vastra pahane hone ke kaaran inhen raste ke musafiron ne sadak kaa bhikhaari samajha or koi tatkaal sahaayta inhen nahin mil pai. antat ek pulicekarmi ne inhen taxi dwaara santa creyu aspataal pahunchaaya jahaan inhen praarambhik dekhbhaal mili. agle din jab sagrada familia ke padri mosen gil pares ne inaki pahchaan kii tab tak inaki haalat itani bighad chuki thi ki atirikt upachaar se bhi laabh milanaa asambhav tha. durghatana ke teen din pashchaat 10 june 1926 ko 73 varsh kii umr main inaki mrityu ho gai or uske do din pashchaat inhen dafan kar diya gaya. sagrada familia ke ower ladi of mount carmel chapel main bahut badi sankhya main log inke antim darshan karne ke liye upasthit the.
