 dow. yaga venugopal reddy, jinhen vaai ve reddy naam se janaa jaataa he, jo andhra pradesh cader se sambandhit 1964 batch ke sevanivrutt bhartiya prashasnic seva adhikari hai. reddy ne 6 sitambar 2003 se 5 sitambar 2008 tak bhartiya rigerv bank  ke governor ke rup main kaary kiya tha. 2010 main, unhen bharat kaa doosraa sarvocch naagarik sammaan, padam vibhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha..
 reddy ne madras uniwarsity, arthshaastra se arthshaastra main em.e. or osmania vishvavidhyalay, hyderabad se piechdi praapt kiya. vah netherland santhaan, soshal studies santhaan se aarthik yojana main diploma bhi rakhte hai.shri venkateshwar vishvavidhyalay, bharat dwaara reddy ko doctor of letters kii degree se sammanit kiya gaya tha; or morishas vishvavidhyalay dwaara doctor of civil lau . 17 july 2008 ko, unhen landan skool of iconomics ke maanad phelo banaaya gaya.
 venugopal reddy ko 6 sitambar 2003 ko bhartiya rigerv bank ke pehle governor niyukt kiya gaya tha or paanch saal tak us sthiti main kaary kiya tha.1996 main, reddy ko arabiai ke dipti governor niyukt kiya gaya tha. unhone 2002 main antarrashtriya mudra kosh ke kaaryakari nideshak ke rup main bhi kaam kiya he. bhartiya prashasnic sevaaon main shaamil hone se pehle, unhone 1961 se ek vyaakhyaata ke rup main kaam kiya.iees main rahete hue, unhone vitt mantralay or andhra pradesh sarkaar ke pradhan sachiv main sachiv kii padon par kaary kiya or china, bahrain, ithiopia or tanzania sarkaron ke saath kaam kiya he. vah ek visiting phelo, landan skool of iconomics, bijness management vibhaag, osmania vishvavidhyalay main purnakalik ujisi visiting professor rahe hai; purnakalik visiting faikalti, prashasnic staff college of india or hyderabad main iconomic and soshal studies centre main maanad professor bane rahe. reddy bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan madras ke vishisht professor bhi the. reddy antarrashtriya maudrik or vittiya pranali ke sudhaar par sanyukt raashtra mahasabha ke raashtrapati ke visheshgyon ke ayog ke sadsy the. adhyaksh ke alaava, professor joseph stiglitz, is sanyukt raashtra ayog ke sadasyon ko japan, pashchimi europe, africa, latin america, dakshin or purvi asia se kiya gaya tha. 2011 ke dauraan reddy da indian iconometric socity ke adhyaksh the. reddy new iconomic thinking ke salaahkaar board of institute board main the. ieniti salaahkaar board main nobel puraskaar vijetaon ke saath-saath anya pramukh arthshaastri shaamil hai. reddy bhartiya aarthik nitiyon, colombia vishvavidhyalay, neuyork par colombia kaarykram ke antarrashtriya salaahkaar board par the. vah antarrashtriya maudrik sudhaaron par pramukh vyaktiyon ke anaupchaarik antarrashtriya samooh ke sadsy the. vah ji -20 muddon par bharat ke vitt mantri ko salaah dene ke liye pratishthit vyaktiyon ke salaahkaar samooh par bhi the. . reddy ko varsh 2014 ke liye indian iconomic association ke sammelan adhyaksh ke rup main nirvaachit kiya gaya tha. vah 3 janvari 2013 se bharat ke chaudaaven vitt ayog ke adhyaksh the. vartmaan main, dow reddy centre for iconomic and soshal studies, hyderabad main maanad professor hai..
 reddy ne vittiya kshetra ke sudhaaron ke liye ek callebrated drushtikon kaa sanchaalan karne par kaam kiya he. neuyork times main 19 disambar 2008 ke lekh ne 2008 ke up-pradhan or taralata sankat se puri bhartiya banking pranali ko bachaane ke liye bhartiya rigerv bank ke governor ke rup main bhartiya bainkon par lagaae gaye kathin udhaar maanakon ko shrey diya he. rigerv bank main, vah do ucch stareey samitiyon ke sadsy sachiv the: ek bhugtan santulan par or doosraa saarvajanik kshetra ke vinievesh par. bhartiya rigerv bank ke purv governor dow si rangarajan donon samitiyon kii adhyakshata main the. reddy bahari rin sankhyikee par bhartiya rigerv bank ke niti samooh ke sadsy bhi the. reddy ne macro-iconomic nitiyon ko taiyaar karne main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai he, jisane 1 990 ke dashak ke ant main dakshin-purvi asian deshon dwaara saamana kiye jane vaale vittiya sankat ke dominos prabhaav se desh ko sangathit karne main madad kii. unhone dow si rangarajan ke saath-saath bhugtan sankat ke tatkaal shesh raashi se baahar aane ke liye desh dwaara sanchalit kiye jane vaale paathyakram ke nirmaan ke saath bhi shrey diya he.bhartiya sandarbh main, vah bhartiya rigerv bank ke governor ke rup main apane vaarshik niti vaktavy main april 2005 main 'vittiya samaveshan' shabd kaa upyog karne vaale pehle vyakti the. baad main, is avadharana ko jameen mili or iskaa vyapak rup se bharat or videshon main upyog kiya jane laga. banking prathaon ke sambandh main chintaaon ko pahachaanate hue, jo aabaadi ke vishaal virgon ko aakarshith karne ke bajaay baahar nikalne ke liye pravrutt hote hai, bainkon ko vaarshik niti vaktavy main vittiya samaveshan ke uddeshy se unhen sanrekhit karne ke liye unke maujuuda prathaon kii samiksha karne ke liye aagrah kiya gaya tha...
 governor ke rup main, unhone yah sunishchit karne ke rup main apana kaam dekha ki bhartiya bank bulbula maansikta main bhi pakade nahin gaye the. unhone kachche bhoomi kii kharid ke liye bank rin ke upyog par pratibandh laga diya, or teji se kam se kam suraksha or derevative, or anivaary rup se pratibandhit balance shit vittaposhan pratibandhit kar diya. unhone vanijyik bhawanon or shopping mal nirmaan or bank arakshit aavashyaktaaon main vruddhi ke jokhim jokhim main vruddhi kii. apane saakshaatkaar main, colombia vishvavidhyalay or nobel puraskaar vijeta, arthshaastra ke professor joseph i. stiglitz ne kaha tha, 'agar america ke paas kendriya bank pramukh vivi jaise tha. reddy, america arthvyavastha itani gadabadi main nahin hoti. gramin banking par vishesh rup se sahakaari bainkon ko punerjeevit karne or aam vyakti par unke dhyaan par unke kaam par kam charchaa kii gai he. unki avadhi ko soochna praudyogiki kii sahaayta se vittiya samavesh par jor diya gaya tha. bharat or duniya bhar main donon sansthaanon dwaara unhen kai vittiya muddon par vyapak rup se paraamarsh diya jaataa he..
 reddy ne 2009 main orient blakeswan dwaara prakashit "india and da global financial chrysis: managing mani and finance" naamak ek pustak likhi. is pustak ne 2003 or 2008 ke dauraan kshetron kii ek spectrum main saarvajanik nitiyon ke nirmaan main antardrushti pradaan kii, ek avadhi bhartiya arthvyavastha ke teji se vikaas ke saath-saath maudrik niti ke aacharan ke liye asaadhaaran chunautiyaan bharat ke sarvshereshth vikritaon main se ek thim. "global chrysis, mandi or asmaan recovery" naamak unki pustak 2009 ke bestsaling kaam kaa ek anukram tha. isane ek vichaarak or anbhavi niti nirmaataa kii utpatti, sharir rachana or vittiya sankat ke prabhaav, or iske dwaara pradaan kiye jane vaale paathon kii samaj pradaan kii. pustak ne bataaya ki sankat ke parinaamon main se ek asia ke paksh main aarthik shakti ke santulan main badlaav he. iske alaava, yah vaishvik vittiya sankat ke vikaas main kendriya bainkon kii bhoomika par prakaash daalaa gaya, vishesh rup se vikasshil deshon main kendriya bainkon dwaara nibhaai gai bhoomika. vittiya kshetra ko viniyamit karne or vaishvik bahes ke prakaash main bharat ke vittiya kshetra main sudhaar kii aavashyaktaa par bharat ke anubhav par irudit charchaaen is pustak kii kuch pramukh visheshtaayen hai..
