 ahmadpur paavan, elahabaad elahabaad jile ke elahabaad prakhand kaa ek gaanv he.
 janaganana 2011 kii jaankaree ke anusaar ahmadapur paavan gaanv kaa sthaan code yaa gaanv code 161750 he. ahmadpur paavan gaanv uttar pradesh main elahabaad jile ke elahabaad tehsil main sthit he. yah up jila mukhyalay sadar se 25 kimi dur sthit he or jila mukhyalay elahabaad se 25 kimi dur he. 2009 ke aankadon ke anusaar, ahmadpur paavan gaanv ek gram panchayat bhi he. gaanv kaa kul bhugolic kshetra 524.91 hectare he. ahmadpur paavan kii kul jansankhya 7090 jansankhya he ahmadpur paavan gaanv main lagbhag 1151 ghar hai. elahabaad ahmadpur paavan ke sabase nikatatam shahar he. yah sthaan elahabaad jile or kaushambi jile kii seemaa main he. kaushambi jila chaayal pashchim kii ore he.
