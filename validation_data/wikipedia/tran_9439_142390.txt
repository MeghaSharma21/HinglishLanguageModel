 madras diwas tamilnadu rajya kii rajdhani madras shahar main manaaya jane vaala ek samaaroh diwas he. yah har varsh 22 agast ke din manaaya jaataa he or isko yah naam shahar ke naam par mila he. 1639 main isi din east india company ne coromandal tat par jameen kharidi thi jahaan unhone madras shahar kii neenv rakhi. yah jameen saint jorge fort ke nirmaan ke liye british east india company dwaara chandragiri ke maharaaja se kharidi gai thi. is diwas par kai aayojan yojit kiye jate hai jinamein shahar ke naagarik hissaa lete hai.
