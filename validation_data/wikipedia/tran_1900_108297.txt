 chikitsa main pootirodhi aise dravya hai jo sookshm jeevon kii vruddhi ko rokte, yaa unka vinaash karte hai . yahan par inkaa vichaar vishesh rup se sharir ke sampark main aane vaale sookshm jeevon ke vinaash kii drushti se kiya gaya he. pratirodhi shabd kii paribhaasha kuch aspasht hi he, kyonki anek aise dravya hai jo jeevanunashak hone ke kaaran roganunashi shrenee main aate hai, par jinhen praritarodhi dravyon ke antargat bhi pariganit kiya jaataa he. vastav main ye donon shabd saapeksh hai. roganunashi dravya pratirodhi dravyon se adhik teevr hote hai or jal main tanukrit karne par aise adhikansh dravya pratirodhi jaisaa kaary karte hai. pratirodhi kriya mandprabhavi hote hue bhi adhik der tak bani rahati he. 
 adarsh jeevanunashak dravya aisa hona chahie jo shaareerik koshikaon ko bina kisi prakaar nuksaan pahunchaae, jeevanuon kaa vinaash yaa pratirodh kar sake. yadhyapi aisa adarsh dravya abhi tak nahin praapt ho sakaa he tathaapi chikitsa kshetra main aise dravyon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he jo kam se kam haani pahunchaaen. in dravyon kaa paraspar moolyakan karnaa kathin he, kyonki inke bahut adhik prakaar ho gaye hai tathaa inako vibhinn paristhitiyon main kaary karnaa padataa he. roganunashak kriya nimnalikhit baton par nirbhar karti he : 
 1. taap, 2. dravya ke saath sampark kii avadhi, 3. jeevanu prakaar, 
 4. jeevanusankhya, 5. sanvardhan posh padaarth kaa swaroop evam 6. anya carbonic dravyon kii upasthiti.
 inkaa shaktipramaapan ridil-vaakar paddati se kiya jaataa he. isamen pehle yah dekhte hai ki kisi nirdhaarit samay ke andar kam se kam kitni phenol kii maatra se basilas tiphosus kaa 24 ghante vaala sanvardhan nasht ho jaataa he, phir us parikshya dravya kii kitni maatra unheen paristhitiyon main vaisaa hi prabhaav dikhalaati he. in donon kaa anupaat phenol gunank yaa ridil-vaakar gunank kehalaata he. is kasuti se pratirodhi dravya kii upyogita kaa gyaan keval kuch seemaa tak hi hota he, kyonki sharir ke saath sampark main aane par in dravyon ke prabhaav main kuch na kuch parivartan hota he.
 inamen se bahut se dravya jeevadravya par vishaiylla prabhaav daalate he. ye samaan rup se shaareerik evam jeevanu koshikaon ko prabhavit karte hai saamaanya swaroop kii rasinic kriya hi sambhavat: anek pratirodhiyon main mahatv kaa kaary karti he, yatha jeevanugat asanyojit amlavargeey, yaa ksharak vargeey, protein kaa udaasinikaran . aise mahatvapunr kriyasheel virgon kaa udasinakaran sambhavat: jeevanu main upaapchayaj parivartan laakar antat: unka vinaash karata he. chikitsa main kabhi kabhi sutara ko kshareey hone par amleey banaakar, yaa amleey hone par kshareey banaakar, shodhan karte hai. iskaa aadhaar sambhavat: uparyukt siddhant hi he.
 rinaynik pratirodhi - yah ucch anubhaar vaale amlon ke udasin yaa mand kshareey lavan hai, jaise sabun, ammonium or calcium mandellette tathaa udasin yaa ranjit dravya kaa varg, jinhen amlaranjak kaha jaataa he. ksharak varg ke jeevanu evam inke amleey ayanon kii anyonya kriya dwaara ksheen ynikrut yoga banakar yah kaary karte hai.
 dhanainic pratirodhi - ye ucch anubhaar vaale ksharakon ke udasin yaa ranjit dravya kaa varg, jinhen amlaranjak kaha jaataa he. ksharak varg ke jeevanu evam inke amleey ayanon kii anyonya kriya dwaara ksheen ayanikrut yoga banakar yah kaary karte hai.
 dhanainic pratirodhi - ye ucch anubhaar vaale ksharakon ke udasin lavan hai, jinhen ksharkeey ranjak kaha jaataa he, jaise briliant green, crystal violet, jentian violet, acrydin ranjak aadi, evam kuch ucch elifatic amine setrymide tathaa paarad ke kuch elifatic amine setrymide tathaa paarad ke kuch carbonic yoga . amlavargeey jeevanuon ke saath ksharkeey ayan anyonya kriya dwaara yah kaaryan karte hai, aisa mana jaataa he.
 kuch dravya jeevanukoshikaon main pravesh kar koshikeey anzimo ke kaary main baadha daalate hai. jeevanuvriddhi ke liye aavashyak salphhaidrill varg ke dravyon ke saath sanyog karke paarad ke yoga kaary karte hai. isi prakaar sulpha aushadhiyaan bhi paraamino benzik amla ke saath milakar unako upyog main laane se jeevanuon ko vanchit karti hai. kuch dravya jeevanuon ke upree str par adhishoshit hokar kaary karte hai. protein ke asamyukt amino varg ke saath vishesh jatil yoga banaakar, formaldihide kaa kaary karata he.
