 2014 isisi vishwa tinti 20 kaa final match 6 april 2014 ko dhaka main sher-e-bangla rashtriya cricket stadium main bharat or srilanka ke bich khelaa gaya tha. yah 5waan isisi vishwa twanti 20 kaa sanskaran tha. srilanka ne 2009 or 2012 main do baar up vijeta banane ke baad is match ko chah wicket se jitkar pehali baar khitaab jeeta. srilanka bharat, pakistan, england, west indies ke baad is khitaab ko jeetne vaali 5veen team thi. is match main stadium main, 25,000 darshakon ne dekhne kaa anand liya tha.
