 vinaa verma you ke main rahane vaali ek punjabi kahanikar he jo kahaniyaan likhti hai. vinaa kahanikara ke alaava ek kavyitri bhi hai.
 vinaa verma kaa janm 2 aktubar 1960 ko bhartiya punjab ke jila bathinda main bulada nagar main rahane vaale ek khatri parivaar main hua. isane apni padhai "bulada" nagar se puri kii or you ke chali gai.
