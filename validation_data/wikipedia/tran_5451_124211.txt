 mohammad abdul rahamaan abdul rauf arafaat alkuvda al husaini, jinhen yasir arafaat ke lokapriy naam se zyada janaa jaataa he ek philistini netaa evam philstini mukti sanghathan ke adhyaksh the.araafaat aise pehle shaks the, jinhen kisi raashtra kaa netritv na karte hue bhi sanyukt raashtra main bhaashan dene ke liye aamantrit kiya gaya tha.
 araafaat ke netritv main unke sanghathan ne shanti kii jagah sangharsh ko badhaava diya or isroyal hameshaa unke nishane par rahaa. shanti se dur sangharsh kii pahal karne vaale araafaat kii chhavi 1988 main achaanak badali hui dikhi. wo sanyukt raashtra main shanti ke doot ke rup main najar aae. baad main unhen shanti ke nobel puraskaar se bhi sammanit kiya gaya.nehru-gandhi parivaar ke saath inaki bahut karibiyaan thim. indira gandhi ko wo apni badi bahan maanate the. inhone bharat main 1991 ke chunaav abhiyaan ke dauraan rajeev gandhi ko jaanaleva hamale ko lekar aagaah kiya tha.
 inaki mrityu kii khabar 11 november, 2004 ko ai. bataaya gaya ki beemaari kii vajah se unki maut hui. kuch samay baad hi unki maut ko lekar isroyal par jehar dene ke aarope lage. iske baad ye sawaal khadaa ho gaya ki unki maut prakritik thi yaa jehar ke chalate hui thi.jaanch ke liye unke shav ko kabr se bhi nikaala gaya tha or switsraland ke vaigyanikon ne daawa kiya tha ki unke shav ke avasheshon main radiodharmee polonium-210 mila tha. haalanki, unki maut logon ke liye ab bhi ek paheli hi bani hui he.
