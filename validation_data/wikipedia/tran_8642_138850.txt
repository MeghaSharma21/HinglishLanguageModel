 emily warren robling ko unke pati washington robling main visampeden beemaari viksit hone ke baad bruekalin brij puraa karne ke liye janaa jaataa he . uske pati ke ek civil ejeeniyar or bruekalin brij ke nirmaan ke dauraan chif ejeeniyar the.
 emily kaa janm sylvanus or fibe warren ke ghar ] main 23 sitambar, 1843 ko hua. vah baaki baarah bacchon main se duusari sabase choti bacchee thim. emily kii padhai main ruchi kaa samarthan unke bade bhai governor ke warren ne kiya. dono bhai bahanon main hameshaa ek kareebi rishta rahaa. 
 1864 main america naagarik yuddh ke dauraan, emily apane bhai jo ki paanchaven sena ke commanding afasar the, se milane unke mukhyalay gayi.yatra ke dauraan, unka parichay bruekalin brij ke diziner jon e robling ke bete washington robling se hua, jo governor warren ke karmchaariyon main civil ejeeniyar sevarat the. emily or washington mili or 18 janvari, 1865 ko vah shaadishuda the. 
 europea adhyayan se vapsi par, emily or washington ke saath swaagat ek durbhaagya se hua; tetanus se washington ke pita kii mrityu ho gai, or washington ne turant bruekalin brij ke nirmaan kaa kaarybhaar sanbhal liya. vah purna rup se is pariyojna main dub gayi, washington main ] fail gayi, jisane unhen bhi prabhavit kiya or wo bhi itani buree tarah se ki vah bistar se grast ho gayi. is maukey par emily ne pehali mahila "field ejeeniyar" ke rup main kadam rakhaa or bruekalin brij kaa adhuraa kaam puraa kiya.
 aaj bruekalin brij par ek pattica kii smriti emily, uske pati washington robling, or uske sasur jon e robling ko samarpit he. 
 bruekalin brij ke puraa hone ke baad, robling parivaar ne ] sthanantarit kr liya. vahaan, emily ne saamaajik sanghathanon main bhaag liya jaise ki spanish–america yuddh ke dauraan raahat socity or vah vishwa colombian pradarshnee main new jersey kii mahila prabandhakon ke board main bhi shaamil hai. unhone vyapak rup se yatra kii—1896 main vah rani victoria ke saamne prastut huin or vah zaar nicholas dwitiya kerajbhishek main shaamil huin thim. unhone shiksha jari rakhi or neuyork vishvavidhyalay se kanoon kii degree praapt kii. 28 farvari, 1903 main maut tak shesh samay unhone apane parivaar ke saath vyateet kiya or saamaajik evam maansik rup se sakriy raheen.
