 abhyaas mandal indaur kaa ek vaicharik manch he. yah ek arajanatik sanstha he jo prativarsh may maah main greeshmakaleen vyaakhyanmala kaa aayojan karata he, jo paanch dashakon se avirat jari he. yah bauddhik aayojan aaj is indaur shahar kii pahchaan ban chukaa he. vichaar goshtiyaan, chintan-shivi, vyaakhyanmalas, samuhik vichaar-vimarsh, sarvadharm sambhaav, samaaj ke sabhi virgon se jeevant sampark or ek jivit samvaad, yahi isaki kaaryastylee he.
