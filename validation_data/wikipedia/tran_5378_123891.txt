 2014 peshawar skool narsanhaar
 disambar 2014,16 par taharik-e-taliban ke saath sambadhd nau bandookdhaariyon ne peshawar ke uttar pashchimi pakistan ke shahar main army public skool par ek aatankwaadi hamale kaa aayojan kiya. videshi nagarikon the, ek chechen, teen arab or do afgaan shaamil the. ve skool main pravesh kiya or umr ke aat or atharah saal ke bich lekar 132 skool ke bacchon, sahit 145 log maare gaye, skool staff or bacchon par aag kholaa. ek bachaav abhiyaan ke sabhi saat aatankwaadiyon ko maar giraaya or 960 logon ko bachaaya he jo pakistani sena ke vishesh seva samooh ke vishesh balon, dwaara shuru kiya gaya tha. mukhya sainya pravaktaa major jeneral aseem bajva kam se kam 130 logon ke hamale main ghayal ho gaya tha ki ek samvaadadaata sammelan main kaha.is ghaatak aatankwaadi hamalaa kabhi 2007 karachi bam visphot, shreshth pakistan main hone ke liye gaya tha. vibhinn samachar agensiyon or tippaneekaron ke anusaar, hamale kii prakruti or taiyaari 2004 main rusi sangh ke uttar osetia-aalania kshetra main hui he ki basalaan skool bandhak sankat kii he ki bahut samaan tha.
 hamale 10:00ke aasapaas main shuru hua saat bandookdhaari pakistani ardhsounik bal kii vardi main prachhann he, jab frontier kor, diwaron par pahunchaa hone ke baad skool ke nikat ek kabristaan ke madhyam se peeche se skool main pravesh kiya. army public skool peshawar chhavnee ke nikat varasak road par sthit he, or army public skool, or pakistan main 146 skulon chalata he ki college system kaa hissaa he. skool main pravesh karne se pahle, bandookdhaariyon ne sujuki bolan es tii 41 vine ko aag laga di, jisamen ve aa gayi the. aatankwaadiyon, swachaalit hathiyaaron asar, parisar ke centre main sthit sabhaagaar kii ore sidhe le jaaya gaya or ek skool ke samaaroh ke liye vahaan ikatthe hue the, jo bacchon par andhadhundh golibari shuru kar di. inter services public relations ke mahanideshak, pakistani sena ke major general aseem bajva ke jansampark vibhaag ke anusaar, aatankwaadiyon ko kisi bhi bandhakon ko lene kaa iraadaa nahin tha, lekin iske bajaay jitne ho sake utane vidhyaarthiyon ko maarane chaahate the. aatankwaadiyon ne goli chalaa di or vidhyaarthiyon ke kai sabhaagaar ke duusare paksh par do raste kii ore bhaaga, lekin unamem se kai bageeche main maar giraaya gaya tha. khabaron ke anusaar vidhyaarthiyon ko principal tahiraa kaaji sahit shikshakon ko jinda jalaate huve dekhne ko majaboor kar diya. 15 minute ke bheetar,es es ji tiimon skool par dhaava bol diy tha or unki baari bakhtarband vahno or trakon main donon pakshon se parisar main pravesh kiya. iske turant es es ji carmiyon ne anya shesh shikshak staff or chaatro kii hatya karne se aatanvaadiyon ko rokane lage. bandookdhaariyon ne skool ke prashasan ke khand ke taraf gayi or vahaan bandhakon ko le liya. unmein se ek, sabhaagaar ke paas sainya carmiyon dwaara goli maar di thi or anya chah prashasan khand main maare gayi. sainya bakhtarband vaahanon main sena chikitsa kor kii apaat aghat tiimon, or ikaayiyon ke skool main bhartee karaayaa gaya, sainya police kii sena ke kor or prantiy naagarik khaybar pakhtoonkhwaa police aatanvaadiyon ke liye kisi bhi sambhavit batch margon se band kii ore se kaam kiya.is bich, esasji commandon kshetra pahunche or prashasan khand ghira hua tha. aaparishan ke adhikansh is khand spasht or bandookdaariyon dwaara uthaae gaye bandakon ko bachaane ke prayaas main jari hua. bandookdaariyon or unke pataa lagaanevaale kii vishesh tiimon aatanvaadiyon ko thik pataa lagaaya; commandon imaarat par akraman kiya or is prakriya main shesh bandhakon ko bachaaya jab anya teen kii maut ho gai, jabaki chah hamlaawaron ke teen, khidkiyaan or hawa sooraakh se bandookdaariyon dwaara maare gaye the. do adhikariyon sahit saat commando, ladai main ghayal ho gayi the. ek khoj or nikasi aaparishan skool parisar ke bheetar yaa aatankwaadiyon pahane hue the ki aatmahatya nihit main bandookdaariyon dwaara lagaae kisi bhi iidi ko shant karne ke liye turant shuru kiya gaya tha. aatankwaadiyon ke hamale ke dauraan unke sanchaalkon ke saat sampark main the, lekin esasji ne suraksha balon ko aatankadwaadiyon ke sanchaar ko roak diya.
 121 ghayal ho gaye the, haalanki 1099 vidhyaarthiyon or shikshan staff kii anumaanit kul pratyuttar dena balon ke lagbhag 960 logon ko bachaane main safal rahe the, jinmein se skool parisar par upasthit the. 134 ladkon, das skool staff ke sadasayon, or teen sainikon sahit 150 logon ko ek kul aatankwaadi hamale main maare gaye the.haalanki, kuch anaupchaarik reporton hataahaton kii vaastavik sankhya ke aasapaas 300-400 kaa daawa he. report main bacchon ke sabake sir main goli maar di thi. khaybar pakhtoonkhwaa kii prantiy sarkaar pi ke aar 200,000 aatanki hamale main mratak ke prayek parijanon ko muaavje ke rup main or pi ke aar 500,000, jo gambhir rup se ghayal he unako ghoshnaa kii he.
 pakistan -taliban taharik- main aaparishan jarb-e-azedbi, garmiyon main 2014 main shuru kar diya he ki uttari vajiristaan main pakistani sena ke akraman ke liye badla lene ke rup main iskaa varnan hamale kii jimmedaari lee he. islaami aatanvaadiyon par ek visheshgy ke mutabik, ahamed rashid ke anusaar, hamale bhi 2014 ke nobel pis puraskaar malala usufaji ko sammanit kiye jane ke pratishodh main kiya gaya he. aadhi raat ko 26 disambar 2014 ko, janvari shikaar kiya gaya tha or chah agyaat ucch mulya lakshya ke saath ek gupt thikaane main cabar agency main vishesh balon dwaara maare gaye.
 pakistani khufia samudaay nirdhaarit epa ai e sabhi videshi ladaakon the, jise aatankwaadiyon kii rashtriyata nirdhaarit karne ke liye ek jaanch kaa aayojan kiya he. chah bandookdhaariyon kii pahchaan jari kiya gaya:• abu shaamil -ek chechan senaanee or samooh kaa sargana hone ke liye sochaa tha.• nauman shah helmond- helmond praant se ek afgaan nagarik; america shah par 500,000 dollars inaam rakhaa tha.• vajeer alam heraat- heraat se ek afgaan naagarik.• khatib al zubaidi- ek arabi vaktaa or misr ke ek naagarik.• mohammad zahedi- ek arabi vaktaa or morocco ke ek naagarik.• jibran al saidi-agyaat rashtriyata kii ek arab.
