 yashovarman kaa rajyakaal 700 se 740 i0 ke bich main rakhaa ja saktaa he. kannauj uski rajdhani thi. kanyakubj par iske pehle harsh kaa shashan tha jo bina uttaradhikari chode hi mar gayi jisase shakti kaa 'nirvaat' paidaa hua.
 yah bhi sambhbhaavana he ki use rajdhikaar isse pehle hi 690 i0 ke lagbhag mila ho. yashovarman ke vansh or uske praarambhik jivan ke vishay main kuch nishchayatmak nahin kaha ja saktaa. keval varman namat ke aadhaar par use maukhari vansh se sambandhit nahin kiya ja saktaa. jain granth bapp bhatta, suricharit or prabhaavak charit main use chandragupta maury kaa vanshaj kaha gaya he kintu yah sandigdh he. uskaa nalanda abhilekh is vishay par maun he. gaudwaho main use chandravanshi kshatriya kaha gaya he. gaudwaho main yashovarman kii vijayyatra kaa varnan he. sarvapratham iske baad bang ke naresh ne uski adheenta sweekaar kii. dakshini pathaar ke ek naresh ko adheen banaata hua, malay parvat ko paar kar vah samudratat tak pahunchaa. usne parsikon ko parajeet kiya or pashchimi ghat ke durgam pradeshon se bhi kar vasuul kiya. narmada nadi pahunchakar, samudratat ke sameep se vah maru desh pahunchaa. tatpashchaat shrikanth or kurushetra hote hue vah ayodhya gaya. mandar parvat par rahanewaalon ko adheen banaata hua vah himalay pahunchaa or apni rajdhani kannauj lautaa.
 is viran main paramparaagat digvijay kaa anusaran dikhalaai padataa he. parajeet raajaaon kaa naam na dene ke kaaran varnan sandigdh lagata he. yadi magadh ke parajeet naresh ko hi goud ke naresh sweekaar kar liya jaay to bhi is mukhya ghatana ko granth main jo sthaan diya gaya he vah atyalp he. kintu us yug kii raajnneetik paristhiti main aisi vijayon ko asambhav kahkar nahin choda ja saktaa. anya pramaanon se vibhinn dishaao main yashovarman kii kuch vijayon kaa sanket or samarthan praapt hota he. nalanda ke abhilekh main bhi uski prabhutaa kaa ullekh he. abhilekh kaa prapthisthaan magadh par uske adhikaar kaa pramaan he. chalukya abhilekhon main sakalottarapathanath ke rup main sambhavat: usee kaa nirdesh he or usee ne chalukya yuvaraj vijayaditya ko bandi banaaya tha. arabon kaa kannauj par akraman sabhavat: usee ke kaaran vifal hua. kashmir ke lalitaditya se bhi aarambh main uske sambandh maitreepurn the or sambhavat: donon ne arab or tibet ke viruddh china kii sahaayta chaahi ho kintu sheeghra hi lalitaditya or yashovarman kii mahatvakankshaon ke falswaroop dirghakaalin sangharsh hua. sandhi ke praytn asafal hue or yashonwarman parajeet hua. sambhavat: yuddh main yashovarman kii mrityu nahin hui thi, phir bhi itihaas ke liye uskaa astitv samaapt ho jaataa he.
 yashevarman ne bhawabhooti or vaakpatiraj jaise prasiddh kaviyon ko aashray diya tha. vah swayan kavi tha. subhashit grathon ke kuch padyon or ramabhyuday naatak kaa rachayita kaha jaataa he. usne magadh main apane naam se nagar basaaya tha. uskaa yash gaudwaho or rajtarangini ke atirikt jain granth prabhaavak charit, prabandhkosh or bappabhatta charit evam uske nalanda ke abhilekh main parilakshith hota he.
 kashmir se yashoverma ke naam ke sikke praapt hote hai. yashoverma ke sambandh main vidwaanon ne atakalbajiyaan lagaai thim. kuch ne use kannauj kaa yashovarman hi mana he. kintu ab isamen andeh nahin rah gaya he ki yashoverma kashmir ke utpalavansheey naresh shankaravarman kaa hi doosraa naam tha.
