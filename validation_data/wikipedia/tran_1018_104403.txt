 Min Dong biusi: Hók-gióng-sēng Hakka Pha̍k-fa-sṳ: Fuk-kien-sén
 foojyaan yaa foojiyaan janavaadee ganatantr china kaa ek praant he. yah china ke dakshin-purvi tat par sthit he or is se taiwan jaldamru ke paar 180 kimi kii samudri duuri par taiwan dweep sthit he. is praant kaa adhiktar bhaag janavaadee ganatantr china ke qabze main he lekin kinamen or matsu naamak do dweepasmooh taiwan dwaara niyantrit hai. is praant kii rajdhani fujhou shahar he.
 foojiyaan praant kaa adhiktar hissaa pahari he or paaramparik rup se is kshetra ko 'aath bhaag pahaad, ek bhaag pani, ek bhaag khet' kahkar vyakhyit kiya jaataa he. san 2009 main iske 62.96% ilaake par van faile hue the or yah puure china kaa sabase vangrast praant he. is praant kaa sabase unchaa shikhar vui parvat shrunkhlaa kaa 2157 meter unchaa hongang pahaad he.
 is praant ke adhiktar log haan chini nasal ke hai or unamem se ek 'hakka' naamak upashakha he jinaki apni hi ek bhashaa or samskruti he. hakka log praant ke dakshin-pashchim bhaag main basate hai. yahan ek shae naamak alpsankhyak jati bhi rahati he. duniya ke anya bhaagon main basane vaale bahut se chini log, visheshkar jo dakshin-purvi asia main basate hai, isi praant main jaden rakhte hai. foojiyaan praant kaa ilaaka pahari hone kii vajah se or sadiyon se yahan aate bhinn samudaayon ke basane ke kaaran yahan sthaan-sthaan par bhashaa badal jaati he. kaha jaataa he ki har 10 kilometre main log ek-dusre ko nahin samaj pate.
 is praant main chini nautanki ke sthaan-vishesh rup hai, jinamein se minju naamak rup mashhoor he.
