 victoria stadium, yaa victoria maidan, british pravasi shasit pradesh jibraltar main sthit bahu-prayojan khel kaa maidan he. maidan main vibhinn prakaar kii khel pratiyogtaon kaa aayojan hota he, inamen mukhyatah cricket, footbal, hockey or athletics kii pratispardhaayen. jibraltar ke kuch vidhyalay bhi apane khel diwas kaa aayojan isi maidan main karte hai. paanch hajaar kii kshamta vaala yah maidan beside sadak ke sameep sthit he. 
 victoria stadium jibraltar kaa sabase pramukh or aadhunik khel kaa maidan he tathaa iskaa swaamitv jibraltar kii sarkaar ke paas he. yahan jibraltar sarkaar ke khel se jude vibhinn vibhaago ke karyaalay bhi maujuud hai. inamen shaamil hai: jibraltar gavarment sports department, jibraltar sports and leisure athority or jibraltar sports adwiseri council.
 stadium main vibhinn khelon se judi kai aadhunik suvidhaayen uplabdh hai, jaise: bahu-prayojan khel haul jahaan basketbaal, badminton, wollyboul, natball, handball, paanch-ek-taraf footbal, tennis, karate, judo tathaa anya marshall art aadi khel khele ja sakate hai; squash court; footbal, hockey or cricket ke liye kritrim turf; footbal abhyaas pich; athletics treks or lambii kud pit; cricket nait abhyaas kend, aadi hai.
