 ramakant rath urisa ke katak nagar main nivas karne vaale oriya sahityakar hai. kete dinaar, anek kothri, sandigdh mrugaya, saptam ritu aadi unke pramukh kavita sangrah hai. unhen sarala puraskaar, sahitya academy puraskaar, vishuv puraskaar, saraswati sammaan tathaa kabir sammaan se alankrita kiya ja chukaa he. ramakant rath ko bharat sarkaar dwaara san 2006 main sahitya evam shiksha ke kshetra main padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. ye urisa ke hai.
