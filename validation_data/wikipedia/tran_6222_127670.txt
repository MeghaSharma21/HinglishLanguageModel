 santan-hatya us aparaadh ko kehete hai jiske antargat mata-pita jaan boojhkar apni hi santan kii hatya karte hai.
 1999 main sanyukt rajya america main kiye gaye shodh ke anusaar 1976 or 1997 ke bich maaen bachapan main marane vaale adhikansh bacchon kii mrityu ke zimmedaar thim. iske vipareet 8 saal yaa uske aage aayu ke bacchon kii hatya ke peeche adhiktar pitaaon kaa haath rahaa he. iske atirikt maaon dwaara hatya ke shikaar 52% ladke the jabaki pitaaon ke dwaara hatya ke shikaar 57% ladke the. kul milakar maan-baap paanch se kam umr ke 61% bacchon kii hatya ke doshi paae gaye hai. 
 kabhi-kabhi, in maamalon main hatya-aatmahatya donon maamalon kii talmail pai jaati he. sarkari aankadon ke anusaar america main prati varsh 450 bacchon ko unhii ke maan-baap badi hi nirmam hatya kar dete hai.
 gaiyr-paariwarik hatyaon kii shabdaawalee:
 iske atirikt, baalkon ke prati crururata or bal-hatya bhi prachlit hai.
