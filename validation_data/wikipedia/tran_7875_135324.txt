 shivsingh senger hindi ke itihaas lekhak the.
 shivsingh senger gram kantha jila unnav ke jameendaar shri ranjitsingh ke putr the. ve police inspector hote hue bhi sanskrit, farsi or hindi kavita ke adhyeta, rasik kavyapremi tathaa swayan bhi kavi the. "brahmottar khand" or "shivpurana" kaa hindi anuwaad karne ke atirikt aapaki prasiddhi hindi kavita ke pehle itihasgranth "shivsingh saroj" likhane ke kaaran he. hindi sahitya main unki apaar nishtha thi. unka nijee pustkaalay hindi sahitya kii pustakon se bharaa tha. jab bahut sari pustaken jamaa ho gayi to unke man main aayaa ki in granthon or kaviyon kaa parichay diya jaay.
 isamen lagbhag ek sahasr kaviyon ke jivan or kavy kaa atyant sankshipt parichay he. kaviyon ke jeevankaal aadi ke sambandh main kuch trutiyon ke hote hue bhi, jinka apane dhang ke pehle granth main hona bahut swaabhaavik he, is kriti ke liye hindi jagat sarvada unka abhaari rahegaa. dow grearsan kaa "modern varnakular litrechar ov hindustan" "shivsingh saroj" par hi lagbhag aadhaarit he. aaj bhi yah kriti hindi kavita ke itihaas ke liye sandarbhgranth bani hui he.
 kisi bhartiya vidwaan dwaara likhaa gaya yah pratham hindi-itihaas he. yah granth hindi vidwaanon main bahucharchit rahaa he. dow ramachandra shukla ne is granth se kaaphi sahaayta lee he. shivsingh saroj kaa pehla prakaashan san 1878 main hua. 1883 main iskaa tritiy sanskaran prakashit hua. isse isaki lokapriyata kaa anumaan lagaaya ja saktaa he. san 1926 main iskaa saatvaan sanskaran prakashit hua. 1970 main hindi sahitya sammelan, prayag se iskaa naveenatam sanskaran prakashit hua he.
