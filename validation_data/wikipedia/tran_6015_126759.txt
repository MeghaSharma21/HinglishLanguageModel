 vidyut aadhunik jivan ke sabhi kshetron ke liye anivaary aavashyaktaa he or ise mul maanviya aavashyaktaa ke rup main mana gaya he. yah mahatvapurn mul sanrachana he jis par desh kaa saamaajik-aarthik vikaas nirbhar karata he. pratispardhi daron par bharosemand or gunvattta vidyut kii uplabdhta arthvyavastha ke sabhi kshetron ke vikaas ko banaae rakhane ke liye bahut hi mahatvapurn he arthath praathmik, dwitiyak or tritiyak. yah gharelu bazaaron ko vaishvik rup se pratispardhi banane main sahaayta karti he or is prakaar se logon kaa jivan str sudharta he.
 bharat ke samvidhan ke antargat bijli samavarti suchi kaa vishay he jisaki saatvin anusuchee kii suchi iii main pravishti sankhya 38 he. bharat vishwa kaa chatha sabase bada urja upabhokta he jo vishwa ke kul urja khapat kaa 3.5 pratishat upbhog karata he. tapiya, jal bijli or nabhikeey urja bharat main bijli utpaadan ke mukhya strot hai. kul santhaapit vidyut utpaadan kshamta 1,47,402.81 megawatt, rahi he, jisamen 93,392.64 megawatt ; 36,647.76 megawatt ; 4,120 megawatt ; or 13,242.41 megawatt shaamil hai.
