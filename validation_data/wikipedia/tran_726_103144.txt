 kisi raktavahika ke andar rakt ke jam jane ) falswaroop rakt ke pravaah ko baadhit karne ko 'ghanaasrata' kehete hai. jab kahin koi raktavahika kshatigrast ho jaati he to platelets or fibrin milakar rakt kaa thakka banaakar rakt kii haani ko roak dete hai. kintu ghanaasrata isse alag he. raktavahiniyon ke bina kshatigrast hue bhi kuch sthitiyon main vahikaaon ke andar rakt kaa thakka ban saktaa he. yadi thakka bahut teevr he or vah vah ek sthaan se duusare sthaan par gatiman he to aise thakke ko vahikarodhi kehete hai.
 ghanaasrata or vahikarodhi dono vidyaman hon to ise 'thromboimbolism' kehete hai.
 jivitavastha main jab tak raktavahikaaon kaa antahkala swasth hoti he tab tak bheetar bahanevaala rakt taral rahata hai, parantu aghat, pradaah, hridayadaurbalya ityadi kaarnon se vah vikrut ho jaataa he. tab vikrut sthaan main rakt jamata he, jisko 'ghanaasrata' kehete hai. dhamniyon kii apeksha shirayen chaudee tathaa unki diwaar patali hone se unamem ghanaasrata utpann hone kii sambhaavna adhik rahati he. jis disha main rakt kaa daab kam hota jaataa he us disha main ghanasr failaa karata he. yah vahika kii sameepvarti shakha tak avashya fail jaataa he. ghanaasrata kaa pariman uske sthaan par, vistaar par, vahika ke prakaar par tathaa use putidooshit, yaa aputidooshit, hone par nirbhar hota he. ati vriddhavastha main mastishk kii tathaa uske aavarnon kii shiraaon main ghanaasrata hone kii adhik sambhaavna rahati he. vriddhavastha main honevli ghanaasrata ek hi saptaah main praaia: dhatak ho jaati he. putidooshit ghanaasrata se phode banate hai or aage ke dushprinaam usee ke kaaran hote hai.
 ghanasr vahika ke ekaadh sthaan par chipakkar baaki swatantra rahata he or aghat, sthanparivartan, aakasmik gati ityadi se tuutakar, yaa alag hokar, doorvarti sthanon main ja atakta he. isko 'vahikarodh' kehete hai. iske dushprinaam ghanasr ke moolsthaan, vistaar tathaa uske putidooshit yaa aputik hone par nirbhar hote hai. shiraaon kii, yaa dakshin hridayardh kii, ghanaasrata kaa vahikarodh phufphuson main jaakar atakta he. yadi vah bada hua to fofphusik dhamniyon main margvirodh karke ghaatak hota he. shalyakarm yaa prasav ke pashchaat honevaali aakasmik mrityu praaia: isi prakaar se hua karti he. yadi vah chota rahaa, to phuffus kaa alpaansh bekaar hokar thodi si bechaini utpann hoti he, jo praaia: alpakaal main thik ho jaati he. antahshalya pootik hone se phoda, koth yaa antahpuyata utpann hoti he. hriday ke vamardh kii ghanaasrata se shaareerik dhamniyon main vahikarodh utpann hota he.
 yadhyapi vahikarodh kaa ghatak saadharantaya rakt kaa thakka hota he, tathaapi vasa or vaayu ke bhi vahikarodh banate hai. vasa-vahikarodh asthibhang main majja se tathaa vaat-vahikarodh shira main vayupravesh se hote hai.
