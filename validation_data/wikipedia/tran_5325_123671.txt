 richa chaddha bollywood main karyrat ek abhinetri hai. richa chaddha ne anuraag kashyap kii film clic karen 'gangs of vasepur' main ek adhed umr mahila kaa kirdaar nibhaayaa he. isse pehle wo dibaakar banerjee kii film 'oe lucky lucky oe' main heroin nitu chandra kii badi bahan kaa role nibhaa chuki hai. phukare, goliyon kii raasalila ramlila, benny and bablu aadi filmo main kaam kiya hai.
 richa kaa janm 28 disambar 1988 main amritsar, punjab main hua, lekin baad main vah dilli aa gayi. richa ne apane filmi safar kii shuruyaat saal 2008 main ai kamedi drama film ‘oe lakki! lakki oe’ se kii thi. shruwati dinon main richa ne bharat ke alaava pakistan main bhi kai naatak prastut kiye hai.
 richa ne apane kariyar kii shuruyaat modelling se kii thi or iske baad unhone theatre kii ore apana rukh kar liya.
 richa ko unki bollywood debyue film ‘oe lakki! lakki oe’ main nibhaae gaye kirdaar ke liye kaaphi vaahvahee mili. is film ke baad richa ‘benny and bablu’ main bhi nazar ain, lekin unki yah film kuch khaas nahin chali.
 richa ne pehali baar surkhiyaan saal 2012 main ai film ‘gangs of vasepur’ se batori. film main nagma khatun kaa kirdaar nibhaane vaali richa ko is film ke liye sabaki jamkar vaahvahee mili. is film ke baad vah ‘gangs of vasepur-2’ main nazar ain or ek baar phir unhone apane abhinay kaa damakham dikhaaya."
 richa beete saal yani 2013 main bhi teen filmon main najar ain. unki ye teen filmein thim ‘phukare’, ‘shorts’ or ‘ram-lila’. in teenon filmon main se film ‘phukare’ or ‘ram-lila’ main richa ke abhinay kii sabane saraahna kii, khaas taur par unhen 'ram-lila' main rasila ke andaaj main kaaphi pasand kiya gaya. richa ke paas 2014 main bhi kai filmein hai, jinamein ‘ghumaketu’ or tamanchey pramukh he.
