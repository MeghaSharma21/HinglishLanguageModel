 bhartiya rajasav seva, laGu rUp IRS, bharat sarkaar kii bhartiya civil seva ke antargatt rajasav seva he. yah seva vitt mantralay ke adheen rajasav vibhaag ke antargatt karyrat he. iskaa mukhya uddeshy vibhinn pratyaksh kar evam apratyaksh karon kaa sangrah k kendra sarkaar ko uplabdh karana he. 1924 main kendriya rajasav board adhinium ne iskar adhinium ke prashasan ke liye prakaaryaatmak jimmedaari ke saath kendriya rajasav board sanvidhik nikaay kaa gathan kiya. prayek praant ke liye iskar ayukta niyukt kiye gaye tathaa sahaayak ayukta tathaa iskar adhikari unke niyantranaadhin rakhe gayi. sarvocya padon ke liye ai si es se adhikariyon ko liya gaya or nimnatar sopan padon ko pronnati ke madhyam se bharaa gaya. iskar seva kii sthapana 1944 main kii gai, jo baad main bhartiya rajasav seva ke naam se jaani gai. 
 iskar adhikariyon kaa pehla batch ai e or e es tathaa sambanddh sevaaon ke liye sandheey seva ayog dwaara sanchalit 1943 pariksha ke madhyam se barsh 1944 main sidhe bhartee kiya gaya.
