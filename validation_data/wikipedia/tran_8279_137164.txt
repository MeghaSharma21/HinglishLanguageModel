 dakkan kaa pathaar jise vishaal prayadwipiya pathaar ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he, bharat kaa vishaltam pathaar he. dakshin bharat kaa mukhya bhu bhaag is hi pathaar par sthit he. yah pathaar tribhujaakar he. isaki uttar kii seemaa satapura or vindhyaachal parvat shrunkhlaa dwaara or purv or pashchim kii seemaa kramshah purvi ghat evam pashchimi ghat dwaara nirdhaarit hoti he. yah pathaar bharat ke 8 rajyo main failaa hua he.
 dakkan shabd sanskrit ke shabd dakshin kaa angreji apbhransh shabd daccan kaa hindikaran he.yah bharat bhu bhaag kaa sabase vishaal khand he!
