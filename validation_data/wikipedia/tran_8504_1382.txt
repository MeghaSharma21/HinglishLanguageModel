 malvan yaa maalvan ,bharat ke rajya maharashtra main dakshin main sthit sindhudurg jile, kaa ek kasbaa he. saanskrithik or aitihaasik drushti se mahatvapurn yah kasbaa sindhudurg jile kaa ek taluka bhi he. malvan shivaji dwaara nirmit sindhudurg or malvani bhojan ke liye mashhoor he. yah sthaan aamon kii vibhinn kismon jaise ki alphonso or malvani hapus ke liye bhi prasiddh he. iske saath hi yahan banane vaali mithaiyaan jaise ki malvani kheja or malvani laddu puure maharashtra or nikatavarti kshetron main prasiddh hai.
 puranic kahaniyon par aadhaarit naatak, malvani dashavathar, malvani samskruti kaa ek bahut hi mahatvapurn hissaa he. prasiddh naatakkaar or tivi abhinayta shri machhindra kambli malvan se aate hai.
 malvan ke naam kii utpatti se sambandhit kai managasaint or kuch vishwaas yogya kahaniyaan prachlit hai. ek prachlit kahani ke anusaar malvan kshetra main badi maatra main namak kaa utpaadan kiya jaataa tha. namak utpaadak is kshetra ko namak kii adhikta ke chalate "mahaalavan" kah kar sambodhit karte the. duusari ek sambhaavna ke anusaar malvan shabd malvani boli ke do shabdon "mad" yani nariyal or "ban" yani ki baag ke dhvanyatmak mail se mil kar bana he or jiskaa arth he wo baag jahaan nariyal ke ped bahutayat main milte hai.
 bharat kii janaganana 2001 ke anusaar malvan kii jansankhya 18675 thi jisamen purushon kaa pratishat 51 or mahilayon kaa pratishat 49 tha. malvan kii sakshartaa dar 81% thi jisamen 85% purush or 77% mahilayen sakshar thim. malvan kii 9% jansankhya 6 varsh se kam aayu ke bacchon kii thi.
