 
 mendhak ubhaychar varg kaa jantu he jo pani tathaa jameen par donon jagah rah saktaa he. yah ek sheetarakti praani he arthath iske sharir kaa taapmaana vaataavaran ke taap ke anusaar ghatata yaa badhata rahata he. sheetkaal main yah thandak se bachane ke liye pokhar aadi kii nichali sataha kii mitti lagbhag do foot kii gaharaai tak khodakar usee main padaa rahata he. yahan tak ki kuch khaataa bhi nahin he. is kriya ko sheetanidra yaa shitasushuptavastha kehete hai. isi tarah kii kriya garmi ke dinon main hoti he. greeshmakaal kii is niskriy avasthaa ko greeshmasushuptavastha kehete hai.
 mendhak ke chaar pair hote hai. pichley do pair agle pairon se bade hoten hai. jiske kaaran yah lambi oochaal letaa he. agle pairon main chaar-chaar tathaa pichley pairon main paanch-paanch jhillidaar unglia hotin hai, jo ise tairne main sahaayta karti hai. mendhakon kaa aakaar 9.8 millimetre se lekar 30 centimetre tak hota he. nar saadharantah maadaa se aakaar main chote hote hai. mendhakon kii twachaa main vishagranthiyaan hotin hai, parantu ye shikari stanpayi, pakshi tathaa saanpon se inaki suraksha nahin kar paati hai.
 bhek yaa dadur tathaa mendhak main kuch antar he jaise dadur adhiktar jameen par rahata he, isaki twachaa shushk evam jhurridaar hoti he jabaki mendhak kii twachaa komal evam chikani hoti he. mendhak kaa sir tikona jabaki tod kaa arddh-vrittakar hota he. bhek ke pichley pair kii anguliyon ke bich jhilli bhi nahin milti he. parantu vaigyanik vargeekaran kii drushti se donon bahut had tak samaan jantu hai tathaa ubhaychar varg ke anura gana ke antargat aate hai. mendhak praayah sabhi jagahon par paae jate hai. isaki 5000 se adhik prajatiyon kii khoj ho chuki he. varsha wanon main inaki sankhya sarvaadhik he. kuch prajatiyon kii sankhya teji se kam ho rahi he.
