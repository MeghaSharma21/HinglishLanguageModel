 bhupatal ke bhugolic platen dabaav yaa tanaav ke kaaran santulan kii avasthaa main nahin rahati. jab bhi platon main khinchaav adhik bad jaataa he, athwa shilaon par donon parshv se padaa dabaav unki sahan shakti ke baahar hota he, tab shilaayein anke prabhaav se visthapit ho jaati hai athwa tut jaati hai. ek ore kii shilaayein duusari ore kii shilaon kii apeksha neeche yaa upar chali jaati hai. ise hi bhransh kehete hai.
 kshetra bhautikee main bhranshon kaa vishesh mahatv he. bhranshon ke parinamswaroop kabhi-kabhi neeche chhipe khanij bhandar sataha par aa jate hai. neeche chhipe bahut se koyale ke staron kaa isi prakaar pataa laga he. iske vipareet kabhi kabhi apradan ke kaaran vigopit bhandar nasht bhi ho jate hai. sopanbhranshon main jalpravaah se bade bade prapaton kii rachana hoti hai, jinase vidyut utpann kii jaati he. bahut se bhransh samtal jharanon ke udgam sthaan bhi hai, abhyantarik jal inke dwaara hi sataha par aata he.
 kshetra main bhranshon kaa pataa lagaana bhuvigyaani ke liye koi duruh kaary nahin he. bhransh ke sthaan par kii shilaayein chikani hoti hai. aisa prathit hota he maanon unpar polish kii gai ho. iske atirikt staron kaa achaanak lupt ho janaa, yaa ek hi se staron kaa do baar milanaa, bhransh sakonshm kii upasthiti aadi bhranshon ko pahchaanane ke anya sadhak chinh hai. par keval koi bhuvigyaani hi in chinhon kaa uchit arthnirnay kar saktaa he, kyonki kabhi kabhi vibhinn rachanaaon main samaan chinh dikhaai dete hai.
 vah samtal, jisapar se shilaayein tuutati hai, bhransh samtal kehalaata he. bhransh samtal urdhwadhar na hokar ek ore ko jhukaa rahata he. urdhwadhar samtal se bhransh samtal kaa jitna jhukaav hota he, vah uskaa unnaman kehalaata he. bhransh samtal or kshaitij samtal ke bich kaa kone bhransh kaa naman kehalaata he. bhransh ke prabhaav main shilaon kaa visthaapan hota he. lambavat visthaapan ko urdhwadhar visthaapan tathaa kshaitij disha main visthaapan ko kshaitij visthaapan kehete hai. bhransh ke parinmaswaroop jo bhaag apekshaakrit upar rahata he, use utkshep kehete hai tathaa jo bhaag apekshaakrit neeche aata he vah ad:kshep kehalaata he.
 bhransh kai prakaar ke hote hai. unamem se mukhya neeche die gaye hai: vah bhransh, jisamen ek ore kii shilaayein apane mul sthaan se apekshaakrit neeche kii ore chali jaati hai, anukram bhransh kehalaata he. iske vipareet kabhi kabhi ek ore kii shilaayein mul sthaan se upar kii ore chadh jaati hai. ise utkrambhransh kehete hai.
 yadi bhransh ke prabhaav main shilaon kaa visthaapan naman disha kii ore hota he, arthath naman disha ke samaantar hota he, to ise naman bhransh tathaa naman se lamb disha main hone par use anudairdhyabhransh kii sangya di jaati he. par yadi bhransh na to naman disha kii or or na naman se lamb disha ke anukool ho, to ise tirachha ya tiryak bhransh kehete hai. kabhi kabhi shilaon main ek ke baad doosraa, phir teesraa, is prakaar kai bhransh hote hai. yadi in bhranshon ke unnaman kii disha ek hi ore ko hoti hai, to seedhi ke aakaar kii rachana ban jaati he. in bhranshon ko sopanbhransh naam diya gaya he. yadi do bhranshon kaa unnaman ek duusare kii ore hota he, to donon bhranshon ke bich kaa bhaag apekshaakrit neeche chalaa jaataa he. ise dronikabhransh kehete hai.
 iske vipareet bhranshotya main bhranshon kaa unnaman vipareet disha main hota he falswaroop donon bhranshon ke bich kaa bhaag ek kutak ke samaan upar uthaa dikhaai padataa he.
