 sudhanoti azaad kashmir kaa ek zila he. san 1995 tak yah poonch zile kii ek tehsil hua karata tha lekin us varsh main ise alag karke zile kaa darja de diya gaya. is zile kii rajdhani palandri he.
 is zile ke log adhiktar punjabi kii upbhashaayen bolte hai. yahan sudhan or awan naamak punjabi samudaayon kaa jor he or is zile kaa naam bhi sudhan logon par padaa he.
 sudhanoti zile main teen tahseelein hai - palandri, maang or traarkaal.
