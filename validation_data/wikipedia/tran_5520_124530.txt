 sanchi bharat ke madhya pradesh rajya ke raisen jile, main betwa nadi ke tat sthit ek chota sa gaanv he. yah bhopal se 46 kimi purvottar main, tathaa besanagar or vidisha se 10 kimi kii duuri par madhya pradesh ke madhya bhaag main sthit he. yahaan kai buddha smarak hai, jo teesri shatabdi i.pus. se barahavin shatabdi ke bich ke kaal ke hai. sanchi raisen jile kii ek nagar panchayat he. raisen jile main ek anya vishwa daay sthal, bheemabetkar bhi he. vidisha se najadik hone ke kaaran logon main yah bhram hota he kii yah vidisha jila main he. yahan chote-bade anekon stupa hai, jinamein stupa sankhya 2 sabase bada he. chaaron ore kii hariyaali adbhut he. is stupa ko ghere hue kai torana bhi bane hai. stupa sankhya 1 ke paas kai laghu stupa bhi hai, unhi ke sameep ek gupt kaaleen pashaan stambh bhi he. yah prem, shanti, vishwaas or saahas ke pratik hai. sanchi kaa mahaan mukhya stupa, multah samraat ashok mahaan ne teesri shatee, i.pus. main banwaaya tha. iske kendra main ek ardhgolakaar eent nirmit dhaancha tha, jisamen bhagwaan buddha ke kuch avshesh rakhe the. iske shikhar par smarak ko diye gayi oonche sammaan kaa pratik rupi ek chhatra tha..
 is stupa main ek sthaan par duusari shatabdi i.pus. main todphod kii gai thi. yah ghatana shunga samraat pushyamitra shunga ke utthan se jodakar dekhi jaati he. yah mana jaataa he ki pushyamitra ne is stupa kaa dhwans kiya hoga or baad main, uske putr agnimitra ne ise punarnirmit karavaaya hoga. . shunga vansh ke antim varshon main, stupa ke mul rup kaa lagbhag duguna vistaar pashaan shilaon se kiya gaya tha. iske gumbad ko upar se chapata karke, iske upar teen chhatariyaan, ek ke upar duusari karke banavaayin gayin thim. ye chhatariyaan ek vargakar munder ke bheetar baniin thim. apane kai manjilon sahit, iske shikhar par dharm kaa pratik, vidhi kaa chakra laga tha. yah gumbad ek oonche golakaar dhol rupi nirmaan ke upar laga tha. iske upar ek do-manjila jeene se pahunchaa ja saktaa tha. bhoomi str par bana duusari pashaan parikrama, ek ghere se ghiri thi. iske bich pradhan dishaao kii ore kai torana bane the. dwitiya or tritiy stupa kii imaaraten shunga kaal main nirmit prathit hotin hai, parantu vahaan mili shilaalekh anusaar ucch str ke alankrita torana shunga kaal ke nahin the, inhen baad ke satvaahan vansh dwaara banwaaya gaya tha. iske saath hi bhoomi str kii pashaan parikrama or mahaan stupa kii pashaan aadharshila bhi usee kaal kaa nirmaan hai.
 torana evam parikrama 70 i.pus. ke pashchaat bane the or satvaahan vansh dwaara nirmit prathit hote hai. ek shilaalekh ke anusaar dakshin ke torana kii sarvocch choukhat satvaahan raja satakarni kii ore se uphaar swaroop mili thi: "yah anand, vasithi putr kii ore se uphaar he, jo rajan satakarni ke kaarigaron kaa pramukh he." stupa yadhyapi pashaan nirmit hai, kintu kashtha kii style main gadhe hue torana, varnatmak shilpon se paripurna hai. inamen buddha kii jivan kii ghatnaayen, dainik jivan style se jodakar dikhaai gayi hai. is prakaar dekhne waalon ko buddha kaa jivan or unki vaani bhalee prakaar se samaj main aata he. sanchi or adhikansh anya stupon ko sanvaarane hetu sthaneey logon dwaara bhi daan diye gayi the, jisase un logon ko aadhyatm kii prapti ho sake. koi sidhaa rajasi aashray nahin uplabdh tha. daankarta, chaahe stri yaa purush hon, buddha ke jivan se sambandhit koi bhi ghatana chun lete the or apana naam vahaan khudva dete the. kai khaas ghatnaon ke dohraane kaa, yahi kaaran tha. in pashaan nakkashiyon main, buddha ko kabhi bhi maanav akruti main nahin darshaya gaya he. balki kaarigaron ne unhen kahin ghoda, jisapar ve apane pita ke ghar kaa tyaag kar ke gayi the, to kahin unke padchinh, kahin bodhi vriksh ke neeche kaa chabutara, jahaan unhen buddhatv kii prapti hui thi, ke rup main darshaya he. buddha ke liye maanav sharir ati tuchh mana gaya tha. sanchi kii deevaaron ke bordaron par bane chitron main unani pahanava bhi darshneey he. isamen unani vastra, mudra or vaadya hai jo ki stupa ke alankaran rup main prayukt hue hai.
 aage kii shatabdiyon main anya buddha dharm or hindu nirmaan bhi jode gayi. yah vistaar barahavin shatabdi tak chalaa. mandir san.17, sambhawatah pracheenatam buddha mandir he, kyonki yah aarmabhik gupt kaal kaa lagata he. isamen ek chapati chat ke vargakar garbhagrih main dwaar mandap or chaar stambh hai. aage kaa bhaag or stambh vishesh alankrita or nakkashikrut he, jinase mandir ko ek paramparaagat chhavi milti he, kintu andar se or shesh teenon ore se samtal he or anlankrita he. bharat main buddha dharm ke patan ke saath hi, sanchi kaa stupa aprayogneey or upekshit ho gaya or is khandit avasthaa ko pahunchaa.
 ek british adhikari general tailor pehle gyaat itihaaskaar the, inhone san 1818 main, sanchi ke stupa kaa astitv darj kiya. avyavsaayee puratatvvettaon or khajaane ke shikariyon ne is sthal ko khub dhwans kiya, jab tak ki san 1818 main uchit jirnoddhar kaary nahin armbh hua. 1912 se 1919 ke bich, dhaanche ko vartmaan sthiti main laayaa gaya. yah sab jon marshall kii dekharekh main hua. aaj lagbhag pachaas smarak sthal sanchi ke tile par gyaat hai, jinamein teen stupa or kai mandir bhi hai. yah smarak 1989 main unesco dwaara vishwa dharohar sthal ghoshit hua he.
 sanchi kii bhugolic avasthiti 23.48° uttar akshansh evam 77.73° purv deshaantar par he. isaki samudratal se ausat oonchai 434 meter he. udaygiri se sanchi paas hi he. yahan buddha stupa hai, jinamein ek kii oonchaai 42 feet he. sanchi stupon kii kala prakhyat he. sanchi se 5 mile sonari ke paas 8 buddha stupa hai or sanchi se 7 mile par bhojpur ke paas 37 buddha stupa hai. sanchi main pehle buddha vihaar bhi the. yahan ek sarovar he, jisaki seedhiyaan buddha ke samay kii kahi jaati hai.
 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar sanchi kii jansankhya 6785 he. purush yahaan kaa 53% or striyaan 47% bhaag hai. yahaan kii ausat sakshartaa dar 67% he, jo ki rashtriya dar 59.5% se kahin adhik he. purush sakshartaa 75% or stri sakshartaa 57% he. yahaan kii 16% janata chah varsh se kam kii he.
 yahan rail maarg, sadak maarg or vayumarg teenon se pahunchaa ja saktaa he. sabase najdiki railway stesan to swany sanchi hi he, par badi gaadiyaan vahaan nahin rukati hai. vidisha yaa bhopal se aap yahan aa sakate hai, par vidisha stesan sabase najadik he. vayumarg se bhopal se utar kar, sadak maarg se yahan pahunchaa ja saktaa he. vishwa daay sthal hone ke kaaran yah suryoday se suryaast tak khulaa rahata he. sameep hi mukhya sadak se aane par sangrahalay bhi he.
