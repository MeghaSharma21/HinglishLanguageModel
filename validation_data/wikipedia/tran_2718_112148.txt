 maniram mishra kannauj ‘saroj surveykshan’ main maniram naamak teen kaviyon kaa ullekh miltaa he, jinamein do mishra hai- maniram mishra, sandi, kanpur or maniram mishra kannauj. kannauj vaale maniram mishra kaa upasthit kaal san0-1839 he. maniram mishra ne ‘chand chappani’ naamak ek sundar granth kii rachana kii he, jisamen pingal ke sanketon ko bhalee-bhanti kholaa gaya he. nagari prachaarini sabha kaashi kii khoj rirpot 1912/106 main chand chappani kaa ullekh he.
