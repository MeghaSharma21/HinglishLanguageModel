 kaha jaataa he ki san 1589 main italy ke vaigyanik galileo gallily ne pisa kii jhuki minar se alag-alag dravyaman ke do gole giraakar ek prayog kiya tha. is prayog kaa uddeshy yah pradarshit karnaa tha ki kisi oonchaai se girakar jameen tak pahunchne main laga samay giraayi gayi vastuon ke dravyaman par nirbhar nahin karata. is baat kaa ullekh galileo ke shishy vincenjo viviani ne kiya he.
