 samir hindi filmon ke ek prasiddh geetkaar hai. inke zyaadaatar geet hit hue or inke dwaara likhen gaye geet aaj bhi logon kii jubaanon par hai. unke pita anjaan bhi geetkaar rahe the. unke paas sabase adhik geet likhane kaa gineez vishwa kirtiman he. unhone lagbhag 650 filmon main 4000 se adhij gane likhe hai. unhen yash bharti puraskaar bhi mila he.
 pehle vah padhai kar bank main naukari karte the. unke pita nahin chaahate ki wo filmi udyog main aae kyonki unhone khub sangharsh kiya tha. lekin samir kaa dhyaan usee taraf tha or 1980 main wo bombay palaayan kar gaye. unhen sabase pehle 1983 kii film bekhbar main geet likhane kaa avasar mila tha. iske baad unhone kai badi filmon main geet likhen jisamen insaaf koun karegaa, jawaab ham denge, do kaidi, rakhwala, mahaasangram or baap nambari betaa das nambari shaamil hai. lekin unako prasiddhi or pahchaan 1990 kii do filmon dil or aashiqui se mili. unhone in filmon ke sangeetkaar anand-milind or nadeem-shravan ke saath kai prashansit or lokapriy geeton kii rachana kii. visheshkar nadeem-shravan ke saath unka vishesh rishta tha. kuchhek filmon ko chhodkar unhone har film main geetkaar ke rup main samir ko hi liya or teenon filmafere puraskaar unhen nadeem-shravan ke sangitabaddh geeton ke liye hi mili. samir majaruh sultanpuri or anand bakshi se prabhavit hai or unhen apane pita sahit prerana strot maanate hai.
