 pulomilo bharat ke nicobar dweepasmooh kaa ek dweep he. yah chote nicobar dweep ke bahut sameep sthit he or in donon kii duuri 1 kimi se bhi kam he.
 pulomilo par dakshin nicobari bhashaa boli jaati he, jo chote nicobar, bade nicobar, condul or kuch anya dwipon par bhi bolte hai, haalanki pulomilo kii "channai payuh" kahalaane vaali upbhasha main kuch sthaneey visheshtaayen hai. yahan kii adhiktar aabaadi nicobari samudaay kii he. dharmik rup se log lagbhag sau varshon se isai hai par mul nicobari dharm kii asthaayen abhi bhi milti hai. 2004 kii sunami kaa is dweep par bhayankar prakope hua tha or dweep kii lagbhag aadhi jansankhya us jalpralay kaa shikaar ho gai thi. 2001 kii bhartiya rashtriya janaganana main pulomilo kii jansankhya 214 thi, jisamen se 2005 main keval 109 jivit the.
 dweep ke 1.3 vag kimi ke kshetrafal main se 1.29 varg kimi van se dhakaa hua he or yahan par keval ek hi maanviya baseraa he, jiskaa naam bhi pulomilo he. dweep par vidyut kaa prabandh nahin he or aane-jane ke liye nauka hi prayog hoti he. nicobar ke anya gaanvo kii tarah yahan bhi gharon ko khambon par zamin se upar uthaai gai kutiyaaon ke rup main banaaya gaya he. pratham drushti main dweep haraa-bharaa or sundar prathit hota he.
