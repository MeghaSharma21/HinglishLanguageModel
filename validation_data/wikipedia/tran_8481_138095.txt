 vyatikaran se kisi bhi prakaar kii tarangon kii ek duusare par parsparik prakriya kii abhivyakti hoti he, jiske parinamswaroop kuch vishesh sthitiyon main companon or unke prabhaavon main vruddhi, kami yaa udaseenta aa jaati he. vyatikaran kaa vistrut adhyayan vishaal vibhedan shakti vaale sabhi yantron ke mul main kaam karata he.
 bhautik prakaashiki main is dhaaran kaa samavesh thomas yang ne kiya. unke baad vyatikaran kaa vyavhaar kisi bhi tarah kii tarangon yaa companon ke samavet yaa tajjanya prabhaavon ko vyakt karne ke liye kiya jaataa rahaa he. sankshep main kisi bhi tarah kii tarangagati ke kaaran laharon ke takaraav se utpann sthiti ko vyatikaran kii sangya di jaati he. jab kabhi jal yaa anya kisi drav kii sataha par do bhinn tarangasamooh ek saath milen to vyatikaran kii sthiti utpann ho sakati he. jahaan ek tarangasamooh se sambanddh laharon ke tarangashringon kaa duusari shrunkhlaa se sambhddhalaharon ke tanrgashringon se sammilin hota he, vahaan drav kii sataha kaa unnayan us sthaan par laharon ke swatantra or ekant astitv ke sambhav unnayanon ke yoga ke baraabar hota he. jab tarangon main se ek ke tarangashring kaa duusare ke tarangagart par samapathan hota he, tab drav kii sataha par tarangon kaa udwelan kam ho jaataa he or pratifalit unnayan ek tarang avyav ke unnayan or duusare ke avanayan ke antar ke baraabar hota he. dhwani main utpann vispand isi vyatikaran kaa ek saadhaaran rup he, jahaan do yaa do se adhik tarangasamooh, jinke tarangadairdhya main maamuli sa antar hota he, kareeb ek hi disha main agrasar hote hue milte hai.
 prakaash kii gat tarangeey hoti he. kisi ekal prakashasrot se ni:sruth urja madhyam ke parshv main saan rup se bikhar jaati he. yadi prakaash ke do swatantra strot, jinase samaan pariman or abhinn kala kii tarangen satat ni:sruth hon, ek duusare ke sannikat rakhe jaaen, to madhyam ke aasapaas urja kaa vitaran samang nahin hota, jahaan ek prakashtarang kaa shrung duusare prakashtarang ke shrung par, yaa ek kaa tarangagart duusare ke tarangagart par girtaa he, vahaan aayam bad jaataa he or aayam swaroop urja yaa prakaash kii tivrata bhi bad jaati he. saath hi, yadi ek kaa tarangashring duusare ke tarangagart par gire, to parinami aayam shunya hota he or prakaash kii tivrata ghat jaati he. pehali sthiti kaa samposhi vyatikaran or duusari sthiti ko vinashi vyatikaran kehete hai.
 paardarshee thos ke patale patton or sabun ke bulabulon par prakaash kii kirnon ke padane par vyatikaran kaa spasht parichay mil saktaa he. jab prakaash kii kirane sabun ke bulabulon, yaa sise kii patale patton par padati hai, to unki bahari or bhitari donon satahon se kirane paravartit hokar prekshak kii aankhon kii ore lautati hai or prakaash ke tarangasamoohon main, jo donon sroton se aankhon tak pahunchati hai, kalaon main sookshm antar hone ke kaaran vyatikaran hota he, jisase utpann prabhaav kaaphi mohak ore chittkarshak hote hai. sabun kaa koi bulbula ekvarni prakaash main praaia: kuch kaali rekhaon se aavruth dikhaai padataa he. kaaran yah he ki kaale dikhaai padanevaale binduon par prakaash ke do tarangasamooh, jo kramash: bulbule kii bhitari or bahari satahon se aate hai, kareeb kareeb yaa purnat: ek duusare ke prabhaav ko nasht kar dete hai. yadi bulbula shwet prakaash main dekha jaae, to hamen saamanyatya kaali rekhaayen nahin dikhaai padatin. unke sthaan par rangon kii pattiyaan hoti hai. aisa isliye hota he ki vibhinn rang, jinke yoga se shwet prakaash kii utpatti hoti he bhinn bhinn tarangon ke hote hai, jisase bulbule ke kisi bindu par vyatikaran se rang ke keval ek ansh maatr kaa vinaash hota he or ujle prakaash ke shesh avyav batch rahete hai, jo aankhon par apana purna varniya prabhaav utpann karte hai.
 vyatikaran ke liye kuch moulik sharte hai jinaki purti aavashyak he. inamen se kuch to prakaash kii prakruti main hi antarnihit he or duusari, yadi parinaam kaa prekshan prayog dwaara karnaa hua to, aavashyak ho uthati he. saralata ke liye ham do vidyut chumbakeey laharon par vichaar kar sakate hai, jo kisi dikbindu par, jahaan se donon lehren guzarati hai, vinashi vyatikaran utpann karen.
 yadi vyatikaran kaa praturup sthir rahaa, arthath yadi prakaash kii tivrata kaa parinami tathakthith bindu par samay ke prayek maan ke liye shunya ho, to nimnlikhit sharton kii purti aavashyak he : 
 prakaash dwaara utpann pratirupon ke safal prekshan ke liye do anya sharten, jinaki purti honi chahie, nimnlikhit hai, 
 yadi do atisannikat prakashasrot ke samaan pariman or kalantar kii taragen kisi kalantar vishesh par kuch dur sthit parde ke ek bindu par milen, to parde par kuch bilkul kii rekhaayen jinke antaraal main adhiktam tivrata kii rekhaayen rahati hai, dekhi jaati hai. ve nunatam or adhiktam tivrata kii rekhaayen vyatikaran fringen kehalaati hai.
 jab kabhi vyatikaran fringen patali filmon ke chalate banati hai, tab unka kaaran vyatikaran main bhaag lenevaali kirnon ke kalantar kaa parivartan hota he. yah parivartan film kii motaai ke parivartan, yaa aaytan kone ke parivartan, ke kaaran hota he. yadi motaai samang nahin hui, to praaia: donon tathy ek hi saath kriyasheel ho uthte hai; lekin ek baat spasht he ki jab koi film aankh dwaara dekhi ja rahi he, to use aankh se kareeb 25 senmi. kii duuri par rakhaa janaa chahie.
 yadi film kaa paraas bahut bada na ho, to hamaari aankhon tak film ke vibhinn binduon se aati hui kirnon ke jhukaav kii bhinnata koi adhik nahin hoti or prayek kiran kaa aaytan kone kareeb kareeb samaan hota he. at: fringen mukhyat: film kii motaai kii bhinnata ke kaaran banati he. yah bhi nitant spasht he ki film ke un sabhi binduon par, jahaan motaai samaan he, vahaan prakaash kii dipti bhi samaan hogi. yadi aisa koi bhi bindu kaala yaa ujwal hua, to shesh bhi tadnurup kaale yaa ujwal honge. isliye kaali yaa ujwal pattiyaan samaan motaai ke film ke vibhinn binduon ke bindupath maatr hoti he. is tarah kii finragen newtoni valay kehalaati hai, kyonki newton ne sarvapratham inkaa adhyayan kiya tha.
