 aadhunik bharat ke itihaas par likhi gai is pustak main bharat ke itihaas ke 1885 se 1947 tak ke kaal kii ghatnaon ko sameta gaya he. is pustak main unhone unhone spasht kar diya he ki yah itihaas ko neeche se dekhne kii koshish he. rashtravaadi or anek marxvaadi itihaaskaaron se bhi thodaa alag rahakar isamen sachet rup se abhijanvaadee itihaas drushti se mukt rahane kii koshish milti he. naveenatam shodhon ke nishkarshon ko saath lekar chalane ke kaaran yah pustak kai paramparaagat manyataon se alag or kahin kahin to uske khilaaf bhi jaati hui prathit hoti he. drushti ke str par or kuch tathy ke str par bhi aai naveenta ise pathneey or sangrahntiya pustak banaati he. 
 modern india naam kii is pustak kaa aadhunik bharat naam se shushila dobhaar ne behtareen hindi anuwaad kiya he. iskaa prakaashan raajakmal pkashan privet limited, nai dilli dwaara pehle-pahal 1993 main kiya gaya. 2006 tak is pustak kii teraa aavritiyaan ho chuki thi.
