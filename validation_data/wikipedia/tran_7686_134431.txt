 donghu pracheen china se uttar-purv main basane vaali ek mongol kanaabadosh qabeelon kii jati thi jinka varnan saatvi shatabdi isapurv se miltaa he. mana jaataa he ki unki jhadpen shiongnu logon se hui jinhonen 150 isapurv main unhen nasht kar diya. aage jaakar donghu log vuhuan or shianbei guton main bant gaye. mana jaataa he ki mongol jati inhi kii vanshaj he.
 chini bhashaa main 'dong' shabd kaa arth 'purv' or 'hu' kaa arth 'videshi' yaa 'mlechh' hota he. donghu kaa arth he 'purvi mlechh'.
