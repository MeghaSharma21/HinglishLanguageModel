 sujatha 1959 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. iske nirmaataa va nirdeshak prasiddh bimal roy the tathaa is film main mukhya bhoomika sunil dutt tathaa nutan ne nibhaai thi. yah film bharat main prachlit chuaachhut kii kupratha ko ujagar karti he. is film kii kahani ek brahman purush or ek achhut kanya ke prem kii kahani he. is film ko 1959 main filmafere sarvshereshth film puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha.
 ek sabhrant brahman dampati upendra choudhary tathaa chaaru ke ghar kaam karne vaale kii patni samet haije ke kaaran mrityu ho jaati he or vah apane peeche ek navjaat bacchee chhod jate hai jise chaaru kii zid se upendra ke parivaar kii aayaa paalane lag jaati he or jiskaa naam sujatha rakhaa jaataa he. upendra dampati kii apni bhi ek navjaat bacchee hoti he jiskaa naam hota he rama . chunki sujatha kaa pita achhut jati se tha isliye jab upendra kii buaa unke ghar aati hai to sujatha ko chupaane kii koshish kiye jane ke baavjood buaa ko pataa chal jaataa he or upendra dampati ko vah nirdesh detii he ki use kisi bhi tarah se usee kii jaat birdari main bhej diya jaye. lekin saare prayaas vifal ho jate hai. sujatha upendra parivaar main hi badi hoti he or upendra dampati ko hi vah apana maan-baap samajhane lagati he. rama bhi usko apni badi bahan maanati he. lekin sujatha anpadh hoti he jabaki rama college main padhati he.buaa kaa navasa adheer jab shahar se padhai kar waapas aata he to use sujatha ko dekhte hi pyaar ho jaataa he jabaki buaa chaahati he ki adheer or rama kaa vivaah ho. sujatha bhi adheer ko chaahane lagati he. ek din jab chaaru or buaa ke bich chal rahi baatcheet ko jab sujatha sunati he to use pataa chalata he ki vah achhut he. vah adheer se kinaaraa karne kii koshish karti he lekin adheer naye khyalaat kaa ladkaa he or vah in dakiyanoosee baton ko nahin maanata he. ek din ek haadase main chaaru ko chot lag jaati he or use khun dene kii zaroorat padati he. keval sujatha kaa hi khun chaaru ke khun se miltaa he isliye chaaru ko sujatha kaa khun chadhataa he. isse pehle chaaru buaa ke bahekaave main aakar sujatha ko adheer se prem karne ke liye dutkaarti thi, lekin ab chaaru sujatha ko apana leti he or aakhirkaar buaa bhi is rishte ko apni manzuri de detii he.
 is film main sangeet diya he this di burman ne or geet ke bol likhe hai majaruh sultanpuri ne.
