 alaayudh mahabharat kaal kaa ek rakshas tha jo usee kaal ke ek anya rakshas alambush ke saath chaudaaven din ke yuddh main ghatotkach ke haathon maaraa gaya.
 alaayudh kaurav paksh kaa ek yoddha tha.alaayudh rakshas jati kaa tha or vah bhimka rishtedaar bhi tha par vah bhim kehidimba se vivaah se naakhush tha. vah bhim kehathon hidimba ke bhai hidimb kii maut se aahat tha.vah vakaashur ke maare jane kaa badla lenaa chahta tha. vahansli bhaavana se peedit tha ourbhim ko apni jati ke apmaan se jod karadekhataa tha. apane kishoravastha se hi vahabhim se ghrinaa paale baitha tha aurkisi naa kisi din bhim sebadla lene kaa swapn dekhtaa. vah bhim ke putraftotkach ko bhi aadha vijaateey maan usasebarabar ghrinaa karata par saath-saath rahane ke kaaran vah usesidhe kuch kah naa paata. anek baar usne ghatotkachako apane vansh kaa kalank or avishvasi siddh karneka prayaas bhi kiya par ghatotkach ke achche vyavhaar,parakram or hidimba ke prabhaav ke kaaran alaayudhaki ek naa chali. us kabeeley mensaamaanya taur par sabhi log pandavon or vishesh karbhim ke liye shraddha rakhte par alaayudh or uske kuchhsathi bhim ko apane vansh ke apmaanaka pratik maanate. yuddh ke pehle bhim ne ghatotkach ko aapane paksh seyuddh hetu nimantran bheja. ghatotkach vahaan apane anya mitron auraswajano ke saath yuddh karne pahunchaa. usne alaayudhako bhi saath aane kaa aagrah kiya par alaayudh ke manamen to kuch or hi tha. yuddh ke chaudaaven din yuddaraatri main bhi jari rahaa to ghatotkachaki shaktiyaan andhkaar ke kaaran ekadamabadi or vah bheeshan rup secauravon kaa ant karne laga. vahamayavi bhi tha or use roak paanaa karn keliye bhi kathin tha.tabhi yuddh bhoomi main alaayudh ne pravesh kiya. usneduryodhana ko sampark kar kaurav paksh se yuddh karneki anumati maangi. uskaa ekmaatr uddeshytha bhim kaa ant karnaa. duryodhana to khush ho gaya parakarna ne virodh karte hue asasuron ko avishvasi bataaya or apane dam parabhim or arjuna ke ant kii batki. ghatotkach ke parakram or pralay se trastaduryodhana ne karnaki ek naa suni or useghatotkach tak hi simit rahane ko kallaayudh kaa swaagat kiya.alaayudh ne sampurna bal, chal or aavesh se bhim parakraman kiya. vah badale kii bhaavana se jalataa huaor bhayankar ho uthaa. lambe yuddh ke baad bhimdhire-dhire shithil padane laga. ek olylayudh kaa chal-bharaa yuddh or duusari oruski maaya, bhim bachaavaki mudra main aane laga. bhagwaan krishna ke chehre parchintaa kii lakeeren ubharne lageen.tabhi hunkaar karata ghatotkach bich main akhda hua. ghatotkach ne alaayudh ko samajhaate hue waapas janeko kaha. usne parivaar ke logon kaa vaasta de kar kaha ki aapas menyuddh karnaa morrkhtaa he. usne bachapan ke saath ke dinon kaa hawala dekar aapasi yuddh taalna chaha. par alaayudh nakaaratmakavish se bharaa tha. usne jehar ugalate hue apanepurvajon kii bhim ke haathon paraajayki baat kii or bhim ke saath-saath ghatotkach kaa bhi vadh karke apane vansh ke apmaanaka badla lene kii ghoshnaa kii.vivash ho kar ghatotkach alaayudh se lard padaa. usne alaayudhako anek maukey diye or baar-baar use samajhaata rahaa par alaayudhbhishan or bheeshan hota gaya. usneghatotkachaki sadashyata ko uski kamajori sejyada kuch nahin samajha. aakhir bhagwaan krishna neghatotkach ko samajhaayaa - he putr ghatotkach ! abhi tumpar waar kar rahaa alaayudh tumhaara swajan nahin haina hi dayaa kaa paatr he. yuddh main shatru ko anek battvasar dene vaale apane vinaash kaa kaaran swayan banate hai atah prathauvasar par hi shatru kaa antakarana hi samajhadaari he.ghatotkach ne agle hi pal alaayudh ko gira karuskaa vadh kar diya.
