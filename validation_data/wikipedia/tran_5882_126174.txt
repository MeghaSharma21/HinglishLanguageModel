 
 hina yaa hina ek pushpiya paudha hota he jiskaa vaigyanik naam losonia inarmis he. ise twachaa, bal, nakhun, chamada or oon rangane ke kaam main prayog kiya jata he. isse hi mehandi bhi lagaayi jaati he.
 menhadi kaa vanaspatik naam 'losonia inarmis' he or yah lithesii kul kaa kantedaar paudha he. yah uttari africa, arab desh, bharat tathaa purvi dweep samooh main paaya jaataa he. adhiktar gharon ke saamne kii batika athwa baagon main isaki baad lagaai jaati he jisaki oonchaai aath das foot tak ho jaati he or yah jhaadi kaa rup dhaaran kar leti he. kabhi kabhi junglee rup se yah taal talaiyon ke kinare bhi ug aati he. tahaniyon ko katkar bhoomi main gaad dene se hi nae paudhe lag jate hai. iske chote safed athwa halake pile rang ke phool gucchhon main nikalate hai, jo vaataavaran ko, visheshat: raatri main apni bhini mahak se sugandhit karte hai. phulon ko sukhaakar sugandhit tel bhi nikaala jaataa he. isaki choti chikani pattiyon ko piskar ek prakaar kaa lep banaate hai, jise striyaan nakhun, haath, pair tathaa ungliyon par shringaar hetu kai abhikalpon main rachaati hai. lep ko lagane ke kuch ghanton ke baad dho dene par lagaaya hua sthaan laal, yaa naarangi rang main rang jaataa he jo teen chaar saptaah tak nahin chhutata. pattiyon ko piskar bhi rakh liya jaataa he, jise garam pani main milakar rang dene vaala lep taiyaar kiya ja saktaa he. ispodhe kii chaal tathaa pattiyaan dawa main prayukt hoti hai.
