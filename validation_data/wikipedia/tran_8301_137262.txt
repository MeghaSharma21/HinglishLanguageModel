 pracheen kaal tathaa madhyakaal main goud ek pradesh tha jo bengal main sthit tha.
 skandapurana maheshwar+vaishnavakhandake anusaar gouddesh kii sthiti vangdesh se lekar bhuwanesh tak thi-: "vangdeshan samaarbhy bhuvneshantag: shive, goud desh: samakhyath: sarvavidya vishaarad:." padmapurana main goud naresh narsingh kaa naam aayaa he. abhilekhon main goud desh kaa sarvapratham ullekh 553 i. ke haraahaa abhilekh main he jisamen ishwar varman moukhari kii gouddesh par vijay kaa ullekh he. banbhatta ne gaudnaresh shashaank kaa varnan kiya he jisane hashavardhan ke jyeshtha bhrata rajyavardhan kaa vadh kiya tha. maadhainagar ke tamrapatt lekh se suchit hota he ki gaudnaresh lakshmanasen kaa kaling tak prabhutv tha.
 goud desh ke naam par sanskrit kavy kii parushavriti kaa naam hi gaudi pad gaya tha. kaaysthon aadi kii kai jatiyaan aaj bhi goud kehalaati he. kuch vidwaanon kaa mat he ki gud ke vyapaar kaa centre hone ke kaaran hi is pradesh kaa naam goud ho gaya tha.
 goud yaa 'lakshmanavati' yaa 'lakhanauti' pashchim bengal ke malda jila main sthit ek purana nagar he. yah hindu rajasatta ke utkarshkaal main sanskrit vidya ke centre ke rup main vikhyath thi or mahakavi jayadev, kaviver govardhanacharya tathaa dhoyi, vyakarancharya umapatighar or shabdkoshkar halaayudh in sabhi vidwaanon kaa sambandh is prasiddh nagari se tha. iske khandhar bengal ke malda naamak nagar se 10 mile dakshin pashchim kii ore sthit hai.
