 cregoan loj, aberdinshir ke bellmoral estate main, belmoral kasal se kareeb ek mile kii duuri par sthit ek saat-kamaron vaala prastar-nirmit bangala he. mukhya mahal se adhik graamy aavesh main hone ke kaaran, is bhawan ko rajkumaar charles or rajkumaari dyna dwaara aksar upyog kiya jaataa tha. aajkal ise mahatvapurn mehmaanon ke liye atithishaala ke rup main upyog kiya jaataa he.
 roosi shahi parivaar ke tatkaaleen varishtataam sadsy, maaikel andrivich romanof ne dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke samay yaheen nivas kiya tha. maharani elizabeth dwitiya aksar apni garmiyaan yaheen vyateet karti hai. garmiyon main is mahal main paryatakon ko simit samay ke liye anumati di jaati he.
