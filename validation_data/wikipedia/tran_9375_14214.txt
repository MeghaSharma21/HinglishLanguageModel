 rasayan shaastra main mishrayata kaa arth aamtaur par ek drav kaa gun he jisame use jal me kisi bhi anupaat me milaayaa jaye vah ek samroop vilyan banaata he. siddhant rup main, yah shabd bhi anya avasthaaon par bhi lagu hota he, lekin mukhyat: yah ek drav kii duusare drav me vilayata se sambandhit he. udhaaharan ke liye, pani or ithenul ko chahen kisi bhi anupaat main mishrit kiya jaye wo ek samroop vilyan banaate hai. 
 iske vipareet padaarth ko agar kisi bhi anupaat main milaane par vah ek samroop vilyan nahin banaate to unhen amishray kaha jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye, diaithail ether pani main ghulansheel he, lekin jab inhen bhinn bhinn anupaat main milaayaa jaataa he to yah aapas me nahin milte.
