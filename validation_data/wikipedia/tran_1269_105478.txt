 tilka maanjhi urf zebra pahadiya bharat ke adividrohi hai. duniya kaa pehla adividrohi rome ke purakhaa adivasee ladaaka spartacas ko mana jaataa he. bharat ke aupniveshik yudhon ke itihaas main jabaki pehla adividrohi hone kaa shrey pahadiya aadim adivasee samudaay ke ladaakon ko jaataa hai jinhone rajmahal, jharkhand kii pahadiyon par britani hukumat se loha liya. in pahadiya ladaakon main sabase lokapriy adividrohi zebra yaa jorah pahadiya urf tilka maanjhi hai.
 zebra pahadiya bharat main british satta ko chunauti dene vaale pahadiya samudaay ke vir adivasee the. singarasi pahaad, pakur ke zebra pahadiya urf tilka maanjhi ke bare main kaha jaataa he ki unka janm 11 farvari 1750 i. main hua tha. 1771 se 1784 tak unhone british satta ke viruddh lambii or kabhi na samarpan karne vaali ladaai ladi or sthaneey mahajanon-saamanton va angreji shaasha kii neend udaae rakhaa. pahadiya ladaakon main sardaar ramana ahaadi or amadapada prakhand ke aamgaachhi pahaad nivasee kariya puzhar or singarasi pahaad nivasee zebra pahadiya bharat ke adividrohi hai. duniya kaa pehla adividrohi rome ke purakhaa adivasee ladaaka spartacas ko mana jaataa he. bharat ke aupniveshik yudhon ke itihaas main jabaki pehla adividrohi hone kaa shrey pahadiya aadim adivasee samudaay ke ladaakon ko jaataa hai jinhone rajmahal, jharkhand kii pahadiyon par britani hukumat se loha liya. in pahadiya ladaakon main sabase lokapriy adividrohi zebra yaa jorah pahadiya urf tilka maanjhi hai. inhone 1778 i. main pahadiya saradaaron se milakar ramgarh camp par kabja karne vaale angrejon ko khaded kar camp ko mukt karaayaa. 1784 main zebra ne cleveland ko maar daalaa. baad main irkut ke netritv main zebra kii gorilla sena par jabardast hamalaa hua jisamen kai ladaake maare gaye or zebra ko giraftaar kar liya gaya. kehete hai unhen chaar ghodon main baandhakar ghasitate hue bhagalpur laayaa gaya. par milon ghasite jane ke baavjood vah pahadiya ladaaka jivit tha. khun main dubi uski deh tab bhi gussell thi or uski laal-laal ankhen britani raaj ko daraa rahi thi. bhay se kaanpate hue angrejon ne tab bhagalpur ke chauraahey par sthit ek vishaal vatvriksh par saream lataka kar unki jaan le lee. hajaaron kii bheed ke saamne zebra pahadiya urf tilka maanjhi hansate-hansate faansee par jhul gaye. taarikh thi sambhawatah 13 janvari 1785. baad main aazaadee ke hajaaron ladaakon ne zebra pahadiya kaa anusaran kiya or faansee par chadhate hue jo geet gaae - haansi-haansi chadhbo faansee ...! - vah aaj bhi hamen is adividrohi kii yaad dilaate hai.
 pahadiya samudaay kaa yah gorilla ladaaka ek aisi kinvadanti he jiske bare main aitihaasik dastavej sirf naam bhar kaa ullekh karte hai, puraa vivaran nahin dete. lekin pahadiya samudaay ke purakhaa geeton or kahaniyon main isaki chhapamar jeevani or kahaniyaan sadiyon baad bhi uske adividrohi hone kaa akatya daawa pesh karti hai.
 tilka maanjhi santal the yaa pahadiya ise lekar vivaad he. aam taur par tilka maanjhi ko murm gotra kaa bataate hue anek lekhakon ne unhen santal adivasee bataaya he. parantu tilka ke santal hone kaa koi aitihaasik dastavej or likhit pramaan maujuud nahin he. vahiin, aitihaasik dastavejon ke anusaar santal adivasee samudaay ke log 1770 ke akaal ke kaaran 1790 ke baad santal pargana kii taraf aae or based. 
 The Annals of Rural Bengal, Volume 1, 1868 By Sir William Wilson Hunter main saaf likhaa he ki santal log birbhum se aaj ke singhbhum kii taraf nivas karte the. 1790 ke akaal ke samay unka migration aaj ke santal pargana tak hua. hunter ne likhaa he, ‘1792 se santalon nayaa itihaas shuru hota he’ . 1838 tak santal pargana main santalon ke 40 gaanvon ke basane kii soochna hunter dete hai jinamein unki kul aabaadi 3000 thi . hunter yah bhi bataata he ki 1847 tak mi. ward ne 150 gaanvon main kareeb ek lakh santalon ko basaaya .
 1910 main prakashit ‘bengal district gajetir: santal pargana’, volum 13 main el.es.es. o meli ne likhaa he ki jab mi. ward 1827 main daamine koh kii seemaa kaa nirdhaaran kar rahaa tha to use santalon ke 3 gaanv patsunda main or 27 gaanv barcope main mili the. ward ke anusaar, ‘ye log khud ko santar kehete hai jo singhbhum or udhar ke ilaake ke rahane vaale hai.’ daaminacoh main santalon ke basane kaa pramanik vivaran bengal district gajetir: santal pargana ke prishth 97 se 99 par uplabdh he.
 iske atirikt aar. carstares jo 1885 se 1898 tak santal pargana kaa dipti kamishnar rahaa tha, usne apane upanyaas ‘haadma kaa gaanv’ kii shuruyaat hi pahadiya logon ke ilaake main santalon ke basane ke tathy se kii he.
 purvajon ke naam par bachche kaa naam rakhane kii parampara anya adivasee samudaayon kii tarah santalon main bhi he. lekin santalon main purv main or aaj bhi kisi vyakti kaa naam ‘tilka’ nahin miltaa he. lekin pahadiya samudaay ke logon main aaj bhi ‘zebra’ naam rakhane kaa prachlan he.
 angrejon ne zebra pahadiya ko khoonkhaar daaku or gussell maanjhi kaha. santalon main bhi maanjhi hote hai or badi aabaadi va 1855 ke hul ke kaaran ve zyada jane gaye, isliye tilka maanjhi urf zebra pahadiya ke santal adivasee hone kaa bhram failaa. darasal, zebra pratyakshtah bhagalpur ke tatkaaleen jila colector cleveland dwaara gathit ‘pahadiya hill rangers’ ke sena nayak ke rup main angreji shashan ke vafaadaar banane kaa dikhaava karte the or naam badal kar ‘tilka’ maanjhi ke rup main apane sainkadon ladaakon ke saath gorilla tareeke se angrej shaasha, samant or mahajanon ke saath yuddhrat rahete the. vaise, pahadiya bhashaa main ‘tilka’ kaa arth he gussell or laal-laal ankhon vaala vyakti. chunki vah gram pradhan tha or pahadiya samudaay main gram pradhan ko maanjhi kahkar pukaarane kii pratha he. isliye hill rangers kaa sardaar jorah urf zebra maanjhi tilka maanjhi ke naam se vikhyath ho gaye. britishkaleen dastavejon main bhi zebra pahadiya maujuud he par tilka kaa kahin namollekh nahin he.
 bangla kii suprasiddh lekhika mahasweta devi ne tilka maanjhi ke jivan or vidroh par bangla bhashaa main ek upanyaas 'shalagirar daake' kii rachana kii he. apane is upanyaas main mahasweta devi ne tilka maanjhi ko murmu gotra kaa santal adivasee bataaya he. yah upanyaas hindi main 'shalagirah kii pukaar par' naam se anuvaadit or prakashit hua he.
 hindi ke upanyaaskaar rakesh kumaar singh ne jabaki apane upanyaas ‘hul pahadiya’ main tilka maanjhi ko zebra pahadiya ke rup main chitrit kiya he. ‘hul pahadiya’ upanyaas 2012 main prakashit hua he.
 tilka maanjhi ke naam par bhagalpur main tilka maanjhi bhagalpur vishvavidhyalay naam se ek shiksha kaa centre sthapit kiya gaya he.
