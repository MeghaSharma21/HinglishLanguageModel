 faras kii khadi, pashchim asia main hind mahaasaagar kaa ek vistaar he, jo iran or arab prayadweep ke bich tak gaya hua he. 1980-1988 ke iran iraq yuddh ke dauraan yah khadi logon ke kautuhal kaa vishay bani rahi, jab donon pakshon ne ek duusare ke tel ke jahaajon par akraman kiya tha. 1991 main khadi yuddh ke dauraan, faras kii khadi ek baar phir se charchaa kaa vishay bani, haalaanki yah sangharsh mukhya rup se ek bhoomi sangharsh tha, jab iraq ne kuwait par hamalaa kiya tha or jise baad main waapas peeche dhakel diya gaya. 
 faras kii khadi main kai acchhee machhlee pakadaney ke jagahen hai, vyapak pravaal bhitiyaan or prachur maatra main moti kasturi he, lekin isaki paristhitiki kaa audyogikaran ke kaaran kshay hua he, vishesh rup se, yuddh ke dauraan faile tel or petroleum ne is par vipareet prabhaav daalaa he.
 aksar "faras kii khadi" ko adhiktar arab rashtron dwaara iske vivaadaaspad naam "arab kii khadi" yaa sirf "khadi" kahkar pukaara jaataa he, haalaanki in donon naamon ko antarrashtriya str par manyata praapt nahin he. antarrashtriya jal surveykshan sanghathan iske liye "iran kii khadi " naam kaa istemaal karata he.
