 abu rehaan mohammad bin ahamed al-bayruni yaa al beruni ek farsi vidwaan lekhak, vaigyanik, dharmajna tathaa vichaarak tha. al beruni kii rachanaayen arabi bhashaa main hai par use apni maatrubhaasha farsi ke alaava kam se kam teen or bhashaaon kaa gyaan tha - syrian, sanskrit, unani. wo bharat or srilanka kii yatra par 1017-20 ke madhya aayaa tha. gazani ke mahmud, jisane bharat par kai baar akraman kiye, ke kai abhiyaanon main wo sultan ke saath tha. alburuni ko bhartiya itihaas kaa pehla jaankaar kaha jaataa tha.
 abbasi shashan ke patanonmukh dinon main uskaa janm khwarazm main san 973 main hua tha. yah sthaan ab uzbekistan main he. usne ganit or khagolvigyaan abu nasr mansoor se sikhi. ve avecinna ke saathi the. afghanistan or dakshin asia kii yatra par wo mahmud gazanvi ke saath uske kaafile main gaya. bharat main rahete hue usne bhartiya bhashaaon kaa adhyayan kiya or 1030 main tarikh-al-hind naamak qitaab likhi. uski mrityu gazani, afghanistan main hui.
 alburuni ne 146 qitaaben likhin - 35 khagolashastra par, 23 jyotishshastra kii, 15 ganit kii, 16 saahitiyik tathaa anya kai vishyon par.
 al-baruni chikitsa visheshgy tha or bhashaaon par bhi acha adhikaar rakhata tha. iske saath hi vah ek mashhoor ganitagya, bhugolvetta, kavi, rasayan vaigyanik or darshanik bhi tha. unhone hi dharti kii trigya naapane kaa ek aasaan formula pesh kiya. baruni ne ye bhi sabit kiya ki prakaash kaa veg dhwani ke veg se adhik hoti he.
