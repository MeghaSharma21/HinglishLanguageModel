 mahamahopadhyaay shri gopinath kaviraj sanskrit ke vidwaan or mahaan darshanik the. 1914 main putkalayakshyaksh se armbh karte hue ve 1923 se 1937 tak varanasi ke shaskeey sanskrit mahavidhyalay ke pradhanacharya rahe. is kalavadhi main ve saraswati bhawan granthmala ke sampadak bhi rahe.
 gopinath kaviraj bengali the or inke pitaaji kaa naam vaikunthanath bagchi tha. aap kaa janm british bharat ke gram dhamrai jila dhaka me hua tha. unka janm pratishthit bagchi gharaane me hua tha or "kaviraj" unako sammaan main kaha jaataa tha. aapane apni praarambhik shiksha diksha shri madhusudan ojha evam shashidhar "tark chudamani" ke nirdesan me jaipur me prarambh kiya.
 mahamahopadhyaay pan gopinath kaviraj vartmaan yug ke vishwavikhyath bhartiya praachyavid tathaa manishi rahe hai. inaki gyaan-sadhana kaa kram vartmaan shatabdi ke pratham dashak se armbh hua or prayaag-kaal tak vah abadharup se chalata rahaa. is dirghakaal main unhone paurasty tathaa paashchaatya gyaan-vigyaan kii vishisht chintan paddatiyon kaa gahan anushilan kar, darshan or itihaas ke kshetra main jo anshdaan kiya he usase maanav-samskruti tathaa sadhana kii antardharaon par naveen prakaash padaa he, nayi drushti mili he.
 unnisvin shatee ke dharmik punarjagran or beesvin shatee ke swatantrya-aandolan se anupranit unki jivan-gaatha main yugachetana saakaar ho uthi he. pracheenta ke samposhak evam naveenta ke puraskarta ke rup main kaviraj mahoday kaa virat vyaktitv sandhikaal kii un sampoorn visheshtaon se samanvit he, jinase jaateey-jivan pragati-path par agrasar hone kaa sambal praapt karata rahaa he.
 inke dwaara rachit ek shodh tantric vaanmay main shakt drushti ke liye unhen san 1964 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
