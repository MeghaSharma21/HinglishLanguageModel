 bhartiya antariksh vigyaan evam praudyogiki santhaan bharat evam asia kaa pratham antariksh vishvavidhyalay he. yah tiruvanantpuram shahar ke valiyamla kshetra main sthit he. isaki sthapana 14 sitambar 2007 ko ganesh chaturthi ke paavan avasar par hui thi. bharat ke purv raashtrapati evam mahaan vaigyanik dow e. pi. je. abdul kalam yahan ke kulapati rah chuke hai. yah santhaan isro evam bharat sarkaar ke antariksh vibhaag dwaara pryojit he. santhaan ko vishvavidhyalay anudaan ayog se samavishvavidhyalay kii manyata praapt he.
 antariksh vigyaan evam takneekee ke kshetra main kushal logon kii kami ko puraa karne ke liye is santhaan kii sthapana kii gai he. tatkaaleen isro sabhapati dow madhavan nair ne is santhaan kaa udghaatan 14 sitambar 2007 ko kiya tha. adhik se adhik chaatro ko aakarshith karne ke liye yahan chaatro kaa samast shaikshik, awaas evam bhojan shulk bharat sarkaar vahan karti he. iske saath-saath chaatro ko prati semester 3,000 rupye pustak anudaan ke rup main milte hai. course puraa ho jane ke pashchaat har chaatr ko isro main paanch varsh tak seva karnaa jaruri he. aisa na kar paane kii sthiti main chaatr ko dand swarup 10,00,000 rupye dene kaa praavdhaan he. yah bharat kaa ekmaatr santhaan he jo antariksh vigyaan ke kshetra main bi tech course uplabdh karaata he. 
 ai ai es tii ke snaatak paathyakram main uplabdh kul 156 siton main pravesh ke liye varsh 2017 main yojit sanyukt pravesh pariksha kii kramank soochi kaa prayog kiya jayega. purv kaal main varsh 2007 se 2009 tak bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan dwaara yojit sanyukt pravesh pariksha kii kramank soochi kaa prayog kiya gaya or varsh 2010 se 2012 tak isat pariksha kaa aayojan kiya gaya. yah pariksha desh ke sabhi pramukh shaharon main yojit kii jaati thi. san 2011 main lagbhag 96,000 pratibhaagi is pariksha main sammilit hue the. sudur dakshin bharat main sthit hone ke baavjood bhi santhaan main har pradesh ke vidhyaarthee pravesh lete hai.
 is santhaan kaa aarambh tiruvanantpuram sthit vikram sarabhai antariksh kendra ke e tii eff campus se hua tha. santhaan kaa sthaayee campus tiruvanantpuram ke nikat sthit valiyamla main nirmaanadhin he. shaikshanik imaaraton evam kshatravaason kaa nirmaan 2013 tak puraa ho jane kii sambhaavna he. is campus kaa udghaatan pradhan mantri dow manmohan singh ne 25 agast 2009 ko dilli se video conference dwaara kiya tha. 15 agast 2010 se santhaan isi campus se kaam kar rahaa he. antariksh vedhashala ke liye iskaa ek vistaar campus tiruvanantpuram kii suramya ponmudi pahadiyon main ban rahaa he.
 yah santhaan snaatak, parasnatak evam pi. ech. di. kii degree pradaan karata he. santhaan nimnlikhit shaikshik kaarykram pradaan karata he: 
 santhaan main chaatro ke sampurna vikaas ke liye nimnlikhit club upasthit hai:
 yah club chaatro ko khagol vigyaan ke anek pahaluon se avgat karaata he. anterrashtriya khagol vigyaan varsh 2009 main is club ke chaatro ne aparimit naamak khagol parv kaa aayojan kiya. saath hi, is club ke chaatro ne do baar telescope banane kii kaaryshaala or raatri-akaash darshan satron kaa bhi aayojan kiya he.
 varsh 2010 main bana yah club un chaatro ke liye he jo vigyaan ke guhya rahasyon ko tatolna chaahate hai. yahan saptahik seminar dwaara anek prakaar ke vishyon par charchaa kii jaati he.
 yah club har saptaah prashnottaree pratiyogitaaon kaa aayojan karata he jinamein kala, sahitya, khel, vigyaan evam praudyogiki, chalchitr, raajnneeti, aadi vishyon par prashn puche jate hai. 
 rasayan vigyaan vibhaag ke saujany se shuru hue is club main chaatr nae padaarthon kii khoj evam nirmaan par dhyaan dete hai. 
 chaatro dwaara cricket, chidi-chhikka, basketbaal evam footbaal ke club shuru kiye gaye hai.
 drushtikon santhaan kii vaarshik patrika he or da sounding rocket chaatro dwaara sampadit evam prakashit samachar patr he.
 ai. ai. es. tii. main nimnlikhit utsav yojit kiye jate hai:
