 maratha "mati" griphith brown ek gulaami-virdohi upanyaaskaar or america safaregist thi.
 griphith brown kaa janm ovensboro, kentki main hua tha, pita thomas griphith or maa martha "mati" yang thi. 
 uske parivaar ke swaamitv main gulam the. .samay main, samay main,use apane pita se aadha darjan das viraasat main mili.
 apni purv gulam-dharak kii sthiti ke baavjood, vah ek unmoolnavaadee ban gai or apane lekhan main mukti ke liye vakaalat kii. vah 1856 main prakashit apane upanyaas, ek mahila das, kii aatmakatha  se sab se adhik jaani he. unke ullekhneey kaaryon main se ek or ek dharawahik upanyaas he, maij vertner, or 185 9 se 18 may 1865 tak rashtriya virdohi daastaa maanak main prakashit hua.
 27 june, 1867 ko, griphith brown ne neuyork shahar main patrkaar, unmoolnavaadee or banker albert galatin brown, junior se shaadi kii uskaa pati pita albert ji brown or maan sara je cox kaa betaa tha. 
 stan cancer se vah 25 may 1906 ko mrityu ho gai, or salem, masachusets main harmany grove kabristaan main dafan he..
