 grami awards kii shabdaawalee main ab paaramparik sangeet shabd kaa prayog "lok sangeet " ke sthaan par kiya jaataa he. is badlaav kaa purna vivaran vishwa sangeet lekh ke shabdaawalee anubhag main paaya ja saktaa he. anya sanghathanon ne isi tarah ke parivartan kiye hai, haalanki gaiyr-shaikshanik halkon main, tathaa sidi bechney vaali kai websiton par, "lok sangeet" vaakyansh kaa prayog bahut saare arthon main kiya jaataa he.
 aitihaasik drushtikon se, paaramparik sangeet kii nimna visheshtaayen thim:
 kshetra yaa samskruti se sambandhit tha. ek apravasi samooh ke sandarbh main, saamaajik saamanjasy ke liye lok sangeet ko ek atirikt aayam praapt he. aisa sanyukt rajya america main vishesh rup se adhik spasht he, jahaan polish mul ke america, irish mul ke america or asian america mukhya dhara se alag hone kaa purajor prayaas kar rahe hai. ve multah un deshon se sambandhit geet or nrity seekhenge jahaan se unke bujurg aae hai.
 ek dushprabhav ke rup main, kabhi-kabhi nimnlikhit visheshtaayen pai jaati hai:
 vaadyayantra sangeet ke atirikt, jo ki paaramparik sangeet, vishesh rup se nrity sangeet paramparaon kaa ek hissaa he, adhikansh paaramparik sangeet gayan sangeet he, kyonki aise sangeet kii rachana main prayukt hone vaale vaadya yantra aam taur par aasaan hai. isliye adhikansh paaramparik sangeet main sarthak bol hai.
 kai samskritiyon ke paaramparik sangeet main kathatmak chand milte hai. yah paaramparik mahakavy kaa rup le lete hai, mulat iskaa adhikansh hissaa maukhik pradarshan se sambandhit tha, jisame kabhi-kabhi vaadya yantron kaa bhi prayog kiya jaataa tha. vibhinn samskritiyon ke kai mahakavy paaramparik kathatmak chhandon ke chote hisson ko jod kar banaae gaye the, jo unki praasangik sanrachana kaa varnan karte hai or aksar uname kathanak ke vikaas kaa samavesh hota he. paramparaagat kathatmak chhandon ke anya rup yudhon tathaa anya traasadiyon yaa prakritik aapadaaon ke parinaamon se sambandhit hai. kabhi-kabhi, jaise ki bible kii book of jajes main milane vaale vijayi geet song of debora main, ye geet vijay kaa jashn manaate hai. kai paramparaon main haari gai ladaaiyon or yudhon, tathaa unamem marane waalon ke liye kiye jane vaale geet rupi vilaap utane hi pramukh hai; ye vilaap us kaaran kii yaad dilaate hai jiske liye yuddh ladaa gaya tha. paaramparik geeton kii kathatmak vyakhyaayein jon henry se le kar robin hud jaise lok nayakon ko yaad karti hai. kuch paaramparik geeton main kahi gai kathaayen alokik ghatnaon yaa rahasyamay mauton kii yaad dilaati hai.
 bhajan or dharmik sangeet ke anya prakaar aksar paaramparik or agyaat mul ke hote hai. pashchimi sangeet chihnon ko mul rup se gregorian mantra kii linon ko yaad rakhane ke liye sangrakshit rakhane ke liye banaaya gaya tha, jo apane avishkaar se pehle math samudaayon main maukhik parampara ke rup main padhaaye jate the. green gro da rashes, o jaise paaramparik gane yaad rakhane vaale dhang se dharmik vidya pradaan karte hai. pashchimi duniya main, christmas carol tathaa anya paaramparik gane dharmik vidya ko geet ke rup main sangrakshit rakhte hai.
 kaam se sambandhit geet aksar pukaar or jawaab sambandhi sanrachanaon ko pradarshit karte hai or is dhang se dizin kiye gaye hai ki unhen gane vaale shramik gaanon kii lay ke saath apane kaam kaa samanvay kar saken. ve aksar, lekin hameshaa nahin, sangitabaddh kiye jate hai. america sashastr balon main, sainikon ke march ke dauraan joudy kol kii ek jeevant parampara gaai jaati he. peshewar navikon ne samudri lokgeeton ke ek bade hisse kaa prayog kiya he. prem kavitaayen, jo aksar dukhad yaa khedjanak prakruti kii hoti hai, kai lok paramparaon kaa ek aham hissaa hai. nursery kavitaayen he or nirarthak kavitaayen bhi paaramparik geeton ke aksar chune jane vaale vishay hai.
 ek samudaay ke madhyam se maukhik rup se sanchaarit hone vaale sangeet main, aane vaale samay main, kai bhinnataaon kaa vikaas hoga, kyonki is prakaar ke sancharan ke liye sateek shabd or vyakhya bananaa sambhav nahin he. darasal, kai paaramparik gayak kaaphi rachanaatmak hai or ve jaan-boojhkar sikhi gai jaankaree ko sanshodhit karte hai.
 udhaaharan ke liye, "ai'm e main you dont meet evari de" ke shabd bodleian librari ke poster se liye gaye hai. tithi lagbhag nishchit rup se 1900 se pehle kii he or yah irish lagata he. 1958 main canada main ek geet record kiya gaya tha . jaini robertson ne 1961 main record hone vaala aglaa sanskaran banaaya. usne apane ek rishtedaar "jock stewart" ke liye ise badal diya he or isamen koi irish sandarbh nahin he. 1976 main archi fisher ne jaanboojhkar goli se marane ja rahe kute kaa sandarbh hataane ke liye geet main parivartan kiya. 1985 main da pogs ne sabhi irish sandarbhon ko behaal karke yah chakra purna kiya.
 kyonki bhinnataayen swaabhaaviktaa ko badhaati hai, isliye aisa maananaa morrkhtaa he ki barbara alan. jaise ek gaatha geet kaa koi ekal pramanik "sanskaran" maujuud he. paaramparik geeton ke anusandhankartaaon ko angreji bhashi duniya main is gaatha geet ke anginat sanskaran mili hai or aksar ye sanskaran ek duusare se kaaphi alag hai. koi bhi majbooti se mul geet hone kaa daawa nahin kar saktaa or bahut sambhav he ki jo kuch bhi "mul" geet tha, vah sadiyon pehle gaaya jaataa tha. koi bhi sanskaran pramanikta ke liye tab tak samaan daawa kar saktaa he, jab tak yah puri tarah se ek paaramparik gayan samudaay se sambandhit he or is par kisi bahari sampadak ne koi badlaav nahin kiya he.
 cesil sharp ke paas lok sangeet main badlaav ke bare main ek prabhaavshaalee vichaar tha: unhone mahasoos kiya ki ek paaramparik geet ke sanskaran taiyaar karne kii hode ek jaivik prakritik chayan ke sadrush thi: keval ve nae sanskaran jo saadhaaran gayakon ko sarvaadhik aakarshith karte the, ko dusron dwaara chunaa jaataa tha or samay ke saath badlaav ke saath sanchaarit kiya jaataa tha. is prakaar, samay bitne ke saath ham ek paaramparik geet main soundaryabodh ke or adhik aakarshak hone kii ummid karenge - kyonki yah samuhik rup se samudaay dwaara apane sarvottam swaroop main bana hoga.
 duusari ore, is drushtikon ke samarthan ke liye bhi saboot maujuud hai ki paaramparik geeton main badlaav ghatiya ho saktaa he. kabhi-kabhi, sangrihith paaramparik geet ke sanskarnon main alag-alag geeton se lee gai jaankaree yaa chand is prakaar ke hote hai jinka iske sandarbh ke saath bahut kam sambandh hota he. sarah cleveland ek sammanit paaramparik irish-america gayika hai. unke geet "let no main steel yor thim" ke sanskaran main ek anya geet - "seeds of lav" kaa mishran he. . donon geeton main phulon kaa ullekh he, lekin vishay kaaphi alag he. isi tarah, kai paaramparik geeton ko keval tukade ke rup main hi janaa jaataa he. shaayad ek aadh maamle main keval ek yaa do panktiyaan record kii gai hai.
 yadhyapi duniya bhar main lokapriy sangeet ko badhaava milane ke kaaran paaramparik sangeet kaa hraas ho rahaa he, kintu aisa duniya bhar main ek samaan dar se nahin ho rahaa he. jabaki kai adivasee samskritiyaan paaramparik sangeet or lok sangeet ko kho rahi hai, yah prakriya un kshetron main sarvaadhik teevr gati se ho rahi he "jahaan samskruti kaa audyogikaran evam vyavsyeekaran sabase unnatahain". phir bhi un deshon yaa kshetron main, jahaan paramparaagat sangeet saanskrithik yaa rashtriya pahchaan ko darshata he, paaramparik sangeet ke nuksaan ko dhimaa kiya ja saktaa he; aisa sach main ho saktaa he, udhaaharan ke liye jaise bangladesh, hungary, bharat, ireland, latvia, turki, portugal, brittani tathaa galicia, yunaan tathaa careet ke maamle main, jo kuch had tak apane paaramparik sangeet ko bachaae hue hai, kuch kshetron main paaramparik sangeet ke str main giravat or samskruti main hraas ko roka gaya he. yah vahaan sabase adhik spasht he jahaan paryatak agenciyon dwaara kuch kshetron ko "keltic" naam diya gaya he. ireland, scotland, cornwall, brittani tathaa nova scotia kii maardarshak pustakon tathaa ishtehaaron main sangeet pradarshnon ke sidhe prasaaran/shravan kaa ullekh he. sthaneey sarkaar aksar paryaton satron ke dauraan aise aayojanon ko pryojit karke tathaa badhaava de kar khoi hui parampara ko purnjivit karti he.
 19veen sadi kii shuruyaat main, isamen ruchi rakhane vaale logon - shikshavidon or shaukia vidwaanon - ne is baat par dhyaan dena shuru kiya ki ve kya kho rahe the or unhonen logon ke sangeet ko bachaane ke liye kai prayaas kiye. aisa hi ek prayaas 19veen sadi ke ant main francis james child kaa sangrah tha, jo angreji tathaa scot paramparaon ke teen sau se adhik gaathagiton kaa granth tha . 1960 ke dauraan or 1970 ke dashak ke madhya kii shuruyaat main, america vidwaan bartrend haris broanson ne us samay ke lekhon tathaa dhunon main badlaavon kaa chaar-bhaagon kaa ek vistrut sangrah prakashit kiya, jise child canan ke rup main janaa gaya. kehane sunne kii parampara ke kaamkaaj ke siddhanton ke sambandh main bhi usne mahatvapurn pragati kii.
 child ke samakaleen shraddhey sabeen bering-guld tathaa iske baad evam vishesh rup se cesil sharp aae jinhonen 20veen sadi kii shuruyaat main angreji ke gramin paaramparik geet, sangeet va nrity kii mahaan viraasat ke sangrakshan ke liye english pholk dance and song socity  ke tattvadhan main kaam kiya. sharp ne america main bhi kaam karte hue maud carpells evam olive dame campbell ke saath mil kar 1916-1918 main appalachian parvaton ke paaramparik geeton kii recording kii. aadhunik film "songkaichar" main abhinaytaon dwaara campbell or sharp kaa pratinidhitiv duusare naamon se kiya gaya he.
 anya deshon main bhi isi prakaar kii gatividhi chal rahi thi. riga main sabase vyapak kaam shaayad krisjanis barons dwaara 1894 evam 1915 ke bich chah sanskarnon ko prakashit kar ke kiya gaya jisame 217 996 latvian lok geeton, latvju denas ke lekh the.
 is samay ke aasapaas, shastreey sangeet ke sangeetkaaron ne paaramparik geeton ke sangrah main atyadhik ruchi dikhaai or kai jane maane sangeetkaaron ne khud paaramparik geeton par gahan kaary kiya. inamen england ke percy granger evam rolf won williams tathaa hungary ke bela bartok shaamil the. in sangeetkaaron ne, apane kai purvavartiyon kii tarah, apni shastreey rachanaaon main paaramparik jaankaree ko shaamil kiya. latviju denas kaa prayog vyapak rup se andrez jurans, jenis simz tathaa emilis melngallis ke shastreey bhajanon main kiya jaataa he.
 uttari america main 1930 va 1940 ke dashak ke dauraan, librari of congress ne paaramparik sangeet sangrahakartaaon robert vinslo gordan, alan lomex tathaa anyon ke karyaalayon ke madhyam se uttari america ke kshetra se sambandhit yatha sambhav jaankaree praapt kii.
 paaramparik geet kaa adhyayan karne vaale logon ne kabhi aashaa vyakt kii thi ki unke kaam ke dwaara logon main paaramparik sangeet behaal hoga. udhaaharan ke liye, cesil sharp ne schuli bacchon ko angreji paaramparik geet sikhaane ke liye chalaae gaye abhiyaan dwaara kuch safalataa praapt kii.
 ek vishay, jo vidwaanon ke paaramparik geet sangrah kii mahaan avadhi ke dauraan chalata rahaa he, "lok" sangeet ke kuch sadasyon kii pravriti par aadhaarit he, jinase aashaa kii jaati thi ki ve adhyayan kaa uddeshy banenge, taaki ve swayan hi vidwaan ban saken or apane paksh ko badhaava de saken. udhaaharan ke liye jin richi viper, kentuki ke bade parivaar kii sabase choti bacchee thi jisane appalachian kshetra ke kai puraane paaramparik geeton kaa sangrakshan kiya. richi us samay hui thi jab appalachian bahari duniya ke sampark main aae hi the, usne uniwarsity se padhai kii thi or antat neuyork shahar main sthanantarit ho gai, jahaan usne parivaar ke pradarshnon kii kai clasik ricarding banaain tathaa in geeton kaa ek mahatvapurn sankalan prakashit kiya. 
 beesvin sadi ke adhikansh samay uttari america loakgeet or paaramparik geeton ke bare main ek or mahatvapurn mudda yah tha ki kya maukhik rup se sanchaarit kii jane vaali jaankaree rupankanon kii shrinkhalaa main ke rup main pradaan kii jaae yaa phir puure geet ke rup main pradaan kii jaae, jisame pehle vaala drushtikon baad vaale se pehle ghatit ho gaya or apane udbhav ke samay donon drushtikonon ne seekhney ke prachlit manovaigyanik siddhanton ko parilakshith kiya. sangeet vidwaanon ke bich nunekaaree drushtikon kaa ek pramukh vaktaa jorge pulen jackson tha, jisane 1930 or 1940 ke dashak main, "tanayukt aavaran" yaa vilakshan sangitamay dhunon ke chihnon kii avadharana sthapit kii or iskaa bachaav kiya, jo aksar prayog main laae jane ke kaaran sangrah kii vastu ban gaye or nai dhunon ko taiyaar karne tathaa maujuuda dhunon ko sanshodhit karne kii prakriya main kadi ban gaye. 1950 main, samual preston beard ne apane dam par kathan or bhaavon dwaara sangeet vigyaan kii duniya sthapit karte hue spasht rup se yah kehete hue samgr drushtikon kaa bachaav kiya ki sangeet ko yaad karne or dohraane kii prakriyaayein us tarah se kaam nahin karatin jaise ki jackson tathaa anya kalpana karte hai. beard ne gestalt sangeet vigyaan, jo sadi ke madhya main lokapriy ban gaya tha, ke hawaale se tark diya ki vaakyansh jaisi badi sanrachanaatmak ikaiyaan tathaa yahaan tak ki puri dhun bhi mote taur par rup-rekha ke roopaatmak niyamon kaa paalan karti hai tathaa yah ki purna sangitamay dhun kii vakreey rekhaaen, na ki lay ke chote namoonay, hi paaramparik sangeetkaar ke mastishk main smriti chihnon ke rup main shaamil ho paati hai. in vichaaron ko apanaaya gaya, vibhinn tareekon se sanshodhit kiya gaya or sirvart polladian tathaa anyon dwaara punah prastut kiya gaya.
 1960 or 70 ke dashak main bartrend baronson or anyon ne is dharna par kaam shuru kiya ki yah prakriya na to puri tarah se nunekaaree ho sakati thi or na hi samgr, apitu yah in donon kaa jatil mishran thi, jabaki inhen aakaar dene main musical scale kaa bhi yogadan tha. is vichaar tathaa isse milte julate vichaaron kaa prayog karke ek nishkarsh par pahunchate hue, kintu saath hi puraane drushtikonon ko dhyaan main rakhte hue tathaa apni khud ke vistrut vishleshan ko isamen jodate hue, 1980 ke dashak main sangitvigyaani je. marshall bevil ne sangitamay peedi, prasaaran, aatmsaat karne tathaa phir se samaran karne kaa siddhant viksit kiya evam ise prastut kiya, jisame samgr drushtikon ke mahatva ko pahachaanaa gaya kintu saath hi smriti sahaayak tathaa sandarbh binduon ke rup main formulabaddh khand ke prarambh tathaa samaapan ke mahatva par bhi jor diya, jisame bade khandon main or adhik parivartansheel prarambh tathaa samaapan vaakyansh the jo kam mahatva ke the lekin phir bhi puri tarah se nakaarne yogya choti ikai ke rup main nahin the. unhonen aage kaha ki dhunon kii shrinkhalaa ke bich pratikriya dwaara shasit kehane-sunne kii prakriya paaramparik geet ke swaroop tathaa vishesh dhun, khand tathaa paaramparik sangitamay prakaar kii vaakyansh ruparekha se judi thi. in drushtikonon tathaa sangeet kii visheshtaon, jinaki vah jaanch kar rahe the, jo ki mukhyatah america dakshini pahaado ke gaathagith tathaa paaramparik bhajanon kii dhune thim, ke aadhaar par bevil ne tulanaatmak sangitamay vishleshan kii ek pranali kaa vikaas kiya jisane kai bhashavidon, vishesh rup se kriyaatmak vyakaran ko sthapit karne vaale nium chomski tathaa anyon ko aakarshith kiya. vistrut vishleshan prakriya main shaamil aankadon ko tivrata tathaa sateekta se sanbhaalane ke liye unhone ek desktop computer software kaa dhaancha bhi taiyaar kiya tathaa dhunon ke bich prakruti kii jhalak tathaa samanta kii had utpann karne ke liye kramadeshit maanakon kaa ek set lagu kiya. unhonen apane nishkarshon ko piech.di. shodhpatr dwaara 1986 ke ek lekh main, jo vishesh rup se paaramparik sangeet scale ke mudde ko samarpit tha tathaa 1987 ke ek adhyayan main prakashit kiya, jisame usee kshetra ke apravasiyon ke dhunon main badlaav kaa sangrah tha jisaki yatra cesil sharp ne sattar saal pehle kii thi. tab se hi bevil ne is lokapriy kshetra se vartmaan or puraane sangeet ke apane siddhanton kaa vistaar kiya he or yah jaanch kii he ki yah kaise anubhav kiya jaataa he, pahachaanaa jaataa he tathaa yaad kiya jaataa he.
