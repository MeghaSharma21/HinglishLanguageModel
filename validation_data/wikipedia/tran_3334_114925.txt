 manushya or maanavtaa ke vartmaan or bhavi jivan str ko dhyaan main rakhte hue prakritik sansadhano ke prabandhan ko prakritik sansaadhan prabandhan kehete hai.
 bhoomi naitikta kii avadharana aldo leopold dwaara di gayi.
 prakritik sansaadhan sangrakshan kaa ek itihaas he jo sangrakshan yug se pehle tak vistrut he. sansaadhan naitikta, prakruti ke saath sidhe sambandhon ke madhyam se aavashyaktaa ke parinamswaroop viksit hui. viniman yaa saamudayik sanyam aavashyak ho gaya taaki swaarth prayojanon ko sthaneey rup se sanbhale jane se adhik lene se roak saken, jisase baaqi samudaay ke liye dderghaavadhik aapurti sankat main na aa jaae.
 prakritik sansaadhan prabandhan ke sambandh main yah saamaajik duvidha aksar "aam kii traasadee" kehalaati he. is siddhant se sangrakshan jeev-vigyaani sampradayik sansaadhan sangharsh ke samaadhaan ke rup main sabhi samskritiyon main naitikta par aadhaarit saamudayik sansaadhan ko dhundh sakate hai. udhaaharan ke liye, alascan tlingit log or uttarpashchimi pasific haida main kabeelon ke bich sokee saleman machhlee pakadaney ke sambandh main sansaadhan simaaen, niyam or pratibandh maujuud the. ye niyam kabeelon ke bujurgon dwaara nirdeshit the, jo unke dwaara prabandhit prayek nadi or dhara ke jivan-paryant vivaran jaanate the. itihaas main aise kai udhaaharan hai, jahaan samskritiyon ne saamudayik prakritik sansaadhan prabandhan ke sambandh main niyamon, rivaajon or sangathit aacharan kaa paalan kiya he.
 sangrakshan naitikta praarambhik dharmik or darshanik lekhan main bhi pai gai he. tao, shinto, hindu, islam or buddha paramparaon main kai udhaaharan hai.
 greek darshan main, plato ne charaagaah bhoomi ksharan ke bare main shok vyakt kiya: "ab jo bachaa he, kehane ke liye, rog se barbaad sharir kaa kankal he; jile kaa samrudh, naram mitti le ja chuki he or keval nanga dhaancha chhod diya he." bibal main, musa ke madhyam se, bhagwaan ne ajna di ki har saatven varsh bhoomi ko kheti se aaraam den. tathaapi, 18veen sadi se pehle, adhikansh europea samskruti ne prakruti ko shraddha se nihaarne ko butaparasti mana. banjar bhoomi kii ninda kii gai jabaki krishi vikaas kii prashansa kii gai. tathaapi, 680 i. main hi saint kuthbert dwaara apane dharmik vishwaason kii pratikriya main farn dweep main vanya-jeev abhayaarany kii sthapana kii gai.
