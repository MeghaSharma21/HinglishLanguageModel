 lhatse, jo chutsar bhi bulaayaa jaataa he, tibet kaa ek shahar he. prashasnic rup se yah china dwaara niyantrit tibet swashasit pradesh naamak prashasnic praant me sthit he. 4,050 meter kii oonchaai par sthit yah shahar bharat main brahmaputra nadi kahalaane vaali yarlung tsangpo nadi kii ghati main basaa hua he. yah vibhaag kii rajdhani shigatse se 151 kimi dakshin-pashchim main us shahar jane vaale ek pahari darre se pashchim main sthit he. aadhunik lhatse basti se 10 kimi uttar main puraane lhatse gaanv kaa sthal he jahaan ek pracheen gelugpa buddha-math he or paas hi ek 150 meter unchi chattan par yarlung tsangpo mahaan ghati ke mukh par khadey ek puraane qile ke khandhar hai.
 lhatse se pashchim main mount overest or kailash parvat jane vaale maarg bant jate hai, jis vajah se bahut se paryatak is basti se hokar guzarate hai. 1962 ke bharat-china yuddh kaa kaaran jo jammu va kashmir ke china-adhikrut ilaake aksai chin se guzarane vaala shinjiyang-tibet rajmarg he vah bhi lhatse se hi shuru hokar 2,743 kilometre dur shinjiyang praant ke kargilic shahar main khatm hota he.
