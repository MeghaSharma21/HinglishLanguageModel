 2 may 2014 ko afghanistan ke badakhshaan praant ke argo jile main musaladhar barish se bhuskhalan kii ghatana hui. is ghatana main maare gayi logon kii sankhya alag-alag sroton se bhinn praapt hui jo 350 se 2700 tak he. lagbhag 300 makaan dhvasth ho gayi jabaki 14000 ghar kshatigrast ho gayi. bachaavkarmiyon ko shuruyaat main anya bhuskhalan ke kaaran pareshaaniyon kaa saamana karnaa padaa.
 2 may 2014 ko afghanistan ke badakhshaan praant ke argo naamak jile main pahaad kaa ek hissaa ek gaanv par gir gaya. vahaan pichley saptaah se musaladhar barish or baadh kii sthiti pehle sse bani hui thi. bhuskhalan paas ke gaanv prabhavit huye. hazaron ghar prabhavit huye jabaki 300 ghar bhuskhalan main dafan ho gayi yah sankhya gaanv kaa lagbhag ek tihaayi he. ye gaanv hajaaron ton malbe or chataanon ke neeche dab gayi jisane keechad se bani lahar kaa rup le liya or raah main aane vaali prayek vastu ko dhwast kar diya.
 praarambhik bhuskhalan subah 11:00 baje se dopaher 1:00 baje tak hua. yah ghatana shuukravaar ke din ghatit hui jab adhiktar log kaarysthaan par jane ke sthaan par ghar par hi puja kaa kaary karte hai. afghanistan sena sahit bachaav kaary jari kiye gayi jinhen shuruaati daur main aavashyak upkarnon kii kami kaa saamana karnaa padaa.
 afghanistan ke raashtrapati hamid karzai ke anusaar bhuskhalan main jaan-maal kaa baari nuksaan hua. america raashtrapati baraak obama ne kaha: "ham un logon ke saath hai jo afghanistan ke main bhayankar traasadee kaa saamana kar rahe hai. ham afghanistan kii is traasadee main har tarah se sahaayta karne ko taiyaar hai."
