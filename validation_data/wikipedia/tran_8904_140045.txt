 
 nawab akbar khan bugti pakistan sthit baluchistan praant ke ek rashtravaadi netaa the jo baluchistan ko pakistan se alag ek desh banane ke liye sangharsh kar rahe the. 26 agast 2006 ko baluchistan ke kohlu jile main ek sainya karrwai main akbar bugti or unke kai sahyogiyon kii hatya kar di gai thi. is abhiyaan kaa aadesh general parvaiz musharraf ne diya tha jo tab desh ke sainya pramukh or raashtrapati donon the.
 musharraf kaa shashan samaapt hone ke baad agali sarkaar dwaara 13 june 2013 ko is hatya ke silsile main unhen giraftaar kar liya gaya. haalanki oktober 2013 main is maamle main sabooton ke abhaav main unhen jamaanat de di gai.
 unke chauthe bete talaal akbar bugti ne oktober 2010 main musharraf ko maarane par ek arab rupee or 100 acre kheti kii jameen inaam main dene kii ghoshnaa kii thi. disambar 2013 main unhone ise badhaakar doguna arthath do arab rupee or 200 acre kheti kii jameen dene kii ghoshnaa kii. 2012 main akbar bugti ke ek pote ne musharraf ke sir par 10.1 crores rupee kaa inaam rakhaa tha.
 pakistani general, rajaneta or pakistan ke praant, punjab ke purv rajyapal.
