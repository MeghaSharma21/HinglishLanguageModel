 bromo parvat java dweep kaa parvat purv main tenger pradesh ke paas he. 2,32 9 meter main yah dravyaman kaa sabase ooncha shikhar nahin he, lekin sabase prasiddh he. mastiff kshetra purvi java, indonesia main sabase zyada daura karne vaale paryatakon main se ek he. jwalamukhi bromo tenger sameru rashtriya udyaan se sambandhit he. bramo kaa naam brahma ke javanese uccharan se liya gaya he, hindu nirmaataa devtaa.
 mount bromo 1 9 1 9 se ek sangrakshit prakruti arakshit "sagar of ret" naamak sade ke bich main baithta he. mount bromo kaa daura karne kaa vishisht tarika, symoro loang ke paas ke pahaad gaanv se he. vahaan se lagbhag 45 minute main jwalamukhi se chalanaa sambhav he, lekin ek sangathit jeep kaa daura bhi sambhav he, jisamen parvat parvanacan parvat par drushtikon par roak shaamil he. parvaan parvaanak par drushtikon lagbhag do ghante main pair par pahunchaa ja saktaa he. jwalamukheey gatividhi kii degree ke aadhaar par, jwaala vigyaan or aapadaa khatre main padane ke liye indonesian centre kabhi-kabhi mount bromo kii yatra ke khilaaf chetavanee detaa he.
