 phu bia laos kaa sabase unchaa parvat he. yah siengkhuang praant main siengkhuang pathaar kii dakshini seemaa par annami pahadiyon main sthit he. apni oonchaai ke kaaran iske shikhar par mausam thanda rahata he or iske aasapaas ke kshetra par koharaa or badal bane rahete hai. yah puraa kshetra wanon se susajjit he or parmparik rup se yahan hmong samudaay basataa rahaa he.
