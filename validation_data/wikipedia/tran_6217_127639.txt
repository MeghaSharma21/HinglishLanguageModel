 islamic ganraajya, yaa islamic jamhuriya ek aisi riyasat yaa rajnatik vyavastha hoti he, jise samvidhan main bataur islamic ganraajya nirdisht evam varnit kiya gaya ho. is shabdaawalee kii vistrut paribhaasha, desh-dar-desh evam samvidhan-dar-samvidhan bhinn ho sakati he, parantu saidhantik taur par aisi vyavastha kii mul-vichaardhaara main islamic siddhanton evam ganatantric siddhanton kaa sammilan hota he. arthath is vyavastha main naahin sampoornatah islamic siddhant hote hai, naahin sampoornatah ganatantric siddhant hote hai, balki in donon vichaardhaaraon kaa sammilit rup hota he. aadhiktar paristhitiyon main, rajanyik tantra va karypranali, ganatantric siddhanton par hoti he, or nyaayik vyavastha evam naagarik va dand samhita islamic usulon par aadhaarit hoti he. pakistan vishwa kaa pehla aisa desh tha jisane sanvaidhanik taur par swayan ko ek "islamic ganraajya" ghoshit kiya tha, evam is shabd kaa upyog bhi sarvapratham pakistan ke 1956 ke samvidhan main hi kiya gaya tha. tatpashchaat, moritania ne 28 november 1958 main apane naam ke saath islamic ganraajya shabd kaa istemaal kiya, iran ne irani kranti ke baad or afghanistan ne 2001 main taliban sarkaar samaapti ke baad apane naam ke saath islamic ganraajya shabd ko joda. varsh 2015 main gambia ne bhi swayan ko ek islamic ganraajya ghoshit kar liya tha.
 vishwa main maujuuda 5 islamic ganraajya hai:
  afghanistan
  iran
  moritania
  pakistan
  gambia
