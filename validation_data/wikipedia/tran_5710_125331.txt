 ron visli je. ke. rolling dwaara rachit harry potter shrunkhlaa main ek jaadugar he. wo iske pramukh kirdaar harry potter kaa khaas dost he. wo bhi tantra-mantra or jaadu-tone ke vidhyalay hoguarts main padhata he. uske maan-baap dono hi jaadugar hai -- ron ek shuddh rakt jaaduugari khandaan se he. anya jaaduugari khandaanon ke muqaabale unka parivaar garib he, magar wo log dil ke bade achche hai. uske parivaar main usko milakar saat bhai bahan hai. padhai main ron thik-thaak he. dusht lord voldemort se ladane main harmaini ne harry kii kai baar madad kii he.
 ron kaa kirdaar rowelin ne pehle hi din likh liya tha. ron kaa kirdaar unke ek dost shaun haris se prerit he, jinhe harry potter or rahasyamayi tahkhaanaa bhi samarpit he. jab bhi hari ko ron kii zarurat hoti he tab vah hameshaa uske liye hai. vah aksar hasya raahat ke rup main prayog kiya jaataa he, nayak ke prati vafaadaar or pratibha harry jaadui shakti ke maamle main kam se kam kaa bahut abhaav he. ron main kuch gun harry ke rup se behatar he, prakaar harry bohot ek amir anaath he athwa ron ek bade or pyaara lekin garib parivaar se aata he. ron jaadui shatranj main bhi maahir he.
