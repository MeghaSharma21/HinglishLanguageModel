 ise janvari 2002 main shuru kiya gaya. iske liecuba kii jameen kaa istemaal kiya gaya,jo america ne 1930 main lij par lee thi. neuyork ke9/11 vaale aatankwaadi ghatana ke baad ise kholaa gaya.lekin jald hi yah nafrat kii nishaani ban gai,jisaki pahchaan naarangi poshaak pahane or bediyon menjakade kaidiyon se hone lagi. kaidiyon ko pehlepinjrenuma baadon main rakhaa jaataa tha, jise baad main band karadia gaya.raashtrapati banane ke baad baraak obama ne kai baar isgel ko band karne kii koshish kii lekin unhenkamyaabi nahin mili.
 is jagah par kul 779 kaidiyon ko rakhaa gaya tha,jinamein se 149 abhi bhi vahiin hai. lagbhag 78kaidiyon ko bina kisi charge ke reeha karadia gaya kyonki ve america kii suraksha ke liekhatra nahin the. inamen 58 yaman ke, paanch tunisia ke,chaar afgaan or chaar syrian the.duusare group main 71 kaidi hai, jinamein se 10 par aropalgaaye gaye hai. unke khilaaf america kii vishesh sainikdaalat kaarywahi kar rahi he. taees duusare kaidiyon kekhilaaf abhiyog laganaa he, jabaki 38 kaidiyon kemaamalon kii samiksha hogi. isi group se paanch talibankidiyon ko reeha kiya gaya he.
 aath saal pehle 2006 main vishesh sainik adalatkaa gathan kiya gaya or uske baad se aath kaidiyon kekhilaaf mukadmaa chalaa or unhen doshi qaraar diya gaya.chah ne aparaadh kabool kiya. sangheey adhikariyon nedo kii sajaa ko ulat diya. do duusare maamalon main apilki gai. in chaaron ko baad main unke ghar bhej diya gaya.
 baaki bache kaidi 19 alag alag deshon ke hai. ekkaidi ko "bina kisi raashtra kaa naagarik"bataaya ja rahaa he. kul mila kar vahaan 87 yemeni, 12afgaan, 11 saudi or kuch misr, pakistan orrus ke kaidi hai. america chahta he ki koi teesraa deshgar in kaidiyon ko lenaa chaahe, to unhenreeha kiya ja saktaa he. 
 america adhikariyon kaa kahana he ki is jail main rahanevaale har kaidi par 27-28 lakh dollar prati varsh kharchahota he. jabaki america kii mukhya jelon main rahane vaalekaidiyon par salanaa 78,000 dollar kharch hota he.
 america kaa kahana he ki jin kaidiyon ko yahaan seriha kiya gaya he, un par khaas najar rakhi jaati he.kuch aankadon ke mutabik 30fisadi reeha kaidi dobaaraa aatankwaad ke kshetra main chalejaate hai. lekin america adhikari kaa kahana heki is sankhya main sandigdh bhi shaamil hai.
 gvantanamo be kii jail main kuch kii bahutjyada charchaa hoti he. inamen pakistan kaa khalid shikhmuhammad bhi he, jis par 9/11 kii sajeesh rachaneka aarope he. uske chaar saathi bhi isi jail main hai. isakelawa saudi arab kaa abd al rahim alanasiri bhi shaamil he. agar ye doshi qaraar die jate hai,to unhen maut kii sajaa ho sakati he.
