 2018 asia cap, sitambar 2018 main sanyukt arab emirate main yojit hone vaala ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket pratispardhaa thi. yah asia cap kaa 14waan sanskaran tha or 1984 or 1995 ke baad teesri baar sanyukt arab emirate main yojit kiya gaya. bharat iske purv sanskaran kaa vijeta rahaa tha. bharat maujuuda champion tha, or final main bangladesh ko teen wicket se haraakar apana khitaab barkaraar rakhaa he. port of spain main 2013 westeindies tri series main bharat kii aakhri jeet ke baad se yah 5 saal baad bharat kii pehali ek divasiy antarrashtriya toornament jeet thi.
 prarambh main, yah pratispardhaa bharat main yojit kiya janaa tha. lekin bharat or pakistan ke bich chal rahe raajnneetik tanaavon ke chalate ise sanyukt arab emirate main sthannrit kar diya gaya.
 29 oktober 2015 ko singapore main asian cricket parishad kii baithak ke baad, bhartiya cricket niyantran board ke sachiv ne kaha ki pratispardhaa kaa 2018 sanskaran bharat main yojit kiya jayega. agast 2017 main bisisiai ne 2017 asisi under-19 asia cap ke malaysia main sthanantarit hone ke baad, is pratispardhaa kii mejabani karne ke liye sarkari manjuuri maangi thi. april 2018 main, abu dhabi ke rajkumaar mohammad bin jayad al nahayaan ne, pakistan kii bhagidaari sunishchit karne ke liye bisisiai or asisi ke saamne abu dhabi main pratispardhaa karaane kaa vichaar rakhaa.
 pakistan april main, pratispardhaa se pehle 2018 asisi imarging team asia cap kii mejabani karne vaala tha. lekin asia cap ke sanyukt arab emirate main sthannrit hone ke baad, imarging team asia cap disambar 2018 tak ke liye sthagit kar diya gaya, jisamen srilanka, pakistan ke saath milakar pratispardhaa kaa sah-aayojan karegaa.
 kaarykram or praaroop 24 july 2018 ko ghoshit kiya gaya tha, jisamen kul chah team ko teen-teen ke do samoohon main vibhajit kiya gaya tha. prayek samooh kii sheersh do team pratispardhaa ke super chaar charan main pravesh kare. is charan kii sheersh do team final main ek duusare ke virudhd khele. is pratispardhaa kaa pehla match bangladesh or srilanka ke bich dubai antarrashtriya cricket stadium main khelaa gaya.
