 ber fal kaa ek prakaar hai. kachche fal hare rang ke hote hai. pakane par thodaa laal yaa laal-hare rang ke ho jate hai.
 ber ek aisa faldaar ped he jo ki ek baar poorak sinchai se sthapit hone ke pashchaat varsha ke pani par nirbhar rahakar bhi falotpaadan kar saktaa he. yah ek bahuvarshiya va bahuupayogi faldaar ped he jisamen falon ke atirikt ped ke anya bhaagon kaa bhi aarthik mahatv he. shushk kshetron main baar-baar akaal kii sthiti se nipatane ke liye bhi ber kii bagwani ati upyogi siddh ho sakati he. isaki pattiyaan pashuon ke liye paushtik chaara pradaan karti he jabaki isamen prativarsh anivaary rup se kii jane vaali kataai-chantai se praapt kantedaar jhaadiyaan kheton va dhaniyon kii rakshaatmak baad banane va bhandarit chaare kii suraksha ke liye upyogi he.
 ber kheti ooshna va uposhna jalwaayu main aasaani se kii ja sakati he kyonki isamen kam pani va sookhe se ladane kii vishesh kshamta hoti he ber main vanaspatik badhwaar varsha ritu ke dauraan va phool varsha ritu ke aakhir main aate he tathaa fal varsha kii bhumigat nami ke kam hone tathaa taapmaana badhane se pehle hi pak jate he. garmiyon main paudhe sushuptavastha main pravesh kar jate he va us samay pattiyaan apane aap hi jhad jaati he tab pani kii aavashyaktaa nahin ke baraabar hoti he. is tarah ber adhik taapmaana to sahan kar letaa he lekin sheet ritu main padane vaale paale ke prati ati samvedansheel hota he. atah aise kshetron main jahaan niyamit rup se paala padane kii sambhaavna rahati he, isaki kheti nahin kerani chahie.
 jahaan tak mitti kaa sawaal he, balui domat mitti jisamen jeevansh kii maatra adhik ho iske liye sarvottam maani jaati he, haalaaki balui mitti main bhi samuchit maatra main deshi khaad kaa upyog karke isaki kheti kii ja sakati he. halki kshareey va halki lavniya bhoomi main bhi isko laga sakate he. ber main 300 se bhi adhik kismein viksit kii ja chuki he parantu sabhi kismein baraani kshetron main visheshkar kam varsha vaale kshetron ke liye upyukt nahin he. aise kshetron ke liye ageti va madhyam avadhi main pakane vaali kismein zyada upyukt pai gai he.
