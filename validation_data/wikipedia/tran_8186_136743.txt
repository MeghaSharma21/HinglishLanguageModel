 pashchim himalayan chaudee pattee van ek prakaar ke samshitoshna chaudee pattee van he jo nepal, bharat or pakistan ke pashchimi himalay ke jaevakshetra main madhyam oonchaai par paae jate hai.
 samshitoshna chaudee pattee wanon kaa yah jaevakshetra 1,500 se 2,600 meter kii oonchaai par 55,900 varg kilometre main faile hua hai. yah nepal main gandki nadi kii tangaghati se, bharat ke uttar pradesh, uttrakhand, himachal pradesh or jammu or kashmir rajyon se hote hue purvi pakistan tak failaa hua he. yah jaevakshetra purvi himalayan uttalpine shankudhar wanon se jo gandki nadi se purv main he, adhik sookhaa he.
