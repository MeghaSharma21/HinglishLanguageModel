 
 basra anterrashtriya vimaanashetra  iraq kaa doosraa bada anterrashtriya vimaanashetra he. yah yahaan ke dakshini shahar basra main sthit he. 
 is vimaanashetra kaa nirmaan 1980 ke dashak main hua tha or baad main iraqi sarkaar ke state organaizeshan for rods and brijez iskaa vikaas iraq ke ekmaatr pattan ke rup main kiya gaya. manyata ke ansuaar is vimaanashetra ko keval ati mahatvapurn vyaktiyon ke prayog hetu hi banaaya gaya tha.
