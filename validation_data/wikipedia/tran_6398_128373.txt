 kaliyug parmparik bharat kaa chauthaa yug he.
 aryabhat ke anusaar mahabharat yuddh 3137 ipu main hua. kaliyug kaa armbh krishna ke is yuddh ke 35 varsh pashchaat nidhan par hua. Encyclopedia of Hinduism ke anusaar bhagwaan shri krishna ke is prithvi se prasthaan ke tunrat baad 3102 isa purv se kali yug armbh ho gaya.
 dharmraj yudhisthir, bhimsen, arjuna, nakul or sahadev paanchon pandav mahaparakramee parakshit ko rajya dekar mahaprayan hetu uttarakhand kii ore chale gayi or vahaan jaakar punyalok ko praapt huye. raja parakshit dharm ke anusaar tathaa brahnnon kii aagyaanusaar shashan karne lage. uttar naresh kii putree iravati ke saath unhone apana vivaah kiya or us uttam patni se unke chaar putr utpann huye. acharya krip ko guru bana kar unhone jahnavi ke tat par teen ashwamegh yajna kiye. un yagyon main anant dhan raashi brahmanon ko daan main di or digvijay hetu nikal gayi.
 unheen dinon dharm ne bail kaa rup bana kar gaurupini prithvi se saraswati tat par bhent kiya. gaurupini prithvi kii netron se ashru bah rahe the or vah shrihin si prathit ho rahi thi. dharm ne prithvi se poocha - "he devi! tumhaara mukh malin kyon ho rahaa he? kis baat kii tumhen chintaa he? kahin tum meri chintaa to nahin kar rahi ho ki ab meraa keval ek pair hi rah gaya he yaa phir tumhen is baat kii chintaa he ki ab tum par shudra rajya karenge?"
 prithvi boli - "he dharm! tum sarvajna hokar bhi mujh se mere dukh kaa kaaran poochhate ho! satya, pavitrataa, kshamaa, dayaa, santosh, tyaag, sham, dam, tap, saralata, kshamta, shaastra vichaar, uparati, titiksha, gyaan, vairaygy, shaury, tej, aishwarya, bal, smriti, kaanti, kaushal, swatantrata, nirbhikta, komalta, dhairya, saahas, sheel, vinay, saubhaahgya, utsaah, gambheertha, kirti, aastikta, sthirataa, gaurav, ahankaarhinta aadi gunon se yukt bhagwaan shrikrishna ke swadham gaman ke kaaran ghor kaliyug mere upar aa gaya he. mujhe tumhaare saath hi saath dev, pitrugan, rishi, sadhu, sanyasi aadi sabhi ke liye mahaan shok he. bhagwaan shrikrishna ke jin charnon kii seva lakshmi ji karti hai unamem camel, vajra, ankush, dhwaja aadi ke chihn virajman hai or ve hi charan mujh par padate the jisase main saubhagyawati thi. ab meraa saubhaahgya samaapt ho gaya he."
 jab dharm or prithvi ye baaten kar hi rahe the ki mukutadhaari shudra ke rup main kaliyug vahaan aayaa or un donon ko maarane laga.
 raja parakshit digvijay karte huye vahiin par se guzar rahe the. unhone mukutadhaari shudra ko haath main danda liye ek gaay or ek bail ko buree tarah peetate dekha. vah bail atyant sundar tha, uskaa shwet rang tha or keval ek pair tha. gaay bhi kamdhenu ke samaan sundar thi. donon hi bhaybheet ho kar kaanp rahe the. maharaj parakshit apane dhanushwan ko chadhaakar megh ke samaan gambheer vaani main lalakaare - "re dusht! paapi! naradham! tu koun he? in nirih gaay tathaa bail kyon sata rahaa he? tu mahaan apradhi he. tere aparaadh kaa uchit dand tera vadh hi he." unke in vachanon ko sun kar kaliyug bhay se kaanpane laga.
 maharaj ne bail se poocha ki he bail tumhaare teen pair cas toote gayi hai. tum bail ho yaa koi devtaa ho. he dhenuputr! tum nirbhiktapurvak apana apana vrutant mujhe bataao. he gaumata! ab tum bhaymukt ho jaao. main dushton ko dand detaa hoon. kis dusht ne mere rajya main ghor paap kar ke pandavon kii pavitra kirti main yah kalank lagaaya he? chaahe vah paapi saakshaat devtaa hi kyon na ho main uske bhi haath kaat doongaa. tab dharm bolaa - "he maharaj! aapane bhagwaan shrikrishna ke parambhakt pandavon ke kul main janm liya he atah ye vachan aap hi ke yogya hai. he rajan! ham yah nahin jaanate ki sansar ke jeevon ko koun klesh detaa he. shastron main bhi iskaa nirupan anek prakaar se kiya gaya he. jo dwait ko nahin maanata vah dukh kaa kaaran apane aap ko hi sweekaar karata hai. koi praarabdh ko hi dukh kaa kaaran maanata he or koi karm ko hi dukh kaa nimitt samajhataa he. katipay vidwaan swabhaav ko or katipay ishwar ko bhi dukh kaa kaaran maanate hai. atah he rajan! ab aap hi nishchit kijiye ki dukh kaa kaaran koun he."
 samraat parakshit us bail ke vachanon ko sun kar bole - "he vrishabh! aap avashya hi bail ke rup main dharm hai or yah gaurupini prithvi mata he. aap dharm ke marm ko bhalee-bhanti jaanate hai. aap kisi kii chugali nahin kar sakate isiliye aap dukh dene vaale kaa naam nahin bataa rahe hai. he dharm! satyuga main aapke tap, pavitrataa, dayaa or satya chaar charan the. treta main teen charan rah gayi, dwapar main do hi rah gayi or ab is dusht kaliyug ke kaaran aapkaa ek hi charan rah gaya he. yah adharmarupi kaliyug apane asaty se us charan ko bhi nasht karne kaa praytn kar rahaa he. bhagwaan shrikrishnachandra ke swadham gaman se dusht paapi shudra raja log is gaurupini prithvi ko bhogenge isi kaaran se yah mata bhi duhkhi hai."
 itnaa kah kar raja parikshith ne us paapi shudra rajveshadhari kaliyug ko maarane ke liye apni tikshn dhar vaali talwaar nikaali. kaliyug ne bhaybheet hokar apane rajasi vesh ko utaar kar raja parakshit ke charnon main gir gaya or traahi-traahi kehane laga. raja parakshit bade sharnaagat vatsal the, unhone sharan main aaye huye kaliyug ko maarna uchit na samajha or kaliyug se kaha - "he kaliyug! tu mere sharan main aa gaya he isaliye maine tuje pranadan diya. kintu adharm, paap, jhooth, chori, kapat, daridrata aadi anek updravon kaa mul kaaran keval tu hi he. atah tu mere rajya se turant nikal ja or laut kar phir kabhi mat aana."
 raja parakshit ke in vachanon ko sun kar kaliyug ne kaatar vaani main kaha - "he rajan! aapkaa rajya to sampoorn prithvi par he, aapke rajya se baahar aisa koi bhi sthaan nahin he jahaan par ki main nivas kar sakun. he bhupati! aap bade dayaalu hai, aapane mujhe sharan diya he. ab dayaa karke mere nivas kaa bhi kuch na kuch prabandh aapako karnaa hi hoga."
 kaliyug is tarah kehane par raja parakshit soch main pad gayi. phir vichaar kar ke unhone kaha - "he kaliyug! dyut, madhpan, parastrigaman or hinsa in chaar sthanon main asaty, mad, kaam or krodh kaa nivas hota he. in chaar sthanon main nivas karne kii main tuje chhot detaa hoon." is par kaliyug bolaa - "he uttaranandan! ye chaar sthaan mere nivas ke liye aparyaapt hai. dayaa karke koi or bhi sthaan mujhe dijiye." kaliyug ke is prakaar maangane par raja parakshit ne use paanchava sthaan 'swarna' diya.
