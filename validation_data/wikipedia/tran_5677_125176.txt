 battlestar galactica ek america kaalpanik vigyaan par aadhaarit sainya televijan shrunkhlaa he va battlestar galactica franchaizi kaa ek bhaag he. sho kaa vikaas ronaald di. moore dwaara 1978 main bani battlestar galactica televijan shrunkhlaa ko maddenzar rakh kar banaaya jiskaa nirmaan glen e larsan ne kiya tha. is shrunkhlaa kaa prasaaran tin ghanton ki laghu shrunkhlaa ke rup main disambar 2003 ko syphaay channel par kiya gaya tha va yah aage chalakar chaar satron tak chali va 20 march 2009 ko samaapt hui. is shrunkhlaa main edward james olmos or meri mcdonald mukhya bhoomikaaon main he or is shrunkhlaa ko samikshakon dwaara behad saraahaa gaya he va use pibody puraskaar, televijan critics association kaa varsh kaa dharawahik puraskaar, times kii abtak kii sarvshereshth tivi shrunkhalaaon kii suchi main naam, kai emi namakan va apane vishwal effecton va dhwani sampadan ke liye emi puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya he.
 battlestar galactica kii kahani ek sudur taramandal main rachi gai he jahaan maanav sabhyataa grahon kii ek shrunkhlaa par rahati he jise tvilv colonies kaha jaataa he. apane bhootkaal main ye coloniyaan apane hi dwaara nirmit saychlons naam kii prajati ke saath yuddh kar rahi thi. gaius balter naam ke ek maanav kii madad se saychlons ne ek coloniyon par achaanak hamalaa kar diya jiske chalate grahon par vidhwans va badi sankhya main jane gai. kai kharbon kii jansankhya main se keval 50,000 maanav hi jivit batch sake jo saarvajanik vimaanon main the jo ladaai se bhaag nikalne main safal rahe. puri colony kii sena main se keval battlestar galactica naam kaa sainya bedaa batch nikalne main kaamyaab rahaa. colonial flit officer commander wiliam "bill" adama or raashtrapati laura roslin ke netritv main galactica kaa dal ek chote bache hue logon ke bade ke saath brahmand main prithvi kii khoj main nikal padataa he jo khoi hui teraavi colony he.
