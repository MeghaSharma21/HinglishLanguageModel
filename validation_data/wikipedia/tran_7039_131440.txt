 padam sammaan bharat sarkaar dwaara shaskeey sevakon va anya bharatiyon ko kisi bhi kshetra main asaadhaaran or vishisht seva ke liye padam vibhushan, padam bhushan or padam shri naamak padam sammaan pradaan kiye jate hai. padam puraskaron kii siforishein rajya sarkaron/sangh rajya prashasano, kendriya mantralayon/vibhaagon, utkrushtataa sansthaanon aadi se praapt kii jaati hai, jin par puraskaar samiti dwaara vichaar kiya jaataa he. puraskaar samiti kii sifarish ke aadhaar par or pradhanmantri, grahamantri tathaa raashtrapati ke anumodan ke baad ganatantr diwas kii purv sandhya par in padam sammaanon kii ghoshnaa kii jaati he. parantu is baar saamaanya nagarikon ko ye sammaan dene kii prakriya main kuch badlaav kiya gaya he.
 
 bhartiya samachar agency bhashaa dwaara jari or nai dilli se ek pramukh samachar patr navabharat times main prakashit samachar ke anusaar kendra sarkaar ke grih mantralay ne padam puraskaar dene kii prakriya ko or adhik paardarshee banane ke uddeshy se aavedan-patr prastut karne hetu sanshodhit prapatr jari kiya he. is prapatr main aavedak ke bare main moolbhoot soochnaaon ke alaava kala, khelkud, saamaajik kaary jaise kshetron main unki gatividhiyon or sambandhit kshetron main unke dwaara praapt kii gayi uplabdhiyon ke bare main aalekh ke rup main jaankaree maangi gayi he. do pannon kaa yah aalekh adhiktam 800 shabdon main hona chahie.
