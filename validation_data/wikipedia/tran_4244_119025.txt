 sheikh mujibur rahamaan bangladesh ke sansthaapak netaa, mahaan agua evam pratham raashtrapati the. unhen saamanyat: bangladesh kaa janak kaha jaataa he. ve avami lig ke adhyaksh the. unhone pakistan ke kilaaf sashastr sangram kii aguwai karte hue bangladesh ko mukti dilaai. ve bangladesh ke pratham raashtrapati bane or baad main pradhanmantri bhi bane. ve 'sheikh mujeeb' ke naam se bhi prasiddh the. unhen 'bangbandhu' kii padavi se sammanit kiya gaya.
 15 agast 1975 ko sainik takhtapalat ke dwaara unki hatya kar di gai. unki do betiyon main ek shaikh hasina takhtapalat ke baad germany se dilli ai or 1981 tak dilli rahi or 1981 ke baad bangladesh ja ke pita kii rajnatik viraasat ko sambhala.
 15 agast 1975 kii subah bangladesh kii sena ke kuch baagi yuva afasaron ke hathiyaarband daste ne dhaka sthit raashtrapati awaas par pahunch kar raashtrapati sheikh mujibur rahamaan kii hatya kar di. hamalavar tanks lekar gaye the. pehle un logon ne bangbandhu mujibur rahamaan ke bete sheikh kamaal ko maaraa or baad main mujeeb or unke anya parijanon ko.
 mujeeb ke sabhi teen bete or unki patni kii baari-baari se hatya kar di gai. hamale main kul 20 log maare gaye the. mujeeb shashan se bagawati sena ke jawan hamale ke samay kai daston main bante the. apratyaashit hamale main mujeeb parivaar kaa koi purush sadsy nahin bachaa. unki do betiyaan sanyogvash batch gayi, jo ghatana ke samay germany main thim. unamem ek shaikh hasina or duusari sheikh rehana thim. sheikh hasina abhi bangladesh kii pradhanmantri hai. apane pita kii hatya ke baad sheikh hasina hindustan rahane lagi thim. vahiin se unhone bangladesh ke nae shaskaon ke kilaaf abhiyaan chalaaya. 1981 main vah bangladesh lautin or sarvasammati se avami lig kii adhyaksh chun lee gayin.
