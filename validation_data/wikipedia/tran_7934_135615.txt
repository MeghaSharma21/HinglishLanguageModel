 nirdeshaank: 29°57′04″N 80°07′49″E / 29.9509932°N 80.13029689999996°E / 29.9509932; 80.13029689999996tejam uttrakhand rajya ke pithauragarh janapad main ramganga nadi ke tat par basaa ek chota sa nagar he. yah nagar uttrakhand rajya rajmarg 11 tathaa 31 ke tirahe par sthit he. tejam kaa kul kshetrafal 2.277 varg kilometre he, tathaa yah samudr tal se 1055 meter kii oonchai par sthit he.
 sitambar 2016 main uttarakhand ke tatkaaleen mukhyamantri, harish ravat ne munsyari ke talla johaar kshetra ko tehsil kaa darja dene kii ghoshnaa kii thi, jiskaa mukhyalay tejam main tay kiya gaya. november 2016 main tehsil astitv main ai, tathaa iskaa naam tejam tehsil rakh diya gaya. haalanki disambar 2017 tak bhi yahaan kisi adhikari kii niyukti nahin hui thi. 
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar tejam main kul 152 parivaar nivas karte hai, or nagar kii jansankhya 495 he, jisamen se 234 purush hai tathaa 261 mahilaayein hai. 0-6 varsh ke bacchon kii sankhya 76 he. tejam kaa linganupaat 1115 mahilaayein prati 1000 he; purushon main sakshartaa dar 95.98 % he, jabaki mahilaon main yah 68.18 % he.
