 dugdhashala yaa dairy main pashuon kaa doodh nikaalane tathaa tatsambadhi anya vyapaarik evam audyogic gatividhiyaan kii jaati hai. isamen praayah gaay or bhens kaa doodh nikaala jaataa he kintu bakrii, bhed, oont or ghodi aadi ke bhi doodh nikaale jate hai. 
 deshon ke bich shabdaawalee alag-alag he. udhaaharan ke liye, sanyukt rajya america main ek farm jahaan doodh nikaalane kaa kaam hota he milking parlor ke naam se janaa jaataa he. newjiland main aisi imaarat kaa aitihaasik naam milkang shed he- haalanki haal ke kuch varshon main pragatisheel parivartan ke falswaroop in imaaraton ko farm deyari ke naam se pukaara jane laga he. 
 kuch deshon main, vishesh rup se jahaan pashuon kii sankhya kam he, udhaaharan ke taur par chote deyari main doodh nikaalane ke saath-saath ek sansaadhan dwaara doodh se makkhan chiz or dahi banane kaa kaam hota he. khaas taur par europe main visheshgy doodh utpaada ke utpaadan kaa yah ek paaramparik tarika he. sanyukt rajya america main deyari vah jagah bhi ho sakati he jahaan deyari utpaadon kaa prasanskaran, vitaran or bikri hoti he, yaa ek cameraa, bhawan yaa sthaan hota he jahaan doodh ko sangrahit kar, sansaadhit kar makkhan yaa chiz jaise utpaada banaae jate hai. newjiland kii angreji main deyari shabd kaa prayog vishesh rup se suvidhajanak dukaan yaa superet ko sanderbhit karata he. iskaa upyog aitihaasik he, kyonki aisi dukaane logon ke liye ek aam jagah thi jahaan se doodh utpaada kharide jate the.
 gunavachak rup main, deyari shabd sanderbhit he doodh aadhaarit utpaadon se, vyutpaadit or sansaadhan or jaanavar evam unke kaaryakartaaon is utpaadan main shaamil hai: udhaaharan ke liye deyari gaay deyari bakrii. ek deyari farm doodh kaa utpaadan karata he or ek deyari kaarkaana sansaadhan dwaara kai kism ke deyari utpaada taiyaar karata he. ye pratishthan deyari udyog kaa gathan karte hai jo khaadya udyog kaa hi ek ghatak he.
 doodh dene vaale pashuon kaa paalan hajaaron varshon se hota aa rahaa he. prarambh main, inkaa upyog nirvaah kheti ke liye khanaabadosh dwaara kiya jaataa tha. jab bhi puraa samudaay duusare desh main sthaanantaran karata tha ye unke saath hote the. jaanwaron kii raksha karnaa or khilaanaa yah jaanwaron or charavaahon ke bich sahjivi sambandh kaa ek bada hissaa tha. 
 haal ke ateet main, krishi karne vaale samaaj ke log dudharu jaanwaron ko kutir udyog ke rup main gharelu or sthaneey main doodh kii khapat ke liye paalate the. jaanavar kai prakaar se inke liye upyogi hai, udhaaharan ke liye javaani ke samay inke liye hal khinchate hai or maranoparant iskaa maance unalogon ke liye upyogi hota he. is maamle main aam taur par jaanwaron ko haath se duha jaataa tha or inke jhund kaa aakaar kaaphi chota hota tha, isliye saare pashuon ke ek ghante main duh liya jaataa tha - kareeb das prati doodh duhane vaale. in kaaryon ko doodh duhane vaali yaa doodh duhane vaala puraa karte the. deyari shabd kii utpatti madhya angreji ke shabd deberi se hui he, deye arthath or puraani angreji kaa shabd dege . 
 audyogikaran or shaharikaran ke saath, doodh kii aapurti ne vanijyik udyog kaa rup le liya he, gomance or dibbaband se bilkul bhinn vishisht naslon vaale maweshiyon ko deyari ke liye viksit kiya gaya. shuru main, adhik log doodh duhane vaale ke rup main karyrat hua karte the, lekin jaldi hi mashinikaran ke saath yah kaam mashiinon se hone laga. 
 aitihaasik rup se deyari formon main, ek hi samay or sthaan par doodh duhane or sansaadhan karne kaa kaam ek saath kiya jaataa he. log haath se pashuon ko duhate hai; farm par inaki sankhya kam hone kii vajah se, abhi bhi haath se doodh duha jaataa he. than ko haathon se pakadakar dabaane se doodh baahar aane lagata he, doodh nikaalane ke liye is prakriya ko lagaataar doharaana padataa he, than ko ungaliyon or anguuthe ke dwaara upree sire se dabaate hue neeche kii ore laane se doodh nikalne lagata he. bhaag main haath yaa ungaliyon kii karrwai karne se than ke ant main doodh vahini khul jaati hai or ungaliyon kii gatividhi se vahini main ruka hua doodh than se nikalne lagata he. prayek baar than ke aadhe yaa chauthai bhaag se doodh vahini kii kshamta ko khaali kar liya jaataa he. 
 anaavruth karne kii kriya ko donon haathon kaa prayog kar teji se doharaana padataa he. donon hi tareekon se doodh vahini main atka hua doodh baahar aane lagata he or ghutanon ke bich ek baaltee ko tikaakar doodh duhane vaale doodh ko baaltee main ikattha kar lete hai, aamtaur par doodh dahanewaale ek choti stool par baithte hai. 
 paramparaagat rup se gaay, doodh duhate samay kisi maidan yaa ahaate main khadi hoti hai. choti gaay, bachhiya ko bhi doodh nikaalane ke samay sthir hokar khadey rahane ke liye prashikshit kiya jaataa he. kai deshon main, gaayon ko doodh nikaalane ke samay ek khambhe se baandh diya jaataa he. is paddati ke saath samasya yah he ki in shant, vinayashil jaanwaron par nirbhar karata he, kyonki gaay ke peeche ke bhaag ko niyantrit nahin kiya ja saktaa he.
 1937 main, yah paaya gaya ki bovine somatotropin doodh kii maatra main vruddhi karti hai. monsento company ne is hormone kaa ek kritrim sanskaran viksit kiya. farvari 1994 main, arabsti ko khaadya evam aushadhi prashasan ne america main prayog ke liye manjuuri de di thi. isse yah america main aam ho gaya, lekin or kahin nahin, dudharu gaayon ko isaki sui dene se unke doodh main 15% tak kii vruddhi ho jaati he. 
 haalanki, aise daawey bhi kiye gaye hai ki is abhyaas se jaanwaron ke liye nakaaratmak parinaam ho sakate hai. europea sangh ke vaigyanik ayog ne deyari gaayon ke vikaron or anya stan kii sujan kii ghatana ke bare main or deyari gaayon ke kalyaan ke anya pahaluon par report karne ko kaha. ayog ke bayaan ko, baad main europea sangh dwaara apanaaya gaya, vaktavy diya gaya ki arabsti ke prayog se gaayon ko swasthya samasyaon kaa saamana karnaa padataa he inamen pair kii samasyaayein, stan kii sujan or injection kii tatkaalik pratikriyaaon sahit pashuon ke kalyaan or prajnan vikaron kii vruddhi hoti he. report main yah nishkarsh nikaala gaya ki swasthya or pashuon ke kalyaan ke aadhaar par, arabsti kaa prayog nahin kiya janaa chahie. swasthya canada ne 1999 main arabsti kii bikri ko nishiddh kar diya; bahari samitiyon kii siforeeshon thi ki, manushya ke swasthya par koi khaas jokhim nahin hone ke baavjood, yah dawa pashu ke swasthya ke liye ek khatra he, isi kaaran ise canada main bechaa nahin ja saktaa.
 adhikansh desh apane deshon main hi dugdh utpaadon kaa utpaadan karte hai, duniya ke vibhinn bhaagon main deyari udyog kii sanrachana bhinn he. pramukh dugdh-utpaadak deshon main sabase adhik doodh thok bazaar ke madhyam se vitarit kiya jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye, ireland or australia main, kisaanon kii sahakaari samiti ke paas bade paimaane par kai prosesar hai, jabaki sanyukt rajya america main kai kisaan or procesar vyaktigat theke ke madhyam se kaarobaar karte hai. sanyukt rajya america main, desh ke 196 kisaanon kii sahakaari samitiyon ne 2002 main america main 86% doodh bechaa, saath hi paanch sahakaari samitiyon ne uskaa aadha hissaa hi lekhankan kiya. yah 1940 ke dashak main 2300 sahakaaritaaon dwaara kiya gaya tha. vikasshil deshon main, apane hi pados main kisaanon ke doodh vipanan ke puraane tareeke teji se badal rahe hai. ullekhneey ghatnakram he ki deyari udyog main kaaphi videshi nivesh ho rahe hai or isamen deyari sahakaari samitiyon ke liye ek badhati bhoomika shaamil he. kuch deshon main doodh kaa utpaadan bahut teji se bad rahaa he or yah kai kisaanon ke samaksh vikaas ke liye aay ke pramukh strot prastut kar rahaa he. 
 khaadya udyog ke kai anya shakhaon ke rup main, pramukh dugdh utpaadak deshon main dugdh deyari prasanskaran par dhyaan kendrit kiya ja rahaa he, jahaan inaki sankhya kam he lekin ye bahut bade or adhik utpaadan kii kshamta vaale plant he jiskaa sanchaalan bahut kam shramikon ke dwaara kiya jaataa he. yah ghatana vishesh rup se sanyukt rajya america, europe, australia or newjiland kii he. 2009 main, sanyukt rajya america ke pramukh deyari udyogon ke khilaaf avishvaas ulanghan ke aarope lagaae. 
 20 veen sadi main doodh bazaar main sarkaar kaa hastkshep ek aam baat thi. america sahakaari deyari samitiyon ke liye ek simit vishwaas-virdohi chhot 1922 main capper-volstad adhinium dwaara banaaya gaya. 1930 main, kuch america rajyon ne mulya niyantran ko apanaaya or 1937 ke krishi vipanan qaraar adhinium ke tahat sangheey dugdh vipanan aadesh shuru kiya gaya or jo 2000 ke dashak main bhi jari he. sangheey doodh mulya sahaayta kaarykram 1949 main shuru kiya gaya. new england main purvottar deyari compact viniyamit thok doodh kii keemten 1997 se 2001 tak. 
 aise kaarkaane jo taral doodh kaa utpaadan or kam dinon tak tikne vaali chijen jaise dahi, kriim or soft cheez kaa utpaadan karte hai shahari kendron ke bahari ilaake main upabhokta bazaar ke paas sthit hote hai. adhik dinon tak tikne vaali chijen jaise makkhan, doodh powder, chiz or mattha powder kaa utpaadan karne vaale sanyantr, gramin kshetron main sthit hote hai jahaan doodh kii aapurti najadik hoti he. adhiktar bade prasanskaran vaale sanyantr ke utpaada vishesh rup se simit hote hai. vishesh rup se, tathaapi, bade ek vistrut shrinkhalaa main utpaadon kaa nirmaan karne vaale plant abhi bhi purvi europe main aam hai, aapurti-chaalit bazaar kii avadharana purv kendrikrut he.
 bade prasanskaran sanyantron kii sankhya kam hoti hai, par ve vishaal or adhik swachaalit hote hai or unke paas adhik kushal upkaran hote hai. haalanki is takneekee pravriti se nirmaan laagat bahut kam rahata he, lekin parivahan kii duuri lambii rakhane kii jarurat aksar paryaavaran par adhik prabhaav daalati he.
 gaay ke jeev vigyaan ke aadhaar par doodh kaa utpaadan aniyamit hota he. nirmaataaon ko maang or aapurti ke parivartan par nirbhar karte hue doodh kaa mishran jo taral rup main bechaa jaataa he banaam sansaadhit khaadya padaarth samyojit karnaa chahie.

 jab badi sankhya main gaayon ko duhana jaruri hota he tab unhen shed yaa baade main le aayaa jaataa he jahaan unke main puaal khaane kii vyavastha rahati he or isi bahaane unka doodh duh liya jaataa he. ek vyakti is tarah se adhik gaayon ko duh saktaa he, kushal kaamgaar kareeb 20 gaayon ko duh lete hai. lekin gaay yard main yaa shed main khadey hokar apni doodh duhane kii baari kaa intazaar karen yah gaayon ke liye acha nahin hota he, jitna adhik sambhav ho unka samay ghaas charne main guzarana chahie. yah saamaanya rup se pratibandhit he ki doodh duhane kii kriya ko din bhar main do baar adhiktam ek ghante main puraa kar lenaa chahie or pratibaar aadhe ghante main. isse koi fark nahin padataa he ki ek vyakti 10 gaayon ko duh rahaa he yaa 1000 gaayon ko, ek gaay ko duhane ke liye dainik teen ghante se zyada nahin laganaa chahie. 
 jaisaa ki pashuon ke jhund kaa aakaar bada ho to usee ke anupaat main vahaan mashin se duhane kii, doodh ke bhandaaran kii suvidhaayen, thok doodh ke parivahan kii, safai kshamtaaon kii or gaayon ko shed se khalihaanon tak le jane or laane kii vyavastha honi chahie. 
 kisaanon ne paaya ki doodh duhane ke samay par gaayen charaai kshetra ko chhodkar apane aap duhane ke kshetra main chali jaati hai. isamen koi aashcharya kii baat nahin he kyonki duhane ke mausam main, gaayen shaayad adhik doodh ke boj se latake hue than ke kaaran asahaj mahasoos karne lagati hai or doodh nikal jane se unhen raahat miltaa he isliye vah duhane kii jagah par chali jaati hai. 
 pashuon kii badi jhund ke saath pashu ke swasthya kii samasyaayein bhi bad jaati hai. newjiland main is samasya main do tareekon kaa istemaal kiya gaya he. pehali dawa behatar pashu aushadhi thim jise kisaanon ne istemaal kiya. duusari dawapashu chikitsa club dwaara srijan kii gai thi jahaan kisaanon ne milakar ek pashu chikitsak ko naukari par rakh liya or puure saal bhar main jab bhi aavashyaktaa hoti ve uski sevaayen praapt karte the. yah doctor ke upar nirbhar tha ki vah pashuon ko swasth rakhe or kisaan kam se kam use bulaaen, na ki kisaanon ko jab bhi jarurat ho use bulaae or seva ke liye use niyamit rup se bhugtan kare. 
 adhiktar deyari kisaan purna niyamitata ke saath din main do baar doodh duhate or kuch ucch utpaadan aushadhi kaa istamaal kar dinabhar main chaar baar tak doodh duh lete taaki gaayon kii than main jamaa hue doodh kaa vajan halkaa ho jaae. yah dainik gaayon ko duhane kii dincharya prati varsh kareeb 300 se 320 dinon ke liye chalati he. kuch chote jhund ko utpaadan chakra ke antim 20 dinon ke liye din main ek baar duha jaataa he, lekin yah bade jhund ke liye saamaanya nahin he. yadi ek gaay ko nahin duha jaataa he turant uske doodh utpaadan kii kshamta kam hone lagati he or mausam ke baaki samay main uski utpaadan kshamta lagbhag suukh jaati he or bina kisi utpaadan vah khaati rahati he. haalanki, din main ek baar doodh duhane kaa abhyaas laabh or jivan style ke liye newjiland main vyapak rup se prachlit he. yah kargar sabit hua he kyonki doodh upaj main giravat se kam se kam aanshik rup se shram or laagat main bachat se santulan bana rahata he. isaki tulana sanyukt rajya america main gahan farm pranaaliyon se kii gai he jahaan shram laagat kam karne ke liye prati din teen baar yaa usase adhik prati gaay doodh duhane kaa kaam hota he.
 kisaan jinhone maanav ke khapat ke liye taral doodh kii aapurti kaa anubandh le rakhaa he aksar ye unke jhund kaa prabandhan karte hai taaki ye saal bhar doodh dete rahen yaa aavashyak nunatam doodh utpaadan bana rahe. aisa gaayon ke sambhog ke dwaara unke prakritik sambhog ke samay ke baahar kiya jaataa he taaki ve jab jhund main rahen ek gaay adhiktam utpaadan de or saal bhar ye parikraman chalata rahe. 
 uttari golarddh ke kisaan jo gaayon ko baade main rakhte hai lagbhag sabhi aamtaur par saal bhar gaay ke jhund kaa prabandhan is prakaar karte hai ki puure varsh ke dauraan nirantar utpaadan hota rahe or unhen aamadani hoti rahe. dakshini golarddh main sahakaari deyari pranali do maheene ke liye utpaadakta kii anumati nahin detii hai kyonki unki pranali main vasant ke maheene main koi utpaadan nahin hota or adhiktam ghaas kaa laabh uthaaya jaataa he kyonki doodh prasanskaran sanyantr shushk mausam main bonus detii he taaki kisaanon ko madhya-sheet tak bina ruke le jaaya ja sake. iskaa yah bhi matlab he ki gaay ko doodh ke utpaadan se aaraam mil jaataa he, jab ve sabase adhik garbhvatee hoti hai. kuch saalon tak deyari formon ko adhik utpaadan ke liye aarthik rup se dandit kiya jaataa rahaa he taaki saal ke kisi bhi samay main apane utpaada ko maujuuda keemton par bechney main asamarth ho jate hai. 
 sabhi ucch utpaadan vaale jhund main kritrim garbhaadhan aam he.
 deyari sanyantr kachche doodh ko kisaanon se praapt kar sansaadhan karti he or vipanan jivan kaa vistaar karti he. do mukhya prakaar kii prakriya karyrat hai: maanav upbhog ke liye hit treetment kar doodh kii suraksha sunishchit kii jaati he jisase doodh lambey samay tak surakshit rahe or nirjalit prakriya dwaara makkhan, hard chiz or doodh ke powder ke rup main deyari utpaadon ko sangrahit kiya ja sake.
 aaj, badi mashiinon dwaara thok maatra main doodh ko alag kar kriim or malai nikaala jaataa he. sansaadhan ke baad kriim se vibhinn prakaar ke upabhokta utpaada banaae jate hai, iske ghanatv ke aadhaar par yah vyanjan ke liye upyog kiya jaataa he or upabhokta kii maang bhinn-bhinn deshon main alag-alag hoti he. 
 kuch kriim or sookhe or powder ke rup main, kuch gadhe or chini ke saath mishrit hote hai jise dibbaband kar diya jaataa he. adhikansh newjiland or australian kaarkaanon main kriim se makkhan bana liya jaataa he. kriim ko manthane kii prakriya tab tak kii jaati he jab tak ekashm ghani vasa upar na aa jaae. is ghaney makkhan ko dhoyaa jaataa he, kabhi kabhi, ise surakshit rakhane ke liye isamen namak milaayaa jaataa he. avshisht chacha aage prasanskaran ke liye chalaa jaataa he. makkhan ko main pack kar diya jaataa he or bhandaaran or bikri ke liye thanda kiya jaataa he. baad main in bade pack ko gharelu khapat ke liye chote pack main baant diya jaataa he. 2007 main makkhan ko antarrashtriya bazaar main 3,200 dollar prati ton daam par bechaa gaya.
 kriim ko hataae jane ke baad jo utpaada bachataa he use schimd doodh kehete hai. avshisht schimd doodh ke saath jaman yaa acid milakar kesain dahi or schimd doodh ko phentkar mattha banaaya jaataa he. kriim ke kuch hisse ko schimd doodh main milakar kam vasa vaale doodh ko maanav khapat ke liye taiyaar kiya jaataa he. kai kismon se kriim kii raashi ko lautaa kar, utpaadak kam vasa vaale doodh apane sthaneey bazaar ke anuroop taiyaar kar sakate hai. anya utpaada, jaise calcium, vitamin di or flevar milakar upabhoktaaon ko aakarshith kiya jaataa he.
 kesain taaje doodh main paaya jane vaala prabal phosphoprotein he. is utpaada kaa vistrut rup se upyog kiya jaataa he maanav khaadya padaarth kaa ek poorak he iske alaava, icecream main, kapade, gond or plastic ke nirmaan main iskaa upyog kiya jaataa he. haalanki, sanyukt rajya america main in mishrit gaiyr khaadya ke upyog ke liye china jaise anya deshon se, ghatiya powder ke aayaat par chintaa jataate hai, jaise ki china main kesain yogaj ke bina kritrim rup se gharelu chiz kaa istemaal kiye jane vaale utpaada ko khaadya evam aushadhi prashasan ke nirikshan ke daur se guzarana padataa he. 
 chiz doodh se banane vaala doosraa utpaada he. purna doodh se dahi banaakar use jamaa kar, sansaadhit kar or sangrahit kar chiz banaaya jaataa he. aise deshon main jahaan doodh ko bina paischorigeshan ke sansaadhit karne kii kanoonee taur par anumati he vahaan swaabhaavik rup se doodh main bacteria paidaa kar kai prakaar ke chiz banaae jate hai. adhikansh anya deshon main, cheez ke prakaar kam hai or kritrim chiz kaa upyog adhik hota he. mattha bhi is sansaadhan kaa pratifal he.
 aitihaasik rup se doodh ko ek varsh se adhik "bhandar" karne kaa ek mahatvapurn tarika he or iske paushanik mulya samrudh saalon tak barkaraar rahete hai. purvi madhya or europea samskritiyon ke praagitihaas, kii tithiyon main waapas jaaen to roti or bear ke saath yahi ek khaadya utpaada tha jise pasand kiya jaataa tha evam jisaki asankhya kismein thim or jise sthaneey vishistataa praapt thi. haalanki gaay ke doodh se bani chiz kaa bazaar bahut bada he, uske baavjood anya doodh vishesh rup se bakrii or bhed ke doodh se bani chiz vyavsaayik rup se kaaphi maatra main paayi jaati hai . 
 pehle ke samay main mattha ko ek bekaar utpaada mana jaataa tha or yah zyaadaatar, niptaan ke suvidhajanak saadhan ke rup main siiron ko khilaayaa diya jaataa tha. 1950 ke shuruyaat se, 1980 ke adhikansh samay tak lactose or kai anya utpaadon main, mukhya rup se kesain or khaadya mattha kaa upyog hota aayaa he.

 aitihaasik rup se: ek deyari farm par duhane or prasanskaran kaa kaam ek hi saath hota he. baad main, mashin dwaara kriim ko doodh se alag kar, kriim ko makkhan banane ke liye kaarkaane bhej diya jaataa he. malai nikaala doodh suar ko khilaayaa jaataa tha. puraani trakon or sadakon kii kharaab gunvattta ke kaaran parivahan kii ucch laagat ke liye anumati di gai . keval vahi farm jo kaarkaanon ke kareeb the purna doodh kii keemat ko jhelane main samarth the jisaki aavashyaktaa chiz banane ke liye audyogic maatra main hoti thi. 1950 ke ant main, sadak parivahan ke vikaas ke saath, prashithan bhi behatar hua or zyaadaatar kisaanon ko gaay ke doodh ko keval asthaayee suvidhaon vaale prashitit thok tanks main rakhana hota tha, vahaan se ise trek dwaara prasanskaran kendra main le jaaya jaataa tha.
 jab haathon se duhana aksham yaa shram gahan ho gaya to gaayon se doodh duhane ke liye mashiinon kaa prayog kiya jane laga. doodh dene kii unit ek than se doodh nikaalane ke liye mashin kaa hi ek hissaa he. yah ek panja he, jo chaar chuchuk kaps, lambii doodh tub, lambii dhadkan tub or ek compymanak se bana he. panje ko ek lambii naadee tub ke saath or choti naadee tub ko chuchuk tub or lambey naadee doodh tub se joda jaataa he. panje saamanytah steneles steel yaa plastic yaa donon se bane hote hai. chuchuk kaps kii rachana ek kathor bahari kavach jiske andar kaa bhaag naram yaa phula hua hota he. khol main paardarshee virgon se doodh ke pravaah ko dekha ja saktaa he. khol or liner ke bich kundalakaar kavach ko naadee kaksh kaha jaataa he.
 doodh dene kii mashin ek prakaar se haath se duhane yaa bachade ke doodh pine ke tareeke se alag he. nirantar vaikum kar naram chuchee ke paar ek dabaav paidaa karke doodh ko nikaalane ke liye maalish kii kriya lagu rahati he. nirvaat gaay se mashin ko jode rakhane main madad bhi karata he. nirvaat ko chuchee par lagu karne se chuchee utakon par jamaav kaa kaaran banataa he. vayumandaleey hawa dhadkan kaksh main daalaa jaataa he ek baar prati second taaki liner chuchee ke ant ke aasapaas patan ke liye or chuchee utakon main jamaav ko raahat dene kii anumati mili. khulanaa or band ke samay ke anupaat ko dhadkan anupaat kaha jaataa he. 
 aamtaur par chuchuk cap dwaara chaar dhaaraon se doodh nikalakar panjon main sanyukt hote hai or doodh kii nalee se hokar baaltee main sangrah hota he uske baad doodh ko yaa hawa ke dabaav dwaara sanyojit kar yantric pump dwaara kendriya bhandaaran tub yaa thok tanks main pahunchaaya jaataa he. lagbhag sabhi deshon main farm par doodh ko thanda kiya jaataa he phir ise garm-excanger dwaar nikaalkar thok tanks main rakhaa jaataa he. 
 tasviir main upar gaay se thodi dur par ek steneles steel kii baaltee dikhaai de rahi he yah backet milking system he. do kathor steneles steel ke chuchuk cap saamne ke do thanon ke sahare latake hote hai. lacheela liner kavach ke baahar dikhaai detaa he jisamen chote doodh tub or chote spandan tubon ke gole ke panjon ke neeche tak faile hote hai. panje ke neeche kaa bhaag paardarshee he jahaan se doodh ke pravaah kaa avlokan kar sakate hai. jab duhane kaa kaam puraa ho jaataa he mashin kaa vaikum band ho jaataa he or chuchuk kaps hataa liye jate hai.
 doodh dene kii mashin doodh ko pruthak or bahya sankraman se surakshit rakhti he. mashin kaa aantarik 'doodh sampark' satahon ko minial yaa swachaalit washing prakriyaaon dwaara saaf rakhaa jaataa he. doodh sampark satahon ke saath vishesh niyamon kii aavashyak khaadya grad saamagree kaa paalan karnaa chahie jise aasaani se saaf kiya ja sake. 
 adhikansh duhane kii mashine bijli dwaara sanchalit hoti hai, lekin bijli na hone par, vahaan ek vaikalpik urja kii vyavastha bhi hoti he jisase aantarik ingine, vaikum or doodh pump chalata he. dudharu gaay duhane ke samay main aniyamitata nahin sah paati hai jabatak doodh utpaadan main koi gambhir katautee na ho.
 pattee-style kii sheden -khule mandu main doodh duhane ke baad, is prakaar se doodh dene kii suvidha kaa sabase pehle vikaas kiya gaya tha. ek lambii sankeern, patali, ek taraf se jhuki or khuli hui lambii shed kaa nirmaan kiya gaya. gaayon ke yard ke khuli taraf rakhaa jaataa or doodh duhane ke samay unhen pattee main khadaa kar diya jaataa tha. aamtaur par gaayon ko ek shrinkhalaa main niyantrit rakhane ke liye unke peeche ke pairon ko rassi se baandh diya jaataa tha. gaay apane sthaan se zyada hill nahin pate or doodh duhanevaale ko laat nahin maar sakate the jab doodh duhanevaale ek stool par baithkar ek baaltee main doodh nikaalte the. jab prayek gaay se doodh nikaalane kaa kaam samaapt ho jaataa to unhen waapas apni jagah par bhej diya jaataa tha. britain main is prakaar kaa shed rex patterson dwaara viksit kiya gaya jisamen chah mobile khadey hone ke shed the jisapar gaayon ko khadaa kar diya jaataa taaki charavaahon ko bahut neeche jhukna na pade. duhane kaa upkaran aaj ke jamaane kaa tha, ek pump se ek vaikum, spandan, pipe ke saath panja, kavach or liners ki uttejit or chuchee se doodh chuusate hai jisase pipe ke saath ek panja tukdaa se ek vaikum. doodh cooler ke madhyam se ek mathni main chalaa jaataa he. 
 jaise hi jhund ke aakaar main vruddhi hoti thi prayek pattee ke saamne ek darwaja sthapit kar diya jaataa tha taaki jaise hi gaay ko duhane kaam puraa ho, pairon main bandhi rassi ko kholkar dur se hi us daravaaje ko kholkar gaay ko baahar charne ke liye bhej diya jaataa tha. darwaja band kar diya jaataa tha, agali gaay pattee main surakshit ho jaati thi. jab doodh dene kii mashin kii shuruuaat kii gai to ek jode main gaayon se doodh nikaala jaataa tha tabatak anya ko doodh dene ke liye taiyaar kiya jaataa tha. pehle kaa samaapt hote hi mashin cap ko anya gaay ko laga diya jaataa tha. yah bilkul swingover doodh duhane kaa parlor ke samaan tha jiskaa varnan neeche kiya gaya he. jhund kii sankhya main vruddhi ke saath saath pattee kii sankhya main vruddhi ke bajaae aasaani se kapon kii sankhya ko doguna kar baari baari se gaay se doodh duh liya jaataa tha. ek shed main ghante bhar main 8 pattiyon ke saath ek vyakti dwaara 50 gaayon ko duh liya jaataa he, baar-baar gaayon ke liye ek hi chuchee cap kaa upyog karne se sankraman, stan kii sujan sanchaarit hone kaa khatra rahata he. kuch kisaanon ne gaayon ke samoohon ko keetanurahit karne ke apane upaay dhund liye hai. 
 herringbon milking parlor -herringbon milking shed yaa parlor main, gaay ekal tareeke se pravesh karti hai or unhen galiyare main lagbhag lambavatt khadaa kar doodh duhanevaale apana kaam karte hai than or chuchuk kii dhulaai ke baad or doodh dene kii mashin ke cap ko gaayon ko laga diya jaataa, unke pichley pairon ke peeche se, kaam karne ke kshetra ke donon kinaaron par. bade herringbon shed main do logon ke saath 600 gaayon se kushaltapurvak doodh nikaala ja saktaa he. 
 swingover milking parlor -swingover parlor bilkul herringbon parlor ke samaan he keval isamen gaayon kii do panktiyon ke bich doodh denevaale cap kaa keval ek hi set hota he, jise gaayon ke bich saajha kiya jaataa he, ek taraf se doodh nikaalane ke baad duusari taraf pratisthapit kar diya jaataa he. is pranali kaa laabh yah he ki yah kam mahangaa he, halaki isse 100 gaayon se adhik ke doodh ke liye koshish nahin kii ja sakati lekin yah aadhe gati se adhik behatar rup se sanchalit he. 
 rotary milking shed -rotary milking shed main ek ghoomane vaala chakra hota he jisamen bahari chor ke aasapaas gaayon ke liye lagbhag 12 se 100 alag stall hote hai. ek "achche" rotary ke saath ek saath 24-32 stallon ek yaa duhane waalon dwaara sanchalit kiya ja saktaa he. chakra ek vidyut motor dwaara sanchalit hota he or is gati se ghumta he ki ek baar main ek gaay se puri tarah se doodh nikal jaae. jaise hi ek stall khaali hota he pravesh dwaar se ek gaay kadam rakh detii he or chakra ke saath ghoomane lagati he. isprakaar agali gaay agle khaali stall ke taraf chali jaati he. oparator yaa doodh duhanevaale nipal saaf kar cap ko laga dete hai or kisi anya bhojan yaa jo bhi kaam jaruri ho use karte hai. jabatak doodh nikaala jaataa he gaayen manch par hi rahati hai. doodh duhanevaale yaa ek swachaalit upkaran, duhane kii mashin kaa cap hataa kar or gaay ke peeth ko pakad kar pravesh dwaar se thik pehle ek nikaas par chhod detaa he. rotary pranali ek hajaar gaayon se bhi adhik bade jhund kaa doodh duhane main sakasham he.
 swachaalit milking shed -swachaalit milking yaa 'robotic milking' shed australia, newjiland or kai europea deshon main dekha ja saktaa he vartmaan swachaalit milking shed svaichhik duhana paddati kaa upyog karte hai. isamen gaayon ko swechha se din ho yaa raat ke kisi bhi samay doodh dene ke liye khud ko prastut karte hai, haalanki dohraane kaa daura computer software ke madhyam se kisaan dwaara simit kiya ja saktaa he. ek robot bhujaa upkaran or nipal ko duhane ke liye istemaal kiya jane se pehle saaf karte hai, jabaki phatakon se gaay swachaalit tareeke se baahar chali jaati hai, is prakriya ke dauraan kisaan ko upasthit rahane jarurat nahin hoti. puri prakriya computer dwaara niyantrit hota he. yahaan swat: pranali kaa vivaran diya gaya he-
 shed main poorak upkaran -kisaanon ko jald hi ehsaas ho gaya ki ek milking shed gaayon ke liye ek acha poorak aahaar he ki sthaneey aahaar kii kami se ubhara ja saktaa he yaa 'gaay kii bhalaai or utpaadan ko joda ja saktaa he. prayek puaal ke box ko is prakaar rakhaa jaataa he ki gaay ke aate hi use khaanaa mil jaae taaki duhane ke samay main vah khaati rahe. ek computer prayek jaanavar ke liye sahi poorak rashan ko pard saktaa he. ek kareebi vaikalpik 'out-of-parlor-feader' ke stallon main, jo ki gaay ke gale main ek transponder he vah kramadeshit karata ki gaay kii umr avasthaa or utpaadan kshamta ke aadhaar par prayek gaay ke liye mukhya rashan pradaan kar laabh pahanchaata he. 
 shed ke pravesh dwaar par gaayon ko pakad kar shed main rakhane ke liye shed yard ek mahatvapurn saadhan he. ek sanchalit get sunishchit karata he ki kaise gaayon ko band rakhaa jaataa he. 
 deyari farm par pani bahut mahatvapurn he: gaayon dinabhar main 20 galen pani pi leti hai or baaki pani kii jarurat shed ko saaf or svachh rakhane ke liye hoti he. panpon or jalaashyon kii suvidha duhane ke sthaan main aam he. pani ko doodh ke saath garmi hastantaran dwaara garam kiya ja saktaa he.

 aise deshon main, jahaan gaay saal bhar baahar jaakar charati hai vahaan bahut kam apshisht niptaan karnaa padataa he. sabase adhik apshisht doodh duhana ke shed par hota he, jahaan pashu apshisht tarlikrut hota he or jo pravaah ke saath yaa thos pump ke saath mishrit talabon anerobics bacteriake saath daalaa jaataa he. sansaadhit pani or poshak tatwon ko pump dwaara waapas nikaalkar urvarak ke rup main sinchai ke kaam main laayaa jaataa he. adhishesh jaanwaron ko maance ke liye bali or ans utpaadon ke liye roopantarit kiya jaataa he. 
 sambaddhit doodh processing kaarkaanon main, aamtaur par dhulaai ke baad bachaa hua ganda pani hi apshisht hota he jise parishkrut karne ke baad jalmaarg main lautaa diya jaataa he. aadhi sadi se pehle kii tulana main yah bahut alag he jab mukhya utpaada makkhan, chiz or kesain hote the or baaki doodh ko apshisht ke rup main nipataara kiya jaataa tha. 
 aise kshetron main jahaan gaayon puure varsh bhar rakhe jate hai, apshisht kii samasya bahut jatil hoti he bachi hui khaadya saamagree he or beding apshisht ko bhi sangrahit kar nipataara karnaa padataa he. is samasya kaa aakaar khadey hone vaale baade main jahaan aise deyari par chalaai jaati he vahaan dekha ja saktaa he. 
 kai maamalon main, aadhunik formon main bahut badi maatra main prasanskaran ke liye doodh ko kaarkaane main bheja jaataa he. agar kuch bhi gadabadi hui yaa duhana, parivahan yaa prasanskaran suvidhaon ke saath kuch galat ho jaae to doodh kii baari maatra main niptaan karne kii koshish ek bahut badi aapadaa ho sakati he. agar sadak par tanker palat jaataa he to sadak par bachaav dal ko 20-45 hajaar galen doodh ko niptaana padataa he. ek patari se utari hui tanker train main isse 10 guna adhik maatra shaamil ho saktaa he. prashithan ke bina, doodh nasht ho jaataa he or ucch jaev rasinic oxigen kii maang ke kaaran yah vaataavaran ke liye bahut hanikaarak hota he. ek bade paimaane par bijli blassout hone par deyari udyog ke liye ek aapadaa aa jaati he kyonki duhana or prasanskaran donon suvidhaayen prabhavit hoti hai. is ke liye, farm aksar mobile janarator kaa upyog kar sakate hai. aisi sthiti kaa saamana cantarbari bukamp 2010 ke dauraan karnaa padaa. 
 deyari pradhan kshetron main, doodh kii ek badi maatra ke liye vibhinn tareekon ke niptaan kaa prastaav rakhaa gaya he. inamen kshetra pashuon ko doodh pilaanaa, spray sinchai or balidaan kshetra shaamil hai. bhoomi par doodh ko failaane se, yaa kisi chhed main ise daalane se bahut badi samasya kaa saamana karnaa padataa he kyonki doodh ke chacha bhoomi ke chhed ko band kar dete hai or pani mitti ke madhyam se jameen ke andar ja nahin paata he. iske prabhaav se ubarne main bahut samay lag saktaa he, kisi bhi bhoomi par aadhaarit anuprayog ko acchhee tarah se prabandhit kiya janaa chahie.
