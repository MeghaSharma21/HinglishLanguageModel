 anuvanshik rog ] kaa ek bada varg gina jaataa he. adhikansh, ekal jin dosha ke kaaran hote hai, jo anzime kii code main sahaayta karata he - taaki vibhinn padaarth ke roopantaran koi duusare main ho jaay.
 chayapachay kii janmajaat truti, ye shabd ek british chikitsak, archibalgarrod ne, 1908 main, pehali baar, 20ve sadi main, istimaal kiya tha. un kaa kaam "ek jin ek anzime"pariklpana kaa purvagami kaam mana jaataa he. vah alakapatanuria kii prakruti or viraasat par adhyayan kiya tha. un kaa moulik paath, chayapachay kii antarjaat truti 1923 main prakashit kiya gaya tha.
 paramparaagat rup se, vanshanugat se mila chayapachay rogon kii shreniyon is tarah he - carbohydrate chayapachay,amino acid chayapachay, carbonic acid chayapachay yaa lysosomal bhandaaran ke rog. haal ke dashakon main, chayapachay ke nae viraasat main mila vikaar ke saikadon kii khoj kii gai he or shreniyon atmpunarjanan karati he. nimnlikhit janmajaat chayapachay rogon ke pramukh virgon hai - prayek varg kii kuch pramukh udhaaharan ke saath hai. kai anya rog in shreniyon main nahin girte he. aisidi 10-code pradaan kii he, jahaan uplabdh hai .
 british colombia ke ek adhyayan main, sahaj chayapachay ke sahaj trutiyon ke kul ghatnaon, 100000 janm main anumaan 70 yaa 1400 main se anumaan 1 lagaaya gaya tha; yaa kul aabaadi main, jin hi vikaron kaa lagbhag 15% se adhik kaa pratinidhitiv.
 darjanon janmajaat chayapachay rogon ab navjaat screening pareekshan dwaara pakde ja sakate hai; vishesh rup main vistaarit pareekshan upyog ke samooh pratibimbmaapan ke istimaal se. yah - teji se - nidaan ke liye ek aam tarika ban rah he. or kabhi kabhi, is se jald ilaaj or behatar parinaam mil sakate hai. ek krantikari ji.si./em.es. aadhaarit - vishleshnatmak pranali sangh - takanic he; jis se ab, har navjaat main 100 se adhik chayapachay anuvanshik vikaron ke liye pareekshan jaanch kiya ja sakate hai.
 sthitiyon kii bahulata ke kaaran; screening ke liye, kai alag alag naidanik pareekshan istemaal kiye jate hai. koi asaamaanya parinaam ke baad, aksar ek "nishchit pareekshan" kiya jaataa he, jo sandigdh nidaan kii pushti karata he.
