 hindu rashtrawaad kaa samuhik rup se sandarbh, bhartiya upmahadweep kii deshaj aadhyatmik or saanskrithik paramparaon par aadhaarit, saamaajik or raajnneetik abhivyaktiyon se hai. kuch adhyetaon kaa yah vaad hai ki hindu rashtrawaad ek sarlikrut anuwaad he, or iskaa behatar varnan hindu rajya-vyavastha shabd se hota hai. 
 bhartiya mul samskruti ke prati jaagrukta or uskaa vichaar bhartiya itihaas main atyadhik praasangik ban gaya jab uski vajah se bhartiya raajnneeti ko ek vishisht pahchaan mili tathaa upniveshwaad ke viruddh prashn uthaane main aadhaar pradaan kiya. mul samskruti kii bhaavana ne british shashan ke viruddh swatantraaa andolanon ko prerit kiya jis main sashastr sangharsh, pratirodhi raajnneeti or gaiyr-hinsak virodh pradarshan shaamil the. isane bharat main saamaajik sudhaar andolanon or aarthik soch ko prabhavit kiya.
 1923 main hindu rashtravaadi vinayak damodar saavarkar dwaara lokapriy kii gai avadharana hindutv, bharat main hindu rashtrawaad kaa mukhya rup he. hindu rashtravaadi swayamsevak sanghathan rashtriya swayamsevak sangh dwaara hindutv kii himaayat kii jaati he, jise vyapak rup se sahabaddh sanghathan vishwa hindu parishad ke saath bhaajapa ke janak sanghathan ke rup main mana jaataa he.
