 misalen chote raajnneetik sikhkh kshetra the.
 mughal badshaah bahadushah kii 10 disambar 1710 ko prasaarit ek rajagna se bade paimaane par sikhon kaa utpeedan aarambh hua. farrukhsier ne bhi is aadesh ko doharaa diya. lahore ke governor abdussamad khan or uske putr tathaa uttaradhikari jakariya khan ne bhi sikhon ko peedit karne ke liye anek upaay kiye.
 atev sikhon ne apane ko do dalon main sangathit kiya- buddha dal or tarun dal. buddha dal kaa netritv kapur singh or tarundal kaa netritv deepasingh ke haathon main tha. ye donon dal jab tab apane chhipane ke sthanon se nikalakar sthaneey adhikariyon ko pareshaan karte the. inhone apni bikhri hui shakti ko sangathit kiya. tarun dal paanch jatthon main vibhajit kiya gaya jinke nimnlikhit netaa the-
  deepasingh shahid
  karamsingh or dharamsingh, amritsar
  khansingh or vinod singh, goindwal 
  dasaundhaa singh, kot buddha 
  biru singh or jeevansingh.
 jab afghanistan se ahmedshah durrani ke punjab par akraman hue to sikhon ko apane ko drudhtar aadhaar par sangathit karne kaa acha avasar mil gaya. unhone sarhind or lahore par adhikaar kar liya.
 1748 or 1765 ke bich buddha or tarun dalon ke paanchon jatthon ne drut gati se apana prasar kiya or anek rajyasang bane jo misalen kahalaai. nimnlikhit 12 misalen mukhya thim :
  bhangi- ise chazzasingh ne sthapit kiya, baad main bhannasingh or harisingh ne bhangi misal kaa netritv kiya. iske kendra amritsar, rawalpindi or multan aadi sthanon main the.
  ahaluwalia- jassasihan ahaluwalia ke netritv main sthapit hui. iskaa pradhan centre kapoorthala tha.
  ramgadhia- is samudaay ko nandasingh sanghania ne sthapit kiya. baad main iskaa netritv jassasingh ramgadhia ne kiya iske kshetra batala, dinanagar tathaa jalandhar doaab ke kuch gaanv the.
  nakai- lahore ke dakshin-pashchim main nakka ke harisingh dwaara sthapit.
  kanhaiya- kaanh kuccha ke jaisingh ke netritv main gathit is misalash ke kshetra gurdaspur, batala, dinanagar the. yah ramgadhia misal main mila jula tha.
  ullewalia- gulabsingh or tarasingh gaiva ke netritv main yah misal thi. rahon tathaa sutluj ke uttar-dakshin ke ilaake iske mukhya kshetra the.
  nishanwalia- iske mukhiya sangatsingh or moharsingh the. iske mukhya kshetra ambala tathaa sutluj ke dakshin or dakshin purv ke ilaake the.
  fazullapuria - nawab kapur singh dwaara sthapit, jalandhar or amritsar jile iske kshetra the.
  karodasinghia- 'panj gaai' ke karod singh dwaara sthapit. baad main baghelsinh iske mukhiya hue. kalsia ke nikat yamuna ke pashchim or hoshiyarpur jile main is misal ke kshetra the.
  shahid- deepasingh is misal ke agua the. baad main gurubakhshasingh ne uttaradhikaar grahan kiya. damdama sahab or talavandi sabo is misal ke mukhya kendra the.
  phulakiyaan- patiala, nabha or jind ke saradaaron ke purvaj phool ke naam par sthapit. ye sardaar iske teen guton ke mukhiya the.
  sukkarchakkia- chadhatsingh ne apane purvajon ke nivas gram sukkarchak ke naam par sthapit kiya. mahatv main chadhatsingh kaa sthaan nawab kapoorsingh or jassasingh ahaluwalia ke sthanon ke baad aata tha. uskaa mukhya kshetra gujaranvaala or aasapaas ke ilaake the. chadhatsingh ke putr mahasingh ne apane pita kaa uttaradhikaar sambhala or uske baad uske putr sherepunjab ranjitsingh ne.
 misalon kaa samvidhan bilkul saral tha. misal ke sardaar ke neeche patteedaar hote the jo apane anuyaayiyon ke bharan-poshan ke liye sardaar ke saath gaanvon or bhoomi kaa prabandh karte the. ghudsawaari or astrashastron ke prayog main dakshata saradaaron, patteedaron or unke sahaayakon kii mukhya yogtaayen maani jaati thim. misalon kaa rup ganatantrvaadi tha. jeet or lut kii saamagree kaa dasham bhaag sardaar ke liye niyat rahata tha. shesh usee anupaat main chote saradaaron or unke anuyaayiyon main baanta jaataa tha. ek sardaar se praapt gaanv or bhoomi chhodkar anya misal main sammilit hona sambhav tha. sardaar se bhoomi praapt karanevaale jagiradaaron ko jaagir kii suraksha ke liye ek nishchit sankhya main ghode or sipaahi uplabdh the. chote saradaaron yaa jagiradaaron kii misal viruddh gatividhiyon par unki sampatti jabt karne kaa adhikaar sardaar ko hota tha. saradaaron ke nijee naukar thavedaar kahe jate the or avagya yaa vidroh karne par unki bhoomi jabt ho sakati thi.
 sabhi misalon kaa samooh dal 'khalsa' kehalaata tha. ve guru ke naam par yuddh karte the or sarbatt khalsa ke naam par sandhiyaan karte the. misalon kii vyapak samasyaon par panth kii saadhaaran sabha dwaara vichaar kiya jaataa tha. yah amritsar main varsh bhar main do baar baisakhi or diwaali ke avasaron par baithti thi. guru granth sahab kii upasthiti main bahumat se prastaav paarit karke nirnay liya jaataa tha. nyaay bahut jaldi hota tha. kanoon or vyavastha kaayam rakhane kaa uttardaayitv chote saradaaron par tha or nyaay kii vyavastha panchayaton ke madhyam se hoti thi. panchayaton ke viruddh nirnay sunne kaa adhikaar sardaar ko tha or ant main, par praaia: bahut kam, panth yaa saadhaaran sabha main apil kii jaati thi. unke yahan mrityudand kaa vidhaan nahin tha. choriyon ke maamalon main padchihnanveshak jis gaanv main choron ke padchihnon ko khoj lete the, us gaanv ke mukhiya ko yaa to ve padchihn gaanv ke baahar kii ore jate hue dikhaane padate the yaa haani ke baraabar dravya dena padataa tha.
