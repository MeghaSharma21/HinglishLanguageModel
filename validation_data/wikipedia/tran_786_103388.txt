 nabarangpur bhartiya rajya oreisha kaa ek jila he. iskaa mukhyalay navrangpur hai. samudr tal se 2000 feet kii oonchai par sthit navrangpur purv main kalahandi, dakshin main koraput or pashchim va uttar disha main chhattisgarh se ghira hua he. 5135 varg kilometre main failaa yah jila 1992 main gathit hua tha. yahaan se bahane vaali indravati nadi navrangpur or koraput jile ko alag karti he. yah jila bharpoor khanijon ke saath-saath vanyajeevon ke liye bhi prasiddh he. penther, tendue, tigar, hena, bhens, kaala bhaaluu, bisan, sambar or barking dear jaise pashuon ko yahaan dekha ja saktaa he. yahaan ke pramukh darshneey sthalon main maa pendrani mandir, shahid minar, khatiguda baandh, maa bhandaragharaani mandir or bhagwaan jagannatha mandir shaamil hai. saath hi yahaan kaa rathyatra parv sailaaniyon ko khub lubhaata he.
