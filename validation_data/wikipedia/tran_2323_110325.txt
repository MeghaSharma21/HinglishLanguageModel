 mewar, rajasthan ke shishodiya rajvansh ke shaasha the.maharana fateh singh:--1911 i.me jorge pancham ke bharat aagaman par fatehsingh dilli darbar me shaamil hone ke liye dilli gaye parantu keshari singh barhat dwaara likhit chetavanee raa chugathiya padhakar dilli darbar me shaamil hue bagaiyr waapas mewar laut aae. 1888 me fatehsagar jhil kaa punahnirmaan fatehsingh ne karavaaya jise kenot band bhi kehete he.
