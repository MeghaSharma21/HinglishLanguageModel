 da 3 mystex of maai life chetan bhagat dwaara likhi gai teesri pustak he. ise may 2008 main prakashit kiya gaya tha or isaki 430,000 pratiyaan chhaapi gai thi. pustak main tin mitron kii kahani bataai gai he jon ahmedabad main rahete he.
 yah chetan bhagat dwaara likhi gai teesri sabase adhik bikne vaali kitaab he.
 kitaab purnataya kaalpanik ghatnaon par 2000-2005 main aadhaarit he jab ahmedabad main rahane vaala ek noujvaan govind bijanas ke sapane dekhne lagata he. apane mitra ishan or omi kii ichchhaaon ke anuroop ve cricket kii duukaan kholte he parantu teenon kaa makasad alag alag hota he. govind kaa lakshya paise kamanaa he, ishan kaa sapana ali ko sudhaarna he jon ek pratibhavan ballebaaz he or omi bas apane doston ke saath rahanaa chahta he. govind pustak kaa mukhya kirdaar va kahani bataane vaala paatr he or kahani uski tin galatiyon ke ird-gird ghumti he.
