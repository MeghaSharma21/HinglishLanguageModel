 dalia yaa daheliya suryamukhi kul, dwidali varg kaa paudha he. iskaa vitaranakendre mexico tathaa madhya america main he. yah bade aakaar kaa anek rangon or aakaaron main paaya jane vaala aisa aakarshak phool he jisamen nile rang ko chhodkar vibhinn rangon or roopakaron kii 50,000 se adhik prajatiyaan pai jaati hai. rangabirange phulon ke kaaran dalia ke paudhe baagon kii shebha badhaane ke liye lagaae jate hai. 'dahl' naamak swedish vrikshavisheshgy kii smriti main linius ne is paudhe kaa naam 'dalia' yaa 'dahalia' rakhaa. iske paudhe do-dhai meter tak unche hote hai, parantu ek bauni jati keval aadha meter unchi hoti he. dalia ke paudhon kii jadon main khaadya padaarth ekatra hokar unhen moti bana dete hai. inke phool suryamukhi ke sadrush hote hai. inaki nai kismon main gend ke samaan gol tathaa rang birenge phool jagate hai.
 dalia kii nai kismein beej se paidaa kii jaati hai. dalia kii jaden, jinamein choti choti kaliyaan hoti he, paudhon kaa rup lene kii kshamta rakhti hai. ye jaden chote chote tukdon main is prakaar kaati jaati hai ki har tukade main ek kali ho. in tukdon ko jameen main bone par, kaliyon se nae paudhe nikalate hai. kabhi kabhi iske tane kii kalam bhi katkar lagaai jaati he or usase bhi nae paudhe paidaa kiye jate hai. dalia ke paudhe adhiktar khuli jagah tathaa khadyukt, balui mitti main bhalee bhunti viksit hote hai. adhik sheet padane par iske phul mar jate he. aisi dasha main isaki jaden saaf karke duusare mausam main bone ke liye rakh lee jaati he iske paudhon par keede bhi lagate hai, jo sankhiya ke chhidkaav se maare jate hai.
 yah ushnkaribandheey shitoshna jalwaayu main ugaaya jaataa he. daheliya ke liye saamaanya varsha vaali thandi jalwaayu kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. shushk evam garm jalwaayu isaki safal kheti main baadhak maani gai he. duusari ore sardi or paale se fasal ko baari kshati pahunchati he. iske liye khuli dhoop vaali bhoomi uttam rahati he phool bade aakaar ke banate hai jo dekhne main atyant aakarshak hote he, jabaki chaaya main uge paudhon ke phool aakaar main chote or aakarshak lagate hai. 
 daheliya kii safal kheti ke liye bhoomi kaa chayan ek atyant mahatvapurn kaary he. daheliya ke sundar or bade aakaar ke phool lene ke liye uchit jal nikaas vaali retili domat bhoomi jiskaa ksharank 6 ho, sarvottam rahati he. adhik chikani or kam jal nikaas vaali bhoomi isaki safal kheti main baadhak maani gai he, kyonki aisi bhoomi main paudhon kaa samuchit vikaas evam badhwaar nahin ho paati he. jiske parinamswarup vaanchit aakaar-prakaar va rang-rup vaale phool nahin mil pate hai yadi kisi kaaran chikani mitti main isaki kheti kerani pade, to us sthiti main paryaapt maatra main gobar kii khaad, chaaval kaa chhilka or raakh milakar bhoomi kii sanrachana main sudhaar kiya ja saktaa he. pratham jutai tractor yaa mitti palatney vaale hal se karne ke uparant kuch dinon ke liye khuli dhoop main chhod di jaati he. uske baad khet main paryaapt maatra main gobar kii khaad yaa pattee kii khaad daalkar bhalee-bhaanti mila kar suvidhanusaar pani kii naaliyaan or kyaariyaan banaai jaati hai.
 daheliya kii pramukh prajatiyon main bhari hui pankhuriyon vaali prajatiyon ke liye- diana grigori, haal mark, little villo, little merion, rohanda, dablyoo je0en0, willian janson, gordon laac wood, gilin place, villo sarprise, martills yalo, lismore peggi tathaa penecord merian vishesh rup se jane jate hai. chote aakaar vaali prajatiyon main alpan marajori, downlaham royal, red admirial neti, vikater di, white neti, brukside dieridre, si miss, koinonia lokapriy hai. golakaar prajatiyon main canora fire bal, el en crese, deep south, voten kupid, peter nelson, krichaton hani, senior bal, vista, raskin jeepsi, snoho tampi, eltemir chairi, fiona steewart shreshth samajhe jate hai or bhartiya prajatiyon main swami vidyanand, jyotsna, lard buddha, bhikkus madar, swami lokeshvaranand, da bi0pi0 paal, brodar sampilicius shreshth hai.
 ropai:- daheliya kii adhik paidaavaar lene ke liye use sadaiv panktiyon main ugaaya jaataa he. bade aakaar or aakarshak phool lene ke liye unhen uchit duuri par ropate hue do paudhon ke madhya kii duuri sabhi prakaar kii kismon yatha badi, madhyam yaa choti prajati ke liye samaan rakhi jaati he jise 70 semi0 upyukt paaya gaya he. do panktiyon ke madhya kii duuri 50 semi0 upyukt samajhi jaati he. ekaantar ropan sarvottam paaya gaya he kyonki aisa karne se prayek paudhe ko paryaapt sthaan mil jaataa he. jiske kaaran use paryaapt maatra main poshak tatva or nami mil jate hai. jisase paudhe kaa vikaas evam badhwaar bhalee-bhaanti ho jaati he. antat: paudhon se bade aakaar ke aakarshak phool milte he. ropai khurpi kii sahaayta se kii jaati he. paudhe ko ropane ke uparant chaaron ore se bhalee-bhaanti dabaane ke baad fuwware se halkaa pani dena upyukt rahata he. yadi adhik kshetra main ropai kii gayi he to halki sinchai kii jaati he.
 parvatiy kshetron main ankurit kandon ko sidhaa bo diya jaataa kehane kaa abhipraay yah he ki pehle unhe gamalon main ropakar ankurit nahin kiya jaataa jaisaa ki maidani kshetron main kiya jaataa he. is vidhi main ankurit kandon ko nirdhaarit duuri par is prakaar ropa jaataa he ki kand bhoomi tal se lagbhag 8 semi0 neeche rahen pratham saptaah main pani nahin diya jaae or yadi aavashyaktaa ho to ek halki sinchai kii jaae kabhi bhi puraa kand nahin ropana chahie balki use vibhajit karke use gamalon main ropakar naye paudhe bana lene chahie.
 sahaara dena:- daheliya ke paudhon ko jitani jaldi ho sake, sahaara dena chahie. anyathaa unke girane kaa bhay bana rahata he, iske paudhe tej hawa, tej varsha yaa khet main adhik pani khana rahane ke kaaran gir jate he paudhon ko sahaara dene ke liye baans-khapche yaa koi anya kathor lakadiyon kii chhadon kaa upyog kiya ja saktaa he. paudhe ke saath baans kii khaptch ko gankar use ek sthaan par sutali se is prakaar baandhe ki vah arabi bhashaa kii aath sankhya jaisaa dikhaai de.
 ropane kaa uchit samay:- daheliya ke ropan ko do bhaagon main vibhakt kiya ja saktaa he yatha parvatiy kshetron main or maidani kshetron main ropai kaa samay ek sthaan se duusare sthaan par bhinn hota he. srinagar, shillong, darjaling, utakmand, pithauragarh, ke liye daheliya ke paudhon kii ropai kaa upyukt samay april or may he or bangalore ke liye may or june upyukt samay he. kolkata main paudh ropan kaa upyukt samay sitambar se navambar tak he. sitambar se disambar tak kaa samay dilli, lakhanu, ranchi, bhubaneshwar, devdhar, nagpur, guwahati ke liye upyukt he mumbai ke liye oktober or navambar or chennai ke liye oktober sarvottam samay he. 
 kharpatvaar-niyantran:- daheliya kii kyaariyon main anek kharpatvaar ug aate he, jo bhoomi main poshak tattvon nami, dhoop, sthaan or hawa ke liye pratispardhaa karte he iske atirikt keedon or rogon ko bhi aashray pradaan karte hai. jiske kaaran uttam gunvattta vaale phool nahin mil pate. at: aavashyaktaanusaar niraai-gunai kerani chahie. daheliya uthali jang vaali fasal he. at: gahari niraai-gunai nahin kerani chahie aamtaur par 10 din ke antar se niraai-gudayi kerani chahie. 
 keet-niyantran:- daheliya ke paudhon ko affid, thrips vishesh rup se kshati pahunchaate he, ye donon paudhe ke vibhinn bhaagon kaa ras chuusate hai. inaki rokthaam samay par kerani chahie.rokthaam- rogaar kaa chhidkaav karnaa chahie. 
 rog-niyantran:- churni fafundi- yah rog ek prakaar kii fafundi ke kaaran hota he. jiske kaaran pattiyon kii upree sataha par safed yaa bhure rang kaa churna dikhaai panta he yah rog shuru kii avasthaa main adhik lagata he or dubaara jab phool aane band ho jate he, yah rog lagata he is rog ke kaaran bijon ke sire gal jate hai. is rog ko lagate hi isaki rokthaam kerani chahie.
 rokthaam:- bajustin, morestn yaa benlete 2 gram prati litre pani kii dar se gholkar chhidkaav karen. inamen kisi ek dawa kaa upyog karnaa hoga yadi in dawaaiyon main se koi uplabdh na ho to us sthiti main ghulansheel gandhak kaa upyog karen.charcol galan- yah rog garm or varsha vaale mausam main daheliya ko adhik kshati pahunchaata he. yah rog mukhya tane or sabhi shakhaon ke aadhaar par lagata he. yadi samay par uski rokthaam na kii jaay to paudha mar jaataa hai.rokthaam- capton prati saptaah bhoomi par chhidakna chahie. 
 kataai:- phulon kii kataai kis avasthaa main or kis samay kii jaye. yah atyant mahatvapurn vishay he jisaki ore aamtaur par kam dhyaan diya jaataa he phulon kii kataai uske ugaaye jane ke uddeshy par nirbhar karti he. yadi aap phulon kii kataai ghat pradarshnee ke liye kar rahe hai to unki kataai subah ko kerani chahie. jahaan tak sambhav ho utani lambaai main phool kaatna chahie kataai 45 degree ke kone par kerani chahie or kaate gayi tanon ko turant pani se bhari baaltee main rakhni chahie. phulon vaali baaltee ko thande or svachh sthaan par rakhana chahie, taaki phulon par mitti dhul na lag sake phulon se gamalaa banaate samay aavashyaktaanusaar tanon ko chota karne kii sthiti main unki kataai kerani chahie.
