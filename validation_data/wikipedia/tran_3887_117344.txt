 
 sadiq khansaansad
 
 sadiq aman khan britain ke mukhya vipakshi lebar party ke netaa or landan ke meyar hai. ve landan ke pehle muslim meyar hi nahin balki european union kii kisi bhi rajdhani ke pehle muslim meyar hai. unhone apane kareebi pratidwandvi conservative ummidavaar jaik goldsmith ko haraaya. unhone bataur maanav adhikaar vakil apane kariyar kii shuruyaat kii. iske baad ve saansad bane. 2005 se ve lagaataar tooting sate lebar party ke saansad hai. 2009-10 main ve gordon brown government main ve parivahan mantri bhi rah chuke hai. ve britain ke aise pehle muslim mantri hai, jo cabinet kii baithkon main shaamil hue hai. unke pita britain kii rajdhani landan main ek bas driver hai. ve pakistani mul ke british naagarik hai.
