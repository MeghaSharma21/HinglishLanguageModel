 
 2005 isisi trophy 1 july se 13 july ke bich ireland main yojit cricket toornament tha. yah antarrashtriya cricket parishad ke 12 associat sadasyon ke bich paksh ke anusaar ek antarrashtriya ekadivasiy toornament khelaa se adhik 50 over tha. yah 2007 ke cricket vishwa cap main jagah banane ke puraskaar ke saath paanch sheersh sthaan par raheen tiimon ke liye aayaa tha, or 1 janvari se aadhikarik ek divasiy antarrashtriya sthiti ke puraskaar ke saath 2006 main kenya ke saath-saath paanch sheersh kram tiimon, jo pehle se hi he jab tak 2009 trophy or 2007 vishwa cap main ek sthaan adhikari ek divasiy darja diya gaya tha ke liye .
 7 july ko, sheersh 4 tiimon scotland, canada or pehali baar ireland or bermuda 2007 cricket vishwa cap ke liye kwalifai or, 1 janvari 2006 ke liye aadhikarik ek divasiy antarrashtriya darja praapt kii. 11 july ko netherlands bhi paanchaven khatm karne ke liye sanyukt arab emirate se haraakar is hasil kii. scotland toornament jeeta, final main 47 ran se ireland kii dhadkan.
 dach ballebaaj baas zuiderent toornament ke sarvshereshth khilaadi ke rup main namit kiya gaya tha.

 1 - 9 agast, 2005 isisi nishkasit sanyukt rajya america, jo unhen dekha tha 2007 divijan van main pratispardhaa nahin he.
