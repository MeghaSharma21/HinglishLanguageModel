 echdiefasi bank bharat kaa ek pramukh bank he. mumbai, bharat main isaki sthapana agast 1994 main kii gayi thi. echdiefasi bank ne janvari 1995 main ek anusuchit vanijyik bank ke rup main kaary shuru kiya. mobile banking bhi hindi main uplabdh he. iske shakhaon kii adhiktam sankhya mumbai or nai dilli main hai.
 26 farvari 2000 ko bhartiya reserve bank ne echdiefasi bank tathaa times samooh/benet colman and kan0 dwaara sanchalit times bank ke vilay ko sweekruti de di. vilay yojana ke anusaar times bank ke shairdharakon ko 5.75 sheyaron ke badale echdiefasi bank kaa ek share mila. nai peedi ke kinhin nijee bainko main hone vaala kaa yah pehla vilay tha.
 23 may 2008 ko bhartiya reserve bank dwaara echdiefasi bank tathaa centurian bank of punjab ke vilay ko manzuri de di. niveshkon ko sibiopi ke 29 sheyaron ke badale echdiefasi bank kaa ek share mila.
 disambar 2013 tak is bank kii 2,104 shaharon main kul 3,336 shakhaayen tathaa 11,473 atm karyrat thim.
 echdiefasi bank ne 6 disambar 2013 ko 709 sthanon par 1,115 raktadan camp lagaae gaye jinamein 61,902 logon dwaara raktadan kiya gaya jo ki gineez book of world records main shaamil kiya gaya.
