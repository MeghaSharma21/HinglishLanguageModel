 deenabandhu chotu ram vigyaan or praudyogiki vishvavidhyalay, purv chotu ram rajkeey college of ejineering, murathal, murathal, sonipat, haryana, bharat main sthit ek rajya vishvavidhyalay he. yah 2006 main haryana sarkaar dwaara ek adhinium dwaara unnayan ke dwaara, 1986 main sthapit ek mahavidhyalay ke unnayan se sthapit kiya gaya tha. 
 yah 1986 main haryana sarkaar dwaara sar chotu ram kii smriti main sthapit kiya gaya tha. tab yah chotu ram rajkeey abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay, murathal ke rup main janaa jaataa tha. haryana sarkaar ne 6 june 2006 ko haryana rajya ke vidhaan-mandal ke ek adhinium ke 29, 2006 ke madhyam se iskaa ek vishvavidhyalay main unnatan kar diya.
 yah vishvavidhyalay dhara 12 vishvavidhyalay anudaan ayog adhinium, 1956 ke tahat march 2009 main anudaan ke liye paatr mana gaya he.
 vishvavidhyalay rashtriya rajmarg sankhya 44 par ai.es.bi.tii., dilli se 50 kimi tathaa sonipat railway stesan se 8 kimi ki duri par sthit he. vishvavidhyalay kaa vishaal parisar kundali se panipat ke bich kii audyogic or vanijyik belt main 273 acre main viksit kiya gaya he. 
 vishvavidhyalay main nimnlikhit vibhaag hai:
 vishvavidhyalay parisar main kul 7 chatravaas hai, jiname se 3 ladakiyon ke liye or baaki 4 ladakon ke liye he. ladakiyon ke chatravaas main 450 ki kshamta he, jabaki ladakon ke chatravaas main 966 ko samyojit kar sakate hai.
 vishisht kshetron main anusandhan ko badhaava dene ke liye vishvavidhyalay ne nimnlikhit videshi sansthaanon ke saath samjhauta gyaapan kiye hai:
 vishvavidhyalay rashtramandal vishvavidhyalayon kii association kaa ek sadsy he.
