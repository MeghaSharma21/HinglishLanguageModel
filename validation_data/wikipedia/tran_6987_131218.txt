 jisat-5pi yaa jisat-5prime ek prayogtmak sanchaar upgrah tha. ise bhusthir upgrah prakshepan yaan mark 1 epa06 kii udaan main bheja gaya tha. lekin bhusthir upgrah prakshepan yaan mark 1 ke booster kii vifaltaa ke kaaran yah kaksha tak nahin panhucha. udaan bharne ke kuch samay baad booster vifal ho gaye or raaket nirdhaarit path se bhatak gaya. isko dekhte hue udaan ke 63 second baad seemaa suraksha adhikari ne raaket ko bengal kii khadi main nasht karne kaa aadesh diya or upgrah raaket ke saath nasht ho gaya.
