 spirogira ek shaiwal he. isame chlorophyl paaya jaataa he atah yah prakaash sanshleshan kii kriya dwaara apana bhojan sway banaata he. yah paudha ek silk ke dhaage kii tarah hare rang kaa dikhaai detaa he jo jal kii upari sataha par tairta rahata he. duniya main is paudhe kii 400 se adhik jatiyaan hai. ise pansilk bhi kaha jaataa he kyonki yah talab main aksar dekha jaataa he. yah bahut teji se viksit hota he atah kuch hi samay main puure talab main is tarah fail jaataa he ki talab kaa pani haraa dikhaai padataa he.
