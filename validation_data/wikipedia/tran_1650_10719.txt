 trikshetreey siddhant ek aarthik-siddhant he jo arthvyavasthaaon ko teen bhaagon main baantta he: praathmik, dwitiya or tritiy . iske anusaar in teenon kshetron kii alag-alag aavashyaktaayen or vikaas-kram hote hai or yah teen kisi desh, rajya yaa samaaj ke aarthik vikaas ke alag-alag str darshaate hai. is siddhant kii vyakhya alen fisher, colin clark or sion furastye naamak teen arthshaatriyon ne kii thi.
 praathmik kshetra main maanav keval un chizon kaa sidhaa upyog karata he jo prakruti use uplabdh karaati he, jaise ki krishi ke liye bhoomi, zamin se khodakar nikaale jane vaale khanij, wanon main maans va anya prayogon ke liye praani, nadiyon-samudron se machhliyaan, ityadi. praathmik kshetra ke kaaryon main bhaag lene ke liye adhik shiksha kii aavashyaktaa nahin hoti lekin anya kshetron kii tulana main jitna shram lagata he uske anusaar kam mulya kaa utpaadan hota he. is kshetra ke kaaryon main aksar maanav ko shaareerik shram karnaa hota he. yah bhi dekha gaya he ki jo arthvyavasthaayen praathmik-pradhan hoti hai unamem saamaajik staran bhi adhik hota he, yani samaaj kii kuch shreniyan adhik shaktishaalee hokar raaj karti hai or adhiktar logon ke kaam kaa kuch bhaag le leti hai.
 dwitiya kshetra main mashiinon dwaara bade paimaane par maanav upyog kii vastuen banaai jaati hai, jis se kam shram se adhik utpaadan ho paata he. dwitiya-pradhan samaajon main ausat aay badhati he. dwitiya kshetra ke kaaryon main shiksha or nipunata mahatvapurn hoti he, jis se saamaaj main shiksha failati he or jagriti badhati he. mashinikaran se praathmik kshetra ke kaary bhi mashiinon dwaara kiye jate hai or adhiktar log mashiinon main kaam karte hai. audyogikaran main samaaj praathmik-pradhan se dwitiya-pradhan banate hai.
 isamen maanav ek-duusare ko sevaayen pradaan karte hai, jinamein vittiya sevaayen, kala sevaayen, shiksha sevaayen, soochna sevaayen, gyaan sevaayen, manoranjan sevaayen, ityadi shaamil hai. tritiy-praghan samaajon main praathmik or dwitiya kshetra ke adhiktar kaam mashiinon dwaara kiye jate he. shiksha str bahut uth jaataa he.
 furastye ke anusaar jaise-jaise samaaj teen charnon se viksit hokar guzarataa he, us samaaj main in teen bhinn kshetron main jute hui jansankhya kaa pratishat badalata he.
