 sewai yaa santhakai dakshin bharat main bahut prasiddh hai, khaskar tamilnadu or karnataka main, ye ek prakaar ke chaaval se bane noodle hote hai. anya anaajon kii bani hui santhakai kaa prachaalan bhi dhire-dhire badhata ja rahaa he, jaise genhu, raagi aadi.
 sewai/santhakai apni hi style ke ek anya khaadya padaarth idiappam se vishesh rup se alag he, yah antar inke banane main prayog kii jane vaali samagriyon, banane kii vidhi or utsaaran ke baad inke dwaara banaaye gaye pakwaanon ke falswaroop he. sewai subah ke nashet/ raat ke bhojan ke rup main prachlit he or yah mana jaataa he ki yah saraltapurvak pachya hoti he kyonki yah bina tel ke yaa bahut hi kam tel main banaayi ja sakati he or ise bhaap dwaara bhi pakaaya ja saktaa he.
 sewai/santhakai aamtaur par chaaval ke kanon se shuru karke taji hi banaayi jaati he. yah sukhi hui sewai ke packet main aane vaali sewai se bhi banaayi jaati he, jaise ki asian grosri store main uplabdh instent sewai. paaramparik rup se, ghar par sewai bananaa nimna charnon main puraa kiya ja saktaa he :
 ghar par banaayi gayi sewai praayah 100 pratishat chaaval dwaara hi banaayi jaati he jabaki sukhi rice sticks main praayah kuch anya padaarth bhi mili hote hai jaise saboodana, makke kaa aataa aadi.
 instent rice noodles main padane vaale anya padaarthon main wheat gluton guar gam, khaadya starch aadi aate hai.
 tamilnadu ke pashchimi bhaagon main sewai ko santhakai kehete hai. kannada main ise 'shavige' kehete hai. saadhaaran bolchaal main log sewai ke liye idiappam or semia shabdon kaa istemaal bhi karte hai, haalanki ye khaadya padaarth kai myanon main sewai se bhinn hote hai. tamil bhashaa main inhen is prakaar likhaa jaataa he, சந்தகை, சந்தவம், சந்தவை.
 sewai ko praayah nibu, imali, tamaato, nariyal, dahi aadi ke dwaara swaadisht banaaya jaataa he or garm khaane karne par iskaa swaad sabase acha lagata he. saadi sewai ke saath liye jane vaali chizon main meetha kiya gaya nariyal kaa doodh, bananaa sugar or ghi sabase zyada prachlit hai, kuch anya padaarthon main kuzhambu, anekon subjiyan se bana korma or matan/chikan/ machhlee kaa korma aadi hai.
