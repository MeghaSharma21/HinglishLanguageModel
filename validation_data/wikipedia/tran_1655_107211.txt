 faraat nadi dakshinpaschim asia kii sabase lambi or aitihaasik rup se sabase mahatvapurn nadiyon main se ek he. dajala nadi ke saath milakar yah mesopotamia kii do paribhashapurna nadiyon main se ek he. yah nadi turki main toras pahaado par armbh hokar - syria or iraq main pravisht hoti he or shaat al-arab main dajala nadi se milti he or faras kii khadi main girti he.
