 bhashavigyaan main lahaza bolchaal main uccharan ke us tareeqe ko kehete hai jiskaa kisi vyakti, sthaan, samudaay yaa desh se vishesh sambandh ho. udahaaran ke taur par kuch dakshin-purvi hindi kshetra ke gramin sthanon main log 'sha' kii jagah par 'sa' bolte hai, jisaki vajah se vah 'shahar' or 'ashok' kii jagah 'sahar' or 'asok' bolten hai - ise us kshetra kaa dehati lahaza kaha ja saktaa he. vyaktigat str par tutalane ko bhi ek bolne kaa lahaza kaha ja saktaa he.
