 meghna nadi bangladesh kii ek mukhya nadi he. yah ganga nadi ke muhane par banane vaali teen mukhya sahaayak nadiyon main se ek he. in nadiyon se vishwa kaa sabase bada delta, sundarbans banataa he, jo bengal kii khadi main khulata he. meghna main padma nadi chandpur jila main milti he or antat ye bholaa jila main bengal kii khadi main mil jaati he.
 puri tarah bangladesh kii simaaon ke bheetar simit nadiyon main meghna nadi sabase chaude paat vaali he. bholaa ke nikat ek bindu par yah 12 ki.me chaudee he. apane antim padaavon main nadi lagbhag sidhi rekha main bahati he. apane bahut hi shant evam saumy drishy ke baavjood bhi yah nadi prayek varsh bahut sae jaan va maal kii haani kaa kaaran banati he. dheron pheri seva kii naaven isamen duubati hai, jo sainkadon kii mrityu kaa kaaran hoti hai. khaskar yah chandpur jila ke paas bahut hi khatarnaak he. 
 nadi kii ausat gaharaai he 1012 feet or adhiktam gaharaai he 1620 feet .
 yah nadi bharat ke pashchimi bengal rajya ke deltai bhaag main eschuary banaati hui bengal kii khadi main girti he. ganga evam brahmaputra nadi kaa adhikansh jal yah nadi samudr tak pahunchaati he. nadi apane saath badi maatra main mitti laakar bichaati he. nadi kabhi kabhi paanch, yaa chah jal dhaaraon main bant jaati he. kabhi yah vishaal kshetra main chadar ke samaan falkar bahati he. iske muhane main teen mukhya dweep hai. isamen saal bhaar naaven tathaa steamer saralata se chalaae ja sakate hai, lekin kinare balua hone se dhans jate hai, jo naavon ke liye haniprad he. monsoon ke samay main yah khatra or bhi bad jaataa he.
