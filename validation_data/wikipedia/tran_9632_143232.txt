 tripura sundari mandir agartala-sabaroom maarg par udaipur shahar se teen kilometre kii duuri par sthit he.
 is mandir kaa nirmaan maharaja dhanya maaniky ke shasankaal main 1501 i. ke dauraan karavaaya gaya tha. yah mandir bharat ke 51 mahapithon main se ek he. 
 puranic katha ke anusaar, is sthaan par mata sati ke sidhe pair ke anguliyon ke nishaan aaj bhi maujuud he. yah mandir rajya ke pramukh payrton sthalon main se ek he. hajaaron kii sankhya main bhakt pratidin mandir main mata ke darshanon ke liye aate hai.
 diwali ke dauraan mata tripura sundari mandir main bhavy str par diwaali meley kaa aayojan kiya jaataa he. jisamen prayek varsh lakhon kii sankhya log is meley main sammilit hote hai. rajmala ke anusaar, mandir kaa nirmaan karne ke pashchaat mandir main bhagwaan vishnu kii murti sthapit kii gai thi. lekin ek raat maharaja dhanya maaniky ke sapane main maha maaya ai or usase kaha ki vah unki murti ko chittong se is sthaan par rakh den. iske baad mata tripura sundari kii sthapana is mandir main kar di gai.
