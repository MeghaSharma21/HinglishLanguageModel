 kisi bhi swatantra raashtra ke nirmaan main moulik adhikaar tathaa niti nirdesh mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaate hai. rajya ke niti nirdeshak tatv janatantric sanvaidhanik vikaas ke naveenatam tatv hai. sarvapratham ye ireland ke samvidhan me lagu kiye gayi the. ye ve tatv he jo samvidhan ke vikaas ke saath hi viksit hue he. in tatwon kaa kaary ek janakalyankari rajya kii sthapana karnaa he.
 bhartiya samvidhan k keched 36 se 51 tak rajya ke niti nirdeshak tatv shaamil kiye gayi hai. bhartiya samvidhan ke bhaag 3 tathaa 4 milakar samvidhan kii aatma tathaa chetana kehalaate he in tatwon main samvidhan tathaa saamaajik nyaay ke darshan kaa vaastavik tatv nihit hai. nideshak tatv karypalika or vidhayika ke ve tatv hai, jinke anusaar inhe apane adhikaron kaa prayog karnaa hota he. 
 rajya ke niti nirdeshak tattvon kaa uddeshy samuhik rup se bharat main aarthik evam saamaajik loktantr kii rachana karnaa tathaa kalyankari rajya kii sthapana karnaa hai. 
 anu 37 ke anusaar ye tatv kisi nyaayalay me lagu nahi karawaaye ja sakate yah tatv vaidhanik na hokar rajnatik swaroop rakhte he tathaa maatr naitik adhikaar rakhte he. ve na to koi vaidhanik baadhyataa hi rajya pe lagu karte he na janata hetu adhikaar kartavya. ve maatr rajya ke liye aise saamaanya nirdesh he ki rajya kuch aise kaary kare jo rajya kii janata ke liye laabhadaayak ho. in nirdeshon kaa paalan karypalika kii niti tathaa vidhayika kii vidhiyaan se ho saktaa he.
 champar duraai rajan vaad 1951 me sarvocch nyaayalay ne yah nirnay diya tha ki ye tatv anivaary rup se moulik adhikaron ke adhinsth he un par prabhutaa nahi rakh sakate donon me vivaad paidaa hone par moulik adhikaar variyata paayenge, kintu jab rajya vidhayika ne jameendaari unmoolan pranrabh kiya taaki anu 39 ba 39 sa ke nideshak tatwa ko prabhavi kar sake tab sarvocch nyaayalay ne in tatwa kaa mahtv samajha tathaa kameshwar singh banaam bihar vaad 1952 se rikeral shiksha bill vaad 1957 tak supreme court ne mana ki niti nirdeshak tatv moulik adhikaar pe prashray nahi paa sakate, parantu baad me nyaayalay ne hormoni siddhant pratipaadit kiya tathaa sahyogaatmak vikaas kaa prayaas kiya tathaa dono ko utana prabhavi karne kaa prayaas kiya jitna sabhanv tha. hormoni siddhant dono ke madhya koi sangharsh nahi maan kar dono ko ek duusare kaa poorak maanata he. is siddhant ke anusaar yadi do vyakhyane sambhav he jo ki niti nirdeshak tatv ko moulik adhikaar ke saath santulan sthapit kare vahi duusari vyakhya uname vivaad bataaye to nyaayalay ko duusari vyakhya par pratham vyakhya kii variyata deni chahie yadi maatr ek vyakhya sambhav ho or jo niti - adhikaro main sangharsh paidaa kare to nyaayalay baadhya he ki niti nideshak tatwa par adhikaro ko variyata 'da' privi purse unmoolan tathaa bankig rashtriyakaran adhinium jo ki anu 39 'ba' tathaa 'sa' kii paalna hetu laaye gayi the ko sarvocch nyaayalay ne moulik adhikaro kaa ullaghann karne ke kaaran asanvaidhanik ghoshit kar diy sansad ne iske pratyuttar me 25 vaa sanshodhan 1971 paarit kar diya tathaa anu 31 sa ko janm diya jo ki kahata he ki yadi koi vidhi paarit kii jaati jo ki 39 ba sa ke niti nirdeshak tatwa ko prabhavi karti he tathaa is prakriya me yadi anu 14, 19, 31 ke adhikaro kaa ullaghann kare to use is aadhaar par asanvaidhanik ghoshit nahin kiya ja saktaa
 anu 31 sa ke anusaar koi vidhi jo anu 39 ba sa ko prabhavi karti he kisi bhi nyaayalay me parakshit nahi kii ja sakegi. keshavanand bharti vaad me is sanshodhan ko chunauti di gayi. sarvocch nyaayalay ne anu 31 sa ke pratham bhaag ko vaid maan liya kintu duusare bhaag ko is aadhaar par khaarij kar diya kyonki vah nyaaypalika kii punarikshan shakti chin leti he jo ki samvidhan ke mul dhaanchen kaa ang he. is vaad ke nirnay se privi purse tathaa bankig rashtriyakaran adhinium vaid ho gayi. 42 vaa samvidhan sanshodhan adhinium 1976 31 sa ko sanshodhit karata he isamen kaha gaya he ki sanshodhit 31 sa ke anuroop yadi rajya koi aisi vidhi banaaye jo ki kisi bhi niti nideshak tatv ko prabhavi kare is aadhaar par asanvaidhanik ghoshit nahin kar sakate ki vah anu 14,19,31 kaa ullaghan karte isi tarah vah vidhi nyaayalay me chunauti praapt nahi kar sakati. is sanshodhan ko minerva mil banaam bharat sangh vaad 1980 me chunauti di gayi jisame supreme court ne anu 31 sa me kiye gayi naye sanshodhan ko gaiyr sanvaidhanik ghoshit kar diya kyoki yah samvidhan ke bhaag 3 tathaa 4 ke santulan ko bigaad detaa tha. 44 ve sanshodhan 1978 ne anu 31 kaa hi vilopan kar diya. vartmaan sthiti yah he ki do nideshan tatv 39 ba, sa do moulik adhikaro 14, 19 pe variyata dekar lagu kiye ja sakate he.
 samvidhan sabha main bhaag 4 ke mahatv pe prashn kiya gaya tathaa kaha gaya ki yah bhaag hi vyarth he kyonki ye tatv koi vaidhaniktaa nahi rakhte he. vastav me ye ve pareekshan gubaare he jo ki satta main vidyaman sarkaar kii kshamta kaa pareekshan karte hai. kuch vidhiyon kii sanvaidhaniktaa kaa pareekshan niti nideshak tatwon ko dhyaan me rakhkar kiya ja sakegaa. inkaa shaikshanik mahatv he.
