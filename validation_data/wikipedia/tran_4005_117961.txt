 aimaq​ heraat nagar se uttar main pashchim-madhya afghanistan main or iran ke koraasaan praant​ main vistrut kuch irani bhashaayen bolne vaale kanaabadosh qabeelon kaa samuhik naam he. yah farsi kii kai aimaq​ upbhashaayen bolte hai, haalanki inke taimaani or maaleki upasamudaayon ne pashto bhashaa or pashtun samskruti apana lee he. afghanistan ke gor praant main aimaq bahusankhyak samudaay samajhe jate hai. iske alaava yah badi sankhya main heraat or baadbais praanton main or kam sankhya main faraah, faaryaab, johjan or sar-e-pole praanton main bhi rahete hai.
 aimaq log jaateey rup se hazaaraa logon or tajik logon se gaharaa sambandh rakhte hai or afghanistan kii sarkari janaganana main unhen tajik samudaay kaa hissaa gina jaataa he. 'aimaq' mongol bhashaa ke aimag shabd kaa ek or rup he, jiskaa arth 'qabeela' yaa ' charaane kaa kshetra' hota he. 'aimaq' shabd main 'qa' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'k' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'qareeb' or 'qeemat' ke 'qa' se miltaa he. aimaaqon kii chaar mukhya shakhaayen maani jaati he, jis karanvash inhen 'chahaar aimaq' bhi kaha jaataa tha . yah chaar mukhya gut taimaani, firozkohi, temuri or jamshidi hai. inke atirikt kuch 'anya aimaq' gut bhi hai jinhen 'aimaq-e-digar' kaha jaataa he, masalan tahiri, zuri, maaleki or mishmast. kuch strot hazaaraa logon ko chahaar aimaq kii ek mukhya shakha bataate hai or temuri ko aimaq-e-digar main se ek kehete hai.
 aimaaqon kii kul sankhya 2.5 lakh se 20 lakh ke bich anumaanit kii jaati he. isamen itnaa andeh isliye he kyonki ek to afghanistan unhen tajikon se alag nahin ginata or doosraa inamen se bahut se banjaaron kaa jivan vyateet karte hue jagah-se-jagah jate hai. temuri aimaq kaa rup-rang mongolian hota he or ve mongol style ke yarton main rahete hai. lekin taimaani, firozkohi or jamshidi aimaq iraniyon kii tarah dikhte hai or aksar apane aap ko tajik bulaate hai. haalanki aimaq hazaaraa logon se sambandhit hai, aimaq adhiktar sunni islam ke anuyaayi hote hai jabaki hazaaraa zyaadaatar shiya hai.
