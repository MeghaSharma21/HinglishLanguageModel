 samaajwaad ek aarthik-saamaajik darshan he. samaajawaadee vyavastha main dhan-sampatti kaa swaamitv or vitaran samaaj ke niyantran ke adheen rahete hai. aarthik, saamaajik or vaicharik pratyay ke taur par samaajwaad nijee sampatti par aadhaarit adhikaron kaa virodh karata he. uski ek buniyadi pratigya yah bhi he ki smapda kaa utpaadan or vitaran samaaj yaa rajya ke haathon main hona chahie. raajnneeti ke aadhunik arthon main samaajwaad ko poonjeewaad yaa mukt bazaar ke siddhant ke vipareet dekha jaataa he. ek raajnneetik vichaardhaara ke rup main samaajwaad europe main atharahavin or unnisvin sadi main ubhare udyogikaran kii anyonyakriya main viksit hua he. 
 british raajnneetik vigyaani harold loski ne kabhi samaajwaad ko ek aisi topi kaha tha jise koi bhi apane anusaar pahan letaa he. samaajwaad kii vibhinn kismein loski ke is chithran ko kaaphi seemaa tak roopayit karti he. samaajwaad kii ek kism vighatit ho chuke soviet sangh ke sarvasattavadi niyantran main charitaarth hoti he jisamen maanviya jivan ke har sambhav pahalu ko rajya ke niyantran main laane kaa aagrah kiya gaya tha. uski duusari kism rajya ko arthvyavastha ke niyaman dwaara kalyankari bhoomika nibhaane kaa mantra detii he. bharat main samaajwaad kii ek alag kism ke sutrikaran kii koshish kii gayi he. raamamanohar lohia, jai prakaash narayan or narendra dev ke raajnneetik chintan or vyavhaar se nikalne vaale pratyay ko 'gandhivaadi samaajwaad' kii sangya di jaati he.
 samaajwaad angreji or francisi shabd 'socialism' kaa hindi roopaantar he. 19veen shatabdi ke purvardh main is shabd kaa prayog vyaktvaad ke virodh main or un vichaaron ke samarthan main kiya jaataa tha jinka lakshya samaaj ke aarthik or naitik aadhaar ko badalanaa tha or jo jivan main vyaktigat niyantran kii jagah saamaajik niyantran sthapit karnaa chaahate the.
 samaajwaad shabd kaa prayog anek or kabhi kabhi paraspar virdohi prasangon main kiya jaataa he; jaise samoohwaad araajaktaavaad, adikalin kabyli samyawad, sainya samyawad, isai samaajwaad, sahakaaritavaad, aadi - yahan tak ki natsi dal kaa bhi puraa naam 'rashtriya samaajawaadee dal' tha. 
 samaajwaad kii paribhaasha karnaa kathin he. yah siddhant tathaa andolan, donon hi he or yah vibhinn aitihaasik or sthaneey paristhitiyon main vibhinn rup dhaaran karata he. mulat: yah vah andolan he jo utpaadan ke mukhya sadhano ke samajikaran par aadhaarit vargvihin samaaj sthapit karne ke liye prayatnsheel he or jo majdoor varg ko iskaa mukhya aadhaar banaata he, kyonki vah is varg ko shoshit varg maanata he jiskaa aitihaasik kaary vargavyavastha kaa ant karnaa he.
 adikalin samyavaadee samaaj main manushya parsparik sahyog dwaara aavashyak chizon kii prapti or prayek sadsy ke aavashyaktaanusaar unka aapas main bantwaaraa karte the. parantu yah samyawad prakritik tha; manushya kii sachet kalpana par aadhaarit nahin tha. aarambh ke isai padariyon kii rahan-sahan kaa dhang bahut kuch samyavaadee tha, ve ek saath or samaan rup se rahete the, parantu unki aay kaa strot dharmavalambiyon kaa daan tha or unka adarsh jansadharan ke liye nahin, varan keval padariyon tak simit tha. unka uddeshy bhi aadhyatmik tha, bhautik nahin. yahi baat madhyakaalin isai samyawad ke sambandh main bhi sahi he. piru desh kii pracheen inkaa sabhyataa ko 'sainya samyawad' kii sangya di jaati he, parantu uskaa aadhaar sainya sanghathan tha or vah vyavastha shaasha varg kaa hitsaadhan karti thi. nagarpalikaon dwaara loksevaon ke sadhano ko praapt karnaa, athwa desh kii unnati ke liye aarthik yojanaon ke prayog maatr ko samaajwaad nahin kaha ja saktaa, kyonki yah aavashyak nahin ki inke dwaara poonjeewaad ko thes pahunche. natsi dal ne bainkon kaa rashtrikaran kiya tha parantu poonjeewaadi vyavastha akshunn rahi.
 samaajwaad kaa bhaavnaatmak dhaanchaa gadhane main england main satrahavin sadi ke dauraan isiyat ke daayre main viksit levelors tathaa diggars va solahavin or satrahavin sadi main madhya europe main viksit hone vaale ainabaptist jaise radical andolanon kii mahati bhoomika rahi he. lekin samaajwaad kii aadhunik or aupchaarik pariklpana francisi vichaarkon sain-simon or charles furiye tathaa british chintak rabart oven kii nishpattiyon se nikalati he. samaajwaad ke ye shuruaati vichaarak vyaktvaad or pratisparddha kii jagah aapasi sahyog par aadhaarit samaaj kii kalpana karte the. unhen vishwaas tha ki maanviya swabhaav or samaaj kaa vigyaan garh kar saamaajik taane-bane ko behatar rup diya ja saktaa he. parantu vaanchit saamaajik roopon ke thos byoron, unhen praapt karne kii ranneeti tathaa maanav prakruti kii samaj ko lekar unke bich kai tarah ke matbhed the. udhaaharan ke liye, sain-simon tathaa furiye ruso ke is mat se sahamat nahin the ki manushya kii prakruti apni banaavat main to acchhee, udaatt or vivekpurna he lekin aadhunik samaaj or nijee sampatti ne use bhrasht kar diya he. iske virodh main unka tark yah tha ki maanviya prakruti ke kuch sthir or nishchit rup hote hai jinka paraspar sahyog ke aadhaar par aapas main mail karaayaa ja saktaa he. oven kaa mat sain-simon or furiye se bhi alag tha. unka kahana tha ki manushya kii prakruti bahari paristhitiyon se tay hoti he or use icchit rup diya ja saktaa he. isliye samaaj kii paristhitiyon ko is tarah badla janaa chahie ki manushya kii prakruti purnataa kii ore bad sake. unke anusaar agar pratisparddha or vyaktvaad kii jagah aapasi sahyog or ekta ko badhaava diya jayega to samoochee manushyata kaa bhala kiya ja saktaa he. 
 samaajwaad kii raajnneetik vichaardhaara unnisvin sadi ke tisare or chauthe dashakon ke dauraan england, frans tathaa germany jaise europee deshon main lokapriy hone lagi thi. udyogikaran or shaharikaran kii tej gati tathaa parmparik samaaj ke avsaan ne europee samaaj ko sudhaar or badlaav kii shaktiyon kaa akhaada bana diya tha jisamen majdoor sanghon or charteravaadi samoohon se lekar aise gut sakriy the jo aadhunik samaaj kii jagah prak-aadhunik saamudayikthawaad kii vakaalat kar rahe the. 
 marks or angels dwaara pratipaadit vaigyanik samaajwaad kaa vichaar saamaajik or raajnneetik uthalputhal kii isi prishthbhumi main viksit hua tha. marks ne sain-simon, furiye or oven ke vichaaron se prerana to lee, lekin apane ‘vaigyanik’ samaajwaad ke mukaable unke samaajwaad ko ‘kaalpanik’ ghoshit kar diya. in purvavarti chintakon kii tarah marks samaajwaad ko koi aisa adarsh nahin maanate ki uskaa spasht kaakaa khinchaa jaae. marks or angels samaajwaad ko kisi swayan-bhu siddhant ke bajaay poonjeewaad kii karypranali se utpann hone vaali sthiti ke rup main dekhte hai. unka maananaa tha ki samaajwaad kaa koi bhi rup aitihaasik prakriya se hi ubharegaa. is samaj ke chalate marks or angels ko samaajwaad kii vistrut vyakhya karne yaa use paribhaashit karne se bhi gurez tha. unke liye samaajwaad mukhyatah poonjeewaad ke nakaar main khadaa pratyay tha jise ek lambi krantikari prakriya ke zariye apni pahchaan khud gadhani thi. samaajwaad ke vishay main marks kii sabase mahatvapurn rachana 'critic of da gotha program' he jisamen unhone samaajwaad ko samyavaadee samaaj ke do charnon kii madhyavarti avasthaa ke taur par vyakhyayit kiya he. 
 gauratlab he ki marks kii is rachana kaa prakaashan unki mrityu ke aath saal baad hua tha. tab tak marxvaadi siddhanton main ise zyaada mahatva nahin diya jaataa tha. is vimarsh ko marxvad ke mul siddhanton main shaamil karne kaa shrey lenin ko jaataa he, jinhone apni kriti 'state and revolution' main marks ke hawaale se samaajwaad ko samyavaadee samaaj kii rachana main pehla yaa nimnatar charan bataaya. lenin ke baad samaajwaad marxvaadi shabdaawalee main is tarah vinyast ho gaya ki koi bhi vyakti yaa dal kisi khaas vaicharik dikkat ke bina khud ko samaajawaadee yaa samyavaadee kah saktaa tha. is vimarsh kii vibhaajak rekha is baat se tay hoti thi ki kisi dal yaa vyakti ke liye krantikari gatividhiyon kaa taatkaalik or doorgaami lakshya kya he. yani agar koi khud ko samaajawaadee kahata tha to iskaa matlab yah hota tha ki vah samyavaadee samaaj kii rachana ke pehle charan par jor detaa he. yahi vajah he ki kai samaajawaadee deshon main shashan karne vaale dal jab khud ko communist ghoshit karte the to ise asangat nahin mana jaataa tha. 
 beesvin shatabdi main samaajwaad kaa antarrashtriya prasar tathaa soviet shashan vyavastha ek tarah se sahavarti parighatnaayen maani jaati hai. yah ek aisa tathy he jisane samaajwaad ke vichaar or uske bhavishya ko gaharaai se prabhavit kiya he. udaahranarth, kranti ke baad soviet sangh uske samarthakon or aalochakon, donon ke liye samaajwaad kaa paryaay ban gaya. soviet samarthakon kii daleel yah thi ki utpaadan ke pramukh sadhano ke samajikaran, bazaar ko kendrikrut niyojan ke maatahat karne, tathaa videsh vyapaar va gharelu vitt par rajya ke niyantran jaise upaay apnaane se soviet sangh bahut kam avadhi main ek audyogic desh ban gaya. jabaki uske aalochakon kaa kahana tha ki yah ek prachaarit chhavi thi kyonki viraat naukarshahi, raajnneetik daman, asamanta tathaa loktantr kii andekhi swayan hi samaajwaad ke adarsh ko khaarij karne ke liye kaaphi thi. samaajwaad ke prasar main soviet sangh kii duusari bhoomika ek sanghathankarta kii thi. samaajawaadee kranti ke prasar ke liye communist international jaise sanghathan kii sthapana karke usne khud ko samaajwaad kaa haraval siddh kiya. is sanghathan ne vishwa kii communist partiyon kaa lambe samay tak disha-nirdesan kiya. soviet sangh kii bhoomika kaa teesraa pahalu yah tha ki usne purvi europe main kai hamshakl shashan vyavasthaayen kaayam kii. antat soviet sangh samaajwaad kii prayogshaala isliye bhi mana gaya kyonki roosi kranti ke baad stallin ke netritv main yah siddhant prachaarit kiya gaya ki samaajawaadee kranti kaa anya deshon main prasar karne se pehle use ek hi desh main majboot kiya janaa chahie. kai vidwaanon kii drushti main yah ek aisa sutrikaran tha jisane rashtriya samaajwaad kii kai kismon ke ubhaar ko vaidhata dilaayi. 
 dwitiya vishwayuddh ke baad shuru hui vi-upniveshikaran kii prakriya ke dauraan samaajwaad or rashtrawaad kaa yah sanshray teesri duniya ke deshon main samaajwaad ke vikaas kaa ek praaroop sa ban gaya. china, vietnam tathaa cuba jaise deshon main samaajwaad ke prasar kii yah ek kendriya pravriti thi. 
 soviet samarthit samaajwaad ke alaava uskaa ek anya rup bhi he jisane poonjeewaadi deshon ko apratyaksh dhang se prabhavit kiya he. masalan, samaajwaad ke tark or uske aakarshan ko prati-santulit karne ke liye pashchim ke poonjeewaadi deshon ko apni arthvyavastha ke saanche ko badal kar use kalyankari rup dena padaa. is sandarbh main scandenevian deshon, pashchimi europe tathaa austrailesia kshetra ke deshon main kines ke lokopkaaree vichaaron se prerit hokar soviet tarz ke samaajwaad kaa vikalp gadhane kaa prayaas kiya gaya. maang ke prabandhan, aarthik rashtrawaad, rojgaar kii guarantee, tathaa saamaajik sevaaon ke kshetra ko munafkhori kii pravritiyon se mukt rakhane kii niti par tike in kalyankari upayon ne ekbargi poonjeewaad or samaajwaad ke antar ko hi dhundla kar diya tha. rajya ke is kalyankari model ko ek samay poonjeewaad kii visangatiyon— bekaari, berojgaare, abhaav, agyaan aadi kaa sthai ilaaj bataaya ja rahaa tha. is model kii aarthik or raajnneetik kamyaabi kaa subut is baat ko mana ja saktaa he ki agar vampanthi daayaron main in upayon kii prashansa kii gayi to dakshinpanthi raajnneeti bhi unka khulaa virodh nahin kar saki. duusare vishwa-yuddh kii samaapti ke baad teen dashakon tak bazaar kendrit samaajwaad kaa yah model kaaphi prabhaavshaalee dhang se kaam karata rahaa. 
 lekin saatven dashak main mandi or mudrasphiti kii dohari maar tathaa kalyankari poonjeewaad ke garh main badhate saamaajik or audyogic asantosh ke kaaran is model ke auchity ko lekar sawaal khadey hone lage. marxvaadi shivi ke vidwaan bhi lagaataar yah kehete aa rahe the ki kalyankari bhangimaaon ne asamanta or shoshan ko khatm karne ke bajaay poonjeewaad ko hi majboot kiya he. is model kaa ek aapattijanak pahalu yah bhi prakat hua ki kaamgaar varg ko jivan kii buniyadi suvidhaayen pradaan karne vaali mashinari ke dwaara rajya us par niyantran karne kii sthiti main aa gaya. 
 bazaar ko kaayam rakhte hue samaajwaad ke kuch tattvon par amal karne vaale is model kaa nav-udaarataavaadi buddhijivi shuru se hi virodh karte aaye the. saatven dashak main yah varg jor-shor se kehane laga tha ki samaajwaad na keval arthvyavastha ko jad bana detaa he balki vyakti kii swatantraaa bhi chin letaa he. nawein dashak main jab soviet sangh ke saath purvi europe kii samaajawaadee vyavasthaayen ek ke baad ek dhahne lagi to samaajwaad kii is aalochana ko vyapak vaidhata milane lagi or samaajwaad ke saath itihaas ke ant kii bhi baaten kii jane lagi. vijay ke unmaad main udaaravaadi buddhijiviyon ne yah bhi kaha ki manushyata ke liye poonjeewaad hi ekmaatr vikalp he lihaaja ab uske vikalp kii baat bhuulakar keval yah sochaa janaa chahie ki poonjeewaad kii koi or shakl kya ho sakati he.

 britain main fabian sosayadi kii sthapana san 1883-84 i. main hui. rowart owain tathaa chartist andolan ke prabhaav se yahan swatantra majdoor andolan kii neenv pad chuki thi, fabian socity ne is andolan ko darshan diya. is sabha kaa naam fabius kanctator fabian ke naam se liya gaya he. fabius pracheen rome kaa ek senaanee tha jisane kaarthenge ke prasiddh senanayake hannibal ke viruddh sangharsh main dhairya se kaam liya or gurila niti dwaara usko kai varshon main parrast kiya. isi prakaar fabian samaajwaadiyon kaa vichaar he ki poonjeewaad ko keval ek muthbhed main krantikari maarg dwaara parrast nahin kiya ja saktaa. iske liye paryaapt kaal tak soch-vichaar or taiyaari kii aavashyaktaa he. inkaa tarika vikaas or sudhaarwadi he. swatantra majdoor dal kii sthapana ke purv ye britain ke vibhinn raajnneetik dalon main pravesh kar apana uddeshy puraa karnaa chaahate the. inkaa mukhya dhyey charam naitik sambhaavnaaon ke anusaar samaaj kaa punarnirmaan tha. ye rajya ko vargshasan kaa yantra na maanakar ek saamaajik yantra maanate hai jiske dwaara samaajakalyaan or samaajwaad kii sthapana sambhav he. in vichaarkon ne na keval sansad varan nagarpalika or gramin kshetreey parishadon dwaara bhi samaajawaadee prayogon kaa kaarykram apanaaya. at: inke vichaaron ko loktantreey, sansadeey, bailat baks, chungi, vikaas athwa sudhaarwadi samaajwaad kii sangya di jaati he. in vichaarkon main pramukh sidney web, jaaj barnad shaw, kol, eni besent, graham walas ityadi hai. in vichaarkon par british parampara, upyogetavaad, rabart owain, isai samaajwaad or chartist andolan tathaa jon stuart mil ke arthshaastriyon ke vichaaron kaa gaharaa prabhaav he.
 germany kaa punraritivaad britain ke fabiaswad tathaa germany kii parivartit paristhatiyon se prabhavit hua tha. germany or purv europea samaaj kaa swaroop samantvaadi tathaa rajya kaa prajatantric or nirankush tha, at: 19veen shatabdi ke uttarardh tak yahan ke samaajawaadee vichaar ugr krantikari tathaa sanghathan shadyantrkaari the. in deshon par marks ke vichaaron kaa prabhaav tha. parantu 19veen shatabdi ke ant main germany main bhi audyogic unnati hui or rajya ne kuch vyaktigat tathaa raajnneetik adhikaar sweekaar kiye. falat: mazdooron kaa jeevanaster unchaa hua tathaa unke raajnneetik dal - saamaajik loktantrvaadi party kaa prabhaav, bhi badha. uske anek sadsy sansad ke sadsy ban gaye. is sthiti main yah bal siddhantat: marks ke krantikari maarg ko sweekaar karte hue bhi vyavhaar main sudhaarwadi ho gaya. aduard bernstin ne is vaastaviktaa ke aadhaar par marxvad ke sanshodhan kaa praytn kiya. bernstin saamaajik lotantravaadi party kaa pramukh darshanik or angills kaa nikat shishy tha. vah britain main kai varsh tak nirvasit rahaa or vahaan fabiaswad se prabhavit hua.
 marks kaa kathan tha ki paraspar pratiyogita or aarthik sankaton ke kaaran poonjeewaadi tathaa madhyavarg sankuchit hota jayega or majdoor varg nirdhan, vistrut, sangathit tathaa krantikari banataa jayega jisase sheeghra hi samaajwaad kii sthapana sambhav ho sakegi. sthiti iske vipareet thi, jisko bernstin ne sweekaar kiya or is aadhaar par usne krantikari kaarykram ke sthaan main taatkaalik samajasudhar or samaajwaad kii safalataa ke liye vargsangharsh ke sthaan main shrenee-sahyog tathaa sansadatmak or sanvaidhanik maarg par jor diya. vah marks ke aitihaasik bhautikwaad ke sthaan par naitik tathaa anaarthik tatwon ke prabhaav ko bhi sweekaar karne laga. bernstin ke vichaaron ko punraritivaad ko naam diya gaya. yadhyapi german majdoor andolan vyavhaar main sudhaarwadi rahaa tathaapi karl kautski ke netritv main usne bernstin ke sanshodhano kaa asweekaar karke marks ke vichaaron main vishwaas prakat kiya.
 fabiuswadi or punraritivaadee vichaarak samaajwaad kii sthapana ke liye rajya ko aavashyak samajhate hai. samyavaadee vichaarak bhi sankraman kaal ke liye adam kii shakti kaa prayog karnaa chaahate hai. at: inako samoohwaadee kaha jaataa he. araajaktaavaadi vichaarak bhi poonjeewaad virdohi or samaajwaad ke samarthak hai parantu ve rajya, raajnneeti or dharm ko shoshanvyavastha kaa samarthak maanate hai or aarambh se hi inkaa ant kar dena chaahate hai. araajaktaavaad jivan or aacharan kaa ek siddhant he jo shashanviheen samaaj kii kalpana karata he. yah samaaj ke aikya kii sthapana shashan or kanoon dwaara nahin, varan vyakti tathaa sthaneey or vyavsaayik samoohon ke swatantra samjhauton dwaara karnaa chahta he. is vichaar ke anusaar uparyukt samoohon dwaara utpaadan, vitaran aadi kii anek maanav aavashyaktaayen puri ho sakati hai.
 araajaktaa shabd ke francisi roopaantar kaa prayog pehali baar francisi kranti ke samay un krantikariyon ke liye kiya gaya tha jo saamanton kii jameen ko jabt karke kisaanon main baantna or dhanikon kii aay ko simit karnaa chaahate the. tatpashchaat san 1840 main francisi vichaarak prudhon ne apni pustak ""sampatti kya he?"" main is shabd kaa prayog kiya. san 1871 ke baad jab antarrashtriya majdoor sangh main foot padi tab marks ke sanghavaadi virodhiyon ko araajaktaavaadi kaha gaya. aae din kii bhashaa main aatankwaad or araajaktaavaad paryaayavachi shabd hai; parantu vastut: darshanik araajaktaavaadi keval rajkeey daman ke viruddh hi aatank or krantikari upayon ke paksh main hai.
 sansar kaa pratham araajaktaavaadi vichaarak chini darshanik lao tse mana jaataa he. pracheen yunaan ke vichaarak aristippas or jino ke darshan main bhi in vichaaron kaa put he. britain kaa godwin or francisi prudhon rajya or shashansansthaaon-nyaayalay aadi kaa virodh karte the. prudhon ke anusaar sampatti chori kaa maal he. vah shram ke aadhaar par panya vinimay or lenden main ek pratishat byaaj kii dar ke paksh main tha. 
 is sambandh main rus ke teen araajaktaavaadiyon ke vichaar mahatvapurn hai. bakunin krantikari araajaktaavaadi tha, pinras cropotkin vaigyanik araajaktaavaadi tathaa leo talsty isai araajaktaavaadi. bakunin rajya ko ek aavashyak durgun or pichhadepan kaa chinh tathaa sampatti or shoshan kaa poshak maanata tha. rajya vyakti kii swadhinata, uski pratibha or krayshakti, uske vivek or naitikta ko simit karata he. is prakaar araajaktaavaad vyaktvaad kii charam seemaa he. bakunin krantikari maarg dwaara rajya or uski sansthaaon police, jail, nyaayalay aadi kaa ant kar swatantra sthaneey sansthaaon kii sthapana ke paksh main tha. ye samudaay parsparik sahyog ke liye apana rashtriya sangh sthapit kar sakate the. ruso or kaant bhi isi prakaar ke swatantra samudaayon or sanghon ke samarthak the.
 cropotkin ne vaigyanik adhyayan dwaara yah siddh kiya ki samaaj kaa vikaas swatantra sahyog kii ore he. shilpic unnati ke kaaran manushya bahut kam shram dwaara apni aavashyaktaaon kii purti kar sakegaa or shesh samay swatantra jivan vyateet karegaa. manushya swabhaavat: saamaajik, at: sahyogi praani he. swatantraaa or sahyog kii vruddhi ke saath saath rajya kii aavashyaktaa kam ho jaaegi.
 talsty bhi rajya or vyaktigat sampatti kaa virdohi tha, parantu vah hinsaatmak tathaa krantikari maarg kaa poshak nahin varan isai or anhisatmak tareekon kaa samarthak tha. vah buddhisangat isai tha, andhvishwasi nahin. gandhiji ke vichaaron par talsty kii gahari chhap he.
 araajaktaavaadiyon kaa vichaar he ki manushya swabhaav se acha he or yadi uske upar rajya kaa niyantran na rahe to vah samaaj main shantipurvak rah saktaa he. rajya ke rahete hue manushya kaa bauddhik, naitik or ragaatmak vikaas sambhav nahin. inke anusaar rajkeey samaajwaad naukarshahivaad or rajkeey poonjeewaad he. ye yuddh or sanyavad ke virdohi or vikendrikaran ke paksh main hai.
 araajaktaavaad se buddhijivi or majdoor, donon hi prabhavit hue hai. anek lekhak or darshanikon ne swadhinata sambandhi vichaaron ko sweekaar kiya he. inamen jon stuart mil, harbart spencer, herold laski or batrend rasal ke naam mukhya hai. is vichaardhaara ke buddhijivi samarthak france, spain, italy, rus, germany, sanyukt rajya america aadi anek deshon main paae jate the, parantu france or britain ke majdoor andolanon ne bhi in vichaaron ko sanshodhit rup main sweekaar kiya. iske francisi swaroop kaa naam sindikawaad or british kaa gild samaajwaad he.
 sindikawaad or gild samaajwaad kaa janm unnisvin shatabdi ke ant or beesvin ke aarambh main hua. us samay tak fabius or punraritivaad main mazdooron kaa vishwaas kam hone laga tha. loktantr mazdooron kii samasyaayen sulajhane main asafal rahaa, aarthik sankat vikat rup dhaaran karne laga or yuddh kii sambhaavna badhane lagi. saath hi mazdooron kii sankhya main vruddhi hui, unka sanghathan majboot hua or ve apni maangon ko puraa karaane ke liye bade paimaane par hadtaal karne lage. in paristhitiyon main sansadatmak or sanvaidhanik tareekon ke sthaan main majdoor varg ko sakriy virodh ke siddhanton kii aavashyaktaa hui. is kami ko uparyukt vichaardhaaraon ne puraa kiya.
 sindikawaad anya samaajwaadiyon kii bhanti samaajawaadee vyavastha ke paksh main he parantu araajaktaavaadiyon kii tarah vah rajya kaa ant kar sthaneey samudaayon ke haath main saamaajik niyantran chahta he. vah is niyantran ko keval utpaadak varg tak hi simit rakhana chahta he. araajaktaavaadiyon kii bhanti sindicavaadi bhi rashtriya tathaa antarrashtriya sanghon ke samarthak or rajya, raajnneetik dal, yuddh or sanyavad ke virdohi hai.
 dhyey kii prapti kaa sindicavaadi maarg kranti he, parantu is kranti ke liye bhi vah raajnneetik dal ko anaavashyak samajhataa he kyonki iske dwaara mazdooron kii krantikari ichha ke kamjor ho jane kaa bhay he. iskaa hadataalon main atoot vishwaas he. soril ke anusaar isai puranic punarutthan kii bhanti yah bhi mazdooron par jaadu kaa asar karti he or unke andar aikya or kranti kii bhaawanaaon ko protsaahan detii he. ye vichaarak mashiinon kii todphod, bicat, poonjipati kii paidaavaar ko badnaam karnaa, kaam taalna aadi ke paksh main bhi hai. ant main ek aam hadtaal dwaara poonjeewaadi vyavastha kaa ant kar ye sindikawaadi samaaj ko sthapana karnaa chaahate hai.
 in vichaaron se anek laathini desh france, italy, spain, madhya or dakshin america prabhavit hue hai. inkaa asar sanyukt rajya america main bhi tha, parantu vahaan vikendrikaran par jor nahin diya gaya kyonki us desh main bade paimaane ke udyog ek vaastaviktaa the. roosi vichaarak prince cropotkin ne isse prerana praapt kii or britain ne isko sanshodhit rup main sweekaar kiya.
 gild samaajwaad sindikawaad kii pratilipi maatr nahin, uskaa british paristhitiyon main abhyanukulan he. gild samaajwaad ke upar swadhinata kii parampara or fabiaswad kaa bhi prabhaav he. iskaa naam europe ke madhyakaalin vyavsaayik sanghathanon se liya gaya he. us samay se sangh aarthik or saamaajik jivan par haaavee the or vibhinn sanghon ke pratinidhi nagaron kaa shashan chalaate the. gild samaajawaadee uparyukt sangh vyavastha se prerana grahan karte the. ve raajnneetik kshetra or udyog dhandhon main loktantratmak siddhant or swaittashasan sthapit karnaa chaahate the. ye vichaarak udyogon ke rashtriyakaran maatr se santusht nahin kyonki isse naukarshahi kaa bhay he parantu ve rajya kaa ant bhi nahin karnaa chaahate. rajya ko adhik loktantratmak or vikendrit karne ke baad ve usko deshraksha or bhokta ke hitsaadhan ke liye rakhana chaahate hai. unke anusaar rajkeey sansad main keval kshetreey hi nahin, vyavsaayik pratinidhitiv bhi hona chahie. ye rajya or udyogon par mazdooron kaa niyantran chaahate hai at: sindikawaad ke nikat hai parantu rajyavirohi na hone ke kaaran inkaa jhukaav samoohwaad kii ore bhi he. ye asafalataa ke bhay se krantikari maarg ko sweekaar nahin karte lekin keval vaidhanik maarg ko bhi aparyaapt samajhate hai or mazdooron ke sakriy andolan, hadtaal aadi kaa bhi samarthan karte hai.
 pratham mahayuddh ke purv or uske bich main is vichaardhaara kaa prabhaav badha. yuddh ke samay mazdooron ne raksha-udyogon par niyantran kii maang kii or uske baad majdoor sanghon ne swayan makaan banane ke theke liye, parantu kuch kaal baad sarkari sahaayta na milane par ye prayog asafal hue. gild samaajwaad ke pramukh samarthakon main arthur painti, habsan, orange or kol ke naam ullekhneey hai. britain kaa majdoor dal or majdoor andolan is vichaardhaara se vishesh prabhavit hue hai.
 mukhya lekh samyawad dekhiye.
 marks ne san 1848 main apane "samyavaadee ghoshnaptr" main jis kranti kii bhavishyavaanee kii thi vah anshat: satya hui or us varsh or uske baad kai varsh tak europe main kranti kii jwaala failati rahi; parantu jis samaajawaadee vyavastha kii usko aashaa thi vah sthapit na ho saki, pratyut krantiyaan dabaa di gayi or patan ke sthaan main poonjeewaad kaa vikaas hua. france or prasha ke bich yuddh ke samay paraajay ke kaaran paris main pratham samaajawaadee shashan sthapit hua parantu kuch hi dinon main usko bhi dabaa diya gaya. paris commune kii pratikriya hui or majdoor andolanon kaa daman kiya jane laga jiske falswaroop marks dwaara sthapit antarrashtriya majdoor sangh bhi teetar bitar ho gaya. majdoor andolanon ke saamne prashn tha ki ve samaajwaad kii sthapana ke liye krantikari maarg apnaayen athwa sudhaarwadi maarg grahan karen. in paristhitiyon main katipay sudhaarwadi vichaardhaaraon kaa janm hua. inamen isai samaajwaad, fabiaswad or punraritivaad mukhya hai.
 isai samaajwaad ke mukhya prachaarak britain ke jaan melcome ludlo, france ke bishap clad foshe or germany ke victor ime hubar hai. poonjeewaadi shoshan dwaara mazdooron kii durdasha dekhakar in vichaarkon ne is vyavastha kii aalochana kii or mazdooron main sahakaari andolan kaa prachaar kiya. unhone utpaadak tathaa bhokta sahakaari samitiyon kii sthapana bhi kii. isai samaajwaad kaa prabhaav britain, france or germany ke atirikt austria tathaa belgium main bhi tha.
 mukhya lekh bhartiya samaajwaad dekhiye.
