 ek samay sabhi prakaar kii whisky kii vartanee "i" ke bina "whiske" ke rup main thi. 1870 ke aas-paas scottish whisky kaa naam kaaphi kharaab tha kyonki scottish asawni ne koffe bhatti main utpaadit saste spirit ke saath whisky ko bazaar main utaara tha. irish or america asawni ne apni ucch gunvattta vaale utpaada ko alag karne ke liye atirikt "i" ke saath "whisky" vartanee ko apanaaya. vartmaan main vartanee whisky kaa istemaal aam taur par scotland, welsh, canada or japan main asavith whisky ke liye kiya jaataa he, jabaki whiskey kaa prayog ireland or america main asavith spirit ke liye kiya jaataa he. haalanki beuro of alcohol, tobaco and phirearms 1968 ke ek nirdesh main america sarkari vartanee ke rup main "whisky" nirdisht karata he or yah parampara se alag "whiskey" ke rup main label kii anumati detaa he or adhikansh america asawni ab bhi aitihaasik vartanee kaa upyog karte hai. arli times, mekars mark or jorge dickel aam taur par apwaad ke rup main scottish viraasat kaa sanket dete hai.

 whisky or anya asut peya jaise cognake or ram, vyapak rup se sugandhit yogic vaale mishrit peya hai, jinamein se kuch 200-300 kaa rasinic vishleshan dwaara aasaani se pataa lagaaya ja saktaa he. swaad vaale rasayanon main shaamil hai "carbonil yogic, alcohol, carboxylik acid or unke rasinic yogic, nitrogen or sulfur yukt yogic, tanin or anya polifenolic yogic, tarapin or oxigen-yukt vishamchakree yogic" or vasa amla ke rasinic yogic. nitrogen yogicon main shaamil hai pyridine, picoline or pirazine.
 whisky kaa swaad aanshik rup se samajanit or fusel tel kii upasthiti se nirdhaarit hota he. fusel tel ethnal se adhik alcohol hai, jo kinchit vishakt hota he or zordaar, apriy gandh or swaad liye hota he. whisky main fusel tel kii adhikta ko ek dosha mana jaataa he. avaanchit fusel tel ko hataane ke liye aswan prakriya main anek paddatiyon ko apanaaya jaataa he. paaramparik rup se, america asavak koyala, bajri, ret yaa linen kaa upyog kar avaanchit asut ko ghataane ke liye dobaaraa chaanane ke kaam par dhyaan kendrit karte hai. canadai asavak paramparaagat rup se stambhakar bhattiyon kaa istemaal karte hai, jise neutral grain spirit yaa grain neutral spirit ke rup main gyaat, lagbhag shuddh vaale ethnaul ke utpaadan ke liye niyantrit kiya ja saktaa he. flavoring whisky ke saath neutral grain spirit ke mishran dwaara swaad ko punah paaya jaataa he.
 asuton main esetal teji se nirmit hote hai or adhikansh asut peya padaarthon main paae jate hai, jinamein sarvaadhik vishisht he acetaldihide diaithail asital . whisky main ucchtam str moult whiski se jude hai. yah asital sheri main ek pramukh swaad yogic he or fas jaisi sugandh kaa yogadan detaa he.
 diketone daiacitile main makkhan jaisaa sugandh hota he or yah lagbhag sabhi asut peya padaarthon main maujuud hota he. aam taur par vodaka kii tulana main whisky or cognake main adhik, lekin ram yaa brandi se kam hota hai.
 whisky lactone ok ke sabhi prakaaron main paaya jaataa he. is lactone main ek majboot nariyal kaa sugandh hota he. whisky lactone ko kvarkas lactone ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he.
 vanijyik rup se jaale ok main phenoli mishran adhik hote hai. ek adhyayan main 40 vibhinn phenolic mishran ko pahachaanaa gaya he. whisky main coumarin scopolatin maujuud rahata he, jiske borbon whisky main ucchtam str paae jane kii soochna he.
