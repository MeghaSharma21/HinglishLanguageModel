 jwalamukheey jhil aisi jhil ko kaha jaataa he jo kisi jwalamukhi ke mukh main pani bhar jane se ban jaati ho. jwalamukheey jhilein jivit yaa mrit jwalamukhi donon main ban sakati hai. aam taur se iname pani varsha yaa himpat se bhar jaataa he. yadi kisi jivit jwalamukhi main jhil bani ho or vah jwalamukhi fatna shuru ho jaye, to aisi jhilein tezi se ubal kar khaali ho jaatin hai. indonesia main sthit tobaa jhil vishwa kii sabase badi jwalamukheey jhil he.
 kyonki jwalamukheey jhilein jwalamukhiyon ke shikhar par unke munh main banati he isliye in jhiilon kaa aakaar aam taur par gol hota he. jab jwalamukhi fatna band ho jaataa he to munh main lava jamkar ek pathar kii sataha bana detaa he. yahi sataha jwalamukheey jhil kaa neeche kaa farsh hoti he. agar lava ubad-khabad rup se jam gaya ho, to vah kuch pahadiyaan-si bhi bana detaa he, jo kii kabhi-kabhi jhil ke pani se bhar jane par jhil main chote dwipon kaa rup dhaaran kar letin hai. agar jwalamukhi shant rahe to samay ke saath saath in dwipon par ghaas, paudhe yaa ped bhi kabhi-kabhi jad pakad leten hai.
 jwalamukheey jhilein aksar oonchaai par hoti hai or unke ird-gird kaa kshetra jwalamukhi ke puraane visphoton ke kaaran saafe ho chukaa hota he. isliye, agar jwalamukhi mrit ho in jhiilon kaa pani aksar bahut saafe or nila nazar aata he. america ke aaregon rajya main sthit cretar lake apane saafe or nile pani ke liye vishwa-bhar main mashhoor he. lekin agar jwalamukhi se abhi bhi gas nikal rahi ho, to us vifailli gas ke bulbule lagaataar is jhil main uthte rahete hai, jis se ek to pani kaa rang gadha ho jaataa he or doosraa gas ke milane se pani main kuch maatra main amla bhi ban jaataa he.
