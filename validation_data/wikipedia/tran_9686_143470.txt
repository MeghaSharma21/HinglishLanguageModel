  kilimanoor kerala ke tiruvananthapuram jile kaa ek chota sa nagar he. yah nagar kshetra 2 gram panchayaton, pa hayakunnummal gram panchayat or kilimanoor gram panchayat dwaara prashasit kiya jaataa he. bharat ke mahaan chitrakaar raja ravi verma kaa janm yaheen hua tha. yahan kaa kilimanoor mahal darshneey he.
 raja ravi verma bharat ke vikhyath chitrakaar the. unka janm 29 april 1848 ko kilimanoor main hua tha. unhone bhartiya sahitya or samskruti ke paatro kaa chithran kiya. unke chitron kii sabase badi visheshta hindu mahakavyon or dharmagranthon par banaae gaye chitra hai. hindu mithkon kaa bahut hi prabhaavshaalee istemaal unke chitron main dikhataa hai. is sangrahalay main unke chitron kaa bahut bada sangrah he.
 kilimanoor pakshi or hiran kii bhoomi kaa matlab) ek adivasee pramukh dwaara travnakor ke ettuvitil pillmar ke dinon ke dauraan shashan tha. mukhya maharaja marthant verma ke khilaaf vidroh kar diya or kshetra par kabja kar liya tha or kilimanoor royal house ko di. 
 kilimanoor kii yah royal house 300 se adhik varshon ke ke itihaas he. 1705 main ittammar bepor thattaricovilakam, ek kolthunadu shahi ghar ke raja ke bete or do ​​betiyon venaad royal ghar dwaara apanaaya gaya. ittammar raja kii bahan or uske bete, ram verma or raghav verma kilimanoor main based hai or ab apanaaya bahanon kii shaadi. marthant verma, travnakor ke rajya ke sansthaapak raghav verma ke putr tha. raghav verma ke bhattije ravi verma koil thamburan, marthant verma kii bahan se shaadi kar lee. unke bete ko dharm raja kaarthik thirunal ram verma ke rup main janaa jane laga. 1740 main, jab ek sambhddha dach captain hokkert dwaara netritv main bal dashinkanatu raja kaa samarthan venaad hamalaa, kilimanoor se ek sena chaturaai kaa virodh or phir unhen haraaya. ek choti si jeet haalanki, yah pehali baar ek bhartiya sena ke ek europea shakti ko haraaya tha. is kartab, marthant verma, 1753 main, kii manyata main kilimanoor mahal ke karon or ghoshnaa swytt sthiti se niyantran ke tahat kshetron chhot di. vartmaan mahal parisar main bhi is samay ke dauraan banaaya gaya tha. parivaar ke devtaa ke liye vartmaan ayyapp mandir, bhi banaaya gaya tha.
 velu thampi dalva yojit baithkon kilimanoor mahal main british par apane vidrohon kii yojana bana. vah mahal main angrejon ke khilaaf antim ladaai ke liye jane se pehle apni talwaar par saunp diya. bharat ke pratham raashtrapati dow rajendra prasad mahal se talwaar praapt he or yah ab dilli main rashtriya sangrahalay main rakhaa he.
 kilimanoor mukhya central road par he. yah kerala rajya sadak dwaara chalaae bason dwaara puure pramukh gantavy ke liye judaa hua he. kilimanoor bas depo se parivahan nigam. kilimanoor shahar rajya rajmarg par sthit he. kilimanoor keval 45 kimi tiruvanantpuram se dur he.
