 hemodialysis, ek outpesheynt yaa inpeshent upachaar ho saktaa he. niyamit hemodialysis ko ek dialysis outpesheynt suvidha main yojit kiya jaataa he, yah yaa to aspataal kaa isi uddeshy ke liye nirmit ek cameraa hota he yaa ek samarpit swatantra clinics hoti he. hemodialysis ko ghar par kam hi kiya jaataa he. ek clinics main dialysis upachaar ko nurson or takanicianon se nirmit vishesh staff dwaara shuru or prabandhit kiya jaataa he; ghar par dialysis upachaar ko swayan aarambh or prabandhit kiya ja saktaa he yaa kisi prashikshit sahaayak kii madad se sanyukt rup se kiya ja saktaa he jo aamtaur par parivaar kaa ek sadsy hota he. 
 hemodialysis kaa siddhant vaisaa hi he jaisaa anya dialysis vidhiyon kaa he; isamen ardh-pargamya jhilli ke aar-paar viley kaa visaran shaamil he. hemodialysis, viruddh dhara pravaah kaa istemaal karata he, jahaan apohit, bahya-daihik circuit main rakt pravaah kii vipareet disha main bahata he. viruddh dhara pravaah, adhiktam par sampoorn jhilli main sankendran pravanta ko banaae rakhti he or dialysis kii kshamta ko badhati he.
 taral padaarth nishkasana kaa kaam apohit kaksh ke hydrostatic dabaav main pherbadal karte hue puraa kiya jaataa he, jisase mukt jal or kuch ghule hue viley ek nirmit dabaav pravanta se hote hue jhilli ke paar jate hai.
 dialysis samaadhaan jiskaa prayog kiya jaataa he vah khanij yanon kaa visankramit samaadhaan hota he. uria or anya apshisht padaarth, potacium or phosphate, dialysis samaadhaan main visarit ho jate hai. haalanki, chloride or sodium kii sandratha, haani ko rokane ke liye saamaanya plasma ke hi samaan hoti he. rakt amlata ko thik karne ke liye sodium bikarbonate ko plasma kii tulana main adhik sandratha main milaayaa jaataa he. glucose kii ek alp maatra bhi aamtaur par istemaal kii jaati he.

 vrikk vifaltaa ke vyaavhaarik ilaaj ke liye dialysis ko viksit karne main kai logon ne mahati bhoomika nibhaai he, jisaki shuruyaat glassgo ke thomas graham se hui, jinhone pehali baar 1854 main ardh-pargamya jhilli ke aar-paar viley parivahan ke siddhanton ko prastut kiya. kritrim gurde kaa vikaas sabase pehle 1913 main abel, rontre or turnar dwaara kiya gaya tha, maanav par pratham hemodialysis haas dwaara or kritrim gurde kaa chikitsa upyogi upkaran ke rup main vikaas kolf dwaara 1943-1945 main kiya gaya. is shodh se darshaya gaya ki vrikk vifaltaa se mar rahe rogiyon ke jivan ko lanbaa kiya ja saktaa he.
 da. wilem kolf pratham vyakti the jinhone 1943 main ek kriyasheel apohak kaa nirmaan kiya. jis rogi kaa sabase pehle safaltaapurvak ilaaj kiya gaya vah uramic coma main ek 67 varshiya mahila thi jise kolf ke apohak dwaara 11 ghanton ke hemodialysis ke baad hosh aa gaya tha. iske nirmaan ke samay, kolf kaa lakshya, teevr vrikk vifaltaa se ubarte samay jivan samarthan pradaan karnaa tha. dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke samaapt hone ke baad, kolf ne apane banaae hue paanch apohak ko vishwa ke vibhinn aspataalon ko daan kar diya, jisamen shaamil tha mount sinai hospital, new york. kolf ne apni hemodialysis mashin ke khaake ko boston main peter bent brigham hospital main jorge thorn ko diya. isse kolf ke apohak kii agali peedi kaa nirmaan hua, steneles steel kii kolf-brigham dialysis mashin.
 1950 ke dashak tak, wilem kolf ke apohak ke avishkaar kaa istemaal gambhir vrikk vifaltaa ke liye kiya jaataa tha, lekin ise stage 5 jeern vrikk rog vaale rogiyon ke liye ek vyavhaarya upachaar ke rup main nahin dekha gaya. us samay, doctoron maananaa tha ki rogiyon ke liye do kaarnon se anischitkaal tak dialysis karwaanaa asambhav tha. sabase pehle, unhone sochaa ki koi bhi maanav nirmit upkaran lambey samay tak gurde kii kriyaaon ko pratisthapit nahin kar saktaa he. iske atirikt, dialysis se guzar rahe ek rogi kii nasen or dhamniyaan kshatigrast ho gai, jisase kai upachaar ke baad, rogi ke rakt tak pahunchane ke liye ek dhamni khojna mushkil ho gaya.
 dow niles alvaal : mul kolf vrikk, chikitsakeey rup se adhik upyogi nahin tha, kyonki yah atirikt drav ke nishkasana kii anumati nahin detaa tha. dow niles alvaal ne ek steneles steel canstar ke andar is vrikk ke sanshodhit sanskaran ko prastut kiya, jiske upar ek nakaaratmak dabaav daalaa ja saktaa tha or is prakaar hemodialysis ke sahi myne main vaastavik vyaavhaarik anuprayog ko prabhaavshaalee banaaya, jise 1946 main lund vishvavidhyalay main kiya gaya tha. alvaal yakeenan, dialysis ke liye dhamnishiraparak shant ke avishkaarak the. unhone pehali baar isaki khabar 1948 main di jahaan unhone aise dhamnishiraparak shant kaa istemaal khargoshon main kiya. baad main unhone kaanch ke bane aise shant or saath hi saath apane canstar-band apohak kaa istemaal 1946 or 1960 ke bich vrikk vifaltaa vaale 1,500 rogiyon kaa ilaaj karne ke liye kiya, jaisaa ki unhone sitambar 1960 main avian main yojit pratham antarrashtriya nefrology congress ko bataaya. alvaal ko 1957 main lund vishvavidhyalay main nefrology ke ek nav nirmit pad par niyukt kiya gaya. iske baad unhone swedish vyapaaree holgar krafurd ke saath sahyog se ek pramukh company kaa gathan kiya jisane baad main pichley 50 saalon main dialysis upkaran, gambro kaa nirmaan kiya. dialysis ke aarmabhik itihaas kii samiksha stanley sheldon dwaara kii gai he.
 dow belding ech. scribnar ne ek sarjan, da. wain quinton ke saath kaam karte hue alvaal dwaara prayukt kaanch ke shant ko sanshodhit kiya or use teflone se banaaya. ek anya mahatvapurn sudhaar tha unhen silicon elastomar tubing ke ek chote tukade se jodna. isse tathakthith scribnar shant ke aadhaar kaa gathan hua, jise shaayad adhik upyukt rup se quinton-scribnar shant kaha jaataa he. upachaar ke baad, you-aakaar ke chote teflone tub ke istemaal se sharir ke baahar donon tub ko jodate hue raktavahi abhigam ko khulaa rakhaa jayega, jo tub ke rakt ko dhamni main roak degaa or waapas tub se shiraaon main jaayegaa.
 1962 main, scribnar ne duniya kii pehali outpesheynt dialysis suvidha kii shuruyaat kii, sietal artifishial kidney centre, baad main jiskaa naam badalakar northwest kidney centre kar diya gaya. turant yah samasya utpann hui ki kise dialysis diya janaa chahie, kyonki centre kii chah dialysis mashiinon kii kshamta kii tulana main maang kaaphi zyada bad gai. scribnar ne faisla kiya ki kise dialysis diya jaae or kise naa diya jaae yah nirnay ve nahin lenge. iske bajaay, iskaa chunaav ek gumnaam samiti dwaara kiya jaayegaa, jise pratham bioethic samitiyon main se ek mana ja saktaa he.
 abel or roundtri, haas or neychelles jaise agraduton sahit dialysis main safal or asafal prayaason ke vistrut itihaas ke liye, kagelstrand kii yah samiksha dekhen.
 dialysis ke liye ek vrikk-chikitsak kaa parchaa dialysis upachaar ke liye vibhinn maapdandon ko nirdisht karegaa. inamen shaamil he aavriti, prayek upachaar kii lambaai or rakt or dialysis samaadhaan pravaah dar, saath hi saath apohak kaa aakaar. dialysis samaadhaan kii rachana ko bhi kabhi-kabhi iske sodium or potacium or bikarbonit str ke sandarbh main samyojit kiya jaataa he. saamanyatya, ek vyakti ke sharir kaa aakaar jitna bada hoga, use dialysis kii utani hi adhik jarurat hogi. uttari america or britain main ek saptaah main 3-4 ghante ke 3 upachaar dena aam he. ek saptaah main do baar kaa satr un rogiyon ke liye thik he jinamein gurde kaa adhikansh hissaa kaam kar rahaa ho. prati saptaah chaar satron ko aksar bade rogiyon ke liye nirdhaarit kiya jaataa he, saath hi un rogiyon ke liye jinamein drav adhibhaar kii samasya he. antat, hemodialysis ke gharelu laghu satron main ab ruchi bad rahi he, jo 1.5 - 4 ghante kaa satr hota he jise prati saptaah 5-7 baar diya jaataa he, aamtaur par ghar par. ratrikalin dialysis main bhi ruchi badi he, jisamen ek marij kaa dialysis kiya jaataa he, aam taur par ghar par, prati raat 8-10 ghante, prati saptaah 3-6 raaten. ratrikalin in-centre dialysis, prati saptaah 3-4 baar kuch chand dialysis ikaayiyon main america main pesh kii jaati he.
 hemodialysis main aksar drav nishkasana shaamil hota he kyonki vrikk vifaltaa vaale adhikansh rogiyon main mutr tyaag bahut nune yaa nahin hota he. drav ko bahut zyada nikaalane ore/yaa bahut jaldi nikaalane se hone vaale paksh prabhaav main shaamil he nune raktchaap, thakaan, seene main dard, paad-anthan, matlee or sir dard. ye lakshan upachaar ke dauraan paae ja sakate hai or ilaaj ke baad tak bache rah sakate hai, unhen kabhi-kabhi samuhik rup se dialysis hangover yaa dialysis vashout ke rup main sanderbhit kiya jaataa he. in lakshanon kii gambhirtha aamtaur par drav nishkasana kii maatra or gati ke anupaat main hoti he. haalanki, drav nishkasana kii di gai maatra yaa dar vyakti or din ke aadhaar par bhinn hoti he. in dushprabhavon se bachaa ja saktaa he or/yaa unki gambhirtha ko upachaar ke bich main taral padaarth kaa seven karte hue simit kiye ja saktaa he yaa dialysis ke khuraaq ko badhaate hue kam kiya ja saktaa he, udhaaharan ke liye, prati saptaah maanak teen baar, prati upachaar 3-4 ghante kii tulana main prati upachaar adhik baar yaa adhik lambe samay tak dialysis karnaa.
 chunki hemodialysis ke liye raktavahini pranali kii aavashyaktaa hoti he, hemodialysis se guzarane vaale rogiyon kii raktasanchaar pranali rogaanuon ke liye khul sakati he, jisase roganutaa ho sakati he, ek sankraman jo hriday valve ko prabhavit karata he yaa ek sankraman jo haddiyon ko prabhavit karata he. sankraman kaa khatra, istemaal ke prakaar par nirbhar karte hue bhinn hota he. raktasrav bhi ho saktaa he, ek baar phir istemaal ke prakaar par nirbhar karte hue jokhim alag-alag hota he. sankraman ko sankraman niyantran kii sarvshereshth prathaon kaa kadai se paalan karte hue kam kiya ja saktaa he.
 heparin, hemodialysis main sabase aam rup se istemaal kiya jane vaala thakkarodhi he, kyonki aam taur par ise acchhee tarah sahan kiya jaataa he or protamin sulphate ke saath ise turant palata ja saktaa he. heparin allergy kabhi-kabhi ek samasya ho sakati he or nune platelet gananaa ko prerit kar sakati he. aise rogiyon main, vaikalpik thakkarodhi kaa istemaal kiya ja saktaa he. raktasrav ke ucch jokhim vaale rogiyon main, dialysis ko anticogulation ke bina kiya ja saktaa he.
 pratham prayog lakshan, kritrim gurda ke liye ek durlabh lekin gambhir tivrgahita sambandhi pratikriya he. iske lakshan main he chheenkna, ghargharaahat, shwasalpata, peeth dard, seene main dard, yaa achaanak maut. yah kritrim gurde main avshisht visankramak ke kaaran yaa swayan jhilli kii saamagree kii vajah se ho saktaa he. haal ke varshon main, rasinic visankramak ke bajaay gamma vikiran, bhaap visankraman, yaa electron beam vikiran ke istemaal se or ucch jaev-anukulata vaali ardh-pargamya jhilli ke vikaas se pratham prayog lakshan kii ghatana main kami ai he. prasanskaran ke nae tareekon ko jo dialysis ke purv ke sweekaarya ghatak hai, un par hameshaa gaur kiya janaa chahie. udhaaharan ke liye, 2008 main, pratham prayog prakaar yaa pratikriyaayein, jisamen shaamil thi rogi kii maut jo ati sulphate vaale condroitin sulphate kii nirmaan prakriya ke dauraan heparin sankraman se hui thi. 
 hemodialysis kii dirghakaalik jatiltaaon main shaamil he amyloydosis, neuropathy or vibhinn hriday rog. upachaar kii aavriti or lambaai badhaane se dekha gaya he ki drav adhibhaar main sudhaar or hriday vruddhi hoti he, jise aise rogiyon main aam rup se dekha gaya he.

 hemodialysis main, rakt tak pahunchane ke liye teen praathmik tareekon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he: ek antahshira catheter, ek dhamnishiraparak nalwran or ek synthetic grapht. abhigam kaa prakaar kuch karkon dwaara prabhavit hota he jaise rogi ke gurde kii vifaltaa kaa ek apekshit samay or uske vahika pranali kii haalat. rogiyon main ekaadhik abhigam ho saktaa he, kyonki aam taur par ek avi nalwran yaa grapht paripakv ho rahaa he or ek catheter kaa abhi bhi prayog kiya ja rahaa he. sanvahani abhigam ke in teenon pramukh prakaar ke nirmaan main surgery kii aavashyaktaa he. 
 catheter kaa upyog, jise kabhi-kabhi sivisi kaha jaataa he, do lumina vaale ek plastic catheter se bani hoti he jise ek vishaal nas main daalaa jaataa he taaki rakt kii badi maatra ko ek lumina se nikaala ja sake or use dialysis circuit main pravesh karaayaa ja sake or anya lumina ke madhyam se lautaaya ja sake. haalanki, rakt pravaah lagbhag hameshaa ek acchhee tarah se kaary kar rahe nalwran yaa grapht kii tulana main kam hota he.
 catheter aamtaur par do saamaanya kismon main paae jate hai, nalika vaale or gaiyr nalika vaale.
 gaiyr nalika catheter abhigam, laghu avadhi abhigam ke liye hai or catheter, nas main pravesh ke sthal par twachaa se ubharta he.
 nalika yukt catheter abhigam main ek lanbaa catheter shaamil hota he, jise nas main pravishti bindu se ek nikaas sthaan ke liye twachaa ke andar daalaa jaataa he. ise aamtaur par gale main aantarik greeva shira main daalaa jaataa he or nikaas sthaan aamtaur par chaati main hota he. yah nalika hamalaa karne vaale rogaanuon ke liye ek baadha ke rup main kaary karti he or vaise nalika catheter ko laghu se lekar madhyam avadhi ke upyog ke liye dizine kiya jaataa he kyonki sankraman abhi bhi ek samasya hoti he. 
 sankraman ke alaava, shiraparak sankuchan, catheter abhigam ke saath ek anya gambhir samasya he. catheter, nas main ek bahya padaarth he or aksar shira kii diwaar par daahak pratikriya utpann karata he. isse dhamni kaa scaring or sankuchan hota he, yahaan naadirodh bhi hota he. isse shira dwaara shushk kshetra main gambhir shiraparak avrodh kii samasyaon kaa srijan ho saktaa he or shiraaon par bhi iskaa prabhaav pad saktaa he, jo baad ke samay nalwran yaa grapht ke nirmaan ke liye bekaar ho saktaa he. lambii avadhi ke hemodialysis ke marij ko vastav main abhigam kii 'kami' ho sakati he, to yah ek ghaatak samasya ho sakati he. 
 catheter abhigam kaa upyog aam taur par twarit dialysis ke liye sheeghra abhigam kii khatir kiya jaataa he, un rogiyon main nalika abhigam ke liye jinhen gambhir vrikk vifaltaa se ubarne kii sambhaavna he or un rogiyon ke liye jo vrikk vifaltaa ke antim charan main hai or jo yaa to vaikalpik abhigam kii pratiksha kar rahe hai yaa jo vaikalpik abhigam praapt karne main asamarth hai. 
 catheter abhigam aksar marijon main lokapriy he, kyonki dialysis mashin se sampark ke liye sui kii aavashyaktaa nahin hoti he. haalanki, upar ullikhit catheter abhigam kaa gambhir jokhim kaa matlab he ki is tarah ke abhigam par keval sabase hataash sthiti main ek dirghakaalik samaadhaan ke rup main hi vichaar kiya janaa chahie.
 avi nalpravan ko pasandida abhigam vidhi ke rup main pahachaanaa jaataa he. ek nalwran banane ke liye, ek sanvahani sarjan sammilan ke madhyam se ek dhamni or ek nas ko jodate hai. chunki yah keshikaon se bachakar nikal jaataa he, rakt, nalwran ke madhyam se tezi se bahata he. ek vyakti apni ungali ko ek paripakv nalwran par rakhkar ise mahasoos kar saktaa hoon. ise 'romaanch' kaa anubhav kehete hai or nalwran ke upar ek vishisht 'gunj' ehsaas ko paidaa karata he. nalwran se guzarate hue rakt kii "fusfusaahat" ko ek stethoscope ke madhyam se suna ja saktaa he, is dhwani ko angreji main broot kaha jaataa he.
 nalpravan aamtaur par gaiyr-prabhavi baanh main nirmit hota he or haath par sthit ho saktaa he, yaa baaju par yaa kohni main . ek nalwran ko paripakv hone main kai saptaah laga sakate hai, ek ausat se 4-6 saptaah. upachaar ke dauraan, do suiyon ko nalwran main daalaa jaataa he, ek khun nikaalane ke liye or ek waapas daalane ke liye.
 e.ve. nalwran upyog ke phaayde main shaamil he nimna sankraman dar, kyonki koi bhi bahya padaarth unke gathan main shaamil nahin he, ucch rakt pravaah dar or ghanaastrata kii nune ghatana. jatiltaayen kam hai, lekin agar ek nalwran main bahut hi ucch rakt pravaah he or raktavahiki jo baaki angon ko aapurti karti he vah kharaab he, ek chori lakshan ubhar saktaa he, jahaan ang main pravesh karne vaala rakt nalwran main khinch liya jaataa he or ang kii keshika main pravesh ke bina saamaanya sancharan main laut jaataa he. isse us ang kii thand bhare charam lakshan paidaa hote hai, anthan dard or, gambhir utak haani hoti he. avi nalwran kii ek dirghakaalin jatilta yah ho sakati he ki dhamneevisphar kaa vikaas hota he, nas kii diwaar par ek ubhaar jahaan yah sui ke baar-baar pravisht karne se kamjor ho saktaa he. ek badi seemaa tak dhamneevisphar viksit hone kaa khatra sui chubhaane kii surakshapurna takanic dwaara kam kiya ja saktaa he. dhamneevisphar, sudharatmak surgery ko jaruri bana saktaa he or ek nalwran ke upyogi jivan ko chota kar saktaa he. nalwran or dhamneevisphar kii kshati ko yaa chhadam-dhamneevisphar ke gathan ko rokane ke liye, yah anushansit he ki sui ko vibhinn binduon par kramvaar daalaa janaa chahie. ek anya drushtikon he nalwran main ek bhothari sui dwaara pravesh karnaa, thik usee jagah par. ise 'batanhole' drushtikon kaha jaataa he. ek die gaye nalwran par aksar do yaa teen batanhole sthaan uplabdh hote hai. isse nalwran kaa jivan lambaa ho saktaa he or nalwran ko kshati se bachaane main madad milti he.
 avi grapht, adhikansh maamalon main bahut kuch nalpravan ke samaan he, sivaay iske ki ek kritrim naadee kaa istemaal dhamni or shira ko jodne ke liye kiya jaataa he. yah grapht aamtaur par synthetic saamagree se bana hota he, aksar PTFE se, lekin kabhi-kabhi rasinic rup se upachaarit hota he, pashuon kii visankramit shira kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he. grapht ko tab daalaa jaataa he jab marij kii deshi raktavahiki ek nalwran kii anumati nahin detii. ve nalpravan se adhik teji se paripakv hote hai or gathan ke kai hafton baad tak upyog ke liye taiyaar rah sakate hai . haalanki, avi grapht ke sankuchit ho jane kaa ucch khatra rahata he, vishesh rup se grapht ko shira main jahaan se daalaa gaya he thik uske neeche vaali shira main. sankuchan se aksar thakka yaa ghanaastrata hoti he. bahari padaarthon ke rup main, ve sankramit ho jane ke khatre se grasit rahete hai. grapht ko lagane ke anya sthanon ke vikalp uplabdh hai, kyonki grapht ko kaaphi lanbaa banaaya ja saktaa he. is prakaar ek grapht ko jaangh main yaa phir gardan main daalaa ja saktaa he.
 avi nalpravan main shiraparak catheter yaa grapht kii tulana main ek behatar upyog pratyakshta or astitv kshamta hoti he. ve behatar rogi bachaav bhi utpann karte hai or grapht yaa shiraparak catheter kii tulana main bahut kam jatiltaayen hai. is kaaran se, centres for medicare and medicade ne ek fistula first inishiative sthapit kiya he, jiskaa lakshya he dialysis rogiyon main e.ve. nalpravan ke upyog ko badhanaa.
 hemodialysis ke teen prakaar hai: paaramparik hemodialysis, dainik hemodialysis or ratrikaleen hemodialysis. neeche otawa aspataal kaa ek broshar kaa saransh diya gaya he.
 jeern hemodialysis ko aamtaur par prayek saptaah teen baar kiya jaataa he, prayek upachaar ke liye kareeb 3-4 ghante, jiske dauraan rogi ke rakt ko ek tub ke madhyam se baahar nikaala jaataa he 3-400 cc/minute kii dar se. yah tub 15, 16 yaa 17 gej kii sui se judaa hua hota he jo dialysis nalwran yaa grapht main ghusi hoti he yaa dialysis catheter ke ek port se judy hoti he. rakt ko phir apohak ke madhyam se pump kiya jaataa he or tab sansaadhit rakt ko ek anya tub ke madhyam se waapas marij ke khun main daalaa jaataa he. is prakriya ke dauraan, rogi ke raktchaap par najdiki najar rakhi jaati he or agar yah kam ho jaataa he, yaa rogi main nimna rakt maatra kaa koi anya lakshan viksit hota he, jaise ki mitli, to dialysis parichar mashin ke madhyam se atirikt taral padaarth de saktaa he. ilaaj ke dauraan rogi kii sampurna rakt kii maatra har 15 minute main mashin ke madhyam se parisanchaarit hoti he.
 dainik hemodialysis kaa istemaal aam taur par un rogiyon dwaara kiya jaataa he jo ghar par hi apana dialysis karte hai. yah kam tanaavpurn he, lekin isamen adhik abhigam kii aavashyaktaa he. yah catheter ke saath aasaan he, lekin nalpravan yaa grapht ke saath adhik samasyagrast he. "batanhole takanic" kaa upyog us nalpravan ke liye kiya ja saktaa he jisamen baarambaar abhigam kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. dainik hemodialysis ko aam taur par ek saptaah ke chah dinon main do ghante ke liye kiya jaataa he.
 ratrikaleen hemodialysis kii prakriya paaramparik hemodialysis ke samaan hoti he, sivaay iske ki ise prati saptaah chah raaton tak kiya jaataa he or prati satr chah se das ghante lagate hai, is dauraan rogi sota rahata he. 
 hemodialysis mashin rogi ke rakt or apohit ko apohak ke madhyam se pump karti he. bazaar main aadhunik dialysis mashin atyadhik computarikrut hai or suraksha ke najuk maapdandon kii lagaataar nigaraanee rakhte hai, jaise ki rakt or apohit pravaah dar; dialysis samaadhaan pravaahakatva, taapmaana or pH; rakt risaav yaa hawa kii upasthiti ke pramaan ke liye apohit kaa vishleshan. koi bhi reeding jo saamaanya shrenee se baahar kii he ek shravy alarm ko shuru kar detii he taaki rogi kii dekhbhaal karne vaale takanisian ko saavdhaan kiya ja sake. dialysis mashiinon ke nirmaataaon main shaamil hai fresenius, gambro, baxtor, bi. brown, nexstage or belko.
 hemodialysis ke liye ek vyapak jal shodhan pranali bilkul mahatvapurn he. kyonki dialysis rogi pani kii vishaal maatra ke prati ujagar hote hai, jo dilaset kaa nirmaan karne ke liye dilaset sankendran ke saath mishrit hota he, mineral pradooshak yaa bacterial antarjeevvish rogi ke rakt main pravesh kar sakate hai. kyonki kshatigrast gurde ashuddhiyon ko dur karne ke liye apane abhipret kaaryon kaa pradarshan nahin kar sakate hai, pani ke madhyam se rakt main pravisht aayan khatarnaak str tak pahunch sakate hai, parinamswaroop kai lakshan dikhaai dete hai yaa mrityu ho jaati he. aluminium, chloramine, fluoride, tamba or jasta, saath hi bacteria tukade or antarjeevvish aadi sabhi is sambandh main samasyaon ke kaaran he.
 is kaaran se, hemodialysis main upyog kiye jane vaale pani ko upyog se pehle saavdhaanee se shuddh kiya jaataa he. shuru main ise filtard or taapmaana samyojit kiya jaataa he or uske piech ko ek acid yaa aadhaar jodakar shuddh kiya jaataa he. tab yah naram ho jaataa he. uske baad jaivik pradooshak ko sokhne ke liye koyale kii lakdi yukt tank ke madhyam se pani choda jaataa he. uske baad chote chhidr ke saath ek jhilli main pani ko jor se chhodane ke madhyam se praathmik shuddhi kii jaati he, jise asamas reverse jhilli kaha jaataa he. isse pani ko jane kii anumati milti he, lekin bahut chote viley ko roke rakhata he jaise electrolites. bache hue electrolites ko antim rup se hataane ke liye aayan exange regin ke saath ek tanks ke madhyam se pani ko choda jaataa he jo ki bache hue sabhi aayan yaa fatayan ko saaf karata he or unhen hydroxil or hydrogen anu ke saath sthanantarit kar detaa he or pani ko atishuddh kar detaa he.
 yahaan tak ki jal shodhan kii is str kii prakriya bhi aparyaapt ho sakati he. haal hi main ek pravriti dilager jhilli ke madhyam se is antim shuddh pani ko paas kiya jaataa he. isse suraksha kii ek or parat praapt hoti he, vishesh rup se jeevanu utpatti vaale dosha se, jo ho saktaa he mul jal shuddhikaran pranali ke madhyam se pani ke guzarane ke baad pani main sanchit ho gaye hon.
 jaise hi shuddh pani ko dilaset sankendran ke saath mishrit kiya jaataa he, isaki chaalakata bad jaati he or tab se pani jo ki aayan yukt hoti he bijli yojit karti he. dialysis ke dauraan, dialysis samaadhaan kii chaalakata lagaataar yah sunishchit karti he ki pani or dilaset sankendran ko uchit anupaat main milaayaa ja rahaa he ki nahin. atyadhik sankendrit dialysis samaadhaan or atyadhik taral samaadhaan, donon hi gambhir naidanik samasyaayein paidaa kar sakate hai.
 apohak upkaran kaa ek tukdaa he jo ki vastav main rakt ko shuddh karata he. vartmaan main upyog kiye jane vaale lagbhag saare apohak khokhale-fibre prakaar ke hai. khokhale fibre kaa ek belanaakaar bandal, jinaki diwaar arddh-pargamya jhilli se bani he, uske donon kinaaron par poting compound laga hota he. is sangyojan ko phir chaar nikaas ke saath ek spasht plastic ke belanaakaar khol main daal diya jaataa he. prayek silendar ke ant par ek khulne vaala kinaaraa yaa rakt port khokhla fibre ke bandal ke prayek ant ke saath sampark banaata he. yah apohak ke "rakt compartment" kaa nirmaan karata he. do anya port ko silendar ke kinare main kaata jaataa he. yah khokhla fibre "dialaset compartment" ke aas-paas ke sthanon se sampark banaata he. bahut patali keshika-jaisi nalee ke bandal ke madhyam se rakt port ke dwaara rakt pump hoti he or fibre ke aas-paas ke sthanon ke madhyam se dilaset pump hoti he. dabaav pravanta ko tab lagu kiya jaataa he jab isaki zarurat drav ko rakt se apohak kampartment main bhejne ke liye hoti he.
 apohak jhilliyaan alag-alag rome chhidr ke saath aati hai. jo kaaphi chote chhed ke aakaar ke saath aate hai unhen "nune-pravaah" or bade romakup aakaar waalon ko "ucch pravaah" kaha jaataa he. beta-2-microglobulin jaise kuch bade anu, nune-pravaah apohak ke saath kabhi bhi hataaya ja saktaa he; baad main ucch pravaah apohak kaa hi istemaal hone laga. haalanki, is tarah ke apohak ko uchit taur par drav dar ko hataane ke liye or jhilli ke madhyam se rogi main apohak samaadhaan ashuddhi ke prativaah ko rokane ke liye nae apohak mashiinon or ucch gunvattta vaale apohak samaadhaan kii aavashyaktaa hoti he.
 apohak jhilli ko mukhya rup se selulose se banaaya jaataa tha. aisi jhilli kii sataha bahut zyada jaivikanuroop nahin the, kyonki ujagar hydroxil samooh jhilli ke dwaara rakt sanchaar main poorak ko sakriy kar detii he. isliye, buniyadi, "ansabstituted" selulose jhilli ko sanshodhit kiya gaya tha. isamen ek badlaav yah tha ki acitate samooh ke saath in hydroxil samoohon ko cover kiya gaya tha; or kuch yogicon main mishrit karnaa doosraa badlaav tha jo ki jhilli sataha main sakriyan poorak ko rokna hoga. mul "ansabstituted selulose" jhilli kaa ab vyapak prayog nahin hota he, jabaki selulose acitate or sanshodhit selulose apohak kaa abhi bhi istemaal kiya jaataa he. sallulosik jhilli yaa to kam pravaah yaa ucch pravaah vinyas main banaai ja sakati he, jo ki unke chhed ke aakaar par nirbhar karti he.
 jhilli ke ek anya samooh ko synthetic saamagree se banaaya jaataa he, jisamen bahulak kaa upyog kiya jaataa he, jaise poliyaralethraslphone, poliyamide, polvinalpirolidone, polikarbonate or poliecrilonitril. in synthetic jhilli ko ansabstituted selulose jhilli ke bajaae ek kam degree ke liye poorak ko sakriy kiya jaataa he. synthetic jhilli ko kam yaa ucch pravaah vinyas main banaaya ja saktaa he, lekin adhikansh ucch pravaah se bane hote hai.
 ek ekikrut rome chhidr ke nirmaan ke liye nanotechnology kaa istemaal sabase haal hi main ucch pravaah jhilli main kiya ja rahaa he. ucch pravaah jhilli kaa lakshya apekshaakrit bade anuon ko paas karnaa he jaise beta-2-microglobulin, lekin albumin ko paarit karnaa nahin he. har jhilli ke paas aakaar kii ek seemaa main chhidr hota he. jaise-jaise chhidr aakaar main vruddhi hoti he, kuch ucch pravaah apohak dilaset main rakt se albumin ko paarit karnaa shuru karte hai. iske liye ise avaanchhneeya mana jaataa he, yadhyapi ek prakaar ke vichaarkon ke samooh ne mana ki albumin ko baahar karnaa protein vaali uremic jehar ko dur karne kii drushti se phydemand ho saktaa he.
 ek ucch pravaah apohak kaa upyog rogi ke parinaamon main sudhaar karata he ki nahin yah kuch had tak vivaadaaspad he, lekin kai mahatvapurn adhyayanon ne suzav diya he ki yah ek chikitsiya laabh he. NIH poshit HEMO pareekshan ne uttarjivita or dialysis ke liye kam pravaah yaa ucch pravaah jhilli ke saath yadrichhik rogiyon main aspataal kii bhartee kii tulana kii. yadhyapi praathmik parinaam, ucch pravaah jhilli kaa upyog karte hue yadrichhik samooh main mahatvapurn sankhyikeey main nahin pahunch paaen, kai madhyamik parinaam ucch-pravaah main behatar the. . haal ke kochran vishleshan main yah nishkarsh nikaala gaya he ki parinaamon main jhilli laabh ke vikalp par abhi tak pradarshan nahin kiya gaya he. . yadrichhik pareekshan se ek europe sahyogi, MPO ke ek adhyayan, main marijon main mrityu shuruuaati apohak se tha jisamen ucch pravaah yaa nune pravaah jhilli kaa istemaal kiya gaya tha, jinhone ucch pravaah jhilli kaa istemaal uttarjivita main sudhaar laane ko mahatvaheen paaya gaya or nune cerum albumin str yaa madhumeh rogiyon main uttarjivita laabh paaya gaya.
 ucch-pravaah dialysis jhilli or/yaa aantrayik online hemodyphiltreshan bhi beta-2-microglobulin kii jatiltaaon ko kam karne main laabhadaayak ho saktaa he. kyonki beta-2-microglobulin ek bada anu he, jiskaa aanvik bhaar kareeb 11,600 dalton he, yah kam pravaah vaale dialysis jhilli ke madhyam se paar nahin hota he. beta-2-em ko ucch pravaah dialysis se hataaya jaataa he, lekin ise IHDF dwaara or adhik kushalta se hataaya jaataa he. kai varshon ke baad, hemodialysis vaale rogiyon main beta-2-em jamaav jatiltaaon kaa vikaas shuru ho jaataa he, jisamen shaamil he carpal tunnel syndrome, majja ulcer or jodon or anya utakon main is amiloid kaa sanchay. beta-2-em amiloidosis bahut gambhir jatiltaaon ko paidaa kar saktaa he, jaise ki spondiloarthropathy or yah aksar kandhe ke jod kii samasyaon se sambhddha rahaa he. europe or japan ke avlokan adhyayan ne suzav diya he ki dialysis mod main ucch-pravaah jhilli kaa prayog, yaa IHDF kaa prayog kam pravaah vaale niyamit dialysis kii tulana main beta-2-em jatiltaaon ko kam karata he. 
 apohak kai vibhinn aakaaron main aate hai. bade jhilli kshetra vaala ek bada apohak aamtaur par, ek chote apohak kii tulana main adhik viley ko nikaalata he, vishesh rup se ucch rakt pravaah daron par. sambandhit viley ke liye, yah jhilli pargamyata gunank K0 par nirbhar karata he. isliye apohak kshamta ko aam taur par K0A ke rup main vyakt kiya jaataa he - pargamyata gunank or kshetra ke utpaada ke rup main. adhikansh apohak kaa jhilli kshetra 0.8 se lekar 2.2 varg meter hota he or K0A kaa mulya aksar 500 se 1500 mL/min hota he. mL/min main vyakt K0A ko bahut hi ucch rakt or apohit pravaah daron par apohak ke adhiktam nikasi ke rup main mana ja saktaa he.
 apohak ko yaa to har ilaaj ke baad khaarij kiya ja saktaa he yaa punah istemaal kiya ja saktaa he. pun: upyog ke liye ek ucch stareey keetanushodhan kii vyapak prakriya kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. dubaara istemaal vaale apohak ko marijon ke bich saajha nahin kiya jaataa he. is bare main praarambhik vivaad tha ki kya apohak ko punah prayog karne se marij ke parinaam kharaab hote he. aaj sarvasammati yah he ki apohak kaa pun: upyog agar dhyaan se or sahi tareeke se kiya jaae to uskaa parinaam vaisaa hi hota he jaisaa ki apohak ke ekal upyog kaa hota he.
 canadai nefrology or takneekee sangh dwaara viksit nursing abhyaas kii siforeeshon se anukulit, sabase behtareen uplabdh saakshyon or nidaan abhyaas nirdesh-pustika ke aadhaar par, ek nefrology nurse ko nimna pradarshit karnaa chahie: 
 hemodialysis sanvahani abhigam:  prayek dialysis se pehle yaa baad main nalwran/grapht or baanh kaa akalan: abhigam pravaah, jatiltaayen kendriya shiraparak catheter kii jatilta kaa akalan: tip avasthaapan, nikasi sthaan, jatiltaayen kisi bhi chintaa ke bare main uchit swasthya dekhbhaal pradaata ko suchit karen. rogi ko nalpravan/grapht or nikaas site kii uchit safai ke bare main rogi ko shikshit karen, sankraman or jatilta ke lakshan kii jaankaree den.
 hemodialysis paryaaptta: aparyaapt dialysis ke sanket or lakshan ke liye rogi kaa satat moolyankan karnaa. aparyaapt dialysis ke sambhav kaarnon kaa moolyankan.paryaapt dialysis praapt karne ke mahatv ko rogi ko bataanaa.
 hemodialysis upachaar or jatiltaayen: hemodialysis se pehle, ke dauraan or baad main jatiltaaon ke bare main sir se lekar pair kii anguli tak shaareerik moolyankan or suraksha kaa akalan karnaa. sabase adhyatan prayogshaala parinaamon kii samiksha ke baad dialysis parche kii pushti karnaa or pradaan karnaa. rogi kii kisi bhi chintaa kaa nivaaran karnaa or shikshan antaraal ko dekhte hue rogi ko sikhaanaa.
 dawa prabandhan or sankraman niyantran abhyaas: dawa ke niyam ko viksit karne ke liye marij ke saath sahyog karen. unit protocol ke anusaar sankraman niyantran dishanirdeshon kaa paalan karen.
