 admiral yaa nausenadhyaksh kisi desh yaa rajya kii nausena ke sarvocch adhikari yaa adhikariyon ko kaha jaataa he. tulana ke liye yah pad thalsena ke 'general' yaa 'senapati' ke baraabar samajha jaataa he.
 'admiral' shabd kai roopon main vishwa kii bahut si bhashaaon main paaya jaataa he. mul rup se yah europe kii bhashaaon main arabi bhashaa ke 'amir al-bahar', yani 'samudr kaa sardaar', se aayaa he.
 adhiktar deshon kii nausenaon kii bardiyon main admiralon ke kandhon par chaar taron vaale yaa teen patali or ek moti dhari vaale chihn rahete hai. lekin neeche dekha ja saktaa he ki kuch rashtron main aisa nahin hota.
