 
 soa yaa soya ek laghu baramasi jadibuti he. yah jinas anetham kii ekmaatr prajati he, haalaanki kuch vanaspati vigyaniyon dwaara ise ek sambandhit jinas pusidenum grevolence si bi clark main vargeekrut kiya jaataa he. paashchaatya deshon main iskaa istemaal adhik he. bharat main bhi iskaa istemaal kiya jaataa he parantu simit maatra main, jaise uttar pradesh main methi ke patton kii sabzi ke saath soya bhi daalane kaa prachlan he. paidaavaar ke ilaake ke anusaar yah paudha sadabahaar yaa mausami ho saktaa he. 
 yah 40-60 semi kii oonchaai tak badhata he. iskaa tana patala or pattiyaan prayavarti, vibhajit or komal hoti hai jinaki lambaai 10-20 semi tak hoti he. phool pile se safed hote hai. 
 soa ke beej ek masaale ke rup main istemaal kiye jate hai or uski taaja yaa sukhi pattiyon kaa khaane main or jadi booti ke rup main upyog kiya jaataa he.
