 gulaag soviet sangh kii us sarkari sanstha kaa naam tha jo soviet sangh main shram-karawas chalaati thi. shram-karawas main bandiyon se baari mehnat karwaai jaati thi or aksar unhen hazaron mile dur siberia jaise nirjan kshetron main bheja jaataa tha. shram-karawas vahi dand pranali he jise puraani bhartiya kanoonee bhashaa main "qaid-e-bamushkkat" or anaupchaarik bhashaa main "pathar todane kii sazaa" kaha jaataa tha. gulaag main in dur-daraaj ilaaqon main adde bane hue the jahaan bandi kaam karte the. yah qaidi chote-mote choron se lekar soviet sangh kii sarkaar kaa virodh karne vaale rajnatik bandi hua karte the or gulaag pranali kaa prayog rajnatik virodh kuchalane ke liye kai dashakon tak kiya gaya. kuch logon ko to sarkaar ke kilaaf chutkula sunaane yaa kaam se kabhi anupasthit hone ke kaaran se bhi gulaag bhej diya jaataa tha.
 anumaan kiya gaya he ki soviet sangh ke daur main in gulaagon main kul lagbhag 1.4 karod logon ko bheja gaya. dwitiya vishwayuddh ke dauraan khaanaa kam padane se 5 lakh se adhik kaidiyon ne in gulaagon main apana dam tod diya. mana jaataa he ki 1929-1953 ke kaal main gulaagon main 16 lakh logon kii mrityu hui.
 gulaag naam roosi ke "glawanoe upravaleniye ispravityelno-trudoviha lageryey i coloneey" kaa ek prakaar kaa adivarni he. dhire-dhire soviet sangh kii puri shram-karawas pranali ko "gulaag" kaha jane laga, haalanki yah sansathan 1960 main band kar diya gaya or anya praadhikaranon ne is kii jagah le lee. alixiander soldenitsin naamak soviet lekhak ko ek gulaag main qaid rakhaa gaya tha or inhonen iskaa varnan apni pustak "gulaag dweepasmooh" main kiya jo europe or america main 1973 main chhapi. is main unhone gulaag karawas addon kii tulana samudr main bikhare hua dwipon se kii thi jahaan kaidiyon ko nirvasit kiya jaataa tha. angrejo ne bharat main jaise andaman dweep bhejkar kalapani kii sazaa dene kii shruwaat kii thi, usee tarah lekhak ne is upanyaas main gulaag naamak ek dweep kii kalpana kii he. is upanyaas ke prakaashan ke baad "gulaag" shabd vishwa bhar main mashhoor ho gaya.
 ain applebom, jinhonen gulaag pranali kaa gaharaa adhyayan kiya, kahati hai ki "bade myanon main, 'gulaag' kaa arth soviet zulmi vyavastha hi ban chukaa he, vah tareeqe jinhen kaidi kabhi 'maance pisane kii chakki' bulaayaa karte the: giraftaariyaan, kathor poochtacha, sard jaanavar le jane vaali gadiyon main safar, jabardastee mehnat karwaanaa, pariwaron kaa naash, varshon kaa nirvaasan, choti umr main bevajaah kii maut aadi."
