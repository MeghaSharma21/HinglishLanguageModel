 sahastradhara dehradun se maatr 16 kilometre kii duuri par raajpur gaanv ke paas sthit he. yahaan sthit gandhak jharnaa twachaa kii bimariyon kii chikitsa ke liye prasiddh he. isaki chikitsa sambandhi kuch anya upadeyataayen bhi hai. khaane-pine or anya tarah kii chijen bechney vaali dukaanon ke hone se yah jagah picnic ke lihaaj se behad upyukt he. kaaphi pariwaro ko yahaan par mouj-masti karte hue dekha ja saktaa he. anya logon kii tarah yah jagah bacchon ke bich bhi samaan rup se lokapriy he.
 yah puri jagah apane aap main ek ajooba he. pahari se girte hue jal ko prakritik tareeke se sanchit kiya gaya he. yahan se thodi dur ek pahari ke andar prakritik rup se taraashi hui kai choti choti guphaayen he, jo baahar se to spasht rup se dikhaai nahin detii kintu in gupha main jab pravesh karte he to dekhte hai ki gufaaon kii chat se avirat rimjhim halki barish kii bauchhaarein tapakati rahati he. bas yahi sahastradhara he. vahaan kai log apane polio grast bacchon ko gandhak ke pani main nahalaate hue najar aate hai. yah jagah choti si he, jahaan kuch ghante jarur ruk sakate hai. chaay pani naashte kaa bhi sucharu intazaam he. kuch hastkala kii chizon kii duukaane bhi he.
