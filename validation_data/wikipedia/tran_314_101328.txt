 
 Herpes simplex virus 1 Herpes simplex virus 2 
 visarpika cymplex vishaanu 1 tathaa 2, jise maanav visarpika vishaanu 1 tathaa 2 bhi kaha jaataa he, visarpika vishaanu parivaar- harpsviridi ke sadsy hai. HSV-1 tathaa -2 donon sarvyaapee evam sankramak hai. sankramit vyakti dwaara vishaanu ke utpaadan or prasar se ye failte hai.
 visarpika cymplex vishaanu ke sankraman ke lakshanon main shaamil hai twachaa athwa munh kii mukas jhilli, hont yaa janan angon par pani bhare fafole. visarpika rog main ghaav par papadi jamane ke baad ghaav thik hote hai. yadhyapi, neurotropic tathaa neuroinvaisive vishaanu hone ke kaaran HSV-1 tathaa -2 vaahak ke sharir main jad jamaa lete hai or vahaan ve supt rahete hai or sharir ke rog-pratirodhi tantra se bachakar tantrikaon ke koshikakaay main chhipe rahete hai. aarambhik yaa praathmik sankraman ke baad, kuch sankramit logon ko vishaanujanit punarskriyan yaa prakopon kii chhitput ghatnaon se guzarana padataa he. sankraman ke dauraan tantrika koshika main vishaanu sakriy ho jate hai or tantrika ke axon ke jariye twachaa main pahunchate hai or vahaan apni sankhya vruddhi karte hai or prasaarit hote hai jis kaaran nae phode paidaa hote hai.
 HSV sankraman kaa koi ilaaj nahin he lekin upachaar dwaara vishaanu ke failane main kami laai ja sakati he.
 HSV-1 tathaa -2 kaa prasar kshaitij rup se hota he jab kisi sankramangrast vyakti, jisaki twachaa, laar athwa janan angon ke srav se vishaanu baahar fail rahaa hota he, ke sampark main koi swasthy vyakti aata he. iske prasar kii sambhaavna tab adhik ho jaati he jab sankramit vyakti kii twachaa or angon par fafole nikle hote hai, haalanki yadi fafole dikhaai na padate hon tab bhi iske failane kii sambhaavna bani rahati he or adhiktar HSV-2 sankraman alakshanik jharan ke karan hota he. vyakti main HSV-1 kaa pravesh praaia: mukh dwaara bachapan main hota he, kintu yah yon rup se bhi sanchaarit ho saktaa he. HSV-2 mulat: yon rup se sanchaarit hone vaala sankraman he.
 donon vishaanu kaa sanchaaran mata dwaara bachche main iske janm ke pehle se bhi ho saktaa he. yadi mata main is vishaanu ke lakshan nahin paae gaye hon athwa prasav ke dinon main fafole nahin dikhaai pade hon to sankraman kii sambhaavna kam ho jaati he. HSV ke kuch rup shishu ke liye jaanaleva ho sakate hai kyonki shishu kaa vikasshil rog-pratirodhi tantra vishaanu se bachaav kar sakane main asafal rahata he jiske kaaran mastishk shoth hota he jisase mastishk kshatigrast ho jaataa he.
 HSV ke shuruaati sankraman se utpann hon vaale lakshan praaia: baad main hone vaale prakope se adhik gambhir hote hai kyonki sharir ko pratipind ke nirmaan kaa avasar nahin mil paata. is pratham prakope main aputik meningitis ke viksit hone kii kam sambhaavna hoti he.
 jantu visarpika vishaanuon main kuch samaan gun paae jate hai. visarpika vishaanuon kii sanrachana apekshaakrit ek deergh dwi-ladi yukt, raikhik DNA jinom se bani hoti he jo ek vishafalkeey protein pinjar main band rahata he jise capcid kehete hai jo enwelp kahalaane vaale lipid ke ek dwi-str main lipta hota he. enwelp capcid se ek tegument dwaara judaa rahata he. is sampurna kan ko virian kehete hai. prayek HSV-1 tathaa HSV-2 main kam se kam 74 jin jinom ke andar paae jate hai, yadhyapi, jin samoohan ke bare main kiye gaye akalan ke anusaar lagbhag 84 vishisht protein koding jin 94 anumaanit ORFs dwaara hote hai. ye jin vishaanu ke capcid, tegument tathaa enwelp ke nirmaan main shaamil anek prakaar ke proteinon ke kuta banaate hai or saath hi vishaanu ke pratikruti tathaa sankramakata ko niyantrit karte hai. ye jin or unke kaary neeche di gai talika main sankshipt rup main darshaae gaye hai.
 HSV-1 tathaa HSV-2 ke jinom jatil hote hai or unamem do vishisht kshetra paae jate hai jinhen deergh vishisht kshetra tathaa laghu vishisht kshetra kehete hai. 74 gyaat ORFs main se UL main 56 vishaanu-jin hote hai jabaki US main keval 12 vishaanu hote hai. HSV jeenon kaa pratilipikaran sankramit parposhi ke RNA polimerej II dwaara utprerit hota he. thik baad vaale aarambhik jin, jo ‘purv ’ evam ‘vilambit ’ kii abhivyakti ko niyantrit karne vaale proteinon ke kuta nirmit karte hai, sankraman ke baad sabase pehle abhivyakt hote hai. aarambhik abhivyakti ke baad DNA pratikruti tathaa kuch enwelp glico proteinon ke nirmaan main shaamil anzimon kaa sanshleshan hota he. vilambit jeenon kii abhivyakti ant main hoti he. jeenon kaa yah samooh mukhya rup se un proteinon ke kuta banaate hai jo virian kan kaa nirmaan karte hai.
  ke paanch proteinon dwaara vishaanu capcid UL6, UL18, UL35, UL38 tathaa mukhya capcid protein UL19 kaa nirmaan kiya jaataa he.
 HSV kaa parposhi kii koshika main pravesh kii prakriya ke dauraan anek glycoproteinon kii, aavaran main band vishaanu ke prishth par grahiyon ke saath, antarkriya hoti he. vishaanu kan ko ghere rahane vaala aavaran jab koshika prishth par vishisht grahiyon ke sampark main aata he to parposhi kii koshika jhilli ke saath sanlayit ho jaataa he or ek dwaara athwa chhidr kaa nirmaan karata he jisase hokar vishaanu parposhi kii koshika main pravesh karata he.
 HSV ke pravesh ke anuvarthi charan anya vishaanuon jaise hi hote hai. pratham charan main, vishaanu ke poorak grahi or koshika prishth dwaara vishaanu or koshika jhilliyon ko ek duusare ke sampark main laayaa jaataa he. madhyavarti charan main, donon jhilliyon kaa aapas main vilay ho jaataa he or hemiphyuzan charan kaa nirmaan hota he. antat:, ek sthai pravesh randhra nirmit hota he jisase hokar vishaanu ke aavaran ke andar ke padaarth parposhi kii koshika main pravisht hota he.visarpika vishaanu kii sthiti main, aarambhik antarkriya tab hoti he jab vishaanu aavaran kaa glycoprotein jise glycoprotein C kehete hai koshika prishth ke kan jise hepairan sulphate kehete hai, ke saath jud jaataa he. ek anya glycoprotein jise glycoprotein D kehete hai vishesh rup se teen gyaat pravesh grahiyon main se ek ke saath jud jaataa he. inamen shaamil hai visarpika vishaanu pravesh madhyasth, nectin-1 tathaa 3-O sulphate hepairan sulphate. grahi dwaara parposhi koshika ke saath ek sabal or sthir sangyojan pradaan kiya jaataa he. in antarkriyaaon ke parinamswaroop jhilli ke prishth ek duusare ke nikat aa jate hai or vishaanu ke aavaran main upasthit anya glycoproteinon ko koshika prishth ke anya anuon ke saath antarkriya karne kaa avasar miltaa he.HVEM ke saath sanyukt ho jane par gD dwaara apana vinyas badal liya jaataa he or vishaanu ke glycoprotein H tathaa L ke saath isaki antarkriya hoti he or ek sankul kaa nirmaan hota he. in jhilli proteinon kii antarkriya ke parinamswaroop hemifgauzan charan kaa aarambh hota he. iske baad, gB kii gH/gL sankul ke saath antarkriya ke falswaroop vishaanu capcid ke liye ek pravesh randhra kaa nirmaan hota he. parposhi kii koshika ke prishth par glycoprotein B kii glicoseminoglaican ke saath antarkriya hoti he.
 vishaanu capcid ke koshikeey koshikadravya main pravesh karne ke baad yah koshikeey cendrak main hastantarit kar diya jaataa he. cendrak par sthit cendrak pravesh chhidr se sanlagn hone ke baad, capcid portal ke jariye capcid apni DNA saamagree asweekaar kar detaa he. capcid portal kaa nirmaan portal protein ke 12 pratiyon, UL6 se hota he, jo ek ring ke rup main vyavasthit rahata he; jisamen emino amla kaa lucin zipper kram samahit hota he, jo unhen ek-duusare se chipakane kii anumati detaa he. prayek ikosahedral capcid main ek ekal portal hota he, jo ek sheersh par sthit rahata he.DNA, capcid se ek ekal raikhik khand main baahar nikalataa he.
 HSV, koshika sataha par upasthit pratijan ke MHC varg I ke pratirakshi tantra kii upasthiti ke saath interphes ke jariye apavanchit ho jaataa he. yah HSV dwaara ICP-47 ke srav se prerit TAP parivahak ke avarodhan ke jariye sampann hota he. TAP, koshika sataha par CD8+ CTLs dwaara pahachaane jane hetu golji upkaran hokar parivahit kiye jane se purv, MHC varg I anu kii akhandta ko banaae rakhata he. CTL hetu citosolic protein ke abhigrahan ko rokkar, ICP-47 is akhandta ko avyavasthit karata he or is prakaar CTL vinaash se batch nikalataa he.
 kisi koshika ke sankraman ke pashchaat, twarit aarambhik, aarambhik tathaa vilambit naamak visarpika vishaanu proteinon kaa nirmaan hota he. visarpika vishaanu kul ke ek anya sadsy, caposi sarcoma-sambhddha visarpika vishaanu par pravaah cytometries ke prayog dwaara kiye shodh se ek atirikt apghaty charan, dilade-let kii sambhaavna kaa sanket miltaa he. apghaty sankraman ke ye charan, visheshkar let lytic, vilambtaa charan se bhinn hota he. HSV-1 kii sthiti main vilambta ke dauraan koi protein utpaada nahin miltaa, jabaki ve apghaty chakra ke dauraan dekhe jate hai.
 aarambhik pratilipikrut proteinon kaa upyog vishaanu ke anuvanshik pratikruti ke niyantran main hota he. koshika main pravesh karne par, ek α-TIF protein vishaanu kan se judata he tathaa twarit-aarambhik pratilipi main madad karata he. vaayran host shatof protein, vishaanu pratikruti ke liye atyant mahatvapurn hota he. yah anzime mejabaan main protein ke sanshleshan ko roktaa he, mejabaan mRNA ko vikhandit karata he, vishaanu pratikruti main madad karata he, tathaa vishaanu protein kii anuvanshik abhivyakti ko niyantrit karata he. vishaanu jinom sheeghra hi cendrak kii ore gaman karata he, par VHS protein koshikadravya main hi rahata he.
 vilambit proteinon kaa prayog capcid ke nirmaan main hota he tathaa ye vishaanu kii sataha ke abhigraahak hote hai. jinom, kor tathaa capcid samet vishaanu kanon kii packaging koshika ke cendrak main hoti he. yahaan vishaanu jinom ke kanketemar vidalan dwaara pruthak kar die jate hai tathaa purv-nirmit capcid par sthit ho jate hai. HSV-1 main praathmik tathaa dwitiya enwelopment kii ek prakriya hoti he. praathmik enwelp kii prapti koshika kii aantarik jhilli main mukulan dwaara hoti he. yah tab bahya koshikeey jhilli ke saath judata he, jahaan koshikadravya main nagn capcid mukt hota he. vishaanu apana antim enwelp kii prapti koshikadravyi riktikaaon ke mukulan dwaara praapt karti he.
 HSVs ek supt kintu nirantar rup main maujuud rahata he, jise prasupt sankraman kehete hai, visheshkar tantrikeey gandika main. HSV-1 praayah trygeminal gangliya ko sankramit karata he, jabaki HSV-2 secral gangliya ko sankramit karata he, par yah pruthakkaran sampurna nahin hota. kisi koshika ke prasupt sankraman ke dauraan HSVs, latency associated transcript RNA se judi prasuptavastha ko abhivyakt karata he. LAT ko mezbaan koshika ke niyantrak ke rup main janaa jaataa he tathaa yah koshika kii prakritik mrityu kriyavidhiyon main hastkshep karata he. mejabaan koshikaon ko barkaraar karte hue, LAT abhivyakti vishaanuon kaa ek bhandaaran sthal sangrakshit karata he, jo unhen agle sankraman ke liye punrariti kii anumati detaa he.
 tantrikaon main paaya jane vaala protein visarpika vishaanu DNA ke saath bandha saktaa he tathaa prasuptavastha ko niyantrit karata he. visarpika vishaanu DNA main ek protein ICP4 ke liye ek jin samahit rahata he, jo HSV-1 main apghaty sankraman se sambhddha hota he. ICP4 ke jin ke chaaron ore ghire tatva ek protein ke saath bandhate hai, jise maanav tantrikeey protein, ‘neuronal restrictive silencing factor’ yaa ‘human represar element silencing tronscripshan factor’ kehete hai. vishaanu DNA tattvon ke saath bandhane par, is jin se pratilipi kii pahal ko rokane ke liye ICP4 jin kram ke upar histone diesitalizeshan hota he, jisase apghaty chakra main shaamil anya vishaanu kii pratilipi ruk jaati he. anya HSV protein ICP4 protein sanshleshan ke nishedh ko mukt karata he. ICP0, ICP4 jin se NRSF ko apaghatit karata he or is prakaar vishaanu DNA ko niskriy banaata he.
 anya bimariyon, jaise zukam tathaa influenza, daad, bhaavnaatmak tathaa shaareerik tanaav, tej dhoop kaa sampark, jathreey pareshaaniyaan yaa chot evam masik-dharm dwaara vishaanu ko punahsakriy kiya ja saktaa he.
 visarpika cimplex vishaanu ke upachaar hetu adhik jaankaree ke liye visarpika cimplex dekhen.
 visarpika vishaanu jivan bhar ke liye sankraman utpann karata he tathaa vishaanu ko vartmaan main sharir se unmoolit nahin kiya ja saktaa. upachaar main praayah shaamil hai saamaanya-uddeshy vaali vishaanurodhi davaaiyaan jo vishaanu pratikruti main hadtakshep karti hai, jisase prakope se sambandhit nuksaan kii shaareerik gambhirtha kam hoti he tathaa anya vyaktiyon main sancharan kii sambhaavna kam ho jaati he. kamjor aabaadi vaale rogiyon ke adhyayan main yah sanket mila ki ecyclovir, vailacyclovir jaise vishaanurodhiyon ke dainik prayog se HSV-2 kii 60-80% tak kami ho sakati he tathaa HSV-2 ke sancharan kaa khatra aadha ho saktaa he.
 prayogshaala shodh ne sanket diya he ki yon visarpika ke liye ghrit kumari prabhavi ho sakati he.
 tike par shodh jari he. ek vikasvaadi pracheen sambandh ke dauraan maanav mejabaanon ke prati HSVs ke anukoolnon se ab tak ek prabhavi tike ke vikaas ke prayaason ko jhatka laga he. vartmaan samay main viksit kiya ja rahaa ek sabase ullekhneey tikaa he immuno VEXHSV2, jo Biovex se nirmit he.
 HSV-1 tathaa alzimer rog ke saath ek sambhavit sambandh kaa varsh 1979 main pataa lagaaya gaya. kuch jin vividhta kii upasthiti main HSV-1 tantrika tantra ko kshatigrast karata paaya gaya he tathaa yah vyakti main alzimer rog hone kii sambhaavna ko badhaata he. vishaanu lypoprotein ke ghatakon tathaa abhigrahakon ke saath antahkriya karata he, jisase alzimer rog utpann ho saktaa he. yah anusandhan HSVs ko alzimer se sarvaadhik spasht rup se judaa rogaanu ke rup main maanata he. jin aleel kii upasthiti ke bina HSV-1 kisi prakaar kii tantrikeey kshati nahin karata yaa alzimer ke khatre ko nahin badhaata.
