 
 mahua ek bhartiya ushnkaribandheey vriksh he jo uttar bharat ke maidani ilaakon or jangalon main bade paimaane par paaya jaataa he. iskaa vaigyanik naam madhuka longpholia he. yah ek teji se badhane vaala vriksh he jo lagbhag 20 meter kii oonchaai tak bad saktaa he. iske pattey aamtaur par varsh bhar hare rahete hai. yah padpon ke sapotacy parivaar se sambandh rakhata he. yah shushk paryaavaran ke anukool dhal gaya he, yah madhya bharat ke ushnkaribandheey parnpaati van kaa ek pramukh ped he.
 garm kshetron main isaki kheti iske snigdh bijon, phulon or lakdi ke liye kii jaati he. kachche falon kii sabji bhi banati he. pake hue falon kaa guda khaane main meetha hota he. prati vriksh uski aayu ke anusaar salanaa 20 se 200 kilo ke bich bijon kaa utpaadan kar sakate hai. iske tel kaa prayog twachaa kii dekhbhaal, sabun yaa disergent kaa nirmaan karne ke liye or vanaspati makkhan ke rup main kiya jaataa he. eendhan tel ke rup main bhi iskaa prayog kiya jaataa he. tel nikalne ke baad bache iske khal kaa prayog jaanwaron ke khaane or urvarak ke rup main kiya jaataa he. iske sookhe phulon kaa prayog mewa ke rup main kiya ja saktaa he. iske phulon kaa upyog bharat ke ushnkaribandheey kshetron main sharaab ke utpaadan ke liye bhi kiya jaataa he. kai bhaagon main ped ko uske aushadhiy gunon ke liye upyog kiya jaataa he, isaki chaal ko aushadhiy prayojanon ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he. kai adivasee samudaayon main isaki upyogita kii vajah se ise pavitra mana jaataa he.
 == vriksh ==
 mahua bharatvarsh ke sabhi bhaagon main hota he or pahaado par teen hajaar foot kii oonchaai tak paaya jaataa he. isaki pattiyaan paanch saat angul chaudee, das baarah angul lambii or donon ore nukili hoti hai. pattiyon kaa upree bhaag halake rang kaa or peeth bhure rang kii hoti he. himalay kii tarai tathaa punjab ke atirikt saare uttareey bharat tathaa dakshin main iske jangal paae jate hai jinamein vah svachhand rup se ugta he. par punjab main yah sivaay baagon ke, jahaan log ise lagaate hai or kahin nahin paaya jaataa. iskaa ped unchaa or chhatnar hota he or daaliyaan chaaron or failati he. yah ped tees-chalis haath unchaa hota he or sab prakaar kii bhoomi par hota he. iske phool, fal, beej lakdi sabhi chijen kaam main aati he. iskaa ped vis pachis varsh main phoolne or phalne lagata or saikadon varsh tak phulataa faltaa he.
 ==phool ==
 isaki pattiyaan phoolne ke pehle phaguna chait main jhad jaati hai. pattiyon ke jhadane par isaki daaliyon ke siron par kaliyon ke guchchhe nikalne lagate hai jo kurchi ke aakaar ke hote he. ise mahue kaa kuchiana kehete hai. kaliyaan badhati jaati he or unke khilne par kosh ke aakaar kaa safed phool nikalataa he jo gudara or donon ore khulaa hua hota he or jiske bheetar jire hote hai. yahi phool khaane ke kaam main aata he or 'mahua' kehalaata he. mahue kaa phool bees vaais din tak lagaataar tapakataa he. mahue ke phool main chini kaa praayah aadha ansh hota he, isi se pashu, pakshi or manushya sab ise chaav se khate hai. iske ras main visheshta yah hoti he ki usamen rotiyaan puri kii bhanti pakaai ja sakati hai. iskaa prayog hare or sookhe donon roopon main hota he. hare mahue ke phool ko kuchalakar ras nikaalkar puriyaan pakaai jaati hai or piskar use aate main milakar rotiyaan banaate hai. jinhen 'mahuari' kehete hai. sookhe mahue ko bhunkar usamen piyaar, poste ke dane aadi milakar kutate hai. is rup main ise lata kehete hai. ise bhigokar or piskar aate main milakar 'mahuari' banaai jaati he. hare or sookhe mahue log bhunkar bhi khate hai. garibon ke liye yah bada hi upyogi hota he. yah gauno, bhaison ko bhi khilaayaa jaataa he jisase ve moti hoti hai or unka doodh badhata he. isse sharaab bhi khinchi jaati he. mahue kii sharaab ko sanskrit main 'madhvi' or aajkal ke ganvara 'tharra' kehete hai. mahue kaa phool bahut dinon tak rahata he or bigadataa nahin.
 ==fal== 
 iskaa fal paraval ke aakaar kaa hota he or 'kalendi' kehalaata he. ise chhil ubalkar or beej nikaalkar tarkari bhi banaai jaati he.
 fal ke bich main ek beej hota he jisase tel nikalataa he. vaidyak main mahue ke phool ko madhur, sheethal, dhaatu- vardhak tathaa dah, pitt or baat kaa naashak, hriday ko hitkar auki baari likhaa he. iske fal ko sheethal, shukrajanak, dhaatu or balabandhak, vaat, pitt, trupa, dah, shwaas, kshayi aadi ko dur karanevaala mana he. chaal raktpitnaashaka or vranshodhak maani jaati he. iske tel ko kaph, pitt or dahnaashak or saar ko bhoot-baadha-nivarak likhaa he.
