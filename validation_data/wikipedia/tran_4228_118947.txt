 jab tak hai jaan ek aagaamee bollywood film jo ki prakhyat filmkaar yash chopra dwaara nirdeshit he. is film kaa nirmaan aditya chopra ne yash raaj films ke binar tale kiya he. shahrukh khan, katrina caff or anoushka sharma pramukh bhoomikaaon main nazar aa rahe hai. yah khan or caff kii saath main pehali film he or khan or sharma kii duusari. unki pehali film rab ne bana di jodi hai. yah yash chopra kii shahrukh khan ke saath chauthi film hai. or yashji ne is film ke madhyam se aath saal ke pashchaat punah nirdesan kiya hai.
 sangeet eaar rahamaan dwaara rachit he, jabaki geet gulzaar dwaara likhe gaye hai. film 13 navambar ko diwaali ke avasar par pradarshit hogi.
