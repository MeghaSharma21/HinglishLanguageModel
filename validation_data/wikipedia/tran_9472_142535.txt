 iareneses-1ji 1e, 1bi, 1si, 1di,1i, va 1eff ke baad saatvaan bhartiya nauvahan upgrah he. yah upgrah iareneses series kaa saatvaan or aakhri upgrah tha parantu 1e upgrah kii parmaanu ghadi karaab hone ke baad iareneses-1ech ko aathve upgrah ke taur par launch kiya gaya. yah upgrah bhartiya kshetra main nevigation sevaayen pradaan karegaa.
 upgrah ko bhoosamakalik kaksha main sthapit kiya gaya. ise 28 april 2016 ko satish dhawan antariksh centre ke pehle launch pad se dhruviya upgrah prakshepan yaan ke madhyam se sriharikota se prakshepit kiya gaya.
 isse bharat ke paas apni swatantra sthaneey nauvahan va disha-suchak pranali hogi. pehle kii tarah hi pieselvi ke exel varjan kaa prayog kiya gaya jisamen 12 ton ke 6 strap on boosters lage hue the.kareeb 20 minute kii udaan main is yaan ne 1,425 kilogram vajani iareneses-1ji upgrah ko 497.8 kilometre kii oonchai par kaksha main sthapit kar diya.iske prakshepan ke saath hi bharat kshetreey upgrah nauvahan pranali rakhane vaale vishisht deshon ke club main shaamil ho gaya he.
 upgrah ko 12 varshon kii avadhi tak kaaryshil rahane hetu dizine kiya gaya he upgrah kaa lift-of dravyaman 1,425 kilogram or shushk dravyaman 598 kilogram he. iske pe-lod main ek si-band transponder shaamil he jo upgrah kii avasthiti sateekta se nirdhaarit karne main sakasham he. yah upgrah L-5 tathaa es-band main kaary karegaa or isamen rubidium parmaanu ghadi lagaai gai he. pramukh avyavon, udaahranarth is ghadi, ke niyantran hetu isamen vishesh thermal niyatran system lagaaye gayi hai. isamen maujuud do solar panel 1660W urja utpann karenge or saath hi lithium-aayan 90A-hr bydaree kaa istemaal hua he. The upgrah ko 497.8 kilometre kii oonchaai par isaki kaksha main sthapit kiya gaya he jo 129.5 ansh purvi deshaantar ke sahare he.
 prakshepan ko pradhanmantri narendra modi ne bhi webcast ke madhyam dekha. unhone isro ke vaigyanikon ko badhai di or kaha, "is safal prakshepan ke saath, ham apni hi praudyogiki dwaara sanchalit raaston kaa nirdhaaran karenge. yah vaigyanikon dwaara logon ke liye ek mahaan uphaar he."
