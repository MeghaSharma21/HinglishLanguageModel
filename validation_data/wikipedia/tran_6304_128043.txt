 yahan kii aadhikarik str kii bhashaayen bangla or angrezi he. tathaa bangladesh ke kisi bhi anya kshetra kii tarah hi, yahan kii bhi pramukh maukhik bhashaa or maatrubhaasha bangla he. bengali ke alaava angrezi bhashaa bhi kai logon dwaara jaani or samajhi jaati he, jabaki saanskrithik or aitihaasik nikatataa tathaa bhashaai samanta ke kaaran, kai log simit maatra main hindustani bhi samajhane main sakasham hai. yahan kaa bahusankhyak dharm, islam he, jabaki pramukh alpsankhyak dharm, hindu dharm he. janasankhyikeek rup se, yahan, islam ke anuyai, aabaadi ke ausatan 91% ke kareeb he. shesh jansankhya pramukhtah hindu dharm kii anuyai he. yah maykhyatah ek gramin kshetra he, or adhikansh aabaadi graamy ilaakon main rahati he.
