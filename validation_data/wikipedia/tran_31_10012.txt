 sonam yadav bharat kii mahila mukkebaaz hai.
 sonam yadav gramin prishthbhumi se sambandh rakhti hai. 5 feet 11 inch lambii mukkebaaz sonam yadav ne san 2006 va 2007 main hui junior rashtriya mahila mukkebaaji main lagaataar do baar swarna padak jite hai. isi tarah varsh 2008 va 2009 main yojit senior mahila mukkebaaji main bhi unhone lagaataar do baar swarna padak jite hai. iske alaava sonam yadav ne faderation cap mukkebaaji nainital main 75 kilogram bhaar varg main swarna jeeta he.
