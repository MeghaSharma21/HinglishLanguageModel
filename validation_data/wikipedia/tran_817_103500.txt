 woolfsan chikitsa kendra israel ke holon main sthit ek aspataal he. yah aspataal tel aviv jile ke dakshini tel aviv mahanagareey kshetra main sthit he jiske aasapaas ke kshetron main lagbhag paanch lakh nivasee rahete hai. yah israel kaa nauvaan sabase bada aspataal he. vulfasan chikitsa kendra kii sthapana vulfasan sansthaapan kii sahaayta se hui thi or iskaa naam shrimati adith spacterman vulfasan ke naam par padaa tha jo shri izac vulfasan kii patni thim.
 holon kaa chikitsa kendra aam janata ke liye 1980 main dakshini tel aviv-jaafaa seemaa par kholaa gaya tha. tabase lekar ab tak is aspataal main uplabdh bistaron kii sankhya 342 se badhkar 2007 main 650 ho chuki he jiske saath atirikt 30 bistar bahari rogiyon ke liye hai.
