 bahut pracheen kaal main arya jati apane pracheen awaas "arya vajeh" main rahaa karti thi jo sudur uttari pradesh main avasthit tha "jahaan kaa varsh ek din ke baraabar" hota tha. us sthaan ko nishchayatmak rup se batala paanaa kathin he. bal gangadhar tilak ne apane granth "di arctic home" main is bhoomi ko uttari dhruv pradesh main batalaaya he jahaan se aaryon ne pamir kii shrikhala main pravaas kiya. bahut samay paryant ek sugathit jan ke rup main ve ek sthaan main rahe, ek hi bhashaa bolte, vishwaason, ritiyon or paramparaon kaa samaan rup se paalan karte rahe. jansankhya main vruddhi tathaa uttari pradesh ke sheet tathaa anya kaarnon ne unki shrinkhalaa chhin-bhinn kar di. aryajan ke vividh kulon main do kulon ke log, jo aage chalakar bhartiya or irani shakhaon ke naam se vikhyath hue, purvi iran main deergh kaal tak or nikatatam sampark main rahe. aage chalakar ek jatthe ne hindookush kii parvamala paar kar punjab ke lagbhag 2000 i.pus. pravesh kiya. shesh jan aaryon kii adibhumi kii parampara kaa nirvaah karte hue iran main hi rah gaye. avestas, visheshat:avesta ke gathasahitya or vaidic sanskrit main nikatatam samanta vartmaan he. bhed keval dhvanyatmak or niruktagat hai. do bahan bhashaaon ke vyakaran or rachanakram main bhi nikat samya he.
 iran or bharat donon hi deshon main lekhan ke avishkaar ke purv maukhik parampara vidyaman thi. avesta granthon main maukhik shabdon, chhandon, swaron, bhashyon evam prashnon or uttaron kaa ullekh hua he. ek granth main ahurmajd apane sandeshvahak zarathustra ko vaani kii sampatti pradaan karte hai kyonki "maanav jati main keval unhone hi daivi sandesh praapt kiya tha jinhen maanvon ke bich le janaa tha." gyaan ke devtaa ne unhen sachcha "athravan" kaha he jo sari raat dhyanavasthit rahakar or adhyayan main samay bitaakar siikhe gaye paath ko janata ke bich le jate hai. pracheen bharat ke brahmanon kii tarah athravan hi pracheen iran main shiksha tathaa dharmopadesh ke ekamatra adhikari samajhe jate the. in puraahiton main vanshanugat rup main dharmagranthon kii maukhik parampara chali aayaa karti thi.
 paigambar ke stvan gaathaayen gaatha main, jo bolchaal kii bhashaa thi, paae jate hai or janashruti tathaa shastreey sahitya ke anusaar zarathustra ko anek granthon kaa rachayita batalaaya jaataa he. arab itihaaskaaron kaa kathan he ki ye grath 12,000 gaay ke charmon par ankit the. pracheen irani tathaa aadhunik parsi lekhakon ke anusaar pangambar ne 21 "nask" athwa granth likhe the. aisa kaha jaataa he ki samrat vishtasp ne do yathatathy anulekh in granthon kaa karakar do pustakaalayon main sangruhit kiya tha. ek anulekhwali saamagree agni main bhasma ho gai jab parsapalis kaa rajaprasaad sikandar ne jala diya or duusari anulekh kii samagree saahitiyik vivaranon ke aadhaar par vijeta sainik apane desh ko lete gaye jahaan uskaa anuwaad unani bhashaa main hua. praarambhik sasaani kaal main sangrahit ye bikhare hue granth phir saatvin shatee main irani samrajya ke hraas ke kaaran vilupt hokar kul sahitya vartmaan samay main lagbhag 83,000 padyon main uplabdh rah gaya he jab ki moulik padyon kii sankhya 20,00,000 thim, jiske bare main plini kaa kathan he ki mahaan darshanik harmippas ne isa kii shatabdi ke prarambh se teen shatee purv adhyayan kar daalaa tha.
 avesta bhashaa kaa dhire-dhire akhaamani samrajya ke hraas ke kaaran utpann hue iran main uthal-puthal ke kaaran hraas prarambh ho gaya. jab uskaa prachaar bilkul lupt ho gaya, avesta granthon ke anuwaad or bhashya "pahalavi" bhashaa main prastut kiye jane lage. is bhashaa kii utpatti isi kaal main hui jo sasaniyon kii rajbhasha ban gai. un bhashyon ko pahalavi main xend kaha jaataa he or vyakhyaayein ab "avestak-u-xend athwa avesta tathaa uske bhashya ke naam se vikhyath hai. viparyay se isi ko "xend-avesta" kaha gaya. anumaan kiya gaya he ki dharmik vishyon par rachit pahalavi granth, jo vinaash se bache rahe unki shabdasankhya 4,46,000 ke lagbhag hogi.
 pahalavi kaa prachaar aadhunik parsi varnmaala ke prarambh se bilkul kam ho gaya. uskaa likhit swaroop arya evam sami banaavat kaa mishran tha. sami shabdon ko hataakar unke sthanon main unka irani paryaayavachi shabd rakhkar uskaa sadharnikan kiya gaya tha. kalantar main pahalavi granthon ko jab samajhane kii aavashyaktaa kaa anubhav kiya gaya, huzavar shabdon ko hataakar unke sthaan par irani paryaayavachi rakhkar duruh pahalavi bhashaa bhi sidhi banaai gai. apekshaakrit saral kii gai bhashaa or aage rachit bhashya evam vyakhyaayein "paznd" ke naam se vikhyath hui. paznd ke granth avesta varnmaalaon main ankit hue jis prakaar iran main arabi varnmaala ke saath pahalavi lipi kaa hraas hua.
 paznd bhashaa hi aage chalakar pahalavi tathaa aadhunik farsi ke bich kii kadi bani. antim zarathustra samrajya ke hraas ke ananthar vijetaon kii arabi lipi ne avesta kii pahalavi lipi ko utkshipt kar diya. arabi akshar aadhunik farsi varnmaala ke akshar maan liye gaye jiskaa prachaar hua. granthrachana jab avesta main hoti thi to use "paznd" kehete the or jab pustak arabi aksharon main lipibaddh hone lagi, use "parsi" kehane lag gaye.
 avesta ke jo granth paigambar ke anuyaayiyon ke paas avshisht hai apane sami rup main paae jate hai. ve aise aksharon main milte hai jo sasaani pahalavi se liye gaye hai, jinka mul aadhaar sambhavat: pracheen armek varnmaala kaa koi na koi prakaar he. yah lipi dahini ore se bai ore ko likhi jaati he or isamen praaia: 50 bhinn chihnon kaa samavesh paaya jaataa he.
 zarathustra mataavlambee iran lagbhag paanch shatee paryant silucid or parthian shasno ke antargat rahaa. dharmik granthon kii maukhik vanshakramanugat parampara ne luptpraaya granthon ke puraruddhar ke kaary ko saral kar diya. sasaani samrajya ke sansthaapak ardshir ne vidvan purohit tansar ke bikhare hue sutron ko, jo maukhik rup se prachlit the, ek pramanik sangrah main nibaddh karne kaa aadesh kiya tha. granthon kii khoj shapur dwitiya ke rajtvkaal paryant hoti rahi jisamen prasiddh dastur adrabad maharspand kii sahaayta saraahneey he.
 avesta yug kii rachanaaon main prarambh se lekar 200 i. tak tithikram se aanevaali sarvapratham rachanaayen "gaathaayen" hai jinaki sankhya paanch he. avesta sahitya ke ve hi mul granth hai jo paigambar ke bhakthisutra hai or jinamein unka maanav kaa tathaa aitihaasik rup pratibimbit he, na ki kaalpanik vyakti kaa, jaisaa ki baad main kuch lekhakon ne apane agyaan ke kaaran unhen abhivyakt karne kii cheshtaa kii he. unki bhashaa baad ke sahitya kii apeksha adhik aarsh he or vaakyavinyas, style evam chand main bhi bhinn he kyonki unki rachana kaa kaal vidwaanon ne pracheenatam vaidic mantron kii rachana kaa samay nirdhaarit kiya he. nape tule swaron main rache hone ke kaaran ve saswar paath ke liye hi hai. unamem na keval gudh aadhyatmik rahasyanubhootiyaan vartmaan hai, ve vishaypradhan hi na hokar vyaktipradhan bhi hai jinamein paigambar ke vyaktitv kii vishesh rup se charchaa kii gai he, unke ishwar ke saath tadatmya sthapit karne or us vishesh avasthaa ke parigyaan ke liye vaanchhneeya aashaa, niraashaa, harsh, vishaad, bhay, utsaah tathaa apane matanuayiyon ke prati sneh or shatruon se sangharsh aadi bhaavon kaa bhi samavesh paaya jaataa he. yadhyapi prithvi par k manushya kaa jivan vaasana se ghira hua he, paigambar ne is prakaar shiksha di he ki yadi manushya vaasana kaa nirodh kar satvik jivan vyateet kare to uskaa kalyaan avashyabhavi he.
 gaathaon ke baad "yasn" aate hai jinamein 72 adhyay hai jo "kushti" ke 72 sutron ke pratik hai. kushti kamarband ke rup main buni jaati he jise prayek zarathustra mataavlambee "sudra" athwa pavitra kurta ke saath dharna karata he jo dharm kaa bahya pratik he. yasn utsav ke avasar par puja sambandhi "visparad" naamak 23 adhyay kaa granth padhaa jaataa he. iske baad sankhya main 23 "yashton" kaa sangayan kiya jaataa he jo stuti ke gaan hai or jinke vishay ahumrajd tathaa amesh-spent, jo daivi gyaan evam ishwar ke visheshan hai or "yazata", poojya vyakti jinka sthaan amesh spent ke baad hai.
 avesta kaal ke dharmik granthon kii suchi ke ant main "vendidad", "videvo daat" kaa ullekh hua he. yah kanoon vishyak ek dharmpustak he jisamen 22 "fargarad" yaa adhyay hai. iske pradhan vanrya vishay is prakaar hai--ahurmajd kii rachana tathaa angr manyu kii prati rachanaayen, krishi, samay, shapath, yuddh, vaasana, apavitrataa, shuddhi evam dahsanskaar.
 pracheen parsi rachanakaal ke bich likhit sahitya kaa sarvatha abhaav tha. us samay keval keelakshar abhilekh bhar the jinamein hakhaamani samraaton ne apane aadesh ankit kar rakhe the. unki bhashaa avesta se milti he, parantu lipi se babuli or asirian utpatti kaa anumaan haata he.
 pahalavi yug main kai prasiddh pustaken likhi gayi jaise "bundahishn" jisamen sristi kii utpatti di hui he, "dinakard" jisamen bahut se naitik or saamaajik prashnon kii mimasaa kii gai he, "shayast-l-shayast" jo saamaajik or dharmik ritiyon evam sanskaron kaa varnan karata he, "shkand" gumaani vijar" jisamen vaasana kii utpatti kii samasya kaa vivechan kiya gaya he tathaa "sad dar" jisamen vividh dharmik or saamaajik prashnon kii vyakhya kii gai he.
