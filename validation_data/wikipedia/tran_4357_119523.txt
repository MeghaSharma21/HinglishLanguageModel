 pracheen unani sabhyataa us samskruti ko kehete hai jo yunaan or uske nazadiiki kshetron main lagbhag aathvi shatabdi isa-purv se lagbhag chhathi shatabdi isaai tak vistrut thi. aadhunik pashchimi samskritiyon kii sab se gahari jad isi sabhyataa ko mana jaataa he, isliye pashchimi itihaaskaaron ke liye unani sabhyataa hameshaa bahut mahatvapurn rahi he. mana jaataa he ke is sabhyataa ke baad aane vaale roman samrajya par pracheen yunaan kaa gaharaa asar tha or us samrajya or samskruti ke kai pahaluon main unani samskruti kii chhap dekhi ja sakati he.
