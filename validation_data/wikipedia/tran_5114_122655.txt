 maanav sharir main adhikansh calcium maatra phosforus ke yogicon ke rup main upasthit hota he, jaise calcium phosphate. atah phosphorus kaa upyog calcium ke upyog se sambaddh he. saamaanya haddi or daant kii sanrachana ke liye phosphorus kii aavashyaktaa rahati he jo bhojan ko urja main badalte hai. isaki kami ke parinaam swaroop saadhaaran kamajori, haddi kaa dard or bhukh kii kami hoti he. isaki adhikta calcium ko grahan karne main baadha pahunchaa sakati he.
 haddi tuutane main phosphorus swasthy hone kii prakriya main shighrata laata he or ghaav se calcium kii haani ko roktaa he. phosphorus strivik swasthya main madad karata he or gurdo kaa apshisht baahar nikaalane main sahaayak hota he. magnesium yaa louh kii bahulata phosphorus ke bhandaaran ko roak sakati he. safed chini or ucch vasa vaala aahaar calcium-phosphorus santulan ko bigaad saktaa he. prataml hadidyon kii faansphoras aapurti kam kar detaa he.
 sampoorn anaaj or dabl roti, faliyaan.daale, doodh or doodh ke utpaada, beej, giridaar fal, andey, machhlee, murge or maance iske mahatvapurn strot he.
