 bhartiya khagolavidya ke sandarbh main, vakri grah, surya or chandramaa ke alaava un saura mandal ke grahon ko kehete hai jo peeche kii ore gati karte hue dikhte hai. yah abhaasi gati prithvi ke ghurnak kii kaksha ke kaaran hoti he.
 vakrigati kaa arth he, 'peeche kii ore gati'. jab dharti kisi grah ke paas se hokar guzarati he tab vah grah peeche kii ore chalata hua lagata he .
