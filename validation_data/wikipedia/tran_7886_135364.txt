 pashchim giniai nimnabhoomi van pashchim africa main sthit ek ushnkaribandheey evam uposhna ardra prithuparni van kshetra he. yahan atlantic mahaasaagar se lekar kai sau mile mukhyabhumi ke andar tak nichali oonchaai par van faile hue hai. yah jangal pashchimi kot divvar, liberia, dakshinpurvi gini, sierra leone ke adhikansh bhaag or phir dakshinpashchimi gini main vistrut hai. kot divvar kii sassandra nadi pashchim giniai wanon ko purvi giniai wanon se alag karti he.
