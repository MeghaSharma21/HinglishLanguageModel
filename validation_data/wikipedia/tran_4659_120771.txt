 abdullah chatla turki sarkaar kii khufia police ke liye kaam karne vaala dandit apradhi or chapa-maar ladaakon se ladane ke liye bhaade kaa hatyara tha. 1970 ke dashak main vah grey vulves naamak sanghathan kaa netritv karata tha jo naishnlist movement party kii yuva shakha thi. susurluk car durghatana main uski maut jisamen kai turki ke adhikari maare gaye the yah dikhaati he ki sangathit aparaadh se rajya kis had tak judaa he. abdullah rajya ke liye mudbhed karne vaala ban chukaa tha jise kurdistan varkars party or arminian sangharsh dal asaalaa ke sandigdh sadsyon ko maar daalane ke kaa aadesh praapt tha.
 madhya anatoli ke chote-se nevashahar praant main abdullah daayen morche or turk teevr-rashtravaadiyon ke vichaaron se bhalee-bhaanti parichit tha jo us kshetra main sakriy the.
