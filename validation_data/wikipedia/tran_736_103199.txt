 kundal kaan main pahanane kaa ek aabhushan, jise stri-purush donon pahante the. pracheen kaal main kaan ko chhedakar jitna hi lanbaa kiya ja sake utana hi adhik vah saundarya kaa pratik mana jaataa tha. isi kaaran bhagavan buddha kii murtiyon main unke kaan kaaphi lambey or chhede hue dikhaai padate hai. is prakaar kaan lanbaa karne ke liye lakdi, hathidaant athwa dhaatu ke bane lambey gol belanaakaar jo aabhushan prayog main aate the, use hi mulat: kundal kehete the or uske do rup the - praakaar kundal or vapra kundal. baad main naanaa roopon main uskaa vikaas hua. sahitya main praaia: patr kundal, makar kundal, shankh kundal, ratna kundal, sarp kundal, mrisht kundal aadi ke ullekh praapt hote hai. devataaon ke moortan ke prasang main brihatsamhita main surya, baldev or vishnu ko kundaladhari kaha gaya he. pracheen murtiyon main praaia: shiv or ganpati ke kaan main sarp kundal, uma tathaa anya deviyon ke kaan main shankh athwa patr kundal or vishnu ke kaan main makar kundal dekhne main aata he.
 nath panth ke yogiyon ke bich kundal kaa vishesh mahatv he. ve dhaatu athwa hiran kii sing ke kundal dhaaran karte hai.
