 kayik koshika sangalankayik koshikaon ke sangalan se sankar koshikaayen praapt hotin hai. saamaanya maanav resha koshikaon yaa shwet rakt koshikaon ko mooshak satat koshika linon se sangalit karte hai. koshika sangalan ke liye, parabangnee kiranit sandei vishaanu yaa poliithaylin glikol kaa prayog karte hai. sankar koshika main aarambh main do kendrak rahete hai, jo baad main sangalit ho jate hai. is prakaar se sankar koshikaon kaa utpaadan kayik koshika sankaran kehalaata he.
