 jharkhand ke adivaasiyon ne raghunatha mahato ke netritv main east india company ke khilaaf jangal, jameen ke bachaav tathaa naanaa prakaar ke shoshan se mukti ke liye 1769 main jo aandolan armbh kiya use chuad vidroh kehete hai. yah aandolan 1805 tak chalaa.
 sthaneey adivasee logon ko utpaati yaa luteraa ke arth main samuhik rup se britishon dwaara chuad kah kar bulaayaa gaya. haal ke kuch andolanon main ise aapattijanak maanate hue is ghatana ko chuad vidroh ke bajaay jangal mahal swatantraaa aandolan ke naam se bulaaye jane kaa prastaav bhi kiya gaya he.
 chuad athwa chuhaad kaa shabdic arth luteraa athwa utpaati hota he. jab 1765 i. main east india company dwaara tatkaaleen bengal ke chotanagpur ke jangalmahal jila main sarvapratham malgujari vasuulana shuru kiya gaya, tab angrejon ke is shadyantrakaari tareeke se jal, jangal, jameen hadapney kii gatividhiyon kaa san 1769 i. main kudmi samaaj ke logon dwaara raghunatha mahato ke netritv main sabase pehla virodh kiya gaya or british shashakon ke khilaaf kranti kaa bigul foonka gaya. jab angrejon ne poocha, ye log koun hai, to unke pitthu jameendaaron ne ghrinaa or avmaananaa kii drushti se unhen 'chuad' kahkar sambodhit kiya, tatpashchaat us vidroh kaa naam 'kudmi vidroh' ke sthaan par 'chuad vidroh' padaa.
 1764 main baxur yuddh kii jeet ke baad angarejon kaa manobal bad gaya. angrej kaarigaron ke saath kisaanon ko bhi luutane lage. 12 agast 1765 ko shah alam dwitiya se angarejon ko bengal, bihar, oreisha va chotanagpur kii divaani mil gayi. uske baad angarejon ne kisaanon se lagaan vasuulana shuru kar diya.
 1766 main angarejon ne rajasav ke liye jameendaaron par dabaav banaaya, lekin jameendaaron ne unki adheenta sweekaar nahin kii. nateeja yah hua ki kisaan angareji julm ke shikaar hone lage. sthiti aniyantrit hone lagi, tab chuad andolan kii sthiti bani.
 angarejon ne chuad kii jameen chin kar jameendaaron ke haathon bechnaa shuru kar diya. inhen shaareerik yatnaa di jane lagi. tang aakar kurmi va adivaasiyon ne raghunatha mahato ke netritv main andolan shuru kar diya. isi kaa naam chuad andolan padaa. raghunatha mahato bachapan se hi deshbhakt va krantikari swabhaav ke the.
 1769 main phalgun poornima ke din unhone nimdih gaanv ke ek maidan main sabha kii. yahi sthaan raghunathpur ke naam se janaa gaya. raghunatha mahato ke samarthak 1773 tak sabhi ilaake main fail chuke the. chuad andolan kaa failaav nimdih, patkum, badabhum, dhalbhum, mednipur, kinchug pargana rajnagar gamhariya od tak ho gaya. unhone angarejon ke naak main dam kar rakhaa tha.
 paanch hajaar se adhik kii sankhya main nimudhal ke kile par akraman kiya gaya, jiske baad sainik bhaag kar narincehagad chale gayi. chuad andolan kii akraamakata ko dekhte hue angreji sarkaar ne chotanagpur ko patna se hataa kar bengal presidency ke adheen kar diya. 1774 main vidrohiyon ne kinchug pargana ke mukhyalay main police force ko gher kar maar daalaa.
 is ghatana se angarejon ne kinchug pargana par adhikaar karne kaa vichaar chhod diya. 10 april 1774 ko sidney smith ne bengal ke regiment ko vidrohiyon ke khilaaf faujee karrwai karne kaa aadesh de diya.
 san 1776 main andolan kaa centre ranchi jile kaa silli bana. paanch april 1778 ko raghunatha mahato ke jangalon main apane saathiyon ke saath sabha kar rahe the.
 vahaan ramgad main angareji chhavnee par hamale kii yojana ban rahi thi. isi bich angareji senaaon ne raghunatha mahato evam unke sahyogiyon ko chaaron ore se gher kar dhokhe se goliyaan chalaayi. donon ore se kaaphi der tak ghamaasan hua. raghunatha mahato ko bhi goli lagi.
 yahaan saikadon vidrohiyon ko giraftaar kar liya gaya. yahaan kai log maare gayi. aaj bhi andolan ke kai sakshya raghunathpur, ghutiadih, silli va lotaa gaanv main maujuud hai. bade bujurg aaj bhi shahid kii kahani bataate hai.
 elahabaad kii sandhi main dilli ke mogul badshaah shah alam dwitiya ne bengal, bihar or urisa kii divaani east india company ko saunp di. jangal mahal ke naam se janaa jane vaala yah kshetra maratha akraman ke baad se hi kaafii deele-dhaale tareeke se shasit tha or sthaneey jameendaar, jinhen raja kaha jaataa tha, kar vasuul kar varsh main ek baar kendriya satta ko bhejte the. company dwaara divaani adhikaar praapt karne ke baad se hi isaki niti adhiktam sambhav kar vasuuli kii rahi. is uddeshy main sthaneey vyavastha ko baadhak maanakar nayi pranaliyaan or niamak lagaaye jane shuru hue or 1765 ke baad ek bilkul nae kism ke kar yug kaa aarambh hua jisase sthaneey vyavastha, sthaneey log or jameendaar bhi barbaad hone lage.
 is prakaar sthaneey chuad logon kii jameenon par se unke prakritik adhikaar samaapt kiye jane se unamem asantosh vyaapt tha or jab unhone vidroh kiya to unhen bedakhal kiye gaye jameendaaron kaa netritv bhi praapt ho gaya.
 saal 1799 ke aarambh main midanapur ke aasapaas ke teen sthanon par chuad log sangathit hue: bahadurpur, salbani or karngarh. yahan se unhone gorilla hamale shuru kiye. iname se karngarh main rani shiromani kaa awaas tha jinhone inkaa sakriy netritv kiya. tatkaaleen colector ke likhe patr ke anusaar, chuad vidroh badhata gaya or farvari 1799 tak midanapur ke aasapaas ke kai gaanvo ke satat vistrut ilaake par inkaa kabza ho gaya. march main, rani ne takreeban 300 vidrohiyon ke saath hamalaa kiya or karngarh ke garh main company ke sipaahiyon ke saare hathiyaar lut liye. hamalon or lut kaa yah kram disambar 1799 tak chalata rahaa.
 chuad vidroh kaa pratham itihaas je si price ne likhaa, "di chuad rebelion of 1799" ke naam se. parvarti itihaaskaaron ne bhi british dastavejon ke aadhaar par is vidroh kaa varnan kiya he.
 parvarti itihaaskaaron main e guha or unke hawaale se edward said jaise lekhakon ne ise gaiyr-tatasth lekhan ke rup main bhi dekha he. jharkhand student federation dwaara prakashit ek pamfalet main chuad shabd ko nakaaratmak mana gaya or is vidroh ko "jangal mahal kaa swatantraaa aandolan" ke naam se pukaare jane kaa prastaav bhi kiya gaya.
