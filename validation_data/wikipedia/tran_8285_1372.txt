 bharat kaa vir putr - maharana pratap ek soni tivi par prasaarit hone vaala ek dharawahik he. yah 27 may 2013 se 10 disambar 2015 tak prasaarit ho rahaa tha. ise abhimanyu raaj singh ne nirdeshit kiya he. yah dharawahik 16veen shatabdi ke mewar ke rajput shaasha, maharana pratap ke jivan par aadhaarit ek bhartiya aitihaasik naatak he. yah aitihaasik dharawahik 10 disambar 2015 ko raat 10 baje antim baar chalaa arthath ab yah puraa ho chukaa he iske ant main maharana pratap kii beemaari kii vajah se mrityu ho jaati he.
