 vansh bhaskar unnisvin shatabdi main rachit rajasthan ke itihaas se sambandhit ek prasiddh aitihaasik pingal kavy granth he. is main bundi rajya evam uttari bharat kaa itihaas varnit he. vansh bhaskar kii rachana chaaran kavi suryamall mishran dwaara kii gai thi jo bundi ke haada shaasha maharav ramsingh ke darbari kavi the. apurna 'vansh bhaskar' ko kavi suryamal ke dattak putr muraridan ne puraa kiya.
