 bharat ke raashtrapati, bharat ganraajya ke karypalak adhyaksh hote hai. sangh ke sabhi karypalak kaary unke naam se kiye jate hai. anuchhed 53 ke anusaar sangh kii karypalak shakti unamem nihit hai. vah bhartiya sashastr senaaon kaa sarvocch senanayake bhi hai. sabhi prakaar ke apatkaal lagane va hataane vaala, yuddh/shanti kii ghoshnaa karne vaala hota he. vah desh ke pratham naagarik hai. bhartiya raashtrapati kaa bhartiya naagarik hona aavashyak he.
 siddhanthata raashtrapati ke paas paryaapt shakti hoti he. par kuch apwaadon ke alaava raashtrapati ke pad main nihit adhikansh adhikaar vastav main pradhanmantri kii adhyakshata vaale mantriparishad ke dwaara upyog kiye jate hai.
 bharat ke raashtrapati nai dilli sthit raashtrapati bhawan main rahete hai, jise raisina hill ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. raashtrapati adhiktam kitni bhi baar pad par rah sakate hai isaki koi seemaa tay nahin he. ab tak keval pehle raashtrapati rajendra prasad ne hi is pad par do baar apana kaarykaal puraa kiya he.
 pratibha patil bharat kii 12veen tathaa is pad ko sushobhit karne vaali pehali mahila raashtrapati hai. unhone 25 july 2007 ko pad va gopneeyata kii shapath lee thi. vartmaan main ram nath kovind bharat ke chaudaaven raashtrapati hai.
 15 agast 1947 ko bharat britain se swatantra hua tha or antarim vyavastha ke tahat desh ek rashtramandal adhirajya ban gaya. is vyavastha ke tahat bharat ke governor general ko bharat ke rashtrapramukh ke rup main sthapit kiya gaya, jinhen british india main britain ke antarim raja - jorge VI dwaara british sarkaar ke bajaay bharat ke pradhanmantri kii salaah par niyukt karnaa tha.
 yah ek asthaayee upaay tha, parantu bhartiya raajnneetik pranali main saajha raja ke astitv ko jari rakhana sahi myanon main samprabhu raashtra ke liye upyukt vichaar nahin tha. aazaadee se pehle bharat ke aakhri british visarai lord mountbeton hi bharat ke pehle governor general bane the. jald hi unhone chakravarti rajagopalaachari ko yah pad saunp diya, jo bharat ke iklaute bhartiya mul ke governor general bane the. isi bich dow rajendra prasad ke netritv main samvidhan sabha dwaara 26 navambar 1949 ko bhartiya samvidhan kaa masauda taiyaar ho chukaa tha or 26 janvari 1950 ko aupchaarik rup se samvidhan ko sweekaar kiya gaya tha. is taarikh kaa pratikatmak mahatv tha kyonki 26 janvari 1930 ko bhartiya rashtriya congress ne britain se pehali baar purna swatantraaa ko awaz di thi. jab samvidhan lagu hua or dow rajendra prasad ne bharat ke pehle raashtrapati kaa pad sambhala to usee samay governor general or raja kaa pad ek nirvaachit raashtrapati dwaara pratisthapit ho gaya.
 is kadam se bharat kii ek rashtramandal adhirajya kii sthiti samaapt ho gaya. lekin yah ganatantr rashtron ke rashtramandal kaa sadsy bana rahaa. kyonki bharat ke pratham pradhanmantri jawaharlal nehru ne tark kiya kii yadi koi bhi raashtra british samraat ko "rashtramandal ke pradhan" ke rup main sweekaar kare par zaruri nahin he ki vah british samraat ko apane rashtrapradhan kii manyata de, use rashtramandal main rahane kii anumati di jaani chahie. yah ek atyant mahatvapurn nirnay tha jisane beesvin sadi ke uttararddh main nae-swatantra ganraajya bane kai anya purv british upniveshon ke rashtramandal main rahane ke liye ek misaal sthapit kiya.
 bharat ke raashtrapati kaa chunaav anuchhed 55 ke anusaar anupatik pratinidhitiv pranali ke ekal sankramntic mat paddati ke dwaara hota he.
 raashtrapati ko bharat ke sansad ke dono sadanon tathaa saath hi rajya vidhayikaon ke nirvaachit sadasyon dwaara paanch varsh kii avadhi ke liye chunaa jaataa he. vot aavantit karne ke liye ek formula istemaal kiya gaya he taaki har rajya kii jansankhya or us rajya se vidhansabha ke sadasyon dwaara vot daalane kii sankhya ke bich ek anupaat rahe or rajya vidhansabhaon ke sadasyon or rashtriya saansadon ke bich ek samaanupaat bani rahe. agar kisi ummidavaar ko bahumat praapt nahin hoti he to ek sthapit pranali he jisase haarane vaale ummidavaaron ko pratiyogita se hataa diya jaataa he or unako mili vot anya ummidavaaron ko tabatak hastantarit hota he, jab tak kisi ek ko bahumat nahin miltaa.
 raashtrapati banane ke liye aavashyak yogyataayen :
 bharat kaa koi naagarik jisaki umr 35 saal yaa adhik ho vah pad kaa ummidavaar ho saktaa he. raashtrapati pad ke liye ummidavaar ko loksabhaa kaa sadsy banane kii yogyata hona chahie or sarkaar ke adheen koi laabh kaa pad dhaaran kiya hua nahin hona chahie. parantu nimnlikhit kuch karyaalay-dharakon ko raashtrapati ke ummidavaar ke rup main khadaa hone kii anumati di gai he: 
 raashtrapati ke nirvachan sambandhi kisi bhi vivaad main ninarya lene kaa adhikaar ucchtam nyaayalay ko he.
 anuchhed 61 raashtrapati ke mahabhiyog se sambandhit he. bhartiya samvidhan ke antargat raashtrapati maatr mahabhiojit hota he, anya sabhi padaadhikari pad se hathaaye jate hai. mahabhiyojan ek vidhayika sambandhit kaarywahi he jabaki pad se hataanaa ek karypalika sambandhit kaarywahi he. mahabhiyojan ek kadai se palit kiya jane vaala aupchaarik krity he jo samvidhan kaa ullaghann karne par hi hota he. yah ullaghann ek rajaniatik krity he jiskaa nirdhaaran sansad karti he. vah tabhi pad se hatega jab use sansad main prastut kisi aise prastaav se hataaya jaye jise prastut karte samay sadan ke 1/4 sadasyon kaa samarthan mili. prastaav paarit karne se purv usko 14 din pehle notice diya jaayegaa. prastaav sadan kii kul sankhya ke 2/3 se adhik bahumat se paarit hona chaiye. phir duusare sadan main jane par is prastaav kii jaanch ek samiti ke dwaara hogi. is samay raashtrapati apana paksh swany athwa vakil ke madhyam se rakh saktaa he. doosraa sadan bhi use usee 2/3 bahumat se paarit karegaa. duusare sadan dwaara prastaav paarit karne ke din se raashtrapati pad se hat jaayegaa.
 samvidhan kaa 72vaan anuchhed raashtrapati ko nyaayik shaktiyaan detaa he ki vah dand kaa unmoolan, kshamaa, aaharan, pariharan, parivartan kar saktaa he.
 raashtrapati kii kshamaakaari shaktiyaan purnatah uski ichha par nirbhar karti hai. unhen ek adhikaar ke rup main maanga nahin ja saktaa he. ye shaktiyaan karypalika prakruti kii he tathaa raashtrapati inkaa prayog mantriparishad kii salaah par karegaa. nyaayalay main inako chunauti di ja sakati he. inkaa lakshya dand dene main hui bhul kaa niraakaran karnaa he jo nyaaypalika ne kar di ho.
 shersingh banaam punjab rajya 1983 main supremecourt ne nirnay diya kii anu 72, anu 161 ke antargat di gai dayaa yachika jitani shighrata se ho sake utani jaldi nipta di jaye. raashtrapati nyaayik kaarywahi tathaa nyaayik nirnay ko nahin badalegaa vah keval nyaayik nirnay se raahat degaa yachikakarta ko yah bhi adhikaar nahin hoga ki vah sunwaayee ke liye raashtrapati ke samaksh upasthit ho
 vidhayika kii kisi kaarywahi ko vidhi banane se rokane kii shakti vito shakti kehalaati he samvidhan raashtrapati ko teen prakaar ke vito detaa he.
 raashtrapati sansad kaa ang he. koi bhi bill bina uski sweekruti ke paas nahin ho saktaa athwa sadan main hi nahin laayaa ja saktaa he.
