 26 june 1975 se 21 march 1977 tak kaa 21 maheene kii avadhi main bharat main apatkaal ghoshit tha. tatkaaleen raashtrapati fairuddin ali ahamed ne tatkaaleen bhartiya pradhanmantri indira gandhi ke kehane par bhartiya samvidhan kii dhara 352 ke adheen apatkaal kii ghoshnaa kar di. swatantra bharat ke itihaas main yah sabase vivaadaaspad or aloktantric kaal tha. apatkaal main chunaav sthagit ho gaye tathaa naagarik adhikaron ko samaapt karke manamaani kii gai. indira gandhi ke raajnneetik virodhiyon ko kaid kar liya gaya or press par pratibandhit kar diya gaya. pradhanmantri ke bete sanjay gandhi ke netritv main bade paimaane par purush nasbandi abhiyaan chalaaya gaya. jaiprakash narayan ne ise 'bhartiya itihaas kii sarvaadhik kaali avadhi' kaha tha.
 1967 or 1971 ke bich, pradhanmantri indira gandhi ne sarkaar or bhartiya rashtriya congress party ke saath hi sansad main baari bahumat ko apane niyantran main kar liya tha. kendriya mantrimandal kii bajaay, pradhanmantri ke sachivalay ke bheetar hi centre sarkaar kii shakti ko kendrit kiya gaya. sachivalay ke nirvaachit sadasyon ko unhone ek khatra ke rup main dekha. iske liye vah apane pradhan sachiv pien haksar, jo indira ke salaahkaaron kii andruni ghere main aate the, par bharosa kiya. iske alaava, parameshwar narayan haksar ne sattarudh party kii vichaardhaara "patibaddh naukarshahi" ke vichaar ko badhaava diya.
 indira gandhi ne chaturaai se apane pratidwandiyon ko alag kar diya jis kaaran congress vibhajit ho gayi or 1969-main do bhaagon, congress  va congress jo indira kii ore thi, bhaagon main bat gayi. akhil bhartiya congress cameti or congress saansadon ke ek bade bhaag ne pradhanmantri kaa saath diya. indira gandhi kii party puraani congress se zyada taqatvar va aantarik loktantr kii paramparaon ke saath ek majboot sanstha thi. duusari or congress ke sadasyon ko jaldi hi samaj main aa gaya ki unki pragati indira gandhi or unke parivaar ke liye apni vafadari dikhne par puri tarah nirbhar karti he or chatukaarita kaa dikhaawati pradarshit karnaa unki dincharya ban gaya. aane vaale varshon main indira kaa prabhaav itnaa bad gaya ki vah congress vidhayak dal dwaara nirvaachit sadasyon kii bajaay, rajyon ke mukhyamantriyon ke rup main swayan chune gaye vafaadaaron ko sthapit kar sakati thim.
 indira kii us sarkaar ke paas janata ke bich unki karishmai apil kaa samarthan praapt tha. isaki ek or kaaran sarkaar dwaara liye gaye faisle bhi the.isamen july 1969 main pramukh bainkon kaa rashtriyakaran va sitambar 1970 main rajbhate se unmoolan shaamil hai; ye faisle apane virodhiyon ko saarvbhaumik jhatka dene ke liye, adhyadesh ke madhyam se achaanak kiye gaye the. iske baad, syndicate or anya virodhiyon ke vipareet, indira ko "garib samarthak, dharm ke maamalon main, arthshaastra or dharmanirpekshata va samaajwaad ke saath puure desh ke vikaas ke liye khadi ek chhavi ke rup main dekha gaya." pradhanmantri ko vishesh rup se vanchit virgon-garib, daliton, mahilaon or alpsankhyakon dwaara bahut samarthan mila. unke liye, vah unki indira ammaa thim.
 1971 ke aam chunaavon main, "garibi hataao" kaa indira kaa loklubhaavan naaraa logon ko itnaa pasand aayaa ki puraskaar swarup unhen ek vishaal bahumat se jeeta diya. " jeet ke itane bade antar ke sambandh main itihaaskaar ramchandra guha ne baad main likhaa tha ki "congress asli congress ke rup main khadi he ise yogyata pradarshit karne ke liye kisi pratyay kii aavashyaktaa nahin he."
 disambar 1971 main, inke sakriy yuddh netritv main bharat ne purv main purvi pakistan ko apane kattar dushman pakistan se swatantraaa dilwaai. agle maheene hi unhen bharat ratna se sammanit kiya gaya, vah us samay apane charam par thim; unki jeevani lekhak inder malhotra, ke liye 'bharat kii samraji' ke rup main unka varnan" upyukt lag rahaa tha. niyamit rup se ek taanashaah hone kaa or ek vyaktitv panth ko badhaava dene kaa aarope lagane vaale vipakshi netaaon ne bhi unhen durga saamaan mana.
 1975 kii tapati garmi ke dauraan achaanak bhartiya raajnneeti main bhi bechaini dikhi. yah sab hua elahabaad ucch nyaayalay ke us faisale se jisamen indira gandhi ko chunaav main dhaandhlee karne kaa doshi paaya gaya or un par chah varshon tak koi bhi pad sanbhaalane par pratibandh laga diya gaya tha. lekin indira gandhi ne is faisale ko maanane se inkaar karte hue sarvocch nyaayalay main apil karne kii ghoshnaa kii or 26 june ko apatkaal lagu karne kii ghoshnaa kar di gai.
 akashwani par prasaarit apane sandesh main indira gandhi ne kaha, "jab se maine aam aadmi or desh kii mahilaon ke phaayde ke liye kuch pragatisheel qadam uthaae hai, tabhi se mere kilaaf gahari sajeesh rachi ja rahi thi."
 apatkaal lagu hote hi aantarik suraksha kaanoon ke tahat raajnneetik virodhiyon kii giraftaari kii gai, inamen jaiprakash narayan, jorge farnandis or atal bihari vaajapeyi bhi shaamil the.
 maamla 1971 main hue loksabhaa chunaav kaa tha, jisamen unhone apane mukhya pratidwandvi raaj narayan ko parajeet kiya tha. lekin chunaav parinaam aane ke chaar saal baad raaj narayan ne hoicourt main chunaav parinaam ko chunauti di. unki daleel thi ki indira gandhi ne chunaav main sarkari mashinari kaa durupyog kiya, tay seemaa se adhik kharch kiye or matadaataaon ko prabhavit karne ke liye galat tareekon kaa istemaal kiy. adaalat ne in aaropon ko sahi tehraaya. iske baavjood indira gandhi tus se mas nahin huin. yahan tak ki congress party ne bhi bayaan jari kar kaha ki indira kaa netritv party ke liye aprihaary he.
 rashtriya swayamsevak sangh ko pratibandhit kar diya gaya kyonki mana gaya ki yah sanghathan vipakshi netaaon kaa kareebi he tathaa iskaa bada sanghathanaatmak aadhaar sarkaar ke viruddh virodh pradarshan karne kii sambhaavna rakhata tha. police is sanghathan par tut padi or uske hajaaron kaaryakartaaon ko kaid kar diya gaya. aresas ne pratibandh ko chunauti di or hajaaron swayamsevakon ne pratibandh ke khilaaf or moulik adhikaron ke hanan ke khilaaf satyagrah main bhaag liya.
 sabhi vipakshi dalon ke netaaon or sarkaar ke anya spasht aalochakon ke giraftaar kiye jane or salaakhon ke peeche bhej diye jane ke baad puraa bharat sadame kii sthiti main tha. apatkaal kii ghoshnaa ke kuch hi samay baad, sikh netritv ne amritsar main baithkon kaa aayojan kiya jahaan unhone "congress kii fasivadi pravriti" kaa virodh karne kaa sankalp kiya. desh main pehle janvirodh kaa aayojan akali dal ne kiya tha jise "loktantr kii raksha kaa abhiyaan" ke rup main janaa jaataa he. ise 9 july ko amritsar main shuru kiya gaya tha.
 apatkaal lagu karne ke lagbhag do saal baad virodh kii lahar tej hoti dekh pradhanmantri indira gandhi ne loksabhaa bhang kar chunaav karaane kii sifarish kar di. chunaav main apatkaal lagu karne kaa faisalaa congress ke liye ghaatak sabit hua. khud indira gandhi apane garh raibareli se chunaav haar gayi. janata party baari bahumat se satta main ai or morarji desai pradhanmantri bane. sansad main congress ke sadasyon kii sankhya 350 se ghat kar 153 par simat gai or 30 varshon ke baad centre main kisi gair congressi sarkaar kaa gathan hua. congress ko uttar pradesh, bihar, punjab, haryana or dilli main ek bhi seat nahin mili. nai sarkaar ne apatkaal ke dauraan liye gaye faisalon kii jaanch ke liye shah ayog gathit kii gai. haalaanki nai sarkaar do saal hi tik pai or andruni antarvirodhon ke kaaran 1979 main sarkaar gir gai. up pradhanmantri choudhary charan singh ne kuch mantriyon kii dohari sadsyta kaa sawaal uthaaya jo jansangh ke bhi sadsy the. isi mudde par charan singh ne sarkaar se samarthan waapas le liya or congress ke samarthan se unhone sarkaar banaai lekin chali sirf paanch maheene. unke naam kabhi sansad nahin jane vaale pradhanmantri kaa record darj ho gaya.
