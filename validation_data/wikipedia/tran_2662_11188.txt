 ur kaa teesraa rajvansh or "nav-sumerion samrajya" 22 veen se 21 veen shatabdi isa purv main eumer shahar main sthit sumerion shaasha rajvansh or ek alpakaalik kshetreey-raajnneetik rajya donon kaa ullekh karata he, jo kuch itihaaskaar maanate hai ek navjaat samrajya tha. ur ke tisare rajvansh ko aam taur par avadhi kaa adhyayan karne vaale itihaaskaaron dwaara ur titruya ke rup main sankshipt kiya jaataa he.
 ur kaa teesraa rajvansh antim sumerion rajvansh tha jo mesopotamia main pramukh shaktishaalee tha. yah akkadiyan or gutian raajaaon dwaara kai shatabdiyon ke niyantran ke baad shuru hua. jinhone izine, lars or eshnnuna ke shaharon ko niyantrit kiya or uttar main jajir ko niyantrit kiya tha.
