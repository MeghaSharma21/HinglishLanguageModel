 prakashvir shastri bhartiya sansad ke saansad the. unka mul naam 'omprakash tyaagi' tha. unhone 'shastri' kii oopaadhi banaras hindu vishvavidhyalay se praapt kii thi.
 unke bhaashanon main tark bahut shaktishaalee hote the. unke virdohi bhi unke prashansak ban jate the. atalji maanate hai ki prakashvirji unase bhi behatar vaktaa the.
 prakashvir shastri ne hindi, dharmantaran, arashtriya gatividhiyon tathaa paanchaven or chathe dashak kii anek jwalanth samasyaon par apane bebaak vichaar vyakt kiye. 1957 main arya samaaj dwaara sanchalit hindi andolan main unke bhaashanon ne jabardast jaan phoonk di thi. saare desh se hajaaron satyagrahi punjab aakar giraftaaryaan de rahe the.
 prakashvirji agar nirdaleey rahete or sirf arya samaaj kaa netritv karte rahete to shaayad adhik prabhavi hote. lekin arya samaaje ke tatkaaleen santhnik netaaon ne unhe bahut pareshaan kiya.
