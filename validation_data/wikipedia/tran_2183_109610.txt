 kamrup,, assam kaa pehla aitihaasik rajya he jo ki lagbhag 800 varshon ke liye- 350 or 1140 CE ke bich astitv main tha. vartmaan din guwahati or tejpur main unki rajdhaniyon se teen rajwanshon dwaara shasit the. yah puure brahmaputra nadi ghati main or uttari bengal or bangladesh ke kuch hisson main vyaapt tha.
 haalaanki yah aitihaasik rajya 12 veen sadi me gaayab ho kar chote raajnneetik sansthaaon me badal gaya. kamrup kii dharna bani or pracheen or madhyayugin itivrutt is naam se is kshetra ko sanderbhit karnaa jari rakhaa. allaudin hossain shah, jo kamta rajya par 15 veen sadi me akraman kiya, uske sikke is kshetra ko kamru yaa kamrud kaha.
 kamrup aaj assam kaa ek jila maatr rah gaya he.
 ramayana or mahabharat main pragjyotichh ko kshetra ke rup main ullekh kiya he. erythrain sagar or Ptolemy 's Geographia, is kshetra ko Kirrhadia ke naam se ullekith karata he. chauthi shatabdi ke samudragupta elahabaad shilaalekh main bhi kamrup kaa ullekith he. chini yatri yuan chuang ne saatvin shatabdi main rajya kaa daura kiya, jo bhaskar varman dwaara shasit tha. assam, bagal, bangladesh or utar bharat ke vibhinn sthanon par bhasakar varman sahit kamrup ke shaskaon dwaara chode gaye shilaalekh jaankaree ke mahatvapurn strot hai.
