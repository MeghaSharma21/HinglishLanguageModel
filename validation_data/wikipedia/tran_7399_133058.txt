 kunain ek prakritik shwet crystalline alcoload padaarth hota he, jisamen fever-rodhi, malaria-rodhi, dardanaashak, sujan rodhi gun hote hai. ye kwainidine kaa sterio samavyav hota he, jo quinine se alag antierymic hota he. ye dakshin america ped cinchona paudhai kii chaal se praapt hota he. isse kunin naamak malaria bukhaar kii dawa ke nirmaan main kiya jaataa he. iske alaava bhi kuch anya dawaaon ke nirmaan main iskaa prayog hota he. ise tonic water main bhi milaayaa jaataa he or anya peya padaarthon main milaayaa jaataa he. europe main solahavin shatabdi main iskaa sabase pehle prayog kiya gaya tha. isai mishan se jude kuch log ise dakshin america se lekar aae the. pehle-pahal unhone paaya ki yah malaria ke ilaaj main kargar hota he, kintu baad main yah gyaat hone par ki yah kuch anya rogon ke upachaa main bhi kaam aa sakati he, unhone ise bade paimaane par dakshin america se laana shuru kar diya. 1930 tak kunain malaria kii rokthaam ke liye ekmaatr kargar aushadhi thi, baad main anti malaria tike kaa prayog bhi isse nipatane ke liye kiya jane laga. mul shuddh rup main kunain ek safed rang kaa crystal yukt powder hota he, jiskaa swaad kadava hota he. ye kadava swaad hi isaki pahchaan ban chukaa he. kunain parabangnee prakaash samvedi hoti he, va surya ke prakaash se sidhe sampark main fluorez ho jaati he. aisa isaki ucchstareey kongugated resonns sanrachana ke kaaran hota he.
 kunain main do pradhan fuzd-ring hote hai: ek aromatic quinolin or doosraa dwickree quineucalidine.
 anya quinolin malaria-rodhi aushadhiyon ke sang quinine kii kriya ke bhaanti hi isaki kriya kaa bhi abhi tak purna gyaan nahin ho paaya he. kunain kaa sarvaadhik prachlit evam maanya hypothissis iske nikat sambandhi or purna adhyayan kiye gaye quinolin drug chloroquin par aadhaarit he. is praturup main hemozoin biocristallizeshan kaa inhibishan shaamil he, jisamen cytotoxic himi kaa agrigeshan sammilit hota he. mukt cytotoxic himi parjiviyon ke sharir main ekatrit hota jaataa he, jo unki mrityu kaa kaaran ban jaataa he. 
 ise tonic water main milaane ke kaaran ek samay ooshnakaribandheey kshetron main rahane vaale kai logon kii mrityu ho gai thi, jahaan ise rog-nirodhi ke rup main prayog ke liye le jaaya gaya tha. is tonic water ko anti malaria aushadhi ke rup main viksit kiya gaya, lekin baad main logon ne ise madiya main milaanaa shuru kar diya kyonki isse unhen sharaab kaa swaad behatar lagane lagata tha. america ke effdie dwaara kunain ko sandigdh aushadhi rup main nishedh kar diya gaya he.
 aaj ke tonic water main paryaapt maatr main kunain nahin milaai jaati, jis kaaran yah malaria ke liye roganirodhak ke taur par prayog nahin hoti. kunain ko bazaar se goli yaa phir taral rup main kharida ja saktaa he. iskaa prayog haddiyon ke marod main bhi kiya jaataa he. prasav ke dauraan garbhashay main sankuchan ke liye bhi kiya jaataa he. isiilie garbhvatee mahilaon ko kunain kaa prayog nahin karnaa chahie.
 cinchona ke ped abhi tak kunain ke ekmaatr vanijyik, mitavyayi va vyavhaarik strot hai. vaise yudhon ke samay aavashyaktaa ke dabaav main iske kritrim utpaadan ke prayaas bhi kiye gaye the. iske liye ek aupchaarik rasinic sanshleshan ko 1944 main amareeki rasayanajna tabase kai adhik daksh quinine total synthesis tareeke praapt kar liye gaye hai. kintu in sabhi main se prakritik sroton se praapt alkelloed ke isolation kii prakriya sabase sasta he.
