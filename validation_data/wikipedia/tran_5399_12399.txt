 jhu jhu ek chini film abhinetri or gayika hai. inhone 2011 main abhinay kii shuruyaat kii thi.
 jhu kaa janm ek sainik ke parivaar main 19 july 1984 ko beijing main hua tha. inke pita jhu hanbin, ek vyapaaree hai or inke dada jhu kshujhi sena main the. inhone teen varsh kii aayu main piano bajaanaa siikhaa or baad main beijing takneekee or vyapaar vishvavidhyalay se snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii. 
 jhu ne 2005 main amtivi china ke ek sangeet kaarykram main hissaa liya, jo china main ek gharelu amtivi network he. iske baad jhu ne beijing ke ek sthaneey gayaki pratiyogita main hissaa liya. iske baad ve rashtriya str par tisare sthaan main pahunch gai.
