 prabandhan paraamarsh praathmik rup se maujuuda vyavsaayik samasyaon ke vishleshan ke madhyam se sanghathano ko apane pradarshan ko behatar banane or sudhaar ke liye yojanaon ke vikaas main madad karne kii pratha or udyog donon ko sanket karata he.
 sanghathan kai kaarnon se prabandhan salaahkaaron kii sevaaon ko hasil karte hai jisamen bahari salaah hasil karnaa or salaahkaaron kii vishisht visheshgyata kaa upyog karnaa bhi shaamil he.
 kai sanghathanon ke saath sambandh hone or jokhim hone kii vajah se, paraamarsh companiyon ko kathith taur par udyog kii "behtareen prathaon" se sachet rahanaa padataa he haalanki ek sanghathan se duusare sanghathan main aisi prathaon ke sthanantaran kii yogyata vichaaradhin paristhitiyon ke aadhaar par samasyagrast ho sakati he.
 paraamarsh companiyaan sanghathanaatmak parivartan prabandhan sahyog, prashikshan kaushalon kaa vikaas, praudyogiki karyanvayan, ranneeti vikaas, yaa parichaalanaatmak sudhaar sevaayen bhi pradaan kar sakati hai. prabandhan salaahkaar aam taur par samasyaon kii pahchaan ke maargdarshan ke liye or vyavsaayik kaaryon ko adhik prabhavi yaa kushal tareeke se karne ke liye siforeeshon ke aadhaar ke rup main kaam karne ke liye apane khud ke, swaamitv vaale tareekon yaa ruparekhaon kaa istemaal karte hai.
 adhyayan ke ek anokhe kshetra ke rup main prabandhan ke udai ke saath prabandhan paraamarsh kaa vikaas hua. sabase pehali prabandhan paraamarsh company kaa naam arthur di. little tha jisaki sthapana 1886 main isi naam ke emiti profesar ne kii thi. haalanki arthur di. little baad main ek mahaprabandhan paraamarsh company ban gai lekin mul rup se takneekee anusandhan main hi isaki visheshta thi. booj alan hamiltan kii sthapana edwin ji. booj ne kii thi jo ki northwestern uniwarsity ke kalog skool of management ke ek snaatak the, unhone isaki sthapana 1914 main ek prabandhan paraamarsh company ke rup main kii thi or yah udyog or sarkaar donon tarah ke graahakon ko seva pradaan karne vaali pehali company thi.
 dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke baad, kai nai prabandhan paraamarsh companiyon kii sthapana kii gai, jinamein se boston kansalting group sabase ullekhneey company thi, jisaki sthapana 1963 main kii gai thi, jisane prabandhan or ranneeti ke adhyayan main ek kathor vishleshnatmak drushtikon pradaan kiya. 1960 or 1970 ke dashakon ke dauraan boston kansalting group, mckinse, booj alan hamiltan or harvard bijness skool main kiye gaye kaam se aise sansaadhan or drushtikonon kaa vikaas hua jinhone vyuhatmak prabandhan ke nae kshetra ko paribhaashit kiya or uske dwaara sthapit aadhaar karmon kaa kai paraamarsh companiyon ne paalan kiya. 1983 main chah prophessoron dwaara monitor group kii sthapana ke saath udyog par harvard bijness skool kaa prabhaav barkaraar rahaa.
 uese main sabase pehle prabandhan paraamarsh company ke udai ke kai kaarnon main se ek gahare saanskrithik kaarak hai: is baat ko vahaan sweekaar kiya gaya ki prabandhan or is tarah ke board sabhi paristhitiyon main sakasham yaa yogya nahin ho sakate the; isliye bahari yogyata kharidne kii kriya ko vyavsaay sambandhi samasya ke samaadhaan ke ek saamaanya tareeke ke rup main dekha jaataa tha. ise prabandhan ke liye ek "anubandheey" sambandh ke rup main sanderbhit kiya jaataa he. iske vipareet europe main prabandhan ko bhaavnaatmak evam saanskrithik ayaamon ke saath joda jaataa he, jahaan prabandhak har samay yogya hone ke liye baadhya hota he. ise "patter familias" paddati ke rup main sanderbhit kiya jaataa he. isliye bahari salaah maangane ko anuchit mana jaataa tha. haalanki kabhi-kabhi yah tark diya jaataa he ki un dinon uese main kaaryakariyon ke shiksha kaa prakaar europe kii tulana main aksar alag hota tha jahaan prabandhak aksar takneekee yaa anya vishesh prishthbhumi ke hote the - jaise frans main takneekee grandes icholes ke snaatak yaa germany main "doctor" ke samaan) - lekin unki vishisht prabandhan shiksha aksar simit hoti thi. haalanki yah kahana jaruri he ki america or europea vyavsaayon kii prabandhan sanrachanaon main bahut zyada antar hone kii vajah se iskaa andaaza lagaana bahut mushkil he.
 keval dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke baad, uese ke netritv main antarrashtriya vyapaar ke vikaas ke maddenjar, europe main prabandhan paraamarsh kaa udbhav hua tha. bazaar main vartmaan chalan prabandhan paraamarsh companiyon kaa ek spasht vibhaajan he.
 aam taur par aisa mana ja saktaa he ki paraamarsh ke vibhinn drushtikon kahin na kahin satatya ke saath sthit he jahaan ek taraf 'visheshgy' yaa aadeshaatmak drushtikon he or duusari taraf suvidhajanak drushtikon he. visheshgy drushtikon main salaahkaar visheshgy kii bhoomika nibhaataa he or graahak ko visheshgy salaah yaa sahyog pradaan karata he or isaki tulana main suvidhajanak drushtikon ke tahat, graahakon se kam input liya jaataa he or bahut kam sahyog pradaan kiya jaataa he. suvidhajanak drushtikon ke saath, salaahkaar vishisht yaa takneekee visheshgy gyaan par kam lekin paraamarsh kii prakriya par zyada dhyaan detaa he. prakriya par dhyaan dene kii vajah se suvidhajanak drushtikon ko aksar 'prakriya paraamarsh' ke rup main bhi sanderbhit kiya jaataa he jiske sabase mashhoor abhyaasakarta ke rup main edgar schin kaa naam liya jaataa he. upar soochibaddh paraamarsh companiyaan is satatya ke visheshgy drushtikon ke kaaphi kareeb he.
 kai paraamarsh companiyon ko ek matrix sanrachana main sangathit kiya jaataa he, jahaan ek 'aksh' vyavsaay ke ek kaary yaa paraamarsh ke prakaar kaa varnan karata he: udhaaharan ke liye, ranneeti, parichaalan, praudyogiki, kaaryakari netritv, prakriya sudhaar, pratibha prabandhan, bikri, ityadi. doosraa aksh udyog kendrit he: udhaaharan ke liye, tel or gas, khudara, motor vaahan. ye sab ek saath ek matrix kaa nirmaan karte hai, jahaan matrix main ek yaa ek se adhik "kaksh " par salaahkaaron kaa kabza hota he. udhaaharan ke liye, ek salaahkaar khudara udyog ke parichaalan main visheshgyata praapt kar saktaa he or ek anya salaahkaar downstream tel or gas udyog main prakriya sudhaar par dhyaan kendrit kar saktaa he.
 prabandhan paraamarsh aam taur par vyavsaay paraamarsh sevaaon ke praavdhaan ko sanderbhit karata he lekin inamen kai visheshgyataayen hoti hai, jaise soochna praudyogiki paraamarsh, maanav sansaadhan paraamarsh, abhaasi prabandhan paraamarsh or anya paraamarsh, jinamein se kai paraamarsh ek saath die jate hai or jinamein se zyaadaatar paraamarsh neeche soochibaddh kaaphi vividh paraamarsh companiyon dwaara pradaan kiye jate hai. haalanki tathakthith "boutique" paraamarsh companiyaan chote sanghathan hai, jinhen ek yaa aisi kuch visheshaitaaon main visheshgyata praapt he.
 prabandhan paraamarsh kaa bahut teji se vikaas hua he or udyog ke vikaas dar main 1980 or 1990 ke dashakon main 20% kii vruddhi hui he. ek vyavsaayik seva ke rup main paraamarsh atyadhik chakree or samgr aarthik paristhitiyon se judaa hua he. 2001-2003 kii avadhi ke dauraan, paraamarsh udyog main sankuchan hua, lekin uske baad se isamen dhire-dhire uttarottar vikaas kaa anubhav ho rahaa he.
  vartmaan main, chaar mukhya prakaar kii paraamarsh companiyaan hai:
 ek paanchaven prakaar kii ubhar rahi company sorcing salaahkaar company he, jo insorcing, outsorcing, vikreta chayan or anubandh baatcheet se sambandhit sorcing vikalpon ke bare main kharidaaron ko salaah detii he. sheersh 10 sorcing salaahkaar companiyaan tipiai, gartner, hacket group, everest group, peedblusi, avasant, pie kansalting or ekua terra thim. ek teevr vikasshil kshetra hone ke baavjood, sampoorn rup se prabandhan paraamarsh udyog par vichaar karne par in sabase badi sorcing salaahkaar companiyon ko sambhawatah boutique companiyon ke rup main vargeekrut kiya jayega, jinamein se ek sabase badi company udhaaharan ke taur par tipiai he, jisaki 2006 kii aay, iesji dwaara iske adhigrahan ke dauraan, 150 million america dollar se kam thi.
 paraamarsh companiyon main antar sthapit karne kaa ek or tarika aay ke model par aadhaarit he:
 1. samay or prayaas ke aadhaar par: zyaadaatar companiyaan samay or prayaas ke aadhaar par bhugtan maangati hai. ve fees nirdhaarit karne ke liye, vishisht adhyayanon or pichley record kaa istemaal karte hai, jaise mckinse, bisiji ityadi.
 2. keval vitarit parinaamon ke aadhaar par: bahut kam or aam taur par boutique companiyaan, jinka safalataa dar kaaphi acha hota he, is aadhaar par bhugtan maangati hai.
 3. donon kaa sangyojan: kai badi companiyaan vitarit parinaamon ke aadhaar par paarishramik kaa ek hissaa grahan karti hai. lekin aamtaur par parivartaneey ghatak kul ghatakon kaa keval 20-30% hota he.
 prabandhan paraamarsh gaiyr-vyavsaay sambandhit kshetron main bhi adhik prachlit hota ja rahaa he. jaise-jaise peshewar or vishesh salaah kii jarurat badhati ja rahi he vaise-vaise sarkari, ardh-sarkari or labharahit agenciyaan unheen prabandhkeey siddhanton kii taraf mud rahi hai jinhone saalon tak nijee kshetra ko sahaayta di thi.
 ikkisvin sadi ke praarambhik bhaag main aayaa hua ek udyog sanrachanaatmak chalan, badi vividh peshewar salaahkaari companiyon kii paraamarsh or lekhankan ikaayiyon kaa upotpaada yaa algaav tha, jinamein ernst and yang, peedblusi or kepiemji kaa naam sabase zyada ullekhneey he. lekhankan or lekhapareekshan companiyon ke rup main apane vyavsaay ko shuru karne vaali in companiyon ke liye prabandhan paraamarsh unke vyavsaay kaa ek nayaa vistaar tha. lekin lekhankan prathaon sambandhi kai atiprachaarit ghotaalon ke baad, jaise enron ghotaalaa, in companiyon ne sakht niamak jaanch kaa adhik aasaani se anupaalan karne ke liye apane prabandhan paraamarsh ikaayiyon kaa adhikarharan karnaa shuru kiya. duniya ke kuch bhaagon main yah chalan ab ulta rup le rahaa he, jahaan companiyaan teji se apane prabandhan paraamarsh sadhano kaa phir se nirmaan kar rahi hai, jaisaa ki unki korporate websiton main saafe dikhaai detaa he.
 in drushtikonon main nigamon ko shaamil kiya gaya he jo apane swayan ke aatanrik paraamarsh samoohon kii sthapana karte hai, jiske liye ve yaa to korporation arthath nigam se yaa bahari companiyon se aatanrik prabandhan salaahkaaron ko niyukt karte hai. kai nigamon main 25 se 30 purnakalik salaahkaaron vaale aatanrik samooh hote hai.
 aatanrik paraamarsh samoohon kaa nirmaan aksar lagbhag pratha yaa abhyaas kshetron kii sankhya ke baraabar kiya jaataa he, jinamein aam taur par nimna shaamil hai: sanghathanaatmak vikaas, prakriya prabandhan, soochna praudyogiki, dizine seva, prashikshan or vikaas.
 aatanrik salaahkaaron ko niyukt karne vaali companiyon ko in aatanrik salaahkaaron se kai sambhavit laabh praapt hote hai:
 dhyaan den: laagat prabhavakaarita ke moolyankan main, pariyojna ke str par evam sanghathanaatmak str par, aatanrik salaahkaar ke kharchon ko kis tarah lagaaya jayega is baat ke prati nigamon ko jaagruk hona chahie or uske saath sangat hona chahie.
 aatanrik salaahkaaron kaa ek samooh baariki se bahari paraamarsh companiyon kii nigaraanee or unke saath kaam kar saktaa he. yah behatar vitaran, gunvattta or samgr parichaalaneey sambandhon ko sunishchit karegaa.
 paraamarsh sevaayen pradaan karne vaali bahari companiyon main praathamikta ke maamle main virodhabhas rahata he. bahari company kii tandoorusti unke client kii tandoorusti se kul milakar adhik mahatvapurn he .
 haal ke dinon main sarkari shashan pranaaliyon main prabandhan paraamarsh kaa istemaal kaaphi bad gaya he. booj alan hamiltan ko khaas taur par ab ek kansaltent arthath salaahkaar company ke rup main acchhee tarah se janaa jaataa he, jo praathmik rup se america sangheey sarkaar kii seva karti he. deloit kansalting elelpi, sarkari vibhaagon or agenciyon kii sabase zyada mushkil samasyaon ke samaadhaan main, unki madad karne main apni udyog visheshta or dashakon ke anubhav kaa istemaal karata he.bharat main krishi vitt nigam limited mukhya rup se sarkari or sambandhit sansthaanon ko paraamarsh detaa he.
 1997 se 2006 tak, lebar sarkaron ne prabandhan salaahkaaron ke liye 20 billian pound or iti pranaaliyon ke liye kam se kam or 50 billian pound kharch kiya he, jo pichley roodhiwaadee sarkaar dwaara ek saal main kharch kiye gaye 500 million pound se kaaphi adhik he. 2003 se 2006 tak salaahkaaron par kiye jane vaale kharchon main ek tihaai vruddhi hui he or 2003 se 2004 tak 2.1 billian pound or 2005 se 2006 tak 2.8 billian pound kharch hua he, jiskaa mukhya kaaran naishanal health sarvice dwaara kiye jane vaale kharch main vruddhi he. pichley teen saalon main badi paraamarsh companiyon se paraamarsh sevaaon liye jane par 7.2 billian pound kharch kiya gaya he.
 web seva "vault.com" har saal kaam karne vaali sheersh 50 paraamarsh companiyon kii ek suchi taiyaar karti he. ranking ke samay kuch anya vikalpon ke uparaant company kii sabhyataa, abhyaas shakti, pratishtha or muaavja par vichaar kiya jaataa he. varsh 2011 kii sheersh 25 companiyaan is prakaar hai: 
 lagaataar ucch or badhati aay ke baavjood, prabandhan paraamarsh company ko lagaataar client evam prabandhan vidwano se aalochana milti rahati hai.
 "prachlit yaa lokapriy shabdon ke atyadhik istemaal or prabandhan sanakon kaa prachaar evam un par nirbharta or client jinhen nishpadit kar sake asi yojanaon ko viksit karne main vifaltaa kii vajah se prabandhan salaahkaaron kii aksar aalochana kii jaati he." prabandhan paraamarsh ke bare main kai mahatvapurn kitabon main yah tark diya gaya he ki vastav main prastaavit parivartan kaa nirmaan karne ke liye prabandhan paramarshkari salaah or vyavsaay kaaryakariyon kii kshamta ke bich ke bemail ke parinamswaroop maujuuda vyavsaayon main kaaphi nuksaan hota he. kris argiris kii kitaab "flaud adwise and da management trep" ke anusaar kris argiris kaa vishwaas he ki aajkaal di jane vaali salahon main se zyaadaatar salahon main asli yogyata kaa kaaphi ansh hota he. haalanki, nazadiiki avlokan se pataa chalata he ki aajkal di jane vaali zyaadaatar salahon main antaraal or visangatiyaan hoti hai, jo bhavishya main sakaaratmak parinaamon ko roak sakati hai.
 bahut zyada fees ke baavjood, apratishthit parmarsh companiyon par aksar khaali vaade karne kaa aarope lagaaya jaataa he. un par aksar "spashtvaadi" na hone or unki salaah jis aadhaar par kahi jaani chahie us anubhav kaa abhaav hone kaa aarope lagaaya jaataa he. ye salaahkaar bahut kam navachaar laate he or uski bajaay saadhaaran or "purvapacaged" vyuhanitiyaan or yojanaayen pradaan karte hai, jo client ke vishesh upyog kii drushti se apraasangik hote hai. ve clienton ke saamne, unase pehle apni company ke hiton ko rakhkar, apni jimmedaariyon ko praathamikta dene main vifal ho sakate hai.
 ek or chintaa kaa vishay paraamarsh companiyon dwaara parinaamon kii sthirataa par diya jane vaala vaada he. client or paraamarsh companiyon ke bich ke kaamkaaj ke ant main, aksar is baat kii ummid rahati he ki salaahkaar apane prayaas tikaau he esa sunishchit karne ke liye kuch samyavadhi tak pariyojna ke parinaamon kaa lekha pareekshan karenge. haalanki kuch paraamarsh companiyon dwaara dharaniyata ko badhaava die jane ke baavjood, pariyojna ke band hone ke baad client or paraamarsh companiyon ke bich sambandh viched kii vajah se, ise lagu karnaa mushkil hota he.
 atirikt aalochanaon main shaamil hai: laagat main katautee karne ke abhiyaan main vyavsaay kaa algaav, keval vishleshan report pradaan karnaa, junior salaahkaar senior salaahkaaron kii dar se bhugtan maangate hai, "custom kaary" ke rup main kai clienton ko ek hi tarah ke reporton ko phir se bechnaa, navachaar kaa abhaav, un dinon ke liye overbiling karnaa jab kaam nahin kiya gaya he, gunvattta ke mulya par gati, anuttaradaayi badi companiyaan or client par dhyaan dene kaa abhaav, anubandhon main parinaamon vishyak spashtataa kaa abhaav or gopneeyata.
