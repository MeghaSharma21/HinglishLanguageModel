 indrayan kii bel madhya, dakshin tathaa pashchimottar bharat, arab, pashchim asia, africa ke ucch bhaagon tathaa bumadhyasaagar ke deshon main bhi pai jaati he. iske pattey tarbooz ke patton ke samaan, phool nar or maadaa do prakaar ke tathaa fal nanragi ke samaan do inch se teen inch tak vyas ke hote hai. ye fal kachi avasthaa main hare, pashchaat pile ho jate hai or un par bahut si shwet dhaariyaan hoti hai. iske beej bhure, chikne, chamakdaar, lambey, gol tathaa chipte hote hai. is bel kaa prayek bhaag kadava hota he.
 indrayan kaa naam banglaa tathaa gujarati main bhi yahi he. sanskrit main ise chitrafal, indravaruni, marathi main kadu indravana, lokbhashaon main gadatumba, papad, pinda aadi naamon se janaa jaataa he. angreji main colocynth yaa 'bitor aipal' tathaa latin main 'citralas colocinths' kehete hai. anya do vanaspatiyon ko bhi indrayan kehete hai.
 iske fal ke goode ko sukhaakar oshadhi ke kaam main laate hai. ayurveda main ise sheethal, rechak or gulm, pitt, udrarog, kaph, kusht tathaa fever ko dur karanevaala kaha gaya he. yah jalodar, pilia or mutr sambandhi vyaadhiyon main vishesh laabhakaari tathaa dhavalarog, khaansi, mandagni, koshthbaddhata, raktaalpata or shlipad main bhi upyogi kaha gaya he.
 unani matanusaar yah sujan ko utaaranevaala, vayunashak tathaa snaayu sambandhi rogon main, jaise lakva, miragi, adhakapari, vismriti ityadi main laabhadaayak he. yah teevr virechak tathaa marod utpann karanevaala he, isliye durbal vyakti ko ise na dena chahie. isaki maatra dedh se dhai mashas tak kii hoti he. iskaa churna teen mashas tak babul kii gond, khurasaani ajvayan ke satv ityadi ke saath, jo isaki twrita ko gata dete hai, goliyon ke rup main diya jaataa hai.
 rasinic vishleshan se isamen kuch upakshaar tathaa colocynthin naamak ek glucoseida, jo is oshadhi kaa mukhya tatv he, paae gaye hai. british materia medica ke anusaar isse fever utarataa he. iskaa upyog twri koshthbaddhata, jalodar, ritusrav tathaa garbhasrav main bhi kiya ja saktaa he.
 laal indrayan kaa latin naam tricosanthan pamata he. ise sanskrit tathaa banglaa main mahakaal kehete hai. isaki bel bahut labi tathaa pattey do se chah inch ke vyas ke, trikon se saptakon tak hote hai. phool nar or maadaa tathaa shwet rang ke, fal kachi avasthaa main naarangi rang ke, kintu pakane par laal tathaa 10 nanragi dhaareyonwale hote hai. fal kaa guda haraapan liye kaala hota he tathaa fal main bahut se beej hote hai. is paudhe kii jad bahut gaharaai tak jaati he or isamen gaanthen hoti hai.
 rasinic vishleshan se iske fal ke goode main colocynthin se miltaa julata tricosanthin naamak padaarth paaya gaya he. laal indrayan bhi twri virechak he. ayurveda main ise shwaas or phuffus ke rogon main laabhadaayak kaha gaya he.
 junglee yaa choti indrayan ko latin main kucumis trigons kehete hai. isaki bel or fal purvokt donon indryanon se chote hote hai.
 iske fal main bhi colocynthin se milte julate tatv hote hai. iskaa haraa fal swaad main kadava, agnivardhak, swaad ko sudhaaranevaala tathaa kaph or pitt ke dosha ko dur karanevaala bataaya gaya he.
