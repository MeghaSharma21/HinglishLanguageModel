 new jersey sanyukt rajya america ke purvottar main ek rajya he. yah uttar or purv main neuyork se, dakshin-purv or dakshin main atlantic mahaasaagar se, pencylvania se pashchim main or delaware dwaara dakshin-pashchim par seemaa rakhata he. new jersey main mul america nivasee ne 2,800 varshon se adhik tak nivas kiya. 17 veen shatabdi kii shuruyaat main, dach or sweden ne pehle europea bastiyaan banaain. baad main angrejon ne is kshetra par kabja kar liya or iskaa naam jersey dweep ke upar rakhaa.
 america main new jersey chauthaa sabase chota rajya he. 2016 ke anumaan ke mutabik rajya kii jansankhya 89,44,469 hai. sabase adhik aabaadi main vah 11waan sthaan rakhata he lekin jansankhya ghanatv ke maamle main iskaa pehla sthaan he. lagbhag 70% log sirf angrezi bhashaa bolte hai. 16% spanish or 2.7% log bhartiya bhashaayen bolte hai.
