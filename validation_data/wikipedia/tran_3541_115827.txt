 saanchaa:Ancient monuments in RomeviSva devalayas yaa /ˈpænθiːɑːn/, latin : Pantheon, se unani : Πάνθειον, "sabhi devataaon ke liye" arth main prayukt mandir ke liye liya gaya greek shabd, ἱερόν, samajha jaataa he) rome main bani ek imaarat he, jo marks agrippa dwaara pracheen rome ke sabhi devi-devataaon ke mandir ke rup main banaayi gayi thi or 126 i. main samraat hadrian ne ise dobaaraa banwaaya tha. lagbhag samakaleen lekhak, casius dio ne anumaan lagaaya ki yah naam yaa to is imaarat ke aasapaas rakhi gayi itani adhik murtiyon kii vajah se, yaa phir swarg ke gumbad se isaki samanta kii vajah se rakhaa gaya. francisi kranti ke baad se, jab sant genevieve ne, paris ke carch ko apratishthit kar use dharmanirpeksh smarak ke rup main badal kar use paris kaa vishwa devalayas bana diya, usee samay se aisi kisi bhi imaarat jahaan kisi prasiddh mritak ko sammanit kiya gaya yaa dafnaaya gaya ho, uske liye saamaanya shabd vishwa devalayas, kaa prayog kiya jane laga he.
 yah imaarat teen panktiyon ke vishaal grenite korinthian kollam kii vajah se golakaar he jiskaa baramada gol ghar main khul rahe trikonika ke neeche ho, kancreet ke gumbad main bane sandook main jiskaa centre  akaash kii ore khulata ho. apane nirmaan ke lagbhag do hajaar saal baad bhi vishwa devalayas kaa gumbad ab bhi vishwa kaa sabase bada asudrudh kancreet gumbad he. aankh kii oonchai or aantarik chakra kaa vyas samaan he, 43.3 meter . ek ytaakar sanrachana baraamde ko golghar ke saath jodatii he. ab tak sangrakshit kii gayi roman imaaraton main yah behtareen he. itihaas main yah sadaiv istemaal kii jaati rahi he or 7veen shatabdi se, vishwa devalayas ko roman catholic carch ke rup main istemaal kiya jaataa rahaa he jo "saint mairi or shaheedon" ko utsargit he lekin anaupchaarik rup se yah "santa maaria rotanda" ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
 actium kii ladaai ke baad, marks agrippa ne apane tisare kaunsal satr ke dauraan mul vishwa devalayas kaa nirmaan kar use samarpit kiya. campus meritas main sthit, apane nirmaan ke samay main vishwa devalayas kaa kshetra rome ke bahari ilaake main tha, jiskaa parivesh gramin tha. roman ganatantr ke tahat campus martius chunaav ke samay or sena ke ekatrit hone ke kaam aata tha. haalanki, augustus or nae raaj ke samay in donon ko anaavashyak samajha jane laga. vishwa devalayas kaa nirmaan, nirmaan kaarykram kaa ek hissaa tha jiskaa jimmaa augustus seizar or unke samarthakon ne liya tha. unhone campus martius main bees se adhik sanrachanaon kaa nirmaan kiya jisamen agrippa kaa snanaghar or septa julia bhi shaamil he. kaaphi lambe samay tak yah samajha jaataa tha ki is imaarat kaa nirmaan agrippa ne kiya tha, jisamen baad main parivartan kiye gayi the or yah hissaa isliye shaamil tha kyonki isamen mandir ke saamne shilaalekh tha. haalanki, puratathvik khudaai se pataa chalaa he ki agrippa kaa vishwa devalayas puri tarah se nasht ho gaya tha or shaayad samraat hadrian ne agrippa ke mul mandir kii jagah par vishwa devalayas ke punarnirmaan kii jimmedaari lee thi.
 agrippa ke vishwa devalayas kii sanrachana par aksar bahes hoti rahati he. 19 veen shatabdi main khudaai ke parinamswaroop, puratatvavid rhodolpho lancian ne nishkarsh nikaala he ki agrippa kaa vishwa devalayas is tarah se banaaya gaya tha ki vah dakshin kii ore se khulaa ho, iske vipareet vartmaan dhaanche main vah uttar kii ore he or "tii" ke aadhaar par dwaar ke saath isamen ek sankshipt tii-aakaar yojana thi. is vivaran ko 20veen sadi ke ant tak vyapak rup se sweekaar kiya gaya. haalanki, haal hi main kii gayi puratathvik khudaayiyon se pataa chalata he ki vah imaarat ek alag rup kii ho sakati thi. agrippa kaa vishwa devalayas trikoniya dyodhi ke saath golakaar swaroop kaa ho saktaa tha or vah baad vaali punarnirmit imaaraton kii tarah uttar kii ore se khulaa hua bhi ho saktaa tha.
 auguston kaa vishwa devalayas anya imaaraton ke saath 80 i. main bhayaavah aag main nasht ho gaya tha. domitian ne phir se vishwa devalayas kaa punarnirmaan karaayaa, jo phir 110 i. main jal gaya. haal hi ke taarikh sahit nirmaataa kaa stamp lagi eenton ke punarmoolyankan ke anusaar duusari aag lagane ke turant baad, phir se nirmaan kaary shuru kar diya gaya. isliye, imaarat kii dizin kaa shrey hadrian yaa unke vaastukar ko nahin diya janaa chahie. iske bajaay, maujuuda imaarat kii dizin trajan ke vaastukar damishq ke apollodoras se sambandhit ho sakati he. sajaavati yojana kaa kitna shrey hadrian ke vaastukar ko diya janaa chahie yah anishchit he. ise hadrian dwaara samaapt kiya gaya lekin unhone ise apane kaaryon main shaamil karne kaa daawa nahin kiya he, balki mukhya dwaar ke mul shilaalekh main likhaa he,, jaisaa ki hadrian dwaara rome main banaai gayi tamaam punarnirmaan kii yojanaon main kiya gaya he .vastav main yah imaarat kaise banaayi gai yah abhi tak gyaat nahin ho paaya he. 
 casius dio, ek grako-roman seenetter, consul or rome ke vyapak itihaas ke lekhak, ne punarnirmaan ke lagbhag 75 saalon ke baad galati se apane lekh main vishwa devalayas ke gumbadadaar nirmaan kaa shrey hadrian ke bajaay agrippa ko de diya. samakaleen lekhakon main keval dio hi the jinhone vishwa devalayas kaa zikr kiya tha. 200 saal baad bhi, is imaarat ke mul or iske uddeshy ke bare main anischitata bani hui thi:
 202 i. main septimius severas or karaklla dwaara is imaarat kii marammat kii gayi thi, jiske liye vahaan ek or chota shilaalekh maujuud he. yah shilaalekh bataata he "pantheyum vetustate corrupatum kam omni kaltu restituerunth" .
 609 main, bizentin samraat phokas ne yah imaarat pope boniphase chaturth ko de di, jinhone ise ek isai carch main parivartit kiya tathaa ise santa maaria evam shaheedon ke prati samarpit kiya gaya, ab yah santa maaria dei martiri ke naam se janaa jaataa he: "ek anya pope, boniphase ne bhi kaha ki ve vishwa devalayas ke naam se jane jane vaale puraane mandir ko unhen de den, jisase butaparast pagan gandagi ko hataane ke baad, use virgin meri tathaa anya shaheedon ke liye samarpit ek carch banaaya ja sake, taaki us sthaan main jahaan pehle devataaon kii nahin balki rakshason kii puja hoti thi santon ke prati shradhanjali di ja sake.
 imaarat kaa carch ke rup main pratisthaapan karne kii vajah se ise tyaagane, vinaash or isse bhi zyada bura hone se bachaaya ja sakaa, jaisaa ki aarambhik madhyayugin kaal main pracheen rome kii adhiktar imaaraton ke saath hua. paul da dicon ne samraat constans dwitiya dwaara is imaarat ke luutane kii ghatana darj kii he jisane july 663 main rome kaa daura kiya tha:
 baarah dinon tak rome main rahete hue usne un sabhi chizon ko gira diya jinhen pracheen samay main shahar ko sajaane ke liye dhaatu se banaaya gaya tha, yahaan tak ki usne us carch kii chat bhi utaar lee, jise kabhi vishwa devalayas kaha jaataa tha or kabhi sabhi devataaon ke sammaan main banaaya gaya tha tathaa jise purv shaskaon kii sweekruti se shaheedon kaa smarak banaaya gaya tha; or usne vahaan se kaanse kii tilon ko lootakar, unhen anya sabhi sajaavati saamaanon ke saath constantinopel bhej diya. 
 bahut hi ucchkoti ke bahari sangemarmar ko sadiyon se hataa diya gaya he or unamem se kuch bhitti stambhon ke shikhar british sangrahalay main hai. do stambhon ko un madhyayugin imaaraton ne nigal liya jo purv disha se vishwa devalayas se sansakt kar di gayi or ve hameshaa ke liye kho gaye. satrahavin sadi ke prarambh main, urban VIII berberini ne baraamde kii kaanse kii chat ko faad daalaa or madhyayugin ghantaghar ko bernini dwaara nirmit prasiddh judawaan toweron se sthanantarit kar diya, jise unnisvin sadi ke uttararddh tak nahin hataaya gaya. keval ek anya nuksaan bahya murtiyon kaa hua, jo agrippa ke shilaalekh ke upar kii trikonika ko savanrati thim. aantarik sangemarmar kaaphi had tak bachaa rahaa haalanki bade paimaane par ise punarsthapit kiya gaya.
 punarjagran ke baad se vishwa devalayas kaa ek kabr ke rup main prayog kiya gaya he. vahaan dafanaaye gaye logon main chitrakaar rafel or anibal karaki, sangeetkaar arkengelo coreli or vaastukar baldassare perujzi shaamil hai. 15 veen sadi main vishwa devalayas chitron se sajaa tha: sabase prasiddh chitra melojjo da forly dwaara nirmit anansieshan he. bruneileshi jaise vaastukaron ne, jinhone 'cathedral of florence ke gumbad kii dizin banane main vishwa devalayas kii sahaayta lee, vishwa devalayas ko apane kaaryon ke liye prerana mana he.
 pope urban VIII ne vishwa devalayas ke baraamde kii kaanse kii chat ko pighalaane kaa aadesh diya. zyaadaatar kaanse kaa upyog saint' angelo kaisal kii kilevandi ke liye gola banane main hua, tathaa bache hue pariman ko dharmik netaaon dwaara vibhinn kaaryon main istemaal kiya gaya. yah kaha jaataa he ki bernini ne kaanse kaa istemaal saint peter ke basilika kii oonchi vedi ke upar apane prasiddh chandova ke nirmaan main kiya tha, lekin kam se kam ek visheshgy ke anusaar, pope ke khaaton se pataa chalata he ki 90% kaanse kaa upyog top ke liye kiya gaya tha or chandova ke liye kansaa venice se aayaa tha. isane prasiddh roman vyangyakaar asquino ko is kahawat tak pahunchaaya: kuod non faserunt barbari faserunt berberini ne nahin kiya vah berberini ne kiya"). 
 1747 main, apni nakli khidakiyon ke saath gumbad ke neeche kii vyapak chitra vallari ko phir se "behaal" kiya gaya lekin isamen mul se bahut kam samanta he. beesvin sadi ke aarambhik dashakon main, punarjagran kaal ke nakshon or chitron ke aadhaar par banaaya gaya, mul kaa ek tukdaa, ek panel main punah nirmit kiya gaya.
 vahaan italy ke do raajaaon: vittorio emanuele dwitiya or ambarto pratham, ke saath ambarto kii rani margherita bhi dafan hai. haalanki italy 1946 se ek ganatantr ban gai he, italvi rajtantrvaadi sanghathanon ke swayamsevak sadasyon ne vishwa devalayas main sthit shahi makbaron par nigaraanee jari rakhi. ganatantrvaadiyon kii ore se samay-samay par iskaa virodh kiya gaya he, haalanki, rakrakhaav or suraksha kaa prabharee italvi saanskrithik viraasat mantralay he, lekin catholic adhikariyon kii anumati se yah abhyaas jari rahaa.
 abhi bhi vishwa devalayas kaa ek carch ke rup main prayog kiya jaataa he. yahaan aam janata ke utsav manaae jate hai, visheshkar mahatvapurn catholic aabhaar diwason or shaadiyon ke avasar par.
 multah imaarat tak pahuchane ke liye sidhiyaan bani thim. pracheenta ke baad se aasapaas ke kshetra main jameen kaa str kaaphi ooncha ho gaya.
 trikonika ko shaayad sone kaa pani chadhe kaanse kii relif murtiyon se sajaayaa gaya tha. murtiyon ko lagane vaale kilkon ke sthaan ko chihnit karne vaale chhedon kii sthiti yah sujhaati he ki isaki akruti ek maala ke bich bane baj jaisi thi, maala se vistaarit feete trikonika ke kinaaron tak jate the.
 vishwa devalayas kii dyodhi mul rup se korinthian style main 50 roman foot lambe dandon or 10 roman foot lambe shikharon ke saath akhand grenite stambhon ke liye bani thi. lambi dyodhi madhyavarti block par dikhaai detii duusari trikonika ko chipa sakati thi. iske bajaay, banane waalon ne 40 roman foot lambe dandon or aath roman foot lambe shikharon ke upyog ke liye kai ajeeb samaayojan kar die the. yah pratisthaapan shaayad vishwa devalayas ke nirmaan ke kisi charan main utpann sahay-sahakaar sambandhi kathinaaiyon kaa parinaam tha. vastav main vishwa devalayas ke saamne ke ahaate main prayukt bhure grenite ke stambh misr main purvi pahaado par mons claudinus main utkhanit the. prayek 39 feet lanbaa, paanch foot vyas kaa or vajan main 60 ton tha. inhen lakdi ke slage par 100 kimi se adhik ghasitkar khadaan se nadi tak laayaa gaya tha. unhen vasant kii baadh ke dauraan jab nil nadi main pani kaa str ooncha rahata tha bajre par lad kar laayaa jaataa tha or baad main bumadhya sagar ko paar kar ostia ke roman bandargah tak pahunchane ke liye jahaaj par sthanantarit kiya jaataa tha. vahaan unhen phir se bajre par sthanantarit kiya jaataa tha or ko rome kii tiber nadi tak laayaa jaataa tha. 
 augustus kii samadhi ke paas utaare jane ke baad bhi vishwa devalayas lagbhag 700 meter dur rahata tha. is prakaar, yah jaruri ho gaya tha yaa to unhen yaa to khinchaa jaataa yaa rolers par nirmaan sthal tak le jaaya jaataa.
 vishwa devalayas kii dyodhi ke peeche kii deevaaron main aale hai, jo shaayad julius sijar, augustus sijar or agrippa kii murtiyon yaa shaayad kapitolin tray, yaa devataaon ke duusare samooh kii sthapana ke liye the.
 sela ke kabhi sone se madhe kaanse ke bade daravaaje, pracheen hai, lekin vishwa devalayas ke mul daravaaje nahin hai. vartmaan daravaaje - daravaaje kii choukhat ke hisaab se bahut chote bane hai- ye 15veen shatabdi ke baad se yahaan hai.
 4,535 metric ton vajan ke roman kancreet gumbad vossoyar 9.1 meter vyas main ek challe par kendrit hai jo aankh banaata he, jabaki gumbad ke neeche kaa jor aath bairal walt dwaara 6.4 meter moti drum diwaar main aath khambhon par bheja jaataa he. gumbad kii motaai gumbad aadhaar par 6.4 meter se aankh 1.2 meter ke chaaron ore bhinn-bhinn hoti he. vishwa devalayas main prayukt kancreet par koi tanyata pariksha parinaam uplabdh nahin hai lekin koven ne libya ke roman khandhiron par hue pareekshanon par charchaa kii, jo 2.8 keesai  kii thos takat di he. ek anubhavjanya rishta is namoonay ke liye 213 pound prati varg inch kii ek tanyata takat detaa he. mark or hachisan dwaara sanrachana ke parimit tatv vishleshan main keval 18.5 pound prati varg inch hi ek adhiktam tanya tanaav paaya gaya, jo us sandhi sthal par mila jahaan gumbad uthaai gai bahari diwaar se miltaa he. gumbad kii oonchi paraton main kam ghanatv vaale samgr pattharon kaa kramik upyog karne par gumbad ke tanaav ko kaaphi had tak kam paaya gaya. mark or hachisan kaa anumaan he ki agar puure gumbad main saamaanya vajan ke kancreet kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he to mehrab main tanaav kuch 80% adhik ho gaya. aantarik antah falak na keval sajaavati he balki chat kaa vajan bhi kam ho gaya tha, jaisaa ki akyalas ke madhyam se shikhar ke unmoolan ke rup main kiya gaya.
 golakaar diwaar ke upar eent ke mehrab kii shrinkhalaa banaayi gayi he, jo baahar se dikhti he or jo adhiktar eenton par bani he. vishwa devalayas main aisi kai chijen bhari hui hai- udhaaharan ke liye, vahaan bheetar gupt sthaan main mehrab hai - lekin andar in sabhi mehrabon ko sangemarmar ke neeche chupa diya gaya or baahar sambhavat inhen pushta yaa plaster dwaara chupaaya gaya tha.
 aankh kii oonchai or aantarik chakra kaa vyas ek hi he, 43.3 meter, isliye puraa abhyantar ghan ke bheetar feet ho jaayegaa vyas ke kshetra main ek vrutt bana saktaa he). in ayaamon ko pracheen rome ke maap kii ikaayiyon main vyakt karne par adhik behatar dhang se samajha ja sakegaa: gumbad kaa failaav 150 roman feet he, aankh kaa vyas 30 roman feet; dwaar 40 roman foot ooncha he. vishwa devalayas abhi bhi duniya ke sabase bade asanrakshit kancreet gumbad kaa record rakhata he. yah pehle ke gumbadon se kaaphi bada he.
 haalanki, aksar yah ek mukt imaarat ke rup main taiyaar kii gayi thi, iske paas ek or imaarat thi jo isse lagakar banaayi gayi thi. haalanki is imaarat ne gol ghar ke pushte ko sahaara diya, lekin ek-duusare ke bich main jane kaa vahaan koi aantarik maarg nahin he.
 gumbad kii aantarik sajja sambhawatah swarg ke dhanushakar walt ko pratik banane ke makasad se kii gayi thi. gumbad ke sheersh par bani aankh or pravesh dwaar hi prakaash andar aane kaa strot hai. puure din, is kshetra main aankh se aanevaala prakaash chaaron ore dhoopghdi ke vipareet prabhaav main ghumta rahata he. aankh thanda karne or hawa nikasi pranali ke rup main bhi kaary karti he. tufaan ke dauraan, farsh ke neeche bani ek jal nikasi vyavastha barish ke samay aankh se girane vaale pani kii vyavastha karti he.
 gumbad main paanch panktiyon main athis dhanse hue falak pradarshit hai. samaan rup se bante is sthaan kaa nakshaa bananaa mushkil tha or yah samajha jaataa he ki yah sankhyatmak, jyamitiya yaa chandra sambandhi pratikatmak arth rakhata he. pracheen samay main, sanduukon main kaanse ke sitare, gulab, yaa anya gahane nihit ho sakate hai.
 andar kii dizin ke ekikrut vishay ke rup main vritton or virgon ko chunaa gaya. shatranj kii bisaat ke namoonay kaa farsh gumbad main bane chaukor sandukon ke sankendrik vritton ke pratikul he. farsh se lekar chat tak, abhyantar kaa har hissaa, ek alag yojana ke anusaar samavibhajit he. natijatan, abhyantar ke sajaavati kshetra vyavasthit nahin hai. imaarat kii pramukh dhuree ke anusaar samgr prabhaav tatkaal darshak abhivinyas he, haalanki ek ardhgol gumbad se dhaki belanaakaar jagah swaabhaavik rup se aspasht he. hameshaa is visangati kii saraahna hi nahin hoti or 18veen sadi main ataari ke str ko nav-shastreey ruchi ke anusaar phir se banaaya gaya tha.
 vartmaan ucch vediyaan or ardhgolakaar jharokhon ko pope klement ileven XI ne banwaaya tha or isaki dizin specchhi alesandro ne taiyaar kii thi. ardhgolakaar jharokhe main, madonna kii bijantin pratimaa kii ek pratikruti pratishthapit kii gayi he. ab vatican main padariyon ke chote giraje main maujuud mul pratimaa ko 13 veen sadi kaa kaalankit kiya gaya he, haalanki paramparaon kaa daawa he ki yah kaaphi puraani he. giraje kaa purvi bhaag ko 1840 main joda gaya tha or iskaa dizin luigi poletty ne bane thi.
 pravesh dwaar ke daahine ke aale main kisi agyaat kalakaar dwaara banaayi gayi girdal kii madonna or baari ke saint nicholas ke chitra pradarshit he.dahini ore ke pehle giraje, da chapel of anansieshan main melojzo da forli ko samarpit ek bhittichitra he. bain ore calement mioli dwaara banaaya gaya saint laurence or saint agnes kaa canvas maujuud he. daahine taraf kii diwaar par potro paolo bonzi dwaara nirmit saint thomas kaa avishvaas  laga hua he.
 duusare aale main tascan sampradaay kaa 15 veen sadi kaa bhitti chitra he, jisamen virgin ke rajbhishek kaa chithran kiya gaya he. duusare girjaghar main samraat victor imanul dwitiya kii samadhi he. yah mul rup se pavitra aatma ko samarpit tha. isaki dizine koun vaastukar banaayegaa yah tay karne ke liye ek pratiyogita yojit kii gayi thi. gusape sexoni ne isamen bhaag liya, lekin haar gaye - baad main unhone usee ke vipareet sthit girjaghar main ambarto pratham kii kabr kii dizin banaai thi. manfradio manfredy ne pratiyogita jeet lee or 1885 main kaam shuru kar diya. kabr main kaanse kii vishaal pattica par roman baj or house of savoy ke aayudh bane hai. kabr ke upar victor immenul tritiy ke sammaan main sunaharaa deepak jalataa rahata he, jinaki 1947 main nirvaasan main maut hui thi.
 tisare aale main saint eni or saubhagyashaaleeni virgin kii murti rakhi hui thi. tisare girjaghar main 15veen shatabdi kii ambrian sampradaay kii tasviir,da madonna of marsi bitwin saint francis or saint jon da baptist rakhi hui he. ise madonna of da railing ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he, kyonki mul rup se yah baraamde ke baayin taraf ke aale main tangi rahati thi, jahaan yah ek railing dwaara sangrakshit thi. ise chapel of anansieshan le jaaya gaya or 1837 ke kuch samay baad ise vartmaan sthaan main pahunchaa diya gaya. kaanse kii sookhti main pope klement gyarahawein dwaara abhayaarany ko punarsthapit karne kaa smarak he. dahini ore kii diwaar par kisi agyaat kalakaar dwaara banaai gai pope boniphase chaturth ko vishwa devalayas pradaan karte hue samraat focus kii tasviir pradarshit kii gayi he. vahaan farsh par teen smarak falak hai, ek main sthaneey bhashaa main likhe hue gismonda ko shradhanjali di gai he. dahini ore ke antim aale main saint enestesiyo 1725 kii pratimaa he jise barneredino kametti ne banaaya he.
 pravesh dwaar ke baayi ore pehle aale main andre kamaase kaa ek asampshan he. baayin ore sthit pehla girizaa, mathrubhumi main saint joseph kaa chapel he or yah vishwa devalayas main guni logon kii sanstha kaa girizaa he. yah kalakaaron or sangeetkaaron kii sanstha kii ore ishaara karata he, jise yahaan 16veen shatabdi main carch ke padri, decidario da segni dwaara yah sunishchit karne ke liye gathit kiya gaya tha ki girjaghar main sahi dhang se puja ho sake. praathmik sadasyon main pramukh the, antonio da sangaalo yuva, jecopo meneghino, giovanni mengon, jukkaari, domenico bekkafumi or fleminio vekka. is sanstha ne sadsy ke rup main rome ke kuleen vaastukaron or kalakaaron ko bhi aakarshith kiya, baad ke sadasyon main hamen burnini, cortona, algardi or kai anya kalakaar milte hai. yah sanstha abhi bhi maujuud he or ab ise acedemia pontephisia di belle arty ke naam se janaa jaataa he, jo canceleria ke prasaad main avasthit he. girjaghar kii vedi nakli sangemarmar se aachhadit he. vedi par vincenzo di rossi dwaara banaai gai saint joseph or pavitra shishu kii ek pratimaa sthit he. kinare par francisco kojja dwaara banaayi gayi tasviren, utkrisht main se ek: baayin taraf charavaahon kii aradhana or dayee taraf megi kii aradhana hai. baayin ore relif plaster par, paolo bengalia dwaara nirmit dream of saint joseph he or daahine ore kaa rest during da flite fram ejipt carlo monaldi dwaara banaaya gaya he. tijori par 17 veen sadi ke kai canvas hai, baayen se daaen: ludovico giminani dwaara kumian sibil, francesco roza dwaara mojes; giovanni perugzini dwaara atornal fadar ; luigi garzi dwaara david ; or giovanni andria carlon dwaara eritrean sibil .
 duusare aale main vincenko phelisi dwaara banaayi gayi saint agnes kii murti he. bain taraf kii arddh pratimaa baldassare perujzi kii portret he, jo giovanni dupre dwaara banaayi gayi plaster portret se banaayi gayi he. raja ambarto pratham or unki patni margharita di savoi kii kabr agle girjaghar main he. yah girjaghar multah mahadoot saint maaikel or phir dharmadoot saint thomas ko samarpit kiya gaya tha. vartmaan dizin gusape sexoni dwaara taiyaar kii gai thi, jise unki mrityu ke baad unke shishy gudo sirilli dwaara puraa kiya gaya. yah kabr sone ke pani chadhe kaanse ke frem main lagaai gai sangemarmar kii ek pattee se yukt he. chitra vallari main, yugenio makagnani dwaara nirmit jenrasity or arnaldo zokchi dwaara nirmit munifisans kaa roopak pratinidhatv he. shahi makbaron kii dekh-rekh, 1878 main sthapit naishanal insticute of honor gards dwaara kii jaati he. ve kabron par paharaa dene ke liye gards kii bhi vyavastha karte hai. shahi hathiyaaron ke saath vedi sirilli dwaara banaai gai he.
 tisare aale main nashwar avshesh hai - unka ossa at sineres, "haddiyaan or raakh", taabut par maujuud shilaalekh ke anusaar - mahaan kalakaar rafel dwaara nirmit he. uski mangetar, maaria bibbiena unke taabut ke dahini ore dafan he, ve shaadi kar pate iske pehle hi uski mrityu ho gai thi. taabut pope gregory XVI dwaara diya gaya tha or is par ankit he ille hik ast rafel timuit kuo sospite vinchi/rerum magna parence at moriente mori, jiskaa arth he "yahaan rafel leta he, jiske jeevankaal main har vastu kii maa usase parajeet hone se darati thi or jab vah mar rahaa tha to vah swayan marane kii ashanka kar rahi thi." puralekh pietro bembo dwaara likhaa gaya tha. vartmaan vyavastha 1811 se he, ise antonio munoj dwaara dizin kiya gaya tha. rafel kii ardh-pratimaa gusape fabris dwaara nirmit he. do falak maaria bibbiena or annible karrakki ko di gai shradhanjali hai. kabr ke peeche boulder he madonna del sasso ke rup main jaani jane vaali murti he, ek pair chattan par tikaa hone kii vajah se ise yah naam diya gaya he. yah rafel dwaara pramanit or 1524 main lorengetto dwaara nirmit he.
 suuli par chadhaane ke giraje main, aalon main roman eent kii deevaaron kaa kaary najar aata he. vedi par lakdi kaa cross 15 veen shatabdi se laga hua he. baayin ore kii diwaar par pitro labruzi dwaara banaaya gaya pavitra aatma kii vansh parampara he. dahini taraf kii nichi relif par danish murtikar bertel thorvaldsen dwaara banaaya gaya cardinal konsalvi pregents to pope pius VII da five provincej ristord to da holi si  pradarshit he. ardhpratimaa cardinal augustino rivarola kaa ek chitra he. is taraf ke antim aale main francesco modereti dwaara banaayi gayi saint resius  he.
 punarjagran ke baad se vishwa devalayas pracheen roman smaarkeey imaaraton main sabase achche sangrakshit udhaaharan ke rup main, pashchimi vaastukala main atyadhik prabhaavshaalee rahaa he, yah florence main bruneileshi ke santa maaria del fiore ke 42-meter gumbad ke saath shuru hokar, 1436 main puraa hua tha. kuch ne to vishwa devalayas kii akruti kaa varnan karte hue yahaan tak kaha ki "shaayad yah pashchimi europe main ...sabase adhik prabhaavshaalee" or yah "utkrisht vaastukala kaa sarvaadhik utkrisht pratik" mana jaataa he. unnisvin or beesvin sadi kii kai imaaraton main vishwa devalayas kii style ko dekha ja saktaa he, anek city haul, vishvavidhyalayon or saarvajanik pustakaalayon main iske baraamde or gumbad kii sanrachana se samarupatha dikhti he.
