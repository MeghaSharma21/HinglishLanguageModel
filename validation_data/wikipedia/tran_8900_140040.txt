  anna rajam malhotra  bhartiya prashasnic seva main shaamil hone vaali pehali bhartiya mahila thi. ve 1951 batch kii ai e es adhikari raheen. ye maharashtra se thim.
 anna kaa janm ernakulam main hua. pehle kalikat or baad main madras main shiksha grahan karne ke pashchaat unhone 1951 main bhartiya civil seva kii pariksha uteern kii or bhartiya prashasnic seva main shaamil hone vaali pehali bhartiya mahila baniin. 1951 main sangh lok seva ayog dwaara sanchalit aar en banerjee or chaar isies adhikariyon ke shaamil saakshaatkaar board main unhen kaaphi hatotsahit kiya gaya or unhen videsh seva or kendriya sevaaon ko chunane hetu kaha gaya, kintu unhone bina hatotsahit huye madras kader chunaa or pehle prayaas main hi usee varsh unka chayan hua. unka praarambhik naam anna jorge he. unhen 1989 main bharat sarkaar dwaara prashaskeey seva ke kshetra main ullekhneey kaary hetu padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. 17 sitambar 2018 ko unka nidhan ho gaya.
