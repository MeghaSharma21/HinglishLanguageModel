 potsgabam rendi singh yaa sirf rendi singh ek bhartiya purv footbal khilaadi he, jo vartmaan main epasi pune city of indian super lig ke sahaayak coach hai.
 vah pehle mohan bagan, east bengal club, everridi ese or jesiti epasi ke liye khele hai. haal hi main unke saathi team ke dost or dost sunil chhetri ne media se kaha ki rennedy desh main sarvshereshth midfilder he, lekin yah bhi sabase kam darja diya or andekha bhartiya footballar he. rennedy or sunil chhetri jesiti epasi team main pramukh aankade the, unhone jesiti epasi ko neshnal footbal lig 2006-2007 or ai-lig 2007-08 main duusare or tisare sthaan par hasil kiya. unki jaden ek prasiddh footbal hub, sekhmai tak hai. vah bharat ke apane hi mrit gend visheshgy hai or 2011 ke asian cap team main sabase anbhavi sadsy hai. kai anya maujuuda bhartiya antarrashtriyon kii tarah, rennedy jamshedpur main prasiddh tiefee academy kaa snaatak he. unhone 1 99 7 main east bengal ke saath apana pehla vyavsaayik anubandh karne se pehle chah saal bitaye. laal or swarna brigade ke saath midfield se rennedy ke pradarshan ne unhen 98 main apana pehla senior antarrashtriya cap diya, jab vah keval 1 9 tha.
 2000 tak, rinedy ne khud ko desh main sarvshereshth freekik lene vaale ke rup main sthapit kiya or mohan bagan ne hastakshar kiye. unke karier kaa sabase acha charan maari janewaalon ke saath tha kyonki unhone 2001-02 ke rashtriya lig kii jeet ke dauraan prabhaavshaalee bhoomika nibhaai thi. rennedy kii donon gendon ke saath gend ko paar karne kii kshamta ne unhen bhartiya rashtriya team main mukhya aadhaar banaaya, khaskar bharat kii 2002 ke alji cap kii safalataa ke pradarshan ke baad.
  chot lagane se unke farm main ek dubkee ai or vah avishwasaneey rup se chiraag united main shaamil ho gaye, tab gyaat har rose tha or 2004 main bagaan chhodane ke baad sheersh udaan main nahin the. haalanki unhone bhartiya team ke liye acha pradarshan kiya or vastav main bharat ke niraashajanak 2006 vishwacap ke kwalification abhiyaan main do gol ke saath sheersh scorer rahe. 2005 main jesiti main shaamil hone ke baad rennedy ke club karier kaa punaruddhar hua kyonki unhone phagavada sthit club ko 06-07 sijan main neshnal lig main doosraa sthaan hasil karne main madad kii.
 Renedy 2008 main apane kariyar main duusari baar east bengale main shaamil hue lekin choton or match fitness kii kami ke kaaran kabhi bhi vahaan niyamit nahin tha. haalanki unhone kuch bade kolkata doriyon main taare banaae jo set ke tukdon se shandaar vitaran ke saath sahaayta pradaan karte hai. roni bob hautan ke kaarykaal ke dauraan bhartiya team kaa ek pramukh sadsy rahe hai, lekin 200 9 ke nehru cap ke baad se vah pehle gyaarah main niyamit rup se nahin rahaa he kyonki choton ke kaaran unki samasyaon ke kaaran vah niyamit rup se nahin rahe hai. bob hautan yug main ab tak unka sarvshereshth vyaktigat kshan andeh ke bina 200 9 ke nehru cap final main syria ke khilaaf kiye gaye romantic karling freekik tha.
  kingfisher east bengal ke saath ek bhuulane vaale 2010 ke mausam ke baad, unhen club dwaara jari kiya gaya tha. vah bhartiya rashtriya footbal team main niyamit rahe or unhone kuch kaushal dikhaaya jo logon ko yaad rakhti he ki vah kya kar sakate hai. 2011 eeffsi asian cap main bharat kaa pratinidhitiv karne ke baad, unhone iayeeji lig dwitiya divijan main baichung bhutia ke apane club sanyukt sikkim epasi ke saath ek anubandh par sahmati vyakt kii.
  16 janvari, 2011 ko, singh ne aadhikarik taur par ai-lig ke shilong lajong ke liye aadhikarik taur par hastakshar kiye the.
  27 farvari 2015 ko, vah balgerian paksh sieskee sophia main kerala blasters se rin ke saath jud gaya, ek balgerian club ke liye sin karne vaale pehle bhartiya the.
  rennedy 1 99 8 se, 2011 tak apni rashtriya team bharat main ek niyamit rup se najar rakhi hui he.
  vah bhartiya team kaa mahatvapurn hissaa tha jisane nehru cap antarrashtriya footbal toornament 2007 or 2008 eeffsi chalange cap jeeta tha. vah paar karne main acha he or unki gati unhen bhartiya team kaa ek abhinn ang banaate hai.
 bharat ne match ganvaaya jabaki, rennedy ne 2011 main asian cap main bahrain ke khilaaf bharat ke liye gol karne kii mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai.
