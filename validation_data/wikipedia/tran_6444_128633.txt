 aavashyak vasiy amla jise praayah vitamin-epa bhi kah dete hai, vasiy amla se bana hota he. isiilie iskaa naam vitamin-epa padaa he. ye do prakaar ke hote hai - omega-3 tathaa omega-6. is vitamin kaa mukhya kaary sharir ke utakon kaa nirmaan or unki marammat karnaa hota he. food and drug administration vitamin-epa ko apane din ke puure calories bhuktakriya main se ek se do pratishat grahan karne kaa sazaav detaa he. iske alaava, sharir ke chayapachay, baalo tathaa twachaa ke liye bhi vitamin-epa kaaphi laabhadaayak hote hai. sharir main jab bhi kahin chot lagati he to usase twachaa ke utakon ko kaaphi hani pahunchati he. vitamin-epa in utakon kii marammat kar unhen thik karte hai.
 keval do prakaar ke aavashyak vasiy amla hote hai: alpha-linolenic amla jo ek omega-3 vasiy amla evam linolenic amla jo ek omega-6 vasiy amla he. kuch shodhakarta gamma linolenic amla, loric amla, evam pamitoleik amla ko kuch sthitiyon sahit aavashyak maanate hai.
 inhen multah vitamin eff kaha gaya tha, jab 1923 main inaki atyaavashyak poshak tattvon ke rup main khoj hui thi. 1930 main barr, barr evam miller ke mushakon par hue shodhon dwaara gyaat hua ki inhen vasa ke sang behatar vargeekrut kiya ja saktaa he, bajaay vitamin ke sang.
 vitamin-epa kii kami ke kaaran log jaldi thak jate hai. iske alaava kamjor bal, rukhi twachaa evam sukhi ankhen, vitamin-epa kii khanpan main kami kaa sidhaa sanket hote hai. isaki kami se twachaa rog jaise egjima kaa khatra bhi bad jaataa he. yah vitamin pet main jeevanu bhi banaata he, jisase pachan shakti main vruddhi hoti he. sharir main vitamin-epa kii purti hetu iske mukhya sroton main machhlee evam moongfalee aate hai, visheshkar macrel, toona machhlee vitamin-epa ke bade strot hai. shaakaahaari logon ke liye vitamin-epa praapt karne kaa sabase pehla lakshya he ki vah apane khaadya tel main badlaav laaen. surajamukhi, rai, akhrot aadi ke tel kaa khaanaa pakaane main upyog karen. in telon ke vitamin-epa ke alaava kai anya poshak tatv bhi hote hai. 
 vasiy amla sidhi kadi hydrocarbon hote hai jinamein ek carboxil samooh ek sire par judaa hota he. is samooh se aglaa carbon α kehalaata he or usase aglaa carbon β or isi prakaar anya carbon anuon kaa namakaran hota he. kyonki jaev vasiy amla vibhinn lambaaiyon ke ho sakate hai, inaki antim posishan "ω" kehalaati he, jo unani bhashaa kii varnmaala kaa antim akshar he. asantrupt vasiy amlon ke sanrachanaatmak gun pratham asantrupti bandh par nirbhar karte hai, na ki carboxelette par; atah ye posishan dwaara dikhaayi jaati he. udhaaharan ke liye ω-3 se gyaat hota he ki pratham doharaa bandh carbon kadi ke CH3 sire se tisare carbon-carbon bandh rup main sthit hota he. carbon parmaanu or dohare bandhon kii sankhya bhi di jaati he. ω-3 18:4 yaa 18:4 ω-3 yaa 18:4 n−3 se gyaat hota he ki ek 18-carbon kii kadi chaar dohare bandhon sahit he, jo CH3 se teesri posishan par hai. dohare bandh sis hai or ek mithaylin samooh se vibhakt hai, yadi anyathaa likhit na ho to. atah ek mukt vasiy amla rup main, stridonic amla kii rasinic sanrachana is prakaa se he:

 . carbon ke rasinic yogicon ko carbonic yogic kehete hai. prakruti main inaki sankhya 10 lakh se bhi adhik he. jivan paddati main carbonic yogicon kii bahut hi mahatvapurn bhoomika he. inamen carbon ke saath-saath hydrogen bhi rahata he. aitihaasik tathaa parampara gat kaarnon se kuch carbon ke yogakon ko carbonic yogicon kii shrenee main nahin rakhaa jaataa he. inamen carbondioxide, carbon monooxide pramukh hai. sabhi jaev anu jaise carbohydrate, amino amla, protein, arene tathaa diene carbonic yogic hi hai. carbon or hydrogen ke yogico ko hydrocarbon kehete hai. methen sabase chote anusutra kaa hydrocarbon he. ethane, propen aadi iske baad aate hai, jinamein kramash: ek ek carbon judata jaataa he. hydrocarbon teen shreniyon main vibhajit kiye ja sakate hai: ethane shrenee, ethilin shrenee or asitilin shrenee. ethane shrenee ke hydrocarbon santrupt hai, arthath inamen hydrogen kii maatra or badhaai nahin ja sakati. ethilin main do carbonon ke bich main ek dwibandh he, asitilin main trigun bandha vaale yogic asthaayee hai. ye aasaani se oxikrut evam hellojanikrut ho sakate hai. hydrocarbonon ke bahut se vyutpann taiyaar kiye ja sakate hai, jinke vividh upyog hai. aise vyutpann chloride, bromide, iodide, alcohal, sodium alcoxide, amin, maracaptain, nitrate, nitrite, nitrite, hydrogen phosphate tathaa hydrogen sulphate hai. asatrupt hydrocarbon adhik sakriy hota he or anek abhikaarakon se sanyukt ha saralata se vyutpann banaata he. aise anek vyutpan audyogic drushti se bade mahatv ke siddh hue hai. inase anek bahumoolya vilaayak, plastic, kriminashak oshadhiyaan aadi praapt hui hai. hydrocarbonon ke oxikaran se alcodaul ether, kitone, aldihide, vasa amla, aster aadi praapt hote hai. alcodaul praathmik, dwitiyak or tritiyak ho sakate hai. inke aster drav sugandhit hote hai. anek sugandhit dravya inase taiyaar kiye ja sakate hai. isi prakaar aavashyak vasiy amla ko bhi vibhinn prayogon main liya ja saktaa he.
