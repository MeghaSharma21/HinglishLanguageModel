 bhautikee or ejiniyari main kisi mekensm dwaara utpann bal tathaa us par lagaaye bal ke anupaat ko yantric laabh kehete hai.
 gharshanrahit adarsh mekenigmon ke liye ise nimna prakaar bhi vyakt kar sakate hai-
 kraine aadi bahut sari yuktiyaan yantric laabh par aadhaarit hai jinamein kam bal lagaakar bhi bahut adhik bal yaa tork paidaa kiya jaataa he. dhyaan rahe ki ki isamen koi atirikt shakti yaa urja utpann nahin kii ja rahi he.
