 ikhtiyaruddin mohammad bin bakhtiyaar khilji, jise bakhtiyaar khilji bhi kehete hai, qutubuddin abak kaa ek sainya sipahasaalar tha. 
 khilji ne 1203 main bihar par jeet hasil kar dilli main apane raajnneetik kad ko ooncha uthaaya. is vijay abhiyaan ke dauraan khilji kii sena ne pracheen nalanda vishvavidhyalay or vikramshila vishvavidhyalay ko nestnabut kar hajaaron kii sankhya main buddha bhikshuon kii hatya kar di. 
 iske agle saal khilji ne bengal par vijay hasil kar bhartiya upmahadweep ke is bhaag par islam ko sthapit karne main ahm bhoomika nibhaai. ek muslim kavi ke anusaar, nadia shahar par chadhaai ke samay vah itani teji se aage badha ki keval 18 ghudsavaar hi uske saath chal paae. shahar main pahunchane par ghoda vyapaaree samajhakar use raja lakshmana sen ko khaane ke bich main hi mulaakaat karne kii ijaajat de di. paristhitiyon ko bhaanp raja lakshmana ko khaali pair hi kile ke pichley daravaaje se bhaagna padaa. haalanki is naatakeey chadhaai ke bare main itihaaskaaron main mat vibhinnata he.
 khilji rajdhani gaur or bengal ke anya bhaagon main kabja jamaane main kaamyaab rahaa, lekin purvi or dakshini bengal swatantra hi rahe or lakshmana sen ke uttaradhikariyon dwaara bikrampur se shasit kiye jate rahe. 1206 main khilji tibet kii ore kooch kiya, jahaan se lautate hue uski maut ho gai.
