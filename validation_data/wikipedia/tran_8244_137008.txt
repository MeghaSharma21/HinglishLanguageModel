 negery sembilan malaysia kaa ek rajya he. yah prayadwipiya malaysia ke pashchimi tat par sthit he. malay bhashaa main "sembilan" kaa matlab "nau" or negery kaa arth "nagari" yaa "rajya" hota he, yani "negery sembilan" kaa arth "nau gram" he. yah us samay padaa jab sumatra dweep ke pashchimi bhaag se minankaabau samudaay ke logon ne yahan aakar nau gaanv basaaye. negery sembilan ke logon ke chaharon main aaj bhi minankaabau nain-naksh jhalkate hai. 
 adhiktar malaysian rajyon main ek hi rajpariwaar se aane vaala sultan aupchaarik rajadhyaksh hota he, lekin negery sembilan main samay-samay par raaj-mukhiyaaon kaa ek gut aapasi sahmati se apane main se kisi ek ko rajya kaa yamatuan besal chunata he. malaysia main kuch rajyon ko aupchaarik arabi bhashaa ke naam bhi diye jate hai or negery sembilan kaa naam "negery sembilan daar-ul-khusoos" he, yani "negery sembilan, vishesh sthaan".
