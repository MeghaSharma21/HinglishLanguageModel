 bagheli yaa bagheli, hindi kii ek boli he jo bharat ke baghelkhand kshetra main boli jaati he. yah madhya pradesh ke reva, satna, sidhi, umariya, evam anuppur main; uttar pradesh ke elahabaad evam mirzapur jilon main tathaa chhattisgarh ke bilaspur evam korea janapadon main boli jaati he. ise "baghelkhandi", "rimahi" or "riwai" bhi kaha jaataa he.
