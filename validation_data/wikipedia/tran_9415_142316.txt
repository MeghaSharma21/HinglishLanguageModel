 ajay kumaar ghosh  bhartiya communist party ke leader the.ye hindustan socialist republiclan association ke sakriy sadsy rahe tathaa 1928 i. main sardaar bhagat singh, sukhdev, raajaguru evam batukeshwar dutt ke saath karawas gaye evam lahore kand main inhen bhi abhiyukt ke rup main sazaa di gai. 1933 i. main inhone communist party se swayan ko jod liya tathaa 1951 i. main inhen iskaa mahasachiv nirvaachit kiya gaya. 1934, i. main inhen sipiai kaa kendriya cameti sadsy or 1936 main paulitabeuro sadsy nirvaachit kiya gaya. 1951 i. main inhen iskaa mahasachiv nirvaachit kiya gaya. 
 ajay ghosh kaa janm 20 farvari 1909 ko mihajam main hua. jahaan ajay naam kii ek nadi he. unke baba ne us nadi ke naam par hi unka naam ajay rakh diya tha. ajay ghosh ke pita kaa naam shacheendra nath ghosh peshey se doctor the or maan kaa naam sudhanshu bala tha. ajay chaar bhai or do bahan the. is ne elahabaad se biessi paas kiya tha.vah abhi skool main hi pard rahe the, unki mulaakaat bhagat singh se hui. unhon ne angreji sarkaar ko sashastr kranti ke jarie ukhaad phenkane ke liehindustan socialist republican association banaai.
