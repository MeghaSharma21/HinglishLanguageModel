 mewar rajasthan ke dakshin-madhya main ek riyasat thi. ise 'udaipur rajya' ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa tha. isamen aadhunik bharat ke udaipur, bhilwara, rajsamand, tathaa chittorgarh jile the. saikadon saalon tak yahan rajputon kaa shashan rahaa or is par gahalout tathaa sisodiya raajaaon ne 1200 saal tak raaj kiya. baad main yah angrezon dwaara shasit raaj bana.
 1550 ke aasapaas mewar kii rajdhani thi chittod. rana pratap singh yaheen ke raja the. akbar kii bharat vijay main keval mewar ke rana pratap baadhak bana rahe. akbar ne san 1576 se 1586 tak puri shakti ke saath mewar par kai akraman kiye, par uskaa rana pratap ko adheen karne kaa manorath siddh nahin hua swayan akbar, pratap kii desh-bhakthi or dilari se itnaa prabhavit hua ki pratap ke marane par uski aankhon main aansu bhar aaye. usne sweekaar kiya ki vijay nishchay hi rana kii hui. yah ek aitihaasik satya he ki desh ke swatantraaa sangram main pratap jaise mahaan deshpramiyon ke jivan se hi prerana praapt kar anek deshbhakt hanste-hanste balivedi par chadh gaye. maharana pratap kii mrityu par uske uttaradhikari amar singh ne mughal samraat jahangir se sandhi kar lee. usne apane patvi putr ko mughal darbar main bhejnaa sweekaar kar liya. is prakaar 100 varsh baad mewar kii swatantraaa kaa bhi ant hua.
 mewar kaa uttari bhaag samtal he. banaas va uski sahaayak nadiyon kii samtal bhoomi he. chambal bhi mewar se hokar guzarati he. mewar ke dakshini bhaag main aravali parvatamala he,jo kii banaas va uski sahaayak nadiyon ko sabarmati va maahi se alag karti he,jo ki gujarat seemaa main he. aravali uttarpashchim kshetra main he. is kaaran yahan ucch gunvattta pathar ke bhandar he. jiskaa prayog log garh nirmaan main karnaa pasand karte he. mewar kshetra ushnkaribandheey shushk kshetra main aata he. varsha kaa str 660 mimi/varsh, uttar purv main adhiktaya. 90% varsha june se sitambar ke bich padati he. dakshin pashchim monsoon ke kaaran.
 mewar kshetra kii parivartit simaaon ke anurup kshetra kii rajdhaniyaan bhi samyaanusaar badalati rahi thi. itihaas prasiddh durg chittod ke uttar main, 1.5 mile dur sthit "nagari' sthaan bhibi- janapad kii rajdhani tha, jise tatkaaleen samay main manzimica ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. janapad ke nasht hone ke pashchaat 7 veen shatabdi tak pramanik vivaranon ke abhaav main is pradesh kii raajnneetik avasthaa kaa vivaran gyaat nahin hota he, kintu bappa rawal dwaara shashan adhikrut karne ke samay se 13 veen shatabdi ke praarambhik dashak tak ekling, delwada, nagadrah, chikha tathaa aghatpur mewar rajya kii rajdhaniyaan va prashasnic centre rah chuke the.
 rawal jaitsingh ke samay yahan kii rajdhani nagdrah thi, jo sultan ilnutamish ke akraman ke kaaran nasht ho gai. tab rawal ne aghatpur ko naveen rajdhani ke rup main viksit kiya. uske putr rawal tejsingh ne samrik mahatv ko dekhte hue chittod ko apni rajdhani banaaya. sultan allaudin ke chittod akraman ke samay mewar kii rajdhani chittod hi thi. 14 - 15 veen sadi tak chittod va kumbhalgarh mewar kii rajdhani rahe hai. rana kumbha ne kumbhalgarh tathaa rana sanga ne chittod ko apni rajdhani banaaya. rana pratap tathaa uske putr rana amarsingh pratham ne mewar mughal sangharsh kaal mengangunda va chownd naamak sthanon ko sangharsh kaaleen rajdhaniyaan banaayi. 
 rana udaisingh ne pichholi naamak gaanv ko apni rajdhani banaaya. pichholi gaanv hi 17 veen shatabdi ke purvarddh main udaipur nagar ke naam se prasiddh ho gaya. rana karnasingh ke baad se udaipur nagar hi mewar kii sthaayee rajdhani rahaa, jabatak rana bhoopalasinh dwaara 18 april 1948 i. main iskaa vilay nahin kar diya gaya.

 vahi vyavsaayik jatiyaan apana puraa din karmshaalaon main bitaate the. vahiin ve din kaa bhojan karte the. shaam main lautakar bhojan ke baad log parsparik baithak, khelkud, kisse kahaniyon, bhajan- kirttan va graamy sthiti kii charchaa ke madhyam se aamod- pramod karte the. sardiyon main log alaav ke chaaron taraf lambii baithkiyaan karte the.
 graamy- nagaron kaa jivan vaise to graamy- jivan ke prabhaav se mukt nahin tha, lekin logon ke paas saadhan hone kii sthiti main ve "bhagatanon' kaa nrity dekhne, mujre sunne, darbari kriya- kalapon main sevakai karne, bhajan- kirttan tathaa darbar- yatraaon kii jai- jai karne main vyast rahete the. dainik goth, amal- pani, bhaang, ganja, sharaab aadi kaa vyasan kuleen varg ke dainik jivan kaa hissaa tha.

 in sikkon ke atirikt trishulia, dhigala tathaa bhiladee tobe ke sikke bhi prachlit hue. 1805 -1870 ke bich salumbar jaagir dwaara "padmashahi' dhigala sikaa chalaaya gaya, vahiin bhindar jaagir main maharaja joravarasingh ne "bhindriya' chalaaya. in sikkon kii manyata jaagir len- dein tak hi simit thi. maratha- atikaraman kaal ke "mehta' pradhan ne "mehtaashahee' mudra chalaaya, jo badi simit sankhya main milte hai.

 british bharat sarkaar ke sikkon ko bhi rajya main vaidhanik manyata thi. in sikkon ko kaldaar kaha jaataa tha. mewari sikkon se iske moolyantar ko batta kaha jaataa tha. chaandi kii maatra kaa nirdhaaran isi batte ke aadhaar par hota tha. udaipuri 2.5 rupaya ko 2 rupee kaldaar ke rup main mana jaataa tha. 1928 i. main naveen sikkon ke prachlan ke baad tatkaaleen rana bhoopalasinh ne rajya main prachlit in pracheen sikkon ke prayog ko band karvaa diya.
 is prakaar ham pate hai ki yahan kii aarthik vyavastha, vastu- vinimay kii parampara tathaa jan- jivan par gramin vaataavaran ke prabhaav ne mudra kii aavashyaktaa ko simit rakhaa. vaise 19 veen sadi uttararddh main sadak nirmaan, rail line nirmaan va rajkeey bhawan nirmaan tathaa raaj kaa parimaapan mudra main hone laga tha, phir bhi adhiktar- chukaara evam vasuuli jinson main hi prachlit thi. dhaatuon se nirmit in mudraon ke saath ek samasya yah bhi thi ki sankatkaal main in mudraon ko hi galaakar shuddh dhaatu ko bechakar laabh kamaane kii koshish kii ja rahi thi. rajya main maharanaon dwaara vaigyanik sikke ke prachlan main vishesh ruchi nahin rahane ke kaaran shaantikaal main bhi rajya- koshagaar samrudh nahin rahaa, duusari tarah bhu- utpaadan dwaara rajya- bhandar samrudh rahe.
 mewar rajya main aaj kii tarah susangathit sanchaar vyavastha nahin thi. jan- saadhaaran main jatiyon ke apane-apane nai, sevak, chaaran yaa bhaat hi paariwarik sandeshon kaa aadan- pradaan karte the. aisi sandesh- prakriya praayah maukhik hoti thi. rajya kaary ke liye paidal, oont savaar, sandivaar tathaa ghudsavaar rakhe jate the. ve raaj- kaaj se sambhddha soochna vaartaon ko praayah maukhik rup se hi idhar- udhar pahunchaate the. maukhik sandesh- prakriya kaa kaaran sambhawatah us samay kaa sanshayatmak raajnneetik vaataavaran tha. vyapaarik patr maal vaahanon athwa yatriyon ke saath bheje jate the.
 19 veen sadi ke purvarddh tak isi rudhigat vyavastha kaa prayog kiya gaya. is ke baad soochna sanchaar ke liye baggiyaan kaam main bhi jane lagi. ab likhit soochna kaa prachlan shuru ho gaya tha. kintu jansadharan main abhi bhi soochna- vinimay kii koi rajya aadhaarit vyavastha nahin thi. yah vyavastha rana swarupasingh ke shashan se aarambh hui, jo niyamit rajkeey dak laane le jane kaa kaary karti thi. yah vyavastha "bamani- dak vyavastha' ke naam se jaani jaati thi.
 bamani dak vyavastha sambhawatah bengal ke harkara- dak vyavastha se prabhavit thi. is vyavastha main bramhan jati ke logon ko vaarshik theke ke aadhaar par dak sambandhi uttardaayitv pradaan kiya gaya tha. brahman varg ke prati logon kaa aadar va dayaa maan tha. saath- hi- saath chunki brahman ko maarna yaa lootnaa paap- karm mana jaataa tha, atah paise va patr zyada surakshit rahete the. theke main haani hone kii sthiti main rajya dwaara aarthik- anudaan kar kshatipurti kiya jaataa tha. theka lene vaale vyakti ko dak- vyavastha banaaye rakhane ke liye harkare rakhane padate the, jinka masik vetan nishchit kiya jaataa tha. 19 veen shatabdi ke uttararddh main yah prati maah 4 rupaya tha. is dak- vyavastha ko jan- saadhaaran ke upyog hetu rana shanbhusingh ke shashan- kaal main kholaa gaya. jan- saadhaaran ko prati patr kii keemat ke aadhaar par ek nishchit laagat chukaani padati thi. mewar rajya ke andar patron ke aadan- pradaan kii laagat nishchit thi, kintu baahar bheji jane vaali dak par alag se prati kos ke hisaab se paisa liya jaataa tha.
 20 veen shatabdi ke ek dashak tak vamani dak niyamit rup se prayek pargane ke mukhyalay tak jaati thi. alag se koi dak- karyaalay nahin tha. thekedaar kaa ghar tathaa harkare swayan dak- ghar kaa kaary karte the. san 1865 i. main angl sarkaar ne dak- ghar sthapit kiya. iske saath hi nasirabad, kherwada, kotda va chhavnee par chhavnee ke chhaak- ghar khule. inkaa prayog british bharat sarkaar, agenton evam rajya ke karmchaariyon ke samaacharon kaa aadan- pradaan karnaa tha. railway ke vikaas ke bare main jan- saadhaaran ke prayog ke liye prayek railway- stesan par prashasan ne ek- ek dak ghar tathaa taar- ghar khol diya. 19 veen sadi ke ant tak jan- saadhaaran kii soochna niyamit tathaa vyavasthit rup se aane- jane lagi thi.
 mewar rajya main jan- jivan main zyaadaatar kutir gramodyog kaa prachlan tha. in udyogon kaa vistaar aatmanirbhar aarthik- vyavastha ke anurup rajya kii maang tathaa purti tak simit tha. zyaadaatar udyog- dhandhe jati samaaj kii jatiyon va vanshanugat sthitiyon par aadhaarit the. jatigat udyogon main karyrat shilpiyon ke do str the -

 vaanijya-vyavastha se vanik- samooh pratyaksh rup se judaa tha. vyavsaay kii drushti se vanikon ko teen shreniyon main baanta ja saktaa he -
 sahuukaar tathaa anya sampann dwij jati ke vyakti graamy- vanik tathaa nagar- vanik ke madhya kii kadi hote the. ve gaanvon main udhaar len- dein tathaa maal kray- vikray kaa kaary karne the tathaa saath - hi saath nagar-vaanijya kii aavashyaktaanusaar graamy- bhandar se maal ko mandi main thok se vikray karte the. is prakaar doharaa vyapaaree gramin praja ke liye bank tathaa mandi maal ke mukhya sangrahakartta evam vitarak kaa kaary karte the. kuch saude boharon ke bina, pratyaksh bhi kiye jate the. maal sangrah praayah krishak athwa gram- bhandar main hi rakhaa jaataa tha tathaa aavashyaktaanusaar mangavaya jaataa tha. isse dallali ke 4 se 6  tak kii bachat hoti thi.
 acchhee sthiti vaale krishak tathaa jagirdaar athwa upaj sidhe mandiyon main bechate the. rajya dwaara dallalon ko dallali- patte diye jate the. dallali kaa vaarshik shulk aamadani ke anupaat main liya jaataa tha. shulkon ko sangrah karne kaa vaarshik theka prayek vaanijya- vyapaar samooh ke pramukh adhtiya ko pradaan kar diya jaataa tha. bina rajagya kaa koi vyakti dallali nahin kar saktaa tha. rajya kii taraf se sahanaa or dhaani mandi main rajyahiton kaa dhyaan rakhte the. patta paddati dwaara rajya ke kshetreey vaanijya- vyapaar par pratyaksh niyantran rahata tha. vyapaaree manamaane bhav nahin badha sakate the. maal kii kami ho jane par maal kii aapurti rajya dwaara rajya bhandar se kii jaati thi yaa phir baahar se rajya kii jamaanat par mangavaya jaataa tha.
 gaanvon main vyapaar kaa kaam saptahik athwa masik hatwad laga kar kiya jaataa tha. aise hatwad prayek 10- 12 gaanvon ke madhya lagaaye jate the. rajya ke aantarik vyapaar ke pramukh kendra udaipur, bhilwara, rashmi, samawad, kapaasana, jahajpur tathaa choti sadadi the. antarraji vyapaar ke liye mewar ke vanik- gana samuh bana kar kray- vikray hetu durastha pradeshon main jate the. ye vyapaarik yatraayen sardi ke baad prarambh ho jaati thi tathaa varshakaal se purv samaapt ho jaati thi.
 vyapaarik yatayaat- vyavastha
 aalochaykaal main vyapaarik yatayaat kaa mukhya saadhan kachche va patharile maarg rahe the. inhen margon se banajaare belon va bhainson dwaara, gadulia luhaar baiylgaadiyon se, rebaari log unton dwaara, kumhaar tathaa od log khachchar va gadhon par maal laane- le jane kaa kaam karte the. vaise sthaan jahaan pashuon dwaara dh�laai sambhav nahin thi, maal aadmi kii peeth par lad kar laayaa jaataa tha. lambii duuri par maal- dh�laai kaa kaary chaaran, banajaara tathaa gadulia luhaar, jaise ladaaku - bahadur jati ke log sampann karte the. chaaran jati ko samaaj main brahman - tulya sthaan praapt tha, atah inke kaphilon kaa lootnaa paap mana jaataa tha. vyapaarik kafile, jo belon ke jhund par maal lad kar chalate the, baalad kehalaate the. ek baalad main ek se ek hajaar tak bail ho sakate the. unton kaa kafila ek din main kareeb 22 mile kii duuri tay karata tha, vahiin ghode se 50 mile tak kii yatra kii ja sakati thi. baiylgaadee, gadhe, khachchar aadi ek din main 25- 30 mile kii duuri tay kar lete the.
 yatra ke dauraan raatri ko maarg par sthit gaanvon, dharmashalaon, dharmik sthalon yaa chhayaadaar vrikshon ke aas- paas vishram kiya jaataa tha, jahaan pani ke liye kune baavadiyon kii vyavastha hoti thi. maarg sthit sabhi baavadiyon ke kinare pashu ke peya hetu pyouen bani hoti thi.
 18 veen shatabdi ke uttararddh main yatriyon va vyapaariyon ko suraksharth sambhddha jagiradaaron kaa rakhwali evam bolaai naamak raahdari dena padataa tha. vaise to british sangrakshan kaal main in shulkon ko samaapt kar diya gaya, phir bhi jagiradaaron in adhikaron kaa anadhikrut prayog karte the. maratha atikaraman kaal main maarg kaa surakshit yatra beemaa tathaa prati bail ke hisaab se vyapaarik maal beemaa dena padataa tha. varsha ke dinon main maarg avaruddh ho jane kii sthiti main kir naamak jati ke log "utraai' shulk lekar logon ko surakshit nadi paar karaati thi.
 abhijatya tathaa sampann varg ke log palakiyon va baggiyon par yatra karte the.
 chungi vyavastha
 vyapaarik maal kii aamad or nikaas par vyapaariyon ko daan, biswa evam mapa naamak shulk rajya ko dena padataa tha. ek gaanv se duusare gaanv maal le jane ke liye gram- panchayaton ko ""mana' chukaana padataa tha. daan va biswa ke adhikaar praayah rana ke paas hota tha, lekin 18 veen sadi main vishisht sainya- yogyata pradarshit karne vaale kshatriyon ko bhi daan lene ke adhikaar pradaan kiye gayi the. in adhikaron kaa san 1818 i. ke baad kendrikaran karne kii vyavastha kii gai, jo rana swarup singh tak chali bhi. phir se theke kii sir vyavastha todakar sthaan- sthaan par rajya ke daani- chontare banaaye gayi.
 rail kii suvidha aa jane ke baad prayek steshnon par daani - ghar banaaye gayi. daani va harkare niyukt kiye gayi. yahan se maal utaarne va maal chadhaane kii chungi lee jaati thi. pehle chugi nagon kii ginati, anaaj kii tol va pashu gananaa ke aadhaar par lee jaati thi. baad main 20 veen sadi ke purvarddh main shulk liya jane laga. aayaat shulk niryaat shulk se adhik liya jaataa tha. punyarth dharmarth vastuon, ladki ke vivaah va mrityubhoj kii vastuon par chungi nahin lee jaati thi.
