 senthrai subarishan
 muthuvel karunanidhi stallin  ek bhartiya raajnneetijna or purv abhinayta hai jinhen emke stallin naam se behatar tareeke se janaa jaataa he. ve tamilnadu ke mashhoor rajaneta karunanidhi ke tisare bete or unki duusari patni shrimati dayaalu ammal kii santan hai. unka naam joseph stallin ke naam par rakhaa gaya tha jinaki mrityu unke janm ke varsh main hi hui thi. stallin ne chennai main madras vishvavidhyalay ke nandanam arts college se itihaas main apni snaatak str kii padhai puri kii he. stallin 2006 ke vidhansabha chunaavon ke baad tamilnadu sarkaar main gramin vikaas or sthaneey prashasan mantri bane. 29 may 2009 ko stallin ko rajyapal surajeet singh barnaala dwaara tamilnadu ke up-mukhyamantri ke rup main namit kiya gaya tha. unke bade bhai emke ajhagiri rasayan or urvarak mantri hai. uski sauteli bahan kanimojhi ek rajya sabha sadsy hai.
 stallin diemke ke koshadhyaksh or yuva morcha ke adhyaksh ke rup main bhi karyrat hai.
 stallin kaa janm madras main hua tha, jise ab chennai ke rup main janaa jaataa he. unka raajnneetik kariyar 14 varsh kii aayu main 1967 ke chunaavon main prachaar ke saath shuru hua tha. 1973 main stallin ko dravid munetra kajhagam kii aam samiti main nirvaachit kiya gaya tha.
 ve us samay surkhiyon main aae jab unhen apatkaal kaa virodh karne ke liye aantarik suraksha rakrakhaav adhinium ke tahat jail main band kar diya gaya tha. stallin 1989 ke baad se tamilnadu vidhansabha ke liye chennai ke thaugend lits nirvachan kshetra se chaar baar chune gaye hai. stallin 1996 main is shahar ke pehle sidhe taur par nirvaachit meyar bane the.
 2001 main stallin ek baar phir se meyar chune gaye, haalaanki uske baad tatkaaleen mukhyamantri je jayalalita ne tamilnadu nagar kanoon adhinium, 2002 ko adhiniyamit kiya tha. yah ek aisa kaanoon he jo ek vyakti ko sarkaar main do nirvaachit pad rakhane se roktaa he. is kaanoon ko purvyaapee rup se stallin ke maamle par lagu kiya gaya tha jise vyapak rup se unhen chennai ke meyar pad se hataane ke uddeshy se uthaaye gaye kadam ke rup main dekha gaya tha. haalaanki madras ucch nyaayalay ne is kaanoon ko yah kehete hue aprabhavi qaraar diya tha ki vaidhanik nikaayon ko purvyaapee rup se logon ke "moulik adhikaron" ko prabhavit karne vaale kanoon banane se "roka" nahin gaya tha. haalaanki adaalat ne yah mana ki madras city nagar nigam adhinium, 1919 ke tahat ek vyakti lagaataar do kaarykalon ke liye meyar nahin ban saktaa he yadhyapi stallin ke vipareet pehle ke meyar sidhe taur par nirvaachit nahin hue the. stallin ne sarvocch nyaayalay main apil nahin kii. 
 misa jo aatankwaad or gunda adhinium ke kaaphi kareeb he, iske tahat 1975 main pehali baar giraftaar kiye jane ke baad se stallin ko vibhinn saarvajanik muddon par kai baar giraftaar kiya gaya he or unhen purv main gambhir shaareerik dand bhi diya ja chukaa he. karunanidhi ko aadhi raat ko giraftaar kiya janaa jisamen karunanidhi, stallin, maaraan or anya logon ko giraftaar kiya gaya or un par fliover ghotaale kaa aarope lagaaya gaya tha. ise vyapak rup se raajnneetik pratishodh kaa kadam samajha gaya tha kyonki exiaar yaa police main shikaayat shuukravaar raat ko darj karai gayi thi or giraftaaryaan iske kuch hi ghanton ke baad shanivaar subah ko kii gayi thim. haalaanki yah giraftaar 2001 main hui thi, adaalat main aarope patr chaar saal baad 2005 main daayar kiya gaya tha.
 tamilnadu main stallin ne 2009 ke aam chunaav main chunaav prachaar or antat diemke-gathbandhan-sanyukt pragatisheel gathbandhan kii jeet main ek mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai thi.
 diemke ke virdohi, kuch raajnneetik prekshak or diemke party ke sabase-varisht sadsy is baat kii aalochana karte hai ki karunanidhi nehru-gandhi parivaar kii tarj par ek raajnneetik vanshavaad shuru karne kii koshish main stallin ko badhaava dene kii koshish kar rahe hai. diemke kaa saath chhod dene vaale vico sabase adhik mukhar rahe hai or kuch raajnneetik prekshak ise vico ko kinare karne ke ek kadam ke rup main dekhte hai kyonki unhen stallin ke liye ek khatre ke rup main dekha gaya tha.
 haalaanki diemke ke sutron or jin logon ne 70 or 80 ke dashak kii raajnneetik uthal-puthal ko dekha he ve iskaa khandan karte hai or kehete hai ki stallin apni swayan kii yogyata ke aadhaar par aage aaye hai. unka kahana he ki stallin apane kadamon ke boote aage bade hai, 1975 se hi unhen bahut kathinaaiyon kaa saamana karnaa padaa he jab unhen misa ke tahat jail main band kiya gaya tha or apatkaal ke dauraan jail ke andar itani krurta se unki pitayee kii gayi thi ki unhen bachaane kii koshish main diemke party ke unke saathi kaidi kii maut ho gayi thi.
 stallin 1989 or 1996 main ek vidhayak the jab unke pita karunanidhi mukhyamantri the, lekin unhen mantrimandal main shaamil nahin kiya gaya tha. unhone apni ladaai khud ladi or 1996 main chennai ke 44wein meyar bane jisamen unhen pehle sidhe taur par nirvaachit meyar ke rup main chunaa gaya tha. vidhayak ke rup main yah unka chauthaa kaarykaal tha jab unhen karunanidhi mantrimandal main ek mantri banaaya gaya, is tarah unki pragati dhimi or sthir he. ve aage bataate hai ki karunanidhi ne yahan tak ki apane anya putron emke mutthu or emke ajhagiri ko bhi galat kaaryon ke liye doshi paae jane par nishkasit kar diya tha. stallin, apni sauteli bahan kanimojhi ke saath milakar karodon kii sampatti jutaney vaale e raja ko samarthan dene ke liye apane pita ke khilaaf hai.
 1980 ke dashak ke dauraan unhone kuch tamil filmon main kaam kiya he. unhone 1990 ke dashak ke madhya main san tivi ke televijan dharawahikon main bhi abhinay kiya he.
