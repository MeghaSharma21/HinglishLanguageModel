 vigyaan express ek rail par savaar bacchon ke liye ek gatisheel vaigyanik pradarshnee he jo puure bharat main yatra karti he. yah pariyojna 30 oktober 2007 ko safadarajang railway stesan, dilli main vigyaan or praudyogiki vibhaag, bharat sarkaar dwaara shuru kii gai thi. haalanki ye sabhi ke liye khulaa he, ye pradarshnee mukhya rup se chaatro or shikshakon ko lakshith karti hai. 2017 ke anusaar train ke nau charan ho chuke hai or teen vishyon par pradarshan kiya gaya he. 2007 se 2011 ke pehle chaar charnon ko science express kaha jaataa tha or ye micro or macro cosmos par kendrit tha. 2012 se 2014 ke agle teen charnon main jaev vividhta vishesh ke rup main pradarshan ko phir se taiyaar kiya gaya. 2015 se 2017 ke aathven or nauven charan ko jalwaayu parivartan par dhyaan kendrit karne ke liye badal diya gaya tha.
 yah pariyojna vigyaan or praudyogiki vibhaag dwaara 2007 main shuru kii gai thi, or mukhya rup se chaatro or shikshakon ke liye thi. isamen koi pravesh shulk nahin hai. 
 pehle chaar charan micro or macro brahmad par kendrit the or duniya bhar main vigyaan or praudyogiki ke kshetra main kiye ja rahe vikasshil naye shodhon par prakaash daalaa gaya. big baing siddhant, jin, samved, or brahmand jaise vibhinn vishyon par dhyaan kendrit kiya gaya.
 doosraa charan bharat main jaev vividhta par kendrit he or ise vigyaan or praudyogiki vibhaag or van evam paryaavaran mantralay dwaara sanyukt rup se taiyaar kiya gaya tha. yah trans-himalayan or himalayan kshetron, ganga ke maidaanon, uttar purvi pahadiyon, registan, tateey pashchimi ghat, dakkan pathaar or vibhinn dwipon main jivan ke vibhinn pahaluon ko darshata tha. bacchon ke liye kids jone kii shuruyaat kii gai jahaan mazedaar gatividhia jaise ki paheliyaan or vigyaan se judi khel khele ja sake. is express ke 8 kochon par bharat evam vishwa kii jaivik bhugolic vishyon par pradarshnee, ladhu filmon, videon, charto, aankadon ko elidi screenon ke madhyam se pradarshit kiya gaya he. isamen jalwaayu parivartan,urja,jal sangrakshan samet mahatvapurn vishyon ko suruchipurna dhang se darshaya gaya he. is train main visheshgy bhi upasthit rahenge jon pradarshit kiye gaye chizon kii kai bhartiya bhashaa main rochakatapurna vyakhya karenge.
 tisare charan ko jalwaayu parivartan vishay ke liye abhikalpit kiya gaya tha or vigyaan or praudyogiki vibhaag, van evam paryaavaran mantralay, jaev praudyogiki vibhaag, rail mantralay, vikram e sarabhai samudaay vigyaan centre or bhartiya vanyajeev santhaan ke bich sahyogaatmak kaary ke rup main viksit kiya gaya tha. pradarshnee ke nauven charan main 16 dibb the jinamein se 8 ko van evam paryaavaran mantralay ne viksit kiya tha. kuch dibbon ke chat par saura panel sthapit kiye gaye hai or shikshakon ko prashikshit karne ke liye suvidha bhi hai. vieessiessi se ek team, jo puure pradarshnee kaa prabandhan karti he, chaatro ko pradarshnee ke madhyam se maargdarshit karti he or railway plateform par bhi pradarshan sambandhit upkram karti hai.
 apane aathvaan charan main is pradarshnee ne 142,000 kilometre kii yatra kii thi or 455 steshnon par rukii thi. 1600 se zyaada pradarshit dinon main, iskaa daura 33,800 se adhik skulon ke chaatro ne kiya or kareeb 15 karod log aa chuke hai. is prakaar pradarshnee sabase badi, sabase lambey samay tak chalane vaali or sabase zyada dekhi jane vaali pradarshnee he, or limca book of ricards main darj hai.
