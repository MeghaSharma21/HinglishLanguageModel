  
 vararuchi katyayan, panineey sutron ke prasiddh vaarthikkaar hai. ve nau shulb sutron main se ek ke rachayita bhi hai.
 purushottamdev ne apane trikandshesh abhidhankosh main katyayan ke ye naam likhe hai - kaatya, punarvasu, medhajit or vararuchi. "kaatya" naam gotrapratyaant he, mahabhashya main uskaa ullekh he. punarvasu naam nakshatra sambandhi he, "bhashaavriti" main punarvasu ko vararuchi kaa paryaay kaha gaya he. medhajit kaa kahin anyatra ullekh nahin miltaa. iske atirikt, kathasaritsagar or brihatkthamanjari main katyayan vararuchi kaa ek naam "shrutdhar" bhi aayaa he. hemchandra evam medini koshon main bhi katyayan ke "vararuchi" naam kaa ullekh he.
 vararuchi katyayan ke vaarthik panineey vyakaran ke liye ati mahatvashaalee siddh hue hai. in vaartikon ke bina panineey vyakaran adhuraa sa rahaa jaataa. vaartikon ke aadhaar par hi peeche se patanjali ne mahabhashya kii rachana kii.
 katyayan vararuchi ke vaarthik padhane par kuch tathy saamne aate hai - yadhyapi adhikansh sthalon par katyayan ne panineey sutron kaa anuvarthi hokar arth kiya he, tark vitark or aalochana karke sutron ke sangrakshan kii cheshtaa kii he, parantu kahin-kahin sutron main parivartan bhi kiya he or yada-kada panineey sutron main dosha dikhaakar unka pratishedh kiya he or jahaan tahaan katyayan ko parishisht bhi dene pade hai. sambhavat: isi vararuchi katyayan ne vedasarvanukramani or praatishakhya kii bhi rachana kii he. katyayan ke banaae kuch bhrajasanjyak shlokon kii charchaa bhi mahabhashya main kii gai he. kaiyat or nagesh ke anusaar bhrajasanjyak shloke vaarthikkaar ke hi banaae hue hai.
