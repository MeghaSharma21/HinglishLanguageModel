 swatantraaa smarak kampuchea kii rajdhani, nom penh main sthit ek smarak he, jise varsh 1958 main kampuchea kii france se swanantraprapati kii smriti main nirmit kiya gaya tha. iskaa udadhatan 9 navambar 1962 ko, swatantraaa kii 9veen varshganth ke avasar par hua tha. drudh parmparik vaastupaili main bana yah smarak nom penh ke kendra main norodom or simhanouk margon ke chauraahey par sthit he. isaki banaavat ko vikhyath cambodian vaastukar vann mauliwann ne taiyaar kiya tha.
 paaramparik kampuchean vaastupaili main bane is smarak ko ek padmakar mandir yaa stupa ke aakaar kaa banaaya gaya he, jisamen vaastukaar vann mauliwann ne khmer vaastuparampara ke anek paramparik roopaankanon kaa upyog kiya he. is mandir rupi smarak ko angakore wat kii roopakriti ke tarz par banaaya gaya he, vishesh kar iskaa shikhar parmparik khmer vaastupaili kii he, jo angakore wat sahit anekon ainihasik kampuchean mandiron or stupon main dekhi ja sakati he. is sanrachana kaa satahadaar shikhar 20foot ooncha he, jo kai vrittakar sheersh-drishy vaale satahon se bana he jisamen se prayek sataha kii trigya oonchai ke saath kam hoti jaati he, saath hi is par vibhinn shilp-roopankan ankit hai jinke parinamswaroop ise ek padmakar miltaa he. isaki vaastuakari main vaastukar moulivann ne drudhtah lokapriy vaastustyli kaa upyog kiya he. raat ke samay is smarak ko cambodia ke rashtriya dhvaj ke rang- laal, nile or rafed rang kii raushnee se prakashit kiya jaataa he.
 swatantraaa smarak rajdhani ke do pramukh sadak: norodom or sihanouk mergon ke chauraahey par sthit he. yah chauraahey ke golumber par sthit he. ise kai samarohon ke liye ek manch ke rup main upyog kiya jaataa he. yahaan aagantukon kaa pravesh nickshulk he evam shahar ke kendra main hone ke kaaran yah sthaan aam taur par chahal-pahal se bharpoor rahata he.
 yah smarak cambodia kii swatantraaa kaa pratik he, saath hi yah swatantraaa sangram main maare gaye shaheedon kii samadhi sthal kaa bhi sthaan he. rashtriya tyoharon, vishesh rup se swatantraaa diwas or samvidhan ke prakhyapan kii varshganth, ke avasar par rajkeey anushthanon kaa yah dhyaan kendra hota he. rajkeey evam rashtriya utsavon par cambodia ke naresh yaheen se apana bhaashan dete hai evam anya mahatvapurn samarohon kaa sanchaalan bhi yaheen se kiya jaataa he. antarashtriya atithigan evam rashtradhyakshagan is smarak main apni shradhanjali arpit karte hai.
