 sabhi dharm ko sweekaarte hue anya dharmon kii siddhant alag ho sakate hai ko maanate hue anya desh, rajya, sthaneey log or unke bhashaa ko sammaan or rang athwa nashl bhed ko sweekaar karte hue duniyaan ke sabhi inshaano ke prati aatmbhaav ko sthapit karnaa or ekadusre kaa sammaan or seva bhav kaa hona akhand maanavtawaad ko paribhaashit karti he. maanav kii moulik kartabya he ki vah apechhit jeevon par dayaa bhav rakhte hue unki sahaayta karen. maanavtaa ko chahie kii sabhi kaa sammaan karte hue sabake hit kii soch rakhte hue kaary karen.
