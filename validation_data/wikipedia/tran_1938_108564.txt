 dhishum bhartiya bollywood film he. iskaa nirdesan rohit dhawan ne kiya he. jisamen varun dhawan ,jon abraham ,akshay khanna tathaa jaclyn furnandis mukhya kirdaar he. yah film 29 july 2016 ko cinemagharon main pradarshit hui.
 janvari 2015 main sajid nadiyadwala ne is film ke nirmaan kii ghoshnaa kii thi, jiske vah nirmaataa hai or uskaa nirdesan rohit dhawan kar rahe hai. isamen mukhya kirdaaron main varun dhawan, jeclin furnandis or jon abraham hai. yah pehali baar he jab varun dhawan apane bhai rohit ke saath kaary kar rahe hai. is film main akshay khanna 4 varshon baad dikhaai denge.
