 anakatuvook paas sanyukt rajya america ke alaska rajya ke north slop baro main sthit ek nagar he. 2010 kii janaganana main yahan kii jansankhya 324 thi jo 2000 kii janaganana kii tulana main 42 adhik thi.
 2000 kii janaganana main anakatuvook paas main 282 log, 84 apaariwarik-samooh, or 57 parivaar the. jansankhya ghanatv 22.5/kimi2 tha. 54/kimi2 ghanatv ke saath kul 101 nivas-yogya ikaiyaan thi. prajateey banaavat is prakaar thi: mul america, shwet, africa america, anya prajatiyon ke, or do yaa do se adhik prajati ke . kisi bhi prajati ke hispanic yaa latino 0.71% the.
