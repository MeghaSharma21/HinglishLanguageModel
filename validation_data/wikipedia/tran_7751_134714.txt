 nathpa jhakdi jalavidyut pariyojna himachal pradesh main rashtriya rajmarg 22 - hindustan tibet maarg par, sutluj nadi par sthit he. is pariyojna ko may,2004 main kamishan kiya gaya tha tathaa aadhikarik taur par 28 may,2005 ko pradhanmantri shri manmohan singh ne ise raashtra ko samarpit kiya.
 nathpa jhakdi panbizli stesan kii kshamta 1500 megawatt he or yah desh kaa chauthaa sabase bada jalavidyut plant he. nathpa jhakdi plant prati varsh 6950.88 million unit bijli kaa utpaadan karne ke liye dizin kiya gaya he. is plant se vidyut kaa aabantan uttar bhartiya rajyon haryana, hi.pra., punjab, jammu evam kashmir, rajasthan, uttar pradesh, uttrakhand tathaa dilli va chandigarh ke sabhi shaharon ko hota he.
 nathpa jhakdi jalavidyut pariyojna par 8187 karod rupye kii laagat ai. ise vishwa bank se sahaayta praapt he.
 is pariyojna ko vishwa kii sabase lambii jal surang hone kaa shrey praapt he.
 varsh 2011-12 or 2012-13 main utkrisht pradarshan ke liye bharat ke sabase bade panbizli stesan, nathpa jhakdi ko kramash: kaansya va rajat shield se puraskrit kiya gaya. ye pratishthit puraskaar kendriya bijli rajya mantri shri jyotiraditya sindhiya ne 4 farvari 2014 ko sutluj jal vidyut nigam limited ke siemdi shri aar. pi. singh or shri sanjeev sood, mahaprabandhak ko pradaan kiye.
