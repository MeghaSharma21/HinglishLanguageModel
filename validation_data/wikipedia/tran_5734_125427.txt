 dow. udit raaj
 
 dow. udit raaj kaa janm 01 janvari 1958 ko uttar pradesh ke ram nagar ke khatic parivaar main hua. wo sohalvin loksabhaa main saansad hai.
 unhone edian justise party kii sthapana kii. farvari 2014 main unhone apni party kaa bhaajapa main vilay kar diya.
 aapaki patni seemaa bahal jo ki khatri hai|
