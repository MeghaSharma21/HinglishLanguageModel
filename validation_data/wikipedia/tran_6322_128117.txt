 valmiki samudaay, hindu dharm ke antargat ek dalit samudaay he, jo rishi valmiki par kendrit he. 
 valmiki samudaay ke log valmiki ko ishwar kaa avatar maanate hai tathaa unke dwaara rachit ramayana tathaa yoga vashishtha ko pavitra granth maanate hai. valmiki samudaay ke log apane kulanaam ke rup main naidu yaa nayudu, nike, doraa, dorabidda tathaa valmiki dhaaran karte hai.
