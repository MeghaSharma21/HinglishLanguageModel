 shrivats goswami ek bhartiya cricket khilaadi he jo ek baayen haath ke ballebaaj or wicket-keeper hai. unka janm kolkata, pashchim bengal main ek bengali hindu parivaar main hua tha. unke pita kaa naam pratibhu goswami he. inhone 11 varsh kii umr main hi cricket khelnaa shuru kar diya tha. inhone bengal ke liye under -19 gharelu cricket khelaa he. janvari, 2008 main dakshin africa main under -19 trikoniya shrinkhalaa ke dauraan, unhone dakshin africa ke khilaaf 97 or bangladesh ke khilaaf 104 ran banaae the. malaysia main 2008 ke under-19 cricket vishwa cap main, unhone lig maichon main se ek main 58 or newjiland ke khilaaf semifinal main 51 ran banaae the.
 ye indian premier lig main royal chalangers bangalore, kolkata nite rides, rajasthan royles ke liye khel chuke he. jabaki janvari 2018 main 2018 indian premier lig kii nilaamee main inhen sunrisers hyderabad ne 1 karod se kharida he.
