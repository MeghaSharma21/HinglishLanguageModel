 manushya ke vividh angon or mastishk ke bich sambandh sthapit karne ke liye taage se bhi patale anek snayutantu hote hai. snayutantuon kii lachchhiyaan alag alag bandhi rahati hai. inamen se prayek ko tantrika kehete hai. prayek main kai ek tantu rahete hai. tantrikaon ke samudaay ko tantrikatantr kehete hai. ye tantra teen prakaar ke hote hai: 
 un tantrikaon ko swytaniyantri tantrikas kehete hai jo mastishk main pahunchakar ek duusare se sambhddha hoti hai or hriday, fefde, amashay, antadi, gurde aadi kii kriya ko niyantrit karti hai. bahm jagat se mastishk tak soochna pahunchaanevaali tantrikas samvedi tantrikas tathaa mastishk se angon tak chalane kii ajna pahunchaanevaali tantrikas chaalak tantrikas kehalaati hai. inamen se swaitaniyantri tantrikaon ko do samoohon main vibhajit kiya gaya he: 
 bhay, krodh, uttejnaa, aadi kaa sharir par prabhaav mastishk dwaara anukanpi tantrikatantr ke niyantran se padataa he. yah niyantran adhiktar sharir ke bheetar adrinalin naamak rasinic padaarth ke utpann hone se hota he. paranukanpi tantrikatantr kaa kaary saadhaarant: anukanpi kaa ulta hota he, jaisaa aage chalakar dikhaaya gaya he.
 kasheruk dand ke saamne donon ore gucchhikaaon kii ek shrunkhlaa pratham vakshiya kasheruka se lekar antim katicaeruka tak sthit he. ye kasheruka gandika kehalaati hai. sushumna ke parshv praant se, soushumnik tantrika kii pashchim gucchhika dwaara, ek sookshm tantu nikalakar gucchhikaaon main jaataa he, jahaan se doosraa tantu prarambh hota he, jo angon yaa aashyon ke sameep adhiksheruki gucchhikaaon main samaapt hota he. in sutron ko gucchhikottaree tantu kaha jaataa he. pehla tantu sushumna ke bheetar sthit koshika kaa langool he, jo adhiksheruki gucchhika kii koshika ke chaaron ore samaapt ho jaataa he. is koshika kaa langool gucdikottaree tantu ke rup main adhiksheruki gucchhika main jaakar samaapt hota he athwa sidhaa angon yaa aashyon kii bhittiyon main chalaa jaataa hai. pratham tantu par medas pidhaan chadha rahata he, duusare tantu par nahin hota. is prakaar uttejnaa ke jane ke liye sushumna se ang tak ek maarg ban jaataa he, jisamen kam se kam do tantu hote hai jinka sangam gucchhikaaon main hota he.
 soushumneey or anukanpi tantrikaon main yahi vishesh bhed he ki pratham prakaar kii tantrikaon main ek hi neuron hota he jo uttejnaa ko sushumna se antim sthaan tak pahunchaata he. duusare prakaar kii naadiyon main kam se kam do neuron dwaara uttejnaa kaa sanvahan hota he. doosraa bhed yah he ki soushumneey tantrikas visheshtya aichhik peshiyon main jaati hai. teesraa bhed sanvahan sambandhi he. soushumneey naadiyon main uttejnaa kaa sanvahan kendron kii ore adhik hota he, arthath unamem samvedak tantu adhik hote hai. anukanpi tantuon main sanvahan keval angon kii ore hota he.
 anukanpi tantra ke atirikt bhi kuch anya tantrikaon main aisi hi rachana hoti he, arthath do neuron paae jate hai, jo anukanpi kii hi bhanti uttejnaa kaa sanvahan or vitaran karte hai. unako paranukanpi tantu kehete hai. in donon ko aatmag tantra bhi kaha jaataa he. anukanpi tantra ke do bhaag hai, ek kapaal bhaag or doosraa trik bhaag. kapaal bhaag ke pun: do vibhaag hai. ek vibhaag madhyamastishk se nikalataa he or doosraa pashchmastishk se jiskaa purvagucchhika tantu vagas, jihwagrasanika or maukhiki tantrikaon main shakhaayen bhejtaa he. pashchgucchhika tantu kii shakhaayen pachanpranali grassnalika se lekar brihadantra tak ke saare peshistar, shwaasanal, phuffus or hriday kii peshiyon tathaa mukh or gale kii shlashmik kala kii raktavahiniyon main jaati hai. trik bhaag ke tantu shroni kii teen badi tantrikaon dwaara, shroniguha ke bheetar sthit angon, brihadantra, malashay, mutrashay, janan angon aadi, main vitarit ho jate hai.
 isko aatmag tantra isliye kaha jaataa he ki isaki kriya dwaara bhitari angon kaa sara kaam hota rahata he. yah swat: hamaare niyantran se vimukt rahakar angon kaa sanchaalan karata rahata he. yadhyapi iske tantu mastishk or sushumna ke kendro se nikalate hai, tathaapi inase soushumnik naadiyon kaa koi sambandh nahin hota. phir bhi unamem uttejnaayen mastishk or sushumna se hi aati hai.
 jaisaa upar bataaya gaya he, anukanpi or paranukanpi vibhaagon kii kriyaayein ek duusare se viruddh hai. ek kriya ko ghataata or doosraa kriya ko badhaata he. pachaknali ke peshisamooh ke sankoch anukanpi se kam hote hai or paranukanpi se badhate hai. raktavahaniyaan anukanpi kii kriya se sankuchit hoti hai or paranukanpi se vistrut hoti hai. paranukanpi ke tantu vagas dwaara pahunchakar hriday ko rokte hai, anukanpi se hriday kii gati badhati he. isse netra kaa tara prabhavit hota he, paranukanpi se sankuchit hota he. vayunal or pranalikaon kii peshiyon main paranukanpi ke sutara mastishk se aate hai.
 sab angon main aatmagatantr ke in donon vibhaagon ke sutara faile hue hai.
