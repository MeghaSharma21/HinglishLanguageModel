 jalka yaa panakua pakshiyon main bak gana ke jalka kul kaa prasiddh pakshi he jisaki kai jatiyaan saare sansar main pai jaati hai. is kul ke pakshiyon kaa rang kaala, chonch lambii, taangen choti or ungliyaan zalpad hoti he. ye apana adhik samay pani main hi bitaate hai or pani ke bheetar machhliyon kii tarah tair lete hai. ye sab machhlikhor pakshi hai.
 jalka kaa doosraa naam panakua bhi he. isaki ek choti jati bhi hoti he jo chota panakua kahi jaati hai. kad kii chhotai ke alaava in donon main koi bhed nahin he.
 teesri jati ke pakshi baanwar kahe jate hai. purvokt donon jatiyon se inaki chonch avashya bhinn hoti he, lekin iske atirikt inke rahan sahan, swabhaav, tathaa bhojan aadi main koi bhed nahin he. panakauon kii chonch jahaan aage kii ore thodi mudi rahati he, vahiin baanwar kii chonch sidhi patali or nukili rahati he.
 pancoe 10- 12 inch lambey pakshi hai jinke nar or maadaa ek hi jaise hote hai. ye yaa to kisi jalashay main machhliyaan pakadte rahete hai yaa pani ke kinare yaa kisi thunth par daine failaae baithe apane pankh sukhaate rahete hai. inkaa joda baandhane kaa samay july he, jab ye saikadon kii sankhya main ikatthe hokar apane bade bade giroh bana lete hai. inkaa garoh ek hi jagah milakar ghonslaa banaata he, jisamen maadaa 4-5 andey detii he.
