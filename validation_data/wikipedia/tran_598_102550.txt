 manushyon kii anuvanshiki main pitruvansh samooh aar yaa waahe-diene hapalogrup R ek pitruvansh samooh he. yah pitruvansh swayan pitruvansh samooh pi se utpann hui ek shakha he. is pitruvansh kii aar1e, aar1bi or aar2 upashakhaon ke purush zyaadaatar europe, madhya asia, purvi asia, pashchimi asia, uttar america or dakshin asia main paae jate hai. isaki aar1 shakha ke purush uttar america ke kuch adivasee samudaayon main bhi paae jate hai or vichaar he ke inke purvaj shaayad chand hazaar saal pehle hi siberia se uttar america upmahadweep pahunche the. mana jaataa he ke mul pitruvansh samooh aar jis purush ke saath armbh hua vah aaj se 35,000-40,000 saal pehle madhya asia yaa madhya purv kaa rahane vaala tha.
 angrezi main "vansh samooh" ko "hapalogrup", "pitruvansh samooh" ko "waahe chromosome hapalogrup" or "matruvansh samooh" ko "amtidiene hapalogrup" kehete hai.
