 sanganak kii bhashaa main kunjeepatal ek input yukti he jo kii tiperiter ke kunjeepatal main kuch aavashyak parivartan karke banaayi gayi he. kunjeepatal main bahut se kunjiyaan yaa batan hote hai jo kisi vishesh tareeke se vinyast hote hai. ye batan electranik switch kaa kaam karte hai or dabaane par computer ko ek vishesh digital sanket preshit karte hai jo ki dabaayi gayi kunjee kii pahchaan hoti he. kujiyaan yantric ho sakate hai yaa electranik. kunjiyon ke upar ek yaa adhik sanket/akshar likhe rahete hai.
 hindi bhashaa ke liye do keyboard hote hai - inascript jo ki sabhi bhartiya bhashaaon kaa maanak keyboard he tathaa remington jo ki puraane jamaane ke tiperiter par prayog hota tha.
