 pradesh sabha nepal ke pradeshon kaa vidhaan sabha he. nepal ke sabhi pradeshon kaa vidhaan sabha ekasadaneey he.
 nepal main sabhi pradeshon ke milakar kul 550 pradesh sabha siten hai, jinamein se 330 siton par har party kaa ek-ek ummidavaar yaa swatantra ummidavaar khadaa ho saktaa he or first past da post matdaan pranali ke dwaara chunaa jaataa he. baanki 220 siton par anupatik pratinidhaan matdaan pranali ke dwaara ummidavaar chune jate hai. anupatik pratinidhaan pranali ke anusaar matadaata puure desh ko ekal chunaav nirvachan kshetra samaj kar raajnneetik dalon ke liye matdaan karte hai.
