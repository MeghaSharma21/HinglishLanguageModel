 leone edward paneta  ek america raajnneetijna, vakil or professor hai. inhone raashtrapati baraak obama ke kaarykaal main 2009 se 2011 tak america khufia sanstha central intelegence agency ke nideshak or 2011 se 2013 tak raksha sachiv kii behad mahatvapurn bhoomikaaye nibhaain. paneta 1977 se 1993 tak sanyukt rajya america kii sansad ke sadsy rahe. 1993-94 bajet prabandhan karyaalay ke nideshak or 1994 se 97 tak tatkaaleen raashtrapati bill clinton ke karyaalay ke adhyaksh rahe. vah paneta skool of public policy ke sansthaapak hai or santa clara vishvavidhyalay main jan niti ke professor hai.
 janvari 2009 main, tatkaaleen raashtrapati baraak obama ne paneta ko siie nideshak ke pad par niyukt karne kii ghoshnaa kii. america seenet ne paneta ke niyukti ko farvari 2009 main manjuuri di. nideshak pad par apane kaarykaal ke dauraan paneta ne pakistan main osama bin laden ko maarane ke liye chalaaye gayi sainya abhiyaan kaa safal sanchaalan kiya.
 robert gates ke padchyut hone ke baad 28 april, 2011 ko obalm ne paneta ke raksha mantri pad par niyukti kii ghoshnaa kii. june main seenet ne paneta ko ekamat se rakshaamantri kaa padbhaar de diya jise unhone 1 july, 2011 ko grahan kiya. unke baad 6 sitambar, 2011 ko david petrius central intiligence agency ke agle nideshak chune gayi.
