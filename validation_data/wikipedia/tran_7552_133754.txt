 korath kaa hindi shabdic arth mandap se liya jaataa he, bharat ke uttar pradesh rajya main itawa jila se unniis mile pashchim main bhadauria jati ke logon kaa ek gaanv he or is gaanv main ek haveli bhagn avasthaa main aaj bhi vidyaman he, is haveli main ek surang he jo korath ke puraane gaanv se chambal nadi tak gai hui he. is surang ko korath gaanv kii mahilaon ko chambal main snaan ke liye concrit se banwaaya gaya tha, bhadauria vansh ke jameendaar sahab raay ne ek nartki ko apharan karne ke baad isi haveli main rakhaa tha, vah nartki musalmaan shaasha akbar kii mashhoor nartki thi, samraat akbar ke sipaahiyon ko jab pataa laga ki unke darbar kii aprahat nartki sahab raay ke paas unki haveli main he to unhone us haveli ko chaaro taraf se gher kar talasi kaa kaam chaalu kar diya, magar sahab raay ko pehle se pataa lagane ke kaaran unhone us nartki ko surang ke raast chambal par jane ke liye kaha, vah surang se chambal nadi tak nahin pahunch paayi, usase pehle hi sahab raay ne us surang ke dono sire apane aadamiyon se band karvaa diye or vah nartki usee surang main mar gai, samraat akbar ke sipaahi use khoj kar chale gayi. jab se aaj tak vah surang band he or us nartki kii aatma har diwali ke din haveli main naty karti he aisa korath ke nivaasiyon kaa vishwaas he, is gaanv ke sau pratishat jawan aaj bhi milatri main apni sevaayen bhartiya sena ko de rahe hai.
