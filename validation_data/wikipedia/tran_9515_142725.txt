 uma shri bharti, bhartiya rajnetri he or bharat kii jal sansaadhan, nadi vikaas or ganga safai mantri he. ve madhya pradesh kii mukhyamantri rah chuki he. unhen gwalior kii maharani vijayraaje sindhiya ne ubhaara. sadhvi ritambhra ke saath unhone ram janmabhumi aandolan main pramukh bhoomika nibhaai. is dauraan unka naaraa tha "shri ramalala ghar aayenge mandir vahiin banaayenge".
 vah yuvavastha main hi bhartiya janata party se jud gayin thi. unhone 1984 main sarvapratham loksabhaa chunaav ladaa, parantu haar gayin. 1989 ke loksabhaa chunaav main vah khajuraho sansadeey kshetra se saansad chuni gayin or 1991, 1996, 1998 main yah seat barqaraar rakhi. 1999 main vah bhopal seat se saansad chuni gayin. vaajapeyi sarkaar main vah maanav sansaadhan vikaas, paryaton, yuva maamle evam khel or ant main koyala or khadaan jaise vibhinn rajya stareey or cabinet str ke vibhaagon main kaary kiya.
 2003 ke madhya pradesh vidhansabha chunaavon main, unke netritv main bhaajapa ne teen-chauthai bahumat praapt kiya or mukhyamantri baniin. agast 2004 unhone mukhyamantri pad se istipha de diya, jab unke khilaaf 1994 ke hubli dangon ke sambandh main giraftaar warrant jari hua.
 uma bharti kaa janm tikamgarh jile ke dunda naamak sthaan par hua. unki shiksha chhathi kaksha tak hui. vah bachapan se hi nidar or bebaak thim. uname saahas or netritv karne kii bachapan se hi dekhi jaati thi. vah lodhi rajput samudaay ke antargat aati hai.
 navambar 2004 ko laalkrishna adwani kii aalochana ke baad unhen bhaajapa se se barkhaast kar diya gaya. 2005 main unki barkhaastagi hat gayi or unhen party kii sansadeey board main jagah mili. isi saal vah party se hat gayi kyonki unke pratidwandi shivaraj singh chouhan ko madhya pradesh kaa mukhyamantri bana diya gaya. is dauraan unhone bhartiya janashakti party naam se ek alag party bana lee.
 7 june 2011 ko unki punah bhaajapa main vaapasi hui. uttar pradesh main party kii sthiti sudhaarney ke liye unhone "ganga bachaao" abhiyaan chalaaya.
 march 2012 main hue uttar pradesh vidhansabha chunaavon main vah mahoba jile kii charkhaari seat se vidhansabha sadsy chuni gayin. ve varsh-2014 main jhaansi lok sabha nirvachan kshetra se 16veen loksabhaa kii saansad chuni gai hai or unhen modi mantrimandal main bharat kii jal sansaadhan, nadi vikaas or ganga safai mantri banaaya gaya hai.
