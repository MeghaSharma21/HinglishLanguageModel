 changthang pashchimi or uttari tibet main sthit ek unchaa pathaar he jo kuch had tak bharat ke laddakh kshetra ke dakshinpurvi hisse main bhi vistrut he. laddakh se shuru hokar yah purv main 1,600 kimi dur chingei praant tak failaa hua he. yah ilaaka tibeti mul ke changpa kahalaane vaale kanaabadoshon kii mathrubhumi he or bhugolic nazariye se tibet ke pathaar kaa bhaag he. changthang kshetra main dur-dur tak khule, shushk or unchaa maidani pathaar or vishaal jhilein faili hui hai. isaki ausat oonchaai 4,500 meter or 4,900 meter ke bich he.
 tibeti bhashaa main 'chang' kaa arth 'uttari' or 'thang' kaa arth 'pathaar' hota he, yani is kshetra ke naam kaa matlab 'uttari pathaar' he.
 apni atyaadhik oonchaai ke kaaran changthang pathaar par bahut sardi rahati he. bahut hi kam din hote hai jab taapmaana 10° centegrade se zyaada hota he or -25° centegrade ke taapmaana yahan aksar dekhe jate hai.
 apni vicitra bhoomi or vaataavaran ke kaaran bharat sarkaar or tibet par niyantran rakhane vaali chini sarkaar donon ne apane kshetron main changthang pathaar ke bade bhaagon ko arakshit ghoshit kiya he.
 bharat sarkaar ne laddakh main changthang sheet marubhumi vanyajeev arakshit kshetra sthapit kiya he. isse yahan 'kiang' naamak milane vaale junglee tibeti gadhe ko suraksha di ja rahi he.
 tibet main changthang kaa adhiktar bhaag 4,96,000 varg kimi bade 'changthang prakritik arakshit kshetra' ke antargat arakshit he. tulana ke liye is prakritik udyaan kaa kshetrafal bharat ke uttar pradesh, uttarakhand, bihar or jharkhand rajyon ko milakar banane vaale bhushetra se bhi bada he.
