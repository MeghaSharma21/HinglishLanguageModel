 wooler​ jhil jammu va kashmir rajya ke bandipora zile main sthit ek jhil he. yah bharat kii mithe pani kii sabase badi jhil he. yah jhelum nadi ke maarg main aati he or jhelum isamen pani daalati bhi he or phir aage nikaal bhi leti he. mausam ke anusaar is jhil ke aakaar main bahut vistaar-sikod hota rahata he - iskaa akar 30 varg kimi se 260 varg kimi ke bich badalata he. apane bade akar ke kaaran is jhil main badi lehren aati hai.
 prachinkaal main 'mahapadam devtaa' is jhil ke adhidevataa the or unke naam par is jhil ko 'mahapadmsar' kaha jaataa tha. jhil kaa akar bada hone se yahan dopaher main badi lehren uthati hai jis se isaki shant sataha par dekhte-hi-dekhte unchi or khataranaak lahare uthne lagati hai. sanskrit main in kuudati hui laharon ko 'ullal' kaha jaataa he or yahi naam vikrut hokar 'wooler' pad gaya.
 wooler ke purvottaree kone main zainul lank naamak ek dweep he . yah ek kritrim dweep he jo san 1444 main kashmir ke sultan zan-ul-abdin​ ne banwaaya tha. ve apni dharmik sahansheeltaa ke liye jane jate the or unhen hindu va muslim kashmiri log izzat se 'bud shah' ke naam se yaad karte hai. 15veen sadi ke kashmiri itihaaskaar jonraj ke anusaar yah dweep bud shah ne navikon ko wooler main tufaani sthitiyon main aashray dene ke liye banwaaya tha. isapar abhi bhi khandhhar maujuud hai. jis kaal main unhone yah dweep banwaaya tha us samay wooler thodi adhik badi thi or tapu jhil ke bich main tha. dhire-dhire wooler ek taraf se sookhkar sikud gai or dweep ab uske ek kone main he.
