 timore, dakshin purv asia ke samudri chor ke dakshini sire par or timore sagar ke uttar main sthit ek dweep he. yah dweep swatantra raashtra purvi timore or indonesian praant purvi nusaa tengara ke ek bhaag pashchimi timore ke bich vibhajit he. dweep kaa bhushetra 30777 varg kilometre he. timore shabd malay bhashaa ke shabd timur par aadhaarit he jiskaa arth malay bhashaa main "purv" hota he, aisa ise isliye kaha jaataa he kyonki yah dwipon kii shrinkhalaa ke purvi chor par sthit he.
 timore malay punjadweep ke laghu-sunda-dweepasmooh kaa sabase bada evam purvi dweep he tathaa yah mukhya dweep se sabu sagar tathaa ombai jaldmarumadhya dwaara or austrailia se timor sagar dwaara alag he.
 raajnneetik drushti se yah 'pashchimi timore' evam 'purvi timore' do bhaagon main vibhajit he. pashchimi bhaag, 5,120 varg mile, indonesia ke adhikaar main he tathaa purvi bhaag, 7,350 varg mile, san 2002 se ek swatantra raashtra he . yah 300 mile lanbaa evam 60 mile chaudaa he, jiskaa kshetrafal lagbhag 12,470 varg mile he. iskaa samudri kinaaraa khadaa, dhalua tathaa agamya he, kyonki kinaaron ke chaaron ore muunge kii chattane pai jaati he. yahan par sarvatra parvatashreniyan hai, jo jwalamukheey utpatti kii hai. isaki ek chotii, mount aitlas, 12,000 foot se bhi adhik unchi he. iske bhitari bhaag ke bare main jaankaree kam he. yahan par anya purvi dwipon kii apeksha kam vanaspatiyaan pai jaati hai. kinaaron par krishi hoti he. isamen bhi dakshini kinare kii apeksha uttari kinare par adhik krishi kii jaati he. yahan par gharelu upyog ke liye kahavaa, dhan, chini tathaa nariyal ugaaya jaataa he. kuch maatra main chandan, mom, kachhue kaa kavach evam kahavaa baahar niryaat kiya jaataa he. yahan ke adhikansh nivasee papua jaati ke log hai, jinamein malay, paalinesian tathaa nigro jatiyon kaa sanmishran he. yahan kaa adhikansh vyapaar chiniyon ke adhikaar main he. indonesian bhaag kii rajdhani kumpang he.
