 shri aadi maanav ek bhartiya hasya dharawahik he. jiskaa prasaaran sab tivi par 25 sitambar 2009 se shuru hua. iske 45 prakaran ke pashchaat 10 july 2010 ko yah samaapt hua. isamen mukhya bhoomika main vishaal kotiyaan, rajesh hirji or kiku sharada hai.
 yah teen doston kii kahani he. isamen shri ko kuch dikhaai nahin detaa, aadi baharaa hota he or maanav kuch bol nahin paata he. yah sabhi lavish naamak ek sthaan par rahete hai. vah pehle se aise nahin hote hai. yah sabhi durghatana ke kaaran aise ho jate hai. lekin yah hameshaa nai kaaryon ke liye taiyaar hote hai. iske kaaran kai baar bhram bhi paidaa ho jaataa he or hasya utpann hota he.
