 sookhaa rog haddiyon kaa rog he jo bacchon praaia: bacchon main hota he. bacchon main haddiyon kii narmai yaa kamjor hone ko sookhaa rog kehete hai. parinamswaroop asthivikaar hokar pairon kaa tedhaapan or merudand main asaamaanya mod aa jate hai. isi prakaar kii vikruti ko bado main austimelsia kaha jaataa he.
 lakshan or chihn prakat hone ke kuch mahinon pehle se vitamin di kii kami hone lagati he. jab iske lakshan prakat hote hai tab ve is prakaar ho sakate hai -
 praayah iskaa kaaran lambe samay tak vitamin di kii kami hota he. aaharanlee se calcium or phosphorus ke shoshan main sahaayta ke liye poshak gha jaruri he. bacchon kii haddiyon kii majbootee or vikaas main calcium or bhasvar kii jarurat hoti he. sharir ko jab rakt main calocium or phosphorus kii kami kaa abhas hota he to vah haddiyon se ise lekar apana santulan banaata he. isse haddiyaan naram pad jaati he or inaki sanrachana kamjor ho jaati he.
 yadi vitamin di yaa calcium kii kami se sookhaa rog hota he to aahaar dwaara isaki purti karne par thik ho jaataa he or bacchon kii haddiyon kii samasya se bachaa ja saktaa he.
 anuvanshik kaarnon se hone par atirikt dawaaon or visheshgy kii jarurat hoti he. kuchhek kankal rogon main shalyachikitsa kii jarurat padati he.
