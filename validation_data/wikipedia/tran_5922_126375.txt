 hamelet shakespeare kaa ek du:khant naatak he, jiskaa abhinay sarvapratham san 1603 i. tathaa prakaashan san 1604 i. ke lagbhag hua tha.
 denmark kaa raja cladius apane bhai kii hatya karke singhasanarudh hua. mrit raja kii patni gartrude, jisaki sahaayta se hatya sampann hui thi, ab cladius kii patni tathaa denmark kii maharani ban gai. is prakaar apane pita kii mrityu ke baad mrit raja kaa putr hamelet uttaradhikaar se vanchit rah jaataa he. hamelet tab vittenberg se, jahaan vah vidhyaarthee tha, waapas lautataa he tab uske pita kii pretatma use kyadius or gartrut ke aparaadh se avgat karaati he tathaa cladius ke prati pratihinsa ke liye prerit karti he. hamelet swabhaav se vishadagrast tathaa dirghasutri he, at: vah pratihinsa kaa kaary taalata jaataa he. apni pratihinsa kii bhaavana chhipaane ke liye hamelet ek veikshipt vyakti ke samaan vyavhaar karata he jisase logon ke man main yah dhaaran hoti he ki vah lard chemberlen polonius kii putree ophelia ke prem main pagal ho gaya he. ophelia ko usne pyaar kiya tha kintu baad main uske prati hamelet kaa vyavhaar anishchit evam vyangapurn ho gaya. apane pita kii pretatma dwaara bataae hue jaghanya tathyon kii pushti hamelet ek aise natya abhinay ke madhyam se karata he jisamen uske pita ke vadh kii katha duharai gai he. cladius kii teevr pratikriya se hamelet ke man main yah nishchit ho jaataa he ki pretatma dwaara bataai hui baaten satya hai. natya abhinay ke uparaant vah apni mata kii bhartsana karata he tathaa cladius ke dhokhe main parade ke peeche chhipe hue polonius ko maar daalata he. ab cladius hamelet kii hatya ke liye vyavastha karata he or is abhipraay se use england bhejtaa he. raste main samudri daaku use bandi banaate hai or vah denmark laut aata he. ophelia kii mrityu hoti he tathaa polonius kaa putr evam ophelia kaa bhai layerties hamelet ko dwand yuddh ke liye chunauti detaa he. layerties ko cladius kaa samarthan praapt he. vah vish se buji hui talwaar dekar hamelet se ladata he. donon ghayal hote hai or marate hai. apni mrityu ke purv hamelet cladius ko maar daalata he or gartrude bhi anjaane main vish mili hui madiraa pikar mar jaati he.
 is naatak main anek mahatvapurn naitik or manovaigyanik prashnon kaa samavesh hua he tathaa samikshakon ne isamen nibaddh samasyaon par gambhir vichaar prakat kiye hai.
