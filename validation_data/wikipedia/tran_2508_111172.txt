 khat bhartiya sangeet ke ek raag kaa naam he. is raag main rishabh, dhaivat, nishad or shuddh or komal donon hi lagate hai. gandhar keval komal lagata he. shadj va pancham ye donon swar achal hai. teesraa swar madhyam or shuddh lagata he. iskaa vaadi swar pancham or sanvaadi shadj he. isamen asaavari, suha, kanada, saarang, deshi, gandhari va sugharaai raag raaginiyon kaa mishran bhi he. is kaaran iskaa gaan sahaj nahin kaha jaataa. ise bhairav raag kaa putr kaha gaya he or praat: kaal gey he.
