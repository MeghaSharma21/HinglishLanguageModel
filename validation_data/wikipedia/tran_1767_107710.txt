 madhya-pashchimanchal vikaas kshetra nepal kaa ek praant he jo nepal ke paanch vikaas kshetron main se ek vikaas kshetra he. yah nepal ke madhya-pashchim main sthit he. is ke purv main nepal kaa pashchimanchal vikaas kshetra tathaa pashchim main nepal kaa sudur-pashchimanchal vikaas kshetra tathaa uttar main china kaa tibet tathaa dakshin main bharat kaa uttar pradesh sthit he. madhya-pashchimanchal vikaas kshetra kaa mukhyalay birendranagar main sthit he. is praant main 3 anchal tathaa 15 gilen hai.
 yah kshetra nepal kaa sab se bada praant he, jiskaa kshetrafal he: 42,378 kimi2 par jansankhya ke hisaab se chauthe ank par he, jiskaa kshetrafal: 3,546,682 he. iskaa jansankhya ghanatv anya kshetro kii apeksha bahut kam he, yahan 83.7 vyakti/kimi2 par rahete hai.is kshetra kii sabase mahatvapurn nadi he karnali yaa kauriyaala, jo bharat main ghaghara naam se jaani jaati he. yah nadi ganga nadi kii mukhya sahaayak nadiyon main se ek he jo ganga nadi ke baanye se aakar milti he, nepal main is nadi kii lambaai 507 kimi he jo nepal kii sabase lambi nadi he. karnali nadi kii kuch sahaayak nadiyaan hai: pashchim-nadi, bheri or seti.nepal kaa sab se bada taal, raara taal isi kshetra main sthit he, jo karnali anchal ke jile main padataa he.
 karnali nadi pranali dwaara kritit he yah kshetra. yahan kii mukhya nadiyaan hai: humlaa karnali, mugu karnali, thuli karnali, bheri, rapti or babai.yahan kuch ucch shikhar bhi hai jaise: mukut himaal, putana himchuli, kanti himaal, gorakh himaal, changla himaal . yahan ke 28.2% bhumi kshetra jangalon se dhakaa he, jahaan vibhinn prakaar ke ped-paudhe or vanya-jantu paae jate hai. yahan ke adhikansh kshetra chattani hai tathaa ekadam khadey pahaad yaa parvat hai. isliye is kshetra main vikaas kam bahut kam dekhne ko miltaa he. yah kshetra badi kathinaayee ke saath kachche sadak ke madhyam se dusre kshetra se jude hue hai, par yahan ke kuch kshetra jaise: simicot, jumala, dolpa, surkhet, tulsipur, nepalganj aadi main kshetreey hawaii adde hai jo in kshetron ko anya kshetra se jodate hai. yah kshetra vikaas ke maamle main sab se peeche he, par paryaton ke maamle main aage he. rashtriya nikunj, jhilein, vibhinn dharmik sthal, gahari khaaiyaan, pahari sthal, ghaatiyaan or khadey chattani pahaaden dekhne ko mil sakate hai.
 itihaas main yah kshetra khasdesh kaa ek hissaa tha. baad main yahan jab khasdesh kai kshetron main tut gaya to yah kshetra karnali ke dullu ke kshetradhikaar main chalaa gaya jiskaa rajya kshetra karnali se lekar uttarakhand ke uttar-ganga tak failaa hua tha. baad main yahanbise rajya tathaa choubise rajya sthapit hue.
 gorkha ke maharaj prithvi narayan saah ke samay main jab nepal ekikaran abhiyaan chalaa to bahadur shah ne san 1789 main is kshetra par akraman kiya or ise nepal main mila liya.
 1814-16 ke angrej-gorkha yuddh main hue sugauli sandhi ke tahat is kshetra kaa tarai bhaag angrejon ko chalaa gaya tha jo unhone nepal ko 1860 main punah waapas kar diya.
 madhya-pashchimanchal vikaas kshetra main 3 anchal tathaa 15 jile hai.
 bardiya rashtriya udyaan ek sangrakshit kshetra he, jisaki sthapana 1988 main kii gai thi jiskaa naam rakhaa gaya tha shahi bardiya rashtriya udyaan. is udyaan ne 968 kimi2 kaa kshetra dhak rakhaa he. yah tarai kshetra main nepal kaa sab se bada tathaa bahut kam baadhit rashtri udyaan he. iskaa purvi kinaaraa karnali nadi se laga he or bardiya jile main babai nadi is ke bich se hokar bah rahi he jo is udyaan ko do bhaagon main baantati he.
 banke rashtriya udyaan kii sthapana 2010 main nepal ke dasven rashtriya udyaan ke rup main hui he. is udyaan kaa sangrakshit kshetra 550 kimi2 main failaa hua he jiskaa adhikansh bhaag churia kshetra main he.
 raara rashtri udyaan nepal ke himalay par ek sangrakshit kshetra he or is ko 1976 main sthapit kiya gaya tha. 106 kimi2 main failaa yah kshetra mugu or jumlaa jile main padataa he. yah desh kaa sab se chota udyaan he.
 raara taal nepal kaa sab se bada taal he. yah karnali anchal ke mugu jile main padataa he tathaa yah raara rashtri udyaan ke tahat aata he. raara taal samudr tal se 2,990 meter kii oonchai par sthit he jo 10.8 kimi main failaa hua he. isaki lambaai 5 kimi tathaa chaudaai 3 kimi he, jisaki gaharaai 167 me he.
