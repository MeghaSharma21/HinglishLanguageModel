 upbuna tara aisa tara hota he jo mukhya anukram ke boune taron se to dhimi chamak rakhata ho. yarkis varnakram shreneekaran main isaki chamak kii shrenee "VI" hoti he. inkaa nirpeksh kantimaan -1.5 se -2 magnitude kaa hota he.
 "upabone taare" ko angrezi main "sabadwarf star" kaha jaataa he.
 mukhya anukram ke aam boune taron kii tarah, thande upabone taare apni urja hydrogen gas ke nabhikeey sanlayan se banaate he. inaki chamak saadhaaran boune taron se kam isliye hoti he ke inamen boune taron se kam dhaatuen hoti hai. taron ke vaataavaran main dhaatuen prakaash ke liye baadha banati hai jis se taare main prakaash kaa andruni dabaav bad jaataa he or taare ko phulaakar use thodaa thanda kar detaa he. upabone taron main kam dhaatuon kii vajah se aisa nahin hota - yah taare isliye bina foole hote hai jis se yah zyaada garam or kam aakaar ke hote hai. saadhaaran boune taron kii tulana main inase parabangnee prakaash bhi adhik nikalataa he.
 yah taare thande upabone taron se bilkul bhinn hote hai. garam upabone taare do tareeqon se ban sakate hai -
 garam upabone taare aksar gol taragucchon main or andakaar akashgangaon main paae jate hai.
