 olvar goldsmath angrezi kaa mashhoor lekhak tha jo ireland main paidaa hua tha. padariyon ke virodh se is kaa sambandh tha.
 trinity kollege dublin main shiksha hasil kii. baad main edinbura or phir leden main doctori kii shiksha hasil kii.
 shuru main kaam kiya kii lekin kamyaabi nahin hui. chunki tabiyat main sahitya kii taraf ruchi zyaada thi, is liye patrikaon ke liye likhnaa shuru kiye. jab uski kitaab ‘duniya ke naagarik’ prakashit hui to log us kii sahitya main haisiyat maanane lage. chand or kitabon se us kii shoharat badhane lagi. goldsmith ko angrezi sahitya main mahatvapurn sthaan hasil he or ye unhen iske do mashhoor kamedi draamon kii vajah se hasil hua he. ek "di good nechared main" or doosraa "shi satopus tu knkur" he. inke anuwaad kai zabaanon main ho chuke hai or ye aaj tak stage par khele jate hai. unhon ne upanyaas ek hi likhaa he or wo he "di vikaar aaf vekfield" thi. goldsmith ke daur main sahitya par samvedansheeltaa bahut chai hui thi. isane apni rachanaaon ke zarie yatharthwaad ko prachlit kiya. goldsmith ne apni rachanaaon se kaafii kamaaya lekin hameshaa garibi kii zindagi hi unhen naseeb hui. basol ne is ke bare main likhaa tha ki wo paagalpan kii had tak mehmaananawaaz tha or naram dil tha. kisi duusare kii pareshaani unase dekhi nahin jaati thi. apane daur ke kalakaaron or sahityakaron or khaastaur par samual johnson se isaki acchhee dosti thi.
