 akashwani bharat ke soochna evam prasaaran mantralay ke adheen sanchalit saarvajanik kshetra kii radio prasaaran seva he.
 bharat main radio prasaaran kii shuruyaat mumbai or kolkata main san 1927 main do nijee transmitaron se hui. 1930 main iskaa rashtriyakaran hua or tab iskaa naam bhartiya prasaaran seva yaa rakhaa gaya. baad main 1957 main iskaa naam badal kar akashwani rakhaa gaya.
 akashwani kii bahut bhashaaon main vibhinn sevaayen hai jo prayek desh bhar ke vibhinn kshetron main karyrat hai.
 vividh bharti akashwani kii sabase lokapriy-gyaat seva he. ise vigyaapan prasaaran seva bhi kaha jaataa he.
