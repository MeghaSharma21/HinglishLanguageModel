 kartavyavaigyanik nitishastra yaa kartavyavigyaan  vah mandandak nitishastreey sthiti hai, jo kisi kaary kii naitikta ko niyam yaa niyamon ke anupaalan ke aadhaar par, jaj karti hai. kabhi-kabhi, iskaa varnan "kartavya-" yaa "daayitv-" yaa "niyam-" aadhaarit nitishastra ke rup main hota hai, kyonki niyam "aapako aapke kartavya se bandhate hai". saamanytah, kartavyavaigyanik nitishastra ko parinamwad, gun nitishastra or vyavhaarik nitishastra ke vipareet mana jaataa hai. is paribhaashiki main, kaary, parinaam se adhik mahatvapurn hai.
