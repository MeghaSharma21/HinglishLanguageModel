 talha ebn ubaidullah  islamic paigambar mohammad kaa ek saathi tha. zyaadaatar das vaada kiye gaye swarg ke hone ke liye janaa jaataa he. uhood kii ladaai or oont kii ladaai main unki bhoomika ke liye sabase acchhee tarah se janaa jaataa he, jisamen unki mrityu ho gai, unhen mohammad dwaara "udaar" sheershak diya gaya.
 talha kaa janm si 594 tha, :171 makka main kuraishi janajati ke taim vansh ke ubaaydalla ebn uthman kaa putr. unki maa, al-saba bin abdulla, hadaraam janajati se thim. :163 
 unhen ek kaale-chamade vaale aadmi ke rup main varnit kiya gaya he jisamen bahut saare baalo vaale bal, ek sundar chehre or ek sankeern naak he. vah kesar-range kapade or kasturi pahananaa pasand aayaa. vah teji se chalaa gaya or, jab ghabrahat, vah apni anguthi ke saath khilonaa hoga, jo sone kaa tha or ek rubi ke saath set. :167–168 
 talhah ek safal kapadaa vyapaaree tha jisane antat 30 million dirham anumaanit sampatti chodi. :153,169-1670 
 612 main unke rishtedaar abu bakar ne unhen mohammad jane ke liye le liya, or talha ek muslim ban gaye. :164 vah pehle aath roopantaranon main se ek mana jaataa tha. :115 
 614-616 main muslimon ke utpeedan ke dauraan, nafal ebn khuvelid ne taahaha ko abu bakar se baandh liya or unhen ek saath rassi chhod di. Taym kabeeley se koi bhi madad karne ke liye aayaa tha. :164 uske baad unhen "do bandhe ek saath" ke rup main janaa jaataa tha. :127–128,337 
 sitambar 622 main tahahah syria se ek kaaravaan ghar laa rahaa tha jab vah al-khaaraar main mohammad or abu bakar se mili the. ve makka se batch nikle the or madina ke liye utsuk the. talahah ne unhen kuch syrian vastra die or ullekh kiya ki madina main muslim samudaay ne kaha tha ki unke paigambar aane main dhimi thim. jaisaa ki mohammad or abu bakar madina jari rakhte the, talhah apane maamalon ko vyavasthit karne ke liye makka laut aayaa. iske turant baad, vah abu bakar ke parivaar ke saath madina gaya, jahaan vah bas gaya. :164 
 pehle vah asad ebn jurarah ke saath darj hua, lekin baad main mohammad ne unhen bhoomi kaa ek block diya jis par unhone apana ghar banaaya. unhen saiyad ebn jayad ke islam main bhai banaaya gaya tha. :165 talahah or saad badr kii ladaai main ladane se chook gaye kyonki mohammad ne unhen abu sufaan ke kaaravaan kaa pataa lagane ke liye scouts ke rup main bheja tha. haalanki, donon ko lut ke share se sammanit kiya gaya tha, jaise ki ve maujuud the. :165 
 tahahah ne mohammad ke kareeb rakhkar uhood kii ladaai main khud ko pratishthit kiya, jabaki adhikansh muslim sena bhaag gayi. usne mohammad ke chehre ko apane haath main goli markar ek tir se bachaaya, jiske parinamswaroop uski do anguliyon ko lakva diya gaya. vah sir main do baar bhi maaraa gaya tha, or kaha gaya tha ki use yuddh main kul 75 ghaavon kaa saamana karnaa padaa tha. :165–166 
 talhah bhi dach kii ladaai main ladaa "or allaah ke massenger ke saath sabhi ladaai". :166 
 july 632 ke tisare saptaah main, madina ko ek swayan ghoshit bhavishyadvaktaa tulhah kii dharmatyagi shaktiyon dwaara aane vaale akraman kaa saamana karnaa padaa. abu bakar ne mukhya rup se hashim kabeeley se ek sena ko tod diya, talahah, ali ebn abi talib or jubyir ebn al-avam ko nav sangathit bal ke ek-tihaai ke cmanderon ke rup main niyukt kiya. haalanki, unhen rida yudhon ke dauraan kisi bhi yuddh ke drishy kaa saamana nahin karnaa padaa. 
 talahah main kam se kam aath alag-alag mahilaon dwaara kam se kam pandrah bachche the. :163–164 :298 
 oont kii ladaai ali ke bich ek taraf or aaisha, talhah or jubayre ke bich 10 disambar 656 ko ladi gai thi. yuddh ke dauraan, maaravaan ebn al-hakam, jo ek hi taraf lard rahe the, ne galati se talahah ko jaangh main goli maar di . maaravaan ne tippanee kii, "iske baad main kabhi bhi uthman ke hatyare kii talash nahin karuunga." talahah ne apane ghode ko gale laga liya or yuddh ke maidan se ghira hua tha. vah ek takiya ke rup main ek pathar kaa upyog kar rakhana, jabaki sahaayak ne rakt pravaah ko rokane kii koshish kii. jab bhi unhone dabaane se roak diya, khun bah rahaa tha. ant main talha ne kaha, "ise roko. yah bhagwaan dwaara bheja gaya ek tir he." 64 varsh kii umr main is chot se unki mrityu ho gai. :170–171 
 mohammad, das vaada kiye gaye swarg ke bich talhah naamak haddees main.
