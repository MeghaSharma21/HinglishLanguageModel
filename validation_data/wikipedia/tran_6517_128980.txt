 vienna sandhi  yah ozone parat ke sangrakshan ke liye ek bahupaksheeya paryaavaran samjhauta he. is par 1985 ke vienna sammelan main sahmati bani or 1988 main yah lagu kiya gaya. 196 deshon ke saath-saath europea sanghon dwaara ise manjuur kiya ja chukaa he.
 vienna sandhi, ozon parat kii raksha ke liye antarrashtriya prayaason ke liye ek dhaanche ke rup main kaary karata he.haalanki isamen siefasi ke istemaal ke liye kanoonee rup se badhyakaari nunata ke lakshya shaamil nahin hai, ozone parat riktikaran kaa mukhya kaaran rasinic kaarak hai. uparokt baaten montrial protocol main rakhi gayinhain.
