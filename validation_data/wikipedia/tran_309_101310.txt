 demlar agi  ; FWB: DAI) ek german car company he. yah duniya kii terahavin sabase badi car nirmaataa or duusari sabase badi trek nirmaataa company he. automobile ke alaava demlar bason kaa bhi nirmaan karti he or apni demlar financial services shakha ke madhyam se vittiya seva bhi pradaan karti he. arospes samooh eadies main bhi company kaa bahut badi hissedari he, jo ek ucch praudyogiki company hone ke saath-saath vodaphone maclaren marsdes racing team maclaren group or japani trek nirmaataa company mitsubishi fuso trek and bas korporation kii mul company he.
 demlar chrisler kii sthapana, 1998 main germany ke statagart kii marsdes-banj nirmaataa company demlar-banj or america aadhaarit chrisler korporation ke vilay ke saath hui thi. is saude se ek nai company demlar chrisler kaa janm hua. haalanki is kharidari se atlantic ke pare kii ek shaktishaalee automotive company kaa nirmaan na ho sakaa jisaki ummid sauda karne waalon ne kii thi or demlar chrisler ne 14 may 2007 ko yah ghoshnaa kii ki yah chrisler ko neuyork kii cerberas capital management naamak ek privet equity form ko bech degi jise sankatagrast companiyon ke punargathan main visheshgyata praapt he. 4 oktober 2007 ko demlar chrisler ke shairdharakon kii ek asaadharan baithak main company ke punarnamakaran par manjuuri di gai. 5 oktober 2007 ko is company ko demlar agi naam diya gaya. 3 agast 2007 ko bikri kaa kaam puraa hone par america company ne chrisler elelsi naam rakh liya.
 demlar kai brands naamon ke tahat kaaron or trakon kaa nirmaan karti he, jinamein shaamil hai marsdes-banj, maybac, smart or fretleiner.
 demlar agi ek sadi se bhi zyada samay se automobile, motor vaahan or injanon kaa nirmaan karne vaali ek german nirmaataa company rahi he.
 karl banj kii banj and si or gotleeb demlar or wilhem maybac kii demlar moteren geselshaft ke bich 1 may 1924 ko aapasi hit ke ek samjhaute par hastakshar kiya gaya.
 donon companiyon ne 28 june 1926 tak apane-apane alag automobile or aatanrik dahan ingine brandon kaa nirmaan jari rakhaa jab banj and si or demlar moteren geselshaft ke milane se demlar-banj agi kaa nirmaan hua or is baat par apni sahmati vyakt kii ki uske baad sabhi kaarkaanon main unke automobile par marsdes-banj brands naam kaa istemaal kiya jayega.
 1998 main demlar-banj agi or america automobile nirmaataa company chrisler korporation ke milane se demlar chrisler agi kaa nirmaan hua. is samooh ne demlar-banj ke gaiyr-automotive vyavsaay jaise demlar-banj inter services agi  ko unke vistaar sambandhi ranneetiyon par chalate rahane kii anumati pradaan kii. praapt khabaron ke anusaar 1997 main dabis kaa rajasav 8.6 billian dollar tha.
 2007 main jab chrisler samooh ko cerberas capital management ko bech diya gaya tab mul company kaa naam badalakar sirf "demlar agi" kar diya gaya.

 chrisler ko haal ke varshon main lagaataar kai baadhaon kaa saamana karnaa padaa he jiske parinamswaroop may 2007 main 6 billian america dollar main is ikai ko cerberas capital management ko bechney ke liye demlar chrisler ko samjhauta karnaa padaa.apane itihaas ke adhikansh samay main chrisler "big 3" america auto nirmaataa companiyon main se teesri sabase badi company rahi he lekin janvari 2007 main isaki lagjri marsdes or maybac linon ko chhodkar demlar chrisler ne paaramparik rup se doosraa sthaan praapt karne vaale ford ko bhi bech diya haalanki yah general motors or toyota se peeche rahaa he.
 praapt khabaron ke anusaar 2006 main chrisler ko 1.5 billian america dollar kaa nuksaan hua tha. uske baad isane 2008 tak laabhakarita ko behaal karne ke liye madhya-farvari 2007 main 13,000 karmchaariyon ko nikaalane, ek pramukh asembli plant ko band karne or anya planton ke utpaadan main katautee karne kii yojanaon kii ghoshnaa kii. 
 yah vilay vivadpurn tha or niveshkon ne is baat par mukadmaa karnaa shuru aar diya tha ki kya yah lenden 'baraabar kaa vilay' tha jiskaa varisht prabandhan ne daawa kiya tha yaa vastav main iske falswaroop chrisler par demlar banj kaa kabza ho gaya. ek varg karrwai niveshak muqadame ko agast 2003 main 300 million america dollar main nipta diya gaya jabaki karmanyatawadi arbapati kirk kercorean ke ek muqadame ko 7 april 2005 ko khaarij kar diya gaya. is lenden ke falswaroop iske vaastukar chairman jarjen i. shremp ko apni naukari se haath dhonaa padaa jinhone is lenden ke baad company ke share kii keemat main hui giravat ke pratikriyaswaroop 2005 ke ant main istipha de diya. yah vilay bill vlasik or bradli e. stertz kii teken for e ride: haau demlar banj drove of wyeth chrisler naamak ek kitaab kaa vishay bhi tha.
 vivaad kaa ek or mudda yah he ki kya is vilay se prastaavit sahakriya par amal kiya gaya or kya iske falswaroop donon vyavsaayon kaa safal ekikaran hua. adhik se adhik 2002 main demlar chrisler ko do swatantra utpaada linon kaa sanchaalan karte hue dekha gaya. baad main usee varsh company ne jin utpaadon kaa shubharambh kiya ve company ke donon pakshon ke tatwon ko ekikrut karata hua dikhaai detaa he jisamen chrisler crossfire bhi shaamil he jo marsdes esailke plateform par aadhaarit tha or jisamen marsdes kii 3.2L V6 kaa istemaal kiya gaya tha or iske saath hi saath isamen dose sprinter/fretleiner sprinter bhi shaamil he jo ek punarbej vaala marsdes banj sprinter vine he. chauthi peedi kii jeep grand cheroki marsdes banj em-class par aadhaarit he haalanki sachai yah he ki demlar/chrisler algaav ke lagbhag chaar saal baad iskaa nirmaan kiya gaya tha.
 kathith taur par demlar chrisler ne chrisler ko bechney ke liye 2007 ke armbh main anya car nirmaataa companiyon or nivesh samoohon se sampark kiya. general motors ne apni ichha prakat kii jabaki reynolt-nissaan auto gathbandhan volkswagon or hundai motor company ne kaha tha ki unhen is company ko kharidne main koi dilchaspee nahin he.
 3 agast 2007 ko demlar chrisler ne cerberas capital management ko chrisler group bechney kaa kaam puraa kiya. mul samjhaute ke anusaar cerberas ko nai company chrisler holding elelsi main 80.1 pratishat hissaa mila. demlar chrisler kaa naam badalakar demlar agi kar diya gaya or alag ho chuke chrisler main uski shesh 19.9% kii hissedari kaayam rahi. 
 samay ke saath demlar ko cerberas ke haathon se chrisler ko hasil karne or isse judi dendariyon se chhutkaaraa paane ke liye cerberas ko 650 million america dollar kaa bhugtan karnaa padaa. yah 1998 main chrisler par kabza karne ke liye chukaai gai 36 billian america dollar kii tulana main ek ullekhneey vipareet bhagya he. kray mulya 7.4 billian america dollar main se cerberas capital management chrisler holdings main 5 billian america dollar or chrisler kii vittiya unit main 1.05 billian america dollar kaa nivesh karegi. alag ho chuke demlar agi ko cerberas se sidhe 1.35 billian america dollar praapt hua lekin isane khud chrisler main sidhe 2 billian america dollar kaa nivesh kiya.
 sanyukt rajya america main chrisler kii 2009 ke divaaliepan kii filing ke baad se chrisler ko italvi auto nirmaataa company phiet dwaara niyantrit kiya gaya he jo demlar ke vipareet chrisler ke utpaadon ko khaas taur par lansia or chrisler ke samaan naam vaale brands ko phiet portpholio main ekikrut karnaa chaahati he.
 7 april 2010 ko reynolt-nissaan kaaryakari carlos ghosan or dow dieter jetshe ne ek sanyukt samvaadadaata sammelan main teen companiyon ke bich ek saajhedaari kii ghoshnaa kii. 
 dow dieter jetshe 1 janvari 2006 se demlar ke chairman or marsdes banj cars ke head hone ke saath-saath 1998 se board of management ke sadsy hai. vah pehle chrisler elelsi ke president or siio the. vah america main shaayad "ask dow jed" naamak ek chrisler vigyaapan abhiyaan ke dow jed ke rup main mashhoor hai.

 1 april 2010 ko america nyaay vibhaag or america pratibhuti evam vinimay ayog dwaara lagaae gaye rishwatakhori ke do aaropon main demlar agi kii german or roosi sahaayak companiyon ko doshi paaya gaya. khud demlar ek niptaan ke rup main 185 million america dollar kaa bhugtan karegi lekin company or isaki chini sahaayak company abhi bhi do varshon se asthgit abhiyojan samjhaute ke adheen he jiske liye niamakon ke atirikt sahyog, aatanrik niyantranon ke anupaalan or waapas adaalat ke kamare main unke lautne se pehle anya sharton ko puraa karne kii jarurat he. do saalon kii avadhi main samjhaute kii sharton ko puraa karne main vifal hone par demlar ko kathor dandon kaa saamana karnaa padegaa.
 iske atirikt demlar dwaara rishwatakhori virdohi kanoonon ke anupaalan kii nigaraanee karne ke liye fedral beuro of investigeshan ke purv diarector louis je. freeh ek swatantra monitor ke rup main apni seva pradaan karenge.
 america abhiyaajakon ne demlar, demlar kii sahaayak companiyon or demlar se judi companiyon ke pramukh kaaryakariyon par duniya bhar main sarkari thekon ko surakshit karne ke liye 1998 or 2008 ke bich gaiyr kanoonee tareeke se videshi adhikariyon ko paise or uphaar dene kaa iljaam lagaaya. is maamle kii jaanch se pataa chalaa ki demlar ne anuchit tareeke se kam se kam 22 deshon main lagbhag 200 se zyada lendenon se sambandhit rishwaton main lagbhag 56 million dollar kaa bhugtan kiya jiske parinamswaroop company ko 1.9 billian dollar rajasav or gaiyr kanoonee laabh ke rup main kam se kam 91.4 million dollar praapt hua.
 2004 main dakshin america main marsdes banj kii uniton dwaara niyantrit bank khaaton ke bare main sawaal uthaane ke liye naukari se nikaale jane ke baad tatkaaleen demlar chrisler corp ke purv lekha pareekshak david bajetta dwaara ek mubir shikaayat darj kiye jane ke baad esaisi kaa mudda bhadak uthaa. bajetta ne aarope lagaaya ki statagart main hui july 2001 kii korporate lekha pareekshan kaaryakari samiti kii ek baithak main unhen pataa chalaa ki vyavsaayik uniton dwaara "videshi sarkari adhikariyon ko rishwat dene ke liye gupt bank khaaton ko banaae rakhaa ja rahaa he" haalanki company ko america kaanoon kaa ulanghan karne vaali is prakriya ke bare main maaloom tha.
 bajetta ko chup karaane ke ek or prayaas main demlar ne baad main unki naukari kii samaapti se jude is muqadame ko adaalat ke baahar nipataane kii peshkash kii jise unhone ant main sweekaar kar liya. lekin bajetta ke saath demlar kii ranneeti naakaamayaab sabit hui kyonki rishwatakhori-virdohi kanoonon ke liye pehle se hi america aapraadhik jaanch chal rahi thi jo ek videshi korporation ke khilaaf chal rahe sabase vyapak maamalon main se ek he.
 aaropon ke anusaar jarurat se zyada chaalaan karne vaale graahakon dwaara aksar rishwatakhori or sheersh sarkari adhikariyon yaa unke pratinidhiyon ko atyadhik raashi kaa bhugtan kiya gaya he. rishwaton ne bhogavilaspurv europea chhuttiyon, ucch padon par asin sarkari adhikariyon ke liye bakhtarband marsdes vaahanon or ek varisht turkmenistan adhikari ke liye ek sone ke bakse or adhikari ke vyaktigat ghoshnapatron kii german bhashaa main anuwaad kii gai 10,000 pratiyon vaale ek janmdin uphaar kaa rup bhi dhaaran kar liya he.
 jaanchakartaaon ko is baat kaa bhi pataa chalaa ki form ne us samay saddaam hossain ke netritv vaali iraqi sarkaar ke tahat kaam karne vaali adhikariyon ko theke ke mulya ke 10% mulya kii rishwat dekar iraq ke saath sanyukt raashtra ke oil-for-food program kii sharton kaa ulanghan kiya he. esaisi ne kaha ki company ko bhrasht oil-for-food saudon main vaahanon or spair parts kii bikri se 4 million dollar se zyada aamadani hui he.
 america abhiyojkon ne aage chalakar yah aarope lagaaya ki kuch rishwaton kaa bhugtan america main aadhaarit shell companiyon ke madhyam se kii gai thi. adaalat kii kagajaton se jaahir hua ki "kuch maamalon main demlar ne rishwat kii rakam pahunchaane ke liye america shell companiyon ke america bank khaaton yaa videshi bank khaaton main in anuchit bhugtan raashiyon ko sthanantarit kiya tha."
 abhiyojkon ne kaha ki is prakriya ko kuch had tak protsaahit karne vaali ek korporate samskruti kii vajah se rishwat kii rakam kaa bhugtan karne ke liye ek "lambey samay se chali aa rahi pratha" main demlar kaa haath tha.
 nyaay vibhaag ke aapraadhik prabhag ke ek principal deputi mithili raman ne kaha ki "apatati bank khaaton, tisare dal ke agenton or bhraamak mulya nirdhaaran prakriyaaon kaa istemaal karke in companiyon ne videshi rishwatakhori ko vyavsaay karne kaa jariya bana liya."
 esaisi ke pravartan prabhag ke diarector robert khujami ne ek bayaan main kaha ki "demlar ke bhrashtachar or rishwat dene kii kriya kaa varnan ek maanak vyavsaay prakriya ke rup main karnaa koi atishyokti nahin he."
 demlar ke board ke chairman dieter jetshe ne ek bayaan main kaha ki "hamane apane pichley anubhav se bahut kuch siikhaa he."

 abhiyojan paksh ke saath samjhaute ke anusaar, do demlar sahaayak vyavsaay bhartee karaayaa jeetne ke liye jaanboojhkar ulanghan videshi bhrashtachar adhinium ko rishwat ke liye videshi adhikariyon or companiyon ko bhugtan salaakhon, jo apane se adhikari shaamil hai. videshi bhrashtachar prakriya adhinium us company par lagu hota he jo jo apane sheyaron ko america stock exenjon main soochibaddh karaati he. demlar agi ko enwiesai main "dieai" sanket ke saath soochibaddh kiya gaya tha jisase nyaay vibhaag ko duniya bhar ke deshon main german car nirmaataa company ke bhugtan kaa kshetradhikaar praapt hua.

 marsdes banj 2009 kii garmiyon main ek hybrid drive system se susajjit apane pehle yatri car marsdes banj es 400 hybrid kaa armbh karne vaali he.
 demlar treks hybrid systamon ke maamle main vishwa bazaar agrani he. apane "shoping future transportesan" pahal ke saath demlar trakon or bason ke liye ek spasht uddeshy ko puraa karne main lagi hui he. mitsubishi fuso kii "airo star iko hybrid" ab japan main vyaavhaarik pareekshanon main nae maanakon kii sthapana kar rahi he.
 16 navambar 2009 ko demlar ne bron jeepi ke 75.1% share kharid liye. company kaa nayaa brands naam marsdes jeepi rakhaa gaya. ross bron team ke pramukh bane rahenge or yah team euke ke bracle main aadhaarit hogi. haalaanki bron kii kharidari kaa uddeshy yah tha ki demlar maclaren main apane 40% share ko phir se kai charnon main bech sake jo 2011 tak chalegaa. marsdes 2015 tak maclaren ko prayojan or ingine pradaan karti rahegi, uske baad maclaren ko sambhawatah ek ingine aapurtikarta company khojani padegi yaa vah khud apane injanon kaa nirmaan karne lagegi. nai company ke 45.1% share par marsdes kaa swaamitv he jabaki aabaar investments ke paas 30% or ross bron ke paas 24.9% kaa swaamitv he. racing team ne purv champion maaikel shoomakar ke saath anubandh kiya he.
