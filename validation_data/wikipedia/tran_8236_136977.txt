 kaans ek prakaar kii lambii ghaas hoti he jo ki multah dakshin asia main pai jaati he. yah ghaas 3 me. tak lambii ho jaati he. bharat, nepal or bhutan main himalay ke talahati vaale tarai kshetra main tathaa bangladesh main bhi yah ghaas baadh ke baad nadiyon dwaara laai gai urvarak mitti par tezi se qaabiz ho jaati he. yah ghaas bhartiya gande ke liye atyant mahatvapurn awasaiya kshetra pradaan karti he.
