 bala bachchan kaa aarambhik jivan kaaphi ksht bharaa rahaa, ve bahut garib parivaar se the unke pitaaji mehnat mazdooree kar apane parivaar kaa paalan poshan karte the | unhone bhi apane pita ke saath kheto me kaam kiya or saath me padhai bhi kii raajnneeti me jaise unki bachapan se hi ruchi thi ve skool ke samay se hi chaatr rajaniti me sakriy the |
 bala bachchanaki aarambhik shiksha unke grah gram kasel me hi hui, madhyamik shiksha gov me paane ke baad hai skool va hai secondari ke liye raajpur kii sarkari vighalay me admician le liya, iske baad ve scollar ke dam par ejeeniyar kii padhai karne indore chale gaye | ejeeniyar ke padhai ke antim varsh shuru hua unka rajanitik jivan.....
