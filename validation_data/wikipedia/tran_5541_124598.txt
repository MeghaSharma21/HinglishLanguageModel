 shah vansh nepalke antim shaasha vansh he jinhone san 1768 se 2008 tak nepal adhirajyaka shashan kiya. nepalke pehle shahvanshi raja prithvinarayan shah he.
 is vanshake utpattimein bohot saare kahani he. shahvanshake daabi anuroop chandravanshi raajput rishiraj ranajike vanshaj he. rishiraj ranaji bhattarak oopaadhi lii thi or wo chittod  maharaj the. shah vanshavalike anusaar unke vanshaj bhoopal ranaji raav nepalke ridi aage or gandki kshetramen shashan karne lagagaye. unke vanshaj kulamandan khannne shah oopaadhi dhaaran kiya or baadame dravya shah gorkha rajya ke gaddi chaalaakhi se kabja kiya.
