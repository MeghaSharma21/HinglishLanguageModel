 sirsa jile kaa naam, iske mukhyayal sirsa ke naam se utpann hua he. aisa mana jaataa he ki sirsa jila, uttar bharat ke sabase puraane jilon main se ek he. sirsa kaa zikr, mahabharat main bhi hua he, haalanki us kaal main ise sairishaka ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. is sthal kaa ullekh, panini ke ashtadhai or divyavadan main bhi miltaa he. mahabharat main, yah bhi ullekh kiya gaya he ki sairishaka ko pashchimi timahi ke vijay abhiyaan main nakul dwaara liya gaya tha. panini ne ullekh kiya he ki sirsa 5 veen sadi kaa ek samrudh shahar tha.itihaas
 sirsa, bharat ke haryana rajya main sthit ek shahar he, jo rashtriya rajmarg 10 par sthit he. 1819 main, is kshetra ko british shashan ne apane adheen kar liya tha or baad main ise dilli kshetra ke uttari - pashchimi jile kaa ek hissaa bana diya tha. ek saal baad, uttar - pashchimi jile ko do hisson main baant diya gaya jinhe uttari or pashchimi jilon ke naam se janaa gaya or sirsa, pashchimi jile kaa hissaa ban gaya jise baad main haryana naam diya gaya.sirsa or uske aasapaas sthit paryatak sthal
 sirsa jile main paryatakon kii saiyr ke kaaphi sthal he. sirsa, deraa sachcha sauda kaa mukhyalay he, yah ek prakaar kaa dharmik samooh he jisaki sthapana shah mastaana ne kii thi, jinka vaastavik naam khemaamal tha. yah sector, apni saamaajik seva gatividhiyon ke kaaran janaa jaataa he or yahaan sabhi ko muft main lungar bhi chakaaya jaataa he, saath hi janata se kisi bhi prakaar kaa daan bhi nahin sweekaar kiya jaataa he. yahaan kaa anya lokapriy dharmik sampradaay, radha swami sampradaay he jo yaheen sthit he.sirsa shahar se 5 kilometre dur puvar kii ore radha swami satsang ghar sthit he. sect, punjab main sthit amritsar ke beas main sthit radha swami mukhyalay kii ek shakha he.
 paryatak jab bhi sirsa jaaen to vahaan kagdana main sthit ram dev mandir main bhi avashya jaaen. jaisaa ki naam se hi spasht he ki yah mandir baba ram devji ko samarpit he jinhe bharat ke kai rajyon, visheshkar rajasthan main or pakistan ke sindh main devtaa ke rup main puja jaataa he.babaaramdev, garibon or pichde varg kii madad karne ke liye jane jate the or unke bare main va unki chamatkaari shaktiyon ke bare main kai kahaniyaan bhi prachlit he. iske alaava, yahaan aakar ramnagariyaa main hanuman mandir or chormaar kheraa main gurudwara guru govind singh kii yatra bhi avashya karnaa chahie. aisa mana jaataa he ki sikh guru apni raat yaheen bitaate he. yahaan deraa baba sarasai nath mandir he, jise 13 veen sadi main yahaan banwaaya gaya tha, is mandir ko sirsa main hisar dwaar ke naam se janaa jaataa he. is mandir kaa nirmaan sarasai nath ne karavaaya tha, jo ek pramukh guru yaa rishi the, jinhone apane anuyaayiyon ke saath milakar yahaan prarthana, dhyaan or kai dharmik anushthan bhi kiye.sirsa or uske aasapaas ke kshetron main ghaggar ghati kii samrudh aitihaasik or saanskrithik kii amuly viraasat yahaan ek swarg samaan he. yahaan aakar paryatak, bhartiya puraatatv surveykshan ke utkhanan sthalon kii yatra bhi kar sakate he.
