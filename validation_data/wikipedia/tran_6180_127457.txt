 kerala ke kasarcode jile kii hosadurg taluka ke pallikera gram main sthit bekal durg rajya kaa sabase bada durg he. 40 acre kshetrafal main failaa ye durg mangalore se lagbhag 65 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. durg kaa nirmaan kelaadi ke shivappa nayak ne 1650 main kiya tha. 1992 main bharat sarkaar ne ise vishesh paryaton kshetra ghoshit kiya.
