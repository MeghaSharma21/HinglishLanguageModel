 aalsar paanch gaanvo kii gram panchayat he. itihaas main yah bahut bana gaanv tha, baad main yah chaar gaanvo main bant gaya. aalsar, aalsar baas, premanagar, amritavasi aadi jat,rajput,megvaal,suthar,nik,saansi aadi. yah bidaasar va rajaldesar kasbo se judata he.
 paas ke gaavon main premanagar, aalsar baas, dhadheru, parasaneu, jeganiyan bikaan aadi shaamil hai.
