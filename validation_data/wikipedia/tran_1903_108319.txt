 
 yakritshoth k ek vishaanu janit rog he. yakritshoth k yakrut kii sujan hoti he jo yakritshoth k vishaanu ke kaaran hoti he. isamen rogi ko kaafii chidchidapan hota he. ise vishaanujanit yakritshoth bhi kehete hai. yah beemaari dooshit bhojan grahan karne, dooshit jal or is beemaari se grast vyakti ke sampark main aane se failati he. iske lakshan prakat hone se pehle or beemaari ke pratham saptaah main andanu taiyaar hone ke pandrah se pantalis din ke bich rogi vyakti ke mel se yakritshoth k vishaanu failata he. rakt evam sharir ke anya dravya bhi sankramak ho sakate hai. sankraman samaapt hone ke baad sharir main na to virus hi shesh rahata he or na hi vaahak rahata he. yakritshoth k ke lakshan flu jaise hi hote hai, kintu twachaa tathaa ankhe pili ho jaati hai, jaise ki pilia main hoti hai. iskaa kaaran he ki yakrut rakt se billirubin ko chaan nahin paata he. anya saamaanya yakritshoth vishaanu, hepatitis bi or hepatitis si he, kintu yakritshoth k sabase kam gambhir he or in bimariyon main sabase maamuli he. anya donon bimaariyaan lambii bimariyon main parivartit ho sakati he. kintu yakritshoth k nahin hoti.
 iske khaas lakshan pilia rog jaise hi hote hai. iske alaava thakaavat, bhukh na laganaa, mitli, halkaa fever, pilaa yaa slate rang kaa mel, pile rang kaa peshaab evam saare sharir main khujali ho sakati he.
 ashuddh bhojan va pani se dur rahen, shouch aadi se nivrutt hokar haath acchhee tarah se dhoen, tathaa prabhavit vyakti ke rakt, fesis yaa sharir ke dravyon ke sampark main aane par acchhee tarah se apane aapako saaf karke virus ko badhane yaa failane se roka ja saktaa he. dainik dekhbhaal suvidhaayen or logo ke ghanisht sampark main aane vaale anya sansthaanon ke kaaran yakritshoth k ke failane kii sambhaavna adhik ho jaati he. kapade badalane se pehle or baad main haath acchhee tarah se dhone, bhojan parosane se pehle or shauchalay ke baad haath saaf karne se iske failane ko roka ja saktaa he.yakritshoth k se grast logon ke sampark main rahane vaale logon ko immune globulin dena chahie. yakritshoth k sankraman ke rokane ke liye tike uplabdh he. tike kii pratham khuraaq lene ke chaar saptaah baad tikaa asar karnaa shuru kar detaa he. lambey samay tak suraksha ke liye 6 se 12 maah kaa booster aavashyak he.
 yakritshoth k se bahut adhik prabhavit kshetron yaa deshon kii yatra karte hon usee samay de diya janaa chahie jab tikaa diya ja rahaa ho lekin yah tikaa us sthaan par nahin diya janaa chahie jahaan pehla tikaa diya gaya ho). iske alaava guda sambhog karne vaale, ai ve dawa ke upyogkarta or jo gambhir rup se hepatitis bi yaa si se sankramit hon unhen tikaa lagaana aavashyak he.
