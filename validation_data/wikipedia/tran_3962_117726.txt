 aazaadee ke baad hindi kahani ko nayaa sanskaar dene vaale kahanikaron ne kahani ko nayi kahani ke naam se abhihit kiya.             
 nayi kahani kaa janm 1956 se mana jaataa he. 1956 main bhairav prasad gupt ke sampadan main nayi kahani naam kii patrika kaa ek visheshank nikaala. isi visheshank ke aadhaar par agali kadi kii kahaniyon ko nayi kahani ke naam se sambodhit kiya jane laga.
