 udaipur jila nepal ke pradesh sankhya 1 ke 14 jilon main se ek jila he. jile kaa mukhyalay triyuga/gaighat he or jile kaa kul kshetrafal 2063 kimi² he. 2011 ke janaganana ke anusaar jile kii jansankhya 317532 he. yah jila samudr sataha se 360 meter se 2310 meter kii oonchai par sthit he.
 uttar main mahabharat shrinkhalaa se lekar dakshin main chure parvatamalaon se ghire bhitri madhesh kshetra ke andar udaipur jila vishwa ke sarvocch shikhar sagarmaatha kii god main avasthit he. yah jila puraane vibhaajan ke anusaar nepal ke purvanchal vikaas kshetra ke sagarmaatha anchal main padataa he. purab main thodaa sikuda sa lekin pashchim kii ore failata gaya is jile kaa kshetrafal 2063 varg kilo metre he. bhu–banaavat ke aadhaar par pahaad, beshi, tar or tarai samet kaa bhu–bhaag samete hue yah jila kshetrafal ke hisaab se nepal kaa 20waan, purvanchal vikaas kshetra kaa chauthaa or sagarmaatha anchal ke duusare sthanon main aata he. is jile ki simaaen uttar main mukhyatah bhojpur tathaa khotong or okhladhunga ke kuch bhaagon se lagata he, pashchim main mukhyatah sindhuli or dhanusha ke kuch bhaagon se lagata he, dakshin main siraahaa or saptari evam purv main mukhyatah sunasri or dhankuta ke kuch bhaagon se lagata he. is tarah udaipur kii simaaen 9 jilon se milti hai. visham bhugolic banaavat vaala yah jila mahabharat shrinkhalaa ke antargat tej or vikat chotiyon se bharaa he, yahaan faile hue kam ghanatv vaale choti-moti bastiyaan he. mahabharat ke talahati se shivalik shrinkhalaa ke bich main samthar phant hai to dakshini sima ke shivalik kshetra main ghaney jangal hai. triyuga nadi ke kinare shivalik mahabharat taraf bich ke bhitri madhes ke phaanton main triyuga nagarpalika triyuga upatyaka naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. isi nagarpalika main avasthit gaighat kii oonchai samudr sataha se 360 metre se lekar puraa jila kshetra 2310 metre oonchai tak he. rashtri janaganana 2011 ke anusaar yahaan kii jansankhya 3 lakh 17 hajaar 532 vyakti he. puraane vibhaajan ke anusaar is jile main 3 sansadeey nirvachan kshetra, 11 jila vikaas samiti ilaaka, 3 nagarpalika or 40 gaanv vikaas samiti hai. 	 udaipur jila prachinkaal main udaysen naamak raja kaa shasit kshetra hone ke kaaran unhi ke naam par is jile kaa naam udaipur rakhaa gaya. is jile ki apni hi visheshtaayen or pahchaan he. nepal desh kaa sab se bada cement udyog "udaipur cement udyog" is jile main hone ke kaaran yah is jile kaa pahchaan ban gaya he.
 yah jila nepal ke maanchitr par 26 degree 26' 39" se 27 degree 1' 10" uttari akshans, 86 degri 0' 9" se 87 degri 1' 0" purvi deshaantar main he. ushn shithoshna or samshitoshna hawa pani vaale is jile kaa adhiktam tapakram lagbhag 38 degree celsius or newnatam 16 degree celsius he. barsha lagbhag 2152 millilitre he.
 bhu-banaavat ke aadhaar par is jile ko 3 prakaar ke dharatleey swarup main baanta ja saktaa he:
 uttar main sunakosi nadi se lekar dakshin main mahabharat ke antim bhaag tak kaa kshetra pahari kshetra he. jile kaa karib 60% bhaag is bhu-bhaag se dhakaa he. takreeban 1100 mi. se lekar 2310 metre tak ki oonchai vaale is kshetra main lekhaani, majhakhark, rauta pokhar jaise oonche pahaad tathaa chotii hai.
 nepali main talahati ko basi kehete hai tathaa pahaado se ghire chote maidani kshetra ko tar kehete hai. karib 550 metre se 1100 metre tak ki oonchai vaala yah dharatleey swaroop mahabharat ke antim bhaag se lekar dakshin main tarai ke bhu-bhaag tak dhakaa he. jile kaa karib 9% bhubhag samete hue is kshetra main nepalter, murkuchi or minatr jaise chote-chote ghati hai tathaa bahunitar, bhuttar, hardeni jaise basi evam khonch hai.
 samudri sataha se 360 metre se lekar 550 metre tak ke oonchai main avasthit yah bhu-bhaag bhitri madhes ke rup main pahachaanaa jaataa he. yah kshetra jile main lagbhag 31% kshetra samete he. mukhya rup se triyuga or tawa nadi simavarti kshetra main aane vaale is dharatleey swarup ke dakshin bhaag main chure pahad hai. yah kshetra nadi kataan samasya se zyada grasit he. jile kaa pramukh sthaan gaighat, kataari or beltr isi bhaag main padataa he.
