 bathinda vimaanashetra  ek bhartiya vimaanashetra he jo bharat ke punjab rajya ke bathinda shahar main sthit he. yah vimaanashetra military or saarvajanik he jo uttar-pashchim se virk kalam gaanv se 20 kilometre dur sthit he.
 25 navambar 2016 ko punjab ke upmukhyamantri sukhbir singh badal ne ghoshnaa kii ki elins air ko 11 disambar se bathinda or dilli ke bich udaanen bharani shuru karegi. air india or sthaneey adhikariyon ke bich vaartalapon kii vyavhaaryata ke bare main ek saptaah baad yah ghoshnaa hui.
