 vibhajyata ke niyam un vidhiyon ko kehete hai jo saralata se bataa dete hai ki koi sankhya kisi duusari sankhya se vibhajit ho sakati he yaa nahin. kisi bhi aadhaar vaale sankhya-paddati ke liye aise niyam banaaye ja sakate hai kintu yahan keval dashamik pranali ke sankhyaon ke liye vibhajyata ke niyam niyam diye gayi hai.
 yadi dahaai sthaan kaa ank visham ho tathaa ikai sthaan par 2, yaa 6.
