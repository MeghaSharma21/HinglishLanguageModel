 sandharitra yaa capaceter, vidyut paripath main prayukt hone vaala do siron vaala ek pramukh avyav he. yadi do yaa do se adhik chaalkon ko ek vidyutrodhi madhyam dwaara alag karke sameep rakhaa jaae, to yah vyavastha sandharitra kehalaati he. in chaalkon par baraabar tathaa vipareet aavesh hote hai. yadi sandharitra ko ek bydaree se joda jaae, to isamen se dhara kaa pravaah nahin hoga, parantu isaki platon par baraabar maatra main ghanaatmak evam rinatmak aavesh sanchay ho jaayenge. vidyut sandharitra kaa upyog vidyut aavesh, athwa sthir vaidyut urja, kaa sanchay karne ke liye tathaa vaidyut filter, snabar aadi main hota he. 
 sandharitra main dhaatu kii do platen hotin hai jinke bich ke sthaan main koi kuchaalak dielectric padaarth bharaa hota he. sandharitra ke platon ke bich dhara kaa pravaah tabhi hota he jab iske donon platon ke bich kaa vibhavaantar samay ke saath badale. is kaaran niyat disi vibhavaantar lagane par sthaayee avasthaa main sandharitra main koi dhara nahin bahati. kintu sandharitra ke dono siron ke bich prayavarti vibhavaantar lagane par uske platon par sanchit aavesh kam yaa adhik hota rahata he jiske kaaran vahya paripath main dhara bahati he. sandharitra se hokar disi dhara nahi bah sakati.
