 sanghnitr ek yantric yukti he jo gas yaa vaashp ko thanda karke drav main badal detii he. sanghnitr kai jagah prayog kiye jate hai. urja sanyatron main inkaa prayog terbine se nikalne vaale bhaap ko sanghanit karne ke liye kiya jaataa he. shitalan sanyantron main ammonia evam florinetad hydrocarbonon jaise shitalk vaashpon ko sanghanit karne ke kaam aata he. petroleum evam anya rasinic udyogon main hydrocarbonon evam anya rasayanon ke vaashpon ko sanghanit karne ke liye kaam main liya jaataa he.
