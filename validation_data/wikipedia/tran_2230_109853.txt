 nepal ke pratham raashtrapati shri ram baran yadav
 dow rambaran yadav nepal ke ek rajaneta he jo july, 2008 ko nepal ke pratham raashtrapati nirvaachit hue. iske purv ve 1999 se 2001 tak swasthya mantri tathaa nepali congress ke mahasachiv the.
 peshey se doctor shri yadav kii ucch-shiksha bharat main hui he. yadav 1991-1994 kii nepali congress sarkaar main swasthya ke liye rajya mantri the. 1999 ke chunaavon main ve pratinidhi sabha ke liye chune gaye. shri yadav nepal ke pehle raashtrapati ke rup main 21 july 2008 ko ek matdaan ke duusare daur main nirvaachit hue. unhone raashtrapati ke rup main 23 july 2008 ko shapath lee.
