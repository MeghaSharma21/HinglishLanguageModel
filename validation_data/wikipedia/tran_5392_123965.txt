 arun govil ek hindi film evam doordarshan abhinayta hai. inhone ramanand sagar nirmit hindi dharawahik ramayana main ram kii bhoomika nibhaai thi. isse inako bahut prasiddhi mili.
 arun govil kaa janm ram nagar uttarapradesh main hua | inaki praarambhik shiksha uttarapradesh se hi hui | unhi dinon yah naatak main abhinay karte the | inke pita chaahate the ki yah ek sarkari naukareepesha bane par arun govil kaa sochna vipareet tha | arun kuch aisa karnaa chaahate the jo yaadgaar bane, isliye san 1975 main yah bombay chale gaye or vahaan khud kaa vyavsaay prarambh kiya us vakt yah keval 17 varsh ke the | kuch dinon ke baad inhen abhinay ke nae nae raste milanaa shuru hue|
 arun govil zyaadaatar rajashrivaalon kii paariwarik filmon main kaam karte the, jinamein jivan mulyon ko andekha nahin kiya jaataa he. ramanand sagar-nirdeshit ramayana main bhagwaan ram kaa kirdaar bhi aise hi tha. isliye govil bhi ram kaa kirdaar achche se kar paae or yahi unki shoharat kaa mukhya kaaran bana.
