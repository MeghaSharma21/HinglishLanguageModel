 kankawadi ek gaanv tathaa durg he jo bhartiya rajya rajasthan ke alvar zile main sthit he. is durg kii sthapana savai raja jai singh pratham ne kii thi. rajya main akaal padane par jai singh ji ne is kile kaa nirmaan kaary shuru karavaaya, jisase ilaake ke logon ko kaam tathaa mehtaanaa miltaa rahe. am log jahaan din main kile par kaam karte the, sambhraant parivaar ke log raat ke akar apane hisse kaa kaam puraa karte the.
 aurangzeb ne gaddi kii ladaayee ke dauraan apane bhai daara shikoh ko kuch samay ke liye is kile main kaid rakhaa tha.
 kankawadi sariska baag parisar ke mukhya ilaake main padataa he or isiliye yahaan rah rahe gaanv waalon ko sarkaar visthapit kar chuki he. kuchhek parivaar abhi bhi yahaan rah rahe hai, par ve bhi sheeghra sariska ke bahari ilaake main visthapit kar diye jaayenge.
 kile tak jane ke liye sariska van parisar se safaari jeep kiraaye par lee ja sakati he. van parisar agast va sitambar main band rahata he.
