 tark kii jis prakriya main ek yaa adhik gyaat saamaanya kathanon ke aadhaar par kisi nishchit nishkarsh par pahunchaa jaataa he, nigamanaatmak tark kehete hai. 'nigamanaatmak tark', 'aagamanaatmak tark' se bilkul bhinn he.
 neeche ek nigamanaatmak tark diya gaya he-:
 1. sabhi manushya nashwar hai.
 2. mohan manushya he.
 3. atah mohan nashwar he.
