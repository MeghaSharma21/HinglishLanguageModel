 baba taahir 11veen shatee i. ke madhya main hue farsi ke utkrisht kavi the. unke nivasasthaan evam jeevankaal kii ghatnaon ke vishay main bada matbhed he, kintu ve sambhavat: adhiktar hamadaan evam luristaan main nivas karte rahe. unki rachanaaon main rubaayiyaan badi prasiddh hai jinase unke svachhand jivan kii jhaanki praapt hoti he. unki lokoktiyaan gudh darshanik vichaaron se paripurna hai.
