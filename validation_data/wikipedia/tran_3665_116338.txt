 mevaat, bhartiya rajya haryana kaa ek jila he. iskaa mukhyalay nuh main sthit he. mevaat bhugolic vishmataaon se bharaa hua he. yahaan par kahin khule maidan hai to kahin aravali parvat shrinkhalaa kii pahadiyaan dekhi ja sakati hai. sthaneey nivasee mia adivaasiyon ke vanshaj hai. mevaat ke logon kaa mukhya kaary krishi par aadhaarit he. krishi ke baad iske sabase zyada log deyari udyog main lage hue hai. aadhunik mevaat kii sthapana 1 navambar 1966 i. ko faridabad or gudgaanv ke kuch kshetron ko milakar kii gai. nuh, tavadu, nagina, firozpur jhirka, punhana or hathin iske mukhya khand hai. yahaan par hindi kii boli mewati boli jaati he.
 mevaat bharat ke pehle country club ke liye janaa jaataa he. yah country club paryatakon ko bahut pasand aata he kyonki paryatak yahaan par aasaani se thahar sakate hai or shandaar chhuttiyon kaa anand uthaa sakate hai. 
 mevaat ke nuh main paryatak gandhi park ghoomane ja sakate hai. yah park bada khubasurat he. paryatak yahaan par shandaar picnic manaa sakate hai.
 mevaat main paryatak khubasurat clasik golf resort ghoomane ja sakate hai. isaki saiyr karnaa paryatakon ko bahut pasand aata he. yah resort aravali parvat shrinkhalaa kii tarai main lagbhag 300 acre main failaa hua he. is golf resort main 27 holes he. yahaan par paryatak behtareen golf khilne kaa anand uthaa sakate hai. central dilli se yah resort maatr 75 mile kii duuri par sthit he.
 yah bharat kaa sabase pehla country club he. yah dilli- jaipur rashtriya rajmarg par manesar ke paas sthit he. country club kaaphi haraa-bharaa he or lagbhag 30 acre main failaa hua he. isaki hariyaali paryatakon ko kaaphi pasand aati he. paryatakon ke thaharne ke liye yahaan kaaphi acchhee suvidha he. yahaan par paryatakon ko spa, disco, health-club or swimming pool kii suvidha di jaati he. 
 mevaat main ghoomane ke baad paryatak tajmahal, vrundavan, mathura, tijaara jain mandir or nimrana kila dekhne ja sakate hai. yahaan se tajmahal 200 ki.me, vrundavan or mathura 150 ki.me, tijaara jan mandir 60 kimi or nimrana kila 70 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he.
 vayumarg se mevaat pahunchane ke liye paryatakon ko pehle indira gandhi hawaii adde pahunchnaa padataa he. vahaan se paryatak taxi lekar aasaani se mevaat tak pahunch sakate hai.
 paryatak dilli-jaipur rashtriya rajmarg se mevaat tak aasaani se pahunch sakate hai. dilli ke kashmiri get bas adde se mevaat ke liye anek basen chalati hai. in bason dwaara aasaani se mevaat tak pahunchaa ja saktaa he.
