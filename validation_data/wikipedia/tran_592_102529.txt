 rabindra sethu bharat ke pashchim bengal main hughali nadi ke upar bana ek "canteliver sethu" he. yah haavda ko kolkata se jodta he. iskaa mul naam "nayaa haavda pul" tha jise badalakar 14 june san 1965 ko 'rabindra sethu' kar diya gaya. kintu ab bhi yah "haavda brij" ke naam se adhik janaa jaataa he. yah apane tarah kaa chhathvaan sabase bada pul he. saamanyatya prayek pul ke neeche khambhe hote he jin par vah tikaa rahata he parantu yah ek aisa pul he jo sirf chaar khambhon par tikaa he do nadi ke is taraf or poun kilometre kii chaudaai ke baad do nadi ke us taraf. sahare ke liye koi rasse aadi kii tarah koi taar aadi nahin. is duniya ke anokhe hajaaron ton bajani ispaat ke gardaron ke pul ne keval chaar khambhon par khud ko is tarah se balance banaakar hawa main tikaa rakhaa he ki 80 varshon se is par koi fark nahin pada he jabaki lakhon kii sankhya main din raat baari vaahan or paidal bheed isse guzarati he. angrejon ne jab is pul kii kalpana kii to ve aisa pul bananaa chaahate the ki neeche nadi kaa jal maarg na ruke. atah pul ke neeche koi khambha na ho. upar pul ban jaay or neeche hughali main pani ke jahaaj or naav bhi bina avrodh chalate rahen. ye ek jhula athwa canteliver pul se hi sambhav tha.
