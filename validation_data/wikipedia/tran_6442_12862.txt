 bharat ke rashtriya rajmarg, bharat kii kendriya sarkaar dwaara santhaapit or sambhaale jaani vaali lambii duuri kii sadake he. mukhyatah yah sadake 2 panktiyo kii he, prayek disha main jane ke liye ek pankti. bharat ke rajmargo kii kul duuri lagbhag 122434 kimi he, jisame se keval 4,885 kimi kii sadako ke madhya pakkaa vibhaajan banaaya gaya he. rajmargo kii lambaai bharat ke sadako kaa maatr 2% he, lekin yah kul yatayaat kaa lagbhag 40% bhaar uthaate he. 1995 main paas sansadeey videhak ke tahat in rajmargo ko banane or rakh-rakhaav ke liye nijee sansthano kii hissedari ko manjuuri di gai. haal ke samay main in rajmargo kaa teji se vikaas hua jinke tahat bharat ke shahar or kasbo ke bich yatayaat ke samay main giravat ai. kuch shahro ke bich 4 or 6 panktiyon ke rajmargo kaa bhi vikaas hua. bharat kaa sabase bada rajmarg rashtriya rajmarg 7 he, jo uttar pradesh ke varanasi shahar ko bharat ke dakshini kone, tamilnadu ke kanyakumari shahar ke saath jodta he. isaki lambaai 2369 kimi he. sabase chota rajmarg 5 kilometre lanbaa rashtriya rajmarg NH71B he,.kaafii saare rajmargon kaa abhi bhi vikaas ho rahaa he. jyadatar rajmargon ko kancreet kaa nahin banaaya gaya he. mumbai pune express-ve iskaa ek apwaad he.
