 yah lekh parloukik shakti ishwar ke vishay main he. ishwar film ke liye ishwar dekhen.yah lekh devataaon ke bare main nahin he.
 parameshwar vah sarvocch paraloukik shakti he jise is sansar kaa srashta or shaasha mana jaataa he. hindi main parameshwar ko bhagwaan, paramatma yaa parameshwar bhi kehete hai. adhiktar dharmon main parameshwar kii pariklpana brahmand kii sanrachana se judy hui he.sanskrit kii ish dhaatu kaa arth he- niyantrit karnaa or is par varach pratyay lagaakar yah shabd bana he. is prakaar mul rup main yah shabd niyantaa ke rup main prayukt hua he. isi dhaatu se samanaarthi shabd ish va isheita bane hai.
 parameshwar ek main teen he or saath hi saath teen main ek he -- parampita, ishvarputra isa messiah or pavitra aatma.
 wo ishwar ko allaah kehete hai. islam dharm kii dharmik pustak quran he or prayek musalmaan ishwar shakti main vishwavaas rakhata he.
 islam kaa mul mantra "lau ilaah ill, allaah, mohammad ur rasul allaah"  he,arthath allaah ke sivaa koi mabood nahi he or mohammad unke aakhri rasul hai.
 islam vaahid ek aisa dharm he jo satyata par aadhaarit he.islam dharm me baaki dharmo se zyaada pakizgi bataai gai he.pavitra kuran ke anusaar ek ishwar he or us ek ishwar kaa naam sirf allaah he.
 isliye agar koi puche ki ishwar ek he or vah koun he to quran ke hisaab se ek hi sateek jawaab miltaa he ki ishwar sirf or sirf allaah he jiske 99 naam hai.
 islam me musalamaano ko khadey khule main peshaab karne kii ijazat nahi kyonki isse insaan napak hota he or namaaz padhane ke laayak nahi rahata isliye islam me baithke peshaab karne ko kaha gaya he or uske baad pani se sharmegaah ko dhone kii ijazat di gayi he.
 islam me 5 vaqt kii namaaz muqarrar kii gai he or har namr farz he.islam me ramadan ek paak maheenaa he jo ki 30 dinon kaa hota he or 30 dinon tak rose rakhana jaays hajiski umr 12 yaa 12 se zyaada ho.12 se kam umr pe rose farz nahi.sehat kharaab kii haalat main bhi rose farz nahi lekin roze ke badale zakaat dena farz he.vaisaa shaks jo roza na rakh sake kisi bhi vajah se to usko uske badale garibo ko khaanaa khilaane or use paise dene yaa us garib kii jaays khwaish puraa karnaa laazmi he.
 veda ke anusaar vyakti ke bheetar purush ishwar hi he. parameshwar ek hi he. vaidic or paashchaatya maton main parameshwar kii avadharana main yah gaharaa antar he ki veda ke anusaar ishwar bheetar or pare donon he jabaki paashchaatya dharmon ke anusaar ishwar keval pare he. ishwar parabrahm kaa sagun rup he.
 vaishnav log vishnu ko hi ishwar maanate he, to shaiva shiv ko.
 yoga sutara main patanjali likhte he - "kleshkarmavipakashayaraparamrishtah purushavishesh ishwarh". vah vishesh purush he.) hindu dharm main yah ishwar kii ek maanya paribhaasha he.
 ishwar praani dwaara maani jane vaali ek kalpana he isame kuch log vishwaas karte he to kuch nahi.
 jain dharm main arihant or siddh hi bhagwaan he. jain darshan ke anusaar is sristi ko kisi ne nahin banaaya.
 naastik log or naastik darshan ishwar ko jhooth maanate hai. parantu ishwar he yaa nahin is par koi thos tark nahin de saktaa.
 bhartiya darshanon main "ishwar" ke vishay main kya kaha gaya he, vah nimnavat he-
 is darshan ke kuch tikaakaron ne ishwar kii satta kaa nishedh kiya he. unka tark he- ishwar chetan he, atah is jad jagat kaa kaaran nahin ho saktaa. punah ishwar kii satta kisi pramaan se siddh nahin ho sakati. yaa to ishwar swatantra or sarvashaktiman nahin he, yaa phir vah udaar or dayaalu nahin he, anyathaa dukh, shok, vaishamyadi se yukt is jagat ko kyon utpann karata? yadi ishwar karm-siddhanth se niyantrit he, to swatantra nahin he or karmasiddhanth ko na maanane par sristivaichitrya siddh nahin ho saktaa. purush or prakruti ke atirikt kisi ishwar kii kalpana karnaa yuktiyukt nahin he.
 haalaanki sankhya or yoga donon poorak darshan hai kintu yogadarshan ishwar kii satta sweekaar karata he. patanjali ne ishwar kaa lakshan bataaya he- "kleshkarmavipakarashayaraparamrishtah purushavisheshah ishwarh" arthath klesh, karm, vipaak or aashay se sarvatha asprisht purush-vishesh ishwar he. yah yoga-pratipaadit ishwar ek vishesh purush he; vah jagat kaa karta, dharta, sanharta, niyanta nahin he. asankhya nity purush tathaa nity achetan prakruti swatantra tattvon ke rup main ishwar ke saath-saath vidyaman hai. saakshaat rup main ishwar kaa prakruti se yaa purush ke bandhan or moksh se koi lenaa-dena nahin he.
 kanad krit vaisheshikasutron main ishwar kaa spashtollekh nahin hua he. "tadvachanadamnaysya pramanyam" arthath tadvachan hone se veda kaa pramanya he. is vaisheshikasutra main "tadvachan" kaa arth kuch vidwaanon ne "ishwarvachan" kiya he. kintu tadvachan kaa arth rishivachan bhi ho saktaa he. tathaapi prashastpad se lekar baad ke granthkaaron ne ishwar kii satta sweekaari he evam kuch ne uski siddhi ke liye pramaan bhi prastut kiye hai. inke anusaar ishwar nity, sarvajna or purna hai. ishwar achetan, adrusht ke sanchaalak hai. ishwar is jagat ke nimittkaaran or parmaanu upadankaaran hai. anek parmaanu or anek atmadravya nity evam swatantra dravyon ke rup main ishwar ke saath virajman hai; ishwar inako utpann nahin karte kyonki nity hone se ye utpatti-vinaash-rahit hai tathaa ishwar ke saath atmadravyon kaa bhi koi ghanishtha sambandh nahin he. ishwar kaa kaary, sarg ke samay, adrusht se gati lekar parmaanuon main aadyaspandan ke rup main sancharit kar dena; or pralay ke samay, is gati kaa avrodh karke waapas adrusht main sankramit kar dena he.
 naiyayik udaynacharya ne apni nyayakusumanjali main ishwar-siddhi hetu nimna yuktiyaan di hai-
  kaaryaat- yah jagat kaary he atah iskaa nimitt kaaran avashya hona chahie. jagat main saamanjasy evam samanvay iske chetan karta se aata he. atah sarvajna chetan ishwar is jagat ke nimitt kaaran evam praayojak karta hai.
  aayojanat- jad hone se parmaanuon main aadya spandan nahin ho saktaa or bina spandan ke parmaanu dvaynuk aadi nahin bana sakate. jad hone se adrusht bhi swayan parmaanuon main gatisanchaar nahin kar saktaa. atah parmaanuon main aadyaspandan kaa sanchaar karne ke liye tathaa unhen dvanukadi banane ke liye chetan ishwar kii aavashyaktaa he.
  dhrutyadeh - jis prakaar is jagat kii sristi ke liye chetan srishtikarta aavashyak he, usee prakaar is jagat ko dhaaran karne ke liye evam iskaa pralay main sanhaar karne ke liye chetan dharta evam sanharta kii aavashyaktaa he. or yah karta-dharta-sanharta ishwar he.
  padaat- padon main apane arthon ko abhivyakt karne kii shakti ishwar se aati he. "is pad se yah arth boddhavy he", yah ishwar-sanket pad-shakti he.
  sankhyavisheshat- naiyayikon ke anusaar dvaynuk kaa parinaam uske ghatak do anuon ke parimandalya se utpann nahin hota, apitu do anuon kii sankhya se utpann hota he. sankhya kaa pratyay chetan drashtaa se sambaddh he, sristi ke samay jeevatmayen jad dravya rup main sthit hai evam adrusht, parmaanu, kaal, dik, man aadi sab jad hai. atah do kii sankhya ke pratyay ke liye chetan ishwar kii satta aavashyak he.
  adrushtaata- adrusht jeevon ke shubhashubh karmsanskaron kaa aagaar he. ye sanchit sanskaar falonmukh hokar jeevon ko karmfal bhoga karaane ke prayojan se sristi ke hetu banate hai. kintu adrusht jad he, atah use sarvajna ishwar ke nirdesan tathaa sanchaalan kii aavashyaktaa he. atah adrusht ke sanchaalak ke rup main sarvajna ishwar kii satta siddh hoti he.
 vedantiyon ke anusaar ishwar kii satta tark se siddh nahin kii ja sakati. ishwar ke paksh main jitne prabal tark diye ja sakate hai utane hi prabal tark unke vipaksh main bhi diye ja sakate hai. tathaa, buddhi paksh-vipaksh ke tulya-bal tarkon se ishwar kii siddhi yaa asiddhi nahin kar sakati. vedantiyon ke anusaar ishwar keval shruti-pramaan se siddh hota he; anumaan kii gati ishwar tak nahin he.
