 2014 fifa vishwa cap 12 june 2014 se 13 july 2014 ke bich brazil main hua ek antarrashtriya purush footbal toornament he. 1950 ke baad, brazil is pratiyogita kii mezbaani duusari baar kar rahaa he. isi ke saath mexico, italy, france or germany ke baad brazil do baar vishwa cap kii mejabani karne vaala paanchvaan desh ban gaya he. saal 2014 kaa fifa vishwa cap germany ne jeeta he.
 battis deshon kii timen final toornament main bhaag liya. sabhi match brazil ke 12 vibhinn shaharon main match khele gaye. argentina main yojit hue 1978 vishwa cap ke baad se dakshin america main yojit hone vaala yah pehla vishwa cap he. isse pehle 2010 kaa toornament spain ne jeeta tha.
 13 july 2014 ko 2014 fifa vishwa cap ke final main germany ne argentina ko atirikt samay ke baad 1-0 se parajeet kiya, yah germany kaa chauthaa khitaab he.
 7 march 2003 ko fifa ne ghoshnaa kii ki 1978 ke baad dakshin america main pehali baar vishwa cap yojit kiya jayega. 3 june 2003 ko dakshin america footbal parisangh ne 2014 vishwa cap kii mejabani ke liye argentina, brazil or columbia ko pratyaashee ke rup main prastut kiya. 17 march 2004 ko, conamebol sanghon ne sarvasammati se brazil ke paksh main matdaan kiya.
 isi ke saath, 2014 vishwa cap kii mejabani ke liye brazil ekmaatr ummidavaar rah gaya. 30 oktober 2007 ko boli kii prakriya main usne toornament kii mejabani kaa adhikaar jeeta.
 nimnlikhit 32 tiimon ne final toornament ke liye aaharta praapt kii he. koshthak main toornament se purv kii ranking darshaayi gayi he.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 toornament ke liye kul inaam raashi $ 57,60,00,000 america dollar rakhi gayi he . yah 2010 kii inaam raashi se 37% adhik he. toornament se pehle, 32 tiimon main se prayek ko taiyaari karne liye $1.5 million america dollar kii raashi pradaan kii jaaegi. toornament main bhaag le rahi tiimon ke bich, puraskaar raashi is tarah vitarit kii jaaegi:
