 haara hachi bu ek japani kahawat or naaraa he jiskaa arth he "das main se aath bhaag bharna". yah japan ke kai kshetron main, visheshkar okinawa, main khaanaa khaane se sambandhit ek pratha he jiske anusaar kisi bhi vyakti ko bhojan karte hue keval utana hi khaanaa chahie jisamen use lage ke uskaa pet 80% bhar chukaa he. yani utana kabhi nahin khaanaa chahie ke puri tripti ho jaye. kaafii adhyayan ke baad paaya gaya he ke okinawa ke logon main motopa, madhumeh or inase sambandhit hriday rog lagbhag na ke baraabar dekhe jate hai. okinawa ke 29% log 100 saal kii umr se zyaada jeeten hai jo pashchimi deshon kaa chaar guna he. is swasthya kaa shrey "haara hachi bu" or okinawa kii anya sehat surakshit rakhane vaali prathaon ko diya jaataa he.
 sharir main bhukh lagane kii prakriya ko dekhte hue vaigyanikon ne paaya he ke khaanaa khate samay pet bhar jane par mastishk ko 20 minute pataa chalata he. agar khaanaa tripti hone tak khaaya jaae to darasal vyakti zaroorat se lagbhag 20% adhik khaanaa kha letaa he. is se pet phool jaataa he or baaripan mahasoos hota he. pet kaa akar phoolne se agali dafaa vyakti or bhi adhik khaanaa khaataa he. lambe arase tak aisa chalane se vyakti niyamit rup se apane sharir kii zaroorat se zyaada aahaar khaataa he jo motaa hone kaa mukhya kaaran he. "haara hachi bu" kaa niyam paalan karne se is dushchakr se bachaa ja saktaa he.
