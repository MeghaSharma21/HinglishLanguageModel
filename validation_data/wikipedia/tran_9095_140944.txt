 modhera surya mandir gujarat ke patan naamak sthaan se 30 kilometre dakshin kii ore “modhera” naamak gaanv main pratishthit he. yah surya mandir bharatvarsh main vilakshan sthapaty evam shilp kala kaa bejod udhaaharan he. san 1026 i. main solanki vansh ke raja bhimdev pratham dwaara is mandir kaa nirmaan kiya gaya tha. vartmaan samay main is mandir main puja karnaa nishedh he. kaha jaataa he ki allaudin khilji ne mandir ko tod kar khandit kar diya tha.
