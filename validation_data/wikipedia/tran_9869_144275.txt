 sar richard hadlee newzealand ke purv cricket khilaadi hai jinhen cricket ke itihaas main mahanatam tej gendbaaj or alrounder main se ek mana jaataa he. 1973 se 1990 tak chale apane kariyar main wo newjiland cricket team ke liye amuly stambh the. wo apane samay ke chaar mahaan alroundaron main se ek the . test jeeton main 150 wicket lene vaale main unki gendbaaji ausat sabase kam he.
 richard ne 86 test main 22.29 ki ausat se 431 wicket liye. jisamen 36 baar pari main paanch wicket or 9 baar match main 10 wicket liye. unke paas lagbhag 5 saal tak test main sabase adhik wicket lene kaa kirtiman rahaa. unhone 2 shatak ke saath 3,124 ran bhi banaae. 115 ek divasiy antarrashtriya main unhone 1,751 ran banaae or 21.56 kii ausat se 158 wicket liye.
