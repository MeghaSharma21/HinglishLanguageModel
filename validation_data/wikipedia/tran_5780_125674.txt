 2011 dilli bam visphot 7 sitambar 2011 ko bharat kii rajdhani dilli main ucch nyaayalay ke baahar hua tha.
 bam ucch nyaayalay ke gaate sankhya-5 ke baahar ek brifces main rakhaa gaya tha. visphot sthaneey samay ke anusaar subah 10:15 par hua tha. is visphot se 11 logon kii mrityu hui or kam-se-kam 50 log ghayal hue.
 dilli police ne dhamaake ke din hi do sandigdh vyaktiyon ke sketch jari kiye jinpar bam lagane kaa andeh tha. unamem se ek kii aayu 50 se 60 varsh ke bich thi or duusare kii 20-30 varsh ke bich.
