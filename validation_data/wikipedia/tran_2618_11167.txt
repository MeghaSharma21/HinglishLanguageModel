 oria, oriya yaa oriya bharat ke oreisha praant main boli jane vaali bhashaa he. yah yahan ke rajya sarkaar kii rajbhasha bhi he. bhashaai parivaar ke taur par oria ek arya bhashaa he or nepali, bangla, asamiya or maithili se iskaa nikat sambandh he. orissa kii bhashaa or jati donon hi artho main oriya shabd kaa prayog hota he, kintu vastav main thik rup "oriya" hona chahie. isaki vyutpatti kaa vikascram kuch vidvan is prakaar maanate hai : odravishay, odravish, odish, aadisha yaa oreisha. sabase pehle bharat ke naatyashaastra main udravibhasha kaa ullekh miltaa he:
 "shabrabhirchandal sachaldraidodraja:. hina vanecharana c vibhaasha naatake smruta:."
 bhashatathvik drushti se oriya bhashaa main arya, dravid or mundaari bhashaaon ke sanmishrit roopon kaa pataa chalata he, kintu aaj kii oriya bhashaa kaa mukhya aadhaar bhartiya aryabhasha he. saath hi saath isamen santhali, mundaari, shabari, aadi mundaari varg kii bhashaaon ke or auraanv, kui telegu aadi dravid varg kii bhashaaon ke lakshan bhi paae jate hai.
 isaki lipi kaa vikaas bhi nagari lipi ke samaan hi brahmi lipi se hua he. antar keval itnaa he ki nagari lipi kii upar kii sidhi rekha oriya lipi main vartual ho jaati he or lipi ke mukhya ansh kii apeksha adhik jagah gher leti he. vidwaanon kaa kahana he ki oriya main pehle taalptr par louh lekhnee se likhane kii reeti prachlit thi or sidhi rekha khinchne main taalptr ke cat jane kaa dar tha. at: sidhi rekha ke badale vartual rekha di jane lagi or oriya lipi kaa kramash: aadhunik rup aane laga.
 sampoorn oria bhashaa ke itihaas ko nimna virgon main baanta ja saktaa he - 
 oria ek purvi indo-arya bhashaa hone ke naate indo-arya bhashaa parivaar kaa sadsy he. ise purv magadhi naamak praakrit bhashaa kaa vanshdhar mana jaataa jise ki 1500 saal pehle purv bharat main prayog kiya jaataa tha. is kaa aadhunik bangla, maithili, nepali evam asamiya bhashaaon se nikat sambandh he. anya uttar bhartiya bhashaaon ke mukaable main uriya, parsi bhashaa dwaara sabase kam prabhavit hua he. likhit oria bhashaa kaa pracheenatam udhaaharan madalaa panji main miltaa he jinhe tad ke patton par likhaa gaya tha.
 oria bhashaa ke pratham mahaan kavi jhankad ke sarala das the jinhone devi durga kii stuti main chandi puraana or vilanka ramayana kii rachana kii thi. arjuna das ke dwaara likhit ram-vivaah oria bhashaa kii pratham deergh kavita he. praarambhik kaal ke baad se lagbhag 1700 tak ke samay ko oria sahitya main panchasakha yug ke naam se janaa jaataa he. is yug kaa prarambh shrichaitanya ke vaishnb dharm ke prachaar se hua. balram das, jagannatha das, yashovant das, anant das evam achyutanand das-in paanchon ko panchasakha kaha jaataa he. is yug ke anya saahitiyikon kii tarah inaki rachanaaen bhi dharm par aadhaarit thim. is kaal ke rachanaaen praayah sanskrit se anuvaadit kii hui hoti thim yaa unhi par aadhaarit hoti thim. anuwaadon main aaksharik ke apeksha bhavanuaad kaa prachlan zyada tha.
