 ilamkulam manakkal shankaran namboodripad bharat ke prasiddh communist netaa, samaajawaadee marxvaadi vichaarak, krantikari, lekhak, itihaaskaar or saamaajik tikaakar the. nambudripad ne 1957 main kerala main pehali communist sarkaar kaa gathan kiya. 
 iemes namboodripad kaa janm 14 july 1909 main kerala ke palghat jile main ek pratishthit brahman parivaar main hua tha. inke pita kaa naam parameshwaran namboodripad tha.
