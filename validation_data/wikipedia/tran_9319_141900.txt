 steven anthoni "steve" balmar janvari 2000 se microsoft corporation ke mukhya kaaryakari adhikari rahe hai. 2010 ke anusaar, ek anumaan ke anusaar vah 14.5 arab dollar kii nijee sampatti ke saath 2010 ke duniya main ek sabase amir logon main se ek hai.
 swiss america pita or yahudee mul kii america maa jinka parivaar purvi europe ke ek shahar pinsk se aayaa tha, ke parivaar main balmar kaa janm datroit, michigan main hua. vah farmington hills, michigan main paley-bade. 1973 main, unhone bevarli hills, michigan ke ek nijee college priperetary skool datroit country de skool se snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii or ab ve usee kii board of director kii kursi par baithte hai. 1977 main, unhone ucch distinction ke saath harvard vishvavidhyalayase {2}ganit{/2} or {3}arthshaastra{/3}main snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii. college main rahete hue, balmar ne footbal team kaa prabandhan, akhbaar harvardecrimson tathaa saath hi harvard edwocket main kaam kiya or dwitiya varsh ke saathi bill gates ke saath haul main rahete the. baad main unhone ho varsh tak proctor and gambal main sahaayak utpaada prabandhak ke rup main kaarmay kiya, jahaan jeffri aar helmit unke sahyogi the, jo baad main general electric ke siio ban gaye. 1980 main, unhone microsoft se judane ke liye stenford university graceuate skool of bijnes chhod diya. 
 balmar 11 june 1980 ko microsoft se jud gaye or gates dwaara niyukt kiye gaye pratham vyapaar prabandhak ke rup menmicrosoft ke 24 wein karmchaaree ban gaye. balmar ko microsoft main kaam karnaa bahut acha lagata tha. shuruyaat main unhen 50,000 dollar vetan ke ke saath company ke kuch pratishat swaamitv kii peshkash kii gai thi. 1981 main jab microsoft ko nigamit kiya gaya, tab balmar company ke paas 8 pratishat kaa swaamitv tha. unhone "oparating system vikaas", "parichaalan" or "bikri or samarthan" ke saath microsoft kii kai divijanon netritv kiya he. janvari 2000 main, unhen aadhikarik taur par pramukh kaaryakari adhikari banaaya gaya. siio ke rup main balmar ne company kaa vitt prabandhan sambhala, haalanki gates ne tabhi bhi "takneekee vijan" par apana niyantran banaae rakhaa. 2003 main balmar ne apane paas company kaa 4% steke rakhte hue 8.3% shares bech die. usee varsh, balmar ne microsoft ke karmchaaree stock vikalp program ko badal diya.
 2009 main, bill gates ke adhyaksh ke rup main microsoft chhodane ke baad unhenne itihaas main pehali baar siies main mukhya vaktaa ke rup main bhaashan diya.
 balmar unke sanakee or asaadhaaran vyavhaar ke liye jane jate hai. udhaaharan ke liye, microsoft ke kaaryakramon main balmar bhadkeeli upasthiti ke video internet par viral video ke rup main vyapak maatra main prachlit kiye gaye hai. inamen se sabase prasiddh video saamanytah "steve balmar going krezi" yaa "dance manki boy" naam se hai. developers ke sammelan main liye gaye ek anya video main paseene main bhiige hue balmar "developers" shabd kaa jaap karte hue dikhaai dete hai.
 wal street jarnal kii report ke anusaar bill gates se balmar ko praadhikaar ke hastantaran ke dauraan san 2000 ke aasapaas ve tanaav main the. baat itani bighad gai thi ki ek meetim main, us meeting main upasthit ek vyakti ke anusaar, gates ne bahut tanatani ke baad jhunjhalaate hue hangaamaa khadaa kiya, jisamen balmar kai saathiyon ke bachaane ke liye bich main kud pade. us vyakti ne kaha ki, parivartan ke baad, balmar ko "pachhtaawaa" ho rahaa tha.
 balmar ne kaha ek baar gates ke chhodane ke baad, "mujhe kisi bhi baat ke liye unki koi jarurat nahi padegi, yahi siddhant he. haa use istemaal karo par uski jarurat nahin he."
