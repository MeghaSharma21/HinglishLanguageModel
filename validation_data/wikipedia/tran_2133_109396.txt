 mrittika ek vyapak shabd he. praayah 2 micron se chote vyas vaale kanon vaale avsaadee shailon ko 'mrittika' kehete hai. yah mukhyatah almunium ke silicket evam anya bahusilicket kaa mishran he. mrittika jin mukhya khanijon se bani hoti he unhen 'mrittika khanij' kehete hai.
 bhootkneekee drushti se mrittika un mrittika khanijon ko kehete hai jinke kanon kaa aakaar 4 micron se chota hota he. 
 mrittika mukhyatah bhure rang kii hoti he kintu anya rangon kii bhi mrittika paayi jaati he. yah nadiyon ke kinaaron par dekhne ko mil sakati he.
