 shashaank vyas ek bhartiya film abhinayta hai. inkaa janm 30 navambar 1986 main ujjain, madhya pradesh main hua tha. yah balika vadhoo main jagadish bhairon singh naam ke kirdaar ko nibhaa kar apane abhinay kii shuruyaat kii. iske baad rishtadot com naam ke ek dharawahik main bhi kaary kiya. varsh 2010 main bollywood film tees maar khan main bhi najar aae. yah inaki pehali film bhi he.
