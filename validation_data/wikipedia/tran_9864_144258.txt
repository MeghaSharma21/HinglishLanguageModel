 nitrogen, bhuyati yaa natrajan ek rasinic tatv he jiskaa pratik N he. iskaa parmaanu kramank 7 he. saamaanya taap or daab par yah gas he tathaa prithvi ke vayumandal kaa lagbhag 78% nitrogen hi he. yah sarvaadhik maatra main tatv ke rup main uplabbdh padaarth bhi he. yah ek rangahin, gandhhin, swadaheen or praayah akriy gas he. isaki khoj 1772 main scotland ke vaigyanik danniel radarford ne kii thi.
 aavart sarini ke 15 wein samooh kaa pratham tatv he. nitrogen kaa rasayan atyant manoranjak vishay he, kyonki samast jaev padaarthon main is tatv kaa aavashyak sthaan he. iske do sthaayee samasthanik, dravyaman sankhya 14, 15 gyaat hai tathaa teen asthaayee samasthanik bhi banaae gaye hai.
 nitrogen tatv kii pahchaan sarvapratham 1772 i. main radarford or shele ne swatantra rup se kii. shele ne usee varsh yah sthapit kiya ki vaayu main mukhyat: do gaisen upasthit hai, jisamen ek sakriy tathaa duusari niskriy he. tabhi prasiddh francisi vaigyanik lavvazye ne nitrogen gas ko oxigen se alag kar iskaa naam 'ajot' rakhaa. 1790 main shaptaal ne ise nitrogen naam diya.
 vayumandal main aaytan ke anusaar nitrogen 78 pratishat maatra main asamyukt avasthaa main upasthit he. prati varsh vidyut visarjan tathaa jeevanusakriyata dwaara kuch nitrogen sanyukt avasthaa main vayumandal se prithvi par aata he. iske mukhya yogic sanyukt avasthaa main vayumandal se prithvi par aata he. iske mukhya yogic shora tathaa chile kaa salt peter prithvi main paae jate hai. proteinon main yah jatil carbonic padaarthon ke rup main sadaiv upasthit rahata he.
 prayogshaala main nitrogen ammonium nitrite par taap ke prabhaav se mukt kiya jaataa he. ammonium nitrite asthaayee padaarth he. is kaaran ammonium chloride evam sodium nitrite ke mishrit vilyan kaa upyog is kaary ke liye karte hai.
 nitrogen ko vayumandal se bhi taiyaar kar sakate hai, jisamen oxigen ko rasinic kriya athwa bhautik sadhano dwaara alag karnaa hota he. yadi vaayu ko pyrogalic amla ke kshareey vilyan athwa amleey kromas chloride,, yaa veneds sulphate, ke vilyan main se pravaahit karen to oxigen rasinic kriya se alag ho jaataa he. bachi gas nitrogen hogi, jisamen sookshm maatra main kuch anay niskriy gaisen bhi mili hongi. audyogic kaaryon ke liye aajkal drav vayu ke prabhaji aswan dwaara nitrogen praapt kiya jaataa he. iskaa kvathanank oxigen se nicha he. is kaaran iskaa oxigen pehle vaashp banakar nikal jaataa he or avshesh drav main pradhanthya nitrogen rahata he.
 nitrogen rangahin, gandhhin or swadaheen gas he. iskaa anu do parmaanuon kaa banataa he. iskaa sanket naa, parmaanu sankhya 7, parmaanu bhaar 14.007, galnaank - 210° sein., kvathanank - 195.8° sein., ghanatv 1.25 gram prati litre, kranthik taap - 147° sein., 0° sein. tathaa saamaanya dabaav par jal main vileyata 23.5 ghan senmi. prati litre he.
 rasinic drushti se nitrogen niskriy tatv he. saadhaaran taap par na to yah jalataa he or na anya dhaatuon se yogic banaata he. ucch taap par yah anek tatwon se kriya kar nitrid padaarth banaata he. nitrogen evam oxigen gas mishran main vidyut sphuling utpann karne par nitric oxide yogic banataa he.
 nune dabaav kii nitrogen gas par ucch tanaav vidyudvisarjan karne par nitrogen kii ek sakriy kism banati he, jise sakriy nitrogen kehete hai. yah gas gandhak, phosphorus tathaa anek tatwon evam yogicon ke saath abhikriya karti he. visarjan nalika main yah pile rang kii chamak utpann karti he.
 habar vidhi se ammonia banane ke liye nitrogen gas kaa bahut upyog hota he. iske atirikt anya nitrogen yogikeekaran prakram main iskaa upyog haata he. iske yogic vishaal maatra main urvarak ke rup main kaam aate hai. nitrogen ke anek yogic visphotak tathaa aushadhiyaan hai.
 anek sthanon main nitrogen kaa upyog niskriy vaataavaran banane main hota he. bijli ke balbon main nitrogen bharne se unki jivan avadhi bad jaati he.
 jaev padaarthon ke liye nitrogen chakra bahut aavashyak kriya he. vanaspati evam jaev padaarthon kaa nitrogen khaad ke rup main manushyon tathaa pashuon ke kaam aata he. vanaspatiyon main saamaanya acarbonic yogicon se yukt nitrogen padaarth banate hai. mitti main asankhya jeevanu vaayu ke mukt nitrogen ko jatil carbonic padaarth main parinat karte rahete hai. kuch vishesh prakaar ke shimbi paudhe nitrogen kaa sidhe yogikeekaran kar, upyogi nitrogen yogic banane kii kshamta rakhte he. inke atirikt, vayumandal main vidyut visarjan dwaara bana nitric oxide varsha ke saath aakar bhoomi main nitrogen kii maatra badhaata he. bhoomi se paudhe apni jad dwaara nitrogen padaarthon kaa avasorana karte hai. in sab kriyaaon dwaara vaayu kaa mukt nitrogen vanaspati evam jeeven ke kaam aata he. dalhani fasalon kii jadon main paye jane vaale rijobium naamak jeevanu vaayu nitrogen kaa mrudaa main sthirikaran karte he jise mrudaa nitrogenikaran kehete he.
 inke atirikt anya prakaar ke jeevanu jatil nitrogen yogicon kaa vighatan kar saral yogic banaate rahete hai or is vidhi se samuchit maatra main nitrogen mukt hokar vaayu main chalaa jaataa he. is prakaar prakruti main nitrogen chakra chalata rahata he.
 hydrogen ke saath nitrogen ke teen yogic banate hai : ammonia, hydrazin or hydrezoic amla . ammonia tathaa hydrazin main kshar gun vartmaan hai or hydrezoic amla main amleey gun hote hai. ammonia saadhaaran taap par gas or hydrazin drav he. hydrazin asthaayee padaarth he, jo garam karne par sheeghra vighatit ho nitrogen or ammonia banaata he. yah amlon se kriya kar do shrenee ke lavan banaata he . hydrazin or uske lavan vifaille hote hai. hydrezoic amla tikshn gandh denevaala padaarth he . yah jaharila tathaa visphotak gun vaala he. yah dhaatuon se lavan banaata he, jinhen azid kehete hai jaise lithium azid, silvar azid .
 nitrogen ke paanch sthir oxide gyaat hai : nitras oxide, nitric oxide, dinitrogen trioxide, nitrogen dioxide or dinitrogen pentaoxide . nitras oxide ko hansaane vaali gas bhi kehete hai, kyonki ise soonghane par kuch der miragi jaisaa anubhav hota he. ammonium nitrate garam karne se yah banataa he :
 ammonium nitrate = nitras oxide + jal
 nitrogen hellojan tatwon ke saath anek yogic banaata he. nitrogen trifloride rangahin gas he, jo ammonium hydrogen fluoride ke vidyudvishleshan dwaara praapt hoti he. nitrogen chloride ammonia par chlorine kii abhikriya dwaara praapt vah pile rang kaa visphotak tel padaarth he, jo thodaa garam karne par, yaa teevr prakaash se, bhayankar visphot dwaara vighatit ho jaataa he. ammonia vilyan par iodine kii kriya se ek kaala padaarth banaata he, jise nitrogen iodide kaa sanyukt yogic, he.
 asamyukt nitrogen ko yogic rup main parivartit karne kii kriya ko nitrogen yogikeekaran kehete hai. nitrogen ke yogicon kii upyogita ke kaaran unka bahut maatra main vyay hota he. bhoomi kii paidaavaar badhaane ke liye apaar maatra main khaad evam urvarak kaa upyog hota he. prithvi par nitrogen yogicon kii maatra simit he, parantu vayumandal is tatv kaa athaah strot he. is kaaran vaayu ke nitrogen dwaara nitrogen yogic banane kaa praytn bahut kaal se hota aayaa he.
 prakruti main nitrogen yogikeekaran kaary anek sadhano dwaara hota he. bhoomi ke anek jeevanu, jaise ezotobacter tathaa kuch paudhon kii jadon main sthit jeevanu, jaise riceovium, is kaary ko sadaiv karte rahete hai.
 nitrogen yogikeekaran ke anek prakram gyaat he. inamen se ek main nitrogen tathaa oxigen kii abhikriya dwaara nitric oxide banaate hai. isamen ooshmaashoshi kriya hone ke kaaran atyadhik urja kii aavashyaktaa hoti he.
 is kriya par aadhaarit ek prakram ko varckland oid prakram kehete hai. isamen vidyucchap kaa chumbakeey kshetra dwaara failaakar nitrogen oxigen sanmishran ko pravaahit karte hai. adhik vidyudurja ke vyay or utpaada kii nune prapti ke kaaran yah prakram vyapaar main safal na ho sakaa.
 duusare prakram dwaara calcium carbide ko nitrogen ke vaataavaran main 1,000rou sein. taap tak garam karte the, jisase calcium sinamide ban jaataa tha :
 calcium carbide + nitrogen = calcium sinamide + carbon
 calcium sinamide kaa urvarak ke rup main upyog ho saktaa he. 1907 i. se 1920 i. tak anek rashtron main iske audyogic kaarkaane bane hai, parantu khoj dwaara anya sulabh reeti ke praapt hone se ab iskaa upyog band ho gaya.
 vibhinn nitrogen yogikeekaran kii vidhiyon main habar prakram dwaara ammonia nirmaan karne kii vidhi sabase uttam he. iske dwaara vishwa ke anek deshon main ammonia or usase anya nitrogen yogic ban rahe hai. in vidhi main nitrogen or hydrogen ke mishran ko 450° se 500° sein. ke taap par 100 - 1,000 vayumandal dabaav par utprerak ke madhya pravaahit karte hai. is avasthaa main nitrogen or hydrogen abhikriya kar ammonia banaate hai :
 nitrogen + hydrogen = ammonia + 24,000 calorie urja
 praapt ammonia dwaara ammonium lavan banaaya ja saktaa he, athwa ostwald vidhi dwaara uskaa oxikaran kar nitric amla, yaa nitrate lavan, bhi banaae jate hai.
