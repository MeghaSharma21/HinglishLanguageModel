 purvottar parvatiy vishvavidhyalay bharat kaa ek kendriya vishvavidhyalay he. vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana sansadeey adhinium ke tahat kii gai jise 19 july 1973 ko adhishuchet kiya gaya. vishvavidhyalay kaa mukhya karyaalay evam sthai parisar shilong he tathaa doosraa parisar tura main sthit he. vishvavidhyalay ke antargat 8 adhyayan sankaay hai, jinke antargat 34 vibhaag evam 2 adhyayan centre hai. 66 mahavidhyalay bhi iske antargat angeebhoot kiye gayi hai.
