 keshavram kashiram shastri vishwa hindu parishad ke vah sansthaapak sadsy the. ve ek jaanmane sahityakar or itihaaskaar the. mahamaharshi pus. ke.kaa. shastri ko "bharatmartand", "vidyavachaspati", "padmashri", "mahamahopadhyaay", "bhashaa bhaskar" jaisi kitni hi padaviyaan praapt hui thim. gujarati sahitya jagat kaa utkrisht "ranjaitram suvarnachandrak" puraskaar or 42 se adhik sammaan unako mili the. shri shastri kirtankaar, sahityakar, pravachankaar, puratatvvid, itihaaskaar, dharmprachaarak, samaaj sudhaarak, tatvachintak, hindutv ke prati prakhar shraddha bhav rakhane vaale rishi the. wein praadhyaapak, vidwaan, anusandhanatmak kosh-rachayita, charitra lekhak, nottlekhak, sampadak, anuvadak, bhashashasthri, sahityakar, pravachankaar, puratatvavid, dharmprachaarak, samaaj sudhaarak or tatvachintak bhi hai.
 vikram sanwat 1961 asadha 11 shuukravaar ko janme vidyavachaspati pra. shastri kaa 102 varsh main bhi ek yuva kii bhaanti atyant chapaltaa se, chusti se apana sara din kaamkaaj main vyateet karte the. saamanyatya koi bhi vyakti 75-80 kii aayu main apni shaareerik maryaadaaon ke kaaran kaamkaaj se nivrutt hokar ghar main samay vyateet karata he, lekin shastri ji se jab yah poocha gaya tha ki aapaki 101 varsh kii aayu main bhi itani sphurti kaa rahasy kya he, to unhone kaha tha -"kam kha or gam kha yani kam khaanaa or koi bhala-bura kahe to main hans detaa hum. koi bhi buree baat yaa ghatana main apane man par haaavee nahin hone detaa or shisht tathaa samaybaddh jivan-yahi mere swasthya kaa rahasy he."
 shri shastri subah paanch-saadhe paanch baje uthakar nity kriya samaapt kar subah paune aath baje apane ghar se lagbhag dedh kilometre dur vallabhacharya ji ke mandir darshan karne jate the. yahaan par ek ghante tak bhajan-kirthan karte the. ghar aakar snaan-bhojan kar baarah baje likhnaa, padhanaa-sanshodhan kaa kaary puraa karte the, jo shaam paanch baje tak jari rahata tha. 
 shri shastri vishwa hindu parishad, gujarat ke pradesh adhyaksh the. is naate vah niyamit rup se har roj shaam paanch se saat baje tak vishwa hindu parishad ke karyaalay main baithte the. saat baje ghar aakar pun: likhnaa-padhanaa aarambh kar dete the. kisi bhi haal main, chaahe koi bhi vyakti milane kyon na aae, vah raat das baje so jate the. shri shastri ne 172 se adhik pustaken likhi hai. inamen dharm, samaaj, samskruti, naatak, vyakaran, sanshodhanatmak aadi vishyon par unke pragadh chintan kii jhalak milti he. shri shastri shikshak, sanshodhak, vyavasthaapak, sampadak, lekhak, maardarshak, vyakaran shastri, sanskrit ke prakand pandit, naat shastri or maanya bhashaa shastri the. yah ascharyajanak baat he ki anek vishyon ke gyaata shri shastri kaa adhyayan sirf madhyamik shiksha tak hi hua tha. iske baavjood vah piech.di. kii maanad oopaadhi praapt kar chuke the. unki pratibha dekhakar gujarat vidya sabha ne 1939 main unako em.e. ke chaatro ko padhaane kaa kaary saunpa tha. mumbai vishvavidhyalay ne 1994 main tathaa gujarat vishvavidhyalay ne 1951 main shastri ko anusnaatak ke praadhyaapak tathaa 1955 main piech.di. ke maardarshak kii manyata di thi. shri shastri ne yah sab apane karmyog ke aadhaar par praapt kiya tha. unhone padmashri se lekar vidyavachaspati tak ke anek sammaan praapt kiye the. padhai bich main chhodane ke bare main puche gaye ek sawaal ke jawaab main shastri ne kaha tha, "1922 main madhyamik pariksha uteern kii. uske turant baad mahatma gandhi ne angrejon ke viruddh abhiyaan chhedaa tha. angreji adhyayan ke prati nafrat paidaa ho gai, is kaaran mahavidhyalay main jane kaa man hi nahin hua. haa, prarambh se hi mujhe sanskrit se gaharaa lagaav tha." hindutv ko lekar utpann vartmaan sthiti ke bare main shri shastri kaa spasht kahana tha -"jab aap kisi cheez par zyada dabaav daalenge to uski pratikriya hona swaabhaavik he. hindu samaaj ab anyaay-apmaan sahane vaala nahin he. kailas yatri ko ek paisa na do or hajayatri ko arabon kii khayraat baanto, yah baat ab nahin chalegi. hindu samaaj ab jaagrath ho chukaa he. maine itani jagrati kabhi nahin dekhi. haa, abhi or tivrata se kaam karne kii jarurat he. rashtriya swayamsevak sangh kii taraf samaaj aashaa bhari najaron se dekh rahaa he." aise ke.kaa. shastri jivan ke antim kshan tak hindu dharm, hindu samskruti or hindu samaaj kii seva karte rahe. unki vishwa hindu parishad ke prati gahari aasthaa thi. shashad isiilie unhone apane antim patr main likhaa tha -"meri mrityu ke baad mere sharir ko shmashaan le jane se purv vishwa hindu parishad karyaalay main le jaaya jaay."
