 saamaajik raajnneetik darshan main marxvad utpaadan ke sadhano par saamaajik swaamitv dwaara vargvihin samaaj kii sthapana ke sankalp kii samyavaadee vichaardhaara he. multah marxvad un aarthik raajnneetik or aarthik siddhanto kaa samucchy he jinhen unnisvin-beesvin sadi main karl marks, fredrick angels or vladimir lenin tathaa saathi vichaarkon ne samaajwaad ke vaigyanik aadhaar kii pushti ke liye prastut kiya.
 saamaajik gathan kii aitihaasik vyakhya karne vaala marks kaa yah siddhant hegel ke dwandvvaadi paddati kii aalochana karata he. puunji bhaag 1 kii bhoomika likhte hue marks ne dwandvvaad or hegel ke prati apana drushtikon vyakt kiya he- "hegel ke liye maanav mastik kii jivan prakriya, arthath chintan kii prakriya, jise 'vichaar' ke naam se usne ek swatantra karta tak bana daalaa he, vaastavik sansar kii srijankatri he or vaastavik sansar 'vichaar' kaa bahari indriyagamy rup maatr he. iske vipareet, mere liye vichaar iske sivaa or kuch nahin ki bhautik sansar maanav mastishk main pratibimbit hota he or chintan ke roopon main badal jaataa he. hegel ke haathon main dwandvvaad par rahasy kaa aavaran pad jaataa he, lekin iske baavjood sahi he ki hegel ne hi sabase pehle vistrut or sachet dhang se yah bataaya tha ki apane saamaanya rup main dwandvvaad kis prakaar kaam karata he. hegel ke yahan dwandvvaad sir ke bal khadaa he. yadi aap uske rahasyamay aavaran ke bheetar chhipe tarkbuddhiparak saratatva kaa pataa lagaana chaahate hai, to aapako use ulatakar phir pairon ke bal sidhaa khadaa karnaa hoga."
 marxvad ke antargat samaajik sanrachana kii aarthik vyakhya karne vaala yah pramukh siddhant he.
 marxvad maanav sabhyataa or samaaj ko hameshaa se do virgon -shoshak or shoshit- main vibhajit maanata he. mana jaataa he saadhan sampann varg ne hameshaa se utpaadan ke sansadhano par apana adhikaar rakhane kii koshish kii tathaa burjua vichaardhaara kii aad main ek varg ko lagaataar vanchit banaakar rakhaa. shoshit varg ko is shadyantra kaa bhaan hote hi varg sangharsh kii zamin taiyaar ho jaati he. vargaheen samaaj kii sthapana ke liye varg sangharsh ek anivaary or nivaranatmak prakriya he.
