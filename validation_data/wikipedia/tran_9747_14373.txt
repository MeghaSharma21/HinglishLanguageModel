 slovakia europe mahadweep me sthit ek desh he. checkoslovakia se alag hone ke baad is ganraajya kaa nirmaan hua tha. yahan kii rajdhani bratislava he.
 puratathvik avasheshon ke adhyayan se yah pataa laga he ki 1000 isa purv se pehle slovakia main banjaare rahete the jo ek jagah se duusari jagah jate rahete the. tatpashchaat ilirian or celt jati ke log yahan sthaayee rup se based. lagbhag 100 isa purv main german senaaon ne in jatiyon ko yahan se khadeda or swayan yahan bas gaye. roman samrajya ne is ilaake par kabza karne kii koshish kii lekin german jatiyon ko haarane main asafal rahe. chhathi shatabdi tak slav log yahan bas chuke the.
 san 833 main slovakia ke kshetra par moravian samrajya ne kabza kar liya tha. san 863 main isai mishanri desh ke logon kaa dharm-parivartan karne main safal rahe, jo aaj tak kaayam he.
 san 907 main moravian samrajya ke bikhar gaya or san 1018 main slovakia hungarian samrajya kaa bhaag ban gaya. is samay main kshetra kii aarthik unnati hui or kshetra main khadaanon se sona, chaandi, evam tamba nikaala gaya. parantu san 1237 main purv se tartar logon ne hamalaa kar diya or arthvyavastha isse dub gayi. in hamalon se bachaav ke liye samrajya ne german logon ko slovakia ke kam jansankhya ghanatv vaale kshetron main basane ke liye bheja. isse german logon kaa yahan aana shuru hua jiske natijatan kai khanan vaale shaharon main german log pramukh janajaatiy samooh ban gaye.
 chaudavin or solahavin shatabdi main hangeirian samrajya ke viruddh vidroh hue. iske baad turkiyon ne hungary par kabza kar liya, parantu 1686 main hungarian samrajya ne unhen haraa diya or slovakia par phir hungarian samrajya kaa prabhutv tha.
 slovakia purvi europe main sthit he. yah sabhi taraf se zamin se ghira he . iskaa kul kshetrafal 49,035 varg kilometre he. iske uttar main poland he, dakshin main hungary, purv main ukraine or pashchim main check ganraajya evam austria. danyube nadi raashtra kii rajdhani bratislava se guzarati he or hungary ke saath slovakia kii seemaa kaa kaam karti he. desh kaa lagbhag 30% ilaaka pahari he.
 2008 ke anusaar, slovakia kii jansankhya 54,12,254 he or jansankhya ghanatv he 110 vyakti prati varg kilometre. 45% jansankhya 5,000 se kam jansankhya vaale gaanvon main rahati he. sakshartaa dar 99.6% he or ausat aayu 38.3 varsh. desh main 51.41% striyaan hai or 48.59% purush. raashtra ke 86% log slowe hai, 10% hungarian, 1.7% roma, 1.4% check, 0.3% rusin, 0.1% german, 0.1% polish, 0.1% ukreniai or 0.07% moravian.
 slovakia main 69% log roman catholic hai, 10% protestant, 4% unani catholic or 0.9% kattarwadi isai . slovakia main 4,000 se bhi kam yahudee hai.
 slovakia ek sansadeey ganatantr he. iske rashtriya parishad main 150 sadsy hote hai jinhen har 4 saal main aam chunaav dwaara chunaa jaataa he. san 2002 tak saansad raashtrapati kaa chunaav kiya karte the or raashtrapati banane ke liye ummidavaar ko kam-se-kam 90 saansadon ke mat hasil karne hote the. 2002 main slovakia ke samvidhan main sanshodhan kiya gaya or ab raashtrapati aam chunaavon dwaara chune jate hai.
 rashtriya parishad main bahumat se sarkaar banati he or is sarkaar ke pramukh pradhanmantri hote hai.
 slovakia 8 prashasnic kshetron or 79 zilon main banta hua he.
 slovakia main 55 zila nyaayalay hai, 8 kshetreey nyaayalay hai; or ek sarvocch nyaayalay he. iske atirikt ek sanvaidhanik nyaayalay bhi he.
 2008 main slovakia kaa sakal gharelu utpaada 95 billian dollar tha. 1 janvari 2009 ko slovakia eurozone kaa bhaag ban gaya or euro ne slovaki crown kii jagah le lee.
