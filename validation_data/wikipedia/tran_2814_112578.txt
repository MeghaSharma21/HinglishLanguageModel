 bharti interprijej, ek vishaal bhartiya vyapaarik samooh he jiskaa mukhyalay bharat ke nai dilli main sthit he, jahaan se iskaa sanchaalan sampurna bharat or kuch anya deshon main bhi kiya jaataa he, jaise shri lanka, bangladesh, jersey, garnasi or seychelles; or saath hi africa ke burkina phaso, chaad, congou brazavil, congou loktantrik ganraajya, gabon, ghana, kenya, madagascar, malawi, nizer, nigeria, sierra leon, tanzania, uganda or zambia main bhi iskaa vistaar ho rahaa he. isaki sthapana sunil bharti mittal dwaara kii gayi thi. bharti enterprijej kaa vyapaar doorsanchaar, khudara, vittiya sevaayen, utpaadan or software kshetron main fail hua he.
 company kii sthapana sunil bharti mittal ne 1976 main apane do bhaaiyon ke saath kii thi. company bharat main apane naam kii bajaay airtel or betel jaise brands naamon se zyada jaani jaati he. haal hi main company ne apane brands ke punarnirmaan kii prakriya ke tahat apane logo or vyavsaayik chhavi main parivartan kiya he.
 bharti praathmik rup se 
 bharti airtel limited upyogkartaaon kii sankhya ke aadhaar par vishwa main paanchavin sabase badi mobile oparator he or yah vyavsaayik rup se 19 deshon main maujuud he. graahakon kii sankhya ke aadhaar par yah bharat kii sabase badi ekikrut doorsanchaar
 "bharti infratel" sunil bharti mittal ke swaamitv main he. company vibhinn doorsanchaar or internet pradaataaon ke liye toweron kaa nirmaan or unke rakh-rakhaav kaa kaam karti he. haal hi main company ne paryaavaran kii behatari ke liye pi7 pahalon kii shuruyaat kii he. bharat ke 18 rajyon or 11 doorsanchaar kshetron main infratel ke 32,000 se bhi zyada tower hai, jo nirantar badhate ja rahe hai. bharti infratel kii inds towers main bhi 42% kii hissedari he, jiskaa nirmaan 12 kshetron main tower vyapaar ko sangathit karne ke liye bharti infratel, vodaphone or edia ke sanyukt udhyam ke rup main kiya gaya tha. 
 "bharti retail limited", bharti interprijej ke purna swaamitv vaali ek sahaayak company he. bharti retail bahu-praroopi store kii shrinkhalaa kaa sanchaalan karti he. company ke pados ke kiraanaa praaroop vaale store "iside" brands or gathile haiparamarket praaroop "iside market" brands ke tahat sanchalit hote hai.haal ke dinon main company, krishi kshetra kii company fieldfresh kii sanyukt saajhedaari ke saath khaadya aarthik kshetron main bhi adhikadhik ruchi dikhaa rahi he.
 bharti interprijej ne puure bharat main khudara store kholane ke liye waal-mart ke saath anubandh kiya he. yadhyapi retail store shrinkhalaa ke udhyam kii shuruyaat hona abhi baaki he, lekin agast 2007 main donon companiyon ke ek ascharyajanak vaktavy main kaha gaya tha ki unhone thok cash-and-kairi anubandh par hastakshar kiye hai. companiyaan 7 saalon main 10-15 cash-and-kairi suvidhaayen kholengi or kareeb 5,000 logon ko rojgaar dengi. prayek store 50,000-100,000 varg foot kshetra main failaa hoga.
 bharti samooh kii anya retail companiyaan bharti retail privet limited, bharti retail resorcej privet limited or cedar saport services limited hai.
 "bharti teletech" bharat kii agrani doorsanchaar or sambaddh utpaada company he. yah vishwa main landline telephone ke sabase bade nirmaataaon main se ek he. puure desh main majboot network ke saath, company antarrashtriya brandon ke iti or telecom utpaadon kii praathmik vitarak bhi he, jaise motorola, blueberi, thomson, policom, transend or logytek.
 comviva technologies limited puure vishwa main mobile operators ke liye viees samaadhaan pradaan karne vaali ek bhartiya sanstha he. comviva ko purv main bharti telesoft ke rup main janaa jaataa tha or ise april 2009 main comviva brands kaa naam de diya gaya.
 "fieldfresh foods pra. limited" bharti interprijej or dmpiel india limited ke bich kaa ek sanyukt udhyam he. company taaja fal or subjiyan tathaa prasanskrit khaadya va peya ko gharelu tathaa antarrashtriya bazaaron main pradaan karti he.
 "bharti eksa general insurance" bharti interprijej or eksa, jo vittiya suraksha or sampatti prabandhan main vishwa kii agrani company he, kaa sanyukt udhyam he. yah company july 2007 main gathit hui.
 "bharti eksa life insurance company limited", bharti interprijej or eksa, jo vittiya suraksha or sampatti prabandhan main vishwa kii agrani company he, kaa sanyukt udhyam he. company jivan bima or sampatti prabandhan utpaadon kii ek shrankhla pradaan karti he.
 "bharti eksa investment managers pra. limited", bharat kii ek parisampatti prabandhan company he jo bharti enterprijej, eksa investment manager or eksa asia pacific holdings kaa ek sanyukt udhyam he.
 bharti interprijej ke purna swaamitv vaali sahaayak company "centom limited", ek shiksha or vikaas sanstha he jo graahak anubhav ke kshetra main maahir he. centom larning, jo bharti kii ek sahaayak company he, bade str par kisi vyapaar or raashtra kii utpaadakta ko prabhavit karne ke uddeshy se sampurna shikshan or kaushal-nirmaan ke samaadhaan pradaan karti he. iske alaava, yah aadhunik bharat kaa cheharaa badalane ke liye yuvaaon ko ucch shiksha bhi pradaan karti he.
 shiksha ke kshetra main, centom larning kaaphi vistrut kshetron ke liye samaadhaan pradaan karti he, jaise prakriya outsorcing, custom content vikaas, mishrit shikshan kaaryakramon ke dwaara prashikshan, kaaryakari koching or pradarshan koching se lekar prakriya prabandhan or pramanikaran tak.
 haal hi main, centom larning ne africa ke 16 deshon main utpaadakta or vyapaar badhaane ke liye bharti airtel ke saath ek samrik sahyog main pravesh kiya he.
 centom larning kii adwitiya visheshta he, vibhinn audyogic kaaryakshetron main shikshan or vikaas main uskaa prachur anubhav. puure bharat main 400 shikshan or vikaas visheshgyon ke saath, ek gatisheel vaataavaran main vyapaar ke uddeshyon ko puraa karne ke liye centom larning vartmaan main bharti airtel or bharti samooh kii anya companiyon ke saath karyrat he, jaise ki bharti eksa life insurance, bharti eksa investment menagers, bharti wallmart, bharti retail, bharti infratel, comviva technologies, bhartee del mont, bharti realty or bharti fundation.
 iske alaava, centom larning kii kai agrani sansthaaon ke saath unki shiksha aavashyaktaaon kii poorti karne ke liye saajhedaari he, jaise ki american express, jenpact, maaruti sujuki, dilli orport, pepsico, echsiel, kamins, alji, jesibi, dush bank, motilal oswaal, blue dart, kaaya, siet, averest industries.
 ucch shiksha ke kshetra main, isane pratibha ke antar ko bharne or rojgaar ko badhaane ke liye puure bharat ke 90 shaharon main 150 se zyada centom shikshan centre sthapit kiye hai. centom larning ne haal hi main centom you- prabandhan evam rachanaatmak shiksha santhaan kii shuruyaat kii he, jo vaishvik manyata praapt vishvavidhyalay or sansthaanon ke sangyojan main snaatak or snatakottar kaarykram pradaan karata he.
 kaushal nirmaan ke kshetra main, rashtriya kaushal vikaas nigam kii saajhedaari ke saath centom larning ne 1.2 karod logon ko praveen banane or jeevanyaapan ke maukey pradaan karke seva yogya banane ke liye centom workskills india limited kii sthapana kii he.

 sankshep main, centom larning sthai parivartan ko sakasham banane vaali sabase prashansaneey shiksha or kaushal vikaas sahyogi banane ke liye samarpit he.

 "bharti foundation" kii sthapana 2000 main uddeshy ke saath kii gayi thi, "apane desh ke suvidhahin bacchon or yuvaaon ko unki kshamta ke anuroop viksit hone main madad karnaa". iskaa uddeshy aise kaaryakramon kaa nirmaan karnaa or unka samarthan karnaa he jo shiksha tathaa takanic evam soochna ke upyog ke dwaara mahatpurna or sthaayee parivartan ko janm den.
