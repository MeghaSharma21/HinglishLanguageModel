 
 da vempaire diaries ek america kishor-alokik drama televijan shrunkhlaa he jo ki elex loyad, kevin williamson or juli plake dwaara viksit kiya gaya he. yah isi naam ki el je smith dwaara likhi gayi upanyaas shrunkhlaa par aadhaarit he. shrunkhlaa kaa premier da CW televijan network par sitambar 10, 2009 ko hua or vartmaan samay main yah apane tisare satr main he, jiskaa premier sitambar 15, 2011 ko hua tha. shrunkhlaa kii kahani ek kaalpanik chote, alokik praaniyon dwaara pretvadhit shahar mistic falls, varjeenia, ke nivaasiyon ke irdagird gumti he. is shrunkhlaa kaa mukhya dhyaan pramukh teen paatro ailena gilbert, stefan selvator or demen selvator ke bich ke prem trikon par he. in teenon hi paatro kaa apana-apana kaala ateet he. is sho ko bahut saare namakan, do pipalas chois puraskaar or bahut saare tin chois puraskaar jite hai, 26 april,2011 ke din.
