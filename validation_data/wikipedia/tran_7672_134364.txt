 coffeekhaanaa, coffeeghar yaa coffeehouse aise sthaan ko bolte hai jo graahakon ko coffee kaa peya banaakar pilaae. yah bahut had tak chikhaane kii tarah hota he or ise ek prakaar kaa vishesh restaraan samajha ja saktaa he. aksar isamen coffee ke saath-saath chaay, chocolate peya or halaki khaane kii chizein bhi uplabdh hoti hai. europe, turki, iran or madhya purv ke anya bhaagon main coffeekhaanon kaa bahut rivaaj he. arab deshon or madhya purv ke anya hisson ke coffeekhaanon main aksar huqqe bhi milte hai. bhartiya upmahadweep main bhi kuch sthanon par coffeekhaane maujuud hai.
 vishwa ke kai sthanon main coffeekhaanon kaa gaharaa saanskrithik mahatv he. uttar bharat ke chikhaanon kii tarah vahi logon ke milane kaa sthaan hote hai. aksar lekhak apni likhaaiyaan coffeekhaanon main rachate hai. apane sthai graahakon ke liye coffeekhaane ek anaupchaarik sabha-sthal kaa kaam dete hai.
