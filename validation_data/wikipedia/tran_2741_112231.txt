 phuldei bharat ke uttrakhand rajya kaa ek sthaneey tyohaar he, jo caitra maah ke aagaman par manaaya jaataa he.sampoorn uttarakhand main is caitra maheene ke prarambh hote hi anek pushp khil jate hai, jinamein funli, laai, guiryal, kingod, hisar, burans aadi pramukh hai . caitra kii pehali gate se chote-chote bachche haathon main kainni lekar praatah kaal 4-5 baje ke lagbhag apane kheton main yaa aangan main jaakar phulon ko ekatra karte hai . anantar sarvapratham gaanv ke mandir kii dehali par phool shradhdha ke saath chadaaye jate hai . tatapashchaat apane gharon kii sabhi dehaliyon par in pushpon ko chadhaaya jaataa he . ye bachche 8-9 dinon tak isi prakaar pushp ekatra karke chadhaate hai . is prakaar phuldei uttarakhand kaa prasiddh tyohaar he .
