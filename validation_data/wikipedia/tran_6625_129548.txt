 1963 main, praarambhik rup se bi.tech. oopaadhi hetu abhisnatak str k prashikshan pradaan kiye jane hetu rasinic abhiyantriki vibhaag sthapit kiya gaya tha. anek rashtriya evam antarrashtriya sammelan /sangoshthio /kaaryshaalaayein yojit kar chukaa he, jisamen saahitiyik anuwaad tathaa saanskrithik ekta, prodyogiki shiksha tathaa maanviya mulya, boudhdadarshan evam samayik samasyaayen evam anuprayukt darshan abhivritti dharm ke sandarbh main pramukh hai.
 1970 main vishvavidhyalay anudaan ayog ne equipment plant dizin main snatakottar paathyakram kholane kii anumati pradaan kii. industial polushan ebetment tathaa advanced transfar process ke do naye kshetra samahit karke in paathyakram ko vividhikrut kiya gaya. isi bich equipment and plant dizin kii visheshgyata ko computer aded process dizin ke rup main pu nahsanrachit, adhyatan avan upyogi bana diya gaya.
 vibhaag ko rasinic abhiyantriki ke vibhinn kshetron main anusandhan ke liye khyaati mili he tathaa yah 50 se adhik pi.ech.di. tathaa 150 se adhik em.i. thisis utpaadit kar chukaa he. maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantralay tathaa akhil bhartiya takneekee shiksha parishad tathaa gaiyr paramparaagat urja sansaadhan mantralay se vittey sahaayta praapt kii, tathaa germany ke hanover vishvavidhyalay ke saath sanyukt anusandhan kaaryakramon par bhi kaary kiya he. yah vibhaag rasinic tathaa usase jude huai udyogon ko anek kshetron main paraamarsh uplabdh kara rahaa he.
