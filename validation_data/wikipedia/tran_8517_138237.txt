 pargrahi jivan yaa parthivetar jivan vah sambhavit jivan he jo prithvi se alag kisi anya pind par vidyaman ho or jisaki utpatti bhi prithvi se na hui ho. yah pariklpit jeev saral akendrik ho sakate hai yaa maanvon se kahin adhik viksit va shaktishaalee sabhyataa vaale jeev bhi ho sakate hai. jin kalpanaaon main aise pargrahi jivan main buddhi kii upasthiti maani jaati he use "pargrahi chetana" kehete hai.
