 sabhi padaarth sthaan gherte hai. isi tri-bemiya sthaan kii maatra kii maap ko aaytan kehete hai. ek-bemiya akritiyaan evam dwi-bemiya akritiyaan kaa aaytan shunya hota he.
  ko sadish rup main vyakt karen to uskaa aaytan in teen sadishon ke adish gunanfal ke baraabar hota he.
 kisi chatushfalki ke chaaro shirshon ke sthiti sadish a, b, c and d hon to uskaa aaytan ke tiryak sadish gunanfal ke 1/6 ke baraabar hota he.
 kisi padaarth ke ikai aaytan main nihit drabyaman ko us padaarth kaa ghanatv kehete hai. lohe kaa ghanatv lakdi ke ghanatv se adhik hota he.
