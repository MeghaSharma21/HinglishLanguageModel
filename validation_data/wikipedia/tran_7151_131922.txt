achaanak atyadhik badlaavon ke kaaran bhi janaa jaataa he.uttar main manali, dakshin main kullu shahar se 51 kilometre dur yah sthaan manali-leh ke mukhyamarg main padataa he.ise lahole or spiti jilon kaa pravesh dwaar bhi kaha jaataa he. puraa varsh yahaan barf kii chadar bichhi rahati he.rajya paryaton vibhaag ke anusaar pichley varsh 2008main kareeb 100,000 videshi paryatak yahaan aae the.yahan mili. rohtang darre main skiing or trekking karne kii apaar sambhaavnaayen hai.har saal yahan hajaaron kii sankhya main paryatak in aakarshak najaaron kaa lutf lene or saahasik khel khilne aate hai.  isi darre se beas nadi kaa udgam hua he. beas kunda beas nadi ke udgam kaa sthaan haivehaan ke kunda ke pani kaa swaad kaa varnan shabdon main sambhav hi nahin he. shaayad amrit kaa swaad aisa hi hota hoga?yah kunda veda beas rishi ke mandir main sthit he.'beas nadi'kullu - manali hi nahin, varan ferozepur main 'hari kaa patan' kii nadi main milakar punjab or sutluj nadi main mil kar pakistan kii zamin ko bhi haraa bharaa karti he phir arab sagar main mil jaati he. is nadi kii kul lambaai 460 kilometre he.kehete hai..lahole -spiti or kullu khetra aapas main alag the tab inhen jodne ke liye sthaneey logon ne yahan ek maarg banane ke liye apane isht dev bhagwaan shiv kii aradhana kii .tab bhagwaan shiv ne bhrigu tung parvat ko apane trishul se kaat kar yah bhrigu -tung maarg yani rohtang paas banaaya.is darre ke dakshin main vyas nadi kaa udgam kunda bana hua he. yaheen par hindu granth mahabharat likhane vaale maharishi veda vyas ji ne tapasya kii thi. isi liye is sthaan par vyas mandir bana hua he.sabhi dharaayen manali se 10 kilometre dur palachan gaanv main mil jaati hai or beas nadi banaati hai. pehle is nadi ko'arjiki' kaha jaataa tha. mahabharat ke baad is nadi kaa naam beas nadi padaa.
