 je. manjula 'dephence research and deveopment organizeshan' main mahanideshak ke pad par niyukt honevaali pehali mahila hai. 
 ye un gini chuni mahila vaigyanikon main shaamil hai jinhone bhartiya sena ko aadhuniktam electronic system se las karne ke kaam ko chunaa.
 ve beete 27 saalon kai atyaadhunik system viksit kar chuki hai jinase bhartiya sena kii dakshata badhati he. 
 in systams kii padtaal ke liye unhen apni tiimon ko registan, pahaad, jangal or samudri pani main le jaakar inpar abhyaas karwaanaa hota he. 
 manjula ne electranix or communication ejineering main post grajuation kiya he. 
 maujuuda vakt main manjula 'dephence research and deveopment organizeshan' kii 'electranix and communication system' kii mahanideshak hai. 
 unki zimmedaariyon main bhartiya sena ke liye behatar radar, aatmsurakshit jaimar, ilketro-optical unit, aleesiaar sorce ityadi viksit karnaa shaamil he.
