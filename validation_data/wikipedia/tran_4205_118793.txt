 idaho sanyukt rajya america kaa ek rajya he jo kii uske uttarpashchimi hisse main sthit he. is rajya kaa sabase bada shahar or rajdhani boise he. is rajya ke nagarikon ko aydohans kehete hai. ydaho sangh main 3 july 1890 ko 43ve rajya ke taur pe samilit kiya gaya.
 is rajya kaa zyaadaatar ilaaka pahari he or kshetrafal main puure england se bhi bada he. yah chaaro taraf se jameen se ghira he or washington, oregon, nevada, utaah, vyoming, montana or canada ke british colombia ilaako se seemaa rakhata he.
 yahan par kaaphi log baahar ke rajyon se aate jate rahete hai isliye is kai bahaari logo kaa ghar bhi kaha jaataa he. madhya ydaho uttar america main sabase puraane saki risorato kaa sthaan bhi he. sanyukt rajya ke censos ke anusaar 2008 main is rajya kii jansankhya 15,23,816 thi. is rajya ko pyaar se jem rajya bhi bulaate hai kyon kii lagbhag har ratan yahan miltaa he. iske alaava ydaho duusari aisi jagah he jaha sitara garnet miltaa he, pehali bharat main himalay parvat he.
 rajya kaa naaraa "yah hameshaa ke liye" he.
