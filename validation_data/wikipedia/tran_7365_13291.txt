 shen watson robert ek australian cricket khilaadi hai. ve daayen haath ke ballebaaj or daayen haath ke madhyam tej gendbaaj hai. antarrashtriya cricket main mukhyatah salaami ballebaaj ke rup main ballebaaji karte hai, haalanki gharelu maichon main ve salaami ballebaaji karte kam hi nazar aate hai.
 unhone austrailian cricket team ke saath apane cricket kariyar kii shuruyaat 2002 main apana pehla ekadivadasiya match dakshin africa ke khilaaf khelte hue kii. ekadivasiy team ke sthaayee sadsy hone ke baavjood, unhone australia kii ore se bahut kam test match khele hai, test cricket main unka pardaapan janvari 2005 ko sidney cricket ground main pakistan ke viruddh hua. australia ke namit test harfanmaulaa khilaadi ke rup main manonith kiye jane ke vabjood, aksar chotil rahane ke kaaran ve test team main apni jagah pakki nahin kar pate the. haalanki, 2009 kii duusari chamahi se, watson ne test cricket main austrilian salaami ballebaaj ke rup main simon katich ke saath ballebaaji karnaa prarambh kiya. watson ko 2010 main alan border medal se sammanit kiya gaya.
 watson kii patni lee fox sports kii australian prastutakarta he. unhone kai modelling shoot kiye hai, jaise 2009 or 2010 main of cricket calendar or alpha magazine kaa shoot.
 watson ne apane pratham-shrenee cricket kariyar kii shuruyaat apane grih rajya queensland ko chhodane ke baad, tasmania tigers ke saath kii, lekin antarrashtriya kariyar shuru karne ke baad ve waapas queensland bulls ke liye khilne lage. ve english county championships hampshire ke liye bhi khel chuke hai. ve terry alderman ko apana guru maanate hai.
 watson ko 2002 main pehali baar tasmania ke liye puraa cap main sabase adhik wicket lene or madhya kram main ballebaaji ke unke sthaayee pradarshan ko dekhte hue, dakshin africa ke daure ke liye australia kii test team main chunaa gaya. australia ke captain rah chuke steve wa ne apane kaarykaal ke dauraan kaha tha ki watson 1950 ke dashak ke kith miller or alan davidson ke baad sambhawatah pehle vaastavik olerounder ban sakate hai. watson ne apane adarsh, wa ke saath australian team main chune jane par bahut khush the. watson ne abhyaas maichon ke dauraan apane pehle match main naabad shatak banaaya tha, lekin australian satr main dakshin africa ko 3-0 se parajeet karne vaali team ke gyaarah sadasyon ko hi chayankartaaon dwaara praathamikta die jane ke kaaran, ve test maichon main nahin khel sake. watson ko is daure main apane vanade karier kii shuruyaat karne kaa mauka mila, jab unhen wa ke sthaan par khilne ke liye maidan main utaara gaya, wa ko australian satr ke dauraan vanade ke farrnal main team ke jagah bana paane main vifal hone ke kaaran team se baahar rakhaa gaya tha. 2003 main peeth main teen chote lagane se pehle tak watson vanade team ke niyamit sadsy ban chuke the, chot ke kaaran hi ve 2003 cricket vishwa cap main nahin khel paae. unka sthaan unke team queensland ke hi sadsy andrew symonds ne liya, jinhone tournament ke dauraan 143* or 91* kii pari khel kar ek safal ol rounder ke rup main sthapit kar diya.
 watson apni chot ke kaaran austrailia ke 2003-04 satr se baahar rahe or apane punarvaas ke dauraan, ve apni ballebaaji kii kshamta ko behatar karne liye satr ke adhikansh mainchon main ballebaaj ke rup main khelte dikhe, us samay bhi unki peeth kii chot thik nahin hui thi. us dauraan, unhone apane club said, lindifarm ke liye khelte hue 300 ranon kii jordar pari kheli.
 watson ne 2004-05 satr main vanade main gendbaaj ol rounder ke rup main punarvaapasi kii. australia ke sidney cricket ground kii sukhi pich par do spinneron or teen tej gendbaaj ke saath utarane kaa faisla karne ke kaaran, watson pakistan ke viruddh tisare test main bhi khele.
 2005 main australia ke upar england ke eshez jeet ke baad, pratyuttar main australia ne watson paanchave gendbaaz or sabhi test ke liye ol rounder ke rup main shaamil kiya. watson ne england ke andrew flintoff kii barabari karne kaa daawa kiya, jinhone ingland kii jeet main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai thi. is bhoomika ke saath watson isisi vishwa ekadash ke khilaaf khele, lekin is bhoomika ke saath khelte hue west indies ke viruddh apane dasre test main dive lagaakar gend ko rokane kaa prayaas karte hue, apane kandhe par chot lag baithe. watson ko phir symonds dwaara badla gaya or uske baad shesh satr main unhen australia ke liye khilne kaa mauka nahin mila.
 unhen dakshin africa ke 2006 ke daure ke liye ekadivasiy team main punah bulaayaa gaya, lekin ol-rounder andrew symonds ke chot se ubarkar waapas aane par unhen phir se team se hataa diya gaya. watson test team main apni jagah talash rahe the, lekin chotil ho jane ke kaaran, andrew symonds ko unke sthaan par khilne kaa mauka mila.
 watson ko apekshaakrit adhik gendbaaji ausat ke kaaran, apni apekshaakrit sapaat gendbaaji or gend ko ghumaane kii aakshamata ke liye aalochanaayen bhi jhelni padi. tasmania team ke purv sadsy or bhavishya ke australian chayankarta jami cox ne mahasoos kiya ki gendbaaz olerowder ke rup main watson kaa upyog galat he, unka vishwaas tha ki watson kaa ek ballebaaz or aanshik gedabaaz ke rup me upyog kiya janaa chahie, na ki ek aise gendbaaz ke rup main jo ki pari ke ant main aakar tezi se ran bator sake.
 yah parivartan 2006 isisi champions trophy main dekhne ko mila, jab watson ko simon katich ke sthaan par, wicket-keeper adam gilcrist ke saath pari kii shuruyaat karne ke liye bheja gaya. us pratiyogita main unhone gend or balle donon se prabhavit kiya or australia ne apni pehali champions trophy jeet darj kii. aalochakon or captain riki ponting ko unke behatar strike rate, sidhe maarane or gendbaazi kii amata ne prabhavit kiya, jis kaaran katich ke sthaan par watson ko variyata di gai. west indies or england ke khilaaf pehle do maichon main asafal hone ke baad, watson ne bharat ke khilaaf australia kii jeet main 50 mahatvapurn ran banaae, jiske parinamswaroop australia ko semifinal main pravesh mila or 2009 main dakshin africa main yojit champions trophy main, watson ne punah pramukh bhoomika nibhaate hue, england or newjiland ke khilaaf semifinal or final main do lagaatar shatak jad kar khitaab australia ke naam kar diya.
 ponting ne england ke khilaaf 2006-07 ashes shrinkhalaa main watson ko number 6 kii pojishan main khelaane kaa suzav diya or unhen team main shaamil kar liya gaya. haalanki, test ke ek saptaah pehle ek gharelu ekadivasiy match ke dauraan ghutane ke pichle hisse kii nas khich jane ke kaaran maidan chhodana padaa, jis kaaran unhen teenon test main baahar baithna padaa. maaikel clark ko watson kii jagah bulaayaa gaya, ek arddh shatkeey pari or fir ek shatkeey pari khelkar clark ne team main apni jagah pakki kar lee.
 watson ke mukkebaaji diwas par or melborn main tha emsiji main chauthe test ke liye feet ho jane or demian martin ke apratyaashit sanyas ke baad khilne kii ummid jaagi or aisa lagane laga ki watson ko teem main shaamil kar liya jayega. haalanki, queensland ke match main ek or chot ke kaaran watson ko ashes shrinkhalaa ke baaki maichon main bhi baahar baithna padaa. ekadivasiy team main watson ne farvari main, olerounder camerun white ke sthaan par vaapasi kii, halanki punah 2007 cricket vishwa cap main chotil ho jane ke kaaran, super 8 ke adhikansh maichon main newjiland ke viruddh 32 gendo main 50 ranon kii karishmai pari se pehle tak baahar baithna padaa. chot ne phir watson ko pareshaan kiya or isisi vishwa twanti 20 ke shuruaati daur main ghutane main chot lagane ke kaaran ve toornament ke adhikansh match nahin khel paae.
 uske baad ve 2007-08 australian satr main baahar rahe.
 symonds ko anushaasanamak kaarnon se australian team se nikaal diya gaya or watson 2008 ke ant main bharat ke daure par olerounder ke rup main gaye, vahaan unhone 6wein number par ballebaaji kii. dilli main tisare test kesan kii dauraan, ve bhartiya salaami ballebaaz gautam gambhir ke saath kai vivaadon main ghire rahe, jinhone us match main doharaa shatak jamaaya or apana shatak watson kii gend par midwicket ke upar se chakka markar puraa kiya tha. pari ke dauraan, ek ran lete samay gambhir ne watson ko kohni se takkar maari or ek samvaadadaata sammelan main yah daawa kiya ki vah jaanboojhkar nahin kiya gaya tha or saath hi yah bhi kaha ki watson main unhen out karne kii kshamta nahin he. unhen baad main doshi paaya gaya or un par ek match kaa pratibandh laga.
 australia lautne ke baad, symonds ko test team main waapas bulaayaa gaya or donon olerounder brisben main newjiland ke khilaaf khele. pich main ghaas hone or barish ke kaaran nami aa jane ke kaaran, pich tej gendbaazi ke liye madadgaar prathit ho rahi thi, isliye spinner jesan kreza ko team se hataakar do tej gendbaaz olerounder ke saath khilne kaa faisalaa liya gaya. australia ke match jeetne ke baad, spinner nathan hauritz ko watson kii jagah team main shaamil kiya gaya or symonds team main bane rahe. symonds kaa kharaab pradarshan jari rahaa or unke sthaan par watson kii team main vaapasi tay lag rahi thi, lekin donon hi khilaadi varsh ke ant main chotil ho gaye, watson balaghat fracture peedit the. watson ne kisanyukt arab emirate main pakistan ke khilaaf vanade shrinkhalaa main shatak jamaate hue antarrashtriya str par vaapasi kii.
 unhone 30 july 2009 ko agebeston main tisare ashes test ke liye farm paane ke liye sangharsh kar rahe salaami ballebaaj philip hughes ke sthaan par australian team main vaapasi kii. varsha se baadhit match main simon katich ke saath ballebaaji karte hue unhone 62 or 53 ranon kii pari kheli. unhone west indies ke khilaaf disambar 2009 ko edilade main apana doosraa sarvocch score 96 ran banaae. unhone or katich ne shatkeey saajhedaari kii or din kaa khel samaapt hone tak ve 96 par khel rahe the, agali subah kii pehali gend par hi shatak puraa karne kii laalasaa main gend ko seemaa paar bhejne kaa prayaas kiya, lekin balle kaa bhitari kinaaraa lete hue gend hawa main uchhal gai. tisare test main, unhone katich ke saath ek or shatkeey saajhedaari karte hue 89 ran banaae. duusari pari main, unhone vipakshi captain kris gail ko out kiya or unke saamne chilla kar khushi jaahir karne ke kaaran unhen dand mila. is kaaran match referi ne unpar fine lagaaya or australia kii janata ne bhi unki aalochana kii.
 pakistan ke khilaaf pehle test main, unhone 93 ran banaae, katich ke saath ballebaazi karte hue aisa teesri baar hua jab watson apana test shatak banane se chuke, katich ke saath wicket ke bich talmail bigadane ke kaaran donon khilaadi ek hi ore bhaage or watson ran out ho gaye. chauthe din, antat watson ne apana pehla test shatak jadaa. dopaher ke bhojan tak ve 98 ke nijee score par khel rahe the or yahaan tak pahunchane main unhen dopaher ke bhojan ke viraam sahit 106 minute kaa samay laga. dopaher ke bhojan ke brake ke baad ve 99 par pahunche or uske baad unhone kai dot gendon kaa saamana kiya. unhone apana pehla test shatak bhi ek khaas andaaj main banaaya, point par khadey abdur roff kii ore unhone gend ko joardaar tareeke se hit kiya, par bhaagyashaalee rahe or roff unka vah kaich lapak nahin paaen. gend dur jaakar giri or watson ko ek ran banane kaa paryaapt mauka mil gaya, jinaki unhen sakht aavashyaktaa thi. unka shatak 293 minute main 186 gendon kaa saamana ke baad puraa hua, jisamen unke 9 chauke or ek chakka shaamil tha. jab ponting ne pari kii ghoshnaa kii, tab ve naabad khel rahe the, unhone 120 ran banaae. watson ko australia kii test jeet main unki bhoomika ke liye 30 disambar ko main of da match se sammanit kiya gaya.
 esasiji main duusare test kii duusari pari main, watson ek baar phir shatak banane se chuke or 97 ke score par out ho gaye. is test ke dauraan, australian media cricket association ne watson ko varsh ke australia ke sarvshereshth cricket khilaadi ke puraskaar se nawaja.
 2010 ke alan border medal puraskaar main, unhone varsh kaa sarvshereshth ek divasiy khilaadi kaa puraskaar jeeta, varsh ke sarvshereshth test khilaadi ke up vijeta rahe or alan border medal bhi jeeta.
 watson indian premier lig main rajasthan royles team kii ore se khelte hai. unhen 125,000 gend donon se hi karishmai pradarshan karte hue, chaar ardhshtkeey pari ke saath apni team ko tournament me teen mukhya mainchon or semi final match main jeet dilaai. unhone 17 wicket bhi liye or apane pehle baarah maichon me se chaar main main of da match bane or saath hi main alf da series kaa khitaab bhi jeeta.
 ipel ke unke pradarshan ko dekhte hue, watson ko ipel satr ke dauraan chotil hue mathew heden ke sthaan par australia ke west indies daureke liye ek divasiy shrinkhalaa ke bhaag ke rup main team main shaamil kiya gaya. watson ne is shrinkhalaa main khud ko ekadivasiy shrinkhalaa main salaami ballebaaj ke rup main sthapit kar diya.
 watson ipel ke duusare satr main apane rashtriya kartavya or chot ke kaaran khel nahin paae or shaayad yahi vajah thi jiske kaaran rajasthan royles apane kwalifing round main sheersh chaar tak pahunchne main bhi asafal rahi.
