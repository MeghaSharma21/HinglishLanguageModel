 turki-mongol madhya asia ke stapi ilaake main rahane vaale vividh kanaabadosh logon ko diya jane vaala naam tha jo mongol samrajya ke adheen the. samay ke saath-saath unki bhashaa or pahchaan main gahari turki chhap aa gai. in logon ne madhyakaal main bahut se bade rajya banaae, jinamein ilkhaani samrajya, chaghtai khaanat, sunaharaa urdu, qaazan khaanat, nogai khaanat, qremian khaanat, temuri rajvansh or mogul samrajya shaamil hai.
