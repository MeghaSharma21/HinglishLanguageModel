 bharpura pehleja ghat jankshan railway stesan, stesan code PHLJ, bhartiya rajya bihar ke saaran jile main sthit ek railway stesan he. yah railway ke uttar purvi sambhag main padataa hai evam sonpur shahar evam patna ke aas paas rahane vaale log is stesan kaa zyaadaatar prayog karte he. yah shahar ghaghara or ganga nadi ke paas sthit he.
 patliputra-sonpur railkhand par sthit bharpura pehleja ghat stesan saura urja sanchalit stesan ban gaya he. purvamadhya railway ke nirmaan vibhaag dwaara stesan bhawan kii chat par 20 kilowat ruf taff hibrid solar plant banaaya gaya he.
 1982 tak old pehleja ghat stesan chaalu tha. 1982 main mahatma gandhi sethu ke udghaatan se pehle, patna main mahendru ghat or sonal main pehleja ghat ke bich bhartiya railway kii niyamit steamer seva thi. pahalaja stesan or sonapur stesan ke bich puraani sankeern gej line thi. stesan upyog main nahin tha or mahatma gandhi sethu ko kholane ke baad chhod diya gaya tha nayaa stesan puraane stesan se 3.2 kimi purv ke aasapaas banaaya gaya tha or 3 farvari 2016 ko shuru hua tha. stesan pehle pahalajghat jankshan namit tha, lekin bharpura ke graminon ke virodh ke baad, iskaa naam badalakar july 2016 main bharpura pehlejghat jankshan kar diya gaya tha. bihar main 2016 ke baadh main, yah stesan baadh peediton ke liye raahat centre ke rup main kaam karata tha
