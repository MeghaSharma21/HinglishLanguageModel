 bharat main rashtriya saanskrithik nidhi 29 march 1997 se shuru kii gai. raa sa nidhi ke gathan kaa mukhya uddeshy sarkaar, gaiyr sarkari agenciyon, nijee sansthaaon, vyaktiyon se bharat kii prakritik viraasat tathaa samrudh samskruti ke vikaas, suraksha, sangrakshan va unki bahaali ke liye dhan jutanaa tathaa saarvajanik-nijee saajhedaari ko badhaava dena he. rashtriya saanskriti nidhi kaa prabandhan tathaa prashasan parishad tathaa karypalak samiti dwaara sanchalit kiya jaataa he. paryaton tathaa saanskrithik mantri parishad kii adhyakshata karte hai. parishad main adhiktam 24 sadsy adhyaksh tathaa sadsy sachiv samet ho sakate he. isamen corporate sector, nijee foundation tathaa laabh ke bina kaary karne vaale sanghathan jaise vibhinn kshetron ke 19 pratishthit sadasyon kaa pratinidhit rahata he. ukt nidhi kii bhartiya sansad tathaa daandaataaon ke prati isaki javaabadari rahati he. pariyojna karyanvayan samiti pariyojna kaa kaary nishpaadan karti he. isamen daandaataaon, rashtriya saanskrithik nidhi, civik adhikari tathaa jahaan apekshit ho bhartiya puraatatv surveykshan kaa pratinidhitiv rahata he. rashtriya saanskrithik nidhi khate main pariyojna ke lekhe shaamil kiye jate hai, jisaki lekha pariksha bharat ke niyantrak or mahalekha pareekshak dwaara kii jaati he. rashtriya saanskrithik nidhi ke liye diya gaya daan iskar adhinium 80-ji ke antargat 100 pratishat main kar laabh ke liye maanya he.
 bharat sarkaar, saanskrithik mantralay ne 19.50 karod rupaye kii samooh nidhi kii puri raashi jari kar di he tathaa rashtriya saanskrithik nidhi ko 19.50 karod rupaye kii samooh nidhi die jane kaa vaada puraa kar liya he. 31 march 2010 ko raa sa nidhi se secondry sangrah 10.90 karod rupaye tha.
 36 pariyojnaayen, moort or amoort donon saarvajanik upakramon/corporate sector se dhan jutakar rashtriya saanskrithik nidhi ke antargat sanchalit kii gai. inamen se 10 pariyojnaayen puri kar lee gai hai.
 yah ek lagaataar prakriya he, jiske dwaara sarkaar rashtriya saanskrithik nidhi main anshdaan karne ke liye vibhinn mantralayon/vibhaagon ko utsahit karti he ki ve apane niyantran ke saarvajanik upakramon/corporato ko apni corporate saamaajik jimmedaari adhidesh nibhaane ke liye protsaahit kare.
 haal hi main bangaluru main corporate sector ke saath rashtriya saanskrithik nidhi main anshdaan karne ke prayojan se publicity abhiyaan chalaaya gaya. rashtriya nidhi kii shuruuaat se sanchalit kii gai prayek yojana par kharch kii gai raashi kaa byora neeche diya gaya he.
