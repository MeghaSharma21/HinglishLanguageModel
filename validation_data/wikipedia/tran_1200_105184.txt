 sandhya agarwal  ek purv bhartiya mahila cricket khilaadi he jo ki bhartiya mahila cricket team kii captain bhi rah chuki he. inkaa janm bhartiya rajya madhya pradesh ke indore shahar main 1963 main hua tha.
 sandhya ne bhartiya mahila team ke liye 1984 se 1995 tak kul teraa test match khele jisamen inhone kul 1110 ran banaae jabaki inhone bhartiya team ke liye 21 ek divasiy antarrashtriya match bhi khele the jisamen 31.50 ausat se 567 ran banaae the.
