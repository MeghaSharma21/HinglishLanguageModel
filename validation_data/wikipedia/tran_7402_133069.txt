 union parishad yaa union council pakistan kaa hinatam prashasnic ikai hoti he. kramshah yah pakistan main chathe str kaa prashasnic nikaay he: yani pehle sangheey sarkaar, phir praant, phir pramandal, phir jile phir tehsil or antytah union parishad. lekin 2007 ke baad parimandal ko samaapt kar diya gaya isliye ab union parishad paanchaven-str kii ikai he. sangh parishad sthaneey sarkaar kaa sabase mahatvapurn hissaa hota he. sangh parishad main 21 parshad hote hai jinaki adhyakshata nazim or up moderator karte hai. pakistan main is samay 6000 se adhik sangh parishad hai.
