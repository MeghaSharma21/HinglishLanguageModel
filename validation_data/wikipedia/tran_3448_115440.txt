 samaan naam ke anya lekhon ke liye dekhen mandakini 
 mandakini yaa gallexi, asankhya taron kaa samooh he jo svachh or andheri raat main, akaash ke bich se jate hue ardhachakra ke rup main or jhilamilaati si mekhala ke samaan dikhaai padataa he. yah mekhala vastut: ek purna chakra kaa ang hai jiskaa kshitij ke neeche kaa bhaag nahin dikhaai padataa. bharat main ise mandakini, swarnganga, swarnadi, surandi, akashanadi, devanadi, nagavithi, harithali aadi bhi kehete hai.
 hamaari prithvi or surya jis gallexi main avasthit hai, raatri main ham nangi aankh se usee gallexi ke taaraon ko dekh pate hai. ab tak brahmad ke jitne bhaag kaa pataa chalaa he usamen lagbhag aisi hi 19 arab gallexien hone kaa anumaan he. brahmad ke visphot siddhant ke anusaar sabhi gallexien ek duusare se badi teji se dur hatati ja rahi hai.
 brahmand main sau arab gallexi astitv main he. jo badi maatra main taare, gas or khagoleey dhul ko samete hue he. gallexiyon ne apana jivan lakho varsh purv prarambh kiya or dhire dhire apane vartmaan swaroop ko praapt kiya. prayek gallexiyaan arabon taron ko ko samete hue he. gurutvkarshan taron ko ek saath band kar rakhata he or isi tarah anek gallexi ek saath milakar tara gucchh main rahati he.
 prarambh main khagolashastriyon kii dharna thi ki brahmad main nai gallexiyon or kwasron kaa janm sambhavat: puraani gallexiyon ke visphot ke falswaroop hota he. lekin yark vishvavidhyalay ke khagolashastriyon-dosi.aar. putern or doe.i rhite ne gallexiyon ke chaar samoohon kii antarkriyaaon kaa adhyayan karke is dharna kaa khandan kiya he. unhone yah bataaya ki gallexiyon ke bich main aisi visphotak antar kriyaayein nahin hoti hai jo nai gallexiyon ko janm de saken.
 adhikansh gallexiyon kaa centre taron se bharaa hua golakaar bhaag hota he, jise nabhik kaha jaataa he or yah nabhik apane chaaron ore ek taleey golakaar discs se juda hota he. khagolvigyaani gallexiyon ko unke aakaar ke aadhaar par mukhya rup se teen bhaagon main vibhajit karaten he. yah koi nahin jaanata ki kyon gallexiyaan ek nishchit rup dhaaran karti he. shaayad yah gallexiyon ke ghurnan ke veg or usame sthit taron ke banane ki gati par nirbhar karata he.
 hamaari gallexi kii chaudaai or chamak sarvatrasman nahin he. dhanu taramandal main yah sabase adhik chaudee or chamakili he. doordarshee se dekhne par gallexi main asankhya taare dikhaai padate hai. vibhinn chamak ke taron kii sankhya ginakar, unki duuri kii gananaa kar or unki gati naapakar jyotishiyon ne gallexi ke vaastavik rup kaa bahut acha anumaan laga liya he. yadi akaash main dikhaai padanevaale rup ke badale trivitiya avkaash main gallexi ke rup par vichaar kiya jaae to pataa chalata he ki gallexi lagbhag samtal vrittakar pahie ke samaan he jisaki dhuree ke paas kaa bhaag kuch phula hua he. chitra main gallexi kaa bagal se chitra dikhaaya gaya he . is pahie kaa vyas lagbhag ek lakh prakashavarsh he mile yaa prithvi se surya kii duuri kaa 63 hajaar guna) or motaai 3,000 se 6,000 prakashavarsh ke bich he. centre ke paas kii motaai lagbhag 15,000 prakashavarsh he. hamaari gallexi main taare samaan rup se vitarit nahin hai. bich bich main anek taraguchh hai or isaki bhi sambhaavna he ki devayani niharika ke samaan hamaari gallexi main bhi sarpil kundaliyaan hon. taron ke bich main sookshm dhuli or gas faili hai, jo dur ke taron kaa prakaash ksheen kar detii hai. dhuli or gas kaa ghanatv sanstha ke madhytal main adhik he. kahin kahin dhuli ke ghaney badal ho jane se kaali niharikas ban gai hai. kahin gas ke badal paas ke taron ke prakaash se uddipt hokar chamakati niharika ke rup main dikhaai padate hai. hamaari gallexi kaa dravyaman surya ke dravyaman kaa lagbhag ek kharab guna he. isamen se praaia: aadha to taron kaa dravyaman he or aadha dhuli or gas kaa.
 hamaari gallexi bich main phuli hui vrittakar puri ke samaan he. isamen ek vrutt ke bheetar hi ve sab taare hai jo hamen akaash main pruthak-pruthak dikhaai padate hai. hamaari gallexi ke chaaron ore bahut dur tak taare or taraguchh viralata se faile hue hai.
 hamaari gallexi ke centre ke paas taare sankhya main adhik ghaney hai or kinare kii ore apekshaakrit bikhare hue hai. sabhi taare centre kii parikrama kar rahe hai, centre ke nikatwale taare adhik gati se or duuravaale kam gati se. hamaara surya centre se lagbhag 30-35 hajaar prakashavarsh dur he or gallexi ke madhya tal main hai. isi kaaran apni gallexi hamen waisi mekhala kii tarah dikhaai paduti hai jiskaa upar varnan kiya gaya he. prithvi se gallexi kaa centre dhanu taramandal kii ore he. isiilie gallexi ke centre kii parikrama karata he. is parikrama main uskaa veg 150 mile prati senkad hai. is veg se bhi puri parikrama main surya ko 20 karod varsh lag jate hai.
 kuch teevr gativale taare or goleey targuch hamaari gallexi kii seemaa ke baahar hai, kintu ye bhi hamaari gallexi se sambhddha hai or usee ke ang maane jate hai lagbhag 100 goleey taraguchh gyaat hai. inkaa vitaran golakaar he. in taragucchon ke vitaran se gallexi kaa centre gyaat kiya ja saktaa he. taron kii gati naapane se bhi centre kii gananaa main sahaayta milti he. rup or vistaar main gallexi bahut si agong niharikaon se milti julati hai.
