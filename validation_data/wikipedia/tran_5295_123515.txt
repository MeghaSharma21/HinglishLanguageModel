 pracheen vaidic sahitya kii vishaal parampara main antim kadi sutaragranth hai. yah sutara-sahitya teen prakaar kaa he: shrautasutra, grihyasutra tathaa dharmasutra.
 veda dwaara pratipaadit vishyon ko samaran kar unheen ke aadhaar par aachaar-vichaar ko prakashit karanevaali shabdarashi ko "smriti" kehete hai. smriti se vihit karm smart karm hai. in karmon kii samast vidhiyaan smart sutron se niyantrit hai. smart sutara kaa namaatar grihyasutra he. ateet main veda kii anek shakhaayen thim. prayek shakha ke nimitt grihyasutra bhi honge. vartmaankaal main jo grihyasutra uplabdh hai ve apni shakha ke karmkand ko pratipaadit karte hai.
 adhikansh pramukh sutaragranthon kii rachana gautam buddha ke samakalik yug main hui jaan padati he, vidwaanon ne unke purna vikaas kaa samay 8veen sadi i. pus. or teesri sadi i. pus. ke bich mana he. 
 shiksha, kalp, vyakaran, nirukt, chand or jyotish - ye chah vedang hai. grihyasutra kii gananaa kalpasutra main kii gai he. anya paanch vedangon ke dwaara smart karm kii prakriyaayein nahin jaani ja saktin. unheen prakriyaaon evam vidhiyon ko vyavasthit rup se prakashit karne ke nimitt aacharyon evam rishiyon ne smart sutron kii rachana kii he. in smart sutron ke dwaara saptapacasanstha evam samast sanskaron ke vidhaan tathaa niyamon kaa vistaar ke saath vivechan kiya gaya he.
 saamanyat: grihyakarmon ke do vibhaag hote hai. pratham saptapacasanstha or dwitiya sanskaar. tretagni par anushthey karmon se atirikt karm 'smart karm' kahe jate hai. in smart karmon main saptapacasansthaaon kaa anushthan smart agni par vihit he. inako vahi vyakti sampadit kar saktaa he jisane grihyasutra dwaara pratipaadit vidhaan ke anusaar smart agni kaa parigrahan kiya ho. smart agni kaa vidhaan vivaah ke samay athwa paitruk sampatti ke vibhaajan ke samay ho saktaa he. aupasana, grihy athwa aavsthy, ye smart agni ke namaatar hai. yag kii ikkis sansthaaon main pehali saat paksanstha ke naam se prasiddh hai. inke naam is prakaar hai : aupasana home, vaishwadaiv, parvan, ashtaka, masishraadh, shravanakarm or shulgav. ek baar is agni kaa parigrah kar lene par jeevanaparyat uski upasna evam sangrakshan karnaa anivaary he. is prakaar se upasna karte hue jab upaask kii mrityu hoti he, tab usee agni se uskaa dah sanskaar hota he. uske ananthar us agni kaa visarjan ho jaataa he.
 garbhaadhan prabhruti sanskaar ke nimitt vihit samay tathaa shubh muhoort kaa hona aavashyak he. sanskaar ke samay agni kaa sakshya paramaavashyak he. usee agni par havan kiya jaataa he. agni or devataaon kii vividh stutiyaan or prarthanaayen hoti hai. devataaon kaa aavahan tathaa pujan hota he. sanskarya vyakti kaa abhishek hota he. uski bhalaai ke liye anek arshirvaad die jate hai. kautumbik sahabhoj, jatibhoj or brahmabhoj prabhruti mangalik vidhaan ke saath karm kii samaapti hoti he. samast grihyasutron ke sanskaar evam unke kram main ekarupata nahin he.
 vibhinn shakhaon ke grihyasutron kaa prakaashan anek sthanon se hua he. "shankhaingriysutra" rugveda kii shankhayan shakha se sambhddha he. is shakha kaa prachaar gujarat main adhik he. kaushitaki grihyasutra kaa bhi rugveda se sambandh he. shankhaingriysutra se iskaa shabdagat arthgat purnat: samya he. iskaa prakaashan madras university sanskrit granthmala se 1944 i. main hua he. aashvalyan grihyasutra rugveda kii aashvalyan shakha se sambanddh he. yah gujarat tathaa maharashtra main prachlit he.
 parskargriysutra shukla yajurveda kaa ekmaatr grihyasutra he. yah gujarati mudranalay se prakashit he.
 yahan se lougakshigrihyasutra tak samast grihyasutra krishna yajurveda kii vibhinn shakhaon se sambanddh hai. baudhayan grihyasutra ke ant main grihyaparibhaasha, grihyasheshsutra or pitrumedh sutara hai. maanav grihyasutra par ashtavakra kaa bhashya he. bharadwajgriysutra ke vibhaajak prashn hai. vaikhansasmart sutara ke vibhaajak prashn kii sankhya das hai. aapstamb grihyasutra ke vibhaajak aath patal hai. hiranyakeshigriysutra ke vibhaajak do prashn hai. vaarahgriysutra maitrayani shakha se sambanddh hai. isamen ek khand he. kathakgrihyasutra charak shakha se sambanddh he. laugakshigrihyasutra par devpal kaa bhashya he.
 gobhilgriysutra samaveda kii kauthum shakha se sambhddha he. isapar bhattanarayan kaa bhashya he. isamen chaar prapathak hai. pratham main nau or shesh main das das condikaaye hai. kalakatta sanskrit series se 1936 i. main prakashit hai. drahyayangriysutra, jaiminigrihyasutra or kauthum grihyasutra samaveda se sambhddha he. khaadirgrihyasutra bhi samaveda se sambhddha grihyasutra he.
 kochikagrihyasutra kaa sambandh atharvaveda se he. ye sab grihyasutra vibhinn sthalon se prakashit hai.
 shrautasutron ke varnya vishay yagyon ke vidhi-vidhaan or dharmik prakriyaaon se sambandhit hai. saadhaaran samaaj ke liye unka vishesh mahatv na tha. grihy or dharmasutron kii rachana kaa uddeshy saamaajik, paariwarik, raajnneetik or vidhi sambandhi niyamon kaa nirupan he. tatsambandhi pracheen bhartiya avasthaaon kii jaankaree main unka bahut bada aitihaasik mulya he. grihyasutron main mukhya hai: katyayan, aapstamb, boudhayan, gobhil, khadir or shankhayan. inke alag alag siddhant sampradaay the parantu kabhi-kabhi un sabamein varnit niyam samaan hai. sambhav hai, unke bhed sthaneey or bhugolic kaarnon se rahe hon or is drushti unhen samasamyik bharat ke anyaanya pradeshon kaa pratinidhi mana ja saktaa he. grihyasutra shrout nahin maane jate balki smart samajhe jate hai or ve paariwarik tathaa saamaajik niyamon kii parampara ko vyakt karte hai. grihy sutron main paariwarik jivan se sambandhit sanskaron kaa vivechan he or ve kaise kiye jane chahie, iske purna vidhi vidhaan die gaye hai. garbhaadhan se aarambh kar antyeshti tak solah sanskaron kaa vidhaan grihyasutron ke yug se hi apane purna viksit rup main bhartiya jivan kaa ang ban gaya tathaa un sanskaron kii dharmik or darshanik bhaawanaaon kaa vikaas hua. aaj bhi ye sanskaar, jinamein mukhya jatakarm upanayan, vivaah or antyeshti maane ja sakate hai, hindu jivan main bada sthaan rakhte hai. par in sanskaar vyavasthaaon ke saath hi grihyasutron main kabhi andhvishwaason ko bhi shaamil kar liya gaya he grihasth jivan se sambandhit kuch anya dharmik kartavyon kii bhi unamem charchaa he.
