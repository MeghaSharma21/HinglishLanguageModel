 hindu puranic kathaon anusaar, jwarasur bukhaar ke daanav or sheetala devi kaa jeevanasathi he.
 ek puranic katha ke anusaar, jwarasuraka janm chintnamay shivaji ke lalatake pasinese hua tha or vah devataaon ke liye ek khatra tha. ek baar vishnu haygrivaka avatar liya tha tab vah jwarasurake bukhaarase peedit the. isliye unhone daanav jwarasurako maar daalaa. unhone apane sudarshan chakrase uske teen tukade kar diye. haalanki, brahmae jwarasuraka punerjeevit kar diya or uske tino hisson ko jod diya. lekin us samay uske tino hissonmen se prayek ke sir or angakaa vikaas ho gaya tha. is prakaar jwarasur teen chehre or teen pairo vaala dikhaai detaa he or ek hi baar main sabhi dishaao main sthanantarit karne kii ek asaadhaaran kshamta rakhata he. baad main ve sheetala devi ke jeevanasathi ke rup chune gaye..
 ek anya katha ke anusaar, maan durga devi katyayani ke rup main avatar liya or kalkeya dwaara bheje gaye kai rakshason ko maar daalaa.
 jwarasur naamake bukhaarake daanavne, katyayanike bachapan ke dostomen laailaaj bimaariyaan jaise kii haija, atisar, khasra, chechak jaise rog failaana shuru kar diya. isliye katyayani apane bimariose grast dostonka ilaaj kiya. uske baad puri duniyaako bukhaar or rogonse raahat dene ke liye katyayanine sheetala devika rup liya. unke chaar haath main se ek haathamein chota jhaadu, duusare haathamein pankhaa,tisare haathamein thande panika bartan or chauthe haathamein pine kaa pyaalaa rakhti he. unhone apni shakti kaa istemaal sabhi bacchon ke rogon ke ilaaj karne ke liye kiya. uske baad katyayanine apane dost batukako aagrah kiya ki vah baahar jaaen or rakshas jwarasur ke saath yuddh kare.isliye yuva batuka or daanav jwarasur ke bich yuddh shuru ho gaya.daanav jwarasur batukako haraane main safal ho gaya. uske baad batuka mrit gir pada. jwarasur hairaan ho gaya tha kyoki batuka kahin dikh nahi rahaa tha. par usko pataa hi nahi chalaa kii batukane ek bhayanak purushaka rup le liya tha. is purush kii teen ankhen or chaar haath the.sir rakshas jesa or uske paas kulhadi, talwaar, trishul tha. unka rang andhera jesa kaala tha. uske bal ud rahe the. ankhen krodhse jal rahi thi. unhone baagh kii twachaa ore khopadioki malas pahani thi. batukane shivaji kaa ek bhayanak avatar kalbhairavaka rup le liya tha. uske baad kalbhairav jwarasuraki ninda karte he or usko bataate ki vah maan durga devi ke sevak he. un donomen ek lambii bahes hoti he, lekin vah phir ladaai main parivartit jaati he. jwarasur apni shaktike madhyam se kai rakshason utpnan hue, lekin sabhi ko kalbhairavane nasht kar diya. ant main, kalbhairavane jwarasurake saath mallayuddh kiya or baadamein apane trishulase usko maar daalaa.
 sheetala devi-jwarasur kaa panth bengali samskritimein vyapak rup se lokapriy he. sanyog se, bengali, oriya or hindi bhashaaon, bukhaarko jwara ke rup main janaa jaataa he or asur kaa arth he rakshas hota he. jwarasuraka naam do shabdon kaa ek sangyojan he. - jwara or asur - jwarasur. is prakaar, jwarasur bukhaarka rakshas he.esa kaha jaataa he ki sheetala devi or jwarasur apane gadhe par ek saath yatra karte he. jwarasur yuvan sevakke rup main hai. uttari bharat ke sabhi gaanvake logon jwarasuraki patni sheetala deviko chechak or bukhaarake rogonmen rakshak ke rup main puja jaataa he..
 buddha samskritimein jwarasurako parnshabari pati ke rup main dikhaaya gaya he. kuch tasviromen devataaonko varjayogini ke prakope se bachane ke liye dur udte bataae jate he..
