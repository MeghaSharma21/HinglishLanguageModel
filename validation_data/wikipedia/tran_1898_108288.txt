 vaigyanik satyam singh ke anusaar- paudhon se bhojan, eendhan, chaara evam tantu kii prapti ke vigyaan evam praudyogiki ko sasyavigyaan kehete hai. sasyavigyaan ke antargat padap anuvaanshiki, padap kriyavigyaan, mausamavigyaan tathaa mrudaa vigyaan samahit hai. yah jeevigyaan, rasayan vigyaan, arthshaastra, paryaavaran, mrudaa vigyaan tathaa anuvanshiki aadi vishyon kaa sammilit anuprayog he. vartmaan main sasyavaigyanik anekon kaaryon main sanlagn hai, jaise- ann utpaadan, adhik swasthkar bhojan kaa utpaadan, padpon se urja kaa utpaadan aadi. sasyavaigyanik praayah sasy aavartan, sinchai evam jalanikaas, padap prajnan, padapkaryiki, mrudaa-vargeekaran, mrudaa-urvarakta, kharpatvaar-prabandhan, keet-prabandhan aadi main visheshgyata praapt karte hai.
