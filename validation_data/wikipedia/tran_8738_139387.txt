 aiz you like it wiliam shakespeare dwaara likhit ek pastoral kamedi he, jise 1599 yaa 1600 kii shuruuaat main likhaa hua maanate hai or yah 1623 ke pholio main pehali baar prakashit hua. yah kriti thomas loj ke gadhy prem-katha roslind par aadhaarit thi. haalanki is naatak ke pehle pradarshan kii jaankaree anishchit he, parantu sujhaavon ke anusaar, 1603 main wilton house main iske ek pradarshan kii sambhaavna jataai gai he. aiz you like it kii kahani nayika roslind ke ird-gird gumti he, jo utpeedan ke dar se apni chacheri bahan celia or mahal ke vidooshak tochastone ke saath, apane chachaa ke mahal se arden ke jangal main suraksha or antat pyaar paane ke liye bhaag jaati he. aitihaasik rup se, is par aalochakon kii raay bhinn rahi he, kuch aalochakon ke anusaar yah kriti shakespeare kii anya kritiyon kii tulana main kam utkrisht he jabaki kuch is naatak ko behad utkrisht kriti maanate hai.
 shakespeare kii ek sarvaadhik prasiddh or aksar-prayukt ukti "ol di varlad iz a stage" isi naatak kaa hissaa he, saath hi yah "tu much of a good thing" vaakyansh kaa mul bhi he. yah naatak darshakon kii pasandida bani rahi or radio, film or sangitamay rangamanch ke liye roopantarit kii gai.

 france main duk kaa kshetra is naatak kii prishthbhumi he, lekin adhikansh ghatnaayen 'arden ke jangal' main hi ghatati hai.
 fredrick ne apane bade bhai, duk senior ke kshetra ko dhokhe se chin liya or use nirvasit kar diya. duk kii beti roslind ko mahal main rahane kii anumati di gai kyonki vah fredrick kii akeli santaan celia kii kareebi dost or chacheri bahan bhi he. orlando, jo rajya kaa ek sajjan yuvak he or jise roslind se pehali hi najar main pyaar ho gaya he, apane bade bhai, oliver dwaara sataae jane ke baad apane ghar se palaayan karne ko majaboor ho jaataa he. fredrick krodhit ho jaataa he or roslind ko sabha se baahar nikaal detaa he. celia or roslind vidooshak tochastone ke saath bhaagane kaa nirnay leti hai, jis dauraan roslind ek yuva purush kaa chhadam vesh dhaaran karti he.
 roslind, jo ab genimad ke chhadam rup main he or celia jo eliena ke chhadam vesh main he, arkedian arden ke jangal main pahunchate hai, jahaan nirvasit, duk apane kuch samarthakon ke saath rahete hai, in samarthakon main "udhaas jacks" bhi shaamil he jiskaa parichay hamen tab miltaa he jab vah ek hiran ke vadh par rota he. "genimad" or "eliena" duk or unke saathiyon se turant nahin milte hai, kyonki ve pehle corin naamak ek garib kiraayedaar se milte hai or usase apane maalik ke kachche ghar ko kharidne kii peshkash karte hai.
 orlando or uskaa naukar adams, is bich, duk or uske saathiyon se milte hai or unke saath hi rahane lagate hai or roslind ke liye ekatarfa pranaygit pedo par chhodane lagate hai. roslind ko bhi orlando se prem he or vah usase genimad ke rup main milti he or use prem rog se bachaane ke liye paraamarsh dene kaa dhong karti he. genimad kahata he ki "vah" roslind kii jagah "legaa" or "vah" or orlando apane sambandh ko naatakeey rup se nibhaayenge.
 is bich, gadarin febe, jisase silvius prem karata he, genimad se prem karne lagati he, jabaki "genimad" vastav main yah zaahir karata he ki use febe main koi dilchaspee nahin he. nandak tochastone bhi ek mudh-buddhi bakrii-charaane vaali ladki audrey kii ore pranayshil pahal karata he or usase vivaah karne kaa prayaas karata he, par usase pehle hi anuchit hastkshep karne vaale jacks dwaara uski yojana vifal kar di jaati he.
 ant main, silvius, febe, genimad or orlando is bahes main ulajh jate hai ki koun kise praapt karegaa. genimad kahata he ki vah is samasya ko sulajhaa legaa, vah orlando or febe se yah vaada letaa he ki yadi febe genimad se vivaah nahin kar pai to ve kramshah roslind or silvius se shaadi kar lenge. agle din, genimad swayan ko roslind ke rup main zaahir karata he or chunki febe kaa prem galat sabit hota he, use silvius ko apnaanaa padataa he.
 orlando jangal main oliver ko dekhtaa he or ek sherni se uski raksha karata he, jiske falswaroop oliver ko orlando ke saath kiye gaye apane durvyavhaar par pachhtaawaa hota he . oliver kii mulaakaat eliena se hoti he or vah usase pyaar karne lagata he or ve vivaah karne kaa nirnay karte hai. orlando or roslind, oliver or celia, silvius or febe or tochastone or audrey sabhi kaa antim drishy main vivaah ho jaataa he, jiske baad unhen pataa chalata he ki fredrick ko bhi apni galatiyon par pachhtaawaa he or vah apane sachche bhai ko duk kii padavi lautaane or swayan ek dharmik jivan jeene kaa nirnay letaa he. jacks, jo sadaa hi udhaas rahata he, mahal main lautne ke unke nimantran ko thukraa detaa he or jangal main rah kar ek dharmik jivan apnaane kaa vichaar karata he.
 4 agast 1600 ko yah naatak stationurs company ke register main darj kiya gaya, lekin 1623 main first pholio main shaamil hone se pehle tak use mudrit nahin kiya gaya tha.
 arden sambhawatah shakespeare ke shahar stretford-apon-evan ke paas ke ek jangal kaa sthaan-naam he. oxford kaa shakespeare sanskaran tarksangat tareeke se is bhugolic visangati kii vyakhya yah maanate hue karata he ki 'arden' keval france ke vanachhadit kshetra ardennas kaa angrejikaran he or ise pratibimbit karne ke liye uske akshar vinyas ko badla gaya. anya sanskaran shakespeare ke 'arden' vartanee ko hi maan kar chalaten hai, taaki yah tark diya ja sake ki gramin style ek vilakshan duniya ko chitrit karti he jahaan bhugolic vivaran apraasangik hote hai. shakespeare ke arden sanskaran kaa yah suzav he ki 'arden' naam kii utpatti arkedia ke parmparik kshetra or bible ke garden of eden ke sangyojan se hui he, kyonki naatak main parmparik or isai manyataon kaa majboot parsparik prabhaav he. iske atirikt, shakespeare kii maa kaa naam meri arden tha or jangal kaa naam is prakaar dwiarthi bhi ho saktaa he.
 punaruddhar ke purv vahaan iske pradarshan kaa koi bhi abhilekh nahin hai. tathaapi, iskaa ek sambhavit pradarshan, wiltshire ke wilton house main ho saktaa he, jo pembroke ke arls kii gramin jagah thi. pembroke ke tisare arl wiliam herbert ne oktober se disambar 1603 tak wilton house main james 1 or unke darbariyon kii mezbaani kii, jabaki jacobean landan bubonic plig kii mahamari se peedit tha. shahi logon ko wilton house main aane or 2 disambar 1603 ko raja or darbariyon ke samaksh pradarshan karne ke liye 30 pound kaa bhugtan kiya gaya. harbart kii ek paariwarik parampara kaa yah maananaa he ki us raat pradarshit naatak aiz you like it hi thi.
 angrezi punaruddhar ke dauraan, raja ke sahyogiyon ko 1669 main shahi adhipatr dwaara yah naatak saunpa gaya tha. 1723 main iskaa druri len main lav in a forest ke naam se roopantaran pradarshit kiye jane kii jaankaree he; jisamen jacks kaa kirdaar kole sibar ne nibhaayaa tha. satraa varsh baad ek or druri len nirmaan shakesperian paath main lautaa .
 aiz you like it ke ullekhneey haalia pradarshnon main 1936 kaa edith ivance abhinit old vik theatre prastuti or 1961 kaa venesa redgrev abhinit shakespeare memorial prastuti shaamil he. sarvaadhik lambey samay tak chalane vaali broadway prastuti main catherine hepabern ne roslind kii, chloris lichaman ne celia kii, wiliam prince ne orlando kii or arnest thesigar ne jacks kii bhoomika nibhaai or ise maaikel benthal dwaara nirdeshit kiya gaya. 1950 main iske 145 pradarshan hue. iskaa ek or ullekhneey pradarshan stretford, onterio main 2005 ke stretford mahotsav main kiya gaya, onterio ko 1960 ke dashak main banaaya gaya or isamen beranaced ladies dwaara likhe sangeet par vinyast shakespeare ke bolon ko vishesh rup se prastut kiya jaataa tha.
 vidwaanon ne lambe samay tak is naatak kii khoobiyon ke bare main asahmati jataai. aalochak samul johnson se lekar jorge barnard shaw tak sabhi ne yah asantosh prakat kiya ki aij you like it main vah kalaatmakataa lupt he jisamen shakespeare sakasham the. shaw sochate the ki shakespeare ne is naatak ko aam logon ko santusht karne ke liye likhaa hoga or is kriti ke bare main apane madhyam vichaar kaa sanket use aiz you like it kehete hue diya - maano is raay se naatakkaar sahamat nahin the. tolstoy ne paatro kii aniatikta or tochastone ke nirantar moorkhtapurna vyavhaar par aapatti jataai. anya aalochakon ne is kriti main mahaan saahitiyik mulyon ko paaya. herold bloom ne likhaa ki roslind shakespeare ke mahila paatro main sabase mahaan or sabase purnataa praapt mahila paatr he. aalochanatmak vivaadon ke baavjood, yah naatak shakespeare kii ek sarvaadhik mahatvapurn or praayah pradarshit kii jane vaali hasyapradhan naatak bani hui he.
 kahani main vistrut ling vyutkraman ling adhyayanon main ruchi rakhane vaale aadhunik aalochakon ke liye vishesh rup se ruchikar hai. naatak ke chaar adhyyon main, roslind - shakespeare ke jamaane main jo bhoomika ladakon dwaara nibhaai jaati thi - swayan ko ladke ke rup main vesh badalanaa aavashyak samajhati he, jabaki gramin mahila febe, "genimad" ke prati aakarshith ho jaati he, jiske naam main ek samalangikkaamuktha kii dhwani he. vastav main, roslind dwaara darshakon se bataaye gayi upsanhaar main, spasht rup se yah prakat hota he ki vah ek mahila nahin he.
 visconsin vishvavidhyalay ke professor richard novles ne, jo 1977 main naatak ke new verioram sanskaran ke sampadak hai, apane lekh "myth and tip in aiz you like it" main varnit kiya he ki kaise is naatak main puranic sandarbh shaamil hai visheshkar eden, harculis or christ ke sandarbh main. haalanki, ve roopak-sambandhi koi thos arth nikaal paane main asamarth rahe or isliye yah nishkarsh nikaala ki yah ek roopak naatak nahin ho saktaa he. baharhaal, anya vidwaanon kaa daawa tha ki vastav main is naatak main lagaataar ek vyanjanapurna arth he or ise prastuti main badla ja saktaa he.
 ank do, drishy 7 main, shakespeare kaa ek sabase prasiddh ekalaap shaamil he, jo kahata he:
 yah prasiddh ekalaap jacks dwaara bolaa jaataa he. isamen aakarshak pariklpana or alankaar he jisase kendriya rupakalankar kaa vikaas ho sake, jiske anusaar: ek vyakti kaa jeevankaal saat ankon vaala naatak he. ye ank, "saat dashaaen," "dai kii baahon main kilkaariyaan bharte or kai karte shishu" se shuru hoti hai or chah suspasht maukhik chitrankan ke madhyam se aage badhate hue "duusare bachapan or niri vismriti main/ bedaant, netraheen, beswad, bina sabake" charam bindu par pahunchati he.
 pastoral kamedi kii vishay vastu hoti he gramin parivesh main apane har rup main prem, orlando kii kritrim bhavukta kii tulana main roslind dwaara moort vaastavik prem or apratyaashit ghatnaayen jo shahari darbariyon ko pravaas, santvanaa yaa swatantraaa kii talash main bhatkaati he, jangal main achaanak hone vaale muthbhedon ke silsile se adhik avastavik nahin he, jo vinodpurn parihaas uttejit karti hai or jise aalekhan kii baariki or charitra vikaas kii aavashyaktaa nahi hoti he. pehle ank kaa mukhya kaary-vyapaar kisi kushti ke khel se badhkar or kuch nahin he or puraa abhinay ek geet se praayah baadhit hota rahata he. ant main, khud hamen vivaah utsav main aashirwad dene aata he.
 wiliam shakespeare dwaara rachit naatak aiz you like it spasht rup se ek gramin romance style ke antargat aati he; parantu shakespeare keval style kaa istemaal hi nahin karte, balki unhen viksit karte the. shakespeare ne aiz you like it ko likhane ke liye gramin style kaa prayog isliye bhi kiya, taaki ve dukh va anyaay ko paidaa karne vaali saamaajik prathaon par ek aalochanatmak drushti daal saken or asaamaajik, murkh or aatmghaatee vyavhaar kaa mazaaq uda saken, sarvaadhik spasht rup se prem kii vastu-vishay ke zariye, jo paramparaagat alabhya premiyon kii avadharana kii asweekruti main parinat hota he. 
 paaramparik paristhitiyon main paariwarik paatr shakespeare va unke darshakon ke liye parichit vishay-vastu thi; vah to keval hajir javaabi or vishay kii vyapaktaa he jo paath ko budhimatta pradaan karti he, jo kaarywahi par ek tazaa mohar lagaati hai. naatak ke centre main roslind kii ashaavadita kii tulana jacks ke stri virodhatmak shok ke saath kii gai he. baad main shakespeare ne kuch vishay-vastuon ko adhik gambhirtha se liya: anyaayi duk or nirvasit duk ne meshar for meshar or tempest ko vishay-vastu pradaan kiya.
 kai nirdeshkon ne yah paaya ki, ek naatak jo jangal main achaanak muthbhedon or kai uljhe prem-sambandhon par aadhaarit he or jisamen sabhi ghatnaayen ek shant gramin parivesh main ghatati hai, vishesh rup se baahar kisi baag yaa uk jaisi jagahon par prabhavi rahegi.
 donnovain ne "under di greenwood tree" ke sangeet ko taiyaar kiya or a gift from a flower tu a garden ke liye 1968 main record kiya.
 thomas morle ne "it waz a lover and hiss las" ke liye sangeet kii rachana kii, ve or shakespeare ek hi muhalle main rahete the or ve kabhi-kabhi shakespeare ke naatakon ke liye sangeet rachana kiya karte the.
 america rajya minesota ke WCAL radio stesan ke itihaas main, aiz you like it radio par prasaarit kii jane vaali sarvapratham naatak rahi hogi. yah 1922 main prasaarit hui thi.
 aiz you like it laurence oliver kii pehali shakespeare film thi, haalanki uskaa nirmaan or nirdesan na kar ke, unhone keval abhinay kiya tha. britain main nirmit or 1936 main pradarshit, is film main nirdeshak paul zinner kii patni elizabeth bergenar ne apane german bhashaa ke mote uccharan ke saath roslind kii bhoomika nibhaai thi. haalanki yah film, lagbhag usee samay bani a midsmar nits dream or romeo or juuliyat sanskarnon se kam hollywood kii rangath liye thi or haalanki usamen kaam karne vaale sabhi kalakaar shakesperian abhinayta hi the, yah film oliver yaa aalochakon dwaara safal nahin maani gayi.
 basil cholemon dwaara nirdeshit aiz you like it ke 1978 BBC video tepe sanskaran main hellen miren ne roslind kii bhoomika nibhaai.
 1992 main, christin adjard ne is naatak kaa ek or film roopantaran banaaya. isamen, james fox, siril cusac, andrew tiarnan, griff rahis jones or even bramnar ne bhoomikaayen nibhaain. kriyakalapon ko ek aadhunik or feeki shahari duniya main sthanantarit kiya gaya he.
 19veen sadi ke japan main set or kenneth branagh dwaara nirdeshit aiz you like it kaa sanskaran jari kiya gaya. isamen brice dailas howard, david oyelovo, romola gerai, alfred molina, kevin cline or bryan blessd ne abhinay kiya. haalanki yah vastav main cinemagharon ke liye banaai gayi thi, lekin ise keval europe ke theatron main hi jari kiya gaya or 2007 main america main HBO par iskaa premier hua.
 dainiel equisito or saimi bak ne naatak ko "like you like it" sheershak ke saath 80 ke dashak kii sangeet-vishyak roopantaran main badla.
