 jabal libanaan​​ praant lebanon kaa ek praant he. iske naam kaa matlab 'lebanon parvat' he, lekin vastav main lebanon parvatamala kaa mukhya bhaag is praant se nahin guzarataa or uttar praant main sthit he. is praant main isai bahusankhyak hai or maronai, unani parmparik or malikiyin unani catholic sampradaayon main bante hai. inke alaava yahan muslim or druj log bhi basate hai. 87.3% log isai hai, 8.5% druj hai, 2.3% sunni muslim hai or 1.9% shiya muslim hai.
