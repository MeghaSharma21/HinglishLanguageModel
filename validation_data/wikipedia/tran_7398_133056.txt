 brihadaaranyak upanishat shukla yajurveda se judaa ek upanishad he. advait vedant or sanyasanishtha kaa pratipadak he. upanishdon main sarvaadhik brihadaakaar iske 6 adhyay, 47 brahman or pralambit 435 padon kaa shanti paath 'uun purnamad:' ityadi he or brahma isaki sampradaay panrapara ke pravartak hai.
 yah ati pracheen he or isamen jeev, brahmand or brahman ke bare main kai bate kahin gayi he. darshanik rup se mahatvapurn is upanishad par aadi shankaracharya ne bhi tikaa likhi thi. yah shatapath brahman granth kaa ek khand he or isko shatapath brahman ke paath main sammilit kiya jaataa he. 
 yajurveda ke prasiddh purush sookt ke atirikt isamen ashwamedh, asato maa sadgamay, neti neti jaise vishay hai. isamen rishi yajyavlky or maitreyi kaa samvaad he jo atyant krambaddh or yuktipurna he.
 iske naam kaa arth 'behad gyaan vaala' yaa 'ghaney jangalon main likhaa gaya' upanishad he. isamen tatvagyan or tadupayogi karm tathaa upasnaon kaa bada hi sundar varnan he.
 brihadaaranyak upanishad advait vedant or sanyasanishtha kaa pratipadak he. upanishdon main sarvaadhik brihadaakaar iske 3 kand, 6 adhyay, 47 brahman or pralambit 435 padon kaa shanti paath 'uun purnamad:' ityadi he or brahma isaki sampradaay panrapara ke pravartak hai.
 is upanishad kaa brahmanirupanatmak adhikansh un vyakhyaaon kaa samuchachya he jinase ajatshatru ne gargy balaaki kii, jaiwali pravaahan ne shwethaketu kii, yajyavlky ne maitreyi or janak kii tathaa janak ke yajna main samavet gargi or jartkarav aartbhaag ityadi aath manishiyon kii brahmajigyaasa nivrutt kii thi.
 is upanishad ke anusaar sristi ke pehle keval brahm tha. vah avyaakrit tha. usne ahankaar kiya jisase usne vyaakrit sristi utpann kii; do pairavaale, chaar pairavaale, poor usne banaae or unamem pakshi banakar paith gaya. usne apni maaya se bahut rup dhaaran kiye or is prakaar naanaa rup se bhasman brahmand kii rachana karke usamen nakhagr se shiksha tak anupravisht ho gaya. sharir main jo aatma he vahi brahmad main vyaapt he or hamen jo naanaa prakaar kaa bhaan hota he vah brahm rup he. prithvi, jal, or agni usee ke moort evam vaayu tathaa akaash amoort rup hai.
 stri, santan athwa jis kisi se manushya prem karata he vah vastut: apane liye karata he. astu, yah aatma kya he, ise dhoondhna chahie, gyaniyon se iske vishay main sunanaa, iskaa manan karnaa or samadhi main saakshaatkaar karnaa hi param purusharth he.
 'chakshurvai satyam' arthath aankh dekhi baat satya maanane kii lokadharna ke vichaar se jagat satya he, parantu vah pratyaksht: anitya or parivartansheel he or nishchay hi uske mul main sthit tatv nity or avikari he. atev mul tatv ko 'satya kaa satya' athwa amrit kehete hai. nashvan 'satya' se amrit dhankaa hua he.
 agyaan arthath aatmaswaroop ko na jaanane ke kaaran manushya sansar ke naanaa prakaar ke vyapaaron main lipta hua sansaarik vitt aadi nashwan padaarthon se akshay sukh kii vyarth aashaa karata he. kaamanamay hone se jis uddeshy kii vah kaamana karata he tadrup ho jaataa hai; punya karmon se punyavan or paap karmon se paapi hota or mrityu kaal main uske praan utkraman karke karmanusaar mrityulok, pitrulok athwa devloak praapt karte hai. jis devtaa kii vah upasna karata he maano usee kaa pashu ho jaataa he. yah agyaan aatma kii 'mahati vinshti' he.
 aatma or brahm ek hai. brahm ke atirikt kuch nahin he. jise nanaatv dikhataa he vah mrityu se mrityu kii ore badhata he. aatma mahan, anant, apaar, avinashi, anuchhittidharma or vigyaandhan he. namak kii dali pani main ghul jane par ekares ho jane se jaise namak or pani kaa abhed ho jaataa he, brahmatmaiky tadrup abhedatmak he. jise samay sadhak ho yah aparokshanubhuti ho jaati he ki main brahm hoon or bhutatmaayen or main ek hoon uske drashtaa or drushti, gyaata or gyey ityadi bhed villin ho jate hai, or vah 'brahm bhavateey evam veda' - brahmabhoot ho jaataa he. uske praan utkraman nahin karte, vah yaheen jivanmukt ho jaataa he. vah vidhi nishedh ke pare he. use sanyas lekar bhikshyacharya kerani chahie. yah gyaan kii parmavadhi, aatma kii param gati or paramanand he jiskaa ansh praaniyon kaa jeevanastrot he.
 yah shok-moh-rahit, vijvar or vilakshan anand kii sthiti he jisase brahm ko 'vigyanmanandambrahm' kaha gaya he. yah swaroop man or indriyon ke agochar or keval samadhi main pratyakshanubhuti kaa vishay evam namrup se pare hone ke kaaran, brahm kaa 'neti neti' shabdon dwaara antim nirdesh he.
 aatmasaakshaatkaar ke liye vedanubandhan, yajna, daan or tapopavasadi se chittashuddhi karke surya, chandra, vidyut, akaash, vaayu, jal ityadi athwa pranroop se brahm kii upasna kaa nirdesh karte hue aatmachintan sarvshereshth upasna batalaai gai he.
