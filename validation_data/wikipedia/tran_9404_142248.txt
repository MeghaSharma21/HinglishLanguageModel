 kaibar darra yaa darr-e-kaibar uttar-pashchimi pakistan kii seemaa or afghanistan ke kabulistaan maidan ke bich hindukush ke safed koh parvat shrunkhlaa main sthit ek prakhyat aitihaasik darra he. yah darra 50 kimi lanbaa he or iskaa sabase sankaraa bhaag keval 10 foot chaudaa he. yah sankaraa maarg 600 se 1000 foot kii oonchaai par bal khaataa hua brihadaakaar parvaton ke bich kho sa jaataa he. is darre ke zariye bhartiya upmahadweep or madhya asia ke bich aayaa-jaaya saktaa he or isane donon kshetron ke itihaas par gahari chhap chodi he. kaibar darre kaa sabase unchaa sthaan pakistan ke sangheey prashasit kabyli kshetra kii landi kotal naamak basti ke paas padataa he. is darre ke ird-gird pashtun log basate hai.
 peshawar se kabul tak is darre se hokar ab ek sadak ban gai he. yah sadak chattani oosar maidan se hoti hui jamaruud se, jo angreji sena kii chhavnee thi or jahaan ab pakistani sena rahati he, teen mile aage shaadibagiyaar ke paas pahaado main pravesh karti he or yaheen se khaybar darra aarambh hota he. kuch dur tak sadak ek khadd main se hokar jaati he phir bai or shangai ke pathaar kii ore uthati he. is sthaan se ali masajid durg dikhaai padataa he jo darre ke lagbhag bichobich oonchaai par sthit he. yah durg anek abhiyaanon kaa lakshya rahaa he. pashchim kii ore aage badhati hui sadak dahini ore gumti he or tede-medhe dhalaan se hoti hui ali masajid kii nadi main utar kar uske kinare-kinare chalati he. yaheen khaybar darre kaa sankaraa bhaag he jo keval pandrah foot chaudaa he or oonchaai main 2,000 foot he. 5 kimi aage badhane par ghati chaudee hone lagati he. is ghati ke donon or chote-chote gaanv or jakkakhel afridiyon kii lagbhag saath minaaren he. iske aage loargi kaa pathaar aata he jo 10 kimi lanbaa he or uski adhiktam chaudaai teen mile he. yah landi kotal main jaakar samaapt hota he. yahan angarejon ke kaal kaa ek durg he. yahan se afghanistan kaa maidani bhaag dikhaai detaa he. landi kotal se aage sadak choti pahadiyon ke bich se hoti hui kabul nadi ko chuumati dakka pahunchati he. yah maarg ab itnaa prashast ho gaya he ki choti laariyaan or motargadiyaan kabul tak saralata se ja sakati hai. iske atirikt landi khaanaa tak, jise khaybar kaa pashchim kaha jaataa he, railmarg bhi ban gaya he. is railmarg kaa bananaa 1925 main aarambh hua tha.
 samrik drushti main sansar bhar main yah darra sabase adhik mahatv kaa samajha jaataa rahaa he. bharat ke 'pravesh dwaar' ke rup main iske saath anek smritiyaan judi hui hai. samajha jaataa he ki sikandar ke samay se lekar bahut baad tak jitne bhi aakraamak shaq-pallav, bakhtri, yavan, mahmud gazani, changez khan, taimur, babar aadi bharat aae unhone isi darre ke maarg se pravesh kiya. kintu yah baat purnatah satya nahin he. darre kii durgamata or is pradesh ke uddand nivisiyon ke kaaran is maarg se sabake liye bahut saal tak pravesh sahaj na tha. bharat aanevaale adhikansh akramanakari yaa to baluchistan hokar aae yaa se ghumkar jalalabad ke raste kabul nadi ke uttar hokar aae jahaan se pravesh adhik sugam rahaa he.
 aitihaasik rup se is darre par jiskaa bhi niyantran hota he use bhartiya upmahadweep or madhya asia donon main dabil dene kii kshamta hoti he. is kaaran isapar hameshaa kheenchaatani rahi he or samay-samay par isapar maury samrajya, parthia, mogul samrajya, sikh samrajya, british raaj, aadi kaa qabza rahaa he. iske zariye hakhaamani samrajya ke kurosh, unani samrajya ke sikandar, skithi log, kushaan, mohammad gori, mogul samrajya kaa sansthaapak babar or bahut se anya aakraamak bharat main daakhil hue hai. vipareet disha main maury, sikh or british aakraamakon ne is se guzarakar bharat se afghanistan par dhaava bolaa he. ati-pracheen kaal main, manushyon ke africa main utpann hone ke baad, jab ve africa se asia main faile to bhi unhone kaibar or bolan darron ke prayog se bharat main pravesh kiya. bahut se bhartiya or dakshin-purvi asia ke log in darron se guzare hue logon ke vanshaj hai.
