 chicago america ke ilinay praant kaa pashchim-madhya main sabase bada shahar he tees lakh aabaadi vaala yah shahar america kaa teesraa sabase badi aabaadi vaala shahar he. michigan jhil ke dakshini nonk par sthit chicago vishwa kaa sabase bada railway jankshan he. yahan tees se bhi adhik railmarg milte hai. michigan jhil par sthit hone ke kaaran yah ek uttam bandargah kaa bhi kaary karata he. yah makka peti ke uttari sire par sthit he. yah sansar main galla or maance kii sabase badi mandi he. yahan vishwa ke sabase adhik pashu kaate jate hai. isi se ise vishwa kaa kasaaikhana kehete hai. yahan se makka khilaakar motaa kiye hue jaanwaron ko katkar unka maance dibbon main bharkar baahar bheja jaataa he. yahan krishi-yantron, aataa pisane, kaagaj, lugdi, ispaat, motor gadiyon ke upkaran, vayuyan ke purjon, vastra evam jalayaan kaa nirmaan kiya jaataa he. dakshini chicago main telshodhak kendra he. chicago metropalitin kshetra jise bolchaal kii bhashaa main chicagoland ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he amareeki parivahan vyavastha, amareeki samskruti, amareeki raajnneeti, amareeki shiksha or amareeki arthvyavastha kaa ek bada centre he. chicago ko pashchim madhya america kii vyavsaayik or saanskrithik rajdhani bhi kehete hai. is shahar kii sthapana 1833 main lake michigan evam mississippi nadi ke bich kii gayi thi. is kshetra ke nikat sthit gairi louh-ispaat udyog kaa kendra he.
