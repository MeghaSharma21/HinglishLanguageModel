 tran rajvansh vietnam kaa ek rajvansh tha jo san 1225 i main shuru hua or 1400 i tak chalaa. iskaa armbh 1225 main samraat tran thai tong ke rajsinghaasana praapt hone ke saath hua jab unke chachaa tran thu do ne lee rajvansh ke antim shaasha, 8 varshiya rani lee chiu hoang, ko gaddi se hataa diya. tran raajaaon ke kaal main uttar se mongol samrajya ne teen baar vietnam par akraman kiya or teenon baar unhen waapas dhakel diya gaya. isamen 1288 kaa baach dang nadi kaa yuddh nirnaayak mana jaataa he. 1400 main 5 varshiya tran theu de samraat tha or use apane naanaa ho kui lay ko gaddi deni padi, jiske saath tran rajvansh kaa kaal samaapt ho gaya.
