 jorkul sarovar, jise sar-e-kol sarovar bhi kehete hai, pamir parvatamala main afghanistan or tajikistan kii antarrashtriya seemaa par sthit ek jhil he. purv se pashchim tak faili is jhil kii lambaai lagbhag 25 kimi he. madhya asia kii prasiddh nadi aamu dariyaa isi sarovar se shuru hoti he. sarovar ke purvi chor par qaareh bulaaq naam kaa ek chota sa afgaan qasbaa he.
