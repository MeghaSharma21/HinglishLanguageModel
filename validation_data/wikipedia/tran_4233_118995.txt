 woolfgaan pauli, nobel puraskaar vijeta tathaa austrian-swiss bhautikavid the. 
 inkaa janm vienna nagar main hua tha. shiksha munich main hui. copenhagen, hensburg evam jurik main inhone anek mahatvapurn shaikshik padon par kaary kiya. 1935 evam 1940 i. main princeton vishvavidhyalay main bhautikee ke atithi praadhyaapak rahe.
 inhone kwantmavaad par mahatvapurn kaary kiya tathaa pauli apvarjan siddhant kii khoj kar prasiddhi praapt kii. is siddhant ke anusaar samaan kwantum sankhyaon vaale electronon kaa astitv nahin hota. kisi urja str main adhiktam electronon kii sankhya, is siddhant dwaara niyat kii jaati he. is mahatvapurn khoj ke liye, san 1945 main, aapako bhautikee main nobel puraskaar praapt hua. ye siddhant parmaanu evam nabhikeey vigyaan main purnroopen vyaapt he. tatwon kaa aavart vargeekaran bhi isi siddhant se samajhaayaa ja saktaa he. prayek electron ko kuch kwantum sankhyaon se pradarshit kiya jaataa he.
 inhone metrics yantriki ko aage badhaane main mahatvapurn yogadan diya. aap hi pratham vaigyanik the, jisane hydrogen parmaanu ke electron chakkar par metrics yantriki prayukt kii. inaki mrityu 15 disambar 1958, ko hui.
