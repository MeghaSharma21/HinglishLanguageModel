 agastavestland helicopter ghotaalaa bharat dwaara agastavestland company se kharide ja rahe helicopteron se sambandhit he. yah 2013-14 main saamne aayaa. isamen kai bhartiya rajanetaaon evam sainya adhikariyon par agastavestland se moti ghoose lene kaa aarope he.
 upie-1 sarkaar ke samay agasta westland se viviipi ke liye 12 helicopteron kii kharid kaa sauda hua tha. yah sauda 3,600 karod rupye kaa tha. isamen 360 karod rupye kii rishwatakhori kii baat saamne ai jiske baad upie sarkaar ne sauda radd kar diya. - espi tyaagi sahit 13 logon par cese darj kiya gaya tha. jis baithak main helicopter kii keemat tay kii gai thi, usamen upie sarkaar ke kuch mantri bhi maujuud the. is kaaran congress par bhi sawaal uthe the. is faisle main congress ke kai netaaon ke naam bhi liye hai.
 april 2014 main italy ke ek nyaayalay ke faisle ke anusaar agasta saude main ghotaalaa hua tha. adaatal ne company finamekkanika ko doshi paaya he. ek angreji samachar patr main chhapi khabar ke anusaar, court ne apane aadesh main finamakenika kii adhinsth company agasta westland ke purv siio bruno speganolini ko bhi saade chaar saal jail kii sajaa sunai he. court ne bhrashtachar ke maamle main oresi ko court se raahat dene vaale aadesh ko badal bhi diya. bhartiya raksha mantralay ke sutron ke anusaar, oresi ko mili sajaa se antarrashtriya nyaayalay main company ke khilaaf bharat kaa cese majboot hoga. is faisle main bharat ke tatkaaleen air chif kaa naam bhi liya gaya he. 
 iske purv italy kii ek adaalat ne may 2014 main italy ke bainkon main jamaa 1818 karod rupye kii bank guarantee bharat ko lene kii anumati de di thi. is par agasta westland company antarrashtriya nyaayalay kii sharan main chali gai or supreme court ke purv nyaayamurti bi en shrikrishna ko madhyasth ke taur par namit kiya. is par bharat ne nyaayamurti bi pi jivan reddy ko doosraa madhyasth niyukt kiya. sitambar 2014 main jab donon paksh tisare madhyasth par sahamat nahin hue to antarrashtriya nyaayalay ne professor wiliam park ko maamle ke niptaare ke liye niyukt kiya.
