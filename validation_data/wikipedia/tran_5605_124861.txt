 ek mudra  ) hindu dharm or buddha dharm main ek pratikatmak yaa aanushthanik bhav yaa bhav-bhangima he. jabaki kuch mudraon main puraa sharir shaamil rahata he, lekin zyaadaatar mudraaen haathon or ungaliyon se kii jaati hai. ek mudra ek aadhyatmik bhav-bhangima he or bhartiya dharm tathaa dharm or taowad kii paramparaon ke pratimaa shaastra va aadhyatmik karm main niojit pramanikta kii ek urjavan chhap he.
 niyamit tantric anushthanon main ek sau aath mudraon kaa prayog hota he. 
 yoga main, aam taur par jab vajrasana kii mudra main baitha jaataa he, tab saans ke saath shaamil sharir ke vibhinn bhaagon ko santulit rakhane ke liye or sharir main praan ke pravaah ko prabhavit karne ke liye mudraon kaa prayog pranayam ke sangyojan ke saath kiya jaataa he.
 november 2009 main rashtriya vigyaan academy main prakashit ek shodh aalekh main dikhaaya gaya he ki haath kii mudraaen mastishk ke usee kshetra ko uttejit yaa protsaahit karti hai jo bhashaa kii hai.
 chini anuwaad yin  yaa yinaksiyang  he. japani or korian vartanee "in" he.
 bhartiya upmahadweep kii hindu or buddha kala ke pratimaa shaastra main mudra kaa prayog kiya jaataa he or dharmagranthon main ise varnit bhi kiya gaya he, jaise ki naatyashaastra, jisamen 24 asaṁyuta or 13 saṁyuta mudraon kii suchi he. aamtaur par donon haath or ungaliyon se mudra avasthaaen banati hai. asana ke saath, ve dhyaan main sthir bhav se or hindu dharm ke Nāṭya abhyaas main gatisheel rup se niojit hoti hai. prayek mudra kaa abhyaas karne vaale par vishesh prabhaav padataa he. saamaanya haath mudraaen hindu or buddha donon ke pratimaa shaastra main dekhi jaati hai. kuch kshetron main, udhaaharan ke liye thailand or laos main, ye ek-duusare se bhinn hai, lekin sambandhit pratimaa shaastra prathaen prayukt hoti hai.
 hevajr tantra par apni tippanee main jamgon kongtrul ne kaha he, pratikatmak asthi aabhushanon ) ko bhi "mudra" yaa "cills" ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
 bhartiya shastreey nrity main "hast mudra" shabdaawalee prayukt hoti he. bhartiya shastreey nrity ke sabhi roopon main mudraaen samaan hai, haalanki unke naam or unka upyog bhinn hua karte hai. bharatnatyam main 28, kathakali main 24 or odissi main 20 mul mudraaen hoti hai. ek haath, do haath, baanh kii gatividhiyon, sharir tathaa chehre kii abhivyaktiyon kii tarah ye mul mudraaen vibhinn tareekon se sanyukt hoti hai. kathakali main, jisamen sabase adhik sankhya main sangyojan hua karte hai, shabdaawalee lagbhag 900 shabdon kii he.
 mudraaen yoga abhyaas kaa moulik rup hai, udhaaharan ke liye, bihar skool of yoga dwaara prakashit asana, pranayam, mudra, bandha sabase prasiddh pustake hai.
 donon haathon ke anguuthe or tarjaniyaan shunya kii tarah judy hoti hai. shesh ungliyaan faili rahati hai, jabaki madhyamaa tarjani ke bagaiyr mude hisse ko chooti rahati hai. vajrasana main baithe hone par hatheliyaan neeche kii ore jaangh par rakhi hoti hai. yah mudra madhyapat ko sakriy karti he, gahare "udar-shwasan" ke liye sahaayak hoti he, kyonki saans lene par yah jab pedu kii ore neeche jaati he tab madhyapat andruni angon ko dhakelti he. 5-2-4-2 kii lay main ek dhime layaaddh shwasan se pedu or pairon main praan kaa pravaah hota he.
 anguuthe or tarjani chin mudra jaise hi hai. baaki ungaliyon kii ek mutthi ban jaati hai. tarjani kaa bina muda hissaa or madhyamaa abhi bhi sparsh kiye hue hona chahie. jaise ki chin mudra main hota he, vajrasana main baithe rahakar hatheliyaan neeche kii ore jaangh par rakhi hoti hai. yah mudra pasaliyon ko sakriy karti he, isse saans lenaa par ek taraf se inkaa vistaar hota he. 5-2-4-2 kii lay main dhimi gati se layaaddh shwasan se dhad or shwaas nalika main praan pravaah hota he.
 angootha hatheli main kanishtha ke muladhar ko choota hua hota he. baaki ungliyaan mutthi banaate hue anguuthe par mudi hui hoti hai. chin mudra kii tarah, vajrasana main baith kar hatheliyaan neeche kii ore jaangh par rakhi hoti hai. yah mudra ant:shwasan karne par chaati kaa vistaar karte hue ansapeshi mansapeshiyon ko sakriy karti hai. 5-2-4-2 kii lay main dhimi gati se layaaddh shwasan se praan kaa pravaah shwasanalika or sir main hota he.
 hatheliyaan aadi mudra main hoti hai, lekin hatheliyon ke saamne kaa hissaa upar kii ore or daahine or baayen haath kii ungaliyon ke jod choote hue naabhi ke samtal main sthit hoti hai. ise vajrasana main baithe kar kiya jaataa he. shwasan purna ho jaataa he: ant:shwasan main, madhyapat neeche kii ore utarataa he, tab pasaliyaan failati hai or tab ansapeshi mansapeshiyaan aage kii ore badhati hai. uchhshwasan yaa saans chhodana isi kram main hota he, jo ek "lahar" yaa lahar prabhaav paidaa karata he. 5-2-4-2 kii lay main ek dhimi layaaddh shwasan puure sharir main praan kaa pravaah karti he.
 ek jatil mudra haath kii mudraon kaa sangyojan karti he, saans chakra ke andar mudra se mudra kii gatividhi or dhyaan ko sankalith karti hai. siddhasana main baithkar mudra kaa abhyaas kiya jaataa he. yahaan tak ki is mudra main saans kaa ek chakra bhi sharir ko protsaahit karne ke liye kaaphi ho saktaa he. hiroshi motoyama dwaara likhit pustak chakra ke siddhant main ise varnit kiya gaya he.
 abhaya mudra suraksha, shanti, paroapkaar or bhay ko dur karne kaa pratinidhitiv karata he. theraoda main, aamtaur par dahinaa haath kandhe kii oonchai tak uthaa hota he, baanh mudi hoti he or hatheli baahar kii ore hua karti he or ungliyaan upar kii ore tani tathaa ek-duusare se judy hoti hai or baayan haath khadey rahane par neeche kii ore lataka hota he. thailand or laos main, yah mudra chalate hue buddha se sambhddha he, aksar donon haathon ko uthaakar doguna abhaya mudra main dikhaaya jaataa he, yah aparivartansheel he. kisi ajanabi ke saamne dosti kaa prastaav rakhane ke achche iraade ke pratik ke rup main yah mudra shaayad buddha dharm kii shuruyaat se pehle istemaal kiya jaataa rahaa tha. gandhar kala main, upadesh dete samay ise dekha gaya he. 4thi or 7veen sadi ke vei or sui kaal main iskaa prayog china main bhi hota rahaa he. ek hathi ke hamalaa karne par buddha dwaara is mudra kaa prayog karke use shant kiya gaya tha, vibhinn bhittichitron or aalekhon main ise dikhaaya gaya he. mahayan main, uttari sampradaay ke devataaon ko aksar duusare haath kaa upyog karke anya mudra ke saath dikhaaya jaataa he. japan main, jab abhaya mudra kaa prayog kiya jaataa he tab bich kii ungali ko thodaa aage kii ore nikaal diya jaataa he, yah shingon sampradaay kaa ek pratik he. ; chini: shivuvei yin )
 bodh gaya main shakyamuni buddha kii gyanoday kii gavaahi ke liye is mudra ko prithvi par bulaayaa gaya. baithi hui murti kaa dahinaa haath bhoomi kii ore failaa hua he, hatheli apni ore he.
 apni gyanoday ke baad sarnath ke hiran park main buddha jab apana pehla upadesh de rahe the, dharmachakr mudra buddha ke jivan ke us ek mukhya pal kaa pratinidhitiv karata he. saamanytah, maitrey ko bachaane ke liye kanoon ke vitarak ke rup main keval gautam buddha ko hi is mudra main dikhaaya gaya he. mudra kii yah avasthaa dharm ke ghoomte chakra kaa pratinidhitiv karti he. dharmachakr mudra tab banati he jab chaati ke saamne donon haath vitark main jude hote hai, daayin hatheli aage or baayin hatheli upar kii ore hoti he, badhati hua karti he.bharat main ajanta kii bhittichitron kii tarah vibhinn tarah kii vividhtaayen bhi hai, jahaan donon haath alag-alag hai or ungliyaan ek-duusare ko sparsh nahin kar rahi hai. bharat-greek style ke gandhar main daahine haath kii bhinchi hui mutthi kii ungliyaan prakat rup se baayen haath ke anguuthe ke saath judy hui lagati hai. japan main horyu-ji kii chitramay patrika main daahine haath ko baayen par aropit dikhaaya gaya he. japan kii amitabh kii kuch murtiyon main 9veen sadi se pehle is mudra kaa upyog karata dikhaaya gaya he., chikichi-jo, hoshin-seppo-in ; chini: juanfalun yin )
 dhyaan mudra dhyaan kii mudra he, jo acchhee bhaavana or kii ekaagratha ke liye he. donon haath god par rakhe hote hai, dahinaa haath baayen par hota he or ungliyaan puri tarah se faili hoti hai tathaa hatheliyaan upar kii ore, ek trikon kaa nirmaan hota he, jo aadhyatmik agni yaa triratn, teen aabhushan kaa pratik he.shakyamuni buddha or amitabh buddha ke abhyavedan main is mudra kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. kabhi-kabhi dhyaan mudra kaa prayog chikitsa buddha ke rup main kuch abhyavedan Bhaiṣajyaguru main kiya jaataa he, jisame unke haathon main aushadhi kaa paatr hota he. isaki paidaaish bharat main hui bahut sambhav gandhar main or ve kaal ke dauraan china main. buddha se bahut pehle is mudra kaa upyog hua karata tha, ekaagratha, chikitsa or dhyaan abhyaason ke dauraan yogi iskaa prayog kiya karte the.dakshin purv asia ke theraoda buddha dharm main iskaa bahut adhik prayog hua he, isamen anguuthon ko hatheliyon par rakhaa jaataa he., jokai jo-in ; chini: ding yin .)
 varad mudra chadhaava, satkar, daan, madad, dayaa or eemaandaaree kaa pratik he. yah lagbhag hameshaa ek aise sammanit vyaktitv dwaara baayan haath dikhaakar kiya jaataa he, jo lobh, krodh or kapat se maanav mukti ke liye samarpit he. ise vakr baanh ke saath kiya ja saktaa he or hatheli ko thodaa upar kar diya jaay yaa agar baanh neeche kii ore ho to upar kii ore yaa jaraa mudi hui ungaliyon ke saath hatheli ho. daahine haath kaa upyog karke anya mudra ke bina varad mudra shaayad hi kabhi dekhi gayi he, khaas taur par abhaya mudra. aksar hi vitark mudra ke saath isamen bhram ho jaataa he, jo isse bahut hi miltaa-julata he. china or japan main kramash: vei or asuka kaal ke dauraan ungliyaan sakht hua karti thim or iske baad dhire-dhire samay ke saath viksit hote hue yah dhili padane lageen, antat: tang rajvansh tak aate-aate ye unglia swaabhaavik rup se mudane lageen. bharat main 4thi va 5veen shatabdiyon main gupt kaal se hi mudra kaa upyog awalokiteshwar kii tasveeron main hua karata tha. varad mudra kaa upyog bade paimaane par dakshin-purv asia kii murtiyon main hota rahaa he. gyaan kii mudra he. daahine haath se mutthi bana kar tarjani ko upar kii ore prasaarit karke ise kiya jaataa he or tarjani ko dhankate hue baayen haath se mutthi banaakar bhi ise kiya jaataa he. bajra mudra kaa sabase badhia anuprayog nine cilebel cills kii saatvin takanic he, ek anushthan ke anuprayog main mantra ke saath is mudra kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. man ko pavitra avasthaa par le jane ke liye yah sanskrit prarthana kaa video he, iske baad kuji-in sanskaar kaa drut sanskaran he, japani kanji uccharan kaa upyog kiya karte he .
 vitark mudra buddha kii shiksha par bahes or sanchaaran kii mudra he. is mudra ko anguuthe or tarjani ke poron ko ek saath jod kar or shesh ungaliyon ko sidhaa rakh kar, bahut kuch abhaya or varad mudraon kii tarah, lekin angootha tarjani kaa sparsh karte hue ise banaayi jaati he. purv asia main mahayan buddha dharm main is mudra ke bahut saare bhinn rup hai. yab-yama main devataaon dwaara kuch vibhinnataaon ke saath tibet main yah taron or bodhisattva kii rahasywaadee mudra he. ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa hamefrajñāliṅganabhinaya, japani main: 'seppo-in, en-ai-in, chini: anvei yin )
 gyaan mudra anguuthe or tarjani ke por ko ek saath sparsh karke, gheraa bana kar banaayi jaati he, tathaa hatheli ke saath haath hriday kii ore hota he.
 karan mudra vah mudra he jo dushtatma ko baahar nikaalati he or beemaari tathaa nakaaratmak vichaaron jaisi baadhaon ko dur hataati he. yah tarjani or kanishtha ungali ko uthaane or anya ungaliyon ko modakar banati he. yah lagbhag kai pashchimi deshon main korna ke rup main jaani janevaali mudra kii hi tarah he, antar yah he ki karan main angootha madhyamaa or anamika ko pakadaa nahin hota he. .
 purvi roodhiwaadee or catholic sanskaron or jhandphoonk ke pavitra anushthanon, pavitra jal ke nirmaan, abhishek, vapatism, param prasad or mangalakamna paavan mudra se jude hai.
 hindu dharm or buddha dharm parampara main baanh, haath or shaareerik sthiti kaa upyog mudraon main hota he. aitihaasik buddha mudraon kaa upyog jaanate the or aksar in aanushthanik mudraon kaa upyog karte hue unhen darshaya gaya he. kung phu ke vibhinn prakaaron main in mudraon kii sthitiyon kii pahchaan hoti he.
 muromoto apane marshall art prashikshan ke sambandh main mudraon par apane anubhav kii charchaa karte hue mikkyo, tendai or shingon kaa sandarbh dete hai: 
 bahut sari ajeeb chizon main ek he jisase meraa saamana apane marshall art prashikshan ke dauraan hua, vah he yuyutsu kala main mudraon kaa upyog. mudra, unke liye jo inase parichit nahin hai, haathon ke ajeeb se ishaare hai jinaki utpatti gudh buddha dharm, vishesh rup se tendai or shingon sampradaayon se hui he. mana jaataa he ki ye mudraaen aadhyatmik centre or shakti paidaa karti hai, iske baad yah kisi tarah bahya rup main prakat hoti hai.
 muromoto marshall arts main mudra ke kram kaa varnan karte he or koryu, rayu, conto tenshin shoden katori shinto-rayu,, risuke otech or don epa. dreger kii yaad taaja karte hai:
 kisi bhi maamle main, koryu main main mudra kaa upyog jaan gaya tha tab se jab main tenshin shoden kyori shinto-rayu, otech risuke or swa. don epa. dregar ke saath prashikshan le rahaa tha. otech senseyi ne apni paddati main kuch mudraon kaa varnan kiya tha, jo puraane marshall rayu main se ek he, jo conto japan main aaj bhi astitv main he.
 japani ladaaku samskruti main mudra ke aitihaasik sahyogi kram sarini main muromoto shinto, samurai, tokugava sarkaar, nav kanfushivaad, zen buddha dharm, kamakura kaal, ido, takuen or hakuin ko shaamil kar lete hai: 
 bahut saare koreau main mudra kaa upyog or mikkyo ke anya pahaluon ke kai udhaaharan paye jate hai, kyonki mikkyo or shinto saamuraai ke dharm hai, jisane un rayu ko sthapit kiya, jise 1600 kii sadi se pehle rachaa gaya tha. baad main tokugava sarkaar, jo prachand rup se nav kanfushivaad se prabhavit rahi or baad main jine buddha dharm se; ke aane ke baad rayu ko viksit kiya gaya. haalanki 1300 kii sadi main, kamakura kaal main yoddha varg ke bich jine lokapriy hua, ido kaal ke baad ke samay tak marshall arts jine purohiton takuen or hakuin ke lekhan se prabhavit nahin hua. or yahaan tak ki ido kaal main marshall arts samaan rup se kanfushivaad se prabhavit tha or isi tarah rahasyamayi shindo ke baad ke hisse main bhi.
 muromoto ne paath kii drushti se nakshaa bana kar shuto mudra kaa nishpaadan kiya:
 mikkyo vibhinn anushthanon, mantrochcharan or isi tarah ke kaamon ke saath aksar mudra sangyojan kar upyog karte hai. ek aam mudra "nif hand" yaa shuto he. pehali do ungaliyon ko prasaarit kiya jaataa he, jabaki angootha or anya ungaliyon ko bheench liya jaataa he. kuch koreau kata main yah gatividhi gudh rup se chhipi hoti he, agar tum kareeb se dekho, to khaastaur par tum ise tenshin shoden katori shinto-rayu paddati main yaa divya buddha pratimaaon main dekh sakate ho. yah gyanoday kii talwaar kaa pratinidhitiv karata he, jo sabhi bhram kaa nivaaran karata he. kabhi-kabhi prasaarit ungaliyon ke poron ko duusare haath kii mutthi main band kar liya jaataa he. iskaa ek pratikatmak arth hota he jo mikkyo se utpann hua he.
