 kohistaan pakistan ke kaibar-pakhtuunakva praant ke purvottaree bhaag main sthit ek purv zila tha. is zile kii rajdhani dasu naamak basti he. varsh 2014 main ise dwibhajit kar, upree kohistaan zila or nichalaa kohistaan zila bana diya gaya.
 
 'koh' farsi bhashaa kaa ek shabd he jiskaa matlab 'parvat' hota he. anya hind-irani bhashaaon main yahi shabd pashto main 'gar', pracheen avastai bhashaa main 'gairi' or sanskrit main 'giri' ke rup main sajaateey shabd ke taur par miltaa he. kohistaan zile main bahut se pahaad hai, jis se yah shabd aayaa he. 
 kohistaan zile main san 1998 main 4,72,570 logon ki aabaadi thi. iskaa kshetrafal qareeb 7,492 varg kimi he. yahan zyaadaatar kohistaani bhashaa or anya dardi bhashaaon ke saath pashto bolne vaale bhi rahete hai. adhiktar log dharm se sunni muslim hai haalanki ek alpsankhyak shiya muslim samudaay bhi yahan miltaa he. kohistaan aarthik rup se ek pichhadaa or aviksit ilaaka samajha jaataa he. 2006 main is zile kaa sakshartaa dar sirf 20% mapa gaya tha. adhiktar log krishi yaa maveshi paalan se jivan basar karte hai or garibi vyapak he. zile kii zamin pahari yaa phir shushk hone se kheti main kathinaayee pesh aati he. paneer, makkhan, oon or lakdi yahan ke mukhya utpaadan hai. is zile se visheshkar akhrot kii lakdi anya kshetron main bheji jaati he. kohistaan zila karakoram rajmarg ke raste main aata he or yahan se yah paak-adhikrut kashmir main sthit gilgit shahar kii taraf nikalataa he.
