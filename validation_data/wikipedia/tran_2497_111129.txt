 tablas dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh ke romablon dweepasmooh va praant kaa sabase bada dweep he, jo prashasnic drushti se mimaropa kshetra kaa bhaag he. philipinoz par spani upniveshi qabza hone se pehle is dweep kaa naam ocigon hua karata tha or yah apane mom ke utpaadan ke liye prasiddh tha. aadhunik kaal main tablas par 9 nagarpalikaay hai or isaki arthvyavastha falon va sabziyon kii krishi or matsyodyog par aadhaarit he.
