 lakshminandan bora asamiya bhashaa ke suprasiddh sahityakar hai. bharat ke assam rajya main sthit naugaanv jile ke kujidah gaanv main janmen lakshminandan bora jorhat ke assam krishi vishvavidhyalay main krishi evam mausam vigyaan vibhaag ke vibhaagadhyaksh hai. unki kriti pataal bhairavi ko 1988 main sahitya academy puraskaar pradaan kiya ja chukaa he. unhen birla foundation dwaara 2008 ke saraswati sammaan se bhi sammanit kiya gaya he. yah sammaan unhen 2002 main prakashit upanyaas kayakalp ke liye diya gaya. ve ab tak 56 pustaken likh chuke hai, jisamen upanyaas, kahani sangrah, ekanki, yatra vrittant or jeevani shaamil he. saraswati sammaan se alankrita hone vaale ve pehle asamiya sahityakar hai.
