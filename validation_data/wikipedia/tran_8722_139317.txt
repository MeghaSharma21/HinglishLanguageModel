 hungary, aadhikarik taur par hungary ganraajya, madhya europe ke panonian basin main sthit ek sthal-ruddha desh he. iske uttar main slovakia, purv main ukraine or romania, dakshin main srbija or kroeshia, dakshin pashchim main slovenia or pashchim main austria sthit he. isaki rajdhani or sabase bada shahar budapest he. hungary, europea sangh, nato, oisidi or visegrad samooh kaa sadsy he or ek shengan raashtra he. isaki aadhikarik bhashaa hungarian he, jo phinno-ugrik bhashaa parivaar kaa hissaa he or europe main sabase vyapak rup se boli jane vaali gaiyr bhaaropee bhashaa he.
 hungary duniya ke tees sabase adhik lokapriy paryaton sthalon main se ek he or prati varsh lagbhag 8.6 lakh paryatakon ko aakarshith karata he. desh main vishwa kii sabase badi garm jal kii gupha pranali sthit he or garm jal kii sabase badi jhiilon main se ek heviz jhil yaheen par sthit he. iske saath madhya europe kii sabase badi jhil balaaton jhil bhi yaheen par he or europe ke sabase bade prakritik ghaas ke maidan hortobagi bhi hungary ke adhikaar kshetra main aate hai. 
 hungary kii sarkaar ek sansadeey ganatantr he, jise 1989 main sthapit kiya gaya tha. hungary kii arthvyavastha ek ucch-aay arthvyavastha he or kuch kshetron main yah ek kshetreey agua he.
  
 keltic or roman kaal ke baad, hungary kii neenv 9 veen shatabdi ke ant main hungary ke shaasha arpad ne rakhi thi, jinke praputra saint stephen pratham kii tajposhi ke liye pope ne san 1000 main, rome se taj bheja tha. hungary kii yah rajshahi 946 saal tak chali, is samay ise pashchimi duniya ke saanskrithik kendron main se ek mana jaataa tha.aanshik rup se otoman samrajya ke adheen lagbhag 150 varsh tak rahane ke baad, hungary ko habsburg rajshahi ke rup main ekikrut kiya gaya or baad main yah austro-hungary dohari rajshahi kaa aadha hissaa bana. pratham vishwa yuddh ke ant tak hungary ek mahaan shakti tha, haalanki truyanon sandhi jisaki sharton ko adhiktar hungary vaasi aaj bhi jarurat se zyada sakht maanate hai, ke kaaran hungary ko apane kshetra kaa 70% se adhik hissaa or hungary jaateeyata kii apni ek tihaai jansankhya ko khona padaa. iske baad hungary main communist yug kaa sutarapaat hua jiske dauraan hungary, 1956 kii kranti or 1989 main austria ke saath apni seemaa kholane ke praathmik kadam ke chalate bade paimaane par antarrashtriya charchaa kaa kendra bana rahaa. hungary ke is kadam ne purvi block ke teevr patan main sahyog kiya. hungary main sarkaar kaa maujuuda swaroop ek sansadeey ganatantr he, jise 1989 main sthapit kiya gaya tha. aaj, hungary ek ucch-aay arthvyavastha he or kuch kshetron main ek kshetreey agua he.

 desh kii jalwaayu shushk he. sheetkaal main adhik sardi or greeshmakaal main adhik garmi padati he. nunatam taap 4rou sein. or adhiktam taap 36rou sein. se bhi adhik ho jaataa he. pahari jilon main ausat varsha 1016 mimi or maidani jilon main 381 mimi hoti he. sabase adhik varsha jaade main hoti he jo kheti ke liye haniprad nahin hoti he.
 raashtra kii aadhe se adhik aay krishi se hoti he. danyube nadi ke maidaanon main makka, gehun, jau, rai aadi anaajon ke atirikt aalu, chukundar pyaas or san bhi ugaae jate hai. chukundar se chini banaai jaati he. yahan achche fal bhi ugte hai. angoor se ek vishisht prakaar kii sharaab toke banaai jaati he. maidaanon main charaagaah hai jahaan hiran, boar or khargosh aadi pashu paale jate hai. peprarika naamak mirch hoti he. yahan ke wanon main chaude pattevaale ped, ok, bich, aish tathaa chestnut paae jate hai.
 desh main khanij dhan adhik nahin he. lohe, mangneej or aluminium ke kuch khanij nikaale jate hai. lohe ke khanij nimna koti ke hai. kuch petroleum evam prakritik gas bhi nikalati he. lignite koyala bhi yahan nikaala jaataa he. jalavidyut ke utpaadan ke sadhano kaa yahan bahut abhaav he.
 aataa pisane ke anek kaarkaane hai. sharaab paryaapt pariman main banati he or baahar bheji jaati he. chini kaa parishkar mahatv kaa udyog he. san se bhi anek saamaan taiyaar kiye jate hai. niryaat kii vastuon main boar, murgiyaan, sooti vastra, aataa, chini, makkhan, taaje fal, makka, sharaab, oon or cement aadi hai. aayaat kii vastuon main kachi rooyee, koyala, imarti lakdi, namak aadi hai. choti choti mashine bhi yahan banati hai or unka niryaat hota he. yahan kaa vyapaar soviet rus, checkoslovakia, germany, poland, yugoslavia aadi se hota he.
 hungary ke adhivaasiyon ko magyaar kehete hai. lagbhag 90 pratishat magyaar hi yahan rahete hai; shesh jansankhya main german, slowe, romanian, croat, sarv or gypsy hai. lagbhag aadhi jansankhya nagaron main rahati he. hungary kii kul jansankhya 1,00,50,000 he. yahan ke nivasee swatantra prakruti ke or aanevaale hote hai. inke loakgeet or nrity suprasiddh hai. yahan ke log rangabirange vastra pahante hai or swaadisht bhojan karte hai1 yahan ke rasoie jagat prasiddh hai. yahan ke nivasee footbal, tennis, ghudsawaari, tairaaki aadi ke shaukeen hai.
 hungary ke 68 pratishat nivasee romancatholic, 27 pratishat protestant tathaa shesh yahudee evam anya dharmavalambee hai. yahan kii bhashaa magyaar hai.
 hungary main 8800 kimi lambii rail, sadaken, 60800 kimi lambey rajmarg or 1920 kimi lanbaa naugamya jalmaarg he. yahan kaa hawaii adda bahut bada he or samast europea deshon se sambhddha he. railmarg bhi anya europea deshon se sambhddha he. desh ke andar bhi paryaapt viksit vaayu yatayaat hahai.
