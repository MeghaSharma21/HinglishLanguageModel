 parasiveeyon ke adhyayan ko paraseete vigyaan kehete hai|
 paraseete vigyaan parajeevon sahit unke mejabaan, tathaa unke bich ke sambandh kaa adhyayan he. jeev vigyaan ke vishay ke taur par, paraseete vigyaan kaa vistaar, charchit praani yaa uske vaataavaran par nirbhar nahin hota, varan unke jivan ke tareeke par nirbhar hota he. atev yah anya vishyon ke, sangyojan se bana he; va kai up-vishyon kii takneekon par aadhaarit he, jaise koshika jaiviki, soochna-jaiviki, jaivaraciniki anvik jaiviki, immunology, anuvaanshiki, vikaswad evam paristhitiki.
 jivan kii paddati prithvi par sarv saamaanya he. jisamen sabhi pradhan texa ke pratinidhi sammilit hai, jo saadharantam ek koshikeey jeev se lekar jatil kasherukiyon tak ho sakate hai. prayek mukt-jeev jati kii apni anupam paraseete prajatiyaan hotin hai.
 in vibhinn prakaar ke jeevon ke adhyayan main; vishay, choti va saadharantar ikaiyon main bant jaataa he, jin par adhik dhyanakarshan hota he, va jo samaan takneekein prayog karte hai, bhale hi adhyayankarta samaan rog yaa jeevon kaa adhyayan naa kar rahen hon. is vigyaan main atyadhik shodh ke parinamswaroop yah in do yaa adhik paribhaashaon se varnit ho paata he. saamanytah procarots kaa adhyayan jeevanu vigyaan ke antargat aata he, naa ki paraseete vigyaan ke. 
 
 paraseete vigyaan kii pracheenatam shrenee, ayurvigyaan yaa chikitsa kshetra se sambandhit he. yah maanav ko prabhavit/sankramit karne vaale parjiviyon kaa adhyayan hota he. isamen nimna kshetra aate hai:-
 chikitsa parjaiviki se aushadhi vikaas, mahamari vigyaan va xunosis ke adhyayan sambandhit hai. 
 jo paraseete krishi aadi ko aarthik haani pahunchaate hai, yaa pashuon main sankraman karte hai; unka adhyayan pashu parjaiviki kehalaata he. isamen adhyyan kii jane vaali prajatiyon main se kuch hai:
 amphestoms
 host vishishton ke bich, paraseete ek sangrahit vitaran darshaate hai, is prakaar parjiviyon kaa bahulya, alpsankhyak host main rahete hai. is gun ke kaaran, parjivijna unnat jaevsankhyikee pranaliyaan prayog kar sakate/pate hai.
 yah parjiviyon ke proteinon kii sanrachana kaa adhyayan hota he. parjaivik protein sanrachana kaa nirdhaaran yah jaanane main madad karata he, ki ye protein kis prakaar maanvon main upasthit homologus proteinon se bhinn karyrat he. saath hi protein sanrachana aushadhi khoj kii prakriya main sahaayak hotin hai. 
 paraseete host jansankhya paristhitiki ke bare main soochna bhi uplabdh karaate hai. matsay udyog jaiviki main, udaa0 paraseete samudaay ko samaan matsay upjati kii vishisht jansankhya ko pahchaanane ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he. iske saath hi parjiviyon main vishesh gunon kii sangati tathaa jivan style hoti he, jo unhen host main sthaan banane ko agrasar karti va sahaayak hotin hai. parjaivik paristhitiki ke pahaluon ko samajhnaa host dwaara paraseete se bachane kii ranneeti par prakaash daal saktaa he.
 parjiviyon main vrahat bhinnata, jeev vaigyanikon ke liye unka varnan karnaa tathaa unhen namavali baddh karnaa ek badi chunauti upasthit karti he. haal hi main hue vibhinn jatiyon ko pruthak karne, pahchaanane va vibhinn texonomi paimaanon par unke vibhinn samoohon ke bich sambandh dhudhane hetu diene prayog parjaivagyon ke liye atyadhik mahatvapurn va sahaayak rahe hai.
