 apatkaaleen prabandhan ek antahvishayak kshetra kaa saamaanya naam he jo kisi sanghathan kii mahatvapurn aastiyon kii aapadaa yaa vipatti utpann karne vaale khatarnaak jokhimon se raksha karne or suniyojit jeevankaal main unki nirantarata sunishchit karne ke liye prayukt samrik sanghathanaatmak prabandhan prakriyaaon se sambandhit he. aastiyaan sajiv, nirjeev, saanskrithik yaa aarthik ke rup main vargeekrut hai. khataron ko prakritik yaa maanav-nirmit kaarnon ke dwaara vargeekrut kiya jaataa he. prakriyaaon kii pahchaan ke uddeshy se sampurna samrik prabandhan kii prakriya ko chaar kshetron main baanta gaya he. ye chaar kshetra saamaanya rup se jokhim newnikaran, khatre kaa saamana karne ke liye sansadhano ko taiyaar karne, khatre kii vajah se hue vaastavik nuksaan kaa uttar dene or aage ke nuksaan ko simit karne or yathasambhav khatre kii ghatana se yathapurv sthiti main lautne se sambandhit hai. kshetra saarvajanik or nijee donon main hota he, prakriya ek si sanjhi hoti he lekin dhyaan centre vibhinn hote hai. apatkaaleen prabandhan prakriya ek nitigat prakriya na hokar ek ranneetik prakriya he atah yah aamtaur par sanghathan main kaaryakari str tak hi simit rahati he. saamaanya rup se isaki koi pratyaksh shakti nahin he lekin yah sunishchit karne ke liye ki ek sanghathan ke sabhi bhaag ek saanjhe lakshya par dhyaan kendrit karen, yah salaahkaar ke rup main yaa kaaryon ke samanvay ke liye kaary karata he. prabhavi apaat prabandhan sanghathan ke sabhi staron par apatkaaleen yojanaon ke sampurna ekikaran or is samaj par nirbhar karata he ki sanghathan ke nimnatam str apaat sthiti ke prabandhan or upree str se atirikt sansaadhan or sahaayta praapt karne ke liye jimmedaar hai.
 kaarykram kaa sanchaalan karne vaale sanghathan ke sabase varisht vyakti ko saamaanya rup se ek apatkaaleen prabandhak yaa kshetra main prayukt shabd se vyutpann par aadhaarit kaha jaataa he.
 is paribhaasha ke antargat shaamil kshetra hai:
 prabandhan kii prakruti sthaneey aarthik or saamaajik sthiti par nirbhar karti he. fred kani jaise kuch aapadaa raahat visheshgyon ne ullekh kiya he ki ek tarah se ekmaatr asli aapadaa aarthik hoti he. kani jaise visheshgyon ne bahut pehle note kiya ki apatkaaleen prabandhan chakra main buniyadi dhaanche, saarvajanik jaagrukta or yahan tak ki maanav nyaay ke muddon par dirghakaalik kaam ko shaamil karnaa chahie. apatkaaleen prabandhan kii prakriya ke chaar charan hai: newnikaran, tatparta, pratikriya or ubarna.
 haal hi main homeland security vibhaag or femaa ne EM ke pratimaan ke bhaag ke rup main "samutthan" or "rokthaam" shabdon ko apanaaya he. duusare shabd ko PKEMA 2006 ne oktober 2006 main adhinium ke dwaara kanoon ke rup main lagu kiya or 31 march 2007 se prabhavi banaaya he. donon shabdon kii paribhaashaayen alag charnon ke rup main saralata se upyukt nahin hoti he. paribhaasha ke anusaar rokthaam 100% shaman he. samutthan chaar charnon ke lakshya kaa varnan karata he: durbhaagya se aasaani se ubarne yaa parivartan se taal-mail bithaane kii kshamta
 khataron ko aapadaaon main puri tarah viksit hone se rokane yaa ghatit hone kii sthiti main aapadaaon ke prabhaav ko kam karne kaa prayaas newnikaran karrwai he. newnikaran charan anya charnon se bhinn he kyonki yah jokhim ko kam karne yaa nasht karne ke liye dirghakaalin upayon par kendrit hota he. aapadaa ghatit hone kaa baad lagu karne par, newnikaran ranneetiyon ke karyanvayan ko ubarne kii prakriya kaa hissaa mana ja saktaa he. newnikaran upaay sanrachanaatmak yaa gaiyr-sanrachanaatmak ho sakate hai. sanrachanaatmak upaay baadh baandhon kii tarah takneekee samaadhaan kaa upyog karte hai. gaiyr-sanrachanaatmak upayon main shaamil hai kanoon, bhoomi-upyog yojana or beemaa. khataron ke prabhaav ko kam karne ke liye newnikaran sabase adhik laagat prabhavi tarika he lekin yah hameshaa upyukt nahin hota. newnikaran main shaamil hai nikasi ke sambandh main viniyamon kii vyavastha karnaa,viniyamon ke paalan kaa virodh karne waalon ke khilaaf pratibandh lagaana or janata ko sambhavit jokhimon kii jaankaree dena. kuch sanrachanaatmak newnikaran upayon se paristhitikeetantr par pratikul prabhaav pad saktaa he.
 newnikaran kii purvavarti gatividhi jokhim kii pahchaan he. bhautik jokhim moolyankan khataron kii pahchaan or moolyankan kii prakriya ko sanderbhit karata he. khatra-vishesh jokhim kisi vishisht khatre kii sambhaavna or prabhaav ke str donon ko jodta he. nimna sameekaran ke anusaar khatra or us khatre se jansankhya kii arakshitta ko guna karne se jokhim tabaahi modelling paidaa hoti he. jitna ucch jokhim utana adhik zaruri he ki khatra vishisht arakshittaayen newnikaran or tatparta ke prayaason se lakshith hai. haalanki, arakshitta nahin to khatra nahin udhaaharan ke liye nirjan registan main ghatanevaala bukamp.
 prakritik aapadaaon, aatankwaadi krityon or anya maanav nirmit aapadaaon kii rokthaam or inase suraksha, pratyuttar, ubarne or prabhaavon ko kam karne ke liye prabhavi samanvay or kshamtaaon main vruddhi sunishchit karne ke liye yojana, aayojan, prashikshan, las karnaa, abhyaas, moolyankan or sudhaar gatividhiyon kaa satat chakra tatparta he.
 tatparta charan main, apaat prabandhak jokhim ke prabandhan or uskaa saamana karne ke liye karrwai kii yojana banaate hai or is tarah kii yojanaon ko lagu karne ke liye aavashyak kshamtaaon kaa nirmaan karne ke liye zaruri karrwai karte hai. aam tatparta upayon main shaamil hai:
 kisi ek ghatana se hone vaali pratyaashit mauton yaa ghayalon kii sankhya kaa adhyayan, hataahat bhavishyavaanee tatparta kaa ek anya pahalu he. isse yojanakaaron ko anumaan ho jaataa he ki kisi vishesh prakaar kii ghatana ke pratyuttar main kya kya sansaadhan taiyaar hone chahie. 
 yojana charan main apatkaaleen prabandhakon ko lacheela or vyapak hona chahie - ve apane sambandhit kshetron main saavdhaanee se jokhim or arakshitta ko pahachaanen or samarthan ke aparamparaagat va asaamaanya saadhan niojit karen. nagar nigam, yaa nijee - kshetra ke anusaar apatkaaleen sevaaon ko kam kiya ja saktaa he yaa baari kar lagaaya ja saktaa he. gaiyr sarkari sanghathanon dwaara pradutt vaanchit sansaadhan jaise ki sthaneey jila skool bason dwaara visthapit grihaswamiyon kaa parivahan, agni vibhaagon or bachaav daston ke bich aapasi sahyogi samjhauton dwaara baadh peediton kii nikasi nishpadit karnaa, kii pahchaan yojana charnon main hi kii jaani chahie or niyamit rup se inke saath abhyaas kiya janaa chahie.
 pratikriya charan main aapadaa kshetra main aavashyak apatkaaleen sevaaon or pehle uttar dene vaale kaa jugad karnaa shaamil he. isamen agnishamak, police or ambulance dal jaisi pehle str kii ghor apatkaaleen sevaaon ko shaamil karne kii sambhaavna he. sainya abhiyaan ke taur par kiye jane par ise aapadaa raahat abhiyaan kaha jaataa he or yah bina ladaai ke nikasi abhiyaan kaa anuvarthi ho saktaa he. inhen visheshgy bachaav dal jaisi anek gaun apatkaaleen sevaaon kaa samarthan mil saktaa he.
 ek acchhee tarah se dohraayi gayi apatkaaleen yojana ko tatparta charan ke bhaag ke rup main viksit karne se bachaav kaa kushal samanvayan hota he.aavashyaktaa padane par khoj or bachaav ko prayaas kii praarambhik avasthaa main hi shuru kiya ja saktaa he. ghayalon ke jakhm, bahari taapmaana, peediton ko hawa or pani kii sulabhta ke drushtigat aapadaa ke adhikansh shikaar sanghaat ke baad 72 ghante ke bheetar mar jaaenge.
 kisi bhi sangeen aapadaa - prakritik yaa aatankwaadi janit - ke liye sanghathanaatmak pratikriya maujuuda apatkaaleen prabandhan sanghathanaatmak pranaaliyon or prakriyaaon: fedral rispance plan or insidence cmand system par aadhaarit hota he. in pranaaliyon ko ekikrut kaman or aapasi sahaayta ke siddhanton ke madhyam se shashakt kiya jaataa he.
 kisi bhi aapadaa kaa jawaab dene ke liye anushaasan or chapaltaa donon kii jarurat hoti he. pehle uttardaata ke niyantran se pare badhane vaale prayaason kaa shighrata se samanvay or prabandhan karne ke liye ucch kaamkaaji netritv team ko shaamil karne or banane kii aavashyaktaa, ek anushaasit, parsparik pratikriya vaale yojanaon ke set ko banane or lagu karne ke liye ek netaa or uske dal kii aavashyaktaa ko ingit karti he. isse team samanvit, anushaasit pratikriyaaon ke saath aage bad sakati he jo kamobesh sahi hon or nai jaankaree va badalati paristhitiyon ke anusaar dhal saken.
 ubarne vaale charan kaa uddeshy prabhavit kshetra ko yathapurv sthiti main behaal karnaa he. iskaa lakshya pratikriya charan se bhinn he, ubarne ke prayaas un muddon or nirnyon se sambandhit hai jo tatkaal aavashyaktaaon kii purti ke baad kiye jane chahie. ubarne ke prayaas mukhya rup se nasht sampatti, punarrozgaar or anya aavashyak buniyadi dhaanche kii marammat sambandhi kaary hai. samudaay or buniyadi dhaanche main aapadaa purv nihit jokhim ko kam karne ke uddeshy se "waapas behatar banane" ke prayaas kiye jane chahie. anyathaa alokapriy newnikaran upayon ke karyanvayan ke liye 'alpakaaleen avasar' kaa laabh uthaanaa, ubarne ke prabhavi prayaason kaa ek mahatvapurn pahalu he. aapadaa kii smriti tazaa rahane par prabhavit kshetra ke nagarikon dwaara adhik newnikaran parivartanon ko sweekaar kiye jane kii adhik sambhaavna he.
 sanyukt rajya america main, neshnal rispance plan darshata he ki kaise 2002 ke homeland security adhinium dwaara praavdhaan kiye gaye sansadhano kaa upyog ubarne ke prayaason main kiya jayega. america main ubarne ke prayaason main atyaadhik takneekee or vittiya sahaayta adhiktar sangheey sarkaar dwaara pradaan kii jaati he.
 anaavashyak jokhim kii jaankaree or usase parhez hi mukhya rup se vyaktigat newnikaran he. nijee/paariwarik swasthya or nijee sampatti ko sambhav jokhim kaa moolyankan isamen shaamil he.
 newnikaran kaa ek udhaaharan yah hoga ki khatre vaali jagah jaise baadh vaale maidan, avatalan yaa bhuskhalan vaale kshetron main sampatti na khareednaa. ho saktaa he ki ghatit hone tak ghar ke maalik ko sampatti ko hone vaale khatre ke bare main pataa na ho. haalanki, jokhim kii pahchaan or moolyankan surveykshan ke liye visheshgyon ko kaam par rakhaa ja saktaa he. pramukh rup se avgat jokhim ke liye beemaa khareednaa ek aam upaay he.
 bukamp pravan kshetron main nijee sanrachanaatmak newnikaran main shaamil hai kisi sampatti main prakritik gas kii aapurti ko turant band karne ke liye ek bukamp valve lagaana, sampatti main puraane bukampiy upkarnon ke sthaan par nae lagaana or gharelu bukampiy suraksha badhaane ke liye imaarat ke andar ke saamaan kii raksha karnaa. saamaan kii raksha main furniture, refricrator, pani ke heater or bhanjaneey saamaan ko deevaaron par lagane ke alaava cabinet main or sitakaniyaan lagavaana shaamil ho saktaa he. baadh pravan kshetron main dakshini asia kii tarah makaan khambhe /baanson par banaae ja sakate hai. jin kshetron main bijli lambey samay tak gul rahati he vahaan janarator kaa lagaaya janaa ishtatam sanrachanaatmak newnikaran kaa udhaaharan hoga. toofaan tahkhaane or bhumigat aashray kaa nirmaan aise hi or udhaaharan he.
 aapadaaon ke prabhaav ko simit karne ke liye kiye gaye sanrachanaatmak or gaiyr sanrachanaatmak upaay newnikaran ke tahat aate hai.

 gaiyr sanrachanaatmak newnikaran: -
 tatparta kaa uddeshy aapadaa ko rokna he jabaki vyaktigat tatparta aapadaa ghatit hone par upkaran or prakriyaaon kii taiyaari arthath yojana par kendrit he. aashrayon ke nirmaan, chetavanee upkarnon kii sthapana, samarthak jivan-rekha sevaaon ke nirmaan or nikasi yojanaon ke purvaabhyas sahit tatparta upayon ke kai rup ho sakate hai. do saral upaay aavashyaktaanusaar ghatana se nipatane yaa nikasi main madad kar sakate hai. nikasi ke liye, ek aapadaa aapurti kit taiyaar kii ja sakati he or aashray uddeshyon ke liye aapurti kaa bhandar banaaya ja saktaa he. adhikari uttarjivita kit jaise "72 -ghante kii kit" taiyaar karne kii aksar vakaalat karte hai. is kit main bhojan, davaaiyaan, torch, mombatiyaan or dhan ho sakate hai. iske alaava, moolyawaan vastuon ko surakshit sthaan par rakhane kii bhi sifaarish kii jaati he.
 kisi apatkaal kaa pratikriya charan khoj or bachaav se shuru ho saktaa he lekin sabhi maamalon main dhyaan jaldi se prabhavit aabaadi kii buniyadi maanviya zaruuratein puraa karne kii ore palat jayega. yah sahaayta rashtriya yaa antarrashtriya agenciyon or sanghathanon dwaara pradaan kii ja sakati he. aapadaa sahaayta kaa prabhavi samanvayan aksar mahatvapurn hota he, khaskar jab anek sanghathan jawaab den or sthaneey apatkaaleen prabandhan agency kii kshamta zarurat se pare yaa aapadaa ke kaaran kam ho gai ho.
 nijee str par pratikriya kisi jagah par aashray yaa nikasi kaa rup le sakati he. jagah-main-aashray paridrishy main, ek parivaar ko unke hi ghar main kisi bhi rup main bahari samarthan ke bina kai dinon tak rahane ke liye taiyaar kiya jayega. nikasi main, ek parivaar automobile yaa parivahan ke anya saadhan dwaara kshetra chhod detaa he or jitna saamaan le ja saktaa he sambhawatah. aashray ke liye ek tamboo sahit, apane saath le letaa he. agar yantric parivahan uplabdh nahin he to paidal nikasi main aam taur par kam se kam teen din kii aapurti or barish-pratirodhi bistar hoga, tirpal or kambal to avashya hi honge.
 manushya ke jivan ko tatkaal khatra kam ho jane par ubarne kaa charan shuru hota he. punarnirmaan ke dauraan sampatti kaa sthaan yaa nirmaan saamagree par vichaar karne kii sifaarish kii jaati he.
 sabase charam grih karawas paridrishyon main shaamil hai yuddh, akaal or mahamari, or yah ek saal yaa adhik ke liye ho saktaa he. tab ghar ke bheetar hi ubarna hoga. in ghatnaon ke yojanakaar aamtaur par thok main khaadya padaarth, uchit bhandaaran or taiyaari ke upkaran kharidte hai or saamaanya jivan kii tarah khaanaa khate hai. vitamin kii goliyaan, gehun, saim, sookhe doodh, makka or khaanaa pakaane kaa tel se ek saadhaaran santulit aahaar taiyaar kiya ja saktaa he. jab bhi sambhav ho subjiyan, fal, masaale or maance, taiyaar or tazaa donon hi shaamil karne chahie.
 apatkaaleen prabandhakon ko vividh kshetron main prashikshit kiya jaataa he jisase unhen puure apatkaaleen jivan-chakra main sahaayta milti he. peshewar apatkaaleen prabandhak sarkari or saamudayik taiyaariyon, yaa nijee vyapaar tatparta par dhyaan kendrit kar sakate hai. prashikshan sthaneey, rajya, sangheey or nijee sanghathanon dwaara pradaan kiya jaataa he or isamen saarvajanik soochna or media sambandhon se lekar ucch str kii ghatana hone par aadesh dene or ranneetik kaushal jaise aatankwaad bam visphot sthal kaa adhyayan yaa ek apaat sthiti ke drishy ko niyantrit karnaa, tak shaamil hota he.
 ateet main, apatkaaleen prabandhan ke kshetra main sena yaa pratham risponder kii prishthbhumi vaale logon kii bharmaar rahi he. vartmaan main, is kshetra main adhik vividh jansankhya he anek visheshgy sena yaa pratham risponder itihaas se itar vibhinn prakaar kii prishthbhumi se hai. apatkaaleen prabandhan yaa isse sambandhit kshetra main snatakapurv or snaatak degree praapt karne waalon ke liye shiksha ke avasar bad rahe hai. america ke 180 se adhik skulon main apaat prabandhan se sambandhit kaarykram hai lekin vishisht rup se apaat prabandhan main doctoret kaa ek hi kaarykram he.
 jaise jaise apaat prabandhan samudaay dwaara vishesh rup se sanyukt rajya america main, ucch peshewar maanakon kii aavashyaktaa ko manyata di jane lagi he, pramanit apaat prabandhak or pramanit vyapaar nirantarata profeshnal jaise vyavsaayik praaanpatr aam banate ja rahe hai.
 2007 main femaa apatkaaleen prabandhan ucch shiksha pariyojna ke dow wain blonsha ne apaat prabandhan ke siddhanton par vichaar karne ke liye femaa apatkaaleen prabandhan santhaan ke adheekshak, dow kortej laurence ke nirdesan main apaat prabandhan vrittikon or shikshavidon kaa ek kaarydal bulaayaa. yah pariyojna is bodh se prerit hui ki "apatkaaleen prabandhan ke siddhanton" par asankhya kitaaben, lekh or nibandh uplabdh to hai lekin sahitya kii itani vishaal sarini main in siddhanton kii sarvamaanya paribhaasha kahin nahin he. samooh aath siddhanton par sahamat hua jo apatkaaleen prabandhan ke siddhant ke vikaas ke liye ek maargdarshi ke taur par prayukt honge. neeche diya gaya saransh in aath siddhanton kii suchi he or prayek kaa sankshipt vivaran diya gaya he.
 siddhant: apatkaaleen prabandhan hona chahie:
 in siddhanton kaa vistrut varnan apatkaaleen prabandhan ke siddhant par uplabdh he.
 haal ke varshon main apatkaaleen prabandhan kii nirantarata kii visheshta ke parinamswaroop apatkaaleen prabandhan soochna system kii nai avadharana ne janm liya he. apatkaaleen prabandhan hitdharakon ke bich nirantarata or antarasankriyata ke liye, sarkari or gaiyr sarkari bhagidaari ke sabhi staron par apaat yojana ko ekikrut karne vaali sanrachana pradaan karke or apaat sthitiyon ke sabhi chaar charnon ke liye sabhi sambandhit sansadhano kaa upyog karke EMIS apatkaaleen prabandhan prakriya kaa samarthan karata he. swasthya dekhbhaal ke kshetra main, aspataal HICS kaa upyog karte hai jo prayek prabhag ke liye zimmedaariyon ke set ke saath spasht rup se paribhaashit kaman shrinkhalaa main sanrachana or sanghathan pradaan karti he.
 kshetra kii paripakvata ke saath saath apaat prabandhan ke vrittik vividh prishthbhumi se aane lage hai. smriti sansthaanon ke peshewar saanskrithik viraasat - apane sangrah main rakhi vastuon or record ke sangrakshan ke prati samarpit hai. 2001 main sitambar 11 ke hamalon, 2005 ke toofaan or colon abhilekhaagaar ke patan ke baad ai ucch jaagrukta ke parinamswaroop yah in kshetron ke bheetar teji se badhata pramukh ghatak he.
 bahumoolya abhilekhon kii safal punah prapti ke avasar badhaane ke liye, ek susthaapit or acchhee tarah se parakshit yojana viksit kiya janaa chahie. yojana bahut jatil nahin honi chahie balki pratikriya or bahaali main sahaayta karne ke liesaadgi par jor diya janaa chahie. saadagi ke ek udhaaharan ke rup main, pratikriya or bahaali charan main karmchaariyon ko usee tarah ke kaary karne chahie jo ve saamaanya paristhitiyon main karte hai. isamen newnikaran ranneetiyon jaise sanstha main chhidkaav yantron kii sthapana, ko bhi shaamil karnaa chahie. is kaary ke liye anbhavi adhyaksh ke netritv vaali sugathit samiti ke sahyog kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. jokhim ko kam karne or adhiktam punah prapti ke liye vyaktiyon ko prachlit upkarnon or sansadhano kii adhyatan jaankaree dene ke liye peshewar sanghathan niyamit kaaryshaalaayein or vaarshik sammelnon main dhyaan satr yojit karte hai.
 aapadaa taiyaari or bahaali yojanaon main peshewaron kii sahaayta ke liye peshewar association or saanskrithik viraasat sansthaaon ke sanyukt prayaason ke parinamswaroop kai vibhinn prakaar ke upkarnon kaa vikaas hua he. kai maamalon main, ye upkaran bahari upyogkartaaon ko uplabdh karaae jate hai. iske atirikt maujuuda sanghathanon dwaara rachit yojana namoonay bhi aksar websiton par uplabdh hote hai jo aapadaa yojana taiyaar karne vaali yaa ek maujuuda yojana ko adhyatan kar rahi kisi bhi samiti yaa samooh ke liye sahaayak ho sakate hai. jabaki prayek sanghathan ko apni vishesh zaruuraton ko puraa karne vaali yojana or upkaran banane kii zarurat hogi phir bhi aise upkarnon ke kuch udhaaharan hai jo sambhawatah yojana prakriya main upyogi praarambhik binduon kaa pratinidhitiv kare. inhen bahya kadiyaan anubhag main shaamil kiya gaya he.
 2009 main, aapadaaon se prabhavit aabaadi ke akalan ke liye antarrashtriya vikaas agency america ne web-aadhaarit ek upkaran banaaya. population explorer ke naam se vikhyath yah upkaran vishwa ke sabhi deshon main 1 km2 ke rezulation main jansankhya ko vitarit karne ke liye ok rij rashtriya prayogshaala dwaara viksit Landscan aabaadi data kaa upyog karata he. aabaadi kii atisamvedansheeltaa or yaa bhojan kii asuraksha ke prabhaav kaa anumaan lagane ke liye uasaid kii FEWS NET pariyojna dwaara prayukt population explorer kaa apatkaaleen vishleshan or pratikriya karrwai main vyapak prayog badhata ja rahaa he, isamen madhya america main baadh or prashanth mahaasaagar main 2009 main tsunami ghatana se prabhavit aabaadi kaa akalan bhi shaamil he.
