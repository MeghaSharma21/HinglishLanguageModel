 halbi bhashaa oriya or marathi ke bich kii ek purvi bhartiya-arya bhashaa he. yah bharat ke madhya bhaag main lagbhag 5 lakh logon kii bhashaa he. ise bastari, halba, halbaas, halbi, halvi, mahari tathaa mehari bhi kehete hai. is bhashaa ke vaakyon main karta ke baad karm or uske baad kriya aati he. isamen visheshan, sangya ke pehle aate hai. yah pratyaypradhan bhashaa he. yah ek vyapaarik bhashaa ke rup main prayog kii jaati he kintu isamen sakshartaa bahut kam he.
 halbi, devanagari lipi main likhi jaati he.
