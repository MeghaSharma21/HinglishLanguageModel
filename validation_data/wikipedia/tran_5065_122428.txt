 kitabgar prakaashan ek hindi pustakon ke pramukh prakaashak hai. pandit jagatram arya iske pehle sansthaapak the.
 varsh 1968 main pandit jagatram arya ne isaki shuruyaat kii. agast 1993 main unka dehavsaan ho gaya. unki smriti main ek vaarshik saahitiyik sammaan sthapit karne kaa nirnay prakaashan ke sanchaalkon dwaara liya gaya. 16 disambar ko unka janmdiwas padataa he, atev yah nirnay kiya gaya kii us din "arya smriti sahitya sammaan" kaa aayojan sammaan samaaroh ke rup main kiya jaye.
