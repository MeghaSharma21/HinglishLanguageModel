 kadambari mehara kaa naam britain ke un pravasi kathakaron ke saath liya jaataa hai jinhone pichley dashak main apni upasthiti se samast hindi sahityakaron kaa dhyaan apni ore khinch. unke lekhan kii shuruyaat varanasi ke 'aaj' akhbaar se hui or baad main ve skool va college kii saahitiyik gatividhiyon se judy raheen.
 angreji sahitya se snatakottar oopaadhi lene ke baad ve landan chali gayin jahaan adhyaapan ko apana kaaryakshetra banaaya or 25 varshon tak isse judy raheen.
 avkaash prapti ke baad ab phir se kahani or upanyaas kii duniya main pravesh kiya he. 'kuch jag kii' sheershak se unka ek kahani sangrah bhi prakashit hua he.
