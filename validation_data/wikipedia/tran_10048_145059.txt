 sharad yadav bharat kii ek raajnneetik party janata dal ke purv rashtriya adhyaksh.hai. unhone bihar pradesh ke madhepura lok sabha nirvachan kshetra se chaar baar lok sabha kaa pratinidhitiv kiya. vartmaan main ve rajya sabha ke saansad hai.
 yadav rashtriya janatantric gathbandhan ke sanyojak the parantu abhi haal main hi unki party dwaara gathbandhan se sambandh viched kar lene ke kaaran unhone sanyojak pad se tyaag patr de diya. raajnneetik gathjod ke maahir khilaadi sharad yadav ko bihar ke mukhyamantri nitish kumaar kaa behad kareebi bhi mana jaataa he.
