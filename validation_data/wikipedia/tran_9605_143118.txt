 gandavyuuh buddha mahayan sampradaay kaa ek mahatvapurn granth he. isamen bodhisattva kaa gungan or unki upasna kii charchaa he. 
 is granth ke sambandh main anushruti he ki ek din bhagwaan buddha shravasti sthit jetavan main vihaar kar rahe the. unke saath samantbhadra, manjushri aadi paanch hajaar bodhisattva the. unhone buddha se gyaan pradaan karne kii prarthana kii. tab buddha ne bodhisattva kii upasna ke sambandh main bataaya. is granth main bodhisattva ke lakshan kahe gaye hai. bodhisattva prapti ke nimitt jo kuch karaniya hai vah bataaya gaya he. samast jeevon se prem or karuna karnaa, unke du:kha kii nivruti ke nimitt prayatnkarna or jeevon ko swarg maarg bataane ke nimitt upadesh karnaa bodhisattva kaa kartavya he. is granth ke ant main bhadracharipranighatgatha naamak ek strotra he. usamen mahayan panth ke tatvagyan ke nimitt buddha kii stuti he.
