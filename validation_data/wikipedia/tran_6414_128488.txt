 tikamgarhjila mukhyalay tikamgarh he. shahar kaa mul naam 'tehari' tha, jo ab puraani tehari ke naam se janaa jaataa he. 1783 i orchha vikramjit ne orchha se apni rajdhani tehari jila tikamgarh main sthanantarit kar di thi. tikamgarh tikam se tikamgarh padaa. tikamgarh jila bundelkhand kshetra kaa ek hissaa he. yah jamani, betwa or dhasaan kii ek sahaayak nadi ke bich bundelkhand pathaar par he.
 is jile ke antargat kshetra orchha ke saamanti rajya ke bhartiya sangh ke saath apane vilay tak hissaa tha. orchha rajya rudra pratap dwaara 1501 main sthapit kiya gaya tha. vilay ke baad, yah 1948 main vindhyas pradesh ke aath jilon main se ek ban gaya. 1 navambar ko rajyon ke punargathan ke baad, 1956 yah nae nakkashidaar madhya pradesh rajya ke ek jile main ban gaya. madhya pradesh ke tikamgarh jile main sthit ek gaanv he. is gaanv kaa naam yahaan sthit prasiddh durg ke naam par garh-kundar padhaa he. garh kundar kaa pracheen naam garh kuraar he. garh-kundar kila us kaal kii na keval bejod shilpakala kaa namoona he balki aitihaasik samruddhi kaa pratik bhi he. garhkundar kile kaa sambandh chandel or khangaar nareshon se rahaa he, parantu iske punarnirmaan or ise nai pahchaan dene kaa shrey khangaaron ko jaataa he. vartmaan main khangaar kshatriya samaaj ke parivaar gujarat, maharashtra ke alaava uttar pradesh main nivas karte hai. 12veen shatabdi main prithviraj chouhan ke pramukh samant khetsingh khangaar ne parmaar vansh ke gadhpati shiva ko haraakar is durg par kabja karne ke baad khangaar rajya kii neenv daali thi.
 choti devi ji tikamgarh.
 shahar ke bichon bich shri shri 1008 shri janaki raman mandir choti devi ke andar tikamgarh main sthit hai.yah mandir tikamgarh main papora chauraahaa ke sameep bukaaria ji kii gali main he. choti devi mandir main prayek navaratri main nau dinon ke liye bhavy mela lagaaya jaataa hai.
 jile ke teen up main vibhajit-vibhaajan he, jo aage chah tahseelein main vibhajit kiya jaataa he. tikamgarh up vibhaajan shaamil tikamgarh or baldevgarh tahseelein. nivadi or prithvipur tahseelein nivadi up vibhaajan farm jabaki jataara up vibhaajan jataara, palera or lidhaura tahseelein shaamil hai. jile ke chah vikaas khandon, arthath tikamgarh, baldevgarh, jataara, palera, nivadi, prithvipur shaamil he.
 betwa nadi jile kii purvi seemaa ke saath bahati he uski sahaayak nadiyon main se ek ke uttar pashchimi seemaa ke saath bahati he. betwa kii sahaayak nadiyon is jile se bah jamani, bagdi or barua hai.
 gram uparaaraa jevar, tikamgarh main yah pracheen mandir bich basti main sthit he, jisamen shankaraji kii keval ek pindi thi. us pindi ke aas-paas kai pindiyaan bhoomi se swayan prakat ho gayin, jo prati varsh badhati jaati hai. samprati teen pindiyaan bahut badi hai, teen majholi hai or do nikal rahi hai. yah sthaan ranipur road stesan se 4 mile dakshin main he.
 tathaa iske saath hi mahaviran mandir kii manyata atyadhik he kyonki yahan sarp se kate hue vyaktiyon kaa sahi ho janaa vikhyath hai. jo uparaaraa gram panchayat kaa mandir he tathaa ranipur maarg par sthit hai.
 iske alaava gram panchayat uparaaraa ke naam ke saath hi ek kavi kaa naam judata he jo yuvaao evam samaaj ke liye chetana kaa kaam karata he . jo ajabendra khangaar ke naam se apane bundelkhand me beshumaar hastiyon me se ek he.
 tikamgarh jila mukhyalay se budera maarg par vakpura naamak ek gaanv sthit he. isi gaanv ke paas hari bhari pahadiyon ke bich vidya kii devi saraswati ji kaa mandir he. jise bagaaj mata ke naam se janaa jaataa he. yah kareeb 1100 varsh pracheen he. devi mandir gaanv se kareeb 2 ki0me0 dur pahadiyon ke bich he. mana jaataa he ki aaj se kareeb 500 varsh purv vakpura gaanv ke logon ne is pavitra sthal kii pahchaan kar yahaan aana janaa shuru kiya.
 baldevgarh tehsil kaa yah gaanv jila mukhyalay se 25 kilometre dur tikamgarh-chhatarpur road par sthit he. yah gaanv jain tirth kaa pramukh kendra kaha jaataa he. anek pracheen jain mandir yahaan bane hai, jinamein shantinath mandir pramukh he. is mandir main shantinath kii 20 feet kii pratimaa sthapit he. ek baandh ke saath chandel kaal kaa jalkund yahaan dekha ja saktaa he. iske alaava shri vardhman mandir, shri bheru mandir, shri chandraprbhu mandir, shri parswanath mandir, shri mahavir mandir, bahubali mandir or panch pahari mandir yahaan ke anya lokapriy mandir hai. bahubali mandir main bhagwaan bahubali kii 15 feet oonchi murti sthapit he.
 tikamgarh se 5 kilometre dur sagar tikamgarh maarg par papora ji jain tirth he, jo ki bahut pracheen he or yahan 108 jain mandir hai jo ki sabhi prakaar ke aakaar main bane hue jaise rath aakaar or camel aakaar yahan kai sundar bhoyare he.
 ek baar ek sanwat 1890 main kalakaar murtiyon ko bechney ke liye 'bamhori ja rahaa tha. achaanak baiylgaadee bamhori ke paas ek pipal kaa ped ke ped ke paas ruk gai or usne anape sabhi prayaason ko bekaar paaya or gaadi ko aage nahin le ja paaya par jab kalakaar ne faisla kiya ki vah main murti sthapit 'bandhaa ji kshetra' main sthapit karegaa or uski gaadi bandhaa ji kii ore bad shuru kar diya yah murti ab bhi bandhaa ji ke vishaal mandir main sthapit he.
 tikamgarh-niwadi road par sthit yah gaanv prithvipur tehsil main he. ek pahari par based is gaanv main mata acharu kaa charchit mandir he. mandir ek kunda ke liye bhi prasiddh he jo sadaiv jal se bharaa rahata he. har saal navaratri ke avasar par gram panchayat kii dekharekh main mela lagata he, jisamen dur-dur se log aakar shirkat karte hai. yah sthaan gram prithvipura, tikamgarh main he. yahaan murti nahin he, ek kunda ke aakaar kaa gaddha he. yahaan caitra-navaratri main prachinkaal se mela lagata aa rahaa he.
 yah nagar tikamgarh-chhatarpur sadak par tikamgarh se 26 kilometre dur sthit he. khubasurat gwaal sagar kunda ke upar bana pathar kaa vishaal kila yahaan kaa mukhya aakarshan he. ek puraani or vishaal bandook aaj bhi kile main dekhi ja sakati he. vindhya vasini devi mandir baldevagarh kaa lokapriy mandir he. chait ke maheene main saat din tak chalane vaale vindhyavasini mela yahaan lagata he. yahaan paan ke patton kaa bade paimaane par utpaadan kiya jaataa he.
 tikamgarh se 40 kilometre dur tikamgarh-mauranipur road par yah nagar sthit he. nagar kii madan sagar jhil kaaphi khubasurat he. is lambii-chaudee jhil par do baandh bane hai. in baandhon ko chandel sardaar madan varman ne 1129-67 i. ke aasapaas banwaaya tha. jataara main anek muslim imaaraton ko bhi dekha ja saktaa he. yahan sultane bundelkhand hazrat khwaja ruknuddin chishti kii dargah he jaha prativarsh urs hota he! yahi par shere bundelkhand hazrat shaikh abdul raashid "abdaal shah baba" kii paidaais hui jinaki dargah chanderi main sthit he. yahan hazrat mushkile aasaan, kadam rasul, taal baale baba, maamu syed, hazrat abdullah syed aadi majaare he. yahan kilewaali shahi masjid jo 700 se 900 saal puraani maani jaati he !
 yah niwadi tehsil kaa lokapriy gaanv he. yah pratham sthal he jise bundelon ne khangron se hasil kiya tha. 1539 tak yah sthaan rajya kii rajdhani tha. is gaanv main ek choti pahari ke upar maharaj beerasingh dev dwaara banwaaya gaya kila dekha ja saktaa he. devi maha maaya griddh vasini mandir bhi yaheen sthit he. mandir main singh sagar naam kaa vishaal kunda he. har somvaar ko yahaan bazaar lagata he.
 tikamgarh se 5 kilometre dakshin main jamdar nadi ke kinare yah gaanv basaa he. gaanv kundadev mahadev mandir ke liye prasiddh he. kaha jaataa he ki mandir ke shivlinga kii utpatti ek kunda se hui thi. gaanv ke dakshin main barighar naamak ek khubasurat picnic sthal or aakarshak usha water fall he. vinoba santhaan or puraatatv sangrahalay bhi yahaan dekha ja saktaa he.
 surya mandir ke liye vikhyath madkhera tikamgarh se 20 kilometre uttar pashchimi hisse main sthit he. mandir kaa praveshdwar purv disha kii ore he tathaa isamen bhagwaan surya kii pratimaa sthapit he. iske nikat hi ek pahari par bana vindhya vasini devi kaa mandir bhi dekha ja saktaa he.
 betwa nadi tat par basaa prithvipur tehsil ke yah bagal main yah tehsil uttar pradesh ke jhansi se 15 kilometre kii duuri par he. kaaphi lambey samay tak rajya kii rajdhani rahe orchha kii sthapana maharaja rudra pratap ne 1531 i. main kii thi. orchha ko hinduon kaa pramukh dharmik kendra mana jaataa he. raja ram mandir, jahangir mahal, chaturbhuj mandir, lakshmi mandir, phulbaag, sheeshmahal, kanchan ghat, chandrasekhar aaazaad mamorial, hardaul kii samadhi, badi chhatari, raay praveen mahal or keshav bhawan orchha ke pramukh darshneey sthal hai.
 muslim shraddhaluon kii aasthaa kaa pramukh kendra hazrat ilhan shah baba rahamat ullah alaih san 1802 main tikamgarh main tasharif laaye or najarbagh main apana mukaam bana liya. vah baba sahab ke paas jo shraddhalu jate baba sahab kii duaon se unki manokamein purna hoti.
 hazrat khwaja rukunuddin chishti rahamat ullah alaih san 1200 main jataara tasharif laaye ore nagar ke kinare se bahane vaali nahar ke paas ek jagah apana makaam banaaya. jataara nagar ke rahane vaale shaikh parivaar ke ek hazrat, khwaja kii khidamat kiya karte the. kai varshon ke baad shaikh sahab ne khwaja sahab se muraad maangi kii unhen bhi apni duaon se navaj diya jaae.
 hazrat daata ilahishah baba rahamat ullah alaih ek aise bali the jinke dar par sadaiv samaaj ke har varg ke log apni mannaten lekar aate or baba sahab kii duaon se unki manokamanaayen kabool hoti. aaj bhi yah ek aisa sthaan he jahaan hindu-muslim va sabhi dharmo ke log apni manokamanaayen lekar shrridhapurvak jate or unki manokamanaayen purna hoti. pichley 68 varshon se baba sahab kii dargah par 4-5 va 6 april ko urs kaa aayojan kiya jaataa he.
 hazrat abdaal shah kaa puraa naam hazrat shaikh abdul russid he jinkajanm 900 saal pehle jataara sharif hua tha. inaki chillaagaah abdalia mauhalla main he. yaheen par ek masjid abdalia tathaa madrasa aaazaad islamisa daarul ulum sthit he. abdalia mohalle main prativarsh ek meley kaa aayojan hota he. mana jaataa he ki ye mela 900 saalon se yani abdaal sahab ke janm ke baad se lagaaya jaataa! inkaa mela mubarak shahar se 5 kimi dur abda pahari par lagaaya jaataa he. yahi par baba kii ibaadatgah or ghar he jo thik halaat main he. isi pahari par sher kii gupha, baba ke ghode kii taap, kund aadi he.
