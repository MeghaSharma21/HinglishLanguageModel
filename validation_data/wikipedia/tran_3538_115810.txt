 
 arhar kii daal ko tuvar bhi kaha jaataa he. isamen khanij, carbohydrate, loha, calcium aadi paryaapt maatra main paaya jaataa he. yah sugamawa se pachne vaali daal he, atah rogi ko bhi di ja sakati he, parantu gas, kabj evam saans ke rogiyon ko iskaa seven kam hi karnaa chahie. 
 bharat main arhar kii kheti teen hajaar varsh purv se hoti aa rahi he kintu bharat ke jangalon main iske paudhe nahin paye jate he. africa ke jangalon main iske junglee paudhe paye jate he. is aadhaar par iskaa utpatti sthal africa ko manajata he. sambhavatya is paudhen ko africa se hi asia main laayaa gaya he. 
 dalhan protein kaa ek sasta strot he jisko aam janata bhi khaane main prayog kar sakati he, lekin bharat main iskaa utpaadan aavashyaktaa ke anuroop nahin he. yadi protein kii uplabdhta badhaani he to dalhanon kaa utpaadan badhanaa hoga. iske liye unnatsheel prajatiyaan or unki unnatsheel krishi vidhiyon kaa vikaas karnaa hoga. 
 arhar ek vilakshan gun sampann fasal he. iskaa upyog anya dalhani fasalon kii tulana main daal ke rup main sarvaadhik kiya jaataa he. iske atirikt isaki hari faliyaan sabji ke liye, khali churi pashuon ke liye raatav, hari pattee chaara ke liye tathaa tana eendhan, jhopadi or tokari banane ke kaam laayaa jaataa he. iske paudhon par lakh ke keet kaa paalan karke lakh bhi banaai jaati he. maance kii tulana main isamen protein bhi adhik pai jaati he.

 yah purv uttari bharat ke dalhan kii mukhya fasal he. purvi uttarapradesh main to daal maane arhar kii daal. yah keval uttar pradesh main 30 lakh acre se adhik rakabe main boi jaati he. iske liye nichi tathaa matiyaar bhoomi ko chhodkar sabhi jaminen upyukt hai. unchi dumat bhoomi main, jahaan pani nahin bharta, yah fasal vishesh rup se acchhee hoti he. yah bahudhaa varsha ritu ke aarambh main or kharif kii fasalon ke saath milakar boi jaati he. arhar ke saath kodo, bagari-dhan, jowar, bajra, moongfalee, til aadi milakar bote hai. varsha ke ant main ye fasalein pak jaati he or kaat lee jaati hai. iske baad jaade main arhar badhkar khet ko purnataya bhar leti he tathaa rabi kii fasalon ke saath march ke maheene main taiyaar ho jaati he. pakane par isaki fasal katkar dane jhaad liye jate hai. 
 anya fasalon ke saath milakar iskaa beej keval do kilo prati acre ke hisaab se daalaa jaataa he. arhar ko varsha ke pehle do maheenon main yadi nikaai va godai do-teen baar mil jaay, to iskaa paudha bahut badhata he or paidaavaar bhi lagbhag dooni ho jaati he. chane kii tarah isaki jadon main bhi hawa se khaad nitrogen ikattha karne kii kshamta hoti he. arhar bone se kheton kii urvara shakti badhati he or ise swayan khaad kii aavashyaktaa nahin hoti. isko pani kii bhi adhik aavashyaktaa nahin hoti. jab dhan ityadi pani kii kami se mar tathaa murjhaa jate hai tab bhi arhar khet main hari khadi rahati he. kamjor arhar kii fasal par paale kaa asar kabhi kabhi ho jaataa he, parantu acchhee fasal par, jo barsaat main godai ke kaaran moti ho gai he, paale kaa bhi asar bahut kam, yaa nahin, hota.
