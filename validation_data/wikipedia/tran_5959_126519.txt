 tribhaasha sutara bharat main bhashaa-shikshan se sambandhit niti he jo bharat sarkaar dwaara rajyon se vichaar-vimarsh karke banaayi gayi he. yah sutara san 1968 main sweekaar kiya gaya.
 bhartiya samvidhan kii dhara 343, dhara 348 tathaa 351 kaa saransh yah he ki devanagari lipi main likhi or mulat: sanskrit se apni paribhaashik shabdaawalee ko lene vaali hindi bhartiya sangh kii rajbhasha he. isamen roman ankon kaa vyavhaar hoga.
 rashtriya bhashaaon main yah moulik siddhant maanya he ki sabhi bhashaaen rashtriya hai. isamen ullikhit bhashaaen-asamiya, bangla, gujarati, hindi, kannada, kashmiri, malayalam, marathi, oriya, punjabi, sanskrit, tamil, telugu, sindhi, urdu, konkani, manipuri, nepali, bodo, santhali, maithili, dogri aadi 22 bhashaaen hai.
 tribhaasha sutara samvidhan main nahin he. san 1956 main akhil bhartiya shiksha parishad ne ise mul rup main apni sanstuti ke rup main mukhyamantriyon ke sammelan main rakhaa tha or mukhyamantriyon ne iskaa anumodan bhi kar diya tha. 1968 kii rashtriya shiksha niti main iskaa samarthan kiya gaya tha or san 1968 main hi pun: anumodith kar diya gaya tha. san 1992 main sansad ne iske karyanvayan kii sanstuti kar di thi.
 yah sanstuti rajyon ke liye baadhyataa mulak nahin thi kyonki shiksha rajyon kaa vishay he. san 2000 main yah dekha gaya ki kuch rajyon main hindi or angreji ke atirikt icchaanusaar sanskrit, arabi, french, tathaa porchugij bhi padhai jaati hai. tribhaasha sutara main 1-shastreey bhashaaen jaise sanskrit, arabi, farsi. 2-rashtriya bhashaaen 3-aadhunik europea bhashaaen hai. in teenon shreniyon main kinhin teen bhashaaon ko padhaane kaa prastaav he. sanstuti yah bhi he ki hindibhashi rajyon main dakshin kii koi bhashaa padhai jaani chahie.
