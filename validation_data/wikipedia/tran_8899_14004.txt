 visayas yaa visaaya dweep dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh main ek dweepasmooh he. yah us desh ke teen pramukh dweep dalon main se ek he. yah luzone dweep se dakshin main sthit hai or yahan ke visaai logon kii apni saanskriti va bhashaayen hai. panaay, negros, sibu, bohol, leyate or samaar visaayon ke pramukh dweep hai. yah dweepasmooh adhiktar visaaya sagar ko ghere hue he.
 itihaaskaaron kaa maananaa he ki is kshetra kaa naam paas ke indonesia desh ke madhyakaalin hindu-buddha shrivijay samrajya par padaa or "visaaya" shabd sanskrit ke "vijay" shabd kaa hi parivartit rup he. 12veen shatabdi main sulu dweepasmooh or visaayaaz ke bade bhubhag isi samrajya ke adheen the.
