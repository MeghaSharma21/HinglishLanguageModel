 jayachand vidyalankar bharat ke mahaan itihaaskaar evam lekhak the. ve swami shraddhand saraswati, gaurishankar hirachand ojha or kashiprasad jaiswal ke shishy the. unhone ‘bhartiya itihaas parishad’ naamak sanstha khadi kii thi. unka uddeshy bhartiya drushti se samast adhyayan ko yojit karnaa or bharat kii sabhi bhashaaon main oonche sahitya kaa vikaas karnaa tha. jayachand vidyalankar hi bhagat singh ke raajnneetik guru the.
 jayachandra vidyalankar bharat main itihaas kii aisi pratibha maane jate hai ki logon ne itihaas kii unki mul dharanaon tak pahunchne ke liye vidhivat hindi kaa adhyayan kiya. unhen apni dharanaen hindi main hi saamne rakhane kii jid thi.
 unki shiksha gurukul kangri, haridwar main hui. 'vidyalankar' kii oopaadhi praapt karne ke baad ve kuch samay tak gurukul kangri main, gujarat viapeeth main tathaa kaumi mahavidhyalay, lahore main adhyaaapak rahe. itihaas main shodh ke prati armbh se hi unki ruchi thi. 1937 main unhone bhartiya itihaas parishad kii sthapana kii jisamen dow rajendra prasad kaa bhi sahyog tha. jayachand videshiyon dwaara likhe gayi bhartiya itihaas kii ekangi drushti ko parimarjeet karne ke liye katibaddh the. iske liye vyapak shodh ke aadhaar par unhone pracheen bharat ke itihaas par vishesh rup se granthon kii rachana kii. unke granthon main unke gyaan, moulik vichaardhaara evam aalochanatmak drushti ke darshan hote hai. unhen hindi ke tatkaaleen manglaprasad paritoshik se sammanit kiya gaya tha.
 unhone bharat chodo aandolan main bhi sakriy rup se bhaag liya or teen varsh tak jail main band rahe.
