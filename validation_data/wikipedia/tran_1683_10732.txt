 ghulaam ahamed farogi bhopal rajya main arabi or farsi bhashaa ke khyaati ke ek vidwaan or shaayar the. unhone bhopal ke jahangira skool main shikshak or sulemania skool main 'head maulavee' ke rup main kaary kiya. ye donon skool bhopal riyasat ke dauraan dhani varg ke chaatro ke daakhile ke liye bahut prasiddh the.
