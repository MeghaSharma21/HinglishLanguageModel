 upnivesh kisi rajya ke baahar kii us duurastha basti ko kehete hai jahaan us rajya kii janata nivas karti he. kisi purna prabhusatta sampann rajya ke logon ke anya desh kii seemaa main jaakar basane ke sthaan ke liye bhi is shabd kaa prayog hota he. is arth main adhiktar europea deshon ke "upnivesh" landan main sthit hai. 
 parantu saadhaarant: adhik sankuchit arth main hi is shabd kaa prayog hota he, visheshkar nimnlikhit dashaon main : 
  ek rajya ke nivaasiyon kii apane rajya ko bhugolic simaaon ke baahar anya sthaan par basii basti ko tab tak upnivesh kehete hai, jab tak vah sthaan us rajya ke hi prashaskeey kshetra main aata ho, athwa 
  koi swatantra raashtra, jo kisi anya raashtra kii rashtriyata, prashasan, tathaa aarthik ekta se ghanisht sambandh rakhata ho. udaahranarth, pratham shrenee ke antargat tyutanik upnivesh hai jo baltic praanton main sthit hai tathaa isi prakaar ke upnivesh balkan prayadweep main bhi hai. duusari shrenee ke upnivesh-or yahi adhik prachlit prayog he-africa athwa austrailia main angrejon ke hai.
 upnivesh banane athwa basaane kii pravriti tathaa dhang anek prakaar ke hai, jaise, rajya kii seemaa badhaane kaa lobh, vyapaar badhaane kii icchaayen, dhanavruddhi kaa lobh, dushkar kaary karne kii pravriti, badhati hui janansakhya ke bhaar ko kam karne kii ichha, raajnneetik padlolupata, vivashtaa, vidrohiyon ko desh se dur rakhane pradhanat: saandhatik evam bheeshan aparaadhiyon ko desh se nishkasit karne kii aavashyaktaa aadi mukhya kaaran hi upniveshwaad ko protsaahan dete rahe hai. saadhaaran rup main yah ek pravasi pravriti kaa hi viksit rup he tathaa upnivesh ko ek prakaar se pravaasiyon kaa sthaayee tathaa vyavasthit rup kaha ja saktaa he.
 upniveshon kii sthapana ne vibhinn samayon evam kshetron main vibhinn rup dhaaran kiye hai. phinishiaayiyon dwaara bumadhyasaagar ke tatavarti bhaagon main sthapit upnivesh apni matrubhuti ke vyapaarkendron ke rup main kaary karte the. vibhinn greek samudaayon ko upnivesh kii sthapana karne ke liye aarthik samasyaon ne baadhya kiya jo ab, athens ke upananiveshon ko chhodkar, mathrubhumi se swatantra the. rome ne samrajyaraksha ke liye apane nagarikon ke chote-chote upniveshon kii sthapana vijit videshiyon ke bich kii thi. dakshin purvi asia ke bhubhag bhartiya bastiyon se bhare pade the, kintu hindeshia es kshetra, jo kisi samay brihad bharat ke ang the, mathrubhumi swatantra the.
 14veen shatabdi tathaa uske ananthar europe asia se aage bad gaya tathaa vaanijya evam anveshan dwaara atlantic, hind or prashanth mahasaagaron ke aar paar usne apana adhikaar badha liya. 16veen shatabdi main madhya tathaa dakshin america main spain ke samrajya kii sthapana hui. portugal ne brazil, bharat ke pashchimi samudratat tathaa masalonvale purvi dweepasmoohon main apana adda jamaaya. inhen kaa anukaran kar, france, england evam holland ne uttari america tathaa pashchimi dweepasmooh main upniveshon kii tathaa africa ke samudratat par, bharat tathaa dakshin-purvi asia main vyapaarik kendron kii sthapana kii. denmark tathaa sweden nivasee bhi, in logon se peeche nahin rahe. kintu mukhya aupniveshik shaktiyaan england, france tathaa holland kii hi siddh hui. in teenon ke samrajya main "surya kabhi nahin ast hota tha" tathaa asia or africa, maanav sabhyataa ke aadi desh, ke adhikansh bhaagon par, inkaa adhikaar ho gaya.
 audyogic kranti tathaa aarthik ritiyon ke naveenatam roopon ke found nikaalane ke saath hi pashchim ke rashtron main samrajya ke liye chhina-jhapati chalati rahi. yah ek lambii kahani he, jiskaa varnan yahan nahin kiya ja saktaa. kintu isak gyaan aavashyak he ki jahaan kahin bhi vistaar kii sambhaavna thi, poonjeewaad apane nae samrajyavaadee rup main saamne aayaa. isliye germany, 19veen shatabdi ke uttarardh main, sansar main apane astitv ke liye bhoomi chahta tha, arthath duusare shabdon main, upnivesh kii lut-khasot main hissaa bantaana chahta tha. italy ne bhi is daud main bhaag liya. rus, saare uttari tathaa madhya asia main falkar, britain ko bhaybhat karne laga. sanyukt america tak pratyaksh rup se, jaise philippins main tathaa anya bahut se kshetron par, apratchaksh rup se shashan karne laga. japan ne pashchimi samrajyavaadiyon se shiksha praapt kii tathaa pehle korea phir sampurna purvi asia par, apana aadhipaty sthapit karnaa chaha. mahaan desh bharat, jo angrejon ke pratyaksh adhikaar main tha, tathaa china, jo nammaatr ke liye swatantra kintu vastut: kai shaktiyon kii gulaami main jakadaaa hua tha, upnivesh pratha ke moort udahaaran hai. itihaas ke is rup kii anya visheshtaayen africa ke bhitari bhaagon main pravesh, laabhadaayak dasavyapaar kii vibheeshikaa, uski bhoomi kaa bantwaaraa or pratispardhaa, samrajyavaadiyon dwaara uske sadhano kaa nirday shoshan aadi he.
 isamen koi andeh nahin ki bhugolic anusandhan tathaa upniveshon kii sthapana ke liye bahut se logon main dussahasik kaary ke prati anuraag tathaa isaki kshamta aavashyak thi, kintu upniveshasthaapan ke peeche dussahas hi pramukh shaktistrot ke rup main nahin tha. vyapaarik laabh sabase bada kaaran tha tathaa rajyavistaar ke saath vyapaar kaa vistaar hone ke kaaran kshetreey vijay aavashyak thi. bahudhaa duurastha upniveshon ke liye europe main yuddh hote the. is tarah holland ne portugal ko dakshin purvi asia ke purvi dweepasmooh se nikaal baahar kiya. england ne canada, bharat tathaa anya sthanon se france ko nikaal baahar kiya. german yudhvisheshgy phan moltake ne ek baar kaha tha, ""purvi bazaar ne itani shakti sanchit kar lee he ki vah yuddh main sainya sanchaalan karne main bhi samarth he."" jab maxim dwaara bandook kaa prasiddh avishkaar hua, anveshak stanli ne kaha tha, "" yah ek aagneyastra he jo murtipujakon ko dabaane main amuly siddh hoga."" samrajya ke samarthakon dwaara ""shweton kii jimmedaari"" ke rup main ek puranarudh darshan hi prastut kar liya gaya. "netive" shabd kaa prayog "niyam rahit nimnastar jati" jinka bhagya hi shweton dwaara shasit hota tha, ke apmanjanak arth main hone laga.
 vikasshil poonjeewaadi shaktiyon ko vistaar evam sanchay ke liye nikaas kii aavashyaktaa thi. aviksit deshon ke kachche maalon kii unhen aavashyaktaa thi. unhen aise deshon kii aavashyaktaa apane utpaadit maalon ke bazaar ke rup main thi or aise kshetron ke rup main thi jahaan atirikt poonji lagaai ja sake tathaa usase akalpit laabh, adheen deshon ke mazdooron kaa saralata se shoshan ho sakane ke kaaran, nishchit kiya ja sake. prayek shaktistrot es kshetron ke ekmev sanniyantrak or ekadhikari hona chaahate the. kabhi kabhi upnivesh kharide bhi gaye, kabhi talwaar ke bal tathaa dhokhe se, jaise bharat main, jeene gaye, kabhi rin vasuulanevaale abhiyaan kaa ant, adhikaar ke rup main hua, kabhi dharmprachaarkon ke upar akraman athwa hatya hi, jaise china main, videshi basti kii sthapana kaa kaaran batalaai gai. kaaran shaktiyon ke bich upnivesh ke liye aapasi spardhaa evam ishrya ke vibhinn asankhya yuddh vishwayuddh se bhi dugune vyapak rup main hue hai.
 19veen shatabdi main, upniveshi kii swatantraaa kaa andolan prarambh hua tathaa canada es "shwet" upniveshon ne, swashaasan kaa adhikaar praapt kar liya. pashchaat 20 veen shatabdi ke pranrabh main bharat, burma aadi deshon ne upnivesh kaa jua utaar phenkaa or swatantra ho gaye. kintu to bhi sansar main anek desh upnivesh bane rahe. san 1960 main sanyukt rashtrasang ne upniveshwaad ke khilaaf apana aitihaasik ghoshnaptr jari kiya jiske baad cyprus, kenya, goa tathaa anek africo deshon kii mukti sambhav hui. 
 dirghakaalin parampara ke kaaran upniveshwaad kaa vyavasthit rup prastut hua he or uske vibhinn parinaam spasht rup se saamne aae hai. shasit desh main loksevakon ke madhyam se shashan hua he. isi aadhaar par "shwet purushon ke daayitv" kaa siddhant viksit hua. shasiton main aatmavishvaas kaa lop saamaanya baat he. falat: sarvamaanya vishwaas kii baat hi nahin uth sakati. sara shashan apratyaksh rup se hota rahaa he. shashan kii bhashaa baahar se aane par rashtriya bhashaa kaa vikaas avaruddh ho jaataa he. sarkari padon par alpsankhyakon kii niyukti kaa anupaat asantulit kiya gaya he. shashan kii krambaddhata nasht hone se janasweekruti janamat, hitraksha aadi asambhav hai. vikashinata main shashan yathasthiti banaae rakhana chahta he or roodhiwaadita evam anudaar paramparaon kaa vah abhibhavak ban gaya. aarthik vikaas utana hi ho sakaa jitna shaskaon ne chaha. aarthik shoshan main saare laabh shaasha praapt karte rahe hai. aarthik niti mulat: kachche maal ke niryaat kii or pakke maal ke aayaat kii rahi he. isi main mukt vyapaar aadi kaa saidhantik vishleshan hua he. shaskaon ke dwaara udyagikaran hone se upnivesh sadaa shaasha raashtra ke poorak bane rahete hai, unka swayan aarthik vyakti ho nahin rahata. shaasha shasit main bhavi shatruta evam hinsa ko raajnneeti kaa bijaropan isi vyavastha kaa fal he. falat: ugrata, dhwans, sangharsh kaa manobhaav bana he. upnivesh daridrata, ashiksha, rog, maansik hinata aadi ke pratik ban jate hai.
 isaki vyakhya prajateey ucchata ke rup main bhi kii gai he. francisi vichaarkon ne upniveshwaad ko sabhyataa ke vikaas kaa saadhan mana he. jon stuart mil ke anusaar is vyavastha ne pichde deshon ko ucch deshon ke sampark main aane kaa avasar diya he. luguard ise do drushtio se prastut karata he. upniveshon ke vikaas kaa daayitv shreshth desh par ho jaataa he or unako sankuchit sthiti vishwa ke sandarbh main pramukh hoti he. lekin bhartiya swatantraaa or afresin jaagran se in manyataon kii chunautiyaan mili hai. panchasheel kii niti upniveshwaad kaa antarrashtriya pratwaad he or 1955 main bandung prastaav iskaa parinaam. sanyukt raashtra kaa ghoshnaptr isi kii sweekruti he. isamen raajnneetik, aarthik, saamaajik, shekshanik aadi sabhi prakaar ke upniveshwaad kii ninda kii gai he. iske saath hi upanvishevad ne nayaa rup dhaaran kiya he. vishwa ko viksit aviksit jaise bhaagon main baant diya gaya he. isamen pravidhik, shaikshanik, sainik, sahaayta aadi kaa kaary upnivesh kar rahaa he. ise abhinav upniveshwaad kaha jaataa he. iskaa pratik he america.
 raajnneeti se adhik mahatvapurn he aarthik paksh. isaki vyavastha adam smith kii pustak "velth aav neshns" se prarambh hoti he. aarthik siddhant se vibhinn kaal rahe hai-1660 se purvavarti, 1660 se 1776 tak, 1776 se 1870 tak, 1870 se aage. beesvin shatabdi main sabhi prakaar ke upniveshwaad kii alachenaayen hui hai. inamen mukhya he macrsvaadi aalochana. lenin ne upniveshon kaa sansthagat evam anrarashtriya swaroop prastut karte hue kaha ki samrajyavad kaa antim charan he "mahajanee poonjeewaad." prativaadee vichaardhaara je.e. habsan ne prastut kii. uske anusaar europe ke videshon ko swayan apane desh main kharch karnaa chahie. upniveshwaad ke prayog ke teen str hai-prarambh se 1830 tak, 1830 se 19veen shatee ke ant tak, 1890 se 1945 tak.
 upniveshwaad main europea deshon ne poonji ke dwaara "sarvocch laabh" praapt kiya he. unhone ekadhikaaravaadi poonjeewaad ko janm diya he. abhinav upniveshwaad kaa taatpary he aarthik drushti se viksit deshon ke dwaara aviksit deshon kaa shoshan. pichde deshon kii sari aarthik vyavastha unpar nirbhar rahati he. unamem vishesh vyapaarik sambandh hote hai. videshi puunji par unhen nirbhar rahanaa padataa he. bazaar, videshi vastu, poonji, pravidhik kushalta, apane desh ke logon ko prashikshan dena sabhi viksit deshon ke niyantran main rahete hai. isi main "sahaayta kii raajnneeti", "videshi poonji", "antarrashtriya ekikaran", "aarthik sanghatan", "antarrashtriya vyapaar niyantran", jaisi vyavasthaayen viksit hui hai. vastut: viksit or aviksit deshon main antarnihit asantulan kaa parinaam he "abhinav upniveshwaad".
