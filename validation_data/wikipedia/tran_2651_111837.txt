 dow ram manohar lohia avadh vishvavidhyalay bharat kaa ek pramukh vishvavidhyalay he. yah uttar pradesh ke faijabad shahar main sthit he. ise uttar pradesh sarkaar dwaara 25 july 1975 ko vishvavidhyalay kaa darja diya gaya. iskaa naam prasiddh samaajawaadee netaa evam aarthik chintak raamamanohar lohia ke naam par rakhaa gaya he. 
 varsh 1984 main yah vishvavidhyalay awasaiya vishvavidhyalay ban gaya. pehle yahan chaar vibhaag armbh hue - itihaas vibhaag, samskruti evam puraatatva vibhaag, gramin arthvyavastha, ganit tathaa sankhyikee vibhaag or thos avasthaa bhautikee vibhaag.
