 aal bin ali arab rajyon main vishesh rup se bahrain, katar, kuwait, sanyukt arab emirate or saudi arab ke purvi praant main sthit ek sunni arab up-janajaatiy sangh he.
 aal bin ali al utabi janajati, mul yutub ke vanshaj hai jinhone bahrain par vijay praapt kii. aal bin ali kabeeley ke sadasyon ke vishaal bahumat yaa to bani sulem yaa aal-maded janajatiyon se nikalate hai.
 bahrain or katar jaise farsi khadi deshon par unka sakaaratmak prabhaav padaa. al bin ali janajati ke bare main kai pathyapustaken or kavitaaon ko likhaa gaya tha. aal bin ali ke paas bahrain or katar main ek swa-shasit janajati ke rup main vyaavhaarik rup se swatantra sthiti thi. unhone saat trikonon ke saath chaar laal or teen safed dhariyon ke saath dhvaj kaa upyog kiya, jise bahrain, katar, kuwait main al-sulaami dhvaj or saudi arab ke purvi praant ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
