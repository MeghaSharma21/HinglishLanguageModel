 srilanka dakshin asia main hind mahaasaagar ke uttari bhaag main sthit ek dweepiya desh he. bharat ke dakshin main sthit is desh kii duuri bharat se maatr 31 kilometre he. 1972 tak iskaa naam ceylone tha, jise 1972 main badalakar lanka tathaa 1978 main iske aage sammansoochak shabd "shri" jodakar srilanka kar diya gaya. srilanka kaa sabase bada nagar colombo samudri parivahan kii drushti se ek mahatvapurn bandargah he.
 shri lanka kaa pichley 3000 varsh kaa likhit itihaas uplabdh he. 125,000 varsh purv yahan maanav bastiyaan hone ke pramaan mili hai. shri lanka kii samskruti atyant sampann he or yahan se 29 isapurv main chaturth buddha sangeeti ke samay rachit buddha granth praapt hue hai. 
 pracheen kaal se hi srilanka par shahi sinhal vansh kaa shashan rahaa he. samay-samay par dakshin bhartiya rajwanshon kaa bhi akraman bhi is par hota rahaa he. teesri sadi isa purv main maury samraat ashok ke putr mahendra ke yahaan aane par buddha dharm kaa aagaman hua.
 solahavin sadi main europea shaktiyon ne srilanka main apana vyapaar sthapit kiya. desh chaay, rabad, chini, coffee, dalacheenee sahit anya masaalon kaa niryaatak ban gaya. pehle portugal ne colombo ke paas apana durg banaaya. dhire-dhire portugaliyon ne apana prabhutv aasapaas ke ilaakon main bana liya. srilanka ke nivaasiyon main unke prati ghrinaa ghar kar gai. unhone dach logon se madad kii apil kii. 1630 isvi main dachon ne portugaliyon par hamalaa bolaa or unhen maar giraaya, lekin iskaa asar srilankai par bhi hua or un par dachon ne or zyada kar thope diya. 1660 tak angrejon kaa dhyaan bhi is par gaya. netherland par france ke adhikaar hone ke baad angrejon ko dar hua ki srilanka ke dach ilaakon par francisi adhikaar ho jayega. taduparant unhone dach ilaakon par adhikaar karnaa aarambh kar diya. 1800 isvi ke aate-aate tateey ilaakon par angrejon kaa adhikaar ho gaya. 1818 tak antim rajya candi ke raja ne bhi aatmasamarpan kar diya or is tarah sampoorn srilanka par angrejon kaa adhikaar ho gaya. dwitiya vishwayuddh ke baad 4 farvari 1948 ko desh ko united kingdom se purna swatantraaa mili.
 hind mahaasaagar ke uttari bhaag me sthit is dweep raashtra kii bhoomi kendriya pahaado tathaa tateey maidaanon se milakar bani he. vaarshik varsha 2500 se 5000 mi.me. tak hoti he. vaarshik taapmaana kaa ausat maidani ilaakon main 27 degree celsius tathaa nuvar eli ke ilaake main 15 degree celsius rahata he. is desh kaa vistaar 6-10 gigri uttari akshansh ke madhya hone, tathaa chaaro ore samudr se ghire hone kii vajah se yah ek ushn katibandheey jalwaayu kshetra he. yahaan kii ausat saapekshik ardrata din main 70% se lekar raat ke samay main 90% tak ho jaati he.
 prashaskeey rup se srilanka 9 praanton main banta hua he. in 9 praanton main kul 25 jile hai. in jilon ke tahat mandaleey sachivalay aate hai or inke ghatak ikaiyon ko gram sevak khand kehete hai.
 yah desh ek bahujaateey tathaa bahudharmik he. yahaan ke nivaasiyon main 74% sinhalai, 18% tamil, 7% isai tathaa 1% anya jatimul ke hai.
 srilankai grihayuddh srilanka main bahusankhyak singhla or alpsankhyak tamilo ke bich 23 july, 1983 se aarambh hua grihayuddh he. mukhyatah yah srilankai sarkaar or algaavvaadi gut littey ke bich ladaa jane vaala yuddh he. 30 maheenon ke sainya abhiyaan ke baad may 2009 main srilankai sarkaar ne littey ko parrast kar diya.
 lagbhag 25 varshon tak chale is grihayuddh main donon ore se badi sankhya main log maare gaye or yah yuddh dweepiya raashtra kii arthvyastha or paryaavaran ke liye ghaatak siddh hua. littey dwaara apanaai gai yuddh-nitiyon ke chalate 32 deshon ne ise aatankwaadi guto kii shrenee main rakhaa jinamein bharat, australia, canada, europea sangh ke bahut se sadsy raashtra or anya kai desh hai. ek-chauthai sadi tak chale is jaateey sangharsh main sarkari aankadon ke anusaar hi lagbhag 80,000 log maare gaye hai. .
