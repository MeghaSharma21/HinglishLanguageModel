 haj, ek islamic tirthayatra or muslim logon kaa pavitra shahar makka main prativarsh hone vaala vishwa kaa sabase bada jamaavada he. yah islam ke paanch mul stambh main se ek he, saath hi yah ek dharmik kartavya he jise apane jeevankaal main kam se kam ek baar puraa karnaa har us muslim chaahe stri ho yaa purush kaa kartavya he jo sakasham sharir hone ke saath saath iskaa kharch bhi uthaa paane main samarth ho. shaareerik or aarthik rup se haj karne main sakasham hone kii sthiti ko isti'taah kaha jaataa he or wo muslim he jo is shart ko puraa karata he mustaati kehalaata he. haj muslim logon kii ekajutata kaa pradarshan hone ke saath saath unka allaah main vishwaas hone kaa bhi dyotak he.
 yah tirthayatra islamic calendar ke 12 wein or antim maheene dhu al hijjah kii 8 veen se 12 veen taarikh tak kii jaati he. islamic calendar ek chandra calendar he isliye isamen, pashchimi deshon main prayog main aane vaale gregorian calendar se gyaarah din kam hote hai, isiilie gregorian calendar ke anusaar haj kii tareekhen saal dar saal badalati rahati hai. iharem wo vishesh aadhyatmik sthiti he jisamen musalmaan haj ko dauraan rahete hai. 
 7 veen shatabdi se haj islamic paigambar mohammad ke jivan ke saath judaa hua he, lekin musalmaan maanate hai ki makka kii tirthayatra kii yah rasm hajaaron saalon se yani ki ibrahim ke samay se chali aa rahi he. tirthayatri un lakhon logon ke juluus main shaamil hote hai jo ek saath haj ke saptaah main makka main jamaa hote hai or yahan par kai anushthanon main hissaa lete hai: prayek vyakti ek ghanaakaar imaarat kaaba ke chaaron ore vamavart saat baar chalata he jo ki muslimon ke liye prarthana kii disha he, al safa or al marwaah naamak pahadiyon ke bich aage or peeche chalata he, zamazam ke kuen se pani peetaa he, chaukasee main khadaa hone ke liye araafaat parvat ke maidaanon main jaataa he or ek shaitan ko pathar maarane kii rasm puraa karne ke liye pathar phenkata he. uske baad tirthayatri apane sar mundavate hai, pashu bali kii rasm karte hai or iske baad id ul-adha naamak teen divasiy vaishvik utsav manaate hai.
