 udit narayan hindi filmon ke ek prasiddh nepali gayak hai.
 udit narayan kaa janm 27 navambar ko bihar ke supaul jile main hua. unhone apana pehla hindi gaanaa mukesh ji ke saath gaaya. unhen teen deshiy puraskaar tathaa paanch film fere puraskaar mili hai. varsh 2009 main bharat sarkaar ne unhen padam shri puraskaar se sammanit kiya.
 udit narayan nepal main or bharat main bhi ek prakhyat gayak ke rup main jane jate hai. nepali film main unhone bahut hit gane gaae hai or unka geet adhiktar logon ko pasand he.unke swar main jaadu he. ve kishor avasthaa se hi gayan kala ke kshetra main lag gayi the jo ki aaj is mukaam par he. sampurna bollywood main unhen aaj bhi ek behatar gayak mana jaataa he. nepal main abhi ke samay main bhi unke swar se tulana kisi bhi gayak se nahin kii ja sakati he.
 udit ji kii maatrubhaasha maithili hai or wo bihar ke mithilanchal ilaake se aate hai. jaise ki nepal or bharat ke bich beti or roti kaa sambandh he usee tarah unka nanihaal bharat ke bihar rajya main he.
