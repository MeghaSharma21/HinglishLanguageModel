 19veen shatabdi ke madhya main ooshmaagatiki ke do siddhanton kaa pratipaadan kiya gaya tha, jinhen ushmagatiki ke pratham evam dwitiya siddhant kehete hai. 20veen shatabdi ke prarambh main do anya siddhanton kaa pratipaadan kiya gaya he jinhen ushmagatiki kaa shoonyavaan tathaa tritiy siddhant kehete hai.
