 saptkand ramayana asmi bhashaa kaa ramayana he. isaki rachana 14veen shatabdi main asmi ke bhaktakavi madhav kandali ne kii thi. sanskrit se kisi aadhunik bhartiya bhashaa main anudit yah pratham ramayana he. iske saath hi yah kriti asmi bhashaa kii sabase pracheen rachanaaon main se ek he. 
 saptkand ramayana kii ek pramukh visheshta yah he ki isamen ram, sita tathaa anya logon kaa chithran nayak ke rup main nahin kiya gaya he.
