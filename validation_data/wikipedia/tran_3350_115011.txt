 nepolian bonapart ke samrajya tathaa spain, britain or portugal kii sammilit senaaon ke bich hua yuddh spain kaa swatantraaa sangram yaa prayadwipiya yuddh  kehalaata he.
 spain ne samay-samay par france ko sahaayta di thi. is samay spain kaa shaasha charles chaturth tha kintu uske niskriy shashan ke sthaan par janamat uske putr rajkumaar ferdinand ko shaasha banaan chaahati thi. atah charles ne ferdinand ke paksh main pad tyaag diya. isi samay nepolian ne ferdinand ko ek tarah se najarband kar apane bhai neples ke raja joseph ko spain kaa raja banaaya or neples apane bahanoi mura ko de diya. vastutah portugal main france va spain ke sanyukt abhiyaan ke kaaran nepolian ko francisi senaayen spain main bhejne kaa avasar mil gaya or mauka dekh usne spane par adhikaar kar liya.
 nepolian ke is kadam se spanish janata apamaanit mahasoos kar rahi thi. spainvaasiyon main rashtriya bhaavana kaa sanchaar hua or puraa raashtra nepolian ke viruddh uth khadaa hua. jagah-jagah prabandh samitiyaan sthapit kii jane lagi, 'pope ke shatru' ke vinaash kaa acha avasar dekhte hue catholic padariyon ne logon ko uksaana shuru kiya. Zunta naamak sanghathan ne gorilla paddati se ladane ke liye logon ko prashikshit karnaa aarambh kar diya. spain kii janata kii najar main nepolian rashtriya ekta kaa sanhaarak or rajmukut kaa vinaashak tha. atah unhone gorilla yuddh shuru kar diya. yah yuddh 1808 se 1814 tak chalata rahaa.
 july 1808 main belan ke yuddh main francisi sena parajeet hui. sthal main, nepolian kii sena kii yah pratham paraajay thi. spainvaasiyon kaa utsaah badha or puure europe main sansani fail gai. jojeff spain chhod bhaag khadaa hua. aisi sthiti main nepolian ne spain par hamalaa kiya or use parajeet kar punah joseph ko singhaasana par baithaaya. spain main fanse rahane ke kaaran samrajya par vah dhyaan nahin de paaya. 1809 i. main europea gatividhyon ke kaaran nepolian ko ek badi sena spain main chhod madhya europe kii ore janaa padaa. iske baad uskaa spain aana sambhav nahin hua. spain main rashtravaadi punah sakriy ho gaye or joseph kaa shashan ladkadaane laga. england ne spain kaa samarthan dekar kai sthanon par francisi sena ko parrast kiya or antat spain francisi aadhipaty se mukt hua.
 spain kaa yuddh nepolian ke liye atyant vinaashkari sabit hua. ek taraf isane jahaan nepolian ke lakhon sainikon va yogya senapatiyon ko nasht kar diya to duusari taraf yuddh main vyast rahane ke kaaran shesh europe kii ore dhyaan bhi nahin de sakaa. spain kii vijay ne nepolian kii aparajeyata ke myth ko todaa or europe main soi rashtriyata kii bhaavana ko jagaaya. isi rashtrawaad kii bhaavana se perrit hokar aage austria ne france ke viruddh yuddh kii ghoshnaa kar di. is prakaar spanish yuddh ke sandarbh main nepolian kaa yah kahana thik he ki spain ke nasoor ne mujhe barbaad kar diya.
