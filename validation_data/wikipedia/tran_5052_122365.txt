 abhayananda amereki mahila evam mahila swami bhikshu thim jab vivekanand' ke mishan kaa armbh hua tha. unke purv ashram kaa naam merry louis tha.
 louis ko vivekanand ke dwaara hi thousand izland park main 1985 main armbh kiya gaya evam unka mathvaasi naam bhi vivekananda ne hi diya kintu baad main vah unase alag hokar swayan hi usee naam se upadesh evam darshan dene lag gayi. abhayananda daawa karte hai kii sampoorn vishwa main keval vahi mahila swami hai.
