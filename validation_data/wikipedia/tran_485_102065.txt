 niyuddh ek pracheen bhartiya yuddh kala he. niyuddh kaa shabdic arth he 'bina hathiyaar ke yuddh' arthath swayan nihshastr rahete huye akraman tathaa sangrakshan karne kii kala. yah ek nihshastr yuddh kala he jisamen haath or paanv ke prahaar shaamil hai. iskaa mukhya uddeshy aatmaraksha he. mahabharat aadi pracheen granthon main niyuddh kaa ullekh miltaa he. vartmaan main niyuddh kaa abhyaas mukhyatahindian niyuddh marshall arts federation, rashtriya swayamsevak sangh evam iske anushangik sanghathanon dwaara apane prashikshan shiviron tathaa shakhaon main kiya jaataa he.
 saadharantah vyakti nihattha hi ghumta he, aisi sthiti main hone vaale akraman se apane sharir ko hi hathiyaar maanakar apana sangrakshan karnaa isamen sikhaaya jaataa he. iske madhyam se sahaj hi apane andar kii soi huyi 80 pratishat se 100 pratishat shakti kaa jaagran karnaa sikh sakate hai. saamanytah saadhaaran manushya apni 20 pratishat shaktiyon kaa hi upyog karata he. 
 nidhyuddh ek pracheen bhartiya kala he. iske adidev bhagwaan shiv maane jate hai. maharishi vashishtha ke kaal main ise nidhyuddh kehete the. vividh kalkhand main bahuyuddh, mallayuddh, pranayuddh aadi iske vibhinn ang the. dakshin bhartiya caloripyttu main bhi is kala ke avyav hai. yah bhartiya vidya buddha bhikshuon tathaa pravasi bharatiyon dwaara anek deshon main gayi tathaa isse anek yuddh kalaon kaa pradurbhaav hua. pashchim main iskaa upyog samrik drushti se kiya gaya or yahi iskaa ekmaatr uddeshy banakar rah gaya jabaki bhartiya drushtikon shaareerik, maansik tathaa aadhyatmik drushti se manushya kaa purna vikaas karnaa rahaa he.
 is kala ke vartmaan swaroop kaa vikaas indian niyuddh marshall arts federation ke rup main san 1985 main grand master shri arjuna singh ghale dwaara kiya gaya ! jinhone nidhyuddh ke utthan ke liye apana jivan samarpit kiya he ! aaj ham log bhi niyuddh ke utthan ke liye kaary kar rahe he
