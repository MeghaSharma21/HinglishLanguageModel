 saarv nirdeshaankit kaal, samay kaa vah praathmik maanak he jisase vishwa kaa samay or ghadiyaan niyamit hotin hai. yah samay, shunya ansh kii deshaantar rekha ke madhya saura samay ke baraabar hota he . angrej log praayah greenich madhya samay ko hi utisi jaisaa maanate hai.
 samanvit saarvatrik samay  vishwa ke samay kaa praathmik maanak he jiskei dwaara vishvabhar main ghadiyaan evam samay niyantrit kiye jate hai. yah greenich madhya samay ke bahut saare anuvatiyon main se ek he. saadhaaran kaaryon kii drushti se samanvit saarvatrik samay or greenich madhya samay ek hi hai, kintu greenich madhya samay ab vaigyanik samudaay dwaara parishuddhta purvak paribhaashit nahin kiya jaataa he.
 anterrashtriya parmanvik samay aniyamit antaraalon par jode gayi lone sellind sahit he. yah lone sellind pathvi kii dhimi hoti gati ke kaaran jode jate hai. inase UTC kii UT1 se sameeptha bani rahati he. UT1 ausat saura samay he, jo ki greenich kii saahi vedhashala main dekha jaataa he.
 UTC or UT1 ke bich kaa antar 0.9 s se adhik nahin ho paata, atev yadi ucch parishuddhta aavashyak naa ho, to ek saamaanya turm vishwavyaapee samay yaa universal time, prayog kii ja sakati he.
 naimitthik prayogon hetu, greenich maanak samay bhi UTC or UT1 samaan hi hota he. sanshay mitaane hetu saamaaytah savis hi prayog hota he, GMT ke prayog se bachaa jaataa he.
