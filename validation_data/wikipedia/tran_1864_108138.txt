 es.aar. bommai banaam union of india SCC1 3) ke aitihaasik faisle main bharat ke sarvocch nyaayalay ne anuchhed 356 or isse jude vibhinn praavdhaanon par vistaar se charchaa kii thi. anuchhed 356 ke durupyog ko is faisle ke dwaara roak diya gaya. is maamle ke kaaran kendra-rajya sambandhon par baari prabhaav padaa.
 sitambar 1988 main karnataka main janata party or lok dal party ne milakar ek nai party janata dal banaakar sarkaar banane kaa daawa kiya tha. janata dal esaaar bommai ke netritv main rajya kii bahumat vaali party bani thi. mantralay main 13 sadasyon rakhaa gaya. lekin iske do din baad hi janata dal vidhayak ke aar molakeri ne rajyapal ke samaksh ek patr pesh kiya jisame unhone bommai ke khilaaf arji thi. unhone apane patr ke saath 19 anya vidhayakon kii sahamati patr bhi jari kiya tha.
 iske baad rajyapal pi venkatsubaiya ne raashtrapati ko ek report bheji jisamen kaha gaya tha ki sattadhari party ke kai vidhayak usase khafa hai. rajyapal ne aage likhaa tha ki vidhayakon dwaara samarthan waapas lene ke ke baad mukhyamantri bommai ke paas bahumat nahin bachataa he jisase unhen sarkaar banane nahin diya ja saktaa. yah samvidhan ke khilaaf tha or unhone raashtrapati se bhi sifarish kii thi ki vah unhen anuchhed 356 ke tahat shakti kaa prayog karen. rajyapal kii is report ke aadhaar par centre sarkaar ne anuchhed 356 ke tahat raashtrapati se ghoshnaa karvaa kar bommai kii sarkaar ko barkhaast kar diya gaya.
 haalanki kuch din baad hi unniis vidhayak jinke hastakshar ke bal par asantosh prastaav pesh kiya gaya tha, unhone yah daawa kiya ki pehle patr main unke hastakshar jaali the or unhone phir se apane gathbandhan ko samarthan dene kii pushti kii. iske baad maamle ko lekar court main yachika daakhil kii gai.
 is mukademe ke nirnay main sarvocch nyaayalay ne rajya kii sarkaron ko barkhaast karne sambandhi anuchhed kii vyakhya kii or kaha ki anuchhed 356 ke tahat yadi kendra sarkaar rajya main chuni hui sarkaar ko barkhaast karti he to sarvocch nyaayalay sarkaar barkhaast karne ke kaarnon kii samiksha kar saktaa he or nyaayalay centre se us saamagree ko court ke samaksh prastut karne ke liye kah saktaa jiske aadhaar par rajya kii sarkaar ko barkhaast kiya gaya he.
