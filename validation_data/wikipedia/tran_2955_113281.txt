 nepal pragna pratishthan bhashaa, sahitya, samskruti tathaa darshan ke sangrakshan, samvardhan or vikaas hetu nepal sarkaar dwaara sthapit sanstha he. isiilie iskaa pehla naam nepal rajkeey pragna pratishthan tha, jise kalantar main parivartit kiya gaya. samprati is pratishthan ke kulapati kavi bairangi kaanila hai tathaa sadsy sachiv sanat regmi hai. 
 nepal main rashtriya saanskrithik academy ke liye ek andolan 20 veen sadi ke dauraan shuru hua, rashtriya aankadon ke saath nepali kavi lakshmi prasad devkota ne isaki sthapana kii pahal kii. unki pahal par yah pratishthan 1957 main astitv main aayaa or iskaa naam padaa nepal sahitya kala pratishthan . yah baad main royal nepal academy adhinium 1974 ke paarit hone ke baad nepal rajkeey pragna pratishthan ke rup main namit kiya gaya. 2008 main ek loktantric sangheey ganraajya main nepal ke sankraman ke baad nepal kii sansad dwaara adhiniyamit nepal academy adhinium 2007 ke praavdhaan ke anusaar iskaa naam nepal pragna pratishthan kar diya gaya.
 bhashaa, sahitya, samskruti, saamaajik vigyaan or darshan nepal pragna pratishthan ke paanch mahatvapurn vibhaag he.
 nepal pragna pratishthan ne 'biihat nepali shabdkosh' kaa nirmaan kiya he.
