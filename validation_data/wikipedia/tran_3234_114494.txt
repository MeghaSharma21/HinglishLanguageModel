 meets brihaspati grah kaa sab se andruni upgrah he. is grah ke astitv ke bare main sab se pehle 1979 main pataa chalaa jab antariksh yaan wayager pratham brihaspati ke paas se guzara or meets ko uske dwaara khinchi gayi tasveeron main dekha gaya. uske baad 1996 se lekar sitambar 2003 tak gallileo yaan ne bhi iske kai chitra utaare. meets ke brihaspati ke itnaa paas hone se or brihaspati se itnaa chota hone se yah upgrah brihaspati kii sthiramukhi parikrama karata he, yani parikrama karte hue meets kaa ek hi rukh hameshaa brihaspati kii ore hota he. brihaspati ke tagade gurutvkarshak khichaav se is upgrah kii golai bhi besangi ho gayi he - isaki lambaai isaki chaudaai se do guna adhik he. yah upgrah brihaspati kii rosh seemaa ke andar aata he or yadi meets main andruni chipkaav kam hota to brihaspati kaa bhayankar gurutvkarshan ise tod chukaa hota. vaigyanikon kaa maananaa he ke is upgrah ke kuch anshon kaa chura banakar brihaspati kii mukhya upgrahi challe ke banane main prayog hua he.
