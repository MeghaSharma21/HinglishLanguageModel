 hemkunt sahib chamoli jila, uttarakhand, bharat main sthit sikhon kaa ek prasiddh tirth sthaan he. yah himalay main 4632 meter kii oonchaai par ek barfili jhil ke kinare saat pahaado ke bich sthit he. in saat pahaado par nishaan sahib jhulate hai. is tak rishikesh-badrinaath saans-rasta par padate gobindghat se keval paidal chadhaai ke dwaara hi pahunchaa ja saktaa he.
 yahan gurudwara shri hemkunt sahib sushobhit he. is sthaan kaa ullekh guru gobind singh dwaara rachit dasam granth main aata he. is kaaran yah un logon ke liye vishesh mahatv rakhata he jo dasam granth main vishwaas rakhte hai.
 hemkunt ek sanskrit naam he jo hem or kunda se aayaa he. dasam granth ke mutabiq yah vah jagah he jahaan pandu rajo ne yogya sudhara tha.
 yahan pehle ek mandir tha jiskaa nirmaan bhagwaan ram ke anuj lakshmana ne karavaaya tha. sikhon ke dasven guru gobind singh ne yahan puja archana kii thi. baad main ise gurudwara dhoshit kar diya gaya. is darshneey tirth main chaaron ore se barf kii unchi chotiyon kaa pratibimb vishalkay jhil main atyant manoram evam romaanch se paripurna lagata he. isi jhil main hathi parvat or sapt rishi parvat shrinkhalaaon se pani aata he. ek choti jaldhara is jhil se nikalati he jise himganga kehete hai. jhil ke kinare sthit lakshmana mandir bhi atyant darshneey he. atyaadhik oonchaai par hone ke kaaran varsh main lagbhag 7 maheene yahan jhil barf main jam jaati he. phulon kii ghati yahan kaa nikatatam paryaton sthal he.
