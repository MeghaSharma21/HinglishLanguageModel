 paath dekhen
 sadafuli yaa sadabahaar barhon maheene khilne vaale phulon kaa ek paudha he. isaki aath jatiyaan hai. inamen se saat madagascar main tathaa aathvin bhartiya upmahadweep main paayi jaati he. iskaa vaigyanik naam ketharenths he. bharat main paayi jane vaali prajati kaa vaigyanik naam ketharenths roses he. ise pashchimi bharat ke log sadafuli ke naam se bulaate he.
 madagascar mul kii yah phooldaar jhaadi bharat main kitni lokapriy he iskaa pataa isi baat se chal jaataa he ki lagbhag har bhartiya bhashaa main isko alag naam diya gaya he- oriya main apanskanti, tamil main sadakadu malliki, telegu main billagennesr, punjabi main ratanjot, bangla main nayantara yaa gulfirangi, marathi main sadafuli or malayalam main ushamalari. iske shwet tathaa bangnee abhavale chote gucchhon se saje sundar laghuvriksh bharat kii kisi bhi ushn jagah kii shobhaa badhaate hue saalon saal baarah maheene dekhe ja sakate hai. iske andakaar pattey daaliyon par ek-duusare ke vipareet lagate hai or jhaadi kii badhwaar itani saafe suthri or salikedaar hoti he ki jhaadiyon kii kaant chhant kii kabhi zarurat nahin padati.
 vaise to yah jhaadi itani jaandaar he ki bina dekhbhaal ke bhi falti-phoolati rahati he, kintu reshedaar domat mitti main thodi-si compost khaad milane par aakarshak phulon se ladi-fadi sadabahaar kaa saundarya kisi ke bhi hriday ko prafullit kar saktaa he. iske fal bahut se bijon se bhare hue golakaar hote hai. isaki pattiyon, jad tathaa danthlon se nikalanevaala doodh vishaiylla hota he. paudhon ke saamne bhi samasyaayen hoti hai. ped-paudhe chaahate hai ki unke fal to jaanavar khaaen, taaki unke beej dur-dur tak ja saken, kintu yathasambhav unki pattiyaan tathaa jad na khaaen. isliye anek vrikshon ke fal to khaadya hote hai, kintu pattiyaan, jad aadi kadave yaa zahreele. sadabahaar ne is samasya kaa samaadhaan apane falon ko khaadya banaakar tathaa pattiyon va jadon ko kaduva tathaa vishakt banaakar kiya he. aise vishesh gun paudhon main vishesh kshareey rasayanon dwaara aate hai.
 viksit deshon main raktchaap shaman kii khoj se pataa chalaa ki 'sadabahaar' jhaadi main yah kshar acchhee maatra main hota he. isliye ab europe bharat china or america ke anek deshon main is paudhe kii kheti hone lagi he. anek deshon main ise khaansi, gale kii karaash or fefdon ke sankraman kii chikitsa main istemaal kiya jaataa he. sabase rochak baat yah he ki ise madhumeh ke upachaar main bhi upyogi paaya gaya he. vaigyanikon kaa kahana he ki sadabahaar main darjanon kshar aise hai jo rakt main shakar kii maatra ko niyantrit rakhte he. jab shodh hua to 'sadabahaar' ke anek gunon kaa pataa chalaa - sadabahaar paudha barood - jaise visphotak padaarthon ko pachaakar unhen nirmal kar detaa he. yah kori vaigyanik jigyaasa bhar shant nahin karata, varan vyavhaar main visphotak-bhandaron vaali lakhon acre zamin ko surakshit evam upyogi bana rahaa he. bharat main hi 'kendriya aushadhiy evam sugandh paudha santhaan' dwaara kii gai khojon se pataa chalaa he ki 'sadabahaar' kii pattiyon main 'vinicrstin' naamak kshareey padaarth bhi hota he jo cancer, visheshkar rakt cancer main bahut upyogi hota he. aaj yah vishakt paudha sanjivani booti kaa kaam kar rahaa he. bagichon kii baat karen to 1980 tak yah phulonvali kyaariyon ke liye sabase lokapriy paudha ban chukaa tha, lekin iske rangon kii sankhya ek hi thi- gulabi. 1998 main iske do nae rang grape cooler or piparmint cooler viksit kiye gaye.
 1991 main ron parker kii kuch nai prajatiyaan bazaar main ain. inamen se prity in white or parasol ko aal america sellection puraskaar mila. inhen pan america seed company dwaara ugaaya or bechaa gaya. isi varsh california main wollar genetics ne parker breeding program kii tropicana shrunkhlaa ko bazaar main utaara. in sadabahaar prajatiyon ke phulon main nae rang to the hi, aakaar bhi bada tha or pankhuriyaan ek duusare par chadhi hui thim. 1993 main parker germaplasm ne pasifaka naam se kuch nae rang prastut kiye. jisamen pehali baar sadabahaar ko laal rang diya gaya. iske baad to sadabahaar ke rangon kii jhadi lag gai or aaj bazaar main lagbhag har rang ke sadabahaar paudhon kii bharmaar he. 
 yah phool sundar to he hi aasaani se har mausam main ugta he, har rang main khilata he or iske gunon kaa bhi koi jawaab nahin, shaayad yahi sab dekhakar neshnal garden beuro ne san 2002 ko year aaf vinka ke liye chunaa. vinka yaa vinkaroza, sadabahaar kaa angrezi naam he.
