 rajesultanpur faijabad-baliya tathaa ye rashtriya rajmarg 233bi maarg par sarayu nadi ke maidan main basaa he. nagar ke peeche se 'choti sarayu' naamak nadi bahati he jo azamgarh tathaa ambedkar nagar ko vibhajit karti he.mukhya sadaken:
 rajesultanpur se najdiki shaharon kii duuri:
 rajesultanpur kaa vyavsaay mukhyatah kheti tathaa gramin aavashyaktaao par aadhaarit he. rajesultanpur ko chaaro taraf hare bhare laharaate gaanv he tathaa unki sabhi aavashyaktaao kii purti rajesultanpur se hoti he. urvarak, kitnashak, kheti kii mashine evam rosemara kii samraagi mil jaati he. rajesultanpur puure purvanchal evam avadh main apni tambaaku ke liai prasiddh he.
 avadh main gonda ke baad sabase adhik tambaaku kaa utpaadan rajesultanpur main hota he. rajesultanpur kii tambaaku vishwa prasiddh he jis kaaran gutko, paan masala main rajesultanpuri tambaaku likhaa jaataa he.
