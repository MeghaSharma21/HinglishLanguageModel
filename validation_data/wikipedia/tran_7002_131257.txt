 prakram abhiyantriki abhiyantriki kii vah shakha he jisamen kisi laabhadaayak jeevvaigyanik, rasinic yaa bhautik prakriya kii ruparekha nirdhaarit, uski gati va utpaadan par niyantran or uske chaalan main sabase adhik dakshata praapt kii jaati he.
 kisi bhi zaruri audyogic kaary main prakram abhiyantriki laane se aarthik or anya prakaar kaa faaydaa ho saktaa he, masalan japani vaahan company toyota ne jab prakram abhiyantriki ke prayog se apane kaarakaanon main purzon ke bahaav kii vidhi badal di to unki gadiyon main aarmabhik karaabi kaa dar ghatakar lagbhag shunya ho gaya. is se bazaar main unhen dhire-dhire sabase vishwasaneey vaahnirmaataa hone kii pratishtha mili or san 2012 main vah duniya kii sabase badi vaahan-company thi. dhyaan den ki 1970 ke dashak tak japani gadiyon ko america gadiyon kii tulana main ghatiya mana jaataa tha.
