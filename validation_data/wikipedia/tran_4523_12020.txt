 aungaari surya mandir kaa nirmaan teesri shatabdi isa purv main karavaaya gaya tha. yah mandir bihar ke nalanda jile ke aungaari naamak sthaan par sthit he. ek prasiddh talab ke tat par sthit yah surya mandir bihar kii kala evam samskruti kaa parichaayak he. is mandir kii khaas visheshta yah he ki yahan manauti purna hone kii aashaa me shraddhalu vishesh mudra main bhagwaan surya ko ardhya dete hai.      yah bahaar rajya ke nalanda jile ke ekangarsarai prakhand main sthit he. yahan biharsharif se30 kimi0 sadak maarg dwaara tathaa ekangarsarai se 05 kimi sadak maarg dwaara jaaya ja saktaa he.   is mandir kii garbh grih pashchimmukhi he,jo desh ke anya surya mandiron se ise vishisht banaata he.
