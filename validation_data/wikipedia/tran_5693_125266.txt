 abjad aisi lipi hoti he jisamen har akshar ek vyanjan hota he. isamen swar likhaa hi nahin jaataa or pathak ko swayan hi swar kaa anumaan lagaana padataa he. prachinkaal main phonishian varnmaala ek abjad lipi thi or us se utpann unani lipi or aramai lipi donon abjad thi. isi prakaar arabi, ibrani or pahalavi lipiyaan bhi abjad thim. kai aadhunik abjad lipiyaan ashuddh hoti hai, yani unamem kabhi-kabhi swar darshaae jate hai. iskaa udhaaharan aadhunik arabi lipi he.
 "abjad" naam arabi ke pehle chaar aksharon ke naamon ko jodakar utapann hua he: a + ba + ja + da. yah naam peter dainielz ne rakhaa tha jo bhashavaigyanikon main lokapriy ho gaya. inhone hi "abugida" kaa naam bhi rakhaa tha.
