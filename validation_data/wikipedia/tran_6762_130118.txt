 edlabs imagika bharat ke khopoli main sthit 300 acre kaa theme park he. iskaa swaamitv edlabs manoranjan limited ke paas he. is park kii anumaanit dainik kshamta 15,000 aagantukon kaa hai. aaj ke taarikh tak is park ne abhi tak 35 lakh se adhik aagantukon kii mejabani kii he. 
 yahan par teen mukhya park hai: theme park, water park or snow park.
 yahan par vibhinn aayu varg ke liye alag-alag tarah ke jhule uplabdh hai. park main aae hue log ek jhule se dusre jhule kii sawari karte samay vibhinn charitron se mil sakate hai jiname tubbi- ek hathi, roberto- ek star shef, khoyaa antariksh yatri, mistar india ke mogambo evam gingerbrade main. 
 is park main milane vaale anya jhule hai alibaba evam chalis chor, bamp it boats, moshan box theatre, splash ehoy, seve da piret, happy wheels, lokaness explorer, mambo chaay chama, prince of the dark waters, 
 yah maconos-theme vaala water park dopaher kii party aayojan karne ke chalate bahut hi lokapriy he.
 yah snow park ek prakaar kaa indoor barf par aadhaarit theme park he. yah park 15,000 vargafoot ke kshetra main failaa hua he,jo ise bharat ke sabase bade barf ke parkon main se ek banaata he. is park main 45 minute ke slots hai, tikat kharidne ke dauraan aapako ek slot chunanaa hoga. aapako usee nirdhaarit samay main hi park main ghusna hai or ek baar jab aap andar aate hai to aapako sabhi sardiyon kii jaruri chijen jaise ki jacket, haath dastaane or barf hiching shoes, ni: shulk di jaati hai.
