 hanuman janmotsav ek hindu parv he. yah caitra maah kii poornima ko manaaya jaataa he. is din hanumanji kaa janm hua mana jaataa he.
 samudrmanthan ke pashchaat shiv ji ne bhagwaan vishnu kaa mohini rup dekhne kii ichha prakat kii, jo unhonen devataaon or asuron ko dikhaaya tha. unka vah aakarshak rup dekhakar vah kamaatur ho gaye. or unhone apana veerypat kar diya. vayudev ne shiv ji ke beej ko vanar raja kesari kii patni anjana ke garbh main pravisht kar diya. or is tarah anjana ke garbh se vanar rup hanuman kaa janm hua. unhen shiv kaa 11vaan rudra avatar mana jaataa he. 
 hanuman jainti ko log hanuman mandir main darshan hetu jate he. kuch log vrath bhi dhaaran kar badi utsukata or josh ke saath samarpit hokar inaki puja karte he. chunki yah kaha jaataa he ki ye bal brahmacharee the isliye inhe janeu bhi pahanaai jaati he. hanumanji kii murtiyon par sindur or chaandi kaa work chadhaane kii parampara he. kaha jaataa he ram kii lambi umr ke liye ek baar hanuman ji apane puure sharir par sindur chadha liya tha or isi kaaran unhen or unke bhakton ko sindur chadhaanaa bahoot acha lagata he jise chola kehete he. sandhya ke samay dakshin mukhi hanuman murti ke saamne shuddh hokar mantra jaap karne ko atyant mahatva diya jaataa he. hanuman jayanti par ramcharitamanas ke sundarkand paath ko padhnaa bhi hanumanji ko prasann karata he. sabhi mandiro main is din tulsidas krit ramcharitamanas evam hanuman chalisa kaa paath hota he. jagah jagah bhandare yojit kiye jate he.tamilanadu va kerala main hanuman jayanti margshirsh maah kii amavasya ko tathaa urisa main baishakh maheene ke pehle din manaai jaati he. vahiin karnataka va andhra pradesh main caitra poornima se lekar baishakh maheene ke 10wein din tak yah tyonhaar manaaya jaataa he.
 indra ke vajra se hanumanji kii thudi tut gai thi. isaliye unako hanuman kaa naam diya gaya. iske alaava ye anek naamon se prasiddh he jaise bajrang bali, maaruti, anjani sut, pavanputra, sankatmochan, kesarinandan, mahavir, kapeesh, shankar suvan aadi.
