 luanadi yaa luaanati yaa shulianadi teesri sadi isapurv se chauthi sadi isvi tak aadhunik madhya asia or china ke kshetra main shashan karne vaali pracheen shiyongnu logon kaa ek rajvansh tha. inkaa zikr mahaan itihaaskaar ke abhilekh, haan granth or anya pracheen chini itihaas-granthon main miltaa he. is parivaar ke log shiyongnu samaaj or prashasan main sarvocch ohadon par paae jate the or chanyu kii oopaadhiyaan bhi rakhte the. luanadi parivaar kaa sabase pracheen gyaat chanyu tuman tha.
