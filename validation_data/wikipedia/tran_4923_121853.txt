 jaipal kabul shahi rajvansh kaa prasiddh shaasha tha jisane 964 se 1001 i tak shashan kiya. uskaa rajya laghamaan se kashmir tak or sarhind se multan tak vistrut tha. peshawar iske rajya kaa kendra tha. vah hatpal kaa putr tathaa anandapal kaa pita tha. baari kot ke shilaalekh ke anusaar uski padavi "param bhattarak maharajaadhiraj shri jaipaldev" thi. 
 musalamaanon kaa bharat main pratham pravesh jaipal ke kaal main hua. 977 i. main gazani ke subuktagin ne usapar akraman kar kuch sthanon par adhikaar kar liya. jaipal ne pratirodh kiya, kintu parajeet hokar use sandhi kerani padi. ab peshawar tak musalamaanon kaa rajya ho gaya. duusari baar subuktagin ke putr mahmud gaznavi ne jaipal ko parajeet kiya. lagaataar paraajayon se kshubdh hokar isane apane putr anangpal ko apana uttaradhikari banaaya or aag main jalakar aatmahatya kar lee.
