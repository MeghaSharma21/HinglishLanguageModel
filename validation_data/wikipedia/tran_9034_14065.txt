 
 rock on! ek 2008 bhartiya rock sangitamay film he jise likhit or nirdeshit abhishek kapur ne kiya, nirmaataa faraan akhtar or shankar ehsaan-loy ne sangeet ke saath nirdeshit he. film farhaan akhtar or praachi desai ke safal kaa pehali bollywood film he. bollywood main un dinon yangsters kii pasand ko lekar filmein banane kaa silsilaa kuch zyada hi raftaar se chal niklaa tha. chand baras pehle tak industry ke jane maane nirmaataa tikit khidki par apni film ko hit karne ke liye aisa subject chunate, jo family class kii kasuti par khara utre. is film se lagata he ki ab najaara badalane laga he. 
 
 musical film 'rock on' kii sikval kii charchaaen to kaaphi samay se chal rahi he lekin isaki star cast ko lekar khabar he ki farhaan akhtar or arjuna rampal ke saath purab kohli sikval kaa hissaa rahenge. star cast to vahiin rahegi lekin 'rock on' ke nirdeshak rahe abhishek kapur is sikval kaa hissaa nahin honge. aid filmmekar shujaat saudagar se is sikval ke liye sampark kiya gaya he. haalanki anya kalakaaron ko lekar abhi nirnay lenaa baaki he. is sikval kii shooting agle saal 2015 main shuru hogi.
