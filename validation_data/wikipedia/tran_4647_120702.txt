 sendha namak, saindhav namak, lahori namak yaa helit sodium chloride, yani saadhaaran namak, kaa crystal pathar-jaise rup main milane vaala khanij padaarth he. yah aksar rangahin yaa safed hota he, haalanki kabhi-kabhi anya padaarthon kii maujuudagi se iskaa rang halkaa nila, gadha nila, jamuni, gulabi, naarangi, pilaa yaa bhura bhi ho saktaa he. bhartiya khaane main or chikitsa main hazme ke liye istemaal hone vaala kaala namak bhi ek prakaar kaa sendha namak hota he.
 aitihaasik rup se puure uttar bhartiya upmahadweep main yah namak sindh, pashchimi punjab ke sindhu nadi ke saath lage hue hisson or kaibar-pakhtuunakva ke kohat zile se aayaa karata tha jo ab pakistan main hai or jahaan yah zamin main miltaa he. 'sendha namak' or 'saindhav namak' kaa matlab he 'sindh yaa sindhu ke ilaake se aayaa hua'. pashchimottaree punjab main namak koh naam kii mashhoor pahari shrinkhalaa he jahaan se yah namak miltaa he or isi ilaake main prasiddh khevda namak khan he. is namak ko 'lahori namak' bhi kaha jaataa he kyonki yah aksar lahore se hota hua puure uttar bharat main bechaa jaataa tha.
