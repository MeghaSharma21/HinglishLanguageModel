 akshapaad, nyayasutra ke rachayita acharya. prakhyat nyaysutron ke nirmaataa kaa naam padmapurana, skandapurana tathaa vishwanath kii nyaayavritti main maharishi gotam yaa tehraaya gaya he. iske vipareet nyaaybhashya, nyaayavarthik, taatparytika tathaa nyaayamanjari aadi vikhyath nyaayashastreey granthon main akshapaad in sutron ke lekhak maane gaye hai. mahakavi bhaas ke anusaar nyaayashastra ke rachayita kaa naam medhatithi he . in vibhinn maton kii ek vaakyata siddh kii ja sakati he. mahabharat ke anusaar gautam medhatithi do vibhinn vyakti na hokar ek hi vyakti hai . gautam spashtatah vanshabodhak akhya he tathaa medhatithi vyaktibodhak sangya he. akshapaad kaa shabdaarth he - 'pairon main ankhwala'. faltah is naam ko sarthakata siddh karne ke liye anek kahaniyaan garh lee gai hai jo sarvatha kalpit, niraadhaar or pramaan shunya hai.
 naiyayik gautam ke bare main anek vidwaanon ne likhaa he. mahamahopadhyaay pan. harprasad shastri kaa kahana he ki chini bhashaa main nibaddh pracheen bhartiya granthon ke anuwaad ke aadhaar par gautam, buddha ke pehle ho gaye the parantu unke naam par prachlit nyayasutra isa kii duusari shatabdi kii rachana he. satishchandra vidhyabhushan kaa mat he ki gautamiya dharmasutra tathaa nyayasutra kaa karta ek hi gautamanamdhari vyakti rahaa hoga. ye buddha ke samakaleen rahe honge tathaa inkaa samay 6thi shatabdi isa purv ho saktaa he. parantu vidhyabhushan yah bhi maanate hai ki is gautam ne nyayasutra ke keval pehle adhyay kii rachana kii hogi. baad ke chaar adhyay kisi or ne bahut baad main likhe honge. pro. yakobi ke anusaar nyayasutra shoonyavad ke nagarjun dwaara pratishthapit ho jane ke baad or vigyaanavaad ke vikaas ke pehle likhaa gaya hoga kyonki isamen shoonyavad kaa to khandan he par vigyaanavaad kaa khandan nahin miltaa. parantu pro. sherwatski ke anusaar nyayasutra main vigyaanavaad kii ore bhi sanket kiya gaya he. at: yah 500 i. ke baad kii rachana hogi. parantu sherwatski kaa yah mat nyayasutra ko na samajhane ke kaaran bhramamoolak he. tarksangrah ke sampadak aathle tathaa boads ke anusaar gautam ke nyayasutra kanad se pehle ke hai. shabaraswami ne upavarsh se uddhran diya he jisase lagata he ki upavarsh nyaay se parichit the. yadi yah upavarsh mahapadam nand ke mantri hi hai to gautam ko 400 i.pus. kaa maananaa hi padegaa. pro. suali ke anusaar ye sutara 300-350 i. ke kaal ke hai. richard garvey ke anusaar aastik darshanon main nyaay sabase baad kaa he kyonki isvi san ke aarambh ke pehle iskaa kahin koi ullekh nahin miltaa. at: ye sutara 100-300 i. ke bich likhe gaye honge. in maton main koun sa sahi he yah kahana vartmaan sthiti main nitant asambhav he.
 nyaysutron kii rachana tathaa gautam kaa kaal in do prashnon par alag alag vichaar hona chahie. jahaan tak nyaysutron kaa prashn he, nishchay hi ye sutara buddhadarshan kaa vikaas ho jane par likhe gaye hai. itnaa or bhi kaha ja saktaa he ki is nyayasutra main samay-samay par sanshodhan tathaa parivardhan hote rahe hai. paratu gautam kaa naam in sutron se isaliye sambandhit nahin he ki ye saare sutara apane vartmaan rup main gautam dwaara hi virachit hai. gautam ko ham sirf nyaayashastra kaa pravartak kah sakate hai, sutron kaa rachayita nahin. ho saktaa he, gautam ne kuch sutara likhe hon, par ve sutara anya sutron main itane ghulmil gaye hai ki unako alag nikaalnaa hamaare liye asambhav he. in drushtiyon se hamen vidhyabhushan kaa mat adhik maanya lagata he.
 gautam ko akshapaad bhi kehete hai. vidhyabhushan gautam ko akshapaad se pruthak maanate hai. nyayasutra ke bhashyakaar tathaa anya vyaakhyaataaon ne akshapaad or gautam ko ek mana he. 'akshapaad' shabd kaa arth hota he 'jiske pair main ankhen hon'. vyakaran mahabhashya gautam ke siddhanton se parichit he.
 nyaysutron main paanch adhyay hai or ye hi nyaay darshan ke mul aadhaar granth he. inaki samiksha se pataa chalata he ki nyaayadarshan aarambh main aadhyatm pradhan tha arthath aatma ke swaroop kaa yatharth nirnay karnaa hi iskaa uddeshy tha. tark tathaa yukti kaa yah sahaara avashya letaa tha, parantu aatma ke swaroop kaa parichay in sadhano ke dwaara karana hi iskaa mukhya taatpary tha. us yug kaa siddhant tha ki jo prakriya aatmatatv kaa gyaan praapt kara sakati he vahi thik tathaa maanya he. usase vitarip maanya nahin hoti:
 parantu aage chalakar nyaay darshan main us tark pranali kii visheshtah udbhaavna kii gai jiske dwaara anatma se aatma kaa pruthak rup bhalee-bhanti samajha ja saktaa he or jisamen vaad, jalp, vitanda, chal, jati aadi sadhano kaa prayog hota he. in tarkpradhan nyaysutron ke rachayita akshapaad prathit hote hai. vartmaan nyaysutron main donon yugon ke chintanon kii uplabdhi kaa spasht nirdesh he. nyaayadarshan ke mul rachayita gautam medhatithi hai or uske prati sanskrit-naveen vishyon kaa samavesh kar mul granth ke sanshodhak-akshapaad hai. ayurveda kaa prakhyat granth charakasamhita bhi isi sanskaar paddati kaa parinat adarsh he. mul granth ke praneta maharishi agnivesh hai, parantu iske pratisanskarta charak maane jate hai. nyayasutra bhi isi prakaar akshapaad dwaara pratisanskrit granth he.
 gautam nyaayashastra ke pravartak hai. pramaanon ke aadhaar par arth kii pariksha karnaa 'nyaay' kehalaata he, at: yah mukhyat: pramanshastra he. prameya kaa bhi is darshan main vichaar kiya gaya he par vah gaun ho gaya he. gyaan kya he, kaise utpann hota he, uski utpatti ke kitne strot hai, un stroton ke dosha koun koun se hai, inkaa vivechan nyaay kaa vishay he. bhartiya parampara main 'nyaay' shabd angreji logic yaa tarkshastra kaa paryaayavachi he. baudhon tathaa jainon ne bhi apni tarkpaddati chalaai he or unhen bhi buddhanyay tathaa jainanyaay kaha jaataa he. par jahaan keval nyaay shabd kaa prayog hua he vahaan 'nyaay' se sampradaay dwaara pratipaadit siddhanton kaa hi grahan hota he.
 is sampradaay kaa mul granth nyayasutra he jisamen paanch adhyay hai tathaa prayek adhyay do ahnikon main vibhajit he. saare sutron kii sankhya 530 he. pratham adhyay main saamanyat: un 16 vishyon kaa varnan kiya gaya he jinka vistrut pratipaadan baad ke chaar adhyyon main hua he. duusare adhyay main sanshay tathaa pramaanon kaa vivechan he. tisare adhyay ke pratipaadya vishay hai aatma, sharir, indriya, indriyon ke vishay, gyaan tathaa man. chaturth adhyay ichha, dukh, klesh or moksh ke swaroop kaa vivechan karte hue bhram ke swaroop tathaa avyav evam avyavi ke sambandh par bhi prakaash daalata he. paanchaven adhyay main jati or nigrahasthaan kaa vivechan kiya gaya he.
 pramaan, prameya, sanshay, prayojan, drushtaant, siddhant, avyav, tark, nirnay, vaad, jalp, vitanda, hetvabhaas, chal, jati, or nigrahasthaan in 16 vishyon ke tatvagyan se ni:shreyas kii prapti nyaayashastra main maani gai he. pratyaksh, anumaan, upmaan or shabd in chaar pramaanon se gyaan utpann hota he. vaisheshik darshan main sweekrut saat padaarthon kaa prameya main atanrbhaav ho jaataa he. isiliye parvarti naiyayikon ne nyaay ko vaisheshik ke saath sambhddha kar diya he.
 nyaay main mukhyat: vadavivaad kii paddati kaa varnan he. kaise kisi siddhant kaa upasthaapan kiya jaataa he, siddhant ke prati kitne akshep ho sakate hai, unka parihaar kis tarah kiya ja saktaa he, ye hi nyaay ke mukhya pratipaadya hai, kaha jaataa he, ki gautam ne hi sarvapratham anumaan ke paanch avyavonvale vaakya kaa prachlan kiya. greek darshanik arestu ke anusaar anumaan teen hi vaakyon main sampann hota he. vidhyabhushan ke anusaar bhartiya nyaay main avyavaatmak vaakya kii kalpana arestu ke prabhaav se utpann hui he. parantu professor kith kaa mat he ki nyaay kii praarambhik avasthaa main greek vichaardhaara kaa prabhaav maanane kaa koi aadhaar nahin he. gautam kii panchavyav vaakya kii kalpana unke mastishk kii hi upaj he.
 bhartiya darshan prasthaanon main nyaay sambhavat: sabase adhik prabhaavshaalee prasthaan rahaa he. nyaysutron main pratipaadit siddhanton kaa buddha acharya nagarjun ne khandan kiya. nagarjun kaa uttar dene ke liye vatsyayan ne nyaysutron par bhashya kii rachana kii. vatsyayan ke upar buddha naiyayik dinnag dwaara kiye gaye akshepon kaa parihaar karne ke liye udyotkar ne nyaayavarthik likhaa. nyaayavarthik par nyaayavarthik taatparytika tathaa usapar tikaparishuddhi kii rachana kramash: vachaspati mishra or udayan ne kii. in granthon ke atirikt nyaayamanjari nyaysutron kii ek swatantra vyakhya he. navyanyay ke pravartak gangeshopadhyaay ne tathaa unke anuyaayiyon ne bhi gautam dwaara pradarshit maarg kaa anusaran karte hue anek granth likhe. nyaayadarshan ne anek bhartiya darshanon ko prabhavit tathaa tarkpaddati kii udbhaavna dekar prerit kiya he. nyaay hi ek aisa sampradaay he jisapar aaj bhi panditon main vishad rup se charchaa chal rahi he.
 gautam ishwarwadi the yaa nahin, yah kahana kathin he kyonki unke sutron main ishwar kaa spasht nirdesh kahin nahin he. baad main ishwar kaa pratipaadan nyaay kii ek visheshta hi ho gai. mokshavastha main nyaay ke anusaar aatma apane saare gunon se rahit hokar apane shuddh dravya rup main avasthit rahati he. buddhi, sukh, du:kha, ichha, dwesh, praytn or sanskaron se pare aatma jab du:khabhaav rup anand kii avasthaa main avasthit ho jaati he, use mukt kehete hai.
