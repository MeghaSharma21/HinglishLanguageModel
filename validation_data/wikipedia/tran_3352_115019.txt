 tao te ching yaa daao de jing prasiddh chini darshanik lao tsu dwaara rachit ek dharm granth he jo tao dharm kaa mukhya granth bhi mana jaataa he. iskaa naam iske do vibhaagon ke pehle shabd ko lekar banaaya gaya he - 'daao' or 'de' - jinke ant main 'jing' lagaaya jaataa he. paaramparik manyata ke anusaar lao tsu china ke jhou rajvansh kaal main sarkari abhilekhi the or unhone is granth ko chhathi sadi isapurv main likhaa tha, haalanki isaki rachana kii asaliyat par vivaad jari he. isaki sabase pracheen pandulipiyaan chauthi shatabdi isapurv se mili hai.
 tao te ching granth kaa sabase pehla vaakya he 'jis maarg ke bare main baat kii ja sake vah sanathan maarg nahin he'. puure granth main baar-baar is 'maarg' shabd kaa prayog hota he or samikshakon main isko lekar aapasi bahes hazaron saalon se chalati ai he. is prashn ke uttar main ki yah kis maarg kii baat kar rahaa he - dharmik, darshanik, naitik yaa rajnatik - sameekshak alen chaiyn ne kaha he ki 'aisi shreniyan taowadi nazariye main ek hi hai or inkaa khandikaran keval pashchimi vichaardhaaraon main hi hota he'. tao-dharmiyon ke anusaar tao main jis maarg kii baaten hoti hai vah satya, dharm or puure brahmand ke astitv kaa strot he.
 tao te ching main aksar kuch paane ke liye us se vipareet karne kii shiksha di jaati he, jinamein 'maarg' or 'ek' shabd mul satya kii taraf ishaara karte hai -
 tao te ching main is baat kaa bhi kaafii byora he ki sachai ke ant main pakhand badhata he -
 netaaon or raajaaon ko shiksha di jaati he ki sab se uttam netritv vah hota he jo logon ko prathit hi na ho:
