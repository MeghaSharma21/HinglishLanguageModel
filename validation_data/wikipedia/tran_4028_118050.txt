 ekanath rande rashtriya swayamsevak sangh ke ek samarpit kaaryakarta the jinhone san 1926 se hi sangh ke vibhinn dayitvon kaa nirvaah kiya. kanyakumari sthit vivekananda smarak shila ke nirmaan ke liye aandolan karne evam safaltaapurvak is smarak kaa nirmaan karne ke kaaran prasiddh hai.
 ekanath rande kaa janm 19 navambar 1914 ko amravati jile ke titila gaanv main hua tha. gaanv chota tha or vahaan shaikshanik suvidhaayen nahin thim. is kaaran ekanath apane bade bhai ke ghar nagpur padhai karne ke uddeshy se aae. unke ghar ke paas sangh kii shakha lagaati thi. aas-paas ke ladke sahaj jigyasaavash shakha par hone vaale kriyakalapon ko dekhne ke liye vahaan ekatrit hote the. unamem ekanath bhi the. sheeghra hi sangh kii bal shakhaaen prarambh hui jinamein ekanath kaa pravesh hua. dow sahab kaa dhyaan unki ore ek honhaar swayamsevak ke naate gaya. yatha samay ekanath ne madhyamik kii pariksha uteern kii or sangh kaary ke liye prachaarak banane kii ichha vyakt kii. parantu dow sahab ne unhen pehle apni padhai puri karne kii salaah di. mahavidhyalay main ve ek mehnatee, nidar tathaa gunsampann vidhyaarthee ke rup main ubhare the. padhai ke saath-saath vah sangh kaary bhi karte rahe. 1936 main shiksha puri karne ke baad ve prachaarak ke rup main madhya pradesh main gaye. mahakaushal tathaa chhattisgarh main unhone sangh kaary kaa vistaar kiya. madhya pradesh pravaas ke samay unhone dow harisingh gaur vishvavidhyalay se tatvagyan vishay main snatakottar bhi kiya.
 ekanath rande kaa naam sunate hi kanyakumari ke teen sagaron ke sangam par sthit "swami vivekananda shila smarak' kii yaad ho aati he or is bhavy smarak ke shilpkaar kaa chitra ankhon ke saamne saakaar ho jaataa he. sarasanghachaalak guruji ne shila smarak kaa kaam shri ekanath rande ke kandhon par daalaa tha. apane netaa dwaara saunpe hue kaam ko apane praanon kii baaji lagaakar kaise puraa kiya jaae, iskaa adarsh shri ekanath ne prasthaapit kiya. sangh ke anushaasan or vicharadhara main taiyaar hone vaala tathaa sabhi kasautiyon par khara utarane vaala vyaktitv yani, shri ekanath rande.
 ve jab madhya pradesh main sangh kaary kaa daayitv yashasvi rup se sambhaal rahe the unheen dinon mahatma gandhi kii hatya hui. raajnneetik badale kii bhaavana se prerit hokar pan nehru kii tatkaaleen congressi sarkaar ne sangh par pabandi lagaai. sangh ne iske virodh main jo satyagrah kiya uskaa netritv bhi shri ekanath ne bhumigat rahakar kiya. sangh par pratibandh samaapt hone ke baad sangh kaary kii bigadi hui avasthaa ko phir se thik maarg par laane ke kaam main shri ekanath ne ek nishthavan kaaryakarta kii tarah pus. guruji kii sahaayta kii. tatkaaleen purvi pakistan se aane vaale nirvasit hindu bandhuon kaa punarwasan karne ke liye sangh ne kalakatta main "vaastuhara sahaayta samiti' kii sthapana kii. is prakalp kaa sab kaam shri ekanath rande ne hi kiya. 1950 se 1952 ke kalkhand main shri ekanath ko dilli tathaa punjab praanton kaa prachaarak niyukt kiya gaya. unhone prayek shakha par jaakar vahaan ke bikhare hue kaam ko sangathit kiya. usee samay un par sangh ke sarkarywah kii jimmedaari saunpi gai. unhone puure desh kaa daura kar sangh kaary kaa vistaar kiya.
 unke sangh ke sarkarywah ke naate kaam karte samay vivekananda janmashatabdi mahotsav kaa avasar aayaa. pus. guruji ramakrishna mishan ke dikshaprapt sanyasi the. unhone sangh kii ore se swami vivekananda shatabdi kaa kaarykram yojit kiya. unheen dinon kerala main prasiddh samajasevi va nair samudaay ke netaa shri mannath padmanabhan ke netritv main kanyakumari sthit "vivekananda rock memorial samiti' kii sthapana kii gai. in chattaanon par swami vivekananda ne teen dinon tak dhyansth hokar sadhana kii thi or vahiin par unka satya se saakshaatkaar hua tha. sh shri padmanabhan ne is smarak ke kaam ke liye sangh ke sahyog kii maang kii. pus. guruji ne is ke liye ekanath rande ko niyukt kiya. chayan kitna sahi tha. is smarak kaa isaayiyon ne virodh kiya tha. sarkaar bhi virodh main thi parantu shri rande ne in virodhon kaa safaltapuwork saamana kiya. unhone smarak nirmiti kaa anumodan karne vaale 386 loksabhaa sadasyon kaa samarthan praapt kiya. smarak nirmaan ke liye prachand dhanrashi ekatra kii or swami vivekananda rashtriya smarak kaa swapn saakaar hua. in sab baton se ekanath ji ke kartruvy kaa bodh to hota hi he, par usase bhi adhik mahatvapurnsh baat yah he ki ekanath ji kaa is smarak ke pratish drushtikon kitna vishaal tha. aaj vivekananda kendra ek shaikshanik tathaa saanskrithik aandolan ban gaya he. 22 agast 1982 ko ganesh chaturthi ke din unka nidhan hone tak ekanath ji kaa jivan vivekanandamay ho gaya tha. apana sarvasv lagaakar kaam karne kaa kya arth hota he, yah jaanane ke liye ekanath ji kaa jivan adarsh he.
