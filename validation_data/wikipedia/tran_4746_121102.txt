 trans-siberian railmarg ek prasiddh railmarg he jo rus kii rajdhani masco ko rus ke siberia kshetra se guzarate hue sudur-purvi shahar vladivostok se jodta he. isaki shakhaayen mongolia se guzarati hui china bhi jaati hai. yah ek 9,259 kimi lambaa railmarg he or isapar ek chor se dusre chor tak puraa safar karne main aath din lag jate hai. ise rus ke shahi jamaane main san 1891-1916 ke kaal main banaaya gaya tha. siberia kii aabaadi badhaane main or vahaan ke aarthik vikaas main is railmarg kaa bahut bada haath rahaa he. hazaron sailaani bhi iske dwaara har varsh siberia kii saiyr karte hai or isapar yatra karnaa vishwa ke anuthe anubhavon main se ek mana jaataa he.
 roosi bhashaa main "trans-siberian railmarg" ko "transsibiraskaaya magistrala" kaha jaataa he.
