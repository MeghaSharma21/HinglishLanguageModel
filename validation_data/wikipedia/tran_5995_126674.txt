 rajasthan ke rajyapal bharat ke rajasthan rajya kaa mukhiya hota hai,
 bharat kaa samvidhan sanghatmak he. isamen sangh tathaa rajyon ke shashan ke sambandh main praavdhaan kiya gaya he. samvidhan ke bhaag 6 main rajya shashan ke liye praavdhaan he. yah praavdhaan jammu-kashmir ko chhodkar sabhi rajyon ke liye lagu hota he. jammu-kashmir kii vishesh sthiti ke kaaran uske liye alag samvidhan he. sangh kii tarah rajya kii bhi shashan paddati sansadeey he.anuchhed-153 main rajyapal ke pad kaa praavdhaan he. 'rajya kii karypalika kaa pramukh rajyapal'hota he,vidhaan mandal kaa abhinn ang hota he,rajya ke sabhi vishvavidhyalayon ke kuladhipati hota he.isaki niyukti kendriya mantriparishad kii shifarish par raashtrapati dwaara ki jaati he jo ki mantriparishad kii salaah ke anusaar kaary karata he. kuch maamalon main rajyapal ko vivekadhikaar diya gaya he, aise maamle main vah mantriparishad kii salaah ke bina bhi kaary karata he. 7waan samvidhan sanshodhan 1956 dwaara yah praavdhaan bhi he ki ek hi vyakti ko ek se adhik rajyon kaa rajyapal niyukt kiya ja saktaa he lekin usko vetan ek hi pad kaa miltaa he.sarvaadhik kaarykaal vaala rajyapal gurumukh nihaal singh hai,pratham mahila rajyapal pratibha devi singh patil hai. di ja rahi suchi rajasthan ke purnakalik / kaaryvahak rajyapalon kii he-
