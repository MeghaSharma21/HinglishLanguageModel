mahavidhyalay ne shiksha ke kshetra main krantikari badlaav laayaa. bagaha ko yadi paryaton kii drushti se dekha jaay to bahut hi manoram or sundar shadval aadhyatmik sthal yahan maujuud he. mata durga kaa ek rup chandisthan he, jo ratanamala ke raste malpurva pul ke najadik sthit he. vahi mata durga kaa siddh peeth ke rup main prasiddh madanpur sthaan he jo valmiki jangal main sthit he.yah bihar kaa ek maatr rashtriya park he jahaan mata sita ne valmiki ashram main apane jivan ke antim kshanon ko byateet kiya tha.bagaha main pakkibauli ek sthaan he jahaan sawan main shiv bhakton kii apaar bheed lagati he or bol bam ke naare ke saath nange paanv kanvariyon kii dhooma machi rahati he.joda mandir main krishna or radha kii jeevant pratimaa kaa darshan bhi adwitiya he.ratanamala gaanv main jalpa maai kaa sthaan prasiddh he vahaan se aaj tak koi khaali haath nahin lautaa har manokamana purna hoti he yadi koi vahaan halava or puri chadaaye to mata prasann hoti hai. ratanmal kaa chhath ghat bhi bagaha kaa sabase purana chhath mata kaa puja sthal he jo apratim soundarya se paripurna he.bagaha yadi aayen to kalisthaan kii mata kaali kaa darshan karnaa na bhoolen kyonki shiv ke sine par mata kaali ke paanv yukt vikaraal pratimaa he or paas main hi hanuman gadhi he jahaan ram bhakt hanuman kii vishaal pratimaa dekha ja saktaa he. bagaha ke kailash nagar main sthit kailashwa baba se koi anbhigya nahin he jisane mare hue machhliyon ko jinda kar diya tha, ve chamatkaar ke liye ek samaay ke prasiddh or siddh vyakti the. unki pratimaa kaa darshan karne yogya he.bagaha ke chakhni gaanv main sthit angrejon kaa banwaaya hua catholic carch apane vishalata or sundarataa ke liye prasiddh he.iske saath hi bagaha main sthit osho ashram ke anokhe drishy bhi smaraneey he.
