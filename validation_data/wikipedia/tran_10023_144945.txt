 jordan aar belphart  ek america prerak vaktaa or purv share dalal he. unhe share bazaar main gadabadi or ek paisa share byler kaksh chalaane ke liye sambandhit dhokhadhadi aparaadhon kaa doshi paaya gaya tha, jis kaaran unhen 22 maah kii karawas dand mila. queens, neuyork main paidaa hua, jordan belphart 1980 ke dashak main ek maance or samudri bhojan vyapaar ke sanchaalan the. ek kam umr main ek vikreta ke rup main ek prakritik pratibha thi. vittiya sankat ke karan us company ke band ho jane ke baad belphart ne 1987 main sheyaron kii bikri shuru kii. ve 1989 se apane hi nivesh aaparishan, striton okmant chalaa rahe the. company ne apane niveshkon ko chuunaa lagaaya, avaidh rup se lakhon dollars banaaye. pratibhuti vinimay ayog ne 1992 main company ke gumraah tareeke ko rokane ke liye prayaas shuru kiya. 1994 belfot ko pratibhutiyon dhokhadhadi or kaale dhan ko vaid karne ke liye doshi paaya gaya. unhe 2003 me 4 saal ke liye jail ki sajaa sunai, lekin keval 22 maheene me unhe reeha kar diya gaya. belphart ne 2008 main apni pehali sansmaran "di woolf off wal street" prakashit kiya. agle varsh, vah film "di volf off wal street" kaa vimochan kiya.
 belfot ki mata kaa naam 'leh' or pitaji kaa naam 'mex' tha, jo accountint the par baad me unki maan ek vakil ban gayi thi. belfot yahudee he or queens main paley he. unhone america vishvavidhyalay se 'jeev vigyaan' me snatakottar hue. belphart ne dental surgery ke balteemor college main bhaag liya. haalanki jaab skool ke dean ne unse kaha ki "danta chikitsa ke swarna yug khatm ho gaya he. agar aap yahan sirf bahut sara paisa banane ke liye aaye he, to aap galat sthaan par hai, tab unhone vah skool chhod diya. neuyork me jordan ross belfot unki nivesh form, striton okmant ke madhyam se 1990 ke dashak main niveshkon se karodon dollar vanchanaa main unki bhoomika ke liye kukhyat ho gayi. belphart ek alag kshetra main upyog karne ke liye apane bikri kaushalta kaa saharaa liya. unhone ek share dalal kaa kaam shuru kiya or share bazaar ke bare me sari jaankari hasil kar lee. do saal baad, vah apane hi trading company, striton okmant kaa kaary kar rahe the. 
 1990 ke dashak main, vah ek share thag ke rup main ek byler kaksh vipanan paisa share, ke rup main kaary kiya jo brokerej form striton okmant sthapit jo 'qualuds' ke liye ek gambhir lat thi. striton okmant ne 1000 se zyaada share dallalon ko liya jisme 1 arab dollar ke kul share ke muddon ko shaamil kiya gaya tha. juute company steve meden limited ke liye equity jutaney me bhi sahaayta ki thi. firm ki badnaami ne 1990 ke dashak main kanoon pravartan adhikariyon dwaara nishaanaa banaaya gaya jispe 'boiller room' naamak film san 2000 me bani or 2013 me "di woolf off wal street" naamak film bani. albama pratibhuti ayukta 'yusuf borg' ne apane karyaalay main dallali ke bare main shikayaton ke saath baadh kiya gaya tha ke baad striton okmant ke khilaaf mukadmaa chalaane ke liye ek bahu rajya task force kaa gathan kiya. belfot pratibhutiyon dhokhadhadi or kaale dhan ko vaid ke liye 1998 main doshi paaya gaya tha. epabiai ke saath sahyog karne ke baad, vah lagbhag 200 million dollar kii niveshak ghate main hui jisamen ek pump or damp yojana, ke liye sangheey jail main 22 maheene kii seva kii. belfot ko $ 110,400,000 waapas dene kaa aadesh diya gaya tha jo usne share kharidaaron se dhokhe se liy tha. jail me 'tommy chong' naamak vykti ne use apni kahaniyaan likhakar or baad main unhen prakashit karne ko protsaahit kiya. ve rihaai ke baad bhi dost bane rahe.
 sangheey abhiyojan paksh ke anusaar, belfot apane 2003 sajaa samjhaute kii bahaali aavashyaktaa par khara utarane main naakaam rahe he. samjhaute vah dhakosla 1,513 graahakon ko kshatipurti ke prati apni aay kaa 50% bhugtan karne ke liye use aavashyaktaa he. belfot ke peediton se baraamad kiya gaya $ 11,600,000 me kul 10,400,000 $ jabt sampattiyon kii bikri kaa parinaam he. sajaa samjhaute bahaali main $ 110,000,000 kii kul janadesh he. oktober 2013 main sangheey abhiyojkon ne belphart ke khilaaf ek shikaayat darj karai ki 2007 ke baad se unki do pustakon ke prakaashan or film adhikaron kii bikri se $ 1,767,209 or prerak bol se ek atirikt $ 24,000 kii aay kii thi jo belfot pichley 4 saalo me keval $ 243,000 maatr kii kshatipurti kaa bhugtan kiya he. sarkaar vartmaan main khuli vaarta rakhane ke liye uske bhugtan ke difolt main belfot ko pakad nahin rahi he. lekin anivaary kshatipurti kii puri raashi kab chukaaya jayega, abhi yah spasht nahin he.
 america ke pratibhuti or vinimay ayog ne striton okmant ko 1992 me yah kehete hue band karne ko kaha ki, company ne niveshkon ko dhakosla or share kii keemton main herpher kiya tha. do saal baad, belphart ne brokerej kaarobaar ke baahar khud ko paaya. striton okmant belphart or unki company ke liye kanoonee share bazaar main kaam karne se aaajeevan pratibandh bhi shaamil he jo esaisi ke saath ek samjhaute par pahunch tha. pratibhuti vyapaariyon ke rashtriya sanghathan 1996 me striton okmant ko apane sahyog se nikaal diya or company ko agle varsh se kai jurmaana or bastiyon ko adaa karne ke liye nasht hone kaa aadesh diya gaya tha. 1999 main, Belfort pratibhutiyon dhokhadhadi or kaale dhan ko vaid karne ke liye doshi paaya gaya. apni jail kii sajaa ko chota karne ke prayaas main adhikariyon ke saath unhone sahyog kiya.
 
 kathith tor par san 1998 ke baad se belfot, do likhi di volf off wal street or katchhinag di volf wal street, lagbhag 40 deshon main prakashit kiya he or 18 bhashaaon main anuwaad ho chukaa he. apane jivan kii kahani leonardo dicapriyo, jonah hill or margot robi abhinit ek film main badal gaya or martin scorcese dwaara nirdeshit kiya gaya tha. filmaane agast 2012 main shuru hua. film disambar 25,2013 par jari kiya gaya tha. tim yah bataata ki usne film main yah bataaya gaya he ki usne apni asli ki hui vaardaat ko darshaay gaya he. ye sab forbase naamak ek arthichle mai bhi aayaa tha or usne us ke bare main bhi likhaa tha. vah ek lokapriy prerak vaktaa ke rup main antarrashtriya str kaa daura rahaa he.
 belfot akale lakjri nauka nadin ke antim maalik the jo unki duusari patni ke naam tha. jo mul rup se banaaya gaya tha. june 1996 ko yah saaridinia ke purvi tat par dub gaya. italvi chuumsubin frugmin pot par savaar sabhi bachaaya.belphart vah apane captain kii salaah ke khilaaf ucch hawaaon main baahar naukayan par jor dekar kaha, laharon foredepak hech todi jab pot dub jaati he.
 apane saathi ke saath, danni porush, jordan belfot, ek "pump or damp" yojana kaa upyog kar nakadi main uthaaya. uske dalal share kii keemten bad mavahadad kii he, jo unke pehle se na sochaa graahakon par sheyaron ko dhakka de diya, tab company ek mahaan laabh main in companiyon ke sheyaron ko apni pakad se dur bechney gaya. nakadi se ata padaa, belphart ek ucch jivan jite the. ek haveli, khel kaaron or anya mahangi kharid, dil kholkar kharch kiya. unhone yah bhi kaha ki unako cocaine va marijuana jaise maadak pardaath vishesh rup se aadi tha. belphart, uski nashili dawaaon ke prayog karne ke kaaran uske liye kai durghatnaon main shaamil kiya gaya tha. apane khud ke yard main apane helicopter durghatnagrast sahit or uski nauka dub, jo ek baar desiner cocao channel kaa tha prabhaav main he. uski apni lat bhi unki duusari shaadi ke tuutane ke liye yogadan diya.saath hi apane karmchaariyon main laaparvaah vyavhaar ko protsaahit kiya. maadak dravyon ke seven, sex or ghudsavaar striton long island, neuyork, karyaalayon main adarsh the. form main ek sahaayak ek baar company ke vyapaariyon main se kuch uske sir dadhi banane ke liye anumati dene ke liye $ 5000 kaa bhugtan kiya gaya tha. karmchaariyon ko bhi, adarsh vaakya se jeena aagrah kiya gaya "graahak kharidta he yaa mar jaataa he jab tak lataka nahin he.". unki mehnat ko bechney kii ranneeti alpavadhi main band kaa bhugtan kiya. belfort neuyork post ko bataaya ki "yadi aap niyamon kaa paalan nahin karte jab yah jaldi amir paane ke liye aasaan he.".
