 ajay singh yadav haryana rajya, bharat se ek bhartiya rashtriya congress ke rajaneta hai. 2008 tak vah vidyut, van or paryaavaran mantri the or purv main us rajya ke liye sinchai, rajasav or chunaav mantri the.
  ajay singh yadav kaa janm 2 november 1958 ko haryana ke rewadi jile ke saharanvas gaanv main hua tha. unke pita raav abhaya singh the, jo haryana main vidhaan sabha ke sadsy bhi rahe the. unhone jaipur, chandigarh, nai dilli or rewadi sahit vibhinn sthanon par padhai kii or biessi or elelbi kii degree praapt kii. singh june 1980 ko bhartiya sena main dwitiya leftinent bane, or saat saal baad 26 june 1987 ko captain ke rup main istipha de diya. apni sainya seva ke baad, singh ne rewadi jile main raajnneeti armbh kii.
 singh haryana vidhaan sabha main ek sadsy ke rup main lagaataar paanch satr, kul 25 saal he manonith hue. 
 apane kaarykaal main unhone nimnlikhit pad sanbhale:
 vah vidhaan sabha ke sadsy rahe hai. 29 july 2014 ko, singh ne apane pad se istipha de diya or apana rajinama patr bhupinder singh hooda ko saunp diya. haalanki, singh ne party se istipha nahin diya he.
 yadav or uski bahan, nyaayamurti nirmal yadav kii punjab or haryana ucch nyaayalay main the aaropee ko prabhavit karne ke ek bhoomi saude main solan, himachal pradesh dwaara 2008 main daalane par anuchit dabaav panchkula tehseeldaar satish kumaar ke liye jari nivas pramaan patr ke bina uchit dastavejon. aadhikarik poochtacha samarthit daavon ke anauchity, jo yadav se inkaar kiya he. yadav or unki bahan, punjab or haryana ucch nyaayalay kii nyaayamurti nirmal yadav, par 2008 main himachal pradesh ke solan main panchkula tehseeldaar satish kumaar par uchit dastavejon ke bina nivas pramaan patr jari kar ke jameen saude ko prabhavit karne ke aarope lagaae gaye the. aadhikarik poochtacha main aniatikta ke daavon kaa samarthan kiya gaya, jise yadav ne inkaar kiya.
