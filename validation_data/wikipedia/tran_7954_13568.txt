 leshaan ke vishaal buddha tang vansh ke shashan ke dauraan banaai gayi gautam buddha kii 71-meter unchi vishaal pratimaa he. is pratimaa ko laal balua pathar kii chattan emi parvat ko taraash kar banaaya gaya he. yah china ke dakshin main sthit sichuaan praant ke shahar leshaan main minziyang, dadu or kingi nadi ke sangam par sthit he. yah duniya main sabase unchi pathar kii pratimaa he or iske nirmaan ke samay to yah duniya main buddha kii sabase unchi murti thi.
 leshaan kii vishaal buddha pratimaa san 1996 ke baad se unesco vishwa dharohar sthal ke rup main soochibaddh he. san 2008 main sichuaan main aaye bukamp se yah kshatigrast nahin hui he.
