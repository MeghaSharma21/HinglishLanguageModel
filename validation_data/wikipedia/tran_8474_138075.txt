 doris lecing 2007 main sahitya kaa nobel puraskaar praapt karne vaali britani lekhika thim. unhen yah puraskaar apane paanch dashak lambey rachanakaal ke liye diya gaya. mahila, raajnneeti or africa main bitaye yauvankaal unke lekhan ke pramukh vishay rahe. 1901 se prarambh is puraskaar ko praapt karne vaali lecing 11veen mahila rachanaakaar thim. british communist party kii sadsy rah chuki doris ne hungary par roosi akraman se va karypranali va nitiyon se kshubdh hokar 1954 main party hi chhod di thi. apane aarmabhik dinon main unhone dicans, scot, stivension, kipoling, di.ech. lorence, stenthol, tolstoy, dostovsky aadi ko ji bhar padhaa. apane lekhkeey vyaktitv main maan kii sunai parikthaon kii badi bhoomika ko doris rekhaankit karti thim. 17 navambar 2013 ko unka nidhan ho gaya.
 iran main janmin doris ke mata-pita donon british the. pita persia ke imperial bank main clerk va maan ek nurse thim. 1925 main parivaar zimbabwe main sthanantarit ho gaya. jin sapanom ko lekar ve yahan aae the- ve chakanachur ho gaye. lecing ke anusaar unka bachapan sukh va du:kha kii chaaya tha, jisamen sukh kam va pidaa kaa ansh hi adhik rahaa. apane bhai harry ke saath prakritik jagat ke rahasyon ko samjhane-boojhne main lagi lecing ko anushaasan, gharelu saafe-safaai va gharelu tathaa saamaanya ladki banane aadi ke prati maan bahut satark va kathor raheen. 13 varsh kii aayu main lecing kii vidhivat shiksha kaa ant ho gaya. kintu ye anya dakshin africa lekhikaon kii bhanti na rahakar shiksha se vahiin virat nahin ho gayi apitu swayan hi shiksharjan kii disha main badhati raheen. abhi pichley dinon die gaye inke ek vaktavy ke anusaar- "du:khee bachapan 'fiction" kaa janak hota he, mere vichaar se yah baat solah aane sahi he". 19 varsh kii aayu main 1937 main salisbari aane par telephone oparator ke rup main bhi inhone kaary kiya. yaheen inke do vivaah hue. pehla 1939 main frank vizdam se, jisase inhen do bachche hue. kintu yah sambandh chaar varsh hi rahaa or 1943 main talaak ho gaya. doosraa vivaah ek german rajnatik kaaryakarta gotfreed lecing se kiya jisaki parinti bhi 1949 main talaak ke rup main hui. is vivaah se hue ekmaatr bete ko lekar ye britain aa gai thim.
 doris lecing ke saahitiyik jivan kaa prarambh 1949 se mana ja saktaa he jab ve duusare vivaah se hue bete ke saath 1949 main landan pahaunchin. unka pehla upanyaas "da singing grass" isi varsh prakashit hua or yaheen se vidhivat inke lekhkeey jivan kii shuruyaat hui. inkaa sahitya adhikansh apane africa jivananubhavon se judaa he, jisamen bachapan kii smritiyaan, raajnneeti se judaav va samaaj-sanlagnta hi adhikansh he. saanskrithik takaraav, jadon main jamaa anyaay, varnbhed, atmadroh, aatmadwandv kii sthitiyaan inke sahitya main bahutayat se hai. dakshin africa main shwet upniveshwaad ke prati asahmat lekhan ke kaaran 1956 main inhen varjit lekhak tak ghoshit kar diya gaya. upanyaas, kahani, kavita, naatak, ekanki, aadi vidhaon ke atirikt bhi inhone likhaa tha.
 "under maai skin:volum van of maai aatobiographi tu1949" ke liye inhen 'james teit blike priaz' se sammanit kiya gaya. 1995 main harvard vishvavidhyalay ne inhen onreri degree pradaan kii. isi varsh ye 40 varsh baad pun: dakshini africa gayi or beti va naati-poton se milin. 40 varsh purv jis lekhan ke liye inhen pratibandhit va nishkasit kiya gaya tha, usee lekhan ke kaaran is baar inkaa vahaan garmjoshi se swaagat-satkar kiya gaya. 1954 se 2005 tak desh-videsh main apane sahitya va lekhan par mili anek pratishthit sammaanon va puraskaron kii shrunkhlaa main 2007 kaa 'nobel' sahitya puraskaar is varsh inhen mila. unka antim upanyaas - " da cleft" tha jiskaa kathanak samudr ke kinare rahane vaale stri-samudaay ‘cleft’ par kendrit he.
