 kalmapi ek vishesh prakaar kii ghadi he jo bahut sachcha samay bataati he. iskaa samay grinich ke sthaneey samay se milakar rakhaa jaataa he, jisase jahaaj par grinich samay turant janaa ja saktaa he. sextent se surya kii sthiti naapakar jahaaj jis sthaan par he vahaan kaa sthaneey samay gyaat kiya ja saktaa he. sthaneey samay or grinich samay ke antar se deshaantar kii gananaa kii ja sakati he. deshaantaron main ek ansh kaa ant padane par sthaneey samayon main chaar minute kaa antar padataa he. 
 dekhne main kalmapi ek saadhaaran badi ghadi ke samaan hota he. yah ek chakra se do dhuridharon dwaara lataka rahata he. chakra swayan duusare do dhuridharo dwaara lataka rahata he. dhuridharon kii jodiyaan ek duusari se samakon banaati hai. kalmapi is prakaar isliye latakaaya jaataa he ki jahaaj ke hilne dolane par bhi vah sarvada kshaitij rahe. sarvada kshaitij sthiti main rahane ke kalmapi adhik sachcha samay bataata he. kalmapi kii balakamaani saadhaaran ghadi kii tarah sarpil na hokar kuntalakaar hoti he. iskaa malmapi vimochak bhi saadhaaran ghadi se bhinn prakaar kaa hota he. . deshaantar gyaat karne ke liye sachhee ghadi banane kaa pehla prayaas vikhyath vaigyanik chrishchiyan higans ne 1662-70 main kiya tha, par unki banaai ghadiyon main taap ke ghatane badhane tathaa jahaaj ke hine dolane ke kaaran bahut antar pad jaataa tha or samay adhik sachaai se nahin napa ja saktaa tha. 1714 main british sarkaar ne aisa kalmapi banane ke liye, jo pratidin teen second se adhik tej sust na ho, 20,000 pound ke puraskaar kii ghoshnaa kii. yah puraskaar jon harrison ne jeeta jisane 1729-60 main chaar kalmapi banaae, parantu harrison ko kalmapi banane main mulya bahut adhik padataa tha. paris ke pier larua ne 1765 main or england ke jon arnold or tamas arnasha ne 1785 main jo kalmapi banaae ve aadhunik yantron se bahut kuch milte-julate the. aadhunik kalmapi kaa prayog thik se karne par vah bahut hi sachcha samay bataata he. din bhar main ek second se adhik antar nahin padane paata. is sookshm antar ke kaaran maheene bhar chalane ke baad bhi jahaaj kii gananaa kii sthiti or sachhee sthiti main aath mile se kam hi antar padane paata he. pracheen kaal main sachche kalmaapiyon kaa mahatv bahut adhik tha, kyonki inke abhaav main lambii yatra karnaa asambhav hota tha. parantu ab radio sanketon dwaara sachche grinich samay kaa pataa din main kai baar miltaa rahata he or kalmaapiyon kaa bahut sachcha rahanaa pehle jaisaa mahatvapurn nahin rah gaya he.
