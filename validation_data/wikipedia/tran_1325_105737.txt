 shatabdi express railgadiyaan tej chalane vaali sawari gadiyon kii ek shrunkhlaa he jiskaa parichaalan bhartiya rail karti he jo bharat ke bade, mahatvapurn evam vyavsaayik shaharon ko aapas main jodatii he. shatabdi express kaa parichaalan din ke samay hota he evam ye apane moolsthaan evam gantvy kii yatra ek din main hi puri kar leti hai.
 shatabdi express 1988 main bharat ke pehle pradhanmantri shri jawahar laal nehru kii janm shatabdi ke uplakshya me shuru kii gai thi. shatabdi express tatkaaleen rail mantri shri madhavrao sindhiya kii soch kaa nateeja thim. pehali shatabdi express ko nai dilli se jhaansi ke bich shuru kiya gaya tha jise baad me badhaakar bhopal tak kar diya gaya. ab is gadee ko bhopal shatabdi ke naam se janaa jaataa he. shatabdi ko kuch paristhitiyon me anya railgadiyon kii tulana main praathamikta di jaati he or adhikansh samay train stesan ke shreshth platform par aati he. 
 shatabdi express train bharat kii sabase tej chalane vaali railgadee he or isaki ausat gati se lagbhag 130 kimi/ghanta he.nai dilli-bhopal shatabdi kii gati nai dilli or agra steshnon ke bich lagbhag 150 kimi/ghanta he jo bharat main sabase adhik he. shatabdi express kaa ek sanskaran he, jise swarna shatabdi express ke naam se janaa jaataa he. yah gadee bhartiya rail dwaara adhik shandaar maani jaati he. bhartiya rail ne baad me shatabdi express kaa ek bina vaataanukoolan or kam kiraye vaala sanskaran shuru kiya jise jan shatabdi ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
 jab yah bharat main 1988 main shuru kii gai thi us samay is train ko ek bady uplabdhi mana jaataa tha. isko samaaj ke ek varg ke dwaara baari aalochana kaa shikaar bhi hona pada unke mutabik bharat jaise ek garib desh me in vilasita purna gadiyon kii koi aavashyaktaa nahin thi. lekin, samay bitne ke saath, is train kaa str vilasita or gati ke vishwa maanakon ke hisaab se kaaphi neeche tak gir gaya he. 
 shatabdi do steshnon ke bich sabase tej gati se apana safar tay karti he or maarg me iske steshnon kii sankhya bhi bahut kam hoti he. ye puri tarah se vaataanukoolit or bhartiya rail dibbon kii tulana main inkaa str bahut adhik hota he. shatabdi express me yatriyon ko naashtaa/ bhojan, coffee / chaay, falon kaa ras aadi parosa jaataa he saath hi ek litre pani kii bottle bhi di jaati he jo railway ke swaamitv vaali sahaayak railway " rail nir " pradaan karti he.
 bhartiya rail, shatabdi express kii 12 or janshatabdi express kii 16 jodiyon ke saath parichaalan karti he. shatabdi express kii 12 jodiyon me se 8 nai dilli se shuru hoti hai, chennai se do or ek kolkata se or ek mumbai .
