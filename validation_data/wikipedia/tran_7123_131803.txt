 kan bhautikee, bhautikee kii ek shakha he jisamen moolbhoot up parmanvik kano ke parsparik sambandho tathaa unke astitv kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he, jinase padaarth tathaa vikiran nirmit hai. hamaari ab tak ki samaj ke anusaar kan kwantum kshetron ke uttejan hai. duusare kanon ke saath inaki antalakriya kii apni gatiki he.
 kan bhautikee ke kshetra main adhikansh ruchi moolbhoot kshetron main he. moulik kshetron or unki gatishiltaao ke saar ko Standard Model siddhanth ke rup main prastut kiya gaya he. isiliye kan bhautikee main adhiktar standard model ke mul kanon tathaa unke sambhavit vistaar ke bare main adhyan kiya jaataa he.
 aadhunik kan bhautikee khoj upaparmanvik kano par kendrit he jisame nabhik ko banane vaale can jaise electrons, protons ore neutrons radioactive ore scattering prakriya se banane vaale can jaise ki photons, neutrinos, ore muons ore saath hi exotic particles shaamil he. agar sahi rup main kahe to particle parishudhd bhotiki main ek ashuddh naam he kyo ki kan bhautikee kii gatiki quantum mechanics dwaara purnataa paribhaasith hoti he. jaise ki kuch praayogic paristhi main yah tarag-kan dwaiti prakarti dikhte he, tathaa kuch jagah ye tarang jaisaa vyavhaar prastut karte he. ore takneekee bhashaa main kaahe to yah Hilbert space main quantum state dwaara varnit hote he, jo ki quantum field theory main bataaya gaya he. kan bhotik vaigyaniko ke anusaar kan bhotiki ke praathmik kan electrons ore photons he, tathaa ye sabhi janate he ki ye can tarag jaisi prakati bhi prastut karte he.
 abhi tak gyaat sabhi kan tathaa unki persparik kriyaaye kwantum siddhant ke maanak model dawaara paribhaashit kii ja sakati he. maanak model main 17 tarah ke prarambhik can paribhaashit kiye gayi hai, 12 fermian, 4 sadish bosaan tathaa ek adish bosaan can. ye praathmik kan un saikado tarah ke samgr kanon ko sanyojit kar sakate hai jinaki khoj 1960 se ab tak hui he. maanak model ab tak ke lagbhag sabhi prayogic pareekshan main safal rahaa he. halaki vahut se can bhotikwaadi yah maanate he ki yah prakruti kaa ek adhuraa varnan maatr he, tathaa ore adhik sarabhoot siddhanto kii khoj abhi baki he. haal hi main neutrino ke dravyaman kii khoj ne maanak model main pratham praayogic vichlan prastut kiya he.
 can bhotiki vigyaanan ke bahut se sidhanto se prabhvit hota he, kuch can bhotikaudi newnikaran ke sidhant ko mante he tathaa kuch sampoornataa ke sidhaant kaa samathan karte he.
