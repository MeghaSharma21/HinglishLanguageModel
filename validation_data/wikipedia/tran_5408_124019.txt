 rakshastaal tibet main ek jhil he jo maansarovar or kailash parvat ke paas, unase pashchim main sthit he. sutluj nadi rakshastal ke uttari chor se shuru hoti he. pavitra maansarovar or kailash ke itnaa paas hone ke baavjood rakshastaal hinduon or buddha-dharmiyon dwaara pavitra yaa poojaneey nahin maani jaati. ise tibeti bhashaa main lagnagar tso kehete hai. prashasnic rup se yah tibet ke ngaari vibhaag main bharat kii seemaa ke paas sthit he.
 rakshas taal lagbhag 225 varg kilometre kshetra, 84kilometre paridhi tathaa 150 foot gahare main failaa he.is jhil ke tat rakshason ke raja ravan ne yahaan par shivaki aradhana kii thi. isliye ise rakshas talaya ravanahrid bhi kehete hai. ek choti nadi ganga-chudonon jhiilon ko jodatii he.
 rakshastaal ke bare main yah aasthaa he ki ise ravan se sambandhit he, jis karanvash ise ravantal bhi kehete hai. jahaan maansarovar kaa pani meetha he, vahaan rakshastaal kaa khaaraa he. maansarovar main machhliyon or jaleey paudhon kii bharmaar he jabaki rakshastaal ke khaare pani main yah nahin punap pate. sthaneey tibeti log iske pani ko vishaiylla maanate hai. maansarovar gol he or ise suraj kaa or din kii roshni kaa pratik mana jaataa he jabaki rakshastaal ke aakaar kii tulana ardhachandra se kii jaati he or ise raatri kaa or andhere kaa pratik mana jaataa he.
