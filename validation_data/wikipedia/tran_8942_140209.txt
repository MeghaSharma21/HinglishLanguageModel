 antararashtriya raikhik sanghattak ) raikhik kan twarak nirmaan kaa ek antarrashtriya project he. iskaa makasad bhi vigyaan ke ansulajhe sawaalon ko khojna hoga.
 vaishvik str par taiyaari : do darjan se adhik deshon ke 300 vishvavidhyalayon tathaa prayogshaalaon ke 2000 se adhik log is prayog se jude he. teen saal pehle shuru hue lagbhag 32 arab rupye ke is project par abhi tak 12 arab rupye kharch ho chuke hai. ummid he ki iskaa final dizin 2012 tak aa jayega. haalaanki ise kis desh main sthapit kiya jayega, iskaa faisla abhi baaki he. par andaja lagaaya ja rahaa he ki ise bhi switzraland main lagaaya jayega.
