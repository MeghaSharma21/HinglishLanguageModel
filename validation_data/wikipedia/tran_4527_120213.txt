 hindu dharm ke mahakavy mahabharat ke anusaar bhim pandavon main duusare sthaan par the. ve pavandev ke vardaan swaroop kunti se utpann hue the, lekin anya pandavon ke vipareet bhim kii prashansa pandu dwaara kii gai thi. sabhi pandavon main ve sarvaadhik balashaali or shreshth kad-lathi ke the evam yudhisthir ke sabase priy sahodar the.
 unke puranic bal kaa gungan puure kavy main kiya gaya he. jaise:- "sabhi gadadhariyon main bhim ke samaan koi nahin he or aisa bhi koi jo gaj kii sawari karne main itnaa yogya ho or bal main to ve das hazaar haathiyon ke samaan he. yuddh kala main paarangat or sakriy, jinhen yadi krodh dilaayaa jaae jo kai dhritarashtron ko ve samaapt kar sakate hai. sadaiv roshrat or balavaan, yuddh main to swayan indra bhi unhen parrast nahin kar sakate." vanvaas kaal main inhone anek rakshason kaa vadh kiya jisamen bakasur evam hidimb aadi pramukh hai evam agyatavaas main viraat naresh ke saale kichak kaa vadh karke draupadi kii raksha kii. yah gada yuvadh main bahut hi praveen the evam dronacharya or balram ke shithy the. yudhisthir ke rajasuya yajna main raajaaon kii kami hone par unhone magadh ke shaasha jaraasandh ko parrast karke 86 raajaaon ko mukt karaayaa. draupadi ke chiraharan kaa badla lene ke liye unhone duhshasan kii chaati faad kar uskaa rakt paan kiya. bhim kamyak van naamak rakshasi van kaa raja tha. maha daitya van kaa purv raja hidimbsur rakshas tha.
 mahabharat ke yuddh main bhim ne hi saare kaurav bhaaiyon kaa vadh kiya tha. inhi ke dwaara duryodhana ke vadh ke saath hi mahabharat ke yuddh kaa ant ho gaya.
