 ganit main avyuuh ek adish raashiyon se nirmit ytaakar rachana he. yah ytaakar rachana laghu koshthak "", dohare dand "|| ||" athwa deergh koshthak "" ke andar band hoti he. isamen sankhyaon kaa ek vishesh prakaar kaa vinyas kiya jaataa he, at: ise avyuuh, yaa matrix, kii sangya di gai he. matrix ke avyav sankhyaayein hoti hai kintu ye aisi koi bhi amoort vastu ho sakati he jinka guna kiya ja sake evam jinhen joda ja sake.
 sarvapratham sylvester ne avyuuh kii yah paribhaasha di thi ki sankhyaon ke kisi ytaakar sarani ko, jisamen se saaranik ban saken, avyuuh kehete hai. aadhunik samay main avyuuh ko ek atisanmishra sankhya ke rup main maanate hai. is drushtikon ke pravartak hai milton or keli .
 kisi bhi aavyuuhon ko likhte samay use koshthak main likhaa jaataa he.
 mukhyatah avyuuh ko "A" se darshaya jaataa he. vahiin a11 yaa a1,1 se pankti or stambhon ke maan ko dikhaaya jaataa he. upar diye gaye udhaaharan main a11 pehle pankti main or pehle stambh main he. is kaaran ise 1,1 likh kar darshaya gaya he. vahiin isi prakaar se a12 yaa a1,2 pehle pankti main he or duusare stambh main he. duusare stambh main hone ke kaaran ant main 2 likhaa gaya he. pankti ke sabhi maanon ko m or stambh ke sabhi maanon ko n se darshaya jaataa he. 
 avyuuh kaa bahut lambaa itihaas he, lekin rekhiy gananaa hetu iskaa upyog varsh 1800 ke baad hi shuru ho paaya. chini paathya "ganit kala ke nau adhyay" duusari sadi main likhaa gaya iskaa pehla udhaaharan tha. jisamen ise ek prakaar ke vyuh sanrachana ke rup main hal kiya gaya tha. iske baad varsh 1545 main italy ke ganitagya girolamo cardno ne is vidhi ko china se le kar europe main ars megna ke naam se prakashit kiya. japan ke ganitagya seki bhi is tarah ke vidhi se varsh 1683 main kai ganitiya sameekaran hal kiye. iske baad dach ganitagya jine de vitt ne iskaa ek parivartit rup 1659 main ek pustak main prakashit kiya.
