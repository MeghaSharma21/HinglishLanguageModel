 bahut dinon tak manushya ne saamaajik ghatnaon kii vyakhya, parloukik shaktiyon, kori kalpanaaon or tark-vaakyon ke shkargat satyon ke aadhaar par kii he. saamaajik anusandhan kaa bijaropan vahiin se hota he jahaan vah apni vyakhya ke sambandh main andeh prakat karnaa prarambh karata he. anusandhan kii jo vidhiyaan prakritik vigyaanon main safal hui he, unheen ke prayog dwaara saamaajik ghatnaon kii samaj utpann karnaa, ghatnaon main kaaranta sthapit karnaa or vaigyanik tatasthta banaae rakhana, saamaajik anusandhan kii mukhya lakshan hai. aisi vyakhya nahin prastut kerani he jo keval anusandhankarta ko santusht kare, balki aisi vyakhya prastut kerani hoti he jo aalochanatmak drushti waalon yaa virodhiyon kaa andeh dur kar sake. iske liye nirikshan kii vyavasthit karnaa, tathy sankalan or tathy-nirvachan ke liye vishisht upkarnon kaa prayog karnaa or prayog main aane vaale pratyayon ko spasht karnaa aavashyak he.
 saamaajik anusandhan ek shrunkhalabaddh prakriya he jiske mukhya charan hai-
  samasya ke kshetra kaa chunaav.
  prachlit siddhanton or gyaan se parichay.
  anusandhanon kii samasya ko paribhaashit karnaa or aavashyaktaanusaar prakalpana kaa nirmaan karnaa.
  aankada sankalan kii upyukyon kaa chunaav, aankadon kaa nirvachan or pradarshan karnaa.
  samanyikaran or nirshksh nikaalnaa.
 anusandhanprakriya kii purvayojna shodh praaroop main taiyaar kar lee jaati he.
 anusandhan kii samasya ke anusaar aankada sankalan kii vidhiyon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he.
 nirikshan ke antargat vah sara gyaan aata he jo indriyon ke madhyam se praapt hota he. prashikshit nireekshak, purvagrahon se mukt hokar, tatasth drashtaa hota he. vah sahabhaagi or asahbhaagi donon hi prakaar ke nirikshan kar saktaa he. niyantrit paristhiti main nirikshan karnaa pareekshan hota he. parantu niyantran kii shart bhautikee ke pareekshan ke samaan kathor nahin hoti. prakritik ghatnaayen, jaise baadh, sookhaa, bukamp, rajkeey kanoon aadi bhi prayogtmak parivarty ke samaan saamaajik ghatnaon ko prabhavit karte hai.
 vyakti ke vichaaron, iraadon, vishwaason, iccha, aadarshon, yojanaon or ateet ke prabhaavon ko jaanane ke liye prashnawali or saakshaatkaar vidhiyon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. prashnawali vidhi main uttardaata ke samaksh anusandhankarta upasthit nahin hota. saakshaatkaar main vah uttardaata ke samaksh rahata he or niyantrit yaa aniyantrit reeti se, uttaron dwaara, aankade praapt karata he. vyakti ke prathithiksh paksh kaa anveshan karne ke liye abhivritti pramaapan pratyakshepan vidhi or samajamiti kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. vyaktivishay adhyayanpranali aankada sankalan kii vah vidhi he jiske dwaara kisi bhi ikai kaa gahan anveshan kiya jaataa he. saamaajik anusandhan main pratinidhi ikaayiyon kii prapti ke liye nidarshan kii vidhiyaan, jo rendom vidhi kaa hi vibhinn rup he, lagaai jaati hai.
 maanav vyavhaaron ke gunatmak paksh ke pramaapan ke prati ab aashajanak drushtikon apanaaya jaataa he.
 gunatmak paksh ko naapane kii mukhya ritiyon, vyavasthit shrunkhlaa sambandh pramaapan or sanketakon ke aadhaar par vargeekaran karne se sambhav hota he. boguards kaa saamaajik duuri naapane main saat banduon kaa paimaana, apni kuch trutiyon ke baavjood, mahatvapurn paimaana he. moreno or jenings ne samajamiti dwaara kisi samooh main paae janevaale saamaajik ant:sambandhon kii sajakari ko naapane kii vidhi bataai he. chapin ne saamaajik str naapane kaa paimaana prastut kiya he. abhivrittiyon ko naapane ke anek paimaanon main tharston tathaa libert ke paimaane prasiddh hai.
 'maanav vyavhaar ganit ke sutron main nahin bandha ja saktaa' is mat ke anusaar, prakritik vigyaanon ke vikaas main itnaa mahatvapurn yogadan denevaala ganit, saamaajik anusandhan main aavashyak bhoomika nahin rakhata. ganit ke paksh main mat rakhanevaalon kaa daawa he ki koi bhi gunatmak tathy aisa nahin he jiskaa maatraatmak adhyayan sambhav na ho. prayek vyakti ke liye samaan rup se vishwasaneey maap kaa ganit ke padon main vyakt karnaa aavashyak he. vastav main ganit bhashaa ke samaan he jiske pratikon dwaara tarkwakyon kaa nirmaan ho saktaa he. samajashastreey siddhant ke vikaas main ganit praroopon kaa prayog badhata ja rahaa he.
 saamaajik anusandhanon main, saamagree ke sangrahan main spashtikaran ke liye, sankhyakeey vidhiyon kaa prayog pratinidhitiv yaa madhyam vrittiyon ko prakat karne ke liye kiya jaataa he. madhyamik, madhya bahulank, sahasambandh pramaan, maapak vichlan, antarang pareechaa aadi vidhiyon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. saamagree kaa sanketan or vargeekaran karke saarinian dwaara pradarshit kiya jaataa he. saranian ke aankadon ko spasht karne ke liye tathaa parivartyon kaa sahasambandh sthapit karne ke liye, vibhinn sheershakon, stambhon evam rekhachitron kaa prayog kiya jaataa he.
 anusandhan kaa vargeekaran, uski prerana or uddeshy ke aadhaar par, kiya ja saktaa he. upyogita or nitinirmaan se rahit, vaigyanik tatasthta ke saath, kisi prakkalpana kaa samarthan karnaa buniyadi anusandhan he parantu uskaa vyaavhaarik upyog do tarah se kiya jaataa he-
 prashasnic samasyaon ke sambandh main honevaala anusandhan he. isamen ganit or sankhyakeey vidhiyon kaa prayog sambhaavnasiddhant, ke aadhaar par kiya jaataa he. aankadon kaa chayan, vishleshan, aamurthikaran, bhavishyavaanee, siddhant, nirmaan aadi is anusandhan kii prakriya hota hai.
 kisi samudaay kii visheshtaon ko dhyaan main rakhkar, niojit prayaas, jo saamudayik jivan ke anek pahaluon ko prabhavit karte hai or saamaajik prayojanon kii purti ke liye kiye jate hai, is anusandhan ke antargat aate hai, jaise awaas, kheti, safai, manoranjan se sambandhit kaarykram. samudaay ke sadasyon kaa sahyog, aarthik sthiti, sangathit virodh aadi visheshtaon kaa moolyankan karke kaarykram ko safal banane kaa praytn kiya jaataa he. yah anusandhan bharat main chalanevaale niyojan kaa ek mukhya upkaran he.
 saamaajik anusandhan kii paddati kaa vikaas vibhinn paraspar virdohi dhaaraon main hua he. mukhya dhara rahi he un siddhanton kii jo saamaajik vigyaan yaa saanskrithik vigyaan ko prakritik vigyaan se bhinn maanate hai. prakritik ghatnaon main sambandh yantric or bahm hote hai, jab ki saamaajik ghatnaon main sambandh 'mulya' or 'uddeshy' par aadhaarit hote hai. 'vigyaan paddati kii ekta' ke samarthak 'prakritik tathy' or 'saamaajik tathy' main samanta maanate hai. prakruti or samaaj par lagu honevaale niyam bhi samaan hote hai. inke anusaar, manushya ke prathithik path kaa adhyayan keval bahya vyavhaaron ke aadhaar par hi kiya ja saktaa he. kaaranta kii khoj main dharmik rahasyavaad kaa put paaya jaataa he. ye keval 'kriyaaon' ko hi mahatv dete hai. prakaarywaadee paddati vikasaavyad ke vipareet he. samaaj ke avyavon main kram or antasambandh paaya jaataa he. shaareerik sanghathan ke saadrishy par saamaajik tathy, sanstha, samooh, mulya aadi kii kriya se utpann samskruti kaa anveshan kiya jaataa he. aitihaasik samuchya main ghatnaon ko samajhane ke vipareet, vyaktivaadee paddati he he jo tatkaal ko hi shrey detii he, kyonki tatkaal main samucch ke ansh vidyaman hote hi hai. is pravriti ko lekar sankethik adhyayan hone lage hai. inke atirikt parichaalan or kriyaatmak anusandhanon kii paddatiyaan prachlit hai.
