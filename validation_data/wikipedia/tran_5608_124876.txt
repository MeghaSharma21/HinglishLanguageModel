 aryaashtangmarg mahatma buddha kii pramukh shikshaaon main se ek he jo dukhon se mukti paane evam aatm-gyaan ke saadhan ke rup main bataaya gaya he. ashtaang maarg ke sabhi 'maarg', 'samyak' shabd se armbh hote hai . buddha pratikon main praayah ashtaang margon ko dharmachakr ke aath tadiyon dwaara nirupit kiya jaataa he.
 kii shrenee kaa hissaa
 buddha dharm ke anusaar, chauthe arya satya kaa arya ashtaang maarg he - dukh nirodh paane kaa rasta. gautam buddha kehete the ki chaar arya satya kii satyata kaa nishchay karne ke liye is maarg kaa anusaran karnaa chahie :
 kuch log arya ashtaang maarg ko path kii tarah samajhate he, jisamen aage badhane ke liye, pichley ke str ko paanaa aavashyak he. or logon ko lagata he ki is maarg ke str sab saath-saath paae jate he. maarg ko teen hisson main vargeekrut kiya jaataa he : pragna, sheel or samadhi.
 bhagavan buddha ne bataaya ki trishna hi sabhi du:khon kaa mul kaaran he. trishna ke kaaran sansar kii vibhinn vastuon kii ore manushya pravrutt hota he; or jab vah unhen praapt nahin kar saktaa athwa jab ve praapt hokar bhi nasht ho jaati hai tab use du:kha hota he. trishna ke saath mrityu praapt karanevaala praani uski prerana se phir bhi janm grahan karata he or sansar ke du:khachakr main pisataa rahata he. at: trishna kaa sarvatha prahaan karne kaa jo maarg he vahi mukti kaa maarg he. ise du:kha-nirodh-gamini pratipada kehete hai. bhagavan buddha ne is maarg ke aath ang bataae hai : 
 is maarg ke pratham do ang pragna ke or antim do samadhi ke hai. bich ke chaar sheel ke hai. is tarah sheel, samadhi or pragna inhen teen main aathon angon kaa sannivesh ho jaataa he. sheel shuddh hone par hi aadhyatmik jivan main koi pravesh paa saktaa he. shuddh sheel ke aadhaar par mumukshu dhyanabhyas kar samadhi kaa laabh karata he or samadhisth avasthaa main hi use satya kaa saakshaatkaar hota he. ise pragna kehete hai, jiske udbuddha hote hi sadhak ko satta maatr ke anitya, anaam or du:khaswaroop kaa saakshaatkaar ho jaataa he. pragna ke alok main iskaa agyanandhkaar nasht ho jaataa he. isse sansar kii sari trishnaayen chali jaati hai. vitatrishna ho vah kahin bhi ahankaar mamkaar nahin karata or sukh du:kha ke bandhan se upar uth jaataa he. is jivan ke ananthar, trishna ke na hone ke kaaran, uske phir janm grahan karne kaa koi hetu nahin rahata. is prakaar, sheel-samadhi-pragnawala maarg aath angon main vibhakt ho arya aashtangik maarg kaha jaataa he.
