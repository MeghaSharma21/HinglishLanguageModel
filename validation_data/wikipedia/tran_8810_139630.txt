 ilkhaani samrajya yaa ilkhaani silsilaa ek mongol khaanat thi jo 13veen sadi main iran or azarbejaan main shuru hui thi or jise itihaaskaar mongol samrajya kaa hissaa maanate hai. isaki sthapana changez khan ke pote halaku khan ne kii thi or iske charam par isamen iran, iraq, afghanistan, turkmenistan, arminia, azarbejaan, turki, jorgia or pashchimi pakistan shaamil the. ilkhaani bahut se dharmon ke prati sahanubhuti rakhte the lekin inamen buddha dharm or isai dharm ko vishesh sweekruti hasil thi. ilkhaani silsile main mahmud gazaan ne 1295 main islam apanaaya or uske baad ke shaskaon ne shiya islam ko badhaava diya. ilkhaani samrajya san 1256 se 1335 tak chalaa.
 mongol bhashaa main 'il-khan' kaa matlab tha 'sarvocch khan ke adheen vaala khan' or is samrajya ke shuru main ise mahaan mongol samrajya ke adheen ek hissaa mana jaataa tha. 'ilkhaan' main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
