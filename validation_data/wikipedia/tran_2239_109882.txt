 china kii vibhinn sansthaaon main lagbhag 80 videshi bhashaayen padhai ja rahi hai. inamen haal ke kuch varshon main hindi yahan kii ek lokapriy bhashaa banakar ubhari he.
 praarambhik rup se china main peking vishvavidhyalay, bejing videshi adhyayan vishvavidhyalay or china ke vibhinn collejon main padhai jaati he. shikshakon ke anusaar hindi kii lokapriyata kaa ek mukhya kaaran is bhashaa se ek vyavsaay ke avasar kaa judana he. bhartiya duutavas ke prayaason ke kaaran china main paanch sansthaaon bhartiya samskruti ke vibhin adhyayan kshetron ke vibhaag bane hai jinamein vibhinn kshetron main jinaan vishvavidhyalay, shenesen vishvavidhyalay or unnan vishvavidhyalay shaamil hai.
 china kii kuch anya sansthaaon kii tarah peking vishvavidhyalay main bhi bhartiya samskruti ke vibhinn vibhaagon kii sthapana 2003 main bharat ke tatkaaleen pradhan mantri atal bihari vaajapeyi ke haathon hui thi. yahan mukhya rup se kendra ke bindu hindi shiksha ke saath-saath sanskrit, urdu or bengali bhashaa ke adhyayan tathaa bharat ke sambandh main dharm, itihaas or samskruti se jude course kaa padhaanaa bhi shaamil he. 2006 tak yahan 50 purnaklik ke chaatr or chah pi ech di chaatr panjikrut ho chuke the. 
 china main hindi shiksha praapt karne vaale chaatr apni padhai ke dauraan aitihaasik bhartiya granth jaise ki ramayana kaa bhi adhyayan karte hai.
 hindi bhashaa kaa adhyayan karne vaale chaatr apni shiksha ko purna karne ke liye kuch samay bharat main bitaate hai. iske atirikt prati varsh kai chaatr mahatma gandhi anterrashtriya hindi vishvavidhyalay, bharat main pravesh pate hai. yah log diploma se lekar pi-ech di0 tak ke course yahan par puure karte hai. iske atirikt, yah log saadhaaran rup se apni pahchaan bhi bhartiya naamon se karavaate hai.
 china se prasaarit hone vaale radio china internashnal ke kaarykram bharat main kaafii lokapriy ho chuke hai. kuch jagahon par bhartiya shrotaaon ke club bhi ban chuke hai. is kaaran se yah radio seva kai chini logon ko rojgaar kaa ek nayaa avasar pradaan karti he. 
 akashwani ne 15 agast 2015 se china kii janata main hindi bhashaa seekhney kii ruchi ko dekhte hue har ravivaar ko bees minute kaa ek kaarykram shuru kiya he jiskaa naam Xue Xi Yindiyu Jie Mu yaa "aao hindi seekhen" he. iske antargat ek chini parivaar jawaharlal nehru vishvavidhyalay ke ek shikshak ke samaksh hindi bhashaa sikhte hue sunai detaa he.
 china ke vishvavidhyalayon or shiksha kendron main hindi diwas dhooma-dham se manaaya jaataa he. is avasar par pratiyogitaayen bhi yojit hoti he. in samarohon kaa ek mukhya kendra bhartiya duutavas bhi he.
 hindi sikhkar bharat vyapaar, vyavsaay yaa keval yatra par aane vaale chini chaatr aashcharya main pad jate hai ki aadhikarik maamalon main bharat main hindi kii tulana main angrezi kaa prayog adhik he. iske vipareet china main lagbhag sabhi kaary chini bhashaa main hi sampann hote hai.
