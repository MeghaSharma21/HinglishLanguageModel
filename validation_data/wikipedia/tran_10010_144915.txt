 johny meraa naam 1970 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. vijay anand dwaara nirdeshit is film main devanand or praan ne do bhaaiyon kaa kirdaar nibhaayaa he jo bachapan main bichad jate hai. hema malini, ai es johar, iftekaar or premnath ne bhi is film main ahm bhoomika nibhaai he.
 monu  or sonu  ek police inspactor ke do putr hai. donon ko boxing main acha anubhav he. unke pita kii ranjit dwaara hatya karvaa di jaati he. mohan hatyare ko markar ek car kii dikky main chup jaataa he or parivaar se bichad jaataa he. kai varsh baad sohan ek si aai di afasar ban jaataa he, jo alag-alag vesh badal kar apane cese sulajhaata he.vah johny naamak ek chor kaa vesh banaakar apane ko police ke haathon saunp detaa he tathaa jail main hira se dosti karata he. rekha kaa pyaar main peechha karte hue vah cese sulajhaa letaa he or mujrim ko pakad letaa he.
 1970 main kul aay ke najariye se yah bollywood kii sabase badi film thi or 70 ke dashak kii saatvin sabase badi film.
