 swami niranjananand saraswati satyanand saraswati ke shishy evam uttaradhikari hai. swami satyanand saraswati ne 'satyanand yoga' kaa pravartan kiya tha. swami satyanand ne san 1988 main sampoorn vishwa ke satyananad yoga se sambandhit kaaryon ke samanvay kaa kaary swami niranjananand ko saunp diya tha.
 swami niranjanand kaa janm madhya pradesh ke rajanaadagaanv main hua tha. unke shish unhen aajanm yogi maanate hai.
