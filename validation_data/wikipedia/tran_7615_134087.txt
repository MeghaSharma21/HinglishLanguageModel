 shaktimaapi yaa watametre kisi vidyut-lod dwaara lee ja rahi vidyut-shakti ko maapne ke liye prayukt hota he. chunki shakti kaa maan dhara evam voltata dono ke maan par nirbhar karti he, isaliye watametre kii rachana aisi hoti he ki yah in dono raashiyon ko napate hue unka gunanfal nikaale. is drushti se paripath main jodne ke liye isamen praaia: chaar-sire pradaan kiye gayi hote hai. do sire dhara ke shreneekram main judate hai or anya do voltage ke samaantar-kram main.
