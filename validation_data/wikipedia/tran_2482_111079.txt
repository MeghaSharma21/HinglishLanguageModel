 qahtaani un arab logon ko kaha jaataa he jinaki mul mathrubhumi arabi prayadweep kaa dakshini yaa dakshinpurvi bhaag, visheshkar yaman kshetra, he. bahut se qahtaani qabeele ab apni purv grihabhumi se dur-daraaj kshetron main rahete hai. inke vipareet 'adnan' un arab samudaayon ko kaha jaataa he jinaki mul mathrubhumi arabi prayadweep kaa uttar, pashchimi yaa madhya kshetra he. qahataaniyon kii do shakhaayen hai: himyar or kehlaan . sabhi arab qabeelon ko 'qahtaani' yaa 'adanani' main vargeekrut kiya ja saktaa he or in donon shakhaon main aitihaasik kheenchaatani rahi he. arabi lok-manyata main qahtaani shuru ke mul arab the or adanani baad main arab bane.
