 mufti mohammad said bharat ke jammu or kashmir rajya ke mukhyamantri the. ve jammu or kashmir pipuls democratic party ke adhyaksh the. ve bharat ke grih mantri bhi rahe. is pad par asin hone vaale ve pehle muslim bhartiya the. 07 janvari 2016 ko dilli main unka nidhan hua.
 2014 ke chunaavon main ve anantnag vidhansabha nirvachan kshetra se congress ke ummidavaar hilaal ahamed shah ko 6028 voton ke antar se haraakar vidhayak nirvaachit hue. saal 1989 main inaki beti rubaiyaa said kaa apharan kar liya gaya tha. rubiya ke badale main aatankwaadiyon ne apane paanch saathiyon ko mukt karvaa diya tha. is ghatana kaa virodh jammu kashmir ke tatkaaleen mukhyamantri faruk abdulla ne kiya tha. bharat ke grahamantri rahete hue bhi 24 disambar 1999 ko indian airlines kaa vimaan apahrit kar liya gaya, parinaam swaroop ajahar masood evam anya do aatankiyon ko reeha karnaa padaa.
 mufti kaa janm 12 janvari, 1936 main jammu kashmir ke anantnag jile main bijabehara naamak sthaan par hua tha. unhone aligarh muslim vishvavidhyalay se arab itihaas kii parasnatak tathaa vidhi snaatak kii shiksha praapt kii thi.
 mufti ne apana rajnatik jivan 50 ke dashak ke antim varshon main democratic neshnal conference ke saath prarambh kiya or varsh 1962 main pehali baar bijabehara se vidhayak chune gaye. varsh 1967 main ve isi seat par punah nirvaachit hue or gulam mohammad sadiq kii sarkaar main upamantri banaaye gaye. kuch samay pashchaat ve democratic neshnal conference se alag hokar bhartiya rashtriya congress main sammilit ho gaye. ve kuch un gine-chune logon main se ek the jinhone congress ko ghati main mahatvapurn rajnatik samarthan dilaane main mahatvapurn bhoomika kaa nirvahan kiya tha. varsh 1972 main rajya kii congress sarkaar main unhen cabinet mantri tathaa vidhaan parishad main congress kaa netaa banaaya gaya. varsh 1986 main unhen rajeev gandhi sarkaar ke mantrimandal main paryaton evam naagarik uddyan vibhaag kaa cabinet mantri banaaya gaya. ve varsh 1987 main vishwanath pratap singh ke netritv vaale janamorcha main sammilit ho gaye. varsh 1989 main unhone mantri pad se istipha de diya or veepi singh ke netritv main bani centre sarkaar main unhen kendriya grahamantri banaaya gaya. ve desh ke grahamantri banane vaale pratham muslim vyakti the. unke mantrikaal main inaki beti rubaiyaa said kaa aatankwaadiyon ne jammu-kashmir main apharan kar liya. aatankiyon ne paanch aatankiyon ko jail se reeha karne ke pashchaat unki beti ko choda. pi ve narsimha raav ke kaarykaal main ve ek baar phir congress ke saath aae lekin ve congress ke saath adhik samay tak saath nahin rah paye. varsh 1999 main unhone congress ko chhodkar ek naye kshetreey rajnatik sanghathan jammu or kashmir pipuls democratic party kaa gathan kiya. varsh 2002 main sampann jammu kashmir vidhaan sabha chunaav main unki party ne sahabhaagita kii or vidhaan sabha kii 16 siton par vijay praapt kii. is vijay ke pashchaat unhone congress ke saath gathbandhan sarkaar banaayi, jisamen mukhyamantri ke rup main 2 november, 2002 se lekar 2 november 2005 tak unhone pehali baar jammu-kashmir sarkaar kaa netritv kiya. varsh 2015 main sampann jammu-kashmir rajya ke vidhaan sabha chunaav main inke netritv main pidipi sabase badi vijeta party bani, jisane bhaajapa ke saath gathbandhan karke sarkaar banaayi or ve dubaara mukhyamantri bane.
