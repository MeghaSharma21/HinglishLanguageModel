 brigadier general elecoundar gore archerite hor-ragaven, gaori ke pehle arl  ek british raajnneetijna the. unhen 23 janvari 1936-30 janvari 1945 ke bich, maharaj edward ashtam or unke uttaradhikari, jorge shashtham dwaara, australia ke governor-general yani maharajyapal ke pad par niyukt kiya gaya tha. is kaal ke dauraan ve, maharaj ke pratinidhi ke rup main, unki anupasthiti ke dauraan shaasha ke kartavyon kaa nirvaah karte the. iske alaava, apane vyavsaayik jivan ke dauraan, unhone british samrajya kii seva main, vishwa bhar main vistrut vibhinn british upniveshon main, anya anek mahatvapurn va varchasvpurn padon par apni seva di thi.
