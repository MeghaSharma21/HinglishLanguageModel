 vriksh rekha, paryaavas kii wo seemaa he jahaan par vriksh ug paane main sakasham hote hai. vriksh rekha ke paar, vriksh paryaavaran kii visham paristhitiyon jaise ki bahut kam taapmaana, aparyaapt vaayu daab or/ yaa ardrata kii kami ke kaaran ug paane main asamarth hote hai. kuch mat ek atirikt gahari kashtarekha kii bhi vyakhya karte hai, jahaan pedo ke tanon kaa vikaas sambhav hota he.
 vriksh rekha ke thik upar, vrikshon kaa vikaas avaruddh rahata he or vriksh aksar ghani jhaadiyon rup main hi ugte hai. agar yah hawa ke kaaran hota he, to ise kramholz ke naam se janaa jaataa he, jo ek german shabd he or jiskaa arth mudi hui lakdi he.
 dur se dekhne par vriksh rekha anya kai prakritik rekhaon ke samaan badi spasht dikhaai padati he, par adhiktar sthanon par nikat se nirikshan karne yah sankraman kramik hota he. jaise jaise ham upar kii ore badhate hai jalwaayu ke visham hone ke saath vrikshon kii oonchaai kam hoti jaati he or ek bindu par aakar vriksh ugna band kar dete hai.
