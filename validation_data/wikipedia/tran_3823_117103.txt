 sabut anaaj arthath dane ke teenon bhaagon ko khaaya jaataa he jisamen resha yukt bahari sataha or poshakta se bharpoor beej bhi shaamil he. sabut anaaj vaale khaadya padaarthon main ek bahari khol, bhusi, chokar yaa bran, beej or mulaayam endosperm paaya jaataa he. gehun kii pisai ke vakt upree bhusi evam beej ko hataa diya jaataa he evam starch bahul endosperm hi batch jaataa he. bhusi evam beej se vitamin i, vitamin bi or anya tatv jaise jasta, selenium, tamba, louh, maiganej evam magnesium aadi praapt hote hai. inamen resha bhi prachur maatr main paaya jaataa he. sabhi sabut anaajon main aghulansheel fibre paye jate hai jo ki pachan tantra ke liye behatar maane jate hai, saath hi kuch ghulansheel fibre bhi hote hai jo rakt main vaanchit cholesterol ke str ko badhaate hai. khaastaur se jayee, jau or rai main ghulansheel fibre kii maatr adhik hoti he, sabut anaajon main routine, lignans, kai antioxidents or anya laabhadaayak padaarth paye jate hai.
 purv manyata anusaar sabut anaaj kai rogon se bachaate hai kyonki isamen fibre kii prachurtaa hoti he. parantu naveen khojon se pushti hui ki sabut anaajon main fibre ke alaava kai vitaminon ke anuthe mishran, khanij-lavan, aghulansheel antioxident or phitosterol bhi paae jate hai, jo ki subjiyon or falon main anupasthit hote hai or sharir ko kai rogon se bachaate hai. iskaa seven karne waalon ko motaape kaa khatra kam hota he. motaape ko body maas index or qamar se kulhon ke anupaat se mapa jaataa he. saath hi sabut anaaj se cholesterol str bhi kam bana rahata he jiskaa mukhya kaaran inamen paye jane vaale phitochemicals or antioxidants hai. jo ghar main bane aate kii rotiyaan khate hai unamem bread khaane waalon kii apeksha hriday rogon kii ashanka 25-36 pratishat kam hoti he. isi tarah stroke kaa 37 pratishat, tip-2 madhumeh kaa 21-27 pratishat, pachanatantr cancer kaa 21-43 pratishat or hamarn sambandhi cancer kaa khatra 10-40 pratishat tak kam hota he. 
 sabut anaaj kaa seven karne vaale logon ko madhumeh, coronary dhamni rog, pet kaa cancer or ucch raktchaap jaise rogon kii ashanka kam ho jaati he. sabut anaaj yukt khaadya padaarthon kaa glysemic index kam hota he, jisase ye rakt main sharkara ke str ko kam karte hai. inamen paae jane vaale fibre ansh pet main gas banane kii prakriya kam karte hai evam pet main sthirataa kaa abhas hota he, isliye ye shaareerik vajan ko kam karne main sahaayta karte hai. sabut anaaj or sabut daalen pratidin ke aahaar main avashya sammilit karne chaiye. dhuli daal ke bajaay chhilake vaali daal ko variyata deni chaiye. saabat se banaae gaye taaje uble hue chaaval, idli, upama, dosa aadi rifined anaaj se bane pack kiye utpaadon jaise pasta, noodles aadi se kahin behatar hote hai. roti, ban or bread se zyada acchhee hoti hai. rifind anaaj ke mukaable sabut anaaj vaale khaadya padaarthon se cardeovscular bimariyon evam pet ke cancer kaa khatra kam ho jaataa he.
