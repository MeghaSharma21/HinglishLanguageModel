 moksh
 dhyaan yoga 
 
 guru gita ek hindu granth he. iske rachayita veda vyas hai. vastav main yah skanda puraana kaa ek bhaag he. isamen kul 352 shloke hai.
 guru gita main bhagwaan shiv or parvati kaa samvaad he jisamen parvati bhagwaan shiv se guru or uski mahatta kii vyakhya karne kaa anurodh karti hai. isamen bhagwaan shankar guru kya he, uskaa mahatv, guru kii puja karne kii vidhi, guru gita ko padhne ke laabh aadi kaa varnan karte hai.
 vah sadguru koun ho saktaa he uski kisi mahima he. iskaa varnan is gurugita main purnataa se hua he. shishy kii yogyata, uski maryaada, vyavhaar, anushaasan aadi ko bhi purna roopen darshaya gaya he. aise hi guru kii sharan main jane se shishy ko purnatv praapt hota he tathaa vah swayan brahmroop ho jaataa he. uske sabhi dharm-adharm, paap-punya aadi samaapt ho jate hai tathaa keval ekmaatr chaitanya hi shesh rah jaataa he vah gunathit va roopatit ho jaataa he jo uski antim gati he. yahi uskaa gantavya he jahaan vah pahunch jaataa he. yahi uskaa swaroop he jise vah praapt kar letaa he.
 guru gita main aise sabhi guruon kaa kathan he jinakon 'suchak guru', 'vaachak guru', bodhak guru', 'nishiddh guru', 'vihit guru', 'karnakhya guru', tathaa 'param guru' kaha jaataa he. 
 inamen nishiddh guru kaa to sarvatha tyaag kar dena chahie tathaa anya guruon main param guru hi shreshth hai vahi sadguru hai.
