 marathi vishwakosh maharashtra sarkaar kii khaas pahal dwaara marathi bhashaa main nirmit incychlopedia britania kii tarah ek gyaanakosh he.
 mudrit swaroop main is vishwakosh ko prakashit karne kaa kaary maharashtra rajya vishwakosh nirmiti mandal dwaara san 1960 main armbh kiya gaya. oktober 2012 tak marathi vishwakosh ke 18 khand prakashit kiye ja chuke he. yojana ke anusaar is vishwakosh ke do or kaaya khand prakashit kiye jaayenge. uske baad pratishbd sangrah, suchi sangrah or maanchitr sangrah bhi prakashit kiye jaayenge. 
 marathi bhashaa kaa pehla gyaanakosh shridhar vyankatesh ketakar ji dwaara prakashit'maharashtriya gyaanakosh' tha jiskaa pratham khand 1921 main main prakashit hua. janvari, 1916 main hi vastut: ketakar ji ne marathi gyaanakosh ke mahan saahitiyik anushthan kaa aupchaarik rup se aarambh kiya. unhen ise paanch varsh main prakashit karne kii aashaa thi kintu vastav main keval pehla khand hi san 1921 main nikal sakaa or ikkisvaan khand 1929 main prakashit hua. 1916 se 1929 tak kaa 13-14 varsh kaa samay ketakar ke liye asaadhaaran daud dhoopwali sakriyata kaa tha, kyonki unhen ek saath hi gyaanakosh ke sampadak, vyavasthaapak, mudrak, prakaashak, yahan tak ki sthaan sthaan par jaakar uske graahak banane kaa bhi kaary karnaa padataa tha.
