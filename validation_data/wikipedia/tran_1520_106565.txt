 13 november 2015 kii shaam ko france kii rajdhani paris or uske uttareey upnagareey ilaake saint denis main aatanki hamalon kii ek shrinkhalaa ko anjaam diya gaya jisamen bade paimaane par golibari, aatmghaatee bam visphot, or logon ko bandhak banaaya gaya. hamalon kii shuruyaat 21:20 siiti main sted di france ke baahar teen aatmghaatee hamalon, bade paimaane par shooting or madhya paris ke paas chaar sthanon par ek or aatmghaatee bam visphot ke saath hui. sabase ghaatak hamalaa 14 november ko 00:58 sieet par bataklen theatre main hua jahaan hamalaavaron ne police ke saath ulajhane se pehle logon ko bandhak banaaya. hamalon main 129 log, maare gaye jiname se 89 kii mrityu jinamein se bataklen theatre main hi hui. 352 logon main se 80 gambhir rup se ghayal hone ke kaaran aspataal main bhartee karaayaa gaya. peediton ke alaava, saat hamalaavaron kii bhi mrityu ho gai, or adhikariyon ne bade paimaane par abhi bhi anya saathiyon kii khoj karne ke liye jari rakhaa he.dwitiya vishwayuddh ke baad france par yah sabase ghaatak hamalaa tha.
 france main janvari 2015 ke hamalon ke baad se hai alert ghoshit kiya gaya tha us hamale main nagarikon or police adhikariyon sahit 17 log maare gaye the. jawaab main, 2005 ke dangon ke baad pehali baar apatkaal ghoshit kiya gaya or asthaayee niyantran desh kii simaaon par rakhaa gaya he. logon or sanghathanon ne apni ekajutata ko soshal media ke madhyam se vyakt kiya. 15 november ko, france ke hamalon ke pratishodh main, al-rakka main ai es ai el ke thikaanon ko lakshya banaakar aaparishan chamal, chalaaya gaya jo abhi tak kaa sabase bada hawaii hamalaa he. 18 november ko, hamalon ke mastermind sandigdh, belgium ke abdel hamid, ko francisi police ne ek chaape main maar giraaya.
 janvari 2015 main hue charlie hebdo par hamalen ke baad se hi france main hai alert laga hua tha or 2015 main 30 november se 11 disambar tak hone vaale sanyukt raashtra jalwaayu parivartan sammelan par hamale kii ashanka se or bhi suraksha badha di gayi thi.
 turki, iraq, or isroyal kii khufia agenciyon ne hamalon se maheenon pehle hi french mitti par hamale kii chetavanee di thi, lekin france ke adhikariyon se unhen koi jawaab nahin mila.
 14 navambar ko raashtrapati francis holand ne bataaya ki in hamalon ko iesael ne france main rah rahe logon kii madad se anjaam diya tha. hamalaavaron kii laashon ke paas se syrian or misri passport mili, lekin misr ke adhikariyon ne ise ek peedit aleed abdal-razzaq kaa bataaya jo ki hamalavar nahin tha. 16 navambar ko belgium or france ke jaanch adhikari abdalhamid aboud kii talash karne lage the jo ek kattarpanthi jihadi tha or jiske is hamale kii sajeesh rachane kaa anumaan lagaaya gaya. belgium or france main hue anya hamalon main shaq ke ghere main aane ke baad aboud jo ki ek morokko mul kaa belgiyamvasi he siria bhaag gaya. mana jaataa he ki aboud ne ibrahim or saalaah abdeslam sahit kai jihadiyon ko in hamalon ki anjaam dene ke liye bhartee kar rakhaa tha. varisht europea adhikariyon ne da washington post ko bataaya ki aboud 18 navambar ko french police dwaara uttari paris main daale gai ek chaape main maaraa gaya; haalanki francis molins ne aboud kii mrityu kii pushti nahin kii he. 
 paris ke abhiyokta ke anusaar hamalaavaron kii teen toliyon ne in hamalon ko anjaam diya. unhone apane badan par bam baandh rakhe the. inamen se ek aatmghaatee hamalavar ko pehle 8 baar giraftaar kiya ja chukaa tha, lekin use aatanki kii shrenee main kabhi bhi nahin rakhaa gaya.

 purv america rashtriya aatankanirodhi centre ke diarector maaikel litre ne kaha kii ye hamale jis suniyojit dhang se kiye gayi vaisaa 2008 ke mumbai hamalon ke baad dekhne main nahin aayaa tha. mumbai or paris ke hamalon main kai samantaayein dekhne ko milin. mumbai police ke sanyukt kamishnar deven bharti ne inamen bahut si samaantaaon kaa zikr kiya he jaise, ek hi baar main ek hi saath vibhinn toliyon main kai jagah hamale karnaa, aisi jagahon ko chunanaa jahaan zyada log ekatrit hote hai, iidi va ghaatak riflon kaa prayog karke zyada se zyada logon ko maarna va aatmghaatee hamale karnaa. bharti ke anusaar sirf ek yahi vibhinnata thi ki mumbai hamalon kii tarah yahan hamalaavaron ne hamalon ko jari nahin rakhaa or pakade jane kaa andeshaa hote hi khud ko uda liya.
 iesas ne in aatanki hamalon kii jimmedaari 14 navambar ko apane upar le lee. hamalon ke 24 ghanto ke andar hi iske media samooh al-hayaat media samooh ne dark web par ek website shuru kii or isaki prashansa karte hue apane samarthakon se kuta sandesh seva telegram kaa upyog karne kii salaah di. isane hamale kii vajah paris ke prati apane vaicharik virodh or paris ke ghrinaa or vikrutikaran kii rajdhani hona bataaya. aies ne ise rakka main aies ke thikaanon par nato ke netritv main france ke hamalon or vishwavyaapee muslimon ke prati france sarkaar kii videsh niti kaa jawaab bhi bataaya.
