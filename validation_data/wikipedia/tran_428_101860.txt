 snowdrop, ek ukraini television dharawahik he jiskaa prasaaran bharat main jindagi channel par 16 janvari se raat 9 baje kiya ja rahaa he. jindagi channel, isse pehle turki or pakistan ke kai television kaarykram bhi prasaarit kar chukaa he. safal korian tivi shrinkhalaa aais adonis se prerit snowdrop ek bhaavnaatmak pariwarik katha he jiskaa prasaaran pehali baar 2015 main tiaarke par kiya gaya tha. snowdop kii kahani do sauteli bahanon, nadia or irina kii pratidwandvita ke bare main he jo ek hi aadmi 'igore' se pyaar karti hai.
