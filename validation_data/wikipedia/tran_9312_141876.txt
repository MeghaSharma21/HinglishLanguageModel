 atiyatharthwaad, kala or sahitya ke kshetra main pratham mahayuddh ke lagbhag prachlit hone vaali style or andolan tha. chithran or murtikla main to yah aadhuniktam style or takanic hai. iske prachaarkon or kalakaaron main chiriko, daalon, moro, arp, breton, maasan aadi pradhan hai. kala main is sristi kaa darshanik nirupan 1924 main andre breton ne apni atiyathartwaadee ghoshnaa main kiya. atiyatharthwaad kala kii, saamaajik yatharthwaad ke atirikt, naveenatam style he or idhar, manovigyaan kii pragati se prabhavit, prabhoot lokapriy hui he.
 atiyatharthwaad kaa siddhant iske pravartakon dwaara is prakaar abhivyakt huah atiyatharth yatharth se, drishy-shravy-jagat se pare hai. yah vah param yatharth he jo avchetan main nihit hota he; sushupt, tandrit, swapnil avasthaa main asaadhaaran kalpit, akalpit, apratyaashit anubhutiyon ke rup main anayas aavegon dwaara maanas ke chitrapat par chadhataa utarataa rahata he. jo vishay athwa drishy saadharantah tarktah paraspar asambaddha lagate hai vastav main unamem alakshith sambandh he jise maatr atiyatharthwaad prakashit kar saktaa he. atiyathartwaadiyon kii pratigya he ki hamaare saare kaaryon kaa udgam avchetan antar he. vahi hamaare kaaryon ko gati or disha bhi detaa he or us udgam se prasphutit hone vaale manobhaavon ko drushtigamy, sthool, rassikt akruti di ja sakati he.
 atiyatharthwaad ke pratik or maan dainandin jivan ke parimaanon, pratibodhon se sarvatha bhinn hote hai. atiyathartwaadiyon kii abhiruchi alokik, adbhut, akalpit or asangat sthitiyon kii abhivyakti main he. aisa nahin ki us avchetan kaa sahitya athwa kala main astitv pehle na rahaa ho. pariyon kii kahaniyaan, asaadhaaran kii kalpana, jaise elice in da vanderland athwa sindbaad kii kahaniyaan, bacchon athwa ardhvikshipt vyaktiyon ke chitrankan sahitya or kala donon kshetron main atiyatharthwaad kii ikaiyaan prastut karte hai. atiyathartwaadiyon kii sthapana he ki ham parthiv drishy jagat ko bhedakar, uske tathokt yatharth kaa atikaraman karke vaastavik param yatharth ke jagat main pravesh kar sakate hai. ankan ko akritiyon ke pratinidhaan kii aavashyaktaa nahin, use jivan ke gahan tatwon ko samajhnaa or samajhaana he, jivan ke prati maanav pratikriyaaon kaa akalan karnaa he or ye tathy nihsandeh drishy jagat ke pare ke hai. ankan ko manoranjan athwa anand kaa saadhan maananaa anuchit he. sthool netron kii seemayen or pratyaksh kii riktata to ghanvaadee kala ne hi pramanit kar di thi, isse aavashyaktaa prathit hui drushti se ateet paroksh se saakshaatkaar kii, jo avchetan he, yuktisangat yatharth ke pare kaa ayuktiyukt atiyatharth.
 is prakaar atiyatharthwaad maanas ke antaraal ko, avchetan ke tamaavisht gahawaron ko aalokit karata he. ghanavaad se bhi ek pag aage dadawad gaya or dadawad se bhi aage atiyatharthwaad. atiyatharthwaad kii jaden dadawad kii jameen main hi lagi hai. swayan dadawad ne kriyaatmak kalpana kii bhoomi chhod nirbandh avchetan kii aradhana kii thi, ab uske uttarvarti atiyatharthwaad ne avchetan or drishy jagat ko paraspar sarvatha swatantra or pruthak mana. maanviya chetanta or parthiv yatharth athwa kayik anubhuti main uske vichaar se koi sambandh nahin. unhone atmadhyayan, jivan ke param tathy kii khoj or drishy se bhinn ek antarjagat kii pahchaan ko apana lakshya banaaya. unhone kaha ki saavyaveey sampoornataa ke bheetar sthultah lakshith hone vaale paraspar virdohi par vastutah anukool tathyon, jaise jivan or mrityu, bhoot or bhavishya, satya or kaalpanik ko ekatra karnaa hoga. atiyathartwaadee ghoshnaakar andre breton ne likhaah meraa vishwaas he ki bhavishya main donon paraspar virdohi lagane vaali swapn or satya kii sthitiyaan param yatharth, atiyatharth main lay ho jaayengi.
 chithran kii pragati main atiyatharthwaad ne paramparaagat kalastyli ko tilanjali de di. uske akalan or abhipraayon ne, chitradarshon ne sarvatha nayaa mode liya, parvarti se antarvarti kii ore. avchetan kii swapnil sthitiyon, veikshiptavastha tak, ko usne shuddh pragna kaa svachhand rup mana. saadharantah atiyatharthwaad ke do bhed kiye jate hai: swapnabhivyakti or aavegankan. unamem pehali style kaa vishisht kalakaar salvador daali he or duusari kaa joan mero. donon spain ke hai. avchetan ke upaask atiyatharthwaad ko phir bhi akalan ke kshetra main raag or rekha kii drushti se sarvatha uchhrunkhal bhi nahin samajhnaa chahie. yah sahi he ki abhipraay athwa ankit vishay ke sambandh main atiyatharthwaad apratyaashit kaa akalan karata he, par jahaan tak ankan kii takanic kii baat he uske aayam-parinaam sarvatha sanyat, spasht or shramsiddh hote hai. daali ke chitra to is disha main dach chitrachaaryon kii kala se hode karte hai. apratyaashit yatharth kaa udhaaharan aise chitra se diya ja saktaa he jiskaa sara vaataavaran to chikitsaalay ke shalyakaksh kaa ho par aaparishan kii mej par, jahaan marij ke hone kii aashaa kii ja sakati he, vahaan vastutah chitrit hoti he silaai kii mashin. yaa nari kaa urdhvardh ankit karne vaale chitra main jahaan upar munh hone kii apeksha kii jaati he vahaan vastutah mej kii daraaj bani rahati he.
