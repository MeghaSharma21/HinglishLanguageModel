  manovaigyanik jyotish yaa jyothishmanovigyaan jyotish or manovigyaan ke sanyog se nirmit nayaa adhyayan kshetra he. isamen jataka ke man kaa adhyayan jyotish ke anusaar kiya jaataa he. isamen rashifal nikaalane kii anek paddatiyaan hai. jyotishi tathaa manochikitsak glen peri manovaigyanik jyotish ko vyaktitv siddhant or upachaar upkaran donon ke rup main chinhit karte hai.
