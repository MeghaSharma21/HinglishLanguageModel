 photheringhe kila angrezi: Fotheringay Castle nothhamptonshir ke oundal ke bazaar va nagar . se uttar main 3 1⁄2 mile par sthit photheringhe ke gaanv main maujuud tha. iskaa nirmaan sambhavat: 1100 i. main northhampton ke arl simon de senlis ne karavaaya tha. 1113 main iskaa malikana haq scotland ke rajkumaar david ke paas chalaa gaya jab unhone simon kii vidhwaa se shaadi kar lee. iske baad se terahavin shatabdi main england ke raja jon ke kabje main aane se pehle tak kile ke maalik vibhinn scotash rajkumaar bane rahe.
 1220 tak photheringhe kila kaa niyantran chester ke chhathen arl, ranulf de blondeville ke haath main tha. agle varsh janvari main kuch samay ke liye kile par england ke raja henry 3, england kaa raja ke virodh main utre wiliam II di forz kaa kabja ho gaya tha. forz ke kile khaali karne ke baad henry ne ise apane niyantran main le liya or edward 2 ke shasankaal tak yah kila shahi sampatti bana rahaa.
 yah york ke dukon kaa pasandida ghar rahaa or raja richard 3 kaa janm yaheen 1452 main hua tha. 
 yah scoton kii rani, mairi kaa antim karawas bhi rahaa jinke khilaaf isi mahal main mukadmaa chalaa va 1587 main mrityudand diya gaya.
 1630 main kila khaali kar diya gaya va iske adhikansh shahi sajo-samaan hataa liye gayi. phir yahan sirf bhawan hi bachaa. kile kii jagah britain kii sangrakshit jagahon main shaamil he or aam janata ke ghoomane phirane ke liye khuli hui he.
