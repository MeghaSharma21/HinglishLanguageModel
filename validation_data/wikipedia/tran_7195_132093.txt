 1918 main rus se swatantraaa kii ghoshnaa karne ke baad, esthonia pehali baar 1920 ke greeshmakaleen olympic main ek raashtra ke rup main bhaag liya, haalanki 1923 main rashtriya olympic samiti kii sthapana hui thi. pehla sheetakaaleen olympic 1924 sheetakaaleen olympic tha. esthonian athleton ko olympic khelon main bhaag liya jab tak desh par akraman kiya gaya tha or 1940 main soviet sangh dwaara kabja kar liya. 1980 greeshmakaleen olympic naukayan reguta rajdhani shahar tolin main yojit kiya gaya tha. 1991 main soviet kabje ke ant ke baad, esthonia ne sabhi olympic main bhaag liya he. esthonia ne kushti, bharottolan, cross-country skiing or athletics main apane adhikansh padak jite hai.
