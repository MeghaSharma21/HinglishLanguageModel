 jabada yaa hanu kisi praani ke munh ke pravesh-kshetra par sthit us dhaanche ko bolte hai jo munh ko kholta or band karata he or jiske prayog se khaane ko mukh dwaara pakadaa jaataa he tathaa chabaaya jaataa he. maanav samet bahut se anya jaanwaron main jabade ke do hisse hote hai jo ek duusare se chul ke zariye jude hote hai jiske prayog se jabada upar-neeche hokar mukh kholta he yaa band karata he. aise praaniyon main khaadya saamagree chabane yaa chirane ke liye jabadon main aksar daant lage hote hai. iske vipareet bahut se kiton ke jabade mukh ke dai-bai taraf lage do chote shakhnuma ang hote hai jo chimate kii tarah khaanaa pakadakar unke mukh tak le jate hai.
 dhyaan den ki sanskrit kaa 'hanu' shabd 'jabade' ke saath-saath kuch sandarbhon main 'gaal' kaa arth bhi rakhata he. isi shabd se hanuman kaa naam utpann hua he or iskaa arth he 'toote/ghaav-lage jabade vaale'. kathanusaar jab hanuman ne suraj pakad liya tha to indra ne unke gaal par vajra de maaraa tha.
 atiprachinkaal main jaanwaron ke jabade nahin the. lampri jaisi kuch rudhirup machhliyon main ab bhi jabade nahin hote, lekin lampriyon ke munh ke andar upasthi ke kuch chaap hote hai jo unke chlom ko dhanchiya sahaara dete hai. mana jaataa he ki aaj se lagbhag 40 karod saal purv yahi dhaanche kram viksit hokar vishwa ke pehle jabade ban gaye.
