 julian or gregorian calendar main isvi isa messiah ke janm ke baad ke varshon ko darshata he or isa purv unke janm se purv ke varshon ko darshata he.
 udhaaharan: 10 i. kaa matlab he isa ke janm se 10 saal baad kaa samay. 10 i.pus. kaa matlab he isa ke janm se 10 saal pehle kaa samay.
 aajkal isvi kii jagah kaaman era or isa purv kii jagah "bifor kaaman era" kaa prayog saamaanya ho gaya he.
