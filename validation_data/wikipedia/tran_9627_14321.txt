 hindi vikipedia par vaakyon main prayukt avyay shabd, kriya shabd tathaa shabdon ke bahuvachan ke prayog main ekarupata nahin he, jaise- gayi / gaye, gayi / gai, naye/nai, huye/hui, aayi/ai ....... aadi kaa prayog kiya ja rahaa he jo ki thik nahin he. " ye, e, yi, i ke prayog main ekarupata ke kuch niyam hai jinka paalan karke lekhon main vartaneegat ekarupata rakh sakate hai-" ye " tathaa " e " kaa prayog avyay, kriya tathaa shabdon ke bahuvachan banane ke liye kiya jaataa he.
 jis kriya ke bhootkaalik pulling ek vachan rup main " yaa " ant main aaye to anya roopon main prayog is tarah hoga-
 gaya, gayi, gayi, gayin, aayaa, aayi, aaye, aayin
 jis kriya ke bhootkaalik pulling ek vachan rup main " aa " ant main aaye to anya roopon main prayog is tarah hoga-hua, hue, hui, huin 
 jahaan ant main kaa prayog kisi bhi rup main na ho rahaa ho vahaan kaa prayog kiya jaataa he, jaise-dijie, pijie, lijie, kiijie honge 
 avyay ko prathak rakhane ke liye " e " kaa prayog hoga- isliye, chahie, jaaie, aaie
 sampradaan vibhakti main bhi " e " kaa prayog hoga- " mohan ke liye kursi lao " " mohan ke liye kursi lao "
