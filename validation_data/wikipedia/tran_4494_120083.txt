 razia bat pakistan se ek urdu upanyaaskaar or naatakkaar thi. unke upanyaason main aam taur par majboot mahila paatr hote hai, or filmon or television naatakon main naatakeey rup se chitrit hai.
 janm samay razia niaz ke rup main 19 may 1924 ko wazirabad main janmi razia niaz ne, peshawar main apana adhikansh bachapan batit kiya. 
 unka naam pehali baar 1940 ke aasapaas ek saahitiyik patrika main prakashit hua tha, jab vah kishoravastha main thim. baad main unhone apni pehali prakashit kahani ko ek upanyaas, naila main viksit kiya.
 1946 main vivaahit hone ke baad, razia batt ne 1950 main kuch varshon ke ek brake ke baad phir se likhnaa shuru kiya. baad main unhen apane samay ke kuch vipul lekhakon main se ek ke rup main sammanit kiya gaya. use takreeban 51 upanyaas or 350 laghu kathaayen kaa credit jaataa he. usne kai radio naatak bhi likhe hai.
 urdu fiction lekhan main kai prasiddh naamon kii samakaleen hone ke naate, razia bat ne ek vishesh brands kii kahani kehane ke liye khud ko paripurna banaakar apane liye ek jagah or bazaar banaaya. aashcharya nahin ki, unke kai upanyaas, jaise saika or naala, bade parade ke liye anukulit kiye gaye the, auranorina, najia, saica or bano tivi naatak dharawahikon ke liye.
 pathakon kii kai pidiyon ke saath lokapriy, razia bat ne ek aatmakatha bichade lamhe bhi prakashit kii.
 razia bat kii lahore par 4 july 2012 ko lambii beemaari ke baad mrityu ho gai.
