 suicide saqwad varsh 2016 kii america superhiro film he jo disi kamiks ke khalnaayakon ke dal par rakhe samaan naam par hi aadhaarit he. disi ke vistrut sristi kii yah bataur teesraa sanskaran he, vahiin film kaa lekhan or nirdesan david aiyer dwaara kiya gaya he, tathaa film main kai nami adaakaaron jaise vil smith, jaired lato, margot raabi, jaayal kinamen, viyola davis, jai cartni, jai hernandays, adevail ekinuye-egabej, ike barinhaltaz, scot eastwood evam kaara delevingne aadi ko shaamil kiya gaya he. film suicide saqwad kii kahani anusaar, ek khufia sarkari agency kii pramukh amanda vailer ke netritv main kuch kaidi suparvillenon ko ek ghaatak va khatarnaak abhiyaan ke liye behaal karti he or ek bhayanak ashanka se duniya bachaai jaati he, jo bataur unako sajaa sunaae jane badale yah avasar miltaa he. farvari 2009 se hi, suicide saquad ke vikaas kaa kaam warner brass. pikchars jari kar chuki thi. aiyer ko bhi sitambar 2014 main film lekhan or nirdesan ke liye niyukt kar liya gaya, tathaa oktober se, casting prakriya aarambh kii gai. pramukh photography kii shuruyaat april 13, 2015 main, toranto, ontario, canada se hui jisamen atirikt filmakan ke liye chicago, illinois ko chunaa gaya or agast main jaakar samaapt kiya gaya. 
 suicide saqwad kaa premear new yark city main agast 1, 2016, ko kiya gaya or agast 5, 2016 ko thiyatron, main 2di, 3di tathaa imax 3di main pradarshit kiya gaya. film ne $267 karod se zyada kaa vyavsaay bhi kiya. film ke pratikriya par samikshakon ne kaaphi nakaaratmakataa raay di, jahaan aalochakon ne film kaa nirdesan va sampadan kaaphi avyavasthit laga, patakatha main bhi gadabadi dekhi gai, or paatro ke vistaar main bhi baariki nahin rakhi gai, haalaanki abhinay pradarshan evam vardhit hasya ko lekar pichhali disi ke vistrut sristi kii filmon main kaaphi saraahneey prashansa kii gai.
 filmakan kaa aarambh april 13, 2015 se kiya gaya. vahiin is april 26 va 27 ko, filmakan ke liye da hai stichouse kii jagah ko rakhaa gaya. film main maujuud "barfile tufaan" vaale drishy ko april 29 main edlydi saint. or ching lain main filmaaya gaya. may 5 ko, kuch vishaal drishyon ke liye toranto ke downtaun baad yonge or dundas sakwae main bhi filmakan kiya gaya. vahiin raat ko filmakan darmyaan kuch nijee vajahon kai sadak chinhon ko hataaya gaya tha. pramukh photography ko agast 2015 main lapetate hue aakhir main chicago sthit illinois main bhi atirikt filmakan kiya gaya. aiyer june 24, 2016 tak filmakan purna hone tak pushti kar chuke the. or isaki pushti zake snyder ne da flash ke drishy ko filmakan karne baad hi kiya. 
 march 2016 main, warner brass. dwaara yah ghoshnaa hui ve aiyer tathaa smith ke saath aagaamee siquil main vaapasi karenge, jab 2017 main shooting hogi.
