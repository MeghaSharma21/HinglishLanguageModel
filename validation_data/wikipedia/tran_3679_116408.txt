 hubert cesil booth ek angrej ejeeniyar the, jinhen pehle vidyut sanchalit vaikum clinar banane ke liye janaa jaataa he . 
 unhone feris pahiyon, nilamban pulon or kaarkaanon ko bhi dizin kiya tha. baad main ve british vaikum clinar or ejineering company ke adhyaksh or prabandh nideshak bhi bane. 
 booth 1871 main gloucester, england main janmen the. ve pradhanadhyaapak ech loyad breton kii dekharekh main gloucester college or gloucester county skool main shikshit hue. 1889 main unhone pravesh pariksha uteern karte hue kendriya takneekee college, landan main daakhila liya. professor wiliam couthorn ke  maargdarshan main civil ejineering or maikenical ejineering kaa teen saal ke course kiya. unhone diploma of associetship puraa kiya, isamen ve ejineering vibhaag main duusare sthaan par aae. aage ve civil ejiniers santhaan ke chaatr bane.
