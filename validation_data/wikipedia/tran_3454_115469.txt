 vitthal bhai patel bharat ke prakhyat vidhanvetta, vaktrutvkala ke acharya or bhartiya swatantraaa aandolan ke desh-videsh main prachaarak, pravartak the. ve sardaar vallabh bhai patel ke bade bhai the. 
 aapkaa janm san 1871 isvi main gujarat ke kheda jila ke antargat "karamsad" gaanv main hua tha. aapaki praarabhik shikshadiksha karamsad or nadiyaad main hui. iske baad aap kanoon kii shiksha praapt karne england gaye. vahaan se varistary kii pariksha main uteern hokar aap swadesh aae or vakaalat prarambh kii. aapke aakarshak evam prabhaavshaalee vyaktitv tathaa kanoon ke paanditya ne, alpakaal main hi apko is kshetra main prasiddh kar diya tathaa aapane paryaapt dhan bhi arjeet kiya. isi bich dharmapatni kii mrityu ne aapke jivan main parivartan kiya or aap sheeghra hi saarvajanik kaaryon main bhaag lene lage.
 sarvapratham aapke bombai karporation, bombai dharasabha tathaa congress main mahatvapurn kaary kiya. 24 agast 1925 isvi ko aap dharasabha ke adhyaksh chune gaye. yaheen se aapke jivan kaa sabase mahatvapurn adhyay prarambh hota he. aapaki nishpaksh, nirbhik vichaardhaara ne dharaatal main aapke vyaktitv kii amit chhap ankit kii. sansadeey vidhi vidhaanon ke aap prakand pandit the. yahi kaaran he ki aap sadan main sabhi dalon ke aadar evam shraddha ke paatr the or aapaki di hui vidvattapurna vyavastha sabhi logon ko maanya hua karti thi. kendriya dharasabha ke adhyaksh ke rup main aap bharat main hi nahin videshon main bhi vikhyath hue. vidhi vishyak apane sookshm gyaan se aap tatkaaleen sarkaar ko bhi pareshaani main daal diya karte the. apane kaary tathaa desh ko aapako sahaj abhimaan tha.
 dharasabha main pandit motilal nehru, shri jina kii singhgarjana se utpann kshobh evam aavesh ke gambhir vaataavaran ko apane prabhaavshaalee vyaktitv tathaa asaadhaaran paanditya se aap dekhte hi dekhte parivartit kar dete the. satya ke aap pujari the or anyaay ke ghor virdohi. isaliye sarkaar bhi aapkaa loha maanati thi. shri vitthal bhai ne dharasabha kii adhyakshata dwaara jaise ucch evam ujwal adarsh upasthit kiye, unhen dhyaan main rakhkar yah baat ni:sankoch kahi ja sakati he ki aapke sadrush sammaan evam gaurav sahit dhara sabha kii adhyakshata aapke kisi purvavarti ne nahin kii.
 pandit motilal nehru ke netritv main jab swarajya party ne kendriya dharasabha ke bahirgaman kiya tha, us samay aapane apni asaadhaaran shakti kaa parichay deshavaasiyon ko diya. aapane tatkaal hi sadan main ghoshit kiya ki dharasabha main bahumat dal kii anupasthiti se uskaa pratinidhimulak swaroop samaapt ho gaya he. at: sarkaar ko is samay koi bhi vivaadaaspad bill sadan main upasthit nahin karnaa chahie. iske saath hi aapane spasht kar diya ki yadi sarkaar yah baat na sweekaar karegi to mujhe apane visheshadhikaar se dharasabha kii baithak sthagit kar deni padegi. apni vishpakshta tathaa nirbhikta ke kaaran hi aap duusare varsh bhi nirvirodh dharasabha ke adhyaksh nirvaachit hue.
 jansuraksha bill ke samay aapane sarkaar kaa jis prakaar maargdarshan kiya vah smaraneey he. aapane sarkaar ko paraamarsh diya ki meerut shadyantr cese ke kaaran is samay desh main sarvatra baari kshobh evam asantosh failaa hua he, aisi sthiti main ab suraksha bill par vichaar ke liye do hi maarg hai. ek to bill ko samprati sthagit rakhaa jaay or duusare, sarkaar meerut shadyantr cese ko uthaa le. jab sarakar ne aapaki yah salaah na maani to bill pesh hone par use aapane apane visheshadhikaar se upasthit kiye jane ke ayogy qaraar de diya. san 1930 main jan congress ne dharasabhaaon kaa bahishkaar aarambh kiya to aapane bhi dharasabha kii adhyakshata se padatyag kar diya. apane tyaagptr main aapane likhaa ki - swatantraaa kii is ladaai main meraa uchit sthaan assembly kii kursi par nahin apitu ran kshetra main he.
 san 1930 isvi main dilli main congress kaarysmiti ke sadasyon ke saath hi aap bhi giraftaar kar jail bhej die gaye. aapako chha: maas kii jail kii sajaa sunai gai. jail main aapkaa swasthya kharaab ho gaya falat: avadhi puri hone ke purv hi aapaki rihaai ho gai. shri vitthalbhai patel ke hriday main mathrubhumi ko swatantra dekhne kii utkar abhilaashaa thi. isi kaaran aap atyant saadagi evam nyaay bhaavana se rahaa karte the. aapako dharasabha se jo vetan miltaa tha uskaa baari ansh mahatma gandhi ko de dete the or kam kharch main hi apana kaam chalaate the. is prakaar aapkaa chalis hajaar rupaya mahatma gandhi ke paas ekatra ho gaya tha jisase balikaon ke liy ek skool kii sthapana kii gai or jiskaa udghaatan aapke nidhan ke baad 31 may 1935 ko mahatma gandhi ne hi kiya.
 jail se chhootane ke baad swasthya thik na rahane par bhi aap america bhraman karne chale gaye or vahaan aapane bhartiya swatantraaa ke paksh main atyant prabhaavshaalee evam ojasvi rup main prachaar kiya. is shram ke kaaran aapkaa swasthya pun: girane laga. antat: aap chikitsarth geneva aae. yahan upachaar ke baavjood aapke swasthya main sudhaar na ho sakaa or vahiin aspataal main san 1933 main aapkaa nidhan hua.
