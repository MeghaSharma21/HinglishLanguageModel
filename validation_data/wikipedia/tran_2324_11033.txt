 erik dainiel piyare cantona ek french abhinayta or purv footbal khilaadi hai. unhone apana peshewar footbal khilaadi kaa karier manchester united main samaapt kiya jahaan unhone paanch varshon main chaar premier lig khitaab jite, jinamein do lig or egge cap dubles shaamil hai.
 cantona ko aksar footbal kii mahashakti ke rup main manchester united ke punaruddhar main ek pramukh karishmai bhoomika nibhaane vaale khilaadi ke rup main sammaan diya jaataa he or unhen club ke saath-saath angreji footbal main ek pratikatmak sthaan bhi praapt he. 2001 main, unhen manchester united ke sadi ke sarvshereshth khilaadi ke rup main chunaa gaya tha or pyaar se unhen "king erik" kaa upnaam bhi diya gaya he. ve france ke samudr tateey footbal team ke vartmaan prabandhak hai.
 footbal se apni sevanivritti ke baad, unhone cinema ko karier ke rup main liya or 1998 kii film elizabeth, sitare kalakaar ket blanchet or 2009 kii film looking for erik main bhoomikaayen nibhaai.
 2010 main, unhone apni patni - rashida brachni dwaara nirdeshit french naatak phase au paradij main ek rangamanch abhinayta ke rup main shuruyaat kii he.
 haalanki yah bataaya jaataa he ki unka janm paris main hua tha, vastav main cantona marsile main albert cantona or eleonnor rorich ke ghar paidaa hue the. paariwarik ghar marsile kshetra main calols kii pahadiyon ke upar ek gupha ke sheersh par, shahar kii 11veen or 12veen arondishment ke bich sthit tha or yah afvaah thi ki dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke aakiri dinon main german sena dwaara iskaa istemaal apane luk-out post ke rup main kiya gaya tha. is sthaan ko 1950 ke dashak ke madhya main cantona kii paitruk daadi, lucien ne chunaa tha, jinke pati, joseph ek sangatraash the. 1966 main cantona ke paidaa hone ke samay tak, pahari ke kinare maujuud gupha paariwarik ghar ke liye ek kamare se kahin behatar ban gayi thi, jo ab rahane yogya sthiti main aa gayi thi. cantona ke do bhai hai: jin mairi, jo unase chaar saal bade hai; or joel, jo 17 maheene chote hai. cantona apravasiyon ke ek parivaar se hai: unke paitruk dada, joseph, sardinia se marsile main aakar bas gaye the, jabaki unki maan ke mata-pita katalan algaavvaadiyon main se the. cantona ke naanaa pedro rorich, 1938 main spanish civil waar main general franko kii senaaon se lade the, jab unke jigar main ek gambhir chot pahaunchi thi or unhen unki patni packita ke saath chikitsakeey ilaaj ke liye france le jaaya gaya tha. marsile main basane se pehle rorich parivaar saint-prest, ardesh main aakar tehre the.
 cantona ne apane footbal karier kii shuruyaat esao calolis ke saath kii, jo unke yahan kii ek sthaneey team he or jisane roger yov jaisi pratibha ko janm diya or jahaan jin tigaanaa or crista galtier jaise khilaadi iske rank main shaamil the. multah, cantona ne apane pita ke nakshekadam par chalanaa shuru kiya tha or aksar ek golkeeper ke rup main khelaa tha, lekin jaise-jaise unki rachanaatmak pravriti ubhartee gayi or ve adhik se adhik baar aage badhkar khilne lage. esao calolis ke saath apane samay main, cantona ne 200 se adhik maichon main khelaa or tab yah kaha gaya tha ki, "nau varsh kii umr main, vah pehle se hi ek pandraa varshiya khilaadi kii tarah khel rahaa tha".
 cantona kaa pehla peshewar club par tha oxere, jahaan 5 navambar 1983 ko nancy par 4-0 kii jeet ke saath apni shuruyaat karne se pehle unhone yuva team ke saath do varsh bitaye the.
 1984 main varsh bhar cantona kaa footbal karier sthir rahaa kyonki is dauraan unhone apni rashtriya seva kaa kaam puraa kiya. yahan se apni sevanivruti ke baad unhen french second divijan main martigus. main le liya gaya. 1986 main oxere se dubaara judane or ek peshewar anubandh par hastakshar karne ke baad, pratham shrenee main unka pradarshan itnaa behatar ho gaya tha unhone apana antarrashtriya cap hasil kar liya. haalanki, unki pehali anushaasanamak samasya 1987 main hi shuru ho gayi thi, tab apane team ke saathi khilaadi bruno martini ke chahare par ghunsa marane ke liye unpar jurmaana lagaaya gaya tha.
 agle varsh, nantes khilaadi, mishael der zakaariyaan ke saath khatarnaak tareeke se nipatane ke kaaran cantona ek baar phir mushkil main pad gaye the, jiske parinamswaroop unhen teen baar ke khel se nilambit hona padaa, jise baad main ghataakar do baar kar diya gaya, kyonki oxere club ne us khilaadi ko rashtriya team ke chayan ke liye anuplabdh rakhane kii dhamkee di thi. ve us under-21 francisi team kaa hissaa the jisane 1988 you21 european championships jiti thi or is kamyaabi ke kuch hi samay baad, unhen francisi record shulk  ke liye, marsele ko saunp diya gaya, jo vahi club tha jiskaa samarthan unhone bachapan main kiya tha. cantona ne apane karier main abhi tak kai baar apane "gussell" hone kaa parichay bhi diya he or janvari 1989 main torpeado moscow ke khilaaf ek dostana match ke dauraan sthanapann kiye jane ke baad unhone gend ko laat markar bheed kii ore oochaal diya or apni jersey utarkar phenk di. pratikriya swarup unke club ne unhen ek maheene ke liye pratibandhit kar diya. iske bas kuch hi maheene pehle, tivi par ek rashtriya coach kaa apmaan karne ke baad unhen ek varsh ke liye antarrashtriya maichon se pratibandhit kar diya gaya tha.
 marsile main basane ke liye sangharsh karne ke baad, cantona chah maheene ke rin par bardiks or uske baad ek varsh ke rin par phir maantpelior chale gaye. montpelior main, ve apane team ke saathi khilaadi jin-cloud lamoult ke saath bhid gaye the or lamoult ke chahare par apana juuta phenk maaraa tha. is ghatana ke kaaran chah khilaadiyon ne cantona ko barkhaast karne kii maang kar di. haalanki, laurent blank or carlos walderrama jaise apane team ke saathiyon ke samarthan ke kaaran, club ne un par das dinon kaa pratibandh lagane ke baavjood unki sevaaon ko barkaraar rakhaa. cantona kii kadi mehnat ke dam par team ko francisi cap jeetne main kamyaabi mili or unke shandaar khel ne marsile ko unhen waapas lene ko raji kar liya.
 marsile main waapas lautakar, cantona ne coach jerard jily or unke uttaradhikari franj bekanbawar ke adheen shuruyaat main acha khel dikhaaya. haalanki, marsile ke adhyaksh barnard tepi parinaamon se santusht nahin the or isane bekanbawar kii jagah remand githls ko le liya, jinhen cantona apane saamne nahin dekhnaa chaahate the. cantona nirantar tepi ke saath ulajhate rahete the or team ko francisi divijan 1 khitaab jitaane main madad karne ke baavjood, unhen agle satr main nimes sthanantarit kar diya gaya tha.
 disambar 1991 main, nimes ke liye ek match ke dauraan unhone referi ke ek faisle se naaraaz hokar, unke upar gend phenk diya tha. unhen french footbal federation dwaara anushaasanamak sunwaayee ke liye bulaayaa gaya or unpar ek maheene kaa pratibandh laga diya gaya. iske pratikraya swarup cantona ne sunwaayee samiti ke prayek sadsy ke paas jaakar unhen "murkh" kahkar bulaayaa. tab unka pratibandh teen maheene ke liye badha diya gaya tha. cantona ke liye, yah aakiri vivaad thi or unhone disambar 1991 main footbal se apane sanyas kii ghoshnaa kar di.
 france kii rashtriya team ke coach maaikel platini cantona ke bade prashansak the or chunki ve unki pratibha kii bahut qadra karte the, unhone unhen vaapasi karne ke liye raji kar liya. apane manovishleshak ke saath-saath jerard holier kii salaah par, apane karier ko punah armbh karne ke liye ve england chale gaye, "unhone mujhe marsile ke liye sin nahin karne kii salaah di or yah paraamarsh diya ki mujhe england chale janaa chahie."
 6 navambar 1991 ko, enfield main yuiede cap duusare daur ke duusare lig taai main oxere par liverpool kii 3-0 kii jeet ke baad, francisi mishael platini khel kii samaapti par liverpool ke prabandhak greem souness se mili, jinhone unhen bataaya ki cantona liverpool ke liye khelnaa chaahate hai. souness ne platini ko dhanyavad diya, lekin unhone dressing room main sadbhaav kaa hawala dete hue is peshkash ko thukraa diya. janvari 1992 main, cantona shefield vednesade ke saath ek saptaah ke pareekshan ke liye england aae, jiskaa prabandhan trevor francis ne kiya tha, jo padonnati praapt karne ke sirf ek sijan ke baad pratham shrenee main teesraa sthaan praapt karne kii sthiti main the.
 pareekshan ke liye ek saptaah kaa atirikt samay kii peshkash milane par, unhone ise thukraa diya or iske badale yorkshaire ke pratidwandvi leeds united ke saath jud gaye, jahaan ve us team kaa hissaa rahe jisane english footbal main pratham shrenee ke rup main premier lig dwaara pratisthapit hone se pehle, footbal lig pratham shrenee championships kaa final jeeta tha. cantona ne leeds ke liye unke championships jeetne ke sijan main pandrah baar saamne aaye or keval teen gol karne ke baavjood unhone unako khitaabi jeet dilaane ke liye kai baar gol daagne main sahyogi bhoomika nibhaate hue kadi mehnat kii, jinamein zyaadaatar gol sarvaadhik gol karne vaale lee chapman dwaara kiye gaye the. 1992 main unhone liverpool par charity shield kii 4-3 se jeet main ek hat-trick banaaya tha or uske baad totenham hotspur par 5-0 kii lig jeet main doosraa hat-trick banaaya. spurs ke khilaaf unki hat-trick premier lig main banaayi gayi sabase pehali hattric thi.
 charity shield main unki hat-trick ne unhen un khilaadiyon kii choti si jamaat main shaamil kar diya jinhone wembali stadium main hue khelon main teen yaa isse zyada gol kiye the.
 26 navambar 1992 ko cantona ne 1.2 million pound par manchester united main shaamil hone ke liye leeds ko chhod diya. leeds ke prabandhak howard vilkinson ne manchester united ke chairman martin edwards ko denis irvin kii uplabdhta ke bare main poochne ke liye telephone kiya tha. edwards us samay united ke prabandhak elex ferguson ke saath ek baithak main the or phir donon is baat par sahamat ho gaye ki irvin bikri ke liye uplabdh nahin tha. ferguson ne haal hi main david hurst, mat lee tisir or bryan dean ke liye boli lagaakar yah pahchaan liya tha ki unki team ko ek striker kii jarurat thi or unhone apane chairman ko yah pataa lagane kaa nirdesh diya tha ki cantona bikri ke liye uplabdh tha yaa nahin. kuch dinon ke andar, yah sauda puraa kar liya gaya tha.
 cantona ne manchester united ke liye apni pehali upasthiti yusibio ke 50wein janmdin kii yaad main lisbon main benfika ke khilaaf yojit ek dostana match main darj karai. unhone apni pratispardhaatmak shuruyaat 12 disambar 1992 ko old trevord main manchester city ke khilaaf duusare haaf main utre ek sthanapann khilaadi ke rup main kii. united 2-1 se vijayi rahaa, haalanki cantona ne us din apana thodaa prabhaav bana liya.
 cantona ko sin karne ta united kaa sijan niraashajanak rahaa tha. baari kharch vaale eston vila or blakebern rovers ke saamne norwich city or kupiaar sahit kai chaunkaane vaale pratidwandviyon ke saath pehle egge premier lig khitaab kii daud main kaaphi peeche rah gaye the. gol karnaa pichley satr ke aadhe samay tak unke liye ek samasya ban gayi thi - jab isaki keemat unhen lig khitaab ganvaa kar chukaani paddy.
 bryan mcclair or mark hughes form main nahin the or garmiyon ke mausam main sin kiye gaye dion dublin ne sijan kii shuruyaat se pehle hi pehle hi apane pair tudwa liye the, jiske kaaran unhen chah maichon ke liye baahar baithna padaa tha. haalanki, cantona ne jaldi hi team main apni jagah bana lee or naa keval swayan gol kiye balki anya khilaadiyon ke liye bhi gol karne ke maukey banaae. united main unka pehla gol 19 disambar 1992 ko stamford brij main chelsi ke khilaaf 1-1 se baraabar rahe match main aayaa tha or unka doosraa gol hilsboro main shefield vednesade ke khilaaf mukkebaaji diwas par yojit ek romaanchak mukaable main aayaa, jahaan unhone madhyantar main 3-0 se pichhadane ke baad ek ank hasil kiya.
 9 janvari 1993 ko totenham hotspur ke khilaaf vah vishesh mauqaa tha, jab cantona ne 4-1 kii jeet main ek gol swayan kar or anya golon ke liye maukey banaakar, vastav main apane shandaar khel kaa parichay diya. haalanki, vivaad bhi unase kabhi dur nahin rahe or kuch hi hafton ke baad leeds ke saath khilne ke liye eland road waapas aane par, unhone ek prasanshak par thook diya or tab egge dwaara unpar 1,000 pound kaa jurmaana lagaaya gaya.
 old trevord main cantona ke pehle do sijan main, united ek adbhut daur se gujara, jab isane sijan ke shandaar uttararddh ke baad - jisamen cantona kii bahut badi bhoomika thi - 1967 ke baad pehali baar inhen england ke champion kaa taj pahane dikhaaya or uske baad 1993 main premier lig ke udghaatan ko 10 ankon se jeet liya.
 is khitaab ko jeetne se, cantona pehle - or yahan tak ki ekmaatr - khilaadi ban gaye jisane kabhi bhi alag-alag klbon ke saath ek-ke-baad-ek english sheersh shrenee ke khitaabon ko jeeta tha.
 unhone premier lig ko banaae rakhaa or cantona kii do panaltiyon ne unhen egge cap ke final main chelsi par 4-0 kii jeet dilaane main madad kii. unhone footbal lig cap main ek upavijeta kaa padak bhi hasil kiya, jisamen united keval eston vila se 3-1 kii haar ke baad final main pahunch gaya. us sijan ke liye unhen varsh kaa sarvshereshth piene khilaadi bhi chunaa gaya. haalanki, yah sijan bhi vivaad ke palon se achoota nahin rahaa jab galatasarai ke haathon champiens lig se baahar kaa rasta dikhaae jane ke baad referi ke saath bahes karne par cantona ko aakhir main waapas bhej diya gaya or jab lagaataar honevaale premier lig khelon se unhen nikaal diya gaya. lagaataar do laal card dikhaae jane ke baad cantona ko paanch maichon ke liye pratibandhit kar diya gaya, jisamen oldham athlettic ke saath egge cap kii vah semifinal bhidant bhi shaamil thi, jise united dwaara 1-1 kii barabari par rokane ke baad dubaara karaae gaye mukaable main jab cantona uplabdh the or unhone unhen 4-1 kii jeet dilaane main madad kii. 
 1993-94 premier lig main vah pehla sijan tha jab team ke khilaadiyon ko number die gaye. cantona ko number 7 kii shart mili, jo ek aisa team number tha jise unhone united ke saath apane shesh karier paryant apane paas rakhaa. 
 agle sijan main, cantona ne anaape prabhaavshaalee pradarshan ko barkaraar rakhaa jiske kaaran united kii nazaren lagaataar teesraa lig khitaab jeetne par tik gayi thi, lekin 25 janvari 1995 ko ve ek aisi ghatana main shaamil paae gaye jisane duniya bhar kii surkhiyaan batorin or vivaadon ko janm diya. crystal pales ke khilaaf ek dur ke match main, pales kii raksha pankti ke khilaadi richard shaw dwaara unki shart khinch lene ke baad use ek pratihinsak kik maarane ke kaaran referi ne cantona ko baahar bhej diya. chunki ve surang kii ore ja rahe the, unhone crystal pales ke prasanshak mathew simons par nishaanaa sadhakar, ek "kung-phu" style kii kik bheed kii ore maari or iske baad kai ghunse bhi chalaa die. simons ke saath lage cantona ke pair kii us pal kii kukhyat tasviir ko anumati lekar, aish ke single "kung phu" ke mukhya prishth par istemaal kiya gaya. keval mukhya prishth ne british rock press main khaasi lokapriyata hasil kii, jisane band ko ek hit ekal praapt karne main madad kii jab usee varsh kii talika main use 57 number kaa sthaan mila.
 baad main simons ne dhamkee bhari bhashaa or aacharan apnaane kii koshish kii thi. use saat din kii jail kii sajaa mili thi, lekin phir agle din hi use chhod diya gaya. yah bhi pataa chalaa tha ki simons par pichley aapraadhik maamle the, jinamein 1992 main ek hinsak dakaiti kii koshish kaa maamla shaamil tha, jab usne croydon main srilanka ke ek petrol pump karmchaaree par spanner se hamalaa kiya tha or yah bhi ki sellherst park kii ghatana ke kuch hi samay pehle usne ek nashnal front raily main hissaa liya tha. uske apradhi sabit hone or sajaa ke rup main 500 pound ke jurmaane ke saath-saath england or wales ke sabhi footbal maidaanon se pratibandh bhi lagaaya gaya tha.
 baad main bulaaye gaye ek patrkaar sammelan main cantona ne jo kaha, vah sambhawatah unka sarvaadhik prasiddh kathan he. shaayad yah sandarbh dete hue ki patrkaar kis prakaar unke aacharan par nazar rakhte the, cantona ne dhime or sanyat swar main kaha: "seagal trollar kaa peechha isiilie karte hai, kyonki unhen lagata he ki sardinon ko samudr main daal diya jayega. aapako bahut bahut dhanyavad." phir ve apni seat se uthe or ikattha hui bheed main se kai logon ko hatprabh chhodkar vahaan se nikal gaye. hamale ke liye do saptaah ke kaid kii sajaa ke baad apil kii adaalat ne unhen iske badale 120 ghanton kii saamudayik seva karne kii sajaa sunai.
 footbal associationon kii ichchhaaon ke anuroop, manchester united ne cantona ko 1994-95 sijan ke baaki bache chaar maheenon ke liye nilambit kar diya, jiske kaaran unhen pehle team ection se baahar rahanaa padaa jabaki united abhi tak duusare dabl kii aas main baitha tha. unhen 20,000 pound kaa jurmaana bhi kiya gaya tha.
 phir footbal association ne is pratibandh ko badhaakar aath maheene kar diya or unpar 10,000 pound kaa atirikt jurmaana bhi laga diya. egge ke mukhya kaaryakari graham keli ne unke hamale kaa varnan is prakaar kiya "hamaare khel par ek dhabba" jisane footbal ko sharminda kiya he. iske baad fifa ne is nilamban ke duniya bhar main lagu hone kii pushti kii, jiskaa matlab he ki cantona kisi duusare videshi club main sthanantarit hokar is pratibandh se bachakar naa nikal paae. manchester united ne bhi cantona par do hafton ke paarishramik kaa jurmaana laga diya or unhen francisi captaini se bhi haath dhonaa padaa; antat unke club ne blakebern ke haathon premier lig kaa khitaab ganvaa diya. 2007 main unhone kaha, "mere paas bahut si acchhee yaaden he, lekin main jis ek ko pasand karata hoon vah he jab maine us badmaash ko laat maari."
 takreeban kung phu kii ghatana ke din se hi, media main is tarah kii anginat atakalein lagaai ja rahi thim ki pratibandh ke samaapt hote hi cantona english footbal ko chhod denge, lekin italian club internejonelle se judane kii unki ichha ke baavjood elex ferguson ne unhen manchester main hi rahane ke liye manaa liya or tab cantona ek baar phir se preranadayak ban gaye.
 apane nae anubandh par hastakshar karne ke baad bhi, cantona apane pratibandh kii sharton ke kaaran niraash the jisane unhen dostana muqablon main bhi khilne se vanchit kar diya tha or 8 agast ko unhone apane anubandh ko samaapt karne kaa ek nivedan saunp diya kyonki ve ab england main footbal nahin khelnaa chaahate the. unke anurodh ko thukraa diya gaya or do din baad paris main elex ferguson ke saath ek baithak main, unhone ghoshnaa kii ki ve club main hi bane rahenge.
 united ne sijan kii shuruyaat main hi kai pramukh khilaadiyon ko bech diya tha or unki jagah club kii yuva team ke khilaadiyon ko shaamil kar liya tha or sijan ke pehle hi din eston vila se 3-1 kii haar ke baad lig ko jeetne kii unki sambhaavnaaen acchhee nahin lag rahi thi.
 1 oktober 1995 ko liverpool ke khilaaf cantona kii vaapasi vaale match ko lekar kaaphi prachaar ho rahaa tha - us samay tak united pehle din kii haar se ubarkar lig main duusare sthaan par aa gayi thi. kai vyaktiyon dwaara yah ashanka bhi jaahir kii ja rahi thi ki ve phir kabhi english footbal team main apni jagah nahin bana paaenge, kyonki vipakshi team ke khilaadiyon or visheshkar unke samarthakon kii yantranaaen or tippaniyan unke liye asahneeya sabit ho sakati hai.
 apni vaapasi ke khel main, cantona ne khel ke duusare hi minute main niki bat ke liye ek gol banaaya or uske baad rayaan gibs ke palati maar dene ke baad ek penalty bhi praapt kar liya. aath maheene tak bagaiyr kisi pratispardhi footbal mukaable ke rahane par nihsandeh iskaa asar hua or cantona apane christmas ke pehle ke form ko paane ke liye sangharsh karte rahe or unke evam agrani team newcastle united ke bich kaa faaslaa disambar 24 tak badhkar 10 ankon tak pahunch gaya.
 haalanki, phir sthitiyaan badal gayin, jab janvari ke madhya main aptan park main west ham united se saath united kii lig bhidant main cantona dwaara kiye gaye ek gol ne lig main 10-match kii shandaar jeet dilaa di. sijan ke uttarardh main, united kii 1-0 se jeet vaale kai anya maichon main cantona ne ekmaatr gol kiya, haalanki vastav main 9 march ko queens park rangers ke saath yah ek barabari kaa muqabla tha jiske baad united ne gol ke antar se newcastle par apni antim badhat bana lee thi. sijan ke baaki samay tak ve vahiin bane rahe or khitaab tak pahunchne ke kisi bhi chhit-put andeh ko khatm karte hue sijan ke aakiri din riverside stadium main midilesbro ko 3-0 ko maat dekar united ne chaar sijanon main apana teesraa khitaab hasil kar liya.
 sanyogvash, yah ek 1-0 kaa score line tha or gol karne vaala khilaadi bhi vahi tha, jab liverpul ke khilaaf us varsh ke egge cap ke final mukaable main, cantona british dwipon ke baahar ke pehle aise khilaadi ban gaye jisane ek captain ke rup main egge cap ko hasil kiya tha. us match kaa aakraamak pal 5 minute shesh rahete aayaa or sambhawatah yah cantona ke karier kaa sabase prasiddh gol tha. dayee ore se liye gaye ek corner ne liverpool ke rakshak david james ko pareshaan kiya jisane gend mukka maar diya. gend cantona kii ore aayi thi, jinhone corner maarthe samay gend kaa peechha kiya tha, phir unhone gend ko uchhaalate hue vijayi gol daag diya. match ke baad ek saakshaatkaar main cantona ne kaha: "aap jaanate hai ki yahi jindagi he. upar or neeche." manchester united do baar "dubles" jeetne vaali pehali team ban gai thi.
 steve bruce ke birmingham city chale jane ke baad cantona ko 1996-97 ke sijan ke liye united kaa captain hone kii pushti kar di gayi thi.
 cantona ne rayaan gigs kii pratibhaaon or david bekaham, paul scoles, niki bat or gairi nevilly jaise yuva khilaadiyon kii pratibha ko apni chhatrachhaaya main ubhaarte hue badi safaltaaon ke saath united kii team main ek nayaa josh bhar diya. jaise ki united ne 1996-97 ke sijan main lig ko barkaraar rakhaa tha, cantona ne united ke saath paanch varshon main chaar lig khitaab jite the, jisamen 1994-95 kaa sijan ek apwaad tha jiske uttararddh main lagaataar nilamban ke kaaran unhen khel se vanchit rahanaa padaa tha.
 unke str ke anusaar ek vaastavik rup se fike sijan kii samaapti par, jisamen yuiede champiens lig ke semi-final main united ko borucia dortmund ke haathon baahar nikaal diya gaya, unhone ascharyajanak rup se yah ghoshnaa kii ki ve 30 varsh kii umr se footbal se ritair ho rahe hai or united ke prasanshkon se gahari niraashaa ke saath mili. unka aakiri pratispardhaatmak bhidnt 11 may 1997 ko west ham ke khilaaf hui or ritair hone se pehle kii aakiri upasthiti paanch din baad 16 may ko hifield road main coventry city ke khilaaf david bast ke liye shradhanjali ke rup main darj hui, jisamen cantona ne 2-2 kii barabari par rahe match main donon gol kiye. cantona ne kul milakar 64 lig gol manchester united ke liye, 11 gharelu cap pratiyogitaaon main or 5 gol champions lig main kiye, jisase unka kul aankada 5 varshon se bhi kam samay main 80 par pahunch gaya.
 1999 kii apni aatmakatha managing maai life main elex ferguson ne yah daawa kiya ki cantona ne united ke european se baahar hone ke 24 ghanton ke andar hi apane ritair hone ke faisle ke bare main unhen suchit kar diya tha, haalanki is faisle ko nishchay hi takreeban ek maheene ke baad tak saarvajanik nahin kiya gaya tha. us samay ke dauraan, united main unke bhavishya ke bare main atakalein lagaai ja rahi thi, jisamen spain ke 0}real zaragoza jane kii baaten bhi shaamil hai.
 2003 main premier lig ke das sijan ke puraskaar samaaroh main dashak ke sarvshereshth videshi khilaadi kaa inam lene ke liye britain aane par, cantona ne premier se apni sevanivruti par kaha, "aap footbal ko kab chhod rahe hai yah bataanaa aasaan nahin he, kyonki isse aapaki jindagi mushkil main pad sakati he. mujhe yah jaananaa chahie kyonki kabhi-kabhi mujhe lagata he maine bahut choti umr main chhod diya. mujhe apane khel se pyaar tha lekin mere paas ab jaldi bistar par chale jane kaa, doston ke saath baahar nahin jane kaa, sharaab nahin pine kaa or bahut si aisi chijen nahin karne kaa junun nahin tha, jise main apni jindagi main karnaa pasand karata tha."
 2004 main cantona ne yah kehete hue tippanee kii, "mujhe itnaa adhik garv he ki prashansak abhi bhi mere naam ke geet gaate hai, lekin majhe yah dar lagata he kal ve ise gaanaa band kar denge. mujhe iskaa dar he kyonki mujhe isse pyaar he. or har us cheez ko jise aap pyaar karte hai, aapako dar lagata he ki aap ise kho denge."
 2006 main da san akhbaar ne cantona ko yah kehete hue bataaya ki manchester united ne apni aatma kho di he or yah ki maujuuda khilaadi bhend kii jhund hai. old traverd idal ne maawaarik intertanners ke dinon ko unki tarah yaad kiya jab jorge best vahaan gaye the or yah ashanka jataai thi ki red davills ubaau or vyaavhaarik tiimon ko pareshaan kar unke ateet ke saath vishwasghaat kar rahe the. haalanki, iske vipareet, agast 2006 main 'united patrika' ke number 7 ke mudde par kiye gaye saakshaatkaar main unhone kaha ki ve manchester united main keval "number 1" ke rup main waapas aa sakate hai or ek aisi team taiyaar karenge jo kisi anya ke paas nahin ho or usee rup main khelenge jaisaa ve sochate hai ki footbal is tarah khelaa janaa chahie.
 cantona ne manchester united dwaara glager ke adhigrahan kaa virodh kiya tha or kaha tha ki jabatak ki glager kaa parivaar prabhaar main he, ve club main waapas nahin lautenge, yahan tak ki ek prabandhak ke rup main bhi nahin. yah kai united prashansakon ke liye ek niraashajanak khabar thi jinhone unhen varsh 2000 kii garmiyon main kiye gaye ek surveykshan main united ke agle prabandhak ke rup main apni pasand chunaa tha. is sthiti main, yah ummid kii gai thi ki prabandhak sar elex fergyusan 2002 main ritair ho jaaenge, lekin prabandhak ne baad main apana iraadaa badal diya or ek dashak ke kareeb hone par bhi abhi tak apane prabhaar par bane hue hai.
 haalanki, july 2008 main sande express dwaara yah soochna di gayi thi ki cantona punarvichaar kar rahe hai, cantona ke ek kareebi dost ne unke bare main yah kehete hue khulaasa kiya: "erik manchester united jaise kisi club main koching kar baahar se madad pahunchaane kaa iraadaa rakhte hai... ve swayan filmon main aakar or unka nirdesan kar or samudr tateey footbal main shaamil hokar bharpoor anand le rahe hai lekin hameshaa apni style kii ek team taiyaar main madad karnaa chaahate hai or yah jaanate hai ki sar elex fergyusan unhen protsaahan denge.
 unki is pratigya ke baavjood ki jabatak manchester united par glagers kaa niyantran rahegaa ve kabhi waapas nahin lautenge, aisa lagata he unhone apane us nazariye ko naram kar liya he.
 cantona ko apni purnatah antarrashtriya karier kii shuruyaat karne kaa mauqaa agast 1987 ko tatkaaleen rashtriya team ke prabandhak henry mishael dwaara pashchimi germany ke khilaaf diya gaya. sitambar 1988 main, rashtriya team se hataa die jane ke baad, cantona ne gusse main match ke baad ek tivi saakshaatkaar main mishael ko "bakawaas kaa pitara" kaha or tab unhen sabhi antarrashtriya maichon se anishchit kaal ke liye pratibandhit kar diya gaya. haalanki, iske kuch hi samay baad 1990 vishwa cap ke liye kwalifai karne main asafal rahane ke kaaran mishael ko barkhaast kar diya gaya.
 nae coach mishael platini the or unke pehle kaamon main se ek tha cantona ko waapas bulaanaa, jo unke pasandida khilaadi the. unhone yah daawa kiya ki cantona ko tabatak chunaa jaataa rahegaa jabatak ki vah pratispardhi sheersh-stareey footbal kheltaa rahegaa; platini ne cantona ke england main jaakar apana karier phir se shuru karne ke faisle kii pahal kii thi. france ne sweden main yojit 1992 european footbal championships ke liye kwalifai kar liya tha, lekin cantona or jin-piyare pepin kii aakraamak saajhedaari ke baavjood ek bhi match nahin jeet paayi. final ke baad platini ne istipha de diya or unki jagah jerard hollior ne lee.
 hollior ke adheen, france apane ghar main hi bulgaria se 2-1 se final muqabla haar jane ke baad america main yojit 1994 vishwa cap ke liye kwalifai karne main naakaam rahi, jabaki ek barabari kaa muqabla unhen yah mauqaa de saktaa tha. david ginola ne is mukaable main apana sthaan chhod diya tha jiske kaaran emil costadinove ke vijayi gol se bulgaria kii jeet hui. mukaable ke baad cantona kathith rup se ginola se naaraaz the. hollior ne istipha de diya or unki jagah am jacket ne padbhaar sambhaal liya.
 jacket ne euro 96 kii taiyaari ke liye rashtriya team kaa punarnirmaan shuru kar diya or cantona ko captain ke rup main niyukt kiya. cantona janvari 1995 main sellherst park kii ghatana tak captain bane rahe. is ghatana ke parinaam swaroop hue nilamban ne unhen antarrashtriya maichon main khilne se bhi roak diya.
 jis samay tak cantona kaa nilamban samaapt hua, unhone team ke ranneetikaar kii apni bhoomika duusare star khilaadi jinedin jidan ke haathon kho di thi, kyonki jacket ne team kaa punarotthana karte hue kuch yuva khilaadiyon ko shaamil kiya tha or ise jidan kii ichha ke anuroop taiyaar kiya tha. cantona, pepin or ginola apni jagah kho chuke the or unhen phir kabhi francisi team ke liye shaamil nahin kiya gaya tha or is prakaar ve euro 96 se bhi vanchit rahe. haalanki cantona ko hataae jane ko lekar aalochana kii gayi thi, kyonki ve premier lig main shandaar khel kaa pradarshan kar rahe the, jacket ne swayan kaha tha kii team ne cantona ke bagaiyr acha khel dikhaaya tha or ve un khilaadiyon par bharosa karnaa chaahate the jo team ko yahan tak lekar aaye the. yah faisla sahe sabit hua tha jab les blais ne baad main 1998 main vishwa cap jeet liya.
 is din ke liye, cantona abhi tak apni rashtriya team ke pramukh padon par maujuud logon ke liye naaraazgi rakhte hai lekin unke dwaara apanaae gaye footbal khel ke desh kii prasansa bhi karte hai; euro 2004 or 2006 ke fifa vishwa cap main, unhone england kaa samarthan kiya, naa ki france kaa. 
 1998 main, apane shatabdi sijan ke samarohon ke ek hisse ke rup main, footbal lig ne cantona ko apane 100 lig lijends kii suchi main shaamil kiya. angreji lig main cantona kii uplabdhiyon ko iske baad varsh 2002 main chihnit kiya gaya jab unhen angreji footbal haul of fem kaa udghaatan pravartak banaaya gaya tha.
 cantona kaa anuvarthi karier zyaadaatar francisi cinema main he, praathmik rup se ek abhinayta ke rup main, haalanki unhone 2002 main ek laghu film eporte-moi ton amor kaa nirdesan bhi kiya tha; france ke baahar, unhen 1998 main sitare kalakaar ket blanchet dwaara abhinit film elizabeth main ek francisi rajdoot kii bhoomika mili thi. unhone ek swatantra british film jaik seyaj main ek rahasyamay baar-room darshanik ke rup main mehmaan kalaakaar kii bhoomika nibhaaye thi, jise sitambar 2008 main dividi par relies kiya gaya. unhone french film main nirdeshak thiyari grimandi ke rup main sah-kalakaar or ken loech kii palme deor namit film looking for erik main mukhya kalakaar or sah-nirmaataa kii bhoomika nibhaai - donon ko 2009 main relies kiya gaya.
 peshewar footbal se ritair hone ke baad se cantona kai europea televijan vigyapanon main dikhaai die, visheshkar nike ke liye. cantona ne do vigyapanon main chemiyo banaaya, ek main brazil kii rashtriya team ko ek hawaii adde par footbal khelte hue dikhaaya gaya he, to duusare main brazil or portugal donon deshon kii rashtriya timen shaamil hai. 2002 fifa vishwa cap ke ran-ap ke dauraan ek vishwa stareey vigyaapan abhiyaan main, unhone thiyare henry, hidetoshi nakaata, francesco toti, ronaldo, roberto carlos or louis figo jaise khilaadiyon ke bich "bhumigat" khelon " ke rup main banaaya gaya brands) ke vyavasthaapak kii bhoomika nibhaai. isse pehle euke nike ke vigyaapan main, ve haikani marches par ian rhit, steve mecmanaman or rubi foular sahit anya sitaron ke saath "shaukia" footbal khelte hue dikhaai die. 2006 fifa vishwa cap ke pehle nike ke ek vigyaapan abhiyaan main, cantona footbal se abhinay or nakli khel ko hataane kii koshish karne vaale ek sanghathan joga bonito ke mukhya pravaktaa ke rup main dikhaai die. unhone ek irish euro milliens vigyaapan main bhi abhinay kiya. 2009 main, unhen reno laguna ke ek nae model ke liye ek british televijan vigyaapan main dikhaaya gaya.
 2007 main, unhone francisi rock band dionisos dwaara nirmit laa maikanik du koyor elbum main ek spoken-verde kii bhoomika nibhaai.
 manchester united se apni vidaai ke fouran baad, cantona francisi rashtriya bich footbal team ke captain ban gaye. cantona ne dakshin asia or braiton city main, 2002 main kronenberg bich soker ke udghaatan main samudr tateey footbal khelon main nirantar apni ruchi dikhaai. unhone us francisi team kaa prabandhan kiya jisane 2005 main rio di generio main yojit shuruaati fifa bich soker vishwa cap ko jeeta tha. ve 2006 fifa bich soker vishwa cap francisi rashtriya team ke coach bhi bane, jo tisare sthaan par rahi. 2007 ke vishwa cap main cantona ek baar phir france ko chauthe sthaan tak laane main safal rahe. yah cap pehali baar 2008 vishwa cap main france aayaa, haalanki quarter final main italy se haar jane ke baad cantona sheersh chaar main apana sthaan banane main naakaam rahe.
 
 cantona kii shaadi isabel pherar se hui thi, unke do bachche hai, rafel or josephin . unhone ab abhinetri rashida brachni se shaadi kar lee he.
 cantona ke bhai, joel bhi ek peshewar footbal khilaadi the, jisane olympika di marsile, ujpesti tii or stockport county ke liye khelaa tha. cantona kii tarah, joel bhi footbal se ritair ho gaye hai or ab ek abhinayta hai.
 unke chachere bhai, saacha opinel vartmaan main dakshini lig premier divijan main farnboro epa.si. ke liye khelte hai.
