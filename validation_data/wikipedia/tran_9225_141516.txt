 mahamaya mandir chhattisgarh rajya ke bilaspur jile main ratanpur nagar main sthit he. yah smarak chhattisgarh rajya dwaara sangrakshit he. yah mandir 51 shaktipeethon main se ek he.
 yah mandir lagbhag 12veen shatabdi main nirmit mana jaataa he. mandir ke andar mahamaya mata kaa mandir he. vaise to salbhar yahaan bhakton kaa tantaa laga rahata he lekin maa mahamaaya devi mandir ke liye navaratri main mukhya utsav, vishesh puja-archana evam devi ke abhishek kaa aayojan kiya jaataa he.
