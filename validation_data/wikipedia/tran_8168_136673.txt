 ifthar  shaam kaa khaanaa he, jab musalmaan suryaast par apana dainik ramajaan upwas samaapt karte hai.
 shaam kii prarthana karne ke liye musalamaanon ne apane upwas ko puraa karke tod dena.
 iftaar ramajaan ke dharmik anushthanon main se ek he or aksar ek samudaay ke rup main kiya jaataa he, logon ko ek saath apane upwas ko todane ke liye ikattha kiya jaataa he. iftaar ko magarib samay ke baad liya jaataa he, jo suryaast ke aasapaas he. paramparaagat rup se lekin anivaary nahin he, islamic paigambar mohammad ke anukaran main teen khajur upwas todane ke liye khaaye the, is tareeke se apana upwas tod diya. kai musalmaan maanate hai ki iftaar main kisi ko khaanaa khilaa dena bahut hi neki mana jaataa he. mohammad sahab ne aisa kiya tha.
 bharat main lagbhag har muslim iftaar ke vaqt bajane vaale sairan yaa azaan ke vaqt iftaar kaa aanandit ho jaataa he. isse pehle, gharon main or sadak ke kinare ke stall par iftaar kii taiyaari ke saamaan kharid letaa he, khajur or pani kaa intizaam bhi kar letaa he. 
 hyderabad jaisi jagahon par, log haleem ke saath apana upwas todate hai kyonki yah kaaphi swaadisht hota he. 
 dakshini rajya jaise tamilnadu or kerala main, musalamaanon nombu kanji ke saath apane roza todate hai, yah padaarth bhi kaaphi swaadisht rahata he. 
 uttari dilli, uttar pradesh, madhya pradesh or pashchim bengal jaise uttari rajyon main, musalmaan parivaar or doston ke saath apane upwas todate hai, saath hi zyaadaatar masjidon ne 'iftaar' ko mukt karne kaa bhi aayojan kiya he. aamtaur par roze ko khajur saath kholaa jaataa he, taaje fal or falon ke ras, samose aadi jaise tale hue vyanjanon ke saath kholaa jaataa he. 'iftaar' kaa failaav shaakaahaari se gaiyr-shaakaahaari vyanjan or vibhinn prakaar ke ras or sharbat bhi istemaal kiye jate hai.
