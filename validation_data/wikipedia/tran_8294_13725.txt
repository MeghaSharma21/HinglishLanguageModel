 nauhid scrusi ek inglish model, video jocky or abhinetri hai. unke naam kaa arth shukr he.
 nauhid kaa janm 19 oktober 1982 ko belafast main ek parsi parivaar main hua. unki shiksha je bi vaachaa skool se armbh hui or unhone snaatak jai hind college evam basant singh vigyaan santhaan, carch get, mumbai se vaanijya sankaay main snatakottar kii degree praapt kii. 
 nauhid kii mul ichha vimaan paricharika banane kii thi lekin teen varsh kii modelling ke pashchaat unhone ek abhinetri banane kaa nirnay kar liya.
 skool ke samay unhone apni ek dost ke saath keval 14 varsh kii aayu main dhara rifind tel ke vigyaapan main kaam kiya. tatpashchaat unhone had & sholder, britania little harts biscuit, aryuvedic avadharana vigyaapan main najar aayi.
 nauhid ne apana kariyar ek model ke rup main armbh kiya.
 filmon main unhone padam kumaar kii udai chopra or rahul dev abhinit film supaari main padaarpan kiya. chunki film asafal rahi, nirdeshak ne unhen inteha film main asmith patel ke saamne kaary diya.
