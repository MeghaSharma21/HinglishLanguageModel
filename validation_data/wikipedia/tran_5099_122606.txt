 baramasa mulat: virah pradhan loksangit he. vah padya yaa geet jisamen baarah maheenon kii prakritik visheshtaon kaa varnan kisi virahi yaa virahani ke munh se karaayaa gaya ho. 
 varsh bhar ke baarah maas main nayak-nayika kii shringaarik virah evam milan kii kriyaaon ke chithran ko baramasa naam se sambodhit kiya jaataa he. shravan maas main hare-bhare vaataavaran main nayak-nayika ke kaam-bhaavon ko varsha ke bheengte hue rupon main, greeshm ke baishakh evam jyeshtha maas kii garmi main pankhons se nayika ko hawa karte hue nayak-nayikaon ke swarup aadi ullekhneey he.
 jab kisi stri kaa pati pardesh chalaa jaataa he or vah dukhi man se apane sakhi ko baarah maheene kii charchaa karti hui kahati he ki pati ke bina har mausam vyarth he. is geet main vah apni dasha ko har maheene kii visheshta ke saath pirokar rakhti he.
 hindi main malik mohammad jaaysi krit padmavat main 'naagmati viyog-varnan' baramasa kaa prasiddh udhaaharan he.
