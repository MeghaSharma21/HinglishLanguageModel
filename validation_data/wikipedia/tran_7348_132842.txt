 mongol bhashaayen purvi asia or madhya asia main boli jane vaali bhashaaon kaa ek bhashaa-parivaar he. isaki sab se numaya bhashaa mongol bhashaa he, jo mongolia or china ke bhitari mongolia praant ke mongol samudaay ki pramukh bhashaa he or jise lagbhag 52 lakh log bolte hai. kuch bhashavaigyanikon ke hisaab se mongol bhashaayen altaai bhashaa-parivaar kii ek upashakha he. mongol ke alaava mongol bhashaa-parivaar main kai anya bhashaayen bhi aati hai, jaise ki rus ke siberia kshetra main boli jane vaali buryaat bhashaa, china ke ching hai praant main boli jane vaali kangajia bhashaa or china ke hi shinjiyang praant main boli jane vaali dongshiang bhashaa.
