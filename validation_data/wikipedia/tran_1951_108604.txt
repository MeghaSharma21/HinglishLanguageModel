 acharya bhootbali pehali shatabdi ke ek digambar jain acharya the. acharya bhootbali ne pavitra jain granth, shatkhandgam kii rachana purna kii thi.
 acharya pushpadanth ne “visadi sutron” kii rachana kii thi. apni alp aayu shesh jaanakar unhone apane shishy palit ko acharya bhootbali ke paas bheja.acharya bhootbali ne phir siddhanth granth shatkhandgam kii rachana purna kari. jis din granth purna hua, vah din shrut panchami ke naam se prasiddh hua. aaj bhi yah din jain bandhuon dwaara parv rup main manaaya jaataa he.
