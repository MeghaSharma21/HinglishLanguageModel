 gangnum jila dakshin korea kii prashasnic ikai - gu jinase milakar tseol shahar bana he, main se ek he. gangnum kaa shabdic arth he " nadi ke dakshin main". varsh 2010 kii janaganana ke anusaar, gangnum jile kii kul jansankhya 527,641 he, jo ise tseol kaa chauthaa sarvaadhik jansankhya vaala shahar banaati he. 39.5 ki.me2 ke kshetrafal se yah tseol kaa tritiy bada jila he.
