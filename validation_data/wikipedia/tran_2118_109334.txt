 
 arabil praant, jise arbel praant or havler praant bhi kaha jaataa he, iraq kaa ek praant he. dohuk praant or sulaymaniah praant ke saath yah praant bhi iraq ke swashasit kurdistan kshetra main aata he jahaan kurdi logon kii apni kurdistan kshetreey sarkaar chalati he. arabil praant kii uttar main turki ke saath or purvottar main iran ke saath antarrashtriya seemaa he.
 prantiy str par arabil praant main kurd log bahusankhyak hai. inke alaava praant main ashuri, arab or dakshini azeri bhashaa bolne vaale iraqi turkmen logon ke bhi samudaay hai.
 is praant main ek zila, makhmoor​, shaamil he jo pehle ninava praant kaa hissaa hua karata tha haalanki usamen kurdiyon kii bahusankhya he. is baat ko lekar kurdi samudaay main bahut rosh tha.
