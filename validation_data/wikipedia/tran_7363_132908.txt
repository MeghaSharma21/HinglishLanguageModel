 vidyut abhiyantriki vidyut or vidyutiya tarang, unke upyog or unase judi tamaam takneekee or vigyaan kaa adhyayan or kaary he. praayah isamen electronics bhi shaamil rahata he. isame mukhya rup se vidyut mashiinon kii kaary vidhi evam dizin; vidyut urja kaa utpaadan, sancharan, vitaran, upyog; power electranix; niyantran tantra; tathaa electranix kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he.
 ek alag vyavsaay ke rup main vaidyut abhiyantriki kaa pradurbhaav unnisvin shatabdi ke antim bhaag main hua jab vidyut shakti kaa vyavsaayik upyog hona armbh hua. aajkal vaidyut abhiyantriki ke anekon upakshetra ho gayi hai.
