 dev samskruti vishvavidhyalay haridwar main sthit he. is santhaan kii sthapana gayatri parivaar ke pitrupurush pandit shriram sharma acharya dwaara 11 april 2002 main kii gayi. 
 is santhaan main raashtra ke yuvaaon ko nikhaar-sanvaar kar shreshthatam naagarik, samarpit swayamsevak, prakhar rashtrabhakt evam vishay-visheshgy banane ke saath-saath mahamanav or dev maanav bananaa he, jisase manushya main devaty utre or dharti par swarg ke avataran kaa swapn saakaar ho sake. 
 samskruti par aadhaarit vigyaan kii dhaaraon - ganit, jyotish, ayurveda, mantra-vigyaan, yoga-vigyaan, manovigyaan aadi se sambandhit paathyakramon kaa adhyaapan evam inke samasamyik binduon par vaigyanik pariprekshya main shodh. gyaan kii dhaaraon-dharm, darshan, samskruti aadi par aadhaarit paathyakramon kaa shikshan evam inke samasamyik binduon par shodh.
 dev samskruti vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana kaa vichaar vedamurti taponishth pan shriram acharya evam shakti swaroopa param vandaniya mata bhagawatidevi sharma ko bhav-samadhi main chaar dashak purv hua. samadhi tuutane par unhone kaha-‘dharti par jab bhi nayaa jivan ankurit hua, vah vishvavidhyalay ke rup main hua. jab bhi nae manushya gadhe gaye, unhen nai disha mili, aise vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana meraa bhi swapn he. mere swapn divaa nahin divya hote hai or ve kabhi adhure nahin rahete.
 atah dev samskruti vishvavidhyalay unke divya swapn kaa murtarup le chuki is yug kii ek abhinay sanstha he. is vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana uttaranchal vidhansabha se paarit ‘dev samskruti vishvavidhyalay vidheyak 2002’ ke antargat vedamata gayatri trust, shaantikunj, haridwar dwaara kii gai he. akhil vishwa gayatri parivaar ke pramukh evam brahmavarchas shodh santhaan ke nideshak, dow pranav pandya is vishvavidhyalay ke pratham kuladhipati hai.
 dev samskruti vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana kaa uddeshy raashtra ke yuvaaon ko nikhaar-sanvaar kar shreshthatam naagarik, samarpit swayamsevak, prakhar rashtrabhakt evam vishay-visheshgy banane ke saath-saath mahamanav or dev maanav bananaa he, jisase manushya main devaty utre or dharti par swarg ke avataran kaa swapn saakaar ho sake.
 1. dev-samskruti ke vikaas hetu athak prayaas.
 2. dev-samskruti par aadhaarit gyaan kii dhaaraon-dharm, darshan, samskruti aadi par aadhaarit paathyakramon kaa shikshan evam inke samasamyik binduon par shodh.
 3. dev-samskruti par aadhaarit vigyaan kii dhaaraon-ganit, jyotish, ayurveda, mantra-vigyaan, yoga-vigyaan, manovigyaan aadi se sambandhit paathyakramon kaa adhyaapan evam inke samasamyik binduon par vaigyanik pariprekshya main shodh.
 4. gurudev ke vichaaron par aadhaarit naveen saamaajik sanrachana ke liye aavashyak rojgarparak paathyakramon kaa shikshan, aapadaa prabandhan, gram prabandhan, vidhyalay prabandhan aadi par adhyayan evam shodh.
 dev samskruti vishvavidhyalay lagbhag 30 hajaar vargametre main nirmit, nirmaanadhin or prastaavit bhawanon kaa kshetra he. shikshan ke liye nirmit ‘takshashila’ bhawan teen manjila he, jisamen 30 bade haul hai. inamen 18 class room, 1 sabhaagaar, 1 samiti kaksh, 1 computer kaksh, 2 granthalay, 3 prayogshaalaayein, 1 bhandargrih evam 4 prashasnic kaksh hai. chatravaas, awaas, ayurveda-anusandhan, farmesi, prashasnic, swaavlamban, sabhaagaar, dhyaan kaksh aadi bhawanon ke sthaan surakshit hai, jo vishvavidhyalay ko divya swaroop pradaan karte hai.
 adhyapakon tathaa anya karmchaariyon hetu awaas kii purna vyavastha he. ek atithigrih ke saath-saath yajyashala kii vyavastha ho chuki he. khelkud tathaa vyaayaamashaala ke liye aavashyak suvidhaayen vidyaman hai. prastaavit bhawanon main bhavy pustkaalay evam chaatr-chaatraon ke liye chatravaas ke nirmaan kaa kaary aarambh ho chukaa he. takshashila bhawan ke saath hi mahakaal mandir kii sthapana bhi ho chuki he.
 dev samskruti vishvavidhyalay main shiksha, swasthya, sadhana, swaavlamban in chaaron sankayon kii alag-alag prayogshaalaayein sthapit kii ja rahi hai, jinamein aavashyak aadhunik upkarnon, saaj-saamaan tathaa gyaan-vigyaan ke shodh ke liye suvidhaayen uplabdh hongi, sabhi tark tathaa tathy pramaan sahit prastut kiye ja sakenge.
 is samay manovigyaan tathaa yoga kii aadhunik upkarnon se susajjit prayogshalas ban chuki hai. inke atirikt brahmavarchas kii shodh prayogshaalaayein bhi vidhyaarthiyon evam shodh chaatro ke liye uplabdh hai, jinamein aadhunik upkarnon, computer evam electronix ke vibhinn yantron ke dwaara tap, titiksha, aahaar, sadhana, dhyaan, dharna, yoga, kalp prakriya, praayshchit vidhaan aadi ke manovaigyanik prabhaavon kaa adhyayan kiya ja saktaa he. aushadhiy jadi-bootiyon kii sookshm shakti kaa sharir, man, vaataavaran, vanaspatiyon par prabhaav tathaa yagnopathy, mantrashakti, sangeet aadi ke prabhaav ke shodhaparak adhyayan kii suvidhaayen bhi uplabdh hai.
 vishvavidhyalay kaa shikshan evam shodh chaar sankayon-shiksha, swasthya, sadhana or swavlamban dwaara sanchalit hoga. praarambhik avasthaa main shiksha sankaay ke antargat keval manovigyaan tathaa yoga vishay main em.e./ em.es.si. tathaa yoga ke diploma or certificate ke paathyakram sanchalit kiye ja rahe hai. vishvavidhyalay ke bhashaa vibhaag dwaara angreji main diploma evam certificate ke course aarambh kiye ja chuke hai. shodh kshetra main pi.ech.di. hetu panjiyan kiye ja rahe hai. janvari 2003 se dharm vigyaan ke vishesh certificate paathyakram kaa bhi shubharambh ho chukaa he.
