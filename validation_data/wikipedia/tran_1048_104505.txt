 kisi bhi khagoleey pind ke vahya antariksh kaa vah bhaag jo us pind ke sataha se dikhaai detaa he, vahi akaash he. anek kaarnon se ise paribhaashit karnaa kathin he. din ke prakaash main prithvi kaa akaash gahare-nile rang ke sataha jaisaa prathit hota he jo hawa ke kanon dwaara prakaash ke prakirnan ke parinamswaroop ghatit hota he. jabaki raatri main hame dharti kaa akaash taron se bharaa hua kaale rang kaa sataha jaisaa jaan padataa he.
 aasmaan kaa rang uskaa apana nahin hota he. surya se aane vaala prakaash jab akaash main upasthit dhul ityadi se miltaa he to vah chhitarataa jaataa he. nila rang, apane apekshaakrit kam tarangadairghy ke kaaran, anya rangon kii apeksha adhik chhitarataa he. isliye akaash kaa rang nila dikhataa he.
 akaash ke anya naam he:
 pracheen bhartiya dharm granthon ke anusaar sristi kaa nirmaan paanch tatwon se hua mana jaataa he jinamein ki ek akaash he . yadi tulana kii jaae to bhartiya dharm-darshan main varnit akaash kii avadharana vartmaan vaigyanik gyaan or shabdaawalee ke ek ayam "sthaan" ke nikat prathit hota he. iskaa ek udhaaharan ashtavakra gita kaa nimna shloke hai- 
 ekan sarvagtan vyom bahirantaryatha ghate | nityam nirantaram brahm sarvabhutagane tathaa ||1- 20||
 tajjyasy punyapapabhyaan sparsho hyantarn jaayate | na hyakashasya dhoomen drishymanapi sangatih ||4- 3||
