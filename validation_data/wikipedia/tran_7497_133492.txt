 bharat main aadhunik banking sevaaon kaa itihaas do sau varsh purana he.
 bharat ke aadhunik banking kii shuruyaat british raaj main hui. 19veen shatabdi ke aarambh main british east india company ne 3 bainkon kii shuruyaat kii - bank of bengal 1809 main, bank of bombai 1840 main or bank of madras 1843 main. lekin baad main in teenon bainko kaa vilay ek naye bank 'imperial bank' main kar diya gaya jise san 1955 main 'bhartiya state bank' main vilay kar diya gaya. elahabaad bank bharat kaa pehla nijee bank tha. bhartiya rigerv bank san 1935 main sthapit kiya gaya tha or baad main punjab neshnal bank, bank of india, kenra bank or indian bank sthapit hue.
 prarambh main bainkon kii shakhayein or unka kaarobaar vanijyik kendron tak hi simit hoti thi. bank apni sevaayen keval vanijyik pratishthanon ko hi uplabdh karaate the. swatantrata se purv desh ke kendriya bank ke rup main bhartiya rigerv bank hi sakriy tha. jabaki sabase pramukh bank imperial bank of india tha. us samay bharat main teen tarah ke bank karyrat the - bhartiya anusuchit bank, gaiyr anusuchit bank or videshi anusuchit bank.
 swatantrata ke uparant bhartiya rigerv bank ko kendriya bank kaa darja barkaraar rakhaa gaya. use 'bainkon kaa bank' bhi ghoshit kiya gaya. sabhi prakaar kii maudrik nitiyon ko tay karne or use anya bainkon tathaa vittiya sansthaaon dwaara lagu karaane kaa daayitv bhi use saunpa gaya. is kaary main bhartiya rigerv bank kii niyantran tathaa niyaman shaktiyon kii mahatvapurn bhoomika rahi he.
 bhartiya rigerv bank kaa rashtriyakaran swatantrata ke uparant san 1949 main kiya gaya. iske kuch varshon ke uparant san 1955 i. main inmperial bank of india kaa bhi rashtriyakaran kiya gaya or uskaa naam badal karke bhartiya state bank rakhaa gaya. aage chalakar san 1959 i. main bhartiya state bank adhinium banaakar aath kshetreey bainkon kaa rashtriyakaran kiya gaya. vartmaan main ye aathon bank bhartiya state bank samooh ke bank kahe jate hai. in aathon bainkon ke naam - state bank of hyderabad, state bank of bikaner and jaipur, state bank of indaur, state bank of mysore, state bank of travankor ityadi hai. deshbhar main inaki lagbhag 15,000 shakhayein hai.
 desh ke pramukh chaudaa bainkon kaa rashtriyakaran 19 july san 1969 i. ko kiya gaya. ye sabhi vanijyik bank the. isi tarah 15 april san 1980 ko nijee kshetra ke chha: or bank rashtriyakrut kiye gayi. in sabhi bees bainkon kii shakhayein deshbhar main faili hai. vartmaan main kul 19 rashtriyakrut bank hai.
