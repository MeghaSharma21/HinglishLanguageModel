 aarthik sahyog or vikaas sanghathan, 35 sadsy deshon kii ek antarsarkari aarthik sanghathan he, jisaki sthapana 1960 main aarthik pragati or vishwa vyapaar ko protsaahit karne hetu kii gai thi. adhikansh oisidi sadsy ucch aay varg arthvyavasthaaen hai, jinaki maanav vikaas suchakank bahut ucch hai, or viksit deshon ke rup main mana jaataa he. oisidi ek aadhikarik sanyukt raashtra prekshak he.
 oisidi ke mukhyalay france ke paris shahar main hai. oisidi ko sadsy deshon se praapt yogadan dwaara vitt poshit kiya jaataa he, or 2015 main iskaa kul bajet 363 lakh euro kaa tha.
 iske aadhikarik sansthaapak sadsy hai:
 
 
 
 sanyukt rajya america
 
 oisidi apane kaaryon kaa nishpaadan parishad, kaaryakari samiti, sachivalay tathaa anek sahaayak angon ke madhyam se karata he.
 oisidi kaa pradhan ang parishad he, jisame sabhi sadsy deshon ke pratinidhi sammilit hote hai. isaki mantri str par kam-se-kam ek or sthaayee pratinidhi ke str par kam-se-kam do baithken aavashyak rup se yojit hoti hai. parishad sanghathan kii saamaanya niti kaa nirdhaaran karti he. sthaayee samiti parishad dwaara prayek varsh nirvaachit 14 sadasyon se bani hoti he. iskaa kaary oisidi kii gatividhiyon kaa paryvekshan karnaa he tathaa saptaah main saamanyatya isaki ek baithak hoti he. sachivalay kaa pradhan adhikari mahasachiv hota he, jo parishad or kaaryakari samiti ke nirnyon kii kriyanvit karne ke liye uttaradaayi hota he. iske atirikt, mahasachiv vaarshik or sahaayak bajaton ko bhi prastut karata he.
 parishad vibhinn kaaryon ke liye sahaayak angon ko gathit karne ke liye adhikrut hoti he. vikaas sahaayta samiti oisidi ke pramukh puunji-niryaatak deshon or europea sangh ke pratinidhiyon se bani hoti he tathaa sadasyon ke adhikarik sansadhano ke hastantaran ke liye jimmedaar hoti he. dissi kaa mukhya uddeshy sthaayee aarthik or saamaajik vikaas ke liye antarrashtriya weetiya poshan main samanvit, ekikrut or prabhaavshaalee prayaas ko protsaahan dena he. sadsy deshon ke varisht adhikariyon se bani aarthik niti samiti sadsy deshon kii aarthik gatividhiyon kii samiksha karti he. aarthik or vikaas samiksha samiti kisi sadsy desh vishesh kaa vaarshik surveykshan taiyaar karti he. vyapaar samiti vyavsaayik nitiyon or abhyaason tathaa vishisht vyapaar samasyaon se judi hoti he. antarrashtriya nivesh or bahurashtriya udhyam samiti ne bahurashtriya kapaniyon ke liye svaichhik aachaar samhita taiyaar kii, jise oisidi ne varsh 1976 main apanaaya.
 iske atirikt, krishi, upabhokta niti, shiksha, urja, paryaavaran, vittiya bazaar, rajkoshiya maamle, udyog, paroksh len-dein, maanavshakti or saamaajik maamle, samudri parivahan, pratibandhkavyaper abhyaason, vaigyanik or takneekee niti or paryaton ke kshetra main samitiyaan gathit kii gayi hai. vastuon, sakaaratmak samaayojan nitiyon or mahila rojgaar par ucch stareey samoohon tathaa uttar-dakshin aarthik sishayon ke liye kaaryakari samiti samooh karyrat hai.
 kai parichaalan abhikaran gathit kiye gaye hai, jaise-vishwa ke nirdhantam logon kii moulik aavashyaktaaon par kendrit oisidi vikaas centre; sadsy deshon kii shiksha pranali main sudhaar laane ke uddeshy se gathit shaikshanik anusandhan or navachaar centre ; shantipurna nabhikeey urja vikaas ke liye nabhikeey urja agency ; madhya evam purvi europea deshon kii prajatantr tathaa bazaronmukhi arthvyavastha viksit karne main sahaayta dene ke uddeshy se gathit europea arthvyavastha pargaman sahyog centre .
