 1992 main sanyukt raashtra sangh kaa paryaavaran evam vikaas ke mudde par kendrit ek sammelan brazil ke rio di genero main hua. is sammelan ko prithvi sammelan ke naam se janaa jaataa he. isamen 170 deshon ke pratinidhiyon, hajaaron swayamsevi sanghathanon or anek bahurashtriya nigamon ne bhaag liya. is sammelan main hi vishwa raajnneeti main paryaavaran ko ek thos swaroop mila. is avasar par agenda-21 paarit kiya gaya. sabhi rashtron se nivedan kiya gaya ki vah prakritik santulan ko banaae rakhen, paryaavaran ke pradooshan ko roke tathaa satat vikaas kaa rasta apanaaen.
