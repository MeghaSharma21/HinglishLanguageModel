 kaplan chikitsa kendra israel ke rihovot main sthit ek aspataal he jo is nagar ke dakshinn main bilu sangam par sthit he. varsh 2001 main is aspataal main 625 bistar the or may 2007 main yah israel kaa dasva sabase bada aspataal tha. yah aspataal rihovot or ashdaud ke madhya samooche shaifela kshetra main sevaayen pradaan karata he, or barzil aspataal ke saath milakar ashklon main seva pradaan karata he. isaki sthapana 1953 main hui thi or elieger kaplan ke naam par iskaa naam padaa jo israel ke pratham vittamantri the.
