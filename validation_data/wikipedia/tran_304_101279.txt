 krishi ke bhinn bhinn kaaryon main anekon yantron kaa upyog hota he. inhen krushiyantra kehete hai. krushiyantron kaa prayog kheton kii jutai, bowni, khaad or kitnashak daalane, sinchai karne, fasalon kii suraksha ke liye, fasal kataai, madhaai, dhulaai aadi ke liye kii jaati he. tractor ek pramukh krushiyantra he.
