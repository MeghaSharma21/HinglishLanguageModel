 moore kaa niyam deergh avadhi main sanganan ke hardware main vikaas kii gati kii ek bhavishyavaanee he. san 1958 main ekikrut paripath ke avishkaar ke baad se kisi isi par nirmit kiye ja sakane vaale transistoron kii sankhya badi teji se badi he. yah sankhya praaia: har do varsh main duguni hoti chali gayi he. intel ke sah-sansthaapak garden moore ne sabase pehle is trend kaa baariki se adhyayan karne ke baad san 1965 main ek shodhpatr main prakashit kiya tha. yadhyapi pehle usne kaha tha ki isi par nirmit hone vaale transistoron kii sankhya prati ek varsh main duguni hoti jaayegi kintu baad main ise sanshodhit karke unhone kaha ki yah sankhya prati do varsh main duguni hoti chali jaayegi.
 moore kii yah bhavishyavaanee lagbhag aadhi shatabdi tak satya sabit hui he or aisa vishwaas he ki kam se kam ek or dashak tak yah kram jari rahegaa.
