 noshak yaa naushak afghanistan kaa sabase unchaa or, tirich mir parvat ke baad, hindu kush parvat shrunkhlaa kaa doosraa sabase unchaa pahaad he. 7,492 meter unchaa yah parvat afghanistan kii pakistan ke saath sarhad ke paas sthit he. puri duniya kaa yah 52vaan sabase unchaa pahaad he or 7,000 meter se zyaada unche pahaado main yah sabase pashchimi sthaan par he. noshak parvat kii uttari or pashchimi dhalaane afghanistan main aati hai jabaki isaki dakshini or purvi dhalaane pakistan main hai. ise sabase pehla san 1960 main japan ke toshiyaki saakai or goro ivatsubo ne chadha tha.
