 ashqabad yaa ashgab madhya asia ke turkmenistan raashtra kii rajdhani or sabase badi nagari he. 2001 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 6,95,300 thi or 2009 main isaki anumaanit aabaadi 10 lakh thi. ashqabad karakum registan or copet daag parvat shrunkhlaa ke bich main sthit he. isamen rahane vaale zyaadaatar log turkmen jati ke hai haalaanki yahan roosi, armenian or azeriyon ke samudaay bhi rahete hai. is nagar kaa naam farsi ke 'ishq' or 'aabaad' shabdon ko milakar bana he or iskaa arth 'ishq kaa shahar' he.
 nagar kii sthapana 1881 main hui, or 1924 main turkmen soviet samaajawaadee ganraajya kii rajdhani ban gai. nagar kaa adhikansh bhaag 1948 ke ashqabad bukamp se nasht ho gaya, lekin uske baad se raashtrapati niazov kii nagareey navinikaran pariyojna ke tahat vyapak punarnirmaan prarambh hua. karakum nahar ko nagar ke madhya se banaaya gaya he, joamu dariyaa kaa jal purab se pashchim tak le jaati he.
 ashqabad apekshaakrit ek nayaa nagar he, jise 1881 main ek durg ke rup main sthapit kiya gaya tha or paas hi sthit "kasbaa askhabad" se yah naam liya gaya. parthian samrajya kii pracheen rajdhani nisa se kuch dur sthit, yah silk road shahar konjikala ke khandhiron par banaaya gaya, jise pehle 2 shatabdi isa purv main sharaab banane vaale gaanv ke rup main ullekh kiya gaya tha or 1 shatabdi isa purv aaye bukamp ke kaaran khandhar ho chukaa tha. konjikla ke silk road par apane laabhprad sthaan par sthit hone ke kaaran iskaa punarnirmaan kiya gaya or 13veen shatabdi main mongolon dwaara iskaa vinaash kiye jane se purv yah bahut phala-phula. uske baad yah ek chote se gaanv ke rup main batch rahaa iske baad roosiyon ne 19veen shatabdi main isamen kabja kar liya.
 jeeyope tepe kii ladaai tak faras kaa hissaa rahaa ashqabad, akhal sandhi kii sharton ke tahat roosi samrajya ko saunp diya gaya. shahar ke british-prabhavit faras kii seemaa ke kareeb hone ke kaaran rus ne is ilaake ko viksit kiya, or 1881 se 1897 main jansankhya 2,500 se badhkar 19,400 ho gai. 1908 main, ashqabad main pehali bahaai prarthanaghar banaaya gaya, 1948 ke bukamp main yah buree tarah se kshatigrast ho gaya or antat 1963 main ise dhwast kar diya gaya. turkmenistan main bahaai aasthaa ke samudaay ke kaaphi log ashqabad main rahete hai.
 ashqabad main soviet shashan disambar 1917 main sthapit hua. 1919 main, turkstan swytt soviet samaajawaadee ganraajya ke rashtriya arthvyavastha ke soviet sangh ke adhyaksh pavel poltaroraski ke naam par, shahar kaa naam badalakar poltoratsk rakh diya gaya. jab turkmen esasaaar 1924 main sthapit kiya gaya, poltaratsca ko uski rajdhani banaai gai. 1927 main mul naam punarsthapit kiya gaya.
 2012 turkmen janaganana ke anumaan ke mutabik, shahar kii aabaadi kaa 85% turkmen he. roosiyon kii 7.7% aabaadi he, uske baad armenian, turk, uzbek or azeriyon log aate hai.
 dakshin main lagbhag 25 kilometre par copet daag parvat shrinkhalaa he, or uttari seemaa par karakum registan he, is vajah se ashqabad main garm, shushk greeshmakaal or thande, choti sardiyon ke saath ek shushk registan jalwaayu he. july main ausat ucch taapmaana 38.3 degree celsius hota he. garmiyon main raat kaa samay praaia: garm hota hai, july main ausat nunatam taapmaana 23.8 degree celsius hota he. ausat janvari main ucch taapmaana 8.6 degree celsius hota he, or ausat nimna taapmaana -0.4 degree celsius hota he. ashgabat main sabase ucch taapmaana 47.2 degree celsius, june 2015 main darj kiya gaya tha. janvari 1969 main -24.1 degree celsius kaa sabase kam taapmaana darj kiya gaya tha. kshetra main kabhi-kabhaar himpat bhi dekhne ko miltaa he. vaarshik varsha keval 201 millimetre hoti he; joki adhiktar march or april ke maheenon main hoti he.
 yahan ke mukhya udyog sooti vastra or dhaatu kala hai. yah trans-caspian railway kaa ek mukhya stesan he. ashqabad main rojgaar kaa ek bada pratishat rajya sansthaanon dwaara pradaan kiya jaataa he; jaise ki mantralayon, adhomukhi, or turkmenistan sarkaar ke anya prashasnic nikaay apane videshi deshon ke duutavaason main rajanyik yaa clerk ke rup main kaam karne vaale kai videshi naagarik bhi hai. madhya asia or faras kii khadi ke bich maal ke parivahan ko suvidhajanak banane ke liye antarrashtriya parivahan or pargaman galiyare banane ke liye, bharat, oman, iran, turkmenistan, uzbekistan or kazakhstan ke bich hua samajhota, ashqabad ke naam par hi, "ashqabad samjhauta" rakhaa gaya he.
