 chandragiri durg bharat ke andhra pradesh rajya main tirupati ke nikat chandragiri naamak sthaan par sthit ek durg he. iskaa nirmaan 11veen shatabdi main shrikrishna devaraaya dwaara karavaaya gaya tha. yah kila teen sau varshon tak devaraaya rajvansh ke adhikaar main rahaa. 1367 main yah vijaynagar ke shaskaon ko haath main chalaa gaya. us samay chandragiri rajya ke chaar bade nagaron main se ek tha. 1646 main chandragiri golkunda ke adheen aa gaya or kaalaantar main mysore rajya kaa bhaag bana. vartmaan main raja-rani mahal bhawan main bhartiya puratvavibhag kaa timanjila sangrahalay he.
