 karnataka ke rashtriya evam rajya rajmargon kii kul lambaaiyaan kramshah 3,973 kilometre evam 9,829 kilometre hai. karnataka rajya sadak parivahan nigam rajya kaa sarkari lok yatayaat evam parivahan nigam he, jiske dwaara pratidin lagbhag 22 lakh yatriyon ko parivahan sulabh hota he. nigam main 25,000 karmchaaree sevarat hai. 1990 ke dashak ke antim daur main nigam ko teen nigamon main vibhajit kiya gaya tha, bangalore metropolitan transport corporation, north-west karnataka transport corporation evam north-east karnataka transport corporation. inke mukhyalay kramshah bangalore, hubli evam gulburg main sthit hai.
 karnataka rajya main vibhinn bahubhashayi or dharmik jati-prajatiyaan basii hui hai. inke lambey itihaas ne rajya kii saanskrithik dharohar main amuly yogadan diya he. kannadigon ke alaava, yahaan tuluv, kodev or konkani jatiyaan, bhi basii hui hai. yahaan anek alpsankhyak jaise tibeti buddha tathaa anek janajatiyaan jaise solig, yerava, toda or siddhi samudaay hai jo rajya main bhinn rang gholte hai. karnataka kii paramparaagat lok kalaon main sangeet, nrity, naatak, ghumkkad kathavachak aadi aate hai. malnad or tateey kshetra ke yakshagan, shastreey nrity-naatikaayen rajya kii pradhan rangamanch shaileyon main se ek hai. yahaan kii rangamanch parampara anek sakriy sanghathanon jaise ninasam, rangashankar, rangayan evam prabhaat kalavidru ke prayaason se jeevant he. in sanghathanon kii aadharshila yahaan gubbi viranna, tii fee kailashm, bi ve karanth, ke ve subanna, prasanna or kai anya dwaara rakhi gayi thi. virages, kamasel, kolat or dolukunita yahaan kii prachlit nrity sheliyaan hai. mysore style ke bharatnatya yahaan jatti taymmaa jaise paarangton ke prayaason se aaj shikhar par pahunchaa he or is kaaran hi karnataka, visheshkar bangalore bharatnatya ke liye pradhan kendron main gina jaataa he.
 karnataka kaa vishwastareey shastreey sangeet main vishisht sthaan he, jahaan sangeet kii karnataka or hindustani sheliyaan sthaan paati hai. rajya main donon hi shaileyon ke paarangat kalakaar hue hai. vaise karnataka sangeet main karnataka naam karnataka rajya vishesh kaa hi nahin, balki dakshin bhartiya shastreey sangeet ko diya gaya he.16veen shatabdi ke haridas andolan karnataka sangeet ke vikaas main abhinn yogadan diya he. sammanit haridaason main se ek, purandar das ko karnataka sangeet pitamah kii oopaadhi di gayi he. karnataka sangeet ke kai prasiddh kalakaar jaise gangubai hangal, mallikarjun mansoor, bhimsen joshi, basavaraj raajaguru, savai gandharva or kai anya karnataka rajya se hai or inamen se kuch ko kalidas sammaan, padam bhushan or padam vibhushan se bhi bharat sarkaar ne sammanit kiya hua he. 
 karnataka sangeet par aadhaarit ek anya shastreey sangeet style he, jiskaa prachlan karnataka rajya main he. kannada bhagwati style aadhunik kaviganon ke bhavatmak ras se prerit prasiddh sangeet style he. mysore chitrakala style ne anek shreshth chitrakaar diye hai, jinamein se sundaraiya, thanjavur kondvy, bi.venkatappa or keshavaiyya hai. raja ravi verma ke banaaye dharmik chitra puure bharat or vishwa main aaj bhi puja archana hetu prayog hote hai. mysore chitrakala kii shiksha hetu chitrakala parishat naamak sanghathan yahaan vishesh rup se karyrat he.
 karnataka main mahilaon kii paramparaagat bhusha sari he. kodagu kii mahilaayein ek vishesh prakaar se sari pahenti hai, jo shesh karnataka se kuch bhinn he. rajya ke purushon kaa paramparaagat pahanava dhoti he, jise yahaan paanche kehete hai. vaise shahari kshetron main log praayah kameez-patalun tathaa salwar-kameez pahana karte hai. rajya ke dakshini kshetra main vishesh style kii pagadi pahani jaati he, jise mysuri peta kehete hai or uttari kshetron main rajasthani style jaisi pagadi pahani jaati he or pagadi yaa pataga kehalaati he.
 chaaval or raagi rajya ke pradhan khaadya main aate hai or jolad rotti, soragham uttari karnataka ke pradhan khaadya hai. inke alaava tateey kshetron evam kodagu main apni vishisht khaadya style hoti he. bise bele bhaat, jolad rotti, raagi bada, upama, masala dosa or maddur vada karnataka ke kuch prasiddh khaadya padaarth hai. mishthann main mysore paak, belgavi kunda, gokk kardantu or dharwar peda mashhoor hai.
 aadi shankaracharya ne shrungery ko bharat paryant chaar peethon main se dakshin peeth hetu chunaa tha. vishisht advait ke agrani vyaakhyaata ramanujacharya ne malkot main kai varsh vyateet kiye the. ve karnataka main 1098 main aaye the or yahaan 1122 tak vaas kiya. inhone apana pratham vaas tondanur main kiya or phir malkot pahunche, jahaan inhone chelluvnarayan mandir or ek suvyavasthit math kii sthapana kii. inhen hoysaal vansh ke raja vishnuvardhan kaa sangrakshan mila tha. 12veen shatabdi main jatvaad or anya saamaajik kuprathaon ke virodh swaroop uttari karnataka main virshaivadharm kaa udai hua. in aandolan main agrani vyaktitvon main basav, akka mahadevi or alaam prabhu the, jinhone anubhav mandap kii sthapana kii jahaan shakti vishishtadvait kaa udai hua. yahi aage chalakar lingayat mat kaa aadhaar bana jiske aaj kai lakh anuyaayi hai. karnataka ke saanskrithik or dharmik dhaanche main jain sahitya or darshan kaa bhi mahatvapurn yogadan rahaa he.
 islam kaa aarambhik udai bharat ke pashchimi chor par 10veen shatabdi ke lagbhag hua tha. is dharm ko karnataka main bahamani samrajya or bijapur sultanate kaa sangrakshan mila. karnataka main isai dharm 16veen shatabdi main portugaliyon or 1545 main saint francis xavier ke aagaman ke saath failaa. rajya ke gulburg or banavaasi aadi sthanon main pratham sahasrabdi main buddha dharm kii jade panpin. gulburg jile main 1986 main hui akasmaat khoj main mili maury kaal ke avshesh or abhilekhon se gyaat hua ki krishna nadi kii tarai kshetra main buddha dharm ke mahayan or hinayan maton kaa khub prachaar hua tha.

 rajya kii aadhikarik bhashaa he kannada, jo sthaneey nivaasiyon main se 65% logon dwaara boli jaati he. kannada bhashaa ne karnataka rajya kii sthapana main ek mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai he, jab 1956 main rajyon ke srijan hetu bhashaayi sankhyikee mukhya maandand rahaa tha. rajya kii anya bhashaaon main konkani evam kodev tak hai, jinka rajya main lanbaa itihaas rahaa he. yahaan kii muslim jansankhya dwaara urdu bhi boli jaati he. anya bhashaaon se apekshaakrit kam boli jane vaali bhashaaon main beyare bhashaa va kuch anya boliyaan jaise sanketi bhashaa aati hai. kannada bhashaa kaa pracheen evam prachur sahitya he, jiske vishyon main kaaphi bhinnata he or jain dharm, vachan, haridas sahitya evam aadhunik kannada sahitya he. ashok ke samay kii rajagyaaon va abhilekhon se gyaat hota he ki kannada lipi evam sahitya par buddha sahitya kaa bhi prabhaav rahaa he. halmidi shilaalekh 450 i. main mili kannada bhashaa ke pracheenatam uplabdh abhilekh hai, jinamein acchhee lambaai kaa lekhan miltaa he. pracheenatam uplabdh sahitya main 850 i. ke kavirajmarg ke kaary milte hai. is sahitya se ye bhi siddh hota he ki kannada sahitya main chattan, beddand evam melvadu chhandon kaa prayog aarambhik shatabdiyon se hota aayaa he.
 kuvempu, prasiddh kannada kavi evam lekhak the, jinhone jai bharat jananeey tanujte likhaa tha, jise ab rajya kaa geet ghoshit kiya gaya he. inhen pratham karnataka ratna sammaan diya gaya tha, jo karnataka sarkaar dwaara diya jane vaala sarvocch naagarik sammaan he. anya samakaleen kannada sahitya bhi bhartiya sahitya ke praangan main apana pratishthit sthaan banaaye hue he. saat kannada lekhakon ko bharat kaa sarvocch sahitya sammaan gyanpith puraskaar mil chukaa he, jo kisi bhi bhartiya bhashaa ke liye sabase bada saahitiyik sammaan hota he. tulu bhashaa mukhyatah rajya ke tateey jilon udupi or dakshin kannada main boli jaati he. tulu mahabharato, arunbj dwaara is bhashaa main likhaa gaya puraatanatam uplabdh paath he. tulu lipi ke kramik patan ke kaaran tulu bhashaa ab kannada lipi main hi likhi jaati he, kintu kuch shatabdi purv tak is lipi kaa prayog hota rahaa tha. kodev jati ke log, jo mukhyatah kodagu jile ke nivasee hai, kodev takk bolte hai. is bhashaa kii do kshetreey boliyaan milti hai: uttari mendle takk or dakshini kiggati tak. konkani mukhyatah uttar kannada jile main or udupi evam dakshin kannada jilon ke kuch samipsth bhaagon main boli jaati he. kodev takk or konkani, donon main hi kannada lipi kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. kai vidyalayon main shiksha kaa madhyam angrezi he or adhikansh bahurashtriya companiyon tathaa praudyogiki-sambandhit companiyon tathaa bipio main angrezi kaa prayog hi hota he.
 rajya kii sabhi bhashaaon ko sarkari evam ardh-sarkari sansthaaon kaa sangrakshan praapt he. kannada sahitya parishat evam kannada sahitya academy kannada bhashaa ke utthan hetu evam kannada konkani sahitya academy konkani sahitya ke liye karyrat he. tulu sahitya academy evam kodev sahitya academy apni apni bhashaaon ke vikaas main kaaryshil hai.
 2001 kii jansankhya anusaar, karnataka kii sakshartaa dar 67.04% he, jisamen 76.29% purush tathaa 57.45% striyaan hai. rajya main bharat ke kuch pratishthit shaikshik or anusandhan santhaan bhi sthit hai, jaise bhartiya vigyaan santhaan, bhartiya prabandhan santhaan, rashtriya praudyogiki santhaan, karnataka or bhartiya rashtriya vidhi vishvavidhyalay.
