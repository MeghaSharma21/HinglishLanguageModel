 vetaal bhairav mandir srinagar ke ranawadi main maujuud 400 saal se adhik pracheen hindu mandir he. ati samvedansheel kshetra main shatabdi puraane vetaal bhairav mandir jo ,kashmir ke adhikansh panditon ko aatankavadio dwaara 1 99 0 main aatankwaad ke kaaran ghati se khadede jane ke baad or unhen jammu or desh ke anya bhaagon main jane ke liye majaboor karne ke baad, band kar diya gaya tha. 27 april 2016 ko yahan phirase kholaa gaya.
 aatankwaad se pehle kashmir main 583 mandir the, inamen se 532 vibhinn aatankwaad se sambandhit ghatnaon main kshatigrast ho gaye the.or 52 mandiro kaa koi namonishaan nahin bachaa. varsh 2012 main is puraane mandir ke jirnoddhar kaa kaam shuru hua tha. mandir kii deevaaron kii marammat kar nae daravaaje lagaae gaye the or charadivari banaai thi.90 ke dashak se pehle yahaan amar nath yatra ke dauraan lungar kaa aayojan kiya jaataa tha. sthaneey logon ke mutabik bhagwaan bhairav kii jayanti par yahaan khaas kaarykram kaa aayojan bhi hua karata tha or kaarykram main desh ke kone-kone se log darshan karne aate the.
