 maari ek bhartiya tamil hasya film he, jiskaa nirdesan balaji mohan ne kiya he. iske nirmaataa radhika sarathkumaar or aar sarathkumaar hai. isamen dhanush, kaajal agarwal, vijay yesudas, robo shankar or aniruddh ravichandar mukhya bhoomika main hai. yah film 17 july 2015 ko cinemagharon main pradarshit hui.
 yah kahani do gunde maari se shuru hoti he. vah apane dushman ek or gunde ko maar detaa he. vah velu ke neeche kaary karata he. vah ek or gunde ravi se ladata rahata he. vah bhi velu ke neeche hi kaary karata he. ek din shridevi vahaan apane vyapaar ke liye aati he. maari jabardasti uske vyapaar main ek hissedaar ban jaataa he. vah uske kuch graahak ko bhaga bhi detaa he, jisase shridevi krodhit ho jaati he. vah arjuna jo kii ek police adhikari he, uski maari ko pakadvane main madad karti he. vah dikhaati he kii vah maari se pyaar karti he or vah uske khilaaf saboot khojati he. lekin isse pehle hi kisi anya ke dwaara hue hatya kaa aarope maari par lag jaataa he or vah giraftaar ho jaataa he. vah jab baahar aata he to use pataa chalata he ki velu bhi giraftaar ho chukaa he. arjuna or ravi donon milakar kai logon ko jail main band kar rahe hai. vah donon se badla lene kaa sochataa he. isi dauraan shridevi usase pyaar karne lagati he. maari arjuna ke ek gaiyr kanoonee saamaan se bhare vaahan ko apane kabje main le letaa he or usase velu ko chhodane ko kahata he. arjuna ravi ko bachaane kii koshish karata he. lekin uske ek hawaldaar ke khulaase ke baad vah bhi pakadaa jaataa he. shridevi aakhri main apane pyaar ke bare main maari ko bataati he, lekin vah inkaar kar detaa he.
 is film ke nirdeshak balaji mohan ne is film ke nirmaan ke baaren main jaankaree janvari 2014 main di. tab unhone bataaya kii isamen dhanush or kaajal agarwal mukhya kirdaar main rahenge.
 ek afvaah thi kii is film main dhanush kuch samay ke liye dikhaai denge. film ke najdiki sutron ne bataaya kii vah is film main ek madras ke sthaneey nivasee ke rup main dikhaai denge or usee main baat karenge. lekin jab kirdaar kaa khulaasa hua tab vah ek mukhya bhoomika main the. yah kirdaar unke pichley film aadukalam se miltaa julata he. isi dauraan kaajal agarwal bhi is film se jud gayi. vah bhi is film main mukhya kirdaar main se ek kaa kirdaar nibhaa raheen hai. vah is film se navambar 2014 main judin. yah unki tamil cinema main ek varsh ke pashchaat vaapasi he. isse pehle vah jilla naamak film main kaary kar chuki thim.
 kaajal polladhavan main dhanush ke saath kaary karne vaali thim. lekin us samay anya film ke kaaran vah isse baahar gai. vah us dauraan ek telugu film chandaamama main kaary kar rahi thim.
