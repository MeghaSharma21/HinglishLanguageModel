 vishnu ganesh pingale bharat ke swatantraaa sangram ke ek krantikari the. ve gadar party ke sadsy the. lahore shadyantra cese or hindu-german shadyantra main unako san 1915 faansee kii sajaa di gayi.
 vishnu kaa janm 2 janvari 1888 ko puna ke gaanv talegaanv main hua. san 1911 main vah ejineering kii shiksha praapt karne america pahunche jahaan unhone cetail vishvavidhyalay main ejineering kalege main pravesh liya. vahaan lala hardayal jaise netaaon kaa unhen maargdarshan mila. mahaan krantikari kartaar singh sarabha se unki mitrataa thi. desh main gadar paidaa karke desh ko swatantra karwaane kaa sunahari mauka dekhakar vishnu ganesh baaki saathiyon ke saath bharat laute or british india kii phujon main kranti laane kii taiyaari main jute gayi. unhone kalakatta main shri raas bihari bose se mulaakaat kii. vah shachindranath saanyaal ko lekar punjab chale aae. us samay punjab, bengal or utar pradesh main sainik kranti kaa puraa prabandh ho gaya tha kintu ek gaddaar kii gaddari ke kaaran sari yojana vifal ho gai. vishnu pingale ko nadir khan naamak ek vyakti ne giraftaar karvaa diya. giraftaar ke samay unke paas das bam the. un par mukadmaa chalaaya gaya or 17 navambar 1915 ko central jail lahore main unhen faansee de di gayi.
