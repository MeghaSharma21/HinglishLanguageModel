 dur shiksha anaupchaarik shiksha kii aadhunik pranali hai. isamen santhaan, shikshak tathaa chaatr kaa sampark bahut kam yaa nahi ke baraabar hota hai. yah patrachaar, sampark kaaryakramon, jan sanchaar ke sadhano dwaara shiksha pradaan kii jaati hai. dur shiksha main patrachaar grah-adhyan, mukt adhyan, parisar mukt adhyan aadi sammilit hai.
 dur shiksha shabd kaa prayog san 1982 se shuru huwa. jab chaar dashak puraani inter neshnal kauncil for korespondence ajukeshan ne apana naam badal kar inter neshnal kauncil for dystens ajukeshan rakh liya.dur shiksha multah ese baalkon/balikaon tathaa praudhon ke kiye hai jo vibhinn kaarnon se niyamit rup se aupchaarik shiksha praapt nahin kar saken or vartmaan dasha main samayabhaav or adhik aayu ho jane ke kaaran nahi kar sakate hai.
 dur shiksha ke antargat chaatro ko padhne ke liye swa-shikshan saamagree pradaan kii jaati hai.
