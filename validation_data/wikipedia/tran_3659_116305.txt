 gulburg kila uttar karnataka ke gulburg jile main gulburg shahar main sthit he. mul rup se iskaa nirmaan warangal rajvansh ke raaj main raja gulchand ne karavaaya tha. iske baad san 1347 main bahamani rajvansh ke allaudin bahaman shah ne dilli sultanate ke saath sambandhon ko todane ke baad ise kaafii bada karavaaya tha. baad main kile ke bheetar masjidon, mehalon, kabron jaise islamic smarakon or anya sanrachanaon kaa nirmaan hua. 1367 main kile ke bheetar banaaya gaya sabhi ore se band jama masjid manohar gumbadon or mehrabadar stambhon sahit farsi vaastu style main nirmit ek adwitiya sanrachana he. yah 1327 se 1424 ke bich gulburg kile par bahamani shashan kii sthapana ke uplakshya main banaaya gaya tha. yah 1424 tak bahamani rajya kii rajdhani rahaa, jiske baad behatar jalwaayu paristhitiyon ke kaaran rajdhani bidar kile main le jaai gayi.
 chhathi shatabdi main kshetra par rashtrakut samrajya kaa shashan tha. iske baad chalukya samrajya ne kshetra vaapis jeet liya do shatabdiyon se adhik samay tak shashan kiya. iske baad kalchuri rajvansh ne isapar kabza kar liya or barahavin shatabdi tak unka kabza rahaa. barahavin shatabdi ke ant main yah devgiri ke yadavon or halibidu ke hoysal rajvansh ke shashan main rahaa. is samay ke dauraan warangal ke shaasha kakatiya vansh bhi shaktishaalee the or unhone gulburg jile or raichud jile par kabza kaa liya tha.
 san 1321 main kakatiya vansh ko haraakar uttari dakkhan par dilli sultanate kaa kabza ho gaya or isamen gulburg bhi shaamil tha.
 chaudaavin sadi kii shuruyaat main, gulburg sahit sampoorn dakkhan par dilli sultanate ke mohammad bin tughlaq ne kabja kar liya tha. dilli se niyukt muslim adhikariyon ke baghawat karne par 1347 main bahamani sultanate kii sthapana hui. sultanate ke pratham raja hasan gangu ne gulburg ko apni rajdhani chunaa. gulburg 1347 se 1424 tak bahamani samrajya kii rajdhani tha or gulburg kila rajdhani kaa mukhyalay tha. 1424 main rajdhani bidar le jaayi gayi. kaha jaataa he ki bahamani rajya kii sthapana ke saath muslim shashan ne dakkhan main jaden bana leen. ahamed shah vali bahamani ne iraq, iran or madhya asia se pravaasiyon ko dakkhan main sthapit kiya. isse dakkhan ke saamaajik, saanskrithik or dharmik jivan main badlaav aaye. phir bhi ve hindu paramparaon ke saath ghul-mil gayi.
 gulburg kila vijaynagar samraat dwaara nasht kar diya gaya tha. baad main yousuf adil shah ne vijaynagar samraat ko yuddh main haraa kar jab bijapur sultanate kii sthapana kii to is kile kaa punarnirmaan karaayaa tha. vijaynagar samrajya ko lut kar jo mila tha us se is kile ko punarnirmit kiya gaya tha.
 pandrahavin shatabdi ke ant or solahavin shatabdi kii shuruyaat tak dakkhan par mukhyatah bahamani samrajya kaa raaj tha, jiske baad rajya paanch rajyon main bant gaya.
 san 1687 main mogul samraat aurangzeb ne kile par kabza kiya or dakkhan ko nizaam-ul-mulk ke shashan ke antargat choda. attharahavin shatabdi kii shuruyaat main mogul samrajya ke patan ke dauraan nizaam-ul-mulk asafajah ne 1724 main mogul samrajya se swatantraaa ghoshit kar ke hyderabad state kii sthapana kii. gulburg kaa ek bada kshetra bhi is samrajya kaa hissaa tha. is tarah se asafajahi rajvansh kii sthapana hui, jo baad main chalakar hyderabad ke nizaam bane.
 nizaam shashan ke dauraan gulburg zila or kila donon hyderabad rajya ke antargat aate the. agast 1947 main bharat kii swatantraaa ke pashchaat 1948 main hyderabad state bharat kaa hissaa ban gaya. 1956 main bhashaa ke aadhaar par hyderabad rajya ko bharat ke rajyon main baanta gaya. andhra pradesh ko diye gayi do talukon ke atirikt baaki gulburg zila mysore rajya kaa hissaa bana, jo baad main karnataka bana.
 yah karnataka ke purvottar bhaag main ek pathaar bhoomi par sthit he. krishna nadi or bhima nadi jile se guzarati hai or bhima nadi kile ke kareeb bahati he. kshetra main mrudaa mukhya rup se kaali mitti kii he.
 kila jile ke us bhaag main he jahaan sookhaa padane kii sambhaavna rahati he or saamanytah yahan saal main kul 46 din varsha hoti he jisamen 777 millimetre barsaat hoti he.
 jalwaayu sardiyon main shushk or thanda he, lekin garmiyon main garm he. dakshin pashchimi monsoon se varsha aati he. garmi main ucchtam taapmaana 45 °se. he or sardi main nunatam 5 °se. he.
 1347 isvi main bahamani samrajya kii sthapana ke saath farsi vaastukala kaa prabhaav padaa jo gulburg kile main dekha ja saktaa he. kile ke andar kii imaaraten bhartiya-farsi vaastustyli main bani hai or unki vaastukala prabhaavshaalee he. prof desai kaa kahana he:
 1347 main bahamani rajvansh kii sthapana ke saath dakkhan main ek nayi bhartiya-farsi vaastukala style kaa janm hua.
 kuch pramukh sanrachanaon kaa vivaran nimna he:
 kile ko bahamani rajvansh ke allaudin hasan baman shah ne baad main pashchim asian or europea sainik style ke vaastushilp kaa prayog karte hue kilaband karavaaya tha. kila ke centre main ek gadhi bhi jodi gayi thi.
 kile kaa kul kshetrafal 0.5 acre he or isaki paridhi kii lambaai 3 kilometre he. isamen duguni kilabandi kaa prayog kiya gaya he, arthath kila dohari deevaaron se surakshit he. kile ke chaaron ore ek 30 feet chaudee khai he.
 kile main 15 burj hai jinamein 26 bandooken rakhi hui hai. har bandook 8 meter lambi he or ab bhi acchhee tarah sangrakshit he.
 kaha jaataa he ki bahamani sultanate saasaani rajvansh ke vanshaj hone kaa daawa karti thi. unki banaayi imaaraton kaa roopankan, vishisht rup se unke dwaara banaae gayi mehrabon ke sheersh balachandra darshaate the or kabhi-kabhi saath main chakra aakaar kaa bhi prayog karte the, jo ki kaafii had tak saasaani samrajya dwaara banawaaye gayi shirshon se mail khate the. kile ke kshetra main kai dharmik or dharmanirpeksh imaaraten is pratik kaa prayog karti hai.
 jami masjid dakshin bharat kii pehali masjidon main se ek he. iskaa nirmaan gulburg ke bahamani samrajya kii rajdhani banane par kiya gaya tha. yadhyapi masjid kii ruparekha saral he, isamen samamiti he or iske bhaag acchhee tarah se yojit hai. yah masjid bharat main adwitiya he or iskaa aakaar 216 feet x176 feet he. iskaa nirmaan spain ke kordoba ke masjid ke tarj par hua he. masjid pehle khandhar kii avasthaa main tha, parantu ab isaki bhalee-bhanti dekh-bhaal kii ja rahi he.
 masjid main khulaa angan nahin he. prarthana kaksh ke teen ore mehrabadar galiyare hai. galiyare aayat ke aakaar main hai. uttar or dakshin disha main galiyare mehrab stambhon dwaara das khandon main vibhajit he or purv main saat khandon main. aayat aakaar ke konon ke khandak kaksh chaukor hai or unke upar gumbaz hai. andar ke khandak kakshon ke upar chapaantar tribhujon ke upyog se banaayi gayi nichi gumbazen hai. kaaba-mukhi diwaar ke aage ke baraamde main nau khand kaksh hai or uske upar ek bada gumbaz he. gumbaz ke nichle hisse kii mehrabon par tridali chaap or lambi paaliyaan hai. gumbaz mukhya chat ke upar ek chaukor udgat khand par aadhaarit he. galiyare pehle lakdi kii oot main the par samay ke saath lakdi hataa di gayi he. unki jagah uttari mukh par ek mehrabadar praveshdwar bana diya gaya he. kul milakar, masjid paanch badi gumbadon, 75 choti gumbadon or 250 mehrabon ke saath vishisht farsi vaastustyli main he.
 uparokt ke atirikt ruchi kii imaarat he khwaja bande nawaz naam se prachlit sufi sant sayed mohammad gesu daraaj kaa makbara, jo bhartiya-muslim vaastustyli main bana he. yah ek bada bhawan-samooh he jahaan sufi sant kii kabr maujuud he. ye sant san 1413 main gulburg aaye the. dargah ke mehrab bahamani vaastustyli main hai or kabr kii deevaaron or chat par turki or irani prabhaav vaale chitra hai. mughalon ne bhi is makbare ke nikat ek masjid banwaaya tha. yahan har varsh navambar main urs hoti he, jisamen sabhi dharmon ke anek shraddhaalu aate hai.
 gulburg rail or sadak margon se acchhee tarah judaa hua he. gulburg madhya dakshini rail par ek mukhya stesan he or rail se bangalore, dilli, mumbai or hyderabad se judaa he.
 yah bangalore or hyderabad se rashtriya rajmargon se acchhee tarah judaa he, jo gulburg se 610 kilometre or 225 kilometre kilometre dur hai. rajya main anya shaharon se sadak maarg dwaara dooriyaan hai: basvakalyaan - 80 kilometre, bidar -120 kilometre or bijapur - 160 kilometre .
 hyderabad hawaii adda gulburg se nikatatam hawaii adda he or gulburg se 225 kilometre dur he.
