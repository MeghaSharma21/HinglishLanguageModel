 raktasharkaraalpata yaa hypoglysemia saamaanya se neeche str main rakt-sharkara kii banane kii avasthaa ke liye chikitsakeey shabdaawalee he. shabdaawalee kaa shabdic arth he, "antah rakt sharkara" . yah kai prakaar ke lakshan or prabhaav paidaa kar saktaa he kintu samasya tab paidaa hoti he jab mastishk ko aparyaapt glucose kii aapurti hone lagati he, jiske falswaroop mastishk kii kriyasheeltaa main vikruti athwa kshati  paidaa ho jaati he. mote taur par iske prabhaav "ghabrahat mahasoos" karne se lekar daure padane, behoshee tathaa sthaayee taur par dimagi kshati athwa mrityu tak bhi doorgaami ho sakate hai.
 sabase aam taur par madhumeh ke insulin athwa khaanevaali dawaaon ke jatil upachaar ke kaaran bhi raktasharkaraalpata paidaa ho sakati he. raktasharkaraalpata kam gaiyr madhumeh vyaktiyon main aam he, lekin kisi bhi umr main kai kaarnon se ho sakati hai. in kaarnon main, sharir main atyadhik maatra main insulin kaa utpann hona, janmajaat trutiyaan, aushadhiyon or vish, sharaab, hormone kii kami lambi mukhabhari, sankraman, tathaa ang kii kriyasheeltaa main vifaltaa se jude chypachay aadi hai.

 raktasharkara kaa nicha str bhinn logon main bhinn sharkaraalpata, bhinn paristhitiyon main, tathaa bhinn uddeshyon ke liye, isaki paribhaasha kabhi-kabhi yah vivaad kaa vishay bana rahaa he. adhikansh swasth vayaskon main upwas kii avasthaa main glucose kaa str 70 migra/diel, evam raktasharkaraalpata ke lakshan viksit hone lagate hai jab glucose kaa str 55 migra/diel tak pahunch jaataa he. kabhi kabhi yah nirdhaarit karnaa kathin ho jaataa he. kabhi-kabhi yah nirdhaarit karnaa kathin ho jaataa he ki kya vyakti ke rog ke lakshan kya raktasharkaraalpata ke kaaran to nahin. raktasharkaraalpata ke rog-nirdhaarit ke vihpal ke trayee ke maandand kaa upyog kiya jaataa he.
 aamtaur par raktasharkaraalpata  lokapriy samskruti main shabdaawalee he evam aam logon ke liye vaikalpik chikitsa he, aksar aatm-parikshan kar kampan, asthirataa tathaa man or soch main badlaav, kintu sharkara ke nimna str ko bina maape hi athwa jokhim bhare nuksaan ke bina hi. aahaar ke abhyaas main parivartan kar isaki chikitsa kii jaati he.

 bacchon main rakt sharkara kaa str vayaskon kii tulana main aksar thodaa kam hota he. 5% swaroop vayaskon main raatbhar ke upwas ke baad sharkara kaa str 70 mg/dL rahata he, kintu 5% bacchon main yah subah kii khaali pet avasthaa main 60 mg/dL se bhi nicha rah saktaa he. jaise hi upwas kii avadhi badhaai jaati he, shishuon or bacchon ke ek ucch pratishat main glucose plasma ke str halake se neeche honge, aamtaur par lakshanon ke bina hi. navjaat shishuon main rakt sharkara ke saamaanya str kii seemaa ko lekar bahes honi chahie. yah prastaav pesh kiya gaya he ki, navjaat mastishk vaikalpik indhan kaa upyog karne main vayaskon kii tulana main tatparta se sakasham ho jate hai jab glucose ke str main giravat aa jaati he. aise staron ke mahatva or jokhim ko lekar visheshgyon ke bich bahes jari he, yadhyapi janm ke tatkaal baad pehle din sharkara ke str ko 60–70 mg/dL tak banaae rakhane kii sifarish kii ore hi visheshgyon kaa jhukaav he.
 swasth vayaskon main anusandhan se pataa chalata he ki maansik kshamta main thodi-si giravat aati he lekin kai logon main kuch had tak raktasharkara 65 mg/dL tak neeche utar jaati he. hormone janit raksha prakriya-tantra saamaanya rup se sakriy hote hai kyonki yah seemaa-rekha ke str adhikansh logon ke liye) neeche utar jaataa he kampan, asthirataa tathaa ghabrahat or bechaini bhare maansik avsaad ke ajeebogareeb raktasharkaraalpata vaale lakshanon ko janm detaa he.:1589 spasht shaareerik aakshamata tab tak nahin aati he jabatak kii glucose kaa str 40 mg/dL se bhi neeche nahin utar jaataa he, evam kai vyaktiyon main bina kisi spasht prabhaav ke subah glucose kaa str kabhi-kabhi 65 bhi ho jaataa he. chunki raktasharkaraalpata ke mastishk par prabhaav, jise neuroglaicopinia namakaran diya gaya he, yah nirdhaarit karata he ki kya diya gaya kam glucose us vyakti ke liye "samasya" he athwa nahin, adhikansh doctor hypoglicami shabd kaa prayog tabhi karte hai ajab saamaanya rup se glucose ke nichle str ke saath lakshan yaa mastishk par prabhaav ek saath parilakshith hone lagate hai.
 is paribhaasha ke donon bhaagon kii upasthiti kaa nirdhaaran sarvada sidhaa-sidhaa hi nahin hota, kyonki raktasharkaraalpata janit lakshan or prabhaav aspasht hote hai evam anya paristhitiyon se bhi paidaa ho sakate hai; aise log jinamein glucose kaa str baar-baar kam ho jaataa he, ve apni seemaa rekha ke lakshanon ko kho sakate hai taaki gambhir neuroglaicopanic aakshamata bina kisi lakshanik chetavanee ke unamem aa sakati he tathaa kai parimaapan paddatiyaan nimna str par galat hote hai.
 madhumeh raktasharkaraalpata kai kaarnon se parimapit glucose evam raktasharkaraalpatajanit lakshanon ke sambandh ke sandarbh main ek khaas kism kaa rog he. sarvapratham, gharelu glucose meter kii reeding aksar bhraamak paathyank suchit karti he, yah bhi sambhaavna he ki kam paathyank, lakshanon ke saath ho yaa na ho, jo vaastavik raktasharkaraalpata suchit karti he, yah bhi sambhaavna he ki kam paathyank, lakshanon ke saath ho yaa na ho, jo vaastavik raktasharkaraalpata suchit karti he vah us vyakti vishesh main adhik ucchtar hota he jo insulin na lene vaale kii tulana main insulin letaa he. doosraa, chunki injection ke jariye andar pravisht insulin ke "pravaah ko roka nahin ja saktaa" madhumehi raktasharkaraalpata main gambhir shaareerik vikruti ke viksit hone kii adhik sambhaavna ho jaati he, agar ilaaj na kiya gaya to, raktasharkaraalpata ke adhikansh anya roopon kii tulana main. teesraa, chunki lambi avadhi ke liye aksar sharkara kaa str madhumeh se peedit logon main saamaanya se upar hota he, to raktasharkaraalpata ke lakshan kabhi-kabhi ucchtar simarekha tak dikhaai de sakate hai un logon kii tulana main jinaki rakt sharkara aamtaur par saamaanya he. in sabhi kaarnon se ucchtar, meter glucose kii seemaa rekha ko aksar madhumeh se peedit logon main "hypoglichemic" samajha jaataa he.

 poshan kii sthiti kaa nirdhaaran or saath hi saath, yah bhi pahchaan ho janaa ki kya raktasharkaraalpata se bhi adhik gambhir koi milti-julati antanirhit beemaari he yaa nahin, aisi sthiti main rogi kii shaareerik pariksha keval kabhi-kabhi hi sahaayak siddh ho sakati he. shaishvavastha main sthulkaaya atimdhusudati kii ore ingit karata he. kuch syndrome evam chayapachay rogon kii pahchaan vardhit yakrut athwa laghulingi hone jaise suraagon se kii ja sakati he.
 behoshee ke saath gambhir raktasharkaraalpata athwa saamaanya rakt sharkara kii purvastha kii prapti ke pashchaat daure padane par is beemaari se aarogylabh karne main kaaphi samay lag saktaa he. jab koi vyakti behosh nahin hota he, carbohydrate kii vifaltaa ke kaaran lakshanon ke 10 se 15 minute main pratikul ho jane kii sambhaavna ho jaati he jisase is baat ke asaar ho jate hai ki raktasharkaraalpata lakshanon kaa kaaran nahin ho sakati. jab aspataal main bhartee kisi rogi main raktasharkaraalpata lagaataar bani rahati he, satoshajnak raktasharkaraalpata kaa str banaae rakhane ke liye sharkara kii aavashyak maatr rog ke antarnihit kaarnon evam rog kii utpatti ke liye mahatvapurn suraag ban jaataa he. shishuon main sharkraqi aavashyaktaa agar 10 milligram/pratikilo gram/pratiminut, athwa bacchon evam vayaskon main 6 mi.gra./ prati ki.gra/ prati minute hone par hyper insulizam ke hone kaa shashakt pramaan samajha jaataa he. is sandarbh main ise sharkara sammishran dar jidhiar ke rup main ullekh karte hai. ant main, jab sharkara kaa str nicha rahata he to glucogan ke die jane par raktasharkaraalpata kii alag-alag pahchaan karne main sahaayta kar sakati he. 30 mg/dl se bhi adhik agar rakat sharkara bad jaati he to, raktasharkaraalpata kaa sambhavya kaaran ban jaataa he or insulin ke adhik hone kii ore ingit karata he.
 kam spasht maamalon main ek "mahatvapurn namoona" nidaan de saktaa he. bahusankhyak bacchon evam vayaskon main jinamein aavarti or apasht raktasharkaraalpata hoti he, unke rog kii pahchaan raktasharkaraalpata ke dauraan rakt-parikshan dwaara kii ja sakati he. agar yah mahatvapurn namoona raktasharkaraalpata ke samay main praapt kar liya jaataa he, isse pehle ki yah samay kar liya jaataa hi, isse pehle ki yah vipareet ho jaae, to yah suchana pradaan kar saktaa he nahin to aspataal main bhartee hokar apriy bhukhe pet parikshan kii aavashyaktaa hogi. shaayad aspashtikrut raktasharkaraalpata ke maamalon main apatkaaleen vibhaag kii sabase saamaanya aparyaaptta is sthiti ko ulat dene ke liye glucose dene se pehle kam se kam moulik namoona praapt karne main vifaltaa he.
 mahatvapurn namoonay ke bas ek ansh kaa moolyankan hi yah pramanit karne ke liye kaaphi he ki rog ke lakshan raktasharkaraalpata ke kaaran hai. adhiktar kai baar, raktasharkaraalpata ke samay hormone evam chayapachayon ke maapan yah ingit karte hai ki sharir kaa koun se ang-pratyang evam shaareerik kriya-pranali uchit tareeke se pratikriyasheel he evam inamen se koun-koun se aavashyak asaamaanya rup se kaary kar rahe hai. udhaaharan ke liye, jab raktasharkara kaa str kam ho jaataa he, sharkara badhaane vaale hormonon ko or bhi upar uthaanaa chahie tathaa insulin ke srav ko puri tarah dabaa diya janaa chahie.
 nimnlikhit hormone or chayapachayon kii sankshipt suchi he jinhen mahatvapurn namoonay main mapa ja saktaa he. prayek rogi par sabhi prakaar ke parikshan nahin kiye jate hai. "moulik sanskaran" main si-screen ke saath insulin, cortisol, evam electrolite vayaskon ke liye tathaa vruddhi hormone bacchon ke liye shaamil hai. atirikt vishisht pareekshanon ke mulyankan, uparokt paristhitiyon ke aadhaar par rogi vishesh ke rog ke nidaan par nirbhar karte hai. inamen se kai str aise hai jo minuton main badal jate hai, khaskar jab glucose diya jaataa he or raktasharkaraalpata ke vipareet ho jane ke baad unke mapankan kaa koi mulya nahin he. duusare visheshkar jo suchi main sabase neeche hai, ve raktasharkaraalpata kii ulti sthiti main bhi namoona nahin bhi miltaa he to bhi upyogi tareeke se mapankan ho saktaa he.
 gambhir rup se biimaar athwa behad kam vajani navjaat shishuon main raktasharkaraalpata ek aam samasya he. agar matruvanshgat raktasharkaraalpata ke kaaran na bhi ho to bhi adhikansh maamalon main yah bahukaariya kshanik evam aasaani se protsaahit hota he. alpsankhyak maamalon ke raktasharkaraalpata ullekhneey rup se adhik insulin lene ke kaaran alp shleshmiyata athwa chayapachay main janmajaat truti ke kaaran aati he tathaa prabandhan ke liye ek bahut badi chunauti khadaa kar detii he.
 raktasharkaraalpata ke ekal prakaran antrashodh athwa upwas ke kaaran ho sakate hai kintu aavartak prakaran sarvada yaa to chayapachay janit janmajaat atimghusundani kii or ingit karte hai. saamaanya kaarnon kii suchi is prakaar he:
 abtak is aayu seemaa waalon main gambhir raktasharkaraalpata kaa sabase adhik aam kaaran madhumeh ke tip 1 ke liye insulin injection lenaa hi mana jaataa he. paristhitiyon kaa avilamb nai beemaari ke kaaran gambhir hone vaali raktasharkaraalpata ke sutara kaa sandhan dena chahie. janmajaat chayapachay ke dosha janmajaat ucch madhusudani ke prakaar tathaa janmajaat alpasleshmiyata ko pehle se nidaan ke rup main pataa laga liya ja chukaa he athwa is aayu main nayi raktasharkaraalpata ke kaaran kii shuruyaat kii sambhaavna he. sharir kaa vajan maan itnaa bada he ki anaahaar raktasharkaraalpata puri tarah asaamaanya lagata he. aavartak halki raktasharkaraalpata pratikriyasheel raktasharkaraalpata ke pattern main sateek feet ho sakati he, kintu yah bhi agyatahetuk bhojan ke pashchaat kii lakshan-samisht he, evam is aayu-varg main aavartak "dauron" kaa pataa nimna raktchaap main sir main chakkar aana athwa joro se saans chalanaa jaisaa ki raktasharkaraalpata kii avasthaa main aksar spasht parilakshith hota he, lagaaya ja saktaa he.
 jatil aushadhiy ant kriyaaon ke kaaran raktasharkaraalpata kii ghatana visheshkar raktasharkaraalpata ke liye khai jaani vaali aushadhiyon ke agent evam madhumeh ke liye lee jane vaali insulin isamen shaamil hai. haalanki aise maamle durlabh hi he, insulin utpann karne vaale tumor badhati umr ke saath badhane lagate hai. insulin kii adhikta ke alaave anya prakriyaaon se bhi vayaskon main adhikansh tumor ke kaaran hote hai.
 jab sandigdh raktasharkaraalpata baar-baar hoti hai evam chhidranveshan ke liye namoona praapt nahin hota he, to aisi sthiti main naidanik moolyankan kai raste pakad sakate hai. phir bhi acha poshan evam avilamb grahan atyaavashyak hai.
 jab saamaanya swasthya acha he, lakshan gambhir nahin hai, tathaa vyakti saadharantaya sari raat upwas kar saktaa he, aahaar ke saath prayog samasya ke samaadhaan ke liye paryaapt he. agar yah anishchit he ki raktasharkaraalpata ke kaaran hi sachmuch daure padate hai, to kuch chikitsak, jab daure padate hai us vishesh vakt main yah pushti karne ke liye ki katha glucose kaa str kam he, gharelu glucose meter ke upyog kii sifarish karenge. jab daure baraabar padane lage athwa rogi/rogini ko yah mahasoos hone lage ki uskaa sir daure padane se pehle chakkar kha rahaa he to aisi sthiti main yah padyati sabase upyogi ho sakati he. is padyati kii sabase badi khami yah he ki yah galat sakaaratmak ucch dar athwa vartmaan samay main uplabdh metron main ashuddhta ke kaaran dwayrthak staron kaa digdarshan: chikitsak or rogi daanon ke liye hi sateek saamaan kii zarurat he ki koun sa meter kaa upyog kar niraashajanak or anirnaayak parimaanon se bachaa ja saktaa he.
 gambhir lakshan ke saath aavartak raktasharkaraalpata ke maamalon main, sangeen halaaton ko chhodkar sabase acchhee vidhi aksar naidanik upwas he. yah aamtaur par aspataal main prabandhit kiya jaataa he or pareekshan kii avadhi rogi kii umr or upwas kii pratikriya par nirbhar karata he. ek swasth vayask aamtaur par 72 ghante ke liye, ek baccha 36 ghante ke liye or ek shishu 24 ghante ke liye ke liye 50 milligram/decilitre  se upar glucose kaa str banaae rakh sakate hai. upwas kaa uddeshy yah nirdhaarit karnaa hota he ki kya log apni rakt sharkara ke str ko lambey samay tak saamaanya banaae rakh sakate hai or chayapachay ke uchit parivartanon ke saath upwas main pratikriya kar sakate hai. upwas ke ant main insulin kii suraag lagbhag nahin milani chahie tathaa kitosis ko puri tarah sthapit kiya janaa chahie. rogi kii raktasharkara ke str kii nigaraanee kii jaati he evam agar sharkara ke str main giravat aati he to mahatvapurn namoona uplabdh kiya jaataa he. iske apriy evam vyaysadhy hone ke baavjood raktasharkaraalpata kii ki sangeen sthitiyon kii pushti athwa khandan karne ke liye vishesh rup se unamem jinamein atyadhik insulin nihit hota he, naidanik upwas hi ekmaatr prabhavi paddati he.
 sandigdh raktasharkaraalpata kii jaanch kii parmparik padyati glucose kii maukhik sahisnuta parikshan paddati he, visheshrup se 3,4 yaa 5 ghanto ke liye. haalanki 1960 ke dashakon main sanyukt rajya main kaaphi lokapriy rahaa, phir bhi baar-baar ke shodh adhyayanon ne yah pramanit kiya ki deergh parikshan kii avadhi main kai swasth logon main sharkara kaa str 70 yaa 60 se neeche ho jate he, evam kai prakaar kii mahatvapurn raktasharkaraalpata kaa pataa nahin chal pataa he. kharaab samvedansheeltaa or vishistataa ke is sangyojan ke parinamswaroop chikitsakon ne anubhav kiya ki sharkara ke chayapachay ke vikaron ke liye is pariksha kaa parityag kar diya janaa chahie.
 raktasharkaraalpata ko vargeekrut karne ke kai tareeke hai. nimnlikhit aam kaarnon evam karkon kaa ullekh aayu-seemaa ke samoohikrut aadhaar par raktasharkaraalpata main apana yogadan kar sakate hai, inke saath hi saath kuch aise bhi kaaran ho sakate hai jo apekshaakrit ke aayu seemaa ke vargeekaran ke swatantra ho sakate hai. nidaanshastra ke dwaara vargeekrut sampoorn suchi ke liye raktasharkaraalpata ke kaaran dekhen.
 raktasharkaraalpata ke or aage ke prakarnon ke nivaaran ke sarvaadhik prabhavi tarika kaaran par nirbhar karata he.
 madhumeh raktasharkaraalpata ke aage ke prakarnon ke jokhim ko aksar insulin kii khuraaq yaa anya aushadhiyon kii maatra ko kam kar, athwa asaamaanya ghanton ke dauraan rakt sharkara ke santulan kii adhik saavdhaanee purvak nigaraanee ke dwaara vyaayaam ke ucch str, yaa sharaab kaa seven kam se kam kar kiya ja saktaa he.
 chayapachay kii kai sahjaat trutiyon ke liye upwas ke antaraal ko kiya janaa, athwa atirikt carbohydrate kaa parihaar kiya janaa jaruri he. adhik gambhir vikaron ke liye, jaise ki tip 1 glicosan sangrahan rog main, har kuch ghanton par makai kaa aanta ke rup main aapurti kar athwa satat jalsek amashay dwaara nidaan kii vyavastha kii ja sakati he.
 ucch madhusudani raktasharkaraalpata ke liye kai upachaar sateek rup or gambhirtha par nirbhar karte hai. janmajaat ucchmadhusudani ke kuch aise rup hai jo diaoxide yaa auctraoid ke upyog main sakaaratmak pratikriya pesh karte hai. agnyashay ke ati kriyasheel ansh ko shalya chikitsa dwaara hataakar kam jokhim ke saath upachaaratmak he jab ucch madhusudhani hi mukhya centre-vindu ho athwa agnashay ke susadhy insulin utpaadak tumor ke kaaran ho. jab janmajaat madhusudhani fail jaati he evam aushadhiyon se pratirakshit ho jaati he, antim upaay ke upachaar ke rup main lagbhag sampoorn panecriyatectomy he, kintu is avasthaa main yah kam prabhavi or adhik jatiltaaon se lagaataar bharaa hota he.
 hormone kii kami ke kaaran jaise ki alpasleshmiyata athwa aadhivrikk kii kami ke kaaran raktasharkaraalpata aamtaur par sthagit ho jaati he jab upyukt hormone badal diya jaataa he.
 kshepan-lakshan evam anya shalp chikitsottar avasthaaon main raktasharkaraalpata sabase achche tareeke se kaabo main karne kaa tarika aahaar main parivartan he. carbohydrate ke saath vasa or protein shaamil pachan kiya ko manthar kar sakate hai tathaa insulin ke srav ko shuru-shuru main hi kam kar sakate hai. isamen kuch aise prakaar hai jo glucosides avarohak se upachaar par aisi pratikriya pradarshit karti he, jo starch ke pachan ko dhimaa kar detii he.
 pramanya rup se raktasharkara ke nimna str ke saath pratikriyasheel raktasharkaraalpata aksar purvanumaan pareshaani he, jise subah ke naashte yaa aparaahn ke alpaahar ke saath vasa or protein kaa seven kar, tathaa sharaab kii kam maatra kam kar parivarjit kiya ja saktaa he.
 sharkara ke nimna str ke sanket ke bina pramanyarup se aahaar ke baad agyaat hetu lakshan prabandhan kii chunauti he. aahaar ke pattern main parivartan laakar kai logon ne sudhaar kaa tarika dhund nikaala he, caffeine jaateey uttejkon kaa seven kam kar, athwa jivan style main parivartan laakar tanaav ko kam kar. is lekh kaa nimnlikhit anuchhed dekhen.
 raktasharkaraalpata ke prabandhan main kaaran kaa nirdhaaran kar, raktasharkaraalpata ke str ko tatkaal saamaanya tak pahunchaa dena shaamil he, tathaa aise upaay bhi kar liye jaay ki bhavishya main aisi ghatnaon kii punarakrit roki jaay.
 raktasharkara ke str ko 10-20 gram carbohydrate lekar minuton main saamaanya tak upar uthaaya ja saktaa he. agar vyakti hosh main ho tathaa nigalane main sakasham ho to vah ise aahaar ke rup athwa peya ke rup main le saktaa he. carbohydrate kii yah maatra lagbhag 3-4 ouns santare seve, athwa angoor ke ras main nihit he, haalanki falon ke ras main fal sharkara kaa ucchtar anupaat hota he jo ki shuddh draksha sharkara kii tulana main bahut dhire-dhire chyapachayit hota he, vaikalpik rup se lagbhag 4-5 ouns niyamit soda bhi utana hi kargaar ho saktaa he jitna ki roti kaa ek tukdaa, lagbhag 4 biscuit yaa shwetsar se bhare jo jan kii ek parasan. shwetsar sahaj hi jaldi pachakar sharkara main badal jaataa he nahin sakate athwa saknami nahin chahie ki muhan ke jariye kuch bhi unhen khilaa diya jaae, chikitsa karmi jaise ki EMTs or parchikitsak, athwa aspataal ke karmchaaree IV kii vyavastha par atah shira draksha-sharkara de sakate hai, jisaki sandratha umr ke aadhaar par alag-alag honi chahie . in taral gholon ko dene main saavdhaanee avashya baratani chahie kyonki agar IV chhan-chhan kar andar fail jaataa he to ve ati asthyikshyi ho sakate hai. agar ek IV kii sthapana nahin kii ja sakati he to, rogi ko glucogan kaa 1 se 2 milligram atah mansapeshiyon injection diya ja saktaa he. madhumehi raktasharkaraalpata main upachaar kii adhik se adhik jaankaree mil sakati he.
 ek aisi sthiti jab shwetsar glucose yaa sukros kii apeksha kam prabhavi ho saktaa he agar madhumeh vyakti acrbose aushadhi le rahaa ho to. chunki acrabose evam anya alp glucosides shwetsar evam anya sharkara kii monosacarides main khandit hone se rokthaam karti hai jo sharir main shoshit ho saktaa he, in aushadhiyon kaa seven karne vaale rogiyon ko monosacarides se yukt aahaar jaise ki glucose kii goliyaan, madhu, yaa ras grahan karnaa chahie taaki raktasharkaraalpata ko viparyit kar diya jaay.
 raktasharkaraalpata samakaleen vaikalpik aushadhi ke paryaay kii shabdaawalee bhi he jo parivartit manodasha evam vyaktiparak sangyaatmak kshamta, kabhi-kabhi adhivrikkeey lakshanon ke saath aavarti sambandh jiskaa raktasharkara ke nimna str se sambandh ho bhi saktaa he or nahin bhi, sandarbhit karti he. praathmik taur par parivartit manodasha, vyavhaar, evam maansik kshamta ke anusaar lakshan bhi hoten hai. is avasthaa kaa ilaaj aamtaur par aahaar main parivartanon ke dwaara kiya jaataa he jisaki seemaa saadhaaran se vyapak or vistaarit bhi hoti he. logon ko is avasthaa ke prabandhan kaa paraamarsh dena vaikalpik aushadhi kaa kendravindu he.
