 munisuvratnath yaa munisuvrat jain dharm ke 20 wein tirthankar maane gaye hai. unke pita kaa naam sumitra or mata kaa naam padyavati tha. ye bhagwaan ram ke samakaleen maane gayi hai.u nakaa janm rajgrih or nirvana sanmedshikhar par hua tha. kachuva unka chihn bataaya gaya he. unke samay main 9wein chakravarti mahapadya kaa janm hua jo vishnukumaar mahapadya ke chote bhai the. aage chalakar vishnukumaar muni jaindharm ke maha uddharak hue. muni suvratnath ke samay main hi ram naam ke 8wein vasudev or ravan naam ke 8wein baldev, lakshmana naam ke 8wein prativasudev kaa janm hua.
