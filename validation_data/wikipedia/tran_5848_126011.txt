 airavateshwar mandir, dravid vaastukala kaa ek hindu mandir he jo dakshini bharat ke tamilnaadu rajya main kumbhakonam ke paas darasuram main sthit he. 12veen sadi main rajraja chol dwitiya dwaara nirmit is mandir ko thanjavur ke brihadishwar mandir tathaa gangeyakonda cholapuram ke gangeyakondacholiswaram mandir ke saath unesco dwaara vaishvik dharohar sthal banaaya gaya he; in mandiron ko mahaan jeevant chol mandiron ke rup main janaa jaataa he.
 airavateshwar mandir bhagwaan shiv ko samarpit he. shiv ko yahaan airavateshwar ke rup main janaa jaataa he kyonki is mandir main devataaon ke raja indra ke safed hathi aravat dwaara bhagwaan shiv kii puja kii gai thi. aisa mana jaataa he ki airavat rishi durvasa ke shraap ke kaaran apana rang badal jane se bahut dukhi tha, usne is mandir ke pavitra jal main snaan karke apana rang punah praapt kiya. mandir ke bhitari kaksh main bani ek chhavi jisamen airavat par indra baithe hai, ke kaaran is dharna ko mana jaataa he. is ghatana se hi mandir or yahaan asin ishtdev kaa naam padaa.
 kaha jaataa he ki mrityu ke raja yama ne bhi yahan shiv kii puja kii thi. parampara ke anusaar yama, jo kisi rishi ke shaap ke kaaran puure sharir kii jalan se peedit the, airavateshwar bhagwaan dwaara thik kar die gaye. yama ne pavitra talab main snaan kiya or apni jalan se chhutkaaraa paaya. tab se us talab ko yamatirtham ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
 yah mandir kala or sthapaty kala kaa bhandar he or isamen pattharon par shandaar nakkashi dekhne ko milti he. haalanki yah mandir brihadishwar mandir yaa gangeyakondacholiswaram mandir se bahut chota he, kintu vistaar main adhik uttam he. aisa isliye he kyonki kaha jaataa he ki yah mandir nity-vinod, "satat manoranjan, ko dhyaan main rakhkar banaaya gaya tha.
 vimaana 24 meter unchaa he. saamne ke mandapam kaa dakshini bhaag pathar ke bade pahiyon vaale ek vishaal rath ke rup main he jise ghoden dwaara khinchaa ja rahaa he.
 bhitari angan ke purv main behtareen nakkashidaar imaaraton kaa ek samooh sthit he jinamein se ek ko balipit kaha jaataa he. balipit kii kursii par ek chota mandir bana he jisamen ganesh ji kii chhavi ankit he. chaowkee ke dakshini taraf shandaar nakkashi se yukt 3 sidhiyon kaa ek samooh he. charnon par prahaar karne se vibhinn sangeet dhvaniyaan utpann hoti hai.
 angan ke dakshin-pashchimi kone main 4 tirth vaala ek mandapam he. inamen se ek par yama kii chhavi bani he. is mandir ke aasapaas ek vishaal pathar kii shila he jis par saptamaataaon kii akritiyaan bani hai.
 mukhya devtaa kii patni periya nayaki amman kaa ek alag mandir he jo airavateshwar mandir ke uttar main sthit he. sambhav he jab bahari angan puraa rahaa ho to yah mukhya mandir kaa hi ek hissaa rahaa ho. vartmaan samay main, yah ek alag mandir ke rup main akelaa khadaa he jiske bade angan main devi kaa mandir bana he.
 is mandir main vibhinn shilaalekh hai. in lekhon main se ek main kulotunga chol tritiy dwaara mandiron kaa naveekaran karaae jane kaa pataa chalata he.
 baraamde kii uttari diwaar par shilalekhon ke 108 khand hai, inamen se prayek main shivacharya  ke naam, varnan va chhaviyaan bani he jo unke jivan kii mukhya ghatnaon ko darshaati hai.
 gopura ke paas ek anya shilaalekh se pataa chalata he ki ek akruti kalyani se laayi gai thi, jise baad main rajaadhiraj chol pratham dwaara kalyanpura naam diya gaya, pashchimi chalukya raja someshwar pratham se uski haar ke baad unke putr vikramaditya shashtham or someshnar dwitiya ne chalukyon kii rajdhani par kabja kar liya.
 is mandir ko varsh 2004 main mahaan chol jeevant mandiron kii suchi main shaamil kiya gaya. mahaan chol jeevant mandiron kii suchi main thanjavur kaa brihadishwar mandir, gangeyakonda cholapuram kaa gangeyakondacholiswaram mandir or darasuram kaa airavateshwar mandir shaamil hai. in sabhi mandiron ko 10 veen or 12 veen sadi ke bich cholon dwaara banaaya gaya tha or iname bahut si samanthaayen hai.
