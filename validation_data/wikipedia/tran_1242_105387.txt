 aadhunik kerala kii aarthik vyavastha main apravasi keraliyon kaa mukhya yogadan he. kerala kii aarthik vyavastha ke sudruth aadhaar hai - vaanijya bank, sahakaari bank, mudra vinimay vyavastha, yatayaat kaa vikaas, shiksha - swasthya aadi kshetron main hui pragati, shaktishaalee shramik aandolan, sahakaari aandolan aadi.

 malayalam kaa sahitya aath shatabdiyon se adhik purana he. kintu aaj tak aisa koi granth praapt nahin hua he jo yahan ke sahitya kii praarambhik dasha par prakaash daalata ho. atah malayalam saahitiyik udgam se sambandhit koi spasht dharna nahin milti he. anumaan he ki praarambhik kaal main lok sahitya kaa prachlan rahaa hoga. aisi koi rachana uplabdh nahin jisaki rachana 1000 varsh pehle kii gai he. dasvin sadi ke uparant likhe gaye anek granthon kii pramanikta ko lekar bhi vidwaan ekamat nahin hai. keraly sahitya se saamanytah malayalam sahitya arth liya jaataa he. lekin malayalam sahityakaron kaa tamil or sanskrit bhashaa vikaas main mahatvapurn yogadan rahaa he. kerala ke kuch vidwaanon ne angrezi, kannada, tulu, konkani, hindi aadi bhashaaon main bhi rachana likhi hai.

 kerala ke adhikansh logon kii maatrubhaasha malayalam he, jo dravid parivaar kii pramukh bhashaa he. yahan arya, arabi, yahudee tathaa mishrit vansh ke log bhi rahete hai. doosraa pramukh varg adivaasiyon kaa he. ye sabhi varg milakar aadhunik keraly samaaj kaa nirmaan karte hai. ek rajya ke rup main kerala kii sthapana 1961 se hi hui. kintu vartmaan kerala ke antargat jitne pradesh hai un pradeshon kii janaganana 1881 main hi taiyaar ho pai. pramaanon ke aadhaar par yah vishwaas kiya jaataa he ki 17 veen sadi ke prarambh main kerala kii jansankhya lagbhag 30 lakh thi. 1850 main yah 45 lakh ho gai. 1881 se jansankhya lagaataar badhati rahi. 1901 main jansankhya 64 lakh thi jo 1991 main 291 lakh ho gai. 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar kerala kii jansankhya 31841374 he.
