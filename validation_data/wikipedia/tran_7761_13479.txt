 masini bhashaa computer kii aadharbhut bhashaa he, yah keval 0 or 1 do anko ke prayog se nirmit shrinkhalaa se likhi jaati he. yah ekmaatr computer programing bhashaa he jo ki computer dwaara sidhe-sidhe samajhi jaati he. ise kisi anuvadak program kaa prayog nahi karnaa hota he. ise computer kaa masini sanket bhi kaha jaataa he.
 computer kaa paripath is prakaar taiyaar kiya jaataa he ki yah masini bhashaa ko turant pahchaan letaa he or ise vidhut sanketo me parivartit kar letaa he. vidhut sanketo kii do avasthaae hoti he- hai or lo athwa Anticlock wise & clock wise, 1 kaa arth he Pulse athwa High tathaa 0 kaa arth he No Pulse yaa low.
 masini bhashaa me prayek nirdesh ke do bhaag hote he- pehla kriya sanket or doosraa sthiti sanket . kriya sanket computer ko yah bataata jaataa he ki kya karnaa he or sthiti sanket yah bataata he ki aakde kahaan se praapt karnaa he, kahaan sangrahit karnaa he athwa anya koi nirdesh jiskaa kii dakshata se paalan kiya janaa he. 
 masini bhashaa me likhaa gaya program computer dwaara atyant shighrata se karyanvit ho jaataa he. iskaa mukhya kaaran yah he ki masini bhashaa me die gaye nirdesh computer sidhe sidhe bina kisi anuvadak ke samaj letaa he or anupaalan kar detaa he.
  
 masini bhashaa me program likhnaa ek kathin or atyadhik samay lagane vaala kaary he. isiliye vartmaan samay me masini bhashaa me program likhane kaa kaary naganya he.
