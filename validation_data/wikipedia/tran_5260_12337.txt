 lunglei zila bhartiya rajya mizoram ke aath zilon main se ek he. bharat kii janaganana 2011 ke anusaar yah mizoram kaa aizol zile ke baad doosraa sabase zyada jansankhya vaala zila he.
 lunglei zile kaa naam iske mukhyalay lunglei ke naam par padaa he. kabhi-kabhi mizo bhashaa main lunglei ko lungleh bolaa jaataa he, jiskaa arth chattaanon kaa pul hota he.
 yah zila uttar main mamit tathaa aizol zile se, pashchim main bangladesh se, dakshin main longtlai zile se, dakshin-purv main saiha zile se, purv main myanmar tathaa uttar purv main serchhip zile se ghira he. is zile kaa kshetrafal 4358 varg kimi he. lunglei kasbaa is zile kaa mukhyalay he.
 is zile main teen sambhaag hnahathial, lunglei tathaa tlabung hai. zile main 7 vidhansabha kshetra dakshin tuipui, lunglei uttar, lunglei purv, lunglei pashchim, lunglei dakshin, thorang tathaa pashchim tuipui hai.
 2011 janaganana ke anusaar lunglei zile kii jansankhya 161,428 he, jisame 82,891 purush tathaa 78,537 mahilaayein hai. is zile kii jansankhya lagbhag saint loosia raashtra ke baraabar he. jansankhya ke anusaar bharat ke 640 zilon main iskaa 597vaan sthaan he. zile kaa jansankhya ghanatv 36 prayek varg kilometre main nivasee he. 2001-11 ke madhya dashakeey jansankhya vruddhi dar 17.64% rahi. zile kaa linganupaat dar 947 mahila prati 1000 purush he tathaa sakshartaa dar 88.86% he.
 yahan kaa pramukh bahusankhyak dharm isai he, jo ki kul jansankhya kaa 78.75% he. anya alpsankhyak dharm buddha 17.06%, hindu 3.24%, muslim 0.80%, jain 0.06% tathaa sikh 0.02% hai. 0.04% logon ne apane dharm kaa ullekh nahin kiya he.
