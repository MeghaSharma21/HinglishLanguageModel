 ardshir​ pratham yaa ardshir yakum iran ke saasaani samrajya kaa sansthaapak or pratham samraat tha. yah pehle dakshini faras praant ke istakr​ kshetra kaa shaasha hua karata tha jisane parthi samrajya ke patan ke saath apana samrajya banaaya. yah saasaani samrajya 400 saalon tak chalaa or iskaa patan tab hua jab arab muslimon ke rashidun kilaafat​ ne 651 isvi main ise haraakar khatm kar diya.
 'ardshir' shabd madhya farsi ke 'ardyasher​', parthi bhashaa ke 'artanshgar​' or pahalavi bhashaa ke 'arthstra' shabdon se sambandhit he, jinka arth he 'vah jiskaa rajya divya vyavastha ho'. kyonki sanskrit or farsi donon hind-irani bhashaa-parivaar kii bahane hai, is se milte-julate sajaateey shabd sanskrit main bhi milte hai: 'rut kshetra' .
