 guyana shield, dakshin america plate ke teen cretano main se ek he. yah 1.7 arab saal puraani ek purvacambrian bhugolic sanrachana he jiskaa vistaar dakshin america ke purvottar main he or yah dakshin america ke uttari tat ke ek bhaag kii rachana karti he. shield ke oonchaai vaale hisson ko guyana adhityaka kaha jaataa he or isi adhityaka main anuthe or rahasyamayi, pathari parvat tepui paye jate hai or yah vishwa ke kuch sabase unche or adbhut jalprapaaton jaise ki angil jalprapaat kaa ghar he.
 guyana shield par guyana, surinam or french guyana sthit hai, iske saath columbia, venezuela or brazil kaa kuch hissaa bhi isi shield par sthit he.
