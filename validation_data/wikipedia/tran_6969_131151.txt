 sarkari vyavathaaon main dwisadaniyata us vidhi ko kehete hai jisamen vidhayika main do sadan hon. udhaaharan ke liye bhartiya sansad main do sadan hai: lok sabha or rajya sabha. iske vipareet philippino jaise kuch deshon main ekasadaneey sansaden hai.
 yah pranali bharat, britain, soviet sangh, america aadi deshon main prachlit he. iske vipareet china, nepal, checkoslovakia aadi kuch aise desh hai, jahaan vidhayika main ek sadan hi he. aisi vyavastha ko ek sadaneey pranali kaha jaataa he. madhyakaalin vidhayikaon main kahin-kahin teen, chaar yaa paanch sadan hote the jimame prayek sadan samaaj ke kisi varg yaa jaagir kaa pratinidhitiv karata tha.
