 gurukul shiksha paddati ---
 gurukul purnatah awasaiya shiksha pradaan karne kaa ekant sthaan hota tha.jahaan 08-10varsh kii aayu me baccha guru ke sanidhya me rah kar visham halaato me rah kar vidya adhyayan karne kii parampara hamaare yaha tha. 
 bachche guru ke nirdesho kaa purnatah paalan karte the.
 gurukul me bachche swayan dincharya ke sabhi kaary karte the.
 gurukul me bachche apane upyog kii samagri kaa nirmaan swayan karte the. 
 baccho apane khaane kii vyavastha swayan kheti yaa bhikshatan se karte the. 
 guru kul me shruti arthath sun kar yaad karte he lipibaddh karne kaa parampara nahi thi.
 upyukt parampara rahaa he. vartmaan me aise shiksha pranali ko puure vishwa ko aavashyaktaa he.
