 abandhan yaa swechha vyapaar aisi aarthik niti yaa vyavastha ko kehete he jisamen nijee sansthaaon ke bich len-dein ko karmukt tathaa anya sarkari pratibandhon se mukt rakhaa jaataa he. sarkari niyam keval itane hote hai taaki chori or jabarajasti hadapney ke viruddh sampatti sambandhi adhikaron kii raksha ho sake. lacephere french bhashaa kaa vankyansh he jiskaa arth 'karne do' hota he. vidwaanon kaa vichaar he ki 'swechha vyapaar' vaala rajya kabhi bhi astitv main nahin rahaa he.
 abandhan siddhant kaa pratipaadan roodhiwaadee arthshaastriyon dwaara kiya gaya tha. unka vishwaas tha ki yadi rajvyavastha ne janata ke aarthik nirnay or abhiruchiyon main hastkshep kiya, to vyakti apane icchaanusaar vastuon kii maatra or gun kaa utpaadan na kar sakenge, falat: kalyaan adhiktam na ho paaegaa. isliye arthshaastriyon ne prashasan ko raksha tathaa desh main shantisthapana aadi praarambhik kartavyon tak hi simit rakhana chaha or rajya kii niti aisi nirdhaarit kii ki rajdhikari samaaj ke aarthik jivan main hastkshep na kar saken.
 is siddhant ne kaaphi samay tak aarthik vyavastha par apana prabhaav banaae rakhaa. kintu samay parivartan ke saath isaki kaarividhi main anek dosha paae gaye. pratham to yah dekha gaya ki aarthik vyavastha sarkaar dwaara pathpradarshan ke abhaav main kisi niti athwa dishavishesh kaa anusaran nahin karti jiske kaaran isamen anek saamaajik or aarthik kamajoriyaan aa jaati hai. ivibhaajan main vishmata aa jaati he tathaa desh ke utpattisadhano kaa purnat: prayog nahin ho paata. dwitiya, aniyantrit bazaar arthvyavastha ke kaaran prajatantreey rajya kii samjik aavashyaktaayen puri nahin ho saktin. tritiy, swechha vyapaar ke antargat desh ke niryaat vyapaar ko protsaahan nahin miltaa, adhik unnat deshon kii audyogic spardhaa ke kaaran desh ke niryaat udyog viksit nahin ho pate. chaturth, is prakaar kii aarthik vyavastha ke antargat aarthik shoshan badhata jaataa he tathaa shramik varg aarthik, saamaajik evam raajnneetik visham kaa shikaar bana rahata he. ant main yah siddhant yadhyapi vyaktigat swatantraaa pradaan karata he tathaapi saamaajik swatantraaa se sambandh nahin rakh paata.
 aaj ke raajnneetik tathaa aarthik vichaarak swechha vyapaar ke siddhant ko vyaktigat arthvyavastha main utana hi apurna maanate hai jitna niojit arthvyavastha ko swechha vyapaar ke ansh ke bina. arthur lavis ke anusaar shat pratishat morganirdhaaran utana hi asambhav he jitna shat pratishat swechha vyapaar. aadhunik kaal main sabhi deshon kii arthvyavasthaaon main, aarthik niyojan main swechha vyapaar ke siddhanton kaa aanshik samavesh avashya hota he.
