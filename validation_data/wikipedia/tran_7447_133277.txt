 baichung bhutia sikkim-bhutia vansh ke ek sevanivrutt bhartiya footbal khilaadi hai jo striker ke rup main khelte the. bhutia ko antarrashtriya str par bhartiya footbal kaa mashaaldaar mana jaataa he. footbal main unki shooting kaushal kii vajah se unhen aksar sikkimi snipper naam diya jaataa he. suprasiddh bhartiya khilaadi aai em vijayan ne bhutia ko "bhartiya footbal ke liye bhagwaan kaa uphaar" bataaya. bhaichung bhutia ke bade bhai kaa naam chevang bhutia hai, chevang or bhaichung donon boarding skool gaye. bhutia kaa 2004 main ek hotel peshewar se vivaah hua or 2014 main unka talaaq ho gaya.
 bhutia ne ai-lig footbal team east bengal club main apana kariyar shuru kiya. jab unhone 1999 main english club bari main shaamil hue, vah europea club ke saath anubandh par hastakshar karne vaale pehle bhartiya footbal khilaadi bane or mohammad saleem ke baad europe main peshewar rup se khilne vaale khilaadi bane. baad main malaysian footbal club pero egge ke liye khele wo bhi udhaar pe. is ke saath hi wo jesiti mills ke liye khele,jo unke kaarykaal ke dauraan ek baar lig jeeta; or mohan bagan, jo apane mul bharat main unki do karyavadhi ke dauraan ek baar lig jeetne main naakaam rahe the. unke antarrashtriya footbal sammaan main nehru cap, alji cap, eseefef championships teen baar or eeffsi chalange cap jeetna shaamil he. vah bharat ke sabase zyada topi paane vaale khilaadi baar hai, unke naam par 104 antarrashtriya cap hai, or nehru cap 2009 main unhone apni 100 veen antarrashtriya cap praapt kii.
 maidan se baahar, bhutia, televijan kaarykram jhalak dikhalaa ja ko jeetne ke liye jane jate hai, jisane uske baad unke tab ke club mohan bagan ke saath bahut vivaad paidaa kiya tha or wo pehle bhartiya khilaadi the jinhone tibeti swatantraaa andolan ke samarthan main olympic mashaal riley kaa bahishkaar kiya. bhartiya footbal main apane yogadan ke sammaan main bhutia ke naam par ek footbal stadium he, unhone arjuna puraskaar or padam shri jaise kai puraskaar bhi jite hai.
 oktober 2010 main, unhone carlos quiuroj or niki dwaara footbal ke saath saajhedaari main dilli main baichung bhutia footbal skool kii sthapana kii. agast 2011 main, bhutia ne antarrashtriya footbal se apni sevanivritti kii ghoshnaa kii unka vidaai match 10 janvari 2012 ko dilli kii jawaharlal nehru stadium main byrn munik ke kilaaf bharat kii rashtriya team ke saath tha.
 bhaichung bhutia kaa janm 15 disambar 1976 ko sikkim ke tinkitam main hua tha. footbal ke alaava, bhutia ne bhi apane skool kaa pratinidhitiv badminton, basketbaal or athletics main kiya hai. unke do bade bhai hai, chevang or bom bom bhutia or kaili naam kii ek choti bahan hai. unke mata-pita, jo peshey se kisaan the, ko shuru shuru main unka khelnaa pasand nahin tha. unke pita kii mrityu hone ke baad unke chachaa, karm bhutia se protsaahan ke baad unhone saint jevirs skool, paakajong, east sikkim main apni shiksha shuru kar di or nau saal kii umr main unhone gangtok main tashi namagyal academy main bhaag lene ke liye bhartiya khel praadhikaran se ek footbal chaatravrutti jiti.
 unhone apane grih rajya sikkim main kai skulon or sthaneey klbon ke liye khelnaa shuru kar diya tha, jisamen gangtok sthit boy club shaamil tha, jo karma dwaara chalaaya jaataa tha. 1992 ke subroto cap main unka pradarshan, jahaan unhone "best player" puraskaar bhi jeeta, unhen footbal pratishthan ke dhyaan main laayaa. purv bharat ke golkeeper bhaskar ganguli ne unki pratibha dekhi or unhen kolkata footbal main shaamil hone main madad kii.
 1993 main, solah varsh kii aayu main, unhone peshewar east bengal epa.si., kalakatta main shaamil hone ke liye skool chhod diya. do saal baad, unako jesiti mills, fagawada ko sthanantarit kar diya or us team ne 1996-97 sizan main india neshnal footbal lig jiti. lig main bhutia sheersh golkeeper the, or unhen nehru cap main unke antarrashtriya padaarpan ke liye chunaa gaya tha. unhone "1996 bhartiya player of da year" kaa kitaab apane naam kiya.
 1997 main, vah east bengal epa.si. main laute. bhutia ko east bengal or mohan bagan ke bich sthaneey darbi main pehali hetric lagane kaa gaurav praapt hua,or saath hi unki team 1997 ke federation cap semifinal main mohan bagan par 4-1 se jeet darj karne kii. vah 1998-99 ke satr main team ke captain bane, jiske dauraan east bengal lig main salgaonkar ke baad duusare sthaan par rahaa. iske alaava, 1999 main arjuna puraskaar praapt karne vaale vah 19veen footballer ban gaye, jo bharat sarkaar rashtriya khelon main utkrisht uplabdhiyon hasil karne vaale athleton ko diya jaataa hai.
 "unki maujuudagi footbal ko yuvaao ke bich main ek bada badhaava hogi"
 bhutia ko videshon main khilne ke liye simit avasar mili hai. 30 sitambar 1999 ko, unhone england ke greater manchester main bari ke liye khilne ke liye videshon kii yatra kii. mohammad saleem ke baad vah europe main peshewar rup se khilne vaale duusare bhartiya footbal khilaadi ban gaye. teen saal kaa qaraar karke vah europea club ke liye sin in karne vaale pehle bhartiya footbal khilaadi bhi ban gaye. iske baad bhutia phulaham, west bramwich albian or eston vila ke pareekshanon main asafal rahe. shuru main unhen vija praapt karne main kathinaayee hui parantu aakhirkaar unki shruwaat cardiff city ke kilaaf 3 oktober 1999 main hui. us match main, vah ian lawson ke vikalp ke rup main aae or unhone bari ke duusare gol main hissaa liya, jo derren bulak dwaara kiya gaya tha, uske baad bhutia kii woli ne footbal ko unke paas pahunchaa diya. 15 april 2000 ko, unhone apana pehla gol chesterfield ke kilaaf english lig main daaga. ek aavarti ghutane kii chot ne unhen bari main apane antim satr main keval teen game tak simit kar diya tha, or club ke prashasan ke baad main unhen sevanivruth kar diya. bari ke liye aakhri match 27 agast 2001 ko swindan toun ke kilaaf khelaa tha, jisamen swindan toun kii 3-0 se haar hui thi.
 2002 main, vah bharat laut aae or ek varsh ke liye mohan bagan ke liye khele. haalanki, vah kaaphi had tak asafal rahe kyonki bhutia mausam kii shuruyaat main ghayal ho gaye the or us sijan main phir se khilne main naakaam rahe, jisase unhone mohan bagan kii ekmaatr trophy jeet main shaamil hone kaa mauqaa ganvaa diya. iske baad, vah phir se east bengal club laut aae, jisase east bengal club ko asian club championships jeetne main madad mili.
  bhutia ne final main ek gol daaga, or unki team kii tiro saasana par 3-1 se jeet hui, or unhen "main of da match" kaa kitaab mila. unhone championships main nau gol karke sheersh scorer kaa kitaab apane naam kar liya. bhutia ne petrocemia putr ke kilaaf 1-1 se drow match main bhi gol kiya or isi toornament main philippine army ke kilaaf 6-0 se jeet darj kii jisamen se paanch gol unke the.
 unhone agast se oktober 2003 tak pero egge ke saath bantiyaan pe chale gaye or niyamit sizan ke liye east bengal club laut aae. haalanki, pero egge main unka kaarykaal kaa ant malaysia cap ke semifinal main sabaa egge se 3-1 se haar ke saath hua. 2003-04 ke satr main, bhutia ne 12 gol kiye or east bengal ne duusare sthaan par rahe dempo par chaar ankon kii badhat ke saath lig jiti. 2004-05 ke sizan ke dauraan, bhutia ne east bengal ke liye 9 gol kiye, jo essi goa or champion dempo ke peeche tisare sthaan par rahe. 2005-06 ke sijan ke ant tak unhone purvi bengal ke liye khelnaa jari rakhaa. apane antim satr main unhen akhil bhartiya footbal mahasang ne ek satr main "neshnal footbal lig kaa player" se sammanit kiya, jisamen unhone 12 gol kiye. iske baavjood, east bengal lig main upavijeta hi rahe.
 2005 main, bhutia ne ek or malaysian club, selegar emke land ke liye hastakshar kiye. club kii tangahaali kii vajah unhone keval paanch baar match khele or ek gol daaga. isse pehle, unhen home united ke mainager steve darbi se ek ofer mila, lekin unhone prastaav ko asweekaar kar diya. baad main darbi ne yah khulaasa kiya ki ve bhutia ko saath laane main isliye asafal rahe, kyonki unhone jo peshkash kii thi wo us samay bharat main jo unhen mil rahaa tha usase kam tha.
 "main mohan bagan samarthakon kii ummiido par khara utarane kii koshish karunga or is baar team ko jeet dilaane kii puri koshish karunga"
 15 june 2006 ko, vah mohan bagan se jud gaye or unhone jose ramirez barato ke saath ek akramak saajhedaari kii shuruwat kii. haalanki, 2006-07 kaa satr bhutia or mohan bagan ke liye kharaab tha, kyonki ve lig main aathven sthaan par rahe the, nishkasana se ek qadam dur. 2007-08 sizan ke dauraan, bhutia ne 18 maichon main 10 gol kiye or mohan bagan ne chauthe sthaan ke saath lig main thodaa behatar pradarshan kiya. bhutia ne 2008 main duusari baar bhartiya khilaadi kaa khitaab jeeta tha. puraskaar jeetne main, vah ek baar se adhik baar jeetne vaale vah keval duusare footbal khilaadi ban gaye; pehle hai aai em vijayan. 2008-09 ke mausam main, lagaataar 10-match jeetne ke baavjood, mohan bagan ne churchill bros ke peeche doosraa sthaan hasil kiya kyonki mahindra united ke saath aakhri match main haar gaye. bhutia ne is sijan main chah gol kiye.
 18 may 2009 ko, bhutia ne ghoshnaa kii ki vah club ke adhikariyon dwaara footbal kii patibaddhta kii poochtacha ke kaaran mohan bagan ko chhod denge. jhaal dikhalaa ja kii ghatana ke parinamswaroop, unhen mohan bagan dwaara chah maheene ke liye nilambit kar diya gaya tha. bhutia ne kaha, "mohan bagan main mujhe ek or sizan main rakhane ke liye sirf ek chaal he, lekin main unke liye or nahin kheloongaa".
 2011 main bhutia ne united sikkim main coach va menager bane.
