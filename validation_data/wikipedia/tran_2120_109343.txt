 mahaan samraat maharaja mahapadmanand jo ek nyaay  shaasha tha jinhone shashan me kshatriya kul ke ekadhikaar ko khatm kiya. inke 10 putr nimna likhit he:-
 chandra nand joki mahaan samraat maharaja mahapadmanand ke dasven putr the jinaki maa mura mahapadmanand kii duusari patni thi aage jake chandranand chandragupta maury ho gaya chandranand ke naam se nand hataa kar chanakya jiskaa doosraa naam vishnu gupt kaa gupt jod kar chandragupta kiya or chandragupta kii maan ke naam mura ke badale maury rakhaa aage jake apane sautele bhai ghanaanand ko jo ek bura shaasha tha use maar kar maury vansh kii nim rakhi.
 inamen se ghanaanand nauvaan putr tha. jo nandavansh kaa aage chalakar uttaradhikari bana.
 jise itihaas main bahut hi croor, aattaayi tathaa nirankush shaasha bataaya gaya he. alag-alag itihaas car apane apane pruthak tark se inke kushasan va sushasan kaal kaa varnan karte he nandavansh nyaay shaasha dwaara sthapit rajvansh tha.
 ghananand ke raaj darbar me upyog me laayi jane vaali ganikaayen adhikanshat: brahman va kshatriya hoti thi. at: in karano se bhi use krur shaasha bataaya gaya.ghananand kaal me buddha dharm apni charam seemaa me tha, at: bahut si kitabon me ghananand ek swatantra va aadhunik bodh dharm se prerit bataaya gaya he.
