 sanchayak, urja sanchit karanevaala upkaran he. dravijiniyari me dravchaalit sampidak tathaa utthapak ko shakti pradaan karne ke liye, ek prakaar kaa sanchayak hota he, jiske urdhwadhar belan main majjak baari bhaar se bhaarit rahata he. belan main pani, jo bharayukt majjak uthaa detaa he, pump dwaara bhar diya jaataa he. bharayukt majjak kii kriya ke kaaran ucchdaab par pani tivrata se visarjeet hota he, jisase yantron ko chalaane ke liye dravchaalit shakti praapt hoti he. sanchayak alpakaal ke liye bade parinaam main shakti sanbharit karata he or iskaa bharan nimna shaktivaale pump se ho saktaa he. jal-vidyutsakti pranali main sanchayak sanyantr ke rup main duusare prakaar ke sanchayak kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. britain main sanchayak bydaree ko bhi sanchayak kehete hai.
