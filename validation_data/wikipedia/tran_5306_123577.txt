 narendra jha ek bhartiya abhinayta the. yah kai dharawahik or filmon main abhinay kiya. unhone havan main hari om bapji, hamane lee he shapath main asipi karanvir, theft of baghdad main sheershak kirdaar or iske alaava unhone captain house, jai hanuman amrapalli ravan aadi dharawahik main kaam kiya. unhone film haidar, shorgul, rais main bhi kaam kiya. 14 march 2018 ko narendra jha kaa 55 varsh kii aayu main nidhan ho gaya. narendra jha ke nidhan kii vajah cardiyak atake ko bataaya gaya.
