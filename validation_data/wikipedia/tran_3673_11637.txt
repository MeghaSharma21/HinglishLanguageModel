 bhimsen thapa, nepal ke mukhtiyaar 1806 se 1837 tak
 bhimsen thapa nepalke sarvaadhik lambe samay takke mukhtiyaar hai. bhandarkhaal parvake baadamein unhone saare ucch adhikariyonki hatya karte hue thapa vanshaki udai karaayaa. 
 
 bhimsen thapaka janm vi.san. 1832 salme gorkhaaka borlabko pipal thok gavame bagaale thapa kshetri parivarame huwa tha. amarsingh thapa or satyarupa mayaaka jeshth betaa the. 
 
 pita sardaar amarsingh or mata satyarupa ke jyeshtha putr ke rupamen unka janm vikram samvat 1832 main hua.
