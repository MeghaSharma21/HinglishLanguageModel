 avadharana 	antarrashtriya str par kiye gaye vibhinn adhyayanon se yah spost ho chukaa he ki duniyaan main har sau logon ke bich 3 jan maansik vimandit hai. bharat main yah aankada 4 pratishat tak mana jaataa he. bharat main in vimanditon hetu anek staron par shikshan, prashikshan or punarvaas ke liye kaary kiye ja rahe hai. yah kaary sarkari or gaiyr sarkari str par sanchalit hai. vimanditon kii sankhya ko dekhte hue yah kaary or prayaas paryaapt nahin hai. yahi kaaran he ki safalataa or uplabdhi kaa pratishat santoshjanak nahin he. agar yah mana jaae ki ham shat pratishat uplabdhi paa sakate hai. phir bhi ham ise apni safalataa nahin kah sakate, kyonki duusari ore vimanditon kii sankhya apni nirdhaarit dar se lagaataar bad rahi he.	'shubhada' samarthit avadharana yah he ki jab ham maansik viklaangata ke adhikansh kaarnon se avgat hai to kyon na apane sansadhano or kshamta kaa ek nishchit bhaag isi ke nivaaran main lagaaya jaae. agar ham maansik vimandita kii barason se sthir chali aa rahi dar ko kam kar saken to shikshan, prashikshan or punarvaas ke liye kiye ja rahe kaary kaa boj kam ho saktaa he. yah bhi sambhav he ki ek din ham chechak, malaria or polio kii tarah is samasya par bhi puri tarah ankush lagane main kaamyaab hon jaaen or vimanditon ke punarvaas ke kaary kii jarurat hi na rahe.	upyukt baton ko sweekaar karte hue 'shubhada' ne ek suraksha kaarykram taiyaar kiya he. samudaay ke sahyog se garbhvatee mahilaon kii swasthya or poshan sambandhi behatar dekhbhaal, phir surakshit prasav or baad main shishu kii suraksha se yah aasaani se sambhav he. aasaani se isliye kyon ki is kshetra main sarkari or gaiyr sarkari kshetra main anek kaarykram pehle se sanchalit he. aavashyaktaa he, usamen behatar talmail baithaana or maansik vimandita ke masale ko pramukhataa se najar main rakhana.	mata ke garbh main aakar pranvan hone ke tatkaal baad se hi bhrun ko bina kisi ling bhedbhaav ke surakshit rup se jivit rahane, vikaas or suraksha praapt karne kaa adhikaar mil jaataa he. aise main unki shaareerik, maansik, sangyanatmak, bhaavnaatmak or saamaajik vikaas kii jaruraton va adhikaron ke liye yah suraksha kaarykram apekshit he.	samaaj main yah samaj viksit honi chahie ki bachapan se hi nahin, garbhakaal se sahi vikaas hi samooche vikaas kaa aadhaar he.	garbhakaal ke jivan kaa shuruuaati samay shaareerik or maansik swasthya or poshan ke liye bahut mahatvapurn hota he, is suraksha kaarykram kaa sarvaadhik jor isi baat par he.	garbhvatee mata or uski kokh main pal rahaa ek or jivan kuposhan, rugnataa evam isse paidaa hone vaali viklaangata or mrityu kaa shikaar ho jate hai. is kaarykram se unhen suraksha pradaan kii jaati he.	yah suraksha kaarykram garbhvatee mahilaon or chote bacchon kii swasthya sambandhi behatar dekhbhaal or ant main shiksha sevaaon tak unki pahunch banane kaa samanvit najariya uplabdh karaata he.	yah vishesh rup se garib samudaay kii garbhvatee mahilaon or bacchon kaa swayan unheen ke vaataavaran or apane hi parichit logon ke bich sarvangin vikaas kaa ek suraksha kaarykram he. is kaarykram ke dwaara kam aay varg vaale saadhan viheen varg tak suvidhaon kii uplabdhta or samaj pahunchaana pramukh lakshya he.	'shubhada' kii manyata he ki prasav se purv kii behatar dekhbhaal va taiyaari, prasav ke samay kii saavdhaaniyaan va prasav ke baad kii suraksha se maansik viklaangata kii dar par ankush lagaaya ja saktaa he.
 uddeshy: 1. garbhvatee mahilaon or prasav ke baad unke teen varsh tak ke bacchon ke maansik or shaareerik poshan, swasthya suraksha.2. garbhakaal se hi bachche kii uchit maansik, shaareerik or saamaajik vikaas kii neenv rakhana.3. maansik viklaangata dar, matra evam shishu mrityu dar, ruganta or kuposhan main kami laana.4. uchit shaareerik va maansik swasthya tathaa poshan sambandhi shiksha ke madhyam se bacchon kii saamaanya va vishesh aavashyaktaaon kii dekhbhaal va bharan-poshan ke liye abhibhavakon kii saamaajik or aarthik kshamta badhanaa.5. garbhvatee mahilaon hetu aavashyak iodine kii poorti sunishchit karnaa.6. prasav se purv kii taiyaariyaan, prasav ke samay kii saavdhaaniyaan or prasav ke baad jaccha-baccha kii dekhbhaal sunishchit karnaa.7. navjaat shishu ko janm ke baad mata kaa pratham doodh pilwane kii vyavastha sunishchit karnaa.8. chah maah tak ke bacchon ko keval mata kaa doodh pilaane kii vyavastha sunishchit karnaa.9. bacchon ke vruddhi vikaas par samaybaddh nigaraanee rakhana va sabhi prakaar ke tike samay par laganaa sunishchit karnaa.10. bacchon ke shaareerik va maansik swasthya, poshan, jivan-kaushal tathaa shiksha sambandhi suvidhaayen pradaan karnaa.11. teen varsh kaa hone par bachche ke vidhyalay pravesh kii vyavastha karana.12. bal vivaah jaisi kuritiyon ke bure prabhaav se avgat karana or abhibhavakon ko jaagruk karnaa.13. surakshit prasav kii sankhya main badhotaree karnaa.14. samudaay kii sahaayta or vikaas ke liye vibhinn sarkari vibhaagon or gaiyr sarkari sansthaaon dwaara pradutt sevaaon kaa prachaar kar jaruratmandon tak inkaa laabh pahunchane main sahaayta karnaa. 15. sarkari va gaiyr sarkari kshetra main mahila evam bal vikaas va poshan ko badhaava dene ke liye shiksha, swasthya, saamaajik nyaay evam adhikarita, aarthik utthan tathaa shaareerik or maansik viklaangata se jude shikshan, prashikshan, poshan va punarvaas kaaryakramon main juty sansthaaon va vibhaagon ke kaarykram lagu karne main aavashyak samanvay sthapit karnaa. 'shubhada' suraksha kaarykram kaa mahatv:'shubhada' suraksha kaarykram kaa rajasthan rajya main matra, shishu va bal mrityu dar niyantrit karne or shaareerik, maansik viklaangata kii dar par ankush lagane main atyadhik mahatv he. isi ke saath jaruratmand pariwaron ko swasthya jaanch, tikaakaran, poshan or swasthya shiksha, saamaajik or aarthik suraksha, sandarbh sevaayen, aupchaarik shiksha va vibhinn sarkari va gaiyr sarkari kshetra kii sevaaon ke bich samanvay sthapit kar vibhinn kalyankari yojanaon kaa laabh unke vaastavik jaruratmandon tak pahunchane main 'shubhadaÓ suraksha kaarykram kii mahatvapurn bhoomika he.
 'shubhada' kaaryakarta kaa swaroop:1. 'shubhada' kaaryakarta ek sevaabhaavi stri yaa purush he, jisane swechha se kaary karnaa sweekaar kiya he.2. 'shubhada' kaaryakarta sambandhit sarkari vibhaagon or gaiyr sarkari sansthaaon dwaara pradat sevaaon ke bich samanvay sthapit kar sabhi tak inkaa laabh pahunchaate hai. 3.'shubhada' kaaryakarta samudaay ko shiksha, swasthya va poshan sahit saamaajik or aarthik suraksha ke bare main uchit paraamarsh dete han or samajopayogi baton ko paryaapt prabhavi dhang se samaaj ko samajhaate hai. 4. 'shubhada' kaaryakarta samudaay main shiksha, swasthya, poshan, viklaangata nivaaran tathaa saamaajik or aathak suraksha va punarvaas ke kshetra main uchit netritv pradaan karte hai. 5. samudaay ko is baat kii naveenatam jaankaree uplabdh karaate hai ki kis sarkari vibhaag yaa gaiyr sarkari sanstha kii ore se samudaay ke hit main kya-kya sevaayen uplabdh karai ja rahi hai or unhen kaise praapt kiya ja saktaa he.shiksha, swasthya or poshan se jude jo achche kaary or vyavhaar ho rahe hai, 'shubhada' kaaryakarta unki bharpoor saraahna, prachaar or sahyog karte hai. jo uchit kaary nahin ho rahe hai yaa galat ho rahe hai, unke vishay main sambandhit vibhaag yaa sanstha ko uchit paraamarsh dete hai.
 samanvay: shiksha- sarv shiksha abhiyaan ke tahat chal rahe vibhinn kaarykram.swasthya- rashtriya gramin swasthya mishan ke tahat 'jajani suraksha yojanaÓ main aashaa kaaryaktaaon ke sahyog se chikitsaalay main surakshit prasav evam nagad sahaayta. praathmik swasthya kendon se jude e.en.em. yaa el.ech.ve. dwaara pradutt swasthya sevaayen tathaa tikaakaran suvidhaayen.poshan- mahila evam bal vikaas vibhaag kii or se sanchalit samekit bal vikaas seva kaarykram dwaara poshan shiksha or poorak poshaahar ke liye rajasthan ke sabhi jilon main vartmaan main lagbhag 36 hajaar anganbad kendron ke madhyam se bahut bada or mahatvapurn kaarykram sanchalit he. jald hi 6 hajaar nae kendra khole jane kii yojana he. in kendron main karyrat anganbari kaaryakarta poshan ke saath hi swasthya jaanch, tikaakaran jaisi sevaaon main bhi sahaayak hai. isi ke saath rajasthan sarkaar kii ore se 'mid-de-mileÓ yojana bhi bade paimaane par chalane vaali yojana he.suraksha- saamaajik or aarthik suraksha ke liye kendra or rajya sarkaar dwaara saamaajik nyaay evam adhikarita vibhaag kii ore chal rahi vibhinn yojanaon ke tahat sanchalit kaaryakramon ke dwaara samudaay ko anek suvidhaayen mil rahi hai. aarthik suraksha ke liye khaadi gramodyog, jila udyog kendra, ruda tathaa vibhinn bainkon ke saath hi samay-samay par lagu hone vaali kendra yaa rajya sarkari kii rojgaar yojanaayen labhaarthi ke liye sahaayak hoti hai. samanvay: shiksha- unicef, krai, vibha.swasthya- unicef, liance club, rotary club. poshan- akshay-paatr, uttarafeeds, bengali baba.
