 kazakhstan ne pehali baar olympic khelon main 1994 main ek swatantra raashtra ke rup main bhaag liya, or tab se athleton ne har game main pratispardhaa karne ke liye bheja he. soviet sangh ke vighatan se pehle, kazakh athleton ne olympic main soviet sangh ke hisse ke rup main hissaa liya, or 1992 main uniphid team kaa bhi hissaa tha.
 kazakh athleton ne greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main kul 60 padak or sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main 7 anya jite hai.
 kazakhstan kii rashtriya olympic samiti 1990 main banaai gai thi or 1993 main manyata praapt hui thi.
