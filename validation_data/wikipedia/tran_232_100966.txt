 inuit greenland, canada, sanyukt rajya america or rus ke sudur uttar main sthit barfile arctic ilaaqon main basane vaali kuch maanav jatiyon kaa samuhik naam he. inuiton main bahut si bhinn upjatiyaan hai lekin in sabhi main aapasi samantaayein or sambandh hai. 'inuit' ek bahuvachan shabd he or ek vyakti ko 'inuk' kaha jaataa he. puraane jamaane main inuit logon ko 'esquimau' bulaayaa jaataa tha lekin bahut se inuit log ise apmanjanak shabd maanate hai isliye ab is jaati ko aamtaur par 'inuit' hi kaha jaataa he. 'inuit' shabd kaa arth 'log' he.
 pichley himyug ke dauraan samudron kaa bahut sa pani barf ke rup main jamaa hua hone se samudr-tal aaj se neeche tha. aadhunik yug main asia ke sudur purvottar ke siberia kshetra or uttar america ke sudur pashchimottar alaska kshetra ke bich samudr he. himyug ke dauraan samudr-tal neeche hone se in donon ke bich ek borinzia naamak jamini ilaaka tha jo ab samudr ke pani ke neeche he. aadhunik inuiton ke purvaj asia se chale or unhone 1000 isapurv ke aasapaas borinzia paar kiya or uttari america mahadweep main aa pahunche. vahaan pashchimi alaska main unhone ek samaaj banaaya jise itihaaskaar thul samskruti kehete hai. yahan se ve purv kii ore uttari canada ke puure arctic ilaake main fail gaye. vahaan thulon se pehle hi ek jati basii hui thi jinke samaaj ko itihaaskaar 'dorset samskruti' ke naam se bulaate hai. yah dorset jati thulon ke failaav ke aage tik nahin pai or vilupt ho gai. doreseton kaa varnan aadhunik inuit mithy-kathaon main miltaa he, jisamen unhen ek 'bhimkaay' yaa 'daanveey' jati kaha jaataa he. is se itihaaskaar andaaza lagate hai ki dorset log kad-but main zarur thulon se bade rahe honge.
 puure uttari canada ke barfile ilaaqon par failane ke baad, in thulon ke vanshaj inuit 1300 isvi tak greenland par bhi pahunchakar bas chuke the. inaki samskruti or takneekein puri tarah is barf-dhake ilaake ke anukool ban chuki thim. inase dakshin main rahane vaali jatiyaan inke kshetron par kabhi bhi sthai niyantran nahin kar pai or na hi inuit apane se dakshin main sthit up-arctic ilaaqon ke kam thande kshetron par fail paae. phir bhi ve apane dakshini padosiyon ke saath vyapaar karte the or unase ladaai-jhadpen hoti rahati thim. isi 1000-1300 isvi kaal main inkaa sampark uttari europe kii viking jati se bhi hua. 17veen sadi ke baad jab europe se log aakar uttar america main basane lage or rus siberia main vistrut hua to uske baad se inuiton kaa europiyon se sampark lagaataar badhata gaya.
