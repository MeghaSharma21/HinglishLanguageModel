 arekhiy tantra ve tantra hai jo rekhiy nahi hai. arthath ve tantra jo adhyaropan siddhanth ke sharton ko santusht nahin karte, unhen arekhiy kaha jaataa he.
 bhautikashastriyon evam engeeron ko arekhiy tantron main ruchi isaliye he ki adhikansh tantra multah arekhiy hi hote hai. yaa, adhikansh tantra keval simit daayre main hi rekhiy hote hai, sampoorn daayre main nahin. iske alaava bahut se kaaryon ke liye arekhiy tantra hi adhik upyukt hote hai, na ki rekhiy.
