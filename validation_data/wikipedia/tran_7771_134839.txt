 kizil kum yaa qizil qum madhya asia main sthit ek registan he. iskaa kshetrafal 2,98,000 varg kimi he or yah duniya kaa 11vaan sabase bada registan he. yah aamu dariyaa or sir dariyaa ke bich ke doaab main sthit he. iskaa adhikansh hissaa kazakhstan or uzbekistan main aata he, haalanki ek chota bhaag turkmenistan main bhi padataa he. turki bhashaaon main 'kizil kum' kaa matlab 'laal ret' he or is registan kii reton main mishrit padaarth bahut se sthanon main ise ek laalimaa dete hai. is se dakshin-pashchim main aamu dariyaa ke paar karakum registan padataa he jiske naam kaa arth 'kaali ret' he.
 kizil kum kaa adhiktar ilaaka lagbhag 300 meter kii oonchaai par sthit maidani kshetra he haalanki kuch jagahon par oonchaaiyaan or droniyan hai. adhiktar jagahon par retile tile hai, jinhen sthaneey bhashaa main 'barchhan' yaa 'barkhaan' kehete hai. pashchimottar main sakht mitti kii moti paraten hai jinhen 'takir' kehete hai or jo kuch-kuch namak ke maidan jaisi hai. kizil kum main aabaadi nadiyon or nachlistaanon ke kinare milti he. garmiyon main taapmaana bahut unchaa ja saktaa he or aamu dariyaa ke kinare kii keraki naamak basti main july 1983 main 51.7 degree centegrade kaa taapmaana dekha gaya tha.
 kizil kum main bahut se khanij hai, jaise ki sona, urenium, tamba, aluminium, chaandi, prakritik gas or petrol. yahan ki murunatau sone kii khan mashhoor he jo 1970 ke dashak main shuru hui thi. yahan kaa ilaaka bahut vishaal or khaali he or jagah-jagah par jhaadi or junglee ghaasein hai, isliye yahan ke sthaneey log maveshi paalan main bhi jute hue hai. navoi, zarafashaan shahar or uchakuduk shahar udyog ke mukhya centre hai.
