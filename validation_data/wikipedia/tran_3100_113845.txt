 rail vikaas nigam limited kii sthapana kii sweekruti bharat sarkaar dwaara 19.12.2002 ko pradaan kar di gai thi or yah company adhinium, 1956 ke adheen company ke rup main 24 janvari 2003 ko panjikrut ho gai thi. yah 100% kendriya sarkaar ke swaamitv vaala saarvajanik kshetra kaa upkram he. mantrimandal kii sweekruti ke anusaar, revinili kaa srijan nimnlikhit prayojanon or adhidesh ke liye kiya gaya he-
 1. revinili ek saarvajanik kshetra kaa upkram he jisaki equity anya piesu, bank, vittiya sansthaanon athwa samrik bhagidaaron ko di ja sakati he.
 2. revinili kii bhoomika ek devlaper kii he, jo gaiyr-bajtiy vittaposhan, vittiya sansadhano kii vyavastha or pariyojna kriyanvayan ke liye pariyojna kaa vikaas karti he. pariyojna ko vishesh vittiya vyavastha ke adheen gadiyon ke parichaalan or anurakshan ke liye relon ko pradaan kiya jaataa he.
 3. kanoonee sthiti ke sandarbh main revinili rail adhinium, 1989 ke adheen ek railway prashasan he.
 4. revinili ek chhatra espivi he, jo pariyojna kaa vikaas or sansadhano kii vyavastha karti he or kisi vishisht pariyojna ke liye upyukt hone par pariyojnaon ko sidhe athwa pariyojna vishisht espivi kaa srijan karke athwa koi anya vittiya sanrachana ko taiyaar karke puraa kar sakati he.
 5. revinili rashtriya rail vikaas yojana ke adheen aane vaali svarnim chaturbhuj or anya bank grahya pariyojnaon ke kshamta vistaar sambandhi kaary karegi. is kaarykram main svarnim chaturbhuj or isaki bhujaaen, antim mile pattan sampark or pashy kshetra galiyaaron kii kshamta vistaar sambandhi pariyojnaayen shaamil hai.
 6. revinili teen adhikariyon kii tainaati ke saath agast, 2003 main shuru hui thi. nideshak mandal ke padon kii sweekruti kii prakriya saath-saath chalati rahi or march, 2005 main nideshak mandal dwaara apana kaary sanbhaalane ke pashchaat revinili puri tarah se chaalu ho gai thi.
