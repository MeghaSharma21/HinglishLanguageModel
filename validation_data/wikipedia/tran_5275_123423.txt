 kosi nadi yaa kosi nadi nepal main himalay se nikalati he or bihar main bhim nagar ke raste se bharat main daakhil hoti he. isamen aane vaali barh se bihar membehut tabaahi hoti he jisase is nadi ko 'bihar kaa abhishaap' kaha jaataa he.
 iske bhugolic swaroop ko dekhen to pataa chalegaa ki pichley 250 varshon main 120 kimi kaa vistaar kar chuki he. himalay kii unchi pahadiyon se tarah tarah se avsaad apane saath laati hui ye nadi nirantar apane kshetra failaati ja rahi he. uttari bihar ke maidani ilaakon ko tarati ye nadi puraa kshetra upjau banaati he. nepal or bharat donon hi desh is nadi par band bana chuke hai; haalaanki kuch paryaavarnavidon ne isse nuksaan kii bhi sambhaavna jataayi thi.
 yah nadi uttar bihar ke mithila kshetra kii samskruti kaa paalna bhi he. kosi ke aasapaas ke kshetron ko isi ke naam par kosi kaha jaataa he.
 hindu granthon main ise kushiki naam se uddhrut kiya gaya he. kaha jaataa he ki vishwamitra ne isi nadi ke kinare rishi kaa darza paaya tha. ve kushik rishi ke shishy the or unhen rugveda main kowshik bhi kaha gaya he. saat dhaaraon se milakar saptakoshi nadi banati he jise sthaneey rup se kosi kaha jaataa he. mahabharat main bhi iskaa zikr kushiki naam se miltaa he.
 kathmandu se everest kii chadhaai ke liye jane vaale raste main kosi kii chaar sahaayak nadiyaan milti hai. tibet kii seemaa se laga naama bazaar kosi ke pahari raste kaa paryaton ke hisaab se sabase aakarshak sthaan he. arun, tamor, likhu, doodhkosi, tamakosi, sunakosi, indravati isaki pramukh sahaayak nadiyaan hai.
 nepal main yah kanchanajangha ke pashchim main padati he. nepal ke harakapur main kosi kii do sahaayak nadiyaan doodhkosi tathaa sanakosi milti hai. sanakosi, arun or tamar nadiyon ke saath triveni main milti hai. iske baad nadi ko saptakoshi kaha jaataa he. barahakshetra main yah tarai kshetra main pravesh karti he or iske baad se ise kosi kaha jaataa he. isaki sahaayak nadiyaan everest ke chaaron ore se aakar milti hai or yah vishwa ke oonchaai par sthit glaciaron ke jal leti hai. triveni ke paas nadi ke veg se ek khadd banaati he jo koi 10 kilometre lambi he. bhimnagar ke nikat yah bhartiya seemaa main daakhil hoti he. iske baad dakshin kii ore 260 kimi chalakar kursela ke paas ganga main mil jaati he.
 kosi nadi par san 1958 evam 1962 ke bich ek band banaaya gaya. yah band bharat-nepal seemaa ke paas nepal main sthit he. isamen pani ke bahaav ke niyantran ke liye 52 dwaar bane hai jinhen niyantrit karne kaa kaary bharat ke adhikari karte hai. is band ke thodaa aage bhartiya seemaa main bharat ne tatabandh banaaye hai.
