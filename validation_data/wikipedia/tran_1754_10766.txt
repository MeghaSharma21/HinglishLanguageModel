 nirdeshaank: 21°55′52″N 86°43′39″E / 21.9312°N 86.7276°E / 21.9312; 86.7276yah baripada naam kaa khubasurat nagar mayurbhanj jila kaa mukhyalay he. is sthaan ko ghume bina urisa aane vaale paryatakon kii yatra puri nahin maani jaati he. simplipal pahadiyon ke tal par sthit is sthaan se mayurbhanj or aasapaas ke sabhi paryaton sthalon tak pahunchaa ja saktaa he. yahaan ek pracheen kile or jagannatha mandir ke avshesh dekhe ja sakate hai.
 baripada kii sthiti 21°55′52″N 86°43′39″E / 21.9312°N 86.7276°E / 21.9312; 86.7276 par he. yahaan kii ausat oonchai 36 meter .
 purvavati shaskaon kaa yah shikaar sthal project tiger ke antargat shaamil kiya gaya he. 1956 main iskaa chayan aadhikarik rup se tiger rigerv ke liye kiya gaya tha. baripada se 60 kilometre dur sthit yah park 2277.07 varg kilometre ke kshetrafal main failaa hua he. ghaney jangalon, jharanon or pahadiyon se samrudh is park main vividh vanyajeevon ko najadik se dekha ja saktaa he. tigar, hiran, hathi or anya bahut se jeev is park main mulat: paae jate hai.
 baripada se 150 kilometre dur sthit khiching nagar apane pracheen mandiron ke liye janaa jaataa he. ek samay main yah sthaan bhanja shaskaon kii rajdhani thi. devi chamunda ko samarpit yahaan kaa mandir pramukh or lokapriy darshneey sthal he. yah mandir pathar kii anokhi murtiyon ke liye prasiddh he. chaulakunj or biratgarh yahaan ke anya charchit sthal hai. chaulakunj vishaal stambhon or biratgarh sangrahalay ke liye janaa jaataa he.
 yah mandir bahalda main sthit he jo 14veen shatabdi main mayurbhanj samrajya kii rajdhani thi. apni khubsuurati ke liye prasiddh devi kichkeshwari kaa yah mandir baripada se 16 kilometre kii duuri par he.
 khakhrua vaidyanath ke mandir ke liye prasiddh manatri baripada se 36 kilometre kii duuri par he. bhagwaan shiv ko samarpit yah mandir vaastukala kaa ek uttam udhaaharan he. mandir kii deevaaron par mayurbhanj raajaaon ke abhilekh oriya bhashaa main khude hue hai. mandir ke pashchim main ek pracheen kila kshatigrast avasthaa main dekha ja saktaa he.
 baripada sthit is mandir kaa nirmaan 1863 se 1867 ke bich raja shri shrinath bhanjadeva kii dekharekh main kiya gaya tha. bhagwaan jagannatha kaa yah mandir yahaan ke sthaneey logon ke bich kaaphi lokapriy he.
 yah pavitra or khubasurat sthal baripada se 65 or baleshwar se 110 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. yahaan kaa jalprapaat or ek pahari par sthit ambika mandir yahaan ke lokapriy darshneey sthal hai.
 yah tirthasthan karanjia se 40 kilometre dur he or yahaan ek pavitra kunda he. mana jaataa he ki bhim ne is kunda main snaan kiya tha, isi kaaran ise bhimkund kaha jaataa he. janvari maah main makar sakranti parv yahaan badi dhoomdhaam se manaaya jaataa he. iske aasapaas kii sundarataa dekhne ke liye logon kaa yahaan aana-janaa laga rahata he.
