 jumatul vidaa muslim pavitra maheene ramadan ke antim jume ke din ko kehete hai. yun to ramadan kaa puraa maheenaa rozon ke kaaran apana mahatv rakhata he or jume ke din kaa vishesh rup se dopaher ke samay namaaz ke kaaran apana mahatv he, chunki saptaah kaa yah din is pavitra maheene ke ant main aa rahaa hota he, isliye log ise ati-mahatvapurn maanate hai.
 
 ramadan ke aakiri jume ke mauqe par masjidon main dopaher ko jumatul vidaa kii namaaz adaa kii jaati he. is vishesh namaaz se pehle masjidon ke pesh imam jumatul-vidaa kaa khuzabaa padhate hai or namaaz ke baad aman or kushahaali kii duaen maangi jaati hai. bharat main adhiktar dargahon se judi kai ek masjiden hai, isliye log vahaan par bhi namaaz padhate hai.
