 burhan mujaffar vaani, kashmiri ugravadi gut kaa ek commander tha. jisko 08july 2016 ko encounter karke maar giraaya. burhan vaani ke alaava inke 7 or aatankwaadi sadasyon ko maar giraaya he.
 burhan kaa janm jammu or kashmir ke pulavama kshetra ke traal gaanv main 19 sitambar 1994 main hua tha, jabaki uski mrityu encounter ke dauraan 08 july 2016 ko hui. 
 hijbul mujahiddeen ke sheersh commander burhan kii mrityu ke baad ab tak jammu or kashmir main 51 logon kii jane ja chuki he. inke alaava lagbhag 400 log ghayal ho chuke hai. is maamle main pakistan ne raajnneeti khelte huye ninda kii. afvaah ko rokane ke liye jammu or kashmir main internet seva ko bhi band karwaanaa padaa. bharat ke pramukh vipakshi dal kii netaa sonia gandhi ne aatankwaad kii ninda karte hue kashmir main jari hinsa par bhi khed vyakt kiya he. burhan vaani kii barsi ke samay hi islamic aatankwaadi sanghathan lashkar-e-toyba ne amanath yatra par akraman kar 7 shraddhaluon ko maar diya. ye akraman 10 july 2017 ko kiya gaya tha.
