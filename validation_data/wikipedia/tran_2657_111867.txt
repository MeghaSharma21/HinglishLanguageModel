 semra gajipur jile kaa ek bada gaanv he. semra ke saamne ganga paar rampur gaanv sthit he. in donon gaanvon ko sthaneey log sanyukt rup se rampur-semra kehete hai. semra gaanv mohammadabad tehsil ke sherpur gram panchayat ke antargat aata he. is gaanv kii aabaadi takreeban 5500 he jabaki matadaataaon kii sankhya 3800 he.
 yah gaanv jila mukhyalay se 30 kimi kii duuri par purab disha main sthit he. yahan se najdiki railway stesan usufpur 8 kimi kii duuri par he. jabaki gajipur-baliya rashtriya rajmarg 19 par based muhammadabad kasbe se is gaanv kii duuri 5 kimi he. semra, sherpur gram panchayat kaa ek hissaa he. vyavahaarat: shiv raay kaa pura or chhanabaiya bhi semra ke hi ang hai. 
 yah gaanv ganga ke kinare basaa he or do dashakon se ganga kataan se buree tarah trast he. is kataan ke chalate shiv raay kaa pura kaa 80 pratishat hissaa or semra kaa 50 pratishat bhaag ganga main samahit ho chukaa he. sarkaar dwaara gaanv ke astitv ko bachaane ke liye kuch asthaayee vyavastha kii gayi he lekin aaj bhi kataan kaa khatra semra va shiv raay kaa pura par mandra rahaa he.
 gaanv ke logon kaa mukhya vsavsaay kheti he or aalu kii kheti yahan bade paimaane par kii jaati he.
