 kanvalji akhet nishidd kshetra bharat ke rajasthan praant main savai madhopur ke savai maansingh abhayaarany se sata hua he, yahan par shikaar karnaa pratibandhit he. is kshetra ke antargat bhagwaan shiv kaa pavitra mandir sthit he jo ki 'kamaleshwar' yaa kanvalji ke naam se janaa jaataa he. kanvalji akhet nishiddh kshetra kaa vaanik mukhyalay savai madhopur jile ke ranthambhore neshnal park se sanchalit hota he.
