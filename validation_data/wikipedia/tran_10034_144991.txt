 badvani zile kii sthapana 25 may 1998 ko hui. purv main yah zila khargon jile kaa ek bhaag tha. badvani zila madhya pradesh ke dakshin pashchim main sthit he, narmada nadi isaki uttari seemaa banaati he. jile ke dakshin main satapura evam uttar main vindhyachal parvatashreniyan he.
 badvani naam kii utpatti bad ke van se hui he, jinase shahar puraane samay main ghira hua tha. vaani shabd baghiche ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he, isaliye shahar ko badvani naam se janaa jaataa he.
 shahar badvani 1948 se pehle badvani rajya kii rajdhani tha. yah chota-sa rajya apni chattani ilaakon or kam utpaadak bhoomi ke kaaran angrez, mughalon or maraathon ke shashan se bachaa rahaa.
 shahar badvani purv main bad nagar or siddh nagar ke naam sein bhi janaa jaataa tha. jila badvani jain tirth yatra centre chulgiri or baavngaja ke liye bhi mashhoor he.
 badvani kaa ek aitihaasik pratik he jo tirgola ke naam sein janaa jaataa he. yah khandwa-baroda maarg par sagar vilaas pales ke saamne sthit he, or raja ranjit singh ke divangat bete kii yaad main banaaya gaya tha.
 aazaadee se pehle badvani shahar 'nimad ke paris' ke rup main janaa jaataa tha. ucch shiksha ke liye yahaan par shahid bhima nayak shaskeey mahavidhyalay sthit he.
 jila badvani 21 ansh 37 minutes - 22 ansh 22 minutes akshansh se 74 ansh 27 minutes - 75 ansh 30 minutes deshaansh ke bich failaa he. is jile ke dakshin main maharashtra rajya, pashchim main gujraj rajya, purv main jila khargon tathaa uttar main jila dhar he. jila pashchim main ucchtam bindu ke saath aakaar main trikoniya he.
 shahar badvani narmada nadi ke dakshin main sthit he.
 badvani kii jansankhya 1,385,881 dashak main 27.57% kii vruddhi ke saath he.
 nikatatam hawaii adda zile se lagbhag 150 ki.me. kii duuri par indaur shahar main sthit hai. jahaan se bharat ke pramukh shaharon jaise: nai dilli, mumbai, chennai, kolkata, bangalore, hyderabad aadi tathaa antarrashtriya shaharon ke liye pramukh hawaii sevaayen uplabdh he.
 badvani main pashchim railway kaa aarakshan counter ambedkar park, rajghat road par sthit he. nikatatam railway stesan lagbhag 150 ki.me. kii duuri par indaur shahar main sthit hai, jo pashchimi railway ke mukhya vanijyik railway steshnon main se ek he. badvani ke nazadik madhya railway kaa railway stesan lagbhag 180 ki.me. dur khandawa main hai, jo badvani ke saath rajya rajmarg kramank-26 ke dwaara judaa hua he.
 badvani kaaphi acchhee tarah se madhya pradesh or bharat ke anya bhaagon se rashtriya or rajya rajmargon dwaara judaa hua he. badvani shahar khandawa-vadodara rajya rajmarg kramank-26 dwaara va 45 ki.me. duuri par sthit gram julwania main agra-mumbai rashtriya rajmarg kramank-03 se judaa hua he. zila mukhyalay sein pramukh shahar indaur, devas, ujjain, bhopal, dhar, ratlam, khargon, khandawa, ahmedabad, vadodara or mumbai tak niyamit yatri bas seva uplabdh hai.
