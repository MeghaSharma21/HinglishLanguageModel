 greenland denmark rajshahi ke adheen ek swytt ghatak desh he, jo arctic or atlantic mahaasaagar ke bich canada arctic dweepasmooh ke purv main sthit he. haalanki bhugolic rup se yah uttar america mahadweep kaa ek hissaa he, lekin 18 veen sadi ke baad se europe se raajnneetik rup se judaa hua he.
 1979 main denmark ne greenland ko swashaasan pradaan kar kiya or 2008 main greenland ne sthaneey sarkaar ko adhik dakshata hastantaran ke paksh main mat diya. jo agle varsh prabhavi ban gai, iske saath hi danish shahi sarkaar keval videshi maamalon, suraksha or aarthik niti tak hi simit rah gai.
 greenland kshetrafal ke hisaab se duniya kaa sabase bada dweep he, jo apane aap main ek mahadweep nahin he. iske alaava yah duniya kaa sabase viral jansankhya vaala desh he. lekin 1950 ke dashak ke baad se vaigyanikon kii yah pariklpana he ki yah desh vastav main teen alag-alag dwipon se bana he, jo jo glacier ke madhyam se ek-duusare se jude hue hai. 
 greenland denmark ke danish kabje vaale kshetra he, lekin greenland europea sangh kaa sadsy nahin he. yah uttar america mahadweep kaa hissaa he, or greenland australia or antarctic ko chhodkar duniya kaa sabase bada dweep he, jo mahadweep hai. greenland ke pradhan mantri kim kilson hai.
 dweep zyaadaatar inuit or scandinavian dwaara aabaadi main he jo greenlandic, esquimau-alet bhashaa bolte hai. adhikansh logon dwaara danish bhi boli jaati he.
 1 janvari, 2008 tak, 218 log greenland main rahete he.
