 jaranel singh bhindranvaale bhartiya punjab main sikhon ke dharmik samooh damdami taksaal kaa pramukh leader tha. unhone anandpur sahib prastaav kaa samarthan kiya.
 unhone sikhon ko shuddh hone ke liye kaha. unhone sharaab pine, nashe karne, dharmik kaamon main laaparvaahee or sikh naujavaanon ko kesh kaatane kii nishiddhta kii. unhone bhartiya samvidhan ke anuchhed 25 kii sakht ninda kii jiske anusaar sikh, jain or buddha dharm ke anuyaayi ko kam sankhya kaha gaya or hindu dharm kaa ek hissaa kaha gaya.
 agast 1982 main bhindranvaale or akali dal ne 'dharm yuddh morcha' shuru kiya. iskaa uddeshy anandpur prasaav main vyakt kiye gaye uddeshyon ko paanaa tha.
