 1990 ke dashak main sholapur shahar main muslim marathi sahitya parishad kii neenv rakhi gai. jiskaa makasad hindu or musalamaano ke bich sampradayik sauhaard ko badhaana tha. 1960 ke dashak main dalit sahitya kaa pravaah sahitya main nai ummide lekar aayaa. pichhade samudaay ke dabe kuchle logon ne apane prashno ko lekar likhte aa rahe the. isi pravaah ko aage badhaate hue marathi musalamaano ne kshetreey bhashaa main likhnaa shuru kiya. par unka likhaa mukhya pravaah ke sahitya patrika ne chhapane se nakaar diya. har baar unka likhaa ve patrika lautaa deto. jisase vyathit hokar kuch longo ne apni khudki ek sahitya parishad kii zarurat mahasoos kii. jise aage jaakar muslim marathi sahitya parishad kaa naam diya gaya. 1990 ke dashak main unhone muslim marathi sahitya parishad kii neenv rakhi. jiskaa makasad hindu or musalamaano ke bich sampradayik sauhaard ko badhaana tha. 
 1980 ke baad maharashtra main shri. bennoor, pha. m. shahajinde, javed pasha, baba mohammad ataar, ajij nadaaf aadi lekhak-kaviyo nen marathi main musalamaano ke samasya tathaa prashno ko marathi sahitya main laayaa. in sab kaa kahena ek hi tha ki, mukhya pravaah ke marathi sahitya main musalamaano kii koi dakhal kyon nahi lee ja rahi hai? marathi sahitya main musalamaano kaa sirf nakaaratmak chithran kyon kiya ja rahaa hai? muslim virodh se bhari ‘calonial history’ kaa varchasv is sahitya main paaya ja rahaa tha, jisko naye se pesh karne ke liye fakruddin bennoor or unke sahyogiyone muslim marathi sahitya parishad kii buniyaad rakhi.
 80 ke dashak main aar. si. mujumdaar, ke. em. munshi se lekar go. sa. saradesai aadi itihaaskaar or anya marathi vichaarco par prabhaav tha. prabhaav kehane ke bajaae ‘calonial history’ ko hi yah log apane sanshodhan ke jariye pesh kar rahe the. tisri baat sabase mahatvapurn yah ho rahi thi ki, pichhade or haashiye par dhakele gayi jati-janajati ke andolan joro par the, unka sahitya bade paimaane par nikal kar aa rahi tha. sholapur main is halaat par vimarsh ho rahaa tha. 1981-82 ke baad se dango kii rajaniti shuru ho chukin thi. 1969 main dango kii lahar aakar chali gai thi. dange bhadkaye ja rahe thea. musalamaano ke gunhegaar sabit kar katghare main khada kiya ja rahaa tha. in bhadkeele bayaanbaji ke changul main funskar musalamaano se hinsak ghatnaaye ghatit ho rahi thi. is baat ko kahi na kahi rekhaankit kar muslim samudaay ko astitv bhi spasht karnaa jaruri tha.
 shahbaano maamle ke baad musalamaano ke dharmaguru kaa bartaav or unki bate galat rukh apana rahi thi. in sab ke vajah se musalmaan sabase zyada musiibat main funs chunkaa tha. sangh parivaar nen babari masjid kii rajaniti apane haath main le lee thi. videshi babar ke naam se yahan ke vansh or saanskrithik drushticon se bhartiya mitti ke musalmaan ko pratadit kiya ja rahaa tha. unka shatrukaran shuru ho chunkaa tha. jisko todne ke liye muslim marathi sahitya kaa andolan shuru kiya gaya.
 12veen sadi ke baad se kareeb 42 muslim santon ne marathi main saahitiyik rachanaaen kin. aazaadee ke baad ke daur main marxvaadi or ambedakarwaadi andolanon se hamid dalwai jaise marathi lekhak paidaa hue, jinhone tripal talaak jaisi saamaajik buraaiyon ke khilaaf andolan chhedaa. 1970-80 ke dashak main ambedakarwaadi andolan se prabhavit hokar muslim lekhakon ne sawaal poochne shuru kiye. nateeja ye hua ki 1990 main sholapur main pehla akhil bhartiya muslim marathi sahitya sammelan yojit kiya gaya. abtak ek hajaar se zyada muslim lekhak marathi main rachanaaen likh rahe hai. aaj muslim marathi lekhat muslim samudaay ke mudde jaise ashiksha, berogari, bahuvivaah or bacchon ke adhikaar kii baaten karte hai. inkaa sahitya hamen tarakkeepsand samaaj kii taraf le chal rahaa he. ye dharmik sahitya nahin he. muslim lekhikaayen jaise julfi shaikh, farjaana daange, hasina mullah, fatima mujawar or naseema deshmukh bhi marathi main likh rahi hai. in sab ke sahitya kii theme streevad he.
