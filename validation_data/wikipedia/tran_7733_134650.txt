 uranium ayask bhandar prithvi ke garbh main sthit sandra uranium he, jo aarthik rup se unnati ke liye nikaala jaataa he. uranium dharti ke garbh main paaya jane vaala bahut aam tatv he, jo chaandi se 40 guna or sone se paanch sau guna adhik paaya jaataa he. yah lagbhag har pahaad, mitti, nadiyon or mahasaagaron main paaya ja saktaa he. lekin kathinaayee keval un ilaakon ko gyaat karne kii he, jahaan yah sandra avasthaa main paaya jaataa he. 
 uranium ek chaandi or bhure rang kaa radiodharmee rasinic tatv he. iskaa rasinic chihn "U" or parmaanu kramank 92 he. 
 uranium ayask ke kai saare alag alag bhandar banate hai, jo bhugarbheey parivartan or pahaado ke rasinic abhikriya or uranium tatv ke kaaran hota he. iske utpatti main mul kaarak vah khanij, uske oxikaran kii kshamta aadi he.
