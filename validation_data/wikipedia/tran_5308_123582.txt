 kamalanath sharma jalavigyaan, sinchai tathaa jal-nikaas, evam jal-vidyut abhiyantriki ke antararashtriya visheshgy, vaidic granthon main jalavigyaan, paryaavaran aadi vishyon ke lekhak, sahityakar, tathaa hindi ke janemaane vyangy lekhak or kahanikar hai. 
 jalavigyaan, jal-vidyut abhiyantriki va vishwa khadyann main ullekhneey yogadan ke atirikt keenderma ne vedon, upanishdon aadi vaidic vaanmay main jal, paryaavaran, paristhitiki aadi par bhi shodh karke prachurtaa se likhaa he. hindi sahitya main saath ke dashak se kamalanath ke naam se unke vyangy tathaa kahaniyaan bhi desh kii vibhinn patrikaon main chhapate rahe hai. 
 apane kaarykaal ke dauraan aapane vishwa ke lagbhag sabhi deshon kii yatra kii, vahaan ke sinchai, jalanikaas, jalavigyaan aadi ke vikaas main yogadan diya tathaa anek antarrashtriya sansthaaon ke kaaryakramon tathaa pariyojnaon se sambhddha rahe.
 march 1946 main jaipur ke pratishthit pravasi andhra-parivaar main inkaa janm hua. ve sanskrit ke yug purush kavishiromani bhatta mathuranath shastri ke kanishtha putr evam suprasiddh vidwaan devarshi kalanath shastri ke anuj hai. inhone jodhpur vishvavidhyalay, jodhpur ke ium.bi.ium. ejineering college se pratham shrenee main civil ejineering main bii or birla institute of technology and science, pilaani se pratham shrenee main hydrolic strecchars main emai kii degree praapt kii. 
 aap 1969 se 1981 tak malviya naishanal institute of technology, jaipur ke civil ejineering vibhaag main sahaayak profesar rahe.
 1981 se 1989 tak aap bharat sarkaar ke udhyam anachapisi limited main jalavigyaan or jal sansaadhan vibhaag ke adhyaksh ke rup main desh kii anek band pariyojnaon ke dizine, karyanvayan tathaa nirmaan se sambhddha rahe.
 aapane 1989 se 2010 tak 110 deshon kii sadsyta vaale international kamishan on irigation and drenez ke nai dilli sthit mukhyalay main sevaayen deen tathaa march 2010 main ukt antararashtriya ayog ke sachiv ke pad se sevanivrutt hue. 
 samprati aap jalsambandhi vaidic gyaan ko samarpit “equavizdam” naamak sanstha ke chairman hai tathaa bharat main jalavigyaan, band evam jal-vidyut pariyojnaon ke liye takneekee visheshgy/ salaahkaar ke rup main sevaayen de rahe hai.

 kamalanath ke vyangy tathaa kahaniyaan 1960 ke dashak se desh kii vibhinn pratishthit patrikaon main prakashit hote aarahe hai. bahut si i-patrikaon main bhi aapke vyangy va kahaniyaan prakashit hote hai tathaa unki kai rachanaayen gadhykoshon main sankalit hai . inkaa ek vyangy sangrah “sahitya kaa dhwani tatva urf saahitiyik big baing” ayan prakaashan, nai dilli dwaara 2015 main prakashit hua he. inke ek anya kahani sangrah "bhunrya mo" ko online gaatha, lakhanu ne 2015 main hi i-book tathaa paperbaque rup main prakashit kiya he.unka ek or vyangy sangrah "moorakh to ekahi bhalo" bhi i-book tathaa paperbaque rup main 2016 main online gaatha, lakhanu ne hi prakashit kiya he.. varsh 2016 main inaki chaar kahaniyaan "samooh-12" dwaara "kahani kaa samay" sheershak se prakashit katha-sangrah main bhi shaamil hai!
