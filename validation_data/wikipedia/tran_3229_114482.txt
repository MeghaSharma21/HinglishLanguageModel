 aisi kai takneekein hai, jinka upyog ek vyapaar vishleshak us samay karata he jab vyapaar main hone vaale parivartanon ko badhaava diya jaataa he. inaki range aavashyaktaaon par prakaash daalane ke liye prayukt takneekon kii kaaryshaala sahajata se lekar, aavashyaktaaon ke vishleshan or sanghathan ke liye prayukt takneekon tak hoti he.

 ek vyapaar prakriya sudhaar main aamtaur par chah charan shaamil hai :
 1. prakriya dalon or netaa kaa chayan prakriya dal main vibhinn vibhaagon se 2-4 karmchaaree shaamil hote hai jo sthapana kii is vishesh prakriya main shaamil hote hai. prayek team ek prakriya dal netaa kaa chayan karti he, praroopik rup se yahi vyakti sambandhit prakriya ko chalaane ke liye uttaradaayi hota he.
 2. prakriya vishleshan prashikshan chaynit prakriya dal sadasyon ko prakriya vishleshan or dastavejikaran kii takneekon main prashikshit kiya jaataa he.
 3. prakriya vishleshan saakshaatkaar prakriya dalon ke sadsy prakriya ke saath kaam karne vaale logon ke saath kai saakshaatkaar karte hai. saakshaatkaar ke dauraan, ve prakriya sanrachana ke bare main jaankaree ikaitha karte hai, saath hi prakriya pradarshan aankadon ke bare main jaankaree praapt karte hai.
 4. prakriya dastavezikaran saakshaatkaar ke parinaamon kaa upyog ek pehali prakriya ke maanchitr ko banane main kiya jaataa he. 
 5. samiksha chakra iske baad prakriya main kaam karne vaale karmchaariyon ke dwaara draft dastavejikaran kii samiksha kii jaati he. sabhi sambandhit karmchaariyon ke saath prakriya ke ek saamaanya drushtikon ko praapt karne ke liye atirikt samiksha chakra jaruri ho sakate hai. 
 6. samasya vishleshan iske baad prakriya samasya kaa ek gahan vishleshan kiya ja saktaa he, jo prakriya ke bare main ekatrit kii jaankaree or prakriya maanchitr par aadhaarit hota he.
