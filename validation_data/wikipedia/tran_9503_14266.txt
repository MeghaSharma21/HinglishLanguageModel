 gammak kaa matlab he 'alankaran'. gammak kaa upyog bhartiya shastreey sangeet ke pradarshan me paaya jaataa he. gammak ko 'gammka' or 'gammakam' ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. gammak har us sangeet swar ko kaha jaataa he jis me koi ek swar yaa swar samooh ko gaate hue yaa bajaate hue ek sundar palta yaa vakrata diya jaataa he, jo kisi raag ke vyaktitv ko badhaava detaa he. har raag ke adwitiya charitra ke peeche, us me prayog kiye gayi gammakon kaa haath hota he. isiliye gammak keval sangeet me sajaavat ke liye hi nahi he, ve bharatiya shaastra sgit me adhik aavashyak bhi he. lagbhag har ek sangeet nibandh me gammakon ke varnan ke liye ek alag anubhag likhaa jaataa he.
 sanskrit me gammak kaa matlab he 'alankrita swar'. gammak me swar kii avritti ko bhaari or shashakt companon ke dwaara sate hue or dur ke swaron ke bich main kisi nirdhaarit swar ko parivartit kiya jaataa he. har ek raag main gammak ke prakaar or unke aavedan ke bare main alag-alag niyam he jo kisi vishisht prakaar ke sgit swar par lagu hote he.
 gammak shaastra sangeet ke 'manodharm sangeet' vibhaag ke mukhya pahalu main se ek he, jis main 22 sookshm sur or shrutiyaan lagati he. kisi bhi raag ke swaron ko anivaary rup se unke upyukt gammakon ke saath hi prastut kiya janaa chaiye kyonki swar apane aap main vichaarsheel sur nahi he balki ve sur maatra he jo kisi sangeet ke madhur chaal ko darshaate he. gammak keval alankaran hi nahi he. vah raag k ek moulik ansh bhi he. kisi bhi sangeet swar ko ek gammak sajivan karte hue use gati pradaan karati he.
