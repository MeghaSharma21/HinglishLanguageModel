 moshan pikchar association of america ) ek america vyapaar sanghathana he jo chah bade holivood studiyon ko pesh karti he. 1922 main moshan pikchars producers and distributors of america ke naam se sthapit yah sanghathan apane sadasyon ke hit ke liye prayatnsheel rahati he or empiee film gunvattta system kii dekh-rekh karti he. bhootpurv democrate senetter kris dodd iske vartmaan chairman he.
