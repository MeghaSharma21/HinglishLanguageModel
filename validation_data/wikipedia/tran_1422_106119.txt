 anuvanshiki main parkraman kisi koshika main virus yaa anya madhyam dwaara bahari dillane pravesh karwaane kii prakriya ko kehete hai. iskaa udhaaharan ek bacteria se kisi virus dwaara dillane lekar use kisi anya bacteria main dalwaanaa he. parkraman main dillane daata koshika kaa dillane grahan karne vaali koshika se sidhaa sampark hona aavashyak nahin he. aanvik jeevvaigyanik parkraman ke prayog se koshikaon ke jinom main paraai jin daalate hai.
