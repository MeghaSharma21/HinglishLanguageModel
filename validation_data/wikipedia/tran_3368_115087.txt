 jagadguru ramanandacharya swami rambhadracharya chitrakoot dham, bharat ke ek hindu dharmikaneta, shikshavid, sanskritvidwaan, bahubhashavid, kavi, lekhak, tikaakar, darshanik, sangeetkaar, gayak, naatakkaar or kathaklakaar hai. unki rachanaaon main kavitaayen, naatak, shodh-niabandh, tikaayen, pravachan or apane granthon par swayan srujit sangitabaddh prastutiyaan sammilit hai. ve 90 se adhik saahitiyik kritiyon kii rachana kar chuke hai, jinamein prakashit pustaken or aprakashit pandulipiyaan, chaar mahakavy, tulsidas dwaara rachit ramcharitamanas par ek hindi bhashya,ashtaadhi par padyarup main sanskrit bhashya or prasthaantrayee shastron par sanskrit tikaayen shaamil hai.unki rachanaaon ke anek audio or video bhi jari ho chuke hai. vah sanskrit, hindi, avadhi, maithili or kai anya bhashaaon main likhte hai.
 shribhargavaraghaviyam unki atyant prasiddh rachana he, jiske liye unhen sanskrit sahitya academy puraskaar samet anek puraskaron se sammanit kiya ja chukaa he..unhen mahakavi tathaa kavikularatna aadi anek saahitiyik oopaadhiyon se bhi alankrita kiya ja chukaa he.
 unki pramukh saahitiyik or sangitamay rachanaayen, shaileyon ke aadhaar par vibhinn samoohon main, neeche soochibaddh hai.
 
 jagadguru rambhadracharya ne prasthaantrayee par shriraghavakrupabhashyam naamak sanskrit tikaaon kii rachana kii he. in tikaaon kaa vimochan 10 baishakhpurv 1998 ko bharat ke tatkaaleen pradhanmantri, atal bihari vaajapeyi dwaara kiya gaya tha.iske atirikt unhone naarad bhakthi sutara par 1991 main shriraghavakrupabhashyam tikaa kii rachana kii. ramanand sampradaay main prasthaantrayee par sanskrit tikaa prastut karne vaale rambhadracharya dwitiya acharya hai, inase purv swayan ramanand ne anandabhashyam naamak pratham tikaa kii rachana kii thi.is prakaar lagbhag 600 varshon kii kalavadhi main prasthaantrayee par pratham baar sanskrit tikaaon ko rambhadracharya ne prastut kiya he.
 rambhadracharya kaa sabase vivaadaaspad kaary ramcharitamanas kaa samalochanatmak sanskaran tha, jo tulsi peeth sanskaran ke rup main prakashit kiya gaya tha. un par mahakavy ko vikrut karne kaa aarope lagaaya gaya tha, lekin rambhadracharya dwaara is sanskaran ke prakaashan se kisi ko ksht pahunchne par khed jataane par yah vivaad shant ho gaya. unke khilaaf ek yachika bhi daayar kii gai thi, lekin use adaalat ne khaarij kar diya tha. yah sanskaran 2005 main shri tulsi peeth seva nyaas dwaara prakashit kiya gaya tha.
