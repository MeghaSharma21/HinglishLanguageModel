 kranti-tirth gujarat kaa ek suprasiddh paryaton sthal he. yah kuccha ke mandavi jile main mandavi nagar se 3 kilometre dur mandavi dharbudi maarg par sthit he. shyaamaji krishna verma dwaara united kingdom main beesvi shatabdi ke prarambh main sthapit higate landan ke aitihaasik india house kii anukriti is paryaton sthal main vishesh rup se darshneey he.
 gujarat ke mukhyamantri narendra modi dwaara 13 disambar 2010 ko raashtra ko samarpit is kranti-tirth ko dekhne dur-dur se paryatak gujarat aate hai. gujarat sarkaar kaa paryaton vibhaag isaki dekharekh karata he.
 bhartiya swatantraaa sangram ke agrani senaanee shyaamaji krishna verma kaa nidhan switzraland kii rajdhani geneva main 31 march 1930 ko ho gaya tha. gujarat ke mukhyamantri narendra modi ne unki asthiyon ko bharat kii swatantrata ke 55 varsh baad 22 agast 2003 ko swiss sarkaar se anurodh karke geneva se swadesh waapas mangaayaor shyaamaji krishna verma ke janm sthaan mandavi main kranti-tirth naam se ek romantic paryaton sthal banaakar usamen unki smriti ko sangrakshan pradaan kiya.
 13 disambar 2010 ko modi ne ise raashtra ko samarpit kar diya. is kranti-tirth ko dekhne dur-dur se paryatak gujarat aate hai. isaki dekharekh ke saath-saath paryatakon ke avaagaman va nivas aadi kii sari vyavastha gujarat sarkaar kaa paryaton vibhaag karata he.
 kranti-tirth ke parisar main landan ke india house kii hu-ba-hu anukriti banaakar usamen shyaamaji krishna verma sahit anek krantikariyon ke chitra va unase sambandhit sahitya bhi rakhaa gaya he. kuccha jane vaale sabhi deshi videshi paryatakon ke liye mandavi kaa kranti-tirth ek ullekhneey paryaton sthal ban chukaa he. ise dekhne dur-dur se baari sankhya main videshi paryatak gujarat aate hai.
