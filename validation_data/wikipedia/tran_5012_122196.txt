 agsim bharat ke goa rajya ke ilhas main bahane vaali zuaari nadi ke uttari kinare par pasa ek gaanv he. yah uttar main panaji, dakshin main madgaanv, pashchim main vasco da gamma or purv main paunda se ghira hua he or is kaaran se yah uttar or dakshin goa ko zuaari sethu dwaara jodne vaala ek mukhya maarg he. agsim goa ke choriso ke liye prasiddh he.
 agsim ne sadaa se hi goa ke rajnatik paridrishy main ek mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai he, chaahe vah goa bachaao abhiyaan rahaa ho yaa 1986 kaa konkani bhashaa aandolan. in ghatnaon ke dauraan saat log maare gaye the.
