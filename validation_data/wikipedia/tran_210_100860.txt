 atyadhik chumbakeey, bahut tej ghurnan karne vaale neutron taare hai, jo kii vidyutchumbakeey vikaran utapann karte hai. inkaa vikaran tabhi abhasith hota he jab vikaran paidaa hone kii disha prathvi kii ore ho. kyonki inke dwaara utapann vikaran nishchit antaraal ke baad hi prithvi par aata he, isliye inhen prakashastambh prabhaav dene vaale taron kii sangya bhi di jaati he. chunki neutron taare bahut hi ghaney nikaay hote hai, uski ghurnan avadhi or isi tarah unki pulse ke bich kaa antaraal bahut hi niyamit hota he. kuch pulsar kii dhadkan kii niyamitata sateek rup main ek parmaanu ghadi jaisi hoti he. unke pulse vistaar kii prekshit avadhi 1.4 mili second se lekar 8.5 mili second he.
