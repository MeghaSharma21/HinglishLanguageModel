 aam aadmi party, sankshep main aap, saamaajik kaaryakarta arvind kejariwal evam anna hajaare ke lokapal andolan se jude bahut se sahyogiyon dwaara gathit ek bhartiya raajnneetik dal he. iske gathan kii aadhikarik ghoshnaa 26 navambar 2012 ko bhartiya samvidhan adhinium kii 63 veen varshaganth ke avasar par jantar mantar, dilli main kii gayi thi.
 san 2011 main india against karpashan naamak sanghathan ne anna hajaare ke netritv main hue jan lokapal andolan ke dauraan bhartiya raajnneetik dalon dwaara janhit kii upeksha ke khilaaf awaz uthaai. anna bhrashtachar virdohi janalokapal andolan ko raajnneeti se alag rakhana chaahate the, jabaki aravind kejariwal or unke sahyogiyon kii yah raay thi ki party banaakar chunaav ladaa jaye. isi uddeshy ke tahat party pehali baar disambar 2013 main dilli vidhansabha chunaav main jhaadu chunaav chinh ke saath chunavi maidan main utari.
 party ne chunaav main 28 siton par jeet darj kii or congress ke samarthan se dilli main sarkaar banaayi. aravind kejariwal ne 28 disambar 2013 ko dilli ke 7wein mukhya mantri pad kii shapath lee. 49 dinon ke baad 14 farvari 2014 ko vidhaan sabha dwaara jan lokapal vidheyak prastut karne ke prastaav ko samarthan na mil paane ke kaaran arvind kejariwal kii sarkaar ne tyaagptr de diya.
 aam aadmi party kii utpatti san 2011 main india against karpshan dwaara anna hajaare ke netritv main chalaaye gayi jan lokapal aandolan ke samaapan ke dauraan hui. jan lokapal banane ke prati bhartiya raajnneetik dalon dwaara pradarshit upekshapurna ravaiye ke kaaran raajnneetik vikalp kii talash kii jane lagi thi. anna hajaare bhrashtachar virdohi janalokapal aandolan ko raajnneeti se alag rakhana chaahate the jabaki aravind kejariwal aandolan kaa lakshya praapt karne ke liye ek alag party banaakar chunaav main shaamil hone ke pakshdhar the. unke vichaar se vaarta ke jariye jan lokapal vidheyak banavaane kii koshishen vyarth ja raheen thim. india against karpshan dwaara saamaajik judaav sevaaon par kiye gayi survey main raajnneeti main shaamil hone ke vichaar ko vyapak samarthan mila. 
 19 sitambar 2012 ko anna or aravind is nishkarsh par pahunche ki unke raajnneeti main shaamil hone sambandhi matbhedon kaa dur hona mushkil he. isaliye unhone samaan lakshyon ke baavjood apana rasta alag karne kaa nishchay kiya. jan lokapal aandolan se jude manish sisodiya, prashanth bhushan va yogendra yadav aadi ne aravind kejariwal kaa saath diya, jabaki kiran vedi va santosh hegade aadi kuch anya logon ne hajaare se sahmati prakat kii. kejariwal ne 2 oktober 2012 ko raajnneetik dal banane kii ghoshnaa kii. is prakaar bhartiya samvidhan kii varshganth ke din 26 navamvar ko aupchaarik rup se aam aadmi party kaa gathan hua.
 party kahati he ki vah kisi vishesh vichaardhaara dwaara nirdeshit nahin hai. unhone vyavastha ko badalane ke liye raajnneeti main pravesh kiya he. aravind kejariwal ke shabdon main - "ham aam aadmi hai. agar vampanthi vichaardhaara main hamaare samaadhaan mil jaayen to ham vahaan se vichaar udhaar le lenge or agar dakshinpanthi vichaardhaara main hamaare samaadhaan mil jaayen to ham vahaan se bhi vichaar udhaar lene main khush hai.
 2014 main prashanth bhushan ne kashmir kii aantarik suraksha or sena kii maujuudagi ke maamle main kashmir ke logon main janamat sangrah karaane kii baat kahi, jiske virodh main hindu raksha dal ke kuch logon ne kaushambi sthit party mukhyalay main aam aadmi party ke khilaaf narebaaji or tod-phod kii.
 4 disambar 2013 ko hue dilli rajya vidhansabha chunaav main party ne pehla chunaav ladaa. usne puri dilli ke liye chunavi ghoshnaptr taiyaar karne ke saath hi prayek nirvachan kshetra ke liye alag-alag ghoshnaptr taiyaar kiya.
 dilli chunaav ke pehle party ko kai vivaadon kaa saamana karnaa padaa. bharat sarkaar ke gruhamantri, susheel kumaar scindia ne party ke videshi daan kii jaanch karaane kii baat kahi. party ne daan raashi kaa sampoorn byora party vevesite par pehle se hi saarvajanik hone kii baat kahi or anya raajnneetik dalon ko bhi apane chande ko saarvajanik karne kii chunauti di. 
 dilli vidhaan sabha chunaav ke kuch pehle ek media portal dwaara aam aadmi ke vidhayak pad ke ummidavaaron kaa sting oparation saamne aayaa jisamen un par gair-eemaandaar hone ke aarope lagaaye gayi. aam aadmi party ne ek press conference kar sting video main kai mahatvapurn bhaagon ko kaat-chhant kar prastut karne kaa aarope lagaaya or media portal ke khilaaf manahani kii yachika daayar kii.
 6 disambar ko ghoshit hue parinaam main 70 sadasyeey dilli vidhansabha main party 28 siton par vijayi rahi. 32 vidhaan sabha kshetron kii vijeta bhartiya janata party ke baad yah duusari sabase badi party ke rup main ubhari. aravind kejariwal ne sattarudhi congress party kii nivartmaan mukhyamantri shila dikshit ko lagbhag 25,000 voton se parajeet kiya. or congress keval 8 siton par simat gayi.
 dilli ke uparajyapal najeeb jang ne bhaajapa dwaara sarkaar banane se manaa karne ke baad aam aadmi party vidhayak dal ke netaa aravind kejariwal ko sarkaar banane ke liye aamantrit kiya. 28 disambar ko congress ke samarthan se party ne dilli main apni sarkaar banaayi. aravind kejariwal sabase kam umr ke mukhyamantri bane. 
 party ne kaha tha ki 2014 bhartiya aam chunaav main 300 siton par chunaav ladane kaa iraadaa rakhti he. 432 siton par apane ummidavaaron kaa elaan kar chuki party kii nazar mukhyatah haryana, gujarat, uttar pradesh sahit shahari kshetron main thi, jahaan party kaa samarthan mukhya rup se aadhaarit he
