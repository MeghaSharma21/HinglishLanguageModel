 ramchandrika ke naam se vikhyath ramchandra chandrika hindi sahitya ke reetikaal ke aarambh ke suprasiddh kavi keshavadas rachit mahakavy he. unchalis prakaashon main vibhajit is mahakavy main kul 1717 chand hai. isamen suvidit bhagwaan ram kii katha hi varnit he, phir bhi yah anya ramaayanon kii tarah bhaktipradhan granth na hokar kavy granth hi he.
 'ramchandra chandrika' keshavadas kii suprasiddh kriti he jo saamanytah 'ramchandrika' kehalaati he. iske rachanakaal kaa spasht ullekh swayan kavi ne grantharambh main kiya he-
 is prakaar iskaa rachanakaal 1601 i0 he. iske mul paath kaa sanskaran litho chapai main kanhaiyalaal radhelaal, lakhanu ke dwaara prakashit hua tha. isaki janaki prasad krit tikaa venkateshwar press, mumbai se san 1907 i0 main or navalkishor press, lakhanu se san 1915 i0 main prakashit hui. lala bhagavanadin kii aadhunik dhang kii hindi tikaa 'ramchandrika' kii sarvaadhik prasiddh tikaa rahi he. deen ji kii tikasahit 'ramchandra chandrika' kaa purvardh sahitya sevasadan, banaras se tathaa uttarardh sahitya bhushan karyaalay, banaras se 1923 i0 main prakashit hua. lalaji kii tikaa kii punraritiyaan san 1929 i0 se ramnarayan laal bukseller, prayag dwaara prakashit hone lageen. iske mul paath kaa ek pramanik sanskaran acharya vishwanath prasad mishra ke sampadan main 'keshav-granthawali, bhaag-2' ke rup main hindustani academy, elahabaad se bhi prakashit ho chukaa he.
 'ramchandra chandrika' 39 prakaashon main katha suchi sahit 1717 chhandon main puraa hua he. acharya vishwanath prasad mishra ke anusaar yadhyapi is main suprasiddh ramakatha varnit he tathaapi yah kavy kaa granth he, bhakthi kaa nahin. keshav 'nimbark sampradaay' main dikshit hone ke naate radha krishna ke upaask the, ram ke nahin. yadhyapi ramchandrika ramakatha sambandhi mahakavy he phir bhi is main varnit katha valmikiy ramayana or ramcharitamanas se bhinnata rakhti he. haalaanki kavi ke ullekh ke anusaar is mahakavy ko rachane kii prerana unhen adikavi valmiki se hi mili. unhone likhaa he ki
 is ke purvardh kii katha valmikiy ramayana se kuch-kuch samya bhi rakhata he kintu uttarardh main kavi kii nijee udbhaavna evam vyavastha hi pramukh he. is kavy ke madhyam se kavi kaa paanditya pradarshit hua he. samvaad-yojana, alankaar-yojana, chand-yojana kii drushti se yah kavy kavi ke acharyatv ke kaushal ko hi prastut karata he. isamen kavi ne susambaddha kathatmak parampara ko tod diya he. vastutah unhone is granth-rachana main pratyaksh sahyog sanskrit ke 'prasanraghav', 'hanumannatak' aadi granthon se liya he. 'kadambari' kaa bhi spasht prabhaav he. naatakon kaa aadhaar lene se or katha bhaag chhod dene se samvaad ke vaktaaon ke naam inhen padya se pruthak rakhane pade hai. samvaad-yojana naatakeey dhang se kii gayi he, isliye drishy kavy ke rup main iskaa upyog vishesh saralata se ho saktaa he. keshav ke is kavyagranth main bhakthi kii pradhanta na hote hue bhi ram ko avtari mana gaya he tathaa anek sthalon par unase upadesh dilaayaa gaya he. parinamswarup upadeshaatmak ansh kaaphi ho gayi hai or kavyatv ko kshati pahaunchi he.
 keshav ko reetikaal kaa yuganirmaataa sahityakar tathaa hindi kaa pratham acharya kavi mana gaya he. is drushti se ramchandrika main bhi acharyatv kii prabalata spasht dikhti he. style kii drushti se isamen vividh prakaar ke chhandon ke udhaaharan prastut karne kii pravriti dikhaai padati he. aisa jaan padataa he ki kavi kisi ko pingal kii paddati sikhaa rahe hon. ramchandrika kii bhashaa sanskritpradhan brajabhasha he. sanskrit shabdon ke atyadhik prayog tathaa alankaar ke chamatkaar ke chakkar main pad jane se rachana bojhil or clisht ho gayi he. is granth main kavi kii sahrudayata se kahin adhik panditya kaa pradarshan prabal he. acharya vishwanath prasad mishra ke shabdon main nihsandeh yah keshav ke mahaan panditya evam acharyatv ko purna rup se abhivyakt karti he. pracheen hindi sahitya kaa marmajna hone ke liye ramchandra chandrika kaa adhyayan nirvivaad rup se anivaary he. hindi sahitya main iskaa mahatvapurn sthaan he. iske katu aalochak bhi iske pathan-pathan par bal dete aaye hai.
