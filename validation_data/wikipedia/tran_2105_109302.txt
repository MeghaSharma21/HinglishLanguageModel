 tengri pracheen turk logon or mongol logon ke dharm ke mukhya devtaa the. inaki manyata shiyongnu or shiyanbei jaise bahut si mongol or turki samudaayon main thi or inke naam par un logon ke dharm ko tengri dharm bulaayaa jaataa he. turki logon kii sabase pehali kagaanath ne, jiskaa naam goekaturk kagaanath tha, bhi inhen apana rashtriya devtaa tehraaya tha or uske kagaan bhi apni gaddi par virajman hone kaa kaaran 'tengri kii ichha' bataate the. tengri ko 'akaash kaa devtaa' yaa 'nile akaash kaa pita' kaha jaataa tha. aksar mongol shaasha apane raaj ke peeche 'sanathan nile akaash kii ichha' hone kii baat kiya karte the.
 pracheen mongolon or turkon kaa maananaa tha ki tengri manushyon ko shakti-daan karte the jise unki bhashaa main 'qut' kaha jaataa tha. 'qut' shabd main bindu-vaale 'qa' ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bindu-rahit 'k' se zaraa alag he . bahut se turki or mongol hastiyon ke naam 'qut' shabd par aadhaarit the, jaise ki 'qutalug', 'tengriqut' or 'qutalmish' .
 haalaanki tengri dharm ko turki or mongol kshetron main islam or buddha dharm aane se lupt ho gaya, lekin uske chihn abhi bhi is kshetra main faile hue hai -
