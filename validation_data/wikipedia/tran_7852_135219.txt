 khafre puraane samrajya ke dauraan chauthe rajvansh ke ek pracheen misr ke raja the. vah khufu kaa putr tha or dijedfre ke singhaasana ke uttaradhikari the. pracheen itihaaskaar manetho ke anusaar, khafara ke baad raja bikharis the, lekin puratathvik sakshya ke anusaar vah raja menkair ke bajaae the. khafara giza ke duusare sabase bade pyramid ke nirmaataa the. aadhunik misrology dwaara bade paimaane par yojit drushtikon yah he ki great sphinks lagbhag 2500 isa purv khafara ke liye banaaya gaya tha.. khefara ke bare main zyada jaankaree nahin he, herodotus kii aitihaasik reporton ko chhodkar, jo unhen ek krur or vidrohi shaasha ke rup main varnit karte hai, jinhone khufu ne unhen sil kar diya tha, misr ke mandiron ko band kar diya tha.
