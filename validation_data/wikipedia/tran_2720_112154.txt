 sar ahamed salmaan rushdi ek british bhartiya upanyaaskaar or nibandhkaar hai. unhone apane duusare upanyaas midnites children se prasiddhi praapt kii, jise 1981 main bukar puraskaar mila. unke adhikansh praarambhik upanyaas bhartiya up-mahadweep par aadhaarit hai. unki style kaa vargeekaran aksar aitihaasik kalpana ke saath sanyojit jaadui yatharthwaad ke rup main kiya jaataa he or unki kritiyon kii pramukh vishay-vastu, purvi or pashchimi duniya ke bich kai rishton ke judane, alag hone or deshantaranon kii kahani rahi he.
 unka chauthaa upanyaas setenic varsez, "da setenic varsej" vivaad ke centre main tha, jiske tahat musalamaanon kii ore se kai deshon main iskaa virodh hua. kuch virodh pradarshan hinsak the, jiske dauraan rushdi ko maut kii dhamkee or farvari, 1989 main tatkaaleen iran ke sarvocch netaa ayatulla ruhollah khomeni dwaara jari kiye gaye fatve kaa saamana karnaa padaa. unki hatya karne ke elaan kii pratikriya ke rup main, rushdi ne lagbhag ek dashak, mukhyatah bhumigat hokar bitaayaa, jiske dauraan kabhi-kabhaar hi ve saarvajanik rup se prakat hote the, lekin un par ek lekhak ke rup main niyantrankari prabhaav daalane vaale or sannihit abhivyakti kii swatantraaa ke khatare ke rup main fatve ke khilaaf ve mukhar rahe.
 "sahitya kii sevaaon" ke liye june 2007 main unhen ek nite bachaler niyukt kiya gaya. unhone france kaa sarvocch pad - Commandeur - in the Ordre des Arts et des Lettres bhi hasil kiya. 2007 main emory vishvavidhyalay main unhone vishisht lekhak ke rup main paanch saal kaa kaarykaal shuru kiya. may 2008 main ve american academy of arts and letters ke liye chune gaye. unka naveenatam upanyaas da anchentress of flaurence june 2008 main prakashit hua.july 2008 ko saarvajanik vot jitate hue midnites children ko sarvshereshth bukar ghoshit kiya gaya, puraskaar ke 40 saal ke itihaas main bukar puraskaar jeetne vaala sarvshereshth upanyaas. 
 
 anis ahamed rushdi, cambridge vishvavidhyalay ke shikshit vakil jo baad main vyavsaayee bane or negin bhatta, ek shikshika ke iklaute bete rushdi kaa janm bombai, bharat main hua tha.unki shiksha cathedral and jon conan skool, mumbai, rugby skool or kings college, cambridge main hui, jahaan unhone itihaas kaa adhyayan kiya. ek purnakalik lekhak banane se pehle unhone do vigyaapan agenciyon ogilvi and mather or ir barker) ke liye kaam kiya.
 rushdi kii chaar baar shaadi hui. apni pehali patni clerissaa luard se ve 1976 se 1987 tak vivaahit rahe jisase unhen ek betaa jafar hua. unki duusari patni america upanyaaskaar marion vigins thim; un donon kaa vivaah 1988 ko or talaaq 1993 main hua. unki teesri patni, 1997 se 2004 tak, elizabeth west thim; unka ek betaa, milan he. 2004 main unhone bhartiya america abhinetri or super model padma lakshmi, america rielity television kaarykram top shef kii mejabaan, se vivaah kiya. shaadi 2 july 2007 ko tut gaya or lakshmi ne ingit kiya ki unhone khud vivaah samaapt karnaa chaha tha. 2008 main, bollywood press ne bhartiya model riya sen ke saath unka prem sambandh joda, jiske ve anyathaa ek dost the. unki dosti ke bare main media kii atakalbaazi ke uttar main, usne sirf itnaa kaha "mujhe lagata he ki jab aap salmaan rushdi hai, to aap zarur un logon se ub jate hai jo hameshaa aapse sahitya ke bare main baat karnaa chaahate hai." 
 1999 main rushdi ne ek "condra dasha" ko sahi karne ke liye oparation karaayaa, jo unke mutabik, unki ankhen kholane kii mushkil ko tezi se badha rahi thi."agar main oparation nahin karaata, to kuch hi varshon main main apni ankhen kholane main sakasham hi nahin ho paata," unhone kaha.
 
 aanshik rup se ek vigyaan sahitya katha vaale unke pehle upanyaas grimes ko aam taur par janata or saahitiyik aalochakon ne nazarandaaj kiya. par unke agle upanyaas midnites children ne, unhen saahitiyik prasiddhi pradaan kii. isane ullekhneey rup se us raah ko nirmit kiya jiskaa abhigaman bhartiya angrezi lekhan ne qareeb agle ek dashak tak kiya or kaiyon dwaara ise pichley 100 varshon kii mahanatam pustakon main se ek mana jaataa he. is kriti ne 1981 bukar puraskaar jeeta or 1993 or 2008 main ise sarvshereshth bukar kaa puraskaar mila, arthath puraskaar ke kramshah pratham 25 or 40 saal main isane bataur sarvshereshth upanyaas puraskaar jeeta. midnites children ko kai puraskaar mili or ise rushdi ke sabase achche, sarvaadhik pravaahamay or preranadayak kriti ke rup main uddhrut kiya gaya he. kahani ek bachche ke jivan par aadhaarit he, jo bharat kii swatantraaa prapti ke saath aadhi raat ko janm letaa he, jo vishesh shakti-sampann he or anya bhartiya up-mahadweep or aadhunik raashtra bharat ke janm ke itihaas main ek nae or ashaant yug ke usha kaal main paidaa hue bacchon ke saath sambandh rakhata he. saleem sinai ke charitra kii tulana khud rushdi se kii gai he.

 rushdi ne nikaragua ke bare main 1980 ke dashak main upanyaas se itar ek pustak likhi, da jaguaar smil . is pustak main ek raajnneetik kendra-bindu he or sandenista raajnneetik prayog ke sthalon par yah unke pratham anubhavon or shodh par aadhaarit he.jaguaar smil ko badhaava dene ke kram main san francisco vishvavidhyalay main ek saakshaatkaar ke dauraan unhone is baat kii vakaalat kii ki chaatr vah naa likhen, jo ve likhnaa chaahate hai, lekin vah likhen jise ve likhe bagair nahin rah saken. unhone ek apurna kriti kaa hawala diya, jo agle varsh prakashit hui, ek aisi pariyojna, jisane unke jivan par aisa prabhaav daalaa jisaki unhen kabhi ummid nahin thi.

 rushdi ke vyavsaayik taur par safal or samikshakon dwaara prashansit upanyaason kii ek kadi rahi he. unke 2005 ke upanyaas shalimaar da cloun ko bharat main pratishthit crossward fiction award praapt hua or britain main wheatbrad book award ke antim chaynit pustakon main yah shaamil tha. ise 2007 ke antarrashtriya IMPAC dublin sahitya puraskaar ke liye chunaa gaya. 
 2002 ke apane cathetar sahitya sangrah step across dis line main, unhone anya logon ke alaava italvi lekhak italo kalvino or america lekhak thomas pinchon ke kaam kii saraahna kii. un par praarambhik prabhaav daalane waalon main james jois, gunter grass, horhe louis borhes, mikhail bulgakove or louis carol shaamil hai. rushdi angela carter ke ek nijee dost bhi the or unke sangrah burning yor boats kii bhoomika main unki atyadhik saraahna kii he.
 
 rushdi ne khamoshi se yuva bhartiya lekhakon kaa disha-nirdesan kiya he, indo-angleey lekhakon kii ek puri peedi ko prabhavit kiya he or saamaanya rup se uttar aupniveshik sahitya main ek prabhaavshaalee lekhak hai. unhone apane lekhan ke liye kai shaabasiyaan pai hai jinamein shaamil hai - european union kaa aristian prize for litarechar sahit premio grinzen kevar or germany main riter of da fir award tathaa sahitya ke anya kai sarvocch sammaan. ve royal socity of litarechar or Commandeur des Arts et des Lettres ke sadsy bhi hai. rushdi 2004 se 2006 tak pen american centre ke adhyaksh the.
 british sarkaar dwaara nasleey or dharmik ghrinaa adhinium kii shuruyaat kaa ve virodh karte hai, kuch aisa jiske bare main unhone free expressant iz no ophens ke apane yogadan main likhaa he, yah november 2005 main penguin dwaara prakashit kai lekhakon kaa niabandh sangrah he. rushdi ek swayan-vyakhyayit naastik hai or maanavtaavaadi british association ke ek pratishthit samarthak.
 varsh 2006 main rushdi ne agle paanch varshon ke liye, varsh main ek maah ke liye rihaishi pratishthit lekhak ke rup main emory vishvavidhyalay adhyaaapak mandali main sammilit hue. haalaanki lekhan main unhen anand aata he, salmaan rushdi kaa kahana he ki ve ek abhinayta ban gaye hote, agar unka lekhan kariyar safal nahin hota. yahan tak ki bachapan se hi, unhone hollywood kii filmon main kaam karne kaa sapana dekha tha .
 rushdi pop samskruti ke prashansak hai or apane kuch lekhanon main tii.ve. or film ke kaalpanik paatro ko shaamil karte hai. film brijet jones diary main unki ek laghu bhoomika he jo isi naam kii pustak par aadhaarit he, jo khud bhi saahitiyik chutkulon se bhari he. 12 may 2006 ko rushdi da charlie rose sho main ek atithi mezbaan the, jisamen unhone bharat-canadai film nirmaataa deepa mehta kaa saakshaatkaar liya, 2005 kii film, water ke liye jinke kaary ne bhi hinsak virodh pradarshnon kaa saamana kiya. ve elinner lipman ke upanyaas dein shi found me ke filmi roopantaran main hellen hunt ke prasutivigyaani- stri rog-visheshgy kii bhoomika main prastut hote hai. sitambar 2008 main or phir march 2009 main, ve HBO kaarykram "real time wyeth bill maher" par panel ke sadsy ke rup main haazir hue.
 
 sitambar 1988 main saetanic varsez ke prakaashan ne islamic duniya main tatkaal vivaad ko janm diya or iskaa kaaran bana paigambar mohammad kaa apmanjanak samajha jane vaala chithran. sheershak ek vivaadit muslim parampara ko sanderbhit karata he, jiskaa sandarbh qitaab main he. is parampara ke mutabik, teen deviyon ko, jinaki divine praaniyon ke rup main makka main puja kii jaati thi, sweekaar karte hue mohammad ne quran main panktiyaan jodin. kinvadanti ke mutabik, mohammad ne baad main in panktiyon kaa pratisanharan kiya, yah kehete hue ki shaitan ne unhen makka waalon ko khush karne ke liye inhen bolne ko uksaaya . haalaanki, varnanakarta, pathak ke saamne yah khulaasa karata he ki yah vivaadit panktiyaan vastav main arkengel giberil ke munh se nikali thim. bade muslim samudaay vaale kai deshon main is qitaab par pratibandh laga diya gaya tha.
 14 farvari 1989 ko rushdi ko maarane ke fatve kii radio tehran par tatkaaleen iran ke aadhyatmik netaa ayatulla ruhollah khomeni dwaara yah kehete hue ghoshnaa kii gai ki pustak "islam ke khilaaf ishanindaatmak" he baari samarthan praapt hua.
 rushdi ke ek purv angrakshak, ron ivance ne ek pustak prakashit karne kii yojana banaai, jo lekhak ke un dinon ke vyavhaar ko saamne rakhti jab vah chhipe hue the. ivance ne daawa kiya ki rushdi ne fatve se aarthik laabh paane kaa prayaas kiya or ve aatmghaatee the, lekin rushdi ne is qitaab ko jhooth kaa pulinda kehete hue khaarij kar diya or ron ivance, uske sahyogi lekhak or prakaashak ke khilaaf kanoonee karrwai kii. 26 agast 2008 ko landan ke ucch nyaayalay main rushdi se teenon pakshon ne maafi maangi.
 
 3 agast 1989 ko jab mustafa mahmud maazeh, padington, central landan ke ek hotel main RDX visphotak se bharaa hua ek kitaab bam taiyaar kar rahaa tha, to bam samay se pehle hi fat gaya, jisamen hotel kii do manzilen baahar aa gayi or maazeh kii mrityu ho gai. purv main agyaat ek lebaniz sanghathan, organaizeshan of da mujahidin of islam ne kaha ki vah "dharm bhrasht rushdi par ek hamale kii taiyaari main maaraa gaya." tehran ke behesht-e-zaharaa kabristaan main mustafa mahmud maazeh ke liye ek makbara he, jis par likhaa he ki vah "landan main shahid, 3 agast 1989. salmaan rushdi ko maarane ke mishan par marane vaala pehla shahid." maazeh kii maan ko iran main sthanantaran ke liye aamantrit kiya gaya or islamic world movement of marters camemoration ne uske makbare ko us kabristaan main banaaya jisamen iran-iraq yuddh main maare gaye hazaron sainikon kii kabren hai. 2006 jeelands-posten mohammad cartoon vivaad ke dauraan hizbullah ke netaa hasan nasarllah ne ghoshnaa kii ki "agar pakhand salmaan rushdi ke khilaaf imam khomen ke fatve ko puraa karne ke liye koi muslim hua hota, to yah nimna vargeey jisane denmark, norway or france main hamaare paigambar mohammad kaa apmaan kiya he, aisa karne kii himmat nahin karte. mujhe vishwaas he ki lakhon musalmaan aise hai jo apane paigambar ke sammaan kii raksha ke liye jivan dene ke liye taiyaar hai or hamen iske liye kuch bhi karne ko taiyaar rahanaa chahie. heritage foundation ke james philips ne america congress ke saamne gavaahi di ki "march 1989" kaa ek visphot rushdi kii hatya ke liye hizbullah kaa ek prayaas tha, jo bam ke samay se pehle visphot ho jane ke kaaran vifal ho gaya, jisase landan main ek hizbullah kaaryakarta kii mrityu ho gai.
 
 1990 main, setenic varsej ke prakaashan ke baad hi ek pakistani film pradarshit kii gai jisamen rushdi ko desh main casino or disco kii shrinkhalaa khol kar pakistan ke patan kii sajeesh rachaate hue dikhaaya gaya tha. film pakistani darshakon ke bich lokapriy rahi or isane "rushdi ko ek rembo sadrush charitra ke rup main prastut kiya, jiskaa peechha chaar pakistani gorilla karte hai. british board of film clacification ne ise pramaan patr kii anumati se inkaar kar diya kyonki, "yah mahasoos kiya gaya ki rushdi kaa chithran sirf unki chhavi kharaab karne kii bajaay, shanti bhang kaa kaaran banate hue aapraadhik pariwaad mana ja saktaa he. is qadam ne britain main prabhavi dhang se film par purna pratibandh laga diya. haalaanki, do maheene baad rushdi ne khud board ko patr likh kar kaha ki unki raay main yah film "ek vikrut, aksham bekaar rachana he" or agar yah pradarshit hoti he to ve mukadmaa nahin karenge. baad main unhone kaha, "agar us film par pratibandh laga diya jaataa, to vah shahar main sarvaadhik icchit video ban jaataa, har koi use dekh letaa." haalaanki film pakistan main bade paimaane par safal rahi, pashchim main vastutah yah kisi kaa dhyaan aakarshith nahin kar pai. unhone kaha ki film kaa ek hissaa sahi myanon main haasyaaspad tha, jab unka charitra unki pustak saetanic varsej ko pard kar ek pakistani ladaaku ko yatnaa detaa he.
 
 16 june 2007 ko queens berthde onors main sahitya kii sevaaon ke liye rushdi ko nisehud se sammanit kiya gaya. unhone kaha, "is mahaan sammaan ko praapt kar main romanchit or vinamr hoon or bahut abhaari hoon ki mere kaam ko is tarah se pahchaan mili he." unke nite kii padavi kii pratikriya main, muslim bahul deshon ne virodh pradarshan kiya. in deshon ke kai sansad sadasyon ne is kriya kii ninda kii or iran or pakistan ne apane british rajdooton ko aupchaarik rup se virodh pradarshan ke bulaayaa. rushdi kii nite kii padavi ke khilaaf pakistan or malaysia main bade paimaane par pradarshan hue. kaiyon ne saarvajanik taur par uski mrityu kii maang kii. kuch gaiyr-musalmaan bhi rushdi kii nite kii padavi se niraash the, yah maanate hue ki lekhak aise sammaan kii yogyata nahin rakhata he.
 BBC kii july 2007 kii ek report ke anusaar al kaayda ne bhi rushdi ke sammaan kii ninda kii he. al kaayda ke up pramukh aimaan al zawaahiri ko ek audio recording main yah kehete hue uddhrut kiya gaya he ki bharat main janme rushdi ke liye britain kaa puraskaar "islam kaa apmaan" he or vah "ek bahut hi sateek pratikriya" kii yojana bana rahaa he. 
 
 rushdi ek shiya muslim parivaar se aae hai, lekin kehete hai ki ve vastav main kabhi dharmik nahin rahe. 1990 main, "aashaa he yah, fatve ke mutabik unhen maarane ke prayaas main muslimon ke khatre ko kam karegaa," unhone ek bayaan diya jisamen yah daawa kiya ki "unhone apni muslim aasthaa ko naveekrut kiya he, islam par apane upanyaas main hamale kii ninda kii he or duniya bhar main is dharm kii behatar samaj ke liye kaam karne ke liye patibaddhta jataayi." lekin baad main kaha ki ve keval "naatak" kar rahe the.
 unki kitaaben aksar samaaj main dharm kii bhoomika, vibhinn aasthaaon ke bich or dharmik or bina aasthaa waalon ke bich takaraav par prakaash daalati hai.

 rushdi apane raajnneetik vichaaron ke liye british shaikshik pratishthanon ke krodh kaa shikaar hote rahete hai. purv main rushdi kii kritiyon ke prashansak rah chuke marxvaadi aalochak terry eagleton ne unke drushtikon ke prati un par yah kehete hue hamalaa kiya ki unhone "pentagon ke iraq or afghanistan main aapraadhik kaaryon par khushi prakat kii". haalaanki, tadnantar unhone rushdi ke vichaaron ko galat rup main prastut karne ke liye maafee maangi.
