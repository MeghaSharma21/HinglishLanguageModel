 borgit assam ke nav vaishnav dharm ke mahapurush shrimant shankardev or madhavdev ke dwaara rachit ucch adhyatmik bhav sampann, nirdisht raag vishist, ek vishesh dhang se gaae jane vaale geet hai. brajavali bhashaa me rachit ye geet shri krishna kaa gungan karte hai. banikant kakoti ne in gito ko angreji me noble numbers kaha, kaliram medhi ne in gito ko great songs yaa songs celestial kaha, or debendra nath bejbaruva ne inhe holy songs kaha.
 vartmaan shankardev ke kul 30 borgit paae jate hai. psorit puthi ke anusaar shankardev ne kul 240 borgit rachana kiye the. lekin bahut saare bonpura jui me jal gaye. yadhyapi kaha jate he haki madhavdev ne kul 240 borgit rachana kiye the, vartmaan keval 180 paae jate hai. iname se kuch onkia naat ke geet hain.
 balgopal ke vibhinn shishu lilae, jaise sharaarat, khel-majaak, chaurya-chaaturya, abhimaan, gop krishna ke gopi lilae, jaise gopiyo ke saath nrity karnaa, gopineo k viched or krishna prem kii vyakulata, uddhav ke apane bandhav krishna ke prati prem or bhakthi, krishnavihin vrundavan ke dukhad vaataavaran kii varnana aadi borgit ke vishay he.
 kuch borgito me maanav jivan kii nashvarata, dhan jan, rup yauvan kii asthayitv, kaam-krodhadi kii dauratm dursi ore bhakthi kaa mahatmya, mukti ke liye bhagwaan ke saamne aatmasamarpan aadi parmarthik chhije bhi varnit he.
 mahapurush shankardev kaa pehla borgit he mon meri ram soronhi lagu jiskaa rachanakaal lagbhag 1515 he. wo tha jurujan ke pehle tirthabhraman kaa samay. anumaan kiya jaataa he ki dhanashri raag par aadhaarit ye borgit badarikashram me likhaa gaya tha. badarikashram kii yah ghatana guru charit me paaya jaataa he "..গৈ বদৰিকাশ্ৰমে উঠি উদ্ধৱক দৰশন হৈছেগৈ৷ তাত দক্ষিণ বাহুত বাঢ়িছে মাংস৷ সৰ্ববজয় আতৈ বোনে, কিবা হবহে৷ গুৰু বোলে আমাৰ সখা ইষ্ট এজন পুৰুষ মাধৱ নামে জন্মিছে৷"
