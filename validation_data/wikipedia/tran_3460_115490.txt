 haivi ejineering karporation limited yaa "HECL" ek saarvajanik kshetra ke upkram ranchi, jharkhand main sthit he . HECL kaa nirmaan san 1958 main bharat ke sabase bade ekikrut ejineering ke rup main hui thi. . ye nirmaan anya kal kaarkaanon main supplai hone vaale upkarnon or bhari mashinari kaa utpaadan karti he. HECL main mashin kii nirmaan ke liye uske alag alag bhaagon kii dhalaai, rachana or unako ekatrit kar parikshan karne kii sari suvidha ek hi campus main uplabdh he. 
 HECL ke kaarkaane ke units kuch is prakaar hai :
 ye unit 570000 varg meter main failaa hua he or unit 200000 varg meter pe bana hua he. HMBP steel plant, khanikarm, kraine, krashar, cement, aluminium, spes research, nuklair power ityadi main istemaal hone vaale upkarnon ko dizine detii he or unka utpaadan karti he. 
 haivi mashin tuls plant checkoslovakia kii company em/es scodaexport ke saath nirmaan kiya gaya tha. HMTP 213500 varg meter main sthapit he. ye plant madhyam or haivi duty siensi evam parmparik mashinari jo railway, raksha udyog, ordenance factory, antariksh ityadi main istemaal hone vaale upkarnon ko dizine kar utpaadan karata he. 
 foundry forj plant vishwa kaa sabase bada foundry and forging parisar he. ye plan 1316930 varg meter pe sthit he. is plant main 76000 ton kii mashinari sanchaalan ke liye sthapit kii gayi he. yahan HEC dwaara banaayin jane vaale upkarnon kii dhalaai kii jaati he jo steel plant, rakhaa udyog, parmaanu vibhaag main istemaal kiya jaataa he. ye forgad rolls jo steel plant evam railway main istemaal kiya jaataa he, uskaa bhi utpaadan karti he. 
 projects divizan dizine, ejineering v vishesh projects jo vishaal str par order detii he unke sanchaaran kaa dhyaan rakhti he.
