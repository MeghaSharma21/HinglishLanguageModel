 hizbul mujahidin april, 1990 main astitv main aayaa ek algaavvaadi sanghathan he. iskaa gathan mohammad ehsaan daar ne kiya tha. 17 oktober 2016 ko jammu-kashmir main zakir musa ko hizbul kaa nayaa commander banaaya. ye burhan vaani kii maut ke baad uski jagah nayaa commander banaaya gaya.
 bharat, sanyukt rajya or europea sangh dwaara is sanghathan ko aatankwaadi mana gaya he. 13 sitambar 2018 ko uttar pradesh aties ne kanpur se is sanghathan ke ek sadsy ko giraftaar kiya gaya.
