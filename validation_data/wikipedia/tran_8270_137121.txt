 brahma yaa oraiga taramandal khagoleey gole ke uttari bhaag main dikhne vaala ek taramandal he. duusari shatabdi isvi main tolmi ne jin 48 taramandalon kii suchi banaai thi yah unamem se ek he or antarrashtriya khagoleey sangh dwaara jari kii gai 88 taramandalon kii suchi main bhi yah shaamil he. is taramandal kaa sab se roshan tara brahmahryday he. puraani unani khagolashastriy pustakon main ise aksar ek rath chalaa rahe vyakti ke rup main darshaya jaataa tha jisane apane kandhe par ek bakrii pakadi hui he or jisaki god main do or choti bakriyaan khadi hai.
 brahma taramandal main aath mukhya taare hai, haalanki vaise isamen 65 taron ko bair naam die ja chuke hai. inamen se 6 ke ird-gird gair-saureey grah parikrama karte hue paae gaye hai. is taramandal ke mukhya taare is prakaar hai -
