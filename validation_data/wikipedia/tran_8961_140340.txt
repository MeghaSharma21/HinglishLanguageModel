 aghosh vartsy nasiky ek prakaar kaa vyanjan he jo vishwa kii bahut kam bhashaaon main miltaa he, jaise ki burmi bhashaa or esthonian bhashaa. hindi va angrezi bolne waalon ko yah 'na' se miltaa-julata prathit hota he. ise anterrashtriya dhvanyatmak varnmaala main n̥ or n̊ ke chinhon dwaara likhaa jaataa he.
