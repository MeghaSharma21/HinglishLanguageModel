 badrinath bharat ke uttari bhaag main sthit ek sthaan he jo hinduon kaa prasiddh tirth he. yah uttrakhand ke chamoli jile main sthit ek nagar panchayat he. yahan badririnath mandir he jo hinduon ke chaar prasiddh dhaamon main se ek he. 
 badrinath jane ke liye teen ore se rasta he. ranikhet se, kotdwar hokar paudi se ore haridwar hokar devprayag se. ye teenon raste rudvaprayag main mil jate he. rudraprayag main mandakini or alakananda kaa sangam he. jahaan do nadiyaan milti he, us jagah ko prayag kehete he. badari-kedaar kii raah main kai prayag aate he. rudraprayag se jo log kedarnath janaa chaahaten he, ve udhar chale jate he.
 kabhi haridwar se is yatra main maheenon lag jate the, lekin ab to sadak ban jane ke kaaran yatri motor-lariyon se theth badrinath pahunch jate he. hafte-bhar se kam main hi yatra ho jaati he. 
 rudraprayag se nau mile par pipal kothi aati. pipal koti main jaanwaron kii khaalen, davaaiyaan or kasturi acchhee milti he. bas kii sadak banane se pehle raasten main chattiyaan thim.
 raasten main thaharne ke liye jo padaav bane hote the, unheen ko 'chatti' kehete the. kahin kachche makaan, kahin pakke. sab chattiyon par khaane-pine kaa saamaan miltaa tha. baratan bhi mil jate the. doodh, dahi, maava, pede sab-kuch miltaa tha, juute-kapade tak. jagah-jagah kaali kamali vaale baba ne dharmashalaayein banavaa di thi. davaaiyaan bhi milti thim. daakkhaane bhi the.
 aage raasten main garuda-ganga aakar alakananda main milti he. yahaan garudji kaa mandir he. kehete he, lautati baar jo garuda-ganga main nahaakar pathar kaa ek tukdaa puja karne ke liye ghar le jaataa he, use sanpon kaa dar nahin rahata. yahaan se pataal ganga kii chadhaai shuru hoti he. saare raste main chid or deodaar ke ped he. unhen dekhakar man khil uthta he. patalganga sachmuch pataal main he. neeche dekhon to dar lagata he. pani matamaila. kam he, par bahaav bada tej he. kinare kaa pahaad hameshaa tuutata rahata he. do mile tak aisa hi rasta chalaa gaya he. patalganga par neeche utre, phir upar chadhe. gulabkoti pahunche. kehete he, satyuga main yaheen par parvati ne tap kiya tha. ve shivaji se vivaah karnaa chaahati thim. iske liye saalon pattey khaake raheen. isise aaj is van kaa naam ‘pakhand’ yani ‘parn khand he. vahaan jane vaale sab log us puraani kahani ko yaad karte he or phir ‘joshimath’ pahunch jate he. joshimath ek vishesh nagar he. saare gadhwal main shaayad yaheen par fal hote he. phulon ko to poochon mat. joshimath kaa naam swami shankaracharya ke saath hi judaa hua he. yah shankaracharya do hajaar saal pehle hue he. jab hindu dharm mit rahaa tha tab ye hue. kul battis saal jivit rahen. is choti si umr main ve itane kaam kar gayi ki achraj hota he. bade-bade pothe likhe. saare desh main dharm kaa prachaar kiya. phir desh ke chaaron cono par chaar math banaaye. purab main puri, pashchim main dwarika, dackhin main shringery or uttar main joshimath. in chaaron mathon ke guru shankaracharya kehalaate he. yahi shankaracharya the, jinhonen badrinath kaa mandir phir se banwaaya tha. sardi ke dino main jab badrinath baraf se dhak jaataa he to vahaan ke rawal murti yaheen aakar rahete he. badrinath ke mandir main jo maalaaen kaam aati he, ve yaheen se jaati he. yahaan ke mandir main paisa nahin chadhaaya jaataa. kisi buree baat ko chhodane kii kasam khai jaati he. yahaan par keemu kaa ek ped he. kehete he, iske neeche baithkar swamiji ne pustaken likhi thim. neeche nagar main kai mandir he. unamem se ek main nrisingh bhagwaan kii murti he. kaale pathar kii us sundar murti kaa baanyaa haath bada patala he. poochne par pataa laga ki vah haath baraabar patala hota ja rahaa he. jab vah gir jaayga tab yahaan se koi aage na bad sakegaa. sab raste tut jaayangen.
 yahaan se aagen bade to jaise pataal main utarate chale gayi. do mile kii khadi utraai he. par bich-bich main milane vaale sundar jharne sab thakaan dur kar dete he. neeche vishnu prayag he, jahaan vishnu ganga or dhauli ganga kaa milan hota he. in nadiyon kaa pul lohe kaa bana hua he.
 pandukeshwar ke paas phulon kii ghati he, jise dekhne duniya bhar ke log aate he. yaheen par lokapal he, jahaan sikhon ke guru govindsingh ne apane pichley janm main tap kiya tha. kehete he, pandukeshwar ko pandavon ke pita pandu ne basaaya tha. pandav yaheen paidaa hue the. swarg bhi yaheen hokar gayi the. vah ve yahaan kai baar aaye the. shivaji maharaj se dhanush lene arjuna yaheen se gayi the. bhim camel lene yahi ke wanon main aaye the. nadi ke bany kinare ke pahaad ko ‘pandu chaowkee kehete he. isaki chotii par chapad bani hui he. yahaan baithkar un logon ne aakhri baar chapad kheli thim kehete he, yahaan kaa ‘yogabadri kaa mandir’ un logon ne hi banwaaya tha. aage kaa pahaad kahin kaala, kahin nila, kahin kaccha he. bich main kahin niri mitti, kahin jamaa hua baraf. yahaan bhoj-patr bahut he. jab kaagaj nahin the tab bhoj-patr par kitaaben likhi jaati thim. yahaan ganga kai baar paar kerani padati he. ’’
 raasten bahut se mandir or tirth he. yahaan hanuman chatti main hanuman mandir he. yahaan pandavo ko hanuman mili the. chadhate-chadhate kanchanganga ko paar karke ‘kuber shila’ ai. aankh uthaakar jo dekha, saamne vishalpuri thi, jiske liye dharamshastra main likhaa he ki teenon lonko main bahut se tirth he, par badari ke samaan na tha, na hoga.
 vishalpuri alakananda ke daahine kinare par basii hui he. chota-sa bazaar he. dharmadhalaayein he. ghar he. thana-daakghar sabakuch he. narayan parvat ke charano main badrinath kaa mandir he, jiske sunahare kalash par suraj kii kirane pad rahi thim. baraf se dhake hue akaash ko chune vaale pahaado ke bich vah choti nagari badi acchhee lagati thi.
 badrinath kii oonmchai he- 10480 foot, yani koi do mile. ek samay tha jab path or bhi behad the. 
 brahmaji ke do bete the. unamem se ek kaa naam tha daksh. daksh kii solah betiyaan thi. unamem se teraa kaa vivaah dharmraj se hua tha. unamem ek kaa naam tha shrimurti. unke do bete the, nar or narayan. donon bahut hi bhale, ek-duusare se kabhi alag nahin hote the. nar chote the. ve ek-duusare ko bahut chaahate the. apni maa ko bhi bahut pyaar karte the. ek baar donon ne apni maa kii badi seva kii. maan khushi se phool uthi. boli, ‘‘mere pyaare betaa, main tumse bahut khush hum. bolo, kya chaahate ho ? jo maangoge vahi duungi.’’
 donon ne kaha, ‘‘maan, ham van main jaakar tap karnaa chaahaten he. aap agar sachmuch kuch dena chaahati ho, to yah var do ki ham sadaa tap karte rahe.’’
 beton kii baat sunakar maa ko bahut dukh hua. ab uske bete usase bichhud jaayangen. par ve vachan de chuki thim. unako roak nahin sakati thim. isliye var dena padaa. var paakar donon bhai tap karne chale gayi. ve saare desh ke wanon main ghoomane lage. ghoomte-ghoomte himalay pahaad ke wanon main pahunche.
 isi van main alakananda ke donon kinaaron par don pahaad he. dahini ore vaale pahaad par narayan tap karne lage. bai or vaale par nar. aaj bhi in donon pahaado ke yahi naam he. yahaan baithkar donon ne baari tap kiya, itnaa ki devloak kaa raja dar gaya. usne unke tap ko bhang karne kii badi koshish kii, lekin use safalataa nahin mili. tab use yaad aayaa ki nar-narayan saadhaaran muni nahin he, bhagwaan kaa avatar he. kehete he, kaliyug ke aane tak ve vahiin tap karte rahen. aakhir kaliyug ke aane kaa samay hua. tab ve arjuna or krishna kaa avatar lene ke liye badari-van se chale. us samay bhagwaan ne duusare muniyon se kaha, ‘‘main ab is rup main yahaan nahin rahoonga. naarad shila ke neeche meri ek murti he, use nikaal lo or yahaan ek mandir banaaon aaj se usee kii puja karnaa.
 yah mandir bahut purana he. likhaa hai muniyon ne murti ko nikaala. vah saawali he. usamen bhagwaan badrinarayan padmasana lagaaye tap kar rahe he. aaj bhi mandir main yahi murti he. isko reshmi kapade or heere-jade gahane pahanaaye jate he. mandir bahut sundar he. padiyaan chadhakar jo darwaja aata he, usamen bahut badhiyara jaaliyaan bani he. upar teen sunahare kalash he. andar chaaro ore garud, hanuman, lakshmi or ghantakarna aadi kii murtiya he. phir bheetar kaa darwaja he. andar murti vaale kamare kaa darwaja chaandi kaa bana he. unke paas ganesh, kuber, lakshmi, nar-narayan udvav, naarad or garud kii murtiya he. yahaan baraabar mantron kaa paath, ghanton kaa shor or bhajanon kii aawaaj goonjati rahati he. akhand jyoti bhi jalati rahati he or chadhaava ! chadhaave kii baat mat pucho. atka aadi bahut se chadhaave he. vaise ab sab sarkaar ke haath main he. yahaan ke sabhi pujari, jo ‘rawal’ kehalaate he, dakshin ke he. isse pataa lagata he ki bharat ke rahanevaale sab ek he. vahaan saat kund he. paanch shilaayein he. brahm kapali he. anek dharaaen he. bahut si gangaaen he. jo muni, rishi yaa avatar yahaan rahete the yaa aaye the, unki yaad main yahaan kuch-na-kuch bana he. jaise nar-narayan yahaan se na laute to unke mata-pita bhi yaheen aa based.
 naarad ne bhagwaan kii bahut seva kii thi. unke naam par shila or kund donon he. prahraad kii kahani to aap log hi he. unke pita ko markar jab nrisingh bhagwaan krodh se bhare phir rahe the tab yaheen aakar unka aavesh shant hua tha. nrisingh-shila bhi vahaan maujuud he. brahm-kapali par pindadaan kiya jaataa he. do mile aage bharat kaa aakhri gaanv mana jaataa he. dhai mile par mata murti kii madhi he. paanch mile par vasudhara he. vasudhara do sau foot se girane vaala jharnaa he. aage shatapath, swarg-dwaar or alakapuri he. phir tibet kaa desh he. us van main tirth-hi-tirth he. sari bhoomi tapobhumi he. vahaan par garam pani kaa bhi ek jharnaa he. itnaa garam pani heki ekaek pair do to jal jaay. thik alakananda ke kinare he. alakananda main haath do to gal jaay, jharne main do to jal jaay.
