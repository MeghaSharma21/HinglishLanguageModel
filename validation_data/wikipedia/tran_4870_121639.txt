 al-andalas muslim, yaa moore dwaara shasit iberian prayadweep ke us hisse ko diya gaya arabi naam jo 711 or 1492 ke bich kii avadhi main islamic raajnneetik kendra rahaa tha. yah umayyad kilaafat kaa praant tha. baad main cordoba kilaafat, or aakhir main cordoba taipha samrajyon kii khilaafat kaa hissaa tha. itihaas ke bade hisson ke liye, vishesh rup se cordoba khilaafat ke tahat, andlus prasiddh tha or cordoba shahar bumadhya basin or islamic duniya donon main agrani saanskrithik or aarthik kendron main se ek ban gaya tha.
 yah sabhyataa vaastukala or shahari niyojan main kaaphi unnat thi. moore yaa muslim bahut amir the kyonki unhone pashchimi africa main ghana samrajya se sone ke vyapaar ko niyantrit kiya tha. unhone niyantrit sabhi bhoomi main kai khubasurat imaaraton kaa nirmaan kiya tha. unki badi imaaraton main abhi bhi kai chijen hai andlucia main, jaise seville, grenada or cordoba main.. 
 muslim spain bahusaanskrithik or sahishnu tha; yahudee, isai or muslim ek saath rahete the. bumadhyasagariya tat ke paas ek badi slav aabaadi bhi thi. haalanki in logon ko pehali baar das banane ke liye laayaa gaya tha, unamem se kuch general ban gaye the or kuch generalo ko kam samay ke liye apane shaharon ke shaasha banaye gaye the.
 1236 isvi main, costil ke isai shaasha ferdinand tritiy kii senaaon ne aakhri shesh islamic garh, grenada tak hamale kiye jo jaldi safal nahi hue. lekin 2 janvari, 1492 isvi ko isaayiyon ne grenada par hamalaa kar musalamaanon se jeet kar purna niyantran kar liya tha. jisase al-andalas main islamic shashan kaa patan ho gaya tha.
