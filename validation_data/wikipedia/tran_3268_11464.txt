 ben stokes  ek england antarrashtriya cricket khilaadi he jo gharelu cricket ke match daraham team ke liye khelte hai. stokes baayen haath ke ballebaaj or daine haath ke gendbaaj bhi he. is kaaran stokes england cricket team ke ek olerounder he.
 ben stokes ne apane antarrashtriya test cricket karier kii shuruyaat australian team ke khilaaf 05 disambar 2013 ko kii thi jabaki vanade karier kii shuruyaat 25 agast 2011 ko ireland ke khilaaf kii thi.
