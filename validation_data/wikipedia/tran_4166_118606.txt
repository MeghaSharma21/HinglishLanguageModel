 bhautikee, yantriki or yantric praudyogiki main uttolak yaa liver ko ek saral yantra kaha jaataa he. uttolak kai roopon main vidyaman hote hai. apane saralatam rup main yah ek lambi chad ho sakati he jiske ek sire ke paas ek avlamb lagaakar kisi baari vastu ko uthaane ke kaam main liya ja saktaa he. uttolak, balaghurna ke siddhanth par kaary karata he. aam jivan main uttolak kaa bahut hi mahatv he or har jagah ise dekha ja saktaa he. si-sa jhula, ek uttolak he.
