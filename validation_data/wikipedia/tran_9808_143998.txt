 pichhola jhil udaipur ke pashchim main pichholi gaanv ke nikat is jhil kaa nirmaan rana lakha ke kaal main kisi banajaare ne karavaaya tha. maharana udaisingh dwitiya ne is shahar kii khoj ke baad is jhil kaa vistaar karaayaa tha. jhil main do dweep hai or donon par mahal bane hue hai. ek he jag nivas, jo ab lake pales hotel ban chukaa he or doosraa he jag mandir, udaipur. donon hi mahal rajasthani shilpakala ke behtareen udhaaharan hai,inhen naav dwaara jaakar inhen dekha ja saktaa he.
 is jhil par chaar dweep he:
 is jhil kaa manoram darshy itnaa sundar he ki paryatakon ko apni or arkshit karti he !
