 ramchandra chhatrapati haryana ke sirsa shahar se prakashit hone vaale ek sthaneey sandhya samachaarpatr puraa sach ke prakaashak va sampadak the. 30 may 2002 main unhone apane samachar patr main deraa sachcha sauda main baba gurmeet ram rahim dwaara sadhviyon ke saath tathakthith rup se ho rahe duraachaar kii khabar chhaapi thi. 19 aktubar 2002 main agyaat hamalaavaron nen unhen goli maar di. 21 aktubar ko dilli ke apollo aspataal main unka nidhan ho gaya. 
 agast 2017 main adaalat dwaara isi maamle main deraa pramukh ko doshi paaya gaya va unhen 20 saal karawas kii sazaa hui.
