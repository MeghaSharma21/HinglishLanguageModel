 kulut, ganraajya ke rup main khyath ek pracheen bhartiya samaaj tha. is jati kaa pracheenatam ullekh maha bharat main praapt hota he. usamen iskaa ullekh kashmir, sindhu-sauveer, gandhar, darshak, abhisar, shaiwal or vahali ke saath hua he. isi granth main inkaa ullekh yavan, china or kamboj ke saath hua he. varahamihir ne inkaa ullekh uttarpashchim or uttarpurv pradesh ke nivasee ke rup main kiya he uttarpashchim main kir, kashmir, abhisar, darsh, tangan, sarindh, kiraat, china aadi ke saath inkaa ullekh he or uttarpurv main tukhaar, taal, haal, bhadra or lahad aadi ke saath inaki charchaa he. mudrarakshas main vishakhdutt ne inhen 'mlechh' kaha he or inkaa ullekh kashmir saindhav, china, hun, aadi ke saath kiya he. yuvanachwang naamak chini ne apane yatravrit main likhaa he ki vah jalandhar se kulut gaya tha. chamba se someshwar dev or asat dev kaa jo tamrashasan praapt hua he usase gyaat hota he ki kulut log trigart or kir ke nikatavarti the. in sabhi ullekhon se aisa prathit hota he ki kulut log kangra jile main kulu ghati ke nivasee the or sambhavat: ve do bhaagon main vibhakt the. sahitya main inhen mlechh kaha gaya he. sambhav ve uttarpashchim kii aakraamak jatiyon main se rahe hon or uttareepashchimi bhaag main aakar bas gaye hon. unke mongoli jati ke hone kaa bhi anumaan kiya jaataa he.
 duusari-pehali shatee i. pus. inkaa apana ek ganraajya tha aisa unke sikkon se gyaat hota he. unke sikkon par jo abhilekh hai unki bhashaa sanskrit he. unase gyaat hota he ki is kaal tak unamem raajaaon kii pratha prachlit ho gai thi. sikkon par raja ke rup main vir yass, vilat-mitra, sachmitra or arya ke naam milte hai.
