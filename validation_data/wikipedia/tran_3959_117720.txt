 gandharva veda chaar upavedon main se ek upved he. anya teen upved hai - ayurveda, shilpaved or dhanurveda. gandharvaveda ke antargat bhartiya sangeet, shastreey sangeet, raag, sur, gayan tathaa vaadya yantra aate hai.
 bhartiya shastreey sangeet kii utpatti vedon se maani jaati he. samaveda main sangeet ke bare main gaharaai se charchaa kii gai he. bhartiya shastreey sangeet gahare tak aadhyatmikta se prabhavit rahaa he, isliye isaki shuruyaat manushya jivan ke antim lakshya moksh kii prapti ke saadhan ke rup main hui. sangeet kii mahatta is baat se bhi spasht he ki bhartiya aacharyon ne ise 'pancham veda' yaa gandharva veda kii sangya di he. bharat muni kaa naatyashaastra pehla aisa granth tha jisamen naatak, nrity or sangeet ke mul siddhanton kaa pratipaadan kiya gaya he.
