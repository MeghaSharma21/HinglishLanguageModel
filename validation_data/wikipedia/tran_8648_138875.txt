 namokar mantra jain dharm kaa sarvaadhik mahatvapurn mantra he. ise 'navkar mantra', 'namaskara mantra' yaa 'panch parameshthi namaskara' bhi kaha jaataa he. is mantra main arihanton, sidhon, aacharyon, upaadhyaayon or saadhuon kaa namaskara kiya gaya he.
 namokar mahaamantra' ek lokottar mantra he. is mantra ko jain dharm kaa param pavitra or anadi mul mantra mana jaataa he. isamen kisi vyakti kaa nahin, kintu sampurna rup se viksit or vikasman vishuddh aatmaswaroop kaa hi darshan, samaran, chintan, dhyaan evam anubhav kiya jaataa he. isliye yah anadi or akshayaswarupi mantra he. loukik mantra aadi sirf loukik laabh pahunchaate hai, kintu lokottar mantra loukik or lokottar donon kaary siddh karte hai. isliye namokar mantra sarvakaarya siddhikaarak lokottar mantra mana jaataa he.
 162 isapurv main hathigumpha abhilekh main namokar mantra evam jain raja kharbela kaa ullekh he.
 is mahaamantra ko jain dharm main sabase prabhaavshaalee mana jaataa he. ye paanch parameshthi hai. in pavitra atmaaon ko shuddh bhavapurvak kiya gaya yah panch namaskara sab paapon kaa naash karne vaala he. sansar main sabase uttam mangal he.
 is mantra ke pratham paanch padon main 35 akshar or shesh do padon main 33 akshar hai. is tarah kul 68 aksharon kaa yah mahaamantra samast kaaryon ko siddh karne vaala va kalyankari anadi siddh mantra he. isaki aradhana karne vaala swarg or mukti ko praapt kar letaa he.
 namokar-samaran se anek logon ke rog, daridrata, bhay, vipattiyaan dur hone kii anubhav siddh ghatnaayen suni jaati hai. man chaahe kaam aasaani se ban jane ke anubhav bhi sune hai.
