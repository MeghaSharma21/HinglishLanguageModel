 australian trikoniya shrunkhlaa australia main yojit ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket toornament kaa ullekh karti he, or australia or do paryaton tiimon dwaara ladi hui he. disambar, janvari or farvari ke garmiyon ke maheenon main, australian cricket sizan kii oonchai ke dauraan shrunkhlaa khelaa jaataa he. australia main ekadivasiy cricket ke adhikansh itihaas main yah shrunkhlaa antarrashtriya ekadivasiy cricket kaa mukhya swaroop he. trikoniya shrunkhlaa pehle 1979-80 main yojit hui thi or 2007-08 tak har sijan main chunaav ladaa tha. iske baad se 2011-12 ke satr main or dobaaraa vishwa cap ke pehle 2014-15 satr main do baar yojit kiya gaya tha.
 cricket main trikoniya series ke rup main jane vaali teen team kii antarrashtriya shrunkhlaa kii avadharana kerri paikar dwaara pryojit vishwa siries cricket kaarykram se utpann hui. paikar, jo unhone oriai cricket main majboot ruchi ke rup main dekha, kaa phayda uthaane ke liye utsuk tha, or 1977-78 or 1978-79 ke mausamon main australia, westeindies or da shesh vishwa kii tiimon main lambii trikoniya shrunkhlaa kaa aayojan kiya. in toornamenton ko kabhi bhi ek divasiy antarrashtriya yaa suchi e darja nahin diya gaya he.
 jab 1979-80 main world series cricket vivaad samaapt hua, to triko-siries praaroop ko barkaraar rakhaa gaya tha. apane astitv ke dauraan, toornament ekadivasiy antarrashtriya shrunkhlaa ke rup main yojit kiya gaya tha, jisamen teen tiimon ke bich round-robin rakhaa gaya tha, iske baad sheersh do ke bich khelaa gaya final series tha. varshon main sabase saamaanya praaroop yah tha ki prayek team round-robin main ek-duusare ke chaar baar khelti thi, jiske baad ek final ne teenon ke sarvshereshth shrunkhlaa kaa faisla kiya chaudaa yaa pandrah ekadivasiy match garmiyon main khelaa jaataa he.

 2007-08 sizan ke baad, trikoniya shrunkhlaa praaroop ko chhod diya gaya tha. teen satron ke liye, australia ne abhi bhi do daure vaali tiimon ke khilaaf ek divasiy match khele, lekin inhen ek hi virdohi ke khilaaf alag ekadivasiy shrunkhlaa ke rup main yojit kiya gaya. australia ke kaamanwealth bank abhi bhi is greeshmakaal main australia main vanade cricket kaa namakaran adhikaar praayojak tha, isliye is samay sabhi shrunkhalaaon ko rashtramandal bank shrunkhlaa ke rup main janaa jaataa tha.
 2011-12 ke satr main trikoniya shrunkhlaa kaa praaroop waapas aayaa, lekin isisi bhavishya paryaton kaarykram ke mutabik, yah praaroop ko sthaayee rup se lautaa nahin degaa. australia main 2015 ke vishwa cap ke netritv main 2014-15 ke sizan main keval saat maichon kii ek choti si series khelaa gaya tha. sabhi shrunkhlaa jo australia ko 2019-20 sijan tak host karne ke liye nirdhaarit kii jaati he, vah ekal virdohi ke khilaaf kheli jaaegi.
 australian cricket ke liye ek majboot yug ke dauraan zyaadaatar khelaa, australia ne 2014-15 tak khelaa tees-trikoniya siries main 20 jeet darj kii. australia keval teen maukon par final tak pahunchane main vifal rahaa. 1980 ke dashak main aksar siries main dikhaae gaye westeindies, agle toornament jitkar agali sabase safal team thim. trikoniya siries jeetne ke liye anya antarrashtriya tiimon main england, bharat, pakistan or dakshin africa the.
