 brussels airlines belgium kii sabase badi vimaan seva he. iskaa mukhya karyaalay evam pramukh aadhaar sthal brussels airport hi he. uttari america, europe or africa ke 90 se adhik gantavyon ke liye udaan seva pradaan karne ke alaava yah kai anya sevaayen jaise ki chartered vimaan, vimaan ke dekharekh or vimaan parichaarak dal ke prashikshan aadi bhi uplabdh karaata he. yah star alliance, da internshnal air transport assosiyeshan tathaa da assosiyeshan of european airlines kaa bhi sadsy he. 
 is kaa gathan 12 april 2005 ko es. en brussels airlines or virgin express ke vilay ke baad hua. is din esaenbie ke malikana haq vaali company esen airholding ne richard branson ke saath ek anubandh kiya or isse use virgin express kaa bhi purna niyantran mil gaya. 31 march 2006 ko esaenbie or virgin express ne ek hi ikai ke rup main vilay kaa nirnay kiya. 7 november 2006 ko ek samvaadadata sammelan main nayi sanyukt ikai ke rup main brussels airlines ke naam kii saarvajanik ghoshnaa kii gayi or 25 march 2007 se is nayi airlines ne apni pehali udaan shuru kii. 
 15 sitambar 2008 ko luphthansaa ne brussels airlines main 45% kii hissedari lene kii ghoshnaa kii or saath hi 2011 tak baaki bache 55% ko bhi kharidne kaa adhikaar surakshit rakhaa. 15 june 2009 ko european kamishan ne is saude ko apni manjuuri de di. isi ke saath brussels airlines ke star alliance kaa sadsy banane kaa rasta bhi khul gaya or 9 disambar 2009 ko brusselz toun haul ek samaaroh yojit kar brussels airlines ko star alliance ke 26wein sadsy ke rup main shaamil kar liya gaya. 
 is bich 26 aktubar 2008 se iskaa ai. e. tii. e. code DAT se badal kar BEL kar diya gaya or 25 aktuber 2009 se hi brussels airlines ko luphthansaa ke frequent flier program miles and mor main shaamil kar liya gaya. 
 15 disambar 2009 ko brussels airlines ne apane bede main ek atirikt e330 shaamil karne kaa nirnay liya. isi din brussels airlines ne democratic republic of congo main ek naye kshetreey airlines ke liye prayaas shuru karne kii ghoshnaa kii. april 2012 main shuru kii gayi is airlines kaa naam "corongo" he or iskaa aadhaar sthal congo ke lubambashi main he. 
 5 july 2010 ko paanchaven airbas e 330-300 ko bede main shaamil kiya gaya. brussels airlines ne abijan ke liye saptaah main 6 din udaan shuru kii saath hi accra, kotneu, vagadugu or lome naye gantavyon ke rup main shaamil kiye gayi. 11 aktubar 2010 brussels airlines or paryatak seva pradta club maid ne ek nayaa anubandh kiya jiske tahat ke club maid 80% yatriyon ko brussels airlines apni saamaanya evam chartered sevyon ke dwaara brussels se baahar le laane-le jane kaa kaary karegi.
 brussels airlines kai naye america gantavyon ko apni vimaan seva main jodatii ja rahi he. june 1, 2012 se is ne new york ke jon epa. kennedy airport ke liye dainik vimaan seva shuru kii or iske liye usne ek nayi aantarik saaj-sajja vaale airbas e 330-300 kii bhi vyavastha kii. sebina or delse airlines ke vighatan ke baad new york ke liye seva shuru karne vaali yah 10 saalon main pehali belgian airlines thi. 18 june 2013 se isane washington dales ke liye bhi saptaah main 5 din udaan shuru kii. april 2016 se toronto pirson bhi brussels airlines ke uttar-america udaan tantra main shaamil ho gaya.
