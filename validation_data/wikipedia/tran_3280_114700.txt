 nirdeshaank: 31°20′N 76°45′E / 31.33°N 76.75°E / 31.33; 76.75 bilaspur bhartiya rajya himachal pradesh kaa ek jila he. nile kaa mukhya nagar bilaspur rajya kii rajdhani shimla ke pashchimottar main ek kritrim jhil govindsagar ke sameep sthit he. bilaspur, 'kahalur' bhi kehalaata he.sutluj nadi ke dakshin purvi hisse main sthit bilaspur samudr tal se 670 meter kii oonchai par he. yah nagar dharmik paryaton main ruchi rakhane vaale logon ko kaaphi raas aata he. new bilaspur township ko desh kaa sabase pratham niojit hill toun ke rup main pratishthit kiya jaataa he. yahaan ke naina devi kaa mandir nikat or dur daraaj ke logon ke bich aakarshan kaa kendra rahata he. yahaan bana bhankhada baandh bhi apni gravity ke liye puure vishwa main janaa jaataa he. bilaspur ko pracheen kilon ke liye bhi janaa jaataa he. yahaan aane vaale sailaaniyon kaa anubhav anya sthanon se ekadam alag hota he. kuch alag tarah ke paryaton ke shaukeen logon ko yah sthaan kaaphi pasand aata he. iske uttar main mandi or hamirpur jile hai, pashchim main oona or dakshin main solan jile kaa nalagarh kaa kshetra he.
 shri naina devi ji kaa yah mandir ek pahari kii chotii par bana he. ropar ke pavitra nagar anandpur sahib se is mandir kii oonchai 915 meter he. pahari par bane is mandir tak pahunchane ke liye pathar kii sidhiyon kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he. mandir tak pahunchane ke liye cabel car kii bhi vyavastha he. mandir ke nikat ek chota sa bazaar bhi lagata he.
 bahadurpur naamak ek pahari kii chotii par bana yah kila 1980 meter kii oonchai par he. ise jile ko sabase ooncha point mana jaataa he. pargana bahadurpur ke tepara gaanv ke nikat bana yah kila bilaspur se 40 kilometre dur he. deodaar or baan ke sundar jangalon ne is sthaan ko chaaron taraf se gher rakhaa he. is kile se fatehpur, naina devi kii pahari, ropar ke maidan or shimla kii parvat shrinkhlaayein dekhi ja sakati hai. yah kila 1835 main banwaaya gaya tha jo ab kaaphi kshatigrast ho chukaa he. 
 yah kila bilaspur se 58 kilometre kii duuri par he. kaha jaataa he ki is kile ko mul rup se suket rajya ke raja ne banwaaya tha. sthaneey logon main yah andhvishwaas prachlit he ki kile main istemaal kiye gaye pattharon ko sthaneey imaaraton main prayukt nahin kiya janaa chahie.
 yah gupha new township ke tal par sthit he. kaha jaataa he ki is gupha main rishi vyas ne tapasya kii thi. vyasapur gaanv ke naam kii utpatti bhi isi gupha ke kaaran maani jaati he. mahabharat se sambandh rakhane vaale vyas rishi ek mahaan darshanik the, jo sutluj nadi ke baane tat par bani is gupha main dhyaan lagaaya karte the. is gupha ko ek pavitra tirthasthal mana jaataa he.
 bilaspur se 40 kilometre dur bilaspur-chandigarh road par swarghat sthit he. samudratal se 1220 meter oonche swarghat se aasaani se naina devi mandir or bhaankhdaa baandh pahunchaa ja saktaa he. swarghat main lakshmi narayan ko samarpit ek mandir bana hua he. kuch din shanti se gujaarne ke liye yah ek adarsh sthaan he. haal hi main himachal pradesh paryaton vikaas nigam ne yahaan ek aath kamaron ko hotel banwaaya he. shri lakshmi narayan mandir parisar kii shobhaa dekhne laayak he jab kabhi bhi aapako yahan aane kaa avasar mili to jaaniyegaa kii is mandir kii shobhaa kitni niraali he.
 bilaspur ke bhaankhdaa gaanv main sthit yah baandh nangal township se 13 kilometrdur he. yah baandh vishwa kaa sabase ooncha gravity baandh he. baandh par bani jhil lagbhag 90 kilometre lambii he. yah baandh lagbhag 168 varg kilometreke kshetra main failaa hua he. yah baandh bilaspur kaa 90 pratishat or oona jile kaa 10 pratishat hissaa gherta he. is baandh ko 20 navambar 1963 ko pandit jawahar laal nehru ne raashtra ko samarpit kiya tha. baandh se aasapaas ke kshetra kaa najaara dekha ja saktaa he. 
 yah lokapriy mandir bilaspur se 20 kilometre dur tehsil sadar main sthit he. pehle is mandir main rishi markandeya rahete the or apane aradhy kii aradhana karte the. isi kaaran is mandir ko markandeya kaha jaataa he. yahaan ek pracheen pani kaa jharnaa bhi he, jahaan baisakhi kii raatri main ek vaarshik parv yojit kiya jaataa he.
 sutluj nadi par bana yah shandaar brij rashtriya rajmarg 88 par bana hua he. is brij kaa nirmaan kaary april 1959 main shuru hua jo 1965 main jaakar puraa hua. yah brij 280 meter lanbaa or 7 meter chaudaa he. nadi ke tal se 80 meter oonche is pul kaa vishwa ke sabase oonche pulon main mana jaataa he. oonchai ke maamle main yah brij asia me pratham sthaan rakhata he. is pul kaa shilanyas parivahan mantri shri raaj bahadur ne 1965 main kiya tha.
 bilaspur kaa nikatatam airport chandigarh or bhuntar main he. chandigarh bilaspur se 135 or bhuntar 131 kilometre kii duuri par he.
 kiratpur bilaspur kaa najdiki railway steshn he, jo bilaspur se 60 kilometreki dur he.
 rashtriya rajmarg 21 bilaspur ko sadak maarg se jodta he. chandigarh se bilaspur ke liye niyamit dealks or saadharan basen chalati hai. shimla se darlaghat hote hue bhi bilaspur pahunchaa ja saktaa he.
