 bairal maatra kii kai ikaayiyon main se ek he jisamen dry bairal, fluid bairal, oil bairal aadi shaamil hai. kuch bairal ikaayiyon kii maatra dusron se dugani hoti he or kai matraayen lagbhag 100â€“200 litre kii range main hoti hai.
 kuch utpaadon ke liye ek maanak vajan yaa maatra hoti he jisase ek bairal banaata he:
 fluid bairal, kis cheez ko or kahan mapa ja rahaa he iske aadhaar par alag-alag hote hai. tel ke baralon ke liye aglaa section dekhen.
 britain main ek bear bairal 36 british galen hota he. america main zyaadaatar fluid bairal 31.5 america galen  hote hai lekin ek bear bairal 31 america galen hota he.
 42 yues galen ke maanak oil bairal kaa prayog sanyukt rajya america main kachche tel or anya petroleum utpaadon ke maapak ke rup main kiya jaataa he. anya sthanon main tel ko aam taur par ghan metron yaa tanon main mapa jaataa he, tanon kaa istemaal zyaadaatar europea tel companiyon dwaara kiya jaataa he. america stock exenjon main soochibaddh antarrashtriya companiyaan vaishvik reporting sambandhi uddeshyon ke liye apni tel utpaadan kii maatra ko baralon main bataanaa pasand karti hai or europea exenjon main soochibaddh companiyaan apane utpaadan ko tanon main vyakt karti hai. ghanatv ke aadhaar par ek ton main tel ke 6 se 8 bairal ho sakate hai. udhaaharan ke liye: prati ton heavi distillat ke 256 you.es. galen, prati ton kachche tel ke 272 galen or prati ton gasolin ke 333 galen .
 19veen sadi ke uttarardh kaa woodan oil bairal aadhunik samay ke 55-galen steel drum se alag he. 42-yues-galen oil bairal maapan kii ek ikai he or ab kachche tel ke parivahan ke liye iskaa prayog nahin kiya jaataa he - zyaadaatar petroleum pipelinon yaa tel tankeron main le jaaya jaataa he.
 maapan kii ek ikai ke rup main bairal ke 42-yues galen kaa aakaar zyaadaatar america tel udyog tak hi simit he kyonki bairal ke anya aakaaron ko sanyukt rajya america main anya udyogon dwaara istemaal kiya jaataa tha. takreeban anya sabhi desh metric pranali kaa upyog karte hai. kai tel utpaadak desh america oil bairal kaa upyog karte hai.
 is maapan kii shuruyaat praarambhik pennsilvania tel kshetron main hui thi. 1860 ke dashak kii shuruyaat main jab tel kaa utpaadan shuru hua tha, us samay tel ke liye koi maanak container nahin tha, isiliye tel or petroleum utpaadon ko bear, machhlee, gud, tarpin aadi ke liye vibhinn aakaaron or swaroopon ke baralon main rakhaa or sthanantarit kiya jaataa tha. donon 42-you es galen bairal, tears or 40-yues-galen whisky baralon kaa istemaal kiya jaataa tha. 45-galen bairal bhi aam upyog main the. 40-galen whisky bairal shuruaati tel utpaadakon dwaara prayog kiya gaya sabase adhik saamaanya aakaar tha kyonki ye us samay aasaani se uplabdh the.
 42-galen oil bairal kii utpatti kii jaankaree aspasht he lekin kuch aitihaasik dastavejon se sanket miltaa he ki 1866 kii shuruyaat ke aasapaas pennsilvania ke tel utpaadak nishkarsh par pahunche the ki alag-alag kismon ke contenron main tel kii shipping tel ke kharidaaron main avishvaas paidaa kar rahaa tha. unhone faisla kiya ki unhen apane kharidaaron ko yah samajhane ke liye ki unako unke paison ke badale main ek uchit maatra praapt ho rahi he, maapan kii ek maanak ikai kii jarurat thi. ve is maapan kaa aadhaar kamobesh maanak 40-galen whisky bairal rakhane par sahamat ho gaye lekin do galenon kii ek atirikt maatra unhone yah sunishchit karne ke liye jod di ki maapan kii koi bhi truti hameshaa kharidaar ke paksh main hogi jo kharidaar kaa vishwaas jeetne kaa ek atirikt tarika tha, jaahir taur par yah usee siddhant par aadhaarit tha jo bekars dajan or maapan ke kuch anya lambii ikaayiyon ke peeche tha. 1872 tak maanak oil bairal 42-yues galen ke rup main majbootee se sthapit ho gaya tha.
 sankshepan 1 embairal or 1 membairal kaa aitihaasik rup se matlab kramshah ek hazaar or ek million bairal tha. ye unani bhashaa ke "mega " kii bajaay latin bhashaa ke "mili " se nikle hai jiskaa matlab "hajaar" hota he. haalanki isse mega ke esai sankshepan ke saath bhram kii sthiti paidaa ho sakati he - bairal hota he.
 bairal prati din, bidi yaa bi/di) ek aisi maap he jiskaa upyog kachche tel ke utpaadan kii dar yaa ek ikai dwaara khapat ko bataane main hota he. udhaaharan ke liye ek tel kshetra 100,000 bipidi kaa utpaadan kar saktaa he or ek desh 1 million bipidi kii khapat kar saktaa he.
 note: bipidi ke saath blpidi kaa bhram nahin hona chahie jiskaa sambandh keval kaccha tel hi nahin balki pani sahit kul liquid kii prapti se he. ye vibhinn maatra ke wolum hai. bipidi kaa sambandh bioi se he jo kachche tel ke maamle main tulana karne ke kram main hydrocarbon gaison main urja kii maatra ke bare main bataane kaa ek aam tarika he.
 beepi statistical rivyuu 2006 ke anusaar:
 bairal prati calendar diwas dizin kii gayi kshamta ke vipareet vaastavik rifinary thruput kii ek maanak petroleum downstream udyog maap he. bipidi kii gananaa praapt kachche tel ke parishkrut baralon kii sankhya ko rifinary ke chaalu rahane ke dinon kii vaastavik sankhya se bhaag dekar kii jaati he.
 utpaadan or khapat ke maamle main hajaar bairal prati din yaa million bairal prati din kaa upyog karnaa aam baat he. inhen aam taur par kramshah embidi or mmbidi ke rup main likhaa jaataa he jisamen "em" ko 1000 ke liye roman sankhya ke rup main liya jaataa he. yah mahatvapurn he ki esai upsargon se inkaa bhram naa ho, jahaan kebidi or embidi kaa matlab kramshah ek hajaar or das lakh bairal prati din hoga.
 ek bairal kaa upyog takneekee rup se kisi bhi maatra ko nirdeshit karne ke liye kiya ja saktaa he. chunki maape jane vaale taral padaarthon kii vaastavik prakruti dhara ke saath badalati rahati he, kabhi-kabhi jo kuch nirdeshit kiya ja rahaa he use yah spasht karne ke liye qualifire kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he. tel kshetra main taral padaarthon ke utpaadan kii daron ke bich antar rakhana aksar mahatvapurn hota he, jo kachche tel or pani ke ek mishran or swayan tel ke utpaadan kii daren ho sakati hai. agar koi kuan 20% kii pani kii katautee ke saath taral padaarthon kii 10 em bi di maatra kaa utpaadan karata he to kuen ke bare main bhi yah kaha jayega ki yah prati din 8 hazaar bairal kachche kaa utpaadan karata he.
 anya paristhitiyon main utpaadan or khapat ke aankadon main gas ko shaamil karnaa mahatvapurn ho saktaa he. aam taur par gas ko maanak ghan feet main mapa jaataa he lekin aavashyaktaa padane par is maatra ko dahan kii tapiya dharita ke baraabar tel kii maatra main badal diya jaataa he. iske sadrush utpaadan or khapat ko prati din samatuly tel ke baralon ke rup main kaha jaataa he.
 pani daalane vaale kuon ke maamle main prati din pani ke baralon kii injactivity dar kaa sandarbh lenaa aam baat he.
