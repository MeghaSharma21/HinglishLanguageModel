 haiman philip minski ek america arthshaastri, saint louis main washington vishvavidhyalay main arthshaastra ke professor, or board college kii levi arthshaastra santhaan main ek pratishthit vidwaan the. vah apane shodh vah ek sambhavit najuk vittiya pranali main jhulon ke liye jimmedaar tehraaya he, jo vittiya sankat kii visheshtaon kii samaj or vivaran pradaan karne kaa prayaas kiya. kinesian parampara main, vah vittiya bazaaron main kuch sarkari hastkshep samarthit 1980 ke dashak main lokapriy vittiya dheel nitiyon ke kuch virodh kiya, kyonki minski kabhi kabhi ek ke baad kinesian arthshaastri ke rup main varnan kiya gaya he, jo pichley saal kii ek rindaata ke rup main fedral rigerv ke mahatv par bal diya sahaara or vittiya bazaaron main nijee rin kii over- sanchay ke khilaaf bahes kii. 
 minski, 1949-1958 brown vishvavidhyalay main ek shikshak the. 1957-1965 california, barkale vishvavidhyalay main arthshaastra ke associat professor the. unhone 1965 main saint louis main washington vishvavidhyalay ke arthshaastra ke professor bane or 1990 main vahaan se sevanivrutt hue. unki mrityu ke samay vah board college kii levi arthshaastra santhaan main ek vishisht vidwaan tha.
 minski paisa or credit par ayog ke liye ek salaahkaar the.
 satta nivesh vittiya bazaaron ke liye antarjaat bulbule ke saath minski, ek arthvyavastha ke saamaanya jivan chakra main, vittiya bazaar kamajori jodne siddhanton kaa prastaav rakhaa. minski samrudh baar main, korporate nakadi pravaah karj kaa bhugtan karne kii jarurat he kya aage bad jaataa he, jab ek satta utsaah viksit karata he ki daawa kiya he, or uske baad jald hi rin udhaarkartaaon ke badale main ek vittiya sankat paidaa karata he jo apni aane vaali aamadani se bhugtan kar sakate hai kya se adhik he. aise satta udhaar bulbule kaa ek parinaam ke rup main, bainkon or udhaardaataaon credit bhi rin bardaasht kar sakate hai ki companiyon ke liye uplabdhta, or baad main arthvyavastha theke kas.
 vittiya asthirataa pariklpana anubhavjanya or saidhantik pahaluon he. aasaani se manaaya anubhavjanya pahalu he ki samay-samay par, poonjivadi arthvyavasthaaon pradarshnee mudrasphitior sambhavit karne lagate hai, jo karj apsfiti baahar spin karne ke liye niyantran kii. is ke liye aarthik pranali kii pratikriyaaon prakriyaaon main arthvyavastha kaa ek andolan andolan badhanaa vaastavik he kyoki mudrasphiti or rin - apsfiti rin - apsfiti par khilaati he par mudrasphiti khilaati. giravat ko rokane ke liye uddeshy se sarkaar ke hastkshep lagate hai aitihaasik sankat se kuch main ayogy kar diya gaya he. in aitihaasik episod saboot he ki dekhne kaa samarthan kar rahe hai arthvyavastha hameshaa smith kii clasik upadeshon ke anuroop nahin he or walrus : ve arthvyavastha ke liye sabase acha samajha ja saktaa he ki nihit yah lagaataar maang ek santulan he ki sanbhaalane ke dwaara or banaae rakhane pranali. ek rin apsfiti kii clasik varnan dwaara kii peshkash kii thi. irving fisher or ek aatmanirbhar kii he ki charles kindolabargar dwaara asantulan prakriyaaon . martin na keval data kaa ek sankalan par prastut karata haivittiya karne ke liye anukool vittiya sambandhon ke udbh asthirataa, lekin yah bhi vibhinn vittiya sankat siddhanton parakh hoti he vyapaar chakra kii. aarthik siddhant rup main, vittiya asthirataa pariklpana keens ke ' general theory " ke padaarth kii ek vyakhya he. is vyakhya ke itihaas main general theory detaa he. general theory jaldi main likhaa gaya tha ke rup main sanyukt rajya america or us samay ke anya poonjivadi arthvyavasthaaon ke mahaan vittiya or vaastavik sankuchan saboot samajhane ke liye uddeshy se siddhant kaa ek hissaa tha. vittiya asthirataa pariklpana bhi joseph shumpiter sankeern arth main vittiya asthirataa pariklpana ke liye mahatvapurn kaam karata he, zaahir he, kar rahe hai haiman pi minski ke dwaara paise or vitt ke credit dekhne par chodta he. vittiya asthirataa pariklpana kii saidhantik tark mahangi puunji sampatti ke saath ek poonjivadi arthvyavastha or ek jatil, parishkrut vittiya pranali ke rup main arthvyavastha ke lakshan varnan se shuru hota he. aarthik samasya nahin balki nitian kii tulana main keens "arthvyavastha kii puunji ke vikaas, " nimna kii pahchaan kii he "vaikalpik rojgaron ke bich die gaye sansadhano kaa aavanton. " dhyaan ek par he vaastavik calendar ke madhyam se chalata he ki poonjivadi arthvyavastha jamate samay. vah ek poonjivadi arthvyavastha kii rajdhani vikaas he.
 bhavishya paise ke liye upasthit paise kaa aadan-pradaan ke saath. vartmaan paise ke utpaadan main jane ki sansadhano ke liye bhugtan karata he nivesh utpaadan, bhavishya paise jabaki "munafa" he jo ke rup main ke utpaadan main kiya jaataa he. kaa ek parinaam ke rup main jo nivesh se prakriya, vastuon par niyantran vitt poshan kiya he utpaadan ikaayiyon dwaara share puunji main se vitt poshan kiya he dendariyon - in tarikhon main paise kaa bhugtan karne ke liye patibaddhtaon hai nirdisht yaa sharton utpann hoti hai. prayek aarthik ikai ke liye,apni balance shit par dendariyon se pehle kii ek samay kii shrinkhalaa kaa nirdhaaran bhugtan patibaddhtaon, sampatti kaa ek samay shrinkhalaa utpann ke rup main bhi anumaan lagaaya nakad praptiyon. is prakaar, ek poonjivadi arthvyavastha main ateet, vartmaan or bhavishya kii puunji aastiyon or shram shakti visheshtaon dwaara lekin yah bhi pramukh vittiya sambandhon ke nirmaan or vittiya kii sanrachana karne ke liye rajdhani kii sampatti ke swaamitv kaa link vittiya sambandh.The se na keval jude hue hai sambandhon or is sanrachana main parivartan. sansthagat jatilta samudaayon ke antim maalikon ' dhan or samudaayon ko niyantrit or sanchalit ikaayiyon ki ' dhan ke bich madhyasthata kii kai paraton main parinaam ho saktaa he. vyapaar ke laabh kii ummiden vyavsaay ke liye vitt poshan ke theke ke pravaah or maujuuda vitt poshan ke theke ke bazaar mulya donon kaa nirdhaaran. vittiya aastiyon vaarta ne sanket diya samarthak ke rup main pradarshan - chaahe laabh pratiti vittiya anubandhon main patibaddhtaon ko puraa kar rahe hai ki kya nirdhaarit karte hai. 
 aadhunik duniya main, vittiya sambandhon kaa vishleshan karti he or system ke vyavhaar ke liye apane prabhaav vyavsaayon or ve aavashyak nakadi pravaah ke daayitv sanrachana ke liye pratibandhit nahin kiya ja saktaa. pariwaron, sarkaron, or ek parinaam ke rup main antarrashtriya ikaayiyon arthvyavastha kii vartmaan pradarshan isaki pushti yaa amaanya he jo yaa to daayitv sanrachana he. vittiya sansthaanon ke liye agent ke rup main acchhee tarah se saadhaaran vyapaar fermon punarvitt ke rup main sarkaron kii adhik se adhik bhagidaari ke sambandh main vittiya sanrachana kii badhati jatilta, system main pehle kii tulana main alag tarah se vyavhaar kar sakate hai yug. vishesh rup se, vitt 1929-1933 kii avadhi main ke rup main patit nahin he ki aashwast main rashtriya sarkaron ke bahut adhik se adhik bhagidaari ke kul munaafe main pravaah ke neeche kii ore bhedyata bahut kam kiya gaya he ki iskaa matlab he. haalanki, ek hi hastkshep acchhee tarah se arthvyavastha ko ulta purvaagrah kaa ek bada degree utpann ho saktaa he. 
 vittiya sambandhon kaa adhik se adhik jatilta ke baavjood, is pranali ke vyavhaar ke pramukh nirdharak munaafe kaa str bana rahata he. vittiya asthirataa pariklpana kaleki kul maang kii sanrachana ke munaafe ko nirdhaarit karata he jisamen laabh kaa dekhne ke liye, ko shaamil kiya gaya. prayek avadhi ke kul munaafe main baraabar kul nivesh main laabh aay or mazdooree ke resiver ke dwaara atyadhik saral khapat vyavhaar, saath kankal model main. ek or adhik jatil sanrachana, kul munaafe main baraabar kul nivesh ke saath saath sarkaar ke ghate main. munaafe kii ummiden munafa nivesh se nirdhaarit hote hai bhavishya main nivesh par nirbhar karti he, or ehsaas : is prakaar, dendariyon pushti kar rahe hai yaa nahin, nivesh par nirbhar karata he. vyapaariyon or unki bankeron nivesh bhavishya main jagah lene kii ummid he, kyonki nivesh ab jagah letaa he. vittiya asthirataa pariklpana he, isliye pranali vyavhaar par rin ke prabhaav kaa ek siddhant he or yah bhi karj maanya he jis tareeke se shaamil kiya gaya he. paise kii roodhiwaadee maatra siddhant ke vipareet, vittiya asthirataa pariklpana ek laabh kii maang gatividhi ke rup main gambhirtha se banking letaa he. bainkon vittiya gatividhiyon or bankeron dwaara munafa chaahate hai. ek poonjivadi arthvyavastha main sabhi udhyamiyon kii tarah, bankeron navachaar munaafe kaa aashwaasan diya he ki sabhi jaanate hai. is prakaar, ve dallalon yaa dealron ho, chaahe bankeron, ve adhigrahan kii sampatti or ve bazaar dendariyon main nayaa karne ke liye prayaas karte hai jo karj ke vyapaariyon ke hai. banking or vitt ke is abhinav visheshta parisancharan kii jiskaa veg nirantar kiya ja rahaa karne ke liye paryaapt rup se band he ek aparivartaneey item he ki prabhaav ke liye paise kii roodhiwaadee maatra ke moulik siddhant purv-manyata amaanya: isliye, is paise kii aapurti main parivartan ek rekhiy anupatik rishta nahin he ek acchhee tarah se paribhaashit mulya str ke liye.
 aarthik ikaayiyon ke liye teen alag-alag aay-rin sambandhon, jo, bachaav, satta, or ponji vitt ke rup main kar sakate hai chihnit kar rahe hai pahachaanaa ja. hegade vittaposhan ikaayiyon ke sabhi ko puraa kar sakate hai jo un logon ke hai unke nakadi pravaah se unke anubandh bhugtan dayitvon: deyta main equity vittaposhan ke adhik se adhik vajan sanrachana, adhik se adhik sambhaavna ikai ek bachaav he ki vittaposhan ikai. satta vitt ikaayiyon ki kar sakate hai ikaiyaan hai par "aay khaataa" par unke bhugtan kii patibaddhtaon ko puraa unki ve se baahar siddhant nahin chukaa sakate hai, bhale hi dendariyon, inakam nakadi pravaah. is tarah kii ikaayiyon "par role" kii jarurat he unki dendariyon: . saath rin chal, nigamon ke saath sarkaron vanijyik patr ke muddon par tair rahi he, or bainkon ko aam taur par kar rahe hai bachaav ikaayiyon. ponji ikaayiyon ke liye, parichaalan se nakadi pravaah nahin hai siddhant yaa kii adaayagi yaa to puraa karne ke liye paryaapt se apane nakadi pravaah se bakaayaa rin par dey byaaj sanchaalan. aisi ikaayiyon kii sampatti ko bechney yaa udhaar le sakate hai. udhaar byaaj kaa bhugtan karne ke liye byaaj yaa bechney kii sampatti kaa bhugtan ke rup main bhi ek ikai kii equity ko kam karti he yah dendariyon or bhavishya kii purv patibaddhta bad jaati he aay. ponji vitt ek ikai he ki suraksha ke margin ko kam karti he ki vah apane rin dharakon pradaan karata he.
 yah bachaav vittaposhan haaavee he, to arthvyavastha main acchhee tarah se maang or system yukt ek santulan ho saktaa he ki dikhaaya ja saktaa he. iske vipareet, adhik se adhik, satta or ponji vitt kaa vajan adhik sambhaavna arthvyavastha ek vichlan badhanaa pranali he ki. vittiya asthirataa pariklpana kii pehali prameya arthvyavastha yah sthir he, jiske tahat vittaposhan vyavasthaaon, or yah asthir he jisamen vittaposhan vyavasthaaon he ki he. vittiya asthirataa pariklpana ke duusare prameya he ki lambey samay tak samruddhi, ek asthir pranali ke liye bana he ki vittiya sambandhon ke liye ek sthir pranali ke liye bana he ki vittiya sambandhon se arthvyavastha pargaman kii avadhi main. vishesh rup se, achche samay kii ek lambii avadhi main ,poonjivadi arthvyavasthaaen vittiya sanrachana se sthanantarit karne ke liye karte hai vahaan he, jisamen ek sanrachana karne ke liye hej vitt ikaayiyon kaa bolbalaa satta or ponji vitt main lage ikaayiyon ko bade vajan. iske alaava, yadi satta kii ek sanstha ke saath ek arthvyavastha vittiya ikaayiyon ke adhikariyon ek mudrasphiti rajya main he, or to, maudrik baadha se mudrasphiti jaadu dena karne kaa prayaas satta ikaayiyon ponji ikaayiyon or ke nival mulya ban jayega pehle se ponji ikaayiyon teji se lupt ho jayega. natijatan, nakadi pravaah khaamiyon ke saath ikaayiyon banane ke liye prayaas karne ke liye majaboor ho jayega sthiti se baahar bech kar sthiti. yah ek karne ke liye netritv kii sambhaavna he parisampatti mulyon ke patan.
 vittiya asthirataa pariklpana ek kaa ek model he ko bahirzaat jatke par bharosa nahin karata he, jo poonjivadi arthvyavastha gambhirtha kaa vyapaar chakra utpann karte hai. pariklpana itihaas ke vyapaar chakra se baahar bhi bad jate hai ki rakhti he aantarik poonjivadi arthvyavastha kii gatishiltaa, or rakhane ke liye taiyaar kar rahe hai ki upayon or niyamon kii pranali arthvyavastha uchit seemaa ke bheetar kaam.
