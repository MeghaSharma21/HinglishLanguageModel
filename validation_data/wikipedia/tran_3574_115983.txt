 pet dard athwa udariy dard, kshanik beemaari athwa gambhir beemaari kaa ek lakshan ho shakta he. pet dard ke kaaran se ek nishchit nidaan karnaa kathin ho saktaa he, kyonki kai bimariyon main ye lakshan dekha ja shakta he. pet dard ek aam samasya he. pet dard main sabase aksar kaaran saumy or / yaa aatm - simit he, lekin or adhik gambhir kaaran ke andar tatkaal hastkshep kii aavashyaktaa ho sakati he.
 teevr udar kii paribhaasha ye he kii, kisi kaaran se bhayankar, lagaataar pet dard rahe, achaanak pet main dard ho to aise kaaran ke ilaaj liye sargical hastkshep kiya ja saktaa he. matlee or ulti, pet kaa failaav, udaar kaa phulav, bukhaar or, aghat ye saare lakshan pet dard ke sanket ke saath jude hote he. teevr pet dard kaa sabase saamaanya kaaran he teevr upantra shoth, jiske andar shleshma athwa mel jamaa honese wo sakht ho jaataa he or us ke mukh ko bandha kar detaa he.
 teevr pet ke kaaran chaynit
 aavartak pet dard kareeb 5-15 % bacchon main hota he or wo bhi 6-19 saal kii umr main . madhyam or ucch vidhyalay ke chaatro ke ek samudaay aadhaarit adhyayan main, 13-17% baccho ko saptahik pet main dard tha. chidchida antra syndrome maapdand ke hisaab se, skool ke chaatro ke bich 14%% or ucch vidhyalay ke chaatro main 6 % IBS ke liye vayask maapdand feet hota he. anya mushkil puraani chikitsa samasya ke hisaab se rap khate ke saath apane karyaalay vaastavik sankhya ke anupaat main or yatraaon chikitsa sansadhano kaa ek bahut badi sankhya main rogiyon ke liye takalif banati he. rap adhikansh rogiyon jo aksar episod ke saath jude rahe hai unako aashwaasan or takanic se chintaa or tanaav, main phayda hua he.
 jab ek chikitsak, chikitsa karne ke liye rog ke kaaran, pet dard kii shikaayat pesh karne ke baad, kitne samay se takalif he wo jaanane ke baad or shaareerik pariksha se rogiyon ke upachaar ke baad lagbhag 90% se adhik ek nidaan praapt kar letaa he.
 yah ek chikitsak ke liye bhi mahatvapurn he kii wo samaran rakhe ki pet dard kisi baahar kii samasyaon ke kaaran bhi ho saktaa he, vishesh rup se dil ke daure or nimonia he jo kabhi kabhi pet main dard kar sakate hai.
