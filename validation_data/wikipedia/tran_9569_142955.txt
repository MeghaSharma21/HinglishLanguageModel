 rashtri sanskrit santhanam, bhopal ek shaikshanik santhaan he. yah bharat sarkaar dwaara purnatah vittposhit manit vishvavidhyalay he. bharat sarkaar ne sanskrit ayog kii anushansa ke aadhaar par sanskrit ke vikaas tathaa prachaar-prasar hetu sanskrit sambaddh kendra sarkaar kii nitiyon evam kaaryakramon ke kriyanvayan ke uddeshy se 15 oktober, 1970 ko ek swytt sanghathan ke rup main rashtriya sanskrit santhaan kii sthapana kii thi. vartmaan main is manit vishvavidhyalay ke teraa parisar desh ke vibhinn pradeshon main karyrat hai. unamem madhyapradesh kii rajdhani bhopal nagar main shikshastr 2002-2003 se bhopal parisar sanchalit he.
 rashtri sanskrit santhaan ke sanstha ke bahirnium main ghoshit uddeshy is prakaar he-
 santhaan kii sthapana ke uddeshy parmparik sanskrit vidya va shodh kaa prachaar, vikaas va protsaahan he or unka paalan karte hueh-
 bharat main sarvapratham dilli se rashtriya sanskrit santhaan kii pravriti kaa prarambh sanskrit ke vikaas or prachaar-prasar hetu tathaa sanskrit se sambaddh kendra sarkaar kii nitiyon evam kaaryakramon ke kriyanvayan ke uddeshy se 15 oktober, 1970 ko ek swytt sanghathan ke rup main hua. bhopal parisar ko sthapit karne ke liye taatkaaleen kendriya mantri da. murali manohar joshi, maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantralay bharat sarkaar ne patr kramank sanskrit-1- sexn dinaank 31 march 2002 ke dwaara sthapana aadeshpatr nirgat kiya tha. ukt shasnadesh ko kriyanvit karne ke liye raa. san. san. nai dilli ne patr kramank-37021/2002-admin/1108 karyaalay aadesh sankhya 107 / dinaank 05.06.2002 ke dwaara etdarth praachaarya aadi vibhaag uplabdh karakar 1 july 2002 se bhopal parisar ko kriyasheel kiya tha. is tithi se praayah das varsh purv hi 1991-92 main tatkaaleen maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantri arjuna singh kii ghoshnaa se bhopal parisar ke sthapana kii yojana prakalpit hui thi or usee samay madhyapradesh shashan ne patr kramank -epa 6730/shas. /sa.-2bi/93 dinaank 27.05.1993/20.07.1993 ke shasnadesh ke dwaara is parisar ke vikaas ke liye 10 acre bhoomi nickshulk aavantit kii thi. is prakaar bhopal main 6 july 2002 se barkatulla vishvavidhyalay ke atithi grih ke kaksh main bhopal parisar kii gatividhi prarambh hone par 02 agast 2002 ko arera colony main bhatak bhawan praapt karke usamen chaatro ke pravesh evam shikshan kii gatividhi prarambh hui. dinaank 16 sitambar 2002 ko bhopal parisar main aupchaarik udghaatan kiya gaya. dinaank 27.02.2003 ko aavantit bhukhand par parisaradhaar shilanyas madhyapradesh ke tatkaaleen mukhyamantri digvijay singh ke haathon hua tha. dinaank 19 sitambar 2005 ko shaikshik evam prashasnic mukhya bhawan kaa shilanyas tatkaaleen maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantri arjuna singh ke haathon sampann hua tha.
 santhaan main madhyapradesh evam anya rajyon se aanevaale chaatro ke liye 333 chaatro kaa purush chatravaas, 108 chaatraon kaa mahila chatravaas, atithi nivas, praachaarya evam karmchaaree awaason kii suvidhaayen hai. yahan bharat or nepal se bhi chaatr sanskrit seekhney hetu aate hai. bhawan main atyaadhunik sanganak prayogshaala bhi he.
 rashtri sanskrit santhaan nimnlikhit kaaryakramon ke kriyanvayan hetu kritasankalp he.
 yahaan uplabdh paathyakramon main nimna hai:-
 parisar main vartmaan main sahitya, vyakaran, jyotish, shiksha, jain darshan tathaa aadhunik vibhaag sanchalit hai. saath hi veda, puraana itihaas, sankhya yoga ityadi vibhaagon kii sweekruti parisar ke sameep uplabdh he. prakashastri, shastri, acharya shikshashasthri, shikshacharya tathaa vidyavaridhi paathyakram parisar main gunvatttapurna paddati se sanchalit kiye ja rahe hai. saath hi anaupchaarik sanskrit shikshan, vaastu, jyotish ke praanpatareey evam patropadhi paathyakramon ke saath mukt swadhyay peeth main duurastha shiksha ke madhyam se bhi paathyakram sanchalit ho rahe hai.
