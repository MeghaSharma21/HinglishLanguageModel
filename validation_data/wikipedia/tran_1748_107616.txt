 gilbert newton luis america ke rasayanajna the jo sahasanyojak bandha kii khoj evam electron-yugma ke kancept dene ke liye prasiddh hai. luis ne ooshmaagatiki, prakashrasayan, tathaa samasthanik vilgan par bhi kaam kiya tathaa amla or kshar kaa concept bhi diya.
 luis kaa janm masachusets pradesh ke boston nagar ke paas hua tha. aap haarward vishvavidhyalay ke snaatak the or yaheen se san 1899 main aapane pi-ech. di. kii degree praapt kii.
 san 1904 main aap philipino dweep main baat or maapon ke paryvekshak tathaa vaigyanik vibhaag main rasayanajna niyukt hue, kintu ek varsh pashchaat ye masachusets ke institute ov technology kii bhautikee rasayan prayogshaala main anusandhan karne lage. san 1912 main aapaki niyukti california vishvavidhyalay main rasayan ke professor tathaa college ke dean ke pad par hui. pratham vishwayuddh ke samay sainik seva kii. iske baad ye phir california vishvavidhyalay laut aae. yahan aapane sanyojaktaa tathaa parmaanuon or anuon kii sanrachana par tathaa randail ke sahyog se, 'ooshmaagatiki tathaa rasinic padaarthon kii mukt urja' par do mahatvapurn granth likhe.
 luis ne sanyojaktaa ke aadhunik siddhant kii neenv daali tathaa amla or ksharak kii dharna ko vistrut rup diya. baari hydrogen ko aapane hi sarvapratham viyukt kiya tha. aapke anusandhanon se jaivik padaarthon ke rangon tathaa pratidipti or sfuradipti kii jatil prakriyaaon ke samajhane main badi sahaayta mili.
