 "bajpeyi" yaa "vaajapeyi" vishesh rup se uttar pradesh tathaa madhya pradesh kaa ek pramukh uttar bhartiya brahman upnaam he| yah brahmanon ke kanyakubj upvarg main avasthi, dikshit, ], treepathi, agnihotri aadi upanaamon ke saath aata he| bajpeyi vyapak rup se vaajapeyi ke paryaay samajha jaataa upnaam|
 bajpeyi yaa vaajapeyi wo hai jo vaajapeya yajna karte hai | vaajapeya yajna kaa vishisht dharmik evam vaidic mahatv he| yah soma yagyon kaa sabase mahatvapurn he| yajna aamtaur par ucch str or bade paimaane par kiya jaataa he| 
 bhartiya itihaas ke adhyayan se pataa chalata he ki savai jai singh ne kam se kam do yajna pradarshan kiye jo ki vaidic sahitya main "vaajapeya" tathaa "ashwamedh" naamon se varnit hai| 'ishwarvilash mahakavy' ke anusaar 'jai singh pratham vaajapeya yajna kiya or "samraat" samraat oopaadhi dhaaran kii| aadhunik bharat main bajpeyi logon ne abhinay shiksha, sahitya, kootneeti, raajnneeti main mahatvapurn sammaan arjeet kiya he|
 duniya bhar main bajpeyi ek hi gotra, "upamanyu" se he or isi naam se ek rishi ke vanshaj maane jate he| ek hi gotra ke hone ke kaaran ye aapas main bhai or bahan maane jate hai or inkaa aapas main vivaah nahin kiya ja saktaa|
