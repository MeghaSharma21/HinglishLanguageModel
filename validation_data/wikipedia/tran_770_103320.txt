 kadpa bharat ke andhrapradesh rajya ke dakshin-madhya main sthit ek shahar he. yah kadpa jila kaa jila mukhyalay bhi he. kadpa shabd kii utpatti telegu shabd gadpa se hui he jiskaa arth dwaar hota he. pehle ye kadappa naam se janaa jaataa tha baad main kadpa ho gaya. madhyakaal kadpa anek mahapurushon ke liye janaa jaataa tha. vemna, potuluri veerabrahmam, annamacharya, pemmsani timmaa nayudu jaise mahapurush yaheen paidaa hue. kadpa andhrapradesh ke roilseema kshetra kaa mashhoor shahar he. yah andhrapradesh ke dakshin madhya main sthit he. yah penna nadi se aath kilometre duuri par sthit he. yah shahar teen ore se nallamala or paalkonda pahadiyon se ghira he. is shahar ko dwaar bhi kaha jaataa he. darasal yah uttar se tirupati sthit bhagwaan shri venkateshwar kii pavitra pahari pagoda aane vaale logon ke liye dwaar jaisaa he.
 ramayana ke saat candon main ek kiskindhakand, kadpa ke vontimetta main hui ghatana mana jaataa he. vontamitta kadpa shahar se bees kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. yahaan kaa aanjaneya swami gandi bhi ramayana kaa hissaa mana jaataa he. aisa mana jaataa he ki bhagwaan shri ram ne apane baan se gandi par aanjaneya swami vigraham banaaya tha, jo shri sita kii khoj main aanjaneya hanuman se sahaayta maangane kaa pratik he.
 kadpa apane itihaas main vibhinn shaskaon ke adheen rahaa he, jinamein nizaam or chola, vijaynagar samrajya or mysore samrajya shaamil hai. yah apane masaaledaar vyanjan ke liye bhi janaa jaataa he. 
 shahar kaa naam telugu shabd "gadpa" se niklaa tha jiskaa arth he daravaaza yaa praveshdwar yaa get. ise thirumala hills ke sambandh main is naam ko adhigrahit kiya; thirumala pahadiyon tak pahunchane ke liye puraane dinon main kadpa se guzarana pasta tha. puraane telegu main shabd kadappa kaa matlab tha ki aadhunik maanak telugu main gadpa ko viksit kiya gaya tha, jabaki shahar ke naam ne puraane swaad ko barkaraar rakhaa tha. ise "kuddapah" likhaa gaya tha lekin naam ke sthaneey uccharan ko darshane ke liye 19 agast 2005 ko "kadpa" main badal diya gaya tha. haal hi main paae gaye kuch shilalekhon ne hirananagaram ke naam ko zaahir kiya.
 gyarahavi se chaudavin sadi tak kadpa shahar renati chola samrajya kaa hissaa rahaa. chaudavin sadi ke ant main yah vijaynagar samrajya kaa hissaa ban gaya. do dashakon tak yah vijaynagar ke shaasha gandicota nayak vansh ke aadhin rahaa he. yahaan kaa sabase mashhoor shaasha pemmasaani thimmaa nayudu tha, jisane is kshetra kaa khub vikaas kiya. usne kai tanks or mandir banawaye. 1565 isvi main mughalon ne golkunda par kabja kar liya. mir jumala ne gandicota kile par chhapemari kii or chinna thimmaa nayudu ko dhokhe se haraa diya. 1800 isvi main angrejon ne kadpa ko apane kabje main le liya. yah shahar kaaphi purana he, lekin qutub shahi commander neknam khan ne shahar kaa vistaar kiya or ise nekanamabad naam diya. kuch dinon tak yah naam prachlit rahaa lekin angrejon ne ise kadpa kahana hi zyada pasand kiya. angrejon ke adheen hone ke baad yah pradhan samahrta major munro ke adheen chaar samahrtaaon main ek kaa mukhyalay ban gaya. kadapapa nabaavon ke smriti avshesh ab bhi shahar main maujuud hai. inamen sabase prasiddh tower or dargah hai. is shahar ke aasapaas kai mandir or teen carch hai. kadpa bahut hi priy sthaan he. yahaan kai darshneey sthal he. ontimitta inhen sthalon main ek he. ontimitta ko yakashila nagaram bhi kaha jaataa he.
 kadpa 14 degree 14°28′ or 14.47°N uttari akshansh tathaa 78°49′ or 78.82° purvi deshaantar ke bich sthit he. hyderabad se 412 kimi or andhra pradesh ke roilseema kshetra main bangalore se lagbhag 280 kimi duuri par sthit he. yah shahar banga yaa ralla vanka main sthit he jo paalakonda pahadiyon se dakshin main or purv main panna ke duusari taraf lankamala ke uttar main pahadiyon kaa bich he. samudratal se isaki ausat oonchai 138 meter he. kadpa jile kaa kshetrafal 8723 varg kilometre he. yah aniyamit samaantar chaturbhuj aakaar main purvi ghat kii pahadiyon se lambavat banta hua he. donon bhaagon kii akruti bilkul alag he. jile kaa uttar-purv or dakshin-purv bhaag nimna dhaal kaa samtal maidan he. jabaki doosraa bhaag dakshini or dakshin-pashchim bhaag ucch he. pashchimi or purvi ghat kii pahadiyaan donon taraf khadey hai, ise garmiyon or sardi kii charam hawaaon se bachaate hai.
 kadpa main ushnkaribandheey geele or shushk jalwaayu varsh ke ucch taapmaana ke aadhaar par visheshta he. isamen 50 degree celsius se adhik tak pahunchane kaa record he. garmi garm or ardra jalwaayu ke saath vishesh rup se asahaj hoti he. is samay ke dauraan taapmaana kam se kam 34 degree celsius se hota he or adhiktam 40 degree celsius tak bad saktaa he. din ke madhya main tisare dashak ke taapmaana main taapmaana hota he. garmi ke maheenon ke dauraan ardrata lagbhag 75% he. monsoon kaa mausam kshetra main kaaphi barish laata he. kadpa ko dakshin pashchim monsoon ke saath-saath uttar purv monsoon donon main barish hoti he. june se oktober aamtaur par monsoon hota he. winter tulanaatmak rup se halke hote hai or monsoon kii shuruyaat ke baad taapmaana kam hota he. is samay ke dauraan taapmaana adhiktam 25 degree celsius hota he or adhiktam 35 degree celsius tak bad jaataa he. sardi ke mausam main ardrata bahut kam he. sheetakaaleen mausam jagah par jane kaa sabase acha samay he. 
 kadpa kii jansankhya pehali baar bharat kii pehali janaganana ke dauraan 1871 main gina gaya tha, jo 1911 tak  tak yojit kiya gaya tha. haalanki, 1961 tak koi aitihaasik record uplabdh nahin hone ke baad. kadappa andhra pradesh ke sabase bade or sabase tej vikasshil shaharon main se ek he. 1991 kii janaganana ke anusaar shahar kii jansankhya 1,21,463 thi. 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar isamen vruddhi nahin hui, jisamen 20 ward aabaadi ke liye 1,26,505 lakh darj kii gai, jo ausat vruddhi dar 0.36 pratishat thi. baad main ise 2005 main nagar nigam main parivartit kar diya gaya. 2011 kii janaganana ke asthaayee aankadon ke anusaar, kadappa shahari samooh kii aabaadi 344,078 he, jinamein se purush 172,969 hai or mahilaayein 171,109 hai. sakshartaa dar 79.34 pratishat he. shahari aabaadi main 65% hindu, 32% musalmaan or 3% isai hai. 
 telegu shahar main aadhikarik or sabase badi boli jane vaali bhashaa he. uttar bhartiya vyapaariyon ke bich shahar ke kai kshetron main hindi bhi boli jaati he. vikaas ke kaaran angreji din ba din lokapriyata praapt kar rahi he. puure shahar main urdu bhi boli jaati he.
 kadpa nagar nigam shahar kii naagarik jaruraton kii dekharekh karata he or varsh 2005 main gathit kiya gaya tha. isamen 50 nagarpalika ward hai jo pratyaksh chunaav ke madhyam se ek nagarsevak dwaara pratinidhitiv karte hai, jo badale main mahayor kaa chunaav karata he. jila adaalat shahar main hi sthit he.
 shahar main vibhinn rajwanshon ke prabhaav ke saath samrudh samskruti or viraasat he. hindu dharm, islam, isai dharm, buddha dharm or jain dharm jaise vibhinn dharmon ke astitv ke saath vibhinn anushthan, reeti-rivaaj or paramparaayen hai. yah shahar apane aitihaasik devuni kadpa or amin pir dargah ke liye janaa jaataa he.
 shilparamam kadappa ke bahari ilaake main sthit ek shilp gaanv he. 
 kadpa apane masaaledaar or paak bhojan ke liye jane jate hai jo dakshin bhartiya bhojan ke samaan hi he. kaaram dosa kadpa nagarikon kii sabase pasandida vastu main se ek he. logon ko apane naashte main kaaram dosa, sambhar or chatnee he. chaaval, daal or kari aamtaur par dopaher ke bhojan ke rup main parosa jaataa he. adhikansh restaraan dakshin bhartiya thali kii seva karte hai jisamen in vyanjanon ko unke dopaher ke bhojan or raat ke khaane ke menu main shaamil kiya jaataa he. yadhyapi isamen apane vyanjanon main ek dakshin bhartiya sparsh he, iske alaava isamen ek vividh prakaar kii vividhta he jisamen raagi sangti yaa raagi mudda, boti kari, natukodi chikan, paaya kari ityadi shaamil hai. chikan kari ke saath raagi sangti kadpa main mukhya bhojan he or yah shahar main sabase prasiddh vyanjanon main se ek bhi he. sthaneey restaraan main vyanjanon kii kai anya kismon ko bhi paaya ja saktaa he. bharat ke kai anya shaharon kii tarah fast food bhi shahar main apni pahunch badha rahaa he. biryani, pulao, rasmalai, halava ityadi bhi pasandida bhojan hai.
 shahar kii arthvyavastha bade paimaane par krishi or mining par aadhaarit he. jila mukhyalay hone ke naate sabhi prakaar ke sarkari vibhaag shahar ke bheetar sthit hai. adhikansh pariwaron ke liye aay kaa strot sarkari naukariyon or nijee kshetra kii naukariyon ke madhyam se vibhinn vibhaagon main he, jisamen vyapaar, dukaane, aatithy udyog, vipanan shaamil he. in ke alaava paryatano ko shahar main arthvyavastha kaa ek hissaa bhi banataa he. kadpa mckinse and company dwaara chune gaye 49 metropolitan cluster main se ek he jo bharat main vikaas sthal ke rup main he. 
 praathmik or madhyamik vidhyalay shiksha rajya ke skool shiksha vibhaag ke sarkari, sahaayta praapt or nijee skulon dwaara pradaan kii jaati he. 
 kadpa city sadak, rail or vaayu margon se acchhee tarah se judaa hua he.
 kadpa ke hyderabad, bangalore, chennai or vijaywada jaise anya pramukh sthanon par acchhee sadak connactivity he. apiesartisi kadappa jile ke vibhinn sthalon or dakshin bharat ke anya shaharon main bas sevaayen pradaan karata he. shahar kii kul sadak lambaai 803.84 kimi he. 
 kadpa ke paas shahar kaa apana railway stesan he. yah rajya ke sabase shuruaati railway stesan main se ek he jo 1866 ke aasapaas kholaa gaya tha. dakshin-madhya kshetra main sabase vyast linon main se ek mumbai-chennai line kadpa railway stesan se guzarati he. yah guntakal railway divijan dakshin madhya railway kshetra main e shrenee railway stesan main se ek he. nai railway line kadpa-bangalore anubhag nirmaan apane charan pankti main he.
 kadpa hawaii adde 7 june 2015 ko hawaii yatayaat ke liye kholaa gaya tha. yah shahar ke 12 kimi uttar pashchim kii duuri par sthit he.
