 uttari america main formon ke pashuon kii rakhwali karne vaale ko couboy kaha jaataa he, paramparaagat rup se ghode par savaar hokar vah yah kaam karata he or aksar farm sambandhit anya anek prakaar ke kaam bhi kiya karata he. 19veen shatabdi ke ant main yah aitihaasik america coubwoy uttari mexico ke vakuero paramparaon se utpann hua or isane vishesh mahatv kaa aakaar grahan kar liya or ek dantakatha ban gaya. iske ek upaprakaar ko rangler kehete hai, jo vishesh rup se maweshiyon ke kaam main prayog kiye jane vaale ghodon kii dekhbhaal karata he. farm yaa khet ke kaam ke alaava, kuch couboy rodio ke liye kaam karte hai yaa usamen bhaag lete hai. cougirls, 19veen sadi ke ant main pehali baar is tarah paribhaashit, kii aitihaasik bhoomika kaa bahut kam dastaveji pramaan he, lekin aadhunik duniya main inhone bilkul samaan kaary karne kii apni kshamta sthapit kii he or apni uplabdhiyon ke liye yathesht sammaan praapt kiya he. vishwa ke anya anek bhaagon main bhi couboy yaa maweshiyon ke prabandhakarta hua karte hai, khaskar dakshin america or australia main, jo apane deshon main couboy jaise hi kaam kiya karte hai.
 spain or americon ke shuruaati europea adhivaasiyon main couboy kii gahari aitihaasik jaden paayi jaati hai. sadiyon se, ilaakon main bhinnata, jalwaayu or anek samskritiyon kii charvaaha paramparaon ke prabhaav ne upkarnon, poshaakon or pashuon ke prabandh kii anek alag shaileyon kaa nirmaan kiya. nity-vyaavhaarik couboy ko aadhunik duniya ke liye anukulit kiya gaya, tab couboy ke upkaran or takanic ko bhi kuch had tak anukulit karnaa padaa, haalanki anek clasik paramparaayen aaj bhi sangrakshit hai.
 angreji shabd couboy kii utpatti anek puraani shabdaawaliyon se hui he, donon yugon main yah maveshi yaa maweshiyon ke dekhbhaal sambandhi kaary ke liye aayaa he.
 angreji bhashaa main "couboy" shabd 1725 memprakat hua. yah vakuero kaa sidhaa angrezi anuwaad prathit hota he, jo ki ek spanish shabd he or ghode par savaar hokar maweshiyon kii dekhbhaal karne vaale ke liye is shabd kaa prayog hota he. yah vaaka shabd se vyutpann he, jiskaa arth hota he "cou", jo ki latin shabd vaakka se aayaa he. couboy ke liye ek anya angreji shabd he bakaaru, jo ki vakuero kaa hi angrejikaran he. kam se kam ek bhashavid kaa anumaan he ki bakaaru shabd arabi bhashaa ke bakra yaa bakhra shabd se vyutpann he, jiskaa arth bhi "hifer" yaa "bachhiya" hota he or sadiyon ke islamic shashan ke dauraan yah shabd spanish main pravesh kar gaya ho saktaa he.
 multah, shabdic rup se is shabd kaa kaa arth ho saktaa he- "ek ladkaa jo gaayon kii dekhbhaal karata he." 1849 tak yah america pashchim ke ek vayask charvaahe yaa maveshi prabandhakarta ke rup main aadhunik arth main viksit ho gaya. "couboy" shabd main vibhinnata baad main saamne aayi. 1852 main "couhand" or 1881 main "coupok" shabd prakat hue, jiskaa prayog mul rup se potparivahan ke liye maweshiyon ko ek lambey barche se konch-konch kar train ke dibbon main ladane vaale vyaktiyon ke liye hi kiya jaataa. america angreji main coubwoy ke naamon main bakaaru, coupok, couhand or coupanchar shaamil hai. puure pashchim or khaskar great plance tathaa rocky mountens main "couboy" shabd aam he, jabaki "bakaaru" kaa prayog mukhya rup se great basin or california main hota he or "coupanchar" kaa prayog adhikanshtah texas tathaa aasapaas ke kshetron main hua karata he.
 couboy shabd mahaj spanish kaa anuwaad hone ke bajaae isaki jade angreji bhashaa main bhi hai. multah, angreji shabd "couhrd" kaa prayog kisi charvaahe ke liye hua karata tha, kii hi tarah, ek bhend charaanevaala yaa gaderia) or yah kisi kishoravastha se pehle yaa kishor balak ke liye kehalaata, jo ki aam taur par paidal hi kaam kiya karata. angreji bhashaa main yah shabd bahut purana he, varsh 1000 se pehle isaki utpatti hui. pracheen kaal main, bhed, maveshi or bakriyon ko charaane or unki dekhbhaal ke kaam aksar hi bacchon ke jimme hote the or abhi bhi vibhinn teesri duniya kii samskritiyon main yah kaam noujvaan hi kiya karte hai.
 aavashyak kaushal viksit karne ke liye samay or shaareerik kshamta kii jarurat kii vajah se, couboy aksar apana karier ek kishor ke rup main shuru kiya karte the, unamem paryaapt kaushal aa jane ke baad ve mazdooree paane lagate or ve, chot lagane se aapng nahin hone par, maweshiyon yaa ghodon kii dekhbhaal jindagi bhar kar sakate the. sanyukt rajya america main, kuch mahilaon ne bhi maweshiyon kii dekhbhaal kaa kaarybhaar sambhala or aavashyak kaushal siikhaa, haalanki "cougirl" ko 19veen sadi kii samaapti tak vyapak rup se manyata yaa abhiswikruti nahin mili thi. aaj pashchimi pashu formon main aam taur par vayask couboy karyrat hai. godhan yaa anya maweshiyon kii jimmedaari ko ab bacchon yaa shuruaati kishoron ke upyukt kaam nahin mana jaataa. haalanki, pashu farm ke maahaul main bade honevaale ladke or ladkiyaan donon hi aksar ghudsawaari sikh lete hai or shaareerik rup se sakasham hote hi aamtaur par vayaskon kii nigaraanee main farm ke buniyadi kaushalon kaa pradarshan karne lagate hai. aise yuvakon, kishoravastha ke aakhri dinon main, ko aksar pashu farm main "couboy" kii jimmedaari di jaati he or ve aisa kaam karne ke yogya hote hai jisamen paripakvata or vivekshiltaa kii jarurat hoti he, jo aam taur par unke shahari saathiyon se ummid nahin kii jaati.
 couboy parampara kaa aarambh spain se hota he, madhyayugin spain kii hessinda pranali ke saath isaki shuruyaat hui. is style kaa pashupaalan iberian prayadweep ke adhikansh bhaag main fail gaya or baad main, americon main laayaa gaya. donon hi kshetron kii jalwaayu shushk he, jahaan kam ghaas hoti he or isiilie maweshiyon ke bade samoohon ko paryaapt chaare ke liye vishaal bhoomi kii aavashyaktaa padati he. kisi paidal vyakti ke liye itane vishaal sthaan kaa prabandhan sambhav nahin, isiilie ghudsavaar vakuero ke vikaas kii jarurat mahasoos kii gayi. spanish ghudsavaar parampara ke vibhinn pahaluon ko spain main arab shashan ke chihn ke rup main dekha ja saktaa he, saath hi purvi prakaar ke ghodon ke upyog, ek chote rakaab dwaara laa gineta savaar style kii visheshta, majboot kaath kii jin or aid kaa upyog, baari nakel yaa hakamor, or ghodon se jude anya upkarnon tathaa takanic jaise muron ke tatv bhi isamen shaamil hai. badale main hakamor jaisi arabi parampara ke kuch pahaluon ke nishaan pracheen faras main paye ja sakate hai.
 16veen shatabdi ke dauraan, kanquistadorse  or anya spanish upniveshi apane saath americon main ghodon or anya maweshiyon ke paalan kii parampara saath lete aaye, aaj ke mexico or florida se isaki shuruyaat kii. spain kii paramparaayen bhugolic, paryaavarniya or saanskrithik paristhitiyon dwaara new spain main roopantarit ho gayin, jo baad main mexico or dakshin-pashchim sanyukt rajya america ban gaya. iske badale main, spanish prabhaav ke kaaran americon kii bhoomi or logon ne bhi naatakeey parivartan dekha.
 is prakaar, haalanki yah lokapriy rup se america mana jaataa he, lekin paaramparik couboy spanish parampara se shuru hua he, jo aaj ke mexico or dakshin-pashchim sanyukt rajya america main vakuero main or jalisko tathaa michnkaan kshetron main chaaro main viksit hua. adhikansh vakuero mestijo yaa desi america mul ke hua karte or adhikansh haveendados spanish hote. mexican paramparaayen dakshin or uttar donon ore failati gayin or argentina se lekar canada tak ghudsavaar paramparaon ko prabhavit kiya.
 ghodon kaa aagaman vishesh rup se mahatvapurn tha, kyonki pragaitihaasik him yug kii samaapti ke baad se americon se ashviya nasl vilupt ho gayi thi. baharhaal, america main ghodon kii taadaad main teji se vruddhi hui or spanish tathaa baad main anya deshon ke adhivaasiyon kii safalataa ke liye mahatvapurn ban gayi. praarambhik ghode multah andaalusi, barb or arabi nasl ke hua karte the, lekin chaynaatmak prajnan or jangal main bhaag jane vaale jaanwaron ke prakritik chayan ke madhyam se uttari or dakshini america main anek vilakshan america ghodon ko paidaa kiya gaya. mastang or anya aupniveshik ghodon kii naslon ko ab "junglee" kaha jaataa he, lekin darasal ye junglee ghode hai - jinhen paaltu jaanavar bana liya gaya.
 angreji bolne vaale vyapaaree or adhivaasee pashchim kii ore fail gaye, angreji or spanish paramparaon, bhashaa or samskruti kaa kuch had tak vilay ho gaya. 1848 main mexican-america yuddh se pehle, new england ke vyapaaree jinhone jahaaj ke dwaara california kii yatra kii to unki mulaakaat vishaal maveshi farm se nikalanevaale chamadi or charbi se bane saamaan kaa vyapaar karanevaale haveenda or vakuero donon se hui. inke saath america vyapaaree, jo baad main santa phe trail ke rup main jane gaye, ke saath bhi vakuero jeevanstyli kaa sampark hua. in praarambhik aakasmiktaaon ke saath shuru karne par vakuero kii jeevanstyli or bhashaa main roopantaran hona shuru hua jo ki angreji saanskrithik parampara ke saath vilay hota chalaa gaya or america parampara main yah couboy ke rup main janaa jane laga. 
 railmarg kii shuruyaat or america grih yuddh ke samay gomance kii maang main vruddhi ko dekhte hue ichonic america couboy ne pashu farm se pashuon ko nikatatam railway stesan le jane kii jarurat ke saath puraani paramparaon ko sanyukt karke viksit kiya, jo aksar saikadon mile dur hua karte.
 america couboy anek sroton se taiyaar hue. 1860 ke dashak ke ant se, america grih yuddh ke baad or maveshi udyog ke vistaar se sangh or rajya sangh donon ke purv sainik pashchim chale aaye, kaam kii maang karne lage, jaisaa ki badi taadaad main adheer gore logon ne aam taur par kiya. badi sankhya main bandhanmukt africa-americanyon ne bhi couboy jivan main pravesh kiya, aanshik rup se isaki ek vajah yah he ki us samay america samaaj ke anya kshetron kii apeksha pashchim main utana adhik bhedbhaav nahin tha. ek badi taadaad main us kshetra main rahane vaale mexican or america indiens bhi couboy ke rup main kaam kiya karte the.
 anek praarambhik vakuero indian the, jinhen spanish mishnon main maweshiyon kii dekhbhaal ke liye prashikshit kiya gaya tha. baad main, khaskar 1890 ke baad, indian logon ke saath "sammilan" kii jab america niti bani, tab kuch indian awasaiya vidyalayon main bhi pashupaalan kii vidya di jane lagi. aaj, pashchimi sanyukt rajya america ke kuch mul americanyon ke paas apane maveshi or chote farm hai or anek abhi bhi couboy kaa kaam kar rahe hai, khaastaur par indian aarakshanon ke kareeb ke pashu formon main. rodio paripath par "indian couboy" kaa dikhaanaa bhi ek aam ban gaya he.
 chunki us avadhi kii saamaajik sanrachana main couboy kaa sthaan neeche hua karata tha, so vibhinn naslon ke vaastavik anupaat ke pukhta aankade uplabdh nahin hai. ek lekhak ne kaha he ki couboy "... do virgon ke the - ek ve jinaki bhartee texas tathaa purvi dhalaan ke anya rajyon se kii gayi; or duusare ve mexican jo dakshin-pashchimi kshetra se the. ..." janaganana record ke anusaar sabhi couboy main se 15% africa- america vanshaj the-jiname se 25% texas se baahar chale gayi, unamem se bahut kam uttar-pashchim kii ore gayi. isi tarah, mexican vansh ke couboy kii sankhya ausat rup se kul kaa 15% thi, lekin ve texas or dakshin-pashchim main bahut aam the.
 vibhinn nasl ke baavjood, adhikansh couboy nichle saamaajik virgon ke hi the or unhen bahut kam mazdooree mila karti thi. ausat couboy pratidin lagbhag ek dollar kamaaya karata tha, iske alaava bhojan ke saath-saath aamtaur par barrakanuma imaarat ke ek khule kamare jaise bankshouse main ek bistar bhi mila karata tha.
 arddh-junglee ilaakon main khule sthanon main badi sankhya main maweshiyon ko rakhaa jaataa or unhen saal ke zyaadaatar samay, adhikanshtah bina rakhwali ke charne ke liye chhod diya jaataa tha. kai maamalon main, vibhinn pashu farm maalik "sangh" bana liya karte or ek hi sthaan par apane maweshiyon ko charne ke liye chhod diya karte the. jaanwaron par apane swaamitv ke nirdhaaran ke liye un par ek vishisht brands chihnit kar diya jaataa, iske liye garm lohe kaa istemaal kiya jaataa, aam taur par tab jab maveshi bachade hua karte. khule had yaa sthaan main dekha jane vaala praathmik maveshi tha laungorn, jo 16veen sadi main aaayaatit mul spanish laughorn se utpann hue the; haalanki 19veen sadi ke ant main anya nasl ke maveshi bhi pashchim laaye gayi, inamen maance se bharpoor hereford shaamil hai or aksar laungorn ke saath inkaa sankar prajnan hua karata.
 yuva bachhadon ko chihnit karne ke liye or paripakv pashuon ko bechney ke iraade se farm maalik aam taur par vasant ritu main pashuon ko ikattha kiya karte. ikattha karne yaa rounssop ke liye couboy or ghodon donon ko hi kai tarah ke kaushal kii aavashyaktaa he. jo vyakti jhund se maweshiyon ko alag karte hai unamem ucchtam str ke kaushal kii jarurat hoti he or saath hi vishesh rup se prashikshit "kating" ghodon par savaar hone ke liye bhi aise kaushal kii jarurat hoti he, ghodon ko maweshiyon kii gatividhiyon kaa anusaran karne ke liye prashikshit kiya jaataa he, unhen anya ghodon kii tulana main teji se rokane or mudane ke laayak banaaya jaataa he. ek baar maveshi alag kar liye jate, tab adhikansh couboy yuva bachhadon ko baandhne kaa kaam karte or uske baad unhen chihnit kiya jaataa or unhen badhiya kiya jaataa. kabhi-kabhi chihnit karne yaa anya upachaar ke liye bade maweshiyon ko vash main karne ke liye bhi yah aavashyak hota.
 maweshiyon ke rounssop ke liye badi sankhya main ghodon kii jarurat padati. harek couboy ko ek din ke kaam ke silsile main teen se chaar taaje ghodon kii aavashyaktaa hoti. ghode khud bhi ikatthe ho jaaya karte. pashchim main yah aam chalan tha ki paaltu ghodi se paidaa hue yuva bachede ko khuli jagah ke arddh-junglee maahaul main "banaila" chhod diya jaataa rahaa. vahaan "banaile" jhund bhi hua karte the, praayah jinhen mustangs naam se janaa jaataa he. donon prakaar ko gher kar ikattha kiya jaataa or viksit pashuon ko upyogi banaaya jaataa tha, is prakriya ko horse braking yaa "broncho-basted"  kehete hai, yah kaam aam taur par un couboy dwaara kiya jaataa jo ghodon ko prashikshit karne ke visheshgy hon. kuch maamalon main, ghodon ko vash main karne ke liye behad krur tareekon kaa istemaal kiya jaataa tha or aise pashu kabhi bhi puri tarah se vishwasaneey nahin hua karte the. haalanki, anya couboy ne pashuon ke saath adhik maanviya dhang se bartaav karne kii jarurat mahasoos kii or apni ghoda prashikshan paddati main sudhaar kiya, aksar hi vakuero dwaara istemaal kii gayi takneekon ko phir se siikhaa gaya, khaskar californio parampara kii takneekon ko. vinamr dhang se prashikshit kiye gayi ghode adhik vishwasaneey hote the or vibhinn prakaar ke kaamon ke liye upyogi the.
 apane maveshi or ghodon ko sanbhaalane ke apane kaushal ke pareekshan ke liye couboy ke bich aapas main anaupchaarik pratiyogita aarambh hui or is tarah, karyrat couboy ke aavashyak kaaryon se rodio pratispardhaa viksit hui. 
 madhya 19veen sadi se pehle, adhikansh pashu farm ke maalik mukhya rup se apni jaruraton ke liye pashupaalan kiya karte or adhishesh maance tathaa khaal sthaneey rup se bech diya karte the. vibhinn nirmaan prakriyaaon main khaal, sing, khur or vasa kaa bazaar bhi simit tha. rashtriya str par, 1865 se pehle, gomance kii maang bhi kam thi. haalanki, america grih yuddh kii samaapti ke baad philip dannforth armar ne chicago main maance packing kaa ek plant kholaa, jo armar and company naam se janaa jane laga or maance packing udyog ke vistaar se gomance kii maang main ullekhneey vruddhi hui. 1866 se, uttari bazaaron main maveshi beche ja sakate the $40 prati kii dar se, isse bazaar se dur maveshi paalan karnaa sambhavi rup se laabhadaayak ho gaya, khaskar texas se.
 pehali baar 1866 main maweshiyon ko bade paimaane par chicago ke liye potvahan ke makasad se texas se kareeb ke railway stesan le jane kaa prayaas kiya gaya; us samay anek pashu farm maalik sabase kareebi railway stesan, jo us samay cedalia, misuri main tha, tak apane maweshiyon ko le jane ke liye ekajute hue. haalanki, purvi consas ke kisaan dare hue the ki laungorn se sthaneey maweshiyon ko maveshi fever ho ja saktaa he, saath hi fasalon ke ronde jane kaa bhi dar tha, is kaaran vahaan ke kisaanon ne samoohon kaa gathan kiya or chetavanee di ki unki jameen par paae jane vaale maveshi karmiyon ko peeta jayega yaa goli maar di jaayegi. isliye, 1866 kaa yah abhiyaan railmarg tak pahunch paane main vifal rahaa or maweshiyon ke jhund kam keemat par bech die gayi. haalanki, 1867 main, abilene, consas main railway stesan ke aasapaas farm country ke pashchim main ek maveshi potvahan suvidha kaa nirmaan kiya gaya or wo sthaan maveshi potvahan kaa ek centre ban gaya, us saal vahaan se 36,000 maweshiyon ko ladaa gaya. texas se abilene kaa wo rasta chisholm maarg se janaa jane laga, kyonki us raste ko jessi chisholm ne chihnit kiya tha. yah vartmaan oklahoma se hokar gujarata tha, jo us samay indian kshetra tha. haalanki, pashchim ke hollywood ke chitranon ke baavjood, desi americanyon ke saath apekshaakrit kam sangharsh the, jo aam taur par prati maveshi das saint ke path kar ke evaj main maweshiyon ke jhund ko jane kii anumati de diya karte the. baad main, vibhinn railway steshnon ke liye anya maarg banaae gayi, inamen doj city or vicita, consas ke maarg shaamil hai. 1877 tak, doj city, consas jaise maveshi-potvahan ke bade shaharon se 500,000 maweshiyon kaa potvahan kiya ja chukaa tha.
 maweshiyon ko haankane ke kaam main maweshiyon kii gati or unke vajan ke bich santulan banaae rakhana jaruri tha. jabaki maweshiyon ko ek din main 25 mile tak haanka ja saktaa he, lekin isse unka vajan itnaa adhik kam ho jaataa ki manzil tak pahunchakar unhen bech paanaa mushkil ho jaataa. aamtaur par unhen pratidin kam duuri tak le jaaya jaataa, unhen aaraam karne or dopaher tathaa raat main unhen charne diya jaataa. ausatan, ek pashu samooh pratidin 15 mile chalakar apana swasth vajan banaaye rakh saktaa he. is gati kaa matlab hua ki pashu farm se railway stesan tak pahunchane main do maheene tak lag jate the. udhaaharan ke liye, chisholm maarg, 1,000 mile lanbaa tha.
 ausatan, maweshiyon ke ek jhund ke ek haank abhiyaan main lagbhag 3,000 maveshi hua karte. maweshiyon ko jhund main le jane ke liye kam se kam 10 couboy kii jarurat padati or ek couboy ke liye teen ghode kii jarurat hoti thi. maweshiyon kii nigaraanee ke liye couboy 24 ghante paali main kaam kiya karte, din ke samay unhen sahi disha main le jane ke liye or raat main bhagdad or choron ko rokane ke liye. karmiyon main ek bawarchi bhi shaamil hota, jo ek bhojan vaahan chalaaya karata, aam taur par jis par ek bail juta hota or ek ashwapal hota jiske jimme remuda yaa atirikt ghodon kaa tabela hua karata. maweshiyon ke haank abhiyaan ke samay ashwapal aksar bahut hi jawan couboy hua karata yaa nimna saamaajik darje kaa, lekin bawarchi vishesh rup se karmi dal kaa ek bahut hi sammanit sadsy hota, jo na keval bhojan kaa prabharee hota, balki uske jimme chikitsa aapurti bhi hua karti or use praayogic aushadhi kaa kaamchalaoo jaankaree bhi hua karti thi.
 1880 ke dashak tak, maveshi udyog ke vistaar ke kaaran atirikt khule sthanon yaa hadon kii jarurat mahasoos kii jane lagi. tab anek pashu farm maalikon ne uttar-pashchim main vistaar kiya, jahaan tab bhi avyavhrut bade ghaas ke maidan maujuud the. texas ke maveshi uttar kii ore rocky mountain west or dakota main chale gayi. couboy ne thandi paristhitiyon ke anusaar khud ko dhaal liya or pashchim kii ore jane vaale udyog ne california se texas tak ke anek kshetreey paramparaon ke saath khud ko antarmishrit kar liya, aksar hi couboy ne prayek ke sabase upyogi tatwon ko khud main samahit kar liya.
 1880 ke dashak main shuru hue kantedaar taar maweshiyon ko ek kshetra vishesh main hi roke rakhte the, taaki maveshi us ilaake main seemaa se adhik char na len. texas or aasapaas ke kshetron main, jansankhya vruddhi ke kaaran pashu farm maalikon ko apni-apni bhoomi ko gher lene kii jarurat mahasoos hui. uttar main, khule kshetra main seemaa se adhik char lene ke kaaran jaade main maweshiyon ke liye chaare kaa abhaav ho jaataa, jisase bhukhmaree kaa saamana karnaa padataa, khaskar 1886-1887 ke bhayaavah jaade ke samay, jab uttar-pashchim main lakhon maweshiyon kii mrityu ho gayi, jisase maveshi udyog kaa patan ho gaya tha. 1890 ke dashak tak, uttari samtal kshetron main bhi kantedaar taar kii baad maanak ban gayi, adhikansh raashtra ko samavisht karne ke liye railmarg kaa vistaar hua or bade pashu farm kshetron ke kareeb maance packing plant banaae gayi, isse texas se consas ke railway steshnon tak maweshiyon ko laane kaa lanbaa haank abhiyaan anaavashyak ho gaya. is vajah se, khule kshetra kaa ant hua or maweshiyon ke bade haank abhiyaan samaapt hue. maweshiyon ke chote haank abhiyaan kam se kam 1940 ke dashak tak jari rahe, kyonki aadhunik maveshi trek banane se pehle tak, stockyard or packing plant tak parivahan ke liye farm maalikon ko sthaneey railway stesan tak pashuon ko haankna tab bhi jaruri tha. is bich, puure vikasshil pashchim main pashu farm badhate chale gayi, couboy kaa rojgaar bhi badhata gaya, haalanki unhen kam vetan hi miltaa rahaa, lekin ve kuch aabaad bhi hue.
 samay ke saath, america pashchim ke couboy ne apni nijee samskruti viksit kii, seemaant or victorian mulyon kaa ek mishran jisamen veerta ke avasheshon ko bhi banaae rakhaa gaya. pruthak paristhitiyon main is tarah ke khatarnaak kaam ne aatm-nirbharta or vyaktvaad kii parampara kaa bhi nirmaan kiya, vyaktigat eemaandaaree par khaasaa jor diya gaya, geet or kavita ke drushtaant prastut kiye gayi.
 haalanki, kuch purush seemaant rekha par chale gayi, kyonki ve purushon kii ore aakarshith hue. ek aise kshetra main jahaan purushon kii sankhya mahilaon se ullekhneey rup se adhik rahi, yahaan tak ki jin saamaajik kaaryakramon main donon ling ke log bhaag liya karte rahe, vahaan bhi, sirf purush hi bhare rahe or vahaan purushon ko nrity ke liye ek-duusare ko saathi bananaa padataa. yuva or avivaahit purushon ke bich samalangik sambandh sthapit hue, lekin couboy samskruti apane aapmen gahare rup se homophobic bani rahi. haalanki puraane pashchim main samalangikta-virdohi kaanoon aam the, lekin aksar unhen chaynaatmak rup se hi lagu kiya jaataa tha.
 19veen sadi ke ant main or 20veen sadi ke aarambh main wild west sho main vikaas ke saath aam logon ke dimag main karyrat couboy kii paramparaon ko or bhi ukera gaya, jisane couboy or desi americanyon ke jivan ko pradarshit kiya or uskaa romansikaran kiya. 1920 ke dashak se shuru karke aaj tak pashchimi filmon ne couboy jivan style ko lokapriy banaaya, lekin saath hi saath sakaaratmak or nakaaratmak donon hi rup main nirantar purvaagrah kaa bhi nirmaan kiya. kuch maamalon main, aksar couboy or hinsak hatyare ko ek-duusare ke saath jod diya jaataa he. duusari ore, couboy kii bhoomika nibhaane vaale kuch abhinaytaon ne sakaaratmak mulyon ko badhaava diya, jaise ki jin otry kaa "couboy code", jo sammanjanak vyavhaar, sammaan or deshbhakti ko protsaahit karata he.
 isi tarah, filmon main couboy ko america indian ke saath ladate hue aksar dikhaaya gaya he. lekin, vaastaviktaa yah he ki, couboy luuteron or maanav choron ke khilaaf hathiyaarband the or praayah maweshiyon kii chori ke prayaas yaa gaiyrkanoonee rup se unhen le jane waalon ko bhagane ke liye bandook kaa istemaal kiya karte rahe, jabaki lagbhag sabhi vaastavik sashastr sangharsh indian logon or you.es. sena ke ghudsavaar ikaiyon ke bich hue.
 darasal, pashu farm main ateet yaa vartmaan main kaam karne waalon ke paas koi or kaam karne ke liye bahut hi kam samay hua karata he, unhen to bas farm ko vyavasthit rakhane ke liye lagaataar kadi mehnat karte janaa padataa he.
 pashchim main mahilaon or khaskar pashu formon main kaam karne vaali mahilaon kaa itihaas purushon jaisi acchhee tarah se pralekhith nahin he. haalanki, neshnal cougirl museum or haul of fame jaise sansthaanon ne haal ke varshon main mahilaon ke yogadan ko ikattha karne or unke dastauez taiyaar karne main mahatvapurn prayaas kiya he.
 aise kuch record uplabdh hai jinamein old west ke maveshi margon tak mahilaon dwaara maweshiyon ko haankane ke ullekh milte hai. haalanki, mahilaon ne pashu formon main kaaphi kaam kiya he or kuch maamalon main to unhen chalaaya bhi he. isamen koi shaq nahin ki mahilaon, khaskar un chote formon ke maalikon kii patniyon or betiyon ne purushon ke kandhe se kandhaa milakar kaam kiya he, jo badi taadaad main bahari majdoor nahin rakh sakate the; or isiilie unhen ghudsawaari or sambandhit kaary puure karne kii kaabiliyat kii jarurat padati. pashchim kii mahilaon ke gaiyr-dastaveji yogadan ko bade str par kanoon main abhiswikruti pradaan kii gayi he; pashchimi rajyon ne mahilaon ko matdaan kaa adhikaar dene ke maamle main sanyukt rajya america kaa netritv kiya he, jisaki shuruyaat 1869 main vyoming se hui. evalin camerun jaise praarambhik photographeron ne 19veen ke ant or 20veen sadi ke prarambh main pashu formon main kaam karne vaali mahilaon or cougirl ke jivan ko pralekhith kiya.
 haalanki pratidin ke kaam ke liye avyaavhaarik hone ke baavjood bagal kii lathi ek aisa saadhan tha jisase "sammanjanak" saarvajanik sthanon main bhi mahilaayein paidal jane yaa taange tak simit rahane kii bajaay ghode kii sawari ke kaabil ho paati thim. grih yuddh ke baad, charles gudnit ne paaramparik angreji bagal kii lathi main sudhaar laakar ek paashchaatya-style dizin kaa nirmaan kiya. mexico ke paaramparik charras ne ek sadrush parampara ko banaae rakhaa or aaj seemaa ke donon ore charreada pradarshaniyon main sidsadel kii sawari ke khel hua karte hai.
 wild west sho ke aagaman ke baad hi "cougirls" ke kartab saamne aaye. ye vayask mahilaayein kushal kalakaar thim, ghudsawaari kaa pradarshan, visheshgy nishanebaaji or rassi ke kartab se duniya bhar ke darshakon kaa manoranjan karatin. ani okale jaisi mahilaayein gharelu naam ban gayin. 1900 tak, taangen failaakar sawari ke liye alag vibhajit skirt lokapriy ban gaya or purushon ke saath pratispardhaa karne ke liye victorian kaal ke darshakon kii ninda ke bina hi mahilaon ko purushon ke kapadey yaa, isse bhi bura, patalun pahanane kii anumati mil gayi. 20veen sadi ke prarambh ke samay se filmon main, cougirl ne lokapriy samskruti main apni bhoomika kaa vistaar kiya or film dizinron ne paashchaatya jeenon par sawari ke upyukt aakarshak kapadon kaa vikaas kiya.
 manoranjan udyog se alag swatantra rup se, rodio kaa vikaas ek anya prakaar kii cougirl ko saamne laayaa - rodio cougirl. praarambhik wild west sho or rodio main mahilaayein sabhi pratispardhaa main bhaag lene lageen, kabhi anya mahilaon ke viruddh to kabhi purushon ke saath. pheni speri steel jaisi cougirl ne purushon kii hi tarah "raf stock" main sawari kii or vaisaa hi jokhim uthaaya or calgary stamped tathaa cheyenne frontier days jaisi badi rodio pratispedhaon main bhi hissaa liya.
 1925 ke baad mahilaon ke liye rodio pratiyogita badal gayi, jab purvi promotaron ne medisson squaire garden jaisi jagahon main indoor rodio rakhni shuru kii. mahilaon ko aamtaur par purushon kii pratispardhaa se nikaal diya gaya or mahilaon kii kai pratispardhaaen kam kar di gayin. aaj ke rodio main, sirf team roping main purush or mahila samaan rup se ek saath pratispardhaa kar sakate hai, haalanki mahilaayein takneekee rup se anya khuli spardhaon main bhi pravesh kar sakati hai. sakal-mahila rodio bhi hai, jahaan mahilaayein bronk sawari, sand kii sawari or anya sabhi paaramparik rodio pratispardhaa main bhaag le sakati hai. haalanki, khule rodio main, cougirl mukhya rup se bairal racing jaisi nishchit samay vaali pratispardhaa main hissaa le sakati hai, jabaki adhikansh peshewar rodio main purush pratispardhaon kii tulana main mahila pratispardhaa kam hi rakhi jaati hai.
 ucch vidhyalay rodio or o-moke-si pratiyogita main ladke or ladkiyaan aapas main pratispardhaa ke liye kahin adhik udyat hote hai, jahaan bairal racing jaisi "mahilaon" kii spardhaa main bhi ladakon ko bhaag lete dekha ja saktaa he. rodio vishwa ke baahar, olympic sahit lagbhag sabhi ghudsawaari pratispardhaon main purushon ke samaan hi bhaag liya karti hai or kating, lagaam lagane or dhairya jaisi paashchaatya sawari main bhi hissaa le sakati hai.
 aaj kii karyrat cougirl aam taur par purushon jaise kapadey, aujaar or saamagree kaa istemaal karti hai, haalanki pratiyogita main zaraa chittkarshak dikhne ke liye aam taur par alag rang or dizin pasand karti hai. sidsadel sirf pradarshaniyon or ek simit sankhya ke vishesh ghudsawaari sho kakshaon main dekhi jaati hai. cougirl genes, kasi hui kameez, juute, topi pahenti he or jarurat hone par chaps or dastaane bhi pahana karti hai. yadi farm main kaam kar rahi hon to ve couboy kii hi tarah vahi ubaau kaam kiya karti hai or unka pahanava sthiti ke anuroop hua karata he.
 bhugol, jalwaayu or saanskrithik paramparaon ke kaaran maweshiyon ke sambhaal-sanchaalan paddatiyon or upkarnon ke vikaas main sanyukt rajya america ke ek bhaag se duusare bhaag main bhinnata dekhi jaati he. aadhunik duniya main, do pramukh couboy paramparaon ke avshesh paae jate hai, jinhen aaj "texas" parampara or "spanish", "vakuero" yaa "california" parampara ke naam se janaa jaataa he. hawaii or florida main bhi kam prasiddh lekin utani hi vishisht paramparaayen viksit huin. aaj, vibhinn kshetreey couboy paramparaon kaa kuch had tak vilay ho chukaa he, haalanki upkaran or sawari style main kuch kshetreey antar ab bhi bache hue hai. or, kuch logon ne jaan-boojhkar adhik samay lene vaali lekin atyadhik kushal takneekon se las vishuddh vakuero yaa "bakaaru" parampara ko sangrakshit karne kaa rasta chunaa he. prakritik ghudsawaari kala kii lokapriy "horse visperor"  style multah california or uttar-pashchim rajyon ke peshewaron dwaara viksit kii gayi thi, jo upkaran ke saath california ke vakuero ke drushtikon or darshan tathaa texas couboy kii bahya drushti kaa spasht sangyojan he.
 1800 ke dashak ke prarambh main, spanish samraat or baad main, swatantra mexico ne sanyukt rajya america se aaye adhivaasiyon jaise texas ke gaiyr-nagarikon ke liye ampreserio  pradaan karne kii peshkash kii. 1821 main, stephen epa. austin or unke east coast ke saathi spanish bolne vaale pehle anglo-sexon samudaay ke log hue. 1836 main texas kii swatantraaa ke baad, texas ke ampreserio pashupaalan kshetron main or bhi adhik america aane lage. yahaan ke adhivaasee mexican vakuero samskruti se bahut adhik prabhavit the, vahaan ke sthaneey logon kii shabdaawalee or pahanava ko apana liya tha, lekin saath hi maweshiyon ko sanbhaalane kii kuch paramparaon tathaa purvi sanyukt rajya america or great britain kii samskruti ko bhi bachaae rakhaa. texas ke couboy aamtaur par kunvaare hua karte, jinhen mausam ke hisaab se vibhinn kaamon ke liye niyukt kiya jaataa.
 america grih yuddh ke baad, vakuero samskruti purab or pashchim kii ore bikhar gayi, gaaypalan kii purvi sanyukt rajya america kii paramparaon ke saath jiskaa sangyojan hua jo adhivaasiyon ke pashchim kii ore jane se viksit hua. consas or nebraska ke railmargon tak pahunchane ke liye maweshiyon ke maarg banaae jane se texas se anya prabhaav viksit hue, iske alaava great plance or rocky mountens front, continental divide ke purab main pashupaalan ke avasaron main vistaar hua.
 is prakaar, pashchimi texas ke bhugol or jalwaayu ke saath anukulan kii jarurat ke liye or bazaar le jane ke liye maweshiyon ke lambey haank-abhiyaanon kii jarurat ke alaava texas kii couboy parampara saanskrithik prabhaavon ke sangyojan se paidaa hui.
 spanish upniveshi avadhi ke dauraan, yuva va aprashikshit ghodon ke saath kaam karne vaale vakuero arthath spanish yaa mexican couboy 1700 ke dashak main aaye or california tathaa simavarti kshetron main phalne-phoolne lage. sanyukt rajya america ke adhivaasiyon ne mexican-america yuddh ke baad hi california main pravesh kiya or isse pehle ke adhivaasee pashu formon kii bajaay adhikanshtah khadaanon main kaam kiya karte the, unhone pashupaalan kaa kaam mukhya rup se spanish or mexican ke jimme chhod rakhaa tha jinhone california main hi rahanaa pasand kiya tha. texas couboy kii tulana main california ke vakuero yaa bakaaru ucch kushal kaamgaar maane jate the, jo aam taur par usee farm main rahaa karte the jahaan unka janm hua yaa jahaan ve bade hue hon or vahin ve apana parivaar banaaya karte the. iske alaava, california kaa bhugol or jalwaayu naatakeey rup se texas se alag thi, kam khule kshetron main adhik gahan charaai hua karti; saath hi california ke maveshi mukhya rup se sthaneey str par hi bik jaaya karte the so unhen railmargon tak saikadon mile kii yatra nahin kerani padati thi. is prakaar, texas kii tulana main majboot pratyaksh spanish prabhaav vaale california or prashanth mahasagariya uttar-pashchim main ghode or pashuon ke sambhaal-sanchaalan kii samskruti bani rahi.

 19veen or 20veen sadi ke prarambh ke florida ke "couhunter" yaa "craikar couboy" texas or california paramparaon se bhinn hua karte the. florida ke couboy maveshi ko pakadaney yaa unhen ikattha karne ke liye kamand kaa upyog nahin kiya karte the. unke praathmik upkaran bulavips or kute the. chunki florida ke couhunter ko kamand ke lungar ke liye lathi ke prakshipt bhaag kii aavashyaktaa nahin padaa karti thi, isliye anek log paashchaatya jin kaa upyog nahin kiya karte the, uske bajaay ve mcclalan jin kaa upyog kiya karte. kuch log saanp se suraksha ke liye ghutane se upar tak ke juute pahana karte, jabaki anya log brogans pahante. ve aamtaur par saste oon yaa khar kii topi pahana karte or barish se bachane ke liye poncho kaa istemaal kiya karte.

 hawaiyan couboy, paniolo bhi california or mexico ke vakuero ke ek pratyaksh vanshaj hai. hawaiyan shabd vyutpatti vigyaan ke visheshgyon kaa vishwaas he ki aspanol kaa hawaiyani uccharan he "paniolo" . kii dhwani nahin he or sabhi shabdaansh or shabd kaa ant swar se hi hona hota he.) uttari america kii mukhya bhoomi ke couboy kii tarah paniolo ne bhi apane kaushal mexican vakuero se siikhaa.

 couboy ke liye yatayaat kaa paaramparik saadhan, yahaan tak ki aadhunik samay main bhi, ghode kii peeth par sawari he. ghode shail pradesh kii bhi yatra kar sakate he jo ki vaahan nahin kar sakate. ghode, gadhon or khachcharon ke saath dhulaai ke kaam aate hai. pashu farm main sabase mahatvapurn ghoda vah hota he jo rosemara ke kaam kiya karata he or jo vibhinn tarah ke kaam kar saktaa he; jhund se maweshiyon ko alag karne yaa fanda daal kar pakadney jaise vishesh tarah ke kaam ke liye aise ghodon ko prashikshit kiya jaataa he, pashu formon main yah bahut hi virala hota he. kyonki pashu ke saath kaam karte hue savaar ko apana ek haath khaali rakhana aksar jaruri hota he, ghode kii gardan par lagaam honi chahie or kau sens - sahaj rup se yah jaananaa jaruri he ki pashuon ke bare main purvanumaan kaise lagaaya jaae or pratikriya kaise jataayi jaae.
 ek achche stock ghode kaa ek chota paksh yah he, ki saamaanya taur par gardan ke neeche peeth kaa hissaa 15.2 haath lanbaa or 1000 pound ke bheetar vajan, choti-si peeth ke saath pusht paanv, majboot puttha; khaastaur par peeche pairon kaa puttha hota he. rassi kaa fanda daal bachade ko pakadaney kaa kaam aanevaale ghode ko kisi baari vayask gaay, bail yaa bachade ko rassi main pakad rahane ke liye apekshaakrit bada or adhik vajandaar hone kii jarurat ho sakati he, jabaki ek chote, furtile ghode kii jarurat pashuon ke jhund se kisi ek ko alag karne yaa fanda daalkar bachade ko pakadaney jaisi gatividhiyon ke liye hoti he. ghode ke liye budhimaan, dabaav ke samay shant hona jaruri he or saath main kuch had tak "gaay ko samajhane kii samaj" - maveshi ke vyavhaar or uski gatividhi ko samajhane kii kshamta hona jaruri he.
 ghode kii bahut sari nasl ko acha stock ghoda banaaya jaataa he, lekin aaj north america main sabase aam american quarter horse he, yah ek ghode kii nasl he jo praathmik rup se texas main mastang ke acchhee nasl ke ghode or anya iberian ghode ke vanshaj ko arabi ghode ke prabhaav ke saath varna sankar prajnan se viksit kiya gaya; or purvi tat main morgan jaisaa ghoda or chikso or virginia quarter-miller jaise vilupt nasl ko viksit kiya gaya.

 peshewar rodio ke aagaman ne couboy ko, bahut saare athleton kii tarah darshakon ke saamne apane kaushal kaa pradarshan karke roji arjeet karne kaa avasar diya. rodio ne aise bahut saare kaamkaaji coubiyon ko rrodogaar muhaiyaa karaayaa, jinhen maweshiyon kaa prabandhan karne kii jarurat thi. bahut saare rodio couboy shramik couboy bhi hai or inamen se zyaadaatar ke paas shramik couboy kaa anubhav he.
 rodio couboy kaa pahanava shahari shramik coubiyon se bahut alag nahin he. bachade yaa sand ke sing se chot lagane se bachane ke liye couboy ke shart ke batan kii jagah istemaal kii janevaali cheez use chutki main tadaak se shart ko utaar dene kii suvidha detii he. style ko aksar puraane film udyog se rodio ke liye roopantarit kar liya jaataa tha. kuch rodio pratiyogi, vishesh rup se mahilaayein paaramparik or khel ke pradarshan ke donon hi main apane poshaak main salama-sitare, rang, chaandi or lambey jhaalar jodne ko sahamat hoti hai. junglee ghode ko jin pehanaanaa yaa sand kii sawari jaise "raf stoke" event main aadhunik savaar kevalor west yaa gardan ke patte jaise kuch suraksha upkarnon kaa istemaal kar sakate hai, lekin jahaan tak suraksha helmet ke istemaal kii baat he to chot lagane ke lagaataar khatre ke baavjood couboy topi ke badale ise sweekaara janaa abhi baaki he.
 "couboy" shabd kaa prayog america kranti ke dauraan un america senaniyon ke liye kiya gaya tha jinhone aazaadee ke liye andolan kaa virodh kiya tha. vafadari ke nimitt aparaadh kaary main lipt rahane ke liye jane janevaale clodius smith ko upniveshon se belon, gaayon or ghodon ko churaane kii pravriti or unhen angrejon ko de dene ke kaaran "couboy of da rampoj" kaha jaataa tha. kuch hi samay tak, westchester county main bahut saare chhapamar jattha sakriy the, jo british or america sena ke bich kii line ko chihnit karne kaa kaam karte the. ye dal sthaneey krishi shramik se bane the, jo suraksha ke liye ghaat lagaate or donon ore chapa maarthe the. vahaan do alag dal the: "skinurs" jo aazaadee-samarthakon kii ore se ladate; or "couboy" angrejon kaa samarthan karte.

 haalanki sanyukt rajya america or canada ke pashchimi ke saath hi saath australia main bhi mehmaan pashu farm chalaanevaale logon ko ghode kii sawari karne kii peshkash karte hai or pashchimi jivan kaa swaad lete hai - haalanki yah aramit se bahut dur hota he. kuch pashu farm chalaanevaale maveshi daudane yaa wagon train se judakar asli couboy kaa kaam main bhaag lete hue chhuttiyaan manaane kaa prastaav bhi dete he. billy crystal abhinit 1991 kii film city slikers se is prakaar kii chuttee lokapriy hui thi.

 pashchim ke lambey itihaas kii lokapriy samskruti main unhen couboy yaa cougirl kehane kaa chalan he, jinka pahanava pashchimi hota, phir chaahe ve ghode par savaar hon yaa na hon. yah un maamalon main vishesh rup se sahi hota he jab yah manoranjan karne ke kaam se jude logon par lagu hota he or un par jo saarvajanik kshetra main hai, apane vyaktitv ke hisse ke rup main pashchimi pahanava pahante hai.
 haalanki, vishesh rup se pashchim main, bahut saare log pashchimi pahanava; khaastaur par couboy boot yaa topi pahante hai, jabaki zyaadaatar maamalon main ve duusare kism kaa kaam karte hai, ve vakil yaa banker ke peshey main hote hai or anya koi safedposh peshewar hote hai. iske vipareet, kuch log jo pashu farming se jude hote hai, ve apane aapako tab tak couboy yaa cougirl nahin bataate hai jab tak ki ve yah mahasoos na karen ki unka praathmik kaam pashuon se judaa hua he yaa phir ve kisi ghudsavaar pratispardhaa main bhaag le rahe hon.
 vaastavik coubiyon main un vyaktiyon ke liye vyangypurna abhivyakti hoti he jinhone asli samskruti ko samajhe bagaiyr couboy ke swaabhaavik vyavhaar ko fishan ke dhab ke rup main apana liya he. udhaaharan ke liye, ek "dragstore couboy" kaa matlab aise kisi vyakti se he jo poshaak to pahentaa he, lekin darasal, kisi bhi cheez kii sawari nahin kar saktaa, sivaay dragstore ke soda fountain stool ke - yaa, aadhunik jamaane main baar stool ke. kahawat he, "ol hat and no catel", jiskaa prayog aise logon ke liye hota he jo vaastavik uplabdhiyon se dur aatmshlagha main vibhor hote hai. shabd "dud" ) aise logon kii ore ishaara he jo vyakti couboy samskruti se anjaan he, khaastaur par unke liye jo inaki nakal karne kii koshish karte hai.

 "couboy" shabd kaa prayog nakaaratmak arth main kiya jaataa he. mul rup isaki vyutpatti cansus ke bumtoun ke kuch couboy ke aacharan se hui he, maweshiyon ko dur-dur tak charaane ke liye le aane ke ant main badi taadaad main charavaahon main, khaastaur par tarun ladakon main iske aprihaary prabhaav ke kaaran charavaahon main hinsa or junglee aacharan viksit ho jaati he or samudaayon main pahunch jane par chhiiput paise milane par inamen se bahut saare sharaab pine or jua khilne lag jate hai.

"couboy" kaa prayog kabhi-kabhi apmanjanak arth main aise logon ke liye bhi kiya jaataa he, jo sambhavit khataron ke prati laaparvaah hote hai yaa upeksha karte hai, yaa gaiyr jimmedaar hai yaa jo samvedansheel yaa khatarnaak kaam karne main laaparvaahee baratate hai. time patrika ne raashtrapati jorge dablyoo. bush kii videsh niti ko "couboy kootneeti" naam diya tha, or press ne, khaastaur par europe main bush ko "couboy" kaha tha.
