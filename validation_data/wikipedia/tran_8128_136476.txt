 krodh par niyantran se abhipraay un manochikitsakeey vidhiyon se he jinka upyog karke evam jinke abhyaas se krodhi swabhaav ke vyakti labhanvit ho sakate hai. krodh ko niyantran main rakhane se bahut se laabh hote hai. iske liye kuch prasiddh vidhiyaan hai - 
 hindu dharm main 'akrodh' ko dharm ke das lakshanon main sammilit kiya gaya he. gita main kaha gaya he-
