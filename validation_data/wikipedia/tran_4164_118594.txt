 san 1877-78 kaa rus-turki yuddh, osmani samrajya tathaa roosi samrajya ke netritv main purvi orthodox samooh ke bich ladaa gaya. purvi orthodox samooh main bulgaria, romania, srbija or montinigro the. is yuddh ne 19veen shatabdi main balcon rashtrawaad ke udai main baari bhoomika nibhaai. 
 is yuddh ke parinamswaroop rus ko kakeshas ke kai pradeshon par apana daawa thokane main safalataa mili. romania, srbija, montinigro ne osmani samrajya se apane swatantra hone kii ghoshnaa kar di. lagbhag paanch shatabdi tak osmani samrajya ke adheen rahaa bulgaria bhi swatantra ho gaya. berlin congress ne yah bhi vyavastha di ki austria-hungary bosnia or harajegovina par adhikaar kar len tathaa britain cyprus par apana adhikaar kar le.
