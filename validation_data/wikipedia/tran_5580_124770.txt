 chitralekha 1964 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he jo bhagwati charan verma dwaara rachit hindi upanyaas ‘chitralekha’ par aadhaarit he. film ke mukhya kalakaar hai ashok kumaar, minaa kumari, pradeep kumaar or mahmud. film ke nirdeshak he kedaar sharma jinhone isi naam se 1941 main bhi ek film banaai thi. film rochak he parantu upanyaas kii tulana main halaki padati he. jahaan lekhak ne vibhinn vichaardhaaraon main santulan banaaye rakhaa film main ''yoga' kii apeksha 'bhoga' ke paksh main asantulan dekhne ko miltaa he. sahir ludhiaanvi ke geet or roshan kaa sangeet karn priy hai. 'sansar se bhaage phirte ho' or 'man re tu kaahe na dheer dhare' aaj bhi lokapriy hai.
