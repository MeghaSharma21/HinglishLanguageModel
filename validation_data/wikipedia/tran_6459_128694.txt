 eliyas kashmiri, jise moulana eliyas kashmiri or mohammad eliyas kashmiri ), muslim jehadi sanghathan al kaayda kaa ek varisht sadsy tha. iske soviet afagaan yuddh, kashmir vivaad evam bharat, sanyukt rajya evam pakistan ke viruddh hamalon main bhi sakriy yogadan rahe hai. agast 2010 main sanyukt rajya america evam sanyukt raashtra ne ise ek aatankwaadi ghoshit kiya tha. en.bi.si news samachar agency ke anusaar kashmiri, osama bin laden kii maut ke baad al-kaayda main uskaa sambhavit uttaradhikari tha.
