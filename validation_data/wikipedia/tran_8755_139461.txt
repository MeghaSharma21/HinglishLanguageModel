 peter lio nidovich kapiza ; 8 july 1894 - 8 april 1984)) rus ke bhautikavid the. unhe 1978 main bhautikee ke nobel puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. 
 inkaa janm 9 july san 1894 ko cronstadt main hua. aapane praarambhik shiksha petrograd main praapt kii. paduparant aap cambridge main lard radarfard ke vidhyaarthee rahe or parmaanu vighatan anusandhan ke kshetra main atyant prabal chumbakeey kshetra utpann karne kii takneekee kriyapranali ke vikaas main vishesh dakshata praapt kii. san 1924 main aapaki niyukti cavendish prayogshaala main chumbakeey anusandhan ke sahaayak nirdeshak ke rup main hui or 1932 i. tak is pad par kaary karte rahe. san 1930 se 1935 tak aap royal socity ke sadsy chune gaye or 1942 main aapako farrede padak pradaan kiya gaya. iske atirikt bhautikee kaa stallin puraskaar aapako san 1941 main or phir 1943 main mila. san 1943 or 1944 main aap order ov lenin oopaadhi se bhi vibhooshit kiye gaye.
 san 1934 main aap jab chuttee par swadesh gaye to soviet sarkaar ne aapako pun: desh se baahar jane kii anumati nahin di. kapiza ke liye masco main cavendish prayogshaala ke takkar kii prayogshaala banaai gai taaki kapiza sucharu rup se apana anusandhan kaary chalaa saken. falswaroop kapiza kuch hi samay uparaant masco kii bhautikeey samasya sanstha ke nirdeshak niyukt kar die gaye.
 aapkaa mukhya kaary 'chumbakatv' tathaa cryogenics se sambandh rakhata he. aapane 30 testla tak kaa chumbakeey kshetra utpann karne main safalataa praapt kii or hydrogen tathaa helium ke draveekaran ke plant kii bhi safal dizine di he.
