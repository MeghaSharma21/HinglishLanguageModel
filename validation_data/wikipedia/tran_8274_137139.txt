 tamil-brahmi yaa tamili, brahmi lipi kaa ek parivartit rup he jo tamil bhashaa ke lekhan ke liye prayukt hota tha. tamil bhashaa ke pracheenatam lekh isi lipi main milte hai. yah lipi cher rajvansh tathaa pandy rajvansh ke kaal main supratishthit ho gayi thi. in rajwanshon kaa shashan un kshetron main tha jo vartmaan samay main tamilnadu, andhra pradesh, kerala, or shri lanka kehalaate hai.
