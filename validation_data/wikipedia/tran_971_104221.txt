 zayd hamid ek pakistani tivi host or rijnithik tikaakar hai. inhone kai baar pakistan ko asthir karne main bhartiya, israeli or america agenton dwaara banaai sajeesh ko jimmevaar kaha he. aarthik aatankwaad ke mudde par unka bayaan pakistan main pasand kiya gaya. inhone karanchi ke enidi vishvavidhyalay se computer science kii padhai kii he. 
 wo bhartiya pradhanmantri narendra modi ke suraksha salaahkaar ajit dobhaal ke aalochak rahe hai. apane bayanon main wo prasiddh shaayar or 1920 ke dashak main pakistan aandolan ke pranetaon main se ek allamaa iqbaal ke sheron kaa zikr aksar karte hai. 
 unako pakistan main har kisi chiz par "pakistan ke kilaaf" or "islam se kilaaf shadyantr" kii aawaaj oochhaalane ke kaaran aalochana jhelni padi he. iske alaave july 2015 main inako saudi arab ne vahaan kii sarkaar kii aalochana ke kaaran 8 saal kaarvaas or 1100 kodo kii sazaa bhi sunai thi.
