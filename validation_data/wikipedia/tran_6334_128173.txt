 maao se-tung yaa maao xedong chini krantikari, rajnatik vichaarak or samyavaadee dal ke netaa the jinke netritv main china kii kranti safal hui. unhone janavaadee ganatantr china kii sthapana se mrityu paryant tak china kaa netritv kiya. marxvaadi-leninvaadee vichaardhaara ko sainik ranneeti main jodakar unhone jis siddhanth ko janm diya use maowad naam se janaa jaataa he.
 vartmaan main kai log maao ko ek vivaadaaspad vyakti maanate hai parantu china main ve rajkeey rup main mahaan krantikari, rajnatik ranneetikaar, sainik purodha evam deshrakshak maane jate hai. chiniyon ke anusaar maao ne apni niti or kaaryakramon ke madhyam se aarthik, takneekee evam saanskrithik vikaas ke saath sansar vishwa main pramukh shakti ke rup main laa khada karne main mukhya bhoomika nibhaai. ve kavi, darshanik, doordarshee mahaan prashaasak ke rup main gine jate hai.
 iske vipareet, maao ke 'great lip forward' or 'saanskrithik kranti' naamak saamaajik tathaa raajnneetik kaaryakramon ke kaaran gambhir akaal kii srujana hone ke saath chini samaaj, arthvyavastha tathaa samskruti ko thes pahunchane kii bhi baaten kii jaati hai, jiske kaaran sansar main san 1949 se 1975 tak karodon logon kii vyapak mrityu hui bataai jaati hai. maao sansar ke sabase prabhaavshaalee vyaktiyon main gine jate hai. time patrika ke anusaar 20veen sadi ke 100 sabase prabhaavshaalee vyaktiyon main maao aate hai.
 maao kaa janm 26 disambar 1893 main hunaan praant ke shaaoshan qasbe main hua. unke pita ek garib kisaan the jo aage chalakar ek dhani krishak or gehun ke vyapaaree ban gaye. 8 saal kii umr main maao ne apane gaanv kii praarambhik paathshaala main padhanaa shuru kiya lekin 13 kii aayu main apane parivaar ke khet par kaam karne ke liye padhanaa chhod diya. baad main kheti chhodkar ve hunaan praant kii rajdhani changasha main madhyamik vidhyalay main padhane gaye. jinhai krati ke samay maao ne hunaan ke sthaneey rejiment main bhartee hokar krantikariyon kii taraf se ladaai main bhaag liya. ching rajvansh ke sattachyut hone par ve sena chhodkar punah vidhyalay gaye. san 1918 main snaatak banane ke bad san 1919 ko chaar may aandolan ke liye apane shikshak evam bhavishya ke sasur praadhyaapak yaan chyaan ji ke saath beijing kii yatra par gaye. praadhyaapak yaan chyaan ji peking vishvavidhyalay main mahatvapurn pad par the or unki sifarish par maao ne sahaayak pustakalayadhyaksh pad par rahakar kaam kiya. maao ne anshkalik chaatr ke rup main panjikrut hokar kuch vyakhaanon or vidwaanon ke seminaron main bhi bhaag liye. shanghai main rahete ve samyavaadee siddhanth main adhyayan main lage.
