 vigyanbhikshu bharat ke darshanik the. ve ek prakaar se sankhya ke antim acharya hai. inhone hi sankhyamat main ishwarwad kaa samavesh kiya tha. inhone teen darshanon par bhashya-granth likhe hai- sankhyapravachanbhashya ; yogavarthik, vignanamritabhashya . 
 vigyanbhikshu puure auchity ke saath sankhyasutra par apana bhashya prastut karte hue kehete hai ki sankhya 'kalbhakshit' ho gaya tha. is tantrapranali ko pun: punerjeevit karne ke liye hi ve prayatnsheel rahe hai. lupt hue sankhya ke swarup nirmaan kii disha main agrasar hote hue ve sadaiv upanishad or puraanon ke yug ke ananthar viyukt hone vaale sankhya or vedant main saamanjasy sthapit karneke prati prayatnsheel the. vigyanbhikshu veydant ke prati bhi aasthashil the. 'vignanamrita' naam se brahmasutra par unka bhashya is tathy ko pramanit karata he. is baat ko sweekaar karne ke anek kaaran vidyaman hai jinke aadhaar par bataaya ja saktaa he ki sankhya darshan dhire-dhire vedant darshan main vilupt hota prathit hota he. yahaan tak ki vigyanbhikshu ne vedantik mithyaa pratiti suchak maaya ko sankhya kii yatharth pratithisuchak prakruti ko ek samaan pratipaadit kiye jane kaa praytn kiya tha. yahi kaaran he ki parvarti buddha tarkikon ke aavirbhaav ke falswarup mimasaa karne ke prayaas main bhale hi anek tatvamanishi avibhoot hue par sankhyamatwaad kaa kisi bhi drushtikon se gambhir adhyayan prastut nahin kiya gaya. vastut: ek aise sanshodhan ke baad jisane sankhyadarshan kaa vedant kii paridhi main pahunchaa diya tha, sankhya kaa ek swatantra sampradaay ke rup main astitv samaapt ho chukaa tha.
 sankhya darshan ke sambandh main mulat: in do paraspar virdohi matagrahon ke niraakaran ke liye shankaracharya kaa brahmasutra hamaari paryaapt sahaayta kar saktaa he. lakshya karne kii baat he ki brahmasutra ne sankhyadarshan ke khandan par atyadhik bal diya tha kyonki brahramasutrakaar sankhya ko apana ghor pratidwandvi sweekaar karata tha or tha bhi, kyonki sankhya ke pradhanvaad athwa karanwad main jahaan ek mul-bhautik tatv ko vishwa kaa aadyakaaran bataaya gaya tha, advait vedant ke mul chetan karanwad ke nitant vipareet khadaa tha. yahi kaaran tha ki brahmasutra ke pratham chaar sutron main brahm tathaa vedant grantho ke swarup ke sambandh main kuch moulik mahatv kii baton kaa spashtikaran karte hi brahm sutarakaar tatkaal agle saat sutron main yah spasht karne ke liye vyagr ho uthte hai ki brahm ek chetan tatv he, jiskaa pruthakkaran sankhya darshan ke 'pradhan'se kiya janaa chahie jo achetan athwa bhautik hone ke kaaran vishwa kaa mul nahin ho saktaa.
