 sangrakshit kshetra yaa rakshit kshetra kisi aise kshetra ko kehete hai jisaki uske prakritik, paryaavarniya yaa saanskrithik mahatv ke kaaran parivartan yaa haani se raksha kii ja rahi ho. rakshit kshetra bhinn prakaaron ke hote hai or unhen alag-alag staron kaa sangrakshan diya jaataa he. kuch samudri kshetra bhi aksar sangrakshit hua karte hai. oktober 2010 main vishwa bhar main 1,61,000 se adhik rakshit kshetra the jinamein duniya kii puri bhoomi kaa 10-15% tha. iske vipareet samudr kaa keval 1.17% rakshit kshetron main sammilit tha jisapar lagbhag 6,800 samudri rakshit kshetra vistrut the.
 antarrashtriya prakruti sangrakshan sangh ke anusaar rakshit kshetra 'ek spasht rup se paribhaashit bhugolic kshetra hota he, jo prakruti or us se sambandhit paryaavarniya sevaaon or saanskrithik manyataon ke dirghakaalin sangrakshan ke liye maanya, samarpit or vyavasthit ho'. aamtaur par in kshetron main maanav-vyavsaayon or prakritik sadhano ke upyog par seemayen lagi hoti hai yaa purna pabandi hoti he.
