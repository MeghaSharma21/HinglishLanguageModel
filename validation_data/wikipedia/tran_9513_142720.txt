 bhutto parivaar sindh praant main sthit pakistan kaa ek pramukh raajnneetik parivaar he bhuto ne pakistani raajnneeti or sarkaar main ek pramukh bhoomika nibhaai he. parivaar ne 1 9 67 main apni sthapana ke baad se pakistan pipuls party kaa netritv kiya he. sindh praant main sthit jaateey samrajya ke musalmaan rajputon kaa ek parivaar, bhuto do se zyada shatakon ke liye is kshetra main bas gaye hai. ve 18 veen shatabdi ke shuruaati daur main shett khan bhutto ke adheen raajapootaana se sindh chale gaye the.
