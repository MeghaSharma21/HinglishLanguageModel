 rucha hasabnis ek bhartiya film abhinetri hai. rucha dharawahik "saath nibhaana saathiya" kii raashi naamak mukhya bhoomika nibhaan raheen thi. 
 mumbai se vaanijya snaatak hone ke baad, rucha ko abhinay kaa asinament marathi dharawahik "chaar chaughee" se se mila. vartmaan main rucha "raashi" naamak mukhya kirdaar nibhaa raheen hai, is kirdaar ke liye unhen 2011 main star plus kii taraf se sarvshereshth abhinetri ke puraskaar se nawaaza gaya he.
