 pandit suryanarayan vyas hindi ke vyangyakaar, patrkaar, swatantraaa senaanee evam jyotirvid the. unhen sahitya evam shiksha ke kshetra main san 1958 main padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. pan vyas ne 1967 main angreji ko anant kaal tak jari rakhane ke vidheyak ke virodh main apana padmabhushan lautaa diya tha.
 ve bahuaiami pratibha ke dhani the - ve itihaaskaar, puraatatvavetta, krantikari, "vikram" patr ke sampadak, sansmaran lekhak, nibandhkaar, vyangyakaar, kavi; vikram vishvavidhyalay, vikram kirti mandir, sindhiya shodh pratishthan or kalidas parishad ke sansthaapak, akhil bhartiya kalidas samaaroh ke janak tathaa jyotish evam khagol ke apane yug ke sarvocch vidwaan the.
 ve maharishi sandipani kii parampara ke vaahak the. khagol or jyotish ke apane samay ke is asaadhaaran vyaktitv kaa sammaan lokmaanya tilak evam pan madanamohan malviya bhi karte the. pan narayanji ke desh or videsh main lagbhag saat hajaar se adhik shishy faile hue the jinhen ve vastra, bhojan or awaas dekar nickshulk vidya adhyayan karavaate the. anek itihaaskaaron ne yah bhi khoj nikaala he ki pan vyas ke us gurukul main swatantrata sangram ke anek krantikari vesh badalakar rahete the.
 pandit suryanarayan vyas kaa janm ujjayini ke singhpuri mohalle main 2 march 1902 ko pandit narayanji vyas ke ghar main hua tha. 
 pandit vyas hindi ke atirikt gujarati, marathi, bangala, sanskrit ke bhi marmajna the. ve ek shreshth vyangyakaar, kavi, nibandhkaar, itihaaskaar the. akele vikram masik main ‘vyas uwach’ evam ‘bindu-bindu’ vichaar sheershak se likhe unke sampadakeey kii sankhya 2500 se upar he.
 1937 main ve sara europe ghume or yatra sahitya par unki kriti ‘sagar pravaas’ mile kaa pathar maani jaati he.
 pan. suryanarayan vyas atharah baras yaa usase bhi kam aayu main rachana karne lage the. siddhanath madhav agarkar kaa saahchary unhen kishoravastha main hi mil gaya tha, lokmaanya tilak kii jeevani kaa anuwaad unhone agarkarji ke saath kiya. siddhanath madhav agarkar kaa panditji ko antarang saahchary mila or shaayad isi vajah se ve patrakarita kii ore pravrutt hue.
 pan. suryanarayan vyas atyant prasanman, hansmukh or vinodpriyat vyakti the. hindi sahitya main vyangy vidha ke praarambhik swaroop or vikaas–kram ke pratindhi sakshya ke rup main unki upasthiti aashwastakaari rahi hai. vyangy vidha apni shaishvavastha main kitni chanchal, paripakv or satark thi yah vyas ji ke vyangyon ko padhakar sahaj hi janaa ja saktaa hai. pan. suryanarayan vyas kaa pehla vyangy–sangrah ‘tu-tu : main-main’ pustak bhawan kaashi se varsh 1935 main prakashit hua tha. baad main isi vishay par lagbhag 60 varsh baad 'tu-tu : main-main' dharawahik satellite channel par prasaarit hua.
 unki pratinidhi rachanaaon ke sampadak dow. prabhakar shrotriy ne ‘anushtup’ main likhaa hai- 
 tilak kii jeevani kaa anuwaad karte-karte ve krantikari bane. vir saavarkar kaa sahitya padhaa. unki kriti andaman kii goonj ne unhen bahut prabhavit kiya. pranavir pustakamala kii anek jabtashuda pustaken ve naujavaanon main gupt rup se vitarit kiya karte the. varsh 1920-21 ke kaal se to unki anek krantikari rachanaayen praapt hoti hai jo maalav mayur, vaani, sudhaa, aaj, saraswati, chaand, maadhuri, abhyuday or swarajya tathaa karmveer main bikhri padi hai. ve anek ‘chhadam’ naamon se likhte the, jaise- khag, ek madhya bhartiya, maalav-sut, dow chakradhar sharan, dow ekaant bihari, vyasacharya, surya-chandra, 'ek madhya bhartiya aatma' jaise anek naamon se ve baraabar likhte rahete the. 1930 main ajmer satyagrah main picketing karne pahunche, malwa ke jatthon kaa netritv bhi kiya, subhash babu ke aahvaan par ajmer main lord mayo kii pratimaa todaa or baad ke kaal main varsh 1942 main ‘bharti-bhawan’ se gupt radio stesan kaa sanchaalan bhi kiya jiske kaaran varsh 1946 main unhen indian difens act ke tahat jail-yatnaa kaa puraskaar bhi mila. bharat ke swatantra hone ke baad pension or puraskaar kii suchi bani to usamen unhone tanik bhi ruchi nahin lee.
 varsh 2002 main bharat sarkaar ne unke sammaan main ek dak tikat jari kiya.
 bharat ke pratham raashtrapati dwaara unhen padmabhushan se alankrita kiya gaya jise unhone angreji ko anant kaal tak jari rakhane vaale vidheyak ke virodh main lautaa bhi diya . hindi sahitya sammelan dwaara sahitya-vachaspati, vikram vishvavidhyalay dwaara di-lit, madhya pradesh shashan dwaara rajkeey farmaan jaise sammaanon se alankrita pan. vyas swatantraaa purv 114 riyasaton ke raaj jyotish bhi rahe . jyotish jagat ke ve sarvocch nyaayalay evam surya kehalaate hai.
