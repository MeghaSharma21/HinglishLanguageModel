 1925 main janme cartoonist ranga kaa naam puraa naam en ke ranganathan tha. bharat ke kuch pratham cartooniston main se ek ranga ne apane kaarykaal main indian express, da statsmen or tribune jaise pratishtheet samachaarpatron ke liye cartoonist ke rup main kaary kiya. kairikechar banane main daksh ranga ko unke dwaara banaaye kairicagers ke liye vishesh rup se janaa jaataa he. ranga dwaara chand rekhaon ke madhyam se banaae gaye mahatma gandhi ke kairicagers kaafii prasiddh hai. 
 july 2002 main dilli main ranga kaa dehanth ho gaya.
