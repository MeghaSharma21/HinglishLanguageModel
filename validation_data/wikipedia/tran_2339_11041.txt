 rahul tevatiya ek bhartiya cricket khilaadi he. ye mukhya rup se ek ek baayen haath ke ballebaaj he or saath hi daahine haath se spin gendbaaji bhi karte he. ye gharelu cricket main ranjai trophy main haryana ke liye khelte he jabaki 2018 indian premier lig main inhen dilli dairedevills kii team ne kharida.
 inhone saal 2014 se 2015 tak ipel main rajasthan royles ke liye cricket kheli thi jabaki iske baad 2016 se 2017 tak kings ilevan punjab ke liye khele or 2018 indian premier lig main inhen dilli dairedevills ne 3 karod main kharida.
