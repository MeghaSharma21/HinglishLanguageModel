 hansadikar abhikriya carboxylic amla ke chaandi ke lavan or hellojan ke bich carbonic abhikriya he jisase carbonic hellide praapt hote hai. yah hellojan pratikriya kaa ek udhaaharan he. pratikriya kaa naam henj hansadikar or clayr hoonsadiker ke naam par he, lekin ise sabase pehle borodin ne 1861 main dekha tha jab unhone rajat acitate se mithail bromide taiyaar kiya tha.
