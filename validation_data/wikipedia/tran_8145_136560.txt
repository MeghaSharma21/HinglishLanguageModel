 kaccha limbu ek 2011 kii bhartiya film he jo 18 farvari 2011 ko release hui thi. isamen atul kulkarni mukhya rup main he or adhik anya nae kalakaaron ne bhi isamen abhinay kiya he. yah film ek 13 saal kii umr ke ek mote bachche champoo ke ird-gird gumti he.
 ek 13 saal kaa motaa ladka dawaab ko jhelane main, jimmedaariyon ko lene main, or jivan style main samaayojan karne ke liye anek prakaar se sangharsh karata he.
