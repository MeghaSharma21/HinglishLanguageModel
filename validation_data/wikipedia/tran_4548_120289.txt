 dynamite ek pramukh visphotak he. iskaa avishkaar alfred nobel ne kiya tha.
 dynamite ke nirmaan main nitroglisreen prayukt hota he. nitroglisreen aavashyaktaa se adhik sugrahi hota he. isaki sugrahita ko kam karne ke liye kizalgar kaa upyog hota he. america main kizalgar ke sthaan main kaath chura, yaa kaath samita or sodium nitrate kaa upyog hota he. dynamite main nitroglisreen kii maatra 20, 40, yaa 60 75 prati shat rahati he. isaki prabalata nitroglisreen kii maatra par nirbhar karti he. 75 pratishat nitroglisreen vaala dynamite prabalatam hota he. kizalgar, yaa kashthachurna, yaa samita ke prayog kaa uddeshy dynamite kaa sangrakshan hota he, taaki yatayaat main vah visphutit na ho jaae. nitroglisreen 13 degree sein. par jam jaataa he. jam jane par yah visphutit nahin hota. at: thandhi jalwaayu main jamkar vah nikamma na ho jaae, isse bachaane ke liye usamen 20 bhaag glisreen dinitromonochlo-rahyderin milaayaa jaataa he. yah jamaavarodhikarak kaa kaam karata he. issse nitroglisreen -30 degree sein. tak drav rahata he. nitroglisreen ke sthaan main nitroglykol kaa upyog ab hone laga he.
