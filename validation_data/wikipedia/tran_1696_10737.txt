 mehndipathar railway stesan meghalaya, bharat ke resubelpada nagar nigam kaa ek railway stesan he.
  dudhnoi-deepa ke bich ek railway maarg rail bajet 1992-93 main prastaavit kiya gaya tha. baad main sanrekhan badal kar dudhnoi-mendipathar kiya gaya. aisa sthaneey logon ke virodh ke kaaran 2007 main kiya gaya. assam evam meghalaya sarkaar dwaara railway ko bhoomi adhigrahan kar deree se saunpane ke kaaran yah kaary vilamb 2013 tak hota gaya. bhoomi adhigrahan march 2013 main sampann hua.
 yah vartmaan main parvatiy rajya meghalaya rajya kaa ekmaatr tathaa pehla railway stesan he. is railway stesan kaa udghaatan bharat ke pradhanmantri narendra modi dwaara 30 navambar 2014 ko ek video ke madhyam se kiya gaya tha. is stesan se nikalne vaala vartmaan main ek hi railmarg he: mendipathar-guwahati.
