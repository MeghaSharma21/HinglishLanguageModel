 saans kii durgandh yaa muh kii durgandh yaa durgandhi prashwasan ke rogi ke mukh se ek vishesh durgandh aati he jo, saans ke saath mili hoti he. saanson kii durgandh grasit vyakti main chintaa kaa kaaran ban sakati he. 
 yah ek gambhir samasya ban sakati he kintu kuch saadhaaran upayon se saans kii durgandh ko roka ja saktaa he. saans kii durgandh un bacteria se paidaa hoti he, jo munh main paidaa hote hai or durgandh paidaa karte hai. niyamit rup se brush nahin karne se munh or daanton ke bich fansaa bhojan bacteria paidaa karata he. in bacteria dwaara utsarjit sulfur, yogic ke kaaran aapaki sanson main durgandh paidaa karata he. lahasun or pyaaj jaise kuch khaadya padaarthaan main tikhe tel hote hai. inase sanson kii durgandh paidaa hoti he, kyonki ye tel aapke fefdon main jate hai or munh se baahar aate hai. saans kii durgandh kaa ek anya pramukh kaaran dhumrapaan he. saans kii durgandh par kaabo paane ke bare main anek dharanaen prachlit hai. 
 munh saaf karne se saans kii durgandh keval asthaayee rup se dur hoti he. munh saaf karne ke liye kisi aise anti septic kii talash karen, jo durgandh paidaa karanevaale keetanuon ko martha ho, daanton kii sadan kam karata ho or vah indian dental association dwaara manyata praapt ho.
 sach yah he ki adhikansh log keval 30 se 45 second tak hi daanton ko brush karte hai, jo samuchit nahin he. daanton kii sabhi satahon ko samuchit rup se saaf karne ke liye aapako har din do baar kam se kam do minute tak brush karnaa chahie. jeebh kii safai bhi karnaa yaad rakhen, kyonki bacteria ko vahaan rahanaa pasand he. kulla karnaa bhi utana hi mahatvapurn he, kyonki keval brush karne se hi aapke daanton or masoodon ke bich fanse hanikaarak sadan or khaane ke tukade dur nahin ho sakate.
 yadi saans kii durgandh kii chintaa he, to munh or daanton kii dekhbhaal kaa dhyaan rakhen. kuch chini mukt gam or mint se bhi asthaayee rup se durgandh dur ho sakati he. yadi sahi tareeke se brush or kulla karte hai or niyamit safai ke liye daanton ke doctor se paas jate hai, lekin aapaki sanson main durgandh barkaraar he, to koi chikitsakeey samasya, jaise sincitis yaa masoodon kii beemaari ho sakati he. yadi kisi samasya kaa andeh ho, to apane chikitsak yaa daanton ke doctor se salaah len. ve tay kar sakate hai ki aapaki sanson kii durgandh ke peeche kya kaaran he or use dur karne main aapaki madad kar sakate hai.
