 sindhi rashtrawaad ek andolan he jiske anusaar pakistan or bharat ke sindhi jaateey-bhashaai samooh, ek hi raashtra hai. sindh main aadhunik rashtrawaad ke sansthaapak ji.em sayed ko samajha jaataa he. vartmaan main kai rashtravaadi sanghathan sindh main sakriy hai.
 saidhantik rup se sindhi rashtravaadiyon ke do mukhya samooh hai. pehla samooh algaavvaadi rashtravaadi hai, jo ek swatantra sindhudesh kaa samarthan karte hai. doosraa samooh kaa maananaa he ki sindh pakistan kaa praant banakar rahanaa chaiye.
