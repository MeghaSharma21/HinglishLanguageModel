 inter sarvice intelegence pakistan kii sabase badi intelegence seva he. 1950 main puure pakistan kii aantarik or bahya suraksha kaa jimmaa iesai ko saunp diya gaya. isamen sena ke teenon angon ke adhikari milakar iesai ke liye kaam karte hai. purv main iskaa mukhyalay rawalpindi main tha or ise "intelegence beauron" ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. vartmaan main iesai kaa mukhyalay islamabad main he or leftinent general ahamed shuja pasha iske nideshak hai. vartmaan main iske staff main lagbhag 25 hajaar log hai.
 1947 main pakistan main do mukhya guptchar agenciyaan thi, intelegence beuro or military intelegence, par 1947 main hue bharat-paak yuddh main pakistani military intelegence sena ke teen angon navy, army or airforce ke bich soochnaaon or vyavasthaaon ke aadan-pradaan main ekadam vifal rahi. is asafalataa se ek nai agency kii jarurat mahasoos hui. natijatan 1948 main iesai kaa gathan kiya gaya. iesai kii neenv australian mul ke british army officer major general aar. cathom ne rakhi thi jo us samay pakistani army staff ke mukhya the.
 iesai ke diarector general ko teen dipti diarector general desh main ghat rahi vibhinn gatividhiyon kii jaankaree dete rahete hai jo raajnneeti, bahya or saamaanya kshetron ke prati javabdeh hote hai.
