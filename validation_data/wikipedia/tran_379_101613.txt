 hafalong hindi assam kii uttar kachar pahari jile kii aam bolchaal kii bhashaa he. yah ek mishrit bhashaa he jo hindi se utpann hui he or isane anya bhashaaon jaise ki bengali, asamiya, dimasa or zeme naga bhashaaon ke shabdon ko bhi aatmsaat kar liya he. is mishrit bhashaa kaa naam hafalong ke naam par padaa he jo uttari kachar pahari jile kaa mukhyalay he.
 hafalong hindi, hindi ke us vrihad rup kaa parichaayak he jo ki puure dakshin asia main kisi naa kisi rup main viddhman he. yah boli hindi bhashiyon ke liye subodh he or isamen hindi ke vyakaran or shabdon ke atirikt sthaneey bhashaaon ke shabdon kaa grahit aasav he.
 bahut si pedjin boliyaan usee sthiti tak simit rahati hai or adhikanshtah vyapaarik uddeshyon ke liye prayukt kii jaatin hai. jab kisi boli kaa vistaar hota he or vah ek praakrit-bhashaa banati he or usamen sthayitv aata he or use bade paimaane par sweekaar kiya jaataa he, to takneekee rup se vah ek bhashaa hoti he. tok pisin or siselva bhashaaon ne yah pad praapt kar liya he. hafalong hindi maanakeekaran ke in chihnon ko varnit karti he or ise bade paimaane par apanaaya ja rahaa he. udaahranarth, ol india radio uttari kachar pahari jile main is boli main prasaaran karata he or hindi bolne waalon, jinamein sarkari karmchaaree bhi sammilit he, se hafalong hindi seekhney kii apeksha rakhi jaati he. isliye, hafalong hindi ko ek creole bhashaa ke rup main vargeekrut kiya jaataa he bajaae kii pedjin ke.
