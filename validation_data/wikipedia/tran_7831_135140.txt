 abhinayta vah purush kalakaar he jo ek chalchitr yaa naatak main kisi charitra kaa abhinay karata he. abhinayta pariklpana evam darshak ke bich madhyam kaa kaam karata he. jo di gayi bhoomika ko kisi manch dwaara darshak ke liye prastut karata he. abhinay kii kala kaa gyaan evam abhinayta ke bhav prastutikaran ko sarthak banaata he.
