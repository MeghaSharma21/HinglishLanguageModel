 tabari athwa tabari ek mahaan arab itihaaskaar or islam dharamshastri tha. 
 sambhavat: 838-39 i0 main tabaristaan kshetra kaa amul naamak sthaan main uskaa janm hua tha. sampann parivaar main janm, kushagrabuddhi or meghavi hone ke kaaran bachapan se hi vah atyant honhaar dikh padataa tha. kehete hai, saat varsh kii avasthaa main hi sampurna quran ise kanthsth ho gaya. apane nagar main rahakar to usne bahumoolya shiksha pai hi, us samay ke islam jagat ke anya sabhi prasiddh vidyakendron main bhi vah gaya or anek prasiddh vidwaanon se padhaa. basra, baghdad, koof or misr kii usne anek baar yatraayen kii. duusari baar misr jate hue syria main usne haddees kaa adhyan kiya or sheeghra hi un kshetron main vah prakand gina jane laga. tabari kii sabase badi visheshta yah thi ki vah vidvan hone ke saath saath atyant charitravan bhi tha. rajya kii ore se arthik labhavale kai sammanjanak padon ko sweekruti ke liye usase aagrah kiye gaye kintu kisi bhi pralobhan main na padakar usne padhane-padhaane or saahitiyik seva main hi apana jivan bitaayaa. yadhyapi koi bhi vishay-itihaas, quran kaa paath or uski vyakhya, kavyrachana, vyakaran or shabdkosh, nitishastra, ganit or bhishajya-usase achoota nahin rahaa, vah mukhyat: itihaas or islamic dharamshastra ke gyaata or lekhak ke rup main hi prasiddh he. uski pramukh rachana taarikh 'al rasul val-muluk' naamak vishwa kaa itihaas he, jo 30 hajaar kaagaj kii takhtiyon par likhaa gaya. anushrutiyon, dantakthaon or boode logon se suni hui kahaniyon ke aadhaar par likhe gaye arab itihaas ke is mahodadhi ko chaan sakna kisi bhi ek vyakti ke liye dushkar he. tabari ke jeevankaal ke thode hi samay pashchaat farsi main uskaa anuwaad hua tha or us anuwaad kaa pun: turki bhashaa main bhi anuwaad hua. is taarikh ke atirikt usne 'jami al-bayaan kii tafaseer al-quran' par ek tikaa bhi likhi thi, jise sankshep main 'tafaseer' kaha jaataa he. lagbhag 75 varshon kii avasthaa tak sahityasarjan karte hue tabari ne 923 i0 main sharir tyaag diya.
