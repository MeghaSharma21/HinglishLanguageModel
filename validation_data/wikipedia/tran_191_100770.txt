 mahasanganak un sanganakon ko kaha jaataa he jo vartmaan samay main gananaa-shakti tathaa kuch anya maamalon main sabase aage hote hai. atyaadhunik takneekon se las super computer bahut bade-bade pariklan or ati sookshm gananaaen tivrata se kar saktaa he. isamen kai microprocesar ek saath kaam karte hue kisi bhi jatiltam samasya kaa turant hal nikaal lete hai. vartmaan main uplabdh computaron main super computer sabase adhik teevr kshamta, dakshata va sabase adhik smriti kshamta vaala computer he. aadhunik paribhaasha ke anusaar, ve computer, jo 500 megaflop kii kshamta se kaary kar sakate hai, super computer kehalaate he. super computer ek second main ek arab gananaaen kar saktaa he. isaki gati ko mega flop se napate he.
 super computaron kaa istemaal mukhyat: vishvavidhyalayon, sainik va vaigyanik anusandhan prayogshaalaon main kiya jaataa he. iskaa upyog khaskar aise kshetron main kiya jaataa he, jinamein kuch hi kshanon main bade paimaane par gananaaen karne kii jarurat padati he. masalan, mausam sambadhi anusandhan, nabhikeey hathiyaaron, kwantum physics or rasinic yogicon ke adhyayan main super computer kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he.
 jyadatar nae superkamputars me linaks oparating system kaam liye jaataa he lekin linaks ke alawa CentOS, bullx SCS, SUSE and Cray linaks oparating system superkamputor ke liye kaam me lete he |
 pehla super computer illiaak 4 he, jisane 1975 main kaam karnaa aarambh kiya. ise danniel slotanic ne viksit kiya tha. yah akele hi ek baar main 64 computaron kaa kaam kar saktaa tha. isaki mukhya memory main 80 lakh shabd aa sakate the or yah 8, 32, 64 bits ke tareekon se ankaganit kriyaayein kar saktaa tha. isaki kaary kshamta 30 karod pariklan kriyaayein prati second thi, arthath jitani der main ham bamushkil 8 tak kii ginati gin sakate hai, utane samay main yah jod, ghataana, guna, bhaag ke 30 karod sawaal hal kar saktaa tha.
 international conference for hai parformens computing reno ne duniya ke top- 500 computaron kii suchi jari kii he. isamen tata ke super computer eka ko duniya main chauthaa or asia main sabase tej super computer qaraar diya gaya he. yah ek second main 117.9 trillian gananaaen kar saktaa he. 40 varsh pehle super computer ke bazaar main jahaan mahaj kai companiyaan thi, vahiin ab is bazaar main cre, del, echpi, ibiem, enisi, esajiai, echpi, san jaise bade naam hi bache hai.

 1980 ke antim dashak main bharat ko america ne cre super computer dene se inkaar kar diya tha. iske peeche america kii apane prabhutv barkaraar rakhane kii manshaa maani ja rahi thi, kyonki vah ek aisa daur tha, jab bharat or china main takneekee kranti kii shuruyaat ho chuki thi. aise main, america nahin chahta tha ki vishwa main koi duusari shakti takanic ke maamle main uske mukaable main khadi ho. chunki super computer ke upyog se rocket prakshepan, parmaanu visphot ke samay gananaaon main aasaani ho jaati he, isliye bhi america ke man main bhay tha ki kahin iske dwaara bharat apane nabhikeey urja prasar kaarykram ko ek nayaa rup na de de. lekin bhartiya vaigyanikon ne si-deck param-8000 computer banaakar apni kshamtaaon kaa ehsaas kara diya. 1988 main rus ne bharat ko super computer dene kii baat kahi thi. lekin hardware sahi na hone ke kaaran rus ke prastaav ko asweekaar kar diya. bharat ne super computer banane ke baad param 8000 germany, euke or rus ko diya.
 bharat bhi ab super computer ke kshetra main ek hasti he. duniya ke awwal 500 super computaron kii nai top ten list main uskaa super computer chauthe sthaan par aayaa he. tata company kii pune sthit ikai - 'computesnal research laboratories' ke banaae hue super computer ‘echpi-3000-biel-460-si’ ko 117.9 terraphlop kii gati ke kaaran america or germany ke super computaron ke thik baad sthaan diya gaya he. haalanki hamaara yah pehla super computer nahin he. isse kaaphi pehle 1998 main si-deck, pune ke vaigyanik ‘param-10000’ super computer bana chuke hai or daawa tha ki us vakt vah super computer maujuuda america super computaron ke mukaable 50 guna tej tha. lekin uske baad super computing ko lekar bharat main waisi utsukata nahin dikhaai di, jaisi anya viksit mulkon main is dauraan rahi he. par ab lagata he ki bharat is daud main pichde nahin rahanaa chahta, jiskaa nateeja he tata kaa yah super computer.
