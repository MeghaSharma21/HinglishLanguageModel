 yah bihar ke dev main sthit surya mandir he. yah mandir purvabhimukh naa hokar pashchimabhimukh he. yah mandir apni anuthi shilpakala ke liye prakhyat he. pattharon ko taraash kar banaae gaye is mandir kii nakkashi utkrisht shilp kala kaa namoona he. yahan chhath parv ke avasar par baari bheed umadti he.
 '
 prachlit manyata ke anusaar iskaa nirmaan swayan bhagwaan vishwakarmane kiya he. is mandir ke baahar sanskrit main likhe shloke ke anusaar 12 lakh 16 hajaar varsh tretayug ke guzar jane ke baad raja ilaputra pururava ail ne is surya mandir kaa nirmaan prarambh karavaaya tha. shilaalekh se pataa chalata he ki purv 2007 main is puranic mandir ke nirmaankaal kaa ek lakh pachaas hajaar saat varsh puraa hua. puratatvvid is mandir kaa nirmaan kaal aathvin-nauveen sadi ke bich kaa maanate hai.
 kaha jaataa he ki surya mandir ke pattharon main vijay chinh va kalash ankit hai. vijay chinh yah darshata he ki shilp ke kalakaar ne surya mandir kaa nirmaan kar ke hi shilp kala par vijay praapt kii thi. dev surya mandir ke sthapaty kala ke bare main kai tarah kii kinvadantiyaan he.
 mandir ke sthapaty se prathit hota he ki mandir ke nirmaan main oriya swaroop nagar style kaa samaayojan kiya gaya he. nakkashidaar pattharon ko dekhakar bhartiya puraatatv vibhaag ke log mandir ke nirmaan main nagar evam dravid style kaa mishrit prabhaav vaali vesar style kaa bhi samanvay bataate he.
 mandir ke praangan main saat rathon se surya kii utkeern prastar murtiyon apane teenon roopon udayachal, madhyachal tathaa astachal ke rup main vidyaman hai. iske saath hi vahaan adbhut shilp kala vaali darjanon pratimaayein hai. mandir main shiv ke jaangh par baithi parvati kii pratimaa he. sabhi mandiron main shivlinga kii puja kii jaati he. isliye shiv parvati kii yah durlabh pratimaa shraddhaluon ko khaasi aakarshith karti he.
 mandir kaa shilp urisa ke konark surya mandir se miltaa he. dev surya mandir do bhaagon main bana he. pehla garbh grih jiske upar camel ke aakaar kaa shikhar he or shikhar ke upar sone kaa kalash he. doosraa bhaag mukhmandap he jiske upar pyramidnuma chat or chat ko sahaara dene ke liye nakkashidaar pattharon kaa bana stambh he. tamaam hindu mandiron ke vipareet pashchimabhimukh dev surya mandir devark mana jaataa he jo shraddhaluon ke liye sabase zyada faldaayi evam manokamana purna karne vaala he.
 is mandir ke dekhbhaal kaa daayitv dev surya mandir nyaas samiti kaa he.
