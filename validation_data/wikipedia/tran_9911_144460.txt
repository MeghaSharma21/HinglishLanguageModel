 vyaktiyon, samoohon, rashtron yaa jaanwaron ke bich bhushetra, pad yaa sansadhano ke aabantan aadi ke liye sangharsh ko pratiyogita yaa pratispardhaa kehete hai. jab kabhi do yaa do se adhik vyakti yaa samooh aise lakshya kii prapti ke liye udyat ho jise kevak ek yaa keval kuch ko hi pradaan kiya ja saktaa ho to pratiyogita kaa janm hota he. ek hi paryaavaran main rahane vaale jeevon ke bich sahaj rup main hi pratiyogita vidyaman hoti he.
