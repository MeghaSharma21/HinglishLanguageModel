 bandargah samudri jahaajon ke thaharne kii jagah hai. vishwa main kai vishaal va chote bandargah hai.
 kandla bandargah bharat main gujarat praant main kuccha zile main sthit desh kaa sabase bada bandargah he. yah bandargah bharat kaa sabase pehla mukt vyapaar kshetra he. kandla bandargah bharat ke sabase bade 12 mukhya bandargaho main se cargo hendling main sabase bada he. yah kandla nadi par bana hua he. adhikariyon kii anumati lekar yahaan ghumaa bhi ja saktaa he. yah bandargah aayaat-niryaat se puure vishwa ke saath judaa hua he. kandla bandargah khaas aarthik kshetra, jo speshyal economik jone se janaa jaataa he. ye bandargah puure bharat evam asia kaa sabase pehla khaas aarthik kshetra he, jisaki sthapana i.sa. 1965 main hui thi.
