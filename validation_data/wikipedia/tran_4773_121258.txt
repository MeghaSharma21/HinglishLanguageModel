 latifur rahamaanলতিফুর রহমান
 latif ur rahamaan, bangladesh ke purv mukhya nyaayadhish evam mukhya salaahkaar the. unka janm jessor main, march 1936 ko hua tha. varsh 1986 main unhen asthai rup se ucch nyaayalay vibhaag main, bataur nyaayadhish niyukt kiya gaya tha, or 1981 main vah usee vibhaag main sthai nyaayadhish ke rup main niyukt hue. 15 janvari 1991 main vah bangladesh kii sarvocch nyaayalay ke apiliy vibhaag main bhataar nyaayadhish niyukt kiye gaye, tathaa 1 janvari san 2000 main vah bangladesh ke mukhya nyaayadhish ke pad par virajman hue. 28 farvari ko unhone sevanivritti lee. tatpashchaat san 2000 kii samayik sarkaar main unhen mukhya salaahkaar banaaya gaya, jisane ashtum sansadeey chunaav kii dekharekh kii thi. ve is pad par 15 july 2000 se 1 oktober 2001 tak rahe.
