 midnites children  main prakashit salmaan rushdi ke dwaara likhaa gaya ek upanyaas he. is upanyaas kaa shilp jaadui yatharthwaad par aadhaarit he.
  'midnites children' swatantraaa ke baad kii ghatnaon par aadhaarit he. is kahani kaa nayak or mukhya charitra saleem sinai he. saleem sinai kaa janm 15 agast 1947, raat ke 12 baje hua tha. isi vajah se saleem kaa janm telepathic shaktiyon ke saath hua tha.
  kahani kii shuruyaat saleem ke dadaji se hoti he. yah kitaab teen hison main banti hui he. saleem kaa janm 15 agast 1947 ke thik aadhi raat par hua tha. use baad main pate chalata he kii jin- jin bacchon kaa janm usee din hua tha unke paas bhi asaadhaaran shaktiyaan thim. unamem se shiv or parvati kahani main pramukh bhoomika nibhaate hai. apni telepathic shaktiyon kaa istemaal karke saleem un baccho se sampark karke ek sabha sthapit karata he jisamen vibhinn vishyon par charchaa hoti he. 
  isi samay main saleem kaa parivaar kai samasyaon se jujhataa he. bharat or saath ke deshon ke bich ladaaiyaan bhi chal rahi hoti hai. baad main saleem indira gandhi kii ghoshit kii hui "immergency" or sanjay gandhi ke jumma masjid vivaad main fas jaataa he. is kaaran use jail bhi hoti he.
 "immergency" ke baad saleem or baaki log apni-apni shaktiyaan kho dete hai. shaktiyaan khone ke baad saleem apni or apane desh kii kahani apane bete ke liye likhata he, jise apane pita se vahi asaadhaaran shaktiyaan milti hai.
