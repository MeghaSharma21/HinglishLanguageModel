 rele va bres ke prayog  kaa uddeshy dwiapavartan siddhanth kii sahaayta se lambaai main sankuchan kaa praayogic adhyayan karnaa tha. yah prayog un un praarambhik prayogon se ek he jo prithvi kii aapekshik gati or prakashwahi ether kaa maapan karne ke liye bane tathaa isaki prakaash ke veg ke liye maapan shuddhta v/c kii dwitiya koti tak thi. iske parinaam nakaaratmak rahe jo lorents roopantaran ke vikaas ke liye bahut mahatv rakhata he jiske falswaroop aapekshikta siddhant kaa nirmaan hua.
 mikelson morle prayog se mili nakaaratmak parinaamon ko samajhane ke liye jorge phitzgerald or hendrick larenz ne sankuchan pariklpana kaa pratipaadan kiya jiske anusaar sthir ether madhyam se guzarane par vastuon kii lambaai main sankuchan paidaa hota he.
