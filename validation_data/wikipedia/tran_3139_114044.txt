 
 rampatri bharat ke pashchim tateey kshetra main paaya jane vaala paudha he jiskaa vanaspati vaigyanik naam 'miristic malabarika' he. yah bharat kaa deshaj paudha he jo lupti ke kagaar par he. iske ped kii lambaai 25 meter tak hoti he. isaki chaal haraa-kaala tathaa chikani hoti he, kabhi-kabhi laal bhi hoti he.
 ise pilav or biryani main sugandh ke liye istemaal kiya jaataa he. bhabha parmaanu anusandhan kendra ne isse cancer kii dawa viksit kii he jiskaa pareekshan chuhon par kiya ja chukaa he. yah dawa fefde ke cancer or bacchon main hone vaale durlabh prakaar ke cancer 'neuroblastoma' ke upachaar main kaaphi asardaar sabit ho sakati he. neuroblastoma ek aisa cancer he jisamen vrikk grathiyon, gardan, seene or ridh kii nerve koshikaon main cancer koshikaayen badhane lagati hai.
