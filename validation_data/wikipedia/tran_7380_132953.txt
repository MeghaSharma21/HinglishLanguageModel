 gopi nath ji mandir vrundavan main sthit ek vaishnav sampradaay kaa mandir he. isaki nirmaan tithi agyaat he. iskaa mul nirmaan kushwaha thakuron kii shekhavat shakha ke sansthaapak ke pautra raisil ne karavaaya tha. baad main 1821 main nandakumar ghosh naamak ek bengali kysth ne nayaa mandir banwaaya. is mandir kii nirmaan style madanamohan mandir se shilp main milti julati he.
