 viki. jiaies yaa jiaies inacychlopedia bhugolic soochna pranali ko samarpit ek vishwakosh he.
 jiaies peshewaron, chaatro or jiaies main ruchi rakhane vaale kisi bhi vyakti ke laabh ke liye jiaies samudaay se is bhandar main yogadan shaamil he. viki main jiaies avadharanaon, nai praudyogikiyon, utpaadon, logon or sanghathanon ke bare main jaankareepurn lekh shaamil hai.
 jiaies kaa koi bhi gyaan kisi bhi vyakti ko khaataa banane ke liye swaagat se adhik he or us prishth ko nae pej banaakar yaa maujuuda prishthon ko vistaarit karke us jaankaree ko saajha karnaa shuru kar saktaa he.
