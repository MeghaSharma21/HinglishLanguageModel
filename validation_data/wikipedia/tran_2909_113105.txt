 langadaa bukhaar saadhaaran bhashaa main jaharabaad, fadsoojan, kaala by, krishnajandha, langadia, ekatanga aadi naamon se bhi janaa jaataa he. yah rog praayah sabhi sthanon par paaya jaataa he lekin nami vaale kshetron main vyapak rup se failata he. 
 mukhya rup se is rog se gaay, bhens evam bhend prabhavit hoti he. yah rog chah maah se do saal tak kii aayu vaale pashuon main adhik paaya jaataa he. yah rog 'langadi beemaari' ke naam se prasiddh he. yah gau jati main praayah tathaa bhais or bhedon main kabhi-kabhi hone vaala chhitput athwa mahamari prakrutiwala jeevanu se paidaa hone vaala pashu rog he jisamen saadhaaran fever tathaa maansal bhaag kaa dardeyukt sujan evam langadaapan pramukh lakshan he. yuva tathaa swasth pashu zyada prabhavit hote hai.
 yah rog go-pashuon main adhik hota he. is rog main pashu ko tej bukhaar aata he tathaa uskaa taapmaana 106 degree forenite se 107 forenite tak pahunch jaataa he. pashu sust hokar khaanaa pinaa chod detaa he. pashu ke pichhali va agali taangon ke upree bhaag main baari sujan aa jaati he. jisase pashu langadaa kar chalane lagata he yaa phir baith jaataa he. tathaa sujan vaale sthaan ko dabaane par kad-kad kii awaz aati he. pashu chalane main asamarth hota he. yah rog praayah pichley pairon ko adhik prabhavit karata he evam sujan ghutane se upar vaale hisse main hoti he. yah sujan shuru main garam evam kshtdaayak hoti he jo baad main thand evam darderhit ho jaati he. pairon ke atirikt sujan peeth, kandhe tathaa anya mansapeshiyon vaale hisse par bhi ho sakati he. sujan ke upar vaali chamadi sookhkar kadi hoti jaati he. 
 pashu kaa upachaar sheeghra karwaanaa chahie kyonki is beemaari ke jeevanuon dwaara hua zehar sharir main puri tarah fail jane se pashu kii mrityu ho jaati he. is beemaari main prokene penicillin kaaphi prabhaavshaalee he. is beemaari ke rog nirodhak tike lagaae jate he.
 mrityu dar : 80-100 pratishat.
 penicillin, salphonamide, tetraciclin group ke antiba yotics kaa saportive aushadhi ke saath upyog, beemaari kii tivrata tathaa pashu kii sthiti ke anusaar laabhakaari he. sujan vaale bhaag main chira lagaakar 2 pratishat hydrogen peroxide tathaa potashium paramanganet se dressing kiya janaa laabhakaari he.
