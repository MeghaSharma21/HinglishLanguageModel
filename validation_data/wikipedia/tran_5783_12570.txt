 aluminium dhaatu vidyut tathaa ooshmaa kaa chaalak tathaa kaafii halki hoti he. iske kaaran iskaa upyog hawaii jahaaj ke purjon ko banane main kiya jaataa he. bharat main jammu kashmir, mumbai, kolhapur, jabalpur, ranchi, sonbhadra, balaghat tathaa katni main boxit ke vishaal bhandar paae jate he. urisa sthit nalko duniya kii sabase sasti aluminium banane vaali company he.
 aluminium shwet rang kii ek dhaatu he. latin bhashaa ke shabd aluman or angreji ke shabd ailam kaa arth fitkaree he. is fitkaree main se jo dhaatu pruthak kii ja saki, uskaa naam aluminium padaa. fitkiri se to hamaara parichay bahut purana he. kankshi, tuvari or saurashtraj iske puraane naam he. fitkaree vastut: potacium salphet or aluminium salphet in donon kaa dvigun yogic he. 
 san 1754 main margraph ne yah pradarshit kiya ki jis mitti ko alumina kaha jaataa he, vah chooney se bhinn he. sar hanferi davy ne san 1807 hi main alumia mitti se dhaatu pruthak karne kaa praytn kiya, parantu safalataa na mili. san 1825 main arsted ne aluminium chloride ko potacium sanras ke saath garam kiya or phir aswan karke paare ko uda diya. aisa karne par jo churna sa batch rahaa usamen dhaatu kii chamak thi. yahi dhaatu aluminium kahalaai. san 1845 main frederick vohalar ne is dhaatu ke taiyaar karne main potacium dhaatu kaa prayog apchaayak ke rup main kiya. use is dhaatu ke kuch chote-chote kan mili, jinaki pariksha karke usne bataaya ki yah nai dhaatu bahut halki he or iske taar khinche ja sakate hai. tadnantar sodium or sodium aluminium chloride kaa prayog karke san 1854 main devil ne is dhaatu kii acchhee maatra taiyaar kii. us samay nai dhaatu hone ke kaaran aluminium kii ginati bahumoolya dhaatuon main kii jaati thi or iskaa upyog abharanon or alankaaron main hota tha. san 1886 main ohayo nagar main chalrs martin haul ne gale hue cryolite main alumina ghola or usamen se vidyudvishleshan vidhi dwaara aluminium dhaatu pruthak kii. europe main bhi lagbhag isi varsh hero ne swatantra rup se isi prakaar yah dhaatu taiyaar kii. yahi haul-hero-vidhi aajkal is dhaatu ke utpaadan main vyavahrut ho rahi he. halaki or sasti hone ke kaaran aluminium or usase bani mishra dhaatuon kaa prachlan tab se baraabar badhata chalaa ja rahaa he.
 aluminium dhaatu taiyaar karne ke liye do khanijon kaa vishesh upyog hota he. ek to bauksoit or doosraa cryolite . bauksite ke vistrut nikshep bharat main ranchi, palamu, jabalpur, balaghat, selem, belgaum, kolhapur, thana aadi jilon main paae gaye hai. is desh main is khanij kii anumit maatra 2.8 karod ton he. 
 aluminium dhaatu taiyaar karne ke nirmitt pehla praytn yah kiya jaataa he ki bauksite se shuddh alumina mili. bauksite ke shodhan kii ek vidhi, bair vidhi ke naam par prachlit he. isamen bauksite ko garam castic soda ke vilyan ke saath abhikrut karke sodium aluminute bana lete hai. is aluminute ke vilyan ko chaan lete hai or isamen se phir alumina kaa avakshepan kar liya jaataa he. .
