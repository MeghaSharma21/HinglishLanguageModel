 aashaa bhonsle hindi filmon kii mashhoor parshvagayika hai. lata mangeshkar kii choti bahan or deenanath mangeshkar kii putree aashaa ne filmi or gaiyr filmi lagbhag 16 hajaar gane gaaye hai or inaki awaz ke prashansak puri duniya main faile hue hai. hindi ke alaava unhone marathi, bengali, gujarati, punjabi, bhojpuri, tamil, malayalam, angreji or roosi bhashaa ke bhi anek geet gaae hai. aashaa bhonsle ne apana pehla geet varsh 1948 main sawan aayaa film chunariya main gaaya. 
 aashaa kii visheshta he ki inhone shastreey sangeet, ghazal or pop sangeet har kshetra main apni awaz kaa jaadu bikheraa he or ek samaan safalataa pai he. unhone aar di burman se shaadi kii thi.
 aashaa bhosle kaa janm 8 sitambar 1933 ko maharashtra ke ‘sangli’ main hua. inke pita deenanath mangeskar prasiddh gayak evam nayak the. jinhone shastreey sangeet kii shiksha kaaphi choti umr main hi aashaa ji ko di. aashaa ji jab keval 9 varsh kii thim, inke pita kii mrityu ho gai. pita ke maranoparant, inkaa parivaar pune se kolhapur or uske baad bombay aa gaya. parivaar kii sahaayta ke liye aashaa or inaki badi bahan lata mangeskar ne gaanaa or filmon main abhinay shuru kar diya. 1943 main inhone apni pehali film ‘maajha bal’ main geet gaaya. yah geet ‘chalaa chalaa nav bala...’ datta dawajakar ke dwaara sangitabaddh kiya gaya tha. 1948 main hindi film ‘chunariya’ kaa geet ‘sawan aayaa...’ hansraj bahal ke liye gaaya. dakshin asia kii prasiddh gayika ke rup main aashaa ji ne geet gaae. film sangeet, pop, gazal, bhajan, bhartiya shastreey sangeet, kshetreey geet, qawwali, rabindra sangeet or najarul geet inke geeton main sammilit he. inhone 14 se zyada bhashaaon main geet gaae yatha– marathi, asami, hindi, urdu, telugu, marathi, bengali, gujarati, punjabi, bhojpuri, tamil, angreji, russian, jich, nepali, malay or malayalam. 12000 se adhik geeton ko aashaa ji ne aawaaj di.
 16 varsh kii umr main apane 31 varshiya premi ‘ganpat raav bhosle’ ke saath ghar se palaayan kar paariwarik ichha ke viruddh vivaah kiya. ganpat raav lata ji ke nijee sachiv the. yah vivaah asafal rahaa. pati evam unke bhaaiyon ke bure vartav ke kaaran is vivaah kaa du:khaant ho gaya. 1960 ke aasapaas vivaah viched ke baad aashaa ji apni maan ke ghar do bacchon or tisare garbhasth shishu ke saath laut ain. 1980 i. main aashaa ji ne ‘rahul dev varman’ se vivaah kiya. yah vivaah aashaa ji ne rahul dev varman ke antim saanso tak safaltaapurvak nibhaayaa.
 aashaa ji kaa ghar dakshin mumbai, pedar road kshetra main prabhukunj apartment main sthit he. inke teen bachche hai. saath hi paanch pautra bhi he. inkaa sabase bada ladkaa hemanth bhosle he. hemanth ne payalat ke rup main adhikansh samay bitaayaa. aashaaji kii beti jo hemanth se choti he “varsha”. varsha ne ‘da sunday objravar’ or ‘radiff’ ke liye kollam likhane kaa kaam kiya. aashaaji kaa sabase chota putr anand bhosle he. anand ne bijness or film nirdesan kii padhai kii. anand bhosle hi aashaa ke kariyar kii in dinon dekhbhaal kar rahe hai. hemanth bhosle ke sabase bade putr chaitanya “boy band” ke safal sadsy ke rup main vishwa sangeet se jude hue he. anika bhosle safal photographer ke rup main kaary kar rahi he. aashaa ji kii bahanen lata mangeskar or ushaa mangeskar gayika he. inaki anya do sahodar bahan minaa mangeskar or bhai hridayanath mangeskar sangeet nirdeshak he. aashaaji gayika ke alaava bahut acchhee cook he. kooking inkaa pasandida shauk he. bollywood ke bahut saare log aashaa ji ke haathon se bane ‘kadhai gost’ evam ‘biryani’ ke liye anurodh karte hai. aashaa ji bhi inkaar nahin karti he. bollywood ke ‘capur’ khandaan main aashaa ji dwaara banaae gayi ‘paaya kari’, ‘gojhan fish kari’ or ‘daal’ kaaphi prasiddh he. ek baar jab ‘da times of india’ ke ek saakshaatkaar main puchha gaya ki yadi aap gayika na hoti to kya karti? aashaa ji ne jabaab diya ki main ek acchhee rasoiyaa banati. aashaa ji ek safal restaro sanchalika he. inke restaro dubai or kuwait main aashaa naam se prasiddh he.‘wafi group’ dwaara sanchalit restaro main aashaa ji ke 20% bhagidaari he. wafi siti dubai or do restaro kuwait main parmparik uttar bhartiya vyanjan ke liye prasiddh he. aashaa ji ne ‘cafs’ ko swany 6 mahino kaa trennig diya he. disambar 2004 ‘menu magazine’ ke report ke anusaar ‘rasel scot’ jo ‘harry ramasadein’ ke pramukh he aane vaale paanch saalo main aashaa ji ke brand ke antargat 40 restaro puure euke ke andar kholane kii ghoshnaa kii he. isi kram main aashaa ji kii kii restaro ‘barmingam’ euke main kholaa gaya he. aashaa ji kii fishan or pahanaave main safed sari chamakdaar kinaaro vaali, gale main motiyon ke haar or hira prasiddh he. aashaa ji ek acchhee ‘mimicri’ adaakara bhi he.

 sangeetkaar o.pi. naiyar kii saajhedaari ne aashaa bhosle ji ko ek khaas pahchaan dilaayaa. kai logon ne inaki aapasi sambandh ko prem sambandh maan liya. naiyar ji pehali baar 1952 main aashaa ji se ek geet cham chama cham... ke sangeet ricardig ke samay milen. pehali baar inhone film ‘maangu’ ke liye aashaa ji ko bulaayaa. phir inhone ek bahut bada mauka film si.ai.di. main aashaa ji ko diya. is prakaar nayaa daur kii safalataa ne in dono kii prasiddhi ko badhaayaa. 1959 ke baad bhavanatmak or vyavsaayik taur par aashaa ji naiyar ji ke saath judi raheen.

 duusare sangeetkaar jinhone aashaa ji ke pratibha ko pahachaanaa or inaki saajhedaari me aashaa ji ne inaki pehali film bibi ke liye gaai. 1950 me khayyam ne kai achche anubandh aashaa ji ke saath kiye yatha—film- dard, phir subah hogi aadi. kintu un dono kii saajhedaari kii sabase yaadgaar film- umrao jaan ke geet rahe.
 ravi:— sangeet nirdeshak ravi, aashaa bhosle ji ko apni pasandida gayika me ek maanate the. aashaa ji inaki pehali film vachan me geet gaai. chandaa mamaa dur ke.... geet rato raat bhartiya maan ke bich prasiddh ho gaya. jab adhikansh sangeetkaar aashaa ji ko bi-grad geet gane ke liye jaanate the un dino ravi ne aashaa ji se bhajan gane ko kaha jinamein ‘gharaana’, ‘gruhasthi’ ‘kaajal’ or ‘phul or pathar’ filmein pramukh he. ravi or aashaa ji ne kai prasiddh gito kaa ricard kiya jiname kishor kumaar ke saath gaae unke mjedaar geet ‘si e tii..cat maane billi... aadi he. ravi dwaara sangitabaddh aashaa ji ke prasiddh bhajan ‘tora man darpan kehalaae...’ aadi he. inaki saajhedaari main kai prasiddh filmon ke geet ricard hue yatha- vakt, chaudahavi kaa chaand, gumraah, bahu-beti, chyna toun, aadmi or insaan, dhundh, hamraaj or kaajal aadi he. chaudaavin ke chaand main ravi gita dutt se geet gavaana chaahate the kintu guru dat ke jid ke kaaran aashaa ji ne geet gaae jo kaaphi prasiddh hue.

 lata mangeskar ke saath aashaa ji marathi sangeet kii sirmaur rahi he . aashaa ji marathi bhashaa kii aneko geet ga chuki he jiname prasiddh kavion ke kavita he, jo bhav geet ke rup me prasiddh he. yatha prasiddh elban ‘rutu heraava’ jo shridhar padke dwaara rachit he. apane bhai hridayanath mangeskar dwaara sangitabaddh aashaa ji ke kai prasiddh geet he. aashaa ji dwaara gaae marathi bhajan kaaphi prachlit or prasiddh he.
 8 sitambar 1987 aashaa ji ke janm diwas par “dil padosi he” naamak elbum, gulzaar dwaara rachit or aar.di.varman dwaara sangitabaddh jari kiya gaya. aashaa ji ustad ali akbar khan se 1995 main vishesh rup se mili, baad main aashaa ji ne ustad ali akbar khan ke saath gyaarah bandise ricard kii ’califirnia me’. jo gremi award ke liye namankit hua. 1990 main aar.di.varman dwaara sangitabaddh gito ko aashaa ji ne ‘remix’ kar prayogtmak anubhav kiya jo kai logon dwaara aalochana kaa vishay bana. sangeetkaar khayyam ne bhi isaki aalochana kii. kintu ‘rahul or mai’ naamak elbum kaaphi prasiddh rahi. indopop elbum ‘jaanam samajha karo’ lesley louis ke saath kaaphi prasiddh rahaa. kai puraskaro sahit 1997 em.tii.ve. eward is khaas elbum ko mila. 2002 i. main apni elbum ‘aap kii aashaa’ jo aath geeton vaala video elbum tha, khud aashaa ji ne sangeet taiyaar kiya. iske geet mujruh sultanpuri dwaara rachit tha. 21 may 2001 ko sachin tendulkar dwaara mumbai main ek khaas party main yah elbum jari kiya gaya.
 jab pakistani gayak adnan sami sirf 10 varsh ke the tab aashaa ji ne unki pratibha ko pahachaanaa. aashaa ji ne adnan sami ko gayan pratibha viksit kar aage badhane ke liye prerit kiya. jab adnan bade hue tab aashaa ji ke saath’kabhi to najar milaao’ elbum main geet gaae jo kaaphi prasiddh rahaa. phir ‘barase badal’ naamak elbum main bhi adnan sami ke saath gaai. aashaa ji ne kai elbamon ke liye gazal gaai yatha-miraj-e-gazal, abshahar-e-gazal or kasheesh. 2005 main aashaa ji ne elbum ‘aashaa’ jo chaar gazal gayako ko samarpit tha- menhadi hasan, gulam ali, farida khanam or jagajeet singh jari kiya. is elbum main aashaa ji ke aath pasandida ghazal yatha- farida khanam kii ‘aaj jane kii jid naa karo..., gulam ali kii ‘chupake chupake...’, ‘aawaargi...’ or ‘dil me ek lahar...’ jagajeet sih kii ‘aahista aahista...’, mehadi hasan kii ‘ranjish he sahi...’,’raftaa raftaa...’ or ‘mujhe tum najar se...’ shaamil tha. iske sangeetkaar pandit somesh maathur the jinhone yuvaao ke dhyaan main rakhte hue sangeet die. aashaaji ke 60wein janmdiwas par i.em.ai. india ne teen casette jari kiye- ‘baba mai bairaagan hongi ’, ‘da golden kalakson-gazal ’ or ‘da golden collection- da evar bharsatile aashaa bhosle’ jo 44 prasiddh gito kaa sangrah tha. 2006 main aashaa ji ne ‘aashaa and frands’ naamak elbum ricard kiya jiname yugal geet sanjay dutt or urmila matondkar ke saath gaae saath hi prasiddh cricketer brett lee ke saath aashaa ji ne ‘you aar da van for me ’ geet gaae. in gito ke sangeetkaar samir tandon the. iskaa video nirmaan es. ramachandran ne kiya .
