 kaali sindh nadi kaa udgam madhyapradesh ke devas jila ke bagali gaanv ke sameep vindhyachal se hua hai. yah chambal nadi kii sahaayak nadi hain. shajapur or narsinhgarh jilo main pravaahit hote hue rajasthan main pravesh karti hain. yahan jhalawad tathaa kota jilon main bahati hui nauneraa naamak sthaan par chambal nadi main mil jaati hain. nadi kii kul lambaai 150 kilometre hain. iske kinare basaa pramukh nagar devas hain.
