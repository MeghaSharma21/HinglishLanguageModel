 sikh dharmapar ek shrenee kaa bhaag
 savaayiye mahale tije kebhale amardaas gun tere,teri upama tohi bani aavai. 1. prishth 1396
 guru amar das ji sikh panth ke ek mahaan prachaarak the. jinhone guru nanak ji maharaj ke jivan darshan ko va unke dwaara sthapit dharmik vicharadhara ko aage badhaaya. ÷tritiy nanak' guru amar das ji kaa janm basrke gilaan jo ki amritsar main sthit he, main vaisaakh sudi 14veen, samvat 1536 ko hua tha. unke pita tej bhaan bhalla ji evam mata bakht cor ji ek sanatani hindu the or har varsh ganga ji ke darshan ke liye haridwar jaaya karte the. guru amar das ji kaa vivaah mata mansaa devi ji se hua tha. unki chaar santaanen thi. jinamein do putriyaan- bibi dani ji evam bibi bhaani ji thi. bibi bhaani ji kaa vivaah guru ramdaas sahib ji se hua tha. unke do putra- mohan ji evam mohari ji the.
 ek baar guru amardaas sahib ji ne bibi amaro ji se guru nanak sahib ke shabad sune. ve isse itane prabhavit hue ki guru angad sahib se milane ke liye khadur sahib gayi. guru angaddev sahib ji kii shiksha ke prabhaav main aakar guru amar das sahib ne unhen apana guru bana liya or wo khadur sahib main hi rahane lage. ve nity subah jaldi uth jate va guru angad dev ji ke snaan ke liye beas nadi se jal laate. or guru ke lungar liye jangal se lakadiyaan kaat kar laate.
 guru angad sahib ne guru amardaas sahib ko ÷tritiy nanak' ke rup main march 1552 ko 73 varsh kii aayu main sthapit kiya. guru amardaas sahib ji kii bhakthi evam seva guru angad sahib ji kii shiksha kaa parinaam tha. guru sahib ne apane kaaryon kaa kendra ek nae sthaan goindwal ko banaaya. yahi baad main ek bade shahar ke rup main prasiddh hua. yahaan unhone sangat kii rachana kii or bade hi suniyojit dhang se sikh vichaardhaara kaa prasar kiya. unhone yahaan sikh sangat ko prachaar ke liye susangathit kiya. vichaar ke prasar ke liye 22 dharmik kendron- ÷manjiyaan' kii sthapana kii. har ek kendra yaa manji par ek prachaarak prabharee ko niyukt kiya. jisamen ek rahatvan sikh apane dayitvon ke anusaar dharm kaa prachaar karata tha. guru amardaas ji ne swayan evam sikh prachaarkon ko bharat ke vibhinn bhaagon main bhejkar sikh panth kaa prachaar kiya.
 unhone ÷guru kaa lungar' kii pratha ko sthapit kiya or har shraddhalu ke liye ÷÷pehle pangat phir sangat ko anivaary banaaya. ek baar akbar guru sahib ko dekhne aaye to unhen bhi mote anaaj se bana lungar chak kar hi phir guru sahib kaa saakshaatkaar karne kaa mauka mila.
 guru amardaas ji ne satipratha kaa prabal virodh kiya. unhone vidhwaa vivaah ko badhaava diya or mahilaon ko pardaa pratha tyaagane ke liye kaha. unhone janm, mrityu evam vivaah utsavon ke liye saamaajik rup se prasangik jivan darshan ko samaaj ke samaksh rakhaa. is prakaar unhone saamaajik dharaatal par ek rashtravaadi va aadhyatmik aandolan ko ukera. is vichaardhaara ke saamne muslim kattapanthiyon kaa jehadi falsafa tha. unhen in tatwon se virodh bhi jhelana padaa. unhone sangat ke liye teen prakaar ke saamaajik va saanskrithik milan samaaroh kii sanrachana kii. ye teen samaaroh the-diwaali, vaisaakhi evam maaghi.
 guru amardaas sahib ji ne goindwal sahib main bauli kaa nirmaan kiya jisamen 84 sidhiyaan thi evam sikh itihaas main pehali baar goindwal sahib ko sikh shraddhaalu kendra banaaya. unhone guru nanak sahib evam guru angad sahib ji ke shabdon ko surakshit rup main sangrakshit kiya. unhone 869 shabdon kii rachana kii. unki baani main anand sahib jaisi rachana bhi he. guru arajan dev sahib ne in sabhi shabdon ko guru granth sahib main ankit kiya.
 guru sahib ne apane kisi bhi putra ko sikh guru banane ke liye yogya nahin samajha, isliye unhone apane damaad guru ramdaas sahib ko gurupad pradaan kiya. yah ek prayog dharmi nirnay tha. bibi bhaani ji evam guru ramdaas sahib main sikh siddhanton ko samajhane evam seva kii sachhee shraddha thi or wo iske purnatah kaabil the. yah pratha yah bataati he ki gurupad kisi ko bhi diya ja saktaa he. guru amardaas sahib ko dehanth 95 varsh kii aayu main bhadon sudi 14 samvat 1631 ko guru ramdaas sahib ji ko gurupad saunpane ke pashchaat guindwal sahib, jo ki amritsar ke nikat he, main hua tha.
