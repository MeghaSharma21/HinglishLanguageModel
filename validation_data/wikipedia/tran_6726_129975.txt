 hotel aakarshak dhang se banaaya gaya he evam yah sthaan golf course kii ore jhaank rahaa he. nivas kaksh main sundar kalatmak vastuen rakhi gai hai. hotel ke chaaron ore goa evam bharat kii pracheen samskruti kii pratimaaen hotel kii gailaree main susajjit hai. yah baahar se aane vaale videshi paryatakon ko na keval ek aanandamay nivas sthaan balki bharat ke aitihaasik samskruti se parichit karaata he.
 aadhunik upkarnon kii suvidhaon se paripurna is hotel main manoranjan kii sabhi suvidhaayen uplabdh hai. parivaar ke saath yadi goa kii saiyr karne yahan par aae hai. khaane ke liye paripurna evam vyaayaam ke liye upyukt sthaan he. rosemara kii zidagi main, thakaan dur karne ke liye yahan uplabdh sadhano kaa prayog kar sakate he. is hotel main vyapaarik sammelan bhi yojit kiye jate hai.
