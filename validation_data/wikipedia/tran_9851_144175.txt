 baba aunghadnath kaa mandir bharat ke uttar pradesh rajya ke meerut mahanagar main chhavnee kshetra main sthit he. yah mandir siddh kshetra kehalaata he.
 yah manyata he, ki is mandir main shivlinga swayambhu he. yani yah shivlinga swayan prithvi se baahar niklaa he. tabhi yah sadhy faldaata he. bhakton kii manokamanaayen aunghaddani shiv swaroop main puri karne ke saath saath hi shanti sandesh dete hai. iske saath hi nataraj swaroop main kranti ko bhi prakashit karte hai.
 chunki yahaan bhagwaan shiv swaroop main vidyaman hai, is kaaran ise aunghadnath mandir kaha jaataa he. vaise yah kaali palton vaala mandir bhi kehalaata he. 	
 is mandir kii sthapana kaa koi nishchit samay gyaat nahin he, lekin janashruti ke anusaar yah pratham bhartiya swatantraaa sangram se bhi pehle se shahar or aasapaas ke logon ke bich vandaneey shraddhasthal ke rup main vidyaman tha. vir marathaon ke itihaas main anekon peshwaon kii vijay yatraaon kaa ullekh miltaa he, jinhone yahan bhagwaan shankar kii puja archana kii thi. 
 1857 kaa pratham bhartiya swatantraaa sangram main is mandir kaa vishesh yogadan rahaa he.
 san 1944 tak sena ke prashikshan kendra se sata yah ek chote se shiv mandir evam kune ke rup main vidyaman tha. baad main badhate-badhate san 2 oktober 1968 main brahmaleen jyotishpeethadhishwar jagadguru shankaracharya krishnabodhashram ji maharaj ne naveen mandir kaa shilanyas kiya.
 aaj yah mandir kaanvadiyon kii shraddha kaa kendra ban gaya he. lakhon kaanvadiye mandir pahunch kar baba bholenath kaa jalabhishek karte hai, va swayan ko dhanya maanate hai. 
 yah mandir shwet sangemarmar se nirmit he, jis par yatha sthaan utkrisht nakkashi bhi kii gai he. bich main mukhya mandir baba bholenath evam maan parvati ko samarpit he, jiskaa shikhar atyant unchaa he. us shikhar ke upar kalash sthapit he. mandir ke garbh grih main bhagwaan evam bhagwati kii saumy adamkad murtiyan sthapit hai va bich main neeche shiv parivaar siddh shivlinga ke saath vidyaman he. iske uttari dwaar ke baahar hi inke vaahan nandi bail kii ek vishaal murti sthapit he. garbh grih ke bheetar mandap evam chat par bhi kaanch kaa atyant hi sundar kaam kiya hua he.
 is mandir ke uttari ore hi doosraa mandir radha-krishna ko samarpit he, jiskaa shikhar bhi mukhya mandir ke samaan hi unchaa he. is mandir kii parikrama main kai sundar chitra bane hue hai. garbh grih main radha krishna kii bahut hi aakarshak evam bhavy murtiyan ek sundar rajat mandap main sthapit hai.garbh-grih ke baahar ek bada mandap nirmit he.
 mukhya mandir ke dakshin main satsang bhawan bana hua he.
