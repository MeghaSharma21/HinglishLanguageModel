 govind ballab pant krishi evam praudyogiki vishvavidhyalay bharat kaa pehla krishi vishvavidhyalay he. iskaa udghaatan jawaharlal nehru dwaara 17 november, 1960 ko uttar pradesh krishi vishvavidhyalay ke naam se kiya gaya tha. varsh 1972 main iskaa naam mahaan swatantrata senaanee govind ballab pant ke naam par govind ballab pant krishi evam praudyogiki vishvavidhyalay kar diya gaya. yah vishvavidhyalay pantnagar naamak parisar-kasbe main padataa he jo uttrakhand ke udhamsinghnagar jile main he. yah vishvavidhyalay bharat main harith kranti kaa agradoot mana jaataa he.
