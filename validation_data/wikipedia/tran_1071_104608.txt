 vitt pariyojna, aise buniyadi dhaanche or audyogic pariyojnaon kaa deergh kaaleenn vittaposhan he jo praayojakon kii balance-shit ke sthaan par pariyojnaon ke anumaanit nakadi pravaah par aadhaarit hota he. aamtaur par, ek pariyojna kii vittaposhan sanrachana anek equity niveshkon ko samahit karti he, jo bainkon ke sindicket kii tarah pariyojna ko rin pradaan karte hai, praayojak kehalaate hai. rin, sabase adhik saamanytah pratibhuti-simit rin hai, jo pariyojna kii parisampattiyon ke dwaara surakshit rahete hai or praayojakon kii saamaanya parisampattiyon yaa rin paatrata ke sthaan par pariyojna ke nakadi pravaah dwaara pradutt kiya jaataa he, yah vittiya modelling dwaara samarthit bhaag main ek nirnay he. vittaposhan, rajasav utpaadan theke sahit pariyojna kii samast parisampattiyon dwaara vishisht rup se surakshit he. pariyojna udhaardaataaon ko in sabhi parisampattiyon par ek grahanadhikaar diya gaya he or agar pariyojna company ko pariyojna kii rin sharton ke anupaalan main kathinaaiyaan he to, ve pariyojna kaa niyantran samaj sakate hai.
 praayah prayek pariyojna main vishesh uddeshy se ek ikai banaayi jaati he jiske dwaara pariyojna ke praayojakon ke swamitvvaali anya parisampattiyon ko is pariyojna ke nishphal hone par honevaale kshatikaarak prabhaavon se surakshit kiya jaataa he. ek vishesh prayojan ikai ke rup main, pariyojna company pariyojna ke atirikt anya koi parisampattiyaan nahin rakhti he. kabhi kabhi pariyojna company ke maalikon dwaara puunji yogadan kii patibaddhta, pariyojna kii aarthik sudrdhta sunishchit karne ke liye aavashyak hoti he. pariyojna vitt aksar vaikalpik vittaposhan tareekon kii tulana main adhik jatil hote he. paramparaagat rup se, pariyojna vittaposhan khanan, parivahan, doorsanchaar or saarvajanik upyogita ke udyogon main sabase adhik istemaal kiya gaya he. abhi haal hi main, vishesh rup se europe main, pariyojna ke vittaposhan ke siddhanton ko nijee saarvajanik bhagidaari ke tahat buniyadi dhaanche yaa, britain main, nijee vitt pahal lenden ke liye lagu kiya gaya he.
 jokhim kii pahchaan or niyatan vitt pariyojna kaa ek mahatvapurn ghatak he. ek pariyojna vikasshil deshon or ubharate bazaaron main anek takneekee, paryaavaran, aarthik or raajnneetik jokhimon kii vishay vastu ho sakati he. vittiya santhaayen or pariyojna ke praayojak yah nirnay kar sakate hai ki pariyojna ke vikaas or sanchaalan main nihit jokhim asweekaarya hai . in jokhimon kaa saamana karne ke liye, in udyogon main pariyojna ke praayojak praayah kaary ko anekon visheshgy companiyon dwaara purna karte hai jo ek duusare ke saath ek anubandh network main sakriy hai or vittaposhan kii anumati ke maarg main jokhim ko niyat karti hai. karyanvayan ke vibhinn pattern kabhi kabhi "pariyojna vitaran vidhi" ke rup main sanderbhit kiye jate he. in pariyojnaon ke vittaposhan kai dalon ke bich vitarit kiya janaa chahie, taaki pariyojna ke saath jude jokhim or saath hi prayek party ke liye nihit laabh sunishchit kiye ja saken.
 ek adhik jokhim bhari yaa adhik mahangi pariyojna ke liye praayojakon se pratibhu dwaara surakshit simit sahaayta vittaposhan kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. ek jatil vitt pariyojna sanrachana main aniyat jokhim ko kam karne ke liye korporate vitt, pratibhutikaran, vikalpon, beemaa praavdhaanon yaa anya prakaar ke samparshvik vruddhi ko shaamil kar sakate hai.
 vitt pariyojna samudri vitt or vimaan vitt ke saath kai visheshtaain baantati he tathaapi, baad ke do adhik vishisht kshetra hai.
 isiemai kol compni koyala aayaat karti he. enrgen corporation upabhoktaaon ko urja kii aapurti karti he. donon companiyaan unke sambandhit lakshyon ko puraa karne ke liye ek bijli sanyantr ke nirmaan ke liye sahamat hai. aamtaur par, pehla kadam dono dalon ke iraadon ko samajhane ke liye ek samjhauta gyaapan par hastakshar karnaa hoga. yah ek sanyukt udhyam banane hetu ek qaraar dwaara anusarit kiya jaayegaa.
 isiemai kol or enrgen ek espisi banaate hai ise power holdings corporation kehete he or unke yogadan ke anusaar un donon ke bich share vibhajit karte hai. isiemai kol, adhik sthapit hone ke kaaran adhik puunji kaa yogadan karti he or sheyaron ke 70% leti he. enrgen ek choti company he or shesh 30% letaa he. nai company kii koi sampatti nahin he.
 power holdings urja sanyantr ke nirmaan ke liye isiemai nirmaan ke saath ek anubandh hastakshrit karata he. isiemai nirmaan isiemai kol kaa ek sahabhaagi he or keval ekmaatr company he jise isiemai vitaran vinirdesh ke anusaar ek bijli sanyantr kaa nirmaan karne kaa tajarba he.
 ek bijli sanyantr ke liye saikadon karodon dollar kaa kharch aa saktaa he. isiemai nirmaan kaa bhugtan karne ke liye, power holdings ek vikaas bank or ek vanijyik bank se vitt poshan praapt karata he. ye bank asiemai nirmaan ke financer ke liye ek guarantee pradaan karte hai ki company nirmaan puraa karne ke liye bhugtan karne main sakasham he. aam taur par nirmaan ke liye bhugtan is prakaar kiya hai jaise: saamne ke nirmaan par 10% bhugtan, madhya raste ke nirmaan par 10%, 10% kaam puraa hone se kuch pehle or 70% power holdings ko swatvadhikaar hastantaran par, jo urja sanyantr ke maalik hogi.
 isiemai kol or enrgen, suvidha prabandhan ke liye, ek anya espisi bijli prabandhan incorporation banaate hai. dono espisiyon ke antim uddeshy, mukhya rup se isiemai kol or enrgen ko surakshit rakhana he. agar sanyantr main koi aapadaa hoti he, to sambhavit abhiyogi isiemai kol yaa enrgen par mukadmaa nahin kar sakate or unki sampatti ko lakshya nahin bana sakate kyonki company sanyantr kaa swaamitv yaa sanchaalan nahin rakhti he.
 ek or bijli prabandhan or asiemai ke bich bikri kharid samjhauta koyala bijli sanyantr ke liye kachche maal kii aapurti karata he. phir ek thok vitaran anubandh dwaara bijli enrgen ko di gayi he. is saude se isiemai kol or enrgen donon kii nakadi pravaah kaa upyog vittposhko ke rin chukaane ke liye kiya jayega.
 upar ek saral vyakhya he jo khanan, shipping or koyala aayaat main shaamil vitaran theke cover nahin karti he or na hi upabhoktaaon ko bijli pahunchane ke liye anubandh shaamil karti he. vikasshil deshon main, ek yaa ek se adhik sarkari sansthaaon ke liye yah asaamaanya nahin he ki wo pariyojna ke praathmik upabhoktaaon, upabhokta aabaadi ke liye "pichley mile" vitaran upkram he. sarkari agenciyon or pariyojna ke bich praasangik kharid samjhauton main ek minimum uthaav guarantee khand shaamil ho saktaa he or isse rajasav kaa ek nishchit str kii guarantee hogi. sadak parivahan sahit anya kshetron main sarkaar sadakon ke liye toll lagu kar sakati he or rajasav sangrah kar sakati, jabaki ek guaranteekrut vaarshik raashi pariyojna ke liye uplabdh karaati he. yah pariyojna niveshkon or udhaardaataaon ke liye yatayaat kii maang ke saath jude jokhim ko samaapt yaa kam se kam karne kaa kaary karata he.
 ek pariyojna ke alpsankhyak maalik "band-tulanpatr" vittaposhan ke upyogke liye ichha kar sakate hai, jisame ve pariyojna main nivesh ke rup main apni bhagidaari kaa khulaasa karte hai or niveshjo se sambandhit ek footnot ke rup prakat karke rin ko vittiya bayanon main shaamil nahin karte he. sanyukt rajya america main yah paatrata vittiya lekhankan maanak board dwaara nirdhaarit kii jaati. vikasshil deshon main kai pariyojnayen yuddh jokhim beemaa se cover kii jaani chahiye, jo yuddh sambandhi hamale, parityakt khaanon or torpados or asainya ashaanti ke krityon ko bhi cover karen jo kii aam taur par "maanak" beemaa nitiyon main shaamil nahin hai. aaj, kuch badali nitiyon ko jinamein aatankwaad shaamil he aatankwaad beemaa yaa raajnneetik jokhim beemaa kaha jaataa he. kai maamalon main, ek baahar kii beemaa company ke thekedaar dwaara pariyojna samay par puraa hone kii guarantee ke liye pradarshan bandha jari karegaa.
 saarvajanik rup se vitt poshit pariyojnayen atirikt vittaposhan tareekon jaise kar vetan vruddhi vitt poshan yaa nijee vitt pahal kaa bhi upyog kar saktaa he. aisi pariyojnaain aksar puunji sudhaar yojana kii dwaara shasit hoti hai jo ek nishchit lekhapariksha kshamtaaon ko jodatii he or prakriya ko pratibandhit karti hai.
 simit pratibhuti udhaar vitt pracheen greece or rome main samudri yatraaon ke liye istemaal main tha. iskaa upyog panama nahar ke vikaas main buniyadi dhaancha pariyojnaon kii thidhi ke liye hota tha or 20 veen sadi ke dauraan bade paimaane par america main tel gas udyog main failaa tha. haalanki, 1970 ke dashak or 1980 ke dashak main uttari sagar ke tel kshetron ke vikaas ke saath ucch jokhimavali buniyadi yojanaon ke liye vitt pariyojna kaa udbhav hua. is tarah ke niveshon ke liye prayek pariyojna ke liye ekaadhik maalikon or jatil beemaa yojanaon ke vitaran, rin, prabandhan or pariyojna sanchaalan ke saath nae vishesh prayojan nigam banaaye the. pehle aisi pariyojnaon ko upyogita yaa sarkari band nirgamon, yaa anya paaramparik korporate vitt sanrachanaon ke madhyam se puraa kiya gaya tha.
 vikasshil duniya main pariyojna vittaposhan asian vittiya sankat ke samay shikhar par tha, lekin baad main audyogic deshon main aarthik giravat ke kaaran oisidi deshon main vikaas kaa prarambh hua, jiske ke kaaran vittaposhan duniya bhar main pariyojna ke liye 2000 ke aasapaas shikhar par tha. duniya bhar main pariyojna ke vittaposhan ke liye kii ucch jarurat bani rahati he jaise ki or adhik deshon main saarvajanik upyogitaaon or buniyadi suvidhaon kii badhati aapurti kii aavashyaktaa he. haal ke varshon main, vitt pariyojna yojanaain madhya purv main teji se aam ban gayin hai, kuch log islamic vitt ko bhi shaamil kar rahe hai.
 nai pariyojna kii vitt sanrachanaain mukhya rup se suvidhaon or sarkari sansthaaon se uplabdh bijli kharidne ke lambey anubandh dwaara uplabdh avasaron ke jawaab main ubhar kar aati hai. in dirghakaalik rajasav dharaain PURPA saarvajanik upyogitaayen nitiyaan niamak adhinium 1978 ke niyamon ko lagu karne ke liye aavashyak the. mul rup se ek urja pahal kii kalpana kii gayi jo gharelu navikarniya sansadhano ko protsaahit or sangrakshit karne ke liye dizine kii gayi, adhinium or udyog ne bijli utpaadan main dheel ko badhaava diya or mahatvapurn rup se antarrashtriya nijeekaran ne baad main saarvajanik upyogitaayen holding company adhinium main 1994 ke sanshodhan karaaye. sanrachana viksit ho chuki he or duniya bhar main urja or anya pariyojnaon ke liye aadhaar banaati he.
