 chakravarti rajagopalaachari
 mahatma gandhi evam chakravarti rajagopalaachari 
 chakravarti rajagopalaachari, rajaji naam se bhi jane jate hai. ve vakil, lekhak, raajnneetijna or darshanik the. ve swatantra bharat ke dwitiya governor general or pratham bhartiya governor general the. 10 april 1952 se 13 april 1954 tak ve madras praant ke mukhyamantri rahe. ve dakshin bharat ke congress ke pramukh netaa the, kintu baad main ve congress ke prakhar virdohi ban gaye tathaa swatantra party kii sthapana kii. ve gandhiji ke samadhi the. unhone dakshin bharat main hindi ke prachaar-prasar ke liye bahut kaary kiya.
 unka janm dakshin bharat ke salem jile main thorapalli naamak gaanv main hua tha. rajaji tatkaaleen salem janapad ke thorapalli naamak ek chote se gaanv main ek tamil brahman parivaar main janme the. aajkal thorapali krishnagiri janapad main he. unki aarmabhik shiksha hosur main hui. kalege kii shiksha madras evam bangaloru main hui.
 san 1937 main hue kaansilo ke chunaavon main chakravarti ke netritv main congress ne madras praant main vijay praapt kii. unhen madras kaa mukhyamantri banaaya gaya. 1939 main british sarkaar or congress ke bich matbhed ke chalate congress kii sabhi sarkarein bhang kar di gayi thim. chakravarti ne bhi apane pad se tyaagptr de diya. isi samay duusare vishwa yuddh kaa armbh hua, congress or chakravarti ke bich pun: than gayi. is baar vah gandhi ji ke bhi virodh main khadey the. gandhi ji kaa vichaar tha ki british sarkaar ko is yuddh main maatr naitik samarthan diya jaae, vahiin raja ji kaa kahana tha ki bharat ko purna swatantraaa dene kii shart par british sarkaar ko har prakaar kaa sahyog diya jaae. yah matbhed itane bad gayi ki raja ji ne congress kii kaaryakarini kii sadsyta se tyaagptr de diya. iske baad 1942 main 'bharat chodo' aandolan prarambh hua, tab bhi vah anya congressi netaaon ke saath giraftaar hokar jail nahin gayi. is kaa arth yah nahin ki vah desh ke swatantraaa sangram yaa congress se vimukh ho gayi the. apane siddhanton or kaaryastylee ke anusaar vah in donon se nirantar jude rahe. unki raajnneeti par gahari pakad thi. 1942 ke elahabaad congress adhiveshan main unhone desh ke vibhaajan ko spasht sahmati pradaan kii. yadhyapi apane is mat par unhen aam janata or congress kaa bahut virodh sahanaa padaa, kintu unhone isaki parvaah nahin kii. itihaas gavaah he ki 1942 main unhone desh ke vibhaajan ko sabhi ke virodh ke baad bhi sweekaar kiya, san 1947 main vahi hua. yahi kaaran he ki congress ke sabhi netaa unki doordarshita or budhimatta kaa loha maanate rahe. congress se alag hone par bhi yah mahasoos nahin kiya gaya ki vah usase alag hai.
 1946 main desh kii antarim sarkaar bani. unhen kendra sarkaar main udyog mantri banaaya gaya. 1947 main desh ke purna swatantra hone par unhen bengal kaa rajyapal niyukt kiya gaya. iske agle hi varsh vah swatantra bharat ke pratham 'governor general' jaise ati mahatvapurn pad par niyukt kiye gayi. san 1950 main ve pun: kendriya mantrimandal main le liye gayi. isi varsh sardaar vallabh bhai patel kii mrityu hone par ve kendriya grih mantri banaaye gayi. san 1952 ke aam chunaavon main vah loksabhaa sadsy bane or madras ke mukhyamantri nirvaachit hue. iske kuch varshon ke baad hi congress kii tatkaaleen nitiyon ke virodh main unhone mukhyamantri pad or congress donon ko hi chhod diya or apni pruthak swatantra party kii sthapana kii.
 1954 main bhartiya raajnneeti ke chanakya kahe jane vaale raja ji ko bharat ratna se sammanit kiya gaya. bharat ratna paane vaale ve pehle vyakti the. vah vidwaan or adbhut lekhan pratibha ke dhani the. jo gaharaai or tikhaapan unke buddhichaaturya main tha, vahi unki lekhnee main bhi tha. vah tamil or angrezi ke bahut achche lekhak the. 'gita' or 'upanishdon' par unki tikaaen prasiddh hai. inke dwaara rachit chakravarti thirumagan, jo gadhy main ramayana katha he, ke liye unhen san 1958 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. unki likhi anek kahaniyaan ucch stareey thim. 'swarajya' naamak patr unke lekh nirantar prakashit hote rahete the. iske atirikt nashaabandi or swadeshi vastuon visheshkar khaadi ke prachaar prasar main unka yogadan mahatvapurn mana jaataa he.
 apni veshbhusha se bhi bhartiyata ke darshan karaane vaale is mahapurush kaa 28 disambar 1972 ko nidhan ho gaya.
