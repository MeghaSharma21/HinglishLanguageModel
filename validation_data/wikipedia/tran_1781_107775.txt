 basmati bharat kii lambe chaaval kii ek utkrisht kism he. iskaa vaigyanik naam he orayza satiwa. yah apane khaas swaad or mohak khushboo ke liye prasiddh he. iskaa naam basmati arthath khushboo vaali kism hota he. iskaa doosraa arth komal yaa mulaayam chaaval bhi hota he. bharat is kism kaa sabase bada utpaadak he, jiske baad pakistan, nepal or bangladesh aate hai. paaramparik basmati paudhe lambe or patale hote hai. inkaa tana tej hawaaen bhi sah nahin saktaa he. inamen apekshaakrit kam, parantu ucch shrenee kii paidaavaar hoti he. yah anterrashtriya or bhartiya donon hi bazaaron main unche daamon par bikta he. basmati ke dane anya daanon se kaaphi lambe hote hai. pakane ke baad, ye aapas main lesdaar hokar chipakate nahin, balki bikhare hue rahete hai. yah chaaval do prakaar kaa hota he :- shwet or bhura. canadian madhumeh sangh ke anusaar, basmati chaaval main madhyam glysemic suchakank 56 se 69 ke bich hota he, jo ki ise madhumeh rogiyon ke liye anya anaajon or shwet aate kii apeksha adhik shreyaskar banaata he.
 basmati chaaval kaa ek khaas pandan  kaa swaad hota he. yah flavor rasayan 2-acetile-1-pyroline ke kaaran hota he. 
 basmati kii anek kismein hoti hai. puraani kismon main – basmati-370, basmati-385 or basmati-ranbirsinhpura, evam anya sankar kismon main pus basmati 1, aati he. khushboodaar kismein basmati stock se hi vyutpann kii jaati hai, parantu unhen shuddh basmati nahin mana jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye pibi2, pibi-3 evam aar.ech-10. nai dilli sthit bhartiya krishi vigyaan anusandhan kendra, pusa santhaan, ne ek paramparaagat basmati kii kism ko shodhit kar ek sankar kism banaai, jisamen basmati ke sabhi achche gun hai or saath hi yah paudha bahut chota bhi he. is basmati ko pusa basmati-1 kaha gaya. pi.bi.-1 kii upaj/paidaavaar anya paramparaagat kismon se apekshaakrit duguni hoti he. kaala shah kaku, main sthit pakistan ke chaaval anusandhan santhaan basmati kii kai kismein viksit karne main karyrat rahaa he. isaki ek utkrisht kism he super basmati, jo ki domjid naamak ek vaigyanik ne 1996 main viksit kii thi. yaheen viksit hua vishwa kaa sabase lambaa chaaval dana, jise pakistan karnail basmati kaha gaya. isaki ausat lambaai kaccha: 9.1 mi.mi. or pakaa hua: 18.3 mi.mi. hoti he.
 iske saath anya anumodith kismon main kasturi, basmati 198, basmati 217, basmati 370, basmati 385, basmati 386, karnail, bihar, dehradun, haryana, kasturi, maahi sugandh, punjab, pusa, ranbir, tarori hai. kuch gaiyr-paramparaagat khushboodaar sankar kismein bhi hoti hai, jinamein basmati lakshan hai.
 basmati chaaval vishvabhar main atyadhik lambey dane or khushboo ke liye prasiddh he. yah na keval rashtriya, varan anterrashtriya bazaaron main bhi atyadhik maang main he, jo ki arabon daalaron kaa niryaat uplabdh karaata he. vishuddh basmati kheti keval bharat ke gangiy samtal kshetron or pakistan ke kuch kshetron main hi hoti he. iske liye upabhoktaaon kii maang, yahaan ucch str kii videshi mudra or kai kshetron main kar-mukti bhi dilaati he.kritrim kismein manhagi kheti maangatee hai or phir bhi unke gun, shuddh kismon kaa muqabla naa kar paane ke kaaran kam keemat laatin hai. saath hi chaaval bazaaron main bina khushboo kii kismon kii nimna shrenee kii kai kismein pehle hi upasthit hai. shuddh basmati or kritrim kismon ko pahchaanane main mushkil aane ke kaaran hi nakli kismon kaa dhokhadhadi kaa bazaar bhi khub chalata he. upabhoktaaon ko isse bachaane hetu, ek policrez chen pratikriya aadhaarit aisey ashuddh kismon or milavati kismon ko pahchaanane main madad karata he. isaki pahchaan kshamta 1% ±1.5% he. chaaval ke niryaatak or aaytak is par aadhaarit shuddhta pramaan patr prayog karte hai. is protocol par aadhaarit, di.en.e. angulachinh or nidaan shaastra kendra, lab-india, dwaara viksit kits prasaarit kii gayin. ye kits basmati main ashuddhikaran maapan karatin thim. 
 sitambar 1997 main, ristac naamak ek texas kii company ne basmati lines or daanon kaa petent jeet liya . is petent se basmati or samaan chawlon kii shodhan prakriya aadi par unka ekadhikaar ho gaya tha. ristac naamak likhtenshtin kii ek company ne anterrashtriya str par virodh kaa saamana kiya, jisase sanyukt rajya or bharat ke bich ek laghu kaaleey rajanyik sankat ubhara tha. is sandarbh main bharat ne is maamle ko trip samjhaute ko todane ke aarope main, dablyoo.tii.o. tak pahunchane kaa sankalp kiya, jisase san.rajya ko kaafii pareshaaniyon kaa saamana karnaa padaa. baad main kuch swechhapurvak, va kuch san.rajya patent karyaalay ke is daawey ke punarawalokan pashchaat, us company ko apane sabhi daawey chhodane pade. antim faisla bharat ke paksh main hua tha. yah bhartiya kisaanon ke liye ek badi vijay hui, kyonki anyathaa unhen atyadhik aarthik haani jhelni padati. iske baad bharat sarkaar ne basmati chaaval se niryaat kar bhi hataa liya. basmati chaaval par 8000 rupye prati ton niryaat kar tha, jisase uskaa nunatam niryaat mulya 1200 dollar prati ton tha. 21 janvari 2009 ko isaki nunatam keemat ghataakar 1100 dollar prati ton kar di gai he.
