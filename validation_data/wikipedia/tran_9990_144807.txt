 roosi bhashaa bhaaropee parivaar kii slav shakha ke purvi slav varg kii bhashaa he. roosi bhashaa kaa itihaas do bhaagon main banta hua tha- 'pragaitihaasik' or 'aitihaasik' . gyarahavin shatabdi ke purvi slav bhashaa kaa pehla likhit sahitya prastut hua.
 roosi sahitya kaa itihaas san 988 se shuru hota he. pehle kaal khand kaa naam pracheen roosi sahitya he. is kaal kaa puraa sahitya dharm-nishth or bible se sambandhit tha. lekhak isai, dharm, bhagwaan or aadmi ke rishton ke bare main likhte the. is kaal kii mukhya vidya itivrutt thi. sab sahitya math main jamaa tha or sirf mathvaasi likhte the. is prakaar se roosi sahitya kii shuruuaat main aadhyatmikta isaki sabase badi visheshta he. is kaal kii sabase prasiddh kriti he- "igore kii regiment ke bare main baat" 
 doosraa kaal main dharmanishth sahitya or duniyaan sahitya alag likhaa gaya. 
 is kaal ke baad roosi sahitya kaa swarna kaal aata he . is kaal ke sabase lokapriy lekhak hai-
 is samay kaa rus "sahitya kaa desh" ho gaya. sahitya saamaajik samasya hal karata tha or lekhak samaaj or raajnneeti main mahatvapurn bhoomika adaa karte the. 
 agar yah kaal gadhy kaa samay tha to iske agle kaal main vishwa sahitya kii sabase acchhee kavitaayen likhi gayin. is kaal ke sabase mahatvapurn rachanaakaar hai-
 is kaal ko roosi sahitya kaa rajat kaal kehete hai. oktober kranti ke baad soviet kaal tha . lekhak do varg main bant gayi. kuch log samyavaadee ho gayi, jaise maxim gorki, bladimir mathkotski, mikhail sholokhov aadi; or kuch logon ne unka bahishkaar kiya, jaise: evan boonin, bladimir nobocove aadi. 
 san 1991 se aadhunik kaal shuru hua. agar soviet kaal main sahitya raajnneetik prabhaav main aa rahaa tha to ab bhuule bisare naam yaad aa rahe hai or saahitiyik parampara punerjeevit ho rahi he.
