 moksh
 dhyaan yoga 
 
 garbhaadhan sanskaar sanathan athwa hindu dharm kii samskruti sanskaron par hi aadhaarit he. hamaare rishi-muniyon ne maanav jivan ko pavitra evam maryaadit banane ke liye sanskaron kaa avishkaar kiya. dharmik hi nahin vaigyanik drushti se bhi in sanskaron kaa hamaare jivan main vishesh mahatv he. bhartiya samskruti kii mahanata main in sanskaron kaa mahati yogadan he.
 pracheen kaal main hamaara prayek kaary sanskaar se armbh hota tha. us samay sanskaron kii sankhya bhi lagbhag chalis thi. jaise-jaise samay badalata gaya tathaa vyastata babbti gai to kuch sanskaar swat: vilupt ho gayi. is prakaar samyaanusaar sanshodhit hokar sanskaron kii sankhya nirdhaarit hoti gai. gautam smriti main chalis prakaar ke sanskaron kaa ullekh he. maharishi angira ne inkaa antarbhaav pachchis sanskaron main kiya. vyas smriti main solah sanskaron kaa varnan hua he. hamaare dharamshastron main bhi mukhya rup se solah sanskaron kii vyakhya kii gai he. inamen pehla garbhaadhan sanskaar or mrityu ke uparaant antyeshti antim sanskaar he. garbhaadhan ke baad punswan, simantonnayan, jatakarm, namakaran ye sabhi sanskaar navjaat kaa daivi jagat se sambandh sthapana ke liye kiye jate hai.daivi jagat se shishu kii pragadhata bade tathaa brahmaji kii sristi se vah acchhee tarah parichit hokar dirghakaal tak dharm or maryaada kii raksha karte hue is lok kaa bhoga kare yahi is sanskaar kaa mukhya uddeshy he.
 
 hamaare shastron main maanya solah sanskaron main garbhaadhan pehla he. grihasth jivan main pravesh ke uparant pratham karttavya ke rup main is sanskaar ko manyata di gai he. garhasthya jivan kaa pramukh uddeshy shreshth santaanotpatti he. uttam santati kii ichha rakhanevaale mata-pita ko garbhaadhan se purv apane tan or man kii pavitrataa ke liye yah sanskaar karnaa chahie. vaidic kaal main yah sanskaar ati mahatvapurn samajha jaataa tha.
