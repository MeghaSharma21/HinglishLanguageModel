 navy nyaay, bhartiya darshan kaa ek sampradaay he jo mithila ke darshanik gangesh upaadhyaay dwaara terahavin shatee main pratipaadit kiya gaya. isamen puraane nyaay darshan ko hi aage badhaayaa gaya he. vachaspati mishra tathaa udayan aadi kaa bhi is darshan ke vikaas main prabhaav he. 
 gangesh upaadhyaay ne sriharsh ke khandankhandkhadyam naamak pustak ke vichaaron ke virodh main apni pustak tatvachintamani kii rachana kii. khandankhandkhadyam main advait veydant kaa samarthan evam nyaay darshan ke katipay siddhanton kaa khandan kiya gaya tha.
