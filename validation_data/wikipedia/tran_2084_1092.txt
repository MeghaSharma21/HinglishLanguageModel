 buddha sthaviravaad ke siddhanton kaa varnan 'abhidharm' ke naam se prasiddh he kintu mahayan ke shoonyavaadee madhyamik vikaas ke saath hi pragnaparmita ko mahatv mila or adhibharm ke sthaan main 'abhismay' shabd kaa vyavhaar, visheshat: maitreyanath ke baad hone laga. maitreyanath ne 'pragnaparmita' shaastra ke aadhaar par 'abhismayalankar' shaastra likhaa jo pragnapaarita athwa nirvana praapt karne ke maarg kaa upadesh detaa he. mahayan main is shaastra kaa atyadhik mahatv hona swaabhaavik tha kyonki us sampradaay ke anusaar pragnaparmita kii sadhana isamen bataai gai he. pragnaparmita shabd kaa prayog nirvana or nirvana kaa maarg in donon arthon main hota he. tadnusaar 'abhismay' ke bhi ye do arth hai. kintu saadhy kii apeksha sadhana, jo saadhy tak le jaati he, saadhakon ke liye vishesh mahatv kii vastu hoti he; atev 'nirvana kii sadhana kaa maarg' arth main hi vishesh rup se 'abhismay' shabd prachlit ho gaya he. 'abhismay' ke naam se prasiddh granthon main sadhanmarg kaa hi vishesh rup se varnan miltaa he.
