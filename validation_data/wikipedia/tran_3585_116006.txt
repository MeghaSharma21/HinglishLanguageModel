 saura varsh or chandra varsh main saamanjasy sthapit karne ke liye har tisare varsh panchangon main ek chandramaada kii vruddhi kar di jaati he. isi ko adhik maas yaa adhimaas yaa malmaas kehete hai.
 saura-varsh kaa maan 365 din, 15 ghadi, 22 pal or 57 vipal hai. jabaki chandravarsh 354 din, 22 ghadi, 1 pal or 23 vipal kaa hota he. is prakaar donon varshamaanon main prativarsh 10 din, 53 ghati, 21 pal kaa antar padataa he. is antar main samanta laane ke liye chandravarsh 12 maason ke sthaan par 13 maas kaa ho jaataa he.
 vastav main yah sthiti swayan hi uttpann ho jaati he, kyonki jis chandramaada main surya-sankranti nahin padati, usee ko "adhik maas" kii sangya de di jaati he tathaa jis chandramaada main do surya sankranti kaa samavesh ho jaay, vah "kshaymas" kehalaata he. kshaymas keval karthik, maarg va paus maason main hota he. jis varsh kshay-maas padataa he, usee varsh adhi-maas bhi avashya padataa he parantu yah sthiti 19 varshon yaa 141 varshon ke pashchaat aati he. jaise vikrami samvath 2020 evam 2039 main kshaymaason kaa aagaman hua tathaa bhavishya main samvath 2058, 2150 main padane kii sambhaavna he.
