 anarjak aasti se taatpary banking va vitt udyog main aise rin se he, jiskaa lautnaa sandigdh ho. angreji main ise en pi e athwa non parfarming asset kaha jaataa he. angreji sanskaran se anuwadswarup or bhi kai naam media main die jate hai - yatha gaiyr-nishpadankaree sampatti aadi.
 bank apane graahakon ko jo rin pradaan karata he, use apane khate main aasti ke rup main darshata he. yadi kisi karanvash yah ashanka ho ki graahak yah rin lautaa nahin paaegaa to ese rinon ko anarjak aasti kaha jaataa he. kisi bhi bank kii aarthik sehat ko maapne ke liye yah ek mahatvapurn paimaana he tathaa isamen vruddhi hona kisi bank kii sehat ke liye chintaa kaa vishay hi hota he. haal hi main bharat ke united bank of india kii pramukh ko apana pad bekaabu ho chuke fanse karje enpie par lagaam lagane main naakaam rahane kii vajah se khona padaa.
 enpie ko niyantran main rakhane ke liye har desh ke niamak maapdand nirdhaarit karte hai jinka anupaalan vittiya sansthaaon ke liye aavashyak hota he.
 2008 ke vaishvik aarthik sankat ke baad anarjak aastiyon ko sheeghra chinhit karne va khaaton main sahi tareeke va imaanadaaree se darshane par jor diya gaya he.
 2013-14 ke aarthik surveykshan main bhartiya banking sactor main enpie main badhotaree par chintaa vyakt kii gai tathaa inhain kam karne ke liye nimnlikhit prayaason par jor diya gaya.
 • bainkon main vasuuli ke liye unke mukhyalay/kshetreey karyaalay/prayek rin vasuuli tribunel main nodel adhikariyon kii niyukti. • bainkon dwaara ghate kii parisampattiyon kii vasuuli par jor or parisampatti punargathan companiyaan sankalp agenton kii niyukti. • rajya str ke bankeron kii samitiyon ko rajya sarkaron ke saath hone vaale maamle sulajhane ke liye sakriy hone ke nirdesh dena. • bainkon main jaankaree saajha karne ke aadhaar par naye rin sweekrut karnaa. • enpie kaa kshetra/gatividhi ke aadhaar par vishleshan karnaa aadi.
