 
 baldaar ganda gande kii ek vilupt jati he jo plystocin yug main europe or asia main faili hui thi. apane puure sharir par ghaney bal hone ke kaaran yah purv himyug ke dauraan bhi bachi rahi. anumaan he ki yah 8,000 isapurv tak siberia main jivit thi. inhen pashaan kaal ke maanvon ne dekha tha or pramanswaroop inke pracheen chitra gufaaon main bane hue milte hai.

 siberia main thand se jame hue shavon or gufaaon kii deevaaron par adimanvon dwaara banaai gai tasveeron se baldaar gandon kaa rup-rang gyaat hota he. sar-se-dum tak yah 10-12 foot lambaa hua karata tha or iskaa vazan 2,700-3,200 kilo hota tha. isaki oonchaai 6.6 foot tak hoti thi. iske sir par do sing hote the or aage vaala zyaada lambaa tha. aage vaala sing 2 foot tak lambaa ho saktaa tha. iske kaan chote, taange moti or jism baari-bharkam hua karata tha. gufaa-kala main kabhi-kabhi aage or peeche kii taangon ke bich gadhe rang kii ek dhari dikhaai jaati he lekin yah gyaat nahin he ki vastav main sabhi baldaar gandon main yah dhari thi ki nahin. bada sharir or ghaney bal hone se yah apane sharir main barfile halaat main bhi garmahat banaae rakh saktaa tha.
 vaigyanikon ko sahi tarah gyaat nahin he ki baldaar gandon ke khatm ho jane kaa kya kaaran tha. ek dharna he ki jaise-jaise aadhunik maanav or niandarthal maanav inkaa shikaar karne lage, dhire-dhire yah vilupt ho gaye. ek anya dharna he ki jab purv himyug samaapt hone laga to mausam garam hua or yah nae vaataavaran kii paristhitiyon main jivit na rah paae. ek teesri dharna yah bhi he ki is jati main ek aisi nai jaanaleva beemaari fail gai jisane puri jati hi tezi se khatm kar daali. vivaad jari he.
