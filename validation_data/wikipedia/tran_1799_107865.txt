 yah saanchaa kisi sangeet se sambandhit aadhaar kii pahchaan karne ke liye istemaal hota he. yah {{asbox}} ko prayog main laata he, jo ek mul saanchaa he or aasaani se aadhaar sandesh saanche banane ke liye prayukt hota he 
 {{sangeet-aadhaar}} tip karne par shuruyaat main likhaa sandesh dikhaai padataa he or yah lekh ko nimnlikhit shreneeshrenee main vargeekrut karata he:
 yah ek aadhaar kaa saanchaa he. in saanchon kaa sankshipt vivaran niche diya gaya he. puri jaankaree ke liye is prishth par jaaen.
 aadhaar lekh ek aisa lekh he jisame chand vaakya hai or jo die vishay kii santoshjanak jaankaree nahin detaa he.
