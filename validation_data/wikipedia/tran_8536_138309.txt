 maulavee khudabaksh khan patna ke prasiddh khudabaksh librari ke sansthaapak the jise istambul saarvajanik granthalay ke baad duniya ke doosraa sabase bade granthalay ke rup main dekha jaataa he. aaj yah rashtriya mahatv kii sanstha maan lee gai he kyonki 1969 main sansad ke ek adhinium dwaara granthalay kaa niyantran bharat sarkaar apane haath main le chuki he. yahan par urdu, farsi or arabi kii hajaaron pandulipiyaan maujuud hai.
 khudabaksh kaa janm 2 agast 1842 ko siwan ke qareeb ukhaai gaanv main hua tha. unke purvaj mughal raja alamgir kii seva main the. unke pita patna main ek prasiddh vakil the par unki kamai kaa bada hissaa kitaaben kareedne main jaataa tha. ve hi patna main khudabaksh ko lekar aaye the. khudabaksh ne 1859 main patna hai skool se bahut achche ankon ke saath metric paas kii. unke pita ne unhen ucch shiksha ke liye kolkata bhej diya. lekin vah nae vaataavaran main khud ko samyojit nahin kar sake or vah aksar swasthya samasyaon se grasit rahe. baad main unhone 1868 main apni kanoon kii shiksha puri kii or patna main abhyaas shuru kar diya. kam samay main hi vah ek prasiddh vakil ban gaye.
 1876 main khudabaksh ke pita kaa dehant ho gaya magar pita ne vasiyat kii ki unka betaa 1700 ke aasapaas pustakon ke sangrah badhaane or ise ek saarvajanik granthalay sthapit karne kaa prayaas kare.
 1877 main ve patna nagar nigam ke upaadhyaksh ban gaye. unhen 1891 main unhen 'khan bahadur' ke khitaab se sammanit kiya gaya. shiksha or sahitya ke kshetra main unke yogadan ke liye 1903 main unhen us samay "siaibi" ke sheershak ke saath sammanit kiya gaya.
 granthalay kaa udghaatan 1891 main bengal ke uparajyapal sar charles iliyat ke dwaara kiya gaya. us samay is pustkaalay main lagbhag 4000 haath se likhi gai pustaken thim.
 1895 main vah hyderabad ke nizaam ke ucch nyaayalay ke mukhya nyaayadhish ke rup main niyukt hue. teen saal ke liye vahaan rahane ke baad, ve phir patna laut aae or patna main abhyaas shuru kar diya. lekin jald hi vah pakshaaghaat se peedit ho gaye or unhone keval granthalay tak hi apni gatividhiyon ko simit kar diya. 3 agast 1908 ko unka nidhan ho gaya.
