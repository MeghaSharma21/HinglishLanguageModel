 poonjeevaad samanyat: us aarthik pranali yaa tantra ko kehete hai jisamen utpaadan ke saadhan par nijee swaamitv hota he. ise kabhi kabhi "vyaktigat swaamitv" ke paryaayavachi ke taur par bhi prayukt kiya jaataa he yadhyapi yahan "vyaktigat" kaa arth kisi ek vyakti se bhi ho saktaa he or vyaktiyon ke samooh se bhi. mote taur par kaha ja saktaa he ki sarkari pranali ke atirikt nijee taur par swaamitv vaale kisi bhi aarthik tantra ko poonjivadi tantra ke naam se janaa ja saktaa he. duusare rup main ye kaha ja saktaa he ki poonjivadi tantra laabh ke liye chalaaya jaataa he, jisamen nivesh, vitaran, aay utpaadan mulya, bazaar mulya ityadi kaa nirdhaaran mukt bazaar main pratispardhaa dwaara nirdhaarit hota he. 
 poonjeewaad ek aarthik paddati he jisamen poonji ke nijee swaamitv, utpaadan ke sadhano par vyaktigat niyantran, swatantra audyogic pratiyogita or upabhokta dravyon ke aniyantrit vitaran kii vyavastha hoti he. poonjeewaad kii kabhi koi nishchit paribhaasha sthir nahin hui; desh, kaal or naitik mulyon ke anusaar iske bhinn-bhinn rup banate rahe hai.
 poonjeewaad kaa itihaas manushya ke itihaas jitna hi purana he; pracheen misr, karthej, rome, babilonia or yunaan main vyaktigat sampatti or ekadhikaar par aadhaarit samaajon kaa itihaas miltaa he. daspratha jaisi nijee swaamitv kii charam sthiti rahi he. madhyakaal main europe main iskaa adhik vikaas hua; 18 veen shatabdi ke pashchaat poonjeewaad ne sansar ke anek bhaagon main samantshahi or vaanijyawad ke beej boe. aadhunik yug ke aarambh main vyapaarik poonjeewaad ne apane vikascram main audyogic poonjeewaad ko janm diya, jise poonjotpaadan poonjeewaad bhi kaha jaataa he.
 poonjivadi aarthik tantra ko europe main sansthagat dhaanche kaa rup solahavin sadi main milanaa armbh hua, haalanki poonjivadi pranali ke pramaan pracheen sabhyataaon main bhi milte hai.
 adam smith ne apni pustak "da velth of neshans" main prakritik aadhaar par aarthik swatantraaa kii baat kahi he, usne poonjeewaad kaa naam nahin liya he. aarthik maamalon main prakritik swatantraaa ko aadhaar maanakar chalane ke sambandh main uskaa vishwaas tha, jaisaa ki anya udaarvaadiyon kaa bhi mat rahaa he, ki yadi aarthik vyapaar ko kisi bhi niyantran se mukt kriyanvit hone diya jaae, to is sthiti main utpaadan-vruddhi apni charam seemaa par pahunch jaaegi, tathaa sarvakalyankari rajya kii sthapana main sahaayta milegi. adam smith kaa yah vyaktigat poonji or swatantra udyog kaa udaaravaadi mat aadhunik poonjeewaad kaa merudand hai.
 18 veen sadi main europe kii audyogic kranti ke saath poonjeewaad ko nayaa bal mila. uske prabhaav se 1770 or 1840 ke madhya aarthik or vyapaarik kshetron main krantikari parivartan hue. purvakaleen sari sabhyataaen shoshit sarvhaara ke shram kii neenv par bani thim. aadhunik sabhyataa maanviya avishkaron or yantric shakti dwaara nirmit hui he. yantric upkarnon kii sahaayta se manushya kii utpaadankshmata main atyadhik vruddhi hui or nijee udyogon main is utpaadan-kshamta-vruddhi ke upyog ne poonjeewaad ke vikaas ko atyadhik bal diya.
 poonjeevaad kaa siddhant sabase pehle audyogic kranti ke falswarup karl marks ke siddhant kii vyakhya ke sandarbh main aayaa. 19 veen sadi main kuch german sinddhantkaron ne is avadharana ko viksit karnaa armbh kiya jo karl marks ke puunji or byaaj ke siddhant se hatakar tha. beesvin sadi ke aarambh main maiks webar ne is avadharana ko ek sakaaratmak dhang se vyakhyit kiya. sheet yuddh ke dauraan poonjeevaad kii avadharana ko lekar bahut bahes chali.
 audyogic kranti ke aarambhik dinon main england main adam smith kaa ahastkshep kaa siddhant vaiyaktik swatantraaa or aniyantrit aarthik vyavastha kaa aadhaar ban gaya. kintu is audyogic parivartan se utpann paristhitiyon ne sarkaar ko udyogpatiyon or shramikon kii samasyaon main hastkshep karne ko baadhya kar diya. inhen dinon samaajwaad shabd kaa prayog pratham baar hua . is sthiti main tathakthith swatantra udyog, 18 veen sadi ke udaarataavaad or aaj ke samaajawaadee niyantran kaa samanvit rup hoga. america poonjeewaad, vyapaar main vyaktigat aadhipaty or rajkeey sanchaalan kaa mishrit rup he. yadhyapi bahukshetreey pratidwandvita udyog kaa mukhya aadhaar he tathaapi pichley varshon vaanijya or shramik sanghathanon main ekadhikarik niyantran kii sthiti drut gati se badi he. sampatti or aay ke avyavasthit vitavaran se logon kaa saamaajik str bahut neeche gaya he. company trustiship or public agency jaise anek sanghathanon ne pratidwandvita ke siddhant ko vikrut kar diya he.
 rashtriyakaran kii yojanaon se niyantran kii maatra main vruddhi hui or swatantra arthvyavastha ko jabardast dhakka laga. prayek raashtra ke sammukh yah samasya rahati he, ki vah apni aarthik paristhitiyon ke anusaar vyavasthaayen kare. vyavastha ke nirnay, uplabdh shramashakti or aarthik sroton par nirbhar karte hai. rajya pratinidhiyon or yojanavidon ke sammukh utpaadan ke rup, maatra or tareeke aadi ke prashn rahete hai. samaajawaadee vyavastha ke antargat iske liye sarkaron or sarkaar dwaara niyukt yojanasmitiyon dwaara nirnay kiye jate hai. poonjeewaadi vyavastha ke antargat ek vyakti yaa samooh ko apane aarthik niyojan kaa swatantra adhikaar rahata he.
