 nayantara sahagal ek bhartiya lekhika hai jo angreji bhashaa main likhti he. inkaa janm 10 may 1927 ko nehru gandhi parivaar main hua tha. ve pehali bheertiya mahila lekhika hai jinhen angreji lekhan ke liye pahchaan mili. ve bharat ke pehle pradhanmantri jawahar laal nehru kii bahan vijay lakshmi pandit kii putree hai. nehru gandhi parivaar kii ek sadsy hone ke baavjood inaki lekhnee hameshaa nirpeksh rahi. philhal kai dashakon se ve dehradun main rah rahi he.
