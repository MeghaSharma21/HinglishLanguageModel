 prashasnic drushti se bharat rajyon yaa praanton main vibhakt he; rajya, janapadon main vibhakt hai, jile tehsil main vibhakt hai. yah vibhaajan or neeche tak gaya he.
 bharat ke sabhi rajyon ko ke bich sahakaari kaaryon main sahabhaagita sunishchit karne ke liye in rajyon ko ek salaahkaar parishad vaale chah anchalon main samoohbaddh kiya gaya he. rajya punargathan adhinium ke tisare bhaag ke antargat 1956 main paanch aanchalik parishadon kii sthapana kii gai thi. purvottar rajyon kii vishesh samasyaon ko dhyaan main rakhte hue uttar purvi parishad adhinium ke antargat 1972 main purvottar anchal kaa gathan kiya gaya. uttar-purvi parishad adhinium dwaara 23 disambar 2002 ko purvottar anchal main sikkim rajya ko bhi shaamil kar diya gaya. andaman or nicobar dweepasmooh or lakshadweep kisi bhi anchal main shaamil nahin hai, haalanki ye dakshini aanchalik parishad ke vishesh aamantriton main hai. vartmaan main prayek kshetreey parishad kii sanrachana nimnanusaar he:
 bharat 29 rajyon or 7 centre shasit pradeshon se milakar bana he. centre shasit pradesh up-rajyapal dwaara sanchalit hote hai, jise bharat ke raashtrapati dwaara niyukt kiya jaataa he. saaton pradeshon main se do ko aanshik rajya kaa darja diya gaya he. in pradeshon main simit shaktiyon vaali nirvaachit vidhayikaon or mantriyon kii kaaryakari parishadon kaa praavdhaan he.
 yah bharat kii anaupchaarik yaa ardh-aadhikarik kshetron kii suchi he. kuch kshetra bhugolic, jaateey, bhashaai, boli, yaa saanskrithik drushti main ek hai, or kuch aitihaasik deshon, rajyon yaa praanton ke anuroop hai.
 kuch rajyon main un kshetron kaa bhi samavesh he, jinke paas koi aadhikarik prashasnic sarkari sthiti nahin he. ve vishuddh bhugolic kshetra hai; haalanki kuch kshetra aitihaasik deshon, rajyon yaa praanton ke anuroop bhi hai. ek kshetra main ek yaa ek se adhik mandal shaamil ho sakate hai, lekin, kshetron kii or mandalon kii simaaain hameshaa bilkul ek nahin hoti he. ab tak in kshetron ko aadhikarik prashasnic sthiti dene ke liye koi bada andolan nahin rahaa he.
 kuch bhartiya rajyon ko mandalon main bhi vibhajit kiya gaya he. in mandalon kii aadhikarik prashasnic sthiti hoti he or prayek mandal kaa netritv ek iees adhikari karata he, jise diviginal kamishnar kehete hai. prayek mandal main kai jile shaamil hote hai:
 rajyon ko aage janapadon main vibhajit kiya jaataa he. 2016 tak bharat main 696 janapad hai. prayek janapad kaa adhyaksh ek iees adhikari hota he, jise district magistrate kaha jaataa he.
 tehsil, taluka, mandal, upkhand, mahakama, kai gaanvon yaa gaanv samoohon kaa samooh hai, jinke adhyaksh tehseeldaar, talukadhar yaa emaro hote hai. tehsil str ke sarkari nikaayon ko panchayat samiti kaha jaataa he. prayek rajya apane upkhandon ke liye alag-alag naamon kaa upyog karte hai:
 vikaskhand, jinhen saamudayik vikaas khand yaa sidi.block bhi kehete hai, praayah tehsil ke baad prashasnic prabhag kaa aglaa str he.
 gram bharat main upavibhaagon kaa sabase nimna str he. gram str ke sarkari nikaayon ko gram panchayat kaha jaataa he, jo ki 2002 main anumaanit 2,56,000 the. prayek gram panchayat ke adhikaar kshetra main ek bada gram yaa chote graamon kaa ek samooh hota he, jinaki kul milakar 500 gram sabha se adhik jansankhya hoti he. graamon ke samoohon ko kabhi-kabhi hobli yaa pattee bhi kaha jaataa he.
 kuch sarkari kaary or gatividhiyaan - jinamein saaf peyajal kii uplabdhta, gramin vikaas or shiksha shaamil hai - ek gram se bhi nichle str par hi kari jaati hai. inako hi "bastiyon" kaha jaataa he. bharat main aisi 17,14,556 bastiyaan hai. kuch rajyon ke adhikansh gaanvon main ek hi basti he; lekin dusron main ke gaanvon main bastiyon kaa ucch anupaat he.
