 berlin sammelan varsh 1884-85 main berlin main hua tha. is sammelan ne nav-upniveshikaran ke kaal main africa ke upniveshikaran tathaa vyapaar ko viniyamit karne kaa kaary kiya. ise 'congou sammelan' bhi kehete hai. is sammelan kaa samay germany ke sahasaa vishwa kii samrajyavaadee shakti ke rup main udai kaa samay bhi tha.
