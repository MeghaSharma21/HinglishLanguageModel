 gajanan vishwanath ketkar hindu mahasabha ke ek varisht kaaryakarta rahe hai. lokmaanya tilak ke pautra the. inhone kala se snaatak karne ke uparaant vidhi snaatak kiya. ye kesari evam maharatta patron ke sampadak bhi rahe hai. inhone hindu mahasabha kaa kaary 1938 main bhaganagar ke ahimsak pratirodh ke dauraan bhi jari rakhaa tha. 1941 main hue hindu mahasabha ke bhagalpur main hue satr main bhaag bhi liya, jise british sarkaar ne pratibandhit kar diya tha. ye kai varshon tak maharashtra hindu sabha ke koshadhyaksh bhi rahe. gandhi hatya kand ke antargat nehru sarkaar dwaara jab vir saavarkar giraftar hue, tab unke mukademen ke liye dhan kaa prabandh bhi kiya. 1948 main ketkar ne nehru sarkaar evam pratibandhit rashtriya swayamsevak sangh ke bich vaarta main madhyasthata kii. 1950 main or phir 1964 main jail bhi gaye.
