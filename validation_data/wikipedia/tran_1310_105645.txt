 sanjivani ek vanaspati kaa naam he jiskaa upyog chikitsa kaary ke liye kiya jaataa he. iskaa vaigyanik naam selaginela brahpatersis he or isaki utpatti lagbhag tees arab varsh pehle carbonipheras yug se maani jaati hai. lakhanu sthit vanaspati anusandhan santhaan main sanjivani booti ke jin kii pahchaan par kaary kar rahe paanch vanaspati vaigyaniko main se ek dow pi.en. khare ke anusaar sanjivani kaa sambandh paudhon ke terrydophia samooh se he jo prithvi par paidaa hone vaale sanvahani paudhe the. unhone bataaya ki nami nahin milane par sanjivani murajhaakar papadi jaisi ho jaati he lekin iske baavjood yah jivit rahati he or baad main thodi si hi nami milane par yah phir khil jaati he. yah pattharon tathaa shushk sataha par bhi ug sakati he. iske isi gun ke kaaran vaigyanik is baat kii gaharaai se jaanch kar rahe he ki aakhir sanjivani main aisa koun sa jin paaya jaataa he jo ise anya paudhon se alag or vishesh darja pradaan karata he. haalaanki vaigyanikon kaa kahana he ki isaki asli pahchaan bhi kaaphi kathin he kyonki jangalon main iske samaan hi anek aise paudhe or vanaspatiyaan ugti he jinase aasaani se dhokhaa khaaya ja saktaa he. magar kaha jaataa he ki chaar inch ke aakaar vaali sanjivani lambaai main badhane ke bajaae sataha par failati he.
 yah uttarapradesh uttarakhand or urisa sahit bharat ke lagbhag sabhi rajyon main pai jaati he. baba ramdev ke sahyogi acharya baalkrishna ne sanjivani ko drongiri kii pahadiyon par 15,000 foot kii oonchaai par bhi paaya. sanjivani kaa ullekh puraanon main bhi he. ayurveda main iske aushadhiy laabhon ke bare main varnan he. yah na sirf pet ke rogon main balki maanav kii lambaai badhaane main bhi sahaayak hoti he.
