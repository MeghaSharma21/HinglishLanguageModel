 colorado sanyukt rajya america kaa ek rajya he. yah america ke madhya-pashchim main dakshini rocky parvaton ko apane main samaae hue hai. yah america main kshetrafal ke adhar par 8vaan or jansankhya ke aadhaar par 22vaan sabase bada rajya he. sanyukt rajya janaganana beuro ke anusaar 1 july 2014 ko colorado kii jansankhya 53,55,866 thi, jo 2010 main hui janaganana kii tulana main 6.5% adhik thi. iskaa kshetrafal 2,69,837 varg kimi he.
 is rajya kaa naam colorado nadi ke naam par padaa, jiskaa naam spani yatriyon ne rio colorado rakhaa tha. colorado ke rajyashetra ko 28 farvari 1861 ko sanghatit kiya gaya tha or america raashtrapati ulisis this grant ne 1 agast 1876 ko ek udghoshnaa-patr par hastakshar kar ise america main sammilit kiya. colorado ko "shatkeey rajya" bhi kaha jaataa he jo iskaa upnaam he kyonki yah america swatantrata kii ghoshnaa ke sau varsh puure hone ke 28 din baad rajya bana tha.
 colorado kii seemayen uttar main vioming, purvottar main nabraska, purv main cansus, dakshinpurv main oklahoma, dakshin main nayaa maxico, pashchim main uta, or dakshin-pashchim main erizona se milti hai. colorado apane jivant paridrishy ke liye prakhyat he jo parvaton, wanon, unche maidaanon, mesu, ghaatiyon, patharon, nadiyon, or retili bhoomiyon se ata padaa he. isaki rajdhani danwar he jo is rajya kaa sarvaadhik jansankhya vaala nagar bhi he. is rajya ke logon ko angrezi main "coloradens" kehete hai, yadhyapi purana shabd "coloradian" abhi bhi prayukt kiya jaataa he.
