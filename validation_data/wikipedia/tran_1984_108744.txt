 choudhary brahm prakaash rajkeey abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay, jafarpur yaa GEC kaa nirmaan dilli sarkaar ke training and technical vibhaag dwaara san 2007 main kiya gaya tha. mahavidhyalay kaa naam, dilli ke pratham mukhya mantri, choudhary brahm prakaash ke naam ke upar samarpit kiya gaya he. yah mahavidhyalay desh kaa pehla aisa abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay he jo civil ejineering ko samarpit he. yah mahavidhyalay guru gobind singh indraprastha vishvavidhyalay se sambaddh he.
 rajkeey abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay jafarpur kaa udghaatan dilli kii mukhyamantri shrimati shila dikshit dwaara san 2007 main kiya gaya tha. shrimati dikshit kaa kahana tha kii "mahavidhyalay kaa nirmaan unki sarkaar kaa gaaon ke unnati ke prati ek or bada kadam tha." 
 dilli main snaatak str ke vishyon main chaatro ke antargrahan kii tulana jab anya bade shahro jaise kii bangalore, mumbai, kolkata ittyadi se kii gayi to dilli kaa antargrahan sabase kam paaya gaya. usee kami ko puraa karne ke liye dilli main or abhiyantriki or praudyogiki str ke shaikshik sansthaanon ke nirmaan kii zarurat padi or usee kaary ke liye rajkeey abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay kaa nirmaan ek aisa kadam tha.
 mahavidhyalay kaa parisar jafarpur kalam gaanv main 15 acre ke vishaal bhoomi par failaa hua he. mahavidhyalay raav tulaaraam smarak aspataal se sata hua he. mahavidyalay ke pashchimi disha main jawahar navoday vidhyalay he to purvi disha main mahavidhyalay ke parisar main audyogic prasikshan centre he, jo kii mahavidhyalay ke parisar main kaaryshil he.
 mahavidhyalay bhavishya main abhiyantriki main snaatak, adhisnatak, tathaa doctoral str ke adhyayan shuru karne kii yojana kar rahaa he. bhavishya main mahavidhyalay main 2000 chaatr honge tathaa aane vaale samay main civil ejineering or anya sambadh kshetron main utkrushtataa kaa centre banaaya jaayegaa. 
 mahavidhyalay kii sarvaadhik suvidhaae abhi vistaar par he. vidhyaarthiyon ke liye chaatralay suvidha ambedkar vishvavidhyalay dwarka parisar main uplabdh he. mahavidhyalay main edusat suvidha bhi uplabdh he, jiske upyog se chaatr or shikshak dur baithe pard or padhaa sakate he. khel kud kii suvidhao me footbol, cricket, vollybol, teble tennis ittyadi chhattro ko uplabdh he. .
 mahavidhyalay main sangh lok seva ayog niyukt tathaa sanvidatmak, nihaayat anbhavi or yogyata praapt shikshak niyukt hai. iske alaava anya abhiyantriki or praudyogiki sansthaanon jaise dilli ejineering college or netaji subhash praudyogiki santhaan se bhi shikshak rajkeey abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay ke chaatro ko padhaate he. rajkeey abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay purvasnatak str kii "baicheller of technology" kii oopaadhiyaan pradaan karata he. mahavidhyalay abhi nimnlikhit purvasnatak paathyakram main oopaadhiyaan detaa he: 
 mahavidhyalay main agle shaikshik varsh se snaatak str ke paathyakramon main bhi padhai shuru hone kii sambhaavna he.
 mahavidhyalay or anya sambadh mahavidhyalayon main pravesh, guru gobind singh indraprastha vishvavidhyalay ke rashtriya str ke saarv pravesh pariksha main rank ke aadhaar par hota he. pravesh ke liye desh bhar se lakhon chaatr pariksha main baithte he. parantu in sab chaatro main se dilli praant ke chhatron ke liye 85 pratishat siten arakshit hoti he. or anya chaatro ke liye 15 pratishat siten arakshit hoti he. chaatr jo rashtriya rajdhani kshetra dilli main sthit vidyalayon/mahavidyalayon se pariksha uteern karte he, ve dilli praant ke chaatro main aate he.
 maidan gaman ke 24 ghante maap ke liye parisar main ek bukampamaan lagaaya gaya he. dilli strang moshan upkaran network ke tahat yah yantra, bukamp abhiyantriki vibhaag, bhartiya praudyogiki sansathan ruraki dwaara varsh 2007 main lagaaya gaya tha. is yantra se, maidan gaman ke maap richter paimaane par kiye jate he
 bharat mausam vigyaanan vibhaag, pune dwaara vayumandaleey aankade ikattha karne ke liye ek vayumandaleey yantra centre, mahavidhyalay main varsh 2009 main lagaaya gaya tha. yah yantra jaffarpur kalam or aas paas ke kshetro ke taapmaana, varsh gadhata, vaayu raftaar, vayumandaleey daab, nami, vikiran maapta he. 
 kendriya pradooshan niyantran board ne varsh 2007 main teen mahino ke liye pradooshan nigaraanee ikai, mahavidhyalay ke parisar main lagaaya. us yantra se nilambit baat kan tathaa shwaas nilambit baat kan ke maap liye gaye tathaa yah avlokan kiya gaya kii jaffarpur main dilli ke sabase shuddh hawa he.
 prarambh mahavidhyalay ke vaarshik saanskrithik festival kaa pehla praaroop tha or excyuburence uskaa doosraa praaroop tha.iske alaava mahavidyalay ek timahi patrika ixpedishan bhi prakashit karata he. yah patrika chaatr, karmchaaree, shikshak samudaay ko ek aam manch pradaan karata he jiske zariye ve, academik ghatana, saanskrithik kriya, khelkud se jude apane tajurbe, khyal, soochna baantate he.
