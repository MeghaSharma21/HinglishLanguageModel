 arunachal pradesh ke lohit jila main sthityah jhil bahut khubasurat he or samudratal se 5000 me. kii oonchai par sthit he. iskaa kshetrafal lagbhag 8 varg kilometre he. jhil ke aasapaas ke drishy bhi bahut aakarshak hai. is jhil se paryatak barf se dhaki chotiyon ko dekh sakate hai. in chotiyon ke alaava yahaan par khubasurat jangal dekhe ja sakate hai or inaki saiyr bhi kii ja sakati he. yahaan tak aane ke liye paryatakon ko pehle tvam gaanv pahunchnaa pasta he kyonki abhi tak yahaan par sadak maarg kaa nirmaan nahin hua he. sadak maarg na hone se yah yatra parvatarohan main badal jaati he.
