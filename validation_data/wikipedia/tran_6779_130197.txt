 rajasthan parmaanu urja stesan yaa rajasthan parmaanu urja pariyojna bharat ke rajasthan rajya ke ravatbhata main sthit he.
 duglas point parmaanu janarating stesan canada kaa nirmaan 1961 main ek candu ke saath 220 megawatt bijli utpaadan karne main sakasham baari jal riector ke saath shuru hua. duglas point parmaanu janarating stesan canada ke nirmaan ke do saal baad, rajasthan rajya main nirmit do samaan riektaron kaa nirmaan shuru hua. das saal baad, 1973 main arapies-1 ne seva shuru kar di. 1974 main bharat ne muskuraate hue buddha kaa aayojan kiya, jo bharat kaa pehla parmaanu hathiyaar pareekshan tha jis kaaran canada ne pariyojna kaa apana kaam band kar diya. jis kaaran 1981 tak arapies-2 kii kamishan main deree hui.
