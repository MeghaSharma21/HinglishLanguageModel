 aniyattapi praani yaa sheetarakti praaniyon ke sharir ke andruni taapmaana or ushmiyata se sambandhit lakshanon ke samooh ke liye ek saamaanya naam he jisamen yah shaamil hai:
 paaramparik jeev vigyaan main sheetarakti praaniyon ke vipareet garmrakti jaanavar maane jate the, jinka sharir apane taapmaana ko swayan santulit rakhata he.
