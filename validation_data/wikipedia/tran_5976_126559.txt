 anganvadi bharat main gramin maan or bacchon ke dekha bhaal centre he. bacchon ke bhukh or kuposhan se nipatane ke liye ekikrut bal vikaas seva kaarykram ke bhaag ke rup main,2 actombar 1975 bansawada jile ke gadhi gaanv khuli unhen bharat sarkaar dwaara shuru kiya gaya tha. anganwari kaa arth he "angan aashray".
 is prakaar kaa anganwari centre bhartiya gaanvon main buniyadi swasthya dekhbhaal pradaan karata he. yah bhartiya saarvajanik swasthya dekhbhaal pranali kaa ek hissaa he. mul swasthya dekhbhaal gatividhiyon main garbhanirodhak paraamarsh or aapurti, poshan shiksha or anupoorak, saath hi purv-vidhyalay kii gatividhiyon shaamil hai. kendron ko maukhik rihydration namak, buniyadi dawaaon or garbh nirodhakon ke liye depo ke rup main istemaal kiya ja saktaa he.
 31 janvari 2013 tak 13.3 lakh anganwari or mini anganvadi centre 13.7 lakh sweekrut edblusi / mini-edblusi se parichaalit hai. ye centre poorak poshan pradaan karte hai, gaiyr - purv-praathmik shiksha, poshan or swasthya shiksha, pratirakshan, swasthya jaanch or refral sevaaon kii baad main teen sevaayen saarvajanik swasthya pranaaliyon ke saath milakar pradaan kii jaati hai. 
 anganwari kaaryakartaaon kii zimmedaari atyant mahatvapurn hai. ve garbhvatee mahilaon ke liye janmpurv or prasavpurv dekhbhaal sunishchit karte hai or navjaat shishuon or nursing maataaon ke liye turant nidaan or dekhbhaal karte hai. ve 6 varsh se kam umr ke sabhi bacchon ke tikaakaran kaa prabandh karte hai.
 iske alaava ve 6 varsh se kam aayu ke bacchon ke liye poorak poshan ke vitaran ke saath-saath garbhvatee or nursing mahilaon kii nigaraanee bhi karte hai. mahilaon or bacchon ke liye niyamit swasthya or chikitsa jaanch kii nigaraanee unki mukhya jimmedaariyon main se ek he. anganwari kaaryakarta aksar ek shikshak kii bhoomika nibhaate hai or 3 se 5 saal ke bich ke bacchon ko purv-skool shiksha pradaan karnaa he.
 mahila evam bal vikaas mantralay ne anganwari kaaryakartaaon kii jimmedaariyon ke liye dishanirdesh nirdhaarit kiye hai. in main shaamil he, is kaarykram ko nishpadit karne, sabhi pariwaron ke niyamit rup se twarit surveykshan karne, purv-skool kii gatividhiyon kaa aayojan karne, bacchon ko vishesh rup se garbhvatee mahilaon ko stanpan karaane ke liye swasthya or poshan shiksha pradaan karne, aadi ko badhaava dene ke liye, parivaar niyojan, bachche ke vikaas or vikaas ke bare main mata-pita ko shikshit karnaa, kishori shakti yojana ke kriyanvayan or saamaajik jaagrukta kaaryakramon aadi ke aayojan dwaara kishor ladkiyaan or mata-pita ko shikshit karne main sahaayta karnaa, bacchon main viklangon kii pahchaan karnaa or isi tarah.
 prayek 40 se 65 anganwari kaaryakartaaon kii nigaraanee ek mukhya sevika dwaara kii jaati he. ve naukari prashikshan pradaan karte hai
 anganwari kaaryakartaaon ke saath zimmedaariyaan karne ke alaava, unke paas anya kartavyon jaise ki kam aarthik sthiti se kaarykram se labhanvit hone kaa trek rakhane ke liye vishesh rup se kuposhit varg se sambandhit hai; anganwari kaaryakartaaon ko umr or bacchon ke vajan kaa akalan karne or unke vajan kii sajeesh rachane ke liye maargdarshan karnaa; prabhavi tareekon kaa pradarshan, udhaaharan ke liye, maataaon ko swasthya or poshan shiksha pradaan karne main; or kya sudhaar kiya ja saktaa he yah nirdhaarit karne ke liye anganwari or shramikon ke aankadon ko banaae rakhen. mukhya sevika tab bal vikaas pariyojnaon ke adhikari ko report karti he. 
 chikitsa or swasthya dekhbhaal visheshgy durbhaagya se bharat main kushal peshewaron kii kami he isliye, anganwari pranali ke madhyam se, desh, badi swasthya suvidhaon ke apane lakshya ko puraa karne kii koshish kar rahaa he jo sthaneey jansankhya ke liye sasti or sulabh hai.
 kai tarah se gramin aabaadi tak pahunchane main ek chikitsak kii tulana main anganbari kaaryakarta behatar susajjit he. chunki kaaryakarta logon ke saath rahata he, isliye ve swasthya samasyaon ke kaarnon kii pahchaan karne ke liye behatar sthiti main hai or isliye unhen pratibandh karte hai. unke kshetra main swasthya sthiti kii unki bahut acchhee jaankaree he duusari baat yah he ki anganwari kaaryakarta peshewaron ke rup main kushal yaa yogya nahin hai, isliye unke paas behatar saamaajik kaushal hai jisase logon ke saath baatcheet karnaa aasaan ho jaataa he. iske alaava, kyonki ye kaaryakarta gaanv se hai, ve bharosemand hai jo logon ke liye unki madad karnaa aasaan banaata he. aakhri lekin kam se kam, anganwari kaaryakarta logon ke tareekon se acchhee tarah jaanate hai, bhashaa ke saath sahaj mahasoos karte hai, gramin logon ko vyaktigat rup se jaanate hai. yah unke liye logon dwaara saamana kii ja rahi samasyaon ko samajhane or sunishchit karne ke liye bahut aasaan he. ki ve hal kar rahe hai 
 anganwari ko saarvbhaumik rup se sabhi paatr bacchon or maa ko uplabdh karaane ke liye saarvajanik nitigat charchaa hui he. iske liye bajtiy aavanton main mahatvapurn vruddhi kii aavashyaktaa hogi or anganwari kendron main 16 lakh se zyada kii vruddhi hogi.
 anganwari ke adhikariyon or unke sahaayakon dwaara karyrat hai, jo aamtaur par garib pariwaron se mahilaayein hai anya sarkari karmchaariyon jaise karmchaariyon ko vyapak sevanivritti ke laabh ke saath sthaayee naukariyaan nahin hai kaaryakarta virodh or is vishay par saarvajanik bahes chal rahe hai. kuch anganwari kendron main mahilaon ke khilaaf bhrashtachar or aparaadhon kii aavadhik reportein hai. jab anganvadi-sevarat bacchon main biimaar ho yaa mar jate hai to kanoonee or saamaajik samasyaayein hai
 2008-2009 ke bajet kii ghoshnaa karte hue, bhartiya vitt mantri pi chidambaram ne kaha ki anganwari kaaryakartaaon ke liye 1500 rupee prati maah or sahaayak prati maah 750 rupee vetan badhaayaa jayega. march 2008 main is baat par bahes hui ki kya pack kiye gaye khaadya padaarth ko bhojan kii seva kaa hissaa ban janaa chahie. nobel puraskaar vijeta amartya sen sahit virodhiyon ne yah kehete hue asahmat vyakt kiya ki yah bacchon dwaara khaaya jane vaala ekmaatr bhojan hoga. nijee kshetra ke saath bhagidaari badhaane ke liye vikalp jari rahe hai.
