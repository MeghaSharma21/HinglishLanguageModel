 ekachakra, pashchim bengal ke birbhum jile kaa ek gaanv he jo rampurhat se 20 kimi dur he.
 mahabharat ke anusaar, yah kichakon ke desh kaa ek nagar he jahaan kabhi vyas ke nirdesh se pandavon ne apane nishkasana kaal main kuch samay nivas kiya tha. jeneral kaningham or unke samarthak bihar ke shahabaad jile main sthit aadhunik aara naamak sthaan ko ekachakra maanate hai. mahabharat ke anusaar uskaa doosraa naam 'panchalanagar' bhi he. ise parichakra yaa parivakra bhi kaha gaya he.
