 kala shabd itnaa vyapak he ki vibhinn vidwaanon kii paribhaashaayen keval ek vishesh paksh ko chhukar rah jaati hai. kala kaa arth abhi tak nishchit nahin ho paaya he, yadhyapi isaki hajaaron paribhaashaayen kii gayi hai. bhartiya parampara ke anusaar kala un sari kriyaaon ko kehete hai jinamein kaushal apekshit ho. europea shastriyon ne bhi kala main kaushal ko mahatvapurn mana he.
 kala ek prakaar kaa kritrim nirmaan he jisame shaareerik or maansik kaushalon kaa prayog hota he.
 maithili sharan gupt ke shabdon main,
 duusare shabdon main -: man ke antahkaran kii sundar prastuti hi kala he.
 kala shabd kaa prayog shaayad sabase pehle bharat ke "naatyashaastra" main hi miltaa he. peeche vatsyayan or ushnas ne kramash: apane granth "kamasutra" or "shukraniti" main iskaa varnan kiya.
 "kamasutra", "shukraniti", jain granth "prabandhkosh", "kalavilas", "lalitvistar" ityadi sabhi bhartiya granthon main kala kaa varnan praapt hota he. adhiktar granthon main kalaon kii sankhya 64 maani gayi he. "prabandhkosh" ityadi main 72 kalaon kii suchi milti he. "lalitvistar" main 86 kalaon ke naam ginaye gayi hai. prasiddh kashmiri pandit kshemendra ne apane granth "kalavilas" main sabase adhik sankhya main kalaon kaa varnan kiya he. usamen 64 janopayogi, 32 dharm, arth, kaam, moksh, sambandhi, 32 matsarya-sheel-prabhaavamaan sambandhi, 64 svachhkarita sambandhi, 64 veshyaaon sambandhi, 10 bheshaj, 16 kysth tathaa 100 saar kalaon kii charchaa he. sabase adhik pramanik suchi "kamasutra" kii he.
 europea sahitya main bhi kala shabd kaa prayog shaareerik yaa maansik kaushal ke liye hi adhiktar hua he. vahaan prakruti se kala kaa kaary bhinn mana gaya he. kala kaa arth he rachana karnaa arthath vah kritrim he. prakritik sristi or kala donon bhinn vastuen hai. kala us kaary main he jo manushya karata he. kala or vigyaan main bhi antar mana jaataa he. vigyaan main gyaan kaa praadhanya he, kala main kaushal kaa. kaushalpurna maanviya kaary ko kala kii sangya di jaati he. kaushalviheen yaa bedab dhang se kiye gayi kaaryon ko kala main sthaan nahin diya jaataa.
 kalaon ke vargeekaran main mataiky hona sambhav nahin he. vartmaan samay main kala ko manviki ke antargat rakhaa jaataa he jisamen itihaas, sahitya, darshan or bhashavigyaan aadi bhi aate hai.
 paashchaatya jagat main kala ke do bhed kiye gayi hai- upyogi kalaayen tathaa lalit kalaayen . paramparaagat rup se nimnlikhit saat ko 'kala' kaha jaataa he- 
 aadhunik kaal main inamen photography, chalchitran, vigyaapan or comics jud gayi hai.
 uparokt kalaon ko nimnlikhit prakaar se bhi shreneekrut kar sakate hai-
 kuch kalaon main drishy or nishpaadan donon ke tatva mishrit hote hai, jaise film.
 jivan, urja kaa mahaasaagar he. jab antashchetana jaagrit hoti he to urja jivan ko kala ke rup main ubharti he. kala jivan ko satyam shivam sundaram se samanvit karti he. iske dwaara hi buddhi aatma kaa satya swarup jhalkata he. kala us kshitij kii bhanti he jiskaa koi chor nahin, itani vishaal itani vistrut anek vidhaon ko apane main samete, tabhi to kavi man kah uthaa-
 rabindranath thakur ke mukh se niklaa “kala main manushya apane bhaavon kii abhivyakti karata he ” to plato ne kaha - “kala satya kii anukriti ke anukriti he.”
 talsty ke shabdon main apane bhaavon kii kriya rekha, rang, dhwani yaa shabd dwaara is prakaar abhivyakti karnaa ki use dekhne yaa sunne main bhi vahi bhav utpann ho jaae kala he. hriday kii gairaiyon se nikali anubhuti jab kala kaa rup leti he, kalakaar kaa antarman maano moort le uthta he chaahe lekhnee uskaa madhyam ho yaa rangon se bhigi tulika yaa suron kii pukaar yaa vaadyon kii jhankaar. kala hi atmik shaanti kaa madhyam he. yah kathin tapasya he, sadhana he. isi ke madhyam se kalakaar sunahari or indradhanushi aatma se swapnil vichaaron ko saakaar rup detaa he.
 kala main aisi shakti honi chahie ki vah logon ko sankeern simaaon se upar uthaakar use aise unche sthaan par pahunchaa de jahaan manushya keval manushya rah jaataa he. kala vyakti ke man main bani swaarth, parivaar, kshetra, dharm, bhashaa or jati aadi kii seemayen mitaakar vistrut or vyapaktaa pradaan karti he. vyakti ke man ko udaatt banaati he. vah vyakti ko “swa” se nikaalkar “vasudaiv kutumbakam” se jodatii he.
 kala hi he jisamen maanav man main samvednaayen ubhaarne, pravritiyon ko dhaalane tathaa chintan ko modane, abhiruchi ko disha dene kii adbhut kshamta he. manoranjan, soundarya, pravaah, ullas na jane kitne tattvon se yah bharpoor he, jisamen maanviyata ko sammohit karne kii shakti he. yah apana jaadu tatkaal dikhaati he or vyakti ko badalane main, loha pighalaakar pani bana dene vaali bhatti kii tarah manovritiyon main baari rupantaran prastut kar sakati he.
 jab yah kala sangeet ke rup main ubhartee he to kalakaar gayan or vaadan se swayan ko hi nahin shrotaaon ko bhi abhibhoot kar detaa he. manushya aatmavismrit ho uthta he. deepak raag se deepak jal uthta he or malhaar raag se megh barasana yah kala kii sadhana kaa hi charmotkarsh he. sangeet kii sadhana; suron kii sadhana he. milan he aatma se paramatma kaa; abhivyakti he anubhuti kii.
 bhaat or chaaran bhi jab yudhsthal main umang, josh se sarabor kavita-gaan karte the, to vir yoddhaon kaa utsaah doguna ho jaataa tha or yudhakshetra kahin hathi kii chinghad, to kahin ghodon kii hinhinaahat to kahin shatru kii chitkaar se bhar uthta tha; yah gayan kala kii parinti hi to he.
 sangeet keval maanavmaatr main hi nahin apitu pashu-pakshiyon va ped-paudhon main bhi amrit ras bhar detaa he. pashu-pakshi bhi sangeet se prabhavit hokar jhum uthte hai to ped-paudhon main bhi spandan ho uthta he. tarangen foot padati hai. yahi nahin maanav ke anek rogon kaa upachaar bhi sangeet kii tarangon se sambhav he. kaha bhi he:
 sangeet ke baad ye lalit-kalaon main sthaan diya gaya he to vah he- nritykala. chaahe vah bharatnatyam ho yaa katthak, manipuri ho yaa kuchipudi. vibhinn bhav-bhangimaaon se yukt hamaari samskruti va puranic kathaon ko ye nrity jeevantata pradaan karte hai. shastreey nrity ho yaa lokanrity inamen khokar tan hi nahin man bhi jhum uthta he.
 kalaon main kala, shreshth kala, vah he chitrakala. manushya swabhaav se hi anukaran kii pravriti rakhata he. jaisaa dekhtaa he usee prakaar apane ko dhaalane kaa praytn karata he. yahi uski atmabhivyanjana he. apni rangon se bhari tulika se chitrakaar jan bhaawanaaon kii abhivyakti karata he to darshak hatprabh rah jaataa he. pashaan yug se hi jo chitra paritoshak hote rahe hai ye maatr ek vidha nahin, apitu ye maanavtaa ke vikaas kaa ek nishchit sopan prastut karte hai. chitron ke madhyam se akhet karne vaale aadim maanav ne na keval apane sanvegon ko balki rahasyamay pravriti or jangal ke khoonkhaar pravaasiyon ke viruddh apane astitv ke liye kiye gayi sangharsh ko bhi abhivyakt kiya he. dhire-dhire chitrakala shilpakala sopan chadhi. sindhughati sabhyataa main paye gayi chitron main pashu-pakshi maanav akruti sundar pratimaaen, zyada namoonay bharat kii adisabhyataa kii kalapriyata kaa dyotak he.
 ajanta, baadh aadi ke gupha chitron kii kalakritiyon purv baudhdhakaal ke antargat aati he. bhartiya kala kaa ujjwal itihaas bhitti chitron se hi prarambh hota he or sansar main inke samaan chitra kahin nahin bane aisa vidwaanon kaa mat he. ajanta ke kala mandir prem, dhairya, upasna, bhakthi, sahanubhuti, tyaag tathaa shaanti ke apurv udhaaharan he.
 madhubani style, pahari style, thanjore style, mughal style, bengal style apni-apni visheshtaon ke kaaran aaj janashakti ke man chinhit he. yadi bhartiya samskruti kii murtti kala va shilp kala ke darshan karne ho to dakshin ke mandir apana vishisht sthaan rakhte hai. jahaan ke minakshi mandir, vrihadishwar mandir, konark mandir apni anuthi pahchaan ke liye prasiddh he.
 yahi nahin bhartiya samskruti main lok kalaon kii khushbu kii mahak aaj bhi apni pracheen parampara se samrudh he. jis prakaar adikaal se ab tak maanav jivan kaa itihaas krambaddh nahin miltaa usee prakaar kala kaa bhi itihaas krambaddh nahin he, parantu yah nishchit he ki sahachari ke rup main kala sadaa se hi saath rahi he. lok kalaon kaa janm bhaawanaaon or paramparaon par aadhaarit he kyonki yah jansamanya kii anubhuti kii abhivyakti he. yah vartmaan shastreey or vyavsaayik kala kii prishthbhumi bhi he. bharatvarsh main prithvi ko dharti mata kaha gaya he. matrubhuti to iskaa saanskrithik va parishkrut rup he. isi dharti mata kaa shraddha se alankaran karke lokmanav main apni atmiyata kaa parichay diya. bhartiya samskruti main dharti ko vibhinn naamon se alankrita kiya jaataa he. gujarat main “saathiya” rajasthan main “mandana”, maharashtra main “rangoli” uttar pradesh main “chowk poorna”, bihar main “ahpan”, bengal main “alpana” or gadhwal main “aapnaa” ke naam se prasiddh he. yah kala dharmanupragit bhaavon se preshit hoti he; jisamen shraddha se rachana kii jaati he. vivaah or shubh avasaron main lokkala kaa vishisht sthaan he. dwaaron par alankrita ghadon kaa rakhana, usamen jal va nariyal rakhana, vandanvaar baandhna aadi ko aaj ke aadhunik yug main bhi ise adarbhav, shraddha or upasna kii drushti se dekha jaataa he.
 aaj bharat kii vaastukala kaa utkrisht udhaaharan “tajmahal” he, jisane vishwa kii apurv kalakrittiyon ke saat aashcharya main sheershasth sthaan paaya he. lalakila, akshardham mandir, qutubameenar, jama masjid bhi bhartiya vaastukala kaa anupam udhaaharan rahi he. murttikala, samanvayvaadee vaastukala tathaa bhittichitron kii kala ke saath-saath parvatiy kalaon ne bhi bhartiya kala se samrudh kiya he.
 satya, ahimsa, karunaa, samanvay or sarvadharm sambhaav ye bhartiya samskruti ke aise tatva hai, jinhone anek baadhaon ke bich bhi hamaari samskruti kii nirantarataa ko akshunn banaae rakhaa he. in visheshtaon ne hamaari samskruti main vah shakti utpann kii he ki vah bharat ke baahar asia, dakshin purv asia main apni jade failaa sake.
 hamaari samskruti ke in tattvon ko pracheen kaal se lekar aaj tak kii kalaon main dekha ja saktaa he. inhen lalit kalaon ne hamaari samskruti ko satya, shiv, soundarya jaise anek sakaaratmak pakshon ko chitrit kiya he. in kalaon ke madhyam se hi hamaara lokajeevan, lokmanas tathaa jivan kaa aantarik or aadhyatmik paksh abhivyakt hota rahaa he, hamen apni is parampara se katnaa nahin he apitu apni parampara se hi ras lekar aadhuniktaa ko chitrit karnaa he.

 "shukraniti" ke anusaar kalaon kii sankhya asankhya he, phir bhi samaaj main ati prachlit 64 kalaon kaa usamen ullekh hua he. "shukraniti" ke anusaar gananaa is prakaar he :-
 vatsyayan ke "kamasutra" kii vyakhya karte hue jaimangal ne do prakaar kii kalaon kaa ullekh kiya he – kaamshaastra se sambandhit kalaayen, tantra sambandhi kalaayen. donon kii alag-alag sankhya 64 he. kaam kii kalaayen 24 hai jinka sambandh sambhog ke asana se he, 20 dyut sambandhi, 16 kamsukh sambandhi or 4 ucchtar kalaayen. kul 64 pradhan kalaayen hai. iske atirikt katipay saadhaaran kalaayen bhi bataayi gayi hai.
 pragat he ki in kalaon main se bahut kam kaa sambandh lalit kala yaa fin arts se he. lalit kala – arthath chitrakala, murttikala aadi kaa prasang inase bhinn or saundaryashastra se sambandhit he.
