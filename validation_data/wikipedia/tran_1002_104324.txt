 riyanka chanda ek bhartiya doordarshan abhinetri he. unka janm 14 sitambar 19992 main mumbai maharashtra bharat main hua. unki aayu ab 25 varsh he.
 riyanka chanda ne apane doordarshan main kaary karne kii shuruuaat maatr 14 varsh kii aayu main ki thi. jai shri swami narayan dharawahik main aapnaa yogadan dekar kiya tha. par us samay unki aayu kam hone kaaran unhone yah nirnay kiya ki ve pehle apni shiksha puri ki uske baad vah phir se 2012 main phir se kuch dharawahikon main nazar ai. saas bina sasuraal,mahadev main sati kii bahan/khyati kii bhumika nibhaai,khwaabon ke darmiyaan main maihjabeen ki bhumika nibhaai.
