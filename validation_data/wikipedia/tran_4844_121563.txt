 purvi africa kaa ithiopia desh prashasnic rup se pradeshon, anchalon, zilon or nagarpalikaon main vibhajit he.
 jaateey samudaayon ke aadhaar par ithiopia main 9 pradesh sangathit hai jinhen amhari bhashaa main 'kililoch' kaha jaataa he:
 inke saath desh main do adhikrut nagar bhi hai:
 in 9 pradeshon ko aage 68 anchalon main vibhajit kiya gaya he.
 anchalon ko zilon main baanta gaya he jo amhari bhashaa main 'vorada' kehalaate hai. kul milakar desh main 670 gramin or 100 shahari vorada hai.
 zilon ko nagarpalikaon main baanta jaataa he. inhen amhari bhashaa main 'kebele' kehete hai jiskaa arth 'mohalle' hota he.
 san 1942 se 1991 tak ithiopia 13 praanton main vibhajit tha. 1942 se pehle anya prantiya vyavastha thi.
