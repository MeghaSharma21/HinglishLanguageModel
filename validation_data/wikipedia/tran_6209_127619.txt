 david wiliam donald camran 2010 se july 2016 tak sanyukt rajshahi ke pradhan mantri rah chuke hai. ve conzarvative party ke netaa the tathaa ye vitane se sansad sadsy the. europea sangh kii sadsyta par hue janamat sangrah main janata ne sangh ko chhodane kaa nirnay diya to unhone istife kii ghoshnaa kar di.
 camran ne brallenos mahavidhyalay, oxford se darshanashastra, arthshaastra evam raajnneetishastra vishay main 1988 main pratham shrenee main snaatak kiya he. inhen inke padhaane vaale profesar varnon bogdenor "samarthatam chaatro main se ek" kaha karte the. apane oxford pravaas kaal main ye bullingdob club ke sadsy rahe the. inhene conzarvative anusandhan vibhaag jwine kiya or norman lemount evam maikel harvard ke vishesh salaahkaar bane. fir ye carlton communications ke karporate maamalon ke nideshak pad par saat varsh tak asin rahe.
 camran pehali baar sansad ke liye 1997 main staleford nirvachan kshetra se maidan main aae. ye eurosceptic manch par apni party se uski ekal-europea mudra main british sadsyta virdohi vichaardhaara ke kaaran usase alag ho gaye. haalanki rashtriya ausat voton se kuch nune rahane par ve haare bhi. iske baad unako sansad sadsyta hetu pratham vijay 2001 ke aam chunaavon main oxfordshire kii vittane nirvachan kshetra se mili. tab inhen aadhikarik vipaksh kaa sthaan mila. 2005 ke aam chunaavon main niti samanvay adhyaksh bhi bane. apni yuva evam udaaravaadi pratyaashee kii chhavi ke kaaran hi 2005 main inhone conzarvative party ke chunaavon main vijay pai. tab ye pratham baar britain ke pradhan mantribane.
 6 may, 2010 ko hue ke sanyukt rajshahi ke aam chunaavon main, conzarvative party ko 306 siten milin, kintu ek trishanku sansad bani. isamen paanch dinon ke libral-democratic party se baatcheet ke athak prayaas uparaant mili-juli sarkaar kaa gathan hua. tab camran ne dwitiya vishwa yuddh uparaant pehali bani mili-juli sarkaar kaa netritva kiya or 198 varsh purv ke robert jekinsons ke baad abtak ke sabase yuva pradhan mantri bane.
 inhone samentha camran se vivaah kiya, jinase inhen teen bachche hue. inke pehle putr ewan kaa 6 varsh kii aayu main hi dehanth ho gaya, jo shaareerik evam maansik rup se viklang tha. uske baad duusari putree nancy gwen kaa janm 2004 main hua evam teesri putree florence kaa janm cornwall main inke chuttee pravaas kaal main hua. uske saint adelion main janm hone ke kaaran iske naam kaa madhya bhaag adelion rakhaa gaya.
 2013 main channel 4 par inke ek kathananusaar ye nariwadi vichaaron ke hai., kintu fir bhi kuch logon kaa maananaa he ki ye jhooth bolaa gaya tha. ye pehle british pradhan mantri the jinhone jaliyanvaala baag kand ke liye saarvajanik rup se britain kii ore se maafi maangi.
