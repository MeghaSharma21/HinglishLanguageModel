 sar creek listen sahaayta·soochna bharat or pakistan kii seemaa par 96 kilometre lambi ek jwariya eschuri he, yah gujarat ke kuccha jile or pakistan me sindh kii seemaa se lagi hui he. yahan bharat or pakistan ke bich seemaa ko lekar vivaad he lekin yah vivaad kam hi charchaa me aata he. yah vivaad kuccha ke ran main 96 kilometre lambey muhane ko lekar he jo bharat ke gujarat ko pakistan ke sindh praant se alag karata he.
 sar creek kii khadi kaa vivaad se purana rishta rahaa he. east india company ke senapati charles napier ne 1842 me sindh jeetne ke baad us pradesh kaa prashasan mumbai rajya ko saunp diya tha. iske baad sindh me apana prashasan chalaa rahi angrez sarkaar ne sindh or mumbai praant ke bich ke seemaa rekha khinchi thi jo kuccha ke madhya se guzarati thi. isamen yah sampoorn khadi sindh praant me dikhaai gai thi. yani ki kuccha ke mul pradesh se use alag kar sindh me jod diya gaya tha. duusari taraf dilli ke angrez sarkaar apane adhikrut nakshe main sindh or kuccha ke bich kii simarekha ko kuccha ke ran tak khinchne ke baad khadi ke pradesh ke paas atka kar dikhaati thi.
 swatantraaa ke baad pakistan ne khadi ke pradesh par apana malikana haq jataayaa. iske jawaab main bharat kaa prastaav tha ki kuccha ke ran se lekar khadi ke mukh tak kii ek sidhi rekha ko seemaa rekha maan lenaa chahie. parantu yah prastaav pakistan ko manjuur nahi tha.
 is prakaar se sir kii khadi kaa kareeb 650 varg kilometre kaa pradesh aaj bhi vivaadit pradesh he or is pradesh se pakistani rangeron dwaara bhartiya machuaaron ko uthaa le jane kii khabaren aati rahati he. siachin ke baad sir crik bharat or pakistan ke bich tanaav badhaane vaali ek or simarekha he. lekin yah siachin jitani charchaa me nahi aati he.
