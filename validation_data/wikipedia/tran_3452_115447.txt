 me shivajiraje bhosle boltoy 2009 main bani marathi film ha. film ko 139 pardon par release kiya gaya tha or isane pehle hi hafte main 1.5 karod kamaaye jo ek marathi film ke liye sabase badi shuruyaat thi. isane kul 26 karod kaa vyavsaay kiya.
 film kii shuruyaat dinakar marutraav bhosle kii dincharya se shuru hoti he jo mumbai kaa ek aam madhyam vargeey bank main kaary karne vaala aadmi he. kahani main bataaya jaataa he kii kis tarah bhosle kabhi garvshaalee marashtriya vyakti ke ke girte vyaktimatv kaa pratinidhatv karata he. bhosle bina virodh kiye apni beijwati sahan karte rahata he.
 film ke geet "o raaje" se sukhwinder singh ne marathi filmon main padaarpan kiya.
