 charmi cor bhartiya film abhinetri he jo mukhyat telugu cinema main kaam karti he. charmi kaa janm ek sikh parivaar main mumbai main hua tha. 13 saal kii umr main unhonen ni todu kawali naam kii telugu film se apana filmi kariyar shuru kiya tha. unki pehali safal film kattuchembakam naam kii malayalam film thi. tabhi se wo kai safal dakshin bhartiya filmon main kaam kar chuki he. unki pehali hindi film buddha hoga tera baap thi, uske baad wo zila ghaziabad main dikhi. charmi ne aar rajkumaar main chemiyo bhi kiya tha. unhonen kai abhinetreeyon kii telugu dabing bhi kari he jaise-kaajal agarwal.
