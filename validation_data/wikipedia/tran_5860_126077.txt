 bharat sarkaar ke sabhi karyaalayon, upakramon, udhyamon, sansthaaon main hindi pakhwada har varsh 14 sitambar se 28 sitambar athwa 1 sitambar se 14 sitambar tak manaaya jaataa he. 14 sitambar ko hindi diwas ke rup main manaaya jaataa he. bharat sarkaar ke kendriya grih mantri ji kaa sandesh 14 sitambar ko prakashit kiya jaataa he. kedriya hindi samiti ke adhyaksh ke naate bharat ke pradhan mantri ji tathaa mahamahim raashtrapati ji kaa sandesh bhi jari kiya jaataa he. rajbhasha hindi ke prati jaagarukta paidaa karne ke liye hindi pakhwade ke dauraan anek hindi kaarykram, pratiyogitaayen, kavi sammelan, sangoshtee, bhartiya str par har vibhaag dwaara rajbhasha sammelan bhi yojit karne kaa praavdhaan he. hindi diwas ke uplakshya main hindi main adhik kaary karanevaale adhikariyon evam karmchaariyon ko sammanit kiya jaataa he. yah dekha jaataa he ki hindi pakhwaade ke dauraan karyaalayon main hindi patrachaar main kitna pratishat kaary batch gaya he. maa, sansadeey rajbhasha nirikshan samiti karyaalay kaa nirikshan karte samay is baat par uchit dhyaan detii he. hindi pakhwade kaa aayojan karyaalay kii suvidhanusaar hindi diwas ke pehle yaa baad main kiya jaataa he.
