 paali bharat ke haryana rajya ke riwaadi jile kaa sabase bada gram he. yah rewadi-narnaul rajmarg par riwaadi se 17 kimi par sthit he. yah sthaan yahan sthit baba nimadi vaale hanumanji mandir ke liye prasiddh rahaa he. is mandir ko tirth kii tarah mana jaataa he evam sthaneey logon ke man main yahaan ke liye apaar shraddha he. yah mandir bhoomi se 600 fit kii oonchai par paali gram se 1.5 ki.me dur sthit he.
 yah jile kaa sabase bada gram he evam isaki jansankhya lagbhag 5,000 he. inamen yaduwanshi ahiron ke kai gotra ke log nivas karte hai, kintu inamen kalaalia pramukh hai. inke ala yahaan harijan, brahman, naayee evam walamiki samudaay ke log bhi rahete hai.
 paali kii simaaen 13 graamon se lagati hai jinamein nangali, balahir, maajaraa bavana, kundal, gothra, saatu evam mamaria kuch hai. yahaan varsh 2009 main sthaneey panchayat ke bhrashtachar ke virodh main ek 'yuva vikaas samiti' gathit hui thi jisane safaltapurvak dhyey praapt kiya evam prasiddhi bhi pai.
 yahaan ke ahirwal gram ke prayek parivaar se kam se kam ek yuva ke bhartiya sashastr senaaon main sadsy bhartee hone kaa garv mahasoos karte hai. sarkaar is gram main sheeghra hi ek sainik skool kii sthapana karne vaali he. haryana shahari vikaas praadhikaran yahaan johad ke nikat awasaiya parisar kaa vikaas kar rahaa he. paali railway stesan ke nikat ek aitihaasik smarak bhi bana he jise nanad-bhavaj kii chhatari kaha jaataa he. yah 500 varsh se adhik pracheen he. 
 paali kii bhugolic sthiti 28°07′N 76°31′E / 28.12°N 76.51°E / 28.12; 76.51 par he.
