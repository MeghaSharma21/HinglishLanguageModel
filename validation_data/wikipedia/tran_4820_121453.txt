 
 
 brasles vimaanashetra  bhi kehete hai) brasles kaa anterrashtriya vimaanashetra he. yah brasles shahar kendra se 6 samudri mile uttar-purv disha main belgium main sthit he. yah vimaanashetra aanshik rup se zaventum main tathaa aanshik rup se macellen ke dijem, kshetra main sthit he. haalanki donon kshetra hi belgium ke flemish kshetra main hi aate hai.
 brasal vimaanashetra main 54 sampark dwaar, evam kul 109 dwaar hai. yahaan lagbhag 260 companiyon main sidhe taur par 20,000 log karyrat hai.
