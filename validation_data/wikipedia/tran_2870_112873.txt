 atiru praant madhya asia ke qaazakhstan desh kaa ek praant he. isaki rajdhani bhi atiru naam kaa shahar hi he. atiru praant desh ke pashchimi hisse main caspian sagar ke purvottaree kinare par sthit he.
 caspian droni main zamin ke neeche tel or gas baari maatra main maujuud hai isliye is praant main tel nikalne kaa kaam zoron se chalata he. yahan ke tengeez tel kshetra or kashakhaan​ tel kshetra se tel kii pipen tel ko anya ilaaqon main le jaati hai. inamen se ek paip tengeez se krishna sagar par rus kii novorossisk bandargah le jaati he, jahaan se ise duniya bhar main niryaat kiya jaataa he. kashakhaan​ tel kshetra caspian sagar ke pani ke neeche tat ke paas sthit he or yahan ek kritrim dwipon kaa samooh banaaya ja rahaa he jahaan tel nikalne vaale karmchaaree rahaa karenge.
 san 2009 kii janaganana ke hisaab se is praant ke 80% se adhik log kazakh samudaay ke the. inke alaava yahan roosi or anya samudaayon ke log bhi rahete hai.
