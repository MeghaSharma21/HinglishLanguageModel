 chaand raat hindi-urdu shabd he, jo bharat or pakistan main upyog hota he. chaand raat vah samay hota he, jab parivaar or mitra baahar khule jagah par nae chaand ko dekhte hai.
 nayaa chaand dekhne kii raat ko "chaand raat" kehete hai. is nae chaand ko dekhne kii raat ke dusre din se islamic calendar ke maheene kii shuruuaat hoti he. yani chaand raat us maheene kaa aakhri din hota he, dusre din se agle maheene kii pehali taarikh shuru hoti he. 
 yah naam sanskrit ke chandra  or raatri  shabd se bana he. jise hindi or urdu main chaand or raat kehete hai.
