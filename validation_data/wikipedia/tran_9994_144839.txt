 delli rajhara judawaan khaane hai or rajhara khan samooh kaa hissaa he. yah captive louh ayask khaane bhilai ispaat sanyantr - ek cell udhyam ke liye hai. vibhinn prakaar ke louh ayask hematit or magnetic is kshetra se khanan hote hai. pados main anya khaanon kaa utpaadan dolomite, chuunaa or anya kachche maal jo ispaat utpaadan main jate hai. delli rajhara durg se 83 kimi dakshin main sthit he, baalod se yah 25 kimi main he or bhartiya railway ke purvi rail ke bhaag main aata he. jabaki donon delli or rajhara main khaane hai, awasaiya kshetra rajhara main mukhya rup se he. aspataalon or shikshan sansthaanon ke kaaphi sankhya main sthapit hone delli rajhara khud ko bispi ke saath paryaapt basti he. delli rajhara sattar ke dashak main shram adhikaron ke andolan par prasiddhi hua tha. ek sandhya par shahar main pravesh karte samay open cast khaanon vaale pahaado par bhavy roshni dekhte hi banati he.
 yatayat:
 yah kshetra rail dwaara jila durg ke saath or sadak ke dwaara judaa hua he. yah acchhee tarah se bas se durg or bastar kshetra se judaa he. sadak bahut kharaab sthiti main he. yahaan ek yatri train delli or durg ke bich main chalati he. evam delli rajhara se raipur express saptaah main teen din chalati he adhik vikaas ke liye rail sampark kii aavashyaktaa he. jagadalpur ke saath rail maarg prastaavit he.
 rahan-sahan:adhikanshat: jansankhya bispi par nirbhar he, bispi ek saarvajanik kshetra kii company hone ke kaaran puure desh ke logon ko naukari ke liye aakarshith karata he. puure bharat se bihar, bengal, kerala, maharashtra, uttar pradesh, urisa, madhya pradesh ke log yahan rojgaar ke liye aate hai. yahan kii samskruti maamuli madhyam varg he. jivan thodaa stareey ek yaa metro shahar kii tulana main aaraam he. id, durgapuja se chhath puja ko olym ke liye christmas, sabhi gahari bhagidaari or utsaah ke saath manaaya lekar tyohaar. sankshep main, samskruti jivan style main vinamrata kaa ek swaad ke saath kaaphi cosmo he. jeene ke mulya kam hai or isliye yahan rahane vaale logon ke liye chintaa kaa vishay kabhi nahin gaya he.
 vaise bhi bhilai steel plant, sabhi samudaay ke logon kii khan he yahan rahete hai. tum mandir, masjid, gurudware or carch paa sakate hai. vahaan bharat ke sabhi hisson se log hai. vahaan saamaajik gatividhiyon ke liye vibhinn club hai. shivi ke durga puja, anand mela, saptagiri park main bispi dwaara yojit flower sho mela har saal dekhnaa chahie.
 shiksha:yahan snaatak or snatakottar kii oopaadhi ke liye ek college nemichand jain vigyaan evam kala maha-vidhyalay he.yahan kai bi es pi or rajya sarkaar dwaara chalaaye ja rahe praathmik or ucch madhyamik vidhyalay hai.

 samaaj:
 bhilai ispaat sanyantr ke kaaran, sabhi samudaay ke logon kii khaanon he yahan rahete hai. tum mandir, masjid, gurudware paa sakate hai. vahaan bharat ke sabhi hisson se log hai. aap kisi bhi samasya ko apni maatrubhaasha main baat kar nahin hoga. vahaan saamaajik gatividhiyon ke liye vibhinn klbon rahe hai. durga puja shivi ke 1 jameen, anand mela basapa dwaara saptgiri park main yojit kii har saal kii ghatnaon dekhnaa hoga rahe hai.
 yuva:khel jaise footbal, cricket or athletics, delli raccra main kaaphi main lokapriy hai. san 80 ke dauraan vaarshik louh ayask footbal toornament ke dauraan bahut lokapriy tha. kai sheersh bharat bhar se is toornament main pratispardhaa hoti footbal klbon nishaan. ek hi praayojan ke antargat, vajan jaise khelon-uthaanaa, athletics main bhi ek acha nimnlikhit tha.
 prakruti:delli rajhara ko prachur maatra main prakritik sundarataa se nawaaza gaya he. rajhara ke paas boirdih baandh evam kusumaksaa se pashcham disha main 7 kimi duuri par devpandum naamak vihangam sthaan ek sundar picnic sthal he. aasapaas ke pahaado or jangalon vanya jivan kii kai prajatiyon ke liye ghar kar rahe hai.
