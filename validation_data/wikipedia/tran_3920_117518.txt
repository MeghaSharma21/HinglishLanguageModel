 kaks athwa cocks rajnatik dalon yaa pratyaashiyon ke upar unke samarthakon dwaara bahes karne kii baithak ko kehete hai. is shabd kii utpatti sambhawatah sanyukt rajya america main hui thi. sanyukt rajya america kii raajnneeti or sarkaar main cocks kii baithkon kaa prayog praayah niti-nirdhaaran yaa rajnatik pratyaashiyon ke chunaav ke liye kiya jaataa he.
