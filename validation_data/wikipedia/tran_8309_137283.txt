 jimikand ek bahuvarshiya bhumigat sabji he jiskaa varnan bhartiya dharmagranthon main bhi paaya jaataa he. bharat ke vibhinn rajyon main jimikand ke bhinn-bhinn naam ol yaa suuran hai. pehle ise grihavatika main yaa gharon ke agal-bagal kii jameen main hi ugaaya jaataa tha. parantu ab to jimikand kii vyavsaayik kheti hone lagi he. jimikand ek sabji hi nahin varan yah ek bahumoolya jadibuti he jo sabhi ko swasth evam nirog rakhane main madad karata he.
 bhojya padaarthon ke sanchan hetu yah bhumigat tana kaa roopaantar he jise ghanakand kehete hai. yah parivartit tana bahut adhik jaisaa-taisa phula rahata he evam isaki sataha par parvasandhiyaan rahati hai jinpar shalk-patr lage rahete hai. sataha par jahaan-tahaan apsthaanik jaden lagi rahati hai. agle sire par agrakalika tathaa shalkapatron ke aksh par choti-choti kalikas hoti hai.is paudhe kaa fal yani jad ko bavasir ke dawa main bhi upyog kiya jaataa he.
