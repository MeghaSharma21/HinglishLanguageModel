 neples kii khadi, italy ke dakshin pashchimi tat par neples praant ke compania kshetra main sthit ek 10 mile chaudee khadi he. yah pashchim main bumadhya sagar se milti he. iske uttar main neples or pozzuoli shahar, purv main vesuviyas parvat or dakshin main sorentine prayadweep sthit he. is prayadweep kaa sabase pramukh shahar soranto he. yah prayadweep neples kii khadi ko salerno kii khadi se alag karata he.
 is khadi main kapri, iskia or prochida dweep sthit hai. yah italy kaa ek pramukh paryatak sthal he kyonki iske nikat pompei or harkulenium ke roman bhagnavshesh sthit he, yah nagar san 79 i. vesuviyas jwalamukhi ke udgaar ke parinamswaroop nasht ho gayi the.
 is khadi main 1960 main rome main hue greeshmakaleen olympic ke dauraan naukayan pratiyogitaayen yojit kii gayi thim.
