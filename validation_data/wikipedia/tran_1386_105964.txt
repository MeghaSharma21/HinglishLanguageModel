 jon cleton meyar ek america sangitajna he. ye kannectict ke bridgeport main paley bade or boston ke barkali college of music main sangeet kii shiksha pai. tatpashchaat san 1997 main georgia ke atlanta shahar main sthanantarit ho gaye, jahaan inhonen apni hunar ko maanja-sanvaara or khyaati arjeet kii. unke pehle do studio album, room for squayres or haviour things ne multi-platinum kaa darja praapt karte hue vyavsaayik str par acchhee kamai kii. san 2003 main unhone "yor body ij a vanderland" ke liye best mail pop vocal parformens grami award jeeta.
 meyar ne dhvanik rock pradarshan se apane kariyar kii shuruyaat kii. lekin dhire-dhire san 2005 main bluez jiner ke prasiddh kalakaaron jaise ki bi.bi.king, bady gaai tathaa erik clapton ke saath kaary karte hue, tathaa jon meyar trayee kaa gathan kar, bluez sangeet style kii disha main sankramit hone lage. bluez sangeet style kaa sur kaa prabhaav unke elbum continnam par suna ja saktaa he, jo sitambar,2006 main relies kiya gaya. san 2007 ke 49wein vaarshik grami award main meyar ne continnam ke liye best pop vocal album tathaa "vatting on da world tu change" ke liye best mail pop vocal parformens ke kitaab jite. navambar 2009 main unhone apana chauthaa studio album, battle studies release kiya.
 meyar ke karier kaa vistaar comidi, dizine or lekhan jaisi kalaon tak rahaa; unhone kai patrikaon ke liye choti-choti rachanaaen likhin hai, jinamein unhone esquaire ke liye vishesh rup se likhaa. "back tu yu" kosh ke madhyam se ve paropkaaree gatividhiyon main bhi shaamil hai. kai nami-garaami aakarshak romantic sambandhon or media ke saath unki gatividhiyon ne unhen akhabaari surkhiyaan pradaan kii jisaki shuruyaat san 2006 main hui.
 meyar kaa janm 16 oktober 1977 ko angrezi kii adhyapika margaret or hai skool ke principal richard se kannectict ke bridgeport shahar main hua tha. apane mata-pita kii teen santanon main ve duusari santan the jinaki parvarish kannectict ke feirefield main hui. pita kii ore se meyar yahudee hai or unka kahana he ki "yahudee dharm se unka naata he". feirefield main bade hote hue unki mitrataa bhavishya ke tennis star james blike ke saath ho gai. unhone bhootpurv feirefield hai skool main daakhila le liya, haalanki apane kishoravastha ke varshon main unhone norwalk sthit bryan macmohan hai skool ke centre for global studies main daakhila liya tha. let nite wyeth conen o' bryan ke ek pradarshan ke dauraan upasthiti main, meyar ne kaha ki choti-moti safalataa ke saath unhone mids skool main thodi der ke liye clerinet bajaayi thi. back tu da future main marti mikeflaai ke rup main maaikel je fox ke guitar pradarshan ko dekhakar, meyar us vaadyayantra ke prati mohit ho gaye or jab ve 13 saal ke hue, tab unke pita ne unke liye kiraye par ek yantra laa diya tha.
 sheeghra hi, ek padosi ne unhen ek steve re won kaa ek casette laakar diya jisane meyar ke dil main bluez ke prati gahan lagaav paidaa kar diya.a meyar ne ek sthaneey guitar ke dukandaar se sabak lenaa shuru kar diya or sheeghra hi is vaadyayantra ke bajaane kii kala ko aatmsaat kar liya. unka yah ekachitt lagan lagan unke mata-pita ke liye chintaa kaa vishay ban gaya or ve unhen lekar ek manochikitsak ke paas do baar gaye - lekin meyar is baat ke prati drudh pratijna the ki ve bilkul thik-thaak hai. do varshon ke sangeet abhyaas ke baad, unhone bluez kii madhushaalaon tathaa us kshetra vibhinn sthanon par bhi bajaanaa shuru kiya, jabaki ve abhi bhi hai skool main adhyayan-rat the. ekal pradarshan ke alaava, ve ek band ke sadsy bhi the jiskaa naam tha villanova jankshan, jisamen tim procassni, jo belejane tathaa rich woolf bhi shaamil the. apane sangeet ko aage badhane ke liye meyar ne kabhi-kabhi college main anupasthit rahane ke bare main soch liya, lekin unke mata-pita kii asweekruti ne unhen aisa karne se roak liya.
 jab meyar satraa saal ke the, ve hriday atalata rog se aakraant ho gaye or unhen ek saptaah ke liye aspataal main bhartee hona padaa. is ghatana par apni pratikriya vyakt karte hue meyar ne kaha he, "yah vah pal tha jab mere andar ek geetkaar kaa janm hua" or unhone aspataal se ghar lautne par usee raat ko apana pehla geet rachaa. uske kuch dinon baad sheeghra hi, ve aatank ke akraman se peedit hone lage or kisi maansik sanstha main bhartee ho jane ke bhay ke palon main jeene lage. ve maansik avsaad ke aise prakarnon ko avsaad pratirodhi dawaaiyon kaa seven karte hue lagaataar apane aapako sambhalate rahe. meyar kaa kahana he ki unke mata-pita ke vivaah kii jhagadaalu pravriti ne unhen "unase ojhal kar diya or maine apni hi ek ansi duniya bana lee jisamen meri aasthaa bani rahe." snaatak-str tak kii padhai ke baad ek gas stesan par ve pandrah maheenon tak kaam karte rahe, jabatak ki unhone itnaa adhik dhan kama nahin liya ki ve 1996 steve re won signechar stretocaster kharid saken.
 jon meyar ne unniis varsh kii umr main masachusets ke boston shahar main barkali college of music main daakhila liya. haalanki ve apane college ke dost or atlanta, georgia nivasee, clay cook ke aagrah par do satron ke baad atlanta ravaanaa ho gaye. atlanta main un donon ne milakar do sadasyon vaala lo fi masters naam se ek band banaaya or sthaneey kahavaa gharon tathaa, pados ke klbon jaise eddy'za atic main apana pradarshan prarambh kar diya. cook ke kathananusaar meyar kii pop sangeet kii ore unmukhataa ke kaaran ve sangeet bhinnataaon kaa anubhav paane lage. parinamswaroop donon alag ho gaye or meyar apane ekal kariyar kii disha main agrasar ho gaye.
 sthaneey nirmaataa or ejeeniyar glen maitullo ke sahyog se, meyar ne swatantra EP insaide vonts out record karaayaa. cook ne EP ke kai geeton kaa sah-lekhan kiya jisamen meyar kaa pehla vanijyik ekal prasaaran, "no sach thing" bhi shaamil tha. is EP main meyar ke mukhya kanth-swar or guitar par dhun ke saath aath gane shaamil the. haalanki, meyar ne keval "kamphortable" ke geet main hi apane kanth-swar kaa hi yogadan diya. "back tu yu" ke aarambhik sangeet ke liye ek pure band kii suchi banaai gai, jisamen base guitar EP ke sah-nirmaataa david "daily laa" labruyar bhi shaamil the. tab meyar or laa bruyar ne milakar puure georgia or najdiki rajyon main pradarshan karnaa prarambh kar diya.
 meyar kii pratishtha badhane lagi or march 2000 ke south bai southwest main pradarshan ne unke prati "launch" label, aware awards kaa dhyaan aakrisht kiya. aware festival ke sangeet samaaroh main unhen shaamil kiye jane ke baad tathaa unke gaanon ko san 2001 ke aarambh main aware ke sankalnon main sangrahit kiye jane ke baad aware ne meyar kaa room for squayres sheershak se keval internet album release kiya. usee vakt ke dauraan, aware ne colombia records ke saath ek samjhaute par hastakshar kiya, jisane colombia ko aware kalakaaron main se chunane kii praathamikta pradaan kii or isiilie usee varsh sitambar ke maheene main, colombia ne room for squayres kaa remex release kiya. pramukh label ke "praveshank" ke ek ansh ke rup main, album kii kalatmak sajja ko naveenta pradaan kii gai, evam "3x5" trek ko sammilit kiya gaya. punah release main uske indi rock album insaide vonts out ke pratham sampadit studio sanskaran bhi shaamil the.
 san 2002 ke ant tak, room for squayres se kai radio hit gane nikle, jinamein "no sach thing", "yor body iz a vanderland" or ant main "whai georgia" bhi shaamil hai. san 2003 main, meyar ne "yor body iz a vanderland" ke liye sarvottam purush pop gayan pradarshan kaa grami purushkaar jeeta. apane sweekruti abhibhaashan main unhone charchaa karte hue kaha, "yah bahut hi tej he or main vaada karata hum ki ise zarur pakad luungaa." unhone apane bare main vyangyatmak dhang se kaha ki abhi to ve solah ke hi hai, yah ek ansi tippanee thi jiskaa logon ne us vakt unki umr sirf solah kii hone kaa galat arth lagaaya.
 san 2003 main, meyar ne albama, birmingham main hue ek sangeet samaaroh ke sidhe prasaaran kii ani giveinn tharsade sheershak CD or DVD release kii. is sangeet samaaroh main aise kuch aise gane pradarshit kiye gaye jo pehle record nahin kiye gaye the, jaise ki "main on da said" tathaa "samthings missing" jo baad main haviour things main pradarshit hue. is sangeet samaaroh main "kaward in rain ko bhi shaamil kiya gaya". saath hi saath pesh kiye jane vaale DVD ke vruttchitr ke anusaar yah gaanaa "city lav" ke gane kaa "dwittiya bhaag" hai, jisamen "kaward in rain" kii panktiyaan bhi shaamil hai. vanijyik str par yah album teji se billboard 200 ke chart par satrahawein paaydaan kii oonchai par pahunch gaya. unki CD/DVD ko un aalochakon se rudhivaadee par samanurup anukool prashansa mili jo unke pop kii adarsh pratimurti tathaa kushal guitar vadak kii ubhartee hui pratibha ke bich vibhajit the. erik crowford ne poocha, "kya ve keval udaahraneey utkrisht guitar vadak hi nahin lagate, jab ve steve re won ke 'lenni' ek cover bajaate hai, athwa kya ve keval navayuvakon kii adarsh pratimurti nahin lagate jab yauvan kii dahleez par kadam rakhane vaali tarunia "yor body iz a vanderland" ke bajaane ke baad cheekhti-chillaati or kilkaariyaan maarthi hai.
 meyar kaa doosraa album haviour things san 2003 main release kiya gaya jisaki aamtaur par anukool samiksha hui. roling stone, olmugec or blendar haalanki sabhi ke bare main arakshit hi sahi lekin sakaaratmak pratipushti milin. popematters patrika ne kaha ki, "jitani koi dharna kar saktaa he, utani khamiyaan isamen nahin hai." vyavsaayik str par yah album safal rahaa or jabaki room for squayres jitani isaki bikri nahin hui, phir bhi yah america ke billboard 200 ke chart main number van kii oonchai par pahunch gaya. meyar ne apane ekal dotrs ke gaanon ke liye number van kii khyaati arjeet to kii hi saath hi saath 2005 ke grami main apane saathi daavedaaron elicia keez or kanye west ko peeche chhodte hue saang of da year kaa khitaab jeeta. unhone is puraskaar ko apni daadi maa, eni hoffmen ko samarpit kiya, jinka swargvaas may 2004 main ho gaya. unhone sarvottam purush pop gayan pradarshan kaa khitaab bhi puraskaar ke anya daavedaaron elvis costelo, prince evam sil ko haraakar jeeta. 9 farvari 2009 ko da ellen di janers sho ke ek saakshaatkaar main, meyar ne kaha ki unhone sochaa tha ki saang of da year ke liye grami nahin jeetna chahie tha kyonki unke khyaal se elicia keez kaa iff aai ent got you behatar geet tha. is kaaran unhone gremi ke upar kaa aadha hissaa hataa kar keez ko de diya or neeche kaa hissaa apane liye rakhaa. san 2006 main, 37wein vaarshik ke geetkaaron ke haul of fame indection award samaaroh main meyar ko haal david starlite puraskaar se nawaaza gaya.
 meyar ne punah san 2004 main kiye gaye america daure ke saat raaton ke sangeet samarohon ke kaaryakramon ke sidhe prasaranon ko record kiya. aiz/iz ke sheershak se in recordingus ko ai tun mujeec store ne is baat kii soochna dete hue release kiya ki achche palon ke saath hi galatiyon ko rahane diya gaya he. kuch maheene baad, aiz/iz nits ke geeton ko sankalit kar "sarvottam" CD taiyaar kii gai. is album main marvin ge ke gane "innar city bluez ", ke pehle hi aprasaarit cover ko shaamil kiya gaya, jisamen meyar kaa ekal abhinay samarthan jaaz or bluez tuntablist, di je logic pradarshit kiye gaye. aiz/iz release ke saare albamon ke cover par praaniyon ke maanvikaran karne hue maanavtarochi chitrakaariyaan darshaayi gai hai.
 pratibha ke prasar or adhik ujagar hone ke saath hi meyar kii maang duusare kshetron main bhi hone lagi. steve jobs ne janvari 2004 main yojit appal ke vaarshik mecoverlds conference and expo main mukhyavaktaa ke bhaashan ke dauraan pradarshan ke liye aamantrit kiya, tabhi jobs ne garrej band ke liye software ke anuprayog se parichit karavaaya. is sangithik pradarshan se meyar kaarykram ke sthaayee pahalu banate chale gaye. mecoworld 2007 ke ek kaarykram main iphone kii ghoshnaa kaa anupaalan karte hue ekal pradarshan ke liye ve jobs ke saath manch par phir se saath ho gaye. meyar ne vigyaapan bhi kiye hai, jaise ki betals guitar ke prachaar-prasar tathaa blike beri karv ke liye volkswagon vanijyik vigyaapan ke rup main.
 meyar ne bade paimaane par sahyog kii disha main kadam badha die, aksar un kalakaaron ke saath kaam karne lage jo unki style ke nahin the. ve kaaman ke gane "go!" main dikhaai die. or kanye west ke "bitaraswit poetry" main dikhe.b in sahyogitaaon ke kaaran meyar rap havivents je-jed tathaa neli kii prashansa ke paatr bane. jab hip hop samudaay main unki maujuudagi ke bare main poocha gaya tab meyar ne kaha, "abhi philhal keval sangeet hi nahin he". yahi kaaran he ki mere liye abhi hip hop vahiin he jo kabhi rock hua karata tha.
