 uttrakhand ke mukhya nyaayadhish uttrakhand rajya ke sarvocch peethasin nyaayik adhikari or uttrakhand ucch nyaayalay ke sangrakshak hote hai. unki niyukti bharat ke raashtrapati dwaara bharat ke mukhya nyaayadhish or uttrakhand ke rajyapal kii salaah par kii jaati he. ke em joseph uttrakhand ucch nyaayalay ke vartmaan mukhya nyaayadhish hai. unhone yah pad 31 july 2014 ko grahan kiya.
