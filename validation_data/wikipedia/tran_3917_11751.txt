 reeta bahuguna joshi,bharat ke uttar pradesh kii uttar pradesh kii solahavin vidhaan sabha main vidhayak raheen. 2012 uttar pradesh vidhaan sabha chunaav main inhone uttar pradesh kii lakhanu cantoment vidhaan sabha nirvachan kshetra, uttar pradesh se chunaav jeeta. 
 reeta bahuguna joshi uttar pradesh ke purv mukhyamantri swargeey ech. en. bahuguna kii beti he. vah mahila rashtriya parishad ke purv upaadhyaksh hai, 2003 se akhil bhartiya mahila congress ke adhyaksh or 2007 ke uttar pradesh congress samiti ke adhyaksh rahe hai. 2012 ke vidhansabha chunaavon main unhen lakhanu chhavnee ke liye vidhaan sabha kaa sadsy chunaa gaya tha.
