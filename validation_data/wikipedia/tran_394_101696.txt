 sanjeev kumaar sharma, 1988 se 1997 ke bich 22 test or 93 ekadivasiy maichon main khilne vaale ek purv bhartiya cricket khilaadi hai. vanade main unka sarvshereshth gendbaaji aankada 1988 ke champions trophy ke dauraan sharajah main westeindies ke khilaaf 6/21 he.
 unhone bharat ke liye 22 test match khele or 8 unka ucchtam test score tha. jab unhone apana test kaa ucchtam score kiya, tab vah 10wein kramank par ballebaaji kar rahen the. or unka bhartiya pari kaa sheersh score bhi tha.
 vah vartmaan main nai dilli ke hari nagar main "sanjeev sharma cricket academy" naamak apni academy main mukhya coach hai. vah vartmaan main dilli kii ranjai trophy team ke coach bhi hai.
