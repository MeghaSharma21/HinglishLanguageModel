 mahabhoj mannu bhandari dwaara likhaa gaya ek hindi upanyaas he. unyas main aparaadh or raajnneeti ke gathjod kaa yathartwaadee chithran kiya gaya he.
 mahabhoj upanyaas kaa taana-bana saroha naamak gaanv ke ird-gird buna gaya he. saroha gaanv uttar pradesh ke pashchimi bhaag main sthit he, jahaan vidhaan sabha kii ek seat ke liye chunaav hone vaala he. kahani bisesar urf bisu kii maut kii ghatana se prarambh hoti he. saroha gaanv kii harijan basti main aagjanee kii ghatana main darjanon vyaktiyon kii nirmam hatya ho chuki thi. bisu ke paas is hatyakand ke pramaan the, jinhen vah dilli jaakar sakasham praadhikariyon ko saunpna or basti ke logon ko nyaay dilaanaa chahta tha. kintu raajnneetik shadyantra dwaara bisu ko hi jail main daal kar is pratirodh ko kuchal dene kaa prayaas kiya jaataa he. bisu kii maut ke pashchaat uskaa saathi bindeshwari urf bindaa is pratirodh ko zindaa rakhata he. bindaa ko bhi raajnneeti or aparaadh ke chakra main phasaakar salaakhon ke peeche daal diya jaataa he. antat naukarshahi varg kaa hi ek charitra, police adheekshak saxena, vanchiton ke pratirodh ko jari rakhata he. jatigat sameekaran kis prakaar bhartiya sthaneey raajnneeti kaa anivaary ang ban gayi hai, upanyaas kii kendriya chintaa he. pichhadi or vanchit jati ke logon ke saath atyaachaar or pratinidhi charitron dwaara uskaa pratirodh katha ko gati pradaan karata he. bhandari ne upanyaas main naitikta, antardvandv, antarvirodh se jujhate sattadhari varg, satta pratipaksh, media or naukarshahi varg ke avasarwaadi charitra par vyangyatmak tippanee kii he.
