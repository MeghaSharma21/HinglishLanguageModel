 pandit brahmadatt 'jigyaasu' sanskrit ke vidwaan tathaa vaidic sahitya ke shikshak the. unhen 'rashtriya pandit' kii padavi se sammanit kiya gaya tha. unhone sanskrit pathan-pathan kii saralatam vidhi viksit kii thi jo panini ke ashtaadhi par aadhaarit tha. 
 pandit brahmadatt jigyaasu kaa janm punjab ke jalandhar jile ke mallupota banga main hua tha. unka mul naam 'labbhuram' tha. 'brahmadatt' naam unke guru dwaara diya gaya naam tha. unke pita ji kaa naam randas saraswat pathak or mataji kaa naam shrimati parameshwari devi tha. 
 nau varsh kii alpaayu main hi unke mata-pita kaa dehant ho gaya. aryasamaj ke kisi sajjan ne unhen 10veen kaksha tak shikshit hone main sahaayta kii. 1912 main unhone ghar tyaag diya tathaa apane guru swami purnanand se panineey vyakaran kii shiksha lee tathaa kaashi main vedang, upang, tathaa vaidic sahitya kaa gahan adhyayan kiya.
