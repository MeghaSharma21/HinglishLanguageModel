 esquimau, canada va greenland ke tundra pradesh main nivas main karne vaali ek pramukh janajati hai. 
 uttari dhruviya kshetra ke chaaro ore greenland se lekar pashchim main alaska or bering jaldamru madhya ke paar siberia ke uttari-purvi chukchi prayadweep kshetra tak ek patali pattee main esquimau logo kaa nivas paaya jaataa hai.
 inke nivas barf kii silliyon ko ardhgolakaar rup main jodakar banaae jate hai jinhe iglu kaha jaataa hai.
 == bhojan= ye log mukhya rup se sil machhlee kaa harpun naamak haddiyon se bane bhaale se shikaar karaten hai tathaa uskaa maance khate hai
 ye vastra caribo kii khaal se bane hote hai. ye vastra sil machhlee kii khaal kii apeksha adhik garm evam halqe hote hai. dhurviy bhaaluu ke samoor se bhi vastra banaaye jate hai.
