 palang ek prakaar kaa furniture hota he jise sone evam letne ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he. palang ke do mukhya bhaag hote hai, palang kaa frem, jo lakdi athwa kisi dhaatu se banaaya jaataa he, evam uske upar rakhaa gadda. palang par aam taur par chadar bichaai jaati he evam takiye rakhe jate hai. palang ek yaa adhik vyaktiyon ke liye banaae ja sakate hai. ek evam do vyaktiyon vaale palang aam taur par paae ja sakate hai. kai palangon main sar tikaane ke liye frem main peeche siraanaa bana hota he, jisamen saamaan rakhane ke liye shelf bhi bana ho saktaa he. kuch palangon main neeche saamaan rakhane ke liye bakse bhi bane hote hai.yah ek bhautik sukh kaa sansaadhan he! is par sharir kii thaan ko dur karne ke liye soya ja saktaa he kintu neend prakritik he! is liye neend aana aavashyambhavi nahi he!
