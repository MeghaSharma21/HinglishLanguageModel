 hisa hilaal ek saudi arab kii kavi he. ve pehle chhadam naam remia ke tahat prakashit hoti thi. usne arab duniya ke baahar prasiddhi praapt kii jab usne ek amariti rialti televijan kavita pratiyogita, million kavi par fatwaon ke khilaaf kavita sunai, or kaarykram ke final tak pahunchane vaali pehali mahila bani.
 hilaa, jiskaa puraa naam, hisa hilaal al-malihaan al-ulji he, saudi arab ke uttar-pashchim main jordan ke paas, ek bedauin samudaay main paidaa hui thi, or 12 varsh kii umr main kavita likhane lagi or lekhan or nyaay ke theme bhi uski lekhnee main shaamil the. vah apni kavitaayen apane parivaar se chipa kar rakhti, jise kavita sweekaar nahin thi. vah bahrain main hai skool gai, jahaan us ne clasik angrezi sahitya padhaa, lekin vittiya kaarnon se vishvavidhyalay main padhai naa kar pai.
 riyaad main ek aspataal main ek lipic sthiti main kaam karte hue hilaal apni kuch kavitaaon ko saudi akhabaaron or patrikaon main prakashit kar paa rahi thi or apni pehali bikri se fax mashin kharidne ke liye paise kaa upyog kiya taaki vah ghar se arts lekh likh sake. hilaal ne saudi arab or faras kii khadi kshetra main kai akhbaaron or patrikaon ke liye ek sampadak or samvaadadaata ke rup main kaam kiya, or al-hayaat kii kavita sampadak bhi thi. usne do kavita sangrah prakashit, ret ke dher kii bhashaa  or da bidiud van. is samay ke dauraan, vah chhadam naam "remia" ke tahat likhti thi.
 hilaal kaa kahana he ki shaadi se uske parivaar se use or adhik rachanaatmak swatantraaa mili he, or uske chaar bachche sthirataa kaa strot hai. uskaa pati bhi ek kavi he. hilaal lakhon kaa kavi ke pehle ke satron par pratispardhaa karnaa chaahati tha, lekin ek saudi mahila ke rup main desh se baahar yatra karne kii pati se likhit anumati aavashyaktaa hoti he. unke pati ne anumati dene se inkaar to nahin kiya lekin vah yah dene main sankoch karata tha. yah chauthe sizan ke liye tha ki usne use anumati di thi. 
 maine vidhvansak fatwon kii ankhon se buraai dekhi haiaise ek samay jab jo vaid he, vah avaidh se bhramit haijab main sachai ko benakaab karata hum,to soch or kerani main barbar, naaraaz or andhamaut kii poshaak pahane or belt se ise lapete hue ek rakshas apni chhipanagaah se nikalataa haiveh ek aadhikarik, shaktishaalee manch se bolta he, logon main aatank failaata he or shanti chaahane vaale harek kaa shikaar karata he.
 hilaal or uski kavita donon kii nyaayadhishon or lakhon ke kavi ke darshakon dwaara utsaahpurvak prashansa kii gai. ek nyaayadhish ne kaha, "uski takat chhaviyon ke avishkaar main nihit he ... uski kavita shaktishaalee he. vah hameshaa vivaadit vishyon par bhi sandesh or majboot raay detii he." pratiyogita se hilaal kii sabase prasiddh kavita "fatwaon kii afaraatafari" thi" usne raimud dactells main "barbar" maulaviyon kii, jo uske desh ko chalaate hai, hinsa kii ninda karte hue or unke kattarpanthi rukh se utpann adhikaron ke pratibandhon kii aalochana karte hue aalochana kii. is kavita ko shaikh abdul-rahamaan al-barake dwaara haalia tippaniyon ke liye vishesh rup se pratikriya dene ke rup main dekha gaya tha, jisamen sex ekikaran ke samarthakon ko maut dene ko kaha gaya tha. hilaal ko is kavita ke liye online maut kii dhamkee mili. vah kahati he ki vah kattarpanthi maulaviyon ke vivaran ke liye apni kavitaaon main uttejk bhashaa or manzarkashi kaa prayog karti he ki aatmghaatee hamalaavaron kii ek chhavi kaa abhas detii he, kyonki "ugravad itnaa majboot he or aap iske bare main kisi anya tareeke se baat nahin kar sakate hai." baad ke saptaah ko hilaal kii kavita isi tarah ke theme par 15 chand the, or use raund ke sheersh score, final main ek sthaan mila or uske saahas ke liye nyaayadhishon kii prashansa mili..
 antim daur main hilaal kii kavita ne kaha ki media, ek vishay jise nyaayadhishon ne chunaa tha, kaa istemaal agyaan or sensorship se ladane ke liye kiya ja saktaa he. "main prakaash kii ladaai main prakaash ke pakshiyon main shaamil hum, ham ek aise sansar ke saath badhanaa chaahate hai jo uski agyanta se lard rahaa he."
 hilaal pratiyogita main tisare sthaan par ai, tees lakh dirham jitkar or adhik mahila shrotaaon ke sadasyon ko pehle kabhi se kahin zyada final main bhaag lene ke liye prerit kiya. unki antim pravishti kavi se unki kavitaaon kaa ek pataa thi: "aapke paas ek laharaata pankh he / aapke khule aasmaan aapako dhokhaa nahin denge." usne jajon ke panel se sarvocch score praapt kiya, jo ki pratiyogi ke antim score ke 60% ko gina jaataa he, lekin pratiyogita jeetne ke liye paryaapt rup se darshakon ke vot us ko nahin mili.
 da independent ne likhaa he ki "lakhon kaa kavi, roodhiwaad ke swaroop ko dekhte hue us ke sandesh ke liye ek vishesh rup se ullekhneey sthal he," jo paaramparik kavita ko badhaava detaa he or adhik pashchim-prabhavit pratibha sho ke mukaable vyapak or adhik roodhiwaadee darshakon tak pahunch saktaa he; kyonki style sammanitore paaramparik he, saamagree simaaon ko aage badhaane main sakasham he.hilaal kahati he ki chunki ativaadee maulavee " har kisi ke maanas main gahare dharmik padon or ijhaaron," kaa upyog karke samarthan jutaaye rakhane main sakasham hai, udaarvaadiyon ko aadhunik bhashaa, jis se log jud nahin pate, kii bajaay un jaise hi muhaavarey kaa upyog karnaa chahie.vishleshkon ne suzav diya he ki kavita ke is rup kaa istemaal saamaajik samasyaon par charchaa karne ke liye bad kar kiya ja rahaa he or hilaal kii sahabhaagita kii sambhaavna is pravriti ko or aage le jaaegi.
 kaarykram par naakaab pahane hilaal kii upasthiti media main darj kii gai thi. usne kaha ki usne aisa kiya he ki uske purush rishtedaar, jo unki kavita kaa samarthan karte hai, kii anya purushon ke duvaara aalochana nahin kii jaaegi, or unhen aashaa thi ki unki betiyon ko unke chehre ko cover nahin karnaa padegaa. hilaal ne kaha ki saudi arab ke baahar nekaab pahane yatra karte vakt unke anubhav us prakiya main shaamil hai jis se "fatwaon kii araajaktaa" kii rachana hui; pashchimi deshon se mili nakaaratmak pratikriyaaon unhen yah sochane kii taraf le gai ki kaise apane dharm ke ugravadiyon ne sabhi musalamaanon ko ek bura naam diya he.
 million kaa kavi main bhaag lene ke baad hilaal ne kai or kitaaben prakashit kin.
 talaak or khulu 'kavy: adivasee sosayadi main mahilaon kii sthiti kii ek reeding or ek sakshi ke rup main nabati kavita, bedion mahilaon dwaara 1 9 50 se pehle likhi jane vaali kavitaaon kaa ek sangrah he. hilaal ne is sangrah ko sampadit kiya, jisamen vah bhaashan kii swatantraaa or parivaar ke maamalon main swaittata kaa pradarshan karte hue dekhati he, jo saudi arab main mahilaon kii purv pidiyon main thi. pustak main vibhinn pachaas bedion janajatiyon se mahila kaviyon kii kavitaayen hai or is main do bhaag, "da rhite of chois" or "asweekruti or pratirodh" hai. kavitaayen kii 'rachana kii tithi bhinn bhinn he, jisamen sabase puraani do shatabdiyon puraani he or sabase nai kareeb chalis saal puraani. hilaal is pustak ke bare main kahati he, "janajaatiy mahilaayein talaak kaa anurodh karne ke liye kavita padhati, or jab unke pati sunate to ve unhen talaak de dete." kitaab kii kai kavitaaon ko pehali baar 1950 or 19 60 ke dashak main abdulla ebn raddas dwaara ekatrit kiya gaya tha or ve maukhik parampara se kahaniyon par aadhaarit hai, jo apane patiyon se alag hone kii maang karti thi, chaahe swatantraaa or swatantra abhivyakti kii ichha, pati se niraashaa se, yaa ghabrahat yaa sexist saas-sasur, yaa jo pati dwaara shuru kiye gaye talaak ke adeshon ke jawaab main majboot thi. hilaal kaa sankalan maujuuda vichaaron ko radd karata he ki aadhunik samaaj puraane dinon ke adivasee samaaj kii tulana main adhik sabhy he, or bedauin registan samudaayon or shahari samudaayon main varchasv vaale lingon ke bich antar ko darshata he.
