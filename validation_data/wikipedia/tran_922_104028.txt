 nakul mehta ek bhartiya abhinayta hai, jo pyaar kaa dard he meetha meetha pyaara pyaara main aditya kumaar kii mukhya kirdaar nibhaane ke kaaran jane jate hai. iske alaava ye star plus ke dharawahik ishqabaaz main shivaay singh obaroy naam kaa mukhya kirdaar bhi nibhaa chuke hai. 
 inkaa janm bharat main rajasthan ke udaipur naam ke shahar main hua tha. ye mewar ke raja prithviraj chouhan ke rajput chouhan vansh se hai. inke pita pratap singh mehta 1971 ke bharat-paak yuddh main shaamil the, or inke par-par-pardada lakshmi laal mehta mewar kshetra ke senapati the.
