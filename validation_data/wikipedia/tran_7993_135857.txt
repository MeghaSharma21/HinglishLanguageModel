 tejas express aadhunik suvidhaon vaali madhyam tej gati vaali bhartiya rail kii train he. isaki adhiktam gati 180 kimi he. lekin abhi vartmaan main ye 56 kimi kii raftaar se chal rahi hamel iskaa nirmaan rail coach factory, kapoorthala main hua he. pehali tejas mumbai or goa ke bich 22 may ko rail mantri suresh prabhu ne hari jhandi dikhaai. 20 dibbon vaali ye desh kii pehali train hai jiske sabhi dibbon main swachaalit daravaaje hai. saath hi har dibbe main chaay va kofi kii vending mashin lagi he, prayek seat par alsidi screen or vaai-faai kii suvidha he. tejas main janemaane shef dwaara taiyaar manapsand khaanaa parosa jayega. train main pani kii kam khapat vaale baio-vaikum shauchalay hai. shauchalay main tachales pani kaa nal, sabun dispencer or haath sukhaane kii mashin lagaayee gai he.
