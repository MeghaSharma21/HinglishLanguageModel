 flora fountain yaa hutatma chowk ek fawwara he. yah mumbai main sthit he. fountain kaa naam rome main samruddhi ke devtaa ke naam par padaa, iskaa nirmaan 1869 main sar bartle ferrere ke sammaan main kiya gaya, jinhone aaj dikhne vaale mumbai ke nirmaan main bahut sahyog diya tha. ab yah fountain us kshetra main he, jahaan maharashtra rajya ke liye shahid hone waalon kii yaad main smarak banaaya gaya he.
