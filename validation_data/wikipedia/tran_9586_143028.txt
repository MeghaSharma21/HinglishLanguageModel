 raay deviprasad 'purna' hindi ke kavi the.
 raay deviprasad ji kaa janm margshirsh krishna 13, sanwat 1925 vi. ko jabalpur main hua tha. inke pita kaa naam raay vanshidhar tha. vanshaparamparaagat 'raay' oopaadhi inke purvajon kaa baadashahi shasankaal main mili thi. inkaa paitrik nivas kanpur jile kii ghatmpur tehsil ke antargat bhadras gram main tha jo bhushan or matiram ke suprasiddh janmagram tikwanpur ke paas he. inhone kalakatta vishvavidhyalay kii bi.e. or bi.el. parikshaayen pratham shrenee main uteern kin or kanpur main vakaalat karte rahe. apane samay ke ye bade hi safal or sammanit vakil the. inkaa sanskrit sahitya kaa adhyayan bada vyapak or gambhir tha. urdu-farsi ke bhi ye achche gyaata the. sangeet kii sadhana bhi inhone kii thi. kavita karne or naatakon tathaa ramlila aadi main abhinay karne kaa shauk inhen baalyavastha se hi tha. ramlila main ye nishadaraaj banate the or inke samvaad aise maarmik evam hridayasparshi hote the ki dekhne sunanevaalon kii ankhen geeli ho jaati thim.
 brajabhasha kii kavita kaa abhyaas inhone pan lalitaprasad trivedi 'lalit' ke sanidhya main kiya tha or kramash: usamen ucch koti kii siddhi praapt kii. hindi sahitya main 'purna' ji kii khyaati brajabhasha-kavy-parampara ke atyant praudh or siddh kavi ke rup main he. kanpur kii 'rasik samaaj' naamak sanstha ke ye agrani sadasyon main the or uske adhiveshnon main, vyavastha karnaa, sthaneey or abhyaagat kaviyon ko aamantrit kar unke kavitapath kaa prabandh karnaa, samasyapurtiyon kaa prabandh karnaa ityadi kaaryon main ve bade hi ruchi or manoyogpurvak sanmilit hua karte the. is samaaj kii ore se prakashit honevaali patrika 'rasikavatika' main praaia: brajabhasha kii shastreey dhang se kii gai tatkaaleen shreshth kaviyon kii samasya purtiyan prakashit hoti thim. jo gadhy lekh hote the ve bhi praaia: chand, ras, alankaar aadi kavy ke shastreey paksh kii vivechna karanevaale rahete the. 'purna' ji jab tak jivit rahe, unki rachanaayen baraabar rasikavatika main nikalati raheen.
 purna ji kaa dehavsaan 47 varsh kii avasthaa main kanpur main sanwat 1972 main hua.
 'purna' ji bahumukhi pratibha ke dhani the. falat: unki rachanaaon main ek ore jahaan paramparanugat vishyon kii kavitaayen hai, vahiin aadhunik vishay bhi unase asamyukt nahin rahe- swadeshprem, rajbhakti, samajasudhar aadi vishyon par bhi unki lekhnee waisi hi praudhata ke saath chalati rahi. haan, us yug main raajnneeti ke antargat asahyog or vidroh ke swar nahin phute the, at:, tatkaaleen deshprem or rajbhakti kii jaisi samanvit or sanyat pravriti bhaaratendu aadi main dikhalaai padati he, waisi hi 'purna' ji main bhi thi.
 aage chalakar 'purna' ji ne khadi boli ko hi apni abhivyakti kaa madhyam banaaya or urdu farsi ke chalate, manje hue shabdon, muhaavaron prayogon aadi ke sanyog se badi aakarshak rachanaayen karne lage.
 purna ji dwaara rachit nimnaankit granth ullekh hai -
 inaki sfoot kavitaaon kaa sangrah 'purna-sangrah' ke naam se san. 1982 main lakhanu ke gangapustakamala karyaalay dwaara nikal chukaa he.
