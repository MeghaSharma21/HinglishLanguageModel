 parmaanu jandaayitv vidheyak yani parmaanu daayitv vidheyak parmaanu durghatana kii sthiti main sambandhit pakshon ke daayitv ko nirdhaarit karne vaala vidheyak he. 25 agast ko sarkaar ne is vidheyak ko sansad main prastut kiya. 2008 ke bharat-america asainya parmaanu sandhi ko lagu karaane kii disha main is vidheyak ko antim kadamon ke rup main dekha ja rahaa he. ye vidheyak un america companiyon ke liye aavashyak he jo bharat main parmaanu samagri yaa riektaron kii aapurti karenge. parmaanu durghatana kii sthiti main un companiyon ko america main beemaa kaa bhugtan mil sake, iske liye ye kanoon aavashyak he. is vidheyak ke adhinium banane ke baad bharat un deshon kii qatari main shaamil ho jayega, jinke paas ye kanoon he.
 is vidheyak ko lekar sarkaar ko vipaksh kii ore se baari pratirodhon kaa saamana karnaa padaa. vidheyak ke kai anshon par vipaksh ko aapatti rahi. vamadalon ne to vidheyak ko asanvaidhanik or janvirohi qaraar diya. vipaksh kaa kahana he ki sarkaar ne america ke dabaav main is vidheyak ko taiyaar kiya. is vidheyak main parmaanu yaa nabhikeey sanyantr sanchaalak dwaara durghatana kii sthiti main die jane vaale adhiktam muaavje ko paanch sau karod rupee rakhaa gaya he.
 is vidheyak ke liye nabhikeey urja adhinium-1962 ko sanshodhit karnaa padegaa. bhopal gas traasadee ko dekhte hue audyogic durghatana ko bharat main samvedansheel mudda mana ja rahaa he, jisamen pachchis hajaar se bhi zyada log maare gaye the, ise vishwa kii sabase badi audyogic traasadee mana jaataa he.
 bharat 2020 tak parmaanu urja sanyantron main vidyut ke utpaadan ko paanch guna yani 20,000 megawatt tak badhanaa chahta he. 2032 tak ise 63000 megawatt tak le janaa chahta he. agar bharat aisa kar legaa he to bharat apni aavashyaktaa kaa pachchis pratishat urja parmaanu sanyantron se utpaadan karegaa. vartmaan main bharat kareeb 4000 megawatt parmaanu bijli paidaa kar rahaa he. parmaanu urja utpaadan badhaane ke liye isamen videshi companiyon or aapurtikartaaon ko shaamil karnaa hoga. haalanki is tarah ke vidhyak ke liye bharat ko antarrashtriya baadhyataa nahin he, par america companiyon general motor or vesting house ko is kshetra main vyapaar ke liye aamantrit karne ke liye is vidheyak kii aavashyaktaa he. is vidheyak se ye companiyaan apane desh main beemaa kaa paisa le sakenge. isliye bharat-america parmaanu samjhaute ke liye kriyanvayan ke liye ye vidheyak or kanoon jaruri he.is vidheyak kaa doosraa uddeshy durghatana kii sthiti main prabhavit logon kii madad ke liye sarkaar or sanchaalkon ko aarthik or kanoonee daayitv anubandhit karnaa he. haalanki takneekee vikaas ne durghatana kii ashankaon ko kaaphi kam kar diya he, lekin iske nakaaratmak prabhaavon ko darkinar nahin kiya ja saktaa he.
 is vidheyak par par aapatti is baat ko lekar he ki durghatana kii sthiti main diya jane vaala muaavja aparyaapt or asantoshjanak he. duusari ore, vidheyak main aise bhi praavdhaan he, jo kaaphi had tak parmaanu samagri aapurtikartaaon or sanchaalkon ko dayitvon se mukt kar detii he.
