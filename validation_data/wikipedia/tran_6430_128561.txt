 utthapak, ucchalitr athwa alevator ek yukti he vastuon evam vyaktion ko urdhv disha main chadhaane-utaarne ke kaam aati he. praayah kisi bahumanjila unche bhawan, jalpot evam anya sanrachanaon main utthapak laga hota he jo golon ko yaa saamaan aadi ko ek manzil se duusari manzil yaa ek str se duusare str par laata or le jaataa he. utthapak praayah vidyut motor dwaara chalate hai.
 anaaj ke uthaane or rakhane kii yantric ritiyon main se ek, jo ab bhi sarvaadhik prayog main aati he, dolwale ucchalitr kii he. isamen mote gadhe yaa canvas ke patte par 10 se 18 inch kii duuri par dhaatu ke chote-chote dol bandhe rahete hai. patta urdhwadhar athwa praaia: urdhvadhar rahata he. upree tathaa nichle siron par ek-ek badi ghirani yaa pahiyaa rahata he, jisapar purvokt patta chadha rahata he. patta or ghirani ke bich paryaapt gharshan ke liye patte par rabar chadha rahata he. ucchalitr ke nichevale bhaag main bane ek gadhe main se chalate hue patte ke dol anaaj uthaa lete hai or use upree sire par jaakar gira dete hai. jaise hi anaaj ucchalitr ke upree sire par pahunchataa he, apkentre badal use ek brihatkaay keep main phenk detaa he. yahan se prithvi kaa gurutvkarshan use bade vyas ke nalon tathaa dhaalu nadiyon dwaara sangrah ke upyukt khatton yaa bhaandon main pahunchaa detaa he.
 anaaj ko kisi bhi bendi athwa khadi disha main le jane kii nai reeti yah he ki vayudhara kaa prayog kiya jaae. isamen dhaatu kii drudh pankhiyonwala pankhaa rahata he. isi par anaaj daalaa jaataa he. pankhaa vaayu kii dhara ke saath anaaj ko bhi aage dhakel detaa he. pankhons kaa prayog mukhyat: krishi ke formon par athwa aise chote kaamon ke liye hota he jahaan dhul uthau yantra kii aavashyaktaa rahati he. pankhe ke prayog main haani yah he ki vah dhul udaata he, usamen bhath jane kii pravriti rahati he tathaa uski pankhiyaan anaaj ke daanon ko bahudhaa tod detii hai.
 chote yaa sankuchit sthanon main athwa thodi duuri ke liye pench ke rup vaale ucchalitr kaa vyavhaar kiya jaataa he. khokhale gol belan ke bheetar kuntalakaar ek fal hota he. is fal ke ghoomane ke saath-saath anaaj bhi aage badhata he. anaaj kii kshaitij gati ke liye yah thik kaam detaa he, kintu khadi athwa praaia: khadi disha main anaaj ko chadhaane ke liye isamen bahut bal lagane kii aavashyaktaa hoti he or isliye yah anupayogi siddh hua he.
 pichley kai varshon se, naukaaon tathaa jahaajon or, isse bhi abhinav kaal main, relon se anaaj utaarne tathaa upar neeche pahunchaane ke liye hawa se kaam liya jaataa he. lacheele nalon se kaam lekar is vidhi kaa prayog vividh kaaryon main kiya ja saktaa he. yadhyapi iske upyog main adhik bal kii aavashyaktaa hoti he or anaaj kii gati simit hoti he, to bhi anya ucchalitron kii apeksha isamen anek gun hai.
 hawa se chalane vaali mashiinon kaa hriday ek pump hota he jo yaa to piston ke aage peeche chalane se athwa keval vegapurvak ghoomte rahane se kaam karata he. yah yantra un nalon se, jinka mukh anaaj ke bheetar duba rahata he, vaayu nikaal letaa he. tab nalon ke mukh se, jinamein anaaj ke saath atirikt vaayu ke pravesh ke liye alag maarg rahata he, hawa tathaa anaaj saath-saath upar chadhate hai.
 anaaj ke uthaane rakhane kii mashiinon se kaam lete samay anaaj kii dhuli se visphot hone kii ashanka par dhyaan rakhana aavashyak he.
 is varg ke yantron main maal pahunchaane kaa kaary aviraam na hokar ruk rukkar hota rahata he. is prakaar kaa ucchalitr bhaar ko samay-samay par upar neeche karata rahata he. bhaar rakhane ke liye ek chaowkee tathaa use upar neeche chalaane ke liye rassi yaa jalsanchalit yantra hota he. chaowkee ek chaukor yaa gol ghar main upar neeche chalati he jise coopak kehete hai.
 rassi se chalanevaale maal ke ucchalitron ko do mukhya virgon main vibhajit kiya ja saktaa : 
 laghu kaaryaksham ucchalitr 20 se 30 man kii samarthy ke, 25 foot prati minute kii gativale tathaa 35 foot oonchaai tak kaary karanevaale hote hai. in ucchalitron ke sab bhaagon kii rachana saadhaaran aavashyaktaa se kahin adhik drudh hoti he or inamen batan dabaane par kaary karanevaale sthir-daab-niyantrak, bhawan ke prayek tal par tathaa chalanevaali chaowkee main bhi, lage rahete hai. yadi neeche utarate samay gati atyadhik ho jaae to yaan main swat:chaalit gatiniyantrak-suraksha-yantra kaam karne lagate hai. chaowkee ke praarambhik or antim sthanon par seemaa sthir karanevaale khatke tathaa suraksha ke anya upaay bhi rahete hai. aise yantron kii ek visheshta yah he ki chaowkee ko chalaanevaala yantra ucchalitr ke pende ke paas rahata he. isliye upar kisi avlamb yaa chat kii aavashyaktaa nahin hoti.
 rassiwale gurukaaryaksham ucchalitr visheshkar motor trakon par kaam karne ke liye banaae jate hai. ve itane pusht banaae jate hai ki bhaar se haanevaale sab prakaar ke jatke aadi sah saken. inke sab niyantrak purna rup se swayanchalit hote hai or inkaa prayog trek kaa driver athwa anya koi karmchaaree kar saktaa he. yatayaat maarg ke kuch sthanon par, sir se upar lage or batan dabaane par kaary karanevaale niyantrakon, se yah baat sambhav ho jaati he. jahaan aavashyaktaa hoti he vahaan aisa prabandh bhi rahata he jiske dwaara koi anuchar bhi niyantran kar saktaa he. jahaan bhawan bahut unchaa ho tathaa maal sheeghra chadhaane kii aavashyaktaa ho vahaan ke liye rassi kii sahaayta se kaary sampadit karanevaale ucchalitr vishesh upyogi hote hai.
 jalchaalit ucchalitr jalchaalit ucchalitron kaa upyog neeche bhawanon main hota he jahaan boj bahut baari rahata he or teevr gati kii aavashyaktaa nahin rahati. in ucchalitron ke kaary main daab main pade drav se kaam liya jaataa he. aise upkarnon ke nirmaataa daawa karte hai ki jalchaalit ucchalitr kii chaowkee par baari boj ladane par chaowkee neeche kii ore nahin bhaagti kyonki uskaa aadhaar tel kaa ek asampidneey stambh hota he. ve is prakaar ke yantron main nimnaankit anya gun bhi bataate hai : inke liye kisi chat kii aavashyaktaa nahin padati; inkaa kupakmaarg khulaa or isliye suprakashit rahata he; chaowkee bina jatke ke chalanaa aarambh karti or rukati he; jahaan rokna chahen thik vahiin rukati he; or mashin ko acchhee dasha main banaae rakhane main vyay kam hota he.
 yatriyon ke liye bane ucchalitron kii rachana bhi boj dhonevaale ucchalitron kii hi tarah hoti he. keval inamen suraksha kii kuch adhik yuktiyaan rahati hai tathaa inke rup or yatriyon kii sukh suvidha par vishesh dhyaan diya jaataa he.
