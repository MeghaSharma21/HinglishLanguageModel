 arakhangai mongolia ke pashchim-madhya main sthit us desh kaa ek aimag he.
 'arakhangai' main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he. is praant kaa naam kanagai parvaton par rakhaa gaya he or mongol bhashaa main 'arakhangai' kaa arth 'uttari kanagai' hota he. iske vipareet ovorkanagai praant ke naam kaa arth 'dakshini kanagai' he.
 arakhangai praant main bahut se jwalamukhi or jwalamukheey jhilein hai jin se iskaa bhu-drishy 'jabardast' bulaayaa jaataa he. iskaa sabase unchaa pahaad orphon or tamir nadiyon ke sangam par sthit 3,529 meter unchaa karalagat​i parvat he. is praant main khorgo naam kaa prasiddh mrit jwalamukhi bhi he. yahan kanagai parvaton se bahut se nadi-jharne ubharate hai jo sabhi ant main jaakar selenga nadi ke jalsambhar kaa hissaa hai. pashchim main terekin tsagaan nur​ naamak jhil he, jiske bare main yah dharna he ki yah ek daanav ke ek mahaan chattan phenkane se ban gai thi. jab daanav ne ise dekha to aashcharya se chillaaya, 'dekho, ek safed jhil' or uskaa yahi kathan hi jhil kaa naam he.
 is praant main sardi or garmi donon bahut padati hai. sardiyon kaa ausat nunatam taapmaana −30 °centegrade se −38 °centegrade hota he jabaki garmiyon main ausat adhiktam taapmaana 25 °centegrade and 36 °centegrade hota he.
 arakhangai praant main maveshi paalan ek pramukh vyavsaay he. yahan bakriyaan, bheden, gaay, ghode or oont paale jate hai. oont is praant ke dakshin-purvi ilaakon main hote hai.
