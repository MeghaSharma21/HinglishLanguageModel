 hind mahaasaagar main sthit sunda dweep samooh, malay dweepasmooh kaa ek hissaa he. in dwipon kaa punahvibhaajan bada sunda dweep samooh or chota sunda dweep samooh main rup main kiya jaataa hai.
 vartmaan main is samooh ke dweep chaar deshon kramash: bruneii, purvi timore, indonesia or malaysia ke bich vibhajit hai. dweep samooh ke zyaadaatar dweep indonesia ke adhikaar kshetra main aate hai. borneo naamak dweep bruneii, indonesia or malaysia ke bich vibhajit he. timore naamak dweep purvi timore or indonesia ke bich vibhajit he. anya chote dweep bhi purvi timore kaa hissaa hai.
