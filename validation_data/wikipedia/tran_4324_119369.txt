 samvidhan diwas bharat ganraajya kaa samvidhan 26 navambar 1949 ko banakar taiyaar hua tha. samvidhan sabha ke nirmaatri samiti ke adhyaksh dow bhimrao ambedkar ke 125wein jayanti varsh ke rup main 26 navambar 2015 ko samvidhan diwas manaaya gaya. dow bhimrao ambedkar ji ne bharat ke mahaan samvidhan ko 2 varsh 11 maah 18 din main 26 navambar 1949 ko puraa kar raashtra ko samarpit kiya. ganatantr bharat main 26 janvari 1950 se samvidhan amal main laayaa gaya.
 ambedkarwaadi or buddha logon dwaara kai dashakon purv se ‘samvidhan diwas’ manaaya jaataa he. bharat sarkaar dwaara pehali baar 2015 se dow bhimrao ambedkar ke is mahaan yogadan ke rup main 26 navambar ko "samvidhan diwas" manaaya gaya. 26 november kaa din samvidhan ke mahatv kaa prasar karne or dow bhimrao ambedkar ke vichaaron or avadharanaon kaa prasar karne ke liye chunaa gaya tha.
