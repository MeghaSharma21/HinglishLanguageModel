 ithaka ghati hamaare saura mandal ke chathe grah shani ke upgrah tathis par sthit ek khagolvaigyanikon main prasiddh tang ghati he. yah 100 kilometre chaudee, 3 se 5 kimi gahari or 2,000 kimi lambi he or tathis ke puure vyuh ke teen-chauthai hisse tak chalati he. isaki chaudaai jagah-jagah par badalati rahati he - kahin to yah sirf chand kilometre hi chaudee he or kahin par 100 kimi tak chaudee he. vaigyanik anumaan lagate hai ke yah tang ghati tathis par pichley 4 arab varshon se maujuud he. vah maanate hai ke tathis par bahut sa pani tha jiske upar barf kii moti parat thi. phir yah andruni pani dhire-dhire jamane laga. pani jab barf banataa he to uskaa aakaar failata he . jaise-jaise yah pani jamaa, uske failane se upar kaa barfila khol tang pad gaya or fat gaya. yahi daraar ab ithaka ghati kehalaati he.
 angrezi main "ithaka ghati" ko "iffaaka kaizma" kehete hai. dhyaan rahe ke isamen "ji" kaa uccharan "tha" ke varna se thodaa bhinn hota he.
