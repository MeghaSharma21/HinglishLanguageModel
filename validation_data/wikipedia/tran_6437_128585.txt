 stacy enn fergyusan jinhen apane manch ke naam fargi se aam taur par janaa jaataa he, ek amareeki gayika, geet lekhakika, abhinetri, television host or fishan diziner he. vah pehle bacchon ke television dharawahik shrunkhlaa kids incorporated or wild orchid kii sadsy thi. vah da blike oid pis kii mahila gayak he jinke saath unhone vishvabhar main ricord safalataa hasil kii he. unke ekal album ke paanch geet billboard hot 100 ke sheersh paanch geet the jiname se tin baad main number ek ke paaydaan par rahe.
 wild orchid ko 2001 main chhodane ke baad fergyusan ne da blike oid pis main shaamil hona pasand kiya. da blike oid pis ke saath unke kai album safal rahe jiske pashchyaat unhone apana ekal album da dches sitambar 2006 main release kiya jon ek safal album rahaa. da blike oid pis ne fergyusan ke saath release apane tisare album da i.en.di main punah apni safalataa doharaai or iske saath unhone billboard hot 100 main apni pehali number ek kii shrunkhlaa sthapit kii. 2009/2010 main fergyusan ne apane group ke saath daura shuru kiya or baad main apana parafum outspoken evon ke antargat may 2010 main launch kiya.
 unhone da blike oid pis kii saath apni safalataa punah doharaai jab unhone da bigining album ko november 2010 main release kiya.
 fergyusan kaa janm hessinda hits, california main terresa enn, jon ek catholic skul shikshika thi, va jon patric fergyusan kii beti ke rup main hua. ve irish, scottish, mexican va mul amareeki vansh kii he. unki parvarish roman catholic ke rup main kii gai or unhone mesa robles middle skul va glen e. wilson hai skul main padhai kii. unhone nrity kaa prashikshan liya or awaz dene kaa kaary shuru kiya jise unhone television ke liye bane cartoon pints main saili ko awaz de kar kiya or saath hi da charlie brown and snupi dog ke chaar episodon main bhi apni awaz di. 1984 se 1989 tak unhone tivi dharawahik kids incorporated main kaary kiya. is dauraan ve ek chiyar leader, ssailing bi vijeta va girl scout thi.
 fargi wild orchid kii tin sadasyon main se ek thi jisame unke saath steffni redelle va kids incorporated kii sahakalakaar rini sandstorm thi. 1992 main inhone record labalon se mulaakaaten shuru kii parantu inhen kahin safalataa nahin mili. aakhirkaar unhen soni publicing ke saath sangeet prakaashan kaa saida mila va 1994 main unhone aaarasie ke saath record ke saude par hastakshar kiye. april 2007 main unhone ek intervyuu ke dauraan yah mana kii ve sex va nashile padaarthon ke jaal main atharah varsh kii hoti hi funs gai or kaha: "maine bhootkaal main samalangi anubhav liye he. main yah nahin kahoongi kii maine kitne purashon ke saath sambhog kiya par main behad sambhog pasand insaan hoon."
 unka pehla ekal geet "at nite ai pre" billboard chart par 1996 ke ant main pradarshit hua. march 1997 main wild orchid ne apana isi naam kaa pehla album release kiya. sitambar 1998 main unhone apana doosraa album oxigen release kiya. 16 june se 28 agast ke bich unhone cher ke du yu bilive? tour main syndi laupar ke saath bhaag liya jo unhen america va canada main kul 52 shaharon main ghuma laayaa. apana teesraa album puraa hone par unke record label ne use pradarshit karne se man kar diya or iske chalate fergyusan ne sitambar 2001 main wild orchid chod diya. 2006 main intertenment vikali ke saath ek intervyuu main fergyusan ne khulaasa kiya kii band kii or swayan kii nashe kii chhavi ke chalate unhone wild orchid choda.
 wild orchid ke saath pradarshnon ke dauraan fergyusan ko crystal methamphetamine kii lat lag gai jon unke saath 2001 main band chhodane tak bani rahi. sitambar 2006 main fergyusan ne time magazine ke saath apni nashe kii aadat chhodane ke bare main charchaa kii. "vah ek aisa premi tha jisase rishta todna maine behad mushkil paaya", unhone kaha. fergyusan ne kai saakshatkaaron main mana he kii vah sammohan pratha kaa upyog crystal meth par kaabo paane ke liye karti he. 2001 se pehle fergyusan ke pop-star justin timberlek ke saath prem sambandh the.
 jab da blike oid pis apana teesraa album, 2003 kaa alerunk, record kar rahe the tab vil.ai.em ne fergyusan ko "shat ap" gaanaa gane ke liye aamantrit kiya. ve iske baad achche se teenon main ghul-mil gai or unhone paanch or gane album ke liye record kiye. vasant ritu ke baad, alerunk release hone se kuch vakt pehle, interscope ke chairman jimi lovin ne fergyusan ko blike oid pis main ek pakke sthaan kaa prastaav diya jon unke parshvagayak kim hill ke 2000 main jane ke kaaran khali ho gaya tha.
 alerunk se "ware iz da lav?" geet blike oid pis kaa pehla behad safal gaanaa ban gaya jon billboard hot 100 kii suchi main aathven sthaan par rahaa parantu anya deshon main pratham kramank hasil karne main kaamiyaab rahaa. iske baad "shat ap" kai bazaaron main release kiya gua. album kaa teesraa geet "he mamaa" kai europea deshon main pehle 10 kii suchi main rahaa or america main teiswein sthaan par pahunchaa. 2004 main blike oid pis alerunk ke vishwavyaapee daure ke liye nikal gaye.
 unka teesraa album manki bijanas june 2005 ko release kiya gaya. manki bijanas billboard 200 main duusare paaydaan par rahaa. album kaa pehla geet "dont fank wyeth maai heart" america main tisare sthaan par pahunchaa va isane unhen sarvshereshth rap parformans by duo or group ke grami puraskaar kaa sammaan arjeet karavaaya. album ke duusare geet "maai hamps" ko turant hi america main vyapaarik safalataa hasil hui or yah australia main hot 100 ke tisare paaydaan par rahaa. 2005 main blike oid pis gwen steffni ke daure par niklaa. baad main blike oid pis honda civik tour par dobaaraa niklaa.
 fergyusan ne apana ekal album da dches sitambar 2009 main release kiya. da dches main fergyusan ke chah gane hit sabit hue jiname "london brij", "fergolishas", "glamras", "big girls dont krai", "clumsi" or "finali" shaamil he. 18 navambar 2007 ko fergyusan ne american music awards main pop or roak "pasandida mahila kalakaar" kaa sammaan hasil kiya. iske saath hi unke gane "big girls dont krai" ne unhen sarvshereshth mahila pop gayan pradarshan kaa grami puraskaar namakan dilaayaa. disambar 2007 main blendar ne fergyusan ko apni "varsh kii mahila" ghoshit kiya.
 fergyusan 2006 main punah abhinay kshetra main posyden ke saath lauti jisame unhone ek manch gayika adaa kii. iske baad be 2007 main bani grind house or 2009 main bani sangitamay film nine main nazar ai.
 fergyusan or josh duhamal ne 10 janvari 2009 main paanch saal chale prem sambandh ke baad shaadi kar lee. fergyusan or duhamal kii mulaakaat sitambar 2004 main hui thi jab vah or unka band duhamal ke sho las vegas main aae the.
 da blike oid pis se march 2009 main apane album da i.en.di kaa geet "boom boom po" release kiya jon america main pehle kramank par rahaa or yah inkaa pehle paaydaan par rahane vaala pehla geet ban gaya. iske baad unhone "ai gota feeling" release kiya jon or adhik safal rahaa or 11 july ko billboard hot 100 ke pehle paaydaan par rahaa. "meet me hafve", jon is album kaa teesraa geet tha, ko sitambar 2009 main release kiya gaya.
