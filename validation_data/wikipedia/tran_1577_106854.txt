 bangladesh kii rashtriya sansad ke adhyaksh, bangladesh kii sansad ke sabhapati evam adhishthata he. iske alaava, kuch avasaron par ve videshon main bhi sadan va desh kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai. unhone sansad ke sadasyon dwaara nirvaachit kiya jaataa he. ve tatasth hota hai evam sansad ko bhang kiye jane ke baad bhi agle adhyaksh ke chayan tak adhyakshata kii jimmedaariyaan nibhaane he. spikar ke atirikt, sadan, unke oopaadhikri ke rup main ek upaadhyaksh kaa bhi chunaav karti he, jinka kaary, sabhapati kii anupasthiti main adhyakshata kaa nirvaah karnaa hota he.
