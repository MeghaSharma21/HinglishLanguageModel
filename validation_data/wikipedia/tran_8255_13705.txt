 apomarphin yaa apomarphin hydrochloride ek alcoloid vyutpann yogic he. 
 yah marthin par hydrochloric amla ke prayog se praapt hota he. yah kendriya vamanakaarak he tathaa vishpan kii chikitsa main 5 milligram kii maatra main adhsravak maarg se vaman karaane ke liye prayukt hota he. isaki matraayen aadhe-aadhe ghante par do baar tak di ja sakati hai.
