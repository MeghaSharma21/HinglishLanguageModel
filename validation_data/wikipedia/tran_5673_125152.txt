 uttar vaidic kaal se pehle kaa vah kaal jisamen rugveda kii richaon kii rachana hui thi in richaon kii jo bhashaa thi wo rigvaidic bhashaa kehalaati he. rigvaidic bhashaa hind europea bhashaa parivaar kii ek bhashaa he. isaki parvarti putree bhashaayen avesta puraani farsi pali praakrit or sanskrit hain.
 rigvaidic bhashaa ke mul maatrubhaashi hind-irani arya the.
 
 jaise homeric greek clasical greek se bhinn he vaisai rigvaidic bhashasanskrit bhashaa se bhinn he. tiwari ne donon ke bich antar ko nimna siddhant swaroop suchit kiya:
 19veen shatabdi main avastai farsi or rigvaidic bhashaa donon par pashchimi vidwaanon kii nazar nai-nai padi thi or in donon ke gahare sambandh kaa tathy unke saamne jaldi hi aa gaya. unhone dekha ke avastai farsi or rigvaidic bhashaa ke shabdon main kuch saral niyamon ke saath ek se dusre ko anuvaadit kiya ja saktaa tha or vyakaran kii drushti se yah donon bahut nazadik the. apni san 1892 main prakashit kitaab "avastai vyakaran kii sanskrit se tulana or avastai varnmaala or uskaa lipyantaran" main bhashavaigyanik or vidwaan ebraham jackson ne udahaaran ke liye ek avastai dharmik shloke kaa rigvaidic bhashaa main sidhaa anuwaad kiya.
