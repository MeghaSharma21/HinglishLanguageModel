 major general shah navaj khan aaazaad hind fouj ke prasiddh adhikari the. dwitiya vishwayuddh ke samaapt hone par general shahanvaj khan, karnal gurbaksh singh dhillon tathaa karnal prem sahagal ke upar angrej sarkaar ne mukaddama chalaaya. ve bollywood film abhinayta shahrukh khan ke naanaa the.
 khan ek bhartiya raajnneetijna the jinhone dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke dauraan bhartiya rashtriya sena main ek adhikari ke rup main kaary kiya tha. yuddh ke baad, use deshadroh ke liye doshi tehraaya gaya, or british bhartiya sena dwaara kiye gaye ek saarvajanik nyaayalay-marshall main maut kii sajaa sunai gai. bharat main ashaanti or virodh ke baad bhartiya sena ke commander-in-chif ne sajaa ko kam kar diya tha.
 dwitiya vishwayuddh samaapt hone par bharat ke swatantraaa sangram main ek naatakeey badlaav aayaa. 15 agast 1947 bharat ko aazaadee milane tak, bhartiya rajnatik manch vividh janandolanon kaa gavaah rahaa. inamen se sabase aham aandolan, aaazaad hind fouj ke 17 hajaar javaanon ke khilaaf chalane vaale mukademe ke virodh main janaakrosh ke samuhik pradarshan the.
 major general shahanvaaj ko muslim lig or le. karnal gurubaksh singh dhillan ko akali dal ne apni ore se mukadmaa ladane kii peshkash kii, lekin in deshbhakt sipaahiyon ne congress dwaara jo dephence team banaai gai thi, usee team ko hi apana mukadmaa pairvi karne kii manjuuri di. majahabi bhaawanaaon se upar uthakar sahagal, dhillan, shahanvaaj kaa yah faisla sachmuch prashansa ke yogya tha.
 hindustani taarikh main 'laal kila trial' kaa aham sthaan he. laal kila trial ke naam se prasikad aaazaad hind fouj ke aitihaasik mukademe ke dauraan uthe is naare 'laal kile se ai aawaaj-sahagal, dhillan, shahanvaaj' ne us samay hindustan kii aazaadee ke haq ke liye lad rahe lakhon naujavaanon ko ek sutara main baandh diya tha. vakil bhuulabhai desai is mukademe ke dauraan jab laal kile main bahes karte, to sadakon par hajaaron noujvaan naare laga rahe hote. puure desh main deshbhakti kaa ek waar sa uthta. 15 navambar 1945 se 31 disambar 1945 yani, 57 hindustan kii aazaadee ke sangharsh main terning point tha. yah mukadmaa kai morchon par hindustani ekta ko majboot karne vaala sabit hua.
 is trial ne puri duniya main apni aazaadee ke liye lard rahe lakhon logon ke adhikaron ko jaagrit kiya. sahagal, dhillan or shahanvaj ke alaava aaazaad hind fouj ke anek sainik jo jagah-jagah giraftaar hue the or jin par saikadon mukademe chal rahe the, ve sabhi reeha ho gaye. 3 janvari 1946 ko aaazaad hind fouj ke jaanbaaj sipaahiyon kii rihaai par 'rieter association of america' tathaa britain ke anek patrakaron ne apane akhabaaron main mukademe ke vishay main jamkar likhaa. is tarah yah mukadmaa antarrashtriya rup se charchit ho gaya. angreji sarkaar ke commander-in-chif sar claude aklanik ne in javaanon kii umr kaid sajaa maaf kar di. hawa kaa rukh bhaanpakar ve samaj gaye, ki agar inako sajaa di gai to hindustani fouj main baghawat ho jaaegi.
 vichaarna ke dauraan hi bhartiya jalsena main vidroh shuru ho gaya. mumbai, karachi, kolkata, vishakhapatnam aadi sab jagah vidroh kii jwaala failte der na lagi. is vidroh ko janata kaa bhi bharpoor samarthan mila.
