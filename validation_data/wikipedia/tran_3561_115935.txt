 devdatt patnayak ek bhartiya lekhak hai. iske saath hi ve puranic kathakaar, netritv salaahkaar, lekhak or sanchaarak bhi hai. inkaa kaam dharm, puraana, myth or mukhya rup se prabandhan ke kshetron par kendrit he. devdatt patnayak ne 2017 main vivaadaaspad padmavati par ek bahes shuru kii, jab unhone rani padmini kii kahani par apni aapatti jataai or ise "swechha se khud ko jalaane vaali mahila ke vichaaron kaa glamareshan or mulya nirdhaaran" kaha.
 unki pustakon main myth = mithyaa: ek handbook of hindu puranic kathaayen ; jaya: mahabharat kaa ek elstrated retail ; sita: ramayana kaa ek elstrated ritling ; bijness sutara: prabandhan ke liye ek bhartiya drushtikon ; shikhandi: or anya kahaniyon main ve aapako nahin bataate, shankar ko shivakaarya: bina formles ke liye form dete hue, jisamen unhone shiv ke ling ke arthon kii paraton kii khoj kii he, hamen pataa chalata he ki kyon or kaise devi, shiv ko shradhanjali badalte hai, grihasth; netaa: puranic kathaon se 50 antardrushti shastron or vaidic gyaan kaa upyog gyaan par pahunchane ke liye he jo samay-pahana or tazaa nayaa he, jo ek acha netaa banaata he; or samskruti: puranic kathaon se 50 antardrushti ek mahatvapurn kaam he jo pracheen granthon kaa sandarbh detaa he or prastaavit karata he ki avadharanaen jivit, gatisheel, dharna ke aakaar kaa he or jis samay main vah rahata he. unhone puure mahabharat ko sirf 36 twit main joda he or 18 twits main bhagavad gita. 
 vah bharat ke sabase bade khudara vikritaon main se ek, future group ke purv mukhya vishwaas adhikari hai. vah times of india, swaraj, scrol ke liye likhte he.
