 water 2005 main bani ek canadien chitra he. chitra kii nirdeshika deepa mehta hai or rachanaakaar anuraag kashyap hai, jinhone sanvaado kaa hindi main anuwaad bhi kiya tha. yah chitra san 1938 ke samay par nirdhaarit he or un vidhwaaon ke jindagi ke bare main he jo banaras ke ashram main rahati thim. yah chitra ke pehle ne fire or arth banaaya tha. water san 1940 ke gaanv kii vidhwaaon kii du:kha bhari kahaniyon kaa aatmvishleshan karata he or stri dweshi or bahishkaar jaise vivadaspad vishyon par prakaash daalata he. yah chitra bharat main 9 march 2007 ko jari kiya gaya tha.
 is chitra ke mukhya kalakaar seemaa biswas, lisa rey, jon ebraham or sarala kariaswami hai. sangeetkaar ear rehamaan hai or geetkaar sukhwinder singh or rakim aalaab hai.
