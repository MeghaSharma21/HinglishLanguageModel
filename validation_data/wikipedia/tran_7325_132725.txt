 hindu puranic kathaayen dharm se sambandhit paaramparik vivaranon kaa ek vishaal sangrh he. yah sanskrit-mahabharat, ramayana, puraana aadi, tamil-sangam sahitya evam periy puranam, anek anya kritiyaan jinamein sabase ullekhneey he. bhagwad puraana; jise pancham veda kaa pad bhi diya gaya he tathaa dakshin ke anya prantiy dharmik sahitya main nihit he.inke mul main smriti granth or smart parampara he. yah bhartiya evam nepali samskruti kaa ang he. ekabhoot vishalkay krati hone kii jagah yah vividh paramparaon kaa mandal he jise vividh sampradaayon, vyaktiyon, dashn shrankhla, vibhinn praanton, bhinn kalavadhi main viksit kiya gaya. aisa aavashyak nahin ki inhen aitihaasik `tnaaon kaa yatha shabd, vastavik vivaran hone kii manyata sabhi hinduon se praapt ho, par gudh, adhikashat:sankethik arthyukt avashay mana gaya he.veda devgaathaon ke mul, jo pracheen hindu dharm se viksit hue, vaidic sabhyataa evam vaidic dharm ke samay se janmen hai. chaturvedon main unek vishaywastu ke lakshan milten hai. pracheen vaidic kathaon ke paatr, unke vi’vaas tathaa moolkatha kaa hindu darshan se atoot sambandh he. veda chaar hai yatha rigaveda, yajurveda, atharvaveda va samaveda. kuch avataran aisi tathvik avadharana tathaa yantron kaa ullekh karte hai jo aadhunik kaal ke vaigyanik siddhanton se bahut milte-julate hai.
 sanskrit kii adhikansh saamagree mahakavyon ke rup main surakshit he. kathaon ke atirikt in mahakavyon main tatkaaleen samaaj, darshan, samskruti, dharm tathaa jeevancharya par vistrut jaankaree praapt hoti he.
 ramayana va mahabharat, ye do mahakavy vishesht: vishnu ke do avtaron ram evam krishna kii katha sunaate hai. ye itihaas kehalaae gaye hai. ye dono dharmik granth to hai hi, darshan evam naitikta kii amuly nidhi bhi hai. in mahakavyon ko vibhinn candon main vibhakt kiya gaya he jinamein anek lagukathaayen hai jahaan prastut paristhiyon ko paatr hindu dharm tathaa naitikta ke anusaar nibhaataa he. inamen se mahakavy kaa sabase mahatvapurn adhyay he bhagavad gita jisamen shri krishna kurukshetra ke yuddh se pehle arjuna ko dharm, karm or nitiparayanata kaa gyaan dete hai.ye mahakavy bhi alag-alag yugon main rache gaye hai. valmiki dwaara rachit ramayana treta yug main vishnu ke saatven avatar ram kii katha he. mahabharat pandavon tathaa vishnu ke aathven avatar krishna se sambandhit dwapar yug main rachi rachana he.
 kul 4 yug maane gaye hai-sat~yug athwa krit~yug, tretayug, dwaparayug tathaa kaliyug.
 puraanon main ve kathaayen samahit hai jo mahakavyon main nahin paayi gayi hai athwa unka kshanik ullekh he. inamen sansar kii utpatti, anekanek devi-devataaon nayak-nayikaon pracheenkaaleen jeevon ; asur, daanav, daitya, yaksha, rakshas, gandharva, apsaraon, kimpurushon aadi ddh ke jivan tathaa saahasik abhiyaanon kii dantakathaayen or kahaniyaan hai.
 bhagavadpurana sambhavat: sarvaadhik pathit evam vikhyath puraana he. isamen bhagwaan vishnu ke avtaron ke vrittanton ko lipibaddh kiya gaya he.
 brahmand srijan evam brahmand vigyaan pracheenatam srijan kii katha rigaveda main milti he jisamen brahmand utpatti hiranyagarbha-sone ke andey se maani gai he.
 purush sookt main kaha gaya he ki devataaon ne ek divya purush kii bali di, uske bhinn angon se sabhi jeevon kii rachana hui. puraanon main vishnu ke varah avatar parabhautik sagar se prithvi ko ubhaar laae the.
 shatapath brahmand main mana gaya he ki aadi main jab prajapati-pratham srujankarta akele the tab unhone apane ko pati-patni ke do swaroopon main vibhakt kar liya.
 patni ne apane srujankarta ke saath is sambandh ko vyabhichaar mana or unke prempash se bachane ke liye vibhinn jeev-jantuon kaa rup dhaaran kiye pati ne bhi unheen roopon ko dharankarke patni kaa anukaran kiya or inhen sanyogo se vibhinn prajatiyon kaa janm hua. puraanon main brahmand, vishnu, maheshwar kii ishwariya trimurti ke gathan kaa varnan he jo kramash:srujankarta, vahankarta or vinash karta maane gaye hai.
 brahmand kaa sarjanbramand ne kiya, vishnu iske sangrakshak hai tathaa mahadev shiva agle srijan ke liye iskaa vinash karaten he. kuch kinvadantiyon main bramand ke srujankarta vishnu maane gaye hai jinaki naabhi se utpann camel par bramand asin hai.
 hindu vyavastha anusaar bramand anant kaal tak samay chakro main vicharan karata he. 360 aise din or raat brahm kaa ek varsh banaate hai. unka jivan aise 100 varshon kii avadhi kaa he.. prati manwantar ke bich lambii avadhi kaa antaraal hota he. is avadhi main sansar kaa pun:srijan hota he or manushya janak rup main nae manu prakat hote hai. is samay ham saatven manwantar main hai jiske manu vaiwaswath manu hai. prati manwantar main 71 mahayug hote hai. prayek mahayug chaar yugon main vibhakt he-kruta, treta, dwapar or kali. inaki avadhi kramsh: 4800, 3600, 2400, 1200 dev varshon ke baraabar he. har yug main pavitrataa, naitikta, ’shaurya, kshamta, jivan avadhi tathaa sukh kaa hayens hota jaataa he. is samay ham kaliyug main hai jon tatwanusaar 3102 sal purv prarambh hua. yah sal mahabharat ke yuddh kaa sal mana jaataa he. kaliyug ke ant ke lakshan maane gaye hai varnsankarta, sthapit mulyon kaa tiraskaar, dharmik prathaon kii samaapti, crur evam bahari raajaaon kaa raaj. iske turant baad sansar kaa pralay evam agni se nash hoga. sabhi grantho ke anusaar pralay kalp ke antim chakra ke baad hi hota he. ek mahayug se duusare mahayug main paraagaman sahajata se ho jaataa he. jeevon kaa ant teen prakaar kaa mana gaya he-naimitthik, jo prati kalp ke ant main brahm ke din ke ant main hota he. prakritik vinash brahm ke jivan ke ant main hota he. atyantik-yah sampurna tatv moksh kii prapti he jahaan se purnjanm ke bandhan se mukti mil jaati he.
 apni chaar bhujaaon main shankh, chakra, gada, padam liye bhagwaan vishnu sangrakshak maane gaye hai. aadi main rigveda main ve gaun devtaa ke rup main prastut kiye gaye baad main ve trimuti ke ang ke rup main tatpashchaat vaishnav dharm main ve akhand bramandswaroop iswar ke rup main prastut hue. dharm kii sansthapana hetu avatarit hane ke kaaran tathaa karunamay vardaan pradaata hone ke kaaran hi unhen yah paramtatv swaroop bhagwaan kii oopaadhi di gai. vishnu ke 10 avatar hai - matsay, kurm, varah, vaaman, narsingh, parashuram, ram, krishna, buddha or kalaki.
 shev sampradaay ke log shiv ko parameshwar maanate hai, jo sarvshereshth srot tathaa charam lakshya hai. pashupat shiva siddhant inhen parabr ke baraabar yaa unase bhi mahaan maanate hai. shiva naitik evam pita swaroop devtaa hai. ve unke bhi devtaa he jo brhmani samaaj kii mukhyadhara se baahar hai. ve anek vidhiyon se poojit hai. tantric bhi shiva kii aradhana karte hai. ang main bhasma lagaae, charmambar dhaaran kiye, naag kaa haar, jataaon main ardhachadradhari vinash ke devtaa yogi s parvat par vaas karte hai. shiva kaa puraatan naam rudr he. vaidic grantho ke anusaar rudr rco yajna ke prasad main sanmilit nahi kiya jaataa tha. shiva ke sasur daksh ne shiva-sati ko apane yajna main aamantrit nahin kiya. sati ne apane pita se shikayat kii to unhone shiva kaa apmaan kiya. sati yah sahan na kar pai or yajna kii agni main apana dah kar liya. apni patni kii mrityu kii soochna paa krodhit shiva ne daksh kaa sar chhin kar diya. baad main bali ke bakre kaa sar lagaakar unhen pun: jivit kiya. apni samadhi bhang karne ke prayaas main apane tisare netra se unhone kaamdev ko bhasma kar diya. tadnantar himalay putree parvati ne apane tap se shiva ko paaya asair inhen ke kehane par unhone kaamdev ko adrishy rup main jivit kiya.
 devi apane puru praturup kii shakti he, bal he, ant:’shakti he. shiv kii patni ke rup main adhinsth dikhaai detii he, to duusari ore mahadevi ke rup main ’shiv ke baraabar yaa brahmand kii ucchtam devi ke rup main sarv jeevon kii chetana, shakti va sakriyata kaa moolbhoot sut he. mata athwa patni ke rup main uski paaramparik prakruti dikhaai iti he-sundar, agyaakaarini jaise parvati-’shiv, lakshmi-vishnu, saraswati-brahma. ug rup main durga mahishasur kaa vadh karne vaali tathaa ati ug bhe main vah chamunda-kaali he jisane raktbeej daanav kaa rakt pi liya anyathaa uske rakt kii har boond se pun: nayaa daanav utpann ho jaataa tha.
 vishnu, ’shiv, durga ke saath anek anya devi-devataaon ko bhi puja jaataa he. uttar vaidic kaal main aranyakon or upanishdon main brahman kaa mahattav badha. pehle prajapati baad main srujankarta maane jane lage.kuch devatagan kisi kaary vidhesh se hi sambandhit maane gaye : indra-devataaon ke raja, asr ke dharak, vaiu rke devtaa, varun-jal devtaa, yama-kaal, mrityu ke devtaa, kuber-dhanaan-sampatti ke devtaa, agnidev, pavandev, chandbandev, adium, indra, varun va kuber lokapal kehalaate hai.’shiv-parvati ke putr skanda-yuddh ke devtaa, ganesh-vighn vina’shaq hai. prayek kaary ko sucharu rup se sampann karne ke liye prarambh main ganesh kii puja kii jaati he. deviyon main lakshmi-saubhajya evam loukik, dhan kii devi, saraswati-vidya evam kala kii devi hai.
 hindu dharmik kathaon main anekanek devi-devtaa maane gaye hai. naag-bahumoolya ko kii raksha karte hai, yaksha-kuber ke gana hai, gandharva-indra ke sangeetkaar hai, kinnar gandharvon ke saath rahete hai, gandharvon kii stri praturup hai apsaraayen, jo sundar or kaamuk hoti hai.
 rishiyon ne vaidic shalokon kii sanrachana kari. inamen mukhya hai sapt rishi- marichi, atri, angiras, pulasthya, kritu, vashishtha. ye taramandal ke rup main akaash main dikhaai dete hai.
 daksh va kashyap devon evam maanvon ke purvaj hai, naarad veena ke aavishkaarak, brihaspati va shuk sur tathaa asuron ke guru, agastya ne dakshin prayadweep main dharm evam samskruti kaa prachaar kiya.
 pitru purvajon kii atmaaen jinhen pind daan diya jaataa he.
 asur mukhya duratmayen jo sadaa devajaaon se yuddhrat rahete the. ye diti kii santaanen daitya kehalaate hai, danu kii santaanen daanav kehalaate hai. asuron ke pramukh raja vrutra, hiranakaship, bali aadi, pulasthya rishi ke putr ravan rakshas hai.
 dev or asuron ke bich trilok ke liye kul 12 bheen yuddh hue. hiranakshya-varah yuddh jisamen hiranakshya divya sagar main maaraa gaya. narsingh-hiranya’ship yuddh-narsingh ne daitya kaa vadh kiya.vajagputra tarakasur skanda dwaara maaraa gaya. andhak vadh isamen andhakasur ’shiv ke dwaara maaraa gaya.

 shastr-jinako haath main pakad kar prahaar kiya jaataa he jaise talwaar, kataar. astr-jinako shatru par phenkaa jaataa he jaise baan.paaramparik astr- shastr jaise dhanu-baann, talwaar, kataar, bhaale, gada, dhaal ke atirikt daivik astr jaise indra kaa asr, bstra, trishul, sudarshan chakra, pinaak aadi hai. anek astr i’varon dwaara devataaon, rakshason yaa manushyon ko vardaan swaroop die jate the jaise, brahmastra, aagneyastra. inhen chalaane ke liye vish gyaan kaa hona aavashyak tha.
 kuch astr ek sunishichat kaary ke liye hi bane hote the yatha - nagastra jiske upyog se virdohi sena par koti-koti sarpon kii vrishti ho jaati thi. aagneyastra virdohi ko jalaane, varunastra agnishman karne athwa baadh laane ke liye, brahmastra kaa prayog keval shatru vishesh par hi pranaghaati waar karne ke liye hota tha. inke atirikt anya daivik upkarnon kaa ullekh bhi he jaise kavach, kundal, mukut, shiristraan aadi.
 
 shatapath brahman main mahapralay kii katha kahi gai he. anya dharmon ke vivaranon se isaki tulana kii jaati he. manu ko aanevaale pralay kii purv soochna dekar bhagwaan vishnu ne matsay avatar liya or dharmaka paalan karne vaale manushyon evam anya jeev-jantuon ko bachaaya.
 
 puranic kathaon main 14 lokon kaa varnan paaya jaataa he inamen grih nahin ateddhan. 7 ucch tathaa 7 nimna. ucch lokon main sabase neeche he prithvi. ucch lok hai- bhu, bhuwas, swar, mahas, janas, tapas va satya jo samajya he. nimna lok hai - atal, vital, sutaal, rasaatal, talaatal, mahaatal tathaa pataal. mrityu ke nirnay dharmraj yama praani ke achche-bure karm dekhakar tay karaten hai ki aatma kis lok main jaae. ye itarlok keval asthai vaas sthaan hai. yahan se aatma ko pun: prithvi par janm lenaa hota he. aisa mana jaataa he ki keval prithvi lok se hi or manushya yoni ke baad hi karmon ke anusaar aatma ko moksh praapt ho saktaa he.
