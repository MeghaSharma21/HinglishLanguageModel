 shyopur zila madhya pradesh ke purvi bhaag main sthit he. shyopur ke purv main madhya pradesh ke shivpuri, pashchim main rajasthan ke kota, uttar main muraina, gwalior evam dakshin main rajasthan ke kota zile se judaa hua he. yah zila sadak maarg se vyavasthit rup se judaa hua he. shyopur gwalior nairogese line se bhi judaa hua he. yaha se vijaipur, karaahal or baroda ke liye parivahan sevaayen uplabdh hai. palpur kii wild life centuri yahan kaa mukhya paryaton sthal he. kaketa jalashay bhi mukhya rup se isi zile main he. yah zila lakdi ke furniture ke liye bhi prasiddh he. yahan par sagaun ke daravaaje, khidki aadi bahut hi khubsurat dhang se banaae jate hai. zile main mukhya rup se chambal, sip or kuno nadiyaan bahati hai. chambal nadi indore se hote hue dakshin-purv madhya pradesh kii ore bahati he. zile ke 15 pratishat gaanv sadak va rail maarg se jude hue hai. sadak maarg sabhi tehsil mukhyalayon se judaa hua he. shyopur kii karaahal tehsil main pradesh kii sahariya janajati nivas karti he.
