 
 sarkari ucch vidhyalay, sahid nagar bhubaneshwar ke centresthal main sthit ek sah-shiksha vidhyalay he. yahan chhathvin se dashavin tak kaksha main, oriya bhashaa main shikshadaan kiya jaataa he. is vidhyalay kii sthapana 1968 main kii gayi thi. pehle yah ek privet skul tha. 1971 main ise sarkari manyata mili. puspa rani praharaj, vartmaan main is vidhyalay kii pradhanadhyapika hai | 
 is vidhyalay main pradhanadhyapika ke alaava 20 shikshak or 2 anya karmchaaree he. skool campus ke andar ek khel maidan he. scout, red cross, iko club, science club or saanskrithik club aadi kii suvidha he. vaarshik patrika "shahid", vidhyaarthiyon ke srijan kala ko nikhaar ne main madad karata he. skool ke bachche vibhinn rajya or rashtriya str ke pratiyogitaaon main bhaag le kar safal hue hai |
