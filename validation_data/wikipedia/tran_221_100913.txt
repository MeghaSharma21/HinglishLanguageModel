 indian airlines flit 814 tribhuvan antarrashtriya hawaii adde se indira gandhi antarrashtriya hawaii adda jane vaali ek indian airlines airbas e300 thi jiskaa apharan kar liya gaya tha. ek pakistan sthit aatankwaadi sanghathan harakat ul mujahidin par iske apharan kaa aarope lagaaya gaya tha.
 lagbhag 17:30 baje iasti par bhartiya hawaii kshetra main pravesh ke turant baad sashastr bandookdhaariyon ne vimaan kaa apharan kar liya tha. amritsar, lahore or dubai kii dharti ko chune ke baad apharan kartaaon ne vimaan ko qandahar, afghanistan main utarane ke liye majaboor kiya. apartaaon ne 176 yatriyon main se 27 ko dubai main chhod diya lekin ek ko buree tarah chaaku se maaraa or kai anya ko ghayal kar diya.
 afghanistan main taliban-hukumat ke bare main bharat kii kam jaankaree ne bhartiya adhikariyon or apartaaon ke bich vaartaon ko mushkil bana diya. taliban ne bhartiya vishesh sainya balon dwaara vimaan par dhaava bolne se rokane kii koshish main apane sashastr ladaakon ko apahrit vimaan ke paas tainaat kar diya. apharan kaa yah silsilaa saat dinon tak chalaa or bharat dwaara teen islamic aatankwaadiyon - mushtak ahamed jargar, ahamed umr said sheikh or moulana masood azhar ko reeha karne ke baad samaapt hua.
 bhartiya or america khufia agenciyon ne apartaaon, al-kaayda or taliban ke bich pramanik sambandh hone kii report di. paanch aparankartaaon or teen reeha kiye gaye aatankwaadiyon ko taliban dwaara ek surakshit maarg pradaan kiya gaya. taliban dwaara sandigdh bhoomika nibhaaye jane kii vyapak rup se ninda kii gayi thi or iske baad yah bharat evam taliban ke bich sambandhon kii samaapti kaa kaaran bana.
 vimaan main 178 yatri savaar the jinamein se zyaadaatar bhartiya naagarik the jo nepal main chuttee bitaane ke baad bharat waapas aa rahe the.
 indian airlines flit 814  kaa apharan shuukravaar, 24 disambar 1999 ko bhartiya maanak samay lagbhag 05:30 baje vimaan ke bhartiya hawaii kshetra main pravesh ke kuch hi samay baad kar liya gaya tha. bharat sarkaar ke anusaar apartaaon kii pahchaan is prakaar thi: 
 isi-814 par maujuud mukhya flit atendent anil sharma ne baad main bataaya ki ek nakaabposh, chashmadhari aadmi ne vimaan ko bam se uda dene kii dhamkee di thi or captain devi sharan ko "pashchim kii ore udaan bharne" kaa aadesh diya tha. apaherta chaahate the ki captain sharan vimaan ko lakhanu ke upar se hote hue lahore kii ore le jaaen lekin pakistani adhikariyon ne turant isaki anumati dene se inkaar kar diya kyonki ve aatankwaadiyon ke saath sambandh jode jane se prati sachet the. iske alaava eendhan bhi paryaapt nahin tha. captain sharan ne apartaaon se kaha ki unhen amritsar, bharat main utarana hoga. 
 amritsar main captain sharan ne vimaan main dubaara eendhan bharne kaa anurodh kiya. haalanki, dilli main aapadaa prabandhan samooh ne amritsar hawaii adde ke adhikariyon ko yah sunishchit kar lene kaa nirdesh diya ki vimaan sthir tha, jahaan punjab police ke sashastr karmi is koshish ke liye pehle se maujuud the. unhen nai dilli se sweekruti nahin mili thi. antat ek eendhan tanker bheja gaya or vimaan tak pahunch ko band kar dene kaa nirdesh diya. jaise hi tanker vimaan kii ore bhaaga, hawaii yatayaat niyantran ne payalat ko dhimaa karne kaa radio sandesh bheja or tanker turant ruk gaya. is tarah achaanak ruk jane se apartaaon kaa andeh bad gaya or unhone hawaii yatayaat niyantran se manjuuri ke bina vimaan ko turant vahaan se dur le jane ke liye majboor kar diya. tanker vimaan se kuch hi feet kii duuri par rah gaya tha.
 atyant nunatam eendhan str ke kaaran vimaan ne lahore, pakistan main apaat landing kaa anurodh kiya. pakistan ne is dar se anurodh ko thukraa diya ki aatankwaadiyon ke saath unke desh kaa naam joda ja saktaa tha. pakistan ne apni hawaii yatayaat sevaaon ko bhi band kar diya, is prakaar indian airlines flit ke liye samooche pakistan ke hawaii kshetra prabhavi rup se prabandhit ho gaya or lahore hawaii adde par sabhi liten band kar di gayin. atisi se koi bhi madad nahin milaane se captain sharan ne apni drishyaatmak prerana par bharosa kiya or vahaan utarana shuru kar diya jise unhone ek ranve sochaa tha or baad main paaya ki yah keval ek acchhee roshni vaali sadak thi or phir samay par vimaan ko uda le gaye. yah samaj lene ke baad ki vimaan ke liye ekmaatr anya vikalp jameen par dharashayi kar dena hi shesh bachaa tha, lahore hawaii adde ne apni liten on kar di or vimaan ko utarane kii anumati de di. lahore hawaii adde ke adhikariyon ne vimaan main indhan bharaa or bhartiya maanak samay 22:32 baje ise lahore chhodane ke liye anumati de di. iske alaava, pakistani adhikariyon ne yatriyon main se kuch mahilaon or bacchon ko utaar dene ke isi-814 ke payalat ke anurodh ko thukraa diya. 
 vimaan ne dubai ke liye udaan bhari jahaan badanaseeb vimaan main savaar 27 yatriyon ko chhod diya gaya. apartaaon ne buree tarah se ghayal 25 varshiya bhartiya purush ripan katyal ko bhi chhod diya jisapar apartaaon ne kai baar chaaku se waar kiya tha. baad main ripan ne apni choton ke aage ghutane tech die or apharan kaa ekmaatr mritak vyakti ban gaya.
 in yatriyon ko chode jane ke fouran baad apahrit vimaan qandahar antarrashtriya hawaii adde ke liye ravaanaa ho gaya.
 qandahar main vimaan ke utarane ke baad talibani adhikariyon ne antarrashtriya manyata praapt karne kii ek koshish main bhartiya adhikariyon ke saath sahyog karne or apartaaon evam bharat sarkaar ke bich madhyasth kii bhoomika nibhaane kii sahmati de di. chunki bharat ne taliban-hukumat ko manyata nahin di thi, isane islamabad main apane ucchayog se ek adhikari ko qandahar bheja. taliban-hukumat ke saath bharat ke purv sampark naa hone ke kaaran baatcheet kii prakriya jatil ho gayi thi.
 haalanki taliban kaa iraadaa tab andeh ke daayre main aa gaya jab iske sashastr ladaakon ne apahrit vimaan ko gher liya. taliban kaa kahana tha ki sainya balon ko apartaaon dwaara bandhakon ko maarane yaa ghayal karne se rokane ke prayaas main tainaat kiya gaya tha lekin kuch vishleshak maanate hai ki aisa apartaaon ke khilaaf bhartiya sainya oparation ko rokane ke liye kiya gaya tha.
 apartaaon ne shuru main bhartiya jelon main kaid 35 islamic ugravadiyon kii rihaai or 200 million america dollar nagad kii maang kii thi lekin bhartiya vaartakar unki maang ko teen kaidiyon kii rihaai tak kam karne ke liye apartaaon ko manaane main kaamyaab rahe. ye kaidi the:
 ahamed umr said shaikh, jise 1994 main bharat main pashchimi deshon ke paryatakon ke apharan se sandarbh main kaid kiya gaya tha, usee ne dainiel parl kii hatya kii thi or kathith taur par america main 11 sitambar ke hamalon kii yojana taiyaar karne main ek mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai he.
 teenon aatankwaadiyon ke qandahar main utarane ke baad vimaan par savaar bandhakon ko reeha kar diya gaya tha. 31 disambar 1999 ko indian airlines falite 814 ke chode gaye bandhakon ne ek vishesh vimaan par waapas bharat ke liye udaan bhari.
