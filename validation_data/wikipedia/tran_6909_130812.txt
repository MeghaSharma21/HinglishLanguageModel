 kakshiya anunaad paaya jaataa he jab do yaa do se adhik pind kisi ek hi bade pind kii parikrama samaan avadhi main puri karte he. 
 udhaaharan ke liye brihaspati ke upgrahon genimad, europe or aayo kaa 1 : 2 : 4 kaa anunaad, tathaa pluto or neptune ke bich 2 : 3 kaa anunaad. aayo, europe or genimad brihaspati ke thik kramshah chaar, do va ek chakkar lagane main samaan samay lete he. isi tarah pluto surya ke do chakkar or neptune surya ke teen chakkar samaan samay main lagaate he. dhyaan rahe yah mahaj ek sanyog nahin he balki pranali in swarupon ko gurutvkarshan ke adheen dhaaran karti he jiske tahat wein ek duusare par prabhaav daalate he.
