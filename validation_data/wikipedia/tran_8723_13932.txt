 pandit chandrakant bali sanskrit sahitya marmajna evam kalganana visheshgy maane jate hai. 1914 main multan main janme pan bali kii ginati multan ke chotii ke vidwaanon main hoti thi. "prabandh panchanad", "doha maansarovar", "punjab prantiya hindi sahitya kaa itihaas", "kharvel prashasti", "aadi shankaracharya", "mahabharat yuddh kaal-mimasaa" or "jain kalganana" naam se unki saat pustaken bhi prakashit ho chuki hai. anek aitihaasik ghatnaon or panchangon ke sangyojan se pan bali ne ek anoothaa kaaltantra viksit kiya he.
