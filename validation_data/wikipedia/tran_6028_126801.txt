 mrityuj shitalan kaa matlab hota he 'marane ke baad sharir ke taapmaana main kramash badlaav aa janaa.' yah maut kaa doosraa charan hota he. marane ke baad sharir kaa taapmaana girane lagata he or sharir ekadam thanda pard jaataa he. kintu aasapaas kaa taapmaana 36.9 degri se adhik ho to shav kaa taapmaana ghatane ke bajaay badhane lagegaa.
 mrityuj shitalan se ham yah anumaan laga sakate hai ki mrityu kitna samay pehle hui hogi. uske liye visheshgy ko uskaa rectal taapmaana naapnaa padataa he kyonki rectal taapmaana hi mrit vyakti kaa sahi taapmaana darshata he. galesatr sameekaran dwaara yah anumaan kar sakate hai ki kitne ghante pehle mrityu hui he. is sameekaran main yah mana gaya he ki shitalan raikhik rup se ho rahaa he. ashitalan ke raikhik yah sameekaran nimnlikhit he-
 jahaan T degree celsius main malashya kaa taapmaana he.
 kyonki jab sharir apaghatit hone lagata he to uskaa taapmaana phir dobaaraa se badhane lagata he.
 mukhyatah taapmaana main parivertan ke kaaran yah ek acha kaarak nahin man ja saktaa jisase ham maut ke sahi samay kaa pataa laga saken. kyonki taapmaana main yah parivertan kai anya karkon par nirbhar karata he.
