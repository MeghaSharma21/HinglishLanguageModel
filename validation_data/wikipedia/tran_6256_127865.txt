 naatak, kavy kaa ek rup he. jo rachana shravan dwaara hi nahin apitu drushti dwaara bhi darshakon ke hriday main rasanubhuti karaati he use naatak yaa drishy-kavy kehete hai. naatak main shravy kavy se adhik romaniyata hoti he. shravy kavy hone ke kaaran yah lok chetana se apekshaakrit adhik ghanishtha rup se sambhddha he. naatyashaastra main lok chetana ko naatak ke lekhan or manchan kii mul prerana mana gaya he.
 naatak kii ginati kaavyon main he. kavy do prakaar ke maane gayi hai— shravy or drishy. isi drishy kavy kaa ek bhed naatak mana gaya he. par drushti dwaara mukhya rup se iskaa grahan hone ke kaaran drishy kavy maatr ko naatak kehane lage hai.
 bharatmuni kaa naatyashaastra is vishay kaa sabase pracheen granth miltaa he. agnipurana main bhi naatak ke lakshan aadi kaa nirupan he. usamen ek prakaar ke kavy kaa naam prakeern kaha gaya he. is prakeern ke do bhed he— kavy or abhinaeya. agnipurana main drishy kavy yaa roopak ke 27 bhed kahe gaye hai— 
 sahityadarpan main naatak ke lakshan, bhed aadi adhik spasht rup se die hai.
 upar likhaa ja chukaa he ki drishy kavy ke ek bhed kaa naam naatak he. drishy kavy ke mukhya do vibhaag hai— roopak or uparupak. roopak ke das bhed hai— roopak, naatak, prakaran, bhaan, vyayog, samavakaar, dim, ihamrig, ankavithi or prahasan. 'uparupak' ke atharah bhed hai— naatika, trotak, goshtee, sattak, natayaraasak, prasthaan, ullapya, kavy, prekshna, raasak, sanlapak, srigadith, shinpak, vilasika, durmallika, prakaranika, hallisha or bhanika.
 uparyukt bhedon ke anusaar naatak shabd drishy kavy maatr ke arth main bolte hai. sahityadarpan ke anusaar naatak kisi khyath vrutt ' kii lekar likhaanaa chahie. vah bahut prakaar ke vilaas, sukh, dukh, tathaa anek rason se yukt hona chahie. usamen paanch se lekar das tak ank hone chahie. naatak kaa nayak dhirodatt tathaa prakhyat vansh kaa koi pratapi purush yaa rajarshi hona chahie. naatak ke pradhan yaa angi ras shringaar or vir hai. shesh ras gaun rup se aate hai. shanti, karunaa aadi jis rupak main main prathan ho vah naatak nahin kahala saktaa. sandhisthal main koi vismayajanak vyapaar hona chahie. upsanhaar main mangal hi dikhaaya janaa chahie. viyogant naatak sanskrit alankaar shaastra ke viruddh he.
 abhinay aarambh hone ke pehle jo kriya hoti he, use purvarang kehete hai. purvarang, ke uparaant pradhan nat yaa sutaradhar, jise sthaapak bhi kehete hai, aakar sabha kii prashansa karata he phir nat, nati sutaradhar ityadi paraspar vaartalap karte hai jisamen khele janevaale naatak kaa prastaav, kavi-vansh-varnan aadi vishay aa jate hai. naatak ke is ansh ko prastaavna kehete hai. jis itivrutt ko lekar naatak rachaa jaataa he use 'vastu' kehete hai. 'vastu' do prakaar kii hoti he—aadhikarik vastu or praasangik vastu. jo samast itivrutt kaa pradhan nayak hota he use 'adhikari' kehete hai. is adhikari ke sambandh main jo kuch varnan kiya jaataa he use 'aadhikarik vastu kehete hai; jaise, ramlila main ram kaa charitra. is adhikari ke upkaar ke liye yaa raspushti ke liye prasangvash jiskaa varnan aa jaataa he use praancegik vastu kehete hai; jaise sugriv, aadi kaa charitra. 'saamne laane' arthath drishy sanmukh upasthit karne ko abhinay kehete hai. atah avasthanurup anukaran yaa swaang kaa naam hi abhinay he. abhinay chaar prakaar kaa hota he— angik, vaachik, aharya or satvik. angon kii cheshtaa se jo abhinay kiya jaataa he use angik, vachanon se jo kiya jaataa he use vaachik, bhes banaakar jo kiya jaataa hai use aharya tathaa bhaavon ke udrek se camp, swead aadi dwaara jo hota he use satvik kehete hai. naatak main beej, bindu, pataakaa, prakari or kaary in paanchon ke dwaara prayojan siddhi hoti he. jo baat munh se kehete hi chaaron ore fail jaay or falsiddhi kaa pratham kaaran ho use beej kehete hai, jaise venisanhaar naatak main bhim ke krodh par yudhisthir kaa utsaahvaaky draupadi ke keshmojan kaa kaaran hone ke kaaran beej he. koi ek baat puri hone par duusare vaakya se uskaa sambandh na rahane par bhi usamen aise vaakya laana jinaki duusare vaakya ke saath asangati na ho 'bindu' he. bich main kisi vyapak prasang ke varnan ko pataakaa kehete hai— jaise uttarcharit main sugriv kaa or abhigyaan- shankutal main vidooshak kaa charitravarnan. ek desh vyapee charitravarnan ko prakari kehete hai. aarambh kii hui kriya kii falsiddhi ke liye jo kuch kiya jaay use kaary kehete hai; jaise, ramlila main ravan vadh. kisi ek vishay kii charchaa ho rahi ho, isi bich main koi doosraa vishay upasthit hokar pehle vishay se mail main maaloom ho vahaan pataakaa sthaan hota he, jaise, ramcharit main ram sita se kah rahe hai—'he priye ! tumhaari koi baat mujhe asay nahin, yadi asay he to keval tumhaara virah, isi bich main pratihaari aakar kahata he : dev ! durmukh upasthit. yahan ' upasthit' shabd se 'virah upasthit' aisi prathit hota he or ek prakaar kaa chamatkaar maaloom hota he. sanskrit sahitya main naatak sambandhi aise hi anek kaushalon kii udbhaavna kii gai he or anek prakaar ke vibhed dikhaae gaye hai.
 aajkal deshbhashaon main jo nae naatak likhe jate hai unamem sanskrit naatakon ke sab niyamon kaa paalan yaa vishyon kaa samavesh anaavashyak samajha jaataa he. bhaaratendu harishchandra likhte hai—'sanskrit naatak kii bhanti hindi naatak main unka anusandhan karnaa yaa kisi naatakang main inako ytnpurvak rakhkar naatak likhnaa vyarth he; kyonki pracheen lakshan rakhkar aadhunik naatakadi kii shobhaa sampadan karne se ulta fal hota he or ytn vyarth ho jaataa he.
 bharat main abhinay-kala or rangamanch kaa vaidic kaal main hi nirmaan ho chukaa tha. tatpashchaat sanskrit rangamanch to apni unnati kii paraakaashtha par pahunch gaya tha-bharat muni kaa naatyashaastra iskaa pramaan he. bahut pracheen samay main bharat main sanskrit naatak dharmik avasaron, saanskrithik parvon, saamaajik samarohon evam rajkeey bolchaal kii bhashaa nahin rahi to sanskrit naatakon kii manchikaran samaapt-sa ho gaya.
 bharatvarsh main naatakon kaa prachaar bahut pracheen kaal se hai. bharat muni kaa naatayashastra bahut purana he. ramayana, mahabharat, harivansh ityadi main nat or naatak kaa ullekh he. panini ne 'shilaali' or 'krishashv' naamak do natsutrakaron ke naam liye hai. shilaali kaa naam shukla yajurvedic shatapath brahman or samavedic anupad sutara main miltaa hai. vidwaanon ne jyotish kii gananaa ke anusaar shatapath brahman ko 4000 varsh se upar kaa batalaaya he. atah kuch paashchaatya vidwaanon kii yah raay haq greece yaa yunaan main hi sabase pehle naatak kaa pradurbhaav hua, thik nahin he. harivansh main likhaa he ki jab pradhyumna, samb aadi yadav rajkumaar vajranabh ke poor main gaye the tab vahaan unhone ramajanm or rambhaavisar naatak khele the. pehle unhone nepathy bandha tha jiske bheetar se striyon ne madhur swar se gaan kiya tha. shur naamak yadav ravan bana tha, manovati naam kii stri rambha bani thi, pradhyumna nalkubar or samb vidooshak bane the. wilson aadi paashchaatya vidwaanon ne spasht sweekaar kiya hai ki hinduon ne apane yahan naatak kaa pradurbhaav apane aap kiya tha. pracheen hindu raja bady bady rangashalas banavaate the. madhyapradesh main sarguja ek pahari sthaan he, vahaan ek gupha ke bheetar is prakaar kii ek rangashaala ke chihn paae gaye hai. yah thik he ki unaniyon ke aane ke purv ke sanskrit naatak aajkal nahin milte hai, par is baat se inkaa abhaav, itane pramaanon ke rahete, nahin mana ja saktaa. sambhav he, kalasampann unani jati se jab hindu jati kaa milan hua ho tab jis prakaar kuch or or baaten ek ne duusare kii grahan kii. isi prakaar naatak ke sambandh main kuch baaten hinduon ne bhi apane yahan lee hon. bahyapati kaa 'jawanika' naam dekh kuch log yavan sansarg suchit karte hai. ankon main jo 'drishy' sanskrit naatakon main aae hai unase anumaan hota he ki in paton par chitra bane rahete the. astu adhik se adhik is vishay main yahi kaha ja saktaa he ki atyant pracheen kaal main jo abhinay hua karte the. unamem chitrapat kaam main nahin laae jate the. sikandar ke aane ke peeche unka prachaar hua. ab bhi ramlila, raasalila bina paradon ke hoti hi hai.
 madhyakaal main pradeshik bhashaaon main loktantr kaa udai hua. yah vicitra sanyog he ki muslimkaal main jahaan shaskaon kii dharmakattarata ne bharat kii saahitiyik rang-parampara ko tod daalaa vahaan lokbhashaon main lokmanch kaa acha prasar hua. raasalila, ramlila tathaa nautanki aadi ke rup main lokdharmee natyamanch bana rahaa. bhaktikaal main ek ore to braj pradesh main krishna kii raasalilaon kaa brajabhasha main atyadhik prachlan hua or duusari or vijaydashami ke avasar par samooche bharat ke chote-bade nagaron main ramlila badi dhoomdhaam se manaai jane lagi.
 saahitiyik drushti se is madhyakaal main kuch sanskrit naatakon ke padhyabaddh hindi chhayanuwaad bhi hue, jaise nevaaj krit ‘abhigyaan shaakuntal’, somnath krit ‘malti-madhav’, hridayramcharita ‘hanumannatak’ aadi; kuch moulik padhyabaddh sanvadatmak rachanaayen bhi huin, jaise lachhiraam krit ‘karunaabharan’, raghuram nagar krit ‘sabhasaar’, ganesh kavi krit ‘pradhyumnvijay’ aadi; par inamen naatakeey paddati kaa purnataya nirvaah nahin hua. ye keval sanvadatmak rachanaayen hi kahi ja sakati hai.
 is prakaar saahitiyik drushti tathaa saahitiyik rachanaaon ke abhaav ke kaaran madhyakaal main saahitiyik rangakarm kii ore koi pravriti nahin hui. sach to yah he ki aadhunik kaal main vyavsaayik tathaa saahitiyik rangamanch ke udai se purv hamaare desh main ramlila, nautanki aadi ke lokmanch ne hi chaar-paanch sau varshon tak hindi rangamanch ko jivit rakhaa. yah lokmanch-parampara aaj tak vibhinn roopon main samooche desh main vartmaan he. uttar bharat main ramlilaon ke atirikt mahabharat par aadhaarit ‘vir abhimanyu’, ‘satya harishchandra’ aadi drame tathaa ‘rup-basant’, ‘hir-raanjha’, ‘hakikatraay’, ‘bilwamangal’ aadi nautankiyan aaj tak prachlit hai.
 aadhunik kaal main angreji rajya kii sthapana ke saath rangamanch ko protsaahan mila. falat: samooche bharat main vyavsaayik naatak mandaliyaan sthapit huin. natyarangan kii pravriti sarvapratham banglaa main dikhaai di. san 1835 i. ke aasapaas kalakatta main kai avyavsaayik rangashaalaon kaa nirmaan hua. kalakatta ke kuch sambhrant pariwaron or raison ne inke nirmaan main yoga diya tha or duusari ore vyavayayik naatak mandaliyon ke asaahitiyik prayaas se alag tha.
 banglaa ke is natya-srijan or natyarangan kaa adhyayan isliye mahatvapurn he, kyonki bharatendu harishchandr ke rangandolan ko isi se dasha, disha or prerana mili thi. banglaa ke is aarmabhik saahitiyik prayaas main jo naatak rache gaye ve mul sanskrit yaa angreji naatakon ke chhayanuwaad yaa roopaantar the. spasht he ki bharatendu kaa aarmabhik prayaas bhi sanskrit naatakon ke chhayanuwaad kaa hi tha.
 hindi rangamanchi saahitiyik naatakon main sabase pehla hindi geetinaty amaanat krit ‘inder sabha’ kaha ja saktaa he jo san 1853 i. main lakhanu ke nawab waajid alishaah ke darbar main khelaa gaya tha. isamen urdu-style kaa vaisaa hi prayog tha jaisaa parsi naatak mandaliyon ne apane naatakon main apanaaya. san 1862 i. main kaashi main ‘janaki mangal’ naamak vishuddh hindi naatak khelaa gaya tha.
 uparyukt saahitiyik rangamanch ke uparyukt chhutput prayaas se bahut aage badhkar parsi mandaliyon-original victoria, ampress victoria, elfinston theetrical company, alfred theetrical tathaa new alfred company aadi-ne vyavsaayik rangamanch banaaya. sarvapratham bombai or baad main hyderabad, lakhanu, banaras, dilli lahore aadi kai kendron or sthanon se ye companiyaan desh-bhar main gum-ghumkar hindi naatakon kaa pradarshan karne lageen. in parsi naatak mandaliyon ke liye pehle-pahal nasarbani khan sahab, raunak banarsi, vinayak prasad ‘talib’, ‘ahasan’ aadi lekhakon ne naatak likhe. janata kaa sasta manoranjan or dhanoparjan hi in companiyon kaa mukhya uddeshy tha.
 isi se ucchkoti ke saahitiyik naatakon se inkaa vishesh prayojan nahin tha. dharmik-puranic tathaa prem-pradhan naatakon ko hi ye apane rangamanch par dikhaati thim. saste or ashleel pradarshan karne main inhen jaraa bhi sankoch nahin tha. isi se janata kii ruchi bhrasht karne kaa dosha in par lagaaya jaataa he. bhraman ke kaaran in companiyon kaa rangamanch bhi inke saath ghumta rahata tha.
 kisi sthaayee rangamanch kii sthapana inke dwaara bhi sambhav nahin thi. rangamanch kaa dhaanchaa balliyon dwaara nirmit kiya jaataa tha or stage par chitra-vicitra parde lataka die jate the. bhadkeeli-chatakili veshbhusha, pardon kii nai-nai chitrakaari tathaa chamatkaarpurn drishy-vidhaan kii ore inkaa adhik dhyaan rahata tha. pardon ko drishyon ke anusaar uthaaya-giraaya jaataa tha. sangeet-vaadya kaa aayojan stage ke agle bhaag main hota tha. gambhir drishyon ke bich-bich main bhi bhadde hasyapurna drishy jaanboojhkar rakhe jate the. bich-bich main shaayari, gajalen or tukbandi khub chalati thi. bhashaa urdu-hindi kaa mishrit rup thi. samvaad padya-rup tathaa tukpurna khub hote the.
 radheshyaam kathavachak, narayanprasad betaab, aagaahashr kashmiri, harikrishna johar aadi kuch aise naatakkaar bhi hue hai jinhone parsi rangamanch ko kuch saahitiyik put dekar sudhaarney kaa praytn kiya he or hindi ko is vyavsaayik rangamanch par laane kii cheshtaa kii. par vyavsaayik vritti ke kaaran sambhavat: is rangamanch par sudhaar sambhav nahin tha. isi se in naatakakaron ko bhi vyavsaayik ban janaa padaa. is prakaar parsi rangamanch na viksit ho sakaa, na sthaayee hi ban sakaa.
 hindi main naatakon kaa prarambh bharatendu harishchandra se mana jaataa he. us kaal ke bharatendu tathaa unke samakaleen naatakakaron ne lok chetana ke vikaas ke liye naatakon kii rachana kii isliye us samay kii saamaajik samasyaon ko naatakon main abhivyakt hone kaa acha avasar mila.
 jaisaaki kaha ja chukaa he, hindi main avyavsaayik saahitiyik rangamanch ke nirmaan kaa shriganesh aagaahasan ‘amaanat’ lakhanvi ke ‘inder sabha’ naamak geeti-roopak se mana ja saktaa he. par sach to yah he ki ‘inder sabha’ kii vastav main rangamanchi kriti nahin thi. isamen shaamiyane ke neeche khulaa stage rahata tha. nautanki kii tarah teen ore darshak baithte the, ek ore takht par raja inder kaa asana laga diya jaataa tha, saath main pariyon ke liye kursiyaan rakhi jaati thim. sazindon ke peeche ek laal rang kaa pardaa lataka diya jaataa tha. isi ke peeche se paatro kaa pravesh karaayaa jaataa tha. raja inder, pariyaan aadi paatr ek baar aakar vahiin upasthit rahete the. ve apane samvaad bolkar waapas nahin jate the.
 us samay natyarangan itnaa lokapriy hua ki amaanat kii ‘inder sabha’ ke anukaran par kai sabhaayen rachi gai, jaise ‘madarilal kii inder sabha’, ‘daryai inder sabha’, ‘hawaii inder sabha’ aadi. parsi naatak mandaliyon ne bhi in sabhaaon or majaliseparistaan ko apanaaya. ye rachanaayen naatak nahin thi or na hi inase hindi kaa rangamanch nirmit hua. isi se bharatendu harishchandr inako 'naatakabhas' kehete the. unhone inaki parody ke rup main ‘bandar sabha’ likhi thi.
 sanskrit naatakon kaa yug dhalane laga tab chaudaavin shatabdi se unnisvi shatabdi tak unka sthaan vibhinn bhartiya bhashaaon main lok naatakon ne liya. aaj alag-alag pradeshon main lok naatak bhinn-bhinn naamon se janaa jaataa he.
