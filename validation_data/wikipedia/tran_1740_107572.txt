 ireland rajshahi, ireland dweep par 1542 se 1800 ke bich vidyaman angrezi rajshahi kaa ek kathputli rajya tha. yah tab astitv main ai jab 1542 main ireland kii sansad ne crown of ireland act paarit kar england ke raja henry ashtam ko ireland kaa raja ghoshit kar diya. rajtantr ghoshit hone se pehle, is rajya ko england riyasat kii ek jaagir hone kaa pad praapt tha.
 is rajya kii sthaapan ke praarambhik varshon main irish rajtantr ko anya europea rajyon dwaara manyata praapt nahin thi. haalaanki, kuch protostant rajyon ne ise manyata di thi, parantu kisi bhi catholic rajya ne ireland ko ek vaazib rajtantr or england ke shaasha ko irish singhasana kaa vaidhik vaaris maanane se inkaar kar diya tha. varsh 1955 main pope paul chaturth ne raja henry kii beti or uttaradhikari, rani mairi pra ko ireland kii rani hone kii manyata pradaan kii.
 varsh 1800 main great britain or ireland kii saansadon main acts of union ke paarit hone ke kaaran iskaa vilay great britain rajshahi ke saath hogaya, or ireland kii sarkaar, prashasan tathaa rajmukut ko great britain ke saath mila kar ek sanyukt riyasat ko sthapit kiya gaya. is sanyukt rajya kaa naam great britain or ireland kaa united kingdom rakhaa gaya, jisame se ireland ke paanch-chhyai bhaag 1922 main britain se irish mukt rajya ke rup main swatantra hogaya.
