 paamulapati venkat ranga raav bhartiya rashtriya congress se bhartiya raajnneetijna the.
 unka janm hyderabad rajya ke karimnagar jile ke vangaara gaanv main hua. unke pita bharat ke purv pradhanmantri paamulapati venkat narsingh raav the or mata kaa naam satyama tha. unhone apni snaatak evam snatakottar kii maanad osmania vishvavidhyalay se praapt kii. unke do chote bhai hai jinka naam pi ve rajeshwar raav evam pi ve prabhakar raav he.
 ve do baar warangal jile kii hanmakonda se vidhayak or vidhaan parishad sadsy bhi the. unhen ke ve reddy ke kaarykaal main shiksha mantri ke rup main kebinet kaa sadsy bhi banaaya gaya. unka 31 july 2013 ko nidhan ho gaya.
