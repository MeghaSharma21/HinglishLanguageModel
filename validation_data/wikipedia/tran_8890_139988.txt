 parabrahm, kaa shabdic arth he 'sarvocch brahm' - vah brahm jo sabhi varnanon or sankalpanaaon se bhi pare he. advait veydant kaa nirgun brahm bhi parabrahm he. vaishnav or shaiva sampradaayon main bhi kramshah vishnu tathaa shiv ko parabrahm mana gaya he.
 vastav main keval itani paribhaasha hi sambhav ho sakati he kiyonki samast jagat brahm ke antargat mana gaya he man vichaar buddhi aadi ! uttam se atiuttam vichaar, bhav, veda, shaastra mantra, tantra, aadhunik vijan yotish aadi kisi bhi madhyam se uski paribhaasha nahin ho sakati! vah gunathit, bhavathit, maaya, prakrati or brahm se pare or param he. vah ek hi he do yaa anek nahin he. manishiyon ne kaha he ki brahm se bhi pare ek satta he jise vaani ke dwaara vyakt nahin kiya ja saktaa. vedon main use neti -neti kaha he. vah sanathan he, sarvavyaapak he, satya he, param he. vah samast jeev nirjeev samast astitv kaa ekmaatr param kaaran sarvasamarth sarvagyani he. vah vaani or buddhi kaa vishay nahin he upanishdon ne kaha he ki samast jagat brahm pe tikaa he or brahm parabrahm pe tikaa he.
