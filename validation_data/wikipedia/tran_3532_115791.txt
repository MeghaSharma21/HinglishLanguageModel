 rajkumaar shukla bharat ke swatantraaa sangram ke samay bihar ke champaran ke nivasee or swatantraaa senaanee the.
 disambar 1916 main congress ke rashtriya adhiveshan main bihar ke kisaanon ke pratinidhi banakar vah lakhanu gaye or champaran ke kisaanon kii durdasha ko sheersh netaaon ke samaksh rakhaa. nil kii kheti karanevaale raiyat rajkumaar shukla ke anurodh par mahatma gandhi champaran aane ko taiyaar hue. rajkumaar shukla ke saath baapu 10 april 1917 ko kolkata se patna or mujffarpur hote hue motihaari gaye. nil kii fasal ke lagu tinkathiyaa kheti ke virodh main gandhiji ne champaran main satyagrah kaa pehla safal prayog kiya.
 angrejon kii tatkaaleen teen kathiyaa vyavastha ke tahat har bighas main 3 katthe jameen par nil kii kheti karne kii kisaanon ke liye vivashtaa utpann karne kaa virodh karte hue pandit rajkumaar shukla ne vahaan kisaan andolan kii shuruyaat kii, jiske evaj main dand swaroop unhen kai baar angrejon ke kode or pratadna kaa shikaar hona padaa. kshetra ke kisaanon kii badahaal sthiti ko dekhte hue pandit shukla ne mahatma gandhi ko baar-baar vahaan aane kaa aagrah kiya to gandhiji inkaar nahin kar sake. ve champaran pahunche or phir vahaan ke kisaanon ke andolan ko jo dhar mili, unhone desh ko aazaadee ke mukaam tak pahunchaa diya. darasal, champaran kisaan andolan hi desh kii aazaadee kaa asli samvahak bane tha. vahaan ke kisaanon ke tyaag, balidaan or sangharsh kii vajah se aaj ham aaazaad bharat main saansen lene ke liye swatantra hai.
