 pracheen aryaavart ke aryapurush astr-shastr vidya main nipun the. unhone aadhyatm-gyaan ke saath-saath aatatiyon or dushton ke daman ke liye sabhi astr-shastron kii bhi sristi kii thi. aaryon kii yah shakti dharm-sthapana main sahaayak hoti thi. pracheen kaal main jin astr-shastron kaa upyog hota tha, unka varnan is prakaar he- 
 vaidic kaal main astrashastron kaa vargeekaran is prakaar tha :
 astron ko do vibhaagon main baanta gaya he- 
 neeche kuch astr-shastron kaa varnan kiya gaya he, jinka pracheen sanskrit-granthon main ullekh he.
 in astron ke atirikt bhushundi aadi anya anek astron kaa varnan vibhinn granthon main miltaa he.
