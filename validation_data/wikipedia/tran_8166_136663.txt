 mccluskieganj jharkhand kii rajdhani ranchi se maatr 40 kilometre kii duri par avasthit he.is shahar main ek samay anglo-indian bady sankhya main basate the.lekin ab inaki sankhya main baari giravat ai.
 mccluskieganj kii sthapana colonigeshan socity of india dwaara 1933 main kigai thi.yah anglo-indian ke liye home land yani mulk ke rup main tha.1932 main edward thomas maikluski ne pure bharat main rah rahe lagbhag 200000 anglo-indian ko yahaan basane kaa nyotaa bheja tha.lagbhag 300 parivaar yahaan aakar basen or ab keval 20 parivaar hi bache, zyaadaatar parivaar dwitiya vishwa yudh ke baad yahaan se austrailia, america, va europee deshon main chale gayi.
 shahar kii shanti, hariyaali or dhul dhusarit sadaken dekhne hi banati.
 aaj zyaadaatar puraani haveli ko guest house me parivartit kar diya gaya he. jiskaa prayog paryatak yahaan thaharne ke liye karte he.dug dugi nadi or jagriti vihaar paryatakon ke mukhya aakarshan kaa kendra hota he.
 mandir, masjid or gurudwara kii aamane saamne kii sthiti bhi logon ko aakarshith karti he.
 shahar me don basco academy kii sthapana kii gai he.
 pura naam arnest timothy mccluski tha. peshey se mccluski kalakatta main ek sampatti dealer tha. vah shikaar ke liye is kshetra main kuch gaanvon kaa daura karata tha or usne harhu naamak sthaan par ek asthaayee makaan banaaya. unke dost peepi sahib ratu maharaja sampatti ke prabandhak ke rup main kaam kiya or yah peepi, jo maharaja ko aashwast kar mccluski ke liye bhoomi patte kaa intjaam karavaaya.ratu maharaja se patte par 10,000 acre jameen arnest timothy mccluski ko mili. is kram main 1933 main, mccluski ne colonoijeshan sosayadi of india limited kaa gathan kiya gaya tha or maharaja ke saath ek samjhaute par hastakshar kiye. yah nirnay liya gaya ki anglo bharatiyon nau gaanvon main un gaanvon ke muulavaasiyon ke jameenon or sampatti par kabja nahi karegein or nadi, naalon, pahado par anglo bharatiyon kaa koi haq nahi hoga.
 Colonisation sosayadi Harhu, Duli, Ramdagga, Konka, Lapra, Hesalong, Mayapur, Mohulia or Baseria ke gaanvon main bhoomi prasar ke 10,000 acre jameen kaa adhigrahan kiya. samaaj ke ek company ke rup main panjikrut kiya gaya tha or anglo bharatiyon ko nai colony main basane kii kaamana ke liye sheyaron kii bikri shuru kar diya.
 isaki sharuaat acchhee tarah se shuru hua. hajaaron share bike or lagbhag 350 pariwaron ke basane ke liye aayaa tha. anglo bharatiyon ne ek shahar ke sansthapana kaa sapana dekha tha jo apni khud kii mathrubhumi kahala sake. yah ek swapnlok hi sabit hua, ek doordarshee ko sapana aayaa tha ki kabhi bhi sach nahin tha.
