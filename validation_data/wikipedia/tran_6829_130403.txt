 janelan guphaayen purvi austrailia main sthit prasiddh paryaton sthal he. blue mountain pathaar, new south wales ke pashchimi kinare par silurine chooney ke patharwale pradesh main, chaude bhubhag par uttar se dakshin kii ore faili hui, sidney nagar se 110 kimi dur pashchim kii ore sthit hai. inke saundarya ko dekhakar 1866 itho main yahan kii sarkaar ne inhen bhramanakendre kaa rup de diya. ab ye guphaayen rangabirange vidyut prakaash se sushobhit kar di gai hai. 
 chaar paanch staron par faili hui in gufaaon kii sankhya lagbhag 12 he, jinamein kai avashail tathaa gulabi or bangnee rang ke unche unche bhrigu bade hi manamohak hai. ye sampurna guphaayen, jenolan nadi ke pani main silyurian chooney ke pathar ghulne se bani hai, sabase puraani gupha jenolan nadi ke tat se 250 foot kii oonchaai par he. yahan par bana 400 foot unchaa prakritik maharaab he, jo prakritik surang kaa kaam detaa he. iske bheetar se hokar sadak gai he. in gufaaon ke avlokanarth prati varsh lakhon bhramanarthi aate hai.
