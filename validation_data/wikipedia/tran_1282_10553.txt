 
 revange ek america television drama shrunkhlaa he, jisaki rachana mike kali ne kii he tathaa jisamen mukhya bhoomika main hai madelin sto va emily vine-camp. shrunkhlaa kaa premear sitambar 21, 2012 ko abisi chanal par hua tha. mukhya kathanak francisi lekhak alexander duma dwaara rachit da count of monte christoâ€Ž upanyaas se prerit he. 
 shrunkhlaa kii kahani new york ke long island ke hampton shahar main rahane vaale dhani va samrudh gracen parivaar ke aasapaas gumti he. kai varsh pehle, conred or victoria gracen ne apane mitra david clark ko aatankwaadi hone va ek vimaan durghatana ke liye, jisamen kai logon kii jaan gai thi, jimmedaar hone ke liye fasaa diya tha. david ko is aparaadh ke liye sajaa hoti he or jail main rahete hue uski mrityu hi jaati he. varshon baad david kii putree amenda clark apane pita kii mrityu kaa badla lene ke liye emily thorn ke naam ke saath hampton shahar waapas aati he.
 shrunkhlaa ko aalochakon ne saraahaa tha tathaa ise acchhee television reating praapt hui. oktober 13, 2011, tak kaarykram ke chaar episod puuren hone ke pashchaat abisi ne shrunkhlaa ko puure 22 episod tak ke liye naveekrut kar diya tha. madelin sto ko kaarykram main apni nibhaai gai victoria gracen kii bhoomika ke liye golden globe kii best actress drama series shrenee main namit kiya gaya tha.
 shrunkhlaa kii kahani long island, new york kii sfak county ke ek samrudh nagar hampton main chalati he. shahar main arbapati emily thorn rahane ke liye aati he jo asal main amenda clark he. amenda david clark kii putree he, jise 1995 main hui ek vimaan durghatana ke kaaran 197 yatriyon kii maut kaa doshi samajha jaataa he. use epabiai agenton ne giraftaar kar liya tha or jald hi doshi qaraar kar diya. david ke doston va sahakarmiyon, conred or victoria gracen, va uski secretary ledia davis ne uske khilaaf gavaahi di thi. victoria kaa david ke saath prem prasang tha, parantu use david ko dhokhaa dene par conred ne, yah dhamkee de kar ki vah donon ke saajhe bete dainiel ko kho sakati he, majaboor kar diya tha. david ko deshadroh ke kiye doshi tehraaya gaya or kuch varshon ke baad jail main uski mrityu ho gai. david kii giraftaar ke baad amenda ko pehle ek anathalay bheja gaya tathaa phir ek kishoron ke liye ek bani vishesh sudhaarak suvidha main, jahaan uski mulaakaat emily thorn se hoti he. anathalay main jane se pehle, amenda ne apane dost jaik porter ko apana kutta saimi de diya tha. jab vah 18 varsh hone ke pashchaat juvenil se bahaar aati he tab use nolan ross lene aata he; nolan ne david kii madad se safalataa hasil kii thi or vartmaan samay main ek karodpati he. vah use bataata he ki uske pita ko galat tareeke se doshi tehraaya gaya tha or vah nolan kii company main 49 pratishat hissedari kii maalik he. 
 varshon baad, amenda un logon se, jinhone uske parivaar kaa jivan nasht kiya tha, badla lene ke liye hampton shahar emily thorn ke naam se aati he. vah usee ghar ko kiraaye par leti he jahaan varshi pehle vah apane pita ke saath rahati thi. vartmaan samay main yah ghar david kii rakhel ledia davis ke paas he. ledia uske pita kii doshasiddhi ke liye jimmedaar vyaktiyon kii suchi main uskaa pehla lakshya he. is bich, vah victoria gracen ke bete dainiel ke saath prem sambandh banaati he, jiske kaaran victoria chintit hon jaati he tathaa apni navagantuk padosi ke ateet ke bare main apane sansadhano kii madad se tahakikat karnaa shuru kar detii he. 
 shrunkhlaa ke pehle satr main ek yaa ek se adhik vishesh rup se pradarshit aavarti paatr bhi shaamil hai. yah vah paatr hai jo mukhya kalakaaron kii tarah sabhi prakarnon main nahin dikhaai die.
