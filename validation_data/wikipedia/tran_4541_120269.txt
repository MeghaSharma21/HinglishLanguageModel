 utsarjan siddhant ko vishisht aapekshikta siddhant kaa pratispardhda siddhant bhi kaha jaataa he, kyonki yah siddhant bhi mikelson morle prayog ke parinaamon ko samajhane main sakasham rahaa tha. utsarjan siddhant prakaash sancharan ke liye koi pradhan nirdesh tantra nahin hone ke kaaran aapekshikta siddhant kaa paalan karte hai, lekin ye siddhant nishcharata abhigruhit ke sthaan par strot ke saapeksh utsarjit prakaash kaa prakaash ke veg c lete hai. atah utsarjan siddhant saral newtoneey siddhant ke saath vidyutgatiki or yantriki ko jodatii he. yaddapi yahan vaigyanik mukhyadhara ke baahar is siddhant ke samarthak ab tak bhi hai, yah siddhant adhikansh vaigyanikon dwaara ant main upyog rahit mana jaataa he.
 utsarjan siddhanto se sabase adhik naam ijak newton kaa naam joda jaataa he.
 utsarjan siddhanton ko parakhane ke liye yah vyavastha di sitor dwaara prastaavit kii gai:
 yahan c prakaash kaa veg, v strot kaa veg, c′ prakaash kaa parinami veg he or k ek niyatank he jiskaa maan 0 se 1 ke madhya ho saktaa he or yah strot ke kshatra par nirbhar karata he. visisth aapekshikta siddhant va sthir ether ke anusaar k=0 hota he jabaki utsarjan siddhant ke anusaar yah maan 1 tak ho saktaa he. anek bhaumik prayog bahut laghu duriyon par kiye gayi, jahaan koi ether ghaseetne athwa koi anya prabhaav prabhavi naa ho or punah parinaamon main yahi pradarshit hua ki prakaash kaa veg strot par nirbhar nahin karata, parinamswaroop utsarjan siddhanton kaa khandan ho gaya.
