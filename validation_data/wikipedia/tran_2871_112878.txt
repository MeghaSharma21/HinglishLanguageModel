 bauddhik smapda adhikaar maanav-mastishk kii upaj hai. duniya ke desh, kai sadiyon se apane-apane kanoon bana kar inhe surakshit karte chale aa rahen hai. san 1995 main vishwa vyapaar sanghathan bana. Agreement on the Trade related aspect of intellectual property rights yaa trips, is sanghathan kaa ek samjhauta he. saare desh jo vishwa vyapaar sanghathan ke sadsy hai, unhe ise maananaa he tathaa apane kanoon isi ke mutabik banane hai. ham bhi boudhik smapda adhikaar se sambandhit kanoonon ko isi ke kaaran badal rahe hai taaki vah trips mutabik ho jaye| kai logon kaa isi liye kahana he ki ham log kanoon isaliye nahi badal rahen hai ki hamen unki aavashyaktaa he par is liye ki trips kahata he tathaa vishwa vyapaar sanghathan evam trips ke kaaran hamane apni prabhutta kho di he. khayr yah vivaad alag he hamen to open sorce sauftver ke bare main baat kerani he tathaa keval isi sambandh main boudhik smapda adhikaron ke bare main thodi bahut jaankaree kar rahen hai.
 trips main saat tarah ke boudhik smapda adhikaar ke bare main charchaa kii gayi he. bharatvarsh me nimna aath adhinium ke andar bauddhik smapda adhikaar surakshit kiye gayi hai
