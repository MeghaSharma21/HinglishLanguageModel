 bhavy gandhi bhartiya abhinayta hai. yah mukhyatah tarak mehta kaa ulta chashma main tapu yaa tipendra jethalaal gada naamak kirdaar ke liye jane jate hai. yah 2010 main striker naamak ek film main balya kalakaar ke rup main sooryakanth naamak ek kirdaar nibhaayaa tha.
 inke abhinay kii shuruyaat tarak mehta kaa ulta chashma naamak dharawahik se hui thi. jiskaa prasaaran sab tivi par 28 july 2008 se shuru hua tha. isamen yah tipendra jethalaal gada jise pyaar se tappu kehete hai, kaa kirdaar nibhaa rahe the. 2010 main striker namak film main bal abhinayta ke rup main sooryakanth kaa kirdaar nibhaayaa tha. is film main inke saath siddharth, vidya malwaade, padam priya, anupam kher, seemaa biswas or aditya pancholi ne bhi kaam kiya tha.
 2017 main tarak mehta kaa ulta chashma ke dharawahik ko inhone chhod diya. bhavy ne telechakkar ko is dharawahik ko chhodane ke kaaran poochne se bataaya ki "haan maine ise janvari main chhod diya, iske aath saal or aath maheene kaa safar kaaphi acha rahaa, maine tarak... kii team or asit modi ke saath iskaa kaaphi anand liya or jis tarah se mere prashansakon ne mujhe pyaar diya, main chahta hoon ki usee tarah bhavishya main bhi mujhe isi tarah pyaar karen." inase jude ek vyakti ne telechakkar ko inke is dharawahik ko chhodane kaa kaaran bataaya ki bhavy ab abhinayta ke rup main or aage badhanaa chaahate hai or nae pariyojnaon main kaam karnaa chaahate hai. bhavy ko haal hi main ek gujarati film main kaam karne kaa mauka mila he. yah unki pehali guzarati film he.
