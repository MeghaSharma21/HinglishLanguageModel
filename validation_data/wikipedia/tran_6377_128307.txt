 australia kshetrafal kii drushti se vishwa kaa chata sabase bada desh he. australia main chah rajya or vibhinn pradesh shaamil hai. australia mahadweep kaa mukhya kshetra paanch rajyon or teen pradeshon or dakshin main mukhya bhoomi kshetra taasamania dweep se milakar bana hua he. australia kaa antarctic mahadweep ke bhi kuch kshetra par daawa he.
 sabhi rajya or teen aantarik pradesh apni sansad or apana prashasan rakhte hai. anya sabhi pradeshon kaa sanchaalan australia kii kendriya sarkaar dwaara kiya jaataa he lekin norfok dweep ko ucch str kaa swashaasan adhikaar praapt he.
