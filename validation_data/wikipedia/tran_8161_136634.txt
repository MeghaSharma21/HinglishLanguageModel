 jadi-booti chikitsa ek parmparik chikitsa paddati yaa 'lok chikitsapaddati' he jisamen paudhon evam unke rasadi kaa chikitsa ke liye upyog kiya jaataa he.
 puratathvik sakshya aushadhiy paudhon ke upyog kam se kam karne ke liye tareekhen ingit karata he ki pashaan kaal, lagbhag 60,000 saal pehle. herbal upachaar ke likhit sakshya paudhon kii suchiyaan banaai jo sumer nivasee, karne ke liye, waapas 5,000 se adhik varshon ke liye tareekhen banaai. pracheen samskritiyon kii ek sankhya paudhon or unki chikitsa kaa upyog karata he par likhaa tha. pracheen misr main, jadi bootiyon main ullekh kar rahe misr ke chikitsa papyri, yaa jadi bootiyon kii maatra kaa pataa lagane yukt chikitsa jaar main paaya durlabh avasaron par kabr chitra main dikhaaya gaya.
