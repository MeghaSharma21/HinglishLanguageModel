 vartmaan kaal main upendranath ashk ne "pardaa uthaao, paradaa giraao" aadi kai nai sukhwaale ekanki likhe hai. dow ramkumar verma kaa ekanki sangrah "rimjhim" is kshetra main mile kaa pathar mana gaya he. unhone smit hasya ke achche namoonay die hai. devraj dinesh, udaishankar bhatta, bhagwaticharan verma, prabhakar machave, jayanath nalin, bedab banarsi, kantanath chonch," bhaiya ji banarsi, gopalprasad vyas, kaaka haathrasi, aadi anek sajjanon ne anek vidhaon main rachanaayen kii hai or hasyaras ke sahitya ko khub samrudh kiya he. inamen se anek lekhakon kii anek kritiyon ne acchhee prashansa pai he. bhagwaticharan verma kaa "apane khilone" hasyaras ke upanyaason main vishisht sthaan rakhata he. yashpal kaa "chakkar club" vyang ke liye prasiddh he. krishnachandra ne "ek gadhe kii aatmakatha" aadi likhakar vyang lekhakon main yashaswita praapt kii he. gangadhar shukla kaa "subah hoti he shaam hoti he" apni niraali vidha rakhata he.
 rahul sankrutyaayan, seth govind das, shrinarayan chaturvedi, amritlal nagar, dow barasanelal ji, vasudev goswami, bedadak ji, wipro ji, bharatbhushan agarwal aadi ke naam ginaae ja sakate hai jinhone kisi na kisi rup main sahitya ke is upadey ang kii samruddhi kii he.
