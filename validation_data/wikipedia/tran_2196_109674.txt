 rajkumaar henry wiliam frederick albert, gloster ke duk  jorge pancham or rani mairi, kii sabase choti santan or tisare putr the. unka janm, 31 march 1900 main hua tha. apane janm ke samay ve, rajgaddi ke anukram main paanchave sthaan par the.
 ve peshey se ek sainik the, or apane vyavsaayik jivan ke dauraan, unhone british samrajya kii seva main, vishwa bhar main vistrut vibhinn british upniveshon main, anya vibhinn padon par apni seva di thi. iske alaava, unhen 30 janvari 1945 se 11 march 1947 ke bich, maharaj jorge shashtham dwaara, australia ke governor-general yani maharajyapal ke pad par niyukt kiya gaya tha. is kaal ke dauraan ve, maharaj ke pratinidhi ke rup main, unki anupasthiti ke dauraan shaasha ke kartavyon kaa nirvaah karte the. ve rani elizabeth dwitiya ke bhavi pratishaasak bhi the.
 10 june varsh 1974 ko unka nidhan ho gaya. ve maharaj george or mairi ke antim jivit santan the.
