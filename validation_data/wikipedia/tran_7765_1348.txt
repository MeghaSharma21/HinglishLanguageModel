 akkadi bhashaa ek mrit sami bhashaa he. yah pracheen mesopotamia main boli jaati thi. yah ankan lipi main likhi jaati thi.
 akkadi kaa yah naam us akkad nagar se padaa jo isa purv 24veen sadi main prasiddh samrat sharrukin kii rajdhani tha. tabhi akkadi ko rajbhasha kaa pad mila. kalantar main akkadi, pradesh or kaal ke anusaar, asuri or babuli naamak janboliyon main viksit hokar bant gai. asuri dajala nadi kii uparali ghati main or babuli dajala-faraat ke sagarvarti doaab main boli jaati thi.

 2. madhyakaal or
 3. uttarkaal .
