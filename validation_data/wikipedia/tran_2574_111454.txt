 andhra pradesh main vishvavidhyalayon or kalejon kii suchi andhra pradesh main vishvavidhyalayon kii vyavastha kendriya, rajya, manit, swayamvatt or privet vishvavidhyalayon ke rup main dekhi jaasakati he.bharat main ucch shiksha pranali main nijee or saarvajanik donon vishvavidhyalay shaamil hai. saarvajanik vishvavidhyalayon ko bharat sarkaar or rajya sarkaron dwaara samarthit kiya jaataa he, jabaki nijee vishvavidhyalayon ko zyaadaatar nikaayon or samaajon dwaara samarthit kiya jaataa he. bharat main vishvavidhyalayon ko vishvavidhyalay anudaan ayog dwaara manyata praapt he, jo vishvavidhyalay anudaan ayog adhinium, 1956 se apni shakti khinchati he. 
 andhra pradeh main ucch shiksha vibhaag dwaara kai saarvajanik vishvavidhyalay sthapit hai.
