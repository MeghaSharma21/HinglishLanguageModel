 pradesh kaa 52vaan jila 30 September 2018 main gathit kiya gaya he.madhyapradesh ke 52wein jile niwadi ke liye aadesh jari kar die gaye hai. rajasav vibhaag ke pramukh sachiv manish rastogi ke hastakshar se 29 sitambar 2018 ko yah aadesh jari kiye gaye. madhyapradesh ke rajyapal ke aadeshaanusaar rastogi ne bataaya ki tikamgarh jile kii prithvipur, niwadi evam orchha tahseelein ab niwadi jile main aaengi. yah jila 01 oktober 2018 se astitv main aa jayega. is jile ke antargat prithvipur tehsil kii 56 panchayatein, niwadi kii 54 panchayatein, orchha kii 17 panchayatein shaamil kii gai. is prakaar yahaan kul 138 gram panchayatein shaamil kii gai hai.
 iskaa kshetrafal 1317.45 varg kilo meter hoga, jo ki madhya pradesh kaa sabase chota jila jansankhya or kshetrafal kii drushti se hoga. vartmaan main isamen 3 tahseelein hai: nivadi, prithvipur evam orchha, tathaa 2 janapad panchayate nivadi va prithvipur hongi. pratham naveen colector akshay singh tathaa police adheekshak mukesh shrivastav hoge.
 bataate chalem ki agar malwa madhyapradesh kaa 51waan jila he. ise shajapur ko vibhajit karke banaaya gaya tha. gathan ke samay iskaa kshetrafal 1318 varg kilometre tathaa aabaadi 4 lakh ek hajaar he.
