  tonk bharat ke rajasthan kaa ek pramukh shahar evam loksabhaa kshetra he. tonk shahar, purvi rajasthan rajya, pashchimottar bharat main sthit he. tonk pehle riyasat thi| tonk kaa doosraa naam nawabi shahar nawabon kii nagari bhi kaha jaataa he |
 yah banaas nadi ke thik dakshin main sthit he. bhootpurv tonk riyasat kii rajdhani rah chuke is shahar kii sthapana 1643 main amir khan pindari ne kii thi or yah choti parvat shrinkhalaa kii dhalaanon par avasthit he. iske thik dakshin main qila or nae based kshetra hai. aasapaas kaa kshetra mukhyat: khulaa or samtal he, jisamen bikhri hui chattani pahadiyaan hai.
 yahan murjaipaalan or matsay paalan hota he tathaa abhrak or berilium kaa khanan hota he. bhootpurv tonk riyasat main rajasthan evam madhya bharat ke chah alag-alag kshetra aate the, jinhen pathan sardaar amir khaan ne 1798 se 1817 ke bich hasil kiya tha. 1948 main yah rajasthan rajya kaa ang bana.
 tonk ke paryaton sthal bisalpurbaandh,sunahari kothi, jama masjid,arabi farsi shok sandesh,hathi bhaata,bhumgarhdurg,red va uniyaara sabhyataa sthal,kalyanji mandir aadi.

 tonk kaa yah kshetra mahabharat kaal main savadalaksh naam se janaa jaataa tha. yahan tonk ke itihaas se jude ke pracheen khandaron se teesri shatabdi ke sikke mili hai.
 tonk is kshetra kaa pramukh krishi bazaar evam nirmaan centre he. jowar, gehun, chanaa, makka, kapaas or tilhan yahan kii mukhya fasalen hai.
 yah gaanv tonk kii uniyaara tehsil ke paas sthit he. yahan par ""vidyut"" banane kaa plant he jisamen sarson ke nasht hone vaale bhaag se bijli kaa utpaadan kiya jaataa he. is prakriya se aas-paas ke logon ko rojgaar miltaa he.
 sooti vastra kii bunai, charmshodhan or namada banane kii hastkala yahan ke mukhya udyog hai. tonk main rajasthan kii mirch mandi maujuud he. iske alaava yahan se nikalne vaali mirch or kharbooze puure rajasthan main prasiddh hai.
 yahan kaa college maharishi dayanand vishvavidhyalay, ajmer se sambadh he. tonk ke nivai sthaan main "bansthali viapeeth" he jo mahilao kii purnataya nivasaiya vishvavidyal he,jo mahilao ke liye kai prakaar ke ucch shiksha ke paathyakram uplabdh karaati he. isi sthaan par doctor ke en modi vishvavidhyalay bhi he.
 tonk main bi aid, bi es si aadi ke kai college hai.
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar tonk zile kii kul jansankhya 14,21,711 he.
 tonk, ajmer, bundi, bhilwara or baaran ke dehi kshetron main ek pratha naata ke naam se prachlit he. iske antargat ek vaivaahit mahila kisi duusare purush ke saath rahane or sharirik sambandh bana sakati he jab uske pehle pati ko muawza kii raqam di jaae. in paison ko jhagada kaha jaataa he jo ek prakaar se vivaah ke samay hue kharch kii bharpai hoti he.
