 dhamaar kaa janm braj bhoomi ke lok sangeet main hua. iskaa prachlan braj ke kshetra main lok- geet ke rup main bahut kaal se chalaa aata he. is loakgeet main varnya- vishay radha- krishna ke holi khilne kaa tha. ras shringaar tha or bhashaa thi braj. graamon ke unmookt vaataavaran main drut gati ke dipachandi, dhumali or kabhi addha jaisi taal main yuvak- yuvatiyon, praudh or vridhon, sabhi ke dwaara yah loakgeet gaae or naache jate the. braj ke sampurna kshetra main rasiyaa or holi jan-jan main vyaapt he. pandrahavin shatabdi ke ant or solahavin shatabdi ke prarambh main us samay ke sangeet paridrishy ko dekhte hue maansingh tomars or nayak baiju ne dhamaar gayaki kii neenv daali. unhone hori naamak loakgeet braj kaa liya or usko gyaan kii agni main tapaakar dhaal diya. pracheen charchari prabandh ke saanche main, jo charchari taal main hi gaae jate the. hori gayan kaa priy taal aadi chachar dipachandi ban gaya. hori kii bandish to purvavat rahi, vah vibhinn raagon main nibaddh kar di gai. gayan style vahi lok sangeet ke aadhaar par pehle vilambit ant main drut gati main purvadhrith taal se hat kar kaharava taal main hoti he or shrota ko rasavibhoot kar detii he. nayak baiju ne dhamaron kii rachana choti kii. isaki gayan style kaa aadhaar dhruvpad jaisaa hi rakhaa gaya. varnya-vishay maatr phag se sambandhit or ras shringaar tha, vah bhi sanyog. viyog shringaar ko dhamaar bahut kam sthaan praapt hai. 
 is style kaa uddeshy hi romantic vaataavaran utpann karnaa tha. dhruvpad kii tarah aalaap, phir bandish gaai jaati thi. pad gayan ke baad lay- baant pradhan upaj hoti thi. vibhinn prakaar kii layakariyon se gayak shrotaaon ko chamatkrit karata tha. pakhaavji ke saath ladnt hoti thi. sam kii lucatipi kii jaati thi. shreshth kalakaar gey padaanshon ke swar sanniveshon main bhi antar karke shrotaaon ko rasmagn karte the. is prakaar dhamaar kaa janm hua or yah gayan style desh bhar ke sangitagnon main fail gai. gat ek shatabdi main dhamaar ke shreshth gayak narayan shasri, dharmadas ke putr baharaam khan, pan lakshmanadas, giddhaur vaale mohammad ali khan, alam khan, agre ke gulam abbaas khan or udaipur ke dagar bandhu aadi hue hai. khyaal gayakon ke vartmaan gharaanon main se keval agre ke ustad faiyaz khan nom-tom se alaap karte the or dhamaar gayan karte the. isi prakaar samay ke saath-saath mrudang or veena vaadan main bhi parivartan hota gaya. mrudang or veena pracheen kaal se hi adhikanshtya geetanugat hi bajaai jaati thim. mukt vaadan bhi hota tha. mrudang or veenavadakon ne bhi dhruvpad or dhamaar gayaki ke anurup apane ko dhaala. veena main dhruvapadanurup aalaap, jodalap, vilambit lay kii gaten, taan, paranen or jhaalaa aadi bajaae gaye. dhamaar ke anurup pakhaavji se lay- baant kii ladnt shuru hui. pakhaavji ne bhi dhruvpad- dhamaar main prayukt taalon main bhi abhyaas kiya, saath hi sangat ke liye vibhinn taalon or layon kii paranon ko rachaa.
