 bundelkhand vindhyaachal kii upavyakaon kaa pradesh he. is giri kii anek unchi nichi shakhaain - prashakhaain hai. iske dakshin bhaag main mekal, purv main camor, uttar-purv main kenjua, madhya main saarang or panna tathaa pashchim main bhimtor or pir jaisi giri shikhaain hai. yah khand lahariyaan leti hui taal-talaiyon, ghahar-ghahar kar bahane vaale naale or chaude paat ke saath ujjwal ret par athwa durgam giri-maalaon ko cheer kar bhairav ninaad karte hue bahane vaali nadiyon kaa khand he. sindh, betwa, dhasaan, ken tathaa narmada is bhaag kii mukhya nadiyaan hai. inamen pratham chaar nadiyon kaa pravaah uttar kii ore or narmada kaa pravaah purv main pashchim kii ore he. pratham chaar nadiyaan yamuna main mil jaati hai. narmada pashchim sagar se milti hai. is kshetra main prakruti ne vistaar lekar apana soundarya chhitakaaya he.
 bundelkhand loha, sona, chaandi, sheesha, hira, panna aadi se samrudh he. iske alaava yahan chuunaa kaa pathar bhi prachur maatra main paaya jaataa he. vindhy parvat par pai jane vaali chattaanon ke naam uske aasapaas ke sthaan ke naamon se prasiddh he jaise - mander kaa chuunaa kaa pathar, gannaur garh kii cheepen, reva or panna ke chooney kaa pathar, vijaygarh kii cheepen ityadi. jabalpur ke aasapaas paaya jane vaala gora pathar bhi kaaphi prasiddh he.
 is praant kii bhoomi bhojan kii fasalon ke atirikt fal, tambaaku or papite kii kheti ke liye acchhee samajhi jaati he.yahan ke wanon main sarai, sagon, mahua, chaar, hari, bahera, aanwala, ghatahar, khayr, dhubain, mahalon, paakar, babul, karaunda, simal aadi ke vriksh adhik hote hai.
 bundelkhand main graamon kii sankhya adhik he. unke naam vrikshon, jalaashyon, pashuon, pakshiyon, ghaas-paat athwa sthaan vishesh ke nikat hone vaali koi dhwani vishesh ke aadhaar par rakhe gayi prathit hote hai. chunki is bhaag main vrikshon kii adhikta he or jalashay bhi anek hai, at: adhikansh naam inhen se hi sambandh rakhte hai. vrikshon main visheshkar pipal, aam, jamun, umar, imali, bel, ber, mahua, chaar or hardu par bahut se naam hai. keval jabalpur jisamen praaia: 50 gaanv piparia, piparhata naam ke hai. isi prakaar anek jamuniyaan, umeria, imali athwa isnia hai.
 ibri, kua, sagar, jhiria aadi naam jalaashyon par aadhaarit hai. jalashay suchak naamon se saath kahin-kahin vrikshon ke naam sanyukti hokar gram naam ban gayi hai. jaise simratal, amanro.
 pashuon ke naam se bhi kai gaanv ke naam pade hai jaise rich se richi yaa richhai, hiran se hiranpuri. vrikshon se naam sthaan par kai gaanvon ke naamak pashuon or jalaashyon ke naam ko bhi mila kar bane hai jaise - baghadbari, hathisra, gidhuta, magarmuha, jheenguri. upyukt gram-naam-parichay se gyaat hota he ki graamon ke bade vicitra naam rakhe gayi hai jo kabhi kabhi hasyapad bhi lagata he. paas-paas based hue gaanvon ke bich kii duuri naak-kaan ke bich kii duuri arthath thodi hi bataate hue kisi lok kavi ne kaha he -
 dhar-masaki or dharampura, lolkachaiya, vijaipura.lakhapateri utani dur, naak-kaan he jitani dur..
 bundelkhand ke beete vaibhav kii jhalak hamen aaj yahan sthit pashaan shilp or sthapaty se hoti he. yahan pragaitihaasik kaal ke adivaasiyon dwaara puji jane vaali murtiyan bundelkhand praapt hui hai. kala kii drushti se inkaa mulya adhik nahin he, kintu murtikla ke aadi rup kaa adhyayan karne ke liye in murtiyon kaa bahut mahatv he. bundelkhand ke vibhinn sthanon main surkh rang kii chitrakaari bhi milti he. uskaa rang itnaa pakkaa he ki ab tak kharaab nahin hua he. inamen manushyon or ghodon ke chitra hai. itihasajna iskaa kaal purv aitihaasik nishchit karte hai. karabi tehsil ke kalyanpur gram main manikpur ke nikat sarhat ke jangal or kathauta main shahgarh or devra ke nikat pipariyaan main fatahpur main aisi laal rang kii chitrakaari bahut he. devra ke nikat shila-bhittiyon par gairik rang ke bane hue chitra maanav prakruti kii aadim anubhutiyon ke sakshi hai. in chitron main pashuon kaa pradarshan kiya gaya he. nikat hi akhetic avasthaa main maanav-chitra bhi hai. nagaudh, jabalpur or mahauba aadi kuch sthanon main buddha yug kii murtikla kahin kahin milti he. shilaalekh bhi praapt hue hai. samraat ashok ne is praant par bhi apana adhikaar sthapit kiya tha or dharm prachaar hetu shilalekhon par dharm prashastiyaan uttakeern karain thi. tritiy shatabdi se teharaveen shatabdi tak is praant main us sthapaty kala kaa srijan hua jo kaling or khajurahon kii krittiyon main jivant rup main aaj bhi vartmaan hai. kaling kii kala chandeli kaal kii he. khajuraho kii usase bhi pehle kii he, kyonki chini yatri huentsang ne bhi khajuraho main mandir kaa hona likhaa he. kadaachit chandelon ne un puraatan mandiron kaa jirnoddhar kara kar prashastiyaan ankit kii hongi. khajuraho kaa vishaal mandir, devgarh kii vishnumurti, datia kaa purana mahal, panna kaa brihaspati kund, jataara kaa madanasagar aadi vaastukala ke sawaal pramaan he. ajaigarh, barua sagar, talbehat, karaira aadi main bhi kala kii prachurtaa he.
 khajuraho main bees mandiron ke samooh kaviklpana ko maano murtimati karke prithvi par utaar laayaa he. shilpi kaa swapn maano saakaar ho gaya he. apane vishaal mandapon, antaraalon, amalak shikhar anushikhar or stupika se sajjit unchi minaaron or apni asankhya anupam shilakritiyon se vibhooshit yah mandir samooh aaj angin ko apane darshan kaa aamantran dene laga he. mandir ke baahar or bheetar donon ore kii deevaaron devataaon, apsaraon, sandariyon, vidyadharon, yugal-meethunon, gaj or shardulon kii sundar kala-kritiyon se sajaai gai hai. khajurahon ke shilpi anupam krititv hai, uski nari pratimaayein itani sundar hai, unhen dekh kar aisa lagata he maano jad main chetan apane sampoorn vaibhav ke saath jaag uthaa ho. itnaa sammohan he ki pashaan main jivan sapandan kaa bhram hone lagata he. khajuraho ke shilpiyon ne nari ke jivan or bhav ke prayek rup kaa ankan kiya he. kabi shalamanjik ke rup main to kabhi apane premi ke saath kaam-krida tathaa bhogavilas karte, sakhiyon ke saath haas, parihaas or vaartalap karte, bacchon ko stanpan karaate, shringaar karte, sote, uthte-baithte, prayek sthiti main tanmay or bhav-vibhor hokar. vishaal ardh nimilit netra, unnat uroj, baari nitamb, anek tedi-medhi bhangimaaon, bhare adharon par tairti taral hansee athwa muskuraahat main prem kaa maun nimantran nari-murti-shilp kii visheshta he. nagaudh ke nikat mumra gram kaa shiv mandir tathaa nachna gram ke sameep choumukhnath kaa mandir donon apane dhang ke hai. itihaaskaaron kaa mat he ki inhen nagvanshiy shaiva shaskaon ne banwaaya tha. nachna kaa shivalay naagkalin sthapaty kaa adbhut udhaaharan he. usame shiv-parvati ko shikharon par kailash parvat par kaa drishy ankit kiya gaya he. kuch log mumra or nachna ke mandiron kii gananaa guptakaleen sthapaty kala main kiya karte hai. in mandiron ke daravaajon par vicitra kalayukta torana utkeern hai. jabalpur jile main tigavaan gram main guptakaleen sundar vishnu mandir hai. sagar jile main anek sthanon par bhi aisi kala kaa kshetra he. binaa ke aas paas to iske bhandar hai, vishesh kar eran gram ke nikat hun rajya kaal main bundelkhand kii rajdhani thi.
 jabalpur ke paas lagbhag aath mile pashchim kii ore tevar naamak gram he. iske aas-paas devalayon or khandaharon ke avshisht paye jate hai, jinamein ukt sthaan ke beete vaibhav kii jhalak milti he. yahaan anek murtiyan pai jaati hai, jinamein vajrapani, bhagwaan buddha or jain teetharkar hi vishesh hai. tevar kii pracheenta hamen isa kii pehali shatabdi main le jaati he. tevar jise tripuri kehete hai kalchuriyon kaa praadhanya rahaa he. kintu kuch murtiyon tathaa ek pracheen baawali ko chhod kar anya koi bhaagnavshesh nahin bachaa he. tripuri se thodi dur karnbel main kuch pracheen bhaagnavsheth he. tripari hi chedi kii rajdhani thi. jabalpur ke paas hi gadha gram puraatan "gadha mandalarajya' ke avasheshon se purna he. gadha-mandla gaudon kaa rajya tha goud raja kalchuriyon ke baad hue hai. jabalpur ke paas kii kalchuri vaastukala kaa vistrut varnan alexender kaninham ne apni report main kiya he. prasiddh anveshak rakhaldas bannerjee ne bi is vishay par ek atyant gaveshnapurna granth likhaa he. bhedghat or nandachand aadi gaanvo main bhi anek kalapurna smarak hai. bhedaghat main prasidd "chaunsath yogini' kaa mandir. iskaa aakaar gol haich. isame 81 murtiyon ke rakhane ke liye khand bane hai. yah 10veen shatabdi main bana he. mandir ke bheetar bich main alhan mandir he. in sabhi sthanon main mandiron or murtiyon main jis prakaar kii kala pai jaati he, usase spasht hota he ki bundelkhand vastav main kala-bhoomi rahaa he. murtiyon kii mukhmudra, sharir kii banaavat, vaksh pradesh, kati, anguliyaan tata nasika aadi kii banaavat se ucchkoti kii kala kaa parichay miltaa he. anek sthanon main praapt murtiyon main to itani dakshata pai jaati he ki shilpkaar ke chheni-hathode kii sampoorn shakti usamen chuk gai hoti he. sharir par ke rome tak darshaye ja sake hai. itnaa hi nahin paardarshak vasr bhi darshaye ja sake hai.
