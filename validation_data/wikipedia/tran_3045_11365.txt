 pu lalathanhawala bhartiya rajya mizoram ke vartmaan mukhyamantri hai. inke netritv main bhartiya rashtriya congress ne 2008 ke mizoram ke vidhansabha chunaavon main jabardast jeet darj kii. 
 pu lalathanhawala hamartwanphunga sello or lalsavnlini ke putr he. inhone 1958 main metric kii pariksha uteern kii. 1961 main intermidet tathaa 1964 main bi. e. kii pariksha uteern kii. ye kattar isai mataavlambee maane jate he.
 lalathanhawala ne apana karier recorder ke taur par shuru kiya. uske baad unhone assam ko-oparative apeks bank main assistant ke rup main kaam kiya. 1967 tak ve rajanithik party mizo neshnal front ke sachiv rahe. 1967 main ve bhartiya rashtriya congress main gaye. 1973 main ve mizoram ke party adhyaksh chune gaye. unke netritv main 1984 main congress party ne mizoram main bahumat hasil kiya or ve rajya ke mukhyamantri chune gaye. vah 1989 se lekar 1998 tak rajya ke mukhyamantri rahe. 2008 main vah rajya ke punah mukhyamantri chune gaye. ve vartmaan main mukhyamantri ke saath mizoram pradesh congress cameti ke adhyaksh bhi hai.
