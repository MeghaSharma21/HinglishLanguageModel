  harry potter or maut ke tohfe je. ke. rolling dwaara angrezi main rachit harry potter kram kii antim, saatvi kadi he. yah kitaab 21 july 2007 ko prakashit hui thi.
 harry potter or maut ke tohafe kadi is kram kaa aakhri upanyaas he. is upanyaas main hari potter kaa saamana anishth dev se hota he. is main horkerax naamak vastu kii mukhya bhoomika he. is vastu main anishth dev apni aatma ko saat hisson baat kar alag-alag jagaah chupa detaa he. harry potter or usske saathi horkerax kii khoj main jate hai or unhe voldemort kaa saamana bhi karnaa padataa he kahani ke ant vah voldemort ko haraane main kaamyaab ho jate he.
