 sextent sabase saral or sugathit yantra he jo prekshak kii kisi bhi sthiti par kinhin do binduon dwaara bana kone paryaapt yatharthata se naapane main kaam aata he. iskaa avishkaar san 1730 main jaan headley or thomas godfrey naamak vaigyanikon ne alag-alag swatantra rup se kiya tha. tab se itani avadhi guzarane par bhi yah yantra prachlit hi nahin he varan bade chaav se prayog main aata he. iskaa mukhya kaaran yah he ki anya konemapi yantron se adhik suvidhajanak visheshtaayen uplabdh hai. pehali visheshta yah he ki anya konemapi yantron kii bhanti ise prekshan ke samay ekadam sthir rakhana yaa kisi nishchit avasthaa main rakhana anivaary nahin he. duusari visheshta yah he ki prekshak sthiti or us par kone banane vaale bindu kshaitij urdhvakar yaa tiryak samtal main hon, is yantra se us samtal main bane vaastavik kone kii maatra naap sakate hai. in visheshtaon ke kaaran sextent navik kii uski yatra kii disha kaa gyaan karaane ke liye aaj bhi bada upyog yantra he.
 do prakaar ke sextent prayog main aate hai. ek, baks sextent or doosraa khagoleey yaa navik sextent. donon kii banaavat main koi saidhantik bhinnata nahin he. inaki banaavat kaa siddhant yah he ki yadi kisi samtal main prakaash kii koi kiran aamane-saamne munh kiye khadey samtal darpanon se ek ke baad duusare par paravartit hone ke baad dekhi jaae to dekhi gai kiran or mul kiran ke bich bana kone paravartak darpanon ke bich parsparik kone se duuna hoga. sextent se 120° tak kaa kone ek baar main hi napa ja saktaa he. isse bada kone hone par do yaa adhik se adhik teen bhaag karke naapnaa hoga.
 baks sextent ek choti, lagbhag 8 senmi beas or chaar senmi oonchaai kii dibiya sa hota he. upar kaa dhakkan khol dene par upar kuch pench or ek burneer thami hui bhujaa dikhaai degi jo anshon par uske chote bhaagon main vibhajit chaap par chal sakati he. daste kii bhanti ek pench bhujaa se judaa hota he. dibiya ke bheetar dhansi pench kii pindi se ek samtal darpan laga rahata he. ise nirdesh darpan kehete hai. pench ghumaane se darpan or saath hi ankit chaap par bhujaa main laga burneer chalata he. isse darpan kii koniya gati gyaat ho jaati he.
 is nirdash darpan ke saamne hi ek doosraa darpan rahata he jiskaa neeche kaa aadha bhaag paardarshee or upar kaa paravartak hota he. jin do binduon ke bich kone naapnaa hota he unamem se ek ko baks main lagi doorabeen yaa bane chhed se kshitij darpan ke paardarshee bhaag se dekhte hai or duusare bindu kaa pratibimb nirdesh darpan se ek paravartan ke baad kshitij darpan main dikhaai detaa he. is samay pench se nirdesh darpan aise ghumaate hai ki kshitij darpan ke paardarshee bhaag se dekhe bindu kii kiran pratibimb kii kiran par sannipati ho jaae. is samay donon darpanon ke bich bana kone prekshak kii sthiti par donon binduon dwaara nirmit kone kaa aadha hoga. darpanon ke bich kaa kone burneer suchak ke saamne ankit chaap par padhaa ja saktaa he jisase binduon ke bich kaa kone gyaat ho sake. burneer suchak par hi sahi paathyank lene ke liye ek aavardhak lens laga rahata he.
 magar chaap par anshankan is prakaar kiya jaataa he ki binduon dwaara nirmit kone sidhaa padhaa ja sake. yah suvidha pradaan karne ke liye nirdesh darpan kii gati kii dooni rashiyaan likhi jaati hai. jaise 10° ke saamne 20°, 20° ke saamne 40°, isi prakaar antim anshankan 60° ke saamne 120° likhte hai. isse padhi gai raashi kone kii maatra hogi. kone ek minute tak sahi pard sakate hai.
 yah dhaatu kaa 60° kaa vruttkhand hota he jiskaa chaap ankit hota he. vakr ke centre se ek bhujaa chaap par faili hoti he. is bhujaa ke sire par burneer or ek sparshi pench lage rahete hai. isi bhujaa par upar nirdesh darpan laga rahata he. centre par bhujaa gum sakati he or uske saath nirdesh darpan or ankit chaap par burneer bhi. chaap ko thame ek ardhavyas par nirdesh darpan ke saamne aadha paardarshee or aadha paravartak kshitij kaanch dridhtaa se laga hota he jisase hokar dekhne ke liye saamne doorabeen hoti he. spasht he ki isaki banaavat baks sextent ke samaan hi he or prekshan kaa dhang bhi. surya ke prekshan ke liye rangeen kaanch rahata he. 60° ke chaap par ansh or uske chote vibhaajan yantra ke aakaar ke anusaar 20¢ yaa 10¢ tak bane hote hai. burneer se 20² yaa 10² tak padhane kii suvidha rahati he.
 sextent se sahi paathyank praapt karne ke liye nimna jyamitiya sambandh hona chahie or na hone par samaayojan karke ye sambandh sthapit kar liye jate hai:
  suchakank or kshitij kaanch chaap ke samtal par lamb hon,
  jab burneer suchakank shunya par ho to nirdeshak or kshitij darpan samaantar hon, tathaa
  drushtirekha chaap ke samtal ke samaantar ho.
