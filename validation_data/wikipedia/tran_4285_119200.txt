 ibrahim hemmatania farsi or dach mul ke ek saahsee vyakti hai jo kisi motor bike se samudr paar karne vaale vishwa ke pehle vyakti hai. inhone 68 dinon main amphibiyas saaikal jo ki jameen or pani donon par maanav urja se chalane vaali motor-saaikal hoti he, se atlantic mahaasaagar ko paar kiya. unhone kai or bade shahar bhi paar kiye jese ke dakaar, netaal, joao pasoa, risifi, arkaazu, sailvador, vittoria, rio di generio or saao poulo. ibrahim dwidha gativala chakra dwaara duniya kii sabase lambii yatra ke liye ginis book of world record rakhti he.
