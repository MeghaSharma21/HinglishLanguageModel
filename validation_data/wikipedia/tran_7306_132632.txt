 kisi padaarth ko dabaane par vikrut hokar daab ke lambavat disha main failane kaa gun aghatvardhaneeyata kehalaata he. aghatvardhaneey padaarthon ko hathode se petakar yaa belkar aasaani se chapata kiya ja saktaa he. dhaatuen praayah aghatvardhaneey hai. sona, loha, aluminium, tanba, pital, chaandi, sisa aadi aghatvardhaneey hai. aadhunik aavart sarini ke 1 se 12 tak ke samoohon ke tatva aghatvardhya hai.
