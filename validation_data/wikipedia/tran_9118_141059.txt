 udyog parv ke antargat 10 upparv hai or isamen kul 196 adhyay hai.
 udyog parv main viraat kii sabha main pandav paksh se shrikrishna, balram, saatyaki kaa ekatra hona or yuddh ke liye drupad kii sahaayta se pandavon kaa yudhsajjit hona, kauravon kii yuddh kii taiyaari, drupad ke purohit laa kauravon kii sabha janaa or sandesh-kathan, dhritarashtra kaa pandavon ke yahan sanjay ko sandesh dekar bhejnaa, sanjay kaa yudhisthir se vaartalap, dhritarashtra kaa vidura se vaartalap, sanatsujat dwaara dhritarashtra ko upadesh, dhritarashtra kii sabha main laute hue sanjay tathaa pandavon kaa sandesh-kathan, yudhisthir ke senabal kaa varnan, sanjay dwaara dhritarashtra ko or dhritarashtra dwaara duryodhana ko samajhaana, pandavon se paraamarsh kar krishna dwaara shaanti prastaav lekar kauravon ke paas janaa, duryodhana dwaara shrikrishna ko bandi banane kaa shadyantra karnaa, garudagalavasamvaad, vidulopakhyaan, laute hue shrikrishna dwaara kauravon ko dand dene kaa paraamarsh, pandavon or kauravon dwaara sainyshivi kii sthapana or senapatiyon kaa chayan, duryodhana ke doot uluk dwaara sandesh lekar pandav-sabha main janaa, donon pakshon kii senaaon kaa varnan, ambopakhyaan, bhishma-parashuram kaa yuddh aadi vishyon kaa varnan he.
