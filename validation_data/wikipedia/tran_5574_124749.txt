 manushya ke bich ke pyaar ko parsparik pyaar kehete hai. ye sirf ek duusare ke liye chaah nahin he balki ek shaktishaalee bhav he. jis pyaar ke bhaawanaaon ko vinimay nahin kiya jaataa use apratideya pyaar kehete hai. aisa pyaar parivaar ke sadasyon, doston or premiyon ke bich paaya jaataa hai. parsparik rishta do manushya ke saath majboot, gaharaa or nikat sahyog hota he. ye rishta anumaan, ekajutata, niyamit vyapaar baatcheet yaa samaajik patibaddhit kaarnon se banataa he. ye samaajik, saanskrithik or anya kaarak se prabhavit hai. ye prasang parivaar, rishtedaari, dosti, shaadi, sahakarmi, kaam, padosi or mandir-masjid ke anusaar badalata he. ise kanoon ke dwaara yaa rivaaz or aapasi samjhaute ke dwaara viniyamit kiya ja saktaa he. ye samaajik samoohon or samaaj kaa aadhaar he.

 manovigyaan main sangyanatmak or samaajik ghatana ko darshaya jaataa he. manovigyaani robert sternberg ne pyaar ke tribhujaakar siddhant ko sutarabaddh kiya hai. unhone tark kiya ke pyaar ke teen bhinn prakaar ke ghatak hai: atmiyata, patibaddhta or josh. atmiyata wo tatv he jisamen do manushya apane aatmavishvaas or apane zindagi ke vyaktigat vivaran ko baantate hai. ye zyaadaatar dosti or romani kaary main dekhne ko miltaa he.
 patibaddhta ek ummid he ki ye rishta hameshaa ke liye kaayam rahegaa. aakhir main yon aakarshan or josh he. aveshpurna pyaar, romani pyaar or aasakti main dikhaaya gaya he. pyaar ke saare prapatr in ghatakon kaa sangyojan hota hai. pasand karne main atmiyata shamil hoti hai. mugdh pyaar main sirf josh shaamil hota hai. khali pyaar main sirf patibaddhta shaamil hai. romani pyaar main dono atmiyata or josh shaamil hota hai. saathi ke pyaar main atmiyata or patibaddhta shaamil hota hai. buddhiheen pyaar main patibaddhta or josh shaamil hai. akhir main, ghaagh pyaar main teenon shaamil hote hai.
 vikasvaadi manovigyaan ne pyaar ko jivit rahane kaa ek pramukh saadhan sabit karne ke liye anek kaaran diya hai. inke hisaab se manushya apane jeevankaal main abhibhavakiy sahaayta par anya stanpayiyon se zyaada nirbhar rahete hai, pyaar ko is vajah se abhibhavakeey sahare ko prachaar karne kaa tantra bhi mana gaya hai. ye isaliye bhi ho saktaa hai, kyonki pyaar ke kaaran yon sanchaarit rog ho saktaa he jisaki vajah se manushya ke janankshmata par asar pad saktaa hai, bhrun par chot aa sakati hai, bachche paidaa karte vaqt ulajhanein bhi ho sakati hai ityadi. ye sab chijen jaanane ke baad samaaj main bahuvivaah kii paddati ruk sakati hai.
