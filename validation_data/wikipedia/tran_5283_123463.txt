 long march 5 china kaa baari kakshiya prakshepan yaan rocket he. iskaa vikaas china antariksh prakshepan yaan praudyogiki academy dwaara kiya gaya he. yah china kaa pehla aisa raaket he. jisame nae tarah kaa taral idhan kaa prayog kiya gaya he. vartmaan main, do long march 5 vaahan ko alag alag abhiyaanon ke liye banaaya ja rahaa he. long march 5 prithvi kii nichali kaksha main 25000 kilogram tathaa bhu-sthir sthaanantaran kaksha main 14000 kilogram tak laanch kar saktaa he.
 yah america ke delta IV haivi kii takkar kaa ek baari kakshiya prakshepan yaan rocket he. yah america or china ke rocket ke bich santulan kaa kaary bhi karegaa.
 isaki pratham udaan 3 november 2016, 12:43 utisi ko hanaan dweep ke venchang upgrah prakshepan centre se hui. jo safal rahi.
