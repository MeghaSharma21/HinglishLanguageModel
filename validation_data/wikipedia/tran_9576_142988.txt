 beesvin sanshodhan vidheyak pakistan ke samvidhan ko pakistan kii neshnal assembly dwaara 14 farvari, 2012 ko paarit kiya gaya tha. yah seenet main 20 farvari, 2012 ko paarit kiya gaya tha. yah pakistan ke raashtrapati dwaara 28 farvari, 2012 ko hastakshar kiye gaye the. iske dwaara sanshodhan ke anuchhed 48, anuchhed 214, anuchhed 215, anuchhed 216, anuchhed 218, anuchhed 219, anuchhed 224, anuchhed 224A pakistan ke samvidhan ke duusare va tisare anusuchee main kiya gaya or. yah neeche ek kaaryvahak vyavastha sthapit karne or swatantra evam nishpaksh chunaav or mukhya nirvachan ayukta or nirvachan ayog ke sadsy ke pad ke liye sambandhit maamalon dhaaran karne ke liye ek prakriya nirdhaarit kii he.
