 spain 17 bhaagon main vibhajit he jinhen swytt samudaay kaha jaataa he. swytt kaa arth he ki prayek swaitshasi samudaay ke paas apni kaaryakarini shakti he, vidhaayi shakti he or apni jyayik shakti he. udhaaharan ke liye, ye kuch-kuch bharat ke rajyon jaise hai lekin bilkul vaise nahin.
 spain ke pachaas or chote-2 bhaag hai jinhen praant kaha jaataa he. 1978 main ye vibhinn bhaag ekatrit hokar swaitshasi samudaayon main vibhakt ho gaye. isse purv, jo praant ek saath the unka vibhaajan kiya gaya. jo samudaay pehle ekbar saath the, unhen "aitihaasik samudaay" kaha gaya, jaise - galicia, paaes vasco or cataluna. in sabhi samudaayon kii do aadhikarik bhashaayen he: ek spani or duusari apni prantiya. jaise in teen pranto kii kshetreey bhashaayen hai: galligo, euskera or catalan.
