 hind mahaasaagar tateey sahyog sandh; Indian Ocean Rim Association: yah sangh kshetreey sahyog or antarmahadwipiya vyapaar main teji laane ke uddeshy se hind mahaasaagar ke teen prayadvipon- asia, africa or austrailia ko ek manch par laata he.
 hind mahaasaagar tateey kshetreey sahyog sanghathan 5 march, 1997 ko port louis morishas main astitv main aayaa. is sanghathan ke 14 sansthaapak sadsy desh the- bharat, austrailia, malaysia, indonesia, srilanka, singapore, oman, yaman, tanzania, kenya, mozambik, dakshin africa or morishas. bangladesh, iran, seychelles, thailand or sanyukt arab emirate ko 1999 main isaki sadsyta pradaan kii gai.
 antim mahatvapurn kshetreey aarthik sanghathan he. shityudh kii samaapti ke saath kshetreey aarthik sahyog evam ekikaran badhate mahatv ke sandarbh main is sanghathan ke auchity ko sthapit kiya ja saktaa he. hind mahaasaagar, jo aitihaasik kaal se vyapaar kaa ek pramukh centre rahaa he, hi maatr ek aisa kshetra shesh rah gaya tha jahaan asian, nafta or apek jaise kisi aarthik sanghathan kaa gathan nahin hua tha himatakshes kshetra main vishwa kii lagbhag ek-tihaai jansankhya nivas karti he. vishwa ke do-tihaai kachche tel, ek-tihaai prakritik gas, 40 pratishat sone, 90 pratishat heere or 60 pratishat uranium ke bhandar isi kshetra main hai. apni bhugolic sthiti or samrik rup se mahatvapurn jalmargon kii upasthiti ke kaaran vastuon kaa sarvaadhik avaagaman hind mahaasaagar se hi hota he. hind mahaasaagar tat par avasthit koi bhi desh himatakshes kaa sadsy ban saktaa he, basharte ki vah iske siddhanton or uddeshyon, jaise-sadsy deshon ke aantarik maamalon main ahastkshep, shantipurna sah- astitv, samanta or sadasyon ko vishisht raashtra kaa darja-se sahamat ho.
 himatakshes khule kshetravaad ke siddhant par aadhaarit hindmahaasaagar ke tateey deshon kaa ek kshetreey sahyog sanghathan he. khule kshetravaad ke siddhant kaa taatpary he ki sanghathan dwaara gaiyr- sadsy deshon ke saath vyapaar suvidhaon main koi bhedbhaav nahin kiya jayega. duusare shabdon main gaiyr-khaadya deshon ko bhi vahi vyapaarik chhuten praapt hogi jo sadsy deshon ko praapt hai. is siddhant ko sabase pehle 1989 main asia- pacific sanghathan dwaara sweekaar kiya gaya tha. vishwa vyapaar sanghathan bhi khule kshetravaad ke siddhant par aadhaarit he. vastav main khule kshetravaad ke siddhant ke aadhaar par hi vishwa vyapaar ke udaarikaran kii prakriya ko aage badhaayaa ja saktaa he iske vipareet band kshetravaad main kisi sanghathan ke sadsy deshon dwaara apane sadasyon ko jo vyapaar suvidhaayen pradaan kii jaati hai, ve suvidhaayen gaiyr-sadsy deshon ko praapt nahin hoti hai himatakshes hind mahaasaagar ke tateey deshon kaa sanghathan he. is sanghathan kaa mahatv isi baat se samajha ja saktaa he ki hind mahaasaagar vishwa kaa teesraa sabase bada samudr he vishwa ke aadhe vyapaarik jahaaj, vishwa vyapaar kii ek-tihaai vasteen tathaa vishwa kaa do- tihaai tel yatayaat hind mahaasaagar kshetra se hi hota he. hind mahaasaagar samudri sansadhano tathaa khanij padaarthon kii uplabdhta kii drushti se bhi mahatvapurn he. vishwa khaadya va urja suraksha kii drushti se iske jaivik va ajaivik sansadhano kaa vishesh mahatv he. hind mahaasaagar kaa samrik mahatv bhi kam mahatvapurn nahin he hind mahaasaagar par prabhavi niyantran ke bina koi desh vaishvik shakti kaa darja praapt nahin kar saktaa bharat ke liye hind mahaasaagar kaa himatakshes kaa vishesh mahatv he bharat teen ore se hind mahaasaagar se ghira hua he. bharat kaa lagbhag sara vishwa vyapaar hind mahaasaagar se hi gujarata he suraksha kii drushti se bhi hind mahaasaagar bharat ke liye behad mahatvapurn he. gat dashak main china dwaara hind mahaasaagar main hi motiyon kii maala ke antargat bharat ko gherne kii koshish kii gai thi. iske antargat china or bharat ke chaaron ore sthit padosi deshon main nausainik addon kii suvidhaayen praapt karne kaa prayaas kiya tha. hind mahaasaagar bharat ke liye tel tathaa gas va anya khanij padaarthon va jaivik sansadhano kaa strot he.
 himatakshes kaa charter bhi 1997 main sweekaar kiya gaya tha jisamen iske uddeshyon va siddhanton kaa ullekh kiya gayaahai. iske uddeshyon main shaamil hai-
 1. hind mahaasaagar kshetra va iske deshon kaa jeevant va santulit vikaas karnaa tathaa kshetreey sahyog ke liye ekasaasa manch taiyaar karnaa;
 2. un kshetron main sadsy deshon ke madhya sahyog ko protsaahit karnaa jinamein sadsy deshon kaa adhiktam saajha hit nihit ho. is drushti se himatakshes dwaara vyapaar tathaa nivesh, takneekee hastantaran, paryaton, dhaanchaagat suvidhaon kaa vikaas tathaa maanav sansaadhan vikaas ke kshetra main kai vikaas kaaryakramon ko taiyaar kar lagu karnaa. 
 3. aapasi laabh kii drushti se sahyog ke anya kshetron ko chinhit karnaa. 
 4. sadsy deshon ke madhya vyapaar ko badhaava deneke liye vyapaar udaarikaran kii nitiyon ko apnaanaa va unhen lagu karnaa. 
 5. sadsy deshon ke vyapaarik samoohon, shaikshanik sansthaaon tathaa vidwaanon evam janata ke madhya antah kriya ko protsaahit karnaa. 
 6. vibhinn vaishvik manchon main samaan drushtikon apnaane kidrushti se sadsy deshon ke madhya vibhinn muddon par vichaar-vimarsh ko protsaahit karnaa. 
 7. sadsy deshon kii sansthaaon ke madhya sampark va sahyog ke madhya se kshetra ke maanav sansadhano ke vikaas kaa prayaas karnaa.

 is sanghathan ke pramukh uddeshy hai:vikasaatmak, tatasthtapurna or aam raay-aadhaarit panchasheel siddhant ko apanaate huye kshetreey sahyog or antarmahadwipiya vyapaar main vruddhi laana.
 mantriparishad or varisht mantri samiti himatakshes ke do pramukh ang hai. mantriparishad kii baithak prayek do varsh ke antaraal par hoti he. is baithak ke uddeshy hote hai-
 vibhinn kaaryakramon main samanvay or vittiya sansadhano ke sanghatan ke liye varisht adhikari samiti kii baithak kabhi bhi or kitni baar bhi ho sakati he.ati afasarshahi se bachane tathaa nitigat nirnyon ke behatar samanvayan, paryvekshan or kriyanvayan ke liye morishas main sachivalay ke sthaan ek payalat sanrachanagathit kii gai he. apane uddeshyon kii prapti hetu himatakshes dwaara ek tristareey dhaanche kii sthapana kii gai he. ismempratham sarkari sanghathan, doosraa shaikshanik samudaay or teesraa dhaancha vyapaarik samoohon kaa he. in dhaanchon ke madhyam se himatakshes main vichaar-vimarsh tathaa aadan-pradaan kii prakriya ko vyapak banane kaa prayaas kiya gaya he. videsh mantriyon ke sammelan ke dauraan hi himatakshes ke teenon samoohon kii niyamit baithken kii jaati hai. videsh mantriyon kaa sammelan hi himatakshes kii sarvocch nirnayakari sanstha he.
 hind mahaasaagar tateey sadsy deshon main aarthik sahyog, visheshkar un kshetron main, jo samuhik hiton or parsparik laabhon kii viksit karne ke adhiktam avasar pradaan karte hai, ko sambhalakar rakhana himatakshes kaa anany uddeshy he. aise kuch pramukh kshetra hai-nivesh- protsaahan, vaigyanik or takneekee aadan- pradaan paryaton or maanav sansaadhan or moulik aarthik dhaanche kaa vikaas or bhedbhaav rahit aadhaar par logon or seva sanbharakon ko avaagaman.himatakshes sadsy deshon ke bich aarthik ekikaran sthapit karne se kahin adhik mahatv aarthik sahyog ko detaa he. is sanghathan ke samvidhan ke antargat dwi-paksheeya or raajnneetik muddon par charchaa kaa praavdhaan nahin he, kyonki inase aarthik sahyog baadhit hota he.

he-himatakshes deshon ke bich vyapaar udaarikaran, vyaparsarleekaran or aarthik evam takneekee sahyog. himatakshes kii 11veen va 12veen mantriparishad vethken,sammelencramshah 2011 va 2012 main bharat ke bangaloru va gudganvashahron main yojit kii gai thim. 11veen baithak is drushti se mahatvapurn he ki isamen himatakshes ke sahyog ke 6praathamikta vaale kshetron ko chinhit kiya gaya tha. ye kshetrahain- samudri suraksha, vyapaar tathaa nivesh kaa udaarikaran, matsay utpaadan kaa prabandhan, aapadaa prabandhan, vigyaan,takneekee va shaikshanik sahyog.paryaton va saanskrithik aadan-pradaan.himatakshes kii 12veen baithak main 21 binduon par ek ghoshnaa-patr jari kiya gaya, jisamen aapasi sahyog ke vibhinn binduon par prakaash daalaa gaya. is ghoshnaa-patr main is baat ko rekhaankit kiya gaya ki hind mahaasaagar kshetra main sthit rashtron ke liye himatakshes ek bahupaksheeya sheersh manch he. isamen himatakshes kii vibhinn sansthaaon ko majboot banane par vishesh bal diya gaya he sadsy deshon ne is baithak main yah nirnay liya ki hind mahaasaagar main sanchaar va vyapaar kii suraksha ke liye samudri dakaiti ko samaapt karne ke liye aapasi sahyog kii aavashyaktaa he.ullekhneey he ki hind mahaasaagar main malakka kii khadi tathaa adan kii khadi ke paas samudri dakaiti kii ghatnaayen haal ke varshon main teji se badi hai. samudri daakaiti ke kaaran is kshetra main vyapaarik vastuon kaa avaagaman bhi prabhavit hua he sadsy rashtron ne vyapaar ke kshetra main sahyog ko aage badhaane par sahmati vyakt kii himatakshes kaa yah bhi prayaas hoga ki uske jo sadsy desh teji se vikaas kar rahe hai, unki kshamtaaon tathaa vikaas kaa laabh anya sadsy deshon ko bhi praapt ho sake. is baithak ke dauraan praathamikta vaale kshetron main sahyog ko aage badhaane ke liye ek vishesh kosh kii sthapana kii gai he. ghoshnaa main kaha gaya ki is vishesh kosh kii dhanrashi ko sahyog kaaryakramon ke kriyanvayan main prabhavi dhang se prayukt kiya jaae. ullekhneey he ki is sammelan main sadsy deshon ne nirnay liya ki camros ko himatakshes ke 20wein sadsy ke taur par sadsyta pradaan kii jaae. isi tarah america ko himatakshes ke chhathwein vaartakar desh ke rup main shaamil kiya jaae. videsh mantriyon kaa 13waan sammelan 1 navambar, 2013 ko australia ke shahar perth main yojit kiya gaya tha. is baithak main ek 25 binduon vaala sanyukt ghoshnaa-patr sweekaar kiya gaya tha, jise perth ghoshnaa ke naam se janaa jaataa he is baithak dwaara sanghathan ke nae naam kii pushti kii gai. pehle iskaa naam indian osan rim association for riginal ko- oparation -OIRAKKC tha. iskaa nayaa naam badalakar indian open rim association kar diya gaya he. isamen samrik drushti se mahatvapurn is kshetra ke tikaoo tathaa santulit vikaas ko aage badhaane main sahyog ko majboot karne par bal diya gaya. is kshetra kii vikasaatmak tathaa suraksha sambandhi chunautiyon ke samaadhaan hetu sadsy deshon ke madhya niti sambandhi samanvay ko majboot karne kaa aahvaan bhi kiya gaya. sammelan main mana gaya ki hind mahaasaagar kaa yah kshetra prakritik aapadaaon kii drushti se adhik samvedansheel he, atah aapadaa prabandhan ke kshetra main sadsy deshon ke madhya adhik sahyog kii aavashyaktaa he. baithak main sadsy desh is baat par sahamat hue ki ve seemaa shulk prakriya tathaa anya vyapaar sanvardhan upayon kedvara is kshetra main vyapaar tathaa nivesh ko protsaahit karneka prayaas karenge. is, uddeshy kii purti hetu nijee kshetra kii bhoomika ko bhi majboot kiya jayega. is drushti se urja suraksha, khaadya suraksha tathaa aadharbhoot dhaanche ke sambandh main nijee kshetra ke anukool nitiyon ko apanaaya jayega. sabhi sadasyon ne hind mahaasaagar ke samudri sansadhano ke utpaadak, tikaoo tathaa shantipurna upyog va dohan hetu apni patibaddhta vyakt kii. ullekhneey he ki is sammelan main samudri sansadhano ke dohan ke liye alag se katipay maardarshak siddhanton ko apanaaya gaya. sadsy deshon ne kuch anya kshetron jaise- shiksha, samskruti, mahila shashaktikaran, shodh tathaa maanav kshamta vikaas ke kshetron main sahyog ko aage badhaane kaa sankalp liya.
