 swadeshjan kulacharvishuddhn upadhashuchim
 mantrajyamavsaneenan vyabhichaarvivarjitam //
 adhitavyavharaarthan maulan khyatan vipaschitam /
 arthsyotpaadakan chaiv vidadhyanmantrinan nrip: //
  kaa gyaata ho, vyasnee na ho, vyabhichaar se dur rahane vaala ho, shashan ke vibhinn pahaluon kaa acha gyaata ho, achche parivaar se ho, prasiddh ho, vidwaan ho, dhan kaa srijan karne vaala ho, ko raja mantri banaaye.)
