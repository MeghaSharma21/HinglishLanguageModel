 thai log apni jaateey visheshtaon main chiniyon ke nikat hai. in logon ne china ke dakshin bhaag main naan chaauu naamak ek shaktishaalee rajya sthapit kiya tha. kintu uttari chiniyon or tibetiyon ke dabaav tathaa 1253 main kubalai khan ke akramanon ke kaaran thai logon ko dakshini purvi asia kii ore hatana padaa. chaao fraya nadi kii ghati main pahunch kar unhone vahaan ke nivaasiyon ko cambodia kii ore bhaga diya or thailand naamak desh basaaya. 17veen shatabdi tak thailand main samanttantr sthapit rahaa. inhen dinon usne dachon, portugaliyon, francisiyon or angrejon se vyapaar sambandh bhi jod liye the.
 19veen shatabdi main mankut  or uske putr chulolongcorn  ke shasankaal main samantvaad kii vyavastha shanai: shanai: samaapt hui or thailand kaa vartmaan sansar main aagaman hua. sammatidaataaon kii ek samiti gathit hui tathaa britain sanyukt rajya america or france se vyapaar sandhiyaan hui. puraane saamanton ke adhikaar simit kar die gaye or das pratha bilkul uthaa di gai.
 1932 main raktahin kranti dwaara sanvaidhanik rajyatantr kii sthapana hui. kintu uske baad se bhi thailand kii raajnneeti main sthirataa nahin ai.
 dwitiya vishwayuddh ke aarambh main thailand ne japan se sandhi kii or britain or america ke viruddh yuddh thaana. lekin yuddh ke baad usne sanyuktarajya america se sandhi kii. 1962 main laos kii ore se samyavaadee sankat se thailand kii raksha ke liye sanyuktarajya kii sainik tukadiyaan bhi vahaan raheen.
