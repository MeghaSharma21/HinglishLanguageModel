 dhaka premier divijan cricket lig main bhi dhaka premier lig ke rup main janaa jaataa he, ek club list e cricket bangladesh main toornament he.
 1974-75 main iske udghaatan ke baad se lig main bangladesh premier club cricket pratiyogita kiya gaya he. yah 2013-14 toornament kii shuruyaat ke saath ek sthiti suchi praapt kii he, is prakaar nashnal cricket lig vanade bangladesh kii mukhya suchi ke rup main ek pratiyogita adhikramit. 
 1974-75 se 2011-12 ke liye varshon main, abahani limited championships 17 baar jeet hasil kii. anya vijetaon the mohammaden sporting club nau baar, bimaan bangladesh airlines paanch baar, victoria sporting club chaar baar, old dohas sports club do baar or bros union ek baar. varsh 2012-13 main koi toornament nahin tha.
