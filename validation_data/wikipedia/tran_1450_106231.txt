 krity greece ke kreet dweep kii pracheen bhashaa he. krity bhashaa or lipi abhi tak padhi nahin ja saki jisase uski bhashaa ke pracheen rup kaa pataa chal sakna sambhav na ho sakaa he. kintu adhiktar vidwaanon kaa mat he ki pracheen kreet kii yah bhashaa aryetar thi. vahaan tak aaryon kii pahunch hone se pehle hi vah mar chuki thi. uske dakshin sagar paar pracheen mixriyon kii hami sabhyataa thi, purv main sumeriyon or babuliyon kii sami sabhyataa use chaape hue thi, jisase aaryon ke sampark se vah vanchit rahaa. kreet or uske greek, laghueshian nagaron kaa 15veen sadi i. pus. ke lagbhag aaryon se sampark hua or vah unke liye maarak siddh hua. asian or dorian arya greek jatiyon ne apane akramanon dwaara unhen nasht kar diya.
 sar arthur evans aadi puraavidon ke adhyavsaay se kreet kii pracheen sabhyataa ke bhagnavshesh khod nikaale gaye hai. isi sabhyataa ke laghuesia ke purvottaree santari traay nagar ke dhvansaavasheshon ko khodakar shliman ne puraatatv ke vigyaan kii neenv daali thi. laghuesia kaa yah traay or greece kaa misini isi krity sabhyataa ke nagar the jinke duusare naam ijian or minosi bhi hai.
 krity sabhyataa ke nagaron kii khudaayiyon main praaia: do hajaar mitti, chooney aadi kii bani mohren mili hai. inke adhyayan se pataa chalata he ki krity bhashaa kii praarambhik lipi chitramay thi jo dhire dhire rekhaankit ho gai. ye rekhaayen kya dhvanit karti hai, iskaa anumaan kar sakna kathin he. baad kii sadiyon kii muharon par jo akritiyaan bani hai unase anumaan kiya gaya he ki ve sambhavat: us varg kii he jise ‘ideograph’ kehete hai, jo chitralipi or varnalipi ke bich kii lipi he. in akritiyon se spasht he ki abhi tak kreet main varnmaala lipi kaa udai nahin hua tha. jab tak in muharon kii lipi pard lee nahin jaati, ijian sabhyataa kii bhashaa ke sambandh main kisi prakaar kaa bhi anumaan keval kaalpanik hoga.
