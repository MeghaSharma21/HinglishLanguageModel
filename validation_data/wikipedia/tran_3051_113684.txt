 yang di-peratua negery malaysia ke kuch rajyon ke aupchaarik adhyaksh hote hai, jinka paatr lagbhag bharat ke rajyapal ke baraabar hota he. "yang di-peratua negery" kaa arth malay bhashaa main "rajya kaa adhyaksh" he . malaysia ke adhiktar rajyon main puraane rajpariwaron ke sultan yaa raja hi aupchaarik adhyaksh hote hai lekin vartmaankaleen malaysia main penang, melaka, sabah or sarawak rajyon ke sarvocch adhikari ko yang di-peratua negery kii oopaadhi mili hui he. inhen in rajyon ke ketua menteri ko niyukt karne kaa or anya sanvaidhanik kaaryon ko chalaane kaa adhikaar mila hua he.
