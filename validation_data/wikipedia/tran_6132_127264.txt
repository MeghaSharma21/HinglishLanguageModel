 bhartiya bhashaai surveykshan british raaj ke aadhin bharat kaa ek pramukh surveykshan pariyojna thi. iske antargat san 1894 se lekar san 1928 tak bhartiya civil seva ke adhikari jorge grearsan ke nirdesan main kaary hua. isamen kul 364 bhartiya bhashaaon evam boliyon kaa vistrut surveykshan kiya gaya.
