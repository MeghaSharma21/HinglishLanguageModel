 batolomo kadusi italy kaa chitrakaar tha.
 vah floronce main janma or jisane vahiin apni kalashiksha lee. apane samay ke prachlit kalakaar amaanati se usne vaastushilp tathaa murtikla sikhi. chitrakala kii shiksha use prasiddh chitrakaar jukero se mili thi. jukero praaia: chitra banane ke liye dur-dur se bulaayaa jaataa tha, jo saath kii kadusi ko bhi sahaayak ke rup main le jaaya karata tha. zakero ke saath vah madrid gaya tha jahaan usne acconrial pustkaalay ke liye chitra banaae tathaa us prasiddh rajmahal kii deevaaron par bhittichitra likhe. dhire-dhire uski pahunch rajdurbar tak ho gai or spain ke raja philip dwitiya kaa vah kripapaatr ban gaya. adhiktar vah spain main hi rahaa or vahiin uski mrityu bhi hui. uske banaae adhiktar chitra spain main hi hai. uskaa sabase prasiddh chitra 'cross se avataran' he. yah saan filip al reel naamak girjaghar main surakshit he.
