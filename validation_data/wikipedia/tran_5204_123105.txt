 muna abusuleman, ek arab or muslim media vyaktitv hai. vah alwalid bin talal foundation ke purv sansthaapak mahasachiv hai or embisi tivi ke sabase lokapriy saamaajik kaaryakramon main se ek "kalam navaem" kii sah-mejabaan hai. agast 2013 main, muna abusuleman ko "silatech" ke vaishvik rajdoot ke rup main ghoshit kiya gaya tha. 
 ek saarvajanik or media vyaktitv ke rup main, unhone samaaj ke bare main, purv or pashchim ke bich ke nirmaan pulon ke bare main, mahila adhikaar, va saamudayik vikaas, ke bare main baat kii or likhi he. 2004 main su shri muna abusuleman ko vishwa aarthik manch dwaara yuva netaa kaa naam diya gaya tha. is kshamta ke kaaran unhen bahut baar aaj ke yuvaaon kii chunautiyon se sambandhit vishyo par bolne ke liye bhi aamantrit kiya gaya. 2007 main, vah ek sadbhaavna rajdoot ke rup main sanyukt raashtra vikaas kaarykram dwaara niyukt hone ke liye saudi arab kii pehali mahila baniin. 2009 main unhen duniya ke "sabase prabhaavshaalee muslim" kii list main shaamil kiya gaya. saudi arab kii mahila kii chhaviyon ko todane ke ek prayaas main, sushri abu suleman vibhinn darshakon tak pahunchane main bahut sakriy hai or davis world iconomic forum, jedda aarthik forum, si- 100 vishwa aarthik manch, brooking institution conference or kai anya sthanon par unhone apane vichaar vyakt kiye hai.
