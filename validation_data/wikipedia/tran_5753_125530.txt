 nagoya japan ke chubu kshetra main sabase bada shahar he. yah japan kaa teesraa sabase bada nigam shahar or chauthaa sabase adhik aabaadi vaala shahari kshetra he. yah prashanth tat par kendriya honshu par sthit he. yah aichi praant kii rajdhani, or tokyo, osaka, cobe, yokohama, chiba, or kitakushu ke saath japan ke pramukh bandargahon main se ek he. yah japan ke tisare sabase bade mahanagareey kshetra kaa kendra bhi he, jise chukyo mahanagareey kshetra ke rup main janaa jaataa he. 2015 tak, chukyo mahanagareey kshetra ke 9.10 million logon main se 2.28 million log is shahar main rahete the.
 nagoya barfili khadi ke uttar main nobi maidan par sthit he. baadh se bachane liye shahar ko pathari kshetra par basaaya gaya tha. is kshetra ke maidan desh ke sabase upjau kshetron main se ek he. shahar kii seemaa ke saath kiso nadi pashchim kii ore bahati he, or shoni nadi uttar-purv se aakar nishi ward se khadi kii ore dakshini disha main mud jaati he. maanav nirmit hori nadi kaa nirmaan 1610 main ek nahar ke rup main kiya gaya tha. yah shoni nadi pranali ke rup main, uttar se dakshin kii ore bahati he. nadi ke dwaara duradraj ke ilaakon tak avaagaman or vyapaar hota hai. shahar ko japan ke centre main sthiti hone se ise aarthik or raajnneetik rup se viksit hone main sahaayta mili hai.
 nagoya main garm garmiyon or thandi sardiyon ke saath ek ardra up-ushnkaribandheey jalwaayu he. garmiyon main sardiyon kii apeksha zyada barish hoti he, haalanki barish puure saal hoti rahati he.
 1889 main kiye gaye sabase praarambhik janaganana ke anusaar shahar ki aabaadi 1,57,496 thi. 1934 main aabaadi 10 lakh tak pahunch gai or disambar 2010 tak 2,259,993 kii anumaanit aabaadi thi, jisamen jansankhya ghanatv 6,923 prati ki.me.2 tha. iske saath hi disambar 2010 tak anumaanit 1,019,859 gharon ki sankhya thi. 1945 main dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke ant main 153,370 gharon ki sankhya main mahatvapurn vruddhi hui.
 shahar kaa kshetrafal 326.45 varg kilometre he. isaki mahanagareey kshetra me or jifu praanton main faili hui he, jisamen lagbhag 90 lakh logon kii kul aabaadi he, joki osaka or tokyo ke baad sabase zyada aabaadi he.
 nagoya, greater nagoya kaa centre he, jo japan ke 2003 ke vyapaar adhishesh kaa lagbhag 70 pratishat arjeet karata he.
 nagoya kaa mukhya udyog, motor vaahan he. toyota kii lakjri brands lexs, denso, acin siki kan, toyota industries, jettiketiti or toyota boshoku kaa mukhyalay nagoya yaa uske paas hi sthit he. mitsubishi motors kaa anusandhan evam vikaas vibhaag nagoya ke paas okazaki upnagar main sthit he.
 audyogikeekaran ke baad se vimaanan kaa itihaas aitihaasik rup se mahatvapurn rahaa he. yuddh ke dauraan mitsubishi e6em zero ladaku vimaan kaa nirmaan nagoya main hi hua tha. abhi bhi yahan mitsubishi ke kai ladaku vimaanon kaa nirmaan karata hai. mitsubishi kshetreey jet vimaan kaa nirmaan hawaii adde se sate kaarkaane main kiya jaataa he. emarje mitsubishi heavi industries or toyota ke bich ek saajhedaari ke taur par banaaya gaya he. isaki kii pehali udaan 11 november, 2015 ko thi.
