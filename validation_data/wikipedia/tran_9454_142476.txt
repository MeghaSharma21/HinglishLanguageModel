 aaisland, newzealand kaa sabase bada nagar he. yah prayadweep ke bahut sankare bhaag main sthit hai. is kaaran donon taton par iskaa adhikaar hai parantu uttam bandargah purvi tat par he. austrailia se america janevaale jahaaj, visheshkar sidney se vainkuvar janevaale, yahan thaharte hai. yah aadhunik bandargah he. yahan par vishvavidhyalay, kalabhavan tathaa ek ni:shulk pustkaalay he jo sundar chitron se sajaa he. is nagar ke aas paas newton, parnell, new market tathaa naurthkot upnagar based hai. aaisland kii aabaadi din prati din badhati ja rahi he. iskaa mukhya kaaran dugdh udyog tathaa anya dhandhe hai. aaisland jahaaj dwaara austrailia, prashantadweep, dakshini africa, great britain tathaa sanyukt rajya america se sambhddha he or relon dwaara newzealand ke duusare bhaagon se. yahan kaa mukhya udyog jahaaj bananaa, chini saaf karnaa tathaa yudhsaamagree bananaa he. iske sivaay yahan lakdi tathaa bhojansamagree ityadi kaa karbar bhi hota he. yahan se lakdi, doodh ke bane saamaan, oon, chamada, sona or fal baahar bheja jaataa he.
