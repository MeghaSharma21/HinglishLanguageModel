 sanskrit kaa sabase pracheen vyakaran 'vaidic vyakaran' kehalaata he. yah panineey vyakaran se kuch bhinn tha. 
 sanskrit main likhit brihad sahitya ke mukhyatah do khand hai - vaidic sahitya or loukik sahitya. vaidic sahitya ke mukhyatah paanch vibhaag hai- 
 kalpasutra jo pradhantah teen prakaar ke hai, 
 ye teen prakaar ke sutara ‘kalpasutra’ ke antargat liye jate hai. inke atirikt hai- shulvasutra jo yagyavedi sambandhi rekhanit roopon kaa niyojan karte hai or is kaaran kabhi-kabhi kalpasutron ke hi bheetar gine jate hai. uparyukt kalpasutron ke atirikt kuch or bhi granth hai jinka sambandh dhwani, vigyaan, vyakaran, chand or nakshatravidya se he. yah granth vedangon main pariganit hote hai. ye granth bhi sutara-style main hi milte hai or inkaa samay he vaidic evam loukik sanskrit kaa sandhikaal.
 loukik sanskrit-sahitya kaa vaidic sahitya se anek prakaar kaa bhed paaya jaataa he. vaidic sahitya shuddhtah dharmik he tathaa is main sabhi loukik tattvon kaa beej samahit he. loukik sanskrit sahitya pradhan rup se dharmik-dharmanirpeksh, athwa dharm main ise lok-paralok se hi sambandhit kaha ja saktaa he. is sahitya main mahakavy, puraana evam anya kavy naatak, alankarishastra, darshan, sutara, vidhi athwa niyamkala, vaastushastra, aushadhi, ganit, mashin tathaa udyog sambandhi tathaa anekon granth or anya vibhinn vidyaon kii shakhaaen bhi praapt hoti hai.
 loukik sahitya kii bhashaa tathaa vaidic sahitya kii bhashaa main bhi antar paaya jaataa he. donon ke shabdarup tathaa dhaaturup anek prakaar se bhinn hai. vaidic sanskrit ke rup keval bhinn hi nahin hai apitu anek bhi hai, vishishtatya ve rup jo kriya roopon tathaa dhaatuon ke swaroop se sambandhit hai. is sambandh main donon saahitiyon kii kuch mahatvapurn bhinnataayen nimnlikhit hai :-
  shabdarup kii drushti se udaahranarth, loukik sanskrit main keval aise rup banate hai jaise - devah, janaah . jabaki vaidic sanskrit main inamen rup devasah, janaamah bhi banate hai. isi prakaar, prathama tathaa dwitiya vibhakti bahuvachan main ‘vishwani’ rup vaidic sahitya main ‘vishwa’ bhi ban jaataa he. tritiya vibhakti bahuvachan main vaidic sanskrit main ‘devai’ ke saath-saath ‘devebhih’ bhi miltaa he. isi prakaar saptami vibhakti ekvachan main 'vyomni' athwa 'vyomani' roopon ke saath-saath vaidic sanskrit main ‘vyoman’ bhi praapt hota he.
  vaidic tathaa loukik sanskrit main kriyaroopon or dhaaturupon main bhi vishesh antar he. vaidic sanskrit is vishay main kuch adhik samrudh he tathaa usamen kuch anya roopon kii uplabdhi hoti he. jabaki loukik sanskrit main kriya padon kii avasthaa batalaane vaale aise keval do hi lakaar hai- lot or vidhilin joki latprakruti arthath vartmaankaal kii dhaatu se banate hai. udaahranarth path se pathtu or pathet ye donon banate hai. vaidic sanskrit main kriyaapadon kii avasthaa ko dyotit karne vaale do anya lakaar hai- let lakaar evam nishedhaatmak lulkaar . in chaaron avasthaaon ke dyotak lakaar vaidic sanskrit main keval lat prakruti se hi nahin banate hai kintu lit prakruti or lu prakruti se bhi banate hai. is prakaar vaidic sanskrit main dhaaturup atyadhik maatra main hai. iske atirikt lin pratyay sambandhi bhed vaidic sanskrit main paye jate hai jaise 'minimsibhi' prayukt hota he parantu loukik sanskrit main 'minimahi' prayukt hota he. jahaan tak dhaatu se bane hue anya roopon kaa prashn he, loukik sanskrit main keval ek hi ‘tumun’ miltaa he jabaki vaidic sanskrit main iske lagbhag ek darjan rup milte hai jaise gantvai, gamdhyai, jeevasai, daatvai ityadi.
  punasch, loukik sanskrit aage chalakar adhikadhik kritrim athwa subaddh hoti gai he or iske udhaaharan hamen subandhu or banbhatat ke gadhykavyon main prayukt bhayaavah samaason main milte hai. is kala main vah apane kshetra ke anya gadhyakaron se atyant utkrisht hai.
  kuch vaidic shabd loukik sanskrit main aprapya hai or kuch naye shabdon kaa udbhav bhi ho gaya he. udaahranarth, vaidic shabd ‘aps’ kaa ‘kaary’ ke arth main prayog loukik sanskrit main lupt ho gaya he. loukik sanskrit main prayukt ‘parivaar’ shabd vaidic sanskrit main anuplabdh he. yah vaidic evam loukik sanskrit kii apni visheshta he.
 shabdaarth vigyaan kii drushti se kuch shabdon main ek vishisht parivartan hua he jaise ‘ritu’ jiskaa vaidic sanskrit main arth he ‘shakti’ or loukik sanskrit main uskaa arth 'yajna’ ho gaya he.
 bhashaa main parivartan ke atirikt donon saahitiyon main kuch or bhinnataayen prapya hai-
  prathamtah, jaisaa ki upar kah chuke hai, vaidic sahitya pradhantah dharmik he jab ki loukik sanskrit apane varnyavishay kii drushti se dharm ke saath-saath loukik jivan ke prayek kshetra se sambaddh he.
  donon kii aatma yadhyapi abhinn he tathaapi abhinnata main bhi bhinnata ke darshan hote hai. vaidic vaanmay, mukhyatah jaisaa ki rugveda tathaa atharvaveda main hamen praapt hota he, ashaavadi he jabaki loukik sanskrit sahitya nirashaavadi he, is nirashavad kii jhalak baudhon ke ‘sarv dukhan’ main bhi he. baudhon ke vyavhaaryapaksh ‘karunaa’ or ‘maitri’ kaa udghosh bhi vaidic sahitya kii moulikta he.
  vaidic dharm bhi parvarti kaal main avyakt rup se vishisht parivardhit hua dikhaai detaa he. yahan tak ki vaidic yug ke pradhan devtaa jaise indra, agni, varun ko loukik sanskrit main apekshaakrit vishistataa praapt nahin hui parantu brahma, vishnu or shiv in teenon ko vedon main keval gaun sthaan hi praapt tha, parvarti kaal main inhen ek mahatvapurn sthaan praapt ho gaya. is kaal main kuch nae devi devataaon- ganesh, kuber, lakshmi or durga ityadi kaa bhi vaidic mul se vikaas hua.
  parvarti kaviyon main atyukti kaa aashray grahan karne kii ore adhik jhukaav he, jaise magh, sriharsh aadi main jabaki purvavarti kaviyon jaise ashwaghosh, bhaas or kalidas main atyukti kaa abhaav he. vaidic vaanmay main atyukti kaa maha abhaav he.
  loukik samskruti main chandobaddh roopon ke prayogon kii ore hamen ek vishisht aagrah dikhaayi detaa he. vaidic yug main bhi chandobaddh roopon kaa aadhiky miltaa he, kintu vahaan visheshtah yajna sambandhi sahitya main gadhy kaa bhi prayog hua, jaise yajurveda or brahmanon main. loukik sanskrit kaal main chandobaddh roopon ke prayog kii ore itnaa adhik jhukaav he ki yahan tak ki vaidyak granth bhi padya main hi likhe gayi. aashcharya to is baat se hota he ki koshon kii rachana bhi chhandon main hi hui. kuch aage chalakar parvarti kaal main baan or subandhu ne gadhy kaavyon ke lekhan kii style kaa vikaas kiya, jo bade-bade samaason se mishrit hone ke kaaran atyant kritrim kahi jaati he. iske atirikt purvavarti kaal main sutara-rup main darshanik granthon ko likhane kii pranali kaa bhi prachlan hua.
 aage chalakar hamen chhandon kii pranali kaa bhi ek parivartan drushtigochar hota he. vaidic chand jagati, trishtubh, anushtubh to loukik sanskrit main sarvatha anuplabdh he. jabaki loukik sanskrit ke chand vanshastha, upendravajra, shikharini aadi vedon main purnatah aprapya hai. haa, yah avashya sach he ki loukik sanskrit main prayukt shloke chand vaidic anushtubh chand kaa hi rup hai.
 vaidic evam loukik sanskrit kii bhinnataaon kii ore drushtipaat karte hue yah dhyaan dena aavashyak he ki siddhant kii drushti se donon ek duusare se kaaphi milti-julati hai. vedon main kuch or adhik dhvaniyaan milti hai, jaise ki l’. anya dhwani-siddhanth donon ke samaan hi hai or unamem koi bhi vaisaa antar nahin dikhaayi detaa jaisaa ki praakrit boliyon main hamen praapt hota he.
 a-car sangya kaa sabase bada varg he. niyamanusaar is varg se sambandhit sangyaayen hrisv- a par khatm hoti hai, yaa to pu yaa fir napus. deergh-aa par khatm hone vaali sangyaayen adhiktar stri hote hai. yah varg bahut bada he kyonki isamen prak-hind-europiai o-car bhi sammilit he.
 
 
 
 
 ri-car kaa prayog ri ant vaale shabdon jaise napusak. datra 'denevaala', pu. pitru 'baap', naptri 'bhatija' or stri. matra 'maan', duhitri 'beti' or swasri 'bahan'.
 
 
 jaise homeric greek clasical greek se bhinn he vaisai rigvaidic bhashaa sanskrit bhashaa se bhinn he. tiwari ne donon ke bich antar ko nimna siddhant swaroop suchit kiya:
 aadharbhoot paddati neeche talika main di gai he — lagbhag sabhi sangyaaon or visheshnon ke liye vaid or tarksangat. phir bhi, ling or shabdamul ke antim vyanjan/swar anusaar, anivaary sandhi ke kuch purvanirdhaarit sutra hai jo antim sarvamaanya shabd ko banaate hain. napusakaling ke liye alag se pratyay die gaye hoyenge, baaki ke pu or stri ke liye hoyenge. jahaan do yaa teen rup die honge vahaan pehla pu, par dusraa or teesraa stri hoga.
