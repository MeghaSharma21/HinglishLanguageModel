 brahmandavidya yaa cosmology khagol vigyaan kii ek shakha he, jisamen brahmand se judi tamaam baton kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. isamen brahmand ke banane kii prakriya ke bare main bhi jaankaree di jaati he. beesvin shatabdi main aae vaigyanik badlaavon ne vaigyanikon kii brahmand ke bare main dilchaspee badha di. vaigyanik usase jude tamaam rahasyon ko talashne lage.
 yah beesvin shatabdi main cosmology ke bare main badhati jigyaasa kaa hi pratifal tha ki vaigyanikon ne brahmand kii utpatti se judi big baing siddhant de daalaa. cosmology kii shuruyaat ek tarah se oinstin ke saamaanya saapekshata siddhant ke baad se maani jaati he. usase purv brahmand ke shuruyaat or ant ke bare main koi nishchit dharna nahin thi.
 oinstin ne kaha ki brahmand main kuch padaarth he. isi siddhant ke aadhaar par fredman ne apana siddhanth diya jisamen kaha ki brahmand fail rahaa he yaa sikud rahaa he. 1927 main jorge lemetri ne big baing theory dekar fredman ke siddhant kii pushti kii. iske baad habal ke cosmological siddhant ne big baing theory or fred ke gallaxi ke niyam ko aadhaar pradaan kiya.
 1965 main microwave kii khoj or brahmand kii utpatti ke niyam ke baad is baat ko mana jane laga ki vah fail rahaa he. microwave kii khoj ke dauraan yah baat bhi saamne ai ki brahmand ke 25 pratishat hisse main dark matter he jisame se keval 4 pratishat ko hi dekha ja saktaa he.
 vaigyanikon ne bhi is baat ko mana ki visphot se pehle brahmand sikuda hua tha. cosmology ke maanak siddhanth anusaar brahmand ko vibhinn samay main baanta ja saktaa he. inhen 'ipos' kehete hai. mote taur par kaha jaae to physical cosmology ke madhyam se brahmand ke bade object masalan gallexi, cluster or supercluster ke bare main jankaree milti he. cosmology kii madad se blike hole ke bare main jaanane main madad mili. cosmology se yah bhi dekha gaya ki bhautikee ke niyam brahmand main har jagah samaan hai yaa nahin.
