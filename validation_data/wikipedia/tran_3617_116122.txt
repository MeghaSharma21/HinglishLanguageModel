 sukhoi esayu-9  soviet sangh dwaara viksit ek ekal ingine, sabhi mausam me kaam karne vaala, missile-sashastr interceptor vimaan tha.
 sukhoi esayu-9 kaa vayugatikeey adhyayan se tisjiai, soviet vayugatikeey centre me korian yuddh ke dauraan ubhara tha. pehle 1956 main tii-405 prototip dizin ke rup main sukhoi esayu-9 ne udan bhari. sukhoi esayu-9 ko sukhoi esayu-7 "fitre" ke saath hi samaantar viksit kiya gaya tha. or donon ko pehali baar 24 june 1956 ko tashino aviation diwas par pashchim desho dwaara dekha gaya tha. isane 1959 main seva main pravesh kiya. 
 sukhoi esayu-9 kaa kul utpaadan lagbhag 1,100 vimaan tha. aisa mana jaataa he ki kam se kam kuch sukhoi esayu-9 ko su -11 "fishpot-si" rup main apgrade kiya gaya tha. soviet sangh ke rashtron main se kisi ko or na hi warsaw sandhi rashtron ko sukhoi esayu-9 niryaat kiya gaya tha. 1970 ke dashak ke dauraan shesh sukhoi esayu-9 or baad main sukhoi esayu-11es sevanivrutt hue. kuch ko pareekshan vaahanon ke rup main rakhaa gaya tha yaa maanav rahit hawaii vaahanon ke rup main upyog ke liye duurastha-chaalit vaahanon main parivartit kiya gaya tha. ise unnat sukhoi esayu-11 or ucch shreshthata vaala sukhoi esayu-15 "flagon" or mig-25 "foxbat" dwaara badal diya gaya tha.
 sukhoi esayu-9 ke ladaaku mishno kaa record agyaat he. yah sambhav he ki sukhoi esayu-9 guptchar abhiyaan ke avarodhan main shaamil tha. jinke vivaran gupt rakhaa gaya he, lekin kuch bhi saarvajanik rup se sweekaar nahin kiya gaya he.
 yah bataaya gaya ki 1 may 1960 ko soviet kshetra par francis gairi powers 'you-2' ke avarodhan main ek sukhoi esayu-9 shaamil tha. 4 sitambar, 1959 ko vladimir sarjegeich ilushin dwaara sanchalit ek sanshodhit sukhoi esayu-9 28,852 meter kii purna oonchai tak pahuch kar nayaa vishwa record banaaya. baad main yah record 6 disambar 1959 ko commander laurence i flint, junior dwaara tod diya gaya, jinhone epa4ech 1 phantum kaa sanchaalan karte hue 98,557 feet kii zum chadhaai karke vishwa record banaaya.
