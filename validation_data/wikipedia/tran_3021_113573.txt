 meitei lipi yaa meitei myek 18veen sadi tak manipuri bhashaa ke liye istemaal hone vaali ek lipi thi. dhire-dhire manipuri likhane ke liye iskaa sthaan bengali lipi ne le liya. 20veen sadi ke ant main ise phir se prayog main laane ke liye kuch prayaas kiye ja rahe the.
 meitei lipi main 15 vyanjan or 3 swar likhane ke chihn uplabdh hai. inke alaava anya bhartiya lipiyon se liye gayi 9 atirikt chihn bhi uplabdh hai. har akshar kaa naam sharir ke kisi bhaag par rakhaa gaya he. masalan pratham akshar 'k' kii dhwani rakhata he or uskaa naam 'coke' he. duusare akshar kaa naam 'sam' or tisare kaa naam 'laai' he.
 meitei myek lipi ko unicode main oktober 2009 main sammilit kiya gaya tha.
