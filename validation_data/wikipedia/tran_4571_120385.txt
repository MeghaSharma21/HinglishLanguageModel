 chandragupta pratham gupt vansh ke tritiy kintu pratham swatantra evam shaktishaalee naresh. sadhrantaya vidvan unke rajarohan kii tithi 319-320 i. nishchit karte hai. kuch log aisa bhi maanate hai ki unhone usee tithi se aarambh honevaale gupt sanwat kii sthapana bhi kii thi.
 gupton kaa aadhipatay aarambh main dakshin bihar tathaa uttar-pashchim bengal par tha. pratham chandragupta ne samrajya kaa vistaar kiya. vayupurana main prayag tak ke ganga ke tatavarti pradesh, saket tathaa magadh ko gupton kii 'bhogabhumi' kaha he. is ullekh ke aadhaar par vidvan chandragupta pratham kii rajyasima kaa nirdhaaran karte hai, yadhyapi is baat kaa koi pusht pramaan uplabdh nahin he. chandragupta pratham ne lichhavi kumaradevi se vivaah kiya tha. sambhav he, samrajyanirmaan main chandragupta pratham ko lichchhaviyon se paryaapt sahaayta mili ho. yah bhi sambhav he ki lichhavi rajya mithila is vivaah ke falswaroop chandragupta ke shashan ke antargat aa gaya ho. 'kaumudi mahotsav' aadi se gyaat evam unpar aadhrut, chandragupta pratham ke rajarohan aadi se sambhddha itihaas nirdhaaran sarvatha asangat he. unhone sambhawatah ek prakaar kii swarnamudra kaa prachlan kiya, evam maharajaadhiraj kaa virud dhaaran kiya. prayag prashasti ke aadhaar par kah sakate hai ki chandragupta pratham ne samudragupta ko apana uttaradhikari niyukt kiya or sambhawatah 380 i. ke lagbhag unke sudeergh shashan kaa ant hua.
