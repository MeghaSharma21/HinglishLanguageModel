 manovigyaan main prakaaryawad ek aisa skool yaa sampradaay he jisaki utpatti sanrachnawad ke varnanaatmak tathaa vishleshnatmak upaagam ke virodh main hua. wiliam james dwaara prakaaryawad kii sthapana america ke harvard vishvavidhyalay main kii gayi thi. parantu iskaa vikaas chicago vishvavidhyalay main jaan divi james aar angil tathaa harvey e car ke dwaara tathaa columbia vishvavidhyalay ke iel tharnadike tathaa aaref budworth ke yogadanon se huyi.
 prakaaryawad main mukhyatah do baton par prakaash daalaa- vyakti kya karte he? tathaa vyakti kyon koi vyavhaar karte he? woodworth ke anusaar in donon prashnon kaa uttar dhudhane vaale manovigyaan ko prakaaryawad kaha jaataa he. prakaaryawad main chetana ko uske vibhinn tatwon ke rup main vishleshan karne par bal nahin daalaa jaataa balki isamen maansik kriya yaa anukool vyavhaar ke adhyayan ko mahatv diya jaataa he. anukool vyavhaar main multah pratyakshan smriti, bhav, nirnay tathaa ichha aadi kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he kyonki in prakriyaaon dwaara vyakti ko vaataavaran main samaayojan main madad milti he. prakaaryavaadiyon ne saahchary ke niyamon jaise samanta kaa niyam, sameeptha kaa niyam tathaa barambharataa kaa niyam pratipaadit kiya jo seekhney main apni mahatvapurn bhoomika banaata he.
 columbia prakaaryavaadiyon main iel tharnadike va ares woodworth kaa yogadan sarvaadhik rahaa. tharnadike ne ek pustak 'shiksha manovigyaan' likhi jisamen inhone seekhney ke niyam likhe hai. tharnadike ke anusaar manovigyaan uddipan-anukriya sambandhon ke adhyayan kaa vigyaan he. tharnadike ne seekhney ke liye sambandhwaad kaa siddhanth diya, jiske anusaar seekhney main prarambh main trutiyaan adhik hoti he kintu abhyaas dene se in trutiyon main dhire-dhire kami aa jaati he.
 woodworth ne anya prakaaryavaadiyon ke samaan manovigyaan ko chetan tathaa vyavhaar ke adhyayan kaa vigyaan mana. inhone seekhney kii prakriya ko kaaphi mahatvapurn bataaya kyonki isse yah pataa chalata he ki seekhney kii prakriya kyon kii gayi. woodworth ne udwipak-anukriya ke sambandh main parivartan karte huye praani kii bhoomika ko mahatvapurn maanate huye udwipak-praani-anukriya sambandh ko mahatvapurn bataaya.
