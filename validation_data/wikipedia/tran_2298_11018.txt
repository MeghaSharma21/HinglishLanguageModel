 osman tritiy  osmani samrajya ke sultan the. osman tritiy ne 1754 se 1757 tak hukumat kii.
 osman tritiy 2 janvari 1699 ko adarna mahal main paidaa hue. vah mahmud pratham ke chote bhai or mustafaa dwitiya ke bete the or unki maan shahasavaar sultan thim. unhone laila naam kii kaadan se shaadi kii.
 sultan usmaan ne maamuli darje kii shiksha pai. isko shatranj khilne kaa shauq tha.
 sultan osman tritiy ne sabase pehle sultan ahamed tritiy ke teenon beton ko qatal karvaa diya taaki baghaavat kaa andeshaa na rahe. usne intihaai alpakaalik arase kii hukumat kii. is arase main vah sultan mahmud pratham ke siyaasi usulon kaa paband rahaa.
 osman tritiy ne 58 saal kii umr main 30 aktubar 1757 ko topkapi mahal, qustutuniya main maut pai.
