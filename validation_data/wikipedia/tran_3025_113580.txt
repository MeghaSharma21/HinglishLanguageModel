 kanwali athwa kaanvali bhartiya rajya uttrakhand ke dehradun nagar nigam antargat ek ward he. 1998 se purv ye hissaa kanwali gram panchayat kehalaata tha. varsh 2011 ke nagareey parisemen ke anusaar kanwali kii ward sankhya 50 he or yahan kaa pincode 248001 he.
 bharat kii 2011 kii janaganana ke aankadon ke anusaar kanwali kii kul jansankhya 10,318 thi or yahan kul 2,124 parivaar nivas karte the. kul jansankhya main purushon kii sankhya 5,506 tathaa mahilaon kii sankhya 4,812 thi. chah varsh se kam aayu varg kii jansankhya 1,275 thi jisamen baalkon kii sankhya 712 or balikaon kii sankhya 563 thi.
