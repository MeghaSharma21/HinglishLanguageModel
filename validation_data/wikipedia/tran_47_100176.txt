 jon edward grey ek british, jeev vigyaani the. vah jorge robert grey ke bade bhai or aushadh vigyaani or vanaspatishastri samuel frederick grey ke bete the. 
 grey, 1840 se 1874 ke christmas tak landan ke british sangrahalay me jeev vigyaan ke keeper the. unhone sangrahalay ke sangrah se sambandhit kai kaitlog prakashit kiye the jiname jeev samoohon or nai prajatiyon kaa vistrut vivaran diya gaya tha.
