 shivacharan maathur ek bhartiya rajaneta he or rajasthan ke mukhyamantri rah chuke he.shiv charan maathur ek bhartiya raajnneetijna the. bhartiya rashtriya congress ke ek netaa, vah 1981 se 1985 tak rajasthan kaa mukhyamantri or phir 1989 se 1989 tak; baad main, vah 2008 se 2009 tak assam ke rajyapal the.
 maathur 14 july 1981 ko rajasthan kii mukhyamantri bane or 23 farvari 1985 tak us pad kaa aayojan kiya. iske baad vah phir se 20 janvari 1988 se 4 disambar 1989 tak mukhyamantri bane. 2003 main unhen mandalgarh se vidhayak ke rup main chunaa gaya.
 bade paimaane par apil hasil karne ke baavjood maathur ne apni vishisht yogyata ke madhyam se ucch pad praapt kiya or congress party ke aantarik pavitra sthaan se mahatvapurn samarthan ke saath.
 2008 main assam ke rajyapal ke rup main niyukt kiya gaya, vah 25 june 2009 ko hriday kii se unki mrityu tak is pad par bane rahe. 
 shiv charan maathur kaa janm 1926 ko ek kysth parivaar main shivaratri ke pavitra diwas par madhya pradesh ke guna jile ke ek chote se gaanv main hua tha. pratikul paariwarik paristhitiyon se majaboor, shiv charan, jab vah keval chaar varsh ke the, apni maa ke saath karauli ke saath apane naanaa, bhavar laal kii sangrakshakata main rahete the.
