 manoranjak ganit ek vistrut arth vaala shabd he jiske atargat ganitiya paheliyaan evam ganitiya khel sammilit hai. is kshetra main aane vaali sabhi samasyaon ke liye ucch ganit kii jarurat nahin hoti; isiliye manoranjak ganit saamaanya logon ko bhi apni ore khinch letaa he or ve ganit main dhire-dhire ruchi lenaa aaramambh kar dete hai.
