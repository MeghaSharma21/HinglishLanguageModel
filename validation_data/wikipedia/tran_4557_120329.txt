 
 thomas hardi kii rachaana da meyar aaf castorbridadj pratham main 'graphic' naamak samachar patr main tathaa 'harpers vikli' main janvari 1886 main prakashit kii gai thi. iske agle varsh ke may main yah kuch parivartan ke pashchaat ek sampoorn upanyaas ke rup main prakashit hua. ise bhaagon main prakashit karne ke liye koi prakaashak mil paanaa bahut mushkil tha. kahani castorbridj naamak pradesh main sthit he. asal main castorbridj dorchester naamak praant kaa chitrankan he. in dono praanton main kai jagahon par ekta dikhaai gai he. thomas hardi ne kahani main kai baar apne vakt ke samaaj ke bare main na sirf bataaya he, balki us smaj ki kuritiyon ko bhi darshaya he. hardi angrezi sahitya main victorian kaal ke lekhak he. victorian samaaj main jahaan ek taraf naitik mulyon ko atyaadhik mahatv diya jaataa tha, vahiin duusari taraf bhrashtachar kaa bhi bahut prachlan tha.
 kahani kii shruwaat main mukhya paatr henchard sharaab ke nashe main apni patni sujan ko meley main bech detaa he. uski patni or beti ko newsan naamak nausainik ko bech detaa he. agle din jab use apni galati kaa ehsaas hot he, to vah jivan main kabhi sharaab ko haath na lagane kii kasam khaataa he. athara varshon ke baad hmen henchard ek sfal saudagar ke rup main dikhaai detaa he. uskaa gussa or aakrosh ab bhi uske charitra kaa hissaa he. en saare varshon main henchard kaa ateet ek raaj hi rah gaya tha. log henchard ko vidhur samajhte the, jabaki usne kabhi kisiko yah nahin kaha tha ki uski pehali patni kii mrityu ho chuki he. in athar varshon main henchard ko yah maan lenaa hi behatar laga ki sujan or uski putree elizbet kii mrityu ho chuki he. isi dauraan jab vah apne vyavsaay ke liye jersey jaataa tha, use luseta naamak ladkee se prem ho jaataa he. jab henchard or luseta shaadi karne ki sochte hai, tabhi sujan or elizbet athara varshon ke baad use dhundte hue aa pahonchte hai. saath hi yahan par donald farfre k bhi pravesh hot he. iske baad henchard kii zindagi puntah badal jaati he. henchard or farfre kii mithrat pehale-pehale ghanisht hoti jati he, magar jald hi dono kii mitrataa shatruta main parivartit ho jaati he. yaheen par henchard apane krodh or aakrosh ke kaaran apana sab kuch khone lagata he. vah apni shatruta ke kaaran elizbet or farfe ke rishte ko tod detaa he. par jaise hi use sujan ke mrityu ke pashchaat yah pataa chalata he ki sujan uski nahin newsan kii putree he or uski asli putree kaa usee vakt dehaant ho gaya tha, to vah usase peechha chudaane ke liye phir farfre ko patr likhrekrar elizbet ko phir se apnaane ko kehta he.isi dauraan luseta kii bhi zindagi poorntah badal jati he or vah bhi castorbridj main nayaa ghar kharid leti he. ab luseta bahut hi amir ho gai he or henchard se vivaah karnaa chaahati he. suzan ke vaapis aa jane ke vajah se henchard luseta ko thukra detaa he, parantu suzan bhi henchard se vivaah karne ke pashchaat zyaada din jivit nahin rahati.uske mrityu ke pashchaat henchard vapis luseta ke paas jaataa he, tab tak luseta ko farfe se prem ho chukaa tha.jahaan henchard ek taraf apana sab kuch gavaan rahaa tha, vahiin farfre safalataa ki udan bhar rahaa tha. is prakaar henchard apn sab kuch kho detaa he or kahani ke ant main akelaa hi rah jaataa he. vah ek-ek kar apane sabase kareebi vyaktiyon ko khone ke dar main vyakul hokar un sabko khud se dur kar detaa he. is prakaar vah pehale to apane achche vakt main apne ahankaar ke kaaran or bure vakt main apane dar ke vajah se akel hi rah jaataa he. yahan tak ki uske mrityu ke samay bhi uske paas koi apana nahin hota.
 henchard shaareerik taur par balavaan he. yadhyapi vah zyaada shikshit nahin he, tathaapi vah padh saktaa he. iske charitra ki khaasiyat he iskaa ahankaar, aavesh, jald-baazi or ati vyavhaar he, jo isko iske ant ki ore le jane main parmukh he.
 farfre asal main scotland se he or henchard ke baad yahi meyar banataa he. yah har taur se henchard kaa viparith he. shaareerik avasthaa se lekar shiksha tak henchard or farfre main koi samanta nahin he. uskaa drishtikon bahut vaigyanik he. henchard ko uski har baat bahut acchhee lagati he or isi vajah se un dono kii dosti itani gahari hoti he or baad main isi vajah se unki mitrataa shatruta main badal jaati he.
 atyaadhik bholi hone kii vajah se sujan ko pehle bahut ksht uthaanaa padaa tha, magar kahani ke duusare bhaag main ham suzan ko bilkul alag vyakti ke rup main dekhte he.
