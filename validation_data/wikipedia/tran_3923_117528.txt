 jayapeed vinayaditya kashmir ke carkotvansh ke lalitaditya muktapid kaa pautra or vrajaditya bappiyak kaa putr jayapeed vinayaditya ke naam se bhi prasiddh tha. vah apane pitamah lalitaditya kii bhanti hi kushal senanayake tha. kalhan ke anusaar usne apane rajya ke praarambhik varshon main hi purv kii ore abhiyaan kiya, panch gaudon ko parajeet karke pundravardhan ke naresh jayant ko unka adhishwar banaaya or kashmir ko lautate hue kanyakubj ke naresh ko parajeet kiya. uttari bharat kii avyavasthit raajnneetik sthiti main aisi vijay asambhav nahin thi. kuch vidvan iske samarthan main madhyadesh ke kuch sthanon se praapt shri ja. pratap ke sikkon kaa ullekh karte hai jinhen ve jayapeed ke sikke maanate hai. kintu kalhan ke vivaran main kuch baaten adbhut or katha jaisi hai. jayapeed kii anupasthiti main uske bahanoi jaj ne singhaasana par adhikaar kar liya tha kintu jaipeed ke lautne par uske saath yuddh main jajj maaraa gaya. kalhan kaa kathan he ki kuch samay baad jayapeed phir vijay ke liye niklaa. uskaa sangharsh purvi bharat ke naresh bhimsen or nepal ke shaasha armudi se hua. uskaa antim yuddh strirajya ke saath tha. ye naam or ye yuddh aitihaasik jaise nahin lagate kintu levi naam ke vidvan inkaa nitant niraadhaar nahin maanate. rajyakaal ke ant kii ore apane utpeedak karon ke kaaran jayapeed jansadharan main apriy ho gaya tha. brahmanon ke ek shadyantr ke falswaroop shashan ke 31wein varsh main uskaa ant hua.
 jayapeed swayan kavi tha. uski rachana ke uddhran subhashit granthon main milte hai. uskaa shasankaal uskaa sangrakshan paanevaale kaviyon ke kaaran prasiddh hai. inke naam hai manorath, shankhadutt, chatak, sandhimat or kuttanimtam ke rachayita damodargupta. kavyashastra main alankaar parampara ke sarvaprasiddh samarthak udbhat jayapeed ke sabharatna the. reeti ko kavy kii aatma maananevaale duusare prasiddh kavyashastri vaaman bhi jayapeed ke hi darbar main the. jayapeed ne do nae shashanvibhag banaae - nyaay ke liye dharmadhikaran or abhiyaan ke kaaran rajdhani se dur rahane par suvidha ke liye ek gatisheel kosh athwa chalganj. jayapeed ne jaipur or dvaravati naam ke do nagaron kii sthapana kii. jaipur main usne buddha kii teen pratimaaen, ek vishaal vihaar evam jayadevi tathaa chaturatman keshav ke mandir banawaye.
