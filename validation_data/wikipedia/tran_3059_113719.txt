 ”’asti samast jagadutpattipalansanhaarkatrubhirbrahmavishnumahesharupasevyamana, jagadadharaoopa, sansarbhaynashini, apunavritikarini, sansartarini tara namni shakti: paramahati.”’yah param harsh kaa vishay he, ko jo shakti vidya bahut pracheen kaal se aparimit tejswani hone ke kaaran anek sampradaayon ke matbhed hote hue bhi sarvottama thi, vaise hi aaj bhi anek mat mataantar vaale manushyon dwaara sammanit sansar ke avaagaman ko hataane main sarvshereshth parampujit rup main upasthit he.tara-shakti kaa rahasy bada good he, use jaanane ke liye bade parishram or adhyavyavsaay kii aavashyaktaa he, yatharthanamavati hone ke kaaran tara namaki shakti sarvottama shakti he. tara-shakti kaa shabdic arth he ”’taratyanya sa tara”’ arthath is sansar sagar se taare vah hi tara he. taravidya kii gananaa das mahavidhyaon main he, iske mahatv kaa digdarshan karaate hue tantra granthom main kaha gaya he-”’vina dhyanam vina jaapyan vina pujaadibhi: priye, vina balin vinabhyasm, bhootshuddhyadibhirvina, vina kleshadibhirdevi deh du:khadibhirvina, siddhiraashu bhavedyasmatsmatsarvottama mata.”’ arthath vbina dhyaan, jap, puja, bali, abhyaas, bhootshuddhi, deh du:kha, klesh ke uthaaye hi isaki siddhi sheeghra ho jaati he;isi se ise sarvasiddhiyon main sarvottam sthaan praapt he. itani saralata bhala kis devtaa kii aradhana main hogi, saralata or bandhanmukti kii had he. aise nishkantak sukhprad maarg par bhala koun na chalanaa chahega, yahi kaaran he ki anant kaal se mata tara kii upasna abaadh rup se hoti chali aa rahi he. tara kaa swaroop kya he, iske varnan main kaha he-”’shunye brahamandgole tu panchashchhunyamadhyage, panchashunye sthita tara sarvante kalika smruta”’, arthath shunya brahmand-gol main pachaas shunya hai, jiname paanch shunya par shri tara or shesh par shri kalika birajman hai. vichaarneey vishay he, ki pachaas shunya kul hai, unamem se paanch shunya par shri taraji sthit hai, or baaki shunya par shri kalikaji bitrajman hai, or viraat chakra tathaa swarat chakra bhed se madhya main jo shunya aata he, usame brhmandnayika shri rajrajeshwari shri mahasundari shri shrividyaji kaa sthaan he. tantra main kaha gaya he-”’tat: shoonya pararoopa shri mahasundari kala, sundari rajrajeshi maha brahmandnayika, mahashunya tatastara, tadwegunyakramen c, muktau sanyojya sarvam tan mahasundaryanantat:”’, isame shri mahasundari ko kala, or shri tara ko mahashunya rup main roopit kiya he, ab dekhnaa yah he, ki shunya rup main hi sab daivata or daivi shaktiyaan hai, or mahatmaon kaa bhi yahi siddhanth he, ki sansar kaa shunya rup main se udbhav shunya main hi paraabhav he, tab nishchay hi in shaktiyon ko aadya shakti maananaa pasta he. sansar ke is shunya parinaam ko dekhakar hi mahatma log mohadi ko chod kar shunya rup nirvikaar brahmroop main leen hokar mukti saadhan karte hai, idhar jitne bhi beej mantra hai, un sabhi main shunya rup bindu he, koi beej mantra bindurahit nahi he, isi liye unka mahatv itnaa shreshth he, or jitna bhi is par vichaarte hai, adhikadhik gyaan or rahasy drushtigat hota chalaa jaataa he.
