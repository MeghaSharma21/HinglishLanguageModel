 saamaajik aandolan ek prakaar kaa 'samuhik kriya' he. saamaajik aandolan vyaktiyon or/yaa sanghathanon ke vishaal anaupchaarik samooh hote hai jinka dhyey kisi vishisht saamaajik mudde par kendrit hota he. duusare shabdon main ye ye koi saamaajik parivartan karnaa chaahate hai, uskaa virodh karte hai yaa kisi saamaajik parivartan ko samaapt kar purvasthiti main laana chaahate hai
 aadhunik paashchaatya jagat main saamaajik aandolan shiksha ke prasar ke dwaara tathaa unnisvin shadi main audyogikaran va nagarikaran ke kaaran shramikon ke avaagaman main vruddhi ke kaaran sambhav hue.
 aadhunik aandolan sansar bhar main logon ko jaagrit karne ke liye praudyogiki tathaa antarjaal kaa sahaara lete hai.
 bharat ke saamaajik aandolan ke sabase bade mahanayake dow babasaheb ambedkar he. dow babasaheb ambedkar kaa shaktishaalee saamaajik andolan vishwa ke sabase prabhaavshaalee aandolanon main se ek he tathaa bharat kaa sabase prabhaavshaalee saamaajik andolan he. babasaheb kaa aandolan bharat desh ke pichhade, garib, shoshit, dalit logon ko unke saamaajik, aarthik, dharmik, rajnatik visheshat: maanav adhikaar dene ke liye tha, yah bharat kii sabase bady saamaajik kranti bhi thi. dow babasaheb ambedkar samvidhan nirmaataa the, isliye unhone desh shoshit logon unke adhikaron ke sangharsh kiya or usamen safalataa pai. dow babasaheb ambedkar ji ke mahaad satyagrah yaa chavadaar talab andolan, nashik kaa kalaram mandir andolan or dalit buddha andolan prasiddh he. vishwa ke sabase mahaan maanavadhikari andolankaareeyon main dow babasaheb ambedkar ji kaa sthaan shirsh par sthaan he.
 raajnneetik bhagidaari ke sansthagat daayaron ke baahar parivartankami raajnneeti karne vaale andolanon ko saamaajik andolanon kii sangya di jaati he. lambe samay tak samuhik raajnneetik karrwai karne vaali ye andolankaaree sanrachanaayen nagar samaaj or raajnneetik tantra ke bich anaupchaarik sutara kaa kaam bhi karti hai. haalaanki zyaadaatar saamaajik andolan sarkari niti yaa aacharan ke kilaaf karyrat rahete hai, lekin swatahspoort yaa asangathit pratirodh yaa karrwai ko saamaajik andolan nahin mana jaataa. iske liye kisi spasht netritv or ek nirnayakari dhaanche kaa hona zaruri he. andolan main bhaag lene waalon ke liye kisi saajha makasad or vichaardhaara kaa hona bhi aavashyak he. kya ye kuubiyaan raajnneeti ke aupchaarik daayaron main kaam karne vaale kisi raajnneetik dal yaa dabaav samooh main nahin hotin? darasal, saamaajik andolan apane buniyadi charitra main anaupchaarik networkon kii anyonyakriya se banate hai. ve aise mudde chunate hai jinhen aupchaarik raajnneeti apnaane se inkaar kar detii he. saath hi ve pratirodh or golbandi ke gair-paramparaagat roopon kaa istemaal karte hai. saamaajik andolanon ne alpsankhyakon, hashiyagrast samoohon or adhikaar-vanchit tabakon kii raajnneeti ko badhaava diya he. isi kaaran se yah bhi mana jaataa he ki samakaleen loktantr apane vistaar or gaharaai ke liye saamaajik andolanon kaa rini he.
 dwitiya vishwa-yuddh ke baad global or rashtriya raajnneetik pranaaliyon main is parighatna kaa udai hua. inke peeche aisi shakhsiyaten, network, samooh or sanghathan the jinka uddeshy aupchaarik raajnneeti ke daayaron ke baahar samaaj, rajya, saarvajanik nitiyon ko jan-hit ke lihaaz se prabhavit karnaa tha. pichley saath varshon main saamaajik andolanon ne raajnneetik pranaaliyon or loktantrikaran kii prakriya par ullekhneey asar daalaa he. sari duniya main paryaavaran kaa andolan, yuddh virdohi andolan, asangathit mazaduron ke andolan, stri-adhikaron ke andolan, vaikalpik youniktaaon ke andolan is parighatna kii safalataa ke pramaan hai. vittiya poonji ke bhoomandaleekaran se upji jan-virdohi pravritiyon ke kilaaf chal rahe andolan bhi isi shrenee main aate hai. saamaajik andolanon kii ek kubi yah bhi he ki bhale hi unki raajnneetik karrwai main sthaniyata yaa zamin se jude hone yaa grassroots ke sambandh ko ahmiyat di jaae, lekin ve samasyaon or muddon ko uttarottar global sandarbhon main dekhte or paribhaashit karte hai. isi kaaran se ve sthaneey str par chalaai ja rahi pratirodh kii karravaayiyon ko global netvacag ke zariye tikaane main samarth ho pate hai. unka naaraa hota he ‘think globali, act lokali’. internet kii parighatna ke ubhaar ke baad saamaajik andolanon ke liye prasar, samanvay or netwarking kii suvidha main or badhotaree ho gayi he.
 dwitiya vishwayuddh ke baad pashchimi deshon main ubhare madhya varg ne swayan ko puraane varg aadhaarit andolanon ke mukaable raajnneetik, saanskrithik or bauddhik rup se vishisht mahasoos karte hue samuhik raajnneetik karrwai ke aise roopon ko apnaanaa pasand kiya jinke daayre main kahin vyapak kism ke naitik, saanskrithik or saamaajik mudde aate the. is jamaane main chale kai saamaajik andolan naagarik adhikaron, stri-adhikaron, yuddh kaa virodh karne or paryaavaran kii hifaazat karne ke agrahon ke ird-gird golband hue. saath kaa dashak aate-aate in andolanon kii gatividhiyaan europe or uttari america main kaafii bad gayin. inhen raajnneetik kamyaabi bhi milane lagi. yah dekh kar samaaj-vaigyanikon ne is parighatna kaa adhyayan shuru kiya. saamaajik andolanon ko samajhane ke liye sabase pehle manovigyaan kaa prayog kiya gaya. isse andolnarat logon, samoohon or networkon ke samuhik vyavhaar par roshni padi. duusari tarika sanrachanagat-prakaarywaadee kism kaa tha. usne yah dekhne kii koshish kii ki in andolanon kaa saamaajik sthirataa par kya asar pad rahaa he. maas socity kaa adhyayan karne vaale manovaigyanikon ko laga ki saamaajik andolan nijee tanaav se jujh rahe vyaktiyon kii abhivyaktiyaan hai. duusari taraf sanrachanagat-prakaarywaadee vidwaanon kaa khyaal tha ki ye andolan saamaajik pranali main aaye tanaavon kaa falitarth hai.
 sattar ke dashak main is anusandhan ne nayi karavat badali. is parivartan ke peeche naye chaatr andolanon or unase nikali pratirodh kii karravaayiyon kaa prabhaav tha. ek naye siddhant ne janm liya jise ‘risource mobilizeshan’ thiyari   ke naam se janaa jaataa he. is siddhant ne saamaajik andolanon ko ek aisi saamaajik prakriya ke rup main dekha jiske tahat raajnneetik udhyami kisi icchit saamaajik parivartan kii khaatir pehle to sansadhano kaa tarksangat sanchay karte hai or phir lakshyon ko vedhane ke liye sansadhano kaa prayog karte hai. is siddhant ke pairokaar maanate hai ki saamaajik andolan puri tarah se apni sansaadhan uplabdh kar paane kii kshamta par hi nirbhar karte hai. ye sansaadhan aarthik or maanviya sahyog jutaney par bhi aadhaarit ho sakate hai or inkaa udgam naitik sarokaaron or praadhikaar vagairah main bhi ho saktaa he.
 saamaajik andolanon kii samaj banane kii prakriya main ek thiyari santhnik vyavhaar ke adhyayan ke rup main bhi viksit hui. usne saamaajik andolan ko kisi bhi anya sangathit samooh kii tarah samajhane kii cheshtaa kii. aage chal kar kai vidwaanon ne saamaajik andolanon or sansthagat raajnneeti ke bich anyonyakriya par vishesh dhyaan diya jisase raajnneeti ko prakriyaaon ke rup main samajhane ke pariprekshya kaa vikaas hua. assi ke dashak main hue kuch anusandhanon ne vampanthi rujhaan vaale kai andolanon par dhyaan diya. ye vampanthi jatthebandiyaan padanukram par aadhaarit sanghathan ke bajaay kshaitij banaavat vaale sanghathan or baari-baari se netritv karne yaa anaupchaarik kism ke netritv main yakeen karne vaali thim. europe kii zamin or vahiin ke pariprekshya ke aadhaar par hue is shodh ne samskruti or asmita sambandhi andolanon ko apni vishay-vastu banaaya.  unhone dekha ki paryaavaran sambadhi andolan or stri-adhikaron ke liye hone vaale andolan kis prakaar pratirodh ke naye-anuthe taur-tareeke istemaal kar rahe hai. is vimarsh se nav-saamaajik andolanon kii thisis nikali. darasal, assi or nabbe ke dauraan prakaash main aayi yah thisis vampanth kii sthapit samaj ko chunauti dene vaali sabit hui. isse pehle vampanthi raajnneetik karrwai kaa matlab varg-aadhaarit gatividhi ke alaava kuch or nahin samajha jaataa tha. lekin, jab america main afro- ameriganon ne apane adhikaron ke samarthan main blike power or blike penther jaise radical or jujharu andolan chalaaye; jab vietnam yuddh ke kilaaf samrajyavad virdohi muhimen sangathit kii gayin; jab anuvik nihshastreekaran ke paksh main shanti andolan chalaa; jab radical feminimum ne apni daavedaariyaan pesh kin; or jab hawa or pani ke pradooshan ke kilaaf awaz buland kii gayi, to varg sangharsh ko hi kendriya saamaajik antarvirodh maanane vaali drushti ko prashnaankit hona padaa.
 in andolanon main ek vichaardhaaratmak vividhta thi. inaki zamin bhi ek-duusare se bhinn dikh rahi thi. parmparik marxvaadi samaj ko thukaraate hue andre groz, rudolf bahro, elaan turin or yuragan havermas ne apane- apane tareeke se daawa kiya ki saamaajik-raajnneetik asmita or raajnneetik karrwai kii khaatir kii jane vaali golbandi ke liye varg ab ek kargar pratyay nahin rah gaya he. in vidwaanon ne nav-saamaajik andolanon ko uttar- audyogic saamaajik sanrachanaon kii paidaaish qaraar diya. unka kahana tha ki lokopkaaree rajya ne shoshan ke puraane roopon ko kaafii-kuch nishprabhavi kar diya he. nayi paristhitiyon main aadhunik samaaj paraayepan ke naye roopon ko janm de rahaa he. isse paidaa hone vaale vikshobh ko nav- saamaajik andolan apana centre bana rahe hai. francisi samajashastri elaan turin ne da ritern of da actor kii rachana kii. turin ne radical ravaiya akhriyaar karte hue apil kii ki samajashastriyon ko saamaajik andolanon kii keval vyakhya tak hi simit na rah kar unamem bhagidaari karte hue unki gahari padtaal kerani chahie. bhoomandaleekaran ke ubhaar ne saamaajik andolan sambandhi europee or america pariprekshyon ke bich mail-milaap kii paristhitiyaan banaayi hai. ab vidwaangan saamaajik andolan main networkon kii bhoomika par vishesh dhyaan de rahe hai. is parighatna or bhoomandaleekaran-virdohi andolan ke sahyojan par bhi khaas jor diya ja rahaa he.
