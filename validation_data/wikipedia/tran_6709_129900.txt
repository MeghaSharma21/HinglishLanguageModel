 man pawan kii nauka kubernath raay kaa nibandh-sangrah he. isamen bharat tathaa suvarnbhoomi evam suvarnadweep ke saanskrithik sambandhon kaa vivaran miltaa he. isamen vrihattar bharat kaa vivaran bhi miltaa he. yah saahitiyik rachana dweepantar bharat ;dakshin-purv asiyaaddh kaa saanskrithik surveykshan prastut karti he. isamen unhone is kshetra kii nrijaateey sanrachana- ‘cham’ ‘man-khmer’- evam saanskrithik sanrachana main bhartiya tattvon kii upasthiti kii talash kii he. kubernath raay ise nrijaateey evam anya tattvon kii drushti se saanskrithik bharat kaa hi ek ang maanate hai.
