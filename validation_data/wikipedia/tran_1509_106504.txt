 sera de traamuntana ek parbat ladi he. ye spain ke dweep melorica main sthit he. ye parbat ladi dakshin pashchim se uttar purab ke taraf failaa hua he. sera de traamuntana ko unesco dwaara 27 june 2011 kii vishwa viraasat sthanon main shaamil kiya gaya. unesco ise bhautik or saanskrithik mahatv kaa ek mahatvapurn kshetra mana jaataa he.
 iskaa ucchtam shikhar 1.445 meter oonchai he jiskaa naam puig major he. duusari pramukh shikhar puig di maasanela he jisaki oonchai 1.364 meter he. sera de traamuntana kaa mausam baaki ke dweep ke mukaable nam he. yahan par 1507 millimetre ausat barish hoti he. jabaki baaki ke dweep main barish kaa ausat 400 millimetre he. yah apni oonchai ke kaaran thanda rahata he. sera de burguesa is dweep kaa dakshini sira he.
