 rajasthan bharat ganraajya kaa kshetrafal ke aadhaar par sabase bada rajya he. iske pashchim main pakistan, dakshin-pashchim main gujarat, dakshin-purv main madhyapradesh, uttar main punjab, uttar-purv main uttarapradesh or haryana he. rajya kaa kshetrafal 3,42,239 varg kimi he. 2011 kii gananaa ke anusaar rajasthan kii sakshartaa dar 66.11% hai.
 jaipur rajya kii rajdhani he. bhugolic visheshtaon main pashchim main thar marusthal or ghaggar nadi kaa antim chor he. vishwa kii puraatan shreniyon main pramukh aravali shrenee rajasthan kii ek maatr parvat shrenee he, jo ki paryaton kaa kendra he, mount abu or vishwavikhyath dilwara mandir sammilit karti he. purvi rajasthan main do baagh abhayaarany, ranthambhore evam sariska hai or bharatpur ke sameep keoladev rashtriya udyaan he, jo sudur siberia se aane vaale sarason or badi sankhya main sthaneey prajati ke anekanek pakshiyon ke sangrakshit-awaas ke rup main viksit kiya gaya he.
 pracheen samay main rajasthan main kshatriya rajput vansh ke rajaao kaa shashan tha; jiname jalaur,mewar mukhya the. rajput jati ke vibhinn vansho ne is rajya ke vividh bhaagon par apana kabja jamaa liya to un bhaagon kaa namakaran apane-apane vansh, kshetra kii pramukh boli athwa sthaan ke anuroop kar diya.ye rajya the- udaipur, duungarpur, bansawada, pratapgarh, jodhpur, bikaner, kishangarh, sirohi, kota, bundi, jaipur, alvar, karauli, jhalawad or tonk. britishkaal main rajasthan 'raajapootaana' naam se janaa jaataa tha. raja maharana pratap apni asaadhaaran rajyabhakti or shaury ke liye jane jate hai. in rajyon ke naamon ke saath-saath inke kuch bhu-bhaagon ko sthaneey evam bhugolic visheshtaon ke parichaayak naamon se bhi pukaara jaataa rahaa he. par tathy yah he ki rajasthan ke adhikansh tatkaaleen kshetron ke naam vahaan boli jane vaali pramukhatam boliyon par hi rakhe gaye the. udaahranarth dhundhadi-boli ke ilaakon ko dhundhaad kehete hai. 'mewati' boli vaale nikatavarti bhu-bhaag alvar ko 'mevaat', udaipur kshetra main boli jane vaali boli'mewari' ke kaaran udaipur ko mewar, brajabhasha-bahulya kshetra ko 'braj', 'marwari' boli ke kaaran bikaner-jodhpur ilaake ko 'marwar' or 'vagadi' boli par hi duungarpur-banswada adi ko 'vagarh' kaha jaataa rahaa he. duungarpur tathaa udaipur ke dakshini bhaag main pracheen 56 gaanvon ke samooh ko ""chappan"" naam se jaanate hai. maahi nadi ke tateey bhu-bhaag ko 'koyal' tathaa ajmer ke paas vaale kuch pathari bhaag ko 'uparmaal' kii sangya di gai he.
 rajasthan bharat kaa ek mahatvapurn praant he. yah 30 march 1949 ko bharat kaa ek aisa praant bana, jisamen tatkaaleen raajapootaana kii taqatvar riyaasaten villin huin. bharatpur ke jat shaasha ne bhi apni riyasat ke vilay rajasthan main kiya tha. rajasthan shabd kaa arth he: 'raajaaon kaa sthaan' kyonki ye rajput rajaao se rakshit bhoomi thi is kaaran ise rajasthan kaha gaya tha.bharat ke sanvaidhanik-itihaas main rajasthan kaa nirmaan ek mahatvapurn uplabdhi thi. british shaskaon dwaara bharat ko aaazaad karne kii ghoshnaa karne ke baad jab satta-hastantaran kii kaarywahi shuru kii, tabhi lag gaya tha ki aaazaad bharat kaa rajasthan praant bananaa or raajapootaana ke tatkaaleen hisse kaa bharat main vilay ek dubhar kaary sabit ho saktaa he. aazaadee kii ghoshnaa ke saath hi raajapootaana ke deshi riyasaton ke mukhiyaaon main swatantra rajya main bhi apni satta barkaraar rakhane kii hode si much gayi thi, us samay vartmaan rajasthan kii bhaugalik sthiti ke najariye se dekhen to raajapootaana ke is bhubhag main kul baees deshi riyaasaten thi. inamen ek riyasat ajmer-merwada praant ko chhod kar shesh deshi riyasaton par deshi raja maharaajaaon kaa hi raaj tha. ajmer-merwada praant par british shaskaon kaa kabja tha; is kaaran yah to sighe hi swatantra bharat main aa jaati, magar shesh ikkis riyasaton kaa vilay hona yani ekikaran kar 'rajasthan' naamak praant banaaya janaa tha. satta kii hode ke chalate yah bada hi dubhar lag rahaa tha, kyonki in deshi riyasaton ke shaasha apni riyasaton ke swatantra bharat main vilay ko duusari praathamikta ke rup main dekh rahe the. unki maang thi ki ve saalon se khud apane rajyon kaa shashan chalaate aa rahe hai, unhen iskaa dirghakaalin anubhav he, is kaaran unki riyasat ko 'swatantra rajya' kaa darja de diya jaae. kareeb ek dashak kii uhapoh ke bich 18 march 1948 ko shuru hui rajasthan ke ekikaran kii prakriya kul saat charnon main ek november 1956 ko puri hui. isamen bharat sarkaar ke tatkaaleen deshi riyasat or grih mantri sardaar vallabh bhai patel or unke sachiv ve pi menon kii bhoomika atyant mahatvapurn thi. inaki sooyeeboojh se hi rajasthan ke vartmaan swarup kaa nirmaan ho sakaa. rajasthan main kul 21 rashtriya rajmarg guzarate hai.
 
 rajasthan kii akruti lagbhag patangakaar he. rajya 23 3 se 30 12 akshansh or 69 30 se 78 17 deshaantar ke bich sthit he. iske uttar main pakistan, punjab or haryana, dakshin main madhyapradesh or gujarat, purv main uttar pradesh or madhyapradesh evam pashchim main pakistan hai.
 sirohi se alvar kii ore jaati hui 480 kimi lambi aravali parvat shrinkhalaa prakritik drushti se rajya ko do bhaagon main vibhajit karti he. rajasthan kaa purvi sambhaag shuru se hi upjau rahaa he. is bhaag main varsha kaa ausat 50 se.me. se 90 se.me. tak he. rajasthan ke nirmaan ke pashchaat chambal or maahi nadi par bade-bade baandh or vidyut grih bane hai, jinase rajasthan ko sinchai or bijli kii suvidhaayen uplabdh hui he. anya nadiyon par bhi madhyam shrenee ke baandh bane hai, jinase hajaaron hactor sinchai hoti he. is bhaag main tamba, jasta, abhrak, panna, ghiyaa pathar or anya khanij padaarthon ke vishaal bhandar paye jate hai.
 rajya kaa pashchimi bhaag desh ke sabase bade registan " thar" yaa 'tharapaakar' kaa bhaag he. is bhaag main varsha kaa ausat 12 se.me. se 30 se.me. tak he. is bhaag main luni, bandi aadi nadiyaan hai, jo varsha ke kuch dinon ko chhodkar praaia: sukhi rahati hai. desh kii swatantraaa se purv bikaner rajya ganganahar dwaara punjab kii nadiyon se pani praapt karata tha. swatantraaa ke baad rajasthan inds basin se raavi or vyas nadiyon se 52.6 pratishat pani kaa bhagidaar ban gaya. ukt nadiyon kaa pani rajasthan main laane ke liye san 1958 main 'rajasthan nahar' kii vishaal pariyojna shuru kii gai. jodhpur, bikaner, churu evam barmer jilon ke nagar or kai gaanvon ko nahar se vibhinn lift pariyojnaon se pahunchaaye gayi pine kaa pani uplabdh hoga. is prakaar rajasthan ke registan kaa ek bada bhaag antat: shasy shyamala bhoomi main badal jaayegaa. suratgarh jaise kai ilaako main yah najaara dekha ja saktaa he.
 ganga basin kii nadiyon par banaai jane vaali jal-vidyut yojanaon main bhi rajasthan bhi bhagidaar he. ise is samay bhakhra-nangal or anya yojanaon ke krishi evam audyogic vikaas main bharpoor sahaayta milti he. rajasthan nahar pariyojna ke alaava is bhaag main jawai nadi par nirmit ek baandh he, jisase na keval vistrut kshetra main sinchai hoti he, varan jodhpur nagar ko peyajal bhi praapt hota he. yah sambhaag abhi tak audyogic drushti se pichhadaa hua he. par ummid he, is kshetra main jyo-jyon bijli or pani kii suvidhaayen badhati jaayengi audyogic vikaas bhi gati pakad legaa. is baag main lignite, phullerperth, tungston, bantonite, jipsom, sangemarmar aadi khanij prachur maatra main paye jate hai. barmer kshetra main silisius arth or kaccha tel ke bhandar prachur maatra main hai. haal hi kii khudaai se pataa chalaa he ki is kshetra main ucch kism kii prakritik gas bhi prachur maatra main uplabdh he. ab vah din dur nahin, jabaki rajasthan kaa yah bhaag bhi samridhishalee ban jayega.
 rajya kaa kshetrafal 3.42 lakh varg ki.me he jo bharat ke kul kshetrafal kaa 10.40 pratishat he. yah bharat kaa sabase bada rajya he. varsh 1996-97 main rajya main gaanvon kii sankhya 37889 or nagaron tathaa kasbon kii sankhya 222 thi. rajya main 33 jila parishaden, 235 panchayat samitiyaan or 9125 gram panchayatein hai. nagar nigam 4 or sabhi shrenee kii nagarpalikaayen 180 hai.
 san 1991 kii janaganana ke anusaar rajya kii jansankhya 4.39 karod thi. jansankhya ghanatv prati varg kimi 126 he. isamen purushon kii sankhya 2.30 karod or mahilaon kii sankhya 2.09 karod thi. rajya main dashak vruddhi dar 28.44 pratishat thi, jabaki bharat main yah ausat dar 23.56 pratishat thi. rajya main sakshartaa 38.81 pratishat thi. jabaki bharat kii sakshartaa to keval 20.8 pratishat thi jo desh ke anya rajyon main sabase kam thi. rajya main anusuchit jati evam anusuchit janajati rajya kii kul jansankhya kaa kramash: 17.29 pratishat or 12.44 pratishat he.
 rajasthan kii jalwaayu shushk se up-ardra monsooni jalwaayu he. aravali ke pashchim main nune varsha, ucch dainik evam vaarshik tapaantar, nimna ardrata tathaa teevr hawaaon yukt shushk jalwaayu he. duusari ore aravali ke purv main ardhashushk evam up-ardra jalwaayu he. akshanshiy sthiti, samudr se duuri, samudr taal se se oonchai, aravali parvat shreniyon kii sthiti evam disha, vanaspati aavaran aadi sabhi yahan kii jalwaayu ko prabhavit karte hai.
