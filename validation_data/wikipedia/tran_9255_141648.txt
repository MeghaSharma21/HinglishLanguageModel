 bairam khan abdul rahim khanekhana ke pita jane-maane yoddha the. vah akbar ke sangrakshak the va turkistan se aae the. unheen ke sangrakshan main akbar bade hue lekin darbar ke kuch logon ne akbar ko bairam khan ke khilaaf bhadkaa diya or unhen sangrakshak pad se hataa diya gaya, vah jab haj ke liye ja rahe the to raste main unki hatya kar di gai. us samay abdul rahim 5 saal ke the. unhen akbar ne apane paas rakh liya. 
 bairam khan teraa varshiya akbar ke atali tathaa abhibhavak the. bairam khan khan-e-khaanaa kii oopaadhi se sammanit the. ve humayun ke sadhu or antarang mitra the. rahim kii maan vartmaan haryana praant ke mewati rajput jamaal khan kii sundar evam gunavati kanya sultanaa begum thi. jab rahim paanch varsh ke hi the, tab gujarat ke patan nagar main san 1561 main inke pita bairam khan kii hatya kar di gai. rahim kaa paalan-poshan akbar ne apane dharm-putr kii tarah kiya.
 madhya kaaleen yudhon ke arabi itihaas granth ke pratham adhyay main bairam khan dwaara banavaai gai `kalla minar' kaa ullekh he. is sthaan kaa naam sar manzil rakhaa gaya tha. sikandar shah suri ke saath ladane main jitne sir kate the yaa sainik mare the, unhen bator kar unhen eent, pattharen kii jagah kaam main laayaa gaya or yah oonchi minar khadi kii gai thi. mughal baadshahon ne or bhi kitni hi aisi kalla minaaren yuddh vijay ke darp-pradarshan ke liye banavaai thim. kalla, farsi main sir ko kehete hai.
 bairam khaan humayun kaa sahyogi tathaa uske naaballig putr akbar kaa vali athwa sangrakshak tha. vah badshaah humayun kaa param mitra tathaa sahyogi bhi tha. apane samast jivan main bairam khaan ne mogul samrajya kii bahut seva kii thi. humayun ko uskaa rajya phir se hasil karne tathaa kitne hi yudhon main use vijit karaane main bairam khaan kaa bahut bada haath tha. akbar ko bhi bharat kaa samraat banane ke liye bairam khaan ne asankhya yuddh kiye or hemu jaise shaktishaalee raja ko haraakar bharat main mogul samrajya kii sthapana main mahatvapurn yogadan diya. mogul samrajya main akbar kii doodhmata maham anaga hi thi, jo bairam khaan ke viruddh saazish karti rahati thi. ye inhen sazishon kaa nateeja tha ki bairam ko haj ke liye aadesh diya gaya, jahaan 1561 i. main uski hatya kar di gai.
 vansh parichayberam khaan kaa sambandh turan kii turkman jati se tha. hyderabad ke nizaam bhi turkman the. itihaaskaar kasim farishta ke anusaar vah iran ke karakuilu turkmaanon ke baharlu shakha se sambaddh tha. alishkar beg turkman taimur ke prasiddh saradaaron main se ek tha, jise hamadaan, dinwar, khujistan aadi par shaasha niyukt kiya gaya tha. alishkar kii santaanon main shephali beg hua. temuri shah husain bikera ke baad jab turan main sultanate barabaad hui, to shephali kabul kii taraf bhagya pariksha karne ke liye aayaa. uskaa betaa yarali or pota sammli afghanistan chale aaye. yarali ko babar ne gazani kaa haakim niyukt kiya. thode hi dinon ke baad uske marane par bete sammli ko vahiin darja mila. vah bhi jaldi hi mar gaya. alp vayask bairam apane gharavaalon ke saath balkh chalaa gaya.
 humayun se mitrataabalkh main vah kuch dinon tak padhata-likhata rahaa. phir vah samavayask shahzada humayun kaa naukar or baad main uskaa mitra ho gaya. bairam khaan ko sahitya or sangeet se bhi bahut prem tha. vah jaldi hi apane swami kaa atyant priy ho gaya tha. 16 varsh kii aayu main hi ek ladaai main bairam khaan ne bahut veerta dikhaai, uski khyaati babar tak pahunch gai, tab babar ne khud usase kaha : shahzada ke saath darbar main hajir karo. babar ke marane ke baad vah humayun badshaah kii chaaya ke taur par rahane laga. humayun ne champaner ke qile par gheraa daalaa. kisi tarah se daal galati na dekhakar chalis mogul bahadur sidhiyon ke saath qile main utar gaye, jinamein bairam khaan bhi tha. qila fatah kar liya gaya. shershah se chausa main ladate vaqt bairam khaan bhi saath hi tha. kannauj main bhi vah ladaa. in sabhi ghatnaon ne humayun or bairam khaan ko ek atoot mitrataa main band diya tha.
 jivan daanakannauj kii ladaai main paraajay ke baad mogul sena main jisaki sing jidhar samaai, vah udhar bhaaga. bairam khaan apane puraane dost sambhal ke miyaan abdul wahaab ke paas pahunchaa. phir lakhanu ke raja mitrasen ke paas jangalon main din guzaartaa rahaa. shershahi haakim naseer khaan ko pataa laga. usne bairam khaan ko pakad mangavaya. naseer khaan chahta tha ki bairam khaan ko katl kar den, par doston kii koshish se bairam khaan kisi prakaar se batch gaya. ant main use shershah ke saamne hajir hona padaa, jisane ek maamuli mogul sardaar ko mahatv na dekar use maaf kar diya or jivan daan de diya.
 qandahar ke haakim kaa padberam khaan phir se gujarat ke sultan mahmud ke paas gaya, par use apane swami se milane kii dhun thi. jab hijri 950 main humayun iran se lautakar kabul lete sindh kii ore badha, to bairam khaan apane aadamiyon ke saath humayun kii ore se ladane laga. humayun ko isaki khabar lagi to uski khushi kaa thikaana nahin rahaa. hindustan main safalataa milane vaali nahin thi, isliye humayun ne iran kaa rasta kiya. bairam khaan bhi uske saath tha. shahi kafile main kul milakar sattar aadmi se zyaada nahin the. iran se lautakar humayun ne qandahar ko gheraa. usne chaha, bhai kamran ko samajha-bujhaakar koon-karaaba roka jaae. use samajhane ke liye humayun ne bairam khaan ko kabul bheja, lekin vah kahan hone vaala tha. qandahar par adhikaar karke bairam khaan ko vahaan kaa haakim niyukt kiya gaya. qandahar vijay ke bare main humayun ne swayan kaha-“roj nauroj bairam’st imroj”.dile ahabaab begum’st imroj. se parajeet hokar mogul amir niraash ho chuke the. vah kabul laut janaa chaahate the, par bairam khaan ne unhen roak diya.
 saazikabar kii doodhmata maham anaga nity hi bairam khaan ke kilaaf saazishen rachati rahati thi. mogul darbar main maham anaga kaa ek samooh tha, jo hameshaa hi bairam ko nicha dikhaane main laga rahata tha. hijri 961 main in logon ne chugali lagaai ki bairam khaan swatantra hona chahta he, lekin bairam khaan namak-haraam nahin tha. humayun ek din jab swayan qandahar pahunchaa tha, tab bairam khaan ne bahut chaha ki badshaah use apane saath le chale, lekin qandahar bhi ek bahut mahatvapurn sthaan tha, jiske liye bairam khaan se badhkar acha shaasha nahin mil sak
