 sanskrit sahitya main bharat ke natya shaastra main adhikanshat: naatakeey paatro ke vargeekaran prastut hue hai or vatsyayan ke kamasutra main etadvishyak bhed prabhed kiye gaye hai jinka sambandh praaia: stri-purush ke yon vyapaaron se he. "agnipurana" main pratham baar nayak-nayika kaa vivechan shringaar ras ke alamban vibhaavon ke rup main kiya gaya he. 
 sanskrit or hindi ke parvarti lekhakon ne "agnipurana" kaa sthiti sweekaar karte hue shringaarras kii simaaon main hi is vishay kaa vistaar kiya he. in simaaon kaa, jinka atikaraman keval apwaad ke rup main kiya gaya he, is prakaar samajha ja saktaa he: 
 bharat ne nayak ke chaar bhed kiye hai : dhiralalit, dhiraprashanth, dhirodatt, dhiroddhat. ye bhed naatak ke nayak ke hai. "agnipurana" main inke atirikt chaar or bhedon kaa ullekh he : anukool, darkshin, shath, dhristh. ye bhed spasht hi shringaar ras ke alamban vibhaav ke hai. bhoj ne "saraswatikanthabharan" tathaa "shringarprakash" main in do ke atirikt anya anek vargikarnon kaa ullekh kiya he. kintu unamem se keval ek vargeekaran hi, jiskaa ullekh purush ke bhedon ke rup main bharat ne bhi kiya tha, parvarti lekhakon ko maanya hua : uttam, madhyam, adham. bhanudutt ne "rasmanjri" main ek nayaa vargeekaran diya, jise aage chalakar pradhan vargeekaran mana gaya. yah he : pati, upapati veshik. anukool ityadi bhed pati or upapati ke antargat sweekaar kiye gaye. bhanudutt ne proshit naam ke ek or bhed kaa ullekh kiya. rup goswami ne "ujjvalaneelamani" main vaishik sweekaar nahin kiya. unhone krishna ko ekmaatr nayak mana he.
 hindi main nayakabhed ke pramukh lekhakon ne uparyukt vargikarnon main se pratham ko chhodkar shesh ko praaia: sweekaar kar liya he. pati, upapati, vaishik ko mukhya vargeekaran maanakar anukool, dakshin, shath, ghrishth bhedon ko pati ke antargat rakhaa he .
 nayak ke uttam, madhyam, adham bhedon ko hindi main keval kuch lekhakon ne hi sweekaar kiya he, jinamein sundar, top or rasline pramukh hai.
 nayak ke kuch anya bhed is prakaar hai : proshit, maani, chatur, anbhigya. maani ke do bhed hai : rupamani, gunmani. chatur ke bhed bhi do hai: vachan chatur, kriya-chatur. rasline ne inhen ke saath swayandoot nayak kaa bhi kathan kiya he. anbhigya ko bhanudutt ke anukaran par padmakar ne bhi nayakabhas mana he. keshav ne nayak ke prachhnn or prakaash bhed bhi maane hai. rasline ke mat se upapati ke teen tathaa vaishik ke do upbhed hai.
 hindi ke nayak-nayika-bhed sambandhi sahitya kaa nirmaan adhikanshat: reetikaal main hua he. nayak-nayika-bhed kii yah kavyasarita do shashakt dhaaraon ke sangam kaa parinaam he. inamen se pehali dhara he sahityashastra evam nayak-nayika-bhed sambandhi shastreey granthon kii, jiskaa aarambh bharat ke "natshastra" se hota he; tathaa duusari dhara he krishna or gopiyon kii shringaar kridaaon ke varnan kii, jo "harivansh", "padam", "vishnu" "bhagwat" tathaa "brahmavaivatan" puraanon kii upatyakaon main bahati hui or umapatidhar, jayadev, chandidas, vidyapati, mira, narsingh mehta tathaa surdaas aadi anek bhakt kaviyon kii madhur vaani se vilsit hoti hui, nimbark, vallabh tathaa chaitanya jaise mahaan aacharyon ke samarthan se sampusht hui he. acharyatv kii drushti se kavyashastra ke is ang kii hindi lekhakon kii dein asaadhaaran he. kavyasaushthav kii drushti se bhi vidwaanon ke matanusaar itane unche str ke sahitya kaa itane bade pariman main nirmaan hindi sahitya ke or kisi kaal main nahin hua.
 bharat ke anusaar nayika ke aath bhed hai : vaasakajja, virahotkanthita, swadhinapatika, kalhantarita, khandita, vipralabdha, proshitabhartrika, abhisarika. ise ham paristhitibhed kahenge. parvarti lekhakon ke anusaar, jise "prakruti-bhed" kaha gaya he, nayika teen prakaar kii hoti he: uttama, madhyamaa, adhama. "agnipurana" ke lekhak ne nayika ke keval ek vargeekaran kaa ullekh kiya he : swakiya, parvikaya, punarbhu, samanya. in chaar bhedon main se punarbhu ko aage chalakar manyata praapt nahin hui. rudrat tathaa rudrabhatt ne ek shodash bhed vargeekaran prastut kiya, jise parvarti lekhakon dwaara sarvaadhik pradhanta di gai. yah vargeekaran is prakaar he : 
 bhoj ne nayak kii bhanti nayika ke bhi kuch moulik vargeekaran kiye hai, kintu unhen parvarti lekhakon ne sweekaar nahin kiya. 
 bhanudutt ne uparyukt teenon vargikarnon ke atirikt ek nae vargeekaran kaa ullekh kiya he, jise aage chalakar hindi ke adhikansh lekhakon ne sweekaar kiya. vah he, : nayika : anyasambhogadu:khitaa, vakroktigarvita, maanavati. vakroktigarvita : premagarvita, saundaryagarvita. iske atirikt bhanudutt ne shodash bhed vargeekaran main mugdha evam oodhaa ke kuch naveen upabhedon kaa ullekh kiya he, jo is prakaar hai, mugdha : gyaatyauvana, agyatayauvana. mugdha pun: navodha, vishrabdhanvodha. oodhaa : gupta, vidagdha, lakshitha, kulata, anushayna,, mudita. bharat ke paristhitibhed vargeekaran main bhi bhanudutt ne proshyatpatika naam kaa ek nayaa bhed joda he, jo baad main pravatsyatpatika naam se prasiddh hua. abhisarika ke antargat unhone jyotsnabhisarika, tamistrabhisarika tathaa divsabhisarika kaa ullekh kiya he. hindi ke lekhakon ne bhanudutt kaa hi sarvaadhik anusaran kiya he. shing bhoopal evam vishwanath ne samanya ke do upabhedon kaa ullekh kiya he : raktaa, virakta. vishwanath ne mugdha ke paanch, madhya ke paanch, tathaa pragalbha ke chah prabhedon kaa ullekh kiya he, kintu nayika kii sankhyaganana main in prabhedon ko sanmilit nahin kiya. goswami ne keval "harivallabhaon" ko nayika maanate hue samanya ko sweekaar nahin kiya. unhone mugdha, madhya, pragalbha bhed parkiya ke antargat bhi maane hai. rup goswami dwaara ullikhit mugdha ke chha:, madhya ke chaar tathaa pragalbha ke saat prabhed vishwanath krit prabhedon se milte julate hai.
 hindi main nayikabhed sambadhi granthon kii sankhya do sau se bhi adhik he, kintu inamen se adhikansh aprakashit hai. sarvaadhik lokapriy granthon main matiram kaa "rasraj" tathaa padmakar kaa "jagadvinod" agrani hai. in granthon main praaia: bhanudutt kii "rasmanjri" kaa hi anusaran kiya gaya he. paristhiti-bhed-vargeekaran main aagatapatika naam kaa ek dasavaan bhed joda gaya he, jise hindi ke adhikansh lekhakon na aarambh se hi sweekaar kiya he. rahim kaa "barbai-nayika-bhed" bhi barbai chand kii madhurata ke kaaran bada prasiddh hua. anya praarambhik granthon main "hit-tarangini", "sahityalahari", "rasmanjri", "rasikapriya", "sundarshringaar", "sudhanidhi", "kavikulakalpataru" tathaa "bhashaabhushan" ke naam vishesh rup se ullekhneey hai. parvarti lekhakon main apni moulik dein ke kaaran kumaarmani shastri, dev, dev, rasline tathaa bhikharidas adhik mahatvapurn hai. nayikabhed ke kshetra main aadhunik kaal main hariudh, biharilal bhatta tathaa prabhudayal mital ne ullekhneey kaary kiya he.
 kriparam kii "hittarangini" yadhyapi hindi main nayikabhed kaa pratham uplabdh granth he, phir bhi isamen vishay kaa vivechan bade vistaar se kiya gaya he. is granth kii kuch moulik udbhaavnaayen is prakaar hai-
 surdaas kii "sahityalahari" kuta style kii rachana he. iskaa prayek pad praaia: kisi ek alankaar tathaa kisi ek nayika kaa udhaaharan prastut karata he. is granth main samanya ko manyata nahin di gai. nandadaas kii "rasmanjri" kii apni visheshtaayen hai. isamen samanya kaa kathan to hua he, kintu use koi mahatv pradaan nahin kiya gaya. isamen nayikaon kii paribhaashaayen itani vistrut hai ki vanrya nayika kaa chitra sa prastut ho jaataa he or udaahranon kaa abhaav nahin khatkata. keshavadas ne bhi apni "rasikapriya" main samanya kaa ullekh maatr kiya he, uski paribhaasha tak nahin di. unhone vishwanath or rup goswami ke anukaran par mugdha, madhya tathaa pragalbha ke prabhed kiye he. abhisarika ke antargat premabhisarika, garvaabhisarika tathaa kamabhisarika kaa ullekh kiya gaya he, tathaa paristhiti bhedon main se prayek ke prakaash evam prachhann naam ke prabhed kiye gaye hai. 
 coccoke ke kaamshaastra sambandhi granth "ratirahasy" ke aadhaar par nayika ke padmini, chitrini, shankhini tathaa hastini bhedon kaa bhi ullekh hua he. in bhedon ko sundar tathaa kuch anya lekhakon ne bhi duharaaya he. "sundarashringaar" ke anya vargeekaran praaia: "rasraj" ke samaan hai. tosh ne "sudhanidhi" main anek moulik vargikarnon kaa ullekh kiya he - 
 chintamani ne "kavikulakalpataru" main mugdha, tathaa pragalbha ke prabhed kiye hai. jaswantsingh kaa "bhashaabhushan" apane laghav ke kaaran vidhyaarthiyon kaa kanthhaar rahaa he. isamen vibhinn nayikaon kii keval paribhaashaayen hi di gai hai. alankarigranth ke rup main iskaa vishesh maan he.
 kumaarmani shastri ne "rasikarsaal" main keval naveen bhedprabhedon kaa hi ullekh nahin kiya, puraane bhedon main nae sambandh bhi sthapit kiye hai. unki pramukh moulik sthapanaayen is prakaar hai - 
 "rasvilas" main unhone pad, jati tathaa prantiyata ke aadhaar par nayikaon kaa varnan kiya he, jo chitropamata evam sookshmdarshita kii drushtiyon se adwitiya he. 
 "sukhsagartarang" kaa ansh bhed prasiddh he : 
 gandharvi ke antargat, arthath saadhe das se saadhe choubees varsh tak kii avasthaa ke bich main, mugdha, madhya tathaa pragalbha ke 13 prabhedon main se prayek kii ayusima nirdhaarit kii gai he. 
 nayikabhed kaa sarvaadhik vistaar rasline ke "rasaprabodh" main uplabdh hota he. unki pramukh moulik uplabdhiyaan is prakaar hai - 
 bhikharidas ne "shringarnirnay" main apane tosh tathaa rasline kii anek manyataon ko duharaaya he. anyasambhogadu:khitaa kaa vipralabdha ke antargat rakhkar unhone apni moulik sujh kaa parichay bhi diya he.
 aadhunik kaal ke lekhakon main "hariudh" ne apni moulikta uttama tathaa madhyamaa ke upabhedon main dikhaai he. unke "rasakalash" main in bhedon kaa pratham baar vibhaajan kiya gaya he, jo is prakaar he - 
 biharilal bhatta ne paristhitibhedon ke sambandh main kumaarmani shastri kii manyataayen praaia: sweekaar kar lee hai. unke "sahityasagar" main dhira, adhira tathaa dhiradhira bhedon kaa sambandh keval kanishtha se mana gaya he. prabhudayal mital ne nayikabhed vishay kaa purna vivechan karne ke pashchaat bhi bhanudutt, matiram, padmakar aadi dwaara sweekrut vargeekaran ko hi manyata pradaan kii he.
