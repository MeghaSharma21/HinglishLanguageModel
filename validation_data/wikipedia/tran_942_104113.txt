 unnisvin sadi main or beesvin sadi ke shuruaati varshon ke bengal main hue samaaj sudhaar andolanon, deshbhakt-rashtravaadi chetana ke utthan or sahitya-kala-samskruti main hui anuthi pragati ke daur ko bengal ke navajagaran kii sangya di jaati he. is dauraan samaaj sudhaarkon, sahityakaron or kalakaaron ne stri, vivaah, dahej, jatipratha or dharm sambandhi sthapit paramparaon ko chunauti di. bengal ke is ghatnakram ne samgr bhartiya aadhuniktaa kii nirmitiyon par amit chhap chodi. isi navajagaran ke dauraan bhartiya rashtrawaad ke shuruaati roopon kii sanrachanaayen saamne aayin. bengal ke navajagaran kaa vistaar raja rammohan raay se armbh hokar rabindranath thakur tak mana jaataa he. 
 is avadhi kaa suvyavyasthit adhyayan karne vaale itihaaskaar sushobhan sarkaar likhte hai, ‘angrezi raaj, poonjividi arthvyavastha or aadhunik pashchimi samskruti kaa sabase pehla prabhaav bengal par padaa jisase ek aisa navajagaran hua jise aamtaur par bengal ke rinesaan ke naam se janaa jaataa he. kareeb ek sadi tak badalati hui aadhunik duniya ke prati bengal kii sachet jaagrukta shesh bharat ke mukaable aage rahi. is lihaaz se kaha ja saktaa he ki bharat ke aadhunik jaagran main bengal dwaara nibhaai gayi bhoomika kii tulana europee rinesaan ke sandarbh main italy kii bhoomika se kii ja sakati he.’ italvi rinesaan kii hi tarah bengal kaa navajagaran koi janandolan nahin tha. isaki prakriya or prasar bhadralok tak hi simit tha. bhadralok main bhi navajagaran kaa prabhaav adhiktar uske hindu hisse par hi padaa. is dauraan kuch musalmaan hastiyaan bhi ubharin, par bengal kaa navajagaran aanshik rup se hi musalmaan navajagaran kaha ja saktaa he. mote taur par mana jaataa he ki bengal ke navajagaran kii shuruyaat rammohan raay se hui or rabindranath thakur ke saath uskaa samaapan ho gaya. sushobhan sarkaar ne ise paanch charnon main baant kar dekhne kaa prayaas kiya he : pehali avadhi 1814 se 1833, jisaki kendriya hasti raja rammohan raay the. 1814 main ve kolkata rahane ke liye aaye or 1833 main unka landan main dehanth hua. duusari avadhi unki mrityu se 1857 ke vidroh tak jaati he. teesri avadhi 1885 main bhartiya rashtriya congress kii sthapana tak faili hui he. chauthi avadhi 1905 main bengal ke vibhaajan tak or paanchavin avadhi swadeshi andolan se asahyog andolan or mahatma gandhi ke netritv kii shuruyaat tak yani 1919 tak maani gayi he. rammohan raay ke bare main ek drushtaant he jise unki shakhsiyat ke roopak kii tarah padhaa ja saktaa he. kaha jaataa he ki unke do ghar the. khan-paan, veshbhusha or rahan-sahan ke lihaaz se ek main kisi victorian jentilmen kii bhanti rahete the or duusare main parmparik bengali brahman kii tarah. darasal, unke vyaktitv or krititv kii sabase badi visheshta thi purv or pashchim kaa sanshleshan. lekin, yah drushtaant bhi unke roodhibhanjak vyaktitv ko paribhaashit nahin karata. 1820 main unki kriti ‘parasepts of jisas’ kaa prakaashan hua jisamen isa ke naitik sandesh ko isai tatvagyan or chamatkaar kathaon se alag karke pesh kiya gaya tha. bengal main sakriy isai padri varg unki is koshish se naaraaz ho gaya. uske virodh ke parinamswaroop rammohan raay ne isai janata ke naam teen apilen jari karke apane vichaaron ko or spashtataa pradaan kii. isi ke saath unhone brahmanical megazin kaa prakaashan karke hindu aastikta ke vaicharik nairantary ko vedon ke hawaale se rekhaankit karne kii cheshtaa bhi kii. rammohan ne apane dharmik vichaaron ko dharti par utaarne ke liye 20 agast 1828 ko ‘brahm sabha’ kaa gathan kiya. is prakriya kaa parinaam 1830 main ek carch kii sthapana ke rup main niklaa or brahm samaaj andolan shuru hua jo lambe samay tak bengali samaaj kii chetana ko prabhavit karata rahaa. 1818 se 1829 ke bich rammohan raay ne sati pratha jaisi buraai par akraman karte hue teen rachanaayen prakashit kin. sati pratha ke kilaaf janamat banane or angrezon ko us par pratibandh lagane ke liye manaane kaa shrey unke prayaason ko hi diya jaataa he. rammohan ne aadhunik shiksha or vishesh taur se stri shiksha ke prasar main agua bhoomika nibhaai. unhen bangla gadhy kaa nirmaataa bhi kaha jaataa he. 
 bengal rinesaan ke is daur main rammohan ke prayaason ko dohare virodh kaa saamana karnaa padaa. ek taraf unka virodh radhakant deb, gaurikant bhattacharya or bhavani charan banerjee jaise sudhaar virdohi paramparanishth lekhak or vidwaan kar rahe the, vahiin unhen francisi kranti or inglish radicalizam se anupranit aalochana kaa bhi saamana karnaa padaa jiskaa netritv ‘yang bengal’ or uske anglo-indine netaa henry vivian derozio ke haathon main tha. yah alag baat he ki derozio ke anuyaayi apane kaamon or vaktrutaaon se samaaj ko chaunkaane ke alaava koi sthai asar nahin daal paye. lekin, rammohan kii parampara ke aadhaar par samaaj sudhaaron kaa naram kaarykram unki mrityu ke baad bhi jari rahaa. uski kaman devendranath thakur or akshay kumaar dutt ke haathon main thi. isi dauraan ishwarchandra vidyasagar ne bal vivaah or bahuvivaah ke kilaaf muhim chalaayi. haalaanki yang bengal andolan 1842 main vidhwaa vivaah ke paksh main awaz uthaa chukaa tha, par vidyasagar ke prayaason se hi ise lokapriy andolan kaa rup mila. 
 1857 ke vidroh ke baad bengal ke punarjagran ne saahitiyik kranti or uske zariye rashtravaadi chintan ke yug main pravesh kiya. nil kii kheti karwaane vaale angrezon ke julmon ke kilaaf kisaanon ke sangharsh main bengali buddhijiviyon ne bhi apni awaz milaayi. deenabandhu mitra ke naatak ‘nil darpan’ ne bengal ke maanas ko jhakzor diya. micel madhusudan dutt ne uskaa angrezi anuwaad prakashit kiya jiske kilaaf adaalat ne ek hazaar rupee ke zurmaane kii sazaa sunaayi. micel ne 1860 main meghnad-vadh kii rachana karke nayi bengali kavita kii sambhaawanaaon kaa udghosh kiya. 1865 main bankim chandra chatarji kaa surya unke pehle aitihaasik upanyaas durgeshnandini ke saath bengali saanskrithik kshitij par uga. 1873 main vishabruksh ke zariye unhone saamaajik upanyaason kii parampara kaa sutarapaat kiya. 1879 main ‘samya’ sheershak se niabandh likh kar bankim ne samtamulakata or utopian samaajwaad ke prati rujhaan dikhaaya, par 1882 main aanandamath likh kar ve deshbhaktipurna punarutthanavaad kii lahar main bah gayi. isi rachana main darj ‘vandemataram’ geet bharat ke teen raashtragiton main se ek he. saahitiyik unmesh ke saath- saath bengali navajagaran ne itihaas lekhan or samaaj-vigyaan ke anya kshetron main yogadan kii parampara kaa sutarapaat bhi kiya. dharmik sudhaaron or punarutthanavaad ke lihaaz se bhi yah daur khaasaa ghatnaprad tha. ek taraf to keshav chandra sen ke netritv main yuva brahm samaji sudhaar andolan kii alakh jagaaye hue the, duusari taraf wahabi asantosh khadbada rahaa tha or nav- hinduvad kii padchaap sunaayi de rahi thi. isi avadhi main dakshineshwar ke sant ramakrishna paramhans ne apane chumbakeey vyaktitv ke madhyam se sabhi dharmik aasthaaon ko pavitra ghoshit kiya. isai dharmantaran ko hawa dene vaala protestant ugravad isse kamazor hua or parampara ke pairokaron ko thodi raahat mili. paramhans ke yuva shishy swami vivekanand ne 1893 main world relijas conference main diye gayi apane bhaashan se antarrashtriya khyaati arjeet kii or apni khaas style ke adarshavaadi rashtrawaad kaa sutrikaran kiya. chaar saal tak pashchim kaa daura karke laute to 1897 main unka rashtriya nayak kii tarah swaagat kiya gaya. 
 1861 ke baad kii avadhi to kuch is tarah kii thi jisamen bengali buddhijivi samaaj kaa sabase priy shabd ‘rashtriya’ ban gaya. rajanarayan bose, navagopal mitra, jyotirindranath thakur or bhoodev mukherjee kii aguai main kai tarah kii gatividhiyaan chalin jinamein hindu meley kaa aayojan ullekhneey tha jo kareeb das saal tak kolkata ko aalodit karata rahaa. 1870 ke baad kii avadhi raajnneetik andolanon kii avadhi thi jisamen surendranath banerjee kaa netritv ubhara jinhen ‘bengal ke betaj ke badshaah’ ke taur par janaa gaya. surendranath ke jujharupan kaaran ke kaaran angrez unka naam bigaad kar ‘sarender note’ banerjee bhi kehete the. 1876 main banerjee ne ‘indine association’ kii sthapana kii jiske 1883 ke rashtriya sammelan main sabhi bharatiyon ke liye ek rashtriya sanghathan banane kaa vichaar paidaa hua. iskaa parinaam 1885 main bhartiya rashtriya congress kii aitihaasik sthapana ke rup main niklaa. bengali buddhijiviyon congress kii gatividhiyon main jam kar bhagidaari kii or 1903 tak congress adhiveshnon kii adhyakshata saat baar unke khate main gayi. bengal ne maharashtra or punjab ke saath mil kar congress ke garmdali dhade kii rachana karne main nirnaayak bhoomika nibhaai. 
 bengal ke navjaaran ke aakiri daur par girish chandra ghosh jaise naatakkaar, bankim kii parampara main ramesh chandra dutt jaise aitihaasik or saamaajik upanyaaskaar, mir musharraf hossain jaise musalmaan kavi, jagadish chandra bose or prafull chandra raay jaise vaigyanikon kii chhap rahi. lekin, rabindranath thakur kii pratibha ke saamne ye sabhi pratibhaaen feeki pad gayin. hindu meley main apni deshbhaktipurna kavitaaon ke paath se sabhi kaa dhyaan apni ore aakarshith karne vaale rabindranath ne apane rachanaatmak or vaicharik sahitya se ek puure yug ko nayi asmita pradaan kii. unhone apane yug par aalochanatmak drushti phenki. 1901 main apni rachana nashtnid ke zariye ve bengali rinesaan ke aise pairokaron ko aade haathon lete nazar aaye jo apane paariwarik jivan main un aadarshon ko lagu karne ke liye taiyaar nahin the. 1913 main gitanjali ke liye sahitya kaa nobel puraskaar jeetne vaale rabindranath ne bang- bhang virodh or swadeshi andolan kii raajnneeti ko sarvatha naye drushtikon se dekha. unhone is andolan main musalamaanon kii na ke baraabar bhagidaari par afsos jataayaa or rashtrawaad main nihit ashubh ayaamon kii aalochana prastut kii. gandhi ke saath unki bahason main aane vaale bharat kii duvidhaon kii jhalak dekhi ja sakati thi.
