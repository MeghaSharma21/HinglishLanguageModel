 kuleentavaad raajnneeti vigyaan kii vah vichaardhaara he jisamen kuleen tantra kii shreshthata ko sarvocch praathamikta pradaan kii jaati he. plato evam arestu, ko kuleentavaad kaa unnayak vichaarak mana jaataa he. is siddhant ke antargat vansh, kul tathaa prajati ko abhijatya kaa pratimaan mana jaataa he. saamanytah ek alpsankhyak visheshadhikrut varg ko hi shashan satta ke parichaalan ke yogya mana jaataa he.
