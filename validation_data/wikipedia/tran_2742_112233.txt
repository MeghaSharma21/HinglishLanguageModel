 doonhuang pashchimi china ke gansu praant main ek shahar he jo aitihaasik rup se resham maarg par ek mahatvapurn padaav hua karata tha. isaki aabaadi san 2000 main 1,87,578 anumaanit kii gai thi. ret ke tilon se ghire is registani ilaake main doonhuang ek nachlistaan he, jisamen 'navachandra jhil' pani kaa ek ahm strot he. prachinkaal main ise shasou, yani 'ret kaa shahar', ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa tha. yahan paas main mogaao guphaayen bhi sthit hai jahaan buddha dharm se sambandhi 492 mandir hai or jinamein is kshetra kii pracheen samskruti par bharat kaa gaharaa prabhaav dikhataa he.
 doonhuang dakshini resham maarg par sthit he jo bharat or tibet kii rajdhani lhasa se hote hue mongolia or dakshini siberia jaataa tha. yah heshi galiyare ke mukh par bhi sthit he jiske zariye madhya asia or china ke bich vyaper or sainik gatividhiyaan chalati thi. idhar se sadak purv kii ore sidhaa changan or luoyang kii pracheen chini rajdhaniyon kii taraf jaati thi. iske ird-gird ke retile pahaado se ret ud-udakar ek gane jaisi awaz karti he. isliye inhen 'gaati ret ke pahaad' kaha jaataa he, jo ki chini bhashaa main 'mingasha shaan' he.
 doonhuang kshetra main manushyon ke vaas ke 2000 saal puraane nishaan mili hai. mahaan itihaaskaar ke abhilekh naamak chini itihaas granth ke anusaar yah ueshi logon ke mul nivas sthaan kaa hissaa tha. teesri sadi isapurv tak yahan shiyongnu log apni dhak jamaa chuke the. 121 ipu main china ke haan rajvansh ke samraat vu ne par niyantran paane ke liye shiyongnuon ko haraa diya or yahan ek chhavnee ke rup se doonhuang shahar sthapit karavaaya. yahan mitti kii minaare banavaai gai jahaan se shiyongnu hamalon ke liye chaukas rahane ke liye paharaa lagaaya jaataa tha. hamalon main inpar mashaale jalaakar sanket die jate the or 'doon huang' kaa arth chini main 'jalati mashaalen' he.
 yahan par madhya asia or bharat se aate-jate karwanon main vyapaaree or buddha prachaarak hote the or doonhuang ke paas sab se pehali buddha guphaayen san 353 main taraashi gayi. is shahar ke china or madhya asia ke bich ke sarahadi ilaake main hone se isapar kheenchaatani chalati rahati thi or aksar isapar gair-chiniyon kaa qabza ho jaataa tha. haan rajvansh ke patan ke baad phir se doonhuang par shiyongnu niyantran ban gaya. baad main turkiyon or tibetiyon kaa bhi isapar qabza hua. 1037 isvi main isapar uiguron kaa or 1068 main pashchimi shiya samrajya sthapit karne vaale tangut logon kaa qabza hua. 1227 main isapar mongol qabza ho gaya jinhonen is shahar ko jala daalaa lekin kubalai khan ke puure china ko fateh karne ke baad iskaa punarnirmaan hua. china ke ming rajvansh kaal tak china kaa baaqi duniya se samudri vyapaar jor pakad chukaa tha or resham maarg se china kaa dhyaan hat gaya. 1516 main isapar tibeti qabza hua or 1715 ke aasapaas phir se china ke ching rajvansh kaa.
 doonhuang unche pahaado se ghira he or yahan sardi or garmi donon zyada hoti hai. july main ausat adhiktam taapmaana 33 centegrade or janvari main ausat nunatam taapmaana âˆ’14.6 centegrade hota he. yahan shushkta ke kaaran barf-barish bahut kam padati he or agar pad bhi jaae to jaldi hi pani waapas hawa main gaayab ho jaataa he.
