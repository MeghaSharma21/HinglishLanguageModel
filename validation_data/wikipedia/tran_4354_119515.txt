 jivan kii gunvattta  vyaktiyon or samaaj kii saamaanya bhalaai hai, jo jivan ke nakaaratmak or sakaaratmak visheshtaon ko darshaati hai. is main jivan kii santushti dekhne ko milti hai, jis main shaareerik swasthya, parivaar, shiksha, rojgaar, dhan, dharmik vishwaas, vitt or paryaavaran se lekar sab shaamil hai. jivan kii gunvattta sandarbhon kii ek vistrut shrinkhalaa he, jis main, antarrashtriya vikaas, swasthya seva, raajnneeti or rojgaar ke kshetra bhi shaamil hai. yah mahatvapurn hai ki QOL kii avadharana ko bilkul haal main hi badhate swasthya se sambandhit QOL ke kshetra ke saath mishrit na karen. HRQOL kaa akalan prabhavi dhang se QOL or uske swasthya ke saath apane sambandh kaa moolyankan hai.
 jivan kii gunvattta ko jivan str kii avadharana ke saath bhramit nahin kiya janaa chahie, kyonki jivan str mukhya rup se aay par aadhaarit hai.
