 yah jal parivahan kaa ek chirkaalin evam mahatvapurn saadhan he. jalmaarg ek pani kaa naugamya sharir hai. ek yaa kai jalmaarg kaa ek nauvahan maarg hote hai. jalmaarg me nadiyo,samudron, mahasaagaron, or naharen shaamil kar sakate hai. agar jalmaarg ko naugamya banane ke liye yah kai maapdand se milane chahie.
 pragaitihaasik samay, purushon or maal donon ko le jane ke liye jal parivahan kaa upyog kiya gaya he. jal parivahan sambhawatah jaanwaron ke upyog se pehle viksit ho gaya tha kyonki jalmaarg un jagahon par yatra kaa aasaan saadhan bana tha jahaan bhoomi par ghaney jangalon ne andolan main baadha daali thi.
 naukaaon yaa kisi anya madhyam ke upyog se hawa kii shakti kaa upyog kiya gaya tha jab pani ke parivahan kii seemaa or mahatv bad gaya. sabase pehle, naaven mukhya rup se antardeshiy jal or aashray tateey kshetron ke liye chote or simit thim.
 naukayan shilp ke aakaar or jatilta main kramik vruddhi ne vyapaar kii sthapana kii anumati di. phonician, misr, greek or rome ke saath-saath arab or bharatiyon ke paas vyapak vyapaarik sampark the. bhaap ke upyog ne lambii duuri par bade saamaan ko laane ke liye ek nayaa aayam, adhik shakti or gati ko jal parivahan pradaan kiya he.
 dijal or anya prakaar kii shaktiyon ke istemaal ne jal parivahan ke puure paridrishy ko badal diya he or aaj duniya bhar main adhikansh vyapaar jal-janit he. jal parivahan ke do sabase bada phaayde yah he ki yah mahasaagaron, nadiyon, samudron or visheshtaon kii jaruraton kaa upyog karata he, or yah bade or baari bhaar ke liye parivahan kaa sabase sasta prakaar he.

 teen prakaar ke antardeshiy jalmaarg, arthath, nadiyon, nadiyon ko sanshodhit yaa naharit kiya gaya he, or vishesh rup se naharon kaa nirmaan kiya gaya he. pehle ke samay main adhikansh, shaayad sabase zyada, vastuon kii antardeshiy pani kaa gaadi tha.
 yah sambhav tha jab jahaaj chote the, yatayaat simit tha, or samay kaarak vishesh rup se nahin dabaa rahaa tha. lekin 18 veen shatabdi ke dauraan jahaajon kaa aakaar badhane lagi, vyapaar kaa vistaar karne ke liye bahut kuch shuru hua, or gaadi kii gati ko adhik mahatv diya gaya.
 kai nadiyon kii simaaon ko dur karne ke liye or kai antardeshiy shaharon ko jal sanchaar ke saath pradaan karne ke liye, naharon kaa nirmaan shuru ho gaya. england main, nahar ke nirmaan main ek agrani, in nae maanav nirmit jalmargon kaa nirmaan lagbhag ek unmaad ban gaya. mahadweep par nahar kaa nirmaan kuch had tak baad main aayaa, haalanki praarambhik naharon ke kuch udhaaharan hai, udhaaharan ke liye, dakshini france main canal du midi kaa nirmaan 1681 main kiya gaya tha.
 1 9veen shatabdi main, railway or sadak parivahan ke vikaas ke kaaran antardeshiy jal parivahan main kuch kami ai thi. lekin kuch samay baad, jal parivahan main vaapasi karne kii pravriti hoti he, yah jal parivahan kii balk karij kii kamajori or kshamta ke saath nikatataa se judaa hua he - zeabrige or gaint ke bich kii nahar, albert nahar ke haalia or vartmaan ghatnaon kaa gavaah, massil, ron ghati yojana, aadi. antardeshiy jalmaarg donon phaayde or nuksaan hai.

  adhikansh anya roopon ke saath tulana main pani ke dwaara parivahan dhimi he or pani ke dwaara gaadi aamtaur par kharaab hone vaale utpaadan ke liye anupyukt he; tathaa
  sadakon yaa railway se jalmaarg kam lacheela he jo audyogic sthaan badalane ke liye khud ko aasaani se anukulit kar sakate hai.
 yadhyapi jal parivahan ek adhik yaa kam degree se adhik duniya main chalaaya jaataa he, antardeshiy jalmaarg kii keval chah pramukh nauvahan pranaliyaan hai: pashchimi or madhya europe kii nadiyon, volga-don pranali, uttar america nadiyon, amejan pranali, parana-paragway pranali, or chini jalmaarg.
 europe or uttari america main antardeshiy jalmaarg sabase acha viksit hote hai; anya mahadwipon main unka vikaas madhyam he.

 europe:
 europe main, france, germany, belgium, netherland or rus ke paas bahut vyapak antardeshiy jalmaarg hai jinamein nadiyon or nahar hai. france main 5,600 kimi kii jalmaarg vaali nadiyon or ek anya 4,800 kimi naharen hai.
 pramukh francisi nadiyon, jaise, loyr, goron, sen, ron, mius or mocel ko sanshodhit kiya gaya he or ve nahar sambandhi system se jude hai taaki puri tarah se nadi or naharon se bumadhya sagar se english channel yaa rin se atlantic mahaasaagar tak yatra kii ja sake. duusari ore, germany main 7,040 kimi kii antardeshiy jalmaarg ho rahi he.

 rin jalmaarg:
 rin duniya kii sabase vyast navik nadi he. iske donon kinaaron par baari udyogon kaa vikaas hua he jo saste pani ke parivahan se laabh uthaate hai. nadi chote aakaar ke sagar-chalane vaale jahaajon ke dwaara naugamya he. aarthik bhugol ke bindu se, nadiyon ne bhoomi ke interior main bade mahaasaagar margon ko lambaa khinch diya.
 "bahane vaali sadakon" main rin sabase ullekhneey he. yah samudr se mahadweep ke bahut dil main jaataa he rin ek "koyala nadi" he achen basin ke lignite or ruhar ke koyale nadi ke maal ke adhik se adhik hisse ko prastut karte hai.
 rin nevigation ke liye prakruti dwaara sabase adhik pasand kii nadiyon main se ek he. basse se strasburg or rin se rin ke bich sabase bada antar strasburg ke neeche se he, jo apstream khand main baari dhaal he, jiske kaaran bahut teji se chaalu hota he.
 strasburg ke upar yatayaat ke paas ek tej vartmaan, kam pani or ek rock lej ke kaaran trekkic maamuli he. lekin us bindu ke neeche slate mountain kii kanth ko chhodkar ek dhimi gati se chalati he garmiyon main pani kii maatra ek samaan he kam pani ke kaaran sardiyon ke maheenon ke dauraan rin nevigation band kar diya gaya he.
 bassel se strasburg tak rin ek alpine mul ke saath vishwasghaat ke ek moosaldhar barish aahaar kii visheshta he. mausami vividhtaon - garmiyon main baadh or sardiyon ke kam pani - adhik spasht or tej hai kyonki basal se strasburg tak dhaal bad jaati he.
 greeshm ritu main strasburg ke liye ek purna cargo le jane vaala bajra sardiyon main aadha maarg kaa nirvahan karnaa hoga. rin navikon ne pani main achaanak giravat kii raksha ke liye naav-naal or nadi-bistar ke bich 30 centimetre ke antar kii anumati di he jo sookhaa ke maamle main bahut teji se ho saktaa he.
 rin uttar atlantic kii ek sahaayak nadi main khaali he jo vishwa vyapaar ke sabase bade vartmaan kii shuruyaat main sthit he. europe kii anya nadiyon kii tulana main yah ek visheshadhikaar praapt sthiti he. iskaa laabh lene ke liye, rin ke nikat anya nadiyon ko naharon ke madhyam se joda gaya he. nateeja yah he ki europe main koi anya nadi nahin he jisamen rin ke rup main itane saare nahar conection hai.

 asia:
 asia main antardeshiy jalmaarg kii vistrut pranali nahin he, lekin kai deshon main nadiyon ko antardeshiy jalmaarg ke rup main upyog kiya jaataa he.

 africa:
 africa main, kuch nadiyon nauvahan hai, jo bahut simit lambaai ke liye bhi hai. nil nadi uttar-purv africa main sabase mahatvapurn nadi he, lekin iskaa bada dosha motiyobind ke uttaradhikaar he. apane upree bhaag main nil nadi ke kinare par he or girti he; iske madhya paathyakram main motiyobind hai yah delta or iske nichle paathyakram main nauvahan he.
 dakshin africa kii nadiyaan yatayaat ke liye bahut kam upyog hoti hai. zambasi keval 350 kimi ke liye noujvaan he, jabaki limpopo ko keval thodi duuri ke liye nevigate kiya ja saktaa he. orange naguide nahin he ushnkaribandheey africa main, congou ek shandaar pranali pradaan karata he jo jalmaarg par he. yah jhiilon tanganyika or nyasa ke bich hilands main ugta he.

 dakshin america:
 dakshin america main kuch lambii nadiyon hai lekin antardeshiy jalmaarg ke rup main iskaa upyog simit he. amejan nadi mahadweep kii sabase lambii nadi he lekin ab tak amejan pranali apekshaakrit kam upyog kii jaati he, kyonki jis kshetra ke madhyam se nadi bahati he vah ghani junglee he, bahut kam aabaadi vaale, aviksit or bade paimaane par berojgaar.

 australia:
 australia jalmaarg main kami he. uske nadi-pranali main hilands se tat tak bahane vaali choti dharaaen hoti hai, is prakaar nevigation ke liye upyukt nahin hai. do sabase mahatvapurn nadiyon mure or darling hai. sardiyon or vasant ritu ke dauraan darling river lagbhag shushk rahata he. nadi mare kaa aanshik rup se jalmaarg ke rup main upyog kiya jaataa he.
 mahaasaagar parivahan sabase mahatvapurn jal parivahan he, kyonki iske paas jameen kii gaadi par kuch phaayde hai. samudr jahaajon ke liye ek taiyaar kiye gaye karieve pradaan karata he, jo sadak yaa railway ke vipareet, rakrakhaav kii aavashyaktaa nahin he.
 pani kii satahen do-aiami hai or, haalanki samudr se ja rahe jahaajon ko aksar len bhejnaa padataa he, jahaajon ko simit disha main simit kar sakate hai, kisi bhi disha main.

 phir, kohre or floating barf ko chhodkar, or kabhi-kabhi tufaani mausam jo pragati main baadha daal saktaa he, sagar se chalane vaale jahaajon main un bhautik baadhaon kii tulana main kam he jo aksar bhumigat parivahan ko baadhit karte hai.
 jahaajon kaa upyog parivahan ke liye shuruaati samay se kiya gaya he graiko-roman kaal ke dauraan, ek sainya bhed lambey samay tak yaa galis ke bich kiya gaya, sainya uddeshyon ke liye upyog kiya jaataa tha, or vyapaar ke liye gol jahaajon. vikings kii ek samanta thi.
 adhik haal ke dinon ke dauraan jahaajon ne ek bhi adhik visheshgyata or kai alag-alag prakaar ke vanijyik jahaajon ko dhire-dhire viksit kiya he. aaj lagbhag aadha darjan mukhya prakaar ke vyapaaree jahaajon ko manyata praapt he - yatri liner, cargo liner, thok vaahak, tremp or copper, or short-sagar vyapaariyon.
 sabase shandaar vikaas, haalanki, thok vaahak kii upasthiti rahi he, jinamein sabase mahatvapurn tanker hai, tel kii aayu kaa utpaada. haal ke varshon main tankeron ke aakaar main baari vruddhi dekhi gai he, jinamein se kuch ab 5,00,000 ton mrit vajan se adhik hai.
 aaj, duniya ke vyapaaree shipping bhaar ke aadhe se adhik main tanker hai, aadhunik duniya main tel ke mahaan mahatv kaa ek sanket he.
 haalanki jahaajon ko andolan kii swatantraaa he or ve samudr kii sataha par lagbhag kahin bhi jane main sakasham hai, ve kuch "len" rakhane kii koshish karte hai. ve aisa isliye karte hai: 
  bhautik paristhitiyaan, or aarthik vichaar
 jaahir he, jahaajon ko keval jahaan maal yaa logon ko le jaaya jaataa he or sabase mahatvapurn nauvahan maarg sabase adhik utpaadak or adhik aabaadi vaale kshetron ko jodne vaale hai. kuch bhautik paristhitiyaan jahaajon ke baad ke margon ko nirdhaarit karne main bhi madad karti hai, udhaaharan ke liye samudr taton par bandargahon or bandargahon kii uplabdhta, mausam kii sthiti jaise kohre or tufaan kii ghatnaayen, or samudri barf or barfbacchon, pandubbi bainkon or uthle pani jaise samudr vigyaan ke kaarak.

 uttar atlantic mahaasaagar maarg main sabhi samudr margon kaa sabase bada yatayaat he. duniya ke vyapaaree jahaajon ke bhaar ke lagbhag ek-chauthai hisse is maarg par kaary karata he. maatra or maal kii vividhta main, yah maarg abhi tak kisi bhi anya se adhik he.
 yah maarg uttari america ke purvi tat par un logon ke saath pashchimi europe ke bandargahon ko jodta he ye do kshetra duniya ke sabase adhik aabaadi vaale or atyadhik viksit kshetra hai.
 uttari america or pashchimi europe maatra or vividhta ke saamaanon kii duniya kaa sabase bada utpaadak he. europe ke pashchimi tat par bandargahon main glassgo, liverpool manchester, southempton, landan, roterdam, bramen, bordo or lisbon hai. sanyukt rajya america ke purvi tat par bandargahon main quebec, montrial helifax, saint jon, boston, neuyork, balteemor, charlston gallweston or new orlions hai.
 yah mahaasaagar maarg duniya kaa sabase vyast vyapaar maarg he. nirmit vastuon kii badi maatra: vastra, rasayan, mashinari, urvarak, ispaat, sharaab aadi aadi in bandargahon se uttari atlantic ke sanyukt rajya or canada main niryaat kii jaati hai.
 canada or america se europe kaa niryaat lakdi, machhlee, gehun, kaccha kapaas, tambaaku, tel, mashinari or vaahan, dhaatu, kaagaj or rasayan hai.
 suvej nahar yaa bumadhya asian maarg:
 trephic kii maatra ke sambandh main yah maarg uttar atlantic se duusare sthaan par he. yah purvi africa, iran, arab, bharat, sudur purv, australia or newjiland ke bazaaron kaa aadesh detaa he. vastav main, yah maarg duniya ke dil se gujarata he or adhik bhoomi ko choota he or kisi anya maarg se adhik logon ko seva detaa he. kol ke apane kai bandargahon ke dauraan, yah duniya kii kul aabaadi ke lagbhag teen chauthai tak pahunchataa he.
 laal sagar paar karne ke baad, maarg do dishaao kaa anusaran karata he - ek africa ke purvi tat ke saath durban main; ek or purv ke liye - bharat, australia, aadi ke liye prasthaan ke bandargahon landan, liverpool, southempton, hamberg, roterdam, lisbon, marcills, genoa or neples hai. kol ke bandargahon main aden, mumbai, kolkata, rangoon, penang, singapore, manila, honkong, perth, edilade, melborn, sidney, mombasa, zanzibar, mozambik or durban hai.
 asian deshon dwaara is maarg kaa istemaal pashchimi bazaaron main kachche maal or kuch khaadya utpaadon ko bhejne ke liye kiya jaataa he or badale main nirmit lekhon main praapt hota he - sudur purv ke utpaadon main chaaval, chaay, chini or resham hote hai; bharat main coffee, chaay, pig aayran, mangneej ayask, jute ke saamaan, indigo, masaalon, kapaas, sagaun, resham, khaal, chamade or tel ke beej or madhya purv ke petroleum, coffee or sookhe fal hai. australia se maance, lakdi, gehun, aataa, fal, oon, makkhan or sharaab bheja jaataa he. china, australia, newjiland or dakshin or dakshin-purvi asia ke desh ab vastuon ke niryaat or aayaat donon ke liye is maarg kaa upyog karte hai.
 good hope maarg ke kep:
 yah maarg ek baar suvej nahar maarg kaa sahaayak vikalp tha, lekin isaki lambii or ghabrahat yatra kii vajah se, zyaadaatar shipping companiyon ne ise taal diya tha 1 9 67 main suej nahar ke band hone ke dauraan sabhi margon ko is maarg ko lene ke liye koi vikalp nahin tha.
 1 9 75 main suej nahar ko phir se khole jane ke baad bhi, is vyapaar kaa paalan karne ke liye bahut zyada vyapaar chal rahaa he kyonki tankeron or anya vaahanon kaa aajkal bahut bada he. chunki suve nahar keval 20,000 ton kii kshamta ke jahaajon ko samyojit kar saktaa he or toll shulk adhik he, kep maarg kaa mahatv bad rahaa he.
 isamen kai anya phaayde hai haal hi main swatantra africa deshon ke adhik se adhik aarthik vikaas or sone, tamba, heere, tin, chromium, mangneej, kapaas, tel hatheli, moongfalee, coffee or falon jaise unke samrudh prakritik sansadhano kaa shoshan, yatayaat ke daur kaa daur kep of good hope or purvi or pashchim africa donon main bandargahon main vruddhi hui he.
 panama nahar: west indian central american root:
 panama nahar kaa nirmaan 1913 main puraa kiya gaya tha. panama nahar 'prashanth ke liye pravesh dwaar' he or kep horn ke chaaron ore lambii or khatarnaak yatra samaapt kar diya. isse donon atlantic or prashanth samudr tat par laabh hua he, khanijon, tel, khaadya padaarthon, kachche maal or vinirmit utpaadon main vyapaar ko suvidhajanak banane main madad mili he. lekin sanyukt rajya america ke purvi or pashchimi taton ke bich yatayaat main sabase bada laabh arjeet kiya he.
 panama maarg ne pashchim bhartiya dwipon or uttar, madhya or dakshin america ke prashanth rajyon, vishesh rup se andin rajyon main vyapaar kii suvidha di he, jo khanij sansadhano main samrudh hai or uttari america main achche bazaar hai. latin america rajya america or pashchimi europea deshon se nirmit vastuon or khanan upkarnon kaa aayaat karte hai.
 sudur purv, prashanth dwipon or uttari america or pashchimi europe ke austrailia ke liye kismat vaala vyapaar bhi panama nahar ke madhyam se chalaa jaataa he. purvi asian deshon ke visheshkar china, japan or dakshin-purvi asian rajyon ke adhik aarthik vikaas ke saath, panama maarg purv or pashchim ke bich utpaadon ke aadan-pradaan main ek badi bhoomika nibhaa rahaa he. kep horn kii bajaay panama ke madhyam se oakland se neuyork tak kii duuri 4000 kilometre se adhik he.
 dakshin atlantic root:
 yah maarg west indies, brazil or argentina kii ore jaataa he maarg par kol ke pramukh bandargahon kingston, hawana, vera cruge, tampako, pernambuko, bahia, rio di janerio, santos, montevideo, bunus yrs or rozaario hai. maarg par niryaat chini, kele, kaccha kapaas, mahogany, tambaaku, coffee, anaaj, oon or maance hai, jabaki aayaat maal kaa nirmaan hota he.
 yah maarg ek taraf europe or west indies, carabian samudr tat, brazil, urugve or argentina ke bich vyapaar sambandhon ko duusare par rakhata he.
 trans-pacific root:
 uttar prashanth main kai maarg hai jo eendhan bharne or servicing ke liye honolulu main ikattha hote hai. sidhe maarg aage uttar ek mahaan sarkal he jo vankuvar or yokohama ko hawaii dweep ke phone kiye bina link karata he, yatra duuri ko aadhe se kam kar detaa he.
 uttar prashanth vyapaar main vainkuvar, sietal, portland, san francisco or los ancols shaamil hai, jo ki gehun, lakdi, kaagaj or lugdi, machhlee, deyari utpaadon or vinirmit vastuon se sambandhit hai. 7,200 kilometre kii vistrut prashanth kshetra main yokohama, cobe, shanghai, guangazu, honkong, manila or singapore hai. purvi asia se uttari america ke purv-baadhya vyapaar main mukhya rup se vinirmit saamaan, jaise, japan, honkong, es korea or taiwan se nirmit vastron, electrical upkaran, or dakshin-purv asia se ushnkaribandheey kachche maal, jaise rabar, kopra, palm tel, sagaun, tin or chaay. antarrashtriya sambandhon ke alaava uttar prashanth america ke mukhya bhoomi se uttar-purv main alaska ke pruthak rajyon or madhya-prashanth kshetra main hawaii ke liye ek mahatvapurn gharelu maarg he.
 dakshin prashanth main, yatayaat mukhya rup se panama nahar ke madhyam se yaa to pashchim europe yaa uttari america or australia, newjiland or vikhyath prashanth dwipon ke bich yatra kar rahe jahaajon ke hote hai. parivahan main maal zyaadaatar gehun, maance, oon, fal, deyari utpaada or nirmit lekh hai.
 anya mahatvapurn maarg purvi uttari america-purv dakshin amareeki, uttari america-pashchimi dakshin america, uttari america-austrailian honolulu ke madhyam se sidney or wellington tak), aadi
 souns transport kaa vishleshan suve or panama naharon ke vistrut vivaran ke bina puraa nahin kiya ja saktaa he. in do naharon ne samudri mahadweep ke puure pattern ko badal diya he, isliye in naharon kii visheshtaon or mahatvon ko jaananaa aavashyak he.
 suej nahar:
 suvej nahar duniya ke mahaan antarrashtriya jalmargon main se ek he - suvez ke istamas main katautee or bumadhya sagar or hind mahaasaagar ke bich nauvahan suvidhaayen pradaan karata he.
 laal sagar ke saath bumadhyasaagar ko jodne vaale nahar kaa itihaas 13 veen shatabdi bisi tak he. jab nil-laal sagar nahar 8 veen shatabdi isvi ke ant tak upyog main jane ke liye janaa jaataa he. 16 veen shatabdi ke baad se ek yaa duusare europea shaktiyon ko is baat main dilchaspee leni padi ki yaa to puraane jalmaarg ko phir se kholana yaa bumadhyasagariya kshetra se ek nayaa kataa hua he.
 1834 main, alexandria main francisi kaunsular seva ke ek sadsy ferdinand di leshps, suvej nahar yojana main ruchi rakhte the. 1854 main, unhone misr ke visarai ke saath is pariyojna par charchaa kii or unki sweekruti mili. nahar ke udghaatan kii taarikh se 99 varsh tak chalane kii riyaayat, kam pabandon ko di gai thi, unhen jalmaarg ke nirmaan ke uddeshy se ek antarrashtriya company banane ke liye adhikrut kiya gaya tha.
 khudaai 1859 main di leshps ke tahat shuru hui thi, jinhone nahar kaa nirmaan karne main das saal puure kiye the. yah november 1869 main kholaa gaya tha. yah lagbhag 160 kimi lambii or 11 se 15 meter kii gaharaai se he. manzil kii chaudaai 40 meter he or sataha par bhinn hoti he. suej nahar laal sagar ke saath bumadhya sagar ko jodta he port sad bumadhya sagar main sthit he; jabaki port suej laal sagar par he
 jaisaa ki ek jahaaj bumadhya sagar se nahar main pravesh karata he, yah port said ko paarit karegaa, duniya main sabase badi bandargahon main se ek or dakshin kii ore chalakar dakshin tat par tunsa main pravesh kiya jayega, jo ki ismailia shahar he. jhil tunsa se suve shahar tak, ek jahaaj geet butter jhil or little bitor jhil ke madhyam se paarit hoga.
 pichhali shatabdi ke dauraan koi bhi maanviya udhyam ne suej nahar se bhautik bhugolic parivartan ke madhyam se rashtron ke niyati ko prabhavit karne ke liye or kuch kiya he. suej nahar maarg ke udghaatan kep maarg kii tulana main landan se mumbai tak yatra par lagbhag 5,820 kilometre bachaaya.
 suej nahar ke udghaatan kaa na keval vishwa vyapaar or vaanijya par balki antarrashtriya raajnneeti par bhi jabardast asar padaa, pashchimi deshon ko africa, asia or australia ke liye ek nayaa maarg kholane ke alaava.
 uttar america or sudur purv ke purvi tat ke bich saamaanya vyapaar maarg kep of good hope ke madhyam se kiya gaya tha. suej nahar ne kep of good hope maarg se yatayaat ko apane aap se alag karke or is tarah, uttar america ko bahut laabh hua. har saal 12,000 se adhik jahaaz suve nahar se guzarati hai.
 suej nahar ne na keval sabase tej lekin na keval europe or purvi ke bich pargaman kii sabase kifayati rekha pradaan kii he. raajnneetik rup se suej maarg madhya purv deshon main telfield ke liye mahatvapurn he kyonki jinke utpaadon par pashchimi europe kii arthvyavastha nirbhar he.
 suej nahar kii kuch samasyaayein hai nahar ke sankeern hisse main jahaajon ko paar karne se bachane ke liye nahar ko gaharaai, chaudaai, mode ke sambandh main sudhaar kii aavashyaktaa he. registan se uda rahe hawaaon ke saath aane vaali gaad ke bayaan ko niyamit safai kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. duusari samasya yah he ki nahar se guzarane vaale jahaajon par lagaae gaye ucch nahar bakaayaa raashi he.
 yah dekha gaya he ki jab gati jaruri nahin he, to kai cargo liner ucch deytaon se bachane ke liye kep of good hope maarg kaa anusaran karte hai. ab kai cargo jahaajon aakaar main itane bade hai ki ve suej nahar se guzar nahin sakate. nahar ke aas-paas rajyon main raajnneetik asthirataa bhi chintaa kaa kaaran he. haalanki, antarrashtriya sammelan ke anusaar suej nahar swatantra or khule he, shanti ke samay yuddh ke samay, vaanijya yaa yuddh ke har jahaaj ke liye, dhvaj ke bhed ke bina.
 panama nahar:
 panama nahar do khand, ek kritrim jhil, ek prakritik jhil, or taale ke teen system ke madhyam se atlantic or prashanth mahasaagaron ko jodta he. yah panama ke sankeern Isthmus bhar main nirmit kiya gaya he jahaan lambey continental divide kam se kam ank main se ek ko gira detaa he. nahar sagaron main gahare pani se gahare pani se 72 kimi lambii he. yah 15 agast 1 9 14 ko kholaa gaya tha.
 sabhi taale dabl hote hai, isliye jahaajon ko yatayaat ke kisi bheed ke bina donon dishaao main paarit kiya ja saktaa he. channel kii gaharaai 12 se 26 meter ke bich hoti he or chaudaai 91 se 305 meter tak hoti he. panama se colon tak nahar ke madhyam se jane kaa samay 14 ghante he.
 panama nahar kisi na kisi desh se gujarata he or ejineering kathinaaiyaan suvej nahar ke maamle main bahut adhik hoti hai, jo ek str ke desh se guzarati hai or kisi bhi taale kii jarurat nahin he. panama nahar apni jalavidyut shakti utpann karata he jiske saath is kshetra kii na keval prakaash vyavastha kii jaati he, balki taale ke madhyam se jahaajon ko khinchne ke liye bijli ke ingine bhi aapurti kii jaati hai.
 panama nahar 'prashanth ke liye pravesh dwaar' he isse donon atlantic or prashanth samudr taton par laabh hua he, jisase khanij, tel, khaadya padaarthon, kachche maal or vinirmit utpaadon main vyapaar kii suvidha milti he. iskaa sabase bada laabh sanyukt rajya america ke purvi or pashchimi taton ke bich yatayaat main arjeet hua he.
 nahar, neuyork or san francisco ke bich kii duuri samudr ke dwaara 12,596 kimi ke bich kii duuri ko kam karata he, or pashchimi europe or pashchimi america ke bich kii duuri or purv america or purvi asia ke uttari or madhya bhaagon ke bich bahut kam duuri ko kam karata he.
 yah europe or newjiland ke bich kii duuri ko bhi thodaa kam karata he, lekin isse europe or asia yaa europe or australia ke bich kam nahin hota he purvi uttari america or uttar-pashchimi europe bahut nahar ke parinamswaroop bahut nishchit rup se praapt hue, kyonki ve is maarg se lagbhag sabhi pashchimi uttari america, pashchimi dakshin america or newjiland tak pahunch gaye the.
 purvi uttari america ke liye nahar kaa arth japan or honkong ke uttar main china ke sabhi hisson main kaaphi kami he, ek aisa pahalu jisane purv asia ke saath vyapaar ke teji se vikaas main yogadan diya he.
