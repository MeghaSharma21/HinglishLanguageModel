 anima bharat ke mahaan hindi kavi or rachanaakaar pandit sooryakanth treepathi 'niraala' kii ek kavy rachana he. anima naamak, 1943 main prakashit is kavita sangrah main nimnlikhit kavitaayen sankalit hai:
 anima niraala ke isse pehle kii kavy rachanaaon se kuch alag swar main virachit kriti he jaise kavi kaa apane pehle ke vichaaron se mohabhang ho rahaa ho. "sneh nirjhar bah gaya he" naamak kavita ke shuruuaati ansh nimnavat hai:
 "sneh nirjhar bah gaya he ret jyon tan rah gaya he aam kii yah daal jo sukhi dikhi kah rahi he - ab yahan pick yaa shikhi nahin aate, pankti main vah hoon likhi nahin jiskaa arth ----jivan dah gaya he"
