 sanathan goswami, chaitanya mahaprabhu ke pramukh shishy the. unhone gaudeey vaishnav bhakthi sampradaay kii anekon granthonki rachana kii. apane bhai rup goswami sahit vrindavan ke chha: prabhaavshaalee goswamiyon main ve sabase jyeshtha the.
 sanathan goswami karnat shreneey panchadravid bharadwaj gotreey yajurvedi brahman the. inke purvaj karnat rajvansh ke the or sarvajna ke putr roopeshwar bengal main aakar gangaresth barisal main bas gaye. inke pautra mukundadev bengal ke nawab ke darbar main rajkarmchaaree niyat hue tathaa goud ke paas ramkeli gram main rahane lage. inke putr kumardev teen putron amardev, santoshdev tathaa vallabh ko chhodkar yuvavastha hi main paralok sidhaar gaye jisase mukundadev ne teenon pautron kaa paalan kar unhen uchit shiksha dilaai. inhen teenon ko shri chaitanya mahaprabhu ne kramash: sanathan, rup tathaa anupam naam diya. 
 sanathan kaa janm san. 1523 ke lagbhag hua tha tathaa sanskrit ke saath farsi arabi kii bhi acchhee shiksha pai thi. san 1483 i. main pitamah kii mrityu par atharah varsh kii avasthaa main yah unheen ke pad par niyat kiye gaye or badi yogyata se kaary sanbhaal liya. husain shah ke samay main yah pradhan mantri ho gaye tathaa inhen darbare khaas oopaadhi mili. rajkaarya karte hue bhi teenon bhai param bhakt, virakt tathaa satsang premi the.
 inhone "kanai naatyashaala" banavaai thi, jisamen krishnalila sabandhi bahut si murtiyon kaa sangrah tha. shri chaitanya mahaprabhu kaa jab prakaash hua tab yah bhi unke darshan ke liye utaavle hue, par rajkaarya se chuttee nahin mili. isliye unhen patr likhakar ramkeli gram main aane kaa aagrah kiya. shri chaitanya jab vrundavan jate samay ramkeli gram main aae tab in teenon bhaaiyon ne unke darshan kiye or sabhi ne sansaarik janjaal se mukti paane kaa drudh sankalp kiya. sabhi rajpad par the. par sanathan inamen sabase bade or mantripad par the at: pehle shri rup tathaa anupam saare kutumb ko swajanmasthan fatehabad vakala main surakshit rakh aae or ramkeli gram main santaan ji ke liye kusamay main kaam aane ko kuch dhan ek vishwasaneey purush ke paas rakhkar vrundavan kii ore chale gaye. jab sanathan ji ne rajkaarya se hatane kaa praytn kiya tab nawab ne inhen karagaar main band kara diya. ant main ghoose dekar yah bandigrih se bhaage or kaashi pahunch gaye. sa. 1572 main yaheen shrigaurang se bhent hui or do maas tak vaishnav bhakthi shaastra par upadesh dekar inhen vrundavan bhej diya ki vahaan ke lupt tirthon kaa uddhaar, bhaktishaastra kii rachana tathaa premabhakti evam sankirthan kaa prachaar karen. 
 yah vrundavan chale gaye par kuch dinon baad shrigaurang ke darshan kii prabal ichha se jagadishpuri kii yatra kii. vahaan kuch din rahakar yah pun: vrundavan laut aae or adityatila par ant tak yaheen rahen. madhukari maangane yah nity mathura jate the or vahiin unhone shri adwaitacharya dwaara prakatit shri madanagopal ji ke vigrah kaa darshan kiya. yah us murti ko vrundavan laae or adityatila par pratishthapit kar seva karne lage. kuch dinon baad ek mandir ban gaya or san. 1591 se seva kii vyavastha thik rup se chalane lagi. isi prakaar anek vigrahon ko khojkar unki seva kaa prabandh kiya, anek lupt tirthon kaa uddhaar kiya tathaa kai granth likh. yah shrigaurang ke pramukh shishyon tathaa parshadon main the. 
 inaki rachanaayen hai - shri brihat bhagwatamrita, vaishnavatoshini tathaa shrikrishnalilastav. haribhaktivilas tathaa bhakthirsamritasindhu kii rachana main bhi inkaa sahyog tha.
