 khaadya evam krishi sanghathan ek antarrashtriya sanghathan he, jo krishi utpaadan, vaaniki or krishi vipanan sambandhi shodh vishay kaa adhyayan karata he. yah sanghathan khaadya evam krishi sambandhi gyaan or jaankariyon ke aadan-pradaan karne kaa manch bhi he. iske saath-saath yah in kshetron main vibhinn deshon ke adhikariyon ke prashikshan kii vyavastha bhi karata he. vikasshil deshon main krishi ke vikaas main isaki bhoomika mahatvapurn he. eff.e.o vikasshil deshon ko badalati takanic jaise krishi, paryaavaran, poshak tatv or khaadya suraksha ke bare main jaankaree detaa he. yah sanghathan sanyukt raashtra sangh kii ek vishisht sanstha he or usee ke antargatt kaary karata he. 
 is sanghathan kii sthapana 16 oktober, 1945 ko quebec shahar, canada main hui thi. 1951 main iskaa mukhyalay washington se rome sthanantarit kiya gaya. vartmaan main 191 raashtra iske sadsy hai, jisamen europian samudaay evam firo dweepasmooh bhi sammilit hai, jo associat sadsy hai.. epa.e.o ke pratham mahanideshak britain ke jon ore the. iske vartmaan mahanideshak senegal ke jacks deopha hai. is sanghathan ko sadsy deshon ke sammelan dwaara shasit kiya jaataa he. isaki baithak dwivarshik hoti he, jisamen pichley do varsh main kiye gaye kaaryo kii samiksha or aagaamee do varsho ke liye bajet paarit kiya jaataa he. varsh 2008-09 kaa bajet 92.98 karod amareeki dollars tha. is sammelan main 49 logon kaa chayan bhi kiya jaataa he jo antarim shasit samiti ke rup main kaary karti he. is sammelan main hi mahanideshak kaa chunaav bhi hota he. sadasyon kaa kaarykaal teen varsh kaa hota he. is sanghathan ke aath vibhaag hai, prashasan evam vitt, aarthik or saamaajik, fisharij, vaaniki, saamaanya vishay or soochna, satat vikaas, krishi or upabhokta suraksha or takneekee sahyog hai. eggeo kaa aam vitt-poshan uske sadasyon dwaara vahan kiya jaataa he.
 sanghathan kaa mukhyalay rome main sthit he, jo itilian east africa vibhaag kaa purv karyaalay rahaa he. is imaarat ek saamne hi aikzam obelisk sthit he. isko ithiopia se 1937 main benito masolini ne chin liya tha, phir baad main 18 april 2005 ko lautaaya gaya.
