 narayan singh ravat dhani purohitan kishangarh ajmer shahar, rajasthan rajya, pashchmottar bharat main sthit he. pehle nayanagar ke naam se gyaat is shahar kii sthapana 1835 main hui thi. udaipur or jodhpur ke madhya laabhadaayak sthiti main hone kii kaaran yah uttarottar samrudh hota gaya.
 beawar pramukh rail or sadak jankshan vaala shahar he. yah krishi-utpaadon or kapadon kaa ek vyavsaayik centre he. udyogon main kapaas kii otai, hathkargha, hojri nirmaan or kashthashilp se jude udyog shaamil hai. yahan ke mahavidhyalay maharishi dayanand saraswati vishvavidhyalay se sambhddha hai. yahan kii tilpattee kii prasiddhi vishwa str par he.
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar beawar shahar kii kul jansankhya 3,25,000 se zyada he.
