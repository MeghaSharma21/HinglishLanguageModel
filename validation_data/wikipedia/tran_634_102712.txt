 chhapra jankshan railway stesan, stesan code CPR, bhartiya rajya bihar ke saaran jile main sthit ek railway stesan he. yah railway ke uttar purvi sambhag main padataa hai evam chhapra shahar evam iske aas paas rahane vaale log is stesan kaa zyaadaatar prayog karte he. yah shahar ghaghara or ganga nadi ke paas sthit he.
 yah stesan uttar purvi railway kshetra ke varanasi railway divijan ke antargat aata he or 2012 main iskaa vidyutikaran kiya gaya.
 yah stesan oonchai kareeb 58 meter ooncha he or yahan 4 platform, 21 patariyon or 5 line he jinhen baliya, sivaan, sonpur, pehleja or mashrakh main shakhabaddh kiya gaya he. is stesan se kul 172 trainon kaa sanchaalan kiya jaataa he jo yaa to yahan se khulti he yaa yahanse guzarati he. 
 yah stesan e-shrenee main vargeekrut kiya gaya hai evam yah jankshan stesan prati varsh lagbhag 60 karod utpann karata he.
