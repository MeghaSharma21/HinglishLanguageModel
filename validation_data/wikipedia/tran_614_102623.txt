 kisi dharmik sampradaay ke dwaara sweekrut vishwaason kaa krambaddh sangrah us sampradaay kii dharmamimasaa he. dharmamimasaa main vigyaan or darshan ke drushtikon kii saarvbhaumata nahin hoti, isaki paddati bhi unki paddati se bhinn hoti he. vigyaan pratyaksh par aadhaarit he, darshan main buddhi kii pramukhataa he or dharmamimasaa main, aapt vachan kii pradhanta sweekrut hoti he. jab tak vishwaas kaa adhikaar prashnrahit tha, dharmamimasakon ko is baat kii chintaa na thi ki unke mantavya vigyaan ke avishkaron or darshan ke nishkarshon ke anukool hai yaa nahin. parantu ab sthiti badal gai he or dharmamimasaa ko vigyaan tathaa darshan ke mail main rahanaa hota he.
 dharmamimasaa kisi dharmik sampradaay ke sweekrut siddhanton kaa sangrah he. is prakaar kii saamagree kaa strot kahan he? in siddhanton kaa sarvopari strot to aisi pustak he, jise us sampradaay main ishwariya gyaan samajha jaataa he. isse utarkar un vishesh purushon kaa sthaan he jinhen ishwar kii ore se dharm ke sambandh main nirbhrant gyaan praapt hua he. roman catholic carch main pope ko aisa pad praapt he. vivaad ke vishyon par aacharyon kii parishadon ke nishchay bhi pramanik siddhant samajhe jate hai.
 dharmamimasaa ke vichaar vishyon main ishwar kii satta or swaroop pramukh hai. inke atirikt jagat or jeevatma ke swaroop par bhi vichaar hota he. ishwar ke sambandh main pramukh prashn yah he ki vah jagat main antaratma ke rup main vidyaman he, yaa isse pare, upar bhi he. jagat ke vishay man poocha jaataa he ki yah ishwar kaa utpaadan he, uskaa udgaar he, yaa nirmaan maatr he. utpaadanavaad, udgaarwaad or nirmaanvad kii jaanch kii jaati he. jeevatma ke sambandh main, swadhinata or mokshasaadhan chirkaal ke vivaad ke vishay bane rahe hai. sant agstin ne purv nirdharanwad kaa samarthan kiya or kaha ki koi manushya apane karmon se doshamukt nahin ho saktaa, doshamukti ishwariya karunaa par nirbhar he. iske vipareet bharat kii vichaardhaara main jeevatma swatantra he or manushya kaa bhagya uske karmon se nirnit hota he.
