 karlook goekaturk kagaanath ke adheen hua karte the. san 742 i main ve uigur or basamil qabeelon ke saath milakar goekaturk kagaanath ke viruddh baghaavat main uthe. 744 main basamilon ne goekaturk rajdhani otugen or raja olymish khan par qabza kar liya. lekin usee saal uiguron or karluqon ne aapasi santhganth kar lee or milakar basamilon par hamalaa kar diya. basamilon ke raja kaa sir kalam kar diya gaya or puure qabeele ke logon ko ghulaam banaakar yaa to anya qabeelon main baant diya gaya yaa chiniyon ko bech diya gaya. uigur sardaar ab is nai kagaanath kaa kagaan bana or karluq uske adheen rajyapal bana. ek saal ke andar-andar uiguron or karluqon main jhadpen shuru ho gai or karluqon ko majabuuran apni zaminein chhodkar pashchim kii ore janaa padaa.
 karlukon ke pashchim jane se unhone turki bhashaaon ko madhya asia ke adhik vistrut hisson main failaayaa. 751 isvi main muslim arab sena madhya asia main china ke tang rajvansh ke saath takaraai. pehle to karlukon ne chiniyon kaa saath diya lekin phir dal badalakar arabon ke saath ho gaye jis se chiniyon kii haar hui or madhya asia kaa ek bada bhu-bhaag chini prabhaav se bahaar ho gaya. 766 main purvi kazakhstan main karlook rajya kii sthapana hui jisaki purv main uigur kagaanath se seemaa thi. jab 840 ke baad uigur kagaanath khatm hone lagi to karlook rajya purv kii or badha or karlukon ne bahut se uiguron ko saath milakar apni nayi karakhaani khaanat sthapit kii. 943 main iske shaasha, saatuk boothra khan, ne islam apana liya or uske baad yah is khaanat kaa rajdharm ho gaya. 12veen sadi ke shuru main saljuk turkon ne karakhaaneeyon se aamu-paar kshetra chin liye. 1130 main kaara-kitaan khaanat ne seljukon or karakhaaneeyon kii mili-juli fuj ko haraa diya. karakhaani phir-bhi kisi tarah apni pahchaan banaae rahe lekin 1211 main khwarizmi rajvansh ne unhen hameshaa ke liye haraa diya or karakhaani phir kabhi ek shakti ke rup main nahin ubhare.
