 mattavilas prahasan ek pracheen sanskrit ekanki naatak he. yah saatvin shatabdi kii shuruyaat main vidwaan raja mahendravarman pratham dwaara likhit do mahaan ekanki naatakon main se ek he.
 yah sanskrit kaa pracheenatam prahasan he. yah prahasan chota hone par bhi bada rochak he. isamen madiraa ke nashe main dhoot ek kapalik kii manodasha kaa varnan he. is naatak main dharmik aadambaro par kataaksh he tathaa buddha tathaa kapalikon kii hansee udayi gayi he. 
 is naatak main ek shakya bhikshu he jise puraa vishwaas he ki buddha ke pitak granthon kii mul prati main surapan or stri-samagam kaa samavesh avashya rahaa hoga. use lagata he ki yah vruddh baudhon kaa yuva baudhon ke viruddh rachaa hua shadyantr he. vah us mul pitak ke anusandhan main rat he, vah use khojna chahta he. yaa unamem yah praavdhaan jodpha chahta he. isi karm main vah shakya bhikshu nimagn rahata he. ek din buddha-math jate hue vah sochataa he-â€™atyant dayaalu bhagwaan buddha ne mehalon main nivas, sundar sej lage palangon par shayan, pehle prahar main bhojan, aparahm main mithe rason kaa paan, paanchon sugandhon se yukt tambul or reshmi vastron kaa pahananaa ityadi upadeshon se bhikshu-sangh par krupa karte hue kya stri-sahavaas or madirapan kaa vidhaan bhi nahin kiya hoga? avashya kiya hoga. avashya hi in nirutsahi tathaa dusht vruddh baudhon ne ham navayuvakon se daah kar pitak-granthon main stri-sahavaas or surapan ke vidhaan ko alag kar diya he.
