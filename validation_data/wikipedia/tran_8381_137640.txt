 amar shahid ramfal mandal ek bhartiya swatantraaa senaanee thea. dinaank 23 agast 1943 ko inhen british sarkaar dwaara phaansi de diya gaya. in par 24 agast 1942 ko bajpattee chowk par angrej sarkaar ke tatkaaleen sitamarhi anumandal adhikari hardip narayan singh, police inspector ramamurti jha, hawaldaar shyaamalaal singh or chaparasi darbeshi singh ko gadasa se katkar hatya karne kaa aarope tha.
