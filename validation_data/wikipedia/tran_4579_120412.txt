 2008 greeshmakaleen olympic yaa beijing olympic 2008 main beijing, china main yojit hue greeshmakaleen olympic khel kaa naam he. iskaa naaraa tha ek vishwa, ek swapn or inkaa aayojan 8 agast se 24 agast, 2008 tak hua, jisamen 11,028 khilaadiyon dwaara 28 khelon kii 302 pratispardhaon main bhaag liya gaya.
 2008 ke olympic khelon ke liye nimnlikhit calendar main, prayek nila box ek pratiyogita kaa pratinidhitiv karata he, jaise ki yogyata daur, us din. pile bakse un dinon kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai, jinke dauraan ek khel ke liye padak vijeta final yojit kiya gaya tha. in bakse main prayek bullate ek kaarykram final he, us din us final kii sankhya kaa pratinidhitiv karne vaale goliyon kii sankhya jo us din ladi gai thi. bain ore, calendar game ke dauraan yojit hone vaali ghatnaon ke saath prayek khel ko soochibaddh karata he, or is khel main kitne swarna padak jite the. pathak kii sahaayta ke liye calendar ke sheersh par ek kunjee he.
 beijing khelon ke liye kaarykram athens main yojit 2004 ke greeshmakaleen olympic ke samaan tha. 2008 ke khelon main 28 khel or 302 ghatnaayen thim. bmex ke nae cycling anushaasan se nau sahit do nae kaarykram yojit kiye gaye. mahilaon ne pehali baar 3000 meter steeplchez main bhaag liya. tairaaki anushaasan main 10-kilometre kii duuri par purushon or mahilaon ke liye khule pani kii tairne kii ghatnaayen joda gaya tha. teble tennis main team kii ghatnaon ne yugal spardhaon kii jagah lee. baad lagane main, mahila team kii panni or mahila team ke dal ne purushon kii team ke foil or mahila team api ko badal diya. do khel keval purush, besabaal or mukkebaaji ke liye khule the, jabaki ek khel or ek anushaasan keval mahilaon, softball or sincranaise tairaaki ke liye khuli thi. ghudsawaari or mishrit badminton ekmaatr aise khel hai jisamen purush or mahilaayein ek saath pratispardhaa karte hai, haalanki salling main teen ghatnaon ne donon purushon or mahila pratibhaagiyon ke liye mauka diya. haalanki, keval purush pratibhaagiyon ne teenon ghatnaon main bhaag liya.
 28 khelon main 302 ghatnaayen huin thim. prayek khel main lade hue ghatnaon kii sankhya koshthkon main darshaya gaya he .
 aadhikarik olympic khelon ke atirikt, beijing aayojan samiti ko khelon ke samanaantar main vushu pratiyogita chalaane ke liye iosi dwaara vishesh anudaan diya gaya tha. vushu toornament beijing 2008 main 43 deshon ke 128 athleton ne 15 alag-alag kaaryakramon main sammanit kiye gaye padak ke saath bhaag liya; haalanki, inhen aadhikarik padak milan main shaamil nahin kiya gaya kyonki vushu 2008 olympic khelon ke kaarykram main nahin tha.
 aath deshon ke saat padak arjeet kiye, jinamein se 54 swarna padak jite, donon olympic khelon ke liye nae record sthapit karte the. 118 bhaag lene vaale deshon ne ek bhi padak jeeta nahin. china ke athleates ne in olympic main 48 swarna padak jite hai, jo 1936 ke greeshmakaleen olympic main germany ke baad se padak jeetne ke liye sanyukt rajya america or rus ke alaava pehle raashtra ban gaye hai. sanyukt rajya kii team ne sabase adhik padak jite, 111 ke saath. afghanistan, morishas, sudaan, tazakastan or togo ne apana pehla olympic padak jeeta mongolia ke athlete or panama ne apane raashtra ke pehle swarna padak jite srbija ke ek athlete ne us naam ke tahat apana pehla padak jeeta, jisane pehle yugoslavia or srbija or montenegro ke hisse ke rup main padak jeeta tha.
 america tairaak maaikel felps ne kul milakar aath swarna padak praapt kiye, jo ki ek bhi olympic khelon main kisi anya olympian se zyada tha, is prakriya main kai vishwa or olympic record sthapit kar rahe the. jamaica ke spirtor usen bolt ne bhi kai ghatnaon main ricard banaaya, 9.69 second ke samay ke saath 100 meter final ko puraa kiya, apane pichley vishwa record ko bhi paar kiya. roosi-janme america jimnast nestia liyucain ne kalatmak jimnastics main swarna padak jeeta, teesri america mahila banane ke liye, 1984 main mairi lotton or 2004 main karli patterson ke baad.

 sabhi 205 manyata praapt rashtriya olympic samitiyon main se ek, jo 2008 main astitv main tha 2008 tak 2008 greeshmakaleen olympic main bhaag liya, yah apwaad bruneii tha. teen deshon ne olympic khelon main apni pehali baar bhaag liya: marshall dweep, montenegro or tuvaalu.
 haalanki iosi dwaara manyata praapt ek purna sadsy nahin he or is prakaar olympic main aupchaarik rup se pratispardhaa karne kii anumati nahin di gai he, makaao sports or olympic samiti ne vushu toornament beijing 2008 main bhaag lene ke liye ek pratinidhimandal bheja, keval ek anjaan rashtriya olympic samiti 2008 greeshmakaleen olympic. yah macau ke madhyam se mashaal riley ko vyavasthit karne ke liye chini olympic samiti ke saath samanvayit prayaas bhi karata he.
 marshall dweep or tuvaalu kramshah 2006 or 2007 main rashtriya olympic samiti kaa darja praapt karte the, or 2008 main ve pehle game the jisamen ve bhaag lene ke liye yogya the. srbija or montenegro ke rajyon, jo 2004 ke khelon main sanyukt rup se srbija or montenegro ke rup main bhaag chuke the, pehali baar alag-alag spardhaa main the. montenigrin olympic samiti ko 2007 main ek nai rashtriya olympic samiti ke rup main sweekaar kiya gaya tha. kosovo ke padosi ne haalanki bhaag nahin liya. kosovo main swatantraaa kii ghoshnaa ke baad, iosi ne iosi dwaara manyata praapt hone se pehle kosovo ko un aavashyaktaaon ko puraa karne kii aavashyaktaa he; sabase vishesh rup se, ise sanyukt raashtra dwaara swatantra ke rup main manyata praapt kerani hogi. china or sanyukt rajya america kii sabase badi team thi, jisamen china ke 639 athlete or sanyukt rajya america ke liye 596 the.
