 santa phe dweep jise bairington dweep bhi kaha jaataa he, 24 varg kilometre kshetra kaa ek chota sa dweep he jo, santakruge dweep ke dakshin purv main or gailapagos dweepasmooh ke centre main sthit he. iskaa naam spain ke ek shahar ke naam par rakhaa gaya he. bhugaulik rup se yah dweep samooh ke sabase puraane dwipon me se ek he, yahan 40 lakh varsh puraani jwalamukhi chattaane bhi pai gayi hai. dweep kii vanaspati kii visheshta vishaal openshia cactus kii sabase badi prajati kaa ek ghanaa jangal he.
 santa phe dweep do sthaneey prajatiyon: bairington sthaliy goh or santa phe chaaval chuha kaa ghar he. dweep par jalsinh badi sankhya main milte hai.
