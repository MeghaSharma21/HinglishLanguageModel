 carch street monmouth, wales kaa ek padchaariyon ke liye khulaa maarg he. yah ab kai swarantra dukaanon, restauranto, vyapaarik kala dalanon va savoy theatre kaa ghar he. 1830 tak, jab priori street kaa nirmaan ise bypass karne ke liye kiya gaya, yah maarg monmouth ke bich awajahi kaa mukhya jariya tha jo uttar purv ke bazaar va parish carch ko isse jodta tha. yah kabhi shahar ke kattalkhano kaa kendra bindu tha or se buchars ro bhi kaha jaataa tha.
 vartmaan main maarg par maujuud imaaraten shuruuaati unnisvi shatabdi jitani puraani he jo teen manzila he va inke donon ore jhuki hui chhaten he. 24 carch street kii shuruyaat ji. vi. madox ne 1840 main dizine kii thi. ek imaarat jo wit swaan court ke liye rasta muhaiyaa karaati he, usee vakt bani thi jab priori street kaa nirmaan hua tha or shaayad ise bhi madox ne hi dizine kiya tha.
 savoy theatre ek duusare darze kii imaarat he jise 1928 main paramparaagat tareeke se punah sajaayaa gaya tha or yah wales main maujuud sabase purana chaalu theatre he. jis imaarat main yah theatre maujuud he uskaa nirmaan purv bel in kii niv par kiya gaya tha or ise 1832 main pehali baar manoranjan kaa license pradaan kiya gaya tha. yah monmouth kaa pehla cinema ban gaya jise "living pikchar pales and rinkariz" kaha jaataa tha par baad main ise albani ward theatre samooh ne punah nirmit kiya. 1960 main yah ek cinema ke rup main band ho gaya or 1983 main bingo haul ke rup main khulaa or iske baad kuch samay ke liye aitihaasik magic lenturn slidon ko dikhaane ke liye khulaa. yah 1990 se lagaataar ek cinema ke rup main karyrat rahaa he or ab ek cheritable trust dwaara sanchalit he.
