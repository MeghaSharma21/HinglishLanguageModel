 kisi shabd kii uccharan-vartanee uski maanak vartanee se bhinn hoti he or yah us shabd ke uccharan ko hi darshane ke liye banaayi gayi hoti he. isaki aavashyaktaa praayah unheen bhashaaon main padati he jinamein lipi or bhashaa main ekaik-sambandh nahin hota . uccharan-vartanee main bhashaa ke saamaanya vartanee niyamon kaa paalan kiya jaataa he. inamen se adhikansh ekbargi prayog ke liye gadhi gayi hoti hai, lekin kuch uccharan-vartaniyaan maanak bhi ban gayi hai. jaise- gonna kaa prayog going to ke uccharan ko darshane ke liye rudh ho chukaa he. yatha- I'm gonna catch you. 
 uccharan vartanee kaa prayog anaupchaarik rup se kiya jaataa he, tathaa yah un shabdon jinka uccharan aniyamit he, yaa jinaki vartanee-maatr se uccharan kaa andaaz lagaana kathin he ke uccharan ko darshane ke liye istemaal kii jaati he.
