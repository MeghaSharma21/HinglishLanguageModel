 andithan yaa andijan madhya asia ke uzbekistan desh kaa chauthaa sabase bada shahar he or us desh ke andithan praant kii rajdhani he. yah faraghna vaadi main uzbekistan kii kirgizstan kii seemaa ke saath sthit he. san 1999 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 3,23,900 anumaanit kii gai thi. yah bharat ke mogul samrajya ke sansthaapak or pehle samraat babar kaa janmasthal bhi he. andithan shahar ke bich main iskaa sabase mahatvapurn saarvajanik centre 'babar chowk' kehalaata he.
 13 may 2005 main yahan babar chowk main uzbek sarkaar main bhrashtachar or anya samasyaon ko lekar pradarshan hue the jinhen 'andithan vidroh' kaa naam diya jaataa he. inhen band karne ke liye sarkaar ne yahan goliyaan chalaai jis se bahut pradarshkon kii mrityu hui.
 'andithan' main 'ji' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'jha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan angrezi ke 'televithan' shabd ke 'ji' se miltaa he. 'andithan' naam kii utpatti ke bare main vidwaanon main matbhed he. bahut se itihaaskaar samajhate hai ki yah naam ek 'andi' naamak turki qabeele par padaa.
