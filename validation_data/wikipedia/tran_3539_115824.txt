 hindu vivaah adhinium bharat kii sansad dwaara san 1955 main paarit ek kanoon he. isi kalavadhi main teen anya mahatvapurn kanoon paarit hue : hindu uttaradhika adhinium, hindu alpsankhyak tathaa abhibhavak adhinium or hindu adopshan or bharanposhan adhinium . ye sabhi niyam hinduon ke vaidhik paramparaon ko aadhunik banane ke dhyey se lagu kiye gayi the.
 smritikaal se hi hinduon main vivaah ko ek pavitra sanskaar mana gaya he or hindu vivaah adhinium 1955 main bhi isko isi rup main banaae rakhane kii cheshtaa kii gai he. kintu vivaah, jo pehle ek pavitra evam atoot bandhan tha, adhinium ke antargat, aisa nahin rah gaya he. kuch vidhivichaarkon kii drushti main yah vichaardhaara ab shithil pad gai he. ab yah janm janmaantar kaa sambandh athwa bandhan nahin varan vishesh paristhitiyon ke utpann hone par, vaivaahik sambandh vighatit kiya ja saktaa he.
 adhinium kii dhara 10 ke anusaar nyaayik pruthakkaran nimna aadhaaron par nyaayalay se praapt ho saktaa he :
 tyaag 2 varsh, nirdayata, kusht rog, ratijrog, vikrutiman tathaa parpurush athwa par-stri-gaman adhinium kii dhara 13 ke anusaar - sansarg, dharmaparivartan, paagalpan, kusht rog, ratij rog, sanyas, mrityu nishkarsh, par naiyayik pruthakkaran kii decri paas hone ke do varsh baad tathaa daanpatyadhikaar pradaan karanevaali decri paas hone ke do saal baad 'sambandhviched' praapt ho saktaa he.
 striyon ko nimna aadhaaron par bhi sambandhviched praapt ho saktaa he; yatha-dwivivaah, balaatkaar, pumithun tathaa pashumaithun. dhara 11 evam 12 ke antargat nyaayalay 'vivahashoonyata' kii ghoshnaa kar saktaa he. vivaah pravritiheen ghoshit kiya ja saktaa he, yadi doosraa vivaah sapind or nishiddh gotra main kiya gaya ho .
 napunsakata, paagalpan, maansik durbalata, chal evam kapat se anumati praapt karne par yaa patni ke anya purush se garbhvatee hone par vivaah vivarjy ghoshit ho saktaa he. .
 adhinium dwaara ab hindu vivaah pranali main nimnaankit parivartan kiye gaye hai :
 vidhivettaon kaa yah vichaar he ki hindu vivaah ke siddhant evam pratha main parivartan karne kii jo aavashyaktaa upasthit hui thi uskaa kaaran sambhavat: yah he ki hindu samaaj ab paashchaatya sabhyataa evam samskruti se adhik prabhavit hua he.
