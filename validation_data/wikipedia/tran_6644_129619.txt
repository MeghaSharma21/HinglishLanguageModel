 charmshodhan vah prakriya he jo ek puyakaari yaa sadane vaali jaanavar kii khaal ko ek tikaoo saamagree yani chamade main badal detii he. is prakriya main tannin naam ke ek amleey rasaynic yogic kaa prayog kiya jaataa rahaa he jiske naam par is prakriya kaa naam 'tanning' padaa. isko 'chamada kamanaa' bhi kehete hai. kamaane kii prakriya ke dauraan hi chamade ko ranga bhi ja saktaa he.
 kamaane se khaal kii protein sanrachana puri tarah badal jaati he or wo phir kabhi waapas apni mul avasthaa yani kachi khaal nahin ban sakati.
 charmshodhan ke kaary kaa praathmik charan he- mrit pashu kii khaal praapt karnaa. iske pashchaat hi charmshodhan kii paramparaagat prakriya prarambh hoti he. sadd praapt khaal ko teen se chaar din tak dhoop main sookhne ke liye lataka diya jaataa he. uske baad pani se khaal kii dhulaai kii jaati he. pani se acchhee tarah dhone ke baad khaal ko chuunaa mili hue pani main daal diya jaataa he. chooney ko pani main daalane ke liye cement kii tank banaakar usamen 1:5 anupaat main chuunaa or pani kaa vilyan banaaya jaataa he. teen chaar ghante tak pratidin is vilyan main daalkar chamade ko acchhee prakaar se pani se bhigoya jaataa he.
