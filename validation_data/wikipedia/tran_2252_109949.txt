 jhum krishi ek aadim prakaar kii krishi he jisamen pehle vrikshon tathaa vanaspatiyon ko katkar unhen jala diya jaataa he or saaf kii gai bhoomi ko puraane upkarnon se jutai karke beej bo diye jate hai. fasal purnatah prakruti par nirbhar hoti he or utpaadan bahut kam ho paata he. kuch varshon tak jab tak mitti main urvartaa vidyaman rahati he is bhoomi par kheti kii jaati he. iske pashchaat is bhoomi ko chhod diya jaataa he jis par punah ped-paudhen ug aate hai. ab anyatra junglee bhoomi ko saaf karke krishi ke liye nai bhoomi praapt kii jaati he or us par bhi kuch hi varsh tak kheti kii jaati he. is prakaar yah ek sthannantaranshil krishi he jisamen thode-thode samay ke antar par khet badalte rahete hai. bharat kii purvottar pahadiyon main aadim jatiyon dwaara kii jane vaali is prakaar kii krishi ko jhum krishi kehete hai. is prakaar kii sthanantaranshil krishi ko srilanka main chena, hindesiya main ladaang or rhodesia main milpa kehete hai.
 yah kheti mukhyatah ushnkaribandheey van pradeshon main kii jaati he.
