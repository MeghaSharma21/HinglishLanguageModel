 dambo 1941 main walt dijani dwaara nirmit anemated film he.
 mukhya charitra jambo junior, ek arddh maanavaakrutiya hathi he jo nirdayatapurvak "dambo" upnaam he. vah uske bade kaanon ke liye uphaas he, lekin vastav main vah pankh ke rup main uske kaan kaa upyog karke udaan main sakasham he. ek chuuhe or hathi ke bich taksaali dushmani nakal sambandh film, apane hi sachche dost, uski maan se alag, sabase bhar main mouse, timothy he.
 dambo kalpana kii vittiya nuksaan sambhalanaa karne ke liye banaaya gaya tha. yah saadagi or digni studio ke liye arthvyavastha kii ek vichaar peechha or 64 minute main, yah ek digni ke kam se kam anemated suvidhaon kii he.
