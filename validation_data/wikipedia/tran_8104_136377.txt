 indira gandhi rashtriya kala centre kalaon ke kshetra main shodh or shaikshik anusandhan tathaa prasar kaa centre he. isaki sthapana bharat sarkaar ke samskruti mantralay ke adheen ek swytt nikaay ke rup main san 1987 main kii gai thi. yah centre 'kala' ke ek aise vyapak pratibimb ke rup main pahachaanaa jaataa he jisamen puraatatva se lekar nrity or maanav vigyaan se lekar chayachitran tak kaa, poorak tathaa anant drishy ke rup main samavesh ho.
 is centre kii chah kaaryaatmak ikaiyaan hai- 
 indira gandhi rashtriya kala centre kaa ek dakshin kshetreey centre he jiskaa mukhyalay bangalore main he. isaki sthapana 2001 main hui thi or iskaa mukhya uddeshy dakshini kshetra kii kala or saanskrithik viraasat se sambandhit gahan adhyayan karnaa he.
