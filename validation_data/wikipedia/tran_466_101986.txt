 timi yaa hwel samudron main rahane vaala ek standhari praani he jise jeev-vaigyanik vargeekaran ke nazariye se siteshya ke gana main shaamil kiya jaataa he. hwel aksar bhimkaay aakaar ke hote hai or sabhi stanadhariyon kii tarah ve saans keval vaayu main le sakate hai . whelon ke siron par ek saans lene kaa chhed hota he or vah samay-samay par pani kii sataha par aakar is se saans khinchate hai. neeli timi vishwa kaa sabase bada gyaat jaanavar he or yah haathiyon or pracheen dinosauron se kai guna bada aakaar rakhata he. neeli timi 30 meter lambi or 180 ton kaa vazan rakh sakati he jabaki pigmi sperm timi jaisi timi kii choti prajatiyaan keval 3.5 meter kii hi hoti he.
 whelen duniya bhar ke samudron or mahasaagaron main basti hai or inaki sankhya lakhon main anumaanit he lekin 20veen sadi main inkaa audyogic paimaane par shikaar hone se inaki bahut si jatiyon par hameshaa ke liye vilupt hone kaa sankat mandaraane laga tha. uske baad bahut se deshon main whelon ke shikaar par pabandi lagaai jis se is khatare se ubharne main kuch madad mili he.
