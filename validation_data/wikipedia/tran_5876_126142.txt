 dashoguz praant turkmenistan kii ek vilaayat he. yah us desh ke uttar main sthit he or isaki sarahadein uzbekistan se lagati hai. is praant kaa kshetrafal 73,430 kimi2 he or san 2005 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 13,70,400 anumaanit kii gai thi. dashoguz praant kii rajdhani kaa naam bhi dashoguz shahar he, jise pehle dashakovuz bulaayaa jaataa tha.
 dashoguz praant ek 'band seemaayi ilaaka' he, yani yahan aane-jane ke liye turkmenistan kii sarkaar se vishesh ijazat kii aavashyaktaa padati he. yah praant adhiktar ek registani kshetra he jahaan aral sagar ke adhiktar suukh jane se ek paryaavarniya vipadaa kii sthiti ban gai he. yahan zamin ke khaare ho jane se hazaron varg kimi kii zaminein banjar pad gai hai. isi praant main pracheen urgench puraatan-sthal bhi he jo 12veen sadi main khwaaresm rajya kii rajdhani hua karata tha. dashoguz praant ke uzbekistan ke sameep hone ke kaaran yahan ek bada alpsankhyak uzbek samudaay bhi rahata he.
