 hastshilp niryaat sanvardhan parishad bharat sarkaar kii ek sanstha he, yah bharat ke hastshilp niryaatkon ko protsaahan tathaa hastshilp niryaat se sambandhit jaankariyaan tathaa suvidhaain muhaiyaa karaati he.
 hastshilp niryaat sanvardhan parishad bharat sarkaar ke vastra mantralay ke adheen ek swytt sanstha he. is parishad kii sthapana 1986-87 main hui thi. vartmaan mukhya karyaalay dakshin dilli ke vasant kunj naamak sthaan ke vyavsaayik kendra main sthit he. is niryaat sanvardhan parishad kaa mukhya uddeshy bharat ke hastshilp niryaatkon ke protsaahan tathaa samay-samay par niryaat sambandhi jaankariyaan tathaa suvidhaain muhaiyaa karana he.
 bharat ke hastshilp niryaatak ko prastut sanstha kaa sadsy banane ke liye parishad dwaara nirdhaarit kuch maanakon ko puraa karnaa hota he. tadoparaant jaach padtaal purna hone par sadsyta milti he. prativarsh sadsyta shulk tathaa samay samay par anya nirdhaarit shulk dey hote hai.
 hastshilp niryaat sanvardhan parishad samay-samay par bharat main hastshilp niryaat sambandhi pradarshaniyon kaa aayojan karte rahati he. jinamein mukhya chaar pradarshaniyon kaa aayojan dilli main hota tha, parantu pichley kuch varshon se hastshilp niryaat sanvardhan parishad ke swanirmit pradarshnee sthal greater noida main hone laga he. in pradarshaniyon se sambandhit sabhi jaankaree parishad yatha samay hastshilp niryaatkon ko patrachaar dwaara suchit karte rahati he.
 yah parishad samay-samay par bharat ke hastshilp niryaatkon ko antardeshiy tathaa anterrashtriya pradarshaniyon me bhaag lene ko utsahit tathaa uchit suvidhaae pradaan karti he.
