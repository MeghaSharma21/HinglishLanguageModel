 dim sanyukt rajya america kaa ek sikaa he jisaki qeemat 10 saint hoti he. ek america dollar main 10 dim hote hai. jitne bhi sikke aadhunik yug main america main prachlit hai unamem dim sabase patala or chote aakaar kaa sikaa he.
 "dim" shabd puraani francisi bhashaa ke "disme" shabd se aata he jise "dime" uccharit kiya jaataa tha. "dime" kaa arth he "dasva hissaa".
 1783 main thomas jafarasan, benjamin franklin, alccounder hamilatan or david retainhouse ne sanyukt rajya main dashamalav par aadhaarit mudra behaal karne kaa pehla prastaav rakhaa. hamilatan sanyukt rajya ke pehle khazaane ke sachiv the or unhone 1791 main us prastaav ko aage badhaate hue america congress main chah aise dashamalav par aadhaarit sikke gadhane kii baat rakhi, jinamein se ek dim tha. 1796 se 1837 tak, dim ke sikke 89.24% chaandi or 10.76% tambe ke bane hote the. chaandi mahangaa hone kii vajah se yah sikke chote akar ke hote the, varanaa kuch log sikkon ko pighaal kar unki dhaatu bechney kaa gair-kanoonee kaam karne lagate. 1837 main chaandi kii taadaad badha kar 90% or tambe kii gata kar 10% kar di gayi or dim kaa aakaar or bhi chota kar diya gaya . 1965 ke baad dim se chaandi hataa di gayi. tab se dim kaa andruni hissaa shuddh tambe kaa hota he jiske upar ek 75% tambe or 25% nikal kii mishrit parat baithai jaati he.
