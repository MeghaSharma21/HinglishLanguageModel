 dhan ke beej ko chaaval kehete hai. yah dhan se upar kaa chhilka hataane se praapt hota he. chaaval sampoorn purvi jagat main pramukh rup se khaae jane vaala anaaj he. bharat main bhaat, khichdi sahit kaaphi saare pakvann banate hai. chaaval kaa chalan dakshin bharat or purvi-dakshini bharat main uttar bharat se adhik he. 
 ise sanskrit main 'tandul' kaha jaataa he or tamil main 'arisi' kaha jaataa he.
 ise kabhi-kabhaar 'shadras' bhi kaha jaataa he, kyonki main swaad ke chhahon pramukh ras maujuud hai. saanskrithik hindi main pake hue chaaval ko bhaat kaha jaataa he, kintu adhiktar hindi bhashi 'bhaat' shabd kaa prayog kam hi karte hai. chaaval kii fasal ko dhan kehete hai. basmati chaaval bharat kaa prasiddh chaaval jo videshon ko niryaat bhi kiya jaataa he.
 chaaval main naa sirf fibre maujuud hota he balki isamen vitamin, calcium, aayran, thymin or minerals jaise poshak tatv samaae hote he
