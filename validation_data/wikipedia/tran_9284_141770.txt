 brahmi lipi evam usase vyutpann adhikansh lipiyon kii varnmaala ke prayek varg ke paanchaven varnon ke samooh ko panchamakshar yaa panchamakshar kehete hai . devanagari main ng, na, na, na tathaa m panchamakshar hai. 
 vibhinn bhashaaon main panchamaaksharon kaa prayog hota he. sabhi bhashaaon main paychamaksharon ke prayog ke apane-apane niyam yaa paripati he. 
 hindi main takneekee tankan yantron kii samasyaon se nikaalane ke liye taatkaalik rup se panchamaaksharon ke ardhswaroop ke sthaan par anuswaar kaa upyog karke kaam chalaa lene kaa upaay sujhaayaa gaya tha jiske falswaroop samasamyik hindi lekhan main panchamakshar ke sthaan par anuswaar ko variyata di jane lagi he. saamanyatya anuswaar kii dhvanyaatmakataa ko spasht karne ke liye pancham varna - 'kavarg', 'chavarg', 'tavarg', 'tavarg' evam 'pavarg' ke ji, ji, n, n evam m- kaa vyavhaar kiya jaataa he. jaise- ganga- ganga, pancham - pancham, kanth - kanth, kandha - kandhaa, kampan- kampan aadi. 
 jahaan tak hindi kaa sambandh he, tankan yantron par devanagari ke upyog ko sugam banane ke liye kuch sthitiyon main panchamaaksharon ke sthaan par anuswaar likhakar kaam nikaalane kaa suzav diya gaya tha. kintu aisa nahin he ki panchamaaksharon kaa prayog varjit kiya gaya tha, yaa panchamaaksharon ke sthaan par anuswaar kaa prayog adhik shuddh ghoshit kiya gaya tha. 
 kuch sthitiyon main subhita ke liye panchamaaksharon ke sthaan par anuswaar ke prayog se sambandhit kuch suzav diye gayi the ve nimnlikhit hai. varnmaala ke vibhinn virgon ke pariprekshya main inke prayog main parivartan sambhav he. jaise- 
 kavarg main ji kaa prayog k, kha, ga, gha ke purv hota he. haalanki 'paranmukh' or 'vaanmay' aadi kuch shabdon main apwaad swaroop 'ji' kaa hi prayog miltaa he. yahan kaa prayog nahin kiya ja saktaa. ng vyanjan shabd ke aadi tathaa ant main nahin aata. iske anusaar vishisht dhvanyatmak prayogon ko chhodkar is varg ke liye anuswaar kaa prayog kiya ja saktaa he.
 chavarg main ji kaa prayog c, chha, ja, jha ke purv hota he. jaise- anchal, panchhi, anjan evam jhanjhat aadi. haalanki na kaa prayog hindi lekhan main praayah nahin ho rahaa he. inke sthaan par ab anuswaar kaa hi prayog ho rahaa he. ji bhi shabd ke aadi yaa ant main nahin aata.
 tavarg main n kaa prayog t, tha, da, dha ke purv hota he. jaise- ghanta, kanth, anda aadi. ab inke sthaan par praayah anuswaar kaa prayog hota he. swatantra rup se na kaa prayog keval sanskrit ke shabdon main hota he. vah bhi madhya or ant main. tavarg ke atirikt ya,va evam na ke purv bhi na aata he, kintu aisi sthiti main iske sthaan par anuswaar nahin aata. jaise- punya, kanv evam vishann.
 tavarg main n kaa prayog t, tha, da, dha ke purv hota he. jaise- jantar, manther, tandoor, gandh aadi. ab inke sthaan par bhi anuswaar ke prayog kii anushansa kii jaati he. jaise- ant, panth, anand evam gandhak.
 pavarg main m kaa prayog pa, pha, ba, bha ke purv hota he. jaise- sampark, gumphit, ambar, gambheer aadi. ab inke sthaan par bhi ji, ji, n evam n kii tarah hi praayah anuswaar kaa prayog hota he. jaise- sampark, gunfit, ambar evam gambhir.
 hindi shabdanushasan kii rachana karne vaale kishori das vaajapeyi kaa maananaa he ki panchamakshar anunasik alpapraan hai. unke anusaar hindi ke rup gathan main 'ng', 'na' tathaa 'na' kaa koi yoga nahin he. jo mithaas 'na' tathaa 'm' main he, vah in teenon main nahin he. isiilie hindi ne 'na' tathaa 'm' ko hi apanaaya he. sanskrit shabd jo hindi main prayukt hote hai, un main hi ye aate hai - 'vaanmay', 'chaanchalya', 'panditya' aadi. vaajapeyi ji kaa maananaa he ki anuswaar kaa sthaan bhi nasika he. sanskrit vyakaran ke anusaar 'm' praayah or 'n' kabhi-kabhi anuswaar hua karata he. yahi kaaran he ki anuswaar lagane se bhi swar-vyanjan madhur dhwani dene lagate hai - 'kankan kinkini noopur dhuni suni'. jaisaa 'na' vaisaa hi anuswaar madhur.
