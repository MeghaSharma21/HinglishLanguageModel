 shopping mal, shopping centre yaa shopping parisar ek yaa adhik aise bhawan hai jo vyapaariyon kaa pratinidhitiv karne vaale dukaanon ke complex kaa rup dhaaran karte hai, jisamen parking kshetra ke saath ek ikai se duusari ikai main aasaani se chal kar jane ke liye raste hote hai - paaramparik bazaar kaa ek aadhunik, bhitari sanskaran.
 1920 ke dashak se hi aadhunik "car-anukool" upkhand mal viksit hue or dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke baad, pashchimi duniya ke kai bhaagon main, vishesh rup se sanyukt rajya america main, upnagar jivan kii vruddhi ke anukool shopping mal ubhare. prarambh se hi, dizine main andruni-rukh rahaa, jisamen maalon ne is siddhant kaa paalan kiya ki graahakon ko niyantrit parivesh main kitni acchhee tarah aakarshith kiya ja saktaa he. isi tarah, mal main ek yaa adhik "lungar" yaa "big box" ke dukaanon ne bahut pehle hi maarg prashast kiya, jahaan vyaktigat dukaane yaa chote paimaane ke shrinkhalaa-dukaanon ko badi dukaanon ke prati aakarshith kharidaaron se labhanvit hona abhipret tha.
 vishwa ke anek bhaagon main shopping centre naam prayukt hota he, vishesh rup se europe, australesia or dakshin america main; lekin uttari america main mukhya rup se shopping mal kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. uttari america ke baahar, shopping prisinct or shopping arched kaa bhi istemaal hota he. uttari america main saamanytah shopping mal shabd band vistrut imaaraton ko lagu hota he, jabaki saamaanya taur par shopping centre khule vistrut complex ko sandarbhit karata he; donon prakaar kii suvidhaon main aam taur par vishaal parking uplabdh hoti he, ye pramukh yatayaat maarg kii ore abhimukh hote hai or ados-pados main avaagaman ke liye main bahut kam padchaari se jude hote hai.
 united kingdom main kharidari ke kendron ko "shopping centre", "shopping parisar" yaa "toun centre" ke rup main sandarbhit kiya ja saktaa he. shabd "mal" kaa maanak british uccharan, buckingham pales, landan tak jane vaale vriksh-maarg - "The Mall, London" or "pal" ke samaan he.mal, shopping mal ko nirdisht kar saktaa he - yani ek aisi jagah jahaan sabhi dukaane ek padchaari kshetra se judi hoti he - yaa phir vishesh rup se ek aisi paidal sadak se, jisamen sabhi kharidaaron ko vaahanon ke yatayaat kii baadha ke bina chalane main suvidha ho. uttari america main mal aam taur par ek vishaal shopping kshetra ko sandarbhit karata he jisamen ek hi imaarat main kai dukaane hoti hai, saamanytah ek yaa anek department stores dwaara "ashrith" jo parking sthal se ghira ho, jabaki shabd arched kaa upyog, vishesh rup se britain main, sankeern paidal chalane waalon ke maarg ko nirdisht karata he, jo saamanytah dhake yaa bahut kam antaraal vaale bhawanon ke bich hote hai . ek bada, aksar aanshik rup se dhakaa hua or vishesh rup se paidal yatriyon ke liye kharidari ilaaka britain main he jise shopping centre, shopping prisinct yaa pedestrian prisinct bhi kaha jaataa he.

 jab 1950 dashak ke madhya main victor gruen dwaara shopping mal praaroop viksit kiya gaya, pariyojna kii vittiya sthirataa or mal ke anya chote dukaanon par bhi jane vaale khudara awajahi ko aakarshith karne ke liye, bade department store ke saath anubandh anivaary tha. ye bade bhandar ankar store yaa dra tenant kehalaate hai. ankaron ko aam taur par unke kiraye main baari chhot milti he or mal ko khulaa rakhane ke liye nakad pralobhan bhi praapt kar sakate hai. bhautik vinyas main, aashray dukaane saamaanya rup se jahaan tak sambhav ho ek duusare se kaafii dur sthit hote hai, taaki ek ankar se duusare ankar tak adhiktam awajahi sambhav ho sake.
 aksar, shopping mal yaa shopping centre kii zamin par hi yaa uske saath lage hue anushanbee bhawan avasthit honge, jin par ekal bhandar rahenge, jo kisi anubandh yaa swaamitv ke zarie kendriya suvidha se kanoonee taur par jude ho sakate hai yaa nahin bhi ho sakate hai. in dukaanon ke paas swayan apni parking jagah hogi, yaa unki parking mal yaa centre ke saath aapas main judi ho sakati he. ekal bhandar kaa astitv kii yojana mal ke vikaasak dwaara banaai gai hogi, yaa anya logon dwaara avasarwaadi karrwai ke madhyam se maujuud ho sakati he, lekin dekhne main kendriya suvidha - mal yaa shopping centre - or anushanbee bhawanon ko aksar ek hi "ekak" mana ja saktaa he, aisi paristhitiyon bhi jahaan duurastha imaaraten sarkari taur par yaa kanoonee taur par mal se kisi bhi tarah judi naa ho.
