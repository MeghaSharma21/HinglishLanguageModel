 galtundika maanav ke gale ke pichley hisse main sthit ek ang he. yah ek prakaar kaa utak hai jo bahari rogaanuon se akraman ke viruddh pehali raksha-pankti kii tarah kaam karata he.
 galaa hamaare sharir kaa ek mahatvapurn ang he. iskaa mahatv isi baat se spasht he ki sharir ke bheetar pahunchane vaale khaadya−padaarth tathaa hawa−pani ke pravesh kaa daayitv isi par he. isi mahatvapurn kaary ko nibhaane ke kaaran yahi hissaa hamaare dwaara grahan kiye jane vaale bhojan, jal tathaa vaayu main upasthit kisi bhi jahrile tatv se sabase pehle prabhavit hota he. naak or kaan bhi isse achoote nahin rahete. haalanki bahari rup se dekhne se hamaare naak, galaa va kaan alag−alag dikhaai dete hai parantu gale ke bheetar jaakar in teenon angon kii koshikaayen aapas main mil jaati hai. is kaaran in teenon angon main se kisi ek bhi ang main kisi prakaar kaa sankraman ho jaataa he to uskaa prabhaav teenon angon par padataa he. is kaaran jaruri he ki koi bhi sankraman hote hi jaldi se uskaa upachaar kiya jaae.
 bahari vaataavaran main upasthit kisi bhi jahrile tatv ke sankraman se in angon ko bachaane ke liye prakruti ne mukh tathaa gale kii sanrachana is prakaar kii he ki un jahrile tatwon ko hamaare munh ke andruni tantra par koi prabhaav na pade. bahari rup main dekhne par naak va mukh ek sampoorn ang ke rup main hi dikhaai dete hai, unke alag−alag hisse dikhaai nahin dete. parantu vastav main yah ang kai hisson main bante hote hai. haalanki nitalacavity marottege or netleceptor dekhne main to alag−alag dikhaai dete hai parantu peeche jaakar gale kii andar aapas main ek cavity main mil jate hai jise 'oropheringells' kehete hai. oropherigles ke upar naak ke peeche ke bhaag ko 'lejopheringles' kehete hai.
 tonsil kii samasya kaa kaaran kisi bhi prakaar kaa sankraman yaa infections ho saktaa he jo hamaare sharir main mukh va naak se pravesh kar rahaa ho. ye pradooshan yaa infections viral yaa bacterial kisi bhi prakaar ke pradooshak se dooshit hawa saans ke madhyam se jab sharir main pravesh kar jaati he to yah turant samasya paidaa kar sakati he. aise sthaan jahaan ke vaataavaran main badboo, sillen tathaa anya kisi rasinic tatv kaa samavesh ho aise sthanon main vaayu ke dwaara nirantar jahrile tatwon ke sampark main aane ke kaaran tonsil rog kii samasya khadi ho sakati he.
 bacchon main is rog ke zyada maamle drushtigat hote hai kyonki unki rog pratirohak kshamta kam hoti he. aisa nahin ki badi umr ke log isse grasit nahin ho sakate unamem bhi yah samasya dekhi ja sakati he. tonsillitis is rog kii ek khatarnaak avasthaa hoti he kyonki sharir main antibadis hone ke kaaran pratikriya hoti rahati he. is avasthaa main jaise hi antigen hamaare sharir main pravesh karata he vaise hi tonsil tishyu usase ladanaa shuru kar dete hai isse jo antigen banataa he vah sharir ke anya bhaagon main upasthit protein ke saath pratikriya karke vahaan bhi isi prakaar ke antibadis bana detaa he. in antibadis kaa dil ke volve par sidhaa prabhaav padataa he tathaa dil ke volve kharaab hone kaa khatra paidaa ho jaataa he. tonsil to zyaadaatar bacchon ko hota he parantu iskaa dil par padane vaala dushprabhav kisi bhi umr main pad saktaa he. bachapan main gale ke sankraman kaa uchit upachaar na hona bhi hamaare desh main dil kii bimariyon kaa ek kaaran he. iske atirikt tonsil se bairaphengills epashish, neutrapheringles epashish jaisi bimaariyaan bhi ho sakati hai.
 sharir kii yantric sanrachana main vifaille tatwon ko gadabadi ke kaaran paidaa hone vaali is samasya kii pahchaan kuch praarambhik lakshan dekhakar kii ja sakati he jaise− baar-baar galaa kharaab hona, gale main sujan hona, dard hona, baar−baar bukhaar aana aadi. yadi aise hi lakshan rogi ko baar−baar ho rahe hon. jaise ek varsh main paanch yaa chah baar to use tonsil kaa aaparishan karvaa lenaa chahie. lager aaparishan main do se teen ghante kaa samay lagata he or bilkul cheer−faad nahin hoti. tonsil se bachaav ke liye jaruri he ki bacchon ko santulit bhojan diya jaae taaki unke sharir kii rog pratirohak kshamta majboot ho. sabase jaruri to yah he ki tonsil ke lakshan dikhte hi turant doctor se sampark karen. doctor dwaara die gaye dawaai ke course ko puraa karnaa chahie. aksar log thodaa sa aaraam milte hi dawaai band kar dete hai parantu aisa karnaa rog ko or adhik ugr bana detaa he. is rog ke dauraan rogi ko khaanaa−pani nigalane main takalif hoti he. aise main bahut se log kam khate hai, yah thik nahin he. rogi ke sharir kii aavashyaktaaon kii purti naram yaa taral khaadya padaarth dekar kii jari chahie. isse sharir kii rog pratirohak kshamta bani rahati he.
