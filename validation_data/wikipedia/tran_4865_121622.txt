 flipcart bharat kii ek i-commers company he. iskaa mukhyalay bangalore main sthit he. isaki sthapana san 2007 main bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan dilli ke snaatak sachin bansal or bini bansal dwaara kii gai thi. multah pustakon kii online kharid-bikri liye bani yah website ab apane graahakon ko electronic upkaran or anya vasteen kharidne kaa vikalp bhi detii he. flipcart par credit card, debit card, net banking, i-gift vauchar or supurdagee par nakad adaayagi ke zariye bhugtan kiya ja saktaa he.
 flipcard ne apane utpaada 'digiflip' naam se bechnaa shuru kiya he jisamen camera-bag, pen-drive,computer tathaa headphone ke saamaan aadi hai.
 09 may 2018 ko america kii bahurashtriya khudara company walmart ne flipcart kii 77 fisadi hissedari, 1.07 lakh karod rupaye main kharid kar uskaa adhigrahan kar liya he. south africa kii internet or antertenment company nasapurs ne bhi flipcart main apni kul 11.18 fisadi hissedari ko walmart ko bech diya he. nasapurs ne yah sauda 14,740 karod rupee main kiya he.
