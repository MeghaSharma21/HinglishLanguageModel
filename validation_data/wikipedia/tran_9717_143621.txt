 bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan, khadgpur bharat sarkaar dbaara 1951 main sthapit abhiyantriki or praudyogiki -unmukh ek swytt ucch shiksha santhaan he. saat iaiti main yah sabase puraani he. bharat sarkaar ne ise aadhikarik taur par rashtriya mahatva kaa santhaan mana he or isaki gananaa bharat ke sarvottam ejineering sansthaanon main hoti he. ai ai tii khadgpur ko vibhinn ejineering shiksha surveykshanon jaise ki india today or outluk main sarvocch ejineering collejon main se ek kaa sthaan diya gaya he.
 1947 main bharat kii swadhinata ke baad ai ai tii khadgpur kii sthapana ucch koti ke vaigyanikon or ejiniyaron ko prasekshit karne ke liye hui thi. iskaa sansthagat dhaancha duusari IIToM kii hi tarah he or isaki pravesh kii vidhi bhi baaki IIToM ke saath hi hoti he. ai ai tii khadgpur ke chaatro ko anaupchaarik taur par kegepian kaha jaataa he. sabhi IIToM main iskaa campus kshetrafal sabase zyada he or saath hi vibhaag or chaatro kii sankhya bhi sarvaadhik he. ai ai tii khadgpur, illumination, rangoli, kshitij or springfest jaise apane vaarshik utsavon ke kaaran janaa jaataa he. 
 bharat main yudhoparant audyogic vikaas hetu ucchtar takneekee sansthaanon ki sthapana ke liye do bhartiya shikshavidon humayun kabir or jogendra singh ne 1946 main tatkaaleen pashchim bengal ke mukhyamantri bidhan chandra roy kii madad se ek kamity kaa gathan kiya. iske baad nalini ranjan sarkaar ki aguwai main 22-sadasyeey ek kamity kaa gathan hua. apane antarim report main kamity main desh ke vibhinn bhaagon main masachusets praudyogiki santhaan kii tarz par sambaddh duusare darje ke sansthaanon ke saath ucchtar takneekee sansthaanon ki sthapana kaa prastaav rakhaa. report main desh ke chaar bhaagon main pramukh sansthaanon ki jald sthapana ke liye kaary aarambh karne par jor diya gaya, saath hi yah bhi kaha gaya ki purv or pashchim main sansthaanon kii sthapana atisheeghra honi chahie.
 us vaqt pashchim bengal main udyogon ke sarvaadhik kendrikaran kii daleel dete hue bidhan chandra roy ne bharat ke pradhanmantri jawaharlal nehru ko is baat ke liye raji kar liya ki pehle santhaan kii sthapana pashchim bengal main hi ho. is prakaar pehle bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan ki sthapana may 1950 main "Eastern Higher Technical Institute" ke naam se hui. .armbh main santhaan kolkata ke purvi asplaned main sthit tha or sitamabar 1950 main apane sthaayee campus kolkata se 120 kimi dakshin purv main hijli, khadgpur main visthapit kiya gaya. jab agast 1951 main pehla satr armbh hua, tab santhaan main 224 chaatr or 10 vibhaagon main 42 shikshak the. sari kakshaen, prayogshaalaayein or prashasnic karyaalay aitihaasik hijli karawas shivi main sthit the jahaan ki angreji shashan kaal main rajanithik krantikarion ko bandi bana kar rakhaa jaataa tha or praan dand diya jaataa tha. is karyaalay ke bhawan main hi dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke vaqt U.S. 20th Air Force kaa mukhyalay bhi tha. 
 18 agast 1951 ko moulana abul kalam azaad ke dwaara aupchaarik udghaatan se purv "bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan" kaa naam grahan kiya gaya tha. 15 sitambar 1956 ko bhartiya sansad ne "bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan adhinium" paarit kar diya jiske tahat santhaan ko "rashtriya mahatv ke santhaan" kaa darja mila.1956 main pradhanmantri nehru ne santhaan ke pehle dikshant abhibhaashan main kaha:
 shahid bhawan ko 1990 main ek sangrahalay ke rup main parivartit kar diya gaya.shrinivas ramanujan parisar ko ek nae shaikshanik parisar ke taur par shaamil kiya gaya jahaan takshashila ne 2002 or vikramshila ne 2003 main kaam karnaa prarambh kiya.
