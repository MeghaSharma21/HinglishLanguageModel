 di oriental insurance company limited kaa aarambh 12 sitambar 1947 ko bombay main hua. company purnat: di oriental insurance government security lif insurance company limited kii sahaayak company thi tathaa saamaanya beemaa kaarobaar karne ke liye banaai gayi thi. 1956 se 1973 tak company bhartiya jivan beemaa nigam kii ek sahaayak company thi. 2003 main bhartiya saadhaaran beemaa nigam ke paas rakhe sabhi share kendriya sarkaar ko antarit kar diye gayi hai. 
 yah company britisho dwaara kalakatta main san 1818 main sthapit huyin bharat kii pehali acemev aadhunik jivan beemaa company hai. kaarobaar ko sahaj evam sucharu rup se chalaane ke liye yojanaayen taiyaar karne main company agrani he. company ke paas purnat: prashikshit evam prerak kaarydal he jo ki vividh kshetron main karyrat he tathaa nipun he. power plants, patrochemicals, steel evam chemical plant jaise vrihad prujecton ke liye vishesh aavaran pradaan karne main oriental vishisht he. company ne bharat kii gramin evam shahari donon prakaar kii jansankhya kii aavashyaktaaon ko puraa karne ke liye vibhinn prakaar ke beemaa aavaran viksit kiye hai. behatar graahak seva pradaan karne ke liye company ke paas ucchtam takneekee yogyata praapt evam sakasham vyavsaayikon kii ek team he. varsh 1950 main 99,946 rupaye kaa pratham premium arjeet karke oriental insurance ne shubharambh kiya. company kaa mukhya uddeshy tha "graahakon ko seva pradaan karnaa" tathaa ise praapt karne main hamen samay-samay par banaayi gayi sudrudh paramparaon se kaaphi sahyog mila. oriental ke nai dilli sthit pradhan karyaalay sahit desh ke vibhinn shaharon main 21 kshetreey karyaalay, va 950 se adhik prachaalan karyaalay hai. . nepal, kuwait or dubai main company ke videshi karyaalay hai. company ke karmchaariyon kii kul sankhya lagbhag 16000 he. aarambh main jo premium 1 lakh rupaye se bhi kam tha, 1973 main vah badhkar 58 karod rupaye ho gaya tathaa varsh 2003-04 ke dauraan ye aankade 2899.74 karod kii oonchi rakam tak pahunch gayi.
