 sanyukt arab emirate madhyapurv asia main sthit ek desh he. san 1873 se 1947 tak yah british bharat ke adheen rahaa. uske baad iskaa shashan landan ke videsh vibhaag se sanchalit hone laga. 1971 main faras kii khadi ke saat shaikh rajyon abu dhabi, sharajah, dubai, umm al kuwin, ajman, fujirah tathaa ras al khema ko milakar swatantra sanyukt arab emirate kii sthapana hui. isamen raas al khema 1972 main shaamil hua.
 19veen sadi main united kingdom or anek arab shekhon ke bich hui sandhi kii vajah se 1971 se pehle sanyukt arab emirate ko yudhviram sandhi rajya ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. iske alaava kshetra ke emirate kii vajah se 18veen shatabdi se lekar 20veen shatabdi ke shuruyaat tak ise pyret coast ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa tha. 1971 ke samvidhan ke aadhaar par sanyukt arab emirate kii rajnatik vyavastha aapas main jude kai prabandhkeey nikaayon se milakar bani he. islam desh kaa rashtriya dharm; or arabi rashtriya bhashaa he. tel bhandar ke maamle main duniya kaa chhathvaan sabase bada desh sanyukt arab emirate kii arthvyavastha madhyapurv main sabase viksit he.
