 oxford uniwarsity cricket club, jo oxford vishvavidhyalay kaa pratinidhitiv karata he, ne hameshaa mahatvapurn yaa pratham shrenee kii sthiti yojit kii he or ise 1827 se 1894 tak paryaapt sroton dwaara ek mahatvapurn team ke rup main vargeekrut kiya gaya he; 1895 se marillebon cricket club or county championships club dwaara aadhikarik pratham shrenee kii team ke rup main vargeekrut; or keval 1973 main ek list e team ke rup main vargeekrut kiya gaya.
 home phiksture uniwarsity park main kendriya oxford ke uttar main khele jate hai. ousisi or cambridge uniwarsity cricket club ke bich udghaatan vishvavidhyalay match 1827 main khelaa gaya tha or ab prayek satr main club kaa ekmaatr pratham shrenee kaa muqabla he. is vaarshik khel ke alaava, june ke ant main yaa july kii shuruyaat main, ousisi oxford uniwarsity centre of cricketting excilence ke hisse ke rup main kaam karata he, jisamen oxford brookes vishvavidhyalay shaamil he. 2010 sijan se pehle usisii ko oxford emsisi vishvavidhyalay ke rup main dobaaraa baanta gaya tha. uniwarsity match ekmaatr aisa he jisamen ek vaastavik ousisi team bhaag leti he: yani, vartmaan oxford ke chaatro se puri tarah taiyaar he.
 oxford main cricket kaa sabase pehla sandarbh 1673 main he. ousisi ne 1827 vishvavidhyalay match main apni pehali shuruyaat kii. maujuuda klbon ke shaamil hone ke maamle main, yah duniya main sabase purana pramukh muqabla he: yani, haalanki kuch antar-county phiksture bahut puraane hai, 1839 se pehle vartmaan county klbon main se koi bhi sthapit nahin kiya gaya tha .
 oxford uniwarsity centre of cricketting excilence team ne 2001 se 2009 tak 26 pratham shrenee ke maichon khelaa. oxford marillebon cricket club vishvavidhyalay ke rup main, team ne 2010 se 2016 tak solah pratham shrenee ke maichon main khelaa he.
