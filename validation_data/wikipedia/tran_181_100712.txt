 
 arabi: عثمان بن عفان‎
 usmaan bin affaan umr bin khattab ke baad musalmaanon ke tisare caliph chune gayi. pehle wo ek dhani vyapaaree hua karte the lekin baad main vah mohammad sahib ke damaad or unke pramukh saathi bane. apane khaliph banane ke baad unhone quran ko sankalit kar pustak kaa rup diya. is dharmik pustak main milawat ke aarope main or misr ke vaasiyon se dawa karne ke aarope main 80 saal kii umr main unka madina main bheed ne qatl kar diya. in aaropon ko itihaas main vivaadit mana jaataa he, lekin pramanit nahin. khaas kar shiya muslim in aaropon par adhik vishwaas karte hai.
 apane dhan ke kaaran unako al-gani bhi kehete hai - inke pita makka ke prabhaavshaalee vyapaaree the, jabaki ye khud kaarobaari silsile main ithiopia main rahane lage the. inke parivaar ke anya sadasyon ne agle 70 saalon ke islamic samrajya-vistaar main mahatvapurn bhoomika adaa kii. baad ke umayya vansh ke shaasha inke vansh se sambandhit the. kukhyat arab general abu sufiyaan, or baad main spain main raaj karne vaale musa bhi inke koon ke rishtedaar the.
 hazrat umr, apane maut ke vaqt par, agle khalipha ko apane bich main se chunane ke liye chah logon kii ek samiti kaa gathan kiya. yah samiti thi:
 hazrat umr ne kaha ki, unki mrityu ke baad, samiti teen dinon ke bheetar ek antim nirnay le, or agle caliph ko chauthe din karyaalay kii shapath leni chahie. talha is avadhi ke bheetar samiti main shaamil ho gaye, to ve vichaar-vimarsh main hissaa lenaa chaahate the, lekin ve is avadhi ke bheetar madina waapas nahin aae, to samiti ke anya sadsy is faisle se aage bad sakate the. abdur rahamaan bin ouf ne madhyasth ke rup main kaary karne ke liye khalipha ke rup main niyukt kiye jane ke liye apni paatrata waapas le lee or samiti ke prayek sadsy se alag se mulaakaat karke apana kaary shuru kiya. unhone unase poocha ki kiske liye ve apana vot denge. jab hazrat ali se poocha gaya, unhone jawaab nahin diya. jab hazrat uthman se poocha gaya, unhone hazrat ali ke liye matdaan kiya, jubair ne ali yaa uthman ke liye kaha or saad ne uthman ke liye kaha.
