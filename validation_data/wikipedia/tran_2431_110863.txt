 princep ghat yaa princep ghat british raaj ke dauraan bharat ke kolkata shahar main hughali nadi ke kinare par san 1841 main nirmit ek ghat he. ghat par san 1843 main prakhyat angl-bhartiya vidwaan or puratatvavid james princep kii smriti main dablyoo fijrald dwaara dizin kiye gayi ek paladian osaare kaa nirmaan bhi kiya gaya tha.
 yah fort wiliam ke water get or saint jorge get ke bich sthit he. princep ke is smarak main unani or gothic style kaa prayog kiya gaya he. november 2001 main rajya ke lok nirmaan vibhaag dwaara is smarak kaa punaruddhar kiya gaya or tab se iskaa anurakshan uchit rup se kiya ja rahaa he. nirmaan ke praarambhik varshon main, sabhi shahi british muhasire arohan or avarohan ke liye princep ghat ghat kaa istemaal kiya karte the.
 princep ghat kolkata ke sabase puraane manoranjan sthalon main se ek he. saptahanth main log shaam ke samay yahan nadi main nauka vihaar karne, nadi kinare tahalne or yahan uplabdh bhojan kaa anand uthaane ke liye aate hai. yahan sthit ek icecream or fast food stall to pichley 40 se bhi adhik se yahan karyrat he. princep ghat or babughat ke bich ke 2 kilometre lambe soundaryikrut naditat kaa udghaatan 24 may 2012 ko kiya gaya. yahan par roshni se jagamagaate sundar bageeche, raste, fawwaare or punarnirmit ghat sthit hai. hindi film parinita ke ek gane ko yahan filmaaya gaya tha.
 princep ghat ke naam par ek railway stesan kaa naam bhi rakhaa gaya he. yah stesan kolkata circular railway kaa hissaa he jiskaa anurakshan purvi railway dwaara kiya jaataa he. stesan code PPGT he.
 yahan paas hi main-o-waar naam kii ek jetty bhi he jo kolkata port trust ke antargat aati he or bandargah dwaara dwitiya vishwa yuddh main nibhaai gayi isaki bhoomika kii yaad dilaati he. ghat ko mukhya rup se bhartiya nausena dwaara prayog kiya jaataa he.
