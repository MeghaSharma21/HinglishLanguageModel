 bhartiya aayudh nirmaaniyan ek bhavy audyogic sanrachana hai jo raksha mantralay ke raksha utpaadan vibhaag ke antargat kaary karti hai. bhartiya aayudh nirmaaniyan jiskaa mukhyalay kolkata main he, vah 39 nirmaaniyon, 9 prashikshan santhaan, 3 kshetreey vipanan kendra ore 4 kshetreey sangraksha niyantranaalayon kaa samooh he.aaj aayudh nirmaani board bharatvarsh main faili 39 nirmaaniyon ke saath pradaan karata he :-
 bharatvarsh main 39 aayudh nirmaaniyan 24 vibhinn bhugolic sthanon par sthit hai. hamaari nirmaaniyan evam mukhyalay vibhinn sthanon main kahan par sthit hai, unhen maanchitr main dekh sakate hai.

 chandigarh 	
 40 veen nirmaani kii sthapana bihar ke nalanda main ho rahi he.
 aayudh nirmaaniyon main utpaadit saamagree kaaphi parishkrut evam jatil prakruti kii hai. utpaadon kii shreniyan saamanyat: ascharyajanak he, jaisaa ki nimnlikhit suchi se spasht he. hamaare utpaada kisi bhi sashastr sena ke brihat evam sarvangpurn aavashyaktaa kii purti karte hai.
 yahaan ke utpaada sanchaalan main surakshit, vishwasaneey evam sahi prayog ke samay pratikul paristhitiyon main visham jalwaayu main bhi samaan kshamta ke saath surakshit rahenge. sanyantr or praudyogikiyon kaa chunaav is tarah kiya gaya he ki ucch shrenee kii gunata or atyaadhunik si en si praudyogiki kaa mishran he. utpaada prakriya, abhiyantriki ke bade bhaag - mecanical, electranical, metallergical, rasinic, textil, optics or electranix ko shaamil karti hai.yah hamaara praytn he ki ham vishwa str ke utpaadon kaa nirmaan suraksha pahaluon, utpaadon evam prakriyaaon se bina samjhauta kiye karen. suraksha maanakon ke paalan ko ucch praathamikta di jaati he evam suraksha ke sidhdant or aapadaa prabandhak yojana sanghathan main chaalu he.
 bhartiya aayudh nirmaaniyon ke chuninda graahak bhartiya sashastr senaayen hai. sashastr senaaon ko aayudh kii aapurti ke atirikt, aayudh nirmaaniyan anya duusare upabhoktaaon kii maangon ko bhi puraa karti hai jaise gola barood, vastra, bullate pruf vaahan or surang pratirodhi vaahan kii aapurti ityadi. niryaat ke aaytan main vruddhi evam iske kaary vistaar main failaav aayudh nirmaaniyon kaa pramukh uddeshy rahata he.
 sheersh board kii adhyakshata, adhyaksh ke rup main mahanideshak aayudh nirmaaniyan dwaara kii jaati he evam isamen apper mahanideshak pad ke 9 sadsy shaamil hai. aayudh nirmaaniyon ko 5 prachaalan prabhag main vibhakt kiya gaya he, jo mukhya utpaadon/praudyogiki par nirbhar karata he. ye nimnaroop hai:1. gola barood evam visphotak 2. shastr, vaahan evam upaskar 3. saamagree evam ghatak 4. kavchit vaahan 5. aayudh upaskar nirmaani samooh 
 uparyukt prayek nirmaani samooh kii adhyakshata sadsy/apper mahanideshak, aayudh nirmaaniyan dwaara kii jaati he. anya chaar sadasyon par staff prakaarya, yatha carmik, vitt, yojana evam saamagree prabandhan, takneekee sevaayen, kaa uttardaayitv hota he evam ve iskaa sanchaalan kolkata se karte hai.
