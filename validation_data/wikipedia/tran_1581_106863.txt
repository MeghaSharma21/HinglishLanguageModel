 ienes rajput bhartiya nausena ke praarambhik dhwansak poton main se ek he jiskaa pahchaan kramank di-51 he. ek nirdeshit-missile vidhvansak he or bhartiya nausena ke rajput shrenee ke vidhvansak bede kaa pramukh pot he. yah 30 sitambar 1980 ko kamishan kii gai thi. camodor gulab mohanlal hiranandani iske pehle commanding officer the.
 ienes rajput ne brahmos krooj missile ke liye ek pareekshan manch ke rup main kaary kiya. do pi-20 em ne ekal lanchar ko do boxing laancharon se badal diya gaya tha, prayek main do brahmos cell the. prithvi-3 missile ke ek nae sanskaran kaa march 2007 main rajput se pareekshan kiya gaya tha. yah bhoomi ke lakshyon par hamalaa karne ke saath tascaforce yaa vaahak escort ke rup main anti-vimaan or anti-pandubbi missile launch karne main sakasham he. rajput ne 2005 main ek safal pareekshan ke dauraan dhanush belistic missile ko trek kiya.
