 bhartiya sashastr senaayen bharat kii tathaa iske prayek bhaag kii suraksha sunishchit karne ke liye uttaradaayi hai. bhartiya shastr senaaon kii sarvocch kaman bharat ke raashtrapati ke paas he. raashtra kii raksha kaa daayitv mantrimandal ke paas hota he. iskaa nirvahan raksha mantralay dwaara kiya jaataa he, jo sashastr balon ko desh kii raksha ke sandarbh main unke daayitv ke nirvahan ke liye nitigat ruparekha or jaankariyaan pradaan karata he. bhartiya shastr sena main teen prabhag hai bhartiya thalsena, bhartiya jalsena, bhartiya vayusena or iske atirikt, bhartiya sashastr balon bhartiya tatarakshak bal or ardhsainik sanghathanon or vibhinn antar-seva adeshon or sansthaanon main is tarah ke samrik bal kaman andaman nicobar kaman or samanvit rup se samarthan kar rahe hai dephence staff. bharat ke raashtrapati bhartiya sashastr balon ke sarvocch commander he. bhartiya sashastr balon bharat sarkaar ke raksha mantralay ke prabandhan ke tahat kar rahe hai. 14 lakh se adhik sakriy karmiyon kii takat ke saath,yah duniya kaa teesraa sabase bada sainya bal he. anya kai swatantra or anushangik ikaiyaan jaise: bhartiya seemaa suraksha bal, assam rifles, rashtriya rifles, rashtriya suraksha guard, bharat tibet seemaa police ityadi. 
 bhartiya sena ke pramukh commander bharat ke raashtrapati shri ramnath kovind hai. yah duniya ke sabase badi or pramukh senaaon main se ek he. sankhya kii drushti se bhartiya thalsena ke javaanon kii sankhya duniya main china ke baad sabase adhik he. jabase bhartiya sena kaa gathan hua he, bharat ne donon vishwayuddh main bhaag liya he. bharat kii aazaadee ke baad bhartiya sena ne pakistan ke khilaaf teen yuddh 1948, 1965, tathaa 1971 main lade hai jabaki ek baar china se 1962 main bhi yuddh hua he. iske alaava 1999 main ek chota yuddh kargil yuddh pakistan ke saath dubaara ladaa gaya. 
 bhartiya sena parmaanu hathiyaar, unnat astr-shastr se las he or unke paas uchit missile takanic bhi uplabdh he. halanki bharat ne pehle parmaanu hamale na karne kaa sankalp liya hua he.
 bhartiya sena kii ore se diya jane vaala sarvocch sammaan paramveer chakra he.
