 badal vipanan ke rup main in upabhoktaaon ko katalog or patrikaon bhejnaa shaamil he, vipanan vitaran maal kii laagat kam karne ke liye ek vyapaar ke liye sakasham banaata he upabhoktaaon ko lakshith karne ke liye ek tej or sasta drushtikon main sakasham banaata he, jo digital swaroop ke madhyam se prachaar saamagree, bhejne ke liye ek vyapaar kii anumati ke digital media. laagat mudran laagat kam kar detaa he or lagaataar online saamagree sulabh kaa upyog kar kshamta bad jaati he.
 samay graahakon ko lakshith karne ke liye mahatvapurn he. ve aksar amaanya ho pehle is tarah ke poster or surveykshan ke rup main paaramparik tareekon kaa upyog kar vigyaapan simit samay he. badal vipanan jarurat padane par vigyaapan kaa utpaadan karne ke liye ek vyapaar ke liye sakasham banaata he. kisi abhiyaan yaa mausam khatm ho gaya he, saamagree internet se mitaa yaa vipanan saamagree apane uddeshy ke liye feet he sunishchit karne ke liye anukulan ke tatv ke saath jodne, ant upyogkarta ke liye saamagree ko badhaane ke liye anukulit kiya ja saktaa ke rup main saamagree aasaani se hataaya ja saktaa he or sahi samay par diya he.
 gorilla vipanan kharch karne ke liye chote bajet ke saath ek aparamparaagat tareeke se apane utpaadon yaa sevaaon ko badhaava dene ke liye chote vyavsaayon ke liye banaaya gaya ek vigyaapan ranneeti avadharana he. yah or adhik vyaktigat or yaadgaar str main janata kaa dhyaan lobhi par dhyaan kendrit kar ucch urja or kalpana karnaa shaamil he. kuch badi companiyon ke gorilla vipanan yah ghoshnaa karti, aparamparaagat vigyaapan kii takanic kaa upyog, lekin un companiyon ke liye bada bajet hoga or brands pehle se hi dikh rahaa he. gorilla vipanan kaa mukhya bindu gatividhiyon sadakon yaa anya saarvajanik sthanon par vishesh rup se kaam kar rahe hai vah yah he ki, shopping centre, adhik se adhik logon kaa upyog ke saath park yaa samudr taton ke rup main zyada darshakon ko aakarshith karne ke liye itani ke rup main.
 hording kaa upyog ki theth saarvajanik vipanan abhiyaanon ke vipareet, gorilla vipanan graahakon ke saath sidhaa sampark sthapit karne ke liye kai takneekon or prathaon ke aavedan shaamil he. is baatcheet ke lakshyon main se ek ek bhaavnaatmak graahakon main pratikriya or paidaa karne ke liye he vipanan ke antim lakshya he ki ve karne ke liye istemaal kar rahe hai kii tulana main logon main ek alag tarah brandon ko yaad karne ke liye mil rahaa he. takanic zyaadaatar sidhe ghatana ko jodne ke lekin avasar kaa upyog kiye bina badi ghatana yaa tyohaar par ek oparation banane ke liye saarvajanik sthalon main udata vitaran se shaamil he. kisi bhi gorilla vipanan abhiyaan ke saath chunauti kanoonee muddon main shaamil ho rahi bina aaparishan karne ke liye sahi jagah or samay mil rahaa he.
 gorilla vipanan ke vibhinn prakaar hai: parivesh, ghaat, chupake, viral or street vipanan.
 saamaajik vipanan ko viksit karne or adhik se adhik saamaajik bhalaai ke liye vyaktiyon or samudaayon ko laabh pahunchane vaale vyavhaar ko prabhavit karne ke liye anya tareekon ke saath vipanan avadharanaon ko ekikrut karne kaa prayaas he. yah prabhavi, kushal, nyyochit or tikaoo hai ki saamaajik parivartan kaaryakramon samvedansheel pratiyogita ke vitaran ko suchit or khandit karne, anusandhan, sabase acha abhyaas, siddhant, darshakon or saajhedaari antardrushti ko ekikrut karne kaa prayaas he.
 "saamaajik vipanan" kabhi-kabhi keval gaiyr-vanijyik lakshyon ko praapt karne ke liye maanak vanijyik vipanan prathaon kaa upyog kar ke rup main dekha jaataa he, yah ek ati sarleekaran he. "vanijyik vipanan" main uddeshy mukhya rup se "vittiya" he, jabaki saamaajik vipanan kaa praathmik uddeshy he, "saamaajik acha he." yah vanijyik vipanak saamaajik achchhai kii uplabdhi ke liye yogadan nahin kar sakate hai ki iskaa matlab yah nahin he.
 teji se, saamaajik vipanan vanijyik or saarvajanik kshetra kii vipanan drushtikon sahit saamaajik vigyaan or saamaajik niti drushtikon, or ek "vipanan janak", "sahit do mata-pita" ek "saamaajik janak", ke rup main varnit kiya ja rahaa he.
 rishta vipanan pehla graahak banaae rakhane or santushti par jor detii he, jo pratyaksh pratikriya vipanan abhiyaanon se viksit vipanan kaa ek rup he, bajaay bikri lenden par ek pramukh dhyaan ke rup main paribhaashit kiya gaya tha. 
 ek abhyaas ke rup main, rishta vipanan yah graahak sambandhon ke dirghakaalik mahatv ko pahachaanata he or dakhal dene se vigyaapan or bikri prachaar sandesh pare sanchaar faili hui he ki main vipanan ke anya roopon se alag he. 
 internet or mobile platformon ke vikaas ke saath, rishta vipanan viksit or praudyogiki or sahyogi or saamaajik sanchaar channel kholta ke rup main aage badhane ke liye jari rakhaa he. yah saral janasankhyikeey or graahak seva ke data se pare chalaa jaataa he ki graahakon ke saath sambandhon ke prabandhan ke liye upkaran shaamil hai. rishta vipanan bheetar kaa vipanan prayaason, piaar, saamaajik media or anuprayog vikaas shaamil karne ke liye faili hui he.
