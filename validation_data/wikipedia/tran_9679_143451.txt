 
 rangabirangi udan gilahari, jiskaa vaigyanik naam hylopitis albonizer, ek prakaar kii udan gilahari he jo bharat, bhutan, nepal, bangladesh, burma, unnan, thailand, vietnam or laos main pai jaati he. jeevvaigyanik vargeekaran kii drushti se yah kritank gana ke hylopitis vansh kii sadsy he. iskaa prakritik paryaavas uposhnkaribandh or ushnkaribandh shushk wanon main hota he or in wanon ke katane se is jati par dabaav ban aayaa he.
