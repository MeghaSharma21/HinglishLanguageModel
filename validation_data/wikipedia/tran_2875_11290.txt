 sandharniya krishi athwa tikaoo krishi padap evam jaanwaron ke utpaadan kii samanvit krishi pranali he jo paryaavarniya siddhanton ko dhyaan main rakhkar kii jaati he. sandharniya krishi dderghaavadhi main :
 21 veen sadi main tikaoo kheti ke nimnlikhit baton par vishesh dhyaan dena hoga-
 krishi main rasayanon ke atyadhik prayog se jahaan kheti kii laagat main vrudhdi huyi he. vahi mrudaa urvartaa main nirantar kami aa rahi he. aaj desh main badhati huyi jansankhya ko paryaapt khadyann uplabdh karaane kii bahoot badi jimmedaari he. vahi bigat saath varsho se prakritik sansadhano ke andhaadhun dohan se hamane bahut kuch kho diya he. din pratidin nai takneekon kaa prayog karke adhik utpaadan kii chaah main hamane paryaavaran pradooshan, jal pradooshan, vaayu pradooshan ko mrudaa pradooshan ko badhaava diya he. ek hi khet main lagaataar dhaany fasalon ke saghan kheti karne se tathaa asantulit urvarkon evam rasaynic keet naashi ke prayog se mrudaa sanrachanaae, vaayu sanchaar kii dasha tathaa mrudaa jaivik padaarth main lagaataar giravat aayi he. iske atirikt mrudaa main paye jane vaale sukshma jeevanuon tathaa krishak mitra kentuon kii sankhya main kami huyi he. iske fasaswarup fasalotpaadan evam mrudaa utpaadakta par pratikul prabhaav pada he. fasalotpaadan kii vrudhdi dar main giravat ai he. jise apanaakar prakritik sansadhano ko bina kshati pahunchaaye samaaj ko khaadya evam poshak tatwon kii aavashyakataaon ko puraa kiya ja saktaa he. dirghakaal tak ham is tarah kii kheti par jivan vyateet kar prashngat he. abhi haal hi ke dashakon main sansar bahut teji se saamaajik, raajnneetik, aarthik, takneekee, evam paryaavarniya evam krishi paristhitiki taur par badla he. kaaran -badhati hui maanviya bhojan, vastra, makaan aadi kii purti ke liye bhoomi, jal evam paryaavaran kaa atyadhik dohan hua jisase tikaoo kheti kii aavashyaktaa mahasoos hui, at: tikaoo kheti vah kheti he maanav kii badalati aavashyakataaon kii aapurti hetu, krishi main lagane vaale sadhano kaa is prakaar safal vyavasthit upyog kiya janaa, taaki prakritik sansadhano kaa hris na hone paae or paryaavaran bhi surakshit rahe. tikaoo kheti koi ek naaraa nahi he, balki yah ek bhavishya kii anivaary aavashyaktaa he, jisamen khadyann-jansankhya, bhoomi, jal-paryaavaran tathaa laabh: kharch anupaat main saamanjasy jaruri he tabhi bhavishya main maanav pet bhar sakenge.tikaoo kheti
 paribhaasha ke anusaar badalte paryaavaran arthathra dharti ke tapakram main vrudhdi, samudr ke str main badhotaree evam ozon kii parat main kshati aadi nai utpann vishmataaon main krishi ko sandharniyata dene ke saath-saath badhati aabaadi ko ann khilaane ke liye utpaadakta ke str par kramagat vrudhdi karnaa hi tikaoo kheti he. duusare shabdon main vah kheti he, jo maanav kii vartmaan evam bhavi peedi kii ann, chaare, vastra tathaa rindhan kii aavashyakataaon ko puraa kare jisamen paramparaagat vidhiyon evam nai takneekon kaa samaavesh ho, bhoomi par dabaav kam pade, jaev-vividhta nasht na ho, rasayanon kaa kam prayog, jal evam mrudaa prabandh sahi ho, sandharniya kheti kehalaaegi.
 1. vibhinn fasalon kaa fasal pranali main samaavesh karne se prati ikai laagat ko kam kiya ja saktaa he. isse vibhinn prakaar kii khadyaan jaise dhaany, daal, tel va resha ityadi aavashyakataaon kii purti ke saath-saath mrudaa swasthya kaa sangrakshan hota he.
 2. santulit urvarkon kaa prayog kare. isse fasalon kaa gunvattta utpaadan yukt bharpur hoga. yaha par santulit urvarak prayog se taatpary sirf yah hai ki natrajan phosphorus va potash kaa sahi anupaat main prayog karen iske abhaav aavashyaktaanusaar sukshma poshak tatwon kaa bhi prayog karnaa he. aavashyak santulit urvarkon ke prayog se mrudaa main poshan tatwon kii uplabdhta main hone vaale asantulan bhi kam hota he.
 3. ekikrut poshak tatwon aapurti prabandhan aavashyak he. iske antargat carbonic khaadein jaise gobar kii khaad, hari khaad, varmi compost ityadi kaa rasinic urvarkon ke saath uchit maatra main prayog kiya jaataa he. isse utpaadakta main vrudhdi ke saath-saath mrudaa swasthya main baari sudhaar hota he.
 4. jal kaa samuchit prayog karen. fasalon main jal ke uchit prabandhan se urvarak evam anya utpaadan ghatakon kii upyog kshamta ko badhaayaa ja saktaa he.
 5. khar patavaaron kaa ekikrut niyantran karnaa. kharpatvaar ke prabhaavashaali niyantran ke liye naashi rasayanon jaivik taur tarika bhi apanaaya jaae pradooshan ko bhi kam kiya ja saktaa he.
 6. rog evam kiton kaa ekikrut niyantran karnaa. rog evam kiton kaa samanyavit niyantran karne se kiton va rogon kaa rasaynic padaarthon ke prati hone vaale sahanshilta ko niyantrit kiya ja saktaa he. tathaa saath-saath krishi laagat main bhi kami kii ja sakati he.
 1. mrudaa kii urvara shakti ko na keval banaaye rakhata hai balki usamen vrudhdi bhi karata he.
 2. poshak tatwon ko santulit evam dirghakaalin upyogi banaata he.
 3. mrudaa main laabhakaari sukshma jeevon kii paryaapt jansankhya ko banaaye rakhata he.
 4. bhumigat jal str ko banaaye rakhana.
 5. rasayanon ke atyadhik upyog se hone vaale pradooshan kaa kam hona.
