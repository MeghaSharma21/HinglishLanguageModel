 
 dekhen lekh kaa paath
 kabutar puure vishwa main paye jane vaala pakshi he. yah ek niyattapi, udne vaala pakshi he jiskaa sharir paron se dhankaa rahata he. munh ke sthaan par isaki choti nukili chonch hoti he. mukh do chanchuon se ghira evam jabada dantaheen hota he. agle pair dainon main parivartit ho gaye hai. pichley pair shalkon se dhanke evam ungliyaan nakhryukt hoti hai. isamen teen ungliyaan saamne kii ore tathaa chauthi ungali peeche kii ore rahati he. yah jantu manushya ke sampark main rahanaa adhik pasand karata he. anaaj, mewa or daalen iskaa mukhya bhojan hai. bharat main yah safed or saleti rang ke hote hai puraane jamaane main iskaa prayog patr or chitthiyaan bhejne ke liye kiya jaataa tha.
