 jeen mata rajasthan ke sikar jile main sthit dharmik mahatva kaa ek gaanv he. yah sikar se 29 kilometre dakshin main sthit he. yahan kii kul jansankhya 4359 he. yahan par jeenmata ek pracheen mandir sthit he. jeenmata kaa yah pavitra mandir saikadon varsh purana mana jaataa he. rajasthan kii rajdhani jaipur se 108 kilometre he l
 lok manyataon ke anusaar jeen kaa janm chouhan vansh ke rajput parivaar main hua. unke bhai kaa naam harsh tha jo bahut khushi se rahete the. ek baar jeen kaa apni bhabhi ke saath vivaad ho gaya or isi vivaad ke chalate jin or harsh main naaraazgi ho gayi. iske baad jeen aravali ke 'kaajal shikhar' par pahunch kar tapasya karne lageen. manyataon ke anusaar isi prabhaav se wo baad main devi rup main parivartit hui. yah mandir chuunaa pathar or sangemarmar se bana hua he. yah mandir aathvin sadi main nirmit hua tha.jinnamata rajasthan, bharat ke sikar jile main dharmik mahatv kaa ek gaanv he. yah dakshin main sikar shahar se 29 kimi kii duuri par sthit he. shahar kii aabaadi 435 9 he jisamen se 1215 anusuchit jati or 113 asti log hai. jinn mata ko samarpit ek pracheen mandir he. jintata kaa pavitra mandir mana jaataa he ki yah ek hajaar saal purana he. lakhon bhakt yahaan navaratri ke dauraan caitra or aswin ke maheene main do baar ek rangeen tyohaar ke liye ikattha hote hai. badi sankhya main aagantukon ko samyojit karne ke liye kai dharmashalaayein hai. is mandir ke kareeb hi uske bhai harsh bhairavnath mandir pahari kii chotii par sthit he.
 jinnamata mandir revsa gaanv se 10 kimi pahari ke paas sthit he. yah ghaney jangal se ghira hua he. uskaa purna or vaastavik naam jayantlal tha. iske nirmaan kaa varsh gyaat nahin he, lekin sarvamandapa or khambhe nishchit rup se bahut puraani hai.
 jinnat kaa mandir shuruaati samay se tirth yatra kaa sthaan tha or isaki marammat or kai baar punarnirmaan kiya gaya tha. ek lokapriy manyata he jo sadiyon se logon tak aati he ki churu ke ek gaangh ghanghgh main raja ghengh ne is shart par apsara se shaadi kar lee or shaadi kii thi ki vah apane mahal main purv soochna ke bina nahin jaaenge. raja gang ko ek putr mila jise harsh kaha jaataa tha or ek beti jin thi. baad main usne phir se kalpana kii lekin maukey ke taur par yah raja gang apane purvajon ko bina mahal main gaya or is tarah unhone apsara se kiye gaye pratigya kaa ulanghan kiya. turant usne raja ko chhod diya or apane bete harsh or beti jin se bhaag kar bhaag liya, jise vah us jagah par chhod diya jahaan vartmaan main mandir khadaa tha. yahaan do bacchon ne atyadhik tapasya kaa abhyaas kiya baad main ek chouhan shaasha ne us jagah par mandir banaaya.
 jine mata ke mukhya anuyaayiyon main kshetra ke mahaan yadav, brahman, rajput, agarwal, zanjeer or minas aninth baniyaan shaamil hai. jin mata, mahaan yadav, agarwal, minaa, shekhawati rajput or rajasi ke yoddha varg ke junglee, ke kuladevi hai. jine mata ke anuyaayiyon kii ek badi sankhya kolkata main rahete hai, jo janima mandir par ja rahe hai. jo log jin mata ko apni maa ke rup main aadar karte hai, unke parivaar main nar bachche ke janm ke liye prarthana karte hai or putr ke janm ke baad hi mandir kii yatra karne kaa pratigya karte hai. nar bachche ke janm ke baad puure parivaar main jin mata ji kaa daura kiya jaataa he or mandir ke parisar main pehle bal kaat kii peshkash kii jaati he. anuyaayiyon ne mandir main 50 kilo mithaiyaan, jo ki soa mani ke naam se jaani jaati hai, kii peshkash karti hai.
 mougal samraat aurangzeb mata ke mandir ke maidan par utarana chahta tha. uske pujaariyon dwaara bulaayaa jane vaala, mata ne bhairon kii apni sena ko chhod diya jisane samraat or uske sainikon ko apane ghutanon par laayaa. usne maafee maangi or dayaalu mataji ne use apane gusse se maaf kiya. aurangzeb ne apane dilli mahal se akhand tel kaa deepak daan kiya. mata ke pavitra sanskaar main yah deepak abhi bhi chamak rahaa he.
