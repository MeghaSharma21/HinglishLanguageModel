 air india udaan 101 bombai se landan ke liye nirdhaarit air india kii yatri udaan thi jo durghatanavash 24 janvari 1966 ko frans main mont blaank pahaad se takaraa gai thi. durghatana main karmidal ke 11 sadasyon ke saath-saath sabhi 106 yatriyon kii mrityu ho gai thi. isi vimaan durghatana main bhartiya parmaanu vaigyanik homi jahangir bhabha kii mrityu hui thi.
 air india flite 101 bombai se landan ke liye nirdhaarit yatri udaan thi or durghatana vaale din boing 707 dwaara sanchalit kii ja rahi thi. iskaa panjikaran vity-dmen or naam kanchanjanga tha. bombai se udaan bharne ke pashchaat yah apane do nirdhaarit thaharaavon, dilli or bayrut, main rukne ke baad geneva kii taraf ja rahi thi. jahaaj ke payalat ko aisa laga ki vah mont blaank ko paar kar chukaa he or isi galatarahami main usne jahaaj kii oonchaai kam kerani shuru kar di or mont blaank se vimaan kii takkar ho gai. vimaan vidhwans ho gaya or sabhi 106 yatriyon or 11 chaalak dal ke sadasyon kii durghatana main mrityu ho gai. yatriyon main bhartiya vaigyanik or parmaanu urja ayog ke adhyaksh homi jahangir bhabha bhi shaamil the, jinka nidhan isi durghatana main hua tha.
 durghatana kii jaanch main gathit kamishan ne paaya ki vimaan ke payalat kii galati ke kaaran yah durghatana hui. usne mont blaank se apni duuri kaa galat akalan lagaaya or radar niyantrak ko yah soochna di. niyantrak ne vimaan kii pahaad se sahi duuri maap kar ke payalat ko suchit kiya. payalat ne suchit kii gai jaankaree ko galat samajha or anumaan lagaaya ki vah mont blaank chotii ko peeche chhod chukaa he or usne vimaan kii oonchaai kam kerani shuru kar di va vimaan kii chotii se sidhi takkar hui.
