 mohammad mansoor ali
 mohammad mansoor ali, ek bangladeshi rajaneta va swatantraaa senaanee the. ve shaikh mujiburrahman ke kareeb vishwaasapatro main shaamil the, or 60-70 ke dashakon ke avami lig ke varisht netaa the. ve 25 janvari 1975 se 15 agast 1975 tak bangladesh ke pradhanmantri the. 15 agast 1975 ko shaikh mujeeb kii hatya kar, sainya takhtapalat kiya gaya. 22 agast ko mansoor ko, anya anek raajnneetigyon ke saath, jail main band kar diya gaya, or 3 november 1975 kii, jail hatya diwas ke naam se kukhyat, raat ko unhen or unke teen saathiyon samet sena dwaara, bina-mukadmaa, jail main hi maar diya gaya.
