 
 hilium ek rasinic tatva he jo praayah gasiy avasthaa main rahata he. yah ek niskriy gas yaa nobel gas he tathaa rangahin, gandhhin, swadaheen, vish-hin bhi he. iskaa parmaanu kramank 2 he. sabhi tatwon main iskaa kvathanank evam galnaank sabase kam he. drav hilium kaa prayog padaarthon ko atyant kam taap tak thanda karne ke liye kiya jaataa he; jaise atichaalak taron ko 1.9 degree kelvin tak thanda karne ke liye. 
 helium akriy gaison kaa ek pramukh sadsy he. iskaa sanket He, parmanubhaar 4, parmanusankhya 2, ghanatv 0.1785, kranthik taap -267.900 or kranthik dabaav 2 26 vayumandal, kvathanank -268.90 sein. or galnaank -272 degree se. he. iske do sthaayee samasthanik He3, parmanvik dravyaman 3.0170 or He4, parmanvik dravyaman 4.0039 or do asthaayee samasthanik He5, parmanvik dravyaman 5.0137 or radioective He6, parmanvik dravyaman 6.028 paae gaye hai.
 1868 i. main surya ke sarvagrass grahan ke avasar par surya ke varnmandal ke spectrum main ek pili rekha dekhi thi jo sodium kii pili rekha se bhinn thi. jansen ne is rekha kaa naam di3 rakhaa or sar je. narman locoyar is parinaam par pahunche ki yah rekha kisi aise tatv kii he jo prithvi par nahin paaya jaataa. unhone hi helium ke naam par iskaa naam helium rakhaa. 1894 i. main sar wiliam ramjem ne cliwite naamak khanij se nikali gas kii pariksha se siddh kiya ki yah gas prithvi par bhi pai jaati he. cliwite ko tanu salphurik amla ke saath garam karne or peeche queewite ko nirvaat main garam karne se is gas ko praapt kiya tha. aisi gas main 20 pratishat nitrogen tha. nitrogen ke nikaal lene par gas ke spectrum pareekshan se spectrum main di3 rekha mili. peeche pataa laga ki kuch ulkaloh main bhi yah gas vidyaman thi. ramje or tavers ne is gas ko bade parishram or badi sookshmata se pariksha kar dekha ki yah gas vayumandal main bhi rahata he. ramje or frederick sody ne radioactive padaarthon ke swat:vighatan se praapt utpaada main bhi is gas ko paaya. vayumandal main badi alp matra, kuch anya khanijon, jaise bogerite or monejite se nikali gaison main yah paaya gaya. monozite ke prati ek gram main 1 ghan semi gas pai jaati he. petroleum kupon se nikali prakritik gas main isaki maatra 1 pratishat se lekar 8 pratishat tak pai gai he.
 prakritik gaison ke dhone se carbon diaksaide or anya amleey gaisen nikal jati hai. dhone main monithenolemin or glykol mila hua jal prayukt hota he. dhone ke baad gas ko suukhaakar use grq se 300 degree taap tak thandha karte hai. us taap par prati varg inch 600 pound se adhik dabaav daalate hai. isse helium or kuch nitrogen ko chhodkar anya sab gaisen tarleebhoot ho jaati hai. ab helium or nitrogen kaa mishran batch jaataa he. ise or thanda kar prati varg inch 2,500 pound dabaav se dabaate hai jisase adhikansh nitrogen tarleebhoot ho jaataa he or helium kii maatra 98.2% tak pahunch jaati he. yadi isse adhik shuddh helium praapt karnaa ho to sakriyakrut nariyal ke koyale ko drav nitrogen ke ooshmak main rakhkar uske dwaara helium ko paarit karte hai jisase keval leshmaatr apdravyavaala helium praapt hota he.
 varnarahit, gandhhin or swadaheen gas he. tapadhvani or vidyut kaa suchalak he. jal main alp viley he. anya vilaayakon main adhik ghulata he. iskaa taralan hua he. drav helium do roopon main paaya gaya he. iskaa ghanatv 0.122 he. iskaa thosikaran bhi hua he. taral drav ke 140 vayumandal dabaav par 272 degree se. par kisam ne 1926 i. main thos helium praapt kiya tha. isaki gas main keval ek parmaanu rahata he. isaki vishisht ooshmaaon kaa anupaat 4 : 1.667 he. kisi bhi tatv ke saath yah koi yogic nahin banataa. isaki sanyojaktaa shunya he. aavartasaarini main iskaa sthaan pratham samooh ke prabal vidyut dhaneey tatwon or saptam samooh ke prabal vidyut runeey tatwon ke bich he.
 vayupoton main hydrogen ke sthaan main ab helium kaa prayog hota he yadhyapi hydrogen kii tulana main isaki utthapak kshamta 92.6 pratishat hi he par hydrogen ke jvalansheel hone or vaayu ke saath visphotak mishran banane ke kaaran ab helium kaa hi upyog ho rahaa he. mausam kaa pataa lagane ke liye balunon main bhi helium kaa aaj upyog ho rahaa he. halki dhaatuon ke jodne or anya dhaatukarmasambandhi upachaaron main niskriy vayumandal ke liye helium kaam main aa rahaa he. aushadhiyon main bhi visheshat: dame or anya shwasan rogon main oxygen ke saath milakar kritrim shwasan main helium kaa upyog bad rahaa he. drav hilium kaa prayog padaarthon ko atyant kam taap tak thanda karne ke liye kiya jaataa he; jaise atichaalak taron ko 1.9 degree kelvin tak thanda karne ke liye.
