 'nirmala nagpal saroj khan ' sabase pramukh nraty koreographerr he. unhone 200 se bhi adhik filmo me kaam kiya he.
 . saroj khan kii maan or pitaaji krishchand sadhu singh or noni sadhu singh he. nirmala jo saroj khan ke naam se prasid he unke mata-pita bharat ke vibhaajan ke baad bharat chale aae the. vah ek muslim se shaadi kii he. unka naam sardar roshan khan he or vahi se unk antim naam khan ata he. unhone nrity ki shiksh bi sohanlal se lee. ek pakistani tivi sho par apane khud ke ikbaalia bayaan ke anusaar ve islam ko shaadi se pehle hi kubul kar liy tha or abhi ek muslman he. teraa saal ki umr me unhone bi sohanlal se shaadi karali jo ki us samay ke ek prasiddh nrity guru the. sohan laal pehle hi shaadi shud the or unhe 4 bacchon bhi the. chaudaa saal kii umr main unhone apne pehle bachche hamid khan ko janam diy, jo ab raju khan ke naam se ek prasiddh nrityrachana-car he. 1965 main vah sohanlal se alag hoi, lekin unke sahaayak ke rup main kaam karti rahi thi. sohanlal or saroj khan k punarmilan hoa jab sohanlal ko dil kaa daura padaa tha. saroj khan ko sohanlal se or ek baccha hoa unki beti hina khan . sohanlal saroj khan or unke do bacchon ko peeche chhodkar madras chhale gayi. uske baad vah sardaar roshan khan ke saath shaadi ki or sukayna khan naam ke ek beti ko janm diya. jo ab dubai main ek nrity santhaan chalaati he. 
 vah film nazraana main teen saal kii umr main ek bal kalakaar ke rup main apane vah film nazrana main teen saal kii umr main ek bal kalakaar ke rup main apane vyavsaay kii shuruyaat ki or us kirdaar kaa naam shyama tha. vah nagina chalchitr "main teri dushman" ke liye bahut prasiddhi mili thi. unhone dubai me nrity sanstha shoru kii 29 navambar prishthbhumi nartki ke rup me 'sa se shruwaat ki. film nrityrachana-car bi sohanlal ke tahat kaam karati hoi unhone nrity siikhaa. baad main vah khud nrity sanyojak ke rup me apne vyavsaay ki shruwaat ki thi. pehle ek sahaayak nrityrachana-car ke rup main or baad main ek swatantra nrityrachana-car ke rup main gita mere naam me shruwaat ki thi. haalanki, unko khyaati prapt karne ke liye kai saal intazar karnaa padaa tha lekin shri devi ke saath mistar india main hawa hawaii or chandani ke gano ki nritykala ke saat unhe khyaati mili thi. baad main maadhuri dikshit ke saath, tejaab main ek do teen thanedaar main tammaa tammaa loge or beta me dhak dhak karne lag me zabardast hit gane diye the. iske baad vah sabase safal bollywood koreographeron main se ek ban gai thi. 2013 me gulab gang main phir maadhuri dikshit ke saath kaam kiya he.
 saroj khan do anya nyaayadhishon ke saath star van par prasaarit jo 2005 nach baliye main nirnaayak mandal ke ek sadsy ke rup main ek reality dance sho main dikhaai di gai thi. nach baliye ke duusare sizan me bhi nyaayadhish rahi. vah haal hi main soni antertenment televijan par prasaarit ho rahe jo sho "ustado kaa ustad 'ke liye ek nyaayadhish bhi rahi he. vah enditivi imagin par prasaarit kiya gaya sho nach leve vit saroj khan 2008 main dikhaai di. vah is sho ke liye nritykala ki thi. vah soni ke sho bugi vugi disambar 2008 me bhi dikhaai di gai thi. vah ek nyaayadhishon me se thi javed jaafaree, naved jaafaree or ravi bahal ke saath. vah jhalak dikhalaa ja farvari 27,2009 ke tisare sizan me purv nach baliye nyaayadhishon vaibhavi marchent or abhinetri juhi chawla ke saath soni antertenment televijan me bhi dikhi di. vah vartmaan main dance reality sho nach lai ve wyeth saroj khan me bhi nyaayadhish rah chhuki he. vah mejabani kii he or nachle ve vith saroj khan puraa kar liya he. 2012 main, da saroj khan storyi pi as bi tii or bharat kaa film prabhag dwaara nirmit or nidhi tuli dwaara nirdeshit vruttchitr jari kiya gaya tha.
