 nilkantha chaturdhar sanskrit sahitya ke suprasiddh tikaakar. sampoorn mahabharat kii tikaa ke liye vishesh prakhyat.
 
 nilkantha chaturdhar kaa janm maharashtra ke ahmednagar jile ke karpur gram main hua tha. inke pita kaa naam 'govind suri' tathaa mata kaa naam 'phullambika' tha. ye gautamagotreey maharashtriya brahman the, parantu inke naam ke saath chaturdhar judaa hua he. 'saptshati' par likhi apni 'subodhini' naamak tikaa main inhone swayan ko chaturdhar mishra kaha he. alpakaal ke liye inhone kaashi main bhi nivas kiya tha. kuch vidwaanon kaa maananaa he ki ye baad main kaashi main hi bas gayi the. apni sarvaadhik prasiddh kriti mahabharat kii 'bharat-bhavadeep' naamak tikaa kii rachana inhone kaashi main hi kii thi. inke putr kaa naam bhi govind hi tha jinke putr shiv ne paithan main rahete hue 'dharmatatva-prakaash' granth kaa nirmaan 1746 isvi main kiya tha. nilkanth kii shivatandav-tikaa kaa rachanakaal 1680 i0 tathaa ganeshagita kii tikaa kaa rachanakaal 1693 i0 he. bharat-bhavadeep ke naanaa hastlekhon kaa samay 1687 i0 se lekar 1695 i0 he. atah acharya baldev upaadhyaay ne inkaa samay 1650 i0 se 1700 i0 tak ke aas-paas mana he.
 nilkanth chaturdhar kii amar kirti kaa aadhaar unke dwaara rachit mahabharat kii 'bharat-bhavadeep' naamak prakhyat tikaa he. yah tikaa mahabharat ke sampurna 18 parvon par uplabdh he. pracheen samay se inke samay tak likhi gayi mahabharat kii samast uplabdh tikaaon main inaki tikaa sarvaadhik upyogi maani gayi he. vastutah mahabharat ke vivechan karne vaale mahapurushon main acharya nilkanth kaa sthaan pramukhatam he. bharat-bhavadeep ke prastut hone ke baad mahabharat kii anya tikaayen praayah lupt hi ho gayi or ab unka ullekh sanskrit sahitya ke itihaas granthon tak simat kar rah gaya he.
 mahabharat par likhi gayi apni is prasiddh tikaa ke aarambh main hi inhone yah vyakt kiya he ki is tikaa ko likhane se pehle inhone desh ke vibhinn bhaagon se mul granth mahabharat kii anek pandulipiyon ko ekatra kiya tha or unhen dekhte hue mul ke sarvopyukt paath kaa nirdhaaran kiya tha, saath hi is sandarbh main pracheen vyakhyakaaron kii vyakhyaaon ko bhi dhyaan main rakhaa tha:-
 bahoon samaahrity vibhinndeshyan koshan vinishchitya c pathmagryam.
 praachaan gurunamanusrity vaacham arabhyate bharatbhavdeepah..
 is sandarbh main sthaan-sthaan par inhone paath-bhedon kii ore tathaa anek pandulipiyon main praapt adhik paath kii ore ingit kiya he, or aisa lagbhag 125 sthanon main hua he. aaj bhi vibhinn pandulipiyon kaa nirikshan karne par paath-bhedon ke sambandh main praayah vahi saamagree praapt hoti he, jiskaa nidarshan inhone apni tikaa main kiya he. in paath-bhedon ke sambandh main apane se purv vidyaman tikaakaron kii tikaaon ke uddhran bhi inhone pramaan swaroop uddhrut kiya he or inkaa yah vaishishty mahabharat ke prachlit mul granth ke sambandh main soochna prastut karne kii disha main atyant mahatvapurn he.
 inhone apni adbhut vyakhya-shakti kaa parichay dete hue rugveda ke mantron main ramakatha tathaa shrikrishna-katha ke sandarbh found nikaale hai. inaki pustak 'mantra-ramayana' evam 'mantra-bhagwat' rugveda ke mantron kaa vyakhya sahit sankalan he. mantra-ramayana main rugveda se krambaddh rup main chune gayi 157 mantron kii ramakthaparak vyakhya karke rugveda main ramakatha kaa mul pratipaadit kiya gaya he.
 'mantra bhagwat' bhi bilkul isi tarah shrikrishna se sambhddha mantron kaa savyakhya sangrah he. is granth main 107 vaidic mantron kii vyakhya karte hue vyakhyaakaar ne veda main bhagwat dharm ko siddh karne kaa praytn kiya he. sabhi mantron ko kramshah gokul cond, vrundavan cond, akrur cond or mathura cond naamak chaar virgon main vibhajit kiya gaya he. unhone na keval krishna shabd ko rugveda main found nikaala he apitu bhagwat ke krishna-sambandhi gokul, vrundavan tathaa mathura kii kalpana ko bhi vaidic aadhaar de diya he.
