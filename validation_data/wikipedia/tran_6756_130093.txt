 killar karaoke ek america dharawahik he, jo ek gane ke khel par aadhaarit he. jisamen koi bhi gaanaa ga saktaa he, isamen gane ke liye koi bhi abhyaas kii aavashyaktaa nahin he, lekin isamen gaanaa gaate samay ruknaa nahin he jab tak kii gaanaa samaapt na ho jaye. isamen kuch log gaanaa gaate hai. is kaarykram main gaanaa gaate samay anek prakaar ke baadhaayen banaai jaati he jisase gayak gaanaa chhod de.
 is main kul 6 log bhaag lete hai or ve sabhi gaanaa gaate hai. jisamen kul teen charan hote he or sabhi do do pratibhaagi ko ek ek charan main gaanaa gaanaa hota he. jo bhi sabase adhik main gaanaa gane main safal ho jaataa he, vah jeet jaataa he. yadi koi bhi pratibhaagi 90 second se pehle apana gaanaa roak detaa he to vah pratiyogita se hat jaataa he. or yadi donon hi pratibhaagi apana gaanaa puraa kar lete hai to iskaa phasela janata ke mat ke anusaar hota he. jise adhik mat milte hai vah jeet jaataa he.
 isamen kuch badlaav kiye gaye hai, jaise bijli kaa chhatka dena yaa pani daalana aadi. isi ke saath isamen yadi koi gane ke bol yaa jor se yaa dhire bhi bolta he. use bhi gina jaataa he. saath hi isamen mukhya rup se janata ke mat kaa hi upyog kar jeetne vaale pratibhaagi ko chunaa jaataa he. yadi koi bhi pratibhaagi ko kam mat milane se vah usee samay baahar ho jaataa he.
