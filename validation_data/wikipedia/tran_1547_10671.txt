 sibar aparaadh ek esa aparaadh he jis main computer or network shaamil he. kisi bhi computer kaa aparaadhik sthaan par milanaa yaa computer se koi aparaadh karnaa computer aparaadh kehalaata he. computer aparaadh me network shaamil nahi hota he. kisi ki niji jaankaree ko parapat karnaa or uskaa galat istamaal karnaa. kisi kii bhi nijee jaankaree computer se nikal lenaa yaa chori kar lenaa bhi sibar aparaadh he.
 computer aparaadh bhi kai prakaar se kiye jate he jase ki jaankaree chori karnaa, jaankaree mitaanaa, jaankaree me pher badal karnaa, kisi ki jaankaree ko kisi or dena yaa computer kii bhaago ko chori karnaa yaa nasht karnaa. 
 sibar aparaadh bhi kahi prakaar ke he jase ki spam email, hekking, fishing, virus ko daalana, kisi kii jaankaree ko online praapt karnaa yaa kisi par har vaqt najar rakhana.
