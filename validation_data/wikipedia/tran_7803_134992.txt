 hindi vikipedia apane drushtibaadhit saathiyon kaa swaagat karata he. iske prayog tathaa sanvardhan main yogadan karne kii drushti se drushtibaadhit hona siddhanthata kisi seemaa kaa nirdhaaran nahin karata he. un sansadhano ke saath jisaki sahaayta se duusare antarjaal prishth padhe or likhe ja sakate hai vehi tareeke sabhi vikipedia prishth ko padhane or usamen yogadan karne ke liye upyogi hai. isi tarah vahi takneekee samasyaayein vikia par bhi mil sakati hai jo duusare wave prishthon par ho sakati hai. vikipedia lekhon kii sanrachana jaise ki prishthon kaa vibhinn sheershakon main vibhakt hona, soochiyon or talikaon kaa prayog, paribhaashik or namvachi shabdon par link bane hona, aadi adhikanshtah saral neyvigation main sahaayak hai. kintu kai talikaayen or saanche mushkil bhi paidaa karte hai. ham aisi baadhaon ko paar karne ke liye utsuk or prayatnsheel hai. is sambandh main apane suzav or jigyaasaaen aap is prishth ke vaarta prishth yaa chaoupal par rakh sakate hai.
 vikipedia padhane ke liye spich synthesizer ke sheershak, suchi, talika, jod, aadi tak pahunchne vaale tareeke bahut upyogi hai. vikipedia ke sabhi prishth is tarah nirmit hote hai ki prishth kaa naam pehla sheershak ban jaataa he. isliye kisi bhi prishth ko kholane par pehle sheershak nirdesh ke saath lekh ke sheershak tak pahunchaa ja saktaa he. yahi tarika shrenee, saanchaa evam anya pariyojna prishth ke shuruaati hisse tak pahunchne ke liye bhi upyogi he. yadi lekh par anukram bana hua ho to doosraa sheershak cmand us tak pahunchaa detaa he. anukram ke die gaye vibhinn link ke sahare lekh ke us khaas hisse tak aasaani se pahunchaa ja saktaa he.
 kisi vishesh prishth tak pahunchne ke liye sampadan baksaa tak pahunchne ke cmand kaa upyog karen. adhiktar prishthon par is tarah kaa ek hi baksaa he. is khoj bakse main manachaaha shabd tankit karen or inter kunjee dabaa den. yadi us shabd par koi lekh bana hoga to vah lekh prishth khul jayega. yadi thik usee shabd kaa lekh pehle se bana hua na ho to ek khoj parinaam prishth khulegaa. pehla sheershak nirdesh synthesizer ko khoj parinaam likhe sheershak tak le jayega. doosraa sheershak nirdesh naam-sthanon main khojein tak pahunchaa degaa. is sheershak ke neeche ke tebul main apane khoj parinaam ko or vyavasthit or uski shrenee ko sankara karne ke liye kai check box bane hai. yadi chahen to iskaa prayog karen. yaa duusare sheershak par pahunchne ke baad suchi nirdesh dene par aapkaa synthesizer sidhe 20 khoj parinaamon kii suchi par pahunch jayega. ero kunjiyon ke sahare manapsand link par jaakar use kholen. yah bhi ho saktaa he ki 20 parinaamon ke bajaay ek bhi khoj parinaam prakat na ho. iskaa matlab yah he ki hindi vikipedia main na to us shabd se sambandhit koi lekh bana he na hin us shabd kaa anya lekhon main vyavhaar hua he.
 vikia par sampadan ke liye adhikanshtah vahi tareeke prayukt hote hai jo kisi bhi sampaditr jaise ki verde, notepad aadi main kaam aate hai. tankit karne, sudhaarney, mitaane ke saare nirdesh ek hi hai. antar keval shrenee lagane, heading banane, sandarbh daalane, saanche lagane aadi ke tareekon main he.
 aap lekh ke jis hisse ko sampadit karnaa chaahate hai uske sheershak par pahunchakar inter kunjee dabaaen. isse lekh ke us hisse ko sampadit karne ke liye ek sampadan anubhag prishth khul jayega. is prishth par teen sampadan kshetra he- anubhag sampadan baksaa, saransh baksaa, or khoj baksaa. sampadan nimna charnon main kar sakate hai-
