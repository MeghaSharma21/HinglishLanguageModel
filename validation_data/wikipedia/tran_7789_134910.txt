 pushyamitra shunga yah ek bramhan tha . uttar bharat ke shunga samrajya ke sansthaapak or pratham raja tha . isse pehle wo maury samrajya main senapati tha. 185 ipurv main isane antim maury samraat kii raat main darvaar main akelaa bulaayaa or unki peeth par chura ghopakar samraat brihadrath kii hatya kar di or apane aapako raja udghoshit kiya. uske baad unhone ashwamedh yajna kiya or uttar bharat kaa adhiktar hissaa apane adhikaar kshetra main le liya. shunga rajya ke shilaalekh punjab ke jalandhar main pushyamitra kaa ek shilaalekh mili hai or divyavdaan ke anusaar yah rajya sangla tak vistrut tha.
 mahabhashya main patanjali or panini kii ashtaadhi ke anusaar pushyamitra shunga bharadwaj gotra ke brahman the. mahabharat ke harivansh parv ke anusaar wo kashyap gotra ke brahman the. is samasya ke samaadhaan ke rup main jesi ghosh ne unhendvaiyamushyayan bataaya jise brahmanon kii ek dwait gotra mana jaataa he. unke anusaar dwaiyamushyayan athwa dait gotra, do alag-alg gotron ke mishran se bani brahman gotra hoti he arthath pita or maatra kii gotra . aise brahman apni pahchaan ke rup main donon gotra rakh sakate hai.
 pushyamitra ne jo vaidic dharm kii pataakaa fahraai usee ke aadhaar ko samraat vikramaditya va aage chalakar gupt samrajya ne is dharm ke gyaan ko puure vishwa main failaayaa.pushya mitra sung ne samoochaa buddha dharm kaa vinaash kar diyausake baad jitne buddha dharm ke anuvaai the sabko maut ke ghat utaar diya jitne bache bauddhist the sabakaa dharm parivartan karavaaya
 samrajya kii rajdhani patliputra thi. pushyamitra pracheen maury samrajya ke madhyavarti bhaag ko surakshit rakh sakane main safal rahaa. pushyamitra kaa samrajya uttar main himalay se lekar dakshin main baraar tak tathaa pashchim main punjab se lekar purv main magadh tak faila hua tha. divyavdaan or taranath ke anusaar jalandhar or syalkot par bhi uskaa adhikaar tha. samrajya ke vibhinn bhaagon main rajkumaar yaa rajkul ke logo ko rajyapal niyukt karne kii parampara chalati rahi. pushyamitra ne apane putron ko samrajya ke vibhinn kshetron main sah-shaasha niyukt kar rakhaa tha. or uskaa putr agnimitra vidisha kaa uparaja tha. dhandev kaushal kaa rajyapal tha. rajkumaar ji sena ke sanchaalak bhi the. is samay bhi gram shashan kii sabase choti ikai hoti thi.
 is kaal tak aate-aate mauryalin kendriya niyantran main shithilataa aa gayi thi tathaa saamantikaran kii pravriti sakriy hone lagi thim.
