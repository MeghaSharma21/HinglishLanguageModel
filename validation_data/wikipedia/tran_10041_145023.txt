 apurna inemaljanan daanton main paae jane vaali vah asaamanyata he jisame daanton par inemal kii parat chadh jaati he yaa phir daanton ke crown kii parat bad jaati he. amilogenesis imperfecta daanton main proteins kii kami ke kaaran hoti he. daanton kii yah asaamanyata X anuvaanshiki par pai jaati he or yah prabal bhi hoti he. is samasya se peedit logo ke daanton kaa rang pilaa, bhura or dhumaila hota he.amilogenesis imperfecta daanton ke kisi bhi ank main paaya ja saktaa he or isse peedit daant bahut hi sooksham hote he or iname danta guha hone ke atyadhik sanyog hote he. 
 aksar purna kawarej crown se prabhavit daanton ko dhakaa jaataa he kyunki amilogenesis imperfecta se daanton kaa rang pilaa ho jaataa he jisase bahut se saundaryashasre-sambandhi vivaad ho jate he. isliye daanton par crown chaday jaataa he. daanton kii atyadhik karaab sthiti main daanton ko nikalnaa hi thik rahata he or uski jagah nae daant lagar jate he.
