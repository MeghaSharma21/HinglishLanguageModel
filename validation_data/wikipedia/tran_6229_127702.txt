 bharat ganraajya main rajyapal 29 rajyon main rajya pramukh kaa sanvaidhanik pad hota he. rajyapal kii niyukti bharat ke raashtrapati paanch varsh ke liye karte hai or ve raashtrapati kii marji par pad par rahete hai. rajyapal rajya sarkaar kaa vidhit mukhiya hota he jisaki kaaryakari karrwai rajyapal ke naam par sampann hoti he.
