 adah kai prakaar ke khanij siliketon ke samooh ko, jo reshedaar tathaa adahya hote hai, kehete hai. iske reshe chamakdaar hote hai. ikattha rahane par unka rang safed, haraa, bhura yaa nila dikhaai padataa he, parantu prayek alag reshe kaa rang chamkila safed hi hota he. is padaarth main anek gun hai, jaise reshedaar banaavat, aattan bal, kadaapan, vidyut ke prati aseem rodhshakti, amla main na ghulana or adahata. in gunon ke kaaran yah bahut se udyongon main kaam aata he.
 adah ko saadhaaran rup se nimnlikhit do jatiyon main baanta ja saktaa he-
 adah kii sabase adhik upyog hone vaali jati kaa chrisotile he. yah padaarth serpentine kii shilaon kii patali dhamniyon main paaya jaataa he or rasinic drushti se saadhaaran meganecium silicket hota he. in dhamniyon main safed yaa hare rang kaa manim reshmi resha paaya jaataa he. is prakaar ke adah kaa 70 pratishat bhaag canada kii quibek khadaanon se nikaala jaataa he. chrisotile-yukt chattan main chrisotile-adah kii maatra bharanusaar 5 se 10 pratishat hoti he. is mail ke reshe bahut achche, majboot, lacheele or atanan bal vaale hote hai. inako aasaani se soot kii tarah kapadon ke rup main buna ja saktaa he. anfebol samooh kii apeksha ushmarodhi shakti kam hoti he tathaa amla main ghulanshiltaa adhik. bharatvarsh main upyukt mail ke adar himachal pradesh, madhya pradesh, andhra pradesh or mysore main paae jate hai.
 reshon ke khadaan main se khodakar or adahayukt pathar ko mashin drillon ke dwaara nikaala jaataa he; tatpashchaat yantric vidhiyon se reshon ko alag kar liya jaataa he. iske liye pathar ko pehle todaa tathaa sukhaya jaataa he, phir krmanusaar ghoomane vaali chakkiyon, belanon, kuttakon, pankhons tathaa adhopati kakshon main pahunchaaya jaataa he or ant main reshon ko ikattha kar liya jaataa he.
 is prakaar kaa adah reshon ke punj ke rup main paaya jaataa he, parantu reshe bahudhaa aniyamit kram ke hote hai.
 in dhamniyon kii lambaai kabhi-kabhi kai foot tak hoti he. is prakaar ke adah nimnlikhit upjatiyon ke paae jate hai:
 pichhali donon upjatiyon ke adah kaa rang safed se halkaa haraa tak hota he. rang kaa gaadhaapan lohe kii maatra ke upar nirbhar he. inke reshe main adhik loch nahin hoti, at ye bunane ke kaam main nahin aa sakate. ye kathinata se pighalate or amla main bahut kam ghulte hai. inaki amla chaanane or vidyut upkaran banane ke kaam main laayaa jaataa he.
 bharatvarsh main adah kii actinolite tathaa tramolite upjatiyaan hi bahutayat se pai jaati hai. inke milane kii jagahen nimnlikhit hai:
 uttrakhand, madhya pradesh, bihar, urisa, tamilnadu or karnataka .
 adah kii khaane mitti kii sataha ke neeche milti hai. 500 se 600 foot neeche tak paae jane vaale adah ko khuli maidan vidhi se nikaala jaataa he. isse or adhik gaharaai main paae jane vaale adah ke nikaalane main ve hi vidhiyaan prayukt hoti hai jo anya dhaatuon ke liye apanaai jaati hai. bharatvarsh main adah haath-barmi se chhedakar or visphot padaarth tathaa hathaudon dwaara phodkar nikaale jate hai, parantu duusare deshon, jaise dakshini america or sanyukt raashtra main, vayuchalit barmon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. adah ko chhedate samay jal kaa prayog nahin kiya janaa, kyonki pani ke saath milane par spanji mishran ban jaataa he, jisamen se isko alag nikaalnaa kathin ho jaataa he. kachche adah ko chaanane ke pashchaat hathaudon se khub peeta jaataa he. isse adah ke reshon main lage hue pathar ke tukade tathaa anya vastuen dur ho jaati hai. iske baad ise kuchalane vaali chakki main daalaa jaataa he. baad main reshon ko hawa ke jhonke se alag kar liya jaataa he. ant main hilte hue chhanane par daalkar unke dwaara shoshak panpon se hawa chuskar dhuli purnataya khinch lee jaati he. iske uparaant adah kaa moolyankan hota he. adah ke nimnlikhit chaar mail bazaar main bheje jate he:
 adah kaa moolyankan isko jalaane ke baad bachi hui raakh ke aadhaar par kiya jaataa he-
 yadi achche adah ko ungliyon ke bich ragada jaae to usase reshmi door jaisi vastu ban jaati he jo khinchne par sheeghra tuutati nahin. ghatiya mail ke adah ke chote-chote tukade ho jate hai; vah kathor bhi hota he.
 achche adah ke patale punj ko yadi anguthe ke nakh se dhire-dhire khinchaa jaae to lacheele tathaa achche atanan vaale reshe milte hai athwa ve maheen reshon main vibhajit ho jate hai, parantu nimna koti ke adah ke reshe bilkul tut jate hai. uttam koti ke adah ke reshon ko masalane se komal goliyaan banaai ja sakati hai, parantu ghatiya adah ke reshe tut jate hai.
 adah ke sabhi prakaar ke vidyutrodhak athwa ushmarodhak banane ke kaam main laayaa jaataa he. iske atirikt inhen amla chaanane, rasinic udyog tathaa rang banane ke kaarkaanon main istemaal kiya jaataa he. lambey reshon ko bun yaa batakar kapadaa tathaa rassi aadi banaai jaati he. inase agnirakshak parade, vastra or aisi hi anya vastuen banaai jaati hai.
 bharat main adah kaa mukhya upyog adahayukt cement tathaa tatsambandhi vastuen, jaise slate, til, pipe or chaadaren banane main kiya jaataa he.
