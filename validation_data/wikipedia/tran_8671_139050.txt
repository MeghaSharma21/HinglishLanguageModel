 dalpat sagar ek jhil he jo jagadalpur ke bheetar sthit he. yah chhattisgarh kii sabase badi kritrim jhiilon main se ek he. varsha jal ko jamaa karne ke liye ise 400 se adhik varsh pehle raja dalpat dev kakatiya ne banwaaya tha. is jhil ke madhya main ek dweep he jis par ek pracheen mandir sthit he. mandir, sthaneey devi kaa he. vahaan tak pahunchne ke liye chappu naav yaa machhlee pakadaney kii naav leni padati he.
 dalpat sagar, indravati nadi par bani he jo jagadalpur ke madhya se guzarati he. yah jhil 350 hectare kshetrafal par faili hui he. yadhyapi is jhil ko mukhya rup se varshajal or fasal ke liye banaaya gaya tha, parantu vartmaan main, yah machhliyon kaa ek pramukh strot he.
