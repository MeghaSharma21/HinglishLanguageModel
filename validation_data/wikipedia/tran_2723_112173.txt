 dach lekhika or raajnneetijna ayaan hirsi ali washington disi sthit american interprise institute kii phelo hai. unki ‘infidel’ naamak sansmaranon kii kitaab kaafii charchaa main rahi he. islam kii kathith aalochana karti film kii patakatha likhane kii vajah se khatre ke saaye main ji raheen holland kii rajaneta or dharm kii pramukh aalochak ayaan hirsi ali kaa maananaa he ki islam par vyapak bahes honi chahie or duniya bhar main uski ‘vyavasthit samiksha’ kii jaani chahie.
 somalia main ek muslim parivaar main janmi or baad main dharm se vidroh karne vaali ali ne kaha ki islam kaa mushkil main hona ek galat vichaar he, agar chizein isi tarah chalati raheen to islam mushkil main zarur pad jayega.
 ali ne varsh 2006 main ‘infidel’ shishark se apana sansmaran prakashit kiya tha or unhone koran shareef kii kuch aayaton kii aalochana karne vaali thev vaan gosh dwaara nirmit film ‘sabamishan’ kii patakatha likhane ke alaava usamen apana swar bhi diya tha. is film se musalamaanon kaa gussa bhadak gaya tha.
 is film se vyapak taur par rosh utpann ho gaya tha or ek kattarpanthi samooh ke sadsy ne varsh 2004 main vaan gosh kii hatya kar di thi. gosh ke sharir main ghonpe gaye chaaku main lage khat main ali kii hatya kii dhamkee di gai thi. ali ke kilaaf fatwa bhi jari kiya gaya he.
