 torai, tori yaa turaai ek lata he jiske fal sabji banane ke kaam aate hai, ise bharat ke kuch rajyon main "jhinggi" yaa "zinga" bhi kaha jaataa he. yah varsha ritu main paidaa hoti he.
 torai bel vaali fasal he, jo jaayad tathaa kharif ritu main safaltaapurvak desh ke kai sthanon main lagaayi jaati he. iske nar va maadaa pushp ek hi bel par alag-alag sthaan par tathaa alag-alag samay par khilte hai. nar pushp pehle tathaa gucchhon main lagate hai jabaki maadaa pushp bel kii parshrv shakhaon par va akele lagate hai. pushp kaa rang chamkila pilaa evam aakarshak hota he. maadaa pushp ke nichle bhaag main fal kii akritiyukt andashay hota he jo nishechan ke pashchaat fal kaa nirmaan karata he. pushp sanykaal main 5 se 8 baje ke dauraan khilte hai. pushpan ke dauraan nar pushpon se jivit va sakriy paragakan praapt hote hai, saath hi maadaa pushpon kii vartikagra nishechan ke liye atyadhik sakriy hoti he. turai me parparagan dwaara nishechan hota he jo mukhyat: madhumakhiyon dwaara sampann hota he.
 yah mulaayam gadhe hare rang kii torai he jo puure bhartiya upmahadweep main ugaai jaati he. pakane ke baad iskaa fibre kathor ho jaataa he, atah yah phir khaane yogya nahi rahati kintu kathor fibre ke kaaran iskaa upyog ragadkar bartanon aadi kii safai main upyog main laayaa jaataa he. paaltu pashuon kii twachaa kii safai main bhi paki torai kaa istemaal hota he. kachi hari torai main vitamins kii bharpoor maatra hoti he, iske adhik supachya hone ke kaaran har aayu ke manushya va rogi ise paushtik aahaar ke rup main prayog main laate hai. bharatvarsh main ghia torai kii prajati bahut adhik prachlit he.
