 lakki maravat pakistan ke kaibar-pakhtuunakva praant ke dakshini bhaag main sthit ek zila he. yah kabhi bannu zile kaa hissaa hua karata tha, lekin 1 july 1992 ko ise ek alag zile kaa darja de diya gaya.
 'lakki maravat' kaa naam kaise padaa is par do kahaniyaan bataai jaati hai. ek katha yah he ki bahut pehle yahan maravat qabeele or vazeer qabeele ke bich yuddh hua tha. iske liye maravat qabeele waalon ne ek lagbhag lakh aadamiyon kii fuj taiyaar kar lee, jis se is sthaan kaa naam 'lakkhi maravat' padaa jo bigadakar 'lakki maravat' ban gaya. duusari katha yah he ki pracheen kaal main iske mukhya shahar kii sthapana ek lakki ram yaa lucco ram naamak hindu vyapaaree ne kii thi. uske or maravat qabeele ke naamo ko jodakar 'lakki maravat' naam pad gaya.
 lakki maravat zile main san 1998 main 4,90,025 logon ki aabaadi thi. iskaa kshetrafal qareeb 3,164 varg kimi he. yahan zyaadaatar pashto boli jaati he haalanki kuch log hindko naam kii ek punjabi upbhasha bhi bolte hai. is zile main maravat qabeele ke pashtun logon kii bahutayat he. yahan ke adhiktar gaanvon or qasbon ne naam 'khel' se ant hote hai, jaise ki aabaa khel, dallo khel, gazani khel, teetar khel, zangi khel, vakairaah. pashto main 'khel' kaa arth 'khandaan' yaa 'birdari' se miltaa-julata hota he. dhyaan dijiye ki in naamon main 'kh' kaa uccharan 'kha' se zaraa bhinn hota he.
 is zile kaa mausam kaafii garam he. garmiyon main 42 se 45 centegrade tak kaa taapmaana hota he or lu bhi chalati he. sardiyon main adhik sardi nahin hoti or is kshetra main aam taur par barf nahin padati he. yahan varsha kaafii kam girti he, haalanki july or agast ke maheene main halaki-si or kam samay ke liye kabhi-kabhi pad jaati he. zile main kuch pahadiyaan or kuch retile maidan hai. yahan kii sabase mukhya nadi kurram nadi he. ek gambila naamak nadi bhi mahatv rakhti he jiskaa pani kurram main hi ja miltaa he.
