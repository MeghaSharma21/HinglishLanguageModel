 yah lekh ganit, vigyaan, praudyogiki aadi main prayukt sutara yaa formula ke vishay main hai. sanskrit sahitya main sutara granthon ke liye sambandhit lekh dekhen.
 ganit main pratikon evam kisi tark-bhashaa ke rachana ke niyamon kaa prayog karte hue banaayi gayi vastu ko sutara kehete hai.
 vigyaan main kisi soochna yaa vibhinn raashiyon ke bich ganitiya sambandh ko sankshipt tareeke se dikhaane ko sutara kehete hai. rasinic sutara bhi kisi tatv yaa yogic ko pratikatmak rup se sankshep main dikhaane kaa tarika maatr he.
