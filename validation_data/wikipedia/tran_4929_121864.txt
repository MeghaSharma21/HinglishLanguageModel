 khoisan yaa khoisaan dakshin africa main rahane vaali do alag jatiyon kaa samuhik naam he jo khoisan bhashaayen bolti hai or apane ird-gird rahane vaale bahusankhyak baantu bhashaa bolne vaali jatiyon se bhinn hai. yah do jatiyaan hai: shikari-faramar jeevani basar karne vaali saan jati or maveshi-paalan karne vaali khoi jati. yah donon samudaay kalahari registan ke kshetra main rahete hai.
 khoisan log apane ird-gird ke baantu logon se alag nazar aate hai. inkaa rang bhura hota he, jabaki baantu log krishna-varniya hote hai. inkaa kad bhi chota hota he: aamtaur par 149 semi se 163 semi tak. inaki aankhon kaa akar bhi baantu logon se thodaa bhinn hota he.
 khoisan logon ke darmyaan anuvanshiki anusandhan karne se pataa chalaa he ki yah pitruvansh or matruvansh ke nazariye se saare maanvon ke nikatatam sanjha purvaj se utpann hui pehali shakhaon ke sadsy hai. inamen atipracheen pitruvansh samooh e, pitruvansh samooh bi or matruvansh samooh bil0 milte hai.
