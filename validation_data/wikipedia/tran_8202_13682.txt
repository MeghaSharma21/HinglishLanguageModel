 ek aspataal kii bhoomi par england ke henry ashtum dwaara banwaaya gaya yah mahal sant james da les ko samarpit tha. nayaa mahal jo henry ke priy whitehaal mahal ke baad duusare kram par aata tha, kaa san 1531 or 1536 ke madhya ek chote ghar ke rup main nirmaan karaayaa gaya tha taaki pratidin ke darbari kaaryon se pruthak samay bitaayaa ja sake. yah paas hi ke whitehaal mahal se aakaar main chota tha. ise tamaam darbaron, sarkari karyaalayon, rajdooton ke awaason va karyaalayon ke ird-gird banaaya gaya tha. isaki sabase pramukh visheshta iskaa uttari dwaar he; jo ek chaar manzila imaarat he or jiske donon ore ashtabhuji minaaren hai. iske upari tal par ek badi ghadi lagi hui he. is vishalkay diwaal ghadi ko baad ke varshon main san 1731 main lagaaya gaya tha. is par henry or uski duusari patni en ke naamon ke prathamakshar H.A. mudrit hai. henry ne is mahal ko laal eenton se banwaaya tha.
 mahal kaa rangarup san 1544 main dobaaraa badla gaya. isaki chhaton ko shahi chitrakaar hans holbain dwaara ranga gaya. ise manamohak shahi bhawan kii sangya di gai. henry ashtum ke do bacchon henry fitzaroy, richmond or somerset kaa pehla duk or mairi pratham kii saint james main mrityu hui thi. elizabeth pratham aksar is mahal main rahaa karti thim or kaha jaataa he ki spani armada ke english channel paar karne se pehle kii raat unhone yaheen intjaar main bitaayi thim. 
 1638 main charles pratham ne yah mahal apni saas mairi di medici ko de diya. mairi yahan teen varshon tak raheen lekin ek catholic purv rani kaa yahan rahanaa sansad ko pasand nahin aayaa or unhen jald hi yah mahal chhodkar cologn chale jane ko kaha gaya. charles pratham ne apni ghoshit mrityu se pehle kii apni raat saint james main hi bitaayi thi. oliver cromwel ne iske baad ise angreji rashtrakul ke dauraan sainya chhavnee main badal diya. charles dwitiya, james dwitiya, mairi dwitiya or ain in sabhi kaa janm isi mahal main hua tha.
