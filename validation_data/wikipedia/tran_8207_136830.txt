 kalam kisi paudhe kaa aisa ansh hota he jiskaa prayog audyaniki main vanaspatik janan ke liye kara jaataa ho. aksar yah ansh ek tahani hota he jiskaa ek ant dharti main lagaakar pani or anya poshak tatv dete rahane se paudha ugane lagata he. kabhi-kabhi kalam ko kisi duusare paudhe ke jad-sahit kate hue tane ke upar lagaaya jaataa he - ise kalam-bandhna kehete he or isse sanyukt paudhe main donon mul paudhon ke kuch mishrit gun aa jate hai.
