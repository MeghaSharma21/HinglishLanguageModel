 ye jawani he divaani 2013 main prakashit bollywood kii hasyapradhan or yuva pasand film he jiske nirmaataa karan johar hai or yah ayaan mukherjee dwaara nirdeshit he. is film main mukhya bhoomikaaon main ranbir kapur or deepika padukon hai. 2008 kii film bachanaa i hasino ke baad ek saath pratham film he. aditya roy kapur or kalki kekalan sahakalakaar hai. pehle ise march 2013 main pradarshit kiya janaa tay hua tha lekin baad main 31 may 2013 ko pradarshit kii gai.
 ye javaani he divaani do ekadam juda ladkaa-ladki ke bich kii prem kahani he. kabir thapar urf bani ek bephikr ladkaa he jiskaa sapana duniya ghoomana he or jise vivaah ke bandhan main yakeen nahin he. wo apane pita or sauteli maa ke saath rahata he. avi or aditi bani ke sabase achche dost hai. aditi ko avi se pyaar he lekin usne ye baat kabhi zaahir nahin kii. ye teenon dost manali jate hai. naina jo bani kii sahpaathee thi or ab medical kii padhai kar rahi he ek brake chaahati he or isliye in teenon doston ke saath manali ke liye nikal padati he. yatra ke dauraan bani or naina ek duusare kaa saath pasand karne lagate hai haalanki donon ke bich zaraa bhi samanta nahin he. phir ek din naina, bani ke saamne apana pyaar jataane vaali hoti he lekin tabhi pataa chalata he ki bani ko chicago university main chaatravrutti mil gai he. ab kyonki bani ko ghoomane kaa shauk he isliye wo is maukey ko ganvaana nahin chahta or chicago chalaa jaataa he. aath varsh pashchaat bani apni dost aditi kii shaadi ke liye bharat lautataa he jahaan uski mulaakaat dobaaraa naina se hoti he. ek baar phir purana pyaar jaagta he lekin naina khud ko aage badhane se roak leti he kyonki bani to duniya ghoomana chahta he lekin wo apane parivaar auki clinics ko nahin chhodana chaahati. is prakaar film aage badhati he.
 film ke nirmaataa hiru yash johar or karan johar hai or yah dharma productions ne banaai he. iske sahanirmaataa apurva mehta hai.
 film kaa kaa filmakan hidimba devi mandir gulba, banjaar, hamta or naggar hai. kuch drishy samudr tal se 14000 foot kii oonchaai oar bhi filmaae gayi hai jahaan taapmaana -10 degree celsius he. may 2012 main kuch drishy udaipur main bhi filmeet kiye gayi jinamein deepika or ranbir par filmaaya gaya romani gaanaa bhi he.baad main garmi kii vajah se sadasyon ko rajasthan main filmakan main pareshaani anubhav hui. agast 2012 main shooting rue mofetad ke street market main armbh hui. anya drishy filmistan, mumbai main filmaae gayi. 26 janvari 2013 ko ek sangeet anukram or kuch drishy filmaane film pratibhaagi kashmir kii yatra par gayi. unhone gulmarg main kongdori, pahalgam or srinagar jaise vibhinn sthanon par filmaaye.
 ranbir kapur ko sarvapratham film ke liye chunaa gaya, uske baad ayaan mukherjee ko nirdesan vek ap cid ke baad duusari ke liye chunaa gaya. kapur ne is film main apni bhoomika ke liye hami kar di. mahila kalakaar ke liye katrina caff, deepika padukon or anoushka sharma ke liye bahut atakalbaazi kii gai. tatpashchaat padukon ko chunaa gaya. aditya roy kapur or kalki kekalan ko baad main sahyogi kalakaar kii bhoomika ke liye chunaa gaya. kekalan or kapur ne kramshah aditi or avi ke kirdaar nibhaaye. sharma ne isaki pushti kii ki vah vimudh kirdaar nibhaayegi.
