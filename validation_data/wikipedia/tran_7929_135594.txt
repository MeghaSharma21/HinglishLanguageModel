 chilas yaa chalas, paak-adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-balatistan kshetra ke diamer zile main sthit ek chota-sa qusba he jo us zile kii rajdhani bhi he. yah karakoram rajmarg ke dwaara resham maarg se judata he saath hi yah rajmarg ise dakshin me islamabad se jodta he. karakoram rajmarg par chilas se islamabad ke raste main daasu, manasehara, abtabad or haripur nagar aate hai jabaki, iske uttar main yah gilgit or sost ke raste chini shaharon kashgaar or tashkurgan se judata he. 
 shahrah-e-karakoram ke saath-saath ab tak taqariban 20,000 se zyaada aisi chattaane or sthaan mili hai jo pracheen samskruti ke chihn dete hai. gilgit-blatistan main hunzaa or hariban kii jaanib das badi jagahen puraatan sthalon kii maujuudagi kii vajah se nihaayat ahm hai. yah un guzare hue hamalaavaron, vyapaariyon or yatriyon kii yaadgaar hai jinhonen prachinkaal main yahan safar kiya. 5000 se 1000 isapurv ke daur kii yahan shilaayein taraashi hui hai jinamein jaanavar, ticone manushya or shikaar karte log dikhaae gaye hai. jaanwaron kaa aakaar manushyon se bada darshaya gaya he. yah chattaanon par pathar ke auzaaron se banaae gaye the or un auzaaron kii chotein bhi aaj tak dikhaai detii hai.
 maahir asaar qadima karal jait maar ne in tamaam asaar kaa babor mushahida kya or is ilaake kii tarikh ko yakaja kya he. in tamaam mushahid at ko in ke mashhoor zamana tasaneef main mulaahizaa kii ja saktaa he. 
 pakistan ke mahakama asaar qadima ke mutabik in asaar main firo sattani lafz obbooh yaa kambwo kasarat se mila he. is lafz kaa taqariban har qadeem nishaani main maujuud hona mahakama ke mutabik is baat kii nishandahi karata he ki chalas bhi ek vakt main zarur qadeem riyasat kambojh kaa hissaa rahaa ho ga.
 chilas kaa intizaam vafaaqi idaare chalaate hai, jo ki gilgit-blatistan kii sarkari vyavastha ke tahat he. yahan garmiyaan garm or sardiyaan kushak or sard hoti hai. yahan pahunchane ke do bade raste hai. pehla rasta karakoram rajmarg he jabaki doosraa kaayaan vaadi se hote huai babusar darre se aata he. chilas vaadi ke thik purv se sindhu nadi bahati he or gair-mulki sailaani ko yahan aane or hotalon main thaharne ke liye vishesh ijazat milani zaruri hoti he.
