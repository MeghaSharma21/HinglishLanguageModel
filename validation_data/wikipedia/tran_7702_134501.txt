 yogachaar buddha darshan evam manovigyaan kaa ek pramukh shakha he. yah bhartiya mahayan kii upashakha he jo chauthi shatabdi main astitv main ai.
 yogacharya is baat kii vyakhya karata he ki hamaara man kis prakaar hamaare anubhavon kii rachana karata he. yogachaar darshan ke anusaar man se baahar samvednaa kaa koi strot nahin he.
