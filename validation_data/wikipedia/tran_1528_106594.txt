 chuttee parampara yaa kaanoon dwaara nirdhaarit aisa din hota he jab saadhaaran dainik kaary sthagit yaa kam kar diye jate hai. aamtaur se chhuttiyaan vyaktiyon ko kisi rashtriya, dharmik, saanskrithik yaa saamaajik parampara yaa ghatana manaane ke liye di jaati hai. chhuttiyaan main kaam kitna ghataya jaataa he, yah sthaneey niyam, parampara, kaary-prakaar or vyaktigat rujhaan par nirbhar karata he.
