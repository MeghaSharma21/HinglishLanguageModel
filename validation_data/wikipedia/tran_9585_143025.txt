 manipur chakra tantra or yoga sadhana kii chakra sankalpana kaa teesraa chakra he. mani kaa arth he gahanaa or poor kaa arth he sthaan. yah naabhi ke peeche sthit hota he. iskaa aadhaar tatv agni hone ke kaaran ise 'agni' yaa 'surya kendra' bhi kehete hai. 
 sadhak kii kundalini ke manipur chakra main pahunchane par vah swaadhishthan chakra ke nishedhaatmak pakshon par vijay paa letaa he. iske saath hi use spashtataa, aatmavishvaas, anand, aatm bharosa, gyaan, buddhi or sahi nirnay lene kii yogyata jaise bahumoolya maniyon sareekhe gun praapt hote hai.yah chakra sphurti kaa kendra he. yah sadhak kaa swasthya sudrudh or pusht karne ke liye uski urja niyantrit karata he. yah brahmand se praan ko apni ore aakarshith karata he.yah chakra agnyashay or pachak tantra kii prakriya ko viniyamit karata he. is kendra main avrodh pachan main kharabiyaan, parisanchari rog, madhumeh or raktchaap main utaar-chadhaav jaisi kai swasthya samasyaon kaa kaaran ban saktaa he.
 is chakra kaa pratinidhi rang pilaa he. iskaa pratinidhi pashu medhaa he. iskaa anuroop tatva agni he. iskaa pratik chinh das pankhudiiyon vaala camel he. yah maanav sharir kii sabhi prakriyaaon kaa niyantran or poshan karne vaale das praanon kaa pratik he. iskaa doosraa pratik neeche kii ore sheersh bindu vaala tribhuj he. yah urja ke failaav, udgam or vikaas kaa dyotak he. is chakra ke devtaa vishnu or lakshmi hai. vishnu udiyaman maanav chetana ke pratik hai. lakshmi bhautik or aadhyatmik samruddhi kii pratik hai. iskaa mantra he ram.
