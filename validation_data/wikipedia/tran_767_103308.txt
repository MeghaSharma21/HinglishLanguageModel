 sahyog kaa arth do yaa adhik vyaktiyon yaa sansthaaon kaa milakar kaam karnaa he. sahyog kii prakriya main gyaan kaa barambar tathaa sabhi dishaao main aadan-pradaan hota he. yah ek samaan lakshya kii prapti kii disha main uthaaya gaya buddhi vishyak kaary he. yah jaruri nahin he ki sahyog ke liye netritv kii aavashyak hota he. parantu yah aavashyaktaa nahin he ki netritv kaa bhi sahyog nahin hota he. sahyog ek dusre ke madhyam se hota he. phir bhi aapasi sahyog kaa hona bahut jaruri hota he. ek dusre ko aage badhaana chahie. iskaa matlab yah nahin ki kisi bhi sanstha ke sahyog karne se aapasi sahyog ki sambhaavna bahut adhik hoti he. phir bhi paaramparik yaa aarthik sahyog kaa hona bahut myne rakhata he. sahyog ki bhaavana har manushya main hona chahie. or main dusro ke sahyog kar sakun. ek dusre ke sahyog se desh ho yaa manushya ki jivan main badhata rahata he. sahyog bhi kai prakaar ke hote hai. aarthik ho yaa takaniki sahyog iske aadi or bhi ho sakate hai. parantu sahyog ek vishisht kaary ke rup main hota he. tab par bhi ghar ho yaa baahar kisi jagah par sahyog kaa aadan-pradaan kiya ja saktaa he. parantu yah aavashyak nahin he ki rupaya se hi sahyog kiya ja saktaa he. jivan main manushya kaa sahyog ek kartatv hi nahin balki manushya kii jivan style main sahyog kaa hona jaruri he. sahyog kii koi seemaa nirdhaarit nahin hota he jo kabhi bhi kisi vyakti kaa sahyog kiskaa mil sake. tab par bhi apane ko ek saamaanya kii bhanti jivan main manushya kii sahyog karnaa chahie. kisi sanstha ho yaa dharm sthaan ko aap sahyog kar sakate hai. chaahe hindu kii dharm sthaan ho yaa muslim kii kisi bhi dharm sthaan yaa sanstha yaa socity main apana sahyog kar sakate hai. kisi bhi desh ke netaa dusre desh jate hai to vah ek dusre ko aapasi sahyog kii bhaavana banaata he. or donon deshon kii aapas main sahyog karata dekha jaataa he. tab par bhi donon desh ke bich aapasi sahyog kaa hona jaruri hota he. sahyog kisi bhi kshetra main aap de sakate hai. aarthik ho yaa takneekee evam anya kshetron main aap sahyog kar sakate hai. sahyog kaa matlab hota he kisi bhi prakaar kaa ho saktaa he. vikaas ho yaa urja yaa parmaanu or takanic aadi main sahyog ho saktaa he. or kisi bhi desh ke saath ek dusre ke liye aap kisi bhi kshetra main aap sahyog karne ke liye taiyaar ho jate hai. kyonki sahyog kaa arth hota he. ek dusre kaa jab tak kisi bhi desh dusre desh se sahyog nahin karte hai. tab tak aage nahin batch saktaa he. or kisi bhi desh chahta he ki kisi bhi desh se sahyog bana rahe. tabhi bhi vikaas ho saktaa he. chaahe netaa ho yaa saadhaaran vyakti koi bhi vyakti chaahate hai ki sahyog bana rahe. or bharat ki baat he. to bharat ke liye ek mahatvapurn baat hota he ki kisi bhi desh ke liye sahyog bana hona. jivan main kisi se bhi sahyog ho saktaa he. parantu yah kehnajaruri nahin he. kab kiskaa sahyog kab mil jaayegaa. kyonki koi kisi ko nahin jaanate hai ki kiskaa sahyog kisko milegaa. or har aadmi chaahate hai ki mujhase jitna ho sake sahyog main karne ke liye taiyaar rahata hun. sahyog har aadmi kii bhaavana hona chahie. ki main sahyog kar sakun. kyonki jivan main kya lekar aaye hai. yahi aadmi kaa ek mauka miltaa he ki main dusre kaa sahyog kar sakun. chaahe apana ho yaa paraayaa kisi ko aap sahyog kar sakate hai. isamen kisi pe sahyog karne ke liye dabaab nahin banaaya jaataa he. kyonki sahyog dabaab kaa jeej nahin he. har aadmi ko koi na koi sahyog karata he. chahen aap ki jivan main patni ho yaa gaanv kaa log zarur sahyog karata he. yah jaruri nahin he ki aap kaa sahyog gaanv kaa hi log kar sakate hai. kahin or kaa aadmi bhi ho sakate hai. sahyog hi ek aise shakti pradaan karata he. jo aadmi ko lakshya tak pahunchaata he. har aadmi main sahyog kii bhaabana hona chahie. manushya hi ek aise praani he. jo har kisi ko sahyog kar saktaa he. kyonki sahyog se kisi ko safalataa milte hai. to use hamen sahyog karnaa chahie. kisi bhi tarah kaa sahyog aap karnaa chaahate hai. to aap kar sakate hai. sahyog ke liye kisi ko koi dabaab nahin diya jaataa he. jo apni shradha ke anusaar aap de sakate hai. lekin kisi bhi santhaan ke liye aap apni sahyog kaa maapdand aap par nirbhal karata he. or main usamen kis tarah kaa bhaagidaari ban sake. har kisi ko sahyog ki bhaavana hona chahie. kyonki sahyog ek aise baat nahin he. jo dusro ke liye kiya ja saktaa he. parantu jab tak kisi bhi sanstha ke rup main kaam karti he. sahyog har aadmi ko karnaa chahie. sahyog se aadmi kaa manoval badata he. sahi samay par kiya gaya kaam acha maane jate hai. sahyog ek dusre ke saath milakar kiya janaa chahie. parantu ek apni disha ke anusaar kaam karnaa chahie. aaj sahyog ke liye apana kaam karata he. sahyog har gaanv yaa samaaj main hota he. chahen kisi bhi jati kaa ho saktaa he. par sahyog sab ki kerani chahie. or kisi bhi prakaar kaa aap ko sahyog karnaa chahie. sahyog sabase bada manushya ke liye mahatvapurn kaam he. jivan ke saare pahalu main hamesh sahyog kaa bahut hi mahatvapurn bhumika hoti he. har harah se manushya kaa sahyog karnaa chahie. sahyog ek aise kaam ke liye jo dusre ke liye aage aane kaa mauka mil jaataa he. sahyog hi ek he jo har desh, gaanv, samaaj yaa kisi bhi jati varg kaa aap sahyog karte hai. aapane dekha hi hoga ki jis tarah kaa uttrakhand main badal fatne se kitne aadmi kaa maut hua he. usee tarah samaaj ko aage laane ke liye log sahyog kar rahe hai. sahyog ek vaise cheez he jo har prakaar se aap sahyog kar sakate hai. jis tarah kaa uttrakhand main hua he agar aap is main sahyog nahin kijie ga to kis main kijie ga. yah bahut bady nuksaan hua he. desh ke liye. is liye sahyog karnaa chahie. har jati dharm ke liye sahyog bahut maine rakhte hai. gaanv ho yaa shahar kahin par bhi aap kisi prakaar kaa sahyog kar sakate hai. sahyog ke liye koi pabandhi nahin he. kisi bhi desh kaa aadmi koi bhi desh main sahyog kar saktaa he. bharat main hi nahin puure sansar main aap kahin bhi sahyog kar sakate hai. or kisi bhi desh main koi aadmi sahyog karnaa chaahate hai. to us aadmi ko koi nahin roak saktaa sahyog karne se chahen sarkaar ho yaa aam aadmi kuch nahin kar sakate hai. kisi bhi vyakti ko personal kaam main bandha nahin karnaa chahie. har manushya kii jivan main ek vyaktigat rup se apni koi na koi khyaal rakhata he. or kisi bhi paristhiti main vah apana kaam karte rahete hai. yahi kaaran hota he. jab logon kii apni bhaavana hoti he ki main dusro kii sahyog kar sakate hai. to is main kisi ko virodh nahin karnaa chahie. or jivan main har prakaar kaa kaam karnaa chahie. jo manushya ke liye laabhadaayak hoti he. vahi karnaa chahie.
