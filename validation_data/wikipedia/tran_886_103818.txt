 kisi dhaatu yaa mishradhatu se dhaale hue varnmaala ke aksharon ko 'tip' kehete hai. tip kaa samooh banaakar or usapar syaahi lagaakar chhapane kii kala ko mudran kala yaa tip kala kehete hai. chapai kii pramukh kala hone ke naate tip kala kaa avishkaar maanav ke sarvottam avishkaron main ullekhneey he. vidwaanon kii vidvatta, budhimaanon kii budhimatta, netaaon ke aadesh, kalakaaron kii kala, sabhi ko tipakala ne amar banaaya he. maanav ko vaigyanik, aarthik, raajnneetik tathaa saamaajik kshetron main pragati ke path par nirantar agrasar karanevaali yahi kala he.
 chapai kii vividh kalaon main tipakala kaa sthaan anyatam he. isaki pramukh pratidwandvi kalaayen, litho ofaset tathaa photogravyor atyadhik pragatisheel hai. kintu tip kala apane vistrut kshetra tathaa aganya suvidhaon kaa nivaaran karne ke jitne saadhan tip kala main uplabdh hai utane chapai kii kisi or kala main nahin hai. tip se chhapanevaali mashine bhi duusari kalaon kii mashiinon kii tulana main atyadhik tej chalati hai. tip kala se chapai, kuch prakaar ke rangeen kaaryon ko chhodkar, sasti bhi padati he. atev tip kala kaa prayog pustakon, samachaarpatron patrikaon, lekhansaamagree, farm, dibbe, dibbiyaan ityadi chhapane main vyapak rup se hota he. photography athwa yantric vidhiyon se chitra banane tathaa yantron dwaara tip ko compoj tathaa photo chitron ko compoj karne kii pranaaliyon kaa vikaas ho jane ke kaaran tip kala kii upyogita atyadhik bad gai he.
 tip kala kaa avishkaar maanav kii pragati ke aarambhik padchihnon ke saath saath hua. apni aantarik bhaawanaaon ko dusron par vyakt karne ke uddeshy se maanav ne shilaon par aakaar khodna siikhaa. aksharon tathaa shabdon ke vikaas ke saath saath sumer, babilonia, misr, bharat, china or korea main lakdi, shila tathaa dhaatu kii mohren or thappe prayog main aane lage. 11veen shatabdi main pi shing ne china main pehali baar mitti ko pakaakar tip banaae. sisa, ronga tathaa surama kii mishradhatu se pehali baar gatanberg ne germany main 15veen shatabdi main tip banaae. unhone chapai kaa yantra tathaa syaahi banaakar tip kala kaa purna rup se avishkaar kiya.
 tip kala upyogi evam audyogic or audyogic kala hone ke saath hi lalit kala bhi he. aksharon, shabdon, chitron tathaa aakaaron kaa pradarshan, prastut vishay ke hetu, aise upyukt dhang se hona chahie ki jahaan padhanevaalon ko vishay kaa abhipraay grahan karne main puri sahaayta mili, vahaan sundarataa or swatchta kaa bhi samavesh ho. pustak yaa anya chapanevaali vastu ke vidhaan, pariman tathaa khaake uske vishay ke anukool hi hone chahie.
 tip kala ke nimnlikhit teen siddhant maane gaye hai -
 parimanik siddhanth ke anusaar pustak yaa anya chapanevaali vastuon kii maap, tip kii maap, tip tathaa blaakon kii akruti or kshetrafal ityadi vyaavhaarik dhang se upyukt hone chahie. iske upkram, kaagaj kaa parimanik maap, mudran kii mashiinon kaa maap tathaa padhanevaalon kii suvidha he.
 vaidhanik siddhant ke anusaar chapanevaali vastu kii ruparekha pehle bana leni chahie or tadantar use karyanvit karnaa chahie. vyaavhaarik kathinaaiyon ko dur karne tathaa mitvyay kii simaaon main rahete hue mudrankaarya vidhipurvak sampann karnaa chahie.
 chakshush siddhant ke anusaar tip kala ko lalit kalaon kii maryaadaaon kaa paalan karnaa padataa he. abhikalp banane main syaahi, rang, tip, akruti, kaagaj kii anurupata, samatolan tathaa sthirataa ityadi kaa yathochit dhyaan rakhana chahie. is kshetra main tip kala ko lalit kalaon kii tarah har nai vichaardhaara ko apnaane ke liye prastut rahanaa chahie.
 aaj ke parmaanu yug main duusari kalaon kii bhanti tip kala bhi pragatisheel he. saarvajanik yantrikaran ke saath-saath anuyantron ne is kala par bhi apana aadhipaty sthapit kar liya he. hastkaushal kaa sthaan anushaktiyaan grahan kar rahi hai, kintu phir bhi tip kala par se lalit kalaon kaa prabhutv praaia: kabhi nahin hat sakegaa.
