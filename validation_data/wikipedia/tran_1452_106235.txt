 dhanik, naatyashaastra ke granth 'dasharup' ke tikaakar hai. dasharup ke pratham prakaash ke ant main 'iti vishnusunordhaniksya kritau dasharoopawaloke' is nirdesh se gyaat hota he ki dhanik dasharupak ke rachayita, vishnusut dhananjay ke bhai the. donon bhai munjaraj ke sabhapandit the. munj or uske uttaradhikari ke shasankaal ke anusaar inkaa samay isa kii dasvin shatabdi kaa ant or gyarahavin kaa prarambh mana jaataa he.
 dasharup 'munjamahishagoshtee' ke vidwaanon ke man ko prasannataa or prem se nibaddh karanevaala kaha gaya he. dhanik dwaara isaki 'avlok' naam kii tikaa munj ke uttaradhikari ke shasankaal main likhi gai.
 dasharup mukhyat: bharatnatyashaastra kaa anugami he or uskaa ek prakaar se sankshipt rup he. chaar prakaashon main yah granth vibhakt he. prarambh ke teen prakaashon main natya ke bhed, upbhed, nayak aadi kaa varnan he or chaturth prakaash ke rason kaa. dasharup main rangamanch par vivechan nahin kiya gaya he or na dhanik ne hi isapar vichaar kiya he. dhanik kii tikaa gadhy main he or mul granth ke anusaar he. anek kaavyon or naatakon se sankalit udaahranon aadi dwaara yah mul granth ko purna, bodhgamy or saral karti he. kuch parvarti vidwaanon ne dhanik ko hi 'dasharup' kaa rachayita mana he or unheen ke naam se 'dasharup' kii karikas uddhrut kii hai, yah bhramaatmak he.
 dhanik abhidhaavaadi or dhvanivirdohi hai. rasnishpatti ke sambandh main ve bhattnayak ke mat ko maanate hai, par usamen bhatta lollat or shankuk ke maton kaa mishran kar dete hai. is prakaar inkaa ek swatantra mat ho jaataa he. dasharup ke chaturth prakaash main dhanik ne isapar vistrut rup se vichaar kiya he. naatak main shant ras ko dhanik ne sweekaar nahin kiya he or aath ras hi maane hai. shant ko ye abhinay main sarvatha nishiddh karte hai, at: sham ko sthaayee bhi nahin maanate.
 dhanik kavi the or inhone sanskrit-praakrit kavy bhi likhaa he. 'avlok' main inke anek lalit padya idhar-udhar udaahranon ke rup main bikhare pade hai. 'avlok' se hi yah bhi gyaat hota he ki dhanik ne sahityashastra kaa ek granth or likhaa jiskaa naam 'kavyanirnay' he. dasharup ke chaturth prakaash kii 37veen karika kii vyakhya main dhanik ne 'yathavocham kavyanirnaye' kaha he or uski saat karikas uddhrut kii hai. inamen vyanjanavadiyon ke purvapaksh ko uddhrut kar unka khandan kiya gaya he.
