 dwitiyak cell yaa 'richargeble bydaree' main vidyut dhara pravaahit karakar unamem vidyut urja bharne vaali electronic yuktiyon ko bydaree aaveshak kehete hai. ye praayah 220 volt asi se vidyut urja lekar use kam voltage kii disi main badal dete hai. inamen praayah aaveshan dhara kaa niyantran karne or adhik charge hone ke pehle hi charge karnaa rokane kii vyavastha hoti he.
 inkaa upyog kshetra bahut vistrut he. jahaan-jahaan dwitiyak bydaree he, vahaan-vahaan bydaree-aaveshak he. car aadi gadiyon ke bydaree aaveshak, laptop ke aaveshak, cell phone ke aaveshak, tathaa upies ke aaveshak kuch pramukh udhaaharan hai.
 kisi bhi bydaree ko charge karne hetu usamen dhara pravaahit kerani padati he jisaki disha aisi ho ki bydaree ke dhanagar main ghuskar uske runagra se nikle. dhara kii maatra or anya baaten charge kii jane vaali bydaree yaa cell kii prakruti par nirbhar karti he. udhaaharan ke liye trek kii bydaree ko aaweshit karne hetu 20 ampear dhara di ja sakati he jabaki kisi pencil cell ko charge karne ke liye ek ampir se bhi kam dhara chaiye.
 bydaree aaveshak ke vidyut paripath main anya baton ke alaava ek asi se disi karne vaala dishtakaari tathaa bydaree main jane vaali dhara ko uchit maan par banaaye rakhane vaala dhara niyantrak hota he. bydaree charger bhinn-bhinn prakaar ke hote hai. kam shakti ke aaveshkon main aajkal esampies par aadhaarit paripath prayog kiya jaataa he jisase inkaa aakaar apekshaakrit bahut chota hota he.
