 mulamattaman power stesan bharat main sabase badi bhumigat jal vidyut pariyojna he, idukki baandh se pani bhumigat pipe dwaara yahan le aakar 669.2 meter uchaai se neeche pani 6 janaretaron pe giraakar 780 megawatt kaa bijli udpaadan karata he. bijli udpaadan ke baad main, pani triveni sangman pe totupurayar nadi ja milte he, isliye totupurayar nadi 365 dinon main pani se bharpur he. kadi suraksha ke vajah se paryatakon ko pravesh pratibandh he.
