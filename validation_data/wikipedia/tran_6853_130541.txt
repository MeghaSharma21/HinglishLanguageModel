 nita lulla ek costum diziner or fishan stylist hai, jinhone 300 se adhik filmon main kaam kiya he. vah shaadi ke kapade 1985 ke baad se dizin kar rahi hai. aishwarya raay or maadhuri dikshit dwaara devdas, main unke paridhaan pahane se unka naam bollywood main ban gaya joki bollywood kii pravriti kaayam karne vaali film he. uski pehali badi graahak, aabhushan diziner varun jaani he, haalanki us samay jaani ne apane kaarobaar kii shuruyaat nahin kii thi. uske baad lulla ne puri tarah se patibaddh hone ke liye ek bollywood graahak aadhaar banaaya jisame unhone abhinayta spana ke liye paridhaan banaae jo bollywood ke dakshin bharat samudaay main pramukh he. is safalataa ke dwaara unhone  abhinetriyon salama aagaa or shridevi ke paridhaan banane kaa mauka mila.
