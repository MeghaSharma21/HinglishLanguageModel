 royal uniwarsity of bhutan yah vishvavidhyalay bhutan kaa rashtriya vishvavidhyalay he. is vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana 2 june 2003 ko hui.
 yah vishvavidhyalay shiksha ke teen stareey prabandhan ko sugathit karne ke uddeshy se sthapit kiya gaya he. yah vishvavidhyalay sambaddh vishvavidhyalay he. iske antargat rajya ke 8 ghatak mahavidhyalay tathaa ek sambaddh mahavidhyalay he. is vishvavidhyalay ke antargat nimna mahavidhyalay he:-
 
 is vishvavidhyalay ke ghatak mahavidhyalay apane apane mahavidhyalay main shaikshanik vyavastha ke liye swatantra hai. vais -chancelar kii dekh rekh main in mahavidhyalayon kii shaikshik kriya kalap or anusandhan kaary rahata he. 
 royal uniwarsity of bhutan nimna aath paathyakramon ko sanchalit karti he. 
 yah vishvavidhyalay do varshiya vibhinn paathyakramon main diploma pradaan karata he. is diploma ke pashchaat degree paathyakram main pravesh kii suvidha rahati he yaa yo kahiye ki degree kii purv tayari hi diploma paathyakram he. 
 yah vishvavidhyalay 90 paathyakramon main 3 varshiya degree pradaan karata he. 
 yah vishvavidhyalay 90 paathyakramon main 4 varshiya onors degree pradaan karata he.
