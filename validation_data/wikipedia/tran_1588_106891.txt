 dwitiya angl-burma yuddh 1852 isvi main hua tha. verma ke pratham yuddh kaa ant yandbu kii sandhi se hua tha parantu yah sandhi burma ke itihaas main zyada kargar siddh nahin hui or yah sandhi samaapt ho gai. is sandhi ke samaapan kaa kaaran yah tha ki sandhi ke pashchaat kuch angreji vyapaaree burma ke dakshini tat par bas gaye or vahiin se apane vyapaar kaa sanchaalan prarambh kiya. kuch samay pashchaat in vyapaariyon ne burma sarkaar ke nirdeshon evam niyamon kaa ulanghan karnaa prarambh kar diya. is kaaran burma sarkaar ne un vyapaariyon ko dandit kiya jiske falswarup angrej vyapaariyon ne angreji shashan se san 1851 isvi main sahaayta maangi. lord dalhousie ne is avasar kaa phayda uthaakar san 1852 isvi main burma ke viruddh yuddh kii ghoshnaa kar di. is yuddh main angrejon ne burma ko parajeet kiya or martavaan evam rangoon par angrejon kaa adhikaar ho gaya. is yuddh ke pashchaat burma kaa samast dakshini bhaag angreji sarkaar ke adhikaar main aa gaya.,.
