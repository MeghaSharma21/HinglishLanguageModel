  indian union muslim lig, saamaanya taur par muslim lig naam se mashhoor, bharat kii ek rajnatik party he jise bhartiya chunaav ayog ne kerala rajya ke dal ke rup main manyata de rakhi he.
 indian union muslim lig kaa gathan to 10 march 1948 ko hua parantu iskaa itihaas or pehle se he jo akhil bhartiya muslim lig ke gathan ke saath shuruyaat hota he. jisane pakistan banavaane main sakriy bhoomika nibhaai. angrejon se mukt hone par or pakistan ke ban jane ke baad muslim lig kai dalon main bant gai or yah isaki kamajori kaa bahut bada kaaran bana. pakistan main muslim lig naam ke kai sanghathan aaj bhi kaary kar rahe hai. 1948 main bharat main indian union muslim lig kaa gathan hua jo pichhade hue musalamaanon ke hit ke liye kaam karti aa rahi he. ab muslim lig kii lokapriyata badhane lagi he or is party kaa dhire dhire bharat main vistaar ho rahaa he.
