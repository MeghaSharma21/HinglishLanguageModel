 2011 kaa senda bukamp or tsunami, japan ke oshika prayadweep, ke purvi tat se 130 kilometre kii duuri par 11 march, 2011 ko 05:46 UTC par aayaa ek mahavinashi bukamp he, jisaki tivrata richter paimaane par 9.0 maapi gayi he. is bukamp kaa adhikendra japan ke purvi tat se 130 kilometre kii duuri par or avakendra 24.4 kilometre kii gaharaai par sthit tha.
 bukamp ke kaaran duniya ke lagbhag 20 deshon jinamein uttari america or dakshin america kaa puraa prashanth tateey kshetra shaamil he, main tsunami chetavanee jari kii gayi or japan ke prashanth tat ko logon se khaali karaayaa gaya. bukamp ke falswaroop 10 unchi tsunami lehren utpann huin jinhone japan ke tateey kshetron main bade paimaane par kahar barapaaya. japan main yah lehren tat se 10 kilometre andar tak aa gayin. 
 japan kii rashtriya police agency ne aadhikarik taur par japan ke 16 praanton main, 10,872 logon ke marane, 2,776 logon ke ghayal hone or 16,244 logon ke laapata hone kii pushti kii he, lekin gaiyr sarkari khabaron ke mutabik hataahaton kii sankhya isse kahin zyada he. bukamp ke kaaran japan main vyapak kshati hui he, jinamein sadakon or railway ko hue baari nuksaan ke alaava jagah jagah aag lagane se hui haani bhi shaamil he. ek baandh ke dhahne kii bhi khabar he.
 purvottar japan main lagbhag 44 lakh gharon kii vidyut aapurti thap ho gayi he or 14 lakh gharon kii peyajal aapurti prabhavit hui he. tsunami ke chalate bahut se bijli gharon main vidyut utpaadan thap ho gaya he or kam se kam do parmaanu bijligharon se radiodharmee vikiran kaa khatra paidaa ho gaya he, jiske kaaran inke aasapaas ke kshetra ko khaali karaayaa gaya he, puure kshetra main apatkaal ghoshit kar diya gaya he. phukushima-1 parmaanu urja sanyantr main praarambhik bukamp ke lagbhag 24 ghante baad ek visphot hua he, haalanki is visphot se sanyantr ke bahari kancreet dhaanche ko nuksaan hua he par bhitari bhaag surakshit bataaya jaataa he. phukushima-1 parmaanu urja sanyantr ke 20 kilometre or phukushima-2 parmaanu urja sanyantr ke 10 kilometre ke daayre main aane vaale ilaake ko puri tarah khaali kara liya gaya he. 
 senda main aayaa bukamp japan ke darj itihaas main aane vaala ab tak kaa sabase vidhwanskaari or vishwa ke aadhunik itihaas main aane vaale paanch sabase vinaashkari bukampon main se ek he.
