 khwaja shams-al-deen mohammad haafiz shiraji ek vichaarak or kavi the jo apni farsi gajalon ke liye jane jate hai. unki kavitaaon main rahasyamay prem or bhakthi kaa mila jula asar dikhataa he jise soofivaad ke ek stambh ke rup main dekha jaataa he. unki gazalon kaa dewan, iran main, har ghar main pai jane vaali kitabon main se ek he or lagbhag sabhi bhashaaon main anudit ho chukaa he. unki mazaar iran ke shiraj shahar main sthit he jahaan unhone apana puraa jivan bitaayaa.
 farsi samskruti par unke amit chhap ke baavjood unki zindagi ke shuruaati daur ke baar main kuch tathypurna jaankaree nahin he. kinvadantiyon ke mutabik apni naujavaani main ek roti banane vaali dukaan par kaam karte the jahaan par unako shaakh-e-nabaat naamak stri se ekatarfa prem ho gaya. milan kii aashaa na dekhakar unhone kavitaayen likhnaa shuru kin jo baad main quran or attaar se bahut prabhavit huin. unki kavitaaon main prem, dharm or dikhaave vaali zindagi kaa mazaaq mukhya rup se miltaa he.
 English translations of Poetry by Hafez
