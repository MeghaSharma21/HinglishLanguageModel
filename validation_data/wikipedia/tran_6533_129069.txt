 just marid 2007 main jari bollywood film he jiskaa nirdesan meghna gulzaar ne kiya he tathaa mukhya abhinay bhoomika main fardin khan, isha devol hai. isamen satish shah,kiran khayr or mukul dev ne sahaayak abhinay bhoomika nibhaai he.
 is film main paanch yugalon ko uti main apane honeymoon par dikhaaya gaya he jahaan wo ek hi hotel main rah rahe hai. unamem se ek yugal apni vaivaahik varshaganth manaa rahai tathaa anya sabhi kaa vivaah abhi hua hi he. sabhi yugal uti main vibhinn paryatano tathaa anya sthalon par jate hai tathaa ek duusare ko vyaktigat str par jaanane kaa prayaas karte hai. dhire-dhire ek-ek karke sabhi kii kahaniyaan saamne aati hai.
 abhaya sachdeva or reetika khanna : parmparik vivaah paddati ke anusaar vivaahit do log. abhaya reetika ke saamne apane aap ko spasht karne tathaa sabhi sthitiyon ko spasht karnaa chahta tha lekin reetika apane aap ko thodaa peeche khinch leti he jisase donon ke jivan main thodaa tanaav tathaa bhram rahata he.
 rishabh or anu : wo apane jivan se pyaar karte hai tathaa wo ek duusare par bahut pyaar karte huye dikhaayi dete hai. wo sabase adhik pyaar karne vaali jodi ke rup main jane jate hai lekin ant main sabhi ko aashcharya hota he.
 kiran kher or satish shah: yahan par apni vivaah varshaganth manaane aaye huye hai or anya logon ke bich ek duusare se ladate huye dikhaayi dete hai. tathy yah he ki wo ek duusare se bahut adhik pyaar karte hai lekin unki ek duusare ke liye abhivyakti bhinn he.
 arjuna kohli/eke or sara : wo lambe samay se desh se baahar rahaa or anya rashtriyata ke liye apni premika ke saath rahata he. wo pehle hi uske saamne vivaah kaa prastaav rakh chukaa he lekin usne asweekrut kar diya. atah vartmaan main vah uske saath apni ek sahakarmi ke rup main vyavhaar karata he jabaki sara uske liye usamen bhaavnaayen viksit karti he.
 shoyab or anaiya : anaiya, shoyab kii bahan kii acchhee saheli he or use bahut chaahati he. antatah wo use vivaah ke liye manaa leti he. lekin shoyab, anaiya ko kisi bhi rup main dekhe lekin hameshaa use apni bahan kii bachapan kii dost hi dikhaai detii he. wo ek duusare se dooriyaan kam karne kii lagaataar koshish karte hai.
 film kaa sangeet preetam ne taiyaar kiya he or gane gulzaar ne likhe hai.
