 mohammad shahzad mohammadi ek afghanistan cricket team ke khilaadi he jo daaen haath se ballebaaji karte he or wicket-keeper kii bhoomika nibhaate he. inhone ek divasiy antarrashtriya kicket kaa padaarpan saal 2009 main 30 agast ko netherland cricket team ke khilaaf kiya tha jisamen inhone 16 ranon kii pari kheli thi. is match main netherland kii team 8 viketon se jiti thi. mohammad shahajad kaa bhi ek anoothaa record he - jab ballebaaj ne oriai shatak banane ke liye sabase kam team score main ekadivasiy shatak banaaya he, tab 2018 asia cap main afghanistan kaa kul bharat ke khilaaf sirf 131 tha, is prakaar pakistan ke shahid afridi ke adwitiya record ko baraabar bana diya gaya.
 shahzad ne apane karier main pehla tii20 antarrashtriya match ireland cricket team ke khilaaf 1 farvari 2010 ko khelaa tha jisamen sirf 10 ranon kii pari kheli thi. ye pakistan super lig main 2018 ke sijan main peshawar zalmi ke liye khele the.
 afghanistan ke pehle test match ke liye inhen afghanistan team main jagah di gayi.
