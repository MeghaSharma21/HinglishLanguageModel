 nirdeshaank: 21°49′42″N 75°37′09″E / 21.82833°N 75.61917°E / 21.82833; 75.61917khargun bharat ke madhya pradesh praant main sthit ek pramukh shahar he. khargun nagar, dakshin-pashchimi madhya pradesh rajya, madhya bharat, narmada nadi kii sahaayak kunda nadi ke purvi tat par sthit he.
 yah ek pracheen nagar he or mogul shasankaal main ise bijagarh kii sarkaar main ek mahal kaa mukhyalay banaae jane par isaki mahatta badi. ab yahan ek purana qila or kai madhbare va mahal hai. nadi ke tat ko pathar ke tatbandh se pakkaa kar diya gaya he or ghaaton kaa nirmaan karke sundar banaaya gaya he. khargun sthit navagraha mandir prasiddh he or yahan prativarsh disambar or janvari main meley kaa aayojan hota he.
 khargun apane zile kaa ek mahatvapurn vaanijya or vyapaar centre he or yahan krishi utpaada or imarti lakdi kaa bazaar he. yah kapaas or anaaj kaa suviksit bazaar he. is shahar main kapaas otane or ganth banane, chaaval or tilhan mil or bidi ke kaarakaane hai. yah sthaan morinda tinctoria se ranjak utpaadan ke liye bhi janaa jaataa he.
 is shahar main ek pustkaalay or kai sarkari mahavidhyalay hai, jo indore ke devi ahilya vishvavidhyalay se sambhddha hai. yahan ek aadharbhoot prashikshan santhaan or sanskritshaala bhi hai.
 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar khargun nagar kii kul jansankhya 86,443 he.
