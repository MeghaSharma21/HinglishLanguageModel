 rashmi ranjan parida  bhartiya media karmi or saamaajik — saahitiyik udhyami hai jinhone 2013 main kalinga saahitiyik mahotsav kii sthapana kii jo aaj purvi bharat kaa sabase bada saahitiyik aayojan mana jaataa he. iske alaava unhone koraput main uriya diary foundation or mistic ​kalinga kii sthapana kii he. patrkaar evam media udhyami ke rup main unhone oriya samudaay ko vaishvik str par badhaava dene ke liye kai pahal kii he. unhone apane drushtikon or samarpan ke madhyam se odisha main mahatvapurn badlaav laae. unhone odisha samudaay ko vaishvik str par badhaava dene ke liye kai pahal shuru kii. vah odisha aadhaarit teen pramukh samachar portalon — odishadiary evam odishan ke sansthaapak sampadak hai. iske alaava vah shiksha portal indiaducationadiary kaa bhi sampadan evam sanchaalan kar rahe hai. parida ne chaatro ko naagarik patrkaar ke rup main kaam karne ke liye prerit karne ke liye kai pahal kii he. 
 rashmi ranjan parida ko oriya rajya ke liye unke yogadan ko dekhte hue 2014 main oriya pua award tathaa 2015 main prarena award se sammanit kiya ja chukaa he. 
 parida kaa janm 15 june 1997 main odisha ke khordha jile main dalaksoti ​gaanv main ek saadhaaran parivaar main hua. unke pita kaa naam shri benudhar parida evam mata kaa naam shrimati kanakalata parida he. 
 shri parida ne 1997 main utkal vishvavidhyalay ke antargat bi je bi kalege, bhubaneshwar se kala main snaatak kiya. unhone vishwa​vidhyalay main doosraa sthaan praapt kiya. unhene 1999 main ravinshow kalege ab ravinshow vishvavidhyalay se raajnneeti shaastra main eme kiya. unhone 2002 main berhampur vishvavidhyalay se varsh 2002 main patrakarita evam jan sanchaar main eme kiya. 
 unhone odisha diary ke tahat 2011 main odisha living lijend award naamak vaarshik puraskaar kii shuruuaat kii. yah puraskaar odisha ke suprasiddh hastiyon ko diya jaataa he. unke kaary kii sirhaana desh ke agrani ​bijnesi skool im, ahmedabad ne bhi kii he.
