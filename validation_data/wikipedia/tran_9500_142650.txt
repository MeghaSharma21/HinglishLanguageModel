  bharat praveshdwar
 chunaav loktantr kaa aadhaar stambh hai. aazaadee ke baad se bharat main chunaavon ne ek lanbaa rasta tay kiya he.
 1951-52 ko hue aam chunaavon main matadaataaon kii sankhya 17,32,12,343 thi, jo 2014 main badhkar 81,45,91,184 ho gai he. 2004 main, bhartiya chunaavon main 670 million matadaataaon ne bhaag liya or iskaa ghoshit kharch 1989 ke mukaable teen guna badhkar $300 million ho gaya. in chunaavon main das lakh se adhik electronic voting mashiinon kaa istemaal kiya gaya. 2009 ke chunaavon main 714 million matadaataaon ne bhaag liya .
 matadaataaon kii vishaal sankhya ko dekhte hue chunaavon ko kai charnon main yojit kiya janaa aavashyak ho gaya he . chunaavon kii is prakriya main charanbaddh tareeke se kaam kiya jaataa he, isamen bhartiya chunaav ayog dwaara chunaavon kii tithi kii ghoshnaa, jisase rajnatik dalon ke bich "adarsh aachaar samhita" lagu hoti he, se lekar parinaamon kii ghoshnaa or safal ummidavaaron kii suchi rajya yaa centre ke kaaryakari pramukh ko saunpna shaamil hota he. parinaamon kii ghoshnaa ke saath chunaav prakriya kaa samaapan hota he or nai sarkaar ke gathan kaa maarg prashast hota he.
 rajya sabha ke sadasyon kaa chayan apratyaksh rup se hota he or ye lagbhag puri tarah se alag-alag rajyon kii vidhansabha ke sadasyon dwaara nirvaachit kiye jate hai, jabaki 12 sadasyon kaa namakan bharat ke raashtrapati dwaara kiya jaataa he, isamen aamtaur par bharat ke pradhanmantri kii salaah or sahmati shaamil hoti he. rajya sabha ke bare main adhik jaankaree http://rajyasabha.nic.in/rsnew/about_parliament/rajya_sabha_introduction.asp par paayi ja sakati he.
 bharat ke raashtrapati kaa chunaav 5 saal ke liye apratyaksh rup se kiya jaataa he. iske liye nirvachan mandal kaa prayog kiya jaataa he jahaan lok sabha va rajya sabha ke sadsy or bharat ke sabhi pradeshon tathaa kshetron kii vidhaan sabhaaon ke sadsy apana vot daalate hai.
 bhartiya sansad main rashtrapramukh- bharat ke raashtrapati - or do sadan shaamil hai jo vidhanmandal hote hai.bharat ke raashtrapati kaa chunaav paanch varsh kii avadhi ke liye nirvaachak mandal dwaara kiya jaataa he jisamen sangh or rajya ke vidhanmandalon ke sadsy shaamil hote hai.
 bharat kii sansad ke do sadan hai. lok sabha main 545 sadsy hote hai, 543 sadasyon kaa chayan paanch varshon kii avadhi ke liye ekal seat nirvachan kshetron se hota he or do sadasyon ko anglo-bhartiya samudaay kaa pratinidhitiv karne ke liye chunaa jaataa he . 550 sadasyon kaa chayan bahumat nirvachan pranali ke tahat hota he.
 rajyon kii parishad main 245 sadsy hote hai, jinamein 233 sadasyon kaa chayan chah varsh kii avadhi ke liye hota he, jisamen har do saal main ek tihaai avkaash grahan karte hai. in sadasyon kaa chayan rajya or centre shasit pradeshon ke vidhayakon dwaara kiya jaataa he. nirvaachit sadasyon kaa kaa chayan anupatik pratinidhitiv pranali ke tahat ekal antarneey mat ke madhyam se kiya jaataa he. baarah namit sadasyon ko aamtaur par prakhyat kalakaaron, vaigyanikon, nyayavidon, khilaadiyon, vyapaariyon or patrakaron or aam logon main se chunaa jaataa he.
 bhartiya rashtriya congress kaa prabhutv pehali baar 1977 main tutaa jab indira gandhi ke netritv main party ko anya sabhi bade dalon ke gathbandhan se haar kaa saamana karnaa padaa, ye sabhi dal 1975-1977 main vivaadit apatkaal lagu karne kaa virodh kar rahe the. isi prakaar vishwanath pratap singh ke netritv main ke ek gathbandhan ne 1989 main bhrashtachar ke aarope jhel rahe rajeev gandhi ko haraakar satta main pravesh kiya. ise bhi 1990 main satta se hatana padaa.
 1992 main, bharat main ab tak chali aa rahi ek party ke prabhutv vaali raajnneeti ne gathbandhan pranali ko rasta diya, jisamen koi ek party sarkaar banane ke liye sansad main bahumat kii ummid nahin kar sakati thi, lokin use anya partiyon ke saath gathbandhan karnaa hota tha or sarkaar banane ke liye bahumat siddh karnaa hota tha. isse majboot kshetreey dalon kaa mahatv bad gaya jo ab tak keval kshetreey aakankshaon tak hi simit the. ek taraf jahaan tedepa or annadramuk jaise dal paaramparik rup se majboot kshetreey daavedaar bane hue the vahiin duusari ore 1990 ke dashak main lok dal, samaajawaadee party, bahujan samaaj party or janata dal jaise anya kshetreey dalon kaa bhi udai hua. ye dal paaramparik rup se kshetreey aakankshaon par aadhaarit hote the, yaa puri tarah se jati aadhaarit hote the, .
 bharat main chunaavon kaa aayojan bhartiya samvidhan ke tahat banaaye gayi bhartiya nirvachan ayog dwaara kiya jaataa he. yah ek acchhee tarah sthapit parampara he ki ek baar chunaav prakriya shuru hone ke baad koi bhi adaalat chunaav ayog dwaara parinaam ghoshit kiye jane tak kisi bhi prakaar kaa hastkshep nahin kar sakati he. chunaavon ke dauraan, chunaav ayog ko badi maatra main adhikaar saunp die jate hai or zarurat padane par yah civil court ke rup main bhi kaary kar saktaa he.
 bharat kii chunavi prakriya main rajya vidhansabha chunaavon ke liye kam se kam ek maheene kaa samay lagata he jabaki aam chunaavon ke liye yah avadhi or adhik bad jaati he. matadaata suchi kaa prakaashan ek mahatvapurn chunaav purv prakriya he or yah bharat main chunaav ke sanchaalan ke liye bahut mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaati he. bhartiya samvidhan ke anusaar koi bhi vyakti jo bharat kaa naagarik he or jisaki umr 18 varsh se adhik he, vah matadaata suchi main ek matadaata ke rup main shaamil hone ke yogya he yah yogya matadaata kii jimmedaari he ki ve matadaata suchi main apana naam shaamil karaae. aamtaur par, ummidavaaron ke namakan kii antim tithi se ek saptaah pehle tak matadaata panjikaran ke liye anumati di gai he.
 chunaav se pehle namakan, matdaan or ginati kii tithiyon kii ghoshnaa kii jaati he. chunaavon kii tithi kii ghoshnaa ke din se adarsh aachaar samhita lagu ho jaati he.
 kisi bhi party ko chunaav prachaar ke liye sarkari sansadhano ko upyog karne kii anumati nahin hoti he. aachaar samhita ke niyamon ke anusaar matdaan ke din se 48 ghante pehle chunaav prachaar band kar diya janaa chahie. bhartiya rajyon ke liye chunaav se pehle kii gatividhiyaan atyant aavashyak hoti hai. aachaar samhita ke anusaar chunaav prachaar ke liye pratyaashee 10 choupihaya vaahan hi rakh saktaa he, jabaki matdaan vaale din teen chupahiyaa vaahanon kii anumati he.
 matdaan ke din se ek din pehle chunaav prachaar samaapt ho jaataa he. sarkari skulon or collejon ko matdaan kendron ke rup main chunaa jaataa he. matdaan karaane kii jimmedaari prayek jile ke jiladhikari kii hoti he. bahut se sarkari karmchaariyon ko matdaan kendron main lagaaya jaataa he. chunaav main dhokhadhadi rokane ke liye matdaan petiyon ke bajaay electronic voting mashiinon kaa prayog adhik maatra main kiya jaataa he, jo bharat ke kuch bhaagon main adhik prachlit he. mysore paints or varnish limited dwaara taiyaar ek amit syaahi kaa prayog aamtaur par matdaan ke sanket ke rup main matadaata ke bain tarjani anguli par nishaan lagane ke liye kiya jaataa he. is karypranali kaa upyog 1962 ke aam chunaav ke baad se farji matdaan rokane ke liye kiya ja rahaa he.
 chunaav ke din ke baad, iviem ko baari suraksha ke bich ek majboot kamare main jamaa kiya jaataa he. chunaav ke vibhinn charan puure hone ke baad, maton kii ginati kaa din nirdhaarit kiya jaataa he. aamtaur par vot kii ginati main kuch ghanton ke bheetar vijeta kaa pataa chal jaataa he. sabase adhik vot praapt karne vaale ummidavaar ko nirvachan kshetra kaa vijeta ghoshit kiya jaataa he.
 sabase adhik siten praapt karne vaale party yaa gathbandhan ko raashtrapati dwaara nai sarkaar banane ke liye aamantrit kiya jaataa he. kisi bhi party yaa gathbandhan ko sadan main voton kaa saadhaaran bahumat praapt karke vishwaas mat ke dauraan sadan main apana bahumat sabit karnaa aavashyak hota he.
 bharat ke kuch shaharon main, online matadaata panjikaran farm praapt kiye ja sakate hai or nikatatam chunavi karyaalay main jamaa kiye ja sakate hai. jaisi kuch saamaajik rup se praasangik websiten, matadaata panjikaran kii jaankaree praapt karne ke liye acha sthaan hai.
 ab tak, bharat main duurastha matdaan pranali nahin he. jan pratinidhitiv adhinium -1950 ke anuchhed 19 ke tahat ek vyakti ko apane mat kaa panjikaran karaane kaa adhikaar he yadi uski umr 18 saal se adhik he or vah nirvachan kshetra main rahane vaala 'aam naagarik’ he, arthath chah maheene yaa usase adhik samay se maujuuda pate par rah rahaa he. ukt adhinium kii dhara 20 kisi apravasi bhartiya ko matadaata suchi main apana naam darj karaane ke liye ayogy tehraati he. isliye, sansad or rajya vidhansabha ke chunaav main enarai ko vot daalane kii anumati nahin di gai he.
 agast 2010 main, jan pratinidhitiv bill -2010 ko lok sabha main 24 navambar 2010 kii baad kii rajptr adhisuchanaaon ke saath paarit kar diya gaya, is bill main enarai ko vot daalane kaa adhikaar diya gaya he. iske saath hi ab enarai bhartiya chunaavon main vot karne ke yogya ho jaaenge, lekin unke liye matdaan ke samay shaareerik rup se upasthit hona aavashyak he. bahut se saamaajik sanghathanon ne sarkaar se aagrah kiya tha ki duurastha matdaan pranali ke dwaara enarai or dur sthit logon dwaara matdaan karne ke liye arpie main sanshodhan karnaa chahie. pipal for lok satta, sakriyata se is baat par bal detii rahi he ki internet or daakpatr matdaan kaa enarai matdaan ke ek vyavhaarya saadhan ke rup main prayog kiya jaae.
 loksabhaa janata ke pratinidhiyon se milakar bani hoti he jinhen vayask matadhikaar ke aadhaar par pratyaksh nirvachan ke dwaara chunaa jaataa he. samvidhan main ullikhit sadan kii adhiktam kshamta 552 sadasyon kii he, jinamein 530 sadsy rajyon ke va 20 sadsy centre shasit pradeshon kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai or do sadasyon ko anglo-bhartiya samudaayon ke pratinidhitiv ke liye raashtrapati dwaara namankit kiya jaataa he; aisa tab kiya jaataa he jab raashtrapati ko lagata he ki us samudaay kaa sadan main paryaapt rup se pratinidhitiv nahin ho rahaa he.
 * : assam ke 12 siton or meghalaya ke 1 seat par chunaav nahin hue the.
 
 agast 1947 main swatantra hone or 26 janvari 1950 ko apana samvidhan lagu karne ke baad1952 main bharat kaa pehla aam chunaav sampann hua. tatpashchaat desh main pehali baar 1952 main loksabhaa kaa gathan hua. isamen bhartiya rashtriya congress 364 siton ke saath satta main ai. iske saath, party ne kul pade voton kaa 45 pratishat praapt kiya tha. puure bharat main 44.87 pratishat kii chunavi bhagidaari darj kii gai. jawahar laal nehru desh ke pehle nirvaachit pradhanmantri bane, unki party ne matdaan ke 75.99% mat praapt karke virodhiyon ko spasht rup se haraa diya. 17 april 1952 ko gathit hui lok sabha ne, 4 april 1957 tak kaa apana kaarykaal puraa kiya.
 pratham aam chunaav se thik pehle nehru ke do purv cabinet sahyogiyon ne congress ke varchasv ko chunauti dene ke liye alag raajnneetik dalon kii sthapana kar lee thi. jahaan ek ore shyama prasad mukherjee ne oktober 1951 main jansangh kii sthapana kii, vahiin duusari ore dalit netaa bhimarao ambedkar ne anusuchit jati mahasang ko punerjeevit kiya. jo anya dal us samay aage aae unamem acharya kripalni kii kisaan majdoor praja parishad, raamamanohar lohia or jaiprakash narayan ke netritv vaali samaajawaadee party or bhartiya communist party shaamil hai. haalanki, in chote dalon ko pataa tha ki ve vastav main congress ke khilaaf kahin khadey nahin hote hai.
 pehali loksabhaa ke adhyaksh shri ganesh vasudev maavlankar the. pehali loksabhaa main 677 baithken hui, yah ab tak hui baithkon kii ucchtam sankhya he. is lok sabha ne 17 april 1952 se 4 april 1957 tak apana kaarykaal puraa kiya.
 bhartiya rashtriya congress 1952 kii apni safalataa kii kahani ko 1957 main yojit hue duusare loksabhaa chunaavon main bhi dohraane main kaamyaab rahi. congress ke 490 ummidavaaron main se 371 siten jeetne main kaamyaab rahe. party ne kul 57,579,589 maton kii jeet ke saath 47.78 pratishat bahumat surakshit rakhaa. jawahar laal nehru achche bahumat ke saath satta main waapas laute. 11 may 1957 ko, em. ananthavyanam ingar ko sarvasammati se nai lok sabha kaa adhyaksh chunaa gaya.
 in chunaavon main congress ke sadsy firoz gandhi kaa udai bhi dekha gaya . unhone uttar pradesh ke raibareli nirvachan kshetra se apane nikatatam pratidwandvi, nand kishor ko 29,000 se adhik maton ke antar se haraaya.
 dilachasp baat yah rahi ki 1957 ke chunaavon main ek bhi mahila ummidavaar maidan main nahin thi. 1957 main nirdaleeyon ko matdaan kaa 19 pratishat praapt hua. duusari loksabhaa ne 31 march 1962 tak kaa apana kaarykaal puraa kiya.
 teesri loksabhaa april 1962 main banaai gai thi. us samay pakistan ke saath sambandh kharaab bane hue the. china ke saath 'dostana' sambandh bhi oktober 1962 ke seemaa yuddh se ek mithyaa hi sabit hue. apni sarkaar dwaara suraksha par paryaapt dhyaan na dene ke mudde par chaaron ore aalochana hone ke baad, nehru ko tatkaaleen raksha mantri krishna menon ko hataane or america kii sainya sahaayta lene ke liye baadhya hona padaa. nehru kaa swasthy teji se bigadane laga or vah 1963 main swasthya laabh ke liye kashmir main kai maheene gujaarne ke liye baadhya ho gaye. 1964 may main unke kashmir se lautne par, nehru sadame se peedit hue or baad main dil kaa daura padane se 27 may 1964 ko unka nidhan ho gaya. visheshgyon kaa kahana he ki 1962 main china ke bharat kii simaaon ke akraman or pakistan ke saath gharelu maamalon ne nehru ko kadavaahat se bhar diya tha.
 vayovruddh congressi netaa gulzarilal nanda ne nehru kii mrityu ke baad do saptaah ke liye unki jagah lee. congress dwaara laal bahadur shastri ko nayaa netaa chune jane tak unhone kaaryvahak pradhanmantri ke rup main kaam kiya. shastri pradhanmantri pad ke liye ek sambhavit vikalp nahin the jinhone, shaayad apratyaashit rup se, 1965 main pakistan par jeet dilaane main desh kaa netritv kiya. shastri or pakistan ke raashtrapati, mohammad ayub khan ne, purv soviet sangh ke taashkant main 10 janvari 1966 ko ek shanti sandhi par hastakshar kiye. haalanki, shastri apni jeet ke phaayde dekhne ke liye zyada samay tak jivit nahin rahe.
 shastri kii mrityu se utpann hue rikt sthaan ke kaaran congress ne ek baar punah netaa viheen ho gayi. nehru kii beti indira gandhi ke pradhanmantri banane se pehle ek baar phir nanda ko ek maheene se kam samay ke liye kaaryvahak pradhanmantri banaaya gaya. indira, shastri ji ke mantrimandal main soochna evam prasaaran mantri ke rup kaam karti thim. tatkaaleen congress adhyaksh ke. kamraj ne 1966 main indira ko pradhanmantri banane main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai thi. puraane congressi netaa morarji desai se kade virodh ke baavjood, indira gandhi 24 janvari 1966 ko pradhanmantri baniin. congress ke liye vastav main yah samay sabase acha nahin tha. party aantarik sankaton se jujh rahi thi or desh haal main lade do yudhon ke prabhaav se ubar rahaa tha. arthvyavastha ko nuksaan hua tha or manobal kaaphi gira hua tha. jin anya muddon ne lok sabha ko hilaa kar rakh diya tha unamem mizo adivasee baghawat, akaal, shramik ashaanti or rupaya avamulyan ke maddenjar garibon kii badahaali shaamil thi. vahiin punjab main bhi bhashaai or dharmik algaavvaad ke liye andolan chal rahaa tha.
 bharat april 1967 main chauthe chunaav kii rajnatik gatividhiyon se guzar rahaa tha. jis congress ne ab tak chunaavon main 73 pratishat se kam siten nahin jiti thi, aage aane vaala samay uske liye or bura sabit hone vaala tha.
 congress ke aantarik sankat kaa asar 1967 ke chunaav ke parinaamon main saaf dikhaai diya. pehali baar, congress ne nichle sadan main kareeb 60 siton ko kho diya. use 283 siten par jeet praapt hui. 1967 tak, sabase puraani party ne vidhansabha chunaavon main bhi kabhi 60 pratishat se kam siten nahin jiti thim. yahan bhi congress ko ek bada jhatka sahanaa padaa kyonki bihar, kerala, urisa, madras, punjab or pashchim bengal main gaiyr congressi sarkarein sthapit huin. is sab ke saath, indira gandhi ko, jo raibareli nirvachan kshetra se loksabhaa ke liye chune gayin thi, 13 march ko pradhanmantri ke rup main shapath dilaai gai. asantusht awaazon ke shant rakhane ke liye, unhone morarji desai ko bharat kaa uppradhanmantri or bharat kaa vittamantri niyukt kiya. morarji desai ne nehru kii mrityu ke baad indira ko pradhanmantri banaae jane kaa virodh kiya tha.
 congress party ke bheetar matbhed badhate rahe. congress ne 12 navambar 1969 "anushaasanheenata" ke liye moraar ji desai ko nishkasit kar diya. is ghatana ne congress ko do bhaagon main vibhajit kar diya: congress - sanghathan ke liye - jiskaa netritv morarji desai ne kiya or congress - indira ke liye - jiskaa netritv indira gandhi kar rahi thim. indira ne disambar 1970 tak sipiai ke samarthan se ek alpamat vaali sarkaar ko chalaaya. vah aage alpamat kii sarkaar nahin chalaanaa chaahati thim, isliye unhone chunaavon kii avadhi se ek varsh pehle madhyavadhi loksabhaa chunavon kii ghoshnaa kar di.
 indira gandhi ne 1971 main congress ko baari bahumat se jeet dilaai. "garibi hataao" ke chunavi naare ke saath prachaar karte hue, vah 352 siton ke saath sansad main waapas aayin; yah pichley chunaavon kii 283 siton ke mukaable ullekhneey sudhaar tha.
 indira gandhi ne 1971 main bharat pakistan yuddh ke dauraan saahasik nirnay liya jiske parinamswaroop bangladesh mukt ho gaya. disambar 1971 main bharat kii jeet kaa sabhi bharatiyon dwaara swaagat kiya gaya kyonki yah china or sanyukt rajya america se rajanyik virodhon kaa saamana karte hue praapt hui thi. us samay tatkaaleen soviet sangh or purvi block ke deshon ko chhodkar shaayad hi kisi anya desh ne bharat kaa antarrashtriya samarthan kiya tha.
 indira or congress donon ke samaksh kuch anya samasyaayein bhi thim. bharat paak yuddh main aayi baari aarthik laagat, duniya main tel kii keemton main vruddhi or audyogic utpaadan main giravat ne aarthik kathinaaiyon ko badha diya tha.
 12 june 1975 ko elahabaad ucch nyaayalay ne chunavi bhrashtachar ke aadhaar par unke 1971 ke chunaav ko avaidh tehra diya. istife ke bajaay, indira gandhi ne desh main apatkaal kii ghoshnaa kii or puure vipaksh ko jail main daal diya.
 apatkaal march 1977 tak chalaa or 1977 main yojit chunaavon main jan morcha naam ke partiyon ke gathbandhan se unhen haar kaa saamana karnaa padaa. aisa pehali baar hua tha jab congress ko ek gambhir haar kaa saamana karnaa padaa tha.
 congress sarkaar dwaara apatkaal kii ghoshnaa 1977 ke chunaavon main mukhya mudda tha. rashtriya apatkaal ke dauraan 25 june 1975 se 21 march 1977 tak naagarik swatantraaaon ko samaapt kar diya gaya tha or pradhanmantri indira gandhi ne vishaal shaktiyaan apane haath main le lee thim.
 gandhi apane is nirnay kii vajah se kaaphi alokapriy huin or chunaavon main unhen isaki keemat chukaani padi. 23 janvari ko gandhi ne, march main chunaav karaane kii ghoshnaa kii or sabhi raajnneetik kaidiyon ko reeha kar diya. chaar vipakshi dalon, congress, jansangh, bhartiya lokdal or samaajawaadee party ne 'janata party' ke rup main milakar chunaav ladane kaa faisla kiya.
 janata party ne matadaataaon ko apatkaal ke dauraan hui jyadatiyon or maanav adhikaron ke ulanghan kii yaad dilaai jaise anivaary bandhyakaran or rajanetaaon ko jail main daalana.. janata abhiyaan main kaha gaya ki chunaav tay karegaa ki bharat main "loktantr hoga yaa taanaashaahee". congress ashankit dikh rahi thi. krishi or sinchai mantri babu jagjivan ram ne party chhod di or aisa karne vaale kai logon main se ve ek the.
 congress ne ek majboot sarkaar kii jarurat hone kii baat kahkar matadaataaon ko lubhaane kii koshish kii lekin lahar iske khilaaf chal rahi thi.
 swatantra bharat main pehali baar congress ko chunaavon main haar kaa saamana karnaa padaa or janata party ne 298 siten jiti. moraar ji desai 24 march ko bharat ke pehle gaiyr-congressi pradhanmantri bane, unhen chunaavon se do mahine pehle hi jail se reeha kiya gaya tha.
 congress kii lagbhag 200 siton par haar hui. indira gandhi or unke bete sanjay gandhi bhi chunaav haar gaye.
 janata party, congress or apatkaal ke khilaaf janata ke gusse par savaar hokar satta main aayi lekin isaki sthiti kamjor thi. loksabhaa main party kii 270 siten thim or satta par uski pakad majboot nahin thi.
 bhartiya lok dal ke netaa choudhary charan singh or jagjivan ram, jinhone congress chhod di thi, janata gathbandhan ke sadsy the, lekin pradhanmantri morarji desai ke se ve khush nahin the.
 apatkaal ke dauraan maanavadhikaar ke hanan kii jaanch ke liye jo adaalatein sarkaar ne gathit kii thim ve indira gandhi ke khilaaf pratishodhi dikhaai padin, indira ne swayan ko ek pareshaan mahila ke rup main chitrit karne kaa koi mauka nahin gavaanyaa.
 samaajwaadiyon or hindu rashtravaadiyon kaa mishran janata party, 1979 main vibhajit ho gai jab bhartiya jansangh ke netaa atal bihari vaajapeyi or laalkrishna adwani ne party ko chhod diya or bijees ne sarkaar se samarthan waapas le liya.
 desai ne sansad main vishwaas mat kho diya or istipha de diya. charan singh, jinhone janata gathbandhan ke kuch bhagidaaron ko barkaraar rakhaa tha, unhone pradhanmantri ke rup main june 1979 main shapath lee.
 congress ne sansad main choudhary charan singh ke samarthan kaa vaada kiya lekin baad main peeche hat gai. unhone janvari 1980 main chunaav kii ghoshnaa kar di or ve akele pradhanmantri the jo kabhi sansad nahin gaye. janata party ke netaaon ke bich kii ladaai or desh main faili raajnneetik asthirataa ne congress ke paksh main kaam kiya, jisane matadaataaon indira gandhi kii majboot sarkaar kii yaad dilaa di.
 congress ne loksabhaa main 351 siten jeetin or janata party, yaa bache hue gathbandhan ko 32 siten milin.
 janata party kaa saal dar saal vibhaajan hota rahaa lekin ye desh ke rajnatik itihaas main ek mahatvapurn mile kaa pathar sabit hua: yah ek gathbandhan tha or isane yah sabit kar diya ki congress ko haraaya ja saktaa he.
 31 oktober 1984 ko pradhanmantri indira gandhi kii hatya ne, congress ke liye sahanubhuti mat banaae. indira gandhi kii mrityu ke baad loksabhaa ko bhang kar diya gaya or rajeev gandhi ne antarim pradhanmantri ke rup main shapath lee.
 november 1984 ke liye chunaav kii ghoshnaa kar di gai or chunaav prachaar ke dauraan rajeev ne logon ko apane parivaar ke yogadan kii yaad dilaai.
 congress ne baari bahumat se jeet hasil kii. isane 409 loksabhaa siten or lokapriy maton kaa 50 fisadi apane naam kiya, yah party kaa sarvshereshth pradarshan tha. telugu desham party 30 siton ke saath sansad main duusari sabase badi party ban gai. yah bhartiya sansad ke itihaas ke un durlabh ricardon main ek he jisamen koi kshetreey party mukhya vipakshi dal ke rup main ubhari.
 9veen loksabhaa ke chunaav bhartiya chunavi raajnneeti main kai myanon main aitihaasik ghatana rahe. in chunaavon ne rajanetaaon ke matadaata se vot maangane ke tareeke ko badal diya. ab jati or dharm ke aadhaar par vot maangana centre bindu ban gaya.
 yadhyapi 1989 ke aam chunaav kai sankaton se jujh rahe yuva rajeev ke saath lade gaye kintu congress sarkaar apni vishwasaneeyata or lokapriyata kho rahi thi. boforce cond, punjab main badhata aatankwaad, altitii or srilanka sarkaar ke bich grih yuddh un samasyaon main se kuch thi jo rajeev gandhi kii sarkaar ke saamne thim. rajeev ke sabase bade aalochak vishwanath pratap singh the jinhone sarkaar main vitt mantralay or raksha mantralay kaa kaamkaaj sambhala rakhaa tha. raksha mantri ke rup main singh ke kaarykaal ke dauraan yah afvaah thi ki unke paas boforce raksha saude se sambandhit aisi jaankaree thi jo rajeev gandhi kii pratishtha ko barbaad kar sakati thi.
 lekin singh ko sheeghra hi mantrimandal se barkhaast kar diya gaya or phir unhone congress or lok sabha main apni sadsyta se istipha de diya. unhone arun nehru or aarif mohammad khan ke saath jan morcha kaa gathan kiya or elahabaad se loksabhaa main pravesh kiya.
 11 oktober 1988 ko, jan morcha, janata party, lokdal or congress ke vilay se janata dal kii sthapana hui taaki sabhi dal ek saath milakar rajeev gandhi sarkaar kaa virodh kar karen. jald hi, dramuk, tedepa or agap sahit kai kshetreey dal janata dal se mil gaye or neshnal front kii sthapana kii. paanch partiyon vaala neshnal front, bhartiya janata party or do communist partiyon- bhartiya communist party - marxvaadi or bhartiya communist party ke saath milakar 1989 ke chunaav maidan main utraa.
 loksabhaa main 525 siton ke liye yah chunaav 22 navambar or 26 navambar 1989 ko do charnon main yojit hue. neshnal front ko loksabhaa main aasaan bahumat praapt hua or usne vam morche or bhartiya janata party ke bahari samarthan se sarkaar banaai. rashtriya morche kii sabase bade ghatak janata dal ne 143 siten jeetin, iske alaava makapa or bhakpa ne kramshah 33 or 12 siten hasil kin. nirdaleey or anya chote dal 59 siten jeetne main kaamyaab rahe.
 haalanki, congress abhi bhi 197 saansadon ke saath loksabhaa main akeli sabase badi party thi. bhaajapa 1984 ke chunaavon main do siton ke mukaable is baar ke chunaavon main 85 saansadon ke saath sabase zyada phaayde main rahi. vishwanath pratap singh bharat ke 10wein pradhanmantri bane or devilal up pradhanmantri bane. unhone 2 disambar 1989 se 10 navambar 1990 tak karyaalay sambhala. bhaajapa netaa laalkrishna adwani dwaara ram janmabhumi-babari masjid mudde par rath yatra shuru kiye jane or bihar ke mukhyamantri lalu yadav dwaara bihar main giraftaar kiye jane ke baad party ne ve.pi. singh sarkaar se samarthan waapas le liya. veepi singh ne vishwaas mat haarane ke baad istipha de diya.

 10 veen loksabhaa ke chunaav madhyavadhi chunaav the kyonki pichhali loksabhaa ko sarkaar ke gathan ke sirf 16 maheene baad bhang kar diya gaya tha. yah chunaav vipareet vaataavaran main hue or do sabase mahatvapurn chunavi muddon, mandal ayog kii sifarisen lagu karne or ram janmabhumi babari masjid vivaad ke chalate inhen 'mandal-mandir' chunaav bhi kaha jaataa he.
 jahaan ek ore ve.pi. singh sarkaar dwaara lagu mandal ayog kii report main sarkari naukariyon main anya pichhadi jatiyon ko 27 pratishat aarakshan diya gaya tha, jiske kaaran bade paimaane par hinsa hui or saamaanya jatiyon ke desh bhar main iskaa virodh kiya, vahiin duusari ore mandir ayodhya ke vivaadit babari masjid dhaanche kaa pratinidhitiv karata tha jise bhartiya janata party apane pramukh chunavi mudde ke rup main upyog kar rahi thi.
 mandir mudde ke falswarup desh ke kai hisson main dange hue or matadaataaon kaa jati or dharmik aadhaar par dhruvikaran ho gaya. rashtriya morche main faili avyavashtha ne congress kii vaapasi ke sanket de die the.
 chunaav teen charnon main 20 may 12 june or 15 june 1991 yojit kiye gaye. yah congress, bhaajapa or rashtriya morcha-janata dal - vamapanthiyon morche ke gathbandhan ke bich ek trikoniya muqabla tha.
 matdaan ke pehle daur ke ek din baad 20 may ko, purv pradhanmantri rajeev gandhi kii tamil ilam libration tiger dwaara shreeperambdur main chunaav prachaar karte hue hatya kar di gai. chunaav ke shesh dinon ko june ke madhya tak ke liye sthagit kar die gaya or ant main matdaan 12 june or 15 june ko hua. is baar ke sansadeey chunaavon main ab tak kaa sabase kam matdaan hua, isamen keval 53 pratishat matadaataaon ne apane matadhikaar kaa prayog kiya.
 in chunaavon ke parinaamon se ek trishanku sansad bani, jisamen 232 siton ke saath congress sabase badi party ke rup main ubhari or 120 siton ke saath bhaajapa duusare sthaan par rahi. janata dal sirf 59 siton ke saath tisare sthaan par rahaa.
 21 june ko, congress ke pi ve narsingh raav ne pradhanmantri ke rup main shapath lee. raav, nehru-gandhi parivaar ke baahar duusare congressi pradhanmantri the. nehru-gandhi parivaar ke baahar pehle congressi pradhanmantri laal bahadur shastri the.
 11 veen loksabhaa ke liye hue chunaav ke parinaamon se ek trishanku sansad kaa gathan hua or do varsh tak rajnatik asthirataa rahi jiske dauraan desh ke teen pradhanmatri bane.
 pradhanmantri pi.ve. narsingh raav kii congress sarkaar ne sudhaaron kii ek shrunkhlaa ko lagu kiya jisane videshi niveshkon ke liye desh kii arthvyavastha ko khol diya. raav ke samarthakon ne unhen desh kii arthvyavastha ko bachaane or desh kii videsh niti ko sphurti dene kaa shrey diya lekin unki sarkaar april se may main chunaav se pehle anishchit or kamjor thi.
 may 1995 main, varisht netaa arjuna singh or narayan dutt tiwari ne congress chhod di or apni party kaa gathan kiya. harshad mehta ghotaale, raajnneeti ke apradheekaran par vohara kii report, jain hawala cond or 'tandoor hatyakand' maamle ne raav sarkaar kii vishwasaneeyata ko kshatigrast kiya.
 bhaajapa va uske sahyogi dal or sanyukt morcha, vam morcha or janata dal kaa gathbandhan, chunaavon main congress ke mukhya pratidwandvi the.
 teen saptaah ke abhiyaan ke dauraan, raav ne apane dwaara lagu kiye gaye aarthik sudhaaron ko mudda banaakar matadaataaon ko aakarshith kiya or bhaajapa ne hindutv or rashtriya suraksha ke muddon par matadaataaon ko rijhaayaa.
 matadaata kisi bhi party se prabhavit nahin lagate the. bhaajapa ne 161 siten jiti or congress ne 140. sansad kii siton kii aadhi sankhya 271 thi.
 raashtrapati ne bhaajapa netaa atal bihari vaajapeyi ko sarkaar banane ke liye aamantrit kiya, chunki ve sansad main sabase badi party ke mukhiya the.
 vaajapeyi ne 16 may ko pradhanmantri ke rup main padbhaar sambhala or sansad main kshetreey dalon se samarthan paane kii koshish kii. vah is kaam main vifal rahe or 13 dinon ke baad unhone istipha de diya.
 janata dal ke netaa ech di devegoda ne 1 june ko ek sanyukt morcha gathbandhan sarkaar kaa gathan kiya. unki sarkaar 18 maheene chali.
 devegoda ke videsh mantri indrakumar gujral ne pradhanmantri ke rup main april 1997 main padbhaar sambhala jab congress baahar se ek nai sanyukt morcha sarkaar kaa samarthan karne ke liye sahamat ho gai. lekin gujral keval ek kaamchalaoo vyavastha ke rup main the. desh main 1998 main phir se chunaav hona tay tha.
 11veen loksabhaa kaa jivan chota tha, yah mushkil se dedh saal chali. alpamat vaali indra kumaar gujral kii sarkaar, 28 navambar 1997 ko gir gai jab seetaram kesari ke netritv vaali congress ne rajeev gandhi kii hatya main dramuk netaa. on ke shaamil hone ke vivaad ke chalate sarkaar se samarthan waapas le liya.
 nae chunaavon kii ghoshnaa kii gai or 10 march 1998 ko 12 veen loksabhaa kaa gathan hua or varisht bhaajapa netaa atal bihari vaajapeyi ke netritv vaale gathbandhan ko nau din baad shapath dilaai gai. 12 veen loksabhaa keval 413 din chali, jo us tithi tak kaa sabase kam samay tha.
 ek vyavhaarya vikalp ke abhaav ke kaaran tab vighatan ho gaya jab 13 maheene puraani bhartiya janata party ke netritv vaali sarkaar ko 17 april ko keval ek mat se bedakhal kar diya gaya. aisa paanchavin baar hua tha jab loksabhaa ko apana kaarykaal puraa karne se pehle bhang kar diya gaya.
 4 disambar 1997 ko lok sabha ke vighatan ke baad samay se pehle hi sabhi loksabhaa siton ke liye chunaav hue.
 chunaav pashchaat gathbandhan kii ranneeti ne bhaajapa ke netritv vaale gathbandhan ko 265 siton kaa kaaryakari bahumat pradaan kiya. is sandarbh main 15 march ko, raashtrapati ke aar narayanan ne vaajapeyi ko agali sarkaar banane ke liye aamantrit kiya. 19 march ko vaajapeyi ne pradhanmantri ke rup main shapath lee.
 17 april 1999 ko, vaajapeyi ne loksabhaa main vishwaas mat kho diya or iske falswaroop unki gathbandhan sarkaar ne istipha de diya. unhone iskaa kaaran apane 24 party vaale rashtriya janatantric gathbandhan main saamanjasy kii kami hona bataaya. bhaajapa, gathbandhan kii apane sahyogi jayalalita ke netritv vaali annadramuk ke peeche hatane ke kaaran matdaan main ek vot se haar gai thi.
 jayalalita apni maange puri naa hone par lagaataar samarthan waapas lene kii dhamkee de rahi thim, in maangon main vishesh rup se tamilnadu sarkaar se barkhaast karnaa shaamil tha jiskaa niyantran ve teen saal pehle kho chuki thim. bhaajapa ne aarope lagaaya ki jayalalita bhrashtachar ke aaropon kii jaanch se bachane kii maang kar raheen thim or partiyon ke bich koi samjhauta nahin kiya ja sakaa jisase sarkaar kii haar hui.
 mukhya vipakshi party congress, kshetreey or vampanthi samoohon ke saath milakar bahumat vaali sarkaar banane ke liye paryaapt samarthan nahin paa saki. 26 april ko, bharat ke tatkaaleen raashtrapati ke. aar. narayanan ne loksabhaa bhang kar di or jaldi chunaav karne kii ghoshnaa kar di. bhaajapa ne matdaan hone tak ek antarim prashasan ke rup main shashan karnaa jari rakhaa, chunaav ayog dwaara chunaavon kii tithi 4 may ghoshit kii gai thi.
 chunki pichley chunaav 1996 or 1998 main yojit hue the, isliye 1999 ke chunaav 40 maheene main teesri baar ho rahe the. chunavi dhokhadhadi or hinsa ko rokane ke liye desh ke 31 rajyon va centre shasit pradeshon main suraksha balon ko tainaat karne hetu ye chunaav paanch saptaah tak chale the. kul milakar 45 partiyon ne 543 loksabhaa siton par chunaav ladaa.
 lambey chunaav abhiyaan ke dauraan, bhaajapa or congress ne aam taur par aarthik or videsh niti ke muddon par sahmati vyakt kii, isamen pakistan ke saath kashmir seemaa vivaad kaa nipataara bhi shaamil tha. unki pratidwandvita keval congress adhyaksh sonia gandhi or vaajapeyi ke bich ke vyaktigat takaraav ke rup main hi adhik prakat hui.
 sonia gandhi ko 1998 main kaaphi kam umr main party ke adhyaksh pad ke liye nirvaachit kiya gaya, sonia kii janmabhumi italy hone kii baat ko mudda banaakar maharashtra ke congressi netaa sharad pavaar ne sonia ke chayan ko chunauti di. is kaaran se congress main aatanrik sankat paidaa ho gaye or bhaajapa ne prabhavi rup se ek chunavi mudde ke rup main iskaa prayog kiya.
 vaajapeyi dwaara kargil yuddh se nipatane kaa sakaaratmak drushtikon bhi ek mudda tha jo bhaajapa ke paksh main kaam kar rahaa tha. yah yuddh chunaavon se kuch maheene pehle hi samaapt hua tha or isane kashmir main bharat kii sthiti ko majboot kiya tha. iske alaava, pichley do varshon main bharat ne aarthik udaarikaran or vittiya sudhaaron ke chalate aarthik rup se kaaphi vruddhi tainaat kii thi, iske saath-saath mudrasphiti kii daren kam or audyogic vikaas kii daren bhi ucch thim.
 anya dalon ke saath majboot or vyapak gathjod ke madhyam se rajnatik vistaar ke aadhaar par, 1991, 1996 or 1998 ke chunaavon main bhaajapa or uske sahyogiyon ne lagaataar vikaas kiya tha; or kshetreey vistaar ke kaaran rajg pratispardhi ban gaya or yahaan tak ki usne congress kii bahulata vaale kshetron jaise urisa, andhra pradesh or assam main bhi sabase zyada vot praapt kiye the. ye kaarak 1999 ke chunaav parinaamon main nirnaayak sabit hue.
 6 oktober ko aae parinaam main rajg ko 298 siten milin, congress or uske sahyogiyon ko 136 siton par viyaj praapt hui. vaajapeyi ne 13 oktober ko pradhanmantri ke rup main shapath lee.
 bhaajapa ke netritv vaali rajg sarkaar ne 2004 main pradhanmantri atal bihari vaajapeyi kii dekharekh main apane shashan ke paanch saal puure kiye or 20 april se 10 may 2004 ke bich chaar charnon ke chunaav hue.
 adhikansh vishleshkon kaa maananaa tha ki rajg, 'feel good factor' or apane prachaar abhiyaan 'bharat udai' kii madad se, satta virdohi lahar ko haraa degi or spasht bahumat praapt karegi. bhaajapa shashan ke dauraan arthvyavastha main lagaataar vruddhi dikhaai di thi or saarvajanik kshetra ke upakramon ke vinievesh ko patari par lagaaya gaya tha. bharat ke videshi mudra bhandar main 100 arab dollar se adhik raashi jamaa thi . seva kshetra ne bhi dheron naukariyaan uplabdh utpann hui thim.
 1990 ke dashak ke anya sabhi loksabhaa chunaavon kii tulana main in chunaavon main, do vyaktitvon kaa takaraav adhik dekha gaya kyonki vahaan koi teesraa vyavhaarya vikalp maujuud nahin tha. bhaajapa or iske sahyogiyon kaa jhagada ek taraf tha or congress or uske sahyogiyon kaa jhagada duusari taraf. haalanki, kshetreey matbhed rashtriya paridrishy par ubhare the.
 bhaajapa ne rajg ke sadsy ke rup main chunaav ladaa, haalanki iske siton ke bantwaare ko lekar iske samjhaute rajg ke baahar kuch majboot kshetreey dalon ke saath bhi the jaise andhra pradesh main telugu desham party or tamilnadu main akhil bhartiya anna dravid munetra kadgam party.
 congress ke netritv main rashtriya str kaa vipakshi morcha banane kii koshish bhi hui. ant main, koi samjhauta nahin ho paaya, lekin kshetreey str par kai rajyon main congress or kshetreey partiyon ke gathbandhan ho gaya. yah pehali baar tha ki congress ne sansadeey chunaavon main is tarah ke gathbandhan ke saath chunaav ladaa tha.
 vampanthi dalon, visheshkar bhartiya communist party or communist party of india, ne apane majboot kshetron jaise pashchim bengal, tripura or kerala main apane dam par chunaav ladaa or congress or rajg donon kaa saamana kiya. anya rajyon jaise punjab or andhra pradesh main unhone congress ke saath siten banti. tamilnadu main ve dramuk ke netritv vaale janatantric pragatisheel gathbandhan kaa hissaa the.
 bahujan samaaj party or samaajawaadee party ne congress yaa bhaajapa donon main se kisi ke saath bhi jane se inkaar kar diya. ye donon bharat ke sarvaadhik aabaadi vaale rajya uttar pradesh main aadhaarit hai.
 haalanki chunaav purv bhavishyavaaniyon main bhaajapa ke liye baari bahumat kii baat kahi gai thi, lekin agsit paul main trishanku sansad kii bhavishyavaanee hui. yah bhi aam dharna he ki jaise hi bhaajapa ne yah maananaa aarambh kiya kii chunaav puri tarah se uske paksh main nahin hue hai, isane bhaajapa ke abhiyaan kaa dhyaan india shining se hataakar sthirataa ke muddon par kendrit kar diya. jis congress ko bhaajapa ne "puraane bhang vaali" kaa naam diya tha use mukhyatah garibon, graminon, nichali jatiyon or alpsankhyak matadaataaon kaa baari samarthan mila jisase congress ne badi jeet hasil kii; in logon ko ek vishalkay madhyamvarg ko janm dene vaali vigat varshon kii aarthik vruddhi kaa koi vishesh laabh praapt nahin hua.
 chunaav purv bhavishyavaaniyon main paraajay ke liye vibhinn kaarnon ko jimmedaar mana jaataa he. rashtriya muddon kii bajaay log apane aasapaas ke muddon, jaise pani kii kami, sookhaa, aadi, ke bare main zyada chintit the; or bhaajapa ke sahyogi satta virdohi bhaawanaaon kaa saamana kar rahe the.
