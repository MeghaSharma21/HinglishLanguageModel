 uttari golardh ke kai deshon main divalok bachat samay lagu hota he. unamem se vah samay mandal ke desh/kshetra jahaan yah utisi antar keval sardiyon main maanak samay ke rup main prayog hota he is prakaar hai:
 dakshini golardh main yah utisi antar namibia main maanak samay ke rup main prayog hota he.
