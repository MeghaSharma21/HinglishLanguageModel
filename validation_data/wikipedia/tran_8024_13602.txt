 azaad hind radio kaa armbh netaji subhash chandra bose ke netritv main 1942 main germany main kiya gaya tha. is radio kaa uddeshy bharatiyon ko angrejon se swatantrata paane ke liye sangharsh karne ke liye prachaar-prasar karnaa tha. azaad hind radio ek prachaar radio seva thi jise 1942 main germany main netaji subhash chandra bose ke netritv main swatantraaa ke liye ladane ke liye protsaahit kiya gaya tha. haalanki shuruyaat main germany main sthit, iske mukhyalay ko dakshin purv asia main yuddh ke dauraan singapore main or baad main rangoon main sthanantarit kar diya gaya tha. dakshin purv asia main netaji ke prasthaan ke baad, german parichaalan germany main bhartiya sena ke pramukh asien nambiar or baad main germany main araji hucumet aaazaad hind ke rajdoot ne jari rakhaa.   
 stesan ne angreji, hindi, tamil, bengali, marathi, punjabi, pashto or urdu main saptahik samachar bulatin prasaarit kiya kyonki ye germany main bhartiya sena or dakshin purv asia main bhartiya rashtriya sena ke liye sambhavit swayamsevakon kii bhashaaen thim. adhikansh swayamsevakon ne keval in bhartiya bhashaaon ko hi baat kii.
 azaad hind radio kaa lakshya sahyogi radio steshnon ke prasaaran kaa muqabla karnaa tha. azaad hind radio par, netaji ne british broadcasting korporation ko blf and blster korporation or ol india radio ko anti indian radio ke rup main sanderbhit kiya.
