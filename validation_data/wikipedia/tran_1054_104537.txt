 
 2016-17 mahila big bash lig yaa WBBL|02 mahila big bash lig, arddh peshewar mahilaon kii tii-20 main australia main gharelu cricket pratiyogita ke duusare satr he. toornament 10 disambar 2016 se 28 janvari 2017 tak chalaane ke liye nirdhaarit he. prayek WBBL team 14 khilaadiyon kii ek team ke hote hai or big bash lig main purushon kii tiimon ke saath jud rahe hai.
 vahaan udghaatan saptahanth par 4 khadey akele khel sahit mausam bhar main live jaanch 12 khel, ke saath, network ten ke madhyam se adhik se adhik live tivi kawarej, ho jayega. australia main khadey akele mahilaon ke khel ke match ke liye pehali baar ek - yah sidney thunder banaam melborn stars match he, jo prime time ke dauraan network ten ke praathmik channel par dikhaaya jayega bhi shaamil he. aage yah bhi ghoshnaa kii gai ki shesh 47 khel cricket australia ke web plateform ke madhyam se jeena pradarshit kiya jayega, or WBBL fasebuk pej.
 group charan kaa praaroop ek dabl round robin toornament he, jahaan prayek team main do baar ek-duusare ko team main khelenge. khel saptahanth, jahaan teen-chaar tiimon ki saptahanth main ek shahar main khel sakate main baanta jaataa he. iskaa matlab yah he ki tiimon ke liye yaa apane pratidwandvi ke ghar shahar main maichon kii mejabani karegaa, ek tatasth shahar main. kyonki vahaan 56 khel saat saptahanth par khelaa jaataa he, yah ek din main kai maichon ke liye aam he, or maiches vibhinn aadhaaron par ek saath khelaa ja rahaa he.
 vahaan purushon kii big bash lig ke saath 14 dabl hader phiksture hai, or semifinal or final bhi ek dabl header ke rup main khelaa jayega.
