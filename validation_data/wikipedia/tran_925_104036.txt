 nicolao manuchi ek italvi lekhak or yatri tha. usne mogul darbar main kaam kiya. iske atirikt usne daara shukoh, shah alam, raja jaisingh or kirat singh kii seva main kaam kiya.
 nikolayo manuksi kaa janm 19 april 1638 ko venice main hua tha. yah paanch bahan-bhaaiyon main se pehla tha. vah chaudaa saal kii choti umr main tha jab vah apane ek chache ke saath corfu chalaa gaya. or phir ek jahaaz main chhip kar izmir ke liye ravaanaa ho gaya. safar ke dauraan henry bared, belomont ke viscount ke saath uski mulaakaat ho gai. uske saath vah iran aa pahunchaa or phir vahaan se aage vah surat, hindustan pahunch gaya.
