 
 astarid​ sapushpak paudhon kaa ek bada claide parivaar he. rozid or astarid​ saare yudicott​ ke parivaar ke do sabase bade claide hai or in donon main hazaron sadsy jatiyaan aati hai. kram-vikaas kii drushti se anuvaanshiki anusandhan se pataa chalaa he ki rozid or astarid ke do parivaar ek hi saanjhe purvaj se lagbhag 10 karod varsh purv viksit hokar alag hue the.
