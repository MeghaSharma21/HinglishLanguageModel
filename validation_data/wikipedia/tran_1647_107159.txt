 bambo airways ek vietnami airline he. isaki sthapana 2017 main hui thi or yah pehali oktober 2018 ko apni pehali udaanen shuru karegi. yah paryatakon ko vietnam main samundar ke kinare resorts ke liye sarwar karegaa. yah asia or utop main gantavyon ke liye udaan bhar jayega.
 2018 main, company ne 24 airbas e 321neo ke liye airbas ke saath ek samjhaute par hastakshar kiye, 20 bowing 787 drimliner ke liye bowing ke saath ek samjhauta.
