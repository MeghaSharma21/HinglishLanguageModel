 condrite aise patthariile ulkaon ko kaha jaataa he jo us dhul va kanon ke bane hon jo saura mandal ke shuruaati sristi-kram main maujuud the. grahon, upgrahon or anya badi vastuon ke nirmaan main unamem shaamil saamagree main pighalaav or paraton main chat jane jaisi prakriya hui. praharon ke kaaran inase bhi ukhdakar ulka bane lekin condrite keval vahi ulka hote hai jinamein aisi prakriyaayein na hui hon or jo kaafii had tak saura mandal ke aarmabhik kaal main jaise the vaise hi hon. dhyaan den ki kisi condrite main aksar zaraa-bahut dhaatu bhi upasthit ho sakati he lekin uskaa adhiktar bhaag patthariilaa hota he.
 condriton kaa naam unamem maujuud condrul naamak golakar kanon se aata he. yah unani bhashaa ke χόνδρος naamak shabd se he jiskaa arth 'kan' he. khondros main bindu-vaale 'kh' ke uccharan par dhyaan den jo bindu-rahit 'kha' se zaraa alag he or 'karaab' or 'kareed' jaise shabdon se miltaa-julata he.
