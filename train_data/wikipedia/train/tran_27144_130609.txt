 kanthe maharaj bharat ke prasiddh tabalaa vadak rahe hai. ye banaras gharaane se the. inkaa janm 1880 main kabir chauraahaa mohalle, varanasi main hua tha. inke pita pandit dilip mishra bhi jane maane tabalaa vadak the. inaki aarambhik sangeet shiksha baldev dahiyaji se aarambh hui thi. kanthe maharaj ko pratham sangitajna mana jaataa he, jinhone tabalaa vaadan dwaara stuti pratut kii thi. 
 lagbhag 70 varsh tak inhone sabhi vikhyath gayakon evam nartakon ko apane table par sangati di. 1954 main inhone dhai ghante lagaataar tabalaa bajaane kaa vishwa kirtiman sthapit kiya tha. inhen sabhi shaileyon main tabalaa vaadan par maharath praapt thi, kintu inaki visheshta banaras baj main thi. 1961 main inhen sangeet naatak academy dwaara sammanit kiya gaya tha. 1 agast, 1970 ko 90 varsh kii aayu main inaki mrityu ho gayi. inke bhattije kishan maharaj bhi vikhyath tabalaa vadak hai.
