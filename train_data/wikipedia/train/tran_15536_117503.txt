 es. paul ek bhartiya sampadak, photographer or curator the. unhone behtareen tasviren khinchi, prakruti ke adbhud najaare se lekar maanviya vyavhaar ke vibhinn roopon ko apane camere main behad baariki ke saath kaid kiya. ve paul saab ke naam se pahachaane jate the or jane maane photographer raghu raay ke agraj the. unka janm 19 agast 1929 main jhang, pakistan main hua. bantwaare ke baad unka parivaar pakistan ke lahore se nikalakar hindustan ke shimla main rahane laga. ve varsh 1960-1989 ke madhya da indian express samachar patr main karyrat the. photography ke liye milane vaale kai puraskaar inke naam hai.
 paul sahab kii photography 1953 se shuru hui. tab wo apane parivaar ke saath shimla main rahete the. tab ve sharmpal the, kintu aage chalakar unhone pehle es paal or phir es paul ke naam se apni pahchaan banaai. photography ke kshetra main pravesh ke purv ve himachal sarkaar main ek sarkari mulajim the. ve 1971 main nickon international photo pratiyogita jeetne vaale pehle bhartiya the. iske alaava, ve 1967 main british jarnal of photographi ke profile hone vaale pehle bhartiya the.
