 kharad yaa terning ek mashining prakriya he jisamen kaatane vaala aujaar praayah na ghoomane vaala hota he or jis vastu par mashning kii ja rahi he vah ghurnan gati karti rahati he. aujaar praayah raikhik gati karata he or ghoomte hue workpees pra kundalinuma rasta tay karata he.
 terning or milling main moolbhoot antar yah he ki terning kii kriya main vastu ghurnan gati karti he or milling kii kriya main aujaar ghumta he, vastu sthir rahati he. atah terning praayah belanaakaar vastuon ke liye prayukt hoti he or vastu ke kisi bhaag kaa vyas kam karne ke kaam aati he.
