 
 vir saavarkar antarrashtriya hawaii adda, jise saamanytah port blair vimaanashetra kehete hai, port blair se 2 kimi dakshin main sthit he. yahan customs vibhaag upasthit nahin he. iskaa ranve pevd he. isaki pranali yantric he. isaki udaan pattee kii lambaai 5700 fee. he. yah ek naagarik hawaii adda he. 
 iskaa naam prasiddh swatantraaa senaanee vir saavarkar ke naam par rakhaa gaya he. unhone yahan kii cellular jail main 4 july 1911 se 21 may 1921 tak kaa samay karawas main bitaayaa tha.
