 james 2 or 7 england or ireland kaa raja tha. james 7 ke naam se vah 1655 se 1688 tak scotland kaa raja bhi tha. 1688 ko hui kranti main vah pad chhod diya. vah igland, ireland or scotland ke antim roman katholic samraat tha. james 2 charlas 1 kaa betaa tha. apane bhai charlas 2 kii mrityu ke baad vah singhaasana chadh gaya. james sansad ke saath apane sangharsh ke liye sabase adhik jane jate hai. 
 james, raja charlas 1 or unki patni france ke henrita mariya kaa putr tha. unka janm 14 aktubar 1633 ko sennt james palas main hua tha. yah london main sthit he. villium lod dwaara usne baptamisa liya tha. janm ke turant baad unhen neuyork kaa raja kaa naam diya gaya tha. 
 sansad ke saath raja ke sangharsh badhane par grihayuddh shuru hua. sansadeey netaaon ne york ke raja ko saint james ke pales main simit karne kaa aadesh diya. 1648 main vah mahal se bhaag gaya.
