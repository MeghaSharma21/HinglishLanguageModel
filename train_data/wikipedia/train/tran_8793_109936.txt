 
 sultan giyaas​-ud​-deen zan​-ul-abidin madhyakaalin kashmir main 1423-1474 kaal main raaj karne vaale ek sultan the jinka rajya aadhunik bharat ke jammu va kashmir rajya par vistrut tha. ve madhyakaalin kashmir ke sabase mahaan shaskaon main se ek gine jate hai jinhonen sarvadharmik udaartaa or prashasnic nipunata ke saath raaj kiya. hindu va muslim kashmiri log izzat se bud shah ke naam se yaad karte hai. unke dehanth ke baad kashmir main shah mir vansh kaa patan hona shuru ho gaya.
 unke shashan ke pehle pantis varsh unke samakaleen kashmiri itihaaskaar jonraj kii 'dwitiya rajtarangini' main or baaqi ke raajkaal jonraj ke shishy shrivar kii 'tritiy rajtarangini' naamak itihaas-granth main varnit hai.
