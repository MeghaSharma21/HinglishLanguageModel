 belgium ne 1900 khelon main apana pehla pradarshan karne ke baad olympic khelon ke adhikansh sanskarnon par pratispardhaa kii he. yah raashtra entwarp main 1920 ke greeshmakaleen olympic ke liye mejabaan tha. 
 belgium ke athleton ne greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main 148 padak jite hai, or sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main paanch anya.
 belgium ke liye rashtriya olympic samiti kaa nirmaan or 1906 main manyata praapt thi.
 belgium ne ek avasar par khelon kii mejabani kii he.
