 
 magadan oblast rus ke siberia kshetra ke sudur purv main sthit rus kaa ek sangheey khand he jo us desh kii shashan pranali main oblast kaa darja rakhata he. isaki rajdhani magadan shahar he. isaki seemayen uttar main chukotka swashasit okrug, purvottar main kamachatkaa kroy, dakshin main khaabarowsk kroy or pashchim main saakhaa ganatantr se lagati hai. purv main yah okhotsk sagar ke saath tatasth he.
 aitihaasik rup se siberia ke sudur purvi ilaake ko colimaa kaha jaataa tha or isi ke ek bhaag main san 1953 main magadan oblast sthapit kiya gaya. yahan par dharti main sona, chaandi, tin or tungsten mila or 1930-1940 ke dashakon main unhen nikaalane ke liye khaane bani or soviet sangh ke taanashaah joseph stallin kii sarkaar ne yahan qaidi-shramikon ke gulaag kheme or sadaken banavaai. stallin ke dehanth par kaidiyon kii bajaae pagaar par kaam karne vaale mazaduron ko rakhaa gaya jo yahan par sone-aadi kii khaanon main kaam karne aaye.
 yahan par mul rup se bahut se siberian adivasee samudaay based hue the, jaise ki even, koryak, upic, chukchi, oroch, chuwai or itelmen, jo yaa to okhotsk sagar main machhliyon ke shikaar se yaa phir colimaa nadi kii ghati main rangifer-paalan se guzaaraa karte the. audyogikaran se inaki mul jivan-pranali chhin-binn ho gai lekin sarkari sahaayta se yah apane nirvaah karte rahe. soviet sangh ke tut jane ke baad yah sahaara band ho gaya or ab in samudaayon ke bahut se log berojgaar or nirdhan ho gaye hai. 1991 tak chukotka swashasit okrug isi oblast kaa hissaa hua karata tha lekin us varsh us okrug ne magadan oblast se alag ho jane kii ghoshnaa kar di.
 jati ke anusaar magadan oblast kii aabaadi is prakaar banty hui he:
 butugicheg qaidi-shram se chalane vaali gulaag khan, jise 1958 main band kar diya gaya
 magadan kshetreey theatre
 talaia naamak basti
 susuman basti main sadak ke ant main ek girizaa
