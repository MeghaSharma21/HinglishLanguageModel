 pratham vishwayuddh ke dauraan major edwin armastrang naamak vaigyanik ne saral sangrahi main apekshit sudhaar kar aisi sangrahi vyavastha ko janm diya jisamen kisi bhi aavriti ke sanket ko kisi samay sugamawa se grahan kiya ja saktaa he. yah vyavastha vastut: aadhunik lokapriy radio sangrahi seton kii janak he. 
 antena se aanevaale radio aavriti ke sanketon ko sarvapratham ek mishran-pad se hokar guzaara jaataa he, jahaan usee kshan upyukt aavriti kaa ek anya radio sanket bhi pravisht karaayaa jaataa he. yah doosraa sanket ek anya sthaneey compitra main utpann kiya jaataa he. mishran pad main donon sanketon ke sangyojan se vispand-siddhant dwaara ek nimna aavriti kaa sanket utpann hota he, jo donon aavritiyon kaa antar hota he. mishran-pad ke aage kaa sampurna paripath isi aavriti ke liye samaswarit hota he. yah aavriti madhya aavriti kehalaati he. is ksheen aavriti ko ek radio-aavriti pravardhak dwaara pradarshit kar, tathaa vimadulak dwaara sanshodhit kar, sansuchak valve dwaara shravyavriti main parinat kiya jaataa he. aage is shravy aavriti kaa pravardhit kar dhwani vistaarak main preshit kar diya jaataa he.
