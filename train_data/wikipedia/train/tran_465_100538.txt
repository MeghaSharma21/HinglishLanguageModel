 yaman main 20 praant hai jinhen muhafazah kaha jaataa he, yani vah kshetra jo kisi rajyapal yaa haafiz kii nigaraanee main rakhe gaye hon. in praanton kaa byora neeche ke vibhaag diya gaya he. in praanton ko aage zilon main vibhajit kiya jaataa he, jinhen lebanon main arabi bhashaa main 'muderiyah' kehete hai. kul milakar yaman main 333 zile hai, jo aage 2,210 upazilon main or phir 38,284 gaanvon main bante hai.
 yaman kisi jamaane main uttar yaman or dakshin yaman ke do deshon main banta hua tha. neeche kii talika main in donon kshetron ke praant sammilit hai.
