 aniake sanyukt rajya america ke alaska rajya main baithal janaganana kshetra main sthit ek nagar he. 2010 kii janaganana main yahan kii jansankhya 501 thi.
 2000 kii janaganana main anderson paas main 572 log, 174 apaariwarik-samooh, or 133 parivaar the. jansankhya ghanatv 33.9/kimi2 tha. 12/kimi2 ghanatv ke saath kul 203 nivas-yogya ikaiyaan thi. prajateey banaavat is prakaar thi: mul america, shwet, asian, africa america, or do yaa do se adhik prajati ke . kisi bhi prajati ke hispanic yaa latino 1.05% the.
