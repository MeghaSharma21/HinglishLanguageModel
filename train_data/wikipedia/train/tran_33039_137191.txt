 dharmik vidhi kaa arth dharmik rasmen hai jo vibhinn dharmon kii manyataon ke anusaar sampann hote hai.
 vishwaas ke anusaar dharmik vidhi ddhara sampann mantra-puja-havan ityadi karne se havan main upyog hone vaali saamagree ke kaaran, vahaan ke vaataavaran main shubh urja paidaa hoti he. jiske kaaran kuch samay ke liye sakaaratmak urja main vruddhi hoti he.
