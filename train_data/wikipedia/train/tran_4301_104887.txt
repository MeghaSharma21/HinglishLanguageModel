 sar-e-sang
 sar-e-sang uttari afghanistan ke badakhshaan praant ke kuraan va munjaan zile main kokecha nadi kii ghati main sthit ek basti he. yah apni lajvard kii khan ke liye mashhoor he jahaan se hazaron saalon se yah moolyawaan pathar nikaala ja rahaa he. pracheen duniya main siberia kii choti-moti do-ek khaanon ko chhodkar yah vishwa kaa ekmaatr lajvard kaa strot hota tha. sar-e-sang kaa lajvard duniya kaa sab se uttam koti kaa lajvard mana jaataa he. 2000 isapurv main apane charam par sindhu ghati sabhyataa ne bhi yahan ke shoratugai ilaake ke paas ek vyapaarik basti banaai thi jiske zariye lajvard bhartiya upmahadweep ke alaava pracheen misr or mesopotamia kii sabhyataaon tak bhi pahunchaaya jaataa tha.
 'sar-e-sang' kaa matlab farsi main 'pathar kaa sthaan' hota he. sang kaa arth 'pathar' hota he or yah avastai bhashaa ke mul "asan" shabd se aayaa he. kyonki sanskrit ore farsi donon hind-irani bhashaa parivaar kii bahane hai isliye thik yahi sajaateey shabd sanskrit main bhi "ashn" rup main miltaa he ore rugveda main prayogit he. iskaa arth bhi "pathar" he.
 kuch itihaaskaaron ke anusaar sar-e-sang kii lajvard kii khan 7,000 saalon se sakriya he. yahan ek puraani surang he jo ab chhod si gai he or do nai surangen hai. yahan ke lajvard se bane zevar pracheen misr, sumer, cocks kshetra or dur pashchimi africa ke moritania tak bhi mili hai. pracheen misr ke firaun tutankhamoon ke madhbare or iraq main sumer sabhyataa ke ur khazaane main bhi yahan kaa lajvard mila he.
