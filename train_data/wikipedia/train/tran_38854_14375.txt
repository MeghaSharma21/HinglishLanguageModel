 vaai ve chandrachud bharat ke sarvocch nyaayalay ke bhootpurv nyaayadhish rahe hai. 
 chandrachud nutan marathi vidhyalay hai skool, elfinston college or les lau college, pune main shikshit the. nyaayamurti vaai.ve. bombai aspataal main bhartee hone ke turant baad 14 july 2008 ko chandrachud kii mrityu ho gai. vah apni patni prabhaa, unke bete dhananjay yashavanth chandrachud, 2022 bharat ke sarvocch nyaayalay ke vartmaan nyaayadhish or unki beti nirmala main bharat ke mukhya nyaayadhish banane ke liye unki patni ban gaye hai. unke pote chintan chandrachud ek kanoonee vidwaan or lekhak hai.
