 hunzaa nadi yaa darya-e-hunzaa paak-adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-balatistan kshetra ke hunzaa-nagar zile kaa ahm dariyaa he. bahut zyaada mitti or chattani zarron kii aamezish kii vajah se ye bahut gadla he. yah nadi kilik naale or khunjaraab naale naamak do jharanon ke sangam se banati he or isamen kai himaniyaan jal detii hai. aage isi main naltar naala or gilgit nadi milte hai. ant main yah sindhu nadi main mil jaataa he. aitihaasik resham maarg is dariyaa ke saath chalata he. hunzaa nadi karakoram parvaton main itani paini diwaron vaali tang ghati katkar nikalati he ki kuch samikshakon ne kaha he ki 'lagata he ki chattaane chaaku se kaati gai hon'.
