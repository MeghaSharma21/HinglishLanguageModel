 bhautikee va chirsmmat yantriki main teen-vastu samasya teen bindu-aakaar kii vastuon kii gati va chaal ko samajhane ko kehete hai jo keval ek-duusare ko hi prabhavit karti hai . aamtaur par is samasya ke adhyayan main newton ke gati niyam ko aadhaar banaaya jaataa he. yah do-vastu samasya se thodaa bhinn he kyonki jahaan bhautikee main do-vastu samasya kaa har paristhiti main maanya saidhantik samaadhaan uplabdh he, vahaan teen-vastu samasya ke samaadhaan ke liye aisa koi samaadhaan uplabdh nahin he or uske liye alag-alag paristhitiyon ke liye sanganakon dwaara anumaan lagane kii aavashyaktaa padati he. prithvi, surya or chandramaa kii gatiyon va chaal kii samasya teen-vastu samasya kaa ek aitihaasik udhaaharan he.
