 shivaraj singh chouhan vartmaan main madhyapradesh praant ke mukhyamantri hai. shivaraj singh chouhan bhartiya janata party ke varisht netaa evam rashtriya swayamsevak sangh ke samarpit kaaryakarta hai. ve 29 november, 2005 ko babulal gaur ke sthaan par rajya ke mukhyamantri bane the.
 shivaraj singh chouhan kaa janm 5 march 1959 ko hua. unke pita kaa naam shri premasingh chouhan or mata shrimati sundarbai chouhan hai. unhone bhopal ke barkatullah vishvavidhyalay se snatakottar tak swarna padak ke saath shiksha praapt kii. san 1975 main model hair secondary skool ke chaatrasangh adhyaksh. apatkaal kaa virodh kiya or 1976-77 main bhopal jail main niruddh rahe. anek jan samasyaon ke samaadhaan ke liye andolan kiye or kai baar jail gaye. san 1977 se rashtriya swayamsevak sangh ke swayamsevak hai. varsh 1992 main unka shrimati sadhana singh ke saath vivaah hua. unke do putr hai.
 san 1977-78 main akhil bhartiya vidhyaarthee parishad ke sanghathan mantri bane. san 1975 se 1980 tak akhil bhartiya vidhyaarthee parishad ke madhya pradesh ke sanyukt mantri rahe. san 1980 se 1982 tak akhil bhartiya vidhyaarthee parishad ke pradesh mahasachiv, 1982-83 main parishad kii rashtriya kaaryakaarani ke sadsy, 1984-85 main bhartiya janata yuva morcha, madhya pradesh ke sanyukt sachiv, 1985 se 1988 tak mahasachiv tathaa 1988 se 1991 tak yuva morcha ke pradesh adhyaksh rahe.
 shivaraj singh chouhan 1990 main pehali baar budhani, vidhansabha kshetra se vidhayak bane. iske baad 1991 main vidisha sansadeey kshetra se pehali baar saansad bane. shivaraj singh chouhan 1991-92 main akhil bhartiya kesharia vahini ke sanyojak tathaa 1992 main akhil bhartiya janata yuva morcha ke mahasachiv bane. san 1992 se 1994 tak bhartiya janata party ke pradesh mahasachiv niyukt. san 1992 se 1996 tak maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantralay kii paramarshdatri samiti, 1993 se 1996 tak shram or kalyaan samiti tathaa 1994 se 1996 tak hindi salaahkaar samiti ke sadsy rahe.
 ye 11 veen lok sabha main varsh 1996 main vidisha sansadeey kshetra se pun: saansad chune gayi. saansad ke rup main 1996-97 main nagareey evam gramin vikaas samiti, maanav sansaadhan vikaas vibhaag kii paramarshdatri samiti tathaa nagareey evam gramin vikaas samiti ke sadsy rahe. chouhan varsh 1998 main vidisha sansadeey kshetra se hi teesri baar 12 veen lok sabha ke liye saansad chune gayi. vah 1998-99 main prakkalan samiti ke sadsy rahe. ye varsh 1999 main vidisha se chauthi baar 13 veen lok sabha ke liye saansad nirvaachit hue. ve 1999-2000 main krishi samiti ke sadsy tathaa varsh 1999-2001 main saarvajanik upkram samiti ke sadsy rahe.
 san 2000 se 2003 tak bhartiya janata yuva morcha ke rashtriya adhyaksh rahe. is dauraan ve sadan samiti ke adhyaksh tathaa bhaajapa ke rashtriya sachiv rahe. chouhan 2000 se 2004 tak sanchaar mantralay kii paramarshdatri samiti ke sadsy rahe. shivaraj singh chouhan pochavi baar vidisha se 14veen lok sabha ke sadsy nirvaachit huye. vah varsh 2004 main krishi samiti, laabh ke padon ke vishay main gathit sanyukt samiti ke sadsy, bhaajapa ke rashtriya mahasachiv, bhaajapa sansadeey board ke sachiv, kendriya chunaav samiti ke sachiv tathaa naitikta vishay par gathit samiti ke sadsy or loksabhaa kii awaas samiti ke adhyaksh rahe.
 shri chouhan varsh 2005 main bhartiya janata party ke pradesh adhyaksh niyukt kiye gayi. shri chouhan ko 29 navambar 2005 ko madhyapradesh ke mukhyamantri pad kii shapath dilaai gai. pradesh kii terahavin vidhansabha ke nirvachan main shri chouhan ne bhartiya janata party ke star prachaarak kii bhoomika kaa bakhubi nirvahan kar vijayshri praapt kii. shri chouhan ko 10 disambar 2008 ko bhartiya janata party ke 143 sadasyeey vidhayak dal ne sarvasammati se netaa chunaa. shri chouhan ne 12 disambar 2008 ko bhopal ke jamburi maidan main madhyapradesh ke mukhyamantri pad or gopneeyata kii shapath grahan kii.
