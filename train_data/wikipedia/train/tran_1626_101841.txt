 sabudane kaa kheer
 vidhi :1. sabudane ko pani se dho len or pani nikaalkar 10-15 minute ke liye rakh den.2.ab dhimi aanch par doodh ke saath sabudane ko pakaaen. isko tab tak chalaaen jab tak ki doodh gadha or kam na ho jaae or saboodana paardarshee na dikhne lage.3.chini or ilaychi powder daalkar chalaaen or aanch se utaar len.4.ek boul main daalkar kar baadaam yaa pista daalkar thanda karke yaa garmagaram sarv karen.
 saamagree :
 2 teble spoon saboodana, 2 cap doodh, 2-3 pisi hui choti ilaychi, 1 teble spoon kate hue baadaam/ pista, 2 te.spoon chini yaa swaadanusaar.kitne logon ke liye : 2
