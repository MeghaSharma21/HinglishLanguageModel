 maariana gart duniya kaa ek pramukh or bahut hi gaharaa mahasagariya gart hai. yah pashchimi prashanth mahaasaagar main maariana dweep ke purv main 200 kilometre kii ausat duuri par sthit he. yadi isaki gaharaai kii tulana mount everest se kii jaye, to yadi aap mount everest ko is gart main daal denge to bhi uski wo 1.6 kilometre pani ke neeche hoga. 
 prashanth mahaasaagar main purvi tathaa pashchimi kinaaron par inaki lagbhag ek nirantar shrankhla milti hai, jisamen meriana gart sarvaadhik gaharaa hai. yah vishwa kaa sabase gaharaa gart hai. yah philippines ke purv main sthit hai.
