 santiyago yaa santiyago, dakshin america ke chile desh kii rajdhani he. yah desh ke kendriya ghati main samudr tal se lagbhag 520 me kii unchai par sthit he. lagbhag do dashakon kii nirbaadh aarthik pragati ne latini america main santiyago ko sabase aadhunik shaharon main shumaar kar diya he or aaj yahaan darjanon shopping mal evam saikadon unchi imaarte hai.
 santiyago kii sthapana ek spanish naagarik pedro de valdivia dwaara 21 farvari, 1541 main kii gayi thi. sthapana samaaroh hewlen haul main kii gayi thi jise baad main sero santa loosia naam diya gaya. shri valdivia ne is jagah kaa chunaav is jagah kii acchhee jalwaayu evam parivahan ke liye aasaani se uplabdh mapocho nadi kii vajah se kii thi. 1 sitambar 1541 ko arako yuddh main yah shahar puri tarah tahas nahas ho gayi thi. 
 praarambhik bhawanon kaa nirmaan sthaneey logon, jinhe pikanch indian bhi kaha jaataa tha, kii madad se kiya gaya. shahar ke dakshini kinare par sthit mapocho nadi ko baad main sukhaya gaya or janata ke avaagaman tathaa anya suvidhaon ke liye khol diya gaya. sukhaye gaye sthaan ko aalaameda kaha jaataa tha. vaise ise ab Avenida Alameda Libertador Bernardo O'Higgins kaha jaataa he. 1810-1818 main shahar ke dakshini-pashchimi chor main chale chile ke swatantrata sangram main yah shahar aanshik rup se kshatigrast ho gaya tha. 1818 main is santiyago shahar ko desh kii rajdhani banaaya gaya.
