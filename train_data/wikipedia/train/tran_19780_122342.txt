 bhartiya cricket team ne 8 se 31 janvari 2016 tak do tour maichon, paanch ek divasiy antarrashtriya or teen twanti-20 antarrashtriya maichon ke liye australia kaa daura kiya. 9 july 2015 ko cricket australia dwaara daure ke liye puure kaarykram kii ghoshnaa kii gai thi.
 australia ne ekadivasiy shrinkhalaa 4-1 se jiti. is series ne paanch maichon yaa usase kam ke dwipaksheeya ekadivasiy shrinkhalaa main ran banaae hai, jisamen kul 3,159 ran shaamil hai. ekadivasiy shrinkhalaa main gyaarah shatak bhi banaae gaye, jo ek record bhi tha. bharat ne tii20i shrinkhalaa ko 3-0 se jeeta or isisi tii-20 championships main number ek ranking team ban gai.
