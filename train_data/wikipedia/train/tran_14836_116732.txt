 kagaan yaa kaaqaan mongolian or turki bhashaaon main 'samraat' ke baraabar kii ek shahi oopaadhi thi. isi tarah kagaanath inhi bhashaaon main 'samrajya' ke liye shabd tha. kagaan ko kabhi-kabhi 'kaanon kaa khan' yaa 'khan-e-kaanaa' bhi anuvaadit kiya jaataa he, jo 'maharajaadhiraj' yaa 'shahanshah' ke baraabar he. jab mongol samrajya vistrut ho gaya tha to uske bhinn hisson ko alag-alag kaanon ke supurd kar diya tha. in sab kaanon se upar ke 'sarvocch khan' ko 'kagaan' kaha jaataa tha.
 'kagaan' main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
