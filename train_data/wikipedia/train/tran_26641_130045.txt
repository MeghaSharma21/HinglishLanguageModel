 kolli hills pashchimi ghat kii pramukh parvat shrankhla he. yah namakkal jila, tamil nadu main faili hui hai. lagbhag 400 varg mile main faili ye pahariyaan 18 mile lambii or 12 mile chaudee hai. apni prakritik sundarataa se yah pahadiyaan sabko aakarshith karti hai. pahadiyon se namakkal maidan ke najaare dekhe ja sakate hai. pragaitihaasik kaal se in pahadiyon main kisi kaa awaas nahin he. tamil sahitya main in pahadiyon kaa ullekh miltaa he. kam se kam saat kaviyon kii kavitaaon main kolli hills kaa zikr miltaa he. sarvayaran kii pahadiyon ke baad keval yahi pahadiyaan barsaati wanon se dhaki rahati hai. kolli hills main bahut se manamohak jharne bhi dekhe ja sakate hai.
