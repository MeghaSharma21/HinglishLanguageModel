 nalchampu ek champookavy he jiske rachayita trivikram bhatta hai. sanskrit sahitya main champookavy kaa pratham nidarshan isi granth main hua he. isamen champu kaa vaishishty sphutatya udbhasith hua he.
 dakshin bharat ke rashtrakutvanshi raja krishna ke pautra, raja jagatug or lakshmi ke putr, indraraj ke aashray main rahakar trivikram ne is ruchir champu kii rachana kii thi. indraraj kaa rajbhishek visan 972 main hua tha or unke ashrith hone se kavi kaa bhi vahi samay he- dasham shatee kaa purvardh. is champu ke saat uchhvaason main nal tathaa damayanti kii vikhyath pranaykatha kaa bada hi chamatkaari varnan kiya gaya he. kavy main sarvatra shubhaga sanbhag shlesh kaa prasad lakshith hota he.
