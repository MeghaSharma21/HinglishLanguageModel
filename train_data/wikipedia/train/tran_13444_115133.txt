 comarbhrity ayurveda chikitsa ke aath angon main se ek ang he jisamen bacchon kii chikitsa kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he.
 ayurveda ke antargat comarbhrity kaa bahut mahatv he kyonki yah ashtaang ayurveda ke anya angon kaa aadhaar he. kaashyapasanhita comarbhrity kaa pramukh granth mana gaya he kintu yah purna rup se uplabdh nahin he.
 atharvaveda main garbhagarbhini, prasav, balshosh, vamshanugat rog aadi ke sandarbh main sthaan-sthaan par varnan miltaa he. brahman, aranyak, upanishad, grihyasutra, smritiyon tathaa puraanon main comarbhrity sambandhi varnan miltaa he. charakasamhita main ayurveda ke aath angon ke namollekh maatr he, parantu prayek ang ke antargat aane vaale vishyon/upavishyon kaa spashtikaran nahin he. kintu maharishi sushrut ne ayurveda ke aath angon main samahit vishyon kaa spasht rup se varnan kiya he.
 'comarbhrity' ang vishesh ke liye maharishi charak ne comarbhrity; sushrut ne comarbhrity evam comartantr; ashtangsangrah, ashtangaruday tathaa harit ne baalchikitsa or comarbhrity shabd kaa prayog kiya he. ashtaang ayurveda main comarbhrity ko charak ne chathe, sushrut ne paanchaven, vagbhat ne dwitiya, tathaa maharishi kaashyap ne ise pratham sthaan diya he.
