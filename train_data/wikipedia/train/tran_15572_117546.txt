 ramanujacharya vishishtadvait veydant ke pravartak the. vah aise vaishnav sant the jinka bhakthi parampara par bahut gaharaa prabhaav rahaa. 
 vaishnav aacharyon main pramukh ramanujacharya kii shishy parampara main hi ramanand hue jinke shishy kabir or surdaas the. ramanuj ne veydant darshan par aadhaarit apana nayaa darshan vishisht advait veydant likhaa tha.
 ramanujacharya ne veydant ke alaava saatvin-dasvin shatabdi ke rahasywaadee evam bhaktimargi alwar santon ke bhakthi-darshan tathaa dakshin ke pancharaatr parampara ko apane vichaaron kaa aadhaar banaaya.
 1017 isvi san main ramanuj kaa janm dakshin bharat ke tamil nadu praant main hua tha. bachapan main unhone kanchi jaakar apane guru yadav prakaash se vedon kii shiksha lee. ramanujacharya alwar sant yamunacharya ke pradhan shishy the. guru kii icchaanusaar ramanuj se teen vishesh kaam karne kaa sankalp karaayaa gaya tha - brahmasutra, vishnu sahasranam or divya prabandham kii tikaa likhnaa. unhone grihasth ashram tyaag kar shrirangam ke yatiraj naamak sanyasi se sanyas kii diksha lee.
 mysore ke shrirangam se chalakar ramanuj shaligram naamak sthaan par rahane lage. ramanuj ne us kshetra main baarah varsh tak vaishnav dharm kaa prachaar kiya. uske baad to unhone vaishnav dharm ke prachaar ke liye puure bharatvarsh kaa hi bhraman kiya. 1137 isvi san main 120 varsh kii aayu paakar ve brahmaleen hue.
 unhone yun to kai granthon kii rachana kii kintu brahmasutra ke bhashya par likhe unke do mul granth sarvaadhik lokapriy hue - shribhashyam evam veydant sangraham.
 ramanujacharya ke darshan main satta yaa paramsat ke sambandh main teen str maane gayi hai - brahm arthath ishwar, chit arthath aatm tatv or achit arthath prakruti tatv.
 vastutah ye chit arthath aatm tatva tathaa achit arthath prakruti tatva brahm yaa ishwar se pruthak nahin he balki ye vishisht rup se brahm ke hi do swaroop hai evam brahm yaa ishwar par hi aadhaarit hai. vastut: yahi ramanujacharya kaa vishishtadvait kaa siddhanth he.
 jaise sharir evam aatma pruthak nahin hai tathaa aatm ke uddeshy kii purti ke liye sharir kaary karata he usee prakaar brahm yaa ishwar se pruthak chit evam achit tatva kaa koi astitv nahin hai. ve brahm yaa ishwar kaa sharir hai tathaa brahm yaa ishwar unki aatma sadrishy hai.
 ramanuj ke anusaar bhakthi kaa arth puja-paath yaa kirthan-bhajan nahin balki dhyaan karnaa yaa ishwar kii prarthana karnaa he. saamaajik pariprekshya se ramanujacharya ne bhakthi ko jati evam varg se pruthak tathaa sabhi ke liye sambhav mana he.
