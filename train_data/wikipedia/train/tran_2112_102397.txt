 
 chaar asian chitah yaa asian dragon asia kii chaar ativiksit arthvyavasthaaen he jo hai taiwan, dakshin korea, singapore or hong kong. ye chaar kshetra asia ke pratham audyogikrut kshetron main se the jinhone aarmabhik 1960 se lekar 1990 ke dashak tak abhootpurv pragati kii thi. 21veen sadi main chaaron kshetra agrim arthvyavasthaaon or ucch aay vaale ban gaye hai. haalanki, ab anya asian arthvyavasthaaen kendra main aa gai hai jahaan aarthik vruddhi dar adhik he.
 chaaron asian chiton ke paas yogya, ucch shiksha praapt kaarybal he or inhone kai kshetron main kaushal praapt kar liya hai jahaan inke paas pratispardhaatmak laabh he. jaise, honkong or singapore vishwa ke do agrani vittiya kendra hai, jabaki dakshin korea or taiwan soochna praudyogiki ke maamle main agrani hai. baad ke do kshetron kii unnati kii kahani ko haan nadi par chamatkaar or taiwani chamatkaar kaha jaataa he or ye donon desh bahut se vikasshil deshon ke liye adarsh maane jate hai, visheshtah tagar club kii arthvyavasthaaon ke liye.
