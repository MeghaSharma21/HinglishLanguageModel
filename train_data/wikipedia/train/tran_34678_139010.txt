 barnrd kaa tara sarpadhari taramandal main nazar aane vaala ek bahut hi kam dravyaman vaala laal bouna tara he. yah prithvi se lagbhag 6 prakaash-varsh kii duuri par sthit he. mitra taare ke mandal ke teen taron ke baad barnrd kaa tara hi prithvi kaa sab se sameepi tara he. yah ek M4 shrenee kaa tara he or prithvi se isaki chamak ko 9. 54 magnitude par mapa gaya he. akaash main yah apana sthaan badalata rahata he isliye ise "barnrd kaa bhaagta tara" bhi kehete hai.
 "barnrd ke taare" ko angrezi main "barnrdz star" kehete hai. "sarpadhari taramandal" ko angrezi main "ofiukas" kehete hai. barnrd ke taare kaa bair naam "ve2500 ofiuki" he.
 barnrd ke taare kii anumaanit aayu 7 se 12 arab varsh ke bich he, jo hamaare saura mandal ke suraj kii 4.57 arab varsh kii aayu se kahin zyaada he. sambhav he ke barnrd kaa tara akashganga ke sab se puraane taron main se ek ho. isaki chamak samay ke saath-saath thodi bahut badalati rahati he, yani yah ek parivarti tara he. san 1963 se 1973 tak bahut se khagolashastri maanate the ke barnrd ke taare ke ird-gird ek brihaspati jaisaa gas daanav grah parikrama kar rahaa he lekin baad main adhyayan se yah baat galat sabit hui. phir bhi iske mandal main kisi gair saureey grah ke hone kii sambhaavna puri tarah khatm nahin hui he. barnrd ke taare kaa dravyaman suraj ke dravyaman kaa keval 17% or uskaa vyas suraj ke vyas kaa 15% se 20% he.
