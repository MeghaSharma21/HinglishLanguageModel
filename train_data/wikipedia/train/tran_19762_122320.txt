 1965 kaa bharat-paak yuddh un muthbhedon kaa naam he jo donon deshon ke bich april 1965 se sitambar 1965 ke bich hui thi. ise kashmir ke duusare yuddh ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. bharat or pakistan ke bich jammu or kashmir rajya par adhikaar ke liye bantwaare ke samay se hi vivaad chal rahaa he. 1947 main bharat-pakistan ke bich pratham yuddh bhi kashmir ke liye hi hua tha. is ladaai kii shuruuaat pakistan ne apane sainikon ko ghuspaithiyon ke rup main bhej kar is ummid main kii thi ki kashmir kii janata bharat ke khilaaf vidroh kar degi. is abhiyaan kaa naam pakistan ne yudhbhiyaan jibraltar rakhaa tha.
