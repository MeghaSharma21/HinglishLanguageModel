 haridwar tehsil bharat ke uttarakhand rajya main haridwar janapad kii ek tehsil he. haridwar janapad ke uttari bhaag main sthit is tehsil ke mukhyalay haridwar nagar main sthit hai. iske purv main uttar pradesh rajya kaa bijanour jila, pashchim main rudki tehsil, uttar main gadhwal janapad kii kotdwar tehsil or dehradun janapad kii rishikesh tehsil, tathaa dakshin main luxsar tehsil he. 
 tehsil ke adhikaar kshetra main kul 121 gaanv aate hai, or 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar isaki jansankhya 6,57,829 he.
