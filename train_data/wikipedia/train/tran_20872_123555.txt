 shaayari, sher-o-shaayari yaa sufun bhartiya upmahadweep main prachlit ek kavita kaa rup hai jisamen urdu-hindi bhashaaon main kavitaayen likhi jaati hai. shaayari main sanskrit, farsi, arabi or turki bhashaaon ke mul shabdon kaa mishrit prayog kiya jaataa he. shaayari likhane vaale kavi ko shaayar yaa sukhnwar kaha jaataa he.
 bhartiya upmahadweep kii samskruti main aisa hota aayaa he ki agar koi sher lokapriya ho jaae to vah lok-samskruti main ek suutaravakya kii tarah shaamil ho jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye:
 sher-o-shaayari ke sambandh main kuch shabd bhartiya upmahadweep or iran main prachlit hai, masalan :
 kripaya kuch dhvaniyon ke uccharan par dhyaan dijiye:
 hindi shaayari nayi shaayari ke liye
