 tulu bharat ke karnataka rajya ke pashchimi kinare main sthit dakshin kannada or udupi jilon main tathaa uttari kerala ke kuch bhaagon main prachlit bhashaa he. pehle tulu brahman vaidic or sanskrit sahitya likhane ke liye 'tiglri' naamak lipi ko upyog karte the. lekin bahut kam sahitya tulu bhashaa main mila he. par aaj is lipi ko jaananevaale bahut kam hai. puraani tiglri lipi malayalam lipi se bahut milti he. ab tulu likhane ke liye kannada lipi kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. yah panch dravid bhashaaon main ek he. dakshin kannada or udupi jilon kii adhikansh logon kii maatrubhaasha tulu he. isliye ye dono jile sammilit rup se tulunadu naam se jane jate hai. kerala ke kasargod jile main bhi bahut log tulu bhashaa bolte hai.
 udupi jile ke ek brahman ne tigalri lipi kaa prayog karke 'bhagwat' naam kaa ek granth kii rachana kii he. kavi mandaar keshav bhat ne 'mandaar ramayana' naam kaa ek aadhunik mahakavy likhaa he.
 bhashavidon ke anusaar tulu kii chaar pramukh rup yaa sheliyaan hai :
 shivalli - tulu brahmanon kii bolanewaali style.
 jain - tulunadu ke attari bhaagon ke jainon kii bolanewaali style.
 saamaanya - tulunadu ke zyaadaatar logon kii bolanewaali style. vaanijya, kala, manoranjan main is style kii upyog hoti he.
 adivasee - adivasee logon kii bolanewaali style.
 kuch puraani malayalam kritiyon ke anusaar jo kshetra kasargod ke chandragiri nadi se lekar uttar kannada ke gokarn tak vyaapit he wo tulunadu naam se janajata tha. par aaj kaa tulunadu dakshin kannada or udupi jilon tak hi simit he. phir bhi kerala ke kasargod tathaa maharashtra ke mumbai or thane main tulu bolanewaale bahut log paye jate hai.
 vartmaan samay main tulubhashik sahitya likhane ke kaary main kannada lipi kaa upyog hota he.
