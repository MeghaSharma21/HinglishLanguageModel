 kendriya vidhyalay, moscow athwa ambysi of india skool masco chaturth evam pancham manzil, 10/2 ulitsa bolshaya dorogomilovskaya bhawan, dorogomilovo jila, moscow, rus main sthit bhartiya shiksha vidhyalay he. yah kiyevski metro stesan ke nikat sthit he.
 vidhyalay moscow main bhartiya duutavas se sambaddh he. isaki sthapana 1987 main hui. varsh 2012 tak vidhyalay main pratham kaksha se aathvin tak 350 vidhyaarthee the.
 rus main bharat kaa rajdoot vidhyalay kaa sangraksh hota he. vidhyalay main kendriya madhyamik shiksha board se sambaddhta ke saath rashtriya shaikshik anusandhan or prashikshan parishad paathyakram padhaayaa jaataa he. vidhyalay main iske chaatr-chaatraon ke liye vigyaan evam manviki ke vikalp uplabdh karawaaye jate hai.
