 ishita raaj sharma ek bhartiya film abhinetri or model hai, or pyaar kaa panchnaama 2 ,sonu ke titu kii sweety, pyaar kaa panchnaama apane kaam ke liye jane jate hai.
 sharma ne pyaar kaa panchachana main abhinay kiya, jise wide frem pikchars dwaara utpaadit kiya gaya jisamen unhone charu naam kii teen ladakiyon main se ek khelaa. film lead main navagantukon ke saath ek sleeper hit ban gai. ab yuvaaon ke bich ek panth kii sthiti hasil hui he. sharma ne 2015 main merathia gangster main bhi kaam kiya tha.
 sharma lav ranjan ke pyaar kaa panchanama 2 main the, jisamen unhen omkar kapur ke saath joda gaya tha. 2018 main, unhone sonu ke titu kii sweety main shaamil kiya, jisane bharat main 100 karod se adhik kamaaye.
