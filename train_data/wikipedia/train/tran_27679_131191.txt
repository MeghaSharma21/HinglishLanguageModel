 sanyutt nikaay buddha granth he. yah suttapitak ke paanch nikaayon main se teesraa nikaay he. iske antargat 2889 sutt hai. 
 tripitak
 yah paanch varggo or 56 sanyutton main vibhakt hai. paanch vaggon main kramash: 11, 10, 13, 10 or 12 sanyutt sangruhit he. is nikaay main chote or bade sutton kaa samavesh he. tadnusaar namakaran kii baat bataai gai he. lekin vishyavaar sutton ke vargeekaran ke anusaar granth ke namakaran kii sarthakata ko samajhnaa adhik samichin he. alag alag sanyutton main sutton ke vargeekaran ko mote rup se chaar siddhanton ke anusaar samaj sakate hai : 
 sanyutt nikaay ke adhikansh sutt gadhy main hai, devtaa sanyutt jaise katipay sanyutt padya hi main he or kuch sanyutt gadhy padya donon main he. ek ek sanyutt main ek hi vishay sambandhi anek sutton ke samavesh ke kaaran is nikaay main anya nikaayon se bhi adhik punaruktiyaan he. isamen devtaa, gandharva, yaksha ityadi manuetar jeevon kaa ullekh adhik aayaa he.
 anya nikaayon kii tarah is nikaay ke sutton kaa bhi mahatv dharm or darshan sambandhi bhagwaan kii shikshaaon main he. lekin prakarantar se unamem tatkaaleen anya dharmachayon ke maton or vichaaron, saamaajik avasthaa, raajnneeti, bhugol ityadi vishyon kaa bhi ullekh he. yahan par un sab kii charchaa sambhav nahin. isliye prayek sanyutt ke mukhya vishay kaa nirdesh maatr karenge.
