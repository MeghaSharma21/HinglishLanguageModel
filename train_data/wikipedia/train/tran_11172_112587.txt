 
  gazanvi dhvaj
 gazanvi rajvansh ek turk muslim rajvansh tha jisane 975 isvi se 1186 isvi kaal main adhikansh iran, aamu paar kshetron or uttari bhartiya upmahadweep par raaj kiya. isaki sthapana sabook tigin ne tab kii thi jab use apane sasur alp tigin kii mrityu par gazanaa kaa raaj mila tha. alp tigin swayan kabhi kuraasaan ke samaani samrajya kaa sipahasaalar hua karata tha jisane apni alag riyasat kaayam kar lee thi.
 sabook tigin ke putr, mahmud gazanvi ne gazanvi samrajya kii simaaon ko bahut badhaayaa or apane rajakshetra ko uttar main aamu dariyaa se lekar purv main sindhu nadi tak or dakshin main arab sagar tak vistrut kar diya. turk nasl ke hone ke baavjood gazanvi vansh samaani samrajya kii irani-farsi sabhyataa se prabhavit tha. vah sainik abhiyaanon main turki bhashaayen prayog karata tha lekin rajdurbar or samskruti main farsi istemaal karata tha.
