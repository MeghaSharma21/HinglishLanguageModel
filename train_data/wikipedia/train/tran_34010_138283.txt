 dilli saarvajanik pustkaalay, paryaton evam samskruti mantralay ke adheen ek swytt sanghathan he jo dilli librari board dwaara shasit tathaa bharat sarkaar dwaara purnatah vittposhit he. dilli public librari bharat sarkaar dwaara varsh 1951 main unesco pariyojna ke rup main prarambh hui thi. is librari kaa udghaatan bharat ke pratham pradhanmantri mananeey pandit jawahar laal nehru dwaara puraani dilli railway stesan ke saamne ek chote se pustkaalay ke rup main kiya gaya tha.
 ab yah dilli ke mahanagareey saarvajanik pustkaalay system ke rup main viksit ho gai he. dilli public librari ke kshetreey pustakaalayon, shakhaon evam upashakhaon, punarvaas colony pustakaalayon, samudaay pustakaalayon, nikshep kedron, khel puskalayon, chal pustakaalayon, braille pustkaalay aadi kaa jaal dilli main sarvatra failaa hua he. librari main 14 lakh se adhik pustakon kaa sangrah he jisamen hindi, angreji, urdu, punjabi evam anya bhartiya bhashaaon kii adhiktar samast vishyon kii pustaken sammilit hai. pustak evam samachar patr vitaran adhinium, 1954 ke praavdhaanon ke adheen dilli public librari chaturth praapi pustkaalay bhi he. yah librari dilli ke nagarikon ko ni:shulk pustkaalay sevaayen pradaan karti he. librari kaa mukhya kaaryaklap pustak parichaalan he. yah librari bal pathakon ko bhi apni sevaayen pradaan karti he tathaa saanskrithik kaaryakalapon jaise- bhaashan, vaad-vivaad, pradarshnee aadi kaa bhi aayojan karti he.
 librari kii vishisht gatividhiyon main se drishtiheenon evam bandiyon ko pustak seva tathaa chal pustkaalay vaahan dwaara pustak seva pradaan karnaa he. dilli public librari bharat kii vishaltam tathaa dakshin purv asia kii vyastatam public librari he.
