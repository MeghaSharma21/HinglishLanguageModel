 yah lekh neuyork praant ke bare main he. shahar ke liye dekhe - neuyork shahar, anya vikalp ke liye dekhe neuyork 
 neuyork purvottar sanyukt rajya america main ek rajya he. neuyork un teraa upnivesh main se ek tha jisane sanyukt rajya kaa gathan kiya tha. rajya ke sabase bade shahar neuyork shahar, main rajya kii 40% se adhik aabaadi rahati hai. rajya kii do-tihaai jansankhya new york mahanagareey kshetra main rahati he, or kareeb 40% log long island main rahete hai. rajya or shahar kaa naam 17 veen sadi ke duk of york yani ke england ke bhavi raja james dwitiya ke upar rakhaa gaya he.
 neuyork shahar sanyukt rajya america main sabase adhik aabaadi vaala shahar he or ek vaishvik shahar he. neuyork shahar main sanyukt raashtra kaa mukhyalay he. ise duniya ke saanskrithik, vittiya or media rajdhani ke rup main varnit kiya gaya he or saath hi yah duniya kaa aarthik rup se sabase shaktishaalee shahar he. anya bade shahar hai bafelo, rochester, yonkars, or siracus, jabaki rajya kii rajdhani albani he. rajya kii seemaa new jersey or pencilvenia se dakshin main or connectikat, masachusets or varmont se purv main lagati he.
 neuyark main europea logon ke aagaman se pehle kai sau varshon tak algonquian or irqusson bolne vaale mul america janajatiyaan basii hui thi. aane vaale pehle europea log francisi upniveshvaadiyon the. 1609 main is kshetra par dach ke liye henry hadsan dwaara daawa kiya gaya, jinhone ek new netherland naam se colony basaai. 1664 main england ne dach se colony lekar us par kaa kabja kar liya.
 2016 ke anumaan ke mutabik rajya kii jansankhya 1,97,45,289 hai. is hisaab se iskaa sabhi rajyon main chauthaa sthaan hua. kshetra ke maamle main iskaa sthaan 27waan he. lagbhag 70% janata sirf angreji bolti he, 15% spani, 3% chini, baaki anya.
