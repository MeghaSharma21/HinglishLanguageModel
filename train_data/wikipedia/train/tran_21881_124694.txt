 yah prithvi par sabase lambii nadiyon kii suchi he. isamen 1,000 kilometre se adhik vaale nadi tantra shaamil hai.
 nadi kii lambaai kii gananaa karnaa bahut kathin ho saktaa he. isamen kai kaarak hote hai, jaise strot, muhane kii pahchaan yaa paribhaasha or strot or muhane ke bich nadi kii lambaai ke maap kaa paimaana, jo "nadi kii lambaai" ke sateek arth ko tay karte hai. natijatan, kai nadiyon kii lambaai kaa maapan keval sanniktan he. vishesh rup se, is baat ko lekar lambey samay tak asahmati bani rahi ki duniya kii sabase lambii nadi amejan he yaa nil. nil ko paaramparik rup se lanbaa mana jaataa rahaa he, lekin haal ke kuch varshon main brazil or peru dwaara kiye gaye adhyayan kaa suzav he ki jab amejan nadi kii lambaai or uske bagal kaa para muhaana or sarvaadhik lambi jwariya nahar kaa maapan kiya gaya to use lambi paaya gaya.
 nadi kii adhiktam lambaai nirdhaarit karne ke liye nadi kaa "vaastavik strot" uski us sahaayak nadi ke strot ko mana jaataa he jo muhane se sabase dur sthit ho. is sahaayak nadi kaa naam mukhya nadi vaala hi ho saktaa he. udhaaharan ke liye, mississippi nadi ke strot ko aam rup se itaska jhil ko mana jaataa he, lekin mississippi tantra kaa sabase duurastha strot jeffrasan nadi he, yah mizori nadi kii ek sahaayak he jo phir mississippi kii ek sahaayak he. jab mississippi kii lambaai ko is muhane se lekar sarvaadhik dur ke is strot tak mapa jaataa he to ise mississippi-mizori-jeffrasan kaha jaataa he. iske alaava, kabhi-kabhi yah bataanaa mushkil ho jaataa he ki nadi vastav main kahaan se shuru hui he, vishesh rup se ve nadiyaan jinka nirmaan alpakaalik dhaaraon, daldal, yaa parivarti jhiilon dwaara hua he. is lekh main, lambaai kaa arth he, naam kii parvaah kiye bina ek die gaye nadi tantra main sabase lambey satat nadi channel kii lambaai.
 ek nadi ke muhane ko nirdhaarit karnaa tab kathin ho jaataa he jab nadi kaa muhaana vishaal hota he jo dhire-dhire chaudaa hote hue sagar main khulata he; udhaaharan ke liye river plate or saint laurence nadi. kuch nadiyaan jaise okavango, hambolt, or kern kaa koi muhaana nahin he; iske bajaay ye nadiyaan pani kii alp maatra ke saath chalati hai or ant main vashpikrut ho jaati hai, yaa jaleey chattani paraton main chali jaati hai, athwa krishi ke liye mode lee jaati hai. in nadiyon ke samaapt hone kaa sateek sthaan mausam ke anusaar bhinn hota he.
 un nadiyon ke udgam ko nirdhaarit karnaa mushkil ho saktaa he jo vibhinn kheton se aane vaale jal strot ke khai main jamaav se gathit hoti hai jinamein sirf barish ke baad jal aata he. isi tarah, chaak kshetra main shuru hone vaali nadiyon main, jaise dakshin england main chiltern, upree pravaah kii lambaai jo sukhi hoti he vah jal-str ke aadhaar par bhinn hoti he or yah str mausam ke anusaar badalata rahata he: sheetakaaleen dekhen.
 udgam or muhane ke bich ek nadi kii lambaai maanchitr paimaane ke muddon ke kaaran nirdhaarit karnaa mushkil ho saktaa he. chote paimaane ke nakshe aam taur par bade paimaanon ke naqshe kii tulana main samanyikaran karte hai, yaa rekhaon ko "chikna" kar dete hai. aam taur par adarsh rup main sweekrut lambaai maapan un maanchitron par aadhaarit hone chahie jo nadi kii chaudaai dikhaane laayak paryaapt bade hon or jis maarg kaa maapan hoga vah vahi maarg hoga jise ek naav nadi ke bichon-bich apnayegi.
 yahaan tak ki jab vistrut nakshe uplabdh hote hai, lambaai maapan hameshaa spasht nahin hota he. ek nadi kii kai shakhaaen yaa anabranch ho sakati hai. lambaai is baat par nirbhar ho sakati he ki nadi kaa madhya hissaa mapa ja rahaa he yaa uske kinare maape ja rahe hai. ek jhil ke madhyam se lambaai ko kaise mapa jaae yah aspasht ho saktaa he. mausami or vaarshik parivartan nadiyon or jhiilon ko badal sakate he. anya kaarak jo nadi kii lambaai ko badal sakate hai unamem shaamil hai kataav chakra or baadh, baandh, tat bandha or channleekaran. iske alaava, ghumaav kii lambaai samay ke saath prakritik yaa kritrim katautiyon ke kaaran parivartit ho sakati he, jab ek nayaa channel bhoomi ke sankare kshetra se hote hue nadi ke ek vishaal mode ko paar karata he. udhaaharan ke liye, 1766 se 1885 ke bich 18 kataav ke kaaran mississippi nadi kii lambaai kahiraa, illinois se lekar orlians, louiciana tak 218 mile ghat gai.
 ye baaten ek nadi kii lambaai ke sateek maapan ko naammumkin nahin to mushkil to banaati hi hai. sateekta or parishuddhta main bhinnata bhi vibhinn nadiyon ke bich lambaai kii tulana ko anischitata ke khatre ke saath kathin bana detii hai.
 nimna talika main die gaye data kaa upyog karte samay upar ullikhit charchaa kaa dhyaan rakhana chahie. adhikansh nadiyon ke liye, vibhinn strot ek nadi pranali kii lambaai par paraspar virdohi jaankaree pradaan karte hai. vibhinn sroton main jaankaree koshthkon ke bich he.

 amejan basin ateet main pashchim kii ore prashanth mahaasaagar main milti thi, jiske baad andes kaa udbhav hua jisane uske bahaav ko palat diya. 
 congou basin puri tarah se ucch bhoomi dwaara ghiri hui he, sivaay kinshasa se guzarane vaale iske nikaas ko chhodkar jisamen shaamil he maanyanga ke aasapaas jharne. jo yah ingit karte hai ki congou basin kaa adhikansh kshetra apekshaakrit adhik ucch bhoomi str par tha or iske adhikansh nimna bahaav ke kam hone se iskaa jirnoddhar hua.
 parmian or aarambhik tryesic kaal main africa or dakshin america ek-duusare ke kareeb the or bich main koi samudr nahin tha or congou sambhawatah amejan basin main mil gai or ant main prashanth main. iskaa baaki bahaav jo dakshin atlantic ke khulne par puri tarah kho gaya tha, iskaa sampoorn bahaav kul lagbhag 12,000 kimi lambaa rahaa hoga.
 yah nadi aakhri him yug main kareeb 10,000 kimi lambi rahi hogi. pashchim siberian himand jhil dekhen. iskaa sabase lanbaa udgam mongolia kii selenga nadi thi: iskaa apvaah him jhiilon se hote hue aral sagar or caspian sagar se lekar kala sagar tak tha.
 purv main jhil tanganyika, uttar kii ore albert nil main apvaah karti thi or nil ko kareeb 700 mile ke aasapaas lanbaa banaati thi, lekin meosin kaal main virunga jwalamukhi ke fatne se uskaa apvaah maarg avaruddh ho gaya. iske alaava, jab bumadhya sagar, messinian lavanta sankat ke dauraan sookhaa tha, to nil shushk samudri kshetra main vistaarit hui or is tarah uski lambaai main 100 mile yaa usase adhik kaa ijapha hua.
 aarmabhik plistocin ke uttarardh main kareeb do milian varsh pehle bavantian charan ke dauraan aredanos ek vishaal nadi thi. yah kareeb 2700 kilometre yaa kareeb 1700 mile lambii thi, yani aadhunik denube se thodi si choti. yah lapaland main shuru hui or phir aadhunik kaal ke bothnia kii khadi se hote hue pashchimi europe ke baltic sagar tak pahunchi, jahaan iskaa ek vishaal delta tha jo maujuuda uttar sagar tak failaa hua tha. aakaar main yah, vartmaan ke amejan nadi ke muhane se tulaneey tha.
 nil ke samaan hi, messinian lavanta sankat ke dauraan, po kaa pravaah dakshin-purv kii ore vistaarit rahaa hoga jo aaj adriatic sagar kaa failaav he, jisase isaki lambaai kamobesh vartmaan kii tulana main dugani rahi hogi, jo mausami rup se tab badalata rahata hoga jab yah nadi suukh jane tak garm sookhe samudri sataha par bahati hogi.
