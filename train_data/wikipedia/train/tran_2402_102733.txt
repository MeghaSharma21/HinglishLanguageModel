 ratika ramswami ek bhartiya vanyajeev photographer he. ve nai dilli main rahati hai or ek swatantra photographer ke rup main kaary kar rahi hai. unhen apni tasveeron ke liye kaaphi prashansha praapt hui he. vah pratham bhartiya mahila hai jinhone antarrashtriya str par ek vanyajeev photographer ke rup main apni pahchaan banaai he. 
 rathika 1999 main apane vivaah ke baad se nai dilli main rah rahi hai. 
 rathika ko apana pehla camera unke chachaa se mila tha or ek shaukia photographer ke taur par unhone ped or phulon ke chitra lene se shuruyaat kii. 2003 main unhone kiyoladayo rashtriya park, bharatpur kaa daura kiya or pakshiyon kii tasviren lee. unhone okhla pakshi abhayaarany main niyamit rup se jaakar vibhinn prakaar ke pakshiyon ke vyavhaar kaa adhyayan kiya. iske baad unhone vanyajeev photography ko apni vyavsaayik ruchi bana liya evam bharat,kenya or tanzania main sthit kai rashtriya parkon kaa bhraman kiya. 
 unki vanyajeev photography ko sitambar 2005 main nai dilli ke india international centre main yojit "svachh ganga abhiyaan" main vishesh rup se pradarshit kiya gaya tha. 2007 main, jawaharlal nehru vishvavidhyalay ke vaarshik calendar main unki pakshiyon kii tasveeron ko chitrit kiya gaya tha. da "bards of india" ne unhen 2008 main bharat ke sheersh 20 sabase achche photographers ke rup main chunaa tha. vah is saamaan ko paane vaali akeli mahila hai. vanya jivan pradarshaniyon main bhaag lene ke alaava vah vanyajeev photography par aadhaarit kaaryshaalaaon kaa aayojan karti rahati hai.
 rathika photography kala association of india kii sansthaapak sadasyon main se ek he. 
 rathika ne apni pratham pustak " da best of wildlife moments" ko 2014 main swayan prakashit kiya.
