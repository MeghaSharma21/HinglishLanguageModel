 es em krishna, janm1932, puraa naam- somanahalli mallaiya krishna, varsh1999 se 2004 karnataka ke mukhyamantri rahe or varsh 2004 se 2008 tak maharashtra ke rajyapal rahe. 22 may 2009 ko pradhanmantri manmohan singh ne krishna ko kendriya cabinet main shaamil kiya gaya or 23 may 2009 videsh mantralay kii jimmedaari saunpi
 es em krishna ke pita kaa naam es si mallaiya he. krishna ne apni snaatak kii padhai mysore ke maharaja college se snaatak kii degree hasil kii. phir bangalore ke sarkari college se kanoon kii degree lee. ucch shiksha ke liye ve america gaye. vahaan se snaatak karne ke baad unhone antarrashtriya kanoon main shikshan shuru kii. america main unki sakriy raajnneeti main ruchi jaagi. vahaan unhone jon epa kennedy ke raashtrapati chunaav kaa prachaar kiya. karnataka se lautne ke turant baad 1962 main unhen karnataka vidhansabha kaa sadsy chunaa gaya.29 april, 1964 ko prema ke saath unka vivaah hua. es em krishna achche tennis khilaadi hai or niyamit tennis khelte hai.
 karnataka ke mandya se ve kai baar saansad chune gaye. indira gandhi or rajeev gandhi ke pradhanmantritv main unhone mantri ke rup main kaary kiya. 1983-84 ke bich indira gandhi or 1984-85 ke bich rajeev gandhi ke kaal main ve udyog or vitt rajya mantri bane. 1996 or 2006 main ve rajyasabha sadsy chune gaye. vibhinn kaalon main ve karnataka vidhansabha ke sadsy rahe. 1989-1992 ke bich ve karnataka vidhansabha ke adhyaksh or upmukhyamantri rahe. 1999 main ve karnataka pradesh congress ke adhyaksh bane or pradesh main party ko jeet dilaai. is tarah ve 2004 tak pradesh ke mukhyamantri rahe. unhone aadhunik bangalore kaa janak kaha jaataa he. unhone sarkari-nijee saajhedaari kaa samarthan kiya.2004 main ve maharashtra ke rajyapal bane. sakriy raajnneeti main lautne ke uddeshy se unhone 5 march 2008 ko unhone apane pad se istipha de diya. 22 march 2009 ko dow manmohan singh ne unhen apane cabinet main shaamil kar liya or unhen videsh mantralay kii jimmedaari saunpi. esaem krishna, manmohan singh mantrimandal ke ek maatr aise mantri hai jo mantri, upmukhyamantri, mukhyamantri, rajyapal or teen baar kendriya mantri kaa pad sambhaal chuke hai.
