 feril kaa niyam : is niyam ke anusaar, “dharaatal par mukhya rup se chalane vaali sabhi hawaaen prithvi kii gati ke kaaran uttari golarddh main dahini ore tathaa dakshini golarddh main baayin ore mud jaati hai.” yah niyam bade kshetron par chalane vaali sthaayee pawanon, chote chakravaaton or pratichakravaaton par lagu hota he. is niyam kaa prabhaav mahasagariya dhaaraon, jwariya gatiyon, raketon, aadi par bhi dekha jaataa he.
