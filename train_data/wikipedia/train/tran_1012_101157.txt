 lagadh rishi vaidic jyotishshastra kii pustak vedang jyotish ke praneta he. inkaa kaal 1350 i pus mana jaataa he. is granth kaa upyog karke vaidic yagyon ke anushthan kaa samay nishchit kiya jaataa tha. ise bharat main ganitiya khagolashastra par aadya kaary mana jaataa he. lagadh rishi kaa ek pramukh navonmesh tithi kaa ek maanak samay maatrak ke rup main kaa prayog he.
