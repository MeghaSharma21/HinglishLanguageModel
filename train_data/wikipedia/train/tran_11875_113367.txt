 jannu yaa kumbhkarna, jise limbu bhashaa main phoktang lungma kehete hai, himalay kaa ek parvat he. yah kanchanajangha se pashchim main purvi nepal main sthit he. yah vishwa kaa 32vaan sarvocch parvat he. kiraat samudaay main is parvat ko pavitra mana jaataa he.
 limbu bhashaa main "phoktang" kaa arth "kandhaa" or "lungma" kaa "parvat" he, yani phoktanglungma kaa matlab "kandhon vaala parvat" he.
