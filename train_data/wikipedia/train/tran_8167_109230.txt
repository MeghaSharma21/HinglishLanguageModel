 titania arun grah kaa sab se bada upgrah he. mana jaataa he ke yah chandramaa barf or pathar kii lagbhag baraabar matraaon se rachaa hua he - isaki sataha barfili he or andar kaa hissaa patthariilaa he. kuch vaigyaniko kii soch he ke bahari barf or andruni pathar ke bich main ek pani kii tah hone kii sambhaavna he, lekin is baat kaa abhi koi pakkaa saboot nahin mila he. satahi barf main kuch padaarthon ke mili hone ke kaaran is upgrah kaa rang thodaa laal prathit hota he. isaki sataha par antariksh se gire hue ulkapindon kii vajah se bahut se bade gadhde bhi hai, jinamein se ek bhayankar gadhde kaa vyas 326 kimi he. phir bhi dekha gaya he ke arun ke ek anya upgrah oberon par is se bhi zyaada gadhde hai. vaigyanik anumaan lagate hai kii titania par shaayad kaafii bhukampon ke aane se bahut se gadhde bhare ja chuke hai or ab nazar nahin aate. wayager dwitiya yaan ke janvari 1986 main arun ke paas se guzarane par titania kii bahut se tasviren lee gayi jinke zariye isaki sataha ke lagbhag 40% hisse ke nakshe banaae ja chuke hai.
 titania kaa akar gol he. iskaa ausat vyas lagbhag 1577 kimi he. iske muqaabale main prithvi ke chandramaa kaa vyas lagbhag 3,474 kimi he, yani kii titania kaa akar hamaare chandramaa ke aadhe se zaraa chota he.
