 
 kyoto japan ke yamashiron praant main sthit nagar he. kwamu shashan kaal main ise 'he yaan jo' arthath 'shanti kaa nagar' kii sangya di gai thi. 11veen shatabdi tak kyoto japan kii rajdhani tha or aaj bhi pashchimi pradesh kii rajdhani he. 1890 i. main is nagar ko biva jhil sein lagbhag 7 mile lambii nahar dwaara sambandhit kar diya gaya.
 vishaal mandiron, bhavy prasaadon or kalatmak bhawanon ke liye kyoto sampurna japan main prasiddh he. yahan resham ke kapade, chini mitti ke bartan, kaseedakaari, ranganirmaan, pankhaa, khilonaa or anya prakaar ke dhaatu ke bartanon kaa udyog adhik viksit he. yah japan main buddha dharm kaa sabase bada centre he. yahan ek vishvavidhyalay tathaa ek kalakendre hai.
