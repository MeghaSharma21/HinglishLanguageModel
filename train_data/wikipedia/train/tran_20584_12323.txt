 hetu kaa arth he kaaran. kavy hetu ke antargat un kaarnon par vichaar kiya jaataa he jo kavy kii rachana ke liye mul or nirnaayak rup se uttaradaayi hote hai. sanskrit kavyashastra main kavy ke mukhaytah teen hetu ginaye gayi hai- pratibha yaa shakti, vyutpatti yaa bahujyata. or abhyaas. abhinav gupt ke anusaar pratibha apurv vastu ke nirmaan main sakasham kavi kii navonmeshshalini buddhi he. shaastra or lok vyavhaar kaa gahan paryaalochan karne ke baad kavi main bahujyata gun kaa samavesh hota he. kavy rachana kii punah puneen rachana kii pravriti hi abhyaas he.
