 maysaan praant, jise arabi main muhaafazaat​ maysaan kehete hai, iraq kaa ek praant he. is praant kii rajdhani dajala nadi ke kinare basaa amarah shahar he or san 1976 se pehle is puure soobe kaa naam hi 'amaara praant' tha. is praant kii purvi seemayen iran se lagati hai.
 likhaeeyon main maysaan praant kaa zikr pehali sadi isvi se miltaa he. pracheen unani itihaaskaar strabo ne apni likhaiyon main is kshetra ko 'mesini' kaa naam diya tha. madhya farsi main yah 'meshaan' ke naam se janaa jaataa tha or pracheen haan chini sroton main ise 'tiao tche' bulaayaa gaya he.
 maysaan praant ke log lagbhag sabhi shiya muslim hai. yahan daldali arabon ke bhi bahut se parivaar basate hai .
