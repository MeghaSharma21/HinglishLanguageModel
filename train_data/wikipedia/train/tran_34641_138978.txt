 filmphere puraskaar samaaroh bhartiya cinema ke itihaas kii sabase puraani or pramukh ghatnaon main se ek rahi he. isaki shuruyaat sabase pehle 1954 main hui jab rashtriya film puraskaar kii bhi sthapana hui thi. puraskaar janata ke mat evam jeuri ke sadasyon ke mat donon ke aadhaar par di har saal di jaati he.
 isaki shuruyaat filmafere patrika main lokapriy abhinayta evam abhinetriyon par karaaye gayi pathakon ke matdaan dwaara 1953 main hui thi jab lagbhag 20,000 pathakon ne isamen hissaa liya tha. 21 march 1954 ko hone vaale pehle puraskaar samaaroh main sirf paanch puraskaar rakhe gayi the jisamen do bigha zamin ko sarvshereshth film, sarvshereshth nirdesan ke liye bimal raay, sarvshereshth abhinayta ke liye dilip kumaar, sarvshereshth abhinetri ke liye minaa kumari, evam isi film main sarvshereshth sangeet ke liye naushaad ko puraskaar die gayi the.
 ab puraskaron kii sankhya badhakar 31 ho gayi he. iske alaava "critics award" bhi diye jate hai jiske faisle main darshak na shaamil hokar filmon ke shreshth aalochak iske nirnaayak hote hai.
