 drugvishay yaa parighatna koi vah chiz hai jo swayan prakat hoti hai. bhale sadaiv nahin, par aam taur par drugvishyon ko "chijen jo drushtigochar hoti hai" yaa samvedan-samarth jeevon ke "anubhav" samajhe jate hai, yaa ve jo saiddhanthik rup se ho sakate hai. yah shabd emanuele kant ke dwaara aadhunik darshanik prayog main aayaa, jinhone ise manasvishay ke vipareet bataaya. drugvishay ke vipareet, manasvishay anveshan ke dwaara pratyaksh rup se abhigamya nahin hai. kant Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz ke darshan ke is bhaag se behad prabhavit the, jisamen, drugvishay or manasvishay parsparik takneekee shabd the.
