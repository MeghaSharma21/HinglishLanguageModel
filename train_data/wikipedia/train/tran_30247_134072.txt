 aitihaasik rup se bharat ek bahut viksit aarthik vyavastha thi jiske vishwa ke anya bhaagon ke saath majboot vyapaarik sambandh the. aupniveshik yug ke dauraan angrej bharat se sasti daron par kachi saamagree kharida karte the or taiyaar maal bhartiya bazaaron main saamaanya mulya se kahin adhik ucchtar keemat par bechaa jaataa tha jiske parinamswaroop sroton kaa dwimargi hraas hota tha. is avadhi ke dauraan vishwa kii aay main bharat kaa hissaa 1700 isvi ke 22.3 pratishat se girakar 1952 main 3.8 pratishat rah gaya. 1947 main bharat ke swatantraaa prapti ke pashchaat arthvyavastha kii punanirmaan prakriya prarambh hui. is uddeshy se vibhinn nitiyon or yojanafun banaai gayin or panchavarshiya yojanaon ke madhyam se karyanvit kii gayi. 
 1950 main jab bharat ne 3.5 fisadi kii vikaas dar hasil kar lee thi to kai arthshaastriyon ne ise british raaj ke antim 50 saalon kii vikaas dar se tiguna ho jane kaa jashn manaaya tha. samaajwaadiyon ne ise bharat kii aarthik nitiyon kii jeet qaraar diya tha, ve nitiyaan jo antarmukhi thim or saarvajanik kshetron ke upakramon ke varchasv vaali thim. haalanki 1960 ke dashak main east indian tigron ne bharat se doguni vikaas dar hasil kar lee thi. jo is baat kaa pramaan tha ki unki bahyamukhi or nijee kshetra ko praathamikta dene vaali aarthik nitiyaan behatar thim. aise main bharat ke paas 80 ke dashak kii bajaay ek dashak pehle 1971 main hi aarthik sudhaaron ko apnaane ke liye ek acha udhaaharan mil chukaa tha.
 bharat main 1980 tak jienpi kii vikaas dar kam thi, lekin 1981 main aarthik sudhaaron ke shuru hone ke saath hi isane gati pakad lee thi. 1991 main sudhaar puri tarah se lagu hone ke baad to yah majboot ho gai thi. 1950 se 1980 ke teen dashakon main jienpi kii vikaas dar keval 1.49 fisadi thi. is kalkhand main sarkari nitiyon kaa aadhaar samaajwaad tha. iskar kii dar main 97.75 pratishat tak kii vruddhi dekhi gayi. kai udyogon kaa rashtriyakaran kar diya gaya. sarkaar ne arthvyavastha par puri tarah se niyantran ke prayaas or adhik tej kar die the. 1980 ke dashak main halke se aarthik udaarvaad ne prati vyakti jienpi kii vikaas dar ko badhaakar prativarsh 2.89 kar diya. 1990 ke dashak main achche-khaase aarthik udaarvaad ke baad to prati vyakti jienpi badhkar 4.19 fisadi tak pahunch gai. 2001 main yah 6.78 fisadi tak pahunch gai.
 1991 main bharat sarkaar ne mahatvapurn aarthik sudhaar prastut kiye jo is drushti se vrihad prayaas the ki inamen videsh vyapaar udaarikaran, vittiya udaarikaran, kar sudhaar or videshi nivesh ke prati aagrah shaamil tha. in upayon ne bhartiya arthvyavastha ko gati dene main madad kii. tab se bhartiya arthvyavastha bahut aage nikal ai he. sakal swadeshi utpaada kii ausat vruddhi dar jo 1951â€“91 ke dauraan 4.34 pratishat thi, 1991-2011 ke dauraan 6.24 pratishat ke rup main bad gayi. 2015 main bhartiya arthvyavastha 2 trillian america dollar se aage nikal gayi.
 bharat ne 1980 ke dashak main sudhaaron ko kaaphi manthar gati se lagu kiya tha, lekin 1991 ke bhugtan santulan ke sankat ke baad yah mukhyadhara kii niti ban gai. isi saal soviet sangh ke patan ne bhartiya raajnneetigyon ko is baat kaa ahsaas kara diya ki samaajwaad par or jor bharat ko sankat se nahin ubaar paaegaa or china main deng shiyaoping ke kaamyaab bazaronmukh sudhaaron ne bataa diya tha ki aarthik udaarikaran ke beshumaar phaayde hai. bharat kii sudhaar prakriya uttarottar or aniyamit thi, lekin iske sanchit prabhaav ne 2003-08 main bharatia ko chamatkaari arthvyavastha bana diya jahaan sakal rashtriya utpaada kii vikaas dar 9 pratishat or prati vyakti vaarshik sakal rashtriya utpaada vikaas dar 7 pratishat se zyada ho gai.
