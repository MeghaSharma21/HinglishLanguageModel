 gini pashchim africa ke tat ke kshetra ke liye paaramparik naam he jo gini kii khadi ke saath sthit he. yah prakritik rup se nam ushnkaribandheey jangal yaa savana he jo tat ke saath faile hue hai. yah kshetra uttar main sahel se seemaa rakhata he. "gini" shabd kii vyutpatti anishchit he. aitihaasik rup se, gini kshetra africa ke sabase pehle hisson main se ek tha jo europea logon ke saath vyapaar karnaa shuru kiya tha. pratham portugali phir spani aae. uske baad dach, francisi or british aae.
 hathidaant, sona or gulaamon main vyapak vyapaar ne is kshetra ko amir bana diya. 18veen or 19veen sadi main kai kendrikrut samrajya viksit hue. ye vyapak khule sahel ke bade rajyon kii tulana main bahut chote the, lekin unke paas jansankhya ghanatv bahut adhik tha or vah raajnneetik rup se adhik kendrikrut the. in samrajyon ke ekajutata ne kshetra ko africa ke anya kshetron kii tulana main europea ghuspaith ke prati adhik pratirodh dikhaane ke liye prerit kiya. europiyon logon ke prati beemaari ke maahaul ke saath is tarah ke pratirodh kaa matlab tha ki 19veen shatabdi ke ant tak europiyon dwaara gini kaa adhikansh upniveshikaran nahin kiya gaya tha.
