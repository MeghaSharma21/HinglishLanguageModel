 antyakshari pracheen kaal se chalaa aata smaranshakti kaa parichaayak ek khel jisamen kahe hue shloke yaa padya ke antim akshar ko lekar doosraa vyakti usee akshar se aarambh hone vaala shloke yaa padya kahata he, jiske uttar main phir pehla vyakti duusare ke kahe shloke yaa padya ke antim akshar se aarambh hone vaala shloke yaa padya kahata he. isi prakaar yah khel chalata he or jab apekshit vyakti kii samaran shakti jawaab de jaati he or usase padyamay uttar nahin ban paata tab uski haar maan lee jaati he. yah khel do se adhik vyaktiyon ke bich bhi vrittakar rup main khelaa jaataa he. vidhyaarthiyon main yah aaj bhi prachlit he or anek sansthaaon main to isaki pratiyogita kaa aayojan bhi hota he.
