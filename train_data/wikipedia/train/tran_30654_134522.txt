 nyaayamurti tirath sinh thakur
 
 tirath singh thakur purv bharat ke mukhya nyaayadhish hai. ve purv main bharat ke sarvocch nyaayalay ke nyaayadhish tathaa punjab or haryana ucch nyaayalay or dilli ucch nyaayalay ke mukhya nyaayadhish rah chuke hai.
 ve jammu or kashmir ucch nyaayalay tathaa karnataka ucch nyaayalay ke nyaayadhish rah chuke hai.
