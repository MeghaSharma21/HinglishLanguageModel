 ries of da gargoyles ek 2009 tivi film bill korkoran dwaara nirdeshit or Syfy channel ke liye utpaadan kiya. is main 18 film he manator shrinkhalaa.
 paris, france main, do shramikon ek chhipe hue kaksh pate hai jabaki saint jin andre carch ke bhumigat khudaai. ve jagah main moolyawaan vastuon ko ikattha lekin ve ek praani se hamalaa kar rahe hai. is bich, badnaam professor jaik randel, jo visheshgyon dwaara asweekaar kar diya gargoyles ke bare main ek pustak likhi he apane dost kairal jhaankna bekham jagah baahar dwaara protsaahit kiya jaataa he. ve raat ke dauraan site main chupake or jabaki carol kuch kalakritiyon kaa sangrah he, jaik apane camere ke saath recording he. baahar ke nile, jaik ek pankh rakshas uski ore aa rahi dekhtaa he or vah kairal ke saath sthaan se chalaata he, lekin apane camere tut jaataa he. ve ek baar main jane ke liye or ek vishaal pathar unki car par gir jaataa he. jaik apane boarding ghar ke liye ek taxi letaa he or kairal par hamalaa kiya or uske apartment main ek parnala dwaara maut kii sajaa di he. agali subah, jaik kairal sharir ko dikhaata he or kai hatyaon ke inspector gibert ke pramukh sandigdh ho jaataa he. jaik ko ek sansanikhej samachar patr, nichol ricard ke samvaadadaata kii talash kaa faisla kiya he or apane cameraman walsh apane tepe detaa he. jab walsh buree tarah aakaar kaa drishy thik nahin he, vah nichol pataa chalata he or ve kehete hai ki jaik pagal nahin he or vah ek parnala carch ke bhumigat vastav main dekha tha. ve carch par lautne ke jaanch karne ke liye tay he.
