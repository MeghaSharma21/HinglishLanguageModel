 mairi ellen webar ek america kaaryakari, vaigyanik, vimanavahak, spikar or purv nasa ke antariksh yatri hai. webar kaa janm cleveland, ohio ke bedford hits main hua tha. unhone 1980 main bedford hai skool se apni vidhyalay kii shiksha puri kii ; ek bi.es. praapt hua 1984 main pardu vishvavidhyalay se rasinic ejineering main bi.es. kii degree praapt kii jahaan vah phu moro kii sadsy thi. unhone apni piech.di.kii degree california vishvavidhyalay main bhautik rasayan vigyaan main kii. or barkalese 1988 main or 2002 main dakshini methodist vishvavidhyalay se ek em.bi.e kii degree praapt ki. webar ohio edison ek undergrad ke rup main, dilko electronics or 3 em main ek rasinic ejineering prashikshu thi. barkale main unke doctoret shodh main, unhone silicon se jude rasinic pratikriyaaon ke bhautikee kaa pataa lagaaya. 
 mairi ellen webar kaa chayan 1992 main antariksh yatri ke chaudaaven group main nabar dwaara kiya gaya tha. nasa ke saath apane das saal ke karier ke dauraan, unhone kai padon kii sthapana kii. unhone praudyogiki vyavsyeekaran main bade paimaane par kaam kiya, or nasa ke mukhya kaaryakari adhikari kii report ke ek dal ke hisse ke rup main, unhone sidhe ek udhyam puunji form ke saath kaam kiya taaki ek antariksh praudyogiki kaa laabh uthaane vaale vyavsaay udhyam ko safaltaapurvak pahchaan or viksit kiya ja sake. do antariksh udaanon, ests -70 or ests -01 kii anbhavi, vah antariksh main udne ke liye sabase kam umr kii thi or unhone 450 ghante tak log in kiya. vah nasa ke asaadhaaran seva medal ke praptakarta hai. disambar 2002 main unhone nasa se istipha de diya.
