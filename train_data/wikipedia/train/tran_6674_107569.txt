 lubumbashi, congou loktantric ganraajya ke dakshin-purvi hisse kaa doosraa sabase bada rajya he . lubumbashi congou loktantric ganraajya kii khanan rajdhani he, jo desh kii sabase badi khanan companiyon ke liye ek centre ke rup main kaary karti he. 
 bharat main lubumbashi ko bhartiya sainik captain gurbachan singh salaaria, paramveer chakra ke shahid sthal ke rup main janaa jaataa he. disambar 1961 main congou sankat ke dauraan sanyukt raashtra ke shantisthaapan mishan ke tahat congou ganraajya main tainaat bhartiya sainikon main salaaria bhi shaamil the jo pruthakwaadiyon se muthbhed main veergati ko praapt ho gaye the.
