 iravati yaa irawadi burma kii ek pramukh nadi he. yah nadi burma ke uttar se dakshin kii disha me behate hue burma ko do bhaag me vibhajit karti he. is nadi kii lambaai 2170 kilometre he or yah burma me bahane vaali sabase lambii nadi he. iravati nadi ek pramukh vyapaarik nauka maarg bhi he.
 iravati nadi nadi kaa naam sanskrit shabd iravati yaa airavat se aayaa he. hindu puranic kathaon ke anusaar iravati ek pavitra nadi evam ek devi kaa naam he or aravat unke putr kaa naam tha jo ki devraj indra ke vaahan the.
 uttari burma me sthit enmai evam mali nadi kaa sangam iravati nadi kaa udgam sthaan he. andaman sagar se 290 kilometre purv yah nadi ek vishaal delta kaa nirmaan karti he. is delta ke pashchimi evam purvi seemaa kramshah pathen evam yangon nadi banaati he.
