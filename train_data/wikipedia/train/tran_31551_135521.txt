  parammanya sar izac albert izex  ek australian nyaayadhish or raajnneetijna the. unhen 21 janvari 1931-23 janvari 1936 ke bich, maharaj jorge pancham or unke uttaradhikari, edward ashtam dwaara, australia ke governor-general yani maharajyapal ke pad par niyukt kiya gaya tha. ve is pad par niyukt hone vaale pehle australia main janme vyakti the. apane sevakaal ke dauraan ve, maharaj ke pratinidhi ke rup main, unki anupasthiti ke dauraan shaasha ke kartavyon kaa nirvaah karte the. ve ek varisht nyaayadhish the, or is niyukti se purv, ve apane vyavsaayik jivan ke dauraan, british samrajya kii seva main, anya anek mahatvapurn va varchasvpurn padon par apni seva di thi.
