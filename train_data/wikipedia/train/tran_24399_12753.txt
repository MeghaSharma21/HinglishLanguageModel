 bharat sarkaar ne san 1997 main nau saarvajanik udhyamon kii pahchaan kii jo tulanaatmak rup se phaayde kii sthiti main the or inamen antarrashtriya str par vishaal udhyamon ke rup main ubharne kii kshamta thi. us samay biechiel, bipisiel, jieiel, echpisiel, iosi, emtnael, antipisi, oengisi or cell ko navratna kaha gaya tha. kintu is samay "navratna" kaa darja praapt saarvajanik upakramon kii sankhya 23 ho gayi he. 
 in saarvajanik udhyamon ko puunjigat kharchon, sanyukt praudyogiki udhyam tathaa samrik saajheedaari sthapit karne, sanghathanaatmak punargathan karne, board str se neeche ke padon ke srijan tathaa samaapan, swadeshi tathaa antarrashtriya bazaar se puunji uthaane, sanyukt vittiya udhyam tathaa purna swaamitv vaali sahaayak companiyaan aadi sthapit karne ke bare main or zyada swytatta tathaa adhikaar die gaye the.
