 gammala baron sweden ke umeo shahar kaa ek pracheen sethu he, jo ume nadi ke upar sthit he. isaki lambaai 301 meter he.
 jab umeo main koi pul nahin tha to yahan ke lok sardiyon main barf ke raaston ke jariye or baaki mausamon main kishtiyon kii madad se ume nadi ko paar karte the.
 finnish jang ke samen jab rus ne duusari baar umeo par kabja kiya to 1809 main roosiyon ne nadi ko paar karne ke liye ek tairta hua pul banaaya tha. haalanki wo pul baadh ke aane kii vajah se barabaad ho gaya tha.
 ume nadi ke paar pul banane kaa kaam bahut hi mahinga mana jaataa tha par jab gustaaf moonth 1856 or 1858 main governor bane to unhone is kaam main dilchaspee dikhaai. unhone hidaayat di ke khoj karke dekha jaae ki kis sthaan par pul bananaa sabase uchit rahegaa, isaki tasviren banavaai jaaen or iske kharche kaa anumaan lagaaya jaae. khoj karne par yah sanket mili ki shahar ke bilkul baahar pul bananaa uchit rahegaa. iske kharche kaa anumaan 65,450 krobor lagaaya gaya or mazdooree ke saath yah 86,000 krobor hoga.
 1863 ko yah pul kholaa gaya or ek lambey arase tak yatrion ko is pul ko istemaal karne ke liye paise dene padate the.
