 rashtriya beej nigam krishi evam kisaan kalyaan mantralay ke prashasnic niyantran main bharat sarkaar ke purna swaamitv ke antargat anusuchee 'bi' kii ek pratham shrenee miniratn company he. enessi kii sthapana 1963 main kii gai. vartmaan main, yah apane panjikrut beej utpaadakon ke madhyam se lagbhag 600 kismon ke bijon kaa utpaadan kar rahi he. desh bhar main iske lagbhag 8000 panjikrut beej utpaadak hai, jo vibhinn krishi-jalwaayu paristhitiyon main beej utpaadan kaa kaary kar rahe hai. vartmaan main nigam kaa kul vyapaar 600 karod se adhik kaa he.
 puure bharat bhar main nigam ke 11 kshetreey karyaalay tathaa 83 prakshetra karyaalay faile hue hai. beej utpaadan main adhik bal tilhanon, dalhanon or sankar prajatiyon or subjiyon ke bijon ke utpaadan par diya jaataa he. enessi ne nai dilli, sikandarabad, bhopal, kolkata or pune main paanch gun niyantran prayogshalayen sthapit kii hai, joki beej kii gunvattta ko banaaye rakhane ke liye beej pareekshan kaa kaary karti he. 
 enessi gunvattta vaale bijon ke utpaadan or vitaran ke atirikt kele ityadi ke utak kheti dwaara taiyaar kiye gaye paudh ke utpaadan or apane emoyu saajhedaaron ke madhyam se falon ke paudh / biju paudh kharidkar kar aapurti karne kaa kaary bhi karti he. bijon kaa vipanan teen madhyamon ke dwaara arthath beej vikritaon ke madhyam se bijon bikri, rajya sarkaron tathaa bharat sarkaar ko bijon kii bikri tathaa mini kiton kii aapurti or apane swayan ke beej bikri kendron ke madhyam se bijon kii bikri ke dwaara kiya jaataa he. nigam ke lagbhag 2800 beej vikreta hai, jo kul bikri kaarobaar ke 65% se adhik bikri karte hai.
