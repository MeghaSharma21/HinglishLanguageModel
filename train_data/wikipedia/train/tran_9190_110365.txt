 
 vishaal cheenteekhor, jise cheentee bhaaluu bhi janaa jaataa hai, ek vishaal kitbhakshi standhari hai, jo madhya or dakshin america kaa mul nivasee hai. yah cheenteekhoron kii chaar jivit prajatiyon main se ek he or pilosa prakaar main sloth ke saath vargeekrut kiya gaya hai. anya jivit cheenteekhoron or sloth ke vipareet jo vanaspatik yaa ardh-vanaspatik hote hai, yah prajati zyaadaatar sthaliy hai. apane parivaar kaa sabase bada sadsy, vishaal cheenteekhor kii lambaai tathaa nar or maadaa kaa vazan kramshah or hota hai. yah apni lambi thuthan, junglee punch, lambey saamne ke panje, or vishisht rang ke urnagin se pahchaanane yogya hai.
