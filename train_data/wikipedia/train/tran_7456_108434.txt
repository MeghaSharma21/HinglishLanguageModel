 haidar ali dakshin bhartiya mysore rajya ke sultan or vastutah shaasha the. unka janm kaa naam haidar nike tha. wo apane sainya kaushal ke kaaran kaaphi pratishthit huye or isi kaaran mysore ke shaskaon kaa dhyaan apni ore khinchaa. iske parinamswaroop wo dalwai pad padonnat huye. iske baad wo kaaryakari raja krishnaraj wadiyaar dwitiya or mysore sarkaar par haaavee ho gayi. wo mysore rajya ke sarvadhikari ke rup main 1761 main vaastavik raja bane.
  isane apni sena ko europea tarj par hi aadhunik karne kaa prayaas kiya.
