 besangi gallexi yaa irregular gallexi aisi gallexi ko kehete jiskaa koi vyavasthit aakaar na ho, yani yah habal anukram kii sarpil, lensanuma or andakaar kii kisi shrenee main naa aaye. inkaa aakaar tedha-medhaa hota he or inamen na to saafe bhujaaen hoti hai na hi kendriya gola jo kii vyavasthit gallexiyon main nazar aate hai. vaigyanikon kaa anumaan he ke hamaare brahmand main lagbhag 25% gallexiyaan aisi besangi hoti hai. mana jaataa he ke inamen se adhiktar kabhi sarpil yaa andakaar rahi hoti hai lekin gurutvkarshan ke ast-vyast prabhaavon se inkaa aakaar bighad jaataa he. dekha gaya he ke besangi gallexiyon main gas or dhul kii bahut taadaad hoti he. hamaari apni gallexi akashganga ke ird-gird gumti kuch upgrahi gallexiyaan, jaise kii bada or chota majalanic badal besangi gallexiyaan hai.
 angrezi main "besangi gallexi" ko "irregular galaksi" kehete hai. ise arabi main "majara gair muntejma" kehete hai.
