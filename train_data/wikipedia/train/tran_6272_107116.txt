 sar garfield sobers jo gairi sobers ke rup main jane jate hai, purv cricketer he jo westeindies cricket team kii taraf se 1954 se 1974 tak khelte the. sobers ko vyapak rup se sabase mahaan harfanmaulaa main se ek mana jaataa he.
 brijtoun, barbados main paidaa hue sobers ne 16 saal kii umr main pratham shrenee cricket main shuruyaat kii or uske agle varsh westeindies ke liye pehla match khelaa. unhone shuruyaat gendbaaj ke rup main kii thi. baad main unhen ballebaaji kram main upar utaara jane laga. shuruaati asafalataaon ke baad 1958 pakistan ke khilaaf unhone apane pehle shatak ke rup main 365 naabad banaae jo ki kisi pari main sarvocch vyaktigat score 36 saal tak rahaa. sobers ko 1965 main west indies kaa captain banaaya gaya, yah bhoomika vah 1972 tak nibhaate rahe.
 kul milakar, sobers ne westeindies ke liye 93 test khele jisamen 57.78 kii ausat se 8032 ran banaae or 34.03 kii ausat se 235 wicket liye. apane 383 pratham shrenee maichon main unhone 28,000 ran banaae or 1000 wicket liye. sobers ko 1975 main cricket ke liye apni sevaaon ke liye maharani elizabeth dwitiya dwaara nite kii oopaadhi di gai. vah 1980 main shaadi ke madhyam se dohare barbados-australian naagarik ban gaye. 1998 main sansad ke ek adhinium dwaara, sobers ko barbados ke das rashtriya nayakon main se ek ke rup main namit kiya gaya.
