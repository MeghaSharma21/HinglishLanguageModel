 tovi bhashaa rus ke tuwa ganraajya main boli jane vaali ek turki bhashaa he. iske maatrubhaashi tuwa ke alawa china, mongolia or rus ke anya bhaagon main bhi paae jate hai. ise maatrubhaasha bolne waalon kii sankhya lagbhag 2.5 lakh anumaanit kii gai he. haalanki yah ek turki bhashaa he, isamen mongol, tibeti or roosi bhashaaon kaa kaafii prabhaav bhi dikhataa he. alag tovi samudaayon main isaki alag-alag upbhashaayen boli jaati hai.
