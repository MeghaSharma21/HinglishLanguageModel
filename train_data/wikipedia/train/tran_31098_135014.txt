  rasulabad bharat ke uttarapradesh rajya ke kanpur dehaat jile main nagar panchayat he jiskaa gathan varsh 2005 main hua tha. british shashan main yah tehsil kaa mukhyalay tha lekin 1894 main is tehsil ko vibhajit kar derapur or bilhor tahseelon main sammilit kar diya gaya tha. varsh 1988 main is tehsil punarsrijan hua.
 yah nagar kanpur se pashchim disha main 65 kilometre duuri par kanpur -bela road par sthit he. is nagar kii jhinjhak railway stesan kii duuri 14 kilometre he jo dakshin disha main sthit he. is nagar se uttar disha main sthit bilhor nagar 30 kilometre dur he.
 yah nagar jile ke mukhyalay akbarpur se road dwaara judaa hua he. jahaan rura hokar ja sakate hai. kanpur nagar road dwaara judaa hua he. uttar disha main yah nagar road dwaara bilhor se judaa hua he. iskaa nikatatam railway stesan jhinjhak he jahaan fast or super fast railgadiyaan thaharti hai. jhinjhak se pashchim kii disha main itawa, agra, dilli meerut aadi nagaron ko ja sakate hai tathaa purv disha main kanpur, lakhanu, patna, haavda aadi nagaron se judaa hua he.
