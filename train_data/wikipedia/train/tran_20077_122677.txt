 ba prashanth mahaasaagar ke fiji desh kaa ek nagar he. yah viti levu dweep ke pashchimottaree bhaag main, ba nadi ke kinare, lautoka se 37 kilometre or nandi se 62 kimi dur sthit he. prashasnic rup se yah fiji ke pashchimi vibhaag ke ba praant kaa bhaag he. ba main bhartiya mul ke logon kii bahusankhya he or yah ek krishi-pradhan kshetra main basaa hua he.
