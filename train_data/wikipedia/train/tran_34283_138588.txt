 khandesh maharashtra ke dakshini pathaar ke uttari-pashchimi kone par sthit prasiddh aitihaasik kshetra, jo mumbai se lagbhag 300kimi uttarpashchim he. 18 veen shatabdi main yah bhaag maratha shashan main tha tathaa yahan anek mahatvapurn aitihaasik ghatnaayen hui thim. uske purv yah ahamed nagar ke sultaanon ke adhikaar main tha. 1601 i. main akbar ne ise apane samrajya main sammilit kiya. puure kshetra kaa kshetrafal 9,918 vargamil he. 1906 i. main is kshetra ko do jilon main vibhajit kar diya gaya :
 iskaa kshetrafal 5,320 vargamil he. iske uttarpurv main satapura parvat, uttarpashchim main narmada nadi tathaa pashchim main pashchimi ghat kaa uttari kinaaraa he. isamen tapti or panjhara nadiyaan bahati hai. pashchimi bhaag main jangal hai, jinamein keemtee lakadiyaan milti hai. is jile kii mukhya upaj jowar, bajra, kapaas, gehun or tilhan hai. is jile kaa kendriya nagar dhulia he, jo vyapaar or shiksha kaa centre he. iske atirikt shirpur, shahada or nandurbaar aadi prasiddh sthaan hai. in sabhi nagaron main kapaas se binaula nikaalane ke kaarkaane hai.
 maharashtra ke uttarpurv main dakshini pathaar par sthit he, jiskaa kshetrafal 4,598 vargamil he. iskaa kendriya nagar jalgaam he. iske uttar main satapura parvat or dakshin main ajanta kii pahadiyaan he. isamen tapti or girnaa nadiyaan bahati he. chalis gaanv ke uttar-uttar-pashchim main aath mile kii duuri par jamada sinchai pranali prarambh hoti he. yahan par kapaas, makka, jowar, gehun or aam utpann hote he. satapura parvat kii dhaalon par parvatiy van main imarti lakadiyaan milti he jinhen fijpur or yaval ke bazaaron main bechaa jaataa he. yahan par kapaas se binola nikaalane ke kaarkaane he. kutir udyog main vastra banaae jate hai. amalner, chalisagaanv, jalgaam or bhusaval main kapaas kaa vyapaar hota he.
