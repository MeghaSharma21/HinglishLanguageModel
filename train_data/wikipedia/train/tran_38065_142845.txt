 malwa express ek dainik superfast express train seva he jo, madhya pradesh kaa sabase bada shahar or vyavsaayik centre indore jankshan biji railway stesan se jammu kashmir, bharat main jammu tavi tak jodta he.
 yah train mul rup se indore or nai dilli ke bich shuru kii gai thi or tab se jammu tavi tak badha di gai he. yah ek kootnithik sanket ke rup main pakistan pahunchane vaali pehali bhartiya rail thi. 22 oktober 1985 ko indore se pakistan main lahore tak, indore-lahore vishesh seva se chal rahi thi, lekin seva vivaadon kii vajah se 55 dinon ke baad samaapt kar diya gaya. is avadhi ke dauraan yah har ek saptaah main ek baar shuukravaar ko donon disha main prasthaan karti rahi. yah bhopal express, revanchal express or ahilyangari express ke baad, ek iesao pramaan patr praapt karne ke liye bharat or madhya pradesh main paanchavi train thi.
