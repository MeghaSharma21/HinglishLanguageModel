 varsh 2013 main dilli vidhansabha ke chunaav ke liye 4 disambar ko matdaan hua. jiske parinaam 8 disambar 2013 ko ghoshit kiye gaye. ye un pratham paanch chunaavon main se the jinamein bharat nirvachan ayog dwaara "uparokt main se koi nahin kaa vikalp" uplabdh karavaaya gaya jiske tahat chunaav main utre sabhi ummidavaaron ko asweekaar karne kaa adhikaar bhi pehali baar matadaataaon ko mil gaya. chunaav ke samay yahan kii kul jansankhya 16,787,941 thi jisamen se 1,15,07113 matadaata the.
 bhaajapa or shiad chunaav purv gathbandhan ke saath chunaav maidan main hai; shiad chaar vidhansabha siton se chunaav maidan main he jabaki bhaajapa ne anya sabhi jagahon par apane sadsy chunaav maidan main utaare hai. chaar main se do siton par shiad ummidavaar bhaajapa ke chunaav chinh camel kaa prayog karenge.
