 vidhansabha chunaav bharat ke rajyon main karaae jane vaale rajya-stareey chunaav hai. in chunaavon main har rajya kii janata apane rajya-str ke shashan ke liye apane pratinidhiyon kaa chunaav karti he jinhen vidhayak kaha jaataa he. aam chunaavon se alag in chunaavon main chune hue vidhayak rajya-str par sarkaar kaa gathan karte hai. in chunaavon main bhaag lene vaale rajnatik dal vah bhi ho sakate hai jo aam chunaavon main bhaag lete hai or iske atirikt vibhinn rajyon ke apni-apni kshetreey dal bhi hai jo rajya-vishesh main lokapriy hote hai jaise tamil nadu main anna dravid munnetra kadgam or pashchim bengal main trinmul congress. yah chunaav prati paanch varsh main ek baar karaae jate hai. yadi apana kaarykaal puraa karne se purv koi sarkaar vidhansabha main bahumat kho detii he to yah chunaav paanch varsh se pehle bhi karaae ja sakate hai.
 in chunaavon main matdaan karne ke liye jo maapdand hai vah vahiin hai jo loksabhaa chunaavon main matdaan karne ke liye hai jaise matadaata kaa bhartiya naagarik hona or uski aayu 18 varsh yaa upar hona.
