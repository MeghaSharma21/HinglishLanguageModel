 hansee yaa hasya maanav bhaawanaaon kii abhivyakti kii ek prakriya he. kisi bhi manushya ko hansee aa sakati he. parantu hansee ko kuch log keval muskuraahat tak simit rakhte hai, kuch log muskuraane ke saath-saath munh bhi kholte hai or kam se kam unke aage daant bhi dikhaai padate hai. hansee maamuli or aupchaarik ho sakati he jisamen chaharaa zyaada hilta nahin to kabhi log sar or sharir hilaa-hilaakar hanste hai. bahut zyaada hansane or der tak hansane ko kahkaha kehete hai.
 aam dharna ke vipareet, zyaadaatar hansee chutkulon yaa aise anya hasyapad kaarnon se nahin aati.
