 swarochish manu dwitiya manu the. pratham manu kaa naam swymbhuv manu tha. swarochish manu agni dev tathaa prajapati daksh kii putree swaha se utpann hue tathaa manu ke pad par asin hue.
 niyamtah har manu ke kaarykaal main bhagwaan kaa rakshak ke rup main anshavatar, indra tathaa alag saptarshi hote hai. swarochish manu main paravat tathaa tushigan naam ke anshavatar rahe. indra ke rup main mahabali pipaschit asin rahe. saptarshimandal ke rishigan praan, brihaspati, dattatreya, atri, chyavan, vayuprokt tathaa mahavrath rahe. in manu ke putr havirdhra, sukruti, jyoti, aap, murti, prathit, nabhasy, nabh tathaa urj hue.
