 mcdonalds super smash newjiland main ek gharelu twanti-20 cricket pratiyogita he, toornament ke sheersh teen play of ke liye kwalifai karne tiimon ke saath, ek dabl round robin ke hote hai. 
 pratiyogita 2006 ke baad se har saal yojit kiya gaya he, or mul rup se 2009 tak rajya twanti-20 namit kiya gaya tha, uske baad 2009-2014 HRV twanti-20 or 2014-2016 jorji pai super lut namit kiya he. 2008-2014 se pratiyogita ke vijeta baad main usee varsh main champions lig twanti-20 toornament ke liye pravesh praapt kii.
