 khagolashastra main, dikpaat bhumadhyi nirdeshaank pranali ke do nirdeshaankon main se ek hota he. doosraa nirdeshaank daayan arohan yaa ghanta kone hota he. jhukaav kii tulana akshansh se kii ja sakati he. iskaa maapan degree uttar yaa dakshin main kiya jaataa he. khagoleey bumadhya rekha ke uttar ke bindu ghanaatmak jhukaav va uske dakshin vaale bindu rinatmak jhukaav par hote hai.
