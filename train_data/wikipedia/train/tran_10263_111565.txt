 bhartiya khanan beuro, bharat kaa bahu-anushaasit sarkari sanghathan he. isaki sthapana 1948 main kii gai thi. beuro khanan vikaas mantralay, bharat sarkaar ke antargatt kaary karata he. iskaa mukhya uddeshy koyala, petroleum evam prakritik gas, anvik khanij evam viral khanijon ke alaava anya khaanon main sangrakshan, khanij sansadhano ke vaigyanik vikaas karnaa he. beuro kii sthapana 1 march 1948 ko hui thi. beuro kaa mukhyalay indira bhawan, civil lines,nagpur, maharashtra-440102 main sthit he.
