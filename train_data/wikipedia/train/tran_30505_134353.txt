 delta america dwaara bahumukhi upbhojit laanch pranali parivaar he. jisane1960 ke baad se sanyukt rajya america main antariksh prakshepan kii kshamta pradaan kii he. abtak 95% safalataa dar ke saath 300 se adhik delta launch rocket kiye gaye hai. do delta laanch system - delta 2 or delta 4 - abhi bhi upyog main hai. haalanki delta 2 jald hi sevanivrutt ho jayega. vartmaan main delta rocket united laanch allins dwaara nirmit or prakshepit kiye jate hai.
