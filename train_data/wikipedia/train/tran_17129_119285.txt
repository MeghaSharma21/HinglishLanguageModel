 shizuoka japan kii shizuoka praant kii rajdhani hai, or aabaadi or kshetra ke anusaar praant kaa doosraa sabase bada shahar hai. yah pragaitihaasik kaal se aabaad hai. shahar kaa naam do kanji lipi shabdo, 静 shizu, jiskaa arth he "sthir" yaa "shant"; or 岡 oka, jiskaa arth he "pahari" se milakar bana hai. 2003 main, shijuoka ko shimiju shahar ke saath vilay kar diya gaya, is prakaar yah japan main bhoomi kshetra ke anusaar sabase bada shahar ban gaya. 2005 main, yah japan ke "manonith shaharon" main se ek ban gaya.
 jo kshetra ab shizuoka kaa shahar he vah pragaitihaasik kaal se basaa hua he. muromchi kaal ke dauraan, sunpu, imagawa kabeeley kii rajdhani thi. imagawa, okehjama ke yuddh main parajeet ho gaye, or sanpu baad main takada shinjan ke shashan main aa gaya, or uske baad tokugava iyasu ke.
 1869 main, tokugava shogunet ke patan ke baad, tokugawa aijeto kii adhyakshata vaali purv shogunal line ko sunpu bheja gaya or alpakaalik sunpu domen ko kaa kaary saunpa gaya. usee varsh, sunpu kaa naam "shizuoka" kar diya gaya.
 aadhunik shahar 1 april 1889 ko sthapit kiya gaya tha. us samay, jansankhya 37,681 thi, or shizuoka japan main sthapit kiye gaye pehle 31 shaharon main se ek thi.
 shizuoka praant ke centre main sthit yah shahar, tokyo or nagoya ke bich lagbhag aadhe raste main tekido corndor main sthit hai. iske dakshin main suruga khadi or uttar main minami alps sthit he. shizuoka, april 2003 main shimiju city ke saath vilay karne ke baad japan ke nagar palikaon main sabase bada kshetra rahaa hai, jab tak kii farvari 2005 main, jab gifu praant main takayama or aasapaas kii nau nagar palikaon ke saath main vilay kar diya gaya. kul kshetra 1,411.90 kimi2 hai. bhugolic kshetra ke sandarbh main tokyoma, hamamatsu, nikako or kitmi ke baad shizuoka japan kaa paanchvaan sabase bada shahar hai. pankhe ke aakaar ke shizuoka maidan or miho prayadweep, samay ke saath tej bahane vaali abe nadi ke saath bahakar ai ret or mitti ke kaaran bani hai. ye kshetra aaj shahar kii neenv banaate hai.
 dakshin-madhya prashanth tat par shizuoka main ek ardra up-ushnkaribandheey jalwaayu hai, joki greeshm ritu main garm or ardra or sardiyon main shaayad hi kabhi barf girti hai..
 april 2015 ke ant main, shahar kii anumaanit aabaadi 715,752 thi, or jansankhya ghanatv 507 prati vyakti prati kimi2 tha.
