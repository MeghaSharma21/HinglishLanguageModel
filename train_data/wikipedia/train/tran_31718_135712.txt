 
 2017 inter-prantiy championships ireland kii inter-prantiy championships ek pratham shrenee cricket pratiyogita kaa pehla sanskaran he. vartmaan main yah 30 may se 7 sitambar 2017 tak yojit kiya ja rahaa he. oktober 2016 main antarrashtriya cricket parishad kii baithak ke parinaam ke baad yah pratham shrenee kii sthiti ke saath khelaa jane vaala pratiyogita kaa pehla sanskaran he. leinster litening ne toornament jeeta, unke paanchaven charan main, or is prakriya main gharelu safai-sweep puri kii.
