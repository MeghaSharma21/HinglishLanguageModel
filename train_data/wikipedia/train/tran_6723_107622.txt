 vikram sarabhai antariksh centre isro kaa sabase bada evam sarvaadhik mahatvapurn centre he. yah tiruvanantpuram main sthit he. yahan par rocket, prakshepan yaan evam kritrim upgrahon kaa nirmaan evam unase sambandhit takneekee kaa vikaas kiya jaataa he. centre kii shuruyaat thamba bumadhyarekhiy rocket prakshepan centre ke taur par 1962 main hui thi. centre kaa punah namakaran bhartiya antariksh kaarykram ke janak dow vikram sarabhai ke sammaan main kiya gaya.
