 dalhedi bharat ke uttarapradesh rajya ke saharanpur zile kaa ek gaanv he. 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar dalhedi kii jansankhya 4840 he. jaankaree ke rup main dalhedi gaanv kaa pincode 247452 he.
 dalhedi gaanv baba mohan singh pundir ji ke dwaara lagbhag 500 saal pehle basaaya gaya tha. shri mohan singh ji shri mahesh singh pundir ke chote bhai the. baba mohan singh pundir ji ne sabhi aavashyak suvidhao ke saath gaanv kii sthapana kii thi. unhone alag alag jaati ke logo ko har suvidha ke saath gaanv main basaaya. gaanv chaar colony bhoja, fusia, khandu, or chuhad main vibhajit he. jo chaar bhai or baba mohan singh pundir ji ke putr the.
 saharanpur se gaanv 33 kimi duri par he. mujffarnagar se 44 kimi or shamali se 48 kimi duri par he. haryana ke najdiki jile karnal or yamunanagar kramas 77 kimi or 65 kimi duri par he. dehradun or haridwar 102 kimi or 80 kimi par he. rajdhani dilli se gaanv 156 kimi duri par padataa he. anya mahanagar chandigarh 165 kimi, mumbai 1586 kimi, kolkata 1615 kimi, chennai 2317 kimi duri par he. gaanv do rashtiya rajmarg jinke bich kii duri 2.5 kimi jahaan par gaanv basaa hua he. isliye yatayaat kii suvidha bhi acchhee he. gaanv se nanota railway stesan 7 kimi par rampur manihar railway stesan 10 kimi par or devvrund railway stesan 18 kimi duri par he. hawaii yatayaat sarasava 49 kimi or dehradun se he.
 gaanv main lagbhag sabhi jaati ke log nivas karte hai. gaanv main pundir rajput bahutay main he jo jansankhya kaa lagbhag 50% he jinka sabhi jaati ke logo se prem bhav he. gaanv ke kuch parivaar ausat se bahut aamir he, lekin zyaadaatar parivaar madhyam vargeey he.
 gaanv dalhedi 763 parivaar kaa bada gaanv he. gaanv main hindu or muslim donon dharm ke log rahete hai. dalhedi gaanv kii jansankhya 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar 4850 he, jinamein se 2,567 purush or 2,284 mahilaayein hai.
 0-6 saal kii umr ke bacchon kii jansankhya 683 jo gaanv kii kul aabaadi kaa 14.08% he. gaanv kii ausat ling anupaat 890 he, jo uttar pradesh rajya ke ausat 912 se kam he, janaganana ke anusaar dalhedi gaanv kaa bal ling anupaat 783, uttar pradesh ke ausat 902 se kam he. anusuchit jati gaanv main kul aabaadi kaa 23.65% he.
 hindi bhashaa mukhya bhashaa he. sanskrit, punjabi or urdu bhi alpatam main boli jaati he.
 gaanv ke mukhya tyohaar holi, deepawali, dashahra, raksha bandhan, bhaiya dooj he. kuch tyohaar jaise hoi ashtami, shiv raatri, ravidaas jayanti, aadi anya tyohaar hai.
 mukhya rup se krishi, shikshan, khud ke vyavsaay par nirbhar he. log sarkari, ardh sarkari or gaiyr sarkari kshetra main bhi bahutayat main he.lekin mukhya vyavsaay krishi he.
 dalhedi gaanv kii ucch sakshartaa dar he. jo 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar utter pradesh kii sakshartaa dar 67.68% kii tulana main 73.39% thi. dalhedi main purush sakshartaa 81.63% thi, jabaki mahila sakshartaa dar 64.26 thi.
 gaanv main ek inter kalege ek junior hai skul or do gurukul he.
 shiv mandir, kaali mata mandir, bhumia kheda, goga ji mahadi, baba bisambar nath mandir, gora mandir, guru ravidaas mandir, gurukul aadi.
