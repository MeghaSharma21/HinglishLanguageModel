 rajesh kumaar - janavaadee naatakkaar. janm -11 janvari 1958 patna, bihar.
 rajesh kumaar nukkad naatak andolan ke shuruaati daur 1976 se sakriy he. ab tak darjanon naatak evam nukkad natya sangrah prakashit ho chuke hai. aara kii natya sanstha yuvaniti, bhagalpur kii disha or shahjahanpur kii natya sanstha abhivyakti ke sansthaapak sadsy. peshey se injeniyar he. in dino lakhanu main karyrat.
