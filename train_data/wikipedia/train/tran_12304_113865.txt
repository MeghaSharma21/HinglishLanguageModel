 karel chapek  check lekhak evam patrkaar the. unka check sahitya main gauravpurn sthaan he. unki sabhi pramukh kritiyon ke asankhya videshi bhashaaon main anuwaad kiye gaye the. chapek kii lokapriyata is baat kaa pramaan he ki unki lekhkeey pratibha udbhut, anokhi tathaa atyant gambhir he. 
 chapek kaa maanavtapurna drushtikon sabhi rachanaaon main spashtataa se vidyaman he. ve naatak, upanyaas, kahaniyaan, niabandh aadi likhte the. chapek ne bahut bhraman kiya. unke 'england se patr', 'holland se patr', aadi sangrah ati priy hai. 'maan' naamak naatak anek bhashaaon main anudit ho chukaa he. bhartiya bhashaaon main banglaa or marathi main bhi yah naatak anuwaad ke rup main miltaa he. 'maan' naatak main lekhak naji satta ke viruddh ladaai karne ko protsaahan detaa he. unki balopayogi pustaken unke bhai yoseph chapek ke chitron se susajjit hai. anya mahatvapurn kritiyaan : 'ise kaise karata hoon', 'krakatit', 'r.u.r.', 'ek jail kii kahaniyaan', 'duusari jail kii kahaniyaan', 'mali kaa varsh', 'keetanu jivan' .
