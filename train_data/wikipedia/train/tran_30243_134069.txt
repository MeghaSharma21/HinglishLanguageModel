 acharya anandavardhan, kavyashastra main dhwani sampradaay ke pravartak ke rup main prasiddh hai. kavyashastra ke aitihaasik patal par acharya rudrat ke baad acharya anandavardhan aate hai or inkaa granth ‘dhvanyalok’ kavy shaastra ke itihaas main mile kaa pathar he. 
 acharya anandavardhan kashmir ke nivasee the or ye tatkaaleen kashmir naresh avantivarman ke samakaleen the. is sambandh main mahakavi kalhan ‘rajtarangini’ main likhte hai:
 kashmir naresh avantivarman kaa rajyakaal 855 se 884 i. he. atev acharya anandavardhan kaa kaal bhi nauveen shatabdi maananaa chahie. inhone paanch granthon kii rachana kii he- vishambanlila, arjunacharit, devishtak, tatwalok or dhvanyalok.
