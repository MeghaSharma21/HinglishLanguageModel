 dow radhelaal hardev richaaria chhattisgarh ke prasiddh krishi vaigyanik the. ve kendriya chaaval anusandhan santhaan, katak ke nideshak bhi rahe.
 ve bharat main dhan par agrani visheshgyon main se ek the. unhone apane karier ke dauraan ek chaaval kii 19,000 prajatiyaan ekatra kii thi. unka anumaan tha ki bharat main chaaval kii 200,000 prajatiyaan hongi. unhone jivan bhar chote kisaanon ko bade vyapaarik companiyon se bachaane evam unki viraasat ko bachaane ke liye kaary kiya.
 dow richaaria ne chhattisgarh main varsh 1971 se 1978 tak dhan par vyapak anusandhan kiya tha. is avadhi main dow richaaria madhyapradesh sarkaar ke krishi salaahkaar ke rup main evam varsh 1975 se 1978 tak madhyapradesh dhan anusandhan centre, raipur ke nideshak bhi rahe. is dauraan unhone chhattisgarh or madhyapradesh ke dhan utpaadak kisaanon se dhan ke 18 hajaar se adhik janandravyon kaa sankalan kiya. da. richaaria dwaara sankalit dhan ke janandravyon kaa upyog kar indira gandhi krishi vishvavidhyalay raipur ne dhan kii ab tak lagbhag 75 kismon kaa vikaas kar liya he.
