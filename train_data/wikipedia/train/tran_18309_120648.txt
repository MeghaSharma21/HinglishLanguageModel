 miguel anjal aasturius guatemala ke kavi or upanyaaskaar the. 1967 i0 main sahitya main nobel puraskaar vijeta.
 
 miguel anjal aasturius kaa janm guatemala main 19 oktober, 1899 i0 ko hua tha. unka ghar samrudh tha. pita magistrate the or mata skool main adhyapika thi. us samay taanaashaahee ke viruddh andolan kaa aarambh ho chukaa tha or 1902 tathaa 1903 isvi main chaatro ne bhi virodh main bhaag liya tha. ek magistrate hone ke naate miguel aasturius ke pita ko un par kathor kaarywahi kerani thi, parantu unhone aisa nahin kiya. atah unhen andolan kaa samarthak maanakar naukari se hataa diya gaya. unki maa kii naukari bhi samaapt kar di gayi. iske baad unka parivaar guatemala se salaama gaanv main dada ke paas chalaa gaya, jahaan unki paitruk jameen thi. 1970 isvi main shanti sthapit hone par unka parivaar punah guatemala lautaa. parantu, raajnneetik shanti ke baad bhi prakritik shanti na rahi or 25 disambar 1917 isvi ko guatemala main aaye bhayankar bukamp main nagar hi nashtpraay ho gaya. teen-chaar varshon tak nirmaankarya hi chalata rahaa or 1920 main sarkaar kaa takhta palat gaya. un dinon aasturious kanoon kii padhai kar rahe the or court ke secretary bhi the. gaddi se utaare gayi taanashaah cowarera se aasturious kii jail main praayah pratidin mulaakaat hoti thi or is tarah se taanaashaahee sena evam uske anya ang tathaa andolanon se sambandhit vibhinn baton kaa bhalee prakaar ve adhyayan karte rahe or is adhyayan ko apane lekhan kaa mukhya vishay banaaya. phir pita ne unhen desh kii avyavasthit sthiti ke kaaran landan bhej diya. unhone kanoon or lok sahitya kaa adhyayan kiya tha. 1920 ke dashak main ve paris main bhi rahe the. vahiin latin america samskruti or dharm ke visheshgy renaan naamak ek praadhyaapak se unki mulaakaat hui thi. isi dashak main ve apane desh kii kootnithik seva main aa gayi the. sarkaar badalane par 1966 se 1970 tak apane desh ke rajdoot ke rup main ve paris main bhi rahe the.
 aasturius ne 1925 i0 main sahitya-rachana kavy-lekhan se aarambh kii or baad main upanyaas ke kshetra main aa gayi. unka upanyaas da president 1946 i0 main prakashit hua. is pustak main unhone apane purv anubhavon dwaara latin america ke taanaashaahon kii bhartsana kii he. vastutah latin america deshon ke taanaashaahon ne anek baar loktantr kaa galaa ghontne kaa ytn kiya tha. aasturius sadaa iske virodh main likhte rahe.
