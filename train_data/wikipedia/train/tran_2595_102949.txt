 sajaateey shabd, jinhen angreji main kagnet shabd kaha jaataa he, bhinn bhashaaon ke ve shabd hote hai jo alag bhashaaon se hone ke baavjood ek hi pracheen jad rakhte hai. is kaaran se in shabdon ko jinka rup or arth aksar miltaa-julata hota he sajaateey shabd kehete hai, haalaanki ye aavashyak nahin he ki unka arth bilkul saamaan ho. udahaaran ke rup main matra, maadar, maatar or madar kaa rup saamaan sa he or sab kaa arth maan he kyonki yah sari bhashaayen ek hi aadim-hind-europea bhashaa se upji santaanen hai.
 
 
 antim udhaaharan main dekha ja saktaa he ke kaise is shabd kaa arth sanskrit, avasti or unani main to "maalik" kaa hi rahaa lekin latini or angreji main zaraa sa badalakar "shaktishaalee" ban gaya.
