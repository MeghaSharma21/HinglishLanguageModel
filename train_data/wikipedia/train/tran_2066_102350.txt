 mukhya imaarat
 seth em aar jaipuria vidhyalay, lakhanu sthit ek prasiddh vidhyalay he. yah saadharantah 'jaipuria skool lakhanu' ke naam se janaa jaataa he. is vidhyalay kaa shiksha madhyam angreji he. is vidhyalay ko 2007 main bharat main "sabase sammanit madhyamik vidyalayon" main 9 waan sthaan diya gaya tha.
 seth em aar jaipuria skool kii ek nai shakha sitapur road, lakhanu par nai shakha 2014 se shuru kar rahaa he. jaipuria skool pehle gomti nagar main hi tha. group ke upaadhyaksh shrivats jayupariya ne kaha ki agle teen -chaar varshon main hamaare 50 skool kholane kii yojana he jiske liye ham uttar va madhya bharat ko kendrit kar rahe hai. yahan par tear 2 or tear 3 shaharon main vikaas kii kaafii sambhaavnaaen he. asal main in ilaakon main ucch gunvattta vaale skulon kii sankhya kaafii kam he. agle saal se agra, faizabad, haridwar or anya sthanon par jaipuria group ke skool khole jaaenge.
