 si di deshmukh bhartiya rigerv bank ke pehle bhartiya governor the, jinhen 1943 main british raaj dwaara niyukt kiya gaya. british raaj ne unhen sar kii oopaadhi di thi. iske pashchaat unhone kendriya mantrimandal main bharat ke tisare vitt mantri ke rup main bhi seva kii.
 si di deshmukh kaa janm maharashtra ke raigarh zile ke naata main ek chandrasenia kysth prabhu parivaar main pratishthit vakil dwarikanath ganesh deshmukh ke yahan 14 janvari 1896 i. ko hua. inaki mata kaa naam bhagirathibai tha, jo ek dharmik mahila thi. inkaa bachapan raigarh zile ke roha main hi beetaa. inkaa parivaar atyant samrudh tha or bhoomi jot prishthbhumi kii saarvajanik seva kii ek parampara se judaa hua tha. deshmukh kaa ek shandaar academik karier rahaa he. unhone bombai vishvavidhyalay se 1912 main metric pariksha pratham shrenee main uteern kii or sanskrit main pehali baar chaatravrutti praapt kiya. iske pashchaat 1917 main unhone yishu college, cambridge, england se vanaspati vigyaan, rasayan vigyaan or bhuvigyaan ke saath snaatak kii pariksha uteern kii, jahaan unhen vanaspati vigyaan main frank smart puraskaar praapt hua. antat: ve 1918 main bhartiya civil seva pariksha main awwal rahe.
 landan se ai.si.es. kii pariksha uteern karne ke baad ve bharat main vibhinn padon par kaam karte rahe.unhone bharat kii british raaj ke pratinidhi ke rup main varshon tak 'vishwa bank' or 'anterrashtriya mudra kosh' ke governor ke rup main bhi kaam kiya. 
 1949 i. main ai.si.es. se sevanivrutt hone ke baad ve bhartiya sansad ke sadsy or 'yojana ayog' ke sadsy banaaye gaye. 1950 main unhen desh ke vitt mantri ke rup main nehru ji ke mantrimandal main sammilit kiya gaya. maharashtra pradesh ke nirmaan ke andolan ke samay deshmukh ne vitt mantri kaa pad to tyaag diya, kintu unako anya mahatvapurn pad milte gaye. ve vishvavidhyalay anudaan ayog ke adhyaksh bane or dilli vishvavidhyalay ke upkulapati banaaye gaye. deshmukh ko 1975 main raashtrapati ne padam vibhushan ke alankaran se sammanit kiya. unhone kai pustaken likhin.
 unka vivaah ek angrej mahila se hua tha, jisase unhen ek beti thi, kintu 1946 main daampaty jivan main matbhed ke drushtigat sulah kaa prayaas vifal hua or unki pehali patni england laut gayi. baad main 1952 main 57 varsh kii umr main unhone 43 varsh kii ek ni: santan vidhwaa, swatantraaa senaanee,congress party or loksabhaa kii sadsy va saamaajik kaaryakarta durgabai deshmukh se shaadi kar lee.
 si di deshmukh kii mrityu 2 oktober 1982 ko huyi.
