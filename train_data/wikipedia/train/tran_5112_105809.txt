 harry potter or haaf-blad prince je. ke. rolling dwaara angrezi main rachit harry potter kram kii chhathi kadi he. is upanyaas main harry potter or anya paatr hoguarts main waapas aate hai or naye romaanchak karnaamon kaa saamana karte hai. isapar isi naam kii ek film bhi bani he. is upanyaas kaa hindi sanskaran ab uplabdh he.
 upanyas kii is chhathi kadi main harry ko dambldor kii yaadon ke dwaara voldemort kii pehle kii zindagi ke bare main kaafii kuch pataa chalata he. usee saal ek nayaa jaadoomantri banataa he : rufas screemager. udhar harry ko jaadui kaadhe kii class main ek ajeeb or benaam kitaab se bahut madad milti he, jiskaa maalik khud ko haaf-blad prince kahata tha. harry or dambldor aisa maanakar chalate hai ki voldemort ne khud ko amar karne ke liye apni aatma ko chah yaa saat tukdon main phada tha or harek ko ek horcrax main daal diya tha. harry or dambldor ek horcrax ko nasht karne ek gupha main jate hai lekin unke niraashaa hi haath lagati he, kyonki horcrax nakli niklaa. iske baad harry kaa sabase kam pasandida adhyaaapak profesar snep awada kedavra abhishaap se dambldor ko maar daalata he.
