 arijona sanyukt rajya america ke dakshinpashchimi hisse main sthit rajya he. iskaa sabase bada shahar or rajdhani finaks he. doosraa sabase bada rajya takson he or uske baad finaks ke mahanagar kshetra sthit shahar mesa, glandele, chandler or scottsdel he.
 arijona sanyukt rajyon main shaamil hone vaala 48va rajya tha jise kii isamen 14 farbari 1912 ko samilit kiya gaya. isi din is rajya kii desh main maane jane vaali bhoomi kii varshageth bhi thi. arijona apane registani mausam, bhayankar garmi or halki thand ke liye janaa jaataa he. par rajya ke ucch ilaakon main bahut pahaad va jangal he, isi kaaran vahaan kaa mausam anya rajya ke mukaable thanda rahata he. july 1 2006 ke anusaar arijona desh main aabaadi ke hisaab se sabase teji se badhata rajya he. isse pehle yah darja nevada ke paas tha.
 rajya kaa ek-chauthai ilaaka mul america adivasee ke liye sangrakshit he. rajya kii aadhikarik bhashaa angreji he jise bolne vaale jansankhya ke 74 pratishat he. spani 19.5 pratishat dwaara boli jaati he. 2015 main rajya kii aabaadi 68,28,065 anumaanit kii gai he. jis hisaab se uskaa america rajyon main 14waan sthaan he.
