 marwar rajasthan praant ke pashchim main thar ke marusthal main aanshik rup se sthit he. marwar sanskrit ke maruwat shabd se bana he jiskaa arth he maut kaa bhubhag. pracheen kaal main is bhubhag ko marudesh bhi kehete the. iske antargat rajasthan praant ke bakhmer, jodhpur, paali, jalor or nagaur jile aate hai.
 is lekh kii saamagree sammilit hui he britnica vishwakosh ekadashwein sanskaran se, ek prakaashan, jo ki jan saamaanya hetu pradarshit he..
