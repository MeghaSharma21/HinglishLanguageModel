 narsingh mandir bhagwaan vishnu ke narsingh avatar kaa uttar bharat kaa pramukh mandir he. itawa main sthit is mandir ko usasaki sampada ke anusaar uttar kaa padmanabh mandir bhi kaha jaataa he. inamen beshkeemtee aabhushan or kai jilon main faili achal sampada he. vartmaan samay main yah mandir mahant parampara or trust ke antargat prabandhit kiya jaataa he.
