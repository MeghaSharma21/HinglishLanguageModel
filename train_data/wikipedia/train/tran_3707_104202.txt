 saraswati hindi sahitya kii prasiddh rupagunasmpann pratinidhi patrika thi. is patrika kaa prakaashan elahabaad se san 1900 i0 ke janvari maas main prarambh hua tha. 32 prishth kii crown aakaar kii is patrika kaa mulya 4 aana maatr tha. 1903 i0 main mahavir prasad dvivedi iske sampadak hue or 1920 i0 tak rahe. iskaa prakaashan pehle jhansi or phir kanpur se hone laga tha.
 mahavir prasad dvivedi ke baad padumalal punnalaal bakshi, devi dutt shukla, shrinath singh, padumalal punnalaal bakshi, devilal chaturvedi or shrinarayan chaturvedi sampadak hue. 1905 i0 main kaashi nagari prachaarini sabha kaa naam mukhaprishth se hat gaya.
 1903 main mahavir prasad dvivedi ne iskaa kaarybhaar sambhala. ek ore bhashaa ke str par or duusari ore prerak banakar maargdarshan kaa kaary sambhalakar dvivedi ji ne saahitiyik or rashtriya chetana ko swar pradaan kiya. dvivedi ji ne bhashaa kii samruddhi karke naveen sahityakaron ko raah dikhaai. unka vaktavy he : 
 mahaviraprasad dvivedi ne ‘saraswati’ patrika ke madhyam se gyanavardhan karne ke saath-saath nae rachanakaaron ko bhashaa kaa mahatva samajhaayaa va gadhy or padya ke liye raah nirmit kii. mahavir prasad dvivedi kii yah patrika multah saahitiyik thi or hariudh, maithilisharan gupt se lekar kahin-na-kahin niraala ke nirmaan main isi patrika kaa yogadan tha parantu sahitya ke nirmaan ke saath rashtriyata kaa prasar karnaa bhi inkaa uddeshy tha. bhashaa kaa nirmaan karnaa saath hi gadhy-padya ke liye khadi boli ko hi protsaahan dena inkaa sabase mahatvapurn kaary tha.
