 agni hindu dharm main aag ke devtaa hai. wo sabhi devataaon ke liye yajna-vastu bharan karne kaa madhyam maane jate hai -- isaliye unki oopaadhi bharat he. vaidic kaal se agni sabase unche devon main se hai. puranic yug main agni puraana naamak ek granth bhi rachit hua. hindu dharm vidhi main yajna, havan or vivaahon main agni dwaara hi devataaon kii puja kii jaati he.
 samuhik yajna naamak pustak ke anusaar agni kii patni kaa naam swaha tha jo ki daksh prajapati tathaa aakuti kii putree thim. unke teen putr paavak, pavamaan tathaa shuchi the. inhen main se ek dwitiya manu swarochish manu hue tathaa inhen teenon se 45 prakaar ke agniyon kaa prakatya hua.
