 guddi 1971 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. is film ko baad main cinema patium naamak tamil film nirmit kiya gaya jiske pramukh kalakaar camel hasan va jaichitra the. 
 kusum  ek chanchal va nishcint vidhyaarthee he jo apane pita, bhaiyya or bhabhi ke saath rahati he. vah film abhinayta dharmendra, jo swayan ko parade par darshaate hai, se premaskt hoti he. uske bombay jane tak kisiko is baat kaa pataa nahin chalata, jahaan uske bhabhi kaa bhai naveen usase prem karne lagata he. guddi naveen ko dharmendra se apane prem kii baat bataati he. uskaa prem jeetne naveen apane chachaa kii sahaayta letaa he, jo dharmendra se parichit he. unki sahaayta se sabhi guddi ko vaastavik jagat or film jagat ke bhed samajhaate hai jinhen samaj guddi naveen se shaadi karne maan jaati he.
 sabhi geet gulzaar dwaara likhit; sara sangeet vasant desai dwaara rachit.
