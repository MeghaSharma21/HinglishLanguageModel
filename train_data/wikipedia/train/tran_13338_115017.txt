 zurich canton switzaraland ke purvottaree bhaag main sthit ek canton he. yahan kii rajbhasha german he lekin yahan ke nivasee adhiktar zurivatuush naamak german kii ek sthaneey upbhasha bolte hai. zurich canton san 1351 main swiss parisangh kaa hissaa bana tha.
 'zurich' main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
