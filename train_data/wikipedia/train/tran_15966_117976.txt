 jal prapaat ek pramukh pravaahee jal krit apradanaatmak sthalarup hai.
 pani ek apekshaakrit vyapak dhara yaa nadi se neeche girtaa he.
 sopani paat yaa kramprapaat main jal chattaanon kii ek shrunkhlaa se neeche girtaa he.
 mahajalprapaat apane naam ke anusaar ek bada or shaktishaalee jalprapaat hota he.
 pani kii ek badi maatra ek urdhvadhar or sankare udgam se neeche girti he.
 jal girane ke saath saath kshaitij rup se failata he or neeche girte samay hameshaa chattan ke sampark main rahata he.
 himadripaat ek aisa jharnaa he jiske jal main barf ke chote chote tukde samahit hote hai.
 avarohi pani chattani aadhaar ke saath kuch sampark rakhata he.
 jal chattani aadhaar kii sataha se sampark tyaag sidhaa neeche girtaa he.
 jal ek sankeern jaldhara ke rup main utarataa he or phir ek vyapak kund main failata he.
 pani alag alag jharanon ke rup main neeche girtaa he.
 sopani or vibhakt paaton kaa mishran is jalprapaat main pani alag alag dhaaraon main chattan kii shrunkhlaa se ek kram main girtaa he.
 lagbhag ek hi aakaar ke ek ke baad ek jharanon kii ek shrunkhlaa jisamen prayek kaa apana ghota-kund hota he.
 catadupa ek mahajalprapaat yaa jharnaa he, . shabd catadupe aise mahajalprapaat ke nikat rahane vaale logon ko ingit karata he, mana jaataa he ki yah log jalprapaat ke nirantar shor ke kaaran bahere ho gayi hai.
