 kaali aandhi dhul bhari us aandhi athwa prachand vaayu athwa alpakaalik jhanjha ko kehete hai jo vasant ritu ke baad bhartiya upmahadweep ke sindhu-ganga ke maidan main dekhne ko milti he. ye saamanytah alpakaalik hoti hai lekin din ke samay main andheraa karne ke liye paryaapt hoti hai jisase nuksaan evam durghatnaayen ghatit hoti hai. uttari bhaagon main ye mansun ke samay or iske aane kaa sanket bhi maani jaati hai.
