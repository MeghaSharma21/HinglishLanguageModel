 
 loricidaye galegidaaye 
 lorisoidea narwanar gana ke strepsirayini upagan ke limriformis adhogan kaa ek adhikul he. isamen africa ke galego or poto, or bharat va dakshinpurvi asia ke loris shaamil hai. kuch jeevvaigyanikon ke anusaar inhen alag "loriceformis" naamak adhogan main hona chaiye. dhyaan den ki limar is ucchkul main nahin hai balki limroidea naamak alag adhikul main aate hai.
