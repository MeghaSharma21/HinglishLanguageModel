 vishnu ke avatar vaaman kaa yah mandir brahma mandir ke uttar purv main avasthit he. 
 isaki lambaai 62' or chaudaai 45' he. yah mandir apekshaakrit adhik unche adhishthan par nirmit hai. iske talchhand ke antah bhaagon kii saamaanya yojana or nirmanstyli devi jagdambi mandir se milti- julati he, kintu iskaa bhawan donon kii apeksha adhik baari va sudrudh he. garbhagrih kaa shikhar samaan aakaar kaa he, kintu isaki manjari pratikrutiyon kaa abhaav he. nirantar dharprasaad main ardhamandap, mahamandap, antaraal or garbhagrih hai. is mandir main mithun murtiyon kaa ankan atyant viral he. shikhar kii choti rathikaaon main hi ye dikhaai detii hai. mandir kii jangha main murtiyon kii keval do panktiyaan hi he. 
 yah mandir is drushti se bhi mahatvapurn he ki iske mahamandap ke upar savaran chat hai or mahamandap ke vaatayanon ke vitan main torana shalabhanjikaon kaa alankaran kiya gaya he. garbhagrih main vishnu ke avatar vaaman kii lagbhag 5' unchi ek murti pratishthit he. garbhagrih ke chaaron ore bani nichali pankti kii rathikaaon main varah, nrisingh, vaaman aadi avatar asin hai. upree pankti kii rathikaaon main brahmani sahit brahma, shiv kii kalyansundar murtiyan or vishnu kii murti ullekhneey hai.
