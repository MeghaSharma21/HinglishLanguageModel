 monazite ek laal-kaaki rang kaa phosphate khanij he jisamen durlabh mrudaa tatv paye jate hai. yah aksar chote crystalon main paaya jaataa he jo kisi ret, mitti yaa anya pattharon ke bich bikhare huye hote hai. monazite thorium, lanthanum or sirium tatwon ko uplabdh karne ke liye mahatvapurn khanij hai. isamen kuch maatra main urenium bhi maujuud hota he. thorium or urenium bahut radiodharmee hai or unke alpha kshay se monazite main helium gas bhi paidaa ho jaati he jo monazite ko garam kar ke nikaali ja sakati he. monazite-yukt ret bharat, madagascar va dakshin africa main bahut maatra main pai jaati he.
