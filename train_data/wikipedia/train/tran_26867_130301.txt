 mutr ko sharir ke baahar nikaalnaa apamutran yaa 'mutr tyaagna' kehalaata he. is kriya main mutrashay kaa mutr urethra, mutradwara hote hue sharir se baahar nikaala jaataa he.
 swasth manushyon main apamutran kii prakriya svaichhik niyantran main hoti he kintu chote bacchon, kuch vridhon, tathaa tantrika kii choton se grasit rogiyon main yah anichhik rup se bhi nikal saktaa he.
