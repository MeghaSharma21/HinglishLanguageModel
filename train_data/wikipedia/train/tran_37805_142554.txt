 ganesh himaal madhyottar nepal main sthit himalay kaa ek khand he. iske adhiktar parvat nepal main hai lekin kuch nepal va tibet kii seemaa par bhi sthit hai. iske purv main sthit trishul gandki ghati ise langtang himaal se alag karti he. pashchim main shyaar kholaa ghati ise mansiri himaal se alag karti he. ganesh himaal nepal kii rajdhani kathmandu se 70 kimi uttar-pashchimottar par sthit he.
