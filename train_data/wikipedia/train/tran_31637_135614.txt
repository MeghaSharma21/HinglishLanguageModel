 jiya mohiuddeen ; kaa janm 20 june 1933 ko faisalabad,pakistan main hua tha. jiya prakhyat pakistani abhinayta he jinhone saahitiyik rachnaao ko padhane kii kala ko abhootpurv or asaadhaaran unchaaiyaan di he. urdu shaayari gadd rachanaaon ko padhnen ke andaaj main ek anokhi or adbhut shaan he jise bhashaa kii gahari samaj aawaaj ke mahiraanaa utaar chadhaav or layaatmakataa paidaa karne kii kshamta ne or bhi nikhaar diya he. unhone sari duniya main aapni kala ke pradarshan se shrotao ko mantrmugdh kiya he or televijan se ek abhinayta,nairator,film nirdeshkaurfilm nirmaataa ke taur par jude rahe he. jiya aaj bhi pakistan media se jude hue he or kai tivi program ko host karte he
