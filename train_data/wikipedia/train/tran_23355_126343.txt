 prithvi ko gherti hui jitne sthaan main vaayu rahati he use vayumandal kehete hai. vayumandal ke atirikt prithvi kaa sthalmandal thos padaarthon se bana or jalmandal jal se bane hai. vayumandal kitni dur tak failaa hua he, iskaa thik thik pataa hamen nahin he, par yah nishchit he ki prithvi ke chaturdik kai sau milon tak yah failaa hua he.
 vayumandal ke nichle bhaag ko kshobhamandal, uske upar ke bhaag ko samtapmandal or uske or upar ke bhaag ko madhya mandalaur uske upar ke bhaag ko inmandal kehete hai. kshobhamandal or samtapmandal ke bich ke bich ke bhaag ko "shantmandal" or samtapmandal or inmandal ke bich ko stratopose kehete hai. saadharantaya upar ke tal bilkul shant rahete hai.
 praaniyon or padpon ke jeevanposhan ke liye vaayu atyaavashyak he. prithvital ke apkshay par bhi iskaa gaharaa prabhaav padataa he. naanaa prakaar kii bhautik or rasinic kriyaayein vayumandal kii vaayu ke kaaran hi sampann hoti hai. vayumandal ke anek drishy, jaise indradhanush, bijli kaa chamakna or kadakna, uttar dhruviya jyoti, dakshin dhruviya jyoti, prabhamandal, kirit, marichika ityadi prakaash yaa vidyut ke kaaran utpann hote hai.
 vayumandal kaa ghanatv ek sa nahin rahata. samudratal par vaayu kaa dabaav 760 millimetre paare ke stambh ke daab ke baraabar hota he. upar uthne se dabaav main kami hoti jaati he. taap yaa sthaan ke parivartan se bhi dabaav main antar aa jaataa he.
 surya kii laghutarang vikiran urja se prithvi garam hoti he. prithvi se dirghatarang bhaumik urja kaa vikiran vayumandal main avashoshit hota he. isse vayumandal kaa taap - 68 degree celsius se 55 degree celsius ke bich hi rahata he. 100 kimi ke upar parabangnee prakaash se oxygen anu yanon main parinat ho jate hai or parmaanu electronon main. isi se is mandal ko inmandal kehete hai. raatri main ye aayan yaa electron phir paraspar milakar anu yaa parmaanu main parinat ho jate hai jisase raatri ke prakaash ke varnapat main hari or laal rekhaayen dikhaai padati hai.
 prithvi ke chaaron ore saikado kimi kii motaai main lapetane vaale gasiy aavaran ko vayumandal kehete hai. vayumandal vibhinn gaison kaa mishran he jo prithvi ko chaaro ore se ghere hue he. nichle staron main vayumandal kaa sanghatan apekshaakrit ek samaan rahata he. oonchaai main gaison kii aapekshik maatra main parivartan paaya jaataa he.
 shuddh or shushk vaayu main nitrogen 78 pratishat, oxigen, 21 pratishat, argon 0.93 pratishat carbon daai oxide 0.03 pratishat tathaa hydrogen, helium, ozone, nion, jenan, aadi alp maatra main upasthit rahati hai. nam vayumandal main jal vaashp kii maatra 5 pratishat tak hoti he. vayumandi jal vaashp kii prapti sagaron, jalaashyon, vanaspatiyon tathaa mrudaaon ke jal se hoti he. jal vaashp kii maatra bumadhya rekha se dhruvon kii ore ghatati jaati he. jal vaashp ke kaaran hi badal, koharaa, paala, varsha, os, him, ola, himpat hota he. vayumandal main ozon parat kii prithvi or us par rahane vaale jeevon ke liye badi hi mahatvapurn bhoomika he. yah parat surya se aane vaali ucch aavriti kii parabangnee prakaash kii 93-99% maatra avashoshit kar leti he, jo prithvi par jivan ke liye hanikaarak he. ozon kii parat kii khoj 1913 main frans ke bhautikavid fabri charles or henry buson ne kii thi.
