 rajdevi mandir saptari jila ke mukhyalay rajviraj ke rajdevi toll main avasthit hai. pracheen itihaas se susampann is mandir ke naam se hi rajviraj nagar kaa naam rakhaa gaya hai. rajdevi mandir ke bheetar maujuud bhagwati pratimaa sen vansh kii ishtadevi maani jaati hai. rajdevi mandir yahan rahane kaa mukhya kaaran yahan sen raajaaon kaa kachhari, durg or darvaar hona hai. is mandir main sthapit bhagwati ko manokamana purna karne vaali devi maane jane ke kaaran nepal evam bharat ke amajanmanas main badi aasthaa hai. is mandir main hindu dharmavalambee ke mahaan tyohaar navaratri main bhaktajanon kii badi taadaad main upasthiti rahati hai.
 pracheen itihaas evam kimbadanti ke anusaar rajdevi mandir kii utpati sen vansh ke samay main hone kaa vishwaas kiya jaataa hai.
