 nirdeshaank: 74°49′55″N 71°18′43″E / 74.83194°N 71.31194°E / 74.83194; 71.31194
 arctic mahaasaagar kaa ek bhaag, kaara sagar siberia ke uttar main sthit he. pashchim main ise berint sagar se kaara jaldmarumadhya or novaya zemlya tathaa purv main, laptev sagar se ise severnaya zemlya alag karte hai.
 kaara sagar kii bhugolic uttari seemaa kaa nirdhaaran kolsat antareep, graham bel dweep, franj joseph bhoomi se lekar arctic antareep jo ki severnaya zemlya kaa sabase uttari sira he, se guzarati rekha ke dwaara hota he.
 kaara sagar kii lambaai 1450 kilometre, chaudaai lagbhag 970 kilometre, kshetrafal 880000 kimi² tathaa ausat gaharaai lagbhag 110 meter he.
 kaara sagar, berint sagar jo ki andh mahaasaagar kii apekshaakrit garm jaldharaon ko grahan karata he, kii tulana main adhik thanda he or varsh main nau maheene se adhik samay tak jamaa rahata he. 
 kaara sagar main ob, yenicey, pyasina or temira nadiyaan visarjeet hoti hai or inke mithe pani ke kaaran isaki lavanta badalati rahati he.
 iske mukhya bandargah novi or dikason hai or yah machhlee pakadaney kaa pramukh sthal he, baavjood iske kii varsh ke sirf do maheene hi is gatividhi ke liye upyukt hote hai, kyonki baaki samay yah jamaa rahata he. pashchim siberian tel basin ke vistaar ke taur par yahan petroleum or prakritik gas ke bhandaron ko khoja gaya he par abhi tak inkaa dohan shuru nahin hua he.architik mahaasaagar ko duniya kaa ‘asi’ kaha jaataa he, ye mahaasaagar duniya kii hifaazat karata he. lekin architik mahaasaagar kii barf kii moti chadar ke neeche dabi he ek newclear pandubbi. is pandubbi main parmaanu bam ke baraabar radio active padaarth he or ye padaarth samudr ke pani main ghulata ja rahaa he. yakeen maniye ye khatra kisi atom bam se kam nahin he.
