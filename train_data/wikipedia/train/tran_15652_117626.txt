 rajasthan bharat kaa ek rajya he jo paryaton ke liye sabase acha rajya mana jaataa he. rajasthan rajya main har zile main kai darshneey sthal dekhne ko milte he, yahaan visheshrup se durg he jo lagbhag har zile main he. inke alaava rajasthan main kai puranic mandir bhi he. 
 prakritik sundarataa or mahaan itihaas se sampann rajasthan main paryaton udyog samridhishalee he. rajasthan deshiy or antarrashtriya paryakaton, donon ke liye ek uchit paryaton sthal he. bharat kii saiyr karne vaala har teesraa videshi sailaani rajasthan dekhne zarur aata he kyonki yah bharat aane vaale paryakaton ke liye "golden triangal" kaa hissaa he. jaipur ke mahal, udaipur kii jhilein or jodhpur, bikaner tathaa jaisalmer ke bhavy durg bhartiya or videshi sailaanoyon ke liye sabase pasandida jagahon main se ek hai. in prasiddh sthalon ko dekhne ke liye yahaan hazaron paryatak aate hai. jaipur kaa hawamahal, jodhpur, bikaner ke dhore or jaisalmer ke dhore kaaphi prasiddh hai. jodhpur kaa mehraangadh durg ,chittorgarh durg kaaphi prasiddh he. yahaan rajasthan main kai puraani haweliyaan bhi he jo vartmaan main heritage hotelein ban chuki hai. paryaton ne yahan aatithy kshetra main bhi rojgaar ko badhaava diya he. yahaan kii mukhya mithai "ghevar" he.
 rajasthan apane pracheen mehalon ,durgon tathaa mandiron ke liye janaa jaataa he.
