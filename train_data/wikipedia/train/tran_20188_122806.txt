 dow ram kumaar verma hindi ke suprasiddh sahityakar, vyangyakaar or hasya kavi ke rup main jane jate hai. unhen hindi ekanki kaa janak mana jaataa he. unhen sahitya evam shiksha ke kshetra main san 1963 main padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. inke kavy main 'rahasyavaad' or 'chhayavaad' kii jhalak he.
