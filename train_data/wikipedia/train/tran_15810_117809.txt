 sahitya academy puraskaar bharat main ek saahitiyik sammaan he, jo sahitya academy prativarsh bharat kii apane dwaara manyata pradutt pramukh bhashaaon main se prayek main prakashit sarvotkrisht saahitiyik kriti ko puraskaar pradaan karti he. bhartiya samvidhan kii aathvin anusuchee main shaamil 22 bhartiya bhashaaon ke alaava ye rajasthani or angrezi bhashaa; yane kul 24 bhashaaon main pradaan kiya jaataa hai. pehali baar ye puraskaar san 1955 main die gaye.
 puraskaar kii sthapana ke samay puraskaar raashi 5,000/- rupye thi, jo san 1983 main kidhaa kar 10,000/- rupye kar di gai or san 1988 main kidhaa kar ise 25,000/- rupye kar diya gaya. san 2001 se yah raashi 40,000/- rupye kii gai thi. san 2003 se yah raashi 50,000/- rupye kar di gai he.
