 long walk tu freedom dakshin africa ke pehle ashwet raashtrapati nelson mandela dwaara likhit ek aatmajivani he, or jise 1995 main little brown and company. ke dwaara prakashit kiya gaya. yah pustak unki praarambhik jivan, javaani, shiksha or 27 varsh ki umr main hui kaid ke bare main he. rangabhed sarkaar ke tahat, mandela ko ANC main netaa kii bhoomika nibhaane par apradhi ghoshit kiya gaya or kukhyat robin dweep par jail main band kar diya gaya. pustak ke antim adhyyon main unhone apane raajnneetik udgam, or africa main rangabhed ke khilaaf sangharsh ko jari rakhane ke apane vishwash ke bare main varnan kiya he.
