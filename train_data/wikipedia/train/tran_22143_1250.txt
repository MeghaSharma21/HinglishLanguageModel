 bharat main sakshartaa dar 75.06 he, jo kii 1947 main maatr 18 % thi. bharat kii sakshartaa dar vishwa kii sakshartaa dar 84% se kam he. bharat main sakshartaa ke maamle main purush or mahilaon main kaafii antar he jaha purushon kii sakshartaa dar 82.14 he vahiin mahilaon main iskaa pratishat keval 65.46 he. mahilaon main kam sakshartaa kaa kaaran adhik aabaadi or parivaar niyojan kii jaankaree kami he. 
 bharat main 6-14 saal ke aayu varg ke prayek balak or balika ko skool main muft shiksha kaa adhikaar he. yahan par 40%se adhik balikayen 10 veen kaksha ke uparaant skool tyaag detii he.
