 andru caranegi vishvavishrut udyogpati the. unke swasthapit treston ne maanavtaavaadi drushti se angreji bhashabhashi vishwa kii sahitya, kala, samskruti, shiksha evam samajaseva kii disha main seva kaa ujwal drushtaant upasthit kiya he. 
 caranegi scotland ke danfarmalin naak sthaan main utpann hue tathaa 13 varsh kii umr ke baad america chale gaye. vahaan amareeki pencelvenian katan mil main babin bvaay ke rup main kaam karne lage. kalantar main ve penselvenian railway board ke mantri or yuddh vibhaag ke adhikari niyat hue. san 1864 i. main unhone udyog or vyapaar ke kshetra main prevash kiya. tel ke vyapaar se apana audyogic jivan aarambh kar san 1865 main ye lohe or koyale kii khaanon ke swami ho gaye; phir 1888 i. tak home steel mil, koyale evam lohe kii khaanon, 425 mile railway line or prapaati yatayaat kii ek line kharid lee. 1901 i. main you.e. steel karporation main apni sansthaaon ke sammel ke pashchaat unhone apana jivan lokseva ke kshetra main samarpit kar diya. vastut: lokseva kaa kaary unhone 31 varsh kii aayu se hi aarambh kar diya tha.
 "pittsburg caranegi institute' kii sthapana caranegi ne 1995 i. main sthaneey logon kii sukh suvidha ke liye kii. scotland vishvavidhyalay hitarth "scotland caranegi trust' tathaa udaatt maanav mulyon ke aadhaar par vyapak paimaane par khoj, shodh evam anusandhan ke liye "washington caranegi trust' kii sthapana san 1902 i. main unhone kii.
 america nivasee hote hue bhi apni janmabhumi kii seva se vimukh nahin rahe or apane janmasthan danfarmalin ke bacchon ke unnayan, vikaas evam sanvruddhi ke liye "caranegi danfarmalin trust' kii sthapana kii. unke dwaara vir kaaryon ko protsaahan, pravardhan evam sangrakshan dene ke liye san 1904 i. main "caranegi hero trust" kii sthapana kii gai. sanyukt rajya america, canada tathaa new foundland se shikshan prashikshan ke vikaas ke liye "caranegi foundation faar da aidvancement ov teaching" kii sthapana hui. yuddh kii sadaa ke liye samaapti ke uddeshy se, uske kaaran or parinaam par anusandhan karne ke liye "caranegi andoument for international pis" naamak trust kii 1910 i. main unke dwaara hui sthapana vishesh mahatv rakhti he.
 "neuyark caranegi karporation" ne 31 karod 50 lakh dollars kaa mahatvapurn anudaan sanyukt rajya america, britain tathaa uske upniveshon evam samrajyon ke logon ke logon ke liye diya. apane jivan ke antim dinon main ek karod dollars se caranegi ne "caranegi united kingdom trust" kii sthapana kii jiskaa uddeshy parivartit sthitiyon ko dhyaan main rakhte hue britain, scotland tathaa ireland ke vidhanantargat rarshti mahatv ke lokopayogi kaary karnaa he. san 1917 i. ke royal charter ke antargat iskaa sanchaalan hota he.
 caranegi ke treston dwaara sangeet, sahitya, kala, naatak, rangamanch, shiksha, pustkaalay, sangrahalay, matra-shishu-raksha, bal tathaa yuva kridakendre, yuvamangal, praudhotthana, grampunarnirmaan evam samajaseva aadi ke kshetron main satat seva kaa mahatvapurn kaary chal rahaa he. apane jeevankaal main 45 karod dollars kaa daan in mahatvapurn treston ko caranegi ne diya tha.
