 ilam dakshin-pashchimi iran kaa ek pracheen samrajya tha jo aaj ke khuzestan praant main kendrit tha. iskaa kaal isapurv 2700 se isapurv 640 rahaa tha jiskaa zikr mesopotamia ke asyrian tathaa sumeri sroton main miltaa he. yah samrajya na to arya tha or na hi semetic, isaki bhashaa ko bhi gaiyr-samooh kii bhashaa mana gaya he. isapurv chhathi shatabdi main jab pehali baar iran kii sampurna satta par arya mul ke hakhaamani shaskaon kaa raaj hua to ilmi bhashaa ko bhi rajkeey bhashaa banaaya gaya. bisitun ke shilaalekh main is bhashaa main bhi utkeern lekh milte hai.
 kai hindu granthon main susha kaa zikr he jis naam se isaki rajdhani thi, yadhyapi yah vahi pradesh rahaa hoga yah satyapit nahin ho paaya he. raja yayati ke vansh main utpann chakravarti raja elin dwaara vasaaya gaya tha elin raja dushyant ke pita the va chakravarti raja bharat ke pitamah. kintu ek anya mat ke anusaar allam rajya kaa sambandh raja el se hona adhik praasangik he. unka naam el pururava bhi he. jo ila ke putr the va som vansh ke sansthapako main ek the.
