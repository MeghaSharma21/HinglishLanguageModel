 toda bhashaa bharat kii ek dravid bhashaa-parivaar kii tamil-kannada shakha kii bhashaa he jo apane swaron main sangharshi va lunthit vyanjanon kii bharpoor maujuudagi ke liye jaani jaati he. ise tamil nadu ke nilgiri kshetra main basane vaala toda samudaay bolta he. iske bolne waalon kii sankhya bahut hi kam he or san 2001 kii janaganana main keval 1,600 gini gai thi.
