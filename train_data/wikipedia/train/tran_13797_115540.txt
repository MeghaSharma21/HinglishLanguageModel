 achitragarh kila rajasthan ke nagaur main sthit he. is kile kaa nirmaan chauthi shatabdi main hua tha. 
 is kile main teen daravaaje hai. pehle daravaaje ko sirhe, duusare ko bich kaa pole or tisare ko kachehari pole kaha jaataa he. is kile main kuch pramukh mahal jaise- haadi rani mahal, deepak mahal, bhakt singh mahal, amar singh mahal, akabari mahal or rani mahal ke alaava do mandir krishna mandir evam ganesh mandir tathaa shah jahani kaa smarak bhi he
