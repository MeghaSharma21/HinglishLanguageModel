 coca-cola cap 1998 main sharajah main khelaa gaya trikoniya cricket toornament tha. yah sharajah main pehla cricket toornament tha jise coca cola dwaara pryojit kiya gaya tha or cricketrs benifit fund shrinkhalaa ke tatwadhan main khelaa gaya tha. round robin praaroop kaa paalan kiya gaya tha jisamen prayek team do anya tiimon ke saath do baar khelti thi. sabhi match din or raat ke khel the or toornament main bharat, australia or newjiland cricket tiimon ko shaamil kiya gaya tha. yah toornament sharajah main yojit kiya gaya tha, jo das saal main pehla aisa toornament tha jisamen pakistan team hissaa nahin le rahi thi. sharajah cricket association stadium, jahaan sabhi maichon kaa aayojan kiya gaya, main ricard 24,000 darshakon ne final muqabla dekha. 
 bharat ne is toornament main australia ke upar final match main jeet darj kii, jabaki vah australia se apane saare lig match haara tha; yah is toornament se thik pehle hue pehle pepsy cap ke dauraan hue maichon ke bilkul vipareet hone vaali ghatana thi, jisamen australia apane saare match haara tha par final main bharat ko haraa diya tha.
 australia ne apane sabhi lig match jite or final ke liye arhata praapt kii, jabaki bharat or newjiland donon ne ek game jeeta tha, jiskaa matlab he ki doosraa finlist behatar net ran rate ke aadhaar par chunaa gaya tha. 
 vijeta bharat ne puraskaar raashi main 40,000 america dollar kii kamai kii, jabaki australia ko upavijeta hone ke liye $30,000 mila or tisare sthaan par newjiland ko $15,000 mila. sachin tendulkar ne toornament puraskaar or opel estra kaa puraskaar jeeta or saath hi sabase adhik chakke or sabase tej pachaas ke liye anya puraskaar bhi jite. demian fleming or australia ke riki ponting ne kramshah sarvshereshth gendbaaj or sarvshereshth kshetrarakshak kaa puraskaar jeeta.
