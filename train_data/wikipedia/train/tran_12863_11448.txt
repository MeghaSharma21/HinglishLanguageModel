 
 roti punjab ke suprasiddh gayak shri gurdas maan kii nai elbum kaa naam he, jo 3 agast 2013 ko release hui. isamen kul 8 geet he, jinhen sangitabaddh kiya he, jatinder singh-shah ne. geet likhe hai swayan gurdas maan ne. 
 1 agast 2013 ko chandigarh main gurdas maan apni nai punjabi mujeec album "roti" relies karne pahunche. yah poochne par ki unhen is elbum kaa koun sa gaanaa sabase acha lagata he, unhone kaha - meri is mujeec album main aathon geet mere laadle bacchon kii tarah he. jaise ek maa ko apane sabhi bachche achche lagate hai, vaise hi mujhe ye sabhi geet. mujeec video diarector - mujeec diarector jatinder shah se jab gurdas maan ke saath ke anubhav ke bare main poocha gaya to vah itane imotional ho gaye ki unki ankhen bhar ai or vah chup ho gaye. tab gurdas maan apni seat se uthakar aae or jatinder ko gale se laga liya.
