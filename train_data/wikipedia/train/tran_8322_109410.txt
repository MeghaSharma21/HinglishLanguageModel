 bikaaneri bhujiyaa yah rajasthan ke bikaner ke naam se judi hui he. yah ek besan ke baarik seve yaa jave jaise banaakar khaadya tel main tale jane par banati he. isamen besan ke alaava mung, moth matar aadi ke aate bhi milaae jate hai. saath hi kai masaale bhi daale jate hai. yah sirf bikaner hi nahin balki rajasthan, maharashtra, gujarat, bihar tathaa pashchim bengal main bhi bahut lokapriy he.
 1877 main, maharaja shri dunger singh ke shasankaal ke dauraan, bhujiyaa kaa pehla batch bikaner kii riyasat main banaaya gaya tha.
