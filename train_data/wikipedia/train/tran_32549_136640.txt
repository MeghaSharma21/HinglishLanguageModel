 nicholas jon spikaman dach mul ke america bhustratejic chintak or yel vishvavidhyalay main 'antarrashtriya sambandh' vishay ke profesar the.unhen continment kaa pitamah kaha jaataa he. spikaman ne paridhibhumi kii sankalpana kaa pratipaadan kiya or yah bataaya ki jo shakti rimland par prabhutv kar legi vahi hridaysthal ko niyantrit kar sakegi. 
 nicholas spikaman kaa janm san 1893 main nederland main hua tha. unke pita kaa naam class spikaman or mata kaa naam elizabeth vaan varstenberg tha. unki shiksha america main california ke barkale main hui jahaan unhone 1921 main snaatak or 1923 main pi ech di kii degree praapt kii. 
 unki patni elizabeth si spikaman angreji kii lekhika thim jinhen unke bal upanyaason ke lekhan liye janaa jaataa he. spikaman kii do betiyaan angelica or patricia huin.
 spikaman 1928 se 1940 tak yel vishvavidhyalay main sterling profesar ke pad par rahe or 1935 se 1940 tak antarrashtriya sambandh vibhaag ke adhyaksh bhi rahe. ve is vibhaag ke sansthaapak adhyaksh the.san 1943 main, new haiven shahar main, nicholas spikaman kii cancer se asamyik mrityu ho gayi.
 spikaman apane purvavarti america sainya chintak alfred theare mahaan or british bhugolvetta helford jon maikindar ke vichaaron se prabhavit the. unhone maikindar ke hrydaysthal siddhant ke sudhaar ke rup main apni paridhibhumi kii sankalpana kaa pratipaadan kiya or yah bataaya ki jo shakti rimland par prabhutv kar legi vahi hridaysthal ko niyantrit kar sakegi. spikaman kaa rimland, maikindar ke inner kricent ke kshetra se miltaa he.
 unki prasidd ukti he, "who controls the Rimland rules Eurasia, Who rules Eurasia controls destinies of the world."
 spikaman ne antarrashtriya sambandhon main shakti kii bhoomika par likhaa.
