 swaraj saamaajik kaaryakarta aravind kejariwal dwaara likhi gayi ek pustak he. is pustak main bhartiya loktantrik dhaanche main badlaav laane evam vaastavik swaraj ke laane kaa rasta dikhaaya gaya he.
 is pustak kaa anaavaran 29 july 2012 ko nai dilli sthit jantar mantar par kiya gaya tha. us samay main arvind kejariwal ne kaha tha - "ye pustak vartmaan kendriyakrut prashasan vyavastha kii kamiyon ko ujagar karti he or bataati he ki vaastavik janatantr kaise laayaa ja saktaa he." unhone yah bhi kaha ki ve is pustak se koi roylty nahin kamaayenge tatha unki ichha he ki yah pustak adhik se adhik logon tak pahunche.prakhyat gandhivaadi samajasevi shri anna hajaare ne is pustak kii prastaavna likhi he.
