 mir qamar-ud-deen khan siddiqui urf nizaam-ul-mulk asafajah  mogul shaasha aurangzeb ke baad ke hyderabad kaa prasiddh nizaam tha, jisane asafajahi rajvansh kii neenv rakhi. usne 1724 main hyderabad rajya kii sthapana kii tathaa 31 july 1720 se lekar 1 june 1748 tak shashan kiya. aurangzeb ne use chinkilich khan ), farruussier ne nizaam-ul-mulk tathaa mohammad shah ne asafajah aadi oopaadhiyaan pradaan kii. 
 nizaam ul mulk asafajah, gaazi uddin khan siddiqui firoz jang pratham or uski pehali bivi wathirunnisa begham kii santan ke taur par 20 agast 1671 ko agra main paidaa hua. asafajah pratham ke bachapan kaa naam mir qamruddin khan siddiqui tha. uskaa namakaran mogul badshaah aurangzeb ne kiya tha.
 1720 se 22 tak nizamul mulk asafajah ne dakkan main apni sthiti sudrudh kar lee thi. vah 1722 se 1724 tak samrajya kaa vajeer rahaa. syed bandhuon ko gaddi se hataane main bhi unhone mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai thi. unhen dakkan kaa vysarai bhi kaha gaya. badshaah mohammad shah ke shashan main vazeer ke kaam se tang aakar dakkan waapas laut gaya or hyderabad rajya kii neenv rakhi. unke maranoparant 1748 main hyderabad dilli shaasha ke adheen ho gaya.
