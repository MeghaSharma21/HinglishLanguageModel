 kisi rasinic abhikriya ke sandarbh main rasinic samya us avasthaa ko kehete hai jisamen samay ke saath abhikaarakon evam utpaadon ke sandran main koi parivartan nahin hota. praayah yah avasthaa tab aati he jab agr kriya kii gati paschkria kii gati ke samaan ho jaati he. dhyaan dene yogya baat yah he ki agrakriya evam pasch kriya ke veg is avasthaa main shunya nahin hote balki samaan hote hai.
 yadi ucch taap par kisi band prakriya paatr main hydrogen tathaa iodine ko anvik anupaat main saath saath rakhaa jaae, to nimnaankit kriya prarambh hoti he :
 is kriya main hydrogen tathaa iodine ke sanyog se hydrogen iodide banataa he tathaa samay ke saath hydrogen iodide kii maatra main vruddhi hoti he. is kriya ke vipareet, yadi shuddh hydrogen iodide gas ko 500 degree celsius tak kriyapaatr main garam kiya jaae, to is yogic kaa vipareet kriya ke dwaara vighatan hota he, jisase hydrogen iodide kaa hydrogen tathaa iodine main vighatan ho jaataa he tathaa in utpaadon ke anupaat main samay ke saath saath vruddhi hoti he. yah kriya nimnaankit rup main hoti hai :
 uparyukt donon hi kriyaaon main kriya kii gati kramash: mand hoti jaati he or ant main purnat: sthir ho jaati he. rasinic kriya kii is sthiti ko rasinic samyavastha kehete hai. kriya ke samyavastha mishran main uparyukt padaarthon kii aapekshik maatra ek hi rahati he, chaahe yah kriya hydrogen or iodine ke sanyog se hydrogen iodide banane kii ho, athwa hydrogen iodide ke vighatan se hydrogen tathaa iodine main pruthakkaran ho, athwa teenon sanghatakon ke samyavastha santulan mishran kii prakriya ho, jisamen hydrogen tathaa aadin parmaanuon kii samaan sankhya upasthit rahati he. iske atirikt prayogshaala ke parinaamon main yah paaya jaataa he ki chaahe hydrogen tathaa iodine ke parmaanuon kii samast sankhya samaan ho athwa nahin, donon hi dashaon main samaan taap par taiyaar kiye hue samyavastha mishranon kii samyavastha sandratha, athwa samyavastha dabaav ke nimnaankit anupaaton kaa maan, sthir rahata he.
