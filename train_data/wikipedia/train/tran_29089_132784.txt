 guru ek sarv utam shakti he jo har jeev aatma main gyaan bharti he. guru, sanskrit shabd he jis kaa arth he andhere se raushnee kii taraf lekar jane vaala.
 sikh dharm main guru akshar "shabd" ke liye istemaal hua he. "shabd" nirankaar dawaara pragat kiya hua, uskaa hukum/shakti he. "shabd" ko dhur kii vaani bhi kaha gaya he. yah vaani sat purashon dawaara bina kaano se suni jaati he or hridaye main pragat hoti he. baba nanak va anya bhakton ne isi ko apana guru mana he. isi dwaara nirankaar kaa gyaan hota he. isi ko aasi granth main sab se upar mana gaya he :""shri guru sab upar"" - bhat gayand 
 isko beej mantra bhi kaha jaataa he. 
 aadi granth main guru or guru donon shabdon main bhed rakhaa gaya he.
 jo sirf manushya jaati ko gyaan de, use guru kaha he, lekin jo har jeev chaahe jaanavar, manushya, ped, podhe adik main gyaan bhare uske liye guru shabd kaa istemaal kiya he. aam bhashaa main yah shabd adal badal liye jate hai. lekin gurmat vichaar main isaki bhinnata saafe samajhaaii he. yahi nahin gur, gurdev, satgur, satguru, satguru shabdon ko bhi log ekasaar maanate hai.
