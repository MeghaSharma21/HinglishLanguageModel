 ganimid hamaare saura mandal ke paanchave grah brihaspati kaa sab se bada upgrah he or yah puure saura mandal kaa bhi sab se bada chandramaa he. iskaa vyas 5,268 kimi he, jo budh grah se bhi 8% bada he. iskaa dravyaman bhi saura mandal ke saare chandramaaon main sabase zyaada he or prithvi ke chandramaa kaa 2.2 guna he.
 ganimid ko angrezi main "Ganymede" likhaa jaataa he. ganimid pracheen unani dharmik kathaon kaa ek paatr tha jo unani dharm ke devon ko sharaab parosata tha .
 ganimid lagbhag baraabar maatra ke pathar or pani kii barf kaa bana hua he or iske kendriya bhaag main pighla hua loha he. is pighale lohe kii vajah se ganimid saura mandal kaa iklauta chandramaa he jiskaa apana chumbakeey gola he. vaigyanikon kaa maananaa he ki isaki sataha ke qareeb 200 kimi neeche do barfili parton ke darmayaan ek pani kaa mahaasaagar he. isaki sataha main halke or gadhe rang ke kshetra dekhe ja sakate hai. mana jaataa he ke halke rang ke kshetron main nai barf kii maatra adhik he or in ilaaqon main khaiyaan or chattaane maujuud hai jinaki vajah brihaspati ke bhayankar gurutvkarshan se paidaa hue jowarbhata bal dwaara kii gayi uthal-puthal ko bataaya jaataa he. gadhe rang kii sataha kii umr bahut puraani lagati he or anumaan he ki is par jagah-jagah par ulkapindon ke girane se prahaar cretar banane ke sivaay yah pichley 4 arab saalon main zyaada nahin badali.
 ganimid kaa apana patala-sa vayumandal he jisamen adhiktar oxygen ke teen bhinn rup hai - oxygen ke parmaanu, aanvik oxygen or ozone . is vayumandal main kam maatra main hydrogen gas bhi maujuud he.
 sandarbh truti: "lower-alpha" naamak sandarbh-samooh ke liye tag maujuud hai, parantu samooh ke liye koi tag nahin mila. yah bhi sambhav he ki koi samaapti tag gaayab he.
