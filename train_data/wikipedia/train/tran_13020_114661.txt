 haada' chouhan rajput hai. ve agnikul ke vanshaj kahe jate hai. kisi samay ve bundi or kota ke shaasha the. haada, hadoti ke nivasee hai. bundi ke swantatra rajya kii sthapana 1342 main devisingh haada ne kii thi. kota purv main kotiabhil ke niyantran ke kaaran kota kahalaaya. kota main swantatra rajya kii sthapana 1631 main madhosingh haada ne kii.1838 main kota ke shaasha ramsingh ne kota kaa kuch bhaag alag kar ke jhalawad rajya kii sthapana kii. jisaki rajdhani jhalarapatan thi. is prakaar jhalawad rajasthan main angrejo dwaara banaai gai antim riyasat thi.
