 id-ul-juha  islam dharm main vishwaas karne vaale logon kaa ek pramukh tyohaar he. ramajaan ke pavitra maheene kii samaapti ke lagbhag 70 dinon baad ise manaaya jaataa he. islamic manyata ke anusaar hazarat ibrahim apane putr hazarat ismail ko isi din khuda ke hukum par khuda ki raah main qurbaan karne ja rahe the, to allaah ne uske putr ko jeevandaan de diya jisaki yaad main yah parv manaaya jaataa he.is shabd kaa bakron se koi sambandh nahin he. na hi yah urdu kaa shabd he. asal main arabi main 'baqar' kaa arth he bada jaanavar jo jibah kiya jaataa he. usee se bigadakar aaj bharat, pakistan va bangla desh main ise 'bakra id' bolte hai. id-e-qurbaan kaa matlab he balidaan kii bhaavana. arabi main 'qarb' najdiki yaa bahut paas rahane ko kehete hai matlab is maukey par bhagwaan insaan ke bahut kareeb ho jaataa he. qurbaani us pashu ke jibah karne ko kehete hai jise 10, 11, 12 yaa 13 jilhijj ko khuda ko khush karne ke liye zibih kiya jaataa he. quran main likhaa he : hamane tumhen hauz-e-qausa diya to tum apane allaah ke liye namaaz padho or qurbaani karo.
 id ul ajaha kaa tyohaar hijri ke aakhri maheene jul hijj main manaaya jaataa he. puri duniya ke musalmaan is maheene main makka saudi arab main ekatrit hokar haj manaate he. id ul ajaha bhi isi din manaai jaati he. vastav main yah haj kii ek anshiy adaayagi or musalamaanon ke bhav kaa din he. duniya bhar ke musalamaanon kaa ek samooh makka main haj karata he baaki musalamaanon ke antarrashtriya bhav kaa din ban jaataa he.id ul ajaha kaa aksharsh: arth tyaag vaali id he is din jaanavar kii qurbaani dena ek prakaar kii pratikatmak qurbaani he. haj or uske saath judi hui paddati hazrat ibrahim or unke parivaar dwaara kiye gaye kaaryon ko pratikatmak taur par dohraane kaa naam he. hazrat ke parivaar main unki patni haajra or putr ismail the. manyata he ki hazrat ibrahim ne ek swapn dekha tha jisamen vah apane putr ismail kii qurbaani de rahe the hazrat ibrahim apane das varshiya putr ismail ko ishwar kii raah par qurbaan karne nikal pade. pustakon main aata he ki ishwar ne apane farishton ko bhejkar ismail kii jagah ek jaanavar kii qurbaani karne ko kaha. darasal ibrahim se jo asal qurbaani maangi gai thi vah thi unki khud kii thi arthath ye ki khud ko bhul jaao, matlab apane sukh-aaraam ko bhuulakar khud ko maanavtaa/insaaniyat kii seva main puri tarah se laga do. tab unhonen apane putr ismail or unki maa haajra ko makka main basaane kaa nirnal liya. lekin makka us samay registan ke sivaa kuch na tha. unhen makka main basaakar ve khud maanav seva ke liye nikal gayi.is tarah ek registan main basna unki or unke puure parivaar kii qurbaani thi jab ismail bade hue to udhar se ek kafila gujara or ismail kaa vivaah us kafile main se ek yuvati se kara diya gaya phir praranmbh hua ek vansh jise itihaas main ishmailits, yaa vanu ismail ke naam se janaa gaya. hazrat mohammad sahab kaa isi vansh main janm hua tha.id ul ajaha ke do sandesh he pehla parivaar ke bade sadsy ko swaarth ke pare dekhnaa chahie or khud ko maanav utthan ke liye lagaana chahie id ul ajaha yah yaad dilaataa he ki kaise ek chote se parivaar main ek nayaa adhyay likhaa gaya.
