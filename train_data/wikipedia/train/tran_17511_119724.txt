 yah bharat kii rajdhani nai dilli main, raisina kii pahaani par sthit he. yahan bharat sarkaar kaa kendriya sachivalay he. isamen do imaaraten, bilkul ek jaisi, par pratibimb rup main rajpath ke uttar va dakshin main sthit hai. inamen pramukh mantralay ke karyaalay bhi sthit hai.
 yah sachivalay do imaaraton main vistrut he:
 yahaan mukhyatah vitt mantralay evam gah mantralay sthit hai.
 yahan mukhyatah raksha mantralay, videsh mantralay evam pradhan mantri kaa karyaalay sthit he.
