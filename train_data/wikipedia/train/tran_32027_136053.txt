 byomakesh bakshi shardendu bandyopadhyaay dwaara rachit bangla jasusi galp he. isaki rachana arthur conan dial dwaara rachit mahaan saahitiyik jasoos sharlok homes se prerana lekar hui he. shardendu ji ne 1942 se 1970 ke madhya byomakesh bakshi kii 32 kahaniyaan likhi kintu asamay mrityu hone ke kaaran 33veen kahani apurna rahi. 
 byomakesh bakshi kii pehali kahani "satyanveshi" he tathaa 32veen kahani "lohaar biscuit" he. byomakesh kii mulaakaat 10veen kahani me shardendu dwaara rachit " baroda - da ghost hanter " se hota he. byomakesh ke pehali kahani me khalanaayak ke rup main dow anukool babu saamne aate hai. isi kahani main byomakesh kii mulaakaat ajit bannerjee se hoti he jo anya kahani me byomakesh ke param mitra ke rup main saamne aata he. dow anukool babu 8veen kahani me khalanaayak ke rup main phir vaapasi karte hai. 
 byomakesh aadhaarit pehali film mahaan bengali lekhak, film nirmaataa, aalochak dwaara nirdeshit 1942 me ai chidiyaakhana he. 90 ke dashak main byomakesh aadhaarit chalchitr doordarshan me aayaa. is chalchitr main byomakesh kaa kirdaar rajit kapur ne nibhaayaa. byomakesh kii patni kaa naam satyavati he tathaa byomakesh ke bete kaa naam khoka he.
