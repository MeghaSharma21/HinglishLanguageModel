 saint mairi roman catholic carch ek isai dharm ke roman catholic sampradaay kaa girjaghar he jo saint mairi street, monmouth, wales, ke centre ke najadik maujuud he. yah girjaghar europea dharmasudhar ke baad wales main anumit pehla roman catholic puja kaa saarvajanik sthal tha. carch kaa sthapaty baad ke georgian roman catholic girjagharon kaa he, jisamen baad main catholic dharmantarit vaastukar benjamin bkanol dwaara victorian parivardhan bhi kiye. girjaghar 15 agast 1974 se grad dwitiya soochibaddh imaarat he tathaa monmouth heritage trail ke 24 sthalon main se ek he.
 solahavin sadi ke pashchaat monmouth rekasency kaa centre tha. 1773 main shahar england or wales main ucchtam anupaat vaale catholic shaharon main se ek tha. 1778 main catholic anuyaayiyon ke prati dandic kanoonon ko dhilaa kar diya gaya tha. monmouth magistriton ke samaksh ek yachika daayar karke shahar main saarvajanik catholic chapel kaa nirmaan karne kii anumati maangi gai thi. yachikakartaon main se ek mouno street pe sthit robin hud sarai ke maalik maaikel watkins bhi the. robin hud sarai main hi upar ke kamare main jab tak maas yojit kiya jaataa tha. baad main chapel ke nirmaan kii anumati to mil gai parantu phir sthaneey kanoon kii jatiltaayen saamne ai, jisamen catholic or un protestant isaayiyon jo carch of england ke abhishasan ko sweekaar nahin karte the unki imaaraton ko jitna sambhav hi sake utana agochar banaae jane kaa uddeshy hota tha. aisi sthiti main yah sweekaar karnaa padaa ki imaarat girjaghar jaisi na lage. pravesh karne ke liye rajmarg kii taraf se aane kii anumati nahin thi or catholic upaskon ko ek baar main ek hi ko chapel ke andar aana padataa tha. multah girjaghar badi chaturaai se cottejo kii ek pankti ke dwaara sadak se chupa diya gaya tha. baad main jab catholic anuyaayiyon ke prati bhedbhaav khatm hon gaya tab bkanol ke girjaghar ke punarnirmaan ke dauraan cottejo ko gira diya gaya tha.
