 airodynamiks main hypersonic gati vah gati he jo bahut zyada supersonic he. 1970 se is shabd ko saamanytah 5 mike yaa usase adhik gati ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he.
 sateek mike sankhya jisapar vimaan udaan bharta he vah alag-alag hoti he kyonki prayek yaan main mike 5 kii gati par anek bhautik or rasinic badlaav hote hai. hypersonic ko alag prakaar se aise bhi paribhaashit kiya jaataa he jab ramjet shuddh thrust paidaa nahin karata.
