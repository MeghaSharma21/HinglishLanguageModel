 swayanchalit mashine aisi mashine hai jo maanav prayaas ke bina bhi kisi prachaalan chakra ko purnat: yaa anshat: sanchalit karti hai. aisi mashine keval peshiyon kaa hi kaary nahin karatin varan mastishk kaa kaary bhi karti hai. swayanchalit mashine purna rup se yaa aanshik rup se swayanchalit ho sakati hai. ye nimnlikhit prakaar kaa kaary kar sakati hai:
 swayanchalit mashiinon ke laabh ye hai : 
 in laabhon ke kaaran jahaan pehle keval manushyon se kaam liya jaataa tha, jaise karyaalayon, grih or sadak ke nirmaanon, khanan, krishi or krishi ke anya kaamkaajon tathaa anek udyog dhandhon main vahaan ab swayanchalit mashine purna rup se yaa aanshik rup se kaary kar rahi hai.
 kisi sanyantr main kitna swachaalit ansh hoga, yah laagat, prapyata or anya pratibandhon par nirbhar karata he. kisi sanyantr ke samast bhaagon ko yaa sanyantr ke kisi ek bhaag ko yaa kisi sanyantr kii anek mashiinon yaa vibhaagon ko swayanchalit rakhana sambhavya or vyaavhaarik ho saktaa he. kuch sanyantr aise ho sakate hai ki unka kuch ansh hi swayanchalit rakhana vyaavhaarik ho saktaa he. kuch swayanchalit rakhana vyaavhaarik ho saktaa he. kuch sanyantr aise ho sakate hai ki unka kuch ansh hi swayanchalit rakhana vyaavhaarik ho saktaa he. kuch swayanchalit mashiinon ke udhaaharan nimnlikhit hai :
 inke atirikt soot kaatne, kapadaa bunane, fasal kaatane or taulne aadi kii bhi swayanchalit mashine bani hai. bhinn-bhinn prakaar ke udyog-dhandhon main kaam aanevaali jo anek prakaar kii vishisht mashine aaj bani hai.
 masini aujaaron main swachaalan kaa prachlan bahut bad gaya he. inase laagat main bahut kami hoti he.
 duusare prakaar ke aujaar main mashin main chad kaa bharan hota or samast chakra tab tak swayanchalit hota he jab tak samaan chad khatm nahin ho jaataa. ab naveen chad daalkar chakra pun: chaalit hota he.
 mashin ek takuawali yaa bahutkuawali ho sakati he. bahutakjvaali mashin main kai chad bhramit hote hai or saath-saath mashin kaa kaary chalata rahata he.
 swayanchalit masini aujaaron ke anya udhaaharan hai - peshan chakki, gear kaatane kii mashin, milling mashin, chhedne kii mashin ityadi.
 rupad ke sansarg main contica mashin slidon ko chaalu karata he or aujaar vaanchit maarg kaa anusaran karte hue samocch rekha kaa punarutpaadan karte hai. contica un vaidyutiya yaa dravchaalit yuktiyon ko prachaalit kar sakati he jo mashin slidon ko chalaanevaali motaron ko niyantrit karti he.
 sthanantaran mashiinon kaa pramaapan - mashin chalate samay vishisht mashiinon main yatharthata kaa nirdisht niyantran vaanchit he. chunki bahut se prachaalan hote hai at: sthanantaran mashiinon main kuch antarprakram or bahirprakram pramaapan pravidhiyon kaa upyog hota he. dhali hui vastuon or mashinit talon kii jaanch tathaa vibhinn bhaagon kii swat: asweekruti bhi rahati he.
 sankhyatmak niyantran ke liye jo mashin aujaar liye gaye hai ve ye hai - jig vedhan mashine, peshan tathaa kharad mashine.
 mashin ke vibhinn prachaalanon ke niyantranaarth ye yuktiyaan saralatam tathaa saamaanya hai. ye swayanchalit bharan main tathaa dabayantra or penchmashinon ke vibhinn purjon ke hataane main bhi prayukt hoti hai. cam vibhinn sladdon kii gati ko niyantrit karte hai tathaa swayanchalit kharad mashiinon kaa sambharan karte tathaa unhen gati pradaan karte hai.
 contica template kaa anusaran karti he or aujaaron kii gati contica dwaara dravchaalit yaa vaidyutiya yuktiyon se niyantrit kii jaati he. anurekhak niyantran ek, do yaa teen vimaao main kaary kar sakate hai. ek disha main niyantran kharaadon par hota he jahaan aujaar bheetar tathaa baahar palyaan ke saath gati karata he. ans main palyaan kaa anudairdhya sanchalan swat: pakad main aa jaataa he.
 dwivim anurekhak niyantran yaa to kartak ko ghumata he yaa samakonik disha main kaary karata he. template ke sampark kaa contica, vikshep kii disha or maatra ke anupaat main sanket bhejtaa he. electraneey yukti do sambharan motaron kii gati niyantrit karte hai taaki much kii parinami gati katic ke saath sansarg main template par sparsheey ho.
 pratilipi vidhi main, jaisaa upar kaha gaya he, template yaa praturup kaa utpaadan aavashyak he jo swayan main kathinaaiyon or vilamb prastut kar saktaa he. electraneey niyantran, template yaa praturup ke prayog kaa niraakaran karata he tathaa chumbakeey or chhidrit tepe dwaara sanchit soochnaaon se vibhinn bhaagon kaa yatharthata se punarutpaadan hota he. tepe par ankit soochna kii vyakhya ke tathaa uchit samay par m/c ko sanket bhejne ke liye upyukt upaskar kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. ye sanket m/c par ek niyantrak yukti dwaara grahan kiye jate hai jo m/c ko aadesh paalan karaate hai. m/c aujaaron ke sankhyatmak niyantran ke do pramukh varg hai :
 in donon pranaaliyon main kuch buniyadi samya hai jinamein 4 tatv mukhya hai-
 mashining ke liye puri soochna "prakram ejeeniyar" dwaara taiyaar kii jaati he taaki mashin kii sabhi gatiyaan purv nirdhaarit rahen mashin parichar par ashrith na ho.
 isamen nimna sopan hai-
 1. sabhi yantric vivaranon ko gyaat karnaa - yatha, kartak kaa prakaar, kartan kaa kram or kartanon ko sankhya.
 2. upyukt dutt se sabhi pramukh vimaaon kaa pariklan 
 dwivim niyantran hetu sabhi binduon ke or nirdeshaankon kii gananaa chune hue dutt se kar lee jaati he. yah part ke blue pinrat se praapt hota he.
 3. kaarykram nirdhaaran - mashining ke liye vistrut nirdesh ankon or shabdon kaa prayog kar sanketon main taiyaar kiye jate hai.
 kartak ke vyas, kartak-bharan-dar or niyantran dar aadi kii rachana ke liye sanket prayukt hote hai.
 4. ye nirdesh vishisht bhashaa main cardon par chhidrit hote hai. ye chhidrit card ek pariklan yantra main chode jate hai jo kaagaj ke tepe par bane chhidrit chhedon main vishisht bhashaa kaa anuwaad kar dete hai. yadi bich kii sthitiyon kii soochna kii aavashyaktaa padati he to tepe, pariklanayantr par laga diya jaataa he jo kartak kii nirdeshaank sthiti ko gananaa kar detaa he, vah phir chumbakeey tepe par lapet diya jaataa he jiskaa upyog nivisht madhyam kii tarah thr/d aujaar niyantrak ikai ke liye kiya jaataa he.
 5. tepe paathyank sire par lagaate he jo niyantran ikai kaa niyantrak ko nirdesh bhejtaa he or baad main mashin slidon ko niyantrit karata he. vahi tepe baar baar prayukt ho saktaa he or is prakaar chakra kii punrariti hoti rahati he.
 vaanchit sthiti se kisi vichlan ko sahi karne ke liye iskaa prayog hota he. yah vaanchit shart se m/c kii chyuti pravriti ko dur karne kaa saadhan he. udhaahrantaya yadi m/c manch kii sthiti niyantrit kii jaati he, to pratisambharan niyantrak ko vaapasi sanket bhejtaa he tathaa aavashyaktaa padane par sanketon main shuddhi kii jaati he.
 manch sthiti kii truti nikaali jaati he tathaa sanket niyantran ikai ko bheje jate hai jo niyaman motor dwaara manch sthiti ko shuddh kar dete hai.
 mashin aujaaron ke prayukt hone par sankhyatmak niyantran, sabhi kartak chaalon, purna path, work pis ke saapeksh kartak kii sambharan dar tathaa anya sahaayak falan yatha kharadan, kartan, taral jodtod aadi ke niyantran hetu, kaary karata he.
