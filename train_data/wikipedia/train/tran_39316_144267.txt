 nyaaypalika kisi bhi janatantr ke teen pramukh angon main se ek he. anya do ang hai - karypalika or vyavasthapika. nyaaypalika, samprabhutasampann rajya kii taraf se kanoon kaa sahi arth nikaalati he evam kanoon ke anusaar na chalane waalon ko dandit karti he. is prakaar nyaaypalika vivaadon ko sulajhane evam aparaadh kam karne kaa kaam karti he jo apratyaksh rup se samaaj ke vikaas kaa maarg prashast karata he. shaktiyon ke pruthakkaran ke siddhanth ke anuroop nyaaypalika swayan koi niyam nahin banaati or na hi yah kanoon kaa kriyanyavan karaati he.
 sabko samaan nyaay sunishchit karnaa nyaaypalika kaa asli kaam he. nyaaypalika ke antargat koi ek sarvocch nyaayalay hota he evam uske adheen vibhinn nyaayalay hote hai.
 bharat kii swatantra nyaaypalika kaa sheersh sarvocch nyaayalay he, jiskaa pradhan pradhan nyaayadhish hota he. sarvocch nyaayalay ko apane naye maamalon tathaa ucch nyaayalayon ke vivaadon, dono ko dekhne kaa adhikaar he. bharat main 24 ucch nyaayalay hai, jinke adhikaar or uttardaayitv sarvocch nyaayalay kii apeksha simit hai. nyaaypalika or vyavasthapika ke paraspar matbhed yaa vivaad kaa sulah raashtrapati karata he.
