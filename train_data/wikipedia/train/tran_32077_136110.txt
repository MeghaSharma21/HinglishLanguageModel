 chakree bahubhuj aise bahubhuj ko kehete hai jiske sabhi sheersh kisi vrutt par sthit hon.
 chunki kinhin bhi teen binduon se hokar ek vrutt kheenchna sambhav he, isaliye sabhi tribhuj chakree tribhuj hote hai. kintu sabhi chaturbhuj, panchabhuj aadi chakree hon, yah jaruri nahin he. chakree chaturbhuj ke aamane-saamne ke konon kaa yoga 180 degree hota he.
