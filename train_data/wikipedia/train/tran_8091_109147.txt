 gurprit singh vadtch, aam taur par gurprit ghuggi ke naam se jane jate ek bhartiya adaakaar, kamedian or siyasatdan hai. ghuggi ne apane peshey kii shuruuaat 1990 ke dashak main rangamanch se thi, jiske baad usne "raunak mela" or "parchaven" jaise tivi dharawahikon main lagaataar bhoomikaaye nibhaain. usne apane video "ghuggi junkashan" or "ghuggi shu mantar" dwaara hasya kii pramukh bhoomikaaon ke saath antarrashtriya janatak manyata praapt kii. usne 'patawaari jhilmil singh' ke kirdaar main "asaan nuun maan vatanaan da" main abhinay kiya. vah film "kairi on jattan" main abhinay kiya or "aradaas" main uski mahatvapurn bhoomika ke liye uski prashansa kii jaati he.
