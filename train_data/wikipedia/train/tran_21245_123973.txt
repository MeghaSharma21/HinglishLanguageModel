 mandana rajasthan evam madhya pradesh kii kala he. mandana sanskrit bhashaa ke shabd mandan kaa rup he. jiskaa arth he 'khojna'. mandane yahaan ke saamaajik utsavon, dharmik parvo, shaadi-vivaah, janmotsav, holi-diwaali par ghar- angan kaa hissaa he jo pracheen vaidic samskruti ke pratik hai. mandanon main kalash, pagalya, swaastik, phool, chowk, diya, vinayak, khedon chang aadi pramukh hai. mandane praaia: jyamitiya aakaar ke hote hai. parantu aajkal golakaar bhi banaae jate hai.
 rajasthan main abhi bhi gaanv ke log biihat rup se mandana banaate hai. deepawali ke dauraan mandana banane ke liye gaanvon main angan ko geru or gobar se lipa jaataa he. inhen banane ke liye geru bhigokar usamen gond milaayaa jaataa he. phir brush se dharaatal par gol yaa chaukor dizin main geru se lipate hai. suukh jane par safed rang, khadia yaa chooney se brush kii sahaayta se yaa seenk par rooyee kii fureri banaakar dizin banaae jate hai.
 rajasthan main deepamala ke is utsap main mandane chaar chand laga dete he. utsavon main chitrankan kii yah anuthi parampara vividhta liye hue he. alag-alag anchalon main vahaan ke reeti-rivaaj ke anusaar mandane banaae jate hai.
