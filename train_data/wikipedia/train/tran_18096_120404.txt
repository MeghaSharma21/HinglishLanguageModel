 dingling siberia main basane vaali ek pracheen jati thi. yah shuru main baykaal jhil se pashchim main lenaa nadi ke kinare basaa karte the lekin samay ke saath dakshin kii ore jaakar mongolia or uttari china ke kshetra main ja based. mahaan itihaaskaar ke abhilekh naamak chini itihaas-granth ke anusaar baad main unhen shiyongnu samrajya ke adheen kar liya gaya haalanki 71 isapurv ke baad unhone shiyongnuon ke kilaaf vidroh kara. teesri sadi isvi ke baad ve tiele logon kaa bhaag ban gaye jo dhire-dhire pashchim kii ore madhya asia main fail gaye. inhi tiele logon kaa ek gut hind-europiyon se mishrit ho gaya jis se uigur jati utpann hui. dingling logon kii ek duusari shakha shiyanbei logon main ja mili.
 itihaaskaar aksar dingling ko ek turk jati samajhate hai, haalanki isapar matbhed he. dikqat yah he ki dinglingon kii bhashaa kaa koi likhit rup nahin tha isliye unki swayan kii likhaaiyaan astitv main nahin hai. unke bare main jaankaree keval pracheen chini sroton se milti he lekin yah strot swayan dinglingon se sidhe sampark main nahin the. dingling shaayad ek turki bhashaa bolte the lekin yah bhi pakkaa pataa nahin he.
