 mopla vidroh : kerala ke mopla musalamaanon dwaara 1921main sthaneey jamidaaro evam britaniyon ke viruddh kiya gaya vidroh mopla vidroh kehalaata he. yah vidroh malabar ke eranad or valluvanad taluka main khilaafat aandolan ke viruddh angrejon dwaara kii gayi damanaatmak kaarywahi ke viruddh armbh hua tha. isamen kaaphi sankhya main hinduon kaa katl or atyaachaar hua. isi ko aadhaar banaakar vinayak damodar saavarkar ne 'mopla' naamak upanyaas kii rachana kii he.
 kerala ke malabar kshetra main mopalaon dwaara 1922i. main vidrah kiya gaya. prarambh main yah vidroh angrez hukumat ke viruddh tha. mahatma gandhi, shaukat ali, moulana abul kalam azaad jaise netaaon kaa sahyog is aandolan ko praapt tha. is aandolan ke mukhya netaa ke rup main 'ali musaliyaar' charchit the. 15 farvari, 1922. ko sarkaar ne nishedhagna lagu kar kilaafat tathaa congress ke netaa yaqub hasan, you. gopal menon, pi. moiddeen koya or ke. madhavan nair ko giraftaar kar liya. iske baad yah aandolan sthaneey mopla netaaon ke haath main chalaa gaya. 1922i. main is aandolan ne hindu-musalamaanon ke madhya sampradaayak aandolan kaa rup le liya, parantu sheeghra hi is aandolan ko kuchal diya gaya.
