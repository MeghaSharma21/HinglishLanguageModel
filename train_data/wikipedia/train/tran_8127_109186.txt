 ooshmaagatiki, bhautik vigyaan, rasayan vigyaan or vyapak rup main vigyaan kaa hi mahatvapurn or moolbhoot vishay rahaa he. ooshmaagatiki vigyaan kii vah shakha he jisamen yantrik kaary tathaa ooshmaa main paraspar sambandh kaa varnan kiya jaataa he, yah pramukh rup se yantrik kaary tathaa ooshmaa ke paraspar rupantaran se sambandhit he.
 ooshmaagatiki ke mukhyatah do niyam he-
 ooshmaagatiki kii mukhya shakhaon kaa pradurbhaav lagbhag nimnlikhit samyavali le anusaar hua-
 ooshmaagatiki ke kuch vichaar duusare kshetron main bhi prayukt huehain-
