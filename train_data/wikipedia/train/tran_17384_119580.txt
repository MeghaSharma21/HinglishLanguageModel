 parvati nadi madhya pradesh kii nadi he, jise 'paara' naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. yah nadi vindhyaachal kii pashchimi shreniyon se nikal kar gwalior pradesh main bahati hui sindh main mil jaati he. parvati-sindhu sangam par pracheen kaal kii prasiddh nagari padmavati basii hui thi.mahakavi kalidaas ke 'meghdoot' kii nirvindhyaa hi parvati nadi ho sakati he.parvati nadi kaa mahabharat, bhishmaparv main bhi ullekh he.kuch logon ke matanusaar nirvindhyaa vartmaan nevaaj nadi he.
 yah nadi madhyapradesh kii vindhyachal parvatamala ke uttari parshv main sthit sihor se nikalakar kadaiyahat ke sameep rajasthan main pravesh karti he.
 yah 18 kilometre tak rajasthan va madhya pradesh kii seemaa banaate hue baanra jile main rajasthan main pravesh karti he tathaa baanra va kota jile main bahane ke baad paali gaanv ke nikat chambal nadi main mil jaati hain.
 isaki sahaayak nadiyon main lhasi, andheri, vilaas, barani, banthali aadi pramukh hain.
