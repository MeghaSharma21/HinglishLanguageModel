 punjab mail, bhartiya rail kii ek express train he jo mumbai ke chhatrapati shivaji terminus or firozpur ke bich chalati he.
 punjab mail kii shuruyaat 1 june 1912 ko hui thi. jab yah railgadee shuru hui thi tab yah ballard pier se lekar peshawar tak jaati thi. yah railgadee veshesh rup se british adhikariyon, civil sevakon or unke pariwaron ko bombai se dilli or phir british bharat ke uttar-pashchim seemaant praant tak le jane ke liye chalaai gayi thi. yahaan wo jaise hi pani ke jahaaj se utarate the rail par savaar ho jate the. 1914 main iskaa shuruaati stesan badalakar victoria terminus kar diya gaya. azaadi ke baad iskaa gantavy bharat paak seemaa par sthit firozpur stesan kar diya gaya.
 2012 ganatantr diwas parade main sau varsho kaa safar tay karne vaali punjab mail ko railway dwaara pradarshit kii gai.punjab mail desh kii ekmaatr train he jisane sau saal puure kiye hai. jahaan maujuuda punjab mail ko electric ingine or sleeper class bogiyon se chalati he, vahiin puraani punjab mail koyale se chalane vaale ingine or lakdi ke coach ke saath chalaa karti thi. punjab mail ko pehle punjab limited ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. aazaadee se pehle angrejon ke jamaane main yah itarasi, agra, dilli, amritsar, lahore or peshawar ke bich 2496 kilometre kaa safar tay karti thi. shuru main ise keval gore angrej saahabon ke liye chalaaya gaya tha. lekin, 1930 se isamen aam janata kii khatir third class ke dibbe bhi lagaae jane lage.
 aazaadee se do saal pehle 1945 main pehali martabaa punjab mail main vaataanukoolit bogiyon kaa samavesh hua. 1947 main swatantraaa ke baad se yah train mumbai ke chhatrapati shivaji terminus se punjab ke ferozepur ke bich chal rahi he. aaj 24 bogiyon vaali is train main asi ke saath saamaanya or sleeper class kii bogiyaan bhi lagati hai. ab iskaa ek taraf kaa safar 1,930 kilometre kaa he.
