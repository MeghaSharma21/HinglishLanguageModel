 bhagdad bheed prabandhan kii asafalataa yaa abhaav kii sthiti main paidaa hui maanav nirmit aapadaa he. yah praayah bheed bhare ilaako main kisi afvaah ke kaaran bhi paidaa hoti he. iske alaava prakritik aapadaaen evam prashasnic avyavashtha ke kaaran bhi yah aapadaa paidaa hoti he. isamen sampatti se adhik jaan kii kshati hone kii sambhaavna rahati he. isamen bheed achaanak kisi afvaah, ashanka yaa bhay ke kaaran teji se ek taraf bhaagane lagati he jiske kaaran praayah balak, vruddh evam striyaan bhaagate logon ke pairon ke neeche aakar kuchle jate hai. logon kii bheed main dabane se maut tak ho jaati he.
