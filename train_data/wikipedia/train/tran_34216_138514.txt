 panchkuta rajasthan main prachlit ek pramukh khaadya he. vastav main yah rajasthan main pai jane vaali ponch vanaspatiyon se banaai gayi ek sabji he. yah vanaspatiyaan nimna hai 
 rajasthan kii jalwaayu ke anusaar yahan registan main pai jane vaali prajatiyon se banaai gai is sabji ko antarrashtriya khyaati praapt he. badi badi panch sitara hotalon main ise parosa jaataa he.
 in vanaspatiyon kaa vivaran nimnanusaar he
 kair
 yah chote ber kii akruti kaa fal he jo haraa hi ped se tod kar sukha liya jaataa he. yah kantedaar choti jhaadi par ugta he jiskaa naam caparis desidua he. yah jangalon main bahutayat se paaya jaataa he. sangari iskaa ped kaaphi bada hota he or isaki lambi faliyaan hoti hai. iskaa naam prosopis sinareria he jise hindi main kheddi kehete hai.is ped kaa registani ilaake main bahut mahatv he. isaki faliyon ko tod kar sukha liya jaataa he jise sangari kehete hai.
 .kumatia
 yah akesia senegal naamak ped kii faliyon ke beej hote hai. iskaa ped bhi jangalon main bahutayat se paaya jaataa he.
 gondiskaa ped kaaphi bada hota he tathaa ek hi ped par yah prachur maatr main lagate hai, inkaa aakaar choti kaanch kii goli jitna hota he. iskaa aachaar bahut swaadisht hota he. iskaa naam cordia mixa he.
 laal mirch
 hari mirch ko tod kar sukha liya jaataa he tathaa chatnee aadi ke liye kaam main liya jaataa he.
 vidhi :-uparokt pancho subjiyan bazaar main sukhi milti hai, agar aap chahen to mausam main jab iskaa fal vrikshon par lagata he aap kisi bhi khet main se ise tod kar ghar par sukha sakate hai. pehle ise raat bhar pani main bhigo diya jaataa he or subah achche tel masaale main chonk liya jaataa he. yah sabji kaaphi din tak karaab bhi nahin hoti.
