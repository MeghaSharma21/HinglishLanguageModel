 hellen sharman ek british rasayanajna he jo 1991 main mir antariksh stesan kaa daura karne vaali pehali british antariksh yatri or pehali mahila thi. sharman kaa janm grenosaida, shefield main hua tha, jahaan unhone greenosaida junior or shishu vidhyalay se shiksha praapt kii. jordanthrope compreephes main padhai karne ke baad, unhone 1984 main shefield vishvavidhyalay main rasayan vigyaan main biessi or landan ke uniwarsity of barkabic se piechdi praapt kii. vah landan main jiisi ke liye ek shodh or vikaas technologist ke rup main kaam karti thim or baad main mangal incorporated ke liye ek rasayanajna ke rup main chocolate ke swaadisht gunon ke saath kaam karti rahi. 
 sharman ne apane mishan ke baad aath varshon tak mir ko swa-rojgaar, janata ko vigyaan ke saath samvaad karne ke liye kharch kiya. 1997 main unhone bacchon kii kitaab, da spes place prakashit kiya. unhone bibisi skulon ke liye radio or tivi kaarykram prastut kiye hai. unhone 2011 tak rashtriya bhautik prayogshaala main sataha ke samooh netaa or nanonalisis samooh ke rup main kaam kiya. sharman 2015 main imperial college landan main rasayan vigyaan vibhaag kii oparation manager ban gai. vah rasayan vigyaan or uske antariksh yaan se sambandhit gatividhiyon tak pahunch gai, or unhen 2015 main british science association se sammanit faillowship se sammanit kiya gaya.
