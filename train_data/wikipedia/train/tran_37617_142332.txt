 biske kii khadi yaa biskai khadi purvottaree andh mahaasaagar kii ek khadi he jo keltic sagar se dakshin main sthit he. yah france or spain ke pashchimi chor ke kuch bhaag ke saath satee hui he. iskaa naam shaayad spain ke bask pradesh va pados ke france ke kshetron main boli jane vaali bask bhashaa se liya gaya he or 'do bandargahon vaali khadi' kaa arth rakhata he.
