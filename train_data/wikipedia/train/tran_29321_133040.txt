 shanivaar, 13 sitambar kii shaam dilli main teen sthanon par 30 minute ke antaraal par ek ke baad ek chaar bam visphot hue. inamen 19 logon ke mrityu or 90 se adhik ke ghayal hone kii soochna he. police ne dilli ke kai ilaakon main jinda bam baraamad kiye hai. do jinda bam gaffaar market main, ek legal building main or ek kanat place par baraamad hue hai. in sabhi ko niskriy karne kii prakriya jari he.
 samaacharon main kaha gaya he ki dhamaakon se pehle indian mujahidin kii ore se ek i-mail bhi bheja gaya tha. is i-mail main kaha gaya tha ki paanch minute main dilli main dhamaake hone vaale hai, roak sako to roak lo. haalanki police kaa kahana he ki bina jaanch kiye is dhamaake ke liye kisi sanghathan ko jaldbaaji main jimmedaar nahin tehraaya ja saktaa. sabhi dhamaake ghani aabaadi vaale sthanon par hue hai. aatankiyon ne dilli kaa dil kahe jane vaale kanat place main dhamaakaa kiya. saath hi, karol baag ilaake main gaffaar market or greater kailash-1 main bhi bam visphot kiye gaye. in teenon ilaakon main subah se hi der raat tak kaaphi bheed rahati he. 
 pehla dhamaakaa karol baag ilaake main electranik saamaan ke liye mashhoor gaffaar market main shaam 6.10 baje hua. ashanka he ki visphotak ek aato main rakhaa tha. is dhamaake main kam se kam 20 log jakhmi hue or kai gaadiyaan kshatigrast ho gai. ghayalon ko paas ke raamamanohar lohia aspataal main bhartee karaayaa gaya. kuch hi der baad kanat place main do dhamaake hue. inamen das logon ke ghayal hone kii khabar he. do or dhamaake greater kailash-1 ke em blaak main hue. yahaan bataaya jaataa he ki visphotak koodaydaan main rakhe gaye the. dhamaakon ke baad dilli main suraksha bandobast badha die gaye hai. iske alaava padosi rajya utttar pradesh, haryana or punjab main bhi alert ghoshit kar diya gaya he.
