 madhyam duuri parmaanu shakti sandhi; Intermediate Range Nuclear ForcesTreaty: sanyukt rajya america or tatkaaleen soviet sangh ne madhyam duuri ke parmaanu prakshepastron ko samaapt karne keliye 8 disambar, 1987 ko madhyam duuri parmaanu shakti sandhi par hastakshar kiye. yah samjhauta hathiyaar niyantranpar chah varshon tak chali vaartaon kaa parinaam tha. 1989 kii ghatnaon jinamenwarsaw sandhi kii samaapti orsoviet rus kaa vighatan bhismmilit hai ke baad iarenef kaa samrik mahatv naganya ho gaya he. warsaw sandhi kii samaapti ke kaaran madhya europe se senaaon kaa ekapaksheeya palaayan ho gaya. atah samudri prakshepastrontthaa peroling prakshepastron ke failaav kaa koiauchity nahin rah gaya. iarenef kaa lakshya inhenprakshepastron par niyantran rakhanath. phir bhi gorbachev kaal ketanav shithily main ek sanketak tathaa on- site nirikshan pranali ke suzav kii disha main is sandhi kaa kuch mahatv he.
