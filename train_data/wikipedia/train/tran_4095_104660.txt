  Param Vishisht Seva Medal
 general bipin chandra joshi, pivisaim, avisem, edisi bhartiya sena ke 17 wein chif of army staff the. unka janm uttarakhand ke pithauragarh main hua tha.
 
 general bisi joshi vishisht seva ke liye param vishisht seva padak ,ati vishisht seva padak praptakarta rahe hai.
 general bisi joshi 1 9 november 1 99 4 ko nai dilli sainya aspataal main hryday aghat se mrityu ho gai. ve 1 99 5 main sevanivrutt hone vaale the. vah bhartiya sena kaa ekmaatr adhyaksh hai jo apane kaarykaal main main mrityu ko praapt hue .
 unke sammaan main ,general bisi joshi army public skool kii sthapana 1 99 3 main pithauragarh, uttarakhand main hui thi.
