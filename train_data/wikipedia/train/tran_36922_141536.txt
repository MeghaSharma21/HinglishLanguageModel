 mahaan vibhaajak parvatamala kaa ek drishy
 mahaan vibhaajak parvatamala yaa purvi oochchabhumi australia kii sabase badi parvatamala he or duniya kii teesri sabase lambi gair-samudri parvatamala he. yah purvottar queenzland rajya ke tat se pare sthit dallan dweep se lekar dakshin disha main 3,500 kimi tak new south velz or victoria rajyon tak chalati he. mahaan vibhajak parvatamala kii chaudaai bhinn sthanon main 150 kimi se 300 kimi ke bich he. australia kii kai nadiyaan isi parvatamala se utpann hoti hai, jisamen darling nadi bhi shaamil he. isaki kai upashrunkhalaayen hai, jinamein snoi pahadiyaan ek he. australia kaa sabase buland pahaad, 2,228 meter unchaa kosiosco parvat, isi snoi shrunkhlaa main sthit he.
