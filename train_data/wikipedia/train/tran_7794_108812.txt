 pyroxin khanijon kaa ek mahatvapurn samooh he jo shail-nirmaan karne vaale inosilicket hote hai. ye bahut si aagney evam roopaantarit shailon main paye jate hai.
 khanij vigyaan main kuch shail banaanevaale khanijon kaa rasinic sanghathan lagbhag ek sa haata he or ye anfibol samooh ke khanijon se bahut milte julate hai. anfibol or pyroxin samooh ke khanijon main vidalan, crystalia rup ityadi main kuch antar bhi he. kuch main sodium or potacium ke silicket bhi paae jate hai. felsapaar ke baad ye aagney shailon ke sabase adhik maatra main paae janevaale avyav hai. baisalt, gabro, dolaraite ityadi pyroxin samooh se bane hai. ye ksharak gunwale padaarth hai or inke crystal prizmiya aakaar ke haate hai. inkaa vidaran kone 87 degree haata he. rasayan kii drushti se: ye sabhi praaia: metacilicket hote hai. crystalon kii samamiti ke aadhaar par inkaa teen vibhinn shreniyon main vibhajit kiya gaya he:
 pyroxin ke crystal chote hote hai tathaa inke siron par do falak hote hai. inkaa rang kaala, haraa yaa bhura, chamak adhatvik, vidar shankhabh, kathorta paanch se saat tak tathaa aapekshik ghanatv 3.2 se 3.5 tak hota he. kuch samay pashchaat inaki chamak kam ho jaati he.
 clinoenstatite or eknataksh metacilicket ulka main bhi paae gaye hai. magnesium metacilicket kaa nirmaan rasayanashalaon main hi ho sakaa he. pyroxinwaali shilaayein anek deshon, jaise turki, tibvat, burma or bharat main pai jaati hai. ye shilaayein sarvapratham bharat hi mili thim. dakshin bharat main chennai ke nikat pallaavarm main saranokite kii sundar pahadiyaan hai. jis shail main brihapersthin hote hai use 'charanokite' bhi kehete hai.
 pyroxin khanijon se bani chattaanon ko pyroxinite bhi kehete hai. ye shail hornblendit se milte julate he, jinamein pradhanthya harnbland hote hai.
