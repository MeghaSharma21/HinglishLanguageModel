 tipu kaa sammer peles, bharat ke shahar bangalore ke qile main ek mahal he jise mysore ke sultan haidar ali ne banvaanaa shuru kiya or tipu sultan ne usko 1791 main puraa kiya. tipu sultan kii mrityu ke baad british sarkaar ke haathon main yah mahal aayaa. aaj kal ise karnataka sarkaar dekh bhaal kar rahi he. yah paryaatkon or sandarshakon se bharaa rahata he. yah mahal shahar bangalore ke bich qile ke andar he, jo kalasipalem bas stand e qareeb he.
 is mahal main rakhe gayi chitra main farse bhasha main likhe kavita main iskaa varnan kiya gaya he. jis main is mahal ko "rashk-e-jananath" kaha gaya.
