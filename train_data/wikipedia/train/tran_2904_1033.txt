 indian bharat ke saarvajanik kshetra kii ek vimaan seva company he. isaki udaane IC ke naam se jaani jaati hai.
 indian airlines kii sthapana 15 june, 1953 ko sansad main rashtriyakaran ke bill dwaara hui. jiske falswarup do naye vimaan seva companiyon air india or indian airlines kaa janm hua. airline ne air nigam adhinium, 1953 rupee kii praarambhik puunji ke saath ke tahat nirdhaarit he. 32 lakh or 1 agast 1953 ko oparation shuru kar diya. yah kanoon ke baad sthapit kiya gaya tha astitv main aayaa bharat main puure airline udyog ke rashtriyakaran. do nae rashtriya airlinon ko usee ke rup main british oversij airways nigam or british airways europea ke saath united kingdom main hua linon ke saath gathan kiya gaya. air india kaa padbhaar sambhaal liya antarrashtriya margon or indian airlines nigam se adhik gharelu or kshetreey margon liya .
 saat purv swatantraaa gharelu airlines, deccan airways, bharat airways, bharat airways, himalay vimaanan kaling airlines, indian neshnal airways or air india sevaaon, gharelu nai rashtriya vaahak prapatr marz kiye gaye the. indian airlines nigam 99 74 duglas disi -3 Dakotas, 12 Vickers Vikings, saat airlinon ki ise banaaya se 3 duglas disi 4s or vibhinn prakaar sahit chote vimaan ke ek bede viraasat main mila.

 indian airlines limited purnatah ek holding company ke madhyam se bharat sarkaar dwaara swaamitv main he or march 2007 ke rup main 19,300 karmchaariyon kii he isaki vaarshik mode par., saath main uski sahaayak company elliance air kii us ke saath, par acchhee tarah se he Rs.4000 karod rupye . apni sahaayak company he, elliance air, indian airlines ke saath milakar 7.5 karod yatriyon kii kul salanaa vahan karti he .
 disambar 2007 main, air india ko star elliance main shaamil hone ke liye aamantrit kiya gaya tha. ke baad se indian airlines air india ke saath vilay ke bich main he, yah bahut prabhavi dhang se ek sadsy honge.

 vimaan poshaak istemaal kiya jabaki company kaha jaataa tha indian airlines samay ke sandarbh main ek lambey samay tak kii gai thi. iske mukhya rup se vimaan safed the. pet prakaash dhaatu grey main tha. windo ke upar, "indian airlines" angreji main ek taraf or duusare par hindi par likhaa gaya tha. poonch ujjwal naarangi safed main apane logo ke saath tha. main vimaan ke sabase adhik he, logo bhi apane nange dhaatu rang ke upar ingine par chitrit kiya gaya tha. iske alaava, jab company indian airlines ke sheershak ke tahat kiya gaya, ke liye apni seva airline naaraa "sone main" unke vimaan ke kai par udaan ke 50 saal daal ke 50 wein saal manaate hai.
 naam bhartiya, company ke vimaan ko badalane ke baad ek nayaa konark surya mandir se urisa main prerit dekho khel rahaa tha. unke vimaan kii poonch ke shesh 3 / 4 vyaavhaarik rup se ek aanshik nila chakra kii thi kaat rahaa he. pahiyaa vaahak naam ke saath ek naarangi prishthbhumi par he, "hawaii jahaaz kaa dhaancha ke ek taraf angreji main bhartiya" likhaa he or duusare par hindi main.
