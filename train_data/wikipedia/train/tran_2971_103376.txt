 sikihor dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh ke visaaya dweep dal ke madhya visaaya prashasnic kshetra main ek dweep va praant he. prashasnic rup se praant main sikihor ke mukhya dweep ke alaava aasapaas ke anya chote dweep bhi sammilit hai. sikihor se pashchimottar main sibu or negros dweep, purvottar main bohol dweep or dakshin main bohol sagar ke paar mindanaao he.
