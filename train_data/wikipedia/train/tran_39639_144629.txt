 raav bahadur "dharmpraworth" gubbi totdappa, ek bhartiya vyapaaree or paropkaaree the. unhone desh bhar ke paryatakon ke liye "totdappa chhatra" naamak ek muft awaas sthaan kii sthapana kii. unhen british sarkaar dwaara "raav bahadur" or mysore ke maharaja krishnaraj wodeyar chaturth dwaara "dharmpraworth" sheershak se sammanit kiya tha.
 totdappa kaa janm 1838 main gubbi ke lingayat parivaar main hua tha. baad ke varshon main unka parivaar bengalooru chale gayi jahaan unhone mamulpet main apana vyapaar shuru kiya.
 apane bachche naa hone ke kaaran, totdappa ne apni sari sampatti kaa upyog paryatakon or chaatro ke laabh ke liye karne kaa nirnay liya. unhone raav bahadur dharmpraworth gubbi totdappa charity naamak trust kii sthapana kii. 1897 main, trust ne bengalooru city railway stesan ke paas jameen kaa ek tukdaa kharida or 11 farvari 1903 ko krishnaraj wodeyar chaturth ne aadhikarik taur par dharmachatra or muft chatravaas kholaa. apane aakhri dinon ke dauraan unhone apni sari sampatti aar bi di ji tii si trust ko daan kar di or ke pi puttana cheti ko us trust ke pehle adyaksh ke rup main niyukt kiya. trust aaj bhi apana kaam jari rakhata he. yah chatravaas suvidha puure karnataka main vistaarit kii gai thi. varsh 2005 main, chatravaas kaa punarnirmaan kiya gaya. apane shatabdi ke liye, trust ne cempegoud bas stesan par bell hotel ko aay ke ek strot ke rup main banaaya. awaas suvidha ek maamuli dar par awaas pradaan karti he or sabhi ke liye khuli hoti he, chaahe vyakti kaa dharm kuch bhi ho. chatravaas kaa upyog virshaiv samudaay se sambandhit chaatro ke liye vishesh he. ab tak chatravaas main sarkari anudaan praapt nahin hua he. har varsh lingayat dharm ke vidhyaarthiyon ko yogyata ke liye trust puraskaar detaa he.
 21 farvari 1910 ko totdappa kii 72 varsh kii aayu main mrityu ho gai.
