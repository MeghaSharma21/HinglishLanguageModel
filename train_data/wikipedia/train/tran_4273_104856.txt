 westeindies cricket team sitambar 2017 main tufaan irma or tufaan maaria dwaara kshatigrast stadimon ke liye dhan jutaney ke liye 31 may 2018 ko england main baaki vishwa ilevan team ke khilaaf twanti-20 international khelnaa he. kshatigrast stadium anguila or dominica ke windsar park main ronaald webster park the.
 antarrashtriya cricket parishad ne sthirataa antarrashtriya sthiti pradaan kii, match kii mejabani ke liye lords cricket ground ko sthaan ke rup main chunaa ja rahaa he. march 2018 main, isisi ne eon morgan ko vishwa ilevan team ke captain ke rup main namit kiya. agle maheene, carlos brethwate ko westeindies team ke captain ke rup main namit kiya gaya tha. haalanki, match se do din pehle, morgan ko ek fracture ungali ke saath sthirataa se baahar kar diya gaya tha. sam billings ne morgan ko team main badal diya or shahid afridi ko vishwa ilevan team ke captain ke rup main namit kiya gaya.
 shaakib al hasan mul rup se baaki vishwa team main namit kiya gaya tha, lekin vyaktigat kaarnon se waapas le liya gaya or sandip lamichhen ke saath badal diya gaya. hardik pandya ko viral beemaari ke saath match se baahar kar diya gaya tha, or mohammad shami ne unhen badal diya tha.
