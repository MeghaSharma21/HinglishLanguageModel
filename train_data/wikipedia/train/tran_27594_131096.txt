 shaikh rajjak ali ek bangladeshi rajaneta, up kanoon mantri or bangladeshi sansad ke upaadhyaksh va do baar ke adhyaksh the. 7 june 2015 ko unka nidhan ho gaya.
 unka janm, 1928 main khulanaa jila ke paikagachha ke hitampur gaanv main hua tha. or unhone dhaka vishvavidhyalay se 1952 me arthshaastra main eme kii digari praapt kii or baad main usee vishvavidhyalay se bengali sahitya main eme bhi kiya, or phir vahiin se elelbi bhi 1954 main kiya.
 unhone bangladeshi swatantraaa sangram main rakruy bhagidaari dithi. or 1952 ke bhashaa andolan, 1969 ke jan andolan or 1971 kii bangladesh mukti yuddh main jam kar hissaa liya tha. saath hi 1982-90 ke ektantr ke khilaaf andolan membhi ve sakriy the.
