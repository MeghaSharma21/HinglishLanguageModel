 maharana kumbha ne lagbhag 50 varsh tak shashan kiya. durbhagyavash use usee ke bete uuda singh ne, jo yuvaraj bhi tha tathaa raja banane kii lambe samay se pratiksha kar rahaa tha, unki burj se dhakka dekar hatya kar di. use yah bhi dar tha ki kahi maharana kumbha apni priy choti rani ke prabhaav main aakar rana rimal ko raja na bana de.
