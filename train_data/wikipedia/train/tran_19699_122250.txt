 manajit indra  punjabi kavi or lekhak he.punjab ke vibhinn sarkari collejon main punjab main lekturer ke rup main seva karne ke baad, vah vartmaan main chandigarh main punjab main ucch shiksha nideshaalay main sahaayak nideshak ke rup main kaam kar rahi he.
 manajit indira kaa janm sa. harbhajan singh kalsi ke ghar 24 farvari1950 ko huya tha. usne em-e, or emphil tak vidya praapt kii. manajit indira apana kalam tarannum main pesh karne kii kabaliyat bhi rakhti he.
