 pasific rim prashanth mahaasaagar ke chaaron taraf kaa bhoomi kaa gheraa he. prashanth ghati main prashanth ghere or prashanth mahaasaagar ke dweep shaamil hai. prashanth agni vrutt kii bhugolic sthiti or prashanth gheraa ek duusare par ativyapt hai.
 yah suchi un deshon kii he jo prashanth ghere main gine jate hai or prashanth mahaasaagar main hai.
 
 
 
 
 
 prashanth videshi vyapaar kaa ek bahut bada kendra he. dubai ke jebel ali bandargah ko chhodkar sabase 10 vyastatam bandargah ghere deshon main sthit hai. vishwa ke 50 sabase vyastatam bandargah:
 vibhinn antarasarkari or gaiyr sarkari sanghathan prashanth ghere par kendrit hai jinamein asia - prashanth mahasagariya aarthik sahyog, da east-west centre, sasteneble pacific rim cities or da institute of asian research shaamil hai. iske atirikt, rim of da pacific eksrasij naval escorsise united stats pasific command dwaara samanvit hai.
