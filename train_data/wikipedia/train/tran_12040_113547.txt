 samhita hindu dharm ke pavithratam or sarvocch dharmagranth vedon kaa mantra vaala khand he. ye vaidic vaanmay kaa pehla hissaa he jisamen kavy rup main devataaon kii yajna ke liye stuti kii gayi he. inaki bhashaa vaidic sanskrit he. chaar veda hone kii vajah se chaar samhitaayein hai :
 bhashavid or itihaaskaar rugveda samhita ko puure vishwa ke sabase pracheen granthon main se ek maanate hai.
