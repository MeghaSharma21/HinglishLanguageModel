 swanvigyaan or swanimavigyaan main ghosh vah dhvaniyaan hoti hai jinamein swar-rajju main kampan hota he, jabaki aghosh vah dhvaniyaan hoti hai jinamein yah kampan nahin hota. udhaaharan ke liye "pa" ek aghosh dhwani he jabaki "ba" ek ghosh dhwani he. isi tarah "sa" or "sha" donon aghosh he, jabaki "za" ghosh he. devanagari ke har niyamit varg main pehle do varna aghosh or un ke baad ke do ghosh hote hai. k/kha, c/chha, t/tha, t/tha, pa/pha aghosh hai, jabaki ga/gha, ja/jha, da/dha, da/dha, ba/bha ghosh hai.
