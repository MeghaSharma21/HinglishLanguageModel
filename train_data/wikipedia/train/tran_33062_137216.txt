 majhagaanv durg bharat ke rajya maharashtra main sthit ek durg he jiskaa nirmaan tatkaaleen bombai main 1680 main hua tha. yah kila vartmaan ke yusuf baptista garden main, bhandarwara pahari par dockyard road railway stesan ke baahar sthit he.
 is kile par june 1690 main siddi general yakuut khan ne akraman kiya tha. atharahavin sadi tak, bombai kai chote dwipon main shaamil the. portugaliyon dwaara 1661 main maharashtra ke 7 dweep england ke raja charles dwitiya se portugali raja braganja kii rajkumaari catherin se vivaah kiya tha to dahej ke rup main diye gaye the. yah bandargah utkrisht evam sarvatha yogya tha, atah british ne surat se apana bandargah yahan laane kii yojana banaai. tab africa se aaye siddiyon ke mughalon se achche sambandh the va unki nausena bhi acchhee thi. tab east india company se british or mogul ke bich lagaataar yuddh jari tha. mughalon ke saath achche sambandh hone se siddiyon ne bhi british company se shatruta banaaye rakhi. 1672 main siddiyon dwaara kiye gaye kai nirdayee hamalo ke baad, angrezon ne kai jagah se kilabandi kii, or 1680 main siwari kile kaa nirmaan purna ho jane par vahaan sthanantarit ho gayi.
 yah durg bhi purvi samudr tat par najar rakhe hue majhagaanv dweep par hi sthit tha . 1689 main siddi general yakuut khan ne 20,000 kii paidal sena ke saath bombai presidensi par akraman kiya. inke pehle bede ne severy kile par adhikaar kar liya, phir majhagaanv kila or phir mahim par bhi. 1689 main siddiyon ne britishon kii dakshin kilabandi ko gher liya. vahaan ke british rajyapal sar jon child ne mughal badshaah aurangzeb se samjhauta kiya va ek nishchit raashi ke evaj main sakat par adhikaar karne ko raji kar liya. is par farvari 1690 main aurangzeb sahamat ho gaya or 1.5 lakh rupee kii keemat tay hui, saath hi child ko naukari se alag karne kaa bhi nirnay liya. child kii 1690 main achaanak mrityu ho jane se vah naukari chhodane kii badanaami se batch gaya. vastu vinimay par gusse main aakar, majhagaanv kila giraane ke baad, 8 june 1690 ko sakat ne apni sena vaapis le lee. 1884 main britishon ne bhandarwaad pahari par ek bada jalashay viksit kiya. aaj yahi jalashay yah dakshin or madhya mumbai ko pani kii aapurti karata he. yahaan kii jameen ke do bhaagon main se ek lokapriy yusuf baptista ke naam par he or duusari swatantraaa senaanee or bhartiya swatantraaa andolan ke kaaryakarta bal gangadhar tilak ke naam par.
