 ramai kaaka avadhi ke prasiddh sahityakar the. unka vaastavik naam 'chandrabhushan trivedi' tha. aap baisawadi avadhi ke uttam hasya kavi the.
 inkaa janm san 1915 main uttarapradesh ke unnav jile ke rawatpur naamak gaanv main hua tha. aapane akashwani lakhanu-elahabaad main san 1940 se 1975 tak kaam kiya tha.
 aapke samakaleen kaviyon main vanshidhar shukla or mrigesh kaa naam aata he.
 inaki prakashit pustakon ke naam hai:
 bauchhaar, bhinsaar, netaji, phuhaar, harpati tarwaar, gulchharre hasya ke chhinte or maati ke mol.
