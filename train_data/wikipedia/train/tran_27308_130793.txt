 pracheen bhartiya rajvansh | agreyavanshi kshatriya hi vartmaan main agarwal naam se jane jate hai. inaki ek shakha rajwanshi bhi kehalaati he. unani badshaah sikandar ke akraman ke falswaroop agreyaganrajya kaa patan ho gaya or adhikansh agreyavir veergati ko praapt ho gayi. bache huve agreyavanshi agroha se nishkraman kar sudur bharat main fail gayi or aajeevika ke liye talwaar chhod taraazoo pakad lee. aaj is samudaay ke bahusankhya log vaanijya vyavsaay se jude huve hai or inaki gananaa vishwa ke saphaltam udhyami samudaayon main hoti he. pichley do hajaar varshon se inaki aajeevika kaa aadhaar vaanijya hone se inaki gananaa kshatriya varna hone ke baavjood vaisya varg main hoti he or swayan agarwal samaaj ke log apane aap ko vaisya samudaay kaa ek abhinn ang maanate hai. inke 18 gotra hai. sansthaapak : maharaja agrasenvansh : suryvansh evam nagvansha gaddi : agroha,pravar : panchpravar,kuladevi: mahalakshami,gotra : 18 :-garg, goyan,goyal, kansal, bansal, sinhal, mittal, jindal, bindal, nagal, kuchchhal, bhandal, dhaaran, tayal, tingal, airan, madhukul, mangal|
