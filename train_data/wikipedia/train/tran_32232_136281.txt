 siddharth mahadevan ek bhartiya parshvagayak or sangeet rachanaakaar he jo bhaag milkha bhaag ke zindaa naamak gane se kaaphi lokapriy hue kyonki wo gane inhone hi gaaya tha. saath hi bhaag milkha bhaag kaa tital gaanaa bhi gaaya tha. siddharth ne hindi hi nahin apitu marathi cinema main bhi kaam kiya he.
 siddharth mahadevan ke pita shankar mahadevan he jo ki khud ek hindi parshvagayak he.
