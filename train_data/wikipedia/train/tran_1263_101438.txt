 mirinda ek sheethal peya kaa utpaada he jo pehali baar spain main 1959 main banaaya gaya tha. "mirinda" shabd kaa asperanto bhashaa main arth "prashansaneey" yaa "saraahneey" hota he. yah naarangi, neembu, angoor, seb, strawberry, raspberi, ananas, anaar, kelaa, hibiscus, gorana, keenu, ke saath-saath imali sahit kai falon ke kismon main uplabdh he. 1970 se mirinda ko pepsico sanchalit kar rahaa he arthath pepsico mirinda kaa maalik hai. 
 bharat main garmi kaa mausam aate hi television par iske vigyaapan shuru hote jate hai. bhartiya vigyapanon main asin mukhya bhumika nibhaati he tathaa yah vigyaapan "paagalpanti bhi jaruri" he sheershak se chalata he. mirinda kii bharat main acchhee bikri hoti he. bhartiya bazaar main coca cola dwaara nirmit utpaada "phanta" mirinda kaa pramukh pratiyogi he. bharat main mirinda ke sabase lokapriy swaad "naarangi" or "lim" he.
