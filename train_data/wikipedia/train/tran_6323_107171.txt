 andies duniya kii sabase lambi parvat shrunkhlaa he jo dakshin america ke pashchimi tat par sthit he. kul milakar yah parvatamala 7,000 kimi tak chalati he or lagbhag 200 kimi kii ausat chaudaai rakhti he. is parvatamala kii ausat oonchaai 4,000 meter he. andes dakshin america ke saat deshon - argentina, chile, bolivia, peru, ecuador or venezuela - se guzarati he, lekin chile main iskaa vistaar sabase adhik he. yah parvat shrunkhlaa naveen moddar hone ke saath iskaa sambandh abhisari plate seemaant hone ke kaaran yah bukamp va jwalamukhi se prabhavit he. 
 andes parvaton main kai upashrankhlaayen shaamil hai, jinke bich main nichle ilaake sthit hai. is kshetra main kai unche pathaar bhi maujuud hai or yahan ke kai mukhya shahar - jaise ki kito, boghota, aarekipa, medeyin, laa paas, sucre or merida - inhen patharon par sthit hai. altiplano kaa pathaar naamak pathaar tibet ke pathaar ke baad vishwa kaa doosraa sabase unchaa pathaar he. titicaca jhil jaisi kai parvatiy jhilein bhi andes shrankhla main sthit hai. itihaas main andes main inkaa jaisi kai mul america samskritiyaan bhi panpi thim. chile or argentina kii antarrashtriya sarhad par khadaa 6,960.8 meter unchaa akonkagua parvat is shrankhla kaa sabase buland pahaad he or yah asia se baahar vishwa kaa sabase unchaa parvat he.
