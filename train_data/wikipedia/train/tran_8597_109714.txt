 keraly rang kalaon ko dharmik, vinodaparak, saamaajik, kayik aadi bhaagon main vibhakt kar sakate hai. dharmik kalaon main mandir kalaayen or anushthan kalaayen aati hai. mandir kalaon kii lambii suchi he. jaise - kuttu, kudiattam, kathakali, tullal, titambu nrittam, ayyapp kuttu, arjuna nrittam, andiattam, pathakam, krishnanattam, coudiattem aadi. iske antargat mohiniattem jaisaa lasya nrity bhi aata he. anushthan kalaayen bhi anek hai. jaise - taiyyam, tira, purakkali, tiyattu, mudiettu, kaliuttu, paranettu, tukkam, padhyani, kalam patte, kendron patte, gandharvan tullal, balikkala, sarpppattu, malayan kettu aadi. anushthan kalaon ke saath anushthan kala sahitya kaa bhi sthaan he.
 saamaajik kalaon main yatrakkali, erramuttikkali, margam kali, oppana aadi kii gananaa hoti he to kayik kalaon main onttallu, parichamuttukali, kalarippayttu aadi aate hai. keval manoranjan ko lakshya maanakar jo kalaayen prastut kii jaati hai ve hai - kakkarishshi naatak, porattukali, tolppavakkuttu, naninmelkahai aadi. inke atirikt kerala main aadhunik lokapriy kalaon kaa bhi vikaas hua he. ye hai - aadhunik naatak, chalchitr, kathaprasangam, ganotsav, mimicri aadi. 
 anushthan kalaon main kai natya rup hai. kuch aise lokanaty bhi hai jo anusthanparak na hokar keval manovinod ke liye hai. udaahranarth - kurattiattem, porattunatak, kakkarishshi naatak aadi. porattu ke bhed hai pankali, aryammala aadi. isi tarah mutiyettu, ayyapp kuttu, taiyyam aadi anusthanparak lokanaty hai. kotamuriattem naatak main anushthan kala kaa ansh bahut hi kam he.
 uttari kerala ke parvatiy lokajanon main prachlit sitakkali, pattanantitta ke malwaitor janajati main prachlit poramati, vaynad ke adivasee kii gaddika, kulliattu, vellattu aadi anushthan par kalaayen lokanaty kii shrenee main aate hai. isi shrenee antargat aane vaale duusare lokanaty hai - kanyar kali, putamakali, kummatti, avernatak, kutirakkali, vannanakuttu, malayikkuttu aadi.
