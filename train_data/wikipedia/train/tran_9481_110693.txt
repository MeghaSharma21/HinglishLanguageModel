 gamma valoram, jiske bair namakan main bhi yahi naam darj he, akaash main paal taramandal main sthit ek paanch taron kaa mandal he jo ek-duusare se gurutvkarshak bandhan rakhte hai. iskaa mukhya tara, jise "γ valoram e" yaa "γ2 valoram" kehete hai, vastav main ek dwitara he jiskaa ek tara ek nila mahadaanav tara he or doosraa ek bahut hi roshan vulf-rayet tara he. prithvi se iskaa ausat saapeksh kantimaan 1.78 he or yah prithvi ke akaash main dikhne vaale taron main se 34waan sab se roshan tara he. agar gamma valoram ke sabhi taron ko ikaitha dekha jaae to inaki mili-juli chamak 1.7 magnitude he. dhyaan rahe ke khagoleey magnitude ek vipareet maap he or yah jitna kam ho chamak utani hi zyaada hoti he.
 gamma valoram ko angrezi main "ragar" kehete hai. yah naam khalaabaaz gas grissom ne mazaaq ke taur par apane sahyatri roger chaiphi kaa pehla naam ulta karke banaaya tha, lekin prachlit ho gaya. ise arabi main "suhail al-muhalif" bhi kehete hai, jiskaa arth he "qasam kaa tejasvi ".
