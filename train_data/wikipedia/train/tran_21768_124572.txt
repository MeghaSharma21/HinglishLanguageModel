 mizo purvottaree bharat, pashchimi burma va purvi bangladesh main jade rakhane vaala ek jaateey samooh he. ve mizo bhashaa or kuki bhashaa-parivaar kii kuch anya bhashaaon ke maatrubhaashi hai. vartmaankaal main mizo samudaay purna bharat ke lagbhag sabhi mukhya nagaron main paye jate hai. adhikansh mizo isai mat ke anuyaayi hai haalanki unki kuch paramparaagat aasthaaen bhi hai. mizo logon kaa sakshartaa dar 91% he, jo bharat ke sabhi samudaayon main sabase unchi shrenee par he.
 "mizo" shabd mizo bhashaa ke do shabdon ko milakar bana he. "mizo" main "mi" kaa arth "vyakti" yaa "log" he. "zo" shabd ko lekar vivaad he. kuch ke anusaar iskaa matlab "uchee bhoomi" he - yadi yah sahi he to "mizo" kaa arth "pahaad ke log" he. itihaaskaar lalathangliana dwaara prastut ek anya mat ke anusaar "zo" kaa matlab "thanda pradesh" he - yani "mizo" kaa arth "thandi jagah vaale log" ho saktaa he.
 mizo samudaay kii kai paramparaagat shakhaayen maani jaati hai jinamein lushai, maaraa, lai, mhaar, paavi or gangte shaamil hai.
