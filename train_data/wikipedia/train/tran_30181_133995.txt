 dow shurhojeli liyojitsu bharat ke uttar-purv main avasthit rajya nagaland se ek bhartiya raajnneetijna evam nagaland piples front ke adhyaksh hai. ve nagaland ke gyarahawein mukhyamantri ke rup main apni sevaayen de chuke hai.
 19 farvari, 2017 ko sthaneey nikaay chunaavon main mahila aarakshan kii maang par jari andolan ke bich tii aar jeliang ne mukhyamantri pad se istipha de diya tha. uske pashchaat 22 farvari, 2017 ko nagaland piples front ke adhyaksh shurhojeli ne rajya ke nae mukhyamantri ke rup main shapath grahan kiya. ek baar phir unhone 19 july 2017 ko apana mukhyamantri pad se tyaagptr de diya tathaa tii aar jeliang punah mukhyamantri bane.
