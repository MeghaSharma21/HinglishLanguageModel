 coromandal tat bhartiya prayadweep kii dakshin-purvi tatarekha ko diya gaya naam he. yah kshetra chol rajvansh dwaara shasit rahaa he. chol kshetra yaa mandal ko tamil main chol-mandalam kehete the. vahiin se portugaliyon evam french logon ne bigaad kar kormandal naam nikaala he. ek anya shodh ke anusaar chol kshetra ke tateey kshetra ko cholmandalam kehete the, jo badal kar kormandal ban gaya..
 tamil main ise cholon dwaara cholamandalam arthath chol raajaaon kaa ilaaka kaha jaataa tha. isi se portugali yatriyon ne naam ko bigaad kar vartmaan kaa coromandal kar diya.
