 shamashaad begum ek bhartiya saamaajik kaaryakarta he, jise chhattisgarh ke pichde virgon kii shiksha ke liye anusuchit jati, anusuchit janajati or anya pichde virgon main shiksha ke liye janaa jaataa he. unhen bharat sarkaar dwaara 2012 main, padam shri ke chauthe ucchtam bhartiya naagarik puraskaar ke saath sammanit kiya gaya tha.
 shamashaad begum kaa janm bhartiya rajya chhattisgarh main baalod jile main hua tha. unhone bharat sarkaar ke duvaara chalaae rashtriya sakshartaa mishan kaarykram main hissaa liya or yah bataaya gaya he ki 1995 main gandardehi main mishan gatividhiyon ko shuru karne ke chah maheene ke bheetar, begum or unke sahyogiyon ne 12269 mahilaon ko sakshar banane main sakasham banaaya, kul 18265 ashikshit mahilaon main se.
 shamsaad begum janakalen samiti ke saath judy hue hai jo ek saamaajik kalyaan samooh, mahilaon or bacchon kii shiksha or kalyaan gatividhiyon main shaamil he.
 shamsaad begum ko 2012 main bharat sarkaar ne padam shri ke puraskaar se sammanit kiya tha.
