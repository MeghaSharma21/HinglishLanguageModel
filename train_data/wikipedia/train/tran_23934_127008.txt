 elefgeria i phanatos yunaan kaa rashtriya naaraa he, jiskaa arth he "azaadi yaa maut". yah sabase pehle 1820 i ke dashak main osmani samrajya ke viruddh lade gaye unani swatantraaa sangram main istemaal hona shuru hua. is naare main nau shabdaansh hai jinase prerit hokar unani dhvaj par nau dhaariyaan rakhi gayi.
 dhyaan rahe ke "elefgeria i phanatos" ke jumale main prayog hone vaale 'ji' varna kaa sahi uccharan 'tha' se zaraa bhinn hota he.
