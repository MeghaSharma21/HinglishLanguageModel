  rajkeey pratibha vikaas vidhyalay  dilli main dilli sarkaar ke shiksha nideshaalay ke dwaara sanchalit ek vidyalayan tantra he. ye vidhyalay kaksha VI se XII tak kii shiksha pradaan karte hai. dilli main sarkari vidyalayon kii hameshaa se hi aalochana kii jaati rahi he lekin ab ve unnat ho rahe hai. sarkari vidyalayon main CBSE kii uteernata dar jo kii san 2005 main 76.5% thi, badhkar 2009 main 87% ho gai he, tathaa RPVV vidhyalay ab duusare vidyalayon ko maat de rahe hai.
