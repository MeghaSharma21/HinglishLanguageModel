 computer banane vaali sheersh company ibiem duniya kaa sabase tej super computer bana rahi he jo prati second main 1.6 hazaar kharab tak gananaa karne main sakasham hoga. company ne iskaa naam 'rodernar' rakhaa he or ise amareeki shahar new mexico kii los allamos prayogshaala main taiyaar kiya jayega. yah nayaa super computer maujuuda sabase shaktishaalee superkamputer bluejin/el se chaar guna adhik shaktishaalee hoga. gauratlab he ki bluejin/el ko bhi ibiem ne hi banaaya he. nae computer main paaramparik super computer procesar or soni play stesan-3 ke liye taiyaar kiye gaye cell chip procesar kaa prayog kiya jayega. yah prayogshaala amareeki urja vibhaag kii he. urja vibhaag ne jari bayaan main kaha, "is mashin kaa prayog yah sunishchit karne main kiya jayega ki amareeki parmaanu hathiyaar surakshit or vishwast hai. ibiem ne kaha he ki super computer 'petaflop speed' hasil kar sakegaa. ek petaflop kaa arth he prati second ek hazaar kharab gananaa kii kshamta. haalanki jab isaki gati charam par hogi to yah ek second main 1.6 hazaar kharab tak gananaa karne main sakasham hoga. maujuuda super computer bluejin/el kii kshamta 'teraflop speed' kii hi he. yani yah prati second 100 arab kii gananaa karne main sakasham he.
