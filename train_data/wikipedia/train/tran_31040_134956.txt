 gindad rajasthan ke shekhavati anchal kaa e prasiddh lokanrity he. iskaa aayojan holi ke parv par kiya jaataa he. yah gujarat ke garba nrity se kaaphi had tak miltaa-julata he. isame garba kii tarah hi kai purush apane dono haathon main lakdi kii dandiyaan lekar apane aage va peeche ke saathiyon kii dandiyon ke saath chot karte huegol ghere main chalate hueage badhate hai. ghere ke kendra main e unchaa machaan banaaya jaataa he. ghere ke kendra par e unchaa jhanda bhi lagaaya jaataa he jo ki machaan ke kendra se bhi nikalataa hua upar tak jaataa he. machaan par baitha hua vyakti nagaada bajaata he. ghere main andar kii khaali jagah main kuch log chang bajaate hueen vishesh prakaar loakgeet gaate hueghoomte hai. is tarah nagaade, chang, dhamal va dandiyon kii e taal main milti hui dhvaniyon se jo samaa bandhata he wo kisi ko bhi madamast karne ke liekaphi hota he or sabhi upasthit log anand se jhum oothte hai.
 gindad ko kahin-kahin ginddi ke naam se bhi pukaara jaataa he.
 garba or gindad main e mukhya bhed dandiyon kaa hota he, gindad kii dandiyaan garba ke dandiyon se aakaar main adhik lambaai liehoti hai.dusraa mukhya bhed yah hota he, garba main stri-purushon ke jode hote hai jabaki gindad main mukhya rup se purush hi bhagidaari karte hai. parantu gindad main purush hi striyon kaa yaa anya rup se swang rachakar naachte hueghoomte hai.
