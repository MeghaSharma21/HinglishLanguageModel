 kukcoureey sambhog ek british angrezi kathboli kaa shabd he jiskaa prayog saarvajanik sthano par kamottejak kriyalap yaa aisa hi arth-saarvajanik sthaan par karnaa yaa dusron ko karte dekhnaa he. is kriyaalap main do se adhik log bhi shaamil ho sakate hai; isamen samuhik sambhog or gang baing bhi ho sakate hai. jaisaaki spasht he, in kaamon main apni ore dusron kaa dhyaan aakarshith karnaa mukhya lakshya he, darshanarati or kamangpradarshan kukcoureey sambhog se jude hue hai. jo bhinn log aam taur se yaa to ittefaaq se yaa phir internet par pehle se hi sampark kar chuke hote hai.
 sitambar 2003 main bi bi si news kii soochna ke anusaar kukcoureey sambhog "nayaa junun" banataa ja rahaa he. usamen internet or paathya sandeshon ke aadhaar par bataaya gaya ki yah dono kisi niyamit mulaaqaat tay karne ke aam tareeqe banate ja rahe hai. kukcoureey sambhog kii mul paribhaasha isi sambandhit gatividhi thi â€” vah kisi yuva jode ko car yaa kisi saarvajanik sthaan par sambhog karne ke maamalon par jasusi karnaa tha. is shabd kaa britain kii rail seva main kai varshon se upyog hota aa rahaa he. yah kam se kam 1951 se jaani-pahani baat he.
 is baat ke internet par pakke pramaan mili hai ki yah "junun" kai anya deshon main failane laga he, jaise ki sanyukt rajya america, canada, australia, barbados, brazil or denmark, nederland, poland, or sweden.
 britain main kukcoureey sambhog darshanarati, kamangpradarshan yaa janata ke saamne kaam vaasana ke pradarshan ke antargat aata he; kukcoureey par vidhi ke praavdhaan aspasht hai. mukademe ko kai aparaadhon kii shrenee main chalaaya ja saktaa he jo saarvajanik vyavastha adhinium 1986 kii dhara 5 ke antargat aate hai, pradarshan ke liye yon aparaadh adhinium 2003 kii dhara 66 ke tahat aate hai, yaa phir saarvajanik shobhaneeyata bhang karnaa. 2010 ke anusaar association of cheef polis oficers kii niti ke anusaar girafatari antim chaara he or is samasya ke liye dhire-dhire qadam uthaae jane chahie.
