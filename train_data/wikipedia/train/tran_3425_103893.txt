 mahaan andamani athwa great andamani un janajaatiy logon ko samuhik rup se kehete hai jo bharat ke andaman dweepasmooh ke mahaan andaman dweep or isse sate dwipon par nivas karte hai.
 kisi samay great andamani logon kii andaman dweepasmooh main sabase adhik aabaadi thi. 1789 main unki anumaanit jansankhya 10,000 thi. varsh 1901 main inaki sankhya ghatakar 625 ho gai or 1969 me inaki sankhya ghatakar maatr 19 ho gai. varsh 1971 kii janaganana ke anusaar maatr unki sankhya 24 thi lekin varsh 1999 main unki sankhya bad kar 41 ho gai. 2010 ke anumaanit aankadon ke anusaar inaki sankhya 52 thi.
 prashasan is janajati ke sangrakshan or parirakshan ke liye bharsak prayaas kar rahaa he. is janajati ko street island naam ke ek chote se dweep main basaaya gaya. great andamani log multah ekatrak hua karte the. aajkal ve chaaval, daal, chapatti or anya aadhunik khaadya saamagree bhi khate hai. ve garm masaalon kaa upyog karke khaanaa pakaa sakate hai. lekin ab bhi ve jarurat padane par shikaar par yaa jangal or samudr tat se bhojan ekatrit karne jate hai. unke paramparaagat bhojan main machhlee, dugong, kachuu, kachuu ke ande, kekada kand mul shaamil hai. ve sukar andaman ke samudr main paae jane vaale monitor chipakali aadi tateey log hone ke kaaran ve vibhinn prakaar ke kekda or machhlee ke alaava octopus, samudri jeev jaise terban shail, scorpian shail sandiyal helment, tokes ore skray shail se nikaala gaya molses pansad karte hai. baad main kuch log subjiyon kii kheti karne lage or kukkut paalan farm kii bhi sthapana kin. ve sharaab pine kii aadat ke alaava unhen gaiyr-jan jatiy, shahari, samudaay ke sampark main aane ke baad sankramak rogon se grast hue hai.
