 banihaal darra himalay kaa ek pramukh darra hai. rashtriya rajmarg 1a enech1e is darre se hokar nikalataa he. yahi darra kashmir ghati ko jawahar surang ke madhyam se jammu ke raste shesh bharat se jodta he.
 banihaal darra himalay kii pir punjaal parvat shrinkhalaa par he, jise chirate hue bharat kii sabase badi surang ke raste, june 2013 main qaazigund se banihaal tak, rail seva shuru kar di gayi he.
