 acharya hirsurishwaraji mughal badshaah akbar ke samakaleen jain acharya the. unka janm 1527 main palanpur ke oswal parivaar main hua. ve 1554 acharya bane the. akbar se sampark ke pashchaat 60 varsh kii aayu main unhone apane vihaar kii ore prasthaan kiya. unke hastkshep ke kaaran akbar ne shriddhalu kar shatrunajay mahatirth se hataa diya tathaa paryushan parv ke dinon main jaanwaron ke vadh par roak laga di. 1596 main saurashtra ke oona gaanv main unka dehant ho gaya.
