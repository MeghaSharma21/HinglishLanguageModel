 khatushamji bhartiya rajya rajasthan ke sikar jile kaa ek mahatvapurn gaanv he. yah khatushamji ke mandir ke liye prasiddh he.
 sabase najdiki hawaii adda jaipur main he.
 khatushamji kaa mandir jo prasiddh makrana marble se bana hua he kasbe kaa sabase mahatvapurn aakarshan he. khatushamji ko kalyug ke devtaa mana jaataa he.
 yahan par mandir ke paas ek pavitra kunda he.
 mandir ke paas main ek samrudh baag he jahaan se devtaa ko samarpit karne ke liye phool praapt kiye ja sakate hai.
 yahan shiv mandir bhi he jo khatushamji ke mandir ke paas main hi he.
