 ambrial arun grah kaa ek upgrah he. akar main yah arun kaa teesraa sab se bada upgrah he. ambrial kaa rang arun ke saare upgrahon main se sab se gadha he. arun ke anya bade chandramaaon kii tarah, ambrial bhi barf or pathar kaa bana hua he. isaki sataha barfili or andar kaa kendriya bhaag patthariilaa he. isaki sataha par antariksh se gire hue ulkapindon kii vajah se bahut se bade gadhde bhi hai, jinka vyas 210 kimi tak pahunchataa he. kuch tile or khaaiyaan bhi dekhi gayi hai. wayager dwitiya yaan ke 1986 main arun ke paas se guzarane par ambrial kii sataha ke lagbhag 40% hisse ke nakshe banaae ja chuke hai.
 ambrial kaa akar gol he. iskaa ausat vyas lagbhag 1169 kimi he, jo firial ke 1158 kimi ke vyas se bas thodaa sa hi bada he. prithvi ke chandramaa kaa vyas lagbhag 3,474 kimi he, yani kii ambrial kaa akar hamaare chandramaa kaa lagbhag ek-tihaai he.
