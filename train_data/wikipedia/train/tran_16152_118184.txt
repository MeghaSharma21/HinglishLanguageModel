 chitra mudgal hindi kii varisht kathalekhika hai. unka jivan kisi romaanchak prem-katha se kam nahin he. unnav ke jameendaar parivaar main janmi kisi ladki ke liye saath ke dashak main antarjaateey premavivaah karnaa aasaan kaam nahin tha. lekin chitra ji ne to shuru se hi kathin maarg ke vikalp ko apanaaya. pita kaa aaleeshaan bangala chhodkar 25 rupye maheene ke kiraye kii kholi main rahanaa or majdoor union ke liye kaam karnaa - chitra ne har chunauti ko hanste-hanste sweekaar kiya.
 10 disambar 1944 ko janami chitra mudgal kii praarambhik shiksha paitruk gram nihaali kheda se lage gram bhartipur ke kanya paathshaala main. hair secondary puna board se kii or shesh padhai mumbai vishvavidhyalay se. bahut baad main snatakottar padhai patrachaar paathyakram ke madhyam se es.en.di.tii. mahila vishvavidhyalay mumbai se kii. chitrakala main gahari abhiruchi rakhane vaali chitra ne je.je.skool of arts se fine arts kaa adhyayan bhi kiya he. somaiya college main padhai ke dauraan shramik netaa datta samant ke sampark main aakar shramik andolan se judin. unheen dinon gharon main jhaadoo-poncha kar, utpeedan or badahaali main jivan-yaapan karne vaali baayiyon ke utthan or buniyadi adhikaron kii bahaali ke liye sangharshrat sanstha 'jaagran' kii bees varsh kii vay main sachiv baniin.
 ab tak nau kahani sankalan, teen upanyaas, ek lekh-sankalan, ek upanyaas, ek lekh-sankalan, ek bal upanyaas, chaar balakatha-sangrah, chah sampadit pustaken. gujarati main do anudit pustaken prakashit. angrezi main 'hyena and adar short stories' bahuprashansit. dilli doordarshan ke liye film 'vaaris' kaa nirmaan.
 bahucharchit upanyaas 'ek zamin apni' ke liye sahakaari vikaas sanghathan mumbai dwaara fanishwarnath 'renu' sammaan se sammanit. hindi academy dilli dwaara 1996 ke sahityakar sammaan se sammanit or 2000 main apane upanyaas aawaan ke liye euke katha sammaan se sammanit.
