 indianapolis america ke indiana praant kii rajdhani he or merian county kii county seat he. varsh 2000 kii janagadhna ke mutabik isaki jansankhya 791,926 he. yah indiana kaa sabase zyada jansankhya vaala shahar he evam america kaa 13wein sthaan kaa shahar he. 
 indianapolis ko pradesh kii nai rajdhani ke taur par 1820 main chunaa gaya. jerimia sulivan jo indiana supreme court ke jaj the, ne pradesh ke naam indiana ko polis shabd se jod kar indianapolis shabd kii utpatti kii. greek bhashaa main polis shabd kaa arth shahar hota he.
