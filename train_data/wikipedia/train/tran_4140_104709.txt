 sir ke bal kiye jane kii vajah se ise shirshasana kehete hai. shirshasana ek aisa asana he jiske abhyaas se ham sadaiv kai badi-badi bimariyon se dur rahete hai. haalanki yah asana kaaphi mushkil he. yah har vyakti ke liye sahaj nahin he. shirshasana se hamaara pachanatantr acha rahata he, rakt sanchaar sucharu rahata he. sharir ko bal praapt hota he.
 shirshasana karne ke liye ke sabase pehle samtal sthaan par kambal aadi bichhaakar vajrasana kii avasthaa main baith jaaen. ab aage kii ore jhukkar donon haathon kii kohniyon ko jameen par tikaa den. donon haathon kii ungaliyon ko aapas main jod len. ab sir ko donon hatheliyon ke madhya dhire-dhire rakhen. saans saamaanya rakhen. sir ko jameen par tikaane ke baad dhire-dhire sharir kaa puraa vajan sir chhodte hue sharir ko upar kii uthaanaa shuru karen. sharir kaa bhaar sir par len. sharir ko sidhaa kar len. bas yahi avasthaa ko shirshasana kaha jaataa he. yah asana sir ke bal kiya jaataa he isliye ise shirshasana kehete hai.
 shirshasana se hamaara pachanatantr swasth rahata he. isse mastishk kaa rakt sanchaar badhata he, jisase kii samaran shakti kaaphi adhik bad jaati he. histeria evam andkosh vruddhi, hernia, kabj aadi rogon ko dur karata he. asamay baalo kaa jhaduya evam safed hona dur karata he. is asana se hamaara puure sharir kii mansapeshiyaan active ho jaati he. is asana se shaareerik bal miltaa he. aatmavishvaas badhata he or kisi bhi prakaar kaa dar man se nikal jaataa he.
 yadi aap purnat: swasth nahin he to is asana ke abhyaas se purv kisi yoga shikshak se paraamarsh avashya karen. jis vyakti blad pressure kii shikaayat he vah is asana ko haragij naa karen. ankhon se sambandhit koi beemaari ho, tab bhi is asana ko nahin karnaa chahie. gardan main koi samasya ho to bhi is asana ko na karen.
