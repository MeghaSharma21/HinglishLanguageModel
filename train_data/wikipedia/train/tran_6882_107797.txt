 khagolashastra main rosh seemaa yaa rosh vyas brahmand main do vastuon ke gurutvkarshan ke talmail ke ek parinaam ko ujagar karata he. aam taur se koi bhi vastu apane hi gurutvkarshan ke prabhaav se ikaitha hokar rahati he, jaise kii grah, upgrah, vakairaah. lekin yadi koi duusari badi vastu paas main aa jaae to vah pehali vastu ke jamkar ek ho jane main khalal daalati he. kisi bhi vastu kii rosh seemaa vah seemaa he jiske andar vah vastu kisi bhi duusari vastu ko jamkar ekatrit nahin hone detii. saadhaaran taur par rosh seemaa kaa prayog grahon ke ird-gird mandaraate hue upgrahon ke liye hota he. agar upgrah grahon kii rosh seemaa se bahaar ban jaae, to saadhaaran upgrah ban pate hai, varna grah kaa gurutvkarshan itnaa prabhaavshaalee hota he ke vah yaa to bada upgrah banane hi nahin detaa, yaa phir upgrahi challa bana detaa he, yaa us malbe ko apni or khinchakar uskaa apane andar hi vilay kar letaa he.
 kabhi-kabhi rosh seemaa ke andar bhi upgrah ban pate he. yah tab hota he jab upgrah main jamaav keval gurutvkarshan kii vajah se naa ho or uske padaarthon kii aapas main chipakan kaa koi or rasinic yaa bhautik kaaran ho. vaigyanik bhashaa main kaha ja saktaa he ke agar upgrah kii tanaav pushti adhik ho to vah rosh seemaa main rahakar bhi ekatrit rah saktaa he. brihaspati kaa upgrah meets or shani kaa upgrah pan iske do udahaaran hai jo rosh seemaa ke andar hote hue bhi barkaraar hai. lekin aise upgrahon main or ajeeb chizein dekhi ja sakati hai. kyonki inaki sataha par bade grah ke gurutvkarshan kaa prabhaav bahut adhik hota he, agar inaki sataha par koi chiz rakh di jaae to kabhi-kabhi vah aankhon ke saamne hi anayas sataha se udakar upgrah se alag ho sakati he.
