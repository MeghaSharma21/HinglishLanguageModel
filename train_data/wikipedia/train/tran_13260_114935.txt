 yah ek pramukh pathaar hai jo turki main paintic evam taras shreniyon ke bich agian tat se arminia ganth tak vistrut he. ise turki kaa pathaar bhi kehete hai. yah kahin sapaat or kahin lahardaar he tathaa isaki ausat oonchaai 800 meter he. iske bich-bich main jwalamukhi nikshep ke kaaran anek ubhari akritiyaan nirmit ho gai hai.is pathaar ke dharaatal par koruh, mejijirmek, kosilarimek, sakaranya aadi nadiyaan bahati hai.yahan par anek khaare tathaa mithe pani kii jhilein hai jinamein tuj, besahir, igreedor,aksehir pramukh hai.is pradesh main khanijon ke utkhanan kaa kaary bad rahaa he.
