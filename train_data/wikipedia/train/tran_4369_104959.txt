 sardan blott ek pranali he jo istemaal hota he aanvik jeev vigyaan me, diene anukram kaa pataa lagaaya jaataa he diene ke namoono main se. sardan blott main diene kaa hastantaran vaidyutakansanchalan ke duaar, diene ke tukdo ko filter jhilli par alag karnaa or prob ko usame daalana or unki jaanch karnaa e sab aata he. sardan blott british jeevigyaani edwin dakshini ne diya tha.
