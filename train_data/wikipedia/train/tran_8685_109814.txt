 kerala kii vaastu kala vishisht prakaar kii he. isaki visheshta laalitya he jiske uttam udhaaharan hai mandir or pracheen imaaraten. inkaa nirmaan vaastuvidya vidhi ke anusaar kiya gaya he. keraly vaastukala ke soundarya kii abhivyakti keval mandiron main hi nahin varan girjagharon tathaa masjidon main bhi hui he. kerala ke pramukh vaastukala se sambandhit granth hai - tantra samucchyam, shilpachandrika, manushyalay chandrika aadi.
