 himalay kii paristhitiki athwa himalayan paaritantr jo ek parvatiy paristhitik tantra kaa udhaaharan he, bharat or vishwa ke kuch vishisht paaritantron main se ek he. himalay parvat tantra vishwa ke sarvaadhik naveen or vishaal parvaton main se ek he. dhruviya pradeshon ke alawa yah vishwa kaa teesraa sabase bada him-bhandar he or yahan kareeb 1500 himand paae jate hai jo himalay ke lagbhag 17% bhaag ko dhanke hue hai. himalay parvat par yahan kii vishisht jalwiviya or bhuakritik visheshtaon kii vajah se kuch vishisht paristhik kshetra paae jate hai jo apane main adwitiya hai. udhaaharan ke liye phulon kii ghati ek aisa hi jaivvividhta kaa kendra he. purvi himalay main varsha kii adhikta or apeksha krit nam jalwaayu ne ek alag hi prakaar kaa paaritantr viksit kiya he. apane vividh jeev-jantuon or vanaspatiyon kii jaivvividhta or iske bhugolic praturup ke kaaran himalayan paaritantr ek mahatvapurn sthaan rakhata he.
 naveen valit parvat hone ke kaaran abhi bhi himalay kshetra main bheekruthik sthirataa nahin aayi he or yah bhukampi rup se samvedan sheel tathaa bhuskhalan se prabhavit kshetra main aata he. jalwiviya rup se bhi yahan ke himandon ke nivartan ki pushti kii gai he jo ki maanav dwaara parivardhit jalwaayu parivartan kaa parinaam mana jaataa he. uparokt kaarnon se himalayan paristhitki apane parivartansheel hone ke kaaran bhi mahatvapurn he.
 is vishisht prakritik paryaavaran main manushya kii kriyaaon dwaara ek anany prakaar kaa maanav paaritantr bhi viksit hua he. himalay kii maanav paristhitiki main hue adhyayan yah sabit karte hai kii yahan manushya or prakruti ke bich kii anyonyashrayata ne ek vishisht maanav paristhitiki nirmit kii he or himalay ke parivartansheel sthitiyon ke kaaran is par nirbhar maanav jansankhya bhi badlaavon ke prati samvedansheel he. ek report ke mutabik himalayan paaritantr asia ke 1.3 billian logon kii aajeevika par prabhaav daalata he.
