 poratomarine ke saan juaan kaa girjaghar spain ke galician shahar main jerushlem ke saint jon ke silsile kaa ek girjaghar-qila he. 
 yah ek girjaghar or ek mahal donon ke rup main tayyar kiya gaya he or isliye donon imaaraton ke vaastushilp-visheshtaon hai. yah ek asaamaanya baad ke samay ke rome kaa girjaghar he. ek girjaghar ke rup main yah ek bairal gumbadadaar chat, ek arth-golaaidaar maharaab rakhata he or rome deshvasi charchon ke sabhi vishisht sajaavat rakhata he. inamen maharaabon ke aakaar ke saath ek nakkashidaar pattiyon vaali khidakiyon or nakkashidaar gulab shaamil hai.
