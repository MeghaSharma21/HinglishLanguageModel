 new york hilton midtown, new york shahar kaa sabase bada or vishwa kaa 101vaan sabase unchaa hotel he. is hotel kaa malikana haq evam prabandhan kaa adhikaar hilton worldwide naam kii company ke paas he.isaki 47 manzil unchi imaarat rockefeller centre ke uttarpurvi chor par sikasth evanew or 53 street par sthit he. is hotel main jon epa. kennedy evam uske baad ke sabhi america raashtrapati atithi ke taur par rah chuke he. 1964 main mahaan kalakaar samooh betals bhi apane aid salivan theatre kii yatra ke dauraan yahan tehra tha. 1973 main duniya kaa sabase pehla mobile phone kol bhi isi hotel ke atithi martin kupar dwaara is hotel ke saamne se kiya gaya tha.
 is pariyojna ko hilton hotels korporation, da rockefellar group, or da uris building korporation dwaara sanyukt rup se puraa kiya gaya tha. iske asli vaastukar morris lapidus the jinhone ise fontnblt hotel building kii sanrachana ke anusaar vakrakaar rup main banane kaa prastaav rakhaa tha. parantu lapidus ko is yojana se hatana padaa kyonki ve paas hi main ek duusare hotel americana hotel vaala precor uese naam kaa ek fitness centre bhi banaaya gaya.
 isi samay ke aasapaas is hotel kaa naam badalakar hilton new york kar diya gaya kyonki hilton samooh kii us samay kii prachaar yojana ke anusaar hotalon ke naam ke pehle shahar kaa naam lagane kaa prachlan chal rahaa tha. 2007 main hue chauthe navnirmaan ke saath hi hotel main 42 se lekar 44 tal par 47 sooyeet banaae gayi.
 2013 main isaki 50veen varshaganth ke avasar par hotel kaa naam punah badal kar new york hilton midtown kar diya gaya. saath hi saath hotel ne kamaron main khaanaa pahuchane kaa niyam bhi khatm kar diya. ek naye restaraan kii sthapana kii gayi jisane "herb and kichan" se naam apni ek alag room delivery shuru kii.
