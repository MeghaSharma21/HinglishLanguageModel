 
 akmola praant madhya asia ke qaazakhstan desh kaa ek praant he. isaki rajdhani kokeshetau shahar he. qaazakhstan kii rashtriya rajdhani, astana, puri tarah akmola ke andar aati he lekin prashasnic rup se vah is rajya kaa bhaag nahin he. is praant main sona or koyala zamin se nikaalane kaa kaam chalata he. san 2009 kii janaganana main is praant ke 43.5% log kazakh samudaay ke the jabaki 36.5% log roosi samudaay ke the.
 kazakh bhashaa main 'aalmola' kaa matlab 'safed dafan ' hota he.
 1950 ke dashak main, jab qaazakhstan soviet sangh kaa hissaa tha, to soviet sangh main anaaj kii bahut kami hui. us jamaane main soviet vyavastha main adhiktar anaaj rus ke upjau kshetron main ugaaya jaataa tha. soviet netaa nikita khrushchev​ ne elaan kiya kii anaaj kii paidaavaar badhaane ke liye madhya asia ke stapi ilaake kii zameenon par nae sire se kheti shuru kii jaaegi. is jumbeish kaa naam 'nai bhoomi abhiyaan' rakhaa gaya. baari maatr main rus or ukraine se log uttari qaazakhstan ke nae kheton main aa based jis se sthaneey samudaayon kaa mishran bhi badal gaya. yahi kaaran he ki akmola praant jaise ilaaqon main roosi logon kii bahutayat he.
