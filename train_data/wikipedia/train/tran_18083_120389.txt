 shah jahaan paanchave mogul shahenshaah the shah jahaan apni nyaaypriyata or vaibhavvilas ke kaaran apane kaal main bade lokapriy rahe. kintu itihaas main unka naam keval is kaaran nahin liya jaataa. shahjahan kaa naam ek aise aashiq ke taur par liya jaataa he jisane apni begham mumtaz begum ke liye vishwa kii sabase kuubasurat imaarat taj mahal banane kaa ytn kiya. 
 samraat jahangir ke maut ke baad, choti umr main hi unhen mughal singhaasana ke uttaradhikari ke rup main chun liya gaya tha. 1627 main apane pita kii mrityu hone ke baad vah gaddi par baithe. unke shasankaal ko mogul shashan kaa swarna yug or bhartiya sabhyataa kaa sabase samrudh kaal bulaayaa gaya he. 
 1658 main aurangzeb ne shahjahan ko tajmahal main shahi khajaane ko karch karne ke zurm main bandi bana liya tha iske baad shahjaha ne apane jivan ke antim aath barsh agra ke kile main ek bandi ke rup main vyateet kiye jaha use aurangzeb pine ke liye napa-tula pani ek phooti hui matki main bhejtaa tha . jab tak shahjahan jivit rahe, tab tak shahjahan kii beti 'jahaan aara' ne jail main rahakar unki timaardaari kii. shahjahan ko nazarband kar aurangzeb ne apane ko badshaah ghoshit karavaaya. kaha jaataa hai ki marane se pehle shahjahan ne apane putr aurangzeb ko ek chitthee likhi thi . is patr ke aakhri panktiyon main shahjahan ne likhaa tha ki “i pisar tu ajab musalamaani ba pidare jinda aab tarasaaniafreen baad hindwan sad baar main dehdan pidare murdarawa daayam aab” .iskaa arth he ki putr tu bhi vicitra musalmaan he jo apane jivit pita ko jal ke liye tarasaa rahaa he . un hinduon ko shat shat naman jo apane mrit purvajo ko bhi pani dete hai . is patr ke jawaab main aurangzeb ne likhaa ki jis syaahi se yah patr likhaa he usee syaahi ko pi lo or mar jaao
