 apane ko 'musalmaan' kehane vaale charampanthiyon dwaara kiye gayi aatank ko islamic aatankwaad kehete hai. ye tathakthith musalmaan bhaanti-bhaanti ke raajnneetik tathaa/yaa majahabi uddeshyon kii purti ke liye aatank failaate hai. 
 islamic aatankwaad madhya purv, africa, europe, dakshinpurv asia, bharat evam america main bahut dinon se maujuud he. aatanki sanghathanon main osama bin laden dwaara sthapit al kaayda naamak sainik sanghathan kukhyat he.
