 santatyak yaa continyam kisi chiz ke aise vistaar yaa failaav ko bolte hai jisamen dhire-dhire bina kisi antaraal yaa daraar ke badlaav aaye. udahaaran ke liye samay kaa bahaav ek santatyak he kyonki har kshan apane se pehle kshan se bina kisi antaraal ke judaa hota he jabaki paise kii ginati santatyak nahin he . isi tarah se dik ek santatyak he. yadi kisi chiz kaa pravaah santatyak ho to us chiz ko "santatya" kaha jaataa he. samay santatya he.
 angrezi main "santatyak" ko "continyam" or "santatya" ko "continus" kehete hai. agar ham kahen ke "samay kaa pravaah ek santatyak he" to use angrezi main kahenge "the flow of time forms a continuum". "samay santatya he" ko kahenge "time is continuous".
