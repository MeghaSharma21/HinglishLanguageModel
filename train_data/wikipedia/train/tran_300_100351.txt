 isisi champions trophy, vishwa cricket parishad dwaara yojit kii jane vaali ek dwivarshik pratiyogita he. ise cricket ke vishwa cap ke baad sabase badi pratiyogita ke rup main dekha jaataa he or kuch log ise mini vishwa cap bhi kehete hai. 1998 se shuru hokar abhi tak kul 8 baar is pratiyogita kaa aayojan kiya gaya he jise, samayvaar kram main, dakshin africa, newjiland, bharat tathaa srilanka, west indies, australia, bharat va pakistan ne jeeta he. toornament ke aath sanskarnon main kul milakar teraa tiimon ne bhaag liya, jisamen aath ne 2017 main aakhri sanskaran main bhaag liya. isisi champions trophy ko antarrashtriya cricket ke teen praroopon main se prayek ke liye keval ek shikhar toornament rakhane ke isisi ke lakshya ke anuroop rakhaa gaya tha. 
 champiens trophy ko chota vishwacap bhi kaha jaataa he. 1998 main isisi ne nock out toornament rakhaa tha jise 2009 se isisi champions trophy naam diya. 2017 main england or wales yojit shrunkhlaa is kadi kii antim pratiyogita he.
