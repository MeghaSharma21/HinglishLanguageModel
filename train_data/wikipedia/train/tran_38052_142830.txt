 machhlee ghar madhya pradesh ki rajdhani bhopal me sthit ek prasiddh jaljivshaala he. raaj bhawan ke paas sthit is jaljivshaala kii sthapana 31 may 1977 ko hui thi. do manzile is avasthaapana main upree tal par chalis chote or nichle tal par 26 bade equarium rakhe gayi the jinamein vividh prakaar kii machhliyaan or jaleey jeev rakhe gayi the. 
 bharat main sthit equaria main ise mumbai sthit tarapur fish equarium ke baad doosraa sabase samrudh equarium mana jaataa he. iskaa ullekh "world diarectory of equaria" main bhi he.
 sarkaar ne ise band karke iske sthaan par paanch sitara hotel or kanveshan centre banane kii yojana banaai thi. bhopal ke logon ke virodh ke baad ise ek duusari jagah nae rup main banane kii sweekruti di gayi or vartmaan main isaki machhliyon ko bhadbhada road sthit ek asthaayee bhawan main rakhaa gaya he. is asthaayee equarium main bhi tikat daren or samay saarini puraane jaisi he.
