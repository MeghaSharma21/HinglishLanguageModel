 kshudragrahon kii varnakram-shreniyan unke utsarjan varnakram, rang or kabhi-kabhi albido ke aadhaar par nirdhaarit hoti hai. bahut had tak yah kshudragrahon kii satahon par maujuud samagriyon kaa bhi sanket detii hai. chote kshudragrahon main kshudragrah kii upar kii sataha or andruni rachana main koi antar nahin hota jabaki 4 vesta jaise bade kshudragrahon kii bhitari sanrachana bahari parat se kaafii bhinn ho sakati he.
 varnakram, albido, aadi ke aadhaar par kshudragrahon ko shreniyon main baantne vaali kai vyavasthaayen ijad kii gai hai lekin unamem se do adhik prachlit hai.
 yah san 1984 main david tholen naamak khagolashastri dwaara prastavit kii gai thi. isaki shreniyan 1980 main 'ashtavarniya kshudragrah surveykshan' ke tahat 978 kshudragrahon par kiye gayi adhyayan ke aadhaar par paribhaashit kii gai thi. is vyavastha main 14 shreniyan hai. adhikansh kshudragrah inamen se keval 3 shreniyon main padate hai or baaqi 11 main kam hi kshudragrah aate hai.
