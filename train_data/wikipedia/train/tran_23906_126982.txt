 miss tanakpur haazir ho ek aane vaali rajnatik vyangatmak film he. iskaa nirdesan vinod kapari ne kiya he. ye film 26 june 2015 ko release hogi. yah film ek satya ghatana se prerit he. pehle yah film 19 june ko release hone vaali thi par vinod kapari ne abisidi 2 jo bhi isi din release hogi, se bachane ke liye ek film kii release date aage badha di.
 film ke trailor ko teen din main das lakh ke zyaada vyuu mili. jise times of india ke mohar basu ne ek rajnatik vyangatmak or kam bajet vaali film ke liye bemisaal bataaya. kuch lekhon main ye bataaya gaya kii film ko megaster amitabh bachchan film nirmaataa rajkumaar hiraani or film diarector subhash kapur se achche rivyu mili hai.
 gaanv ke yuvak par gaanv kii inami bhais ke saath sambhog kaa aarope he.
 kapari ke anusaar 21 june 2015 ko film kii cast kamedi sho kamedi nits vid kapil par dikhaai denge.
 nirdeshak vinod kapari ne bataaya kii ye film ek rajnatik vyang he jo kaaphi had tak rajasthanmen hui satya ghatana par aadhaarit he. jisamen ucch nyaayalay ne ek ladke ko jisapar bhens ke saath kathith rup se balaatkaar karne kaa aarope tha, ko jail bhej diya tha.kapari ne film ko rajasthan kii jagah haryana main filmaaya kyonki unhen haryaanavi bhashaa kaaphi pasand he. isamen aapako hindi or haryaanavi donon hi ke mili-jule samvaad milenge. is film ke nirdeshak ko prerana bibisi par chape ek samachar se mili.
