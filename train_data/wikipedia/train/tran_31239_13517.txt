 tajmahal bangladesh mul tajmahal kaa ek praturup he jo ek mughal samadhi he or jo agra, bharat main sthit he. yah praturup bangladesh kii rajdhani dhaka se lagbhag 29 kimi kii duuri par sthit ek sthaan sonargaanv main he. apane mul ke vipareet, is bhawan ke nirmaan main keval paanch varsh lage or kathith rup iske dhani maalik ne isapar 5.8 karod dollar kharch kiye. ahsanullah moni, ek dhani bangladeshi chalchitr-nirmaataa, ne yah bataaya ki unhone kis prakaar yah pratikruti banaai taaki unke desh ke nirdhan log padosi desh bharat ke prasiddh smarak tajmahal ko dekhne kaa apana sapana puraa kar saken.
 is pratikruti ke nirmaan kaa bharat main virodh kiya gaya. bangladesh sthit bhartiya ucchayog kaa kahana he ki vah ahsanullah moni ke viruddh mul bhawan ke copirite ke ulanghan kaa mukadmaa daayar karegaa. mul bhawan lagbhag 300 varsh purana he.
 moni kaa kahana he ki unhone italy se sangemarmar, belgium se heere or gumbad ke liye lagbhag 160 kilo taanbe kaa aayaat kiya he, par kuch logon kaa kahana he ki yah sampoorn pratikruti nahin he or yah us saamaan se nahin bana he jiskaa kii daawa kiya gaya he.
