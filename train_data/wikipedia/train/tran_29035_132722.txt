 ghudsawaari ek prakaar kii kala he, jisamen vyakti ghode par baith kar sawari karata he. isamen use disha nirdesh dena or bina gire uski sawari karnaa hi isamen ek kala kaa rup letaa he. pehle jab gaadi aadi vaahan nahin hote the. tab manushya isi par baith kar dur dur tak sawari karata tha. yah teji se bhaagta he, is kaaran kisi vyakti ko kisi sthaan par jaldi pahunchnaa hota tha to vah isi kii sawari karata tha.
 kuch tathy or saboot bataate hai ki manushya 6,000 varsh purv bhi ghodon kaa upyog karata tha.
 jab manushya chakkon aadi kaa vikaas karne main sakasham ho gaya, tab vah ghodon, belon aadi ko gaadi se jodakar apana ek gaadi bana diya karata tha. ise ghoda gaadi kehete hai.
