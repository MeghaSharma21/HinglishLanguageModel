 pandit ramnarayan mishra mahaan hindisevi the jinhone shyamsundar das or thakur sivakumar singh ke saath milakar nagariprachaarini sabha kii sthapana kii thi. ve san 1937 main iske sabhapati hue. 
 ramnarayan mishra kaa janm 1873 main amritsar main hua tha. aap apane mata-pita ke saath banaras aa gayi or aakar yaheen ke ho gayi. banaras ke queens college main shiksha ke dauraan hi shyamsundar das or thakur sivakumar singh ke saath milakar nagariprachaarini sabha kii sthapana kii. shiksha puri karne ke baad unhone shiksha vibhaag main sab dipti inspektar tathaa dipti inspetor ke pad par kaam kiya. unhone skulon main 'jai-jai pyaara desh' tathaa 'maatu pitu sahaayak sakha tumahi ekanath hamaare ho' aadi prarthanaayen shuru kin. isse avibhavakon evam vidhyaarthiyon kaa dhyaan hindi bhashaa kii ore gaya.
 1953 main aapkaa dehavsaan ho gaya.
 aapane kai pustakon kii rachana kii.
