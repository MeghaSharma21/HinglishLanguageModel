  ashok mitra pashchim bengal se ek arthshaastri, raajnneetijna or bengali bhashaa ke vikhyath sahityakar the. ve pashchim bengal main vam morche kii sarkaar main varsh 1977 se 1987 tak vitt mantri rahe. iske alaava ve varsh 1970 se 1972 tak centre sarkaar ke mukhya aarthik salaahkaar bhi the. bengali sahitya main yogadan ke liye unhen sahitya academy aword se sammanit kiya gaya tha. inke dwaara rachit ek niabandh taal vetaal ke liye unhen san 1996 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
