 zahraan ek badu arabi kabila he. yah un ginati ke arab qabeelon main se he jinhen arabi prayadweep kaa mul nivasee mana jaataa he. is kabeeley ke log gamid​ qabeele ke bhi sambandhi hai or unke aitihaasik mitrapaksh main rahe hai. inaki mul mathrubhumi arabi prayadweep kaa dakshini yaa dakshinpurvi bhaag, visheshkar yaman or saudi arab kaa al-bahah kshetra, kahi jaati he lekin vartmaankaal main iske bahut se sadsy pashchimi saudi arab main based hue hai. madhya purv ke kai anya ilaaqon main logon kaa paariwarik naam 'zahraan' hota he lekin yah zaruri nahin ki ve sabhi zahraan qabeele ke hon.
 zahraan or belad gamid​ qabeele apni alag vishesh arabi upbhasha bolte hai jise zahraani kaha jaataa he. kuch sroton ke anusaar yah puraane jamaane kii shastreey arabi kii sab se nitakatam jivit upbhasha he. sabhi jivit arabi boliyon main se iskaa vyakaran quran kii arabi ke sabase nazadik paaya jaataa he.
