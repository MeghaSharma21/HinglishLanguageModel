 kaartaveery arjuna pracheen haihay vansh ke raja the jinka ullekh mahabharat main bhi he. ve pracheen mahishmati nagari ke raja the jo ki vartmaan maheshwar nagar main he. ve chandravanshi raja kritaviry ke putr the. raja arjuna kii rajdhani narmada nadi ke tat par thi jise inhone carcotak naag se jitkar basaaya tha. 
 unhen sahasrabahu arjuna bhi kehete hai. unki ek sahasr bhujaayen thim, jiske kaaran inhen sahatrarjun bhi kehete hai . ve dattatreya ke param bhakt the.
