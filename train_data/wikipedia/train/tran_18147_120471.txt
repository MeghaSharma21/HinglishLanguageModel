 marusthal yaa registan aise bhugolic kshetron ko kaha jaataa he jahaan jalpaat anya kshetron kii apeksha kaaphi kam hoti he. praayah retile registani maidaanon ko marusthal kaha jaataa he joki galat he. yah baat or he ki bharat main sabase kam varsha vaala kshetra ek retila maidan he. marusthal kaa retila hona aavashyak nahin. marusthal kaa garm hona bhi aavashyak nahin he. antarctic, joki barf se dhakaa pradesh he, vishwa kaa sabase bada marusthal he ! vishwa ke anya deshon main kai aise marusthal hai jo retile nahin he.
 marusthalon ko varsha, ausat taapmaana, saal main bina varsha ke dinon kii sankhya ityadi ke aadhaar par baanta ja saktaa he. bharat kaa thar marusthal ek ooshna katibandheey marusthal he jiske kaaran hi yah retila bhi he.
 varsha tathaa himpat ke kul ko jalpaat kehete hai. yadi kisi kshetra kaa jalpaat 200 millimetre se bhi kam ho to vah ek prakaar kaa pradesh he. isi prakaar 250-500 millimetre tak ke kshetra ko alag varg main rakhaa ja saktaa he. isi prakaar anya kshetra bhi vargeekrut kiye ja sakate hai.
 in kshetron ko taapmaana kii drushti se bhi vargeekrut kiya ja saktaa he.
 jab ek vishaal parvat varsha ke badalon ko aage kii disha main badhane main baadha utpann karata he tab uske aage kaa pradesh vrishtiheen ho jaataa he or ise vrishtichaaya kshetra kehete hai.
 adhik oonche parvaton par varsha nahin hoti isliye inhen bhi marusthal kaa shrenee main rakhaa jaataa he.
 marusthal kaa saamaanya gun to yah he ki isamen varsha kam hoti he. ye kshetra praayah viral aabaadi, naganya vanaspati, pato kii jagah kaaten, jal ke stroton kii kami, jalaapurti se adhik vashpikaran hai. keval 20% marusthal retile hai. ret praayah paraton main bichhi hoti he. retile kshetron ke dainik taapmaana main bahut vividhta hoti he. vishwa ke keval 20% marusthal retile hai. ret praayah paraton main bichhi hoti he. retile kshetron ke dainik taapmaana main bahut vividhta hoti he. lagbhag sabhi marusthal samtal hai.
