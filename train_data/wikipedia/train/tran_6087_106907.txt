 rajasthan hindi granth academy rajasthan sarkaar kaa swaitshasi santhaan he jo hindi madhyam se ucch shiksha praapt karne vaale vidhyaarthiyon ke liye vibhinn vishyon kii paathya evam sandarbh pustakon kii rachana karata he. 
 bharat sarkaar ne praathmik evam madhyamik shiksha ke baad san 1968 main ucch shiksha ke madhyam parivartan par bal diya tha. bharat sarkaar kii is rashtriya shiksha niti ke antargat bhartiya bhashaaon main ucch shiksha ke vidhyaarthiyon ke liye vibhinn vishyon kii pustakon kaa lekhan] prakaashan evam vipanan kiya janaa tha. iske liye hindi pradeshon main granth acadamiyon evam hinditar pradeshon main paathya pustak-nirmaan mandalon kaa gathan kiya gaya tha. san 1969 main ek swaitshasi sanstha ke rup main rajasthan hindi granth academy kii sthapana hui.
 apni sthapana se yah academy hindi madhyam main ucchshiksha praapt kar rahe vidhyaarthiyon ke liye vigyaan, takneekee, manviki, vaanijya, saamaajik vigyaan evam shiksha aadi vishyon kii paathya saamagree uplabdh karwaati he. is paathya saamagree main paathya pustaken hai to sandarbh granth bhi hai. academy 'na laabh-na haani' ke siddhanth par pustaken prakashit karti he taaki kam keemat kii in pustakon ko adhik se adhik vidhyaarthee kharid saken. pustaken vishay visheshgyon ke dwaara likhi jaati hai taaki gunvattta kaayam rahe.
