 1854 main alexender won hambolt kii sifaarish par east india company ne harman, adolf or robert ko bhartiya upmahadweep main apane qabze vaale kshetron par vigyaanik anusandhan karne ke liye naukari par rakh liya or visheshkar prithvi ke chumbakeey kshetra par chaanbin karne kaa zimmaa diya. unhone agle teen saalon main dakshin bharat ke dakkhan pathaar or phir himalay, karakoram or kunlun parvaton kaa daura kiya. harman or robert kunlun shrunkhlaa paar karne vaale pehle europea log the.
