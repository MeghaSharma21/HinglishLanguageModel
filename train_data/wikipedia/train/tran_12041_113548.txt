 hindu raaj uttari pakistan main hindu kush parvatamala or karakoram parvatamala ke bich sthit ek parvat shrunkhlaa he. iskaa sabase unchaa pahaad 6,872 meter lambaa koyo zum he. iske do anya janemaane pahaad booni zum or gamubaar zum hai. is shrunkhlaa main koi bhi 7,000 meter yaa us se adhik oonchaai kaa shikhar nahin isliye ise hindu kush shrunkhlaa jitani khyaati nahin mili he.
