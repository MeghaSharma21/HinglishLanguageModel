 guam yaa guam, jise sthaneey chamorro bhashaa main gvahan kehete hai, prashanth mahaasaagar ke pashchimi bhaag main sthit ek dweep he ko prashasnic rup se sanyukt rajya america ke adheen he. isaki rajdhani hagaatna he, jiskaa purv naam aganya hua karata tha. yah maariana dweepasmooh kaa sabase dakshini or sabase bada dweep he, tathaa saath-hi-saath micronishia bhushetra kaa sabase bada dweep bhi he. yahan kaa mul nivasee samudaay va unki bhashaa dono "chamorro" kehalaate hai.
