 jilaluddin khilji ne visan 1347 main ranthambhore par akraman kiya. sabase pehle usne chhangarh par akraman kiya. muslim sena ne kade pratirodh ke baad is durg par adhikaar kiya. tatpashchaat muslim sena ranthambhore par akraman karne ke liye aage badi. usne durg par adhikaar karne ke liye akraman kiya lekin hammir dev ke netritv main chouhan veeron ne sultan ko itani haani pahunchaai, ki use vivash hokar dilli laut janaa padaa. chhangarh par bhi chouhanon ne dubaara adhikaar kar liya. is akraman ke do varsh pashchaat muslim sena ne ranthambhore par dubaara akraman kiya, lekin ve is baar bhi parajeet hokar dilli waapas aa gaye. visan 1353 main sultan jilaluddin khilji kii hatya karke allaudin khilji dilli kaa sultan bana. vah sampoorn bharat ko apane shashan ke antargat laane kii aakanksha rakhata tha. hammir ke netritv main ranthambhore ke chouhanon ne apni shakti ko kaaphi sudrudh bana liya or rajasthan ke vistrut bhubhag par apana shashan sthapit kar liya tha. allaudin khilji dilli ke nikat chouhanon kii badhati hui shakti ko nahin dekhnaa chahta tha, isliye sangharsh hona avashyambhavi tha.
 i.sa. 1299 main allaudin kii sena ne gujarat par akraman kiya tha. vahaan se lut kaa bahut sa dhan dilli laa rahe the. maarg main lut ke dhan ke bantwaare ko lekar kuch senanayakon ne vidroh kar diya tathaa ve vidrohi senanayake raav hammirdev kii sharan main ranthambhore chale gaye. ye senanayake mir mohammad shah or kamru the. sultan allaudin ne in vidrohiyon ko saunp dene kii maang raav hammir se kii, hammir ne uski yah maang thukraa di. kshatriya dharm ke siddhanton kaa paalan karte hue raav hammir ne, sharan main aae hue sainikon ko nahin lautaaya. sharan main aae hue kii raksha karnaa apana karttavya samajha. is baat par allaudin krodhit hokar ranthambhore par yuddh ke liye taiyaar hua.
 allaudin kii sena ne sarvapratham chhangarh par akraman kiya. unka yahan aasaani se adhikaar ho gaya. chhangarh par musalamaanon ne adhikaar kar liya he, yah samachar sunakar hammir ne ranthambhore se sena bheji. chouhan sena ne muslim sainikon ko parrast kar diya. muslim sena parajeet hokar bhaag gai, chouhanon ne unka lutaa hua dhan va astr-shastr lut liye. visan 1358 main allaudin khilji ne dubaara chouhanon par akraman kiya. chhangarh main donon senaaon main bhayankar yuddh hua. is yuddh main hammir swayan yuddh main nahin gaya tha. vir chouhanon ne veertapurvak yuddh kiya lekin vishaal muslim sena ke saamne kab tak tikte. ant main sultan kaa chhangarh par adhikaar ho gaya.
 tatpashchaat muslim sena ranthambhore kii taraf badhane lagi. turki senanayakon ne hamir dev ke paas soochna bhijwayi, ki hamen hamaare vidrohiyon ko saunp do, jinako aapane sharan de rakhi he. hamaari sena vaapis dilli laut jaaegi. lekin hammir apane vachan par drudh the. usne sharnaagaton ko saunpane athwa apane rajya se nirvasit karne se spasht manaa kar diya. turki sena ne ranthambhore par gheraa daal diya. turki sena ne nusrat khan or ulug khan ke netritv main ranthambhore par akraman kiya. durg bahut unche pahaad par hone ke kaaran shatru kaa vah pahuchanaa bahut kathin tha. muslim sena ne gheraa kada karte hue akraman kiya lekin durg rakshak un par pattharon, baanon kii bauchhaar karte, jisase unki sena kaa kaaphi nuksaan hota tha. muslim sena kaa is tarah gheraa bahut dinon tak chalata rahaa. lekin unka ranthambhore par adhikaar nahin ho sakaa.
 allaudin ne raav hammir ke paas dubaara doot bheja kii hamen vidrohi sainikon ko saunp do, hamaari sena waapas dilli laut jaaegi. hammir hath purvak apane vachan par drudh tha. bahut dinon tak muslim sena kaa gheraa chaluta rahaa or chouhan sena muqabla karti rahi. allaudin ko ranthambheer par adhikaar karnaa mushkil lag rahaa tha. usne chal-kapat kaa sahaara liya. hammir ke paas sandhi kaa prastaav bheja jisko paakar hammir ne apane aadmi sultan ke paas bheje. un aadamiyon main ek surjan kothyari va kuch ronaa nayak the. allaudin ne unako lobh laalach dekar apni taraf milaane kaa prayaas kiya. inamen se gupt rup se kuch log sultan kii taraf ho gaye.
 durg kaa dheraa bahut dinon se chal rahaa tha, jisase durg main rasad aadi kii kami ho gai. durg waalon ne ab antim nirnaayak yuddh kaa vichaar kiya. rajputon ne kesharia vastra dhaaran karke shaakaa kiya. rajput sena ne durg ke daravaaje khol die. bheeshan yuddh karnaa prarambh kiya. donon pakshon main aamane-saamne kaa yuddh tha. ek ore sankhya bal main bahut kam rajput the to duusari ore sultan kii kai guna bady sena, jinke paas paryeti yuddhadi saamagree evam rasad thi. rajputon ke parakram ke saamne musalmaan sainik tik nahin sake ve bhaag chute bhaagate hue musalmaan sainiko ke jhande rajputon ne chin liye va waapas rajput sena durg kii ore laut padi. durg par se raniyon ne musalamaanon ke jhando ko durge kii ore aate dekhakar samajha kii rajput haar gaye atah unhone johar kar apane aapako agni ko samarpit kar diya. kile main pravesh karne par johar kii lapaton ko dekhakar hamir ko apni bhul kaa gyaan hua. usne praayshchit karne hetu kile main sthit shiv mandir par apana mastak kaat kar shankar bhagwaan ke shivlinga par chadhaa diya. allaudin ko jab is ghatana kaa pataa chalaa to usne laut kar durg par kabja kar liya.
