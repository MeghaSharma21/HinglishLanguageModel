 kurukshetra vishvavidhyalay bharat kaa ek vishvavidhyalay he. yah bharat ke haryana rajya ke kurukshetra main sthit he. isaki sthapana 11 janvari 1957 ko kii gayi thi. yah vishvavidhyalay punjab ke tatkaaleen rajyapal chandreshwar prasad narayan singh kaa sapana tha jo bhartiya samskruti or parampara ko poshit karne vaale ek santhaan kii sthapana chaahate the. jab dow rajendra prasad ne is vishvavidhyalay kaa udghaatan kiya tha tab yahan keval sanskrit vibhaag hi tha. is vishvavidhyalay kaa praangan lagbhag 400 acre main failaa hua he.
