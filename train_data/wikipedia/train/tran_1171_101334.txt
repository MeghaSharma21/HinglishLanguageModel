 prem shakti 1994 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. isamen karishma kapur, govinda or kadar khan mukhya abhinayta he. anya kalakaaron main shakti kapur, raja muraad, punit issar or nitish bharadwaj shaamil hai. film ausat rahi thi. isane karishma or govinda kii shuruyaat sah-sitaron ke rup main kii thi. aage jaakar inaki kai hit filmein ek saath ai.
 ganga or gauri pyaar main hai, lekin un yuva premiyon ko shaadi karne kii anumati nahin milti he. ek raat ve bhaag jate hai or khud ko ek dusht rishi kii gupha main pate hai. wo sanpon ke bhagwaan ko ek mani dene ke liye majaboor karne kii koshish kar rahaa he. jisase use amar hone ke liye amrit banane main jarurat padegi. yah ghatana keval poornima kii is raat ko ho sakati he. yah din keval pachchis varsh main hi aata he. yuva premi joda saanp kii raksha karte hai or rishi kii yojana ko dhwast karte hai. krodhit rishi gauri ko ek pathar main badal detaa he or sadame kaa saamana karne main asamarth he, ganga mar jaataa he. lekin jaadui saanp yah ghoshnaa karata he ki ganga kaa punarjanm hoga, gauri ke liye dil main pyaar ke saath or pachchis saal baad ve ek duusare ko phir se milenge.
