 raghuveer choudhary gujarati ke prasiddh upanyaaskaar, kavi evam aalochak hai. ve anek samachaarpatron main stambhalekhak bhi rahe hai. unhone gujarat vishvavidhyalay main adhyaapan kiya or vahaan se 1998 main sevanivrutt hue. gujarati ke alaava unhone hindi main bhi lekhan kaary kiya he. inke dwaara rachit ek upanyaas uparavaas kathathrayee ke liye unhen san 1977 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
