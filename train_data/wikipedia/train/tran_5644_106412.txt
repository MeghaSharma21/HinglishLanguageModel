 lim dosan  ek england cricket team ke profeshnal cricket khilaadi he. dosan daayine haath se ballebaaji karte hai inke alaava ye gendbaaji bhi karte hai is kaaran ye harfanmaulaa khilaadi kehalaate hai. inkaa janm swinden main hua tha. dosan ne apane test cricket ke karier kii shuruyaat bhartiya cricket team ke khilaaf 16 disambar 2016 ko kii thi jisamen inhone 66 ranon kii pari bhi kheli thi. inke alaava inhone ek divasiy antarrashtriya kii shuruyaat 4 sitambar 2016 ko hi pakistan cricket team ke khilaaf kii. dosan ko bhartiya cricket team ke khilaaf vanade shrinkhalaa main bhi team main shaamil kiya gaya tha lekin antim ekadash main nahin liya gaya.
