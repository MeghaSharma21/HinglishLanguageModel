 sabji kisi paudhe ke us bhaag ko kehete hai jise aanch par pakaakar khaaya jaataa he. bijon or mithe falon ko praayah sabji nahin kaha jaataa he. khaae jane vaale pattey, tane, danthal or jaden praayah sabji kahi jaati hai. saanskrithik drushti se 'sabji' kii paribhaasha sthaneey pratha ke anusaar alag-alag hoti he. udhaaharan ke liye bahut se log kukuramutton ko sabzi maanate hai jabaki anya logon ke anusaar yah sabzi nahin balki ek anya khaane kii shrenee he.
 kuch sabziyaan kachi khai ja sakati hai jabaki anya sabziyon ko pakaana padataa he. aam-taur par sabziyon ko namak yaa khattai ke saath pakaaya jaataa he lekin kuch sabziyaan aisi bhi hai jinhen chini ke saath pakaakar unki mithai yaa halva banaae jate hai .
 'sabz' shabd kaa matlab aadhunik farsi main 'haraa', yaa kabhi-kabhi 'kaala' hota he. farsi main 'sabzi' keval vastav main hare rang ke patton-sabziyon ko bulaayaa jaataa he jabaki hindi, urdu, punjabi, kashmiri or uttari bhartiya upmahadweep kii anya bhashaaon main 'sabzi' kii shrenee main kisi bhi rang kii sabziyaan shaamil hai. dhyaan den ki sanskrit or farsi hind-irani bhashaa parivaar kii bahanen hone ke kaaran hazaron sajaateey shabd rakhti hai or 'sabz' bhi inamen se ek he. sanskrit main isi shabd kaa rup 'sasy' he, jiskaa mul arth ' dana yaa fal' tha.
