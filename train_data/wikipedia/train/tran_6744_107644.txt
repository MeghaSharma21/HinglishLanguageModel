 tehri band tehari vikaas pariyojna kaa ek praathmik band he jo uttrakhand rajya ke tehri jile main sthit he. ise swami ramtirth sagar baandh bhi kehete hai |yah band himalay kii do mahatvapurn nadiyon par bana he jinamein se ekaganga nadi kii pramukh sahyogi nadi bhagirathi tathaa duusari bhilangana nadi he, jinke sangam par ise banaaya gaya he. tehri band kii oonchaai 261 meter he jo ise vishwa kaa paanchava sabase unchaa band banaati he. is band se baandh se 322 million ghan meter pani ke sansay kii aashaa he jiske jalashay se lagbhag 2,70,000 hectare bhoomi kii sinchai ke saath saath 2400 mega wat vidyut utpaadan or pratidin 102.20 karod litre peyajal dilli, uttar pradesh evan uttrakhand ko uplabdh karana prastaavit he. is baandh se puri tarah se duube tehri shahar or 23 gaanvon, jabaki 72 anya gaanvon ko aanshik rup se laabh hoga, yadhyapi bhavishya main bharat sarkaar dwaara chalaae gaye kaarykram grid jodo pariyojna ke tahat ise joda jayega, jis se vidhut kaa pravaah anya rajyon main bhi hoga.
 tehri baandh pariyojna hetu praathmik jaanch kaa kaam 1961 main purna ho gaya. iske baad iske ruparekha tay karne kaa kaary 1972 main hua. iske liye 600emdbalu kaa bijli sanyantr lagaaya gaya. iske nirmaan kaa kaary 1978 main shuru ho gaya, lekin aarthik, paryaavarniya aadi prabhaav ke kaaran isamen deree hui. iske nirmaan kaa kaary 2006 main puraa ho gaya.
