 swadhinata milane se purv ireland desh british shashan ke aadhin tha. 3 may 1921 ko is desh kaa vibhaajan ho gaya or 6 disambar 1922 ko yah you0 ke0 se swatantra hokar ek alag rajya ke rup main sthapitya ho gaya. rajya se ise desh kaa darja 29 disambar 1937 ko praapt hua or san 1949 ko yah desh purna rup se republic of ireland ke naam se duniya ke maanchitr par ankit ho gaya.
 san 1949 main yah desh britain se swatantra to ho gaya parantu aarthik sansadhano ke abhaav main isaki arthvyavastha unnati kii ore agrasar nahin ho saki. san 1973 main yah europea sangh kaa sadsy bana.
