 ghonslaa ek praani vishesh taur par ek pakshi kaa sharan sthal he jahaan par yah andey dete hai, rahete hai or apni santano ko paalate hai. ek ghonslaa aamtaur par carbonic saamagree jaise tahani, ghaas or pattee; aadi se bana hota he par, kabhi kabhi yah jameen main ek gaddha, ped kaa kotor, chattan yaa imaarat main chhed ke rup me bhi ho saktaa he. maanav nirmit dhaage, plastic, kapade, bal yaa kaagaj jaise padaarthon kaa istemaal bhi praani ghonslaa banane me karte hai. 
 aamtaur par prayek prajati ke ghonsle kii ek vishisht style hoti he. ghonslon ko kai alag alag paryaavaason main paaya ja saktaa he. yah mukhyatah pakshiyon dwaara banaaye jate hai par standhari jantu, machhlee, keet or sarisrup bhi ghonslon kaa nirmaan karte hai.
