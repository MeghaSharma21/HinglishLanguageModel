 aruna kumari gaila purv bhartiya sansadeey, saamaajik kaaryakarta putiri rajagopal naidu kii beti he. vah andhra pradesh sarkaar, bharat main bhuvigyaan or khan mantri the or chandragari ummidavaar ke liye vidhayak the.8 march 2014 ko, vah telugu desham party main shaamil hui.
 aruna kumari kii shaadi amara raja group of companyj ke ek udyogpati or sansthaapak dow ramchandra naidu gaila se hui he. unhone bi.es. kii he lake vyuu college se computer science main. unhone chrisler corporation ke liye computer programmer or management information systams ke departmental head ke rup main bikri divijan main kaam kiya. unke raajnneetik jivan main unke purv bhoomika main andhra pradesh mahila congress ke adhyaksh or pradesh congress samiti ke mahasachiv shaamil hai. vah 1989 se 1994, 1999 se 2014 tak chandragiri nirvachan kshetra ke liye aamdaar the or andhra pradesh ke mukhya mantri ke pehle kaarykaal main swasthya shiksha or beemaa mantri the. aruna kumari ne telugu main kai upanyaas likhe.
