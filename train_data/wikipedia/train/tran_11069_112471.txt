 manovigyaan main sanrachnawad yaa sanrachanaatmak manovigyaan, wilhem vunt tathaa edward bradford tichanar dwaara viksit ek chetana siddhanth he.
 manovigyaan ko darshanashastra se alag karke krambaddh adhyayan karne kaa shrey sanrachnawad ko jaataa he, jiske pravartak wiliam bunt tathaa i.bi. tichanar the. inhonen america ke carnell vishvavidhyalay main isaki shuruuaat kii. bunt ne 1879 main lipjing vishvavidhyalay main manovigyaan kii pratham prayogshaala kii sthapana kii or manovigyaan ke swaroop ko prayogtmak bana diya. bunt ne manovigyaan ko chetan anubhuti kaa adhyayan karne vaala mana, jise multah do tatwon main vishleshit kiya ja saktaa tha. ve do tatv the- sanvaden tathaa bhav. bunt ne chetan ko vastunishth tatv bataaya. bunt kaa vichaar tha ki prayek bhav kaa adhyayan teen vibhaaon main avasthit kiya ja saktaa he- uttejnaa-shant, tanaav-shithilan tathaa sukhad-dukhad. ise bhav kaa trivimiya siddhanth kaha gaya. tichanar ne bunt kii vichaardhaara ko unnat banaate huye kaha ki chetana ke do nahin teen tatv hote he- sanvaden, pratimaa tathaa anuraag. tichanar ne bunt ke samaan antahnirikshan ko manovigyaan kii ek pramukh vidhi mana he. bunt ne chetana ke tatwon kii do visheshtaayen bataayi thi- gun tathaa tivrata. tichanar ne inaki sankhya chaar kar di- gun, tivrata, spashtataa tathaa avadhi. tichanar ne dhyaan pratyakshan, saahchary, samvedan aadi kshetron main bhi apana yogadan diya.
