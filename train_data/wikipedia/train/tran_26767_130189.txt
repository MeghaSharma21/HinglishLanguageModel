 aalu ek sabji he. vanaspati vigyaan kii drushti se yah ek tana he. isaki udgam sthaan dakshin america kaa peru he. yah gehun, dhan tathaa makka ke baad sabase zyada ugaai jane vaali fasal he. bharat main yah vishesh rup se uttar pradesh main ugaaya jaataa he. yah jameen ke neeche paidaa hota he. aalu ke utpaadan main china or rus ke baad bharat tisare sthaan par he.
 america vaigyanikon ne ek anusandhan se yah nishkarsh nikaala ki peru ke kisaan aaj se lagbhag 7000 saal pehle se aalu uga rahe hai. solahavin sadi main spain ne apane dakshin america upniveshon se aalu ko europe pahunchaaya uske baad britain jaise deshon ne aalu ko duniya bhar main lokapriy bana diya. aaj bhi ireland tathaa rus kii adhikansh janata aalu par nirbhar he. bharat main yah sab se lokapriy sabji he. ise sabhi parkar ke subjiyon ke milakar pakaa sakate he.
 aalu se anek khaadya saamagree banati he jaise vadapav, chaat, aalu bhari kachaudee, chips, papad, frencfrice, samosa, tikki, chokha aadi. aalu ko anya subjiyon ke saath mila kar tarah-tarah ki pakwaan banaaye jate hai.
 vaise to aalu bharat main zyaadaatar logon kii pasandida sabji he lekin kai log ise adhik charbi vaala samajhakar khaane se parhez karte hai. parantu aalu main kuch upyogi gun bhi hai. aalu main vitamin si, bi complex tathaa aayran, calcium, mangneej, phosphorus tatva hote hai. iske alaava aalu main kai aushadhiy gun hone ke saath saundaryavardhak gun bhi he jaise yadi twachaa kaa koi bhaag jal jaataa he us par kaccha aalu kuchalakar turant laga dene se aaraam miltaa he.
