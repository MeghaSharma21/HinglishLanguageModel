 zabaaykalski kroy rus ke siberia kshetra main sthit ek 'kroy' kaa darja rakhane vaala sangheey khand he. san 2010 main kii gai ek janaganana ke anusaar isaki aabaadi 1,106,611 thi. is kroy kaa kshetrafal 4,31,500 varg kilometre he. dakshin main zabaaykalski kroy kii sarahadein china or mongolia se milti hai.
