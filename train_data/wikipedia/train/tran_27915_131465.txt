 colocium yaa kolicium italy desh ke rome nagar ke madhya nirmit roman samrajya kaa sabase vishaal ellyptical anphithieatre he. yah roman sthapaty or abhiyantriki kaa sarvotkrisht namoona mana jaataa he. iskaa nirmaan tatkaaleen shaasha vaspian ne 70veen - 72veen isvi ke madhya prarambh kiya or 80veen isvi main isko samraat titus ne puraa kiya. 81 or 96 varshon ke bich isamen domitian ke raaj main isamen kuch or parivartan karwaae gaye. is bhawan kaa naam amphithiatrem flavium, vaspian or titus ke paariwarik naam flavius ke kaaran he. 
 andakaar colocium kii kshamta 50,000 darshakon kii thi, jo us samay main saadhaaran baat nahin thi. is stadium main yoddhaon ke bich maatr manoranjan ke liye khooni ladaayeeyaan hua karti thim. yoddhaon ko jaanwaron se bhi ladanaa padataa tha. gladiator baaghon se ladate the. anumaan he ki is stadium ke aise pradarshnon main lagbhag 5 lakh pashuon or 10 lakh manushya maare gaye. iske atirikt puranic kathaon par aadhaarit naatak bhi yahan khele jate the. saal main do baar bhavy aayojan hote the or romanvaasi is khel ko bahut pasand karte the. purv madhyakaal main is imaarat ko saarvajanik prayog ke liye band kar diya gaya. baad main ise nivas, kaaryshaalaaon, dharmik kaaryon, kile or tirth sthal ke rup main prayog kiya jaataa rahaa. 
 aaj ikkisvi shatee main yah bukamp or pathar chori ke kaaran keval khandhar ke rup main hi bachi he lekin iske khandhar ko paryatakon ke liye sajaa sanvarkar rakhaa gaya he. unesco dwaara iskaa chayan vishwa viraasat ke rup main kiya gaya he. yah aaj bhi shaktishaalee roman samrajya ke vaibhav kaa pratik he, paryatakon kaa sabase lokapriy gantavy he or roman carch se nikat sambandh rakhata he kyon ki aaj bhi har good friday ko pope yahan se ek mashaal jaloos nikaalte hai.
