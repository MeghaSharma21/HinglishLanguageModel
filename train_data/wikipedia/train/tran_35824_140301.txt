 malay bodhgamyata ke prayek bindu ke aadhaar par parsparik rup se nikat bhashaaon kaa ek samooh he par bhashavid in sabhi bhashaaon ko alag alag maanate hai. yah sabhi bhashaayen ek bade samooh jise "sthaneey malay" kehete he me samoohikrut kiya gay he, is bade samooh ke ek bhaag ko austronesian bhashaa parivaar kii malayo-polinesian shakha ke antargat "malayan" kaha jaataa he. malay bhashaa vibhinn roopon main bruneii, indonesia, malaysia, singapore or dakshini thailand main boli jaati hai.
 malay bruneii or malaysia kii aadhikarik bhashaa he, jabaki singapore main ise ek aadhikarik bhashaa kaa darja mila hua he. indonesia kii rashtriya bhashaa indonesian he, jise aupchaarik rup se bahaasaa indonesia kaha jaataa he or jiskaa shabdic arth "indonesian bhashaa" he. ise bahaasaa kebangsaan or bahaasaa parsaatuan/ pemerasatu bhi kaha jaataa he. indonesian sainya kabje main 20 varsh se adhik rahane ke kaaran indonesian purvi timore main bhi boli jaati he. malaysia main is bhashaa ko ab aadhikarik rup se bahaasaa malaysia pukaara jaataa he, yadhyapi sanvaidhanik naam bahaasaa melaayu he. singapore, bruneii or dakshini thailand main ise bahaasaa melaayu hi kaha jaataa he.
