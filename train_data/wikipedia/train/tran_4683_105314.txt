 raisina kii pahari bharat kii rajdhani nai dilli ke hriday main sthit he. isapar hi yahan ke raashtrapati kaa aadhikarik awaas va karyaalay raashtrapati bhawan sthit he. raashtrapati bhawan ke nirmaan ke peeche ek lanbaa itihaas he. sankshipt main kahen to raisina hills par bana raaj bhawan desh kaa sabase mahatvapurn bhawan he. yahaan par raashtrapati kaa awaas or karyaalay dono sthit hai. iske chaaron taraf hariyaali or saath main hai ek se ek khubasurat imaaraten- sansad bhawan, india get, vijay chowk or rajpath. raisina hills kaa naam padaa malchaa gaanvon ke raisina pariwaron se. asal main is imaarat ko banane ke liye raisina ke 300 pariwaron kii jameenon kaa adhigrahan kiya gaya or kareeb 4000 acre jameen par imaarat banaai gai. ise banane ke liye 4 saal nirdhaarit kiye gayi the, lekin vishwayuddh ke kaaran ise banane main 19 saal lage. 23 janvari 1931 ko pehali baar angrejon ke jamaane main waisaroy of india lord irvin yahaan rahane aaye. 1950 ke pehle tak ise waisaroy house kaha jaataa tha or is ilaake kaa naam lutiuns tha. raisina hills kaaphi uchaayin par sthit he, lagbhag 18 meter. raisina hills par waisaroy house bana hua tha or angrejon kaa shashan vahiin se chal rahaa tha. 1911 main angrejon ne bharat kii rajdhani kalakatt se dilli sthanantarit karne ke liye raisina hills ke baaki ke ilaake kaa adhigrahan kiya or naye prashasnic bhawan banaae gaye
