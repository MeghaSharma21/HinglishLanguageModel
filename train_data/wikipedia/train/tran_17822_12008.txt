 gurujada apparav aadhunik telegu bhashaa ke prasiddh rashtriya kavi the.
 apparav kaa janm andhra pradesh ke vishakhapatnam kshetra main ek vidwaan parivaar main hua tha.unke pita venkatramdaas sanskrit or telegu ke vidwaan tathaa veydant tathaa jyotish ke gyaata the. inaki aarambhik shiksha ghar par hi hui. vidhyalay main sanskrit or darshan unke priy vishay the.
 kuch samay tak adhyaapan karne ke baad vijaynagar riyasat main naukari kii. yahaan inhen itihaas par shodh karne kaa avasar mila or unhone 'kaling kaa itihaas' naamak granth kii rachana kii. 
 gurujada apparav rashtriya bhaawanaaon se ot prot bade ojasvi kavi the. unki kavitaaon ke anek sangrah prakashit hue. unhone jati bhed, varna bhed, aadi kaa virodh kiya. dharm ke brahmachar or murti puja main unka vishwaas nahin tha. kavita ke saath saath unhone naatakon, kahaniyon or aalochanaon kii bhi rachana kii. ve apni rachanaaon main jansadharan kii bhashaa kaa prayog karte the. vireshillingam ke prayatnon se samaaj sudhaar kii jo lahar paidaa hui thi usko aage badhaane main apparav ne bhi yoga diya.
 unki pramukh rachanaayen nimnlikhit hai-
