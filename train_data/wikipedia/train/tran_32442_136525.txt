 
 nikal ek rasinic tatv he jo rasinic rup se sankraman dhaatu samooh kaa sadsy he. yah ek shwet-chaandi rang kii dhaatu he jisamen zaraa-si sunahari abha bhi dikhti he. yah sakht or tanya hota he. haalaanki nikal ke bade tukdon par oksaide kii parat ban jaati he jis se andar kii dhaatu surakshit rahati he, nikal vaise oxigen se tezi se rasinic abhikriya kar letaa he. is karanvash prithvi kii sataha par nikal shuddh rup main nahin miltaa or agar miltaa he to iskaa strot antariksh se gire louh ulka hote hai. vaigyanik yah maanate hai ki prithvi kaa crode nikal-louh ke mishrit dhaatu kaa bana hua he.
 nikal hawa main upasthit oxigen se saath abhikriya karke apane upar ek patali surakshadyani patali parat bana letaa he jis se andar kii dhaatu bachi rahati he. is vajah se iskaa prayog lohe va anya dhaatuon par nikal kii parat chadhaakar unhen zang lagane se bachaane ke liye kiya jaataa he. yah ek louhachumbakatv rakhane vaala tatv he or is se bane chumbak udyog va anya prayogon main istemaal hote hai. iske alaava nikal ko ispaat main milakar use 'steneles' banane ke kaam main bhi laayaa jaataa he.
