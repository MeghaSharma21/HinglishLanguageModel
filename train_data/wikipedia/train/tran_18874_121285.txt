 2015 ballabhagarh dange wo ghatana he jab hinduon kii bheed ne ataali gaanv, ballabhagarh, haryana ke musalamaanon par hamalaa kar diya tha. 
 ye ghatana 25 may 2015 ko ataali gaanv, ballabhagarh, faridabad main hui. . is ghatana kaa kaaran 30 saal puraani masjid thi. 2009 main hinduon ne ise apni sampati bataaya. musalamaanon kaa kahana tha ki yah sampati vakf board kii he. march 2015 ko faridabad nyaayalay ne muslimon ke paksh ne faisla diya. lekin hinduon ne apana aitaraaz jari rakhaa. kuch lok is masjid ko vidhwans karnaa chaahate the.
 2000 updraviyon kii bheed ne mil kar gaanv par lathion, patharon or aag se hamalaa kar diya. gaanv ke muslim lok gaanv chhod kar bhaag gaye.
