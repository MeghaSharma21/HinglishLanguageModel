 i devanagari lipi kaa saatvaan varna he or ek swar bhi he. anterrashtriya dhvanyatmak varnmaala main iske uccharan ko e ke chinh se likhaa jaataa he. iskaa prayog aadhunik maanak hindi ke bahut se shabdon main hota he or is se rup main milte-julate 'i' or 'ji' swaron ke uccharan se alag he. 'e' kaa swar bahut si anya bhashaaon main bhi miltaa he, jaise kii angrezi, unani or japani.
 'e' ko bhashavigyaan ke nazariye se "ardhasanvruth prasruth agraswar" kaha jaataa he. angreji main ise "chlose-mid front anaroundid vaval" kehete hai.
