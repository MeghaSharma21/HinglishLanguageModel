 narayan bhatta sanskrit ke prakand pandit tathaa pratibhashali the. inhone lagbhag 60 rachanaayen sanskrit main prastut kin. brajbhaktivilas, brajotsav chandrika anek granthon main braj ke sthanon, utsavon aadi kaa vistaar se varnan he. lila sambandhi ek premakur naatak bhi likhaa he. inhone braj ke lupt tirthon ke uddhaar, brajayatra ke prachaar tathaa bhavuk bhakton ke liye rasamandaliyon kii sthapana main bahut prayaas kiya.
 narayan bhatta dakshin bharat ke madura nagar ke maadhv sampradaayi bhriguvanshi brahman bhaskar bhatta ke putr the. mata kaa naam yashomati tha tathaa janm baishakh shu. 14, san. 1588 ko hua tha. 12 varsh kii avasthaa hi main yah grihatyagi ho gaye or yatra karte sanwat 1602 main braj pahunch gaye. shri chaitanya ke parshad gadadhar pandit ke shishy krishnadas brahmacharee radhakund par madanamohan ji kii seva karte the, jinase inhone diksha lee or vahiin rahane lage. 12 varsh ke ananthar yah braj ke unche gaanv main jaakar rahane lage or garhasthya jivan aarambh kiya. yahan baldev ji ko tathaa barasaane main ladily lalji ko pratishthapit kiya, jinaki seva abhi tak inhen ke vanshaj tathaa shishy karte hai. inkaa dehavsaan 17veen shatee ke praaia: ant main vaaman dwadashi ko hua.
