 prema athwa hamakurmaa va hamsawab  premchand kaa pehla upanyaas he. yah 1907 i. main multah urdu main prakashit hua tha. is upanyaas main 12 adhyay hai. yah vidhwaa vivaah par kendrit he. isamen dharmik aadambaron or mandiron main vyaapt paakhand ko ujagar kiya gaya he. yah premchand ke bhavishya kii disha kii ore sanket karne vaala upanyaas he.
