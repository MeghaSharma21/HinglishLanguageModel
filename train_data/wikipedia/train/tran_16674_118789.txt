 avasorana prasheetak aisa prasheetak he jo prasheetak tantra ko chalaane ke liye kisi ooshmaa strot kaa sahaara leti he.
 ammonia avasorana yantra ek prakaar kaa prasheetak yantra he. jo gharon or kaarkaanon main thandak utpann karne ke kaam aata he. avasorana yantron kii upyogita kaa kshetra bahut simit he lekin jab bahut nimna taap apekshit ho to aise yantron kaa mahatv adhik ho jaataa he.
 is yantra kii karypranali chitra dwaara samajhaaii gai he. janitr  main ammonia kaa sandra jaleey ghol bharaa hota he or jwalak se yaa bhaap kii naliyon se isko garam kiya jaataa he. ghol main se ammonia gas nikalakar sanghnitr main dubi sarpil main se jaati he. main sheethal pani nirantar pravaahit hota rahata he. at: sarpil main gas swayan apni hi daab se sanghanit ho jaati he. yah drav ek sankare niamak valve ke maarg se sheet sangrahagar  main rakhi sarpil main pravesh karata he jisamen nimna daab ke kaaran drav vashpit ho jaataa he. valve ko is tarah se samyojit kiya jaataa he ki uske donon siron ke bich daab kaa abhisht antar bana rahe. sheetasangrahagar main se namak kaa ghol pravaahit hota rahata he, jo sarpil main ammonia ke vaashpan se sheethal hota jaataa he or phir kahin bhi jaakar prashithan kaa kaam karata he.
 sarpil main bani ammonia gaus avasorak main rakhe pani yaa ammonia ke tanu ghol dwaara avashoshit hoti rahati he or is prakaar alp daab bana rahata he.
  main ghol sandra hota jaataa he or pump dwaara janitr ke upree bhaag main pahunchaaya jaataa he. iske vipareet janitr ke pende se tanu ghol avasheshk main aata jaataa he. is tarah purna chakree prakram se nirantar prashithan hota rahata he.
