 tamas edward laurence prakhyat british sena ke afasar, anveshak evam lekhak the.
 15 agast 1888 i. main inkaa janm wales ke tramadok naamak sthaan par hua tha. inhone oxfard main shiksha praapt kii thi. 1910 i. main syria gaye or inhone chaar varsh ke apane pravaas main arab logon kaa adhyayan kiya. 1915 i. main ye misr bheje gaye or isi varsh turki britain ke viruddh pratham vishwayuddh main sammilit ho gaya. is samay larens ne arab kabeelon kaa sanghathan tukon ke viruddh kiya, jisase is pradesh main turkiyon kaa prabhaav nasht ho gaya. apane is kaary ke kaaran ye arab ke larens naam se prasiddh hue.
 inkaa 'da seven pilrs ov vijadam' naamak granth prasiddh he. jisamen inhone apane saahasik kaaryon kaa vivaran diya he. 1927 i. main 'revolt in da desert' prakashit hua. 19 may 1935 main ek maarg durghatana main inaki mrityu ho gai.
