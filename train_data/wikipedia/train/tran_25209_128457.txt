 kudukh yaa 'kurukh' ek bhashaa he jo bharat, nepal, bhutan tathaa bangladesh main boli jaati he. bharat main yah bihar, madhya pradesh, chhattisgarh, jharkhand evam pashchim bengal ke uraanv janajatiyon dwaara boli jaati he. yah dravin parivaar se sambandhit he. isko 'uraanv bhashaa' bhi kehete hai.
 chhattisgarh main basane vaali uraanv jati kii boli ko kurukh kehete hai. is bhashaa main tamil or canari bhashaa ke shabdon kii bahutayat he. kudukh bhashaa ko pashchim bengal main rajkeey bhashaa ke rup main farvari 2018 main sweekruti mili thi.
