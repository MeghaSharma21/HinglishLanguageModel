 karnataka sangeet yaa sanskrit main karnataka sangita bharat ke shastreey sangeet kii dakshin bhartiya style kaa naam he, jo uttari bharat kii style hindustani sangeet se kaaphi alag he. 
 karnataka sangeet zyaadaatar bhakthi sangeet ke rup main hota he or zyaadaatar rachanaayen hindu devi devataaon ko sambodhit hota he. iske alaava kuch hissaa prem or anya saamaajik muddon ko bhi samarpit hota he. 
 jaisaa ki aamtaur par bhartiya sangeet me hota he, karnataka sangeet ke bhi do mukhya tatv raag or taal hota he.
 karnataka shastreey style main raagon kaa gayan adhik tej or hindustani style kii tulana main kam samay kaa hota he. tyagaraj, muthuswami deekshitaar or shyama shastri ko karnataka sangeet style kii 'trimurti' kaha jaataa he, jabaki purandar das ko aksar karnataka style kaa pita kaha jaataa he. karnataka style ke vishyon main puja-archana, mandiron kaa varnan, darshanik chintan, nayak-nayika varnan or deshbhakti shaamil hai.
 varnam: iske teen mukhya bhaag pallavi, anupallavi tathaa muktyishwar hote hai. vastav main isaki tulana hindustani style ke thumari ke saath kii ja sakati he.
 jawali: yah prem pradhan geeton kii style he. bharatnatyam ke saath ise vishesh rup se gaaya jaataa he. isaki gati kaaphi tej hoti he.
 tillanaa: uttari bharat main prachlit taraana ke samaan hi karnataka sangeet main tillanaa style hoti he. yah bhakthi pradhan geeton kii gayan style he.
