 dilli dairedevills indian premier lig kii ek franchizi cricket team he jo indian premier lig main dilli ke shahar kaa pratinidhitiv karti he. yah team saal 2008 indian premier lig se hi ipel kaa hissaa rahi he. yah franchiji jiemr samooh dwaara swaamitv he. inkaa gharelu maidan firoz shah kotla ground or chhattisgarh main raipur antarrashtriya cricket stadium hai. 2012 ke champions lig twanti 20 main dilli dairedevills semifinal tak gayi thi or usamen haar kaa saamana karnaa padaa tha. 2018 indian premier lig main gautam gambhir ne captaini chhod di thi is kaaran unki jagah shreyas ayyar nae captain bane he.
