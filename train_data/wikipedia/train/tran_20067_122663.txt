 kohbar uttari-madhya bharat or isse sanlagn nepal ke tarai kshetra main prachlit chitrakaari kii ek vidha he. vivaah ke samay, ghar ke kisi ek kamare main purvi diwaal par gobar se lip kar, pisi haldi or pise chaaval se chitrakaari kii jaati he; iske thik neeche jameen par gobar se lip kar vividh pujan samagriyan rakhi jaati hai. var evam vadhoo paksh donon ke ghar main, vivaah kii vividh rasmon main se ek kaarykram kohbar pujan kaa bhi hota he jisamen bhit par bane is kohbar kaa pujan kiya jaataa he. in kaaryakramon ke dauraan kuch vishesh sanskaar-geet bhi gaaye jate hai jinhen kohbar geet kehete hai or inamen var-vadhoo ke bich prem bhav badhaane kii bhaavana hoti he. kohbar ghar ke us kamare ko bhi kehete hai jahaan yah chitrankan kiya jaataa he. vadhoo ke aagaman par use isi kaksh main rahanaa hota he.
