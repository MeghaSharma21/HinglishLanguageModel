 dravid vishvavidhyalay, kuppam, chitoor jila, andhra pradesh, bharat main sthit vishvavidhyalay he jise andhra pradesh sarkaar dwaara ek vidhanmandal adhinium ke antargat oktober 1997 main sthapit kiya gaya tha jise anya dakshini rajyon karnataka, kerala or tamil nadu kaa bhi samarthan praapt tha. dravid vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana kaa mukhya uddeshy dravid bhashaa bhashiyon kii sajhi viraasat par ekikrut adhyayan dwaara un ke bich ekta kii majboot dharna ko sugam bananaa he.
 is vishvavidhyalay ne 1998 se do vibhaagon ke saath karyarambh kiya tha or ab isamen 20 shaikshanik or 6 shodh kendra hai. i. this. bil. narsinhan yahan ke vartmaan kuladhipati hai.
 vishvavidhyalay ke ab tak ke upkulapatiyon kii suchi is prakaar he:
 kuch dravid bhashaaon main vishvavidhyalay kaa naam is prakaar he:
 varsh 2009-2011 ke dauraan vishvavidhyalay dwaara duurastha shikshaarthiyon ko 8,000 piechdi digriyaan pradaan kii gai thi jiske kaaran yah vishvavidhyalay vivaadon ke ghere main aayaa. sarkaar dwaara is baat kii jaanch ke liye ek samiti gathit kii gai jisane upkulapati par kaarywai karne kii baat kahi. iske turant baad hi ek or vivaad shuru ho gaya kyonki upkulapati dwaara apni sevanivruti se ek din pehle staff niyukt kiya gaya tha.
 ek anya vivaad 2010 ke dauraan chaatro ke daakhile ko lekar tha jiske kaaran may 2011 main chaatro ne virodh kiya or shaikshanik bhawan main lutpaat kii.
