 pakistan ke rajpratikchinh ko san 1954 main pakistan sarkaar dwaara apanaaya gaya tha. is chinh ko mul rup se kul chinh ke rup main vargit kiya ja saktaa he. yah pakistani ganraajya ke aadarshon ko, uski vaichara niv ko, uski arthvyavastha ke mulyon ko evam pakistan kii saanskrithik dharohar or maardarshak siddhanton ko darshata he. ise pakistan sarkaar ke chinh ke rup main bhi upyog kiya jaataa he.is chinh ke mukhya rup se chaar ghatak ang hai:
 dhaal ke chaaron khandon main nav-swatantra pakistan ke mukhya fasalon ko darshit kiya gaya he. chakra-kram main chaaron chauthaaiyon main kapaas, chipatti, gehun evam patson ko darshit kiya gaya he. sahaayak ke rup main darshaai gaipushpamala chameli/jasmin ke phulon kii he jihe paaramparik mogul chitrakala ke laize main banaaya gaya he. antat suchipatr par ankit dhyayvakya qaaed-e-aazam mohammad ali jinnah dwaara diya gaya naaraa imaan, ittehad, nazam ankit he jo pakistan ke maardarshak siddhanth mana jaataa he
