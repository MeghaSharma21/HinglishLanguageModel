 chhindvin uttari verma ke sanging mandal main bahane vaali nadi he, jo irawadi kii mukhya sahaayak he. yah lagbhag 885 kilometre lambii he. chhindvin nadi tanai, taabaan or tarun nadiyon ke milane se bani he. kintu inamen se koun mukhya dhara he, yah sandehaaspad he.
 in nadiyon ke strot hookang ghati ke parshvavarti pahaado main hai. mengine nagar ke nikat chhindvin purvavahini ho jaati he, parantu kuch hi dur baad pun: dakshin-purv kii ore bahane lagati hai or gaani, alon tathaa moniva naamak nagaron se hoti hui irawadi main mil jaati he. muhane se lagbhag 322 kilometre dur kindar nagar ke paas tak nadi varsh bhar nauparivahneey rahati he ore baadh kii avasthaa main naukaaye 209 kilometre upar homalin nagar tak chalati hai.
