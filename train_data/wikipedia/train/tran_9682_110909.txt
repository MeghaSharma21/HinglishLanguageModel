 vivaran:
 shantal sietal ke baahar chote shahar main bade hue. niraash hone ke baavjood vah hameshaa ek model or abhinetri hone kaa sapana dekh rahi thi. 1 9 saal kii umr main usne apane portpholio kaa nirmaan shuru kar diya or erizona main chale gaye jahaan unhone kai naukariyaan kin. do saal ke bheetar usne plaboy ke liye nagn hone kaa faisla kiya or ise apane jivan ke sabase romaanchak dinon main se ek ke rup main varnit kiya. vah ek plaboy sibar girl ke rup main do baar pradarshit hui thi. freelans modelling ke alaava agle kuch varshon main shantila ne vigyaan kii ek associats kii degree hasil kii or arizona state uniwarsity main bhaag lenaa shuru kiya jahaan unhone baio-chemistry kaa adhyayan kiya.
 shaitan ke vigyaan, ganit or unke kaam ke bhaar ke prati samarpan ke baavjood unhen model kii teevr ichha mahasoos hui. yah is samay ke dauraan tha ki vah vastav main modelling duniya main gahari khud ko prerit karti thi. vah vibhinn shoots, adhovastr suchi or ghatnaon par kaam karne ke liye los angeles ke masik yatra karne lagi. may 2014 main unhen plaboy dakshin africa ke dwaara plamet of da mani kaa naam diya gaya tha or ve apane plamet of da year banane ke liye apane janvari / farvari 2016 sanskaran ke aavaran model ke rup main ghoshnaa kii. is suvidha kii safalataa spasht thi jab teen anya plaboy sanskarnon ne sachitr prakashit kiya, jinamein se do ne cover ko phir se prakashit kiya. badhate avasaron ke chalate shantila ne apni schuli shiksha ko aage badhaayaa or los angeles kshetra main apane modelling karier ke vistaar ke iraade se sthanantarit kiya.
