 white masjid israel ke nazarath shahar main sthit sabase puraani masjid he 1or nataketh ke puraane bazaar ke centre main haraat alghama yaa "masjid quarter" main sthit he. isaki utkrisht pencil ke aakaar kaa minar, kriim rang kii deevaaron, hari trim or hare rang kaa gumbad puure shahar main otoman vaastukala kaa ek udhaaharan he.
 masjid kaa nirmaan atharahavin shatabdi ke uttarardh main misr ke shaasha suleman pasha ke dwaara kiya gaya tha, or nazarath ke ucchayukta shaikh abdulla al-fahom ne isaki dekharekh kii. masjid ko 1804 or 1808 ke bich puraa kiya gaya tha or shaikh abdulla ko vakf ke rup main trustiship pradaan kiya gaya tha or 1815 main apni mrityu ke samay tak use prashasit kiya gaya tha. shaikh abdulla kii kabr masjid ke angan main he.shaikh abdulla kii mrityu ke baad, masjid ke maamalon ke prabandhan ko shaikh amin al-fahom main sthanantarit kar diya gaya tha. vartmaan main, masjid al-fahom parivaar vakf kaa hissaa bana hua he.
