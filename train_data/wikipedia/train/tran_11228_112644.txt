 
 trimurti bhartiya kalaon main vah akruti he jise brahma, vishnu or mahesh ko ek hi greeva par teen shish ke rup main chitrit kiya jaataa he. is akruti kaa prayog mandiron, chitrakala, murtikla tathaa anek sthalon par paaya jaataa he. bhartiya sahitya or puraana main in teenon devataaon kii mahatvapurn bhoomika he. yah mana jaataa he ki brahma janm, vishnu paalan or shiv sanhaar ke devtaa hai or sari sristi inhen ke dwaara sanchalit hoti he.
