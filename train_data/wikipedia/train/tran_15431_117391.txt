 hridayaghat aaj manushya-jati ke sarvaadhik pranaleva rogon main se pramukh he jo maansik tanaav evam avsaad se judaa hua he. doshapurn bhojan-vidhi, madhpan, dhumrapaan, maadak padaarthon kaa seven tathaa vishramrahit parishram ityadi hriday-rog ke pramukh kaaran hai. iske nidaan ke liye jivan-style main parivartan laane kii aavashyaktaa pramukh he. aadhunik sabhyataa kaa yah abhishaap he ki manushya ke jivan main keval bahya paristhitiyaan hi dawaab utpann nahin karti balki insaan swayan bhi kaalpanik evam mithyaa dawaab utpann karke apane ko akaaran hi peedit karne lagata he. jab maanav mastishk ko koi utejnapurna sandesh miltaa he, kuch granthiyon me tatkaal srav prarambh ho jaataa he jiske prabhaav se saare deh yantra main visheshat: hriday tathaa rakt-sanchaar main ek uthal-puthal si much jaati he, jiskaa abhas naadee ke bhadakne se hone lagata he. hriday sharir kaa atyant samvedansheel ang he tathaa saare jivan bina kshambhar vishram kiye hue hi nirantar sakriy rahakar samast avyavon ko rakt tathaa poshak tatwon kii aapurti karata he. mastishk kaa atyaadhik dawaab hriday main itani dhadkan utpann kar detaa he ki use sahasaa aghat ho jaataa he. 
 yadhyapi hriday aghat ke kaaran anek hai tathaapi dabaav hi aghat kaa pramukh kaaran he. pushtiprad bhojan lete rahane par bhi maansik dabaav hriday ko aahat kar detaa he. dirghakaal tak maansik dabaav evam tanaav ke rahane par pet main vran, bhayanak sirdard, amlapit, twachaa-rog ityadi bhi utpann ho jate hai.
 manushyon main vyaktigat bhed hote hai tathaa bahya paristhitiyon ko hi sara dosha dena uchit nahin he. kuch log anyant visham paristhiti main bhi paryaapt seemaa tak sam or shant rahete hai tathaa kuch anya saadhaaran-si vishmata main bhi ashaant ho jane ke kaaran sahasaa bheeshan hriday-rog se aahat ho jate hai. atev yah spasht he ki manushya kaa doshapurn swabhaav athwa doshapurn chintan hi hridayghaat kaa pramukh kaaran he. 
 hamen yah samaj lenaa chahie ki praaia: maansik dawaab bahya paristhiti kii pratikriya hota he kintu prayek vyakti kii maansik pratikriya bauddhik-shakti ke bhed ke kaaran bhinn hoti he. atev prayek vyakti ko jivan main apni chintan-shakti evam sankalp-shakti ko jagaate rahane kaa nirantar praytn karte rahanaa chahie tathaa apane swabhaav evam samarthy tathaa paristhitiyon ke anusaar hi vyavhaar karnaa chahie.prayek manushya ko apane maansik dabaav or usase utpann tanaav ke mul kaaran ko apane bheetar hi arthath apni chintan-style evam swabhaav ke bheetar hi khojkar uske samaadhaan kaa budhimatapurna upaay karnaa chahie. atev yah atyant aavashyak he ki ham apane vichaaron or swabhaav ko puri tarah se jaane jisase ki ham vivek ke sadupyog dwaara apane maansik dabaavon kaa niyantran evam niraakaran kar saken. hamen aatm-vishleshan dwaara apane gun, apane dosha, apane vishwaas, apni aasthaaen, manyataayen, apane bhay or chintaaen, apni durbalataayen, hinataayen, apni samasyaayein, apane daayitv, apni abhiruchi, samarthy or simaaen jaanakar apane chintan, swabhaav evam jivan-style main aavashyak parirvatan laane kaa praytn karnaa chahie.
