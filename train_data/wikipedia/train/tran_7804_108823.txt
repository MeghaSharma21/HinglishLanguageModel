 mahabharat ke anusaar ahichhatra uttar paanchal kii rajdhani tha. sabase pracheen lekh main adhichhatra miltaa he.
 uttar pradesh ke bareli jile ke aanwala stesan se koi 10 kimi uttar pracheen ahimhatra ke avshesh aaj bhi vartmaan hai. inamen koi teen mile ke trikanakar ghere main iton kii kilabandi ke bheetar bahut se unche-unche tile hai. sabase unchaa tila 75 foot kaa he. karnigham ne sabase pehle vahaan kuch khudaai karai or baad main furar ne uskaa anusaran kiya. 1940-44 main yahan chune hue sthanon kii khudaai hui jisamen bhuri mitti ke thikre mili. mahabharatakaal kaa to koi pramaan yahan nahin mila, par shunga, kushaan or guptkaal kii anek mudraayen, pathar or mitti kii murtiyan milin. baad ke kaal ke rahane ke sthaan, sadaken or mandiron ke avshesh bhi mili hai.
 mahabharat ke anusaar uttar paanchaal kii rajdhani ahimhatra ko kuruon ne vahaan ke raaj se chhinakar dron ko de diya tha. kaha jaataa he, dron ne drupad ko apane shishyon kii sahaayta se haraakar pratishedh liya tha or uskaa aadha rajya baant liya tha. ahimhatra ke paanchaal janapad kaa itihaas i.pus. chhathi shatabdi se miltaa he. tab yah 16 janapadon main se ek tha. mudraon or lekhon se gyaat hota he ki i.pus. pehali shatabdi main mithravansh ke raajaaon ne ahimhatra main raaj kiya. kuch vidwaanon ne is vansh ko shunga raajaaon kaa vansh siddh karne kaa prayaas kiya. par vastav main ye prantiy shaasha the, jaisaa is vansh kii lambii, mudrankin naamon ke aadhaar par bani, talika se prathit hota he. iske baad kaa itihaas nahin miltaa. guptasamrajya main ni:andeh yah ek bhukti tha. chini chatri yuvan chwang ne yahan par 10 buddha vihaar or nau mandir dekhe the. 11veen shatabdi main iskaa raajnneetik mahatv jaataa rahaa.
