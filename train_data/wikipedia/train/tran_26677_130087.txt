 hawa ke prachandtapurvak chakran karne vaale stambh ko bavandar kaha jaataa he jo prithvi kii sataha or kapaasi varshi badal donon ko jodta he. kuch durlabh avasthaaon main aisa bhi paaya jaataa he ki yah kapaasi megh kaa aadhaar hota he. inhen aksar twisters athwa chakravaat kaha jaataa he. haalanki mausam vigyaan main 'chakravaat' shabd kaa prayog vyapak arth main alpadaab vaale parisancharan ke liye kiya jaataa he. 
 bavandar vibhinn aakaar or akritiyon vaale hote hai lekin ve aamtaur par sankshepan keep ke rup main khikhte hai jinka sankeern bhaag prithvi kii sataha ko sparsh karata he or iskaa doosraa sira dhul ke badalon dwaara gher liya jaataa he. adhiktar bavandaron main hawa kii gati 110 mile prati ghanta se kam or lagbhag 250 feet se adhik hoti he tathaa chhitarane se purv kuch milon tak chalata he. mukhya charam maan tak pahunchne vaale bavandar 300 mile prati ghanta se bhi adhik gati praapt kar sakate hai tathaa 3 kimi se bhi adhik vistaarit ho sakate hai evam dariinon mile prithvi kii sataha par chal sakate hai.
