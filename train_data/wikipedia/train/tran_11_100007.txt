 swatantra bharat lakhanu evam kanpur se prakashit hindi kaa ek hindi dainik samachar patr he.
 15 agast 1947 ko lakhanu se 'swatantra bharat' kaa prakaashan ashok ji ke sampaadan main hua. prarambh main hi isane avadh kii samskruti, log jivan or itihaas par bahut dhyaan diya. dainik vyangy vinod kaa stambh kaanv-kaanv isaki visheshta thi. ashok ji or baldev prasad mishra is stambh ke mukhya lekhak rahe. san 1953 main ashok ji ke kendriya soochna vibhaag main chale jane par sahakaari yogendra pati treepathi ne iskaa sampaadan sambhaala or 31 agast 1971 i ko apni asamyik mrityu tak ise badi yogyata se chalaaya. uttarapradesh kii raajnneeti main is patr kaa acha prabhaav he. kaanv-kaanv, agralekh tippanee, vyangy-chitra, desh chakra, desh-deshaantar, videsh-charchaa, aapke vichaar, suzav-shikaayat, rajyon kii chiththiyaan aadi iske sthaayee stambh hai.
