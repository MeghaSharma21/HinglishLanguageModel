 amla ek rasinic yogic he, jo jal main ghulkar hydrogen aayan detaa he. iskaa pH maan 7.0 se kam hota he. johans nicholas bronsted or martin lorie dwaara di gai aadhunik paribhaasha ke anusaar, amla vah rasinic yogic he jo pratikaarak yogic ko hydrogen aayan pradaan karata he. jaise- asitic amla or salphurik amla . amla, thos, drav yaa gas, kisi bhi bhautik avasthaa main paae ja sakate hai. ve shuddh rup main yaa ghol ke rup main rah sakate hai. jis padaarth yaa yogic main amla ke gun paae jate hai ve kehalaate hai. maanav antra main hydrochloric amla kii adhikta se hone vaali beemaari ko amlata yaa acidity kehete hai.
 amla un padaarthon ko kehete hai jo pani main ghulne par khattey swaad ke hote hai, haldi se bani roli ko pilaa kar dete hai,tathaa inkaa jaleey vilyan nile litmus paper ko laal karata he. adhikansh dhaatuon par abhikriya karke hydrogen gas utpann karte hai, or ksharak ko udasin kar dete hai. mote hisaab se ksharak un padaarthon ko kehete hai jinka vilyan chikna-chikna sa lagata he, swaad kadva hota he, haldi ko laal kar dete hai or amlon ko udasin karte hai. udasin karne kaa arth he aise padaarth kaa bananaa jisamen na amla ke gun hote hai, na ksharak ke.
 lavaazie ne oxygen ke gunon kaa adhyayan karte samay dekha ki carbon, gandhak or phosphorus sadrush tatv jab oxygen main jalte hai tab unase bane oxide jal ke saath milakar amla banaate hai. ve is parinaam par pahunche ki amlon main oxygen rahata he or amlon kii amleeyta kaa kaaran oxygen he. isi kaaran is gas kaa naam "oxygen" padaa, jiskaa arth hota he "amla banaanevaala padaarth" tathaa isi kaaran german bhashaa main oxygen ko "sir staf" arthath amla padaarth kehete hai.
 lavaazie ne hi amlon ko do virgon, acarbonic amalon or carbonic amlon main vibhakt kiya tha. peeche dekha gaya ki kuch tatwon ke oxide pani main ghulkar amla nahin balki kshar banaate hai or kuch amlon main oxygen bilkul nahin hota. bertile ne san 1787 main hydrosienic amla, davy ne san 1810-11 main hydrochloric amla or san 1813 main hydrodic amla kaa avishkaar kiya. inamen se kisi main oxygen nahin he.
 aage chalakar dekha gaya he ki jo padaarth bilkul sookhe hote hai, unamem koi amleey abhikriya nahin hoti. tab logon ne amlon ko do virgon main vibhakt kiya, ek hydro-amla or doosraa oxy-amla. peeche san 1815 main davy ne suzav rakhaa ki amlon kii amleeyta oxygen ke kaaran nahin, varan hydrogen ke kaaran he. duulang ne san 1815 main oxylik amla kaa adhyayan kiya or is parinaam par pahunche ki oxynwaale or bina oxynwaale amlon main koi bhed nahin he.
 amlon main koi aisa gun nahin he jise ham amlon kaa vishisht lakshan kah saken. saadhaaran gun upar bataae ja chuke hai. amla or dhaatu kii abhikriya main amla ke anu kaa ek, yaa ek se adhik, hydrogen parmaanu dhaatuon, dhaatuon ke oxidon, hydroxidon athwa carboneton se visthapit ho jaataa he.
 aise bhi kuch amla hai jo khattey hone ke badale mithe hote hai. aisa ek amla amido-fosphrik amla he. kuch aise bhi amla hai jo ksharhar nahin hote. kuch aise bhi kshar hai jinka hydrogen dhaatuon se visthapit ho jaataa he. fitkaree amla nahin he. isamen visthapit honevaala koi hydrogen bhi nahin he. par yah swaad main khatta or kriya main ksharhar hota he. yah nile litmus ko laal bhi kar detaa he. isi prakaar sodium baislphide khatta or ksharhar hota he. yah nile litmus ko laal karata he. isamen visthapit honevaala hydrogen bhi he, par yah amla nahin he. mithen amla nahin he, par iskaa hydrogen jaste se visthapit ho jaataa he or is prakaar zinc diemethil banataa he jo lavan nahin he.
 at: amla kii koi santoshprad paribhaasha ab tak nahin di ja saki he. aayan siddhant ke aadhaar par yadi ham amlon kii paribhaasha dena chahen to kah sakate hai ki amlon main hydrogen yanon kaa rahanaa atyaavashyak he.
 silavian ne san 1659 main pehle pahal amlon or ksharakon main vibhed kiya tha. rule ne san 1774 main ksharak naam us padaarth ko diya jo amlon ke saath milakar lavan banaata he. aajkal ksharak un oxygen vaale padaarthon ko kehete hai jo amlon ke poorak hote hai. kshar dhaatuon, kshariyamruda dhaatuon or anya dhaatuon ke oxide or ve sabhi vastuen ksharak hai jo amlon ke saath milakar lavan banaati hai. aarambh main ksharak keval un dhaatuon athwa dhaatuon ke oxidon ke liye vyavahrat hota tha jo lavenon ke "base" yaa aadhaar the. lavenon ke ksharak aavashyak avyav hai.
 ksharak vastav main ve padaarth hai jo amla ke saath milakar lavan or jal banaate hai. udhaahrant:, zinc oxide salphurik amla ke saath milakar zinc sulphate or jal banaata he. daahak soda salfurik amla ke saath milakar sodium sulphate or jal banaata he. dhaatuon ke oxide saamanyat: ksharak hai. par iske apwaad bhi hai.
 ksharakon main dhaatuon ke oxide or hydraksaide hai, par suvidha ke liye tatwon ke kuch aise samooh bhi rakhe gaye hai jo amlon ke saath milakar bina jal bane hi lavan banaate hai. aise ksharakon main ammonia, hydraxylemin or phosphin hai. drav ammonia ghul jaataa he par phinolfaileen se koi rang nahin detaa. at: kahan tak yah ksharak kaha ja saktaa he, yah baat sandigdh he.
 yadhyapi upar kii ksharak kii paribhaasha badi asantoshprad he, tathaapi isse acchhee paribhaasha nahin di ja saki he. ksharak or kshar paryaayavachi jaabd nahin hai. sab kshar ksharak hai par sab ksharak kshar nahin hai. kshar-dhaatuon ke oxide, jaise sodium oxide, jal main ghulkar hydraksaide banaate hai. ye prabal ksharkeey hote hai. kshareey mrudadhatuon ke oxide, jaise kellisyam oxide, jal main alp viley or alp kshareey hote hai. anya dhaatuon ke oxide jal main ghulte nahin or unke hydraksaide paroksh ritiyon se hi banaae jate hai.
 dhaatuon ke oxide or hydraksaide ksharak hote hai. kshar dhaatuon ke oxide jal main sheeghra ghul jate hai. kuch dhaatuon ke oxide jal main kam viley hote hai or kuch dhaatuon ke oxide jal main tanik bhi viley nahin hai. kuch adhaatuon ke hydride, jaise nitrogen or phosphorus ke hydride bhi bhasma hote hai.
