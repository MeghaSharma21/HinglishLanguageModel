 bikaner ke tatkaaleen maharaja anupasingh ]dwaara apane sankalan kii pandulipiyon or nijee pustkaalay kii pustakon se armbh kiya gaya]lalgarh peles, bikaner main avasthit ek anoothaa pustak-centre, jisamen anekanek granthon or pandulipiyon kaa vyavasthit sankalan he.] 
 maharaja anupasingh ko aurangzeb ne aurangabad kaa shaasha niyukt kiya tha.] vir hone ke saath-saath vah swayan vidwaan va sangitajna bhi the or bade pustak-premi the.] un ke darbar main bhav bhatta jaise sangitajna or kai vidwaan aashray pate the. inke aashray main rahakar tatkaaleen chitrakaaron ne ek moulik kintu sthaneey parimarjeet chitrastyli bikaner kalam ko janm diya. ] yah pustkaalay unheen anup singh ]ke naam par he. 
 maharaj anupasingh ke bare main likhaa gaya he ]-..."maharaja anupasingh vir, kootneetijna or vidyanuragi shaasha tha. usne 'anupavivek', 'kamprabodh', 'shradhprayog chintamani' or 'gitgovind' par ‘anoopoday’ tikaa likhi thi. use sangeet se prem tha. usne dakshin main rahete hue anek granthon ko nasht hone se bachaaya or unhen kharidkar apane pustkaalay ke liye le aayaa. kumbha ke sangeet granthon kaa puraa sangrah bhi usne ekatra karavaaya tha. aaj anup pustkaalay alabhya pustakon kaa bhandar he, jiskaa shrey anupasingh ke vidyanurag ko he. dakshin main rahete hue usne anek murtiyon kaa sangrah kiya or unhen nasht hone se bachaaya. murtiyon kaa yah sangrah bikaner ke ‘‘taitis karod devataaon ke mandir’’ main surakshit he...."
