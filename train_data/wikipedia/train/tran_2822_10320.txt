 harela ek hindu tyohaar he jo mul rup se uttrakhand rajya ke kumaun kshetra main manaaya jaataa he . harela parv vaise to varsh main teen baar aata he- 
 kintu uttrakhand main shravan maas main padane vaale harela ko hi adhik mahatv diya jaataa he! kyonki shravan maas shankar bhagwaan ji ko vishesh priy he. yah to sarvavidit hi he ki uttrakhand ek pahari pradesh he or pahaado par hi bhagwaan shankar kaa vaas mana jaataa he. isliye bhi uttrakhand main shravan maas main padane vaale harela kaa adhik mahatv he.
 sawan lagane se nau din pehle asadha main harela bone ke liye kisi thalinuma paatr yaa tokari kaa chayan kiya jaataa he. isamen mitti daalkar gehun, jau, dhan, gahat, bhatta, urad, sarson aadi 5 yaa 7 prakaar ke bijon ko bo diya jaataa he. nau dinon tak is paatr main roj subah ko pani chhidakte rahete hai. dasven din ise kaata jaataa he. 4 se 6 inch lambe in paudhon ko hi harela kaha jaataa he. ghar ke sadsy inhen bahut aadar ke saath apane sheesh par rakhte hai. ghar main sukh-samruddhi ke pratik ke rup main harela boyaa va kaata jaataa he! iske mul main yah manyata nihit he ki harela jitna bada hoga utani hi fasal badhiya hogi! saath hi prabhu se fasal acchhee hone kii kaamana bhi kii jaati he.
 is din kumaunni bhashaa main geet gaate hue chhoton ko aashirwad diya jaataa he –
 “ji raye, jaagi raye, tishtiye, penapiye,dub jas hari jad ho, byar jas faiye,himaal main hyun chhan tak, gang jew main panni chhan tak,yo din or yo maas bhetnai raye, agasa chaar ukaav, dharti chaar chakaav he jaaye,syav kas buddhi ho, syu jas paraan ho.”
