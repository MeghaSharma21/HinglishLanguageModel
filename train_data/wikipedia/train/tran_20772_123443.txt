 anglo-sexon bhashaa, puraani angrezi yaa anglisk vah bhashaa he jo aaj ke england main 450 se 1100 isvi ke kaal main boli jaati thi. yah ek jarmaini bhashaa he jo us kaal main germany or denmark se aaye anglo-sackson log bolaa karte the. 
 puraani angrezi aadhunik angrezi se bahut bhinn he; or isamen bahut se german shabd hai. iskaa vyakaran bahut kathin he or yah german bhashaa ke adhik nikat he. vyakaran kii drushti se yah anya pracheen hind-irani bhashaaon ke qareeb samajhi jaati he, jaise ki latini, sanskrit or pracheen unani bhashaa. puraani angrezi kaa swaroop wiliam vijayi ke norman akraman ke baad madhya angrezi main badla or unhone 300 varshon tak ise vidyalayon main padhaane par roak laga di.
