 sweden kii countiyaan sweden ke sabase oonche str ke prashasnic vibhaag hai. inhen swidi bhashaa main 'lain' kaha jaataa he. vartmaan main sweden 21 countiyon main baanta gaya he. inhen san 1634 main sthapit kiya gaya tha. us se pehle sweden kuch praanton main vibhajit tha jinhen tab radd kar diya gaya. in countiyon ko aage nagar-palikaon main vibhajit kiya jaataa he.
