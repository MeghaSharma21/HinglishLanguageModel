 drakshashetra yaa angoorbaghan angoor phalne vaali lataaon kaa baagaan hota he. in angooron kaa prayog haalaa banane ke liye hota he, lekin kuch maatra main khaane-vaale angoor, kishmish or angoor ras kaa bhi utpaadan hota he. drakshakshetron main lataaon or angooron kii dekharekh or krishi ko drakshakrishi kehete hai.
