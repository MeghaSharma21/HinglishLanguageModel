 taak thailand kaa ek praant he. yah pashchimi thailand kshetra main malay prayadweep ke uttari bhaag main sthit he or isaki pashchimi seemaa burma ke kayin rajya ke saath satee hui he. donon desh kii yahan kii seemaa par tenacerim pahadiyaan or daavna pahadiyaan ek prakritik sarhad ke rup main vistrut hai.
 praant ke lagbhag 25% log janajatiyon ke sadsy hai. in janajati samudaayon main karen, yaao, akha, lahu, hmong ar lisu pramukh hai.
