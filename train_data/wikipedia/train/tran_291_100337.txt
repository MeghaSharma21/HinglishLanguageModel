 philipino gart, jo kabhi-kabhi mindanaao gart bhi kehalaata he, pashchimi prashanth mahaasaagar ke philipino sagar bhaag main sthit ek 1,320 kimi tak chalane vaala ek mahasagariya gart he. is gart kii chaudaai lagbhag 30 kimi he or iskaa sabase gaharaa bindu samudratal se lagbhag 10,540 meter neeche he. yah prithvi kaa teesraa sabase nichalaa sthaan he. 
 philipino gart philipinoz ke luzone dweep se purv main armbh hokar dakshinpurvi disha main chalakar indonesia ke maluku dweepasmooh ke uttari bhaag main halamahera dweep ke paas tak chalata he. is gart ke uttar main purv luzone gart he or in donon garton ke bich philipino sagar plate ke upar sthit benham pathaar aata he. philipino gart do bhugolic takhton ke aapasi takaraav main banane vaale nimnaskhalan kshetra kaa nateeja he.
