 sami bhashaayen yaa samatic bhashaayen 27 karod se adhik logon dwaara boli jane vaali bhashaaon kaa ek bhashaa parivaar he jo swayan sami-hami bhashaa-parivaar kii ek upashakha he. yah bhashaayen madhya purv, uttar africa or africa ke sing ke kshetron main boli jaati hai. sab se adhik boli jane vaali sami bhashaa arabi he, jise 20 karod se adhik log apni maatrubhaasha ke rup main bolte hai. iske alawa amharik, tigrinya, ibrani or aramike kuch anya prachlit sami bhashaayen hai.
 sami bhashaaon ko itihaas main bahut hi jaldi likhit rup main dekha gaya tha. san 3000 isapurv main sumer sabhyataa dwaara viksit ankan lipi kaa prayog eblai or akkadi bhashaaon ke liye armbh ho gaya jo sami bhashaayen thim. sami bhashaayen ab ibrani, syrian, arabi or geez lipi main likhi jaati hai. inamen aksar swaron ko prayog nahin kiya jaataa kyonki adhiktar sami bhashaaon main arth vyanjanon se hi aata he. hind-europea bhashaaon or sami bhashaaon main ek bada antar yah he ki hind-europea bhashaaon main zyaadaatar har shabd kii ek jad hoti he jiske aage-peeche akshar jodakar uskaa arth parivartit kiya jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye 'likh' jad main akshar jodakar 'likhaai', 'likho', 'likhnaa', 'likhaa', ityadi banate hai. sami bhashaaon main jade aam taur par teen vyanjanon kaa samooh hoti hai jinke bich main swar bharkar unka arth parivartit kiya jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye arabi main: kitaab, qutub, kaatib, kuttaab, kataba, yaktubu . isamen k-t-ba ke vyanjanon ke bich main swar badalakar matlab badale ja rahen hai.
 angrezi main "sami bhashaaon" ko "samatic langvejiz" kehete hai.
