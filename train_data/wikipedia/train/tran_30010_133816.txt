 reetha yaa areetha ek vriksh he jo lagbhag harajgah bharatvarsh main paaya jaataa he. iske pattey gooler ke patton se bade, chaal bhuri tathaa fal gucchhon main hote hai. isaki do jatiyaan hai. pehali sapindoos mookorossi or duusari sapindoos trifolyatus . 
 sapindoos mookorossi ke junglee ped himalay ke kshetra main adhik paye jate jate hai. iske atirikt uttar bharat main tathaa assam aadi main lagaaye hue ped baag-bagichon main yaa gaanvon ke aasapaas paye jate hai. iske falon ko pani main bhigone or mathne se phen utpann hota he or isse sooti, oonee tathaa reshmi sab prakaar ke kapade tathaa bal dhoe ja sakate hai. ayurveda ke mat main yah fal tridoshnashak, garam, baari, garbhpaatak, vamanakaarak, garbhashay ko nishcesht karanevaala tathaa anek vishon kaa prabhaav nasht karenwala he. sambhavat: vamanakaarak hone kaaran hi yah vishnashak bhi he. vaman ke liye isaki maatra do se chaar mashas tak bataai jaati he. fal ke churna ke gadhe ghol kii buundonko naak main daalane se adhakapari, mirgee or vatonmad main laabh hona bataaya gaya he.
 sapindoos trifolyatus ke ped dakshin bharat main paae jate hai, isamen 3-3 fal ek saath jude hote hai. iske falon kii akruti vrikkakaar hoti he or alag hone par jude hue sthaan par hridayakar chinh paaya jaataa he. ye pakane par laalimaa liye bhure rang ke hote hai. duusare prakaar ke vriksh se praapt bijon se tel nikaala jaataa he, jo aushadhi ke kaam aata he. is vriksh se gond bhi miltaa he.
