 uresia ek bhugolic bhukhand he jo europe or asia mahadweep ko milakar namit he. uresia mukhyatah uttari tathaa purvi golardh main sthit he. iske pashchim main atlantic mahaasaagar, purab main prashanth mahaasaagar, uttar main arctic mahaasaagar or dakshin main africa mahadweep, bumadhya sagar tathaa hind mahaasaagar sthit hai. europe or asia ko do alag-alag mahadweep kahana ek aitihaasik evam saanskrithik vibhaajan he jabaki donon ke bich main koi spasht bhautik vibhaajak he hi nahin. isi kaaran vishwa ke kuch deshon main uresia ko hi paanch yaa chah mahadwipon main sabase bada mana jaataa he.
