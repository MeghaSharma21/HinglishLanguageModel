 jaipur rajya ke maharaja savai madhosingh pratham pramukh maharaja the. rajasthan ke savai madhopur shahar kii sthapana jaipur ke purv maharaja savai madhosingh ne 1765 isvi ke lagbhag kii thim. savai madhosingh pratham kii vajah se savai madhopur shahar 17veen va 18veen shatabdi ke aasapaas ek khyaati praapt shahar mana jane laga tha. rajasthan sarkaar ne 15 may 1949 main savai madhopur shahar ko savai madhosingh ke naam par jila banaakar unke dwaara sthapit shahar kaa sammaan badha diya. tab se lekar aaj tak jile kaa naam savai madhopur ke rup main bharat str par janaa jaataa he.
