 ksharan prerakatv ek vidyut-raashi he jo yah ingit karti he ki do kundaliyaan chumbakeey rup se kitni acchhee tarah yugmit hai. jab ek kundalee dwaara utpann adhikansh chumbakeey flux duusari kundali main bhi chalaa jaataa he to ham kehete hai ke ye donon kundaliyaan acchhee-tarah yugmit hai. isi ko duusare shabdon main kehete hai ki inke bich ksharan prerakatv bahut kam he. transformar kii vinding ke liye ksharan prerakatv kaa bahut mahatv he.
