 kaashew ek prakaar kaa ped he jiskaa fal sookhe mewa ke liye bahut lokapriy he. kaashew kaa aayaat niryaat ek bada vyapaar bhi he. kaashew se anek prakaar kii mithaiyaan or madiraa bhi banaai jaati he.
 kaashew kaa ped teji se badhane vaala ushnkaribandheey ped he jo kaashew or kaashew kaa beej paidaa karata he. kaashew kii utpatti brazil se hui he. kintu aajkal isaki kheti duniya ke adhikansh deshon main kii jaati he. saamaanya taur par kaashew kaa ped 13 se 14 meter tak badhata he. haalanki kaashew kii bauni kaltiver prajati jo 6 meter kii oonchai tak badhata he, jaldi taiyaar hone or zyada upaj dene kii vajah se bahut phydemand sabit ho rahaa he. 
 kaashew kaa upbhog kai tarah se kiya jaataa he. kaashew ke chhilake kaa istemaal paint se lekar snehak tak main hota he. asian deshon main adhikansh tateey ilaake kaashew utpaadan ke bade kshetra hai. kaashew kii vyavsaayik kheti dinon-din lagaataar badhati ja rahi he kyonki kaashew sabhi ahm kaaryakramon yaa utsavon main alpaahar yaa naashtaa kaa jaruri hissaa ban gaya he. videshi bazaaron main bhi kaashew kii bahut acchhee maang he. kaashew bahut teji se badhane vaala ped he or isame paudharopan ke teen saal baad phool aane lagate hai or uske do maheene ke bheetar pakakar taiyaar ho jaataa he. bageeche kaa behatar prabandhan or zyada paidaavaar denevaale prakaar kaa chayan vyavsaayik utpaadakon ke liye behad phydemand sabit ho saktaa he.
