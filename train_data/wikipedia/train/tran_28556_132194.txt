 bharat main jila str par nyaay dene ke liye nirmit nyaayalay jila nyaayalay kehalaate hai. ye nyaayalay ek jila yaa kai jilon ke logon ke liye hote hai jo jansankhya tathaa mukaddamon kii sankhya ko dekhte hue tay kii jaati he. ye nyaayalay us pradesh ke ucch nyaayalay ke prashasnic niyantran main kaam karte hai. jila nyaayalay dwaara die gaye nirnyon ko sambandhit ucch nyaayalay main chunauti di ja sakati he.
