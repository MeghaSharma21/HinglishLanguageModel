 sarvatmavaad
 anya negrito jatiyaan, visaaya jatiyaan, or philippini
 batak ek negrito maanav jati he jo dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh ke palaavan dweep ke purvottaree bhaag main rahati he. inaki jansankhya keval 500 ke aasapaas bachi he. inkaa luzone ke aeta logon se kareebi sambandh mana jaataa he. inkaa kad chota, rang gadha or bal ghunghraale hote hai. dhyaan den ki yah batak janajati indonesia ke sumatra dweep par milane vaali batak jati se bilkul bhinn he.
 aadhunik kaal se 20,000 se 30,000 varsh pehle himyug ke dauraan samudri sataha neeche thi kyonki bahut-sa jal barf ke rup main jamaa hua tha. bhuvaigyanik maanate hai ki tab borneo ke dweep or philipinoz ke bich ek jamini pul astitv main tha jo himyug kii samaapti par samudr main dub gaya. mana jaataa he ki aati, aeta, batak va anya philippini negrito logon ke purvaj isi pul par chalakar dasiyon hazaar saal purv philipinoz ke visaaya kshetra main pahunche the.
 batak adhiktar sarvatmavaadi hote hai lekin inke dharm main kuch hindu dharm ke tatv bhi milte hai. ve maanate hai ki prakruti main kai atmaaen basati hai. kuch "panyeon" kahalaane vaali atmaaen manushyon ko andeh se dekhati hai lekin unhen adhiktar akelaa chhod doti hai. kuch "divaataa" dayaalu hoti hai or manushyon ko madad karne kii cheshtaa karti hai. in atmaaon se sampark karne ke liye ojhaaon kii sahaayta lee jaati he.
