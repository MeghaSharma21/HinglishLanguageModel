 estadio santiyago bernboo madrid, spain main sthit footbal stadium he. iskaa udghaatan 14 disambar 1947 ko kiya gaya. yah real madrid ke swaamitv main he. isaki vartmaan kshamta 81.044 darshakon kii he.real madrid ke purv adhyaksh santiyago bernboo ke sammaan main namakaran kiya gaya santiyago bernboo stadium duniya ke sabase prasiddh or pratishthit footbal maidaanon main se ek he. yah chaar baar antim europea cap kii mejabani kar chukaa he: 1957, 1969, 1980 or 2010 main yuiefee champions lig final. 1964 europea raashtra cap or 1982 fifa vishwa cap kaa final match bhi yaheen yojit kiya gaya tha.
