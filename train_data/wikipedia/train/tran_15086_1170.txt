 "naga regiment" bhartiya sena kaa ek sainya-dal he. naga regiment bhartiya sena ke ek paidal sena kii regiment he. yah bhartiya sena kii sabase kam umr kii regimenton main se ek he - 1970 main ranikhet main pehali batalian uthaaya gaya tha. regiment mukhyatah nagaland se bhartee hoti he. 
 vartmaan main, naga regiment main 3 niyamit batalian or 1 pradeshik sena batalian hote hai.
 naga yuvaaon ne kargil yuddh main prashansa arjeet kii. puure rajya main 325 nag yuvaaon kii bhartee ke liye sena ne sabhi shreniyon ke padon ke liye vishesh bhartee abhiyaan chalaaya. jiosi nagaland, major general aar en capur, ne kaha ki kam se kam 3,000 yuva yuvaaon ko 2014 main sena, assam rifles or arakshit bataliyanon main bhartee kiya jayega or ummid he ki yuvaaon ko sashastr balon main shaamil hone kaa mauka milegaa.
