 matsay kaa arth he- machhlee. is asana main sharir kaa aakaar machhlee jaisaa banataa he, at: yah matsyasana kehalaata he.
 chaati va gale main atyadhik dard yaa anya koi rog hone kii sthiti main yah asana na karen. badi saavdhaanee se yah asana karnachahie, shighrata se gardan main moch aa jane kaa bhay rahata he, kyonki dhad ko bilkul upar kar dena hota he. yah asana ek minute se paanch minute tak kiya ja saktaa he.
 isse aankhon kii roshni badhati he. galaa saaf rahata he tathaa chaati or pet ke rog dur hote hai. raktabhisaran kii gati badhati he, jisase charm rog nahin hota. dame ke rogiyon ko isse laabh miltaa he. pet kii charbi ghatati he. khaansi dur hoti he.
