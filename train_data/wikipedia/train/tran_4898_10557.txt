 akhabaaron main samachar or feechar ke alaava vichaarparak saamagree kaa bhi prakaashan hota he. kai akhbaar apane vaicharik rujhaan se pahachaane jate hai. akhabaaron main sampadakeey prishth par prakashit hone vaale sampadakeey agralekh ,lekh or tippaneeyaan isi vichaarparak lekhan kii shrenee main aati hai.
 stambh lekhan vichaarparak lekhan kaa pramukh rup he. kuch mahatvapurn lekhak apane khaas vaicharik rujhaan vaale hote hai, aise lekhakon kii lokapriyata ko dekhakar akhbaar unhen niyamit stambh likhane kaa jimmaa detaa he. stambh kaa vishay chunane or usamen vichaar vyakt karne kii use puri swatantraaa rahati he.
 sabhi akhbaar sampadakeey prishth par samasamyik muddon par varisht patrakaron or un vishyon ke visheshgyon ke lekh prakashit karte hai. in lekhon main kisi vishay yaa mudde kii charchaa kii jaati he or isamen lekhak ke vichaaron ko pramukhataa di jaati he. isamen lekhak ke vichaar tathyon par aadhaarit hote hai.
 sampadakeey prishth par or patrikaon ke prarambh yaa parishisht main sampadak ke naam pathakon ke patr bhi prakashit hote hai. sabhi akhabaaron main yah sthaayee stambh hota he. isamen pathak vibhinn muddon par apni raay vyakt karte hai, samasyaon ko bhi uthaate hai or janamat ko bhi pratibimbit karte hai.
 samachar madhyamon main saakshaatkaar ke dwaara hi samachar ,feechar ,vishesh report or anya kai tarah ke patrakaariya lekhan ke liye kachi saamagree ekatra hoti he. patrakaariya saakshaatkaar or saamaanya baatcheet main yah antar hota he ki saakshaatkaar main vyakti se tathy, uski raay or bhavanaayen jaanane ke liye sawaal puche jate hai jabaki saamaanya baatcheet uddeshyheen samvaad he.
