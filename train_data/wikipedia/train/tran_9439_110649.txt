 saraswati sammaan ke. ke. birla foundation dwaara diya jane vaala sahitya puraskaar he. yah sammaan prativarsh samvidhan kii aathvin anusuchee main darj bhashaaon kii main prakashit utkrisht saahitiyik kriti ko diya jaataa he. yah kriti sammaan varsh se pehle das varsh kii avadhi main prakashit hone vaali koi pustak hi ho sakati he. is sammaan main shawl, prashasti patr, pratik chihn or paanch lakh rupee kii sammaan raashi di jaati he. saraswati sammaan kaa aarambh 1991 main kiya gaya tha. pehla saraswati sammaan hindi ke sahityakar dow harivansh raay bachchan ko unki chaar khandon kii aatmakatha ke liye diya gaya tha.
