 ranjhna anand el raay dwaara nirdeshit or krishika lulla dwaara nirmit, 2013 main pradarshit hindi film he. yah film dhanush, sonam kapur, abhaya deyol aadi kalakaaron dwaara abhinit he. iros international kii yah film ek prem kahani he. jisamen kundan shankar ek dakshin bhartiya hindu he jo varanasi main apane maa-baap ke saath rahata he. uske pita ek mandir main pujari hai. iske tamil sanskaran ko chhodkar film 21 june 2013 ko cinemagharon main pradarshit hui. iskaa tamil sanskaran kaa naam ambikapathi he.
 kundan shankar varanasi main rahane vaala ek bhartiya hindu he or vah bholenath main prabal vishwaas rakhata he. bachapan se vah zoya naam kii muslim ladki se pyaar karne lagata he.
 e aar rahamaan dwaara diya gaya sangeet karnpriy he. film main banarsia, tum tak, tital trek or tu man shuddhi gane hai. irshaad qamil ne bol diye hai or bosco-seizar dwaara koreography kii gai he.
 80 rupye
