 yahova yahudee dharm main or ibrani bhashaa main parameshwar kaa naam he. yahudee maanate hai ki sabase pehle ye naam parameshwar ne hazarat musa ko sunaaya tha. ye shabd isaiyon or yahudiyon ke dharmagranth bible ke puraane niyam main kai baar aata he.
 note karen ki yahudiyon kii dharmabhasha ibrani kii lipi ibrani lipi main kaval vyanjan likhe ja sakate hai or hrasv swar to bilkul hi nahin. so ye shabd chaar vyanjanon se bana hua he : י ה ו ה, yaa יהוה yani ki ya-ha-va-ha. isamen log vibhinn swar ghusaakar ise vibhina uccharan dete hai, jaise yahova, yahaveh, yahaveh, jehova, aadi . yahudi log bekaar main ishwar kaa naam lenaa paap maanate the, isaliye is shabd ko kam hi bolaa jaataa tha. zyaada mashhoor shabd tha "adonai" . bible ke puraane niyam / ibrani shastro main "el" or "elohim" shabd bhi parameshwar ke liye prayukt hue hai, par hairat kii baat ye he ki yahudee kehete hai ki wo ek hi ishwar ko maanate hai, par "elohim" shabd vahuvachan he !yahova hi allaah he.
 yahshaha 45:18, hami parmeshver kaa saahi arth samajhaata he. 
 yahudee or isai maanate hai ki yahova kaa shabdik arth hota he : "main hoon jo main hoon" -- arthath swayambhu parameshwar. jab bible likhi gayi thi, tab yahova yah naam 7000 baar tha. - nirgaman 3:15, bhajan 83:18
