 maanavtaa dharm auguste comate dwaara sthapit dharmanirpeksh dharm he. is dharm ke anuyaayion ne france or brazil main 'maanavtaa' ke prarthana kaksh yaa mandir banaae hai.
 'comatian sakaarwaad' saapeksh rup se brazil main lokapriy tha. 1881 main miguel lemos or remundo tixira ne 'brazil kaa sakaaravaadi carch' sangathit kiya. 1897 main 'maanavtaa mandir' banaaya gaya. mandir main prarthana aadi kaa kaary chaar ghante tak chalata jiske kaaran or vishesh kathor naitik anushaasan ke kaaran republican kaal main iske kaary main kuch kami ai. tathaapi yah senaanee varg main lokapriy haa jab benjamin constant is samooh main aae. baad main ve isse alag bhi ho gaye kyonki ve mendis or lemos ko bahut adhik kattarwadi maanate the. candido rondon kaa isamen aana ise dridhtaa dene vaala tha kyonki ve paramparawaadi sakaaravaadi bane rahe jabaki bahut baad main carch kaa mahatv ksheen ho gaya tha.
 auguste comate se prerit ho kar jon stuart mil ne bhi maanavtaa dharm shuru kiya. sakaarwaadiyon dwaara maanavtaa dharm shuru kiye jane ke or bhi udhaaharan hai. kai lekhak hai jinhone apane dwaara samarthit dharm, chaahe jo bhi dharm ho, kaa gungan kiya he. bharat main baba fakir chand ne maanavtaa mandir, hoshiyarpur, bharat kii sthapana apane maanav-dharm ke vistaar ke liye ek vaigyanik drushtikon se kii jisaki vyakhya david si. len ne apni pustak 'da ananoeing sej' main kii he.
