 1987 cricket vishwa cap, cricket vishwa cap kaa chauthaa sanskaran tha. yah bharat or pakistan main 8 oktober-8 november 1987 ko yojit kiya gaya. toornament rilians industries dwaara pryojit kiya gaya tha or isame aath timo ne bhaag liya tha. prayek match 50 over prati team kaa tha or paaramparik safed kapadon main or laal gendon ke saath kheli gaya tha or saare match din ke dauraan khele gaye the.
 toornament kaa final australia or england ke bich tha, or australia ne england ko idan gardence main khele gaye final main 7 ran se parajeet kar apana pehla cricket vishwa cap jeeta.
