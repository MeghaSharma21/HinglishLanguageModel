 argun nadi yaa virgun nadi purvi rus or purvottaree china kii antarrashtriya seemaa par sthit 1,620 kilometre lambi ek nadi he. iskaa janm china dwaara niyantrit bhitari mongolia kshetra main sthit mahaan kingan parvat shrunkhlaa ke pashchimi bhaag main hota he. argun nadi aage chalakar shilka nadi se milti he or in donon ke milaap ke baad ise amur nadi ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
 mongol bhashaa kaa sab se pracheen granth 'mongolon kaa gupt itihaas' naamak pustak he, jo 13veen shatabdi main likhaa gaya tha. isamen ek mithy-katha he jisamen kaha gaya he ki mongolon main kabhi bahut koon-karaaba hua jis se argun-kun naamak mongolon kaa ek qabeela nasht ho gaya or uske keval do purush or do striyaan hi bache. vah bhagkar argun nadi ke aarmabhik kshetra main pahunch gaye, jo bahut hi viraan or durgam ilaaka tha. yahan unke ghodon ke liye ghaas bhi thi or mausam bhi sehat ke liye laabhadaayak tha. yah qabeela yahan par panpane laga or unhen lohe ke saath auzaar or shastr bananaa bhi aa gaya. inamen itani kshamta aa gai ki ve mongolia ke anya bhaagon main fail sake.
