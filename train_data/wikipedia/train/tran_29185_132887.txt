 vikramaditya shashth pashchimi chalukya shaasha tha. vah apane bade bhai someshwar dwitiya ko apdasth kar gaddi par baitha. chalukya-vikram sanwat uske shasnarudh hone par armbh kiya gaya. sabhi chalukya raajaaon main vah sabase adhik mahaan, parakramee tha tathaa uskaa shashan kaal sabase lambaa rahaa. usne 'parmadidev' or tribhuvanmall' kii oopaadhi dhaaran kii.
 vah kala or sahitya kaa sangrakshak or sanvardhak tha. uske darbar main kannada or sanskrit ke prasiddh kavi shobhaa dete the. uske bhai kirtiverma ne kannada main 'govaidhya' naamak pashuchikitsa granth likhaa. brahmashiv ne kannada main 'samayparikshe' naamak granth likhaa or 'kavichkravarti' kii oopaadhi praapt kii. 12veen shatabdi ke purv kisi or ne kannada main utane shilaalekh nahin likhvaaye jitne vikramaditya shashth ne. sanskrit ke prasiddh kavi bilhan ne 'vikramakadevacharit' naam se raja kaa prashasti granth likhaa. vigyaneshwar ne hindu vidhi se sambandhit mitakshra naamak prasiddh granth likhaa. chandaladevi naamak uski ek rani acchhee nrutyaangna thi. apane charmotkarsh ke samay chandragupta shashth kaa vishaal samrajya dakshin bharat main kaveri nadi se armbh karke madhya bharat main narmada nadi tak vistrut tha.
