 larence ferlyatti america ke prakhyat kavi, anuvadak, chitrakaar, natyakaar or udaaraniatik bhavik hai. pachaasake dashakamein unhone city laitus bucus prakaashan sanstha ki sthapana kii, californiyake sanfrancisco me. us raste kaa naam abhi jaik keruyak ali ke naam se janaa jaataa he. unke prakaashan sanstha se hi beet kavi, lekhak evam natyakaron kaa pehla kitaab prakashit huye the, yanha tak ki ginsburgke houl unke yahaan se prakashit hone par usapar jo ashleelata kaa mukadmaa huya tha vah bhi larence ferlinghettico hi jhelana pada tha.ve jane jate hai unka pehla kavyagranth e koni iland of di mind ke kaaran jo ab tak das bhashaayon main anudit huye hai or jisaki das lakh se bhi zyada kaapi ab tak bik chuke hai. bhartiya bhukhi peedhee andolankaariyaan ke lekhan bhi vah prakaash kar chuke hai. malay raichaudhuri ke khilaaf jab sathke dashakamein mukadmaa chalaa tha tab ve unki kavita prakashit kar america ke pathakoke saamne laaye the.
