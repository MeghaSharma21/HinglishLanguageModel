 aahaareey magnes kaa maanav sharir ke suraksha-tantron se sidhe sambaddh he. yah vidhi anzimon ko sakriy karata he or vitamin ‘bi’ tathaa ‘i’ ke uchit upyog main sahaayta detaa he yah pachan main madad karata he.
 magnes madhumeh ke liye bhi laabhadaayak hota he kyonki yah glucose sahan shakti badhaata he. praayah magnes kii kami maanvon main nahin pai jaati. isaki adhik maatra sharir kii loha sokhne kii kshamta kam kar detii he.
 kaali chaay kaa ek sada cap, giridaar, fal, beej, sampoorn anaaj, chokar, machhlee or avyavon ke maance.
