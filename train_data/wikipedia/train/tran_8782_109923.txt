 pashchim varjeenia kii muhar sitambar 1863 main apanaai gai thi. muhar ke bich main ek pathar kaa chitra he jisapar june 20, 1863 likhaa hua he jo ki pashchim varjeenia ke america sangh main shaamil hone kii taarikh he. pathar ke saamne do raifal or ek swatantrata topi rakhi hui he jo rajya ke logon dwaara swatantrata kii ladai ke mahatv ko darshaati he. pathar ke donon ore khadey do vyakti krishi or udyog ko pratibimbit karte hai. pashchim main ek kisaan jau ke dher ke paas hal or kataar ke saath khada he. duusari ore ek khadankarmi kudaal ke saath khada he jiske peeche hathoda rakhaa he. bahari pattee par "State of West Virginia" pashchim varjinia kaa rajya or rajya kaa dhyey vaakya "Montani Semper Liberi", likhe hue hai. muhar ke peeche kaa hissaa rajyapal kii aadhikarik muhar he.
 Montani Semper Liberi pashchim varjeenia ke rajya kaa aadhikarik dhyey vaakya he. yah pashchim varjeenia ke aadhikarik dhyey vaakya ke rup main rajya ke samvidhan ke lekh II, bhaag 2-7 main san 1872 main apanaaya gaya tha. lekh ke anusaar "rajya kii vartmaan muhar, "Montani Semper Liberi," pashchim varjeenia kii mahaan muhar hogi, or rajya ke rajyapal ke paas kanoon ke anusaar aadhikarik upyog karne ke liye rakhi jaayegi.". rajya kii muhar banane vaale kalakaar joseph eh dis debaar ke dwaara hi yah vaakya sujhaayaa gaya tha.
