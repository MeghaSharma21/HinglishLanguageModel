 'wild pacific' kaarykram main kuch is tarah kaha gaya ki easter island ke pashaan ke but dashakon kii visheshgyata se bane hai. lekin kisne in bhimkaay buton ko banaaya or ye yahaan dur-daraaj ke prashanth dbeep par kaise laae gaye? pacific island par ye but kaise pahunche, is bare main gumraah karne vaali kuch baaten hai. jaise in bade buton ko kaaphi dur se kisi agyaat, rahasyamay uddeshy ko lekar laayaa gaya.
