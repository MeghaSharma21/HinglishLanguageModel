 harihar natu maharashtra ke prasiddh kirtankaar hai. ve naradiya kirthan style main kirthan karte hai. shri nathu pichley 32 varshon se kirthan kar rahe hai or bahut se kirthan sammelnon main bhaag liya he. 
 unke kirthan kaa uddeshy bhartiya samskruti ke arth evam uski mahanata kaa janata main prasar karnaa he. unke kirthan bhinn-bhinn darshanik vishyon par hote hai. ve pichley 25 varshon se akashwani par bhi apane kaarykram dete rahe hai. doordarshan par bhi unke kaarykram hue hai.
 shri natu peshey se bankkarmi hai.
