 zulu yaa isizulu africa ke dakshini bhaag main lagbhag 1 karod logo dwaara boli jane vaali ek bhashaa he. ise bolne vaale 95% log dakshin africa desh main rahete hai jahaan yah lagbhag 25% jansankhya kii maatrubhaasha he or jahaan ke lagbhag 50% log ise samaj-bol sakate hai. san 1994 main yah dakshin africa kii 11 rajbhashaon main se ek ban gai.
