 ramprasad niranjani hindi ke pratham praudh gadhylekhak maane jate hai. aap patiala darbar main kathavachak the. patiala riyasat kii maharani deso ko sunaane ke liye hi aapane ekmaatr rachana 'bhashaa yogawasishth' kaa parimarjeet khadi boli gadhy main pranayan kiya tha. bich bich main sanskrit ke shuddh tatsam shabd or hindi ke katipay puraane prayog bhi uplabdh hote hai.
 inkaa yah granth lallulaal dwaara rache premsagar se bahut pehle rachaa gaya tha. atah yah kahana ki galat he ki aadhunik maanak hindi kaa vikaas fort wiliam kalege main lallulaal ne jorge grearsan kii prerana se premsagar rachakar kiya tha.
