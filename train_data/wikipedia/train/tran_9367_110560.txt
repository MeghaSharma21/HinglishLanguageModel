 dhruviya vrutt prithvi ke uttari golardh main 66°33′47.2″ uttar ke akshansh par sthit arctic vrutt or dakshini golardh main 66°33′47.2″ dakshin ke aakshaansh par sthit antarctic vrutt main se kisi ek ko bolaa jaataa he. in se aage dhruvon kii ore jane par prithvi ke dhruviya kshetra armbh ho jate hai. prayek dhruviya vrutt se uske sambandhit dhruv tak ke kshetra main saadhaaran rup se atyant sheet jalwaayu rahati he. yahan har varsh main kam-se-kam ek 24 ghanton kaa kaal hota he jisamen suraj kshitij se upar nazar aata he.
