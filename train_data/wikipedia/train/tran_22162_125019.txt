 aayan aise parmaanu yaa anu he jisamen electranon or protonon kii sankhya asamaan hoti he. is se aayan main vidyut aavesh hota he. agar electron kii taadaad protone se adhik ho to aayan main rinatmak aavesh hota he or use runayan bhi kehete hai. iske vipareet agar electron kii taadaad protone se kam ho to aayan main dhanatmak aavesh hota he or use dhanayan bhi kehete hai.
 ek electron or ek protone main baraabar kaa vidyut aavesh hota he. isaliye kisi aayan kaa awesh usamen maujuud protonon kii sankhya ko usamen maujuud electronon se ghataakar bataaya jaataa he. agar protonon kii sankhya adhik ho to yah aavesh dhanatmak hota he or agar electronon kii sankhya adhik ho to yah aavesh rinatmak hota he.
