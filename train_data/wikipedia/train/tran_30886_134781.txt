 niyantran rekha, bharat or pakistan ke bich khinchi gayi 740 kilometre lambii seemaa rekha he. yah rekha dono deshon ke bich pichley 50 varshon se vivaad kaa vishay bani hui he. vartmaan niyantran rekha yahaan 1947 main donon deshon ke bich hue yuddh ko viraam dekar tatkaaleen niyantran sthiti par khinchi gayi thi, jo aaj bhi lagbhag waisi hi he. tab kashmir ke kai bhaagon main pakistan ne akraman kar diya tha or bhartiya senaayen kashmir kii suraksha hetu aage aayin thim. uttari bhaag main bhartiya sena ne pakistani sena ko kargil sactor se peeche srinagar-leh rajmarg tak pachhad diya tha. 1965 main pakistan ne phir akraman kiya kintu ladaai main gatirodh utpann hua, jiske chalate yathasthiti 1971 tak bani rahi. 
 1971 main bangladesh yuddh ke uttar main pakistan ne phir kashmir par akraman kiya jisase niyantran rekha ke donon ore donon hi deshon ne ek duusare kii chaukiyon par niyanntran kiya tha. bharat ko niyantran rekha ke uttari bhaag main ladakh kshetra se lagbhag 300 varg mile bhoomi mili thi. 
 3 july, 1972 main shimla samjhaute ke parinamswaroop shantivaarta ke baad niyantran rekha ko bahal kiya gaya. parsparik samjhaute main aapasi vaarta se maamle ke sulajh jane tak yathasthiti behaal rakhe jane kii baat maani gayi. yah prakriya kai maah tak chali or field commandaron agle paanch maahon main lagbhag bees maanchitr ek duisare ko diye or antat kuch samjhaute hue. phir bhi donon deshon ke bich samay samay par chhitput yuddh hote rahete hai. saath hi ek bada yuddh kargil yuddh bhi ho chukaa he.is rekha ke bhartiya ore indian kashmir barier he jo 550 kimi lanbaa pruthakkaran avrodh he or 740 kimi lambii vivaadit 1972 line of control par bana he. yahaan bharat dwaara rekha ke kaaphi andar bhartiya niyantran kii ore dohari baad lagaayi gai he. iskaa uddeshy hathiyaaron kii taskari or pakistani aatankwaadiyon va algaavvaadiyon dwaara ghuspaith rokna he. 
 yah avrodh dohari baad or cancertina taron ke 8-12 feet oonchai tak bana he or vidyutikrut he. isamen gati-sensor, taap-chitra va alarm syranon kaa jaal he, jahaan jahaan vidyut aapurti uplabdh he. ek chota bhaag aisa bhi he, jisamen donon baadon ke bich khandak bhi khudi hui he. is avrodh kaa nirmaan 1990 ke dashak main aarambh hua tha, jo 2000 main paak ghuspaith ke chalate kuch dhimaa pad gaya tha, kintu november, 2003 ke baad ghoshit ruddha viraam ke uparaant phir aarambh hua or 2004 ke ant tak purna hua. kashmir ghati or jammu kshetra main baad 30 sitambar 2004 ko purna hui thi. bhartiya sena sroton va aankadon ke anusaar is avrodh se paak ghuspaith main 80% kii kami aayi he. yaheen se pehle paak ghuspaithiye va aatankwaadi aakar bhartiya kshetra main sainikon par hamale kiya karte the.
