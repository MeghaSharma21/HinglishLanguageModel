 vaagh kii guphaayen madhya pradesh main indaur ke paas dhar main sthit hai. vaagh kii guphaayen pracheen bharat ke svarnim yug kii adwitiya dein hai. vaagh kii guphaayen indore se uttar-pashchim main lagbhag 90 mile kii duuri par, baadhini naamak choti si nadi ke baayen tat par or vindhya parvat ke dakshin dhalaan par sthit hai. baagh-kukshi maarg se thodaa hatakar baagh kii guphaayen baagh gram se paanch mile dur hai. yah sthal us vishaal pracheen maarg par sthit he, jo uttar se ajanta hokar sudur dakshin tak jaataa he. isapurv teesri shatabdi or isvi san kii 7veen shatabdi ke madhya, jab bharat ke pashchimi bhaag main buddha dharm apni khyaati kii paraakaashtha par tha. isi samay china ke buddha dharm ke mahan vidvan yatri huensang, fahyaan or suantai madhya or pashchimi bharat aaye the.
