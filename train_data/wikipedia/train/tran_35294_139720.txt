 aagam parampara se aaye hindu dharm ke mahatvapurn granth hai. ye veda ke sampoorak hai. inke vaktaa praayah shivaji hote hai. yah shaastra saadharantaya 'tantrashastra' ke naam se prasiddh he.
 nigamagamamoolak bhartiya samskruti kaa aadhaar jis prakaar nigam he, usee prakaar aagam bhi he. donon swatantra hote hue bhi ek duusare ke poshak hai. nigam karm, gyaan tathaa upasna kaa swaroop batalaata he tathaa aagam inke upaybhoot sadhano kaa varnan karata he. isiilie vachaspati mishra ne 'tatvavaisharadi' main 'aagam' ko vyutpatti is prakaar kii he : aagchhnti budhimarohnti abhyudayni:shreyasopaya yasmaat, sa aagam:.
 aagam kaa mukhya lakshya 'kriya' ke upar he, tathaapi gyaan kaa bhi vivaran yahan kam nahin he. 'vaarahitantr' ke anusaar aagam in saat lakshanon se samavit hota he : 
 'mahanirvana tantra' ke anusaar kaliyug main praani medhya tathaa amedhya ke vichaaron se bahudhaa hin hote hai or inhen ke kalyanarth mahadev ne aagmon kaa upadesh parvati ko swayan diya. isiilie kaliyug main aagam kii puujaapaddati vishesh upyogi tathaa laabhadaayak maani jaati he- kalau aagamasammat:.
 vaidic dharm main upasya devtaa kii bhinnata ke kaaran iske teen prakaar he: 
 dwait, dwaitadvait tathaa advait kii drushti se bhi inamen teen bhed maane jate hai. anek aagam vedamulak hai, parantu katipay tantron ke upar bahari prabhaav bhi lakshith hota he. visheshat: shaktagam ke kaulachar ke upar china yaa tibet kaa prabhaav puraanon main sweekrut kiya gaya he. aagmik puja vishuddh tathaa pavitra bhartiya he. 'panch makaar' ke rahasy kaa agyaan bhi iske vishay main anek bhramon kaa utpaadak he.
