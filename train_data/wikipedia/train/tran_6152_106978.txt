 esthonia, aadhikarik taur par esthonia ganatantr uttari europe ke baltic kshetra main sthit ek desh he. isaki simaaen uttar main finland khadi, pashchim main baltic sagar, dakshin main latvia or purv main rus se milti he. esthonia mausami samshitoshna jalwaayu se prabhavit he.
 estonian baltic phins ke vanshaj he or finnish bhashaa se estonian bhashaa main bahut si samanthaayen hai. esthonia kaa aadhunik naam roman itihaaskaar tessitus kii soch mana jaataa he, jinhone apni kitaab jarmenia  main vyakti kaa ullekh aisiti ke rup main kiya.
 esthonia ek loktantric sansadeey ganatantr he or pandraa countiyon main vibhajit he. desh kii rajdhani or sabase bada shahar talinn he. keval 1.4 karod kii aabaadi ke saath, esthonia europea sangh kaa sabase kam kii aabaadi vaala sadsy he. esthonia 22 sitambar 1921, se lig of nesan, 17 sitambar 1991 se sanyukt raashtra, 1 may 2004 ke baad se europea sangh or or 29 march 2004 ke baad se nato kaa sadsy he. esthonia ne kioto protocol par bhi hastakshar kiye hai.
