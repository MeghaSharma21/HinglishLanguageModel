 uttrakhand vidhansabha chunaav, 2007 bharat ke uttrakhand rajya main hua doosraa vidhansabha chunaav tha. isse pehle varsh 2002 main rajya main pratham vidhansabha chunaav hua tha. doosraa vidhansabha chunaav 21 farvari 2007 ko hua tha tathaa matganana 27 farvari 2007 ko hui thi. chunaav matdaan kaa samay praatah aath baje se saayam paanch baje tak kaa tha. in chunaavon main kul matadaataaon kii sankhya 60,82,755 thi jisamen se 30,32,191 purush or 29,68,351 mahilaayein thi.
 is vidhansabha chunaav main kul pratyaashiyon kii sankhya 806 thi jisamen se 750 purush or 56 mahilaayein thi.
 in chunaavon main bhartiya janata party sarvaadhik siten jitkar rajya vidhansabha main sabase bade dal ke rup main ubhari. iske baad duusare or tisare sthanon par kramash: bhartiya rashtriya congress or bahujan samaaj party the. chunaav parinaam is prakaar rahaa:
