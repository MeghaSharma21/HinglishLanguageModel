 ureshian awaar yaa pracheen awaar madhya asia ke stapi maidaanon kii ek jati thi jo armbh main to shaayad ju-jaan kagaanath kii turki mul kii thi lekin baad main mishrit ureshian jatiyon kaa parisangh ban gai. awaar ek kagaan dwaara shasit parisangh tha jiske shakti ke centre main kanaabadosh yoddhaon kaa ek jattha tha. in logon kaa varnan paanchavi sadi ke baad itihaaskaaron dwaara kiya jane laga lekin madhya or purvi europe par inhonen apana awaar kagaanath naam kaa samrajya madhya chhathi sadi main hi ja ke qaayam kiya. inkaa rajya navi shatabdi ke armbh tak chalaa.
