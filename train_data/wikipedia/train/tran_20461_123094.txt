 diya mirza bhartiya cinema kii ek pramukh abhinetri hai. vah miss asia pacific bhi rah chuki hai. unka janm 9 disambar 1981 ko hyderabad, andhra pradesh, bharat main hua. unhone 2 disambar san 2000 ko manila, philippines main “miss india eshia pacific” jeeta. isi puraskaar samaaroh main unhone do or puraskaar bhi jite, “miss biutiphul smil” evam “da soni viharaz chois award”.
 unke pita frank hendrick ek german interior diziner he. unki maan deepa mirza bengali hai. jab diya mirza 6 saal kii thi tab unke mata-pita alag ho gaye. 9 saal kii umr main unke pita kaa dehanth ho gaya. unki maan ne ahamed mirza se shaadi kar lee. ahamed mirza kaa upnaam diya ne apane naam ke saath jod liya. ahamed mirza kii san 2004 main mrityu ho gayi. diya mirza ne oktober 2014 main apane premi sahil sangha se arya samaaj taur-tareeke se shaadi kii.
 unhonen apane filmi kariyar kii shuruyaat rahanaa he tere dil main se kii thi. isamen unhonen madhavan ke saath kaam kiya tha. haalanki ye film box offis par safal nahin hui par is film kaa sangeet safal rahaa. iske baad unhone divaanaapan or tumko na bhul paayenge main kaam kiya.
 2005 main diya mirza ne vidhu vinod chopra kii film parinita main kaam kiya. 2006 main unhonen ek baar phir vidhu vinod chopra kii sanjay dutt ke saath lage raho munna bhai film main kaam kiya. unhonen sonu nigam kii mujeec video "kazara muhabbat vaala" main bhi kaam kiya. iske baad unki das kahaniyaan, fite club, alag, honeymoon travels privet limited or cash jaisi asafal filmein ai.
