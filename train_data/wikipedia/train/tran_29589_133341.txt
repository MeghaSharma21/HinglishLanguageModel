 gugnhaem ajaayabaghar bilbao aadhunik samay kaa ek ajaayabaghar he jo spain ke bilbao shahar main sthit he. iskaa udghaatan 18 aktubar 1997 ko kiya gaya. ise kenadian-amareeki vaastukar frank gairi dwaara dizine kiya gaya tha. ye nervion nadi ke paas hi banaaya gaya he, ho kii shahar main se hokar guzarati he. ye solomon gugnhaem sanstha ke anek ajaayabagharon main se ek he or isamen spain or antarrashtriya kalakaaron kii clakritin maujuud hai.
