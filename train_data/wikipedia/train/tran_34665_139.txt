 hanotiyaa bharat desh ke madhyapradesh rajya kaa ek shahari sthaan hai. jo chhindwara se 38 ki.me. tathaa junnardev se 7 ki.me. kii duuri par hai.
 hanotiyaa takia nadi ke tat ke nikat basaa he jo junnardev ke satapura kii pahadiyon or vaadiyon se nikalakar bahati hui kanhan nadi main ja girti hai.
 hanotiyaa kaa lokation code = 494605 hai.
 hanotiyaa main koi bhi bade str par dharmik sthal nahin hai phir bhi kuch pramukh dharmik sthal hai -
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar hanotiyaa kii kul jansankhya 1350 thi.
