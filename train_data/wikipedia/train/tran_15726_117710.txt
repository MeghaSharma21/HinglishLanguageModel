 sevak  kaa radhavallabh sampradaay main pramukh sthaan he.
 inkaa janm shreshth brahman kul main goundwane ke gadha naamak gram main hua tha. yah gadha gram jabalpur se 3 kilometre dur sthit he. inke janm-samvath tathaa mrityu-samvath ke vishay main nishchit rup se kuch bhi pataa nahin he. kintu vidwaanon ke anusaar anumaanatah inkaa janm vi 0 san 0 1577 tathaa mrityu vi 0 san 0 1610 main hui. 
 upasya ke rup main sevak ji shyama-shyaam donon kaa ek saath saath samaran kiya he. unki drushti main donon abhinn hai,ek ke vina duusare kaa astitv hi nahin. isamen aradhya shyama hai or nity prati unka naam samaran karne vaale shyaam aradhak hai. apane is upasya-yugal kii chhavi or swarup kaa  varnan sevak ji ne apane padon main kiya he. sevak ji kii  radha sarvang-sundari sahaj maadhuri-yuta tathaa nity nai -nai keli kaa vidhaan rachane vaali hai :
 vividh aabhushanon se bhooshit rasik shiromani shrikrishna kaa soundarya bhi apurv athach darshneey he:
 upasya-yugal shyama-shyaam kii prem lilaon kaa  gaan evam dhyaan hi inaki upasna he. in lilaon ke prati ruchi upaask main tabhi aati he jab uske hryday priti kaa ankur foot padataa he or iskaa keval harivansh krupa he~~
 shri harivansh kii krupa ho jane par jeev sabase prem karne lagata he,shatruor mitra main ,laabh or haani main,maan or apmaan main sambhaav vaala ho jaataa he. harivansh krupa ke parinamswaroop vah satat shyama-shyaam ke nity vihaar kaa pratyaksh darshan karata he or is lila darshan se use jis anand kii kii anubhuti hoti he vah premashruon tathaa pulak dwaara spasht suchit hota he :
