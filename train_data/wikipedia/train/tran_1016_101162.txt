 vishrambagh waada, kendriya pune ke thorle bajirao road par sthit ek badhiya haveli, unnisvin shatabdi kii shuruyaat main maratha sangh ke aakhri peshav peshwe bajirao dwitiya kaa shandaar nivas tha. vartmaan main 20,000 varg feet waada par apane ground floor par ek daakghar, nagar palika ke kuch anya karyaalayon or maratha kalakritiyon kaa ek chota sangrahalay he, jo prasiddh maratha itihaaskaar balavant moreshwar purandare dwaara ek rakhe gaye hai. yah sanrachana apane badhiya pravesh or nakkashidaar lakdi ke kaam ke saath balakani ke liye prasiddh he. 
 vishrambagh waada 1807 isvi main 200,000 rupee kii keemat par banaaya gaya tha. nirmaan ko puraa karne main chah saal lag gaye the. bajirao dwitiya ne is nivas ko peshwa ke garh shanivaar waada ke jaise pasand kiya tha. bajirao dwitiya tritiy angl-maratha yuddh main unki haar tak gyaarah saal tak yahan rahe the. yuddh ke baad angrejon ne kanpur ke paas bithur ko pension ke saath desh nishkaashan kar diya tha. unki patni varanasibai bithur main usase judane se pehle kuch samay tak yahaan rahi thi lekin jald hi angrejon ne unhen pune le gayi the.
