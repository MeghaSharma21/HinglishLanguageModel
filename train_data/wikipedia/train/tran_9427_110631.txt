 dhaal jwalamukhi jise shield jwalamukhi, bhi kaha jaataa he, jwalamukhiyon kaa ek prakaar he. in jwalamukhiyon kaa nirmaan aamtaur par lagbhag puri tarah se taral lava pravaah ke dwaara hota he. in jwalamukhiyon kaa yah vishisht naam isliye padaa he kyonki, dekhne main yah kisi yoddha kii dhaal ke samaan prathit hote hai, yani ek dhaal ke samaan inkaa aakaar bada or parshv oonchaai kam hoti he. in jwalamukhiyon ke dwaara udgaarit atyadhik taral lava, jo ki anya adhik visphotak jwalamukhiyon se nikle lave kii tulana main adhik dur tak bahata he or lave kii ek vyapak chadar kaa nirmaan kar, kisi dhaal jwalamukhi ko iskaa yah vishisht rup kaa pradaan karata he.
