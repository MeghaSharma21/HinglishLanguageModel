 shri nilkantheshwar mahadev mandir bharat ke uttar pradesh rajya ke antargat mathura nagar main mathura-vrundavan maarg par moksh dham ke nikat sthit he. is mandir kaa ullekh varah puraana ke antargat gokarn saraswati mahatmaya shukr vrittant, mathura puraagaman moksh prapti vrittant main he. kalantar main yahan naga santon ne mahamrityunjay yantra ke aadhaar par mukhya mandir main kasuti pathar se nirmit vishaal anupam alokik shivlinga kii praan pratishtha kar lagbhag 400 varshon tak tap sadhana kii. lambey samay tak yah mandir sunsaan or vismrit rahaa. san 1850 main lakhanu nivasee ayodhya prasad ne naga santon dwaara sthapit puraane mul mandir kaa vistaar kar parisar ko bhavyata pradaan kii. mukhya mandir ke ek ore mahalakshmi, mahakali or maha saraswati ke mandir hai tathaa duusari taraf ganga or garuda ke mandir he. mukhya mandir ke bagal main hanuman kaa mandir he. manyata he ki shri nilkantheshwar ke darshan maatr se shraddhaluon kii samast manokamanaayen purna hoti hai. puratathvik mahatv ke is puranic siddhapitha ke vibhinn mandir, praveshdwar, parcote aadi samay or prakruti ke thapedo se atyant jeernsheern haalat main hai. lambey samay se logon kii upeksha or atikaraman ne ise nasht-bhrasht kar girasu haalat main pahunchaa diya he. vartmaan main is mandir ke puraatan puranic swaroop ko bachaane ke liye kuch bhaktajan or samskritipremi logon dwaara prayaas kiye ja rahe hai.
