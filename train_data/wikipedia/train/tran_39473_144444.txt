 chuwashi madhya rus main boli jane vaali ek turki bhashaa he. yah chuwash ganatantr or uske padosi ilaaqon main boli jaati he or turki bhashaa-parivaar kii ogur shakha kii iklauti jivit bhashaa he . chuwashi turki parivaar main niraali he kyonki apni ek alag shaakh main hone ke kaaran isamen or anya jivit turki bhashaaon main bahut antar he or unako bolne vaale chuwashi nahin samaj sakate. chuwashi sirilik lipi main likhi jaati he. ise san 2002 kii janaganana main 16.4 lakh logon ne apni maatrubhaasha bataaya tha.
