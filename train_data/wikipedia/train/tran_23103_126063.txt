 tholing, jise china kii sarkaar zanda bulaati he, dakshin-pashchimi tibet main bharat kii seemaa ke nazadik sthit ek shahar he. 3,723 meter kii oonchaai par sthit yah nagar tibet ke ngaari vibhaag ke zanda zile kii prashasnic rajdhani bhi he. itihaas main yah pashchimi tibet ke guge rajya kii rajdhani hua karata tha. 997 main sthapit tholing gompa vishwa-prasiddh he or sutluj nadi ke kinare khadaa hua he. tibet main yah nadi 'langachen tsangpo' ke naam se jaani jaati he. 1950 main tibet par janavaadee ganatantr china kaa qabza hone ke baad china ne yahan baari maatra main sainik tainaat kar die or ab shahar kaa bada bhaag ek chhavnee kaa rup liye hue he.
