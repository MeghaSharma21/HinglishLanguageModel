 mishran ke prakaar
 mishran ko mukhya rup se do bhaagon main baanta gaya he mishran jin padaarthon yaad in kanon se milakar bane hote hai unhen mishran ke avyavi kan kehete he
 1 samaangi mishran - aise mishran jinke avyavi kanon ko alag alag nahin kiya ja saktaa he jaise namak ke jaleey willian ko namak or jal main alag alag nahin kiya ja saktaa yah ek samaangi mishran kaa udhaaharan he
 2 vishmangi mishran aise mishran jinke avyavi kanon ko alag alag dekha ja saktaa he jaise pani ke upar tel daalane par tathaa hilaane par tel upar rah jaataa he or pani neeche baith jaataa he pani or tel ko alag-alag kar sakate hai atah ek vishmangi mishran he
