 Mesut Ozil ek german footbal khilaadi he jo angreji club arsenal or german rashtriya team ke liye kheltaa he. ojil 2006 ke baad se ek yuva rashtriya team sadsy rahe hai, or 200 9 ke baad se german rashtriya team kaa sadsy rahe hai. unhone 2010 fifa vishwa cap ke dauraan antarrashtriya dhyaan aakarshith kiya or unhen golden ball puraskaar ke liye namankit kiya gaya, jo toornament ke sarvshereshth khilaadi ko diya jaataa he. ozil ​​ne bulthesliga main grihnagar club shalke main 2006 main apana varisht karier shuru kiya, 2008 main verder bramen ko sthanantarit kar diya or germany ke saath fifa vishwa cap main apane brake-out pradarshan ke baad agast 2010 main real madrid dwaara hastakshar kiye gaye. greeshmakaleen 2013 kii antim tithi ko hastantarit karne par vah arsenal ke liye 42.5 million pound ke liye, club   ke liye ek fees or ek german khilaadi ke liye sabase adhik shulk kaa bhugtan kiya gaya.
 ozil ko unki chaalaaki or akraman ke liye ek akramanakari midfilder ke rup main prashansit he. unki style or unki team ke sadasyon ke liye sahaayta pradaan karne kii kshamta kii tulana purv manager jose morinno ne real madrid ke lijend jinedin jidane kii tulana main kii thi. 2010-11 main, ozil ne pramukh europea or gharelu pratiyogitaaon main 25 wein sthaan par sahaayta main pehla sthaan diya. 2011-12 main, unhone 17 veen ke saath laa liga main pehle sthaan par raheen.  ozil 2010 ke fifa vishwa cap or yuiefee euro 2012 donon main sanyukt-sarvocch sahaayak pradaata the, prayek toornament main teenon ko darj karte hue
