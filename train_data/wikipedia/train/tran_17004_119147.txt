 dharmik rashtrawaad rashtrawaad kaa kisi vishisht dharmik vishwaas, hathdharmita, yaa sahabaddhata se sambandhan he. is sambandhan ko do pahaluon main todaa ja saktaa hai: dharm kaa raajnneetikaran or dharm kaa raajnneeti par prabhaav.
 bhartiya jansankhya kii vyapak bhashik, dharmik or sanjateey vividhta ke maddenzar, bharat main rashtrawaad, rashtrawaad ke eklaute prakaar ke paridhi main saamanytah nahin aata. bhartiya log apane raashtra ke saath naagarik rashtrawaad, saanskrithik rashtrawaad, yaa tritiy-vishwa rashtrawaad ke dwaara tadatmya sthapit kar sakate hai. kuch tippaneekartaaon ne yah vichaar abhivyakt kiya he ki aadhunik bharat main, hindu rashtrawaad kaa ek samakaleen rup, yaa hindutv, bhartiya janata party or rashtriya swayamsevak sangh ke dwaara anumudit hua hai, yadhyapi bahusankhyak bharatiyon dwaara hindu rashtrawaad sweekrut nahin hai.
