 jarayujata prajnan kii ek vidhi he, jinamein bacchon ke bhrun ke vikaas maan ke sharir ke andar hota he or jivit shishuon ko janm diya jaataa he. andajata ke vipareet, jarayujata main bhrun kaa poshan maan ke sharir upasthit aparaa ke madhyam se hota he. 
 jarayujata stanadhariyon main sabase aam prajnan vidhi he, haalaanki yah kuch sarisrupon, ubhaycharon, sandhipadon or machhlee kii kuch prajatiyon main bhi pai jaati he.
