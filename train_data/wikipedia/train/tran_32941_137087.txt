 nong khaai thailand kaa ek praant he. yah desh ke purvottaree bhaag main khoraat pathaar par sthit he.
 nong khaai praant isaan kshetra kaa sarvottaree praant he or isaki uttari seemayen laos desh se lagi hui hai. laos kii rajdhani vyentyaan prantiya rajdhani nong khaai se keval 25 kimi dur he. mikang nadi yahan par donon desho ke bich ek prakritik seemaa banaati he or usapar thai-lao mitrataa pul naamak ek pul khadaa hua he.
