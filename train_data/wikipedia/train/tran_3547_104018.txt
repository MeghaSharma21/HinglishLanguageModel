 pawan chakkiyon kaa istemaal sadiyon se hota aayaa he, lekin bijli paidaa karne ke liye iskaa vikaas pichhali sadi main hi hua. 
 pawan chakki kai tarah kii hoti hai, lekin jo sabase adhik prachlit hai unamem ek vishaal khambhe ke upree bhaag par ek silendar laga hota he jiske munh par 20 se 30 foot lambe or 3 se 4 foot chaude pankhe lage rahete hai. jab hawa chalati he to ye pankhe ghoomane lagate hai. isse paidaa hui urja ko generator dwaara bijli main parivartit kiya jaataa he. 
 hamaare vayumandal main itani pawan urja he jo duniya kii vartmaan bijli khapat se paanch guna adhik bijli paidaa kar sakati he. varsh 2008 main duniya kii kul bijli khapat kaa 1.5 pratishat hissaa pawan urja se paidaa kiya gaya. lekin is disha main tezi se pragati ho rahi he or bade str par wind farm banaae ja rahe hai. aise kaam karti he pawan chakki
 denmark main 19 pratishat bijli isi tarah paidaa kii jaati he. 
 bharat main is samay pawan urja se 9587.14 megawatt bijli paidaa karne kii kshamta he or 2012 tak isamen 6000 megawatt kii badhotaree kii jaaegi. 
 hawa urja kaa svachh, pradooshan rahit akshay strot to he, lekin isse judi kuch samasyaayein bhi hai jaise ye mahangaa he, yah hawa ke bahane par nirbhar he isliye hameshaa uplabdh nahin hota, iske liye khule maidaanon, samudri kinaaron yaa oonche sthanon kii aavashyaktaa hoti he jahaan hawa kaa bahaav adhik ho or ye dekhne main achche nahin lagate.
