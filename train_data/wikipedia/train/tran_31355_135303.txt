 shri jainarayan raay marishas ke pehle gandhivadi vidwaan the. unhone janata kaa sampadan kaary sanbhaalane se pehle hindi kahani kaa sutarapaat 1937 main kiya. ve marishas ke pehle kathakaar or naatakkaar the. unki jivan sangini naatak 1941 main prakashit hua tha. hindi prachaarini sabha ke adhyaksh ke rup main unhone hindi kii bahut seva kii.
