 madhya pradesh rajya vidhansabha chunaav, 2013 bhartiya rajya madhya pradesh main 25 navambar 2013 ko hue vaale vidhaan sabha chunaav hai jisamen madhya pradesh kii 230 vidhansabha siton ke liye matdaan hua. iske parinaam 8 disambar 2013 ko ghoshit kiye jaayenge. chhitput ghatnaon ko chhodkar chunaav shantipurvak sampann hua. madhya pradesh main 71 pratishat matdaan hone kaa anumaan he.
