 narsingh prasad kalidas prakhyat shikshavid, vichaarak, swatantraaa senaanee or ek rachanaatmak lekhak the. unka janm 11 navambar 1882 ko bhavnagar main hua. nanabhai, gijubhai badheka or harbhai trivedi ke samakaleen or bhavnagar kii mashhoor trimurti the jinhone bharat main shiksha sudhaar, gramin vikaas or bal shiksha ke kshetra main mahaan prerana or drushti chodi. nanabhai bhatta dakshinmurthi vidhyaarthee bhawan/dakshinarthi vinay mandir - bhavnagar, gram dakshinamurti - ambala or sihor block sanosara sthit lokbharati, bhavnagar jaisi pratishthit or adwitiya shaikshik sansthaanon ke sansthaapak rahe.
