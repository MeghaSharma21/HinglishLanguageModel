 sena diwas, bharat main har varsh 15 janvari ko leftinent general ke. em. kariyappa ke bhartiya thal sena ke sheersh commander kaa padbhaar grahan karne ke uplakshya main manaaya jaataa he. unhone 15 janvari 1949 ko british raaj ke samay ke bhartiya sena ke antim angrej sheersh commander general roy francis buchar se yah padbhaar grahan kiya tha. yah din sainya paradeon, sainya pradarshaniyon va anya aadhikarik kaaryakramon ke saath nai dilli va sabhi sena mukhyalayon main manaaya jaataa he. is din un sabhi bahadur senaniyon ko salaami bhi di jaati he jinhone kabhi naa kabhi apane desh or logon kii salaamati ke liye apana sarvocch nyochaavar kar diya.
 15 agast 1947 ko jab bharat swatantra hua, to desh bhar main vyaapt dange-fasaadon tathaa sharnaarthiyon ke avaagaman ke kaaran uthal-puthal kaa maahaul tha. is kaaran kai prashasnic samasyaayein paidaa hone lagi or phir sthiti ko niyantrit karne ke liye sena ko aage aana padaa. iske pashchaat ek vishesh sena cmand kaa gathan kiya gaya, taaki vibhaajan ke dauraan shanti-vyavastha sunishchit kii ja sake. parantu bhartiya sena ke adhyaksh tab bhi british mul ke hi hua karte the. 15 janvari 1949 ko field marshall ke em kariappa swatantra bharat ke pehle bhartiya sena pramukh bane the. us samay bhartiya sena main lagbhag 2 lakh sainik the. unase pehle yah pad commander general roy francis buchar ke paas tha. uske baad se hi prayek varsh 15 janvari ko sena diwas manaaya jaataa he.
 sena diwas ke uplakshya main prayek varsh dilli chhavnee ke kariappa parade ground main parade nikaali jaati he, jisaki salaami thal senadhyaksh lete hai. 2018 main 70waan sena diwas manaaya gaya, jisamen parade kii salaami general bipin ravat ne lee.
