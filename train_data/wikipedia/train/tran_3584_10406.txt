 bharat main raashtrapati ke baad uparashtrapati kaa pad kaaryakarini main doosraa sabase bada pad hota he. bharat kaa uparashtrapati rajyasabha ke adhyaksh ke taur par vidhaayi kaaryon main bhi hissaa letaa he. 
 bharat ke vartmaan uparashtrapati shri venkaiya naidu hai jo 5 agast 2017 ko chune gayi the.
 uparashtrapati kaa rajya sabha kaa paden sabhapati hona-- uparashtrapati, rajya sabha kaa paden sabhapati hoga or anya koi laabh kaa pad dhaaran nahin karegaa. parantu jis kisi avadhi ke dauraan uparashtrapati, anuchhed 65 ke adheen raashtrapati ke rup main kaary karata he yaa raashtrapati ke krityon kaa nirvahan karata he, us avadhi ke dauraan vah rajya sabha ke sabhapati ke pad ke kartavyon kaa paalan nahin karegaa or vah anuchhed 97 ke adheen rajya sabha ke sabhapati ko sandey vetan yaa bhatte kaa hakdaar nahin hoga.
 raashtrapati ke pad main aakasmik rikti ke dauraan yaa uski anupasthiti main uparashtrapati kaa raashtrapati ke rup main kaary karnaa yaa uske krityon kaa nirvahan--
 uparashtrapati ko us avadhi ke dauraan or us avadhi ke sambandh main, jab vah raashtrapati ke rup main is prakaar kaary kar rahaa he yaa uske krityon kaa nirvahan kar rahaa he, raashtrapati kii sabhi shaktiyaan or unmuktiyaan hongi tathaa vah aisi uplabdhiyon, bhatton or visheshadhikaron kaa jo sansad, vidhi dwaara, avdhaarit kare or jab tak is nimitt is prakaar upbandh nahin kiya jaataa he tab tak aisi uplabdhiyon, bhatton or visheshadhikaron kaa, jo duusari anusuchee main vinirdisht hai, hakdaar hoga.
 uparashtrapati kaa nirvachan
  uparashtrapati kaa nirvachan sansad ke donon sadanon ke sadasyon se milakar banane vaale nirvachakagan ke sadasyon dwaara anupatik pratinidhitiv paddati ke anusaar ekal sankramntic mat dwaara hoga or aise nirvachan main matdaan gupt hoga.
  uparashtrapati sansad ke kisi sadan kaa yaa kisi rajya ke vidhaan-mandal ke kisi sadan kaa sadsy nahin hoga or yadi sansad ke kisi sadan kaa yaa kisi rajya ke vidhaan-mandal ke kisi sadan kaa koi sadsy uparashtrapati nirvaachit ho jaataa he to yah samajha jayega ki usne us sadan main apana sthaan uparashtrapati ke rup main apane pad grahan kii taarikh se rikt kar diya he.
  koi vyakti uparashtrapati nirvaachit hone kaa paatr tabhi hoga jab vah--
  koi vyakti, jo bharat sarkaar ke yaa kisi rajya kii sarkaar ke adheen athwa ukt sarkaron main se kisi ke niyantran main kisi sthaneey yaa anya praadhikari ke adheen koi laabh kaa pad dhaaran karata he, uparashtrapati nirvaachit hone kaa paatr nahin hoga.
 spashtikaran--is anuchhed ke prayojanon ke liye, koi vyakti keval is kaaran koi laabh kaa pad dhaaran karne vaala nahin samajha jayega ki vah sangh kaa raashtrapati yaa uparashtrapati yaa kisi rajya kaa rajyapal 2 * * * he athwa sangh kaa yaa kisi rajya kaa mantri he.
 uparashtrapati kii padaavadhi
  uparashtrapati apane pad grahan kii taarikh se paanch varsh kii avadhi tak pad dhaaran karegaa: parantu--
  uparashtrapati, raashtrapati ko sambodhit apane hastakshar sahit lekh dwaara apana pad tyaag sakegaa;
  uparashtrapati, rajya sabha ke aise sankalp dwaara apane pad se hataaya ja sakegaa jise rajya sabha ke tatkaaleen samast sadasyon ke bahumat ne paarit kiya he or jisase loksabhaa sahamat he; kintu is khand ke prayojan ke liye kai sankalp tab tak prastaavit nahin kiya jayega jab tak ki us sankalp ko prastaavit karne ke aashay kii kam se kam chaudaa din kii soochna na de di gai ho;
  uparashtrapati, apane pad kii avadhi samaapt ho jane par bhi, tab tak pad dhaaran karata rahegaa jab tak uskaa uttaradhikari apana pad grahan nahin kar letaa he.
 uparashtrapati ke pad main rikti ko bharne ke liye nirvachan karne kaa samay or aakasmik rikti ko bharne ke liye nirvaachit vyakti kii padaavadhi--
  uparashtrapati kii padaavadhi kii samaapti se hui rikti ko bharne ke liye nirvachan, padaavadhi kii samaapti se pehle hi purna kar liya jayega.
  uparashtrapati kii mrityu, padatyag yaa pad se hataae jane yaa anya kaaran se hui uske pad main rikti ko bharne ke liye nirvachan, rikti hone ke pashchaat yathashighra kiya jayega or rikti ko bharne ke liye nirvaachit vyakti, anuchhed 67 ke upbandhon ke adheen rahete hue, apane pad grahan kii taarikh se paanch varsh kii puri avadhi tak pad dhaaran karne kaa hakdaar hoga.
 uparashtrapati dwaara shapath yaa pratigyaan--
 prayek uparashtrapati apana pad grahan karne se pehle raashtrapati athwa uske dwaara is nimitt niyukt kisi vyakti ke samaksh nimnlikhit praroop main shapath legaa yaa pratigyaan karegaa or us par apane hastakshar karegaa, arthath: --ishwar kii shapath letaa hoon
