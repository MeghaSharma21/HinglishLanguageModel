 bal shauri reddy hindi ke suprasiddh lekhak or bal sahityakar hai. unka janm gram mollar guder, jila kann guder andhra pradesh main hua. ve mul rup se telegu bhashi hai. unhone dakshin bharat kii hindi prachaar sabha madras main lekhan evam sampadan kaary kiya. ve kaafii dinon tak masik bal patrika chandamama ke sampadak rahe. iske pashchhaat ve bhartiya bhashaa parishad kalakatta main niyukt hue. samprati swatantra lekhan main sanlagn hai. moulik lekhan ke atirikt unhone hindi se telugu or telegu se hindi main anuwaad bhi kiya he.
