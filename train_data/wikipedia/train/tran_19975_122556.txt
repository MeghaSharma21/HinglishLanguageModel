 dhamoni: sagar ke uttar main jhaansi maarg par kareeb 50 kimi kii duuri par sthit dhamoni ab ujaad ho chukaa he lekin iskaa aitihaasik drushti se bahut mahatv he. gadha mandla ke rajyakaal main mahatvapurn hone ke kaaran ise garh banaaya gaya tha or iske saath 50 mauje the. gadha mandla vansh ke ek vanshaj surat shah ne is kile ko banaaya tha. 
 ek danta katha ke anusaar mughalkaal kaa mashhoor itihaaskaar abul fazal bhi yaheen paidaa hua tha lekin iskaa koi pramaan aaj uplabdh nahin he. ine-e-akabari main iskaa ullekh malwa soobe main raisen kii sarkaar ke mahal ke rup main kiya gaya he. kisi jamaane main yahaan hathi bechney ke liye bazaar bharta tha. yah orchha ke raja veerasinghdev ke rajya main sammilit tha. unhone kile kaa phir se nirmaan karaayaa tha.
 puraane khandhiron ke kaaran dhamoni puraatatv kii drushti se bahut mahatvapurn sthaan he. kile ke alaava yahaan rani mahal ke naam se vikhyath ek mahal bhi he. yahaan kaa ek anya ullekhneey sthaan muslim santon kii do majaaren bhi hai. inamen se ek balajit shah kii majaar he, jinhen abul fazal kaa guru samajha jaataa he. 
 duusari majaar mastaan shah vali kii maani jaati he. unke bare main kaha jaataa he ki gaanv main pani nahin milane ke kaaran unhone dhamoni ko badadua di thi. santon kii majaaron ke bare main kai kathaayen prachlit hai. yahaan saal main ek baar garmiyon ke mausam main urs kaa aayojan kiya jaataa he. gaanv ke kinare kuch jain mandir bhi hai, jinka nirmaan 1815-19 ke bich hua tha.
