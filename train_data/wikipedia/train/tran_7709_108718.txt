 sharavati ek nadi he jiskaa udgam bharat ke karnataka rajya main he or isi rajya main bahati hui yah isi rajya main samaapt ho jaati he. yah nadi bharat kii gini chuni nadiyon main se he jo pashchim kii taraf bahatin hai. is nadi kaa adhikansh bhaag pashchimi ghat main he. prasiddh jog jalprapaat isi nadi ke dwaara nirmit he. yah nadi tathaa iske aas-paas kaa kshetra jaivvividhta kii drushti se bahut samrudh he.
