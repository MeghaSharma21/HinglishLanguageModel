 chedi aaryon kaa ek ati pracheen vansh he. rugveda kii ek danstuti main inke ek atyant shaktishaalee naresh 'kashu' kaa ullekh he. rugvedakaal main ye sambhavat: yamuna or vindhyas ke bich based hue the. 
 puraanon main varnit paramparaagat itihaas ke anusaar yadavon ke naresh vidarbha ke teen putron main se dwitiya kaishik chedi kaa raja hua or usne chedi shakha kaa sthapana kii. chedi rajya aadhunik bundelkhand main sthit rahaa hoga or yamuna ke dakshin main chambal or ken nadiyon ke bich main failaa rahaa hoga. kuru ke sabase chote putr sudhanvan ke chauthe anuvarthi shaasha vasu ne yadavon se chedi jitkar ek nae rajvansh kii sthapana kii. uske paanch main se chauthe ko chedi kaa rajya mila. mahabharat me shishupal chedi kaa raja tha. mahabharat ke yuddh main chedi pandavon ke paksh main lade the. chhathi shatabdi isa purv ke 16 mahajanapadon kii talika main cheti athwa chedi kaa bhi naam aata he. chedi logon ke do sthanon par basane ke pramaan milte hai - nepal main or bundelkhand main. inamen se doosraa itihaas main adhik prasiddh hua. mudrarakshas main malyketu kii sena main khash, magadh, yavan, shaq, hun ke saath chedi logon kaa bhi naam he.
