 don pheng 4 yaa deef-4 ek taral eendhan vaali do charan kii chini intercantinental belistic missile he. mana jaataa tha ki 1970 or 1980 ke dashak kii shuruyaat main bhumigat sailo main simit sankhya main tainaat kiya janaa tha. don pheng 4 main 1,224.00 kiloneuton trust, 82000 kilogram techof vajan, 2.25 meter kaa vyas, 28.05 meter kii lambaai or 2.74 meter lambey pankh he. don pheng 4 kii seemaa 2,190 kilo parmaanu bam se susajjit ke saath 5,500 kimi kii he. yah prashanth kshetra main rus, bharat or america thikaanon tak lakshya ko maarane ke liye paryaapt range detaa he. missile ek jadtviya maargdarshan pranali kaa upyog karti he jisase isaki sateekta 1,500 me kii he. 2017 tak, 10-15 lanchar tainaat kiye gaye hai.
 dieff-4 ko viksit karne kaa nirnay 1965 main guam main america ke belistic missile pandubbi gashti ke jawaab main kiya gaya tha. missile ke diziner ko kai tarah se rain zinamin yaa tu shu ke rup main pahachaanaa gaya he, or ise factory 211 main banaaya gaya tha.
 1972 main yues intelligence ne is pranali ke liye 1974 yaa 1975 main shuru hone vaali ummid kaa anumaan lagaaya tha. tainaati vastav main 1975-76 main shuru hui, lekin 1984 tak keval chaar deef-4 kii tainaati kaa anumaan lagaaya gaya tha.
 missile ke do sanskaran viksit kiye gaye, ek sanskaran gufaaon yaa garrej main launch kiye jane vaala or anya silo aadhaarit sanskaran main pesh kiya gaya tha.
 you.es. department of dephence kaa anumaan he ki missile ek kshetreey pratirohak kshamta ke rup main kaam karnaa jari rakhega, jab tak ki ise don pheng-31 dwaara pratisthapit nahin kiya jaae. duusari artilari kor ke liye yah mahatvapurn kshamta laabh hoga. lekin ab china kii don pheng 31e, don pheng 4 se bahut adhik shaktishaalee he. don pheng 31e kii seemaa 11,700 kimi he tathaa ise rail or road donon platfarm se choda ja saktaa he.
