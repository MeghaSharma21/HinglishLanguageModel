 munh jantuon kii aahaar nalee kaa pratham bhaag hota he jisko aahaar or laar miltaa he. oral mucosa munh ke andar kii upakalaa main shleshm jhilli hoti he. apni mukhya kriya yani pachak tantra kii pehali kadi ke atirikt manushyon main munh ek or ahm kaary karata he jo ki he ek duusare ke saath vaartalap ke dwaara sampark karnaa. haalanki dhwani kaa mukhya strot galaa hota lekin is dhwani ko bhashaa kaa rup jeebh, hont, jabada or upree munh kaa taalu dete hai. munh kaa andaruni bhaag amuman laar kii vajah se geela rahata he or hont se munh ke andar kii shleshm jhilli twachaa-jo ki baaki sharir ko dhankati he- main parivartit ho jaati he.
 maukhik kshidra pachan nalee ke pehle bhaag kaa pratinidhitiv karata he. iske antargat hont, masoode, jeebh, daant, munh kaa taalu ityadi ang aate hai.
 manushyon main vaarta ke dwaara sampark sthapit karnaa munh kaa khaane ke baad doosraa sabase pramukh kaam hota he. anya pashu bhi ek duusare se sampark sthapit karte hai lekin sirf gale se nikali dhvaniyon ke dwaara. manushya ekmaatr aisa pashu he jo ki munh kii madad se samaj main aane vaali awaazen nikaal saktaa he jise ham vaartalap kehete hai.puraane kaal main jab shabd sanrachana ke saath-saath bhashaa kaa bhi vikaas ho rahaa tha lekin lipi tab tak ijad nahin huyi thi, manushya aitihaasik, saamaajik tathaa anya ghatnaon ke bare main ek duusare ko yah ghatnayen kahaniyaan sunaane ke zarie bataate the. kyonki is tareeqe main lekhan kriya kaa abhaav tha, isliye ise maukhik rup se prachaar kaha gaya.
