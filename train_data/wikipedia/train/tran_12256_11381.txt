 irvikulam rashtriya udyaan bharat ke kerala rajya ke iddukki zile main pashchimi ghat main sthit ek rashtriya udyaan he jiskaa kshetrafal 97 varg kimi he. yah kerala van or vanya jeev vibhaag, munnar vanyajeev prabhag dwaara prashasit kiya jaataa he jo paas ke mathikettan sholaa rashtriya udyaan, anamudi sholaa abhayaarany, pampadum sholaa abhayaarany, chinnar vanyajeev abhayaarany or kuringimla abhayaarany ko bhi prashasit karata he. pashchimi ghat, annamalai up kunj sahit eravikulam rashtriya udyaan kaa puraa ilaaka unesco kii vishwa dharohar samiti dwaara ek vishwa viraasat sthal ke rup main chayan ke liye vichaaradhin he.
 is rashtriya udyaan kaa mukhya bhaag unche lahardaar parvatiy pathaar kaa kshetra he, jiskaa aadhaar ausatan 2000 meter kaa he.
