 uttara bhadrapada akaash mandal main 26vaan nakshatra he. yah min raashi ke antargat aata he. ise du, tha, jha naam se janaa jaataa he. uttara bhadrapada nakshatra kaa swami shani he. vahiin raashi swami guru he. shani guru main shatruta he. kahin inkaa talmail va panchadhametri chakra main jis jataka kii kundali main mitra kaa sam ho to iske shubh parinaam bhi dekhne ko milte hai.
 is nakshatra main janm lene vaala jataka soch-vichaar kar bolne vaale, sukhi santan, dharm-karm main aasthavan, uttam vaktaa, udaar hriday ke sammanneey bhi hote hai. shani aadhyaatmavaadi he to guru kaa gyaan kaa kaarak he. guru shani kaa mail dhanu yaa min raashi main ho to iske shubh parinaam milenge.
 dhanu main guru shani kii yuti aadhyaatmavaadi lekin parishramee bhi banaaegi. min main guru shani ke chandra hone se aisa jataka param aadhyaatmavaadi paropkaaree, gyanvaan, svaprayatnon se safalataa paane vaala upadeshak bhi ho saktaa he.
