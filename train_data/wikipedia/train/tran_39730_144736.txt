 babarnama yaa tujk-e-babari mogul samrajya ke pehle samraat babar kii aatmalikhit jeevani he. yah unhone apni maatrubhaasha chaghtai turki main likhi thi. isamen unhone apana uzbekistan kii faraghna vaadi main guzaaraa hua bachapan or yauvan, baad main afghanistan or bhartiya upmahadweep par akraman or qabza or anya ghatnaon kaa vivaran diya he. unhone har kshetra kii bhoomi, raajnneeti, arthvyavastha, prakritik vaataavaran, shaharon-imaaraton, falon, jaanwaron, ityadi kaa bakhaan kiya he. isamen kuch farsi bhashaa ke chote-mote chand bhi aate hai, haalanki farsi bolne vaale ise samajhane main aksham hai. haalanki chaghtai bhashaa vilupt ho chuki he aadhunik uzbek bhashaa usee kii vanshaj he or use bolne vaale uzbek log babarnama pard sakate hai. is kitaab ko chaghtai or uzbek bhashaaon ke sahitya kaa ek mahatvapurn ang mana jaataa he.
 babarnama in saral shabdon ke saath armbh hota he:
 unhone apni faraghna kii mathrubhumi ke bare main kaha:
 unhone ek muthbhed kaa varnan yah diya:
 kuch prishthbhumi dene ke baad ve apane madhya asia ke ek chote se shaasha ke banate-bigadate bhagya kaa bakhaan karte hai, jisamen unhone samarkand do dafaa jeeta or haara or phir 1504 main kabul kooch kar gaye. phir 1508 se 1519 ke kaal ke bare main kitaab main kuch nahin he. tab tak ve kabul main apni satta majboot kar chuke the or vahaan se unhone pashchimottaree bharat par dhaava bolaa. babarnama kaa antim bhaag 1525 se 1529 ke kaal main bharat main mogul samrajya ke niv rakhane kii gatividhiyon kaa varnan karata he, jisapar aage chalakar babar ke vanshajon ne teen sau saal raaj kiya.
