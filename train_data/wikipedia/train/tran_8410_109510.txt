 manu smriti jise maanav dharm shaastra bhi kaha jaataa he | yah hinduo kaa ek mahatvapurn granth kehalaata he | is granth main saamaajik virgo ke bare main upadesh kahe gayi he|
 manyatta yah he ki rishi bhrigu iske rachiyata the prayek adhyay ke ant main unka naam liya jaataa he | jis vyakti ne yah likhaa tha uskaa naam nahin likhaa gaya. lekin naarad smriti 4 sadi main yah ullekh miltaa ki manu smriti ke lekhak kaa naam sumati bhargav tha jo kii koi mahaan vyakti nahin tha. jis kaal main yah likhaa gaya tha vah kaal 170-150 isa purv man jaataa he |us samay is bharat main pratham pushyamitra sung kaa samrajya sthpit hataa tha. vah boddho par atyaachchaar ke liya prasidd tha.
