 pankalanghi ek prakaar kii ubhaychar machhlee hoti he, yani aisi machhlee jo lambe samay ke liye pani se baahar geeli ret main rah sake or kuch had tak chalane main bhi sakasham ho. pankalanghi apane finon kaa prayog kar ke dharti par chal paati hai or aise kshetron main rahati hai jo jowar-bhaata se kabhi to samudri pani se dhake hote hai or kabhi pani se baahar ho jate hai. in kshetron main anya machhliyaan bhi rahati hai lekin, jahaan ve pani waapas jane par bachane vaale pani ke chote pokharon main aashray leti hai, vahaan pankalanghi khuli ret par hi bhraman karne lagate hai.
