 acharya samantabhadra duusari sadi ke ek pramukh digambar acharya the. vah jain darshan ke pramukh siddhant, anekantwaad ke mahaan prachaarak the. unka janm kanchinagari main hua tha. unki sabase prakhyat rachana ratnakarand shravakachar hai.
 acharya samantabhadra muni ko sanyas jivan ke shruwati varshon main bhasmak naam kii vyaadhi ho gayi thi jisamen tirshna bhukh lagati hai. chunki digambar muni ek baar se zyada aahaar nahin lete, unhen isse kaaphi ksht hone laga jiske kaaran ant main unhone sallekhana vrath dhaaran karne kaa sochaa jab unhone apane guru se iske liye ajna maangi to guru ne isaki ajna nahin di. unke guru ne unhen muni vraton kaa tyaag kar rog upachaar karne ko kaha. upachaar ke baad acharya samantabhadra swami ne dobaaraa muni vraton ko dhaaran kiya or mahaan acharya hue.
