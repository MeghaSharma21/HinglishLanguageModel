 maniram dutt barua, assam ke ek samant the jinhen 1857 ke pratham swatantrata sangram main angrejon ne phaansi de di. unhone assam main pehla chaay bagaan sthapit kiya tha. pehle ve angrejon ke sahyogi the kintu 1857 main unhone unke viruddh hokar bharat kii janata kaa saath diya. ve maniram devan ke naam se adhik prasiddh hai.
