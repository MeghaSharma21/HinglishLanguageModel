 lutpaat sainya yaa raajnneetik jeet, yuddh, prakritik aapadaa yaa dange main tabaahi ke dauraan, bal dwaara jaan-maal kaa andhadhundh lenaa hota he. lutpaat yaa lut shabd kaa prayog saadhaaran chori yaa gaban ke liye bhi hota he.
 yuddh ke dauraan vijayi sena dwaara lutpaat karnaa itihaas bhar main aam baat raheen he. paidal sainikon ke liye yah ek tarah se unke aksar alp aay ke poorak ke rup main dekha jaataa tha or jeet ke jashn kaa hissaa hua karata tha. ucch str par lut kii pradarshnee garv kaa ek abhinn ang tha. bhartiya itihaas main mohammad gauri, mahmud gazanvi, temurlang or nadir shah kii lutpaat bahut badnaam he.
