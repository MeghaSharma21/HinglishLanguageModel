 qvin ek british rock band he, jiskaa gathan main landan main 1970 main hua tha. fredy mercury, bryan main, roger tailor, or jon dicon uske shruwati sadsy the. qvins ke shruwati kaary pragatisheel rock, hard rock or haivi metal se prabhavit the. lekin band baad main dhire-dhire wo or adhik paaramparik, pop rock or radio priy sangeet karne lagata he.
 iske gathan se pehle, bryan may or roger tailor ek saath ek band smil main karte the. fredy mercury smil ke ek prashansak the jinhone unhen adhik vistrut manch or recording takneekon ke saath prayog karne ke liye protsaahit kiya. mercury 1970 main band main shaamil ho gaye or band kaa nayaa naam, queen rakhane kaa suzav diya. jon dicon 1973 main band ke pehle album kii recording se pehle band main bhartee kiye gayi the. qvin 1974 main britain ke sangeet bazaar main apane duusare album, qvin 2 ke saath utre, lekin varshaant main shear hart atake va 1975 main album e nite at opera ne unhen antarrashtriya safalataa va khyaati dilaai. album ke gane vishesh rup se bohemian dhun 9 hafton tak sangeet bazaar main sheersh par rahaa or band va uske video ko prasiddh kar diya. unke 1977 ke elbum news of da world ke gane,"ve vil rock you" or "ve aar champion" khel aayojanon ke liye mukhya joshilay dhun ban gaye. 1980 ke dashak kii shruwaat main, qvin duniya main sabase bada stadium rock band ban gaya tha. unka 1985 kaa live aid sangeet kaarykram itihaas ke vibhinn sangeet pradarshnon main sarvshereshth main se ek or 2005 main kiye gayi ek audyogic surveykshan ke anusaar sabase acha mana gaya. 1991 main mercury kii bronchonimonia se mrityu ho gai. dicon 1997 main sevanivrutt ho gayi. tab se bryan or fredy ne kabhi kabhi ek saath pradarshan kiya he.
