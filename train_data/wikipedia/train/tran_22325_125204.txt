 indragarh bhartiya rajya rajasthan ke bundi jile kii ek nagarpalika he.
 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar indragarh kii kul jansankhya 5265 he. jisamen 51% purush evam 49% mahilaayein shaamil hai. indragarh kii ausat sakshartaa dar 63% he jo rashtriya ausat 59.5% se adhik he. yahan kii purush sakshartaa dar 72% or mahila sakshartaa dar 53% he. yahan chah varsh se kam umr ke bacchon kii sankhya kul jansankhya kaa 17% he.
