 govindpur jhakharahaa bharat main bihar rajya ke aitihaasik vaishali jilantargat ek chota gaanv he. nearesth shahar evam jila mukhyalay hajipur se yah gaanv 13 kilometre purv rashtriya rajmarg par avasthit he. saghan aabaadi vaale is krishipradhan gaanv main bajjika boli jaati he lekin shiksha kaa madhyam hindi or urdu he. lagbhag 1 varg kilometre kshetra vaale is chote se gaanv kii vartmaan jansankhya lagbhag 1800 he.
 ganga ke apvaah kshetra main sthit hone se yahan kii jameen upjau he jisamen dhan, gehun, makka ke atirikt anya khadyann evam dalhan tathaa tilhan kaa acha utpaadan hota he. saghan jansankhya evam kheton kaa aakaar chota hone se krishi kaa vyavsaayik rup nahin dikhaai detaa lekin tambaaku, mirch, sabji aadi ugaakar kuch log aarthik madad praapt kar lete hai. sinchai ke liye nijee nalakup yaa indradev kii krupa par log nirbhar hai. gaanv ke purvi seemaa se guzarane vaali nahar kaa uddhaar kar krishi main khushiyaali laai ja sakati he. nijee kshetron main vagabani tathaa vaaniki kaa acha vikaas hua he or gaanv main paryaapt hariyaali he. aam, lichi, pakad, sisam ke alaave tad or kele sarvatra dikhaai dete hai. adhikansh logon ne krishi ko vyavsaay rup main apana rakhaa he lekin bhoomihin logon ke liye mazdooree, dukandaaree yaa chota vyavsaay hi jeevika kaa saadhan he. sarkari naukari karnaa gaurav kii baat samajha jaataa he isliye shiksha praapt kar pratiyogita pariksha kii taiyaari main lag janaa aam baat he.
