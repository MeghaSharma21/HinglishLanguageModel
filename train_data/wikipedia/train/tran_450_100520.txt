 chunne miyaan kaa mandir bareli shahar main ek muslim vyavsaayee fajalurrahman khan kaa banbaaya hua hindu mandir he jise chunne miyaan ke naam se thik usee prakaar janaa jaataa he jaise nai dilli kaa birla mandir. yah swatantra bharat main hindu-muslim ekta kaa anupam udhaaharan he.
 bareli shahar main bada bazaar ke paas kohadapir kshetra main sthit is mandir main lakshminarayan kii bhavy murtiyan sthapit kii gayin hai.
 16 may 1960 ko bharat ke pratham raashtrapati dow rajendra prasad ne iskaa vidhivat udghaatan kiya tha.
 is mandir ke bare main jo jaankaree milti he uske anusaar bharat vibhaajan ke pashchaat bareli aa based kuch hinduon ne chunne miyaan ke naam se mashhoor ek muslim kii jameen par puja-paath ke liye ek chota-sa mandir bana liya. paas main hi budhvaari maszid bhi thi jisamen musalmaan namaaj padhaa karte the. chunna miyaan bahut paise vaale the unke paas sher chhap bidi kii agency hua karti thi. unhone jameen par kabza karne vaale hindu sharnaarthiyon par court main cese daayar kar diya.
 mukadmaa chal hi rahaa tha tabhi haridwar se ek sanyasi swami harmilapi ne aakar fajalurrahman khan sahab ko samajhaayaa ki muslimon ke paas to maszid he parantu in bechaare hinduon ke paas kya he. aap to seth chunna miyaan ke naam se mashhoor ho ye chota sa jameen kaa tukdaa apane in hindu bhaaiyon ko mandir banane ke liye daan nahin de sakate? kehete hai ki chunna miyaan swamiji kii baat se itane adhik prabhavit hue ki unhone na sirf mukadmaa waapas liya balki lakshminarayan bhagwaan kaa bhavy mandir bhi banavaa diya. puure bareli shahar main aaj bhi yah chunne miyaan ke mandir ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
