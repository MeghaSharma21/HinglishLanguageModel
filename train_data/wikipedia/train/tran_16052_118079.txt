 conrad kongkal sangama meghalaya rajya se ek bhartiya rajaneta he tathaa meghalaya rajya ke barahawein va vartmaan mukhyamantri hai. ve purv loksabhaa adhyaksh pi e sangama ke putr hai. ve purv main aanthvi meghalaya vidhansabha main netaa vipaksh the, jahaan ve pashchim gaaro hills jile ke sellsella vidhansabha nirvachan kshetra kaa pratinidhitiv karte the. iske saath hi ve 2008 main meghalaya ke sabase kam umr ke vitt mantri bhi bane the. ve vartmaan main tura lok sabha nirvachan kshetra se saansad he.
 apni padhai puri karne ke baad unhone 1990 ke dashak main apana rajnatik jivan shuru kiya. us samay ve apane pita pi e sangama ke chunaav prabandhak the. ve meghalaya vidhansabha chunaav, 2008 main apane bhai james sangama ke saath pehali baar rashtravaadi congress party se vidhayak bane or baad main unhone pradesh cabinet main mahatvapurn vibhaag sanbhale, jisame vitt, vaanijya, paryaton tathaa soochna prasaaran pramukh the. unhone das din ke andar rajya kaa vaarshik bajet pesh kiya.
 meghalaya vidhansabha chunaav, 2018 main kisi bhi dal ko purna bahumat na mil sakaa. 59 main se 21 siten jitkar bhartiya rashtriya congress sabase badi party bani. uske baad sangama kii neshnal piples party ne 19 siten jiti. bhartiya janata party ke 2, united democratic party ke 6, piples democratic front ke 4, hill state piples democratic party ke 2 tathaa ek nirdaleey vidhayak ke samarthan ke kaaran meghalaya ke rajyapal ganga prasad ne sangama ke dal ko sarkaar banane kaa nimantran diya. iske pashchaat 6 march 2018 ko unhen mukhyamantri pad kii shapath dilaayi gayi. 
 sangama meghalaya ke purv mukhyamantri pi e sangama ke putr hai. unki bahan agaatha sangama pandrahavin loksabhaa main saansad tathaa kendra sarkaar main rajya mantri thim, jabaki unke bhai james sangama meghalaya vidhansabha main vipaksh ke mukhya sachetak hai. 
 unki vidyalayan shiksha saint columbus vidhyalay, nai dilli se tathaa ucch shiksha landan vishvavidhyalay tathaa pencylvania vishvavidhyalay se hui.
