 khoi bhashaayen africa ke dakshini hisse main boli jane vaali bhashaaon kaa vah sabase bada parivaar he jo baantu bhashaa-parivaar kaa sadsy nahin he. yah pehle khoisan bhashaaon kii ek shakha maani jaati thi or 'madhya khoisan' ke naam se jaani jaati thi, lekin aadhunik kaal main yah ek alag parivaar samajha jaataa he. khoi bhashaaon main se sarvaadhik boli jane vaali bhashaa namaa bhashaa he jo namibia main boli jaati he. anya khoi bhashaayen botswana main kalahari registan main boli jaati hai. khoi bhashaayen apane clic vyanjano ke liye jaani jaati hai.
