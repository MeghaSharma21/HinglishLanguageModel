 madhya pradesh hindi granth academy bhopal main july 1969 se hindi granth academy sthapit he. sanstha kaa daayitv kendra pravartit yojana ke antargat vishvavidhyalay str kii 25 vishyon kii pathyapustakon ke sahitya ko hindi main prakashit kar uplabdh karana he. academy dwaara ab tak vibhinn vishyon kii saikadon pustakon kaa prakaashan kiya gaya he. madhyapradesh hindi granth academy kaa apana swayan kaa prashasnic bhawan he. madhyapradesh hindi granth academy dwaara chaatr-chaatraon ke liye 'na laabh na haani' ke siddhant par pustaken sulabh karaayi jaati hai. academy vibhinn 35 vishyon main 1000 se adhik granthon kaa prakaashan kar chuki he. inamen sabhi sankayo kii pustaken shaamil hai. durlabh paathya saamagree ke anuwaad bhi prakashit kiye jate hai. rashtriya str par academy ke anek aalekh evam granthon ko prakashit kiya jaataa he.
 kshetreey bhashaaon ke madhyam se ucch shiksha sansthaanon hetu paathya saamagree kaa nirmaan kiye jane kii bharat sarkaar, maanav sansaadhan mantralay kii kendra pravartit yojanantargat sarkaar dwaara riyayati daron par praapt kaagaj se mudran tathaa prakaashan hetu m.pra. hindi granth academy kii sthapana july 1969 main kii gai thi. kendra sarkaar ne pochavi panchavarshiya yojana se academy kaa anudaan dene kaa praavdhaan kiya. yojanantargat ejiniyari, chikitsa evam krishi tathaa sanderbhit granth evam saanskrithik or saahitiyik tathaa saamaajik prishthbhumi par aadhaarit praapt pandulipiyon se paathya samagri taiyaar karaayi jaati he. yah kaary kendriya hindi nideshaalay ke nirdesan main kiya jaataa he.
 is prakaar kii saamagree kaa prakaashan karaane hetu ek ore jahaan chaatro kaa utkrisht paathya saamagree uplabdh karana he vahiin kshetriy sahitya tathaa sthaneey praapt saamagree ke aadhaar par bhugol,itihaas,puraatatv se sambandhit anek paathya saamagree taiyaar karaayi jaati he jise chaatr sandarbh granth ke rup main upyog karte he.
