 parhimalay tibet main himalay se samaantar chalane vaali ek parvatamala kaa naam he. tibet main himalay brahmaputra nadi se dakshin main chalate hai jabaki parhimalay shrunkhlaa us nadi se uttar main himalay ke saath-saath chalati he. hindu dharm kaa pavitra kailash parvat parhimalay parvatamala ke pashchimi hisse main sthit he isaliye is parvatamala ke pashchimi bhaag ko 'kailash parvatamala' bhi kehete hai. purvi hisse main nyenchen tangalha shrunkhlaa he. isaliye parhimalay ko kailash-nyenchen thangalha shrunkhlaa bhi kehete hai. tibeti bhashaa main kailash parvat ko 'gangarinpoche' kehete hai. isliye china kii sarkaar parhimalay ko gangadise-nyenchen thangalha shrunkhlaa kahati he.
