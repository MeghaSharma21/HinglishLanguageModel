 roman kaanch, pracheen rome main kaanch se nirmit un sabhi vastuon ko sanderbhit karata he, jinka nirmaan gharelu, audyogic yaa phir antyeshtic upyog ke liye kiya jaataa tha. roman kaal main kaanch kaa istemaal mukhyat: sajaavati paatr, mosek tile yaa khidakiyon ko banane ke liye kiya gaya tha. romanon ke kaanch utpaadan kii takanic greek mul kii he.
