 sorathi brijbhaar yaa sorathi birjaabhaar bhojpuri kaa premakavy he jo purvi uttar pradesh tathaa bihar main gaaya or suna jaataa he. iske gayak bahut kam bache hai. ‘ekia ho rama’, ‘ho ram’, ‘ram na re kii’ jaise tech le yah katha gaayi jaati rahi he.
 isamen saat janmon kii katha sau bhaagon main milti he. indra ke shaap se ek dev or ek apsara mritylok main janm lete hai or aage janam janam milte-bichhadte rahete hai.
