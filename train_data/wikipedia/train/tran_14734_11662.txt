 sanyukt rajya america ke rajya indiana ke 12 aadhikarik rajya pratik va anya nirdisht aadhikarik vastuen hai. mukhyatah suchi main varnit pratik aadhikarik taur par indiana general assembly ke adhinium ke dwaara banaae gaye va manyata praapt hai tathaa rajya ke rajyapal dwaara kanoon main hastakshar kiye hue hai. ye indiana code sheershak 1, anuchhed 2, rajya pratik main soochibaddh hai jo in vastuon kii dikhaawat or upyuktata ko bhi viniyamit karata he.
 anya america rajyon kii tulana main indiana ke kuch hi aadhikarik pratik hai. sabase pehla pratik indiana kii muhar tha, jise 1801 main indiana rajyashetra ke liye tathaa 1816 main indiana rajya dwaara aadhikarik banaaya gaya tha. lagbhag ek sadi ke liye keval isane hi rajya ke ekmaatr pratik kii bhoomika nibhaai thi. 1913 main rajya geet ko abhigrahan karke use rajya kaa doosraa pratik ghoshit kar diya gaya. kai varshon tak indiana hi ekmaatr aisa rajya tha jiskaa koi bhi aadhikarik dhvaj nahin tha. aadhikarik rajya binar ko 1917 main apanaaya gaya or 1955 main iskaa naam badalakar ise rajya dhvaj kaa darja diya gaya. indiana kaa naveenatam pratik rajya ricel he, jiskaa naam grouisland ricel he. isko varsh 2012 main 117ve indiana mahasabha ke duusare niyamit satr main 48â€“2 ke maton ke saath apanaaya gaya tha.
