 kapler-22bi pehla gyaat gair-saureey grah he jo prithvi jaisaa he or ek suraj jaise taare ke vaasayogy kshetra main parikrama kar rahaa he. yah prithvi se 600 prakaash varsh dur hans taramandal ke kshetra main sthit kapler-22 taare kii parikrama kar rahaa he.
 vaigyanikon ne is grah kaa pataa kapler antariksh yaan ke zariye lagaaya or iske astitv kii ghoshnaa 5 disambar 2011 ko kii. andaaza lagaaya jaataa he ki yah prithvi kaa 2.4 guna vyas rakhata he, lekin isaki sataha kisi he iskaa koi anumaan nahin lag paaya he. agar iskaa ghanatv prithvi jaisaa hua to iskaa dravyaman prithvi kaa 13.8 guna hoga or isaki sataha par iskaa gurutvkarshan prithvi kaa 2.4 guna hoga. yani isaki sataha par khadaa 70 kilogram ke bhaar vaala aadmi apane aap ko 168 kilo kaa mahasoos karegaa. is shrenee ke grahon ko mahaprithvi kaha jaataa he.
