 ve hindi sahitya ke prasiddh samalochak or janavaadee lekhak sangh ke sansthaapak mahasachiv the.
 unka janm gajipur jila main hua tha. unhone agra ke raja balavant kalege or banaras ke upi kalege main angreji kaa adhyaapan kiya. ve janavaadee lekhak sangh ke adhyaksh bhi rahe. girane ke kaaran unke kuulhe kii haddi tut gai thi. is kaaran ve lagbhag ek saal tak bistar par rahe. 87 varsh kii avasthaa main 6 june 2011 kii subah unka nidhan ho gaya. laal jhande main lipte unke parthiv sharir ko bhartiya communist party ke dashashwamedh karyaalay par le jaaya gaya jahaan bhaavbheene shradhanjali di gai. iske baad harishchandra ghat par unka antim sanskaar kiya gaya.
 unki pramukh aalochanatmak kritiyon main ‘lokadrushti or hindi sahitya’ or ‘sahitya aalochana kaa janapaksh’ prasiddh rahihui. unhone pablo neruda, maaychovsky, emily dickinson, walt hvitman, najim hikmat sareekhe lekhakon kii kavitaaon kaa anuwaad kiya.
 nahin rahe marxvaadi chintak chandrabali singh
