 canadian footbaal, gridiron footbal kaa ek rup he, yah canada main lokapriy he. yah baarah khilaadiyon kii do tiimon ke bich khelaa jaataa he jisamen har team kaa uddeshy he ki vah gend ko duusari team ke "and zone" main pahunchaakar ank batore. pehla canada footbal match 9 november 1861 ko khelaa gaya tha.
