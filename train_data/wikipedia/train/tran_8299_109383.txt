 kshareey parthiv dhaatuen aavart sarini ke samooh-2 main sthit rasinic tattvon vah samooh he jisamen berilium, magnesium, calcium, stronsium, barium evam radium hai.
 prarambh main rasayanajna un padaarthon ko mrudaa kehete the jo adhatuen thim or jinpar atyadhik taap kaa koi prabhaav nahin padataa tha. inamen kuch padaarthon ke gun kshaaron ke gunon se bahut milte julate the. isse unhone use 'kshareey mrudaa' naam diya.kshareey mrudaa main chuunaa, stronsia or barita 1807 i. tak rasinic tatv samajhe jate the. hamphri davy ne pehle pahal pramanit kiya ki ye vastut: calosium, stronsium or barium dhaatuon ke oxide hai.
 ye dhaatuen asamyukt dasha main nahin pai jaati. inke do prakaar ke oxide banate hai. ek saamaanya oxide, jo ushmakshepan ke saath jal main ghulte hai or duusare peroysaide, jo jal main ghulkar hydraksaide banaate hai or vaayu main khulaa rakhane se carbon daai oxide kaa avasorana karte hai. dhaatuon ke donon hi oxide samaakshareey hote hai or amlon main sheeghra ghulkar tadnukool lavan banaate hai. tatwon ke parmanubhaar kii vruddhi se hydraxidon kii vileyata badhati jaati hai, par sulpheton kii vileyata ghatati jaati he.
 ye dhaatuen vaayu main khuli rahane se jald upachayit ho jaati he. inke lavan achche manibh banaate hai. chloride or nitrate jal main sheeghra ghul jate hai, par carbonate, phosphate or sulphate k m ghulte athwa ghulte hi nahin.
