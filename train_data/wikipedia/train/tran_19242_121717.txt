 uttar korea pehle, 1964 main olympic khelon main bhaag liya he ki varsh keval sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main dikhaai de rahaa. aath saal baad 1972 main, raashtra ne pehle greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main bhaag liya. tab se, har greeshmakaleen khelon main raashtra saamne aayaa he, sivaay iske ki jab uttari korea ne 1984 ke greeshmakaleen olympic ke soviet netritv ke bahishkaar main shaamil ho gaye, or jab unhone dakshin korea main sol, 1988 main khelon kaa bahishkaar kiya. sheetakaaleen khelon main uttar korea kii upasthiti vilakshan ho gai he; keval pichley saat khelon main se saat main ek uttar korian team shaamil he. 2010 main ek team vainkuvar main sheetakaaleen olympic main bhaag lee thi.
 uttari korian athleton ne kul 56 padak jite, jinamein se do sheetakaaleen khelon main jite the.
 uttar korea ke liye rashtriya olympic samiti, democratic pipuls republic of korea kii olympic samiti he, or ise 1953 main banaaya gaya tha or 1957 main manyata praapt thi.
 1998-2007 ke sunshine policy yug ke dauraan, uttar korea or dakshin korea ne 2000 or 2004 ke olympic ke udghaatan samarohon main pratikatmak rup se ek team ke rup main march kiya; lekin alag se pratispardhaa.
 isaki kharaab arthvyavastha ko dekhte hue, uttar korea kaa pradarshan prabhaavshaalee he. 2016 main, isaki padak sankhya main prati vyakti jidipi kii tulana main atyadhik moolyankan kiya gaya.
 agar uttar korea 2018 ke sheetakaaleen olympic ke liye uteern he, to unhen dakshin korea main DMZ ko paar karne kii anumati di jaaegi.
