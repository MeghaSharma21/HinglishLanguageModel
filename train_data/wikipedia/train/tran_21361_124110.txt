 
 tibeti raamchakor  fizant kul kaa ek pakshi he jo pashchimi himalay tathaa tibeti pathaar ke oonchaai vaale ilaakon main rahata he or himalay ke kuch ilaakon main yah himalay ke raamchakor ke saath ilaaka baantta he.
 iskaa sir saleti rang kaa hota he or isaki aankh ke peeche safed ardhachandrakaar dhabba hota he. iske saamne ke bhaag main safed par kaali dhaariyaan hoti hai. yah himalay ke raamchakor se aakaar main thodaa chota hota he.
 turki bhashaa main ise ular, kirghis bhashaa main utar, mongol bhashaa main ise hylic tathaa tangut bhashaa main ise ganamo kehete hai.
 tajikistan, china, bhutan, nepal tathaa bharat
 yah pakshi garmiyon main 17,000 fit tak kii oonchaai main paaya jaataa he lekin sardiyon main yah 10,000 se 14,000 fit tak paaya jaataa he. yah jangalon ko nahin balki jhaad jhankad vaale ilaakon ko adhik pasand karata he, yaa yah patharile or ganje ilaakon main bhi paaya ja saktaa he or amuman jahaan tak barf kii rekha ho yaa usase bhi oonchaai main paaya jaataa he..
