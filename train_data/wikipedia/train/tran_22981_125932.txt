 paraayaa naatak ko kuch paatro ke madhyam se samajhane kaa praytn kiya gaya he
 jisame mukhya paatr ravi jisaki umr maatr 13 varsh he .or ek dadaji he said role main do mahilaye ,ek baccha or anya paatr rakhe gaye jo keval puraa naatak ko samajhane ke liye rahe gaye he .
 paraayaa naatak kii shuruyaat
 us samay hoti he jab naatak kaa pramukh paatr ravi ghar se saiyr par niklaa to raste main usne do mahilao ko baat karte dekha or suna ,
 savita boli –
 anitha boli –
 kya ?
 savita boli –
 wo jo mahendra kaa ladkaa tha wo adhikari banate hi ghar chhod ke chalaa gaya
 anitha boli –
 kya jamaana aa gaya jab apana hi nahi to wo to khayr paraayaa tha
 savita boli –
 sahi kah rahi ho anitha
 
 ‎phir ye bate sunakar ravi aage kii ore badha to usne do ladakiyon ko ladate dekha jo kah rahi thi ...
 ek ladki bolti –
 i tuhar kapadaa ha t i hamaar kapadaa ha
 duusari ladkee bolti –
 kii i hamaar kapada ha .
 or donon aapas main hi lard gayi kabhi ek ladki duusare ladki par chillaati to kabhi duusari pehle par jaise maano ek duusare ko maar daalegi . ye hamaar or tuhar dekhakar ravi kaaphi udhaas ho gaya or phir wo aage kii saiyr par nikal padaa apane safar kii aage kii kadi main wo apane ek sage ke ghar pahunchaa jaise hi wo panhucha uske paas ek baccha aayaa or ‎ bolaa – ‎ kya karne aaye ho ? ‎ravi bolaa - ‎ aise hi ghoomte chale aayaa . ‎ ‎phir wo saiyr kii kadi main aage ek vivaah main gaya jahaan usne kuch ghatnaaye dekhi or suni jiske baad usne nirnay kiya kii vah ye jaan kar rakhega kii ye paraayaa kya hota he ye shabd uske liye anjaanaa tha wo samaj nahi paa rahaa tha kii kya kare vivaah atend karne ke baad wo ghar kii ore laut rahaa tha kii raste main uski nazar ek vriksh ke paas baithe ek dadaji par padi ravi ko aabhaar ho gaya kii uske prashno kaa uttar dadaji ke paas jarur mil jaayegaa ravi ne tanik bhi der nahi kiya jhat se us dadaji ke paas pahucha gaya. sammaan janak shabdon kaa prayog karte hue ‎ravi bolaa - ‎dada ji mere jehen main ek vichaar aayaa he kya aap mujhe samajha sakate he kii iskaa matlab kya hota he ? dadaji ne tanik bhi sankoch nahi kiya
 dadaji ‎turant bolaa -
 ‎ haa betaa bolo !
 ‎ravi bolaa -
 ‎ ye paraayaa kya hota he?
 ‎dadaji mujhe dhyaan se dekhne lage jaise maano wo hamase ye kahana chaah rahe ho ‎kii itane chote bachche ke dimag main ye baat aayin kaise or kyon
 ‎‎dadaji bole -
 ‎kisne tumse ye baat kahi betaa ?
 ‎
 ravi bolaa -
 ‎ aksar apana or paraayaa shabd logo se sunne ko mil jaataa hai .
 ‎
 ‎dadaji bole -
 ‎ tumne koi to aisa vaakia jarur dekha hoga jise dekh tumhe laga ho kii tumhe paraayaa kya he ye jaan lenaa chahie ?
 ‎
 ‎ravi bolaa –
 ‎
 ‎dadaji bole -
 ‎ wo kisi vastu ke liye aapas main ghichaa tani kar thi to isamen apana paraaye kaa kya ?
 ‎
 ‎ravi bolaa -
 ‎ mujhe laga kii shaayad isi ko paraayaa kehete ho
 ‎dadaji bole -
 ‎ or koi vaakia jaanate ho to bataao jo dekh tumhe laga kii tumhe paraayaa jaananaa chahie ?
 ‎
 ‎ravi bolaa -
 ‎ iske alaava ek baar main kisi ke ghar gaya tha wo mere kisi sage sambandhi kaa ghar tha jo ab alag rah rahe the jaise hi main waha pahucha ek chota sa baccha mere paas aayaa or dhire se poocha - kya karne aaye ho ?
 ‎maine uttar diya -
 ‎kya aise poochne ko paraayaa kehete he ?
 ‎
 ‎ dadaji bole -
 ‎ise bhi paraayaa nahi kehete wo to tumhaare aane kaa kaaran puch rahaa tha wo to chota sa baccha tha usase isse kya lenaa dena. or koi vaakia jaanate ho to bataao?
 ‎
 ravi bolaa -
 ‎ ek ghatana mujhe or bhi yaad aa rahi he jab mai kisi ke ghar shaadi main gaya thi maine waha dekha puraa ghar dulhan kii taraf sajaa tha
 mere ek mitra kii bahan kii shaadi thi main bhi bahut khush tha mujhe yaad he aaj bhi wo manoram din kahu yaa use kaali raat kahu jab apana kehanaale vaala sambandhi us bahan kii shaadi bich main hi chhod kar chalaa gaya jaise maano kisi kii shaadi atend karne aaye wo bhojan kiye or chal die iske alaava bhi bahut si ghatnaaye ghati .
 ‎kya ise paraayaa kehete he ?
 dadaji bole –
 ravi bolaa –
 dadaji bole –
 ravi bolaa –
 ek mahila dusre ko bataa rahi thi kii wo to uskaa sagaa ladkaa nahi isliye chhod kar chalaa gaya ..
 
 ‎dadaji bole -
 ‎ betaa teri baaten sunaqar to meri aankhe bhar aayin
 mujhe nahi lagata jisane itnaa dekha ho use ab paraayaa shabd ko mujhe bataate kii zarurat he phir bhi main tuje bataa rahaa hoon... paraayaa samaaj main samaanyataur par khun ke rishte se lagaaya jaataa he.
 ravi bolaa -
 ‎ khun kaa rishta iskaa matlab dadaji kya hota he ?
 ‎
 ‎dadaji bolo -
 ‎ jo ek maan kii kokh se janm lete he uname khun kaa rishta hota he unhen saaga kehete he or jo duusare kii kokh se janm le unhen paraayaa kehete he
 ‎ravi bolaa -
 ‎ tab ho dadaji wo jo do ladkia aapas main lard rahi thi ek kapadey ke liye wo to sagi nahi hui kyoki uname khun kaa rishta nahi tha ?
 ‎dadaji bole -
 ‎ haan betaa tu sahi kah rahaa he .
 ‎ravi bolaa -
 ‎ to isamen sagaa koun he ?
 ‎
 ‎ ‎dadaji bole -
 ‎ wo jo shaadi apni bahan kii bich main chhod gaya .
 ‎ ravi bolaa -
 ‎ to iskaa matlab ye hua kii jisame khun kaa rishta ho vahi sage hue ? dadaji bole -
 ‎ nahi betaa samaaj main to usee ko sagaa yaa apana kehete he par aisa sagaa kis kaam kaa jisame apanepanaka bhav nahi wo bhi paraayo ke hi samaan hote he .
 ‎
 ravi bolaa -
 ‎ iskaa matlab wo jo shaadi chhod gaye wo bhi paraaye ke samaan he ?
 
 ‎dadaji bole -
 ‎ haa betaa toom sahi kah rahe ho
 ‎isi ko duniya main apana or paraayaa kehete he
 ‎
 ‎ravi bolaa -
 ‎ bahut achche dadaji aapane to mujhe sab samajha diya ‎dadaji ko dhanyavad bolkar ravi chalaa gaya yahi par is naatak kii samaapt ho jaati he.
