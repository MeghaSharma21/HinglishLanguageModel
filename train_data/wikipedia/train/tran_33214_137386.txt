 tathy : indian council act, 1892 bhartiya netaaon ko santusht karne ke main vifal rahaa. bharat kii rajnatik paristhitiyon ne british netaaon ko bhartiya rashtri congress ke naram dal yaa udaarvaadiyon kaa samarthan praapt karne ke liye sanvaidhanik sudhaaron par sochane ke liye majaboor kiya. shri gopal krishna gokhale, jo udaarvaadiyon ke netaa the, "bharat mantri" lord jon morle ke samaksh apane vichaar prastut karne ke liye unase milane england gaye. shri gokhale ne morle ko yah bharosa dilaayaa kii bharat main sanvaidhanik sudhaaron kii taatkaalik aavashyaktaa he. lord morle, shri gokhale he ke vichaaron se sahamat the or lord minto, jo us samay bharat ke visarai the, bhi sanvaidhanik sudhaaron ke laaye jane ke paksh main the. bhartiya parishad adhinium, 1909, jise saamanytah morle-minto sudhaaron ke rup main janaa jaataa he, united kingdom kii sansad ke ek adhinium ke rup main laayaa gaya tha jiske dwaara british bharat ke shashan main simit rup main bharatiyon kii bhagidaari badhaane hetu prabandh kiya janaa tha. 
  udaar dal ke bharat sachivjon morle evam roodhiwaadee dal ke bharat ke vysarai, gilbert iliyat-mare-caninamound, 4 arl of minto, kaa mat tha ki lord karzan ke bengal vibhaajan se upje asantosh ko dabaanaa jaruri tha lekin british raaj ke sthayitv ke liye daman hi paryaapt nahin tha. unka spasht mat tha ki bharat ke vafaadaar sambhraant varg or samaaj ke sanvardhansheel pashchimikrut varg ko ek naatakeey kadam uthaa kar santusht karnaa behad aavashyak tha. 
 is tarah unhone bhartiya parishad adhinium, 1909 ko prastut kiya . bhartiya rashtri congress ki mukhya maang thi "swashaasi british coloniyon kii bhaanti sarkaar kii vyavastha" lekin is maang ko maanane ki is adhinium main koi ichha nahin dikhaai gayi.
