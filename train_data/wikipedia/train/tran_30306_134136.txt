 rashtriya praudyogiki santhaan, tadepalligudam andhra pradesh main sthit rashtriya mahatv kaa santhaan he. yah bharat ke prashiddh rashtriya praudyogiki sansthaanon main se ek he. ise 'enaiti tadepalligudam' yaa 'enaiti andhra pradesh' ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. is santhaan main shikshan kaary san 2015 se prarambh hua tha. purnakalik campus kaa nirmaan 400 acre main shuru ho chukaa he. iske liye paisa maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantralay detaa he.
