 vellnadu 
 venkateshwar press, bombay se prakashit 'jati-bhaskar' granth ke anusaar " utkal ke dakshin, dravid ke uttar, karnataka ke purvottar, maharashtra ke pashchim, arthath shrishail se chola sthaan ke madhya, tailang-pradesh he!" nathdwara ke prasiddh vidwaan or vidyavibhag pramukh potkurchi pan. kanthmani shastri ke shabdon main- " tailang pradesh main tailang brahmanon ke anek bhed hai. jo brahman pardesh se aa kar based the, ve 'vellanati' athwa 'velnadi' naam se prasidd hue.... vril=bahari bhaag, nadu=desh." baad main yahi 'vellanati' vellnadu kehalaae.
