 mesar ek aisa yantra he jo uddipt utsarjan dwaara pravardhan ke madhyam se sambanddh vidyutchumbakeey tarange utpann karata he. aitihaasik rup se yah parivarni naam mesar se vyutpann hota he jiskaa arth vikiran ke uddipt utsarjan dwaara sookshmtarang pravardhan he.
 mesar albert oinstin dwaara 1817 main prastaavit uddipt utsarjan ke siddhant par kaary karata he. jab parmaanu apni uttejit urja avasthaa main hota he, to ve yathochit aavruti par vikiran pravardhit kar saktaa he.
 aise pravardhak madhyam ko anunaad guha main rakhane par, punarniveshan kaa nirmaan hota he jo sambanddh vikiran utpann kar saktaa he.
 i san 2012 main, neshnal fijical laboretory, united kingdom va imperial college landan ke ek shodh samooh ne pravardhak madhyam ke rup main pentasana-doped p-temphenl kaa upyog karte huye kamare ke taap par thos avasthaa mesar ke nirmaan kaa tarika dhund nikaala. 
 mesar kaa upyog ucch koti aavruti sandarbhon ke liye kiya jaataa he.
 2012 tak kaa sabase mahatvapurn tarah kaa mesar hydrogen mesar he jo vartmaan main parmanvik aavruti maanak ke rup main kaam main liya jaataa he.
