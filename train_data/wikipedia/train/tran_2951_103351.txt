 ganit main char satat yaa asatat ho sakate hai. yadi koi char, do vaastavik maanon ke bich ke sabhi vaastavik maan grahan kar saktaa he to kaha jaataa he ki vah char in do maanon ke bich satat he. kintu yadi koi char do maanon ke bich sabhi vaastavik maanon ko nahin grahan kar saktaa balki keval kuch hi maan grahan kar saktaa he to us char ko asatat char kehete hai. aisa bhi sambhav he ki koi char sankhya rekha ke kuch bhaag main satat ho jabaki kisi anya bhaag main asatat.
