 santari burj yaa paryvekshan burj aise burj ko kaha jaataa he jiskaa prayog aasapaas ke kshetra par nigaraanee rakhane ke liye kiya jaataa ho. aksar aise santari burjon kaa prayog faujee yaa jail sambandhi kaarnon se kiya jaataa he. jahaan bahut se saadhaaran burj kisi imaarat ke saath jude hote hai, vahaan santari burj aksar imaaraton se alag khadey hote hai or unka nirmaan kabhi-kabhi qilenuma hota he taaki unpar tainaat paharedaar upar se shatruon par hamalaa kar sake or apane kshetra par hue akraman se raksha kar saken.
 dakshin bharat ke chinnar praani udyaan main van main bana ek santari burj
 siberia ke irkutsk shahar main pradarshit ek ostrog kaa santari burj
 rus ke noginsk shahar main ek santari burj
 kirghizstan main chu nadi ke paas ek santari burj
 japan main tokyo ke shahi mahal kaa ek timanzila santari burj
 america ke grand canyan arakshit kshetra kaa yavapai santari burj
