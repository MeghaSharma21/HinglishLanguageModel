 rajeev gandhi udyaan bharat ke rajasthan rajya ke udaipur zile main sthit ek bada udyaan he. yah udyaan fatehsagar jhil ke najadik sthit he.
 rajeev gandhi udyaan kaa nirmaan or shubharambh 08 may 2008 ko raajnneetijna vasundhara raaje ne kiya tha. yah purv raajnneetijna rajeev gandhi kii yaad main banwaaya gaya rajeev gandhi jo ki sabase choti umr ke pradhanmantri rah chuke hambharat ke. yah udyaan bahut sundar he jisamen kai prakaar ke ped evam paudhe he.
