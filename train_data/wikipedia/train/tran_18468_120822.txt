 madhya-mahaasaagar parvatamala kisi mahaasaagar ke jal ke andar plate vivartaniki dwaara bani ek parvatamala hoti he. saamanytah isamen kai pahaad shrunkhalaaon main yojit hote hai or inke bich main ek lambi rift naamak ghati chalati he. is rift ke neeche do bhugolic takhton kii sanmilan seemaa hoti he jahaan dabaav or ragad ke kaaran bhupraavaar se pighla hua megma ugalkar lava ke rup main upar aata he or nayaa sagar kaa farsh banaata he - ise prakriya ko sagar nital prasaran kehete hai.
