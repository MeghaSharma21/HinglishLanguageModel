 birbal : asli naam:mahesh das yaa mahesh das bhatta, mughal badshaah akbar ke prashasan main mughal darbar kaa pramukh vazeer tha or akbar ke parishad ke nau salaahkaaro main se ek sabase vishwast sadsy tha jo akbar ke navratno the yah sanskrit shabd he jiskaa arth nau ratnon he. akbar ke alaava yah dusraa vyakti tha, jisane deen- e -ilaahi dharm mana tha. 
 bachapan kaa naam mahesh das tha.
 akbar ke darbar main akbar inhen "vir var" kaa khitaab diya, chalate chalate vah birbal hogaya. 
 akbar ke darbar main birbal kaa zyaadaatar kaary sainya or prashasnic the tathaa vah samraat kaa ek bahut hi kareebi dost bhi tha, samraat aksar buddhi or gyaan ke liye birbal kii saraahna karte the. ye kai anya kahanio, lokkathaon or kathao kii ek samrudh parampara kaa hissaa ban gaye hai.
