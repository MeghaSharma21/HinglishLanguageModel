 twashta yaa twashtra yaa twashtri sanskrit sahitya main jati or vyaktivaachak sangyaaon kaa bodh karaane ke liye anek arthon main prayukt hua he. mahabharat tathaa vishnupurana ke anusaar 12 adityon main 11 kaa naam twashta he. uskaa hindu dharm main pramukh sthaan he. is devtaa ke sambandh main ek devakatha milti he ki usne apane putree sangya kaa vivaah surya se kar diya kintu sangya surya kaa tej na sahan kar saki or pita ke paas laut ai. twashta ne surya ke prabhamandal main thodi kaant chhaant kar di, tab sangya kaa us alpatejas surya ke paas rahanaa sambhav ho gaya. dharmik hinduon kaa aisa vishwaas he ki surya ke kate hue bhaag se vishnu kaa chakra or shiv kaa trishul bana. mahabharat ke ek anya sthal main prajapati ko tathaa rugveda ke sayan bhashya main indra ko twashta kaha gaya he. twashta ke mul kaary, surya ko chhaantane, ke kaaran dhire dhire uskaa arth badhai se liya jane laga, jo kaashthadikon ko swaroop pradaan karata he. isi kaaran twashta se vishwakarma kaa bhi bodh hua.
 rugveda ke dasave mandal va purush sookt main unka ullekh hua he. ve divya lohaar va dhaatu se shastr va auzaar nirmaan karte hai. unhen indra ke vajra kaa nirmaataa or som kaa rakshak kaha gaya he. rugveda main unka 65 baar zikr aata he or unhen jaanwaron va maanvon ke shariron kaa rachiyata bhi bulaayaa gaya he. santan paane waalon ke liye unhen garbh-pati kaha gaya he. unke liye rathkaar or taksha bhi naam upyog hue hai.
 in devtaa ke naam par brihaspati grah ke aayo chandramaa par ek jwalamukheey kshetra kaa naam rakhaa gaya he.
