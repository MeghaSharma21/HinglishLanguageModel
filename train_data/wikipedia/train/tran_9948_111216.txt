 kripaya vitt prabandhan se bhramit na hon.
 vittiya prashasan shabd kaa prayog vyapak arth main kiya jaataa he. isamen ve sab prakriyaayen sammilit kii jaati hai jo ki nimna kaaryon ko sampann karne main utpann hoti haih 
 vitt ke bina sarkaar apane uddeshy main purnatah safal nahin ho sakati. prashasan ke liye vitt kii itani adhik mahatta hone ke kaaran vitt ke prashasan kaa adhyayan bhi atyant mahatvapurn ho gaya he. jo sarkaar vittiya prashasan kii ek santoshajanak vyavastha kaa nirmaan kar leti he vah apane kaaryon kaa prabandh kushalta ke saath karne kii disha main kaaphi aage bad jaati he. is prakaar vittiya prashasan, joki ek si vyavastha tathaa ritiyon kaa nirmaan karata he jinke dwaara lok sevaaon ke sanchaalan ke liye dhan praapt kiya jaataa he, vyay kiya jaataa he or uskaa lekha rakhaa jaataa he, aadhunik sarkaar kaa hriday mana jaataa he.
 vittiya prashasan ek aisi gatisheel prakriya he jo ki nimnlikhit sankriyaaon kii ek satat shrunkhlaa kaa nirmaan karti he -
 vittiya prashasan main upar bataai gai prakriyaayen sammilit hai. ye vittiya kriyaayen nimnlikhit abhikaranon dwaara sampann kii jaati hai-
 vittiya prashasan kaa sanchaalan tathaa niyantran inhen abhikaranon dwaara kiya jaataa he.
