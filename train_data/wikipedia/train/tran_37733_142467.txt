 2018 emsisi tri-raashtra shrinkhalaa ek cricket toornament tha jise 29 july 2018 ko england main lords main yojit kiya gaya tha. series ne nepal, netherland or marillebon cricket club ko twanti-20 phiksture ke rup main khele gaye maichon ke saath dikhaaya. nepal or netherland ke bich teesraa muqabla antarrashtriya cricket parishad dwaara purna twanti-20 antarrashtriya kii sthiti di gai thi.
 barish ne sabhi teen maichon ko prabhavit kiya. pehle do phiksture donon taraf chah over kam ho gaye the. tisare or antim match main, khel ke keval 16.4 over sambhav tha, jiske parinamswaroop khel khatm nahin hua. nepal or netherland ne shrinkhalaa saajha kii.
