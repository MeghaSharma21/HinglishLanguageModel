 mikeno parvat kaa shikhar
 virunga pahadiyaan, jo mufumbiro bhi kehalaate hai, purvi africa main ruanda, congou loktantrik ganraajya or uganda kii simaaon par sthit ek jwalamukhiyon kii shrunkhlaa he. yah adward jhil or kiwu jhil ke bich main khadi hui hai. inamen se lagbhag sabhi prasupt hai sivaay 3,462 meter unche nyiragongo parvat or 3,063 meter unche nyamuragira parvat ke jo sakriya hai. yah donon sakriya jwalamukhi congou loktantrik ganraajya main hai or inke 2006 or 2010 main visphot hue the. virunga pahadiyaan parvatiy gorila kaa ghar hai jahaan yah praani vilupti ke khatare main he, jis vajah se yah ek mahatvapurn sangrakshan kshetra he.
