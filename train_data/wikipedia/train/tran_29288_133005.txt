 orenburg, rus kaa ek oblast he. soviet sangh ke jamaane main, 1938 se 1957 tak, iskaa naam badalakar chkalov oblast rakhaa gaya tha.
 orenburg oblast kii dakshini seemayen kazakhstan se lagati hai. ural nadi is oblast kii pramukh jaldhara he.
 2010 kii janaganana ke hisaab se orenburg oblast ke samudaay is prakaar the: roosi, tartar, kaazaakh, ukraini, bashqir, mordavini, german, chuwash, azeri or belarusi .
