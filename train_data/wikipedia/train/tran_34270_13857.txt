 saarc fountain, disambar 1985 main bangladesh, bharat, pakistan, srilanka, nepal, bhutan or maldive rajyon ke pramukh dwaara sthapit kiya gaya tha. haalanki yah dhaka shahar main pantha path par sthit he. ek saarc shikhar sammelan kii purv sandhya par bangladesh sarkaar ne is smarak kii sthapana kii thi. bangladeshi kalakaar nitu kundu ne ise dizine kiya or banaaya. yah keval steel se bana he.
