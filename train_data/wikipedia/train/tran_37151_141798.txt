 laghuparashari sanskrit shlokon main rachit laghu granth he. ise 'jatakachandrika' bhi kehete hai. yah vinshottaree dasha paddati kaa mahatvapurn granth he tathaa vrihad parashar horashastra par aadhaarit he. iske rachanaakaar ke bare main thik-thik pataa nahin he kintu mana jaataa he ki parashar ke anuyaayiyon ne isaki rachana kii. 
 laghuparashari main 42 shloke hai jo paanch adhyyon main vibhakt hai.
