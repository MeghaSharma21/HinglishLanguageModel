 khaliph rashidoon : - rushd va hidaayat paye khaliph. sunni musalamaanon ke liye paigambar mohammad kii mrityu ke baad, san 632 se lakar san 661 ke madhya ke khaliph ko rashidoon yaa al khalif urr-rashidoon kehete hai. kaarykaal ke anusaar ye chaar khaliph hai. 
 khaliph, islam kaa rajnatik tathaa dharmik pradhan hota tha jisaki niyukti ek samiti dwaara yaa uske purv ke khaliph dwaara hoti thi. hasan ko san 661 main khaliph banaaya gaya tha or usee saal unki mrityu ho gai thi. kuch sunni musalmaan unhen bhi rashidun khaliph maanate hai.
 pehle chaar caliph jinhone mohammad kii maut ke baad shashan kiya unhe aksar khaliph rashidoon ke rup main ullikhit kiya jaataa hai rashidun yaa to ek parishad dwaara chune gaye yaa unke purvavarti kii ichha ke aadhaar par chune gaye the. uttaradhikaar ke kram main, rashidun the:
