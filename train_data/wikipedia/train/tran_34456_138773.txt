 aise balon ke samooh ko balyugma kehete hai jinka parinami bal shunya ho kintu unka balaghurna ashoonya ho. iske prabhaav main keval ghurni gati hi sambhav he or sthaan-parivartan nahin hota. arthath kisi pind par balyugma laga ho to dravyaman kendra kaa twaran nahin hota. 
 balyugma ke parinami aghurna ko balaghurna kehete hai. yah tork kisi bhi sandarbh bindu ke saapeksh niyat hota he. is drushti se yah 'tork' saadhaaran 'aghurna' se bhinn he.
