 saaran, bihar kaa ek prakhand . varsh 2002 main sonpur nagar panchayat gathit hua.
 saaran jile ke sonpur prakhand main 232,340 kii aabaadi he. 14% log shahari kshetron main rahete hai jabaki 86% gramin kshetron main rahete hai. vartmaan main jile main 20 saamudayik vikaas block hai. sonpur prakhand main 98 gaanv hai. sonpur anumandal main sonpur, parsa, gaarkha, dighavaara, dariyapur block shaamil hai. sonpur prakhand main 23 gram panchayat or 73 gaanv hai.
 2002 main digha-sonpur rail-sah-sadak pul ke nirmaan kii shuruyaat ke baad sonpur main real estate kii jameen kii keemten bad gayi. real estate companiyon jaise RAV global solution privet limited, tii.si.dablyoo. realty privet limited, kazari infratech privet limited bade hisse kaa adhigrahan kar rahi hai or chote bhukhndon main unako vibhajit karke bech rahi hai.
