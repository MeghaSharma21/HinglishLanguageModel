 china kii sabhyataa pracheenatam maanav sabhyataao me se ek jo kii 5 hajaar se vidyaman he evam jatil he. is raashtra kii sanskruthik vividhta iski kshetra ke anusaar vividh he. china me kai samudayo ke log rahte he jiname se 'haan' kii aabaadi sarvaadhik he. china me adhiktar log naastik he tathaa kuch budh ke anuyaayi he. mandarin yaha kii rashtri bhashaa he jo ki 'ming sultanate' kii he. puratathvik saakshyon ke aadhaar par china main maanav basaav lagbhag saadhe baees lakh saal purana he. china kii sabhyataa vishwa kii puraatanatam sabhyataaon main se ek he. yah un gine-chune sabhyataaon main ek he jinhonen pracheen kaal main apana swatantra lekhan paddati kaa vikaas kiya. anya sabhyataaon ke naam hai - pracheen bharat, mesopotamia kii sabhyataa, misr sabhyataa or maaya sabhyataa. chini lipi ab bhi china, japan ke saath-saath aanshik rup se korea tathaa vietnam main prayukt hoti he.
