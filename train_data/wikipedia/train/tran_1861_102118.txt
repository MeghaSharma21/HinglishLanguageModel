 vishwa vyapaar kendra srilanka kii rajdhani colombo main sthit desh kii sabase unchi imaarat or sabase unchi judwaan imaaraten hai. ye judwa imaaraten colombon main biraa jhil ke nikat sthit hai. 152 meter unchi in imaaraton main prayek main 40 tal hai or ye dakshin asia kii sarvaadhik unchi imaaraton main se ek hai. in imaaraton ko 12 oktober, 1997 main aam kaamkaaj ke liye khol diya gaya tha.
