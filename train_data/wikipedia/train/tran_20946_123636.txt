 yah madhya nayi dilli kaa ek mukhya maarg he.
 uttar main yah maarg krishna minan maarg, akbar maarg ke purv va pashchim bhaag va tees janvari maarg ke sangam / gol chakkar se aarambh hota he.
 dakshin main yah maarg safadarajang maarg, prithviraj maarg va shriaerobindo marg ke vilay tak jaataa he, lagbhag safadarajang kaa makbara tak.
 yah maarg aurangzeb maarg ko bhaag karata he, tughlaq ardhachandra par.
 yah maarg dekhne hetu yahaan clic karen.
