 tapanushithan yaa 'anilan' yaa aningling dhaatukarm or padaarth vigyaan main ek prakaar kaa ooshmaa upachaar he. iskaa upyog kisi vastu yaa padaarth main aavashyak gun laane ke liye kiya jaataa he. is prakriya main ek krantik taap se adhik taap tak vastu ko garam kiya jaataa he tatpashchaat kuch samay tak ek niyat taap par banaae rakhte hai or antatah niyantrit rup main use thanda kar diya jaataa he. tapanushithan kaa upyog tanyata badhaane, padaarth ko mulaayam banane, padaarth main kisi kaaran utpann pratibalon ko samaapt karne, padaarth kii sanrachana ko badalane aadi ke liye kiya jaataa he.
 tapanushithan kaa upyog nimnlikhit main se kisi ek yaa adhik lakshyon kii purti ke liye kiya jaataa he-
 tapanushilan kaa upyog mukhyatah ispaat or taanbe par kiya jaataa he.
