 joseph jesan naamakah momoa ek hawaii-america abhinayta, model tathaa produsar hai. unhen military science fiction televijan shrinkhalaa sturget atlantis main ronan dex kii, echbio fantasy televijan shrinkhalaa game of throns main khal drogo kii, or netflix shrinkhalaa frontier main deccan harp kii bhoomikaayen nibhaane ke liye janaa jaataa he.
 momoa ne 2011 kii talwaar or jaaduugari par aadhaarit film conan da barbian main mukhya bhoomika nibhaai. vartmaan main vah disi ekstended univers main equaman kii bhoomika nibhaa rahe hai. is bhoomika main vah 2016 kii superhiro film bitman banaam superman: don of justise main atithi bhoomika main dikhaai die, or uske baad 2017 kii film justise lig or 2018 main apni ekal film equaman main vah dikhaai die.
 road tu paloma ek nirdeshak, lekhak or nirmaataa ke rup main momoa kii pehali film thi. 11 july 2014 ko release hui is film main unhone mukhya bhoomika bhi nibhaai thi.
