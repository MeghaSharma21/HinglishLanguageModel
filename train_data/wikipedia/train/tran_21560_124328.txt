 dawanal yaa van kii aag us durghatana ko kehete hai jab kisi van ke ek bhaag main yaa puure van main hi aag lag jaati he or us van ke sabhi ped-paudhe, jeev-jantu ityadi jalane lagate hai. dawanal ke liye sthiti tab utpann hoti he jab vanaspatiyaan or mitti suukh jate hai or aadrata bhi bahut kam hoti he. aag prakritik kaarnon jaise akashiya bijli girane se yaa prakritik rup se van main utpann gharshan se lag sakati he yaa yah maanav nirmit bhi ho sakati jaise koi laaparvaahee se sookhe van main sulagati hui cigarette yaa maachis kii tili phank de. ek baar chingaari bhadakne ke baad aag hawa ke prabhaav main tezi se failati he, visheshkar tab jabaki aag sookhe jhandphus ko pakad leti he. 
 dawanal anya agniyon se bhinn hota he, jiske kaaran hai iskaa vishaal aakaar, iske udgam sthaan se aage failane kii gati evam isaki disha badalane va rikt sthanon jaise sadakon, nadiyon aadi se aage bad jane kii kshamta.
 dawanal se nibatane hetu agnishman main ek vishesh paathyakram hota he or jo agnishman karmi isamen daksh hoten he unhen vaahanon, vayuyanon, helicopteron ityadi main agnishman ke liye rakhaa jaataa he.
 dawanal kii samasya mukhyatah greeshm ritu main hoti he or vishwa ke bahut se bhaagon main yah samasya bahut aam he jaise europe main 2007 kaa unani dawanal, australia kaa 2009 kaa dawanal, america ke california kaa dawanal or bharat main bhi uttari rajyon main yah samasya hai.
