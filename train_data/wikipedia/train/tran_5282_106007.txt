 rabad ke vriksh bumadhya rekhiy sadabahaar wanon main paae jate hai, iske doodh, jise letex kehete hai se rabad taiyaar kiya jaataa hai. sabase pehle yah amejan basin main junglee rup main ugta tha, vahiin se yah england nivaasiyon dwaara dakshini-purvi asia main le jaaya gaya. pehle iskaa prayog pencil ke nishaan mitaane ke liye kiya jaataa tha. aaj yah vishwa kii mahatvapurn vyavsaayik fasalon main se he. iskaa prayog motor ke tub, tair, water pruf kapade, juute tathaa vibhinn prakaar ke dainik upyog kii vastuon main hota he.thailand, indonesia, malaysia, bharat, china tathaa srilanka pramukh utpaadak desh he. bharat kaa vishwa utpaadan main chauthaa sthaan he parantu gharelu khapat adhik hone ke kaaran yah rabar kaa aayaat karata he.
 rabar kaa aadimasthaan america he. america kii ek aadi jati 'maaya' thi, jisamen rabar ke gend prachlit the. columbus ne san 1493 i. main vahaan ke adivaasiyon ko rabar ke bane gedon se khelte dekha tha. aisa maaloom hota he ki dakshin purv asia ke adivasee bhi rabar se parichit the or usase tokariyaan, ghade or isi prakaar kii vyavhaar kii anya chijen taiyaar karte the. dhire-dhire rabar kaa prachaar saare sansar main ho gaya or aaj rabar aadhunik sabhyataa kaa ek mahatvapurn pratik mana jaataa he. rabar ke bane saamaanon kii sankhya or upyogita aaj itani bad gai he ki uske abhaav main kaam chalaanaa asambhav samajha jaataa he. rabar kaa upyog shanti or yudhkaal main, gharelu or audyogic cormon main samaan rup se hota he. sansar ke samast rabar ke utpaadan kaa praaia: 78 pratishat gadiyon ke tiron or tubon ke banane main tathaa shesh juuton ke tale or ediyaan, bijli ke taar, khilone, barsaati kapade, chaadaren, khel ke saamaan, botalon or baraf ke thilon, sarajri ke saamaan ityadi, hajaaron chizon ke banane main lagata he. ab to rabar kii sadake bhi banane lagi hai, jo paryaapt tikaoo siddh hui he. rabar kaa vyavsaay aaj dinondin bad rahaa he.
