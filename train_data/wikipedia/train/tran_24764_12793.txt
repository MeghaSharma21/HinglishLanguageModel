 rashtriya shram ayog bharat kii ek sanvidhik sanstha he jo shram kanoonon main sanshodhan prastaavit karti he.
 pratham rashtriya shram ayog kii sthapana bharat sarkaar ne disambar, 1966 main kiya. shri pi0ve0 gajendradkar kii adhyakshata main desh ke maalikon tathaa shramikon ke madhya achche sambandhon ko protsaahan dene kii disha main is ayog ko sthapit kiya gaya. isamen vishesh vishyon tathaa kuch mahatvapurn udyogon kii shram sambandhi samasyaon ke adhyayan ke liye 37 adhyayan dal va samitiyaan niyukt kii thi. ayog ne apni rirpot agast, 1969 main sarkaar ko di.
 pratham rashtriya shram ayog kii pramukh siforishein nimna prakaar hai -
 dwitiya rashtriya shram ayeg bharat sarkaar ne 15 oktober, 1999 ko gathit kiya. shri rabindra verma ko iskaa adhyaksh niyukt kiya gaya.
