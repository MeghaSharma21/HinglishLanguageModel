 dow rajendra kumari bajpeyi bharat ke purv kendriya mantri or pandicherry ke uparajyapal rah chuki hai. ve teen baar kramash: 1980, 1984 or 1989 main sitapur se loksabhaa ke liye nirvaachit hui. ve bhartiya rashtriya congress kii ek samarpit kaaryakartri or purv pradhan mantri indira gandhi kii vahad kareebi maani jaati thi. 
 unka janm 8 farvari 1925 ko bihar ke bhagalpur jile ke laluchak main pandit es ke mishra ke yahan hua tha. ve ravishankar shukla kii nathin thi or shyama charan shukla or vidya charan shukla kii bhanji. vidyalayan shiksha ke pashchaat unhone elahabaad vishvavidhyalay se snatakottar kii shiksha tathaa pi ech di kii.
 unki shaadi peshey se shikshak or 1942 main bharat chodo andolan main bhaag lene vaale di.en. bajpeyi se 1947 main hui. jisase unhen ek putr ashok bajpeyi or ek suputree dow ranjana bajpeyi hui.
 ve 1962 se 77 tak uttar pradesh kii vidhaan sabha kii sadasya raheen. is dauraan ve uttar pradesh congress cameti kii adhyaksha bhi rahi. ve purv pradhan mantri indira gandhi kii behad kareebi maani jaati rahi hai. 1970 se 1977 ke dauraan ve uttar pradesh main kai mantralayon kaa netritv kiya. iske baad ve 1980 main sitapur se loksabhaa ke liye nirvaachit hui or yaheen se unhone lagaataar 1984 or 1989 main bhi loksabhaa ke liye chuni gayi. ve 1984 se 1986 tak ve kendriya saamaajik kalyaan mantralay main swatantra prabhaar ke saath rajya mantri raheen. 1986 se 1987 tak shram mantralay main swatantra prabhaar ke saath rajya mantri raheen or 1987 se 1989 tak kalyaan mantralay main swatantra prabhaar ke saath rajya mantri raheen. uske baad ve 2 may 1995 se 22 april 1998 tak pandicherry ke leftinent governor ke rup main kaary kiya.
 ve lambey samay tak gurde sambandhi beemaari se grast rahi. 17 july 1999 ko elahabaad main unki mrityu ho gayi. unki mrityu ke samay unki suputree dow ranjana bajpeyi uttar pradesh mahila congress kii adhyaksha thi or putr ashoka bajpeyi, jila congress cameti ke adhyaksh.
