 nepal ke dakshini bhaag ke maidani kshetra ko madhes kehete hai or yahan nivas karne vaale nepali logon ko madhesi kehete hai. is kshetra ko 'tarai kshetra' bhi kehete hai or tarai main vaas karne vaale in nepali logon ko taraibasi bhi kehete hai. madhesh shabd 'madhyadesh' kaa apbhransh he.
 maithili, tharu, avadhi, bhojpuri or anya bhashaayen bolne vaale log jo bihar or uttar pradesh ke logon jaise dikhte hai or jinaki samskruti or reeti-rivaaj bihar or uttar pradesh ke logo jaisi he lekin jo nepali hai, wo log madhesi kehalaate hai. madhesi mul ke nepali or bharat ke bihari yaa uttar pradesh ke uttari hisse main rahane vaale logon main kuch bhi asamanta nahin he, antar keval yah he ki madhesi nepali hen jo seemaa ke us paar rahete hai.
