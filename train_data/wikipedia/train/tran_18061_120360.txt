 bah bah, agra, uttar pradesh sthit ek gaanv he. bah agra jile ke purv main antim tehsil he, is tehsil ke kinare se chambal nadi guzarati he. chambal or yamuna kaa doaba hone ke kaaran is bhu-bhaag me kheti kii jameen kam se kam he, pani kaa jamini label pachaas meter neeche hone ke kaaran yahan pine ke pani kii bhi killat hoti he. bhadauria rajput samudaay adhik hone ke kaaran is doabe main adhiktar berogari kii maar yuvaaon ko jhelni padati he or khun garm hone ke kaaran yuva varg jaraa si baat par marane maarane ke liye utaaru ho jaataa he. adhiktar yuva apane prayaason ke kaaran sena main bhartee ho jate hai or unke parivaar ke vyaktiyon kaa jivan aasaani se chal uthta he. ek taraf chambal or duusari taraf yamuna nadi hone ke kaaran bihad jinhen nirjan jangal ke rup me janaa jaataa he, bahut lambe bhu-bhaag main faile hai. shiksha kii kami ke chalate 70 pratishat log bihad ke andar apana jivan bitaana jaanate he, sookhaa ilaaka hone ke kaaran yahan par bajra or sarson ke alaava chanaa alsi aadi k fasalen bha paidaa ho jaati hai, rajya sarkaar ne chambal ko abhaarany ghoshit karne ke baad, is bhu-bhaag ke nivaasiyon ne baahar ke rajyon rajasthan dilli bengal punjab aadi main palaayan shuru kar diya he, jo log rah gayi he, uname das pratishat log apane ko jangalo me vanavaasi rup me vaasit kar rahe hai. purv pradhan mantri shri atal bihari bajpeyi isi bhu-bhaag main bateshwar naamak sthaan ke he.
