 abhishek sharma ek bhartiya cricket khilaadi he. inhone 25 farvari 2017 ko 2016-17 vijay hajaare trophy main punjab ke liye apane list e cricket kii shuruyaat kii. inhone 6 oktober 2017 ko ranjai trophy 2017-18 main punjab ke liye apana pehla pratham shrenee cricket match khelaa tha. disambar 2017 main, unhen newjiland main yojit 2018 isisi under-19 cricket vishwa cap ke liye bharat kii team main shaamil kiya gaya tha. inhone apane ipel ke pehle hi match main royal chalangers bangalore ke khilaaf 46 ranon kii naabad pari kheli thi.
