 hindi niabandh lekhanavaad-vivadvichaar goshteekavy goshtishruti lekhanhindi tankan pratiyogitakavi sammelanpuraskaar samaaroh
 rajbhasha saptaah yaa hindi saptaah ko hindi diwas ke aas paas ke dinon main hindi bhashaa ke vikaas ke liye banaaya jaataa he. isamen mukhyatah sarkari karyaalay main ise manaaya jaataa he. iske alaava yah vidhyalay aadi main bhi kai tarah ke pratiyogita ke rup main manaaya jaataa he.
 iskaa mukhya uddeshy hindi diwas ke prachaar ke saath hi karyaalay or vidhyalay main hindi bhashaa ke mahatv ko samajhaana or use badhaava dena he.
