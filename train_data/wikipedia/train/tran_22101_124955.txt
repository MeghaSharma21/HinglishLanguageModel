 sarra rog merudand vaale praaniyon ko lagane vaala rog he. yadi iskaa ilaaj nahin kiya gaya to pashu mar saktaa he. yah rog tripanso-evensai naamak paraseete ke kaaran hota he. yah protojova pashu ke rakt main pravesh kar jaataa he jisase fever, kamajori, susti, vajan kam hona or khun kii kami ho jaati he. kuch pashu chakkar kaatane lagate hai. is beemaari ke chalate pashuon main tej bukhaar, thartharaahat, aankh se dikhnaa band ho jaataa he. pashu daant kitkitata he. jaldi jaldi peshaab karata he. pet phool jaataa he or gir kar mar jaataa he.
