 farsi-arabi lipi yaa sirf farsi lipi arabi lipi par aadhaarit ek lipi he jiskaa prayog farsi, urdu, sindhi, punjabi or anya bhashaaon ko likhane ke liye kiya jaataa he. iskaa izaad mukhya rup se isliye hua kyonki farsi main kuch dhvaniyaan hai jo arabi bhashaa main nahin hai isliye unhen darshane ke liye arabi lipi main kuch nae aksharon ko jodna padaa.
 arabi bhashaa main 'pa', 'c', 'ga' or 'ji' kii dhvaniyaan nahin milteen jo farsi main maujuud hai. inamen 'ji' ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah 'jha', 'ja' or 'za' teenon se alag he . inke liye binduon or chote chinhon ke zariye chaar nae akshar jode gaye - پ‎, چ‎, گ‎ or ژ‎ .
 in farsi dhvaniyon ke alaava urdu-hindi main bhi kuch atirikt dhvaniyaan thi jinhen darshane kii zaroorat thi - 't', 'da', 'd'. inke liye ٹ‎, ڈ‎ or ڑ‎ aksharon kaa izaad kiya gaya.
 pashto main 'sha' kii dhwani hoti he jo in anya bhashaaon main nahin milti. dhyaan dijiye ki pashto main 'sha' kaa uccharan vaisaa hota he jaisaa mul sanskrit main hota tha or yah 'sha' se bhinn he. iske liye ښ‎ akshar lipi main joda gaya.
