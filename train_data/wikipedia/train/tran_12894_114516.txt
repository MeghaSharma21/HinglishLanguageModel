 sulabh kulkarni bhartiya vigyaan shiksha evam anusandhan santhaan, pune, bharat main bhautik vigyaan kii profesar hai. wo nanopraudyogiki, padaarth vigyaan or prishth vigyaan ke kshetra main shodh kaary karti hai.
 sulabh kulkarni ne bi es si, em es si or pi ech di pune university se kiya tha. 1976 main pi ech di karne ke baad vah or research karne ke iraade se vah munic, germany main sarfes technolji par khoj kar chuki hai.
 sulabh kulkarni 2009 tak pune university main fizix kii professor thim. vah vanasthali vishvavidhyalay, rajasthan kii pro vischanslar bhi rah chuki he.
