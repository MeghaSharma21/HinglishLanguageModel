 adhivarsh, har chaar varsh baad aane vaala varsh he jisamen saal main 366 din hote hai.
 darasal prithvi surya kaa chakkar lagane main 365 din or kareeb 6 ghante lagaati he. aisa hone se har chaar saal main ek din adhik ho jaataa he, atah prayek chaar saal baad farvari maah main ek din atirikt jod santulan banaaye rakhane kii koshish kii jaati he.
 adhivarsh sankhya 4 se bhajya hote he, 2004 adhivarsh tha. magar '00' se ant hone vaale varsh, adhivarsh nahin hote, 2000 adhivarsh tha. 2000, 400 se bhajya he
 isi prakaar 1900, 1800, 1700 adhivarsh nahin he jabaki 2000, 1600, 1200 adhivarsh tha.
