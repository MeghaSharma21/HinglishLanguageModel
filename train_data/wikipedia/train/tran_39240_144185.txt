 shambhunath shukla, bharat ke ek rajaneta, swatantratasangram senaanee tathaa vindhyas pradesh ke pratham mukhyamantri the. ve bhartiya samvidhan sabha ke bhi sadsy the.
 unka janm 18 disambar 1903 ko hua tha. shiksha elahabaad main hui. prayag vishwadyilay elahabaad se 1926 main bie or san 1928 main elelbi kii degree hasil kar lee. elelbi main bhi gold medal mila tha. 1930 main munsif magistrate budhaar main vakaalat shuru kii thi. ve mahatma gandhi ke 1920 main asahyog andolan main hissaa lekar jail bhi pahunche the. 1945 main reva maharaja ke kanoonee salaahkaar niyukt hue. 1952 main vindhyapradesh vidhaan sabha amarpur se sadsy nirvaachit hue or vindhapradesh ke nirvirodh mukhaymantri bane.
