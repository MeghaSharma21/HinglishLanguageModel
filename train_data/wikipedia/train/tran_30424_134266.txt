 ardhendu bhushan bardhan athwa abi bardhan bharat ke sabase puraane raajnneetik dalon main se ek bhartiya communist party ke bhootpurv mahasachiv the. wo ol india difens imploiz association ke upaadhyaksh, maharashtra state electricity federation or ol india electric imploiz federation ke adhyaksh rahe the.
 e bi bardhan kaa janm bengal ke barishaal main hua tha jo ab bangladesh main he. jab vah 15 varsh ke the tab ve nagpur gayi jahaan ve samyawad ke anuyaayi ban gayi. 1940 main nagpur vishvavidhyalay main ve aal india studants federation ke sadsy bane. usee varsh ve bhartiya communist party ke sadsy bane jo us samay pratibandhit thi. ve nagpur vishvavidhyalay chaatrasangh ke adhyaksh rahe. unhone vidhi main snaatak tathaa arthshaastra main parasnatak kii oopaadhi hasil kii thi.
 unhone nagpur se anek chunaavon main bhaag liya kintu keval 1957 ke maharashtra rajya vidhaan sabha ke chunaav main hi unhen safalataa mili. ve 1967 or 1980 ke loksabhaa chunaavon main nagpur se haar gayi. 
 1990 ke baad ve kendra kii raajnneeti main aa gayi. 1994 main ve aal india trade union congress ke adhyaksh bane. 1995 main ve bhartiya communist party ke up mahasachiv bane. 1996 main indrajit gupt ke pad chhodane ke baad baad ve bhartiya communist party ke mahausachiv bane.
