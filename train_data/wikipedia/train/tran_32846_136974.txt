 baldev prasad mishra hindi sahityakar, nyayavid tathaa locasevak the. ve bharat ke aise pratham shodhakarta the jinhone angreji shasankaal main angreji ke badale hindi main apana shodh prabandh prastut kar di lit kii oopaadhi arjeet kii . unhone tulsi darshan par vishesh kaary kiya tha. lagbhag 85 prakashit athwa aprakashit kritiyaan unke naam hai.
 shri baldevprasad mishra kaa 12 sintambar san 1898 main rajanandgaanv main hua tha. ve shri shivaratan mishra ke pautra or shri narayanprasad mishra ke putr the. ve bachapan se hi ve pratibhashali the. unki ruchi sahitya main shuru se hi thi. bi. e. el. el-bi. tathaa em. e. darshanashastra kii pariksha maaris kalege nagpur se paas karne ke baad unhone vakaalat shuru kii magar usamen ve safal nahin huye or san 1923 main raja chakradhrasingh ke bulaave par raigarh aa gayi. yahaan ve 17 varsho tak nyaayadhish, naayab dewan or dewan rahe or anek mahatvapurn kaary kiye jiske liye ve hameshaa yaad kiye jaayenge. 11-12 varsh kii aayu main unhone pehali kavita likhi.
 shri baldev prasad mishra ne 100 se adhik granthon kaa pranayan kiya he. jinamein jeev vigyaan, kaushal kishor, raamarajy, saket-sant, tulsi darshan, bhartiya samskruti, maanas main ramakatha, mansamadhuri, jivan sangeet, udaatt sangeet pramukh hai.
