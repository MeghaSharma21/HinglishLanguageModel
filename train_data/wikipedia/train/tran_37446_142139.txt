 shivneri durg yaa shivneri kila, bharat ke maharashtra rajya ke pune ke junnar gaanv ke paas sthit ek aitihaasik kila he. shivneri chhatrapati shivaji kaa janmasthan bhi he. shivaji ke pita, shahji bijapur ke sultan adil shah kii sena main ek senapati the. lagaataar ho rahe yuddh ke kaaran shahji, apni garbhvatee patni jijabai kii suraksha ko lekar chintit the, is liye unhone apane parivaar ko shivneri main bhej diya. shivneri chaaron ore se khadi chattaanon se ghira ek abhedya garh tha. 
 shivaji kaa janm 19 farvari 1630 ko hua tha or unka bachapan bhi yaheen beetaa. is garh ke bheetar mata shivai kaa ek mandir tha, jinke naam par shivaji kaa naam rakhaa gaya. kile ke madhya main ek sarovar sthit he jise "badami talab" kehete hai. isi sarovar ke dakshin main mata jijabai or bal shivaji kii murtiyon sthit hai. kile main mithe pani ke do strot hai jinhen ganga-jamuna kehete hai or inase varsh bhar pani kii aapurti chaalu rahati he. 
 kile se do kilometre kii duuri par lenyadri gufaaen sthit hai jahaan ashtavinayak kaa mandir bana he.
