 compozity yaa asteraciye phuulavaale paudhon kaa ek kul he. is kul ko hindi main sangrathit kul kah sakate hai. is kul main anya kulon kii apeksha bahut adhik paudhe hai or ye vishwavyaapee bhi hai. 
 is kul main 12 upkul, lagbhag 1620 prajatiyaan or 23,000 spesij hai. prayek phool vastutah kai pushpon kaa gucchh hota he. saadhaaran gendaa naamak phool kaa paudha isi kul main he. is kul ke paudhon main badi bhinnata hoti he. adhikansh paudhe shaak ke samaan hai. sansar ke ushn bhaagon main bhi is kul ke anek jhaadiyaan or vriksh paae jate hai. kul paudhe arohi hote hai. pattiyaan bahudhaa gucchhon main hoti hai. jin paudhon main tane lambey hote hai, unamem pattiyaan saadharantah ekantar hoti hai. jad bahudhaa moti hoti he or kabhi-kabhi usamen kand hota he, jaise dalaya main. kuch paudhon ke tanon main doodh ke sadrush ras rahata he. jaisaa pehle bataaya gaya he, phool shirshon main ekatra rahete hai. ye chaaron ore hare nipatron se ghire rahete hai. jab phool kalikavastha main rahata he to inhen se uski raksha hoti he. ye hi bahyadal-punj kaa kaam dete hai. ye phool ke sheersh paragan ke liye atyuttam rup se vyavasthit hote hai. phulon ke ek saath ekatra rahane ke kaaran kisi ek keet ke aa jane se anek kaa paragan ho jaataa he. vartika kii jad par makrand nikaalata he or dalpunj nalika ke kaaran varsha se athwa os se bahane nahin paata. chote hont ke keet bhi is makrand ko praapt nahin kar sakate, kyonki dalpunj nalika lambii hoti he.
 phool kaa jeevanetihaas do bhaagon main vibhakt kiya ja saktaa he. aarambh main to phool nar kaa kaam karte hai or ant main nari kaa. is prakaar in phulon main saadhaarant: parparagan hota he, swayamparagan nahin. parantu kuch phulon main ek teesri avasthaa bhi hoti he, jisamen vartikagra peeche mud jaataa he or bache khuche paraganon ko, jo neeche kii vartika par pade rahete hai, chhu detaa he. yadi parparagan nahin hua rahata to is prakaar swayamparagan ho jaataa he.
 falon ke vitaran kii vidhiyaan bhi anek hoti hai. kuch falon main beej main roen lage rahete hai, jisase ve dur-dur tak ud jate hai. kuch main kaante hote hai, jinase ve pashuon kii khaal main chipakkar anyatra pahunch jate hai. kabhi-kabhi beej apane sthaan par hi pade rahete hai or paudhe ko jhatka lagane par idhar-udhar bikhar jate hai.
 is parivaar ke kuch sadsy aarthik laabh ke hai, jaise lacteuca sativa, chikri, hathi chok . bahut se sadsy apane sundar phool ke kaaran udyaan main ugaae jate hai, jaise zinnia, surajamukhi, gendaa, daalia ityadi. kuch aushadhi ke bhi kaam main aate hai. artimizia valgeris se 'santonin' dawa banati he. paithrom se keet maarane kaa churna banaaya jaataa he. yah pushp prasiddh guldaudi kii prajati kaa he. parthenium kii ek jati se ek prakaar kaa rabar hota he.
