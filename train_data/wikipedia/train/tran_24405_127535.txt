 sar osborn arkel smith 1 april 1935 se 30 june 1937 tak bhartiya reserve bank ke pehle governor the. 
 smith ek peshewar banker the or unhone australia ke kaamanwealth bank main 20 saal evam new south wales bank main 10 saal kii seva kii. 1926 main ve imperial bank of india ke prabandhak governor bane. 1 april 1935 ko unhen bhartiya reserve bank kaa governor niyukt kiya gaya. apane kaarykaal main unhone kisi bank note par hastakshar nahi kiye.
