 stronsium sulphate, jiskaa rasinic sutara SrSO4 he, stronsium tatv kaa sulphate lavan he. yah ek safed rang kaa crystalia powder hota he or prakruti main selestin naamak khanij main paaya jaataa he. yah pani main aasaani se nahin ghulata lekin hydrochloric amla yaa nitric amla ke kamazor gholon main ghul jaataa he. jeev-jagat main paaya gaya he ki acanthareyaa naamak protozoa kaa dhaancha stronsium sulphate kaa hi bana hua hota he.
