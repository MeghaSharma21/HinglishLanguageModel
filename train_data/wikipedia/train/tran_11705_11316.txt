 varali adivasee bharat ke maharashtra rajya ke kuch bhaagon main paye jate hai. varali ek swadeshi janajati yaa adivasee hai. ye maharashtra or gujarat ke seemaa ke tateey kshetron or aasapaas ke k pahari main rahete hai. varali jan jati kii apni khud ki hindu manyataon, paramparaon, samskruti - sankraman hai jinako unhone apanaaya he. yah log varali naam kii ek alikhit bhashaa main baat karte hai jo kii dakshini kshetra ke bhartiya-arya bhashaaon main aati he.
 varali apni varali chitrakala ke liye bhi prasidh he. varali chitrakala ek pracheen bhartiya kala he jo kii maharashtra kii ek janajati varali dwaara banaai jaati he.
