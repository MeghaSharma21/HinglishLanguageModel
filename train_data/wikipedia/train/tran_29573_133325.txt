 hanuman sethu mandir, lakhanu main gomti nadi ke kinare ek hanuman mandir he. yah mandir nadi par bane ek pul ke kinare bana he. is kaaran yah pul hanuman sethu evam mandir hanuman sethu mandir kehalaata he. yah mandir neem karori baba ne banwaaya he. is mandir se laga baba kaa bhi ek mandir bana he.
 yah sethu lakhanu vishvavidhyalay se hazaratganj ko jodta he. pehle is sthaan par manki brij hua karata tha, jo 1972 main ai gomti nadi kii baadh main bah gaya tha. us hi sthaan par nayaa sethu bana he. sethu ke kinare hi lakhanu vishvavidhyalay kaa union bhawan evam kendriya aushadhi anusandhan santhaan kii awasaiya colony he. duusari ore sethu se utarate hi parivartan chowk he, jiske daayin ore hotaal clarks avadh evam chatar manzil he.
