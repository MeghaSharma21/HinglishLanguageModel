 afghanistan islamic ganraajya dakshini madhya asia main avasthit desh he, jo chaaro ore se jameen se ghira hua he. praayah isaki ginati madhya asia ke deshon main hoti he par desh main lagaataar chal rahe sangharshon ne ise kabhi madhya purv to kabhi dakshin asia se jod diya he. iske purv main pakistan, uttar purv main bharat tathaa china, uttar main tajikistan, kazakstan tathaa turkmenistan tathaa pashchim main iran he.
 afghanistan resham maarg or maanav pravaas kaa ek pracheen kendra bindu rahaa he. puratatvavidon ko madhya pashaan kaal ​​ke maanav basti ke sakshya mili hai. is kshetra main nagareey sabhyataa kii shuruyaat 3000 se 2,000 i.pus. ke rup main maani ja sakati he. yah kshetra ek aise bhu-ranneetik sthaan par avasthit he jo madhya asia or pashchim asia ko bhartiya upmahadweep kii samskruti se jodta he. is bhoomi par kushaan, haftholit, samaani, gaznavi, mohamad gauri, mughal, durrani or anek duusare pramukh samrajyon kaa utthan hua he. pracheen kaal main faras tathaa shaq samrajyon kaa ang rahaa afghanistan kai samraaton, akramanakariyon tathaa vijetaon kii karmbhumi rahaa he. inamen sikandar, farsi shaasha daara pratham, turk,mughal shaasha babar, mohammad gauri, nadir shah ityadi ke naam pramukh hai. british senaaon ne bhi kai baar afghanistan par akraman kiya. vartmaan main america dwaara taliban par akraman kiye jane ke baad nato kii senaayen vahaan bani hui hai.
 afghanistan ke pramukh nagar hai- rajdhani kabul, qandahar. yahan kai nasl ke log rahete hai jinamein pashtun sabase adhik hai. iske alaava uzbek, tajik, turkmen or hazaaraa shaamil hai. yahan kii mukhya bhashaa pashto he. farsi bhashaa ke afagaan rup ko dari kehete hai.
 afghanistan kaa naam afgaan or stan se milakar bana he jiskaa shabdic arth he afagaanon kii bhoomi. stan is kshetra ke kai deshon ke naam main he jaise- pakistan, turkmenistan, kazakhstan, hindustan ityadi jiskaa arth he bhoomi yaa desh. afgaan kaa arth yahaan ke sabase adhik vasit nasl ko kehete he. afgaan shabd ko sanskrit avgaan se niklaa hua mana jaataa he. dhyaan rahe kii "afkhaan" shabd main ga kii dhwani he or "ga" kii nahin.
 maanav basaahat 10,000 saal se bhi adhik purana ho saktaa he. isa ke 1800 saal pehle aaryon kaa aagaman is kshetra main hua. isa ke 700 saal pehle iske uttari kshetra main gandhar mahajanapad tha jiske bare main bhartiya strot mahabharat tathaa anya granthon main varnan miltaa he. isapurv 500 main faras ke hakhaamani shaskaon ne isko jeet liya. sikandar ke faras vijay abhiyaan ke tahate afghanistan bhi unani samrajya kaa ang ban gaya. iske baad yah shakon ke shashan main aae. shaq skithiyon ke bhartiya ang the. isapurv 230 main maury shashan ke tahat afghanistan kaa sampurna ilaaka aa chukaa tha par mauryon kaa shashan adhik dinon tak nahin rahaa. iske baad parthian or fir saasaani shaskaon ne faras main kendrit apane samrajyon kaa hissaa ise bana liya. saasni vansh islam ke aagaman se purv kaa aakhri irani vansh tha. arabon ne kuraasaan par san 707 main adhikaar kar liya. samaani vansh, jo farsi mul ke par sunni the, ne 987 isvi main apana shashan gajanaviyon ko kho diya jiske falswaroop lagbhag sampurna afghanistan gazanaviyon ke haathon aa gaya. gor ke shaskaon ne gazani par 1183 main adhikaar kar liya.
 madhyakaal main kai afgaan shaskaon ne dilli kii satta par adhikaar kiya yaa karne kaa praytn kiya jinamein lodi vansh kaa naam pramukh he. iske alaava bhi kai muslim akramanakariyon ne afgaan shahon kii madad se hindustan par akraman kiya tha jisamen babar, nadir shah tathaa ahamed shah abdali shaamil he. afghanistan ke kuch kshetra dilli sultanate ke ang the.
 unnisvin sadi main angl-afakhaan yudhon ke kaaran afghanistan kaa kaaphi hissaa british india ke adheen ho gaya jiske baad afghanistan main europea prabhaav badhata gaya. 1919 main afghanistan ne videshi takaton se ek baar phir swatantraaa pai. aadhunik kaal main 1933-1973 ke baach kaa kaal afghanistan kaa sabase adhik vyavasthit kaal rahaa jab zaahir shah kaa shashan tha. par pehle uske jija tathaa baad main communist party ke sattapalat ke kaaran desh main phir se asthirataa aa gai. soviet sena ne communist party ke sahyog ke liye desh main kadam rakhaa or mujahidin ne soviet senaaon ke khilaaf yuddh ched diya or baad main america tathaa pakistan ke sahyog se sovieton ko waapas janaa padaa. 11 sitambar 2001 ke hamale main mujahidin ke sahyog hone kii khabar ke baad america ne desh ke adhikansh hisse par sattarudh mujahidin, jisko kabhi america ne soviet senaaon ke khilaaf ladane main hathiyaaron se sahyog diya tha, ke khilaaf yuddh ched diya.
 afghanistan naam afgaan samudaay kii jagah ke rup main prayukt kiya gaya he, yah naam sabase pehle 10 veen shatabdi main hudood ul-alam naam kii bhugolic kitaab main aayaa tha iske rachanaakaar kaa naam agyaat he' saal 2006 main paarit desh ke samvidhan main afghanistan ke sabhi nagarikon ko afgaan kaha gaya he jo afghanistan ke sabhi naagarik afgaan he'
 vartmaan main desh main nato kii senaayen bani hai or desh main loktantric sarkaar kaa shashan he. haalanki taliban ne phir se kuch kshetron par adhipaty jamaa liya he, america kaa kahana he ki taliban ko pakistani jameen par phalne-phoolne diya ja rahaa he.

 afghanistan chaaron ore se zamin se ghira hua he or isaki sabase badi seemaa purv kii ore pakistan se lagi he. ise duurand rekha bhi kehete hai. kendriya tathaa uttarpurv kii disha main parvatamalas hai jo uttarpurv main tajikistan sthit hindookush parvaton kaa vistaar hai. aksar taapmaana kaa dainik antaran adhik hota he.
