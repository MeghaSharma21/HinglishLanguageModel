 sigiri, tamil : சிகிரியா ; sanskrit ke 'singhgiri' se vyutpann) srilanka ke kendriya maatale jile main sthit vishaal pashaan, pracheen shail-durg tathaa rajmahal kaa khandhar he. iske chaaro ore ghaney baag, jalashay tathaa anya bhawan hai. yah ek prasiddh paryaton sthal he. yah apane pracheen chitrakala ke liye bhi prakhyat he jo bharat kii ajanta kii gufaaon kii yaad dilaate hai. yah srilanka ke saat vishwa dharohal sthalon main se ek he. unesco ne ise 'vishwa kaa aathvaan aashcharya' ghoshit kiya he.
