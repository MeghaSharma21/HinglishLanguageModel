 shandur darra paak-adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-balatistan kshetra ke gizar zile main sthit ek pahari darra he jo gizar zile ko kaibar-pakhtuunakva praant ke chitral zile se jodta he. darre ke upree kshetra main 12,200 foot kii oonchaai par ek pathari ilaaka he jise shandur top ke naam se bulaayaa jaataa he. yahan shandur jhil sthit he jo paas kii himani se bharti he or gilgit nadi kaa strot he. 
 shandur darre ke dono taraf basane vaale sthaneey log khovaar bhashaa bolte hai.
 shandur top par 1936 se ek vaarshik polo pratiyogita yojit kii jaati rahi he. isamen gilgit or chitral kii timen muqaabala karti he or jis maidan main yah khelaa jaataa he vah vishwa kaa sabase unchaa polo kaa maidan he. pratiyogita ke saath-saath sthaneey samskruti darshane vaale naach-gane ke kaarykram bhi hote hai jo paryatakon main lokapriy hai.
