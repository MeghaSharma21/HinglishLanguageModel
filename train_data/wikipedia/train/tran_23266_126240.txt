 shahid yashodhar bandwal bharat ke ek uttrakhand aandolankaaree the. ve pruthak uttrakhand pradesh ke nirmaan hetu sangharshrat sanghathan uttrakhand kranti dal  ke sadsy the. aandolan ke dauraan police ke haathon unhen apni jaan ganvaani padi.
 yashodhar kaa janm uttrakhand ke rudraprayag zile ke benji naamak gaanv main hua tha. chaatr jivan se hi ve raajnneeti main sakriy the. baad main ve pruthak uttrakhand rajya ke nirmaan aandolan se jud gayi.
 srinagar shahar se 2 ki0me0 dur sthit shriyantra tapu par aandolankariyon ne 7 november, 1994 se pruthak uttrakhand rajya hetu aamaran anshan armbh kiya. 10 november, 1994 ko police ne is tapu main pahunchakar apana qahar barapaaya, jisamen kai logon ko gambheer chotein bhi ai, isi kram main police ne do yuvakon yashodhar bandwal tathaa rajesh ravat ko riflon ke bat or lathi-dandon se markar alakananda nadi main phenk diya or unke upar pattharon kii barsaat kar di, jisase in donon kii mrityu ho gai. in donon shaheedon ke shav 14 november, 1994 ko bagwan ke sameep alakananda main tairte huye paye gayi the.
 uttrakhand ke nirmaan ke pashchaat srinagar sthit hemavathi nandan bahuguna gadhwal vishvavidhyalay ke ek get kaa namakaran shahid yashodhar ke naam par kiya gaya.
