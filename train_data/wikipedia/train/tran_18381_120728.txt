 1967 ke chah din ke yuddh main isroyal ne is hisse par, vistrut seemaa ke saath, niyantran kar liya. is hisse main jerusalem kaa purana shahar or kuch yahudee, isai tathaa islam dharmon ke pavitra sthaan jaise temple mount, pashchimi diwaar, al-aksa masjid, dom of da rock tathaa pavitra kabr vaala carch he. 1980 main isroyal ne is par kabza kar liya. 
 philistini mukti sanghathan dwaara 1988 main philistin kii aazaadee kii ghoshnaa kii gayi or jerusalem ko rajdhani ghoshit kiya. iskaa adhyaksh yasir arafaat tha. haalanki isroyal ne kabhi philistini sarkaar ko purv jerusalem main daftar nahin sthapit karne diya.
