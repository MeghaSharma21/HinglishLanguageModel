 baraamula zila, jise kashmiri bhashaa main varmul kehete hai, bharat ke jammu or kashmir rajya kaa ek zila he. iskaa mukhyalay baraamula shahar he or gulmarg kaa prasiddh paryatak sthal bhi isi zile me sthit he.
 "baraamula" naam sanskrit ke "varahamul" se utpann hua he or aaj bhi kashmiri bhashaa main "varmul" kehalaata he. "varah" kaa arth boar hota he or "mul" kaa arth uskaa painaa daant he. manyata he ki kashmir ghati adikaal main ek satisar naamak jhil ke neeche dubi hui thi, jisamen jalodbhav naamak jal-rakshas rahakar bhay failaata tha. bhagwaan vishnu ne ek bhimkaay varah kaa rup dhaaran kar ke aadhunik baraamula ke paas jhil ko gharene vaale pahaado par apane lambe daant se prahaar kara. is se banane vaali daraar se jhil kaa pani bah gaya or kashmir vaadi ubhar ai or vaas-yogya ban gai.
