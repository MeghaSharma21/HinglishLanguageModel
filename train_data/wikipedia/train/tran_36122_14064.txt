 13 april 2017 ko sanyukt rajya america ne doosraa sabase bada gaiyr-parmaanu bam giraay jise jibiyu-43/ bi air blast ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he. america dwaara giraae gayi is bam kaa uddeshy purvi afghanistan ke nangahaar praant ke achinn jile main iesas dwaara upyog kiye jane vaale surang parisaro ko nasht karne ke liye giraaya gaya tha iesas ne is kshetra main 2015 kabja kiya tha jo kisi arab kshetra ke bahaar iesas kii pehali shakha thi.
 jibiyu -43 ek 9797 kigra bhaar, or jeepies- nirdeshit bam he, america ke shastraagaar main sabase shaktishaalee paaramparik bam he, pehle march 2003 main pareekshan kiya gaya, iraq yuddh kii shuruuaat se kuch din pehle upyog kiya gaya tha. ye bam bade aakaar kaa hone ke kaaran ise cabel bade cargo vimaan ke pichley hisse main hi dhokar le jaaya ja saktaa he or cargo vimaan ke pichley hisse se hi daaga ja saktaa he.
