 opsilan sagitteri jiske bair namakan main bhi yahi naam darj he, akaash main dhanu taramandal main sthit ek dwitara he. yah prithvi se dikhne vaale taron main se 35vaan sab se roshan tara he. yah hamase 144.64 prakaash-varsh kii duuri par sthit he or prithvi se iskaa ausat saapeksh kantimaan 1.79 he. iskaa ek bahut hi dhundhala saathi tara bhi he jise opsilan sagitteri bi bulaayaa jaataa he.
 bhartiya samskruti main opsilan sagitteri or dalta sagitteri taron ko ikaitha purvasadha ke naam se janaa jaataa he. opsilan sagitteri ko angrezi main "kos australis" bhi kaha jaataa he jiskaa arth "dakshini dhanush" he. yah arabi bhashaa ke "qus" or latini bhashaa ke "australis" kaa sanyukt shabd he.
 opsilan sagitteri kaa mukhya tara ek B9.5 III shrenee kaa daanav tara he. isaki andruni chamak hamaare suraj kii 375 guna he. iskaa vyas hamaare suraj ke vyas kaa 7 guna or iskaa dravyaman suraj ke dravyaman kaa 5 guna he.
