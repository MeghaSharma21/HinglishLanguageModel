 bacha khan vishvavidhyalay charasadda, kaibar pakhtuunakva pakistan main sthit ek jan vishvavidhyalay he. vishvavidhyalay kaa naam mahaan swatantraaa senaanee, bharat ratna se sammanit va shanti samarthak pashtun kaaryakarta bacha khan ke naam par rakhaa gaya tha. pakistan ko vishwa patal par sammanit raashtra kaa darza dilaane ke liye bacha khan kaa vaishvik bhaichaare va shanti kaa sandesh is vishvavidhyalay kaa dhyey he.
 20 janvari 2016 ko is vishvavidhyalay par taharik-e-taliban pakistan ke aatankwaadiyon ne aatanki hamalaa kar diya. isamen kam-se-kam 25 logon kii mrityu ho gayi.
