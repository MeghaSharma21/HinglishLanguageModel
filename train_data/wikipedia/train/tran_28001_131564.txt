 yogic aankh kiton or crusteshia jaise sandhipadon main drishy bodh kaa ek shaareerik ang hota he. isamen omatedia kahalaane vaali hazaron prakaash ke rang va chamak kaa bodh karne vaali alag ikaiyaan hoti hai, jinamein prayek main alag cornia, lens or prakaash samvedak koshikaaye hoti hai. kisi sandhipad dwaara dekhi jane vaali chhaviyaan in hazaron omatedia dwaara ekatrit jaankaree ko sammishrit kar ke banati hai. maanvon va anya praaniyon kii aankh kii tulana main yogic aankh kaa chhavi vibhedan kamazor hota he lekin uskaa drushtikon vistaar, tej hilavat ko bhaanpane kii kshamta or kabhi-kabhi prakaash main dhruvan bhaanpane kii kshamta adhik hoti he.
