 antarrashtriya pratibandh kisi desh, kshetra yaa sanghathan ke sambandh main kisi anya desh, sanghathan yaa mitrapaksh dwaara liye gayi aise rajnatik yaa aarthik nirnay hote hai jinke antargat unki kuch aarthik, vyapaarik, kootnithik, saanskrithik yaa anya gatividhiyon par roak laga di jaati he. aksar in pratibandhon kaa dhyey kisi suraksha, rajnatik yaa arthik lakshya kii prapti hota he. masalan aparthid kaal main dakshin africa par antarrashtriya khelon main hissaa lene par pratibandh laga hua tha, taaki vahaan ke naslwad sattadhari log swayan ko vishwa main akelaa mahasoos karen or unka manobal kamazor ho.
