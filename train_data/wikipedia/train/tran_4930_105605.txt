 sangemarmar yaa sirf maramar ek kayantarit shail he, jo ki chuunaa pathar ke kayantaran kaa parinaam he. yah adhiktar calsite kaa bana hota he, jo ki calcium carbonate kaa sphatikeey rup he. yah shilpakala ke liye nirmaan avyav hetu prayukt hota he. iskaa naam farsi se niklaa he, jiskaa arth he mulaayam pathar.
 sang-pathar, e-kaa, marmar-mulaayam = mulaayam pathar.
 iske naam par sthanon ke naam bhi hai, jaise: -
 --marble arch, landan; --si of marmar; --bharat kii sangemarmar kii chattaane, --marble minessota, --marble, colorado, evam --marble hill, manhaton, new york shahar aadi. 
 elgin marble ke sangemarmar shilp jo ki british sangrahalay main pradarshit hai. 
 sangemarmar ek kayantarit chattan he jiskaa nirmaan avsaadee carbonate chattaanon ke kshetreey or kabhi kabhi sampark kayantaran ke falswaroop hota he. yah avsaadee carbonate chattaane chuunaa pathar yaa dolostone, yaa phir purana sangemarmar ho sakati he. kayantaran kii is prakriya ke dauraan mul chattan kaa punarcristaleekaran hota he. avsaadee nikshepon se sangemarmar banane kii is prakriya main ucch taapmaana or dabaav ke chalate mul chattan me upasthit kisi bhi prakaar ke jeevashmik avshesh or chattan kii mul banaavat nasht ho jaati he.
