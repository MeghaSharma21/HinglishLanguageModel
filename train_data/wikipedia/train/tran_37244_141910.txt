 jatland, 31 may, 1916 ko hue british tathaa german nausenaon ke yuddh ke liye prasiddh he. german nivasee ise 'stogtech kaa yuddh' kehete hai. denmark ke samudratat se 115 kimi duuri par yah yuddh hua tha. pratham mahayuddh ke samay yah ekmaatr sangram rahaa, jisamen do nausenaayen aamane-saamne morche par ladiin. is yuddh ke baad germany ke aatmasamarpan tak british nausena, germany kii nausena par purnataya haaavee rahi.
