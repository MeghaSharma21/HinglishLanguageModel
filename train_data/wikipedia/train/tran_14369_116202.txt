 sarangarh ek prakaar se bahut bada shahar he' kyonki is sarangarh tehsil main sabase pehle raajaaon kaa shashan kaal tha or yahan bahut adhik sankhya main jaminen or shasnic shakti tha. mana gaya he ki yahan baba ghasidas ke gyaan bhoomi ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. tathaa is kshetra par sagon ped adhik maatra main the.
 jile main paryaavaran ke najar se raigarh se 52 kimi dur saarangarh ki kaali mata ke mandir,16 kimi dur cosir ke koshlai ,26 kimi dur chandpur ke chandarahasini tathaa timralaga ke nathaldai.
 jila centre se 52 kimi dur sthit hai, yah aja ke liye vidhaan sabha rigerv hai.
 sarangarh santnameyo ke garh hai tathaa samaaj ke pramukh log swa bhaiyaram khunte purv vidhayak, swa manohar das, gyaandas ji hai jinhone sant shiromani baba guru ghasidas ji gyaan sthali ko puure rajya main khyaati dilaai.
 abhyaarany- gomerda abhyaarany saarangarh main sthit he yahaan pramukh rup se van bhainsa paaya jaataa he sonkutta, barasinha, tendua, baraha, udan gilahari aadi bhi paae jate hai ghumne kaa upyukt samay sitambar se june tak he 200abhyaarany dharmik sthal - rashtriya rajmarg 153 se saller 1.5ki.me. kii duuri gaanv- bagindih sameep maan navadurga or maan vaishnavadevi kaa mandir,saath paas main hi ghoraghati baandh nirmaandhin he,ghoraghati main hi muni dongari ek darshneey sthal he.
