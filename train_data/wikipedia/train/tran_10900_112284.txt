 chembra shikhar/ parvat bharat ke kerala pradesh main pashchimi ghat ke antargatt vynad parvat kaa sagar sataha se 2,100 meter oonchaai vaala ek parvat shikhar he. chembra kalpetta 8 kimi dakshin main meppadi kasbe ke nikat sthit he. isse nikatavarti rajya tamil nadu kii nilgiri parvatamala evam kerala ke kosikod jile kii vellarimalay parvat shrunkhlaayein judati hui hai. chembra par meppadi se paidal chadhaai kaa rasta he. yahaan kii jila paryaton utthan parishad paryatakon ko maardarshak evam trekking upkaran evam aujaar kiraaye par uplabdh karaati he.chembra shikhar par trekking karne hetu meppadi sthit van vibhaag karyaalay se anumati lee ja sakati he. shikhar yatra ke raste main ek hridayakar jhil yahaan kaa mukhya aakarshan he. mana jaataa he ki ye jhil kabhi bhi sookhti nahin he or yah shikhar ke aadhe raste main padati he. meppadi kasbe se lagbhag 5 ki.me duuri par erumakolli ke chaay bagan dekhe ja sakate hai.
