 karvaa yaa karak mitti kaa lote yaa kalashnuma paatr hota he. iskaa prayog hindu dharm main karvaa chouth or ahoi ashtami naamak tyohaaron main kiya jaataa he. isse arghya diya jaataa he. is tarah ke paatr tambe, chaandi va pital ke bhi hote hai. is karak yaa karvaa paatr ko shri ganesh kaa swaroop maanate karak ke daan se sukh, saubhaahgya, achal lakshmi evam putr kii prapti hoti he, aisa shaastra sammat he. aisi bhi manyata va atoot vishwaas he ki karak daan se sab manorathon kii prapti hoti he.
