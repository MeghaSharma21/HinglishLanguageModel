 5 march san 1931 ko landan dwitiya gol mej sammelan ke purv mahatma gandhi or tatkaaleen visarai lard irvin ke bich ek rajnatik samjhauta hua jise gandhi-irvin samjhauta kehete hai. 
 british sarkaar pratham golmes sammelan se samaj gai ki bina congress ke sahyog ke koi faisla sambhav nahin he. vysarai lard irvin evam mahatma gandhi ke bich 5 march 1931 ko gandhi-irvin samjhauta sampann hua. is samjhaute main lard irvin ne sweekaar kiya ki - 
 
 congress kii ore se gandhiji ne nimna sharten sweekaar kii - 
 yah samjhauta isaliye mahatvapurn tha kyonki pehali baar british sarkaar ne bharatiyon ke saath samanta ke str par samjhauta kiya.
