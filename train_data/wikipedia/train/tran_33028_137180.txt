 yantriki main kisi lambe sanrachanaatmak avyav ke lambavat disha main lage bal ke kaaran us avyav ke rup main parivartan ko banken kaha jaataa he. banken ke kaaran sidhe avyav kuch mud jate hai or unka aakaar saralrekhy na hokar kuch alag ho jaataa he. praayah banken se sambandhit vishleshan main jin avyavon par vichaar kiya jaataa he unka koi ek vima anya do bimaaon se 1/10 yaa isse bhi choti hota he. jab kisi sanrachanaatmak avyav kii lambaai uski chaudaai or motaai kii tulana main bahut adhik hoti he to use dharan kaha jaataa he. banken ke falswaroop sanrachanaatmak avyavon ke kuch bhaagon main tanaav utpann hota he jabaki anya bhaag dab jate hai. banken kaa adhyayan kisi sanrachana ke dizin or sthayitv ke liye bahut mahatvapurn he.
 nevier kaa sutarayah sutara us samay lagu hota he jab beam par keval y-disha main bal laga ho or diflection bahut kam ho.
