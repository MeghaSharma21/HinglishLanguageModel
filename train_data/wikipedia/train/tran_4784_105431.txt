 canoeing or kaiking ko berlin main 1936 ke khelon ke baad se greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main pratispardhaa khel ke rup main chitrit kiya gaya he, haalanki ve paris main 1924 ke khelon main pradarshan khel rahe the. olympic pratiyogita main canoeing ke do vishyon hai: slalam or sprint.
 is khel main do style kii naukaaon kaa upyog kiya jaataa he: 1 yaa 2 canors ke saath 1 yaa 2 canors or kayakon ke saath canos. yah har ghatana ke naam padnaam kii ore jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye, "si-1" ek dongi ekal ghatana he or "ke-2" ek kayak yugal kaarykram he. daud aam taur par 500 meter yaa 1000 meter lambey hote hai, haalanki 1936 se 1956 tak 10 kimi kii ghatnaayen bhi thim. 13 agast 2009 ko international canoeing federation ne yah ghoshnaa kii thi ki 200 meter kii ghatnaon se 2012 greeshmakaleen olympic main purushon kii 500 meter kii ghatnaon ko badal diya jayega or unamem se ek mahila ke liye ke-1 200 meter hoga. 200 meter main purushon ke liye anya kaarykram si-1, ke-1 or ke-2 honge. 5 disambar 2009 ko windsar, barkshaayar, great britain main unke 2009 board of diarectors kii baithak main isaki pushti hui.
 nimna talikaon main dikhaaya gaya ki prayek game main koun si ghatnaayen lagi thim.
