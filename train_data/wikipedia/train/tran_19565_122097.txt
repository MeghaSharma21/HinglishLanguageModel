 monikangana datta ek mumbai kii rahane vaali bhartiya model va abhinetri hai. vah guhaavati, assam se hai. unhone army public skul naarangi, guhaavati se apni schuli shiksha puri kii or dilli vishvavidhyalay ke maitri college se snaatak kiya. 
 2001 main monikangana datta ne france kii metropolitan pratiyogita jiti thi. vah apni jeet ke parinamswaroop bharat se paris chali gayi do saal tak, jiske dauraan ve fishan diziner vivian westwood, yoji yamamoto, chrishchiyan diar, leonard, or chloe ke liye ramp vaak kari. paris se unki vaapasi par, vah agrani paridhaan or gahane brandon ke liye tivi vigyapanon main kaam karne lagi. unka pratinidhitav iemji modells dwaara kiya gaya hai. 
 2010 main bhartiya film "guzarish" se unhone apane kariyar kii shuruyaat kii thi, jon kii sanjay lila bhansaali ke nirdesan main banaayin gayi thi.
