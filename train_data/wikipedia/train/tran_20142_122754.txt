 aath kaa samooh samooh-8 ek anterrashtriya manch he. is manch kii sthapana france dwaara 1975 main samooh-6 ke naam se vishwa ke 6 sabase dhani rashtron kii sarkaron ke saath milakar kii thi, yah raashtra the frans, germany,italy, japan, britain or sanyukt rajya america. 1976 main isame canada ko shaamil kar liya gaya or manch kaa naam badalakar samooh-7 kar diya gaya. 1997 main isamen rus bhi samil ho gaya or manch kaa naam samooh-8 ho gaya. samooh-8 ke antargat sadsy raashtra european sangh kaa pratinidhitiv bhi karte hai par ise ek sadsy yaa mejabaan ke rup main abhi shaamil nahin kiya gaya hai. "samooh-8" ko iske sadsy rashtron yaa vaarshik rup se hone vaale samooh-8 shikhar sammelan jisame sadsy rashtron kii sarkaron ke pramukh bhaag lete hai, ke liye prayog kiya ja saktaa he. prayek varsh, is baithak kii mejabani kaa daayitv sadsy rashtron main is kram se ghumta he: frans, sanyukt rajya america, united kingdom, rus,germany, japan, italy or canada.
