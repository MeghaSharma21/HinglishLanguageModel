 ganabhavan, bangladeshki rajdhani dhaka main jaateey sansad bhawan ke nikat sthit ek bhawan he. yah bangladesh ke pradhanmantri kaa aadhikarik nivas he. yah dhaka ke sher-e-bangala nagar kshetra main, sansad bhawan ke uttari kone par sthit he.
 is bhawan ko bangladesh ke pradhanmantri ke aadhikarik nivas ke rup main upyog kiya jaataa he, or vashv ke anya aise kai sarkari nivaason ke viruddh, yah bhawan pradhanmantri ke sachivalay kii mejabani nahin karata, balkiyah keval nivas ke liye hi istemaal hota he. pradhanmantri kaa sachivalay athwa karyaalay, dhaka ke tejagaanv main pradhanmantri karyaalay ke bhawan main avasthit he, jo pradhanmantri ko sachiviya, suraksha va anya suvidhaon va sahyogon se bharpoor rakhata he.
 id ke din is bhawan ko aam aagantukon ke liye khol diya jaataa he, or pradhanmantri samaaj ke vibhinn tapakon ke logon se miltehain, jinamein vibhinn dalon ke varisht raajnneetijna, varisht sainya va jan adhikari, rajanyik, nyaayadhish va saadhaaran log bhi shaamil hote hai.
 yah bhawan mirpur road or lake road ke chauraahey ke pashchimottaree kone par sthit he. yah rashtriya sansad bhawan se, paidal, paanch minute kii duuri par sthit he. yah rajdhani ke teevr suraksha kshetron main se ek main sthit he or sansad bhawan evam pradhanmantri karyaalay donon hi ganabhavan se kuch kshanon kii duuri par sthit hai.
