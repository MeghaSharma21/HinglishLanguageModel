 
 hylobatis hoolok nomascus simphalangus 
 giban lambii bahonvaala, pedo par daudanevaala ek copi kaa jeevvaigyanik kul he jo uttarpurvi bharat, purvi bangladesh or dakshinpurvi china se lekar indonesia ke kai dwipon main paaya jaataa he. giban kul ke chaar jeevvaigyanik vansh or 17 jeevvaigyanik jatiyaan astitv main hai. inkaa rang kaala, bhura or shwet kaa mishran hota he. puure shwet rang ke giban bahut kam hi dikhte hai. giban jatiyon main siamang, shwet-hast yaa laar giban or hoolok giban shaamil hai.
 yah maanav, chimpanzi, orang utaan, gorila or bonobo jaise mahakapiyon kii tulana main aakaar main chote hote hai or unase adhik bandar-jaise dikhte hai lekin sabhi kapiyon kii bhanti inaki bhi punch nahin hoti or yah aksar apane pichley paanvon par chalate hai. inhen aksar hinakpi kaha jaataa he. ye prithvi par khadey hokar to chal sakate hi hai, pedo par bhi haath ke sahare se khadey hokar chalate hai. yah vrikshon main daal-se-daal latakkar bahut tezi se ek sthaan se duusare sthaan jane main sakasham hote hai. inhen ek daal se 50 foot dur kii daal tak kudate hue or 55 kimi prati ghante kii gati se pedo main ghoomte hue mapa gaya he. ud sakane vaale stanadhariyon ko chhodkar, yah vishwa ke sabase gatisheel vriksh vichrani standhari hai.
 giban aaajeevan ek hi jeevanasathi chunakar uske saath rahane ke liye jane jate hai haalanki kuch jeevvaigyanik isapar vivaad karte hai or unke anusaar gibanon main kabhi-kabhi talaak jaisi prakriya bhi dekhi ja sakati he.
