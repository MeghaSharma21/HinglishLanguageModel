 yarlung tsangpo mahaan ghati, jise tsangpo tang ghati bhi kehete hai, dakshini tibet main yarlung tsangpo nadi dwaara banaai gai ek bahut hi badi tang ghati he. kuch sroton ke anusaar yah duniya kii sabase gahari tang ghati he or, america ke grand canyan se zaraa lambi hone se, vishwa kii sabase badi tang ghaatiyon main se ek bhi he. brahmaputra nadi pashchimi tibet main kailash parvat ke paas shuru hokar 1,700 kimi purv ko daudti he or dakshinpurvi tibet main pei gaanv ke paas pahunch kar is gahari yarlung tsangpo mahaan ghati main pravesh karti he jo namacha barwa parvat ke ird-gird ek khai kii tarah mudi hui he or jiske baad brahmaputra dakshin kii taraf bharat ke arunachal pradesh rajya kii ore nikal padati he. kul milakar yarlung tsangpo mahaan ghati 150 kimi lambi he. pei gaanv main ghati kii shuruyaat par nadi 2,900 meter kii oonchaai par he jo upree ghati ke ant tak 1,500 meter tak gir chuki hoti he. upree ghati ke ant main po tsangpo nadi yarlung tsangpo se aa milti he. is se aage nichali ghati main nadi 1,500 meter ke str se bharat kii sarhad tak pahonchte hue 660 meter tak gir jaati he.
 jab nadi namacha barwa or gyaala peri parvaton ke bich se nikalati he to namacha barwa ke charnon main khai kii gaharaai 5,000 meter he. apane 150 kimi ke vistaar main khai kii ausat gaharaai 2,268 meter he. apane sabase gahare bindu par ghati kii gaharaai 6,009 meter he, jo kii nirdeshaank 29°46′11″N 94°59′23″E / 29.769742°N 94.989853°E / 29.769742; 94.989853 par sthit he. yah prithvi kii sabase gahari tang ghaatiyon main se ek he.
 1950 ke dashak main china ne tibet par qabza kar liya or is kshetra ko band kar diya. 1990 ke dashak main chini sarkaar ne kuch paryatakon ko yahan aane-jane kii anumati deni shuru kii or 'yarlung tsangpo mahaan ghati prakritik arakshit kshetra' kii ghoshnaa kar di. lekin saath-hi-saath chini prashasan ne yahan jalavidyut pariyojna banane ke manasoobe taiyaar kiye jinke antargat is nadi kaa kuch pani sarovaron main roka jayega or kuch jal naharon ke zariye bharat se alag disha main china ke anya hisson main bheja jayega. anumaan he ki isse 40,000 megawatt bijli banaai ja sakati he. bharat ne nadi pravaah ko rokane kii kisi bhi yojana kaa sakht virodh kiya he kyonki is se bharat ke purvothar rajyon main krishi or anya aarthik, prakritik or vaataavaran-sambandhi mushkilen utpann ho sakati hai. bhartiya raksha-visheshgyon ko is baat kii bhi chintaa he ki yuddh kii sthiti main achaanak aise baandhon ko kholkar china manachaahe samayon main purvottar bharat ko baadh kaa shikaar bhi bana saktaa he.
