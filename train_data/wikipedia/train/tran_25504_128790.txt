 rogajanak unhen kaha jaataa he, jinke kaaran kai tarah ke bimariyon kaa janm hota he. isamen vishaanu, jeevanu, kavak, paraseete aadi aate hai. yah kisi bhi jeev, ped-paudhe yaa anya sookshm jeevon ko biimaar kar sakate hai. maanav main jeevon ke kaaran hone vaale rog ko bhi rogajanak rogon ke rup main janaa jaataa he.
 in rogajanakon ke kaaran chechak, influenza, galsua, khasra, ibola or rubella aadi rog ho sakate hai. samanytah isaki lambaai 20-300 nanometer ke madhya hoti he.
