 parmanuwaad, parmaanu model yaa parmaanu siddhanth ke vikaas main anek vichaarkon ne bhaag liya he. pracheen vichaarkon main demacraitis or kanad ke naam or aadhunik vichaarkon main newton, radarford or hisenberg ke naam vishesh mahatv ke hai.
 demacraitis ke anusaar parmaanu pariman, aakaar or sthaan main ek duusare se bhinn hai, parantu inamen gunbhed nahin. sanyog viyog main gati aavashyak he or gati avkaash main hi ho sakati he. isaliye parmaanuon ke atirikt avkaash bhi satta kaa antim ansh he. bojhil hone ke kaaran parmaanu neeche girte hai; baari parmaanu adhik veg se girte hai or neeche ke halake parmaanuon se aa takaraate hai. is tarah parmaanuon main sanyog hota he. 
 newton ne parmaanuon ko baari, thos or ekares mana or unamem gunbhed ko bhi sweekaar kiya. parmaanu kii saralata chirkaal tak maanya rahi; naveen bhautikee ne ise amaanya tehraaya he. 
 radarford ke anusaar parmaanu ek nanha sa soramandal he, jisamen anek electron atyadhik veg se centre ke gird chakkar laga rahe hai. hysenvarg kaa anirnitata kaa niyam ek or puraane vichaar ko thokar lagaataa he, is niyam ke anusaar parmaanuon ke samoohon kii dasha main niyam kaa shashan prathit hota he, parantu vyaktigat kriya main parmaanu niyam kii upeksha karte hai; inaki gati anirnit he.
 kanad ne parmaanuon main gunbhed dekha. bhautik dravyon main gunbhed he or is bhed ke kaaran hamen rup, ras, gandh, sparsh or shabd kaa bodh hota he. sambhavat:, savednaaon ke bhed ne use parmaanuon main gunbhed dekhne ko prerana kii. naveen vigyaan kahata he ki kuch tatwon ko chhod anya tatwon ke parmaanu akele nahin milte, apitu 2, 3, 4 ke samoohon main milte hai. kanad ke vichaar main parmaanuon kaa milakar bhi 'chaturanuk' bananaa sristi main moulik ghatana he, is sanyog kaa tootna hi sanhaar yaa pralay he.
