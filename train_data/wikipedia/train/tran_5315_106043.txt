 aaj jane kii zid naa karo ek mashhoor nazm he jise pakistani kavi fayyaaz hashmi dwaara likhaa gaya he. is nazm kii dhun pakistan ke prasiddh rachayita sohail rana dwaara rachit he. ise pracheen gayak habib vali mohammad dwaara lokapriy banaaya gaya jinhone ek parshv gayak ke rup main pakistani film badal or bijli main yah geet gaaya tha. iske pashchaat mashhoor ghazal gayika farida khaanum unke television pradarshan or saarvajanik sangeet samaaroh main bhi yah geet gaaya karti thim. farida khaanum dwaara yah geet raag yaman kalyaan main gaaya gaya tha.
 yah geet anek kalakaaron dwaara gaaya gaya he. aashaa bhonsle ne lav supreme album main yah geet gaaya he. golden loyan kaa khitaab jeet chuki mira nair kii monsoon vaiding ke ek drishy main parshv sangeet ke rup main is geet kaa upyog kiya gaya he. habib vali mohammad ke is geet kaa rupantar bhi atyant lokapriy he.yah shankar takar kii online sangeet shrunkhlaa 'da shruti box' kaa bhi ek bhaag he jise rohinee rawada dwaara gaaya gaya he.e aar rahamaan ne is geet ko em.tii.ve anplagd sizan-2 main pradarshit kiya tha.
 2015 main yah geet coke studio pakistan ke liye pakistani band 'strings' dwaara banaaya gaya or sangeet se lambe interval ke baad farida khaanum ne coke studio ke aathve sizan ke aakhri prakaran main yah geet phir se gaaya.
 arijit singh ne television dharawahik namakaran ke liye yah geet phir se banaaya jise star plus channel par prasaarit kiya jaataa he.
 yah geet preetam dwaara 2016 kii film i dil he mushkil ke liye phir se banaaya gaya jise shilpa raav dwaara gaaya gaya he.
