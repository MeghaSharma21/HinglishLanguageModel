 tatkaal sakal niptaan athwa vaastavik samay sakal bhugtan pranali ), yaa aar tii ji es ek aisi nidhi antaran paddati he jisamen ek bank se duusare bank main mudra kaa antaran 'vaastavik samay' or 'sakal' aadhaar par hota he. yah kisi banking channel dwaara mudra antaran kaa sabase tej madhyam he. 'vaastavik samay' main bhugtan se taatpary he ki bhugtan lendenon ke liye koi pratiksha avadhi nahin hoti. jaise hi koi lenden prasanskrit hota he thik usee samay uskaa niptaan ho jaataa he. 'sakal bhugtan' se taatpary lendenon kaa kisi anya lenden jaise ki banching yaa neting aadi ke liye pratiksha kiye bina, ek ke liye ek aadhaar par niptaan hona he. kisi lenden ke prasanskrit hone ke baad bhugtan antim or apratisanharneey ho jaataa he.
