 rinshabd aise shabd hote hai jo ek bhashaa main utpann hue hon lekin kisi anya bhashaa main, bina anuwaad ke, prayog hote hon. udhaaharan ke liye, angrezi main ek panch naamak peya he, jo britain, australia, canada or sanyukt rajya america main piya jaataa he. iskaa naam hindi ke "paanch" shabd se utpann hua or angrezi main rinshabd he kyonki isamen parmparik rup se paanch chize daali jaati thi .
