 rudki prodyogiki sansathan, rudki, ke uttarakhand, bharat main sthit ek abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay he. yah ai. ai. tii. rudki or uttarakhand takneekee vishvavidhyalay, dehradun se sambhddha rakate he. india today ke may 2017 ke sanskaran ke anusaar yah sabase badhiya mahavidhyalayon kii suchi main sheersh 5 mahavidhyalayon main se ek tha. isaki sthapana himalayan cheritable trust ke antargat varsh 2005 main kii gai thi. 
 rashtriya rajmarg - 58 se thodi duuri par sthit araiti kaa parisar 20 acre main failaa he.
 araiti rudki main abhiyantriki, prabandhan or anya kaaryakramon main shiksha pradaan karne vaale 12 shaikshanik vibhaag hai:
 araiti main ek pustakon se bharpoor or wifai sakasham aadhunik pustkaalay he. pustkaalay ko niyamit aadhaar par ejineering, praudyogiki or prabandhan ke sambandhit kshetron main naveenatam patrikaon or pustakon ke saath unnat kiya jaataa he.
 parisar main 2 chatravaas hai, ladakon or ladakiyon donon ke liye ek-ek. prayek chatravaas main apana bhojanalay he. adhikansh chaatr chatravaas main hi rahete hai, jisase ki ve padhai par puraa dhyaan de sakate hai.
 phinics sthaneey chaatr nikaay he jo parisar main atirikt pathyakram gatividhiyon kaa aayojan karata he. yah har saal takneekee or saanskrithik kaaryakram yojit karata he.
 araiti rudki purv chaatr sangh kii sthapana 2009 main hui thi. yah un purv chaatr, jinhone academik / anusandhan, saamaajik vigyaan, abhiyantriki, lok prashasan, korporate vikaas / udhyamshiltaa or samaaj seva ke kshetron main baari yogadan diya he. purv chaatr santhaan kaa daura karte hai or chaatro or karmchaariyon ke saath baatcheet karke sansathan ke sudhaar ke liye apane suzav saajha karte hai.
