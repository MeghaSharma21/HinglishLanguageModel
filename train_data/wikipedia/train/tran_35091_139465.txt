 'ozon'OZONE' oxygen ke teen parmaanuon se milakar banane vaali ek gas he jo vayumandal main bahut kam matra main pai jaati hai. yah tikhe gandh vaali atyant vifailli gas he. jameen ke sataha ke upar arthath nichle vayumandal main yah ek khatarnaak dooshak he, jabaki vayumandal kii upari parat ozon parat ke rup main yah surya ke parabangnee vikiran se prithvi par jivan ko bachaati he, jahaan iskaa nirmaan oxigen par parabangnee kirnon ke prabhavaswaroop hota he. ozon oxigen kaa ek aparrup he. yah samudri vaayu main upasthit hoti he.
 won mairam ne san 1785 main vidyut visarjan yantron ke paas ek vishesh prakaar kii gandh kaa anubhav kiya jiskaa ullekh unhone apane lekhon main bhi kiya. 1801 main crik shank ko bhi oxigen main vidyut visarjan karte samay yahi anubhav hua. 1840 main shanbin nen is gandh kaa kaaran ek nayi gas ko bataaya or unhone ise ozon naam diya jo unani shabd ojo yani main soonghta hum par aadhaarit tha. san 1865 main sorat ne yah siddh kiya ki yah gas oxigen kaa ek aparrup he or iskaa anusutra O3 he. omshanti
