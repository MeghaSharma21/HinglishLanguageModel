 salvador de avila bada girjaghar ek romaniskiu or gothic style main bana hua girjaghar he. ye spain main old kastile main avila shahar main sthati he. ise ek girjaghar-kila banane kii yojana banaai gayi thi. iske aas paas bahut saare ghar or mahal sthit hai. evening mahal, infent king mahal, or valdrabons mahal iske paas main hi maujuud hai. ye saare puerta de los leales ke suraksha karte hai.
 iske nirmaan ke bare main pakke saboot nahin milte. zyaadaatar iske bare main do siddhant prachlit hai. pahile ke anusaar alwaar garsia ne iskaa nirmaan sevir girjaghar ke avshesh par karavaaya gaya tha. jise muslim samay ke dauraan nasht kar diya gaya tha. phir isse kastile ke alphonso chathe ne dubaara banwaaya tha. kuch itihaaskaar maanate hai ki ise maaikel frukal ne banwaaya tha. or burgundi ke remand ne is shahar ko aabaad kiya tha. 13veen sadi main iske tower or giraje kaa bagali rasta banwaaya gaya. 14veen sadi main iske math, tower or gumbad banaae gaye. 15 sadi main puri tarah ban kar taiyaar ho gayi thi. 1475i. main jaan guas ne makenical ghadi banaai thi.
 avila girjaghar or kuenka girjaghar donon 12veen sadi main banaaye gayi pahile girjaghar the. is par francisi prabhaav bhi dekha ja saktaa he. ye abe carch of denis ke samroop he. jo kii yurap kaa pahila gothic girjaghar tha.
