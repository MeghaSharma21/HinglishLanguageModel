 kaulpit dolitr kundalee-sandharitra se nirmit electronic dolak he jo nishchit aavruti kii ke dolan janit karne ke liye sandharitra or prerak ke sangyojan se banataa he. iskaa avishkaar 1918 main america abhiyantaa edwin ech kaulpit ne kiya. kaulpit dolitr kaa visisth gundharm yah he ki isamen sakriya paripath kaa punarnivesh prerak ke saath shrenee kram main jude do sandharitron se nirmit vibhav vibhajak dwaara diya jaataa he.
