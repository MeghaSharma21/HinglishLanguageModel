 vijay nagar rajya ke samay main hi 1612 i0 main oriyar naamak raja ne mysore rajya kii sthapana kii is mysore rajya main aage chalakar do pramukh shaasha hue-haidar ali evam tipu. inhone angrejon ke viruddh sangharsh jari rakhaa. tipu, haidar ali dakshin bharat kaa pehla shaasha tha jise angrejon ko parajeet karne main safalataa mili. dakshin main mysore rajya or angrejon ke bich kul chaar yuddh hue. in yudhon kii ek khaas visheshta yah thi ki isamen marathe or hyderabad ke nizaam angrejon dwaara banaaye gayi trigut main shaamil the.
