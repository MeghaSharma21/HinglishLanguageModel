 anya upyog hetu dekhen - kaali nadi
 kaali nadi yaa kalindi bharat ke karnataka rajya main bahane vaali ek nadi he. bijli utpaadan ke liye is nadi par bahut se band bane hai. is nadi kaa udgam sthal pashchimi ghat main he or yah pashchim kii ore bahati hui karavar naamak kasbe ke nikat arab sagar main mil jaati he. nadi mukhyatah uttari kanar jile se hokar bahati he. is nadi kii sahaayak nadiyaan hai, upree kanery or tatthihalla.
 yah nadi uttari kanar jile main 4 lakh logon kii jeevanarekha he or kai hazaar anya logon, jinamein karavar tat ke machuaare bhi hai, ke liye yah nadi jeevika kaa saadhan he.
 audyogic ikaayiyon dwaara anupachaarit nissari nadi main chhodane, avaidh baaluu khanan jo soopa kshetra main andhadhundh jari he, ke kaaran nadi paristhitiki ko bahut kshati pahaunchi he. iske atirikt nadi muhane par bahut si rasinic companiyaan hai jahaan se dashakon se vishakt apshisht nadi ke jal main ris rahaa he.
