 aam janata kaa yah adhikaar ki vah kuch saarvajanik sevaao ko tay samyavadhi main paane kaa haq rakhti he - 'seva kaa adhikaar' kehalaata he. iske tahat tay samaysima main kaam kaa nibatara karnaa sambandhit adhikariyon kii baadhyataa hoti he. samaysima ke andar seva nahin uplabdh karaanevaale adhikariyon ke liye dand kaa praavdhaan kiya jaataa he.
 bharat main madhya pradesh rajya ne lok seva guarantee adhinium 2010 ke dwaara sabase pehle yah kanoon lagu kiya. ab bihar, punjab, jharkhand uttarakhand main bhi yah niyam lagu he. dilli or kerala sarkarein yah niyam lagu karne ja rahi hai.
