 kundesar, uttar pradesh main gajipur jile kaa ek gaanv he. raja bhairav shah ke sabase bade pote talcooker babu madhav raay ne ganga nadi ke kinare par 1507 i di main isko sthapit kiya tha.
 raja malhan dikshit kii paanchavin peedi main, raja bhairo shah antim vyakti the jo sahamdh se gondor tak chale gaye or ek kila kaa nirmaan kiya. vanshavali ke record ke anusaar kashyap godria 'kishwar' dikshit yoddha parivaar ne gadhwadalon ke liye ladaaiyon kii ek shrinkhalaa ke baad giripuri or aasapaas ke kshetra ke cheru adivasee shaskaon ko upar se ukhaad diya. dikshit brahman or gahadvila donon dakshinapath se kannauj gaye the. gadavadala raja chandradev ke ek akshep main, yah ullekh kiya gaya he ki vah brahman yoddhaon kii madad se pavitra shahar kaashi ke nikat gadipuri pakade.gahadwala sena ke abhiyaan main 'mooldhan dikshit' kii pramukh bhoomika vivaran main 'shri pothee bansuri' main varnit he. ek sadbhaavna ke sanket ke rup main, gahdala raja ne unhen azamgarh,mau,baliya or gajipur jilon main 700 gaanvon ke anudaan ke saath 'raja' kaa khitaab pradaan kiya. baad main kabeeley teen mukhya shakhaon main viksit huin jaise birpur, narayanpur-kundaser or karimuddinpur.
