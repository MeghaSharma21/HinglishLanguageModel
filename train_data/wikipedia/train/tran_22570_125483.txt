 arjungarh sthaan shankaragarh vikaskhand ke jokapat ke bihad jangal main sthit he. yahaan pracheen keele kaa bhagnavesh dikhaai padta he. ek sthaan par pracheen lambii into kaa gheraav he. is sthaan ke neeche gahari khai he, jahaan se ek jharnaa bahata he. kivdanti he ki pehle ek siddhpurush kaa nivas tha. is pahari kshetra me ek gupha he jise dhiria lata gupha ke naam se janaa jaataa he. arjunagarh me pracheen puratathvik puratathvik mahatv ke avshesh aaj bhi dekhne ko milte hai.
