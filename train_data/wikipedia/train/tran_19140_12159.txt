 pali, uttar bharat, or vishesh rup se magadh janapad kii ek pracheen praakrit he. ise 'magadhi' bhi kehete hai. jainon kii ardhamagadhi kii apeksha yah sanskrit ke adhik nikat he. jaise sanskrit kii shakuntala ko pali main 'sakuntala' kahenge.
 aisa ullekh miltaa he ki bhagwaan buddha ke pradhan shishy mahakacchan ne pali kaa ek vyakaran rachaa tha kintu vah nahin miltaa. bodhisatt or sabbagunakar naam ke do vyakaran the jo ab nahin milte. aajkal kacchan, moggallan or saddaniti- inhi teen vyakaranon kaa adhik prachaar he. in teen vyakaranon main kachan vyakaran adhik pracheen he jo sambhavtah srilanka main likhaa gaya tha. yah vyakaran bade saral dhang se likhaa gaya he. pali vyakaran ke kuch anya granthon ke naam ye hai-
 vaidic bhashaa evam sanskrit kii apeksha madhyakaalin bhashaaon kaa bhed pramukhataa se nimna baton main paaya jaataa he : 
  vyakaran kii drushti se 
 ye visheshtaayen madhyayugin bhartiya aryabhasha ke saamaanya lakshan hai or desh kii un lokbhashaon main paae jate hai jinka suprachaar ukt avadhi se koi do hajaar varsh tak rahaa or jinka bahut-sa sahitya bhi uplabdh he.
