 
 
 viper dweep bharat ke centre shasit pradesh andaman evam nicobar dweep samooh ke anekon dwipon main se he. yah andaman evam nicobar dweep samooh ke dakshini andaman jile kaa bhaag he. yah port blair se 4 kilometre pashchim main sthit he. 
 british kaal main bharat ke anya bhaagon se laaye gayi 'khatarnaak' bandiyon bandiyon ko isi dweep par utaara jaataa tha. ab yah dweep ek picnic sthal ke rup main viksit ho chukaa he. yahaan ke toote-phute faansee ke fande nirmam ateet ke sakshi banakar khadey hai.
 yahaan par is dweep kii sabase pehali angrejon dwaara banaai gai jail thi. iske upar hi tab kaa bana faansee ghar bhi he.yaheen par sher ali ko bhi faansee di gai thi, jisane 1872 main bharat ke governor general lord mayo kii hatya kii thi.
