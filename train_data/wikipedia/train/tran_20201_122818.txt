 tiyaan shaan madhya asia kaa ek pahari silsilaa he.
 tiyaan shaan parvatamala taklamkaan registan ke uttar or pashchim main qazakstan, kirgizstan or china ke pashchimi soobe shinjiyang kii sarhad par sthit he. pashchim main yah pamir parvatamala se milti he. qazakstan or china par sthit isik kul jhil iske uttar-purv main sthit he. is silsile kii sab se buland chotii 4,148 meter unchaa jengish chokusu he. isaki duusari sab se buland choti 7,010 unchaa khan tangri he. is parvat shrunkhlaa se ubharne vaale ahm dariyaaon main sir dariyaa, ili nadi or tarim nadi shaamil hai. is ilaake main pahunchane vaale pehle europi peter saminof the jinhone 1850 kii dahaai main yahan kaa daura kiya or tiyaan shaan kii tafasilaat ko bayaan kiya.
