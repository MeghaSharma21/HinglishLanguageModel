 maaikel joseph jackson, lokapriy amareeki pop gayak the, jinhen king of pop bhi kaha jaataa he. maaikel, jackson danpati kii saatvin santan the, jinhone maatr gyaarah varsh kii aayu main hi vyavsaayik rup se gayaki aarambh kar di thi. us samay ve jackson-5 samooh ke sadsy hua karte the. 1971 main unhone apana vyaktigat karier prarambh kiya, haalaanki us samay bhi ve group ke sadsy the. jackson ne gayaki kii duniya main sheeghra hi sikaa jamaa liya or king of pop ke naam se prasiddh ho gaye. unke sabase adhik bikri vaale albamon main, of da waal, bed, dangeras or history pramukh hai. haalaanki 1982 main jari thriller unka ab tak ke sabase adhik bikne vaala album mana jaataa he.
 1980 ke praarambhik varshon main hi jackson america pop gayaki or manoranjan kii duniya ke sabase lokapriy sitare banakar ubhare the. amtivi par unke video ne bahut dhooma machaai. thriller ne to video sangeet kii avadharana hi badal di thi. nabbe ke dashak main blike or hwite or screem ne unhen acchhee prasiddhi dilaai. manch pradarshnon ke dwaara unki nrity style prasiddh ho gai. ve ek chamakate sitare ban gaye.
 maaikel jackson kai baar ginis book main sthaan praapt kar chuke hai. ab tak ke sabase safal manoranjankarta ke 13 grami award jeetne vaale jackson akele kalakaar he. 25 june 2009 ko dil kaa daura padane ke kaaran jackson kaa nidhan ho gaya.
