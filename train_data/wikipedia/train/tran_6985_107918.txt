 haryana main sthit mahendragarh apane khubasurat paryatak sthalon ke liye prasiddh he. yah mahendragarh jila mukhyalay he. pehle yahaan par prithviraj chouhan ke vanshaj angpaal kaa samrajya tha. baad main is par maraathon, jhazzaar ke nawabon or british shaskaon ne bhi shashan kiya. aadhunik mahendragarh kii sthapana 1948 i. main kii gai thi. narnaul, dadri or mahendragarh iske pramukh shahar hai. iske uttar main bhivani va rohtak, purv main rewadi va alver, dakshin main sikar va jaipur or pashchim main sikar va jhanjhanu sthit he. pichley dinon mahendragarh kii dadri tehsil ko bhivani main or gudgaanv ke bhivani ko mahendragarh main joda gaya he. yahaan ke nivasee bade hansmukh or milansaar hai. vah apane yahaan aane vaale paryatakon kaa swaagat badi garmjoshi se karte hai. paryatakon ko yahaan kahin bhi boriyat yaa neerasata kaa saamana nahin karnaa padataa. vah yahaan par shandaar chhuttiyaan vyateet kar sakate hai. 
 mahendragarh main sthit jalmahal bahut khubasurat he. mahal kii deevaaron par likhe shilalekhon ke anusaar iskaa nirmaan 1591 i. main shah kuli khan ne karaayaa tha. yah mahal ek talab ke binchobich bana hua he. lekin ab yah talab puri tarah se suukh chukaa he. mahal main paanch choti-choti dukaanon kaa nirmaan kiya gaya he. paryatak in dukaanon se khubasurat smritikaayen kharid sakate hai.
 chor gumbad kaa nirmaan jamaal khan ne karaayaa tha. ise narnaul kaa sainboard' ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. gumbad ke nirmaan kaal kaa abhi tak pataa nahin chalaa he. lekin iskaa vaastu shaastra shah vilaayat ke gumbad se mail khaataa he. is gumbad kii arco kaa nirmaan angreji ke S varna ke aakaar main kiya gaya he. kaha jaataa he pracheen samay main yah chor-daakuon ke chupane kii jagah thi. isiilie iskaa naam chor gumbad pad gaya. iske andar janaa manaa he
 birbal kaa chaataa paanch manjila imaarat he or yah bahut khubasurat he. pehle iskaa naam chaataa raay mukunda das tha. iskaa nirmaan narnaul ke dewan raay-e-rain ne shahjahan ke shashan kaal main karaayaa tha. pracheen samay main akbar or birbal yahaan par tehre the. uske baad ise birbal kaa chaataa naam se janaa jane laga. isaki vaastukala bahut khubasurat he. dekhne main yah bahut saadhaaran lagata he kyonki isaki sajaavat nahin kii gai he. isamen kai sabhaagaar, kamaron or mandapon kaa nirmaan kiya gaya he. kathaon ke anusaar yah bhi kaha jaataa he ki iske neeche chaar surange bhi bani hui hai. yah surange isko jaipur, dilli, dausi or mahendragarh se jodatii hai.
 ibrahim khan ke makbar ke paas hi shah vilaayat kaa makbara bana hua he. iske vaastu shaastra main paryatak tughlaq kaal kii chhavi dekh sakate hai, jo bahut khubasurat he. yah paryatakon ko bahut pasand aata he. prasiddh lekhak gulzaar ke anusaar isaki stambhavli or gumbad kaa nirmaan alam khan mewari ne kiya tha.
 is makbare kaa nirmaan sher shah suri ne 1538-46 i. main apane dada ibrahim khan kii yaad main karaayaa tha. shaikh ahamed niaji ne is makbare ke nirmaan main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai thi. iske paas hi inaki kabren bhi bani hui hai. makbara chaukor aakaar kaa he. yah makbara, chaukor makbara style va pathaan nirmaan style kaa behtareen namoona he.
 narnaul se 3 kimi kii duuri par nasibpur sthit he. yahaan par british shaskaon ne swatantraaa senaniyon kaa katl kiya tha. kaha jaataa he ki jis samay yah raktapaat hua us samay yahaan kii dharti khun se laal ho gai thi. un swatantraaa senaniyon ko shradhanjali dene ke liye yahaan par park kaa nirmaan kiya gaya he. yah park bahut khubasurat he. paryatak is park main picnic bhi manaa sakate hai. 
 narnaul main sthit shah kuli khan kaa makbara bahut khubasurat he. iske nirmaan main saleti or laal rang ke pattharon kaa prayog kiya gaya he. yah makbara aath konon vaala he. iske vaastu shaastra main pathaan style kaa prayog kiya gaya he. makbar main tripolia dwaar kaa nirmaan bhi kiya gaya he. is dwaar kaa nirmaan 1589 i. main kiya gaya tha. makbare ke paas khubasurat talab or bageeche bhi hai. talab or bageeche kaa nirmaan pehle or makbare kaa nirmaan baad main kiya gaya tha. yah bagichaa bahut khubasurat he. iskaa naam aram-e-kausar he.
 raja naun karn maa chamunda devi kaa bhakt tha. unhone hi is mandir kaa nirmaan karaayaa tha. yah mandir pahari kii tarai main bana hua he. raja naun karn ke baad is kshetra par mughalon ne adhikaar kar liya. mughalon ne chamunda devi ke mandir ke paas hi ek masjid kaa nirmaan karaayaa tha. yah masjid bahut khubasurat he. mandir ke saath paryatakon ko yah masjid bhi bahut pasand aati he.

 narnaul mahendragarh sadak par sthit gonv gulavala main pahari kii chotii par bana pracheen hanuman ji kaa mandir logon kii aasthaa or vishrvaas kaa centre bana hua he. kehete hai, is pahari kii chotii par pandavon ne apane agyatavaas ke kuch din bitaaye the or hanuman ji kii murti kii sthapana karke apni safalataa kii kaamana kii thi. baba bajrangi kii krupa se pandavon kaa agyatavaas nirvighn puraa hua. tabhi se lekar aaj tak log badi shradgha or vishrvaas ke saath yahaan pahaad kii chotii par sthapit bajrang bali ji kii pratimaa kii puja-archana kar, mannaten maangate hai.
 vartmaan main is pracheen hanuman mandir ko kailash ashram ke naam se janaa jaataa he. is kshetra ke mahaan sant baba khetnaath ji ne is ashram ko chamkaaya. logon kii shradgha or aasthaa kaa alam itnaa tha ki gaanv gulavala ke har ghar ke sadsy ne apane sir par pathar, bajri, cement, pani or anya saamaan rakhkar ek bhavy ashram kaa nirmaan kiya. yah ashram pahari kii chotii par sthit he. chadhaai chadh kar jab mandir main pahonchte hai to thakaan kaa namonishaan tak nahin rahata he. hanuman ji kii pracheen murti ke sammukh baithkar aradhana karte hai to aisa lagata he ki maano chiranjeevi hanuman ji vahiin viramaan hai. jo bhi yahaan aakar sachche dil se mannath maangata he, uski muraad avashya puri hoti he. mandir main shri shri 1008 shri baba khetnaath, baba shukranath, baba sevanath ji kii vishaal murtiyon bhi logon kii shradgha kaa centre hai. mandir kii deevaaron par bhagwaan shankar, nrisinghavatar, hanuman ji, maa bhagwati, ram-darbar, shiv-parivaar or swarg-narak ke sundar chitra bane hai, jo mandir kii sundarataa main chaar chand lagaate hai. deevaaron par hanuman chalisa, gita saar or chaoupaaiyaan likhi gai hai, jo mandir ke vaataavaran ko bhakthimay banaati hai.
 vartmaan main ashram ke dhune par baba santoshnath ji virajman hai. unke sanchaalan main pratidin mandir main gyaan kii dhara bahati rahati he. yahaan har mangalvaar ko kirthan evam bhandara hota he or guru poornima ko vishaal bhandare kaa aayojan kiya jaataa he. baisaakh kii saptami ko baba shukranath ji kii jayanti bade dhoomdhaam se manaai jaati he. sawan kii shivaratri ko vishesh puja-archana kii jaati he. kehete hai ki sawan kii shivaratri ko hi yahaan pandavon ne hanuman ji kii murti kii sthapana kii thi. isliye is din hanuman ji yahaan avashya padhaarte hai. har tyohaar par mandir ko sajaayaa jaataa he or bhajan-kirthan kiye jate hai.
 hanuman ji kaa mandir logon kii shradgha or aasthaa kaa centre bana hua he. yahaan shradghaluon kii bheed lagi rahati he. pratidin subah-shaam shankh bajaakar logon ko aarti ke liye bulaayaa jaataa he.
 vayumarg se bhi paryatak aasaani se mahendragarh tak pahunch sakate hai. paryatakon kii suvidha main liye chandigarh oud dilli main hawaii adde banaae gaye hai. in hawaii addon se paryatak taxi va bason dwaara aasaani se mahendragarh tak pahunch sakate hai.
 paryatak mahendragarh jane ke liye dilli railway stesan se rail pakad sakate hai. mahendragarh ke liye dilli se kai rail chalati hai.
 mahendragarh jane ke liye paryatak dilli ke kashmiri get bas adde se bas le sakate hai. yahaan se mahendragarh ke liye anek basen chalati hai.
