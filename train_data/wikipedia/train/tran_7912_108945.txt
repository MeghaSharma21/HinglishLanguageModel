 praan hindi filmon ke ek pramukh charitra abhinayta the jo mukhyatah apni khalanaayak kii bhoomika ke liye jane jate hai. kai baar filmafere puraskaar tathaa bengali film jarnlists association awards jeetne vaale is bhartiya abhinayta ne hindi cinema main 1940 se 1990 ke dashak tak damdaar khalanaayak or nayak kaa abhinay kiya. unhone prarambh main 1940 se 1947 tak nayak ke rup main filmon main abhinay kiya. iske alaava khalanaayak kii bhoomika main abhinay 1942 se 1991 tak jari rakhaa. unhone 1948 se 2007 tak sahaayak abhinayta kii tarj par bhi kaam kiya.
 apane urvar abhinay kaal ke dauraan unhone 350 se adhik filmon main kaam kiya. unhone khandaan, pilpili sahib or halaaku jaisi filmon main mukhya abhinayta kii bhoomika nibhaai. unka sarvshereshth abhinay madhumati, jis desh main ganga bahati he, upkaar, shahid, ansu ban gayi phool, johny meraa naam, victoria nambar 203, be-imaan, zanjeer, don or duniya filmon main mana jaataa he.
 praan ne apane karier ke dauraan vibhinn puraskaar or sammaan apane naam kiye. unhone 1967, 1969 or 1972 main filmafere sarvshereshth sahaayak abhinayta puraskaar or 1997 main filmphere lifetime echievement award jeeta. unhen san 2000 main stardst dwaara 'milinium ke khalanaayak' dwaara puraskrit kiya gaya. 2001 main bharat sarkaar ne unhen padam bhushan se sammanit kiya or bhartiya cinema main yogadan ke liye 2013 main dada sahab phalke sammaan se nawaja gaya. 2010 main sienen kii sarvshereshth 25 sarvakalik asian abhinaytaon main chunaa gaya.
 12 farvari 1920 ko dilli main paidaa huye praan ne saikadon filmon main yaadgaar bhoomikaaye nibhaain. praan ke pita lala keval krishna sikand ek sarkari thekedaar the, jo aam taur par sadak or pul kaa nirmaan karte the. dehradun ke paas kalsi pul unka hi banaaya hua he. apane kaam ke silsile main idhar-udhar rahane vaale lala keval krishna sikand ke bete praan kii shiksha kapoorthala, unnav, meerut, dehradun or rampur main hui.
 bataur photographer lahore main apana karier shuru karne vaale praan ko 1940 main ‘yamala jet’ naamak film main pehali baar kaam karne kaa avasar mila. uske baad to praan ne phir palat kar nahin dekha.
 ravivaar ke anusaar unhone lagbhag 400 filmon main kaam kiya. ek taraf unke naam ‘ram or shyaam’ ke khalanaayak kii aisi tasviir rahi he, jisase logon ne parade ke baahar bhi ghrinaa shuru kar di thi, vahiin unke naam ‘upkaar’ ke mangal chachaa kii bhoomika bhi he, jise darshakon kaa beintahaa pyaar or sammaan mila. 1968 main upkaar, 1970 ansu ban gayi phool or 1973 main praan ko beyimaan film main sarvshereshth sahaayak abhinayta ke liye film fere award diya gaya. iske baad mili sammaan or award kii sankhya saikadon main he.
 1945 main shukla se vivaahit praan bharat-pakistan bantwaare ke baad bete aravind, sunil or ek beti pinki ke saath mumbai aa gayi. aaj kii taarikh main unke parivaar main 5 pote-potiyaan or 2 padpote bhi shaamil hai. khelon ke prati praan kaa prem bhi jagajaahir he. 50 ke dashak main unki apni footbal team ‘dynamos footbal club’ bahucharchit rahaa he..
 is mahaan kalakaar ne 12 july 2013 ko mumbai ke leelavati aspataal main antim saans lee. ullekhneey baat yah bhi he ki unke janm or mrityu kii tithi kii sankhya ek hi thi- 12 .
