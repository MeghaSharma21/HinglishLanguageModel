 1981 europea cap ke final england ke liverpool or spain ke real madrid ke bich ek footbal match park des princes par 27 may 1981, paris, france thi. yah europe ke pramukh cap pratiyogita, europea cap ke 1980-81 satr ke antim match tha. liverpool 1977 or 1978 main do dikhaave ke baad, apane tisare final main dikhaai de rahe the. real madrid ke apane nauven final main dikhaai de rahe the, ve pehle se pratiyogita chah baar jeeta he or do ​​baar kho diya tha. 
 48,360 kii bheed ne dekha, pehali chamahi gol tha. alan kennedy ran banaae jab liverpool duusari chamahi main le liya. unhone liverpool kaa teesraa europea cap or ek english team ne ek lagaataar paanchavin jeet hasil karne, match 1-0 se jeetne ke liye is netritv kaa aayojan kiya. liverpool ke prabandhak bob paisle pratiyogita teen baar jeetne vaale pehle manager ban gaya. 
 
 
 
 
 liverpool unki teesraa europea cap final main dikhaai de rahe the. ve 1977 or 1978 main apane pichley do dikhaave jeeta tha. real madrid ke apane nauven final main dikhaai de rahe the, ve pratiyogita 1956 se 1960 tak ek record lagaataar paanch jeet sahit chah baar jeet hasil kii thi. unke do ghaataa 1962 or 1964 main the, jabaki unki chhathi jeet, 1966 main kiya gaya tha. 
 donon pakshon ne is match se pehle chot chintaa thi. liverpool khilaadiyon ke ek number par andeh tha. alan kennedy ek tuuti hui kalai ke saath chah saptaah ke liye darkinar kar diya gaya tha, jabaki keni dalglish, kai hafton ke liye prashikshit nahin kiya gaya he. real november ke baad se darkinar kiya gaya tha jo striker lorie kaningham se adhik chintaa thi.
