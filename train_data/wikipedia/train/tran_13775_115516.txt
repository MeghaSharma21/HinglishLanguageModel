 gangabai, peshwa narayan kii patni. atharah varsh kii avasthaa main hi jab narayan raav ko 30 agast 1773 i. ko samay se vetan praapt na hone ke kaaran krodhonmatta sipaahiyon ne maar daalaa tab raghunatha raav peshwa banakar rajkaaj dekhne lage. kintu yah baat anek logon ko apriy thi. at: raghunatha raav ke virdohi logon ne naanaa fadanvis or harimpath fadake ke netritv main ek parishad kii sthapana kii. us samay gangabai garbhvatti thim. at: in logon ne raghunatha raav ko padchyut karne kii yojana banaai jiske anusaar gangabai ke garbh se balak kaa janm hone par use padchyut karnaa sahaj tha. at: un logon ne ganga bai ko purandar bhejne kii vyavastha kii taaki unka koi anisht na kar sake. or ve log is bhavi peshwa ke janm kii pratiksha main gangabai ke naam se peshwa kaa kaam chalaane lage. 18 may 1774 i. ko gangabai ke putr hua jise madhavrao narayan ke naam se abhihit kiya gaya or janm ke chalisven din use logon ne peshwa ghoshit kar diya. peeche yahi savai madhavrao ke naam se prakhyat hua. uske bade hone tak gangabai uske naam par shasankarya dekhati raheen or ve naanaa fadanbis ke paraamarsh ke anusaar hi chalati thim. isse parishad main matbhed utpann ho gaya or logon ne yah apwaad failaana aarambh kiya ki gangabai kaa fadanbis ke saath avaidh sambandh he or usase unhen garbh he. ve is lokapvaad ko sahan na kar sakin or vish khaakar unhone apana pranant kar liya.
