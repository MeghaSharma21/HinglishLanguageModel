 
 actinomicitalis gram-dhanatmak bacteria kaa ek jeevvaigyanik gana he. is gana ke sadasyon ko aksar actinomicit kehete hai. actinomicit jatiyaan vividh hai or kabhi-kabhi ek hi jati ke rup unki paristhitiyon ke anusaar bahut pruthak dikhte hai. adhikansh actinomicit mycelium ke saath shakhaayen banaate hue failte hai. kuch jatiyaan ahaanikaarak hai lekin kuch aise rogajanak bhi hai jo pratijaivik se bhi aasaani se nahin marate. actinomicit ko jeevanu bhoji virus dwaara sankramit kara ja saktaa he.
