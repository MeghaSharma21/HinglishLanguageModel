 cracken sagar saura mandal ke shani grah ke sabase bade chandramaa titan ke uttar dhruviya kshetra main sthit ek jhil he. yah titan par sabase badi gyaat jhil he . san 2007 main casini-hoygens antariksh yaan ne ise khoj niklaa tha or iskaa naam 2008 main ek kaalpanik samudri daanav par rakhaa gaya. iskaa aakaar prithvi ke caspian sagar ke aasapaas anumaanit kiya jaataa he. titan kii anya jhiilon kii tarah isamen bhi pani kii jagah methane jaise hydrocarbon dravavastha main bhare hai.
 cracken sagar main bahut se dweep hai lekin inamen se jhil ke uttari bhaag main sthit 'myda insula' naamak ek dweep kaafii bada he. iskaa vyas 168 kimi anumaanit kiya gaya he.
 radar chithran or avarakt chithran se cracken sagar ke puure aakaar kaa pataa chalata he
 radar chithran, jisamen cracken sagar kaa uttari bhaag or usamen sthit myda insula dweep
 cracken sagar kaa ek hissaa jisamen khurdura tat or kai dweep dikh rahe hai
