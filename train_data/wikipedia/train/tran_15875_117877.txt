 kerala main kannur kile ke rup main vikhyath is kile kaa nirmaan 1505 i. main pratham portugali vysarai don francisko di almida dwaara banwaaya gaya tha. portugaliyon ke baad is kile par dachon ko niyantran ho gaya, uske baad angrejon ne is par adhikaar kar liya. malabar main angrejon kaa yah pramukh sainya thikaana tha. vartmaan main yah kila bhartiya puratathvik surveykshan ke adheen he or yahaan se mappila be fishing harbar ke sundar drishy dekhe ja sakate hai. yah kila kannur se 3 kilometre kii duuri par he.
