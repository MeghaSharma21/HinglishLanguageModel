 saiyad salaar sahu gaji yaa salaar sahu sultan mahmud gazanvi kii sena main senapati the jo 11veen shatabdi kii shuruyaat main dakshin asia aae the.
 salaar sahu ali ke putr mohammad ebn al-hanafiyah ke vanshaj the. unke pita kaa naam taahir ataulla tha or manyata ke taur par vah senapati saiyad salaar masood gaazi ke pita the. vah shaayad sultan mahmud gazanvi ke bahanoi the or unhone kathith taur par unki bahan sit-e-mullah se shaadi kar lee thi. vah sultan mahmud gazanvi ke saath sena ke senapati ke rup main bharat aae.
 vah lagbhag 1000 saal pehle satreekh main mar gaye or unhen vahaan dafnaaya gaya.
 salaar sahu kaa makbara satreekh main sthit he, jo uttar pradesh main barabanki se 8 kilometre dur he. garmi ke dauraan hindu maheene jyeshtha ke poornima ke dauraan log unki kabr par tirthayatra main ikatthe hote hai. paanch din lambey urs ke dauraan hajaaron bhakt prarthana karte hai. unka makbara "buudhe baba kii majaar" ke rup main janaa jaataa he.
