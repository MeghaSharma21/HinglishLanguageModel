 prashanth chandra mahalanobis ek prasiddh bhartiya vaigyanik evam sankhyikivid the. unhen duusari panchavarshiya yojana ke apane masaude ke kaaran janaa jaataa he. bharat kii swatranta ke pashchaat navgathit mantrimandal ke sankhyikee salaahkaar bane tathaa audyogic utpaadan kii teevr badhotaree ke jariye berogari samaapt karne ke sarkaar ke pramukh uddeshy ko puraa karne ke liye yojana kaa khaakaa khinchaa.
 mahalanobis kii prasiddhi mahalanobis duuri ke kaaran he jo unke dwaara sujhaayaa gayi ek sakhyikeey maap he. unhone bhartiya sankhyikeey santhaan kii sthapana kii.
 aarthik yojana or sankhyikee vikaas ke kshetra main prashanth chandra mahalanobis ke ullekhneey yogadan ke sammaan main bharat sarkaar unke janmdin, 29 june ko har varsh 'sankhyikee diwas' ke rup main manaati he. is din ko manaane kaa uddeshy saamaajik-aarthik niyojan or niti nirdhaaran main pro mahalanobis kii bhoomika ke bare main janata main, visheshkar yuva peedi main jaagrukta jagaanaa tathaa unhen prerit karnaa he.
