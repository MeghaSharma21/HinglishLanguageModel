 1997 pepsy independence cap bharat kii swatantraaa kii 50 veen varshganth kii smriti main yojit ek chauthai oriai cricket toornament tha. isamen newjiland, pakistan, srilanka or mejabaan bharat kii rashtriya cricket tiimon ko shaamil kiya gaya. toornament srilanka ne jeeta tha, jisane pakistan ke teenon final main sarvshereshth ko haraaya tha.
 round robin praaroop kaa upyog karte hue, prayek team ne ek baar dusron ko khelaa. newjiland ne toornament ke salaami ballebaajon main pakistan ko haraaya, lekin iske duusare maichon main haar gai. isi tarah, bharat newjiland ke khilaaf apane shuruaati match main safal rahaa, lekin srilanka or pakistan ko haar kaa saamana karnaa padaa. newjiland ke apane nuksaan ke baad, srilanka or bharat ke khilaaf pakistan kii jeet ne ise final ke liye arhata praapt karne main sakasham bana diya. srilanka ne pakistan ko ek ucch scoring match ganvaa diya, lekin final main pahunchane ke liye newjiland or bharat ko haraaya.
 
 pakistan or srilanka ke teenon main se ek final shrinkhalaa kaa sarvshereshth score he. pehle final chandigarh main tha, or duusare final kalakatta main eden garden main yojit kiya gaya. haalanki, srilanka ne pehle or duusare final main jeet hasil kii, toornament jeetne ke baad tisare final main khilne kii jarurat nahin thi.
