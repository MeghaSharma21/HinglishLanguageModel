 vaastushilp main chaap yaa mehrab vrutt ke chaap kii si sanrachana ko kehete hai jo do aalambon ke bich kii duuri ko patata he tathaa apane upar bhaar vahan karata he. jaise pathar kii diwaar main daravaaje ke liye banaayi gayi sanrachana. vaise to vaastushastra main chaap kaa upyog do hazaar varsh se bhi purana he, kintu iskaa vidhivat upyog pracheen romavaasiyon dwaara armbh hua dikhaai padataa he.
