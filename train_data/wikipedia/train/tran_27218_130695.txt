 aabnoos atyadhik saghan kaali kashtha tathaa tatsambandhit vrikshon ke liye ek saamaanya naam he. vaigyanik drushti se dekha jaae to ye diospiros vansh kii bahut si jatiyon se milti he, parantu kabhi-kabhi duusari baari, kaali lakadiyon ko bhi aabnoos kaha jaataa he. sarvshereshth aabnoos sambhavatah diacepyros ebenum se milane vaali lakdi he, jo dakshin bharat va srilanka kii muulavaasi he, iskaa srilankai aabnoos naam se vyapaar kiya jaataa he. 
 yah paudha tinduk kul abinesi kaa sadsy he. iske anya naam is prakaar hai: tinduk sfoorjaf, kalskandh, gaam, tendu .
 yah samast bharatvarsh main paaya jaataa he. yah ek madhyapraman kaa vriksh he jo anek shakhaon prashakhaon se yukt hota he tathaa saghan, sadahrith pattiyon se aachhadit hota he. tana kathor tathaa krishna varna kaa hota he. isaki pattiyaan chikani, ytaakar paanch se lekar aath inch tak lambii tathaa dedh do inch chaudee hoti hai. iskaa pushp shwetvarna or sugandhit hota he. fal gol, kathor tathaa gurchai rang kaa hota he. pak jane par iskaa rang pilaa or swaad madhur ho jaataa he. prayek fal main vrikkakriti sharife ke samaan chah se lekar aath tak beej hote hai. fal main kashay dravya hota he. kachche fal, chaal ore pushp main kashay dravya bahut hota he. iske atirikt isamen glutonik amla kii bhi 12.8% maatra hoti he.
 isaki lakdi kaa upyog imarti saamaan aadi banane main kiya jaataa he. aushadhi ke rup main isaki chaal, fal, beej tathaa pushp kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. isaki chaal kaa lep phodon par kiya jaataa he tathaa rakt srav hone par iskaa churna chhidakane se rakt band ho jaataa he. iske kwath kaa prayog raktavikar tathaa kaph-pit-janya rogon main karte hai. yah yonivsti pradar, raktasrav tathaa garbhashay kii shleshmakata ke shoth ko dur karne main bhi upyogi he. isaki chaal kaa kwath prameh, shighrapatan, raktpradar tathaa shwetpradar main bhi diya jaataa he. iske atirikt kusht, vishmajvar, sarpdansh or chamada rangane ke kaam main bhi isaki chaal kaa upyog kiya jaataa he.
