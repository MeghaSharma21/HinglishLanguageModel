 anoxijani prakashasanshleshan kuch bacteria jatiyon main prakaash urja ke prayog dwaara baad main prayog ho sakane vaali rasinic urja main parivartit karne kii kshamta ko kehete hai. yah paudhon dwaara kare jane vaale prakashasanshleshan se bhinn prakriya he. paudhe prakaash ke prayog se carbon dioxide or jal ko apane urja-prayogon ke liye sharkara or oxigen anuon main badalte hai, or yah oxigen paudhe se baahar chhod di jaati he. isaki bajaay anoxijani prakashasanshleshan main bacteria hydrogen sulphide kaa prayog karte hai or gandhak ke anu chhodte hai. anoxijani prakashasanshleshan karne vaale sabhi gyaat jeev anivaary avayujeev, jo oxigen kii adhik maatra upasthit hone par mar jate hai. haraa gandhak bacteria, laal va haraa reshedaar prakaashahari or jamuni bacteria aise jeevon ke udhaaharan hai.
