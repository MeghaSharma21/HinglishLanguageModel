 bhartiya kapaas nigam limited bharat sarkaar kii sanstha he jo kapaas ke kray, vyapaar evam niryaat se sambandhit kaaryon kaa nishpaadan karne ke liye hai.
 sisiai kii sthapana 31 july 1970 ko companez adhinium 1956 ke adheen panjikrut ek sarkari company ke rup main hui. apni sthapana kii aarambhik avadhi main nigam ko saarvajanik kshetra kii ek agency ke rup main vastrodyog ke vibhin ghatakon ke bich kapaas ke samaan sanvitaran kii jimmedaari saunpi gayi tathaa kapaas ke aayaat ke sarneekaran ke liye ek saadhan ke rup main bhi jimmedaari saunpi gayi.
 kapaas paridrishy main parivartan ke saath, nigam kii bhoomika or kaary-kalapon kii samay-samay par samiksha bhi kii gayi or usamen sanshodhan kiya gaya. vastra mantralay, bharat sarkaar dwaara varsh 1985 ke niti nideshon ke anusaar jab kabhi kapaas ke mulya samarthan str par pahunchne lage, tab nigam ko bharat sarkaar kii nodel agency ke rup main samarthan mulya kaary karne ke liye namit kiya gaya he.
 varsh 1985 kii vastra niti ke anusaar nigam kii vinirdisht bhoomika sankshep main neeche diye anusaar hai:
 bharat sarkaar kii nodel agency ke rup main, nigam apane aap ko samarthan mulya kaary sanchaalan kii sthitiyon kaa saamana karne ke liye taiyaar rakhata he. jab kabhi kapaas ke mulya, nunatam samarthan mulya ke str tak pahunch jate hai, tab kapaas kii kharid nunatam samarthan mulya kaary ke adheen bina kisi maatraatmak seemaa ke, kapaas kii kharid kii jaati he. in nunatam samarthan mulya kaaryon ke antargat kapaas kisaan apni kapaas upaj sisiai ko bech sakate hai or nigam is tarah kii kapaas kii kharid tab tak jari rakhega, jab tak kapaas mulya nunatam samarthan mulya par bane rahete hai.
