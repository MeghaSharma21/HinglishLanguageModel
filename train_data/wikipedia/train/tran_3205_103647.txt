 jyoti lakshmi ek dakshin bhartiya abhinetri or nrutyaangna thi. unhone varsh 1963 main 10 varsh kii aayu main tamil film ‘periya idathu penn’ ke saath apane abhinay karier kii shuruyaat kii thi. unhone adhikanshtah item sang or nakaaratmak bhoomikaayen kin.unhone tamil, telugu, kannada or malayalam bhashaaon kii filmon main abhinay kiya. unhone 300 se adhik dakshin bhartiya filmon main abhinay kiya tha. unhen aakhri baar tamil vayask kamedi ‘trisha illaanaa nayantara’ main dekha gaya tha. unki beti jyoti minaa bhi ek abhinetri sah nartki thi.
