 1992 ke sizan main 1992 cricket vishwa cap se thik pehle bhartiya rashtriya cricket team ne australia kaa daura kiya. team kaa netritv mohammad ajaharuddin ne kiya or 5 test match khele. australia ne test shrinkhalaa 4-0 jiti. ravi shastri ke doharaa shatak, sachin tendulkar kaa 114, perth main oochaal vaale pich par bharat ke liye ek bhartiya drushtikon se ullekhneey rahaa he jabaki anya bhartiya ballebaajon ne sangharsh kiya or kapil dev ko test maichon main 400 wicket lene vaale pehle bhartiya gendbaaj bane.
 test series se pehle, bharat ne australia se jude vishwa siries cap trikoniya vanade toornament main bhi hissaa liya or west indies ne vivian richards ko hataa diya, or gordon grinis or jeff dujon kii haalia sevanivruttata se chaabuk kar rahaa tha or ek apekshaakrit anaupchaarik team he. bharat ne apane 8 round robin maichon main se 3 jeeta or westeindies ke khilaaf bhi ek match taai diya. australia ke saath teen final main sarvshereshth main ve 2-0 se haar gaye.
