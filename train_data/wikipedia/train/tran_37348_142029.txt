 
 aresibo vedhashala ek prakaar kii radio doordarshee he jo purto rico ke aresibo shahar ke najadik sthapit kii gai he. yah rashtriya vigyaan foundation ke saath sahyog samjhaute ke tahat es. aar.ai. international dwaara sanchalit he. is vedhashala ko National Astronomy and Ionosphere Center ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. haalanki "NAIC" naam vedhashala or usamen kaam karne vaale karmchaaree donon ko sanderbhit karata he. vedhashala kaa 305 meter radio doorabeen vishwa kaa sabase bada ekal dwarak doorabeen he. isamen teen pramukh kshetron par anusandhan kiya jaataa he : radio khagol vigyaan, aronomi or radar khagol vigyaan |
 is vedhashala ke lokesan kaa upyog james bond kii prakhyat film ' golden ai ' main kiya gaya tha.
 aresibo doordarshee kii sahaayta se kai mahatvapurn vaigyanik khojein kii gai he -
 khagolashastra se sambandhit shabdaawalee
