 prakshepik abhighaat yaa bandook kii goli ke ghaav, shaareerik aghat kaa ek rup he jo kii hathiyaaron ke lagane se hota he. belistic aghat, ghaatak sabit hota he or lambii avadhi ke parinaamon kaa kaaran bhi ban saktaa he. utak vyavdhaan kii degree kaa nasht hona bandook se nikali projectil sthaayee guha banaam or asthaayee guha banaam ke aakaar par nirbhar karata he. guhikayan kii had projectil kii nimna visheshtaon par nirbhar karata he:
 bandook kii goli lagane ke turant baad aam taur par gambhir khun bahata he or iske saath-saath hypowelmic sadame kii sthiti bhi utpann hoti he. hypowelmic sthiti vah sthiti he jisame mahatvapurn angon ko oxigen kii aparyaapt vitaran praapt hoti he. yah isliye hota he kyunki bandook se aghat hone se bahut khun bahata he or isi kaaran oxigen nahin mil pati vishesh ango ko. vinaashkari prabhaav tab hote he jab goli mahatavpurn ango ko lagati he jaise dil, fefde, ridh kii haddi yaa mastishk kii kendriya tantrika ke tantra ko nuksaan pahunchataa he. bandook kii goli kii chot ke baad maut ke aam kaarnon main shaamil hypoxia, dil or mastishk par lagi chonte atyadhik hanikaarak hoti he. bandook kii goli ke ghaav aamtaur par ek badi degree main shaareerik or utak vighatan ke vinaash kaa kaaran hote hai. belistic aghat bahut se prakaar ke ho sakate he kyunki goli bhinn-bhinn ango par lagati he. gaiyr-ghaatak bandook kii goli ke ghaav aksar bahut lambaa or gaharaa asar chhodte he sharir ke ang par, kabhi-kabhi to umrbhar vah ang aabhij bhi ho jaataa he. bandook kii goliyon kaa aghat sharir ke alag-alag hisson par alag hota he kyunki har hisse main bandook kii goli ke pravesh or nikalne kaa sthaan alag hota he. goliyon kaa sharir main ghusane kaa maarg or unka vikhandan hona bhi apratyaashit hota he. bandook kii goli kii choton main goliyon kii gatishiltaa kaa adhyayan karne ke vigyaanan ko prakshepiki kaha jaataa he.
