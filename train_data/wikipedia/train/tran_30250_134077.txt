 vriddhavastha yaa boodhaapa jivan kii us avasthaa ko kehete hai jisamen umr maanav jivan kii ausat kaal ke sameep yaa usase adhik ho jaati he. vruddh logon ko rog lagane kii adhik sambhaavna hoti he. unki samasyaayein bhi alag hoti hai. vriddhavastha ek dhire-dhire aane vaali avasthaa he jo ki swabhaavik va prakritik ghatana he. vruddh kaa shabdic arth he badha hua, pakaa hua, paripakv.
