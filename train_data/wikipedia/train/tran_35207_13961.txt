 film samaaroh nideshaalay ek bhartiya sanghathan he jo bhartiya antarrashtriya film mahotsav, rashtriya film puraskaar or bhartiya panorama prastut karata he.
 yah 1973 main bharat sarkaar dwaara sthapit kiya gaya tha or soochna evam prasaaran mantralay ke ek bhaag ke rup main kaam karata hai. yah nideshaalay nai dilli main sthit hai.
