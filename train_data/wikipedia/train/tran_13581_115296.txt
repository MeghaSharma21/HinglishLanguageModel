 stadio olimpico rome, italy kii mukhya or sabase bada khel suvidha he. yah italvi rashtriya olympic samiti ke swaamitv main he, stadium zyaadaatar footbal maichon ke liye upyog kiya jaataa he. yah footbal club lezio or e.es. roma ke ghar stadium he or koppa italia final ke aayojan sthal he. sthal kuch rugby union maichon kii mejabani karata he, yah vartmaan main italy rashtriya rugby union team kaa ghar he. yah kabhi kabhi sangeet samarohon or vibhinn prakaar kii ghatnaon mejabani karata he.
 1960 main olympic ke dauraan is stadium main udghaatan samaaroh, band samaaroh or athlettic ghatnaon kaa aayojan kiya gaya tha. 1990 fifa vishwa cap ke final is stadium main yojit kiya gaya tha, jisamen pashchim germany ne vishwa cap jeetne ke liye argetina ko haraaya.
