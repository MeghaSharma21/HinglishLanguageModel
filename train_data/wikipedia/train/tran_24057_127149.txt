 aakeli athwa aakeli-a, bhartiya rajya rajasthan ke nagaur jile kii medta tehsil main sthit ek gaanv he.
 varsh 2011 ke janaganana aankadon ke anusaar yahan kii kul jansankhya 2,454 thi jisamen 1,262 purush or 1,192 mahilayen thim. aakeli main sakshartaa dar 54.57 % he jo rajasthan kii ausat sakshartaa 66.11% se kam he. yahan maanav linganupaat 945 he jo rajasthan ke ausat 928 se adhik he; 6 varsh se kam aayu varg main linganupaat 833 darj kiya gaya tha.
