 bhaarmal raja prithviraj kushwaha ke putr the. itihaaskaar 'tod' ne inhen 'biharimal' likhaa he. ye amer ke shaasha the. akbar kii adheenta sweekaar karne vaale tathaa akbar kii shahi manasabadari me pravesh karne vaale pehle rajput shaasha the. inhone haji khan vidrohi ke viruddh majanun khan kii sahaayta kii thi, isaliye majanun khan ne mughal samrat akbar se inhen darbar main bulawaane kii prarthana kii. pehali bhent main hi inkaa badshaah par acha prabhaav padaa or inhen akbar kii seva kaa avasar mila. baad main inkaa bhai rupasi bhi mughal samrat kii seva main upasthit hua. inhone apni putree bhaaramali/harkhabai kaa vivaah samrat akbar se kar diya. inke putr bhagavaandas or pautra raja maansingh bhi baad main akbar ke darbar main pahunch gaye. san 1569 ke lagbhag bhaarmal kii mrityu hui.
