 
 
 padma shri giriraaj kishoralghirirage Kishore 
 giriraaj ji kaa janm 8 july 1937 ko mujffarrenagar main hua, inke pita zameendaar the. giriraaj ji ne kam umr main hi ghar chhod diya or swatantra lekhan kiya. 
 shiksha : master of soshal work 1960, samaaj vigyaan santhaan, agra
 anubhav : 1960 se 1964 tak sevaayojan adhikari va probeshan adhikari u.pra. sarkaar
 1964 se 1966 tak elahabaad main swatantra lekhan
 july 1966 se 1975 tak kanpur vi.vi main sahaayak or upkul sachiv ke pad par sevarat.
 disan.1975 se 1983 tak IIT KANPUR main kul sachiv.http://www.iitk.ac.in/infocell/iitk/newhtml/padmaaward.htm
 1983 se 1997 tak vahiin par rachanaatmak lekhan kendra ke adhyaksh. 1 july 1997 avkaash grahan. rachanaatmak lekhan kendra unke dwaara hi sthapit.
 faillowship : samskruti mantralay, bharat sarkaar kii amerits faillowship - 1998-1999. bhartiya ucch adhyayan santhaan, raashtrapati nivas shimla main faillo - may 1999 -2001
 raashtrapati dwaara 23 march 2007 main sahitya or shiksha ke liye padmashri se vibhooshit
 maanad: 2002 main charpati shahuji maharaj vi.vi kanpur dwaara di.lit. kii maanad oopaadhi.
 sahitya academy, nai dilli kii kaaryakarini ke sadsy
 hindi salaahkaar samiti, railway board ke sadsy
 samprati: swatantra lekhan or sampadan 'akar'
 
 neem ke phool, chaar moti beab, paperwet, rishta or anya kahaniyaan, shahar -dar -shahar, ham pyaar kar len, jagattarani evam anya kahaniyaan, vald roji, yah deh kiski he?, kahaniyaan paanch khandon main ,'meri raajnneetik kahaniyaan' va hamaare maalik sabake maalik aatma ram and sans se prakashit
 log, chideaghar, do, indra sunen, daavedaar, teesri satta, yatha prastaavit, parishisht, asalaah, anridhwans, dhai ghar, yatnaghar, aath laghu upanyaas ashtachakra ke naam se do khandon main aatma ram and sans se prakashit.pehla girmitiyaa - gandhi ji ke dakshin africa anubhav par aadhaarit mahakavyatmak upanyaas
 narmedh, praja hi rahane do, chehre - chehre kiske chehre, keval meraa naam lo, jurm aayad, kaath kii top. bacchon ke liye ek laghunatak ' mohan kaa du:kha'
 samvadasetu, likhane kaa tark, sarokaar, cath-akath, samparni, ek janabhasha kii traasadee, jan-jan sansatta.
 doordarshan, en. di. tii. ve., bi.bi.si.landan, roj broadcasting stesan, durban tathaa morishas radio par saakshaatkaar. doordarshan dwaara film, o. ech. em. nederland dwaara documentary taiyaar.
