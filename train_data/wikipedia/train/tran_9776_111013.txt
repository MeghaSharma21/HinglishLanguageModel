 roosi vaayu sena roosi airospes bal kii ek shakha he, jise 1 agast 2015 ko roosi vaayu sena or roosi airospes raksha bal ke ke saath vilay kar diya gaya tha. aadhunik roosi vaayu sena multah 7 may 1992 ko boris yeltsin dwaara raksha mantralay ke nirmaan ke baad sthapit hui thi. haalanki, roosi sangh kii vaayu sena ko imperial roosi vaayu seva or soviet vaayu sena ke rup main bhi dekha ja saktaa he.
 roosi nausena ke paas apana swayan kaa swatantra vaayu sena roosi nausena vimaanan he. jo purv soviet aviatiya moezeno-marsakogo flota he.
 2009 main, roosi vaayu sena kii sanrachana puri tarah se air army-air vibhaajan yaa kor-air regiment kii pichhali sanrachana se cmand-air base sanrachana main badal di gai thi. roosi vaayu sena ab 4 sanchaalan ke kaman main vibhajit he, airospes dephence oparational stritegic kaman, sainya parivahan vimaanan kaman, or long range aviation kaman.
 roosi vaayu sena ke upkaran kaa sateek maatraatmak or gunatmak sanrachana agyaat he or aankadon main donon upyogi or aparivartaneey vimaan shaamil hai. saath hi bhandaaran yaa rigerv rakhe gaye upkaran ke bare main bhi zyada jaankaree nahin hai. fliteglobal ne anumaan lagaaya tha ki 2015 main suchi main lagbhag 3,547 vimaan the. roosi raksha mantralay ke anusaar, 2014 main vaayu sena main aadhunik hathiyaar kaa hissaa lagbhag 35% tak pahunch gaya tha. yah aankada 2016 ke ant tak 66% tak badha he or 2017 ke ant tak 72% tak.
 samrik adhyayan ke liye antarrashtriya santhaan dwaara pradaan kiye gaye anumaan bataate hai ki roosi vaayu sena ke payalat prati varsh ausatan 60 se 100 ghante or parivahan vimaan main payalat prati varsh 120 ghante udaan main bitaate hai.
 swatantra roosi vaayu sena ko soviet sangh kii rank main viraasat main mili, haalanki pratik chinh or vardi ko thodaa badal diya gaya tha. roosi vaayu sena roosi ground bal ke samaan rank sanrachana kaa upyog karti he.
