 apollo 11 vah antariksh udaan thi jisane chandramaa par pehle insaan, nil armastrang or edwin "baz" eldrin. junior ko 10 july 1969 ko antarashtriya samay anusaar 20:17:39 baje utaara tha. sanyukt rajya america kaa yah mishan maanav itihaas va antariksh anusandhan ke itihaas main ek bady uplabdhi ke rup main dekha jaataa he.
 16 july 1969 ko merit island main sthit kennedy spes centre laanch complex 39 se udaan bharne vaala apollo 11 nasa ke apollo abhiyaan kaa paanchava maanvi mishan va teesraa chandra abhiyaan tha. iske karmidal ke rup main armastrang commander va eldrin lunar madul payalat or maaikel colins cmand modyul payalat the. armastrang or eldrin chandramaa par tranquility ke sagar main utre or chaand par 21 july ko kadam rakhane vaale pehle insaan ban gaye. unka lunar modyul igal 21 ghante va 31 minute chaand kii prashthabhumi par rahaa or colins cmand sarvice modyul colombia main chaand ke upar bane rahe. 24 july ko tino antariksh yatri prithvi par prashanth mahaasaagar main utre or apane saath 21.5 kilogram ke chaand ke namoonay laae.
 apollo 11 ne america raashtrapati jon eff. kennedy kaa chaand par soviet unin se pehle utarane kaa sapana saakaar kar diya jisaki unhone 1961 main guzarish kii thi.
 iske baad 1969 se 1972 ke darmiyaan chah apollo mishan chaand par bheje gaye jisamen se paanch chaand par utre main sakasham rahe.
