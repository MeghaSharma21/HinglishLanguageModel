 ek million yaa ek hajaar hajaar, 999,999 ke baad or 1,000,001 se pehle kii praakrit sankhya he. yah naam italvi se vyutpann hua he, jahaan 1,000 ke liye mile or 1,000,000 ke liye milione, "ek bada hajaar" kaa istemaal hota he.
 vaigyanik sanketan main ise 1×106 yaa sirf 106 ke rup main likhaa jaataa he. SI ikaayiyon kaa vyavhaar karte samay SI upsarg mega kaa istemaal karke bhi bhautik raashiyon ko vyakt kiya ja saktaa he. udhaaharan ke liye, 1 megawatt 1,000,000 wat ke baraabar hota he.
 "million" shabd chote paimaane or lambey paimaane kii sankhya paddati main saamaanya he, jo badi sankhyaon se bhinn hote hai or donon paddatiyon main iske alag-alag naam hote hai.
 million kaa istemaal kabhi-kabhi angreji bhashaa main kisi bahut badi sankhya kii upama ke rup main hota he, jaise "nevar in e million ears" or "you'aar van in e million", yaa ek atishyokti ke rup main, jaise "ai'haev walkd e million miles" .
 Il milione marco polo kii apni china yatra ke varnan kaa sheershak he. yah naam polo ke dhan va jan-saadhaaran kii kahaniyon ke aadhaar par uske upnaam se aayaa mana jaataa he.
 yadhyapi is par aksar jor diya gaya he ki ek million kii sateek ginati karnaa samay or ekaagratha kii aavashyaktaa ke kaaran ek bahut hi thakaaoo kaam hoga, aniyamittaaon yaa gathan ke prabhaav kii andekhi karke sankhya ko anumaanit pariman main "down tu saise yaa chote aakaar" main laane ke kai tareeken hai.
