 avantivardhan avanti ke pradyotkul kaa antim raja jo sambhavat: magadharaj shishunag kaa samakaleen tha. vaise, puraanon ke anusaar shaishunag vansh kaa pravartak shishunag is kaal ke paryaapt pehle hua, parantu sinhalai itihaas ke anusaar, jo sambhavat: adhik sahi he, vah bimbisar se kai pidiyon baad hua. magadh or avanti ke bich vatson kaa rajya tha or deergh kaal tak magadh-koshal-vatsa-avanti kaa paraspar sangharsh chalaa tha. phir jab vatsa ko avanti ne jeet liya tab magadh or avanti prakrutyamitra ho gaye the. or ab magadh or avanti ke sangharrsh main avanti ko apane munh kii khaani padi. usee sangharsh ke ant main magadh kii senaaon dwaara avantivardhan parajeet hua or madhyapradesh kaa yah bhaag bhi magadh ke haath aa gaya.
