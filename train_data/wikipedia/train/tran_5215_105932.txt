 vaalapay bharat ke goa rajya ke uttar goa jile main sthit ek nagar or nagpalika he. yah satari taluk kaa mukhyalay nagar he. pashchimi ghat is kasbe ke purv main sthit he.
 bharat kii 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar, vaalapay kii jansankhya 8,532 he. kul jansankhya main purush 51%, or mahilaayein 49% hai. yahan kii ausat sakshartaa dar 78% he jo rashtriya sakshartaa dar 59.5% se adhik he jisamen se purush sakshartaa dar 83% or mahila sakshartaa dar 72% he. 10% log 0-6 varsh ayuvarg ke hai.
 is kasbe main saarvajanik parivahan ke rup main nijee or sarkari basen uplabdh hai. bas sevaayen acchhee hone ke kaaran goa ke kisi bhi bhaag main bahut kam pareshaaniyon ke saath pahunchaa ja saktaa he. yahan kaa nikatatam railway stesan thivim main he jo vaalapay se bas dwaara adhiktam 45 minute kii duuri par sthit he. is stesan se mumbai or dakshin bharat ke liye railgadee pakadi ja sakati he.
