 bandi chhod diwas sikh tyohaar he jo ki deepawali ke din padataa he. deepawali tyohaar sikh samudaay dwaara aitihaasik rup se manaaya jaataa he. guru amar das ne ise sikh utsav mana he. 20veen sadi se sikh dharmik netaaon dwaara diwali ko bandi chhod diwas kaha jane laga. shiromani gurudwara prabandhak cameti dwaara ise maan liya gaya. is naam kaa sambaddh guru hargobind kii rihaai se he jinhen jahangir dwaara swatantra kiya gaya tha. bandi chhod diwas ko diwali ke samaan hi manaaya jaataa he, jisamen gharon or gurudwaron ko roshan kiya jaataa he, uphaar dena or parivaar ke saath samay bitaana hota he.
