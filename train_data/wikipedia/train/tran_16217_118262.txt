 neshnal aronutics and spes administration ne 17 june, 2016 ko eks-57 hybrid electric research vimaan launch kiya. is vimaan ko ‘maxwell’ upnaam diya gaya he.
 is vimaan main propeller ke taur par 14 electric motor lagi hai tathaa iske pankh bhi vishesh rup se taiyaar kiye gayi hai. nasa is vimaan ko naveen propalzan takanic ke liye prayog karegaa. 
 nasa ke is prayog se eks-57 se vimaanan kshetra main ek nae yug kii shuruyaat ho sakati he. 
 varsh 1947 main eks series kaa pehla vimaan eks-1 laayaa gaya jo dhwani kii gati se tej tha. iske baad eks-1 pariyojna se america kii sainya jaruraton, audyogic kshamtaaon or anusandhan suvidhaon tathaa yuddh ke uparaant aavashyaktaaon kii purti hetu sahaayta praapt hui.
