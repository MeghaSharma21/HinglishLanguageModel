 yadi kisi sthaan par sthit kisi sthir aaweshit kan par bal lagata he to kehete hai ki us sthaan par vidyut-kshetra he. vidyut kshetra aaweshit kanon ke dwaara utpann hota he yaa samay ke saath parivartit ho rahe chumbakeey kshetra ke kaaran. vidyut kshetra kii avadharana sabase pehle maaikel farade ne prastut kii thi.
