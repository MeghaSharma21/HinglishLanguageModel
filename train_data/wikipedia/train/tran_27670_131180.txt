 marathon kaa yuddh pracheen yunaan main lada gaya jisamen faras kii sena parrast hui. is ghatana kii soochna dene hetu ek senic ne pehali baar marathon kii dod lagaai thi.
 yah yuddh 490 i.pus. main yunaan va faras ke bich marathon ke maidan main hua tha. darius faras kaa raja tha. vah atyant parakramee tha. pashchim main igian sagar se lekar, purv main sindhu nadi tak, va uttar main sithian ke maidaanon se lekar, dakshin main misr kii nil nadi tak uske rajya kaa vistaar tha. 
 uski samrajya lipsaa, dinondin badhti hi ja rahi thi. jab usne asia ke pashchimi kinare par based greek logon, jinhen ionian kaha jaataa tha, ko adheenta kaa sandesh bheja, to unamem se thibs va ijina kii janata ne to sweekaar kar liya, parantu athens va sparta ke logon ne virodh prakat kar diya. tab darius ne baari jahaji sena sahit yunaan par chadhaai kar di. isi se marathon ke yuddh kaa aarambh hua.
 irani sena ne maarg main aane vaale tapu bhi jeet liye, ubia main aakar eritreaa ko gherkar usamen aag laga di. phir maratham]] ke maidan main padaav daal diya. platica ke unani logon ne sparta ko sahyog sandesh bheja. sandesh vaahak 150 mile 48 ghanton main pahunche. tab sparta ke sahyog se, tathaa pratibhashali militiyads ke kushal netritv main 11000 kii unani sena ne, 20000 kii farsi sena ko buree tarah haraa diya. 
 is yuddh main, unani sena main, bahut se gulam bhi the, jinhen kaha gaya tha, ki yuddh jeetne par, unhen mukt kar diya jaayegaa.is yuddh se senapati miliads kii prasiddhi bahut bad gayi. or darius ko bahut aghat pahunchaa, va usne badla lene kii joardaar taiyaari kii, parantu usase pehle hi uskaa dehanth ho gaya.
