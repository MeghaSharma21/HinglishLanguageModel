 militant socialist movement vamapanthi raajnneeti vaali morishas kii raajnneetik party he. iske dhyey hai samaajwaad evam raajnneetik janatantr. is party ne 2014 ke saamaanya chunaavon main 69 main se 34 seat jeetne vaali sabase badi raajnneetik party kaa sthaan praapt kiya tha. kaalaantar main vipakshi party ke 6 saansadon ne bhi is party main aagat kiya or vartmaan bahumat 40 ho gaya. 
 emsam desh kii teen sabase badi raajnneetik partiyon main se ek he, jinamein se shesh do hai: lebar party evam movement militant morician .
 prayek party saamaanya chunaavon ke liye bahut se pratyaashiyon ko maidan main utaarti hai. morishas main 60 nirvachan kshetra hai evam 10 siten prati 2 rodrigs siten tathaa 8 siten sarvshereshth luzar kii hoti hai, jisase ki baraabar jaateey pratinidhitiv sunishchit ho sake.
