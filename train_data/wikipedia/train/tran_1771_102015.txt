 digaaro bhashaayen yaa digaru bhashaayen yaa uttari mishmi bhashaayen bharat ke arunachal pradesh rajya or tibet main mishmi samudaay dwaara boli jane vaali kuch bhashaaon kaa ek chota bhashaa-parivaar he. is baat par vivaad he ki yah tibeti-burmi bhashaa-parivaar kii ek shakha he yaa swayan ek swatantra bhashaa-parivaar he. milte-julate naam ke baavjood inkaa dakshini mishmi bhashaaon se koi sameepi sambandh nahin he.
