 creadihealth ek bhartiya swasthya seva kii jaankaree or chikitsa sahaayta kii website he. yah doctoron, aspataalon or anya diagnostic centre se croudsorcing kaa yuz karke marijon kaa pratikriya ikattha karata he. website par rogi ko apni beemaari ke bare main bataane suvidha he or vahaan se doctors uskaa samaadhaan karte he, farvari 2017 main, company kii sthaneey aspataal ke saath saajhedaari hui or gudgaanv jila main ambulance kii fast seva dene kii suvidha launch kiya, yah bharat kii pehali aisi suvidha jo chikitsa sahaayta ke saath ambulance kii bhi seva detii he.
 creadihealth kii sthapana ravi virmaani, saurabh uchheja or gaurav gaagar ne oktober 2013 main kii thi, rajat malhotra iske sabase bade niveshak the. tathaapi praarambhik nivesh ravi virmaani ne kii thi or unheen ko yah edia ek duusari swasthya seva company se aayaa tha jahaan par ravi virmaani kaam karte the.
