 brahm hindu darshan main is saare vishwa kaa param satya he or jagat kaa saar he. wo duniya kii aatma he. wo vishwa kaa kaaran he, jisase vishwa kii utpatti hoti he, jisamen vishwa aadhaarit hota he or ant main jisamen villin ho jaataa he. wo ek or adwitiya he. wo swayan hi paramgyan he, or prakaash-strot kii tarah roshan he. wo niraakaar, anant, nity or shaashvat he. brahm sarvashaktimaan or sarvyaapee he. bramh hindi main brahm kaa galat uccharan or likhaawat he.

 vah brahm hi jagat kaa niyanta he.
 aparabrahm brahm kaa wo rup he, jisamen anant shubh gun hai. wo puja kaa vishay he, isaliye use hi ishwar mana jaataa he. advait veydant ke mutabik brahm ko jab insaan man or buddhi se jaanane kii koshish karata he, to brahm maaya kii vajah se ishwar ho jaataa he.
