 wiliam severd baroj america ke beet andolanke prakhyat upanyaaskaar, kavi, aalochak tathaa shabd-karigar hai. unako 20veen sadi kaa sabase prabhavi paap kalchar chintak mana jaataa he. unhone gadhy likhane kaa ek nayab tarika avishkaar kiya tha jise cat-ap methd kaha jaataa he. likhane ke baad pandulipi kainchi se kaat kar ve phirase use ulatpher karke sajaate thi or vahinse ek nayaa gadhy nikaal lete the. naye-naye maadakon kaa seven karke uske prabhavamein likhane kaa prayaas karte the evam maadak sangrah karne ke liye dakshin america or africa main ja kar bahut din tak bas jate the.mizorike dhani parivaar main janme baroj saahitiyik banane ki iccha se 1943 main niw yark ja basen jahaan unase alen ginsburg tathaa jaik keruyakse parichay huya. phir kya kahana tha. tinon mil kar jo andolan shuru kiye use hi beet andolanke naam se janaa jat he.1951 main unki dusri patniko hatya ke aropmen ve funs gayi the. is dukhad ghatana se unka lekhan mukt nahin ho paaya.
