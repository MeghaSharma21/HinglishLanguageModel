 
 hylobatis giban ke chaar jeevvaigyanik vanshon main se ek he. yah naam unani bhashaa se liya gaya he, jisamen "hule" kaa arth "van" or "betis" kaa arth "chalanevaala" hota he, yani naam kaa arth "van main chalane vaala" hota he. yah gibanon kaa sabase vistrut vansh he or dakshini china ke unnan praant se lekar pashchim or madhya java main failaa hua he. hylobatis gibanon ke mukhon par aksar shwet rang ke baalo kaa ek chakra hota he or anuvaanshik drushti se inamen 44 gunasutra hot hai.
