 matasaan gajipur janapad kaa ek gaanv he. yah ganga ke tat par gajipur se jamaaniyaan jane vaale maarg par sthit he. yah hindi ke prasiddh sahityakar kubernath raay kii janm bhoomi or punya bhoomi donon he. isa gaanv kaa bhartiya swatantraaa andolan ke itihaas main vishesh yogadan rahaa he. 'kamdhenu' 'tarun bharat' jaise rashtravaadi patron ke sampadak or prasiddh swatantry yoddha pandit batuka dev sharma kaa janm bhi yaheen hua tha.
