 bharat kii mahila hockey team ne japan ke kaakamigaharaa main asian champions trophy main rajat padak jeeta. final main bhartiya team japan se 0-1 se haar gai. bhartiya team ne is toornament main shandaar pradarshan karte hue apane se oonchi variyata praapt china ko 4-2 or malaysia ko 5-1 se haraaya tha. japan ke khilaaf use lig match main bhi 1-2 se haar kaa saamana karnaa padaa tha. bharat kii golkeeper rajani itimarpu ko toornament main sarvshereshth golkeeper kaa puraskaar mila. bharat ko iske alaava fere play trophy bhi mili.chaar tiimon kii is pratiyogita main japan ko swarna padak, jabaki malaysia ko kaansya padak mila.
