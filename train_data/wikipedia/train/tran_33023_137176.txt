 mohammad ali purv america peshewar mukkebaaz the, jinhen khel itihaas main duniya kaa sabase bada heviveet mukkebaaz kaha jaataa he. ali 3 baar heviveet champion rahe hai. unhen bibisi se sports persenality of da centuri tathaa sports elstrated dwaara sportsman of da centuri kaa sammaan mil chukaa he. akhaade main ali apane footwork or mukke ke liye jane jate the.
 ali teen baar leniel championships jeetne vaale iklaute vishwa haiviveet champion hai inhone ye kitaab 1964, 1974, or 1978 main khitaab jeeta. 25 farvari, 1964, or 19 sitambar, 1964 ke bich, ali ne haiviveet boxing champion ke rup main shashan kiya. inhen "mahanatam" upnaam diya gaya. ye anek aitihaasik boxing maichon main shaamil rahe. inamen se sabase ullekhneey "fite of da centuri ", "super fite 2 " or " thrilla in manila " banaam apane pratidwandvi jo frezier, " rambal in da jangal" banaam jorge forman aadi hai. ali ne 1981 main mukkebaaji se sanyas le liya.
 clay kaa janm kentki main hua tha or 12 saal kii umr main inhone prashikshan shuru kar diya. 22 kii umr main unhone lalaa liston se vishwa haiviveet championships, 1964 main jeet hasil kii, iske kuch hi samay baad, clay islam main parivartit ho gaye or apane "das" naam ko badal "ali" kar liya or 1960 ke dashak ke naagarik adhikaar andolan ke dauraan africa americanyon ke liye jaateey gaurav kaa sandesh diya. 
 clay ne 29 oktober, 1960 ko apane peshewar karier kii shuruyaat kii jisamen inhone tanni hansekar par chah daur mennirnay jeet hasil kii. tab se 1963 ke ant tak, clay ne petakar 19-0 kaa ricard banaaya jisamen 15 jeet nock out se praapt kin. inhone tony esapreti, gym robinsan, doni fliman, alonzo johnson, jorge logaan, villi besamenof, laimor clark, dag jones or henry kupar sahit mukkebaajon ko parajeet kiya. clay bhi 1962 ke match main apane purv prashikshak or diggaj mukkebaaz archi moore ko bhi haraaya.inhone pehali baar 1964 main phir 1974 main or phir 1978 main vishwa championships kaa kitaab jeeta tha.
 1966 main, haiviveet khitaab jeetne ke do saal baad, ali ne apane dharmik vishwaason or vietnam yuddh main america bhagidaari ke virodh kaa hawala dete hue america sainya main jane ke liye manaa kar diya, iske baad inhen giraftaar kar liya gaya. inhen masauda kaa paalan na karne kaa aarope paaya gaya or inke mukkebaaji khitaabon ko chin liya gaya. unhone safaltaapurvak america sarvocch nyaayalay main apil kii jisane 1971 main inaki sajaa ko palat diya lagbhag chaar varsh tak ye boxing se baahar rahe. yuddh par sawaal uthaane ke kaaran ye yuva peedi ke liye ek iken ban gaye.
 ali ne 1979 main pehali baar sanyas kii ghoshnaa kii, lekin 1980 main unhone ring main vaapasi kii lekin nae vishwa champion laire homes se ye haar gaye. mukaable ko 11wein round main roak diya gaya.ali 1981 main boxing se vaastavik rup main ritairement le liya. inke khate main 56 jeet 37 nokout or mahaj 5 haar shaamil hai.
 1996 ke atlanta olympic main inhen olympic jyoti prajvalit karne kaa gaurav pradaan kiya gaya. inhen 1960 ke rome olympic kaa nayaa swarna padak bhi pradaan kiya gaya. america ke purv raashtrapati jorge dablyoo bush ne 2005 main inhen presidential medal of freedom se sammanit kiya. 2012 ke landan olympic ke shubharambh samaaroh main bhi ye maujuud rahe.
 inhone chaar shaadiyaan kii thim or inke nau bachche hai. inamen inaki ek beti laila ali peshewar mahila mukkebaaz hai.inhone 1964 main sonji roi se pehali shaadi kii. do varsh baad 1966 main ye donon alag ho gaye.
 inhone 26 farvari 1965 ko inhone apana naam mohammad ali rakh liya tathaa khud ko ashwet muslim ghoshit kiya. inhone apane muslim dharm kii baat rakhte hue america sena se judane se manaa kar diya. sena se judane se manaa karne ke kaaran neuyork state athlettic kamishan ne inhen boxing ke license se vanchit kar diya.
 huston kii juri ne inhen dhokhadhadi ke aarope main 5 varsh kii jail or 10 hajaar dollar kii sajaa sunai. inkaa passport bhi jabt kar liya gaya tha parantu apil karne ke baad is sajaa ko 10 din kaa kar diya gaya. inhone agast 1967 main belinda boid se duusari shaadi kii. unhen teen ladkiyaan or ek ladkaa hua. 1976 main ye donon bhi alag ho gaye. ali ne veronica porsh se 1977 main teesri shaadi kii. inaki chaar ladkiyaan hui. inaki shaadi 1986 main tut gai.1986 main yolanda williams se inhone chauthi shaadi kii. inhone ek betaa bhi god liya.
 1984 se ye parkinson rog se peedit the. isse pehle peshaab kii nalee main sankraman kii shikaayat kii vajah se disambar 2014 main bhi ye aspataal main bhartee hue the.2 june 2016 ko inhen saans kii samasya ke kaaran aspataal main bhartee karavaaya gaya tha. haalaanki inaki sthiti ko acha bataaya gaya, par inaki halaat karaab hoti chali gayi. inaki maut kaa kaaran septic shock ko bataaya gaya.3 june 2016 ko inhen mrit ghoshit kar diya gaya.
