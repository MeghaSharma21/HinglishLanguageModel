 harisingh burdak rajasthan pradesh ke sikar jile main palathana gaanv ke swatantraaa senaanee the. aap burdak gotra jat parivaar main paidaa hue. aapane sikar jile main kisaanon ko jagiradaaron ke shoshan se mukt karaane ke liye andolan kiye or kai baar jail gayi. aapane 1934 main khudi gaanv main tathaa 1935 main kudan gaanv main kisaan andolan main aguwai kii. kisaan andolan main aguwai ke kaaran sikar ke raav raja ne jila badar kiya tathaa 14 mahine ke liy devgarh kii jail main band rakhaa. aap praja mandal sikar tathaa jat-panchayat sikar ke sakriya mahatv purna netaa the.
