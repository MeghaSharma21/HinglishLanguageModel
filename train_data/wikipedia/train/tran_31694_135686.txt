 nagaland kaa mukhyamantri bhartiya rajya nagaland kaa pramukh hota he. yahan par nagaland ke mukhyamantriyon kii suchi di gai he.
 1963 se, ab tak aath vibhinn partiyon ke gyaarah vyakti mukhyamantri is pad par virajman ho chuke hai. rajya ke pehle mukhyamantri naga nesanalist organijeshan ke pi shilu ao the. vartmaan mukhyamantri nefiu rio nesanalist democratic progressive party naamak dal se hai or is pad par 8 march 2018 se virajman hai.
