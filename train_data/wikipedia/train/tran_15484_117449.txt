 kendriya taapan pranali main ek sthaan par taral ko garam kiya jaataa he or puure bhawan ke sabhi aantarik bhaagon ko garam kiya jaataa he. kendriya taapan ko kuch anya pranaaliyon ke saath milakar jab bhawan kii jalwaayu ko niyantrit kiya jaataa he to ise taapan, sanvathan tathaa vaataanukoolan kehete hai.
