 sanyukt rajya america main arlington kii sthiti
 arlington, sanyukt rajya america ke taxs rajya kii tarant counti main sthit ek nagar he. yah daalas-fort worth-arlington mahanagar kshetra ke bheetar aata he. dow robert clak is nagar ke mahayor hai. arlington, texas rajya main saatvaan or sanra america main pachaasavaan sabase bada nagar he.
 taxs vishvavidhyalay, arlington main sthit he. iske atirikt tarant counti mahavidhyalay kaa dakshinpurvi campus or arlington baptist mahavidhyalay yahan sthit hai. finaks vishvavidhyalay bhi arlington se hokar jane vaale interstate 20 par sthit he.
 arlington kaa taxs vishvavidhyalay, taxs vishvavidhyalay vyastha kaa teesraa sabase bada santhaan he. is samay vishvavidhyalay main 2007 kii sharad ritu tak 24,888 vidhyaarthee namankit hai or yah arlington kii ek moolyawaan sampatti he jo isaki sthaneey arthvyastha ke liye bahut mahatvapurn he.
 arlington, taxs rangers besabaal team or daalas coubois kaa grih-nagar he. cubois kaa nayaa stadium 2011 main soupar boul XLV kii mezbaani karegaa.
 arlington nagarpalika hawaii adda, arlington main sthit he. 
 arlington, sanra america kaa sabase bada nagar hai jahaan par koi vistrut saarvajanik parivahan seva uplabdh nahin he. matadaataaon ne teen baar sthai root kii bas transit pranali asweekaar kar diya he. lekin arlington main chaar transit pranaliyaan he jo vibhinn janasankhyikeey samoohon ko sevaayen pradaan karatin hein. handitron varisht nagarikon or viklangon ke liye he. arlington ke hotel apane yahan aane vaale atithiyon ke liye chalaai jane vaali bason ke kiraye kaa bhugtan karte hai. arlington kaa texas vishvavidhyalay apane chaatro ke liye shatal seva pradaan karata he.
