 madras share bazaar bharat ke chennai shahar main sthit ek share bazaar he. san 1937 main sthapit hone vaala yah share bazaar bharat kaa chauthaa or dakshin bharat kaa pehla share bazaar tha. 2001 main isamen 30.9 arab rupayon kii kareed-bikri hui jo bharat ke mukhya bombay share bazaar or bharat ke rashtriya share bazaar ke lenden kaa keval 3.5% tha.
 1996 main masheba kaa purna computarikaran kar diya gaya or usapar chennai-bhar kii 120 dalal companiyaan sakriya ho gai. is share bazaar main 1,785 companiyaan panjikrut hai jinke share khride-beche ja sakate hai. yahan rozaana 10 baje subah se 3:30 baje dopaher tak vyapaar chalata he.
