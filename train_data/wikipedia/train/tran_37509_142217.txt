 pulah, jiskaa bair namakan "betaa arse majoris" he, saptarshi taramandal kaa paanchava sabase roshan tara or prithvi se dikhne vaale sabhi taron main se 78vaan sab se roshan tara he. yah hamase qareeb 79 prakaash-varsh kii duuri par sthit he or prithvi se iskaa ausat saapeksh kantimaan +2.34 he. is taare kaa naam maharishi pulah par rakhaa gaya he.
 pulah ko angrezi main "merock" bhi kaha jaataa he. yah arabi bhashaa ke "al-maraq" se liya gaya he jiskaa arth "kati" he. arab khagolashastra main is taramandal main jo kaalpanik bhaaluu kii akruti banaai jaati thi yah tara uske kati-bhaag main sthit he.
 pulah ek safed rang kaa A1 V shrenee kaa mukhya anukram tara he. isaki sataha kaa taapmaana 9,800 calvin he jo ki kaafii adhik he. pulah kii nihit chamak suraj kii 69 guna he. iskaa dravyaman hamaare suraj ke dravyaman kaa lagbhag 3 guna or vyas hamaare suraj ke vyas kaa 2 guna he. is taare ke ird-gird ek malbe kaa chakra he, thik jaise abhijeet or minasy taron ke ird-gird paaya gaya he. haalaanki pulah ke paas goi gair-saureey grah nahin mila he, aise dhul or malbe ke chakra kaa matlab aksar yah hota he ke yahan grahon kaa nirmaan ho rahaa he, isliye abhi is taare ke ird-gird parikrama karte kisi grah ke milane kii sambhaavna bani hui he.
