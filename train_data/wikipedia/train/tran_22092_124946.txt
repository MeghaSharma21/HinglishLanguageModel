 kila raay pithora dilli main sthit ek durg he, jise chouhan raajaaon ne lalkot ke kile ko mohammad gori se jitkar, badhaaya, evam phir iskaa naam kila raay pithora rakhaa.
 dilli kaa naam raja dhillu ke "dilhika" ke naam se mana gaya he, jo madhyakaal kaa pehla basaaya hua shahar tha, jo dakshin-pashchim seemaa ke paas sthit tha. jo vartmaan main mahrauli ke paas he. yah shahar madhyakaal ke saat shaharon main sabase pehla tha. ise yoginipura ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he, jo yogini ke shashan kaal main tha.
 lekin isko mahatva tab mila jab 12veen shatabdi main raja anangpal tomars ne apana tomars rajvansh lalkot se chalaaya, jise baad main ajmer ke chouhan raja ne mohammad gori se jitkar iskaa naam kila raay pithora rakhaa. 1192 main jab prithviraj chouhan mohammad gori se taraaen kaa yuddh main parajeet ho gayi the, tab gori ne apane ek das ko yahan kaa shashan sanbhaalane hetu niyukt kiya. vah das qutubuddin aibak tha, jisane 1206 se dilli sultanate main das vansh kaa armbh kiya.in sultaanon main pehle sultan qutubuddin aibak jisane shashan tantra chalaaya is dauraan usne qutub minar banvaanaa shuru kiya jise ek us shashan kaal kaa pratik mana gaya he. usne praathamikta se hindu mandiron evam imaaraton parkabja kar ke yaa tod kar unpar muslim nirmaan kiye. isi main lalkot main bani dhruv stambh ko qutub minar main parivartan evam kuwwat ul islam masjid, aadi kaa nirmaan bhi shaamil he.
 baad main uske vansh ke bad, khilji vansh ke allaudin khilji yahan alaai minar banavaani armbh kii, jo qutub minar se doguni unchi banani prastaavit thi, parantu kinhin karanvash nahin ban paayi. usee ne siri kaa kila or hauj khaas bhi banawaaye.
