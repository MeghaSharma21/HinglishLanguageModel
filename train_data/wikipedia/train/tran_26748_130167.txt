 kisi desh kii utpaadan vyavastha se antim upabhokta ke haathon main jane vaali vastuon yaa desh ke poonjigat sadhano ke vishuddh jod ko hi rashtriya aay kehete hai. kisi desh ke nagarikon kaa sakal gharelu evam videshi output sakal rashtriya aay kehalaata he. 
 rashtriya aay kii paribhaasha - 
 kisi bhi arthvyavastha main vastuo or sevaao kaa pravaah, utpaadan or uname hone vaali vruddhi ko rashtriya aay se joda jaataa he 
 rashtriya aay kii gananaa nimna vidhi se karte he - 
 aay vidhi – utpaadan ke karkon ke milane vaale pratifal kaa yoga 
  samasya – wahi par lagu ho saktaa tha, jahaan utpaadak kaarak gyaan he .
      sangathit kshetra – jahaan utpaadan kaa registration ho 
      asangathit kshetra – utpaadan karne vaale jiskaa registration ho ,utpaadan ke kaarak, aay kii ghoshnaa nahin karte 
               jaise – bharat main adhiktar log aise hate   is vidhi se keval sarkari or privet job tak gananaa simit rahaa 
 vyay vidhi – 
   aay  =  vyay   +   bachat 
         yadi arthvyavastha ke har bindu par hone vaale vyay gyaat ho 
  agar vyay ,bachat gyaat ho to in donon ko jodakar rashtriya aay kaa anumaan laga sakate he .
     samasya – bharta main bill na lene dene kii samasya, zyaadaatar kharch registerd nahin hote the 
 utpaadan vidhi – rashtriya aay kii gananaa main sabase sahi tarika banakar ubhara 
  kisi vittiya varsh main antim utpaadit vastu or seva kaa maudrik, rashtriya aay he.
 atah rashtriya aay -  1. ek vittiya varsh main, 2. utpaadit antim vastuo va sevaao kaa shudhd mulya kaa yoga
