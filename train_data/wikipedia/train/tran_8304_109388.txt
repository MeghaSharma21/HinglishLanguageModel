 meruvakrata yaa scoliosis ke rog ke kaaran merudand sidhi na rahakar kisi ek taraf jhuk jaati he. isse zyaadaatar chaati or peeth ke neeche ke hisse prabhavit hote hai. ise 'ridh vakrata' yaa 'parshvakubjata' bhi kehete hai.
 aamtaur par scoliosis kii shuruuaat bachapan yaa kishoravastha main hoti he. jab sharir kii vruddhi yaa badhat ruk jaati he, tab tak yah jhukaav saaf najar aane lagata he. kai baar dekha jaataa he ki ridh mudakar angreji ke ‘’ akshar ke aakaar kii ho gai he. is samasya ke kisi thos kaaran kaa abhi tak pataa nahin chal sakaa he. yadi samay rahete iskaa upachaar na karaayaa jaae to yah sharir main vikruti va viklaangata paidaa kar sakati he. ridh kii haddi main kisi janmajaat asaamanyata ke chalate yaa ridh kii haddi ke durghatana kaa shikaar ho jane par bhi scoliosis ho sakati he. kabhi-kabhi shroni pradesh ke jhuk jane ke kaaran ek pair chota, ek pair bada ho jaataa he. natijatan ridh bhi jhuk jaati he. agar scoliosis ke sahi kaaran kaa pataa chal jaae jaise ki yah slipdisk kii vajah se he to ‘bedrest’ ke jariye, pairon kii lambaai asmaan hone kii vajah se he to vishesh kism ke orthopedic juuton ke upyog se isse bachaa ja saktaa he. yadi ridh kaa jhukaav lagaataar hota rahe to orthopedic sarjan se paraamarsh karke shalyakriya bhi karai ja sakati he.
