 rokad bahi  praarambhik lekhe kii pustak he jisamen mudra kii praptiyon tathaa bhugtanon kaa lekha kiya jaataa he. rupaya kahan-kahan se kitna aata he kahan-kahan kitna chalaa jaataa he, phir shesh kitna batch jaataa he - ise prakat karne ke liye rokad bahi banaayi jaati he.
 rokad kii samast praptiyaan debit paksh main or samast bhugtan credit paksh main likhe jate hai. rokad bahi vah pustak he jo rojnaamachaa va khaataa-bahi donon ke uddeshy kii purti karti he. rokad bahi ko sahaayak pustak ke saath-saath ek pradhan pustak bhi mana jaataa he. chunki rokad bahi main len-dein kii praathmik pravishti kii jaati he isliye ise 'mul pravishti kii bahi' bhi kaha jaataa he.
