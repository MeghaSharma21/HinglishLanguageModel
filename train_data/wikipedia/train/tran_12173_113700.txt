 kavak vigyaan mashroom, fafundi, nae nae saanche, likene, or khamir sahit kavak ke sabhi prakaar ke adhyayan he. forencik kavak vigyaan aapraadhik jaanch main istemaal hota he. 
 kavak vigyaan, kavak ke gyaat vikaas dar kaa upyog kar sakshya kaa pataa lagane pradaan karne main, or laashon kaa pataa lagane main se baar yaa maut yaa ghatnaon kaa akalan karne main prayog kiya jaataa he. 
 kavak jaivik yuddh main prayog kiya jaataa he.
