 maslo pyramid yaa maslo kaa aavashyaktaa padanukram abraham maslo dwaara pratipaadit ek manovaigyanik siddhanth he jo unhone 'e thiari of human motiveshan' naamak apane granth main 1943 main prastut kiya tha. yah siddhanth na keval manovigyaan ke kshetra main, balki vigyaapan ke kshetra main bhi prasiddh hua. apane is siddhanth main maslo ne maanav aavashyaktaaon ke kai str bataaye jo ye hai- daihik aavashyaktaayen, suraksha aavashyaktaayen, sanvardhan evam prem kii aavashyaktaayen, atmasamman aavashyaktaayen, aatmsaat aavashyaktaayen, bhavathit aavashyaktaayen. unhone kaha ki maanav kaa abhipreran isi kram main gati karata he. arthath moolbhoot aavashyaktaaon ke puraa hone par hi ucchstareey aavashyaktaayen janm letin hai. 
 maslo kaa siddhanth unki 1954 main rachit pustak 'motiveshan and personality' main apane purna rup main saamne aayaa. yadhyapi yah aavashyaktaaon kaa yah padanukram ab bhi samajashastreey anusandhan, prabandhkeey prashikshan aadi main bahut mahatv rakhata he, phir bhi bahut seemaa tak iskaa sthaan sneh siddhanth ne le liya he.
