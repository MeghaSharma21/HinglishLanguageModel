 buddhanath kathmandu ke purvi bhaag main sthit prasiddh buddha stupa tathaa tirthasthal he. esa mana jaataa he ki yah vishwa ke sabase bade stupon main se ek he. yah vishwa dharohar main shaamil he. 
 stupa 36 meter ooncha he or kala kaa sundar udhaaharan prastut karata he. is stupa ke bare main mana jaataa he ki jab iskaa nirmaan kiya ja rahaa tha, tab kshetra main bhayankar akaal padaa tha. isliye pani na milane ke kaaran os kii buundon se iskaa nirmaan kiya gaya.
