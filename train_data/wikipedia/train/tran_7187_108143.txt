 1956 europea cap final akhil europea footbal pratiyogita, ab europea cap ke rup main janaa yuiefee champions lig main pehle kabhi antim tha. yah real madrid spain or france kii sted di rams ke bich tha. yah 38,000 logon ke saamne 13 june 1956 park des princes paris main khelaa gaya tha. match ek bejod lagaataar paanch europea cap khitaab record par chalaa gaya jo real madrid, ko 4-3 se samaapt ho gaya.
