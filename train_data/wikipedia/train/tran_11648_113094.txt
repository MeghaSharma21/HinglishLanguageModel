 Rebel Heart ek irish samooh gaan mandali "the course" kaa ek gaan he. yah unki teesri album "in blue" main shaamil hoti hai . yah gaan ek vaadya sangeet yani -instrumental - style kaa udahaaran hai . is gaan par celtic - pashchim europe kii ek nasli kii vishisht samskruti kaa prabhaav he. is gaan main violin, bodhran athwa tin wistale - in vaadyon kaa sammelan he.
 bibisi ireland tivi kii 4-kisht vaali ek tivi dharrahvaahik rebel heart ke liye sharon corr ne is gaan kii  rachana kii jiske baad unhone is gaan ko is album main prayukt kiya. januari 2001 main is gaan ko  - vaadya sangeet main atyaadhik prabhaavshaalee pradarshan - kii shrenee - main graamy namakaran mila .
 "corrce " ke website main, sharon kahati hai - 
 "maine is gaan ko piaano main rachaaya jab ham "tok on corners " kii recording kar rahe the. yah bahut samay thak aise hi padaa hua tha. thub bibisi - apane nae autuman drama  ke liye - nayi sangeet kii khoj main thi. is gaan main irish dhun or liy tho tha hi, hamane isamen tin wistale ithyaad kaa vishleshan kiya or yah jachaa."
