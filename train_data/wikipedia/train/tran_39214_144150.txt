 sampoornanand sanskrit vishvavidhyalay uttarapradesh ke varanasi nagar main sthit ek sanskrit vishvavidhyalay he. yah purvatya shiksha evam sanskrit se sambandhit vishyon par ucch shiksha kaa kendra he.
 yah vishvavidhyalay multah 'shaskeey sanskrit mahavidhyalay' tha jisaki sthapana san 1791 main kii gai thi. varsh 1894 main saraswati bhawan granthalay naamak prasiddh bhawan kaa nirmaan hua jisamen hajaaron pandulipiyaan sangruhit hai. 22 march 1958 ko uttar pradesh ke tatkaaleen mukhyamantri dow sampoornanand ke vishesh praytn se ise vishvavidhyalay kaa str pradaan kiya gaya. us samay iskaa naam 'varanasey sanskrit vishvavidhyalay' tha. san 1974 main iskaa naam badalakar 'sampoornanand sanskrit vishvavidhyalay' rakh diya gaya. 
 bharat or nepal ke mahavidhyalay iske vishvavidhyalay banane ke pehle se hi isse sambaddh the. keval uttar pradesh ke sambaddh mahavidhyalayon kii sankhya 1441 thi. is prakaar yah santhaan na keval bharat ke liye balki duusare deshon ke mahavidhyalayon ke liye bhi vishvavidhyalay ke samaan hi tha.
 is vishvavidhyalay ke saath 1200 se adhik sanskrit vidhyalay evam mahavidhyalay sambhddha hai.
 is vishvavidhyalay ke vartmaan kulapati pro. rajaraam shukla hai jinhone 24 may 2018 se vishvavidhyalay main kaarybhaar grahan kiya he. pro. shukla isse purv biechayu ke vaidic darshan vibhaag, sanskrit vidya dharm vigyaan sankaay ke professor ke rup main karyrat the.
