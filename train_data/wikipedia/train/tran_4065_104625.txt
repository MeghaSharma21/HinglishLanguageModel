 archensa sanyukt rajya america ke dakshin-purvi kshetra main sthit rajya he. iskaa naam kunpav indian logo kaa ek algonquin naam he. archensa chah rajyon ke saath seemaa rakhata he, apane purvi hisse main mukhyatah mississippi nadi ke dwaara paribhaashit seemaa ke saath. rajya main isaki oworks or chhachita parvat he, jo kii america ke grih uche jameenon ke saath mississippi nadi ke purvi neeche bhugol ke janak he. rajdhani or sabase adhik aabaadi vaala shahar little rock, rajya ke madhya bhaag main sthit he. angrezi adhikarik bhashaa he. 2017 main isaki jansankhya 30,00,942 anumaanit kii gai he. 
 archensa main kshetra ke hisaab se 29waan sabase bada or aabaadi ke hisaab se 33waan sabase bada america rajya he. 15 june 1836 ko isko rajya banaaya gaya tha. 1861 main archensa sanyukt rajya se alag ho gaya or america grihayuddh ke dauraan parisangheey rajya america main shaamil ho gaya. 1868 main sangh main lautne par, gulaami or vriksharopan arthvyavastha par iske nirbharta ke kaaran rajya ko bhugatna padaa or rajya aarthik or saamaajik rup se peeche rah gaya. dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke baad archensa ne arthvyavastha main vividhta laana shuru kiya or ab wo apane seva udyog, vimaan, murgi paalan, ispaat, paryaton, kapaas, or chaaval par nirbhar karata he.
