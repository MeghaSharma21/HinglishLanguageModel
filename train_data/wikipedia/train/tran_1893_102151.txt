 faishan 2008 kii bhartiya drama film he. iske sah-nirmaataa or sah-lekhak madhur bhandarkar hai tathaa isamen mukhya bhoomika main priyanka chopra or kangana ranavat, mugdha godse, arjan bajva or arbaz khan sahaayak bhoomika main hai. iske saath-saath kai asal jivan kii faishan model ne apane aapako film main kirdaar ke taur par nibhaayaa he. film kii kahani ek chote shahar kii mahatvakankshi model meghna maathur ke irdagird gumti he. film darshaati he ki kaise maathur ek supermodel banati he or is lakshya ko praapt karne ke liye use jivan main kitni kathinaayeeyaan jhelni padati hai. faishan main bhartiya faishan udyog or kai model ke karier ke utaar-chadhaav ko bhi dikhaaya gaya he.
