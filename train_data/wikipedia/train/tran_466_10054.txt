 lalgudi saptrushi ramamritam ek varisht tamil upanyaaskaar the. unhone 300 laghukathaayen, 6 upanyaas or 10 nibandh sangrah likhe hai. 29 oktober 2007 ko apane ikyanweven janmdin par unka nidhan ho gaya. inke dwaara rachit ek sansmaran chintandi ke liye unhen san 1989 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
 ve 1916 main janme, tathaa lalgudi ke mulnivaasee the. ve manikodi yug ke lekhakon main se ek the. unhone tab likhnaa shuru kiya jab ve bees varsh ke the. pehle unhone angrezi main likhaa baad main tamil kii ore mud gayi. unhone punjab neshnal bank main tees varshon tak kaary kiya tathaa sevanivritti ke baad chennai main bas gayil.sa.r. ne teen saal tak vohini pikchars main tankangya ke taur par kaary kiya, jisane tab kuch pramukh telegu filmon jaise vande maataram, sumangali or devtaa kaa nirmaan kiya. tab dakshin bhartiya film nirdeshak ke.ramanoth ne unase anurodh kiya ki ve apni pratibha ko barbaad na hone den, yani yah ishaara kiya ki filmon main karier banane kii unki ummid utani laabhprad nahin hogi. antatah ve ek bankkarmi ban gayi parantu lekhan jari rakhaa.
