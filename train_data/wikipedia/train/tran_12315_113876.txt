 kazakh bhashaa madhya asia main kazakh logon dwaara boli jane vaali ek turki bhashaa he. yah turki bhashaa-parivaar kii pashchimi yaa kipachak shakha kii bhashaa he or kaaraqalpaq or nogai bhashaaon se milti-julati he. 2009 kii janaganana ke anusaar ise kazakhstan main lagbhag 1 karod log bolte hai or 2000 main ise kazakhstan se baahar bolne waalon kii sankhya 30 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. kazakh ko kazakhstan main rashtrabhasha hone kaa darja hasil he. kazakhstan ke alaava ise china, mongolia, afghanistan, tajikistan, turki, turkmenistan, ukraine, rus or iran ke kuch samudaay bhi bolte hai. bhugolic drushti se kazakh tian shaan parvaton se lekar caspian sagar tak ke vishaal kshetra main boli jaati he.
 kazakhstan main lambe arase tak soviet sangh kaa niyantran rahane se vahaan ise sirilik lipi main likhaa jaataa he. china ke pashchimi shinjiyang praant main basane vaale 10 lakh kazakh log ise uigur bhashaa main prayog hone vaali arabi-farsi lipi ke saath likhte hai. 2006 main kazakhstan ke raashtrapati nursultan nazarabaev ne kazakh ko roman lipi main likhane kaa prastaav rakhaa or isapar adhyayan kiya gaya. 2007 main kazakh sarkaar dwaara jari report ke anusaar yah lipi kaa badlaav 30 karod dollar ke karche ke saath 10-12 saalon main kiya ja saktaa he. soch-vichaar ke baad raashtrapati main apana faisla sunaaya ki is samay kazakh kii lipi main koi badlaav nahin kiya jayega or sirilik lipi kaa istemaal jari rahegaa.
