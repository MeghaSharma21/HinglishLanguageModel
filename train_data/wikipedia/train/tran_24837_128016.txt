 gathbandhan sarkaar ek sansadeey sarkaar kii cabinet hoti hai, jisamen kai raajnneetik dal sahyog karte hai, jisase gathbandhan ke bheetar kisi bhi ek dal kaa prabhutv kam rahata hai. is vyavastha kaa aam kaaran yah diya jaataa hai ki koi dal apane balboote sansad main bahumat praapt nahin kar saktaa. ek gathbandhan sarkaar rashtriya sankat ke samay bhi banaai ja sakati hai, jo sarkaar ko ucch str kii raajnneetik auchitypurnataa yaa samuhik pahchaan de sakati hai, jo use chaiye va saath hi, aantarik raajnneetik vigrah ko kam karne main bhi bhoomika nibhaa sakati hai. aise samay main, dalon ne "sarvadaleey gathbandhan" banaayen hai. yadi koi gathbandhan gir jaataa hai, to ek vishwaas mat hota hai yaa avishvaas prastaav liya jaataa hai.
 jab kisi aam chunaav main kisi akele dal ko saafe bahumat nahin miltaa, tab dal yaa to sansadeey bahumat ke samarthan se gathbandhan cabinet banaate hai, yaa phir alpamat cabinet banaate hai, jisamen ek yaa anek dal ho sakate hai.
