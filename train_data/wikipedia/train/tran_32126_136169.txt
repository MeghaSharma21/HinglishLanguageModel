 
 1995 asia cap, paanchaven asia cap toornament tha, or doosraa sanyukt arab emirate main sharajah main yojit hone vaala tha. toornament 5-13 april, 1995 ke bich hua. chaar tiimon ne toornament main hissaa liya: bharat, pakistan, srilanka or bangladesh.
 1995 asia cap ek round robin toornament tha jahaan prayek team ne ek baar duusare ko khelaa or antim do tiimon ne final main jagah banane ke liye kwalifing kiya. bharat, pakistan or srilanka ke paas round robin charan ke ant main chaar ank the, lekin bharat or srilanka ne behatar ran-rate ke aadhaar par final ke liye arhata praapt kii. bharat ne srilanka ko 8 wicket se haraaya, jo lagaataar teesri baar asia cap main jeet darj karata rahaa.
