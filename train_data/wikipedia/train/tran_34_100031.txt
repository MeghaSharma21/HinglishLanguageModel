 imaaraton main jal, gas, jalanikasi, shauchagrih, rasoighar, snanagrih aadi ke liye nalon kii judai, marammat, jhalaai aadi kii kala ko nalkarm yaa nalakaari kehete hai. angreji main ise pluming kehete hai, kyonki is kaam main sise kaa vishesh prayog hota he. vaise ab tin, jaste, tambe, aadi anya dhaatuon kaa prayog bad gaya he.
 nalakaari kaa sabase adhik prayog to jalstrot, nadi or kuon se pani laakar gharon main vividh prakaar ke upkarnon dwaara vitaran karne main hi hota he. sadak yaa gali main lage mukhya nal se harek imaarat ke liye pruthak vitaran nalee lagaai jaati he. mukhya nal se is nalee kaa jod bhoomi se lagbhag dhai foot neeche rakhana avashyak hota he. ghar ke bheetar is nalee se jahaan jahaan ghar main jal kii aavashyaktaa ho anya naliyaan lagaai jaati hai or jal nikaalane ke liye tonti yaa chilmachi aadi lagaai jaati he. shauchakaksh main jalabahaav sandas ke liye ek alag tank ghar kii chat par lagaai jaati he jisase shauchakaksh ke liye pani har samay sulabh rahe.
 varsha kaa yaa anya phaltu pani ghar se baahar nikaalane ke liye dhalwaan lohe ke nal diwaar ke saath lagaae jate hai. shauchakaksh ke baahar ke nal bhi jo dooshit hawa ko nikaalane ke liye lagaae jate hai dhalwaan lohe ke 3fa fa vyas ke hote hai. paashchaatya dhang ke gharon main harek shayankaksh ke saath snanagar or shauchakaksh hote hai. deevaaron ke upar lage nal bure dikhaai dene ke kaaran unhen deevaaron main zairi khodakar bheetar dabaa diya jaataa he or upar plasters karke rangarogan kar diya jaataa he.
 gas yaa garam pani vahan karanevaali nalee ispaat yaa pitwaan lohe kii banaai jaati he or uske uske chudeedaar jodon main sise or tel kaa mishran bharaa jaataa he. adhiktar inako bhi diwaar main chipa diya jaataa he.
