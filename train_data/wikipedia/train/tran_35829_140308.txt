 bahamani sultanate dakkan kaa ek islamic rajya tha. isaki sthapana 3 agast 1347 ko ek turk-afagaan soobedaar allaudin bahaman shah ne kii thi. iskaa pratidwandi hindu vijaynagar samrajya tha. 1518 main iskaa vighatan ho gaya jiske falswaroop - golkonda, bijapur, bidar, biraar or ahmednagar ke rajyon kaa udai hua. in paanchon ko sammilit rup se dakkan sultanate kaha jaataa tha.
