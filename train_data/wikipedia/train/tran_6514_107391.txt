 shahi hamaam kaa nirmaan mughal badshaah shahjahan ne apni begum mumtaj mahal ke liye tapti nadi ke kinare burhanpur ke kile main karavaaya tha. yah smarak farukhi kile ke andar sthit he. is imaarat ke bichon bich ashtakoniya snaan kund he. yah snanakund khooni bhandare kii jal aapurti pranali se judaa hua he. is smarak kii chhaton par rangeen mughal chitrakala darshneey he.
 shahi hamaam do hisson main banta hua he. pehle hisse main ek badi si hauj he, jisamen sangemarmar kaa fawwara laga hua he. is hauj main thande or garm pani kii vyavastha kii gai thi. garm pani ke liye hauj ke baahar kai vishaal bhatthiyaan bani huin thim. inamen bade-bade kadahon main pani garm kiya jaataa tha. ye garm pani hauj ke channelon main daalaa jaataa tha. iske saath-saath duusare channel se thanda pani aata tha. shahi hamaam ke duusare hisse main teen haujen or banaai gayi thim, jinamein gulab, kewda or khas ke itr kaa pani bharaa rahata tha.
 hamaam main roshni ke liye diya rakhaa jaataa tha jisaki roshni diwaron par jade kaanch ke tukdon or hamaam main rakhe hiron se takaraakar hamaam ko prakaash se jagamaga detii thim.
