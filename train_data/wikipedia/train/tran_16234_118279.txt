 australia kii rashtriya cricket team ne 2004-05 ke sijan main bharat kaa daura kiya or bharat ke khilaaf oktober or november 2004 main chaar maichon kii test series kheli, australia ne shrinkhalaa main 2-1 jitkar ek drow match khele, bharat kii mitti par apni pehali shrinkhalaa jeet unke 1969-70 daure ke baad se jeet darj kii. bhavishya ke australian captain maaikel clark ne pehali pari main 151 ran banane ke pehle match main apana pehla test shatak match khelaa. shrinkhalaa ke chauthe match main, clark ne duusari pari main 9 ran ke liye 6 wicket liye.
