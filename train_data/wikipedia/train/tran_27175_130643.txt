 
 spice jet ek kam keemat vaali vimaanan seva he jiskaa malikana haq sun group of india ke paas he. iskaa panjikrut karyaalay chennai tathaa tamilnadu main he evam vyavsaayik karyaalay gudgaanv haryana main he. isne apni sevaayen san 2005, may se shuru kii tathaa 20102 tak yah bazaar hissedari ke maamle main air india, kingfisher, airline tathaa go-air ko pachhad ke, bharat kii teesri sabase badi vimaanan seva ban chuki thi. spice jet bas ek yatri varg ke anusaar bane vimaanon kaa hi sanchaalan karti he. yatri seva ke saath saath spice jet usee vimaan se saamaan ke parivahan kii suvidha bhi pradaan karti he.
 spice jet vartmaan samay main rojaanaa 330 udaanon kaa sanchaalan kar rahaa he jo kii 57 lakshyon ke liye hoti hai. iname se 47 sthaan bhartiya hai tathaa shesh 10 sthaan antarrashtriya hai. paanch saal tak apni sevaayen pradaan karne ke baad spice jet ko bhartiya vimaanapttan praadhikaran ke dwaara antarrashtriya udaan bharne kii anumati de di gayi thi. spice jet nen aarambh main dilli se kathmandu tathaa chennai se colombo kii flites shuru kii. pehali antararashtri udhaan 7 oktober 2010 ko dilli hawaii adde se bhari gayi.
 spice jet nen pehla aadesh 20 agali peedi ke bowing 737 -800 es vimaanon ke liye march 2005 main diya jiskaa delivery kaal 2010 tak tha. navambar 2010 main punah spice jet nen 30 bowing 737 -800 vimaanon ke liye aadesh diya.
 farbari 2014 ke aankadon ke anusaar is airline ke paas nimnavat vimaan hai-
 is airline nen nimnlikhit vimaan bedon kaa sanchaalan kiya he-
 is vimaan seva main yatriyon ke manoranjan ke bharpoor intjaam kiye gaye hai. vahin spice jet maiks naamak ek ofer bhi is vimaan seva ke dwaara chalaaya jaataa he jisamen ki yah boarding ke dauraan khan paan kii suvidha, booking ke samay seat chunane kii suvidha tathaa prayikta ke anusaar air port par check in kii suvidha pradaan karti he.
