 afghanistan cricket team ke sitambar or oktober 2016 main bangladesh daure ke liye teen ek divasiy antarrashtriya match khilne ke liye nirdhaarit he. yah ek test khilne vaale zimbabwe se duusare paksh ke khilaaf afghanistan kii pehali purna shrinkhalaa hogi or donon pakshon ke bich pehali dwipaksheeya shrinkhalaa hogi. vanade siries se pehle vahaan bangladesh cricket board ekadash or afghanistan fatullah main ke bich ek divasiy abhyaas match hoga.
