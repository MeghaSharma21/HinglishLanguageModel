 new hampshire purvottar sanyukt rajya ke new england kshetra main ek rajya he. rajya kaa naam england kii county hampshire par rakhaa gaya he. yah dakshin main masachusets dwaara, pashchim main varmont, purv main main or atlantic mahaasaagar or uttar main canadai praant quebec se seemaa rakhata he. janvari 1776 main, yah great britain ke adhikaar se swatantra sarkaar sthapit karne vaali pehali colony bana tha. chah maheene baad yah mul 13 rajyon main se ek ban gaya jisane sanyukt rajya america kii sthapana kii.
