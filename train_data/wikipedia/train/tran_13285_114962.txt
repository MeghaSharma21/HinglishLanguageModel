 konyak bhashaayen dakshin-purvi arunachal pradesh tathaa uttar-purvi nagaland ke kshetron main boli jane vaali bhashaayen hai, jinhen konya naga samudaay ke log bolte hai. inhen 'uttari naga bhashaayen' bhi kehete hai. ye bhashaayen tibeti-burmi bhashaa-parivaar kii hai. isamen koi chah bhashaayen aatin hai jinamein konya tathaa nocte pramukh hai.
