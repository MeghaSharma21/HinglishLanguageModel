 ladkana yah ek shahar he jo pakistan ke sindh praant main he.
 ladkana main muslim samudaay ke bahusankhyak hone ke baavjood badi sankhya main hindu dharm ke log hai. isi kaaran se pakistan piples party ke netaa ke adhyaksh bilaval bhutto or yahan kii prantiya sarkaar ne 2014 desh main pehali saarvajanik rup se yahan ke hindu mandir ke darshan bhi kiye or deepawali manaakar apane desh kii vishwa ke aage ek udaar chhavi prastut karne kaa prayaas kiya.
