 
 2016-17 riginal 4-din toornament riginal 4-din toornament, westeindies cricket board ke deshon ke liye gharelu pratham shrenee cricket pratiyogita ke 51 wein sanskaran he. pratiyogita 11 november 2016 se 18 april 2017 tak chal rahaa he. dablyooisibi chah maichon main is tarah ke rup main khelaa ja rahaa he ke saath pratiyogita main phir se pesh kiya he din/raat judanar.
 chah tiimon ke toornament par chunaav lard rahe hai - barbados, guyana, jamaica, liward dweep samooh, trinidad or tobago, or windward dweep.
