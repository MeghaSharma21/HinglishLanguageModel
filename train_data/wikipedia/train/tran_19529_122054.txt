 ayurveda ke moulik siddhanton main sapt dhaatuon kaa bahut mahatv he. inase sharir kaa dhaaran hota he, isi kaaran se inhen 'dhaatu' kaha jaataa he . ye sankhya main saat hai -
 sapt dhaatuyen vaataadi dosha se kupit hontin hai. jis dosha kii kami yaa adhikta hoti he, sapt dhaatuyen tadnusaar rog athwa shaareerik vikruti utpann karti hai.
 aadhunik ayurvedagy sapt dhaatuon ko 'patholangical basis aanf disijes' ke samatuly maanate hai.
