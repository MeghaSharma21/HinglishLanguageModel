 utpal kumaar basu  bangla sahitya ke bhukhi peedi aandolan ke ek pramukh kavi hai. 1960 tak vah krittivas goshtee ke sadsy the. bhukhi peedhee aandolan main yogadan ke kaaran unke khilaaf bhi kolkata police saman nikaala tha. iske chalate unhe yogamaya devi kalege ke praadhyaapak ke naukari se barkhaast kiya gaya tha. tatapashchaat vah videsh chale gayi evam das saal ke liye kavita likhnaa tyaag diya. das saal baad kolkata laut kar unhone jo kavitaayen prakaash karne lage, sahitya jagat main maano tahalaka machaa diya. bhukhi peedhee aandolan ke samay likhe unka poper samadhi ko saraahaa gaya he.
