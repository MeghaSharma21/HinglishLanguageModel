 tripitak korea yaa tripitak koriana yaa palmaan daejanggayeong buddha tripitak dharmagranthon kaa ek aitihaasik korian sangrah he. ise 13veen sadi isvi main 81,258 kaath ke chapai saanchon par taraasha gaya tha. yah korian bhashaa kii hanja lipi main vishwa kaa sabase purana or sampoorn tripitak he. kul milaakaar isamen 5,23,82,960 akshar hai jinhen 1,496 vishyon main or 6,568 adhyyon main yojit kiya gaya he. har chapai sancha 70 semi chaudaa or 24 semi lambaa he. inaki motaai 2.6 se 4 semi tak he or har ek kaa vazan 3 se 4 kilo ke bich main he. is puure sangrah ko dakshin korea ke dakshin geongsang praant main sthit haeinsa buddha mandir main surakshit rakhaa gaya he. 'tripitak' sanskrit bhashaa kaa shabd he jiskaa arth 'teen petiyaan' hota he or yah buddha dharm shiksha ke teen stambhon ko kaha jaataa he - sutara, vinay or abhidharm .
