 abodh 1984 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. ye film maadhuri dikshit kii pehali film hone ke liye jaani jaati he.
 bholi, balavat or akalik, gauri apane mata-pita ke saath ek chote se shahar main rahati he. ek meley main, vah shankar ke saath ek bahes main aati he. iske turant baad, gauri ko bataaya jaataa he ki uski shaadi kii vyavastha kii gai he. pehle gauri romanchit hoti he, phir use pataa chalata he ki uskaa duulhaa shankar he, lekin aakhirkaar is vivaah ke liye wo maan jaati he. shankar ke nivas par pahunchne ke baad, vah shankar ke chote bhai ke saath apana adhikansh samay bitaati he. uske pati ko yah mahasoos hua ki gauri abhi tak paripakv nahin hui he. vah uske saath ghanishtha hone main asamarth he. gauri jald hi ub jaati he, or apane mata-pita ke ghar main phir se chali jaati he, jahaan vah kai maheenon tak rahati he. yahan vah vastav main badi hoti he, shaadi or antarangta ke mahatv ko mahasoos karti he. tab vah shankar ke ghar laut aati he, jahaan uskaa swaagat hota he - haalanki kaaphi garmjoshi se nahin. gauri ko yah mahasoos hona shuru ho gaya ki uske bachakaanepan ne uske or shankar ke bich duuri banaai he. vah duusare shahar main adhyayan karne ke liye chalaa gaya he. kai maheenon tak parivaar ko uske bare main koi khabar nahin milti he. shankar ke pita gajanan singh chatravaas kaa daura karte hai jahaan unka betaa rahata he. haalanki, gajanan ne paaya ki shankar ne chatravaas chhod diya he. 3 maheene ke liye, shankar gaayab ho gaya. ant main, shankar gauri ke bachapan kii dost ratna se ittifaaq se miltaa he. ratna se baat karne ke baad, shankar ko pataa chalata he ki gauri paripakv ho gai he or usase pyaar karti he. vah gauri se milane ke liye waapas daud chalaa jaataa he. ve donon main sulah ho jaati or donon khushi se rahete hai
