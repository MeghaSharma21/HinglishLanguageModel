 teratali rajasthan kaa prasiddh lok nrity he. yah kamd jaati dwaara kiya jaataa he. shiv ke taal or paravati ke lay se taal shabd bana he kamd. striyaan sharir par teraa manjira bandh kar is nrity ko karti he. purush ramdev or hinglaj ke bhajan gaate hai. yah lok nrity parampara se kamd jaati karti aa rahi he.
 mewar, bikaner, marwar kshetron kii kamad jatiyon main teraa tali nrity kaa prachlan hai.
 is nrity main chautaara,dholak va taal vaadan purush karte hai.
