 dravyasangrah  9-10 veen sadi main likhaa gaya ek jain granth he. yah sauraseni praakrit main acharya nemichandra dwaara likhaa gaya tha. dravyasangrah main kul 58 gaathaayen he. inamen chah dravyon kaa varnan he: jeev, pudgal, dharm dravya, adharm dravya, akaash or kaal dravya. yah ek bahut mahatvapurn jain granth he or jain shiksha main ek mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaataa he. dravyasangrah granth ko aksar yaad kiya jaataa he kyonki isamen sankshipt par bahut achche se dravyon ke swaroop kaa varnan he.
 dravyasangrah main vyavhaar nay or nishchay nay kii apeksha se kathan kiya gaya he. granth kaa angrezi bhashaa main anuwaad karne vaale, sarat chandra ghoshaal ne dravyasangrah ko 3 bhaagon main baanta tha â€”pehle bhaag main chah dravyon kaa varnan, duusare main saat tatva or tisare bhaag main moksh yaa kii mukti maarg kaa nirupan he .
 dravyasangrah kii gaatha 49 se 54 main panch parameshthi or unki visheshtaon kaa varnan he. 
 dravyasangrah par likhi gayi tikaaon main pramukh tikaa brahmadev kii he.
