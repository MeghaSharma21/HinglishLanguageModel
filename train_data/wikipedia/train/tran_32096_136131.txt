 vam-hast orthodox spin gendbaaz saamanytah dakshin hast ballebaaz ke liye hawa main apvaah karti hui gend daalate hai or tapa khaane ke baad yah gend ballebaaz ke pichley stump kii ore ja lage. hawa main apvaah or ghumaav akraman takanic hai.
