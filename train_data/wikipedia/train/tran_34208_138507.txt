 
 ghanatv
 hindchin, france ke upniveshon kaa samooh kaa naam tha. iskaa aadhikarik naam tha hindchini sangh . 
 1887 main vietnam ke teen kshetron ko cambodia ke saath jod diya gaya. 1893 main laos or 1898 main kwangachau vaan ko joda gaya. 1902 tak isaki rajdhani sigon thi, phir 1939 tak hanoi, or 1945 tak da laat. uske baad isaki rajdhani phir se sigon ho gai.
 dwitiya vishwayuddh main frans ke haar ke baad, iskaa prashasan vishi sarkaar dwaara kiya gaya, or march 1945 tak yah japani kabze main tha. 1941 se viyat minh naamak samyavaadee sena ne ho chi minh ke netritv japaniyon ke kilaaf vidroh shuru kiya. agast 1945 main unhone swatantraaa kii ghoshnaa kii or frans ke kilaaf yuddh jari rakhaa, ho pratham hindchin yuddh bana.
 sigon main samyavaadee-virdohi vietnam rajya ko 1949 main swatantraaa pradaan kiya gaya, jiske raja baao dai the. 9 november 1953 ko laos samrajya or cambodia samrajya swatantra hue. 1954 ke geneva sammelan ke baad francisi vietnam se nikal gaye or francisi hindchin kaa ant ho gaya.
