 ethilin ek hydrocarbon he jiskaa anusutra C2H4 or H2C=CH2 he. yah ek rangahin, jvalansheel gas he jisaki gandh halki mithi kasturi hoti he. 
 rasinic udyogon main ethilin kaa bahutayat main upyog hota he. 2006 main iskaa vaishvik utpaadan 109 million ton se bhi adhik tha jo kisi anya carbonic yogic se adhik he. ethilin ek mahatvapurn prakritik padap hormone bhi he jiskaa upyog falon ko jaldi pakaane main kiya jaataa he. ethilin kaa jalyojan karne par ethil alcohol miltaa he.
 ithilin ek saralatam alkin he. yah ek asantrupt hydrocarbon he jisamen ek carbon-carbon dwi-bandh paaya jaataa he. ithilin kaa sanrachana sutara chitra main dikhaaya gaya he. yah ek prakritik gas he tathaa kol gas main pai jaati he. yah ek mahatvapurn audyogic rasayan he. iskaa upyog bade paimaane par palithine naamak plastic banane ke liye kiya jaataa he.
