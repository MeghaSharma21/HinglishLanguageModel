 vijay sheshadri pulitzer puraskaar vijeta kavi, nibandhkaar or saahitiyik samalochak hai. wo brukaleen neuyork ke hai. unka janm bharat ke bangalore main hua or paanch varsh kii aayu main 1959 main america chale gayi. unka paalan poshan ohio ke columbus main hua. unhen 2014 main unke kavita sangrah '3 sections' ke liye pulitzer puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
