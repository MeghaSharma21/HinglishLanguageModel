 yah maarg aurangzeb maarg south and maarg va tees janvari maarg ke gol chakkar tak se aarambh hokar rajeev chowk kii ek ardhvyasiya maarg jaataa he.
 is maarg par padane vaale mukhya chauraahey hai * ek pramukh chauraahaa to jahaan rajpath katata he, vahaan he.* doosraa chauraahaa he akbar maarg va motilal nehru maarg se milan par.* teesraa chauraahaa he vindsar place par jahaan ashok maarg ko firozshah maarg va raisina maarg kaa mail katata he.* dorajendra prasad maarg bhi isse chauraahaa banaata he.* moulana aaazaad maarg bhi ise katata he..

 janpath nai dilli kii pramukh margon main se ek he.ise angrezon dwaara qvins ve kaha jaataa tha. yah lootyans kii rachit nai dilli kaa ek abhinn evam pramukh hissaa he.
 uttar me^ yah rajeev chowk kii ek ardhvyasiya maarg se jo ki palika bazaar ke bagal se hi prarambh hota he. phir yah uttar dakshin me^ rajpath, ke abhilamb hokar use katata hua dakshin ko jaataa he.
 dakshin main yah aurangzeb maarg south and maarg va tees janvari maarg ke gol chakkar tak jaataa he. yaheen hotel clariges he.
