 kudasia begum, mogul badshaah mohammad shah kii patni or badshaah ahamed shah bahadur kii maan thim. unhone vaastavik regent, bharat main 1748 se 1754 tak seva kii or vahaan kii vyavasthaapakahi raheen. 
 vah janm se hindu thim or unka praarambhik naam udham bai tha. pehali baar unka parichay shahi adaalat main ek nartki ke rup main hua tha or is sthiti ko unhone sadasyon se paksh hasil karne ke liye istemaal kiya. samay ke saath samraat mohammad shah ke man main unke liye sneh utpann hua or unhone use apni teesri patni banaaya. baad main unhen manasabadaar ke rup main niyukt kiya, jo ki sena main ek ucch darja tha or jiskaa mukhya kaam samraat kii anupasthiti main niyam or kanoon banaae rakhana tha. 
 mohammad shah kii 1748 main mrityu ke baad, unke bete ahamed shah bahadur samraat ban gaye. ek vidhwaa ke rup main, unhone kudasia begham kaa naam liya. ahamed shah bahadur ek aprabhavi shaasha tha or dridhtaa se apni maa se prabhavit tha. unke bare main ek afvaah thi ki unka chakkar, nawab bahadur javid khan ke saath tha, jo ki janaana ke ek hijdaa adheekshak the. javid khan kii baad main hatya kar di gai. jab nawab imaad-ul-mulk 1754 main dilli pahunchaa to usne samraat or uski maa ko giraftaar kar kaidi bana liya. unki shaayad karawas main hi mrityu ho gai, haalanki sahi taarikh or kabr agyaat hai.
 laal kila ke paas sunahari masjid kaa nirmaan 1747 se 1751 ke bich nawab bahadur javid khan ke liye kiya gaya tha. 
 laal kile ke paas yamuna nadi ke tat par, unke apane mahal or udyaan kaa nirmaan 1748 main kiya gaya tha.
