 lallu bajpei ek bhartiya lok gayak va lok kalakaar the. unhone na sirf uttar pradesh main balki desh ke kai alag alag bhaagon main bhartiya loakgeet aalha ko apane vishesh gayan style se prachlit karaayaa. unka aalha gayan lakhanu doordarshan ke chaoupal kaarykram main aksar aayaa karata tha. ve ek ore to apane joshilay gayan ke liye jane jate the, duusari ore talwaar or moonchhon ke liye. aalha gaate hue ve apane haath main ek talwaar rakhte the or gaate-gaate use bhanjte rahete the. duble-patale sharir par khub badi-badi moonchhen unhen ek alag vyaktitv pradaan karti thim.
 lallu unnav jile ke narayandas kheda gaanv ke rahane vaale the, 2 may 2013 ko unki mrityu huyi he. unka asli naam pan. chandranath tha. par log unhen lallu bajpeyi ke naam se jaanate the. unhone lok gayaki ko ek pradarshnkaree kala ke rup main vikhyath kiya he.
