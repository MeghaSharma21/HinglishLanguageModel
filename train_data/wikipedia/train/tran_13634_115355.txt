 mahavir jayanti caitra shukla 13 ko manaaya jaataa he. yah parv jain dharm ke 24wein tirthankar mahavir swami ke janm kalyanak ke upalaksh main manaaya jaataa he. yah jainon kaa sabase pramukh parv he.
 bhagwaan mahavir swami kaa janm isa se 599 varsh purv kundgram, bharat me hua tha. janm se purv tirthankar mahavir kii mata trishla ne 16 shubh swapn dekhe the jinka fal raja siddharth ne is prakaar bataaya tha:-
 jain granthon ke anusaar janm ke baad devon ke mukhiya, indra ne sumeru parvant par le jaakar balak kaa ksheer sagar ke jal se abhishek kiya tha. ise hi janm kalyanak kehete he. har tirthankar ke jivan main panchakalyanak manaae jate he.
 jain granthon ke anusaar tirthankar bhagwan ke janm se hi das atishay hote he. yah he:-
 yah atishay unke dwaara purv janm main kiye gaye tapashcharn ke fal swarup prakat hote he.
 is mahotsav par jain mandiron ko vishesh rup se sajaayaa jaataa he. bharat main kai jagahon par jain samudaay dwaara ahimsa raily nikaali jaati he. is avasar par garib evam zaruratmandon ko daan diya jaataa he. kai rajya sarkaron dwaara maance evam madiraa kii dukaane band rakhane ke nirdesh die jate hai.
