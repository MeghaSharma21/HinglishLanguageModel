 swati singh ek bhartiya raajnneetijna or uttar pradesh sarkaar main kii ek mantri hai. ve enarai, baadh niyantran, krishi aayaat, krishi vipanan, krishi, videsh vyapaar kii mantri tathaa mahila kalyaan mantralay, parivaar kalyaan, maatrutiv or bal kalyaan mantralayon kii rajya mantri hai. 
 unke pati kaa naam dayaa shankar singh hai. 
 vah mukhyamantri yogi adityanath ke kebinet main rajya mantri ban gaye.
