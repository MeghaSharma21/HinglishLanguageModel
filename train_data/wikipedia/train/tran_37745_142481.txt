 varshan yaa avakshepan ek mausam vigyaan kii prachlit shabdaawalee he jo vayumandaleey jal ke sanghanit hokar kisi bhi rup main prithvi kii sataha par waapas aane ko kehete hai. varshan ke kai rup ho sakate hai jaise varsha, phuhaar, himavarsha, himpat or olavrishti ityadi. atah varsha varshan kaa ek rup yaa prakaar he.
 varshan kaa mahatv jalavigyaan main bhi he kyonki kisi bhi jalsambhar kaa jal bajet tay karne main isaki pramukh bhoomika hoti he.
 upar uthati garm evam ardra vaayu ke santrupt hone tathaa osank kii prapti ke baad sanghanan hone par vayumandaleey jalawaashp ke yaa to taral rup yaa thos rup main neeche girane ko varshan kehete hai.
