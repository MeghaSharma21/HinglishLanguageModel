 2014-2015 antarrashtriya cricket satr oktober 2014 se april 2015 tak tha.
 sijan kii shuruyaat main ranking nimnlikhit hai.
 dharmashala main chauthe ekadivasiy match ke baad tour radd kar diya gaya tha. oktober main bharat kaa westeindies daura mul rup se teen test maichon, paanch vanade international maichon or ek twanti-20 antarrashtriya match ke liye nirdhaarit kiya gaya tha. chauthe oriai ke dauraan, westeindies ke khilaadiyon, westeindies cricket board or khilaadiyon ke sahyog ke bich vetan vivaad ke kaaran shesh daura radd kar diya gaya tha.
