  purv express 2304 bhartiya rail dwaara sanchalit ek mail express train he. yah train nai dilli railway stesan se 04:20PM baje chhootati he or haavda jankshan railway stesan par 04:45PM baje pahunchati he. isaki yatra avadhi he 24 ghante 25 minute.
 purva express bhartiya railway kii ek pratidin chalane vaali superfast express train he, jo haavda or nai dilli bharat kii rajdhani ke bich chal rahi he.
 purva naam bharat ke purvi bhaag kaa pratik he. bihar, jharkhand or pashchim bengal ke yatriyon ke liye yah rajdhani ke baad sabase suvidhajanak train he.
 train ko alechabi coach ke 22 coach rack aavantit kiye gaye hai, jo ki 30 april 2013 se aavanton ke aadhaar par chalaanaa shuru kiya gaya tha. vartmaan main yah train 130.00 kimi prati ghanta kii adhiktam raftaar se ja sakati he.
 2000 ke dashak ke shuruuaati dinon main is train ke parichaarkon ke poshaak main parivartan krimyukt bhure rang se aadhikarik nile rang main badlaav kiya. is train main pehle ek pratham asi coach bhi tha. jab is train kii shuruyaat hui thi tab is rain ko logon ke dwaara kaaphi ucch praathamikta di jaati thi. lekin jabase rajdhani express kii shuruyaat kii gayi tab se is train ko bahut zyada praathamikta milti kam ho gayi.
 14 disambar 2014 ko 12381 haavda - nai dilli purva express subah subah .15 minute main ravaanaa hone ke turant baad 8.27 baje patari se utar gai. haavda stesan ko chhodane ke kuch samay baad hi lilua naamak jagah main nai dilli jane is train purva express ke 11 sleeper coach or ek pentry car patari se utar gayi. railway ke adhikariyon ne kaha ki is durghatana main kisi bhi yatri ko chot nahin pahunchi. unhone kaha kii durghatana ke samay yah train dhimi gati se aage bad rahi thi is liye koi badi ghatana nahin ghati. ek adhikari ne kaha, "durghatana ke dauraan purva express 10 se 15 kilometre kii gati se chal rahi thi. train kii ausat gati 63 kimi prati he, haalanki yah 120 kimi mile kii raftaar se yatra kar sakati he.
