 sakshya vidhi un kanoonon or siddhanton kaa samooh he jo nirdhaarit karte hai ki kisi vidhik kaarywahi main kya-kya tathy ke pramaan ke rup main sweekaarya hai. pracheen hindu kaal main dharamshastron main vibhinn prakaar ke saakshyon kaa ullekh miltaa he, jaise – lekh, dastaveji sakshya ke rup main, sakshi jo maukhik bayaan detaa he, vah shruti yaa kabja aadi.
 sakshya vidhi prakriya vidhi he tathaa poorak vidhi he. is vidhi kaa bhootlakshi prabhaav he. sakshya vidhi deshkari vidhi he arthath jis desh main mukadmaa chal rahaa he, us desh kii vidhi lagu hoti he. iske antargat yah dekha jaataa he ki –
 yadhyapi bhartiya sakshya adhinium prakriya vidhi he lekin isamen mul vidhi ke niyam shaamil hai, jaise- dhara 6-55 tak tathyon kii susangati se sambandhit niyam, dhara 115-117 tak vibandh ke niyam, ye sabhi moulik vidhi ke niyam hai.
