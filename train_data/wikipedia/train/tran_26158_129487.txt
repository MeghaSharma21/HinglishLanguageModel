 roza sharif yaa shaikh ahamed faruki sarhind ke rup main janaa jaataa hagurudwara fatehgarh sahib ke uttar main ek chote se duuri par sarhind -bassi pathana road par sthit he. shaikh ahamed faruki 1563 se 1624 tak akbar or jahangir ke samay is sthaan par rahete the.
 mujdid ke urs utsav yahaan 300 se adhik varshon ke liye yojit kiye jate hai or bade paimaane par bharat, pakistan, afghanistan, indonesia, bangladesh or anya muslim deshon ke musalamaanon isamen bhaag lete hai
 shaikh ahamed ke ghar ke sadasyon ke zyaadaatar yogicon main kai anya kabr hai.makbare eenton se bana ek acha imaarat he, jo pathar or sangemarmar ke saath aanshik rup se madha he.iske paas mujdid ke purvajon rati-ud-deen kaa makbara he.prarthana ke pehle snaan karne ke liye ek tahkhaane or ek chote se tanks ke saath ek bhavy masjid he.tab se bharat sarkaar ne ek aitihaasik smarak ke rup main kabja kar liya he or niyamit karmchaariyon ko rakrakhaav,or dekhbhaal ke liye yahaan rakhaa gaya he.
 roza kii ore se afgaan shaasha, shah jaman or unki rani kii kabr hai.
