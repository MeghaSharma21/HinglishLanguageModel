 vishwa gauraiya diwas ko gauraiya ke prati jaagrukta laane ke uddeshy se manaaya jaataa he. iske alaava ye shahari vaataavaran main rahane vaale aam pakshiyon ke prati jaagrukta laane hetu bhi manaaya jaataa he. ise har saal 20 march ke din manaaya jaataa he. ye nechar forevar socity or iko-sis ection foundation ke mili jule prayaas ke kaaran manaaya jaataa he.
 nasik nivasee mohammad dilawar ne gharelu gauraiya pakshiyon kii sahaayta hetu nechar forevar socity kii sthapana kii thi. inke is kaary ko dekhte hue time ne 2008 main inhen heroj of di enwyrement naam diya tha. vishwa gauraiya diwas manaane kii yojana bhi inhen ke karyaalay main ek saamaanya charchaa ke dauraan bani thi.
 paryaavaran ke sangrakshan or is kaary main madad kii saraahna karne hetu eneges ne 20 march 2011 main gujarat ke ahmedabad shahar main gauraiya puraskaar kii shuruyaat kii thi.
