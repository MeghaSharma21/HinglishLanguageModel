 
 Ficus glomerata Roxb.
 gooler ficus kul kaa ek vishaal vriksh he. ise sanskrit main udumbar, bangla main dumur, marathi main udumbar, gujarati main umbra, arabi main jamiz, farsi main anjire aadam kehete hai. is par phool nahin aate. isaki shakhaon main se fal utpann hote hai. fal gol-gol anjir kii tarah hote hai or isamen se safed-safed doodh nikalataa he. iske pattey labhede ke patton jaise hote hai. nadi ke udumbar ke pattey or phool gooler ke patton-fal se chote hote hai. 
 gooler, 2 prakaar kaa hota he- nadi udumbar or kathumar. kathumar ke pattey gooler ke patton se bade hote hai. iske patton ko chune se haathon main khujali hone lagati he or patton main se doodh nikalataa he.
 gooler sheethal, garbhasandhankarak, vranropak, ruksh, kasaiyla, baari, madhur, asthisandhan kaarak evam varna ko ujjwal karne vaala he kafapitt, atisar tathaa yoni rog ko nasht karne vaala he. 
 gooler kai tarah gun vaala tathaa rasviry or vipaak main usase kuch hin he. gooler kaa ek bhed kakodumbari athwa kathumar he. 
 sanskrit - kakodumbari, 
 hindi - kathumar, 
 ban- kakdumur, kalaumbar tathaa bokhada, 
 gujarati- tedumbaro, 
 arabi - tanvari, 
 farsi - anjiredasti,
 angreji - kiguti. 
 gun- kathumar stambhak, sheethal, kasaiyla, tathaa pittakapha, vran, shwetkusht, pandurog, arsh, kamla, dah, raktaatisar, raktavikar, shoth, urdhvashwas evam twag dosha vinaashak he.
