 lochan prasad pandeya prasiddh hindi sahityakar the. inhone hindi evam oriya donon bhashaaon main kavy rachanaayen bhi kii hai. san 1905 se hi inaki kavitaayen 'saraswati' tathaa anya masik patrikaon main nikalne lagi thim. lochan prasad pandeya kii kuch rachanaayen kathaprabandh ke rup main hai tathaa kuch footkar. ve 'bhaaratendu sahitya samiti' ke bhi ye sadsy the. madhya pradesh ke sahityakaron main inaki vishesh pratishtha thi. aaj bhi inkaa naam bade aadar se liya jaataa he.
 lochan prasad pandeya kaa janm 4 janvari, san 1887 i. main madhya pradesh ke bilaspur zile main balpur naamak gram main hua tha. bilaspur ab chhattisgarh rajya kaa hissaa he. lochan prasad pandeya ke pita pandit chintamani pandeya vidyavyasnee the. unhone apane gaanv main baalkon kii shiksha ke liye ek paathshaala khulwaai thi. lochan prasad ji apane pita ke chaturth putr the. ve aath bhai the- purushottam prasad, padmalochan, chandrasekhar, lochan prasad, vidyadhar, vanshidhar, muralidhar or mukutadhar tathaa chandan kumari, yajna kumari, surya kumari or anand kumari, ye chaar bahanen thim.
 lochan prasad pandeya kii praarambhik shiksha balpur kii nijee paathshaala main hui. san 1902 main middle skool sambalpur se paas kiya or 1905 main kalakatta se inter kii pariksha paas karke banaras gayi, jahaan anek sahitya manishiyon se unka sampark hua. unhone apane praytn se hi oriya, bangala or sanskrit kaa bhi gyaan praapt kiya tha. lochan prasad pandeya ne apane jivan kaal main anek jagahon kaa bhraman kiya. saahitiyik goshtiyon, sammelnon, congress adhiveshan, itihaas-puraatatv khoji abhiyaan main ve sadaa tatpar rahe. unke khoj ke kaaran anek garh, shilaalekh, tamrapatr, gupha prakaash main aa sake. san 1923 main unhone 'chhattisgarh gaurav prachaarak mandali' kii sthapana kii, jo baad main 'mahakaushal itihaas parishad' kahalaaya. unka sahitya, itihaas or puraatatv main samaan adhikaar tha.
 lochan prasad pandeya swabhaav se saral evam nishchal the. inkaa vyavhaar atmiyatapurna hua karata tha. unhone apni rachanaaon ke madhyam se pathakon ko charitroththana kii prerana di. us samay upadeshak kaa kaary bhi sahitya ke sahare karnaa aaj kii tarah nahin tha, isliye inaki rachanaaon ne pathakon ke sanyam ke prati ruchi utpann kii. ye 'bharatendu sahitya samiti' ke ek sammanit sadsy the. madhya pradesh main inke prati bada aadar, sammaan evam pratishtha kaa bhav he.
 lochan prasad pandeya kaa saahitiyik-krititv, charitroththana, niti-poshan, upadesh-daan, vaastavik-chithran evam lokkalyaan ke liye hi parisrisht hua he. inke kavy kaa vastugat roopaadhar abhidhamulak, nishchit evam asankethik he. ye katha evam ghatana kaa aadhaar lekar vrittatmak kavitaayen likhaa karte the. san 1905 i. se ye 'saraswati' main kavitaayen likhane lage the. bharatendu kaa jaagran-truyan baj chukaa tha. dvivedi yug ke shakti-sanchay kaal main lochan prasad pandeya kaa abhyaagaman hua. isi samay sahruday samayikta, oj, santulit pad-yojana evam tatsam padaavalee se purna inaki kavita ne sanketikta evam dhyanyatmakataa ke abhaav main bhi hriday-samprukt itivrutt ke kaaran logon kaa dhyaan apni ore aakrisht kiya. sfoot evam prabandh, donon hi prakaar kii kavitaaon dwaara lochan prasad ji ne sudhaar-bhav ko pratishthapit kiya. 'mrigi du:khamochan' naamak kavita main vriksh-pashu aadi ke prati bhi inaki sahrudayata sundar rup main vyakt hui he. ye madhya pradesh ke agraganya sahitya netaa bhi rahe.
 lochan prasad pandeya kii pramukh rachanaaon kaa vivaran is prakaar he-
 'do mitra' uddeshy pradhan, saamaajik upanyaas, maitri adarsh, samaaj-sudhaar, stri-charitra se prerit evam paashchaatya sabhyataa kii pratikriya par likhit lochan prasad pandeya kii 1906 main prakashit pratham kriti he.
 1907 main madhya pradesh se hi prakashit 'pravasi' naamak kavy-sangrah main chhayavaadi, rahasyamayi sankalnon kii bhanti kalpanaagat, murtimatta evam ishat lakshanikta kaa prayaas dikhaai padataa he.
 1910 main 'indian press', prayag se 'kavita kusum maala', balopayogi kavy-sankalan evam 1914 main 'niti kavita' dharmavishyak sangrah nikle.
 lochan prasad pandeya kaa 1914 main 'sahityaseva' naamak prahasan prakashit hua, jisamen vyagya-vinod ke liye hasyotpaadan kii atinaatakeey ghatana-charitra-sangyojan style kaa prayog hua he.
 san 1914 main samaaj-sudharmulak 'prem prashansa' va 'grihasth-dasha darpan' natya-kriti prakashit hui thi.
 unka 'mewar gaatha' aitihaasik khand-kavy san 1914 main hi prakashit hua tha.
 san 1915 main 'padya pushpanjali' naamak do kavy-sangrah bhi prakashit hue the.
 1915 main hi unke saamaajik evam rashtriya naatak 'chaatr durdasha' evam atinaatakiyatayukta vyangy-vinodaparak 'graamy vivaah' aadi naatak nikle.
