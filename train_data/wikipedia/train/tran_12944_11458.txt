 fairuddin ali ahamed bharat ke paanchave raashtrapati the. ve 24 agast 1974 se lekar 11 farvari 1977 tak raashtrapati rahe.
 fairuddin ahamed ke dada khaliluddin ali ahamed assam ke kacharighat se the. ahamed kaa janm 13 may 1905 ko dilli main hua. unke pita karnal zalnur ali the. unki maa dilli ke lohari ke nawab kii beti thim.
 ahamed ko gonda jill le ke sarkari hai-skool or dilli sarkari hai-skool main shikshit kiya gaya. ucch shiksha ke liye ve 1923 main england gaye, jahaan unhone saint catherine college, cambridge main adhyayan kiya. 1928 main unhone lahore ucch nyaayalay main kanoonee abhyaas aarambh kiya.
 1925 main nehru se england main mulaakaat ke baad ve bhartiya rashtriya congress main shaamil ho gaye. unhone swatantraaa andolan main sakriy rup se bhaag liya. 1974 main pradhanmantri indira gandhi ne ahamed ko raashtrapati pad ke liye chunaa or ve bharat ke duusare muslim raashtrapati ban gaye. indira gandhi ke kehane par unhone 1975 main apane sanvaidhanik adhikaar kaa istemaal kiya or aantarik apatkaal kii ghoshnaa kar di.
 1977 main hridaygati ruk jane se ahamed kaa karyaalay main nidhan ho gaya.
