 argyle pink jubilee australia main khudaai se praapt bagaiyr taraasha hua ab tak kaa sabase bada gulabi hira he. is heere ko pashchimi australia ke rio tinto argyle hira khadaan main paaya gaya he. aamtaur par argyle pink jubilee jaise keemtee pathar yaa to kisi ajaayabaghar main rakh diye jate hai yaa christy'ja jaise unche nilaamee gharon main chale jate hai. christy ne apane 244 varshon ke itihaas main keval 18 aise taraashe hue gulabi hiron kii nilaamee kii he jinka vajan 10 carat se adhik rahaa ho. mul rup se 12.76 carat vajan ke halke gulabi heere ko richard haau kim cam ne farvari 2012 main perth main tarashna shuru kiya. taraashe jane ke dauraan hi is heere ke bheetar ek badi kami kaa pataa chalaa jise dur nahin kiya ja sakaa. keval mute taur aakaar dekar or sanvarkar, 8.01 carat ke is heere ko melbern ajaayabaghar ko daan kar diya gaya.
