 opava purvi check ganatantr main opava nadi ke tat par basaa ek nagar he. yah ostrowa ke uttar-pashchim main sthit he. opava silesia kaa ek aitihaasik kendra he. yah check silesia kaa aitihaasik rajdhani tha. vartmaan samay main isaki jansankhya 57,843 he.
 opava checkoslovakia ke vistrut maidan ke madhya bhaag main oder nadi kii opava naamak sahaayak nadi par sthit nagar he. is shabd kaa nirmaan german shabd traupav se hua he. 13veen shatabdi main purana nagar basaaya gaya tha. yah nagar udyanon se ghira hua he jiske baahar kii ore nayaa nagar basaa he. is nagar main anek udyog dhandhe viksit hai, jaise madiraa, chini tathaa audyogic yantra ityadi bananaa. san 1968 i. main isaki jansankhya 46,000 thi, jisamen adhikanshat: german the. san 1938 i. main munio samjhaute ke uparaat yah germany ko mil gaya tha parantu 1945 i. main yah nagar checkoslovakia ko mil gaya.
