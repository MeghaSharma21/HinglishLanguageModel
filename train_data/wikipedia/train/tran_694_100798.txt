 di ve gundapp yaa diviji kannada sahityakar evam darshanik the.
 gundapp ko apane kaaryakshetra karnataka se baahar adhik prasiddhi nahin mil saki. yahaan unhone raajnneetik sudhaar or saamaajik jagriti ke liye 50 varshon tak kaam kiya. unhone is kaary ko apane lekhan ke dwaara praapt karne kii koshish kii. unke lekhan main geet, kavitaayen, naatak, raajnneetik parche, jivaniyaan or bhagavatagita par tikaa shaamil hai. ve puri tarah se adarsh loktantr ke samarthak the anushaasan par bahut jor dete the. unhone jor dekar kaha ki anushaasanheenata loktantr kii dushman he. unhen karnataka sarkaar ne pension dene kii peshkash kii lekin unhone ise yah kahkar asweekaar kar diya ki isse janata ke bich apane vichaar swatantraapurvak rakhane ke unke adhikaar par ankush lag jayega.
 inke dwaara rachit ek darshanik vyakhya shrimadbhagavadgita taatpary athwa jivan dharmyog ke liye unhen san 1967 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
