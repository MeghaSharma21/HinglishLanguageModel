 gustaakh dil ek bhartiya televijan dharawahik he jo life oke par somvaar se shuukravaar prasaarit hota he. iskaa pratham prasaaran 05 agast 2013 ko hua tha. is dharawahik main pyaar ke ek trikoniya rishte ko darshaya gaya he.
 yah kaarykram laajo, nikhil evam ishaanaa ke bich pyaar ke trikoniya rishte ko pradarshit karata he. laajo ek gramin ladki he or nikhil ek shahari aadhunik ladkaa he jo bachapan se hi apni dost ishaanaa se prem karata he. yah kahani laajo or nikhil ke aas-paas gumti rahati he. laajo nikhil ke jivan main aane vaali duusari aurat he.
