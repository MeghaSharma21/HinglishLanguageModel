 patavaar naav khene kii dand ko kehete hai jo saamanytah lakdi kii bani ek yukti hota he jiskaa ek sira chapata hota jisase pani ko peeche hataaya jaataa he or parinaam swaroop naav aage badhati he. iske anya naam chappu or karn hai. vaigyanik drushti se yah ek dwitiya varg kaa uttolak he. vartmaan samay main lakdi ke alaava anya halake or majboot padaarthon se patavaar banaai jane lagi hai.
