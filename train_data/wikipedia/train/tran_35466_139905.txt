 mohammad sahab kii mrityu ke baad islam ke pramukh ko khaliph kehete the. is vichaardhaara ko khilaafat kaha jaataa he. pratham chaar khalifaaon ko rashidun kehete hai. ummyad, abbasi or fatimi khalipha kramshah damishq, baghdad or kahiraa se shashan karte the. iske baad osmani khilaafat aayaa.
 mohammad sahab ke netritv main arab bahut shaktishaalee ho gaye the. unhone ek bade samrajya par adhikaar kar liya tha jo isse pehle arabi itihaas main shaayad hi kisi ne kiya ho. khaliph banane kaa arth tha - itane bade samrajya kaa maalik. atah is pad ko lekar vivaad hona swaabhaavik tha.
 pratham chaar khalifaaon ko rashidun khaliph bhi kehete hai. pahal khaliph abu bakar tha jo muhammd sahab ke sabase aarambh ke anuyaayiyon main se ek tha. iske baad umr khalipha bane jiske baad osman ke baad ali khalipha bane par unki hatya bhi pichley do khalifaaon kii tarah kar di gai. ali ne apni rajdhani madina se hataakar kufa main sthapit kii thi.
 ummyad pehla khalipha vansh tha. iske khaliph vanshanugat rup se bane. is samay islamic samrajya purv kii disha main sindh or baluchistan tak fail gaya. ye sansaarik or vilaasi the or mohammad sahab dwaara bataae gaye raaston se alag rahe.
 iske baad abbasiyon kaa shashan aayaa.
 abbasiyon ne baghdad se shashan kiya. naunvi sadi ke ant tak unki shakti sthaneey shaskaon yaa amiron kaa haath chali gai thi. gyarahavin sadi main unheen ke dwaara nirmit ghulaam pratha ne samrajya ko sainik rup se kai bhaagon main baant liya tha. 1258 main mongolon ke akraman ke kaaran baghdad kaa patan ho gaya par unki ek shakha ne kahiraa se apana prabhutv 1517 tak banaaya.
 pandraveen sadi se lekar 1924 tak islamic jagat par osmanon kaa dabadabaa bana rahaa. makka par osmanon kaa adhikaar ho gaya. 1924 main turki ke shaasha kamaal pasha ne khilaafat kaa ant kar diya or turki ko ek ganraajya ghoshit kar diya.
 iesas ne 29 june 2014 ko apane pramukh abu bakr al-bagadadhee ko ek nayaa 'khalipha' ghoshit kiya hai.
