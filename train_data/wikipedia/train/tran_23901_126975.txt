 
 35 lakh anumaanit
 bahun nepal ke pahari khas samudaayake brahman evam pujari jati he. ye nepalke dusre sarvaadhik jansankhya vaale samudaay he. nepali sahityame is jatike joddar yogadan he. nepali raajnneetime is jatike yogadan he.
 nepali khojakarta evam itihaaskaar dorbahadur visht or suryamani adhikarike anusaar ye jati anya khas jati jaise nepalke pashchimi karnali ilaakake vibhinn gounse utpatti hue he. ye hind-arya bhashaa parivaar ke nepali bhashaa bolte he. 
 bahun jatike thar va paariwarik naam nimna likhit he:
 khas bahun
