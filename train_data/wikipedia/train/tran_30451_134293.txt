 chidyaghar yaa praniupawan vah santhaan he jahaan jivit pashu pakshiyon ko bahut badi sankhya main sangrahit kar rakhaa jaataa he. log in sangrahit pashu pakshiyon ko suvidha or surakshapurvak dekh saken isaki bhi vyavastha kii jaati he. yahan unke prajnan or chikitsa aadi kii bhi vyavastha hoti he. duniya bhar main aam janata ke liye khole gaye pramukh pashu sangrahalayon kii sankhya ab 1,000 se bhi adhik he or unamem se lagbhag 80 pratishat shaharon main hai.
 jivit pashu pakshiyon ke sangrah ko rakhane kii paripati bahut pracheen he. aise upawanon ke hone kaa sabase purana ullekh china main isa ke 1200 varsh purv main miltaa he. china ke chaauu vansh ke pratham shaasha ke paas us samay aisa ek pashu pakshiyon kaa sangrahalay tha. isa ke 2000 varsh purv ke misravasiyon kii kabron ke aasapaas pashuon kii haddiyaan pai gai hai, jisase pataa lagata he ki ve log aamod pramod ke liye apane aasapaas pashuon ko rakhaa karte the. peeche roman log bhi pashuon ko pakadakar apane paas rakhte the. pracheen romanon or unaniyon ke paas aise sangrah the jinamein singh, baagh, chita, tendue aadi rahete the. aisa pataa lagata he ki isa ke 29 varsh purv augustus octavius ke paas 410 baagh, 260 chitah or 600 africa jantuon kaa sangrah tha, jisamen baagh rhinosiris, hipopautaimas, bhaaluu, hathi, makar, saanp, sil, eagle ityadi the. peeche jantuon ke sangrah kii disha main uttarottar vruddhi heti rahi he or aaj sansar ke prayek desh or prayek bade-bade nagar main praniupawan vidyaman hai.

 chidyaghar ke jaanwaron ko aamtaur par ek gheraav main rakhaa jaataa he jise unke prakritik awaas kii hi tarah banane kaa prayaas kiya jaataa he, jo aagantukon or jaanwaron ke liye laabhadaayak hota he. nishachar jaanwaron ke liye unke paas ek vishesh bhawan ho saktaa he jisamen din ke dauraan maddhim safed yaa laal prakaash kaa istemaal hota he, jiske kaaran jab angutak aate hai to jaanavar sakriy ho jate hai or raat main tej roshni unhen sone main madad karti hai. penguin jaise nitant bhinn jalwaayu ke jaanwaron ke liye vishesh jalwaayu paristhiti kaa nirmaan kiya jaataa he. pakshiyon keede, rengne vaale jaanavar, machhlee or anya jaleey jeev-jantuon ke liye vishesh gheraav kaa bhi vikaas kiya gaya he. kuch chidyagharon main bheetar pravesh kii anumati hoti he jahaan aangtuk gaiyr-aakraamak prajatiyon ke gheraav main pravesh karte hai, jaise limar, african bandar, pakshi, chhipakali or kachua. aagantukon ko path par rahane ke liye bolaa jaataa he or khaadya padaarth ko dikhaane yaa khaane se bachane ke liye kaha jaataa hai kyonki ho saktaa he jaanavar unase chin len.
