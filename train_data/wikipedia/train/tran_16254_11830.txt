 isisi champiens trophy 2006, cricket kii vishwa-stareey pratiyogita he. yah bharat me 7 oktober se 5 navambar 2006 me yojit kii gai. yah isisi champions trophy shrinkhalaa kii paanchavi pratiyogita he.
 is pratiyogita ke muky daur ke liye chaar tiimon westeindies, srilanka, bangladesh or zimbabwe ko kwalifaaing round khelaa gaya. westeindies or srilanka kii timen mukhya daur ke liye kwalifaai kar gai.
 austrailia ne final mukaable me westeindies ko 6 wicket se haraa kar pratiyogita jiti.
 
 das test-khilne vaale desh bhaag le rahe he.
