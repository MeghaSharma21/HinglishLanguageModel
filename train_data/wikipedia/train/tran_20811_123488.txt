 pradeep narwal pradeep narwal pro kabddi lig main ek achche redar ke rup main ubhare hai. inkaa sabase ghaatak hathiyaar dubkee he. isliye inhen dubkee king ke naam se janaa jaataa he. ye pro kabddi lig main sijan-5 main patna pyrets ke captain hai. inkaa janm 16 farvari san 1997 ko bharat ke haryana rajya main hua tha. ye pro kabddi lig main patna kii team patna pyrets ke liye khelte hai. inhone pro kabddi lig main sijan-3 or sijan-4 main lagaataar do baar patna pyrets ko final jitayaa. is liye patna pyrets ne sijan-5 main rittin kiya he. ye sijan 5 main tej 4 match main 50 red point liye.
