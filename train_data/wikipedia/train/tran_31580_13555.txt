 dembugranthi stri jananaang yaa stri prajnan pranali kaa ek bhaag hai. mahilaon main garbhashay ke donon ore dembugranthiyaan hoti he. yah dekhne main baadaam ke aakaar kii lagbhag 3.5 semi lambi or 2 semi chaudee hoti he. iske upar hi dimbanalikaaon ki tantrikaayen hoti he jo andon ko apni ore aakarshith karti he. dembugranthiyon kaa rang gulabi hota he. umr badhane ke saath-saath ye halke safed rang kii ho jaati he. vridvavastha main yah sikudakar choti ho jaati he. inkaa pramukh kaary andey bananaa tathaa uttejit drav or hormones bananaa hota he. dembugranthiyon ke mukhya hormones istrogen or propeestron he. maahvaaree sthapit hone ke purv iskaa koi kaam nahin hota he. parantu maahvaaree ke baad isamen prayek maheene dimb banate or chode jate he, jo shukranuon ke saath milakar garbhadhaaran karte he.
 dimbavahiniyaan garbhashay ke upree bhaag ke donon ore se nikalati he tathaa donon taraf kuulhe kii hadidyon tak jaati he. inaki lambaai lagbhag 10 semi or motaai lagbhag aadha semi tak lambi hoti he. donon ore iskaa aakaar ek keep kii tarah kaa hota he. is keep kaa antim chor lambi-lambi anguliyon kii taraf hota he jisko tantrikaayen kehete he. inkaa pramukh kaary dembugranthiyon se nikle andey ko gherkar use vahiniyon me bhejnaa hota he. yah naliyaan mansapeshiyon se bani, tathaa inke andar kii diwaar ek jhilli kii bani hoti he jisko mukas jhilli kehete he.
 dembugranthiyon se pakade andey, vahiniyon ke aage ke bhaag main jaakar rukte he. jahaan ye purush ke shukranu ke saath milakar ek naye jivan kaa nirmaan hota he. stri janan ang main is sanrachana ko jaigot kehete he. jaigot ke chaaron taraf ek khaas parat utpann hoti he.
