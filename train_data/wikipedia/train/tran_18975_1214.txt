 malayalam vikipedia vikipedia kaa malayalam bhashaa kaa ek mukt sanskaran he jis par koi bhi sampaadan kar saktaa he or yah 21 disambar, 2002 ko armbh kiya gaya tha. anya bahut-si bhartiya bhashaaon ke vikipediaon ki tulana main yah vikipedia prishth gaharaai, prati prishth sampaadan, panjikrut sadsy or sakriy prayoktaaon ke maamle main bahut aage he . 17 janvari, 2012 tak is vikipedia par lekhon kii kul sankhya 22,183+ or panjikrut sadasyon kii sankhya 32,002+ he.
 yadhyapi puraani or nai malayalam lipi ke liye anya malayalam bhashi unicode phont uplabdh hai, lekin adhisankhya upyogkarta anjalipuranilipi chunate hai, jo malayalam lekhan kii prachin/parmparik style kaa paalan karti he. praarambhik sampadakon dwaara vishesh malayalam unicode aagat asr aadhaarit vaaramozi kunjeepatal ko apanaaya gaya, jo dhvanyatmak anuwaad upkaran he. antarlikhit kunjeepatal ko bhi bahut se upyogkartaaon dwaara variyata di gai he, haalaanki ab pariyojna main antanirhit aagat upkaran samahit he.
 2 sitambar, 2007 ko lokapriy malayalam dainik mathrubhumi ne apane ravivaar ke anupoorak patr main malayalam vikipedia pariyojna kii bahut vyapti kii.
