 haribhaau upaadhyaay bharat ke prasiddh sahityasevi evam rashtrakarmi the.
 ujjain jila bhavarasa main, haribhaau ji kaa janm hua |swatantraaa andolan main inako bhi karawas hua ||rachanaaen inaki he anek anuvaadit, moulik, sampadit |bhashaa triguni he inaki saral, saskrit or mishrit ||
 haribhaau upaadhyaay kaa janm madhya pradesh ke ujjain ke bhavarasa main san 1892 i0 main hua.
 vishvavidhyalayan shiksha anyatam na hote hue bhi sahityasarjana kii pratibha janmajaat thi or inke saarvajanik jivan kaa aarambh "audumbar" masik patr ke prakaashan ke madhyam se sahityaseva dwaara hi hua. san 1911 main padhai ke saath inhone is patr kaa sampadan bhi kiya. san 1915 main ve pandit mahaviraprasad dvivedi ke sampark main aae or "saraswati' main kaam kiya. iske baad shri ganeshashankar vidhyaarthee ke "pratap", "hindi navajivan", "prabhaa", aadi ke sampadan main yogadan kiya. san 1922 main swayan "maalav mayur" naamak patr prakashit karne kii yojana banaai kintu patr adhik samay tak nahin chalaa.
 haribhaau ji kii moulik sahityasarjasna "baapu ke ashram main", "sarvoday kii buniyaad", "sadhana ke path par", "bhagwat dharm", "manan", "punya samaran", "durvadal" tathaa anya anek pustakon ke rup main hindi sansar ke saamne hai. vividh patr-patrikaon main lekh to baraabar hi likhte rahe. is atirkti inhone jawaharlal ji kii aatmakatha "meri kahani' tathaa pattabhi seetaramaiya krit "congress kaa itihaas' ityadi ke angreji se hindi anuwaad bhi prastut kiye.
 gandhi ji se prabhavit hokar ye rashtriya andolan main kud pade. puraani ajmer riyasat main inhen kai baar jail janaa padaa. swatantraprapti ke baad ye ajmer ke mukhya mantri nirvaachit hue. hriday se ye atyant komal, pardu:khakaatar vyakti the, kintu siddhanton par koi samjhauta nahin karte the. rajasthan kii sab riyasaton ko milakar rajasthan rajya bana or iske kai varshon baad shri mohanlal sukhadiya mukhyamantri bane. unhone atyant aagrahpurvak upaadhyaay ji ko pehle vitt phir shikshaamantri banaaya. bahut dinon tak ye is pad par rahe kintu swasthya thik na rahane ke kaaran antat: tyaagptr de diya.
 upaadhyaay ji kai varshon tak rajasthan kii shaskeey sahitya academy ke adhyaksh bhi rahe. inhone mahila shiksha sadan, hatoondy tathaa sasta sahitya mandal kii sthapana kii.
 25 agast 1972 ko inkaa dehanth hua.
 swatantraaa kii ore, baapu ke ashram main, sadhana ke path par, durvadal, sarvoday kii buniyaad, acharya dvivediji, meri jeevani, yugdharm, tyagabhumi saraswati, hindi nav jivan.
 bhashaa	vyaavhaarik or pravahpurn khadboli ke saath videshi shabdon kaa prayog.
 style	vivechanaatmak, varnanaatmak tathaa sanvadatmak.
