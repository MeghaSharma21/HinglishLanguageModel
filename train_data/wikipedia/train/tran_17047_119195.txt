 shamsurrahman faruki saraswati sammaan se sammanit sahityakar or urdu zabaan va adab ke namvar aalochak hai. unako urdu aalochana ke tii.es.aliot ke rup main mana jaataa he or unhone saahitiyik samiksha ke nae model taiyaar kiye hai. inke dwaara rachit ek samalochna tanqidi afakaar ke liye unhen san 1986 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
 
 shamsurrahman kaa janm 15 janvari 1935 ko bharat main hua tha. unhone angreji main kii degree 1955 main elahabaad vishvavidhyalay se praapt kii. unke mata-pita alag alag prishthbhumi ke the - pita devbandi musalmaan the jabaki maa kaa ghar kaaphi udaar tha. unki parvarish udaar vaataavaran main hui, vah muharram or shabe baraat ke saath holi bhi manaa liya karata tha.
