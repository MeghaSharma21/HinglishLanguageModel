 utsav kaa arth hota he parv yaa tyohaar.
 bhartiya samskruti main parvon kaa vishesh sthaan he. yahan tak ki ise tyohaaron kii samskruti kahana galat nahin hoga. saal bhar koi na koi parv yaa utsav chalata hi rahata he. har ritu main, har maheene main kam se kam ek pramukh tyohaar avashya manaaya jaataa he. kuch utsav kisi anchal main manaae jate hai to kuch bharat bhar main bhale hi naam alag-alag hon.
