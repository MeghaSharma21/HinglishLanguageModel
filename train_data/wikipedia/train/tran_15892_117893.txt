 saatyaki banerjee ek bhartiya bengali sangeetkaar. ve ek anglo bengali band parapar kaa ek hissaa he jo ki kolkata or landan kaa ek mashhoor band he. saatyaki do-tara or sarod jaise upkarnon ko bhalibhaanti or nipunta se baja lete hai tathaa donon shastreey or lok par apane pradarshan dete hai. unhone apana ye jhaan sw pandit deepak choudhary se siikhaa he or pandit tejendra narayan majumdaar ke tahat ab saath sarod kaa adhyayan karte hai. 
 saatyaki shastreey sangeet or lok sangeet ke shandaar gayak hai or ve lok sangeetkaaron abhyaas bhi karte rahete hai. saatyaki ek bahu pratibha vaale vyakti hai jinhone oud or rebaab apane aap siikhaa or dakshata se baja bhi lete hai. iske atirikt ve bengali rahasyamay kavita kaa gayan baul or kirthan paramparaon main karte hai. 
 saatyaki prashikshan ke dwaara ek itihaaskaar or sangeet vah prathaon main gambhir academik ruchi lete he.
 saatyaki 2005 se "parapar" band kaa hissaa hai. lekin vah bhi mausmi bhowmik ke saath swatantra rup se sangeet kaarykram main pradarshan karte hai. unka band desh va videshon main neuyork, landan, leeds, dhaka or chatgaanv main bhi kaarykram pradarshit kar chuke hai.
