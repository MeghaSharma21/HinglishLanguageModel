 tripitak
 angutar nikaay ek mahatvapurn buddha granth he. iske anuvadak bhadant anand koshalyayan hai. isko vartmaan samay main mahabodhi sabha, kalakatta dwaara prakashit kiya gaya he. buddha granth-baudhdhamtawalbiyon ne jis sahitya kaa srijan kiya, usamen bhartiya itihaas kii jaankaree ke liye prachur samagriyan nihit hai. ‘tripitak’ inkaa mahaan granth he. sut, vinay tathaa amidhamm milakar ‘tripitak’ kehalaate hai. buddha sangh, mixuon tathaa bhikshuniyon ke liye aacharniya niyam vidhaan vinay pitak main praapt hote hai. sutt pitak main buddhadev ke dharmopadesh hai.
 gautam nikaayon main vibhakt hai- isamen se 
 iske atirikt paanchaven, khuddak nikaay laghu granthon kaa sangrah he jo chhathi shatabdi i. purv se lekar maury kaal tak kaa itihaas prastut karata he. amidhamm pitak main buddha dharm ke darshanik siddhanth hai. kuch anya buddha granth bhi hai. milindmanh main unani shashak minundar or buddha mixu nagsen ke vaartalap kaa ullekh he.
