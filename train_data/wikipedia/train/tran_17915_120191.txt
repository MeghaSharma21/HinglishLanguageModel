 shubha mudgal bharat kii ek prasiddh hindustani shastreey sangeet, khyal, tumari, daadaraa or prachlit pop sangeet gayika hai. inhen 1996 main sarvshereshth gaiyr-feechar film sangeet nirdesan kaa neshnal award amrit beej ke liye mila tha. 1998 main sangeet main vishesh yogadan hetu gold plak award, 34wein chicago antarrashtriya film utsav main unki film dance of da wind ke liye mila tha. iske alaava inhen 2000 main padmashri bhi mil chukaa he.
