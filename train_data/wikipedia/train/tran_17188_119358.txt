 subharit cor ek pair kii dancer he jise uski pratibha va kaushal ke kaaran indiyaaz got tallent ke duusare round ke liye chunaa gaya. iskaa upnaam 'shubh' he. isane ek durghatana main apana ek pair kho diya he.
 subharit cor jo "ek pair kii dancer 'ke rup main lokapriy, ghumman gaanv, amargarh, jila sangrur kaa nivasee he. 21 oktober, 2009 ko skuti se hui ek sadak durghatana main apana ek pair kho diya he. doctor kii laaparvaahee ke kaaran usne apane ek pair kho diya he. usne vinod thakur kaa ek tivi sho par naach dekha tha or us ke baad usne ek pair ke saath naachna shuru karne kaa faisla kiya. shubh ne dainik abhyaas shuru kiya. usne nrity ke liye uske junun ko jari rakhaa or vah, "kuch bhi naammumkin nahin he ..... haar kabhi nahin" ko uske jivan kaa adarsh vaakya sabit kar diya.uskaa vishwaas he ki jivan sundar he, to ise khulkar jeeyen va anand len. maan shrimati charanjit cor, uski role model hai vah uske liye prerana hai. vah hameshaa use jivan main aage dekhne ke liye prerit karti hai.
 shubh ko uske nrity pradarshan ke liye india got tallent tivi sho main chunaa gaya.
 uske pradarshan ke baad jujon se use standing oveshan mili. subharit ke dance ne bollywood ke super star salmaan khan ko bhi uskaa kaayal bana diya.. salmaan khan ne twitor par uski prashansa karte hue likhaa "vaah yaar. salaam he. kamaal he.". ab subharit ko jhalak dikhalaa ja main ek pratiyogi ke rup main dekha jaataa he.
 subharit ne yash makkad shaadi kar lee lekin shaadi ke 2 maheene ke baad usne pati par utpeedan aarope laga us par cese kar diya. uske pati ke anusaar usne talaak ke liye ek badi rakam kii maang kii he. ab vah apni maan shrimati charanjit cor ke saath rah rahi he.
