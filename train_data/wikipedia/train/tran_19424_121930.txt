 maaikel terry "mike" duk ek america vyapaaree he. unhone 2009 se 2013 tak wal-mart main mukhya kaaryakari adhikari ke pad main sevarat rahen.
 1995 main duk wal-mart main shaamil ho gaye, vahaan ve company ke antarrashtriya parichaalan main seva kaaryakari ke rup main seva dene lage. farvari 2009 main ve wal-mart ke siio ban gayi.
 2012 main duk ne kaha he ki siio ke rup main unki sabase bada pachhtaawaa rahaa kii unhone i-commers main adhik nivesh karne par dhyaan nahin diya jabaki amejan ne is par kaam kar pratispardhaa main aage nikal gaya. "kaash ki ham ise or teji se le jate. hamane kai kshetron main khud ko safal sabit kiya he, or aashcharya he ki kyon ham is par adhik jaldi se kadam nahin uthaa sake. vishesh rup se i-commers ke kshetra main." duk ne us samay kaha ki "abhi ham jabardast pragati kar rahe hai, or vyapaar bhi bad rahaa he, par hame is kshetra main teji se vistaar karnaa chahie tha"
