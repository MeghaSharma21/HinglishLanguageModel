 dwarka prasad mishra bharat ke ek swatantraaasangram senaanee, rajaneta, patrkaar evam sahityakar the. ve madhya pradesh ke mukhyamantri rah chuke he.
 mishra ji shreshth kavi or patrkaar the. 1942 main jail main rahete hue unhone 'krishnayan' mahakavy kii rachana kii thi. krishna ke janm se lekar swargarohan tak kii katha is mahakavy main kahi gai he. mahabharat ke krishna hameshaa mishra ji ke adarsh rahe. prakhar patrkaar ke rup main mishra ji ne 1921 main 'shri sharada' masik, 1931 main 'dainik lokmat' or 1947 main saptahik 'sarathi' kaa sampaadan kiya. lala lajpat raay kii angrejon kii lathi se hui maut par 'lokmat' main likhe unke sampadakeey par pan motilal nehru ne kaha tha ki bharat kaa sarvshereshth faujadaari vakil bhi isse acha abhiyog patr taiyaar nahin kar saktaa. jawaharlal nehru se matbhed ke kaaran mishra ji ko teraa varshon tak raajnneetik vanvaas bhogana padaa.
 san 1954 se 64 tak unhone sagar vishvavidhyalay ke kulapati ke rup main vyateet kiya. mishra ji ke vidya-vyasan ke sambandh main kaha jaataa tha ki vishvavidhyalay ke kisi praadhyaapak yaa vidhyaarthee se adhik uske kulapati adhyayan-rat rahete hai. 1971 main raajnneeti se avkaash lekar unhone sara samay sahitya ko samarpit kar diya.
 angreji main apni aatmakatha 'living en era' likhi, jisamen beesvin sadi kaa puraa itihaas samahit hai. aitihaasik shodh granth likhe, jinamein 'studies in da proto history of india or 'search of lanka' vishesh ullekhneey hai. hindi, angreji, sanskrit or urdu bhashaa ke sahitya se unka gaharaa lagaav tha.
 sanskrit kaviyon or urdu ke shaayaron ke hindi anuwaad main unhen kaaphi ras miltaa tha. shatranj ke ve maahir khilaadi the. aise sahityakar, itihasvid or prakhar rajaneta kaa 5 may 1988 ko dilli main dehavsaan ho gaya. unka parthiv sharir jabalpur main narmada ke tat par panchtatv main leen hua.
