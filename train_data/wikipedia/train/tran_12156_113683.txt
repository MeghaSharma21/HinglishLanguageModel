 rishi bankim chandra college pashchim bengal ke naihati main sthit teen mahavidhyalayon kaa ek samooh he. ye college kala, vaanijya or vigyaan vishyon main snaatak paathyakram sanchalit karte hai. teenon college pashchim bengal rajya vishvavidhyalay se sambhddha hai.
 rishi bankim chandra college kii sthapana 1947 main kuch shikshavidon ke dwaara kii gai thi. aarambh main yah naihati mahendra skool bhawan main sanchalit hua karata tha tathaa kakshaayen shaam ko lagati thim. tab iskaa naam rishi bankim chandra college tha. baad ke varshon main college se diwas evam praatah kaaleen pariyon ke anubhag jude.
