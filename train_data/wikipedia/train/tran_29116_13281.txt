 vidyut ejiniyari main armechar, vidyut mashiinon kaa mukhya bhaag he. vidyut-yantric mashiinon yaa vidyut-mashiinon ke do mukhya bhaagon main se ek ko armechar kehete hai. kintu sthaayee chumbak yaa vidyut chumbak ke pole pis ko bhi armechar kaha jaataa he. isi prakaar parinalika yaa riley ke lohe se nirmit chalyman bhaag ko bhi armechar kehete hai.
