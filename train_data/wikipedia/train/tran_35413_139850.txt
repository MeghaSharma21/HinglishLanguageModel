 tripprayar shri rama mandir
 
 trippaiyar, yani tripprayar shri rama mandir bharat ke kerala rajya main trishur naamak shahar se lagbhag 25 kilometre dur main sthit dakshin pashchimi shahar he.
 yah mandir bhagwaan shri ram ko samarpit he. hindu puraano ke anusaar bhagwaan shri vishnu kii rup dharen murtiyon ram, lakshmana, bharat or shatrughna ko milte jhulate murtiyan tripprayar ke kinare aa pahunchaa or vakkel camel naamak ek sthaneey netaa dwaara un murtiyon ko kramshah tripprayar, thirumuzikkalam, kudalmaanikkam or pammel aadi punya sthalon par veda ghoshon ke sahare prathishtit kiya gaya. vakkel kovilakam ke vanshaj dakshin kii ore chale gaye or trikpaleshwar ke bhakt ban gayi or bhagwaan shri krashna ke camel charnon par mukti paane ke liye, niraanan naamak ek jagah or talwari naamak gaanv main bas gaye. tachchuday caimaal, jo kaaryvahak ke rup main kaam karata tha irinjalakura main nivas karte the. ek hi din main in sabhi chaar devataaon kii puja karnaa vishesh rup se shubhadhayak mana jaataa he.
 murti kaa rup bahut hi vishesh purv he or chatur bhuj dhari vishnu kaa rup he or ram daanav kaada par vijeta ke rup main dikhaaya gaya he. kaha jaataa he ki brahma or shivaji ke ansh bhi is murti main shaamil he jiske hisaab se yah vigrah ek trimurti mana jaataa he. is mandir kii bahari angan main bhagwaan shri ayyapp ko darshan karke samaran karne kaa mandir bana hua he.
 mandir ke mukhya tyohaar puram hai or ekadshi utsav bhi manaaya jaataa he . mandir main lokapriy balidaan main se ek-uttu he, jo nadi main tairne vaali machhliyon ke liye chaaval kaa anaaj khilaane kaa prabandh mandir ke ek seemaa ke andar nadi ke kinare kiya gaya he.
 kaise vahaan pahuunche ?triprayar, trishur se sadak maarg main bahut aaraam se pahunch sakate he, jo ek pramukh railway stesan bhi he. yah kochi ke hawaii adde se kareeb 60 kilometre dur he.
