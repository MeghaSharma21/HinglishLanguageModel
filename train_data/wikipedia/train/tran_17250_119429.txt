 sandhya shrikant vishweshwariyaa bhartiya vigyaan santhaan, bangalore main ek vaigyanik or shikshak hai. vah vartmaan main aanvik prajnan vibhaag, vikaas or anuvanshiki vibhaag ke adhyaksh hai or biosystems science and ejineeringanvik kii sah-adhyaksh he.
 vishweshwariya ne 1977 main osmania vishvavidhyalay, hyderabad main bachaler of science kii degree praapt kii or phir 1980 main bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan, kanpur se rasayan vigyaan main vigyaan kii degree praapt kii. vishweshwariya ne 1987 main bangalore ke estra research centre, main post-doctoral phelo ke rup main apana kariyar shuru kiya tha. phir unhen usee sanghathan main ek vaigyanik sthaan par padonnat kiya gaya, jahaan vah 1993 tak rahe. vishweshwariyaa ne 25 se adhik piechdi chaatro ko salaah di he.
