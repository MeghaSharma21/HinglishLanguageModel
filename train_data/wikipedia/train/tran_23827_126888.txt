 kisi vastu ko ekbar se adhik baar prayog karne ko punahprayog yaa punarprayog kehete hai. yah baar-baar prayog vastu ke mul kaam ke liye ho saktaa he yaa alag-alag kaam ke liye. punarchakran isse is arth main alag he ki punarchakran main prayog kiye gayi saamaan ko todkar yaa barbaad karke padaarth rup main badal diya jaataa he. upyogi utpaadon ko kharidne or unka vinimay karne se samay, dhan, urja, evam sansadhano kii bachat hoti he.
