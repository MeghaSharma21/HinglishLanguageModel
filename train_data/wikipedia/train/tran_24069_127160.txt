 bhartiya darshan main kashay shabd kaa prayog vishesh rup se raag, dwesh aadi dosha ke liye hua he. 
 chhandogya upanishad ke anusaar mridit kashay naarad ko bhagavan sanatkumar ne avidhyaroopa tam ke paar parmarthatatv ko dikhalaayaa. shankaracharya ke mat se gyaan, vairaygy or abhyaas se kashay kaa naash hota he. 
 buddha darshan main is shabd kaa prayog ashuddhi, patan tathaa kshay ke arth main hua he. uske anusaar kashay paanch prakaar ke hai- aayu, drushti, klesh, satv tathaa kalp. kashayon ke kaaran aayu ksheen hoti he, mithyaa drushti utpann hoti he, klesh hote hai, praaniyon kaa hraas hota he tathaa sansar ke ek kalp athwa yug kaa kshay hota he. 
 jain darshan main kashay ke mukhya chaar bhed- krodh, maan, maaya tathaa lobh maane gaye hai. inke kaaran jeev main pudgal kanon kaa ashrav hota he or yah karmbandhan se adhikadhik grast hota jaataa he. jeev kii kashay sahit tathaa kashairahit, ye do avasthaaen hoti hai. kashayon kaa vinaash hone par hi jeev ko moksh praapt hota he.
