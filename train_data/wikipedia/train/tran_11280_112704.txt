 harith vaahan aise vaahanon ko kehete hai, jisase bahut kam pradooshan hota he or paryaavaran par bahut kam dushprabhav padataa he. yah petrol aadi se chalate hai, lekin inase utana adhik pradooshan nahin hota he. kuch harith vaahan vaikalpik strot se chalate hai. jaise vidyut, prakritik gas dwaara aadi.
 yah vidyut dwaara chalati he. iskaa mukhya kharch isaki bydaree he. yah petrol or dijal kii tulana main 0% se 99% tak carbon CO2 ko or badhane nahin detii he. arthath anya kii tulana main yah paryaavaran ke liye laabhadaayak he.
 yah vidyut ke saath saath aanshik rup se jeevashm eendhan kaa bhi upyog karte hai. yah bahut mahangaa hota he. lekin paanch varshon main isaki bharpai ho jaati he.
 iske saath hi anya kai prakaar ke vaahan paryaavaran ko pradooshan se bachaane ke liye laabhadaayak he. inamen saura urja or pawan urja dwaara vidyut paidaa kar chalane vaale vaahan bhi shaamil he. lekin yah sabhi vaahan mukhyatah saura urja or pawan urja par nirbhar karte hai. yadi badal vaala mausam hota he yaa raat hota he to saura urja se chalaane vaale vaahan kaary nahin kar sakate hai. pawan urja se chalane vaale vaahan bhi keval tej hawa ke dauraan hi acchhee tarah se kaary kar sakate hai. lekin yah aaweshit bydaree kaa upyog kar bhi anya samay par iskaa upyog kar sakate hai.
