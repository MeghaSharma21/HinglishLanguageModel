 
 ashtapaad praani jagat main ek varg he jis main sandhipad aksheruki praani hai. jaise ki naam bataata he is varg ke sabhi praaniyon ke aath paanv yaa paad hote hai.
 lagbhag sabhi maujuuda ashtapaad bhuchar praani hai. parantu kuch mithe jal ke paryaavarnon main tathaa, mahasagariya katibandh ko chhod kar, samudri paryaavarnon main bhi basate hai. is varg main ek lakh se adhik naamak jatiyaan hai jin main makadi, bichchhu, kilni adi shaamil hai.
