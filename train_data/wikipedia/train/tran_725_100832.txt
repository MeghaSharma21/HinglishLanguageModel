 anterrashtriya antariksh stesan yaa kendra bahari antariksh main anusandhan suvidha yaa shodh sthal he jise prithvi kii nikatavarti kaksha main sthapit kiya he. is pariyojna kaa aarambh 1998 main hua tha or yah 2011 tak ban kar taiyaar hoga. vartmaan samay tak ieses ab tak banaaya gaya sabase bada maanav nirmit upgrah hoga. ieses kaarykram vishwa kii kai spes agenciyon kaa sanyukt upkram he. ise banane main sanyukt rajya kii nasa ke saath rus kii russian fedral spes agency, japan airospes exploration agency, canada kii kenadian spes agency or europea deshon kii sanyukt european spes agency kaam kar rahi hai. inke atirikt brazilian spes agency bhi kuch anubandhon ke saath nasa ke saath karyrat he. isi tarah italian spes agency bhi kuch alag anubandhon ke saath karyrat he.
 prithvi kii nichali kaksha main sthapit hone ke baad ieses ko nangi ankhon se dekha ja sakegaa. yah prithvi se kareeb 350 kilometre upar ausatan 27,724 kilometre pratighanta kii raftaar se parikrama karegaa or pratidin 15.7 chakkar puure karegaa. pichley ieses main, jise november 2000 main kaksha main sthapit kiya gaya tha, ke baad se usamen lagaataar maanviya upasthiti bani hui he. vartmaan samay main isamen teen vyaktiyon kaa sthaan he. bhavishya main isamen chah vyaktiyon ke rahane laayak jagah banegi. antarrashtriya spes stesan antariksh main sthit ek vedhashala ke taur par kaary karata he. anya antariksh yanon ke mukaable iske kai phaayde hai jisamen isamen rahane vaale estronots ko adhik samay tak antariksh main rahakar kaam karne kaa mauka miltaa he.
