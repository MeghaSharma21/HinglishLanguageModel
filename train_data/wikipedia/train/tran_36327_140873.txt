 hazarat shahajalaal anterrashtriya vimaanashetra
  hazarat shahajalaal anterrashtriya vimaanashetra , purv naam zia anterrashtriya vimaanashetra evam dhaka anterrashtriya vimaanashetra, bangladesh kaa sabase bada vimaanashetra he. yah dhaka shahar main sthit he or yahaan 1980 se prachaalan aarambh hua or puraane tejagaanv airport ke baad yahaan se desh ke sabhi anterrashtriya prachaalan aarambh ho gayi. yah sabhi bangladeshi vayusevaon kaa grih-base evam hub he, jinamein bimaan bangladesh airlines, jiemji airlines, regent airways evam united airways aate hai.
 yah vimaanashetra 1,981 acre main bana he or desh ke lagbhag 66% anterrashtriya evam antardeshiy aagaman evam prasthaan yahaan se hi sampann hote hai. iske baad desh kaa doosraa bada vimaanashetra he shah amaanat anterrashtriya vimaanashetra jo chittagong main sthti he yatri sankhya kaa 21% bhaag vahan karata he. dhaka vimaanashetra se vaarshik lagbhag 40 lakh anterrashtriya evam 20 lakh antardeshiy yatri tathaa 1,50,000 ton maal yatayaat sampann hota he. is vimaanashetra kii yatri kshamta 80 lakh yatri vaarshik he, or si.e.e.bi dwaara yahaan ke liye 2026 tak ke liye paryapshnumanit kii gai he. vartmaan main yahaan se 26 vayusevaayen vishwa ke 19 deshon ke 34 shaharon ko anterrashtriya evam antardeshiy gantavyon ko jodatii hai. inke alaava 7 maal evam cargo vayusevaayen bhi yahaan se sanchalit hoti hai. yahaan kii dainik yatayaat gatividhi maatra 190 he.
 yahaan kii rajkeey vayuseva bimaan bangladesh airlines yahaan se europe evam asia ke 20 gantavyon ko udaanen uplabdh karaati he.
