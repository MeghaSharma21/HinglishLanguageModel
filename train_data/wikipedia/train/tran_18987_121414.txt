 rasinic jati aise parmaanu, anu, anuon ke ansh or aayan hote hai jinpar koi rasinic prakriya yaa maapan kara jaae. kisi ek jati ke sadsy rasinic drushti se bilkul ek jaise hote hai, yani inamen ek jaise rasinic tatv ek hi style se jude hue hote hai. kisi bhi mishran main dravyaman varnakrammap jaisi takneekon se alag-alag rasinic jatiyon ko alag kara ja saktaa he.
