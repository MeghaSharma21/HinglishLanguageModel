 kerala rajya aids niyantran socity, echivi / aids mahamari kaa muqabla karne main rajya kii ranneeti main ek nirnaayak bhoomika nibhaati he. iskaa kaam ek shaskaiya nikaay kii dekharekh ke dwaara kiya jaataa he jo rajya ke mukhya sachiv kii adhyakshata main hota he. kerala rajya aids niyantran socity kaa rajya main rashtriya aids niyantran kaarykram ko lagu karne ke liye gathan kiya gaya tha. yah rashtriya aids niyantran sanghathan, jo bharat sarkaar ke swasthya evam parivaar kalyaan mantralay kaa ek hissaa he, uske tahat kaam karti he. rashtriya aids niyantran kaarykram puri tarah se bharat sarkaar dwaara vitt samarthit he tathaa use vishwa bank, global fund aids, tibi or malaria, diefidi or uasaid kaa samarthan praapt he.
 rashtriya aids niyantran kaarykram ke lakshya or uddeshyon ko rokane ke liye or rokthaam, upachaar, dekhbhaal or sahaayta kaaryakramon ke ekikaran ke madhyam se rajya main mahamari bachaav hai. iske chaar ghatak hai:
 ekikrut paraamarsh or pareekshan kendron-128anti retroviral therapy clinics - 614 swaagat kendra sanchaarit rog clinics-21
 socity ke bare main puri jaankaree ke saath apane sampark kaa vivaran fesabook par daal diya gaya he. diwaar par kai sandesh or unke uttar hai. vartmaan main, vahaan 8 prashansakon jo keval samay or badhati jaagrukta ke saath sudhaar hoga.
 yah dilachasp he ki kerala rajya aids niyantran socity kaa niskriy khaataa twitter par maujuud he jo 2010 ke baad se niskriy he. 2009 main 5 or 2010 main 2 twitter sandesh dekhe gaye, jisase yah spasht hota he ki socity is manch ke liye taiyaar nahin thi.
