 jyaan christof france ke suprasiddh sahityakar romaa rolland likhit vrihad upanyaas he, jis par unhen 1915 i0 main sahitya kaa nobel puraskaar praapt hua tha.
 'jyaan christof' multah french main likhit ek vrihad upanyaas he. yah jeniyas ke ek sangeetkaar ke vikaas kii itihaas-katha he. yah vrihad granth 3 khand tathaa 10 bhaagon main vibhajit he.
 mul french main iskaa prakaashan 1904 i0 se 1912 i0 tak main sampann hua. french se angreji main iskaa anuwaad gilbert canan naamak vidwaan ne kiya. angreji main 1910 se 1913 i0 tak main iskaa prakaashan america ke henry holt and company, neuyork se sampann hua.
 is vrihat granth ke khandon kaa vibhaajan is prakaar he :-
  
 angreji main anudit is vrihat upanyaas main bhoomika, vishay-suchi aadi ke 8 prishth sahit kul prishth-sankhya 1586 he.
