 pashchim qazakhstan praant madhya asia ke qazakhstan desh kaa ek praant he. isaki rajdhani oraal naam kaa shahar he, jise 'uralsk' bhi kaha jaataa he. is praant kii sarahadein rus se lagati hai or yah ural pahaado ke paas sthit he. ural nadi praant se guzarakar caspian sagar kii taraf jaati he. bhugolic rup se is praant ko aksar purvi europe main mana jaataa he.
 pashchim qazakhstan pradesh kaa pehla gathan soviet sangh ke jamaane main san 1932 main kiya gaya. 1962 main iskaa naam badalakar uralsk pradesh kar diya gaya lekin 1992 main ise phir se 'pashchim qazakhstan' kaa naam de diya gaya.
 2009 kii janaganana main pashchim qazakhstan ke 69.8% log kazakh samudaay or 27.4% roosi samudaay ke the. haalanki qazakhstan ke bahut se anya bhaagon main german, ukraini or polish log rahete hai in samudaayon kii aabaadi pashchim qazakhstan main kaafii kam he.
