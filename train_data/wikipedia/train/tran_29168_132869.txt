 krishngupta chhathin saatvin shatee i. main magadh par shashan karanevaale uttarvarti guptavansh ke sansthaapak. inkaa ullekh apsadh or dev barnarak se mili abhilekhon main hua he. kuch itihaaskaaron kii dharna he ki ye chandragupta ke jyeshtha putr the jinka ulalekh vaishali se praapt muhar par govind gupt ke naam se hua. kintu is kalpana ke liye koi sabal aadhaar nahin he.
