 OpenSolaris ek open sorce oparating he Sun Microsystems Solaris par aadhaarit pranali he. yah bhi Sun dwaara shuru kii chaaron ore ek develper or upyogkarta samudaay ke nirmaan pariyojna kaa naam he.
 OpenSolaris Unix System V Release 4 se praapt, mahatvapurn Sun dwaara kiye gaye sanshodhano ke saath he, kyonki yah 1994 main us code ke adhikaar kharide. yah keval mukt strot pranali ve vyutpann uplabdh he. khulaa ghatakon ke vikaas ke antargat naveenatam relies Solaris ke snapashot hai. Sun kii ghoshnaa kii he ki isaki vanijyik Solaris oparating system ke bhavishya ke sanskarnon OpenSolaris pariyojna se praudyogiki par aadhaarit hoga.
