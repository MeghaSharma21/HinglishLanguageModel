 gauri scindia ek bhartiya vigyaapan-film or feechar film nirdeshika hai. scindia ne apane dishaatmak karier kii shuruyaat atyadhik prashansit english vinglish se kii, jisase abhinetri shridevi ne apni vaapasi kii. scindia financial times kii 2012 kii suchi '25 indian tu vaach" main shaamil hui. radiff kii suchi 'bollywood ke 2012 ke 5 sabase achche nirdeshkon' main bhi wo shaamil hui he. 
 gauri scindia kaa laalan paalan pune main hua, jahaan unhonecent joseph hai skool main adhyayan kiya or uske pashchaat simbyosis institute of maas communication pune se snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii. unamem film banane kii akanksha apane college ke antim dinon main hi utpann ho gayi thi. 
 scindia kaa vivaah aar balki se 2007 main hua tha.
