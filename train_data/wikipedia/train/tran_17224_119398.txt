 jalawaashp athwa jal vaashp pani kii gasiy avasthaa he or anya avasthaaon ke vipareet adrishy hoti he. prithvi ke vayumandal main isaki maatra lagaataar parivartansheel hoti he. drav avasthaa main sthit pani se jalawaashp kaa nirmaan kvathan athwa vashpikaran ke dwaara hota rahata he or sanghanan dwaara jalawaashp drav avasthaa main bhi parivartit hoti rahati he. barf se iskaa nirmaan urdhvapathan kii prakriya dwaara hota he.
