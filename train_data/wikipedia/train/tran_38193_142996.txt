 chaanod pashchim bhartiya rajya gujarat ke valsad jile kaa ek kasbaa he.
 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar, chaanod kii jansankhya 11,958 he. isamen purush 61% evam mahilaayein 39% hai. chaanod kii ausat sakshartaa dar 72% he jo bharat ke rashtriya ausat 59.5% se adhik he. chaanod kii 16 pratishat jansankhya 6 varsh se kam aayu ke bacchon kii he.
