 chaitanya mahaprabhu par bhi unke charitra ko prakaash main laane vaale jeevani ke sutron par sadhe granth brajabhasha main likhe gaye. aisa ek mahatvapurn granth shri chaitanya charitamrita he. yah kaviraj shri krishnadas ke isi naam ke prasiddh granth kaa sukal shyaam athwa benikrishna krit "brajabhasha" rupantar he. yah 1775 ke lagbhag pranit hua.
