 nuristaani samudaay purvi afghanistan ke nuristaan ilaake main rahane vaali ek jaati he. nuristaan ke log apane sunahare baalo, hari-neeli aankhon or gore rang ke liye jane jate hai. unnisvi sadi ke ant tak nuristniyon kaa dharm hindu dharm se miltaa julata ek ati-pracheen hind-irani dharm tha or nuristaan ko ird-gird ke muslim ilaaqon ke log "kaafiristaan" ke naam se jaanate the. sainkadon-hazaron varshon tak yahan ke log swatantra rahe or yahan tak ki pandrahavi sadi ke akramanakari taimurlang ko bhi haraa diya. 1890 ke dashak main afghanistan ke amir abdur rahamaan khan ne is ilaake par hamalon ke baad is par qabza kar liya. iske baad yahan ke logon ko majabuuran muslim bananaa padaa or is kshetra kaa naam bhi badal kar nuristaan kar diya gaya, jiskaa arth he "nur" kaa sthaan. yahan ke logon ke reeti-rivaaj or dharmik manyataon main abhi bhi unke islam se purv ke dharm ke tatv milte hai.
 nuristaan se lage hue pakistan ke uttar-pashchimi chitral ilaake ke kalash log nuristniyon se milte-julate hai, lekin ve unnisvi sadi main british samrajya ke adheen bharat main aate the, isliye ve abdur rahamaan khan ke hamalon se bache rahe or unamem se kai abhi bhi apane purvajon ke dharm ke anuyaayi hai. alag-alag nuristaani or kalash kshetron main bhashaa kaa bhi antar he, lekin ye sari bhashaayen dardi bhashaaon main hi gini jaati hai.
