 dwadashi shiya yaa barahave shiya yaa imami shiya shiya islam kii sabase badi shakha he. yah maanate hai ki inke pehle baarah imam divya rup se chune hue the. inaki manyata he ki barahave imam jo 873-874 isvi main gaayab ho gaye the, bhavishya main laut kar aayenge. in aane vaale barahave imam ko 'mahadi' kaha jaataa he. puure shiya samudaay main se lagbhag 85% barahave shiya hi hote hai.
 barahave shiyaon kii manyataayen zadi shiya or ismaili shiya jaise anya sambandhit samudaayon se milti hai haalanki unamem aapas main imaamon kii sankhya ko lekar or ek imam se agle imam kii uttaradhikarita ko lekar asahmati he. yah imam kii bhoomika or paribhaasha ko lekar bhi aapas main asahmat hai.
 is silsile ko maanane waalon ko baarah imami yaa isneshari shiya kaha jaataa he.
