 fritaun sierra leone kii rajdhani or sabase bada shhar he. yah andh mahaasaagar ke tat par ek pramukh bandargah he or sierra leone ke pashchimi kshetra main sthit he. yah desh kaa shahari, vyapaarik, samskruthik, shaikshik or rajnatik centre he. 2004 mardumshumaari ke mutabiq shahar kii aabaadi 772, 873 thi. 2010 main isaki aabaadi kaa andaaza 12 lakh par laga he. isaki arthvyavastha mote taur par isaki bandargah par gumti he - jisane sierra leone dariyaa jowar muhaano kaa zyaadaatar bhaag gheraa hua he or jo duniya kii sabase bade gahare qudarti bandargahon main se ek he.
