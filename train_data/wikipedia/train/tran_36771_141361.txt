 dishtdhaara motor vidyut mashin he jo vidyut urja ko yantric urja main badalati he. 
 disi motor main bahut se aapas main sambhddha chaalkon kaa tantra rahata he, jo ek armechar par aropit hota he. armechar, naram lohe kii bahut si patticoon ko jodakar bana hota he or belanaakaar hota he. isamen chaaron ore khaanche kate hue hote hai, jinamein chaalak samoohon ko kundali athwa dandon ke rup main rakhaa jaataa he. in chaalkon ko, ek nishchit yojana ke anusaar, aapas main ek duusare se sambhddha kiya jaataa he. is nishchit kram ko armechar kundalan kehete hai. vibhinn prakaar ke kundalanon ke vishisht lakshan hote hai, jinke vishisht prakaar ke kundalanon ke vishisht lakshan hote hai, nike vishisht laabh hote hai. chumbakeey kshetra bhi ek duusare chaalak samooh main se dhara ko pravaahit kar praapt kiya jaataa he. disht dhara motaron ke armechar chaalkon main dhara burushon dwaara le jaai jaati he. ye burush, vastut: armechar se sambhddha dikparivartak par aropit hote hai or sambharan se sambhddha hote hai. chumbakeey kshetra utpann karanevaale kundalanon se sambhddha hote hai. chumbakeey kshetra utpann karanevaale kundalanon ko saamanyat: kshetra kundali kehete hai. ye kundaliyaan armechar kundalan se shrenee main sambhddha yaa samaantar main sambhddha yaa samaantar main sambhddha ho sakate hai. yah bhi ho saktaa he ki unke kuch kundalan shrenee main hon or kuch samaantar main. 
 kshetra kundalan ke is prakaar sangyojan ke aadhaar par teen vibhinn praroop ke disht dhara motor praapt hote hai-
 shrenee motor main jo dhara armechar main se hokar pravaahit hoti he, vahi kshetra kundali main bhi pravaahit hoti he. at:, isaki kshetra kundali main mote taar ke bahut kam kundalan hote hai. shant motor main purna dhara kaa kuch ansh hi kshetra kundali main hokar bahata he, jo uske aarpaar boltata tathaa kundalan ke pratirodh par nirbhar karata he. at: isi kshetra kundali main bahut patale taar ke bahut adhik kundalan hote hai, jisase is kundali kaa pratirodh saamanyat: kai sau om hota he.
 vibhinn prarupon ke disht dhara motaron ke lakshan bhi bahut bhinn bhinn hote hai or unheen ke anusaar inkaa prayog bhi bhinn bhinn prayojanon ke liye hota he. shant motor lagbhag sthir chaal par chalate hai or bhaar ke saath unka chaal vicharan adhik nahin hota. at: ve un sab upyogon main prayukt hote hai jahaan ekasam chaal kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. ye tram, lift, kraine ityadi ke liye bade upyogi hai. kisi bhaar ko chalan main laane se pehle adhik bal lagaana padataa he, par jab vah chalane lagata he tab utane bal kii aavashyaktaa nahin rahati. atev shrenee motor in prayuktiyon ke liye adarsh hote hai or inkaa upyog vistrut rup main hota he .
 adhikansh prayojanon ke liye shant tathaa shrenee prarupon ke bich kii aavashyaktaa hoti he, jo sanyukt motor dwaara praapt kii ja sakati he.
