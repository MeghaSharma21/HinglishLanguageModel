 rashtrakul, yaa rashtramandal desh, 53 swatantra rajyon kaa ek sangh he jisame saare rajya angreji rajya kaa hissaa the . iskaa mukhyalay landan main sthit he. iskaa mukhya uddeshy loktantr, sakshartaa, maanavadhikaar, behatar prashasan, mukt vyapaar or vishwa shanti ko badhaava dena he. england kii maharani elizabeth dwitiya prayek chaar varsh main hone vaale rashtramandal khelon or baithak main bhaag leti hai. isaki sthapana 1931 main hui thi, lekin iskaa aadhunik swaroop 1947 main bharat or pakistan ke swatantra hone ke baad nishchit hua. 
 rashtramandal yaa rashtrakul deshon kaa koi samvidhan yaa charter nahin he. iske pramukhon kii prayek do varsh main ek baar baithak hoti he. bharat sahit antigua, australia, bangladesh, bruneii, canada, cyprus, ghana aadi iske sadsy hai. zimbabwe ko 2002 main rashtrakul kii sadsyta se hataaya gaya tha or 2003 main yah pratibandh anishchit kaal tak badhaayaa gaya tha. rashtrakul samooh ke deshon kii kul jansankhya 1.9 arab he, jo vishwa kii jansankhya kii ek-tihaai bhaag he. fiji ko kaamanwealth samooh se 2000-01 main pratibandhit kiya gaya tha, uske baad pun: us par 2006 main pratibandh laga. nigeria ko 1995 se 1999 tak pratibandhit kiya gaya. pakistan par 1999 main pratibandh laga tha.
 landan ghoshnaa ke tahat britain kii maharani elizabeth dwitiya rashtramandal deshon ke samooh kii pramukh hoti hai. rashtramandal sachivalay kii sthapana 1965 main hui thi. iske mahasachiv mukhya kaaryakari ke taur par kaam karte hai. vartmaan main kamalesh sharma iske mahasachiv hai. unka chayan november, 2007 ko hua. iske pehle mahasachiv canada ke ornald smith the.
 desh jinaki sadsyta abhi nilambit he ve tede likhe gayi hai.
