 nagapattanam ke nikat, karaikal ke uttar main sthit tarangambadi 1620 se 1845 ke dauraan ek danish colony thi. tamil bhashaa main tarangambadi kaa taatpary hota he laharon ke geet gane kaa sthaan. 1620 main thanjore ke shaasha kii anumati ke baad raghunatha nayak ne yahaan bich ke purv main ek shandaar kila banwaaya. yahaan bich ke nikat hi governor kaa mahal bana hua he. tarangambadi nagapattinam se 35 kilometre kii dur sthit he jahaan denis vaastukala ke anek namoonay dekhe ja sakate hai.
