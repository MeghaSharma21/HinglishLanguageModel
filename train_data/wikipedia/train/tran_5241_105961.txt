 
 bhaangadh durg bharat ke rajasthan main sthit 17veen shatabdi main nirmit ek durg he. ise maan singh pratham ne apane chote bhai maadho singh pratham ke liye banwaaya tha. is durg kaa naam bhaan singh ke naam par he jo maadho singh ke pitamah the.
 is durg kii seemaa ke baahar ek nayaa gaanv basaa he jisamen lagbhag 200 ghar or jansankhya 1300 he. yah durg or iskaa ahaata acchhee tarah sangrakshit he.
 is kile kii dekh rekh bharat sarkaar dwaara kii jaati he. kile ke chaaron taraf bhartiya puraatatv surveykshan kii team maujuud rahati hai. puraatatv vibhaag dwaara is kshetra main suryaast ke baad kisi bhi vyakti ke rukne kii anumati nahin he.
 bhaangadh kila satrahavin shatabdi main banwaaya gaya tha. is kile kaa nirmaan maan singh ke chote bhai raja maadho singh ne karavaaya tha. raja maadho singh us samay akbar ke sena main general ke pad par tainaat the. us samay bhangad kii jansankhya takreeban 10,000 thi. bhaangadh alvaar jile main sthit ek shandaar kila he jo ki bahut hi vishaal aakaar main taiyaar kiya gaya he.
 chaaro taraf se pahaado se ghire is kile main behtareen shilpakalao kaa prayog kiya gaya he. iske alaava is kile main bhagwaan shiv, hanuman aadi ke behtareen or ati praachin mandir vidhyamaan he. is kile main kul paanch dwaar hai or saath saath ek mukhya diwaar he. is kile main drin or majboot pattharon kaa prayog kiya gaya he jo ati praachin kaal se apane yatha sthiti main pade huye hai.
 philhal is kile kii dekh rekh bharat sarkaar dwaara kii jaati he. kile ke chaaron taraf bhartiya puraatatv surveykshan kii team maujuud rahati hai. puraatatv vibhaag dwaara suryaast ke baad is kshetra main kisi bhi vyakti ke rukne kii manaahi he.
