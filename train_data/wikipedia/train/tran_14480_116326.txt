 proseonidi maansaahaari gana ke jaanwaron kaa ek kul jo uttar america or dakshin america main paae jate hai. inamen rekun, koati, kinkashew, olingo, challa-dum or kakomisal shaamil hai.
 prosyonidaaye gana ke jaanavar sarvahari hote hai. is vajah se unke daant maansaahaari jaanwaron jaise tikhe nahin hote haalanki ve maance khaane main bhalee bhaanti sakasham hai. in pashuon kaa aakaar chota, sharir patala or dumen lambi hoti hai. kinkashew ke alaava har prosyonidai kii punch par challe-jaisi dhaariyaan hoti hai. bhaaluon or manushyon kii tarah yah apane paanv ke taluon par chalate hai, jabaki gaay or oont jaise jaanavar apane khuron par or kute or kyoti jaise pashu apni ungliyon par chalate hai. koati ko chhodkar sabhi prosyonidai jaanavar nishachari hote hai. prosyonidai akele jivan basar karne vaale pashu hai. isaki maadaa har varsh aamtaur par chaar bacchon ke daste ko janm detii he.
