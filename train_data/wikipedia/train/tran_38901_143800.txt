 aatma yaa aatman pad bhartiya darshan ke mahatvapurn pratyayon main se ek he. yah upanishdon ke moolbhoot vishay-vastu ke rup main aata he. jahaan isse abhipraay vyakti main anternihit us moolbhoot sat se kiya gaya he jo ki shaashvat tatva he tathaa mrityu ke pashchaat bhi jiskaa vinaash nahin hota. 
 aatma kaa nirupan shrimadbhagavadagita yaa gita main kiya gaya he. aatma ko shastr se kaata nahin ja saktaa, agni use jala nahin sakati, jal use geela nahin kar saktaa or vaayu use sukha nahin sakati.jis prakaar manushya puraane vastron ko tyaag kar naye vastra dhaaran karata he, usee prakaar aatma puraane sharir ko tyaag kar naveen sharir dhaaran karata he. 
 jain darshan main aatma ke liye jeev shabd kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. jeev ko ajeev se pruthak bataaya jaataa he.
