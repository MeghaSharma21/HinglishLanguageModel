 pradhyumna krishna kaav plasma bhautik vigyaani evam plasma anusandhan santhaan ke purv nideshak hai. unka janm 15 janvari 1948 ko srinagar, bharat main hua. unhone apni metric pariksha punjab vishvavidhyalay se or snatokatar agra vishvavidhyalay se 1964 main uteern kii. unhone apni piechdi bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan dilli se 1966 main purna kii. unke piechdi paryvekshak pro em es sodha the or unhone bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan dilli se pratham piechdi purna karne kaa gaurav praapt kiya. 
 unhe bharat ke nagarik puraskaar ke rup main 1985 main padam shri or 1986 main shanti swaroop bhatnagar puraskaar se nawaja gaya.
