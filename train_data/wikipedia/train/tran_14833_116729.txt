 ob nadi yaa obi nadi uttari asia ke pashchimi siberia kshetra kii ek nadi he or duniya kii saatvi sabase lambi nadi he. yenicey nadi or lenaa nadi ke saath yah arctic sagar main bahane vaali teesri mahaan siberian nadi maani jaati he. ob kii khadi duniya kii sabase lambi eschueri maani jaati he .
 ob kii shuruyaat rus ke altaai kroy pradesh ke biyask shahar se 26 kimi dakshin-pashchim main bia nadi or katun nadi ke sangam se hoti he. yah donon hi nadiyaan altaai parvaton main shuru hoti hai - bia nadi teletscoye jhil se or 700 kimi lambi katun nadi byelukha parvat ke upar sthit ek himani ) se. kaanti-manseinsk shahar ke paas iskaa vilay irtish nadi ke saath hota he, jo swayan ek mahaan nadi he or ob kii sabase badi upanadi he. agar irtish nadi kii shuruyaat se ob nadi ke arctic sagar main vilay tak kii duuri ko dekha jaae to ob-irtish kii puri dhara 5,410 kilometre lambi he. ob nadi main irtish ke alaava or bhi nadiyaan vilay hoti hai, jaise kii tom nadi, chulayim nadi, ket nadi, tyim nadi, vaakh nadi, vasayugan nadi or soswa nadi. yahan se yah tedi-medhi hoti hui uttar kii ore chalati he or kaara sagar kii ob khadi main ja milti he.
 china kii yangtse nadi or pili nadi ke baad ob-irtish nadi asia kii teesri sabase lambi nadi he. iskaa jalsambhar ilaaka bhi vishaal he or uskaa kshetrafal 29,90,000 varg kimi he.
