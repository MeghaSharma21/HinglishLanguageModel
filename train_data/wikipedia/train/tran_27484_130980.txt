  bishap udamula bala shoreddy warangal ke roman catholic suba ke sevarat bishap hai
  unka janm 18 june 1 9 54 ko ghanpur, andhra pradesh, bharat main hua tha.
 unhone alfonsienam, rome se naitik dharamshastra main doctoret kaa adhigrahan kiya he.
 unhen 20 farvari 1 9 7 9 ko ek pujari banaaya gaya. unhone bishap banane se pehle apane suba main kai dehati or prashasnic bhoomikaayen kin. vah catholic bishps sammelan of india, bangalore ke up-mahanideshak the. 1994 se 2006 tak unhone saint jon kshetreey seminary, hyderabad main naitik dharamshastra ko padhaayaa.
 13 april 2013 ko pope francis dwaara reddy ko warangal ke bharat ke bishap niyukt kiya gaya tha. oswald cardinal gratius dwaara 23 may 2013 ko unhen bishap kaa sthaan diya gaya tha.
