 baagh gufaaen, madhya pradesh main dhar jile se 97 kilometre dur vindhya parvat ke dakshini dhalaan par hai. ye indore or vadodara ke bich main baghini nadi ke kinare sthit hai. 
 in gufaaon kaa sambandh buddha dharm se he. yahaan anek buddha math or mandir dekhe ja sakate hai. in gufaaon main chaitanya haul main stupa hai or rahane kii kothri bhi bani hai jahaan boddha bhikshu rahaa karte the.
 kuch itihaaskaar inhen chauthi or paanchavi sadi main nirmit maanate hai, adhiktar 7 veen sadi main. 
 ajanta or ellora gufaaon kii tarj par hi baagh gufaaen bani hui hai. in gufaaon main bani pracheen chitrakaari manushya ko aashcharya main daal detii he. in gufaaon kii khoj 1818 main kii gai thi. mana jaataa he ki dasvin shatabdi main buddha dharm ke patan ke baad in gufaaon ko manushya ne bhula diya tha or yahaan baagh nivas karne lage. isiilie inhen baagh gufaaon ke naam se janaa jaataa he. baagh gupha ke kaaran hi yahaan based gaanv ko baagh gaanv or yahaan se bahane vaali nadi ko baagh nadi ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
