 nilgiri parvatiy rail, bharat ke rajya tamilnadu main sthit ek rail pranali he, jise 1908 main british raaj ke dauraan banaaya gaya tha. shuruuaat main iskaa sanchaalan madras railway dwaara kiya jaataa tha. is railway kaa parichaalan aaj bhi bhaap injanon dwaara kiya jaataa he. nilgiri parvatiy rail, navgathit salem mandal ke adhikaar kshetra main aata he. july 2005 main unesco ne nilgiri parvatiy rail ko darjeeling himalayan rail ke vishwa dharohar sthal ke ek vistaar ke rup main manyata di thi or tab se inhen sanyukt rup se "bharat kii parvatiy rail" ke naam se janaa jaataa he. ise yah manyata milane ke baad isaki aadhunikeekaran kii yojana kaa parityag kar diya gaya. pichley kai varshon se kunoor or udgamandalam ke bich ke khand par bhaap ke injanon ke sthaan par liye dijal injanon kaa prayog kiya ja rahaa he. sthaneey logon or paryatakon ne is khand par ek baar phir se bhaap injanon dwaara railgadee chalaane kii maang kii he.
 shahrukh khan dwaara abhinit hindi film "dil se" ke prasiddh hindi geet chainya chainya kaa filmakan isi railway kii railgadee kii chat par kiya gaya he.
