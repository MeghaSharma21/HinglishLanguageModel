 riyaad praant, jise aupchaarik arabi main mintaqaah​ aar-riyaad kehete hai, saudi arab ke madhya najd kshetra main sthit praant he. yah jansankhya ke aadhaar par doosraa sabase bada praant he or kshetrafal ke hisaab se bhi doosraa sabase bada praant he. saudi arab kii rashtriya rajdhani riyaad shahar bhi isi praant main sthit he or 75% prantiy aabaadi isi shahar main rahati he. 2004 kii janaganana ke anusaar praant kii 31% aabaadi gair-saudi videshi the or inamen se adhiktar riyaad shahar main hi based hue the.
 'riyaad' ko arabi lipi main 'رياض‎' likhaa jaataa he jiskaa antim akshar 'ض‎' bhartiya upmahadweep, afghanistan or iran main 'za' kaa uccharan rakhata he, jabaki arab deshon main iskaa uccharan 'da' yaa 'dha' se miltaa-julata kiya jaataa he. isliye is nagar or praant ke naam ko - 'riyaaz', 'riyaad' or 'riyadh' - teenon uccharanon ke saath paaya jaataa he. thik yahi uccharan kaa antar 'ramadan' shabd main bhi dekha jaataa he jo 'ramadan' or 'ramaadaan' donon roopon main miltaa he.
 riyaad praant najd ke pathaar par sthit he or yahi saudi rajpariwaar kii grihabhumi thi. jab 20veen sadi kii shuruyaat main saudi parivaar ne arabi prayadweep ke bhinn bhaagon ko apane niyantran main lenaa shuru kiya to unhone riyaad se hi apana vistaar armbh kiya tha.
