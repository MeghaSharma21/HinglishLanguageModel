 paakhi hindi kii ek masik patrika he. hindi sahitya kii patrikaon kii bheed main alag pahchaan banane vaali 'paakhi' kaa prakaashan sitambar, 2008 se niyamit jari he. praveshank kaa locarpan hindi ke varisht aalochak namvar singh ne kiya. ab tak paakhi patrika gyanaranjan, rajendra yadav, namvar singh or sanjeev par visheshank nikaal chuki he.
