 aagaa khan pales pune ke yerawada me sthit ek aitihaasik bhawan he. sultan mohammad shah aagaa khan dwitiya ne 1892 me banwaaya tha. is bhawan me mahatma gandhi ko unke anya sahyogio se saath san 1940 me bandi bana kar rakhaa gaya tha. kasturba gandhi kaa nidhan isi bhawan me hua tha. unki samadhi bhi yaho sthit he. ab yah bhawan ek sangrahalay he. 
 yah smarak 6.5 hectare main failaa hua he. 1892 main shah aagaa khan tritiy ne ise banwaaya tha. 1956 tak yah bhawan unka mahal rahaa. 1969 main, aagaa khan chaturth ne ise bharat sarkaar ko daan de diya tha. mahatma gandhi kii patni kasturba gandhi or mahatma ji ke sachiv mahadevabhai desai 2 saal tak yahaan rahe or isi pravaas ke dauraan unki mrityu hui thi. unki asthiyaan smarak ke bagiche main rakhi gai hai. gandhi ji ke jivan par ek photo-pradarshnee or unki vyaktigat upyog kii vasteen jaise unki chappalein or chashme yahaan rakhe gaye hai.
