 shinnan chattaane purvi antarctic ke rani maud dharti kshetra main tat ke paas shinnan himani se pashchim main sthit aisa ek kshetra he jahaan kai badi shilaayein barf se baahar dikhti hai. ise san 1957-1962 kaal main chalane vaale japani antarctic shodh yatra ne vimaan se dekha tha or "shinnan eva" kaa naam diya.
