 
 protostomia praaniyon kaa ek jeevvaigyanik adhisangh he. yah apni bahan claide, duterostomia, ke saath apane se unchaa nefrosoa naamak claide banaata he. duterostomia or protostomia ke sadsy praaniyon main yah antar he ki duterostomia ke bhrun ke vikaas main sabase pehle jo chhidr khulata he vah guda banataa he jabaki adhiktar protostomia bhrun main sabase pehle viksit hone vaala chhidr uskaa munh hota he. nefrosoa swayan bilateria claide kaa sadsy he.
