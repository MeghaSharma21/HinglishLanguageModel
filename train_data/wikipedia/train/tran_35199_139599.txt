 padmastri variyar america ke siio hai, ek electric vaahan company enaio ke me, or fortune patrika dwaara "electric car bij kii rani" kaha gaya he. vah cisco systams ke purv chif technology and stretage officer or motorola, ink ke purv sitio hai. vah forbs dwaara 2014 me duniya kii 71veen sabase shaktishaalee mahila ke rup main soochibaddh he. 
 yelipeddy padmastri kaa janm or bharat ke dakshini rajya andhra pradesh main vijaywada shahar main hua tha. unhone vijaywada main bal montesri skool or maris stela college se padi puri kii he. 1982 main indian institute of technology, dilli se rasinic ejineering main snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii. cornell vishvavidhyalay se rasinic ejineering main master kii degree bhi praapt kii he.
 yelipeddy padmastri ne 1984 main motorola company me kaam suru kiya. 23 saal tak vah uparashtrapati or motorola ke energy system group ke mahaprabandhak, or iske semiconductor products sector main corporate upaadhyaksh or chif technology adhikari rahi.
 fortune magazine ne apni sabase shaktishaalee mahilaon kii suchi main unhen chaar badhate sitaron me shaamil kiya he, unhen 10 "ucchtam bhugtan" or "yuva or shaktishaalee" shreniyon ke bich rakhaa tha 2005 me. iconomic times ne 11 veen sabase prabhaavshaalee vaishvik bhartiya ke rup main warrior ko sthaan diya. 2001 main vah kaary mahila mahila patrika se "mahilaon ko unnat vigyaan or praudyogiki" puraskaar praapt karne ke liye rashtravyapee chah mahilaon main se ek thi.
