 pramila nesargi, ek bhartiya vakil, shikshavid, ek prasiddh mahila kaaryakrtta, or kai companiyon ke nideshak hai. unhone bahut hi kam umr main kanoon kii degree praapt karke kanoon kaa abhyaas karnaa shuru kar diya tha. vah angreji-kannada anuwaad ke liye shabdkosh samiti kii sadsy rahi he. vah pichley 50 varshon main baar association ke adhyaksh ke rup main karnataka baar council main nirvaachit hone vaali pehali mahila he. unhone ucch profil se lekar vivaadaaspad maamalon tak ek vyapak cese kaa pratinidhitiv kiya he jiske liye unhen aksar gambhir aalochana kaa saamana karnaa padaa he. 
 pramila nesargi kaa janm 25 march, saal 1938 main hua tha. unka janm masore, karnataka main hua tha. unki mata ek swatantraaa senaanee thi va pita ek swanirmit vyakti the. pramila apane parivaar kii pehali sadsy he jon padhne ke liye college gayi. apni biessi kii degree 1958 main unhone puri kii. 1960 main elelbi kii degree praapt kii. vah em.el. yogyata praapt karne vaali pehali mahila thim. 
 pramila nesargi ne snaatak kaarykram ke dauraan apane praadhyaapak ke khilaaf ek rit daayar karte hue apana karier shuru kiya, jo keval ek under graduate tha or snaatak kakshaen yojit kar rahe the. vakil vyavsaay kuch dashakon pehle purush pesha tha; vah is pravriti ko badalane ko chunauti ke rup main uthi or ek prasiddh vakil banane main safal rahi. unhone vivaadaaspad maamalon main kai mantriyon ke khilaaf cese lade tha or kai durbhagyapurna maamalon main nyaay laane ke liye jimmedaari uthaai.
