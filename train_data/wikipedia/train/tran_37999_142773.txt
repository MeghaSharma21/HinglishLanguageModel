 khanij aise bhautik padaarth hai jo khan se khod kar nikaale jate hai. kuch upyogi khanij padaarthon ke naam hai - loha, abhrak, koyala, boxite, namak, jasta, chuunaa pathar ityadi.
 multah khanij shabd kaa arth he- khani + ja. arthath khan se utpann . iskaa angrezi shabd mineral bhi mine se sambandh rakhata he.
 khanij hone ke liye us padaarth ko kathor va crystalia hona aavashyak he. kuch paribhaashaon ke anusaar khanij vah padaarth he jo crystalia ho or bhugolic paristhitiyon ke parinamswaroop bana ho.
 wese to khanijon kaa vargeekaran bahut kathin he phir bhi inako kuch visheshtaon ke aadhaar par vargeekrut kiya ja saktaa he-
 khanijaj or chattaanon main bahut antar he. chattaane khanijon se bani hoti hai, khanij prakritik rup se paye jate hai.
