 shaareerik sandarbh main, ek teen aiami sadish kshetra ke vichlan kis had tak vector kshetra pravaah ek bhi bindu par ek strot kii tarah bartaav karti he. kis had tak vahaan adhik yah pravesh karne se antariksh ke ek atyalp kshetra se baahar nikal rahaa he - yah apane 'outgoingness "kii ek sthaneey upaay he. agar vichlan kuch bindu par ashoonya he to vahaan ek strot ho saktaa he yaa us sthiti main sinc chahie. jab ham shabdon kaa prayog pravaah, strot or itane par.)
