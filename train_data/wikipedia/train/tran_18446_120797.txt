 deshiy code ucchtam domen yaa sisiticaldi kisi desh, raashtra yaa adheen kshetra ke liye prayog hone vaali yaa arakshit internet ucchtam domen ko kehete hai. sabhi aski par aadhaarit sisiticaldi do aksharon ke hote hai, masalan nepal kaa sisiticaldi '.np' or srilanka kaa sisiticaldi '.lk' he.
