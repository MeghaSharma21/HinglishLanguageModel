 hinduon ke vaishnav sampradaay main gautam buddha ko bhagwaan vishnu kaa avatar mana jaataa he. hindu dharm main varnit dashavathar main vartmaan main 9vaa avatar buddha ko mana jaataa he. bhavishya main antim kalki avatar hoga.
 buddha kaa ullekh sabhi pramukh puraanon tathaa sabhi mahatvapurn hindu granthon main hua he. kintu sabhi ek hi 'buddha' ke bare main hon, aisa sambhavtah nahin he. kintu in granthon main se adhikansh spashtatah buddha mat ke pravartak 'buddha' kii hi baat karte hai. in granthon main mukhyatah buddha kii do bhoomikaaon kaa varnan he- dharm kii punah sthapana ke liye naastik mat kaa prachaar tathaa pashu-bali kii ninda. neeche un kuch puraanon main buddha ke ullekh kaa sandarbh diya gaya he-
