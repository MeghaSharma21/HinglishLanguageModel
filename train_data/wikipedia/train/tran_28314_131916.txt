 ashuri yaa asyrian nav-aramai pashchimottaree iran, uttari iraq, purvottaree syria or dakshinpurvi turki main basane vaale ashuri samudaay ke sadasyon dwaara boli jane vaali bhashaa he. yah pracheen aramai bhashaa kii ek aadhunik vanshaj he. ashuri log ab duniya bhar main fail chuke hai isliye ashuri bhashaa bolne vaale ab vishvabhar main milte hai. ise likhane ke liye syrian lipi kaa ek rup prayog kiya jaataa he.
