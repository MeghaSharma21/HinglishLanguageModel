 soochna suraksha ke sandarbh main saamaajik enginiari vah kala he jisaki sahaayta se koi duusare vyaktiyon ko apni ichha ke anuroop kaary karne ke liye yaa kuch gopneey suchnaayen ugalane ke liye taiyaar kar detii he. yah ek prakaar kii vishwaas yukti yaa dusron ko vishwaas main lekar dhokhaa dena he. 
 yah suvidit he ki kisi sanstha ke sadasyon kaa vyavhaar us sanstha ke soochna-suraksha ko pusht yaa lachar bana saktaa he. yadi koi 'ghar kaa bhedi' agyantavash duusare ke bahekaave main aa jaay to sanstha kii soochna-suraksha ko bahut haani pahunch sakati he.
