 muuli vaa mulak bhumi ke andar paidaa hone vaali sabzi he. vastutah yah ek roopantirat pradhan jad he jo bich main moti or donon siron kii ore kramshah patali hoti he. muuli puure vishwa main ugaayi evam khaayi jaati he. muuli kii anek prajatiyaan hai jo aakaar, rang evam paidaa hone main lagane vaale samay ke aadhaar par bhinn-bhinn hoti he. kuch prajatiyaan tel utpaadan ke liye bhi ugaayi jaati he.
 muuli kachi khaayen yaa is ke patton kii sabji banaakar khaaen, har prakaar se bavasir main laabhadaayak he. gurde kii kharabi se yadi peshaab kaa bananaa band ho jaae to muuli kaa ras do ounce prati maatra pine se vah phir banane lagata he. muuli khaane se madhumeh main laabh hota he. ek kachi muuli nity praatah uthte hi khate rahane se kuch dinon main pilia rog thik ho jaataa he. garmi ke prabhaav se khatti dakaaren aati ho to ek cap muuli ke ras main mishri milakar pine se laabh hota he. masik dharm kii kami ke kaaran ladakiyon ke yadi muhaanse nikalate hon to praatah patton sahit ek
