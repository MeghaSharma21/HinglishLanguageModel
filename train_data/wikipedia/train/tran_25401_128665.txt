 plastination nikaayon yaa sharir ke angon kon sangrakshit karne ke liye istemaal kii jane vaali ek takanic he. yah takanic 1977 main gunther von hagens ne di thi. pani or vasa kuch plastic padaarthon se badal die jate hai. phir aise namoonay taiyaar kiye jate hai jinaki na to koi gand hoti he naa hi kshay or dekhne main mul namoonon kii tarah hi dikhte hai. or naa hi ye apaghatit hote hai.
 plastination ki prakriya ke kool chaar step hai: 
 jal or lipid ke utak polimar se badal die jate hai. susadhy polimar jo is prakriya ke liye istemaal kiye jate hai wo nimnlikhit hai: silicon, ipoxi, poliistor kopolimar. 
 plastination kaa sabase pehla charan hota he, nirdhaarit karnaa. nirdhaaran main formaldehoid ki aavashyaktaa hoti he. yah rasayan utakon kon apaghatit hone se roktaa he. 
 jaruri vichchhedan ke pashchaat sample ko asitone ke bath main rakhaa jaataa he. thand kii sthiti ke tahat, asitone sab pani ko baahar kii or khinchataa he or koshikaon ke andar pani ki jagah khud kon prasthaapit kar letaa he. 
 tisare charan main sample kon draviny polimar ke bath main rakhaa jaataa he. phir vahan nirvaat kii stithi banaai jaati he. asitone ko kam taapmaana par ubala jaataa he. jaise jaise asitone vashpikrut hota jaataa he, vaise hi draviny polimar sample main isaki jagah le letaa he. 
 phir ise sakht banane ke liye garm kiya jaataa he or ultra vilet kirnon ke sanmukh rakhaa jaataa he. 
 ek sample kuch bhi ho saktaa he. ek puure bade insaani sharir se lekar chote kisi bhi jaanavar ke sharir ke ang kon plastinet kiya ja saktaa he.
