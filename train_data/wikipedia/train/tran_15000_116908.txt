 drav avasthaa main sthit hilium ko drav hilium kehete hai. maanak daab par bahut kam taap tak le jane par hi hilium drav avasthaa main aa paati he. drav hilium kaa upyog atichaalakata praapt karne ke liye kiya jaataa he, jaise atichaalak chumbakon ke liye.drav hilium ek vicitra vilakshan vaala ek asaamaanya padaarth hota he iske vicitra lakshan nimna he 1.   drav helium ek hi rang hin paardarshee or ucch suvashpay hota he.2.   drav helium kaa na to koi trik bindu hota he or naa hi saamaanya taap par iskaa koi galnaank bindu hota he.3.   iske kranthik taap, kranthik daab, boil taap, vyutkraman or kvathanank kramash: 5.25K, 2.26atm, 17K, 34K va 4.25K hote he.
