 nagkesar yaa nagachampa ek sidhaa sadabahaar vriksh he jo dekhne main bahut sundar hota he. yah dwidal angur se utpann hota he. pattiyaan isaki bahut patali or ghani hoti hai, jisase iske neeche bahut acchhee chaaya rahati he. isamen chaar dalon ke bade or safed phool garamiyon main lagate hai jinamein bahut acchhee mahak hoti he. lakdi isaki itani kadi or majboot hoti he ki kaatanevaale kii kulhadiyon kii dhaaren mud mud jaati he; isi se ise 'vajrakath' bhi kahat hai. falon main do yaa teen beej nikalate hai. himalay ke poorabi bhaag, poorabi bengal, assam, barma, dakshin bharat, sihal aadi main iske ped bahutayat se milte hai.
 nagkesar ke sookhe phool aushadh, masaale or rang banane ke kaam main aate hai. inke rang se praayah resham rangaa jaataa he. shri lanka main bijon se gaadha, pilaa tel nikaalte hai, jo diya jalaane or dawa ke kaam main aata he. tamilnadu main is tel ko vatarog main bhi malate hai. isaki lakdi se anek prakaar ke saamaan banate hai. lakdi aisi acchhee hoti he ki keval haath se rangne se hi usamen varanish]] kii si chamak aa jaati he. baidyak main nagkesar caseli, garam, rukhi, halaki tathaa fever, khujali, durgandh, konne, vish, pyaas, matlee or paseene ko dur karanevaali maani jaati he. khooni bavasir main bhi vaidya log ise dete hai. ise 'nagachampa' bhi kehete hai.
