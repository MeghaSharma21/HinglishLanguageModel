 haalaa yaa drakshira yaa wine angoor ke ras ko kinvit karne se banane vaala ek maadak peya he. isamen angooron kaa kinvan bina kisi sharkara, amla, prakinv, jal yaa anya kisi poshak tatv ko daale hota he. khamir angoor ras main upasthit sharkara ko kinvit kar ke ithenul va carbon dioxide main parivartit kar dete hai. angoor or khamir kii alag-alag naslon se alag-alag swaad, gandh va rangon vaali haalaa banati he. haalaa par angooron ke ugaae jane ke sthaan, varsha, suraj va angooron ko todane ke samay kaa bhi asar padataa he.
 angooron ke alaava anya falon kii bhi haalaa banaai jaati he haalanki uski maatra va lokapriyata angooron kii haalaa kii tulana main bahut kam he. shahatut, anaar, seb, naashpati, aalu bukhaaraa, aalubaaloo or anya falon kii haalaa banati he. haalaa banane kii pratha ati-pracheen he or cocks kshetra main georgia main 8,000 varsh puraane haalaa kii suraahiyaan mili hai. 
 bharat or europe main angoor se maadak peya banane se pehle madhu ko kinvit kar ke us se sharaab banaai jaati thi. iske kaaran us sharaab kaa naam bhi yahi pad gaya or madhu kaa arth "shahad" va "sharaab" donon ho gaya. haalaa angoor se banati he or yah madhya-purv se baaqi vishwa main baad main faili.
 angoor jamuni se lekar hare kai rangon main aate hai lekin lagbhag sabhi ke ras kaa rang haraa-shwet hi hota he. laal haalaa banane ke liye usamen laal angooron kii khaal chhod di jaati he jis se use rang dene vaale anthocyanin rasayan bhi riskar haalaa ko rang dete hai. is se vipareet shwet haalaa ke liye keval ras ko hi kinvit kara jaataa he. gulabi haalaa main laal angooron kii khaal kii kuch maatra daali jaati he lekin itani nahin ki haalaa kaa rang puraa laal hry jaae.
 san 2007 main france, italy or spain haalaa ke sabase bade utpaadak the. pramukh utpaadak deshon main banaai jane vaali haalaa kii maatra nimna talika main he.
