 kumaar vikal punjabi mul ke hindi bhashaa ke ek jane mane kavi the. wo vampanthi vichaardhaara se prabhavit the or 1970 ke dashak main kaaphi makabool rahen the. unki kavita main dabe kuchle logon kii samasyaon kaa vivaran miltaa he. unki kavita main prastut maanavaadee saamagree ne 1980-1990 main punjab main hindu-sikh tanaav kam karke sadbhaavna kaayam karne main bhi yogadan diya. kumaar vikal kii tulana hindi sahitya kii jani maani hasati krishna sobati se karte he jinke sahitya main punjab ke janajivan kaa chithran hota he.
