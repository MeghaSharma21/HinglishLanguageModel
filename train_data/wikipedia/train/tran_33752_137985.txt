 bhagavatsharan upaadhyaay kaa janm 1910 i0 ko ujiyarpur, jila- baliya main hua. nidhan 12 agast 1982 i0 ko hua.upaadhyaay ji ne sanskrit, hindi sahitya, itihaas, samskruti evam puraatatva kaa gahan adhyayan kiya. aapaki bhashaa style tatsam shabdon se yukt saahitiyik khadiboli he. aapane vivechanaatmak bhavukta purna, chitratmak bhashaa kaa prayog tathaa kahin-kahin rekhachitr style kaa prayog kiya he.
 vishwa sahitya kii ruparekha, kalidas kaa bharat, kadambari, thuntha aam, laal china, ganga-godavari, buddha vaibhav, sahitya or kala, sagar kii laharon par. 
 banaras hindu vishvavidhyalay kii shodh patrika kaa sampadan. hindi vishwakosh sampadak-mandal ke sadsy. vishesh-marishas main bhaarte ke rajdoot tathaa vikram vishvavidhyalay main itihaas ke professor rahe.
