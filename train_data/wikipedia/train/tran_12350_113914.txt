 antarctic vrutt ke dakshin main koi sthaayee maanav aabaadi nahin he, lekin mausami aadhaar par vibhinn deshon ke vaigyanikon kii team antarctic main kai anusandhan steshnon main karyrat he. pichhali shatabdiyon main mahadweep par kuch arddh sthaayee wheling stesan sthapit kiye gaye the or kuch whalers ek saal yaa usase adhik samay ke liye vahaan rahe. yadhyapi antarctic vrutt ke uttar steshnon kam se kam teen bacchon ke janm kii pushti hui he.
