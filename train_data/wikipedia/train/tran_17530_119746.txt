 tuvaalu prashanth mahaasaagar main hawaii or austrailia ke bich sthit ek polinesian dweepiya desh he. iske nikatavarti desh kiribati, samoa or fiji hai. yah desh chaar dweep or or paanch etaal se milakar bana he. 12,373 logon kii jansankhya ke saath yah duniya kaa teesraa kam jansankhya vaala samprabhu desh he, isse kam aabaadi vaale deshon main keval vatican or naaru hi hai. kshetrafal ke lihaaj se mahaj 26 varg kimi ke daayre ke saath yah duniya kaa chauthaa sabase chota desh he, keval vatican city, monako or naaru isse chote hai. yah dweepiya desh 19veen shatabdi ke ant main united kingdom ke prabhaav kshetra main aayaa. 1892 se lekar 1916 tak yah britain kaa sangrakshit kshetra or 1916 se 1974 ke bich yah gilbert or ilaais island colony kaa hissaa tha. 1974 main sthaneey rahavaasiyon ne alag british nirbhar kshetra ke rup main rahane ke paksh main matdaan kiya. 1978 main tuvaalu rashtrakul kaa purna swatantra desh ke rup main hissaa ban gaya.
