 vikramaditya pratimaa madhya pradesh ke ujjain nagar main mahakaal mandir ke peeche 'vikram tila' par sthit 30 foot unchi vikramaditya kii pratimaa he. pital se nirmit is murti ke nirmaan main 1 karod rupe kharch hue hai. ise ujjain nagar nigam or 'sinhasth taiyaari samiti' ne indore ke murtikar mahendra kodwani se nirmit karaayaa he.
