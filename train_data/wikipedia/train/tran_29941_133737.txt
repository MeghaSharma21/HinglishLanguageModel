 vishnu puraana ke anusaar yah prithvi saat dwipon main banty hui he. ye saaton dweep chaaron ore se saat samudron se ghire hai. ye sabhi dweep ek ke baad ek duusare ko ghere hue bane hai or inhen ghere hue saaton samudr hai. mithe jal kaa sagar pushkaradweep ko ghere hue he. pushkar dweep ko ghere mithe jal ke sagar ke paar usase dooni suvarnmayi bhoomi dilkhaayi detii he. vahaan das sahasr yojan vaale lok-alok parvat hai. yah parvat oonchai main bhi utane hi sahasr yojan he. uske aage prithvi ko chaaron ore se ghere hue ghor andhkaar chaaya hua he. yah andhkaar chaaron ore se brahmand kataah se aavrutt he. and-kataah sahit sabhi dwipon ko milakar samast bhu-mandal kaa pariman pachaas karod yojan he.
