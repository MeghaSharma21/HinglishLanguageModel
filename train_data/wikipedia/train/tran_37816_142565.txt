 tay / tay) france main jansamoohon par laganevaala ek bhoomi-kar tha. shuru main yah bade bade saamanton dwaara apane adheen krishi-mazdooron par lagaaya jaataa tha. baad main yah france ke raja dwaara lagaaya jane laga. kuch kshetron main yah ek tarah ke sampattikar ke rup main lagaaya jaataa tha. saamaanya rup se samant yaa amir umara tathaa baad main padri log bhi is kar se mukt rahete the, jo us asantosh kaa kaaran bana jisane france kii rajyakranti utpann karne main sahaayta pahunchaai.
