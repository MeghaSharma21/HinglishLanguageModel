 patil  ek bhartiya upnaam he. is naam kaa udgam paati yaa paathi shabd se mana jaataa he, jiskaa arth he shikshit vyakti.
 puraane jamaane main kheti hi bharatiyon kaa mukhya vyavsaay tha, or kisaanon kaa kisi bhi aapadaa se sangrakshan karnaa tathaa unhe sinchai ke saadhan uplbdh karana us kshetra ke raja kaa kartavya hua karata tha. is kaam main unki sahaayta gaanv kaa mukhiya karata tha, jo paathi kehalaata tha. uskaa mukhya kaam gaanv ke rajasav kaa sanchayan karnaa, or sanchit raashi, jo fasal yaa dhan ke swaroop main hoti thi, uskaa lekha-jokha rakhana or use kshetra ke raja ke bhandar me jamaa karnaa hota tha. sookhaa padane par yah bhandar praja ke liye kholaa jaataa tha, or vitarit kiya jaataa tha.
 gaanv me niyam kanoon banaaye rakhane kaa daayitv bhi gaanv ke patil ko hi nibhaana hota tha.
