 bhartiya shastreey sangeet main jab koi swar apni shuddhavastha se upar hota he tab use teevr vikrut swar kehete hai. aisa swar madhyam he. jise m ke laghu rup main bhi janaa jaataa he. anek raagon main shuddh ke saath athwa keval teevr madhyam kaa prayog hota he. udhaaharan ke liye raag hindol main keval teevr madhyam kaa prayog hota he. jiskaa arth he hindol raag main madhyam swar apane nishchit sthaan se upar kii ore prayog kiya jaataa he. raag yaman kalyaan main shuddh or teevr donon madhyam prayog kiye jate hai.
