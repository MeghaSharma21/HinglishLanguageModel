 valmiki dalit samudaay va jati he. inkaa parmparik kaam mel-mutr ko saaf karnaa or anya safai kaary karnaa hota he. inhen bhartiya jati vyavastha main sabase nicha sthaan diya jaataa he or is kaaran ye bahut shoshit varg he. punjab main based chuhadaa ko bhi inkaa bhaag mana jaataa he jo sikh dharm ke majahabi sikh anubhag ko banaate hai. valmiki naam valmiki se liya gaya he jinhen ye samudaay apana guru maanata he . inkaa mul naam bhangi he jiskaa arth bhaang pine vaala hota he. lekin ye ab apmanjanak mana jaataa he or iskaa upyog saamaajik rup se sahi nahin mana jaataa he. kuch valmiki apane ko harijan kahlaana bhi pasand karte hai.
 valmiki aaj bhi saaf safai kaa kaary hi karte hai or ye unka rojgaar kaa mukhya hissaa he. unke kai saganthan bhi maujuud he jo sarkari naukari main is kaary main kisi or jati ke logon ke aagaman kaa virodh karte hai.
