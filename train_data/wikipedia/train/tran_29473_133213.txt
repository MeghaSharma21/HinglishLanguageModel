 rashtriya padap jaevpraudyogiki anusandhan centre kii sthapana bhartiya krishi ke liye ati aavashyak jaev praudyogiki ke laabh pradaan karne ke liye san 1985 main kii gai. majbootee va aatm nirbharta ke liye shuruuaati dinon main kendra ne uttak sanrvadhan se sambandhit anusandhan kaary kiya. ab yah kendra plant biotechnology ke agragami kshetron main asar dene vaale anusandhan de rahaa he. jin chloning or sasy vigyaan main upyogi promotor, jaivik or ajaivik stas pratirodhi tansjainik fasalon kaa vikaas, banaavat or kaary sambandhi jinomix, aanvik jeev vigyaan anuvanshik vibhinnataaon kaa prayog, hatrosis kaa prayog genetic ejineering ke dwaara gunvattta sudhaar or jaivik nitrogen sthirikaran, mukhya anusandhan kaary hai jo is kendra main chalaae ja rahe hai. bha.kri.a.pa. pranali main padap jaev praudyagiki anusandhan ko protsaahit karne or use majboot banane ke liye paraspar or antara-santhaangat sampark viksit karne ke liye yah kendra agrani bhoomika nibhaa rahaa he or isane maanav sansaadhan kii unnati main mukhya bhoomika nibhaai he.
