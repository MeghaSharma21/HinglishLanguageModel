 gufa dharti main aise bhumigat sthal ko kehete hai jo itnaa bada ho ki koi vyakti usamen pravesh kar sake. agar aisa koi sthaan itnaa chota ho ki usamen keval ek chota jaanavar hi pravesh kar paae to use aam taur se hindi main gupha kii bajaae 'bill' kaha jaataa he. yah sambhav he ki koi gupha samudr ke pani ke andar bhi ho - aisi gufaaon ko samudri gupha kaha jaataa he.
