 datia rajya ke barauni kshetra ke jameendaar prithvisingh, 'rasanidhi' naam se kavyrachana karte the. inkaa rachanakaal sanwat 1660 se 1717 tak mana jaataa he. inkaa sarvshereshth granth 'ratanhjaraa' he jo bihari satsai ko adarsh maanakar likhaa gaya prathit hota he. bihari kii dohapaddati kaa anukaran karte samay rasanidhi kahin-kahin jyon kaa tyon bhav hi apane dohe main likh gaye hai. ratanhjaraa ke atirikt vishnupadkirthan, baramasi, rasanidhisagar, geetisangrah, arill or maanjh, hindola bhi inaki rachanaayen bataai jaati hai. inke dohon kaa ek sangrah chhatarpur ke shri jagannathprasad ne prakashit kiya he.
 rasanidhi premi swabhaav ke rasik kavi the. inhone reetibaddh kavy na likhakar farsi shaayari kii style par prem kii vividh dashaon or cheshtaaon kaa varnan kiya he. farsi ke prabhaav se inhone premadashaon main vyapaktaa praapt kii kintu bhashaa or abhivyanjana kii drushti se inkaa kavy adhik safal nahin ho sakaa. shabdon kaa asantulit prayog tathaa bhaavon kii abhivyakti main shaleenta kaa abhaav khatakanevaala ban gaya he. haan, prem kii saras uktiyon main rasanidhi ko kahin kahin acchhee safalataa mili he. vastut: jahaan inkaa prem swaabhaavik rup se vyakt hua he vahaan inke dohe bade sundar ban pade hai.
