 parati pariktha ek upanyaas he jiske rachaayita fanishwar nath renu hai.mailaa anchal ke baad yah renu kaa doosraa aanchalik upanyaas he. is upanyaas ke saath hi hindi sahitya jagat do bhaagon main vibhakt ho gaya: pehla varg to isko mailaa anchal se bhi behatar upanyaas maanata tha, vahin, doosraa varg ise upanyaas hi nahin maanata tha. sach main agar upanyaas ke paaramparik maanakon se dekha jaye to parati pariktha main kaaphi algaav dikhegaa. yah mailaa anchal jaisaa bhi ekarekheey dhara main nahin chalata, varan yah to desh-kaal kaa atikaraman kar ek hi samay main saare kaal ko prastut or apresent donon karata he. yah jitna dikhaata he usase bhi zyada chhipaata he. kabhi yah pathakon kii pariksha letaa he to kabhi yah use bilkul bhauchak hi kar detaa he.
 jis prakaar mailaa anchal main puraa kaa puraa anchal hi nayak tha usee prakaar yahan bhi naayakatv kisi khaash paatr kii bajaay anchal ko hi man jaayegaa kintu yahan par kuch paatr jise kii jithendra mishra ko naayakatv ke kareeb man ja saktaa he. yaa yun kahe kii aanchalikta kii dhaaran karte hue bhi is kahani main misar parivaar hi kendriya tatva he.
