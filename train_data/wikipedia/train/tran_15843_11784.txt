 bauni andakaar gallexi aisi boune aakaar kii andakaar gallexi ko kehete hai jo anya andakaar gallexiyon kii tulana main kaafii choti ho. inhen habal anukram kii chihnavali main dE kii shrenee di jaati he. bauni andakaar gallexiyaan aam taur par anya gallexiyon kii upgrahiy gallexiyon ke rup main milti hai yaa phir gallexiyon ke jhundon main pai jaati hai.
 angrezi main "bauni andakaar gallexi" ko "dworf alliptical galaksi" kehete hai.
 bahut se khagolashastri maanate hai ke bauni andakaar gallexiyaan gas or andhra padaarth ke gurutvkarshan ke prabhaav se ekatrit hone se dhire-dhire ban jaati hai or yahi bauni gallexiyaan aapas main milakar phir badi gallexiyaan banati hai. agar is vichaar ko mana jaae to brahmand main dikhne vaali bahut si mahaan gallexiyaan aisi bauni gallexiyon ke takaraav or sammilan se bani hai.
 anya khagolashastri is raae se asahmat hai. unka maananaa he ke jab mahaan sarpil gallexiyaan kisi samooh main hoti hai to unamem gurutvkarshan se aapasi kheenchaatani chalati rahati he. ise kabhi-kabhi "gallexic utpeedan" kehete hai or is se gallexiyaan dhire-dhire apana aakaar badal leti hai. phir sarpil gallexiyon kii bhujaayen or anya bhaag in bauni gallexiyon kaa rup dhaaran kar leti hai. is dharna main vishvas rakhane vaali vaigyanik pramaan ke taur par bahut-si bauni andakaar gallexiyon ke andar bhujaa-rupi dhaanchon kii maujuudagi kaa varnan karte hai.
