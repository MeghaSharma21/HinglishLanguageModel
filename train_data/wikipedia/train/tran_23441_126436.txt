 vidyut bhatthiyaan saamanyat: dhaatu khanijon or dhaatuon ko pighalaane ke liye prayukt kii jaati hai.
 vidyut urja se utpann hui ooshmaa vidyutdhara ke varg ke anupaat main hoti he. vidyut bhatthiyaan koyale kii bhatthiyon se adhik ooshmaa utpann kar sakati hai or aakaar main bhi choti hoti hai. ye hanikaarak dhuen athwa gaisen nahin utpann karatin, parantu inkaa mukhya laabh inamen saralata se ooshmaa niyantran karne kaa he. dhara kaa parivartan kar ooshmaa kaa niyantran bahut saralata se kiya jaataa he. inkaa duurastha niyantran or swat: chaalan bhi kiya ja saktaa he. in kaarnon se vidyut bhatthiyaan saamaanya upyog main aa gai hai.
 vidyut bhatthiyon ke bahut se parishkrut rup ab saamaanya ho gaye hai or jyon jyon vidyut shakti sambharan aarthik drushtikon se sasta hota jaataa he, vidyut bhatthiyon kaa prayog nirantar badhata hi jaataa he.
 vidyut bhatthiyon ke teen mukhya praroop hai -
 1. pratirodh bhatthiyaan .
 2. chaap bhatthiyaan 
 3. preran bhatthiyaan tathaa
 4. aadhunik maffil bhatthi 
 bhatthi, oven se is arth main alag he ki oven praayah kam taap tak hi pahunchati he.
