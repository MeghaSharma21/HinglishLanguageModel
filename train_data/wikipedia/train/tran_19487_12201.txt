 gaverilo princip bosnia kaa ek sarb rashtravaadi chaatr tha jisane austria ke rajkumaar archedu france ferdinand or unki patni kii 28 june 1914 ko hatya kar di. antatah yahi ghatana pratham vishwayuddh kaa kaaran bani.
 princip sarbon ke liye 'nayak' kii tarah tha, jo austria-hungary samrajyavad se unhen aazaadee dilaanaa chahta tha. princip kii jail main 1918 main mrityu ho gai. do varsh baad uski asthiyon ko sarayevo laakar dafna diya gaya or haal tak use 'mitti kaa laal' kaha jaataa rahaa.
