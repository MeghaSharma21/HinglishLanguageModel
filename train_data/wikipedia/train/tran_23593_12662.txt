 sardaar vallabhbhai patel stadium, ahmedabad, gujarat rajya, ke navrangpur ilaake main sthit ek khel stadium he. ise sports club of gujarat stadium ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. is stadium ko bharat main khel gaya sabase pehla vanade international cricket match kii mezbaani karne kaa gaurav praapt he. is stadum ne kai antarrashtriya evam gharelu cricket maichon kii mezbaani kii he. 1982 main, montera main issi naam ke nae stadium ke udghaatan ke baad se isse ab kissi bhi anterrashtriya pratiyogita ke liye nahin upyog kiya jaataa he. yah stadium, ranji trophy kii gujarat cricket team kii grihabhumion main se ek he.
 isamen, de-nit maichon ke liye, flud lite jaise suvidhaayen bhi vidyaman hai, or gharelu pratiyogitaayen yahan aaye din yojit hoti rahati hai. ahmedabad nagar nigam is khel bhoomi kii adhishthata he. haalaanki yah mukhyatah cricket stadum he, parantu gujarat sarkaar dwaara yojit anya kai kaarykram yahan yojit kiye jate rahe hai.
