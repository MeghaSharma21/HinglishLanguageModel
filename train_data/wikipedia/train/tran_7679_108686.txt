 katholin rubin ek nasa kii antariksh yatri he jinka janm 14 oktober 1978 ko farmington, california main hua. 6 july, 2016 ko international spes stesan par soyuj antariksh yaan par launch karne ke baad vah antariksh main udne vaali 60 veen mahila bani. vah 30 oktober 2016 ko ek soyuj par dharti par laut ai or us samay vah international spes stesan kii expendishan 48 or expendishan 49 ke chaalak dal kaa sadsy thi. rubin kaa chayan nasa ke antariksh yatri ke rup main july 2009 main 14 sadasyon main se ek ke rup main hua tha. unhone antariksh yatri ummidavaar prashikshan se snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii jahaan unke prashikshan main antarrashtriya antariksh stesan pranali, extroheheicolor gatividhi, robotics, shaareerik prashikshan, tii -38 udaan prashikshan or pani or jangal jivit rahane ke prashikshan shaamil the. unhen ieses expadishan 48/49 ke liye flite ejeeniyar ke rup main chunaa gaya tha. agast 2016 main, rubin antariksh main diene ko anukramit karne vaala pehla vyakti ban gaya. ieses par, usne mouse, i. kolai bacteria, or lambda phej virus ke diene anukramon ko nirdhaarit karne ke liye oxuard nainopor technologies dwaara banaae gaye minion naamak ek haath se yojit, yuasabi sanchalit diene sikvencer kaa istemaal kiya. yah biomolacul sequancer prayog kaa ek hissaa tha, jiskaa lakshya "yah sabit karnaa tha ki antariksh main diene anukraman sambhav he, jo sookshmjivon kii pahchaan ko sakasham karne kii kshamta rakhata he, antariksh yaan ke jawaab main rogaanuon or manushyon main parivartan kii nigaraanee karata he, or sambhawatah brahmad main kahin or diene aadhaarit jivan kaa pataa lagane main sahaayta. vah 30 aktubar, 2016 ko prithvi par laut aane par unhone international spes stesan kii expendishan 48 or expadishan 49 ke bhaag ke rup main seva kii. jaivik shodh ke alaava, unhone eva par 12 ghante or 46 minute bitaye. in mishnon ke tahat unhone antariksh main 115 din or antariksh main 12 ghante or 46 minute bitaye.
