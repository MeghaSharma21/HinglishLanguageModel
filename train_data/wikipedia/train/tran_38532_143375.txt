 dharmaputra 1961 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he.
 ye film acharya chaturasen dwaara likhe upanyaas par 1961 main yash chopra dwaara nirdeshit kii gayi thi. yah film british raaj ke dauraan bharat ke vibhaajan se paidaa hue sampradayik ghatnao par aadhaarit he. is film main shashi kapur ne vayask hokar pehali baar kaam kiya tha. jisame unhone ek hindu kattartavaadi kii bhoomika nibhaai thi. film nawab badaruddin, gulshan raay or husn bano kii bhoomikaao ke saath abhinit he.
