 harit samhita, chikitsapradhan ayurveda granth he. isaki safal chikitsa vidhi vaidya evam rugn ke liye upyukt he. iske rachayita maharishi harit hai, jo aatreya punarvasu ke shishy the. aatreya punarvasu ke chah shishy the or sabhi shishyon ne apane-apane naam se apane-apane tantron kii rachana kii. acharya punarvasu ne apane sabhi shishyon dwaara rachit pustakon kii shankaaon kaa samaadhaan purnroopen kiya he, taduparant unka anumodan kiya. harit samhita aaj bhi uplabdh he, kintu yah vahi he yaa nahin, yah nishchit rup se kaha nahin ja saktaa.
 haritsanhita main chikitsa kii sabhi vidhaon kaa varnan he. is pustak main ayurvedic vanaspatiyon dwaara chikitsa kii samyak vyavastha rvint he, jo ati upyogi evam mahatvapurn he. is granth main desh, kaal, vay kaa bhi varnan he tathaa chikitsakeey jadi-bootiyon se paripurna evam ekal aushadhi chikitsa ke kshetra main samrudh he.
