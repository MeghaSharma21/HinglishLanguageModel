 kashmira ram joglekar  pune se ek bhartiya badminton khilaadi he. vah gambhirthapurvak bahere he.
 vah 2000 or 2001 main baharon ke liye bhartiya rashtriya team khilaadi thi. 2001 main unhone dephilampix main rajeev bagga ke saath mishrit dabl championships main ek swarna padak jeeta. unhone 2000 main taiwan main baharon ke liye asian khelon ke yugal ke singles or ek rajat main kaansya hasil kiya.
 kashmira ne badminton ke apane peshewar kariyar ke dauraan hyderabad, andhra pradesh rajya, maharashtra rajya kii sthaneey tiimon ke liye khelin. hyderabad main unhen shri es. em. aarif ke tahat prashikshan diya gaya tha. 1998 main, vah pune chali gayi or shri devardhar ke tahat prashikshan shuru kiya. 2001 main takhne kii chot ke baad unhone peshewar badminton chhod diya.
 kashmira ne pune main renuka swaroop hai skool main padhai kii. unhen praathmik or madhyamik shiksha ke dauraan 6 varshon ke liye adhyayansheel baharaa chaatr ke liye alexander graham bel chaatravrutti se sammanit kiya gaya. unhone 2001 main esayssi pariksha main maharashtra bhar main viklang virgon ke bich pratham rank hasil kiya. 2003 main echessi pariksha ke dauraan bhi unhone puure maharashtra rajya main pratham sthaan hasil kiya. kashmira ne government college of ejineering pune main computer ejineering ke liye daakhila liya. unhone norge jane se pehle lagbhag 3 varshon ke liye echpi main or ek saal ke liye kaale kansaltency main kaam kiya.
