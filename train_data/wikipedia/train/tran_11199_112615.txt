 dhaagaa yaa soot se kapadaa banane yaa oon se sweater aadi banane ko niting kehete he. niting main ek ke baad ek fande lagaakar kii jaati he. puraani pankti ke fandon se naye fande nikaalkar nayi pankti taiyaar kii jaati he. sakriy fandon ko ek salaai ke sahare bigadane se roke rakhaa jaataa he or jab inase hokar naye fande laga diye jate hai to salaai nikaal lee jaati he. isi prakriya se antatah kapadaa ban jaataa he. niting haath se kii jaati he or mashin se bhi kii jaati he. haath se niting ke anekanek sheliyaan evam vidhiyaan hai.
