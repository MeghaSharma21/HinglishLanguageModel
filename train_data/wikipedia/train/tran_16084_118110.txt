 ajmer rajasthan praant main ajmer jile kaa mukhyalay evam mukhya nagar he. yah aravali parvat shrenee kii taragarh pahari kii dhaal par sthit he. yah nagar saatvin shatabdi main ajayraj singh naamak ek chouhan raja dwaara basaaya gaya tha. is nagar kaa mul naam 'ajayameru' tha. san 1365 main mewar ke shaasha, 1556 main akbar or 1770 se 1880 tak mewar tathaa marwar ke anek shaskaon dwaara shasit hokar ant main 1881 main yah angrejon ke aadhipaty main chalaa gaya.
 nagar ke uttar main anasagar tathaa kuch aage fwysagar naamak kritrim jhilein hai. yahan kaa prasiddh musalmaan fakir muinuddin chishti kaa makbara prasiddh he. dhai din ke jhopade main kul 40 stambh hai or sab main nae-nae prakaar kii nakkashi he; koi bhi do stambh nakkashi main samaan nahin hai. taragarh pahari kii chotii par ek durg bhi he.
 iskaa aadhunik nagar ek prasiddh railway kendra bhi he. yahan par namak kaa vyapaar hota he jo sambhars jhil se laayaa jaataa he. yahan khaadya, vastra tathaa railway ke kaarkaane hai. tel taiyaar karnaa bhi yahan kaa ek pramukh vyapaar he.
 ajmer ke jain mandir main bhagwaan parswanath kii murti

 ajmer shahar kii sthapana 7 veen sadi main raja ajay paal chouhan ne kii he, jinhone muslimon ke hamale tak shashan nahin kiya jane tak behad sammanit chouhan vansh ko sthapit kiya he jo lagaataar bharat kaa shashan karata he.
 chouhan kaa majboot pakad 11 9 5 main ajmer main bana rahaa, jab tak mohammad gori, ek afgaan shaasha ne aakhri chouhan ke samraat ko haraaya. us samay tak antim chouhan samraat prithviraj chouhan the. uske baad se teji se badalte samraaton ke baad se ajmer ko arajak kaal kaa saamana karnaa padaa tha.
 ant main, 1556 main, mughal samraat akbar ne ajmer jeeta or ajmer ko rajasthan rajya main apane sabhi abhiyaanon ke mukhya mukhyalay ke rup main istemaal kiya. mughalon kii giravat ke baad, ajmer shahar kaa niyantran maraathon ko paarit kar diya gaya he, khaskar gwalior kii sindhiyaan!!
