 hampi madhyakaalin hindu rajya vijaynagar samrajya kii rajdhani thi. tungabhadra nadi ke tat par sthit yah nagar ab hampi naam se janaa jaataa he or ab keval khandhiron ke rup main hi avshesh he. inhen dekhne se prathit hota he ki kisi samay main yahan ek samrudhshali sabhyataa nivas karti hogi. bharat ke karnataka rajya main sthit yah nagar unesco ke vishwa ke viraasat sthalon main shaamil kiya gaya he. har saal yahan hazaron kii sankhya main paryatak or tirthayatri aate hai. hampi kaa vishaal failaav gol chattaanon ke tilon main vistrut he. ghaatiyon or tilon ke bich paanch sau se bhi adhik smarak chihn hai. inamen mandir, mahal, tahakaane, jal-khandhar, puraane bazaar, shahi mandap, garh, chabutare, rajkosh.... aadi asankhya imaaraten hai.
 hampi main vithala mandir parisar nihsandeh sabase shandaar smarakon main se ek he. iske mukhya haul main lage 56 stambhon ko thapthapaane par unamem se sangeet lahariyaan nikalati hai. haul ke purvi hisse main prasiddh shila-rath he jo vastav main pathar ke pahiyon se chalata tha. hampi main aise anek aashcharya hai, jaise yahan ke raajaaon ko anaaj, sone or rupayon se taula jaataa tha or use garib logon main baant diya jaataa tha. raniyon ke liye bane snanagar mehrabadar galiyaaron, jharokhedaar chazzon or camel ke aakaar ke fawwaron se susajjit hote the. iske alaava camel mahal or janaankhaana bhi aise aashchayon main shaamil hai. ek sundar do-manjila sthaan jiske maarg jyamitiya dhang se bane hai or dhoop or hawa lene ke liye kisi phool kii pattiyon kii tarah bane hai. yahan hathi-khaane ke pravesh-dwaar or gumbad mehrabadar bane hue hai or shahar ke shahi pravesh-dwaar par hajaaraa ram mandir bana he.
