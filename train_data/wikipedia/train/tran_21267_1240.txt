 shekhar sen hindi natya jagat ke gayak, sangeetkaar, geetkaar evam abhinayta hai. ve apane ek patriya prastutiyon "tulsi", "kabir", "vivekananda" or "sahib" ke liye jane jate hai.
 ve sangeet naatak academy ke vartmaan adhyaksh hai. 
 shekhar sen kaa janm evam paalan poshan raipur, chhatisgarh main ek bengali parivaar main hua. unke pita dow arun kumaar sen indira kala sangeet vishvavidhyalay, kharagarh ke kulapati the or maa dow anitha sen gwalior gharaane ke prasiddh shastreey gayika or sangitajna thi. unhen sangeet viraasat main mila.
