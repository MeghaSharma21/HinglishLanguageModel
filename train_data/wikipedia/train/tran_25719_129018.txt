 janaki ballab patnayak bhartiya raajnneetijna hai jo 2009 se assam ke rajyapal the. wo bhartiya rashtriya congress netaa ke rup main 1980 se 1989 or punah 1995 se 1999 tak odisha ke mukhyamantri rah chuke hai. wo naveen patnayak se pehle sabase lambe samay tak odisha ke mukhyamantri rahe.
 unka janm rameshwar, puri jile main 3 janvari 1927 ko hua. unhone khurd hai skool se apni vidyalayan shiksha purna kii or utkal vishvavidhyalay se 1947 main sanskrit main snaatak or 1949 main raajnneeti vigyaan main kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay se snatakottar kii shiksha purna kii.
 unhen 1980 main rajya sarkaar main paryaton, naagarik uddyan or shram mantri banaaya gaya. 1980 se 1981 or punah 1995 se 1999 tak wo odisha ke mukhyamantri rahe.
 wo kathith taur par anjana mishra balaatkaar maamle main shaamil the.
