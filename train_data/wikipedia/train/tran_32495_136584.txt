 udhyami,kaarobaari 
 samir treepathi Samir Tripathi ek bhartiya udhyami he, jinhone power kansalting company ‘medhaj techno concept privet limited’ kii sthapana kii. udhyami ke saath-saath samir treepathi jyotish vidya ke achche-khaase gyaata bhi hai.
 samir treepathi kaa janm uttar pradesh ke ek chote se jile mirzapur main samaanya brahman parivaar main hua. samir treepathi ne na keval apane college kii padhai chaatravrutti ke madhyam se puri kii balki padhai ke prati apana samarpan bhi arjeet kiya. samir ne apane kariyar kii shuruuaat ‘jiisi alstom’ main ek diziner ejeeniyar kii. iske baad apane kariyar ko aage badhaane ke liye samir ne antarrashtriya market main apane hunur ko pahchaan di or ‘abu dhabi’ kaa ruukh kiya. antarrashtriya str par kai deshon main kaam karne ke baad samir ne thaana kii vah apane desh main hi is anubhav ek nayaa mukaam denge. ant unhone uttar pradesh ke lakhanu main medhaj kansalting company kii neenv rakhi. vartmaan main is company main 1500 se zyada karmchaaree kaam kar rahe hai.
 samir treepathi ko kai puraskaar se bhi sammanit kiya ja chukaa he. unhen do baar rashtriya puraraskaar se sammanit kiya ja chukaa he.modi sarkaar ne shri samir treepathi ko ‘laghu evam sookshm udyog’ ke kshetra main saal ‘2014- rashtriya puraskaar’ se sammanit kiya gaya he, unhen yah sammaan kendriya mantri shri calraj mishra ne diya tha. vahiin, saal duusari baar ‘sookshm evam laghu udyog’ main utkrisht kaary ke liye ‘rashtriya puraskaar-2015’ se sammanit kiya gaya he.
 lagaataar duusari baar sookshm evam laghu udyog main utkrisht karyanishpaadan kii manyata hetu ‘rashtriya puraskaar-2015’ se bhi sammanit kiya gaya he. shri samir treepathi ne medhaj techno concept ke chairmen env managing diarector hai. kareeb 10 saal pehle unhone medhaj kii neenv rakhi. unki mehnat or lagan se sanstha ko gramin vidyutikaran env urja ke kshetra main salaahkaar ke rup main utkrisht kaam kiya. lagbhag 28 praanton main apni company medhaj ko safaltaapurvak sthapit kiya he. samir treepathi kushal udhyami ke saath-saath jyotish vidya ke kshetra main vishesh gyaan praapt he.
 -pradhanmantri narendra modi or calraj mishra dwaara bharat ke rashtriya puraskaar ‘inoveshan in sarvice’ se shri samir treepathi ko sammanit kiya gaya.
 -shri samir treepathi ‘udyog ratna’ se bhi sammanit ho rakhe hai.
 -grih mantralay dwaara shri samir treepathi ko vibhinn mantralayon ke hindi salaahkaar samiti ke sadsy ke rup main niyukt kiya gaya he.
 -shri samir treepathi ‘dow abdul kalam technical uniwarsity’ garvaning council ke bhi sadsy hai.
 -saal 2017 main samir treepathi ko ‘vijanary of uttar pradesh-2017’ sammaan se sammanit kiya gaya. yah sammaan unhen uttar pradesh ke tatkalin upmukhyamantri keshav prasad or dinesh sharma ne diya.
 -samir treepathi ko do baar ‘sookshm evam laghu’ udyog main apana utkrisht yogadan dene ke liye ‘rashtriya puraskaar’ se bhi nawaja gaya he.
 -sookshm evam laghu udyog rashtriya puraskaar 2014 or sookshm evam laghu udyog rashtriya puraskaar 2016
 samir treepathi ek madhyam parivaar kii prishthbhumi se aate hai or unhone hameshaa desh ke liye ek majboot buniyadi dhaanche kaa sapana dekha he, taaki prayek bhartiya ke jivan kii gunvattta ek nishchit str par sudhaar kare. medhaj company gramin vidyutikaran va navinkaraneey urja kshetra main apana mahatvapurn yogadan nibhaati aa rahi he. bharat sarkaar kii tarj par medhaj kaa lakshya he ki bharat ke har gaanv tak 100 fisadi vidyutikaran kiya jaae. medhaj company ke 500 se bhi zyada ejeeniyar 3 lakh se bhi zyada gaanvon main bharat sarkaar kii yojanaon ke tahat vidyutikaran kaa kaam kar rahe hai. jaise ‘RGGVY, RAPDRP, NEF, DDUGJY, IPDS, SMART GRID’ ityadi.
 samir treepathi swasth jivan ke liye yoga, dhyaan or khel kii taraf kaaphi aakarshith he. samir apane chaaro or logon ko swasth rahane kii aadaton ko aage badhaane ke protsaahit karte hai. samir feet or surakshit bharat ke liye khelon main bhi ruchi rakhte he or logon ko khel ke prati jaagruk rakhte hai. samir treepathi "karate association of uttar pradesh" ke adhyaksh ke rup main manyata praapt hai. 
 samir treepathi kaa maananaa he ki bharat bhavishya kaa mahashakti tab-tak nahin ban saktaa, jab-tak yahaan ke prayek vyakti kii skil ko ubhaara na ja sake. iske liye samir treepathi ne pradhanmantri kaushal vikaas yojana ke tahat kai kaarykram main yuvaaon ko aage badhaane kaa kaam kar rahe hai. 
 in sab ke vipareet samir treepathi girl child ajukeshan ke liye bhi saraahneey kaam kar rahe he. vahi bina kisi prachaar-prasar ke kai ladakiyon kii shiksha pryojit kar rahe hai, jinke mata-pita unako padhaane main sakasham nahin he. samir treepathi is tathy ke prati bahut hi bhavuk hai ki medhaj main kaam karne vaale ek chaparasi tak kii beti acchhee shiksha praapt kar, jis str kii padhai unke khud ke bachche kar rahe hai.
