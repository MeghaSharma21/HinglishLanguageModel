 rangarasia ek bhartiya televijan dharawahik he jo colors tivi par prasaarit hota tha. yah dharawahik ek gramin ladki parvati evam ek seemaa suraksha dal ke adhikari rudra par aadhaarit he. rangarasia jaisalmer or jodhpur jaise sthanon par filmaaya gaya he.
 yah kaarykram ek gramin ladki parvati va ek sena adhikari rudra pratap ranavat kii anokhi prem kahani se sambandhit he. rudra ke liye pyaar kaa koi arth nahin hota. vah pyaar se nafrat karata tha. uske liye kaam hi karttavya he. parantu paro ke liye pyaar ek aashaa he.
 baad main, yah kahani rudra evam paro kii prem kahani ke aasapaas hi gumti rahati he.
