 e es kiran kumaar bharat ke ek prasiddh vaigyanik hai tathaa ve bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan ke adhyaksh hai. 14 janvari 2015 ko unhone isro ke adhyaksh kaa padbhaar grahan kiya. isse purv ve antariksh anuprayog centre, ahmedabad ke nideshak the.
 shri kiran kumaar ne 1975 main isro ke antariksh anuprayog centre se apane kariyar kii shuruuaat kii thi. baad main vah is centre ke associat diarector or march, 2012 main antariksh anuprayog centre ke nideshak bane. unhone airborn ke liye electro optical imaging sensor ke nirmaan or vikaas, bhaskar tivi pelod se lekar vartmaan mars color camera, thermal infredid imaging spectrometer or bharat ke mars arbitor antariksh yaan ke mars upkarnon ke liye methane sensor ke nirmaan main mahatvapurn yogadan diya. 
 shri kiran kumaar ne mangal grah kii ore bheje gaye mars arbitor antariksh yaan ke saath saath ise mangal kii kaksha main sthapit karne ke maamle main safaltaapurvak ranneetiyaan banaai. unhone bhoomi, mahaasaagar, vaataavaran or grah se jude adhyayanon main bhi apana mahatvapurn yogadan diya he. 
 shri kiran kumaar ne antarrashtriya mausam vigyaan sanghathan, prithvi nigaraanee upgrah samiti or naagarik antariksh sahyog par bharat-america sanyukt kaaryakari samooh jaise antarrashtriya manchon main isro kaa pratinidhitiv kiya.
