 radha mohan singh bhartiya janata party ke raajnneetijna evam bharat ke vartmaan kendriya krishi kendriya mantri hai. radha mohan singh naunvi, gyarahavin, terahavin, pandrahavin or solahavin loksabhaa ke saansad nirvaachit hue hai. vah purvi champaran, bihar se loksabhaa saansad hai. 
 akhil bhartiya vidhyaarthee parishad, jansangh va rashtriya swayamsevak sangh ke swayamsevak rahe shri singh, saarvajanik jivan main sanghathan ke prati samarpan ke liye jane jate hai.
