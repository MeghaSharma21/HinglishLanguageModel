 bhartiya rashtriya upgrah pranali isro dwaara shuru bahuuddeshiy bhu sthir upgrahon kii ek shrinkhalaa he jo doorsanchaar, prasaaran, mausam vigyaan or khoj or bachaav kaary ke liye upyog hota he. 1983 main shuru kiya hua insat, asia-prashanth kshetra main sabase badi deshiy sanchaar pranali he. yah bharat sarkaar ke antariksh vibhaag, doorsanchaar vibhaag, bhartiya mausam vigyaan vibhaag, akashwani or doordarshan channel ke ek sanyukt udhyam he. sachiv-str insat samanvay samiti ke upar insat pranali ke samgr samanvay or prabandhan tiki hui he. insat upgrahon bharat ke tivi or sanchaar aavashyaktaaon kii seva karne ke liye vibhinn band main transponder pradaan karte hai. isro antarrashtriya kospus-sarast kaarykram ke ek sadsy ke rup main dakshin asian or hind mahaasaagar kshetra main khoj or bachaav abhiyaan ke liye sankat chetavanee sanketon ko praapt karne ke liye upgrahon ke transponder kaa istemaal karte he.
 bhartiya rashtriya upgrah pranali agast 1983 main insat -1 bi ke prakshepan ke saath sadhikaar hua. insat pranali bharat ke doordarshan or radio prasaaran, doorsanchaar or mausam sambandhi kshetron main ek kranti kii shuruyaat kii. doordaraj ke kshetron or band kinare dwipon ke liye tivi or aadhunik doorsanchaar suvidhaon kaa teji se vistaar sakasham hone main iskaa haath he. saath main, pranali sanchaar sevaaon ke liye si, vistaarit si or you main transponder kaa pradaan karte he. kalpana-1 ek vishesh mausam sambandhi upgrah he. upgrahon par najar rakhane or niyantran karne ke liye hasan or bhopal main master niyantran suvidha maujuud he.
