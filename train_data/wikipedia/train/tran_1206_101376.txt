 puniyaal yaa poonyal paak-adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-balatistan kshetra ke gizar zile kii ek ghati he. prashasnic drushti se yah gizar zile kii ek tehsil he. iskaa prashasnic mukhyalay gaahkooch he, jo gizar zile kii rajdhani bhi he. puniyaal vaadi kii oonchaai 5,000 se 9,000 foot ke bich main he or yah kai paryatakon ko aakarshith karti he. pehle jamaane main yah ek riyasat hua karti thi isliye ise kabhi-kabhi aaj bhi 'puniyaal riyasat' yaa 'puniyaal jaagir' bula diya jaataa he.
 puniyaal ke adhiktar log shina bhashaa bolne vaale shiya muslim hai jo ismaili shakha se sambandh rakhte hai. is kshetra par kabhi chitral ke raja kaa qabza ho gaya tha or tab yahan kuch sunni parivaar bhi aa gaye the jo rajput samudaay ke hai. shiya or sunni alag-alag qabristaanon kaa prayog karte hai.
