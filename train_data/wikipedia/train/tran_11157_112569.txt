 satguru yaa sadguru kaa arth he sachcha guru. yah shabd guru ke anya roopon jaise sangeet vidya ke guru, padhaane vaale guru, mata-pita rupi guru aadi se alag mana jaataa he. satguru naam keval aise gyanprapt rishi/ sant ko diya jaataa he jiske jivan kaa uddeshy aadhyatmik maarg par guru-shishy parampara ke anusaar banaae gaye shishy ko maargdarshan dena he jiske parinaam se ishwar prapti ho kar atmagyaan ho jaataa he.
 anushansa yah kahati he ki sachche guru yaa satguru kaa sabase bada gun yah he ki vah swayan bhi sachche maalik ishwar ko jaan chukaa ho. kabir ke ek shabd main satguru ko sachcha sant kaha gaya he. bhagwaan ram ke guru vasishtha, treta yug main satguru the. swami shankar purushottam tirth yoga vaasishth kaa sandarbh dete hai:-"vaastavik guru vah he jo drushti, sparsh, yaa anudesh se shishy ke sharir main anand samvedan utpann kar de". satguru kaa akhand brahmacharee hona sabhi ne aavashyak nahin mana he. udhaahrant: hindu satguru tukaraam, ek grihasth the. moinuddinchishti ke bhi parivaar tha. satguru kabir ke bhi do bachche the kamaal or kamali jo bahut bhakt the. * gularpura ashram vaale sadgurudev shri brajmohan gotam bhi ek grihasth sant the, aapan apane jivan main kabhi swaym ke liye daan, dakshina nahin lee, ekadam sada jivan, ore kai pratyaksh pramaan diye, jisase vishaal jan sailaab aapako bhagwaan tulya pujte the, kabhi koi pravachan nahin kiye apane karm se hi ve apane shishyo ko shiksha diya karte the!
