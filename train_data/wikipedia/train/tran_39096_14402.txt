 tommy thomas ek malaysian samvidhan kanoon visheshgy, civil lijigater or bhartiya mul ke malaysia ke atorni general he. 4 june, 2018 ko malaysia ke sultan mohammad pancham ne unhen malaysia kaa nayaa atorni general niyukt kiya. ve 55 varshon main is par pad niyukt pehle alpsankhyak vyakti hai. is pad par unhone mohammad apandi ali kaa sthaan liya he. sultan ne islamic samoohon ke virodh ke baavjood is pad par thomas kii niyukti ko manjuuri di.
