 saiha zila bhartiya rajya mizoram ke aath zilon main se ek he. saiha kasbaa is zile kaa mukhyalay he. bharat kii janaganana 2011 ke anusaar yah mizoram kaa sabase kam jansankhya vaala zila he.
 saiha zila pehle chhimatuipui zile kaa ang tha. san 1998 main saiha alag zila bana.
 yah zila uttar tathaa uttar-pashchim main lunglei zile se, pashchim main longtlai zile se, dakshin tathaa purv main myanmar se ghira he. zile kaa kshetrafal 1399.9 varg kimi he. saiha kasbaa is zile kaa mukhyalay he. 
 san 2006 main gramin vikaas evam panchayat raaj mantralay, bharat sarkaar ne saiha ko desh ke 250 sabase pichde zilon kii shrenee main rakhaa. is zile ko vartmaan main pichhadaa kshetra anudaan nidhi ke antargat anudaan mil rahaa he. 
 is zile main teen gram vikaas khand saiha, sangau tathaa tuipang hai. zile ke teen vidhansabha kshetra saiha, sangau tathaa tuipang hai. 
 2011 janaganana ke anusaar, is zile kii jansankhya 56,574 he, jisame 28,594 purush tathaa 27,980 mahilaayein hai. is prakaar se is zile kii jansankhya lagbhag greenland ke baraabar he. jansankhya ke anusaar iskaa bharat ke 640 zilon main 628vaan sthaan he. is zile kii jansankhya san 2001 kii tulana main 2011 main 61,056 se ghatakar 56,574 ho gayi. is zile kii 2001-11 ke madhya dashakeey jansankhya vruddhi dar -7.34% rahi. is zile kii jansankhya ghanatv 40 prayek varg kilometre main nivasee he. zile kii linganupaat dar 979 tathaa sakshartaa dar 90.01% he. zile kii 55.62% jansankhya gaanvon main tathaa 44.38% jansankhya nagaron main nivas karti he.
 yahan kaa pramukh bahusankhyak dharm isai he, jo ki kul jansankhya kaa 97.18% he. anya alpsankhyak dharm hindu 1.65%, muslim 0.91%, buddha 0.18%, sikh 0.02% tathaa jain 0.01% hai. 0.05% logon ne apane dharm kaa ullekh nahin kiya he.
 yahan kaa bahusankhyak samudaay maaraa logon kaa he, jinka ek swytt zila parishad maaraa swytt zila parishad bhi he, jo do gramin vikaas khandon saiha tathaa tuipang se milakar bana he. vahiin sangau gram vikaas khand main laai logon kii bahusankhya he, tathaa yah laai swytt zila parishad kaa ang bhi he.
 1997 main yahan par phongpui rashtriya udyaan kii sthapana hui, jiskaa kshetrafal 50 kimi2 he.
