 andkosh purush ke neeche ek thili hoti he. ise scrotum kaha jaataa he. is thili kii twachaa dhili hoti he. jo garmiyon main adhik badhkar latak jaati he tathaa sardiyon main sikudakar choti hoti he. iske andar vrushan hote he inkaa mukhya kaary shukranu or purush uttejit drav ko bananaa hota he. ve purush jo aag ke saamne kaary karte he, adhik garm pani se nahaate hai. yah kachha ko adhik kasakar baandhte hai. unke andkosh se shukranu kam maatra main yaa nahin ban pate he.
 andkosh kii lambaai 5 cm semi or chaudaai 2.5 semi hoti he. isamen rakt kaa sanchaar bahut adhik hota he. donon taraf ke andkosh ek nalika ke dwaara jude hote hai. jisko vaas diference kehete he tathaa duusari taraf ye anya granthi se jude rahete hai. jinako seminal vesaikal kehete he.
