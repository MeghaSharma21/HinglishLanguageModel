 mikoyayan-gurevitch mig-1 dwitiya vishwa yuddh kaa ek soviet ladaaku vimaan tha, jise 1939 main jari ek ucch oonchai vaale ladaaku vimaan ke liye aavashyaktaa ko puraa karne ke liye banaaya gaya tha. aluminium jaise samrik samagriyon par maang ko kam karne ke liye, vimaan ko zyaadaatar steel tubing or lakdi se banaaya gaya tha. udaan pareekshan main kai kami dekhi gayi, lekin inhe thik karne se pehle vimaan ke utpaadan kaa aadesh diya gaya tha. haalanki ise sanbhaalanaa mushkil ho gaya tha. iske dizin ko sanshodhit karne se pehle ek sau mig-1 kaa nirmaan kiya gaya tha. iske sanshodhit dizin ko mig-3 vimaan kaha jaataa he. 1941 main soviet vaayu sena kii ladaaku regiment ke liye vimaan ko jari kiya gaya tha, lekin zyaadaatar oparation barbarosa ke shuruaati dinon main jahiraa taur par nasht hue the. oparation barbarosa june 1941 main soviet sangh kaa german par akraman tha.
