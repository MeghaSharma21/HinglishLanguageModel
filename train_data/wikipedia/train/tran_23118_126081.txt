 lamark yaa jin baptist piyer antvan da monet shivelior da francisi jaivvigyaani the. lamark pratham vaigyanik the, jinhone kasheruki or aksheruki jeevon main bhed kiya or sarvapratham invartibratt shabd kaa upyog kiya. 1802 i. main inhone jeev, yaa paudhon ke adhyayan ke liye biology shabd kaa upyog kiya. inhone mausam vigyaan or mausam kii purvasuchanaaon se sambandhit vaarshik reporton kaa bhi prakaashan kiya tha. ye vikaswad ke janmdata hai. inkaa vikaswad kaa siddhant lamarkwaad kehalaata he.
 kaa janm 1 agast 1774 ko bagentine ke picardi main hua tha. 17 varsh kii aayu main ye sena main bhartee ho gaye or san 1763 main sainik jivan tyaag kar paris chale gaye, jahaan inhone vanaspatishaastra kaa adhyayan kiya. 1778 i inaki floor franshies naamak pustak prakashit hui. iske duusare varsh acedemi siance ke vanaspati vibhaag main inaki niyukti ho gai, par ye ise chhodkar samakaleen, francisi, prakritik vigyaani, bufdhan ke putron ke saath yatra par prashikshak ke rup main chale gaye. yatra se do varsh baad lautne par ye shahi baag ke vanaspati sangrahayal ke rakshak niyukt hue. 1793 i. main ye ajaayabaghar main professor niyukt hue or aksheruki sangrah kaa uttardaayitv liya. yahan inhone 1819 i. tak, apane andhe hone tak, kaary kiya. inkaa budhapa badi garibi main beetaa. 18 disambar 1829 i. ko inkaa dehavsaan ho gaya.
