 pankaj singh bharat ke uttar pradesh rajya ke varttman chandoli jile kii chakiya tehsil ke ek chote se gram bhabhora ke rahane vaale he. pankaj sinhakaa janm apane nanihaal jharkhand ke palamu jile ke daltonganj main ko hua tha. ve bhartiya janata party ke rajaneta hai. varttman main uttar pradesh bhartiya janata party ke mahasachiv hai. bharat ke grih mantri rajnath singh ke putr hai.
 2001 main bhartiya janata party main sakriy hue. party kii ore se yojit dharna-pradarshan main janaa shuru kiya.2004 main unhen bhaajapa yuva morcha kii pradesh kaaryakarini kaa sadsy banaaya gaya.2007 main unhen bhaajapa yuva morcha kaa pradesh adhyaksh banaaya gaya. lekin yah kahkar pad chhod diya kii ve kuch din bina pad ke hi kaary karange.2007 main unhen pradesh bhaajapa kaaryakarini kaa sadsy banaaya gaya.2007 main vidhansabha chunaav kshetra chirigaanv seat, varanasi se ummidavaari kii ghoshnaa bhi ho gai; lekin khud pankaj singh ke pita rajnath singh ne unhen salaah di ki abhi kuch saal or bhartiya janata party ke sanghathan main kaam karnaa chahie iske baad chunavi raajnneeti main janaa chahie. pankaj singh vah chunaav nahin lade. 2010 main unhen uttar pradesh bhaajapa kaa sachiv banaaya gaya. 2012 main ve pradesh bhaajapa ke mahasachiv ke rup main padaunnat kiye gayi. 2013 main unhen uttar pradesh bhaajapa kaa duusari baar mahasachiv banaaya gaya. vartmaan main july 2016 se teesri baar mahasachiv ke pad par kaary kar rahe hai. in kaarykalon ke dauraan pankaj singh saamaajik or raajnneetik taur par behad sakriy rahe. gaanv or kisaani main dilchaspee rakhane vaale pankaj singh ko jab bhaajapa ke gaanv chalo abhiyaan main shaamil hone kaa avasar mila unhone is abhiyaan ke tahat 100 se adhik gaanvon kaa daura kiya. vahaan ke logon se gaanv or kheti kii samasyaon ko samajha or janaa.
 12 disambar, 1978 ko jharkhand ke palamu jile ke daltonganj main janme pankaj skool ke baad college kii padhai ke liye dilli aae. emity vishvavidhyalay se emb kii padhai kii.
