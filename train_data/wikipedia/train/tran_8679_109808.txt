 ganesh narayandas devi prasiddh saahitiyik aalochak evam bhashaa anusandhan evam prakaashan kendra, vadodara ke sansthaapak nideshak hai. ve pehle sayajirao gaekwad vishvavidhyalay, vadodara ke angreji ke praadhyaapak the. dow ganesh or unki patni surekha devi 'bhashaa' naamak gaiyr raajnneetik trust sanchalit karte hai, jo gujarat ke janajaatiy kshetron main vishesh rup se sakriy hai. dow ganesh kaa prabal daawa he ki bharat se 'lok' kii jubaan kaatane kii suniyojit sajeesh rachi ja rahi he. 'bhashaa' isi ke viruddh ek abhiyaan he.
 bharat kii lupt hoti bhashaaon par vyapak surveykshan, sanvardhan or sangrakshan tathaa dastavejikaran karne kaa unhone swechha se bidaa uthaaya he. isi surveykshan ke madhyam se unka kahana he ki hindi aam dharna ko todatii hui teji se aage bad rahi he. 2010 unke netritv main piples linguitic survey of india ne bharat kii 780 jivit bhashaaon kaa surveykshan prakashit kiya.
 shri ganesh devi ko sahitya evam shiksha ke kshetra main utkrisht yogadan ke liye 2014 main bharat sarkaar ne padmashri se sammanit kiya.
