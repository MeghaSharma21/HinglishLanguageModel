 mathura rail durghatana 2009 21 oktober, 2009 ko bharat main hui ek rail durghatana he jisamen lagbhag 22 log maare gaye the. buudhvaar subah dilli jane vaali goa samparkkranti express kaa ingine mewar express kii aakiri bogi se takaraa gaya. durghatana ke turant baad rail mantralay dwaara jaanch ke aadesh de die gaye or yah jaanch railway ayog dwaara kii gai.
 railway adhikariyon ke aarmabhik aakalnon ke anusaar durghatana kaa kaaran maanviya bhul bataaya gaya jo ki signal ko andekha karke rail gaadi ko usee patari par le gaya jahaan mewar express pehle se hi khadi thi. mathura ke bajna pul ke paas udaipur se dilli ke nizamuddin jane vaali mewar express khadi thi ki peeche se aa rahi goa saptakranti express ne use takkar maar di. takkar se goa express kaa ingine mewar express ke mahila dibbe main ja ghusaa.
 railway sutron ke anusaar mewar express ko chen khinchakar roka gaya tha. pratyaksh darshiyon ke anusaar police vaale kuch atikat yatriyon ko pakadana chaah rahe the. isiilie is train ko vaajana pul ke paas roka gaya. peeche se aa rahi goa express ko aage train ke hone kii jaankaree nahin mili or vah mewar express se ja takaraai.
