 adukala dharmjaigarh mandal main bharat ke chhattisgarh rajya ke antargat raigarh jile kaa ek gaanv he.yahan jane ke liye raigarh se gharghoda road se hote hue gerwani se katata he or rabo dame hote hue jaataa he tenda nawapaara road par janaa padataa he phir pani gaav jate hue edukala padataa he.iske alaava chaal se nawapaara road hote hue bojia haute janapadataa he phir katapali se hote hue edukala padataa he yahan 8th kaksha tak skool he.
