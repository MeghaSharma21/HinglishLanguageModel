 spidar-main maarval comics kaa kaalpanik super hero he. iske rachayita stan lee evam steve ditco hai. sabase pehle 1962 main ai comics main isaki pratham upasthiti hui thi. comics main vah peter parker ke rup main ek anaath baccha hota he jiske mata-pita richard or mairi parker ek hawaii jahaaj kii durghatana main maare jate hai. new york shahar main use uski mausi aant me or ankal ben paalate hai. use kishoravastha or vittiya muddon ke sangharshon se nipatate hue dikhaaya jaataa he. uski utpatti kahani main use ek radiodharmee makadi kaat leti he jisase usamen makadi se sambandhit shakti or kshamtaayen aa jaati hai. jisamen shaamil he:- adhikansh satahon par chadhane kii kshamta, apane swayan ke avishkaar upkarnon ko kalai par pahanakar upyog karke makadi ke jaal ko shoot karnaa jise vah "web-shooter" kahata he or apane "spidar cens" se khataron ke aane se pehle abhas hona. isi kahani main spidar main mul rup se prasiddhi paane ke liye is shakti kaa upyog karata he. ek din vah ankal ben ko goli maarthe hue bhaag rahe ek chor ko jane dene detaa he. uske baad vah apni shakti ko jimmedaari se upyog karnaa sikhta he.
