 federation internationel di footbal association, jise aamtaur par fifa ke naam se janaa jaataa he, footbal kaa antarrashtriya niyantran nikaay he. iskaa mukhyalay jurikh, switzraland main he or iske vartmaan adhyaksh giyani infentino hai. fifa footbal ke pramukh antarrashtriya pratiyogitaaon ke sanghathan or aayojan, jiname sabase ullekhneey fifa vishwa cap he ke liye jimmedaar he or iskaa aayojan 1930 se kar rahaa he.
 fifa ke 208 sadsy sangh hai, jo sanyukt raashtra ke sadasyon se 15 adhik or antarrashtriya olympic samiti se teen zyada hai, haalanki yah sankhya international association of athletics federation se paanch sadsy kam he.
 fipha sammaan or puraskaar
 fipha har saal, saal ke sarvashresth purush va mahila khiladi ko fipha valon-dor ke khitaab se sammanit karti he. saal 2013 k fipha valon-dor puraskaar karishmai khiladi christiano ronaldo ko mila. star footballar leonal mesi ko pachhaadne main ronaldo ko paanch saal lag gaye lekin baavjood iske vah khud ko roak nahin sake or ro pade.
