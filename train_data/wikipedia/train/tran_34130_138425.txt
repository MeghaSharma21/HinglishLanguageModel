 beta rijeeyo, "jwalamukhi udai" ke rup main janaa gaya shukr grah kaa ek kshetra he. 3000 kilometre aaklit, 25°18′N 282°48′E / 25.3°N 282.8°E / 25.3; 282.8par kendrit yah shukr kaa ek pramukh ucchbhumi kshetra gathit karata he..
 grah ke purv radar surveykshan main dikhi in praarambhik sthalakritiyon ko greek varnmaala ke aksharon ke naam die gaye the. beta rijeeyo un sthalakritiyon main se ek tha. yah 1964 main dick goldstin dwaara khoja gaya or unhi ke dwaara namit hua tha. yah naam 1976 or 1979 ke madhya antarrashtriya khagoleey sangh ke grahiy pranali namakaran ke kaaryakari samooh dwaara anumodith hua tha. shukr kii sthalakriti striyon par namit kii jaati he, yaa to devi par yaa kisi mahila par, lekin maxwell montes, alpha rijeeyo or beta rijeeyo is niyam ke teen apwaad hai.
