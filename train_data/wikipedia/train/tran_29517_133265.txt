 shivani bharat ke madhya pradesh praant kaa ek shahar he. 
 shivani shahar rashtravyapee samaacharon main us samay aayaa jab surat main darj hue balaatkaar ke cese ke baad se faraar chal rahe asaram baapu ke bete narayan sai ko madhya pradesh ke shivani shahar main dekha gaya he. sai apane teen saathiyon ke saath shivani ke banjaari mata mandir kii dharmashala main ek raat ke liye tehra tha.
