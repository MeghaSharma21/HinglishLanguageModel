 banking lokapal yojana 2006 bhartiya bainkon ke graahakon kii shikayaton evam samasyaon ko sulajhane ke liye armbh kii gayi ek yojana he. iske antargat ek 'banking lokapal' kii niyukti kii jaati he jo ek ardh-nyaayik praadhikari he. vaise to banking lokapal yojana 1995 main lagu kii gai thi, lekin 2002 evam 2006 main is yojana kaa dayera badhaate hue sanshodhan kiye gaye, taaki bainkon dwaara svachh, paardarshee, bhedbhaav rahit or jimmedaari purvak banking sevaayen pradaan kii ja saken. yah ek swashaasi swatantra sanstha he jo bainkon dwaara pradaan kii gai sevaaon kii nigaraanee rakhti he. graahak kisi bhi bank ke adhikari va karmchaaree kii shikaayat va samay se sevaayen na milane par banking lokapal ko shikaayat dak, i mail, aan line darj kara saktaa he. nishulk kii jane vaali is shikaayat kaa nistaaran tees din ke andar kiya jaataa he. graahakon kii suvidha va bainkon main paardarshita laane ke liye yah yojana sanchalit he.
 bank graahakon dwaara banking sevaaon main pai gai kamiyon ke sambandh main apni shikayaton ke samaadhaan hetu unhen drutagati vaala or kam kharch vaala manch pradaan karne ke liye bhartiya rigerv bank dwaara mukhyatya kshetreey gramin bainkon par bhi lagu karke iskaa vistaar karne main sanshodhit kiya gaya tha.
 bhartiya rigerv bank ne nae kshetron main graahakon kii shikayaton jaise credit card sambandhi shikayaton, bainkon ke cells agenton dwaara vachanabaddh sevaayen pradaan na karne or bainkon dwaara vachanabaddhata ko puraa na karne, graahakon ko purv soochna die bina seva prabhaar lagane tathaa chote moolyavarg ke note va sikke sweekaar na karne yaa chote moolyavarg ke note sweekaar karne hetu kamishan lene ke sambandh main shikaayat kar sakenge.
 banking lokapal yojana sabase pehle 1995 main armbh kii gayi thi. ise 2002 main punarikshit kiya gaya. vartmaan yojana 1 janvari 2006 se lagu he.
