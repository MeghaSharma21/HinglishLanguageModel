 mudies inwestors sarvice mudies corporation kii bond-credit kii reating karne vaali company he. isko sankshep main keval 'mudies' kaha jaataa he. mudies kii niveshak seva vanijyik or sarkari sansthaaon ke dwaara jari kiye gaye bondon par antarrashtriya vittiya anusandhan pradaan karti he. mudies kaa naam duniya kii sabase badi teen credit reating agenciyon main standard and purs or fich samooh ke saath shaamil he. company ek manakikrut reating paimaane kaa upyog karke udhaarkartaaon kii rinpaatrata ko rank detii he. is reating paimaane main chook kii sthiti main niveshak ke sambhavit nuksaan kii gananaa kii jaati he. mudies kii niveshak seva bond bazaar ke vibhinn khandon main rin pratibhutiyon ko reating detii he. inamen sarkari, municipal or corporate bond; mani market fundon tathaa niyat-aay vaale fundon jaise prabandhit nivesh; bainkon tathaa gaiyr-banking vittiya companiyon sahit vittiya sansthaaen; or sanrachanagat vitt main aasti shreniyan shaamil hai. mudies kii niveshak seva kii reating main pratibhutiyon ko eee se si tak kii reating di jaati he jisamen eee ucchtam gunvattta or si nimnatam gunvattta ko darshata he. 
 mudies kii sthapana 1909 main jon mudi ne stock or bond tathaa bond or bond reating se sambandhit sankhyikee kaa minial banane ke liye kiya tha. you.es.secorities and exange kamishan ke dwaara varsh 1975 main company ko rashtriya manyataprapt sankhyikee reating sanghathan ke rup main chihnit kiya gaya tha. kai dashakon tak dan and bradstreet ke swaamitv ke baad mudies niveshak seva varsh 2000 main ek swatantra company ban gai. mudies kii sthapana ek holding company ke rup main hui. 
 kabhi-kabhi saath milakar inhen teen sabase badi credit reating agenciyon ke rup main sanderbhit kiya jaataa he.
