 hindustan petroleum korporation limited ek fortune 500 company he, jo bharat sarkaar kii duusari sabase badi ekikrut tel shodhan or vipanan karne vaali saarvajanik kshetra kii company he. hindustan petroleum ko sarkaar dwaara navratna shrenee main rakhaa gaya he. bharat main iskaa petroleum utpaadon ke vipanan me kul yogadan 20.9% or tel shodhan me 10.3% he. iske swaamitv me do tateey tel parishodhan kaarkaane hai. in parishodhikaon me kai prakaar ke petroleum utpaada jaise indhan tel or snehak kaa nirmaan hota he.
