 trishanku yaa kraks taramandal antarrashtriya khagoleey sangh dwaara jari kii gai 88 taramandalon kii suchi kaa sab se chota taramandal he. angrezi main ise kabhi-kabhi "sadarn cross" ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. yah bharat main sardiyon or basant ke mausam main gujarat se dakshini kshetron main dekha ja saktaa he, lekin uttar bharat se nahin. prithvi ke dakshini golardh se yah kisi bhi mausam main dekha ja saktaa he.
 trishanku taramandal main paanch mukhya taare hai, haalanki vaise isamen 19 taron ko bair naam die ja chuke hai. inamen se ek ke ird-gird gair-saureey grah parikrama karte hue paae gaye hai. is taramandal ke mukhya paanch taare is prakaar hai -
