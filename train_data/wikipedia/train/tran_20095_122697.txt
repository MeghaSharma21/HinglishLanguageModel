 nyaayamurti sujatha vasant manohar supreme court kii nyaayadhish or rashtriya maanavadhikaar ayog kii sadsy rah chuki he.
 28 agast 1934 main janmi sujatha manohar ek majboot kanoonee prishthbhumi vaale parivaar main paidaa huin. unke pita gujarat ucch nyaayalay ke mukhya nyaayadhish rah chuke hai. unki praarambhik shiksha mumbai main hui. tatpashchaat elfinston college, mumbai se snaatak karne ke baad ladi margaret haul, oxford, britain chali gayi jahaan unhone darshan shaastra, raajnneeti shastr or arthshaastra kii padhai kii. 14 farvari 1958 ko unhone adhivaktaa kii padhai hetu daakhila liya. 1970-71 ke dauraan unhen sahaayak sarkari adhivaktaa, city civil court, bombai main niyukt kiya gaya. 23 janvari 1978 se ve mumbai ucch nyaayalay kaa atirikt nyaayadhish niyukt kiya gaya. 28 navambar 1978 ko unhen sthaayee nyaayadhish ke rup main niyukt kiya gaya. 15 janvari 1994 main ve mumbai ucch nyaayalay kii mukhya nyaayadhish baniin. 21 april 1994 main unhen kerala ucch nyaayalay ke mukhya nyaayadhish ke rup main sthanantarit kiya gaya. 8 navambar 1994 ko ve supreme court kii nyaayadhish baniin or 27 agast 1999 ko is pad se sevanivruth hui.
