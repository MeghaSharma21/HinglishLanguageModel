 anjum chopra bharat kii rashtriya mahila cricket team kii sadsy or bhartiya mahila cricket team kii purv captain hai. 
 12 farvari 1995 ko newjiland ke christchurch main newjiland ke khilaaf unhone pehali baar ek divasiy antarrashtriya match khelaa. kuch maheenon baad yani 17-20 navambar 1995 ko unhone kolkata ke eden garden main england ke khilaaf test kariyar kii shuruyaat kii. ve vaaen haath ke ballebaaj or daaen haath ke madhyam tej gendbaaji karti hai. unhone 12 test or 116 ekadivasiy match khele hai. ve air india mahila cricket team, india red aurat team or bhartiya mahila cricket team kaa hisa rahi hai. unhone apana antim test match varsh 2006 main townton main 29 agast se 1 sitambar ke bich england ke khilaaf khelaa. unhone pichhlaa ek divasiy antarrashtriya match 16 march 2012 ko australia ke khilaaf mumbai main khelaa. apane lambey kariyar main anjum ne tii-20 match bhi kheli hai. pehla tii-20 match pehali baar unhone darbi main australia ke khilaaf khelaa or antim tii-20 match 23 march 2012 ko unhone australia ke khilaaf vishakhapatnam main khelaa.
