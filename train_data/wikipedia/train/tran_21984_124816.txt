 carbon tantu 5-10 micrometer kaa vyas rakhane vaale reshe hote hai jinka adhikansh bhaag carbon parmaanuon kaa bana hua ho. in resho kaa nirmaan carbon parmaanuon ko aise crystalon main jodakar ke hota he jo reshe ke lambe aksh se samanaantar vyavasthit hon. is panktiyojna se reshe ko ek bada bal-ghanfal anupaat mil jaataa he or yah apane aakaar ke liye bahut takat rakhata he. aise hazaron reshon ko lekar unhen sutron main piroyaa ja saktaa he or in sutron ko bunakar kapade-jaise tukdon kaa nirmaan bhi kara ja saktaa he.
 carbon tantu apni ucch sakhti, ucch tanaav pushti, kam bhaar, ucch rasinic dridhtaa, ucch taapmaana sahansheeltaa or kam tapiya prasar ke liye jane jate hai. isliye ve wantariksh, civil abhiyantriki, sainik kaaryon or res-ityadi main prayog gadiyon va cikalon main prayog hote hai. inaki ek kami yah he ki kaanch tantu or plastic tantu kii tulana main inhen bananaa adhik mahangaa hota he. isliye inhen aksar anya prakaar ke tantuon ke saath milakar banaaya jaataa he.
