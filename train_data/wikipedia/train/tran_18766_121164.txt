 allaudin bahaman shah jise allaudin hasan gangu bahaman shah or hasan kongu ke naam se bhi jaan jaataa he, mohammad bin tughlaq kii sena kaa ek subedaar tha jisane dakshin bharat ke pehle islamic rajya bahamani sultanate kii neenv rakhi thi. allaudin bahaman shah kaa janm ke samay kaa naam hasan tha. muslim itihaaskaar firishta ke anusaar apane jivan ke aarambh me vah dilli me ek gangu naamak brahamn kaa sevak tha. anya itihaaskaaro ne allaudin ko farsi shaasha bahaman kaa vanshaj bataaya he.
