 savitribai phule pune viapeeth pune me sthit ek vishvavidhyalay he, jo pune ke uttarpashchim main sthit he. yah bharat ke pramukh vishvavidhyalayon main se ek he. isaki sthapana 10 farvari, 1949 ko kii gai thi. 400 acre main faile is vishvavidhyalay mam 46 shaikshanik vibhaag hai.
 pune vishvavidhyalyaki sthapana pune vishvavidhyalay adhinium ke adheen kii gai thi, jise 10 farvari 1948 ko bombay vidhaan-mandal ne paarit kiya tha. usee varsh, da em aar jayakar ne vishvavidhyalay ke pratham upkulapati kaa padbhaar grahan kiya. shri bi ji khayr, jo bombay sarkaar ke mukhyamantri or shikshaamantri the, ne apane prayaason se vishvavidhyalay ko bada bhukhand dilaane main sahaayta kii. praarambhik 1950 main, vishvavidhyalay ko 411 acre bhoomi aavantit ki gai.
 prarambh main vishvavidhyalay kshetradhikaar pashchimi maharashtra ke 12 jilon main tha. lekin, 1964 main kolhapur main shivaji vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana ke baad, pune vishvavidhyalay kaa kshetradhikaar 5 jilon tak hi simit rah gaya, jo is prakaar hai: pune, ahmednagar, nasik, dhule or jalgaam. inamen se do jile - dhule or jalgaam- uttar maharashtra vishvavidhyalay se jude hai, jo ahast 1990 main sthapit ki gai thi.
