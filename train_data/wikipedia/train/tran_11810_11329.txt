 luteron gupha rajya park main america ke rajya oklahoma ke saath bhramit nahin hona karne ke liye. 
 daaku kii gupha ek nadi gupha gathan dehradun shahar se lagbhag 8 kimi he. gupha lagbhag 600 meter lanbaa or do mukhya bhaagon main vibhajit he. gupha kaa sabase ooncha jharnaa 10 meter kii oonchai se girtaa he. madhya bhaag main ek kile kii diwaar sanrachit he jo ab tut gaya he. yah ek prakritik gupha gathan he jahaan nadiyaan gupha ke andar se bahati he. jagah ab uttaranchal rajya dwaara anurakshit he.
