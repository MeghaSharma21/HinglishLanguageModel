 pandurang sadashiv sane marathi ke prasiddh lekhak, shikshak, saamaajik kaaryakarta evam bharat ke swatantraaa sangram senaanee the. ve sane guruuji ke naam se adhik prasiddh hai.
 pandurang sane ji kaa janm 24 disambar 1899 ko maharashtra ke ratnagiri janapad ke palgarh kasbe main hua tha. inke pitaaji kaa naam sadashiv sane tathaa mataji kaa naam yashodhabai sane tha.
 unke jivan me unako maa ki shiksha kaa bahut prabhaav mila. shiksha puri hone ke baad unhone amalner ke pratap hai skool me shikshak ke pad pe kaam kiya.pratap hai skool me chatravaas ki jimmedaari sambhaalate hue unhe bahut prasiddhi mili. unhone chatravaas me chatro ko khud ke jivan ke swavlamban kaa paath padhaaya.amalner me unhone tatvagyan mandir me tatvagyan ki shiksha lee.
 san 1928 me unhone ‘vidhyaarthee’ naam se masik ki shuruvat ki. un par mahatma gandhinji ke vichaaro kaa bahut prabhaav tha. wo khaadi ke kapado kaa upyog karte the.san 1930 me unhone shikshak ki naukari chod di. shikshak ki naukari chhodne ke baad unhone savinay kaayadebhang upkram me bhaag liya.
 sane guruji kaa jeevancharit anek lekhakon ne likhaa he. unamem se kuch pustakon evam unke lekhakon ke naam neeche diye gayi hai-
