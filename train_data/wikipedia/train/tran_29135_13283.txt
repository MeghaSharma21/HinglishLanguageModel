 ramswarup chaturvedi kaa janm 1931 i main kanpur main hua. chaturvedi ji kii aarmabhik shiksha unke paitruk gaanv kachpura main hui. chaturvedi ji ne bie christ carch kanpur se, eme kii oopaadhi elahabaad vishwa vidhyalay se 1952 main, diphil 1958 main tathaa dilit kii oopaadhi 1972 main praapt kii. aap 1954 se elahabaad vishwa vidhyalay main hindi vibhaag main praadhyaapak rahe. hindi nayi kavita ke pramukh kaviyon-jagadish gupt, ramswarup chaturvedi or vijayadevanrayan saahi main se ek hai.
