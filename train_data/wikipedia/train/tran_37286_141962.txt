  shri shivanand ashram jane maane shivanand ashram kii sthapana varsh 1932 main swami shivanand dwaara kii gai. ek sthaneey itihaaskaar shri badola ke anusaar muni kii reti swami shivanand kii tapobhumi thi, jo ek medical doctor the evam man kii aantarik shanti ke liye is kshetra main aae. varsh 1972 main jab shri shivanand ashram main narayan kutir ke agrabhag kii khudaai hui to puratathvik mahatv kii chijen pai gai jisane is ashram ko anterrashtriya khyaati mili.
 
 
 jo bhi log unke paas upachaar ke liye aae swami shivanand ne unka ilaaj kiya or aaj yah ashram keval sthaneey rogiyon kaa hi ilaaj nahin karata hai varan pashchim uttar pradesh tathaa sampoorn uttarakhand ke rogiyon kaa bhi ilaaj karata he. iske alaava ilaaj ke liye anyatra bheje jane vaale rogiyon kaa kharch bhi uthaata he. yah ashram garib chaatro ke pustakon kaa mulya tathaa kharche bhi uthaata he tathaa unhen chaatravruti ke alaava niraashriton ko rahane kii suvidha tathaa logon ke bich saamaanya chikitsa evam aankh ke aspataalon dwaara pahunchataa he. yah ashram kusht rog se grasit logon ko bheekh maangane se bachaane ke uddeshy se un rogiyon kii chikitsa tathaa brahrimpuri ke paas basaaye in rogiyon kii colony kii dekh bhi karata he.
 shahar ke bichobich sthit shivanand ashram aadhyatmik gyaan kaa shrot evam vedant tathaa yoga ke kendra ke rup main janaa jaataa he.
