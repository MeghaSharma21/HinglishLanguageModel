 steve rikson ek purv australian team ke khilaadi he jo apane khel jivan main mukhya rup se wicket-keeper ke liye or ballebaaji ke liye jane jate the. inkaa janm elbari, elbari, new south wales ,australia main 25 farvari 1954 main hua tha inhone australia cricket team ke liye 13 test or 06 vanade match khele the. inhone apane ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket kii shuruyaat 1978 ko kii thi jabaki test cricket kii shuruyaat 1977 main kii thi.
 australia ke is diggaj khilaadi ne apane ek divasiy antarrashtriya karier kii shuruyaat 22 farvari 1978 ko westeindies team ke khilaaf kii thi or inhone apana antim vanade match 29 march 1984 ko bhartiya team ke khilaaf khelaa tha. inhone apane vanade karier main kul 40 ran banaae the jabaki wicket keeping karte hue 9 kaich or 2 stump kiye the.
 steve rikson ne apane test cricket kii shuruyaat vanade cricket se purv 2 disambar 1977 ko bhartiya cricket team ke khilaaf kii thi. inhone apane test cricket karier main kul 13 match khele the jisamen inhone 364 ran banaae the or apane wicket keeping karte hue 42 kaich or 4 stamping kiye the. rikson ne apana antim test cricket match 30 disambar 1984 ko westeindies cricket team ke khilaaf khelaa tha.
