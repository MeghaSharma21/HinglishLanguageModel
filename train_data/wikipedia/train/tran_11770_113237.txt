 san 2000 main zanzibar main 207 sarkari skool or 118 nijee skool the. yahan do vishvavidhyalay or ek college bhi hai: zanzibar uniwarsity, state uniwarsity of zanzibar or chukvani college of ajukeshan.
 suja kii sthapana 1999 main hui he or yah stone toun main purv kiswahili and fahrren language santhaan kii imaaraton main sthit he. yah zanzibar kaa ekmaatr saarvajanik ucch shiksha santhaan he, anya do santhaan nijee hai. 2004 main, teenon sansthaanon main kul 948 chaatr-chaatraon kaa namakan hua tha jinamein se 207 mahilaayein thim.
 zanzibar main praathmik or madhyamik shiksha pranali tanzanian mukhya bhu-bhaag kii shiksha pranali se thodi alag he. mukhya bhu-bhaag main keval saat saal tak praathmik shiksha anivaary he jabaki zanzibar main atirikt teen saal madhyamik shiksha anivaary or muft he. mukhya bhu-bhaag ke chaatro kii tulana main zanzibar ke chaatr padhai or ganit ke manakikrut parikshaon main kaaphi kam ank praapt karte hai.
 1970, 1980 or 1990 ke dashakon main madhyamik shiksha ke baad rashtriya seva jaruri thi lekin ab yah svaichhik ban gai he or keval kuch chaatr hi swechhapurvak rashtriya seva main bhaag lete hai. zyaadaatar chaatr rojgaar kii talash karne yaa adhyaaapak ke collejon main bhaag lene kaa vikalp chunate hai.
 zanzibar main 1,600 kimi tak sadakon kaa jaal bicha hua he jisamen se 85% pakki yaa arddh-pakki sadaken hai. shesh kachi sadaken hai jinhen varsh bhar gamaneey banane ke liye har varsh unhen unki purv dasha main laayaa jaataa he. zanzibar main philhal sarkaar ke swaamitv vaali koi saarvajanik parivahan vyavastha nahin he lekin daladala ekmaatr saarvajanik parivahan he jis par nijee maalikon kaa swaamitv he; daladala shabd kii utpatti 1970 or 1980 ke dashakon ke dauraan swahili shabd daalaa yaa paanch shilling se hui he.
 zanzibar main ab ek behatar or sampann samudri parivahan network he jisase saarvajanik swaamitv vaali jahaaj or nijee speed boat zanzibar ke bandargahon par chalate hai jise europi sangh kii madad se punarnirmit kiya gaya tha. zanzibar or pemba ke dwipon main paanch bandargah hai. zanzibar port korporation ek saarvjaanik ikai he jise bandargahon ke sanchaalan or vikaas kii purna swytta praapt he. mukhya samudri bandargah ke ghaaton ko europea sangh kii vittiya sahaayta se 1989-1991 main nirmit kiya gaya tha. yah bandargah zanzibar ke 90% se adhik vyapaar ko sambhalata he. 1925 main ek maamuli halke bandargah ke rup main malindi bandargah kaa nirmaan kiya gaya.
 buniyadi suvidhaon kii drushti se bandargah kii haalat kharaab he or iske saath hi saath sanchaalan kshetra or bhandaran suvidhaayen kaaphi simit hai. 1995 or 2001 ke darmyaan malindi bandargah kii dasha se sambandhit kai moolyankan kiye gaye. lekin phir bhi koi marammat kaary na kiye jane ke parinamswaroop ghaaton kii haalat or bigadati ja rahi he. mukhya bandargah ke ghat kii haalat itani kharaab ho gai he ki isaki ab marammat bhi nahin kii ja sakati.
 sabase haal kii durghatana may 2009 main ghati jab ek malwahi jahaaj daar-es salaam ke liye ravaanaa hone se pehle dub gaya. abhi bhi spasht rup se yah nahin maaloom ho paaya he ki isamen kitne logon ko apni jaan se haath dhonaa padaa tha or ab tak durghatana ke kaaran kaa bhi pataa nahin lagaaya ja sakaa he. jahaaj ko nikaalane main ek saptaah se bhi zyada samay laga tha. zanzibar duniya ke baaki hisson se kaaphi acchhee tarah se judaa he. zanzibar kaa mukhya hawaii adda, zanzibar antarrashtriya hawaii adda, ab bade vimaanon ko bhi sanbhal saktaa he jiske parinamswaroop yatriyon or saamaanon kii avaagaman main kaaphi vruddhi hui he.
 zanzibar ke urja kshetra main avishwasaneey vidyut shakti, petroleum or petroleum utpaada shaamil he; kami ko jalaavan or isse sambandhit utpaadon se puraa kiya jaataa he. gharelu or audyogic prayojanon ke liye shaayad hi kabhi koyale or gas kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he. zanzibar ko apni vidyut shakti jaruraton kaa 70 pratishat ek pandubbi cabel ke madhyam se mukhya bhu-bhaag tanzania se praapt hota he or shesh urja kaa utpaadan taap vidyut ke madhyam se kiya jaataa he. zanzibar kii krantikari sarkaar or norge rajya kii sarkaar ne agast 2008 main ek samjhaute par hastkshar kiya jiske tahat norge tanga-pemba sab si cabel project ke liye paise dene ke liye raji ho gaya jisase pemba dweep ko tanga kshetra se naishanal grid se bijli praapt ho paaegi; 40 megawatt ke samudri cabel ko bichaane kaa kaam disambar 2009 main shuru hua. utpann 70 se 75 pratishat bijli kaa istemaal gharelu kaamon main kiya jaataa he jabaki 20 pratishat se bhi kam bijli kaa istemaal audyogic str par kiya jaataa he. zyaadaatar gramin or shahari kshetron main khaanaa pakaane or raushnee karne ke liye urja ke strot ke rup main vyapak taur par eendhan kii lakdi, koyala or mitti ke tel kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he. petroleum, gas, tel, mitti ke tel or idio kii khapat kshamta varsh dar varsh badhati ja rahi he, jahaan 1997 main kul 5,650 ton kii khapat hui thi vahiin 1999 main 7,500 ton se zyada khapat hui thi.zanzibar ko 10 disambar 2009 se 23 march 2010 tak ek baar phir se ek bahut bade andhere kaa saamana karnaa padaa or tanzania dweep ke urja mantri kaa kahana he ki yah baat abhi bhi spasht nahin ho pai he ki is samasya se kab chhutkaaraa milegaa. 21 may se 19 june 2008 tak ise pehali baar ek bade andhere kaa saamana karnaa padaa tha jahaan dweepavasiyon ko bijli rahit dasha main rahanaa padaa tha or bijli utpaadan ke liye puri tarah se vaikalpik tareekon par nirbhar rahanaa padaa tha. jis mukhya bhu-bhaag par isse sambandhit galati hui thi, vahaan ise usee samay purv dasha main laane kaa prabandh kar diya gaya tha.
 zanzibar ke sthaneey log ek mishrit jaateey prishthbhumiyon se sambandh rakhte hai jo is kshetra ke rangeen itihaas kaa ek sanket he. zanzibar ke log swahili bolte hai jo purv africa main bade paimaane par boli jaani vaali bhashaa he. kai logon kaa vishwaas he ki iske sabase shuddh rup ko zanzibar main bolaa jaataa he kyonki yah is bhashaa kaa janmasthan he. kai sthaneey log angrezi bhi bolte hai.
 zanzibar kaa sabase mashhoor kaarykram zanzibar antarrashtriya film samaaroh he jise festival of daau countries ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. har varsh july ke maheene main is kaarykram main zanzibar ke pasandida sangeet, tarab sahit swahili coast ke behtareen kalatmak drishyon kaa bhi pradarshan kiya jaataa he.
 stone toun kii mahatvapurn vaastu visheshtaayen livingstone house, zanzibar kaa purana aushadhaalay, gilaani brij, ngom kongwe or house of vanders hai. barghaash bin said ke shashan kaal main iran ke shiraj ke apravasiyon dwaara nirmit hamamni percian baths kidichi nagar kii mukhya visheshta he.
 zanzibar purvi africa deshon main ekmaatr aisa sthaan bhi he jahaan sabase lambey awasaiya bhawan hai jinhen aupchaarik taur par michenzani flats ke naam se janaa jaataa he jise zanzibar main awasaiya samasya ko dur karne ke liye 1970 ke dashak main purv german kii sahaayta se banwaaya gaya tha.
 zanzibar africa kaa pehla aisa kshetra tha jahaan sabase pehle 1973 main rangeen televijan kaa istemaal shuru hua. mukhya bhu-bhaag tanzania par pehali televijan seva iske lagbhag bees saal baad tak chaalu nahin hui thi lekin pradaan kii jane vaali kharaab seva or aadhunik utpaadan upkarnon ke saath-saath anbhavi karmchaariyon ke abhaav kii vajah se africa deshon main vartmaan main iskaa sthaan kaaphi neeche he. vartmaan tivi stesan ko tivijed kaha jaataa he. yahan lagbhag 8 nijee radio stesan hai.
 1980 or 1990 ke dashakon ke dauraan tivijed ke mashhoor samvaadadaata swargavaasi alvia alaavi, nima musa, sharifa moulid, phatama mazi, zayanab ali, ramaadhan ali or khamis faki the.
 sanchaar ke sandarbh main, zanzibar main nav punargathit saarvajanik doorsanchaar company or chaar nijee swaamitv vaali mobile pranaaliyon kii acchhee seva uplabdh he. in pranaaliyon ke madhyam se puure zanzibar ko vyapak rup se cover kiya jaataa he or duniya ke zyaadaatar hisson se jod diya gaya he.
 1999 ke baad se mukhya bhu-bhaag main apane mukhya mukhyalayon ke sthanantarit hone tak jantel ke naam se mashhoor zanzibar telecommunikeshio pehali or ekmaatr zanzibar aadhaarit dur sanchaar company thi. mukhya bhu-bhaag tanzania main seva pradaan karne vaali lagbhag sabhi mobile or internet companiyaan zanzibar main maujuud hai.
 zanzibar footbal association kii dekharekh main khelaa jane vaala association footbal zanzibar kaa sabase lokapriy khel he. zanzibar confederation of african footbal kaa ek sahyogi sadsy he. iskaa matlab he ki zanzibar rashtriya footbal team african neshans cap jaisi rashtriya sieef pratiyogitaaon main pravesh paane ke yogya nahin he lekin zanzibar ke footbal klbon ko sieef confederation cap or sieef champiens lig main pratinidhitiv praapt hota he.
 rashtriya team gaiyr-fifa antarrashtriya pratiyogitaaon jaise fifi wild cap or lef cap main bhaag leti he. chunki zanzibar fifa kaa ek sadsy nahin he, isliye unki team world cap ke yogya nahin he.
 sheersh klbon ke liye zanzibar footbal association kii ek premier lig bhi he jiskaa nirmaan 1981 main kiya gaya tha.
 1992 ke baad se zanzibar main judo kaa khel bhi khelaa jaataa he. sansthaapak shri tsuyoshi shimaoka ne ek majboot team kii sthapana kii thi jo rashtriya or antarrashtriya pratiyogitaaon main bhaag leti he. 1999 main zanzibar judo association ko panjikrut kiya gaya or yah tanzania olympic kamity kaa ek sakriy sadsy ban gaya.
