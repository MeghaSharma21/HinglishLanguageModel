 british east india company kii sthapana 31 disambar 1600 isvi main hui thi. ise yadakada jon company ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa tha. ise britain kii maharani ne bharat ke saath vyapaar karne ke liye 21 saalo tak kii chhot de di. baad main company ne bharat ke lagbhag sabhi kshetron par apana sainik tathaa prashasnic adhipaty jamaa liya. 1858 main iskaa vilay ho gaya.
