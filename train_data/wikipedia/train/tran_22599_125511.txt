 pashchimi bharat sanadi lekhaakaar vidhyaarthee sangh, thane, "vikasa, thane" ke rup main sankshepaksharit, bhartiya sanadi lekhaakaar santhaan ke pashchimi bharat kshetreey parishad ke thane shahar ke adhyay kaa vidhyaarthee ang hai. is sangh kaa mukhya sanghathan, pashchimi bharat sanadi lekhaakaar vidhyaarthee sangh hai, jo purna pashchimi bharat ke sanadi lekhaakaar ke vidhyaarthiyon kaa sangh hai. 
 thane vikasa ek saat-sadasyeey samiti hai, jisamen adhyaksh ek sie hote hai or baaki chah sadsy sie abhyaskram ke vidhyaarthee hote hai. vittiya varsh 2016-17 kii samiti nimna prakaar hai -
