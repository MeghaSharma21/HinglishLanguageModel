 kal mado kshetra kaa ek nazaaraa
 kal mado yaa al-mado purvottaree somalia main sthit ek parvatamala he. haalanki somalia ek shushk desh he, is pahari kshetra main 700-800 meter kii oonchaai par ek failaa hua jangal he, jahaan varsha or dhundh se paudhon ko pani miltaa rahata he. yahan ke ghaney jangal main kai pashu-pakshi bhi apana ghar banaate hai.
