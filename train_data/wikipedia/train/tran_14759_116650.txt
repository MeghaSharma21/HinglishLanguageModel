 vistaarit pradarshan pahchaan data  kisi digital disple dwaara apni kshamtaaon kaa pradarshan kisi video strot ko karne ke liye pradaan kii jane vaali data sanrachana he. yahi ek aadhunik personal computer ko yah jaanane kii kshamta pradaan karata he ki usamen kis prakaar kaa monitor joda gaya he. idiaidi ko video electronics standrds associyeshan dwaara prakashit ek maanak ke dwaara paribhaashit kiya jaataa he. idiaidi main utpaadak kaa naam or serial number, utpaada kaa prakaar, fosfar yaa filter kaa prakaar, disple dwaara samarthit timming, disple kaa aakaar, dipti sambandhi data or pixel masping data shaamil hota he.
 idiaidi sanrachanaayen sanskaran v1.0 se v1.4 tak kii shrenee main hoti hai; ye sabhi 128 bite sanrachanaon main ucchtar sangatta ko paribhaashit karti hai. idiaidi sanrachana v2.0 ke dwaara ek nai 256-bite sanrachana paribhaashit kii gai, lekin baad main ise anupyukt maanakar hataa diya gaya or v1.3 ne iskaa sthaan le liya. HDMI 1.0-1.3c dwaara EDID sanrachana v1.3c prayog kiya jaataa he.
 DisplayID ek maanak he, jiskaa lakshya idiaidi or i-idiaidi vistaaranon ko kisi aise ekal praaroop dwaara pratisthapit karnaa he, jo pisi monitor va upabhokta electronics upkarnon ke liye upyukt ho.
 idiaidi ko disple se graphics card tak sanchaarit karne ke liye channel saamanytah I²C bas hoti he, jise didisi2bi main paribhaashit kiya gaya he .
 didisi or idiaidi ko paribhaashit kiye jane se purv, kisi graphics card ke paas yah jaanane kaa koi maanak tarika nahin tha ki use kis prakaar ke disple upkaran ke saath joda gaya he. personal computaron main lage kuch vijie conectors ek, do yaa teen pinon ko ground ke saath jodakar pahchaan kaa ek buniyadi rup pradaan kiya karte the, lekin yah koding manakikrut nahin kii gai thi.
 idiaidi ko aksar serial prom  yaa iiprom  naamak ek memory upkaran main monitor main sanchit kiya jaataa he or yah adress 0x50 par I²C bas ke madhyam se abhigamya hota he. idiaidi prom ko aksar host pisi dwaara padhaa ja saktaa he, chaahe swatah disple band kiya hua ho yaa nahin.
 anek software package idiaidi soochna ko pard sakate hai va pradarshit kar sakate hai, jaise microsoft vindos ke liye read-idiaidi or powerstrip tathaa linaks va biesadi unics ke liye eksfri86  hone par idiaidi ko log par output karegaa). linaks main, yadi aap "xrandr--verbose" ko kriyanvit karen, to aap aprishkrut idiaidi ko hexadesimal praaroop main bhi dekh sakate hai. mike oes eks mul rup se idiaidi soochna ko padhata he or swicharesex yaa displeconfigex jaise program soochna ko pradarshit karte hai or saath hi vishisht rizolutionon ko paribhaashit karne ke liye iskaa prayog karte hai.
 anhanced idiaidi bhi usee samay prastut kiya gaya tha, jab i-didisi ko prastut kiya gaya; isane idiaidi sanrachana 1.3 prastut kii, jo anek vistaaran khandon kaa samarthan karti he or isane idiaidi sanskaran 2.0 sanrachana ko avamaanit kar diya . i-idiaidi main adhimaanya samyaavadhiyon, shrenee simaaon, monitor ke naam ke liye data fields kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. i-idiaidi dohari jitief samyaavadhiyon evam abhimukhataa anupaat parivartan ke liye bhi samarthan pradaan karti he.
 vistaaranon ke prayog ke saath, i-idiaidi string kii lambaai 32 kilobites tak ho sakati he.
 aitihaasik rup se, idiaidi ke saath kuch graphics card drivers kaa pradarshan bura rahaa he, ve iske vistrut samyavadhi nirupakon  ke bajaay maanak samyavadhi nirupakon kaa hi prayog karte hai. yahaan tak ki jin maamalon main ditidi ko padhaa gaya, unamem bhi drivers aksar is maanak samyavadhi nirupak seemaa dwaara simit hai/the ki kshaitij/urdhv vibhedan 8 se purnatah vibhajya hone chahie. iskaa arth yah he ki anek graphics card chaudee screen vaale adhikansh aam flats panel disple or liquid crystal disple televijan ke mul vibhednon ko vyakt nahin kar sakate. urdhv pickselon kii sankhya kii gananaa kshaitij vibhedan tathaa chaynit abhimukhataa anupaat ke dwaara kii jaati he. atah purnatah abhivyakti-yogya hone ke liye, chaudee screen vaale disple kaa aakaar 16x9 pickselon kaa ek gunaj hona chaiye. 1366x768 pixel chaude eksgie panel ke liye, idiaidi maanak samyavadhi nirupak vaakyavinyas main abhivyakti-yogya nikatatam vibhedan 1360x765 pixel he, jo vishisht rup se 3 pixel patale kaale stambhon ke rup main miltaa he. screen kii chaudaai ke rup main 1368 pixel nirdisht karne par 769.5 pixel kii ek aswabhaavik screen oonchai praapt hoti he.
 anek chaude eksgie panel maanak samyavadhi nirupakon main apane mul vibhednon ko vigyaapit nahin karte, iske bajaay keval 1280x768 kaa vibhedan hi prastaavit karte hai. kuch panel mul vibhedan se keval kuch hi kam vibhedan ko vigyaapit karte hai, jaise 1360x765. in panelon dwaara ek pixel sateek chitra pradarshit karne main sakasham ho paane ke liye, disple driver dwaara idiaidi data ko upekshit kiya janaa chaiye athwa driver ko ditidi kii sahi vyakhya kerani chaiye or vah un vibhednon ke nivaaran main sakasham hona chaiye, jo 8 dwaara vibhajya na hon. idiaidi data se maanak samyavadhi nirupakon kaa adhirohan karne ke liye vishesh program uplabdh hai; microsoft vindos ke liye powerstrip or mike oes eks ke liye swicharesex . haalanki yah bhi sadaiv sambhav nahin hota kyonki apni pasand ke anusaar vibhednon ko kriyanvit karne ke liye kuch vikritaon ke graphics drivers ke liye vishisht registry hack kii aavashyaktaa hoti he, jiske kaaran screen ke mul vibhedan kaa prayog karnaa bahut kathin ban saktaa he.

 sanskaran 2 main paribhaashit he) ne anek ditidi ko "mul " ke rup main namit karne kii kshamta jodi or is baat ke liye kuch "buniyadi khoj" kaaryaatmakataa shaamil kii ki kya pradarshan upkaran main "buniyadi audio", vysibisiar pixel praroopon, tathaa anderscan ke liye samarthan uplabdh he.
 sanskaran 3 ke anusaar, uplabdh ditivi praroopon kii samyavadhi nirdisht karan ke do bhinn tareeke hai: 18-bite vaale vistrut samyavadhi nirupakon ke prayog ke madhyam se, jaisaa ki sanskaran 1 va 2 main hota he, tathaa sankshipt video nirupakon ke prayog ke madhyam se. echdiemai 1.0-1.3si is sanskaran kaa upyog karata he.
