 2017-18 bangladesh tri-raashtra shrinkhalaa ek aagaamee cricket toornament he jise janvari 2018 main hona he. yah bangladesh, srilanka or zimbabwe ke bich ek trikoniya shrinkhalaa hogi, jisamen sabhi maichon ek divasiy antarrashtriya ke rup main khele gaye the. sher-e-bangla rashtriya cricket stadium sabhi match kii mejabani karegaa, dopaher se shuru hone vaale prayek match ke saath.
 trikoniya shrinkhalaa ke baad, srilanka bangladesh ke khilaaf do test match or do twanti-20 antarrashtriya khelenge. srilanka-zimbabwe kaa khel 17 janvari ko shere bangla neshnal stadium main yojit 100 wein vanade hoga or stadium 100 vanade kii mejabani karne ke liye chathe sthaan par hoga.
