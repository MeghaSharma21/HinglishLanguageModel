 uttarkand valmiki krit ramayana or goswami tulsidas krit shri ram charit maanas kaa ek bhaag he.
 uttarkand ram katha kaa upsanhaar he. sita, lakshmana or samast vanar sena ke saath ram ayodhya waapas pahunche. ram kaa bhavy swaagat hua, bharat ke saath sarvajanon main anand vyaapt ho gaya. vedon or shiv kii stuti ke saath ram kaa rajbhishek hua. vaanron kii vidaai di gai. ram ne praja ko upadesh diya or praja ne kritagyata prakat kii. chaaron bhaaiyon ke do do putr huye. raamarajy ek adarsh ban gaya.
 vishesh:- mul valmiki ramayana main uttar kand nahin he. keval yuddh kand sahit chah kand hai. uttar kand ko swaarthvash joda gaya he.
