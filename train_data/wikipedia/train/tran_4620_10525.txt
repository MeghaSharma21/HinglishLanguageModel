 arjuna vaajapeyi ek bhartiya parvatarohi hai. unhone 23 may, 2016 ko vishwa kii paanchavi sabase oonchi chotii mount makaalu par tirangaa fahraane main safalataa praapt kii. is chotii par chadhane vaale sabase kam umr ke parvatarohi hai. isse pehle ve vishwa kii sabase oonchi chotii mount everest, vishwa kii chauthi sabase oonchi chotii lhotse va vishwa kii aathvin sabase oonchi chotii manasalu par chadh chuke hai. iske saath hi ve vishwa ke pehle aise parvatarohi ban gaye hai, jinhone sabase kam umr main 8,000 meter se oonchi chaar chotiyon par chadhaai kii he.
