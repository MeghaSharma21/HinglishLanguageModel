 trutip aipal or microsoft dwaara 1980 main viksit kiya gaya 'outline font' he jo postscript main prayukt adob ke tip 1 font se spardhaa karne ke liye viksit kiya gaya tha. vartmaan samay main yah mike oes tathaa microsoft vindose donon par sarvaadhik prayukt hone vaala font format ban gaya he. 
 multah tru tip kii sabase badi shakti yah he ki alag-alag sies kaa hone par font kaisaa dikhegaa, is par font diziner kaa purna niyantran hota he.kintu aajkal anekanek renderingg takneekon ke kaaran yah sunishchit nahin rah gaya he ki sabhi main yah gun vidyaman rahegaa.
