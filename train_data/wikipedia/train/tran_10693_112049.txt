 amarinder singh gil ek punjabi gayak, sangeetkaar va sangeet nirmaataa he. unhone apana gayak ke rup main pradarshan 2000 main aaye ek album apni jaan ke se kiya tha. ve har samay ek safal punjabi abhinayta rahe he.
 gil boorchand, amritsar, punjab se he. unhone khalsa college, amritsar main adhyayan kar krishi vigyaan main master kii degree praapt kii. ve guru nanak dev vishvavidhyalay ke ek purv chaatr he. college ke dauraan ve sarbajit cheema jaise vyaktitv ke liye ek samarthan kalakaar ke rup main bhangada nrity pradarshan kiya karte the. apane gayan karier shuru karne se pehle amarinder singh ek prabandhak ke rup main firozpur kendriya sahakaari bank main kaam kiya karte the.
 unhone jalandhar doordarshan kaarykram kaala doriya ke liye apana pehla geet darj kiya. gil ko safalataa trek "paigaam" se mili or ve surkhiyon main aaye. unke dwaara gaaye hit gaanon main "daaru", "madania" or "khedan de deen" shaamil he. sunidhi chouhan ke saath, or kai anya duusare kalakaaron ke saath "mail kara de" or "dildaaria" jaise gane record kiye. unka album juda us samay kaa sabase safal punjabi album main se ek mana jaataa he. 2012 main juda sarvshereshth album ke liye brit asia sangeet puraskaar praapt kiya. juda ke madhya 2014 main safalataa ke baad amarinder gil ek uttarkatha juda 2 ke saath aaye or adhik safalataa praapt kii. juda 2 punjab main ek super hit film ban gayi.angrej kii ek film thi jo us samay kii sabhi filmo main sabase vyavsaayik rup se safal punjabi filmon main se ek ban gai.
