 bajiprabhu deshpandey ek nami vir the. maraathon ke itihaas main unka mahatvapurn sthaan he.
 baaji ke pitaaji, hirds, mawal ke desh kulkarni the. baaji kii veerta kii dekhakar hi maharaj shivaji ne unako apni yusena main ucchapad par rakhaa. i.sa. 1648 se 1649 tak unhone shivaji ke saath rahakar purandar, kondana or rajapur ke kile jeetne main bharsak madad kii. baaji prabhu ne rohida kile ko majboot kiya or aasapaas ke kilon ko bhi sudrudh kiya. isse vir baaji ko maawalon kaa jabardast kaaryakarta samajha jane laga. is praant main uskaa prabhutv ho gaya or log uskaa sammaan karne lage. i. san 1655 main jawali ke morche main or iske baad dedh do varshon main mawala ke kile ko jeetne main tathaa kilon kii marammat karne main baaji ne khub parishram kiya. i. san 1659 ke november kii das taarikh ko afajal khan kii mrityu hone ke baad paar naamak van main adilshahi chhavnee kaa naash bhi baaji ne bade kaushal se kiya or swarajya kaa vistaar karne main shivaji kii sahaayta kii. i. san 1660 main mughal, adilshaah or siddiki ityadi ne shivaji ko chaaron taraf se gherne kaa praytn kiya. panhala kila se nikal bhaagna shivaji ke liye atyant kathin ho gaya. is samay bajiprabhu ne unki sahaayta kii. shivaji ko aadhi sena dekar swayan baaji ghod kii ghati ke daravaaje main data rahaa. teen chaar ghanton tak ghanghor yuddh hua. baaji prabhu ne badi veerta dikhaai. uskaa bada bhai phulaji is yuddh main maaraa gaya. bahut si sena bhi maari gai. ghayal hokar bhi baaji apni sena ko protsaahit karata rahaa. jab shivaji rogana pahunche to unhone top kii aawaaj se baaji prabhu ko garh main apane sakushal pravesh kii soochna di. top kii aawaaj sunakar swami ke kartavya ko puraa karne ke saath 14 july 1660 i. ko is mahaan vir ne mrityu kii god main sadaa ke liye sharan lee.
