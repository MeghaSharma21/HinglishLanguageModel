 daanveer bhamashah sammaan yah bharat main ek rajyastareey sammaan hai. yah sammaan mahaan deshbhakt or daanveer bhamashah ke naam par rakhaa gaya hai. bhartiya itihaas main mevadoddharak daanveer bhamashah kaa naam bade hi gaurav ke saath liya jaataa he. daanveer bhamashah sammaan shiksha ke chhetra main ucch sthaan praapt karne vaale vidhyaarthiyon ko pradaan kiya jaataa hai.
