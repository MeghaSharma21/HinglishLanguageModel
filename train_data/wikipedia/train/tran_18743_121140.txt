 kalan ke moolbhoot prameya ke do bhaag hai. inako kabhi-kabhi 'kalan kaa pratham moolbhoot prameya' evam 'kalan kaa dwitiya moolbhoot prameya' kaha jaataa hai. 
 is prameya kaa pratham bhaag pradarshit karata he ki anishchit samakal ko avkalan dwara ulataa ja saktaa he. 
 is prameya kaa doosraa bhaag kisi falan ke nishchit samakal nikaalane ke liye uske aseemit vyutkram-avkaljon ke upyog kii chhot detaa he.
