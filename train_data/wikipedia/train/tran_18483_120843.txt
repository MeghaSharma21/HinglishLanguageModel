 guda maithun ek prakaar kaa maithun hi he. is prakaar ke maithun main shishn, ungali, dildo yaa anya kisi vastu ko yoni kii bajaay guda main pravisht kiya jaataa he. duusare shabdon main kaha jaay to guda main ling daalkar sambhog karne kii prakriya ko hi guda maithun kehete hai..
 guda maithun ke vibhinn prakaar hai masalan guda kaa maukhik uksaav, uksaav ke liye ungliyon athwa anya vastuon kaa upyog. sarvapratham to guda sharir kaa hi ek bhaag he jo ksaa hua hota he or stri yoni kii bhanti swat: chikna nahin ho saktaa. isliye is ang main kuch bhi pravesh karaate samay is baat kaa vishesh dhyaan rakhana chahie ki is ang ki bhitari deevaaron ko koi kshati na pahunche kyonki guda kaa mukhya kaary mel tyaag karnaa he maithun karnaa yaa karwaanaa nahin.
 is prakaar ke maithun se purushon ko anand prostate ke uksaav se aata he jo guda ke atyadhik nikat paaya jaataa he. jabaki striyon ko anand isaliye aata he kyonki guda par bahut si tantrikaon kii samaapti hoti he.
 bhartiya shastron main kamasutra ke janak vatsyayan ne ise bhi sambhog kaa ek prakaar bataaya he. guda maithun kaa mahatv bhartiya samaaj main paashchaatya samaaj ke samaan nahin he. atah bhartiya joda guda maithun se purv iske bare main purna jaankaree tathaa sahi tareeke kaa adhyayan karen. sabase aavashyak baat guda maithun karte samay apane saathi ko purna uttejit kar len, tathaa chiknayee kaa vishesh dhyaan rakhen. guda maithun ko dard rahit banaakar hi iskaa purna anand liya ja saktaa he.par binaa gyaan ke guda maithun khatarnaak ho saktaa he guda maithun karte samay vibhinn saavdhaanee vartanee chaiye.jaise ki guda saaf ho mel kaa tyaag kar lenaa chaiye mahila kisi guda ke rog se grast naa ho.lekin bhartiya mahilaye guda maithun ko kam raji hoti he.
