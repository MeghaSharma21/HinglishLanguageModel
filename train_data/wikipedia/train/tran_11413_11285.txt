 pattee bharat ke uttar pradesh rajya ke pratapgarh jile kaa tehsil or vidhaan sabha kshetra he. yah multah avadh ke antargat aata he. pattee tehsil apane itihaas ke liye bhi janaa jaataa he.tehsil mukhyalay se lagbhag 5 kilometre duuri par mahadaha or damdama jaise madhya pashaan kaaleen sthal vidyaman he.
 vahiin mukhyalay ke sameep sthit rur gaanv se sarvapratham aitihaasik avadh kisaan andolan kaa aarambh kiya gaya tha. bharat ke pratham pradhanmantri pandit jawahar laal nehru ne apane raajnneetik kariyar kii shuruyaat bhi yaheen se kii.
 yahan kaa mela bahut prasiddh he, is meley ko dekhne ke liye yahan har saal november maheene main hajaaron kii sankhya main log aate hai. mela shuru hone se purv yahan par lagbhag baarah dinon tak bhavy ramlila kaa aayojan kiya jaataa he.
 yatayaat kii drushti se yah kshetra bhalee bhaanti viksit he. yahan basen, motor cycle, cykil, jeep, car, rickshaw, trek, ityadi vaahan bahutayat chalate hai.
