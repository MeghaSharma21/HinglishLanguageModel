 umrao singh gurjar - san 1857 kii kranti ke ek nayak the.
 raav umravsingh gurjar kaa janm san 1832 main dadri ke nikat gram catahada main ] ke putr ke rup main hua tha.
 10 may ko meerut se 1857 kii jan-kranti kii shuruuaat kotwaal dhansingh gurjar dwaara ho chuki thi. rashtriya bhaavana se otprot bharat-putr umravsingh gurjar ne aasapaas ke gramino ko prerit kar 12 may 1857 ko sikandrabad tehsil par dhaava bol diya. vahaan ke khajaane ko apane adhikaar main kar liya. isaki soochna milte hi bulandashahar se city magistrate sainik bal sindrabad aa dhamka. 7 din tak krantikari saina angrej saina se tkkar leti rahi. ant main 19 may ko sashstra saina ke saamne krantikari vero ko hathiyaar daalane padey 46 logo ko bandi banaaya gaya. umravsingh gurjar batch nikle. is krantikari saina main gurjar samudaay kii mukhya bhoomika hone ke kaaran unhe british satta kaa coup bhaajan hona pada.
 umravsingh gurjar apane dal ke saath 21 may ko bulandashahar pahuche evam jila karagaar par ghava bolkar apane sabhi rajbandiyo ko chudaa liya. bulandashahar se angreji shashan samaapt hone ke bindu par tha lekin baahar se saina kii madad aa jane se yah sambhav nahin ho sakaa. hindon nadi ke tat par 30 va 31 may ko krantikari saina or angreji saina ke bich ek aitihaasik yuddh hua. 26 sitambar, 1857 ko kasana-surajpur ke bich umravsingh gurjar kii angreji saina se baari takkar hui. lekin dilli ke patan ke kaaran krantikari saina kaa utsaah bhang ho chukaa tha.
 baari jan haani ke baad krantikari sena ne paraajay sweekaar karali. umravsingh gurjar ko giraftaar kar liya gaya. bulandashahar main kaleam ke chauharaahe par hathi ke pair se kuchalavakar phaansi par lataka diya gaya.
