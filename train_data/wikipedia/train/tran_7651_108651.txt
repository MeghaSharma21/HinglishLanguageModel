 serial ata  hard drive ko computer se jodne kaa ek tarika hota he. yah pata kaa pratisthaapan he. sata ke aane se pehle pata ko ata yaa idii hi kehete the. sata kii sabase mahatvapurn visheshta jo ise pata se bhinn karti he wo he iske cabel. sata hard drive ko computer se jodne ke liye patale cabel kaa upyog karata he. pata abhi bhi sata se adhik upyog main laayaa jaataa he, kintu ab nae computaron main sata laga hua miltaa he. 2005 ke baad ke computaron main sata interfes lag kar aa rahaa he. pata main 'P' kaa arth he parelal or ise computaron main kai taron ke rup main dekha ja saktaa he jo riban cabel main ek hi disha kii ore samanaantar jaati hai or hard drive se judati hai.
 sata or pata main bahut si bhinnataayen hoti hai. saidhantik rup se sata pata se teevr hota he. yadhyapi jin atitivr gatiyon ke bare main companiyaan daawey karatin he, unki aavashyaktaa praayah kam hi padati he. anya laabhon main hai nai cabel kaa saral rakh-rakhaav. kuch drive ko computer ke chalate rahete hue hi computer se joda yaa alag kiya ja saktaa he, yani computer ko shat-down karne kii aavashyaktaa nahin hoti. ise hot sweaping kaha jaataa he. antat, kuch driven "netive cmand kuing" samarthit hai. 
 iskaa arth he ki drive kisi kaary ke kram ko punarvyavasthit kar sakati he jisase kii vah kaary teevr gati se ho. in donon ke alag jod or bhinn madraboard prakaar bhi hote hai. puraane pata vaale madraboard main sata kii drive nahin lag sakati he, na hi iskaa ulataa sambhav he. haa aise madraboard milte hai, jo pata va sata donon prakaar kii harddisk ko saport karte hai. vishesh taur par digaayan kiya gaya pi7pi55di premium apni tarah kaa aisa pehla madraboard he jo sata ke interphes ke saath 6 gigabide prati sacand kii raftaar se data antaran karaata he.
