 shafi mohammad burfat ; janm : 25 navambar, 1965), jiyai sindh muttahida mahaz ke sansthaapak adhyaksh hai. yah pakistan ke sindh praant kaa ek udaar rajnatik dal he jo pakistan se swatantra sindhudesh kaa samarthak he. shafi baraafaat 1988 se hi chhipakar rah rahe hai. samachar madhyamon ke anusaar shafi mohammad, afghanistan chale gaye hai or vahiin kabul main apana niyantran kendra sthapit kar liya he.
