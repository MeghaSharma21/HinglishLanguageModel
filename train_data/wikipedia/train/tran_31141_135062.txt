 khand aarekh yaa blaak aarekh kaa prayog kisi tantra kii rachana athwa kisi prakriya ke ucch-stareey drushyaatmak pradarshan ke liye kiya jaataa he. praudyogiki ke sansar main inkaa hardware dizin, saftware dizin, tathaa prakriyaaon ke pravaah aarekh ko pradarshit karne ke liye bahutayat se upyog kiya jaataa he.
 blaak aarekh banane ke liye pradhantah aayat or vrut aadi jyamitiya aakaaron kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. in akritiyon ko tir-yukt yaa teeraviheen rekhaon se joda jaataa he jo unke aapasi sambandhon yaa prakriya kii disha kaa bodh karaati he.
 blaak arekhon kaa uddeshy ucch-stareey sanrachana yaa pravaah ko bataanaa hota he jisase kii puri tasviir ek najar main dikhaayi padey tathaa aasaani se samaj main aa jaay. isiliye isamen nimna-stareey vistrut jaankaree nahin dikhaayi jaati. nimnastareey jaankaree dikhaane ke liye duusare prakaar ke aarekh prayog kiye jate hai ; jaise- yojanatmak aarekh or leout aarekh aadi.
 udhaaharan ke liye, parshv chitra main kisi cell-phone kaa blaak aarekh dikhaaya gaya he.
 blaak aarekh, andhra baksaa ke siddhanth par kaam karte hai. andhra-bakse ke andar kii vistrut jaankaree yaa to gyaat nahin hoti yaa jaanboojhkar uskaa zikr nahin kiya jaataa. aantarik jaankaree ke bajaay isamen yah bataaya jaataa he ki isamen andar kya jaataa he or vah kya banakar baahar nikalataa he
