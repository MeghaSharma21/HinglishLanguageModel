 keli patraisi muradabad jile kii sanbhal tehsil kaa ek gaanv hai jo ki ab sanbhal jile kii bhugolic seemaa main sthit he. bharat ke mahargister evam janaganana ayukta karyalayke aankadon ke anusaar san 2011 main hui janaganana ke mutabik is gaanv ke kul pariwaron kii sankhya 184 he. keli patraisi kii kul jansankhya 1068 he jisamen purushon kii sankhya 589 or mahilaon kii sankhya 479 he.
 bharat ke samvidhan or panchayat raaj adhinium ke tahat keli patraisi gaanv sthaneey prashasan gaanv ke pradhan ke tahat he. gram pradhan gaanv ke matadaataaon dwaara chunaa gaya gaya jan-pratinidhi he jo gram panchayat ke jariye gaanv ke sansadhano or vikaas kaaryon kii dekharekh karata he. gaanv ke pradhan ko panchayat raaj adhinium ke tahat simit kanoonee adhikaar bhi praapt hai.
