 jiolite sookshmrandhreey, aluminosilicket khanij hai jo adhishoshak ke rup main prayukt hote hai. yah naam sweden ke khanijashastri fredrick chronsted dwaara diya gaya tha jisane paaya ki material cilibite ko teji se garm karne par yah bahut adhik maatra main bhaap utpann karata he jo iske dwaara avashoshit jal ke kaaran he.
 is varg ke mukhya khanij nimnaankit hai :
 1 - enelasite Na Al 2 H20]
 2 - natrolite Na2 Al2 Si3 O10 2H20]
 3- stillbite 
 jaisaa inke rasayik sanghathan se vidit he, ye sabhi khanij garam karne par pani chhodte hai. is varg ke sabhi khanij aagney shilaon main vidyaman felspar tathgya aluminium vaale anya khanijon ke parivartan se banate hai.
 anellsite cubic samudaay main sphatit hota he. yah rangahin yaa shwet hota he. isaki kathorta 5-5.5 tathgya aapekshik ghanatv 2.25 he. natrolite ke manibh sooyee ke aakaar ke hote hai tathaa kaach ke samaan chamakate hai. isaki kathorta ekanat samudaay kaa khanij he. iskaa rang shwet yaa laal hota he. isaki kathorta 3.5 se 4 tathaa aapekshik ghanatv 2.1 se 2.2 tak he.
 in khanijon kaa mukhya upyog baari pani ko halkaa karne ke liye kiya jaataa he. bharatvarsh main in khanijon ke sundar manibh rajmahal kii pahadiyon main, kathiyawad main girnaar parvat par tathaa dakshin trep main milte hai.
