 ramakrishna mishan kii sthapana 1 may san 1897 ko ramakrishna paramhans ke param shishy swami vivekanand ne kii. iskaa mukhyalay kolkata ke nikat belud main he. ramakrishna mishan dusron kii seva or paroapkaar ko karm yoga maanata he jo ki hindu dharm kaa ek mahatvapurn siddhanth he. 
 ramakrishna mishan kaa dhyeyvakya he - aatmano moksharthan jagad hitay c ramakrishna mishan ko bharat sarkaar dwaara 1996 main dow ambedkar rashtriya puraskaar se or 1998 main gandhi shanti puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha.
