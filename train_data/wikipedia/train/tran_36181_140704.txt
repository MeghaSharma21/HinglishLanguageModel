 fred aar shapiro, kii kotesan ke yel pustak ke sampadak, pataa chalaa he ki 1952 main kahawat eni ro dwaara ek pustak main "murphys lau" kaha jaataa tha, ek anaam bhautik vigyaani ke hawaale se:
 unhone bataaya ki " murphys lau thermodynmix kaa chauthaa lau he" | 
 may 1951 meinne roe ne saidhantik bhautik vigyaani ke saath ek interviev main batay ki unhen ehsaas hua ki unka kaam, sexond lau of thermodynmix yaa murphys lau ek nishthur kaary he |waha ke abhilekh ne us vyagyanik ko pehchaanaa hoverd percy "bob" robertson |robertson ke kagajaat caltech abhilekhaagaar main hai; vahaan ek patr main robertson ro ek saakshaatkaar 1949 ke pehle teen maheenon ke bheetar pradaan karata he |
 naam " murphys lau " turant surakshit nahin tha | farvari 1951 main lee correy dwara ek kahani main ascharyajanak vigyaan katha main usee lau ko " rallys lau " aisa bataaya | parmaanu urja ayog ke adhyaksh louis straas ne chicago daili main 12 farvari 1955 ko bataaya ki " mujhe aashaa he ki yah strans 'lau ke rup main janaa jayega |" arthur bloch ne unke "murphys lau, and adar rizons vaay things go vrong" ke pehle kitaab main zorge i. nicols dwaara milen patr kaa ullekh kiya he jo jet propalashan leboretori ke gunvattta aashwaasan prabandhak he |
 
 oxford vishvavidhyalay ke richard Dawkins ke anusaar, murphys lau ek bakawaas he kyonki unhen nirjeev vastuon kii aavashyaktaa hoti he, unki khud kii ichchhaaon he, ek kii apni ichchhaaon ke anusaar pratikriya hoti he | unhone ek udhaaran diya | vimaan hameshaa aakash main rahete he parantu un pe tabhi gaur kiya jataa he jab vah samasya kaa kaaran ban jate he | anveshak unke pehale se hi bane vichaaro ki pushti karne ke liye saboot dhundhata he parantu vah ese saboot nahi dhundhata jo uske vichaaro ko vipareet karte he |.

 sangathit prapatr kii bhavishyavaanee karte he | atanu chatarji aupchaarik rup se ganitiya sandarbh main murphys lau ko bataate hue is vichaar kii jaanch kii. chatarji ne paaya he ki murphys lau ko kam se kam karrwai ke siddhant se galat sabit kiya ja saktaa he |
 lekhak nik tii spark dwaara likhit pustak ' a histre of murphys lau ' ke anusaar vibhinn pratibhaagiyon dwaara bhinn ke anusaar yah asambhav hua ki kisne sabase pehle murphys lau ,yah gadha kiya | yah muhavara tab gadha gaya tha jab murphy ke mutabik unke upkaran pradarshan karne main naakaam rahe or yah vartmaan rup main daali gai thi, sammelan press karne se pehle jo dow jon stapp, ek america dwaara die gaye vaayu sena ke karnal or 1950 ke dashak main flite sarjan dwaara hui thi | jab tak spark ne baat ki chhabin kii yah sangharshon ki soochna nahi thi |
 1948 se 1949 tak, stapp sena air field par anusandhan pariyojna MX981 adhyakshata main teji se mandi ke dauraan ji-balon ke liye maanav sahishnutaa pareekshan kiya tha | pareekshan ek rocket slage ant main hydrolic brake kii ek shrinkhalaa ke saath ek rail trek par muhim kaa istemaal kiya. praarambhik pareekshanon main ek humanoid krash pareekshan kaa istamaal hua, lekin baad main pareekshan us samay ek vaayu sena ke captain par, Stapp dwaara pradarshan kiya gaya tha | pareekshan ke dauraan, upkarno ki satikta maapne ke sandharbh main sawaal uthaayen gaye the jo stapp anubhav kar rahe the | edward murphi electronic tanaav bal uski teji se mandi ke dwaara un par lagaae gaye maapne ke liye Stapp ke dohan ke nirodhak akadan se judi gej kaa upyog kar prastaav rakhaa. murphi isi tarah ke anusandhan kaa samarthan karne ke liye ucch gati centriphyus kaa upyog kar ji balon ko utpann karne main lage hue the | murphi ke sahaayak dohan taar, or ek pareekshan chimpanze kaa upyog kar chalaa gaya tha |
 sensor ek shunya padhane pradaan kar rahe the; haalanki, yah spasht ho gaya tha ki ve galat tareeke se sthapit kiye gaye the, prayek samvedak ke saath peeche kii ore vird thi | yah vah samay tha jab niraash murphy ne apni ghoshnaa kii | nik spark dwaara kiye gaye ek saakshaatkaar main, jorge nicols, ek or ejeeniyar jo maujuud the, ne kaha ki ne asafal pareekshan ke peeche apane sahaayak ko doshi tehraaya or kaha," agar us aadmi ko jalati kerani he to vah karegaa | nicols 'khaataa he ki "murphys lau" team ke anya sadasyon ke bich baatcheet ke madhyam se aayaa he | dusron ko, edward murphi ke jivit betaa robert murphi sahit nicols 'khaataa inkaar karte hai, or unhone daawa kiya ki vastav main murphys lau edward murphi ke saath aarambh hua tha | robert murphi ke khate ke anusaar, unke pita kaa bayaan tha, "agar koi kaam ek se adhik tarah se kiya ja saktaa he or agr vah saare raste aapado main parinaam honge, to vah usee tarah se kaam karenge |"
 is muhavare ko janata kaa dhyaan ek samvaadadaata sammelan main mila jisame stapp se puchha gay tha ki kaise koi rocket slage pareekshan ke dauraan ghayal nahi hua | sttapp ne kaha ki vah hameshaa murphys lau ko lekar vichaaradhin the or unhone kaha ki pareekshan karne se pehle sabhi sambhaavnaaon ko dhyaan main rakhana chahie or uske baad ki unpar kaary karnaa chahie |is prakaar stapp ke upyog or murphi kii kathith upyog drushtikon or vyavhaar main bahut antar he | hill or nicols kaa maananaa tha ki murphi divaais ke aarambhik asafalataa ke liye jimmedaari lene ke liye taiyaar nahin the |
 vyapak anusandhan ke baavjood, murphys lau ke rup main kah ke pralekhan kaa koi nishaan 1951 se pehle paaya nahi gaya he.agle prashansa patr 1955 tak nahin mila rahe hai, jab vimaanan yantriki bulatin ke may-june ank line shaamil "murphi kii vidhi: "agar ek vimaan kaa hissaa galat tareeke se sthapit kiya ja saktaa he, to koi naa koi use us tareeke se sthapit karegaa," or 1962 main budh antariksh yatriyon ne america nausena ke prashikshan filmon ke liye murphys lau ko jimmedaar tehraaya..
 apni aarambhik saarvajanik ghoshnaa se, murphys lau jaldi vibhinn takneekee airospes ejineering se jude samskritiyon main fail gaya.
