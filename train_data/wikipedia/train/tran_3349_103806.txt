 mahamad tritiy 1595 se apni maut tak osmania samrajya ke sultan the. ve apane pita muraad tritiy kii jagah sultani takht par asin hue.
 osmania samrajya main takht par asin hone ke saath hi "bhratruvadh" kii vivadaspad parampara ke aagaaz sultan mahamad faatih ke daur main hua or aahista-aahista yah parampara jor pakadati gai. isaki buniyadi vajah nae sultan ke liye baghaavat ke khataron ko kam karnaa tha or mahamad tritiy takht par asin hone ke turant baad bhratruvadh ke is silsile main, ne unke 27 bhaiyon kaa vadh kiya. iske saath-saath unhone apni bees se zyaada bahanon ko bhi vadh kiya. ve shashan karne main koi ruchi nahin rakhte the or tamaam rajnatik adhikaar unki maan safia sultan ke haathon main the. inke daur kii mahatvapurn ghatana hungary main austria or osmanion ke darmiyaan yuddh tha jo 1596 se 1605 tak jari rahaa tha. yuddh main osmanion kii paraajay ke kaaran se sultan ko sena kii aguwai khud sanbhaalani padi or ve suleman pratham ke baad yudhakshetra main utarane vaale pehle osmani shaasha the. inaki sena ne 1596 main ager par vijay praapt kii or kerrestzes kii ladaai main habsburg or tronsylvania kii senaaon ko haraa di. agle saal sultan ke vaidyon ne unki bigadati sehat kii vajah se unako yudhakshetra main utarane se manaa kar diya.
