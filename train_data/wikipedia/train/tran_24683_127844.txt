 badabharati bihar rajya ke muzaffarpur jile ke minapur prakhand main gaanv he. iske uttar main budhigandak nadi env ghasout gaanv, dakshin main badahasan gaanv, bhelaipur gaanv, purv main madhuban kaanti gaanv or pashchim main bhi ghasout he. badabharati gaanv ke naam se panchayat he, jis ke antargat badabharati, madhuban kaanti, badahasan, bajramuria, bhelaipur, pakhanaha or jitwaar gaanv shaamil hai.
