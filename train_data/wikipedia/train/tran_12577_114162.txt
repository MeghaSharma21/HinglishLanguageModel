 shaaligram ek prakaar kaa jeevashm pathar he, jiskaa prayog parameshwar ke pratinidhi ke rup main bhagwaan kaa aahvaan karne ke liye kiya jaataa he. shaaligram aamtaur par pavitra nadi kii tali yaa kinaaron se ekatra kiya jaataa he. shiv bhakt puja karne ke liye shiv ling ke rup main lagbhag gol yaa andakaar shaligram kaa upyog karte hai.
 vaishnav pavitra nadi gandaki main paaya jane vaala ek golakaar, aamtaur par kaale rang ke amonoid jeevashm ko vishnu ke pratinidhi ke rup main upyog karte hai.
 shaaligram ko praayah 'shila' kaha jaataa he. shila shaligram kaa chota naam he jiskaa arth "pathar" hota he. shaaligram vishnu kaa ek kam prasiddh naam he. is naam kii utpatti ke saboot nepal ke ek doordaraj ke gaanv se milte he jahaan vishnu ko shaaligramsm ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. hindu dharm main shaaligram ko saalagram ke rup main janaa jaataa he. shaaligram kaa sambandh saalagram naamak gaanv se bhi he jo gandaki naamak nadi ke kinare par sthit he tathaa yahaan se ye pavitra pathar bhi miltaa he.
 hindu dharm main aamtaur par maanavarupi dharmik murtiyon pratinidhitiv karti hai haalanki pratik chinhon kaa bhi samaan rup se prayog hota he. shaaligram ke rup main bhagwaan ke amoort rup kaa pratinidhitiv kiya jaataa jis par maanav aasaani se dhyaan kendrit kar sakenore jaisaa kii bhagwaan krishna ne gita main kaha he ,
