 nagendranath basu bangla bhashaa ke gyaanakoshkar the. unke dwaara sampadit bangala vishwakosh hi bhartiya bhashaaon se pranit pratham aadhunik vishwakosh he. yah san 1911 main 22 khandon main prakashit hua. 
 nagendranath vasu ne hi anek hindi vidwaanon ke sahyog se bangala vishwakosh kaa hindi roopaantar 'hindi vishwakosh' kii rachana kii jo san 1916 se 1932 ke madhya 25 khandon main prakashit hua.
 nagendranath basu puratatvvid, itihaaskaar tathaa lekhak the. unka janm kolkata main hua tha. kam aayu main hi unhone kavita evam upanyaas likhnaa shuru kar diya tha kintu sheeghra ve sampaadan kaary main lag gaye. 'tapaswini' tathaa 'bharat' naamak do masik patrikaon kaa unhone sampaadan kiya.
