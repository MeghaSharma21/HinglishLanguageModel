 banapura ek nagar he jo ki hoshangabad jile kii siwani malwa tehsil ke antargat he. yahan rail, yatayaat env bas kii suvidha he. yahan shaskeey nehru smriti ucchtar madhyamik vidhyalay, shaskeey madhyamik balika shaala, shaskeey madhyamik balak shaala, ward kramank 01 praathmik shaala, ward kramaak 03 praathmik shaala, ganj praathmik shaala jaise shaskeey paathshaala he. yahan shaskeey kusum mahavidhyalay he jo barkatulla vishvavidhyalay bhopal se sambhddha he.
 banapura nagar main saanskrithik vaataavaran kaaphi garm he. yahan sabhi dharm, jati, sampradaay, varg ke log ek saath apane-apane parv ko manaaten he. yahan ekta shandaar darshan hota he.
 khel ke kshetra main bhi banapura nagar ne apni chhap chhod rakhi he. iskaa udhaaharan 2012 main baitulatn satapura trophy cricket spardhaa main hoshangabad jile ke banapura nagar kii team ne bhopal ke sahaara club ko karaari maat di. banapura ke khiladinnon ne gendbaaji or ballebaaji donon main behtareen pradarshan kiya. sahaara club bhopal ne pehle ballebaaji karte hue 30 over main 137 ran banaae. javaabi pari main siwani banapura kii team ne 5 wicket ke nuksaan ke baad 138 ranon kaa lakshya 19 over main hi hasil kar liya.
