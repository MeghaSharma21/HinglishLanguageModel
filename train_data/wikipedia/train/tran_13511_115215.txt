 maharashtra main 100 karod kii aabaadi or 3.08 skvar kilometre kshetra ke saath bharat kaa doosraa sabase bada rajya he. rajya main pehali baar aids kaa maamla mumbai se 1986 main suchit hua tha. isi ke sandarbh main maharashtra rajya aids niyantran socity kaa gathan hua tha.
 maharashtra rajya aids niyantran socity ke uddeshy is tarah hai:
  echivi or aids kii rokthaam,
  aids se sankramit or prabhavit logon kii dekhbhaal or sahaayta or
  asli yaa anumaanit echivi sthiti ke aadhaar par kaarysthal main bhedbhaav, kalank or rojgaar-samandhit samasyaon kii rokthaam.
 aids prasar ko rokane ke liye maharashtra rajya aids niyantran socity samay-samay par jan-jagriti sabhaayen karti ai he. aisa hi prayaas disambar 2010 main socity kii ore kiya gaya jab rajya main janajagriti saptahas prarambh hua. iske antargat vividh kaarykram yojit kiye gaye. 'kritishiltaa-janajagriti', 'janajagritisathi kaam karu yaa' jaise naaron se bhare kaarykram yojit hue. isamen mahavidhyalayon ke prabodhan or vividh udyogic karmchaaree unionon ne sakriy rup se bhaag liya. vibhinn nagar palikaon or swasthya sansthaaon kaa bhi is kaarykram main sakaaratmak sahyog praapt tha.
