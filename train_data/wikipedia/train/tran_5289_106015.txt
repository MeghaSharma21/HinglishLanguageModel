 mandiro kii nagari kaashi main baba kaal bhairav mandir varanasi cant se lagbhag 3 ki0 me0 par shahar ke uttari bhaag main sthit he. yah mandir kaashi khandokt puraatan mandir main ski ek he. is mandir kii puranic manyata yah he, ki baba vishwanath ne kaal bhairav ji ko kaashi kaa kotwaal niyukt kiya tha. kaal bhairav ji ko kashivasiyo ke dand dene kaa adhikaar he. yahan ravivaar evam mangalvaar ko apaar bhij aati he. aarti ke samay nagade, ghanta,damru,kii dhavni bahut hi manamohak lagati he. yahan baba ko parasaad main bada,sharaab paan kaa vishesh mahatv he. yahan vishesh rup se bhoot-pishaachadi ke upachaar hetu log aate hai, tathaa baba kii kapa se thik ho jate hai. yahan baalkon ko kaale dhaage diya jaataa he, jisase bachche bhay-mukt ho jate hai. kaashi main aisi manyata he, ki koi bhi praani ko mrityu se purrv yama yatnaa coke sthaan par baba kaal bhairav ke sote kii yatnaa kaa saamana karnaa hota he,haan parantu manyata yah bhi he,ki kedaar khand kaashi basi ko bhairav yatnaa bhi nahin bhogani padati he.
