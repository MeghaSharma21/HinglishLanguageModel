 vyankatesh digambar madgulakar  marathi lekhak the. inke dwaara rachit ek upanyaas sattantar ke liye unhen san 1983 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
 modgulakar ne 8 upanyaas, 200 se adhik laghu kathayen, lagbhag 40 patakatha, kuch lok naatak, yatra vrittant or kuch prakruti par nibandh likhe. unhone kuch angrezi pustakon kaa marathi main anuwaad bhi kiya jisamen mukhyatah vanya jivan par aadhaarit hai. isse unhen "karnal bahadoor" upnaam bhi mila.
 unki pehali pustak "mandeshi manse" 1949 main prakashit hui jab wo keval 22 varsh ke the. unka 1954 kaa upanyaas banagarbari, angrezi, german or hindi sahit vibhinn bhashaaon main anuwaad kiya gaya. unka upanyaas vaavatal 'raduga prakaashak" dwaara angrezi, kannada, or roosi bhashaaon main anuvaadit kiya gaya. amol palekar ke nirdesan main unke upanyaas banagarbari par aadhaarit ek film banaayi gayi.
