 izebiti main kai achchhaayiyaan he. vastut: isamen mossphate or bijetti dono kii achchhaayiyon kaa mail he. isko drive karnaa bijetti se aasaan he or chaalu state main isamen shakti kii haani mosphet kii apeksha kam hota he. is kaaran madhyam shakti ke kaaryon ke liye is divaais ne bijetti or mosphate dono ko baahar kar diya he. ucch dakshata ke saath switch karne main samarth hone ke kaaran yah aaj ke vidyut kaaron, vairieble speed refrigertaron, air-condishanron, varieble speed drive aadi main prayukt ho rahi he.
