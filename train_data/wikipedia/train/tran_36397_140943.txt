 nepal main gopal vanshi, mahishpaal vanshipachhi kiraant vansh kaa shashan prarambh hota he. naveen shatabdi ke baad kiratvanshi raja galisanga kaa pota shirijhnga ne purvi nepal ke anek swatantra raajaaon ko parajeet kar apana aadhipaty kaayam kiya or nayi lipi kaa avishkaar kar kiraantashetra main vidya prachaar kiya aisi janashruti he. kiraant vansh dwaara banaai gai lipi hone ke kaaran is lipi ko kiraant lipi or chandra puling lipi bhi kehete hai. yah lipi dharaan ke boodhasubba mandir ke aasapaas ke kshetron main prachlit he. aajkal rai jati ke log is lipi kaa prayog or prachaarprasar kar rahe hai. kiraant shashan nepal kaa praarambhik shasankaal hote hue bhi gupt lipi or kutila lipi ke baad hi kiraant lipi prachlan main ai thi. iskaa mulkaaran kiraantkaaleen itihaas aaj tak pramanik na hona bataaya jaataa he.
