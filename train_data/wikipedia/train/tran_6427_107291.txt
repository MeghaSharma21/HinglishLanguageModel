  newzealand
 newzealand dollar  newzealand, cook dweepasmooh, niuye, ross adheenakshetra, tokelau tathaa british kshetra pitakern dweepasmooh kii vaid mudra he. newzealand main ise lagbhag sadaiv ise dollar chihn $ chihn ke saath pradarshit kiya jaataa he tathaa kabhi-kabhi anya dollar mudraon se vilag karne ke liye "NZ$" se bhi pradarshit kiya jaataa he. mudra vyapaar ke sandarbh main kabhi kabhi anaupchaarik rup se ise "kiwi" bhi kaha jaataa he, kyonki newzealand kii pahchaan saamanytah vahaan ke swadeshi pakshi kiwi ke saath judi hui he.  
 1967 main prachlan main ai yah mudra 100 saint main vibhajit he. isame paanch moolyavarg hai—jisame paanch note tathaa paanch sikke hai—jisame sabase chota 10 saint kaa sikaa he. aupchaarik rup se isse chote bhi moolyavarg hai, parantu mahangaai va atyadhik utpaadan mulya ke kaaran prachlan se baahar hai. 
 newzealand dollar lagaataar vishwa kii 10 sabase adhik mudra vyapaar mudraon main bana hua he, jiskaa 2013 ke anusaar vaishvik vinimay bazaar main lagbhag 2.0% dainik bhaag he.
