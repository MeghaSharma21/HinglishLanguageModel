 pracheen raikhik maapan ikai ko shahi cubit kehete the or yah 523.5mm lambaai kii thi. ise 7 hatheliyon main, jo 4 sankhya prati main vibhakt thi, parinamtah 28 sankhya. yah maapan maanak kam se kam 2,700 B.C. se prayog hota aa rahaa tha. asli cubit ke kai udhaaharan abhi bhi uplabdh hai. inamen se kai utsaviya maukon par prayog hote hai or mandiron main abhi bhi mil jate hai.
 pracheen misri maapan maanak lagbhag kai sahasr varshon purv do pranaaliyon ke milan se bane the. pracheenatam maanav sharir maap gliff ke kaal ke hai, jisamen se cubit ko kai palats main dekha gaya he. pracheenatam krishi sambandhi gliff bichchhu raja kii paleton main darshata he khet, jo ki sinchai kii naaliyon se bante hai.
 ek pranali dashamalvadhaarit thi, jo ki horas-akshi dwidhari khand main likhe the or surveykshankartaaon dwaara kheton ke maapan hetu prayog hote the. 
 duusari pranali yatharth va shuddh thi, jisamen hekat ko horas akshi bhagfal main vibhajit kiya gaya tha or age misri khnd parimanikrut remenderon main banta tha. yah 1/320 veen ikai rho naam se bani thi. donon hi pranaliyaan ikai khandon ko dhyaan main rakhkar baniin thim, jisamen dashmalav vaali aseemit shrenee thi auryatharth vaali simit shrenee thi.
 unaniyon ne maanviya haath aadhaarit kubet ko prayog jari rakhaa, jabaki roman logon ne hatheli aadhaarit ko.
 misri maap is maanak par suvyavasthit hai, parantu asal maapan rad or upkaran ek millimetre prati cubit tak badal sakate hai.
 For longer distances the Egyptians used a minute of march of 350 royal cubits and an atur or itrw of 21,000 royal cubits.
