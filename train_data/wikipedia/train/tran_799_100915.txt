 jordan ko prashasnic rup se 12 praanton main vibhajit kiya gaya he. anya arabi-bhashi deshon kii tarah jordan main bhi praanton ko muhafazah kaha jaataa he. har muhafazah ko "liva" naamak ikaiyon main vibhajit kiya gaya he jo "zile" ke jaisaa darja rakhte hai. yah liva phir swayan "kda" kahalaane vaale upazilon main baante jate hai.
 bhugolic rup se jordan ke praant desh ke teen kshetron main vargeekrut hai: uttar kshetra, madhya kshetra or dakshin kshetra. madhya or dakshin kshetron ke bich main muaab ke pahaad sthit hai.
