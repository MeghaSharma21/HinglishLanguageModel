 nyingchi vibhaag yaa nyingkhri vibhaag, jise janavaadee ganatantr china kaa prashasan chini main linjhi vibhaag bulaata he, tibet kaa ek prashasnic vibhaag he jo vartmaan main janavaadee ganatantr china dwaara niyantrit tibet swashasit pradesh kaa hissaa he. yah kshetra tibet swashasit pradesh ke dakshinpurv main sthit he. isaki seemayen bharat ke arunachal pradesh rajya se lagati hai or china kii sarkaar us bhartiya rajya ke is vibhaag kaa hissaa hone kaa daawa karata he haalanki bharat is daawey kaa khandan karata he. vishwa-prasiddh yarlung tsangpo mahaan ghati or namacha barwa naamak parvat isi vibhaag ke medog zile main sthit hai. aitihaasik rup se nyingchi vibhaag kaa bhushetra tibet ke parmparik kham praant kaa hissaa hua karata tha.
 prashasnic rup se nagachu vibhaag 7 zilon main banta hua he. chini sarkaar ke anusaar bharat ke arunachal pradesh ke kai hisse is vibhaag ke medog or zaayu zilon ke bhaag hai, lekin bharat iskaa khandan karata he or yah kshetra bharat ke niyantran main bhi hai.
