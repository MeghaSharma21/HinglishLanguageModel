 ashlesha nakshatra main janma jataka prayek kaary main safal hota he. ashlesha nakshatra nauvaan nakshatra he. yah kark raashi ke antargat aata he. iske charnanusaar naam di du de do he. is nakshatra kaa swami budh he. in nakshatra main janm lene waalon ko budh va chandra kaa prabhaav padataa he. budh gyaan kaa kaarak he. yah vanik grah bhi mana gaya he.
 surya ke najadik hone se ise praatah dekha ja saktaa he. yah surya ke ek ghar aage yaa ek ghar peeche rahata he va surya ke saath hota he. isaki mahadasha 17 varsh chalati he. budh kaa rang harith hone se iskaa ratna panna he. budh pradhan jataka safal vyapaaree, adhivaktaa, bhaashan kala main nipun hote hai. aise jataka bolne main chatur, chaalaak, kaam nikaalane main maahir hote he. budh pradhan jataka swaarthee kism ke bhi ho sakate hai.
 yah napunsak grah hone se duusare grahon ke saath ho to uttam faldaayak hote he. singh raashi par swatantra hokar centre main ho to bhi shubh faldaayak hote he. budh jis raashi main hoga uskaa bhi prabhaav jataka ke jivan par padegaa.
