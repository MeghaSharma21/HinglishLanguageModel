 antarrashtriya khagoleey sangh ), peshewar khagolashastriyon kaa ek sanghathan he. iskaa kendriya sachivalay paris, frans main he. is sangh kaa dhyey khagolashastra ke kshetra main anusandhan or adhyayan ko antarrashtriya str par badhaava dena he. jab bhi brahmand main koi nai vastu pai jaati he to khagoleey sangh dwaara die gaye naam hi antarrashtriya str par maanya hote hai.
 antarrashtriya khagoleey sangh ko angrezi main "international astronaumical union" or francisi main "uniyaan astronomik anteranasyonal" kaha jaataa he.
 antarrashtriya khagoleey sangh kaa sanghathan 1919 main kiya gaya tha jab bahut si anya khagoleey sanghathanon ko isamen vilay kar diya gaya. iske pehle adhyaksh francisi khagolashastri baindamein balaud the.
 1922 ke baad, akhas har teen saal main ek saamaanya baithak karata aa rahaa he. isamen sirf ek baar dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke kaaran 1938-1948 ke das saal ke antaraal main koi baithak nahin hui thi.
