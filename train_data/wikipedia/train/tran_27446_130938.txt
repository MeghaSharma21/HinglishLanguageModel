 mishaal raheja ek bhartiya televijan abhinayta hai. inhone chote parde par apni shuruyaat amtivi ke pyaar vyaar and ol det se kiya tha. inhen inke laagi tujhse lagan ke datta bhaau naam ke kirdaar ke kaaran kaaphi janaa jaataa he. ye lav story main akaash naam ke kirdaar main bhi najar aa chuke hai. ye colors ke ishq kaa rang safed main viplab naam ke ek ladke kaa kirdaar nibhaa rahe hai, jo ek vakil he or logon kii madad ko taiyaar rahata he.
 inhone tivi par apane abhinay ke safar kii shuruyaat 2005 main amtivi ke pyaar vyaar and ol det se shuru kiya, uske baad paayal sarkaar ke saath lav story or colors ke "laagi tujhse lagan" main maahi vij ke saath abhinay kiya.
