 get out 2017 kii ek america horer film he, jiske nirdeshak jordan pile hai. dainiel kaluya film main ek ashwet vyakti, kris kii bhoomika main he, jiske samaksh apni premika ke parivaar se milte samay ek pareshaan karne vaala rahasy ujagar ho jaataa he. bradli whitford, kaleb landry jones, stephen root, or catherine kenner film main sahaayak bhoomikaaon main hai.
 24 janvari 2017 ko sundence film festival main film kaa premier hua, or universal pikchars dwaara 24 farvari 2017 ko ise sanyukt rajya ke cinemagharon main release kiya gaya. film ko duniya bhar main sakaaratmak samikshaayen praapt hui, vishesh rup se film ke mul vishay, pile kii patakatha or nirdesan, or kaluya ke abhinay ko khub saraahaa gaya. neshnal rivyuu board of america, american film institute or time patrika ne is film ko varsh 2017 kii sheersh 10 filmon main se ek chunaa. yah film box offis par bhi safal rahi; $4.5 million ke bajet par bani is film ne duniya bhar main $255 million kii kamai kari.
 get out ne kai puraskaar praapt kare. 90wein academy puraskaron main film ne chaar namakan arjeet kiye: sarvshereshth film, sarvshereshth nirdeshak, sarvshereshth mul patakatha, or kaluya ke liye sarvshereshth abhinayta. iske atirikt ise 23wein critics chois puraskaron main paanch namakan, 75wein golden globe puraskaron main do or 71wein british academy film puraskaron main bhi do namakan praapt hue.
