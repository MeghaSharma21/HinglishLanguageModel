 ghumar rajasthan kaa ek paramparaagat lokanrity he. iskaa vikaas bhil kabeeley ne kiya tha or baad main baaki rajasthani birdariyon ne ise apana liya. yah naach zyaadaatar auraten ghunghat lagaakar or ek ghumerdaar poshaak jise "ghaghara" kehete hai, pahan kar karti hai.
 saamanytah nimna geeton par ghumar nrity kiya jaataa he.
