 helen thomas ek america lekhika, patrkaar, agrani mahila reporter or white house press kor kii pehali mahila sadsy thi. unhone america raashtrapati dwite ijanhavar se lekar baraak obama tak desh ke vibhinn raashtrapatiyon se sambanghit khabaron kii dashakon tak kawarej kiya. israel-falstin sangharsh ke sambandh main unhone vivaadaaspad tippanee kii thi jiske parinamswaroop unhen june 7, 2010 ko patrakarita chhodani padi. thomas ne united press or uske baad united press international main 57 varshon tak kaam kiya. unhone pehle ek samvaadadaata ke rup main or baad main white house ke beuro manager ke rup main kaary kiya. wo white house corespondand association kii pehali mahila adhyaksh baniin or unhen neshnal press club kii pehali mahila adhikari or giridiron club kii pehali mahila sadsy bhi bani. july 20, 2013 ko 92 varsh kii aayu main helen thomas kaa nidhan ho gaya.
