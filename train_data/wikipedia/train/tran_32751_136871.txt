 mongolia kaa pathaar madhya asia main sthit ek vishaal pathaar ilaaka he jiskaa kul kshetrafal lagbhag 26 lakh varg kilometre he. aadhunik yug main yah uttar main mongolia or dakshin main china ke bhitari mongolia rajya ke darmiyaan banta hua he. isamen gobi registan or stapi ke ghaas ke maidaanon kaa kuch kshetra bhi sammilit he. isaki oonchai 900 se 1,500 meter ke darmiyaan he.
 angrezi main mongolia ke pathaar ko 'mongolian plato' kehete hai.
