 yah sahaayta prishth vibhinn online evam ofline typing tuls kii jaankaree hetu he. vikipedia kii difolt typing pranali ke liye vikipedia:naraayam dekhen.
 devanagari tip karne ke liye kai vidhiyaan hai. aap chahen to apana swayan kaa koi tul prayog kar sakate hai yaa phir vikipedia main anternirmit tip tul bhi uplabdh he.
 ve log jo kabhi computor main devanagari lipi tip nahin kiye hai, unke liye phonetic typing bahut aasaan tarika he, jo windos par kaary karte hai. inamen do mukhya ofline aujaar microsoft kaa indic input tul or google input tul he. inhen aap apane computer main sthapit karne ke baad vikipedia yaa kahin bhi sidhe hi hindi likh sakate hai.
 google main aapako purnaviram likhane main pareshaani hogi, uske liye aapako shortkat taiyaar karnaa hoga, tabhi aap purnaviram kaa upyog kar sakate hai. yah samasya microsoft ke input tul main nahin he. in donon main se sabhi se aap acchhee tarah hindi likh sakate hai, haalanki aapako shuruyaat ke ek do din pareshaani ho sakati he, par uske baad aap aasaani se tej gati se hindi likh sakenge.
 devanagari main likhane ke liye ek sthaayee upaay inascript kunjipat kaa prayog he. inscript ek tach typing kunjipatal he jo bhartiya bhashaaon kii lipiyon main computer par likhane hetu prayukt hota he. yah bhartiya lipiyon ke liye bharat sarkaar dwaara maanak ke rup main sweekrut he. neeche diya chitra tivinus company ke inascript dwibhashiya keyboard kaa he. 
 aajkal ke naye prachaalan tantron, jaise - windos 2000, windos axpi, linaks evam makintosh par inascript anternirmit aati he.
 yadi aapako puraane maikenical tiperiter par yaa phir puraane non-unicode font kritidev kii typing kaa abhyaas he to aap remington typing aujaaron kaa prayog kar sakate hai. remington hetu ek pramukh aujaar indic ikhmai he.
