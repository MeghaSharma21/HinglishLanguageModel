 guldaan yaa phooldaan ek vishesh aakaar kaa bartan hota he jisamen tahaniyon ke samet phulon kaa pradarshan kiya jaataa he. guldaan aksar mitti yaa shishe ke bane hote hai or unpar chitrakaari yaa naqkaashi se adhik lubhaavna banaaya gaya hota he.
 guldaan ko angrezi main "vase" bolte hai jise "wez" yaa "vaaz" uccharit kiya jaataa he . puraani hindi main guldaan ek "basana" naam kii bartanon kii shrenee kaa sadsy he, jisamen kalash bhi shaamil he.
 guldaan aksar neeche se khule hue hote hai, taaki phulon kii tahaniyaan pani main rahakar phulon ko zyaada der tak murajhaane se bachaa saken. upar ek tang gardan hoti he, jinase tahaniyon ko sidhaa khadey rahane ke liye sahaara miltaa he. sab se upar guldaan kaa mukh aksar thodaa failaa hota he. puraane jamaane main guladaanon par hathe bhi aam the, lekin ab yah kam dekhe jate hai.
