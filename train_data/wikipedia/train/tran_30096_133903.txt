 vikividyalay yaa vikiwarsity angrezi: Wikiversity ek viki pariyojna he, jiskaa sanchaalan vickemedia santhaan karti he. iskaa uddeshy nickshulk seekhney kii saamagree or logon ko badhaava dena he. yah vikipedia se kaaphi alag he or jaankaree ko paathyakram or abhyaas kii tarah prastut karata he. 
 vikividyalay beta or angreji bhashaa main vikividyalay kii shuruyaat aadhikarik rup se 15 agast 2006 ke ko hui thi. lekin iskaa vichaar vikipustak pariyojna ke bheetar hua tha, tab kai saare lekhon ko hataane ke liye namankit kiya gaya tha, jiske baad ise ek alag pariyojna ke rup main banane kaa prastaav rakhaa gaya. or jald hi yah ek vickemedia ke pariyojna ke rup main shuru ho gaya.
 iske liye do prastaav bane the, jisamen se pehla vaala 2005 ko bana tha, jise sweekaar nahin kiya gaya or usee prastaav kaa badla hua rup 2006 main sweekrut kar diya gaya. is pariyojna ke banane kii ghoshnaa vikemania 2006 main kii gai thi.
