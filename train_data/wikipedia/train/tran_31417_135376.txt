 ashtsahasrika pragnaparmita aath hajaar shlokonvaala yah mahayan buddha granth pragna kii parmita ke mahatmaya kaa varnan karata he. pragnaparmita ko moort rup main avatarit kar uske chamatkaar dikhaae gaye hai. isamen 32 pariched hai jinamein praaia: griddhakut parvat par bhagavan buddha apane subhuti, sariputra, purna maitrayniputra jaise shishyon ko upadesh dete hue upasthit hai. aage chalakar is granth ke kai choti or bade sanskaran bane.
 ashtsahasrika pragnaparmita kii rachana sambhavtah isapurv pehali shatabdi main hui.
