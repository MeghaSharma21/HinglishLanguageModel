 paryaavaran bhugol paryaavarniya dashaon, unki kaaryashiltaa or takneekee rup se sabal aarthik maanav or paryaavaran ke bich sambandhon kaa adhyayayan sthanik tathaa kaalik sandarbhon main karata he.
 yah ek tarah kaa sanshleshnatmak vigyaan he or ise integrated bhugol ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he. 
 bhugol ki yah shakha ek prakaar se bhautik bhugol or maanav bhugol ke vibhaajan or badhati duriyon ko kam karne kaa kaary karti he| bhugol sadaiv hi maanav or uske paryaavaran kaa adhyayan sthaan ke sandarbhon main karata rahaa he lekin 1950-1970 ke bich bhautik bhugol or maanav bhugol ke bich babbti duriyon ne ise paryaavaran ke adhyayan se vimukh kar diya tha or is dauraan paryaavaran ke jhandabardaar jeev vigyaani rahe. baad main tantra vishleshan or paristhitikeey upaagam ke badhate mahatv ko bhugol main teji se sweekruti mili or paryaavaran bhugol, bhautik or maanav bhugol ke bich ek bahuaiami sanshleshan ke rup main ubhara.
 paryaavaran bhugol ke adhyayan kaa kshetra: paryaavaran kaa ek paaritantr ke rup main sanghathan or uski kaaryashiltaa, maanav-paaritantreey sambandh vishleshan or paryaavarniya avanayan, paryaavarniya dashaon or maanav-paryaavaran sambandhon kaa sthanik sandarbh main adhyayan, tathaa paryaavaran sangrakshan or prabandhan se jude sthanik pahalu bhugolic soochna pranali or sudur samvedan takanic kaa paryaavarniya adhyayan main anuprayog ki dasha or disha nirdhaarit karnaa he.
 atah prakritik yaa bhautik bhugol or maanav bhugol ke madhya samanvay sthapit karne or inke bahu aiami sanshleshan ke kaaran paryaavaran bhugol ko ek teesri nai shakha ke rup main bhi kuch vidwaanon dwaara dekha gaya he.
