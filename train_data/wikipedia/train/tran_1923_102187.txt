 guru kaa nakshatra purva bhadrapada kaa antim charan min raashi main aata he. ise hi naam se pahachaanaa jaataa he.
 guru kaa nakshatra va guru kii raashi min main hone se aisa jataka aakarshak vyaktitv kaa dhani, gunwan, dharm, karm ko maanane vaala, eemaandaar, paropkaaree, nyaaypriy hota he. guru yadi apni raashi dhanu yaa min main ho to aise jataka sadaachari hote hai.
 aise jaatakon ko shikshan se sambandhit kaaryon main bhi uttam safalataa milti he. guru yadi mesh lagn main navam bhav, lagn yaa pancham bhav yaa ucch kaa hokar chaturth bhav main sthit ho to aise jataka mata, bhoomi, bhawan, santan, vidya bhagya ke kshetra main uttam sthiti pate he.
 min raashi main ho to janm sthaan se dur bhagyoday hota he.
 vrishabh lagn main guru kii sthiti ekadash, tritiy pancham saptam main ho to laabh ke maamalon main jataka eemaandaaree adhik rakhata he. patni guni milti he.
