 paaonta sahib, bhartiya rajya himachal pradesh kaa eka silp nagari he. yah sirmaur jilla kaa uttar disha me jamuna nadi ke kinare he. yahan jaateey rajpath 7 gujarey hai. yahan cement, kapade, dabaai, rasayan drabaya, khaadya upapaadan kaa silp samooh sthapit he. asia mahadesh kaa sabase bada chunaa kaa bazaar yaheen par he. jamuna nadi ke kinare rahete "paaonta sahib" kaa gurudwara sikh sampradaay ke liye bahut bada tirth mana jaataa he. 
 is shahar ko dasham sikh guru guru gobind singh dwaara sthapit kiya gaya tha . isi jagah kaa naam pehle " paontica " tha. kaha jaataa he kii sikh guru guru gobind singh ji ne ashv rohan karke ja rahe the. wo isi jagah pahunch kar ruk gaye the. isi liye " paaon" or " tike " ko milakar " paaonta " naam diya gaya tha. guru gobind singh ji ne yah jagah par gurudwara sthaapan kiye or yaheen par saadhe 4 saal tak gujarey. yahi sthaan par wo bahut dharmagranth rachana kiye the. yaha ek miujium bana hua he. miujium main guru gobind singh ji kaa istemaal kiya hua bahut samaan bhakton ke darshan ke liye rakhaa gaya he. 
 paaonta kaa absthiti 30°26′17″N 77°37′26″E / 30.438°N 77.624°E / 30.438; 77.624. isi jagah samudr kaa ster se 1,276 foot upar he. najdiki sahar himachal pradesh kaa nahaan, haryana kaa jamunanagar, uttarapradesh kaa saharanpur or uttarakhand kaa deheradun he. kalesar jaateey park yahan se kareeb 14 kilometre dur he. 
 2011 sensoos ke mutabik yahan 1,58,268 log rahete the. isame se mard 53 % or mahila 47 % the. sakshyarata kaa haar 76 % tha, jo jaateey haar se adhik tha. 6 saal se kam aabaadi 13 % tha. yahan hinduon kaa sankhya 78.63 % or sikh 15.01 % or baaki annya sampradaayon kaa the.
