 bernwell minar, glocaster ke duk kaa aitihaasik purv nivas he. yah northhamptonshir, england ke bernwell gaanv ke nikat sthit he. is estate main aitihaasik bernwell kasal bhi sthit he. jise 1266 main baranger lee moen ne banwaaya tha. moen ne is makaan ko raimase abi ko 1276 main bech diya tha. ise 1536 tak is abi ne rakhaa tha, jiske baad ise raaj ko saunp diya gaya. elizabethan kaal kaa minar ghar, yahan kaa mukhya nivasashala ban gaya, jabaki baaki ke saare rihaishi kamare or kile ke anya sari imaarton ko 1704 main gira diya gaya tha. maujuud makaan main chaar resepshan room or saat mukhya shayankaksh hai. yah ek 40-kamaron vaala 18veen sadi kaa minar ghar he, jise multah 1586 main banwaaya gaya tha. yah 2500 esar ke estate par sthit he, jisapar malikatv, glocaster ke duk ke paas he.
