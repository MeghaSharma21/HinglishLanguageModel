 aditya shrivastav ek bhartiya film abhinayta or tivi prayokta hai. yah bharat ke sabase lambey samay tak chalane vaale dharawahik siaidi main varisht nireekshak abhijit ke abhinay ke liye jane jate hai. inhone hindi filmon satya, gulaal, paanch, blike fraide or dil se main bhi nirnaayak bhoomika nibhaai he. iske alaava kaalo film main bhi bakhubi bhoomika nibhaai he. aditya shrivastav abhi abhijit ke naam se kaaphi prasiddh hai.
 aditya shrivastav ne elahabaad vishvavidhyalay se adhyayan karne ke pashchaat dilli sthapit ho gaye or 1989 main tak theatreon main kaam karte the. iske baad inhone siaidi dharawahik main kadam rakh diya jo abhi tak isamen lage hue he.
