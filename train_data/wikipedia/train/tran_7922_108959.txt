 sthalakritik vigyaan main shikhar dharaatal par sthit vah bindu hota he jiskaa unnayan uske aas paas sthit anya sabhi binduon se adhik hota he. ganitiya rup se shikhar unnayan kaa sthaneey adhiktam he. sthalakritik shabdon main "chotii", "sheersh", "shikhar", "paraakaashtha" or "shirobindu" samanarthak shabd hai.
