 
 badria hoshgar ek turk sangeetkaar thi.
 hoshgar lekke sangeet parampara se bachapan se prabhavit thi kyonki unka bachapan konya main guzara. jab ve or unka parivaar istambul sthanantarit hue,hoshgar ne oud kaa prashikshan ismat aafandi liya or usul ke prashikshan kalid beg se liya jo raaj darbar ke muazzan the. hoshgar tanburi jameel beg ke saath bhi kaam liya jinase unka sampark ek jansabha main hua tha. jameel ne hoshgar ko daarul-bidaai mausiqi-e-osmani main shiksha paane ke liye protsaahit kiya jisase sangeet kaa gyaan or bad gaya. hoshgar ne udi navras beg ke saath bhi kaam kiya.
