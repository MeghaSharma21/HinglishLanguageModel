 anupriya goyanka ek bhartiya bollywood tathaa telywood abhinetri he jinhone 2018 main salmaan khan or catarina caff kii mukhya paatr ke saath tiger jinda he tathaa padmavat film main abhinay kiya he. ye hindi ke alaava telegu bhashi filmon main bhi kaary karti he.
 anupriya goyanka kaa janm 29 may 1987 ko uttar pradesh ke kanpur main hua tha. inke pita ek kapade ke udyogpati he jabaki maan gruhinee he. goyanka kii do badi bahan he jabaki ek bhai he. inhone apni vidyalayan shiksha gyaan bharti vidhyalay, saket, nai dilli se kii thi or iske baad inhone snaatak kii padhai commers main shahid bhagat singh college, dilli vishvavidhyalay se kii thi.
