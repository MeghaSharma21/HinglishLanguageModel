 munshi dayanarayan nigam beesvin sadi ke prarambh main kanpur se prakashit hone vaali urdu patrika zamana ke sampadak the. unhone premchand kii pehali kahani duniya kaa sabase anmol ratan prakashit kii thi. nawabrai ke naam se likhane vaale lekhak ko premchand naam bhi munshi dayanarayan nigam ne diya tha.
 janm tathaa shiksha munshi dayanarayan nigam kaa janm 22 march, 1882 ko uttar pradesh rajya ke kanpur shahar main hua tha. unhone 'elahabaad vishvavidhyalay', uttar pradesh se shiksha pai thi. nigam ji vidvan vyaktitv ke dhani the. ve angrezi, urdu, farsi ke saath-saath ye bangala, gujarati or marathi bhashaaon ke bhi gyaata the. sampadak va samaaj sudhaarak dayanarayan nigam ne masik patr 'zamana' ke madhyam se 'urdu sahitya' kii abhootpurv seva kii. yadhyapi nigam ji rashtriya vichaaron ke vyakti the, kintu sangharsh kii raajnneeti se ve praaia: alag hi rahe. samaaj sudhaar unka priy vishay tha, or ve antarjaatiy or 'vidhwaa vivaah' par bada bal dete the. kehete hai ki unke aagrah par hi munshi premachand ek vidhwaa stri se vivaah karne ke liye taiyaar hue the. munshi premachand kii adhikansh urdu rachanaayen 'zamana' main hi chhapati thim. mohammad iqbaal kii prasiddh rachana 'saare jahaan se acha hindustaan hamaara' bhi 'zamana' main hi pehali baar prakashit hui thi.
