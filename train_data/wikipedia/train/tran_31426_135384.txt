 ilkal karnataka rajya ke bagalkot jile ke hunagund taluk kaa ek chota sa nagar he. yah hunagund se 12 kimi dakshin-purv main ghati main sthit he. 
 yah nagar jile ke bade vyapaarik nagaron main se ek he or yahan bunai evam rangai kaa udyog pramukh he. yahaan se niryaat kii janevaali vastuon main reshmi tathaa sooti kapade or krishi kii upjen mukhya hai. dakshini striyon ke pahanaave ke udyog ke liye bhi yah prasiddh he. yahan aadhunik dhang se nirmit basankari, basavanna evam vyancoba ke teen mandir bahut hi prasiddh hai. yahan pus poornima ko prati varsh bada mela lagata he.
