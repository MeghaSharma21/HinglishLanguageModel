 bhoot ankal 2006 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he.
 is film kii shooting ke dauraan jangal kaa vah seen, jahaan bachche bhoot ankal se pehali baar milte hai or uske baad skool main sabhi bacchon ke saamne bhoot ankal pehali baar dikhaai dete hai, vah khaas seen special effect ke dwaara banaaya gaya tha, jisamen jaiki shroff achaanak prakat hote hai.
 yah jaankaree meghraaj rohalan 'munshi' dwaara copirite kii gai he. iskaa upyog karne ke liye meghraaj rohalan 'munshi' se likhit anumati lenaa anivaary he. yadi aap meghraaj rohalan 'munshi' kii likhit anumati ke bina ukt jaankaree kaa istemaal karte hai to yah copirite niyamon kaa saraasar ulanghan hoga, jiske liye aapako bhartiya dand samhita main varnit dhaaraon ke anusaar sajaa ho sakati he.
