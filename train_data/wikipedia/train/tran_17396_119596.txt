 vyapaar chakra bazaar arthvyavastha main ek varsh athwa kuch maah main utpaadan, vyapaar or sambandhit gatividhi ko sandarbhit karne vaala ek shabd he.
 poonjeewaadi vyavastha main sadaa teji yaa sadaa mandi nahin rahati balki teji ke baad mandi tathaa mandi ke baad teji kaa kram aata rahata he. yah poonjeewaadi arthvyavastha kii pramukh visheshta he. ise hi vyapaar chakra kaate hai. kai deshon kii arthvyavastha kaa adhyayan karne ke pashchaat france ke arthshaastri clement jagalar ne sabase pehle 1862 'vyapaar chakra' kii sankalpana rakhi thi. 
 pramukh aarthik siddhanth mukhyatah 4 prakaar ke chakron kii baat karte hai. ye sabhi chakra sadaa chalate rahete hai kintu inke aavartan kii avadhi alag-alag hoti he.
