 deepika singh ek bhartiya televijan abhinetri he.
 singh ne apane karier kii shuruyaat star plus kii shrinkhalaa diya or baati ham main sandhya kii bhumika nibhaakar kii, joki mouj masti karne vaali, utsaahi yuva ladki kii kahani he. vah ipies adhikari banane kii aakanksha rakhti he, lekin kuch tanaavpurn paristhitiyon main, uskaa vivaah mithai kii dukaan ke maalik se ho jaataa he. uskaa pati uske ipies banane ke sapane ko puraa karne main uski madad karata he. televijan main kaam karne se purv unhone ek punjabi geet elbum main bhi kaam kiya he. 
 singh ne punjab takneekee vishvavidhyalay se vipanan par vyavsaay prashasan main masters kii degree lee he. 
 singh ne apane televijan sho ke nirdeshak rohit raaj goyal se 2 may 2014 main vivaah kiya tha.
