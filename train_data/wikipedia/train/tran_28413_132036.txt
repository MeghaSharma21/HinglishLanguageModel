 pangasau darre se patkai pahaado kaa nazaaraa
 patkai bharat ke purvottar main burma ke saath lagi antarrashtriya simavarti kshetra par sthit pahari shrunkhalaaon kaa naam he. iske pahaado kii oonchaai himalay kii tulana main kaafii kam he. patkai pahaado main teen mukhya shrunkhalaayen shaamil hai: patkai bum, khasi-gaaro-jayantiya shrunkhlaa or lushai pahadiyaan. yah parvat bharat ke nagaland, manipur, meghalaya or mizoram rajyon main vistrut hai. pangasau darra patkai pahaado kaa sabase mahatvapurn pahari darra he or bharat ko burma se jodne vaali ledo road isi darre se nikalati he. bharat-burma sarhad par khadaa 3,826 meter unchaa saramati parvat patkai pahaado kaa sabase buland shikhar he or yah bhartiya upmahadweep kaa sabase unchaa gair-himalian parvat bhi he. dhyaan den ki bharat-burma seemaa par is se unchaa 5,881 meter unchaa khaakabo raji pahaad bhi khadaa he lekin vah himalay parvaton kii ek upashrunkhalaa kaa sadsy he.
