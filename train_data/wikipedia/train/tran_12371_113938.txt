 
 † Calliasterellidae † Trichasteropsida† dwaara namankit kul vilupt ho chuke hai
 tara machhlee ikainodermeta sangh kaa aprishthvanshi praani he jo keval samudri jal main hi paayi jaati he. iske sharir kaa aakaar tara jaisaa hota he, sharir main discs or paanch bhujaaen hoti he jo kade plates se dhanki rahata hai. upari sataha par anek kantedaar rachanaayen hoti hai. discs par madhya main guda sthit hoti he. nichali sataha par discs ke madhya main munh sthit he. bhujaaon par do kataron main tub feet hote hai. prachlan kii kriya tubafeet ke dwaara hoti he tathaa papuli dwaara shwasan kii kriya hoti he. isaki ek prajati,Gohongaze ko japani log bade chaav se khate hai.
