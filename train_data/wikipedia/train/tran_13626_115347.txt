 york kii, rajkumaari biatris, british rajpariwaar kii ek sadsy he. wo rajkumaar andru, york ke duk or sara, york kii dches ke jyeshtha putree he. vah rani elizabeth dwi kii pautri he, or british singhasana ke anukram main saatven sthaan par hai, tathaa duusari mahila hai. vah apni mata-pita kii pehali santan hai.
 biatris kaa janm 8 agast 1988 ko portland aspataal, landan main hua tha. unki parvarish englandmein hui he. unhone apni praathmik shiksha windsar, barkshaayar ke upatoun house skool or tatpashchaat escot ke georgez skool se praathmik or madhyamik shiksha praapt kii he. tatpashchaat unhone landan ke goldsmiths college se itihaas main kala snaatak kii padhai puri kii he. haalaanki ve shahi parivaar kii sakriy sadsy nahin he, parantu aksar niyamit rup se unhen kai shahi kaaryakramon or samarohon main shariq hote dekha ja saktaa he. iske alaava ve anek daankaryon or cheritable sansthaanon kii seva main shaamil hoti hai. inamen, aids peediton, sankat kii sthiti main fansen maaon or bacchon kii madad se sambandhit kaary shaamil hai. unhone april 2010 main daan ke liye dhan jugadne ke liye ek marathon daud main hissaa liya tha, jise vibhinn charitiyon ko laab pohonchane ke liye upyog kiya gaya. july 2016 tak, virgin galactic ke bijansman, david clark ke saath unka ek lanbaa nijee rishta tha.
 rajkumaari biatris, rajpariwaar kii, aadhikarik taur par ek "kaaryshil" sadsy nahin he, atah unhen shahi bhatte se koi anudaan nahin muhaiyaa karai jaati he. or unke nijee kaaryon ko court sarcular main report nahin kiya jaataa he. baharhaal, ve shahi parivaar dwaara prastut anek kaaryakramon main shaamil rahati hai. saath hi kai sarkari or rajkeey samarohon main bhi unki upasthiti rahati he.
