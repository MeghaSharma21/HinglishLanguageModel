 pavlodaar praant madhya asia ke qaazakhstan desh kaa ek praant he. isaki rajdhani bhi pavlodaar naam kaa shahar hi he. is praant kii sarahadein uttar main rus se lagati hai. siberia kii mahatvapurn irtish nadi china dwaara niyantrit altaai parvaton ke ek bhaag se nikalakar is praant se guzarati he or uttar main rus main nikal jaati he. kuch samikshakon ke anusaar qaazakhstan kaa yah bhaag rahan-sahan or samskruti main siberia jaisaa adhik or madhya asia jaisaa kam lagata he.
 1950 ke dashak main, jab qaazakhstan soviet sangh kaa hissaa tha, to soviet sangh main anaaj kii bahut kami hui. us jamaane main soviet vyavastha main adhiktar anaaj rus ke upjau kshetron main ugaaya jaataa tha. soviet netaa nikita khrushchevâ€‹ ne elaan kiya kii anaaj kii paidaavaar badhaane ke liye madhya asia ke stapi ilaake kii zameenon par nae sire se kheti shuru kii jaaegi. is abhiyaan kaa naam 'nai dharti' rakhaa gaya. baari maatr main rus or ukraine se log uttari qaazakhstan ke nae kheton main aa based jis se sthaneey samudaayon kaa mishran bhi badal gaya. yahi kaaran he ki pavlodaar praant jaise ilaaqon main roosi logon kii bahutayat he.
 2007 kii janaganana main pavlodaar praant ke 46.15% log kazakh, 38.26% roosi, 6.39% ukraini, 3.21% german or 2.07% tartar the. inke alaava yahan belarusi, moldovi, ingush, chechen, azeri or bashkiri log bhi rahete hai. soviet sangh ke khatm ho jane ke baad se yahan roosiyon or germanon kii taadaad ghat rahi he kyonki un samudaayon ke bahut se log dhire-dhire qaazakhstan chhodkar rus or germany ja rahe hai.
