 aarany devi mandir, aara, bhojpur, bihar main sthit ek hindu mandir he.
 sanwat 2005 main sthapit aarany devi kaa mandir nagar ke sheesh mahal chowk se uttar-purv chor par sthit he. yah devi nagar kii adhishthatri maani jaati hai. bataaya jaataa he ki ukt sthal par pracheen kaal main sirf adishkti kii pratimaa thi. is mandir ke chaaron ore van tha. pandav vanvaas ke kram main aara main tehre the. pandavon ne adishkti kii puja-archana kii. devi ne yudhisthir ko swapan main sanket diya ki vah aarany devi kii pratimaa sthapit kare. dharmraj yudhisthir ne maa aarany devi kii pratimaa sthapit kii. 
 yah bhi kaha jaataa he ki bhagwaan ram, lakshmana or vishwamitra jab baxur se janakapur dhanush yajna ke liye ja rahe the to aarany devi kii puja-archana kii. tadoparaant sonbhadra nadi ko paar kiye the. bataaya jaataa he ki dwapar yug main is sthaan par raja mayooradhvaj raaj karte the. inke shasankaal main bhagwaan shrikrishna arjuna ke saath yahaan aaye the. shrikrishna ne raja ke daan kii pariksha lete hue apane singh ke bhojan ke liye raja se uske putr ke daahine ang kaa maance maanga. jab raja or rani maance ke liye apane putr ko aara se chirane lage to devi prakat hokar unako darshan di thim. 
 is mandir main sthapit badi pratimaa ko jahaan saraswati kaa rup mana jaataa he, vahiin choti pratimaa ko mahalakshmi kaa rup mana jaataa he. is mandir main varsh 1953 main shriram, lakshmana, sita, bharat, shatrudhan va hanuman ji ke alaave anya devi-devataaon kii pratimaa sthapit kii gayi thi.
