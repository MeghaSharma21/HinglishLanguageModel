 madhav kandali asmi ke prasiddh kavi the. inke kavitaakaal ke sambandh main itihaaskaaron tathaa samalochakon main adhik matbhed he. kanakalal baruva ke matanusaar inke ashrayadata vaarahi naresh capli upatyaka ke shaasha the or madhav kandali inhen ke raajakavi the. is prakaar inaki kavita kaa rachanakaal 14veen shatee kaa uttarardh maaloom hota he. madhavchand bardaloi ne swasampadit ramayana kii bhoomika main inaki kriti ramayana ko 14veen athwa 15veen shatee kii rachana or inhen navagaanv kaa nivasee pramanit kiya he. shankardev ne ram katha ke padakarta madhav kandali kii bhuri-bhuri prashansa kii he. unki tulana gaj se kii he or kaha he ki ve swayan unke sammukh shashak ke samaan laghu hai. madhav kandali ko log 'kaviraj kandali' kehete the. vartmaan navagaanv jile ke kandali naamak sthaan se anek prakhyat kandali brahmanon kaa sambandh tha parantu madhav kandali yahan ke nivasee nahin the.
 varahraaj shri mahamaniky ke anurodh par madhav kandali ne sarsaadhaaran ke liye subodh style main ramayana kaa payarbaddh anuwaad kiya . madhav kandali ke ramayana kii sabhi pratiyon main aadi tathaa uttarkand nahin milte, yadhyapi unhone lankakad ke ant main ramayana ke saat candon kaa ullekh kiya he . kandali ne valmiki krit ramayana ko vedon ke samakaksh rakhaa he. mul katha ko adhik rochak banane ke liye yatratatra sundar kavyakalpana kaa sahaara liya he. 'devjit' inaki duusari rachana he kintu prayog evam style kii drushti se yah kisi anya kavi kii rachana prathit hoti he.
