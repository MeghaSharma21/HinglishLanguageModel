 madh kila maharashtra kii rajdhani mumbai ke uttari bhaag main madh dweep par sthit ek chota sa kila he. yah portugali logon ke adheen bharat main dwaara banaaya gaya tha. 1739 ke farvari maah main unka maraathon se yuddh hua, jisamen ve yah kila haar gayi evam is par maraathon kaa adhikaar ho gaya. kaalaantar main 1774 main angrejon ne salset dweep, thana kila or karanja ke dweep kile ke saath saath varsova kile par apana aadhipaty jamaa liya.
 iskaa nirmaan 17veen shatabdi main portugaliyon dwaara ek pehre kii minar ke rup main karavaaya gaya tha. yah samudr tat ke ek samrik drushti se bahut mahatvapurn hua karata tha or marve creek par najar rakhaa karata tha. iske bahari bhaag abhi bhi jaise ke taise bane khadey dikhaai dete hai, jabaki andaruni bhaag aantarik rup se jeern-sheern ho chale hai. 
 yah kila ekant main bana he or yahaan pahunchnaa kathin he. malad se lagbhag 15 kilometre dur mumbai city bas seva bas se se antim bas stop se pahunch sakate hai. iske alaava varsova ke madhyam se nauka se ja sakate he. yah kila madh gaanv ke paas sthit he or madh bas stop se lagbhag 1 kilometre kii duuri par he. vartmaan main yah bhartiya vaayu sena ke niyantran main he. iskaa kaaran isaki bhartiya air force base se nikatataa he. isiliye yah tak pahunchne ke liye vaayu sena se anumati aavashyak he. 
 madh dweep kaa kila sthaneey machawaron kii bastiyon se ghira hua he. kai bollywood kii filmon kaa filmakan bhi yaheen hua he, jaise lav ke liye kuch bhi karegaa, shootout at wadala, 1985 kii manmohan desai kii film mard, zamana divaana, khalanaayak, shatranj, or taraazuu, aadi. i prasiddh doordarshan dharawahik jaise chandrakanta or si.ai.di kii bhi shooting yahan hui he.
