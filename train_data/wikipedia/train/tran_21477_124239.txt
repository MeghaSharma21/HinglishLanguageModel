 bhagwaan vishnu kaa awaas.bhagwaan vishnu jis lok main nivas karte hai use baikuntha kaha jaataa he. saral shabdon main baikuntha dham jagatpalak bhagwaan vishnu kii duniya he. vaise hi, jaise kailash par mahadev va brahmalok main brahmadev basate hai. iske kai naam hai - saket, golok, paramdhaam, paramasthan, parampad, paramavyom, sanathan akaash, shaashvat-pad, brahampur. shastron ke anusaar bahut hi punya se manushya ko is lok main sthaan miltaa he. jo yahaan pahunch jaataa he use punah garbh main nahin aata kyonki use moksh kii prapti ho jaati he.aadhyatm kii najar se baikuntha dham man kii avasthaa he. baikuntha koi sthaan na hokar aadhyatmik anubhuti kaa dharaatal he. jise baikuntha dham janaa ho, uske liye gyaan hi ummid kii kiran he. isse vah ishwar ke swaroop se ekaakaar ho jaataa he. lekin jiske bheetar param gyaan he, bhagwaan ke prati anany bhakthi he, ve hi baikuntha pahunch sakate hai.
 vahiin maanviya jindagi ke liye baikuntha kii sarthakata dhunde to baikuntha kaa shabdic arth he - jahaan kuntha na ho. kuntha yani niskriyata, akarmanyata, niraashaa, hataasha, aalasy or daridrata. iskaa matlab yah hua ki baikuntha dham aisa sthaan he jahaan karmaheenta nahin he, niskriyata nahin he. vyaavhaarik jivan main bhi jis sthaan par niskriyata nahin hoti us sthaan par raunak hoti he.
 baikuntha ke naam hai- vishnulok,vaikunthadham, vaikuntha sagar aadi.
