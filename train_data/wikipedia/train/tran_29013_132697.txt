 vichaaron ke itihaas ke sandarbh main, satatya siddhanth kii pariklpana he ki madhyakaal, punarjagran kaal tathaa aadhunik kaal ke aarmabhik charnon ke bich bauddhik drushti se koi moolbhoot asatatya nahin he. is prakaar yah siddhanth punarjagran ke baad kisi vaigyanik kranti hone kii baat ko ek myth maanata he.
 satatya siddhanth ke samarthakon main duhem, saarton, johaan hingija, franklin, pasnau, graham, saliba, grant, hatfield or arun bala aadi pramukh hai.
