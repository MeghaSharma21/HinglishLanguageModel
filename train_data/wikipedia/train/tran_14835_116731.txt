 aditi arya, ek bhartiya model, anusandhan vishleshak, or saundarya pratiyogita sheershak dharak hai jinhen 2015 main femina miss world india kaa taj pahanaaya gaya tha. 
 aditi arya dilli main kaam karti hai. vah kai samooh jaise amitasha or protsaahan ke saath judy hui hai. 
 aditi ne femina miss india 2015 pratiyogita, dilli main hissaa liya. or unhen pratham ranor ap ghoshit kiya gaya. 28 march, 2015 ke din mumbai main ek samahroh main uske baad unhen epabibi miss india world 2015 ke vijeta ke rup main taj pahanaaya gaya. 
 femina miss india world kaa khitaab jeetne ke baad unhone miss world 2015 main bharat kaa pratinidhitav kiya. vah multimedia puraskaar main sheersh 5, pipuls chois award main sheersh 5, world faishan diziner dress puraskaar main sheersh 10, saundarya ke saath ek uddeshy puraskaar main top 25, pratibha up-pratiyogita main sheersh 30 main shaamil thi. 
 femina miss india 2015 ke baad aditi ne nandmuri kalyaan ram ke saath apni pehali tollywood film "ism" se apane kariyar kii shuruyaat kii.
