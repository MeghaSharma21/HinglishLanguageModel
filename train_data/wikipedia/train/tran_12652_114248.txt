 dow dwarika prasad sakesa hindi sahityakar tathaa samalochak the. ve prasad sahitya ke gabhir adhyeta the or unhone prasad sahitya kaa vistrut adhyayan-vivechan kiya he. 'kamayani main kavy, samskruti or darshan' naamak shodhaprabandh par unako piechdi mili tathaa 'prasad darshan' naamak shodhaprabandh par di-lit pradaan kiya gaya. dwarika prasad saxena un aalochakon va samikshakon main se hai jo prasad ko pakke yathartwaadee ke rup main dekhtaa/pahachaanata he. unhone mana/bataaya he ki prasad nirantar drushtaa tathaa prakhar manovishleshak the.
 dow saxena kaa janm mathura main hua tha. jab ve keval dhai varsh ke the tabhi unke pita shri ramswaroop saxena kaa dehant ho gaya. unki maan kalavati saxena ne gaanv ke atyant kathin paristhitiyon main unhen paala posha or bada kiya. 
 unhone lagbhag 28 vishaal granthon kii rachana kii he.
 unka jo kaam abhi tak prakashit ho sakaa he, usamen 'prasad darshan', 'ansu-bhashya', 'kamayani bhashya', 'prasad kaa muktak-kavy', 'kamayani main kavy samskruti or darshan', 'prasad ke naatakon main itihaas, samskruti, dharm, darshan or kala' aadi pramukh hai. duusare log jab prasad se 'batch' rahe the, tab unhone prasad kii rachanaaon se jujhane kaa kaam kiya. 
 unki kuch rachanaayen bhashavigyaan, darshan evam kavyashastra se sambandhit hai.
