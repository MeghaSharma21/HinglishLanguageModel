 yah lekh yaa kuch ansh apni mul bhashaa main he, jisase ki in tathyon kii moulikta bani rahe. kripaya ise anuwaad karne kaa prayaas na karen, yaa pehle samvaad prishth par charchaa karke phir sudhaar karen.'intaraashtriya maanakeekaran sanghathan, jise adhiktar ISO kaha jaataa he, vibhinn rashtron ke maanak sanghathanon ke pratinidhiyon se gathit ek anterrashtriya maanak-vinyas sanstha he. isaki sthapana 23 farvari, 1947 ko hui thi, vishwavyaapee audyogic evam vanijyik maanakon ko ghoshit karne hetu. iskaa mukhyalay geneva, switzraland main sthit he.
 yadhyapi ISO swayan ko ek gaiyr sarkari sanghathan kahata he, isaki maanak sthapit karne kii kshamta, jo ki praay vidhi ban jate hai, yaa to samjhauton ke dwaara, yaa phir rashtriya maan; yah isko gaiyr sarkari sanghathanon se adhik shaktishaalee banaata he. vaise vyavhaar main yah ek sankaay yaa alpakaaleen sanghathan jaise karyrat he, jisaki sarkaron se majboot kaniyaan hai..
 is sanghathan kaa logo yaa chihn do aadhikarik bhashaaon main he amareeki angreji evam french. isamen ISO akshar bhi sammilit hai, jinase yah praayah janaa jaataa he, jo ki isaki sankshipti he. balki yah sankshipti unani/greek shabd ἴσος se bana he, jiskaa arth he baraabar. yah maanate hue ki sanghathan kaa bhinn bhashaaon main bhinn sankshipti banegi, yah tay kiya ki ek sarvyaapee sarvamaanya naam ISO hi chalaaya jaae. yah swayan main is sanghathan kaa uddeshy siddh karata he.
 ISO ke mukhya utpaada hai anterrashtriya maanak. ISO dwaara hi prakashit hai : takneekee report, takneekee vishesheekaran, saarvajanik uplabdh vishesheekaran, takneekee shuddhipatr, evam and pathpradarshak.
 anterrashtriya maanak kaa roopakar hota he ISO nnnnn sheershak, jahaan nnnnn he maanak sankhya, yyyy he prakaashan kaa varsh, evam sheershak bataata he vishay. yadi maanak sanyukt takneekee samiti JTC1 ke kaary kaa parinaam he, to IEC bhi sammilit kiya jaataa he. ASTM International ke sahyog se viksit maanakon main ASTM ko bhi sammilit kiya jaataa he. dinaak evam IS kaa prayog apurna yaa aprakashit maanakon ke liye aprayogneey he, evam kuch paristhitiyon main prakashit kaary kaa sheershak bhi chhona ja saktaa he.
 takneekee report jari hoti he, jab koi takneekee samiti yaa up-samiti ne saamanyatya prakashit anterrashtriya maanak se bhinn prakaar kaa koi aankana sangrahit kiya ho, jaise ki sandarbh yaa vyakhyaayein. inaki namakaran pratha maanakon kii bhaanti hi he, keval TR lagaaya jaataa he IS ke sthaan par, report ke naam se pehle.udhaahrantah
 takneekee vishesheekaran ko tab rachit kiya ja saktaa he, jabaki "sambandhit vishay abhi bhi vikaas kii sthiti main hi he, yaa kisi anya karanvash bhavishya main kisi anterrashtriya maanak ko prakashit karne kii sahmati kii sambhaavna hoti he" . saarvajanik uplabdh vishesheekaran koi "madhyavarti vishesheekaran ho sakate hai, jo ki purna anterrashtriya maanak ke vikaas se purv hi prakashit hua ho", yaa, IEC main 'dwicihn' prakaashan, jo ki kisi bahari sanghathan ke sahyog se prakashit hua ho " donon ke hi namakaran takneekee report kii bhaanti hi kiya jaataa he. udhaahrantah
 ISO kabhi kabhi takneekee/paribhaashik shuddhipatr bhi jari karata he. ye vartmaan maanakon ke sanshodhan hote hai, jo ki kshudra takneekee/paribhaashik trutiyon, prayogneeyata sudhaar yaa simit tareeke se prayojyata badhaane hetu kiye jate hai. praayah inhen is aashaa ke saath jari kiya jaat he ki prabhavit maanak yato uddhrut yaa waapas liya jayega, apane agle anusoochna panravlokan tak. 
 ISO path-pradarshak ardh-maanak hote hai, anterrashtriya maanakeekaran se sambandhit vishyon ko liye hue. in kaa namakaran "ISO Guide N:yyyy: sheershak" main hota he, udhaahrantah
 ISO pralekh prakashnadhikaar surakshit hote hai, evam ISO adhiktar kii pratiyon kaa mulya letaa he. vaise adhiktar ke mulya keval electronic roopakar main hi liye jate hai. yadhyapi upyogi, parantu in pralekhon ko prayog karte hue yah dhyanyogy he, ki inaki prachur maatra main bhi parivartan kii sambhaavna he, isse pehle ki yah maanak hi naa ban jaae. ISO evam iske aadhikarik sanyukt rajya pratinidhi evam anterrashtriya vidyutatakneekee ayog dwaara kuch maanak ni:shulk uplabdh karaae jate hai.
 ISO ke 157 rashtriya sadsy hai, out of the 195 total countries in the world.
 ISO kii teen sadsyta shreniyan hai:
 bhagidaar sadsy "P" yaa pi sadsy kehalaate hai, jabaki anupaalan karne vaale "O" yaa o sadsy kehalaate hai.
 is tathy ne, ki kai ISO-srujit maanak sarvgat, sarvyaapee hai; kai maukon par, "ISO" kaa saamaanya prayog prerit kiya he, us utpaada ko varnit karne hetu, jo ki maanakon ko maanata he. iske kai udhaaharan hai:
 maanakeekaran ke kshetron main, prachur aachhadan ke parinaamon se, evam soochna praudyogiki se sambandhit karyon se, nibatane hetu; ISO evam IEC yaa anterrashtriya vidyutatakneekee ayog se ek sanyukt takneekee samiti gathit kijise ISO/IEC JTC1 ke nam se janaa jaataa he. yah asi pratham samiti he, evam ajtak ek hi he. 
 iskaa aadhikarik aadesh he, vishwavyaapee bazaaron main vyapaar evam upayokta kii aavashyaktaaon hetu, IT maanakon kaa vikaas, anurakshan, prachaar karnaa. 
 vartmaan main 18 up-samitiyaan hai :-
 ISO/IEC JTC1 kii sadsyta bilkul va lagbhag apane donon mul sanghathanon kii bhaanti hi pratibandhit he. ek sadsy yaa to bhagidaar yaa prekshak ho saktaa he. donon main keval prastaavit maanakon evam anya utpaadon par matdaan kii kshamta kaa hi bhed he. kisi sadsy sanstha ke liye kisi yaa prayek up-samiti par str/raay banaae rakhane kii, koi aavashyaktaa nahin he. yadhyapi yada-kada, parantu nai parsesthitiyon se saamana karne hetu up-samitiyaan gathit kii ja sakati hai, yaa bhang bhi kii ja sakati hai, yadi unka kaary kshetra ab lagu nahin he.
 ISO/TS jaise, antarrashtriya kaaryshaala samjhauta International Workshop Agreement bhi ISO kaa ek or shastraagaar he, jo ki un kshetron main, jahaan takneekee sanrachana evam nipunata abhi nahin pahaunchi he; vahaan maanakeekaran kii aavashyaktaaon par twarit pratikriya uplabdh karaata he. yah upyogita vishwavyaapee takneekee atyaavashyaktaa ko bharan karti he.
