 krishnakumar birla bharat ke prakhyat udyogpati or rajyasabha ke purv rajya sabha sadsy the. ghanashyam das birla ke putr krishnakanth birla kaa janm 12 oktober, 1918 ko rajasthan ke pilaani main hua tha. unke pita bhartiya swatantraaa sangram ke samay bhartiya rashtriya congress ke samarthakon or gandhi ji ke nikatwartiyon main shaamil the. unki ucch shiksha kolkata, dilli or punjab vishvavidhyalay se hui. ye 1984 se 2000 tak lagaataar 18 varshon ke liye rajyasabha ke sadsy bhi rahe or is dauraan sansad kii kai samitiyon kii adhyakshata kii. bhartiya chini udyog ke ve sansthaapak sadasyon main the. birla ke audyogic samrajya main chini, urvarak, rasayan, haivi ejineering, vastra, jahajrani or samachar-patr jaise mukhya udyog shaamil hai. krishna kumaar ek udyogpati hone ke alaava ek sammanit saansad, samajasevi or vidwaan vyakti the. 1961 main ve kalakatta ke sheriff chune gaye the or 1997 main unhen pandicherry vishvavidhyalay ne doctor of letters se sammanit kiya tha. unhone bhartiya chini udyog esocysan, federation of indian chambar of commers or indian chambar of commers kii bhi adhyakshata kii. unhone keke birla foundation kii sthapana kii, jo bhartiya sahitya, vaigyanik anusandhan or bhartiya darshanashastra main utkrisht pradarshan ke liye vaarshik puraskaron kii ghoshnaa karata he.
 hindustan times or birla samooh ke anek udyogon ke adhyaksh birla ke parivaar main unki teen putriyaan nandini nupani, shobhana bhartiya or saroj potdar hai. shobhana bhartiya bharat ke bade samachaarpatr hindustan times kii sampadakeey salaahkaar hai. unki patni manorama devi birla kaa inase ek maah purv july, 2008 main nidhan ho gaya tha.
