 haranava rajasthan ke nagaur jile kaa ek gaanv he. yah banu se maatr 7 kimi dur he. yahaan prasiddh viraanganaa ranabai kaa san 1543 main choudhary jaalamsingh dhun ke ghar main janm hua. 
 haranava he dev bhoomi 
 haranava kii sthapana harji ji bhaati ne kii.harji ji bhatner hanuman garh ke bhaati jat shaasha the.bhatner par hue akramanon se pareshaan hokar malwa  ja rahe the.raatri ho jane ke kaaran haranava main ruke. haranava main pagal nath ji apane dhune par tapasya kar rahe the.
