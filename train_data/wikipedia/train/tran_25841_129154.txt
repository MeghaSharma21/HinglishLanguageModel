 hindosthaan 19 ve sadi main kalakankar riyasat se prakashit hone vaala ek dainik samachar patr tha. kalakankar naresh raja rampal singh ne november, 1858 i. main aapane kalaakankar se hi 'hindosthaan' naam kaa hindi dainik nikaala jiskaa saptahik angreji sanskaran bhi isi naam se nikalataa tha. in donon patron ke sampadak raja rampal singh swayan hi rahe parantu sampadan kaa kaam pan. madanamohan malviya, pan. pratapanarayan mishra, balmukund gupt aadi karte the. angreji saptahik ke sampadan ke liye aap ek angarej sahakari ko england se laae the. 'hindosthaan' main shabdon kii vartanee saamaanya se bhinn rahati thi, jaise 'jitna' ko 'jyatna', 'kitna ko 'kyatna', 'manager' ko 'myanager'.
