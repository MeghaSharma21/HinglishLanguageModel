 sapana mukherjee hindi filmon kii parshavagayika hai. unke dwaara gaaye gayi geet filmi duniya main charchit hai. inkaa ek janaa pahachaanaa naam hain. inke sumdhur filmi giton ke liye kai baar puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya he. 1989 kii tridev ke gane tirchhi topi vaale ke liye unhen filmafere sarvshereshth parshv gayika puraskaar mila tha. sabase pehle unhen kalyanji-aanandji dwaara jaanbaaz film ke liye chunaa gaya tha.
