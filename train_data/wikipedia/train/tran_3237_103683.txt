 hindi ke prayog main tarah-tarah kii galatiyaan hoti hai. in galatiyon ke kaaran kabhi-kabhi bhashaa atpati lagati he; kabhi arth kaa anarth ho jaataa he to kabhi iske kaaran bhashaa clisht ho jaati he evam banaavati lagati he.
 galat - vinay ne kaha ki vah nahin aayegaa.
 sahi - vinay ne kaha ki main nahin aauungaa.
 galat - ladkaa, jo hans rahaa he, bahut pratibhashali he.
 sahi - hansata hua ladkaa bahut pratibhashali he.
 galat - main bhi ek aadmi hoon.
 sahi - main bhi aadmi hoon.
 ashuddh - "mai bahut garib hoon", kaha usne.
 shuddh -- usne kaha ki mai bahut garib hoon.
