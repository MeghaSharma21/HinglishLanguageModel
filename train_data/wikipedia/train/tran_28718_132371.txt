 meher chand khanna bharat ke ek rajaneta the jo 1954 se 1962 tak bharat ke punarvaas mantri tathaa 1954 se 1957 tak vidhi mantri rahe.
 meher chand khanna kaa janm peshawar main hua tha. unke pita karam chand khanna ek dhani vyavsaayee thi. unki vidyalayan shiksha peshawar main hi hui or peshawar ke hi edward college se snaatak hue. unhone vidhi snaatak kii bhi shiksha praapt kii or vakil ke rup main kuch dinon tak kaam bhi kiya.
 meher chand kam aayu main hi raajnneeti main aa gayi the. unhone hindu sabha banaayi. 1926 main ve hindu pratinidhi ke rup main peshawar ke cantonment board ke sadsy rahe.
