 kadru daksh prajapati kii kanya, maharishi kashyap kii patni. puranic itivrutt he ki ek baar maharishi kashyap ne kaha, 'tumhaari jo ichha ho, maang lo'. kadru ne ek sahasr tejasvi naagon ko putr rup main maanga . shwet ucchanshrava ghode kii punch ke rang ko lekar kadru tathaa vinata main vivaad chhidaa. kadru ne use kaale rang kaa bataaya. haarane par daasi hone kii shart thahari. kadru ne apane sahasr putron ko ajna di ki ve kaale rang ke bal banakar punch main lag jaay jin sarpon ne uski ajna nahin maani unhen usne shaap diya ki pandavavanshi budhimaan rajarshi janamejay ke sarpastr main prajvalit agni unhen jalaakar bhasma kar degi. shighragamini kadru vinata ke saath us samudr ko laanghakar turant hi ucchanshrava ghode ke paas pahunch gai. shwetvarna ke mahavegshali ashv kii punch ke ghaneebhoot kaale rang ko dekhakar vinata vishaad kii murti ban gai or usne kadru kii daasi hona sweekaar kiya. kadru, vinata tathaa kadru ke putr garuda kii peeth par baithkar naglok dekhne gaye. garud itani oonchaai par ude ki sarp surya taap se moorchhit ho uthe. kadru ne meghavarsha ke dwaara taapshman karne ke liye indra kii stuti kii.
