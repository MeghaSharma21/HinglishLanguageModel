 dilli vishvavidhyalay ke do parisar hai jo dilli ke uttari or dakshini bhaag main sthit hai. inhen kramash: uttari parisar or dakshini parisar kaha jaataa he. dilli vishvavidhyalay kaa uttari parisar main dilli metro kii pili line ke saath suniyojit dhang se judaa hua he or metro stesan kaa naam 'vishvavidhyalay' he. uttari parisar dilli vidhaan sabha se 2.5 kimi or antarraji bas adde se 7.0 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he.to vahiin iskaa dakshini parisar gulabi line se judaa he or metro stesan kaa naam 'durgabai deshmukh south campus 'he|
 dilli vishvavidhyalay bharat ke sabase pramukh shikshan sansthaanon main se ek he or apane ucch stareey shikshan or shodh ke liye desh videsh ke chaatro ko aakrisht karata he. isaki sthapana san 1922 main ek ekaatmak, shikshan awasaiya vishvavidhyalay ke rup main kendriya vidhayika dwaara hui thi. tab is vishvavidhyalay main sirf teen college/mahavidhyalay : saint steffens college, hindu college or ramajas college hua karte the or 750 vidhyaarthiyon ke saath isaki shuruyaat hui thi. lekin aaj chaatro ke namakan kii drushti se yah duniya ke sabase bade vishvavidhyalayon main se ek he. hari singh goud is vishvavidhyalay ke pehle upkulapati bane. or shuruaati daur main hi chotii ke vidwaanon jaise dody.es. kothaari, tii.aar. sheshadri, pi.maheshwari or em.el. bhaatia ityadi is vishvavidhyalay main aaye. aaj 79 kalege is vishvavidhyalay se sambhddha hai.
 hindi vibhaag
 hindi vibhaag ke sansthaapak adhyaksh dow nagendra the. ve do dashak se bhi zyada adhyaksh pad par rahe. ve hindi ko ek gyananushasan ke rup main viksit or sthapit karanevaale vidwaan ke rup main jane jate hai. unhone hindi ko sanskrit se swatantra ek alag gyaan vibhaag ke rup main pratishtha di. sahitya or bhashaa ke taur par hindi kii peedi ke saath-saath anuwaad or jivan ke anya kshetron main hindi ke anya upyogon main prashikshan kii bhi vyavastha unhone aarambh kii.
 aaj hindi vibhaag main har varsh alag-alag paathyakram main pravesh lenevaale chaatr-chaatraon kii kul sankhya lagbhag ek hajaar kii hoti he. yahan bhashaa, anuwaad, patrakarita ke vividh paathyakram sanchalit hote hai.
