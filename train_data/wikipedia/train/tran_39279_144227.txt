 vichaarak samooh yaa think tanks us sanstha ko kehete hai jo saamaajik niti, rajnatik ranneeti, arthniti, sainya niti, praudyogiki or samskruti jaise vishyon par gambheer vyaavhaarik chintan karatin hai. inhen anusandhan santhaan yaa niti santhaan bhi kehete hai.
 adhikansh think tanks praayah laabh-nirpeksh sansthaayen hotin hai or in sansthaanon ko uese aadi deshon main kar se chhot bhi di jaati he. kuch anya vichaarak samoohon ko sarkarein fund deteen hai, kuch ko bade vyavsaay fund dete hen, kuch ko aidvocacy samooh paise dete hai.
