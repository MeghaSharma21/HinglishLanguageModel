 naiman yaa naiman turk yaa naiman mongol madhya asia ke stapi ilaake main basane vaali ek jati kaa mongol bhashaa main naam tha, jinke kaara-kitaan ke saath sambandh the or jo san 1177 tak unke adheen rahe. mongol naam hone ke baavjood, nymanon ko ek mongol jati nahin balki ek turki jati mana jaataa he. us jamaane main ve uigur or kitaani logon kii tarah buddha dharm or isai dharm kii nestorian shakha ke anuyaayi hua karte the.
 san 1203 main unke khan kaa mongol samraat changez khan se yuddh hua jisamen vah maaraa gaya. tayan khan kaa betaa kuchlug khan apane bache-kuche naiman sainikon ko lekar kaara-kitaan ke paas sharan maangane gaya. use satkar se panaah di gai or kitaan khan ne apni beti kii shaadi bhi us se karwaai. kuchlug ne ehsaan-farramoshi kii or apane hi sasur ke viruddh shadyantr rachakar use marava diya or satta par qabza kar liya. uske baad vah apane kshetra ke kuch muslim shahriyon par zulm karne laga jis se vidroh bhadkaa or mongolon ne mauqaa paakar use haraa daalaa.
 iske baad nymanon kaa raaj to khatm ho gaya lekin ve mongol samrajya ke kai bhaagon main bas gaye. vartmaan kaal main kazakstan ke lagbhag 4 lakh log naiman jati ke hai. kuch naiman afghanistan main bhi milte hai, jahaan ve hazaaraa samudaay ke bhaag hai or sheikh ali vaadi main rahete hai.
