 hyderabad rajya, british bharat kii riyasat thi. isamen vartmaan telugana, marathavada, uttar karnataka, vidarbha ke kuch bhaag sammilit the. san 1724 se san 1948 tak nizaam hyderabad rajya ke shaasha the. swatantraaa senaniyon ke pradhirgh hyderabad mukti sangram ke uparant 1948 main bharat sarkaar dwaara nizaam shashan ke viruddh police karwai karke hyderabad ko bharat main samahit kar liya gaya.
 17 sitambar 1948 kaa din bharat ke swatantraaa sangram ke itihaas main vastav main ek nirnaayak mode tha. hyderabad kii janata kii samuhik ichha-shakti ne na keval is kshetra ko ek swatantra desh banane hetu nizaam ke prayaason ko nishphal kar diya balki is praant ko bharat sangh main milaane kaa bhi nishchay kiya. jab 15 agast 1947 ko puraa bharat swatantraaa diwas manaa rahaa tha to nizaam ke rajasi shashan ke log hyderabad rajya ko bharat main milaane kii maang karne par atyaachaar or daman kaa saamana kar rahe the. hyderabad kii janata ne nizaam or uski nijee sena 'rajakaron' kii krurta se nidar hokar apni aazaadee ke liye puure josh se ladaai jari rakhi.
 bharat ke tatkaaleen grahamantri evam 'louh purush' sardaar vallabhbhai patel dwaara police karrwai karne hetu liye gaye saahasik nirnay ne nizaam ko 17 sitambar 1948 ko aatm-samarpan karne or bharat sangh main sammilit hone par majaboor kar diya. is kaarywai ko 'aaparishan polo' naam diya gaya tha. isliye shesh bharat ko angreji shashan se swatantraaa milane ke baad hyderabad kii janata ko apni aazaadee ke liye 13 maheene or 2 din sangharsh karnaa padaa tha. yadi nizaam ko uske shadyantra main safal hone diya jaataa to bharat kaa nakshaa vah nahin hota jo aaj he.
