 greenlandic bhashaa ek esquimau-eliut bhashaa he, jo greenland ke bahusankhyak rahavaasiyon dwaara boli jaati he. yah canada main bole jane vaale inyut bhashaa kii nikat sambandhi he. is bhashaa ko bolne vaale logon kii sankhya takreeban 54,000 he, jo anya esquimau-eliut bhashaa ko milakar bolne vaale logon kii sankhya se adhik he. is bhashaa main se sabase zyada prachlit boli pashchimi greenlandic he, jo greenland kii aadhikarik bhashaa he. uttari boli inuktun bhashaa kwana shahar ke aas-paas boli jaati he. iske alaava purvi greenlandic or uparnavik boli he.
