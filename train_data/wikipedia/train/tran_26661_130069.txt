 paitrisia ain hell nepal 
 ve.es. naypaul yaa vidyadhar surajprasad naypaul aadhunik yug ke prasiddh angreji sahityakar the. unhen nutan angrezi chand kaa guru kaha jaataa he. 2008 main di times ne ve es nepal ko apni 50 mahaan british sahityakaro kii suchi me 7vaan sthaan diya. unhen 2001 me sahitya me nobel puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya.
 ve kai saahitiyik puraskaron se sammanit kiye ja chuke hai, jinamein jone lilavelin rij puraskaar, di somerset mogam award, di hovethoradan puraskaar, di dablu ech smith saahitiyik award ,] bukar sammaan, tathaa di david kohen puraskaar, pramukh hai. 
 vidyadhar surajprasad nepal kaa janm 17 agast san 1932 ko trinidad ke chagavanas main hua. inkaa parivaar naam nepal desh par aadhaarit he, atah nepal, "jo nepal se ho". aisi dharna he ki inke purvaj gorakhpur ke bhoomihaar brahman the jinhen trinidad le jaaya gaya isaliye is parivaar kaa nepal ko chhodna isse pehle hua hoga. trinidad main janme bhartiya mul ke nobel puraskaar vijeta lekhak hai. unki shiksha trinidad or england main hui. ve dirghakaal se britain ke nivasee hai. unke pitaaji shriprasad nepal, chote bhai shiv nepal, bhattije nil bisunadat, chachere bhai vahni kapildev sabhi nami lekhak rahe hai. pehle patrkaar rah chukin shrimati nadira nepal unki patni hai.
 inke anuj shiv nepal bhi bahut achche lekhak the.
 inkaa sabase mahaan upanyaas "e haus for mistar biswas" he. 
 nepal parivaar or kapildev parivaar trinidad main bahut prabhaavshaalee rahe hai.
 11 agast 2018 ko raat main landan sthit apane ghar main 85 varsh kii aayu main unka nidhan hua.
