 da prince of egypt 1998 main bani america anemated sangeet drama film he. yah dreamworks dwaara paaramparik tareeke se nirmit pehali anemeshan film he. yah exods kii pustar par aadhaarit he va moses ke jivan kii kahani bayaan karti he jo egypt ke ek rajkumaar se kis tarah israel ke bacchon ko egypt se baahar le gaya. film kaa nirdesan brenda chapman, simon wales or steve hiknar ne kiya tha. film main stephen shwarthz dwaara likhit va haans zimer dwaara dhvanit geet shaamil he. film main hollywood ke kai diggaj abhinaytaon kii awaz shaamil he.
 film ko "when yu bilive" geet ke liye 1999 ke academy puraskaar main sarvshereshth nayaa geet or sarvshereshth dhwani ke liye namakan mila tha. iskaa pop sanskaran samaaroh ke dauraan whitney hoston or maaria kerri ne gaaya tha.
 film ko cinemagharon main 18 disambar 1998 ko release kiya gaya tha. isane vishwa bhar main $218,613,188 kaa vyavsaay kiya jisase yah $100 million kaa aankada paar karne vaali duusari gaiyr-dijani film ban gai.
