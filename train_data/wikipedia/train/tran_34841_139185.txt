 5,33,600 
 taiwani adivasee taiwan ke mul nivasee hai. ve ek austronishian samudaay hai or philipinoz, malaysia, indonesia, madagascar tathaa oshiania ke anya austronishian bhashaayen bolne vaale samudaayon se anuvaanshik va bhashiya sambandh rakhte hai. taiwani adivasee china ke haan chini logon se bilkul bhinn hai or itihaaskaaron kaa anumaan he ki ve taiwan par pichle 8,000 varshon se based hue hai. inaki mul boliyaan formosi bhashaayen kehalaati hai. san 2014 main taiwan kii sarkaar ne inaki kul aabaadi 5,33,600 anumaanit kii gai thi jo taiwan kii puri jansankhya kaa 2.3% tha.
