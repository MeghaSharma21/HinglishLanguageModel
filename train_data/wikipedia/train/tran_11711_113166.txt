 berilands ek dakshinpashchimi landan main kingston apon tems baro kaa nagar he. berillands ek awasaiya pados he jo mul rup se surbitone ke nagar baro kaa hissaa he, or 1965 se tems par kingston ke royal baro kaa hissaa he. yah ek upnagareey vikaas he jo chering cross ke dakshin pashchim main sthit 10.1 milesthit he. aasapaas ke sthanon main surbaton, new malden, old malden, tolworth or chessington shaamil hai berilland railway stesan train se landan waterlu se 24 minute kii duuri par he. barilands anglo-sackson mul kaa ek samjhauta he jo tems ke najadik he. bariland mul rup se surbitone ke nagarpalika kaa hissaa ban gaye, haalanki 1965 main ise tems par kingston ke royal baro ke hisse ke rup main shaamil kiya gaya tha.
