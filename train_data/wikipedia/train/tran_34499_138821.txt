 ayushman khurana hindi filmon ke ek abhinayta hai. ayushmaan khurana ek televijan ankar, film abhinayta or gayak hai. 2012 me ayushmaan khurana ne shujit sarkaar kii romani hasyapradhan film viki donor me kaam kiya tha jo kii shukranu daan tathaa banjhapan par aadhaarit thi. is film ko cinema samikshakon ne bahut hi saharaaya tha. iske pashchaat ayushmaan kii ek or film 'bareli kii barfi' jari hui thi. 2017 main unki film shubh mangal saavdhaan jari ho rahi he.
 ayushmaan chandigarh ke nivasee hai. unhone apni schuli shiksha saint jon hai skool tathaa college kii padhai daw college, chandigarh se kii he.
