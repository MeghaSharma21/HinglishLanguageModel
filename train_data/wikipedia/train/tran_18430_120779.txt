 belladonna atropa belladonna naamak paudhe se paaya jaataa he jo kii gahare andhere ke naam se prasiddh he. yah paudha himalay kii 6 se 12 hajaar foot kii oonchaai vaale parvatiy shetron main paaya jaataa he. is paudhe kaa sampoorn bhaag jehrila hota he jo kii isamen upasthit atropine naamak alkaloyad ke kaaran hota he. is paudhe ke phool bengani rang ke hote he or thode bal sugandhit hote he. yah ek neurotoxin he jo mastishk par apana prabhaav daalata he. is paudhe kii pattiyaan or jade atyadhik jehrili hoti he parantu adhikanshat: prakaran durghatanavash pattiyon aadi khaane se paae jate he. is paudhe kii ghaatak maatr 100 se 200 milligram atropin or ghaatak kaal 24 ghante hoti he.
