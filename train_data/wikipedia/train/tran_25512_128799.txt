 swetlana savitskaaya ek sevanivrutt soviet aviator or antariksh yatri he jinhone 1982 main soyuj tii-7 par savaar hokar antariksh main udaan bharne vaali duusari mahila thi. swetlana savitskaaya kaa janm 8 agast 1984 ko hua tha. unke pita soviet sainya commander the. swetlana ne apane airospes kariyar kii shuruyaat test or khel payalat ke rup main kii thi.  
 1974 main apane kariyar kii shuruyaat main hi unhone 18 antarrashtriya vishwa record MiG vayuyan par banaae or 3 record team parachute jumping par bhi banaae. 1970 main jab FAI kii chhathi vishwa arobtic championships hui tab unhone pratham sthaan praapt kiya. or unki antariksh yatra 1980 main prarambh hui. 
 1982 main swetlana ne soyuj T-7 mishan ke hisse ke rup main antariksh ke liye udaan bhari or us mishan main unke saathi the leonirh popov or cerebrove the. swetlana savitskaaya antariksh main jane vaali duusari aisi mahila antariksh yatri thi jo kii 19 saal ke baad velantina tereshkova ke baad antariksh main gayi thi. 25 july 1985 main apni antariksh kii duusari udaan ke dauraan swetlana ne antariksh main saiyr karne vaali pehali mahila kaa khitaab bhi hasil kiya. unhone 2010 main 3 ghante or 35 minute ke liye Salyut 7 antariksh stesan ke baahar ek eva kaa aayojan kiya jisame unhone dhaatuon ko kaanta or veld bhi kiya apane 57 roosi soviet sahyogiyon ke saath. prithvi par lautne par savitskaaya ko antarrashtriya mahila diwas ke uplakshya main sabhi mahila soyuj chaalak dal kaa commander bhi banaaya gaya.savitskaaya 1993 main roosi vaayu sena se major ke pad ke saath sevanivrutt hui.
