 oman kii khadi, jise arabi main calij uman or farsi main calij-e-makraan kehete hai, arab sagar or hormuz jalsandhi ke bich sthit ek jaldamru he. yah hormuz jalsandhi ke paar faras kii khadi se judata he. haalanki ise khadi bulaayaa jaataa he, bhugolic rup se yah vastav main ek jaldamru he. iske uttar main iran or pakistan kaa makraan kshetra he jabaki iske dakshin main sanyukt arab emirate or oman sthit hai. faras kii khadi kii tulana main isaki gaharaai kaaphi adhik he.
