 guangazo,, yah up-prantiy shahar va guangdong praant kii rajdhani he. yah china ke paanch rashtriya kendriya shaharon main se ek he. yah nagar or iske aasapaas ke kshetra, vishesh rup se is nagar or hong kong ke madhya ke kshetra, saadhaaran rup se apane angrezi naam canton ke naam se jane jate hai. yahan parl nadi par ek bandargah he jo dakshini china sagar kii ore navy he or yah hong kong se 120 kimi uttarpashchim main sthit he. 2014 kii janaganana ke anusaar is nagar kii kul jansankhya 4.4 karon he or mahanagareey kshetra kii 1.5 karon he jo ise praant kaa sarvaadhik or chini mukhya bhoomi kaa teesraa sarvaadhik jansankhya vaala nagar banaata he. prantiy sarkaar ke aadhikarik anumaan ke anusaar 2006 main yahan kii jansankhya 97,54,600 thi. guangazo kaa nagareey kshetrafal beijing or shanghai ke baad china main sabase adhik he. 2008 main ise beta world city ghoshit kiya gaya he.
 sarvapratham is kaa nirmaan panyu shahar ke rup main 214 i.pus. main hua tha. iske baad 206 i.pus. main yah nanyu samrajya kii rajdhani bani, jo kii ab vartmaan vietnam main padataa he.
 haan rajvansh ne nanyu-samrajya pe 111 i.pus. main kabja karke ise apni prantiya rajdhani banaaya, jo kii ab tak he. 226 isvi main panyu guang praant kii rajdhani bani, iske pashchaat sthaneey nivaasiyon ne ise guangazo kahana shuru kar diya.
 dakshin china main sthit hone kii vajah se isaki jalwaayu nam up-ushnkaribandheey he. garmia main taapmaana va nami kaaphi zyada hoti he tathaa sardiyaan halki va sukhi hoti hai. guangazo main monsoon kaaphi lambaa hota he.
 guangazo parl nadi delta kaa sabase bada audyogic kendra he. iskaa china kii arthvyavastha main bahut bada yogadan he.
 pipuls daily akhbaar ke anusaar, cantonies guangazo shahar kii lagbhag aadhi aabaadi kii pratham bhashaa he, va shesh mandarin bhashaa kaa istemaal karte hai. bahut saare log hakkaa bhashaa kaa bhi prayog karte hai.
 guanganou shahar 16wein asian khelon kaa safal aayojan kar chukaa hai.
