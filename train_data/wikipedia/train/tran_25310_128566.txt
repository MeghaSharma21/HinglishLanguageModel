 piku du phogu varde antareep kaa sarvocch shikhar he, jisaki oonchaai samudr tal se upar 2829 meter ke lagbhag he. phogo dweep par yah ek sakriy mishrit jwalamukhi he. iske mukhya shanku se pichhlaa udgaar 1675 main hua tha, jo dweep se bade paimaane par hue utpravas kaa kaaran bana, jabaki iske ek sahaayak shanku se 1995 main lava dadhak uthaa tha. iskaa ekmaatr ghaatak visphot 1847 main hua tha jiske parinamswaroop sabhi dweep par aaye bukamp ne kai logon kii jaan lee thi.
 piku du phogu kii pahari dhalaanon par coffee kii kheti hoti he, jabaki isse udgaarit lava ko nirmaan saamagree ke rup main prayog kiya jaataa he. iske shikhar ke nikat iskaa jwalamukh-kund or ek chota sa gaanv, chaa das caldyraas, is jwalamukh-kund ke andar sthit he.
