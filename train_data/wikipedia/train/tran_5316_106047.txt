 jarmaini bhashaa parivaar hind-europi bhashaa parivaar kii ek shakha he. is parivaar kii sari bhashaaon kii sanjhi purvaja "aadim jarmaini" naam kii ek kalpit bhashaa he. itihaaskaar anumaan lagate hai kii aadim jarmaini bhashaa louh yug main lagbhag 800 isapurv ke kaal main uttar europe main boli jaati thi. angrezi isi bhashaa parivaar kii sadasya he or isaki anya jaani-maani bhashaayen german, dach or scandinavia kshetra kii bhashaayen hai . kul milakar vishwa main lagbhag 56 karod log kisi jarmaini bhashaa ko apni maatrubhaasha ke rup main bolte hai. duniya main angrezi ke failaav ke kaaran lagbhag 2 arab log kisi jarmaini bhashaa ko bol sakate hai .
