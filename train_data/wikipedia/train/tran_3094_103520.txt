 brigadier ,ek sainya rank he, jisaki varishthata desh par nirbhar karti he. kuch deshon main yah karnal ke upar ek varisht ranking he, jo ek brigadier general ke baraabar hoti he,jo visheshkar kam se kam ek hajaar sainikon kii brigade ko niyantrit karata he. anya deshon main, yah ek gaiyr-kamishan rank he .
 kai deshon main, vishesh rup se british samrajya ke purv ang, brigadier yaa to sabase oonchi field rank yaa sabase junior "general " niyukti he, namatah ek brigade ko commanding karata he yah karnal ke upar or major general ke neeche kii rank he .
 rank kaa istemaal british sena, royal marine, australian sena, bhartiya sena, srilanka sena, newjiland sena, pakistan sena or kai anya deshon dwaara kiya jaataa he. nato senaaon main, brigadier kramshah paimaane par o fif -6 kaa he .
