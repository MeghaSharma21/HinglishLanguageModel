 kheechad ek jat jati kii gautra he jo bhartiya praant rajasthan ke jhunjhunu, churu, nagpur, ganganagar, hanumangarh or sikar jilon main pai jaati he. veerabhan kheechad ne rajasthan ke sikar nagar kii sthapana kii. unhone haryana ke sirsa or fatehabad jilon kii bhi sthapana kii. khichadon kii utpati joiya se maani jaati he. sambhavtah wo chouhanon kii khichi gautra ke samaan hai.
 gotra siddhanth ke udbhav ke bare main yah mana jaataa he ki haryana ke raja jinke teen putr the. ek kheechad tha duusare kaa naam mahala evam tisare kaa naam kulahari tha. ye teenon putr rajasthan main aa gayi. manyataon ke anusaar mahala jhunjhunu ke gaanv bhalriya main rahane lage. vahaan se wo rajasthan ke anya bhaagon main basate gayi. isi prabhaav ke anusaar mahala or khichadon ko bhai mana jaataa he or aapas main vaivaahik sambandh sthapit nahin karte.
