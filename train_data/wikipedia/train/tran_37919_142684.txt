 gudgaanv, bhartiya rajya haryana kaa chatha sabase bada shahar he. yah haryana ke 4 pramandalon main se bhi ek he. gudgaanv haryana kaa odyogic or weetiya centre he. gudgaanv bharat kii rajdhani dilli se 30 kilometre, dwarka se 10 kilometre, chandigarh se 268 kilometre dur he. gudgaanv dilli ke chaar pramukh upgrah shahro main se ek he or rashtriya rajdhani kshetra kaa ek hissaa he. gudgaanv dilli se rashtriya rajmarg or dilli metro ke madhyam se seemaa saanjhaa karata he.
 gudgaanv kaa bharat main prati vyakti aay main chandigarh or mumbai ke baad teesraa sthaan he. gudgaanv bharat kaa ekmaatr pehla esa shahar he jiske pratek ghar main bijli kii purti hoti he. bijanas today magazine ke dwaara gudgaanv ko bharat main rahane ke liye 11 sthaan praapt he. pichley 25 saalo main gudgaanv ne bahut teji se pragati kii he or apane aap ko duniya ke naqshe par sthapit kia he.
 gudgaanv kaa naam hindu grantho main bhi miltaa he. gudgaanv gaanv, jo kii shahar ke ekadam madhya main he, guru dronacharya kaa gaanv he. yahi par guru dronacharya ne pandavo or corwo ko shiksha di thi. pandav or korva, hindu granth mahabharat ke paatr he. gudgaanv kaa puranic naam gurugram he, arthath guru kaa gram | guru dronacharya ko gurugram pandvo or corwo ne uphaar swarup diya tha, jo kii rishi bharadwaj ke putr the. mahabharat main darshaya gaya kua, jisame pandavo or corwo kii gend chali gai thi, abhi bhi guru dronacharya college ke andar mojood he.
 itihaas main gudgaanv par hameshaa se dilli par raaj karne vaale raajaaon kaa hi adhikaar rahaa he jese kii rajput, yaduwanshi, mogul, maratha aadi. 
 2001 kii bharat kii janaganana ke anusaar gudgaanv kii jansankhya 2,28,820 he. bharat kii rajdhani dilli se apni nikatataa ke kaaran gudgaanv ne pichley dashak ke dauraan bade paimaane par tarakkee kii he. gudgaanv ke viksit hone main uskaa ek pramukh outsorcing gantavy hona va uttari bharat main achal sampatti bazaar ke rup main ubharna bhi ek mukhya kaaran he.
 pracheen hindu puranic kathaon main gudgaanv kaa ullekh ek mahatvapurn nagar ke rup main miltaa he. yah dilli ke chaar pramukh upnagaron main se ek he isliye ise bharat ke rashtriya rajdhani kshetra kaa ek hissaa mana jaataa he. gudgaanv ko rashtriya rajdhani kshetra kaa ek sabase abhijatya kshetra bhi ek mana jaataa he. pichley kuch varshon main shahar kaa atyadhik vikaas hua he tathaa desh ke bheetar ek outsorcing gantavy ke rup main viksit hone ke atirikti is kshetra ne ek achal sampatti main aayaa ek abhootpurv oochaal dekha he.
 gudgaanv kii sthapana 15 agast 1979 i. ko kii gai thi. mahabharat kaal main raja yudhisthir ne gudgaanv ko apane dharmaguru dronacharya ko uphaar swaroop diya tha or aaj bhi unke naam par ek talab ke bhagnavshesh tathaa ek mandir pratik ke taur par vidyaman hai. is kaaran iskaa naam gurugaanv padaa tha. baad main samay ke saath iskaa naam gudgaanv ho gaya.
 gehun, tilhan, bajra, jowar or dalhan mahatvapurn fasalen hai.
