 
 karmanshah iran kaa ek praant he. 1969 se 1986 tak ise 'karmaanshahan' or 1986 se 1995 tak ise 'bakhtar' ke naam se janaa jaataa tha.
 karmanshah praant main 14 shaharistaan hai. isaki rajdhani 8.2 lakh kii aabaadi vaala karmanshah shahar he jo safeed kuh pahaad kii dhalaanon par basaa hua he. yahan ek vishesh prakaar kaa irani qaaleen banataa he jiskaa naam hi karmanshah qaaleen he. is ilaake kii chaaval se bani 'naan birenji' mithai prasiddh he or yahan se 'rogan karmaanshahi' naam kaa milane vaala tel iran-bhar main mashhoor he. idhar ek 'give' naam kii khaas juuti bhi banati he.
 is praant main bahut se pracheen khandhhar milte hai jo hakhaamani samrajya or saasaani samrajya ke jamaane main nirmit hue the. inamen bistun shilaalekh prasiddh he jo darush mahaan ne banwaaya tha or jisamen teen bhashaaon main likhaa he. isi shilaalekh ke zariye puraani farsi main prayog hone vaali ankan lipi samajhi ja pai he.
