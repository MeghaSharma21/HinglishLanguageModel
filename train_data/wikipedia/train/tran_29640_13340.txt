 shithily yaa 'hysterisis' padaarthon yaa tantra kaa vah gun he jiske kaaran koi output, keval input par hi nirbhar nahin karata balki nivesh evam nirgat signal kii purv sthitiyon par bhi nirbhar karata he. duusare shabdon main kah sakate hai ki shithilyyukt kisi padaarth yaa tantra main smriti hoti he.
 bhautikee main bahut se nikaay apni prakritik main shithily pradarshan karte hai. udhaaharan ke liye loha kaa chumbakan shithily prakat karata he.
 louhachumbakeey padaarthon main chumbakeey shithily bahut jaani-pahachaani cheez he. jab kisi louhachumbakeey padaarth ke upar koi vahya chumbakeey kshetra lagaaya jaataa he to iske aanvik dwidhruv swayan ko us vahya kshetra kii disha main ghuma lete hai. kintu us kshetra ko shunya kar dene ke baad bhi in paramanvik dwidhruon kaa kuch bhaag usee disha main bana rahata he. isi kaaran lohe ko chumbakit karne ke baad uskaa chumbakatv anant kaal tak bana rah saktaa he. or yadi ise vichumbakit karnaa ho to iske liye ulti disha main vahya chumbakeey kshetra lagaana padataa he. chumbakeey shithily ek prakaar kii smriti ke sadrush he jiskaa upyog computer ke aarmabhik dinon main smriti ke rup main kiya jaataa tha.
