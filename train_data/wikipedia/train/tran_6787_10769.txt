 banaras riyasat bharat main british raaj ke samay maujuud ek riyasat thi. 15 oktober 1948 ko is riyasat ke tatkaaleen raja ne bhartiya sangh main milane ke liye samjhaute par hastakshar kiye.
 kaashi riyasat kii jade kaashi rajya tak jaati hai jo 1194 tak ek swatantra rajya tha. iske shaasha bhoomihaar brahman the. 1775 main yah angrejon ke adhikaar main aa gaya. san 1911 main yah ek riyasat ban gayi.
