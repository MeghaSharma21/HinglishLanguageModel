 nakaaratmak daab shwasayantr jise praayah louh fefde ke naam se bhi sanderbhit kiya jaataa he, shwasayantr kaa ek prakaar he jo fefdon kii mansapeshi niyantran khone se saans lene main asamarth yaa kamjor hone kii sthiti main vyakti ko saans lene main samarth banaata he. ab nakaaratmak daab shwasyantr kaa sthaan puri tarah se dhanatmak daab shwasa yantra yaa bipasik curas swasayantr ne le liya he. 
 manushya bhi adhikans praaniyon kii tarah nakaaratmak dabaav paddati se saans letaa he. fefdon kii peshiyaan anwarat rup se failati or sikudti rahati he. peshiyon ke failane par fefdon kaa aakaar bad jaataa he. isse vahaan bahari vaataavaran kii tulana main hawa kaa dabaab kam rah jaataa he. isse vaataavaran se vaayu naak kii shwaas nalee se hokar fefdon main pahunch jaati he. phir mansapeshiyon ke sankuchit hone par uchachch daab nirmit ho jaataa he or vaayu fefdon se nirishwaas ke rup main baahar chali jaati he. kisi kaaran sankuchan or vistaar karne vaali peshiyaan yadi kamjor pad jaaen, niyantran main na rahen to vyakti ke liye saans lenaa mushkil yaa asambhav ho jaataa he.
 louh fefde kaa prayog karne vaale vyakti ko ispaat ke band belan main niyantrit daab main rakhaa jaataa he. vyakti kaa sir tathaa gardan baahar or shesh sara sharir vayuruddh sthiti main belan ke bheetar rahata he. belan se sambhddha pump niyamit rup se belan main hawa daalkar or nikaalkar vyakti ke chaaron ore ke or khaas taur par fefdon ke paas chaati par vayudab ko badhaata or ghataata rahata he. vayudab kam hone kii sthiti main bahari vaataavaran kii hawa swasanalee ke raste fefdon main pravesh kar jaati he belan ke bheetar kaa daab badhane par fefdon kii hawa baahar nikal jaati he.
