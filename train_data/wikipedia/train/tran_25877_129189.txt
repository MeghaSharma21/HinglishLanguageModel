 dinesh nandan sahay tripura or chhattisgarh ke rajyapal the.
 inkaa janm madhepur, bihar main ek madhya vargeey parivaar main hua tha. inaki mata kaa naam kishori devi or pita kaa naam dev nandan sahay he. inkaa paalan poshan patna main hua tha. inhone english main apana em e puraa kar ech di college, arah main pravaktaa ke rup main apana kariyar prarambh kiya or iske baad san 1960 main bhartiya police se jud gaye. inhone manju sahay se shaadi kii tathaa inkaa ek betaa or do betiyaan hai.
 seva nivritti ke baad ye samta party se jud gaye.ye chhattisgarh ke pehle rajyapal the, 2000 se 2003 ke dauraan. ye san 2003 main tripura ke rajyapal bane.
