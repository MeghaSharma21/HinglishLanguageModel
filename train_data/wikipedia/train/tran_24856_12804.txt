 upasthi maanav sharir evam anya praaniyon main paaya jane vaala lacheela sanyoji uttak he. yah hamaari majja main sthapit condrocites koshikaon se bane hote hai. kaan kii haddi, naak kii haddi, asthiyon ke jod aadi upasthi ke bane hai.
 upasthi kii sanrachana ke anusaar ye colejan yaa alostin ke bane hote hai. ye teen prakaar ke hote hai- hylin upasthi, elastic or fibro upasthi. upasthi sharir ke utakon ko majboot banane kaa kaam karte hai. ye hamaare sharir ke jodon ko lacheela bhi banaate hai. isaki maujuudagi kii vajah se hi hamaare sharir ke kai ang sucharu rup se kaam karte hai. upasthi rakt vahikaaon se jude nahin hote balki inke andar poshak tatv bikharaa rahata he. aamtaur par ye lacheele hote hai lekin jarurat or prakaar ke hisaab se inaki prakruti main antar hota he. kuch aise ang jinamein upasthi paaya jaataa he, ve hai- kaan, naak, panjar or intervartebral discs.
 teen tarah ke upasthi main hylin ko hi aamtaur par upasthi kaha jaataa he kyonki sharir main zyaadaatar hylin upasthi hi hota he. yah haddiyon ko jodon main baantta he taaki ve mudakar sahajata se kaam kar saken. hylin upasthi aamtaur par colejan fibre kaa bana hota he. lochadar upasthi baaki anya upasthi se zyada lacheele hote hai kyonki inamen alostin fibre paaya jaataa he. is tarah kaa upasthi kaan ke bahari hisse or eustacian tub main maujuud hota he. lacheela hone ke kaaran yah in angon kii sanrachana ko behatar santulit karata he, jisase kaan main baahar rahane vaali golakaar sanrachana khuli rah sake. fibro upasthi teenon upasthi main sabase adhik majboot or drudh sanrachana vaala hota he. isamen hylin upasthi se zyada tip van colejan hote hai, jo tip second se zyada majboot hote hai. fibro upasthi antarvartibal discs kaa nirmaan karte hai. iske alaava ye shiraaon or asthimajja ko haddiyon se jodne kaa kaam bhi karte hai. hylin upasthi kshatigrast hone par fibro upasthi main badal jate hai. haalanki dridhtaa kii vajah se in upasthi kaa vajan kam hota he.
