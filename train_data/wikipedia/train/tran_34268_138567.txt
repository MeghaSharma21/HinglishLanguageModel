 
 ardra yaa bitaljus, jiskaa bair naam α orynis he, kalpurush taramandal main sthit ek laal mahadaanav tara he. yah us taramandal kaa doosraa sab se chamkila tara or prithvi ke akaash main aathvaan sab se chamkila tara he. aadra prithvi se lagbhag 640 prakaash-varsh dur he lekin tezi se hill rahaa he isliye yah duuri samay ke saath-saath badalati rahati he. iskaa nirpeksh kantimaan -6.05 magnitude anumaanit kiya jaataa he . yarkis varnakram shreneekaran main isko ek "M2Iab" kaa tara bataaya jaataa he. aadra kaa dravyaman 18-19 M☉ or ardhavyas lagbhag 1,180 R☉ he.
 khagolashastri maanate hai ke ardra keval 1 karod saal kii aayu kaa he lekin apane atyadhik dravyaman kii vajah se tezi se apane jeevankram se guzar rahaa he. vaigyanik anumaan lagate hai ke yah kuch hi lakhon varshon main bhayankar visphot ke saath mahanova ban jayega. aisa bhi sambhav he ke yah pichley 600 varshon ke andar fat chukaa ho, lekin uskaa prakaash ham tak pahonchte-pahonchte sainkadon saal guzar sakate hai. is samay jo aadra ham aasmaan main dekhte hai vah 640 varsh puraani chhavi he. jab ardra fatega to iske peeche ek 20 kimi ke vyas kaa neutron tara rah jayega.
 ardra ko angrezi main "bitaljus" or marathi main "kakshi" kaha jaataa he. is taare ke hindi naam "ardra" kaa matlab hota he "nam" or yah naam "rudra" se sambandh rakhata he.
