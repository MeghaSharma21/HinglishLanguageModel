 vattatouttu  ek abugida lekhan pranali he jiskaa upaj dakshin bharat or shri lanka ke tamil logon dwaara hui. is uccharan-aadhaarit varnmaala ke 6thi sadi se 14veen sadi ke bich ke sakshya vartmaan kaal ke bhartiya rajyon tamil nadu or kerala main milte hai. baad main isaki jagah aadhunik tamil lipi or malayalam lipi ne le lee. vattatouttu jaise vyapak shabd kaa prayog jorge koids va dijii haul jaise dakshinpurv asia adhyayan karne vaale vidwaanon ne kiya he. 
 duusari sadi tak tamil ko tamil brahmi main likhaa jaataa tha. baad main tamil ke liye is lipi kaa prayog hone laga. tamil brahmi bhi brahmi aadhaarit lipi hi he. is gol lipi kaa prayog kerala main tamil, pracheen-malayalam va malayalam bhashaa likhane ke liye bhi kiya jaataa tha. is samay malayalam ke liye malayalam lipi kaa prayog hota he.
 tamil bhashaa ke 300 i.pus. se 1800 i.pus. ke shilaalekh milte hai or inamen samay ke saath kai badlaav hue hai.
 granth lipi main vattaluttu ke mukaable adhik akshar hai. isamen or tamil lipi main kai samantaayein hai sanskrit likhane ke liye lekin tamil ke mukaable granth main adhik akshar hai.
