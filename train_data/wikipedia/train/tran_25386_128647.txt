 honshu japan kaa sabase bada dweep he. yah tsugaru jaldamru ke paar hokkaido dweep se dakshin main, seto bhitari sagar ke paar shikoku dweep ke uttar main or kanamon jaldamru ke paar kyushu dweep se purvottar main sthit he. honshu duniya kaa saatva sabase bada dweep he or indonisia ke java dweep ke baad vishwa kaa doosraa sarvaadhik jansankhya vaala dweep bhi he. japan kii rajdhani tokyo honshu ke madhya-purv main sthit he. honshu par san 2005 main 10.3 karod log rah rahe the. iskaa kshetrafal 2,27,962 varg kimi he, jo britain se zaraa bada he or bharat ke uttar pradesh rajya se zaraa chota he.
 japani bhashaa main 'hon shu' kaa arth 'mukhya praant' hota he.
 honshu kaa adhiktar bhaag ek pahari ilaaka he jisapar bahut se jwalamukhi bhi faile hue hai. is dweep par aksar bukamp aate rahete hai or march 2011 main aaye zalazale ne puure dweep ko apni jagah se 2.4 meter hilaa diya tha. japan kaa sabase unchaa pahaad, 3,776 meter lambaa fuji parvat, honshu par sthit he or ek sakriya jwalamukhi he. is puure dweep ke madhya main kuch parvatiy shrinkhlaayein chalati hai jinhen samuhik rup se 'japani alps' ke naam se janaa jaataa he. honshu par kai nadiyaan sthit hai, jinamein japan kii sabase lambi nadi, shinano nadi bhi shaamil he.
 honshu ke madhya purv main 'kanto maidan' he, jahaan baari krishi kii parampara he or udyog bahut viksit he. tokyo shahar isi maidan main sthit he. tokyo ke alaava osaka, cobe, nagoya or hiroshima jaise kuch anya mahatvapurn nagar bhi honshu par hi sthit hai. kanto maidan or nobi maidan naamak ek anya maidani kshetra main chaaval or sabziyon kii baari paidaavaar hoti he. inke atirikt honshu par seb or anya fal bhi ugaae jate hai.
