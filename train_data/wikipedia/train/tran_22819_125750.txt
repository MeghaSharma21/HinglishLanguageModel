 namakan prakriya :
 mongolian tii wyeth milk mongolian sabase zyada pi jane vaali chaay main se ek he or isko banane main minute tak lag sakate hai. samigri : - pani, doodh, namak, hari chaay ke patton
 mongolian tii wyeth milk banane kii vidhi : ek katori le or pani or namak daale or acchhee tareeke se ubaale. pani ko ubaal jane ke baad doodh milaaye or phir se chizon ko garam karen or mishran ubal jane ke baad chaaye ke patto ko alag karen.
