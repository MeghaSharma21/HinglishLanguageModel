 sharnaarthi yani sharan main upasthit asahaay, laachaar, niraashray tathaa raksha chaahane vaale vyakti yaa unke samooh ko kehete hai. ise angreji bhashaa main refugee likhaa va sambodhit kiya jaataa he. is prakaar vah vyakti vishesh yaa unka samooh jo kisi bhi karanvash apana gharbaar yaa desh chhodkar anyatra ke sharnangat ho jaataa he, vah sharnaarthi kehalaata he. 
 udhaaharan ke liye syria main jang chhidane kii vajah se vahaan ke lakhon naagarik duusare mulkon main sharnaarthi banakar sharan le rahe hai.
 bharat main sharnaarthiyon ke aane kaa itihaas bahut purana he or bharat main based kanoonee or gaiyrkanoonee sharnaarthiyon kii jansankhya kai viksit deshon kii jansankhya se bhi zyada he.
 aamtaur par anbhigyata vas log kai baar ke liye bharat or pakistan ke vibhaajan ke samay pakistan ke hisse vaale punjab, sindh aadi pradeshon ke lakhon log jo apana gharbaar chhodkar bharat ke vibhinn bhaagon main aakar bas gayi the unhen bhi sharnaarthi maan lete hai, parantu yah ghatana purna rup se sharnaarthi shabd ko paribhaashit nahi karata,kyonki jo log pakistan se bhagkar bharat main bas gaye unhen vahaan ek naagarik ke taur par rahane kaa adhikaar vibhaajan ke samay diya gaya tha.
 bharat main vibhaajan ke baad aaye logon ko chhod de to bhi azaadi ke baad kaaphi sharnaarthi aaye. udhaaharan ke liye tibet ke sharnaarthi or bangladesh se aaye kanoonee gaiyr-kanoonee sharnaarthi.
