 pi satsivam
 nyaayamurti shri pi satsivam
 shri palanisami satsivam vartmaan main kerala ke rajyapal he tathaa purv bharat ke mukhya nyaayadhish rahe hai.,
 shri satsivam ne 25 july 1973 se madras main vakaalat shuru kii. 8 janvari 1996 ko ve madras ucch nyaayalay main nyaayadhish bane tathaa 20.04.2007 ko unka sthanantaran punjab va haryana ucch nyaayalay main ho gaya. 21.08.2007 ko unki niyukti ucchtam nyaayalay ke nyaayadhish ke pad par hui or 19 july 2013 ko unhen bharat ke mukhya nyaayadhish ke pad par niyukt kiya gaya. ve 27 april 2014 ko ve sevanivrutt hue tathaa unke pashchaat is pad par shri rajendra mel lodha asin hue.
