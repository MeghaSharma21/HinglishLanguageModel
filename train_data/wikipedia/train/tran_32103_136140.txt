 shimshaal paak-adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-balatistan kshetra ke hunzaa-nagar zile kii gozaal tehsil main sthit ek gaanv he. yah 3,100 meter kii oonchaai par basaa hua he or hunzaa vaadi kii sabase unchi basti he. shimshaal gaanv main vaakhi bhashaa bolne vaale lagbhag 2,000 log rahete hai jo shiya dharm kii ismaili shakha ke anuyaayi hai. yahan pahunchnaa bahut kathin hua karata tha lekin oktober 2003 ke baad passu se yahan sadak banaakar ise karakoram rajmarg se jod diya gaya.
 gaavan se upar 4,735 meter unchaa shimshaal darra sthit he. yah sindhu nadi or madhya asia kii tarim nadi ke jalsambhar kshetron ke bich sthit he. kisi jamaane main hunzaa se aane vaale daaku is darre se guzarakar leh-yarakanda maarg par bharat or purv turkistan ke bich chalane vaale vyapaarik kaafilon par hamalaa kiya karte the. yahan ek chota-sa durg bhi bana hua tha jiskaa prayog kabhi to yah daaku or kabhi hunzaa-naresh ke sainik kiya karte the. aajkal yahan paryatakon ke liye ek yaks-dhawan pratiyogita yojit kii jaati he. is darre ke paar shimshaal braldu nadi kii vaadi he jo shaxgam nadi kii ek upanadi he.
 shimshaal ke ird-gird kai buland parvat khadey hai jinamein distagil sar, odaver sar, minglik sar, lupagar sar, yajanil sar or kanjut sar shaamil hai. is kshetra main kai himaniyaan bhi maujuud hai jinamein malangudhi, yajanil, khurdopin, braldu, odaver or ver airav jane-maane hai. in naamon main binduyukt 'kh', binduyukt 'ga' or binduyukt 'ji' ke uccharanon par dhyaan den. itane parvaton, himanion or darron se ghire hone ke kaaran sthaneey shimshali log parvatarohan main maahir maane jate hai or karakoram shrunkhlaa par chadhane vaale unhen maargdarshan ke liye aksar saath le lete hai.
