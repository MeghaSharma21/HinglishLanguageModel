 hinduon main brahman, rajput, mahajan, kysth, jat, chaaran, bhaat, sunar, darji, luhaar, khaati, kumhaar, taili, mali, nai, dhobi, gujjar, kanjar, ahir, bairangi, gonsai, swami, daakot, kalaal, lekhara, chheenpa, sevak, bhagat, bhadbhoonja, raigar, mochi, chamaar aadi kai jatiyaan hai. brahman, mahajan aadi kai jatiyon kii anek upjatiyaan bhi ban gai he, jinamein paraspar vivaah sambandh nahi hota. adivaasiyon main meena, baawari, thori aadi hai. ye log kheti or mazdooree karte hai. musalamaanon main shaikh, saiyad, mughal, pathan, kaayamkhaani, raath, johia, rangarej, bhishti or kunjade aadi kai jatiyaan hai.
 yahaan ke adhikansh log kheti karte hai. shesh vyapaar, naukari, dastakaari, mazdooree athwa len-dein kaa kaary karte hai. pashu paalan yahaan kaa mukhya pesha he. parijaade or rarh jati ke musalmaan is dhandhe main lipt he. vyapaar karanevaali jatiyon main pradhan mahajan hai, jo dur-dur sthanon main jaakar vyapaar karte hai or ye varg sampann varg bhi he. brahman visheshkar puja-paath tathaa purohitai karte hai, parantu koi-koi vyapaar, naukari tathaa kheti bhi karte hai. kuch mahajan krishi se bhi apana nirvaah karte hai. rajputen kaa mukhya pesha sainik-seva he, kintu kai kheti bhi karte he.
