 soons samudri standhari jeev hai or timi tathaa shinshumar ke nikat sambandhi hai. inke 17 vansh or 40 prajatiyaan hai. inkaa aakaar 1.2 me evam 400 ki.gra se lekar 9.5 me 10 ton tak ho saktaa he. ye vishwa bhar main pai jaati hai, khaas taur par mahadwipiya jalseema ke uthle sagariya kshetron main. ye maansaahaari hoti hai or choti machhliyon or vidrupon ko khaati hai. sitesian gana main delfinidi sabase bada or apekshaakrit naveen kul he. suunso kaa pradurbhaav prithvi par lagbhag 1 karod varsh pehle miosin kaal ke dauraan hua tha. soons prithvi ke kuch sabase adhik budhimaan jeevon main se ek he or unke aksar dostana vyavhaar or hameshaa khush rahane kii aadat ne unhen maanvo ke bich khaasaa lokapriy bana diya he. ganga nadi main paayi jane vaali soons ko bharat sarkaar ne bharat kaa rashtriya jaleey jeev ghoshit kiya he.
