 purvi irani bhashaayen irani bhashaa-parivaar kii ek upashakha hai jo madhya irani kaal se ubharin. avastai bhashaa aksar is shakha kii ek pracheenatam sadasya maani jaati he. aadhunik kaal main sab se zyaada boli jane vaali purvi irani bhashaa pashto he, jiske duniya main lagbhag 5 karod maatrubhaashi hai. yah afghanistan or pashchimottaree pakistan ke kaibar-pakhtuunakva praant main boli jaati he. iske alaava purvi tajikistan ke kuhistoni-badakhshaan swashasit praant or china ke sudur pashchimi shinjiyang praant main bhi purvi irani bhashaayen boli jaati hai. pracheen soudai se viksit hui pashchimottaree tajikistan kii yagnobi bhashaa or skithi-saramati se viksit hui cocks kshetra kii oseti bhashaa bhi donon purvi irani bhashaayen maani jaati hai.
