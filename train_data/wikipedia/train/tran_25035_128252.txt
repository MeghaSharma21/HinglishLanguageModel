 anterrashtriya maanak ISO 80000 yaa IEC 80000, ISO 31 or IEC 60027 kaa uttaradhikari he. yah bhautik ikaayiyon ke prayog or maapan kii ikaayiyon kii, evam unamem sanlagn sutron kii sarvaadhik prashansit style sandarshika he. yah vaigyanik or shaikshik pralekhon main vishwavyaapee prayukt hoti he. adhiktar deshon main ganit vigyaan kii paathya pustakon evam vishvavidhyalayon main iskaa purna paalan kiya jaataa he.
 ISO/IEC 80000 maanak samooh abhi pragatisheel hi he or iske kuch hi bhaag abhi tak 2008 ke sanskaran main prakashit hue hai. yah bhi prastaavit he, ki SI ke sang prayukt hone vaali matraaen or samikarnon ko anterrashtriya maatra pranali kaha jaye.
