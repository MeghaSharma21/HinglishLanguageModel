 bharat main cartoon kala kii shuruyaat british kaal main maani jaati he or keshav shankar pillai jinhen "shankar" ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he ko bharat cartoon kala kaa pitamah kaha jaataa he. shankar ne 1932 main hindustan times main cartoon bananaa prarambh kiye. shankar ke alaava aar ke lakshaman, kutti menon, ranga, abu abraham or mario miranda aise naam he jinhone bhartiya cartoon kala ko aage badhaaya or pahchaan di. shankar ke baad dhire dhire cartoon kala kaa bharat main vikaas hua or aaj bharat main har praant or bhashaa main cartoonist kaam kar rahe hai. bhartiya cartooniston ke bich saanskrithik or vyavsaayik gatividhiyon ko badhaava dene, cartoon kala kii sarthakata evam sakaaraatmakataa ke liye ek pragatisheel manch pradaan karne ke lakshya se ek panjikrut sanghathan cartoonists club of india kii bhi sthapana kii gayi
