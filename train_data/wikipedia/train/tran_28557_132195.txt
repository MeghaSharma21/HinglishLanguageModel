 rasil kii ladaai; Battle of Rasil: rasil kii ladaai 644 isvi main rashidun khilaafat or raay rajvansh ke virudhd ladi gayi thi or dakshin asia main rashidun khilaafat kii pratham muthbhed thi or ladaai kaa sateek sthaan gyaat nahin he lekin itihaaskaaro kaa mat he kii sindhu nadi ke pashchim tat par ladi gayi thi. is abhiyaan kaa netruv calipha hazrat umr ne suhail ebn adi ko saunpa tha suhail 643 isvi main vasra iraq se chalakar makraan av vartmaan pakistan kaa hissaa he jaha aaye yah kshetra sadiyon ke liye sasadiyano farsiyo kaa paramparik kshetra tha lekin raay rajvansh ke shaskaon ne farsiyo par akraman karke vijay praapt kii inke baad rashidun khilaafat ne akraman kar is kshetra ko jeet liya lekin ise arab musalamano ne jitne ke vaad vahut jaldi hi ganvaana padaa tha.
