 aathven asian khel 9 disambar se 20 disambar, 1978 ke bich bainkak, thailand main yojit kiye gaye the. pakistan ne bharat or bangladesh ke saath vivaadon ke chalate in khelon kii mejambani sthagit kar di. vittiya kaarnon ke chalate singapore khelon kii mezbaani karne se peeche hat gaya.
 thailand ne sahaayta karne kii peshkash kii or isliye asiad bainkak main yojit kiya gaya. raajnneetik morche par, israel ko asian khelon se nishkasit kar diya gaya. kul 25 asian deshon ke 3,842 khilaadiyon ne in khelon main bhaag liya. pehali baar sammilit kiye gaye khel the teerandaazi or gendbaaji.
 strot : kul padak sthiti - bainkak 1978
