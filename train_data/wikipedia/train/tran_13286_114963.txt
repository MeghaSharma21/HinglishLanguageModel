 gaanche main tibeti mul ke balati va laddakhi log rahete hai. saanskrithik rup se yah bharat ke laddakh kshetra kaa bhaag he, haalanki yahan ke adhiktar log dharmik drushti se muslim hai. yahan adhiktar balati bhashaa boli jaati he jo laddakhi bhashaa ke qareeb he or aksar tibeti bhashaa kii upbhasha samajhi jaati he.
 gaanche kaa mausam bahut sard he or sardiyon main yahan kaa taapmaana -20 °centegrade se bhi neeche chalaa jaataa he. pakistan dwaara niyantrit sabhi kshetron main se yahan kaa taapmaana sabase kam he. shyoke nadi ke kinare vistrut khapalu vaadi is zile kaa mukhya aabaadi-centre he. khapalu se hote hue maarg hushe vaadi ko jaataa he or vahaan bhi ek mahatvapurn basti he.
