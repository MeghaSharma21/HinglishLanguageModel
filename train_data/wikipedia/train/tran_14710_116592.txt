 shabari mandir bharat ke chhattisgarh praant main sthit kharud nagar ke dakshin pravesh dwaar par sthit shauri mandap shabari kaa hi mandir he. eent se bana purvabhimukh is mandir ko saurine dai athwa shabari kaa mandir bhi kaha jaataa he. mandir ke garbhagrih main shriram or lakshmana dhanush baan liye virajman hai. pujari ne bataaya ki shriram or lakshmana ji shabari ke juthe ber yaheen khaaye the. tatkaaleen sahitya main ullekh miltaa he ki nagar ke dakshin disha main shabari devi kaa mandir sthit he. eent se bana yah mandir purvabhimukh he. lakshmaneshwar mahadev mandir ke shilaalekh main iske nirmaan ke kaal kaa ullekh karte hue kaha gaya he ki gangadhar naamak amaatya ne ek shauri mandap kaa nirmaan karakar punya kaa kaary kiya he. shauri vastav main vishnu kaa ek naam he or yah kshetra bhi vishnu pratimaaon ke kaaran shri narayan kshetra yaa shri purushottam kshetra kehalaata he, jiskaa ullekh skanda puraana main miltaa he. is mandir ke garbhagrih ke pravesh dwaar par garuda ji kii murti he. at: iske vishnu mandir hone kii kalpana kii ja sakati he. samprati yahan devi kii murti he. is mandir main devi kii murti kab or kisne sthapit kii, yah pataa nahin chalata. sambhavat: vishnu or shakti main saamanjasy sthapit karne ke liye is mandir main devi kii sthapana kii gai he. pracheen kaal main yah kshetra dandakaarany kehalaata tha. dandakaarany main shriram lakshmana or janaki ne kai varsh vyateet kiye. isi kshetra se sita kaa haran hua tha. shriram or lakshmana ne sita kii khoj ke dauraan shabari ke ashram main aakar uske juthe ber khaaye the, isi prasang ko lekar is sthaan ko shabari-narayan kaha gaya. kaalaantar main yah bigadakar shivarinarayan kahalaane laga. pracheen kaal main is kshetra main khar-dooshan kaa raaj tha, jo shriram ke haathon veergati ko praapt hua tha. kadachit unheen ke naam par yah nagar kharud kahalaaya.
