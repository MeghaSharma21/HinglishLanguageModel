 hemlata gupta ek bhartiya chikitsak or ladi harding medical college ke chikitsa vibhaag kii pramukh chikitsak or nirdeshika thim. unhone medical kii padhai ladi harding medical college, dilli vishvavidhyalay se kii or baad main vah uski nirdeshak ban gayi. bharat sarkaar se unhen chikitsa vigyaan main yogadan ke liye tisare sarvocch naagarik sammaan, padam bhushan se 1998 main sammanit kiya gaya. vah avivaahit thim or nai dilli main rahati thim jahaan par 13 may, 2006 ko unhen, unke nivas uttam nagar main mrit paaya gaya tha. kai varshon kii jaanch ke baad, jis maamle ne media kaa dhyaan aakarshith kiya, vah maamla aaj bhi ansulajhaa he.
