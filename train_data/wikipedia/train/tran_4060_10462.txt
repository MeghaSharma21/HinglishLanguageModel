 koshika linepraathmik koshikaon ke up-sanvardhan se praapt sanvardhan, koshika line yaa cell line kehalaati he.
 yah do prakaar kii ho saktin hai:
 jo saamaanya koshika se praapt hotin hai, tathaa simit aayu kii hotin hai. inhen finite cell line bhi kehete hai.
 jo arbudi koshika yaa malignant cell ke sanvardhan se praapt hotin hai. inhen continus cell line bhi kehtehain. ye sadaiv jivit rah saktin hai.
