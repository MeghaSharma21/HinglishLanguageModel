 saamanytah varsh kii samaapti ke baad vyapaaree dwaara samast lekhon ko yoga kar unhen band karne kii kaarywahi kii jaati he evam samast khaaton ke shesh nikaale jate hai. in sheshon ke aadhaar par ek suchi taiyaar kii jaati he jise shesh pareekshan yaa talapat yaa pariksha suchi kaha jaataa he. 
 is talapat main debit sheshon kaa jod credit sheshon ke jod ke baraabar hona chaiye. yadi talapat ke donon pakshon kaa yoga baraabar nahin he to iskaa arth yah he ki lekhon main ganitiya ashuddhi he jise khoj kar dur kiya jaataa he. baad main talapat ke aadhaar par antim lekhe taiyaar kiye jate hai.
 weetiya vivaran patrak ko taiyaar karne se pehle talapat taiyaar kiya jayega. talapat aisi suchi he jisamen khatabahi ke vibhinn lekha shirshon ke credit evam debit sheshon ko bataaya jaataa he. talapat main likhit debit evam credit sheshon ke yoga main samanta, vyavhaar kii ganitiya shuddhtasunishchit karnaa he. sabhi khaaton ke shesh uplabdh ho jane se weetiya vivaran patrak taiyaar karne main sahaayta hoti he.

 saamaanya khatabahi se talapat taiyaar karte samay nimna baaten dhyaan main rakhni hogi :-
 chunki har debit pravishti ke samatuly credit pravishti kii jaati he tadanusaar debit shesh ke vibhinn lekha shirshon kaa shesh, credit shesh ke samast lekhon ke shesh ke samatuly hoga. jab ki saamanytah talapat yatharthata kii pehali pariksha he.
 kabhi-kabhi donon pakshon arthath debit evam credit kaa jod baraabar hota he phir bhi trutiyaan hoti he. nimna trutiyon kii jaankaree talapat se nahin ho paati he :-
 yadi talapat kaa yoga baraabar na ho to galatiyon kaa pataa lagane hetu nimnanusaar kadam uthaae jate hai.
