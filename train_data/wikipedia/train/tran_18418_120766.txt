 sukhoi esayu-30emkeke  sukhoi esayu-30 kaa ek sanshodhan he, jisamen sukhoi esayu-35 sanskaran se unnat takanic shaamil kii gai he. rus or china ke bich nivida ke liye pratyaksh anurodh ke parinamswaroop sukhoi esayu-30emkeke ko 1997 main sukhoi ne viksit kiya tha. yah ek baari shrenee vaala, sabhi mausam me kaam karne vaala, lambii duuri kaa strike ladaaku vimaan he yah sukhoi esayu-30 kii tarah he. yah american mcdonelle duglas epa-15i strike eagle kii takkar kaa vimaan he. sukhoi esayu-30emkeke me unnat-uddyan or samudri strike kshamtaaon ko bada kar ek or nayaa isi shrenee kaa vimaan banaaya gaya. jise sukhoi esayu-30emke2 kaha jaataa he. emkeke or emke2 vartmaan main chini vaayu sena, indonesian vaayu sena, vietnam pipuls vaayu sena, venezuela vaayu sena or uganda vaayu sena dwaara sanchalit he.
