 lagbhag 30 karod
 arab log yaa arabi log ek maanav jatiyon kaa samooh he jo mukhya rup se pashchimi asia or uttar africa main rahete hai. arab log mukhya rup se arabi bhashaa or samskruti se jude hue hai haalanki aitihaasik rup se 'arab' kii paribhaasha main pher-badal aayaa he. kuch kaalon main yah shabd keval badu logon ke liye prayog hua he jabaki anya kaalon main barbar log bhi isamen shaamil rahen hai. yadi 'arab logon' kii sabase vistrut paribhaasha lee jaae to inaki jansankhya 30 karod se adhik he or kuch sroton ke anusaar yah haan chiniyon vishwa kii duusari sab se badi maanav jati he.
 'arab' naam ke strot or arth ko lekar vidwaanon main vivaad he. yah sab se pehle 9veen sadi isapurv ke ek akkadi bhashaa ke shilaalekh main mila he jisamen asyrian samrajya ke syria ko 'karkar ke yuddh' ke baad qabze kar lene kaa varnan he. isamen asyria ke raja shalmanser tritiy kii faujon dwaara luti chizon main 'gindbu arab ke 1000 oont' bhi shaamil hai. yah gindbu is yuddh main asyrian ke viruddh ladane vaale mitrapaksh main arab gut kaa sardaar tha.
 'arab' naam kii utpatti kii sabase lokapriya kahani yah he ki yah ek 'yarab' naamak aadmi se aayaa he jo arabi bhashaa bolne vaala pehla vyakti tha. pracheen arab buddhijivi al-hamadani kii alag raay thi. unka kahana tha ki kyonki arab log mesopotamia se pashchim main basate the, isliye unke liye 'garab' shabd istemaal hone laga, jiskaa arth arabi or anya sthaneey bhashaaon main 'pashchim' hota he. yahi bigadakar 'arab' ban gaya. ek anya pracheen arab itihaaskaar, al-masudi, kaa kahana tha ki shuru main yah naam 'arabah vaadi' ke nivaasiyon ke liye tha jo vistrut hokar puri jati ke liye prayog hone laga.
 haalaanki bahut se log arab pahchaan ko muslim pahchaan jaisaa hi maanate hai, vastav main yah do alag chizein hai. bahut se arab isai hote hai or kuch yahudee bhi hote hai. vartmaan kaal main 7% se 10% arab logisai dharm ke anuyaayi hai.
