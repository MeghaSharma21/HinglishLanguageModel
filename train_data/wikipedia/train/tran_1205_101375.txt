 ajay sunil darshan dwaara nirmit or nirdeshit 1996 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. sani deol, karishma kapur, rina roy or sadashiv amarapurkar mukhya kalakaar hai. anya kalakaaron main kiran kumaar, suresh oberoi, mohanish bahal, sharat saxena, farida zalaal, lakshmikant berde or anya shaamil hai.
 maharaja or ranvir manorama urf rama ko abhibhavak ke rup main chaahate hai. ve use jo bhi pasand he dete hai lekin vah unki maang se inkaar kar detii he or jo chaahe karti he. ranvir durga ko garbhvatee kar detaa he or apane bade bhai maharaja ke saamne inkaar kar detaa he. durga gusse main kahati he ki vah unhen barbaad kar degi. ek baar ajay rama ko dekhtaa he or pehali najar main uske saath pyaar ho jaataa he. lekin rama usase nafrat karti he. maharaja or ranvir donon chaahate hai ki rama roopesh se shaadi karen kyonki vah bhi ek amir vyakti he. lala maharaja or ranvir kaa mukhya aadmi he. uskaa betaa rupapayya ajay kaa ek acha mitra he. durga maharaja or ranvir ko nasht karne kii koshish karti he. ajay kii bahan kii shaadi ke samay ranvir police nireekshak bhejtaa he or use chori ke liye giraftaar kiya jaataa he. is bich ajay ko giraftaar kiya jaataa he or ranvir or maharaja ajay kii maa ko pareshaan karte hai.
