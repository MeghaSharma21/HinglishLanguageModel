 sankhya 1729 ko ramanujan sankhya yaa hardi-ramanujan sankhya kaha jaataa he. yah vah sankhya he jise do sankhyaon ke ghanon ke yoga ke baraabar likhaa ja saktaa he. is prakaar kaa gun rakhane vaali bahut hi kam anya sankhyaayein hai.
 shrinivas ramanujan ek prakaar se sankhyaon ke jaadugar the. sankhyaon ke saath unka gaharaa sambandh tha. ham kah sakate hai ki ve sankhyaon ke saath khelte the. shrinivas ramanujan jab england main the to aksar biimaar rahete the. unke paramarshdaata ji.ech. hardi ne, na keval unki pratibha ko duniya ke saamne laane main nirnaayak bhoomika nibhaai, balki unke videsh pravaas ke dauraan unke swasthya kaa dhyaan bhi rakhaa. lagbhag pratidin hi vah ramanujan ko thik se khaanaa khaane or niyamit rup se davaaiyaan na lene ke liye daantate the. aise hi ek din jab ramanujan aspataal main the to hardi unase milane aae. hardi us din bahut udhaas lag rahe the. ramanujan ne unase poocha, ‘‘aap itane pareshaan kyon lag rahe hai, aaj to main apekshaakrit pehle se behatar hoon.’’
 hardi ne uttar diya, tum to sankhyaon ke jaadugar ho, parantu aaj main jis taxi main aayaa hoon mujhe uskaa nambar bahut hi neeras laga. ‘‘kya number tha?’’ ramanujan ne poocha. hardi ne kaha, ‘‘uskaa number tha 1729. turant ramanujan ne uttar diya, shaayad 1729 se adhik rochak sankhya to koi ho hi nahin sakati. aisi kuch hi sankhyaayein hai jinhen do ghanon ke yoga ke rup main do alag-alag dhang se likhaa ja saktaa he or 1729 unamem sabase choti sankhya he.’’
 tab se yah sankhya ramanujan sankhya ke naam se prasiddh ho gai. 
 vastav main hi yah sankhya ek atyant vishisht sankhya he. isaki vishistataa ke kuch pahaluon ko aage spasht kiya gaya haih
 tathaapi, yadi ham rinatmak purnaakon par bhi vichaar karen to yah shart puri karne vaali sabase choti sankhya 91 hogiah
 sanyogvash 91 sankhya 1729 kaa ek gunankhand bhi he.
