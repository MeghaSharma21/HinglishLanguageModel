 ek arohan maarg yaa arohan path yaa chadhaai kaa rasta vah rasta hota he jiske dwaara ek parvatarohi kisi pahaad, chattan, yaa barf shila kii chotii tak pahunchataa he. vibhinn maarg naatakeey rup se mushkil or bhinn ho sakate hai or ek baar agar koi chotii par pahunch bhi jaye to vahaan par thaharnaa or avarohan mushkil ho saktaa he. maarg ke vikalp mahatvapurn ho sakate hai. nirdeshak pustika, yadi uplabdh ho to isame die gayi margon ke vistrut chitra or tasviren sahaayak siddh ho sakate hai.
