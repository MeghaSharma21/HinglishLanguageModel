 raashid khan armaan, aamtaur par rashid khan ke naam se jane jate hai, vah ek afgaan cricket khilaadi he jo rashtriya team kaa pratinidhitiv karte he. rashid sunrisers hyderabad ke liye 2017 indian premier lig main khele the jo vartmaan main bhi sunrisers hyderabad team main shaamil he. june 2017 main, unhone ek divasiy antarrashtriya match main sarvshereshth gendbaaji kii. farvari 2018 main, vah isisi player vanade ranking main gendbaajon main sabase kam umr ke khilaadi ban gaye. baad main usee maheene, unhone tii twanti main gendbaajon kii isisi player ranking main bhi sheersh sthaan hasil kiya.
 raashid khan ne indian premier lig ke 11wein sijan main sunrisers hyderabad kii taraf se kaaphi prabhaavshaalee gendbaaji karte hue 21 wicket liye the or purple cap andrew taay ke sabase zyada wicket lene kaa record inhen ke naam tha.
