 ana lee fisher america rasayanajna, chikitsak or NASA kii antariksh yatri he.
  unka janm 24 agast 1949 ko new york shahar main hua tha. unhone apni grajuation 1967 main san padro hai skool se kii thi. unhone apane purv antariksh saathi bill fisher se shaadi kii thi or vah 1984 main antariksh main sabase pehali maan bani. nasa main apane karier ke dauraan, unhen teen pramukh kaaryakramon ke saath shaamil kiya gaya:spes shatal, antarrashtiya spes stesan, orion project. fisher ko janvari 1978 main ek antariksh yatri ke ummidavaar ke rup main chunaa gaya tha. agast 1979 main, unhone apane prashikshan or moolyankan kii avadhi puri karke, antariksh shatal mishan udaan ke karmidal ke visheshgy ke rup main kaam ke liye khudako ummidavaar banaaya. unhone STS-5 or STS-7 kii antariksh udaan main vaahan ekikrut pareekshan or pelod ke pareekshan ko samarthit kiya. uske saath-saath unhone kakshiya udaan pareekshan, laanch or landing or bachaav prakriyaaon ke vikaas ke liye donon chikitsa or parichaalan jaankaree pradaan kii or bachaav helicopteron main ek chikitsak ke rup main bhi kaary kiya. ana lee fisher STS-9 kii yatra main udaan niyantrak thi. STS-51A kii udaan ke dauraan fisher ek mishan visheshgy bhi thi. use udaan ke puraa hone ke saath, fisher ne antariksh main 192 ghanto kaa log in samay puraa kiya. unhen bahut se puraskaron se sammanit kiya gaya jiname se NASA spes flite award,madar of da year award, california science centre nari kaa award, usiele chikitsa peshewar echievement award aadi.
