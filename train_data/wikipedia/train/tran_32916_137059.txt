 dahi chaaval ek bhartiya vyanjan he jo bharat ke chaaron dakshini rajyon tamilnadu, andhra pradesh, kerala or karnataka main vishesh rup se lokapriy he. ise dopaher yaa raat ke khaane main khaaya jaataa he.
 dahi chaaval taiyaar karne kii vidhi bahut saral he. chawlon ke ubaal kar khatti dahi main milakar upar se urad daal, rai, adarak, jira or hing kaa tadakaa lagaaya jaataa he. swaadanusaar namak or doodh milakar is vyanjan ko parosa jaataa he.
