 iglacia de saan andres bedriana, asturiyas, spain kaa ek girjaghar he. yah 9 veen sadi kaa he or 1931 main ek rashtriya smarak ghoshit kiya gaya tha. 
 is girjaghar kaa mahatvapurn atirikt vistaar 12 veen 13 veen shatabdi main or phir 16 veen main hua tha. 1916 main atirikt vistaar karke yahan ek skool ke rup main upyog ke liye nirmaan ke kiya gaya tha.
