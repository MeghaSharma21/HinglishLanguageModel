 alpha romeo 159, 2005 se italvi nirmaataa company alfaa romeo dwaara nirmit ek compect akjicutive car he. 159 ko safal alfaa romeo 156 kii jagah 2005 ke geneva motor sho main nirmaan rup main pesh kiya gaya tha. 159 main brera, spidar; or camel tathaa visconti naamak sankalpana kaaron ke saath saajha taur par jiem/phiet premium plateform kaa istemaal hota he. 2006 ke european car of da year puraskaron main 159 ko teesraa sthaan praapt hua.
 159 ko alfaa centro style ke sahyog se giorgetto giugiyaaro dwaara dizin kiya gaya tha. iuke saamne ke hisse par ek paaramparik alfaa romeo ve ke aakaar ke gril or bonete, tathaa belanaakaar hedlit samoohon ko dekha ja saktaa he. isaki qamar unchi he or peeche kii taraf sthit "si" stambh tak chaudee hoti dikhaai detii he. iske aatanrik bhaag main bhi 156 sahit aarambhik kaaron kii tarah kii style dikhaai detii he jaise gaharaai se dhanse hue upkaran jo driver kii taraf jhuke hote hai. haalanki 159 apane purvavarti kaaron se badi he or yah 4660 millimetre lambii lambii), 1828 mimi chaudee or 1417 mimi oonchi he.
 159 saamne or chaar pahiyon vaali drive, donon vinyas main uplabdh he. "kyuu4" chaar pahiyon vaali drive system main ek torshan prakaar kii si judawaan antar kaa istemaal hota he or yah .2 litre petrol or 2.4 litre dijal injanon ke saath uplabdh he. iskaa gearbox adhikansh modelon par chah gati vaala minial he or chah gati vaala swachaalit kyuu-tronic gearbox 1.9 dijal, 2.4 dijal or 3.2 petrol modelon ke liye uplabdh he. kuch deshon main selespied gearbox 2.2 petrol ingine ke saath bikri ke liye uplabdh he.
 bazaar ke aadhaar par trim ke kai str uplabdh he. trim ke chaar str is prakaar hai: pragati, vibhedak, vishisht or tourismo international . britain main trim ke teen str hai: tourismo, lusso or tourismo international . anya vikalpon main 159 blue and me imphotonment system ke saath bhi uplabdh he.
 chasis kii torshanal kathorta 180.000 daNm/rad par is varg kii ek behtareen visheshta he.
 2006 main geneva motor sho main ek sportwagon sanskaran ko pesh kiya gaya. us varsh 2.4 jettidm dijal model ke liye ek swachaalit gearbox vikalp ko bhi shuru kiya gaya tha or baad main anya sanskarnon kaa vistaar kiya gaya. 2007 main chaar pahiyon par chalane vaale ek dijal model ko relies kiya gaya or 2.4 litre dijal kshamta vaale injanon ke shakti utpaadan ko badhaakar 210 metric ashwashakti kar diya gaya jiske saath ek vikalp ke rup main ek navnirmit tiai trim str bhi uplabdh tha.
 model varsh 2008 ke liye 159 kii yantriki or andruni hisson ko or viksit kiya gaya. 250 kilometre prati ghanta kii sheersh raftaar hasil karne ke liye saamne ke pahie ke chaalan vinyas main 3.2 litre ve6 model kii peshkash kii gai he. sabhi model bhinnrup alfaa kii electronic "kyuu2" simit slip antar se susajjit hai. navnirmit aluminium ghatakon ke parinamswaroop iske vajan main 45 kilogram kii katautee kii gai he. 2008 main geneva motor sho main haal ke model sanskarnon ko pesh kiya gaya.
 model varsh 2009 ke liye alfaa romeo ne "tibiai" baij vaale ek naveen terbocharged petrol ingine ko pesh kiya. is 1742 sisi ikai main pravesh or donon nikaas kaimon main pratyaksh injection or parivartaneey kapaat samay kii vyavastha he. is nae ingine main 200 metric ashwashakti or 320 new.me tork he. aakhirkaar yah ikai jiem vyutpann 2.2 or 1.9 jettis ikaayiyon kii jagah legi. iske alaava ek nayaa 170 metric ashwashakti jettidm dijal bhi uplabdh ho gaya he.
 2010 main 1750 tibiai ko chhodkar petrol par chalane vaale sabhi injanon ko seva se mukt kar diya gaya jisase 159 main general motors aadhaarit injanon ke istemaal kaa ant ho gaya. ab ekmaatr barkaraar dijal injanon main 120 or 170 pies 2.0 jettidm ingine rah gaye hai.
 petrol chaalit sabhi ingine pratyaksh eendhan injection prakaar ke hote hai jinhen jettis naam diya gaya he or jettidi dijal ingine aam rail pratyaksh eendhan injection hote hai.
 159 main maanak rup se saat airbag lage hote hai or saath hi vaikalpik taur par ghutane kaa ek atirikt airbag bhi uplabdh hota he. is car ne apni "moch-virdohi" siton ke kaaran peeche kii taraf se hone vaali durghatana ke prati suraksha ke pareekshanon main acha pradarshan kiya he.
