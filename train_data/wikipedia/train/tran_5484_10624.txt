 un sabhi praudyogikiyon ko nabhikeey praudyogiki kehete hai jinamein koi nabhikeey abhikriya sammilit ho. nabhikeey urja, nabhikeey aushadhi, parmaanu hathiyaar aadi nabhikeey praudyogiki ke pramukh udhaaharan hai. vastav main nabhikeey praudyogiki kaa kshetra bahut vistrut ho chukaa he tathaa yah dhooma sansuchak se lekar nabhikeey riector or parmaanu astron tak faili hui he.
