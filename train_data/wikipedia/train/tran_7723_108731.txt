 urisa main bhubaneshwar se saat mile dur pashchimottar main udaygiri ke nikat kii pahari khandgiri kehalaati he. khandgiri kaa shikhar 123 foot unchaa he, jo aas-paas kii pahadiyon main sabase unchaa he. kaling naresh kharvel kaa prasiddh hathi gumpha abhilekh khandgiri se kuch hi duuri par he.
 khandgiri kii guphaayen jain sampradaay se sambandhit hai. ye guphaayen lagbhag pratham shatabdi isa purv main nirmit kii gai maaloom padati hai.
 khandgiri or isse sambhddha udaygiri main utkhanit jain layan hai. khandgiri sthit layanon kii sankhya 19 he. isi prakaar udaygiri main 44 or nilgiri main 3 guphaayen hai. ye sabhi isapurv duusari-pehali shatee kii anumaan kii jaati he. unke anek bhaagon main murtiyon kaa ucchitran hua he. in gufaaon main sabase prakhyat hathi gumpha he jiske upar mahameghavaahan kharvel kii prashasti ankit he jo aitihaasik drushti se bade mahatv kaa he.
