 tansen kii vansh parampara hi 'senia gharaana' kehalaati he. senia gharaana dhrupad gayaki se sambandh rakhata he. tansen swayam ek uttam dhrupad gayak tathaa dhrupad rachanaakaar the. ve bindraban ke prasiddh sant gayak swami haridas ke shishy the. tansen ke teen putr: bilas khan, surat sen, sharat sen tathaa ek putree saraswati inke madhyam se senia gharaane ne jad pakadi. yah gharaana kaalaantar se kai shakhaon main bant gaya jaise jaipur senia gharaana, rampur senia gharaana, maihar senia gharaana aadi. is gharaane main dhrupad gayan ke alaava tat vaadya jaise sur bahar, sur singar, sitar, rabab, sarod, violin aadi kaa bhi chalan he. rampur ke ustad vajeer khan ke pita amir khan apane jamaane ke kushal vaadk hone ke sath, gayan kala se bhi parichit the. unke shagird ustad allaudin khan maihar aa kar bas gayi tathaa vahiin se apane gharaane kaa vistaar kiya, isiliye unki parampara ko 'maihar senia gharaana' kaha jaataa he. allaudin khan kaa jivan ek sangeet maharishi jaisaa hi tha. unhone apane jivan kaal main kai kalakaaron ko sangeet vidya kaa daan kiya. vishwa vikhyath sitar vadak pandit ravi shankar, ali akbar khan jaise sangeet ratna isi gharaane ne sangeet jagat ko pradaan kiye he.
