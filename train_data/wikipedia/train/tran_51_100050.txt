 balati ek tibetai bhashaa he jo paak-adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-blatistan kshetra or bharat ke jammu or kashmir rajya ke kargil kshetra main boli jaati he. yah aadhunik tibeti bhashaa se kaafii bhinn he. puraani tibeti kii kai dhvaniyaan jo aadhunik tibeti main khoi ja chuki hai, aaj bhi balati main prayog hoti hai.
