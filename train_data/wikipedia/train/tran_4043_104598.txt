 punjab pakistan kaa ek praant he. isamen 36 jile hai. punjab aabaadi ke anupaat se pakistan kaa sab se bada rajya he. punjab main rahane vaale log punjabi kehalaate hai. punjab kii dakshin kii taraf sindh, pashchim kii taraf kaibar-pakhtuunakva or balochistan,‎‎‎ uttar kii taraf kashmir or islamabad or purv main hindustani punjab or rajasthan se miltaa he. punjab main boli jane vaali bhashaa bhi punjabi kehalaati he. punjabi ke alaava vahaan urdu or saraiki bhi boli jaati he. punjab kii lahore he. punjab farsi bhashaa ke do shabdon - 'panj' yani 'paanch' or 'aab' yani 'pani' se mil kar bana he. in paanch dariyaaon ke naam hai:
 punjab ke do hisse hai: ek purvi hissaa jo ki bharat main he or ek pashchimi hissaa jo pakistan main he. punjab pakistan kaa sab se bada praant he, jabaki pakistan ke 48% log punjabi bhashaa samajhate or bolte bhi hai.
 id ul-fitar, id-ul-azaha, shab-e-baraat or id milad e nabi punjab ke alaava puure pakistan main ‏khaas tyohaar hai or puure josh o- karosh se manaaye jate hai. in tyohaaron ke alaava puraa ramadan kaa maheenaa bhi khaas ahmiyat rakhata he.magar basant ek aisa tyohaar he jo punjab se munsalik he. ye tyohaar bahaar ke mausam ko kush aamad kehane kaa ek khaas tareeqa he. jis main log patang uda kar or punjab ke khaas khaane bana kar or kha kar is tyohaar ko manaate hai.
 farvari se pehle punjab main bahut sardi hoti he or phir is maheene se mausam main tabdili aana shuru ho jaati he or mausam kushgavaar hone lagata he. farvari se april tak bahaar kaa mausam rahata he or phir garmi shuru hone lagati he.garmi kaa mausam may se shuru hota he to oktober ke akhir tak rahata he. june or july sab se zyaada garm maheene hai. sarkari malumat ke mutabik punjab main 46°C tak darja haraarat hua he magar akhbaaraat kii malumat ke mutabik punjab main 51°C tak darja haraarat pahunchaa he. sab se zyaada garmi kaa ricard multan main june ke maheene main qalambnid kya gaya jab ataarad kaa darja haraarat 54 °C se bhi aage bad gaya tha. agast main garmi kaa jor thodaa tut jaataa he or phir oktober ke baad punjab main shadid sardi kaa mausam shuru hojaataa he.
