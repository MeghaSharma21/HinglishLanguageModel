 ooshmagatik tapakram yaa 'param taap' ' taapmaana kaa vishuddh maap he. yah ooshmagatiki ke mukhya prachalon main se ek he.
 ooshmaagthik tapakram ooshmaagatiki ke dwitiya niyam dwaara paribhaashit he jisamen siddhanth rup main nunatam sambhav taap ko 'shunya bindu' mana jaataa he. is taap ko 'param shunya' bhi kehete hai. is taap par padaarth ke kan nunatam gati kii sthiti main hote hai tathaa isse kam thande nahin ho sakate. kwantem yantriki kii bhashaa main, param taap par padaarth apni nimnatam avasthaa main hota he jo isaki nunatam urja kii avasthaa he. is kaaran hi ooshmaagthik tapakram ko 'param taap' bhi kaha jaataa he.
