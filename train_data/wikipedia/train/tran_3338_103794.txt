 swatamphelashio hastmaithun kaa ek prakaar he, jis ke antargat koi vyakti apane khud ke ling ko maukhik rup se uttejnaa pradaan karata he. haalanki ise aam taur par ek shahari kinvadanti ke rup main janaa jaataa he par, yah shaareerik rup un purushon ke liye sambhav he jinamein paryaapt lacheelaapan ho yaa jinka ling lanbaa ho yaa phir in donon hi gunon kaa sangyojan ho. swatamphelashio ko donon samalangik or paaramparik ashleel sahitya main ek vishesh sthaan mila he. lokapriy samskruti main swatamphelashio se sambandhit sandarbh aam hai.
