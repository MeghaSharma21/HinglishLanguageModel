 pahanane yogya computer, yah bhi sharir janit computer ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he jo laghu electronic upkaran he joki vaahak dwaara kapado ke andar, saath main yaa sheersh par pahane jate hai. pahanane yogya is praudyogiki ke is varg ko saamaanya yaa vishesh uddeshy soochna praudyogiki or media ke vikaas ke liye viksit kiya gaya he. pahanane yogya computer pakadaney main asana or kisi or hardware computer kii tulana main vishesh rup se upyogi hote hai.
