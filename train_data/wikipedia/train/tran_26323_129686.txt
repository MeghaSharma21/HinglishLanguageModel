 newfoundland canada ke purvi tat par sthit ek dweep he or yah newfoundland or labrador praant kaa ek bhaag he. yahan kii rajdhani sant jones he, jo dweep ke purvottar bhaag par he. is dweep par lagbhag 5 lakh log rahete hai.
 newfoundland kii adhikansh jansankhya angrezi bolti he lekin is dweep par francisi bhashi log bhi rahete hai, jinamein adhiktar dakshinpaschim main rahete hai. newfoundland ke kuch kshetron main log kuch alag prakaar se angrezi bolte or shabdon kaa upyog karte hai jo maanak angrezi main nahin hai. yahan tak kii newfoundland main boli jane vaali angrezi kaa ek alag shabdkosh bhi he.
