 kingsford kolkata ke chif presidency magistrate the. krantikariyon ko apamaanit karne or unhen dand dene ke liye bahut badnaam tha. british sarkaar ne kingsford ke prati janata ke aakrosh ko bhaanp kar uski saraksha kii drushti se use seshan jaj banaakar mujffarpur bhej diya. 
 bengal ke krantikariyon ne apane bich se prafull chaaki or khudiram bose koi inaki hatya ke liye taiyaar kiya or ve uske peeche-peeche mujffarpur pahunch gaye. donon ne kingsford kii gatividhiyon kaa baariki se adhyayan kiya. iske baad 30 april 1908 i0 ko kingsford par us samay bam phenk diya jab vah buggy par savaar hokar european club se baahar nikal rahaa tha. lekin jis buggy par bam phenkaa gaya tha us par kingsford nahin tha balki buggy par do european mahilaayein savaar thim. ve donon is hamale main maari gayi.
