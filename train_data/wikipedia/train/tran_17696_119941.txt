 rajasthan praant main jaipur jile se 325 kilometre dur baaraan jile main kawai naamak gaanv he. is gaanv ke 90 pratishat log gaanv ke baahar shaadi vivaah nahin karte hai. is kaaran bharat main ise vishesh rup se sthaneey vivaah karne vaale gaanv ke rup main janaa jaataa he. darasal ye gaanv vaale, rishishwatior samudaay he, inke purakhe lagbhag teen sau saal pehle, madhya bharat se rojgaar kii talash me rajasthan me aakar based the. tab se ve yaheen ke hokar rah gaye. dhire dhire aapas me vivaah kaa silsilaa shuru hua, jisane baad me parampara kaa rup le liya. safal shaadiyon ne inke utsaah ko or badhaaya.
