 hamun-e-helmand, jise dariyacheh-e-hamun bhi kehete hai, purvi iran ke sistan va balochistan praant main afghanistan kii sarhad ke nazadik mausami taur par ban jaani vaali ek kam gaharaai kii daldali jhil he. yah helmand nadi ke pani se banati he jo afghanistan ke hindu kush parvaton se shuru hoti he. iran ke pathaar par aisi adhik pravaah vaale mausam main bhar jane vaali jhiilon ko hamun kaha jaataa he.
 1976 main afghanistan se nadiyaan puure pravaah par chalaa karti thi, jis se yah jhil bhi falkar lagbhag 4,000 varg kimi tak vistrut ho jaaya karti thi. 1999 se 2001 ke kaal main sookhaa padaa or jhil qareeb-qareeb gaayab hi ho gai. uske baad se kabhi ban jaati he or kabhi nahin. jab afghanistan main sookhaa padataa he yaa vahaan helmand kaa pani roak dene se pravaah kam hota he jo yahan ret ke bade tile ban jate hai or puraa registan laut aata he. is jhil ke kinare kuch machuaare gaanv hai or iske suukh jane par vah bhi kabhi-kabhi ret se dhake jate hai.
