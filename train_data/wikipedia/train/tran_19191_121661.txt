 lahore main shahdara nagar ke nikat sthit jahangir makbara mughal samraat jahangir ko samarpit he. ise jahangir kii mrityu ke 10 saal baad unke putr shahjahan ne banwaaya tha. ek bageeche ke andar sthit makbare kii minaaren 30 meter oonchi hai. makbare ke bhitari hisse main bhittichitron kii sundar sajaavat he.
