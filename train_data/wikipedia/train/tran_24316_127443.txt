 khost purvi afghanistan kaa ek shahar he. yah khost praant kii rajdhani bhi he, jo pakistan ke seemaa ke nazadik ek parvatiy ilaaka he. khost shahar kii aabaadi lagbhag 1.6 lakh he or puure khost praant kii jansankhya qareeb 10 lakh he. yahan ke adhiktar nivasee pashto bhashi pathan hai or saamaajik vyavastha qabeeliayi he. afghanistan main soviet yuddh ke dauraan khost july 1983 se navambar 1987 tak vidrohi phujon dwaara ghira rahaa tha.
 'khost' main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
 khost kaa mausam afghanistan ke anya bhaagon se thodaa alag he kyonki yah dakshin-purv kii taraf khulaa he jis se bhartiya upmahadweep main garmiyon main aane vaala monsoon yahan par bhi pani barasata he. is barish ke alaava yahan kaa mausam shushk he. yahan kii sardiyaan bhi zyada bhayankar nahin hotin - paala to padataa he lekin barf bahut kam padati he. sardiyon main nunatam ausat taapmaana shunya se zaraa kam hota he.
