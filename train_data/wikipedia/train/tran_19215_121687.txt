 kuelar kila kuelar shahar kaa mashhoor smarak he. ye spain main kastile or lion ke swytt samudaay ke segovia praant main sthit he. ise 3 june 1931 i. ko bien de interes cultural kii suchi main shaamil kiya gaya. 
 is kile ko sambhaal ke rakhaa gaya he. 13veen or 18ve sadi main isamen alag alag shaleon main kaam kiya gaya. iskaa nirmaan gothic or punarjagran style mane hua tha.
