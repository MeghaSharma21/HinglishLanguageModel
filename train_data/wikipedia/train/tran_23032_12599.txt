 yosafatal chikitsa kendra israel ke ilaat nagar main sthit ek aspataal he. is aspataal kii sthapana 1968 main kii gai thi or yah israel kaa sabase dakshini aspataal he or dakshini negaev registan main sevaayen uplabdh karaane vaala ekmaatr aspataal bhi he. iskaa naam jeeyora yosafatal ke naam par he. 65 bistaron ke saath yah israel kaa sabase chota aam aspataal he. vittiya pareshaaniyon ke kaaran is aspataal ko band kar dene ke prastaav aane lage the lekin sthaneey nivaasiyon or sarkari adhikariyon ke virodh ke kaaran is aspataal ko chaalu rahane diya gaya kyonki un logon kaa tark tha ki teen kilometre tak ke vyas kshetra ke bheetar koi bhi aspataal naa hona bahut ghaatak ho saktaa he.
 is aspataal kaa sanchaalan clelit swasthya sudhaar sanghathan dwaara kiya jaataa he. gotakhori se sambandhit durghatnaon ke upachaar ke liye yahan ek purnatah susajjit ricompraishan chambar he. chhuttiyaan manaane aae logon or sthaneey nivaasiyon ke liye is aspataal main gurda dialysis kii suvidha bhi upalbadh he.
