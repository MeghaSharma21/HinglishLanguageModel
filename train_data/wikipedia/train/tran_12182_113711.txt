 medan das mandir uttar pradesh ke barabanki jile kii nawabganj tehsil se uttar disha main saddipur-jahangirabad maarg par gram ajagana ke paas ek kilometre kii duuri par sthit he.
 attavadham ek pracheen dharmik sthal he janha par hindu sant medan das kii kutiyaa thi. medan das ke sharir tyaagane ke pashchaat attavadham main hi unki samadhi banaai gayi or iske uparaant vanha par bhavy mandir bhi banavaa diya gaya. mandir ke chaaro taraf jangal he jahaan par zyada sankhya main bandar paye jate hai.
 prayek mangalvaar ko attavadham kaa vishaal mela lagata he, jisame dur-dur se shraddhalu darshan hetu aate hai. yahan kii aasthaa yah he ki agar bhakt sachche dil se koi bhi manokamana maang le to uski manokamana avashya purna hoti he. har varsh thandi ke mausam main yahan par vishaal meley ke saath hi bhandare kaa bhi aayojan kiya jaataa he tathaa garib logon ko vastra ityadi daan die jate hai.
