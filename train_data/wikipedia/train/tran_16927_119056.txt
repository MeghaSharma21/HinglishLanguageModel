 gajanan vishnu daamale hindu mahasabha ke niyaswaarth sevak rahe. shirgaanv ke vishnupant daamale ke putr the. ratnagiri main plig mahamari ke dauraan vir saavarkar ne inke yahaan 24 november, 1924 se 20 june, 1925 tak awaas kiya tha. yaheen par saavarkar ne apni pustak hindu padpaadashahi likhi thi. 1937 main saavarkar jab mumbai aae, tab daamale inke nijee sachiv rahe the. ye saavarkar kii sabhi yatraaon main unke saath rahe the. 1938 main bhaganagar ke ahimsak pratirodh main inhone bhaag liya va giraftaar bhi hue. inhen 1946 ke mumbai dangon main bhi giraftar kiya gaya tha. baad main gandhi hatyakand ke aarope main inhen 1948 se lambey samay tak karawas bhogana padaa tha.
