 hattiyaan bala azaad kashmir kaa ek zila he. san 1947 tak yah jammu or kashmir rajya ke baramullah zile kaa hissaa hua karata tha. uske baad 2009 tak yah mujaffarabad zile kaa bhaag tha. is zile kii rajdhani hattiyaan bala he.
 is zile main gurjar or rajput samudaay pramukh hai.
 sudhanoti zile main teen tahseelein hai - hattiyaan bala, chikaar or lipa.
