 kisi parmaanu yaa samasthanik ko duusare parmaanu yaa samasthanik main badal dena nabhikeey tatwantaran kehalaata he. chunki kisi bhi tatva yaa samasthanik ke nabhik main protonon evam neutronon kii sankhya hi uski pahchaan he, atah aise kisi bhi prakram ko nabhikeey tatwantaran kehete hai jisamen nabhik ke protronon yaa neutronon kii sankhya badal jaati he. tatwantaran, kisi nabhikeey abhikriya ke dwaara kiya ja saktaa he yaa radioskriy hraas dwaara. 
 'transmuteshan' yaa 'prakaarya-parivartan' kii pariklpana rasayan vigyaan ke vikaas ke itihaas ke kemiyagaari ke kalkhand main bhi bahut charchit rahi thi. madhyayug main alchemist paras pathar kii khoj main rahete the jo saadhaaran dhaatuon ko swarna main badal de. is par bahut charchaa hui or prakaarya-parivartan ko asambhav ghoshit kiya gaya. bahut se vaigyanikon ne 'sona banane' vaale dhokhebaajon kaa bhandaphod bhi kiya.
