 manushyon kii anuvanshiki main pitruvansh samooh tii yaa waahe-diene hapalogrup T ek pitruvansh samooh he. yah pitruvansh swayan pitruvansh samooh ke se utpann hui ek shakha he. is pitruvansh ke purush bhartiya upmahadweep ke kuch samudaayon main milte hai. dakshin bharat ke jerukala adivasee, pashchim bengal ke baodi janajati or rajasthan ke lodha samudaay ke adhikansh purush iske vanshaj hai. africa main somalia or dakshini misr ke purushon main iske vanshaj baari maatra main paae jate hai. iran main keraman shahar ke parsi samudaay or dakshin-pashchimi iran ke bakhtiari banjaaraa samudaay main bhi iske kaafii vanshaj milte hai. europe main germany, italy ke sardinia dweep, spain or portugal main iske sadsy milte hai. uttar-purvi portugal ke yahudee samudaay main iske vanshaj baari maatra main maujuud hai. mana jaataa he ke mul pitruvansh samooh tii jis purush ke saath armbh hua vah aaj se 25,000-30,000 saal pehle madhya purv kaa rahane vaala tha.
 angrezi main "vansh samooh" ko "hapalogrup", "pitruvansh samooh" ko "waahe chromosome hapalogrup" or "matruvansh samooh" ko "amtidiene hapalogrup" kehete hai.
