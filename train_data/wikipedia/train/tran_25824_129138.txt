 fliper 1996 ection se bharpoor adventure film he. yah 1963 main bani isi naam kii film kii uttarkrutti film he. isamen abhinay paul hogan or elizah wood ne kiya he. film ek ladke ke bare main he jise apana garmiyon kaa samay apane chachaa ke saath bitaana he, jo florida keez main rahete hai. yadhyapi vah sochataa he ki is baar punah garmiyaan ubaau hongi, uski muthbhed soons se hoti he or vah uskaa naam fliper rakhata he or vah uske saath dosti kar letaa he.
