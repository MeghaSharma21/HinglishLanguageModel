 westerbaton-kuriran ek swedish akhbaar he jisaki sthapana 1900 main hui thi. yah umeo, westerbaton main chhapata he. yah westerbaton kshetra kii khabaron ke saath saath umeo ke bare main vishesh khabaren detaa he. isamen rashreey evam antarrashtriya khabaren bhi hoti hai.
 vi.ke. pahila baar 17 may 1900 ko chapa tha or 1903 tak westerbaton county kaa sabase mashhoor akhbaar ban gaya tha. astrid tarid iske saath jude pahile patrakaron main se ek tha..
 gustav rosen kii sweden ke itihaas main vishesh bhoomika rahi he or vah 1926 tak is akhbaar kaa sanchaalak rahaa kyonki 1926 main usko karl gustav ekaman ke aphis main suraksha mantri bana diya gaya tha. iskaa utraadhikari arnest gafavelin bana jo che maheene baad mar gaya. uske baad gustav rosen kaa eklota ladkaa staln rosen 23 saal kii aayu main iskaa sampadak or prakaashak bana. sitambar 1928 main ekaman ke astipha dene ke baad gustav rosen umeo main vaapis aa gayi; pehle ve.ke. ke rajanithik sampadak ke taur par kam kiya or phir 1931 se lekar 1942 tak westerbaton county ke governor rahe.
 1929 main rosen ne apane takreeban saare share mashhoor nirmaankar begstram ko bech die. 1930/1931 tak ve.ke. kii imaarat puri ban gai or jisamen 1988 tak ve.ke. kaa kaam hota rahaa. 1949 main yah norland kaa pahila aisa akhbaar tha jiskaa apana pres photographer tha, harry lindwal, jo 1985 nivritti tak naukari par rahe.
