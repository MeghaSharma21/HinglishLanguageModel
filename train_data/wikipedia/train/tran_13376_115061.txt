 kailashhar bhartiya rajya tripura main sthit ek nagar mahapalika tathaa unakoti zila kaa mukhyalay bhi he. is nagar bangladesh ke saath seemaa bhi lagati he. 
 kailashhar tripuri samrajya kii puraani rajdhani thi. yahan bhagwaan shiv kaa ek prasiddh mandir he. bhagwaan shiv kaa ek naam kailash bhi he. sambhawatah nagar ke naam kii utpatti yaheen se hui he. 
 bharat kii janaganana 2011 ke anusaar yahan kii kul jansankhya 23,599 he.
