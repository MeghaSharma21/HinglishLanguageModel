 gabriel daannutasyo italy kaa lekhak, kavi, patrkaar, naatakkaar, tathaa pratham vishwayuddh kaa yoddha ttha. 1889 se 1910 tak italvi sahitya main tathaa 1914 se 1024 tak italy ke raajnneetik jivan main uskaa pramukh sthaan tha. usko praayah il wate yaa il profeta ke naam se janaa jaataa tha.
 anutsiyo kaa jivan bahut ghatnapurna rahaa. vah vilaas or vaibhav kaa premi tha. europea romancekaaleen parvarti sahitya kii pravritiyon ke samanvay kii apurv kshamta anutsiyon kii rachanaaon main milti he. bhashaa kii drushti se use alankaarawaadi kaha ja saktaa he. kavita, naatak, upanyaas, gadhykavy sabhi kuch usne likhaa.
 isaki praarambhik rachanaayen primo bete main sangruhit hai. anya kavyakritiyon main 'kanto nivo' 'intarmejjo di rime', 'alejie romane', 'isonteo e laa keemera', 'poema paradisiako', 'le laudi', hai. prasiddh upanyaason main 'il pyache', 'lare', 'intochele', 'il phuwako' aadi hai. natyakritiyon main 'francheska da rimini', 'laa filya di yorio', 'laa naave' aadi hai. 'le novelle della paskara' uski kahaniyon kaa prasiddh sangrah he. aatmakathaatmak gadhykavy kii drushti se 'kontelatsiyone della morte' tathaa 'livero segreto' ullekhneey hai.
