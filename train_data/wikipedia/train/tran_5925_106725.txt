 'airsel' bharat kii doorsanchaar company he.yah preeped or postpad jiesem suvidha puure bharat main uplabdh karti he. yah malaysia ke vipul seth or chennai ke dhiraj chaudhuri kaa sanyukt udhyam he.or filhal iskaa vilay reliance communication main hone vaala he, jis kii ghoshnaa ho chuki he.
 telecom company airsel diwalia hone kii sthiti main aa gai he. media reports ke anusaar company ne neshnal company lau tribunel main diwalia hone kii arji di he. company yah kadam lene se pehle board ko bhang kar degi. diwalia ghoshit hone ke baad airsel company ke taur par samaapt ho jaaegi. iske baad bazaar main airtel, jeeyo, vodaphone, idia or beasenel shesh rah jaayengi. is samay in sabhi companiyon ke bich kadi pratispardhaa chal rahi he.
