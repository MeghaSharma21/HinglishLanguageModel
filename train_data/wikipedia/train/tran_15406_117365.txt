 
 thakuri nepal kaa ek pramukh jati samuh he. ye nepali kshatriya jati kshetrike antargat kaa ek samuh he kabhi kusunda to kabhi babusaheb namase pukaare janevaali ye jati nepali bhoomi ke bhumiputra ke rup main parichit ek mukhya jati he. nepal ke shahvansheey rajpariwaar, sen raja ,bajhaangi raja,evam pashchimi nepal ke rajpariwaar sampoorn thakurivanshi kshatriya hai.in kii maatrubhaasha nepali bhashaa or doteli bhashaa he. ye bharopeli bhashaa parivaarake sadsy he. 
 khand, chand, ju, paal,deuva ,bam, banshi, mall, medasi, rathore, shah, shahi, singh, sen, hamaal, aadi thakuri logke paariwarik naam he.
