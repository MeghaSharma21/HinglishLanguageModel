 dhaatu sansuchak kaa prayog dhaatu se jude saamaanon kaa pataa lagane main kiya jaataa he. iske alaava land mins kaa pataa lagane, hathiyaaron, bam, visphotak aadi kaa pataa lagane jaise kai kaamon main bhi kiya jaataa he. ek saralatam dhaatu sansuchak main ek vidyut dolak hota he jo prayavarti dhara utpann karata he. yah dhara ek taar kii kundali main se pravaahit hokar alag chumbakeey kshetra utpann karti he. isamen ek kundali kaa prayog chumbakeey kshetra ko maapne ke liye kiya jaataa he. chumbakeey padaarth ke hone par chumbakeey kshetra main hone vaale parivartan ke aadhaar par isko mapa jaataa he. isamen lage microprocesar dhaatu kii prakaar kaa pataa lagaate hai. dhaatu sansuchak vaidyut chumbakatv ke siddhant par kaam karte hai. alag-alag kaaryo ke prayog ke anusaar dhaatu sansuchakon kii samvedansheeltaa alag hoti he.
 unnisvin shatabdi main vaigyanik aise yantra kii khoj karne main lage the jisase dhaatuon ko khoja ja sake. aarambh main dhaatuon kii khoj ke liye jo upkaran banaae gaye, unki kshamta simit thi or vah urja kaa prayog adhik karte the. aise main ve har jagah kargar nahin hote the. 1881 main graham bel ne is prakaar ke yantra kii mul khoj kii thi. 1937 main jerard fisher ne is prakaar kii yukti kaa vikaas kar dhaatu vekshak yaa sansuchak kaa anveshan kiya jisamen yadi radio kisi dhaatu ko khojane main kharaab ho jaae to use uski radio aavriti ke aadhaar par khoj sakane kii kshamta thi. vah safal hue or unhone iskaa patent karvaa liya.
 praayah airport par, railway stesan, cinemagharon main, metro steshnon main saamaan kii talaashi karte samay dhaatu sansuchak kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. dhaatu sansuchak praayah do prakaar ke hote hai:
 isse adhik byorevar jaanch hetu eks-re detector hote hai. unamem saamaan ke andar rakhi vastuon kaa rangeen chayachitr bhi monitor par dikhaai detaa he.
