 gres mugabe-marufu zimbabwe ke bhootpurv raashtrapati robert mugabe kii duuji patni, tathaa 1996 se 2017 tak zimbabwe kii pratham mahila raheen.
 unki pehali shaadi stanley gorenza se hui thi, gorenza vaayu sena payalat or zimbabwe ke antarrashtriya sambandh hetu ve chini janavaadee ganraajya main rajdoot the. jab gres raashtrapati mugabe kii sachiv thim, us vaqt unke pehle do bacchon kii paidaaish hui thi, pehle bachche kaa naam 'bolaa' rakhaa gaya tha or duje bachche kaa naam 'robert peter junior' rakhaa gaya tha. saal 1997 ko unke tije bachche, chathunga, kii paidaaish hui thi. gres mugabe apni badhiya karidaari ke liye jaani jaati he. log kabhi kabhi gres mugabe ko 'dis gres' yaa 'da countries first shopper' ke naamon se bulaate hai.
