 maale, jansankhya 153379, maldive kii rajdhani he. paaramparik rup se yah pracheen maldive shahi rajvansh kaa rajdweep tha. pehle yah kile va deevaaron se ghira rahata tha.
 yaddapi maale bhugolic rup se maale atol,kaafu atol, me sthit he parantu prashaskeey rup se ise uskaa hissaa nahi mana jaataa he. ek vyapaarik bandargah dweep me sthit he, yah raashtra ki sari vanijyik chahal pahal kaa pramukh thikaana he.dweep kaa adhiktam bhaag nagarikrut he. yah vishwa kaa sabase ghani aabaadi vaala shahar he nagar ko chaar bhaagon me bata gaya he;henviru, galolhu, mafannu va maikchangolhi. paas k wellingili, purv paryatak sthal, ko sarkaari rup se paanchava bhaag mana jaataa he.
 maale ne sunami, disambar 2004, ko saha he jisane ek tihaai shahar ko pani se dhak diya tha.29 sitambar 2007 ko maale ne bam dhamaake ko bhi jhela tha, yah maldive ke isihas k pehla dhamaakaa tha..
 
 maale shabd sanskrit bhashaa se mahaale shabd se liya gaya he. maha=bada, aale=ghar. parantu lokkatha naam ki utpatti kaa doosraa strot bataati he.
