 
 lithium ek rasinic tatv he. saadhaaran paristhitiyon main yah prakruti kii sabase halki dhaatu or sabase kam ghanatv-vaala thos padaarth he. rasinic drushti se yah kshar dhaatu samooh kaa sadsy he or anya kshar dhaatuon kii tarah atyant abhikriyasheel he, yani anya padaarthon ke saath tezi se rasinic abhikriya kar letaa he. yadi ise hawa main rakhaa jaye to yah jaldi hi vaayu main maujuud oxigen se abhikriya karne lagata he, jo iske sheeghra hi aag pakad lene main prakat hota he. is karanvash ise tel main dubo kar rakhaa jaataa he. tel se nikaalkar ise kaate jane par yah chamkila hota he lekin jald hi pehle bhura-sa banakar chamak kho detaa he or phir kaala hone lagata he. apni is adhik abhikriyasheeltaa kii vajah se yah prakruti main shuddh rup main kabhi nahin miltaa balki keval anya tatwon ke saath yogicon main hi paaya jaataa he. apane kam ghanatv ke kaaran lithium bahut halakaa hota he or dhaatu hone ke baavjood ise aasaani se chaaku se kaata ja saktaa he.
