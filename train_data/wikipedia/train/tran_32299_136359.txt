 yah varsh kii aakhri grand slam tennis pratiyogita he jo america main neuyork shahar yojit kii jaati he.
 pehali baar yah pratiyogita sanyukt rajya america main 1881 main agast ke maheene main newport main kheli gai. iskaa aayojan 1918 tak newport main hi hua. chelenz round 1911 main hataa diya gaya. amareeki federation se jude club ke sadsy hi is spardhaa main bhaag le sakate the. mahilaon kaa ekal pehali baar 1887 main philadelphia main khelaa gaya. 1919 main is pratiyogita kaa sthaan badalakar forest hill west said tennis club,neuyork kar diya gaya. 1974 tak yah pratiyogita ghaas par hoti thi, 1975 se 1977 ke bich main yah pratiyogita clay court par hui. 1970 main yah pratiyogita open ho gai. yah pratiyogita taai brake ko apnaane vaali pehali mukhya pratiyogita thi.
 The US Open moved for the final time in 1978, with the Flushing Meadows stadium, in the Queens district of New York, having the honour of hosting the fourth and final Grand Slam event of the year since then. The clay courts have been replaced by Decoturf hard court surfaces.
 The US Open has the largest centre court in the world, with room for 23,000 spectators in the Arthur Ashe Stadium, opened in 1997. In 1999, the old centre court was renovated and renamed the Louis Armstrong Stadium.
