 ragesh asthana ek bhartiya abhinayta the jinhone vibhinn bhartiya televijan dharawahikon main abhinay kiya. unke dwaara abhinit dharawahikon main pramukh agle janam mohe bitiyaa hi kijo, palampur express, kathputli or channel ve par gumraah hai.
 rajesh kaa janm 17 may 1962 ko uganda main hua. unka vivaa 1994 main jyoti se hua jinase unke nanaki or yuvaraj naamak do bachche hue. 
 ragesh ne 1987 main bharat rangacharya dwaara nirdeshit dharawahik subah main abhinayta ke rup main padaarpan kiya jisamen unhone appu swaami kaa abhinay kiya tha. ragesh ko pehle ek anya abhinay prem ke liye chunaa gaya tha lekin vahaan se baad main unhen nirast kar diya gaya.
 20 march 2014 ko mumbai main hridayghaat ke kaaran unka nidhan ho gaya.
