 jaan-maari lahn francisi rasayan vigyaani hai. unhe donald cram or charles pederson ke saath 1987 main rasayan par unke kritand ke sanshleshan ke liye nobel puraskaar puraskaar mila. ve vishaal anukanika rasayan shaastra ke kshetra main agragami vaigyanik hai; is kshetra main kaam karte hue kai upyogi mishranon kaa avishkaar kiya. unhone takreeban 800 lekh rasayan vigyaan par likhen hai.
