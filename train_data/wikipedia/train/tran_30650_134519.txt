 babil aajkal ke iraq kshetra kii sumer ke pashchaat duusari sabhyataa. is kii bhashaa sumer kii bhashaa se bhinn thi. ise babylonian sabhyataa ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. babil nagar jo isi naam ke samrajya kii rajdhani tha, aajkal ke baghdad se 80 kilometre dakshin main sthit tha. babylon pracheen mesopotamia kaa ek nagar tha. yah babylonia samrajya kaa kendra tha.
