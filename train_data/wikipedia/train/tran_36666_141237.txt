 kafir ; bahuvachan كفّار kuffaar) islam main arabi bhashaa kaa bahut vivaadit shabd he jiskaa shabdic arth - "asweekaar karne vaala" yaa "dhakne vaala" hota he kintu islamic mat main "kafir" shabd us vyakti ke liye prayukt hota he jo allaah ko nahin maanata, yaa jo mohammad ko allaah kaa rasul nahin maanata . aajkal is shabd ko apmaanasoochak mana jane laga he; isi liye kuch muslim iske sthaan par "gaiyr-muslim" shabd kaa prayog karne kii salaah dete hai.
