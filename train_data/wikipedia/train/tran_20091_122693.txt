 
 ardbeel praant iran ke 31 praanton main se ek he jo us desh ke pashchimottaree bhaag main sthit he. isaki rajdhani ardbeel naam kaa shahar hi he. yahan bahut se azeri log rahete hai or ise 'irani azarbejaan' kaa hissaa mana jaataa he.
 ardbeel praant main 9 shaharistaan hai - ardbeel, bilasavaar, germi, khalaal​, kausar​, meshaginshhar​, namin​, nir​ or parsabad​. praant main sabalan parvat vistrut hai jinase yahan kaafii sardi rahati he. bahut se sailaani yahan garmiyon main thande mausam kaa anand lene aate hai, jabaki sardiyon main yah ilaaka barngrast hota he or yahan kuch dhalaanon par ski kaa bandobast bhi he. bahut se log ise iran kaa sabase sard praant maanate hai or sardiyon main yahan taapmaana -25° centegrade tak gir jaataa he. yahan bahut si jhilein, nadi-jharne or garm chashmen bikhare hue hai. ardbeel praant kii adhiktar aabaadi azeri, taalish or taat samudaay kii he.
 kaha jaataa he ki parsi dharm ke sansthaapak zarathushtra aras nadi ke kinare paidaa hue the or unhone apane granth kii rachana sabalan pahaado main hi kii. jab iran par muslim qabza hua tab ardbeel azarbejaan kshetra kaa sabase bada shahar tha or uskaa yah sthaan mongol akramanon tak bana rahaa. prasiddh sufi sant sheikh safiuddin kaa mazebara bhi ardbeel praant main sthit he.
