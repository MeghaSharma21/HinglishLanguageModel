 sendai japan ke miyagi praant kii rajdhani, tohoku kshetra kaa sabase bada shahar or tokyo ke uttar main doosraa sabase bada shahar he. 1 agast 2017 tak, shahar kii aabaadi 1,086,012 thi, or japan ke 20 manonith shaharon main se ek he. shahar kaa kul kshetrafal 786.30 varg kilometre he.
 11 march, 2011 ko, shahar ke tateey ilaakon main 9.0 apatati bukamp se bhayaavah kshati hui, jisase ek vinaashkari tsunami bhi utpann hui.
 sendai 38°16'05" uttar evam 140°52'11" purvi akshansh main sthit he. shahar kaa kshetrafal 788.09 kimi² he, or yah prashanth mahaasaagar se oyu parvat tak failaa hua he, joki miyagi praant kii purvi or pashchimi simaaen hai. natijatan, shahar kaa bhugolic paridrishy kaaphi vividhpurna he. jaise purvi sendai ek maidani kshetra he, shahar kaa centre paharinuma he, or pashchimi kshetra par oonchi parvatamalayen hai. shahar kaa sabase ooncha sthaan fanagata parvat he, joki samudratal se 1,500 meter ooncha he. herose nadi sendai main 45 kilometre tak faili hui he. nadi ke prakritik nahar kaa upyog kar iske kinare sendai kila banaaya gaya tha. 1950 ke dashak tak nadi main aksar baadh aayaa karti thi, lekin 1960 or 1970 ke dashak main banaaye gayi baandhon ke kaaran yah ab kabhi-kabhi hi aate he.
 senda main ek ardra up-ushnkaribandheey jalwaayu hai, isiliye yahan na to tokyo kii tarah bahut garm garmiyaan hoti hai, or na hi sapporo kii tarah sardiyon main barfbaari hoti hai. halanki mahatvapurn mausami antar barkaraar rahata he, sardiyaan shant or apekshaakrit shushk hoti hai, jisamen janvari kaa ausat taapmaana 1.5 degree celsius tak chalaa jaataa hai. greeshmakaal main ausatan taapmaana 24.1 degree celsius tak chalaa jaataa he. shahar main tufaan bahut kam hi aate he. yahan 10 centimetre se adhik varsha vaale ausatan keval 6 din hi hote he.
 2005 main, shahar kii anumaanit aabaadi 1,028,214 thi or jansankhya ghanatv 1,304.10 vyakti prati kimi² tha. shahar kaa kul kshetrafal 788.09 kimi² hai. is samay shahar ke adhikansh aabaadi train or metro steshnon ke kareeb shahari kshetron main basate hai. sendai main ausat aayu 38.4 he, jo is shahar ko japan ke sabase kam ausat aayu vaale shaharon main se ek bana detaa he.
 sendai tohoku kshetra kii arthvyavastha kaa centre he, or yah kshetra ke rasad or parivahan kaa adda he. 2010 main sendai mahanagar kshetra kii jidipi, 61.7 yues dollar billian thi. shahar kii arthvyavastha khudara baazaaraa or sevaaon par nirbhar karti he, rojgaar kaa lagbhag do tihaai hissaa inhen do udyog se aata hai. 2016 main sendai ke liye anumaanit sakal utpaadan 45.4 million america dollar thi. kshetra main vaarshik vastu kii bikri lagbhag 73.5 million america dollar tak pahunchati he, jabaki vinirmit saamaan kii maatra 10 arab america dollar tak pahunchati he. tohoku electric power, bijli ke ek pramukh kshetreey saplir, kaa mukhyalay sendai main he. 2016 main, sendai paryaton ne anumaanit 2.229 million aagantukon ko aakarshith kiya.
