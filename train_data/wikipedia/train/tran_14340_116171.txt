 ganga nadi apni yatra main jitna bada bhu bhaag paar karti he usamen pahari or maidani jalwaayu kaa ek bada hissaa aata he. ghaney van, khule maidan or unche pahaado ke saath chalati yah nadi anek prakaar ke pashu pakshi or vanastapatiyon ko aashray detii hai. isamen machhliyon kii 140 prajatiyaan, 35 sarisrup tathaa iske tat par 42 standhari prajatiyaan pai jaati hai. is kshetra main paye jane vaale saamaanya jeev-jantuon main hai langur, laal bandar, bhure bhaaluu, saamaanya lomadee, chitah, barfile chitah, bhunkane vaale hiran, sambar, kasturi mrug, sero, barad mrug, saahi, tahar aadi. vibhinn rangon kii titliyaan tathaa kiten bhi yahaan paayi jaati hai. himalayan siti bajaati sarikaayen, swanirm kiritadhari, paashchaatya rang-virange hasod, saakhien monal evam kocal teetar, chakve aadi yahan ke pramukh pakshi hai.
 is kshetra main vanaspatiyon kii vishaal prajatiyaan he. himalay kaa balut sarvaadhik pramukh he. anya main shaamil hai burans, safed saro, svachh ped, sadabahaar ped tathaa nile deodaar aadi hai. jab balut ke ped villin ho rahe hote he to panger, kabasi, kanjula tathaa ringal isaki jagah aa jate hai. pararnang, visarpi paudhe tathaa shaivak kii yahaan bahutayat he.
