 kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay main ‘madhvi’ kaa natya-manchan
 kaashi ke saanskrithik gaurav kii pahchaan he.
 ‘madhvi’ mahabharat ke udyog parv se lee gai ek samvedansheel katha he. isamen katha nayika madhvi ek pitrisataatmak samaaj main vidambnaapurna jivan jeene ko abhishpt he. purusharth dambh ke prabal aaveg main madhvi ke khud kaa koi astitv nahin. madhvi apane mahatvakankshi pita raja yayati dwaara munikumar gaalav ko bhent kar di jaati he. kaaran yah he ki vah munikumar gaalav ke 800 ashvamedhi ghodon kii yachana ko puraa nahin kar paata he. duusari ore munikumar gaalav rishi vishwamitra ko gurudakshina ke rup main 800 ashvamedhi ghodon ko dene ke liye vachanabaddh he.
 madhvi ko paakar munikumar gaalav yah sochataa he ki ab uske paas ekmaatr madhvi hi vah saadhan he jiske dwaara vah apane guru ke vachan ko purna kar saktaa he. atisundar rupavati madhvi ko nav-yauvan prapti kaa aashirvad mila he saath hi yah bhi ki madhvi ke garbh se utpann balak bhavishya main ek pratapi samraat hoga. munikumar gaalav madhvi ko lekar aryaavat ke un samraaton ke paas jaataa he jo madhvi ke badale use 800 ashvamedhi ghode pradaan kar sake. kai raajaaon ko pati rup main swikaarne ko vivash madhvi har jagah maansik evam shaareerik pratadna ko jhelti he. use apane mamatv or vatsalya kaa bhi tyaag karnaa padataa he. sabase kaarunik sthiti tab utpann hoti he jab bebas madhvi bhavaheen mudra main rishi vishwamitra tak ko varan kar leti he kyonki munikumar gaalav madhvi ko kai raajaaon ko saunpkar bhi 800 ashvamedhi ghodon ko nahin juta paata he. yah puri kahani madhvi ke ird-gird gumti hui is sach ko ujagar karti he ki har purush stri deh ko apni ichhaapoorti kaa saadhan-maatr samajhataa he. use stri man, buddhi, aatma or chetana kaa khyaal nahin hota he. vah saundarya kaa upaask to he kintu vah stri ke upar swachand shashan kii bhi adamya ichha rakhata he.
