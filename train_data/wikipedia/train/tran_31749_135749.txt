 
 lagmaan afghanistan kaa ek praant he jo us desh ke purv main sthit he. is praant kaa kshetrafal 3,843 varg kimi he or isaki aabaadi san 2008 main lagbhag 3.8 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. is praant kii rajdhani mehtar laam shahar he. is praant main pashtun logon kii bahusankhya he or ve kul jansankhya kaa 58% hai. yahan nuristaani log or pashai log bhi rahete hai or kam sankhya main farsi-bhashi tajik logon ke bhi samudaay hai. lagmaan main aramai bhashaa main likhi ashok ke aadesh vaali do shilaayein bhi maujuud hai.
 'lagmaan' main 'ga' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'ga' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'galati' or 'garib' shabdon ke 'ga' se miltaa he.
 sikandar mahaan ne jab is kshetra par akraman kiya to is ilaake kaa naam 'lampaak' hua karata tha. saatvi sadi isvi main buddha chini dharmayatri hwain tsang apane bharat-daure main lagmaan se guzare the or unhone likhaa ke yahan ke log hindu hai or kam sankhya main buddha bhi yahan based hai. yahan par bahut se hindu mandir or buddha dharm kii mahayan shakha ke kai math the. baad main yahan jaipal janjua ke adheen hindushahi raaj rahaa jise gazanvi rajvansh ke pehle raja sabook tigin ne parajeet kiya. uske uparant yahan islamickaran hua.
