 uttari sagar andh mahaasaagar kaa ek simavarti samudr he jo united kingdom, scandenevia, germany, frans, netherland or belgium ke bich sthit he. yah andh mahaasaagar ke saath dakshin main angrezi khadi ke dwaara or uttar main narveyi sagar ke dwaara judaa hua he. yah 970 kimi se lambaa or 580 kimi chaudaa he or kshetrafal lagbhag 750,000 varg kimi he. 
 uttari sagar purab main europe mahadweep or pashchim main great britain se ghira he. echosina ke anusaar isaki gaharaai 308 foot he. is prakaar yah ek uthlaa sagar he. iskaa nital us mahadwipiya nidhaay kaa ek bhaag he jiske upar british dweepasmooh sthit he. is nidhaay ko dhaal uttar se dakshin tak praaia: ek samaan he. dougar banks naamak samudr main nimagn baaluu kaa maidan uttari sagar ke madhya main sthit he. england ke samudratat ke sameep is sagar kii gaharaai 65 foot he jo purv kii or badhkar 130 foot ho jaati he. is sagar kii saamaanya lavanta 34 se 35 prati sahasr he.
 uttari sagar sookshm jeevon or paudhon main vishesh rup se dhani he. isliye machhliyaan idhar prachur maatra main, apane bhojan kii khoj main, aakarshith hoti he. falat: uttari sagar vishwa kaa ek mahatvapurn matsay utpaadak kshetra he. matsay ke prapthisthanon main dougar banks or mahadwipiya samudratat ke sameep sthit uthle samudr pramukh hai. pakadi janevaali machhliyon main hering kaa anupaat sabase adhik rahata he; iske baad krmanusaar hadak, cod, place, huiting, maikerala ityadi aati hai.
