 darkhan-ool mongolia ke uttari bhaag main sthit us desh kaa ek aimag he. yah puri tarah selenge praant se ghir hua he or 1994 tak usee praant kaa hissaa hua karata tha. rashtriya rajdhani ulanbatar ke baad yah desh kaa doosraa sabase bada odyogic kendra he.
 'darkhan-ool' main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he. mongol bhashaa main 'darkhan' kaa arth 'pavitra' or 'ool' kaa arth 'parvat' hota he, yani praant ke naam kaa matlab 'pavitra parvat' he.
