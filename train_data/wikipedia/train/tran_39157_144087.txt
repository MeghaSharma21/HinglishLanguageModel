 macidonian dinar macidonia ganraajya kii mudra he. yah 100 dini se samavibhajit he. dinar naam pracheen roman maudrik ikai dinariyas se lee gai he.
 pehali dinar 26 april 1992 ko jari kii gai thi, jisane 1990 main jari kiye gaye yugoslav dinar ke sthaan par samamoolya par jari kii gai thi. 5 may 1993 ko nai dinar jari kii gai, puraane 100 dinar ke sthaan par ek dinar ke mulya par.
 pehle dinar ke saath koi sikaa jari nahin kiya gaya tha. 1993 main duusari dinar jari karte samay 50 dini, 1, 2 or 5 dinar moolyavarg ke sikke jari kiye gaye. haal hi main 10 or 20 dinari sikke jari kiye gaye hai.
 1992 main 10, 25, 50, 100, 500, 1000, 5000 or 10,000 dinari moolyavarg ke banknote jari kiye gaye. 
 1993 main 10, 20, 50, 100 or 500 moolyavarg ke nae dinar jari kiye gaye. pehali shrinkhalaa main keval 20 dinari ke note jari kiye gaye the. 1996 main 1000 or 5000 dinari ke note isamen shaamil kiye gaye.
