 kamaljit sandhu ek purv bhartiya mahila khilaadi hai jinhone 1992 bainkak asian khelon main 400 meter daud main swarna padak jeeta tha. vah 57.3 second main duuri par thi. vah asian khelon main swarna padak jeetne vaali pehali bhartiya mahila khilaadi thim. vah bharat ke punjab rajya se he . 1971 main unhen padam shri puraskaar mila. 1971 main, vah 400 meter kii daud main turine, italy main yojit vishwa vishvavidhyalay khelon main final main pahunchne vaali mahilaon main se ek thi. unhone 1973 munik olympic main mahilaon kii 400 meter daud main bhaag liya. kamaljit 1973 main athletics se sevanivrutt hui. vah ek rashtriya str ke basketbaal or antar vishvavidhyalay hockey khilaadi bhi thi. 1982 main unhone bhartiya mahila sprint team ke coach ke rup main asian khelon main bhaag liya.
