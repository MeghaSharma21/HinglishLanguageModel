 raman singh ek bhartiya rajaneta he or chhattisgarh ke vartmaan mukhyamantri hai. raman singh 1990 or 1993 main madhyapradesh vidhansabha ke sadsy rahe. uske baad san 1999 main ve loksabhaa ke sadsy chune gayi. 1999 or 2003 main unhone bharat sarkaar main rajya mantri kaa bhi pad sambhala. 2004 main huye vidhansabha ke chunaavon main unhone safalataa pai or chhattisgarh rajya ke mukhyamantri bane.
 2008 ke vidhansabha chunaavon main unhone phir se safalataa paayi or rajya ke punah mukhyamantri bane. teesri baar ve 2013 main chhattisgarh ke mukhyamantri chune gayi.
 dow raman singh kaa janm chhattisgarh ke vartmaan kabirdham zile ke gram thathapur main ek krishak parivaar main 15 oktober, 1952 ko hua tha. 1975 main ayurvedic medicine main bi.e.em.es. kii oopaadhi praapt kii. raman singh ke parivaar main patni vinaa singh or do bachche hai.
 raman singh ne apane rajnatik jivan kii shuruyaat bhartiya jansangh ke yuva sadsy ke taur par kii thi. raman singh 1990 or 1993 main madhyapradesh vidhansabha ke sadsy rahe. uske baad san 1999 main ve loksabhaa ke sadsy chune gayi. varsh 1999 main hue rajnandagaanv, chhattisgarh se loksabhaa chunaavon main jeet milane ke baad unke rajnatik kariyar ko ek nayaa aayam mil gaya. tatkaaleen pradhanmantri shri atal bihari bajpeyi ne unhen apni sarkaar main vaanijya or udyog rajyamantri banaaya. 2003 main jab chhattisgarh rajya kaa pehla chunaav hona nishchit hua to bhartiya janata party main aise vyakti kii talash hui jo chunaavon se purv ke nirnaayak maheenon main party sanghathan ko gatisheel kar sake. sanghathan kshamta or sabko saath lekar chalane kii dakshata se sampann dow raman singh ko yah daayitv saunpa gaya. pehali baar bhaajapa ko chhattisgarh ke vidhansabha chunaavon main badi safalataa mili. ek disambar 2003 ko chhattisgarh ke itihaas main bhaajapa ke vijay diwas ke rup main darj kiya gaya. dow raman singh chhattisgarh ke pratham nirvaachit mukhyamantri bane. jiske baad se ve abhi tak is pad par kabiz he. kuch raajnneetigyon ke anusaar ve 2019 ke aam chunaav ke pashchaat bhaajapa pradhan mantri ho sakate hai.
