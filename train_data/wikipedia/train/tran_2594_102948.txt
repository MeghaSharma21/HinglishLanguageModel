 
 hydrilla ek jaleey paudha he. yah puure varsh talabon main paaya jaataa he tathaa sharad ritu main yah adhik maatra main ugta he. yah jal main 2 meter kii gaharaai tak ugta he tathaa isak tana 1-2 meter lanbaa hota he. hydrilla kaa paudha komal va kuch hare pile rang kaa hota he. il paudhe main jaden nahin hoti hai tathaa patale tanen par choti va patali pattiyaan bhrami rup main lagi rahati hai. isaki pattiyaan 5-20 millimetre lambii evam 0.7-2 millimetre tak chaudee hoti hai.
