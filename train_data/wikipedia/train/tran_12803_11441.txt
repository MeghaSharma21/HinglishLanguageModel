 dilli kii vidhansabha rashtriya rajdhani kshetra dilli kii ekasadani vidhaayi nikaay he. is vidhaan sabha main kul 70 vidhayak sadsy hai.dilli vidhansabha kii baithak purana sachivalay bhawan main hoti he.
 dilli rajya vidhansabha kaa gathan pehali baar 17 march 1952 ko part-si rajya sarkaar adhinium, 1951 ke tahat kiya gaya tha. lekin 1 oktober 1956 ko iskaa unmoolan kar diya gaya. phir sitambar 1966 main, vidhansabha kii jagah 56 nirvaachit or 5 manonith sadasyon vaali ek metropolitan council ne lee.haalanki, dilli ke shashan is parishad kii bhoomika keval ek salaahkaar kii thi or parishad ke paas kaanoon banane kii koi shakti nahin thi.
 varsh 1991 main 69wein samvidhan sanshodhan adhinium, 1991 or tatpashchaat rashtriya rajdhani kshetra adhinium, 1991 ne centre-shasit dilli ko aupchaarik rup se dilli rashtriya rajdhani kshetra kii pahchaan di or vidhaan sabha evam mantri-parishad se sambandhit sanvaidhanik praavdhaan nirdhaarit kiye.
 vidhansabha paanch saal kii avadhi ke liye chuni jaati he or vartmaan main dilli vidhansabha chunaav, 2013 dwaara chaynit yah paanchavi vidhansabha he.
 i. monteg thomas dwaara dizine kiye gaye purana sachivalay bhawan kaa nirmaan kaary 1912 main puraa kiya gaya. vidhaan parishad kii pehali baithak 27 janvari 1913 ko purana sachivalay ke chembar main hui.
 bharat kii rajdhani kalakatta se dilli sthanantarit hone ke baad is imaarat ne ek dashak ke liye bharat sarkaar ke sachivalay kii bhoomika bhi nibhaai. purana sachivalay ke chembar main hui charchaaon evam vidhaayi prakriyaaon ne bharat ke vartmaan sansad kii neenv rakhi.
