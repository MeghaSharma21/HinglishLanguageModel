 samaar dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh ke visaaya dweep dal ke purvi visaaya prashasnic kshetra main ek dweep he. yah desh kaa teesraa sabase bada dweep he or prashasnic rup se teen praanton main vibhajit he: samaar praant, uttari samaar praant or purvi samaar praant. samaar paas se leyate dweep se saan huaniko jalsandhi dwaara banta hua he lekin yatayaat ke liye isapar ek samudri pul banaaya gaya he.
