 vishakha utsav ek hindu tyohaar hai.anya tyoharon kii nason me, vishaakh utsav ek or tyohaar he jo andhra pradesh paryaton vikaas nigam dwaara yojit kiya jaataa he, taaki rajya me paryaton ko badhaava diya ja sake. yah tyohaar sabase vishisht ghatana he, jo kala, shilp, sangeet, nrity or kai saanskruthik ke kaaryakramon ke rup me paramparaon or samskruti ko manaata he. vishakhapatnam, bandargah shahar, is bhavy chaar divasiy tyohaar ke liye mahaan sthal banaata he. vishaakh utsav main bhaag lete hue andhra pradesh kii saanskruthik viraasat kii ek puri jhalak dekhne kaa ek adarsh tarika he. desh ke sabhi hisso or duniya ke log vishakha ke tyohaar kaa anand lete hai.
