 
 surendra kumaar sinha
  surendra kumaar sinha ek bangladeshi nyaayadhish tathaa purv bangladesh supreme court ke pratham hindu mukhya nyaayadhish hai. 17 janvari 2015 ko bangladesh ke raashtrapati ne desh ke supreme court ke pradhan nyaayadhish ke pad par niyukt kiya. sinha bangladesh supreme court ke 21 wein pradhan nyaayadhish hai.
 pita naam lalit mohan sinha or mata kaa naam dhanabati sinha he.
 inhone chittagong vishvavidhyalay se bidhi main snaatak oopaadhi prapti kii. 
 varsh 1974 main jila court silahat main adhivaktaa ke rup main panjikrut hue. 24 /10/1999 ko hai court divizan ke jaj bane or 16/07 /2009 ko bangladesh ke supreme court ke apiliy divizan main ucchikrut hue. 12/06/2011 ko bangladesh judishal sarvice kamishan ke chairman niyukt hue. 17//01/2015 ko yah bangladesh supreme court ke mukhya nyaayadhish bane. sinha ne bharat, nepal, katar, dakshin korea, singapore, thailand, indonesia, malaysia, honkong, china, sanyukt rajya america, britain or canada kaa daura kiya.
