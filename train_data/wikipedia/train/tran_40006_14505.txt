 maseehaa ibrahimi dharmon main aatma ko paap se moksh dilaane vaala yaa muktidaata he. maseehaa kaa mul yahudee dharm or hebrew bible main he. uske anusaar maseehaa vah manushya he jo daaud or suleman ke vansh kaa he or yahudiyon kaa mukhya pujari he. saath hi uskaa abhishek pavitra tel se kiya gaya ho. aisi manyata he ki vah apane bhavishya ke aagaman main purv nirdhaarit chizon ko puraa karegaa.
 isai dharm main isa ko maseehaa mana jaataa he. is manyata main isa ko ishvarputra mana gaya he or yah bhi mana jaataa he ki hebrew bible main likhit bhavishyavaanee ko unhone puraa kar diya he. saath main yah bhi manyata he ki isa dobaaraa aaenge or baaki bhavishyavaaniyon ko puraa karenge. islam main isa ko paigambar ke saath-saath maseehaa mana gaya he. aisi manyata he ki isa qayamat ke din laut kar aaenge or mahadi ke saath nakli maseehaa parajeet karenge.
