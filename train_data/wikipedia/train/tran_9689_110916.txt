 alambaag ) bhartiya shahar lakhanu main kanpur maarg par sthit he. yah lakhanu ke sabase mahatvapurn awasaiya, vanijyik or shahar ke ghani aabaadi vaale kshetron main se ek he. alambaag lakhanu chhavnee kshetra main aata he.
 isse pehle alambaag main ek mahal, ek masjid or anya imaaraton ke saath hi saath ek sundar bagichaa bhi tha. ise november 1857 main pratham swatantraaa sangram ke dauraan ek kile main parivartit kar diya gaya. sar james outrum, kile par baar-baar hamalaa karne ke baavjood march 1858 tak asafal rahaa jab tak ki sar colin campbell lakhanu par hamalaa karne ke liye laut nahi aayaa. angrejon dwara vidrohiyon ko parajeet karne ke baad yah lakhanu or aasapaas ke shaharon ke liye sainya cmand centre ke rup main kaary me laayaa gaya.:489
 alambaag lakhanu ke dakshin chor kii taraf he or aasapaas ke gaanvon ke liye ek aam bazaar sthaan ke rup main kaary karata he. aas-paas ke gaanvon ke kisaan har subah alambag main puri tarah subjiyan or vitarakon or sthaneey khudara vikritaon ko fasal bechate hai, jo badale main lakhanu shahar bhar main khudara vikritaon ko subjiyan or fasal vitarit karte hai.
 alambaag ek bas depo bhi he. yah dakshin main kanpur road, pashchim main sitapur road or anya do dishaao main rajya sarkaar dwaara banaai gai sadakon se shahar ke anya hisson se acchhee tarah se judaa hua he. 
 alambaag main lakhanu ke kuch sabase pratishthit skool hai.
