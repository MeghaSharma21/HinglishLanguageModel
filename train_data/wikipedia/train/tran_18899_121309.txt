 
 sil yaa piniped maansaahaari samudri standhari praaniyon kaa ek vividh claide he. isamen bhinn prakaar kii seelein, jalavyaghr, valras, ityadi shaamil hai. vartmaan vishwa main 33 gyaat piniped jatiyaan astitv main hai lekin jeevashmon se 50 vilupt jatiyon kii pahchaan bhi kii gai he. piniped maansaahaari jeevvaigyanik gana ke sadsy hai or bhaaluu, mustalidaye kul ke praani inke sab se qareebi anuvaanshiki sambandhi hai. kramavikas adhyayan se pataa chalaa he ki in sabhi praaniyon ke purvaj ek-duusare se aaj se lagbhag 5 karod varsh purv alag hue.
