 24 sitambar 2015 ko makka main haj yatra ke dauraan hui ek bhagdad main kam se kam 719 hajiyon kii maut ho gayi or 863 anya yatri ghayal ho gayi. 1990 ke haj bhagdad ke baad jisamen 1426 logon kii maut ho gayi thi, makka main hui yah sabase khatarnaak or bady durghatana he. iran kii sarkari samachar agency kaa kahana he ki iran kii hajayatra samiti ke anusaar 1300 se zyada logon ke maut hone kii ashanka he. lebanon ke ad-diyaar kii soochna ke anusaar rajkumaar mohammad bin salmaan al saood ke kaafile ne is bhagdad main kendriya bhoomika nibhaai.
 haj yatra makka main hone vaali muslimon kii ek tirth yatra he jo unhen jivan main kam se kam ek baar kerani hoti hai. paramparanusaar iske dauraan tirthayatriyon ko vibhinn reetiyaan nibhaani hoti hai jiname se ek he shaitan ko pathar maarna jo ki minaa main sthit jamaaraat pul ke paas he. yah shahar makka se kuch mile dur he. jamaraat pul ek paanch manjila paidal pul he jisapar se tirthayatri jamaaraat pattharon par kankad maar sakate hai. pathar maarane kaa yah rivaaz tirthayatra kaa antim padav he or aksar ise bahut zyada bheed, kam jagah or kam samay kii vajah se haj yatra kaa sabase khatarnaak hissaa mana jaataa he. pehle bhi tirth yatra ke is padaav par dheron bhagdad ke haadase ho chuke hai. 
 2006 main hue isi tarah kii ek ghatana main 346 logon kii mrityu ho gayi thi jisaki vajah se saudi sarkaar ko is shahar main dhaanchaagat vyavasthaaon main sudhaar ke kadam uthaane padey the. 11 sitambar 2015, ko hi haj yatra shuru hone se hafton pehle makka kii ek masjid main ek vishaal kraine ke girane se 118 log maare gayi.
 17 sitambar 2015, ko 1000 se zyada tirthayatriyon ne apane hotel aag lagane kii vajah se chhod die, is ghatana main 2 indonesian naagarik ghayal ho gayi the.
 saudi arab kii sarkaar grand masjid ke vistaar main 60 arab dollar kaa nivesh kar rahi thi jisamen kaaba sthit he. yahaan 1 lakh se zyada surakshakarmi va 5000 se zyada sisitivi camere bheed par nazar rakhane ke liye lage hue the.
