 kaibar-pakhtuunakva pakistan kaa ek praant yaa suba he. ise suba-e-sarhad ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he jo afghanistan kii seemaa par sthit he.yahan par pashtunon kii aabaadi adhik he jinhen sthaneey rup se pakhtun bhi kehete hai. inaki maatrubhaasha pashto he. is praant kii jansankhya kareeb 2 karod he jisamen afghanistan se aae sharnaarthiyon kii 15 lakh kii aabaadi sammilit nahin he.
 aaryon kaa aagaman isa ke koi 2000 saal pehle aarambh hua. is kshetra main indo-iranian shakha ai. mana jaataa he ki saatvin sadi isapurv main hindu mahajanapad gandhar yaheen yaa isi ke sameep sthit tha. isa ke 200 saal pehle buddha dharm yahan bahut lokapriy hua. mauryon ke patan ke baad isapar kushaanon kaa shashan aayaa. yah kushaan samrajya kii rajdhani tha or islam aane se pehle isapar irani aakarman bhi hote rahe. isse yahan zarathushtra ke anuyaayiyon kii bhi aabaadi thi.
 saatvin sadi main china ke paryatakon ne yahan ke buddha dharm kaa zikr kiya he. gyarahavin sadi main gazani ke mahmud ne buddha tathaa joroastry shahon ko haraakar apana shashan sthapit kiya. gazani tathaa gazani par gore ke shashan ke baad yahan turk tathaa arabon kii aabaadi badhati gai. dilli sultanate ke shashan main bhi yahan islam apanaaya gaya. mughalon tathaa faras ke safaviyon ke bich is kshetra ko lekar sangharsh hota rahaa. 1893 main angrezon ne afagaanon se yah kshetra ek samjhaute main le liya or 1947 main jab pakistan azaad hua to yah pakistan kaa ang ban gaya.uttar pashchim seemaant praant main 24 jile hai.
