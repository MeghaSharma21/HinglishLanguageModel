 mira sanyaal ek bhartiya investment banker or raajnneetijna hai. ve abn amro bank kii mukhya kaaryakari adhikari rah chuki hai,jinhone nirdaleey pratyaashee ke rup main varsh 2009 main south mumbai seat se loksabhaa chunaav ladane kaa faisla kiya. ve lambey samay se saamudayik vikaas kaaryakramon or nitigat sudhaaron se judi rahi hai.vartmaan main ve ek raajnneetijna ke rup main aam aadmi party se judi hui hai tathaa 2014 ke loksabhaa chunaav kii pratyaashee hai.
 mira kaa janm kochi main hua. unki praarambhik shiksha cathedral or jon conan skool mumbai, lorato convent, dilli or kile convent skool, mumbai se hui tathaa insid, france se unhone emb kii shiksha grahan kii. saath hi ve harvard bijness skool se 2006 main unnat prabandhan kaarykram ke liye chaynit hui. ve chartered institute of bankers se felo or sidenham college, mumbai se vaanijya vishay ke saath snaatak hai.
 mira sanyaal bhartiya nausena ke itihaas main veerta puraskaar paane vaale tathaa kai prakashnon ke lekhak or sangh lok seva ayog ke ek sadsy admiral swargeey gulab mohanlal hiranandani or bano hiranandani kii beti he. unka vivaah e em pi retail services ke nideshak ashish je sanyaal se hua. ve dow maanik hiranandani kii bahan hai.
