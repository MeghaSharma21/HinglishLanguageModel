 swami saradanand ramakrishna paramhans ke sanyasi shishyon main se ek the. unka purvashram kaa naam sharathchandra chakrabarti tha. ramakrishna mishan kii sthapana ke baad ve iske pratham sampadak bane or mrityu tak is pad par bane rahe. shrimaan sharada devi ke rahane ke liye unhone kolkata main udvodhan bhawan kaa nirmaan karavaaya or bangla patrika udbodhan kaa prakaashan kiya. saradanandane shriramkrishna leelaprasang naamak vikhyath pustak kii rachana kii.
 swami saradanand ne ramakrishna kii pramanik jeevani granth "shriramkrishna leelaprasang" kii rachana kii.paanch khandon main rachit yah granth ramakrishna kii jivaniyon main sarvshereshth hain. iske atirikt ve 'bharat main shaktipuja' or 'gitatv' naamak do pustak bhi likhe.
