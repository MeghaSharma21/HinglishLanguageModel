 sankhyikeey prakram niyantran ) gunvattta niyantran kii ek vidhi he jo sankhyikeey vidhiyon kaa prayog karti he. iskaa vikaas bell prayogshaala ke dow walter shevheart ne 1920 ke dashak main kiya tha. dwitiya vishwayuddh ke baad dow dablyoo edwards deming ne japan main iskaa vistaar kiya. jab japani companiyon ko isse aarmabhik safalataa mili, tab ise vishwavyaapee khyaati or sweekaaryata mili.
