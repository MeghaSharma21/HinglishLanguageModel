 katal suryavanshi rajput raashtra kii ek shakha he.
 katal raashtra bharat or pakistan main maujuud punjab ke kshetron main pai jaati he. is janajati ke 360 gaanv based hai, jinamein se 100 ke kareeb jile narowal or gurdaspur main paae jate hai. or jammu main unki anusmarak rahe hai. yah apane aap psoria nashiy jataate hai. ye log khokhron se shaadi nahin karte.
 mughal samraat aurangzeb ke daur main katal, raav, baleel, maal, nahaalah jati ke log islam main chale gaye. or tehsil shakargarh ke katal muslim hai magar jammu or bharat ke katal hindu hai.
 1- drishyata main he ki raja tido, kiran, sompaal, barshapat, vigidhitha, didhdata, munh, aadhaar paal, ratan paal, tara, raja saahasrano, raja santeel, raja carat, raja kod, raja chit, raja gora, raja bharat, raja bill, raja shikha or uskaa betaa raja jisarath. unhen khirio jaipur se gazanah mohammad ne nikaalkar jammu main managalah devi ke sthaan par basaaya. unamem se ek raja carat vansh samba gaanv ke paas jangal main dakaiti karne lagi. unhone samba gaanv kii ek ladki kaa apharan kar liya. is ladki ke bhaaiyon ne unhen tehsil shakargarh ke bahut bade kshetra main faili hui jameen de di. unhone is jagah par baseraa kiya or is jagah ko katal naam diya k en pidiyon aajkal katal kaha jaataa he.
 2- drishyata main he ki mughalon ke dur main rajputon kaa ek raja tha jise mughalon se haar hui to mughalon ne unhen apane kshetra se nikaalkar ] kshetra main basaaya. or in dinon shakargarh or jammu ke bich ek jangal hua karata tha. is raja ko kaha gaya ki subah se lekar shaam tak tum is jangal ke jitne kshetra ko tah kar loge to kshetra tumhaare hawaale kar diya jayega. is raja ne aisa hi kiya or jo kshetra bhi hasil kiya uskaa naam katal rakh diya jo punjabi shabd katli jiske arth jangal kshetra ke hai rakh diya. aajkal is raja kii pidiyon jo is kshetra main basti hai katal ke naam se pukaara jaataa he.
