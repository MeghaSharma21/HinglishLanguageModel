 tshona zila dakshinpurvi tibet main lhokha vibhaag kaa ek zila he. janavaadee ganatantr china kaa tibet par 1950 ke dashak main qabza ho jane ke baad china bharat ke arunachal pradesh ke ek bade bhubhag ko is zile kaa hissaa bataane laga. yah vivaadit kshetra bharat ke niyantran main hai. is zile kii rajdhani bhi tshona naamak basti hi he.
