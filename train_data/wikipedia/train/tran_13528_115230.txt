 pakistan khape punjabi, sindhi or urdu bhashaaon kaa ek vivaadit naaraa he. iskaa punjabi or urdu main arth hota he "pakistan khap jaae " lekin sindhi main arth hota he "pakistan chahie ." iske vipareet sindhi bhashaa main "pakistan na khape" kaa matlab he ke "hamen pakistan nahin chahie."
 sindhi algaavvaadi aksar apane jalson main "pakistan na khape" ke naare lagate hai. benazir bhutto kii maut par unki "pakistan piplz party" ke kaaryakartaaon ne "pakistan na khape" ke raashtra-virdohi naare lagaae the. is vidrohi bhaavana ko shant karne ke liye benazir bhutto ke pati or aage chalakar pakistan ke banane vaale raashtrapati asif ali zaradaari ne "pakistan khape" kaa jawaab diya. isapar unke aalochakon ne prashn uthaaya he ke unka taatpary is naare kaa sindhi arth he yaa punjabi.
