 sankhyatmak ganit main punraritimulak vidhi gananaa kii vah vidhi he jisamen kisi anumaanit hal se shuru karte hai or ek hi sutara yaa kalanvidhi kaa barbar prayog karte hue adhik shuddh hal praapt karte hai. yah hal punah us sutara main prayukt hokar or bhi adhik shuddh hal detaa he. udhaaharan ke liye kisi sameekaran kaa mul nikaalane ke liye yaa ishtatmakaran ke liye iskaa prayog kiya jaataa he. 
 punraritimulak vidhiyon ke vipareet ek hi charan main hal pradaan karne vaali vidhiyon ko pratyaksh vidhi kehete hai. udhaaharan ke liye, raikhik samikarnon ke nikaay Ax = b kaa hal pratyaksh vidhi se nikaalane ke liye A kaa invars matrix nikaala jaataa he.)
 computer prograamon main in punraritimulak vidhiyon kaa bahut hi dakshata se upyog karke ganitiya samasyaon ke hal nikaale jate hai. newton vidhi, gouse-cidal vidhi sahit anekon vidhiyaan is varg main aati hai.
 punraritimulak vidhiyaan kahan adhik upyogi hoti hai?
 kintu punraritimulak vidhiyon main apsaar kii samasya ho sakati he yaa bahut dhimi gati se abhisar kii samasya aa sakati he. atah kisi punraritimulak vidhi ke abhisar kii sharton kaa adhyayan ek bahut mahatvapurn or vishaal kshetra he.
