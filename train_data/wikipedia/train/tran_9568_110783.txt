 panun kashmir kashmir ke visthapit hinduon kaa sanhathan he. isaki sthapana san 1990 ke disambar maah main kii gayi thi. is sanghathan kii maang he ki kashmir ke hinduon ke liye kashmir ghati se ek alag rajya kaa nirmaan kiya jaay. dhyatavya he ki san 1990 main kashmir ghati se lagbhag sampoorn hinduon ko pakistan samarthit aatankwaad ke chalate ghati se visthapit hona padaa tha.
