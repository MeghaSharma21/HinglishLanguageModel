 shrimati marina abramovik vishwa-prasiddh pradarshan kalakaar hai, jinhen "pradarshan kala kii matamahi" ke rup main janaa jaataa he. ve mulat: srbija desh se hai, parantu varttman main ve america ke neuyork nagar main rahati hai. inaki kala kaa uddeshy kalakaar or darshakon ke bich ke sambandh, maanav deh kii seemaa, evam man kii sambhaavnaaon kaa samanveshan karnaa he.
 marina devi kaa janm shaq 1868 main beograd nagar main hua. dwitiya vishwa yuddh main naayakatv ke liye unke mata-pita ko yugoslavia shashan ne "rashtranayak" ke padavi se sammanit kiya. shaq 1894 main marina devi ne kroeshia kii lalit kala academy se snatakottar shiksha puri kii. tatpashchaat ve amsterdam main bas gayi. shaq 1869 main unhone edinburgh nagar main unki pehali kala pradarshnee lagi. agle paanch dashakon main unki kala ko vishvabhar main saraahaa gaya. shaq 1919 main unhen swarnasingh puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. shaq 1925 main antarrashtriya kala aalochak sabha ne unke pradarshnee ko sarvshereshth ghoshit kiya. shaq 1931 main plymouth vishvavidhyalay ne unhen maanad kala doctoret kii oopaadhi pradaan kii.
