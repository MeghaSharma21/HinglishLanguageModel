 purvajagat bandar asia or africa ke mahadwipon par milane vaale bandaron kaa jeevvaigyanik kul he. isamen varshawanon, savana ghasbhumi or anya sthanon par rahane vaale kai bandar aate hai. europe main bhi is kul ke bandaron ke jeevashm mili hai or jibraltar par bhi kuch bandaron kii toliyaan astitv main hai haalanki yah shaayad anya jagahon se laai gai ho. jeevvaigyanik naam se purvajagat bandaron ke kul ko cercopithacidaye bulaayaa jaataa he. dhyaan de ki keval purvajagat ke bandar hi is kul main aate he. navjagat ke bandaron kaa kul alag he.
 purvajagat bandar madhyam se bade aakaar ke hote hai. inamen se kuch colobus bandaron jaise vrikshon par rahane vaale or kuch babunon jaise adhiktar dharti par ghoomane vaale hote hai. sabase choti jati tellapoin he, jisaki bina-punch kii lambaai 34-37 centimetre kii or vazan keval 700 gram se lekar 1.3 kilogram ke bich hota he. sabase bada purvajagat bandar mandrill jati kaa nar he jo 70 semi lambaa or 50 kilogram tak ke bhaar kaa hota he.
 dekhne main purvajagat bandar kapiyon se alag hote hai kyonki jahaan kapiyon kii koi vishesh dum nahin nahin hoti vahaan adhiktar purvajagat bandaron kii spasht punch hoti he. lekin yah navjagat bandaron se bhi alag dikhte hai kyonki adhiktar navjagat bandaron kii parigrahi punch hoti he jabaki purvajagat bandaron kii dumon main yah kshamta nahin hoti. purvajagat or navjagat bandaron ke daanto kii sankhya baraabar hoti he lekin inke daanto kaa akar alag-alag he. inaki nasikaon main bhi antar he - navjagat bandaron kii nasikaaye neeche kii ore khulti hai jabaki purvajagat bandaron kii dai-bai ore.
