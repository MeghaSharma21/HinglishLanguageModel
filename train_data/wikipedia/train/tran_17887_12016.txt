 savaiya ek chand he. yah chaar charnon kaa sampad varnachand he. varnik vritton main 22 se 26 akshar ke charan vaale jati chhandon ko samuhik rup se hindi main savaiya kehane kii parampara he. is prakaar saamaanya jati-vritton se bade or varnik dandakon se chote chand ko savaiya samajha ja saktaa he. 
 kavitt - ghanaakshari ke samaan hi hindi reetikaal main vibhinn prakaar ke savaiya prachlit rahe hai.
