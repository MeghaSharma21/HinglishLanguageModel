 chrometography ke siddhantonghatakon ke antar judaai main sthir or mobile charan ke parinaam kii ore analai ke vibhinn ghatakon ke antar samanthaayen : vibhinn ghatakon ke alag hone kaa siddhant. 'sokhna' or 'ghulanshiltaa': atmiyata, baari main, anu kii do sampattiyon se nirdhaarit hota he.ham kaise acchhee tarah se mishran kaa ek ghatak sthir charan ke liye lathi kii sampatti ke rup sokhna paribhaashit kar sakate hai, jabaki ghulanshiltaa kaise acchhee tarah se mishran kaa ek ghatak mobile charan main ghul kii sampatti he.ucchtar sthir charan ko sokhna, dhimi anu kollam ke madhyam se kadam hoga.ucchtar mobile charan main ghulanshiltaa, teji se anu kollam ke madhyam se kadam hoga.to, upar do karkon ke bich paraspar kriya antar daron jis par analai ke vibhinn ghatakon ke kollam ke madhyam se kadam hoga nirdhaarit karata he. sokhna or ek anu kii ghulanshiltaa uchit sthir charan or mobile charan chunane ke dwaara chaalaaki se kiya ja saktaa he.chrometography main ek taral kanon kii ek bistar ke madhyam se pump he. taral mobile charan or kanon sthir charan kaha jaataa he. anuon he ki alag kiya jayega kaa ek mishran mobile charan main shuru kii he. nile rang ke anuon neeche anemeshan main laal anuon se alag kiya jayega or anuon ke in do prakaar se yukt ek mishran sthir charan ke saamne mobile charan main shuru kii he. laal or nile rang ke anuon kaa mishran to sthir charan ke madhyam se mobile charan se le jaaya jaataa he. mishran he ki, laal anuon sthir charan ke liye sabase addsorbs is vishesh maamle main anu, kan bistar ke madhyam se dhimi chal rahi he. laal anuon nile rang se alag ho jate hai.
 chrometography ke vibhinn prakaar
 is lekh main ham, ham kya saamaanya charan chrometography ke rup main dekhen ke saath kaam kar rahe hai jiskaa arth he ki hamaare sthir charan prakruti main dhruviya he or hamaare mobile charan prakruti main gaiyr dhruviya he. vishesh anuprayogon ke liye, vaigyanikon kabhi kabhi reverse charan kromatographik takanic jahaan paridrishy ulat he yani sthir charan gaiyr dhruviya he, jabaki mobile charan dhruviya he rojgaar.prayek sthir or mobile charan kii tarah ve upyog main bhinn chrometography ke kai prakaar ke hote hai. ghatakon ke antar judaai main sthir or mobile ke charan ke parinaam kii ore analyte ke vibhinn ghatakon ke antar samanthaayen: mul siddhant haalanki hi rahata he. phir, sthir or mobile charnon ke saath vibhinn ghatakon kii baatcheet kii vidha kaa istemaal kiya chromatographic takanic ke aadhaar par badal saktaa he.
