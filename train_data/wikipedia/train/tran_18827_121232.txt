 vinayapatrika tulsidas rachit ek granth he. yah braj bhashaa main rachit he. vinay patrika main vinay ke pad he. vinayapatrika kaa ek naam ram vinayavali bhi he vinay patrika kii bhashaa braj he tathaa isamen 21 raagon kaa prayog hua he vinay patrika kaa pramukh ras shantaras he is ras kaa sthai bhav nirved hota he. vinay pratrika aadhyatmik jivan ko parilakshith karti he. is main sammalit padon kii sankhya 280 he.
 tulsidasji ab asighat par rahane lage. ratko ek din kaliyug murtarup dharankar unke paas aayaa or unhen traas dene laga. goswamijie hanumanji kaa dhyaan kiya. hanumanji ne unhen vinay ke pad rachneko kaha; isapar goswamiji ne vinay-patrika likhi or bhagavan ke charnonmen use samarpit kar diya. shriram ne usapar apane hastakshar kar diye or tulsidasji ko nirbhay kar diya.
