 sarvapratham shri ram kii katha bhagwaan shri shankar ne mata parvati ji ko sunaaya tha. us katha ko ek kauwe ne bhi sun liya. usee kauwe kaa punarjanm kakbhushundi ke rup main hua. kakbhushundi ko purvajanm main bhagwaan shankar ke mukh se suni vah ram katha puri kii puri yaad thi. unhone yah katha apane shishyon sunaaya. is prakaar ram katha kaa prachaar prasar hua. bhagwaan shri shankar ke mukh se nikali shriram kii yah pavitra katha aadhyatm ramayana ke naam se vikhyath he. aadhyatm ramayana ko hi vishwa kaa pratham ramayana mana jaataa he.
