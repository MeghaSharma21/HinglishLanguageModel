 kalash-ek vishwaas ek bhartiya hindi dharawahik he, jo 23 march 2015 se life oke par prasaarit ho rahaa he. isamen krip suri or aparnaa dikshit mukhya kirdaar nibhaa rahe hai. 
 yah kahani ek mahatvakankshi ladki devika kii he, jise uski daadi padhane ke liye vidhyalay aadi main nahin bhejti or devika ghar main hi rahati he. vah bhagwaan ko hi apana dost maanati he.
