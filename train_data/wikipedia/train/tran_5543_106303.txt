 penelope obin ek angreji upanyaaskaar, kavyitri or anuvadak hai. vah sar richard temple or eni charleton kii beti thi. uski maan eni chikitsak or prakritik darshanik walter charleton kii beti thi. obin kii shaadi ibrahim obin ke saath hui or unke teen bachche the: mairi, ibrahim, or penelope. obin ne apane paariwarik kaarobaar ko kamyaabi se chalaaya jabaki jabaki unke pati vyavsaayik yatra pe rahete the. unhone 1721-28 tak saat upanyaas prakashit kiye. obin ne 1707 main kavita prakashit kii or unhen 1721 main upanyaas main tabdeel kar diya. 1720 main unhone french anuwaad kiye or 1729 main unhone saarvajanik rup se naitik muddon par bhaashan die evam 1730 main ek naatak likhaa. obin kii mrityu april 1738 main hui. lekhika kii mrityu ke baad, uske kaam ko ekatrit karke "e collection of antertaning historis and novels, dizind tu pramot da coj of varchu and honor" rup main prakashit kiya gaya. 18 veen sadi main, obin ke kaam kii pathakon main lokapriyata kam ho gayi jiskaa kaaran unki lekhan style or unke lekhon main avishwasaneey bhukhndon kaa hona tha.
