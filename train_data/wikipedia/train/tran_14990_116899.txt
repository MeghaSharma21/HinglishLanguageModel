 hathi sound parvat yaa gajnasa parvat, guilin, guangshi, china main sthit ek parvat or paryatak aakarshan he.
 hathi sound parvat guilin shahar kaa pratik he. isko yah naam isliye mila he kyonki dekhne main yah aisa lagata he jaise ki koi hathi pani pi rahaa ho. parvat main bani gol surang sadrush sanrachana jo ki hathi kii sound or sharir ke bich sthit he jal-chandra gupha ke naam se jaani jaati he, kyonki raat main chaand kaa pratibimb ko mehrab ke madhyam se dekhne se aisa lagata he jaise ki chaand pani ke neeche he or thik usee samay yah pani kii sataha par tair bhi rahaa he. hathi sound parvat or jal-chandra gupha taohua nadi or lijing nadi ke sangam par sthit hai.
