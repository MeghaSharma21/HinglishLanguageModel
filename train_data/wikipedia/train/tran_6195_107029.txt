 tirthankar suvidhinath, jo pushpadant ke naam se bhi jane jate hai, vartmaan avasarpini kaal ke 9wein tirthankar he. inkaa chinh 'magar' hai.
 bhagwaan pushpadant kii tonk ko suprabh kuta bhi kaha jaataa he. vaadiyon main basii bhagwaan pushpadant kii tonk bhagwaan parswanath kii tonk se lagbhag 1.8 ki.mi. kii duuri par sthit he. yahan se bhagwaan pushpadantnath ne ek hajaar saadhuon ke saath moksh praapt kiya tha. bhagwaan pushpadant ji kaa janm kakandi nagar main krishna paksh kii panchami ko mul nakshatra main hua tha. behad kam aayu main hi inhen gyaan kii prapti ho chuki thi.
