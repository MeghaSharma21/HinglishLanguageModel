 jagadguru ramanandacharya rajasthan sanskrit vishvavidhyalay rajasthan ke jaipur main sthit ek saarvajanik vishvavidhyalay he. pehle iskaa naam' rajasthan sanskrit vishvavidhyalay' tha. 
 sampoorn sanskrit vaanmay ke sangopang adhyayan or adhyaapan kaa sanchaalan karne, satat visheshgyeey anusandhan or usase aanushanbik anya vishyon kii vyavastha karne tathaa sanskrit vaanmay main nihit gyaan-vigyaan kii anusandhan par aadhaarit saral vaigyanik paddati se vyaavhaarik vyakhya prastut karne ke saath hi anya mahatvapurn anusandhanon ke parinaamon or uplabdhiyon ko prakaash main laane ke uddeshy se rajasthan rajya main sanskrit vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana ke liye rajasthan sanskrit vishvavidhyalay adhinium 1998 kii anumati mahamahim rajyapal mahoday dwaara dinaank 2-9-1998 ko digai. dinaank 6 farvari 2001 ko rajasthan sanskrit vishvavidhyalay ne murtt rup liya jiske pratham kulapati padmashri domendan mishra niyukt kiye gayi. upashasan sachiv, shiksha dwaara jari aadesh kr.pa. shiksha - 6/2000 dinaank 27-06-2005 ke anusaar dinaank 27-06-2005 se vishvavidhyalay kaa naam jagadguru ramanandacharya rajasthan sanskrit vishvavidhyalay, jaipur kar diya gaya he.
 yah tha ki chunki anadikaal se bharat desh gyanopasna kaa kendra rahaa he or yah shabdi sadhana rishiyon ke anhad main mukherit hoti hui saakshaat shruti-swaroop main is dharaa par avateern hui. sanskrit ke is vishaal vaanmay kii kaaljayita kaa yahi rahasy he ki sahastrabdiyon se gurukulon or rishikulon aadi main iskaa adhyaapan hota rahaa. is gurushishy-parampara ko suniyojit rup dete hue sanskrit ke anek adhyayan kendra desh bhar main chalate rahe usee parampara main hi 20 veen sadi main anek sanskrit vishvavidhyalay sthapit hue. uttarapradesh, bihar, urisa, kerala, andhrapradesh aadi main sanskrit vishvavidhyalay vigat kai varshon se chal rahe the. isi kram main rajasthan sanskrit vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana hetu varshon se chal rahaa prayaas varsh 2001 main safal hua.
