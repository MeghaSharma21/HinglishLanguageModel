 koshika vigyaan or anuvaanshiki main jin vyavhaar vah prakriya hoti he jisamen kisi jin main upasthit soochna ke prayog se kisi jin utpaada kaa nirmaan hota he. yah utpaada aksar protein hote hai lekin kuch jeenon kaa kaary antaran araane kii utpatti hota he. vishwa ke sabhi gyaat jeevon kii jeevvaigyanik prakriyaaon ke liye jin vyavhaar aavashyak he. yah zaruri nahin he ki kisi jeev kii jeenon main upasthit har sambhav jin vyavhaar sakriya ho. kai jin vyavhaar jeev kii paristhitiyon ke aadhaar par sakeey yaa asakriya hote hai. maanvon main baalo kaa safed ho jane ke peeche saadhaarant jin vyavhaar hota he, jisamen kesh shwet kar dene kaa vyavhaar kisi paristhiti ke kaaran yaa badhati aayu main swayan sakriya ho jaataa he.
