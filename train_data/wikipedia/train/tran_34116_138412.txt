 sainya bhugol maanav bhugol kii ek pramukh shakha he. is shakha main anterrashtriya seemaa-vivaadon, samrik mahatv ke kshetron, yudhon evam sandhiyon ke bhugolic karkon, vibhinn rashtron ke upniveshon, sanyukt raashtra sangh kii gatividhiyon evam anterrashtriya shaanti tathaa sadbhaavna kaa adhyan bhugolic pariprekshya main kiya jaataa he.
