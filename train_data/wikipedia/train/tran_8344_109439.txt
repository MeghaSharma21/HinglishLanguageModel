 bhartiya sansad kaa bahut sa kaam sabha kii samitiyon dwaara niptaya jaataa he, jinhen sansadeey samitiyaan kehete hai. sansadeey samiti se taatpary us samiti se he, jo sabha dwaara niyukt yaa nirvaachit kii jaati he athwa adhyaksh dwaara naam-nirdeshit kii jaati he or adhyaksh ke nideshaanusaar kaary karti he tathaa apana prativedan sabha ko yaa adhyaksh ko prastut karti he. samiti kaa sachivalay lok sabha sachivalay dwaara uplabgh karaayaa jaataa he.
 aadhunik yug main sansad ko na keval vibhinn or jatil prakaar kaa, balki maatra main bhi atyadhik kaary karnaa padataa he. sansad ke paas is kaary ko nipataane ke liye simit samay hota he. isliye sansad un sabhi vidhaayi tathaa anya maamalon par, jo uske samaksh aate hai, gaharaai ke saath vichaar nahin kar sakati.
 apni prakruti ke anusaar sansadeey samitiyaan do prakaar kii hoti hai: sthaayee samitiyaan or tadarth samitiyaan. sthaayee samitiyaan sthaayee evam niyamit samitiyaan hai jinka gathan samay-samay par sansad ke adhinium ke upbandhon athwa lok sabha ke prakriya tathaa kaary-sanchaalan niyam ke anusaran main kiya jaataa he. in samitiyon kaa kaary anwarat prakruti kaa hota he. vittiya samitiyaan, vibhaagon se sambhddha sthaayee samitiyaan tathaa kuch anya samitiyaan sthaayee samitiyon kii shrenee ke antargat aati hai. tadarth samitiyaan kisi vishisht prayojan ke liye niyukt kii jaati hai or jab ve apana kaam samaapt kar leti hai tathaa apana prativedan prastut kar detii hai, tab unka astitv samaapt ho jaataa he. pramukh tadarth samitiyaan vidheyakon sambandhi pravar tathaa sanyukt samitiyaan hai. rail abhismay samiti, sansad bhawan parisar main khaadya prabandhan sambandhi sanyukt samiti ityadi bhi tadarth samitiyon kii shrenee main aati hai.
