 piskarevscoe shahid smarak rus ke saint petersburg main sthit leningrad kii gheraabandi ke shikaar logon ko samarpit kabristaan he.
 alexander wesilive or yevgeni levinson dwaara dizin kiye gayi is smarak ko 9 may, 1960 main kholaa gaya tha. dwitiya vishwayuddh ke samay bhukh va bambaari main maare gayi nagar ke 420000 nagarikon tathaa leningrad morche ke shahid 50000 sainikon ko yahan 186 samuhik kabron main dafnaaya gaya tha. iske pravesh dwaar ke paas ek akhand jyoti sthit he.yahan ek sangemarmar kii takhti lagaayee hui he jis par ukera hua he ki 4 sitambar, 1941 se 22 janvari, 1944 tak 107158 bam akaash se shahar par giraae gaye, 148478 gole daage gaye, 16744 purushon kii mrityu hui 33782 ghayal hue the tathaa 641803 kii bhukh se mrityu hui thi.
 smarak ke madhya bhaag main mathrubhumi kii kaansya pratimaa sthit he. akhand jyoti se hokar grenite se bana 480 meter kaa maarg he jo is bhavy pratimaa tak jaataa he.
 1 june 2017 ko bharat ke pradhanmantri narendra modi ne yahan aakar shaheedon ko pushpanjali arpit kii thi.
