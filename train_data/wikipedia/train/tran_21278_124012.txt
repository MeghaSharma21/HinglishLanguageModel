 pales of westominster, jiskaa arth he westminster kaa mahal or jise house of parliament yaa westominster pales ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he, britain kii sansad ke do sadanon kaa sabha sthal he. inamen se ek he ''house of lords'' or doosraa he ''house of comons''. yah landan shahar ke hriday maane jane vaale westominster shahar main thames nadi ke uttari kinare par sthit he. yah sarkari bhawan whiteul or down street tathaa aitihaasik sthal westominster abi ke kareeb he. yah naam nimna do main se kisi ek sanrachana ko sanderbhit kar saktaa he, da old pales, jo ek madhyakaalin imaarat he jo ki 1834 main hi nasht ho gai thi or uske sthaan par banane vaala new pales jo aaj bhi maujuud he. lekin isaki mul style or shahi thaathbaat purvavat bani hui he.
 is jagah par pehla shahi mahal gyarahavin shatabdi main banaaya gaya tha or 1512 main is imaarat ke nasht hone se pehle westominster hi landan ke raja kaa praathmik landan nivas tha. iske baad se hi yah sansad bhawan ke rup main kaary kar rahaa he. 13 veen shatabdi se yahaan sansad kii sabhaayen hoti hai or shahi nyaay peeth evam westominster haul bhi yaheen par he. punah puri bhavyata se banaaye gayi is sansad bhawan main 1834 main bhayanak aag lag gai. is aag se jo imaarte batch gayi unamem shaamil hai westominster haul, da clauistors of saint stephens, chapel of saint mairi andercraft or jaal tower.
 mahal ke purnirmaan kii pratiyogita main shilpkaar charles bairi kii jeet hui or is imaarat ke nirmaan main unki abhilamb gothic style ko apanaaya gaya. puraane mahal ke avasheshon ko inke sthaan par bade evam bhavy rup main banaaya gaya, jisamen 1100 kaksh shaamil hai. ye kaksh angan kii do shrinkhalaaon ke ird gird banaaye gayi hai. is naye mahal kaa kuch bhaag3.24 hectare thames par banaaya gaya he, jiskaa pramukh hisse kaa munh 265.8-meter nadi kii taraf he. bairi kii sahaayta agastas dablyoo. en. pugin ne kii thi jo gothic shilpakala ke ek mukhya adhikari the. unhone hi mahal kii saaj sajja ke liye dizin taiyaar kiye the. 1814 main nirmaan kaary shuru hua tha or tees saal tak chalaa. iske nirmaan main kai baadhaaen ain, donon mukhya shilpakaaron kii mrityu ho gai, to kabhi isamen bahut adhik vilamb or dhan laga. beesvin shatabdi tak bhi andar kii saaj sajja kaa kaam ruuk ruuk chalata rahaa. landan ke vaayu pradushan ke kaaran iske sangrakshan kaa kaary tab se chal hi rahaa he. duusare vishwa yuddh ke dauraan jab 1941 main iske kaaman chembar main bambaari hui thi tab se yahaan par purnirmaan kaa kaam chal rahaa he.
 yah mahal landan ke raajnneetik jivan kaa centre rahaa he. westominster landan kii sansad ke liye methome ban chukaa he. iske naam par hi sarkaar ke westominster tantra kaa naam padaa he. vishesh rup se clock tower, jo apni mukhya ghante ke kaaran big ben ke rup main janaa jane laga he, landan kaa pratishthit aitihaasik sthal or shahar kaa mukhya paryaton centre he. ise sansadeey loktantr kaa pratik bhi kaha jaataa he. 1970 se hi pales of westominster ucchkoti kii imaarat maani jaati he or 1987 se yah unesco ke vishwa dharohar sthalon kaa hissaa he.
 pales of westominster madhya kaal ke dauraan apni sthiti ke kaaran kootnithik drushti se bhi mahatvapurn tha kyonki yah thames nadi ke kinare bana hua tha. madhyakaal main throni island ke naam se mashhoor is jagah ko 1016 se 1035 main canut da great ne apana shahi mahal banaaya hua tha. saint edward kanfesar ne england ke raja sackson ke ant se pehle throni dweep par ek shahi mahal banwaaya. yah landan shahar se pashchim kii taraf tha. ise thik usee samay banaaya gaya tha jab usne westominster abi banwaaya tha. throni dweep or uske aasapaas kaa kshetra jaldi hi westominster kahalaane laga . in imaaraton kaa upyog naa to sackson ne kiya or naa hi wiliam pratham ne. sabase lambii avadhi tak tikaa rahanaa vaala mahal kaa hissaa wiliam 1 se lekar raja wiliam 2 ke samay tak tha.
 madhya kaal ke ant tak pales of westominster shaasha kaa mukhya grih tha. sansad kaa pehle kaa jo praaroop tha usamen isi jagah westominster haul main curia regis ekatrit hua karti thi. england kii pehali adhikarik sansad da model parliament kii baithak is mahal main 1295 main hui. lagbhag iske baad kii sabhi sansad vahaan par sabha kar chuki he.
 1530 main raja henry VIII ne cardinal thomas volse se york place hathiyaa liya tha. vah ek shaktishaalee mantri tha jisane apane raja kaa samarthan kho diya tha. henry ne ise pales of whiteul kaa naam dekar apana mukhya grih bana liya. yadhyapi westominster aadhikarik taur par shahi mahal hi rahaa. ise sansad ke donon sadanon evam shahi nyaayalayon dwaara istemaal kiya jaataa tha.
 kyonki vastav main yah ek shahi grih tha, isliye yahaan par donon sadanon ke kisi anya kaary ke liye koi kaksh nahin banaaya gaya tha. rajya ke mukhya mahotsav range hue kakshon main manaaye jate the. house of lords kii baithak vastav main rani ke kaksh main hoti thi. yah shaleen haul imaarat ke dakshin sire par sthit tha jise madhyakaal main banaaya gaya tha. 1801 main ucch sadan bade safed kaksh main sthanantarit ho gaya. yah pehle court of request main hua karata tha. jise 18 veen shatabdi main raja jorge tritiy ne banwaaya tha. isi samay ireland ke saath sandhi bhi hui thi. yah kadam isliye uthaaya gaya kyon ki purv kaksh main bade hue mitra samooh ke liye jagah nahin thi.
 house of comons ke paas apana koi kaksh nahin tha, isaki sabhaayen kabhi kabhi westominster abi ke chapter house main hua karti thim. kamans ne pales main stephen chapel ke rup main apane liye ek sthai jagah bana lee. stephen chapel edward VI ke shashan ke samay shahi pales kaa purv chapel tha. 1547 main yah imaarat kamans ke istemaal ke liye uplabdh ho gayi. iske liye saint stifan college ko todna padaa. agali teen shatabdiyon main stifan ke chapel main kai badlaav kiye gayi, taaki nichle sadan kii suvidha barkaraar rahe. phir ek dam se madhyakaal ke uske rup ko bilkul hi khatm kar diya gaya.
 kyonki sansad ko apane simit jagah me kaary karne main pareshaani ho rahi thi isliye pales of westominster main 18 veen shatabdi ke baad se kai prakaar ke parivartan kiye gayi. ek puri tarah se nae mahal banane kii baat ko ansuna kar diya gaya, iske sthaan par isamen nai imaaraten jodi gayi. 1755 or 1770 ke madhya ek nayaa pashchimi mukhya dwaar banaaya gaya. yah darwaja saint margaret street kii ore khulata tha. yah padilion style main banaaya gaya tha. ab saamudayik kaksh evam sangrakshan ke liye kaaphi jagah ho gayi thi. 1795 main house of comons ke spikar ke liye ek nayaa aadhikarik nivas sthaan banaaya gaya. yah saint stephen chapel ke saath sata hua tha. 1799 or 1801 ke bich naye gothic shilpkaar james vyat ne dono sadanon, house of lard or house of kamans main apana kaam shuru kar diya.
 1824 or 1827 ke bich mahal kii puri imaarat ko ek baar phir se banaaya gaya. or is baar yah kaam sar jon son ne kiya. madhya kaaleen house of laders kaa chembar jo ki 1650 main gan powder plot ke fail hone par nishaanaa bana tha. use navinikaran ke is kaam ko anjaam dene ke liye tabah kar diya gaya. taaki is jagah ke dakshin sire par ek nayaa shahi galiyara or bhavy pravesh dwaar banaaya ja sake. son ne jo kaary mahal main kiye unamem sansad ke dono sadanon ke liye puskalay kii nayi suvidha evam raja kii kanoon peeth or ucch nyaayalya ke liye naye kaksh kaa prabandh karnaa shaamil tha. son dwaara kii nayi kalatmak shilpkaaree ko lekar kai virodhabhas bhi uthne shuru ho gayi. jo log mul imaarat ke gothic kala kaa samarthan karte the wo iske virdodhi ban gayi.
 16 oktober 1834 ko pales main aag lag gai jab lathi bhandar main rakhe aag ke stove ke atyadhik tap jane ke kaaran house of laders ke kaksh ne aag pakad lee. iske parinaam swaroop sansad ke dono sadan nasht ho gayi or aas paas kii anya imaaraten bhi nahin bachiin. aag bachaav dal kii sartakta ke kaaran or hawa kii disha ke anukool hone kii vajah se westominster haul batch gaya. da jaal tower, da under craft chapel, da chloistors or chaptor house of saint stifan, ye mahal ke ve anya hisse hai jo ki is aapadaa se batch gayi the.
 aag lagane ke turant baad hi raja wiliam IV ne apana buckingham pales sansad ko dene kii peshkash kii, jo banakar lagbhag taiyaar ho chukaa tha. iske peeche kaaran yah bhi tha ki vah use apane nivas sthaan ke rup main pasand nahin karata tha. is imaarat ko sansad ke kaary ke liye upyukt nahin paaya gaya. isliye is peshkash ko asweekaar kar diya gaya. charing cars or james park par bhi vichaar nahin ban paaya. vesminster se aitihaasik or raajnneetik judaav hi sthanantaran ke liye taqatvar sabit hua. jabaki vahaan kai kamiyaan thim. ab praathamikta is baat ko di ja rahi thi ki jald se jald sansad ko uskaa sthaan mil jaye. isliye house of lard tathaa house of kamans ke liye jaldi main chitrakala vaale kaksh or safed kaksh ko taiyaar kiya gaya. iske liye disha nirdesan board of workas ke bache hue shilpkaar sar rorbat smirk ne kiya. farvari 1835 sadan chalaane ke liye kaksh taiyaar karne kaa kaam jaldi jaldi kiya gaya.
 mahal kii nai sanrachana ke adhyayan ke liye ek shahi sanghathan niyukt kiya gaya iske prastavit style par bhi logon ke bich logon ke bich bahes hone lagi. ab yah nai uttam padyati jo ki sanyukt rajya ke fedral capital or white house jaisi thi, us samay kaaphi prachlit hui. yah style pehle son dwaara puraane mahal ke kuch hisse main bhi istemaal kii ja chuki thi. ise naye praaroop main kranti or ganatantr ke sanket the. jabaki gothic sanrachana main sangrakshan ke mulya ke mulya chhipe the. is ayog ne 1835 main hi yah ghoshnaa kar di thi ki is imaarat kaa praaroop yaa to gothic yaa phir elizabethan hoga. is shahi ayog ne sabhi vaastukaron ko is drushti se yojana banane ko kaha.
 1836 main 97 paraspar virdohi yojanayen padhane ke baad shahi ayog ne gothic style kaa mahal banane ke liye bairi kii yojana ko chunaa. isaki neenv to 1840 main hi daal di gai thi. lard kaksh 1847 tathaa kamans kaksh 1852 main banakar taiyaar hua . yadhyapi is kaary ke liye 1860 karmchaaree lage the phir bhi yah kaary ek dashak tak puraa nahin ho paaya. bairi jisaki apni shilpakala gothic kam or panrparik zyada he. isane yah nayaa pales banaaya santulan ke naye niyamon ke aadhaar par. vah andar kii saajasja or bhavy nirmaan ke liye augustus pugin par nirbhar rahaa. jisamen kii deevaaron kii saaj, nakkashi kaa kaam, til lagane kaa kaam, furniture aadi shaamil the.
 duusare vishwa yuddh ke dauraan jab germany ne landan par bambaari kii thi tab alag alag avasar par chaudaa baar pales of westominster bhi is bambaari kaa shikaar hua tha. 26 sitambar 1940 main puraane pales main ek bam gira tha. is bambaari main saint stifan porch kii dakshini diwaar nasht ho gayi thi or saath hi iskaa pashchimi mukhadwar bhi nasht ho gaya tha. da loin richard kii pratimaa bhi is joardaar visphot se apane sthaan se kuch upar uth gaya thi or uski upar kii ore uthi hui talwaar jhuk gai. is pratimaa ko loktantr ke pratik ke rup main janaa jaataa he. kehete hai ki "akraman kii sthiti main ye thodaa jhuk bhi jaye par tuutegi nahin". ek doosraa bam jo 8 disambar ko gira tha, usne kai mathon ko tabah kar diya.
 10 /11 may 1941 ko sabase zyada khatarnaak hamalaa hua tha, tab pales ko kam se kam 12 jatke lage the or teen logon kii mrityu ho gai thi. ek aag lagane vaala bam house of kamans ke kaksh par aa gira or vahaan par aag laga di. duusare bam ne westominster haul kii chat ko dahala diya. aag bachaav dal donon ko hi na bachaa paaya or yah nirnay liya gaya ki kam se kam andal haul ko surakshit rakhane kaa kaam kiya jaye. is kaary main ye log safal hue the. lekin comons kaksh puri tarah se nasht ho chukaa tha or sadsy lobby bhi nahin bachi thi. ek bam lord ke kaksh main bhi gira tha, lekin zamin par bina fate aage nikal gaya. yaa to koi chota bam upar clock tower se takaraayaa tha yaa phir koi vimaan bhedi chat ke chajje par gira tha. jis kaaran se chat ko kaaphi kshati pahunchi thi. ghadi kaa sheesha puri tarah se nasht ho chukaa tha. lekin iske haath or ghanti ab bhi kaam kar rahi thim or ab bhi yah badi ghadi bilkul sahi vakt bataa rahi thi.
 kamans chembar ke nasht hone ke baad, lard ne swayan kaa vaad vivaad kaksh kamans ke istemaal ke liye de diya . or lard ke swayan ke istemaal ke liye quins robing room ko kuch samay apane prayog main laane ke liye lard ne rakh liya. yuddh ke baad kamans kaksh ko shilpkaar sar gills gilbert scot ke nirdesan par phir se banaaya gaya. is baar yah bahut hi saadhaaran puraane kaksh kii style main banaaya gaya. 1950 main yah kaam khatm hua. ab donon sadanon ko apana apana kaksh mil gaya.
 ab karyaalay ke liye jagah kii aavashyaktaa badhane lagi or parliament ne paas ke norman shaw imaarat main san 1975 main kuch jagah kaa adhigrahan kar liya. or haal hi main san 2000 main custom bilt portculis house banakar taiyaar hua he. is badhat se sabhi saansadon ke liye apana khud kaa karyalya banane kii jagah ho gayi he.
 sar chalirs bairi ne pales of westominster ke liye jo sahyogi dizin taiyaar kiya tha. isamen 15 veen shatabdi main mashhoor abhilamb gothic style kaa prayog tha or isi se 19 ve shatabdi main gothic style kii vaapasi hui thi. bairi ek panrparik shilpkaar the. lekin inaki gothic shilpkaar augustus pugin ne sahaayta kii thi. gyarahavi shatabdi main bana westominster haul jo 1834 kii aagjanee ko jhel chukaa he. is haul kaa dizin bairi ne hi taiyaar kiya tha. pugin is kaam ke nattejey se khush nahin the. mukhya rup se bairi dwaara taiyaar kiye gayi dizin kii yojana se ve asantusht the. unhonen iske liye ye tippanee bhi kii thi ki sabhi gracian, sar, yah paaramparik imaarat par tuder style kaa vivaran he".
 is imaarat pe jo pathar kaa kaam kiya gaya tha wo vastav main anston tha. yah ek baaluu ke rang kaa magangian limestone he. jisaki khan dakshini yorkshaire ke anston gaanv main he. yah pathar halaki pradooshan or duusare istemaal ho rahe kam gunvattta vaale pattharon ke kaaran jaldi hi kharaab hone laga. yadhyapi yah sab kharabiyaan jald se jald 1849 tak thik kar lee gayi thim or iske baad 19 veen shatabdi main kuch nahin kiya gaya tha. 1900 ke dauraan ek chiz to bilkul saaf ho gayi thi ki pattharon kii nakkashi kaa kuch kaam ab badalanaa padegaa.1928 is baat ki aavashyaktaa mahasoos kii gai ki ab rutland ke shahad se rang vaale limestone pathar clipsam kaa prayog kar kharaab pade annston ko badla jaye. yah pariyojna 1930 main shuru hui thi, lekin duusare vishwa yuddh ke kaaran iskaa kaam ruuk gaya or 1950 main hi yah kaam samaapt ho paaya. 1960 tak pradooshan ab phir se prabhavi hone laga. 1981 main towers or bahari unnayan ke pattharon ke sangrakshan kii ek yojana shuru hui. is yojana kii samaapti 1994 main hui. tab se house ke adhikariyon ne kai bhitari baramadon kii bahari marammat ke liye yojanayen shuru kii hai. yah kaary san 2011 main bhi jari he.
 westominster pales me teen mukhya stambh hai.inamen se sabase bada or lanbaa 98.5-meter victoria tower he. jisamen ki pales kaa dakshini pashchimi konaa aata he. us samay ke shaasha wiliam 4 ke shashan ke sammaan main is tower ko bairi ke hi vaastavik dizin main rakhaa gaya tha. isko bairi ne sabase zyada yaadgaar banane kii koshish kii thi. yah shilpakala jo vidhaayi kile kii dekharekh ke liye he, ise great sakwai tower kaha jaataa he or yah pales kii shahi pravesh dwaar ke rup main istemaal kiya jaataa he or parliament ke pustak sangrah ke liye bhi yah ek agni niropak kaa kaam karata he. is victoria tower ko kai baar punah banaaya gaya or isaki oonchai bahut teji se badi. 1858 main puraa banane tak yah sansar kii sabase badi dharmanirpeksh imaarat ban gaya tha.
 tower ke tal par shaasha pravesh dwaar he. is pales main pravesh karne ke liye or parliament ko kholane ke liye raja dwaara istemaal kiya jaataa he. yah 15.2-meter unchaa mehrabadar path kalakritiyon se sajaayaa gaya he, jinamein saint jorj, andrew or patterik ke saath-saath rani victoria ke putale bhi shaamil hai. is victoria tower ke mukhya hisse main sansadeey lekhaagaar ke tees lakh dastavej hai. 8.8 kilometre inamen sansad ke 1497 se ab tak ke sansad adhinium kii sabhi mukhya pandulipiyaan maujuud hai or anya mukhya pandulipiyaan jaise ki bill of ritis kii mul pratiyaan evam king chalirs ke death warrant se sambandhit pratiyaan bhi yahaan maujuud hai. sabase upar louhe se bani pyramid numa chat 22.3-meter dhvaj fahraane kii jagah he. yahaan par raja kii upasthiti main royal standard fahraaya jaataa he. jhandaa diwas ke avasar par tathaa jis din sansad ke kisi bhi ek sadan kii sabha hoti thi, us din sabase upar jaakar sangheey jhande ko fahraaya jaataa he.
 is pales ke dakshini sire par ek bahut hi charchit tower hi jise clock tower ke naam se janaa jaataa he, ise big ben bhi kehete hai. yah 96.3 meter ke victoria tower se thodaa hi chota he lekin bahut hi patala he. isamen westominster kii badi vaali ghadi he. jise ki edward jon dent ne banaaya tha or iskaa dizin shaukia horologist edmund backet denisan ne taiyaar kiya tha. ek kshan main hi isaki ghantii puri tarah baj uthati he, is badi ghadi kii khaasiyat yah he ki iskaa samay bilkul sateek hota he, jise 19 veen shatabdi ke ghadi banane vaale kathin samajhate the. or 1859 se jab se yah astitv main ai he is par bharosa kaayam he. iskaa samay vyas ke maapan ke anusaar chaar dial 7 meter main dikhaaya jaataa he, jo ki dudhia rang ke shishe ke bane hote hai or raat ke samay yah peeche se chamakta he. iskaa ghante vaala haath 2.7 meter lanbaa he or minute vaala haath 4.3 meter chota he.
 paanch ghantiyaan ghadi main ghanta ghar ke upar kii ore lataki hai. chaar chaturthaansh ghantiyaan westominster ke ghantanad ko har ghante ke har chauthe bhaag main bajaati rahati hai. sabase badi ghanti jab ghanta bajaati he to use aadhikarik bhashaa main great bel of westminster kehate hai. saamanyata ise bigaben bhi kehete hai. jo ki anishchit udgamon ko diya gaya naam he, yah naam samay ke saath saath tower ke liye istemaal hone laga. wo pehali ghanti jise yah naam diya gaya tha, vah parikshan ke dauraan hi tut gai or use phir naye rup main dhaala gaya. jo bel aaj hamaare saamne he usamen to swayan hi ek daraar aa gai. jiske kaaran isaki dhwani bhi kuch alag ho gayi he. agar iskaa bhaar dekha jaye to yah britain kii teesri sabase badi ghadi he 13.8 ton . clock tower ke sabase upar jo laalaten rakhi he vah yraton lite he, yah keval tabhi jalati he jab andhera ho jane ke baad bhi sansad ke kisi na kisi sadan kii sabha jari rahati he. yah rani victoria ke anurodh par 1885 main lagaai gai thi taaki ve buckingham pales se bhi yah dekh saken ki sabhi sadsy kaam par hai yaa nahin. iskaa namakaran pehle kamisionar of work ekatan smi yraton ke naam par kiya gaya tha.

 yah seedhiyaan norman baraamde ke baad padati hai, yah vargakar zamin kaa tukdaa he, yah apni guchchhedaar akruti or gahan bhitari chat ke kaaran shesh khand se alag lagata he. yah jagah chargupha jaise kaksh hai or iskaa nakkashidaar agrabhag ke saath isaki mehrab kii daat hi isaki sabase badi visheshta he. iske baraamde kaa namakaran isaki sajaavat ke tareeke par kiya gaya he. yah tarika norman itihaas kaa hi hissaa he. is ghatana main na to norman ke raja kaa bimb or na hi kisi bhitti chitra ko kriyavint kiya gaya he or is theme main keval ek shishe ko dikhaaya gaya he jisamen vijeta wiliam kii chhavi hi pratibimbit hoti he. is kaksh main rani victoria ko do baar dikhaaya gaya he, ek jagah kisi shishe main rani ko ek jawan stri ke rup main dikhaaya gaya he or duusari jagah rani ko apni mrityu ke nikat house of da lord ke singhasana par baithe dikhaaya gaya he. yah sab jin joseph benjamin constant kii 1900 painting vaali copy kaa ek hissaa he, jo ki purv kii diwaar par tangi he. is kaksh main aise solah stambh hai jahaan par house of lords ke purv pradhanmantri kii murti bani hui he, yah murtiyon hai arl grey or selis bari kii maarakyon. sidhiyon ke duusari taraf do darvaaze hai jinamein se ek sidhaa shahi gailaree kii taraf le jaataa he or doosraa robing room par jaakar khulata he.
 rani kaa robing kaksh pales ke samaaroh vaale haul ke dakshini sire par he or yah is imaarat ke dakshini mukhya dwaar ke centre ke bade hisse ko gherta he. iske thik saamne victoria garden he. jaisaa ki iske naam se hi pataa chalata he, yah us sthaan par padataa he jahaan par shaasha aadhikarik poshaak pahane or shahi mukut ko sar par dhaaran kiye hue parliament kii state opening kii ghoshnaa karte hai. is sundarataa se sajaaye gayi kaksh ke archeshan kaa mukhya centre bindu raja kaa singhasana hota he. raja yahaan par teen seedhiyaan ke upar sthit apane singhasana par scot land, england, ireland ke sainikon kii suraksha main in rashtron ke phulon ke pratik se saje mandap par baithta he. is singhasana ke peeche bangnee rang kaa makhmali chakhauta hota he, is chakhute par royal skool of ambrodari dwaara sundar kadhai kaa kaam kiya gaya he. is jagah sitaron or ve aar monogram kii kadhai kii gai he. edward bairi ne kursi, takiya or uske peeche ke hisse par bhi kadhai kii hui he. or kaksh ke chaaron taraf alankrita pathar lagaakar aag kii jagah banaai gai he. is jagah par saint jorj or saint maaikel ke chamakati hui murti banaai gai he.
 raja arthor kii kahani hi is kaksh kii sajaavat kii theme banaai gai he. kai victorian ise apni rashtriyata kaa strot maanate hai. wiliam diase dwaara 1864 or 1848 ke beet chitrit kii gai paanch murtiyon is kaksh kii deevaaron ko sajaati hai. yah murtiyon raja kii kahani kaa roopaatmak drushya chitrit karti he. dono daravaajon ke bich laga bada chitra par bana har drushya raja kii bahaduree ko dikhaata he or ise admician of sar trisdram tu da round teble kaa naam diya gaya he or saath hi yahaan ke aatithy sanskaar ke drushyon ko bhi dikhaaya gaya he. yahaan par lage saat drushya manyata praapt hai jab ki baaki ke do drushya chitrakaar kii mrityu ho jane ke kaaran adhure hi rah gayi the. iske alaava franz jevar winter hollater dwaara banaaye deevaaron par bane bhitti chitra rani victoria or prince elbert ko singhasana par darshaate hai. is kaksh main kii gai baaki saaj sajja bhi raja se hi prerit he. pating ke neeche atharah shrankhlaon vaali jo base relif he ise henry hug amarsted dwaara ok par ukera gaya he or chat ke neeche jo chitra vallari lagi hui he, ye round teble kii senaaon ke ghodon ko darshaati he. bhitari chat ko bhi rajkeey ghoshnaaon ke billon se sajaayaa gaya he or bilkul isi tarah se isaki lakdi kaa angan bhi he, jise thik isi prakaar se kaaleen dwaara sajaayaa gaya he.
 robing kaksh ke thik dakshin main shahi galiyara he. is33.5 x 13.7 meter pales ke bade kakshon main se ek he. iskaa mukhya uddeshay parliament ke shahi kii state opening ke shahi juluus ke taur par manch uplabdh karwaanaa he. is julus ko janata sadak ke donon kinaaron par lagi kursiyon par baith kar dekhati he. isko tab bhi istemaal kiya jaataa he jab rajya ke baahar se raajnneetijna parliament ko sambodhit karne ke liye aate hai. or saath hi baahar se aane vaale shahi mehmaanon kii aguai ke liye bhi ise prayog main laayaa jaataa he. or oard chancelar ke naashte ke liye to ise kai baar istemaal kiya jaataa he. purv main yah house of lords ke kai kuleen jan dwaara kai krayakramon ke liye ek manch kii tarah prayog main laayaa jaataa tha. shahi gailaree main sansad ke lekhaagaar ko dastavejon ko bhi prakashit kiya gaya he or yahaan par lagi kursiyaan or baithne kii alag vyavastha vaad vivaad kaksh ke paas lods ke sadasyon ke liye suvidha janak he.
 shahi galiyare kii saaj sajja kii yojana british military ke itihaas main ahm kshanon ko darshaati he or isaki deevaaren danniel maclis dwaara banaai gayi do badi paintig se saji hai jinka aakaar 13.7 x 3.7 meter he. yah dono deth of nelson or da meeting of wellington and bruchar . anya kai karanon se par mukhya rup se vaataavaran pradooshit hone ke kaaran yah sabhi bhitti chitra bahut teji se puri tarah se bighad gayi or aaj isi pradooshan kii vajah se yah sab rang main ek hi jaise lagate hai. iske baad baaki ke bhitti chitron ko hataa diya gaya. or yahaan ki deevaaren jorj 1 ke baad se raja or raniyon ki tasveeron se bhari hui hai. yahaan ki deevaaron par sena ke rangon ke alaava aath chamakate hue keyan ke pathar hai. jone barney philip nakkashi kiye gayi yah pathar gailaree ke raste main hi daravaajon evam khidakiyon par lagen hue hai. yahaan har jagah ek raja ko darshaya gaya he jiske shasankaal ke dauraan ek mahatvapurn ladaai yaa yuddh ladaa gaya tha. 13.7 meter baraamde ke upar kii taraf jo pattee daar bhitari chat he yah tuder rose or line ko darshaati he. or upar lageen shishe kii khidkiyaan par england or scotland ke sainiko kaa sudanr chithran he.
 rajkumaar kaa cameraa shahi gailaree or lard kaksh ke bich main ek chota soupakaksh he. iskaa namakaran venceminster ke puraane pales main parliament se sate kaksh ke naam par kiya gaya he. apni sthiti ke kaaran yah wo jagah he jahaan par lard ke sadsy house ke vyapaar par baatcheet karne ke liye aate hai. yahaan par kuch aise daravaaje bhi hai jo kaksh se baahar house of lard kii divijan lobby tak le jate hai or iske saath saath kai anya mahattavapurn karyaalyon kaa bhi rasta yaheen se padataa he.

 sabha ke sadsy kaksh ke teen ore laal bench par baithte hai. lords adhyaksh ke dayee taraf aadhyatmik paksh or baayein taraf asthaayee paksh vaale baithte hai. lords ke aadhyatmik sadsy sabhi aadhyatmik chor kii ore baithte hai. lords ke asthaayee sadsy party sambhddhata ke anusaar baithte hai,: sarkaar kii party ke sadsy aadhyatmik paksh kii ore baithte hai, jabaki vipaksh ke sadsy asthaayee sadasyon kii ore baithte hai. kuch saathi, jo koi party se sambhddhata nahin rakhte he ve oon kii theliyon vipareet sadan ke bich main bench par baithte hai, jinhen cross benture ke rup main janaa jaataa hai.
 lards kaa chambar rashtriya str par prasaarit hone vaale aayojanon kaa sthal hai, jisamen sabase mahatvapurn sansad kaa rajkeey udghaatan hai, jo ki prayek vaarshik sansadeey satr ke armbh main yojit kiya jaataa hai, yah yaa to prayek aam chunaav ke baad yaa sharad ritu ke yojit kiya jaataa he. is avasar par sarkaar ke prayek sanvaidhanik ang kaa pratinidhitiv kiya jaataa hai: mukut, aadhyatmik or asthaayee lards or comons, nyaaypalika or kaaryakari or oonchai main 10 meter, bhi samaan hai. jisaki mukhya visheshta farsh kaa kendriya tukdaa hai, jisamen ek ujjwal tuder darbishaire pathar se bana gulab hai jo ki utkeern pital kii ek ashtakona ke bheetar nirdhaarit kiya jadaa hua he. baaki bachi hui manzil matchinia heraladi dizin or latin adarsh vaakya ke tiles ke se susajjit he. deevaaron ko safed pattharon se sajaayaa gaya he or prayek main dwaar ke liye chhidr kiya gaya hai. unke upar mehrab par hathiyaar pradarshit kiye gayi hai jo vibhinn chah shahi rajwanshon ne rani victoria ke shasankaal tak shashan kiya tha kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai or bich main, khidkiyaan hai jisamen england ke praarambhik kuleen pariwaron ko pradarshit kiya gaya hai.

 westominster pales kaa bahari hissaa, khaskar clock tower, duniya bhar main janaa jaataa he or landan main sabase zyada dekha janevaala paryatakon ke aakarshan kaa centre he. sanyukt raashtra shaikshik, vaigyanik or saanskrithik sanghathan ne pales of westominster ke saath saath, westominster ebbe or saint margaret ko vishwa viraasat sthal ke rup main manyata di he. yah grad 1 main soochibaddh imaarat bhi he.
 yadhyapi mahal ke andar jane ke liye koi anaupchaarik pahunch nahin he, lekin vahaan pravesh karne ke kai tareeke hai. britain ke nivasee house of comons ke gailaree dekhne ke liye sthaneey saansad se tikat praapt kar sakate hai yaa house of lord kii gailaree dekhne ke liye lord se tikat le sakate hai. yah bhi sambhav he ki britain ke nivasee or videshi darshakon donon pravesh ke liye qatari laga sakate hai, lekin kshamta simit he or vahaan pravesh kii koi guarantee nahin he. koi bhi sadan "ajanabiyon" ko baahar kar saktaa hai agar ve akele main baithne kii ichha karata he. aam janata bhi qatari bana sakati he agar ve kamaty satr main baithaana chaahate hai, jahaan parvesh nickshulk he or jagah arakshit nahin kii ja sakati he yaa ve anusandhan prayojanon ke liye sansadeey abhilekhaagaar dekh sakate hai. pahchaan kaa saboot uttararddh maamle main aavashyak he lekin iske liye kisi saansad ko pehle se milaane kii zarurat nahin he.
 pales ke ni: shulk nirdeshit paryaton britain ke nivaasiyon ke liye sansadeey satr ke dauraan yojit kiye jate hai, jo apane saansad yaa house of lard ke sadasyon ke madhyam se aavedan kar sakate he. yah yatra 75 minute kii hoti he or isamen rajkeey kaksh, donon sadanon ke chembar or westominster haul ke kaksh shaamil hai. pradutt paryaton garmi ke avkaash ke dauraan britain or videshi paryatakon donon ke liye uplabdh hai. britain ke nivasee clock tower dekhne ke liye bhi apane sansad ke sthaneey sadsy ke madhyam se aavedan kar sakate hai; videshi paryatakon or chote bachche kii anumati nahin he.
 vaastu itihaaskaar dan crockshenk ne apane 2006 ke bibisi ke tivi vruttchitr shrinkhalaa, britain kii sarvshereshth building ke liye apane paanch vikalpon main se ek mahal ke bhi chunaa.jila, cirkil or jubilee lines main nikatatam landan bhumigat stesan westominster he.
