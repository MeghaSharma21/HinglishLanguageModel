 hiroshima japan kaa ek nagar he jahaan dwitiya vishwayuddh ke dauraan 1945 main sanyukt rajya america dwaara parmaanu bam giraaya gaya tha jisase puraa kaa puraa nagar barabaad ho gaya tha. is vibhishaka ke parinaam aaj bhi is nagar ke log bhugat rahe hai. japan ke ek duusare nagar nagasaki par bhi parmaanu bam se hamalaa kiya gaya tha. isi kaa parinaam he ki japan ne parmaanu hathiyaar kabhi nirmaan na karne kii niti sthapit kii he.
 aaj hiroshima japan kaa ek bada nagar he. hiroshima main 11,96,274 log rahete hai. baraak obama hiroshima gaya 2017 main or kaagaz ke saras banaaye hiroshima pis memorial park main. hiroshima main bahut log kaagaz ke saras banaate hai, shanti ke liye. yah he kyonki jab hiroshima pe bam gira ek ladki, jiskaa naam sadako sasaki tha, ko leukemia hui. usne hazaar saras banane kii koshish kii kyonki japan main kehete hai ki agar aap hazaar saras banaaye, aapaki ek khwaahish sach hogi. sadako ne hazaar saras banaaye magar behatar nahin hui or mari. sadako ke liye aaj bhi log saras banaate hai hiroshima main.
