 
 actinobacteria gram-dhanatmak bacteria kaa ek jeevvaigyanik sangh hai. yah jal or thal donon par ugte hai, or inkaa maanvon ke liye bahut aarthik mahatv he kyonki krishi or van donon mrudaa main actinobacteria dwaara die gaye yogadanon par nirbhar hai.
 dharti main actinobacteria kii kuch jatiyaan fafoond kii tarah mrit jeevon ke shariron ke carbonic padaarthon ko todakar khaad bana detii hai, jiske anu vanaspatiyon kaa poshan karte hai. aise actinobacteria coloniyaan banaakar apane micelium failaa dete hai. kuch actinobacteria jatiyaan vanaspatiyon kii jadon main sahjivi banakar rahete hai or vanaspatiyon se aahaar paakar unhen badale main nitrogen pahunchaate hai. lekin kuch anya jatiyaan aisi bhi hai jo anya jeevon main rogakaarak hai.
