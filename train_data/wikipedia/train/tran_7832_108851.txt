 malgudi angreji sahitya ke suprasiddh bhartiya lekhak aar ke narayan kii anek rachanaaon main kendriya mahatv praapt ek kaalpanik shahar kaa naam he. unhone is kaalpanik shahar ko aadhaar banaakar apni anek rachanaayen kii hai. malgudi ko praayah dakshin bharat kaa ek kaalpanik kasbaa mana jaataa he.
 aar ke narayan ne is kaalpanik shahar 'malgudi' kaa rachanaatmak prayog apni kai pustakon main kiya he, parantu isi naam par aadhaarit pehali pustak thi 1942 isvi main prakashit unki kahaniyon kaa suprasiddh sangrah malgudi kii kahaniyaan . is kaalpanik sthaan ke sandarbh main swayan lekhak kii tippanee is prakaar he:- "maine is sankalan kaa naam malgudi kasbe par diya he, kyonki isse ise ek bhugolic vyaktitv mil jaataa he. log aksar poochhate hai : 'lekin yah malgudi he kahan?' jawaab main main yahi kahata hoon ki yah kaalpanik naam he or duniya ke kisi bhi nakshe main ise dhundha nahin ja saktaa . agar main kahoon ki malgudi dakshin bharat main ek kasbaa he to yah bhi adhuri sachai hogi, kyonki malgudi ke lakshan duniya main har jagah mil jaayenge."
