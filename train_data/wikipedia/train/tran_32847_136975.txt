 mississippi nadi uttar america ke mahadweep kaa sab se bada nadi mandal he. yah puri tarah sanyukt rajya america ke kshetra ke bheetar bahati he. is nadi kaa strot minesota rajya kii itaska jhil main he jahaan se yah ghumaavon ke saath dakshin kii ore chalati he. 2,320 mile kaa safar tay karke yah mexico kii khadi main nadimukh banaakar vilay ho jaati he. mississippi ko bahut si upanadiyaan pani pradaan karti hai or iskaa jalsambar main 31 america rajya or dakshini canada kaa kuch bhubhag aata hai. yah nadi vishwa kii chauthi sab se lambi nadi he or pani ke prati-ghante bahaav kii maatra main dasvi sab se badi he.
 mississippi ne sanyukt rajya america ke itihaas main bahut ahm bhoomika nibhaai he. mississippi ke kinare mul america adivasee qabeele 10,000 saalon se rahete the. bahut se shikaar-jugad kaa jivan basar karte the, lekin inamen se kuch krishi aadhaarit samaajon main bhi rahete the. europian upniveshikaran ke baad bahut se europea mul ke log yahan aakar bas gaye or zyaadaatar mul adivaasiyon ko khaded diya yaa maar daalaa gaya. kuch kaal ke liye to yah mahaan nadi europian logon ke yahan se aage pashchim main failane main baadha bani rahi lekin dhire-dhire is navi yatayaat ke liye prayog kiya jane laga.
 yah nadi apane bahaav se apane jalsambar kshetra main itani upjau mitti daalati he ke ise vishwa ke sabase adhik upjau kshetron main gina jaataa he. is nadi ko america ke purvi or pashchimi bhaagon ke bich kii seemaa rekha mana jaataa he. america samskruti main kisi vastu ke bare main baat karte hue is tarah kii chizein kahana aam he ke "falaana kaarkaanaa mississippi se pashchim main sab se bada he" yaa "falaana parvat mississippi se purv main sab se ooncha he."
 mississippi kaa naam ek mul america adivasee bhashaa se aata he jise anishnabe yaa ojibve kehete hai. isamen is nadi ko missi-ziibi kaha jaataa tha, jiskaa arth he "mahaan nadi". yah naam francisi sroton ne bigaadkar "mississippi" kar diya.
