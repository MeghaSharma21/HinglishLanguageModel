 aranmula nauka daud bharat ke dakshin-pashchimi rajya kerala kii sarvapracheen nauka daud he. iskaa aayojan olym parv ke avasar par aranmula naamak sthaan par bhagwaan shrikrishna evam arjuna ko samarpit ek mandir ke nikat kiya jaataa he. spardhaa ke darshakon dwaara kiye jane vaale ucchswariya gaan evam kolahal se utsahvardhan karte hue naukaaen jodon main chalati hai. 1972 main utsav ke kaarykram main nauka-spardhaa ko bhi joda gaya tha. is sarpanauka-spardhaa ko dekhne hetu hazaron kii sankhya main darshak pampa nadi ke taton par khadey rahete hai. varsh 2009 main iktalis sarp naukaaen, jinhen chundan vallam kaha jaataa he, ne saprdha main bhaag liya tha. nauka-chaalak shwet mundu evam pagadiyaan baandhe paramparaagat nauka geet gaate hue nauka chaalan karte hai. nauka ke agr-sire par bandhi sunahari jhaalar, pataakaaen evam nauka ke madhya bhaag main lagi saji dhaji chhatariyaan uski chata ko chaar chaand lagaati hai.
 aranmul kerala rajya kii rajdhani se 128 ki.me dur sthit sthaan he. yah pampa nadi ke tat par sthit he. yahaan prasiddh aranmul mandir bhagwaan shri parthasarathi ko samarpit he, jo yahaan apane priy sakha arjuna ke sarathi bane hue darshaye gaye hai. is mandir kii anumaanit aayu lagbhag 1700 varsh he.
 aranmul kii vishesh sarp-nauka ko palliodam kehete hai. inhen bhaktagan yahaan ke poojya aradhy bhagwaan parthasarathi ke divya vaahan ke rup main chalaate hai. ye palliodam pampa nadi ke vibhinn tateey bhaagon ke vibhinn karon se aati hai. prayek main saamanytah 4 chaalak, gayak evam sahaayak hote hai. nauka ko svarnim jhaalaron evam pataakaaon se sajaayaa jaataa he. inke madhya main do se teen saji-dhaji chhatariyaan bhi lagi rahati hai.
