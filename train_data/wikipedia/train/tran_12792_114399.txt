 abhivah yaa flux kii avadharana kaa bhautikee va vyaavhaarik ganit main kai tarah se upyog hota he. mote taur par, kisi sthaan, sataha yaa anya padaarth ko paar karne vaali kisi padaarth, kshetra aadi kii maatra ko abhivah kehete hai. vidyutchumbakatv main vidyut abhivah or chumbakeey abhivah bahut mahatvapurn or upyogi sankalpanaayen hai. 
 kisi kshetra   A  {\displaystyle A} ke prayek bindu par kshetra kaa maan F ho to us kshetra A se nikalne vaala abhivah 
 dhyaan rahe ki yah pravaah se sambandhit lekin bhinn hota he - flux vah maatra he jo kisi sataha ko bhed rahi he or isamen bahaav aavashyak nahin he, jabaki pravaah main kisi prakaar ke bhautik bahaav kaa hona aavashyak he.
 ganitiya sankalpana ke rup main, abhivah ko kisi sadish kshetra ke tal samakalan ke rup main abhivyakt kiya jaataa he. 
 jahaan F koi sadish kshetra he, or dA sadish kshetrafal he . yahan do sadish raashiyon kaa adish gunanfal kaa samakalan kiya gaya he. 
 "flux" mul rup se latini bhashaa kaa ek shabd he or iskaa arth "pravaah" hota he, haalanki aadhunik vigyaan main "flux" or pravaah ke do zaraa bhinn arth hai. shabdashastriyon kaa maananaa he ki "flux" shabd swananukrnaatmak prakriya se behate jal kii dhwani par padaa tha. sanskrit or latini donon hind-europi bhashaa-parivaar kii bahan bhashaayen hai, isliye sanskrit main bhi iskaa ek sajaateey shabd he, "srut". unani bhashaa main bhi iskaa sajaateey "roi" shabd maujuud he.
