 mudar yaa mudhar yaa muzar ek adanani qabeela he. paaramparik rup se iske sadsy al-jazira kshetra ke pashchimi bhaag main based hue hai, jis se us ilaake ko 'diyaar mudar' kaha jaataa he.
 arabi qabeele do bhaagon main baante jate hai: adanani qabeele or qahtaani qabeele . adanani qabeelon kii do badi shakhaayen hai - ek to mudar he or duusari rabiah he. adanani qabeele apane-aap ko abraham ke bete ismail ke vanshaj maanate hai. aitihaasik rup se mudaaron or qahtaani qabeelon main ladaayeeyaan raheen hai.
