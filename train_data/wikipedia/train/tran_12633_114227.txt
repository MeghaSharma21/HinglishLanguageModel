 vaishnavadas rasajaani nabha ji krit bhaktamaal kii tikaa 'bhakthirsbodhini' ke karta priyadas ji ke pautra the, jinhone inhen 'rasajaani' kii oopaadhi di. inke guru sriharijivan ji the. inhone shrimadbhagwat ke barhon skandhon kaa padyanuwaad kiya he. bhagwatmahatmaya ke anuwaad main rachanakaal san. 1802 diya he. jayadev ke gitgovind kaa padyanuwaad san. 1814 main purna hua. inkaa samay sanwat 1770 se san. 1830 ke lagbhag he.
