 tudo ne apane raajnneetik jivan kii shruwaat adhivaktaa, buddhijivi or kaaryakarta ke taur par quebek kii raajnneeti main shruwaat kii. 1960 main wo canada kii libral party ke jariye rashtriya raajnneeti main shaamil hue. unhen lester bi. pieresan ke sansadeey sachiv chunaa gaya or baad main nyaay mantri chune gayi. trudo 1968 main liberlon ke netaa chune gayi. phir 1960 se madhya 1980 tak wo canada kii raajnneeti main chaaye rahe. aavesh se pehle tark unka vyaktigat dhyey tha. unhone 1984 main raajnneeti se sanyas le liya or unke baad jon turnar canada ke pradhanmantri bane. unke jyeshtha putr justin trudo vartmaan main canada ke teiswein pradhanmantri hai.
 buddhijivi unke prabhaavshaalee vyaktitv, budhimatta or rajnatik netritv kshamta ko quebek swatantraaa sangram ke dauraan rashtriya ekta ko bachaae rakhane, oktober samasya kaa daman karne, ek akhil canadian pahchaan ko badhaava dene va kanoonee bahubhashavad ko anumati dilaane ke liye unhen salaam karte hai. aalochak unhen ghamand, aarthik kuprabandh or quebek samskruti or canadian preyarion kii aarthik kshamtaaon ko kuchalane ke liye canada kii nirnay lene kii samiti ko gaiyrkanoonee rup se kendrikrut karne ke liye unki aalochana karte hai. vaise janata ke bich to unki prasiddhi banty hui he lekin shikshavid va itihaas ke scollar unhen canada ke mahanatam pradhanmantriyon main ginate hai or unhen aadhunik canada kaa pitamah maanate hai.
