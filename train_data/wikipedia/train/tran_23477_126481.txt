 rettai waal kuruvi tamil bhashaa main bani bhartiya dharawahik he. jiskaa prasaaran star vijay par 1 june 2015 se somvaar se shuukravaar ko raat 6:30 baje detaa he. isamen mukhya kirdaar main aswin or pavani hai.
 yah kahani do talaak shuda jode kii he, jo alag vichaaron ke kaaran alag ho jate hai. kai varshon ke baad vah ek hi karyaalay main milte hai. vah ek duusare ke khilaaf dushman kii tarah vyavhaar karte hai. nila ke pita apane beti kaa badla lene ke liye do gunde ko supaari detaa he, jisase vah bala ko maar den or uskaa badla puraa ho jaye. lekin nila kii sahaayta se vah batch jaataa he or vah donon ek duusare ke kareeb aa jate hai or ek duusare ko pasand karne lagate hai. bala ko pataa chalata he kii uske pita hi use maarna chaahate hai. yah jaanane ke baad vah bhi nila ke upar krodhit ho jaataa he. iske baad vah apane ek mitra kii shaadi main jaataa he, jahaan uskaa man taaja ho sake. vahaan vah punah nila se miltaa he. iske baad kahani punah hasya or prem kii vidha main aa jaati he.
