 kababachini naam se kaali mirch sadrush savrunt fal bazaar main milte hai. inkaa swaad katu-tikt hota he, kintu chabane se manoram tikshn gandh aati he or jeebh sheethal maaloom hoti he. ise kankol, sugandhmarich, shitalcheenee or qubeb bhi kehete hai. yah piperasii kul kii pyper qubebaa naamak lata kaa fal he jo java, sumatra tathaa borneo main swat: paidaa hoti he. lanka tathaa dakshin bharat ke kuch bhaagon main bhi ise ugaaya jaataa he.
 kababachini kii lata arohi evam varshanuvarshi, cond spasht tathaa moti sandhiyon se yukt or patr chikne, lambaagra, savrunt or spasht shiraonvaale tathaa adhiktar ytaakar hote hai. pushp avrunt, dwikshayak or shooki manjari se nikalate hai. vyavhaar ke liye apakv parantu purna viksit falon ko hi todakar sukhaya jaataa he. ye golakaar, sookhne par gadhe bhure rang ke kintu dhulidhusarit, vyas ke lagbhag chaar millimetre or ek bijwale hote hai. faltvak ke upar silavaton kaa jaal bana hota he. fal ke sheersh bhaag par trirashmyakaar vartikagra or aadhaar par lagbhag chaar millimetre lambii vrunt sadrush bahyavriddhi upasthit rahati he.
 ayurvedic chikitsa main iskaa upyog bahut kam haata he, parantu navy chikitsa paddati main iskaa bahut mahatv he. ise katu tikt, deepak-pachak, vrishy tathaa kaph, vaat, trisha evam mukh kii jadta or durgandh dur karanevaali kaha gaya he. shleshmal kalaon, visheshat: mutr maarg, guda evam shwaasamarg kii shleshmal kalaon par isaki uttejk kriya hoti he. puraane sujak, arsh tathaa puraane kafarog main uttejk, mutrajnak, putihar, vaatnashaka, deepak or kafaghn gunon ke kaaran iskaa prachur upyog hota he. kababachini main 5-20 pratishat udanevaala tel hota he, jisamen tarapin, sesqui-tarapin tathaa kedinini aadi shrenee ke kai dravyon kaa mishran hota he.
