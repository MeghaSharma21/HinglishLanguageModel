 aataa, anaaj ke daano ko pis kar praapt hua ek powder yaa churna he. yah roti banane ke kaam aata he, jo ki kai sabhyataaon main ek pradhan bhojan he. itihaas main vibhinn samay par aate kii paryaapt aapurti ek pramukh aarthik or raajnneetik mudda rahaa he. gehun kaa aataa uttar bharat, europe or uttar america samskruti main sabase mahatvapurn khaadya padaarthon main se ek he or yah naanaa prakaar kii roti, dabl roti or adhikansh europea shaileyon kii bread or pastery banane kaa pramukh sanghatak he. makka kaa aataa pracheen kaal se latin america bhojan kaa ek pradhan ang he.
 aate main bade anupaat main mand upasthit hota he jo ki ek jatil carbohydrate he or jise policecride bhi kehete hai. 
 aate ko praapt karne ke liye anaaj ko chakki se pisa jaataa he. yah chakki aam gharelu pathar kii chakki se lekar hawa kii chakki, pani kii chakki yaa bijli kii chakki bhi ho sakati he. aataa ko prayog se pehle ek chalani ke madhyam chaanaa jaataa he, jisase isame maujuud bhusi yaa chokar isse alag ho jaati he.
