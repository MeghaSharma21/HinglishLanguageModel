 okhlee, okhal yaa kharal ek aise majboot katore ko kehete hai jisamen ek baari moosal naamak gadanuma danda se chizein todi or pisi jaati hai. okhlee or moosal aksar sakhtâ€‹ lakdi yaa pathar ke bane hote hai. pise jane vaali chizein okhlee main daali jaati hai or phir unhen moosal ke prahaar se todaa yaa moosal ke saath raundkar pisa jaataa he. iskaa prayog masaale banane, chatniyaan banane or aushadhi pisane main bahut kiya jaataa he. kuch okhliyaan bahut badi hoti hai or parmparik samaajon main usamen khaane ke padaarth banane ke liye kai gruhaniyan milakar baari moosal chalaati hai. bahut si samskritiyon main iskaa prayog davaaiyaan banane ke liye hone se ise chikitsa yaa swasthya kaa chihn bhi mana jaataa he. sushrut or charak jaise pracheen chikitsakon ke dwaara prayog kiye jane kii vajah se is ayurveda kaa bhi chihn mana jaataa he.
 angrezi main okhlee ko 'mortar' or moosal ko 'pasal' kehete hai. pasal shabd vastav main latini bhashaa ke 'pistilam' jo latini ke hi 'pinsere' shabd se sambandhit he. latini main iskaa arth 'kuchalakar todna' he. kyonki latini or sanskrit donon hind-europea bhashaayen hai, isliye yahi shabd sajaateey rup main sanskrit ke 'pinsti' shabd main dekha ja saktaa he jiskaa hindi rup 'peesna' he. 
 marwari bhashaa main 'okhlee' ko 'ukala' tathaa 'moosal' ko 'sobila' kehete he, evam pisane kii kriya ko 'khodna' kaha jaataa he.
