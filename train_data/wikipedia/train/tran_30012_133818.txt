 samudr vigyaan bhuvigyaan kii ek shakha he jo samudron kaa adhyayan karti he. iske antargat samudr, tateey kshetra, esturis, tateey jal, shelavj or oshan bed, samudri jeevon, samudri dhaaraon, tarangon, bhubhautikeey taralagatiki evam anek anyaanya vishyon kaa adhyayan kiya jataa he. isamen jeevigyaan, rasayan vigyaan, jiology, bhautikee aadi sabaki aavashyaktaa padati he. is kshetra main kaam karne waalon ko oshnographer kehete hai.
 ociyanography vah vigyaan he jisamen sagaron tathaa mahasaagaron ke harpahalu kaa vaigyanik adhyayan kiya jaataa he. ociyanography main samudr, uske tat, samudri shakhaon se lekar costal wateror samudri chattaanon kii gaharaai kaa jayja lenaa hota he.
 ociyanography kabhi na khatm hone vaali jigyaasaaon kaa samandarhai. mahaasaagar main dheron jaankaree ke khajaane chhipe hai jinke rahasy par se paradaa uthanaa baaki he. is kaam main samudr ke bheetar ghanton guzarkar cemple jutanaa, survey karnaa, data vishleshit karnaa hota he. yah khoj aadhaarit kshetra he isliye isamen kaam karne vaale logon ko samudr ke aas-paas ke ilaakon main lanbaa samay gujaarna padataa he.
 ociyanographer mahasaagaron va costal water ke rahasy baariki se jaanchata he. vah mahasagariya jal kii gati, jal ke vitaran or uske physical va chemical gun va lakshan kaa adhyayan karata he aurayah jaanane kii koshish karata he ki inkaa samudr ke tateey ilaakon main rahane vaale logon or jalwaayu parakya asar padataa he.
 yah kshetra shodh aadhaarit he jahaan ek lanbaa samay samudri uthal-puthal ke bich bitaana hota he. milane vaali chunautiyon va khataron ke kareeb se guzarana hota he.
