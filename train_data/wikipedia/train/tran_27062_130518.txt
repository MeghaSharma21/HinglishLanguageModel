 bhautikee main kanabh unmajji sanvruti he jo sthool rup se ek jatil pranali he jaise ek thos kaa vyavhaar jisamen ki mukt akaash main durbal anyonya kriya karne vaale bhinn kan hon. udhaaharan ke liye electron kisi ardhchaalak main gati karata he to anya electronon or nabhik se takkaron ke kaaran isaki gati jatil rup se pathit hoti he lekin yah lagbhag usee tarah vyavhaar karata he jaise bhinn dravyaman kaa koi electron vyavdhaan rahit mukt akaash main gati karata he. bhinn dravyaman ke is "electron" ko "electron kanabh" kehete hai.
 sabhi thos teen tarah ke kanon: electron, protone or neutron se milakar bane hote hai. kanabh inamen se koi bhi nahin he balki yah ek unmajji sanvruti he jo thoson ke andar ghatit hoti he. isliye yah sambhav he ki ek kan akaash main vicharan kar saktaa he lekin ek kanabh' keval thos ke andar hi rah saktaa he.
 is anubhag main kanabh or samuhik uttejan ke udhaaharan diye gayi hai. pehle upanubhag main wo kanabh diye gayi hai jo vibhinn padaarthon main saamaanya paristhitiyon main paye jate hai; duusare upanubhag main vishisht paristhitiyon main praapt hone vaale kanabh diye gayi hai.
