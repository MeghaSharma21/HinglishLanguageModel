 zee kaar praant, jise dhi kaar praant or qui kaar​ praant bhi kehete hai, iraq kaa ek praant he.
 zee kaar ke naam main 'za' arabi lipi ke 'ذ‎' akshar kaa bhartiya upmahadweep or iran main uccharan he. kuch arabi-bhashi kshetron main iskaa uccharan 'dha' yaa 'ji' jaisaa hota he. dhyaan den ki is 'ji' akshar kaa uccharan bindu-rahit 'tha' akshar se zaraa alag he or angrezi ke 'kiin' or 'oard' se miltaa-julata he. saath hi yah bhi dhyaan den ki bindu-vaale 'qa' kaa uccharan bina-bindu vaale 'k' se miltaa-julata lekin thodaa sa bhinn he. 'qa' kaa uccharan 'qeemat' or 'qareeb' jaise shabdon main miltaa he.
 zee kaar praant atipracheen sumer sabhyataa kii mukhyabhumi he. us samskruti ke bahut se shaharon ke khandhhar isi praant main sthit hai, jinamein ur, laagaash, aridu, larsa, girasu, ummaa or baad-tibira shaamil hai.
 is praant ke lagbhag sabhi log shiya islam ke anuyaayi arab jati ke log hai.
