 tiangong-1 china kaa pehla prototip antariksh stesan he. yah donon maanavyukt prayogshaala or ek prayogtmak docking kshamtaaon kaa pradarshan karne ke liye sevarat he. china ne mobile doorsanchaar hetu pehla upgrah tiaanatong-01 prakshepit kiya. 
 china ne 6 agast 2016 ko mobile doorsanchaar suvidhaon ko sudrudh karne hetu pehla upgrah safaltaapurv prakshepit kiya. ise china ke jichang upgrah prakshepan centre se long march-3bi raaket dwaara prakshepit kiya gaya. 
 yah china dwaara banaaya gaya pehla swadeshi mobile doorsanchaar upgrah he. yah desh ke antariksh soochna evam sanchaar seva ke buniyadi dhaanche main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaayega.
