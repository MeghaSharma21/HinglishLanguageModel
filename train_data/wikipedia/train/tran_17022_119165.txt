 chitral pakistan ke kaibar-pakhtuunakva praant ke sabase uttari bhaag main sthit ek zila he. yah us praant kaa sabase bada zila he. iskaa kshetrafal 14,850 varg kimi he or 1998 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 3,18,689 thi. 7,708 meter unchaa tirich mir, jo duniya ke sabase oonche pahaado main se he, is zile main sthit he. chitral zile kii rajdhani chitral shahar he.
 chitral zila pakistan or kaibar-pakhtuunakva praant donon kaa sabase uttar kaa zila he. iske pashchim or uttar main afghanistan he. uttar main afghanistan kaa vaakhaan galiyara aata he jo kuch sthanon par sirf 16 kimi chaudaa he jiske paar tajikistan sthit he. chitral zile ke purv main gilgit-balatistan he or dakshin main kaibar-pakhtuunakva ke hi upree dir or swat zile sthit hai. chitral zile ke kuch bhaag main pamir parvat aate hai isliye yah zila bahut hi pahari kshetra he.
 chitral zila pakistan or gilgit-balatistan se sirf do sadakon se judaa hua he. ek to lovari darre se hoti hui dir jaati he or duusari shandur top se hoti hui gizar zile ke raste se gilgit jaati he. donon hi raste sardiyon main barfabaari ke kaaran band ho jate hai. pakistani sarkaar lovari darre ke neeche ek surang banavaa rahi he. chitral ke ird-gird or bhi pahari darre hai jinase paidal log zile se baahar aa-ja sakate hai.
 chitral main adhiktar kho log rahete hai jinhen chitrali log bhi kaha jaataa he, jo khovaar bhashaa naam kii ek dardi bhashaa bolte hai. is zile kii bumburet vaadi main kalash log rahete hai.
