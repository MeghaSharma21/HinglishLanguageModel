 kofi annan ek ghanai kootneetijna hai. ve 1962 se 1974 tak or 1974 se 2006 tak sanyukt raashtra main karyrat rahe. ve 1 janvari 1997 se 31 disambar 2006 tak do kaarykalon ke liye sanyukt raashtra ke mahasachiv rahe. unhen sanyukt raashtra ke saath 2001 main nobel shanti puraskaar se sah-puraskrit kiya gaya. lambii beemaari ke chalate annan kaa 18 agast 2018 ko nidhan ho gaya.
 kofi annan kaa janm 8 april 1938 ko gold coast ke kumsi naamak shahar main hua. 1954 se 1957 tak kofi annan ne maphintism skool main shiksha lee. annan 1957 main ford foundation kii di chatravriti par america gaye. vahaan 1958 se 1961 tak unhone minesota rajya ke sant paul shahar main macalester college main arthshaastra kii padhai kii or 1961 main unhen snaatak kii degree mili. 1961 main annan ne "antarrashtriya sambandh" main geneva ke graduate institute of international studies se diie kii degree kii. unhone 1971 se june 1972 main elored slown falo ke taur par emiti se management main emes kii degree praapt kii. annan angreji, french, kru, akan kii anya boliyon or anya africa bhashaaon main dharapravaah he.
 1962 main kofi annan ne sanyukt raashtra kii sanstha vishwa swasthya sanghathan ke liye ek bajet adhikari ke rup main kaam shuru kar diya. ve vishwa swasthya sanghathan ke saath 1965 tak rahe. 1965 se 1972 tak unhone ithiopia kii rajdhani addis ababa main sanyukt raashtra kii iconomik kamishan for afrika ke liye kaam kiya. wo agast 1972 se may 1974 tak geneva main sanyukt raashtra ke liye prashasnic prabandhan adhikari ke taur par rahe. 1973 kii arab-israeli jang ke baad may 1974 se november 1974 tak wo misr main shanti abhiyaan main sanyukt raashtra dwaara karyrat asainik karmchaariyon ke mukhya adhikari ke pad par karyrat rahe. uske baad unhone sanyukt raashtra chhod diya or ghana laut gaye. 1974 se 1976 tak vah ghana main paryaton ke nideshak ke rup main rahe.
 1976 main ve sanyukt raashtra main kaary karne hetu geneva laut gaye. 1980 main ve sanyukt raashtra ke sharnaarthi ucchayog ke up-nideshak niyukt hue. 1984 main vah sanyukt raashtra ke bajet vibhaag ke adhyaksh ke rup main new york vaapis aae. 1987 main unhen sanyukt raashtra ke maanav sansaadhan vibhaag or 1990 main bajet evam yojana vibhaag kaa sahaayak mahasachiv niyukt kiya gaya. march 1992 se farvari 1993 tak ve shanti abhiyaanon ke sahaayak mahasachiv rahe. march 1993 main unhen sanyukt raashtra kaa avar mahasachiv niyukt kiya gaya or ve disambar 1996 tak is pad par karyrat rahe.
 annan 1 janvari 1997 se 31 disambar 2006 tak sanyukt raashtra ke mahasachiv rahe. 2001 main unhen or sanyukt raashtra ko nobel shanti puraskaar se sah-puraskrit kiya gaya.
 13 disambar 1996 main sanyukt raashtra suraksha parishad ne annan kii sifaarish kii taaki vah purv mahasachiv dow botrus botrus ghali kii jagah le saken. dow ghali ke duusare kaarykaal ko america ke vito kaa saamana karnaa padaa tha. chaar din baad mahasabha ke chunaav se unhone apana pehla kaarykaal 1 janvari 1997 se prarambh kiya.
 unka vivaah titi se hua tha or unki beti aama kaa janm november 1969 main nigeria ke lagos shahar main hua. july 1973 main unke putr kojo kaa janm hua.
