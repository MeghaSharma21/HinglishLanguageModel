 australian footbaal, atharah khilaadiyon kii do tiimon ke bich khelaa jaataa he. yah ek roopantarit cricket ground par khelaa jaataa he. khel ke pehle niyam 1859 main likhe gaye the. yah australia main teesraa sabase lokapriy khel he or sardiyon ke maheenon main yah sabase adhik lokapriy he. yah nauru kaa rashtriya khel he.
