  murshid kuli khan 1717 main mughal shaasha farukhsier dwaara bengal kaa soobedaar banaaya gaya. yah mogul samraat dwaara niyukt antim soobedaar tha, isi ke saath bengal main vanshanugat soobedaari shashan kii shruwaat huyi. yadhyapi vah 1700 se hi uskaa vaastavik shaasha ban gaya tha. dewan banane ke baad usne khud ko kendriya niyantran se mukt kiya phir bhi badshaah ko najarane ke rup main badi rakam adaa karata rahaa.
