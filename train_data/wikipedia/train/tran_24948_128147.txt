 kamtapur bharat ke pashchim bengal ke uttari bhaag ke kuch zilon or un se lagane vaale assam rajya ke kuch zilon par vistrut bhubhag kaa parmparik naam he. is kshetra kii ek vishesh saanskrithik or aitihaasik pahchaan he or yahan par "kamtapur" naamak ek alag rajya banane kii maang he. sthaneey logon kii bahusankhya rajbongshi tathaa coach bhashaa bolne vaale rajbongshi samudaay kii sadsy he. alag rajya ke aandolan kaa netritv kamtapur piples party naamak rajnatik party kar rahi he.
 pracheen kamtapur rajya brahmaputra nadi kii ghati ke pashchimi bhaag me sthit tha. kuch itihaaskaar maanate hai ki chilpata van main nalrajar garh isaki sarvapratham rajdhani thi. lambe kaal ke vikaas va ghatnakram ke baad rajdhani badalakar pehle mayanguri, phir pruthu raajar garh, phir singijani or ant main gosanimari kar di gai, jo 7veen shatabdi se ek mahatvapurn nadi-bandargah thi.
 650 isvi se 1498 i kaal tak kamtapur purv bharat main ek swatantra hindu rajya tha. kamta rajya ke naam se yah raja vishwa singh dwaara sthapit coach rajvansh ke ubharne se pehle yahan par panapata rahaa. 1498 main, jab kamtapur par nilambar naamak naresh kaa raaj tha, bengal sultanate ke allaudin hossain shah ne yahan akraman kiya or rajdhani gosanimari ko dhwast kar diya. iske baad doaar kshetra main hingulav main rajdhani rakhane vaala coach rajya janma.
