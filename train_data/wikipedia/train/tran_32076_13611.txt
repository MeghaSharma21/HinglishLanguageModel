 
 stronsium ek rasinic tatv he. yah kshareey parthiv dhaatu naamak tatv samooh kaa sadsy he. apane shuddh rup main yah ek mulaayam shwet-chaandi yaa pile se rang kii dhaatu hota he jo tezi se anya tatwon ke saath rasinic abhikriya kar letaa he. agar ise hawa main khulaa rakhaa jaae to iskaa rang jald hi pilaa hone lagata he. iske rasinic or bhautik gun aavart sarini main iske do padosi tatv - calcium va barium - se milte-julate hai. yah selestin, patanisite or stronshianite naamak khanijon main prakritik rup se paaya jaataa he.
