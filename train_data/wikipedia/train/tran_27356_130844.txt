 'sanjhi parv' malwa va nimad anchal kaa pramukh parv he, saanji, rajasthan, gujarat, brajpradesh, malwa, nimad tathaa anya kai kshtron main kuwanri kanyaon dwaara manaaya jane vaala tyohaar he jo bhadrapada kii poornima se aswin maas kii amavasya tak arthath puure shraadh paksh main solah dinon tak manaaya jaataa he. saanjhe kaa tyohaar kunvaari kanyaayein atyant utsaah or harsh se manaati hai. 
 ghar ke baahar, daravaaje par deevaaron par kunvaari gaay kaa gobar lekar ladkiyaan vibhinn akritiyaan banaati hai. unhen phool patton, malipanna sindur aadi se sajaati hai or sandhya samay unka pujan karti hai. 
 kunari balaaye gobar se diwaar lipakar gobar se hi sanjhadevi kii kalakritiyaan banaati hai. ise phool, pattee va rangeen chamkile kaagajon se sajaayaa jaataa he. pratidin shaam ko kanyaayein ghar-ghar jaakar sanjhadevi ke geet gaati hai evam prasad vitaran karti hai. prasad aisa banaaya jaataa he jise koi tad na sake. jis kanya ke ghar kaa prasad tad nahin pate uski prashansa hoti he. 
 ant ke paanch dinon main hathi-ghode, kila-kot, gaadi aadi kii akritiyaan banaai jaati hai. solah din ke parv ke ant main amavasya ko sanjha devi ko vidaa kiya jaataa he.
