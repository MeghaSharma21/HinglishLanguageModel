 sanyavad kisi desh kii sarkaar yaa janata kii vah darshan he jo vishwaas karata he ki unke desh ko ek shaktishaalee sena banani evam rakhni chaiye tathaa apane desh ke hiton kii suraksha karne evam unke sanvardhan ke liye iskaa jamkar upyog karnaa chaiye.
 sanyvaadee vichaardhaara apane sainya taiyaariyon ke peeche tark detii rahati he ki 'shakti se hi shaanti aati he'. duusare shabdon main, unka maananaa he ki 'yadi aap shanti chaahate hai to yuddh ke liye taiyaar raho.' sanyavad, aadhunik shantvaad kaa thik ulta darshan he. aitihaasik rup se 'sanyavad' kaa prayog un rajyon ke liye hua tha jo samrajyavad kii niti par chalane vaale the, jaise- sparta, britani samrajya, japani samrajya, sanyukt rajya, german samrajya, naaji germany, pratham francisi samrajya, fasivadi italy, soviet sangh, saddaam hossain ke adheen iraq aadi. vartmaan samay main 'sanyavad' shabd kaa prayog praayah pashchimi deshon ke liye kiya jaataa he jinka netritv yues kar rahaa he.
