 bhikha sahab baawari panth kii bhurakuda, gajipur shakha ke sant gulaal sahab ke shishy the. inkaa janm azamgarh jile ke khanpur bohana gaanv main hua tha. san 1760 main gulaal sahab kii mrityu ke baad bhikha bhurakuda gaddi ke mahant hue. inke do pramukh shishy hue - gobind sahab or chaturbhujadas. gobind sahab ne faijabad main apni alag gaddi sthapit kii. chaturbhujadas bhurakuda main hi rahe.
