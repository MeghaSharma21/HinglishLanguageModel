 karishma kaa karishma bhartiya hindi dharawahik he, jiskaa prasaaran star plus par 24 janvari 2003 se lekar 16 april 2004 tak hua. iske nirmaataa sunil doshi hai. iskaa nirdesan swapn joshi ne kiya he.
 yah kahani ek vaigyanik vikram ki he. jo ek aise robot kaa avishkaar karata he jise koi bhi maanav samaj le. vah uskaa naam karishma rakhata he. uski patni sheethal ko uski padosi yah kah ke kaan bhartee he ki vikram kaa kisi or ke saath prem sambandh he. kyonki vah der raat tak ghar nahin lautataa tha. vah apane parivaar ke saamne karishma ko laata he. iske baad sheethal use vikram kii duusari patni kii beti samajhati he. lekin vikram use samajha detaa he. iske baad keval vikram, sheethal or unke bachche rahul ko hi is bare main pataa hota he ki vah ek robot he. unke padosi shraddha or paresh hameshaa unke kaam main taang adaate rahete hai.
