 aavarti jamaa ek vishesh prakaar kaa saavadhi jamaa he jisamen prati maah yaa prati varsh ek nishchit raashi jamaa kii jaati he. isamen lagbhag vahi byaaj miltaa he jo saavadhi jamaa main miltaa he. is prakaar kaa jamaa bhi ek nishchit tithi ko paripakv hota he. yah jamaa un logon ke liye vishesh suvidhajanak he jinako ek nishchit niyamit aay hoti he.
