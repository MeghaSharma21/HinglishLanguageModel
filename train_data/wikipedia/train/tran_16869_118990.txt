 retro uttar aadhunik samay kii saanskrithik rup se aprachlit yaa pracheen style, pravriti, pranali yaa tarika he, lekin us kaal se hi ek baar phir vyavhaarik yaa satahi taur par prachlit maapdand ban chukaa he. retro style kii iconografi or chitrakaari kaa uttar aadhunik yug kii kala, vigyaapan, maas media aadi main prayog audyogic kranti ke samay se le kar vartmaan samay tak kiya gaya he|
 retro shabd kii vyutpatti latin bhashaa ke upsarg retro se hui he, jiskaa arth he "peeche kii ore" yaa "pracheen kaal main"-jaisaa ki kai shabdon jaise ki retrogred main dikhaai detaa he, is shabd kaa aashay ateet kii ore janaa he na ki bhavishya kii ore badhanaa. isi tarah retrospective shabd bhi ateet ko yaad karne kaa sandarbh detaa he.

 uske baad jald hi is shabd kaa prachaar fishan or samskruti se sambandhit media ke logon ne angrezi bhashaa main kiya, jahaan iskaa prayog apekshaakrit pracheen lekin saapeksh rup se aadhunik chalanon ke punaruddhar ke liye kiya gaya. . simulekra and simulation main, francisi siddhantvaadi jin bodrillard retro ko ateet ko puranikta se alag karne, aadhunik yug kii ore le jane vaale bade vichaaron se vartmaan ko dur le jane vaale shabd ke rup main varnit karte hai.
 retro kaa saadhaaran arth kaaljayi yaa clasik kii hi tarah puraane fishan kaa yaa purana ho saktaa he. yuddh ke turant baad ke varshon main ise aadhuniktaa se bhi jodakar dekha gaya, jahaan kedilax par telphins se lekar godaamon tak ke liye saundaryabodhak shabd ke rup main prayukt hua. kabhi-kabhi yah sampurna jivan darshan ko bhi darsha saktaa he, udhaaharan ke liye, saamaajik ruudhivaad, ghar main mili shiksha yaa stri-purush kii paaramparik bhoomikaayen. takneekee rup se prachlit na rahi chizon ke liye bhi retro shabd kaa prayog kiya ja saktaa he, udhaaharan ke liye, manual tiperiter, cash register, baari cell phone, yaa puraane computer games ke liye bhi is shabd kaa prayog ho saktaa he. lekin adhiktar retro kuch samay pehle prachlit un chizon or drushtikonon ko varnit karne ke liye upyog kiya jaataa he jo ab aadhunik nahin lagatee. yah us tareeke main buniyadi badlaav kii ore ishaara karata he jisase ham ateet ko jodakar dekhte hai. punaruddhar ke paaramparik swaroopon se bhinn, retro shabd kuch samay pehle peeche chute samay kii kuch vidambnaapurna to kuch chaav ke saath vyakhya karata he. ise bhavashunya nostalgia bhi kaha gaya he. kabhi kabhi "retro" bhi pachaas ke dashak tak ke yug ko darshata he.
