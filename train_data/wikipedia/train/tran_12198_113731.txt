 
 2013 isisi mahila twanti-20 vishwa cap kwalifire ek antarrashtriya cricket toornament dublin, ireland main yojit 23 july se 1 agast 2013 tha. toornament ke sheersh teen timen bangladesh main 2014 ke twanti-20 vishwa cap ke liye aage badhane ke saath, mahila vishwa twanti-20 kwalifire ke udghaatan sanskaran tha.
 aath tiimon ke toornament main khelaa tha. mejabaan, ireland, kshetreey kwalifing toornament se 2012 twanti-20 vishwa cap main pakistan or srilanka ke do sabase kam rakhaa tiimon, saath hi paanch tiimon se jude hue the. pakistan or srilanka donon par chale gaye toornament main aparajit hone ke liye, sheershak saajha karne ke baad antim barish se baadhit kiya gaya tha. ireland tisare sthaan ke play of main netherlands ko parajeet bhi twanti-20 vishwa cap ke liye kwalifai karne ke liye.
 mul rup se, isisi nirdhaarit kiya tha ki keval toornament ke vijeta, twanti -20 vishwa cap ke liye kwalifai karegaa ki toornament to keval aath tiimon hone ke saath. yah nirnay ek thos mahila cricket kaa samarthan karne ke prayaas ke hisse ke rup main 2013 antarrashtriya cricket parishad june 2013 main vaarshik sammelan, badal gaya tha. kwalifire main aath tiimon ko chaar tiimon ki semifinal ek rapeches toornament main bhaag lene ke liye chal rahaa banane main asafal saath, unki ranking ke aadhaar par do samoohon main vibhajit kiya gaya.
