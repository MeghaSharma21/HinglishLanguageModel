 madhyahn rekha yaa yamyottar prithvi ke uttari evam dakshini dhruv ke milaane vaali or uttar-dakshin disha main khinchi gayi kaalpanik rekhaon ko kehete hai. 
 prithvi ke bich-bich 0° deshaantar par khinchi gai madhyahn rekha pradhan madhyahn rekha, pradhan yamyottar, yaa greenich rekha kehalaati he. duniya kaa maanak samay isi rekha se nirdhaarit kiya jaataa hai . landan ke ek shahar greenich isi rekha par sthit hai isli ise greenich rekha bhi kehete he. pradhan madhyahn rekha ke purv ne sthit 180° tak ke deshaantar, purvi deshaantar tathaa pashchim kii ore sthit deshaantar, pashchimi deshaantar kehalaate hai.
