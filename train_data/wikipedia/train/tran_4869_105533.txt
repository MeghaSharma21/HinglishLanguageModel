 amir al-muminin :, aamtaur par "vishvaasiyon ke commander" yaa "vafaadaaron ke netaa". kuch caliph or anya swatantra muslim shaskaon ke naam kii arabi style he jo ek samudaay se vaidhata kaa daawa karte hai. musalamaanon kaa leader, muslim deshon or samrajyon main shaskaon ke khitaab ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he or abhi bhi kuch muslim netaaon ke liye upyog kiya jaataa he
 khitaab kaa upyog ke liye caliph kaa bananaa yaa hona hi zaruri nahin he. lekin ek nishchit rup se vishvaasiyon kaa yaa kaaryakartaaon ke netritv kaa varnan bhi kiya gaya he jo ki ek khalipha se judaa hua ho. turk sultaanon ne vishesh rup se is khitaab kaa istemaal kiya he. iske alaava, is shabd kaa upyog purushon dwaara kiya jaataa he, jinka khalipha hona zaruri bhi nahin or daawa bhi nahin kiya gaya.
