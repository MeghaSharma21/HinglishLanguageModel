 yah madhya dakshin dilli kaa ek laal doraa kshetra he. yahan sasta bazaar he, va saath hi kai thok vastuon kaa bazaar bhi he.
 yah syed vansh, jo ki dilli par san 1414-1451 tak shashan karte the. inhone tughlaq vansh ke baad shashan sambhaala va maatr 37 varsh baad hi afgaan lodhi vansh dwaara shashan adhikrut karaliya gaya.us syed vansh dwaara basaaya hua kshetra tha, jo aaj kotla mubarakapur ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
 kotla mubarakapur kaa nakshaa dekhne hetu clic karen.
