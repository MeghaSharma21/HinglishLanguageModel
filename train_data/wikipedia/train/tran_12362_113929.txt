 alexey varabiov roosi gayak or abhinayta hai jo rusi or angrezi donon main pradarshan karte hai. intaraashtriya pariyojno ke liye varabiov elex spairo naam kaa bhi prayog karte hai, jo ki unke rusi naam kaa anuwaad he. inhen 2005 main 17 varsh kii umr main da eks factor ke rusi avataran main bhaag lene se khyaati praapt hui. 2006 main inhone universal music rashia ke saath qaraar kiya. 2011 main varabiov ne apane desh kaa germany ke duceldorf shahar main yojit hui eurovision sangeet pratiyogita main pratinidhitiv kiya tha.
