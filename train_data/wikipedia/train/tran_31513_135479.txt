 exbox microsoft dwaara banaaya gaya ek khilne vaala utpaada he. jisamen exbox 360 bhi shaamil he.
 yah khilne vaala upkaran exbox kaa pehla sanskaran he.
 navambar 2005 main nikle vaastavik exbox kii safalataa ke pashchaat ise banaaya gaya. iskaa vikaas exbox next, jenon or exbox 2 ke samay hua. farvari 2003 main ise jenon software ke saath utaarne kii taiyaari kii gai thi. jise microsoft ke upaadhyaksh je allord ne nirdesh main kiya gaya. ise 400 develper ne milakar banaaya tha. ise 22 navambar 2005 main bazaar main nikaala gaya. tab ise america or canada main hi pradarshit kiya gaya. europe main ise 2 disambar 2005 or japan main ise 10 disambar 2005 main nikaala gaya. iske pashchaat ise mexico, brazil, chile, colombia, hong kong, singapore, dakshin korea, taiwan, australia, newzealand, dakshin africa, bharat or rus main nikaala gaya. yah pehle varsh 36 deshon main utraa.
 jab microsoft ne exbox 360 ko bazaar main utaara tha, tab yah online khilne kii seva 24 ghanton ke liye band pad gai thi. ise ek nae sanskaran main banane ke liye band karnaa padaa tha. tab ise exbox live silver kehete the. baad main ise exbox live free naam kar diya gaya. ise nickshulk upyog kiya ja saktaa he. isamen prayokta ke naam or chhavi ko daalkar profil banaaya ja saktaa he. iske alaava microsoft ne exbox live gold naam ke ek paise vaali seva bhi banaai.
 22 navambar 2013 main yah uttari america ke bazaar main nikaala gaya.
