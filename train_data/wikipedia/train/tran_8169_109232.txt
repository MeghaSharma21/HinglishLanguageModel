 hetukar jha ek bhartiya lekhak, professor, shodhakarta or fulbrit scollar hai. samprati ve maharajaadhiraj kameshwar singh kalyani nyaas ke maanad prabandh nyaasi hai.
 jha kaa janm bihar ke madhubani jila ke sarisb paahi jile main hua tha. unke pita babu baldev jha or mata shrimati mohini devi thim. unki aarambhik shiksha sarisb paahi ke sthaneey vidhyalay main hui thi.
 jha kaa vivaah 1960 main shrimati indira jha se se hua or unke teen putr tejkar jha, shrutikar jha or madhukar jha kaa janm kramshah 1968, 1972 or 1975 main hua. unhone apni sari paariwarik sampatti chandradhari mithila rajya sangrahalay, darbhanga ko de di taaki unke pita swargeey baldev jha ke naam par ek ddeergha kaa nirmaan ho sake.
 unhone patna siance college main namakan liya or uttar snaatak ke liye patna vishvavidhyalay. unhone apana snaatak patna college se 1965 main, uttar snaatak 1967 main or piechdi bhi patna vishvavidhyalay se 1980 main kiya.

 1984-85 main USEFI ke dwaara di gai phulbrit scolership ke saakshaatkaar panel main the. ve do saal tak sulabh institute of sosal science ke nideshak bhi rahe.
 1968 ke aarambh main unhone bihar kii samskruti or samaaj par shodh kaa bhi kaam kiya. unka nirnaayak nishkarsh yah rahaa he kipaaramparik gramin samudaay main bataur prakaaryaatmak samgr tuutan vighatan kaa mukhya kaaran rahaa
 unhen 1972 main bharat main amareeki shaikshanik nyaas kii ore se phulbrit scolership pradaan kii gai.
