 sharir ke andar nirantar honevaali dhvaniyon ke sunne kii kriya ko parishravan kehete hai. is shabd kaa prayog chikitsa vigyaan main kiya jaataa he. roganidan main is kriya se bahut sahaayta milti he. sharir ke andar kii dhvaniyon ko sunne ke liye praayah aalaa kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. 
 is kala ke badhate hue prayog kaa shrey lenek ko milanaa chahie, jinhone san 1819 main parishravan yantra kaa avishkaar kiya. is yantra ke avishkaar ke pehle ye dhvaniyaan kaan se suni jaati thim. aadhunik parishravan yantra dhwani ke bhautik gunon par aadhrut he. isamen kaanon main laganevaala bhaag dhaatu kaa hota he or lagbhag 10 inch lambii rabar kii naliyon dwaara vakshagolak se judaa rahata he.
 chikitsak is yantra dwaara hriday, shwaas or antadiyon kii dhwani sunate hai. saamanyat: hriday jab sikudta he to labb dhwani hoti he or phir jab vah failata he to dab dhwani hoti he, jinhen kramash: pratham or dwitiya hridayadhvani kehete hai. beemaari main anya prakaar kii dhvaniyaan or 'mar mar' dhwani sunai detii he. hawa fefde main jate or nikalate samay dhwani karti he, jise shwasadhvani kehete hai. fefde kii bimariyon main is dhwani main parivartan ho jaataa he or duusare dhang kii dhwani bhi sunai dene lagati he, jise ronki yaa crepetesan kehete hai. antadiyon kii dhwani kii 'barborism' kehete hai. yadi yah na sunai de to antadiyon kaa avrodh yaa peritonium kii sujan kaa nidaan samajhnaa chahie. parishravan yantra kii sahaayta se bhrunhruday kii dhwani tathaa dhamniyon kii 'maramar' dhwani bhi suni ja sakati he. isaki sahaayta se hi 'raktchaap' kii maap kii jaati he.
