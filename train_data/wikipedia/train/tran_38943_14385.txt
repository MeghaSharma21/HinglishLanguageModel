 vindhyaachal uttar pradesh ke mirzapur jile kaa ek dharmik drushtikon se prasiddh shahar he. yahan maan vindhyavasini devi kaa mandir he. markendeya puraana ke anusaar, maan vindhyavasini ne mahisasur kaa vadh karne ke liye avatar liya tha. yah nagar ganga ke kinare sthit he. bhartiya maanak samay kii rekha vindhyaachal ke railway stesan se hokar jaati he.
