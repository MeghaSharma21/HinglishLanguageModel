 asuri bhashaa, sami parivaar kii pracheen akkadi kii ek shakha he he.). akkadi kaa yah naam us akkad nagar se padaa jo isa purv 24veen sadi main prasiddh samrat sharrukin kii rajdhani tha. tabhi akkadi ko rajbhasha kaa pad mila. kalantar main akkadi, pradesh or kaal ke anusaar, asuri or babuli naamak janboliyon main viksit hokar bant gai. asuri dajala nadi kii uparali ghati main or babuli dajala-faraat ke sagarvarti doaab main boli jaati thi.

 swaabhaavik hi yahi kalkram asuri or babuli janboliyon kaa bhi apni vikasparampara main hoga. i.pus. 500 ke baad bhi asuri or babuli boli or likhi jaati raheen, par saadhaarant: tab un iraqi nadiyon ke kaanthe main praaia: sarvatra arami kaa prachaar ho gaya tha.
 akkadi athwa babuli asuri bhashaaon kii lipi gairsami sumeri keelaksharon se nikali he. dakshini mesopotamia main basanevaale in soomeriyon se tritiy sahasrabdi i.pus. main pehle babuliyon ne unki lipi sikhi, phir praaia: hajaar varsh baad uttar ke asuriyon athwa asuron ne. hajaaron vichaarsanketon ko dhvanit karanevaale 600 chih sumeri main the. in chihon main se kuch keval shabdamulak, kuch inke saath saath padanshmoolyak bhi the. babuliyon ne aarambh main is lipi ke keval padaansh chihnon kaa upyog kiya. babuliyon or asuron ne kalantar main, jab sumeri bhashaa kaa prayog mandiron main band ho gaya, sumeri chihnon or shabdon kii brihat suchiyaan bani leen. inase kai boliyon ko bada bal mila kyonki sumeri shabdon ke unke lipichihon ke saath babuli or asuri main bhi paryaay prastut ho gaye. parinaam yah hua ki asuri main, iske sami hone or sami bhashaaon se shabdaruddh hone ke baavjood, sumeri shabdon kii bahutayat ho gai or sumeri lipi main likhi jane ke kaaran iskaa uccharan bhi puraatan or asampratik ho gaya.
