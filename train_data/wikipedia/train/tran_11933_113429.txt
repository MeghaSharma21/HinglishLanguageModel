 lakshadweep bharat ke dakshin-pashchim main hind mahaasaagar main sthit ek bhartiya dweep-samooh he. isaki rajdhani kavaratti he.
 samast kendra shasit pradeshon main lakshadweep sab se chota he. lakshadweep dweep-samooh kii uttapatti prachinkaal main hue jwalamukheey visphot se nikle lava se hui he. yah bharat kii mukhyabhumi se lagbhag 300 kimi dur pashchim disha main arab sagar main avasthit he.
 lakshadweep dweep-samooh main kul 36 dweep he parantu keval 7 dwipon par janajivan he. deshi payartkon ko 6 dwipon par jane kii anumati he jabaki videshi payartkon ko keval 2 dwipon par jane kii anumati he.
 andarot par payartkon ko jane kii anumati nahin he.
