 electronics ke sandarbh main, kisi electronic paripath ke vibhinn binduon par purvanirdhaarit voltata sthapit karne ke liye aavashyak prabandh karnaa abhinati yaa bisingh kehalaata he. samuchit abhinati ke bina electronic paripath thik se kaam nahin karte, yaa yon kahen ki abhinath karne par electronic paripath behatar dhang se kaary karte hai. mote taur par bisingh kaa arth he- kisi electronic paripath main sahi maan vaala disi voltage lagaana tathaa upyukt maan vaale anya avyav lagaana taaki us paripath main maujuud bijetti, mossphate, yaa anya sakriy avyav sahi oparating avasthaa main rahete hue signal kaa thik dhang se prasanskaran kar saken.
