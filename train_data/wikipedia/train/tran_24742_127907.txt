 satya ke prayog, mahatma gandhi kii aatmakatha he. yah aatmakatha unhone gujarati main likhi thi.
 yahaan kuch uktiyaan he jo gandhi ji ne apni aatm katha - satya ke prayog -- main kahi hai. ye unke jivan darshan ko darshaati he.
 pichley tees saalon se jis cheez ko paane ke liye lalayit hum wo he swa kii pahchaan, bhagwaan se saakshaatkaar, or moksh. is lakshya ke paane ke liye hi main jivan vyateet karata hum. main jo kuch bhi bolta or likhata hum yaa phir raajnneeti main jo kuch bhi karata hu wo sab in lakshyo kii prapti ke liye hi he.
