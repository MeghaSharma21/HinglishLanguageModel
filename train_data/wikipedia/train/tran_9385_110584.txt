 rituparan ayodhya ke ek purakalin raja the. iske pita kaa naam sarvakaam tha. yah akshavidya main atyant nipun the. jue main rajya haar jane ke upanraat apane agyatvaaskaal main nal 'bahuk' naam se isi ke paas sarathi ke rup main rahaa tha. isane nal ko apni akshavidya di tathaa nal ne bhi apni ashvavidya ise di. 
 nalviyukti damayanti ko jab apane char parnaad dwaara pataa chalaa ki nal rituparan ke sarathi ke rup main rah rahaa he to usne rituparan kaa sandeshaa bheja, nal kaa kuch bhi pataa na lagane ke kaaran main apana doosraa swayamvar kal suryoday ke samay kar rahi hoon, at: aap samay rahete kundanipur padhaaren. nal ne apni ashvavidya ke bal se rituparan ko thik samay par kundanipur pahunchaa diya tathaa vahaan nal or damayanti kaa milan hua.
 boudhayan shrautrasutra ke anusaar rituparan bhangasina kaa putr tathaa shafaal ke raja the. vaayu, brahm tathaa harivansh ityadi puraanon main rituparan ko ayuthayuputr bataaya gaya he.
