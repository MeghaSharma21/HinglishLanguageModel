 rajim kumbh ko prati varsh hone vaale kumbh ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he, kuch varsh pehle yah ek meley kaa swarup tha yahan prativarsh magh poornima se mahashivaratri tak pandrah dinon kaa mela lagata he rajim teen nadiyon kaa sangam he isliye ise triveni sangam bhi kaha jaataa he, yah mukhya rup se teen nadiya bahati he, jinke naam kramash mahanadi, peri nadi tathaa sodhur nadi he, rajim teen nadiyon kaa sangam sthal he, sangam sthal par kuleshwar mahadev ji virajman he, rajim kumbh yahan hone vaale rajim meley kaa vartmaan swarup he, 2001 se rajim meley ko rajeev lochan mahotsav ke rup main manaaya jaataa tha, 2005 se ise kumbh ke rup main manaaya jaataa he, prativarsh rajim kumbh kaa aayojan chhattisgarh shashan samskruti vibhaag, evam sthaneey aayojan samiti ke sahyog se hota he,kumbh kii shuruyaat kalpavash se hoti he pakhwaade bhar pehle se shraddhalu panchakoshi yatra prarambh kar dete he panchakoshi yatra main shraddhalu pateshwar, phingeshwar, bramhaneshwar, kopeshwar tathaa champeshwar nath ke paidal bhraman kar darshan karte he tathaa dhuni ramaate he, 101 kimi kii yatra kaa samaapan hota he or magh poornima se kumbh kaa aagaaj hota he, rajim kumbh main vibhinn jagahon se hajaaro sadhu santo kaa aagaman hota he, prativarsh hajaaro ke sankhya main naga sadhu, sant aadi aate he, tathaa shahi snaan tathaa sant samagam main bhaag lete he, prativarsh hone vaale is mahakumbh main vibhinn rajyon se lakho kii sankhya main log aate he,or bhagwaan shri rajeev lochan, tathaa shri kuleshwar nath mahadev ji ke darshan karte he, or apana jivan dhanya maanate he, logo main manyata he kii bhanwaan jagannathpuri ji kii yatra tab tak puri nahi maani jaati jab tak bhagwaan shri rajeev lochan tathaa shri kuleshwar nath ke darshan nahin kar liye jate, rajim kumbh kaa anchal main apana ek vishesh mahatv he.
 rajim apane aap main ek vishesh mahatv rakhane vaala ek chota sa shahar he rajim gariyaaband jile kaa ek tehsil he pracheen samay se rajim apane puratatvo or pracheen sabhyataao ke liye prasidd he rajim mukhya rup se bhagwaan shri rajeev lochan ji ke mandir ke kaaran prasidd he. rajim kaa yah mandir aathvin shatabdi kaa he. yahan kuleshwar mahadev ji kaa bhi mandir he. jo sangam sthal par virajman he. rajim kumbh kaa mela prativarsh magh poornima se shivaratri tak chalata he. is dauraan prashasan dwaara vividh saanskrithik v dharmik aayojan aadi hote rahete he.
