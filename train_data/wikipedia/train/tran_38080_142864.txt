 khong myanmar ke arakaan pradesh kaa nivasee ek jansamaj he. inaki apni bhashaa or apni lipi he. is kaaran vidwaanon kii dharna he ki ati pracheen kaal se ek sushikshit samaaj rahaa he. ye log buddha dharmavalambee hai. vivaah prasang main ladke kaa pita ladki ke ghar jaakar apane ladke ke liye ladki maangata he. is sambandh ke sweekaar karne yaa na karne par gaanv ke chaar pratishthit janon ke saamne vichaar hota he or ek murge ke madhyam se shubhashubh kaa vichaar kar nirnay kiya jaataa he.
 is samaaj main stri purush donon kii ek si veshbhusha hoti he. inke bich is sambandh main ek anushruti prachlit he. kaha jaataa he ki pracheen kaal main arakaan ke kisi raja kii ek rupavati rani thi. raja usee ke prem main nimagn rahakar rajkaaj kii upeksha karne laga. jab rani ne yah dekha to vah swayan rajkaaj dekhne lagi. uski suvyavastha dekhakar praja use dev samaan maanane lagi. rani ne dekha ki log striyon ko purushon kii upeksha hey maanate hai. use yah baat acchhee na lagi. usne aadesh diya ki striyaan purushon ke samaan lungi dhaaran karengi or purush kesh badhaayenge or haath paanv main gudana gudwaayenge. praja ne rani kii is ajna kaa paalan kiya or aaj tak yah baat maanya hoti chali aa rahi he.
