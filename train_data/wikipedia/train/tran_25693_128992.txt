 'kiswah' yaa kaswah he ki kapade ko shaamil kiya gaya kaaba main makka, saudi arab ke shahar ] main sthit kaaba grih par udhaayaa gaya gilaaf he. is ko har varsh ju al hajja, ke din udhaayaa jaataa he, us din jis din haj yatri arphat ke maidan main jate hai. is shabd kaa arth "dibiya par dhanka gaaya gilaaf" he. is shabd kaa mul hebrew kaa shabd "kisui" he. 
 
 har saal, puraane kiswa ko hataa diya jaataa he, chote tukdon main cat jaataa he, or kuch vyaktiyon ko diya jaataa he, videshi muslim ganmaanya vyaktiyon or sanghathanon kaa daura kiya jaataa he. unamem se kuch haj ke smriti chinh ke rup main apana hissaa bechate hai pehle ke dinon main, umr bin al-khattab ise tukdon main kaat diya or un tirthayatriyon main vitarit kiya, jo unhen makka kii garmi se aashray ke rup main istemaal karte the.
 kiswah ko banane main saudi riyal kii vartmaan laagat 17,000,000 ho. is kaa kapadaa 658 meter 2 he or 670 & nbsp; se bana he resham kadhai main 15 & nbsp; kilo sone ke dhaage shaamil hai. isamen kapade ke 47 tukade hote hai or prayek tukdaa 14 meter lanbaa or 101 & nbsp; semi chaudaa he. kiswa kaaba ke chaaron ore lapeta hua he or ise apane tambe ke challe ke saath tay kiya gaya he. quran kii aaayaaton kaa chand likh kar taiyaar kii gai kadhai he. lekin aaj kal hastkala kii jagah computer dwaara sahaayta praapt karke is kaa dijin or kadhai ambrydari kii jaati he.
