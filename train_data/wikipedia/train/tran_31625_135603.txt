 ekatantr sarkaar kaa ek tantra he, jis main sarvocch shakti ek vyakti ke haathon main kendrit hoti he, jiske nirnay na to bahya vidhik ankushon ke adheen hote hai or na hi lokapriy niyantran kii niyamit kriyavidhiyon ke adheen hote hai . purna rajtantr or taanaashaahee ekatantr ke pramukh aitihaasik prakaar hai. bahut shuruuaati kaal main, "ekatantri" shabd shaasha kii ek anukool visheshta ke rup main gadha gaya tha, jiskaa kuch sambandh "hiton ke sangharshon kaa abhaav" kii avadharana se tha.
