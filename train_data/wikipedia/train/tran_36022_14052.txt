 vyera ludikova check kavi he. uskaa janm 16 june 1943 hua. vah aadhyatmik kavy likhti he. uski kavitaaon kaa bahut-si bhashaaon main anuwaad kiya gaya he. aksar kavitaaon kaa asperanto bhashaa main anuwaad kiya gaya he. kuch uski kavitaayen hindi main bhi uplabdh hai. jin pustakon main hindi main likhaa hua kavy maujuud he unke naam neeche diye jate hai.
 yah pustak san 2000 prastut hui. is kitaab main ek kavita he jiskaa 16 bhashaaon main anuwaad kiya gaya he. hindi main anuwaad chhedi laal or dagmar markova.
 yah kitaab san 2001 prastut hui. gagan naamak kavita kaa anuwaad swetislav kostitch se kiya gaya he.
  yah kitaab san 2002 prastut hui. is pustak main ek aadhyatmik lekh he jiskaa 37 bhashaaon main anuwaad kiya gaya he. hindi main anuwaad swetislav kostitch.
  yah kitaab san 2011 prastut hui. yah kavita sirf check bhashaa main likhi hui he lekin zdenyek vagner ne is kavita ke bhaag kaa hindi main anuwaad kiya or duusare anuwaadon ke saath use sunaaya. hindi main anuwaad anuvadak ke jaal prishth se uplabdh he.
  san 2018 prakashit thi. yah kaavyatmak kitaab he, aitihaasik nahin. rachaayitri is main pracheen sabhyataaon jaise pracheen misr, mesopotamia, pracheen china, tibet aadi ke bare main likhti hai or dikhaati hai ki in sabhyataaon kaa itihaas va samskruti vartmaan samay se kaise sambandhit he. kitaab main sindhu ghati sabhyataa ke bare main bhi kuch likhaa jaataa he. yah mahatvapurn or dilachasp bhi he kyonki aksar europea log hadappa tathaa mohan jodado bahut kam jaanate hai. kitaab ke aavaran prishth par rugveda ke chaar chand dikhaae jate hai.
