 chandragup, jise kabhi-kabhi chandrakup bhi kaha jaataa he, pakistan ke pashchimi balochistan praant ke tateey makraan kshetra main sthit ek garaamukhi he. 58 meter unchaa yah garaamukhi ek shuddh shanku kaa aakaar rakhata he. is kshetra main kai saare garaamukhi milte hai or chandragup inamen se ek he. yah hingol nadi ke mukh ke paas sthit he.
 is kshetra ke garaamukhi hinduon dwaara pavitra maane jate hai or inamen chandragup kaa vishesh sthaan he. paas ke hinglaj tirth sthal jate hue tirthayatri vishesh rup se chandragup par rukate hai. kuch hindu samudaayon main in garamukhiyon ko babhakhanath naamak devtaa kaa deraa mana jaataa he.
