 radhakanth dev anek bhashaaon ke vidwaan, hindu samskruti ke sangrakshan ke pakshdhar evam vichaarak the. unhone 'shabdakalpadrum' naamak sanskrit ke aadhunik mahashabdkosh kii rachana kii.
 shri radhakanth dev gopimohan dev ke putr the jo maharaja navakrishna dev ke dattak putr evam uttaradhikari the. 1830 main inhone 'dharmasabha' naamak sanstha chalaayi jo anya kaaryon ke alaava pashchimonmukhi vichaaron kaa virdohi tha. is sabha ne sati pratha ke samarthan main apana vichaar rakhaa jabaki raja rammohan raay aadi ne iske unmoolan ke pakshdhar the.
