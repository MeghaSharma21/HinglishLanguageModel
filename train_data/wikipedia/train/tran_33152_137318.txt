  chernobil parmaanu durghatana 26 april 1986 ko ukraine ke chernobil main hui ab tak kii sabase bhayanak parmaanu durghatana he. yah aapadaa shanivaar, 26 april 1986 ko ek pranali ke pareekshan ke dauraan cheranobil parmaanu sanyantr, ke chauthe hisse se shuru hui. vahaan achaanak vidyut utpaadan main vruddhi ho gai thi or jab use aapaatkaaleen sthiti ke kaaran band karne kii koshish kii gai to ulte vidyut ke utpaadan main atyadhik vruddhi ho gai. isse ek sanyantr tut gaya or aniyantrit nabhakeey visphot shrinkhalaa shuru ho gai. ye ghatnaayen sanyantr ke grafite main aag lagane kaa kaaran ho sakati hai. tej hawa or aag ke saath radiodharmee padaarth teji se aas-paas ke kshetron main fail gaye. isamen baari sankhya main jaan maal kii kshati hui or lagbhag 350,400 log visthapit kar alag sthanon par basaae gaye. is durghatana se sarvaadhik prabhavit belarus hua.
