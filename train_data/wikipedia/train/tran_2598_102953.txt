 kaihlur riyasat jise aajkal bilaspur naam se bhi janaa jaataa he bharat desh ke uttar me based ek pahari rajya himachal-pradesh kaa abhinn ang hote hub is rajya ke hryday ke rup me janaa jaataa he. bilaspur bhartiya rajya himachal pradesh kaa ek jila he. sutluj nadi ke dakshin purvi hisse main sthit bilaspur samudr tal se 670 meter kii oonchai par he. yah nagar dharmik paryaton main ruchi rakhane vaale logon ko kaaphi raas aata he. new bilaspur township ko desh kaa sabase pratham niojit hill toun ke rup main pratishthit kiya jaataa he. yahaan ke naina devi, siddh peeth baba balak nath ji va baba nahar singh bajiye kaa dhulra mandir, nikat or dur daraaj ke logon ke bich aakarshan kaa kendra bana rahata he. jile ke dakshin pashchim main bana bhankhada baandh bhi apni gravity ke liye puure vishwa main janaa jaataa he. bilaspur pracheen kile va gadhon ke liye bhi janaa jaataa he. yahaan aane vaale sailaaniyon kaa anubhav anya sthanon se ekadam alag hota he. kuch alag tarah ke paryaton ke shaukeen logon ko yah sthaan kaaphi pasand aata he. iske uttar main mandi or hamirpur jile hai, pashchim main oona or dakshin main solan jile kaa nalagarh kaa kshetra he.-
