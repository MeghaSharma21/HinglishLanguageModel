 andria kontuchi del monte sonsovino italy kaa murtikar or bhawan shilpi tha. arejjon ke sameep monthe sonsovinon main vah paidaa hua, isliye uskaa yahi naam prasiddh ho gaya. vah kalaguru poliula antonio kaa shishy tha. suprasiddh samakaleen italian murtikar or bhavanshilpi jocoposonsovino isi kaa shishy tha.
 pandrahavin shatabdi kii florence style par sarvapratham usne terracotta tathaa sangemarmar par monte sonsovino or florence ke girjagharon main anek dharmik or pracheen aakhyaanon tathaa bible ke katha-prasangon kaa chithran kiya. 'virgin kaa rajarohan', 'piyata', or 'antim bhojan' jaise chitrankanon ke atirikt usne anek prastar murtiyon kaa bhi nirmaan kiya. 1440 i. main samraat jaan dwitiya dwaara use portugal aane kaa aamantran mila. coyambra ke vishaal carch main ab bhi uski banaai kuch murtiyan milti hai.
 in praarambhik chitrankanon or murtishilp main donatello kaa vishesh prabhaav drashtavy he, kintu florentine bapatistry ke uttari dwaar par saint jon or isa kii katipay pratimaaon main rudhivaadee pracheen paddati bhi apanaai gai he. ek varsh tak vah voltera main sangemarmar par kaary karata rahaa or genoa carch main virgil or jon di baptist kii murtiyon kaa nirmaan kiya. usne kuch girjagharon main samadhiyaan ore smarak bhi banaae jinamein es meria hell popolo carch kii samadhi uski sarvaadhik prasiddh kriti he. 1512 i. main saint ani ke saath madonna or balak christ kii group murtiyan usne ankit kin. 1513 se 1528 tak lorato main rahaa jahaan santakasa ke bahirbhaag or kaksh stambhon par ubhara hua chitrankan or prastar pratimaaen gadhin. anek sahaayakon se use madad mili, phir bhi uski apni kaary pranali or kalatechnic niraali he.
