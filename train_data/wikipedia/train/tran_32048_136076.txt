 pandrahavin loksabhaa ke mantri mandal sahit 22 may, 2009 kii shaam raashtrapati bhawan ke ashok haul main yojit ek samaaroh main raashtrapati pratibha devisingh patil ne unhen pad or gopneeyata kii shapath dilaai. jawahar laal nehru ke baad manmohan singh pehle aise pradhanmantri hai, jo paanch saal kaa pehla kaarykaal puraa karne ke baad phir pradhanmantri bane hai. april-may 2009 main sampann hue loksabhaa chunaav 2009 main bhartiya rashtriya congress sabase badi party banakar ubhari thi or sanyukt pragatisheel gathbandhan ko 250 se zyaada siten mili thim. manmohan singh ke baad pranab mukherjee, sharad pavaar, e ke antni or pi chidambaram ne shapath lee. 
 pradhanmantri karyaalay ne pehle hi ek press vijyapti jari karke bataa diya tha ki pradhanmantri ke saath 19 mantriyon ko shapath grahan karai jaaegi. jin naamon kii mantrimandal main shaamil kiya gaya he unamem pranab mukherjee, pi chidambaram, eke antni, kapil sibbal, kamalnath, sharad pavaar, mamata banerjee ke naam pramukhataa se shaamil hai. uttar pradesh se congress ke 21 saansad chunakar aae hai magar unamem se kisi kaa bhi naam is suchi main nahin he. 
 shesh mantrimandal ke vistaar ke liye 26 may kii taarikh bataai gai he. pradhanmantri karyaalay se mili jaankaree ke anusaar agle vistaar main anya cabinet mantriyon ke saath hi rajya mantriyon or anya rajya mantriyon ko shapath dilaai jaaegi.
