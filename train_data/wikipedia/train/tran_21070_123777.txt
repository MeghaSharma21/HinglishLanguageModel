 duusare asian khel philippines kii rajdhani manila main 1 may se 9 may, 1954 ke bich yojit kiye gaye the. in khelon kaa aupchaarik udghaatan raashtrapati ramon magsese dwaara 1 may 1954 ko 4:02 aparaahn kiya gaya tha. udghaatan samaaroh ke avasar par manila ke malate sthit rizal memorial stadium main 20,000 log upasthit the. antaos ke anurodh par mashaal riley or kauldron prajvalan ko udghaatan samaaroh se baahar rakhaa gaya taaki olympic khelon kii parampara ko parirakshit rakhaa ja sake. mashaal vidhi ko 1958 ke asian khelon main punah laayaa gaya. haalaanki mezbaan dwaara antim khilaadi ke liye parade main pravesh karne ke liye ek vishesh prashasti patr kaa samaadhaan diya gaya. bataur mezbaan, philippines stadium main pravesh karne vaala antim desh tha. andres franko, philippines ke dhvaj vaahak the.
