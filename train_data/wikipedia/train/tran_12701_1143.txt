 net4 ek bhartiya company he, jo 29 navambar 1985 main nai dilli main bani thi.
 mangala chemicals ne 29 navambar 1985 main ek company mangala chemicals altidi naam se shuru kiya. baad main varsh 2001 main ise badalakar net 4 india kar diya. 2011 main pehali baar company ne .IN ko apane panjikaran ke liye diya. saath hi 7 nae data kendra or 15 sanchaar kendra kaa nirmaan karaayaa. yah pehla seva pradaata bana jise iesao 27001 mila.
 30 agast ko 2012-13 ke kar sambandhi prakaran main 9 karod rupee nahin lautaane ke kaaran iskar vibhaag ne jasjit singh ko giraftaar kar liya tha. unhen upabhoktaaon dwaara diye gaye seva kar ko bhi iskar vibhaag ko bharna tha.
