 jhang pakistan ke punjab praant kaa ek zila he. jhang zile kii rajdhani jhang shahar he. is zile kii chaar tahseelein hai - jhang, athara hazari, shorkot or ahmadpur siyaal. yah zila pakistani punjab praant ke madhya main sthit he. isaki seemayen uttar main sargodha zile se, uttar-purv main gujaranvaala zile se, purv main faisalabad or toba tech singh zilon se, dakshin main khaanewal or mujaffargarh zilon se, pashchim main laiya or bhakkar zilon se or pashchimottar main khushaab zile se lagatee hai.
 jhang zile main san 2006 main 33,53,000 logon kii aabaadi thi. iskaa kshetrafal qareeb 8,809 varg kimi he. jhang zile kaa ilaaka punjab or puure bhartiya upmahadweep ke sabase puraane aabaad hue kshetron main se ek samajha jaataa he. yahan punjabi bhashaa kii ek alag 'jhangochi' naamak upbhasha boli jaati he, jise kabhi-kabhi 'jhangavi' yaa 'rachanavi' bhi kehete hai. punjab ke do lok-nrity - jhummar or sammi - jhang se hi utpann hue hai. punjab kii prasiddh hir-raanjha premakatha kii hir bhi jhang zile kii hi rahane vaali thi. yahan sadiyon se siyaal samudaay kaa jor rahaa he.
 trimu jahaan jhelum or chanaab nadiyon kaa sangam hota he
 shorkot main sultan bahu kii mazaar
 jhang shahar main hir-raanjha kii mazaar
