 krishi himachal pradesh kaa pramukh vyavsaay he. yah rajya kii arthvyavastha main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaati he. yah 69 pratishat kaamkaaji aabaadi ko sidhaa rojgaar muhaiyaa karaati he. krishi or usase sambandhit kshetra se hone vaali aay pradesh ke kul gharelu utpaada kaa 22.1 pratishat he. kul bhugolic kshetra 55.673 lakh hectare main se 9.79 lakh hectare bhoomi ke swami 9.14 lakh kisaan hai. manzole or chote kisaano ke paas kul bhoomi kaa 86.4 pratishat bhaag he. rajya main krishi bhoomi keval 10.4 pratishat he. lagbhag 80 pratishat kshetra varsha-sinchit he or kisaan indra devtaa par nirbhar rahete hai.
 himachal pradesh main kul fasal utpaadan kaa 91 pratishat khadyann he. inamen se 85 pratishat dhaany fasalein hai tathaa paanch yaa saat pratishat dalhan fasale hai. kul utpaadan kaa saat pratishat subjiyan va masaale hai tathaa maatr teen pratishat bhaag main akhaadya fasalon kaa utpaadan hota he. is pradesh main gehun, makka, jau, va dhan khadyann fasalein pramukh rup se utpaadit hoti hai.
 rajya krishi vibhaag shimla jile main mashobara tathaa mandi jile main sundernagara main do prashikshan centre chalaata he. iske alaava, gaanv, khand tathaa jila str par krishak prashikshan shivi bhi yojit kiye jate he.
