 mangala band paak-adhikrut kashmir ke mirpur zile main jhelum nadi par sthit ek band he. yah vishwa kaa saatvaan sabase bada band mana jaataa he or iskaa naam nikatavarti mangala gaanv par padaa. iskaa nirmaan san 1961 main armbh or 1967 main purna hua. ise ek british vaastu company dwaara nirdhaarit ruparekha par 8 america companiyon ke ek gut ne nirmit kara. bharat is puure kshetra par apni samprabhutaa kaa daawa karata he or ise apane jammu or kashmir rajya kaa bhaag maanata he.
 mangala band ke nirmaan se aasapaas kaa lagbhag 65,000 acre kshetra ek nayaa jalashay ban gaya jise aaj mangala jhil ke naam se janaa jaataa he. is kritrim jhil ke banane se lagbhag 260 gaanv va qasbe pani main hameshaa ke liye dub gaye, jinamein zile kaa mukhyalay, purana mirpur shahar, bhi shaamil tha.
