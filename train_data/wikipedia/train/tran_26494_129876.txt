 henon janavaadee ganraajya china ke kendriya bhaag main sthit ek praant he. haan rajvansh ke jamaane main is kshetra main ek yuzou naamak rajya hua karata tha isliye chini bhavchitron main henon praant ko sankshipt rup main '豫' likhte hai. 'henon' naam do shabdon ko jodakar bana he: 'he' yani 'nadi' or 'naan' yani 'dakshin'. henon pili nadi ke dakshin main he, isliye iskaa naam yah padaa. henon ko chini sabhyataa kii janmabhumi mana jaataa he kyonki china kaa ek ati-pracheen rajvansh, shang rajvansh, yaheen kendrit tha.
 henon kaa kshetrafal 1,67,000 varg kimi he, yani bharat ke urisa rajya se zaraa zyaada. san 2010 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 9,40,23,567 thi, yani pashchim bengal se zaraa zyaada. henon ko aarthik rup se ek pichhadaa praant mana jaataa he. yahan krishi, koyale or aluminium kii khaane, udyog or paryaton aay ke mukhya strot hai. isaki rajdhani jhengzou shahar he. iske alaava kaayeefeng or luoyang is praant ke pramukh shahar he.
