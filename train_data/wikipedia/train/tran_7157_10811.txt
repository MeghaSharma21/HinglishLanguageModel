 bimbwad 20veen sadi kii angl-america kavita kaa ek andolan tha jisamen bimb arthath imageri kii parishuddhta tathaa spasht, tej bhashaa ko mahatvapurn mana jaataa he.
 bimbwad ko angreji kavita main purv-raafeleey andolan ke baad sabase prabhaavshaalee andolan ke rup main varnit kiya gaya he. ek kavy style ke rup main isane 20veen sadi kii shuruuaat main aadhuniktawaad kaa path-pradarshan kiya. angreji bhashaa ke sahitya main main ise pehla sangathit aadhuniktaavaadi saahitiyik andolan mana jaataa he. 
 bimbwad par ezara pound kaa kathan he, "aisi kavita jisamen chitrakala or shilpakala maanon samvaad ke liye ekatra hue hon."bimbwad ko kabhi-kabhi vikaas kii nirantar yaa satat avadhi ke bajaay rachanaatmak kshanon ke ek silsle ke rup main dekha jaataa he. rene topin ne tippanee kii he ki, bimbwad ko ek siddhant or ek kavy sampradaay ke rup na samaj kar kuch kaviyon, jo ek nishchit samay ke liye ek choti sankhya ke mahatvapurn siddhanton par ekamat the, kii ek association ke rup main samajhnaa adhik sateek he.
 hindi sahitya main bimb kaa naveen arth-pratipaadan ramachandra shukla kii aalochana dwaara hua or unhone arth-grahan par bimb-grahan ko variyata di.
