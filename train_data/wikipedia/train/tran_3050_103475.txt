 mirabal aalu bukhaaraa ek gadhe pile rang ke aalu buchaare kii nasl he. mana jaataa he ke yah ek junglee nasl thi jo anatolia se shuru hui jise samay ke saath-saath viksit kiya gaya.
 mirabal aalu bukhaaraa chote aakaar kaa or mulaayam twachaa vaala fal paidaa karata he. iskaa rang gadha pilaa hota he jisapar dhire-dhire halake bhure rang ke kuch til pad jate hai. yah apni mithaas or swaad ke liye mashhoor he.
