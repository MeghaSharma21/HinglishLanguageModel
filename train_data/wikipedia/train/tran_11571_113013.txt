 
 jaswant singh bharat ke ek varisht raajnneetijna hai. ve may 16, 1996 se june 1, 1996 ke dauraan atal bihari vaajapeyi kii sarkaar main vittamantri rah chuke hai. 5 disambar 1998 se 1 july 2002 ke dauraan ve vaajapeyi sarkaar main videsh mantri bane. phir saal 2002 main yashavanth sinha kii jagah ve ekbar phir vittamantri bane or is pad par may 2004 tak rahe. vittamantri ke rup main unhone bazaar-hitkaari sudhaaron ko badhaava diya.ve swayan ko udaaravaadi netaa maanate the. 15veen loksabhaa main ve darjeeling sansadeey kshetra se saansad chune gaye. ve rajasthan main barmer ke jasol gaanv ke nivasee he or 1960 ke dashak main ve bhartiya sena main adhikari rahe. pandrah saal kii umr main ve bhartiya sena main shaamil hue the. ve jodhpur ke purv maharaja gaj singh ke kareebi maane jate hai. jaswant singh mayo college or rashtriya raksha academy, kharkwasla ke chaatr rah chuke hai. 2001 main unhen "sarvshereshth saansad" kaa sammaan mila. 19 agast 2009 ko bharat vibhaajan par unki kitaab jinna-india, partishan, indapendence main nehru-patel kii aalochana or jinna kii prashansa ke liye unhen unke raajnneetik dal bhaajapa se nishkasit kar diya gaya or phir waapas liya gaya.
 2014 ke loksabhaa chunaav main rajasthan ke barmer loksabhaa sansadeey kshetra se bhaajapa dwaara tikat nahin die jane ke virodh main unhone nirdaleey ummidavaar ke taur par chunaav ladane kaa nirnay liya. unhen is baghawat ke liye chah saal ke liye party se nishkasit kiya gaya.
 jaswant singh bhartiya raajnneeti ke un thode se raajnneetigyon main se hai jinhen bharat ke raksha mantri, vittamantri or videshmantri banane kaa avasar mila. ve vaajapeyi sarkaar main bharat ke videsh mantri bane, baad main yashavanth sinha kii jagah unhen vittamantri banaaya gaya.
