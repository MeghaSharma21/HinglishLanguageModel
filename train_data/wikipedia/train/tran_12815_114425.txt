 zakaar prelepin kaa janm 1975 main hua. unhone labachevsaki neezegarodaski rajkeey vishvavidhyalay ke bhashaa sankaay se em.e. kiya he. zakaar ne apana jivan ek aam mazdoor ke rup main shuru kiya. phir ve chaukeedaar se lekar palledaar tak rahe. zakaar prelepin ne chechenia ke yuddh main bhi bhaag liya. ab ve patrkaar hai. zakaar prelepan lambey samay tak yevgeni lavalinski ke naam se internet patrikaon main lekh likhte rahe. parantu san 2004 main unhone rus kii kendriya or kshetreey saahitiyik patrikaon main bhi likhnaa shuru kar diya. ve rus ke anek yuva lekhak sammelnon main bhaag le chuke hai.
 zakaar prelepin kii pehali kitaab 'paralogia' chechen-yuddh ke bare main likhaa gaya ek upanyaas he. apni is pehali hi pustak se zakaar duniya bhar main prasiddh ho gaye. yah pustak rashtriya str par bestceller puraskaar ke liye ant tak sangharsh karti rahi. unki duusari pustak 'sanakya' kii tulana aalochak maxim gorki ke upanyaas 'maan' se karte hai. is pustak kaa bhi roosi aalochakon ne hardik swaagat kiya or yah bhi neshnal bestceller puraskaar kii short-list main rahi.
 zakaar prelepin kii teesri pustak he - 'grekh' . yah unki un kahaniyon kaa sangrah he jo samay-samay par patrikaon main prakashit hoti raheen. lekin ek saath in kahaniyon ke saamne aate hi unka nayaa arth nikaala jane laga or in nae sandarbhon main pathakon ne unhen gahari dilchaspee ke saath punah padhaa. zakaar prelepin 2006 main roosi bukar puraskaar or 2007 main 'yasnaaya palyana' puraskaar ke antim daavedaaron main shaamil rahe.
