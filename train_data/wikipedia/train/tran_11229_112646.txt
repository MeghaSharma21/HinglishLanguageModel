 champat raay jain 20veen sadi ke ek prabhaavshaalee jain lekhak or tulanaatmak dharm ke vidwaan the. unhone kai europi deshon kaa daura kiya or jain dharm or tulanaatmak dharm par bhaashan die. inhen bharat dharm mandal ne "vidya varidhi", kii sammanjanak oopaadhi pradaan kii.
 champat raay jain kaa janm 6 agast 1867 ko dilli main hua tha. 13 varsh kii aayu main unka vivaah tay kar diya gaya tha. 1982 main vah barrister kii padhai karne england chale gaye. 2 june, 1942 ko inkaa nidhan ho gaya tha. vah ek sakasham barrister-at-lau, prabhavi vaktaa, prabhaavshaalee lekhak or mahaan sudhaarwadi or darshanik the.

 champat raay jain ne apane samay kii teen mukhya bhashaaon main pustaken likhi: angreji, hindi, urdu.
 champat raay jain dwaara likhi ek pustak "da kii ouf nolej" pratham baar 1915 main prakashit hui thi. yah vidwaanon ke bich vyapak rup se prashansit hui thi.
