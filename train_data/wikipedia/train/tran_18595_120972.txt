 fiji hindi fiji main boli jane vaali pramukh bhashaa he. ise figian hindi yaa figian hindustani bhi kehete hai. yah fiji kii aadhikarik bhashaaon me se ek he. yah adhikanshtah bhartiya mul ke fiji logon dwaara boli jaati he. fiji hindi devanagari lipi or roman lipi donon main likhi jaati he.
 yah mukhya rup se avadhi or hindiki anya boliyon se vyutpann he, jisamen anya bhartiya bhashaaon kaa bhi samavesh he. isamen fiji or angreji se badi sankhya main shabd udhaar liye gaye hai. fiji hindi main badi sankhya main aise anuthe shabd bhi hai, jo fiji main rah rahe bharatiyon ke nae maahaul main dhalane ke liye jaruri the. fiji bharatiyon kii pehali peedhee, jisane is bhashaa ko bolchaal ke rup main apanaaya ise 'fiji baat' kehete the. bhashavidon ke haal ke adhyayan main is baat kii pushti hui he ki fiji hindi bharat main boli jane vaali hindi bhashaa par aadhaarit ek vishisht bhashaa he, jisamen fiji ke anukool vishesh vyakaran or shabdaawalee hai.
 kuch pramukh desh jahaan fiji hindi bolne vaale log hai-
 armbh main angrejon dwaara bharat ke uttar pradesh ke purvi jilon tathaa bihar se shramik fiji main laaye gayi the. ve log apane mul nivas sthaan kii hindi kii boli bolte the. ise neeche kii sarini main darshaya gaya he:
 dhyaan rakhane yogya he ki bhojpuri, jise lagbhag 35.4% uttar bhartiya pravasi bolte the, ko bihari kaha gaya he; isi tarah avadhi, jise lagbhag 32.9% log bolte the, ko purvi hindi kaha gaya he. sheeghra hi fiji main ek aisi bhashaa kaa janm hua jo in sabhi boliyon kii mail thi.
