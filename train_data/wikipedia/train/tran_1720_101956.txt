 pratiduterium, duterium ke nabhik pratikan he jisamen ek pratiprotone tathaa ek pratinutron hota he. pratiduterium kaa sarvapratham nirmaan 1965 main sarn sthit protone cincotron tathaa brookehaven rashtriya prayogshaala sthit alterneting gradient cincotron main kiya gaya. pojitron sahit ek purna parmaanu ko pratiduterium kaha jaataa he lekin 2013 tak iskaa nirmaan sambhav nahinho paaya. pratiduterium ke liye prastaavit pratik chihn D he jo angreji akshar D ke upar rekha lagane par praapt hota he.
