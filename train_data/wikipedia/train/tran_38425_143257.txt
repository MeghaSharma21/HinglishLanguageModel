 belingham dange sanyukt rajya america ke washington​ rajya main belingham shahar ke paas 4 sitambar 1907 ko hue dangon kaa naam he. isamen 400-500 shwetvarni aadimayon kii bheed ne bhartiya-mul ke gharon par hamalaa bol diya. hamalaavaron kaa dhyey bharatiyon ko sthaneey lakdi milon main kaam karne se rokane kaa tha. yah bhartiya adhiktar sikh the, haalanki america akhbaaron main unhen 'hindu' kaha gaya tha. unpar hamalaa karne waalon main se bahut se 'ashiatic oxkluthan lig​' ke sadsy the, jinka maqasad asian logon ko america main na basane dena tha.
 bheed ne bharatiyon ko gharon se nikaalkar sadakon par phenkaa, unhen peeta or unki moolyawaan sampattiyaan chin lee. police ne peediton kii sahaayta karne kii bajaae bharatiyon ko gherkar jabardastee nagarpalika grih main yah kahkar band kar diya ki yah unki apni suraksha ke liye he. 6 bharatiyon kaa aspataal main upachaar hua or 410 ko belingham jail main band kar diya gaya. daleel phir yahi thi ki yah unki apni suraksha ke liye he. hamalaa karne waalon main se kisi ko bhi nahin pakadaa gaya.
 dange-peediton main se kuch belingham chhodkar washington​ rajya ke hi yavaret​ ilaake chale gaye, lekin do maheene baad vahaan bhi unke viruddh hamale hue. california rajya main or canada ke vankuvar kshetra main bhi us kaal main aise dange hue.
