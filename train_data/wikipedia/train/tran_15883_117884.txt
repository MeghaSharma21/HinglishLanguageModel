 vinay kumaar sarkaar bharat ke samaajavigyaani, praadhyaapak evam rashtravaadi chintak the. unhone kolkata main anekon sansthaaon kii sthapana kii jinamein bengali samajashaastra santhaan, bengali asia academy, bengali daante sosayadi, tathaa bengali america samskruti santhaan pramukh hai.
 paitruk nivas bikrampur, dhaka. pita sudhanya kumaar sarkaar. 1901 i maldah jila skool se pratham sthaan praapt karke 1905 i main presidensi kalege main pravesh. vahaan se chaatravrutti ke saath bi e tathaa 1906 i main em e kiya. ve angreji or bangala ke alaava 6 anya bhashaayen jaanate the. vidhyaarthijivan main unhone ‘dan socity’ main yogadan diya. 1907-11 i tak bangeey jaateey shiksha-parishad main adhyaapan ke samay maldah main anek vidyalayon kii sthapana kii tathaa rashtriya shiksha-bishyak anekon granth rache. unhone kai europea lekhakon ke granthon kaa anuwaad bhi kiya. 1909 i main elahabaad ke panini karyaalay ke gaveshak rahe. 1914 i se 1925 i tak unhone vishwabhraman kiya evam vishwa ke vibhinn vishvavidhyalayon main adhyaapan kiya. 1926-1949 i tak kolkata vishvavidhyalay ke arthshaastra vibhaag ke adhyaaapak rahe. unhone 'dhanvigyaan parishad' kii sthapana kii pratishthata o ‘aarthik unnati’ naamak masik patrika kaa sampaadan kiya. unke dwaara rachit granthon kii sankhya 100 se adhik he. videsh main bharat kii swadhinata sangraami krantikariyon kii tarah-tarah se sahaayta kii. unke anuj dhiren sarkaar pravasi krantikariyon main anyatam the. unhone bahut se chaatro ko ucchshiksha praapt karne ke liye videsh bheja. swadhin bharat kii vaani ke prachaar ke liye america safar ke samay unka dehant ho gaya.
