 vishwa mohan bhatta yaa pandit vishwa mohan bhatta bhartiya sangeet kii pramukh hastiyon main se ek hai.
 inkaa janm 1950 main rajasthan ke jaipur main hua. ye pandit ravishankar ke shishyon main se ek hai. ye mohan veena ke janak hai.
 inhen 2002 main padmashri sammaan,gremi award va2012 rajasthan ratna se nawaja gaya. varsh 2017 main inhen bharat sarkaar dwaara padmabhushan se sammanit kiya gaya he.
