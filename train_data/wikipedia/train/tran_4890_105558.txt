 ahmad ali barqi aazmi : janm 25 disambar 1954 ko uttar pradesh ke azamgarh main huwa. in kii shaayari kaa sampradaay daag dehalavi se he. in kaa qalami naam "barqi" he.
 bachapan main vidya azamgarh main huvi. 1977 main dilli aaye. javaharlaal nehru vishvavidhyalay se 1996 main farsi bhasha main pi.ech.di. degree bhi lee.
 1984 aal india radio dilli main kaam karne lage. aal india radio main farsi vibhaag se jud gayi. farsi - urdu tarjumakar kaa kaam bhi kiya. urdu or farsi bhaashaon main praveen hone kaa inhen kaafii fida bhi huwa. radio anounser kaa ohda bhi khub nibhaayaa. 31 disambar 2014 ko ve padavi se ritaiyr huve.
 barqi aazmi bahubhasha kavi hai. inhon ne urdu, hindi athwa farsi main kavitaayen likhi. urdu kavita jagat main inako barq raftaar shaayar ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. in ke likhe lekh bharat ke anek patrikaon main prakashit ho chuke hai. in kii tippaniyan, saahiti vimarsha drushtikon saraahneey maani gayi he. vishesh karke duniya bhar ke urdu vaarta patrikayen or internet se inkaa rishata bahut niraala he. soshal media main urdu shaayar ke rup main bhi inaki acchhee prashansha huvi.
 barqi aazmi kii kavitaaon kaa sankalan "ruh-e-sukhan" ke naam se prakashit hua he, jiske liye unhen dilli urdu academy kii ore se award mila.
