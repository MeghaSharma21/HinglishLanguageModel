 ishvarsen aabhir samrajya ke sansthaapak the. puraanon main varnan ke anusaar ishvarsen va unke uttaradhikariyon ne dackhin kii vrihad simaaon par shasit rahe the. unhone 'rajan' kii oopaadhi grihan kii tathaa bhartiya kaal gananaa samvath bhi unke naam par shuru kii gayi thi. unke baad unki nau pidhia sattarudh rahi.
 maharashtra ke vibhinn bhaagon main aabhir shashan ke adheen kai raajaaon ne raaj kiya. aise hi ishvarsen dwaara sthapit ek rajvansh kaa ullekh ajanta kii gupha XVII ke ek abhilekh me he, shloke 10 me ashamak.
 ishvarsen se ek samvath kaa aarambh kiya jo kaaltar main kalgauri chedi samvath ke naam se jaani gayi.
 ishvarsen ke sikke saurashtra va dakshin raajapootaana main hi paye gaye hai jin par uske shashan kaal ke pratham va dwitiya varsh hi ankit hai.
 1200 BC main ahir raja ishvarsen ne satapura manudevi mandir kii sthapana kii jo ki ek hemandapanthi mandir he.
