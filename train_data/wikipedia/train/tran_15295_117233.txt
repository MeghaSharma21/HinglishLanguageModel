 baagbaan 2003 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. is film kaa nirdesan ravi chopra ne kiya he. iske nirmaataa bi aar chopra hai. is film main mukhya kirdaar ke rup main amitabh bachchan, hemamalini, salmaan khan, paresh rawal, mahima choudhary, remi sen or asraani ne kaary kiya he.
 raaj or pujas malhotra ek duusare se or apane bacchon se bahut pyaar karte rahete hai. lekin unke bachche zimmedaari se bhaagane ke chakkar main yah kah dete hai kii vah donon kuch kuch maheene alag alag ek ek bachche ke ghar main rahen. raaj or pujas apane bacchon ke liye alag alag ho jate hai. in dinon main raaj apane dost hemanth kii madad se apni kitaab likhata he. jab raaj or pujas mil jate hai to uske baad unki mulaaqaat alok se hoti he. jise un donon ne bachapan main god liya tha. vah baad main jab alok ke paas aa jate hai to uske baad hemanth patel raaj ko uske kitaab ke safalataa ke bare main bataata he. kuch hi samay main raaj bahut amir ho jaataa he. jab vah amir ho jaataa he tab uske bachche bhi uske paas aa jate hai. lekin vah unhen nahin sweekaarta he. par baad main un bacchon ko apni galati kaa ehsaas ho jaataa he.
 sabhi aadesh shrivastav, uttam singh dwaara sangitabaddh.
