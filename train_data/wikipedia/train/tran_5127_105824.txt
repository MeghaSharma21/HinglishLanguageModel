 vatsalya gram, paramashkti peeth dwaara sanchalit ek anathalay he. yah sadhvi ritambhra dwaara sthapit he. isamen anaath bacchon evam parityakt mahilaon ko awaas, bhojan, swasthya evam shiksha kaa prabandh kiya jaataa he. iskaa mul mantra yah he ki parityakt mahilaayein evam bachche ek duusare ke poorak hokar ek-duusare kii bhavnatmak aavashyaktaaon kii purti karen.
 vrindavan shahar se kuch baahar vatsalya gram ke naam se prasiddh yah sthaan apane aap main anek patakathaon kaa kendra ban saktaa he. is prakalp ke mukhyadwar ke nikat yashoda or baalkrishna kii ek pratimaa vatsalya ras ko saakaar rup pradaan karti he. vastav main is anuthe prakalp ke peeche kii soch anathalay kii vyavsaayikta or bhavaheenata ke sthaan par samaaj ke samaksh ek aisa model prastut karne kii abhilaashaa he jo bharat kii parivaar kii parampara ko sahej kar sanskaarit balak-balikaon kaa nirmaan kare na ki unamem hin bhaavana kaa bhav vyaapt kar unhen apni parampara or samskruti chhodane par vivash kare. 
 samaaj main didi maan ke naam se khyath sadhvi ritambhra ne is parisar main hi bhare-puure pariwaron kii kalpana saakaar kii he. ek adhed yaa bujurg mahila nani kehalaati hai, ek yuvati usee parivaar kaa ang hoti he jise mausi kaha jaataa he or usamen do shishu hote hai. yah parivaar ikai parisar main rahakar bhi puri tarah swytt hoti he. mausi or nani apana ghar chhodkar puraa samay is prakalp ko detii hai or vatsalya gram kaa yah parivaar hi unka parivaar hota he.
