 jagadish gupt kaa janm 03 agast 1924 i ko shahabaad, hardoi main hua. aadhunik hindi kavita main da jagadish gupt kaa mahatvapurn sthaan he. hindi nayi kavita ke pramukh kaviyon-jagadish gupt, ramswarup chaturvedi or vijayadevanrayan saahi main se ek hai. aapane elahabaad vishvavidhyalay se em.e. tathaa di.phil. kii oopaadhi praapt kii or 1950 main elahabaad vishvavidhyalay ke hindi vibhaag main praadhyaapak ke pad par niyukt ho gaye. 1987 main da gupt hindi vibhaag ke vibhaagadhyaksh pad se seva nivrutt hue. 1954 main "nai kavita" ke sampaadan ke saath armbh kii gai apni sahitya yatra main da gupt ne 35 granthon kii rachana kii. gupt ji kaa nidhan 26 may 2001 main hua.
 da gupt ko uttar pradesh ke bharat bharti tathaa madhya pradesh ke maithilisharan gupt puraskaar se sammanit kiya ja chukaa he.
