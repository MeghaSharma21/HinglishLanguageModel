 dayaal chandra soni varisht sarvodayi, gandhivadi evam buniyadi shiksha ke mul vichaarak the. inkaa maananaa tha ki shiksha kaa prarambh maatrubhaasha main hona chaiye. inhone sthaneey mewari boli main bhi kaafii kaam kiya tha or gita kaa bhi gambhir manthan kiya tha.
 gandhi ji kii vichaardhaara par aadhaarit buniyadi shiksha pradaan karne ke liye vidyabhavan ke sansthaapak mohansingh mehta dwaara sthapit vidyabhavan basic skool ke soni pratham pradhanadhyaapak niyukt hokar san 1941 se 1955 tak karyrat rahe. soni ne buniyadi shiksha ke kshaitra main aadharbhoot kaary kiya. prauji shiksha kshetra main inke ullekhneey kaary ko desh bhar main saraahaa gaya. uttar pradesh sarkaar ne inhen 1992 main madan mohan malviya puraskaar va bhartiya prauji shiksha sangh nai dili ne san 2002 main tagore puraskaar se sammanit kiya. inaki 30 se adhik pustaken va 300 se adhik lekh vibhinn patron main prakashit hui. 
 inaki smriti main udaipur main ‘shri dayaal chandra soni smriti trust’ sthapit he.
