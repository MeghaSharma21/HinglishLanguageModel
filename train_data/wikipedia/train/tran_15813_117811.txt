 ganit main atiparavalay ek aisa shankav hota he jisaki utkendrata ikai se adhik hoti he. ek anya paribhaasha ke anusaar atiparavalay aise binduon kaa bindupath he jinaki do nishchit binduon se duriyon kaa antar sadaiv achar rahata he. in nishchit binduon ko atiparavalay kii nabhiyaan kehete hai.
 ek atiparavalay, ek dwivimiya samatleey vakr he, jo iske jyamitiya gunon yaa samikarnon dwaara paribhaashit kiya jaataa he. ek atiparavalay main do bhaag hote hai, jinhen sanyugmi ghatak kaha jaataa he, jo ek-duusare kii darpan chhaviyaan hoti hai or donon anant lambey dhanush kii tarah hoti hai. atiparavalay, shanku pariched ke teen prakaaron main se ek he, jo ek samtal or ek dwishanku dwaara pratichhedan par nirmit hota he. yadi ek samtal, ek dwishanku ke donon hisson ko pratichchhed karata he lekin vah samtal shankuon ke sheersh se nahin gujarata he, to shankav ek atiparavalay hota he .
