 hindu dharm main vishnu puraana ke anusaar prithvi kaa varnan is prakaar he.yah varnan shriparashar ji ne shri maitrey rishi se kyn tha. unke anusaar iskaa varnan sau varshon main bhi nahin ho saktaa he. yah keval ati sankshep varnan he.
 yah prithvi saat dwipon main banty hui he. ve dweep es prakaar se hai:-
 ye saaton dweep chaaron ore se kramshah khaare pani, ichuras, madiraa, ghrit, dadhi, dugdh or mithe jal ke saat samudron se ghire hai.ye sabhi dweep ek ke baad ek duusare ko ghere hue bane hai or inhen ghere hue saaton samudr hai. jambudweep in sab ke madhya main sthit he.
 is dweep ke swaami veeravar dhyutimaan the. inke saat putron : kushal, mandag, ushn, pivar, andhkaarak, muni or dundubhi ke naam sangyanusaar hi iske saat bhaagon ke naam hai. ghi kaa sagar apane se duune vistaar vaale kronch dweep se chaaron ore se ghira hua he. yahaan bhi saat parvat, saat mukhya nadiyaan or saat hi varsh hai. 
 kronch, vaaman, andhkaarak, ghodi ke mukh samaan ratnamay swahini parvat, divaavruth, pundarikwan, mahaparvat dundubhi naamak saat parvat hai.
 gauri, kumudwati, sandhyaa, raatri, manijva, kshanti or pundarica naamak saat nadiyaan hai.
 kushal, mandag, ushn, pivar, andhkaarak, muni or dundubhi.yahaan pushkar, pushkal, dhanya or tishya naamak chaar varna hai. yah dweep apane hi baraabar ke dadhimand se bhare samudr se chaaron ore se ghira hua he.yah sagar apane se dugune vistaar vaale shaak dweep se ghira he.
