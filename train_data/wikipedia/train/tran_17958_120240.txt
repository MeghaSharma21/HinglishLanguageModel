 hariyaanvi bharat ke haryana praant main boli jane vaali bhashaa he.
 vaise to hariyaanvi main kai lahaze hai saath hi vibhinn kshetron main boliyon kii bhinnata he. lekin mote rup se isko do bhaagon main baanta ja saktaa he. ek uttar haryana main boli jane vaali tathaa duusari dakshin haryana main boli jane vaali.
 uttar haryana main boli jane vaali hariyaanvi thodaa saral hoti he tathaa hindi bhashi vyakti ise thodaa bahut samaj sakate hai. dakshin haryana main boli jane vaali boli ko theth hariyaanvi kaha jaataa he. yah kai baar uttar haryana waalon ko bhi samaj main nahin aati.
 iske atirikt vibhinn kshetron main hariyaanvi ke kai rup prachlit hai jaise bongar, raanghadi aadi.
 hariyani main a, a, i, i, u, uu, e, i, o, ou sahit das swar hai.
 hariyaanvi main 32 vyanjan hai.hariyaanvi main vyanjankram devanagari lipi main dhwanikram ke anusaar hota he. isamen vargeey vyanjan or avargeey vyanjan donon maujud hai.
