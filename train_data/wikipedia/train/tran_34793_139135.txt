 maalik mohammad rafique rajuna pakistan ke praant punjab ke vartmaan rajyapal hai, vah 10 may 2015 ko is pad kaa kalamandaan sambhala.unase pehle punjab ke rajyapal choudhary mohammad sarwar the jo ek prasiddh raajnneetijna the. aap 2 agast 2013 ko yah pad, pichley rajyapal makhdoom ahamed mahmud ke istife ke baad sambhala tha.29 janvari, 2015 ko unhone punjab, pakistan ke rajyapal ke pad se istipha de diya unke istife ke baad sangheey sarkaar ne maalik rafique rajuna ko yah pad diya. 
 desh rafique rajuna 1987 main nyaaypalika se jude hue varsh 1996 main apane nyaayalay lahore main multan baar association ke adhyaksh chune gaye.
 raajnneeti kii shuruyaat unhone pakistan muslim lig kiya or isi party se vah do baar 1997 or 2012 main sadsy pakistan kii senet chune gaye. farvari 2014 main sansadeey samiti chayan mukhya chunaav ayukta ke adhyaksh chune gaye. iske alaava rafique rajuna kai anya sansadeey samitiyon ke adhyaksh rahe hai. janvari 2015 main choudhary mohammad sarwar ne rajyapal punjab ke pad se istipha diya, unke baad may 2015 main desh rafique rajuna ne yah pad sambhala.
