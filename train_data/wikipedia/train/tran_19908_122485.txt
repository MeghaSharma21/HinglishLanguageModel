 parivarti urja cychlotron kendra ) bharat sarkaar ke parmaanu urja vibhaag kaa ek anusandhan evam vikaas kendra he. yahan par moolbhoot evam anuprayukt nabhikeey vigyaan main anusandhan hota he. yah bharat ke kolkata nagar main sthit he.
 is kendra main 224 semi cychlotron sthapit he jo bharat main apane tarah kaa pratham he. yah 1977 se hi karyrat he. isse vibhinn urja vaale proton, dutron, alpha kan evam anya baari oin ke kiran punj praapt kiye jate hai.
 yah kendra arnet ke liye tranjit node bhi he jo ki duusare sansthaanon se aane vaale electronic mail evam antarjaal kaa aavashyak sansaadan karata he.
