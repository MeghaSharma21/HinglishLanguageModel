 islamabad pakistan kii rajdhani he. bharat vibhaajan ke pashchaat pakistan ko ek rajdhani nagar kii aavashyaktaa thi. naa to lahore or na hi karachi jaise nagar is hetu sahi paae gaye antah ek nae nagar kii sthapana kaa nirnay liya gaya jo puri tarah se niojit ho. is kaary hetu francisi nagar niojak tathaa vaastukar lee corboojier kii seva lee gai. inhen mahoday ne bharat main chandigarh kii sthapana kii yojana banaai thi. is kaaran ye donon nagar dekhne main ek jaise lagate hai.
 2009 ke anumaan anusaar is nagar kii jansankhya 6,73,766 he.
 1958 tak karachi pakistan kii rajdhani rahaa. karachi kii bahut tezi se badhati jansankhya or arthshaastra ke kaaran rajdhani ko kisi duusare nagar main sthanantarit karne ki sochi gai. 1958 main tatkaaleen raashtrapati ayyub khan ne rawalpindi ke paas is sthaan kaa chayan kiya or yahaan is nagar ke nirmaan karne kaa aadesh diya. asthai taur par rajdhani ko rawalpindi sthanantarit kiya gaya or 1960 main islamabad main vikaas kaary armbh hua. 1968 main rajdhani ko islamabad sthanantarit kar diya gaya.
 islamabad main islamabad rajdhani kshetra main marglah hills ke pair main pojowar pathaar ke kinare par sthit he. isaki oonchai 507 meter he. yah bahut rawalpindi ke kareeb he.
 islamabad kii 70% jansankhya punjabi bolti he. hindi/urdu, pashto, sindhi or angreji ityadi bhi yahan boli jaati he.
 islamabad parkon kaa nagar he. kuch mukhya park hai: shakarpadiaan, daaman koh, faatmaa jinah park, jane pashane park he.
