 buddha dharm ke dharmagranth keval pali main hi nahin hai balki bahut badi sankhya main sanskrit main bhi hai. kuch granth pali or sanskrit ke mishrit bhashaa main bhi rache gayi hai. buddhavachanon ke alaava buddha vidwaanon ne darshan, nyaay, aadi ke granth sanskrit main likhe hai. bahut se granth kaal ke gaal main samaa gayi kintu uske baavjood sanskrit main rachit buddha garnth vipul maatra main milte hai.
