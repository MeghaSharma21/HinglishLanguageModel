 acharya hemchandra ne samast vyakaran wandmayaka anushilan kar shabdanushasan evam anya vyakaran granthoki rachana kii. purvavato aacharyonke granthoka samyak adhyayan kar sarvandag paripurna upyogi evam saral vyakaranki rachana kar sanskrit or praakrit donon hi bhashaonko purnataya anushaasit kiya he.
 hemchandrane 'sidwahem' naamak nutan panchang vyakaran taiyaar kiya. is vyakaran granthka shwetantra sushobhit do chamrake saath chal samaaroh hathi par nikaala gaya. 300 lekhakone 300 pratiyaan 'shabdanushasan' kii likhakar bhinn-bhinn dharmadhyakshonko bhet dene ke atirikt desh-videsh, iran, ceylone, nepal bheji gayi. 20 pratiyaan kashmirake saraswati bhandarmen pahunchi. gyaanapanchami ke din pariksha lee jaati thi.
 acharya hemchandra sanskritke antim mahavaiyaakaran the. apbhransh sahityaki pracheen samrudvi ke sambadhamen vidwaan un padhonke stotraki khojamen lag gayi. 18000 shloke pramaan brihadavritti par bhashya katichid durgaapadakhya vyakhya likhi gayi. isabhashyaki hast likhit prati berlin main he.
