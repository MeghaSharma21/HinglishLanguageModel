 uttari sagar maarg ek jahajrani maarg he jo andh mahaasaagar se shuru hokar rus ke sudur purvi kshetra tak jaataa he. is maarg main prashanth mahaasaagar, rus kaa arctic samudri tat jisamen berint sagar shaamil he se siberia se guzarakar yah rus ke sudur purv main samaapt hota he. maarg kaa arctic mahaasaagar vaala bhaag varsh main keval do maheene ke liye hi barf se mukt hota he. 20 veen shatabdi kii shuruyaat main ise uttar purv jalmaarg ke naam se janaa jaataa tha or aaj bhi kabhi kabhi ise isi naam se sambodhit kiya jaataa he.
