 rosh hashana yahudee dharm main vishwaas karne vaale logon kaa ek pramukh tyohaar he.
 yah yahudee navvarsh ko chihnit karata he. isaki utpatti bible se judi he : ek pavitra avasar jo joardaar dhamaakon ke saath manaaya jaataa he. rosh hashana shabd rabinical he, jiskaa zikr saal kii shuruyaat ke rup main aata he. is tyohaar ke prasang hai : praayshchit, divine faisle ke din kii taiyaari or laabhadaayak saal ke liye prarthana. yah do divasiy tyohaar yahudee calendar main 1-2 tishari ko manaaya jaataa he, jo angreji calendar ke mutabik aamtaur par sitambar main padataa he. sabhi yahudee tyoharon kii tarah yah bhi pichhali shaam se shuru ho jaataa he. rosh hashana kii mukhya rasmon main lambey samay tak chalane vaali ek sinegog sarvice ke bich shobar bajaanaa or nae saal kii khushi main gharon main tarah-tarah ke pakwaan bananaa shaamil he. kai myanon main israel nayaa saal rosh hashana se shuru hota he. sarkari prapatron, akhabaaron or prasaranon main sabase pehle yahudee taarikh kaa zikr hota he.
