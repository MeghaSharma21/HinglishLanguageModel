 
 himalayan van chidiya chidiya kii ek prajati he, jise 2016 main khoja gaya tha. iskaa naam bharat ke pakshi vigyaani saalim ali ke naam par "juthera salikali" rakhaa gaya. vanshavali adhyayan ke aadhaar par yah pataa chalata he ki is prajati ki aabaadi kam se kam 3 lakh varsh purv apane purvaj se alag ho gai thi. inkaa nivas sthaan himalay ke wanon main he.
 iskaa naam bharat ke prasiddh pakshi vigyaani saalim ali ke dwaara is kshetra main kiye gaye kaaryon ko dekhte hue, unke sammaan hetu unke hi naam par "juthera salikali" rakhaa gaya.
 bharat, china, sweden, rus or america ke vaigyanikon dwaara 2009 se iske khoj kaa kaary shuru hua tha. lekin 2016 main is nai prajati kaa pataa chalaa. iskaa pataa purvottar bharat main himalay ke wanon main chalaa. is nai prajati ke hone kaa pataa tab chalaa jab ek jaisi dikhne vaali do prajati main swar kaa antar mila. isamen se ek prajati kaa swar atyadhik madhur tha, jabaki duusari prajati ke pakshi kaa swar kaaphi karkashta yukt tha.
 michigan vishvavidhyalay se pamela rasmussen ne kaha ki jab ham 2009 main is par shodh kar rahe the, to hamen yah ek hi prajati lag rahaa tha. baad main hamen pataa chalaa ki yah donon alag alag prajati he.
 in donon ke dhwani main antar milane ke baad bhi isaki pushti hetu kai tarah ke jaanch kiye gaye, jisamen inke aakaar, rahane khaane ke tareeke aadi shaamil he. inke diene or anya prakaar ke jaanch karne par is baat kaa pataa chalaa ki yah ek nai prajati he. bharat ke aazaadee ke baad 1947 se abtak pakshi kii kul 4 nai prajati kaa pataa chal chukaa he. saath hi china main is tarah kii ek prajati kaa pataa chalaa he. lekin ab tak us prajati kaa puraa adhyayan nahin kiya gaya he ki vah ek is prajati se bhinn koi nai prajati he yaa yahi prajati he. is khoj main kai deshon ke vaigyanikon ne apana yogadan diya. jisamen chaao chaao, jingju, shashaank dalvi, tiyanglong kai, yuyan guyaan, ruiying, mikhail kalyakin, fumin lei or urabaan olssan shaamil he.
