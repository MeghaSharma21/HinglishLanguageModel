 saura kalank, surya ke prakaash mandal kii asthaayee ghatnaayen hai. jab surya ke kisi bhaag kaa taap anya bhaagon kii tulana main kam ho jaataa he to dhabbe ke rup main dikhataa he, ise saura kalank kehete hai. is dhabbe kaa jeevankaal kuch ghante se lekar kuch saptaah tak hota he. kai dinon tak saura kalank bane rahane ke pashchaat radio sanchaar main baadha aati he.
