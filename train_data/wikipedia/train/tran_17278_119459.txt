 18veen sadi main kai desh jaise germany, italy tathaa switzraland aadi us rup main nahin the jaisaa ki aaj ham inhen dekhte hai. ye chote-chote rajyon main vibhajit the jinka apana swatantra shaasha tha. 1789 i kii francisi kranti se pehle france ek aisa rajya tha jinke sampoorn bhubhag par ek nirkunsh raja kaa shashan tha. nepolian kii samhita - ise 1804 main lagu kiya gaya. isane janm par adhrith visheshadhikaron ko samaapt kar diya isne na keval nyaay ke samaksh samanta sthapit kii balki sampatti ke adhikaar ko bhi surakshit kiya. 
 19veen shatabdi main europea mahadweep main rashtrawaad kii ek lahar chali jisane europea deshon kaa kayakalp kar diya. germany, italy, romania aadi navnirmit desh kai kshetreey rajyon ko milakar bane jinaki rashtriya pahchaan 'samaan' thi. yunaan, poland, balgaria aadi swatantra hokar raashtra ban gayi. europe ke raajnneetik vikaas main rashtrawaad kii pramukh bhoomika
