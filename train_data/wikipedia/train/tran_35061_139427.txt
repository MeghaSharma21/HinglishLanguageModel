 aid-di-camp ek fransisi shabd he jiskaa arth he yubhumi main sahaayak. saamanytah yah ek padavi he jo sena ke un varishthatam adhikariyon ko di jaati he jo kisi rashtradhyaksh, senadhyaksh, kisi ucch sarkari adhikari athwa rajasi parivaar ke sadsy ke liye vyaktigat sachiv, sahaayak yaa salaahkaar aadi kii bhoomika nibhaate hai. ese adhikari apane naam ke saath sammaanon kii fehrist main edisi likhte hai.
 vibhinn deshon main rashtradhyakshon ke liye edisi kii paramparaayen, vyavastha, pranaliyaan aadi alag alag tareeke se nirdhaarit hoti hai. bharat main major general va isse upar rank ke adhikari saamanytah edisi rakhte hai. saamanytah bharat ke senadhyaksh teen va bharat ke raashtrapati ke paanch edisi hote hai. bhartiya rajyon ke rajyapal saamanytah do edisi rakhte hai jinamein se ek sena se tathaa duusare police seva se hote hai, sivaay jammu kashmir ke rajyapal ke jinke donon edisi sena se hi hote hai.
