 gharelu hinsa sahavaas athwa vivaah jaise bandhanon ke baad gharelu str par ek saathi kaa anya saathi ke saath marpet athwa durvyavhaar ko prakat karne vaala shabd he. antarang saathi athwa jivan saathi ke saath durvyavhaar bhi gharelu hinsa kii shrenee main aata he. gharelu hinsa vipareet lingi athwa samalangik sambandhon main bhi ho sakati he. gharelu hinsa ke shaareerik, bhaavnaatmak, maukhik, aarthik or yon shoshan sahit vibhinn rup ho sakate hai, jisamen dhoortta se lekar vivaah pashchaat balaat yon sambandh or hinsak shaareerik shoshan bhi shaamil hai evam iske parinamswaroop maansik athwa shaareerik virupan athwa maut bhi sambhav he.
 vaishvik rup se saamanytah patni athwa mahila saathi gharelu hinsa kii shikaar adhik hoti he haalanki iskaa shikaar purush saathi athwa donon ek duusare ke khilaaf gharelu hinsa kaa shikaar ho sakate hai athwa doshi aatmaraksha yaa pratishodh ke kaaran bhi gharelu hinsa kaa shikaar ho saktaa he. jabaki viksit vishwa main gharelu hinsa kii shikaar mahilaon ko praadhikariyon ke paas khule aam shikaayat karne ke liye protsaahit kiya jaataa he, yah tark diya jaataa he ki purushon ke saath hone vaali gharelu hinsa ko prativedit nahin kiya jaataa kyonki isse unhen saamaajik rup se kaayar or purushatvheen maan jaataa he.
