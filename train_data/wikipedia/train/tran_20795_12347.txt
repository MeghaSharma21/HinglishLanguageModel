 england kii mahila cricket team oktober 2016 main west indies daure ke liye nirdhaarit he. tour shrinkhalaa 2014-16 isisi mahila championships kaa hissaa hone ke antim teen maichon ke saath paanch vanade kii ek shrinkhalaa ke hote hai. england kii mahila prashikshan kii taiyaari tufaan mathew kii vajah se baadhit kar rahe the. tufaan ke khatre ke baavjood, shrinkhalaa aage jane ke liye yojana ke rup main nirdhaarit he. england mahilaon 5 maichon kii siries 3-2 se jeet lee.
