 1996 pepsy sharajah cap ek trikoniya vanade cricket pratiyogita thi, jo sharajah, sanyukt arab emirate main 12 se 19 april 1996 main hui thi. isamen dakshin africa, pakistan or bharat kii rashtriya cricket tiimon ko shaamil kiya gaya. iskaa aadhikarik praayojak pepsy tha. toornament dakshin africa ne jeeta, jisane final main bharat ko haraaya.
 dakshin africa ne apane sabhi chaar round-robin match jite. bharat or pakistan ne prayek par ek jeet kaa daawa kiya. dakshin africa ke khilaaf sarvshereshth ran-rate par aadhaarit final ke liye kwalifai karne ke liye prayek bhartiya team ne 2 ankon kaa muqabla kiya.
