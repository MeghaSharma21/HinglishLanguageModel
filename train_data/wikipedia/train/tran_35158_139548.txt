 peelaapan kshan shabd ek latin shabd se liya gaya he, . pelnes kaa arth he, pilaa padana. tathaa mortis kaa arth he "maut kaa".
 peelaapan kshan maut kaa sabase pehla kshan hota he. matlab marane ke baad sharir sabase pehle pilaa padana shuru hota he. uske baad anya kshan aate hai.
 maut ke 15-25 minuton ke antargat hi sharir pilaa padana shuru ho jaataa he. peelaapan itani jaldi at he ki yah maut kaa samay pataa lagane main bilkul bhi sahaayak nahin banataa.
 sharir main keshika sanchalan band ho jane kii vajah se yah stithi aati he ki sharir pilaa pad jaataa he. phir gurutvkarshna bal ke kaaran khun sharir ke nichle hisson main sthir hona shuru ho jaataa he, is kshan ko livore kaha jaataa he. 
 ek jivit vyakti bhi pilaa dikh saktaa he. aisa tab hota he jab twachaa ki sataha se rakt nikalne lagata he. jaise ki koi gaharaa karant lagane kii vajah se ityadi. dil kaa daura padane ki vajah se bhi cheharaa feekaa nazar aa saktaa he. is tarah ke maamle main vakti ke hont nile pads jate hai. vitamin di ki kami ke kaaran bhi sharir pilaa pad jaataa he, is tarah ke maamle ham aamtaur par un vyaktiyon main dekh sakate hai jo ghar se bahaar dhup main nahin jate. atah dhup na milane ki vajah se uname vitamin di ki kami ho jaati he or sharir pilaa pad jaataa he.
