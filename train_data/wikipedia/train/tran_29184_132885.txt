 bushara rahamaan  ek pakistani lekhak he. usne kai pustaken likhi he use 2007 main apane saahitiyik kaam ke liye raashtrapati puraskaar, sitara-e-imtiyaaj bhi praapt hua he.
 usne 1983 main prantiy vidhansabha se apana raajnneetik karier shuru kiya or teen baar punjab vidhansabha sadsy ke rup main chuni gai. vah vartmaan main pakistan muslim lig se pakistan neshnal assembly kii sadsy he.
