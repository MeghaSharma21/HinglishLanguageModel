 sos ek pakwaan he jo drav-avasthaa main yaa ardh-drav avasthaa main hoti he tathaa duusare bhojan ke saath yaa use sajaane ke liye prayog kii jaati he. sos praaia: akele kabhi bhi nahin grahan kii jaati he. sos ke prayog se kisi duusare vyanjan kaa flavor, nami badali jaati he yaa iske prayog se bhojan dekhne main sundar/aakarshak dikhne lagata he.
 sos francisi bhashaa kaa shabd he jiskaa arth he - namak milaayaa hua yaa namakeen.
