 avadh kshetra kii apni ek alag khaas nawabi khanpan style he. isamen vibhinn tarah kii biryaniyaan, kebab, korma, nahari kulche, shirmaal, zarda, rumali roti or varki parantha or rotiyaan aadi hai, jinamein kakori kebab, galaavati kebab, patili kebab, boti kebab, ghutwaan kebab or shaami kebab pramukh hai. shahar main bahut si jagah ye vyanjan milenge. ye sabhi tarah ke evam sabhi bajet ke honge. jahaan ek ore 1805 main sthapit ram aasare halwai kii makkhan malai evam malai-gilori prasiddh he, vahiin akabari get par milane vaale haji muraad ali ke tunde ke kebab bhi kam mashhoor nahin hai. iske alaava anya nawabi pakwaano jaise 'dampukht', lachchhedaar pyaaj or hari chatnee ke saath parose gay sikh-kebab or rumali roti kaa bhi jawaab nahin he. lakhanu kii chaat desh kii behtareen chaat main se ek he. or khaane ke ant main vishwa-prasiddh lakhanu ke paan jinka koi sani nahin he.
