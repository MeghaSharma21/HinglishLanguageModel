   hind irani  hind arya  mahal
 mahl yaa mahal yaa divehi ek hind-arya bhashaa he jo bhartiya upmahadweep ke dakshin-pashchimi hisse main sthit maldive dweepasmooh main or bharat ke lakshadweep sangheey-kshetra ke minicoy dweep par boli jaati he. mahl or srilanka main boli jane vaali sinhalai bhashaa ek duusare ke kaafii qareeb hai or inhen hind-arya bhashaa-parivaar ke adheen kabhi-kabhi 'dweepiya hind-arya' naamak shakha main daal diya jaataa he.
