 23 november gregory calendar ke anusaar varsh kaa 327wa din he. saal main abhi or 38 din baaki he.
 23 navambar ko bilaspur jile ke bilhaa nagar main janmen ratan jaisavani vartmaan main jaanjgir-champa jile main dainik chhattisgarh akhbaar ke beuro chif hai. haive channel, deshbandhu, haribhumi, dainik bhaskar, sadhana news ko apni sevaayen de chuke shri jaisavani patrkaar hone ke saath saath swatantra lekhak va vyangyakaar bhi hai. jinaki rachanaaen haribhumi, swabhimaan times, nai duniya sahit kai news portal main bhi prakashit hoti rahati hai. nishpaksh vichaardhaara vaale shri jaisavani kii khabar express va vyangy baan naamak do blog bhi hai, jis par ve apane vichaaron ko vyakt karte hai.
