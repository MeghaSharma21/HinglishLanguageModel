 misr ke 27 bade prashasnic kshetreey vibhaag hai jinhen muhafazah kehete hai, yani kisi rajyapal kii hifaazat yaa nigaraanee main rakhaa gaya kshetra. inkaa darja lagbhag bharat ke praanton jaisaa he. misr ke muhaafazaat yaa to shahari hote hai yaa phir shahari-dehati kaa mishran hote hai.
 shahari-dehati mishrit muhaafazaat ko 'marakaj' naam ke kshetron main baanta jaataa he. yah vahi shabd he jiskaa matlab hindi-urdu main 'kendra' hota he lekin misr kii arabi upbhasha main iskaa arth 'gaanvon kaa samooh' he. purna rup se shahari muhaafazaat main gaanv nahin hote isliye unhen marakaz main bhi nahin baanta jaataa.
