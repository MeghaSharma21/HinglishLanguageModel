 cypen uttari maariana dwipon kaa sabase bada dweep he. uttari maariana dweep prashanth mahaasaagar main sthit sanyukt rajya america kaa ek kaamanwealth hai jisamen 15 dweep aate hai or jisamen guam ke alaava maariana dweepasmooh ke sabhi anya dweep shaamil hai.
 cypen, guam ke baad, puure mariyana dweepasmooh kaa doosraa sabase bada dweep he. yah tiniyan se cypen jalsandhi ke paar 9.3 kimi kii samudri duuri par he. cypen kaa prashasan dweep par sthit capital hill naamak gaanv se chalata he, jo puure uttari maariana dwipon kaa prashasnic kendra bhi he. kyonki puraa cypen keval ek nagarpalika main gathit he isliye aam bolchaal main kabhi-kabhi puure cypen ko hi uttari maariana dwipon kii rajdhani kah diya jaataa he.
