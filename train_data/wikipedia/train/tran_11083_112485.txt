 indore samachar hindi kaa ek samachar patr he. 
 22 march 1916 ko indore se `indore samachar' kaa prakaashan purushottam vijay ke sampaadan main armbh hua. do prishthon se armbh kiya gaya yah patr vartmaan main chah bade prishthon vaale samachar-patr ke rup main vidyaman he. lagbhag ek yaa dedh varsh baad shri lakshmichand nanoria ne `indore samachar' ke sampaadan kaa bhaar sambhaala. lagbhag 1960 tak `indore samachar' madhyapradesh kaa lokapriy samachar-patr rahaa. purushottam vijay kii fadakati hui style, bewak sampadakiyon tathaa khojpurn samaacharon ke kaaran `indore samachar' kii dhak madhya pradesh ke baahar bhi jam gai. 1 june 1971 se suresh seth is patr kaa sanchaalan kar rahe hai. vartmaan main sohan meraha is patr ke pradhan sampadak hai.
