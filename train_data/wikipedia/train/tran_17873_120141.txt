 vidhwaa punarvivaah adhinium, 1856 main british bharat main brahman, rajputon, baniyaa or kysth jaise kuch anya jatiyon ke bich mukhya rup se vidhwaapan abhyaas par roak lagane hetu paarit kiya gaya tha| yah kanoon bachche or vidhwaaon ke liye ek raahat ke rup main taiyaar kiya gaya tha jiske pati kii samay se pehle mrityu ho gai ho|
 
 hindu vidhwaa punarvivaah adhinium 1856 hindu jati main jo purv kii vivaah parampara thi usamen vivaah adhinium 1856 ke adhinium ke dwaara sabhi adchane dwesh aadi ko is adhinium ke tahat samaapt kar diya gaya or isamen naveen paddatiyon ko janm diya gaya us samay bharat british adheen tha isliye bharat ko british bharat kaha jaataa tha yah sudhaar hindu vivaah ke vidhwaaon ke liye sabase bada sudhaar tha puraatan samay main kisi aurat ke pati kii mrityu ho jane par use uski chita ke saath jalanaa hota tha yaa sar mundwanaa hota tha aadi aisi jatil prakriyaayein thi lekin is adhinium ke tahat kuch pramukh sudhaar laae gaye he ki jin ke mukhya bindu nimnlikhit he1. yadi kisi stri ke pati kii mrityu ho jaati he to vah punarvivaah kar sakati he2. is vivaah main uske sage sambandhi arthath mata pita bhai dada naanaa nani aadi sambandhiyon ke dwaara baat karke duusare vivaah ko manjuuri di ja sakati he yatha punarvivaah karne vaali mahila alpavayask he3. vivaah main sahmati kaa hona atyant aavashyak he4. jis ghar ki vah pehle bahu thi arthath uske mrityu vaale pati kaa ghar us par uskaa koi sampatti ke taur par adhikaar nahin hoga jahaan vah punarvivaah ke baad jaaegi vahaan uskaa adhikaar mana jayega
 tathaa nishchit rup se kaha ja saktaa he ki hindu jati ki jo puraatan samay ke anusaar jo vivaah kii parampara thi vah kai rup se aaj kii apeksha bikhri hui thi jisase ki us main kai bandhit niyam the jisamen ek pati kii mrityu hone ke pashchaat uski patni ko uski chita par jinda jalanaa yaa bal mundava dena yaa duusari shaadi naa karnaa aadi kai sari paramparaayen sammilit thi jiske anusaar 1856 isvi main british india hindu vidhwaa punarvivaah adhinium banaaya gaya jisamen kai sudhaar laae gaye arthath purv kii apeksha vartmaan vivaah saral evam sukhadai he|
