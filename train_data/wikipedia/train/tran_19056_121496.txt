 saamaajik vigyaan anusandhan ke uddeshyon-
 saamaajik vigyaan anusandhan ke uddeshy se ek or nai vaigyanik upkaran, avadharanaon or siddhanton, jo vishwasaneey or vaid maanav vyavhaar or saamaajik jivan ke adhyayan kii suvidha hogi ko viksit karne ke liye he.

 vyaktigat purvagrahon or purvaagrah-
 vyaktigat purvagrahon" keval aankadon par ek vikrut prabhaav he, lekin ve itani nihit he, isliye sookshm hote hai, hamen unhen khud ko he vichaar karne ke liye itani gaharaai se dhyaan diya he ki yah mushkil he, yaa jab ve hamaare dhyaan main kaha jaataa he, bhi behad ghaatak hai nahin ho saktaa he, unhen yuktisangat banane ke bajaay, unhen nishpakshta kii jaanch se bachane ke liye.
