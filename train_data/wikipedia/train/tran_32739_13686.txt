 elkhart indiana ke elkhart county kaa ek shahar he. yah elkhart county kii county seat nahin he; goshen shahar elkhart couti kii couti seat he. jansankhya 2010 kii janaganana main 50,949 thi.
 jab 1787 main northwest territary kaa aayojan kiya gaya tha, to ab elkhart ke rup main janaa jane vaala kshetra mukhya rup se otawa, chippe or potawatomi bhartiya janajatiyon dwaara based hue the. 1829 main, pulski kaa gram sthapit hua, jisamen ek daakghar, mil or saint joseph nadi ke uttar kii ore kuch ghar shaamil the. do saal baad, dow havila berdeli ohio se pashchim kii taraf chale gaye or piyare moran se ek varg mile jameen kharidi. elkhart county vishesh rup se nai england se apravasiyon dwaara sthapit kiya gaya tha ye puraane stock "yani" pravaasiyon the, jiskaa matlab he ki ve english puritanon ke vanshaj the jinhone 1600 ke dashak main new england main based.
