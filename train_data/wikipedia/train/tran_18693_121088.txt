 pockemon japani video game company nintando dwaara prakashit va satoshi tajiri dwaara 1996 main rachit media frenchizi he. shuruyaat main game boy ke liye game frick dwaara banaae gaye khel ke rup main release ke baad pockemon vishwa main mario shrunkhlaa ke baad kii duusari sabase badi safal video game par aadhaarit media frenchizi he. pockemon kii vastuon ko aneme, maanga, khilne ke pattey, khilone, upanyaason va anya mediaon main parivartit kiya gaya he.
