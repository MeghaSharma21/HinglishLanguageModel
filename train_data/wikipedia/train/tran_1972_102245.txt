 kroeshia rashtriya footbal team, antarrashtriya footbal main kroeshia kaa pratinidhitiv karti he. footbal team ko desh ke shaasi nikaay kroeshian footbal sangh dwaara niyantrit kiya jaataa he. ek fifa-sweekrut rashtriya team ne 1940 or 1944 ke bich unniis maitreepurn maichon main alpakaalik raashtra banovina ke kroeshia or swatantra rajya kroeshia kaa pratinidhitiv kiya tha. is team ko 1945 main bhang kar diya gaya kyonki kroeshia, esayaar yugoslavia kaa ek sangheey ganraajya ban gaya tha, or is prakaar pratispardhi maichon ke liye ek alag team banane ke liye apaatr tha. 1990 tak, sthaneey khilaadiyon ne iske bajaay yugoslavia rashtriya footbal team ke liye khelaa.
 aadhunik kroeshian team kaa gathan 1990 main yugoslavia se desh kii swatantraaa se kuch samay pehle hua tha, or 1993 tak fifa or yuiefee donon ne ise manyata de di thi..
 zagreb ke maximir stadium main adhikansh gharelu match khele jate hai, ab kuch sudhaar ke baad poljud stadium main yaa anya chote sthanon par bhi hote hai, jaise ki rijeka main contrida stadium yaa ossijek main gradeski viarti. meximir main team ne pratispardhi gharelu maichon main lagaataar 36 jeet hasil kii thi, sitambar 2008 main england se haar ke saath hi yah silsilaa samaapt hua tha. team kaa paaramparik upnaam vetran he.
 kroeshia ko 1994 or 1998 main fifa ke "best muvar of da year" naam diya gaya tha, colombia ke saath yah team ek baar se adhik puraskaar jeetne ke vaali team bani. fifa main pravesh ke samay, kroeshia duniya main 125wein sthaan par thi; 1998 ke vishwa cap abhiyaan ke baad, team ranking main tisare sthaan par pahunch gai, jisase yah fifa ranking itihaas kii sabase asthir team ban gai he.
