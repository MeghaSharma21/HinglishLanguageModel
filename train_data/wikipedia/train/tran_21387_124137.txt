 
 † Stylonurina deanar, 1924 † Eurypterina burmistor, 1843
 urypterida yaa samudri bichchhu aarthropoda kaa ek vilupt jeevvaigyanik gana he jo arakanida se sambandhit he or jisamen vishwa ke sabase bade gyaat aarthropod the. haalanki inkaa naam "samudri bichchhu" he, yah bichchhu nahin the or inaki keval sabase pehali utpann hui jatiyaan hi sagar main rahati thim . isaki adhikansh jatiyaan 20 centimetre se kam thi lekin sabase badi jati kii lambaai 2.5 meter thi.
