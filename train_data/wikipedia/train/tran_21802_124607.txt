 mahaarakshak devi zee tivi par prasaarit hone vaala ek bhartiya hindi dharawahik he. jiskaa prasaaran 14 march 2015 se shanivaar or ravivaar raat 7 baje hota he.
 yah kahani ek ladki kii he, jisamen aseem shaktiyaan hoti hai, parantu use iskaa pataa nahin chalata or devguru brihaspati use apni shaktiyon ke upyog karnaa bataate hai. jisase vah shukracharya ko haraa sake.
