 1920 greeshmakaleen olympic main pehali baar newjiland ek swatantra raashtra ke rup main bhaag liya. iske pehle 1908 or 1912 greeshmakaleen olympic main, newjiland or australia ke athleton ne sanyukt australia team main ek saath pratispardhaa kii. 1952 se newjiland ne sabase sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main bhi bhaag liya he, jisamen keval 1956 or 1964 ke khelon main kami nahin he.
 newjiland olympic samiti newjiland ke liye rashtriya olympic samiti he. engedosi kii sthapana 1911 main hui thi, or 1919 main iosi dwaara manyata praapt thi.
 newjiland ke athleton ne kul milakar kul 118 padak jite hai, lekin greeshmakaleen khelon main jite hai. sabase safal khel roing or athletics prayek 24 padak ke saath kiya gaya he; naukayan 22 padak ke saath nikatataa kaa anusaran karata he. ekmaatr sheetakaaleen olympic padak, 1992 ke sheetakaaleen olympic main alpine skiing main annelis colbarar ne rajat padak jeeta he, yah ek dakshini golardh raashtra dwaara sheetakaaleen khelon main pehla tha. newjiland ne jeeta 118 padak newjiland ko kul 34 padak or 26 wein sthaan par ol time olympic khelon padak talika main 34 wein sthaan par 34 ankon ke saath padak ke bhaar se bhaarit kar diya.
 2016 greeshmakaleen olympic ke baad, 1371 pratiyogiyon ne olympic khelon main newjiland kaa pratinidhitiv kiya he. harry ker ko pehali kiwi olympian or adrian blinko 1000 wein mana jaataa he. 11 june 2009 ke anusaar, 1111 olympiyanon kii us taarikh tak, 114 mritak the or 21 ke thikaane agyaat the. 25 june 2009 tak, keval 9 olympian nahin the. landan main 1948 ke olympic se pehle koi jivit kiwi olympian nahin hai
