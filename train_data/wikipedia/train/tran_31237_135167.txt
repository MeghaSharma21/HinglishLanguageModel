 'sariska' baagh abhayaarany bharat main sab se prasiddh rashtriya udyanon main se ek he. yah rajasthan ke rajya ke alvar jile main sthit he. is kshetra kaa shikaar purv alvar rajya kii shobhaa thi or yah 1955 main ise vanyajeev arakshit bhoomi ghoshit kiya gaya tha. 1978 main baagh pariyojna yojana rigerv kaa darja diya gaya. park vartmaan kshetra 866 varg kimi main failaa he. park jaipur se 107 kimi or dilli se 200 kimi duuri par he. sariska baagh abhayaarany main baagh, chitta, tendua, junglee billi, carrakal, dharidaar bijju, siyaar swarna, chital, sabar, nilgai, chinkaara, chaar sing shaamil 'mrug' chousingha, junglee suar, khargosh, langur or pakshi prajatiyon or sarisrup ke bahut saare vanya jeev milte he. yaha se baaghon kii aabaadi 2005 main gaayab ho gayi thi lekin baagh punarvaas kaarykram 2008 main shuru karne ke bad ab yaha pach baagh ho gayi the. july 2014 main baaghon kii sankhya 11 ho gayi he jisame 9 vayask or 2 shaavak hai.
 jangalon main prabhavi vriksh dhok he. anya pedo jaise hai salaar, kadaya, dhak, gol, ber or khayr . bargarh, arjuna, guggul yaa baans bhi kuch sthanon par kiya ja saktaa he. Shubs rup main kai hai, jaise kair, adusta or jhar ber .
 sariska kii visheshta baaghon kii vajah se he ore yah pahadiyon ke bich basaa he.
 baaghon kii ginati unke upar upasthit rekhaon ke aadhaar par kii jaati he.rekhaon kii banaavat sabhi baaghon main alag alag hoti he,jo inhen ek vishesh pahchaan detii he. camere se snapashot liye jate he. phir unki ginati shuru hoti he.shodhakarta prayek snapashot manual rup se jaanch karte hai or phir baaghon ke dhari pattern kaa vishleshan karte hai, jo fingerprint kii tarah adwitiya hote hai.or ant main baaghon kii sankhya bataa di jaati he.
