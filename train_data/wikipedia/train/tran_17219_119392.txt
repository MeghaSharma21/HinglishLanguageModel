 hal ek krishi yantra he jo jameen kii jutai ke kaam aata he. isaki sahaayta se beej bone ke pehle jameen kii aavashyak taiyaari kii jaati he. krishi main prayukt aujaaron main hal shaayad sabase pracheen he or jahaan tak itihaas kii pahunch he, hal kisi na kisi rup main prachlit paaya gaya he. hal se bhoomi kii upari sataha ko ulat diya jaataa he jisase naye poshak tatv upar aa jate hai tathaa khar-patavaar evam fasalon kii danthal aadi jameen main dab jaati he or dhire-dhire khaad main badal jate hai. jutai karne se jameen main hawa kaa pravesh bhi ho jaataa he jisase jameen dwaara pani banaaye rakhane kii shaktabadh jaati he.
