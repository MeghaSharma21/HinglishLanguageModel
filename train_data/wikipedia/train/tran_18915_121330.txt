 
 islamic calendar
 ramadan yaa ramadan islamic calendar kaa navaan maheenaa he. muslim samudaay is maheene ko param pavitra maanata he.
 ityadi ko pramukh mana jaataa he. kul milaakaar punya kaary karne ko praadhanyata di jaati he. isi liye is maas ko nekiyon or ibaadaton kaa maheenaa yani punya or upasna kaa maas mana jaataa he.
 musalamaanon ke vishwaas ke anusaar is maheene kii 27veen raat shab-e-qadra ko quran kaa nuzul hua. isi liye, is maheene main quran zyaada padhnaa punyakarya mana jaataa he. taraavih kii namaaz main maheenaa bhar quran kaa pathan kiya jaataa he. jis se quran padhnaa na aane waalon ko quran sunne kaa avkaash zarur miltaa he.
 ramajaan kaa maheenaa kabhi 29 din kaa to kabhi 30 din kaa hota he. is maheene main upwas rakhte hai. upwas ko arabi main "soum", isi liye is maas ko arabi main maah-e-siam bhi kehete hai. farsi main upwas ko roza kehete hai. bharat ke muslim samudaay par farsi prabhaav zyaada hone ke kaaran upwas ko farsi shabd hi upyog kiya jaataa he.
 upwas ke din suryoday se pehle kuch khalete hai jise sahari kehete hai. din bhar na kuch khate hai na pite hai. shaam ko suryaastmay ke baad roza khol kar khate hai jise iftaari kehete hai.
 muslim samudaay main ramajaan ko lekar nimna baaten aksar dekhi jaati hai.
