 marcus oralius rome kaa samraat tha jisane 161 se 180 i tak shashan kiya. vah un paanch samraaton main antim samraat tha jinhen 'paanch achche samraat' kaha jaataa he. vah stoic darshan kaa abhyaasi tha. usne bina sheershak ke ek pustak kii rachana kii thi jise aajkal 'meditations' naam se janaa jaataa he. vartmaan samay main pracheen stoic darshan ko samajhane kii yah mahatvapurn strot he. bahut se tippaneekar darshan kii mahanatam pustakon main ise ginate hai.
 sandarbh truti: "notes" naamak sandarbh-samooh ke liye tag maujuud hai, parantu samooh ke liye koi tag nahin mila. yah bhi sambhav he ki koi samaapti tag gaayab he.
