 karakoram-paar kshetra yaa shaxgam vaadi ek lagbhag 5,800 varg kimi kaa ilaaka he jo kashmir ke uttari karakoram parvaton main shaxgam nadi ke donon ore failaa hua he. yah bharat ke jammu or kashmir rajya kaa hissaa hua karata tha jise 1948 main pakistan ne apane niyantran main le liya. 1963 main ek seemaa samjhaute ke antargat pakistan ne is kshetra ko china ko bhent kar diya. pakistan kii daleel thi ki is se pakistan or china ke bich main mitrataa ban jaaegi or unka kahana tha kii aitihaasik rup se is ilaake main kabhi antarrashtriya seemaa nirdhaarit thi hi nahin isliye is zamin ko china ke hawaale karne se pakistan kaa koi nuqasaan nahin hua. bharat is baat kaa purazor khandan karata he or shaxgam ko apni bhoomi kaa ang bataata he. uske anusaar yah pura kshetra bhartiya jammu evam kashmir rajya kaa abhinn bhaag he.
