 
 loris bharat, srilanka or dakshinpurvi asia main milane vaale lorinaaye kul ke nishachari narwanar praani hote hai. 
 loris wanon main rahete hai or dhimi gati se pedo par chalate hai. kuch loris jatiyaan keval keet khaati hai jabaki anya fal, vrikshon ke chaal se nikalne vaala gond, pattey or ghonghe khaati hai. maadaa loris pedo par ghar banaakar unamem apane shishu chhodkar khaane ke liye nikalatin hai. iske liye ve apane bacchon ko apni kohniyon ke bhitari bhaag chaatane ke baad chaatati hai. unki kohniyon ke peeche ke kshetra main ek halkaa vish banataa he jo shishuon ke baalo main fail jaataa he or parabhakshiyon ko unhen khaane se roktaa he. kuch kshetron main iske baavjood bhi orang utaan inke shishuon ko kha lete hai.
 lauris nishachar jeev he. yah bharat, shri lanka tathaa dakshin purv asia ke anya bhaagon ke ushnkaribandheey wanon main paye jate hai. lauris kii chaal dhimi or satark si dikhti he or vriksharohan main choupayon kaa istemaal hota he.
 kuch lauris keval kitbhakshi hote hai jabaki kuch apane aahaar main fal, gond, pattiyaan or ghonghe ityadi sammilit kar lete hai. 
 maadaa lauris apane bacchon ko chhodkar bhojan kii talash main nikalati hai, lekin jane se pehle apane allerji bhare thook se unako chaat kar jaati hai jisase parabhakshi bacchon se dur hi rahen. lekin aisa dekha gaya he ki phir bhi orangutan lauris ko kha letaa he.
