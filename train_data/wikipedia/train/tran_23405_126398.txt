 red cloud  lakota oglala janajati ke mul american adivaasiyon kaa ek mukhiya tha. purvottar vioming or dakshini montana kii ladaaiyon main isane sanyukt rajya america ke sena kii maan-bhen ek kar di. baad main 1868 kii fort laramiye kii sandhi par hastakshar karke isane sarkaar ke saath shaanti sthapit kar lee.
