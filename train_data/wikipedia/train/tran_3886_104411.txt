 amrapali dube ek bhartiya film or televijan abhinetri he. unhone 'rahanaa he teri palkon kii chhaanv main' main suman ke rup main mukhya bhoomika nibhaai. usne zee tivi par saatven or maayka main abhinay kiya. vah meraa naam karegi roshan main bhi dikhaai di thi. dube sahaara van fiction sho pret nits main the.
 amrapali dube mul rup se uttar pradesh ke gorakhpur kii rahane vaali hai. amrapali ne mumbai ke bhavan college se padhai kii he. 2014 main, unhone bhojpuri cinema main dinesh laal yadav ke khilaaf nirahua rikhshavala main agrani bhoomika nibhaai. 2017 main bhojpuri cinema industry ke itihaas main pehali baar kisi gane ko saadhe 10 karod se zyada utyub par mili hai. yah gaanaa 'raate diya butaake' pawan singh kii film 'satya' kaa pramotional song he, jise pawan singh or amrapali dube par filmaaya gaya tha. amrapali dube ek bhojpuri film ke liye kareeb 7-9 lakh rupye leti hai.
