 karez, kaariz yaa qanaat shushk yaa ardh-shushk or garam ilaaqon main niyamit rup se lagaataar pani uplabdh karaane kii ek vyavastha he.
 karez banane ke liye pehle adhik oonchaai vaale kshetra main zamin ke neeche pani kaa strot dhundha jaataa he or phir zamin ke neeche hi naala nikaalkar us pani ko dur tak kisi nichle kshetra main le jaaya jaataa he or vahaan jal ke baahar nikalne kaa ek mukh hota he. is mukh se pani gurutvkarshan ke prabhaav se swayan hi unche jalashay se nichle kshetra kii taraf bahakar nikalataa rahata he. naala dharti ne neeche hone ke kaaran shushkta or garmi ke baavjood bhi pani vashpikaran se khoyaa nahin jaataa, jabaki yadi naala zamin se upar hota to bahut-sa bhaap banakar gaayab ho jaataa. naala banane ke liye or uski dekharekh ke liye har thodi duuri par naale se dharti kii sataha tak ek khol banaaya jaataa he, jo vashpikaran se bachane ke liye aksar upar se dhakaa jaataa he. iran main adhiktar karez 5 kimi se kam duuri ke bhumigat naale rakhte hai lekin keraman ke paas kaa ek karez 70 kimi lambaa he.
 karez takanic sabase pehle iran main 1000 isapurv ke aasapaas khoji gai thi or vahaan se anya kshetron main fail gai. dhyaan den ki karez tabhi pani kaa bandobast kar sakati he jab bhumigat jalashay us sthaan se adhik oonchaai par ho jahaan pani sinchai va anya prayogon ke liye pahunchaana he. isaliye yah bhartiya upmahadweep ke uttari maidani ilaaqon main kam dikhte he. sard yaa gair-shushk kshetron main pani vashpikaran se kam khoyaa jaataa he, isaliye himalay kshetra main nahar-naale zamin se upar hi banaae jate hai or karez banane ke liye mehnat karne kii aavashyaktaa nahin hoti. lekin dakshin bharat main karnataka jaise ilaaqon main or pashchimottaree bhartiya upmahadweep main balochistan jaise kshetron main karez milte hai. iske alaava uttar africa main karez bahut prachlit hai, jahaan ve 'qanaat' ke naam se jane jate hai. yah madhya asia main kazakhstan or shinjiyang jaise kshetron main bhi bahut milte hai.
