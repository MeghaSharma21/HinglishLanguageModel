 kongda bhartiya rajya himachal pradesh kaa ek jila he. kongda jile kaa mukhyalay dharmashala he.
 trigart ke naam se prasiddh kongda himachal pradesh kii pracheenatam riyasat he. mahabharat kaal main isaki sthapana raja susharma ne kii thi. kongda ko ‘trigart’ ke alaava ‘nagarkot’ ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. prachinkaal main yah katoch raajaaon kaa kendra rahaa. gyaravin shatabdi main hindu shahi vansh ke shaasha jaipal kii purvi seemaa kongda tha. san 1399 main taimur ne kongda par akraman kiya tha. jahangir ke samay 1620 i main kongda ko mughal samrajya main jila mila liya gaya. uske pashchaat kongda kii sthaneey rajput style or mughal style se mishrit chitrakaari kii style viksit hui. 1785 i ke pashchaat sansarchand va ranjit singh kaa bhi kongda par adhikaar rahaa. san 1966 ke punjab punargathan ke falswaroop kongda ko himachal pradesh ko saunp diya gaya. 1 sitambar, 1972 ko kongda jila ke teen bhaag kar oona, hamirpur, kongda jilon kaa nirmaan kiya gaya.
