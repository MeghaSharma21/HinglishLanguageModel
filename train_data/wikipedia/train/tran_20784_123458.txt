 awash ithiopia kii ek pramukh nadi he jiskaa puraa maarg ithiopia ke andar hi padataa he. yah adis ababa se pashchim main sthit varaqe parvat se shuru hoti he or lagbhag 1,200 kimi kaa rasta tay karke ek jhiilon kii shrunkhlaa main ant hoti he jo gargori jhil se shuru or abbe jhil main ant hoti hai. yah jhilein jibooti kii sarhad par sthti hai. awash ithiopia ke amhara pradesh, oromia pradesh or somali pradesh par vastrut ek band jalsambhar kii mukhya nadi he. isaki ghati main maanav jati apane aarmabhik kaal se hi rah rahi he or ise 1980 main ek vishwa dharohar sthal kaa darja praapt hua.
