 karpurmanjari sanskrit ke prasiddh naatakkaar evam kavyamimasak rajasekhar dwaara rachit praakrit kaa naatak he. praakrit bhashaa kii vishuddh saahitiyik rachanaaon main is kriti kaa vishisht sthaan he.
 karpurmanjari main kaha gaya he - naatika main bahut si baton main milti-julati natyarachana ko sattak kehete hai. parantu usamen praveshak, vishkanbhak or ank nahin hote. sahityadarpan ke anusaar sattak aadi se ant tak praakrit bhashaa main rachit hota he, na ki sanskrit naatakon ke samaan jisamen keval kuch paatr hi praakrit bhashaa main rachit hota he. usamen adbhut ras kaa vaishishty hota he. ank ke liye jawanikantar shabd kaa prayog hota he. shesh baton main sattak praaia: naatika ke samaan hota he. donon main sheershak nayika ke naam par hota he.
 praakrit bhashaa main paanch sattakon kii prasiddhi he jinamein vilasavati ke atirikt sabhi uplabdh hai. in sabamein karpurmanjari sarvotkrisht or praudh rachana he. rajasekhar kaa sanskrit or praakrit bhashaaon par asaadhaaran adhikaar tha. ve sarvabhashanishann kahe jate the. karpurmanjari kii praakrit praudh evam pranjal he. pehle kaha jaataa tha ki iskaa padyabhag shauraseni praakrit main hai. par dow manmohan ghosh ne is mat ko amaanya siddh kiya he. isamen mukhyat: shauraseni kaa hi prayog he. isamen kavi ne sragdhara, shardoolavicreedit, basantatilka aadi sanskrit ke chhandon kaa praudh evam safal prayog kiya he. praakrit ke chand bhi isamen hai. praakrit main is satt ke likhane kaa kaaran karpurmanjari main kavi ne bataaya he ki sanskrit bandha purush hote hai or praakrit bhashaa ke bandha sukumaar. donon main purush or lalana ke samaan antar he. praakrit bhashaa ke praudh aadyant prayog ke kaaran is sattak main dikhaaya gaya he ki raja chandrapal ne kuntalrajputree karpurmanjari se vivaah karke chakravartipad praapt kiya. andrajalik bhairavanand ne indrajaal dwaara isamen adbhut ras kii yojana kii gai he.
