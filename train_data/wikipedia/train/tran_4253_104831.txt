 khond, yaa kondh bharat ke urisa rajya kii pahadiyon or jangalon ke nivasee hai. ye pond, kaand yaa kondh bhi kehalaate hai. inaki sankhya anumaanath: 8 lakh se adhik he, jinamein se lagbhag 5 lakh 50 hajaar dravid parivaar kii kui or uski dakshini boli kuvi bolte hai. adhikansh khond ab chaaval kii kheti karte hai, lekin ab bhi kutiyaa khond jaise aise kuch samooh hai, jo jhum kheti par nirbhar hai.
 khond kai shatabdiyon se pashchim, uttar or purv kii ore ke oriyabhaashi or dakshin kii ore ke telugubhaashi samoohon ke sampark main he. kuch had tak unhone apane padosiyon kii bhashaayen or prathaen apana lee hai. baood maidaanon main aise khond hai, jo keval oriyabhaashi hai, aage pahadiyon main khond dwibhashi hai, duurastha wanon main keval kui boli jaati he. jati, asprishyata or hindu devi-devataaon ke bare main gyaan sambandhi hindu prathaon ke paalan main ek samaan kramik parivartan dikhaai detaa he. beesvin shatabdi ke uttararddh main parasamskrutigrahan kii prakriya teji se badi he.
