 emirate aise rajnatik kshetra ko kehete hai jis par amir kii oopaadhi rakhane vaala vanshanugat taanashaah raaj karata he. isaki tulana hindi ke 'sultanate' shabd se kii ja sakati he jo kisi aise rajya ko kehete hai jahaan 'sultan' kaa rajya ho. isi tarah 'badshahath' main 'badshaah' kaa, 'riyasat' main kisi 'rais' kaa or 'khaanat' main kisi 'khan' kaa raaj hota he.
 shabdotpatti ke hisaab se emirate yaa emirate ek amir kaa ek ucchstareey karyaalay yaa kshamta hoti he.
 sanyukt arab emirate ek sangh rajya he, jisamen saat sangheey emiraten hai, jo ki prayek ek vanshanugat amir dwaara shasit hai. yahi saat amir is sangh ke raashtrapati or pradhanmantri kaa chunaav apane saat main se karte hai. vartmaan yug main jab ki adhikansh emiraten gaayab hokar yaa to kisi sangh kaa bhaag ban gai hai, yaa kisi bade aadhunik rajya yaa desh kaa hissaa; yaa phir unhone apni shashan style ko badal kar maalik yaa sultan bana diya he, aise mul emirate rajya ab virale hi bache hai.
 yahan kahana uchit hoga ki, arabi bhashaa main yah shabd samanyikrut arth main kisi desh kaa praant hota he, jise abhi bhi shaasha varg hi shashan karte hai, khaskar shahi parivaar kaa sadsy, jaise ki saudi arab ke gavarnorates main.
