 2006 ke antarrashtriya cricket sizan ke dauraan bharat ne west indies kaa daura kiya. bharat isisi test championships or isisi vanade championships donon main westeindies se bhi upar tha lekin baad main team ne is maheene ke pehle ekadivasiy antarrashtriya maichon kii shrinkhalaa main zimbabwe ko 5-0 se haraaya. westeindies ant main is oriai shrinkhalaa ke vijetaon ke rup main ubhara, yah antim mukaable main pehla match haarane ke baad 4-1 se baraabar ho gaya.
 bharat ne 35 varshon main westeindies main ek test shrinkhalaa nahin jiti thi, lekin bharat ne test siries main westeindies ko 1-0 se haraaya tha. bhartiya team ke captain rahul dravid ko unke pradarshan ke liye main of da siries puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha.
 westeindies ke purv tej gendbaaj or tikaakar maaikel holding ne ekadivasiy or test maichon ke bich kaha ki westeindies kii ekadivasiy shrinkhalaa jeetne vaali ek aisi ghatana thi jise "koi bhi aagaah nahin kiya" tha.
