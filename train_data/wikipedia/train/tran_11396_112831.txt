 balavant pandurang anna sahab kirloskar marathi rangamanch ke aadi sangeet-naatakkaar the. 
 aapkaa janm maharashtra ke belgaum jile ke ek gaanv main hua tha. vidyadhyayan ke liye 1863 main puna bheje gaye kintu sangeet or naatak main aarambh se hi ruchi hone ke kaaran schuli padhai main man nahin laga. padhai chhodkar aapane adhyaaapak, sipaahi aadi kii naukari kii par unke jivan kaa vikaas naatak ke kshetra main hi hua. unhone 1866 main bharat shastrottejak mandali kii sthapana kii or apane likhe naatak shri shankar-digvijay or 'allaudin' kaa manchan kiya. isamen unhen paryaapt safalataa mili. isse utsahit hokar unhone apane sahakarmiyon ke saath milakar kirloskar sangeet naatak mandali ke naam se ek vyavsaayik sanstha kii sthapana kii or 1880 i. main puna main abhigyaan shakuntal kaa marathi sangeet roopak sangeet shakuntal prastut kiya. is naatak kii safalataa ne marathi rangamanch main ek nayaa yug upasthit kar diya. kirloskar ne 'sangeet shakuntal' ke atirikt saubhadra raamarajy viyog aadi anya kai naatak likhe or ve sabhi samaadarit hue. 
 42 varsh kii avasthaa main aapkaa 1885 i. main dehanth ho gaya.
