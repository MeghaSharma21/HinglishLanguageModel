 sar herbert baker ek british shilpkaar the jinka do dashak tak dakshin africa vaastukala main bahut mahatvapurn yogadan tha. unka janm or nidhan cobum, kent main oulats main hua tha, or unki samadhi westominster abi main he.
 dakshin africa main unke dwaara banaae gaye girijagharon, vidyalayon or gharon main se pramukh hai pretoria kii unian imaaraten, johansburg kaa saint johnge college, kep toun kii khrout scur riyasat main sthit vinburgh boys hai sakool, or boshkhendaal main shampagne homsted or rodz cottage. sar edwin lutyans ke saath ve nai dilli ke raashtrapati bhawan kii ruparekha ke liye bhi zimmedaar the.
 1912 main edwin lutyans ke saath kaam karne ke liye baker bharat gaye. yahan unhone nai dilli main kendriya sachivalay imaarat, raashtrapati bhawan or kai saansadon ke bangalon kii abhiklpana banane par kaam kiya.
