 kalimantan dakshinpurv asia ke borneo dweep ke us bhaag ko kehete hai jo indonesia ke adheen he. indonesian bhashaa main puure borneo ko kalimantan kehete hai.
 "kalimantan" shabd indonesian main sanskrit se aayaa he or yah "kalmanthan" kaa ek rup he jise "khulata hua garam" ke arth ke saath liya jaataa he. borneo ko sthaneey log isi naam se pukaarte aaye hai or dweep ke liye "borneo" kaa prayog vastav main europe kii upniveshi shaktiyon ne armbh kiya tha.
 dweep kaa 73% bhaag indonesia main aata he or paanch praanton main vibhajit he: madhya kalimantan, purv kalimantan, uttar kalimantan, dakshin kalimantan or pashchim kalimantan. malaysia kii samprabhutta vaala bhaag teen hisson main vibhajit he: sabah rajya, sarawak rajya or labaan sangheey kshetra. inke alaava brunei desh bhi isi dweep ke uttari bhaag main sthit he. indonesian bhaag kaa kshetrafal 5,44,150 varg kimi he. yah ek vishaal kshetra he, jo tulana ke liye bharat ke uttar pradesh va madhya pradesh rajyon ko milakar banataa he.
