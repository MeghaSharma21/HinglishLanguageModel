 jeevigyaan main bijaanu langik va alangik prajnan kii ek sanrachana he jise koi jeev yaa jeev jati swayan ko failaane yaa visham paristhitiyon main dirghakaal tak jivit rahane ke liye banaati he. bijaanu bahut se paudhon, shaiwal, kavak or protozoa ke jeevanchakra kaa mahatvapurn bhaag hota he. bacteria ke bhi bijaanu banate hai, jo antarbijanu kehalaate hai, jo kisi prajnan chakra kaa bhaag nahin hote, balki kathin paristhition main bacteria ko jivit rakhane ke liye bana ek nishkray sikuda dhaancha hota he. kuch paraseete anya jeevon ke shariron main alag-alag prakaar se bijaanu daal dete hai, jo un jeevon ke bheetar viksit hote hai.
