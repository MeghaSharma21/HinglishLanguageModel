 paraval yaa 'patol' ek prakaar kii sabzi he. isaki lata jameen par pasarti he. isaki kheti assam, bengal, oreisha, bihar evam uttar pradesh main kii jaati he.paraval ko hindi main 'paraval', tamil main 'kovakkai', kannada main 'thonde kaai' or asamiya, sanskrit, oriya or bengali main 'potol' tathaa bhojpuri, urdu, or avadh bhashaa main 'paroraa' ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he.
 inke aakar chote or bade se lekar mote or lambe main - 2 se 6 inch tak bhinn ho sakate hai. yah acchhee tarah se saadharantaya garm or ardra jalwaayu ke andar panapati he.
