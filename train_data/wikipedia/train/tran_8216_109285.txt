 laayal mounir abbaud  lebanon ke ek pop gayak he, aagaamee abhinetri, nartki or muslim paropkaaree;jo honge 2017 ke saanskrithik puraskaar ke liye kalatmak rachanaatmakataa ke dwaara lebanon ke samskruti mantralay.  main gaati alag arabi boliyon, Abboud ek sadsy he main sindicket ke peshewar kalakaaron main lebanon.
 
 main paidaa hua 15 may, 1982 main Kanisah ek gaanv main tair jila, dakshin praant main ek shiya muslim parivaar, uske pita kaa naam munir or maan marayam. vah 9 bhai-bahan, teen bhai or chah bahan he. baad main snaatak oopaadhi ke saath master degree main angreji sahitya or anuwaad main, shuru kar diya sangeet karier kii he. vah dikhaai diya, pehali baar main hit sho "studio el-prashansak" 2001 main.
