 stephen norman o'kife  ek australian cricket khilaadi he or vartmaan main gharelu cricket main new south wales ke captain bhi he.
 o'kife ne apana pehla pratham shrenee match new south wales ke liye tasmania ke navambar 2005 khilaaf khelaa tha. jisamen unhone apana pehla ran chakka lagaakar liya tha or us match main 2 wicket bhi liye or ballebaaji karte hue 10 ran bhi banaae the.
 o'kife ne apane tinti-tinti karier kii shuruyaat england ke daure par kii thi.
