 juvari nadi bharat ke goa rajya main bahane vaali sabase lambi nadi he. yah ek jwariya nadi he. is nadi kaa udgam pashchimi ghat main sthit hemad-barsham main he. juvari nadi ko aantarik kshetron main aghanashini naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. yah nadi dakshin-pashchim disha main bahati he or in talukon se hokar guzarati he: tisawadi, paunda, salseti, sangum or kupam.
 juvari nadi 92 kimi lambi he, lekin yah anya nadiyon or naharon se judi hui he jaise mandavi nadi or kambarjua nahar . goa main bahane vaali anya nadiyaan kam lambaai kii hai jaise terekhol, chapora, baga, saal, talpona, or galjibg . in nadiyon kii lambaai or chaudaai jowar bhaate or mausami baadh ke saath-saath badalte rahete hai.
 juvari or mandavi nadiyaan aapas main milakar ek muhaana banaati hai. ye nadiya goa ke krishi kshetra kaa pramukh kaarak hai. in donon nadiyon ko jodne vaali kambarjua nahar ke kaaran rajya ke aantarik sthanon main sthit louh ayask kii khaadaanon tak jalpoton kaa nauchaalan sambhav ho sakaa he. in donon nadiyon kaa jal kaabo aguada sthaan par arab sagar main girtaa he, jahaan in nadiyon ke milan se mormugaao bandargah aakaar letaa he. bandargah nagar vasco da gamma juvari nadi ken nadimukh par sthit he.
