 sonal shah ek sufi gayika evam sangeetkaar hai. sufi ke alaava vah ghazal bhi gaati hai. 
 
 sonal shah ke bekhudi, gujhar ja, juleha, prem divaani, mehboob, biraha agan lagaaye, dard e dil aise kayi gitoko sangeet diya tathaa apni aawaaj se sajaayaa | in gaano ke baad se sonal kii prasiddhi din pratidin babbti gayi. se aaj tak utabanke gaaye huye geeton ne lokapriyata kii nayi unchaaiyon ko chuaa he |
 sonal shah kaa janm mumbai me hua | sonal ne urdu bhashaa ki shiksha praapt ki he | sonal shah pichle kayi salonse ol india readio se judi hui hai | pakistan radio par haal hi main unka ek krayakram bhi hua | sonal shah ne mausiqi kii talim baroda kii em.es. univarsity se hasil kii he or vocal mujeec main do martabaa sone ke tamge hasil karne ke ilaava sonal shah ne bachalaraz aaf parfarming art main awwal divijas se kamyaabi hasil kii he. is ke ilaava sonal shah ko sufi or gazal gaiki kii khidmaat kii badaulat sur sangam, samasad mumbai nami idaare ne suramani ke rashtray khitaab se bhi nawaaza he. sonal ne unki sangeet kii shiksha khanderao aavde, lata prabhune, padmashri dow. prabhaa atre, dow. anita sen  se praapt kii he |
