 qubul he ek zee tivi prasaarit hone vaala bhartiya dharawahik he. iske nirdeshak amandeep singh, aarif ali or ajay es mishra hai. gul khan, nisaar parvaiz or karishma jain ne iskaa nirmaan kiya he. yah zee tivi par 29 oktober 2012 se shuru hua.
 yah kahani asad or zoya kii he. yah donon kii hatya ho jaati or kahani 20 varsh aage bad jaataa he. un donon ke do judwa bachche sanam or sehar rahete hai. punjab main sanam ke daadi ke mrityu hone ke pashchaat vah bhopal naukari kii talash main aa jaati he. vah ibrahim ke haveli main aa jaati he. tab use nahin pataa rahata ki yah haveli pehle uske parivaar kii thi. vahaan jane ke baad use yah sachai kaa pataa chalata he ki aahil tanwir kaa sautela betaa he or tanwir ne hi uske mata pita ki hatya kii thi.
