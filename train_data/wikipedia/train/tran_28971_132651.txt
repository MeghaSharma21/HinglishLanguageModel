 kshupabhumi kisi aise bhushetra ko kaha jaataa he jahaan ke vanaspatiyon main kshupon pradhan ho. aksar aise ilaaqon main ghaas, bootiyaan or jadpradhan paudhe bhi vistrut hote hai. kshupabhumi prakritik hoti he yaa phir maanviya gatividhiyon ke kaaran bhi ban sakati he. kabhi-kabhi van main aag lagane ke kaaran vriksh jal jate hai or kuch varshon ke kaal ke liye yahan kshup hi faile hue hote hai jiske baad dhire-dhire pedo ke badhane se yah phir se kshupabhumi se parivartit hokar van ban jaati he. agar samay-samay par prakritik kaarnon se agni kaa prakope ho to aise sthaan sthaayee rup se bhi kshupabhumi bane rah sakate hai.
