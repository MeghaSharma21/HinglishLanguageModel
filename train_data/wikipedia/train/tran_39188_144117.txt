 british columbia, canada kaa ek praant he jo canada ke prashanth mahaasaagar se lagate pashchimi tat par sthit he. yah canada kaa teesraa sabase bada praant he jiskaa kshetrafal 9,44,735 varg kimi he. 2006 kii janaganana ke anusaar is praant kii kul jansankhya 41,13,487 thi.
 is praant kii rajdhani victoria he or rajya kaa sabase bada nagar vainkuvar he. isi nagar main british columbia kii lagbhag aadhi jansankhya nivas karti he . anya bade nagar hai: calovna, abotsford, camlups, nanimo or prince jorge.
 is praant ke bade udyog hai: vaaniki, paryaton, khanan or machhlipaalan.
 yah praant 1971 main canada se judaa. is praant kii simakan ko lekar canada or sanyukt rajya america main kuch asahmati thi. dakshini seemaa 49wein samaanaantar par sthit he, jaisaa oregan sandhi main sweekrut kiya gaya tha jo 1846 main hui thi. saan juaan dwipon or alaska kii seemaa se bhi kuch vivaad the, par vah sulajhaa liye gaye.
 is praant ke pramukh gordon campbell hai or ve libral party ke netaa hai.
 2010 ke sheetakaaleen olympic khel is praant ke sabase bade nagar vainkuvar main yojit kiye jaaenge. sking pratiyogitaayen wislar main yojit hongi jo ek vishwa-prasiddh sking sthaan he.
