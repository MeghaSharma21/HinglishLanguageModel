 rio tinto dakshin-pashchimi spain kii ek nadi he jo andalusiya kshetra main sthit sierra morena pahaad shrunkhlaa se armbh hokar kadiz kii khadi pahunchakar andh mahaasaagar main ja milti he. yah nadi apane laal-naarangi pani ke liye duniya bhar main mashhoor he.
 prachinkaal se is nadi ke saath ke ilaake main tambe, sone, chaandi or anya padaarthon kii khaane chal rahi hai. 3,000 ipu main shuru hui yah khaane iberian, tartesian, phonikian, unani, roman, vicigore or moore sabhyataaon ne lagaataar hazaron saal chalaai. phir kuch samay tak inamen kaam band ho gaya, lekin 1724 i main spain kii sarkaar dwaara phir armbh ho gaya. itane lambe arase kii khanan se is nadi kaa pani bahut tezaab-grast ho chukaa he . iske pani main itnaa loha mila hua he ke iskaa rang laal-naarangi sa hi rahata he, jisaki vajah se iskaa naam bhi "rio tinto" yani "laal" rang kii "nadi" padaa. isamen kuch charampasandi sookshmjeev rahete hai jinhen iskaa tezaaby pani pasand he. nadi kii laalimaa badhaane main kuch haath in jeevon kaa bhi he.
 san 1873 main is nadi ke saath kii dhaatuon kii khaanon ko chalane ke liye ek "rio tinto company" naam kaa udyog shuru kiya gaya. tab se yah company duniya bhar main fail gayi or bahut si anya khaane bhi chalane lagi. haalanki ab yah rio tinto kii khaane nahin chalati, lekin phir bhi ab yah vishwa kii sab se badi khanan companiyon main gini jaati he. bharat main bhi yah company sakriya he or 2009 main ise madhya pradesh rajya main zamin ke neeche ek 3.7 karod ton kaa heere kaa jamaavada mila jiske liye isane bharat sarkaar se vahaan ek khan shuru karne kii anumati maangi.
