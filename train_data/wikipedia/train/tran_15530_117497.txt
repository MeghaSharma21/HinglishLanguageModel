 shiksha, veda ke chha: angon main se ek he. iskaa rachanakaal vibhinn vidwaanon ne 1000 isapurv se 500 isapurv bataaya he. bhashavaigyanikon kii drushti main shiksha kaa uddeshy saamaanya swanvigyaan evam swanimavigyaan mana ja saktaa he. shiksha ne sabhi praapt dhvaniyon kaa vishleshan kiya he. iskaa uddeshy veda kii richaon kaa thik prakaar se paath karnaa he. 
 purakaal main mata pita or acharya sabhi varnoccharan kaa vigyaan apane putron or shishyon ki bataaya karte the. dhire-dhire pustaken likhi gai or baad main sutara rachana bhi hone lagi. vedamantron ke uccharan kii shiksha hi varnoccharan shiksha ke rup main prasiddh ho gai. is vishay main anek rishi granth likhen he, jinhen praatishkhya or shiksha kaha jaataa he. maharishi panini kii shiksha vishesh prasiddh he.
 is shiksha ke chaar sopan the.
