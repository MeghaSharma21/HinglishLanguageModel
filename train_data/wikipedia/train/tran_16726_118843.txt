 kavango, jo 1998 se pehle okwango ke naam se janaa jaataa tha, dakshini africa main sthit namibia desh ke 13 pradeshon main se ek he. isaki rajdhani rundu he.
 kavango pradesh main namibia ke anya hisson kii tulana main adhik varsha hoti he, jis se yahan krishi or van-sambandhi aarthik vikaas hone kii acchhee sambhaavnaayen hai. uttar main is pradesh kii seemayen angola se or dakshin-purv main botswana se lagatee hai. pradesh ke uttari bhaag se kavango nadi guzarati he or us ke kinare bahut se log based hue hai jabaki pradesh ke andaruni hisson main jan-ghanatv bahut kam he.
