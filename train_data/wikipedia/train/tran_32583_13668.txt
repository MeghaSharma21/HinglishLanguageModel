 agnishmak un bachaav karamchariyon ko kaha jaataa he jo kisi sthaan par lag hanikaarak aag par niyantran paane, use bujhane or us se logon or sampatti ko bachaane kaa kaam karte hai. agnishman ke liye vibhinn paristhitiyon main aag par kaabo paakar use band karne ke liye bahut si takneekein seekhni padati hai or un kathin paristhitiyon main jaakar unhen jhelane ke liye shaareerik-kshamta bhi zaruri he. aadhunik shahari jivan main police or doctoron ke saath-saath agnishman ko bhi ek aavashyak apatkaaleen seva samajha jaataa he. kabhi-kabhi agnishmak daste aisi apaat-sthitiyon main bhi sahaayta karte hai jo aag-sambandhi nahin hoti, jaise ki vimaan durghatana yaa kisi kaarakaane se zahreele rasayan kaa chuunaa.
