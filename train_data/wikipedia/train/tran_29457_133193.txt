 ek handgan ek prakaar kii bandook he jo haath me pakadi jaati he, chaahe to ek haath se yaa donon se. isi visheshta se handgan alag he rifl or lambi banduko se. 
 major handgan upaprakaar revolver or pistal hai. 
 multi bairal pistaul ke kuch udhaaharan hai:
 revolver ek kaartoos bhare silendar, jisamen prayek kaartoos apane swayan prajvalan kaksh main nihit he, or kramik rup se aagneyastron trigar se judaa hua ek anukraman tantra dwaara aagneyastron bairal ke saath sanrekhan main laayaa jaataa he.
