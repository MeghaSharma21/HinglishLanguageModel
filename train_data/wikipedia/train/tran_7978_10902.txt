 himalya kii talahati ke naiysargik saundarya se paripurna paavan dharti kii god me basaa ev nepal tibit kii seemaa se sanlagn janapad pithuragarh kaa dharchula shetra vishaal prakritik sampadaao kaa garh he. isi kasbe main 24 december 1941 ko shri. di. es. aitwal ke ghar chandraprabha ne jaanam liya tha. inaki mata kaa naam shrimati padi devi etval tha, jinhone balykaal se hi ucchkoti ke saahasik kaaryo ko karne kii prerana di. pithauragad se 96 kimi kii duuri par based durgam pichde shetra main us samay na sadak thi or na hi avaagaman ke koi vishesh saadhan the. paidal hi ek jagah se duusari jagah yatra kerani padati thi. chandraprabha ke pita krishi kaary kar apane parivaar kaa paalan poshan kiya karte the. adhik shikshit or garib hone ke baavjood unhone apane baccho ko vidyale beja. chandraprabha ne apni pratmik shiksha changru se san 1954 main purna kii. ve phir raa.i. college main padhi. san 1959 main haai skool kii pariksha uteern kii. kathin pahari raste se ghanto paidal chalanaa, samay par vishvavidhyalay pahunchnaa, vidhyalay ke baad ghar par mata ke kaaryo main haath baantna unki roj kii dincharyo kaa ang tha. uname bachapan main hi chitah jaisi furti thi. vidhyalay main wo apane purush sahpaathiyon se kisi bhi kaary main peeche nahi rahati thi. hiskool kii pariksha ke uparaant aapane intermidiat me rajkeey balika intercolege nainital main pravesh liya. yahi se aapkaa khel jivan shuru hua. vidhyalay str main daudkad, bhaalaa, gola, discus phenkane aadi khelo main bhaag lekar apane ullekhneey uplabdhia hasil kii. taduparant san 1963 main apane rajkeey snatakottar mahavishwavidhyalay nainital se bi. e. kii pariksha uteern kii. san 1964 main aapane ai.tii.ai almoda se silaai-kadai main diploma praapt kiya. phir unhone si.pi.aid kii pariksha illahbaad se kii or usame uteern hui. pariksha ke kuch samay baad aapako sahaayak adhyapika ke rup main rajkeey inter college pithauragarh main niyukti mil gayi. ek shikshika kaa farz aapane bakhubi nibhaayaa. ve garib ghar kii chaatraon ko hameshaa shiksha hetu protsaahit karti thi evam atyant garib balikao kaa shulk dekar pathan-pathan sahaayta bhi karti thi. etval bhalee bhaanti jaanati thi kii pahaad kii ladki ko shiksha praapt karne main garibi sabase badi baadh thi. gadhwal kshetra kii paristhitiya bhi kumaun ke samaan hi he. ashiksha kaa samrajya puure uttaranchal main tab bahut failaa tha. skool 10 se 30 kimi ke duuti par hote the. sushri etval ne kumaun evam gadhwal kshetra ke gaavon main brahman kar balikaon ko skool bhejne kaa abhiyaan chalaaya. iss abhiyaan ke kaaran unhe apeiar lokapatiyata mili. pahari parivesh main pali badi hone ke kaaran bacchpan se hi aapaki parvatarohan main bahut ruchi thi. at: shikshan kaary karte hue shri etval ne bich bich main anekon parvatarohan course bhi puure kiye saath hi anek chotio par bhi aapane vijay praapt kii. san 1972 ke june main basic parvatarohan paathyakarm evam san 1975 agravarti parvatarohan paathyakarm uttarkashi se pratham shrenee main uteern kiya tathaa 1976 main bharat scout evam guide course bhi aapane uttarkashi se uteern kiya. phir gadhwal vishwavidhyalay se 1979 main arthshaastra main em.e. kii oopaadhi hasil kii. san 1979 februvari-march main basic skiing paathyakarm tathaa san 1980 main intermidiat skiing course vditya shrenee main uteern kiya. parvatarohan ke in corso ko karne ke dauraan aapane vibhinn chotio par chadhane main bhi safalataa hasil kii. aapaki utkrisht sevaao ko dekh kar u.pra.
