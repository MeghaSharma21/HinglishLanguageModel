 mihidana, bardhaman, pashchim bengal, bharat kii ek bhartiya mithai he. paaramparik rup se ise bundi ke rup main bhi varnit kiya jaataa he . mihidana, do shabdon se liya gaya he. mithai powder kaminibhog, gobindbhog or basmati chaaval se banaai jaati he, jabaki sunahare rang ke liye isamen besan kaa aataa or kesar kii thodi maatra kii mishrit hoti he. iske baad ise tab tak pani main mix kiya jaataa he jab tak vah apana sunaharaa rang na le. phir yah mishran ek pital ke lettel ke madhyam se chote chhed ke saath ghi or gahare tale hue bartan main daalaa jaataa he. is tarah yah mihidana banakar taiyaar ho jaataa he.
