 "sai dharam tej" ek bhartiya film abhinayta he, jo chiranjeevi ke parivaar se kaam karte hai. vah telugu film udyog main kaam karata he.
 sai dharam chiranjeevi kii bahan "vijaya" durga kaa putr he. vah tollywood ke abhinayta "nagendra babu" or pawan kalyaan ke bhattije hai. unke chachere bhai ram charan, allu arjuna, "allu shiris", varun tej or "niharika konidela" bhi tollywood main abhinayta hai.
 unhone film "pilaa nuvu lenni jivitam" ke saath apni shuruyaat kii, haalanki vastav main unhone pehali film "re" kiya jinke nirmaataa YVS choudhary the, jo 2013 main puri hui thi. film kii relies 2015 tak deree hui thi or unki duusari film ke rup main release hui thi. unki haalia film "subrahmanyam" bikri ke liye ek badi hit he or is udyog main acchhee pahchaan hui he or aalochakon ke lekhak ne film main apane abhinay kaushal kii prashansa kii he. anil ravipudi dwaara nirdeshit unki naveenatam film, supreme 5 may 2016 ko relies hui. is film ko aalochakon se sakaaratmak samiksha mili, jo sai dharam tej ke pradarshan kii prashansa karte hai. unhone episod main "vid or drive" naamak ek utyub channel naam main vivaa naamak ek vishesh upasthiti bhi banaaya he.
