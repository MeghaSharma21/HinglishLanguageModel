 sankhyikee main, jansankhya samanrupi chizon yaa ghatnaon kaa samucchy hoti hai, jo kisi prashn yaa prayog hetu rochak ho. ek sankhyikeey jansankhya, vastav main maujuuda vastuon kaa samooh ho sakati hai athwa anubhav se samanyikaran ke rup main kalpit ek prakkalpanaatmak or sambhavit anant vastuon kaa samooh ho sakati hai . sankhyikeey vishleshan kaa ek aam uddeshy amuk chuni hui jansankhya ke bare main jaankaree kaa utpaadan hota he.
 sankhyikeey nishkarsh main, jansankhya kaa ek upasamucchy chunaa jaataa hai, jo sankhyikeey vishleshan main, jansankhya kaa pratinidhitiv karata hai. yadi koi namoona uchit rup se chunaa gaya ho, to us namoonay kii tatsambandhi visheshtaon se, puri jansankhya kii visheshtaayen, jisase vah namoona nikaala gaya ho, anumaanit kii ja sakati hai.
 ek jansankhya kaa upasamucchy jo ek yaa ek se adhik atirikt gunon ko saajha karata hai, use upajansankhya kaha jaataa hai. udhaaharan ke liye, yadi saare misr ke logon kii ek jansankhya hai, to saare misr ke mardon kii ek upajansankhya hogi; yadi vishwa main saare farmesiyon kii ek jansankhya hai, to misr main saare farmesiyon kii ek upajansankhya hogi. iske vipareet, ek namoona ek jansankhya kaa upasamucchy hai, jise atirikt gun saajha karne ke liye nahin chunaa gaya.
