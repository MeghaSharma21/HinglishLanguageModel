 
 50 - 67 jatiyaan
 hapusha ek konedhari vriksh he jisaki 50 se 67 jeevvaigyanik jatiyaan hai jo prithvi ke uttari golardh par vistrut hai. yah kupresaaciye jeevvaigyanik kul main aate hai, jiskaa sabase prasiddh vriksh-prakaar saro he. hapusha ayurveda or anya parmparik chikitsa pranaaliyon main bahut mahatvapurn he.
