 tujiyaa china ke ek alpsankhyak samudaay kaa naam he. china main inaki aabaadi lagbhag 80 lakh kii he or yah china kaa chatha sab se bada alpsankhyak gut he. yah wooling parvaton ke vaasi hai, jo hunaan, hubei or guizou praanton or chongking zile kii simaaon par vistrut hai. inaki apni ek tibeti or burmi se sambandhit tujiyaa bhashaa he jisamen yah apane samudaay ko 'bijika' bulaate hai, jiskaa arth 'mul nivasee' he. chini log inhen 'tujiyaa' bulaate hai jiskaa matlab 'sthaneey log' hota he.
 itihaaskaaron kaa maananaa he ki tujiyaa log aaj se lagbhag 1200 varsh pehle chongking kshetra ke ird-gird basane vaale 'ba logon' ke vanshaj hai, jinka itihaas chini aitihaasik varnanon main miltaa he. 600 se 400 isa purv main is ilaake main ba logon kaa apana rajya hua karata tha lekin china ke chin rajvansh ne akraman karke unka raaj tod diya or unhen apane adheen kar liya.
