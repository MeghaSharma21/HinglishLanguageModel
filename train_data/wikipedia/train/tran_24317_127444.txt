 daant mukh kii shleshmik kala ke roopantarit ankur yaa ubhaar hai, jo chooney ke lavan se sansikt hote hai. daant kaa kaam he pakadana, kaatna, faadna or chabana. kuch jaanwaron main ye kutarne, khodne, sanvaarane or ladane ke kaam main bhi aate hai. daant, aahaar ko kaat-piskar gale se utarane yogya banaate hai.
 daant kii do panktiyaan hoti hai, 
 upar kaa jabada sthir rahata he or neeche kaa sachal. khopadi se mandibul ko baandhanevaali peshiyon kii sahaayta se yah aage peeche tathaa upar neeche chalakar kaatane kii or chakrakaar gati dwaara chabane kii, kriya karata he. daant sharir kii sabase majboot asthi he.
 dekhiye - maanav ke daant
 anek praani dantavihin hote hai, jaise sterjan, kachua aadi. machhliyon ke daant papadi ke roopaantar maatr hote hai. in daanton main keval shikhar hote hai. ye jabade ke gart main baithe nahin hote. shark machhlee main daant tikshn or trikon or kai panktiyon main hote hai, so machhlee main aari ke daante nahin hote. sarisrupon main bhinn prakaar ke daant milte hai. vishaheen sarpon ke upar do danta panktiyaan or neeche ek pankti tathaa vishdhar sarpon main upar do vishdant hote hai. chhipakali main khuntiyon kii pankti ke sadrush daant milte hai. magar ke daant gart main sthit hote hai. pakshiyon ko daant nahin hote.
 nimna shrenee ke jeevon main sabhi daant ek jaise hote hai, par stanpayi jeevon main vividh prakaar ke daant, kuch main do baar jaise manushya main, to kuch main anek baar . chuhon or ghodon ke bhedak or agracharvanak ke bich sthaan hota he. hathi ke 'dikhaane ke daant' vastav main uske chhedak danta hai. shookar or walrus ke baahar nikle daant unke bhedak danta hote hai. neeche vibhinn praaniyon ke dantasutra die gaye hai.
