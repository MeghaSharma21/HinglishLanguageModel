 swami achutanand dalit chetana prasarak sahityakar tathaa samajasudharak the. unka mul naam 'heeralaal' tha. unka janm uttar pradesh ke mainpuri jile main hua tha. unhone 'aadi-hindu' aandolan chalaaya.
 sampurna bharat main 'aadi hindi' kaa danka bajaane vaale, kavita, naatak or patrakarita main urjasvi paith rakhane vaale achutanand kaa janm unnisvin sadi ke aathven dashak main hua tha. jivan kii shuruyaat main hi shudra jatiyon se begaar karwaane vaale brahmanon se unka jhagada hua or farrukhabad ke apane gaanv ko unhen chhodana padaa tha. baad main arya samaaj ke sampark main aakar ve 'hariharaanand' ho gaye. unki bhaashan kala adbhut thi. jaldi hi ve arya samaaj ke lokapriy prachaarak ban gaye lekin sheeghra hi arya samaaj se bhi alag ho gaye. unka yah vishwaas pakkaa ho gaya ki 'shudra' kahi gayi samast jatiyaan hi 'aadi hindu' hai or unki saanskrithik aitihaasik pahchaan pun: sthapit honi chahie.
 iske baad ve apane lekhan or bhaashanon ke madhyam se lambii yatraaen karte hue aamajan ko sambodhit karne lage. apni baat ko adhik prabhavi banane ke liye ab swami hariharaanand ke bajaay swami achutanand hokar kavita, lekh, naatak aadi ke madhyam se samaaj ko sambodhit karne lage.
