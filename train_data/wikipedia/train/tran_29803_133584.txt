 antoni ramaets i simon spani katalonyai footbaal khilaadi the. barsilona ke nivasee ramaets golkeeper kii bhoomika main khelte the. peshewar footbaal se sanyas lene ke pashchaat inhone ek manager ke taur par bhi kaary kiya. apana zyaadaatar karier inhone fifasi barsilona main 1950 dashak se le kar agle dashak ke shuruyaat takvyateet kiya. club main rahete hue inhone ricardo zamora trophy laa liga main sarvshereshth golkeeper ke taur par paanch avasaron par jiti va iske saath hi inhen 17 mukhya club sammaan bhi mili. 1950 fifaa vishwa cap main ramaets ne apane desh kaa pratinidhitiv kiya or 1960 ke dashak main vibhinn clubo main manager kii bhoomika nibhaai, jinamein pramukh rup se real zaragoza team ne inke netritv main do mukhya trophy jeetin.
 antoni ramaets simon kaa janm 1 july 1924 ko katalonya kii rajdhani barsilona main hua. 'ramaets' or 'simon' kramshah inke pita or mata ke paariwarik naam hai. 
 1947 main 23 varsh kii umr main inhone fifasi barsilona ke saath qaraar par hastakshar kiye. us se pehle ye real valadolid club main khelte the. apana zyaadaatar khel karier inhone barsilona main hi bitaayaa or club ke liye 1961 tak 473 match khele. club ke saath inhone chah laa liga kitaab, paanch spanish cap or do inter-citis fares cap jite. inhen paanch avasaron par zamora trophy se nawaaza gaya, jo laa liga ke us golkeeper ko di jaati he jo ki liga ke ek satr main prati match sabase kam gol khaataa he. 
 ramaets ne 11 varsho kii avadhi main spain kaa 35 anterrashtriya maichon main pratinidhitiv kiya. apana sabase pehla match inhone chile ke viruddh 29 june 1950 ko brazil main 1950 fifaa vishwa cap ke dauraan khelaa. toornament ke dauraan inhen 'marakanya kii billi' kaa upnaam mila. vishwa cap ke is sanskaran main inhone apni team ko semifinal tak pahunchne main madad kii. 
 ramaets ne 11 match anadhikarik catalan rashtriya team ke liye bhi khele.
 ramaets kii 31 july 2013 ko lambi bimaari ke pashchaat 89 varsh main mrityu hui.
