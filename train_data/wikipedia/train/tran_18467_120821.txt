 
 empire state building neuyark shahar ke paanchaven avenue or pashchim 34wein maarg ke bich khadi ek 102 manzili gaganchumbi imaarat he. iskaa naam neuyork rajya ke upnaam se liya gaya he. san 1931 main is imaarat ke nirmaan purna hone se lekar world trade centre ke uttari tower ke 1972 main nirmaan tak chalis saalon kii avadhi main yah duniya kii sabase oonchi imaarat bani rahi. 
 2001 main world trade centre kii tabaahi ke baad ke empire state building ek baar phir neuyork shahar or neuyork rajya kii sabase oonchi imaarat ban gai. empire state building america kii duusari sabase badi imaarat or or duniya kii 5veen sabase badi imaarat he. yah america kii chaunthi sabase badi swatantra rup se khadi imaarat he.
 is imaarat ke maalik or vyavasthaapak dablyoo evam ech prapartis hai.
 
 empire state building kaa nakshaa sharvi, lemb or hormone associats naamak vaastukaron ne taiyaar kiya tha. is imaarat kaa nirmaan aise samay main kiya gaya, jab bahut se log duniya kii sabase oonchi imaarat banane kaa prayaas kar rahe the, lekin empire state building sabase oonchi imaarat thi. iskaa nirmaan 410 dinon main puraa hua or raashtrapati herbat hover ne 1 may, 1931 ko imaarat kii batti jalaai.
