 shantaram nike, bharat ke varisht sadan rajyasabha ke sadsy the. ve goa congress ke purv adhyaksh rah chuke hai. ve varsh 1984 main 8veen loksabhaa main uttari goa se nirvaachit hue the. unhone goa ko purna rajya kaa darja diye jane kii bhi maang kii thi jo varsh 1987 main puri hui thi. unhone rajyasabha main goa kaa do baar pratinidhitiv kiya tha. ve varsh 2005 se 2011 tathaa varsh 2011 se 2017 tak lagaataar do baar rajyasabha ke liye nirvaachit hue the.
