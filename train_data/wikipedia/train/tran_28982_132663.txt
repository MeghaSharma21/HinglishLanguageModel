 balqaan praant turkmenistan kii ek vilaayat he. yah us desh ke sudur pashchim main sthit he or isaki sarahadein iran, uzbekistan, kazakhstan or caspian sagar se lagati hai. is praant kaa kshetrafal 1,39,270 kimi2 he or san 2005 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 5,53,500 anumaanit kii gai thi. balqaan praant kii rajdhani balqanabat shahar he, jise pehle nebit daag bulaayaa jaataa tha. balqaan praant kii aabaadi kaa ghanatv turkmenistan ke sabhi praanton main sabase kam he.
 balqaan praant main prakritik gas or petrol ke khanij hai. yahan se turkmenistan kaa 94% gas utpaadan or 12% petrol-sambandhi utpaadan aata he. yah praant turkmenistan kii 18% bijli bhi paidaa karata he. yahan kaa vaataavaran bahut shushk he jis karanvash yahan krishi predavar bahut kam he. turkmenistan kii upjau zamin kaa keval 4.5% balqaan praant main he. caspian sagar main sthit ogurja adaa dweep bhi prashasnic rup se isi praant kaa hissaa he, jahaan kabhi samudri daaku chhupate the or or phir baad main kodhiyon kaa hospital tha.
