 manoj tibdewal akaash ek bhartiya tivi ankar or patrkaar he, jo doordarshan samachar se ek dashak tak televijan news ankar or varisht samvaadadaata ke rup main jude rahe. inhone didi news par prasaarit hone vaale lokapriy tok sho, ek mulaaqaat kii mejabani kii. ye dynamite news ke sansthaapak or pradhan sampadak he.
 tibdewal ne apane raajnneetik patrakarita jivan kii shuruyaat india today se kii. iske baad inhone uttar pradesh ke mahrajganj jile main indian express samooh ke beuro chif ke rup main kaam kiya. print media ke baad tibdewal ne electranik media kii ore rukh kiya or janvari 2006 main didi news, nai dilli main televijan patrkaar ke rup main jud gayi. is dauraan tibdewal ne bharat ke raashtrapati or pradhanmantri ke saath unke vishesh vimaan main desh or videsh kii kai yatraaen kii.
 raajnneetik reporting karne ke alaava, tibdewal ne paanch saal tak doordarshan samachar par lagaataar ek saptahik saakshaatkaar aadhaarit tok sho- ek mulaakaat kii mejabani kii, jisamen inhone desh ke kai kendriya mantriyon, mukhyamantriyon, rajyapalon, bhartiya sansad ke sadasyon, udyogpatiyon, bollywood kii hastiyon, khel sitaron, iees toppers or saamaajik kaaryakartaaon kaa saakshaatkaar kiya.
 iske baad manoj ne khud ke media samooh dynamite news kii shuruyaat karne ke liye doordarshan news se istipha de diya. tibdewal ne media samooh dynamite news 16 oktober 2015 ko nai dilli se laanch kiya.
