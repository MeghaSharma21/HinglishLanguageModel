 amritraay aadhunik kahanikar hai. inkaa janm uttar pradesh ke varanasi main hua tha. amritraay prasiddh lekhak premachand ke suputr han. ye pragatisheel sahityakaron main mahatvapurn sthaan rakhte hai. kahani tathaa lalit nibandh ke lekhan main bharat vikhyath is lekhak ko kalam kaa sipaahi naamak pustak par sahitya academy kaa puraskaar mil chukaa he. inkaa upanyaas beej tathaa kahani-sangrah tirangaa kafan bahu-charchit he.
