 biodijal jaivik sroton se praapt tathaa dijal ke samatuly indhan he jo paramparaagat dijal injanon ko bina parivartit kiye hi chalaa saktaa he. bharat kaa pehla biodijal sanyantr austrailia ke sahyog se kakinada sej main sthapit kiya gaya he
 biodijal shat-pratishat navinikarniya sroton se banaaya jaataa he. yah paramparaagat indhano kaa ek svachh vikalp he. isko bhavishya kaa indhan mana ja rahaa he. biodijal main patroleum nahin hota kintu ise samyak anupaat main petroleum main milakar vibhinn prakaar kii gadiyon main prayog kiya ja saktaa he. biodijal vishaiylla nahi hota; yah biodegradable bhi he.
 biodijal vanaspatik telon se praapt anya vaikalpik indhanon se bhinn he. biodijal ko bina kisi parivartan kiye hi dijal injanon main prayog kar sakate hai jabaki vanaspati telon se praapt indhanon ko keval 'ignishan kambasshan' vaale injanon main hi prayog laa sakate hai or vah bhi kuch parivartanon ke baad. is kaaran, biodijal prayog main sarvaadhik aasaan indhanon main se ek he. or sabase acchhee baat yah he ki kheti main kaam aane vaale upkarnon ko chalaane ke liye sabase upyukt he.
 biodijal jis prakriya dwaara nirmit kiya jaataa he use trns-eastarikaran kaha jaataa he. is prakriya main vanaspati tel yaa vasa se glicrin ko nikaalnaa hota he. is prakriya main methil easter or glicrin aadi sah-utpaadabhi milte hai. biodijal main sulfur or aromatics nahin hote jo ki paramparaagat indhanon main paye jate hai.
 biodijal ke bare main sabase acchhee baat yah he ki yah duusare indhanon kii bhaanti paryaavaran ke liye hanikaarak nahin he. iske alaava yah aise sroton se praapt hota he jo punah naveen kiye ja sakate hai. paramparaagat indhanon kii tarah yah pradooshan karne vaala dhuvaan nahi paidaa karata.
