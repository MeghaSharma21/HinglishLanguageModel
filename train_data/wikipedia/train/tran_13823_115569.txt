  raajnneeti praveshdwar
 nepal ki raajnneeti bahudaleey pranali ke tahat ek ganatantric dhaanche ke anusaar kaary karti he. vartmaan main raashtrapati ke pad par vidya devi bhandari virajman hai. pradhanmantri ke pad par sher bahadur deuba hai. karypalika shakti kaa upyog pradhanmantri tathaa unke mantrimandal dwaara hota he, jabaki vaidhanik shakti sansad main nihit he.
 28 may 2008 tak nepal ek sanvaidhanik rajtantr tha. usee din nepali samvidhan sabha dwaara nepal ko ganatantric rajya banane ke liye samvidhan badal diya gaya.
