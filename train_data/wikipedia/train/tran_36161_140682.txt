 tsethang tibet kaa ek shahar he. yah yarlung nadi kii ghati main sthit he jo tibeti samskruti kaa janmasthal mana jaataa he. prashasnic drushti se yah tibet ke lhokha vibhaag main aata he or us vibhaag kii rajdhani bhi he. yah shahar tibet ke sabase bade nagaron main se ek he or tibet ke kai pracheen raja yaheen se raaj kiya karte the. 19veen sadi main yahan se guzarane vaale yatriyon ke anusaar yahan 1,000 ghar, gompa, bazaar or ek dzong khadey hue the. lhasa ke baad yah tibet ke eu-tsang kshetra kaa doosraa sabase bada shahar tha. 3,100 meter par based hue is shahar kii aabaadi 2005 main 52,000 anumaanit kii gai thi. iske aasapaas tibeti itihaas kii kai mahatvapurn imaarte sthit hai. nedong basti is se lagbhag 4 kimi dakshinpaschim main sthit he lekin shahari vistaar se donon kaa vilay hone laga he.
