 james pratham great britain tathaa ireland kaa shaasha tha jo 'james shashth' ke naam se scotland kaa shaasha bhi tha. vah meri scot tathaa uske dwitiya pati lard darnale kii ekmaatr santan hone ke saath saath henry saptam kii putree margaret kaa pautra tha. scotland ke shaasha kii sthiti se usne sanmaton kii shakti ko vighatit kiya tha.
 denmark kii ain se usne 1589 i vivaah kiya. 1603 i main england kaa shaasha hone par england or scotland donon rajasattaaon ko ek kiya. atyadhik vidwaan tathaa kaushalhin hone ke kaaran use krissendam ke 'sabase budhimaan murkh' kii oopaadhi mili thi. raja ke daivi adhikaar kaa kattar poshak hone ke kaaran usne ek charchpradhan rajya sanchaalan kii niti apanaai. praarambhik varshon main uske pramukh salaahkaar salisburi kaa arl tathaa buckingham kaa duk the. uske shashan se raja tathaa sansad ke dirghakaalin sangharsh kaa prarambh hota he. uske shashan main sab milakar chaar sansaden yojit huin. yadhyapi james vidhaan ke atikaraman karne kaa doshi nahin tha par vah uski pratishtha kaa sahi anumaan nahin laga saktaa tha. sangharsh ke pramukh kaaran dharm, kar-vyavastha, vaideshik niti the. bina parliment kii sweekruti ke james ne impojishns naamak tex lagaae. parliment ne raja ke mantriyon par lagaaya janevaala impichament kaa pracheen adhikaar punerjeevit kiya. james kii vaideshik niti ne jiskaa uddeshy spain se maitreepurn vyavhaar tathaa saadhaaran shanti sthapit karnaa tha, us janata ko aprasann kar diya jo tees varshiya yuddh main yah aashaa karti thi ki james, palatin ke elector fredrick kii, jo ki uskaa damaad tha, sahaayta karegaa. apane putr charles kaa vivaah spain kii rajkumaari se karaane main asafal hone par james ne 1624 i main spain ke viruddh yuddh kii ghoshnaa kii. james ne madhyam shrenee kii saahitiyik rachanaayen apane peeche chhodin.
