 drakshasav angoor se banaai jane vaali ek paaramparik ayurvedic aushadhi he. drakshasav he ek kamjor sharaab, kyonki angoor kaa ras aamtaur par keval aanshik rup se kinvit he. yah bhi kabhi kabhi kaa upyog karke taiyaar kishmish dhyaan kendrit. tonic kaa daawa kiya he karne ke liye phydemand ho saktaa he ke liye vikrutiyon ke rup main is tarah susti, kamajori or garmi thakaavat. drakshasav mana jaataa he pataa karne ke liye swasthya ke asantulan se utpann hone vaali kii ek atirikt vaat-vaayu dosha main ayurvedic pranali he. 
 draksh ek sanskrit shabd he jiskaa arth he angoor. "aasav" kaa arth he asut yaa ras. is prakaar "drakshasav" kaa arth he angoor kaa ras.
 drakshasav paaya main ullekh sushrut samhita, ek kitaab ke upachaar ke liye dating 3-4 shatabdi CE.
