 vaaman das basu civil sarjan evam prasiddh lekhak the. unhone us kaal main bhi anekon vishyon par pustaken likhi jinke bare me anya logon ne ek dashak ke baad lekhan tathaa shodh kiye.
 inamen se 'rise of chrishchiyan power in india' angreji shashan ke virodh kii bhaavana se otprot he. isane apane samay main tahalaka machaa diya tha. isi pustak ke aadhaar par sundar laal ne hindi main 'bharat main angreji raaj' likhaa. is pustak ne bhi tahalaka machaaya. swadeshi parampara ke gurukul kangri vishvavidhyalay ke snaatak evam itihaaskaar jayachandra vidyalankar ne apni pustak vamandas vasu ko samarpit kii.
 vaaman das basu 1909 se 1919 tak panini press se bhi jude rahe. is dauraan 38 pustaken likhi gayi. inhone vedon kaa angreji bhashaa me anuwaad bhi kiya.
