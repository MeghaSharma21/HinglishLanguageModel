 
 faljyothish astrology kaa bharat kii parampara main naam he.
 bhartiya falit jyotish main anek shakhayein or upashakhayein hai 
 jyotish main mukhyat: do paddatiyon ke sahare samast fal kathan kiya jaataa he. prathmat: ganit or uske baad falit kathan kiya jaataa he. 
 janm kundalee ke nimna ghatak hai 
 fal jyotish kii bhartiya paddatiyaan or pranali : bhartiya jyotish ke mul par vichaar karen to yah samajhnaa va jaananaa atyant saral ho jaayegaa ki fal jyotish main kitni sachai he. or bhartiya jyotish kii yah visheshta he ki isamen yah jaan paanaa aasaan he ki falit kitna sahi he athwa kitna galat. kyonki fal jyotish main uttam falit saadhan ke liye uttam ganit va akalan ke saath abhyaas sadhana or atindriya gyaan vishesh aavashyak he. anyathaa kabhi bhi falit sateek nahin baithta. 
 fal jyotish main kundalee kram kaa sookshm adhyayan karne par yadi jyotish kaa acha jaankaar jyotishi hoga to purv janm arthath pichley janm kaa haal bataa degaa. pichley janm kaa haal jaanane ke liye jyotishi nakshatra kaal guzarane yani nakshatra ke kis charan yaa ansh par janm hua he ise aadhaar maanate hai. isamen nakshatra kaa gujara samay vinshottaree dasha kaa bhukt kaal mana jaataa he or shesh bache samay ko bhogy kaal kaha jaataa he. nakshatra kii gujri avadhi balak kii maan ke garbh main guzari gayi avadhi kii gananaa bataati he, tathaa isse purv kii avadhi kii gananaa karte hi pichley janm kaa vivaran praapt hona prarambh ho jaataa he. ...
