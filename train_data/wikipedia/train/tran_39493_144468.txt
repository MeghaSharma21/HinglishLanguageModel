 jogikha bhutan main boli jane vaali rashtriya bhashaa he. iskaa naam "jose" shabd se liya gaya he, jiskaa arth "jila" hota he, or "jogikha" kaa matlab "jila kii mukhyalay main boli jane vaali bhashaa" he. san 2013 main ise kul milakar lagbhag saadhe-chah lakh log bolte the. bharat ke pashchim bengal ke kalimpong shahar main bhi ise bolne vaale kuch log hai. jogikha ko tibeti lipi main likhaa jaataa he.
 jogikha bhashaa bhutan ke desh bhar main 17 veen sadi ke baad se lokapriy sanchaar kii bhashaa ke rup main istemaal kiya gaya he. san 1971 main, bhutan kii teesri raja jigmed dorje vaichhyug dwaara bhutan kii rashtriya bhashaa ghoshit kar diya. jogikha bhashaa aupchaarik rup se lekhan ke liye patibaddh tha. rashtri bhashaa ghoshnaa ke baad vishay ke rup main skulon main shuru kii gai thi. san 1986 main, bhutan kii chauthe raja jigmed senge vaichhyug dwaara jogikha kii unnati ke liye ek swatantra sarkaar ke karyaalay ke rup main jogikha vikaas ayog kii sthapana kii. bhutani saanskrithik viraasat ke bhandar ke rup main jogikha kii bhoomika he jo sangrakshan or bhashaa ko badhaava dene ke liye kai adhiniyamon bane rashtriya sabha ke kai satron se zyada samarthan mila.
