 varun hindu dharm ke ek pramukh devtaa hai. pracheen vaidic dharm main unka sthaan bahut hi mahatvapurn tha par vedon main uskaa rup itnaa amoort hai ki uskaa prakritik chithran mushkil he. mana jaataa he ki varun kii sthiti anya vaidic devataaon kii apeksha pracheen he, isiilie vaidic yug main varun kisi prakritik upadan kaa vaachak nahin he. agni va indra kii apeksha varun ko sambodhit sookhton kii maatra bahut kam he phir bhi uskaa mahatv kam nahin he. indra ko mahaan yoddha ke rup main janaa jaataa he to varun ko naitik shakti kaa mahaan poshak mana gaya he, vah rut kaa poshak he. adhiktar sookhton main vasrn ke prati udaatt bhakthi kii bhaavana dikhaai detii he. rugveda ke adhiktar sookhton main varun se kiye gaye paapon ke liye kshamaa prarthana kii gai hai. varun ko anya devataaon jaise indra aadi ke saath bhi varnit kiya gaya he. varun se sambandhit prarthanaaon main bhakthi bhaavana kii paraakaashtha dikhaai detii he. udhaaharan ke liye rugveda ke saatven mandal main vasrn ke liye sundar prarthana geet milte hai.unako "asur" kii upadhi di gayi thi, matlab kii wo "dev" devataaon ke samooh se alag the. unke paas jaadui shakti maani jaati thi, jiskaa naam tha maaya. unako itihaaskaar maanate hai ki asur varun hi parsi dharm main "ahura mazda" kehalaae. baad kii puranic kathaon main varun ko maamuli jal-dev bana diya gaya.
