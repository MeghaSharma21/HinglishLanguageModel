 firoz khan nun, ek pakistani rajaneta, evam pakistan ke 7wein pradhanmantri the. unka janm 7 may 1893 i. ko pakisatan ke jila sargodha kii tehsil bhalwaal ke gaanv hamokah main hua tha. vah sir mohammad heyan nun ke putr the.
 unhone praarambhik shiksha public skool bherah jile sargodha se praapt kii. 1905 main unhone, ellison college lahore main daakhila liya. 1912 main ucch shiksha praapt karne ke liye england chale gaye. 1916 main wiliam college oxford se history main bie kiya. 1917 main barrister banakar waapas bharat chale aae. jan 1918 i. main sargodha se apni practice shuru kii. jan 1921 se janvari 1927 hoicourt main practice karte rahe.
 1920 ke chunaav main tehsil bhalwaal se lahore lejaslito parishad ke sadsy chune gaye. 1925 ko bharat cabinet main gramin aabaadi ke mantri bane. is samay vah sabase kam umr mantri the or is pad par das saal se adhik samay tak pakad rahe or lagbhag solah vidhansabha ke sadsy rahe. oktober 1931 ke chunaav main vah nirvirodh chune gaye. june 1936 ta sitambar 1941 landan memhai ayukta ke pad par sevaayen saranjaam den. pehali baar 1938 or duusari baar 1939 membin antarrashtriya sanstha baithkon bharat kaa pratinidhitiv kiya. sitambar 1941 ta sitambar 1945 vysarai mantrimandal ke sadsy rahe. sitambar 1945 mantri raksha ke pad se istipha gaye. november 1946 main ko rawalpindi chunaav main safal hasil kii. november 1947 main samvidhan sabha ke sadsy chune gaye. 1947 main kaayade ajam mohammad ali jinna aap apane vishesh pratinidhi ke rup main muslim duniya main bheja.
 april 1950 taaki oktober 1952 purvi pakistan ke duusare rajyapal rahe. 13 april 1953 main ta 21 may 1955 punjab ke tisare mukhyamantri rahe. 1955 main muslim lig chhodkar republican party main shaamil ho gaye or is tarah vipaksh main gaye. 12 sitambar 1956 ko pradhanmantri pakistan hossain shahid suhravardi cabinet menkhaarajah maamalon or rashtramandal vibhaag unke paas rahe. 1956 main unke sangharsh se suraksha parishad menkashmir vidhansabha ke liye chunaav kaa prastaav das maton se paarit hua. 18 oktober 1957 ko ai ai chandrigar mantrimandal main republican party ke tikat par mantri videsh pad par asin hue.
  11 disambar 1957 ko ibrahim ismail chandrigar pradhanmantri se istipha gaye usee din desh firoz khan nun ne bataur pradhanmantri pakistan shapath lee. pradhanmantri ke alaava unke paas raksha or aarthik, rashtramandal, rajya, seemaa kshetra, videsh kashmir or kanoon vibhaag rahe. unke shasankaal main 8 sitambar 1958 ko guadar sthanantaran samasya kaa samaadhaan hua or pakistan ko do hajaar chaar sau varg mile ke kshetra waapas mil gaya.
 raashtrapati pakistan major general sikandar mirza ne 7 oktober 1958 dinaankit vidhansabhaon, kendriya sansad, centre or rajya mantrimandal tod di.
 firoz khan nun kaa nidhan 1970 main hua.
 firoz khan nun kai pustak ke lekhak bhi hai jinamein canada, moorkhon se praapt buddhi, chashmadid, bharat or aapbeetee ullekhneey hai.
