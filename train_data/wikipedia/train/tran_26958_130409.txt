 jammu evam kashmir rajya ranbir dand samhita bharat ke jammu evam kashmir rajya main lagu aparaadh evam dand samhita he. bhartiya samvidhan kii dhara 370 kii binduon ke tahat bhartiya dand samhita jammu-kashmir rajya par aadhipaty nahin kii ja sakati. aanya bharat ke ulat jammu-kashmir par bhartiya dand samhita nahin ranbir dand samhita aavrat he. yah jammu-kashmir main british kaal ke samay se lagu he, arthath tab, jab jammu kashmir ek swatantra riyasat tha. iskaa karyanvayan 1932 main hua tha or yah abhi bhi lagu he. is prakriya ko tab avgat karaayaa gaya tha jab yahan dogra rajvansh kaa shashan tha or maharaj ranbir singh shaasha the. ranbir aachaar samhita ipisi ke padon ke aadhaar par hi banaaya gaya he jise thomas babinton maikale ne rachit kiya tha.
