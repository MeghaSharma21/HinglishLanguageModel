 mohit chouhan ek bhartiya parshv gayak hai jinhen mukhyatah unke romantic gaanon ke liye janaa jaataa he. wo do baar filmafere sarvshereshth parshv gayak puraskaar praapt kar chuke hai.
 mohit chouhan kaa janm himachal pradesh ke sirmaur jile main hua. unki shuruaati padhai sant jevirs skool, dilli main hui or aage ki padhai himachal pradesh se kii jahaan par dharmashala college se master of science in jiyology kii degree praapt kii. dilli aa kar vahaan silk root naam kaa band banaaya, 1996 main is band kaa pehla elbum boond reelij hua. is elbum kaa ek geet duba..duba bahut hit hua.
 mohit chouhan ne ramgopal kii film road main pehali baar plabac gaaya.
