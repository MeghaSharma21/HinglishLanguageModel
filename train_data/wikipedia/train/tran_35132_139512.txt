 gale main rassi ke kasne ke kaaran hui maut ko faansee kaha jaataa he. pracheen kaal main aparaadhiyon ko dand dene ke liye faansee kii sajaa di jaati thi or vartmaan main bhi jaghanya aparaadhon ke dand hetu yah pratha prachlan main he. arab deshon main faansee bahut saamaanya sajaa he. bharat main bhi faansee kii sajaa prachlan main he or desh kii pramukh jelon main iske liye faanseeghar bane huye hai. in jelon main faansee dene vaale karmchaarion kii niyukti hoti he jinhe jallaad kaha jaataa he.
 aatmahatya ke liye bhi faansee ek sarvaadhik prayukt hone vaala tarika he. faansee main vyakti ke gale main rassi kaa fanda kas jaataa he or uskaa saans maarg avaruddh ho jane se uskaa dam ghut jaataa he or is prakaar us vyakti kii dardanaak maut ho jaati he.
