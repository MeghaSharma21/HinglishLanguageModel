 suleman yahudiyon ke raja daud or bethasabe kaa putr. apni mata, thajak sadok tathaa nabi nayan ke sammilit prayaas se suleman apane agraj adonya kaa adhikaar asweekaar karaane main samarth hue or vah swayan raja ban gaye.
 suleman ne jerusalem kaa vishwavikhyath mandir tathaa bahut se mahal or durg banawaye. unhone vyapaar ko bhi protsaahan diya. apane antarrashtriya sambandhon ko sadrudh bana lene ke uddeshy se unhone faron kii putree ke atirikt or bahut si videshi rajkumariyon ke saath vivaah kiya. vah kushal prashaasak the. unhone jerushlem ke mandir ko desh ke dharmik jivan kaa centre banaaya or anek anya baton main bhi kendrikaran ko badhaava diya.
 apane nirmaan kaaryon ke kaaran unhone praja par karon kaa anuchit bhaar daal diya tha jisase unki mrityu ke baad vidroh hua or unke rajya ke do tukade ho gaye- 
 parvarti peediton ne suleman ko adarsh ke rup main dekhakar unako yahudiyon kaa sabase pratapi raja maan liya he kintu vaastaviktaa yah he ki atyadhik kendrikaran tathaa karbhaar ke kaaran unka rajyakaal vifaltaa main samaapt hua. unke dwaara nirmit bhawan unki khyaati ke ek maatr aadhaar the. vah apni budhimaani ke liye prasiddh hue or is kaaran niti, upadeshak, shreshth geet, jaise bible ke anek parvarti pramanik granthon kaa shrey unako diya jaataa tha. kuch anya pramanik granth bhi unke naam bhi prachlit hai.
