 cholistaan, jise sthaneey bhashaa main rohi bhi kehete hai, pakistani punjab or bharat va sindh ke kuch padosi bhaagon main failaa hua ek registan va ardh-registani kshetra he. yah thar registan se judaa hua he. yahan hakaraa nadi kaa sukha hua pracheen maarg he jiske kinare sindhu ghati sabhyataa ke bahut se khandhar milte hai. 9veen sadi main raay jajja bhatti dwaara banaaya gaya deravad qila bhi cholistaan ke bahawalpur kshetra main khadaa he.
 'chol' kaa arth kai turki bhashaaon main 'marubhumi' hota he.
