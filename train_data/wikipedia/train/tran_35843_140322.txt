 tadit chonte vah chonte hoti he jo kii bijli ke girane se hoti he. bijli ke achaanak girane se vyakti kii mrityu ho jaati he kyunki bijli kii voltage bahut hi adhik hoti he. tadit chonte hone ke bahut se kaaran hote he jiname se bijli ke nuksaan, teevr garmi or inase utpann hone vaali yantric urja kaa parinaam he. tadit chonte vyakti ko yaa to sidhe lagati he, yaa kisi vastu se takaraane ke baad yaa phir dharti se takaraake. tadit chonte lagane ke kaaran kuch vyaktiyon kii mrityu ho jaati he or kuch vyakti jo marate to nahin he apitu umrbhar ke liye apahiz ho jate hai. sabase zyada sankatpurna chont sanchaar pranali par hui chont se hoti he, or bijli ke jatke se jab fefde tak peedit hote hai. tadit chonte sharir ke baaki ango par bhi prahaar karti he or kabhi-kabhi bijli kaa voltage zyada hone ke kaaran insaan kii mrityu bhi ho jaati he.
