 yah sthaan bikaner se 28 mile dakshin-purv main he. yahaan kaa susawaani devi kaa mandir ullekhneey he. yah mandir ek unche tile par bana he tathaa isamen ek tahkhaana, khulaa hua praangan evam baramada he. 
 morkhana kaa mukhya mandir,maa susawaani kaa darbar
 yah sara jaisalmeri pattharon kaa bana he or iske tahkhaane kii bahari chahardivaaron par devataaon or nartkiyon kii akritiyaan khudi he. isi prakaar dwaar bhaag bhi khudaai ke kaam se bharaa hua he. tahkhaane ke chaaron taraf ek nichi diwaar bani he. pragann par chat he jo 16 khambhon par sthit he, jinamein 12 to chaaron ore ghere main lage hai. shesh 4 madhya main hai. madhya ke chaaron stambh or tahkhaane ke saamne ke do stambh ghatpallabh style main bane hai. ghere main lage hue stambh shridhar style ke hai. madhya ke stambhon main se ek par baithe hue manushya kii akruti khudi he.
 tahkhaane ke saamne dai taraf ke stambh par do lekh khude hai. ek taraf kaa lekh spasht nahin he tathaa duusari taraf kaa lekh 1172 i0 kaa he tathaa iske upree bhaag main ek sree kii akruti bani hui he.
 morkhana jane ke liye nokha jo kii rajasthan ke bikaner jile se 45 kimi duri par he vahaan janaa padataa he uske baad nokha se koi saadhan karke yaa apane personal saadhan se kakda chauraahaa hote hue beraasar gaanv aata he waha se sidhe morkhana ke liye rasta jaataa he jahaan par khaane pine or thaharne ke liye sab suvidhaae he|
