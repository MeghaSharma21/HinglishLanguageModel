 neuyork nagar shiksha vibhaag neuyork shahar main nagarpalika sarkaar kii shakha he jiskaa uddeshy shahar kii public skool pranali kaa prabandhan karnaa he. yah sanyukt rajya america kii sabase badi skool pranali he, jiske antargat 1.1 million se adhik chaatr aate hai jo 1,700 vibhinn vidyalayon main shiksha praapt karte hai. vibhaag main neuyork shahar ke paancho boro shaamil hai.
 vibhaag kaa sanchaalan new york city schools chancelar karata he. nivartmaan kuladhipati denis em walkot hai, jinhone yah pad kathi blike ke pashchaat sambhala tha. blike ne keval 95 din ke andar hi apane pad se istipha de diya tha.
