 sthanik maan paddati yaa sthiti-chihn sankhyaon ko nirupit karne kii vah pranali jisamen kisi sanket kaa maan is baat par nirbhar karata he ki sankhya main us ank kaa sthaan kahan he. udhaaharan ke liye 325 main 5 kaa sthaneey maan paanch he kintu 523 main 5 kaa sthaneey maan 'paanch sau' he. is tarah sankhyaon ke nirupan kii yah paddati roman ank paddati aadi anya nirupan paddatiyon se bhinn he. sthaneey maan par aadhaarit sankhya nirupan se bahut si ankaganitiya sankriyaayein bahut saralata se kii jane lageen or is kaaran yah paddati sheeghra hi puure sansar main apana lee gayi. 
 aajkal sthaneey maan par aadhaarit bahut si paddatiyaan prachlit hai jinamein das aadhaar vaali hindu ank paddati sabase puraani or sarvaadhik prayukt paddati he. iske atirikt dwiadhari sankhya paddati, ashtadhari sankhya paddati tathaa shodashadhari sankhya paddati bhi prayukt hote hai .
 sthaneey maan paddati main dashamalav bindu kaa prayog karke bhinnaatmak sankhyaon ko bhi nirupit karne kii kshamta rakhti he. arthath yah paddati sabhi vaastavik sankhyaon ko nirupit karne kii kshamta rakhti he.

 jahaan a koi prakritik sankhya he. isko 'aadhaar' kehete hai.
