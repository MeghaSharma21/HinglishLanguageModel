 uttaradhikaar parishad yani yakseshan council, united kingdom kii ek paramparaagat samarohik nikaay he, jo shaasi raja yaa rani ke nidhan ke pashchaat, singhasana par uttaradhikaar ke baad, saint james pales main ekkrut hoti he, taaki singhasana ke uttaradhikari ke singhasana-virajan or nae shaasha ke raaj kii shruwaat kii aadhikarik ghoshnaa kii ja sake. varsh 1707 main paarit samaadhaan ke adhinium ke anusaar, kisi shaasha kii mrityu hone ke saath hi, uttaradhikaar ke niyamon ke anukool, unke vaidhik uttaradhikari, bina kisi samaaroh yaa aupchaarikta ke, turant hi nae shaasha ban jate hai. atah uttaradhikaar parishad hameshaa uttaradhikaar hone ke pashchaat sangathit hoti he, ek aadhikarik ghoshnaptr jari karne ke liye, jiske dwaara nae shaasha kii naam or pahchaan samet pushti kii jaati he, or puure praja ko is pushti or uttaradhikaar se avgat karaayaa jaataa he.
 is parishad main privi parshadgan, rajya mahadhikarigan, house of lords ke sadsygan, london shahar ke prabhu mahayor or aldermen, rashtramandal prajabhumiyon ke ucchayuktagan tathaa anya janasevakgan shaamil rahete hai. yah parishad uttaradhikaar kii aadhikarik ghoshnaa rachit karti he, jise landan or edinburgh samet united kingdom ke tamaam bade-chote shaharon ke mahatvapurn chauraahon or saarvajanik sthanon par saarvajanik rup se padhaa jaataa he. amuman, paramparatah is ghoshnaa ko sabase pehle saint james pales kii friyari court kii balcony se padhaa jaataa he. is ghoshnaa padhe jane ke din ko prativarsh, yakseshan de ke rup main manaaya jaataa he.
