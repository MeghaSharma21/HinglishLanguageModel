 jon prakaash raav janumala naamak andhra pradesh ke prakasham jile main san 1957 agast 14 ko janme, johny liver hindi film ke ek prasiddh hasya abhinayta he.
 johny liver ko film staron kii mimicri karne main maharath hasil thi. unki isi khaasiyat ne unhen stage sho karne kaa mauka diya. aise hi ek stage sho main sunil dutt kii unpar nazar padi. unhone johny liver ko film ‘dard kaa rishta’ main pehla brake mila or aaj yah silsilaa 350 se adhik filmon tak pahunch gaya he. ‘dard kaa rishta’ke baad vah ‘jalawaa’ main nasiruddin shah ke saath dekhe gaye, lekin unki pehali bady safalataa ‘baazeegar’ ke saath shuru hui. uske baad vah lagbhag ek sahaayak abhinayta ke rup main har film main hasya abhinayta ke role main dekhe gaye. unki pehali feechar film kabhi tamil ‘anabrikku allavillaai’ he. saath hi johny varsh 2007 main chote parde ke riyaltee sho main jaj ke rup main bhi dikhaai die.
