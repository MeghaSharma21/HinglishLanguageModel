 sangharshi vyanjan aisa vyanjan varna hota he jisamen munh ke do uccharan sthanon ko paas laakar ek bahut hi tang khol se hawa ko baahar dhaleka jaae. masalan nichle hont ko upar ke daant se jodne se "fa" kii dhwani yaa jihva ke pichle hisse ko munh kii chat ke pichley hisse se jodne se "kh" kii dhwani . isi tarah sha, sha, ji, ji or za bhi sangharshi vyanjan hai. sangharshi vyanjanon ki visheshta he ki unki dhwani ko vaayu-pravaah jari rakhkar lambe samay tak bina ruke shuddh rup se jari rakhaa ja saktaa he.
