 martin nodel ek america cartoonist or kalakaar the, jinhen superhiro green lantrn ke nirmaataa ke rup main janaa jaataa he. unke kuch kaam upnaam mart delan ke naam se bhi prakashit hue.
 philadelphia, pencylvania main janme nodel ke mata-pita yahudee apravasi the. unhone kala santhaan chicago main praarambhik shiksha praapt kii. 1930 ke dashak main vah neuyork shahar chale gaye, jahaan unhone prat institute main daakhila liya. nodel ne 1938 main ek freelansar ke rup main apana chitrakaari kariyar shuru kiya. 1940 main unhone ol-american publications ke ek sampadak sheldon meyar ke liye kuch kaam kiya, jo antat disi comics banane vaali teen companiyon main se ek he. 1940 main manahattan main ek subway stesan par unhone andheri patariyon ke upar laalaten laharaate ek trainman ko dekha, or phir richard vagner ke operetic ring chakra ke saath-saath chini lokgeeton or unani puranic kathaon ke tatwon se prerana lekar nayak alan scot kaa nirmaan kiya, jo aage jaakar pehla green lantrn bana. nodel ne 1947 main ol-american choda or 1930 ke dashak ke maarval comics ke agradoot 'timeli comics' main shaamil ho gaye, jahaan unhone captain america, human torch or sab-mariner kii kahaniyon ko chitrit kiya.
 9 disambar 2006 ko muskego, visconsin main prakritik kaarnon se unki mrityu ho gai, or 2011 main unhen maranoparant da vil isaner puraskaar ke haul of fame ke liye namit kiya gaya.
