 swatantra party, bharat kaa ek rajnatik dal tha jisaki sthapana chakravarti rajagopalaachari ne agast, 1959 main kii thi. is dal ne jawaharlal nehru kii samaajawaadee niti kaa virodh kiya or tathakthith "license-permit raaj" ko samaapt kar mukt arthvyavastha kii vakaalat kii. bharat kii us samay kii sthiti aisi thi ki durbhaagya se ise jameendaaron or udyogpatiyon kii hitaishi party mana gaya.
 swatantra party ne madhya pradesh, rajasthan or gujarat main acchhee safalataa praapt kii thi lekin pilu modi kii adhyakshata vaali is party ke bhartiya lokdal main vilay ke saath iskaa astitv khatm ho gaya.
