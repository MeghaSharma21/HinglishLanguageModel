 lahanda yaa lahanda punjabi bhashaa kii pashchimi upbhaashaon ke samooh ko kaha jaataa he. punjabi main 'lahanda' shabd kaa matlab 'pashchim' hota he, jis se in bhashaaon kaa yah naam padaa he - tulana ke liye hindi kii purvi upbhaashaon ko parmparik rup se aksar 'purbia' kaha jaataa he. lahanda samooh main hindko, pothohari or saraaiki jaisi kai upbhashaayen shaamil hai. saraaiki jaisi dakshini lahanda bhashaaon main kuch sindhi bhashaa se milte-julate lakshan hai jabaki hindko jaisi uttari lahanda bhashaaon main dardi bhashaaon se kuch milti-julati chizein milti hai.
