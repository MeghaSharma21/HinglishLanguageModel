 kaveri jha ek bhartiya film abhinetri he. ve hindi, malayalam, telugu, tamil filmon main kaary kar chuki he. kala vishay main upadhi grahan karne ke baad jha ne apane filmi kariyar kii shuruyaat bhul bhulaiya main 2007 se kii. kaveri air india main air hostess ke pad par bhi kaam kar chukin hai. san 2005 main kaveri ne femina miss india main sushri vyaktitv puraskaar se sammanit huin. dakshin bharat main kaveri jha apane swatantra vyavhar ke liye jaani jaati hai.
 kaveri kaa janm bihar ke sitamarhi main hua tha. unke pita ek sarkari karmchaaree the. kaveri bachapan main ladakon jaise rahati thi, unhone apane pita kii parshvabhumi ko apane parivaar ke rahan sahan par behad prabhavi bataaya. 
 sushri vyaktitv puraskaar praapt karne ke kaveri ne modelling se apane kariyar kii shruwaat kii. sajid khan ke netritv main unhone tivi sho kii mejabani bhi kii hai. iske alaava unki anya filmon main hazjaik, flats, bam bam bole or jail jaisin filmein shaamil hai. iske baad unhone kai vigyapanon main bhi ullekhneey kaary kiya he. kaveri ne dakshin bhartiya cinema jagat main sabhi filmon main bataur abhinetri se apni shuruyaat kii he. abhinay ke liye unhen samikshakon dwaara behad saraahaa gaya he. unhone aage chalakar bhinn-bhinn prakaar ke kirdaar adaa kiye va unke abhinay va kirdaaron ne hindi film abhinetriyon kii ek nai kalpana ko janm diya. aaj ke samay main kaveri jha dakshin bhartiya cinema ke safal abhinetriyon main gini jaatin hai.
