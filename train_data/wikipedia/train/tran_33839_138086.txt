 raju ban gaya jentalman 1992 main bani hindi bhashaa kii hasya premakahani film he. shahrukh khan, juhi chawla, naanaa patekar or amrutha singh abhinit is film ke adhikaar shahrukh kii red chiles antertenment ke swaamitv main hai.
 raaj maathur darjeeling se ek yuva ejineering hai jo ek bada ejeeniyar banane ke liye bombai aata he. bombai main, vah ek dur ke rishtedaar kii talash main nichle madhyam varg ke ilaake main aata he. pataa chalata he ki usne kai saal pehle hi jagah chhod di he. vah raat ek mandir main bitaata he, jahaan vah ek darshanik sadakon ke kalakaar jai se miltaa he, jo ek kareebi dost ban jaataa he or use rahane ke liye ek jagah detaa he.
 bina kisi sampark or anubhav ke, use shahar main naukari paane main kathinaayee hoti he. ek sundar ladki renu, use nirmaan company ke saath prashikshu ke rup main naukari dilaati he. jahaan vah chhabdiya ke sachiv ke rup main kaam karti he. ve antat ek-duusare ke saath pyaar main pad jate hai.
 jaise hi vah safal ho jaataa he, vah chhabdiya kii beti sapana ke dhyaan main aata he. ve ek saath adhik se adhik samay bitaate hai or jald hi vah amiron kii or glamras jeevanstyli main aa jaataa he. sapana raju ke saath pyaar main pad jaati he, lekin jab use pataa chalaa ki vah renu se pyaar karata he, to uskaa dil tut jaataa he.
 is bich, raju ke dushman uske khilaaf sajeesh kar rahe the or ve raju kii dekharekh vaale ek pul ko gira dete hai. use dosha mil jaataa he or use jald hi yah mahasoos hota he ki amiron kii glamras duniya vah nahin chahta he. vah or renu aakhirkaar phir se mil jate hai.
 film kii kahani raaj kapur kii shri 420 par aadhaarit he.
 sangeet jatin lalit kii sangeetkaar jodi dwaara diya gaya he.
