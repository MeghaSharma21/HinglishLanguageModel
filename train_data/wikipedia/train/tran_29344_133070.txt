 time america saptahik samachar patrika he jiskaa prakaashan new york shahar se hota he. isaki sthapana 1923 main hui thi or kai dashakon tak is par henry lus kaa prabhutv rahaa. time ke vishwa main kai vibhinn sanskaran prakashit hote hai. europea sanskaran time europe kaa prakaashan landan se hota he or yah madhya purv, africa or 2003 se latin america ko cover karata he. asian sanskaran time asia hong cong se sanchalit hota he. dakshin prashanth sanskaran sidney main aadhaarit he or isamen australia va newzealand sahit prashanth mahaasaagar ke dweep samooh cover kiye jate hai. 2008 main time ne apana canada main sthapit vigyapandaata sanskaran band kar diya tha.
 saptahik samachar patrikaon kii shrenee main time kaa sanchalan vishwa main sarvaadhik he. varsh 2014 ke anusaar, iskaa kul sanchalan 3,281,557 pratiyon kaa he, jo ise america kii sarvaadhik sanchalan vaali sabhi patrikaon main dasavaan, tathaa saptahik patrikaon main doosraa sthaan detaa he. 
 may 2006 se oktober 2013 tak is patrika ke prabandh sampadak richard stungal the, jiske baad se nansi gibs is pad par hai.
