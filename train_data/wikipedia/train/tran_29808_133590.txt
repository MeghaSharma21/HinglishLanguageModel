 mahmud tululaq dilli ke tululaq vansh kaa antim sultan tha.uske rajyakaal main anwarat sangharsh chalate rahe or duravastha apni charam seemaa par pahunch gayi.mahmud tululaq ke samay tak dilli sultanate se dakshin bharat, bengal, khandesh, gujarat, malwa, rajasthan, bundelkhand aadi praant swatantra gayi the.mahmud tululaq ke samay main malik sarwar naam ke ek hijde ne sultan se ‘malik-us-rshak’ kii oopaadhi grahan kar jonpur main swatantra rajya kii sthapana kii.mahmud tululaq kaa shashan is samay dilli se palam tak hi simit rah gaya tha.is samay nasarat shah tululaq evam mahmud tululaq ne ek saath shashan kiya.mahmud tululaq ne dilli se tathaa nusrat shah ne firozabad se apane shashan kaa sanchaalan kiya.mahmud tululaq ke samay main taimur langs ne 1398 i. main dilli par akraman kiya. ek pair se langadaa hone ke kaaran uskaa naam ‘taimur langs’ pad gaya tha.taimur ke akraman se darkar donon sultan rajdhani se bhaag gayi.15 din tak dilli main rahane ke pashchaat taimur waapas chalaa gaya or khizr khaan ko apane vijit pradeshon kaa rajyapal niyukt kiya.ek manyata ke anusaar taimur akraman ke baad dilli sultanate kaa vistaar simat kar palam tak hi rah gaya tha.taimur ke waapas jane ke pashchaat mahmud tululaq ne apane vajeer mallu imbar kii sahaayta se pun: dilli singhaasana par adhikaar kar liya, par kaalaantar main mallu ikbaal multan ke soobedaar khizr khaan se yuddh karte hue maaraa gaya.mallu ikbaal ke marane ke baad sultan ne dilli kii satta ek afakhaan sardaar daulat khaan lodi ko saunp di.1412 i. main mahmud tululaq kii mrityu ho gai.1413 i. main dilli singhaasana ke liye daulat khaan lodi evam khizr khaan ne dilli kii gaddi par adhikaar kar ek naye rajvansh ‘syed vansh’ kii sthapana kii.
