 sangheey kshetra 
 pakistan cap rashtriya list e cricket pakistan ke toornament he, or 4 praanton or rajdhani kshetra se 5 tiimon ne chunaav ladaa he. yah purv list e cricket toornament panchkona trophy badal diya.
 pakistan gharelu sanrachana main parivartan har do saal main hone vaali ke saath dashakon ke liye asangat kiya gaya he. is kaaran ve ab apane gharelu circuit ke punargathan or pakistan cap namit nayaa toornament he jo panchkona trophy kii jagah legaa shuru kar rahe hai.
 har team main ek 15 sadasyeey team kii ghoshnaa kii prati-captain or 150 khilaadiyon ke masaude se coach dwaara uthaaya ja rahaa hoga.
