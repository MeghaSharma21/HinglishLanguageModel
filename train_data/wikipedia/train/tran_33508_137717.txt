 shivnath nadi mahanadi kii pramukh sahaayak nadi he. yah rajanandgaanv jile kii ambagarh tehsil kii 625 meter unchi panabaras pahari kshetra se nikalakar shivarinarayan ke paas mahanadi main mil jaati he. isaki pramukh sahaayak nadiyaan lilagar, maniyari, agar, haanp, khaarun, arapa, aamner, sakari, kharkhara, taandula tathaa jamuniya nadi aadi hai. isaki kul lambaai 290 kimi he. prasiddh mongra bairaj pariyojna rajanandgaanv main shivnath nadi par sanchalit he. shivnath nadi ke kuch bhaagon main sirf varsha ke samay hi naaven chalati hai. or kahin-kahin kuch bhaagon main july se farvari tak naaven chalati hai.

 balaudabazar- jamuniya
 kawardha- hof, aamner
 mungeli- maniyari
 bilaspur- arappa, lilagar
