 shailesh lodha kaa janm 8 navambar 1970 ko rajasthan ke sirohi main hua. ve hindi ke ek pratishthit hasya kavi hai. sab tivi ke lokapriy dharawahik tarak mehta kaa ulta chashma ke mukhya kirdaar tarak mehta kii bhoomika main prasiddh hai. hasya kaviyon ke sabase lokapriy kaarykram vaah-vaah kya baat he kaa sanchaalan bhi inaki pramukh uplabdhiyon main se ek he.
