 india get,, nai dilli ke rajpath par sthit 43 meter unchaa vishaal dwaar he. yah swatantra bharat kaa rashtriya smarak he, jise purv main kingsve kaha jaataa tha. iskaa dizin sar edward lutiyans ne taiyaar kiya tha. yah smarak paris ke arq de troym se prerit he. ise san 1931 main banaaya gaya tha. mul rup se akhil bhartiya yuddh smarak ke rup main jane vaale is smarak kaa nirmaan angrej shaskaon dwaara un 90000 bhartiya sainikon kii smriti main kiya gaya tha jo british sena main bhartee hokar pratham vishwayuddh or afakhaan yudhon main shahid hue the. united kingdom ke kuch sainikon or adhikariyon sahit 13,300 sainikon ke naam, get par utkeern hai. laal or pile balua pattharon se bana hua yah smarak darshneey he. 
 jab india get banakar taiyaar hua tha tab iske saamne george pancham kii ek murti lagi hui thi. jise baad main british raaj ke samay kii anya murtiyon ke saath coronaton park main sthapit kar diya gaya. ab george pancham kii murti kii jagah pratik ke rup main keval ek chhatari bhar rah gayi he.
 bharat kii swatantrata ke pashchaat india get bhartiya sena ke agyaat sainikon ke makbare kii site maatr banakar rah gaya he. isaki mehrab ke neeche amar jawan jyoti sthapit kar di gayi he. anaam sainikon kii smriti main yahan ek ricel ke upar sainik kii topi sajaa di gayi he jiske chaaro konon par sadaiv ek jyoti jalati rahati he. is amar jawan jyoti par prati varsh pradhan mantri va teenon senadhyaksh pushp chakra chadhaakar apni shradhanjali arpit karte hai. india get kii deevaaron par hajaaron shahid sainikon ke naam khude hai or sabase upar angreji main likhaa haih
 dilli kii kai mahatvapurn sadaken india get ke konon se nikalati hai. raat ke samay yahan meley jaisaa maahaul hota he.
 1920 ke dashak tak, puraani dilli railway stesan puure shahar kaa ekmaatr railway stesan hua karata tha. agra-dilli railway line us samay lutian kii dilli or kingsve yani raajaaon ke guzarane kaa rasta, jise ab hindi main rajpath naam de diya gaya he, par sthit vartmaan india get ke nirmaan-sthal se hokar guzarati thi. aakhirkaar is railway line ko yamuna nadi ke paas sthaanantarit kar diya gaya. taduparant san 1924 main jab yah maarg prarambh hua tab kahin jaakar smarak sthal kaa nirmaan shuru ho sakaa.
 42 meter unche india get se hokar kai mahatvapurn maarg nikalate hai. pehle india get ke aasapaas hokar kaaphi yatayaat gujarata tha. parantu ab ise baari vaahanon ke liye band kar diya gaya he. shaam ke samay jab smarak ko prakashit kiya jaataa he tab india get ke chaaro ore evam rajpath ke donon ore ghaas ke maidaanon main logon kii baari bheed ekatra ho jaati he. 625 meter ke vyas main sthit india get kaa shatbhujiya kshetra 306,000 varg meter ke kshetrafal main failaa he.
 india get ke saamne sthit vah chhatari abhi bhi jyon kii tyon he. is chhatari ke neeche kisi jamaane main george pancham kii bhavy murti hua karti thi. bharat ke azaad hone ke baad us murti ko sarkaar ne vahaan se hataa kar coronaton park main sthapit kar diya. 
 prati varsh ganatantr diwas par nikalne vaali parade raashtrapati bhawan se shuru hokar india get se hote hue laal kile tak pahunchati he.
