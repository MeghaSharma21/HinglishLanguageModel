 vinod raina
 vinod raina ek bhartiya shikshavid the. wo pratham vyakti the jo shiksha kaa adhikaar vidheyak kaa masauda taiyaar kiya tha.
 vinod raina ne dilli vishvavidhyalay se bhautik vigyaan main piechdi kii. unhone bharat main shiksha sudhaaron par kaam karne ke liye dilli vishvavidhyalay main apni naukari se tyaagptr de diya. wo bharat gyaan vigyaan samiti kii kaaryakarini samiti ke sadsy sahit akhil bhartiya janavaadee vigyaan network ke sah sansthaapak main se ek the.
 chaar varshon tak cancer se jujhane ke baad shikshavid vinod raina kaa 12 sitambar 2013 ko nidhan ho gaya.
