 2002 vah varsh tha jab dravid ne tendulkar kii chaaya se ubharna shuru kiya or apane aap ko bharat ke pramukh test ballebaaj ke rup main sthapit kar liya. april ke maheene main, jorge toun, west indies main shrinkhalaa ke pehle test mech main, unhone naabad 144 ran banaae isaki pehali pari main marvin dillan kii delivery par out ho gaye the. baad main usee varsh main unhone england or west indies ke khilaaf lagaataar chaar shatak lagaaye.
 agast 2002, main, england ke khilaaf hedingle stadium, leeds main shrinkhalaa ke tisare match main pehali pari main 148 kaa score bana kar unhone bharat ko prasidd jeet dilaayi. unhen is pradarshan ke liye main of di match kaa puraskaar mila. england ke khilaaf chaar test match kii shrinkhalaa main kul 602 ran bana kar unhone main of di siries kaa puraskaar bhi jeeta.
 2003- 2004 ke daure main, dravid ne teen dohare shatak lagaaye, newjiland, australia or pakistan prayek ke khilaaf ek ek. australia ke khilaaf edilade main chaar match kii shrinkhalaa ke duusare match main, dravid or vivies lakshmana kii ballebaaji jodi australia par baari padi, australia ke vishaal 556 ke score ke jawaab main bharat ne 4 wicket par keval 85 ran banaae the jab is jodi ne haath milaayaa. jab unki saajhedaari ko todaa gaya tab tak ve 303 ran bana chuke the. lakshmana 148 par out ho gaya lekin dravid ne apni pari ko jari rakhte hue 233 ran banaae. us samay, yah videsh main kisi bhartiya ke dwaara banaaya gaya adhiktam vyaktigat score tha. jis samay dravid waapas lautaa tab bharat ko australia kii pehali pari ke score tak pahunchne ke liye 33 ran or chahie the. iske baad dravid ne duusari pari main bahut zyada dabaav ke chalate naabad 72 ran bana kar ek prasidd vijay dilaai. dravid ne australia ke khilaaf chaar match kii shrinkhalaa main, 103.16 ke ausat par 619 ran kaa score banaaya or main of di siries kaa puraskaar jeeta. is daure ke baad ke bhaag main, dravid ne ganguli kii anupasthiti main, multan main pehle test match main bharat ko pakistan par vijay dilaayi. rawalpindi main is shrinkhalaa ke tisare or final match main, dravid ne adwitiya 270 kaa score bana kar bharat ko pakistan par ek aitihaasik jeet dilaayi.

 ek majboot takanic ke saath, vah bhartiya cricket team ke liye ridh kii haddi sabit hue. unki praarambhik chhavi ek rakshaatmak ballebaaj kii ban gai thi jise keval test cricket tak hi simit hona chahie, unhen ek divasiy antarrashtriya maichon se hataa diya gaya kyonki unki ran banane kii gati bahut dhimi thi.
 haalanki, apane karier main ve lagaataar ekadivasiy maichon main ran banane lage, unhen varsh ke isisi khilaadi kaa puraskaar bhi mila.
 unka upnaam 'da wal' ribok vigyapanon main ab unki sthirataa ko hi bataata he. dravid ne 55.11 ke ausat ke saath test cricket main 26 shatak banaaye hai, jinamein 5 dohare shatak shaamil hai. ek divasiy maichon main haalanki unka ausat 39.49 kaa rahaa he or ran rate 71.22 kii rahi he. ve aise kuch hi bharatiyon main se ek hai jo ghar ke bajaay baahar adhik ausat banaate hai, bhartiya pich ke mukaable main videshi pich par unka ausat 10 ran adhik kaa rahata he.
 9 agast 2006 ko videshi test main dravid kaa ausat 65.28 rahaa jabake unka kul ausat 55.4 tha. or unka ekadivasiy ke alaava ausat 42.03 he jabaki ekadivasiy kaa ausat 39.49 he.

 dravid ab puri tarah ek ballebaaj he, jinhone 1 janvari 2000 ke baad se khele gaye maichon main 63.51 kaa ausat banaaya he.
 dravid ekadivasiy maichon main sabase zyada bhagidaari ke do maichon main shaamil the: saurav ganguli ke saath 318 ran kaa saajha, yah pehla joda tha jisane 300 ran kii saajhedaari kii or uske baad sachin tendulkar ke saath 331 ran kii saajhedaari jo vartmaan vishwa record he.
