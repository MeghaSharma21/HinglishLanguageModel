 vidisha jilabhartiya rajya madhya pradesh kaa ek jila he. jile kaa mukhyalay vidisha he. yah vindhyachal pathaar par mukhya vindhyachal parvatamala main sthit he, jo uttar or uttar-purv kii ore failaa he. pathaar uttar kii ore dhalaan or yah kai nadiyon se ghira he. adhikansh vidisha betwa nadi kii ghati main sthit he jo dakshin se uttar main bahati he. is ghati kii seemaa purv main gadhi-tenda range or pashchim main ganiri-raghogarh range dwaara kii gai he. in donon shreniyon main malwa pathaar par vindhyachal kii simaaon kaa hissaa banataa he or dakshin se uttari tak kaa vistaar hota he.
 saansad-shrimati sushamaa swaraj
 vidhayak-kalyaan sinh thakur
