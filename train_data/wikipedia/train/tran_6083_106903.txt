 uttari sweden main aahakkaa parvat
 scandinavian pahaad yaa scandes uttari europe ke scandinavian prayadweep par sthit ek parvatamala he. haalanki yah pahadiyaan adhik unchi nahin hai phir bhi kuch sthanon par yah achaanak bahut gahari khaiyaan prastut karti hai. visheshkar pashchimi chor main inaki uttari sagar ke saath lagi khaiyaan 'fyord' ke naam se vishwaprasiddh hai. yah parvatamala 1,700 kimi tak chalati he or 320 kimi chaudee he.
