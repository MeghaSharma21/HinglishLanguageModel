 ajanta express bhartiya rail kii ek mahatvapurn rail seva hai, jo sikanderabad se manmaad ke bich chalati hai. manmaad, maharashtra ke nasik jile main padataa hai 
 ajanta express, purvi maharashtra evam uttar pashchimi telugana kaa ek pratishthit train hai. isko kacheguda express bhi kaha jaataa hai. yah kacheguda-nizamabad-nanded-aurangabad maarg par yatra karne vaale logon dwaara bahut baar kacheguda express bhi kaha jaataa he, jo purv main hyderabad-godavari ghati railway dwaara sanchalit hoti thi. is train kii shuruyaat meter gej train ke taur par kacheguda railway stesan se manmaad ke bich kii gayi thi, jo nizamabad, nanded evam aurangabad se hokar guzarati thi. shuruyaat ke kuch dinon ke baad se hi ye yah train atyant lokapriy ho gayi thi kyunki manmaad jankshan 1,676 mili meter broad gej hone chalate log yahan se anya gantavyon ke liye yahan se chalane vaali broadgej train pakadte the.
 manmaad se parabhani ke bich kaa maarg jab 1992-95 ke bich jab project unigez ke tahat broad gej main parivartit hua. tab 1995-96 main kacheguda evam manmaad ke bich chalane vaali train bidar, parabhani hokar hi jaati thi kyunki tab tak vikarabad or parabhani ke bich ke maarg ko broadgej main parivartit kar diya gaya tha.
 2007 main yah train apane usee meter gej root nizamabad, mudkhed evam purna, jo 2002-2003 main hi broad gej main parivartit ho gayi thi waapas daudane lagi. 1967 isvi main ajanta express, 42.5 kilometre/ghante kii raftaar se meter gej par chalane vaali sabase tej train thi.
 aurangabad ke nikat ajanta kii gufaaon ke aadhaar par is train kaa naam ajanta express rakhaa gaya hai.
