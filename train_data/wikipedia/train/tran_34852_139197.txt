 
 banavaari laal joshi
 banavaari laal joshi bharat ke kai rajyon ke uparajyapal evam rajyapal rah chuke hai. ye uttarakhand ke rajyapal rahe oktober 2007 se. isse purv ye dilli ke bhi uparajyapal rah chuke hai, 2004 se 2007 ke bich. phir ye meghalaya ke rajyapal bane 2007 main.
 joshi ji kaa janm rajasthan ke nagaur main choti khatu naamak gram main hua tha. ye dilli ke attharahawein rajyapal bane. inhone apana snaatak kolkata ke scottish carch kalege se kiya. uske uparaant vishvavidhyalay vidhi mahavidhyalay, kolkata se vidhi main snaatak hue.
