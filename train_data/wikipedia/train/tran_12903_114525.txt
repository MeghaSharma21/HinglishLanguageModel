 jaltapiya chhidr prithvi yaa anya kisi grah par upasthit aisa vidar chhidr hota he jis se bhutapiya sroton se garam kiya gaya jal ugalta he. yah aksar sakriya jwalamukheey kshetron, bhugolic takhto ke alag hone vaale sthanon or mahaasaagar droniyon jaise sthanon par milte hai. jaltapiya chhidron ke astitv kaa mul kaaran prithvi kii bhuvaigyanik sakriyata or uski sataha va bhitari bhaagon main pani kii baari maatra main upasthiti he.
 jab jaltapiya chhidr bhoomi par hote hai to unhen garam chashmon, foomarolon or ushnotson ke rup main dekha jaataa he. jab ve mahasaagaron ke farsh par hote hai to unhen kaale dhuaandaari or shwet dhuaandaari ke rup main paaya jaataa he. gahare samudr ke baaqi kshetra kii tulana main kaale dhuaandaariyon ke aasapaas aksar bacteria or orchia jaise sookshmjivon kii bharmaar hoti he. yah jeev in chhidron main se nikal rahe rasayanon ko khaakar urja banaate hai or jivit rahete hai or phir aage aise jeevon ko grass banane vaale anya jeevon ke samooh bhi yahan panpate hai. mana jaataa he ki brihaspati grah ke europa chandramaa or shani grah ke enceleds chandramaa par bhi sakriya jaltapiya chhidr maujuud hai or ati-prachinkaal main yah sambhavtah mangal grah par bhi rahe hon.
