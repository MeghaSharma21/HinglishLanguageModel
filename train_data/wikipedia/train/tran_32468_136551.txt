 adhyayan ke aadhaar par, eks-re, jaanch karne kaa ek apekshaakrit surakshit tarika he or iskaa vikiran anaavaran yaa jokhim apekshaakrit kam he. haalanki, prayogtmak or mahamari vigyaan ke aankade is prastaav kaa samarthan nahin karte hai ki vikiran kii ek seemaa rekha vaali maatra se neeche cancer kaa zyada khatra nahin rahata he. naidanik eks-re kaa pariman, maanav-nirmit or vishwavyaapee prakritik sansadhano ke kul vaarshik vikiran anaavaran kaa 14% he. anumaan he ki 75 varsh kii aayu tak ke logon main atirikt vikiran se cancer hone ke sanchayi jokhim main 0.6-1.8% kii vruddhi hogi. avashoshit vikiran kii maatra, eks-re pareekshan ke prakaar or usamen bhaag lene vaale sharir ke ang par nirbhar karti he. siti or pratideeptidarshan, saadhaaran eks-re kaa istemaal karne ke bajaay vikiran kii atyadhik maatra ko aavashyak bana dete hai.
 vardhit jokhim ko pariprekshya main rakhane ke liye, seene kaa ek saamaanya eks-re yaa dant chikitsiya eks-re, prishthbhumi vikiran se ek samaan maatra ke liye ek vyakti kaa anaavaran karegaa jiske prati ham 10 dinon kii samyavadhi main pratidin anaavruth hote hai. aisi prayek eks-re sampurna jivan-kaal main cancer ke jokhim main 1 prati 1,000,000 se bhi kam kii vruddhi karegi. ek udar yaa seene kaa siti, prishthbhumi vikiran ke 2-3 varshon ke baraabar hoga, jo 1 prati 10,000 or 1 prati 1,000 ke bich jeevanaparyant cancer jokhim main vruddhi karegaa. ye sankhya hamaare jeevankaal ke dauraan kisi bhi cancer ke viksit hone kii lagbhag 40% sambhaavna kii tulana main bahut kam he.

 eks-re ke khojakarta kaa shrey aam taur par german bhautikashastri wilhem rontagan ko diya jaataa he kyonki unhone hi sabase pehle vyavasthit rup main inkaa adhyayan kiya tha, haalanki inke prabhaavon ko dekhne vaale vah pehle vyakti nahin hai. "eks-re" ke rup main unka namakaran bhi unhone hi kiya he, haalanki unki khoj ke baad kai dashakon tak kuch log unhen "rontagan kirnon" ke rup main sanderbhit karte the.
 pehali baar naliyon main nirmit cathod kirnon arthath urjavan electron punjon kii chaanbin karne vaale vaigyanikon ne 1875 ke aasapaas crukes nalee naamak prayogtmak visarjan naliyon se eks-re ko nikalate hue dekha tha. crukes naliyaan, kuch kilovolt or 100 kevi ke bich kahin bhi ek ucch disi voltage dwaara nalee main avshisht hawa ke ynikaran ke dwaara mukt electronon kaa nirmaan karti thim. yah voltage kaaphi ucch veg se cathod se aate hue electronon kii gati ko badha dete the jisase ve nalee kii kaanch kii diwaar yaa anode se takaraate samay eks-re kaa nirmaan karte the. kai aarambhik crukes naliyaan beshak eks-re ko vikeern karti thim, kyonki aarambhik shodhakartaaon ne un prabhaavon ko dekha tha jinka shrey unhen diya ja saktaa tha, jiskaa vistrut vivaran neeche diya gaya he. wilhem rontagan ne hi sabase pehle 1895 main unka vyavasthit rup se adhyayan kiya tha.
 evan pului, wiliam crukes, johaan wilhem hitorf, uzen goldstin, henrich hertz, philip lenard, harman won helmholtz, nicola testla, thomas edison, charles glover barkla, maiks won loe or wilhem conred rontagan kii ginati eks-re ke pramukh aarambhik shodhakartaaon main kii jaati he.
 crukes nalee ke ek sah-avishkaarak or aarambhik shodhakarta ke rup main jane jane vaale german bhautikashastri johaan hitorf ne jab nalee ke paas anaavruth photographic platon ko rakhaa, tab unhone dekha ki chaaya dwaara unamem se kuch platon main daraaren pad gayi, haalanki unhone is prabhaav kii chaanbin nahin kii. 
 ukraine main janme or uniwarsity of vienna main prayogtmak bhautikee ke vyaakhyaata ke rup main karyrat pului ne vaikum discharge tub ke gunon kii jaanch karne ke liye unke anek dizine taiyaar kiye. praha polytechnic main professor ke pad par hui niyukti ke baad bhi unhone apana kaary jari rakhaa or 1886 main unhone dekha ki naliyon se nikalne vaale padaarthon ke saamne aane par cield photographic plates dark ho gaye. 1896 ke aarambhik daur main, rontagan dwaara apni pehali eks-re tasviir prakashit karne ke bas kuch saptaah baad, pului ne paris or landan kii patrikaon main ucch-gunvattta vaale eks-re chitron ko prakashit kiya. haalanki pului ne varsh 1873 se 1875 tak strasburg vishvavidhyalay main rontagan ke saath adhyayan kiya tha, lekin unke jeevani lekhak gaida kaa daawa he ki unka parvarti shodh swatantra rup se kiya gaya tha.
 april 1887 main, nicola testla ne ucch voltage or khud dizin kii gai naliyon ke saath-saath crukes naliyon kaa upyog karke eks-re kii jaanch kerani shuru kii. unke takneekee prakashnon se is baat kaa sanket miltaa he ki unhone ek vishesh ekal electrode eks-re nalee kaa avishkaar or vikaas kiya tha jo anya eks-re naliyon se alag thi jinamein koi lakshya electrode nahin hota tha. testla ke upkaran ke peeche ke siddhant ko bramlestrolang prakriya ke naam se janaa jaataa he, jisamen padaarth se hote hue aaweshit kanon ke guzarane par ek ucch-urja dwitiyak eks-re utsarjan kii utpatti hoti he. 1892 tak testla ne aise kai prayog kiye, lekin unhone in utsarjanon ko vargeekrut nahin kiya jinhen baad main eks-re ke naam se janaa gaya. testla ne is ghatana ko "adrishy" prakaar kii vikiran urja ke rup main samanyikrut kiya. testla ne neuyork aikadmi of sciences ke saamne apane 1897 ke eks-re vyaakhyaan main vibhinn prayogon ke vishay main apane tareekon ke tathyon kaa varnan kiya. iske alaava isi vyaakhyaan main testla ne eks-re upkaran ke nirmaan or surakshit sanchaalan kii vidhi kaa varnan kiya. vaikum ucch kshetra utsarjan dwaara unke eks-re prayog ke madhyam se unhone eks-re anaavaran se jude jaivik khataron se vaigyanik samudaay ko sachet bhi kiya.
 stenford vishvavidhyalay ke bhautikee ke buniyadi profesar furnando senford ne 1891 main eks-re ko utpann kiya or unka pataa lagaaya. 1886 se 1888 tak unhone berlin kii harman helmholtz prayogshaala main adhyayan kiya tha, jahaan ve vaikum naliyon main utpann hone vaale cathod kirnon se parichit hue jab alag-alag electrode se ek voltage pravaahit kiya gaya jaisaa ki henrich hertz or philip lenard ne iske pehle iskaa adhyayan kiya tha. da physical rivyuu ko 6 janvari 1893 ko likhe gaye unke patr ko vidhivat prakashit kiya gaya or san francisco igzamnar main vidout lens or lit, photographs teken wyeth plate and object in darkeness naamak ek lekh chapa gaya.
 henrich hertz kaa philip lenard naamak ek chaatr yah dekhnaa chahta tha ki cathod kirane, hawa main crukes nalee se hokar guzar sakati hai yaa nahin. usne ek crukes nalee kaa nirmaan kiya jiske ant main ek "khidki" thi jo patali aluminium se bani hui thi jiske saamne kaa hissaa cathod kii taraf tha taaki cathod kirane isse takaraa sake. unhone dekha ki usamen se kuch niklaa jiske photographic platon ke prati anaavruth kii vajah se pratidipti utpann hui. unhone vibhinn padaarthon ke madhyam se in kirnon kii bhedan shakti ko mapa. isse yah soochna mili he ki inamen se kam se kam kuch "lenard kirane" vastav main eks-re thim.harman won helmholtz ne eks-re ke ganitiya samikarnon ko sutarabaddh kiya. rontagan kii khoj or ghoshnaa se pehle unhone ek prakirnan siddhant kii kalpana kii. ise prakaash ke vidyut chumbakeey siddhant ke aadhaar par taiyaar kiya gaya tha. haalanki, unhone vaastavik eks-re ke saath kaam nahin kiya.
 8 navambar 1895 ko lenard or crukes naliyon ke saath prayog karte samay german bhautikee professor wilhem rontagan kaa saamana eks-re se hua or unhone in par adhyayan karnaa shuru kar diya. unhone "on e new kind of re: e priliminary communication " naamak ek aarambhik report taiyaar kiya or 28 disambar 1895 ko unhone is report ko vurzaberg ke physical-medical socity patrika ko saunp diya. eks-re par likhaa gaya yah pehla shodh-patr tha. rontagan ne yah suchit karne ke liye vikiran ko "eks" ke rup main sanderbhit kiya ki yah ek agyaat prakaar kaa vikiran tha. yah naam isse jud gaya, haalanki unke kai sahyogiyon ne un kirnon kaa namakaran rontagan kirnon ke rup main karne kaa suzav diya tha. unhen abhi bhi german sahit kai bhashaaon main isi tarah ke naam se sanderbhit kiya jaataa he. rontagan ko apni khoj ke liye bhautikee main pratham nobel puraskaar praapt hua.
 unki khoj ke vivaranon ko lekar kaaphi matbhed hai kyonki rontagan apni maut ke samay apane lab nots jala die the, lekin yah unke jeevani lekhakon kii managasaint kahani ho sakati he: rontagan, ek crukes nalee or barium platinocynide se paint kiye gaye ek pratideept parade ke saath cathod kirnon kii chaanbin main lage hue the jise unhone ek kaale karboard main lapet diya tha taaki nalee se nikalne vaali drishy roshni koi hastkshep na kare. unhone lagbhag 1 meter duuri par parade se ek dhundhlee hari chamak nikalati hui dekhi. unhen laga ki nalee se aane vaali kuch adrishy kirane cardboard se hokar guzar rahi thi jisase paradaa chamakane laga tha. unhone paaya ki ve unki mej par rakhi kitabon or kaagajon se hokar bhi guzar sakati thi. rontagan ne turant vyavasthit rup se in agyaat kirnon kii chaanbin shuru kar di. apni praarambhik khoj ke do maheene baad, unhone apana shodh-patr prakashit kiya.
