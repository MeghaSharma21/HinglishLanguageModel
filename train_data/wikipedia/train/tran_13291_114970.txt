 sanglakh hindu kush parvat shrunkhlaa kii ek shakha he. is se afghanistan kii do mahatvapurn nadiyaan - helmand nadi or kabul nadi - utpann hoti hai. sanglakh shrunkhlaa ke sab se pramukh darre kaa naam 'unai darra' yaa 'unai kotal' he.
 hindi ke 'lakh' shabd ko pashto main bindu vaale 'kh' kii dhwani ke saath bolaa jaataa he, jo bindu-rahit 'kha' se zaraa alag he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he. 'sanglakh' ek sanyukt shabd he - sang kaa arth farsi main "pathar" hota he, yani 'sanglakh' kaa matlab he 'lakhon pattharon vaala' yaa 'patthariilaa'. dhyaan rahe ke farsi kaa "sang" shabd avastai bhashaa ke mul "asan" shabd se aayaa he. kyonki sanskrit ore farsi donon hind-irani bhashaa parivaar kii bahane hai isliye thik yahi sajaateey shabd sanskrit main bhi "ashn" rup main miltaa he ore rugveda main prayogit he. iskaa arth bhi "pathar" he.
