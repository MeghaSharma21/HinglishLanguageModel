 mongol lipi, jise uigurgin bhi kehete hai, mongol bhashaa ko likhane kii sarvapratham lipi or varnmaala thi. yah uigur bhashaa ke liye prayog hone vaali pracheen lipi ko lekar viksit kii gai thi or bahut arase tak mongol bhashaa likhane ke liye sab se mahatvapurn lipi kaa darja rakhti thi. san 1646 main roosi prabhaav se mongol likhane ke liye sirilik lipi kaa istemaal shuru ho gaya or dhire-dhire mongol lipi kaa prayog khatm hota chalaa gaya. mul rup se mongol lipi main shabdon ko upar se neeche likhaa jaataa tha, lekin aadhunik yug main isko aksar baayein-se-daayen likhaa jane laga he. kuch anya bhashaaon ne bhi mongol lipi ko lekar us par apni lipiyon ko aadhaarit kiya. isaki ek badi misaal manchhu bhashaa he jisaki manchhu lipi isi mongol lipi par aadhaarit thi. iske alaava shibe, oirat or avenki ne bhi apni lipiyaan mongol lipi se banaai.
 mongolon se pehle naiman log uigur lipi kaa prayog pehle se kar rahe the. san 1204 main naimanon ke saath mongolon ke yuddh kiya or usamen tartar-tonga naam ke ek uigur lekhak ko bandi bana liya jo naiman sarkaar main kar-vasuuli vibhaag chalaane kii zimmedaari rakhata tha. usee ne mongolon ko uigur lipi se avgat karaayaa. kuch sainkadon saalon tak lipi main koi parivartan nahin hua, haalanki yah lipi mongol dhvaniyon ko puri tarah dikhne main sakasham nahin thi. phir dhire-dhire is lipi main mongol bhashaa ke liye uchit nae aksharon ko laayaa gaya. mongolon ne puraani lipi ko is nai lipi se alag bataane ke liye use 'uigurgin' kaa naam de diya.
 mongolia main or china ke bhitari mongolia rajya ke mongol samudaay main buddha dharm kaa jor he, jis se unka sanskrit or tibeti bhashaaon se dharmik sambandh rahaa he. sanskrit or tibeti swaron ko likhane ke liye mongol lipi main kuch anya akshar bhi jode gaye the. jab unicode main mongol lipi jodi gai to usamen in vishesh aksharon kaa bhi prabandh kiya gaya tha. unicode main U+1800 se U+18AF tak kii jagah mongol lipi ke liye arakshit he.
