 roosi sangheey antariksh abhikaran yaa russian fedral spes agency rus kaa sarkari antariksh abhikaran he jo kii rus main antariksh se judi gatividhiyaan sanchalit karata he. ise roscosmos ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. roscosmos kaa mukhyalay masco main sthit he. mukhya mishan niyantran centre corolev main he evam cosmonaut prashikshan centre star city main sthit he. kazakhstan sthit byconur cosmodrome main maanviya evam amanviya donon prakaar kii prakshepan suvidha uplabdh he. uttari rus sthit plasteke cosmodrome se zyaadaatar amanviya udaanon ko sanchalit kiya jaataa he. anterrashtriya antariksh stesan main is sanghathan kaa mahatvapurn yogadan he. soyus rocket evam proton rocket pramukh prakshepan yaan hai.
