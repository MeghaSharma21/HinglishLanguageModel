 mahamudiya masjid; Mahmoudiya Mosque: israel ke jaafaa shahar main sthit sabase badi or sabase mahatvapurn masjid he, lekin ab yah tel aviv shahar kaa hissaa he. masjid main do bade angan he, masjid kii aasapaas kii vyavastha ek jatil imaaraton se bani he. 18 veen or 19veen shatabdi main vibhinn charnon main imaaraton, phatak or aanganon kaa nirmaan kiya gaya tha atoman shashan ke dauraan dakshini syria otoman shashan ke adheen thi.. 
 kaha jaataa he ki mahamudiya masjid kaa praarambhik nirmaan 1730 main rajyapal shaikh mohammad al-khalili ke aadesh par hua tha. ek sabil, masjid kii dakshini diwaar main he, 18 veen or 19veen shatabdi kii shuruyaat main ekar ke governor salmaan pasha ke dwaara nirmaan gaya he.vartmaan masjid kaa adhikansh bhaag 1812 main gaja ke turk rajyapal or jafaa, mohammad abu-nabboot dwaara banaaya gaya tha. mukhya angan, masjid ke pashchimi bhaag main sthit he,
