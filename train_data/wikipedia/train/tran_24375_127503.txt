 purulia pashchim bengal praant kaa ek jila he. jo jharkhand or pashchim bengal kii seemaa par sthit he. yah "manbhum city" ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he. shahar 1876 main gathit kiya gaya tha. jila mukhyalay purulia he. purulia jile ke uttar main kasaai nadi or ek pramukh sadak or purulia railway jankshan sthit he. yah 6251 kai varg ki.me.ke kshetra me failaa hua he. yah "choudhary nrity" ke liye prasiddh he. chau nrity purulia kaa pramukh mukhota nrity he.
 ramakrishna mishan viapeeth pracheen gurukul pranali he jisaki vichaardhaara ke shant vaataavaran ke saath ladakon ke liye ek awasaiya skool dwaara swami vivekanand ne sthapit kii thi. sainik skool ranchi road par he. GRK diavi ek awasaiya skool arya samaaj, swami dayanand dwaara kii sthapana kii kii vichaardhaaraon par aadhaarit he. bhagwaan carch skool kii ek subah batch or ek din batch he. skulon kii pracheenatam purulia jila he jo 1853 main sthapit kiya gaya tha.
