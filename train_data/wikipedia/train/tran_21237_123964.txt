 bharat kii anek jatiyon va janajatiyon main peedhee dar peedhee chali aa rahi paaramparik kalaon ko lokkala kehete hai. inamen se kuch aadhunik kaal main bhi bahut lokapriy hai jaise madhubani or kuch lagbhag mritpraaya jaise jadopatia. 
 kalamkaari, kangra, gond, chittar, thanjavur, thangak, paatchitr, pichwai, pithora chitrakala, fad, batik, madhubani, yamunaghat tathaa varali aadi bharat kii pramukh lok kalaayen hai.
