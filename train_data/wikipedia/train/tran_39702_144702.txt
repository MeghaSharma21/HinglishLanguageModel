 upar die gaye udhaaharan kai baar sateek bhi hote hai or kai baar inamen or baaten bhi shaamil hoti hai. udhaaharan ke liye, sharir kaa taapmaana badhanaa bukhaar kaa sanket ho saktaa he parantu yah malaria yaa typhoid]] kaa bhi sanket ho saktaa he. sanketo par kisi nishkarsh par pahunchne ke liye doctoron ko saare pahaluon ko jaanchna pad saktaa he. iske liye kai baar lab pareekshan bhi aavashyak hote hai.
