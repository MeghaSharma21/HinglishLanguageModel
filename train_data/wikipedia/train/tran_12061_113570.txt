 aravind vishnu gokhale marathi nav-katha ke pramukh hastakshar hai. pune ke krishi mahavidhyalay main vanaspit shaastra kaa adhyaapan kiye evam 1940 se kahani lekhan prarambh kiye. inhone 600 se adhik kahaniyaan likhin, jo chalis se adhik sangrahon main sankalit hai. iske alaava katha or kathakaar vishay se sambadd teen anya kritiyaan prakashit hui. inaki kahaniyaan gujarati, hindi or angreji main anudit hui hai. maharashtra tathaa bihar rajya sarkaar evam ancounter patrika dwaara vishesh rup se puraskrit evam sammanit hue. bharat sarkaar ke samskruti mantralay dwaara emiratus fellowship se gauravanvit kiye gayi. bas yaatr ke dauraan sir main chot aa jane ke kaaran nidhan.
