 chakma swytt jila parishad kaa gathan bhartiya samvidhan kii chhathi anusuchee ke tahat 29 april 1972 ko kiya gaya tha. yah parishad rajya vidhaan sabha kii pratikruti he or vishesh rup se aavantit vibhaagon par karypalika rup se prashasan kiya karti he. yah uttar-purv bharat main mizoram main sthit teen swytt jila parishadon main se ek he. yah dakshin-pashchimi mizoram or bangladesh or myanmar kii seemaa se lage kshetron main rahane vaale chakma logon ke liye ek swytt parishad he. parishad ke adheen is kshetra kaa 1500 varg kilometre kshetra aata he jisamen jansankhya 2001 main 34,528 se badhkar 2008 tak 40,265 ho chuki he. sthaneey chakma logon kii maang ab ek chakma swytt jila "chakmaland" ke rup main ek sangh shasit kshetra banane kii he.
 mizoram kii kul chakma jansankhya 100,000 se adhik hone kaa anumaan he. chakma swytt jila parishad ke antargat puri jansankhya anusuchit janajati hi he. 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar is kshetra kii kul jansankhya 43,528 he jisamen se 70% log krishi par nirbhar he.
 chakma swytt jila parishad kaa mukhyalay kamalanagar he jiskaa chakma bhashaa main santare kaa shahar he. ise choungte-si kehete hai. iske kamalanagar-1,2,3 or 4 naamak chaar bhaag hai. yahaan ek mahavidhyalay bhi he jiskaa naam kamalanagar government kalege he or kamalanagar-1 footbal maidan ke nikat sthit he. 
 chakma jila parishad ke adhikansh log theravad buddha dharm ke anuyaayi hai. ye is dharm kaa anusaran lambe samay se kar rahe hai.
