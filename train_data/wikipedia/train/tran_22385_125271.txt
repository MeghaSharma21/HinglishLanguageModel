 2001 canada masters – yugal canada masters shrunkhlaa kaa 112vaan sanskaran tha jo purush yugal ke rup main khelaa gaya. yah dablutie tear pratham pratiyogita kaa bhaag tathaa canada main hone vaale sabase pratishthit purushon kii tennis pratiyogita thi. sebestian lareu or dainiel nestor pichhali pratiyogita ke vijeta the lekin is baar wo alag-alag jodi banaakar khele. lareu ke saath justin gimelstob or nestor ke saath sandon stole jodidar bane. gimelstob or lareu kii jodi pehle hi daur main mark noles or bryan maikaphai se haar gai tathaa nestor or stole kii jodi jain-maaikel gambil or simon laroj se harkar pratiyogita se baahar hui. jiri nowak or david rical ne donald johnson or jared paamar se is pratiyogita kaa final 6–4, 3–6, 6–3 se jeeta.
 vijeta khilaadiyon ko gahare aksharon main likhaa gaya he jabaki tirchhe aksharon main khilaadiyon ke us daur ko pradarshit kiya gaya he jisamen wo pratiyogita se baahar huye.
