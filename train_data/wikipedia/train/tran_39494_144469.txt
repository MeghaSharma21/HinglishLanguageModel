 kii gompa himachal pradesh kii lahaul spiti jile main kaja se 12 kilometre kii duuri par he. is math kii sthapana 13veen shatabdi main hui thi. yah spiti kshetra kaa sabase bada math he. yah math dur se leh main sthit thikse math jaisaa lagata he. yah math samudr tal se 13504 feet kii oonchai par ek shankvakar chattan par nirmit he. sthaneey logon kaa maananaa he ki ise ringchhen sangapo ne banwaaya tha. yah math mahayan buddha ke jelupa sampradaay se sambandhit he. is math par 19veen shatabdi main sikhon tathaa dogra raajaaon ne akraman bhi kiya tha. iske alaava yah 1975 i. main aae bhucamp main bhi surakshit rahaa. is math main kuch pracheen hastlipiyon tathaa thangkas kaa sangrah he. iske alaava yahaan kuch hathiyaar bhi rakhe hue hai. yahaan prayek varsh june-july maheene main 'cham utsav' manaaya jaataa he.
 san 2000 ke kalchakra abhishek kaa aayojan is math main kiya gaya tha jisamen swayan dalai lamas dwaara puja archana kii gai.
