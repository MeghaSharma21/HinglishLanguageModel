 srilanka cricket team vartmaan main oktober se november 2016 ke liye zimbabwe daura kar rahe hai. is daure main mul se milakar karne ke liye anusuchit gaya tha ek do test match, teen ek divasiy antarrashtriya or ek bhi twanti -20 antarrashtriya, lekin sitambar 2016 main ek divasiy maichon main ek trikoniya zimbabwe, srilanka kii visheshta shrinkhalaa ke dwaara badal diya gaya tha or westeindies or kaha ki zimbabwe daure kam ho jayega karne ke liye sirf do test match.
 srilanka pichley 2004 main ek test match main zimbabwe nibhaai. shrinkhalaa kaa pehla test match 100 waan test match zimbabwe dwaara khelaa gaya tha.
 angelo maithyuj kii chot ke kaaran daure se baahar ho gaye or dwaara rangana herath captain ke rup main badal diya gaya tha.
