 khunjaraab darra ) karakoram parvatamala main sthit ek darra he. yah paak adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-blatistan kshetra or china dwaara niyantrit shinjiyang praant ke bich sthit he. 
 khunjaraab darre kaa naam vaakhi bhashaa se liya gaya he. iske arth par vivaad he. yaa to iskaa matlab 'koon kii vaadi' he yaa phir 'khan kii vaadi'. 'khunjaraab' shabd main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
