 bair namakan taron ko naam dene kaa ek tareeqa he jisamen kisi bhi taramandal main sthit taare ko ek unani akshar1 or uske taramandal ke unani naam se bulaayaa jaataa he. bair naamon main taramandal ke unani naam kaa sambandh rup2 istemaal hota he. misaal ke liye, parnin ashv taramandal ke taron main se teen taron ke naam is prakaar hai - α pagasaaye, β pagasaaye or γ pagasaaye .
 german khagolashastri yohan bair ne san 1603 main bair namakan shuru kiya. unhone apni taraf se puri koshish kii ki taron kaa namakan unki prakaash kii shakti ke mutabik hi ho, lekin us jamaane main prakaash ko maap paanaa bahut kathin tha or bahut se taramandal hai jinamein alfaa namankit kiya gaya tara us taramandal kaa sab se roshan tara nahin hota.
 unani varnmaala main sirf choubees akshar hai, jabaki bahut se taramandalon main to pachaas se bhi adhik taare hote hai. aise main unani ke aakhri akshar ke baad angrezi varnmaala ke chote akshar istemaal hote hai. is vidhi main pachaasave taare kaa naam "z" hoga. agar pachaas se bhi zyaada taare hon to phir ikyavanave taare se angrezi ke bade aksharon kaa prayog shuru ho jaataa he. is tarah se puure 76 taron ko naam die ja sakate hai.
