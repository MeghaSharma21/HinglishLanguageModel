 kushadhvaj kai hue hai-
 kushadhvaj : bhagwat ke anusaar raja janak ke putr jinaki bahan sita or urmila thim. inke putr dharmadhvaj or pautra kritadhvaj evam mitdhvaj the.
 kushadhvaj : valmiki ramayana, vishnu puraana, ramcharitamanas aadi ke anusaar janak ke bhai mandavi or shrutikeerti ke pita. in donon ladakiyon kaa vivaah kramash: bharat or shatrughna se hua tha.
 kushadhvaj : devguru brihaspati ke ek putr, jinaki kanya vedavati thim.
 inke atirikt kushadhvaj naam ke or kai raja tathaa raajputr hue jinamein sabase prasiddh kashiraj kushadhvaj hai jo param shivbhakt the.
