 varanasi nagar nigam bharat ke uttar pradesh rajya ke varanasi shahar kaa nagar nigam he. nigam kii sthapana 24 janvari 1959 ko uttar pradesh sarkaar ke nagar nigam adhinium - 1959 ke antargat nagarmahapalika ke rup main kii gai thi. 1994 main ise nagar nigam adhinium-2 ke tahat nagar nigam main badla gaya. ye vishwa ke kuch ati ghanatv aabaadi vaale kshetron ke nagar nigam main se ek he. nigam ke antargat 79.79 varg ki.me kshetra ke kul 90 ward aate hai. nigam ke pratham mahayor shri.pi.ke kaul nigam ayukta bane. nigam ke adheen sadakon kii kul lambaai 1170 kimi he or ye bi-1 str ke shahar main aata he. kul jansankhya 10 lakh he.
 varanasi shahari kshetra kii 2001 ke anusaar jansankhya 13,71,749 thi; or ling anupaat 879 striyaan prati 1000 purush thi. varanasi nagar nigam ke adhinsth kshetra kii jansankhya 11,00,748 jiskaa ling anupaat 883 striyaan prati 1000 purush tha. shahari kshetra main sakshartaa dar 77% or nigam kshetra main 78% thi. nigam kshetra ke lagbhag 1,38,000 log jhuggi-jhonpadiyon main rahete hai. varsh 2004 kii aparaadh dar 128.5 prati 1 lakh thi; jo rajya kii dar 73.2 se adhik he, kintu rashtriya aparaadh dar 168.8 se kahin kam he.
