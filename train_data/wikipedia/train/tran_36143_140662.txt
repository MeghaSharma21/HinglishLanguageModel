 pracheen aleppo shahar, aleppo, syria ke aitihaasik shahar kaa centre he. syrian grihayuddh ke kaaran shahar bade paimaane par nasht ho gaya he. alepo purana shahar - deevaaron ke bheetar pracheen shahar se bana he jiskaa anumaanit kshetra 350 hectare, jansankhya 120,000 nivaasiyon se adhik he. iske bade bhawanon, sankeern galiyon, dhake hue souk or pracheen imaaraton kii visheshta, pracheen aleppo shahar 1986 isvi main unesco vishwa dharohar sthal ke rup main ghoshit kiya gaya tha.. al-madina souk or shahar main anya madhyayugin imaaraton dharohar sthalon ko nasht kar diya gaya jo syria kii arab sena or jabat al-nusar ke vidrohi balon ke bich sangharsh ke parinamswaroop barbaad ho gaya. jise aleppo kii ladaai kaha jaataa he, yah ladaai 25 sitambar 2012 ko vipakshi samooh sashastr dwaara shuru kii gai thi. grihayudhd main 30% pracheen aleppo shahar ko nasht ho gaya he.
