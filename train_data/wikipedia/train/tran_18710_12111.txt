 anukriti gosai ek model or tii.ve main prastota hai. 2013 main miss india pratiyogita main sundari pratiyogita jiti thi. baad main miss india asia pacific - 2014 pratiyogita jiti. abhi vah modelling kar rahi hai or zum tii.ve main tivi ankar bhi hai. 2015 main prakashit list main vah 49wein number par hai.
 anukriti gosai kaa janm uttarakhand rajya ke gadhwal praant main hua tha. anukriti ke pita kaa naam uttam gosai he or mata kaa naam narvada gosai hai, jo ki ek shikshika hai. anukriti teen bhai bahanon main sabase badi bahan hai. anukriti kii praarambhik shiksha army public skool lansdon main hui hai or baad main bi.tech dehradun institute of technology se kiya. apni shiksha puri karke vah mumbai chali gai.
 anukriti kaa modelling karier 2013 main shuru hua jahaan par "miss india dely 2013" kaa khitaab jeeta or baad main isi pratiyogita main chauthe sthaan par rahi. iske agle varsh vah "miss india asia pacific-2014" pratiyogita jiti or baad main dakshin korea main vah top paanch main rahi. aaj maani jaani model ke saath tivi ankar bhi hai.
