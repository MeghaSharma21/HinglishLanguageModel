 latvia ne pehle 1924 main olympic khelon main bhaag liya tha. 1940 main desh ko soviet sangh dwaara kabja kar lene ke baad, latvian athleton ne 1952 or 1988 ke bich olympic main soviet sangh ke liye pratispardhaa kii. latvia kii swatantraaa or 1991 main soviet sangh ke vighatan ke baad, raashtra 1992 main olympic khelon main laut aayaa or uske baad se har game main pratispardhaa kii gai.
 latvian athlete ne greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main kul unniis padak or sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main saat padak jite hai. unhone teen swarna padak ke saath, rajat padak kaa ek ullekhneey ucch anupaat jeeta he. soviet sangh ke liye pratispardhaa karte hue latvia ke athleton dwaara jiti jane vaali in yogon main shaamil nahin hai.
 latvia kii rashtriya olympic samiti ko pehali baar 1922 main banaaya gaya tha. vartmaan enosi latvian olympic samiti he, jise 1991 main antarrashtriya olympic samiti ne manyata di thi. 
 olympic safalataa ek taraf, 2017 taco bel skills chalange main christeps porgingis jeet vyapak rup se latvian khel ke itihaas main sabase garv pal mana jaataa he.
