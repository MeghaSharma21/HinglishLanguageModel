 shandong janavaadee ganraajya china ke purvi tat par sthit ek praant he. is praant ne china ke itihaas main ek ahm kendriya bhoomika nibhaai he. chini sabhyataa yaheen pili nadi ke antim bhaag main janmi thi. yah china main tao dharm, buddha dharm or kunfushiasi dharm ke liye ek ati-mahatvapurn saanskrithik or dharmik bhoomi rahi he. shandong kaa tai parvat tao dharm ke sabase pavitra tirthon main se ek he or yahan 3,000 se puja chalati aa rahi he. praant kii rajdhani jinaan se dakshin main china ke sabase puraane buddha sthal hai. chufu shahar canafusious kaa janmasthan he. uttar-dakshin or purv-pashchim jane vaale bahut se margon par sthit hone se shandong aarthik nazariye se hameshaa kendriya rahaa he. 19veen sadi main shuru hui rajnatik asthirataa or aarthik mushkilon ke baad vartmaan main shandong praant china ke sabase viksit or badi aabaadi vaale praanton main se ek he.
