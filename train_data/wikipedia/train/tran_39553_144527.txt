 saanchaa:Politics of Bhutan
 bhutan kaa samvidhan, 18 july 2008 ko bhutan kii shahi sarkaar dwaara lagu hua. isaki yojana kai varshon tak kai sarkari adhikariyon or vibhaagon dwaara banayi gayi thi. yah bhutan ke adhikadhik loktantrikaran kaa ek bhaag tha. vartmaan samvidhan buddha darshan, maanavadhikaron par antarrashtriya sammelnon, 20 anya samvidhanon ke tulanaatmak adhyayan, tathaa janamat, vartmaan kanoonon or purvanirnyon par aadhaarit he. rajkumaari sonam wangchuck ke anusaar, samvidhan samiti dakshin africa ke samvidhan se vishesh rup se prabhavit thi kyonki dakshin africa kaa samvidhan maanavadhikaron kaa bada sangrakshak he.
