 bandikui bharat ke rajasthan rajya ke dausa jile main ek gram panchayat or prasiddh nagar he.
 vartmaan bandikui itihaas kaa rajasthan main rail aagaman ke saath shuru hota he. april 1874 main agra fort evam bandikui ke madhya tatkaaleen raajputana main pehali tren chali. uske baad tatkaaleen jaipur maharaja savai madhosingh ji ne yahaan par madhoganj ke naam se anaaj mandi kii sthapana kii. vyapaar or rail avaagaman ke kaaran bandikui ne kalantar main ek shahar kaa rup le liya. kul milakar yahi kahana sahi hoga ki bandikui ke vikaas kii neenv rail hi he. is liye bandikui ko rail nagari ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. vartmaan main shahar kii aabaadi lagbhag 65 hajaar he. yahan kii nagarpalika 30 vardo main vibhakt he. yah shahar railway kaa bada jacshan he jahaan se puure desh ke liye train uplabdh he. bandikui desh main paryaton kii drushti se golden triengal dilli, jaipur or agra ke madhya avasthit he. yahan se dilli 200 kimi, jaipur 90 kimi or agra 150 kimi dur he.bharatpur or alvar kramshah 90 or 60 kimi dur he. darshneey sthal-1.aathvin sadi main nirmit chand baavdi or harshad mata kaa mandir.2.mehandipur balaji kaa mandir.3. britishkaleen protestant or catholic carch.4. jhanzirampura or aleva ke jharne.5. yahan se sariska baagh abhyaayran bhi ghuma ja saktaa he.
