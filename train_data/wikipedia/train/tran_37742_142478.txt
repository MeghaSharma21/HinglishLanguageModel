 andakaar akashganga kisi dirghavrittabh aakaar vaali akashganga ko kehete hai, jiske har bhaag se lagbhag baraabar kii chamak aa rahi ho. inkaa aakaar ek shuddh gole se lekar bahut hi pichake chapate andey kii tarah ho saktaa he or inamen dasiyon karod se lekar das kharab taare ho sakate hai. lensanuma or sarpil akashgangaon ke saath, andakaar akashgangaayen habal anukram kii teen mukhya akashgangaon kii shreniyan hai. andakaar akashgangaon main puraane taare hote hai or inkaa antartaarkeey madhyam kam ghanaa hota he. inamen navjaat taare kam hi milte hai. hamaare ird-gird ke brahmand main lagbhag 10-15% akashgangaayen is shrenee kii hoti hai, lekin puure brahmand main inaki pratishat sankhya is se kam maani jaati he.
 angrezi main "andakaar akashganga" ko "alliptical galaksi" kehete hai.
