 dhar louh stambh bharat ke madhya pradesh rajya ke dhar nagar main sthit ek vidhvansit lohe kaa stambh he jiske saare tukdon kaa bhaar milakar 7300 kilogram he, yani dilli ke louh stambh se lagbhag 1,000 kilogram adhik. isaki nirmaan-vyutpatti aadhikarik rup se gyaat nahin lekin sthaneey sroton ke anusaar yah 11veen shatabdi isvi main parmaar rajvansh ke raja bhoj dwaara khadaa kiya gaya vijay stambh tha.
 aadhukanik kaal main iske teen ansh 15veen shatabdi main bani laat masjid ke paas sthit hai. sthaneey hindi upbhasha main laat kaa arth "stambh" hota he or masjid kaa naam isi stambh par rakhaa gaya he. stambh kaa ek chauthaa ansh laapata he. mul rup se stambh upar kii ore adhik tang tha or neechala bhaag adhik chaudaa, jisase yah upree ansh kaa bhaar uthaane main sakasham tha. sabase nichle hisse kaa anuprastha kaat chaturbhuji tha, madhya anuprastha chaturbhuji or ashtabhuji, or sarvopari ashtabhuji lekin uskaa sabase upree bhaag kaa anuprastha chota or golakaar tha. milakar teenon anshon kii kul lambaai 13.21 meter he, jo yah sanket dete hai ki yah sabut stambh dilli ke louh stambh se dugani oonchaai kaa tha. jab yah khadaa kiya gaya, us samay yah shaayad vishwa kaa sabase bada kuttit weldit louh stambh rahaa hoga.
