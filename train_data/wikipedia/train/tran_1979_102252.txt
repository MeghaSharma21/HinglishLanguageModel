 bharatvani, bharat sarkaar kaa ek web portal he. is pariyojna kaa nirmaan maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantralay dwaara kii gai he. is pariyojna kaa lakshya ek online portal ke zarie lagbhag sabhi bhartiya bhashaaon main or unke bare main multimedia praaroop main gyaan kaa aisa bhandar bananaa he jisamen se manachaahi soochna dhundkar nikaali ja sake. iskaa kriyanvayan bhartiya bhashaa santhaan, mysoreu, karnataka ke dwaara kiya ja rahaa he. is portal par copirite kanoon, 2012 kii ‘uchit prayog’ se sambandhit dhaaraon ke antargat shaikshanik uddeshyon ke liye mukt gyaan saamagree uplabdh karaayi ja rahi he.
 is pariyojna kaa maargdarshan ek rashtriya salaahkaar samiti or ek takneekee salaahkaar samiti karegi or inke atirikt prayek bhashaa ke liye alag-alag sampadakeey samitiyaan bhi hongi.
