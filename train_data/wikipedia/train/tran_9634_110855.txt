 dudhnoi-mehndipathar rail line purvottar bhartiya rajyon assam ke dudhnoi se meghalaya ke mehndipathar ke bich ek rail sampark line he. yah mul rup se mukhya shakha barauni-guwahati rail line kii ek shakha he.
 assam main dudhnoi or meghalaya main deepa ke bich rail line kaa kendriya rail-bajet 1992-93 main prastaav aayaa tha. kaalaantar main sthaneey logon ke virodh ke kaaran varsh 2007 main ise dudhnoi-mehndipathar main badal diya gaya. assam or meghalaya sarkaron dwaara bhoomi adhigrahan main vilamb ke kaaran yah pariyojna 2013 tak lambit ho gayi. antatah 2013 main bhoomi adhigrahan kaary sampann hua. 
 yah rail line parvatiy rajya meghalaya kii pehali or ekmaatr rail line he. mehndeepaththar kaa udghaatan tatkaaleen pradhanmantri narendra modi dwaara 30 november 2014 ko mehandi pathar main yojit kaarykram main upasthit jansamooh ko ek video kanfrencing dwaara sambodhit kar kiya gaya tha. 
 mehndipathar-guwahati paisenger is railmarg kii ekmaatr gaadi he.
