 ju-jaan kagaanath yaa rour kagaanath yaa nirun kagaanath ek kanaabadosh qabeelon kaa parisangh tha jo china ke uttari bhaag or uske padosi ilaaqon main chauthi sadi isvi ke ant se chhathi sadi isvi ke madhya tak ke kaal main vistrut tha. kuch itihaaskaaron ke anusaar yah qabeele vahi the jo baad main europe main ureshian awaar logon ke rup main ubhare.
 'ju-jaan' ko chini bhashaa main 'ruanruan' bulaayaa jaataa tha, jiskaa arth 'kulbulate keede' he. haalanki bahut se itihaaskaaron ne is jaateey dwesh se bhare tiraskaarapurvak naam kii ninda kii he phir bhi yah naam 'rour' ke rup main abhi bhi prachlit he.
 ju-jaan log mongol nasal ke shiyanbei log ke netritv main un qabeelon kaa parisangh tha jo adhiktar shiyanbei logon ke uttari china main chale jane ke baad bhi mongolia ke stapi kshetra main based rahe. itihaas main inkaa pehla zikr inke dwaara tiele logon ke haraae jane par miltaa he. iske baad inhone mongolia se purv main aadhunik china ke jilin praant tak vistrut ek samrajya bana liya. ju-jaan ke pashchim main hefthali logon kaa qabeela rahata tha jo 5veen sadi kii shuruyaat tak ju-jaan ke adheen the. hefthaliyon or ju-jaan main samay ke saath kuch anban ho gai or is ladaai ko chini guptcharon ne badhaava diya. is se ju-jaan ke parisangh ke andar vidroh bhadke or 555 i main ek tujue naamak qabeele ne 3,000 ju-jaani logon ke sir kalam kar die. yah bhi kaha jaataa he ki ashina naamak ek turki qabeela ju-jaan parisangh kaa hissaa hota tha lekin unhone chini rajyon ke saath santh-ganth karke ju-jaan ko khaded diya or uske baad sthapit hone vaali goekaturk samrajya kaa rajpariwaar bane. mana jaataa he ki in haadason ke baad ju-jaani log stapi main pashchim kii ore bhaag khadey hue. unka aage kya bana, is baat ko lekar itihaaskaaron main vivaad he, lekin kuch yah maanate hai ki ve purvi europe ke awaar log ban gaye.
