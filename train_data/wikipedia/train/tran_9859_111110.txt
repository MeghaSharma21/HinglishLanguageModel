 
 condreeikthies​ yaa upasthidaar machhliyaan jabade-vaali machhliyon kii vah shrenee hoti he jinke andruni dhaanche haddiyon kii bajaay upasthi ke bane hote hai. inase vipareet vah machhliyaan hoti hai jinke bhitari dhaanche haddi ke bane hote hai, yani haddidaar machhliyaan. upasthidaar machhliyon ke jodedaar chlom or nasikaaye hoti hai or inke hriday dvikaksh hote hai. hongar or shankush kuch prasiddh upasthidaar machhliyaan hai. aamtaur par upasthidaar machhliyon kaa akar anya machhliyon se bada hota he.
