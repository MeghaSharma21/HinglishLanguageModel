 sikandar lodi lodi vansh kaa dwitiya shaasha tha. apane pita bahalul khan lodi kii mrityu july 17, 1489 uparaant yah sultan bana. iske sultan banane main kathinai kaa mukhya kaaran tha iskaa bada bhai, barbak shah, jo tab jonpur kaa rajyapal tha. usne bhi is gaddi par, apane pita ke sikandar ke namakan ke baavjood, daawa kiya tha. parantu sikandar ne ek pratinidhi mndal bhej kar maamla sulajhaa liya or ek bada khun-kharaba bachaa liya. asal main isane barbak shah ko jonpur sultanate par rajya jari rakhane ko kaha, evam apane chachaa alam khan se bhi vivaad sulajhaa liye, jo ki takhta palat karne kii yojana bana rahaa tha.
 sikandar ek yogya shaasha siddh hua. vah apni praja ke liye dayaalu tha. usne apane rajya ko ko gwalior evam bihar tak badhaaya. usne allaudin hossain shah evam uski bengal ke rajya se sandhi kii. vah apane deshi afgaan nawabon ko niyantran main rakhane main safal hua, evam apane rajya paryant vyapaar ko khub badhaava diya. san 1503 main, usne vartmaan agra shahar kii neenv rakhi.
 uski mrityu san 1517 main praja ke liye bada aghat thi, jaisaa ki uske dilli main shandaar makbare se bhi siddh hota he.
