 taittiriya shakha krishna yajurveda kii pramukh shakha he. vishnupurana ke anusaar is shakha ke pravartak yaksha ke shishy tittiri rishi the. yah shakha dakshin bharat main adhik prachlit he. yah shakha apane main paripurna kahi ja sakati he kyonki is shakha ke samhita, brahman, aaranyak, upanishad, shrautasutra, tathaa grihyasutra aadi sabhi granth uplabdh hai. maharashtra kaa kuch bhaag tathaa dakshin bharat kaa bahushah bhaag iskaa anuyaayi he.
