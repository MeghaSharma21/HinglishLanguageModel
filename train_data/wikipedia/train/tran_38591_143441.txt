 masaan bhartiya hindi film he. iskaa nirdesan niraj ghevon ne kiya he. sanjay mishra, richa chaddha, vicky kaushal or shweta treepathi isamen mukhya kirdaar main hai.
 yah banaras main do alag-alag kahani ko saath main dikhaata he. pehali kahani devi kii he jo apane saathi vidhyaarthee piyush ke saath ek hotel main jaati he. jahaan police use ashleel film dekhte hue pakad leti he. iske baad piyush apni kalai kaat kar aatmahatya kar letaa he. devi par police jabardasti gunaah qabool karaati he. iske baad police use is baat ko dabaane ke liye paise maangati he.
 duusari kahani shmashaan ghat main kaam karne vaale deepak kii he, jo nichle varg kaa he. ek din uski mulaaqaat ek ucch varg kii ladki shaalu gupta se hoti he. vah donon ek duusare se pyaar karne lagate hai.
