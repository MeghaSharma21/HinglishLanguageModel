 chandigarh metro chandigarh shahar, chandigarh kendra shasit pradesh ke liye yojanabaddh twarit yatayaat pariyojna he. is pranali ko 4 galiyaaron main vibhajit kiya gaya he, jisaki kul lambaai 37.573 kilometre he.
 16 agast 2012 ko dilli metro rail nigam, punjab ke rajyapal or kendra shasit pradeshon ke prashaasak shivaraj patil ko chandigarh metro pariyojna kii vistrut pariyojna report prastut kar chuki he.
 pehle charan main, 37.573 kilometre metro rail network kaa nirmaan kiya jayega, jisamen se 23.468 kimi utthit rail line ke rup main nirmit hogi, tathaa 14.105 kimi rail network ko bhumigat rakhaa jayega.pratham coridor uttar se dakshin disha main capital parisar ke paas se shuru ho kar mohali tak jaayegaa. dwitiya coridor jo kii east west coridor ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he, sector 21, panchakula se shuru ho kar mullampur tak jayega.
 metro network ke vibhinn galiyaaron ke liye prastaavit margon is prakaar hai:
 khuda lahora se iti park - punjab uniwarsity, pijiai, government college, general aspataal, sector 17 interchange, sector 8, sector 7, sector 26, grain market, transport nagar, chandigarh railway stesan, manimajaraa, ke raste lagbhag 16.00 kilometre kii duuri tay karegaa.
 sichvalay, chandigarh sector 1 se bas terminal sector 104 esees nagar, mohali - rock garden, sector 9, sector 17 interchange, sector 17 aiesabiti, sector 22-aroma hotel sector 34, bas terminal sector 43, sector 52, mohali sector 62, sector 60, sector 72, sector 71, sector 75, sector 76, sector 77, sector 78, sector 87, sector 97, sector 106, sector 105 ke raste lagbhag 22.00 kilometre kii duuri tay karegaa.
 timber market chowk sector 26 se sector 38 - dadu maajaraa, purv maarg or vikaas maarg ke raste lagbhag 14.6 kilometre kii duuri tay karegaa.
 houseing board chowk se sector 21, panchakula - panchakula sector 17, sector 16, sector 15, sector 14 or sector 21 ke raste lagbhag 5 kilometre kii duuri tay karegaa.
 is pariyojna par lagbhag 11,375 karod karch honge, jisamen se chandigarh prashasan dwaara 8,995 karod, punjab sarkaar dwaara 1,680 karod, tathaa haryana sarkaar dwaara shesh 700 karod saajhe jaaege.utthit rail line ansh kii anumaanit laagat 140 karod rupee prati kimi or bhumigat ansh kii prati kimi 350 karod rupye he. metro rail ko do charnon main puraa kiya jayega. pehle charan kii anumaanit laagat 8,995 karod he vahin dwitiya charan kii anumaanit laagat 2,380 karod he.
