 siparasi ghaas sadrush shaak kaa kul he jiske paudhe ekbijpatri tathaa daldali bhoomi main ugte hai. is kul ke paudhe mukhyat: bahuvarshi hote hai.
 siparasi kul ke 85 vansh or lagbhag 3,200 spishij gyaat hai. tadkul tathaa lilissi kul ke bijon ke ankuran kii tarah sytherasi kul ke bijon kaa ankuran hota he. prati varsh kii naveen shakha pichhali parvasandhi se sanlagn rahati he. praaia: tana vaayav tathaa tribhuji hota he or pattiyaan teen panktiyon main rahati hai. sookshm pushp spikika main vyasthit rahete hai. cypiras vansh tathaa kairex yaa narivansh ke phool nagn hote hai. viral dasha main hi phool main chah shalkwala paridalpunj rahata he. paridalpunj kaa pratinidhitiv roen yaa shook se hota he. fal main saamanyat: teen or kabhi-kabhi do punkesar hote hai. stri kesar main do yaa teen andap hote hai, jo milakar andashay banaate hai jisamen kai vartikas evam ek bijand hota he. pushp praaia: ekalingi hote hai or vaayu dwaara paragan hota he. fal main ek beej hota he tathaa iskaa chhilka kathor evam charm sadrush hota he. sapas, ringcospora, cypiras tathaa kairex is kul ke pramukh vansh hai. kairaques vansh ke paudhe chataayee banane ke kaam main aate hai.
