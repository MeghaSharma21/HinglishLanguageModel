 king baudouin stadium' uttar pashchim brusselles, belgium main ek khel maidan he. yah 23 agast 1930 par udghaatan kiya gaya. yuvaraj leopold udghaatan samaaroh main bhaag liya tha. 
 stadium hesal stadium durghatana ke liye janaa jaataa he, jisamen 39 logon ko ek footbal ke dauraan mrityu ho gai thi. is kaa kaaran kharaab rakrakhaav stadium tha jo sachmuch dhahte tha, donon tiimon ke prashansakon ke bich or achaanak updravi hinsa.
