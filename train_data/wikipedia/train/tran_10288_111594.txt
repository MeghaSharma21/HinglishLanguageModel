 avakendra vah bindu hota he jahaan se kisi bhucamp yaa bhumigat parmaanu visphot kaa armbh hua ho. bhukampon main avakendra vah sthaan hota he jahaan shilaon main upasthit virupyan urja sabase pehle mukt hoti he or jahaan se bhransh fatna armbh ho jaataa he. yah bindu prithvi yaa anya grah kii sataha par sthit uparicendre ke thik neeche sthit hota he. uparicendre se avakendra kii gaharaai ko avakendriya gaharaai kaha jaataa he.
