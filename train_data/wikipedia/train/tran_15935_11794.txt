 satnam singh bhamara bhartiya mul ke peshewar bassketbal khiladi hai jinka amrika kii "rashtriya bassketbal association" main 25 june 2015 ko chayan hua he. vah is pratishth team main chune jane vaale pehle bharti khiladi hai. unka janm 10 disambar 1995 ko bharat ke punjab rajya ke jila barnaala ke ballo ke gaanv main hua. shri bhamara kii uchai 7 foot 2 inch he. unhone 2010 main wo amrika chale gaye the. 2014-15 main ai.em.ji. academy kii taraf se khelte hue 20 mint main prati game ausat 9.2 points,8.4 rebond 2.2 blok banaae. 
  
  
 satnam singh bhamara kaa janm bhartiya punjab ke ballo ke gaanv ke ek sikh parivaar main hua. unke pita shri balbir singh 2 acre jameen vaale or aataa chakki kaa kaam karne vaale ek kisaan hai. bhamara ke sharir kii teji se bad rahi lambaai ko saare gaanv ne bhaanp liya tha. 9 varsh kii aayu main hi wo 6 foot 2 inch lambe ho gaye the. isko dekhte hue parivaar ne unako ludhiana kii " ludhiana bassketbal academy" main daakhil karvaa diya tha. 
 april 2015 main bhamara 2015 anbie draft . ke liye chun liye gaye jab unhen khilne ke liye koi or wajipha praapt nahin tha. or iske baad wo aisi pratishth khel sansathan main chayan praapt karne vaale pahile bhartiya ban gaye.  jinhone bina kisi kalege ke yaa bina videshi khiladi kii pahichaan is chayan main kamyaabi hasil kii.
