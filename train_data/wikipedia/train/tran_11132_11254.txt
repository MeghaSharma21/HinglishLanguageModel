 sanrachanakrit parjiviki main parjiviyon ke proteinon kii sanrachana kaa adhyayan hota he. parjaivik protein sanrachana kaa nirdhaaran yah jaanane main madad karata he, ki ye protein kis prakaar maanvon main upasthit homologus proteinon se bhinn karyrat he. saath hi protein sanrachana aushadhi khoj kii prakriya main sahaayak hotin hai.
