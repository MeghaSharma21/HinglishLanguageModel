 kumhada yaa kaddu ek sthaliy, dwibijapatri paudha he jiskaa tana lambaa, kamjor va hare rang kaa hota he. tane par chote-chote royen hote hai. yah apane aakarshon kii sahaayta se badhata yaa chadhataa he. isaki pattiyaan hari, chaudee or vrittakar hoti hai. iskaa phool pile rang kaa savrunt, niyamit tathaa apurna ghantakaar hota. nar evam maadaa pushp alag-alag hote hai. nar evam maadaa donon pushpon main paanch jode bahyadal evam paanch jode pile rang ke dalpatr hote hai. nar pushp main teen punkesar hote hai jinamein do ek joda banaakaar evam teesraa swatantra rahata he. maadaa pushp main teen sanyukt andap hote hai jise yuktandap kehete hai. iskaa fal lanbaa yaa golakaar hota he. fal ke andar kaaphi beej paye jate hai. fal kaa vajan 4 se 8 kilogram tak ho saktaa he. sabase badi prajati mexima kaa vajan 34 kilogram se bhi adhik hota he. yah lagbhag sampurna vishwa main ugaaya jaataa he. sanyukt rajya america, mexico, bharat env china iske sabase bade utpaadak desh hai. is paudhe kii aayu ek varsh hoti he. 
 aahaar visheshgyon kaa kahana he ki kaddu hridayrogiyon ke liye atyant laabhadaayak he. yah cholesterol kam karata he, thandak pahunchane vaala or mutrvardhak hota he. yah pet kii gadabadiyon main bhi asardaayak he. kaddu rakt main sharkara kii maatra ko niyantrit karata he or agnyashay ko sakriy karata he. isi kaaran chikitsak madhumeh rogiyon ko kaddu khaane kii salaah dete hai. iskaa ras bhi swasthyavardhak mana gaya he. kaddu main mukhya rup se beta caroten paaya jaataa he, jisase vitamin e miltaa he. pile or naarangi rang ke kaddu main caroten kii maatra adhik hoti he. kaddu ke beej bhi aayran, zinc, potacium or magnesium ke achche strot hai. duniya bhar main istemaal hone ke kaaran hi 29 sitambar ko 'pampkin de' ke rup main manaaya jaataa he.
