 wilks dharti purvi antarctic ek bada bhubhag he, jiskaa anumaanit kshetrafal 26 lakh varg kilometre he. tulana ke liye bharat kaa kshetrafal lagbhag 32 lakh varg kimi he. wilks dharti kshetra tat se dakshini dhruv tak jaataa he or qareeb puraa-kaa-puraa himchadar se dhakaa hua he. iske pashchim main rani meri dharti he or purv main aadeli dharti he. australia yahan par apni samprabhutaa bataata he, lekin na to use vishwa ke anya desh sweekaarte hai or na hi ise antarctic sandhi main manyata di gai he.
 2006 main antariksh yaan dwaara ekatrit gurutvkarshan-sambandhit jaankaree se yah maaloom hua ki wilks dharti ke kshetra main himchadar kii moti barf ke neeche chupa hua ek 300 mile chaudaa prahaar cretar he. adhyayan se vaigyanik anumaan lagate hai ki yah aaj se lagbhag 25 karod varsh purv hue ek kshudragrah prahaar se bana tha.
