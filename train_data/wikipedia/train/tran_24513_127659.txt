 bhupraavaar yaa mental yaa praavaar bhuvigyaan main kisi patharile grah yaa prakritik upgrah kii ek parat ko kehete hai. yah sabase bahari bhupatal naamak parat ke neeche lekin bhukendra ke upar or use dhake hue hoti he. bhupatal ke muqaabale main bhupraavaar parat bahut moti hoti he. hamaari prithvi main bhupraavaar lagbhag 2,900 kimi moti he or yah prithvi ke kul ghanfal kaa 84% bhaag isi main sammilit he.
