 "benipattee" bihar rajya main madhubani jile ke antargat aata he. yahan kalidas ko vidwaan banane vaali mata ucchaith vasini kaa mandir benipattee se 5 kilometre ke duri par he. kaha jaae to mahakavi vidyapati dih or mata ucchaith bhagwati aashirwad praapt kalidas ke dih main madhya main benipattee he. jo kii mithila main apana ek alag pahchaan banaaye hue he.
