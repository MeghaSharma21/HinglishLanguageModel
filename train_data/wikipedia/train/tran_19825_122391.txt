 bhimber zila azaad kashmir kaa dakshintam zila he. pakistan ne adhikrut kashmir ko do bhaagon main vibhajit kara he: azaad kashmir or gilgit-blatistan. bhimber zila inamen se "azaad kashmir" naamak hisse main aata he. iskaa pramukh nagar bhimber he. bharat ise apana bhaag maanata he. is zile kii sthapana san 1996 main hui or us se purv yah mirpur zile kaa bhaag tha.
