 aamu dariyaa madhya asia kii ek badi nadi he jo vaksh or panj nadiyon ke sangam se banati he. is nadi kii kul lambaai 2,400 keelometre he jisamen se 1,450 keemi kii gaharaai or bahaav naav-yatayaat chalane yogya he.
 aamu dariyaa pamir parvatamala main sthit jorkul sarovar se shuru hota he. yahan par iskaa naam pamir nadi he. pamir nadi thodaa dur chalakar vaakhaan nadi ke saath sangam karti he or inke vilay se jo nadi banati he uskaa naam panj nadi he. panj nadi afghanistan kii tajikistan ke saath kii uttari seemaa maani jaati he. isi seemaa par chalate hue tajikistan se dakshin kii ore aati hui vaksh nadi kaa panj nadi main vilay ho jaataa he or is se aage is nadi ko aamu dariyaa kaha jaataa he. kuch dur chalakar is aamu dariyaa kaa rukh uttar kii ore ho jaataa he or ant main jaakar yah kazakhstaan or uzbekistan ke bich sthit aral sagar main villin hoti he.
