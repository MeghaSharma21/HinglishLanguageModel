 jain mandir jain dharm ke anuyaayiyon ke liye bhagwaan kii puja or darshan kaa sthaan he .
 jain mandir kai nirmaan style main banaae jate hai.
 uttar bharat ke jain mandir dakshin bharat ke jain mandiron se kaafii bhinn hote hai. jain mandir do prakaar ke hote hai :
 mandir kaa mukhya bhaag '''garbh grah''' kehalaata he. yahan bhagwaan kii pratimaa sthapit kii jaati he. isamen bina nahaaye or puja ke vastron ke bina nahin janaa chahie.
 100 se adhik varsh purana mandir tirth kehalaata he. mukhya vedi par virajman bhagwaan mandir ke moolnaayak kehalaate he. jain samaaj main aise kai mandir hai jahaan murti ke sthaan par jinvani rakhi hoti hai.aise mandiron ko chaityalayon kehete hai inamen digambar jain taaran panthi puja karne jate hai.yah bundelkhand main adhik paye jate hai.
