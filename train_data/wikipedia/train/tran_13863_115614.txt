  baahubal upjila, bangladesh kaa ek upazila he, joki bangladesh main tritiy str kaa prashasnic anchal hota he . yah cilette vibhaag ke habiganj zile kaa ek upjila he, jisamen, zila sadar samet, kul 8 upazile hai, or mukhyalay habiganj sadar upjila he. yah bangladesh kii rajdhani dhaka se uttar-purv kii disha main avasthit he. yah mukhyatah ek gramin kshetra he, or adhikansh aabaadi graamy ilaakon main rahati he. 
 yahan kii aadhikarik str kii bhashaayen bangla or angrezi he. tathaa bangladesh ke kisi bhi anya kshetra kii tarah hi, yahan kii bhi pramukh maukhik bhashaa or maatrubhaasha bangla he. bengali ke alaava angrezi bhashaa bhi kai logon dwaara jaani or samajhi jaati he, jabaki saanskrithik or aitihaasik nikatataa tathaa bhashaai samanta ke kaaran, kai log simit maatra main hindustani bhi samajhane main sakasham hai. yahan kaa bahusankhyak dharm, islam he, jabaki pramukh alpsankhyak dharm, hindu dharm he. cilette vibhaag main, janasankhyikeek rup se, islam ke anuyai, aabaadi ke ausatan kareeb 88% he, jabaki shesh jansankhya pramukhtah hindu dharm kii anuyai he. yah mukhyatah gramin kshetra he, or adhikansh aabaadi graamy ilaakon main rahati he.
 baahubal upjila bangladesh ke purvi seemaant main sthit, cilette vibhaag ke habiganj jile main sthit he.
