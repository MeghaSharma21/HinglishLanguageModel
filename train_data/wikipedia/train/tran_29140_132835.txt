 karpathi parvat purvi or madhya europe main sthit lagbhag 1,500 kimi lambi ek parvatamala he. 1,700 kimi chalane vaali scandinevian parvatamala ke baad yah europe kii duusari sab se lambi parvat shrunkhlaa he. isamen europe ke vanya jivan ek bada hissaa rahata he, jisamen bhure bhaaluu, bhediye, shamva or links shaamil hai. europe main milane vaali paudhon-vrikshon kii jatiyon main se ek-tihaai inhi pahaado main milti he. rus ke alaava, europe ke wanon kaa bada ansh karpathi kshetra main hi sthit he. yah pahaad check ganatantr, slovakia, poland, hungary, ukraine or romania main vistrut hai, lekin inkaa sabase bada failaav romania main he.
 paaramparik rup se karpathi parvaton ko teen bhaagon main dekha jaataa he: pashchimi karpathi, madhya karpathi . karpanthiyon kii sabase unchi upashrenee kaa naam tathra parvat shrunkhlaa he, jinhen anaupchaarik rup se 'unche tathra' bhi kaha jaataa he. karpathi parvaton kaa sabase unchaa pahaad - slovakia main sthit 2,655 meter lambaa gerlachovsaki shtit - isi tathra shrenee kaa hissaa he.
