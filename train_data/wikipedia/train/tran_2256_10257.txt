 sindhi topi ek vishesh topi he jo ek mahirabi shakl main aage se kati hui hoti he. aajkal ye topi pathaanon or sarayaki logon ke dwaara bhi pahani jaati he. prayek varsh sindhi samskruti evam bhashaa kii hamil is topi ko 21 disambar ko ek vishesh din ke rup main nirdisht kiya gaya he. yah topi sindhi rashtrawaad kaa ek pratik he.
