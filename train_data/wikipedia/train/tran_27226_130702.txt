 pagamark vah shabd he jo kii bahut se jaanwaron ke pairo ke nishaan ke liye bolaa jaataa he! sabhi jaanwaron kaa pagamark or pad chinh alag-alag hota he to yah unako ek dusre se vishesh banataa he or unki pahchaan main bhi madad karata he!
 pad chinh kii visheshtaye:-
 baagh ke padchinh kaa sancha banaaya jaataa he or ucch str par vishleshan karke yah pataa kiya jaataa he kii wo maadaa baagh ke pad chinh he yaa nr baagh ke! or phir un sabakaa record rakhaa jaataa he! baagh ke padchinh se ham unke hone kii ginati kaa record rakh sakate he jisase kii ham yah jaan sakate he kii kitne baagh ab bache hue he!
 pad chinh kaa sankalan ham 2 prakaar se kr sakate he:-
 is prakaar se padchinh kaa sankalan kiya jaataa he or yah pahchaan karke us padchinh kaa record banane main madadgaar he!
