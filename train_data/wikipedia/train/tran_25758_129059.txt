 borosilicket kaanch ek prakaar kaa kaanch hota he, jo silica or boron trioxide se mil kar bana ek yogic he.
 is kaanch kaa nirmaan germany ke vaigyanik oto shot ne kiya tha.
 iskaa upyog prayogshaala main upyog main laaye jane vaale kaanch ke upkaran jaise kvathan nalee main kiya jaataa he. jisase yah ucch taapmaana main bhi thik rahata he or saamaanya kaanch kii tarah tuutata yaa pighalta nahin he.
 borosilicket kaanch ke gun nimnlikhit teen binduon dwaara bataaye ja sakate ham-
 'borosilicket' naam isamen sarvaadhik maatra main upasthit do yogicon - silica or borate ke kaaran padaa he.
