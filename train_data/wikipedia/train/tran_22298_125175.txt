 furnando jose torace sanj kaa janm 20 march 1984 ko hua tha. fernando torress chelsi or spain kii rashtriya team ke liye ek striker ke rup main khilne vaale ek spanish footbal khilaadi hai. unako al-nino ke naam se bhi bulaayaa jaataa he jiskaa matlab spanish main baccha hota he.
 torace ne atletico madrid ke saath apane karier kii shuruyaat kii thi or us team ke saat torress ne 174 laa liga ki match kheli or jisme torress ne 75 gol daage. torress ne apane l liga ke pratham pravesh ke alava torress ne segunda prabhag main do satron nibhaaye he jisme torress ne 40 match me 7 gol daage he.
 torress 2007 main premier lig ke liverpool namak club main shaamil ho gaye. torress ke liverpool me apane fehele charan me 20 lig gol dagi jo torress ke alava sreeff robi baheliya ne 1995-96 me digi thi.
 vah liverpool ke itihaas main sabase teji se 50 lig gol score karne vale khilaadi bane. vah 2008 or 2009 main fifa vishwa 11 me saamil kiye gayi the. torress ne janvari 2011 me chelsi main shaamil hone ke liye club chhod diya or unka 50 lakh pound kaa ek record british hastantaran shulk dekar use itihaas main sabase mahangaa spanish khilaadi banaaya. chelsi ke apane pehale charan me torace ek apekshaakrit kam gol scoring record ke liye aalochana praapt hone ke baavjood, egge cap or uef champions lig jite. unke chelsi ke baad ke charan me torress ne 2012/13 ke uef europa lig ke final me ek gol dag kar chelse ko uef europa lig ko peheli jeetneme madaat kii. torress team ke saathi juaan mata ke saat shaamil ho gaye jo champions lig, europa lig, vishwa cap or europea championships ko ek saath jeeta.
 torace ek spanish antarrashtriya he or 2003 main portugal ke khilaaf apni shuruyaat kii. unko kaha ki 100 se adhik baar chaaya hua he or sabhi samay ke apane desh kii teesri sabase oonchi gol karne vaale khilaadi kar diya gaya he. yuiefee euro 2004, 2006 fifa vishwa cap, yuiefee euro 2008, 2010 fifa vishwa cap or UEFA euro 2012; spain ke saath vah paanch pramukh toornament main bhaag liya he. span ne bad me 3 toornament jite, jisme torress ne euro 2008 or euro 2012 me gol dagaa.
 fooeenlabiada main janme, madrid ke samudaay, torace ek bachche ke rup main footbal main dilchaspee ban gaye or paanch saal kii umr main apni pehali team, parkue 84 main shaamil ho gaye. unke pita jose torace, torace kbae chapan ke dauraan kaam kiya karete the or uski maan flori sanj prashikshan satr ke liye uske saath jati thi.
 torace ek golkeeper ke rup main footbal khelnaa shuru kare jo sthiti unke bhai khelte the. vah saat saal kaa the jab unhone striker ke rup main niyamit rup se khelnaa shuru kar diya. teen saal baad, 10 varsh kii aayu main, vah 11 ore ki team rayo 13 mein samil huye. torress ne vah 50 gol dage jiske karan unke atletico ke pareekshan kamaane moka mila.
 farvari 2001 main torace, spain rashtriya under -16 team ke saath algarve toornament jeeta aurmai 2001 main yuiefee europea under -16 footbal championships main bhaag liya or us toornament ko jite jisme torress ne ek matra gol dag tha. sitambar 2001 main, torace, ne fifa under -17 vishwa championships main under -17 team kaa pratinidhitiv kiya tha. torace portugal ke khilaaf ek dostana main 6 sitambar2003 ko varisht team ke liye padaarpan kiya tha. spain ke liye unka pehla gol28 april 2004 ko italy ke khilaaf aayaa tha. unhe yuiefee euro 2004 ke liye spanish team ke liye chunaa gaya tha. unhone 2006 fifa vishwa cap ke 11 maichon main 7 gol dage jo unhe span kai sheersh scorer banay or unhone belgium ke khilaaf mahatvapurn do gol or san marino ke khilaaf apana pehla antarrashtriya hetric mara. 2010 me fifa vishwa cap main spain ke liye apana 60 waan prakat kiya jisse ve pele aese yuv khilaidi bane jisne ye mukam paye tha.
 torace ko may 2010 me fifa vishwa cap team ke liye chunaa gaya tha. torace final main 105 minute par ek vikalp ke rup main aae the or vah fifa vishwa cap 11 july 2010 ko netherland par 1-0 se jeeta. torace bosk kii yuiefee euro 2012 span ke team main chunaa gaya tha. apane pehle shurgat me unhone do gol dage or ireland ko 4-0 se haray or unko toornament se baahar kar diya. yuiefee euro 2012 ke final main torress ek vikalp ke rup main aae the or uehone ek gol or ek gol marne me sahaayta kii or spen 4-0 kii jeet ke saath lagaataar duusari baar europea championships jeeta. iske karan torress golden boot jitne me khmeyab hua. 20 june 2013 ke fifa confedereon cap me taahiti par ek 10-0 kii jit mila jisame torress ne 4 gol dage jise vah chaar gol score karne ke liye itihaas main pehle vyakti bane. torace fifa confederesan cap main do hetric score karne vaale pehle khilaadi ban gaye.
 rank dar rank pragati karte hue torress ne 1998 main apana pehla mahatvapurn yuva khitaab jeeta. 1999 main, 15 saal ki umr me torress ne atletico ke saat peshewar anubandh par hastakshrar kiye. july 2003 may jaldi hi sangh ke aadhigrahan ke baad chelsi ke maalik roman abramovich ne torress ke liye 28 million euro ki boli lagaai jise atletico ke board ne kaarij kar diya.
 san 2003-04 main saatven sthaan par aakar atletico UEFA cap ke liye arhata praapt karte karte reh gaya or 2004 main ve UEFA intertoto cap ke liye art hry, torress ko pehali baar european str par pratyogita kaa swaad dete hue.
 2006 FIFA vishwa cap ke baad unhone 2005-06 ke ant main chelsi main shaamil hone kaa prastaav thukraa diya tha.
 torress ne 2006-07 kaal main 14 lig gols liye. kuch dinon baad khaabaron se aisa vyateet hua ki torress ko lekar atletico kaa liverpul ke saath samjhauta hua tha. aisa kaha jaataa tha ki is samjhaute kii raashi 25 million euro thi.
 torress ne austn vila ke khilaaf liverpool ke liye 2-1 kii jeet se,11 agst 2007 ko apni pratispradhi shruwaat kii.tuluus ke upar apane pehle pradarshan main 1-0 kii jeet hasil karne ke chaar din baad unhone UEFA champions lig main apni pehali upasthiti banaai. jab media ne kaha ki aisi afvaah thi ki unke liverpool chhodane se pehle 'kai saal' guzar chuke honge. liverpool ke sah maalik ne bhi is hastaantaran ke vichaar ko yah kah kar nakaar diya ki ve torress ko kisi bhi keemat par sangh chhodane ki anumati nahin denge.
 torress ne 24 mai, 2009 ko totenham ke kilaaf, liverpool ke liye apana pachaasavaan gol 2008-09 kaal ke antim din par liya.2009-10 kaal ke anth ke baad unhon kaha ki vah14 agst main liverpool jo hstakshrar kiya uske saath ek nae anubandh par sahamat hue. is anubandh ko hasttakshar karne se, 2013 main anubandh kii samaapti ke baad torress ek saal kaa vistaar karne kaa viclp tha.
 torress ne apane purv club liverpul ko 1-0 se haraakar, 6 farvari 2011 ko, chelsi main apana pehla pradarshan kiya. chelsi main unka pehla puraa kaal 49 khelon main 11 gol banaakar hua.
 22 oktober 2013 ko torress ne ek champions lig match main epa si skalake ke khilaaf apni sauveen shuruyaat kii or teen golon kii jeet se us avasar ko chinhit kiya. 14 disambar ko torress ne chelsi kii crystal pales ke upar do golon kii jeet kii shuruyaat kii.
 unke bare main kaha jaataa he ki, ve takneekee rup se vishwa str kii kushalta rakhte hai or ek atyaadhik safal strikar hai. unki gati or sthiti unke khel kaa mukhya hissaa he ev sabase ucch scoring ke liye ve apni gati par bharosa rakhte hai. lekin haal hi main apane ghutane or hemestring kii choton kii vajah se unki gati dhimi hoti nazar ai he.
 unki shaadi 27 may 2009 main sirf do mehmaanon ki upasthiti main olala dominiguez liste se hui jiske saath ve 2001 se the. is dampatti ke do bachche hai - ek beti, nora, jiskaa janm 8 july 2009 ko laa rosaileda aspataal, santiyago de compestela main hua or ek betaa, lio, jiskaa janm 6 disambar 2010 ko liverpul vimens aspataal main hua.
 2009 main yah soochna mili thi ki unki niji sampatti 14 million euro kii thi. unhen ek spanish pop rock dal ke sangeet video main bhi dekha gaya he. 2009 main unhone 'torress el nino : maai story' naamak apni aatmakatha bhi prakashit kii.
