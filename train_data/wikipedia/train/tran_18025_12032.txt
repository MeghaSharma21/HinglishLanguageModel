 kapalkundla bankimachandra chattopadhyaay dwaara rachit bengali bhashaa kaa ek prem upanyaas he. isaki rachana 1866 main hui thi. isamen 'kapalkundala' naamak ek vanavaasi kanya kii kahani he jo saptagram ke navakumar naamak ek ladke se pyaar karne lagati he or phir usase vivaah kar leti he. upanyaas main dikhaaya gaya he ki vah vanavaasi kanya nagar ke jivan se talmail nahin baitha paati he.
