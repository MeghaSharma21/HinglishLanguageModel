 vaidic jayotish main, lagn us kshan ko kehete hai jis kshan aatma dharti par apni nayi deh se sanyukt hoti he. vyakti ke janm ke samay purvi kshitij par jo raashi udit ho rahi hoti he uske kone ko lagn kehete hai.
 janm kundalee main 12 bhav hote he. in 12 bhaavon main se pratham bhav ko lagn kaha jaataa he. iskaa nirdhaaran balak ke janm ke samay purvi kshitij main udit hone vaali raashi ke aadhaar par kiya jaataa he. saral shabdon main ise is prakaar samajha ja saktaa he. yadi puure aasmaan ko 360 degree kaa maanakaar use 12 bhaagon main baanta jaye to 30 degree kii ek raashi nikalati he. inhi 12 raashiyon main se koi ek raashi balak ke janm ke samay purv disha main sthit hoti he. yahi raashi janm ke samay balak ke lagn bhav ke rup main ubhar kar saamne aati he. 
 ek lagn samay lagbhag do ghante kaa hota he. isaliye do ghante ke baad lagn samay swat: badal jaataa he. kundalee main anya sabhi bhaavon kii tulana main lagn ko sabase adhik mahatv purna mana jaataa he. lagn bhav balak ke swabhaav, ruchi, visheshtao or charitra ke gunon ko prakat karata he. maatr lagn jaanane ke baad kisi vyakti ke swabhaav va visheshtaon ke vishay main 50% jaankaree di ja sakati he. 
 jyotish main lagn kundali kaa bada mahatv he. vyakti ke janm ke samay akaash main jo raashi udit hoti he, use hi uske lagn kii sangya di jaati he. kundali ke pratham bhav ko lagn kehete hai. prayek lagn ke liye kuch grah shubh hote hai, kuch ashubh.. yadi lagn bhav main 1 ank likhaa he to vyakti kaa lagn mesh hoga. isi prakaar agar lagn bhav main 2 he to vyakti kaa lagn vrishabh hoga. anya lagnon ko isi prakaar samajha ja saktaa he.
