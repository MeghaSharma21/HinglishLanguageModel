 sukhoi esayu-9 dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke kuch samay baad soviet sangh main banaaya gaya ek jet tha. dizin 1944 main shuru hua or iskaa uddeshy soviet dizin terbojet ingine kaa upyog karnaa tha. iskaa dizin par kabja kiye gaye german jet kaa prabhaav dikhataa he. sukhoi esayu-9 pratispardhi soviet vimaan se dhimi tha or jiske parinamswaroop ise radd kar diya gaya tha. iskaa aadhaar par vibhinn injanon ke saath ek sanshodhit sanskaran or ek sanshodhit pankh sukhoi esayu-11 ban gaya. lekin iskaa utpaadan nahin kiya gaya.
