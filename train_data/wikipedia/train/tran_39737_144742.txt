 ajay singh deol hindi filmon ke ek bhartiya abhinayta hai. vah beete jamaane ke mashhoor bollywood abhinayta dharmendra ke putr he. tathaa inaki mata kaa naam prakaash cor tathaa hema malini inaki sauteli maa he. sani deol ke bhai kaa naam bobi deol he.
 sani kaa janm 19 oktober 1956 ko sahanewaal, ludhiana main hua tha. sani kii maa or dharmendra kii pehali patni kaa naam prakaash cor he. abhinayta bobi deol tathaa isha deol sani ke bhai-bahan he.
 sani deol ne kaaphi filmon main abhinay kiya he inaki pehali film betaab thi jo 1983 main pradarshit hui thi. 2016 main sambhavtah farvari maah main inaki ghayal vans again film pradarshit hui thi, is film ko sani deol ne khud ne likhi he. is film kaa sikval ghayal film he jo 1990 main bani thi. 
 sani deol kii border tathaa gadar ityadi filmein kaaphi hit hui he.
