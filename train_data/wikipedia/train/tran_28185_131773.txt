 ahimsa kii murti maangithungi, nasik, maharashtra main sthit hai. murti kii oonchai 108 feet he. murti rishabhagiri pahari par sthit he. maangi tungi ek siddh kshetra he, yani kii yahan se kai muniyon ne moksh praapt kiya he. isiilie jain dharmavalambiyon ke liye yah ek pramukh tirth sthal he. aisa mana jaataa he kii shri ram or unke senapati ne yaheen se moksh praapt kiya tha.
