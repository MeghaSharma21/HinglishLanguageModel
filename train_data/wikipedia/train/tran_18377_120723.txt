 tamanna bhaatia bhartiya abhinetri or model hai. keval tamanna naam se bhi jaani jaati hai, jo mukhyat dakshin bharat ke cinema main kaam karti hai. tamanna ne apane kariyar kii shuruyaat 2005 main hindi film chaand sa roshan cheharaa se kii thi. jiske baad wo dakshin kii filmein karne lagi. 2013 main unhonen hindi filmon main vaapasi kii himmatwala se jisamen wo ajay devgan ke saath thi, 2014 main unki pehali hindi film hamshakl thi jisamen saf ali khan, ritesh deshmukh, bipasha basu or isha gupta bhi pramukh bhoomikaaon main the. unki agali hindi film antertenment thi jisamen wo akshay kumaar ke vipareet hai. telugu or tamil filmon kii wo jaani-maani abhinetri hai.
