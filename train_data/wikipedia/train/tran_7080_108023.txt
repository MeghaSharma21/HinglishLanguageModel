 kapler-10, jise kapler-10e or keoai-72 ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he, prithvi se 564 prakaash varsh dur shishumaar taramandal ke kshetra main sthit ek G shrenee kaa mukhya anukram tara he. yah aakaar main hamaare suraj se zaraa bada, dravyaman main zaraa chota or or taapmaana main us se thanda he. 2011 main is taare ke ird-gird hamaare saura mandal se bahaar mila sab se pehla patthariilaa grah parikrama karata paaya gaya tha.
 kapler-10 taare ke ird-gird do gair-saureey grah parikrama karte hue paae gaye hai, jinamein se kam-se-kam ek prithvi jaisaa patthariilaa grah he. yah donon grah is prakaar hai:
 kapler-10 ek G shrenee kaa mukhya anukram tara he. iskaa dravyaman hamaare suraj ke dravyaman kaa 0.895 guna or iskaa vyas hamaare suraj ke vyas kaa 1.056 guna he. isaki aayu 11.9 arab varsh anumaanit kii gai he - tulana ke liye hamaare suraj kii aayu keval 4.6 arab varsh he. iskaa satahi taapmaana 5,627 calvin he, jabaki hamaare suraj kaa taapmaana 5,778 calvin he.
