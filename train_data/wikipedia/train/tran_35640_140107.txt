 1959 europea cap final main chauthe final tha europea cap, ab yuiefee champions lig ke rup main janaa jaataa he. yah dwaara chunaav ladaa tha real madrid ke spain or sted di rams-shampagne se france. yah main nekar stadium main khelaa gaya tha statagart 80,000 logon ke saamne june 1959 3 par. match 2-0 samaapt hua, jo real madrid jeeta or ek pankti main apane chauthe europea cap jeeta.
