 'kanda ne apana pyano-prashikshan pro0 guhardil karmanolu chaakarsoy se praapt kiya. 1989 main ve bilqend vishvavidhyalay main sangeet or nishpaadan kalaon kii shiksha se judi or pro0 erasin oriaye ke maargdarshan main padhi. 1989 main use is kshetra main pratham sthaan praapt hua. 1999 main usne snatakottar oopaadhi praapt kii or kala main nipunata kii shiksha usne 2003 main puraa kiya. uski bahan yaasamin dalkalach vishwa-kirtiman sthapit karanevaali swatantra tairaak he.
