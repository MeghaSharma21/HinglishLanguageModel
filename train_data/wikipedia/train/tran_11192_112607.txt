 peloyosea prithvi ke bhuvaigyanik itihaas main ek kalp tha jo aaj se 6.6 karod varsh pehle armbh hua or 2.303 karod varsh pehle ant hua. yah drishyajivi eon ke nutanjivi mahakalp kaa aarmabhik kalp tha or iske baad usee mahakalp kaa niogen kalp aayaa. is se pehle madhyajivi mahakalp kaa chaakmay kalp chal rahaa tha. peloyosea or chaakmay kii samay-seemaa par kriteshs-peliosin vilupti ghatana hui jisamen prithvi kii bahut-si jeev jatiyaan maari gai or stanadhariyon ko ubharne kaa avasar mila.
