 jayanti natarajan ek bhartiya vakil or raajnneetijna hai. ve bhartiya rashtriya congress kii sadasya hai or rajya sabha main tamilnadu rajya ke pratinidhi ke taur par teen baar sansad sadsy nirvaachit hui hai. vah kendriya mantrimandal main mantri bhi rah chuki hai.
 jayanti natarajan kaa janm tamilnadu main ek mudalier parivaar main hua tha. unke dada em.bakthvatsalam ek pramukh congressi netaa the or 1963 se 1967 ke bich tamilnadu ke mukhyamantri the. jayanti ne kanoon kaa adhyayan kiya or madras main apni vakaalat karne lageen. apane kanoonee abhyaas ke atirikt ve akhil bhartiya mahila sammelan or kanoonee sahaayta board jaise kai saamaajik sanghathanon ke liye niyaswaarth kaam bhi karti rahi hai. 
 unka raajnneetik kariyar, 1980 ke dashak main rajeev gandhi kii unpar najar padane ke saath shuru hua. ve pehali baar 1986 main rajyasabha ke liye chuni gayi or 1992 main phir se chunakar ain.
 90 ke dashak main jayanti natarajan or tamilnadu ke anya netaa jo narsimha raav se naakhush the, unhone party chhodane kaa nirnay kiya. unhone ji ke mupanaar ke netritv tamil manilla congress kii sthapana kii. jayanti natarajan ne rajyasabha se istipha de diya or 1997 main tiemasi sadsy ke rup main dobaaraa chuni gayi.
 tiemasi tamilnadu main dravid munetra kashgam ke saath judi thi or centre main sanyukt morcha sarkaar kaa hissaa thi. jayanti natarajan ko 1997 main koyala, naagarik uddyan or sansadeey kaaryon kaa rajya mantri niyukt kiya gaya.
 unka betaa ek peshewar vakil he.
 mupanaar kii mrityu ke baad tiemasi ke netaaon ne congress ke saath milane kaa nirnay liya. jayanti natarajan par sonia gandhi kii najar padi or unhen party kii pravaktaa niyukt kar diya.
