 bhartiya chartered vittiya vishleshak santhaan kii sthapana 1984 main chaatro, kaamkaazi igzextivon or vyavsaayiyon ko vitt or prabandhan main ucch gunvattta shiksha pradaan karne ke uddeshy se kholaa gaya tha. yah vishvavidhyalay uttrakhand, tripura, sikkim, meghalaya, mizoram, nagaland or jharkhand rajyon main kramshah vidhayikaon ke adheen he. rajasthan, punjab or chhattisgarh kii rajya sarkaron ne vishvavidhyalay ko apane rajyon main isaki shakhaayen kholane ke liye ichha patr jari kiye hai. prayek rajya kaa vishvavidhyalay pruthak or swatantra vaid sanstha he.
