 bhartiya sena ke yuddh sammaan un ullekhneey seva dene vaale sainiko ko die jate he jinhone shanti athwa yuddh kaal main apni vishesh sevaaye di. bhartiya sashastr senaayen sainya sammaan ke asankhya padako kii paatr hai. asaadhaaran bahaduree or saahas ke liye inhen sammanit kiya jaataa he, saath hi saath yuddh or shanti ke dauraan aneko seva or abhiyaan padak se sammanit kiya gaya he.

 yuddh kshetra main veerta ke krityon ko manyata dene ke liye ,1 9 47 ke baad se preshan kaa ullekh kiya gaya he,ye un sainiko ke liye hai jo veerta puraskaar praapt karne ke liye paryaapt paatrata nahin rakhte. yogya karmiyon main sena, nausena or vayusena ke sabhi karmchaaree shaamil hai jinamein rigerv force, pradeshik sena, milicia or anya kanoonee rup se gathit sashastr balon, nursing seva ke sadasyon or sashastr balon ke tahat kaam karne vaale naagarik shaamil hai.preshan main mrityu ke baad carmikon kaa ullekh kiya ja saktaa he or kai puraskaar bhi sambhav hai. preshan main ullekith praptakarta ko praasangik abhiyaan padak ke riban par ek camel ke rup main ek pratik pahanaaya jaataa he. unhen raksha mantralay se aadhikarik pramaan patr jari kiya jaataa he.
