 samay niyantran kaa kaary do khilaadiyon vaale lagbhag sabhi board khelon main kiya jaataa he taaki khel kaa prayek pari samay se samaapt ho sake or khel aage badhata rahe. is kaary ke liye praayah khel ghadi prayog main laayi jaati he. jab kisi khilaadi ke paas apana chaal chalane ke liye bahut kam samay shesh bachaa hota he to us sthiti ko 'samay dabaav' kaha jaataa he.
