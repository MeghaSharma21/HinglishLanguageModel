 sulabha deshpandey, hindi filmon kii ek abhinetri thim. unhone vijay tendulkar jaise prakhyat natya lekhakon dwaara likhe naatakon main abhinay ke alaava kai marathi or hindi filmon tathaa tivi serialon main kaam kiya tha. unhone hindi cinema main ‘bhoomika’, ‘arvind desai kii ajeeb daastaan’,‘gaman’ aadi filmon main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai thi. unhone apane pati arvind deshpandey ke saath varsh 1971 main theatre group ‘avishkaar’ kaa gathan kiya tha. varsh 2012 main unhone gauri scindia nirdeshit film ‘english winglish’ main kaam kiya tha. unhen varsh 1987 main hindi-marathi theatre main abhinay ke liye sangeet naatak academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha.
