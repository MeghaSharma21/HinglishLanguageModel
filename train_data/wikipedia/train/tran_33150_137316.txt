 scot keli ek amareeki antariksh yatri hai, jo antarrashtriya antariksh stesan main lagbhag ek varsh rahane ke liye jane jate hai.keli kaa pehla antariksh yaan spes shatal discovery ke payalat ke rup main tha, disambar 1 999 main ests -103 ke dauraan. yah habal spes telescop ke liye teesraa servicing mishan tha, or yah aath dinon ke liye chali thi. keli kaa doosraa antariksh yaan ests -118 ke mishan commander ke rup main tha, jo agast 2007 main antarrashtriya antariksh stesan ke liye 12-divasiy antariksh shatal mishan tha. keli kaa teesraa antariksh yaan ieses par ekspedishan 26 ke commander the. vah 9 oktober 2010 ko ek roosi soyuj antariksh yaan par pahunchaa, expedishan 25 ke dauraan, or yah samaapt hone tak ek flite ejeeniyar ke rup main kaam kiya. unhone 25 november 2010 ko stesan kii kaman sanbhali, 26 abhiyaan kii shuruyaat main aadhikarik taur par shuru hua jab antariksh yaan soyus tieme -19 andcold, stesan ke pichley commander duglas ech. whealock ko le gaye. 16 march 2011 ko soyuj tieme 01 em ke prasthaan ke saath abhiyaan 26 kaa samaapan hua. yah keli kii pehali lambii avadhi antariksh udaan thi
 november 2012 main, keli ko mikhail corneanko ke saath, ek vishesh 340 din ke liye chunaa gaya, taaki antarrashtriya antariksh stesan ke liye varmbhar kaa mishan kiya ja sake. antariksh main unka varsh 27 march 2015 ko expishdan 43 kii shuruyaat ke saath shuru hua, jisamen nirantar 44 abhiyaan or 45 ke dauraan jari rakhaa gaya tha, jo donon ne keli kaa aadesh diya tha. unhone 29 farvari 2016 ko timothy kopra ko aadesh paarit kiya, jab ieses 11 maheene kaa mishan samaapt hua. vah 1 march 2016 ko soyuj tieme -18 em par dharti par laut aayaa.
 oktober 2015 main, unhone antariksh ke kharch main 520 ankon kii kul sanchit sankhya ke liye record banaaya. yah record 2016 main antariksh yatri jaiff williams dwaara kholaa gaya tha. tathakthith ieses saal ke lambey mishan ke liye, keli ne antariksh main lagaataar 340 din kharch kiye. keli ke samaan judawaan bhai, mark keli, ek purv antariksh yatri he keli bhai antariksh main yatra karne vaale ekmaatr bhai hai. 12 march 2016 ko, kaili ne april 2016 ke liye apni sevanivritti kii ghoshnaa kii.
