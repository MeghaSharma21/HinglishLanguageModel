 yarakanda zila china ke shinjiyang praant ke kashgar vibhaag main sthit ek zila he. iskaa kshetrafal 8,969 varg kimi he or san 2003 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 3,73,492 anumaanit kii gai thi. isaki rajdhani yaarakanda naam kaa hi ek aitihaasik shahar he jiskaa bhartiya upmahadweep ke saath gaharaa saanskrithik or vyapaarik sambandh rahaa he. yarakanda tarim droni or taklamkaan registan ke dakshini chor par sthit ek nachlistaan kshetra he jo apana jal kunlun parvaton se uttar kii ore utarane vaali yarakanda nadi se praapt karata he. iske marudyan kaa kshetrafal lagbhag 3,210 varg kimi he lekin kabhi is se zyaada hua karata tha. teesri sadi isvi main yahan registan ke failane se yah upjau ilaaka thodaa ghat gaya.
 yarakanda main adhiktar uigur log basate hai. yahan kapaas, genhu, makki, anaar, khubaani, akhrot or naashpati ugaae jate hai. is zile ke unche ilaaqon main yaks or bhed paale jate hai. yahan kii dharti ke neeche bahut se moolyawaan khanij maujuud hai, jaise kii petrol, prakritik gas, sona, tamba, sisa or koyala.
 yarakanda shinjiyang praant ke mahatvapurn or aitihaasik kashgar or khotaan shaharon ke bich sthit he. kisi jamaane main kashmir se vyapaaree laddakh or karakoram darre se hote hue yahan maal liye hue aayaa karte the. aadhunik yug main china kaa shinjiyang-tibet rajmarg yahan se shuru hokar, aksai chin ke raste se hokar tibet ke lhatse shahar tak jaataa he. yahan se ek or aitihaasik rasta dakshin-pashchim kii taraf nikalataa he jo tashkurgan shahar se hote hue afghanistan ke vaakhaan galiyare main daakhil hota he or phir broanil darre se guzarate hue afghanistan ke badakhshaan kshetra pahunchataa he.
 yarakanda kaa mausam shushk he or kul milakar har varsh sirf 53 millimetre kii barf-barish padati he. janvari main ausat taapmaana −5.5 °centegrade or july main 25.4 °centegrade rahata he.
