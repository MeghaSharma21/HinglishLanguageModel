 aarthik mulya, kisi aarthik agent ko koi vastu yaa seva dwaara pradutt laabh kaa maap hai.
 yah dhyaan main rakhaa janaa chahie ki aarthik mulya or bazaar bhav samaan nahin hai, or naa hi aarthik mulya or bazaar mulya ek samaan hai. mulya kii avadharana arthshaastra main kendriya he. isko maapne kaa ek tarika vastu kaa bazaar bhav he. adam smith ne shram ko mulya ke mukhya shrot ke rup main paribhaashit kiya. "mulya ke shram siddhanth" ko karl marks sahit kai arthshaastriyon ne pratipaadit kiya he. is siddhanth ke anusaar kisi seva yaa vastu kaa mulya uske utpaadan main prayukt shram ke baraabar hot he. adhikansh logon kaa maananaa he ki iskaa mulya vastu ke daam nirdhaarit karata he. daam kaa yah shram siddhanth "mulya ke utpaadan laagat siddhanth" se nikatataa se judaa hua he.
