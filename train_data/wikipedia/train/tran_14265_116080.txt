 taral yantriki taral padaartho ke swabhaav evam gati ke siddhanton ko samajhane vaali yantriki kii ek shakha he. taral, drav yaa gas ho sakate hai or unamem simit maatra main thos ke mili yaa ghule rahane par bhi in siddhanton kaa prayog kiya ja saktaa he. taral padaarth bhi newton ke gati niyamon kaa anusaran karte hai, par aakaar aasaani se badal jane ke swabhaav ke kaaran inke gati niyamon ko vishesh rup diya jaataa he. nevier-stokes sameekaran taral yantriki ke samikarnon kaa sabase vistrut rup he. taral yantriki vivy, yantriki, civil tathaa rasinic yantaaon dwaara mukhya rup se prayukt hota he. 
 jatil taral gatik prashnon ke hal ke liye sanganit taral yantriki kaa prayog kiya jaataa he.
 taralyantriki ganit kii vah shakha he, jisamen taralon ke vyavhaar kaa adhyayan hota he. yadi taral gatiheen he to is adhyayan ko dravasthiti vigyaan kehete hai or yadi taral gatiyukt he to use [[taral gatiki|dravgati vigyaan) kehete hai. donon upashakhaon main maan liya jaataa he ki drav satat padaarth he or visaran tathaa prishthtanav upekshniya hai. dravgati vigyaan main saamanytah drav kaa ghanatv achar maan liya jaataa he, kintu jab vipul dravpunj kii athwa atyat drutagami drav kii baat ho to ghanatv kii yah manyata anaupchaarik ho jaati he. 
 pehle taral-yantriki ke antargat gas-punj kii bhi santulanavastha athwa uski gati kaa adhyayan hota tha, kintu ab yah vayugativigyaan naam se alag vishay kii ban gaya he.
