 indrabhuti gautam tirthankar mahavir ke pratham ganadhar the.jinhone bhagwaan mahavir ke nirvana ke pashchaat baarah barsho tak jain dharm kii acharya parampara kaa nirvaah kiya.
 inkaa janm magadh rajya ke gochhar gaanv main brahman vasubhuti or prithvi ke ghar hua tha. vah apane gotra 'gautam' se jane jate the.
 digambar parampara ke anusaar jab indra ne indrabhuti se ek shloke kaa arth poocha tha :
 jab vah nahin bataa paae to indra ne une uttar ke liye bhagwaan mahavir ke samavasaran main jane ko kaha. 
 digambar parampara main gautam ganadhar kaa sthaan bahut unchaa he. unka naam bhagwaan mahavir ke turant baad liya jaataa he -
 jis din bhagwaan mahavir ko moksh kii prapti hui thi usee din gautam ganadhar ko keval gyaan kii prapti hui thi. jain dharmavalambiyon dwaara isi din ko diwali ke rup main manaaya jaataa ha
