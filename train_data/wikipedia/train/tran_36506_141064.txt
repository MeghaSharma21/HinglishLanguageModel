 kirtirpu kathmandu ghati ke dakshin-pashchim mai avasthit ek nagar he. yah nagar kathmadon jila mai avasthit he. yah nagar ek pahad mai basaa huwa he. yah ek aitihaasik nagar he. yahan aitihaasik baghbhairav mandir he. is nagar ke uttar-purv mai ghati kaa sabase bada jalbhandar taudaa avasthit he. nepal kaa pratham vishvavidhyalay tribhuvan vishvavidhyalay yahi nagar mai avasthit he.
 kirtipur shabd do sanskrit shabd "kirti" or "poor" mil kar bana he. kirti kaa arth glory or poor kaa arth sthaan hota he. nevaar samudaay ke zyaadaatar nagaron ke naam us nagar se sambandhit kaary mai "poor" jodkar bana hota he . atah, kirti se sambandhit nagar hone ke kaaran yah nagar kaa naam kirtipur ho gaya. 
 yah nagar kaa sthapana lichhavi raja shivdev tritiy ne kiya tha. madhyakaalin nepal mai yah nagar kabhi kantipur, kabhi lalitpur ke adheen mai rahata tha to kabhi swatantra nagar ke rup mai rahata tha. gorkha ke naresh prithvinarayan shah ne is nagar ko teen baar akraman karne ke baad jeeta tha. teen baar akraman karte samay unka senapati kaalu pandey kaa nidhan, unka bhai shurpratap shah ne apana aankh gavaane ke saath hi veh bhi ek baar bal-bal bache the. in yuddhu me kirtipur se purush ke saath-saath mahila bhi apane nagar ke liye lade the. un ke pehle akraman mai sabhi mall rajyoun ne milakar unke biruddh mai lade the. dusre baar mai sirf kirtipur ke sena ne unako haraaya tha or teesri baar mai unhaunne chal se yah nagar jeeta tha. nagar jitne ke baad unhonen is nagar ke sabhi logon ke naak or mahilaon ke bal kaat die the. is beijjati ko kirtipur ke log ab bhi yaad karte he. at, nepal mai raja ke shakti ko simit karne kaa aandolan mai sarvada kirtipur aage rahata he. 
 yah nagar kaa pramukh jati nevaar he. yah nagar nevaar samskruti kaa sab se sangrakshit sthano mai ek he. yahaan kaa mukhya bhashaa nepalbhasha or nepali he. yahaan ke pramukh dharm hindu dharm or buddha dharm he.
