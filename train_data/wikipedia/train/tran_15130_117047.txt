 khuli jagah, khel kaa maidan or picnic manaane kii jagah, ye sab chijen banksia park ko sabhi umr ke logon ke bich lokapriy banaati hai. cycling, jogging or taaji hawa lene ke liye yah park sarvatha upyukt he. yahan par pakshiyon kii lagbhag 80 prajatiyaan pai jaati hai. yahan nadi ke kinar ranbo loricith ko dekha ja saktaa he. talab main tairti kaali batakhon ke jhund bade sukhad prathit hote hai.
