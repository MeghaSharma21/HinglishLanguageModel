 amita sharma jo ek bhartiya mahila cricket khilaadi he ye balle or gend donon se hi jaani jaati he isliye amita harfanmaulaa cricketron kii suchi main aati he.
 sharma ne apana bhartiya team ke liye pehla match 2002 main vanade cricket match ke rup main khelaa tha. sharma ne ab tak a0ne karier main paanch test ,109 vanade or 38 tinti-tinti match khele he sharma ne 2015 mahila cricket vishwa cap main 14 wicket bhi liye the jisaki vajah se bharat aage unnati kar sakaa tha vishwa cap main.
 amita sharma gharelu cricket narthran railway jon ke liye khelti he.
