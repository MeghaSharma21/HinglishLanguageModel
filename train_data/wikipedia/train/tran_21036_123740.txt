 sanyukt raashtra sangh ke apane kai kaaryakramon or sansthaaon ke alaava 14 swatantra sansthaaon se isaki vyavastha gathit hoti he. swatantra sansthaaon main vishwa bank, antarrashtriya mudra kosh or vishwa swasthya sanghathan shaamil hai. inkaa sanyukt raashtra sangh ke saath sahyog samjhauta he. sanyukt raashtra sangh kii apni kuch pramukh sansthaaen or kaarykram hai. 
 ye is prakaar hai:
 vienna main sthit yah agency parmaanu nigaraanee kaa kaam karti he. 
 hegg main sthit yah ayog purv yugoslavia main yudd aparaadh ke sadingdh logon par muqadama chalaane ke liye banaaya gaya he. 
 unicef naamak yah sanstha bacchon ke swasthy, shiksha or suraksha kii dekharekh karata he. 
 yuandipi naamak sanstha garibi kam karne, aadharbhoot dhaanche ke vikaas or prajatantric prashasan ko protsaahit karne kaa kaam karata he.
 yah sanstha vyapaar, nivesh or viks ke muddon se sambandhit uddeshy ko lekar chalati he.
 apane sadsy deshon kii vaishvik aarthik sthiti par nazar rakhane kaa kaam karti he. yah apane sadsy deshon ko aarthik or takanic sahaayta detii he. yah sanghathan anterrashtriya vinimay daron ko sthir rakhane ke saath-saath vikaas ko sugam karne main sahaayta karata he.
 paris main sthit is sanstha kaa uddeshy shiksha, vigyaan samskruti or sanchaar ke madhyam se shanti or vikaas kaa prasar karnaa he. 
 yuanipi naamak yah sanstha nairobi main sthit he. is sanstha kaa kaam paryaavaran kii raksha ko badhaava dena he.
 iskaa kaam sharnaarthiyon ke adhikaron or unke kalyaan kii dekharekh karnaa he. yah jiniva main sthit he. 
 bhukh ke virudd ladaai ke liye banaai gai yah pramukh sanstha he. iskaa mukhyalay rome main he.
 antarrashtriya aadhaaron par mazdooron tathaa shramikon ke hiton kii raksha ke liye niyam vanata he.
 yah sanghathan anterrashtriya vaayu nauvahan ke siddhant or takneekon ko niyat karti he or anterrashtriya vaayu yatayaat ke vikaas or yojana kaa paalan karti he, jisase ki saurakshit or kramvaar vikaas sunishchit ho sake.
