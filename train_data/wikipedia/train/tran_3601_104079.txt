 
 angrezi Long pepper
 pippali, , piperasi parivaar kaa pushpiya paudha he. isaki kheti iske fal ke liye kii jaati he. is fal ko sukhaakar masaale, chhaunk evam audashadheey gunon ke liye ayurveda main prayog kiya jaataa he. iskaa swaad apane parivaar ke hi ek sadsy kaali mirch jaisaa hi kintu usase adhik tikhaa hota he. is parivaar ke anya sadasyon main dakshini yaa safed mirch, gol mirch evam green papper bhi hai. inke liye angrezi shabd papper inke sanskrit evam tamil/malayalam naam pippali se hi liya gaya he.
 vibhinn bhashaaon main iske naam is prakaar se hai:sanskrit pippali, hindi- pipar, pipal, marathi- pipal, gujarati- pipar, bangla- pipul, telugu- pippalu, tippali, farsi- philphil. angrezi- long pipar, latin- pyper langam.
 pippali ke fal kai chote falon se mil kar bana hota he, jinamein se harek ek khaskhas ke dane ke baraabar hota he. ye sabhi milakar ek hejal vriksh kii tarah dikhne vaale aakaar main jude rahete hai. is fal main alkaloid piperine hota he, jo ise iskaa tikhaapan detaa he. isaki anya prajatiyaan java evam indonesia main paayi jaati hai. isamen sugandhit tel, piperine tathaa piplartin naamak ksharabh paae jate hai. inke atirikt do nae taral ksharabh sichemin or piplastirol bhi haal hi mengyaat hue hai. pipar kii jad jise pipla mul bhi kaha gaya he piperin, piplartin, piperlaunguminine, ek stiroed tathaa glicosaida se yukt hoti he.
 pipar yunaan main chhathi evam paanchavin shatabdi ke madhya pahunchi thi. iskaa ullekh hipocratis ne pehali baar kiya or ise ek masaale ke bajaay ek aushadhi ke rup main kiya ta. unaniyon evam romavaasiyon main, amareeki mahadwipon kii khoj se purv hi pipari ek mahatvapurn evam sarvagyaat masala tha.
