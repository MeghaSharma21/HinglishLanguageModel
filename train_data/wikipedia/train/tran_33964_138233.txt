  kaaji nazarul islam, agrani bangla kavi, sangitajna, sangitasrashta or darshanik the. ve bangla bhashaa ke anyatam sahityakar, deshpremi tathaa bangladesh ke rashtriya kavi hai. pashchim bengal or bangladesh dono hi jagah unki kavita or gaan ko samaan aadar praapt he. unki kavita main vidroh ke swar hone ke kaaran unako 'vidrohi kavi' ke naam se janaa jaataa he. unki kavita kaa varnyavishay 'manushya ke upar manushya kaa atyaachaar' tathaa 'saamaajik anachar tathaa shoshan ke viruddh sochchaar pratwaad'.
 najarul kaa janm bharat ke pashchim bengal pradesh ke vardhaman jila main asanasol ke paas churulia gaanv main ek daridr muslim parivaar main hua tha. unki praathmik shiksha dharmik shiksha ke rup main hui. kishoravastha main vibhinn theatre dalon ke saath kaam karte-karte unhone kavita, naatak evam sahitya ke sambandh main samyak gyaan praapat kiya.
 najarul ne lagbhag 3000 gaanon kii rachana kii tathaa saath hi adhikansh ko swar bhi diya. inako aajkal 'najarul sangeet' yaa "najarul geeti" naam se janaa jaataa he. adhed umr main ve 'picks rog' se grasit ho gaye jiske kaaran shesh jivan ve sahityakarm se alag ho gaye. bangladesh sarkaar ke aamantran par ve 1972 main sapariwaar dhaka aaye. us samay unako bangladesh kii rashtriyata pradaan kii gai. yaheen unki mrityu hui.
