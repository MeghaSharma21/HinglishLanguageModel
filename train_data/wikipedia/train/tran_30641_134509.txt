 pitra dure, ek aitihaasik kala he. isamen utkrisht paddati se kate, va jade hue, taraashe hue evam chamakaae hue rangeen pattharon ke tukdo se pathar main chitrakaari kii jaati he. yah sajaavati kala he. is kaary ko, banane ke baad, ekatra kiya jaataa he, evam adhah str par chipkaaya jaataa he. yah sab itani baariki se kiya jaataa he, ki pattharon ke bich kaa maheenatam khaali sthaan bhi adrishy ho jaataa he. is pattharon ke samooh main sthirataa laane hetu ise jig so paheli jaisaa banaya jaataa he, jisase ki prayek tukda apane sthaan par majbootee se tehra rahe. kai bhinn rangeen pathar, khaskar sangmarmar evam bahumoolya pattharon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. yah pratham rome main prayog kii dikhaai detii he 1500 ke aasapaas. jo ki apane charmotkarsh par florence main pahaunchi.
 pitre dure shabd sakht pathar kaa italvi bahuvachan he, yaa tikaau pashaan. 
 yah apane aarmabhik rup main italy main thi, parantu baad main 1600 shatee main, iske chote rup europe main, yahan tak ki mughal darbar main jahaan is kala ko nae aayam mili, sthaneey/ deshi kalakaaron kii style main, jiskaa sabase utkrisht udhaaharan tajmahal main miltaa he. mughal bharat main, ise parchinkaaree yaa pachchikari kaha jaataa tha, jiskaa arth he jadna.
