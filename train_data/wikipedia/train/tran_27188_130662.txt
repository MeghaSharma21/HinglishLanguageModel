 jaspinder narula punjabi gayika or bollywood parshv gayika hai. 1998 kii film pyaar to hona hi tha ke sheershak geet ko remo furnandis ke saath jugalbandi main gaae gaye geet se unhen atyant prasiddhi praapt hui. iske liye unhone 1999 main filmafere sarvshereshth parshv gayika puraskaar jeeta tha. unhone kuch samay ke liye aam aadmi party kii sadsyta bhi lee thi.
