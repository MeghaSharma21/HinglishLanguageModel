 computer vastutah ek abhikalak yantra he jo diye gayi ganitiya tathaa taarkik sankriyaaon ko kram se swachaalit rup se karne main sakasham he. ise ank ganitiya, taarkik kriyaaon va anya vibhinn prakaar kii gananaaon ko sateekta se purna karne ke liye yojanabaddh tareeke se nirdeshit kiya ja saktaa he. chunki kisi bhi kaary yojana ko purna karne ke liye nirdesho kaa kram badla ja saktaa he isliye sanganak ek se zyada tarah kii kaarywahi ko anjaam de saktaa he. is nirdesan ko hi computer programing kehete he or sanganak computer programing bhashaa kii madad se upyogkarta ke nirdesho ko samajhataa he. yantric sanganak kai sadiyon se maujuud the kintu aajkal abhiklitr se aashay mukhyatah beesvin sadi ke madhya main viksit hue viddut chaalit abhiklitr se he. tab se abtak yah aakaar main kramshah chota or sankriya kii drushti se atyadhik samarth hota gaya hai. ab abhikalak ghadi ke andar samaa sakate hai or vidyut kosh se chalaaye ja sakate hai. nijee abhikalak ke vibhinn rup jaise ki suvahya sanganak, tablet aadi rosemara kii jarurat ban gaye hai.
 paramparaagat sanganakon main ek kendriya sanchaalan ikai or soochna bhandaaran ke liye smriti hoti he. sanchaalan ikai ankaganit va taarkik gananaao ko anjaam detii he or ek anukraman va niyantran ikai smriti main rakhe nirdesho ke aadhaar par sanchaalan kaa kram badal sakati he. paridhiy yaa sataha pe lage upkaran kisi bahari strot se soochna le sakate he va kaarywahi ke fal ko smriti main surakshit rakh sakate he va jarurat padane par pun: praapt kar sakate hai.
 ekikrut paripath par aadhaarit aadhunik sanganak puraane jamaane ke sanganakon ke mukable karodo arbo guna zyada samarth he or bahut hi kam jagah lete he. saamaanya sanganak itane chote hote he ki mobile phone main bhi samaa sakate he or mobile sanganak ek choti si vidyut kosh se mili urja se bhi kaam kar sakate he. zyaadaatar log “sanganakon” ke bare main yahi raay rakhte he ki apane vibhinn swaroopon main vyaktigat sanganak soochna praudyogiki yug ke nayak he. haalaanki embedded system|sannihit sanganak jo ki zyaadaatar upkarnon jaise ki aankik shravy vadak|em.pi.3 vadak, vayuyan va khilauno se lekar audyogic maanav yantra main paye jate he logo ke bich zyada prachlit he.
 
 computer shabd kaa pratham prayog varsh 1613 main angrej lekhak richard brethwate kii pustak '"da yang main glinings"' main paaya gaya. maine samay ke sabase sahi computaron ko or dharaa pe janme sarvottam ank ganitagya ko padhaa he. yah us vyakti ke bare main bataata he jo gananaayen karata tha, tabhi se yah shabd 20ve shatabdi ke madhya tak is sandarbh main hoobahoo prayog hota aa rahaa he. unnisvi shatabdi ke ant se is shabd ne or zyada vyavhaarik rup le liya, yani ki wo yantra jo gananaayen karata he.sanganak va abhiklitr naam bharat sarkaar ke maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantralay dwaara jari kiye gaye hai.

 gananaayen karne ke liye yantro kaa istemaal hazaaron varsho se hota aa rahaa he khaskar uglio se ginati karne vaale upkarno kaa. shruwati ganan yantra sambhavat: milan chadi|wo lakdi jis par ginati ke liye daant khode gayi ho yaa milan chadi kaa ek rup thi. baad main madhya purv main upjau bhoomi ke ek bhugolic kshetra jo ki aakaar main arddh chandra jaisaa dikhataa he main abhilikho ko rakhane ke liye calculi kaa istemaal hota rahaa jo kii adhpake or khokhale mitti ke bartano main rakhaa hota tha. inkaa upyog saamaan kii ginati darshane ke liye kiya jaataa tha. ginati kii chade|ginati kii chhadon kaa upyog iskaa ek udahaaran he.
 shruwaat main gintaare kaa upyog ankaganitiya kaaryo ke liye hota tha. jise aaj ham roman gintaara kehete he uskaa upyog 2400 isa purv ke prarambh main babylonia main hua tha. tab se ab tak gadanaa va hisaab lagane ke liye kai anya ganan pattio va golio kaa avishkar ho chukaa he. ek madhyayugin europee gadana ghar|gadanaa ghar main mej par chitkabare kapadey ko rakh diya jaataa tha or kuch vishesh niyamo ke anusaar usapar moharon ko chalaakar paise jodne ke liye ek saadhan ke taur pe istemaal kiya jaataa tha.
 derek je. di-sola ke anusaar antikithera prakriya ko shruwati yantrik anuroop abhiklitr mana jaataa he. ise khagolia sthitiyo kii gadana ke liye banaaya gaya tha. ise antikithera ke unani ddhip ke antikithera bhagnavshesh main 1901 main khoj gaya tha. ise 100 isa purv ke samay kaa paaya gaya. aisa mana jaataa he ki antikithera prakriya jaisi jatilta vaale yantra agle 1000 varsho tak milane mushkil he.
 pracheen or madhyayugin kaalon main khagoleey gananaaon ke nishpaadan ke liye kai analog computaron kaa nirmaan kiya gaya tha. inamen shaamil hai pracheen greece kii anticithera prakriya or estrolab, jinhen aam taur par sabase praarambhik gyaat yantric analog computer mana jaataa he. ek yaa anya prakaar kii gananaaon ke nishpaadan ke liye istemaal kiye jane vaale yantric upkarnon ke anya praarambhik sanskarnon main shaamil hai planisfere or abu rehaan al biruni  ddhara avishkrut anya yantric sanganan upkaran; abu ishak ibrahim al zarkaali  dwaara avishkrut equatorium or universal latitude-independent estrolabel; anya madhyayugin muslim khagolavidon or ejiniyaron ke khagoleey analog computer; or song rajvansh ke dauraan su song kaa khagoleey clock tower.
 al jajari dwaara 1206 main avishkrut ek khagoleey ghadi ko sabase pehla program yogya rekhiy sanganak mana jaataa he. yah raashi chakra, surya or chandramaa kii kakshaon ko darshaati thi, isamen ek arddh-chandrakaar suchak ek sampurna pravesh dwaara se hokar guzarati thi jiske kaaran har ghante par swachaalit ddhaar khul jate the or paanch robotic sangeetkaar jo ek pani ke pahiye se jude camshaft dwaara sanchalit liveron dwaara maare jane par sangeet baja diya karte the. din or raat kii lambaai ko varsh bhar main din or raat kii badalati lambaaiyon ke liye upyukt banane ke kram main har din phir se program kiya ja saktaa he.
 ek abhiklitr nimnlikhit chaar bhaagon se milakar banataa he : nivisht yantra, sansaadhan yantra, nirgam yantra or bhandaaran yantra. 
 abhiklitr kaa mukhya kaary diye gayi aankade ko jamaa kar usapar die gaye nirdeshon ke anuroop kaam kar parinaam dena he. kaaryakshmata ke aadhaar par ise nimnalikit shreniyon main baanta gaya he- super sanganak, menfrem sanganak mini sanganak, ev micro sanganak aadi. super sanganak inamen sabase bady shrenee hoti he, tathaa micro sanganak sabase choti.
 sanganak hamaare dwaara diye jane vaale har kaary ko bakhubi karne main sakasham hote hai. inke kuch gun is prakaar hai :
 sanganak kaaphi tej gati se kaary karte hai, jab ham sanganak ke bare main baat karte hai, to ham mini second, micro second main baat nahin karte, balki ham 10-12 second main ek computer kitna kaary kar letaa he, is rup main uski gati ko aankate hai.
 sanganak kabhi bhi ubate nahin hai or yahi inkaa sabase acha gun he, kyonki yah ek yantra hai, isaliye ye kaaphi dinon tak bina kisi shikaayat ke kaary karne main sakasham hote hai.
 ek saamaanya sanganak bhi ek baar diye gayi nirdesh ko kaaphi samay tak samaran rakhane main sakasham hota he, tathaa jab bhi aavashyaktaa padey, use phir se likhaa or bharaa ja saktaa he.
 abhiklitr jis bhashaa ko samajhataa he use dwiadhari bhashaa kehete hai. vastav main yah yantra keval vidyut dhara ke chaalu yaa band hone ko hi samajhataa he. vidyut pravaah hone evam rukne ko 0 yaa 1 ke jariye vyakt kiya jaataa he. isliye isapar koi kaam karne ke liye ise is bhashaa main nirdesh yaa soochna dena hota he.
 shuruuaati dinon main abhiklitr ko sidhe dwiadhari bhashaa main nirdesh yaa soochna di jaati thi. yantra se sidhaa sampark rahane ke kaaran ise yantra bhashaa bhi kaha jaataa tha. is tarah se nirdesh yaa soochna dene kii yah prakriya kaaphi jatil thi.
 yantra bhashaa kii jatilta ko kam karne ke liye sanyojak kii sahaayta lee gai. yah aisa program tha jo kuch khaas shabdon ko dwiadhari sanketon ke samooh main badal detaa tha. is bhashaa main pratek prakriya ke liye ek saral shabd chun liye gaye the. isse dwiadhari sanket samooh ke bajaay keval sanket shabd likhakar kaam ho jaataa tha. is sanketon dwaara sanyojit tathaa sanyojak kii sahaayta se kaam karne vaali bhashaa ko sangyojan bhashaa kaha gaya.
 asembli lengvej ke aane se sanganak programmers ko suvidha jarur mili, kintu iske liye programmer ko sanganak ke hardware, tathaa isaki kaary pranali kaa sampoorn gyaan hona aavashyak hota tha. atah ab or bhi saral bhashaayon kaa vikaas kiya gaya, jinhen ucch stareey bhashaa kaha gaya. iname se kuch pramukh aarambhik bhashaae cobol, basic, si thi.ucch stareey bhashaayon yaa hai level lengvejon ko mashin bhashaa main parivartit karne ke liye sankalak or vyaakhyaata kii jarurat padati he.sankalak yaa compiler ucch stareey bhashaa main likhe gaye program ko sthaayee rup se mashin bhashaa main parivartit karata he, jabaki vyaakhyaata yaa interprator ek ek pankti karke parivartit karata he.
