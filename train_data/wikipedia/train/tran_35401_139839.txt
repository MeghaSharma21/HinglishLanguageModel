 pakatika afghanistan kaa ek praant he jo us desh ke purv main sthit he. is praant kaa kshetrafal 19,482 varg kimi he or isaki aabaadi san 2009 main lagbhag 4 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. is praant kii rajdhani sharana shahar he. pakatika kii sarahadein pakistan se lagati hai. yahan ke lagbhag 96% log pashtun hai. yahan chand hazaar uzbek log bhi rahete hai jo aabaadi ke 2% se bhi kam hai.
 kisi jamaane main pakatiya, khost or pakatika praant ek hi soobe ke bhaag hua karte the or in teenon ko ab 'loya pakatiya' bulaayaa jaataa he. prasiddh unani itihaaskaar herodotus ne 5veen sadi isapurv main bhi is jagah ke nivaasiyon ko 'paktian' naam diya tha. pakatika praant kii rajdhani kabhi 'urgun' shahar hua karata tha, lekin yah kisi bhi mukhya sadak se dur he isliye 1970 ke dashak main rajdhani badalakar sharana shahar kar di gai.
