 vidhyarjan vishay gyaan kii kisi bhi vishesh shakha ko kehete hai. isamen vah sabhi nipunataayen, vyakti, pariyojnaayen, sansthaayen, sabhaayen or anusandhan kshetra shaamil hote hai jo us vishay ke saath ullekhneey rup se sambandhit hon. bhautikee, vaastu shaastra, itihaas, ityadi iske udhaaharan hai. kisi vidhyarjan vishay se sambandh rakhane vaale vyaktiyon ko aksar visheshgy kaha jaataa he.
