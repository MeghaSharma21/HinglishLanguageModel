 chaitanya mahaprabhu ke parvarti kaviyon main govinddas kaviraj sarvshereshth kavi hue hai. inhone keval "brajabuli" main par rachana kii he. samast pad radha-krishna-lila sambandhi he. in padon main samast kavyagun bahut adhik maatra main paae jate hai. chand main atyant sundar gati shabdon ke chayan dwaara prastut kii gai he. anupraason kii chata bhi anupam he. tatsam evam ardhatatsam shabdon ke prayog se kavy atyant sundar ho uthaa he. prakrutichitran, nakh-sikh-varnan atyant manomuguakari he. kaha jaataa he, kavi ne apane padon kaa sangrah gitamrita naam se swayan kiya tha.
 inkaa janm 1530 i. or mrityu 1613 i. lagbhag hui. govinddas kaa ullekh pramukh vaishnav jeevanigranth jaise bhaktamaal, bhakthiratnakar or premavilas main vistrut rup se he. in sabake anusaar govinddas kaa janm shrikhand main hua tha. inkaa gram "taeliabudhri" tha. inke pita kaa naam chiranjeev sen evam mata kaa naam sunanda tha. inke naanaa ne, jinka naam damodar sen tha anaath ho jane par inako or inke bhai ramchandra ko paala tha. govinddas pehle shakt the phir vaishnav ho gaye. shrinivas acharya inke guru the. inke praapt padon kii sankhya 450 se upar he.
