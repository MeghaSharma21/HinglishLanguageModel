 maajid majidi ek irani film nirdeshak, nirmaataa or patakatha lekhak hai. majidi ne film nirmaan main apana kariyar bataur abhinayta shuru kiya.
 majidi kaa janm tehran ke ek madhyamvargeey parivaar main hua the. unhone 14 saal kii umr main rangamanch par abhinayta ke rup main kaam karnaa shuru kar diya tha. baad main unhenne tehran ke rangamanch kala santhaan main adhyayan ke liye daakhila le liya. 1979 main iran main hui islamic kranti ke baad majidi ko irani film nirdeshak mohsin makhmalabaf kii film boykat main kaam karne kaa mauka mila.
 1998 main maajid majidi ne bataur nirdeshak children of heaven kaa nirdesan kiya jise sarvshereshth videshi bhashaa film kii shrenee main auskar sammaan ke liye namit kiya gaya. haalanki italvi nirdeshak roberto bejini kii film life ij beautiphul ke saamne is film ko safalataa nahin mili lekin is film ke kalatmak nirmaan ko chahunor saraahna mili. is maamle main majidi pehle irani filmkaar hai jinhen antarrashtriya str par is tarah kii pahchaan mili.
