 chittu pandeya ko pyaar se sher-e-baliya yani baliya kaa sher kehete hai. baliya ke rattuchak gaanv main 10 may 1865 ko janme chittu pandeya ne 1942 ke british virdohi andolan main sthaneey logon kii fouj bana kar angrejon ko khaded diya tha. 19 agast,1942 ko vahaan sthaneey sarkaar bani tab kuch dinon tak baliya main chittu pandeya kaa shashan bhi chalaa, lekin baad main angreji hukumat ne gadar ko dabaane ke kram main andolankariyon ko ukhaad phenkaa. chittu pandeya kii mrityu ko 1946 main hui thi.
