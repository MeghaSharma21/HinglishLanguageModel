 dhruviya upgrah prakshepan yaan yaa pi.es.el.ve bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan dwaara sanchalit ek upbhojit prakshepan pranali he. bharat ne ise apane sudur samvedi upgrah ko surya samakalik kaksha main prakshepit karne ke liye viksit kiya he. pieselvi ke vikaas se purv yah suvidha keval rus ke paas thi. pieselvi chote aakaar ke upgrahon ko bhu-sthir kaksha main bhi bhejne main sakasham he. ab tak pieselvi kii sahaayta se 70 antarikshayan vibhinn kakshaon main prakshepit kiye ja chuke hai. isse is kii vishwasaneeyata evam vividh kaary karne kii kshamta siddh ho chuki he.
 22 june, 2016 main is yaan ne apni kshamta kii charam seemaa ko chuaa jab pieselvi si-34 ke madhyam se record 20 upgrah ek saath chode gaye. isse pehle 28 april 2008 ko isro ne ek saath 10 upgrahon ko antariksh main bhejkar ek hi baar main sabase zyaada upgrah antariksh main bhejne kaa vishwarecord banaaya tha.
