 abdulla ebn alaajiz saood 2005 se 2015 tak saudi arab ke raja the. unhone apane sautele-pita raja fahd ke nidhan ke baad 1 agast 2005 ko rajgaddi praapt kii thi. fors ke varsh 2013 ke ank ke anusaar vishwa ke sabase taqatvar vyaktiyon main wo 8wein sthaan par the.
 24 janvari 2015 ko unka 90 varsh kii aayu main nidhan ho gaya. unke sautele bhai salmaan bin abdul azeez al soud unke uttaradhikari hai.
