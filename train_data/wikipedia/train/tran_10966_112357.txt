 itawa safari park ek prastaavit safari park he jo itawa, uttar pradesh main sthit he. yah 350 hectare main failaa hua he.
 is safari park kii khaas baat yah he ki isamen 5 tarah kii safari karavaayi jaaegi jisamen line safari, dear safari, bear safari or lepard safari shaamil hai. iske alaava yahaan ek 4di theatre bhi banaaya gaya he jisamen vanya jivan ko behad najadik se dekhne kaa mauka milegaa. yah asia kaa sabase bada vanya jivan safari park he.
