 laazarev sagar dakshindhruviya mahaasaagar kaa ek seemaant sagar he jis se purvi antarctic kii rani maud dharti tatavarti he. 2002 main yah is samudri kshetra ke liye prastaavit naam tha. raja hakon 7 sagar is se pashchim main or rizar-larsan sagar is se purv main sthit he. laazarev sagar naam par matbhed he - kuch strot iskaa prayog karte hai or kuch nahin.
