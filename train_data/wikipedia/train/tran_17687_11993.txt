  sardaar mohammad yaqub ek pakistani rajaneta or pakistan kii qaumi asembli ke purv sadsy the. ve 19/11/2002 se 19/03/2008 ke liye pakistan kii qaumi asembli ke upaadhyaksh niyukt kiye gaye the. upaadhyaksh hone ke naate, ve sabhadhyaksh, kii anupasthiti main, unke ke kaaryon kaa nirvaah karte the.
