 vyavastha chitra yaa 'schimatic chitra' aisa chitra he jo kisi tantra ko amoort graphic sanketon ke madhyam se chitrit karata he. yah us tantra kaa vaastavik chhabi nahin hota. udhaaharan ke liye vidyut paripathon ko pradarshit karne ke liye schimatic chitra hi prayukt hote hai. 
 vyavastha chitra tathaa anya prakaar ke chitron ke udhaaharan:
 yah parvahan maanchitr bhi ek vyavastha chitra hi he.
 ek drive-shrunkhlaa kaa vyavastha chitra
 rasinic ejiniyari kaa vyavastha chitra
 3D-ejiniyari drawing kaa vistaarit drushya
 jansankhya ghanatv kaa maanchitr
