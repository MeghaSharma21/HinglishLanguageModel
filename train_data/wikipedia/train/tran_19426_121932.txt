 harsonth  ek tahadaar khanij he jise 'sailenite' bhi kehete hai. rasinic sanrachana kii drushti se yah calcium kaa sulphate he, jisamen jal ke bhi do anu rahete hai. garam karne se jal ke anu nikal jate hai or yah ajal ho jaataa he. akruti main yah daanedaar sangmarmar sadrush hota he. aise harsonth ko selenite yaa selkhadi kehete hai. namak kii khaanon main namak ke saatha harsonth bhi mila rahata he. samudr ke pani main bhi harsonth rahata he. samudri pani ko sukhaane par jo lavan praapt hote hai unamem harsonth ke manibh paae jate hai.
 yah khanij visheshtah oosar bhoomi or shushk bhaagon main adhik paaya jaataa he. harsonth sansar ke anya deshon main prachurtaa se paaya jaataa he. bharat main rajasthan, gujarat, tamilnadu or bihar main iske nikshep mili hai.
 harsonth ke manibh prism ke aakaar ke yaa nalaakaar hote hai. anek sthalon main selenite ke sundar, sookshm manibh paae gaye hai.
 harsonth komal hota he. nakhon se isapar kharoch pad jaati he. isaki kathorta 1.5 se 2 tak hoti he tathaa vishisht gurutv 2.3 ke lagbhag. yah jal main alp viley hota he. harsonth se hokar behate hue pani main harsonth kaa kuch ansh ghulaa hua rahata he, jisase pani kathor ho jaataa he.
 shuddh harsonth safed yaa varnarahit hota he. par saadhaaran: apdravyon ke kaaran iskaa rang dhoosar, pilaa yaa gulabi dikhaai detaa he. yadi 75% jal nikaalkar harsonth ko pis daalaa jaae to utpaada plaster ov paris ke naam se vyapak rup se cement ke rup main prayukt hota he. harsonth ko plaster pathar yaa saanchaa pathar bhi kehete hai kyonki is plaster ov paris badi maatra main or saanche badi sankhya main banate hai.
