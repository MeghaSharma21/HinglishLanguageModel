 masjid-e-nabavi, jise aksar paigambar kii masjid kaha jaataa he, saudi arab ke shahar madina main sthit islam kaa doosraa pavitra sthaan he.
 makka main masjid-e-haram musalamaanon ke liye pavitra sthaan he. jabaki baitul muqaddas main masjid-e-aksa islam kaa teesraa pavitra sthaan he.
 masjid-e-nabavi kaa nirmaan pigambar mohammad ne san 622 athwa 623 main karavaaya tha. mul masjid aayat aakaar kaa tha. purv se pashchim isaki chaudaai 63 cubit thi or uttar se dakshin isaki lambaai 70 cubit thi. ye kachi eenton se banaaya gaya tha or isaki chat tad ke patton or mitti kii thi or isamen tad ke tanon se khambon banaae gaye the. chat 7 cubit unchi thi. baramda khulaa choda gaya tha. san 628 main kaibar kii jang main vijay ke baad isamen uttar, purv or pashchim kii ore vistaar kiya gaya or yah lagbhag 93 cubit kaa varg ban gaya.
 pratham caliph abu bakar ke samay main masjid main koi khaas badlaav nahin kiye gaye. san 639 main dusre caliph umr ebn al-khattab ne masjid main punah vistaar kiya or masjid purv se pashchim 113 cubit chaudee or uttar se dakshin 130 cubit lambi kar di gayi. chat ko 11 cubit unchaa kar diya gaya.
 tisare caliph usmaan bin affaan ne san 649 main puraani imaarat ko tudwa kar masjid kaa punarnirmaan karaayaa. punarnirmaan main das maheene lage. uttar se dakshin masjid 160 cubit thi or purv se pashchim 123 cubit . is nirmaan main diwaar pathar or chinai ke masaale kii banaai gayi thi or puraane khambon kii jagah lohe ke khambon kaa prayog kiya gaya tha. chat kaa nirmaan tik lakdi se kiya gaya.
