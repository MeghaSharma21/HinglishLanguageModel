 vidin temptesan ek dach simphonic metal band he jisaki sthapana 1996 main gayika sheron den edal or guitar vadak robert westerolt ne kii thi. inke sangeet ko simphonic metal kaha gaya he haalanki unki shuruuaati saamagree, jaise kii inter, gothic metal thi. ek saakshaatkaar main edal ne kaha tha kii ve simphonic rock prakaar main kai preranasroton ke karan utre.
 apane pehle album inter kii release ke pashchyaat band ek dach band ke rup main sthapit ho gaya. 2001 main aam janata ke samaksh ve apane album madar arth ke geet "aais queen" ke kaaran lokapriy ban gaye jo dach chart par duusare kramank par rahaa.
