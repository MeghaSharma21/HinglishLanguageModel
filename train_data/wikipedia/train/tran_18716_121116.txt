 melbern ko kala or samskruti kaa shahar kaha jaataa he jiskaa pramaan he neshnal gailaree of victoria. saint kilda road par sthit yah imaarat raat ko bahut kii khubasurat dikhaai detii he. ise banane kii yojana kii shuruyaat 1956 main hui thi. iske baad tees saalon se australia kaa sabase bada manch melbern concert haul, art museum or westpek gailaree astitv main aae.
