 gorakhpur haryana anu vidyut pariyojna ek prastaavit nabhikeey urja sanyantr he jo haryana ke fatehabad jile ke gorakhpur gaanv main main sthit hoga. 13 janvari 2014 ko is pariyojna ke pehle charan kaa shilanyas pradhanmantri shri manmohan singh dwaara kiya gaya.
 2800 megawatt kii pariyojna ke antargat 700 megawatt kshamta vaale chaar riektaron ko do charnon main sthapit kiya janaa prastaavit he. pratham charan main 700 megawatt ke 2 unit honge. yah kaary 2020-21 main puraa hone kii sambhaavna he.
 newclear power korporation of india dwaara banaai ja rahi is pariyojna ke pratham charan main unit 1 tathaa 2 ko puraa karne kii laagat 20,594 karod rupye he. 2013-14 main iske liye 80 karod rupye kii raashi abantit kii gai.
 yah sanyantr bhartiya vaigyanikon dwaara viksit swadeshi praudyogiki par aadhaarit he. or nirmaanadhin kakarapaar atomic power stesan kii unit 3 va 4 tathaa rajasthan atomic power stesan kii unit 7 va 8 ke samaan hai. aakaar or dizin visheshtaayen tarapur atomic power stesan kii 540 MWe kshamta vaali unit 3 va 4 ke lagbhag samaan hai.
