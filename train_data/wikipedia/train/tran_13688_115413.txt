 28 farvari 1951 paris sandhi ne europea koyala or steel sanghathan kaa gathan hua. is par chah deshon-france, germany, belgium, netherland, lakjamberg or italy ne hastakshar kiye. koyala or steel udyogon kii dekhbhaal ke liye ek ucch darje kaa praadhikaran banaaya gaya. is praadhikaran ke adhikaron ko santulit karne ke liye germany ke samarthan se netherland ne ek mantri parishad ke gathan par bhi jor diya jisamen sadsy deshon se mantri shaamil kiye jaaen.
