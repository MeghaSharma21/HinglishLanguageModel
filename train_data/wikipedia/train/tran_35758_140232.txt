 gintaara jise ganak sancha bhi kah sakate hai, ek ganan upkaran hota he, jiskaa prayog asia ke bhaagon main ankaganitiya prakaayon ke liye kiya jaataa tha. aaj, gintaara apane vartmaan rup main, taron par bandhe motiyon vaale ek bans frem ke rup main dikhaai padataa he, lekin ve mul rup se ye fali ke bijon yaa pattharon yaa lakdi, pathar yaa dhaatu kii goliyon ko ret main khaanchon/kyaariyon main chalaa kar prayog kiye jate the. likhit pranali ke aarambh se shatabdiyon purv inkaa prayog kiya jaataa tha. aaj bhi asia, africa aadi kai sthanon par vyapaariyon dwaara iskaa prayog kiya jaataa he. iske prayokta ko gintaraare kehete hai.
