 america kii pracheen maaya sabhyataa guatemala, mexico, honduras tathaa eukatan prayadweep main sthapit thi.maaya sabhyataa mexico kii ek mahatvapurn sabhyataa thi. is sabhyataa kaa armbh 1500 i0 pus0 main hua. yah sabhyataa 300 i0 se 900 i0 ke dauraan apni unnati ke shikhar par pahunchi is sabhyataa ke mahatvapurn kendra mexico, guatemala, honduras evam al - sailvador main the. yadhyapi maaya sabhyataa kaa ant 16 ve shatabdi main hua kintu iskaa patan 11 ve shatabdi se armbh ho gaya tha.
 maaya sabhyataa kii shashan vyavastha ke sambandh main hamen koi vishesh jaankaree praapt nahin he. maaya shaasha saamanytah purush hua karte the. kabhi - kabhi raniyaan bhi shashan karti thi. raja kaa pad paitruk tha. uski mrityu ke pashchaat uskaa bada betaa singhaasana par baithta tha. raja kaa bahadur hona anivaary tha. ek bahadur raja hi samrajya kaa vistaar evam unki suraksha kar saktaa tha. raja ke singhaasana par baithte samay devataaon ko prasann karne ke uddeshy se maanav bali di jaati thi. raja abhijaat varg evam purohiton kii sahaayta se shashan chalaata tha. raja bhavy mehalon main rahata tha. uski seva main badi sankhya main log das evam dasiyaan hote the.
