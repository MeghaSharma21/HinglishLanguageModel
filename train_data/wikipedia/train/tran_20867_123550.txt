 shaashvat gati kaa shabdic arth he - vah gati jo sadaa chalati rahati he. kintu praaia: iskaa arth aisi kisi yukti yaa pranali se he jo kam urja lekar adhik urja utpann karti he - jiskaa arth he ki yah anant kaal tak kuch na kuch 'shuddh urja' utpann karti rahati he. aisi mashin kii kalpana or nirmaan kaa praytn bahut dinon tak chalaa kintu ab yah maan liya gaya he ki aisi mashin sambhav nahin he kyonki yadi aisa hua to vah urja sangrakshan kaa niyam kaa ulanghan hoga.
