 bahooraani 1963 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. yah ek aise ladke ke bare main he jiske maan baap uskaa vivaah ek gaav kii ladki se karvaa dete he. kyonki duulhaa is baat se khush nahin, is kaaran wo dahez main aaye sone ko nakli bataakar talaak lene kii kashish karata he. phir dulhan va uskaa bhai use samajhaate hai ki wo bhi shahar kii ladki ban sakati he.
