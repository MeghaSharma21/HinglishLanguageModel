 pashchimi bahan dweep bharat ke andaman va nicobar dweepasmooh ke andaman dweepasmooh bhaag main ratland dweep or chote andaman ke bich dancan jalsandhi main sthit do bahan dwipon main se ek he. doosraa tapu purvi bahan dweep hai or vah is dweep se thodaa bada he. yah donon 250 meter dur hai lekin ek coral reef dwaara jude hue hai. yah paisej dweep se 6 kimi dakshinpurv or uttari bhai dweep se 18 kimi uttar main hai.
 dweep kaa aakaar nashpaati he. purvottar-dakshinpaschim disha main lambaai 380 meter or chaudaai 340 meter he. iskaa adhikansh hissaa van se dhakaa he. tat har ore patharilaa he lekin purvottar main ek nanha-sa retila bhaag he. iskaa sarvoch bindu samudratal se 71 meter unchaa he.
