 tabasheer yaa banslochan bans kii kuch naslon ke jodon se milane vaala ek parbhasi safed padaarth hota he. yah mukhya rup se silica or pani or kam maatra main khaar or chooney kaa bana hota he. bhartiya upmahadweep kii ayurveda or unani chikitsa pranaaliyon kii dawa-soochiyon main iskaa ahm sthaan he. parmparik chini chikitsa ke kai nuskhon main bhi iskaa prayog hota he.
 parmparik chikitsa vidhiyon main tabasheer ke kai faayde bataae jate hai, jaise ki bukaar utaarna, spazm se raahat dilaanaa, lakve main sahaayta karnaa or kamottejak bananaa . banslochan kaa upyog bharat main garbhvatee mahilaon ko ji matlane kii avasthaa main ek tukdaa muh main rakhkar chusane se laabh miltaa he.
 halaki nilimaa vaale tabasheer saadhaaran pile yaa safed tabasheer se uttam mana jaataa he.
 har bans kii tandi main tabasheer nahin hota. tabasheer dhundne ke liye dandiyon ko hilaayaa jaataa he. agar andar tabasheer bana hua ho to aksar uske dale khadkane kii awaz paidaa karte hai. in baanson ko chirakar tabasheer niklaa jaataa he.
 haalanki tabasheer pracheen ayurveda chikitsa pranali kaa hissaa he, kuch itihaaskaaron kaa maananaa he ki iskaa pehla prayog bharat kii adivasee samudaayon main shuru hua. hazaron saal tak bharat se tabasheer niryaat hota tha or madhya kaal main yah aksar arab saudagaron ke dwaara kiya jaataa tha. barahavi shatabdi isvi main bharat ke pashchimi tat ke qareeb sthit thane shahar main niryaat hone vaale tabasheer kii mandi laga karti thi. rome main nero ke jamaane main rahane vaale pedanios dioscoridis naamak unani chikitsak ne apni likhaaiyon main tabasheer ko saakhaaron kaa naam diya.
 "tabasheer" shabd sanskrit ke "tvakshir" shabd se aayaa he, jiskaa arth "twachaa kaa ksheer" yani " chaal kaa doodh" he. is ke liye kuch anya sanskrit naam bhi prayog hote hai, jaise ki "vans sharker" or vans karpur . mandarin chini bhashaa main ise "tian jhu huang" kaha jaataa he, jiskaa matlab "divya bans pilaa" he.
