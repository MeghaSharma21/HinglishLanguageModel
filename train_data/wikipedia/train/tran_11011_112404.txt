 datson go bharat main japani car nirmaataa company nissaan motor company dwaara datson brand ke naam se punerjeevit kii jane vaali nayi choti car kaa naam he. beesvin sadi ke prarambh main japan main bani is car kaa utpaadan 27 varsh purv san 1986 main band ho gaya tha. datson kii go model kii yah car bharat ke alaava dakshin africa, indonesia or rus main bhi launch kii jaayegi. bharat main nissaan kaaron kii lokapriyata ko dekhte hue sabhi suvidhaon se yukt chote aakaar kii is car ko bhi yahan ke bazaar main utaarne kaa nirnay liya gaya he.
 bharat main yah car agle saal 2014 kii shuruyaat main graahakon ko uplabdh hogi. choti car ke vartmaan sabhi modelon ke mukaable isaki keemat bharat main 4 lakh rupee ke aasapaas hogi jabaki videshon main 10,055 yues dollar tathaa 10,970 austrailiayan dollar rahane kii sambhaavna he. 
 indonesia main iskaa model datson go plus ke naam se utaara jaayegaa. vahaan par 7 siton vaali yah multi parapaj vehikil 100 million indonesin mudra ke andar uplabdh hogi. nisaan motors dwaara jari ek vijyapti ke anusaar nai dilli, mumbai va chennai sahit sampoorn bharat ke 90 shaharon main is car ko pradarshit kiya jaayegaa.
