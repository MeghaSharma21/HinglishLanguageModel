 
 kushvaha bhartiya samaaj kii ek vansh/jati he. paramparaagat rup se kisaan the parantu 20veen shatabdi main unhone swayan ko rajput vansh kaa bataaya. is jati kaa kai swatantra rajyon va riyasaton par shashan rahaa hai jiname se alvar, amer va maihar pramukh hai. kushvaha yaa kushwaha, swayan ko ayodhya ke suryavanshi raja ram ke putr kush kaa vanshaj maanate hai.
 kushwaha parivaar ne amer par shashan kiya, jise baad main jaipur rajya ke naam se janaa gaya, kushavaahon kii is shakha ko rajput mana gaya. mogul shaasha akbar ke sahyog va samarthan se kushavaah sammanit raajaaon ke rup main sthapit huye.
