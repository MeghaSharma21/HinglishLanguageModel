 olympic park ek khel parisar he jo landan, united kingdom main sthit he. iskaa nirmaan 2012 greeshmakaleen olympic or 2012 greeshmakaleen paralampic khelon ke liye kiya gaya tha. yah shahar ke purv main upnagar stretford ke sameep he. yahan khilaadeeyon ke liye khel gaanv hone ke saath-saath vibhinnakhelon ke liye aayojan sthal bhi hai, jaise olympic stadium va landan equatics centre. khelon ke pashchaat yah elizabeth dwitiya kii hirak jayanti ke punyasmaran main queen elizabeth olympic park ke naam se janaa jayega.
