 barli gramin mahila vikaas santhaan indaur sthit ek santhaan he jo gramin tathaa janajaatiy mahilaon ke liye awasaiya prashikshan kaarykram chalaati he jinako vidhyalay jane kaa avasar nahin mila yaa jinhone bich main hi padhai chhod di. gyatavya he ki bhilala janajati main 'barli' ladakiyon kaa ek bahut hi saamaanya naam he. is sanstha kaa maananaa he ki mahilaayein samaaj kii kendriya stambh hai. yah samsthaan indaur ke uttari bhaag main sthit he. 
 is santhaan main prashikshan paane vaali adhikansh mahilaayein pashchimi madhya pradesh se aati hai. yahan saamaajik or aarthik rup se pichde (jaise anusuchit jati, janajati, pichde varg, shaareerik rup se viklang, anaath, vidhwaa, parityakta aadi ko praathamikta di jaati he. chhattisgarh kshetra main kanker, imlipada main isaki teen shakhaayen hai jahaan kii vyavastha barli se prashikshan-praapt ladkiyaan chalaatin hai.
