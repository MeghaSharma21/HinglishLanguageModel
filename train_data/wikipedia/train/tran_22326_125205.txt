 zaahida hina  ek urdu kahanikar, sampadak, lekhak v naatakkaar he!
 zaahida hina kaa janm 5 oktober 1946 ko sasaram namak gaanv main bihar, bharat main hua ! vibhaajan ke baad inke pita mohammad abul khayr karachi, pakistan aakar based ! inhone apni pehali kahani kii rachana nau saal kii umr main kii ! inhone karachi vishvavidhyalay se snaatak kii oopaadhi lee tathaa unka pehla niabandh 1962 main prakashit hua ! unhone patrakarita ko apana kaaryakshetra chunaa or 1960 ke madhya main unhone patrakarita aarambh kii ! 1970 main unhone ne vikhyath kavi jon eila se vivaah kiya ! ve 1988 se 2005 tak dainik jang naamak akhabar se judy rahi or 2005 main unhone dainik express naamak akhbaar kaa rukh kiya ! 2006 se ve ek bhrity akhbaar dainik bhaskar main pakistani diary naamak lekh likh rahi he ! varttman main ve radio pakistan, bi bi si urdu, tathaa vois of america par karyrat he ! ve karachi pakistan main rahati he !
 jaahida hina ne lagbhag do hazaar se bhi zyada patrakaari lekh likhe he ! unke kai kaahaania hindi marathi, bengali tathaa angreji main anuvaadit huyi he ! unke kuch pramukh lekh is prakaar he :-
 ve parmaanu hathiyaaron kii virdohi rahi he !
 unhen kai sammaanon se nawaaza gaya he uname se kuch is prakaar he !
 2006 main unhen pakistani kii sainik taanashaah puruskaar kaa virodh main jan aandolan ko majboot karne ke liye pakistan kaa sarvocch sahitya sammaan diya gaya !
