 shamaa jain
 
 shamaa jain ek varisht bhartiya rajanyik he, jinhen june 2017 main greece main bhartiya rajdoot ke rup main niyukt kiya gaya he. ve varsh 1983 batch kii iesces adhikari hai. iske purv ve kot da'evoar ke bhartiya rajdoot rah chuke hai. saath hi inhen sierra leone, liberia or gini hetu bhi samavarti manyata di gayi thi.
 shamaa jain jammu evam kashmir rajya ke antargatjammu main paidaa huyi. unhone jammu vishvavidhyalay se raajnneeti vigyaan main snaatak kii pariksha pratham shrenee main praapt kii or swarna padak se sammanit huyi. tatpashchaat unhone jawaharlal nehru vishvavidhyalay, nai dilli se parasnatak or em. phil kii shiksha praapt kii. ve em. phil ke liye ujisi faillowship ke praptakarta hai.
