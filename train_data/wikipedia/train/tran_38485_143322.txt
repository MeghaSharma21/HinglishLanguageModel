 rajendra narayan singh dev ke raajnneetijna the. wo 1950 se 1962 tak ganatantr parishad rajnatik dal ke adhyaksh the. 1962 main ganatantr parishad kaa vilay samaajawaadee party main ho gaya or wo samaajawaadee party kii odisha shakha ke adhyaksh bane. 1967 se 1971 tak wo odisha ke mukhyamantri rahe.
