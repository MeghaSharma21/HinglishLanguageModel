 param si-dack dwaara viksit bharat ke swadeshi superkamputers kii ek shrinkhalaa he. shrinkhalaa main naveenatam superkamputer param ishan hai.
 sanskrit main param kaa arth hai "sarvocch". 
 praudyogiki pratibandh ke parinamswaroop cre super computer se vanchit kiye jane ke baad, bharat ne swadeshi super computer or superkamputing praudyogikiyon ke vikaas ke liye ek kaarykram shuru kiya. super computer parmaanu hathiyaaron ke vikaas ke liye sahaayta karne main sakasham mana jaataa tha. is kshetra main aatmanirbharta praapt karne ke uddeshy se 1988 main centre for deveopment of advanced computing kii sthapana kii gai. phir iske nideshak ke rup main electronics vibhaag ke dow vijay bhatkar ko niyukt kiya gaya. pariyojna 3 saal ke praarambhik ran or ₹30 karod praarambhik dhan ke saath shuru hui. kuch dhan america se super computer kii kharid ke liye kharch kiya gaya. 1990 main, ek prototip kaa utpaadan kiya gaya or iskaa 1990 ke jurikh superkamputing sho main benchmark kiya gaya tha. isane zyaadaatar anya pranaaliyon ko peeche chhod diya. jisase america ke baad bharat ko duusare number par rakhaa gaya.
 in prayaas ke parinamswaroop param 8000 astiv main aayaa. jo 1991 main sthapit kiya gaya tha.
 1991 main jari param 8000 main inmos tii800 transputer kaa istemaal kiya gaya tha. transputer main samanaantar prasanskaran ke liye ek nae or abhinav microprocesar ko dizine kiya gaya tha. yah ek parivarti vinyas, interconection network ke saath ek vitarit smriti emmdi vaala architekchar tha. isame germany, britain or rus ko niryaat 64 sipiu the.
 param 8600 ,param 8000 par ek sudhaar tha. yah ek 256 sipiu kaa computer tha. har chaar Inmos T800 ke liye, isamen ek intel i860 ko-procesar laga tha. iskaa parinaam vector processing main peak 5 GFLOPS niklaa. in modelon main kai ko niryaat kiya gaya.
 param 9900 / es es ko ek empipi pranali hone ke liye dizin kiya gaya tha. isamen SuperSPARC 2 procesar kaa istemaal kiya gaya. dizin ko modular karne ke liye badla gaya taaki nae procesar aasaani se shaamil kiye ja saken. aamtaur par, isamen 32-40 procesar kaa istemaal kiya gaya lekin, Clos network topology kaa upyog kar ise 200 sipiu badhaayaa ja saktaa tha. param 9900 / yu es, UltraSPARC sanskaran tha or param 9900 / ee ek diisi alpha sanskaran tha.
 1998 main, param 10000 kaa anaavaran kiya gaya tha. param 10000 kai swatantra nodes kaa prayog karata tha, prayek san enterprize 250 sarwar par aadhaarit the or prayek sarwar main do 400 MHz UltraSPARC 2 procesar lage the. base concigareshan main teen compute nodes or ek sarwar node tha. is aadhaar pranali kii adhiktam gati 6.4 GFLOPS thi. ek tipical pranali main 160 sipiu shaamil hote or 100 GFLOPS tak sakasham ho sakati thi lekin, yah aasaani se TFLOP shrinkhalaa ke liye scalabel tha. rus or singapore ko iskaa niryaat kiya gaya.
 param padam april 2003 main pesh kiya gaya tha. yah 1024 GFLOPS kii peak speed or 1 tibi kii peak storage se yukt tha. isamen 1 gigahertz ke 248 ibiem Power4 sipiu kaa istemaal kiya gaya. oparating system ibiem AIX 5.1L tha. isane apane praathmik interconect ke rup main PARAMnet 2 kaa istemaal kiya. yah pehla bhartiya super computer tha jisane 1 TFLOP kii seemaa ko paar kiya tha.
 param yuva, november 2008 main pesh kiya gaya tha. isaki adhiktam sasteneble speed 38.1 TFLOPS kii he or peak speed 54 TFLOPS kii he. isamen 4608 kor hai jo 2.9 GHz ke intel 73XX par aadhaarit hai. isaki storage kshamta 25 tibi se 200 tibi tak he. yah primary interconect ke rup main PARAMnet 3 kaa upyog karata he.
 july 2009 main yah ghoshnaa kii gai he ki si-dack ek nae ucch gati param ko viksit kar rahaa tha. 2012 tak iske anaavaran kii apeksha thi or 1 PetaFLOPS kii seemaa ko todane kii ummid thi. november 2014 main yah bataaya gaya he ki bharat sabase tej super computer jisaki speed 132 ExaFLOPS hogi par kaam kar rahaa he.
 param yuva dwitiya ₹ 16 karod kii laagat se teen maheene kii avadhi main si-dack dwaara viksit kiya gaya tha, or iskaa anaavaran 8 farvari 2013 ko kiya gaya. yah 524 TeraFLOPS par kaary karata he par khapat main param yuva kii tulana main 35% kam urja kharch karata he. isane samudaay maanak linpac benchmark par 360.8 TeraFLOPS kaa nirantar pradarshan kiya or ise top500, november 2012 kii ranking suchi main 62 waan sthaan diya gaya he. bijli kshamta ke sandarbh main, yah november 2012 tak vishwa kii sheersh green500 super computer kii suchi main 33 wein sthaan par tha. yah pehla bhartiya super computer he jisane 500 se adhik TeraFLOPS kii gati praapt kii he.
 param yuva 2 ko jaev soochna vigyaan, mausam purvanumaan, bukamp data vishleshan, aronotical ejineering, vaigyanik data processing or dawa ke vikaas ke kshetra main anusandhan ke liye istemaal kiya jayega. indian institute of technology or neshnal institute of technology jaise shaikshik sansthaanon ko rashtriya gyaan network ke madhyam se computer se joda ja saktaa he. yah computer bharat main bhavishya ke PetaFLOP-range ke super computer ke nirmaan kii disha main ek kadam he.
 PARAMNet ek ucch gati ucch bandwidth or kam vilambtaa vaala ek network he jiskaa vikaas param shrinkhalaa ke liye kiya gaya he. mul PARAMNet 8 port cascadable non-blocking switch kaa prayog karata tha jise si-dack dwaara viksit kiya gaya. prayek bandargah donon dishaao main 400 Mb/s ke rup main yah ek purna-duplex network tha. yah pehali baar param 10000 main istemaal kiya gaya tha.
 PARAMNet 2 ko param padam ke saath pesh kiya kiya gaya tha. ye purna-duplex main kaam karte hue 2.5 GB / s kii speed pradaan karata he. yah virtual interfes architekchar or active messej jaise anek tarah ke interphes saport karata he. yah 8 yaa 16 port san switch prayog karata he. grid computing network GARUDA bhi is par aadhaarit he.
 param super computer vibhinn prayojanon ke liye saarvajanik or nijee donon operataron dwaara prayog kiye jate hai. 2008 tak, 52 param ko tainaat kiya gaya he. inamen se 8 canada, rus, singapore or germany main sthit hai. kuch tanzania, arminia, saudi arab, singapore, ghana, myanmar, nepal, kazakhstan, uzbekistan, or vietnam ko bhi beche gaye hai.
