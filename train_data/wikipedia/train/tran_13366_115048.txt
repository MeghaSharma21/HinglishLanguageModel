 
 tamba ek bhautik tatva he. iskaa sanket Cu he. isaki parmaanu sankhya 29 or parmaanu bhaar 63.5 he. yah ek tanya dhaatu he jiskaa prayog vidyut ke chaalak ke rup main pradhanta se kiya jaataa he.
 maanav sabhyataa ke itihaas main tambe kaa ek pramukh sthaan he kyonki pracheen kaal main maanav dwaara sabase pehle prayukt dhaatuon or mishradhaatuon main tamba or kaanse kaa naam aata he.
 kam kshayshil samasthanik 63 tathaa 65 ke alaava tambe ke koi 2 darjan samasthanik hai jo kshayshil hai.
 tamba dhaatu vidyut tathaa ushma kaa suchalak he. is suchaalakata kii shrenee main yah chaandi ke baad duusare kram par aata he. isamen ek laalimaa jaisi chamak hoti he. yah saamanytah jal se abhikriya nahin karata he par vivy jarak se dhire dhire sanyog kar oxaid banaata he. lekin lohe main jang lagane se bilkul alag iskaa oxaid dhaatu ke upar ek parat banaata he jo iske or oxikaran ko roktaa he. yah parat stachu of liberty par bhi dekhi ja sakati he.
 tambe ke pramukh ayskon main tamba glans, tamra piritis, kuprite tathaa makelit 2.CuCO3) ke naam aate hai. pehle phen plavan vidhi se ayask kaa sandran kar liya jaataa he. sandrit ayask ko maheen churna karke use ek parvartanee bhatthi main vaayu pravaah kii upasthiti main bharjit karte hai. is prakriya dwaara kuprus sulphid or pheras sulphid kaa mishran praapt hota he. ashuddhiyaan oxykrut hokar nil jaati hai.
 iske baad ise coke ke saath vatya bhatthi main garm karte hai jisase pheras sulphid pheras oxaid banaata he tathaa silica ke saath abhikriya karke dhaatumal banaata hua nikal jaataa he. iske baad is ko ek bhatthi main garm karte hai to tambe ke sulphid pehle oxide main badal jate hai tathaa phir bache hue sulphide se abhikriya karke sulfur daai oxaid tathaa tamba dete hai.
 is kriya se lagbhag 99.99% tanba praapt hota he|
