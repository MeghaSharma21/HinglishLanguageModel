 hazbek motargadi kisi bhi car ke bahari dhaanche ke nirmaan kii ek lokapriy style he. is style kii gadiyon main saamanytah paanch daravaaje hote he. do daravaaje chaalak kii taraf evam do daravaaje yatriyon kii bagal main hote hai. paanchavaan darwaja gaadi ke prishth bhaag main hota he. sedan style kii gadiyon ke vipareet is prakaar kii gadiyon main saamaan rakhane ke liye alag se sthaan nahin hota. hazbek gadiyon main baithne ke liye do kataron main jagah hoti he. pichhali qatari ko mode kar samaan rakhane ke liye upyog main liya ja saktaa he. 
 bharat main adhiktam lokapriy motargadiyaan hazbek style kii hai jaise maaruti 800, olto, wagon-aar, swift, hundai centro, tata indica. is prakaar kii kaaren chote pariwaron ke liye upyukt maani jaati hai.
