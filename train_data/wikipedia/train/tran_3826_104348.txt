 kendriya vyapaar kshetra kisi nagar kaa pramukh vyavsaayik kshetra hota he or bahut baar yah bhugolic rup se bhi nagar ke madhya bhaag main hota he. is paribhaashi-shabd kaa upyog mukhyatah angrezi bhashi deshon main kiya jaataa he, jahaan yah is baat ko batalaane ke liye prayukt hota he ki kisi nagar kaa sabase pramukh vyavsaayik kshetra kahaan par he yaa kaunsa he. angrezi main ise cantral bijnis district kehete he. hindi main aamtaur par nagar-kendra yaa vyavsaayik kendra kah diya jaataa he. 
 sibidi kisi nagar kaa madhya kshetra hota he jahaan vyapaarik or vyavsaayik pratishthanon kii saghanta hoti he. nagar kendra pad kaa upyog bhi sibidi ke samaan hi hota he or donon yah ingit karte hai ki nagar ke kis bhaag main vyavsaayik pratishthanon kii saghanta anya bhaagon se adhik he. sibidi yaa nagar-kendra main unchi imaaraton kii saghanta bhi praayah anya kshetron se adhik hoti he.
