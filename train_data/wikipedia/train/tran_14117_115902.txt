 kshtaharni bhavani mandir uttar pradesh ke gajipur jile ke karimuddinpur gram main sthit he. is dham ke bare main manyata he ki yah padmapurana or valmiki ramayana main varnit devi kaa wo mandir he jahaan bhagwaan ram ne guru vishwamitra or anuj lakshmana ke saath ayodhya se baxur jate samay puja archana kii thi.
 puranic kathaon ke anusaar yah sthaan pehle darukavan ke naam se prasiddh tha. to vahiin janashrutiyon ke anusaar vanprastha ke samay maharaj yudhisthir apane bhaaiyon, patni draupadi va kul purohit dhaumy rishi ke saath is sthaan par kuch dinon tak vishram karte hue devi kii puja-archana kii thi. treta yug main ram-lakshmana ne apane guru vishwamitra ke saath yodhya se baxur jate samay devi ke pujan-archan ke pashchaat vishram kar baxur jaakar tadka kaa badh kiya. aghoreshwar baba kinaram ko to maa kshtaharni ne swayan apana prasad pradaan kar pehali siddhi se paripurna kiya tha.
