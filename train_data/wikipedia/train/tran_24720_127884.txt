 radox abhikriyaayein ke antargat ve sab rasinic abhikriyaayein sammilit hai jinamein parmaanuon ke oxikaran avasthaaen badal jaatin hai. saamanytah radox abhikriyaaon ke abhikaarakon ke parmaanuon ke bich electranon kaa aadan-pradaan hota he.
 kabhi bhi oxikaran yaa apchayan abhikriya akele nahin hoti. dono saath-saath hotin hai. ek hi abhikriya main yadi kisi cheez kaa oxikaran hota he to kisi duusari kaa apchayan hota he. isiliye inkaa alag-alag adhyayan na karke ekasaath adhyayan karne hai or donon ko milakar 'radox' kehete hai.
