 firophakoh yaa firuzakuh aadhunik afghanistan ke gor praant main sthit ek shahar tha jo gori rajvansh kii pratham rajdhani thi. kaha jaataa he ki apane samay main yah vishwa ke mahaan nagaron main se ek tha lekin san 1220 ke dashak main changez khan ke putr ogtai khan ke netritv main mongol faujon ne isapar dhaava bolaa or ise tahas-nahas kar daalaa. uske baad yah shahar itihaas ko khoyaa gaya or iskaa thik sthaan kisi ko gyaat nahin he. kuch itihaaskaaron kaa manana he ki gor praant ke sheharak zile main khadi jam kii minar is shahar kaa antim bacha hua nishaan he.
