 atal nagar chhattisgarh rajya kii prashasnic rajdhani he. yah vartmaan raipur se 24 kimi dur basaaya gaya ek nayaa shahar he, jo rashtriya rajmarg 53 tathaa 30 ke bich main sthit he.
 21 agast 2018 ko chhattisgarh ke tatkaaleen mukhyamantri, raman singh ne ghoshnaa kii ki nayaa raipur kaa naam badalakar bharat ke purv pradhanmantri, atal bihari vaajapeyi ke naam par atal nagar rakh diya jaayegaa.
 chhattisgarh kii rajdhani ke taur par nayaa raipur prastaavit kiyya gaya he. yah ek vishwastareey pariyojna he jisame kii vartmaan raipur se 24 kimi dur ek nae shahar kaa srijan kiya ja rahaa he. is ke liye ek bade chhetra kii bhoomi kaa anugrahan kiya ja rahaa he, prastaavit he kii kul 80000 hectare bhoomi anugrahit kii jaayegi. naye raipur main 225 kimi pakki sadak bananaa prastaavit hai. varsh 2014 main mantralay nayaa raipur sthantarit ho chuki he.
