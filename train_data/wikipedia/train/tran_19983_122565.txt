 nat mandir, urisa ke konark ke surya mandir ke praangan main bana ek mandir he. 
 yah vah sthaan he, jahaan mandir kii nartakiyaan, suryadev ko arpan karne ke liye nrity karatin thim. puure mandir main jahaan tahaan phool-bel or jyamitiya namoonon kii khudaai kii gai he. inke saath hi maanav, dev, gandharva, kinnar aadi kii akrutiyaan bhi andrik mudraon main darshit hai. inaki mudraaen kaamuk hai or kamasutra se leen gayi hai. mandir ab anshik rup se khandhar main parivartit ho chukaa he. yahaan kii shilp kalakritiyon kaa ek sangrah, bhartiya puraatatv surveykshan vibhaag ke surya mandir sangrahalay main surakshit he.
