 shri anadi panchmukhi mahadev mandir yah sthaan ayodhya kii shastreey seemaa ke antargat guptar ghat par avasthit he, jo ki vyaavhaarik rup se vartmaan main faijabad sainya kshetra he. ayodhya ke pratishthit shivalay nageshwaranath va kshireshwarnath kii bhanti is mandir kii sthapana bhi ati pracheen he. panchasya upasna ke anusaar yahan virajman shivlinga paanch mukhon se yukt he. ling kaa saamaanya arth chihn hota he. shiv brahmand ke pratik purush hai. inkaa panchmukhi swaroop panchatattvon kaa pratik he. jinka varnan karte huye kaha gaya he-"sadyojatan vamdeomaghoranch mahabhuj. tathaa tatpurushan gyeyamishanan panchman mukhan.." sadyojaat, vamdev, aghor, tatpurush ar ishan ye paanch mukh hai. jo kramash: prithvi, jal, tej, vaayu ar akash kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai. acharypeeth shri lakshmana kila kii dekh-rekh main yah shivalay ayodhya-faijabad samet anyatra ke bhakton ke liye bhi astha kaa bada kendra he.
