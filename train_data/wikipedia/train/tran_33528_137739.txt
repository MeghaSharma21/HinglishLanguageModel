 tulanaatmak sulaabh kii sankalpana ke anusaar, kuch vyakti, form yaa raashtra anya vyaktiyon, fermon yaa rashtron kii apeksha kam mulya main utpaadit kar sakate hai. tulanaatmak sulaabh kaa siddhanth antararashtriya vyapaar kii moolbhoot sankalpana he jo kahati he ki raashtra un vastuon ke nirmaan or niryaat main vishistataa praapt karne kii ore agrasar hote hai jinako ve anya kii apeksha kam mulya par utpann karne kii kshamta rakhte hai, tathaa un vastuon kaa aayaat karte hai jinka utpaadan ve dusron kii apeksha adhik mulya par hi kar sakate hai
 tulanaatmak sulaabh kaa siddhanth, angrej arthshaastri david ricardo ne 1817 main apni pustak ' Principles of Political Economy and Taxation' main pratipaadit kiya tha. yah siddhanth aadam smith ke nirpeksh mulya sulaabh siddhanth kaa vistaar yaa sanshodhan kaha ja saktaa he.
