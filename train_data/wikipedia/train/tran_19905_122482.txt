 miraben kaa mul naam 'madelin slade' tha. ve ek british sainya adhikari kii beti thim. gandhiji ke vyaktitv ke jaadu main bandhi saat samandar paar kaale logon ke desh hindustan chali ai or phir yaheen kii hokar rah gayi. gandhi ne unhen naam diya tha - 'mira ben'. mira ben saadi dhoti pahenti, soot katati, gaanv-gaanv gumti. vah gori nasl kii angrej thim, lekin hindustan kii aazaadee ke paksh main thi. unhone jarur bharat kii dharti par janm nahin liya tha, lekin vah sahi myanon main hindustani thim. gandhi kaa apni is videshi putree par vishesh anuraag tha.
 madelin slade admiral sar admand slade kii putree thim. jab unke pita mumbai main 'east india squadran' ke commander-in-cheef ke pad par karyrat the us samay unhone kuch varsh bharat main bitaaye. vah prakruti se prem karatin thi tathaa apane bachapan se hi sada jivan se unhen pyaar tha. sangeet main unki gahari ruchi thi tathaa bithoven kaa sangeet unhen bahut bhaataa tha.
