 
 samarkand uzbekistan kaa doosraa sabase bada nagar he. madhya asia main sthit yah nagar aitihaasik or bhugolic drushti se ek mahatvapurn shahar rahaa he. is nagar kaa mahatv resham maarg par pashchim or china ke madhya sthit hone ke kaaran bahut adhik he. bharat ke itihaas main bhi is nagar kaa mahatv he kyonki babar isi sthaan ke shaasha banane kii cheshtaa karata rahaa tha. baad main jab vah vifal ho gaya to bhagkar kabul aayaa tha jiske baad wo dilli par kabza karne main kaamyaab ho gaya tha. 'bibi khaanim kii masjid' is shahar kii sabase prasiddh imaarat he. 2001 main unesco ne is 2750 saal puraan shahar ko vishwa dharohar sthalon kii suchi main shaamil kiya. iskaa us suchi main naam he: 'samarkand - samskruti kaa chauraahaa'.
 yah turki-mongol badshaah taimur dwaara sthapit temuri samrajya kii rajdhani rahaa. samarkand 719 meter kii oonchaai par zarafashaan nadi kii upjau ghati main sthit he. yahan ke nivaasiyon ke mukhya vyavsaay baagbani, dhaatu evam mitti ke baratanon kaa nirmaan, kapade bananaa, resham, gehun va chaaval kii krishi or ghoda va khachchar kaa paalan he. shahar ke bich rigistan naamak ek chauraahaa he, jahaan par vibhinn rangon ke pattharon se nirmit kalatmak imaaraten vidyaman hai. shahar kii charadivari ke baahar taimur ke pracheen mahal hai. isapurv 329 main sikandar mahaan ne is nagar kaa vinaash kiya tha. 1221 i. main is nagar kii raksha ke liye 1,10,000 aadamiyon ne changez khan kaa muqaabala kiya. 1369 i. main taimur ne ise apana nivasasthaan banaaya. 18veen shatabdi ke prarambh main yah china kaa bhaag rahaa. phir bukhaaraa ke amir ke antargat rahaa or ant main san 1868 i. main roosi samrajya kaa bhaag ban gaya.
