 DBS singapore kaa ek pramukh bank he jo 1968 main singapore sarkaar dwaara sthapit kiya gaya tha. 1998 main isane pioeshbi bank kaa adhikaar apanaaya or aaj dakshin-purv asia ke pramukh bainkon main se ek he. isaki kul aay 150 arab rupee se bhi jyade he. isaki 25 deshon main shakhaaen hai - bharat main bhi 12 shakhaaen kaam karti hai. 
 iskaa puraa naam Development Bank of Singapore tha jo ab sirf DBS ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
