 parmaanu urja vibhaag dwaara bharat parmaanu beemaa pool kaa shubharambh kiya gaya he. 
 parmaanu urja vibhaag ne 12 june 2015 ko bhartiya parmaanu beemaa pool kaa shubharambh kiya. 
 ienypi parmaanu urja jokhim hastantaran ke antargat vishwa kaa 27waan beemaa pool ban gaya he, is shrenee main france, rus, dakshin africa tathaa america jaise desh shaamil hai. 
 yah pool parmaanu operataron or aapurtikartaaon ke daayitv ke jokhim ko parmaanu kshati ke naagarik daayitv kii dhara 6 tathaa dhara ke tahat cover karegaa. 
 yah teen prakaar kii nitiyon par kaary karata he, operataron ke liye policy, pramukh aapurtikartaaon ke liye policy tathaa pramukh aapurtikartaaon ke atirikt anya aapurtikartaaon ke liye policy. 
 premium kaa mulya jokhim kii sambhaavnaaon, haani ke akalan tathaa us kshetra ke aas-paas rah rahe logon kii maujuudagi par nirbhar karegaa. 
 isaki dekharekh bhartiya saadhaaran beemaa nigam dwaara kii jaaegi, yah bharat kii ekmaatr punarbeema company he. 
 kul nidhi 1500 karod rupe main jiaaisi aarai tathaa anya chaar saarvajanik kshetra ke upakramon new india, oriental insurance, neshnal insurance tathaa united india insurance kaa nivesh 750 karod he. isamen british parmaanu beemaa pool ne bhi 500 karod rupye kaa nivesh kiya he. 
 yah raashi adhinium ke praavdhaan ke anusaar oparator ko adhiktam 1500 karod rupye kii deyta pradaan karata he. 
 yah hot zone tathaa cold zone ke liye beemaa cover pradaan karegaa. abhi bharat main keval unheen kshetron ke liye beemaa suvidha praapt he jo sayantron tathaa radiation kshetra ke baahar sthit hai.
