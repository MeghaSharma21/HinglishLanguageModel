 sarayu raay bharat ke jharkhand rajya kii jamshedpur pashchim seat se bhartiya janata party ke vidhayak hai. 2014 ke chunaavon main ve indian neshnal congress ke ummidavaar banna gupta ko 10517 voton ke antar se haraakar nirvaachit hue. 
 2014 ke chunaavon ke baad purna bahumat main ai bhartiya janata party kii sarkaar ke mukhyamantri bane raghuvar das evam kebinet mantri bane sarayu raay. raghuvar das sarkaar main shaamil mantri sarayu raay ne 1994 main sabase pehle pashupaalan ghotaale kaa bhandaphod kiya tha. baad main is ghotaale kii sibiai jaanch hui. raay ne ghotaale ke doshiyon ko sajaa dilaane ko ucch nyaayalay se lekar sarvocch nyaayalay tak sangharsh kiya. iske falswaroop rajd adhyaksh lalu prasad yadav samet darjanon raajnneetik netaaon va afasaron ko jail janaa padaa. sarayu raay ne 1980 main kisaanon ko aapurti hone vaale ghatiya khaad, beej, tathaa nakli kitnashkon kaa vitaran karne vaali sheersh sahakaarita sansthaaon ke viruddh bhi aawaaj uthaayi thi. tab unhone kisaanon ko muaavja dilaane ke liye safal andolan kiya. sarayu raay ne hi sanyukt bihar main alkatraa ghotaale kaa bhi bhandaphod kiya tha. iske alaava jharkhand ke khanan ghotaale ko ujagar karne main sarayu raay kii ahm bhoomika rahi. itane ghotaalon ke pardaafash ke baad to sarayu raay kaa naam bhrasht adhikariyon main khauf kaa paryaay ban gaya.
