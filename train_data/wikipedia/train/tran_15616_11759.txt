 plasmid kisi koshika main ek chota dillane anu hota he jo gunasutron ke dillane se alag ho or swatantraaa se apane praturup bana sake. yah adhiktar bacteria main chote golakaar do-rajjuon vaale dillane anuon ke rup main milte hai, haalanki inhen kabhi-kabhi orchia or eukeriot jeevon main bhi paaya jaataa he. jahaan gunasutron kaa dillane jeev ko saadhaaran paristhitiyon main jeene ke liye anuvaanshik soochna detaa he, vahaan plasmid dillane asaadhaaran paristhitiyon main jeev ko laabh pahunchaa saktaa he. udhaaharan ke liye kai bacteria pratijaivik pratirodh utpann karne ke liye plasmidon kaa prayog karte hai.
 plasmid ko replicon kii shrenee main daalaa ja saktaa he, jo dillane yaa araane ke aise anu yaa anuon ke ansh hote hai jo apane-aap kii nakal kar ke or pratiyaan bana saken. plasmid kii tarah, virus bhi replicon kii shrenee main aate hai. haalanki in donon main prajnan se milti-julati kshamta hoti he, adhikansh jeevvaigyanik inhen jeev kii shrenee main nahin daalate.
