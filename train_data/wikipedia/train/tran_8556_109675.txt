 1943-44 main bengal ek bhayanak akaal padaa tha jisamen lagbhag 30 lakh logon ne bhukh se tadapakar apni jaan jaan ganvaai thi. ye dwitiya vishwayuddh kaa samay tha. mana jaataa he ki akaal kaa kaaran anaaj ke utpaadan kaa ghatana tha, jabaki bengal se lagaataar anaaj kaa niryaat ho rahaa tha. haalanki, visheshgyon ke tark isse alag hai. 
 ek nai pustak kaa kahana he ki dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke dauraan britain ke pradhanmantri rahe winston churchill ne jaanboojhkar lakhon bharatiyon ko bhukhe marane diya. burma par japan ke kabje ke baad vahaan se chaaval kaa aayaat ruk gaya tha or british shashan ne apane sainikon or yuddh main lage anya logon ke liye chaaval kii jamkhori kar lee thi, jisaki vajah se 1943 main bengal main aae sookhe main tees lakh se adhik log maare gaye the.
 chaaval kii kami hone ke kaaran keemten aasmaan chhu rahi thi or japan ke akraman ke dar se bengal main naavon or baiylgaadiyon ko jabt yaa nasht kiye jane ke kaaran aapurti vyavastha dhwast ho gai. bazaar main chaaval mil nahin rahaa tha, gaavon main bhukhmaree fail rahi thi or churchill ne khadyann kii apaat khep bhejne kii maang baar baar thukraa di.
 1943 ke bengal main kolkata kii sadakon par bhukh se haddi haddi hui maanen sadakon par dam tod rahi thim. log sade khaane ke liye ladate dikhte the to british adhikari or madhyavarg bhartiya apane klbon or gharon par gulchharre uda rahe the. bengal kii maanav-rachit bhukhmaree british raaj ke itihaas ke kaale adhyyon main se ek rahi he, lekin lekhak madhushri mukherjee kaa kahana he ki unhen aise saboot mili hai jo dikhaate hai ki logon kii dusthiti ke liye churchill sidhe taur par jimmedaar the.
