 bhoopendranath bose avibhajit bengal ke krishnanagar main ek kysth parivaar main janmen the. bhoopendranath kii shiksha krishnanagar or kolkata main hui thi. ve sanskrit or farsi ke vidwaan the. chaatr jivan main hi ve england or europe kii yatraaen kar chuke the. kolkata se unhone 1881 main em.e. or 1883 main vakaalat pariksha paas kii. kolkata main vakil ke rup main kaam karne ke dauraan ve raajnneetik aandolan kii ore mud gaye.
 1905 ke bang-bhang ke aadesh ko radd karaane ke liye bhoopendranath bose 1907 main pratinidhi ke rup main england gaye. 1904 se 1910 tak bengal legislative concil ke sadsy ke naate unhone aazaadee ke liye aawaaj uthaai or rashtriya aandolan ke kaam main puri tarah jute gaye. ve akhabaaron kii swatantraaa ke pakshdhar the. 1910 main bane press act ko sweekruti milane par uskaa virodh kiya tha.
 surendranath banerjee, bipinchandra paal, bal gangadhar tilak or abdul rasul unke samakaleen raajnneetijna the, parantu ve dadabhai nauroji se sarvaadhik prabhavit rahe. unhone 1922 main geneva ke antarrashtriya shram sammelan main pratinidhi ke rup main hissaa liya.
 1923 main ve bengal ke rajyapal kii akjicutive concil ke sadsy bane. ve shiksha ke masalon se kareeb se jude rahe or unhen rashtriya sandarbhon main adhik viksit or sakasham banane par bal diya. 1924 main avsaan ke vakt ve kalakatta vishvavidhyalay ke kulapati ke pad par kaam kar rahe the.
