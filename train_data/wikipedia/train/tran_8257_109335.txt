 grahiy niharika aisi niharika ko kehete hai jo kisi badi aayu ke taare ke ird-gird yonikrut gas ke failte hue khol se bani hui hoti he. inkaa kisi grah se koi sambandh nahin he lekin inkaa naam "grahiy niharika" 18ve sadi ke khagolashastriyon ne yah rakh diya kyonki apni kamazor doorabeenon se unhen aisi niharikas bhimkaay grahon kii tarah lageen. grahiy niharikas keval kuch dasiyon hazaar saalon tak hi rahati hai or phir vyom main teetar-bitar ho jaati hai, jabaki taron kii umr arabon varshon kii hoti he.
 aisi niharikas taron se utpann taarakeey aandhi se ban jaati hai. kisi bhi gallexi main inaki mahatvapurn bhoomika hoti he kyonki yah taare main bane carbon, oxygen, nitrogen or calcium jaise baari rasinic tatwon ko antartaarkeey madhyam main failaa detii hai.
 "grahiy niharika" ko angreji main "planteri nebula" kehete hai.
