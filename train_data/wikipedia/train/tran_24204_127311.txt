 arq, niryaas yaa jeevanaras vrikshon kii daaru or valkal naamak nason main chalane vaale taral ko kehete hai jo ped ke har bhaag main aahaar, pani, hormone or jivan ke liye aavashyak anya rasayan pahunchaata he. arq kaa istemaal bahut se anya jeev bhi karte hai. bahut se keede pedo kii shakhaayen chhedakar ise pite hai. thande ilaaqon main bhurj kii tahaniyaan chhedakar uskaa meetha arq ekatrit kiya jaataa he jiskaa prayog khaane-pine main or sharaab banane ke liye kiya jaataa he. dakshin bharat main tad ke ped ke taane se nikle arq kii 'tadi' naamak sharaab banati he. canada main mapel ke vriksh ke atyant mithe arq ko ekatrik karke uskaa ek shahad-jaisi chaashni banati he.
