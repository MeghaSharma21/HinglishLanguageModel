 rashtriya pareekshan or anshshodhan prayogshaala pratyaayan board, bharat sarkaar ke vigyaan or praudyogiki vibhaag ke antargat ek swytt nikaay he jiskaa panjikaran sanstha panjikaran adhinium-1860 ke tahat kiya gaya he.
 enebl ko mukhyat: sarkaar, udyog sanghon or udyogon ko pareekshan or anshshodhan prayogshaalaon kii gunvattta or takneekee kshamta kaa akalan kisi tisare paksh se karwaane kii ek yojana uplabdh karaane ke uddeshy ke saath sthapit kiya gaya he. bharat sarkaar ne enebl ko pareekshan or anshshodhan prayogshaalaon ke ekmaatr pratyaayan nikaay ke rup main adhikrut kiya he.
