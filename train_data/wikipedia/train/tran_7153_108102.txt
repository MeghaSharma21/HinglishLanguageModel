 dow baalkrishna shivaram munje bharat ke ek prasiddh swatantraaa senaanee tathaa hindu mahasabha ke sadsy the. ve san 1927-28 main akhil bharat hindu mahasabha ke adhyaksh rahe. rashtriya swayamsevak sangh ko banavaane main inkaa bahut yogadan tha. sangh ke sansthaapak keshav baliram headgavar ke ve rajanithik guru the. dow munje bharat main sainik-shiksha ke purodha the.
 dow munje kaa janm 1872 main madhya praant ke bilaspur main hua tha. unhone 1898 main mumbai main grant medical college se medical degree lee. phir mumbai nagar nigam main chikitsa adhikari ke rup main kaam karne lage. sainya jivan main unki gahari ruchi ke chalate wo sena main kamishan adhikari ban gaye. sena kii chikitsa shakha unhone dakshin africa main boar yuddh main bhaag liya.
 gandhiji kii muslim tushtikaran kii niti se asahmat hone ke kaaran dow munje congress se alag ho gaye or unki ginati hindu mahasabha ke tejasvi netaaon main hone lagi. 1930 or 1931 ke golmes sammelnon main ve hindu mahasabha ke pratinidhi ke rup main gaye the jabaki gandhiji keval 1931 ke sammelan main congress ke pratinidhi ke rup main gaye. golmes sammelan ke baad farvari se march 1931 tak europe kaa bhraman kiya. 15 march se 24 march tak ve italy main bhi ruke. vahaan ve musolini se bhi mili. 
 1934 main munje ne central hindu military ajukeshan socity kii sthapana kii jiskaa uddeshy mathrubhumi kii raksha ke liye yuva hinduon ko sainya prashikshan dena or unhen 'sanathan dharm' kii shiksha dena tha. saath hi, nijee suraksha or rashtriya suraksha kii kala main yuvaaon ko prashikshit karnaa tha.
