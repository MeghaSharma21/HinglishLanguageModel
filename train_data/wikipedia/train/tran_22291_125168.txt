 sambalpur vishvavidhyalay, orisa ke sambalpur main sthit ek rajya vishvavidhyalay he. yah 'jyoti bihar' naam se prasiddh he. yahan purvasnatak tathaa snatakottar paathyakram uplabdh hai. orisa ke rajyapal iskaa paden kulapati hote hai. is vishvavidhyalay kaa praangan sambalpur se 15 kilometre dur burla naamak sthaan par he.
