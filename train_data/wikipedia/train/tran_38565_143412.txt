 punjakant uttar-pashchimi tajikistan ke subaad praant main zarafashaan nadi ke kinare basaa hua ek shahar he. san 2000 kii janaganana main yahan kii aabaadi 33,000 thi. yah prachinkaal main sonda kaa ek prasiddh shahar hua karata tha or us puraani nagari ke khandhhar aadhunik punjakant shahar ke bahari ilaaqon main dekhe ja sakate hai.
 "punjakant" shabd main 'k' par lagi 'ji' kii maatra ke uccharan par dhyaan den. yah 'e' or 'i' donon ke swaron se zaraa bhinn he.
 pracheen punjakant 5veen sadi isvi main shuru hua or sondaiyon ke "panch" naamak rajya kii rajdhani hua karata tha or iskaa naam "panchekanth" tha. yahan ke log adhiktar zarthushti dharm ke anuyaayi the lekin mana jaataa he ki sonda par bhartiya samskruti kii gahari chhap bhi thi, jis se kuch vaidic reeti-rivaaj bhi maane jate the. yahan vyapaar or krishi donon panape, kyonki yah resham maarg par padataa tha .
 san 722 i main arabi faujon ne dhaava bolkar punjakant par qabza jamaa liya. shahar kaa aakhri shaasha zarafashaan nadi ne upar vaale hisson kii taraf bhaag gaya lekin pakadakar maar diya gaya. aane vaale 50 saalon tak aakraamakon ne nagar par shashan kiya jis dauraan shahar kaa upree hissaa logon se khaali kar diya gaya or viraan ho gaya. nagar ke is bhaag main paae gaye khandhiron se pataa chalata he ki yahan ke logon kaa rahan-sahan, reeti-rivaaj or kala samskruti kya thi.
