 
 kachche yaa hare chane ko hola yaa horaha kehete hai. isaki choti jhaadiyon main bahut choti faliyaan lagati hai. har fali main 2 yaa 3 hare dane aate hai. isko anek prakaar se pakaaya jaataa he or kaccha bhi khaaya jaataa he. holi ke avasar par is paudhe kaa bahut adhik mahatv hota he. unheen dinon isaki nai fasal aati he or isaki faliyon ko holi kii aag main bhun kar khaanaa aavashyak samajha jaataa he.
