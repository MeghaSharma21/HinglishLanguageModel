  kamaladevi chattopadhyaay  bhartiya samajasudharak, swatantraaa senaanee, tathaa bhartiya hastkala ke kshetra main navajagaran laane vaali gandhivadi mahila thim. unhen sabase adhik bhartiya swatantraaa andolan main yogadan ke liye; swatantra bharat main bhartiya hastshilp, hathkargha, or theatre ke punarjagran ke peeche prerana shakti ke liye; or sahyog kii aguai karke bhartiya mahilaon ke saamaajik-aarthik str ke utthan ke liye yaad kiya jaataa tha. unhe samaaj seva ke liye 1955 main padam bhushan se alankrita kiya gaya.
 dow kamaladevi ne aazaadee ke turant baad shilpon ko bachaae rakhane kaa jo upkram kiya tha usamen unki najar main bazaar nahin tha. unki paini drushti yah samaj chuki thi ki bazaar ko hameshaa sahaayak kii bhoomika main rakhana hoga. yadi vah karta kii bhoomika main aa gaya to iskaa bachanaa mushkil hoga, parantu pichley teen dashakon ke dauraan bhartiya hastshilp jagat par bazaar haaavee hota gaya or gunvattta main lagaataar utaar aata gaya. bharatbar ke hastshilp vikaas nigamon ne shilpon ko bazaar ke najariye se dekhnaa shuru kar diya. unako isamen aasaani or sahajata bhi mahasoos ho rahi thi.
 kamaladevi chattopadhyaay ko likhane kaa bhi shonk tha or unhone kai pustaken likhi jo logo main kaaphi charchit huyin.logon ne unke dwaara likhin pustakon ko kaaphi saraahaa.
 isi tarah unhone kai pustaken likhin or isi ke saath lekhankaarya main bhi apana mahatvapur yogadan diya.
 smriti irani dwaara march 2017 main antarrashtriya mahila diwas ke avasar par mahila bunakaron evam shilpiyon ke liye ‘kamaladevi chattopadhyaay rashtriya puraskaar’ shuru karne kii ghoshnaa kii gayi.
