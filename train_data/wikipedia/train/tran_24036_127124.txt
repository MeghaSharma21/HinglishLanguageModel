 sirian pound : Syrian pound (arabi: الليرة السورية‎‏‎ sirian pound syria kii aadhikarik mudra he, yah sau piyastre se vibhajit he, lekin mudra sfiti kii vajah se yah chalan se baahar he. pratham vishwa yuddh se pehle otoman lira istemaal kiya jaataa tha. otoman samrajya ke patan ke baad 1918 main misri pound kaa chalan shuru ho gaya. syria or lebanon par france ke niyantran ke baad donon deshon ke liye ek nai mudra sirian pound jari kiya gaya, jo francisi frank se judaa hua tha. lekin lebanon ne 1924 se swayan ke sikke or 1925 se swayan ke banknote jari kiye. 1939 main lebanon kii mudra aadhikarik taur par syria kii mudra se alag ho gai.
