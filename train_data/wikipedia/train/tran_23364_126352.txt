 yajyavlky smriti dharamshastra parampara kaa ek hindu dharamshastra kaa granth he. yajyavlky smriti ko apane tarah kii sabase acchhee evam vyavasthit rachana mana jaataa he. isaki vishay-nirupan-paddati atyant sugrathit he. isapar virachit mitakshra tikaa hindu dharamshastra ke vishay main bhartiya nyaayalayon main pramaan maani jaati rahi he.
 iske shloke anushtup chand main hai - isi chand main gita, valmiki ramayana or manusmriti likhi gai he. isi vishay par manusmriti ko aadhunik bharat main adhik manyata mili he. isamen aacharan, vyavhaar or praayshchit ke teen alag alag bhaag hai. 
 is smriti main 1003 shloke hai. isapar vishvarupakrit 'balakrida', aparark krit yajyavalkeey dharamshastra niabandh or vigyaneshwarkrut mitakshra tikaayen prasiddh hai. kaarne kaa mat he ki isaki rachana lagbhag vikram purv pehali shatee se lekar teesri shatee ke bich main hui. smriti ke ant:sakshya isamen pramaan he. is smriti kaa sambandh shukla yajurveda kii parampara se hi tha. jis tarah maanav dharamshastra kii rachana pracheen dharmasutra yug kii saamagree se hui, aise hi yajyavlky smriti main bhi pracheen saamagree kaa upyog karte hue nayi saamagree ko bhi sthaan diya gaya. kautilya arthshaastra kii saamagree se bhi yajyavlky ke arthshaastra kaa vishesh samya paaya jaataa he. 
 yajyavlky smriti main teen cond hai-
 manusmriti or naarad samhita is vishay par anya do pramukh granth hai. chaaron ashram, dand, raja-praja, vivaah jaise kai vishay in granthon main saajha hai. jahaan manusmriti brahmanon ko shudron se vivaah karne kii ajna detaa he, yajvalkysmriti main iskaa nishedh he.
