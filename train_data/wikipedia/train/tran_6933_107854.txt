 shrikrishnalila tarangini narayan tirth dwaara rachit ek tarangini he. tarangini, nrity naatika ke liye atyant upyukt hoti he or isiliye pichhali do shatabdiyon se bhartiya shastreey nritak krishnalila tarangini kaa upyog bahut acchhee prakaar se karte aa rahe hai.
 is tarangini main 12 tarangam, 153 geet, 302 shloke tathaa 31 churnika hai. narayan tirth ne veda vyas ke bhagwatam kaa anusaran kiya he tathaa dasham skanda par vishesh rup se kendrit kiya he.
