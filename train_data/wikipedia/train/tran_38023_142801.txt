 guru ram charan yaa raamacharan ji maharaj atharahavin sadi ke armbh main rajasthan ke jaipur main utpann ke ek sant the. inhone ramasanehi sampradaay kii sthapana kii. ye nirgun bhakthi shakha ke sant the.
 raamacharan ji kaa janm magh shukla 14 shanivaar sanwat 1776 ko apane nanihaal soda naamak gram main hua. yah sthaan jaipur jile ke malpura naamak nagar ke sameep he. aapke pitaaji kaa naam bakhtraam ji tathaa mataji kaa naam deuji tha. ye malpura ke sameep banawada naamak gram ke rahane vaale the. inaki jati vijayawargeey vaishya gautra kapadi thi. swami ji kaa bachapan kaa naam ramkishan tha.
 vinateeramji dwaara likhit jivan charitra pustak main inke vivaah kaa ullekh kiya he. aapkaa vivaah chandsen naamak gram main, ek sampann parivaar main girdharilal khunteta kii kanya gulab kanvar bai ke saath hua. is avadhi ke aapke ek putree kaa janm hua jinka naam jadaav kanvar tha. keval shri jagannatha ji krit guru lila vilaas main iskaa ullekh miltaa he.
 inhone ne jaipur rajya ke antargat kisi ucch pad par nishtha purvak rajkeey seva kii. kuch anya lekhakon evam shri laldaas ji kii parachi ke anusaar unhone jaipur rajya ke dewan pad par kaam kiya. unke pita ke mosar ke avasar par rajya kii ore se tikaa pagadi kaa dastur aana is baat kaa spasht pramaan he ki ve kisi sammanit pad par asin the. 
 inke guru kriparam ji maharaj the jinhone inhen ram bhakthi kii shiksha di.san. 1817 main ye bhilwara gayi or vahiin apni anbhaivani kii rachana kii. inke nivas hetu vi. san. 1822 main devkaran ji toshnival ne ramdvara kaa nirmaan karaayaa gaya.
 swamiji raamacharan ji maharaj vaishakh krishna panchami guruuvar san. 1855 ko shahpura main hi brahamlin hue.
