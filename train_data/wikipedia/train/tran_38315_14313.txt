 kshareey bhoomi us mitti ko kehete hai jinka piech maan 9 se adhik hota he 
 kshareey or lavanamay bhoomi us prakaar kii bhoomi ko kehete hai jisamen kshar tathaa lavan vishesh maatra main paae jate hai. shushk jalwaayu vaale sthanon main yah lavan shwet yaa bhure-shwet rang ke rup main bhoomi par jamaa ho jaataa he. yah bhoomi purnataya anupjau evam oosar hoti hai or isamen shushk ritu main kuch lavanpriy paudhon ke alaava anya kisi prakaar kii vanaspati nahin milti. pani kaa nikaas na hone ke kaaran barsaat main in bhoomikhandon par barsaati pani atyadhik maatra main bharaa rahata he. yah pani kritrim naaliyon ke abhaav, prakritik dhaal kii kami evam neeche kii mitti ke apraveshya hone ke kaaran bhoomikhandon se baahar nahin nikal paata or garmi padane par vayumandal main udakar suukh jaataa he. barsaat main yah gandala bana rahata he or sookhne par bhoomi kii sataha par lavan chhod detaa he tathaa saath hi saath ise kshareey bana detaa he.
 vibhinn praanton main is bhoomi ko alag alag naamon se pukaarte hai, jaise uttar pradesh main 'oosar' yaa 'rehala', punjab main 'thur', kallar yaa baaraa, mumbai main chopan, karal ityadi. aisi bhoomi adhiktar uttar pradesh, punjab evam maharashtra praanton main pai jaati he. andhra pradesh tathaa tamilnadu main bhi yah milti he. 
 aisi bhoomi teen mukhya shreniyon kii hoti he. 
 rasinic tareekon dwaara is bhoomi ko pahachaanaa jaataa he. is bhoomi kaa punanirmaan karne ke liye adhik maatra main pani bharkar lavan ko ghul jane dete hai. phir yah pani kritrim naaliyon dwaara baahar nikaal dete hai. adhik ksharwali bhoomi main jipsom kaa churna or viley calsiumyukt padaarth kaa prayog aavashyak ho jaataa he. prarambh main keval lavan or jalpriya paudhe, jaise dhan vaa jau, ugaae jate hai.
