 gaurade, goud haiti kii mudra he. iskaa iesao 4217 code HTG he or yah 100 centaimes yaa santim main vibhajit kii jaati he. 
 pehali gaurade 1813 main livre ke sthaan par ek gaurade = aath livre or paanch sous kii dar se jari kiye gaye the.
 sikkon ke pehle sanskaran chaandi main dhale 6, 12 or 25 ke centime ke rup main saamne aae. 1827, 50 or 100 centime ke sikke jari kiye gaye or 1828 main 1 or 2 centime jari kiye gaye.
 haiti kii sarkaron ne 1, 2, 4, 5, 8, 10, 12, 16, 20, 24, 25, 48 or 100 gaurade ke mulya main kagaji mudra jari kii.
 1870 main gaurade kaa das ke anupaat par ek kii dar par mudra kaa punarmulyan kiya. duusari gaurade ke liye sarkaar ne keval 10 or 25 ke note jari kiye.
 1872 main gaurade kaa phir punarmulyan kiya or is baar dar teen sau par ek tha.
