 punjabi tandoor paaramparik rup se mitti se banaaya jaataa he or voh ek bel shaped oven he or use dharti main lakadiyon or charcol ke saath jalaayaa jaataa he kariban tapaman 480 degree par. punjabi tandoor ko zamin kii sataha par bhi rakhaa ja saktaa he. punjabi tandoor ke liye isi tarah kii kalakritiyon sindhu ghati citon main khoj kii gai he haalanki, punjabi tandoor ke upyog ke avibhajit punjab main punjabi khaanaa pakaane ke saath judaa hua he.
 punjabiyon ko paaramparik rup se maance vyanjan or bread pakaane ke liye ek kshetreey str par punjabi tandoor kaa istemaal kiya he. punjabi tandoor ke upyog punjabi samskruti he ki yah punjabi lok geet kaa ek hissaa he itane main aropit he.
 punjabi tandoor ke upyog, bharat ke anya kshetron main lokapriy ho gaya he 1947 ke baad. pashchimi punjab chhodane punjabiyon dilli jaise kshetron main basaaya or unke saath unke punjabi tandoor laayaa.
