 mithen ek rangahin tathaa gandhhin gas he jo eendhan ke rup main upyog kii jaati he. yah prakritik gas kaa mukhya ghatak he.
 mithen gas kaa rasinic sutara CH4 he. yah alken shrenee kaa pratham sadsy he or sabase saadhaaran hydrocarbon he.
 jab yah sataha or vaataavaran pahunchataa he, yah vayumandaleey methane ke rup main janaa jaataa he prithvi ke vayumandal main methane kaa ghanatv 1750 ke baad se lagbhag 150% kii vruddhi hui he or yah ek green house gas he. methane gas kii khoj sabase pehle november 1776 main italy or switzraland ke vaigyanik elesandro volta ne kii thi. unhone magior jhil ke daldal main is gas kii pahchaan kii thi.
