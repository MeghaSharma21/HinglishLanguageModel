 sushila karki
 sushila karki  ek nepali mahila nyaayamurti hai. ve nepal supreme court kii pratham mahila mukhya nyaayadhish rah chuki hai. 13 april 2016 ko nepal supreme court kii pratham mahila mukhya nyaayadhish niyukti kii gayi pradhanmantri ke pi oli kii adhyakshata main sambedhanik parishad ne nepal ke supreme court ke mukhya nyaayadhish ke pad ke liye inke naam kii sanstuti kii. karki nyaaypalika main bhrashtachar ke khilaaf shunya sahishnutaa ke liye jaani jaati hai. 
 karki apane pita kii saat santanon main sabase badi he. inkaa sambandh kisaan parivaar se he.banaras hindu vishvavidhyalay varanasi ,bharat main adhyayan ke samay nepali congress ke prasiddh yuva netaa durga prasad suvedi se milane ke pashchaat inkaa vivaah durga prasad suvedi se hua. 
 inhone banaras hindu vishvavidhyalay se raajnneeti vigyaanan main em 0 e 0 kii oopaadhi prapti kii. varsh 1978 main tribhuvan vishvavidhyalay se vidhi snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii 
 prarambh main inhone shikshan kaary kiya. 1979 main yah vakaalat peshey main ain. supreme court kii mukhya nyaayadhish niyukti hone pehle karki supreme court kii barishthatam nyaayadhish thim. 22 janvari 200 9 ko supreme court kii asthaayee jaj baniin or 18 november 2010 ko sthaayee jaj ban gayi. 
 30 april 2017 ko karki ke viruddh sansad main mahabhiyog prastut kiya gaya or isi ke saath niyamanusaar karki nepal ke supreme court ke mukhya nyaayadhish ke pad se nilambit ho gayin.
