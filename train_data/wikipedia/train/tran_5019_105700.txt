 sharad joshi sammaan ,utkrisht srijan ko rashtriya str par sammanit karne kii apni supratishthit parampara kaa anusaran karte hue madhyapradesh shashan ne hindi vyangy, lalit nibandh, sansmaran, reportaj, diary, patr ityadi vidhaon main rachanaatmak lekhan ke liye sthapit kiya he. yah gaurav kii baat he ki sharad joshi madhyapradesh ke nivasee the, jinhen unki shashakt or vipul vyangy rachanaaon ne sahitya ke rashtriya paridrishy par pratishthit kiya. sharad joshi ne vyangy ko nayaa tevar or vaividhya diya tathaa samay kii visangati or vidmbana ko apni prakhar lekhnee se ujagar karte hue samaaj ko drushti or disha pradaan karne kaa uttardaayi rachanakarm kiya. unki vyangy rachanaaon ne hindi sahitya kii samruddhi main apana sunishchit yogadan diya he.
 rashtriya sharad joshi sammaan kaa uddeshy sahitya kii aisi vidhaon kii shreshthatam pratibhaaon ko sammanit karnaa he jo kavita, kahani, upanyaas, naatak, aalochana aadi kendriya vidhaon main rachana na karte hue bhi anya sarjanaatmak vidhaon ke madhyam se sahitya kii samruddhi or bahulata main apana yogadan detii hai. nishchay hi yah sammaan rachanaatmak utkrushtataa, sudeergh sadhana or asaadhaaran uplabdhi ke nivirvaad mandandon par hi dey he.
 sammaan ke antargat 51 hajaar rupee kii raashi or prashasti pattica di jaati he.
