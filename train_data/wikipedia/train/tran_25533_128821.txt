 anubhuti kavitaaon kii hindi jaal-patrika he. iskaa pehla ank 1 janvari 2001 ko prakashit hua tha. maheene main iske chaar ank pehali, 9veen, 16veen or 24veen taarikh ko prakashit hote hai. 1 janvari 2008 se yah patrika prayek somvaar ko prakashit hoti he. isamen pracheen kaviyon kii kavitaaon ke stambh kaa naam 'gauravgram' he or ubharate hue kaviyon kii kavitaaon ko 'nai hawa' main rakhaa gaya he. isamen dohe, gazal, hiku, muktak aadi vidhaon ke liye alag stambh to hai hi, pravasi kaviyon ke liye dishantar naam kaa bhi ek stambh he. vibhinn vishyon par kavitaaon ko sankalit karne kaa bhi kaam yahaan kiya gaya he jise sankalan ke antargat rakhaa gaya he. patrika se sambandhit 'anubhuti hindi' naam kaa ek yahuu gut he jahaan kavitaaon kii kaaryshaalaaon main koi bhi bhaag le saktaa he.
 yah abhivyakti kii sahyogi patrika he.
