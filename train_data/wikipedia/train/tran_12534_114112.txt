 bum laa yaa bum darra bharat ke arunachal pradesh rajya or tibet ke lhokha vibhaag ke bich himalay kaa ek pahari darra he. yah samudratal se 15,200 foot kii oonchaai par arunachal pradesh ke tawang shahar se 37 kimi kii duuri par sthit he.
 tibeti bhashaa main "laa" shabd kaa arth darra hota he.
 yahan kai bodh ashram hai jinamein duniyaan kii doosraa bada ashram bhi he jahaan tibet se palaayan karke aae dalai lamas ne ek samay par sharan lee thi. yah stham trikoni pahadiyon ke bich sthit he. yah prachinkaal main vyapaarik daravaaje ke rup main vikhyath tha.
