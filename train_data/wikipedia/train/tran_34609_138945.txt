 bhim datta nagarpalika, nepal ke mahakali anchal kaa kanchanpur jila kii ek nagarpalika he. iske andar lagbhag 14450 ghar he. yah pashchim nepal se utranchal rajya pravesh kaa mukhya naakaa he. bhim datta maahakaali anchal kaa anchal mukaam honeke saath-saath kanchanpur jilekaa bhi mukaam he.
 paryatako ko rahane ke liye paanch saat besht hotel bhi gorkha me he. bhim datta me nepal imigrasan kaa aphis bhi he. utranchal himanchal se nepal jane vaale bahari deshon ke paryatak yahi se nepal kaa vija le sakate he. bhim datta sukhlaphanta vanya jantu rigerv kaa ddhaar he isilie yahan paryatako kaa chahalpahal hota hi rahata he. bhim datta sudurpaschim nepalka ek pramukh sahar he.
 bhim datta sahar mahakali anchalka pramukh swasthya va shiksha kaa kendra bhi he. yanhepe siddhanath bahumukhi kyampus va bhim datta bahumukhi kyampus ke alaava bahut se ucch madhyaamik bidhaalay, madhyamik bidhaalay bhi he, maahakaali anchal aspataal ke alava vahut se shwasth sanstha bhi bhim datta me he. aai es di, pre ped, poshtped movil, imail internet uplabdh he, bhim datta nepal kaa pramukh mahendra rajmarg kaa paschim kaa antim vinduhai. yaanha se desh ke har hisse me jane ke liye yaata yaat saadhaan uplabdh he, saath saath yah maahakaali anchal ke paahadi jilonka pramukh supplaai point bhi he nagar va jile ke andaruni yatayaat ke liye kai pakki kachi nai sadak banrahe he. 
 is sthaan kaa akshansh or deshaantar kramshah 28.92° uttar or 80.33° purv hai. samudr sataha se yah jagah 198 meter unchi he. 
 nepal ke 2001 kaa janaganana anusaar bhim datta kii jansankhya 99,265 he. isamen purush 49% or mahila 51% he.
