 dehdaan kaa arth he 'deh kaa daan' arthath kisi uttam kaary apana jivan hi de dena. rajkumaar satva ne saath saavakon ko bhukh se marane se bachaane ke liye apane sharir kaa tyaag kar diya tha. dadhichi ne apana sharir isliye tyaag diya tha taaki unki haddiyon se dhanush banaaya ja sake jisase daityon kaa sanhaar ho sake. raja shivi ne kapot ko bachaane ke liye apane sharir ko prastut kar diya tha.
 anek samudaayon main deh ko nadi main pravaahit karne kii parampara he, taaki pani main rahane vaale vibhinn jeevon ko aahaar uplabdh ho sake. parsi samudaay main mrityu ke uparaant deh ko ek 'kuen' main rakhane kii parampara he, taaki pakshi us deh ko aahaar bana saken.
