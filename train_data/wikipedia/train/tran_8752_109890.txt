 talwadya sangeet main aisa vaadya yantra jis se dhwani usapar haath yaa anya kisi upkaran se prahaar dbaara yaa ragadkar utpann ho. iske udhaaharan tabalaa, damru, jhanjh or mrudang hai. jhanjh jaise talwadyon main do ek-samaan bhaag hote hai jinhen aapas main takaraakar sangeet banaaya jaataa he. itihaaskaar maanate hai ki maanav swar ke baad talwadya hi sabase pehle avishkrut sangeet vaadya the.
