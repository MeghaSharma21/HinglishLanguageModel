 1900 greeshmakaleen olympic main ghudsawaari greeshmakaleen olympic kii shuruyaat kii, paris, france main. yah 1912 tak gaayab ho gaya tha, lekin baad main har greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main dikhaai diya. vartmaan olympic ghudsawaari vishyon main dresses, evanting or jumping he. prayek anushaasan main, vyaktigat or team donon padak se sammanit kiya jaataa he. mahilaon or purush samaan sharton par ek saath pratispardhaa karte hai.
 ghudsawaari vishyon or aadhunik pentathalon ke ghudsavaar ghatak bhi ekmaatr olympic ghatnaayen hai jinamein pashuon ko shaamil kiya gaya he. ghode ko rider ke rup main zyada athlete mana jaataa he. 
 ghudsavaar khel ke liye antarrashtriya shaasi nikaay, fadet equetre internationel he.1924 olympic pehla tha jisamen epaiai ke adhikaar ke tahat ghudsavaar pratiyogitaaon kaa aayojan kiya gaya tha.
 dhyaan den: andhere grey varg un varshon kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai jisamen enosi yaa to astitv main nahin tha yaa olympic khelon ke ghudsavaar bhaag main pratispardhaa nahin karata tha.
