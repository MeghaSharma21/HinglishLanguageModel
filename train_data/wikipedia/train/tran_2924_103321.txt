 dow karn singh chouhan varisht lekhak or aalochak hai. inaki ek darjan se adhik aalochana-pustaken, kavita-sankalan, kahani-sangrah, yatra-vrittant, diary or anuwaad prakashit hai. mul rup se dilli vishvavidhyalay main adhyaapan kiya lekin kai varshon tak sophia vishvavidhyalay balgaria, seoul, korea main atithi profaisar ke rup main adhyaapan kiya. inaki samalochanaayen vibhinn patr-patrikaon main prakashit hoti rahati hai. 
 purna parichay
 janm–tithi :  28 farvari‚ 1948 ko uttarapradesh ke bulandshahr main janm. 
 shiksha   
 •     skool kii padhai dilli ke skulon main.      
 •     hindu kalege‚ dilli vishvavidhyalay se hindi main bi.e. anars or em.e.. 
 •     dilli vishvavidhyalay se em. lit. or pi.ech. di.. 
 vyavsaay  
 •     1970 se 2012 tak vibhinn padon par dilli vishvavidhyalay main adhyaapan kaary. 
 •     1987 se 1991 tak sophia vishvavidhyalay‚ balgaria ke praachya vidya vibhaag main atithi profaisar. 
 •     1999 se 2002 tak seoul‚ korea main hankuk vishvavidhyalay ke bharat 
 vidya vibhaag main atithi profaisar. 
 •     2009 se 2011 tak punah korea ke hankuk vishvavidhyalay main atithi profaisar.

 1.    aalochana ke nae maan   
 2.    sahitya ke buniyadi sarokaar  
 3.    pragativaadee aandolan kaa itihaas  
 4.    ek sameekshak kii diary  
 5.    europe main antaryatraaen  
 6.    america ke aarpaar  
 7.    himalay nahin he vitosha 
 8.    yamuna kachaar kaa man  
 9.    pahaad main phool : korian kavita–sangrah  
 10.   samakaleen yatharthwaad : gyarge lukach  
 11.   itihaasadrushti or aitihaasik upanyaas : lukach 
 12.   lu sun kii chuni hui rachanaaen  
 13.   meraa jivan, meraa samayh pablo neruda 
 anya  
 •     desh–videsh kii patrikaon main hindi–angreji main 100 se adhik lekh. 
 •     europe ke anek vishvavidhyalayon main bhartiya sahitya‚ samskruti or darshan par abhibhaashan. 
 •     vampanthi raajnneeti or sahitya sanghathanon main sakriy bhagidaari.
