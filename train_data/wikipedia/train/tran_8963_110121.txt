 barf stupa, glacier graphting takanic kaa ek rup he jiske dwaara kritrim himandon kaa nirmaan kiya jaataa he. shanku aakaar ke in barf ke dheron ko sardiyon main pani ko sangrahit karne ke liye istemaal kiya jaataa he. garmiyon ke dauraan, jab pani kam hota he, to barf stupon se pighali barf fasalon ke liye pani kii aapurti karti he. kyonki inkaa aakaar buddha stupon ke samaan hota he isiilie inhen barf stupa kaha jaataa he. bharat ke kshetra ladakh main sonam wangchuck dwaara barf stupa kaa avishkaar kiya gaya or is pariyojna ko 'students ajukational and cultural movement of ladakh ' dwaara sanchalit kiya jaataa he. yah pariyojna oktober 2013 main shuru kii gai or janvari 2014 main da aees stupa project ke antargat ek pareekshan pariyojna kii shuruwat hui. 15 november 2016 ko, sonam wangchuck ko barf stupa par unke kaam ke liye rolex puraskaar for enterprize se sammanit kiya gaya.
