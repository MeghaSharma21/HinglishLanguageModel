 uttar upniveshwaad adhyayan kii ek shakha he jisamen upniveshwaad or samrajyavad se utpann hone vaale saanskrithik or saamaajik prabhaavon ko samajha jaataa he. isamen kisi videshi shakti dwaara kisi kshetra par qabza hone se sthaneey logon ke jivan par hone vaale prabhaavon kaa adhyayan kara jaataa he. uttar aadhuniktawaad ke drushtikon se in upniveshi yaa bhootpurv upniveshi kshetron main soochna va gyaan ke prasar kaa adhyayan kara jaataa he or yah bhi samajha jaataa he ki kis prakaar bahari shakti sthaneey logon kii maansikta main hin bhaavana utpann kar ke unpar lambe arase tak niyantran rakhte hai. videshi samrajyavaadee shakti dwaara shaasha samaaj or adheen samaaj kii rajnatik, saanskrithik or aitihaasik chhaviyon ko prastut karne kii shaileyon ko bhi dekha jaataa he, kyonki yah bhi videshi samaaj dwaara sthaneey samaaj ko adheen kar lene kaa ek mahatvapurn saadhan hota he.
