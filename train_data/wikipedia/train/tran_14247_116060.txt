 hemburg germany kaa ek pramukh nagar evam bandargah he| ek samay yah hemburg rajya kii rajdhani tha.
 yahan kii bhoomi badi upjau he. rai, jau, gehun tathaa aalu kii acchhee fasalein hoti hai. hemburg ke atirikt bargedorf or kuksahaheaven anya bade nagar hai. hemburg nagar samudr se 120 kimi andar alway nadi kii uttari shakha par berlin se 285 kimi uttar pashchim main sapaat bhoomi par sthit he. is nagar main naharon kaa jaal bicha hua he. iske bich se elster nadi bhi bahati he jo ise do bhaagon main vibhakt karti he. chote bhaag ko binen elstor kehete hen. dwitiya vishwayuddh main bambaari se ise bahut kshati pahaunchi thi parantu yuddh ke baad nagar kaa purnirmaan ho gaya he.
 dwitiya yuddh ke pehle yah coffee kaa bahut bada centre tha or yahan mudra kaa bhi vinimay hota tha. aajkal yahan se chini, coffee, oonee or sooti saamaan, lohe ke saamaan, tambaakoo, lohe, anaaj or coffee ke kachche maal mangaae jate hai. jahaaj nirmaan kaa acha vyavsaay hota he, jahaajon kii marammat bhi hoti he. yah bandargah varsh bhar khulaa rahata he. yahan kaa vishvavidhyalay suprasiddh he. isamen anek aadhunik vishyon kii padhai hoti he.
