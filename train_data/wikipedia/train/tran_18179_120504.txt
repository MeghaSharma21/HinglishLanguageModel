 bhartiya buddha mahasabha bharat kaa rashtriya buddha sanghathan he. ise bhimrao ambedkar dwaara shuru kiya gaya, isaki sthapana 4 may 1955 ko mumbai, maharashtra main gai thi. 8 may 1955 ko nare park, bombai main yojit ek samaaroh main ambedkar ne buddha dharm ke prachaar ke liye is sanghathan kii sthapana kii aupchaarik ghoshnaa kii. iskaa mukhyalay mumbai main he. vartmaan main iske rashtriya adhyaksh ke rup main rajratna ambedkar kaary kar rahe hai, vah dow ambedkar ke bhai aanandarav ke praputra hai. yah antarrashtriya buddha sanghatan world faillowship of buddhists kaa sadsy hai. isse kai bhartiya rajyon ke buddha anuyaayi sadsy ke rup main jude hue hai.
