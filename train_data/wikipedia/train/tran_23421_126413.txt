 jab kisi chaalak main prayavarti dhara bahati he to chaalak ke pariched main dhara ghanatv samaan nahin hota balki chaalak ke kendriya bhaag main kam or bahru bhaag main adhik hota he. is prabhaav ko uparistar prabhaav kehete hai. duusare shabdon main, vidyut dhara mukhyatah 'chaalak kii twachaa' main bahati he. is prabhaav ke kaaran chaalkon kaa prabhavi pratirodh adhik aavritiyon par adhik hota he. uparistar prabhaav,bhavar dhara ke kaaran hoti he. 60 hertz par tanba main uparistar gaharaai lagbhag 8.5 mimi hoti he. adhik aavriti par pratirodh ke badhane ko rokane ke liye vishesh rup se bune hue litz taar prayog kiye jate hai. chunki adhik kshetrafal vaale chaalkon ke aantarik bhaag main bahut kam dhara pravaahit hoti he, tub ke aakaar ke chaalak kaa prayog karne se chaalak kaa vajan or mulya kam rakhaa ja saktaa he.
