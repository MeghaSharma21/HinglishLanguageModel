 sedal parvat bharat ke andaman or nicobar dweepasmooh kaa sabase unchaa pahaad he. yah uttar andaman dweep par sthit he or sedal peak rashtriya udyaan dwaara ghira hua he. yah parvat digalipur se 10 kimi dakshin main khadaa he. andaman dweepasmooh kii ekmaatr nadi, kalpong nadi, isaki dhalaanon se armbh hoti he.
