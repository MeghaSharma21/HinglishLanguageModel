 general aagaa mohammad yahaya khan qizilbash pakistan ke sainik taanashaah or tisare raashtrapati the. unka shashan san 1969 main shuru hua or disambar 1971 main bharat-pakistan ke darmyaan lade gaye bangladesh yuddh ke baad khatm hua. ve ayub khan ke shashan se istifa dene ke baad pakistan ke mukhya shaasha bane the or unke baad zulfikaar ali bhutto ne shashan kii bagdor sanbhali. purvi pakistan ke bengali samudaay main vidroh kii bhaavana ko kuchalane ke prayaas main use or bhi bhadkaa dene or phir bhartiya sahaayta se bangladesh ke tuutakar alag hone jane ke liye aksar yahaya khan kii nitiyon ko zimmedaar tehraaya jaataa he.
