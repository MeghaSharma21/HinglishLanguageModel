 thutamos tritiy pracheen misr ke navin samrajya ke atharahawein rajvansh kaa chatha firo tha or misr ke itihaas kaa sabase mahaan firo bhi| apane shashan kaal ke pehle 22 varsh vah rajdhikari ke rup main apni sauteli maan hatshapsut ke saath raaj karata rahaa| hatshapsut ne thutamos ko apana senapati niyukt kiya tha.
 hatshapsut kii mrityu ke baad thutamos aglaa firo bana, usne laghbhag 17 sainya abhiyaan kiye or misr kaa sabase bada samrajya khadaa kiya jo uttar main phonishia or faraat nadi tak,pashchim main libya tak or dakshin main nil nadi ke chauthe jal-prapaat tak yani nubia tak tha. 
 uskaa sampoorn shashan kaal 54 varsh kaa he, pratishaasak ke rup main 22 varsh milakar| uske naam kaa arth he "devtaa thoth ne janm liya",yah sanket he kii vah devtaa thoth kaa upaask tha.
