 kubj vishnuvardhan badami ke chalukya shaasha pulakeshi dwitiya ke chote bhai. inkaa viruddh prithveevallabh, yuvaraj, vishamaviddhi aadi tha. saatvin shatabdi i. ke purvarddh main pulakeshi ne unki sahaayta se purvi tat ke deshon ko vijit kiya or unhen vishaakhpatnam se lekar nellore jile ke uttari bhaag tak kaa shaasha niyukt kiya. is vansh ke abhilekhon ke anusaar unka shashan sampurna vengimandal par tha. unka rajvansh 11veen shatabdi tak chalata rahaa or vah itihaas main purvi chalukya ke naam se prakhyat he. sanskrit ke prasiddh kavi bharavi unki sabha ke ratna kahe jate hai.
