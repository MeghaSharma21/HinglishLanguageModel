 krichar 3di vikram bhatta dwaara nirdeshit 2014 kii triayami kalpit vigyaan hindi film he. film main bipasha basu or imran abbaas naqvi ne mukhya kirdaar nibhaae hai. tii-series ke binar tale film kaa nirmaan kiya gaya he. mukul dev or mohan kapur ne bhi sahaayak kirdaar nibhaae. film kii kahani brahmarakshas naam ke rakshas ke upar chalati he.
