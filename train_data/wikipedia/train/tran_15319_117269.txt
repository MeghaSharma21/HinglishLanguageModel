 gulburg vishvavidhyalay karnataka rajya main karnataka vishvavidhyalay kaa ek snatakottar kendra he. 1980 main karnataka sarkaar ne ek kanoon ke tahat ise vishvavidhyalay kaa darja diya tha. is vishvavidhyalay ke adhikarashetra main gulburg jila, bidar jila, raichur jila, bellari jila evam coppal jila aate hai.
