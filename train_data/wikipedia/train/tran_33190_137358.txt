 condishnarabalo kii dekhbhaal sambandhi ek utpaada he. condishnar baalo kii sanrachana or chamak main parivartan laata he or baalo ko kadhana yaa unki sajja karnaa sugam banaata he. aamtaur par condishnar kaa upyog shampoo karne ke baad kiya jaataa he or visheshrup se yah lambey baalo kii dekhbhaal main sahaayak hota he. iske upyog se bal mulaayam or chamakdaar ho jate hai. prakritik kesh condishnar ke rup main tel jaise ki nariyal yaa jojoba ke tel kaa prayog kiya jaataa he.
