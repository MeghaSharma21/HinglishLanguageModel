 aagneyastron main prakoshth ko handguard yaa forestock ke rup main janaa jaataa he. yah resiver or thuthan ke bich main pe jane vaala hathiyaar kaa ek khand he. yah agnyashastra ko pakadaney main madad karata he or yah amtur par lakdi kaa bana hota he. handguard ke aage hameshaa ek bairal band hota he jo kii uske kavach kii tarah istemaal hota he. kuch handguard upyogkarta kii garmtaap se raksha karne ke liye garmi dhaal ke saath susajjit kiya jaataa he.
