 bonalu yaa devi mahakali bonulu ek hindu tyohaar he, jisamen devi mahakali kii puja kii jaati he. bonalu, telugana kaa vaarshik tyohaar jo hyderabad, sikanderabad or telugana ke alaava bharat ke kai anya hisson main manaaya jaataa he. yah asadha maheene main arthath july / agast main manaaya jaataa he. tyohaar ke pehle or antim din yelmmaa ke liye vishesh puujaaen kii jaati hai. pratigya kii purti ke liye tyohaar ko devi ko dhanyavad bhi mana jaataa he.
