 armaan kohli ek bhartiya film abhinayta hai, jo mukhyatah hindi filmon main abhinay karte hai. wo film nirmata evam nirdeshak rajkumaar kohli ke putr hai. unhone filmon main abhinay ek bal-kalakaar ke rup main badale kii aag evam raaj tilak filmon main apane pita ke saath kiya tha. iske alaava unhone big bos tivi shrunkhlaa ke saatven sanskaran main bhi bhaag liya.
