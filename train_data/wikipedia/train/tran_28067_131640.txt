 shipraga bosnia or harzegovina main ek gaanv he.
 shipraga, varbanya nadi ke tat par sthit he or banya shahar se takreeban 60 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. yah gaanv ek lambii ghati main basaa hua he. iske ird-gird ke pahari shrunkhalaaon main, uttar purab main glavik shikhar, dakshin main: jasik, dakshin purv main shahinovina-strajbenika, pashchim main radohova or uttar pashchim main: Borčići hai.
 is avasthaapana kaa vistaar, varbanya or isaki sahayika nadiyon ke sahare kareeb teen kilometre kii lambaai or do kilometre kii chaudaai vaale hisse main he. yah ilaaka peyajal ke maamle main dhani he or sthaneey jalsurangon main nadijal kii aapurti hoti he.
 bosnia main yuddh ke dauraan, kotor vars kii nagar palika main hirakit bosniaks ke 18 shiviron main se ek siparge main police stesan main tha.
 kshetra main Šiprage aasapaas or vahaan ek madhyam mahadwipiya jalwaayu, chaar mausam, sardi ke saath he. pahaad or subalpina chaaron ore pahadiyon main, taapmaana or varsha main vaarshik utaar-chadhaav apekshaakrit adhik he, lekin lambey samay tak sthirataa kar rahe hai.
