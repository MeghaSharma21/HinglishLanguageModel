 mall vansh ke shaskaon ne 12veen shatabdi se 18veen shatabdi tak nepal main shashan kiya. mall kshatriya he. sanskrit main 'mall' shabd kaa arth 'yoddha' hota he. mall vansh kaa shashan lagbhag 1200 i ke aasapaas kathmandu upatyaka main armbh hua. unhone lagbhag 550 varshon tak nepal main shashan kiya. unka shashan nepal kaa swarnyug mana jaataa he. mall raajaaon ke shasankaal main hi kathmandu ghati or uske aasapaas ke kshetron main rahane vaale log 'nevary' kahalaaye.
