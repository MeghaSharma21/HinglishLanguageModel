 sir jaipal meghavaal samudaay ke meghavanshi jati ke vyakti the tathaa pokharan ke jagirdaar raja ajmal sinh tanvar ke ghodo ko charaate the| avtari baba ramdev pir kaa avataran unke ghar hua tha. jise avatar bataaya gaya jo ek rahasy he.
 baba ramdev ke janm ke itihaas main is mahaan rahasy ko chipaaya gaya he. jisamen baba ramdev pir kaa avatar raja ajmal tathaa mata menade bataaya gaya he. bina maa kii kokh se kisi bachche kaa janm ho saktaa he. baba ramdev kaa avatar nahin janm hua tha. jo prasiddh itihaaskaar ramachandra kadela ne 'avatharavaad ke shikaar lok krantikari mahamanav-baba ramsapir' naamak apni kriti me likhaa he."sir sut mangani raa jaaya, gyaari mahima baari,bhent kiyo sut ajamalji ne, sir ne balihaari |meghrikha sang tanvar vansh raa, bhaag jaagia baari,duniya jaane ramdevji ne ajmal ghar avtari." ||is rahasy se sabase pehle pardaa jodhpur ke uttam ashram ke peethadhishwar swami ramprakashacharya ji ne apni kitaab "ramdev gapp puraana" tathaa "dhol main pole" main kiya. jisamen baba ramdev kaa janm sir meghavanshi ke ghar mata mangani kii kokh se hua bataaya gaya. prasiddh dalit lekhika kusum meghavaal ne apni pustak 'meghavaal baba ramdev' varsh 2006 main ek shodh granth - 'ramdev pir' ek punarvichaar main prakashit hua jisamen ek baat samaan thi. sir jaipal meghavaal tathaa mata magani devi the. tathaa barmer jile ke undu kashmir gaanv ke rahane vaale the. undu kashmir main baba ramdev kaa sir meghavaal ke ghar janm he. daali bai baba ramdev kii sagi bahan he.
 baba ramdev pir ke janm sambandhit maamla rajasthan ucch nyaayalay main gaya jaha se faisla meghavaal samudaayake paksh main hua. baba ramdev meghavaal he or ve sir meghavaal ke putr he.
