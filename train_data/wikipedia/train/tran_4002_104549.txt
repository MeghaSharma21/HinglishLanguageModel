 un vrikshon ko faldaar vriksh kehete hai jin par lagane vaale fal manushya evam kuch jaanwaron ke khaane ke kaam aate hai. pushp vaale sabhi vriksh fal bhi dete hai. fal vastav main pushp kaa pakaa hua andashay hi he. inamen ek yaa adhik beej hote hai.
 kintu udhyaniki main 'faldaar vriksh' se taatpary keval un vrikshon se he jo maanav ke bhojan ke kaam aane vaale fal dete hai.
