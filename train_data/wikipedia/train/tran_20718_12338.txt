 jaan jeshua ketler yaa yon yoswa ketler, kaa naam dach bhashaa main likhe gaye unke hindi vyakaran ke granth ke liye janaa jaataa he. yon yoswa ketler kaa paariwarik naam ketler tha, uskaa janm purvi prashia ke elbing naamak nagar main 1659 i. main hua tha or uski mrityu 1718 main faras main. vah san 1683 main bharat aayaa tha or dach east india company ke adheen clerk, assistant, acountint, book keeper, provisional 'cheef', junior marchent, marchent, senior marchent, doot, director ow trade, rajdoot padon par kaary kiya. is dauraan use surat, bharuch, ahmedabad, agra evam lakhanu main rahane kaa avasar mila. agra nivas ke samay usne hindi vyakaran dach bhashaa main likhaa, jisaki pratilipi uske sahaayak ijaak phan dir hiv ne 1698 i. main lakhanu main prakashit kii. san 1743 main iskaa latin anuwaad david mil ne prakashit karavaaya. isi latin anuwaad se pehali baar yah vyakaran prakaash main aayaa. dow suniti kumaar chatarji ne is latin anuwaad kaa anushilan kar apana lekh "The Oldest Grammar of Hindustani" prakashit karavaaya. laidan vishvavidhyalay ke dow fogal ne mul dach bhashaa main likhit hindi vyakaran kaa anushilan kar apane do lekh "The Author of the First Grammar of Hindustani" or "Joan Josua Ketelaar of Elbing, Author of the First Hindustani Grammar"14 prakashit karaaye. ketler kii jeevani or uske hindi vyakaran kii vistrut charchaa prakrut lekhak ke "hindi bhashaa kaa pratham vyakaran" naamak lekh main kii gayi he.
