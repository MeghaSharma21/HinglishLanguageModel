 anker naabard ke tatvadhan main bharat sarkaar dwaara prarambh kii gayi ek yojana he jisamen sookshmavitt ke sanvardhan ke liye mahila swayan sahaayta samooh or bainkon bich sampark badhaayaa jaayga. yah yojana tamilnadu ke nagapattanam se prarambh kii jaaygi. isamen mahila swayan sahaayta samoohon ko ek gaiyr sarkari sanghathan kii tarah mana jaataa he. yah ek payalat pariyojna he jise desh ke 113 jilon main prarambh kiya gaya he.
