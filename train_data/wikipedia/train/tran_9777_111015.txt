 diod ek P tip semiconductor or N tip semiconductor kaa jankshan hota he isliye diod ko ham P-N jankshan diod bhi kehete hai. jaisaa kii ham jaanate hai ki P tip semiconductor main hole ki adhikta rahati he or N tip semiconductor main electrons kii. hamen ye bhi pataa he ki vipareet charged partical ek dusre ko apni ore aakarshith karte hai or samaan vikarshith. chunki electrons negative charged partical hote he or hole posetive, isliye jab ham donon prakaar ke semiconductor ko milaate hai to electrons or hole ek dusre ko aakarshith karte he. kuch samay baad donon kaa aakarshan simit ho jaataa he or jankshan ke paas electrons or hole kii parat ban jaati he or use ham deplation rigen kehete hai. diod ek bipolar divaais hota he matlab isamen negative or posetive done said hote he. yahan aap diod kii banaavat or uskaa electronic simbol dekh sakate hai. isamen anode rinatmak or cathod dhanatmak siron ko darshata he. diod main karant ke pravaah ke liye hamen use kisi voltage sorce se jodna padataa he jaise kii bydaree. jaisaa kii ham jaanate hai ki bydaree main bhi negative or posetive donon chor hote hai isliye diod ko ham bydaree ke saath do tarah se jod sakate hai or in tareekon ko ham bisingh kehete hai. jab ham bydaree ke ko diod ke se or bydaree ke ko diod ke se jodate hai to ise forward bisingh kehete hai or jab ham bydaree ke ko diod ke se or bydaree ke ko diod ke se jodate hai to ise reverse bisingh kehete hai.
