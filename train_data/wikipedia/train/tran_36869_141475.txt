 guuruuvaar saptaah kaa paanchava din he. ise brihaspativaar, veeravaar yaa bife bhi kaha jaataa he. yah buudhvaar ke baad or shuukravaar se pehle aata he. musalmaan ise zumeraat kehete hai kyonki yah jumma se ek din pehle aata he.
 hindu dharm main is din kaa vishesh mahatv he. yah din bhagavan vishnu ko samarpit he. is din bal nahin katwanaa chaiye. sabun or tel kaa prayog nahin karnaa chaiye. dhan or vidya ki haani hoti he. avadhi bhashaa main ek kahawat he :-
 dhana dhan se adrani, to bife ko tel nahaani.
 matlab agar aapako lagata he ki aapke paas bahut sa dhan ho gaya he to guuruuvaar ko tel lagaana shuru kar den.
 is din kele kii puja kerani chahie. ghar ko gobar se lipana chahie. kalavati kanya or leelavati kanya kii katha sunne chahie. pilaa bhojan karnaa chahie.
