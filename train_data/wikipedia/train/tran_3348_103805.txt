 pakistan murdaabaad hindustani bhashaa or ek had tak punjabi bhashaa main bharat ke bantwaare or khaas kar punjab bantwaare ke samay ke dauraan lagaaya gaya ek rajnatik naaraa he. iskaa matlab he: "pakistan kii maut ho" yaa or zyaada uchit "pakistan kaa vinaash ho" or isaki shabd nirukti farsi bhashaa se he.
 yah slogan bharat bantwaare se pehle pakistan lahar yani dakshin asia main ek naye islamic mulk kii sthapana karne ke liye andolan ke dauraan pehali baar sikhon ke leader master tara singh dwaara uccharit kiya gaya tha kyonki punjab kshetra ke sikh musalamaanon kii prasavit hukumat ke zordaar kilaaf the. yah mohammad ali jinna ke slogan "pakistan zindaabad" ke ulat main lagaaya gaya tha.
 pichley kuch saalon main yah slogan kaa varnan dakshin asin sahitya main mila ja rahaa he, saadat hasan manto, baapsi siddhwa or khushvanth singh jaise harman pyaare lekhak kii rachnaayon main is slogan ullikhit he.
 bharat bantwaare se kuch hi samay baad naye mulk pakistan main rafyuji campon main fanse gaye logon ne nayi pakistani hukumat kii naqabaliyat ke viruddh apane gusse vyakt karne ke liye yah slogan uccharit kiya tha.
