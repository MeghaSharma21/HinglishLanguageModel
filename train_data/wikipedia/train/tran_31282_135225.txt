 dhawaliram meena lala rampur, karauli ke mulnivaasee hai. pad geet main inhone pure purvi rajasthan main jo mukaam paaya hai wo kaabile taareef hai. inhone hajaaro pado ko likhaa, unhen apni mandali ko sikhaakar sangeet kii is vidha ko praasangik va aakarshith banaaye rakhaa. inhone dharmik vishyo ke alaava samaaj main prachlit paramparaao ko bhi nishaanaa banaaya, or pado ke madhyam se dahez, bal vivaah, bhedbhaav, ashiksha par jamkar prahaar kiye or logo ko in manovritiyon se nikal baahar aane kaa aavan kiya.
 inkaa janm karauli ke lala rampur gaanv main hua tha. inkaa rujhaan shuru se hi pad gayan kii taraf badha. or inhone apni mandali banaakar iname bhaag lenaa shuru kiya. ye media ke naam se jane jate hai
 dhawaliram ke pado kii vishaywastu main samakaleen vishyo ke alaava samjik sthiti or vyavastha bhi shaamil hoti he. dharmik vishay bhi mahatvapurn rahe.
