 bam kaa nibatara vah vidhi he jisase khatarnaak visphotak padaartho se surakshit hua ja saktaa he. mukhya visphotak nireekshak kaa mukhyalay nagpur main sthit he tathaa isaki shakhaye agra, kolkta, gavahati, chandigarh, mumbai, chennai, sikandarabad main sthit he. is kaam ke liye sena kii nikatatam unit kii bhi sahaayta lee jaati he. or nyaayalayik vigyaan kii prayogshaala main bhi bam kaa nibatara karne ke liye visheshgy uplabdh he. bam kaa nibatara karne kii sthiti main deshi bam, pipe bam, transistor bam aadi ko bina hilaae pani se bhari baaltee main 72 ghanto ke liye rakh dene se usame maujuud sara barood geela ho jaataa he, jisase vah bam visphot nahin hota.
