 koi bhi yukti jo urja lekar kuch kaaryaklap karti he use yantra yaa mashin kehete hai. saral mashin vah yukti he jo lagaaye jane vaale bal kaa pariman yaa disha ko badal de kintu swayan koi urja khapat na kare.
 bhartiya jyotish, rasshastra, ayurveda, ganit aadi main is shabd kaa prayog hua he.
 bharadwaj muni krit yantrarnav granth ke vaimaanik prakaran main di gai mantra, tantra or yantra kii paribhaasha-
 raja bhoj dwaara rachit samarangansutradhar ke 'yantravidhan' naamak 31wein adhyay main yantron kii visheshtaon or vibhinn prakaar ke yantron kaa varnan he.
 siddhanth shiromani tathaa brahmasputsiddhanth main yantradhyay naam se alag adhyay main khagoliki main prayukt yantron kii mahatta tathaa unka vishad varnan he. 
 ayurveda main naanaa prakaar ke ras shodhan karne ke yantra hai.
