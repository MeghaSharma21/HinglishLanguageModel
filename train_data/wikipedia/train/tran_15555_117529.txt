 air vaais marshalakpila jayampati srilanka ke vayusena pramukh hai. ve march, 1982 main vayusena main shaamil hue the. 12 sitambar, 2016 ko srilanka ke raashtrapati maitripala sirisena ne air marshall kapila jayampati ko srilanka kaa nayaa vayusena pramukh ‘airforce commander’ niyukt kiya. jayampati ne purv commander gagana bulatsinghla kii jagah liya. purv pramukh 55 saal ke hone par sevanivrutt ho gaye the.
