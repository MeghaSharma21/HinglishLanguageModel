 rohilkhand yaa ruhelkhand uttar pradesh ke uttar-pashchim main ek kshetra he..
 rohilkhand ganga kii upatyaka ke upree 25000 varg kimi kshetra main vistrut he. iske dakshin pashchimi ore ganga he, pashchimi ore uttarakhand or nepal uttar main hai. purvi ore avadh he. iskaa naam yahaan kii ek janajati rohilla ke naam par padaa. mahabharat main ise madhya desh ke naam se janaa gaya he..
 is lekh kii saamagree sammilit hui he britnica vishwakosh ekadashwein sanskaran se, ek prakaashan, jo ki jan saamaanya hetu pradarshit he..
