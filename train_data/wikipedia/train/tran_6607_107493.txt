 chhadam vigyaan ek aise daawey, aasthaa yaa pratha ko kehete hai jise vigyaan kii tarah prastut kiya jaataa he, par jo vaigyanik vidhi kaa paalan nahin karata he. adhyayan ke kisi vishay ko agar vaigyanik vidhi ke mandandon ke sangat prastut kiya jaae, par wo in mandandon kaa paalan nahin kare to use chhadam vigyaan kaha ja saktaa he.
 chhadam vigyaan ke ye lakshan hai: aspasht, asangat, atiranjit yaa apramanya daavon kaa prayog; daawey kaa khandan karne ke kathor prayaas kii jagah pushti purvaagrah rakhana, vishay ke visheshgyon dwaara jaanch kaa virodh; or siddhant viksit karte samay vyavasthit kaarividhi kaa abhaav. chhadam vigyaan shabd ko apmanjanak mana jaataa he, kyonki ye suzav detaa he kisi cheez ko galat yaa bhraamak bhang se vigyaan darshaya ja rahaa he. isliye, jinhen chhadam vigyaan kaa prachaar yaa vakaalat karte chitrit kiya jaataa he, ve is chithran kaa virodh karte hai.
 vigyaan ampirical anusandhan se prakritik jagat main antardrushti detaa he. isliye ye dev shruti, dharamshastra or aadhyatm se bilkul alag he.
