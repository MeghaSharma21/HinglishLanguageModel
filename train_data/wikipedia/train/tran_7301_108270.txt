 radiojaiviki ayurvigyaan kii ek shakha he jisamen jeevon ke upar inkaree vikiran ke prabhaav kaa adhyayan kara jaataa he. inkaree vikiran jeevon kii koshikaon or utakon par bahut hanikaarak yaa jaanaleva prabhaav rakhti hai lekin kuch sandarbhon main unki simit maatra se chikitsa bhi kari jaati he, masalan kark rog ke kuch upachaaron main yah bhi shaamil he. adhik maatra main inkaree vikiran praapt hone ke bhayankar dushaprinaam hote hai, jinamein utakon kaa jal janaa or varshon baad tak karkarog kaa ho janaa sammilit hai. sharir ke andar kaa drushya dekhne ke liye halki maatra main oaks kirnon se bhi inkaree vikiran praapt hota he.
