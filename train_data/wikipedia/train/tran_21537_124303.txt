 vishwa paryaavaran diwas paryaavaran kii suraksha or sangrakshan hetu puure vishwa main manaaya jaataa he. is diwas ko manaane kii ghoshnaa sanyukt raashtra ne paryaavaran ke prati vaishvik str par raajnneetik or saamaajik jagriti laane hetu varsh 1972 main kii thi. ise 5 june se 16 june tak sanyukt raashtra mahasabha dwaara yojit vishwa paryaavaran sammelan main charchaa ke baad shuru kiya gaya tha. 5 june 1974 ko pehla vishwa paryaavaran diwas manaaya gaya.
 1972 main sanyukt raashtra dwaara maanav paryaavaran vishay par sanyukt raashtra mahasabha kaa aayojan kiya gaya tha. isi charchaa ke dauraan vishwa paryaavaran diwas kaa suzav bhi diya gaya or iske do saal baad, 5 june 1974 se ise manaanaa bhi shuru kar diya gaya. 1987 main iske centre ko badalte rahane kaa suzav saamne aayaa or uske baad se hi iske aayojan ke liye alag alag deshon ko chunaa jaataa he.
 isamen har saal 143 se adhik desh hissaa lete hai or isamen kai sarkari, saamaajik or vyavsaayik log paryaavaran kii suraksha, samasya aadi vishay par baat karte hai. 
 vishwa paryaavaran diwas ko manaane ke liye kavi abhaya kumaar ne dharti par ek gaan likhaa tha, jise 2013 main nai dilli main paryaavaran diwas ke din bhartiya saanskrithik parishad main yojit ek samaaroh main bharat ke tatkaaleen kendriya mantriyon, kapil sibbal or shashi tharur ne is gane ko pesh kiya.
