 udar chirakar striyon kii dimbugrathiyon ke arbud nikaalane ke shalyakarm ko dimbugranthichhedan kehete hai. jab arbud maarak maaloom hota he, jab uske maarak yaa amaarak hone kaa thik thik gyaan nahin ho paata, jab uske maarak na hote hue bhi dhire-dhire bahut badhane kii tathaa sharir ko ksheen karke maarak hone kii ashanka hoti he, jab usamen vrunt hone se aksheeya ghurnan yaa marod utpann hone kaa dar bana rahata he or jab eks kirnon kii tathaa anya chikitsa nishphal siddh hoti he, tab yah shalyakarm kiya jaataa he. yah bada shalyakarm he, jisamen arbud ek granthi main hote hue bhi uske saath donon dimbugranthiyaan nikaali jaati hai. arbud bada hone par uske saath dimbavahi nalika kaa bhi kuch ansh nikaala jaataa he or jab arbud maarak hota he tab uske saath donon dimbugrathiyon ko tathaa garbhashay ko bhi nikaal diya jaataa he. yah shalyakarm sarvapratham kentki ke iffrem mcdowell dwaara kiya gaya tha.
