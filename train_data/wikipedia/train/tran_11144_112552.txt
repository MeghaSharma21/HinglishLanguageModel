 ranikhet bharat ke uttrakhand rajya kaa ek pramukh pahari paryaton sthal he. yah rajya ke almoda janapad ke antargat sthit ek faujee chhavnee he. deodaar or balut ke vrikshon se ghira ranikhet bahut hi ramnik hill stesan he. is sthaan se himachhadit madhya himalayan shreniyan spasht dekhi ja sakati hai. ranikhet se suvidhapurvak bhraman ke liye pindari glacier, kausani, chaubatiya or kalika pahunchaa ja saktaa he. chaubatiya main pradesh sarkaar ke falon ke udyaan hai. is parvatiy nagari kaa mukhya aakarshan yahan virajati naiysargik shaanti he. ranikhet main faujee chhavnee bhi he or golf premiyon ke liye ek sundar park bhi he.
 1869 main british sarkaar ne kumaun regiment ke mukhyalay kii sthapana ranikhet main kii, or bhartiya garmiyon se bachane ke liye hill stesan ke rup main is nagar kaa prayog kiya jane laga. british raaj ke dauraan ek samay main, yah shimla ke sthaan par bharat sarkaar ke greeshmakaleen mukhyalay ke rup main bhi prastaavit kiya gaya tha. 1900 main isaki garmiyon kii 7,705 jansankhya thi, or usee saal kii sardiyon kii jansankhya 1901 main 3,153 maapi gai thi. svachh surveykshan 2018 ke anusaar ranikhet dilli or almoda chhavniyon ke baad bharat kii teesri sabase svachh chhavnee he.
 ranikhet naam ek sthaneey lokkatha se liya gaya he, jisamen kaha gaya he ki isi sthaan par katyuri raja sudhardev ne apni ​​rani kaa dil jeet liya tha. is kshetra ko unhone baad main apane nivas ke liye bhi chunaa, haalanki is kshetra main koi mahal sthit nahin he.
 3 may 1815 ko gorkhaon dwaara kumaun ko angrejon ko saunp diya gaya tha. almoda par vijay prapti ke baad, 1839 tak angreji sena ke sainik or adhikari havalbagh main rahete the. baad main sena ke karyaalayon ko almoda sthanantarit kiya gaya or sena ko lohaghat or pithauragarh main tainaat kar diya gaya. is sena ko hi baad main kumaun batalian kaha jane laga. 1846 main ise waapas almore ke lalmandi kile main sthanantarit kar diya gaya, jahaan yah ranikhet chhavnee banane tak tainaat rahi.
 1830-56 ke bich, vibhinn europea samoohon ne jile main chaay baganon kii sthapana kii aashaa main bhoomi adhigrahan kiya. unamem se ek trope parivaar bhi tha, jisane chaubatiya, holm khet or upat ke graminon se jameen kharidi. 1868 main pehali baar is sthal kaa chayan british sainikon ke awaas liye kiya gaya tha, or agle hi varsh sarna, kotli or taana ke graminon se 13.024 rupaye ke muaavze par bhoomi adhigrahan kar yahaan chhavnee kaa gathan kar diya gaya tha. trope parivaar kii sari sampattiyon ko bhi usee varsh adhigrahit kiya gaya tha, holm sampatti ko chhodkar, jise 1893 tak chhavnee main shaamil nahin kiya gaya tha. 1869-70 main nagar main police stesan kii sthapana hui. 1887 kii shuruuaat main hi yahaan ke jangalon ko ek kaary yojana ke tahat laayaa gaya tha, haalanki inhen 1915 tak aam chhavnee kanoonon or niyamon ke tahat prabandhit karnaa jari rakhaa gaya tha. in niyamon ke tahat charaai or anumati ke bina ghaas kaatane or jangalon ko jalaane se manaa kiya gaya tha. 3 disambar 1915 ko 1878 ke bhartiya van adhinium ke tahat chhavnee ke hisse ko arakshit van ke rup main adhishuchet kar diya gaya.
 british raaj ke dauraan ek samay main is nagar ko shimla ke sthaan par british bharat ke greeshmakaleen mukhyalay ke rup main bhi prastaavit kiya gaya tha. 1900 main isaki 7,705 kii garmiyon kii jansankhya thi, or usee saal kii sardiyon kii jansankhya 1901 main 3,153 maapi gai thi. 1913 main ise paali tehsil kaa mukhyalay banaaya gaya, jiskaa naam baad main ranikhet tehsil kar diya gaya tha.:170 
 bharat kii swatantraaa ke baad se hi almoda jile ko baantkar alag ranikhet jila banane kii maang uthati rahi he. 1960 ke dashak se hi ranikhet jile ke liye andolan shuru ho gaye the, or 1985 tak in andolanon kii sankhya or gaharaai, donon hi kaaphi bad chuki thi. 1987 main uttar pradesh rajasav parishad ke adhyaksh vainkat ramani kii samiti ne jile kii sanstuti kii or phir iske 2 varsh baad 1989 main aathven vitt ayog ne jile ko vittiya manjuuri bhi pradaan kar di. iske baad bhi jab jile kaa gathan nahin hua, to 1993-94 main phir ek andolan shuru ho gaya, jiske baad upi ke tatkaaleen mukhyamantri mulaayam singh yadav ne pahal kii, or ranikhet main sio tathaa ediem kii niyukti kii gai, parantu baad main ediem ko hataa diya gaya.
 2004, 2005 main phir se log jile kii maang ko lekar andolnarat hue. 2007 main prashasan ne rajya sarkaar ko ranikhet jile kaa aadhikarik prastaav bhej. is prastaav ke anusaar ranukhet jule main chah block, paanch tahseelein, 1309 rajasav gaanv, 59 nyaay panchayatein tathaa 120 patawaari kshetra shaamil kiye gaye the. 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar prastaavit jile kii jansankhya 3,40,456 or kshetrafal 13735.740 hectare tha. 2010 main bhi adhivaktaaon ke netritv main ek bada jan andolan hua. aath maheene tak chale is andolan ke baad 2011 main uttrakhand ke tatkaaleen mukhyamantri ramesh pokharial ne almoda jile kii ranikhet, salt, bhikiasann, dvaarahaat, or chaukhutiyaa tahseelon se ranikhet jile kii ghoshnaa kii thi, parantu gajet notification jari na hone ke kaaran jila astitv main nahin aayaa. varsh 2012 ke vidhansabha chunaavon ke baad maamla thande baste main chalaa gaya.
 ranikhet kaa mausam sardiyon main bahut thanda ho jaataa he, or garmiyon main madhyam rahata he. sardi ke mausam main ranikhet main barf bhi padati he, mukhya rup se disambar, janvari or farvari ke maheenon main. haalanki baaki maheenon main ranikhet kaa mausam sukhad rahata he.
 ranikhet chhavnee uttrakhand rajya main almoda tathaa nainital se kramshah 50 or 60 kimi kii duuri par 29.64 ke ankshashon tathaa 79.42 ke deshaantar par sthit he. prashasnic rup se ranikhet almoda janapad ke pashchimi bhaag main sthit he, jahaan yah isi naam kii tehsil kaa mukhyalay bhi he. ranikhet chhavnee do parvat chotiyon par faili hui he; pehali, jise ranikhet rij kaha jaataa he, 5,983 feet kii oonchai par sthit he; or duusari, chaubatiya rij 6,942 feet kii oonchai par he. 
 ranikhet chhavnee main bhartiya sena kii kumaun tathaa naga regiment ke regimental centre sthit hai. chhavnee kul 4,176.031 acre kshetra main faili hui he, jisamen se 2,580.135 acre reserve van kshetra, 920.328 acre stesan kshetra tathaa 675.568 acre military kshetra he.:155 chhavnee ke chaaron ore chid, banj, or deodaar ke jangal hai, jinamein tendue, pahari bakrii, hiran, sambar, pine marton, khargosh, laal chehre vaale bandar, junglee langur, laal lomadee or sahidaar sahit kai vanyajeev nivas karte hai.
 ranikhet nagar main prashasan kaa kaary ranikhet chhavnee parishad dekhati he. ranikhet chhavnee parishad kaa gathan 1924 ke chhavnee board adhinium ke antargat hua tha. ranikhet ek pratham shrenee kii chhavnee he, tathaa prashasnic kaaryon se 7 vaardon main vibhakt he. ranikhet chhavnee parishad main kul 14 sadsy hote hai, jinamein se 7 inhi vaardon se chunakar aate hai.
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar, ranikhet kii jansankhya 18,886 he, jisamen se purushon kii sankhya 11,712 he jabaki mahilaon kii sankhya 7,474 he. 1881 main jab imperial gajetir aaf india ke pratham sanskaran kaa prakaashan hua, us samay ranikhet kii jansankhya 5,984 thi, or nagar main 3,313 hindu, 1,090 musalmaan, tathaa 1,573 european log rahete the. ranikhet nagar kii sakshartaa dar 95.21% he, jo rajya kii ausat 78.82% se adhik he. purushon main sakshartaa lagbhag 97.79% he jabaki mahilaon main sakshartaa dar 91.18% he.
 nagar kii kul aabaadi main se 85.11% ​​log hindu dharm kaa jabaki 9.22% log islam kaa, or 5.09% log isai dharm kaa abhyaas karte hai. iske atirikt nagar main alp sankhya main sikh or buddha dharm ke anuyaayi bhi hai. hindi or sanskrit rajya kii aadhikarik bhashaaain hai jabaki kumaunni yahaan kii sthaneey boli he. angreji kaa bhi prayog hota he.
 ranikhet ke pramukh vidhyalay nimna hai:
 rashtriya rajmarg 109 ranikhet se hokar gujarata he, jo ise rudrapur, haldwani, nainital, almoda, dvaarahaat, chaukhutiyaa or karnaprayag nagaron se jodta he. iske atirikt ranikhet sadak maarg se ramnagar tathaa khernaa se bhi judaa he. pantnagar main sthit pantnagar vimaanashetra sabase nikatatam hawaii adda he, jabaki ramnagar tathaa kathgodam nikatatam railway stesan hai.

 ranikhet golf course ranikhet ke pramukh aakarshanon main se ek he. asia ke ucchtam golf corson main se ek ranikhet golf course main 9-hole kaa course he. yah ranikhet nagar se 5 kimi kii duuri par he.
 saint brijet carch ranikhet nagar kaa sabase purana carch he.
 kumaun regimental centre kumaun tathaa naga regiment dwaara sanchalit ek museum he. yahan vibhinn yudhon main pakde gaye astr tathaa dhvaj pradarshan karne ke liye rakhe gaye hai. iske atirikt museum main oparation pawan ke samay pakadi gayi altitii kii ek naav bhi he.
 ashiana park ranikhet nagar ke madhya main sthit he. kumaun regiment dwaara nirmit or viksit is park main bacchon ke liye visheshkar jangal theme sthit he.
 yah mandir kumaun regiment ke nar singh maidan se sanlagn he. mandir ke saamne ek gurudwara, tathaa ek shawl kii factory he.
 rani jhil nar singh maidan ke sameep vir nari awaas ke neeche sthit he. is jhil main naukayan kii suvidha uplabdh he.
 binsar mahadev bhagwan shiv ko samarpit ek mandir he. mandir ke sameep bahati ek gaad vihangam drishy prastut karti he. mandir ke paas deodaar tathaa chid ke jangalon ke madhya sthit ek ashram bhi he.
 bhaludham nagar ke sameep sthit ek kritrim jhil he. yahan se himalay shrankhlaon kaa manoram drishy dekha ja saktaa he.
 majkhaali almoda-ranikhet road par ranikhet se 12 kimi dur sthit ek picnic sthal he. yahaan se bageshwar main sthit trishul chotii ke drishy dekhe ja sakate hai.
