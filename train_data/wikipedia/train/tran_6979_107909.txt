 tarang dairghy: 10 mm to 1 mm
 atyadhik ucchavriti radio aavriti pattee main sthit sarvaadhik radio aavriti he. iskaa range he 30 to 300 giga hertz, jiske upar ke vidyut chumbakeey vikiran ko adhorakt prakaash kaha jaataa he, jise teraa hertz vikiran bhi kehete hai. is pattee kaa tarang dairghy das se ek millimetre hota he, no ki is kaa millimetre band namakaran karata he jise laghu rup main MMW or mmW bhi kehete hai
