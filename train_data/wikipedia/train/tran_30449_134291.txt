 nihaali bhashaa pashchim-madhya bharat ke madhya pradesh va maharashtra rajyon ke kuch chote bhaagon main boli jane vaali ek bhashaa he. yah ek bhashaa viyojak he, yani vishwa kii kisi bhi anya bhashaa se koi gyaat jaateey sambandh nahin rakhti or apane bhashaa-parivaar kii ekmaatr gyaat bhashaa he. bharat main iske alaava keval jammu or kashmir kii burushaski bhashaa hi duusari gyaat bhashaa viyojak he. nihaali samudaay kii sankhya lagbhag 5,000 he lekin san 1991 kii janaganana main inamen se keval 2,000 hi is bhashaa ko bolne vaale gine gaye the.
 nihaali samudaay aitihaasik rup se korku samudaay se sambandhit rahaa he or unheen ke gaanvon main basataa he. is kaaran se nihaali bolne vaale bahut se log korku bhashaa main bhi dwibhashiya hote hai. nihaali boli main bahut se shabd asapas kii bhashaaon se liye gaye hai or saadhaaran bolchaal main lagbhag 60-70% shabd korku ke hote hai. bhashavaigyanikon ke anusaar mul nihaali shabdaawalee ke keval 25% shabd hi aaj prayog main hai.
