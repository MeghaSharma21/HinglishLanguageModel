 iskaa mukhyalay bharat kii vittiya rajdhani mumbai main he. bhartiya jivan beemaa nigam ke 8 aanchalik karyaalay or 101 sambhageeya karyaalay bharat ke vibhinn bhaagon main sthit hai. iske lagbhag 2048 karyaalay desh ke kai shaharon main sthit hai or iske 10 lakh se zyada agent bharat bhar main faile hai.
