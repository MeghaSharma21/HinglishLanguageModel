 
 shatpaad praaniyon ke aarthropoda sangh kaa ek upsang he. isamen aarthropoda sangh kii sarvaadhik jatiyaan shaamil hai, jin sab kii chah taangen hoti hai. keet or teen anya bina paron vaale aarthropoda - colembola, protura or diaplura - is upsang main sammilit hai. is upsang se baahar kii adhikansh anya aarthropoda jatiyon kii chah se adhik taangen hoti hai.
