 hikerikh gustav adolf angler german vanaspati shastrajna the. unhone padap-vargiki evam padap-bhugol par ullekhneey kaary kiya. 
 bracelau vishvavidhyalay main inhone shiksha pai or yaheen se 1866 i. main inhen doctor ov philaasphi kii oopaadhi mili. chaar varsh adhyaapan karne ke pashchaat ye munich botanical institute ke sarankshak niyukt hue. iske pashchaat chah varsh keel vishvavidhyalay main professor, paanch varsh bracelau vishvavidhyalay main professor tathaa audbhid udyaan ke sanchaalak or 1889 se 1921 i. tak berlin audbhid udyaan ke sanchaalak rahe.
 anunsadhan ke liye inhone teen baar africa kaa tathaa ek baar bharat tathaa java kaa paryaton kiya. isi uddeshy se inhone rus, japan tathaa sanyukt rajya hote hue vishwabhraman bhi kiya. inaki vishesh dein vargeekaran tathaa audbhid bhutrutt ke kshetra main he, kintu vanaspati vigyaan kii anya shakhaon main bhi inkaa kaary mahatvapurn rahaa he. inaki mrityu 1930 i. main hui.
 swayan tathaa anya logon ke sahyog se inhone kai bahumoolya granth likhe hai, jinamein 'di natiraliken pflantsen famileen', 'das pflantsenrikh' tathaa 'silabas dar pflantsen famileen' pramukh hai. inhone 'botonishe yarabudar' naamak ek patrika bhi chalaai, jiskaa sampadan ve san 1880 se lekar mrityu paryat karte rahe.
