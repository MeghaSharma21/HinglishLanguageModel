 gursharan corਗੁਰਸ਼ਰਨ ਕੌਰ
 Gursharan Kaur Singh
 gursharan cor bharat ke purv pradhanmantri manmohan singh kii patni hai. gursharan kaa janm sardaar chhattar singh kohli, or sardarani bhagwanti cor ke ghar san 1937 main jalandhar main hua tha. inke pita sardaar chhattar singh kohli burma shail main ek karmchaaree the. inaki praathmik shiksha guru nanak kanya paathshaala main hui iske baad inhone patiala ke sarkari mahila college se or iske baad khalsa college amritsar se degree praapt kii. gursharan cor kaa vivaah dow manmohan singh se 1958 main amritsar main hua. dilli ke sikh samudaay main gursharan cor ko kirthan karne ke liye janaa jaataa he. sardaar manmohan singh ko jab vishwa bank kii ek naukari ke silsile main america janaa padaa to yah unke saath gayin par isse pehle inhone ol india radio par kai baar kirthan or gane gaaye the.
 inaki teen putriyaan hai- upindar singh, damandeep cor or amrit singh. tino hi putriyaan apane apane kshetra main kaaphi safal hai.
