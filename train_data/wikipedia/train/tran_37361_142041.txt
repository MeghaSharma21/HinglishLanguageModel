  raas mahotsav bharatvarsh main vibhinn vibhinn kshetron main vibhinn vibhinn sthanon par lagane vaala mela/utsav he. yah utsav mukhyat mathura-vrindavan kshetra main lagata he. yah mahotsav braj samskruti kaa ang he. haalanki yah samskruti braj kshetra ke baahar tak faili hui he is liye is meley kaa aayojan desh ke alag alag rajyon main hota he. raas kaa mela kai sthanon par ek divasiy to kai sthanon par do divasiy hota he. paigaanv main do divasiy raas mahotsav kaa aayojan prativarsh hota he. manyata he jahaan jahaan bhagwaan shrikrishna ne raas rachaae vahaan vahaan raas mahotsav kaa aayojan hota he.
