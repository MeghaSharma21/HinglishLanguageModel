 durga vahini vishwa hindu parishad kii mahila shakha hai. isaki sthapana 1991 main sadhvi ritambhra ne kiya. vihip ke anusaar durga vahini kaa uddeshy dharmik or saanskrithik kaaryo ke liye mahilao ko prerit karnaa or mahilao ko shaareerik, maansik rup se prabal bananaa. 2002 main durga vahini ke 8,000 sadsy the, jiname se 1,000 sirf ahmedabad ke the.
