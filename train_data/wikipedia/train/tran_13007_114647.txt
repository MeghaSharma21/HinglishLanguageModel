 lakhjohar main shri adorji maharaj apane 18 pariwaron sahit samarpit hue the. lily jhaadiya ko katkar ek shaiya banavaakar sabhi jan oos par baithkar aag lagaakar bhagwaan kii sharan shidhaar gaen the. kehete hai ki aag mantro se lagaai gai thi or lakh kii tarah leeli jhaadiyaan jalane lagi thi. bachche, auraten, jawan, bude sab log, taqariban 150 jan is lakhjohar main samarpit hue the. bhatund gaanv ke mukhiya the.
