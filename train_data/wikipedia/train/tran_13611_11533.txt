 roopim bhashaa ucchar kii laghuttam arthvaan ikai he. roopim swanimon kaa aisa nunatam anukram he jo vyakaranik drushti se sarthak hota he. swanim ke baad roopim bhashaa kaa mahatvapurn tatv va ang he. roopim ko 'rupagram' or 'padgram' bhi kehete hai. jis prakaar swan-prakriya kii aadharbhoot ikai swanim he, usee prakaar rup-prakriya kii aadharbhoot ikai roopim he. roopim vaakya-rachana or arth-abhivyakti kii sahaayak ikai he. swanim bhashaa kii arthhin ikai he, kintu isamen arthbhedak kshamta hoti he. roopim laghutam arthvaan ikai he, kintu roopim ko arthim kaa paryaay nahin maan sakate hai; yatha-parameshwar ek arthim he, jabaki isamen ‘param’ or ‘ishwar’ do roopim hai.
 paribhaasha - vibhinn bhashaa vaigyanikon ne roopim ko bhinn-bhinn roopon main paribhaashit kiya he. kuch pramukh vidwaanon kii paribhaashaayen drashtavy hai-
 dow udaynarayan tiwari ne roopim kii paribhaasha is prakaar di he, 
 dow saryuprasad agarwal ke anusaar, 
 dow bholanath tiwari ke matanusaar,
 dow jagadev singh ne likhaa he, 
 blaak kaa roopim ke vishay main vichaar he- 
 glison kaa vichaar he- 
 aar. ech. robins ne vyakaranik sandarbh main roopim ko is prakaar paribhaashit kiya he- 
 swaroop- roopim ke swaroop ko uski arth-bhedak sanrachana ke aadhaar par nirdhaarit kar sakate hai. prayek bhashaa main roopim vyavastha uski arth-pravati ke aadhaar par hoti he. isliye bhinn-bhinn bhashaaon ke roopimon main bhinnata hona swabhaavik he.
