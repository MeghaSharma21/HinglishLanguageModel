  raajnneeti praveshdwar
 pakistan ke samvidhan kaa ikkisvin sanshodhan donon pakistan kii neshnal assembly or pakistan ke seenet dwaara paarit kiya gaya tha 6 janvari 2015 ko. isane 7 janvari 2015 ko raashtrapati kii sweekruti praapt kiya. is vidheyak ne anuchhed 175 or samvidhan kii pehali anusuchee main sanshodhan kiya tha. yah ek aatm nihit khand he, jo sanshodhan 7 janvari, 2017 ko samaapt ho karne kaa kaaran banataa he. 
 sanshodhan kii sthapana kii twarit sunwaayee sainya adaalaton ke liye - aatankwaadi aparaadhon, pakistan ke khilaaf yuddh chhedane or pakistan kii suraksha kii dhamkee kaary karata he. in adaalaton kii avadhi do varsh he. samvidhan main sanshodhan karne kaa nirnay 2014 peshawar skool narsanhaar ke baad aayaa he.
