 kaashi bhagirathi ganga ke tat par ek adbhut nagari he. yahan ke sabhi ghat atyant hi manoram he. ganga tat par mir ghat adhik ullekhneey he, isi ghat par shakti peeth vishalakshi evam baba vishwanath mandir sthit he, jisaki sthapana swami karpatri ne kii thi. sandhya kaal main chandramaa kii dudhia roshni main ghat kaa nazaaraa dekhte hi banataa he, naavon par savaar hokar dashashwamedh ghat kii aarti kaa anand liya ja saktaa he.
