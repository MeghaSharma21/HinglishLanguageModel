 macier 81 ek chakree akashganga he jo ki saptarshi taramandal se 1,2 prakaash-saal se he. prithvi ke nazadik hone, iske bade aakaar or kriyasheel akashiya nabhik hone ke kaaran isapar kaafii khoj kii gai he. akashganga ke bade aakaar or chamkile hone ke kaaran akaash yatriyaan isko dekhne main kaafii ruchi hoti he.
 macier 81 kii khoj yohan ilrat bod ne 1774 main kii thi. isliye yah akashganga ko bod kii akashganga bhi kaha jaataa he. pyri maiken or charls macier ne is akashganga ko ek nayaa pahchaan dekar isko macier shrenee main soochibaddh kar diya.
