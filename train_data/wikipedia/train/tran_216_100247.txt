 kotrikodu kanyakumari jile main shahar kii ek tamilnadu ke bhartiya rajya main ek he. kothanallur maa gaanv bastiyon kothanallur, saral viley, iththa viley, mekka mandabam, chembaruththi viley, kotrikodu main gaanv kshetra he, muttaikadu, kumarpuram, manalikkaarai, puvankapirambu, maarankonam, munda viley, valikkalam padu, maruvur konen, atrukonaam or gaanv ke maa velimale bastiyon velimale, kulakadai, perunchilambu, karumbarai, pannipoththi .
 1999 main kotrikodu par lakki star doston club shuru kar diya .varsh 2001 kotrikodu lakki star sports club or 2004 kanyakumari jila sir main panjikrut club main badla hua naam club panjikaran ke tahat karyaalay register. ve garib logon ke adhyayan ke liye nots or kitaaben daan kar rahe hai. muft chikitsa shivi or saath hi pichley baarah varshon main samaaj seva.
