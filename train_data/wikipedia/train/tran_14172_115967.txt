 paath dekhen
 penicilayam ek saadhaaran faphund he. yah ek prakaar kaa kavak shrenee kaa mritjivi vanaspati he. ise neeli yi hari faphund bhi kaha jaataa he. yah sadi-gali subjiyon, kate hue falon, roti, sade hue maance, chamade aadi par ugta he. vishesh kar yah neembu ke upar bahut hi sahaj rup se ugta he. penicillium paudhe kaa sharir patale soote jaisi rachanaaon se bana hota he. in rachanaaon ko hyphi kehete hai. iske saare sharir ko micelium kehete hai. iskaa kavak jaal anek shakhaon main banta rahata he. isamen vikhandan dwaara vardhi prajnan hota he. penicium main spore naam konidia kii shrunkhlaa pai jaati he jiske dwaara yah alangik prajnan karata he. penicillium ke paudhe se penicillin naamak upakshaar praapt hota he. yah ek chamatkaari aushadhi he. iskaa vyavhaar tapedic tathaa anya vibhinn rogon main kiya jaataa he. 
 penicillin kaa avishkaar britain ke vaigyanik sar alexander fleming ne 1929 main kiya tha. is khoj ke liye 1954 main unhen chikitsa shaastra ke nobel puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. penicillin pehla aadhunik pratijaivik tha. penicillium kii kai jatiyaan paneer-vyavsaay main paneer taiyaar karne main vyavahrut hoti hai. carbonic amla ke sanshleshan main bhi iskaa upyog hota he. alcohol banane main bhi iskaa istemaal kiye jate hai. rang taiyaar karne main bhi iskaa upyog hota he.
