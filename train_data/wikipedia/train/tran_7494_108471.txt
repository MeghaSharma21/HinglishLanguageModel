 tiruvellore tattai krishnamacharisthiruvellore Thattai Krishnamachari
 
 tiruvellore thattai krishnamachari 1956 se 1958 tak evam 1964 se 1966 tak bharat ke vitt mantri the. ve NCAER kii pratham shaskeey sanstha ke sadsy the jo 1956 main sthapit bharat kii arthniti sambandhi pratham swatantra sanstha thi. ve swatantra bharat ke pratham mantri the jise bhrashtachar main lipt hone ke kaaran pad tyaagna padaa tha. ve samvidhan nirmaatri samiti ke sadsy bhi the.
