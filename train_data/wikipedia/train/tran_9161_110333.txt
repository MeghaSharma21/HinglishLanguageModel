 gogaanv bhartiya rajya chhattisgarh ke raipur jile main ek janaganana nagar he.
 varsh 2001 ke janaganana aankadon ke anusaar gogaanv kii kul jansankhya 10,453 thi, jisamen purush 53% or 47% jansankhya mahilaon kii thi. nagar main sakshartaa dar 56% jo rashtriya ausat 59.5% se kam he. yahan purush sakshartaa 67%, or mahila sakshartaa 43% darj kii gayi.
