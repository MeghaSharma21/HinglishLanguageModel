 atyant vishaal str vaali pariyojnaon kaa karyanyavan macroijiniyari kehalaata he. vishaal aakaar kii aisi pariyojnaon ko 'mahapariyojnaayen' kehete hai. mahapariyojnaon ke karyanyavan main bahut saare visheshgyata vaale kshetron kaa talmail or samanvay karnaa padataa he. inamen na keval ejeeniyar, balki vakil, udyogpati, sainik or rajaneta aadi bhi sammilit hote hai. mahapariyojnaayen praayah antararashtriya hoti hai kyonki ek hi desh ke paas inako chalaane ke liye aavashyak saamaajik, vittiya evam bhautik saadhan jutanaa atyant kathin hota he.
