 gatchiroli jila, bharat ke rajya maharashtra kaa ek prashasnic jila he. gatchiroli shahar jile kaa prashasnic mukhyalay he. gatchiroli jile kaa gathan 26 agast 1982 ko chandrapur jile se gatchiroli or sironcha tahseelon ko alag karke kiya gaya tha.
 gatchiroli jila maharashtra ke dakshinpurvi kone main sthit he. yah pashchim main chandrapur jila, uttar main gondia jila, purv main chhattisgarh rajya or dakshin or dakshin pashchim main andhra pradesh rajya se ghira he.
