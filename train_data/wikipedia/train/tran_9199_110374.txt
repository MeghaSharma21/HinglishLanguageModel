 deep joshi bharat ke ek saamaajik kaaryakarta hai jinhen san 2009 kaa raman magsese puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya he.
 deep joshi "profeshnal assistance for deveopment ection" naamak ashaaskeey sanstha ke sah-sansthaapak hai. philhal ve gramin garibon ke liye karyrat tathaa swa sahaayta samoohon ko aage badhaane main jute enjio ke swatantra salaahkaar hai. ve mesachusets institute of technology tathaa slone skool, emiti se management main master degree hasil kar chuke hai. unhone systams research institute or ford foundation ke saath kaam kiya he tathaa gramin vikaas va aajeevika protsaahan main kareeb 30 varshe kaa anubhav he. ve sarkaar ko garibi unmoolan ranneeti main bhi salaah dete hai.
 pradaan kii sthapana 1983 main dilli main hui thi. is enjio se jude peshewar sadasyon ke dal desh ke 3,044 gaanvon main 1,12,000 se adhik pariwaron ke bich karyrat hai. pradaan kaa copererat karyaalay dilli main sthit he. iske dalon ke karyaalay- madhyapradesh, chhattisgarh, rajasthan, assam, bihar, jharkhand, urisa tathaa pashchim bengal main hai.
