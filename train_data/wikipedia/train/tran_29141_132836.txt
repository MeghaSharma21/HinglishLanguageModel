 jvalansheeltaa yaa prajvalansheeltaa, kisi padaarth kaa vah gun he jiske anusaar vah padaarth kitni aasaani se jal yaa sulag kar aag athwa dahan utpann kar saktaa he. kisi vastu ke dahan hetu aavashyak kathinaayee kii maatra ko agni pareekshan dwaara mapa jaataa he. antarrashtriya str par, jvalansheeltaa ke maapan ke liye vibhinn prakaar kii pareekshan vidhiyaan maujuud hai. in pareekshanon se praapt moolyankanon ko bhawan nirmaan samhita, beemaa aavashyaktaaon, agni samhita ke atirikt un niyamon or viniyamon main prayog kiya jaataa he jo kisi bhawan nirmaan saamagree ke upyog or bhandaaran ke saath saath kisi atyant jvalansheel padaarth kii kisi imaarat ke andar yaa baahar sambhaal or uske sadak athwa vaayu parivahan ko nirdhaarit karte hai.
 jvalansheel taral wo taral padaarth hote hai jo bhadak kar aag lagane yaa visphot karne main sakasham hote hai, jiske kaaran yah maanav jivan ke liye khatra sabit ho sakate hai. in taralon ke udhaaharan hai, petrol, deazal, mitti kaa tel aadi.
 suraksha ke aadhaar par in padaarthon ko inke is gun ke liye chinhit kiya janaa nitant aavashyak he saath hi inkaa parivahan bhi bahut saavdhaanee se kiya janaa chahie.
