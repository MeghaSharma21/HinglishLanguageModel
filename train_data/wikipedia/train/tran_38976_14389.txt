 radha regent hotel jisase purv me radha park in ke naam se janaa jaataa tha yah chennai ke arumbakkam sthit ek five star hotel he. yah sarovar hotels evam resorts samooh kaa doosraa hotel inner ring road par khulne vaala pehla star hotel he. isi hotel me "jeffri" ne apana pehla pub chennai me 2001 me kholaa tha. 
 1997 me khole gayi is hotel me kul 91 kamare he jisame 62 superior kamare, 23 eczecutive kamare evam 6 suite hai. is hotel kii sabase badi visheshta yaha ke restaurants hai jisame har tareeke ke vyanjan parose jate he. ye saare restaurants vishwa ke kisi bhi restaurants ko chunauti pesh kar sakate he.
 is hotel ke restaurants me lobby kaph ek multikusine restaurant bhi he jo 1997 me kaph ke naam se kholaa gaya tha. da oriyant blossom jo 2004 me yaha par kholaa gaya tha yah kaafii prasiddh hotel hai jo purv ke lajiz khaano ke liye prasiddh he. jeffri pub evam ora yaha ke prasiddh aakarshan he.
 yaha par kul 6 banquet haal he jo kul 6500 varg fit kshetra me faile hue he. is hotel ne 2013 me garden banquet lon bhi shuru kiya tha. bauganviile ke naam se prasiddh is banquet haal kaa kshetrafal 20000 varg fit hai evam yaha par kul 3000 atithiyon ke baithne kii vyavastha he.
 is hotel kii aantarik saz sajja bhi kaafii aakarshak hai jo ramanan je ke dwaara 1997 me kii gayi thi. isaki vaastu sajja aapke man ko ek hi nazar me moh legi. iske alaava 2004 me sthapit oriyant blossom kii interior dizining prakaash mankad evam asosiats ke dwaara kii gayi thi.
 is hotel samooh ke do anya thri star hotel bhi bangalore me kholaa gaya tha. jisame ek radha hometel bhi tha jo 2005 whitefield me kholaa gaya evam doosraa hotel 2008 me electronic city me kholaa gaya tha. radha hometel sarovar hotel evam resorts ke dwaara pehla hometel brands tha.
