 olympia main jiyas kii murti pracheen vishwa ke saat aashcharyo main se ek he. is murti kaa nirmaan unani murtikar fidias ne isa se 432 saal pehle kiya tha. is murti ko yunaan ke olympia main sthit jiyas ke mandir main sthapit kiya gaya tha. is murti main jiyas ko baithe hui avasthaa main dikhaaya gaya he. murti kii unchai 12 meter thi.
