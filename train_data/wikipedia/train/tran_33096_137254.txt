 arXiv jise archive uccharit karte hai ganit, bhautikee, rasayaniki, khagoliki, sanganiki, maatraatmak jeevigyaan, sankhyikee or maatraatmak vitt ke shobon main vaigyanik lekhon kaa ek kosh he jise internet par khoja or padhaa ja saktaa he. san 1991 main isaki sthapana hui or yah tezi se badhane laga. vartmaan main bahut se vidwaan kisi nai khoj yaa soch par lekh likhane ke baad swayan hi use 'archive-kosh' par daal dete hai. aktubar 3, 2008 tak isamen 5 lakh se adhik lekh the. 2012 tak isamen har maheene 7,000 se adhik nae lekh jode ja rahe the.
