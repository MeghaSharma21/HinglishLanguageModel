 dand prakriya samhita, 1973 bharat main aapraadhik kanoon ke kriyanyavan ke liye mukhya kanoon he. yah san 1973 main paarit hua tathaa 1 april 1974 se lagu hua. 'siarpisi' dand prakriya samhita kaa sankshipt naam he. jab koi aparaadh kiya jaataa he to sadaiv do prakriyaayein hoti hai, jinhen police aparaadh kii jaanch karne main apanaati he. ek prakriya peedit ke sambandh main or duusari aaropee ke sambandh main hoti he. siarpisi main in prakriyaaon kaa byoraa diya gaya he. 'ipisi' bhartiya dand samhita kaa sankshipt naam he.
 kuch prakaar ke maanav vyavhaar aise hote hai jisaki kanoon ijaajat nahin detaa. aise vyavhaar karne par kisi vyakti ko unke parinaamon kaa saamana karnaa padataa he. kharaab vyavhaar ko aparaadh yaa gunaah kehete hai or iske parinaam ko dand kaha jaataa he. jin vyavhaaron ko aparaadh mana jaataa he unke bare main or har aparaadh se sambandhit dand ke bare main byoraa mukhyatya ipisi main diya gaya he.
 jab koi aparaadh kiya jaataa he, to sadaiv do prakriyaayein hoti hai, jinhen police aparaadh kii jaanch karne main apanaati he. ek prakriya peedit ke sambandh main or duusari aaropee ke sambandh main hoti he. siarpisi main in donon prakaar kii prakriyaaon kaa byoraa diya gaya he.dand prakriya samhita ke dwaara hi apradhi ko dand diya jaataa he !
