 pratyaksh pareekshan jo he jab kisi ek gavaah se side poochtacha kii jaati he jo use adaalat me bhulaayaa jaataa he aazamaaish kii liye. pratyaksh pareekshan aamtaur par saboot prakaash main laana karne ke liye kiya jaataa he. tathyon jo ek party kaa daawa yaa raksha kaa ek aavashyak tatv ko puraa karegaa ke samarthan main.pratyaksh pareekshan main, aam taur par pramukh sawaal poochne se jate he.is niyam ke apwaad tab hota he, to ek paksh ke ek gavaah kaa aahvaan kiya he, lekin yah bhi samajha jaataa he, yaa spasht ho jaataa he, ki gavaah vivaad kaa prashnkarta ke paksh main karne ke liye pratikul he.
 pratyaksh pariksha kii takanic pareekshan vakaalat par paathyakramon main sikhaaya jaataa he.prayek pratyaksh pariksha yaa to ek vishay he or siddhant ke madhyam se samgr maamle kii ranneeti yaa adhik unnat ranneetiyon, prayaas kii ek line ke saath, ke saath ekikrut he.
 yah dhara 4 me aata he.
