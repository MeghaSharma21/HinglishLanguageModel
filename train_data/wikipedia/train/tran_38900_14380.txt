 udai bharat kii vidyut vitaran karne vaali companiyon ke aarthik purutthan ke liye shuru kii gayi bharat sarkaar kii ek yojana he. yah 5 navambar 2015 ko armbh hui thi jiske ke tahat rajya evam centre shasit pradesho kii ghate main chal rahi vidhut vitaran companiyon ko ghate se ubaarane evam unki weetiya sthiti ko sudrava karne kaa kaary kiya ja rahaa he. isi yojana ke kaaran hi rajasthan kii vidhut vitaran companiyon ke ghate main kaaphi had tak sudhaar hua he in companiyon kaa ghaataa 27.3 pratishat se ghatakar vartmaan main 23.6 pratishat ho gaya he.
