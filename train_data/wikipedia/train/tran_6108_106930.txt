 rose dweep italy kii jal seemaa se 500 meter bahaar ek samudri platform pe bana ek lahurashtra he. yah italy ke shahar remini ke tat se 11 kilometre dur tha.ise ek itliyaan engeenior giorgio roza ne banaaya tha.yahan kii sarkaar ganatantric thi. iskaa kul chhetra fal keval 400 varg meter tha.adhikarik naam tha- 'rose dweep ganraajya'raashtra bhashaa aspiranto thi.yahan kaa apana ek raashtrapati, mudra athwa radio stesan bhi tha!isi aadhaar par isane 24 june 1968 main azaadi kaa elaan kiya. parantu tatkaaleen italy sarkaar ne ise shaq ke nazariye se dekha or dweep ke kabza kar use visphotako se uda diya. rose dweep kii sarkaar ne italy ke tatkaaleen raashtrapati giuceppe saraagat ko taar sandesh bheja. parantu ispe koi dhyaan nahin diya gaya.
