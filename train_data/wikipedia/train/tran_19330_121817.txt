 bharat main solahavin lok sabha ke liye aam chunaav 7 april se 12 may 2014 tak 9 charnon main hue. matganana 16 may ko hui. iske liye bharat kii sabhi sansadeey kshetron main vot daale gayi. vartmaan main pandrahavi lok sabha kaa kaarykaal 31 may 2014 ko khatm ho rahaa he. ye chunaav ab tak ke itihaas main sabase lanbaa kaarykram vaala chunaav tha. yah pehali baar hoga, jab desh main 9 charnon main loksabhaa chunaav hue. nirvachan ayog ke anusaar 81.45 karod matadaata apane matadhikaar kaa prayog karenge.
 sabhi nau charnon main ausat matdaan 66.38% ke aasapaas rahaa jo bhartiya aam chunaav ke itihaas main sabase ucchtam he. chunaav ke parinaam 16 may ko ghoshit kiye gayi. 336 siton ke saath rashtriya janatantric gathbandhan sabase bada dal or 282 siton ke saath bhartiya janata party sabase badi party banakar ubhari. sanyukt pragatisheel gathbandhan ne 59 siton par or congress ne 44 siton par jeet hasil kii.
 bijepi ne keval 31.0% vot jite, jo aazaadee ke baad se bharat main bahumat vaali sarkaar banane ke liye party kaa sabase kam hissaa he, jabaki rashtriya janatantric gathbandhan kaa sanyukt vot hissaa 38.5% tha. 1984 ke aam chunaav ke baad bijepi or uske sahyogiyon ne sabase badi bahumat vaali sarkaar banane kaa adhikaar jeeta, or yah chunaav pehali baar hua jab party ne anya partiyon ke samarthan ke bina shashan karne ke liye paryaapt siten jiti hai. aam chunaav main congress party kii sabase kharaab haar thi. bharat main aadhikarik vipakshi dal banane ke liye, ek party ko loksabhaa main 10% siten hasil kerani hongi; haalanki, bhartiya rashtriya congress is number ko hasil karne main asamarth thi. is tathy ke kaaran, bharat ek aadhikarik vipakshi party ke bina bana hua he.
 sanvaidhanik aavashyaktaa se, lok sabha ke chunaav har paanch saal kii avadhi par yojit kiye jane chahie. 15 veen loksabhaa ke gathan ke liye pichhlaa chunaav april se may 2009 main yojit kiya gaya tha. 15 veen loksabhaa kii avadhi 31 may 2014 ko swaabhaavik rup se samaapt ho gayi. chunaav kaa aayojan bharat nirvachan ayog dwaara kiya jaataa he. bade chunavi aadhaar or suraksha kaarnon ko sanbhaalane ke liye chunaav kai charnon main yojit kiye jate hai.
 2009 main pichley aam chunaav ke baad se, anna hajaare, aravind kejariwal or baba ramdev dwaara bhartiya bhrashtachar virdohi andolan ne gati or raajnneetik hit praapt kiye hai. bhaajapa bhi vibhin vidhaan sabha chunaavon main bahumat jitkar aam chunaav ke liye ashanvit he. goa chunaav main bhaajapa ko bahumat praapt hua or punjab main satta virdohi lahar kii ek parampara ke baavjood jeet hasil kii. haalanki, bhaajapa uttarakhand, himachal pradesh or karnataka ke dakshini garh main satta kho di.
 disambar 2013 main yojit hue chaaron vidhaan sabha chunaavon main bhaajapa ne jeet praapat kii. bhaajapa ne dilli main sabase bade dal ke rup main ubhari. congress ko chunaavon haraa kar bhaajapa ne rajasthan main do-tihaai se zyada seat praapt kii. madhya pradesh or chhattisgarh main bhaajapa ne teesri baar sarkaar banaai.
 prati ummidavaar chunaav kharch kii seemaa ko badhane ke prastaav ko bhartiya mantrimandal ne manjuuri di. bade rajyon main yah seemaa 40 lakh se badhaakar 70 lakh or chote rajyon or dilli ko chhodkar sabhi centre prashasit kshetron main seemaa badhaakar 54 lakh kar di gayi.
 5 march 2014 ko mukhya nirvachan ayukta ve es sampath ne chunaav kaarykram kii tarikhon or taiyaariyon kaa elaan kiya. kul 9 charnon main mat daale jaayenge. 7 april ko pehle, 9 april ko duusare, 10 april ko tisare, 12 april ko chauthe, 17 april ko paanchaven, 24 april ko chathe, 30 april ko saatven, 7 may ko aathven, 12 may ko nauven charan kaa matdaan hoga.

 bharat main 'bhrashtachar' bade paimaane par he. bharat transparency international ke bhrashtachar dharanaen suchakank main 179 deshon main se 95 wein sthaan par he. lekin bharat ke score main lagaataar sudhaar hua he jo 2002 main 2.7 se 2011 main 3.1 ho gaya. aitihaasik rup se, bhrashtachar, bhartiya raajnneeti or naukarshahi kaa ek vyapak pahalu kii bhoomika main he.
 bharat main bhrashtachar ghoose, kar apvanchan or gaban, aadi ke rup main upasthit he. 2009 main pichley bhartiya aam chunaav ke baad se 2011 bhartiya bhrashtachar virdohi andolan or anna hajaare or baba ramdev dwaara anya isi tarah ke andolanon ke dwaara bhrashtachar rokane ke prayaas hue hai. bhrashtachar virdohi andolan ke kaaryakarta anna hajaare dwaara jantar mantar,nai dilli main shuru kii gayi bhukh hadtaal main bhrashtachar ko kam karne ke liye vidhaayi uddeshy ke saath agast 2011 main bharat sarkaar ke madhyam se jan lokapal vidheyak ko paarit karne kii shuruyaat kii gayi. ramdev ke netritv main ek anya uddeshy se swiss or anya videshi bainkon se kaala dhan ke prayaavartan ke liye andolan kiye gaye.

 is charan main kul 64 siton par matdaan hua. chunaav ayog ke anusaar pashchim bengal main 80.51% matdaan darj kiya gaya. andhra pradesh ke simandhra kshetra kii 25 siton par 76%, uttar pradesh kii 15 siton par 55.52%, bihar kii saat siton par 58%, jammu or kashmir kii do siton par 50% matdaan hua. uttarakhand kii sabhi paanch siton par 62% or himachal pradesh kii sabhi chaar siton par 65% matdaan hua. aathven charan ke saath hi 16veen loksabhaa kii 543 main se 502 siton ke liye yani 92 fisadi matdaan sampann ho gaya he.
 is charan main kul 41 siton par matdaan hua. chunaav ayog ke anusaar uttar pradesh kii 18 siton par ausatan 54.21 fisad matdaan hua. pashchim bengal kii 17 siton par 79.96 fisadi matdaan hua. bihar main loksabhaa chunaav ke chathe or antim charan ke tahat chah sansadeey siton ke liye aaj sampann matdaan ke dauraan 56.67 fisadi matadaataaon ne apane matadhikaar kaa upyog kiya, jo 2009 ke loksabhaa chunaav kii tulana main kareeb 12 pratishat adhik rahaa. iske saath hi matdaan ke sabhi charan samaapt ho gayi. is baar chunaav ke sabhi nau charnon main kul milakar 66.38 fisadi matdaan hua, jo loksabhaa chunaavon main ab tak kaa sarvaadhik matdaan he. pichhlaa sarvaadhik matdaan 1984 main darj kiya gaya tha jab 64.01 pratishat matadaataaon ne apane matadhikaar kaa istemaal kiya tha. 2009 ke aam chunaav main 58.19 fisadi vot pade the.
