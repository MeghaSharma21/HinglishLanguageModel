 
 kaner kaa phool bahut hi mashhoor he. kaner ke ped kii oonchai lagbhag 10 se 11 haath se zyada bade nahin hote hai. pattey lambaai main 4 se 6 inch or chaudai main 1 inch, sire se nokdaar, neeche se khurdare, safed ghatidaar or upar se chikne hote he. kaner ke ped van or upavan main aasaani se mil jate he. phool khaskar garmiyon ke mausam main hi khilte hai faliyaan chapati, golakaar 5 se 6 inch lambii hoti he jo bahut hi jahrili hoti hai. phulon or jadon main bhi jehar hota he. kaner kii chaar jatiyaan hoti hai. safed, laal va gulabi or pilaa. safed kaner aushadhi ke upyog main bahut aata he. kaner ke ped ko kuredne yaa todane se doodh nikalataa he.
