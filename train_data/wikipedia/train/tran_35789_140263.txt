 kantki, aadhikarik taur par kantki rashtramandal, sanyukt rajya ke purvi dakshin-madhya kshetra main sthit ek rajya he. kantki chaar america rajyon main se ek he jise rashtramandal ke rup main gathit kiya gaya he . mul rup se kantki varjeenia kaa hissaa tha. 1792 main ise 15wein rajya ke rup main sangh main shaamil kiya gaya. kantki america kaa kshetra main 37waan or aabaadi main 26waan sabase sabase bada rajya he. 
 2016 main kantki kii jansankhya 44,36,974 anumaanit kii gai he. angrezi adhikarik bhashaa he jise 96% nivasee maatrubhaasha ke rup main bolte hai. lagbhag aadhi janata apane ko naastik maanati hai, 42% protestant or 8% catholic. franccort rajdhani he. 2010 main iskaa sakal rajya utpaada 163.3 arab dollar tha, jisase iskaa desh main 28waan sthaan rahaa. isaki prati vyakti vyaktigat aay 28,513 america dollar thi, jo ise desh main 43waan sthaan detii he. 2014 main, kantki ko you.es main rahane ke liye sabase sasta rajya paaya gaya.
