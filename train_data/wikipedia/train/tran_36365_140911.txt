 camerun ek pramukh jwalamukhi parvat hai.
 camerun pashchimi africa ke camerun pradesh ke uttar or uttarpashchimi chor par kataavpurn visham dharatleey chaap kii tarah faili parvatashrenee ke pashchimi chor par sthit he. iskaa padtaleey aadhaar lagbhag 1800 varg kilometre he. iske do pramukh shikhar hai - bada camerun, jisamen anek jwalamukhi vivar hai or chota camerun, jisaki dhaal sarvatha vanachhadit he. yah parvatakshetra sansar ke sarvaadhik varshavale kshetron main he. parvat ke thik dakshin 30 kimi chaudee camerun ischuary yaa khadi he jisamen mungon tathaa buree nadiyaan bahati hai.
