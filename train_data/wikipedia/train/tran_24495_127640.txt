 finland, aadhikarik taur par finland ganraajya uttari europe ke phenoscanadian kshetra main sthit ek nordic desh he. isaki seemaa pashchim main sweden, purv main rus or uttar main norge sthit he, jabaki finland khadi ke paar dakshin main esthonia sthit he. desh kii rajdhani helsinki he. 
 lagbhag 53 lakh kii aabaadi vaale is desh ke zyaadaatar log dakshini kshetra main rahete hai. kshetrafal ke hisaab se yah europe kaa aathvaan sabase bada or janaghanatv ke aadhaar par europea sangh main sabase kam aabaadi vaala desh hai. desh main rahane vaale bahusankhyak logon kii maatrubhaasha finish he, vahiin desh kii 5.5 pratishat aabaadi kii maatrubhaasha swedish he. 
 finland aitihaasik rup se sweden kaa ek hissaa tha or 1809 se roosi samrajya ke antargat ek swytt grand dachi tha. rus se grihayuddh ke baad 1917 main finland ne swatantraaa kii ghoshnaa kii. finland 1955 main sanyukt raashtra sangh main, 1969 main oisidi or 1995 main europea sangh or eurozon main shaamil hua. ek surveykshan main saamaajik, raajnneetik, aarthik or sainya sanketakon ke aadhaar par finland ko duniya kaa doosraa sabase adhik sthir desh qaraar diya gaya he. 
 yahan kaa mausam bahat hi suhaavna or manamohak he. garmiyon ke samay raat baarah baje ke baad kuch andhera hota he iske pehle das baje ke aas-paas to aisa lagata he ki jaise abhi-abhi shaam hui hai. jabaki thand ke vakt din main adhikansh andhera hota he dopaher main kuch samay ke liye suraj dev ke darshan ho pate he.
