 amino amla, ve anu hai jinamein amine tathaa carboxil donon hi group paaen jate hai. inkaa saadhaaran sutra H2NCHROOH he. isamen R ek parshv kadi he. jo parivartansheel vibhinn anuon kaa group hota he. carboxil tathaa amine group carbon parmaanu se laga rahata he. amino amla probhujin ke gathankarta anu hai. bahut saare amino amla peptid bandhan dwaara yukt hokar probhujin banaate hai. probhujin banane main 20 amino amla bhaag lete hai.
 yah probhujin nirmaan ke karnadhaar hote hai. prakruti main lagbhag bees ameenon amlon kaa astitv he. probhujin anuon main sainkadon yaa hajaaron amino amla ek duusare se jude rahete hai. prayek probhujin main praayah sabhi amino amla ek vishesh anukram se jude rahete hai. vibhinn amino amlon kaa yahi anukram prayek probhujin ko uski visheshtaayen pradaan karata he. amino amlon kaa yahi vishisht anukram di en e ke neuclotids ke kram se nirdhaarit hota he.
