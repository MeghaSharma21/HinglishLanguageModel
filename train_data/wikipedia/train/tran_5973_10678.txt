 sudhindra chandra dasgupta tripura se bhartiya janata party ke bhartiya raajnneetijna the. wo tripura main bhaajapa adhyaksh bhi rahe hai.
 unhone chunaav 2014 ke loksabhaa chunaav se tripura pashchim lok sabha nirvachan kshetra se bhaajapa/rajg ke ummidavaar ke rup main chunaav ladaa. 
 12 janvari 2017 ko 79 varsh kii aayu main unka nidhan ho gaya.
