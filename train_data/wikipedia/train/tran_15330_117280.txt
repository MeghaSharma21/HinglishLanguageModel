 china 3 lichi fal kaa ek prakaar hai. vaigyanikon ne lichi kii vibhinn kismon kaa vikaas kiya he, is sabase acchhee kismon dakshin asia main lichi he. yah saamanytah bharat, bangladesh, pakistan, taiwan, vietnam paayi jaati he. iskaa madhyam oonchai kaa sadabahaar ped hota he, jo ki 5-7 meter tak hota he, olternet pinet pattiyaan, lagbhag 15-25 sein.me. lambi hoti hai. ped prati 124 kilo kii ausat upaj. haalanki, har saal ek niyamit aadhaar par, yaa fal sahan nahin karata he. lichi fal kii kismon kaaphi badi or 25-30 gram ke ek ausat vajan ke saath daur he. tses -18%. bhojan or beej 15:1 ke paatr anupaat.
