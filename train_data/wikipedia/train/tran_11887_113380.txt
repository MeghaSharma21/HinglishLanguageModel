 pandrahaven asian khel 1 disambar se 15 disambar, 2006 ke bich qatar kii rajdhani doha main yojit kiye gaye the. in khelon kaa aayojan karne vaala doha is kshetra kaa pehla or puure pashchim asia main doosraa mahanagar tha. isse pehle tehran main 1974 main asian khelon kaa aayojan kiya gaya tha. 
 yahi ve asian khel bhi the, jisamen asian olympic parishad ke sabhi 45 sadsy rashtron ne bhagidaari kii. urosport dwaara in khelon kaa pehali baar europe main prasaaran kiya gaya. 
 is khelon main dakshin korian ghudsavaar kim hyung kii mrityu kii apriy ghatana bhi hui jab ve ek ghudsawaari kii pratispardhaa ke dauraan hi maare gaye.
 12 navambar 2000 ko 2006 ke khelon ke liye busaan, dakshin korea main matdaan kiya gaya. is matdaan main asian olympic parishad ke 41 sadasyon ko sammilit kiya or matdaan ke teen daur hue jisamen prayek daur main ek boli lagane vaale nagaron ko bahaar kiya gaya. pehle hi daur ke baad, nai dilli, keval do maton ke saath bahaar ho gai. teen shesh pratyaashee nagar agle daur main pahunche or antat doha ko chunaa gaya.
 mashaal riley 8 oktober 2006 ko doha ke "flem of hospitality" golf club se armbh hua or 12 asian deshon se hota hua 55 din baad punah doha pahunchaa. riley maarg kii kul lambaai 50,000 kimi thi jo asian khelon ke itihaas main ab tak kii sabase adhik thi. mashaal rele nimnlikhit nagaron or rashtron se hokar gujara :-
