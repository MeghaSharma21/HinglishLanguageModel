 lahartakhta yaa sarfaboard us fatti ko kehete hai jiskaa prayog laharbaazi ke khel main samudr kii laharon par sawari karne ke liye kiya jaataa he. lahartate halake hote hai taaki laharbaaz unhen aasaani se uthaa saken or agar sawari karte hue ve gir jaaen to takhte kii thokar se adhik chot na lage. is halkepan ke baavjood ve majboot bhi hote hai taaki savaar hue laharbaaz kaa bhaar uthaa saken.
 laharbaazi kaa avishkaar hawaii main hua tha, lekin vahaan ke adivasee koa jaise sthaneey pedo kii lakdi se lahartate banaate the. hawaiyai bhashaa main in takhton ko papa hee naalu bulaayaa jaaya tha. yah 15 foot se bhi adhik lambe or bahut hi baari hote the. jab se laharbaazi duniya bhar main lokapriya hui he lahartakhton main bahut sudhaar hua he. ab inkaa andruni bhaag poliurithene yaa polistyrine kaa bana hota he jo ek prakaar kaa bahut hi halkaa plastic he. iske upar fibarglass, kapade or policster kii paraten hoti hai jinhen epoxi ghaney kii gond se joda hota he. epoxi ek dafaa suukh jaae to bahut hi sakht hota he or pani ke liye bilkul band ho jaataa he. aise fatti atyant halake, sakht or majboot hote hai. sawari karte hue laharbaazon ko adhik niyantran dene ke liye aadhunik lahartakhton ke neeche fin bhi lage hote hai.
