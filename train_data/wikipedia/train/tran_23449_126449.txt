 dulhery naamak vyakti ne sarvapratham kachwah vansh kii sthapana kii, 1137 i. badgujaron ko haraakar dulhery ne dhundhar rajya ko basaaya tha dulhery ne sarvapratham dausa ko apni rajdhani banaaya, jo is rajya kii sabase pracheen rajdhani thi, dulhery ne is rajdhani ko meenaon se praapt kiya tha |
 dulhery ne ramgarh naamak sthaan par jamuvamata ke mandir kaa nirmaan karaayaa tathaa ' jamuvaa mata ' ko kachwah vansh kii kuladevi ke rup main sthapit kiya tha, dhundhar main pracheen ramgarh gulab kii kheti ke liye prasiddh tha, jiske kaaran ramgarh ko ' dhudhand kaa pushkar ' kaha gaya |
 dulhery ne ramgarh ko jitkar ise rajdhani banaaya is prakaar dulhery ke shashan kaal kii do rajdhaniyaan astitv main aayin, dulhery ke pashchaat kokildev ne amer ke meenaon ko parajeet kar is sampann bhu-bhaag ko kachwah vansh kaa ek ang banaaya, baad main kokildev ne amer durg ko rajdhani ke rup main sthapit kiya |
