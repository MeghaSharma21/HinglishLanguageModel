  rahatgarh jalprapaat bharat ke madhya pradesh pradesh me sthit ek jalprapaat he. 
 sagar-bhopal maarg par kareeb 40 kimi dur sthit rahatgarh kasbaa waterfol ke kaaran ab ek behad lokapriy picnic spot he. pracheen kaal main yah apane kanguredaar durg, pracheer dwaaron, mahal or mandiron-masjidon ke liye prasiddh tha. kalantar main sab nasht hota chalaa gaya or ab yahan durg ke sirf avshesh bache hai. 
 binaa nadi ke unche kinare par sthit rahatgarh kasbaa puravasheshon ke anusaar gyarahavin shatabdi main parmaron ke shasankaal main bahut acchhee sthiti main tha. kasbe se kareeb 3 kimi dur sthit kile kii bahari deevaaron main kabhi badi-badi 26 minaaren thim. bheetar pahunchne ke liye 5 bade daravaaje the. kalantar main yahaan hui ladaaiyon or dekharekh ke abhaav main rahatgarh kaa vaibhav ateet kii kaali gupha main dafan ho gaya. sagar ke sthaneey nivasee varsha-ritu main yahan chuttee ke din samay bitaane ke liye badi sankhya main jate hai. shahar ke aas-paas aise sthalon kaa abhaav hone ke kaaran yah picnic manaane kaa atyant lokapriy sthaan he.
