 madan laal daayen haath ke ballebaaj hai unhen sadaiv madhya kram main hi ballebaaji karne bheja gaya. iske atirikt ve madhyam gati ke tej gendbaaj bhi hai jo hameshaa daayen haath se hi gend phenkate hai. iske atirikt ve furtile kshetrarakshak bhi hai. kul milakar ve ek vishwasaneey alraundar rahe hai or kai maukon par unhone apane harfanmaulaa andaaz main khelte hue yah siddh karke bhi dikhaaya he.
