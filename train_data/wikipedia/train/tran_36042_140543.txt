 moulana aaazaad librari aligarh, bharat main aligarh muslim vishvavidhyalay kaa pustkaalay he. is kendriya pustkaalay me 80 se adhik mahavidhyalay or vibhageeya pustkaalay hai. in pustakaalayon ko snatakottar or vyavsaayik paathyakramon ke chaatro kii jarurat he. yah asia kaa doosraa sabase bada pustkaalay bhi he. 
 kendriya pustkaalay 1875 main sthapit kiya gaya tha jab vishvavidhyalay madrasatul ulum naamak ek madrase ke rup main sthapit hua. 1877 main, mohamadan anglo-oriental college madrasa bana. robert rogers, bharat ke vysarai ne aadharshila rakhi or pustkaalay ko unke naam se litn librari ke rup main namit kiya gaya tha. kai prasiddh vidwaanon ne unke shikshan jimmedaariyon ke saath-saath maanad pustakadhyaksh ke rup main kaary kiya. 
 pustkaalay ko 1960 main moulana aaazaad pustkaalay se namit kiya gaya tha, jab pradhanmantri jawahar laal nehru ne isaki vartmaan imaarat kaa udghaatan kiya. 
 is imaarat main saat manzilen hai. yah maidan or udyanon kii zamin se ghira hua he. 4.75 acre  ====
