 haavda jankshan railway stesan haavda evam kolkata shahar kaa railway stesan he. yah hughali nadi ke daahine kinare par sthit he. iske 23 platform ise bharat ke sabase bade railway steshnon main ek banaate hai. 1853 main bharat main pehali rail gaadi mumbai se evam 1854 duusari haavda se chali.
 suprasiddh krantikari yogesh chandra chatarji kakori kand se purv hi haavda railway stesan par giraftaar kar liye gayi the. najarbandi kii haalat main hi inhen kakori kand ke mukademe main shaamil kiya gaya tha or aaajeevan karawas kii sajaa di gayi thi.
