 sanskrit in indonesia denmark ke vaigyanik evam sanskrit vidwaan prof dow jan gonda dwaara 1952 main rachit ek pustak he. yah pustak nayi dilli ke antararashtriya bhartiya samskruti academy dwaara prakashit kii gayi thi. yah pustak 1973 or 1998 main punarmudrit kii gayi.
 yah pustak vyutpattishastra tathaa indonesia kii bhashaaon main sanskrit se aaye shabdon ke upar ek maanak granth he. is pustak main 6 adhyay tathaa 2 parishisht hai. pratham adhyay main vishay kaa parichay diya gaya he tathaa indonesia kii bhashaaon main sanskrit se aaye shabdon ke bare main charchaa kii gayi he. duusare adhyay main sanskrit tathaa bhartiya lipiyon ke malay prayadweep tathaa madagascar main prasar kaa itihaas diya gaya he. tritiy adhyay main un kshetron kii charchaa he jinamein sanskrit shabd apanaaye gayi hai. shri gonda ne 11 shreniyon kii charchaa kii he. chauthe adhyay main sanskrit shabdon ke indonesin bhashaaon main parivartit roopon kii charchaa he. paanchaven adhyay main sanskrit shabdon main hue mahatvapurn parivaratanon kii charchaa he. chathe adhyay main sanskrit par indonesian bhashaaon ke shabdon ke prabhaav ke bare main he.
