 bharat kii antarim sarkaar 2 sitambar 1946 ko banaayi gayi thi. iskaa nirmaan navnirvaachit bhartiya samvidhan sabha se hua tha. iskaa kaary britani bharat ke swatantra bharat main sankraman ke kaary main sahyog karnaa tha. iskaa astitv 15 agast 1947 tak rahaa jab bharat kaa vibhaajan karte hue use swatantra ghoshit kiya gaya.
