 kalidas sanskrit bhashaa ke mahaan kavi or naatakkaar the. unhone bharat kii puranic kathaon or darshan ko aadhaar banaakar rachanaaen kii or unki rachanaaon main bhartiya jivan or darshan ke vividh rup or mul tatva nirupit hai. kalidas apni inhen visheshtaon ke kaaran raashtra kii samgr rashtriya chetana ko swar dene vaale kavi maane jate hai or kuch vidwaan unhen rashtriya kavi kaa sthaan tak dete hai.
 abhigyanshakuntalam kalidas kii sabase prasiddh rachana he. yah naatak kuch un bhartiya saahitiyik kritiyon main se he jinka sabase pehle europea bhashaaon main anuwaad hua tha. yah puure vishwa sahitya main agraganya rachana maani jaati he. meghdutam kalidas kii sarvshereshth rachana he jisamen kavi kii kalpanaashakti or abhivyanjanavadbhavabhivyanjana shakti apane sarvotkrisht str par he or prakruti ke maanvikaran kaa adbhut rakhndkavye se khandakavy main dikhataa he. 
 kalidas vaidarbhi reeti ke kavi hai or tadnurup ve apni alankaar yukt kintu saral or madhur bhashaa ke liye vishesh rup se jane jate hai. 
 unke prakruti varnan adwitiya hai or vishesh rup se apni upamaaon ke liye jane jate hai. sahitya main audaarya gun ke prati kalidas kaa vishesh prem he or unhone apane shringaar ras pradhan sahitya main bhi adarshavaadi parampara or naitik mulyon kaa samuchit dhyaan rakhaa he.
 kalidas ke parvarti kavi banbhatta ne unki sukhtiyon kii vishesh rup se prashansa kii he.
 kalidas kis kaal main hue or ve multah kis sthaan ke the isamen kaafii vivaad he. chunki, kalidas ne dwitiya shunga shaasha agnimitra ko nayak banaakar malvicagnimitram naatak likhaa or agnimitra ne 170 isapurv main shashan kiya tha, atah kalidas ke samay kii ek seemaa nirdhaarit ho jaati he ki ve isse pehle nahin hue ho sakate. chhathin sadi isvi main banbhatta ne apni rachana harshcharitam main kalidas kaa ullekh kiya he tathaa isi kaal ke pulakeshin dwitiya ke ehole abhilekh main kalidas kaa zikr he atah ve inke baad ke nahin ho sakate. is prakaar kalidas ke pratham shatabdi isa purv se chhathi shatabdi isvi ke madhya hona tay he. durbhagyavash is samay seemaa ke andar ve kab hue is par kaafii matbhed hai. vidwaanon main dwitiya shatabdi isa purv kaa mat pratham shatabdi isa purv kaa mat tritiy shatabdi isvi kaa mat chaturth shatabdi isvi kaa mat paanchavi shatabdi isvi kaa mat, tathaa chhathin shatabdi ke purvarddh kaa mat; prachlit the. inamen zyaadaatar khandit ho chuke hai yaa unhen maanane vaale ikke dukke log hai kintu mukhya sangharsh 'pratham shatabdi isa purv kaa mat or 'chaturth shatabdi isvi kaa mat' main he.
 pratham shatabdi isa purv kaa mat - 
 parampara ke anusaar kalidas ujjayini ke un raja vikramaditya ke samakaleen hai jinhone isa se 57 varsh purv vikram sanwat chalaaya. vicramorvasheey ke nayak pururava ke naam kaa vikram main parivartan se is tark ko bal miltaa he ki kalidas ujjayani ke raja vikramaditya ke rajdrabari kavi the. inhen vikramaditya ke navratnon main se ek mana jaataa he. 
 chaturth shatabdi isvi kaa mat -
 pro kith or anya itihaaskaar kalidas ko gupt shaasha chandragupta vikramaditya or unke uttaradhikari kumargupta se jodate hai, jinka shasankaal chauthi shatabdi main tha. aisa mana jaataa he ki chandragupta dwitiya ne vikramaditya kii oopaadhi lee or unke shasankaal ko swarnyug mana jaataa he. 
 vivaad or paksh-pratipaksh - 
 kalidas ke janmasthan ke bare main bhi vivaad he. meghdutam main ujjain ke prati unki vishesh prem ko dekhte hue kuch log unhen ujjain kaa nivasee maanate hai.
 sahityakaron ne ye bhi siddh karne kaa prayaas kiya he ki kalidas kaa janm uttarakhand ke rudraprayag jile ke kaviltha gaanv main hua tha. kalidas ne yaheen apni praarambhik shiksha grahan kii thi auki yaheen par unhone meghdoot, kumarasambhav auki raghuvansh jaise mahakavyon kii rachana kii thi. kaviltha chardhaam yatra maarg main guptkashi main sthit he. guptkashi se kalimath siddhapitha vaale raste main kalimath mandir se chaar kilometre aage kaviltha gaanv sthit he. kaviltha main sarkaar ne kalidas kii pratimaa sthapit kar ek sabhaagaar kii bhi nirmaan karavaaya he. jahaan par har saal june maah main teen dinon tak goshtee kaa aayojan hota he, jisamen deshbhar ke vidwaan bhaag lete hai. 
 kuch vidwaanon ne to unhen bengal or urisa kaa bhi sabit karne kii koshis kii he. kehete hai ki kalidas kii srilanka main hatya kar di gai thi lekin vidwaan ise bhi kapol-kalpit maanate hai.
 kathaon or kimvadantiyon ke anusaar kalidas shachlo-surat se sundar the or vikramaditya ke darbar ke navratnon main ek the. kaha jaataa he ki praarambhik jivan main kalidas anpadh or murkh the. 
 kalidas kaa vivaah vidyottama naam kii rajkumaari se hua. aisa kaha jaataa he ki vidyottama ne pratigya kii thi ki jo koi use shastrarth main haraa degaa, vah usee ke saath shaadi karegi. jab vidyottama ne shastrarth main sabhi vidwaanon ko haraa diya to apmaan se dukhi kuch vidwaanon ne kalidas se uskaa shastrarth karaayaa. vidyottama maun shabdaawalee main gudh prashn puuchhati thi, jise kalidas apni buddhi se maun sanketon se hi jawaab de dete the. vidyottama ko lagata tha ki kalidas gudh prashn kaa gudh jawaab de rahe hai. udhaaharan ke liye vidyottama ne prashn ke rup main khulaa haath dikhaaya to kalidas ko laga ki yah thappad maarane kii dhamkee de rahi he. uske jawaab main kalidas ne ghunsa dikhaaya to vidyottama ko laga ki vah kah rahaa he ki paanchon indriyaan bhale hi alag hon, sabhi ek man ke dwaara sanchalit hai. 
 vidyottama or kalidas kaa vivaah ho gaya tab vidyottama ko sachai kaa pataa chalaa ki kalidas anpadh hai. usne kalidas ko dhikkara or yah kah kar ghar se nikaal diya ki sachche pandit bane bina ghar vaapis nahin aana. kalidas ne sachche man se kaali devi kii aradhana kii or unke aashirwad se ve gyani or dhanwaan ban gaye. gyaan prapti ke baad jab ve ghar laute to unhone darwaja khadkaa kar kaha - kapatam udghaatya sundari . vidyottama ne chakit hokar kaha -- asti kashchid vagvisheshah . 
 is prakaar, is kimvadanti ke anusaar, kalidas ne vidyottama ko apana pathpradarshak guru mana or uske is vaakya ko unhone apane kaavyon main jagah di. kumarasambhavam kaa prarambh hota he- astyuttarasyam dishi… se, meghdutam kaa pehla shabd he- kashchitkanta… or raghuvansham kii shuruyaat hoti he- vagarthviv… se.
 choti-badi kul lagbhag chalis rachanaayen hai jinhen alag-alag vidwaanon ne kalidas dwaara rachit siddh karne kaa prayaas kiya he. inamen se maatr saat hi aisi hai jo nirvivaad rup se kalidaskrut mani jaati hai: teen naatak: abhigyaan shaakuntalam, vicramorvasium or malvicagnimitram; do mahakavy: raghuvansham or kumarasambhavam; or do khandakavy: meghdutam or rutusanhaar. inamen bhi rutusanhaar ko pro kith andeh ke saath kalidas kii rachana sweekaar karte hai.
 malvicagnimitram kalidas kii pehali rachana he, jisamen raja agnimitra kii kahani he. agnimitra ek nirvasit naukar kii beti malvika ke chitra ke prem karne lagata he. jab agnimitra kii patni ko is baat kaa pataa chalata he to vah malvika ko jail main dalava detii he. magar sanyog se malvika rajkumaari sabit hoti he or uske prem-sambandh ko sweekaar kar liya jaataa he.
 abhigyaan shaakuntalam kalidas kii duusari rachana he jo unki jagatprasiddhi kaa kaaran bana. is naatak kaa anuwaad angreji or german ke alaava duniya ke anek bhashaaon main hua he. isamen raja dushyant kii kahani he jo van main ek parityakt rishi putree shakuntala se prem karne lagata he. donon jangal main gandharva vivaah kar lete hai. raja dushyant apni rajdhani laut aate hai. isi bich rishi durvasa shakuntla ko shaap de dete hai ki jiske viyog main usne rishi kaa apmaan kiya vahi use bhul jayega. kaaphi kshamaprarthana ke baad rishi ne shaap ko thodaa naram karte hue kaha ki raja kii anguthi unhen dikhaate hi sab kuch yaad aa jayega. lekin rajdhani jate hue raste main vah anguthi kho jaati he. sthiti tab or gambhir ho gai jab shakuntla ko pataa chalaa ki vah garbhvatee he. shakuntla lakh gidgidai lekin raja ne use pahchaanane se inkaar kar diya. jab ek machuaare ne vah anguthi dikhaayi to raja ko sab kuch yaad aayaa or raja ne shakuntla ko apana liya. shakuntla shringaar ras se bhare sundar kaavyon kaa ek anupam naatak he. kaha jaataa he kavyeshu naatakan ramyan tatra ramya shakuntala 
 vicramorvasium ek rahasyon bharaa naatak he. isamen pururava indralok kii apsara urvashi se prem karne lagate hai. pururava ke prem ko dekhakar urvashi bhi unase prem karne lagati he. indra kii sabha main jab urvashi nrity karne jaati he to pururava se prem ke kaaran vah vahaan acha pradarshan nahin kar paati he. isse indra gusse main use shaapit kar dharti par bhej dete hai. haalanki, uskaa premi agar usase hone vaale putr ko dekh le to vah phir swarg laut sakegi. vicramorvasium kavyagat saundarya or shilp se bharpoor he.
 kumarasambhavam unke mahakavyon ke naam he. raghuvansham main sampoorn raghuvansh ke raajaaon kii gaathaayen hai, to kumarasambhavam main shiv-parvati kii premakatha or kartikeya ke janm kii kahani he. 
 raghuvansham main kalidas ne raghukul ke raajaaon kaa varnan kiya he.
 meghdutam ek geetikavy he jisamen yaksha dwaara megh se sandesh le jane kii prarthana or use doot bana kar apni priy ke paas bhejne kaa varnan he.meghdoot ke do bhaag hai -purvamegh evamuttaramegh.
 rutusanhaarm main sabhi rituon main prakruti ke vibhinn roopon kaa vistaar se varnan kiya gaya he.
 inke alaava kai chhitput rachanaaon kaa shrey kalidas ko diya jaataa he, lekin vidwaanon kaa mat he ki ye rachanaaen anya kaviyon ne kalidas ke naam se kii. naatakkaar or kavi ke alaava kalidas jyotish ke bhi visheshgy maane jate hai. uttar kalamritam naamak jyotish pustika kii rachana kaa shrey kalidas ko diya jaataa he. aisa mana jaataa he ki kaali devi kii puja se unhen jyotish kaa gyaan mila. is pustika main kii gai bhavishyavaanee satya sabit huin.
 kalidas ko kavikulguru, kanishtikadhishthit or kavitaakaminivilas jaisi prashanshatmak oopaadhiyaan pradaan kii gayi hai jo unke kavyagat vishistataaon se abhibhoot hokar hi di gayi hai. kalidas ke kavy kii vishistataaon kaa varnan nimnavat kiya ja saktaa he:
 kalidas vaidarbhi reeti ke kavi hai or unhone prasad gun se purna lalit shabdayojna kaa prayog kiya he. prasad gun kaa lakshan he - "jo gun man main vaise hi vyaapt ho jaay jaise sukhi eendhan kii lakdi main agni sahasaa prajvalit ho uthati he" or kalidas kii bhashaa kii yahi visheshta he.
 kalidas kii bhashaa madhur naad sundari se yukt he or samaason kaa alpaprayog, padon kaa samuchit sthaan par nivesh, shabdalankaron kaa swaabhaavik prayog ityadi gunon ke kaaran usamen pravaah or pranjalata vidyaman he. 
 kalidas ne shabdalankaron kaa swaabhaavik prayog kiya he or unhen upama alankaar ke prayog main siddhhast or unki upamaaon ko shreshth mana jaataa he. udahaaran ke liye:
 sanchaarini deepashikhev ratri yan yan vyatiyay patinwara sa.
 narendramargott ive prapede vivarnabhaavan sa sa bhumipalah..
 arthath swayamvar main baari-baari se prayek raja ke saamne gaman karti hui indumati raajaaon ke saamne se chalati hui deepashikha kii tarah lag rahi thi jiske aage bad jane par raajaaon kaa mukh vivarna ho jaataa tha. 
 kalidas kii kavita kii pramukh visheshta he ki vah chitron ke nirmaan main sabakuch na kahkar bhi abhivyanjana dwaara puraa chitra khinch dete hai. jaise:
 evam vaadini devarshau parshwe piturdhomukhi.
 lila camel patrani ganyamas parvati..
 arthath devarshi ke dwaara aisi baat karne par, pita ke sameep baithi parvati ne sir jhukaa kar haath main liye camel kii pankhudiyon ko ginana shuru kar diya.
 kudiyattam main sanskrit aadhaarit naatakon kaa ek rangamanch he, jahaan bhaas ke naatak khele jate hai. mahaan kudiottem kalakaar or natya shaastra ke vidwaan swargeey natyacharya vidushkarattanam padmashri guru mani madhav chakyar ne kalidas ke naatakon ke manchan kii shuruuaat kii. kalidas ko lokapriy banane main dakshin bhartiya bhashaaon ke filmon kaa bhi kaaphi yogadan he. kannada bhashaa kii filmon kaviratn kalidas or mahakavi kalidas ne kalidas ke jivan ko kaaphi lokapriy banaaya. in filmon main special effect or sangeet kaa bakhubi upyog kiya gaya tha. ve shantaram ne shakuntla par aadhaarit film banaayi thi. ye film itani prabhaavshaalee thi ki isapar aadhaarit anek bhashaaon main kai filmein banaai gai.
 hindi lekhak mohan rakesh ne kalidas ke jivan par ek naatak asadha kaa ek din kii rachana kii, jo kalidas ke sangharkshil jivan ke vibhinn pahaluon ko dikhaata he. 1976 main surendra verma ne ek naatak likhaa, jisamen is baat kaa zikr kiya gaya he ki parvati ke shaap ke kaaran kalidas kumarasambhav ko puraa nahin kar paae the. shiv or parvati ke garhastha jivan kaa ashleeltapurvak varnan karne ke liye parvati ne unhen yah shaap diya tha. is naatak main kalidas ko chandragupta kii adaalat kaa saamana karnaa padaa, jahaan panditon or naitiktavaadiyon ne unpar anek aarope lagaae. is naatak main na sirf ek lekhak ke sangharkshil jivan ko dikhaaya gaya he, balki lekhak kii abhivyakti kii swatantraaa ke mahatv ko bhi rekhaankit kiya gaya he.
 asti kashchit vagarthium naam se dow krishna kumaar ne 1984 main ek naatak likhaa, yah naatak kalidas ke vivaah kii lokapriy katha par aadhaarit he. is katha ke anusaar, kalidas ped kii usee tahani ko kaat rahe hote hai, jis par ve baithe the. vidyottam se apamaanit do vidwaanon ne uski shaadi isi kalidas ke kara di. jab use thage jane kaa ahsaas hota he, to wo kalidas ko thukraa detii he. saath hi, vidyottama ne ye bhi kaha ki agar ve vidya or prasiddhi arjeet kar lautate hai to vah unhen sweekaar kar legi. jab kalidas vidya or prasiddhi arjeet kar laute to sahi rasta dikhaane ke liye kalidas ne unhen patni na maanakar guru maan liya.
 kalidas or dandi main koun shreshth kavi the, is sawaal ko donon ne mata saraswati ke saamne rakhaa. saraswati ne jawaab diya, dandi. dukhi kalidas ne poocha, "to maan main kuch bhi nahin"? mata ne jawaab diya, tvamevahan, yani tum or main donon ek jaise hai.
