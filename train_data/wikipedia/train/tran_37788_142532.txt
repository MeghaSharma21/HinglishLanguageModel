 temuri rajvansh, jo swayan ko 'gurkani rajvansh' kehete the, madhya asia or uttari bhartiya upmahadweep ke vistrut ilaaqon par raaj karne vaala turki-mongol nasl kaa ek sunni muslim vansh tha. apane charam par iske samrajya main samast iran, afghanistan or uzbekistan ke saath-saath pakistan, uttar bharat, anatolia, cocks or mesopotamia ke bade bhubhag shaamil the. is rajvansh kii niv 14veen shatabdi isvi main temurlang naamak aakraamak or vijeta ne rakhi thi. 16veen sadi main uzbekistan kii faraghna vaadi se bharat par akraman karke mogul sultanate kii sthapana karne vaala babar bhi isi temuri rajvansh kaa hissaa tha. kyonki temurlang ko aksar 'amir taimur' kaha jaataa tha isliye is rajgaraane ke vanshaj apane naamon main aksar 'mirza' jod liya karte the jo 'ameerzaada' kaa sankshipt rup he.
