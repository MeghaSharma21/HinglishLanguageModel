 lasika ek ant:sravit drav he jo maanav kii koshikaon ke bich main paaya jaataa he. yah lasika paatro main capilri chhidron se hokar chhanata hua pravesh karata he. 
 lasika kii rachana rakt-plasma se milti-julati he kintu yah maamuli rup se bhinn ho sakati he. lasika main shwet rakt kanikas paayi jaati hai.
