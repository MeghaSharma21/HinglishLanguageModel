 madrid kaa riyal banaspati baag spain ke madrid main palasa de muriyo main praado ajaayabaghar ke saath sthit ek vanaspati baag he. yah 20 acre main faila hua he. 
 is baag kii sthapana 17 oktober 1755 ko raja ferdinand 6wein dwaara kii gai thi. us samay isamen 2,000 se adhik paudhe maujuud the jo ki vanaspati vigyaani khose kuer i marthines dwaara ekatrit kiye gaye the.
