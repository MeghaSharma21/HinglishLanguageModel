 shiyanbei prachinkaal main manchuriya, bhitari mongolia or purvi mongolia main basane vaali ek mongol kanaabadosh qabeelon kii jati thi. mana jaataa he ki khan kii oopaadhi kaa istemaal sabase pehali inhi logon main hua tha. chini sroton ke anusaar shiyanbei log donghu logon ke vanshaj the. prasiddh chini itihaaskaar seemaa chian ne apane mahaan itihaaskaar ke abhilekh naamak itihaas-granth main darj kiya tha ki shiyanbei log 699 isapurv se 632 isapurv ke kaal main bhitari mongolia main rahete the. yah log pehle shiyongnu logon kii seva main the, phir inhonen china ke haan rajvansh kii shiyongnu ke viruddh sahaayta kari lekin uske baad swayan hi chini samrajya par akraman kaa silsilaa shuru kiya. inkaa sabase prasiddh raja tanshihuai tha jisne ek vistrut shiyanbei samrajya kii sthapana kii.
