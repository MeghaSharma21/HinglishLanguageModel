 naam sudhaa aroda
 janm 04 oktober 1948
 janmasthan lahore pakistan
 pesha lekhika
 rashtriyata bhartiya
 sudhaa aroda kaa janm lahore main san 1948 main hua. unki ucch shiksha kalakatta vishwavidhalay se hui. isi vishwavidhalay ke do collejon main unhone san 1969 se 1971 tak adhyaapan kaary kiya. sudhaa aroda katha sahitya main ek charchit naam he. 
 sudhaa aroda kii kahaniyaan lagbhag sabhi bhartiya bhashaa ke alaava kai videshi bhaashaon main anuvaadit he. unhone bhartiya mahilaon, kalakaronke aatmakathyon ke do sankalan'‘dahalij ko laanghte hue’' tathaa '‘pankhoki udaan’' taiyaar kiya hai lekhan ke str par patr-patrikaon main bhi unki sakriyata bani hui hai. pakshik patrika '‘sarika’' main ‘aam aadmi jinda sawaal’ or rashtriya dainik '‘jansatta’' main mahilaon se sambandhit mudde par unka saptahik stambh ‘vama’ bahucharchit rahaa he mahila sanghathano ke saamaajik kaaryon ke prati unki sakriyata evam samarthan jari he. mahilaon par hi kendrit‘aurat kii duniya banaam duniya kii aurat’ lekhon kaa sangrah sheeghra prakaashya he. ‘uttar pradesh hindi santhaan’ dwaara unhen khaas puruskaar sesammanit kiya gaya.
