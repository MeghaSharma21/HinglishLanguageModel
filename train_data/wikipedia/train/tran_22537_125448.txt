 sar jorge mallett  saint loosia ke ek rajaneta hai. unhen 1 june 1996 se 17 sitambar 1997 ke bich, saint loosia kii rani, elizabeth dwitiya dwaara, saint loosia ke governor-general yani maharajyapal ke pad par niyukt kiya gaya tha. is kaal ke dauraan ve maharani ke pratinidhi ke rup main, unki anupasthiti ke dauraan shaasha ke kartavyon kaa nirvaah karte the.
