 taklakot, jise tibeti main purang or chini main burang kehete hai, tibet ke ngaari vibhaag ke purang zile main sthit ek shahar he jo purang zile kii rajdhani bhi he. yah bharat, tibet or nepal ke bich main ek mahatvapurn vyapaarik or saanskrithik kendra bhi rahaa he. kailash or maansarovar ke tirthon ko jate hue hindu va buddha tirthayatri aksar taklakot se guzarakar jate rahe hai. 4,755 meter kii oonchaai par sthit yah shahar kailash parvat se dakshin main ghaghara nadi kii ghati main basaa hua he. kuch sroton ke anusaar 1955-1960 tak taklakot kaa 90% khaane kaa saamaan bharat se aayaa karata tha.
