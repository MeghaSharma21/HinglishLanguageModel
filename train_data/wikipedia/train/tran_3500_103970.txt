 2001 kii bhartiya janaganana ke anusaar, karnataka kii kul jansankhya 52,850,562 he, jisamen se 26,898,918 purush or 25,951,644 striyaan hai. yani prayek 1000 purush 964 striyaan hai. iske anusaar 1991 kii jansankhya main 17.25% kii vruddhi hui he. rajya kaa jansankhya ghanatv 275.6 prati varg ki.me he or 33.98% log shahari kshetron main rahete hai. yahan kii sakshartaa dar 66.6% he, jisamen 76.1% purush or 56.9% striyaan sakshar hai. yahan kii kul jansankhya kaa 83% hindu hai or 11% muslim, 4% isai, 0.78% jain, 0.73% buddha or shesh log anya dharmavalambee hai.
 karnataka kii aadhikarik bhashaa kannada he or sthaneey bhashaa ke rup main 64.75% logon dwaara boli jaati he. 1991 ke anusaar anya bhashaayi alpsankhyakon main urdu, telegu, tamil, marathi, tulu, konkani, malayalam or kodev takk bhashi 0.25% hai. rajya kii janm dar 2.2% or mrityu dar 0.72% he. iske alaava shishu mrityu dar 5.5% evam matra mrityu dar 0.195% he. kul prajnan dar 2.2 he.
 swasthya evam aarogy ke kshetra main karnataka kii nijee kshetra kii companiyaan vishwa kii sarvshereshth sansthaaon se tulaneey hai. karnataka main uttam jan swasthya sevaayen uplabdh hai, jinke aankade va sthiti bharat ke anya adhikansh rajyon kii tulana main kaaphi behatar he. iske baavjood bhi rajya ke kuch ati pichde ilaakon main praathmik swasthya sevaaon kaa abhaav he.prashasnic uddeshy hetu, karnataka ko chaar revenu mandalon, 49 up-mandalon, 29 jilon, 175 talukon or 745 hobleez/revenu vritton main baanta gaya he. prayek jila prashasan kaa adhyaksh jila upayukta hota he, jo bhartiya prashasnic seva se hota he or uske adheen karnataka rajya sevaaon ke anek adhikarigan hote hai. rajya ke nyaay or kanoon vyavastha kaa uttardaayitv police upayukta par hota he. ye bhartiya police seva kaa adhikari hota he, jiske adheen karnataka rajya police seva ke adhikarigan karyrat hote hai. bhartiya van seva se van upsanrakshak adhikari tainaat hota he, jo rajya ke van vibhaag kii adhyakshata karata he. jilon ke sarvangin vikaas, prayek jile ke vikaas vibhaag jaise lok seva vibhaag, swasthya, shiksha, krishi, pashu-paalan, aadi vibhaag dekhte hai. rajya kii nyaaypalika bangaleru main sthit karnataka ucch nyaayalay or prayek jile main jile or satr nyaayalay tathaa taluk str ke nichle nyaayalay ke dwaara chalati he.
