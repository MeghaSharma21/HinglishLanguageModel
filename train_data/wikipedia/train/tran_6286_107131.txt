 keemati dhaatu aisi raasayanik dhaatu tatv hoti he jo prakruti main durlabh ho or aarthik rup se bahumoolya ho. rasinic drushti se keemati dhaatu anya tatwon se kam abhikriya karte hai. anya dhaatuon kii tulana main yah aksar adhik chamkile hote hai or adhik mulaayam hone ke kaaran inhen kheenchna yaa manachaaha aakaar dena bhi aasaan hota he. inhen itihaas main mudra ke rup main bahut prayog kiya gaya he lekin aadhunik kaal main inhen aabhushan, nivesh or audyogic prayogon main zyaada lagaaya jaataa he. keemati dhaatuon main sona, chaandi, platinum or paladium adhik vikhyath hai, haalanki kai anya dhaatuen bhi is gut main aati hai.
