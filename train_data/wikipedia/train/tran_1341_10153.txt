 jujharsingh bundela, orchha ke raja veerasingh dev ke putr the. 
 jahangir shashan ke antim kaal main inhen raja kii oopaadhi mili or ye chaar hajaari mansabadar bane. san 1627 itho shahjahan ne inhen uphaaron se sammanit kiya. kintu kuch samay pashchaat ashuddh karalekha kii shahjahan dwaara jaanch kiye jane kii soochna se darkar ye agre se bhagkar orchha chale gaye. vahaan apni sainya shakti badhaane lage. jab jahangir ko ye samachar gyaat hua to usne senaayen bhejkar orchha main gheraa dalava diya. falat: yuddh hua, kintu 'arich' durg baadashahi senaaon ke adhikaar main aa jane se jujharsingh ke paraajay kii sambhaavna utpann ho gai. tab inhone mahavat khan kii sharan main jaakar, jo baadashahi sena ke ek bhaag kaa netritv kar rahaa tha, kshamaa maangi. mahavat khan inhen shahjahan ke darbar main le aayaa. shahjahan ne inhen kshamaa kar diya. 
 shahjahan jab khanjahan lodi or nizamulmulk par akraman karne ke uddeshy se dakshin kii ore gaya tab yah bhi dakshin ke soobedaar aazam khan ke saath niyukt hue. tatpashchaat ye chandaval main niyukt hue. dakshin praant purnat: vijit hone par kuch dinon ke liye apane desh chale gaye. desh pahunchne par inhone chauragarh durg par akraman kar use jeet liya. jab jahangir ko yah soochna mili to usne ek aadesh jujharsingh ke liye jari kiya ki vijit praant or kosh kii sampatti kaa bahut bada bhaag badshaah ko saunp de. kintu vah taal gaye or apane putr ko bhi, jo dakshin main tha, bulavaa liya. shahjahan ne unhen dandit karne ke liye senaayen bhejin. vah apane putr ke saath sara saamaan liye idhar se udhar bhaagate phirte rahe. is bich inkaa bahut sa bahumoolya saamaan inase chhinata gaya. kintu apane putr vikramajit singh ke saath ye jangalon main chhipe rahe. san 1644 itho main devgarh ke gondon ne in donon ko maar daalaa. khaanedauraan ne donon ke sar katwakar badshaah ke paas bhijwa die. chauragarh ke kosh se praapt 1 karod rupaya bhi darbar main bhej diya gaya.
