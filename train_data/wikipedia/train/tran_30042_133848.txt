 muzart nadi, jise kabhi-kabhi muzaat nadi bhi kehete hai, janavaadee ganatantr china dwaara niyantrit shinjiyang praant ke aqsu vibhaag main bahane vaali ek nadi kaa naam he. yah tarim nadi kii ek upanadi he or usamen bai taraf se vilay hoti he. 20veen sadi kii shuruyaat ke kuch sroton main iskaa naam shah yaar dariyaa bhi miltaa he.
 muzart nadi tiyaan shaan kii parvatiy shrunkhlaa main khan tengri pahaad ke paas sthit muzart himani se shuru hoti he. phir yah bicheng zile main tiyaan shaan or chueletag parvaton ke bich kii vaadi main purv-dakshinpurv disha main nikalati he. bicheng zile ke log zyaadaatar isi nadi kii sinchai par nirbhar karte hai. yah pracheen kuuchaa shahar ke paas se nikalati he jahaan yah chueletag parvaton main ek tang ghati banaati he. vaadi kii uttari chattaanon main 230 se adhik gufaayen kati hui hai jo kizil gupha parisar he or prachinkaal ke tushaari logon se sambandhit puraatatv ke najariye se bahut mahatv rakhti hai.
 kizil gufaaon se aage is nadi ko baandhakar ek 50,000 varg meter ke satahi kshetrafal kaa bada sarovar bana diya gaya he. kuuchaa, toksu or shayar zilon ko yahan se sinchai kaa pani bheja jaataa he. haalanki muzart nadi pehle tarim nadi main jaakar vilay hua karti thi ab yah us badi nadi tak keval garmiyon or basant main hi pahunch paati he.
