 laddu ek prakaar kii mithai he jo se kai tarah se banaai jaati he. yah besan, motichur, gond ityadi kai alag-alag chizon se taiyaar kiya jaataa he. bharat ke alaava yah pakistan main bhi banaaya or pasand kiya jaataa he. laddu sainkado prakaar ke hote hai or banaaye ja sakate hai. har jagahon ke ladduon kii alag alag visheshtayen hoti he. pracheen kaal main laddu kisi bhi utsav main bhojan ke aayojan main vishesh prakaar kaa mahatv rakhata tha. anek mandiron main bhagwaan ke prasad ke rup main laddu milte hai. har mandir ke ladduon kii alag visheshta hoti he.
 tirupati ke laddu apni alag pahchaan rakhte hai.
