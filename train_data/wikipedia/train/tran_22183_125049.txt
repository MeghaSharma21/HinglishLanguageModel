  pan rajeshwar jha maithili bhashaa ke vikhyath sahityakar hai. inke dwaara rachit ek saahityetihaas avahatth : udbhav o vikaas ke liye unhen san 1977 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.{{cite web | url=http://sahitya-akademi.gov.in/sahitypaM rajeshwar jha kaa janm 21april 1922 ko tatkaaleen saharsa jila ab supaul jila antargat rasuaar gaanv main hua tha. rasuaar nirmali railway stesan se parv kii disha main avasthit he . madhyam vargeey parivaar main janm lene vaale pan jha kii shiksha diksha gaanv or phir nirmali hai skool se hua. pan nokhelaal jha ke jeyashth putr rajeshwar jha apane gaanv se metric paas karne vaale eklaute chaatr the . us samay gaanv main kosi kii traasadee aisi kii log dane dane ke liye mohataaj hua karte the. parivaar kii mali haalat ke bich pan jha aage kii padhai chhod raaj darbhanga ke bousi cirkil se naukari kii shuruyaat kii phir samay antaraal ke baad patna main bihar research socity aaye. kam samay main pan rajeshwar jha maithili sahitya ke liye do darjan se adhik pustaken likhi . inke dwaara likhi gayi pustakon main shodhagranth bhi shaamil he.
