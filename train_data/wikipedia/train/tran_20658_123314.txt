 volkan woolf, gailapagos dweepasmooh kii sabase unchi chotii he or yah dweepasmooh ke isabela dweep par sthit he. isaki oonchaai 1,707 meter he jo is dweepasmooh kaa sabase unchaa sthaan he. yah ek ulte rakhe katore ke aakaar kaa jwalamukhi parvat he.
 is jwalamukhi kaa naam german bhuvigyaani theodor woolf ke naam par kiya gaya he, jinhone 19 veen shatabdi main in dwipon kaa adhyayan kiya tha, gailapagos ke uttar me sthit woolf dweep kaa namakaran bhi unke naam par kiya gaya he.
 isaki oonchaai ise ek charam udagr shikhar banaati he, yah wo shikhar hote hai jinaki oonchaai 1500 me se adhik hoti he.
