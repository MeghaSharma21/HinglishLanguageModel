 
 piyare frede, baron de cobertin ek francisi shikshashasri or itihaaskaar the, anterrashtriya olympic samiti ke sansthaapak or aadhunik olympic khelon ke janak maane jate hai. francisi kuleen parivaar main janmen, piyare frede shaikshanik bane va vyapak shrenee ke vishyon kaa adhyayan kiya, sabase ullekhneey rup se shiksha or itihaas.
 piyare frede kaa janm 1 janvari 1863 ko paris main ek sthapit kuleen parivaar main hua. yah baron charles lui frede, baron de cobertin or meri-marsaal jiglt de chrisnoy ke chauthe bachche the.
 piyare kaa janm 1january 1863 ko paris ke unche kuleen parivaar me hua tha vah apne mata-pita kii chauthi santaan the unke khandaan ke itihhas se pataa chalata he ki 11 wein lui ne unke purvajon keelankaran se sammanit kiya tha unke pita charles kii banaai pentigon ko pradarshit va sammanit kiya gaya 
 piyare apane parivaar ke naam ke antim sadsy the. inke jeevanikar jon maulun ke shabdon main, "apane vansh ke antim, piyare de cobertin hi ekmaatr sadsy the jinaki khyaati atijivit rahegi".
