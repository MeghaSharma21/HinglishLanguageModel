 shri shankaracharya sanskrit sarvaklashaala kerala main kochi ke nikat kalti main sthapit ek saarvajanik vishvavidhyalay he. isaki sthapana 1993 main hui thi. iskaa naam aadi shankaracharya ke naam par kiya gaya he. yah purna nadi ke tat par sthit he. vishvavidhyalay kaa adarshavakya he : gyanadev tu kaivalyam 
 yah vishvavidhyalay sanskrit tathaa anya bhashaaon kii pandulipiyon ke prakaashan evam sangrakshan ke liye kaary karti he. iske nau kshetreey kendra hai- kalti, tiruvananthapuram, trishshur, panman, turavur, errumanur, tirur, koilanti or payyannur.
