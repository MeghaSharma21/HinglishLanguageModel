 swarnavahini srilanka kaa ek manoranjan va samachar channel he. isaki sthapana 16 march 1997 main kii gai thi. yah sinhalai bhashaa main apane kaaryakramon ko dikhaata he. ise kai puraskaar bhi mil chuke hai. yah kai bhartiya dharawahikon ko anuwaad kar bhi dikhaata he. yah pehle itivi kaa hi ek bhaag tha. lekin iepi samooh ne ise kharid liya or iskaa naam itivi 1 se badal kar swarnavahini kar diya.
 ise adhik puraskaar nahin mili hai, kyonki iske aadhe se adhik kaarykram anya bhashaa se lekar anuwaad kiye gaye hote hai. iske alaava isamen samachar or vaastavik kaarykram bhi adhik hota he. ise mukhyatah ramya suramya naamak dharawahik se hi adhik puraskaar mili hai.
