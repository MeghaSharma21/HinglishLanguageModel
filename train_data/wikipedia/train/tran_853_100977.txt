 ilemi trikon purvi africa kaa ek vivaadit kshetra he. isaki simaaon ko lekar bhi kuch bahes he or iskaa kshetrafal 10,320 or 14,000 varg kimi ke bich anumaanit kiya jaataa he. ilemi trikon kaa naam anuaak samudaay ke 'ilemi akvon' naamak ek mukhiya par rakhaa gaya tha. dakshin sudan or kenya donon isapar samprabhutaa kaa daawa karte hai haalanki yah ilaaka kenya ke qabze main he. ithiopia ise aupchaarik rup se apana to nahin kahata lekin usne samay-samay par yahan sainik hastkshep kiya he. bhugolic drushti se ilemi trikon turkana jhil ke pashchimottaree kone ko choota he.
