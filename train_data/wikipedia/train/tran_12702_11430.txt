 Mice Galaxies, IC 819 / 820, UGC 7938 / 7939,
 chuha gallexiyaan yaa anjisi 4676 prithvi se 29 karod prakashavarsh dur sthit do sarpil gallexiyon kaa ek joda he jo ek-duusare se takaraane or vilay hone kii prakriya kar raheen hai. prithvi kii sataha se dekhne par yah akaash main coma berenisis taramandal main dikhti hai. inkaa naam "chuha" isliye padaa kyonki ek-duusare par bhayankar gurutvkarshak khinchaai karne se jowarbhata bal ne ek lambi dum kii akruti vaala dhaancha bana diya he.
