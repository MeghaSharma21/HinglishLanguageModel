 zolotoy rog ek sing ke aakaar vaali samudri khadi he jis se rus ke sudur purvi praant premorski kroy kaa tat lagata he. yah khadi aage chalakar japan sagar main mil jaati he. iske seengnuma aakaar kii lambaai saat kilometre he or chaudaai lagbhag do kilometre he. pani kii gaharaai 20 se 27 meter ke darmiyaan he. apane aakaar kii vajah se yah ek badhiya prakritik bandargah he, jis vajah se iske mukh par sthit vladivostok nagar bhi phala-phula he.
 19veen shatabdi main chini sroton main ise khadi ko 'gaamat khadi' bulaayaa gaya he. san 1850 ke dashak main ek francisi jahaaz ne yahan kuch der ke liye lungar daalaa tha or yah pehali gyaat europea nauka thi jo kuch samay ke liye zolotoy khadi main rukii. kuch varshon baad kreemia ke yuddh ke dauraan 'vinchaster' naam kii british jangi nauka ne bhi yahan lungar daalaa or ise 'may bandargah' kaa naam diya. 1859 me rus ke rajdurbar se sambandh rakhane vaale nikolay muravayov-amurski ne jab is bandargah to dekha to unhen yah turki ke istanbul shahar kii bandargah se milti julati lagi. us bandargah kaa naam 'sone kaa sing' he. isliye unhone is bandargah kaa naam roosi bhashaa main 'zolotoy rog', rakhaa jiskaa arth bhi 'sone kaa sing' he.
