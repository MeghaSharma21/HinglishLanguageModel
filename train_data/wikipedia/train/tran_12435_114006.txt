 asigarh haryana rajya ke hisar shahar main sthit ek durg he. yah amati sarovar ke purvi tat par sthit he or ise prithviraj chouhan kaa qila yaa haansi kaa kila bhi kehete hai. prithviraj chouhan ne mogul shaskaon se raksha ke liye is durg kaa nirmaan karaayaa tha. kaalaantar main mogul shaskaon ne is durg par adhikaar kar liya tha. baad main unhone is durg main ek masjid kaa nirmaan bhi karavaaya tha. vartmaan main yah adhikanshtah ek khandahar tile main parivartit ho chukaa he.
 vartmaan main iskaa anurakshan bhartiya puratathvik surveykshan vibhaag ke adheen he.
