 rashtriya krishi aarthiki evam niti anusandhan santhaan ) bharat main krishi arthshaastra kaa pramukh santhaan he. isaki sthapana bhartiya krishi anusandhan parishad dwaara varsh 1991 main, arthshaastra ke ekikaran madhyam se krishi anusandhan ko majboot karne tathaa rashtriya krishi anusandhan kii kshamta ko badhaane ke liye kii gayi thi. is santhaan kaa uddeshy saamaajik, aarthik evam nitigat shodh vishleshanon, tathaa nirnyon main parishad kii sahabhaagita ko rashtriya str par parilakshith karnaa he. 
 is santhaan main nideshak sahit 19 krishi arthshaastra anusandhan vaigyanik he. ek prabandhan samiti he jo santhaan ko bhartiya drushtikon se krishi arthshaastra or niti yojana ke anusandhan prashasan sambandh kii salaah dete hai. is samiti main varisht anusandhan prashasnic adhikari or anya prashasnic adhikari evam kisaanon ke hitkar pratinidhi sammilit hai. anya kai aantarik samitiyaan bhi santhaan ke kriyakalapo ke vikendrikrut prabandhan main sahaayta pradaan karti hai.
