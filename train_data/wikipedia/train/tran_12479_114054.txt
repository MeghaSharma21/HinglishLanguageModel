 brijesh singh ek rajaneta or uttar pradesh kii 17veen vidhaan sabha ke sadsy hai. ye uttar pradesh ke saharanpur jile ke devband vidhaan sabha kshetra kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai.brijesh kaa naam pashchim uttar pradesh ke yuva evam kaddavar netaaon main shaamil he. 
 brijesh singh ne uttar pradesh vidhansabha chunaav, 2017 main bhartiya janata party ke pratyaashee ke rup main chunaav ladaa or apane nikatatam pratidwandvi bahujan samaaj party ke pratyaashee maajid ali ko 29,400 maton se parajeet kar jeet darj kii.
