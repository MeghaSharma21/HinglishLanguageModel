 nagar nigam ke ek sthaneey shaasi nikaay ke liye kanoonee naam hota he, jo shahar, countiyon, kasbon, basti, charter basti, gaanvon or nagar sahit sthalon ke liye prayukt shabd hota he. nagar nigam ke samavesh tab hota he jab aise nagar palikaon ho swayan rajya yaa praant main ve sthit hai ke kanoonon ke tahat sansthaaon ke sanchaalak. aksar, is ghatana ko puraskaar yaa nagar nigam ke ek charter kii ghoshnaa se chihnit he.
 bharat main ek nagar nigam ek sthaneey sarkaar ke rup main 2 lakh yaa usase adhik kii jansankhya ke ek shahar kaa prashasan karata he. panchayat raaj pranali ke tahat, yah rajya sarkaar ke saath sidhe sampark rakhata he, haalanki yah prashasnic drushti se jile ke adheen hi aata he va usee main sthit hota he. bharat main sabase bade nagar nigamon vartmaan main dilli, kolkata or chennai ke baad mumbai rahe hai.
