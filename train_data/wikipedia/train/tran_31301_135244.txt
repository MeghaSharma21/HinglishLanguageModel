 mautatthu adur gopalakrishnan unnithan chah baar rashtriya film puraskaar jeet chuke bhartiya film nirdeshak, patakatha lekhak or nirmaataa hai. unki adhiktar filmein duniyabar main mushkil se hi relies ho pai hai. adur gopalakrishnan ne bahut kam hi film samarohon main hissaa liya. unki filmi logon se kam hi milanaa-julna hota he.
