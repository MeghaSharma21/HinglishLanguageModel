 minakshi natarajan ek bhartiya raajnneetijna or 15 veen loksabhaa main mandsaur se purv sansad sadsy hai..
 minakshi natarajan kaa janm birlagram nagda, ujjain main hua tha . unhone jaev rasayan main snaatak kiya evam kanoon main snaatak kii degree devi ahilya vishvavidhyalay se praapt kii. NSUI main shaamil hone ke pashchaat unhone raajnneeti main bhaag lenaa prarambh kiya.
 ve neshnal student union of india, adhyaksh raheen 1999 se 2002 tak. unhone madhya pradesh youth congress main 2002-2005 ke dauraan adhyaksh pad sambhala or shri rahul gandhi dwaara akhil bhartiya congress cameti ke sachiv main chaynit kiya gaya 2008 main.
 mandsaur seat se bhartiya aam chunaav, 2009 main pehali baar main hi apane pratidwandvi bhaajapa ke lakshminarayan pandey 30,000 se adhik voton se haraa diya . gauratlab he ki pandey ji 1971 se yahan se lagaataar chunaav jitate hue aa rahe the.
 carmik, lok shikaayat, kanoon or nyaay, mahilaon ke shashaktikaran samition kii sadsy raheen hai. 
 2014 main, mandsaur sansadeey kshetra main bhaajapa ke sudhir gupta ne sushri minakshi ko 300,000 se adhik maton ke antar se haraa diya . 
 ek chunavi raily ke dauraan, congress party ke diggaj netaa digvijay singh, khud ko "johari" or meenakshi natarajan ko "sau tanch maal" kahkar vivaadon main ghir gaye. is bayaan kii bahut aalochana kii gayi.
