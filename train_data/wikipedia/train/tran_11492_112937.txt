 baalod jila chhattisgarh ke 27 jilon main ek jila he, baalod raipur mandal kaa jila he or iskaa mukhyalay baalod he. 
 baalod jile kaa kshetrafal 3,527 varg kilometre he, or 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar baalod kii jansankhya 826,165 or jansankhya ghanatv 230 /km2 vyakti he, baalod kii sakshartaa 74.16% he, mahila purush anupaat yahan par 1022 mahilaye prati 1000 purusho par he, jile kii jansankhya vikasdar 2001 se 2011 ke bich 1.9% rahaa he.
 baalod jila bharat ke rajyo main dakshin purv kii taraf kii andar kii taraf sthit chhattisgarh rajya main he, baalod jila chhattisgarh ke aantarik dakshini bhaag kaa jila he baalod 22 ° 73’25 ” uttar akshansh or 81 ° 02’40 purvi deshaantar ke bich sthit he, baalod kii samudratal se oonchai 324 meter he, baalod raipur se 99 kilometre dakshin pashchim kii taraf he or desh kii rajdhani dilli se 1255 kilometre dakshin purv kii taraf hi he.

 kharun nadi shivnath nadi kii pramukh sahaayak nadi he. yah shivnath nadi main milakar mahanadi kii sampann jalarashi main bhi apratyaksh rup se sahyog karti he. baalod jile ke sanjari kshetra se nikalne vaali yah nahin rajdhani raipur kii seemaa se bahati hui simga ke somnath ke paas shivnath nadi main mil jaati he. isi jagah par yah bemetara or balaudabazar tehsil ko alag karti he. balaudabazar ke uttar main arapa nadi, jo bilaspur jile se nikalati he, shivnath se aa milti he. haavda nagpur rail line is kharun nadi ke upar se guzarati he. kharun main ek shaanti he jo dekhne waalon ko apni ore khinchati hai.
