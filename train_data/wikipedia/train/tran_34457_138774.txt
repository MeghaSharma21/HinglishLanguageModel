 
 thanale gufaaen pracheen buddha gufaaen hai jinhen 'nadsur gufaaen' naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. yah gufaaen maharashtra ke raigarh jile main paali naamak gaanv ke paas 18 ki.me. par sthit he. yahan kul 23 guphaayen hai.
 aisa mana jaataa he ki ye isa purv pehle shatak main bani guphaayen hai. do chaityas, do stupa or anya vihaar yahaan dikhaai dete hai. buddha sthapatyakala kaa vishesh prabhaav in gufaao main paaya jaataa he.
 tatkaaleen suprasiddh puraatatvajna henri cacins ne in gufaaon par shodhakarya kiya he. 1911 saal main unhone in gufaaon par aadhaarit ek pustak prakashit kii thi.
 swatantry sangram ke dauraan british raaj se chhutkaaraa paane ke liye krantikari vasudev balwant fadake ne in gufaaon kaa aashray liya tha or vah yahaan bhumigat hokar rahe the.
