 
 paath dekhen
 chiku fal kaa ek prakaar hai. 
 chiku mukhya rup se teen prakaar ke hote hai. 
 chiku sheethal, pittanashaka, paushtik, mithe or ruchikaarak hai. isamen sharkara kaa ansh zyaada hota he. yah pachne main baari hota he. bhojan ke baad yadi chiku kaa seven kiya jaae to yah nishchit rup se laabh pradaan karata he.
 chiku kaa utpaadan bahut acchhee maatra main kiya ja saktaa haiyadi acchhee fasal kaa utpaadan karnaa he to uski dekhbhaal achche se kerani hoti he.
