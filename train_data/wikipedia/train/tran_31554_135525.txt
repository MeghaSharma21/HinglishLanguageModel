 jain dharm main mathon ke swami bhattarak kehalaate hai. adhikansh bhattarak digambar hote hai. pracheen kaal main baudhon or sanatani hinduon main bhi bhattarak hone ke pramaan hai kintu aajkal keval jain dharm main hi bhattarak milte hai, purv samay main sampurna bharat main hi bhattarak virajit rahete the parantu kalkram or visham paristhitiyon ke kaaran ve dakshin bharat tak hi simit rah gayi. vartmaan main punah jindharm ke sangrakshan va sanvardhan ke liye bhattarak parampara ko sampurna bharat main punah sthapit karne kii sanyojna karyrat he.
