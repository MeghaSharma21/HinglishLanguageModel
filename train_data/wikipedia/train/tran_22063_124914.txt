 robert beadle simpasan apane laghu naam bob simpasan se vikhyath purv cricket khilaadi hai jo new south wales, western australia or australia cricket team ke liye khelte the. inhone 1963-64 se 1967-68 tak or phir 1977-78 main rashtriya team kaa netritv kiya. baad main unhone australian team ke coach ke rup main behad safal kaarykaal puraa kiya tha. simpasan daaen haath ke ballebaaj or ardh niyamit leg spin gendbaaj ke rup main khelte the. sevanivritti ke das saal baad vah world series cricket ke yug ke dauraan 41 saal kii umr main australia kii kaman sambhalate vakt surkhiyon main lauten. 1986 main unhen australian team kaa coach niyukt kiya gaya tha. jis pad par vah july 1996 main geoff marsh dwaara unki jagah lene tak kaayam rahe.
 bob ne khilaadi ke rup main apane pehle kaarykaal main 1957 se 1967 tak cricket khelaa. shuru main vah harfanmaulaa ke rup khelte the. 1963 main anbhavi khilaadiyon nil harvey or alan davidson ke sanyas lene par unhen up captain banaaya gaya. phir 1964 main richi benode ke sanyas lene par unhen captain banaaya gaya. unhone apane pehle shatak ke rup main 311 ran banaae. aisa karne vaale wo garfield sobers ke baad duusare khiladi bane the . captain ke rup main shuruyaat main safal rahane ke baad, unke kariyar ke ant tak aate-aate team kii asafalataa ke kaaran captaini bill lorie ko de di gai thi.
 jab test cricket ko 1977 main world series cricket ne tod diya tha, tab simpasan ne 41 saal kii umr main new south wales or australia ke captain ke rup main sevanivritti ke ek dashak ke baad vaapasi kii. jeff thomson ke alaava australia ke sabhi pehali pasand khilaadiyon ne dal badal kar liya tha. bob ne bhartiya daure par team kii captaini kii. australia ne test shrinkhalaa 3-2 se jiti. bob ne 53.90 ke ausat se 539 ran banaae or chaar wicket liye. iske baad unhone westeindies ke daure par australia kaa netritv kiya jo us samay vishwa kii sabase majboot team thi. unke liye yah niraashajanak series rahi. unhone 22.11 kii ausat se 199 ran banaae or 52.28 kii ausat se saat wicket liye. westeindies ne shrinkhalaa se 3-1 se jiti. agali shrinkhalaa ke liye australian cricket board ne unhen baahar kar diya or graham yalope ko captain ke rup main sthapit kiya.
 phir 1986 main simpasan ne australian team ke coach ke rup main padbhaar sambhala jisapar vah 1996 tak rahen.. unhone captain alan border ke saath milakar kamjor australian team ko majboot team main badal diya. coach ke rup main team kii sabase badi uplabdhiyon main se kuch hai: 1987 vishwa cap jeetna, 1989 main england main da ashes ko phir se hasil karnaa or 1995 main apane gharelu maidan par purv main haaavee westeindies ko haraana.
 kul milakar unhone 62 test match khelen jisamen 10 shatak lagaakar 46.81 kii ausat se 4,869 ran banaae. unhone 42.26 kii ausat se 71 wicket bhi liye. pratham shrenee cricket ke 257 match main bob ne 56.22 kii ausat se 21,029 ran banaae. jisamen 60 shatak or 100 ardh-shatak shaamil he or 349 wicket shaamil he. bob ucch koti ke kshetrarakshak bhi the. unhone test main 110 or pratham shrenee main 383 kaich liye.
