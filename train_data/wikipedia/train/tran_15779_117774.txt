 emmersan nangagwayammerson Mnangagwa
 
 emmersan nangagwa zimbabwe ke raashtrapati hai. is pad par unhone robert mugabe kaa sthaan liya. ullekhneey he ki 22 november, 2017 ko zimbabwe kii sattarudh party zaanu-pief party ke adhyaksh pad se hataae jane ke baad robert mugabe ne raashtrapati pad se istifa de diya. ve purv main zimbabwe ke up raashtrapati rah chuke hai.
