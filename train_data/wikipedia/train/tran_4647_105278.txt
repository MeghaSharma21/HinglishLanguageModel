 khasia yaa khas kumayun or tehari gadhwal or pure nepal main basanevaali ek jati jo kadachit us pracheen khas logon kii vanshaj he, jiskaa ullekh mahabharat, puraana evam anya sahitya main miltaa he. prachinkaal main khason ne kashmir kii seemaa se lekar nepal paryant himalay ke nichle bhaag par adhikaar kar liya tha. us kaaran kumayun pradesh ko 'khas desh' bhi kehete he. mul khas or anya sthaneey logon ke parsparik saamaajik aadan pradaan ke falswaroop khasia jati ne rup dhaaran kiya. khas rajput logonen uttrakhandamein shashan kiya hua itihaas hai. 
 katyuri vansh ke raajaaon ne is pradesh ke apane samgr rajya main baahar se brahman or kshatriyon ko laakar basaaya tha. unka sthaneey khasia logon ke saath jab roti-beti kaa vyavhaar aarambh hua to unamem khas brahman or khas kshatriya ke rup main do bhed ho gaye.
