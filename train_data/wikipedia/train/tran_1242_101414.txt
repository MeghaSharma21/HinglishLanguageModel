 bangdarshan ek bangla kii masik saahitiyik patrika thi jise bankim chandra chattopadhyaay ne san 1872 main prakashit karnaa armbh kiya. april, san 1876 tak ve hi is patrika ke sampadak rahe. isaki adhikansh rachanaayen ve swayan likhte the. vande maataram sabase pehle isi patrika main chapa tha, baad main anand math main aayaa.
