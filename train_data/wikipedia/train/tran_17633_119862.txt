 professor yashpal bhartiya shikshavid va vaigyanik the. 
 unka janm 26 november, 1926 ko jhang main hua tha. unhone punjab vishvavidhyalay se 1949 main bhautik vigyaan main snatakottar tathaa 1958 main masachusats institute of technology se bhautikee main hi piechdi kii oopaadhi praapt kii thi.
 yashpal ne apane karier kii shuruuaat mumbai sthit tata moolbhoot anusandhan santhaan se shuru kiya tha. varsh 1973 main ve centre sarkaar dwaara ahmedabad sthit antariksh anuprayog centre ke pehle nideshak niyukt hue the. ve 1983 se 1984 tak yojana ayog main mukhya salaahkaar va 1984 se 1986 tak vigyaan va taknalaji vibhaag main sachiv ke rup main apni sevaayen de chuke hai. ve varsh 1986 se 1991 tak vishvavidhyalay anudaan ayog ke adhyaksh the. or varsh 2007-12 tak ve jawaharlal nehru vishvavidhyalay ke kulapati rahe. ve doordarshan par atyant charchit vigyaan kaarykram terning point main bhagidaari va vigyaan ko saadhaaran shabdon main aam janata tak pahunchane ke prayaason ke kaaran lokapriy rahe the. ve bharat kii chhap jaise tivi ke vigyaan kaaryakramon ke salaahkaar mandal main bhi shaamil rahe the.
 yashpal ko bharat sarkaar dwaara san 1976 main vigyaan evam abhiyantriki ke kshetra main padam bhushan tathaa varsh 2013 main padam vibhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. iske alaava unhen varsh 2009 main laal bahadur shastri rashtriya puraskaar evam kaling puraskaar se bhi sammanit kiya gaya tha.
