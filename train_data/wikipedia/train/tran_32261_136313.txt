 saura mandal main surya or vah khagoleey pind sammalit hai, jo is mandal main ek duusare se gurutvkarshan bal dwaara bandhe hai. kisi taare ke ird gird parikrama karte hui un khagoleey vastuon ke samooh ko grahiy mandal kaha jaataa he jo anya taare na hon, jaise kii grah, boune grah, prakritik upgrah, kshudragrah, ulka, dhoomaketu or khagoleey dhul. hamaare suraj or uske grahiy mandal ko milakar hamaara saura mandal banataa he. in pindon main aath grah, unke 166 gyaat upgrah, paanch boune grah or arabon chote pind shaamil hai. in chote pindon main kshudragrah, barfila kyper gheraa ke pind, dhoomaketu, ulkayen or grahon ke bich kii dhul shaamil hai.
 saura mandal ke chaar chote aantarik grah budh, shukr, prithvi or mangal grah jinhen sthaliy grah kaha jaataa he, mukhyatya pathar or dhaatu se bane hai. or isamen kshudragrah gheraa, chaar vishaal gas se bane bahari gas daanav grah, kyper gheraa or bikharaa chakra shaamil hai. kaalpanik aurt badal bhi sanadi kshetron se lagbhag ek hajaar guna duuri se pare maujuud ho saktaa he.
 surya se hone vaala plasma kaa pravaah saura mandal ko bhedata he. yah taare ke bich ke madhyam main ek bulbula banaata he jise heliomandal kehete hai, jo isse baahar fail kar bikhri hui tashtaree ke bich tak jaataa he.
 kuch ullekhneey apwaadon ko chhod kar, maanavtaa ko saura mandal kaa astitv jaanane main kai hajaar varsh lag gaye. log sochate the ki prithvi, brahmand kaa sthir centre he or akaash main ghoomane vaali divya yaa vivy vastuon se spasht rup main alag he. lekin 140 i. main cladius tolmi ne bataaya kii prithvi brahmand ke centre main he or saare grih pind isaki parikrama karte hai lekin copernicus ne 1543 main bataaya kii surya brahmand ke centre main he or saare grih pind isaki parikrama karte hai.
 soramandal surya or uski parikrama karte grah, kshudragrah or dhoomaketuon se bana he. iske kendra main surya he or sabase bahari seemaa par varun he. varun ke pare yama jaise boune graho ke atirikt dhoomaketu bhi aate he.
 surya ke centre se chuni hui nikaayon kii shrinkhalaa. 
 surya athwa suraj saura mandal ke kendra main sthit ek G shrenee kaa mukhya-anukram tara he jiske ird-gird prithvi or soramandal ke anya avyav ghoomte hai. surya hamaare saura mandal kaa sabase bada pind he, jisamen hamaare puure saura mandal kaa 99.86% dravyaman nihit he or uskaa vyas lagbhag 13 lakh 90 hazaar kilometre he, jo prithvi se lagbhag 109 guna adhik he. urja kaa yah shaktishaalee bhandar mukhya rup se hydrogen or helium gaison kaa ek vishaal gola he. parmaanu vilay kii prakriya dwaara surya apane centre main urja paidaa karata he. surya se nikali urja kaa chota sa bhaag hi prithvi par pahunchataa he jisamen se 15 pratishat antariksh main paravartit ho jaataa he, 30 pratishat pani ko bhaap banane main kaam aata he or bahut si urja ped-paudhe samudr sokh lete hai.
 soramandal saura vaayu dwaara banaae gaye ek bade bulbule se ghira hua he jise hiliosphior kehete he. is bulbule ke andar sabhi padaarth surya dwaara utsarjit hai. atyant zyaada urja vaale kan is bulbule ke andar hiliosphere ke baahar se pravesh kar sakate he.
 yah kisi taare ke bahari vaataavaran dwaara utsarjan kiye gaye aaweshit kanon kii dhara ko saura vaayu kehete hai. saura vaayu visheshkar atyadhik urja vaale electron or proton se bani hoti he, inaki urja kisi taare ke gurutv prabhaav se baahar jane ke liye paryaapt hoti he. saura vaayu surya se har disha main pravaahit hoti he jisaki gati kuch sau kilometre prati second hoti he. surya ke sandarbh main ise saura vaayu kehete he, anya taron ke sandarbh main ise brahmand vaayu kehete he.yama se kaaphi baahar saura vaayu khagoleey madhyam ke prabhaav se dhimi ho jaati he. yah prakriya kuch charnon main hoti he. khagoleey madhyam or saare brahmand main failaa hua he. yah ek atyadhik kam ghanatv vaala madhyam he.saura vaayu super sonic gati se dhimi hokar sab-sonic gati main aane vaale charan ko termineshan shock yaa samapti sadma kehete he.sab-sonic gati par saura vaayu khagoleey madhyam ke pravaah ke prabhaav main aane se dabaav hota he jisase saura vaayu dhoomaketu kii poonch jaisi aakriti banaati he jise heliosyth kehete he.heliosyth kii bahari sataha jahaan hiliosphior khagoleey madhyam se miltaa he hiliopaj kehalaati he.heliopaj kshetra surya ke akashganga ke kendra kii parikrama ke dauraan khagoleey madhyam main ek halchal utpann karata he. yah khalabali vaala kshetra jo heliopaj ke baahar he bo-shaak yaa dhanu sadma kehalaata he.
 grahiy mandal usee prakriya se banate hai jis se taron kii sristi hoti he. aadhunik khagolashastra main mana jaataa he ke jab antariksh main koi anuon kaa badal gurutvkarshan se simtane lagata he to vah kisi taare ke ird-gird ek aadigrah chakra bana detaa he. pehle anu jamaa hokar dhul ke kan bana dete hai, phir kan milakar dale ban jate hai. gurutvkarshan ke lagaataar prabhaav se, in dalon main takaraav or jamaavade hote rahete hai or dhire-dhire malbe ke bade-bade tukade ban jate hai jo vaqt se saath-saath grahon, upgrahon or alag vastuon kaa rup dhaaran kar lete hai. jo vastuen badi hoti hai unka gurutvkarshan taaqatvar hota he or ve apane-aap ko sikodkar ek gole kaa aakaar dhaaran kar leti hai. kisi grahiy mandal ke srijan ke pehle charnon main yah grah or upgrah kabhi-kabhi aapas main takaraa bhi jate hai, jis se kabhi to vah khandit ho jate hai or kabhi judakar or bade ho jate hai. mana jaataa he ke hamaari prithvi ke saath ek mangal grah jitani badi vastu kaa bhayankar takaraav hua, jis se prithvi kaa bada sa satahi hissaa ukhadkar prithvi ke ird-gird parikrama grahapath main chalaa gaya or dhire-dhire judakar hamaara chandramaa ban gaya.

 kshudragrah gheraa yaa astaroed balt hamaare saura mandal kaa ek kshetra he jo mangal grah or brihaspati grah kii grahapathon ke bich sthit he or jisamen hazaron-lakhon kshudragrah suraj kii parikrama kar rahe hai. inamen ek 950 kimi ke vyas vaala siris naam kaa bouna grah bhi he jo apane swayan ke gurutvkarshak khichaav se gol akar paa chukaa he. yahan teen or 400 kimi ke vyas se bade kshudragrah paae ja chuke hai - vasta, palace or higia. puure kshudragrah ghere ke kul dravyaman main se aadhe se zyaada inhi chaar vastuon main nihit he. baaqi vastuon kaa akar bhinn-bhinn he - kuch to dasiyon kilometre bade hai or kuch dhul ke kan maatr hai.
 2008 ke madhya tak, paanch chote pindon ko boune grah ke rup main vargeekrut kiya jaataa he, siris kshudragrah ghere main he or varun se pare chaar surya grahapath - yama, haumeya, makemake or siris.
 chah grahon or teen boune grahon kii parikrama prakritik upgrah karte hai, jinhen aam taur par prithvi ke chandramaa ke naam ke aadhaar par "chandramaa" hi pukaara jaataa he. prayek bahari grah ko dhul or anya kanon se nirmit chhallon dwaara parivruth kiya jaataa he.
