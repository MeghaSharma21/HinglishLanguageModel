 ranadhambor rashtriya udyaan uttar bharat ke bade udyanon main se ek he. yah jaipur se 130 kilometre dakshin or kota se 110 kilometre uttar-purv main rajasthan ke dakshini jile savai madhopur main sthit he. iskaa nikatatam railway stesan or kasbaa savai madhopur yahan se 11 kilometre duuri par sthit he.
 san 1973 main 1113.364 varg kimi ke kshetra ko project tiger ke antargat ranthambor vyaghr sangrakshit kshetra ghoshit kiya gaya. baad main san 1980 main iske 392 varg kimi ke ilaake ko rashtriya udyaan ghoshit kiya gaya.
