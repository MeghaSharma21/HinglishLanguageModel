 yajna main havan ke pashchaat havishya kii bachi buji hui bhasma sarvakaarya siddhi kaa saadhan maani gai he. isko puure sharir main lagaakar nahaane se swasthya miltaa he tathaa twachaa ke rogon se mukti hoti he. aisa bhi kaha gaya he ki yah asaadhya rogadi durghatnaon se peedtajanon ko akaal mrityu kaa bhay nahin hone detii. is bhasma ko bhasmi bhi kaha jaataa he. manusmriti main yajna ke saat prakaar bataae gaye hai. inamen se ek snaan bhasma se bhi kiya jaataa he. 
   k. ^ veda smriti main snaan bhi saat prakaar ke bataae gaye hai. mantra snaan, bhoum snaan, agni snaan, vivy snaan, divya snaan, karun snaan tathaa maansik snaan jo kramshah mantra, mitti, bhasma, gaukhur kii dhul, surya kirnon main, varshajal, gangaajal tathaa aatmachintan dwaara kiye jate hai.
