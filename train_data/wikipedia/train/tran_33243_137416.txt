 saasaani iran ke itihaas main sabase prabhaavshaalee shaskaon main se ek hai. inkaa sthaan iran main hakhaamani vansh ke baad sabase shashakt shaasha vanshon main gina jaataa he. inkaa udai iran ke dakshin main fars main hua tha - ye vahi rajya he jise hakhaamani shaskaon kaa mul sthaan mana jaataa he.
 april 224 main ek arsasid parivaar ke log shashakt ho gaye. par unako quzestan main lade ek yuddh main haraa kar ardashir naam ke ek vyakti ne apni prabhutaa sthapit kii. unhone apane ko hakhaamani vansh ke mul kaa bataaya. iske baad bane vansh ko saasaani kehete hai kyonki unka purvavarti saasaan tha.
