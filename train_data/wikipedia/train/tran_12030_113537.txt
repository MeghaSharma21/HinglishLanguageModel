 pelloponiz yunaan ke dakshini bhaag main sthit ek bada prayadweep he. iske or yunaan ke mukhya bhaag ke bich main kaurinth kii khadi aati he. osmani samrajya ke kaal main, jab yunaan par turki-kendrit osmanion kaa raaj tha, ve is prayadweep ko morea kaha karte the or yah naam abhi bhi anupachaarit rup se istemaal hota he. san 1893 main kaurinth nahar khodi gai jis se vastav main yah ek dweep ban gaya lekin aam taur par ise ab bhi prayadweep hi bulaayaa jaataa he.
 pelloponiz kaa kshetrafal lagbhag 21,550 varg kimi he. iske or yunaan ke baaqi mukhya bhaag ke bich main san 1893 main kaurinth nahar ban gai thi lekin us ke upar 2004 main rio-antirio pul taiyaar kar diya gaya. pelloponiz kaa andruni bhaag ek pahadiyon se bharaa kshetra he, jinamein tayegetas pahari shrunkhlaa sab se prasiddh he. isamen pelloponiz kaa sab se ooncha pahaad sthit he: 2,407 meter ooncha profitis iliaas shikhar . pelloponiz prayadweep ke swayan chaar prayadweep hai jo dakshin kii taraf ungliyon kii tarah faile hai: messinia, maani prayadweep, malea antareep or argolida . pelloponiz ke purvi tat se aage argo-saronic dweep samooh he or iske pashchimi tat se aage ionian dweep samooh he.
 pracheen yunaan main pelloponiz sparta naamak samskruti kaa ghar tha. unani itihaas main sparta or ethns kaa muqabla bahut mahatv rakhata he.
