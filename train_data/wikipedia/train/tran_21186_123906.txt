 yousuf meher ali ek swatantraaa senaanee or samaaj sudhaarak the. ve rashtriya milishia, bombai chuth lig or congress soshilist party main shaamil the. usne mazdoor or kisaan sanghathan ko majboot karaane main yogadan diye. usne swatantraaa sangram ke dauraan aath baar jail main jeena padaa. ve 1942 main lahore jail se bahaar aae or phir mumbai ke meyar ke rup main chune gayi. usne 'bharat chodo' andolan main bhi mahatvapurn yogadan diye.
