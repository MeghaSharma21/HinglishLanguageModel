 uchchhedwad aatma ke bhi nasht ho jane kaa siddhant he. pracheen kaal main ajit keshakambali ke siddhant ko uchchhedwad ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. is siddhant ke anusaar mrityu ke baad koi bhi padaarth sthaayee nahin rahata. sharirastha sabhi padaarthon ke asthayitv main vishwaas karanevaale is mat kii manyata thi ki mrityuparant prithvi, jal, tej or vaayu naamak chaar tatv apane mul tatv main leen ho jate hai. deh ke bhasma ho jane ke baad kuch bhi shesh nahin rahata, aatma bhi nahin. aatma kii satta mithyaa he. is siddhant kaa doosraa naam jadwaad bhi he. buddhakaal main iskaa virdohi mat shashwatvaad ke naam se prasiddh tha jo paanch tatwon ke saath hi sukh, du:kha evam aatma ko bhi nity evam achal maanata tha. yah mat prakrudh katyayan ke mat ke rup main vikhyath tha. buddha ne in donon anton kaa tyaag kar madhyam maarg kaa anusaran karne kaa upadesh diya tha. uchchhedwaadi siddhant pracheen bhautikwaad main mahatvapurn sthaan rakhata he.
 england ke vichaarak edward hwite ne bhi paashchaatya dhang se uchchhedwad siddhant kaa pratipaadan kiya he. iske anusaar kutil or paapi log mrityu ke saath purnat: vinasht ho jate hai, lekin sadaachari vyaktiyon ke saath aisa nahin hota. dharamshastra or nitishastra ke atirikt is siddhant main darshan kii peethika bhi gruhit he kyonki iske pratipaadan ke bich edward hwite ne kuch darshanik prashnon ko bhi uthaaya he. lekin darshan ke kshetra main is siddhant kaa vishesh mahatv nahin he.
