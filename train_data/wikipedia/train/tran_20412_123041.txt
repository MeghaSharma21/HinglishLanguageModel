 harry lamport ek america cartoonist or lekhak the.
 new york shahar main janme harry ne 16 varsh kii umr sahi kaam karnaa shuru kar diya tha, or unhone us samay popi da celler, beti boop or cocao da cloun jaise cartoon charitron kaa chithran kiya. 40 ke dashak main vah disi comics ke liye chithran karne lage, or unhone gardenar fox ke saath milakar flash kii rachana kii. iske atirikt unhone da king or atom jaise charitron ko bhi banaaya. 
 iske vaad vah new york times samet kai samachar patron ke liye cartoon banane lage. 1976 main ritair hone ke baad unhone kuch pustaken bhi likhi. 13 november 2004 ko florida main prostrate cancer ke kaaran unka nidhan ho gaya.
