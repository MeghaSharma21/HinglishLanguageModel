 Latin  = paranium
 maanav sharir main ke donon taangon ke bich ke trikon bhaag ko muladhar yaa paranium bhi kehete he. paranium bari maladwar ke aage tathaa janandwar ke peeche hoti he. isamen kuulhe ke nichle bhaga kii sabhi mansapeshiyaan aapas main milti he. yah prayek vyaktiyon main alag-alag hoti he. kisi main yah kamjor or kisi main yah adhik shaktishaalee hoti he. is prakaar se paranium janandwar ke peeche tathaa neeche kii diwaar ko sahaara diye rahati he. sambhog kriya ke samay paranium janandwar ko peeche kii ore se saadhe rahati he. purushon main ye andkosh or gudadwar ke bich hoti he. badhati aayu ke saath-saath yah kamjor ho jaati he. kuulhe ke charon ore kii mansapeshiyon ke yahaan ekatra hone ke kaaran yahaan kaa rog yaa pas chaaron ore fail saktaa he. bachche ke janm ke pehle janandwar ko yaheen se katkar chaudaa banaaya jaataa he. taaki bachche ke janm ke samay adhik se adhik sthaan praapt ho sake tathaa bachche ke janm ke liye adhik chid-faad na karnaa pade. prasav ke baad taanke inhen mansapeshiyon main lagaaye jate he jisko apigiotomi kehete he
