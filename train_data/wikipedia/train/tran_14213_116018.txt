 havelock dweep bharat ke andaman va nicobar dweepasmooh ke andaman dweepasmooh bhaag ke biihat andaman dwipon se purv main sthit richi dweepasmooh kaa sabase bada dweep he. yah dweep andaman va nicobar kii rajdhani port blair se 57 kimi purvottar main sthit he.
 havelock ke kai nivasee san 1971 ke bangladesh swadhinata yuddh ke dauraan aaye sharnaarthi or unke vanshaj hai, jinhen bharat sarkaar ne yahan basaaya tha. is dweep par paanch gaanv hai: govinda nagar, vijay nagar, shyaam nagar, krishna nagar or radha nagar. bharat sarkaar ne yahan paryaavarniya paryaton viksit karne kaa nirnay liya or ab yahan ke tat vishwaprasiddh ho chuke hai. time ne 2004 main chape ek ank main pashchimi tat ke tat nambar 7 asia kaa sarvottam tat ghoshit kiya tha.
