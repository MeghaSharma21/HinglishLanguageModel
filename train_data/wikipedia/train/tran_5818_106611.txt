 mahatma gandhi toun haul madhya bharat ke rajya madhya pradesh ke nagar indaur kii ek aitihaasik imaarat he. yah imaarat british kaal kii he or tab samay bataane kaa kaary bhi karti thi or ise ghantaghar bhi kehete the. is imaarat main varsh paryant vibhinn prakaar ke saanskrithik kaarykram yojit hote rahete hai. is imaarat kaa nirmaan 1904 main kiya gaya tha or tab iskaa naam king edward haul rakhaa gaya tha. san 1947 main bharat ke swatantra hone ke baad iskaa naam gandhi haal kar diya gaya. haul main bacchon ke liye park or ek pustkaalay bhi he.
 is bhawan kaa nirmaan angrej vaastukar steevenson ne kiya tha. bhawan ke upar raajputana style ke ghatak gumbad or minaaren hai. imaarat safed siwani or patan ke pattharon se indo-gothic style main bani he tathaa aantarik sajaavat plaster of paris se kii gayi he. iske farsh ko kaale or shwet sangemarmar se susajjit kiya gaya he tathaa isamen bich kii minar ytaakar bani he. is minar ke upree bhaag main chaaron ore ek ek ghadi lagi he. ye ghadiyaan itani bady hai ki dur se hi dikhaai detii hai, or isi kaaran se ise ghantaghar bhi kaha jaataa he. iske nirmaan ke samay isaki laagat dhai lakh rupye thi.
 bhawan kaa udghaatan november, 1905 main prince of wales dwaara bharat aagaman par kiya gaya tha.
