 zumil kii ladaai 633 isvi main ladi gai thi, jo ab iraq he. us kshetra kii vijay par yah ek pramukh muslim vijay thi. raat ke aavaran ke tahat muslim sena ne isai-arab sena ke teen alag-alag pakshon par hamalaa kiya. isai- arab balon ne muslimon ke ascharyajanak hamale kaa saamana karne main asamarth the or jald hi haar gayi yuddh ke maidan se bhaagane main vifal rahe or khalid ebn al walid kii sena ke teenon paksheeya hamale ke shikaar ban gaye. zumil main lagbhag puure isai arab senaco khalid ebn al walid ke kor dwaara bandhak bana liya gaya tha.
