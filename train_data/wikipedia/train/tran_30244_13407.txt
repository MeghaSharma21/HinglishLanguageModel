 nutrino yah ek nayaa kan he jiskaa sarvapratham avishkaar san 1930 main pauli ne kiya tha. is kan kaa pratham saidhantik aadhaar prasiddh bhautikeevid fermi ne san 1934 main batalaaya. 
 nutrino ke gun sankshep main nimnlikhit hai :
 un abhikriyaaon kii, jinase beta kirane milti he, jaanch karte samay yah dekha gaya ki nikle hue kanon kaa urja varnakram elfaa kiran ke urja varnakram se bhinn he. elfaa kirane pruthak rekha varnakram ke anusaar milti hai, par beta kirane unase purnat: bhinn prakaar ke santat varnakram kaa anukaran karti hai. radium-i ke liye praapt beta kiran kaa urja varnakram chitra main dikhaaya gaya he. beta kirnon kii urja kaa shunya se lekar adhiktam maan i a ke bich koi bhi maan ho saktaa he. aisa hi santat varnakram un abhikriyaaon main bhi miltaa he jinase pozitron praapt hote hai.
 beta kirnon dwaara die santat varnakram kaa saidhantik aadhaar sthir karnaa bahut samay tak kathin samasya bana rahaa. maan liya jaay ki kisi nabhik k se, jo ek vishesh urja ke tal par he, ek beta kiran nikalati he or is abhikraya dwaara ek doosraa nabhik kha banataa he, jo pun: ek vishesh urja ke tal par he. puj evam urjasthirataa ke siddhant: ke anusaar, nikle hue beta kan kii urja nabhik k evam kha ke urjatalon k antar ke baraabar honi chahie. yah urja siddhant sarvada E=mc2 ke tulya praapt hoti he. pariksha se kan shunaya se lekar E=mc2 tak sabhi maan kii urja lekar nikalate hai. aisa prathit haata he ki aisi abhikriyaaon main urja kaa kuch ansh lupt ho jaataa he or punj evam urja sthirataa ke siddhant kaa atikaraman hota he.
 is samasya ko fermi ne beta kiran ke baad vaali apni nutrino upakalpana dekar sarvapratham safaltaapurvak suljhaayaa. unhone yah suzav diya ki beta kiran dene vaali abhikriyaaon main ek or kan nutrino bhi praapt hota he or vahi lupt prathit hone vaali urja ko grahan kar letaa he. aaj tak pariksha se nutrino kii pahchaan nahin ho pai he, falat: iske gun aise hone chahie jinke kaaran isaki pahchaan ati kathin ho. isaliye yah dharna kii gai ki nutrino aavesh rahit he or iskaa bhaar electron kii tulana main atinune he, shunaya ke hi lagbhag he. nutrino kaa aaveshrahit hona, beta kiran kii abhikriya ke liye aaveshasthirataa ke siddhant ke anusaar he. nutrino pariklpana ke anusaar, beta kiran abhikriya main praapt hui urja kii maatra ir a he. yah urja beta kan nutrino evam pratikshipt nabhik ko praapt hoti he. teen kanon main urja vibhaajan anekanek bhanti ho saktaa he, isaliye santat varnakram ban jaataa he.
 jab ek nabhik se beta kiran praapt hoti he, tab nabhik ke aavesh kaa ikai dwaara parivartan hoti he, tab nabhik ke aavesh kaa ikai dwaara parivartan hota he, bhaar aparivartit rahata he. yadi ek electron praapt ho, to nabhik ke proton kii sankhya main ikai kii vruddhi hoti he tathaa clebanu ikai dwaara sankhya ikai dwaara kam ho jaati he. usee bhanti yadi beta kiran abhikriya main ek positron praapt ho to proton sankhya ikai dwaara kam tathaa cliban sankhya main ikai kii vruddhi hoti he. in beta roopantaron ko nimnlikhit dhang se spasht kiya ja saktaa he:
 in abhikriyaaon main neutron ko proton, electron evam nutrino se bana hua nahin mana gaya he. beta-utsarjan ke samay, neutron kaa teen kanon main tatkshan parivartan ho jaataa he. isi prakaar kaa nivartan beta +utsarjan main proton main ho jaataa he.
  evam samikarnon dwaara nutrino ke anya gunon ke bare main bhi soochna milti he. koniya gamata h/2 maan lee jane par hi uski sthirataa kaa niyam thik thik ghatit hota he. usee bhanti, sankhyikee ke bare main bhi soochna milti he. sameekaran evam main yadi sankhyikee kii sthirataa dekhi jaay, to yah niyam tabhi satya thahartaa he jab nutrino fermi-diraaq sankhyikee kaa anusaran kare.
 meson ke apkshay kii samasyaon ko hal karne ke liye bhi nutrino pariklpana kaa prayog kiya gaya. mu-meson jab ek electron main parivartit hota he tab beta-kiran abhikriya kii bhanti, mrami tathaa urja sthirataa ke niyam khandit ho jate hai. in niyamon kii satyata ke liye nimnlikhit vidhi batalaai gai :
 usee prakaar elfaa-meson apkshay nimnlikhit sameekaran dwaara dikhalaayaa ja saktaa he.
  evam samikarnon ke viruddh koi samparikshawya sakshya nahin he
 is bhanti nutrino dwaara beta kiran evam meson ke apkshya kii samasyaon kaa samaadhan hua he. is kan ke liye sabhi sakshya abhi tak apratyaksh hi hai.
