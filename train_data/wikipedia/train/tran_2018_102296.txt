 bharat kaa puranic itihaas sristi ke aarambh se lekar kaliyug main hue raajaaon evam mughal shashan tak kaa itihaas he jiskaa varnan veda vyas rachit vibhinn puraanon, ramayana aadi granthon main kiya gaya he. kaal ko satyayug, dwaparayug, tretayug or kaliyug sahit chaar bhaagon main vibhajit kiya gaya he. is itihaas main bhoot, vartmaan or bhavishya kaa gaurav kiya gaya he. puranic itihaas ke anusaar bharat kaa itihaas karodo varsho kaa he or atyant hi gauravpurn tha. bhashaa, samskruti kaa udgam bharat se hua tha or aadhyatmikta evam ucch aadarshon ke liye bharat ko janaa jaataa he. bhavishya puraana tathaa anya kai puraanon main mahabharat kaal se gupt kaal tak ke samast raajaaon kii soochi di gayi he, jiske aadhaar par kai vidwaanon ne bharat kaa puranic itihaas kram banaaya he. ramayana granth main shriram kaa itihaas or mahabharat main bharat main hue bheeshan mahayuddh kaa itihaas likhaa gaya he. donon grantho main ukt kaal main hue raja ke vansh kaa bhi varnan he or bhavishya puraana main bharat main mughal shashan kaal or baadshahon ke vansh kaa bhi varnan he.
