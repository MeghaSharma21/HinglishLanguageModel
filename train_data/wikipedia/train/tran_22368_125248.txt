 navachaar arthshaastra, vyapaar, takneekee evam samaaj shaastra main bahut mahatv kaa vishay he. navachaar kaa arth kisi utpaada, prakriya yaa seva main thoda yaa bahut bada parivartan laane se he. navachaar ke antargat kuch nayaa or upyogi tarika apanaaya jaataa he, jaise- nayi vidhi, nayi takanic, nayi kaary-paddati, nayi seva, nayaa utpaada aadi. navachaar ko arthtantr kaa sarathi mana jaataa he. 
 kisi sanstha ke sandarbh main navachaar ke dwaara dakshata, utpaadakta, gunavata, bazaar main pakad aadi ke sudhaar sammilit hai. aspataal, vishvavidhyalay, gram-panchayatein aadi sabhi santhaayen nawachaari ho sakati hai.
 jo santhaayen navachaar nahin kar paatin ve naash ko praapt hoti hai. unka sthaan navachaar main safal hui santhaayen le letin hai. navachaar main sabase mahatvapurn chunauti prakriya-navachaar tathaa utpaada-navachaar main saamanjasy baithaana hota he.
