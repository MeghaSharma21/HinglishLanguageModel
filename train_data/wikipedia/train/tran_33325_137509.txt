 roodhiwaad saamaajik vigyaan ke antargat vyavahrut ek aisi vichaardhaara he jo paaramparik manyataon kaa anukaran taarkikta yaa vaigyaniktaa ke sthaan par keval aasthaa tathaa prahanubhavon ke aadhaar par karti he. yah saamaajik, raajnneetik or naitik manyataon kaa samucchy he jo chirkaal se prachlit manyataon or vyavastha ke prati sammaan ko badhaava detii he. yah vichaardhaara nae or bina aazamaae hue vichaaron or sansthaaon ko apnaane ke bajaay puraane or aazamaae hue vichaaron or sansthaaon ko qaayam rakhane kaa samarthan karti he. david hum or edmund bark roodhiwaad ke pramukh unnayak maane jate hai. samakaleen vichaarkon main micel okshot ko roodhiwaad kaa pramukh siddhantkaar mana jaataa he.
