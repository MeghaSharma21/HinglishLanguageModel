 khokhla sing bhaaluu  brule suz janajati ke mul american adivaasiyon kaa ek netaa tha. 1866 or 1868 ke bich main usne sanyukt rajya america kii sena ke captain wiliam faterman ko haraa diya. 1870 ke dashak main usne sarkaar ke saath shaanti prastaav kii baaten kerani shuru kar deen. 1913 main raashtrapati vudro wilson se mulaakaat ke samay use nimonia ho gaya, jis kaaran uski maut ho gai.
