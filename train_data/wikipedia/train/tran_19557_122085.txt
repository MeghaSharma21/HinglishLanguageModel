 cubbon park or sangrahalay bangalore shahar ke madhya main sthit yah sundar park 1864 main bana tha. iskaa naam purv mukhya ayukta mark cubbon ke naam par rakhaa gaya he. saath hi aap park main bana sarkari sangrahalay dekhne yogya he. yahan hoyasla shilpakala, mohenjodaro se praapt pracheen tiron, sikkon or mitti ke paatro kaa avishwasaneey sangrah he.
