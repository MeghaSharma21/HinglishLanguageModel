 bhautik vigyaanon main pravastha se taatpary kisi ooshmaagatikeey pranali ke us prakshetra se he jisamen padaarth ke sabhi bhautik gun aparivartit rahete hai. ghanatv, paravartan gunank, rasinic sanrachana aadi bhautik gunon ke antargat aate hai. 
 kabhi-kabhi 'padaarth kii avasthaa' ko bhi 'padaarth kii pravastha' ke samanaarthi kii tarah prayog kar diya jaataa he. donon main bahut kuch samanta he kintu donon ek nahin hai. 'pravastha', 'avasthaa' kii apeksha adhik vyapak concept he. 
 ek udhaaharan se yah baat spasht ho jaayegi - kisi bartan main do drav bhare hai jo paraspar amishra hai . vartan main rakhaa padaarth 'drav' avasthaa vaala he ; kintu usamen do pravasthaayen hai kyonki pani kaa ghanatv evam anya gun tel se bhinn hai.
