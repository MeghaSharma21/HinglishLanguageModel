 nabhikeey abhiyantriki, engeeniri kii vah shakha he jo nabhik ke vikhandan evam sanlayan ke upyogon se sambandhit he. nabhikeey vikhandan ke antargat parmaanu riector, nabhikeey urja sanyantr, tathaa/yaa parmaanu hathiyaar aadi aate hai. isi ke antargat inkaree vikiran ke chikitsiya evam anya upyog, nabhikeey suraksha, ooshmaa kaa sancharan, nabhikeey eendhan, radioskriy kachare kaa niptaan, tathaa nabhikeey prachurodbhavan se sambandhit khatre aate hai.
