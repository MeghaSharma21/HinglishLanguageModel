 sharad kumaar ek purv bhartiya police seva adhikari hai, ve bharat ke kendriya satarkta ayukta hai tathaa purv rashtriya jaanch agency ke mahanideshak the. sevanivritti par ek varsh kii avadhi ke liye unhen usee pad par anubandhit kiya gaya tha. ek thekedaar ke rup main vah aal india sarvice ke niyam mandando ke baadhya nahin hai.
 sharad kumaar kaa janm uttar pradesh ke bareli jile main hua tha. ve vigyaan main snaatak hai. 
 1979 main kumaar ne bhartiya police seva ke haryana cader main shaamil hue. vah apni pichhali post main haryana main jail mahanideshak the. unhen gurugram, ambala, or rohtak main dspi ke rup main tainaat kiya gaya tha. lagbhag teen saal ke liye vah police mahanireekshak rohtak range main bane rahe or november 2007 main unhen atirikt mahanideshak police ke pad par padonnat kiya gaya. kumaar ko july 1991 se july 1999 tak espi or diaiji ke rup main kendriya jaanch beuro main tainaat kiya gaya.
