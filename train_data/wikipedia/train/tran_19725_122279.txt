 tigari gol chakkar ghaziabad or greater noida kaa border he jahaan se neshnal hive 24 gujara he. hive se greater noida or noida jane ke liye aasaan rasta he . yah rasta abhi 4 line kaa he or 8 line kaa banane kaa kaary pragati par he. yah gol chakkar pehla gol chakkar he jo taj hive ke raste main padataa he. neshnal hive 24 se is raste se taj nagari agra, aligarh, hathras, dadri, mathura, jane kaa sidhaa rasta he. jo aage ja kar yamuna expresve se miltaa he.
