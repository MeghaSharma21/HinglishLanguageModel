 vaishampayan veda vyas ke vidwaan shishy the. hinduon ke do mahakavyon main se ek mahabharat ko maanav jati main prachlit karne kaa shrey unheen ko jaataa he. pandavon ke pautra mahabali parakshit ke putr janamejay ko vaishampayan ne ek yajna ke dauraan yah katha sunai thi. krishna yajurveda ke pravartak bhi rishi vaishampayan hi he jinhe unke guru krishnadwaipayan ne yah karya saupa tha. inke shishy yajywalky rishi the jinase vaad vivaad hone ke kaaran yajyavlky ne shukla yajurveda ko prasaarit kiya|
