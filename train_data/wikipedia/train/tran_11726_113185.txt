 
 maharana kumbha yaa maharana kumbhkarna san 1433 se 1468 tak mewar ke raja the. maharana kumbhakarna kaa bharat ke raajaaon main bahut unchaa sthaan he. unase purv rajput keval apni swatantraaa kii jahaan-tahaan raksha kar sake the. kumbhakarna ne musalamaanon ko apane-apane sthanon par haraakar rajputi raajnneeti ko ek nayaa rup diya. itihaas main ye 'rana kumbha' ke naam se adhik prasiddh hai.
 maharana kumbha rajasthan ke shaskaon main sarvshereshth the. mewar ke aasapaas jo uddhat rajya the, un par unhone apana aadhipaty sthapit kiya. 35 varsh kii alpaayu main unke dwaara banawaye gaye battis durgon main chittorgarh, kumbhalgarh, achalgarh jahaan shashakt sthapaty main sheershasth hai, vahiin in parvat-durgon main chamatkrit karne vaale devalayas bhi hai. unki vijayon kaa gungan karata vishwavikhyath vijay stambh bharat kii amuly dharohar he. kumbha kaa itihaas keval yudhon main vijay tak simit nahin thi balki unki shakti or sanghathan kshamta ke saath-saath unki rachanaatmakataa bhi ascharyajanak thi. ‘sangeet raaj’ unki mahaan rachana he jise sahitya kaa kirti stambh mana jaataa he.
 maharana kumbhkarna, maharana mokal ke putr the or unki hatya ke baad gaddi par baithe. unhone apane pita ke mamaa ranmal rathod kii sahaayta se sheeghra hi apane pita ke hatyaron se badla liya. inke teen santaanen thi jisamen do putr udaa singh, rana rimal tathaa rana ek putree ramabai the | san 1437 se purv unhone devda chouhanon ko haraakar abu par adhikaar kar liya. malwa ke sultan mahmud khilji ko bhi unhone usee saal saarangpur ke paas buree tarah se haraaya or is vijay ke smarak swaroop chittod kaa vikhyath kirtistambh banwaaya. rathod kahin mewar ko hastgat karne kaa praytn na karen, is prabal andeh se shankit hokar unhone ranmal ko marava diya or kuch samay ke liye mondor kaa rajya bhi unke haath main aa gaya. rajarudh hone ke saat varshon ke bheetar hi unhone saarangpur, nagaur, narana, ajmer, mondor, modalgarh, bundi, khatu, chatus aadi ke sudrudh kilon ko jeet liya or dilli ke sultan saiyad mohammad shah or gujarat ke sultan ahmedshah ko bhi parrast kiya. unke shatruon ne apni paraajayon kaa badla lene kaa baar-baar praytn kiya, kintu unhen safalataa na mili. malwa ke sultan ne paanch baar mewar par akraman kiya. nagaur ke swami shams khan ne gujarat kii sahaayta se swatantra hone kaa vifal praytn kiya. yahi dasha abu ke devdon kii bhi hui. malwa or gujarat ke sulatanon ne milakar maharana par akraman kiya kintu musalamaani senaayen phir parrast hui. maharana ne anya anek vijay bhi praapt kiye. usne deedwanaa kii namak kii khan se kar liya or khandela, amer, ranthambor, duungarpur, sihare aadi sthanon ko jeeta. is prakaar rajasthan kaa adhikansh or gujarat, malwa or dilli ke kuch bhaag jitkar usne mewar ko maharajya bana diya.
 kintu maharana kumbhakarna kii mahatta vijay se adhik unke saanskrithik kaaryon ke kaaran he. unhone anek durg, mandir or talab banawaye tathaa chittod ko anek prakaar se susanskrit kiya. kumbhalgarh kaa prasiddh kila unki kriti he. bansatpur ko unhone pun: basaaya or shri ekling ke mandir kaa jirnondwar kiya. chittod kaa kirtistambh to sansar kii adwitiya kritiyon main ek he. iske ek-ek pathar par unke shilpanurag, vaidushya or vyaktitv kii chhap he. apni putree ramabai ke vivaah sthal ke liye chittod durg main   shringaar chanwari kaa nirmaan karaayaa tathaa chittod durg main hi vishnu ko samarpit kumbhashyam ji mandir kaa nirmaan karaayaa |
 mewar ke rana kumbha kaa sthapaty yug swarnakaal ke naam se janaa jaataa he, kyonki kumbha ne apane shasankaal main anek durgon, mandiron env vishaal rajprasadon kaa nirmaan karaayaa, kumbha ne apni vijayon ke liye bhi anek aitihaasik imaaraton kaa nirmaan karaayaa, vir-vinod ke lekhak shyaamaldas ke anusaar kumbha ne kul 32 durgon kaa nirmaan karaayaa tha jisamen kubhalgarh, alchagarh, machaan durg, bhausath durg, basantgadh aadi mukhya maane jate hai |
 ve vidyanuragi the, sangeet ke anek granthon kii unhone rachana kii or chandistak evam gitgovind aadi granthon kii vyakhya kii. ve naatyashaastra ke gyaata or veenavadan main bhi kushal the. kirtistambhon kii rachana par unhone swayan ek granth likhaa or mandan aadi sutaradhaaron se shilpashaastra ke granth likhwaaye. is mahaan rana kii mrityu apane hi putr uuda singh ke haathon hui.
