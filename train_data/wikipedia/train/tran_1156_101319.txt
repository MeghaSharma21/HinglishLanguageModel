 vijay manjrekar ek purv bhartiya cricket khilaadi the. inhone bhartiya cricket team ke liye varsh 1951-52 se 1965 tak test cricket khelaa tha. ye purv bhartiya cricket khilaadi sanjay manjrekar ke pita hai. sanjay manjrekar ne bharat ke liye 37 test or 74 ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket match khele the. vijay manjrekar mukhya rup se daahine haath ke ballebaaj the jinhone 55 test match khele the jisamen 3208 ran banaaye the.
 inhone bhartiya cricket team ke liye inhone 30 disambar 1951 ko test cricket main padaarpan kiya tha or vah match england cricket team ke khilaaf kolkata ke eden gardens maidan par khelaa gaya tha jisamen 48 ranon kii pari kheli thi. jabaki antim match inhone 27 farvari se 2 march 1965 ko newzealand cricket team ke khilaaf khelaa tha jisamen duusari main 102 ranon kii shatkeey pari kheli thi.
 vijay manjrekar kaa nidhan 52 saal kii umr main 18 aktubar 1983 ko chennai) tamilnadu main hua tha.
