 nirpeksh kantimaan kisi khagoleey vastu ke apane chamkilepan ko kehete hai. misaal ke liye agar kisi taare ke nirpeksh kantimaan kii baat ho rahi ho to yah dekha jaataa he ki yadi dekhne vaala us taare ke thik 10 parsac kii duuri par hota to vah kitna chamkila lagata. is tarah se "nirpeksh kantimaan" or "saapeksh kantimaan" main gaharaa antar he. agar koi tara suraj se bees guna zyaada mul chamak rakhata ho lekin suraj se hazaar guna dur ho to prithvi par baithe kisi darshak ke liye suraj kaa saapeksh kantimaan adhik hoga, haalaanki duusare taare kaa nirpeksh kantimaan suraj se adhik he.
 nirpeksh kantimaan or saapeksh kantimaan donon ko maapne kii ikai "magnitude" kehalaati he.
 angrezi main "nirpeksh kantimaan" ko "ebsolute magnitude" or "saapeksh kantimaan" ko "aparrant magnitude" kehete hai.
