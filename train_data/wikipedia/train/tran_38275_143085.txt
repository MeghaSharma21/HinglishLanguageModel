 tatva he jisase yatharth bana hua he. 'tatva' kaa shabdic arth he, vaastavik sthiti, yayarthata, vaastaviktaa, asaliyat. 'jagat kaa mul kaaran' bhi tatva kehalaata he.
 sankhya ke anusaar 25 tatva hai or shaiva darshan ke anusaar 36.
 sankhya main 25 tatva maane gaye hai—purush, prakruti, mahattatv, ahankaar, chakshu, karn, nasika, jihva, tvak, vaak, paani, paayu, paad, upsth, mel, shabd, sparsh, rup, ras, gandh, prithvi, jal, tej, vaayu or akaash. mul prakrutti se shosh tattvon kii utpatti kaa kram is prakaar he—prakruti se mahattatv, mahattatv se ahankaar, ahankaar se gyaarah indriyaan or paanch tanmaatr, paanch tanmatro se paanch mahabhoot . pralay kaal main ye sab tatva phir prakruti main kramshah villin ho jate hai.
 yoga main ishwar ko or milakar kul 26 tatva maane gaye hai. sankhya ke 'purush' se yoga ke ishwar main visheshta yah he ki yoga kaa ishwar klesh, karmvipak aadi se pruthak mana gaya he.
 vedantiyon ke mat se brahm hi ekmaatr parmarth tatva he.
 shoonyavaadee baudhon ke mat se shunya yaa abhaav hi param tatva he, kyon ki jo vastu he, vah pehle nahin thi or aage bhi na rahegi.
 kuch jain to jeev or ajeev ye hi do tatva maanate hai or kuch paanch tatva maanate hai—jeev, akaash, dharm, adharm, pudgal or astikaay.
 charvak ke mat main prithvi, jal, agni or vaayu ye hi tatva maane gaye hai or inhen se jagat kii utpatti kahi gai he.
 nyaay main 16, vaisheshik main 6, shaivadarshan main 36; isi prakaar anek darshanon kii bhinn bhinn manyataayen tatva ke sambandh main hai
