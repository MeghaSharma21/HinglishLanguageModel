 bhuvaigyanik plate vivartaniki main sanmilan seemaa vah seemaa hoti he jahaan prithvi ke sthalmandal ke do bhugolic takhte ek duusare kii ore aakar takaraate hai yaa aapas main ghiste hai. aise kshetron main dabaav or ragad se bhupraavaar kaa pathar pighalane lagata he or jwalamukhi tathaa bhukampan ghatnaon main se ek yaa donon maujuud rahete hai. sanmilan simaaon par yaa to ek takhte kaa chor duusare takhte ke neeche dabane lagata he yaa phir mahadwipiya takaraav hota he.
 yadi sanmilan seemaa par ek plate duusare ke neeche khiskkar dabane lage to is prakriya ko nimnaskhalan kehete he or us dabi hui plate ko nimnaskhalit plate kehete hai. is sthiti main upar chadhi hui plate ko uparaskhalit plate kehete hai. asia main bhartiya plate ke ureshian plate kii sanmilan seemaa par bhartiya plate ke nimnaskhalan se hi himalay va tibet ke pathaar kii ucchbhumi kaa nirmaan hua he.
 jab do takhte takaraate hai, or yadi ek mahasagariya ho or doosraa mahadwipiya ho, to mahadwipiya takhta aam taur par mahasagariya takhte se kam ghanaa hota he. isaliye, saamanytah mahasagariya takhta duusare takhte ke neeche duubataa he. aisi sthitiyon main ek ore to gaharaa mahasagariya gart ban jaataa he or duusari ore ek parvatamala ban jaati he. thik yahi prashanth mahaasaagar main mahasagariya nazaka plate ke mahadwipiya dakshin america plate ke saath bani sanmilan seemaa par hua he. atyant dabaav ke kaaran yahan pathar pighalkar lava ban jaataa he or baari maatra main gas bhi banati he. is karanvash in parvatamalaon ke kuch parvat vishwa ke sabase visphotak jwalamukhion main gine ja sakate he. dakshin america kii andes parvatamala ke sakriya jwalamukhi nazaka va dakshin america takhton main ho rahi isi aapasi prakriya kaa nateeja hai.
