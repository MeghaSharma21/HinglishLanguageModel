 shaikh saleem chishti kii samadhi bharat ke agra jile main nagar se 35 kilo meter dur fatehpur sikri shahar main, zanaanaa rauja ke nikat, dakshin main buland daravaaje kii ore mukh kiye huye, jami masjid kii bheetar sthit he. shaikh saleem chishti ek sufi sant the unhone akbar or uske bete ko aashirwad diya tha ki bhavishya main saleem, jahangir ke naam se pahachaanaa jayega. june 1573 main akbar ne gujarat vijay ke saath is kshetra ko bhi jeet liya to iskaa naam fatehpur sikri rakhaa gaya or akbar ne is samadhi kaa nirmaan sant ke sammaan main varsh 1580 or 1581 ke bich karavaaya. aaj yah samadhi vaastukala or dharm nirpekshata kaa anupam udhaaharan he jahaan iske darshanath vibhinn samudaayon ke log aate hai. 
 samadhi lagbhag 1 meter unche manch par nirmit he jiskaa pravesh dwaar paanch seedhi upar he. mukhyadwar par chaar khambhe hai jinase paaramparik gujarati style ke atyant kalatmak sarpakar shilp upar jate hue chat ko sahaara dete hai. inke bich nirmit jaali main jyamitiya aakaaron ke saath belbooton kii aakarshak nakkashi he. khambhon ke neeche ke bhaag ghante kii bhaanti lambaakar hai jabaki upree sire par ardhachakra banaaya gaya he. mukhya samadhi ke chaaro ore sangemarmar kii jaali lagaai gai he, jo baarik kaam ke kaaran dekhne main hathidanth ke samaan lagati he. samadhi arddh golakaar gumbad vaale mukhya kaksh main sthit he jisaki ashtakomi kursi kaale or pile rang ke mojaik sangemarmar se bani he. is par ek aabnoos chhatra sanlagn he, jo aamtaur par hare kapade kaa hota he. mukhya kaksh kaa darwaja jatil arabesk aadhaar par bana he or is par quran se chand likhe gayi hai.
