 om thanvi hindi bhashaa ke ek lekhak, varisht patrkaar, sampadak tathaa aalochak hai. thanvi vartmaan main patrika samachar patr samooh ke salaahkaar sampadak hai or isse purv hindi ke pramukh dainik akhbaar jansatta ke sampadak rah chuke hai. inaki charchit kriti sansmaran vidha main likhi gayi kitaab "muanjodaro" he.
 om thanvi kaa janm 1 agast 1957 ko rajasthan ke jodhpur jile ke falodi naamak qasbe main hua. inke pita ek shikshak the.
 thanvi nipani padhai puri karne ke baad, 1978 main rajasthan ke jaipur se nikalne vaale patrika samooh ke saptahik patr 'itavaari' se apane patrkaar jivan kii shuruuaat kii. iske baad ve is 'itavaari' naamak patr ke sampadak bhi bane. saal 1980 se 1989 tak inhone rajasthan patrika main kaary kiya. iske baad ye express samooh ke hindi dainik jansatta se jude or bataur patrkaar, sthaneey sampadak, or sampadak kii bhoomikaaon kaa nirvaah karte hue agle chhbbees varshon tak  is akhbaar se jude rahe.
 jansatta se alag hone ke baad thanvi ne desh ke prasiddh vishvavidhyalay jawaharlal nehru vishvavidhyalay main visiting profesar ke rup main centre for media studies main adhyaapan kaa kaary bhi kiya.
 sahitya, kala, cinema, paryaavaran, puraatatva, sthapaty or yatraaon main thanvi kii gahan ruchi he or ye mexico, misr, canada, sanyukt rajya america, england, japan, france, germany, italy, spain, switsraland, austria, netherland, finland, sweden, poland, check ganraajya, belgium, romania, thailand, armenia, belarus, china, brazil, malaysia, tunisia, morocco, peru, kenya, ithiopia, singapore, guyana, trinidad evam tobago, surinam, srilanka, afghanistan, pakistan, bangladesh, turki, greece or cuba aadi anek deshon kii yatraaen kar chuke hai.
 patrakarita ke liye thanvi ko bharat ke raashtrapati dwaara sammanit kiya ja chukaa he. inaki prasiddh kriti 'muanjodaro' ke liye inhen shamsher sammaan, saarc litereri eward, tathaa bihari puraskaar praapt hua.
