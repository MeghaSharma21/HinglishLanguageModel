 nilimaa joglekar  ek purvabhartiya mahila cricket khilaadi he jinhone apana pehla antarrashtriya str kaa match 1978 main khelaa tha. ye ek madhyam kram kii ballebaaj or wicket-keeper thi. nilimaa gharelu cricket maharashtra kii ore se khelaa karti thi.
 nilimaa joglekar ne apane cricket karier main kul 6 test cricket match or 20 ek divasiy antarrashtriya match khele the. inhone nimnaankit shrinlaaon main match khele :-
 nilimaa joglekar ne bharat kii ore se ekmaatr test match main captaini kii thi jo newjiland mahila cricket team ke kilaaf khelaa gaya tha.
