 gutenberg bible aadhunik dhang ke chhapakhane se mudrit hone vaali duniya kii pehali bible thi. 23 agast, 1456 ko iskaa prakaashan germany ke mins shahar main aadhunik dhang kaa duniya kaa pehla chhapamshin banane vaale german vaigyanik yohaness gutenberg dwaara kiya gaya tha. gutenberg ne 380 isvi ke ek latin anuwaad se yah bible safed kaagaj par kaale aksharon main chhaapi thi. ise 42 panktiyon vaali bible, mazaarin bible yaa bi42 bhi kaha jaataa he. isaki teen sau pratiyaan chhapakar europe ke paris sahit vibhinn shaharon main bheji gai thi. 1847 i. main america james lenaa ke saath isaki ek prati america pahaunchi jo ab neuyork public librari main rakhi gai he.
