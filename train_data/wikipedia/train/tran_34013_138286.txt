 kannada sahitya sammelan kannada sahityakaron, lekhakon tathaa cananadigru logon kaa sammelan he. iskaa lakshay kannada bhashaa, kannada sahitya, kala, sangeet or samskruti kaa vikaas karnaa he. iskaa armbh 1915 main ech ve nanjunaiya ne kiya tha. pehla sammelan bangaleru main hua tha. 1948 tak iskaa udghaatan kisi suprasiddh kavi yaa lekhak dwaara kiya jaataa tha kintu 1948 ke baad se karnataka ke mukhyamantri iskaa udghaatan karte hai. is sammelan kaa aayojan kannada sahitya parishad karti he.
