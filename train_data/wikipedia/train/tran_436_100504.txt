 shem angreji sahityakar salmaan rushdi dwaara midnites children ke baad likhaa gaya teesraa upanyaas he. 1983 main prakashit yah upanyaas unki adhikansh rachanaaon kii tarah hi jaadui yatharthwaad kii style main likhaa gaya he. kaa prayog kiya gaya he. is main zulfikaar ali bhutto or general mohammad zia-ul-haq ke jivan or unke sambandhon kaa chithran kiya gaya he. upanyaas kii kendriya vishaywastu bataati he ki sharam se hi hinsa paidaa hui he. sabhi charitron ke jariye shame or besharmi kii soch kii padtaal milti he kintu drushti ke centre main sufia zinobia or umar khaiyyaam hai. iskaa hindi anuwaad lalit kartikeya ne sharam naam se kiya he.

 upanyaas kaa kathanak 'kyuu' sthaan ke aasapaas rachaa gaya he jo queta, pakistan kaa ek kaalpanik sanskaran he. 'kyuu' main teen bahanen ek hi saath umr khayyam shakeel ko janm dene kaa naatak karti he.vastav main teenon ko hi nahin pataa he ki umr kii asli maan koun he yaa ki uskaa baap koun he. ve teenon hi ek gharelu jalse ke dauraan garbhvatee ho gai thim. dhire-dhire umr bada hota he to usamen natkhtapan badhata jaataa he or vah sammohan kii kala bhi sikh letaa he. umr ko uske janmdin ke avasar par uski teenon maaon kii taraf se 'kyuu' se baahar jane kaa mauka miltaa he jo uske liye ek tohfe kii tarah tha. uskaa namakan paas ke ek skool main kara diya jaataa he jahaan par vah apane adhyaaapak ke sampark main aata he or doctor banane ke liye prerit hota he. aage chalakar umr kaa sampark iskundar hadappa or raza haidar se hota he jinka uske jivan par ullekhneey prabhaav padaa.
