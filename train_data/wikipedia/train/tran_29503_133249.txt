 talvender singh parmaar  kaa janm pansata, kapoorthala, punjab, bharat main hua tha. vah sikh rajput samudaay kaa the.
 parmaar ne 1979 main bharat ke baahar canada ke vainkuvar se babbar khalsa international ko ladane main santhaapit kiya. parmaar babbar khalsa kaa samgr adhyaksh tha. jabaki sukhdev singh babbar keval bharat kaa adhyaksh tha. parmaar baad main ek prakritik canadai naagarik ban gaya. air india flite 182 ke bam visphot kii jaanch main jaanch ayog ne nishkarsh nikaala ki talvinder singh parmaar, haalanki kabhi doshi nahin hue, 1985 main air india kii udaanon par hamalaa karne kii sajeesh kaa netaa tha. 15 oktober 1992 ko punjab police dwaara police muthbhed main use maar giraaya gaya tha. is ghatana kaa vivaran vivaadit he.
 july 2007 main, jaanch patrika tahalaka ne bataaya ki parmaar ne apni mrityu se pehle poochtacha ke dauraan punjab police kii sari bate kabuuli thi. usne kaha tha ki usne jaranel singh bhindrawaale ke bhattije lakhbeer singh rhode ko dynamite kii aapurti kii thi, jo air india flite 182 ke bam visphot ke peeche mastermind the.
