 randle kith "randi" ortan ek america peshewar pahalwaan va abhinayta he, jo peshewar kushti company dablyoodablyoo se anubandhit he. ve 13-baar ke vishwa heviveet championships he, unhone aath baar dablyoodablyoo championships or chaar baar world heviveet championships jiti he. dablyoodablyoo championships se ekikrut hone se pehle ve hin world heviveet championships ke antim dharak rahe he ab yah tital dablyoodablyoo world heviveet championships ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
 ortan teesri peedi ke peshewar pahalwaan he unase pehle unke dada bob ortan senior, pita couboy bob ortan or chachaa bairi ortan sabhi peshewar kushti main haath aajamaa chuke he.
 ortan kaa janm 1980 me peshewar pahalwaan bob ortan va unki patni ellen ke ghar hua. unke dada bob ortan senior or chachaa bairi ortan bhi peshewar pahalwaan the. ortan ne hejalwood haai skool se shiksha praapt kii jahaan ve pahalwaan bhi the.
 navambar 2005 main, ortan ne apni garlfrand samantha spano ke saath sagaai kii ghoshnaa kii, 21 sitambar, 2007 ko dono ne vivaah kar liya. july 12, 2008 ko unke ghar ek beti kaa janm hua jiske naam kaa tatu randi ne apane baayein haath par banwaaya. san 2013 main spano se talaak lene ke baad ortan ne july 2015 main kim mairi kesalar se sagaai kii ghoshnaa kii.
 san 1998 me snaatak kii padhai puri karne ke pashchaat ve america marine main shaamil hue par unke abhadra vyavhaar ke kaaran unka court marshall kar diya gaya or 38 din kii kaid fouj kii jail me gujaarni paddy.
 san 2000 main oviablyoo par kushti kaa prashikshan praapt kiya.
 randi ne mukhya bhaag kii shuruuaat legend killer ke rup me kii jo dablyoodablyoo ke mahaan pahalawaano ko haraakar unhe kuchal detaa he.
