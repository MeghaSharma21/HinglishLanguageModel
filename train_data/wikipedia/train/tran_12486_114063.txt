 amaar shonar bangla, bangladesh kaa raashtragan he, jise gurudev rabindranath tagore ne likhaa tha. yah bangla bhashaa main he. gurudev ne ise bang bhang ke samay san 1906 main likhaa tha jab majahab ke aadhaar par angrejon ne bengal ko do bhaagon main baant diya tha. yah geet bengal ke ekikaran ke liye maahaul banane ke liye likhaa gaya tha. 
 swatantra hone ke baad bangladesh ne san 1972 main is geet kii pratham das panktiyon ko raashtragan ke rup main sweekaar kiya.

 amar shonar bangla,aami tomaae bhaalobashi.
 meraa sone jaisaa bengal,main tumse pyaar karata hoon.

 chirodin tomaar akaash,tomaar bataash,amaar praane bajaae baashi.
