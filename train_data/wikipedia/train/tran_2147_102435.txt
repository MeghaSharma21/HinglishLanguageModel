 cheharaa kai praaniyon ke sir ke saamne vaali ore paaya jane vaala bhaag he jisamen kai gyanendriyaan upasthit hoti hai, haalanki har praani kaa cheharaa nahin hota. stanadhariyon main aamtaur par mukh par naak, kaan, munh or ankhen hoti hai. maanav va anya standhari inase apni bhavanaayen bhi prakat karte hai, tathaa ise ek-duusare ko pahchaanane va maukhik sanchaar ke liye bhi prayog karte hai.
