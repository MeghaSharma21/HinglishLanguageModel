 trishshur kerala ke saanskrithik rajdhani ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. ise pehle 'trichur' ke naam se janaa jaataa tha.
 iskaa shabdic arth hota he bhagwaan shiv kaa vasasthaan 
 trishshur kaa sandhiviched he: trishshur = thiru + shiv + ur 
 thiru ek tamil aadarsuchak shabd he jiskaa hindi samaanaantar he shri . ur kaa matlab hota he poor. trishshur shri shiv ke mandir ke naam se prasiddh he.
