 upanadi yaa sahaayak nadi aise jharne yaa nadi ko bolte hai jo jaakar kisi mukhya nadi main vilay ho jaati he. upanadiyaan sidhi kisi sagar yaa jhil main jaakar nahin milteen. koi bhi mukhya nadi or uski upanadiyaan ek jalsambhar kshetra banati hai jahaan kaa pani upanadiyon ke zariye mukhya nadi main ekatrit hokar phir sagar main vilay ho jaataa he. iskaa ek udhaaharan yamuna nadi he, jo ganga nadi kii ek upanadi he. prayag main vilay ke baad yamuna kaa pani ganga main mil jaataa he or us se aage sirf mukhya ganga nadi hi chalati he.
 'upanadi' ko angrezi main 'tribuitari' kehete hai. unani bhashaa main 'upanadi' ko 'parapotamos' kehete hai.
