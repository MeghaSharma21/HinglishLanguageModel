 swastik mantra yaa swasti mantra shubh or shanti ke liye prayukt hota he. swasti = su + asti = kalyaan ho. aisa mana jaataa he ki isse hriday or man mil jate hai. mantrocchar karte hue darbh se jal ke chhinte daale jate the tathaa yah mana jaataa tha ki yah jal parsparik krodh or vaimnasy ko shant kar rahaa he. swasti mantra kaa paath karne kii kriya 'swastivachan' kehalaati he. 
 gruhanirmaan ke samay swastik mantra bolaa jaataa he. makaan kii neenv main ghi or dugdh chhidaka jaataa tha. aisa vishwaas he ki isse grihaswami ko dudharu gaayen praapt heti hai evam grihapatni vir putr utpann karti he. khet main beej daalate samay mantra bolaa jaataa tha ki vidyut is ann ko kshati na pahunchaae, ann kii vipul unnati ho or fasal ko koi keedaa na lage. pashuon kii samruddhi ke liye bhi swastik mantra kaa prayog hota tha jisase unamem koi rog nahin failata tha. gaayon ko khub santaanen hoti thim.
 yatra ke aarambh main swastik mantra bolaa jaataa tha. isse yatra safal or surakshit hoti thi. maarg main hinsak pashu yaa chor or daaku nahin milte the. vyapaar main laabh hota tha, achche mausam ke liye bhi yah mantra japa jaataa tha jisase din or raatri sukhad hon, swasthya laabh ho tathaa kheti ko koi haani na ho.
 putrajanm par swastik mantra bahut aavashyak maane jate the. isse baccha swasth rahata tha, uski aayu badhati thi or usamen shubh gunon kaa samavesh hota tha. iske alaava bhoot, pishaach tathaa rog uske paas nahin aa sakate the. shodash sanskaron main bhi mantra kaa ansh kam nahin he or yah sab swastik mantra hai jo shariraraksha ke liye tathaa sukhprapti evam ayuvruddhi ke liye prayukt hote hai.
