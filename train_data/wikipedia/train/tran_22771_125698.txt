 railway platform rail kii patariyon kii disha main sthit bhaag hota he, yah railway stesan, metro rail stesan athwa tram stesan par sthit us jagah ko kehete hai jahaan yatri railgadee main chadhate hai, utarate hai athwa badalte hai. sabhi railway stesan par kisi na kisi tarah kaa platform jarur hota he. samanyatya kisi bhi stesan par platformon ko anko se namit kiya jaataa he.
 vaise platform kaa upar se band hona aavashyak nahin he kyonki kuch steshnon par bane platform keval chabutra banaakar bhi nirmit kiye jate hai. lekin saamaanya rup se dekha jaae to prayek platform par dhup va baaris se bachane kii kuch vyavastha jarur hoti he. atah saamaanya rup se platform kii oonchaai railgadee kii unchaai se adhik rakhi jaati he.
