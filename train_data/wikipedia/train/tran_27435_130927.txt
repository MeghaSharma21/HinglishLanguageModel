 "obon" yaa "bon" ek japani buddha rivaaj he jo purvajon ko aadar karata he. yah buddha rivaaj viksit hokar ek paariwarik chuttee ho gaya he. is din log apane paitruk parivaar ke sthano jaakar vah apane paitruk kabron ko svachh karte hai. tab paitruk atmaaon shanti pahunchaate hai. yah tyohaar japan main paanch so varsh se zyaada samay se manaaya gaya he or is tyohaar main ack paramparaagat nrity bhi he or uskaa naam he bon-aadhori. yah tyohaar teen din ke liye hoti he. magar alag kshetron main uski taarikh badalati rahati he. yahan teen prakar ke calendar he. saurya calendar main "shichigatas bon" yani bon sajaa 15 july me puvri japan me manaaya jaataa he. chandra calendar main "hakigtsu bon" yani is bon sajaa 15 agast main manaaya jaataa he.
 bon odhori kii kahani yah he ki ek maha "mokuren" jo ek gautam buddha kaa shishy tha. vah apane alokik shakti se apane mritak maan ko bachaane ke liye koshish kii thi. usko pataa chalaa kii uski maan bhukhe bhoot ke daayre main pad gai thi. isi kaaran se usne buddha se jaakar ack upaay poocha. buddha ne kaha ki ve buddha bhikshuo ko prastaav de jo apane greeshmakaleen nivas katam kiya tha. yah prastaav saatven maheene ke pansehaven din kiya tha. isse shishy kaa maan pehle jaisi ho gai.
 baan odori kaa and he baan nrity jo ack nrity style he jo obaan samaaroh main kiya jaataa he.
 ajetena main japani samudaayon jab garmi mausam aata he tab is baan tyohaar ko manaate he. sabase bada tyohaar colonia "urakvija" or "laa plata partido" main manaaya jaataa he. is samaaroh main "taiko" sho or theth nrity bhi hota he.
 korea sskaran ke hisaab se bon utsav ko "bejung" bulaate he. pratibhaagi is din buddha viharon main vivaah kaa prastaav rakhte hai. is din vishesh nrity bhi pradshan karte hai. yah tyohaar dharmik se zyaada krishi ke bare main he.
