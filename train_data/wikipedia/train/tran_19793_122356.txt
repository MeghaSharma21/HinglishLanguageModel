 1973 europea cap final may 1973, 30 par, red star stadium main beograd main yojit ek footbal match tha, italy kii juvents ko 1-0 se netherlands kii ajaks haraa diya. ajaks unki lagaataar teesri baar european cap jeeta. is jeet ajaks sthaayee rup se trophy rakhane kaa visheshadhikaar arjeet kiya tha. juvents, haalanki, 23 saal baad rome main 1996 final main ajaks ko haraakar badla mila.
