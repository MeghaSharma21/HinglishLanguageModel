 hindi yaa inds khagoleey gole ke dakshini bhaag main sthit ek taramandal he.
 hindi taramandal kaa sab se roshan tara alfaa indi he jisaki roshni 3.11 magnitude he. is taramandal kaa ek or tara capsilon indi he jo prithvi se 11.82 prakaash varsh dur he or jisamen bhure bunon kaa ek joda he.
 kul milakar is taramandal main bair namakan vaale 16 taare hai, jinamein se 3 mukhya hai or 2 ke ird-gird gair-saureey grah parikrama karte paae gaye hai.
