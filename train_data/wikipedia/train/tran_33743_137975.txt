 saylugem​ uttari asia ke siberia kshetra main rus ke altaai ganatantr or mongolia main sthit ek parvat shrunkhlaa he. yah altaai parvaton kii ek dakshinpurvi upashrunkhalaa he.
 130 kimi tak chalani vaali is parvatamala main 1,500 se 1,700 meter kii ausat oonchaai ke pahaad hai. iske kuch parvat 3,500 meter ke aasapaas kii lambaai ke hai - jinamein 3,502 meter lambaa sari nokhoit​ parvat or 3,499 meter unchaa sarkemati parvat shaamil hai . parvaton ke bich main kuch unche pathaar bhi sthit hai.
 in parvaton main mausam bahut sard hota he. janvari main ausat taapmaana -32 °centegrade hota he lekin ratri main -62 °centegrade tak gir saktaa he. garmiyon kii dopaher main yahan taapmaana 30 °centegrade tak chadh saktaa he. in parvaton par bahut barf girti he or kuch pahaad sadaiv barngrast rahete hai.
