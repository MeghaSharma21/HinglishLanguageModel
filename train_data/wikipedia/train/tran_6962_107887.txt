 shefali jarivaala ek bhartiya abhinetri he, jo kai angreji or hindi sangeet video or bollywood filmon main pradarshit hui he. vah 2002 ke mujeec video kanta laagaa main dikhaai di, jiske baad vah "thong ladki" ke naam se jane lageen. vah 2004 main ek bollywood film 'mujhase shaadi karogi' main abhinay kiya tha. phir vah nrity rielity sho nach baliye 5 main apane premi ke saath dikhaai deen. 
 shefali ne 2005 main sardaar patel college of ejineering se soochna praudyogiki main master degree hasil kii. apane ejineering adhyayan ke dauraan unhen sangeet album main kaam karne kaa avasar mila. usne saint joseph ke convent, kalimpong se shiksha praapt kii. 
 2002 ke video album "kanta laagaa" main ek thong dikhaane ke liye shefali prasiddh hui. vah "kanta laiga girl" or "thong ladki" ke rup main jane jaani lagi. "kanta laagaa" ke baad unhone lagbhag 10-15 sangeet elbum kiye. 2004 main film 'mujhase shaadi karogi' main salmaan khan or akshay kumaar ke saath unhen ek chemiyo upasthiti main dekha gaya tha. 2009 main, vah apane gayak pati harmit gulzaar ke saath saarvajanik talaak ke vivaad main dikhaai di thim. 2012 main, vah apane premi ke saath reality sho nach baliye 5 main dekhi gai thi.
