 chhap tilak sab chhini, 14veen sadi ke sufi sant amir khusro kii ek kavita he jo brajabhasha main likhi gayi thi. aksar qavvaali kii tarah gaaya jaataa he. bhartiya upmahadweep ke mashhoor gayakon ne ye gaanaa gaaya jaise nusrat fateh ali khan, naahid akhtar, mehanaz begum, aabida parveen, sabari bradaz, iqbaal hossain khan, ustad vilaayat khan, ustan shujaat khan, zila khan, hadeeqa kiyani or ustad raahat fateh ali khan.
 sufimat main, bahut log allaah ke pyaar kii jagah main ladakiyon ke pyaar or sharaabon ke bare main likhte the. ye gaanaa vaisaa he: zyaadaatar gaanaa ek ladki ke pyaar ke bare main he or sharaab ke bare main bhi bolta magar gane kaa asli matlab allaah kaa pyaar he .
