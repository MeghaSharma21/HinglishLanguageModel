 pasu sar yaa passu sar yaa pasu 1 vishwa kaa 54vaan sabase unchaa parvat he. yah karakoram parvatamala kii pashchimtam upashrenee, batura muzataag, kaa ek unchaa pahaad he. prashasnic rup se yah paak-adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-blatistan kshetra main aata he jisapar bharat apni samprabhutaa kaa daawa karata he. yah pasu punjak kaa sarvocch bindu he, jisamen pasu diyaar bhi shaamil he. pasu sar batura sar se 7 kimi purv main sthit he.
