 bhartiya samskruti shodh pratishthan ek antararashtriya, alabhechhu tathaa maanavtaavaadi sanstha he. iskaa mukhyalay uttrakhand ke rishikesh main sthit he. poojya swami chidanand saraswati iske sansthaapak adhyaksh hai. yah pratishthan ni:shulk vidhyalay chalaata he; kanyaon/striyon ke liye vyavsaayik kaarykram sanchalit karata he; swasthya sevaayen, anathalay, gurukul tathaa paryaavaran raksha ke kshetra main kaam karata he. 
 is pratishthan ne hindu dharm evam darshan kaa vrihad vishwakosh taiyaar kiya he jiskaa march 2010 main haridwar kumbh meley ke avasar par locarpan kiya gaya.
