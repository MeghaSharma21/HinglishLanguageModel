 jibril yaa jibrail muslim samudaay kii manyata ke anusaar jibril, allaah ke dwaara sristi kiya gaya ek malak yaa farishta he. is malak kaa kaam yah tha ki allaah ke adenusaar nabi ke paas allaah kaa aadesh yaa divya vaani lekar jaataa he. paigambar mohammad apane jeevankaal main ek gupha main jaaya karte the, jahaan par jibril ne prakat hokar unhen allaah kii baton ko logon tak pahunchane kaa kaam saunpa tha.
