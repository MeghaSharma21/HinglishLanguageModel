 dow alka beotra bhartiya mahila ko vishwa doping rodhi vaigyanik sangh ke kaaryakari samiti kii sadsy hai. 13 march, 2015 ko unhen is pad hetu namit kiya gaya. ve sangh ke 6 sadasyeey kaaryakari samiti main namit hone vaali ek maatr asian mahila hai. vartmaan main dow alka yuva kaary or khel mantralay ke adheen rashtriya dope pareekshan prayogshaala kii vaigyanik nideshak ke rup main karyrat hai.
 alka ne kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay, varanasi se 1977 main parasnatak tathaa 1981 main piech.di. main karne ke baad farvari 1982 se march 1990 tak akhil bhartiya ayurvigyaan santhaan, nai dilli ke pharmacology vibhaag main pool officer or research officer ke pad par kaam kiya. tatpashchaat march 1990 main ve bhartiya khel praadhikaran ke dope niyantran centre main vaigyanik ke pad par niyukt huin or uske baad athleton kii dawa ke pareekshan kaary se judi. ve march 2002 se agast 2009 tak dope pareekshan prayogshaala nai dilli main up vaigyanik nideshak tathaa padonnati ke pashchaat sitambar 2009 se lagaataar vaigyanik nideshak ke pad par karyrat hai. unhen dope pareekshan prayogshaala main kaam karne kaa 24 saal kaa anubhav he. teen varsh kii avadhi ke liye march 2015 se ve anti doping vaigyanikon kii world association ke kaaryakari board kii sadsy namit huyi he. 
 ve 1999 se lagaataar indian association of sports medicine main vibhinn padon par kaary kar chuki hai. vartmaan main ve indian association of sports medicine main upaadhyaksh hai or khel chikitsa asian federation ke doping ayog kii sadsy hai.
