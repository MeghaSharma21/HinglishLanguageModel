 shaikh sirri saqti bin al muflis baghdad ke sunni sampradaay ke ek sufi the. junayd baghdadi ke chachaa hote the. nuri, kharazi tathaa khayr nassaaj se dikshit the. apane samay ke mahan sufi, sristi ke pathpradarshak or bade aalim samajhe jate the. aadhyatmik siddhanton main al muhasvi ke anuyaayi the. 
 unke kathananusaar ishwar or maanav premasutra main sambhddha hai, or sachche premi ko shaareerik santaap sahan nahin karnaa padataa. mard vah he jo bazaar main bhi ishwar ke gungan main sanlagn rahe. mahabali tathaa mall vah he jo apni durbhilaashaaon ko apane vash main kar le. unhone yah bhi kaha ki jab hriday main or koi vastu hoti he to yah paanch baaten vahaan nahin hotin - ishwarbhay, aashaa, prem, lajja tathaa anukanpa. purush vah he jisase sristi ko kisi prakaar kaa ksht na pahunche. junayd baghdadi ke kathanusaar sari saqti chintan tathaa ishwar-gungan main adwitiya the. 98 varshon tak kabhi dharti par nahin baithe. 
 ibne habal ne unke is mat kaa khandan kiya he ki quran ke akshar manushya dwaara rachit hai. vyapaar karte the. 98 varsh kii aayu main 28 ramadan 250 athwa 253 ko swargvaas hua. samadhi baghdad main he.
