 adhidaksh kan channi, vaayu ko saafe karne kii ek channi he jisase bahut chote kan bhi chaanakar nikaale ja sakate hai. iskaa avishkaar 1950 ke dashak main america ke parmaanu urja santhaan main shodh ke baad hua. ise shodh kaa uddeshy parmaanu-sanyantron main chote kanon ke hawa main chale jane or electro-magnetic vikiran failaane se rokna tha. iskaa aarambhik naam hipa tha, jo ab High Eficciency Particulate Arrest he.
 aajkal iskaa upyog parmaanu sanyantron, microilectronic kaarkaanon, hawaii jahaajon, aupreshan theatreon jaisi jagahon par hota he jahaan adhik shuddh hawa kii jarurat hoti he. microilectonic kaarkaanon main bade kanon ke chale jane se utpaadan kii gunvattta or utpaadon ke sahi chalane kaa bharosa bahut kam ho jaataa he. kuch gharon or kaaron main bhi iskaa istemaal hota he. daawa kiya jaataa he ki yah atyadhik chote kan, yahan tak ki jeevanuon ko bhi nikaal detaa he jisase rogon ke failane kii sambhaavna bahut kam ho jaati he.
 iske andar main fibre kaanch kii kai paraten hoti hai, jisase takaraakar kan funs jate hai. kan agar 1 micron se kam duuri par hote hai to bhi khinche chale aate hai.
