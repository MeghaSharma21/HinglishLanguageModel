 bana kaa baas bharat ke rajya rajasthan tathaa jodhpur zile main sthit ek chota sa gaanv he tathaa yah tinwari tehsil ke antargat aata he. isamen ek rajasav gram vir tejanagar he. is gaanv main nivas karne vaale logon kii aajeevika kaa mukhya saadhan krishi he tathaa gaanv main jat evam bishnoi jati ke bana gotra ke logon kii adhikta ke kaaran hi is gaanv kaa naam bana kaa baas rakhaa. bana kaa baasagaanv kii jansankhya 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar 2956 he. iske nazadiiki gaanvon main cherai khetasar ,khabada khurd tathaa tinwari ityadi hai.
