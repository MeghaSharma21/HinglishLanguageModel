 uttrakhand vidyalayan shiksha parishad, uttrakhand sarkaar ke adheen shiksha vibhaag kii ek sanstha he, jiskaa kaary rajya ke madhyamik vidhyalay ke chaatro ke liye paathyakram taiyaar karnaa tathaa hai skool evam intermediat str kii vaarshik parishadiy parikshaayen yojit karana he. isaki sthapana 2001 main kii gayi tathaa ramnagar main iskaa mukhyalay he. vartmaan main 10,000 se adhik skool parishad se sambhddha hai. parishad prativarsh 1300 se adhik pariksha kendron par 300,000 se adhik parikshaarthiyon ke liye vaarshik parikshaayen sampann karaati he.
 2002 main parishad dwaara pratham baar parikshaon kaa swayan aayojan kiya gaya.
