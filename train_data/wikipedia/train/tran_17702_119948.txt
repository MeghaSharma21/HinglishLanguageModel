 general dynamik epa-16 fiting falcon ek america multi role ladaku vimaan he. jise multah america kii hi general dynamiks ne america vaayu sena ke liye banaaya tha. shruwaat main ise din main istemaal hetu pradhan ladaaku vimaan ke upyog ke liye banaaya gaya tha par baad main nirantar badlaav or praudyogikeey vikaas kii vajah se yah sabhi mausamon main udne main sambhav ek safal multi role ladaaku vimaan ke taur par ubhara. san 1976 se ab tak 4,500 se bhi adhik epa-16 vibhinn deshon ke liye banaae ja chuke hai va inamen se adhiktam aaj bhi kaary seva main hai. haalaaki america vaayu sena ise ab nahin kharidti va anya vimaan ke jariye puraane epa-16 ko badalane ke prayaas main he, iske vishesh taur pe videshi senaaon ke liye bane unnat varjan abhi bhi beche jate hai.
