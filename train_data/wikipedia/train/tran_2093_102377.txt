 umr abdulla ek bhartiya kashmiri netaa or kashmir ke 'pratham parivaar' ke vanshaj hai. unka janm britain main hua. unke pita faruk abdulla hai. umr jammu or kashmir ke ab tak ke sabase yuva or pradesh ke 11 wein mukhyamantri rah chuke hai.
 umr abdulla ne congress party ke saath milakar 5 janvari 2009 ko gathbandhan sarkaar banaai.
 2014 ke vidhansabha chunaavon main main unki party kii haar hui. unhone sonwar tathaa birwaah siton se chunaav ladaa. sonwar seat se ve haar gaye lekin birwaah seat se ve vidhayak nirvaachit hue.
 vah lok sabha ke bhi sadsy rah chuke hai.
