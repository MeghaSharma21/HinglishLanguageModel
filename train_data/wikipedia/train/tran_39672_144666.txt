 labaan malaysia kaa ek sangheey kshetra he. iske alaava sabah or sarawak do rajya hai jo borneo dweep par sthit hai. labaan sangheey kshetra main sabah rajya ke tat se aage saat dwipon kaa samooh aata he. inamen sabase bade dweep kaa naam labaan hi he or isi ke naam par sangheey kshetra kaa naam rakhaa gaya he. yahan par malaysia ne vishesh kaanoon lagu kiye hai jinase kai videshi companiyaan apana paisa yahan rakhti hai yaa ise paise kii adla-badali kaa kendra banaati hai. yahan ke nikatavarti samudri kshetra main tel gas nikaala jaataa he or labaan main is kaary main zaruri sevaaon or mashin-marammat kaa bhi kendra banaaya gaya he.
