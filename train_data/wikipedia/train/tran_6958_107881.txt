  prarthana samaaj bhartiya navajagaran ke daur main dharmik or saamaajik sudhaaron ke liye sthapit samudaay he. ko isaki sthapana atmaram pandurang ne bombai main 31 march 1867 main kii.
 prarthnasmaj kii prishthbhumi 19veen shatee ke prarambh athwa usase bhi pehle 18veen shatee main hui kai ghatnaon se ban chuki thi. angreji shiksha kaa pravesh or isai mishnariyon ke kaary, ye do ghatnaayen us prishthbhumi ke nirmaan main vishesh sahaayak baniin. angreji shiksha ke prasar se shikshit bharatiyon main apane saamaajik or aarthik vishwaason tathaa reeti rivaajon ke dosha or trutiyon ke prati chetana jaagi. isai mishnariyon ne anekanek logon, visheshtya hinduon, kaa dharmaparivartan kar unhen isai bana liya, isse bhi logon kii ankhen khul gayi. phir mishnariyon ne apni kathor prahaari aalochana dwaara bhi dharmaparivartan ke anicchhuk logon ke vichaaron main bada parivartan laa diya. hindu darshan ke un netaaon ne jo in tatwon ke prabhaav kaa anubhav kar rahe the or naveen gyaan se bhi parichit ho rahe the, saanskrithik mulyon ke aadhaar par hindu samaaj ke bauddhik or aadhyatmik punarutthan ke kaary kaa shriganesh kiya. hindu vichaardhaara ke inhen netaaon main se kuch ne prarthnasmaj kii sthapana kii.
 prarthnasmaj ke andolan ne, raja rammohan raay dwaara bengal main sthapit brahmsamaj se prerana grahan kii or vyaktigat tathaa saamaajik jivan ke swasth sudhaar ke liye apni sari shakti dharmik shiksha ke prachaar main arpit kar di. bombai ke pashchaat dhire-dhire iskaa vistaar puna, ahmedabad, satara or ahmednagar aadi sthanon main bhi hua.
 prarthnasmaj ke pramukh prakashastambhon main atmaram pandurang, vasudev babaaji naurange, ramakrishna gopal bhandarkar, mahadev govind rande, vaaman abaaji modak or narayan ganesh chandaavarkar the. prarthnasmaj ke aalochakon dwaara kiye gaye asaty prachaar ko mitaane ke liye in netaaon ko bahut sangharsh karnaa padaa. asaty prachaar ke antargat yah kaha jaataa tha ki prarthnasmaj isai dharm ke anukaran par aadhrut he or yah desh ke pracheen dharm ke viruddh he.
 prarthana samaaj ke mukhya niyam or siddhant nimnlikhit hai :
 
 prarthnasmaj kaa uddeshy prarthana or seva dwaara ishwar kii puja karnaa tha. brahmsamaj kii bhanti upanishdon or bhagavadgita kii shikshaayen uddeshy ke aadhaar hai kintu ek baat main yah brahmsamaj se bhinn he, isamen bharat ke, visheshtya maharashtra ke, madhyakaalin santon - gyaneshwar, namdev, ekanath or tukaraam - kii shikshaaon ko gauravpurn sthaan praapt he.
 prarthnasmaj ne ranade ke netritv main jati pratha, bal-vivaah, murti puja tathaa hindu samaaj kii anya kuritiyon ke viruddh adolan kiya. usne 19veen shatee ke nawein dashak main narijagran kii yojanaon kaa aarambh kiya. arya-mahila-samaaj kii sthapana unheen yojanaon kaa fal he.
 1878 main prarthnasmaj dwaara sthapit pehla ratrividyalay janshiksha or praudhshiksha ke kshetra main agrani rahaa. vasudev babaaji naurange balkashram kii sthapana lalashankar umashankar dwaara pangarpur main 1875 main hui yah balkashram baad main prarthnasmaj ke sangrakshan main aa gaya. yah apane dhang kii sarvaadhik pracheen or badi sanstha he or yah 1975 main apni shatabdi puri karegi. prarthnasmaj ke sangrakshan main do balkashram or chalate hai - ek viley parley main di.en. sirur home or doosraa satara jile ke vaai naamak sthaan main he.
 "di depressd class mishan sosayadi ov india" naam kii sanstha, jo achhutoddhar ke liye prasiddh he, prarthnasmaj ke ek kaaryakarta bitthal raamji scindia dwaara sthapit hui.
 1917 main prarthnasmaj ne rammohan angreji vidhyalay kii sthapana kii. ab iske sangrakshan se das se adhik vidhyalay bombai or uske aas paas chal rahe hai.
