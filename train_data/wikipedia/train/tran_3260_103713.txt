 sphatik yaa quartz ek khanij he. yah ret evam grenite kaa mukhya ghatak he. prithvi ke mahadwipiya bhu-parpati par quartz doosraa sarvaadhik paaya jane vaala khanij he . yah SiO4 ke silicon-oxygen chatushfalki se bana hota he jisamen prayek oxygen do chatushfalakiyon main saajha hota he. is prakaar iskaa prabhavi anusutra SiO2 he.
 quartj anekon prakaar ke hote hai. inamen se kai ardh-moolyawaan ratna hai. visheshtah europe or madhyapurv main tarah-tarah ke quartz atipracheen kaal se aabhushan banane ke kaam main liye jate rahe hai. quartz shabd german shabd 'Quarz' se niklaa he jiskaa arth 'kathor' hota he.
 quartj ek prakaar kaa prakritik khanij jo saadharantaya ek aksheeya, rangahin, paardarshee or kathor hota he. yah do prakaar kaa hota he- vamaghurnak yor dakshinghurnak. ghurnakari prijmon ke nirmaan main iskaa upyog kiya jaataa he.
 quartj kabhi-kabhi vidalan bhi pradarshit karata he tathaa hydrochloric amla ke atirikt anya sab amlon main aviley hota he. quartj shuddh hone par hi rangahin rahata he. praaia: yah antarveshon kii prakruti ke anusaar laal, naarangi, pile, hare, begni tathaa kaale rangon main paaya jaataa he. garm karne par iske bahut se rang adrishy ho jate he. quartj ke ek khanij sphatik, shail crystal main daab vidyut gun hote he.
