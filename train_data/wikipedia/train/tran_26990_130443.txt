 kumari amorna madrid ke europea vishvavidhyalay se lalit kala main snaatak hai.
 19 varsh kii aayu main unhone apane pratham chalchitr main na keval abhinetri ke rup main kaam kiya, balki is chalchitr kii nirmaataa va nirdeshika bhi ve swayan hi thim.
 2015 ke barsilona antarrashtriya kaamuk chalchitr mahotsav main unhen sarvshereshth abhinetri paritoshik se sammanit kiya gaya. las vegas main 23 janvari 2016 ko yojit hone vaale vayask video samachar puraskaron main unhen sarvshereshth videshi mahila kalakaar paritoshik ke liye namit kiya gaya he.
