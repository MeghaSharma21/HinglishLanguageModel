 oyyarattu chantu menon malayalam ke upanyaaskaar the. 
 unka janm malabar main hua tha. tatkaaleen madras pradesh main nyaayadhish kaa kaam karte the. unka 'idulekha' upanyaas ab bhi malayalam ke ucchtam upanyaason main se ek he. yah ek saamaajik sukhaant upanyaas he jisamen vah un mudh evam tuchh reeti rivaajon or vyavhaaron kaa varnan karata he jo adarsh ke rup main nambudiriyon or ucch varg ke nairon main prachlit the. nayak evam nayika madhavan or indulekha prabud naveen peedi kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai jo jivan ke maanviya mulyon kaa samarthan karte hai. saamaajik prishthbhumi or paatro kaa chithran oj, marmagrata evam shuddhta se kiya gaya he. chantumenon ne 'sharada' naam kaa doosraa upanyaas likhnaa prarambh kiya tha kintu abhaagyavash ise puraa karne ke purv hi unki mrityu ho gai. isamen vishwa ke nyaayalayon kaa sajiv chithran kiya gaya he or usame anek smaraneey paatr milte hai.
