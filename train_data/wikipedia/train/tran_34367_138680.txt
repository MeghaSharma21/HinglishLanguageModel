 tulyanki bhaar rasayan vigyaan kaa ek paribhaashik shabd he jo bhinn-bhinn sandarbhon main prayukt hota he. tulyanki bhaar kii sabase saamaanya paribhaasha yah he-
 tulyanki bhaar kii beemaa or maatrak dravyaman ke hi bima or maatrak hote hai. jabaki parmaanu bhaar bimaheen raashi he. multah tulyanki bhaaron kaa nirdhaaran prayog dwaara kiya gaya tha kintu ab ab ye molr dravyaman se nikaale jate hai. iske alaava, kisi yogic kaa tulyanki bhaar uske anubhaar ko n se bhaag dene se bhi tulyanki bhaar nikaala jaataa he, jahaan n dhanatmak yaa rinatmak vidyut aaveshon kii sankhya he jo us yogic ke vighatan se praapt hote hai.
