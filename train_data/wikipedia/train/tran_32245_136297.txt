 great britain athwa britain europe main sthit ek dweep he. yah dweep, united kingdom desh kaa sabase bada hissaa he. england, scotland or wales isi dweep par sthit hai. 
 great britain europe mahadweep ke uttar-pashchim sthit yah brihat dweep he jisamen scotland, wales tathaa england sanmmilit hai. 1282 i. main igland ne wales par vijay praapt kii tathaa 1707 i. main scotland vidhanat: englad main mila gaya. in sanyukt rajyon kaa naam tabhi se great britain pad gaya. great britain pracheen roman britania major shabd kaa anuwaad he.
