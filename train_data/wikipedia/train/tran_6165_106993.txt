 arun prakaash hindi ke jane-maane kathakaar or patrkaar hai. unka kahani sangrah 'bhaiya express' kaaphi charchit hua.
 arun prakaash kaa janm 22 farvari 1948 ko begusarai ke nipaniyaan gaanv main hua tha.
 arun prakaash hindi kahani, patrakarita va anuwaad kshetra se jude rahe. unhone ne doordarshan kii bahucharchit tivi saanskrithik patrika ‘parakh’ ke kareeb 450 apisod likhe the or vah sahitya academy kii saahitiyik patrika ‘samakaleen bhartiya sahitya’ ke sampadak rahe.
