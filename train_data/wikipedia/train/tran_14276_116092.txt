 kairana
 kairana mujffarnagar se kareeb 50 ki. me. ,pashchim main haryana panipat se sata yamuna nadi ke paas tathaa jila shamali se 12kimi he or kareeb 12 lakh kii aabaadi vaala yah tehsil kairana pracheen kaal main karnpuri ke naam se vikhyath tha jo baad main bigadkar kiraanaa naam se janaa gaya or phir kiraanaa se kairana main parivartit ho gaya. yahan muslim bahumat zyada he yahan nagareey chhetra me sabase adhik ansaari jati va gramin chhetra me gurjar he shahar me shiksha k.
