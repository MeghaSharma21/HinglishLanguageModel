 shalimaar baag, bharat ke jammu or kashmir rajya main vikhyath udyaan he jo mughal baag kaa ek udhaaharan he. ise mughal badshaah jahangir ne srinagar main banwaaya tha. srinagar bharat ke uttartam rajya jammu evam kashmir kii greeshm-kaaleen rajdhani he. ise jahangir ne apni priy evam budhimati patni mehrunnisa ke liye banwaaya tha, jise noorjehan kii oopaadhi di gai thi.
 is baag main chaar str par udyaan bane hai evam jaldhara bahati he. isaki jalaapurti nikatavarti harivan baag se hoti he. ucchtam str par udyaan, jo ki nichle str se dikhaai nahin detaa he, vah haram kii mahilaon hetu bana tha. yah udyaan greeshm evam patjhad main sarvottam kehalaata he. is ritu main patton kaa rang badalata he evam anekon phool khilte hai.
 yahi udyaan anya baagon kii prerana bana, khaskar isi naam se lahore, pakistan main nirmit or viksit baag kii. yah vartmaan main ek saarvajanik baag he.
