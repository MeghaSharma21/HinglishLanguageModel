 sikkim kaa mukhyamantri bharat ke purvottar rajya sikkim kaa pramukh hota he. yahan par sikkim ke mukhyamantriyon kii suchi di gai he. 
 1975 se sikkim main ab tak paanch mukhyamantri rah chuke hai. bhartiya rashtriya congress ke kaaji lendup dorji is rajya ke pehle mukhyamantri the. pawan kumaar chamling rajya ke vartmaan mukhyamantri hai. unhone 12 disambar 1994 ko pad sambhala tha.
