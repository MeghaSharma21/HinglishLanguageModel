 sabah malaysia kaa ek rajya he. yah or sarawak do rajya hai jo borneo dweep par sthit hai. sabah malaysia kaa sabase purvi rajya he or yah borneo dweep ke purvottaree bhaag main sthit he. dakshin main rajya kii seemaa indonesia se lagati hai or uttar main isaki philippino ke saath samudri seemaa he. sarawak ke baad yah desh kaa doosraa sabase bada rajya he.
 borneo ke is bhaag main sthaneey samudaay sarvaadhik hai, jinamein kaadazan, dusun or baju mahatvapurn hai or jo adhiktar isai mat or sarvatmavaad ke anuyaayi hai. malaysia kaa bhaag banane ke baad, bahut se muslim malay log bhi yahan aakar bas gayi hai. iske alaava, philippino kaa dakshini bhaag bhi samudr ke paar sameep hi sthit he. vahaan dashakon se grihayuddh kii sthiti hone ke kaaran vahaan se bhi kai sharnaarthi yahan aa gayi hai. haalanki philippino ek isai-bahusankhyak desh he, dakshini philippino main muslimon kii bahutayat he or yah sharanarthi bhi adhiktar muslim hai. is aniyantrit apravasana se sthaneey mul nivaasiyon kaa jansankhya-pratishat ghatata chalaa gaya he or yah yahan ek bade rajnatik va saamaajik tanaav kaa kaaran ban gaya he.
