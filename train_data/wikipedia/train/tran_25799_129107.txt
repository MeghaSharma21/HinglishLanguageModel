 samual yusuf eganan israel ke kathakaar evam kavi the. 1966 i0 main german mul kii swedish kavyitri nelli sakhs ke saath sahitya main nobel puraskaar vijeta.
 samual yusuf eganan kaa janm 17 july, 1818 ko poland main hua tha, parantu ve baad main isroyal aakar yiddish bhashaa main likhane lage the. aadhi shatabdi ke lagbhag ve isroyal main rahe or bahut sa samay unhone germany main ek yahudee aadhyatmavaadi phirake hassidim se sambandhit rabbiyon kii kahaniyaan khojane main lagaaya. in vibhinn paristhitiyon main rahakar unhone jo kuch dekha, saha or sochaa, unka lekhan main upyog kiya.
 eganan kii praarambhik shiksha yahudiyon ke praathmik skool header main hui, jahaan unhone bible or talmud kaa adhyayan kiya. yah shiksha 3 varsh kii avasthaa se 9 varsh kii avasthaa tak hui. pita ne ghar par hi unka parichay yahudee dharmik sahitya se karvaa diya tha. mata ne unhen german sahitya se parichay karaayaa. eganan ne german bhashaa kaa adhyayan bhi kiya. iske saath hi unhone hebrew or scandinavian deshon ke sahitya kaa bhi adhyayan kiya.
 eganan ne sahitya-rachana mukhyatah apni maatrubhaasha yiddish main hi kii. yiddish ke atirikt unhone hebrew main bhi likhaa. jab ve philistin gayi the to hebrew main likhane lage the. aadhunik hebrew sahitya main bhi eganan kaa sthaan bahut unchaa he or unke sahitya ko hebrew kaa clasik mana jaataa he. unki kahaniyon main yatharth ke saath aadhyatm kii adbhut kalpanaaon kaa anokhaa mail he or unki jaden pracheen or madhyayugin dharmik sahitya main dikhti hai. jerusalem main eganan ko bahut sammanit vyakti mana jaataa tha. jahaan ve rahete the us sadak ke kinare ek board tangaa tha, jis par likhaa tha shant rahen, eganan likh rahe hai. rajya kii ore se unhen kai baar puraskrit bhi kiya gaya tha.
