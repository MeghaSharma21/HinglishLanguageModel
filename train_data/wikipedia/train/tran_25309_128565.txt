 kul jeevvaigyanik vargeekaran main praaniyon ke vargeekaran kii ek shrenee hoti he. ek kul main ek-dusre se samantaayein rakhane vaale kai saare jeevon ke vansh aate hai. dhyaan den ki har praani vansh main bahut-si bhinn praaniyon kii jatiyaan sammilit hoti hai.
 kulon ke naam adhiktar latini bhashaa main hote hai kyonki jeevvaigyanik vargeekaran kii pratha 17veen or 18veen sadiyon main europe main shuru hui thi or us samay vahaan latini gyaan kii bhashaa maani jaati thi. yah rivaayat abhi tak chalati ai he. aadhunik kaal main istemaal hone vaali vargeekaran vyavastha 18veen shatabdi main karl liniyas naamak swidi vaigyanik ne kii thi.
