 shinto dharm japan desh kaa ek pramukh or mul dharm he. isamen kai devi-devtaa hai, jinako kaami kaha jaataa he. har kaami kisi na kisi prakritik shakti kaa pratinidhitiv karata he. buddha dharm ke saath iskaa kaafii mail milaap hua he or isamen buddha dharm ke kai siddhanth jud gayi hai. ek jamaane main shinto dharm japan kaa rajdharm hua karata tha. is dharm main japan ke raja ko pradhan guru maanate the kintu duusare vishwa-yuddh ke baad se aisa karnaa band kar diya gaya he samurai isi dharm ko maanate he
