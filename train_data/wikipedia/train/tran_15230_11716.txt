 vaastu tathaa sanrachana ejiniyari main stambh vah sanrachanaatmak avyav he jo swayan sampidit hokar apane upar aane vaale chat aadi kaa bhaar apane neeche ke avyavon par transfar kar detaa he. atah stambh ek sampeden avyav he jo urdhvadhar khadaa rahata he. kintu bhucamp ejiniyari kii drushti se stambh kii dizin karte samay parshv balon ko sahane kii kshamta bhi di ja sakati he. stambhon kaa upyog praayah dharanon evam chaapon ko avlamb karne ke liye kiya jaataa he.
