 utaran bhartiya hindi dharawahik he, jiskaa prasaaran colors par 1 disambar 2008 se 16 janvari 2015 tak hua. iskaa nirmaan kalyaan guha or roopali guha ne kiya he. iskaa nirdesan kethan dube ne kiya he. isamen mukhya kirdaar main rashmi deshaai, tina dutt, shrijita de or mrinal jain hai.
 yah kahani do doston kii he jo bhinn bhinn prishthbhumi ke hote hai. tapasya ek bahut amir jogi thakur kii beti hoti he or ichha ek naukaraani kii beti hoti he. donon ko hi vir naamak ek ladke se pyaar ho jaataa he. vah donon hi ek duusare ke dushman ho jate hai, jab tapasya kii shaadi vir se ho jaati he, jabaki vir ichha se pyaar karata he. lekin vah phir se dost ho jate hai or ichha kii mrityu ho jaati he. iske baad yah kahani unke bacchon par aadhaarit ho jaati he.
