 dar 1993 main bani hindi bhashaa kii thriller premakahani film he. yash chopra dwaara nirdeshit is film main pramukh bhoomikaaon main sani deol, juhi chawla or shahrukh khan hai. isamen anupam kher, tanvi aazmi or dalip tahil bhi hai. film donon aalochakon or darshakon dwaara pasand kii gai thi. film or uske kalakaaron kii prashansa karte hue, ise gharelu or videshi box offis par "blockbster" ghoshit kiya gaya tha. 
 kiran holi ke liye college se lautati he. uske ghar main bhai vijay or bhabhi punam he. uskaa peechha sahpaathee rahul dwaara kiya ja rahaa jo uski laalasaa rakhata he. kiran kaa premi sunil ek nausena adhikari hai, or ucch samudron par kuch mishan par hai. sunil ke captain mehara rahul ke pita bhi hai. kiran ke kareeb aane ke liye rahul sunil ke saath dostana banane kii koshish karata he.
 jab rahul ko kiran or sunil kii sagaai kii khabar praapt hoti he, to vah shaadi kii anguthi ke liye kharidari karte samay sunil ko maarane kii koshish karata he. kiran or sunil shaadi kar lete hai lekin rahul abhi bhi kiran ko chhodane se inkaar karata he. sunil kiran ko switzaraland honeymoon ke liye le jaataa he. rahul vahaan bhi pahunch jaataa he or kiran kaa dost banakar unke ghar main ghus jaataa he. kiran kaa bhai vijay rahul ko pahchaan letaa he ki wo hi peechha karne vaala he. vah kiran kii sahmati ke bina shaadi karnaa chahta he. lekin sunil aakar use use maar detaa he.
