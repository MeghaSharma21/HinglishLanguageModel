 tungusi bhashaayen yaa maanchu-tungusi bhashaayen purvi siberia or manchuriya main boli jane vaali bhashaaon kaa ek bhashaa-parivaar he. in bhashaaon ko maatrubhaasha ke rup main bolne value samudaayon ko tungusi log kaha jaataa he. bahut si tungusi boliyaan hameshaa ke liye vilupt hone ke khatare main hai or bhashavaigyanikon ko dar he ki aane vaale samay main kahin yah bhashaa-parivaar puraa yaa adhikansh rup main khatm hi na ho jaae. bahut se vidwaanon ke anusaar tungusi bhashaayen altaai bhashaa-parivaar kii ek upashakha he. dhyaan dijiye ki mongol bhashaayen or turki bhashaayen bhi is parivaar ki upashakhaayen maani jaati hai isliye, agar yah sach he, to tungusi bhashaaon kaa turki, uzbek, uigur or mongol jaisi bhashaaon ke saath gaharaa sambandh he or yah sabhi kisi ek hi aadim altaai bhashaa kii santaanen hai. tungusi bhashaayen bolne vaali samudaayon ko samuhik rup se tungusi log kaha jaataa he.
 tungusi bhashaaon ke andruni shreneekaran ko lekar bhashavaigyanikon main vivaad chalata rahata he, lekin adhiktar vidwaan inhen uttari tungusi or dakshini tungusi main baantate hai:
 tungusi bhashaaon main abhishleshan dekha jaataa he, jahaan shabdon kii mul jadon main akshar or dhvaniyaan jodakar unke arth main izaafaa kiya jaataa he. udahaaran ke liye maanchu bhashaa main yah dekha jaataa he 'embi', 'ambi' yaa 'imbi' jodne se 'karne', 'aane' yaa kisi or prakaar kaa sandarbh aa jaataa he:
 in bhashaaon main swar sahyog bhi miltaa he, jisamen kisi shabd ke andar ke swaron kaa aapas main mail khaanaa zaruri hota he. kuch had tak yah sabhi altaai bhashaaon main dekha jaataa he. maanchu main dekha gaya hi ki ling main maamalon main shabd ke ek se zyaada swaron ko badla jaataa he:
