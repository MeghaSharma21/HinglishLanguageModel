 banu khujah ek arab, kanite janajati kaa naam he, jo arab prayadweep ke mukhya paitruk janajatiyon main se ek he. unhone lambii avadhi ke liye makka par shashan kiya, or adhikansh janajati ab anya deshon, mukhya rup se israel, philistini pradeshon, iraq or jordan main kam sankhya main maujuud hai. lekin choti sankhya main yah janajati ke log lebanon, misr, libya, syria, katar, bahrain, kuwait or sanyukt arab emirate main nivas karte hai.
