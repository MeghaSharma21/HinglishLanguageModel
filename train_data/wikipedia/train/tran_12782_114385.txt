 addis ababa kii sthapana 1886 main kii gai thi. yah ithiopia or africa sangh kii rajdhani or sabase bada shahar he. varsh 2007 kii janaganana ke anusaar shahar kii aabaadi 27,38,248 thi. ek chartered shahar ke rup main, addis ababa ko shahar or rajya kaa darja diya he. shahar ke aitihaasik or raajnneetik mahatv ke kaaran ise aksar africa kii rajdhani yaa "africa rajdhani" kaha jaataa he. shahar main ithiopia ke vibhinn kshetron se alag-alag rashtriyata, bhashaa or jaateey samudaay ke aakar based log nivas karte hai.
 is sthal kaa chayan maharani taytu betul ne kiya tha, jahaan unke pati samraat menelik dwitiya ne 1886 main shahar sthapit kiya. shahar kii vartmaan main lagbhag 2.7 karod kii jansankhya he. intoto parvat kii talahati main based is shahar main addis ababa vishvavidhyalay sthit he.
