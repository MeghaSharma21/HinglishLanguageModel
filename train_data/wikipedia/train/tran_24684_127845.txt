 gandhi shaanti pratishthan ek ashaaskeey sanstha he. iskaa lakshya sansar main shaanti evam bhaichara sthapit karnaa he. iske liye sanstha bacchon ko shaanti kii samskruti se avgat karaane kii shiksha detii he; navayuvakon ko sammilit kiya jaataa he; tathaa gandhiji dwaara apanaaye gayi jeevanamoolyon ko badhaava dene kii disha main praytn kiye jate hai.
