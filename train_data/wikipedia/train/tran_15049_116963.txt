 natasha aalianova romanova, jo apane anya naam; natasha romanoff yaa blike vido se adhik prachlit he, maarval comics dwaara prakashit america comic pustakon main dikhne vaali ek kaalpanik superhiro he.
 yah charitra pehali baar tels of suspence #52 main prakashit hua tha, or ise sampadak-ploter stan lee, lekhak don rico or kalakaar don hack ne banaaya tha. blike vido ko prarambh main ek roosi guptchar ke rup main darshaya gaya tha, jo aayran main kii comic shrinkhalaa main khalanayika thi. vah baad main america chali gai, jahaan vah shield agency ke liye jasusi karne lagi, or iske kuch samay baad hi super hero team avangers kii sadsy ban gai.
 abhinetri scarlett johansan maarval cinemetic univers kii filmon main natasha romanoff kii bhoomika nibhaa rahi hai. johansan aayran main 2, da avangers, captain america: winter soljar, avangers: age of ultron, or captain america: civil war main blike vido kaa abhinay kar chuki hai, or vah hi emsiu kii aagaamee avangers: infinity war, or uske sikval main bhi blike vido kii bhoomika kaa nirvahan karengi.
