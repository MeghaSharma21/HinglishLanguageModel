 mirpor azaad kashmir kaa sab se bada shahar he or zila mirpor kaa marakaj bhi he. mirpor azaad kashmir ke intihaai janub main vaakia he or sataha samudra se is kii oonchai taqariban 459 meter he. ye pakistan ke darulahakumat islam aabaad se taqariban 125 kilometre janub mashriq main vaakia he. pakistan kaa doosar bada dam mangalah dam isi zila main he. 1960 kii duhaaye main taqarica 50,000 logon ne is dam kii vajah se duusare ilaaqon jaisaaki new mir por, pakistan ke dusron ilaaqon or bartaania main hizrat kii. 
 mir por shahar kii bulandi sataha samudra se 459 meter he. tehsil dinah ke zariye ye lahore pishawar marakaji shahrah se munsalik he. ye mirpor zila kaa sadar muqaam he jo ki teen sab divizano- mirpor, dadyal or chak sawari par mastamal he. mir por kaa nayaa shahar mangalah jhil ke kinare vaakia he. purana shahar jhil ke pani main dafan he. saradiyon ke dinon main jab mangalah jhil ke pani kii sataha kam ho todurbaror mandir, baula or puraane khandraat nazar aate hai.
