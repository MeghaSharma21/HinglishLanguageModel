 bandhavgarh rashtriya udyaan madhyapradesh ke umariya jile main sthit he. yah varsh 1968 main rashtriya udyaan banaaya gaya tha. iskaa kshetrafal 437 varg kimi he. yahaan sher aasaani se dekha ja saktaa he. yah madhyapradesh kaa ek aisa rashtriya udyaan he jo 32 pahadiyon se ghira he 
 yah bharat kaa ek pramukh rashtriya udyaan hai. baaghon kaa garh 448 varg kilometre ke kshetra main failaa he. is udyaan main ek mukhya pahaad he jo 'bandhavgarh' kehalaata he. 811 meter unche is pahaad ke paas choti-choti pahadiyaan hai. park main saal or bambu ke vriksh prakritik sundarataa ko badhaate hai. bandhavgarh se sabase najadik vimaanatal jabalpur main he jo 164 kilometre kii duuri par he. rail maarg se bhi bandhavgarh jabalpur, katni or satna se judaa he. khajuraho se bandhavgarh ke bich 237 kilometre kii duuri he. donon sthanon ke bich ken nadi ke kuch hisson ko crocodiles rigerv ghoshit kiya gaya he.
 bandhavgarh kii pahari par 2 hajaar varsh purana kila bana he.
 bandhavgarh kaa van kshetra vibhinn prakaar ke vanaspatiyon or jantuon se bharaa hua he. jangal main nilgai or chinkaara sahit har tarah ke vanyapraani or ped hai.
 is rashtriya udyaan main pashuon kii 22 or pakshiyon kii 250 prajatiyaan pai jaati hai. hathi par savaar hokar yaa phir vaahan main baithkar in vanyapraniyon ko dekha ja saktaa he.
