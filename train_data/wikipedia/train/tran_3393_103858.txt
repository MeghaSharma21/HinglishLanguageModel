 sardaar mehtab ahamed khazbbasi ek pakistani raajnneetijna hai, evam ve kaibar-pakhtuunakva ke rajyapal bhi rah chuke hai.ve pakistan ke rashtriya vidhansabha main ve NA-17 nirvachan kshetra kaa pratinidhitiv kiya karte the, joki pakistan kaa uttar pashchim seemaant praant main he. iske alaava ve 1997 se 1999 tak kaibar-pakhtuunakva ke mukhyamantri ke pad par bhi rah chuke hai.
