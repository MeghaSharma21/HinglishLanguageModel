 barsaat 1995 kii hindi bhashaa main bani premakahani film he. yah bobi deol or tvinkal khanna kii shuruaati film thi. yah rajkumaar santoshi dwaara nirdeshit kii gai thi. sanyog se, 10 varshon ke baad abhinayta bobi deol ne isi film sheershak se barsaat main kaam kiya. film superhit rahi thi. 
 badal ek sidhaa lekin budhimaan yuva vyakti he jo college main jane ke liye ek chote se gaanv se bade shahar main aata he. use damru jo khud ko danni kahata he, dwaara shahar kii jeevanstyli se parichit kiya jaataa he. jab badal college main jaataa he, to vah sundar tina oberoi se miltaa he or kuch galatfahami ke baad, ve pyaar main pad jate hai.
 yah sambandh tina ke amir vidhur sautele-pita suresh oberoi dwaara anumodith nahin he, jo tina se apane dost ke bete ke saath shaadi karne kii ummid karte hai. suresh college ke principal se shikaayat karte hai ki badal ek raat main college main ek ladki ke yon utpeedan ke liye jimmedaar he. lekin tina badal ke paksh main sakshya detii he or jor detii he ki vah nirdosh he. suresh tab sahaayta ke liye shahar ke sahaayak ayukta or bhrasht police adhikari, negi se madad letaa he.
 negi ne badal ko giraftaar kar liya or use kaid kar diya. badal ke vidhur pita bhairon shahar main aate hai taaki yah samajhane kii koshish kii ja sake ki badal jail main kyon he. suresh ne negi ko badal ko maarane ke liye anubandh diya. negi sahamat hai or badal ko maarane ke liye gundo ke giroh ko kiraye par letaa he. jab tina jaanati he ki badal mar nahin gaya he, to vah uske paas jaati he. use rokane ke prayaas main, suresh kii maut ho jaati he or badal negi ko maar daalata he. ant main, tina or badal ko mar rahaa suresh unki jodi ko manjuuri de detaa he.
 
 sangeet nadeem shravan dwaara diya gaya he or bol samir ke hai.
