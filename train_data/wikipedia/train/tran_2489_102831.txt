 fatehpuri masjid chandani chowk kii puraani gali ke pashchimi chor par sthit he. iskaa nirmaan mughal badshaah shahjahan kii patni fatehpuri begum ne 1650 main karavaaya tha. unheen ke naam par iskaa naam fatehpuri masjid padaa.) ye begum fatehpur se thim. taj mahal parisar main bani masjid bhi inhen begum ke naam par he..
 laal pattharon se bani yah masjid mughal vaastukala kaa ek behtareen namoona he. masjid ke donon ore laal pathar se bane stambhon kii kataren hai. is masjid main ek kunda bhi he jo safed sangemarmar se bana he. yah masjid kai dharmik vaad-vivaad kii gavaah rahi he.
 angrezon ne is masjid ko 1857 ke pratham bhartiya swatantraaa sangram ke baad, nilaam kar diya tha, jise raay lala chunna mel ne maatr 19,000/- rupye main kharid liya tha. jinke vanshaj aaj bhi chandani chowk main chunnamal haveli main rahete hai.), jinhone is masjid ko sanbhale rakhaa tha. baad main 1877 main sarkaar ne ise chaar gaanvon ke badale main waapas adhikrut kar musalamaanon ko de diya, jab unhen dilli main rahane kaa dobaaraa adhikaar diya gaya tha. aisi hi ek duusari masjid, akbarabaadi begum dwaara banavaai gai thi, jise angrezon ne barbaad karvaa diya tha.
