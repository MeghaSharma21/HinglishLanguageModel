 vaishnav sampradaay, bhagwaan vishnu ko ishwar maanane waalon kaa sampradaay he.iske antargat chaar sampradaay mukhya rup se aate hai.pehle hai acharya ramanuj, nimbarkacharya, ballabhacharya, madhavacharya.iske alaava uttar bharat main acharya ramanand bhi vaishnav sampradaay ke acharya hue or chaitanyamhaprabhu bhi vaishnav acharya he jo bengal main hue. ramandacharya ji ne sarv dharm sambhaav kii bhaavana ko bal dete hue kabir, rahim sabhi varnon ke vyaktiyon ko sagun bhakthi kaa upadesh kiya. aage ramanand sanmpradaay main goswami tulsidas hue jinhone shri ramcharitamanas kii rachana karke jansamanya tak bhagavat mahima ko pahunchaaya. unki anya rachanaayen - vinay patrika, dohavali, gitavali, barbai ramayana ek jyotish granth ramajna prashnawali kaa bhi nirmaan kiya.
