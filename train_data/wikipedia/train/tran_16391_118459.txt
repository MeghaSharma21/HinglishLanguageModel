 brigadier sant singh bhartiya sena main ek adhikari the. vah bhartiya sena ke chah adhikariyon main se ek the, jinhen maha vir chakra, bharat kaa doosraa sabase bada yudhkalin sainya sajaavat, do baar se sajaayaa gaya tha. vah 1971 main bharat-pakistan yuddh ke dauraan mukti bahrain ke prashikshan main shaamil bhartiya adhikariyon main se ek the. 
 sant singh kaa janm 12 july 1 9 21 ko punjab ke faridakot main pajagnan kalaan main hua tha. unhone brijendra hai skool, faridakot or aresadi college, ferozepur main adhyayan kiya. 16 farvari 1 9 47 ko unhen sikh lite infentry main niyukt kiya gaya tha. unki ek beti satidar cor or ek putr brigadier sarbajit randhava hai. 
 unhone 1 9 64 main sikh lite infentry ke commander ke rup main padbhaar sambhala. unhone 1 9 65 main bharat-pakistan yuddh main opi hill kii ladaai main regiment kii jeet kaa netritv kiya. vah 1 9 68 tak regiment ke commander bane rahe. vah bhi mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai mukti Bahini or uske brigade ke gorilla takaton ko prashikshan main pakistani sena kii raksha ke liye guard ke baad, dhaka main chadhaai, or is tarah dushman ko aatmasamarpan karne ke liye majaboor kiya. unhone 1 9 73 main sevanivrutt hue..
