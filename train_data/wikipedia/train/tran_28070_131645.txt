 rashtriya doorasanvedi centre bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan kaa ek pramukh centre he. yah hyderabad main sthit he. yahan doorasanvedi gatividhiyaan hoti hai. yahan upgrah dwaara liye gaye chitron ko upyogkartaaon evam jansamanya tak pahunchaane kaa kaary kiya jaataa he. nagareey yojana, krishi, khanan or matsyiki main is centre kaa yogadan ullekhneey he.
