 
 nisoth ke kai bhed hote hai -safed nisoth, shyaam nisoth or laal nisoth |
 safed nisoth kii beli jangal main hoti he | isamen safed phool aate hai | gol -gol fal aate hai | unamem chaar -chaar beej aate hai | pattey nok daar gol hote hai | isaki beli kii lakdi main teen dhaaren hoti hai | safed nisoth sabase uttam hota he | 
 kaale nisoth kii bhi lata hoti he | phool kaala pen liye bangnee ranga kaa hota he | pattey gol-gol nokdaar hote hai | parantu fal tathaa pattey safed nisoth se kuch chote hote hai | or sab aakaar miltaa he | 
 laal nisoth bhi isi prakaar kaa hota he | laalapan liye pattey hote hai | 
 maatra - safed nisoth kii 2 maase se 4|| maase tak maatra he | kaale kii maatra 1se 3 maase tak he or laal kii 3 maase se 6 maase tak maatra he | gun -nisoth kadua, charpara, garam, rechak, ruksh, tikshn, tikt, rasanvit or katu paaki he | iskaa julab hitkaari he | yah mel stambhak, grahani, udar, krimi, kaph, pandu, pleeha, fever, pitt, vaat rakt, udaavart, udaavart, shoth tathaa kusht, kandu, vran, tathaa hriday rog naashak he | safed nisoth - rechak, swaadisht, garam, vaatnashaka, he | yah soth, pitt, kaph, fever tathaa udar rogon ko dur karne vaala he | kaala nisoth - safed nisoth kii apeksha hin gun vaala kintu isamen virechan gun ati teevr he tathaa murchha, dah, mad, bhranti naashak evam kanth utkarshan karne vaala he | laal nisoth - madhur, kasaiyla, mridurechi, ruksh, charpara he | isse kaph, sangrahani or mel vishtambh kaa naash hota he |
