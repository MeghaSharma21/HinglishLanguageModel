 ustad zakir hossain bharat ke sabase prasiddh tabalaa vadak hai. zakir hossain tabalaa vadak ustad allaa rakhaa ke bete hai.
  zakir hossain ko san 2002 main bharat sarkaar dwaara kala ke kshetra main padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. ye maharashtra se hai.
 zakir hossain kaa bachapan mumbai main hi beetaa. 12 saal kii umr se hi zakir hossain ne sangeet kii duniya main apane table kii awaz ko bikherna shuru kar diya tha. praarambhik shiksha or college ke baad zakir hossain ne kala ke kshetra main apane aap ko sthapit karnaa shuru kar diya. 1973 main unka pehla elbum living in da material world aayaa tha. uske baad to jaise zakir hossain ne thaan liya ki apane table kii awaz ko duniya bhar main bikherenge. 1973 se lekar 2007 tak zakir hossain vibhinn antarrashtriya samarohon or elbamon main apane table kaa dam dikhaate rahe. zakir hossain bharat main to bahut hi prasiddh hai saath hi saath hi vishwa ke vibhinn hisson main bhi samaan rup se lokapriy hai.
 1988 main jab unhen padam shri kaa puraskaar mila tha tab vah mahaj 37 varsh ke the or is umr main yah puraskaar paane vaale sabase kam umr ke vyakti bhi the.isi tarah 2002 main sangeet ke kshetra main unke yogadan ke liye unhen padam bhushan kaa puraskaar diya gaya tha.zakir hossain ko 1992 or 2009 main sangeet kaa sabase pratishthit puraskaar grami award bhi mila he.
