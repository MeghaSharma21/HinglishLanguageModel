 irafaan khan kaa janm madhya pradesh ke gwalior shahar main hua tha. sthaneey akhbaar dainik bhaskar or swadesh main san 82-1989 tak cartoon banaate rahe. dilli kii shridharani art gailaree main apni pradarshini ke dauraan ve navabharat times lakhanu ke liye chun liye gaye. 1994 main dilli aakar iconomik times, finenshial express,asian aij, main staff cartoonist rahe. 2000 main zee news main varisht cartoonist ke pad par kaam karte hue apana taak sho shakshiyat host kiya, 2003 main enditivi ke sho gustaakhee maaf kii script likhi or sahaara samay par itani si baat host kiya. ab tak 3 sankalan prakashit ho chuke hai. insiiarti ke paathyakram kii pustakon main irafaan sahit desh ke vibhin anya cartooniston ke sampadakeey cartoonon ko bhi shaamil kiya gaya tha. japan foundation ne "asian cartoon pradashani" ke apane vaarshik kaarykram ke tahat, 2005 main nauvi pradarshini ke liye prayek asian desh se ek cartoonist ke chayan hetu bharat kii ore se irafaan kaa chayan kiya. anek puraskaron se sammanit irafaan ab tak 6 cartoon pradarshiniyaan kar chuke hai. jinamein cricket, aatankwaad, sampradayikta or global varming par pradarshiniyaan kaafii charchit rahi hai. 2007 main mid de akhbaar main bharat ke purv mukhya nyaayadhish par cartoon banane par dilli ucch nyaayalay ne irafaan ko 4 maheene kii sazaa sunai. yah maamla abhi ucchtam nyaayalay main vichaaradhin he.
