 
 venkataraman raghavan ek sanskrit vidwaan tathaa sangitajna the. unhen padmabhushan, sanskrit ke liye sahitya academy puraskaar sahit kai puraskaar pradaan kiye gayi the. unhone 120 se adhik pustakon kii rachana kii tathaa 1200 se adhik lekh likhe. 
 inke dwaara rachit ek soundaryashastra bhoj shringaar prakaash ke liye unhen san 1966 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
