 alirajpur bhartiya rajya madhya pradesh kaa ek jila he. yah madhya pradesh ke pashchimi bhaag main padataa he. yah kuch saalon pehle hi jhabua jile se kuch kshetron ko vilag karke ek alag jila banaaya gaya tha. iske naam ko parivartit karke 'aalirajpur' karne kii maang bhi kuch samoohon dwaara kii gayi he.
