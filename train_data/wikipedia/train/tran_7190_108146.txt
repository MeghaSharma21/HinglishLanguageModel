 vyangyachitr yaa cartoon drushya kala kaa ek ek rup he. cartoon shabd ke arth main samay ke saath vistaar hota gaya he. is samay cartoon shabd kaa prayog kai arthon main hota he. mul rup main, us aarmabhik rekhachitr ko cartoon kehete the jo kisi painting ko taiyaar karne ke dauraan banaayi jaati thi. iske baad, aadhunik yug main samachar patr evam patrikaon main chhapane vaale hasyodapadak rekhachitron ko cartoon kaha jane laga. aajkal to kai anya prakaar ke chitron evam chalit-chitron ko bhi cartoon kaha jane laga he.
 bhartiya vyangyachitrakaar
