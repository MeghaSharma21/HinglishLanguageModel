 shardul thakur  ek bhartiya cricket team ke khilaadi he jo gharelu cricket mumbai ke liye khelte he. ye pratham shrenee cricket khelte hue bhartiya antarrashtriya cricket team ke 218wein khilaadi he. inhone indian premier lig main kings ilevan punjab ke liye khele he jabaki abhi mumbai indians ke liye khelte he.
 shardul thakur ne apane schuli cricket jivan main ek baar 6 gendon par lagaataar 6 chakke lagaae the. inhone apane pratham shrenee cricket kii shuruyaat navambar 2012 main jaipur ke savai maansingh stadium main rajasthan ke khilaaf khelaa tha. inke karier kii shuruyaat acchhee nahin rahi or pehle chaar maichon main 82.0 kii gendbaaji ausat se mahaj 4 wicket liye the. lekin 2012-13 kii ranjai trophy main inhone 26.25 kii ausat se 6 test maichon main 27 wicket liye jisamen ek baar ek pari main 5 wicket bhi liye the. iske alaava inhone 2013-14 kii ranjai trophy main 20.81 kii gendbaaji ausat se 10 maichon main 48 wicket liye the.
 2015-16 kii ranjai trophy ke final match main saurashtra kii team ke khilaaf aath wicket liye the or mumbai kii team ko 41veen jeet mili thi.
 2015 indian premier lig ke liye inhone 20 lakh ke liye kings ilevan punjab ke khelnaa kaa nirnay liya tha. inhone apana apana indian premier lig kaa match dilli dairedevills ke khilaaf khelaa tha jisamen inhone 4 overon main 38 ran dekar 1 wicket liya tha.
 march 2017 main inhen 2017 indian premier lig ke liye rising pune superjayants ke liye chunaa gaya tha.
 inhen bhartiya cricket team ke 2016 ke west indies daure ke liye 16 sadsy team main chunaa gaya tha lekin inako khilne kaa mauka nahin mil paaya tha. iske baad agast 2017 main inkaa naam bhartiya cricket team kaa srilanka daura 2017 ke liye vanade siries main shaamil kiya gaya tha jisamen inhen khilne kaa mauka bhi mila. inhone apane ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket kii shuruyaat 31 agast ko srilanka ke khilaaf kii thi.. shardul 10 nambar kii jersey pahanane vaale duusare bhartiya khilaadi ban gaye he pehle sachin tendulkar pahana karte the is kaaran saamaajik media par iskaa kaaphi vivaad hua he.
