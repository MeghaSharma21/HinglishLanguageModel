 shiya chandra, madhya-purv main shiya muslim avasthiti ko vyakhyayit karne vaali, ek bhu-rajnatik avadharana he. is pad kaa prayog madhya-purv kii raajnneeti main shiya drushtikon se kshetreey sahyog kii kshamta ko darshane ke liye kiya jaataa rahaa he. germanon main avadharana ke taur par is pad kaa prayog aam he. german bhashaa main ise shiya ardhchandra kehete hai. shiya chandra naamak pad gadhane kaa shrey jordan ke raja abdulla dwitiya ko jaataa he, jiske baad yah shabd raajnneetik bahason main lokapriy ho gaya.
 azarbejaan, iran, iraq, bahrain tathaa lebanon aadi deshon main shiya bahusankhyak hai. sammilit taur par in deshon se ek ardhchandrakaar akruti banati he.turki, yaman, afghanistan, kuwait, pakistan, saudi arab, sanyukt arab emirate, bharat tathaa syria main bhi shiya alpsankhyakon kii ek badi maatra nivas karti he.
