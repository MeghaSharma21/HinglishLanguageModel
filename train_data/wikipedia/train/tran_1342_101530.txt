 lev bulgaria kii aadhikarik mudra he, jise sau stotinki se vibhajit kiya jaataa he. bulgarian bhashaa main lev kaa matlab line hota he. bulgaria ke european union main milane ke baad anumaan lagaaya ja rahaa he ki 1 janvari 2012 se lev kaa sthaan euro le legaa, lekin kuch vishleshkon kaa maananaa he ki vartmaan vaishvik mudra sthiti ko dekhte hue 2015 se pehle bulgaria main euro prachlan main nahin aa paaegaa.
