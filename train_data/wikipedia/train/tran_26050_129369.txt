 ampedocliz ek pracheen unani vichaarak tha.
 ampedocliz kaa sochna tha ki vishwa main sab kuch chah tatwon se bana he. in main se chaar he niskriy  prithvi, jal, vaayu, agni. yah chaaron tatv hameshaa se hai or hameshaa tak hi rahenge. na to inkaa utpaadan hota he na dhwans. duusare do samaanya dhang ke tatv to nahin he par tattvik shaktiyaan he  prem or vivaad. in do shaktiyon ke kaaran chaar niskriy tatv sammilit or alag hote hai. ampedocliz shaakaahaari ke liye hota he.
