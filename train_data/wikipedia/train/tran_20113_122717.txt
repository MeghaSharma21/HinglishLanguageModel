 da offis  ek amareeki hasya television shrinkhalaa he, jiskaa prasaaran NBC dwaara kiya jaataa he. BBC kii shrinkhalaa da offis kaa ek anukulan, yah shrinkhalaa kaalpanik dandar miflin paper company kii scranton, pencilvenia shakha ke karyalayn karmchaariyon ke dainik jivan ko pradarshit karti he. ek vaastavik vruttchitr ke pradarshan kii nakal karne ke liye, ise ek ekal-camera seoup main studio darshakon yaa ek hasya trek ke bina filmaaya gaya he.
 da offis ko kaaryakari nirmaataa greg daniels, saturde nite live, king of da hill or da simpasans ke anbhavi lekhak, dwaara amareeki darshakon ke liye anukulit kiya gaya tha. mul BBC shrinkhalaa ke rachanaakaar, riki garvais or stephen marchant iske kaaryakari nirmaataa hai or unhone daniels ke saath pratham bhaag kaa sah-lekhan bhi kiya or teesraa sanskaran, "da kanvict", likhaa. ise universal media studios ke saath daniel ke dedal-di productions or reveli productions dwaara sah-nirmit kiya gaya he. yah kaarykram pehali baar 24 march 2005 ko NBC par ek madhya-kaal pratisthaapan ke rup main prasaarit hua.
 sanyukt rajya america main da offis kaa network-se-pare samoohan, ullekhneey rup se TBS or fox ke swaamitv vaale steshnon main, 2009 kii sharad ritu main shuru hua.
 vartmaan main da offis kaa chatha sanskaran prasaarit ho rahaa he or saatven sanskaran ke liye iskaa navinikaran kiya gaya he.
 NBC ke programmer kevin reli ne multah nirmaataa ben silverman ko maaikel scot kii bhoomika ke liye paul giamati ko lene kaa suzav diya, lekin abhinayta ne yah bhoomika nibhaane se inkaar kar diya. martin short, hank azaaria or bob odenkark kii bhi isamen ruchi hone kii jaankaree praapt hui. janvari 2004 main, variety ne bataaya ki is bhoomika ke liye lokapriy kamedi central kaarykram da daily sho wyeth jon stewart ke steve kairell se baatcheet chal rahi thi. us samay, ve pehle hi NBC ke ek anya madhya-kaal pratisthaapan hasya dharawahik, kam tu papa ke prati vachanabaddh the, lekin yah shrinkhalaa bahut sheeghra nirast kar di gai, jisase ve puri tarah da offis ke prati vachanabaddh ho gaye. baad main kairell ne bataaya ki unka audishan kiye jane se purv unhone british shrinkhalaa kii mul pratham kadi kaa lagbhag aadha bhaag hi dekha tha. unhone ise dekhnaa is bhay ke chalate band kar diya ki ve gravis ke charitra-chithran kii nakal prarambh kar denge.
 rain wilson, jinhen shakti ke bhukhe chaapluus dwite shrut kii bhoomika ke liye chunaa gaya tha, ne unke audishan se purv is shrinkhalaa kii prayek kadi ko dekha tha. multah wilson kaa audishan maaikel kii bhoomika, jise ve ek "bhayanak gravis]] hamshakl" kehete hai, ke liye kiya gaya tha; haalanki kalakaaron kaa chayan kar rahe nirdeshkon ne unke audishan ko dwite ke liye adhik pasand kiya or unhen us bhoomika ke liye chun liya gaya.
 kendriya prem charitron, gym or palm, kii bhoomikaaon ke liye chune jane se purv tak jon crossinsky or jena fisher vastutah agyaat the. jon ne B.J. novak ke saath schuli shiksha pai thi or unase unki acchhee mitrataa thi, jiske parinamswaroop unka audishan hua or unhen gym halport kii bhoomika mili. crossinsky ne is shrinkhalaa ke audishan ke dauraan durghatanavash daniels kaa apmaan karne kii ghatana ko yaad kiya, jisamen unhone kaha tha ki "mujhe aashaa he ki ise band nahin karenge." iske baad daniels ne apana parichay diya or crossinsky ko bataaya ki ve koun hai. fisher ne yathasambhav ubaau banane kaa prayaas karte hue apane audishan kii taiyaari kii or mul palm kesh-sajja banaai. NPR ke fresh air ko diye ek saakshaatkaar main fisher ne palm or gym ke liye audishan prakriya ke antim charnon ko yaad kiya, jisamen nirmaataa vibhinn sambhavit paamon or jimon kii ek saath jodiyaan banaakar unke taal-mail ko aankane kaa prayaas kar rahe the. jab fisher ne crossinsky ke saath apana drishy samaapt kiya, to unhone unhen bataaya ki ve unki pasandida palm hai, jiske pratyuttar main unhone kaha ki ve unke pasandida gym hai.
 apane unnatisheel kaary ke liye jane jane vaale samarthak kalakaaron main shaamil hai: angela kinse, ket flanneri, auskar nunes, lesley david baker, bryan bomgartner, melora hardin or david denman. kinse kaa audishan multah palm kii bhoomika ke liye kiya gaya tha. nirmaataaon ne mahasoos kiya ki ve us charitra ke liye "atyadhik shaktishaalee" thim, lekin unhone angela martin kii bhoomika ke liye unhen punah bulaayaa, jo unhone hasil kar lee. meredith palper kii bhoomika praapt karne se purv flannary kaa audishan jine levinson-gold kii bhoomika ke liye hua tha. bomgartner kaa audishan multah stanley ke liye kiya gaya tha, lekin antat unhen kevin kii bhoomika main liya gaya. ken kwapis ko philis smith, ek kalakaar chayan sahyogi, kaa anya kalakaaron ke saath padhane kaa dhang itnaa adhik pasand aayaa ki unhone unhen philis kii bhoomika ke liye chun liya. tisare sanskaran ke prarambh main aid helms or rashida jones dandar miflin stamford ke sadasyon ke rup main is samooh se jude. haalanki baad main kalakaaron ke is samooh ko jones ek aavarti bhoomika ke liye chhod dene vaale the, lekin farvari 2007 main NBC ne ghoshnaa kii ki helms ko ek niyamit shrinkhalaa ke rup main pronnat kiya ja rahaa tha.

 da offis kii prayek kadi ke liye ek puri patakatha likhi gai he; haalanki, abhinaytaon ko filmakan prakriya ke dauraan taatkaalik pradarshan ke avasar diye jate hai. "hamaare sho 100 pratishat patakatha-yukt hote hai," fisher ne samajhaayaa. "sab kuch kagaz par likhaa hota he. lekin hamen bhi thodaa khilwaad karne kaa mauka miltaa he. steve or rain pratibhashali taatkaalik pradarshak hai."
 maaikel dwaara tisare sanskaran ke bhaag "ge wich hant" main auskar par pravisht chumban-drishy taatkaalik rup se pradarshit tha. "steve achaanak hi utana aage bad gaye," steve ne likhaa.  da offis ek kaalpanik vruttchitr he. is sho kaa praathmik vaahak yah he ki ek camera dal dandar miflin or iske karmchaariyon ko filmaane, sambhawatah choubison ghante, kaa nirnay liya he. camere kii upasthiti ko paatro, visheshtah mike scot, dwaara pasand kiya jaataa he, jo utsaahpurvak filmakan main shaamil hote hai. duusare, udaahranarth jine levinson, aksar isaki upasthiti se naaraaz yaa asahaj ho jate hai. pratyaksh saakshaatkaar yaa "sweekaarokti" sho kii mukhya kriya kii poorak hoti he, jisamen paatr ek-ek kar camera-dal ke saath din bhar kii ghatnaon ke bare main baatcheet karte hai. kabhi-kabhi do paatr ek hi samay par aapas main tathaa camere se baat karte hue ek hi saakshaatkaar ko saajha karte hai. sabase ullekhneey rup se aisa gym or palm ke saath, yaa kabhi kabhi, auskar or kevin yaa keli or rayaan, gym or dwite ke saath hota he or ek baar maaikel or tobi ke saath bhi hua he. dwite aksar maaikel ke saakshaatkaar main vyavdhaan utpann karte hai kyonki ve maaikel kaa saakshaatkaar prarambh hote samay aksar screen ke baahar maaikel kii bagal main khadey rahete hai. kuch paatr camere kii upasthiti kaa prayog apane laabh ke liye karte hai. udhaaharan ke liye, "christmas party" main, philis ke premi bob vens ne apane vyapaar ke liye prachaar paane ke liye baar-baar "bob vens, vens refrijreshan" ke rup main apana parichay diya. anya udaahranon main, aisa prathit hota he ki camere ne kathanak kii panktiyon ko prabhavit kar diya he. "da dandis" main, sharaab ke nashe main dhutt palm koi baat gym se lagbhag sweekaar kar leti he, lekin camere kii upasthiti kaa ahsaas hote hi vah sharma kar dur hat jaati he. "i-mail servilians" main, palm camera dal ke sadasyon se dwite or angela ke gupt sambandh ke bare main pramaan dhundne main sahaayta karne ko kahati he, jo baad main unke dwaara pradaan bhi kiya jaataa he. "fan ran" main, camere gym or palm ko chumban lete hue pakad lete hai, jiske parinamswaroop unhen camera dal ke samaksh yah sweekaar karnaa padataa he ki ve vastutah dating kar rahe hai.
 shuruaati kadiyon main, camera dal mukhyatah karyalayn rachana tak simit dikhaai dete hai, lekin jaise-jaise sho kaa vistaar hua or isamen paatro ke vyaktigat jivan ke bare main adhik jaankaree shaamil kii gai, to camaron ne ek aksar-sarvyaapee upasthiti praapt kar lee or yahaan tak ki ve kalakaaron kii chhavi main ghuspaith bhi karne lage. aksar karyaalay se baahar or kabhi-kabhi charitron kaa unke gharon tak bhi peechha kiya jaataa he. camere gym ke barbacue or maaikel ke raatri-bhoj main or yahaan tak ki us samay bhi upasthit the, jab gym or palm ek baar dwite ke beet farm main raat-bhar chuttee bitaane gaye- tarksaadhay rup se ye sabhi vyaktigat ghatnaayen thim, jinka kaary se koi sambandh nahin tha. aksar band darwaazon ke peeche hone vaali baatcheet ko kisi khidki yaa darvaaze kii daraar ke madhyam se filmaaya jaataa he. "da ingeuri" main yah darshaya gaya he ki maaikel ne ek betaar lavelior microphone pahana hua he, jisase yah spasht hota he ki camere aksar band darwaazon ke peeche hone vaali baatcheet ko sun paane main bhi sakasham hote hai. camaron ne "vailentins de" par jine ko maaikel kaa chumban lete hue pakadaa he, jisase jine ko adhik khiz hui or jaisaa ki upar ullekh kiya gaya he, dwite or angela tathaa gym or palm donon ke vyaktigat sambandhon kaa khulaasa bhi kiya he. gaiyr-praathmik paatro yaa un atirikt kalakaaron, jinhone camera-dal kaa saamana kiya he, ko saamanytah isse koi aashcharya nahin hota or ve isse aprabhavit rahete hai or yahaan tak ki maaikel, gym or kairen ke nigam se sambandhit saakshatkaaron main bhi camaron ko pravesh karne kii anumati di gai. ek aisa drishy bhi pradarshit kiya gaya he, jisamen maaikel or holi ko shaareerik sambandh banane ke liye karyaalay main chupake-se waapas aate hue bhi dikhaaya gaya he or haalanki ve pehle hi camaron ko chakma dekar baahar fansaa chuke hote hai, lekin phir bhi un donon kii baatcheet or aapasi antahkriyaaon ko suna ja saktaa he kyonki maaikel ne ek microphone pahana hua he, jisaki awaz ko band karne kii koshish karte samay vah galati se use badha detaa he.
 da offis ke liye theme geet je fergyusan dwaara likhaa gaya or da scrantons dwaara ise prastut kiya gaya. ise sheershak anukram ke saath bajaayaa jaataa he, jisamen kalakaaron or nirmaan dal ke sadasyon ke ullekh ke saath kuch scrantan drishy or karmchaariyon ko karyaalay ke dainik kaary karte hue pradarshit kiya jaataa he. shrinkhalaa kii kuch kadiyon main theme geet ke ek sankshipt sanskaran kaa prayog kiya gaya he. chauthe sanskaran se shuru karte hue, theme geet ko samaapti-ullekhon, jo pehle dhwani-viheen guzarate the, ke saath bajaayaa jaataa he. multah prayek kadi kii shuruyaat theme geet ke saath hoti thi; duusare sanskaran ke praarambhik daur main "offis olympix" kadi se shuru karte hue, adhikansh kadiyaan ek shant shuruyaat ke saath khulti hai or baad main theme geet bajaayaa jaataa he. sheershak anukram main pradarshit imaaraton ke bahya-bhaag scranton, pencilvenia main sthit vaastavik imaaraten hai, jinka filmakan kalakaar dal ke sadsy jon crossinsky dwaara kiya gaya tha.
