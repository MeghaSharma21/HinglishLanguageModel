 yuiefee europea championships praathmik sangh footbal pratiyogita yuiefee ke sadasyon ke varisht purushon kii rashtriya tiimon se chunaav ladaa he. 1960 ke baad se har chaar saal yojit, yah mul rup se yuiefee europea raashtra cap bulaayaa gaya tha, 1968 main vartmaan naam ko badal rahaa he. 1996 toornament ke saath shuru, vishisht championships aksar euro ke rup main sanderbhit kar rahe.
 pehle toornament main pravesh karne ke liye mejabaan deshon ke alaava anya sabhi tiimon ko ek kwalifing prakriya main pratispardhaa. championships vijetaon nimnlikhit fifa confiderations cap main pratispardhaa karne kaa avasar praapt karte hai, lekin aisa karne ke liye baadhya nahin kar rahe hai.
 sabase haal championships, 2016 main france dwaara mejabanit or championships portugal ne jiti. agle europea championships europe bhar main yojit kiya jayega.
 
 
 
 
 prayek toornament ke liye, prayek final toornament main tiimon kii sankhya dikhaae gaye hai.
