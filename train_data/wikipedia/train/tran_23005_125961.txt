 gheraa kaa arth he kisi nagar yaa durg ko sainikon dwaara gherna taaki isko jeeta ja sake. gheraa daalkar yuddh karnaa ek prakaar kaa lagaataar chalane vaala kintu kam tivrata kaa yuddh he jisamen ek paksh rakshaatmak ravaiya apanaate hue kisi durg yaa diwaar ke andar 'majboot' sthiti main hota he or doosraa paksh use chaaron ore se ghere rahata he taaki baahar se andar koi na ja paye na hi andar se koi bhaag nikalne main safal ho. is tarah kii gheraabandi ke dwaara shatru kii bhojan saamagree, jalaapurti, evam anya sabhi suvidhaayen roak di jaatin hai. iske alaava 'gheraa injanon', top, surang aadi ke dwaara shatru par prahaar kiya jaataa he.
