 bagaale thapa va bagaalya thapa nepal ke arya kshatriya samudaay khas kshetri gharaana he. ye gharanake log thapa namka prayog karte he. nepal, bharat tathaa anya sthanon me nivas vaale hindu dharmavalambi visheshtayah nepali thar thapa ke ek gharaana he. ye purnatay kshetri varg ke bharopeli bhashi parvate khas hai. inke gotra aatreya he. 
 ye parivaar ne thapa vanshaka udai karaayaa. bagaale thapa itihasase shashakt padomen jaise mukhiya, jimmal, panchpradhan, badahakim, kaaji aadi se sushobhit he. ye nepali raajnneetike vritame agraj he. basnet, pande, kunwar or bishtake saath 5 kaaji parivarame aate hai. bagaale thapa or pandeke bich aitihaasik bhayankar dusmani thi. jyundo baagh namase kahalaanevaale badakaji amar singh thapa bagaale thapa he. amarsinghke putr ranjor singh thapa bhi nepal-angrej yudhke sarjak the. 
 mukhtiyaar jarnel pra.m.bhimsen thapa gorkhaake bagaale thapa parivaarake he. unke bhatijaa mathaverasingh thapa bhi nepalka pradhanmantri rahe. bhimsenke bhaai nainsingh thapa general the or thapathali darbarake maalik the. chote bhaai ranvir singh thapane nepal-angrej yume makwanpur se hariharpurtakaka morcha samhaala tha. nainsinghke naati kaaji ujir singh thapane palpame angrej viruddhake chaturyin nepalmein charitaarth he. pichle samayme nepali senake pradhansenapati hue dharmapalavarsinh thapa bhi bagaale thapa hi he.
