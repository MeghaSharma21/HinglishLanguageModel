 is khel main teen patale dhaatu yaa plastic ke stambh hote hai. savase baayin ore ke stambh main teen chhidradaar platenuma gutke hote he jo alag alag beas ke hote he. sabase neeche sabase bada gutka hota he upar sabase chota gutka hota he. khel main teenon gutkon ko tisare stambh main le janaa hota he. ek baar main ek gutke ko uthaakar duusare yaa tisare stambh main rakh sakate he. parantu kisi tulanaatmak rup se bade gutke ko bade ke upar hi rakhaa ja saktaa he.
