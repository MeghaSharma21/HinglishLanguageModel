 yah sabase pracheenatam rajvansh tha. mahabharat tathaa puraanon ke anusaar jaraasandh ke pita tathaa chediraj vasu ke putr brahadrath ne brahadrath vansh kii sthapana kii. is vansh main das raja hue jisamen brahadrath putr jaraasandh evam pratapi samraat tha. jaraasandh ne kaashi, kaushal, chedi, malwa, videh, ang, vang, kaling, kashmir or gandhar raajaaon ko parajeet kiya. mathura shaasha kansa ne apni bahan kii shaadi jaraasandh se kii tathaa brahadrath vansh kii rajdhani vashumati yaa girivraj yaa rajgrih ko banaai. bhagwaan shrikrishna kii sahaayta se pandav putr bhim ne jaraasandh ko dwand yuddh main maar diya. uske baad uske putr sahadev ko shaasha banaaya gaya. is vansh kaa antim raja ripunjay tha. ripunjay ko uske darbari mantri pulik ne markar apane putr ko raja bana diya. iske baad ek anya darbari ‘maheey’ ne pulik or uske putr kii hatya kar apane putr bimbisar ko gaddi par baithaaya. isa purv 600 main brahadrath vansh ko samaapt kar ek naye rajvansh kii sthapana hui. puraanon ke anusaar manu ke putr sudyumna ke putr kaa hi naam “gaya" tha.
