 arun krishnaji kamble . arun krishnaji kamble marathi saahitya main lekhak or daliton main agrani netritv the. arun kamble, dalit panthars ke sansthaapak hai or vartmaan main vishvavidhyalay mumbai main marathi vibhaag ke professor ke rup main kaam kar rahe the. vah janata dal ke mahasachiv the. unhone daliton, pichhadaa varg ke logon or alpsankhyakon ke paksh main pramukh nirnay liye the.
 arun kamble ji kaa janm 14 march 1953 ko sangli jile me kargani gaav main hua | unke mata-pita shikshak the | unki schuli padai kargani main hui | kalege ke din sangli jile me wellingdon mahavidhyalay main vyatit huye |mumbai ke siddharth mahavidhyalay me em. e. ko marathi vishay se suvarna padak praapt kiya. 1973 me professor kar jude|
 arun kamble, ek lekhak, kavi or sampadak the. saanskrithik sangharsh ramayana, chiver, vaad samvaad, yug pravartak ambedkar, chadhwaliche diwas jaise kai kitaaben likhi he. unhe prabud ratna puraskaar, life time achievement antarrashtriya puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya he. unki kuch kitaaben or lekh angreji, german, french, gujarati, kannada, telegu, malayalam, urdu or hindi main anuwaad kiya gaya he |
 arun kamble ji 13 disambar ko hyderabad main " antarrashtriya ambedkar santhaan " main ek sangoshtee main mukhya atithi ke rup main bhaag lene gayi the |unhe hyderabad main ek jhil main mrit paaya gaya | ek rahasyamay tareeke se unki sandigdh maut kii khabar ek bade jatke ke rup main aayaa thi | " unka jivan ke prati bahut hi sakaaratmak drushtikon tha | unhone mujhase kaha tha ki vah 15 disambar ko aayenge or unke chaatro ke anusaar usee din vyaakhyaan honge " yah unki bahan mangal tiramare ji ne kaha |
