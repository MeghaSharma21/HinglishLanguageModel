 divangat film va tivi kalakaar priya tendulkar dwaara abhinit rajani doordarshan par ravivaar ko prasaarit atyant lokapriy dharawahik tha. iske nirdeshak basu chatarji the. isamen unke pati kii bhoomika vaastavik jivan main unke pati karan raazdaan ne nibhaai thi, putree kii bhoomika bal kalakaar baby guddu ne kii thi. dharawahik kaa sheershak geet aashaa bhonsle ne gaaya tha. basu prarambh main is bhoomika ke liye abhinetri padmini kolhapure ko lenaa chaahate the par ve is prakalp ko samay na de sakin.
 rajani ek aam gruhani kii samaaj main vyaapt bhrashtachar va anyaay ke khilaaf kii roj kii jang kii kahaniyaan dikhaai jaati thi, dharawahik hone ke uparaant bhi aam taur par ek apisod main ek hi prahasan dikhaaya jaataa tha. rajani ne samaaj par bhi khaasaa prabhaav daalaa or logon ko in saamaajik buraaiyon ke khilaaf loha lene kii prerana di. 2002 main priya kii asamay mrityu par tatkaaleen pradhanmantri atal bihari vaajapeyi ne kaha, "rajani ke rup main unki jihadi bhoomika ne anek saamaajik muddon ko mukherit kiya".
