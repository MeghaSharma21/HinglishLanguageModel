 ritu vaasu primalani ek bhartiya stand up komedian va paryaavaranvaadi hai. 2015 main apane kaam ke liye unhone bharat sarkaar dwaara "naari shakti puraskaar" se sammanit kiya gaya tha. 
 primalani nayi dilli main pali badi hai or unhone usielai se bhugol main master kii degree hasil kii hai. 
 vaasu primalani ne sanyukt raashtra main paryaavaran shiksha ke liye ek gaiyr laabhakaari santhaan "thimakka" kii sthapana kii. is sanghathan ne restaraan ke liye paryaavaran paraamarsh sevaayen pradaan kin or 2003 main california governors paryaavaran or aarthik netritv puraskaar praapt kiya. primalani ke sanghathan ko 2003 main yues ipie kshetra 9 puraskaar bhi mila tha. primalani ne neuyork shahar, san francisco, mumbai, dubai, bangalore, hyderabad, chandigarh, pune or dilli sahit duniya bhar ke saikadon corporate or kamedi club shoj main pradarshan kiya he. vah apni komedi ke madhyam se saahsee muddon jaise maanavadhikaar, paryaavaran, or balaatkaar ko aawaaj detii hai va saamaajik sandesh pahuchaati hai.
