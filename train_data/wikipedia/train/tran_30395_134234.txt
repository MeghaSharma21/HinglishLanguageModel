 sankt gaalan canton switzaraland ke purvottar bhaag main sthit ek canton he. yah canton san 1803 main swiss parisangh kaa hissaa bana tha. ise angrezi main kabhi-kabhi saint gallen bulaayaa jaataa he haalaanki iskaa sahi german naam 'sankt gaalan' he. aapanatsel ousaroden or aapanatsel innarroden ke do canton lagbhag puri tarah se sankt gaalan canton dwaara ghire hue hai. sankt gaalan canton kaa kshetra nepolian bonapart ne switzaraland main hastkshep karte hue san 1803 main kritrim rup se apane 'madhyasthata vidheyak' ke tahat paribhaashit kiya tha.
