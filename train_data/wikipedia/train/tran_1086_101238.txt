 nachiket dighe ek bhartiya abhinayta or aawaaj abhinayta he. uski maatrubhaasha bhashaaon hindi or marathi hai. vah sabase acha uski hindi ron visli ke rup main rupert grint bhoomika par harry potter film shrinkhalaa main, aawaaj kii dabing ke liye janaa jaataa he. vah duusari film or baad ke baad se yah kar gaya he.vah aadhikarik taur par karne ke liye aadhikarik hindi aawaaj dab se adhik kalakaar hone kii pushti kii he: kevin jonas or tobi amiisu.
 kevin or tobi vibhinn aayu samoohon main kramshah rahe hai ke baavjood, dighe unki aawaaj kii gunvattta match jab yah dab hindi ke liye aata he sakasham he.
