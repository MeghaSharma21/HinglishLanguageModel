 shastreey sangeetkaar shrimati thanjavur brunda karnataka sangeet ke veena dhannammal style kii sarvshereshth pratinidhi maani jaati hai. aap na keval gayan, balki veena vaadan main bhi nipun thim. aapke prashansak aapako aadar se 'brundamma' pukaarte the. 
 shrimati brunda apane parivaar sadasyon dwaara veena dhannammal gayan style main shikshit huin. aapke shikshak the aapaki mata kamakshi, mausi lakshmiratnam or swayan aapaki vikhyath daadi veena dhannammal. iske pashchaat aapane kanchipuram naina pillai ke nirikshan main sangeet kaa adhyayan kiya. aapane veena dhannammal style ke mand, mohneey or gamakayukt sangeet tathaa naina pillai ke sphurtiman or laypurvak sangeet ko saraahneey dhang se apane gayan main sammishrit kiya. 
 sangeetkaar evam sangeet premi shrimati brunda ke panditya ko aaj bhi saraahte hai. aapke sangeet kaaryakramon main gamakapurna raag hameshaa shaamil rahete the. karnataka sangeet ke kai prasiddh gayak, jaise shemmangudi shrinivas ayyar, em. es. subbulakshmi, aar. ke. shrikanthan, ramnad krishnan, aruna sairam or chitraveena ravikiran ne aapse gayan siikhaa. 
 shrimati brunda ne kai sammaan praapt kiye jinmein agraganya he sangeet kalanidhi, jo aapane 1976 main hasil kiya.
