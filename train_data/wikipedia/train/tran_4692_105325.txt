 hariyaali or vishaal bhubhag ke liye janaa jane vaala sepahijaala vanya jeev abhyaarany agartala city centre se 35 kimi dur he. 18.5 varg kimi main faile is abhyaarany main badi sankhya main pravasi pakshi or jaanavar paae jate hai. is vanya jeev abhyaarany kii sthapana 1972 main kii gai thi or samay ke saath-saath yah ek paryaton sthal ke rup main lokapriy hota gaya. aisa mana jaataa he ki yahaan 150 se zyada prajati ke pakshi paae jate hai, jinamein se kai pravasi pakshiyaan he. sepahijaala vanya jeev abhyaarany main hiran park or kai jhilein bhi hai. yahaan rahane vaale jeevon ko alag-alag karne ke liye yah vanya jeev abhyaarany 5 section main bata hua he.
