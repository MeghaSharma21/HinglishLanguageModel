 masjid kii minar 2011 main 
 aleppo kii mahaan masjid; yaa aleppo kii umayyad masjid jammu bani umayah bi-halaab sabase badi or shahar main sabase puraani masjidon main se ek he., syria, aleppo shahar main al-madina souk ke pravesh dwaar ke nikat he. masjid kathith taur par jon baptism dene vaale pita jakaryah ke ghar ke avasheshon par he.. yah 8 veen shatabdi kii shuruyaat main umayyad shaskaon dwaara banaayi gayi thi haalanki, vartmaan imaarat 11 veen se 14 veen shatabdiyon tak kii he or minar kaa nirmaan 1090 main kiya gaya tha, lekin april 2013 main syrian grihayuddh main ladaai ke dauraan masjid ko nasht kar diya gaya tha.
