 pukadil jon ek pramukh bhartiya plaza bhautik vigyaani he. vah plasma research institute main plazama science and technology main meghnad saaha chair par hai. 
 apana piechdi aligarh muslim vishvavidhyalay main puraa karne ke baad, jon bhautik anusandhan prayogshaala main shaamil hue, jahaan unhone 1972 main ek prayogtmak plasma bhautikee kaarykram kii sthapana kii. vah 1982 tak bhautik anusandhan prayogshaala main plasm bhautik vigyaani group ke adhyaksh the.
 2010 main, professor jon ko bharat sarkaar se pratishthit padam shri puraskaar mila. jon kavita bhi likhte hai.
