 rajasthan rajya vidhansabha chunaav, 2013 bhartiya rajya rajasthan main 1 disambar 2013 ko hua vidhaan sabha chunaav he jisamen rajasthan kii 200 main se 199 vidhansabha siton kaa chunaav hua. isamen kul matdaan kendron kii sankhya 45,334 evam matadaataaon kii kul sankhya 4,07,14,984 thi. praarambhik anumaanon ke anusaar lagbhag 74% matadaataaon ne apane mat kaa prayog kiya. chhitput ghatnaon ko chhodkar matdaan shantipurvak sampann hua. churu vidhansabha kshetra se bahujan samaaj party pratyaashee jagadish meghavaal kii mrityu ke kaaran chunaav ko sthagit kiya gaya. ab churu vidhaan sabha kshetra par matdaan 13 disambar 2013 ko hua. bhartiya janata party 200 main se 163 sthanon par vijay praapt karke sabase badi party ke rup main ubhari balki congress 21 siton ke saath dwitiya sthaan par rahi.
 rajasthan main disambar 2013 main hue is chunaav main bhartiya janata party 84.4 pratishat vidhayakon ke saath ekatapha jeet hasil karne main safal rahi.
