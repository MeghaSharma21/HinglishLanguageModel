 farsi samrajya faras se shashan karne vaale vibhinn vanshon ke samrajya ko sammilit rup se kaha jaataa he. faras ke shaskaon ne apana samrajya aadhunik iran ke baahar kai pradeshon tak failaa diya tha - iraq, armenia, turki, azarbaijan, afghanistan, turkmenistan, tajikistan, pakistan, madhyapurv, misr aadi. lekin ye sabhi pradesh hameshaa faras ke niyantran main nahin rahe the. khud faras saatvin se dasvin sadi tak arabon ke prabhutv main rahaa tha. 
 sikandar ke akraman ke kaaran puraa samrajya yawanon ke haath chalaa gaya tha. sikandar ke senapatiyon ne uske samrajya ko aapas main baant liya. senukas nector isamen se sabase shaktishaalee shaasha banakar ubhara. jald hi parthiyanon ne yawanon kaa prabhutv kar diya. samrajya ke uttar purv main yawanon kaa samrajya bana rahaa.
 iske baad arabon kaa shashan aayaa jisamen pehle damishq or phir baghdad main sthit khilaafat ne islamic samrajya kaa shashan chalaaya. dasvin sadi ke madhya tak sthaneey shaasha swytt ho gaye the. iske baad purv main gazani or phir gor ke shaasha, uttar-pashchim main seljuk turk or pashchim main misr ke shaasha swatantra ho gaye the.
