 manjari chaturvedi bharat ke ek lokapriy sufi kathak nartak he. vah lakhanu gharaana se sambandhit rakhti he. vah bhartiya shastreey nrity kaa ek nayaa kala banane ke liye jaani jaati he jo sufi kathak hai. 
 chaturvedi kaa janm 9 disambar, 1974 lakhanu main hua tha. un ke pita ek unhone punjabi sufi paramparaon main baba buleh shah ke yogadan kaa baariki se adhyayan kiya. 
 unhone apni nrity kii shruwaat kathak nartak se kii. unhone rajasthan, kashmir, avadh, punjab, turkmenistan, iran or krigistan sangeet par bhi nrity pradarshan kiya. 
 unhone taj mahal or sidney opera house main pradarshan kiya he. unhone vishaal bharadwaj dwaara nirdeshit ek sufi sangeet video main kaam kiya jo gulzaar dwaara likhaa gaya.sabhi qawwali gane amir khusro ke sufi siddhanton par aadhaarit hai.
