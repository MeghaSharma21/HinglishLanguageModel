 morcha yaa jang kai bhinn prakaar kii lohe kii oxidon kaa naam he. aam bhashaa main ise laal or naarangi rang ke un padaarthon ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he jo pani yaa hawa kii maujuudagi main lohe or oxigen kii rasinic abhikriya se ban jate hai. lohe ke sanksharan main zang kii ahm bhoomika he.
 agar shuddh loha shuddh pani yaa shuddh oxigen ke sampark main ho to zang nahin banataa. uski bajaae lohe or oxigen main abhikriya se ek loh oxide kii patali parat ban jaati he jo kas kar lohe se chipakkar usko puri tarah dhak leti he. is parat ko nishkreey parat kaha jaataa he. is parat ke andar kaa loha surakshit rahata he. lekin salfar diaoxide, carbon diaoxide yaa anya ashuddhiyon kii upasthiti main yah parat zang kaa rup dhaaran kar leti he jo lohe se chipakata nahin balki alag chhilane lagata he. is se andar kaa loha baar-baar bahari tatwon se sampark main aa jaataa he or vah bhi zang banataa rahata he. isi kriya ko aam boli main "zang khaanaa" kehete hai.
