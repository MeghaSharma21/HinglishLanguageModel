 bhugoleey nirdeshaank pranali ek prakaar kii nirdeshaank pranali hoti he, jiske dwaara prithvi par kisi bhi sthaan kii sthiti teen nirdeshaankon ke madhyam se nishchit kii ja sakati he. ye golakaar nirdeshaank pranali dwaara diye jate hai.
 prithvi purna rup se golakaar nahin he, balki ek aniyamit aakaar kii he, jo lagbhag ek ilipsoed aakaar banaati he. iske liye is prakaar kii nirdeshaank pranali bananaa, jo prithvi par upasthit prayek bindu ke liye ankon ke adwitiya mail se banane vaala spasht nirdeshaank prastut kare, apane aap main ek prakaar kii chunauti tha. 
 akshansh prithvi kii sataha par ek bindu se bhumadhyi samtal tak bana kone hota he, jise globe ke kendra par napa jaataa he. samaan akshansh binduon ko jodne vaali rekhaon ko akshansh rekhaaen kehete hai. akshansh kii rekhaaen is prakshep main kshaitij evam sidhi prathit hoti hai, parantu ve bhinn ardhvyaason vaali or vrittiya hoti hai. ek akshansh par sthit sabhi sthaan ekasaath judakar akshansh kaa vrutt banaate hai. ye sabhi vrutt bumadhya rekha ke samanaantar hote hai. inamen bhugolic uttari dhruv 90° uttar kone par rahata he; va bhugolic dakshini dhruv 90° dakshin kone par. shunya ansh akshansh rekha ko bumadhya rekha kehete hai. ye globe ko uttari va dakshini, do golardhon main baantati he. 
 deshaantar donon bhugoleey dhruvon ke bich khinchi hui kaalpanik madhyahn rekhaon kaa sandarbh deshaantar rekha se purv yaa pashchim main bana kone hota he or jo madhyahn rekha jis bindu yaa sthaan se guzarati he uskaa koniya maan us sthaan kaa deshaantar hota he. sabhi deshaantar rekhaaen ardh-vrittakar hoti hai. ye samanantar nahin hoti hai va uttari va dakshini dhruvon par abhisarit hokar mil jaati hai.
 kone ko likhane ke kai formats hote hai, sabhi samaan akshansh, deshaantar ke kram main likhe jate hai.
 prithvi ke upar, andar yaa oonchai par sthit kisi sthlakritik fichar kly purnataya bataane hetu, iske kendra ya sataha se us bindu kii lambavat oonchai bhi bataani hogi. isaki sataha main aniyamitata va oobad-khabad prakritik swabhaav ke kaaran ye oonchai us bindu ke neeche behatar spashtataa se paribhaashit lambavat datom jaise samudr-sataha ke sandarbh main bataayi jaati he. prayek desh ne apane swayan ke datom nishchit kiye hue hai, udhaahrantaya united kingdom kaa sandarbh bindu neulin he. prithvi ke kendra se duuri bahut gahare binduon evam antariksh kii sthitiyon ko bataane ke liye prayog kii jaati he.
 goleey nirdeshaank dwaara bataaya gaya prayek bindu ab kartiya nirdeshaank paddati dwaara x y z bhi vyakt kiya ja saktaa he. ye maanchitron par kisi sthaan kii sthiti ko ankit karne hetu ati-prayogneey tarika to nahin he, kintu ye dooriyaan naapane evam anya ganitiya prakaarya sampann karne hetu prayog kiya jaataa he. iskaa udgam praayah gole kaa kendra hi hota he, jo lagbhag prithvi ke kendra ke nikat hi hota he.
 sagar sataha par ek goleey sataha par, el akshanshiy second baraabar 30.82 meter or ek rekhansheey minute 1849 meter hota he. ek akshanshiy digari 110.9 kilometre ke baraabar hoti he. rekhaanshon ke vrutt bhugoleey dhruvon par milte hai. inaki purv-pashchim kii chaudaai akshansh par nirbhar karti he. bumadhya rekha ke nikat sagar sataha par ek rekhansheey second baraabar 30.92 meter, ev rekhansheey minute baraabar 1855 meter, tathaa ek rekhansheey digari 111.3 kilometre ke baraabar hoti he. 30° par ek rekhansheey second 26.76 meter, greenvich main is 19.22 meter, evam 60° par ye 16.42 meter hota he.
