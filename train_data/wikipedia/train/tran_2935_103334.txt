 khandala, bharat ke rajya maharashtra main, pashchimi ghat main sthit ek parvatiy sthal he. yah lonawala se teen kilometre or karjat se saat kilometre kii duuri par sthit he.
 khandala, bhor ghat ke ek chor par sthit he, jo ki dakkan ke pathaar or konkan ke maidan ke bich ke sadak sampark par sthit ek pramukh ghat he. is ghat se baari sadak or rail yatayaat gujarata he. mumbai or pune ke bich kii mukhya kadi mumbai-pune expresve, khandala se hi hokar gujarata he. 
 paas ke shaharon se aasaan pahunch ke kaaran, khandala hiking karne vaale paryatakon ke bich vishesh rup se lokapriy he. duk kii naak naamak pahari chotii se khandala or bhor ghat ke sundar nazaaron kaa anand liya ja saktaa he. 
 khandala kaa zikr film ghulaam ke prasiddh gane " aati kya khandala" main kiya gaya he. 1975 kii kaaljayi film choti si baat main ashok kumaar dwaara abhinit charitra jo ki sena se sevanivruth ek karnal he, khandala main rahata he.
