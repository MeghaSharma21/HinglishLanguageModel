 sohaval uttar pradesh ke faijabad jile kii ek tehsil he. yah janapad mukhyalay se 19 kimi pashchim disha main he. yahan ek railway stesan bhi he joki tehsil se 500 me kii duuri par he.
 sohaval se kuch duuri par 'khironi' gaanv he. yah gaanv ekta vividhta evam sampoornataa kaa pratik he. prasiddh sharada sahaayak nahar yahan se hokar gai he. is gramsabha main choti badi milakar 27 panchayate hai. sarayu nadi yahan se 4 kimi dur he joki dhemawa nadi ke sthaneey naam se prasiddh he. khironi gramsabha main 'puure ahlad' naamak chota sa gaanv he.
 'sohaval' prasiddh logon kii janm sthali he. yahan janme lallu singh, avadhesh prasad aadi mantri rah chuke he. pehle sohaval ek vidhansabha hua karti thi parantu ab sohaval ko ab bikapur vidhansabha main parivartit kar diya gaya he.divakar dvivedi prasiddh bhojpuri gayak yaheen ke he. iske atirikt en:Narendra Dev University of Agriculture and Technology ke prasiddh vaigyanik ajay kumaar maury sohaval ke moiyakapur poor gaanv ke he.
 sohaval tehsil ke paas hi suchithtaganj bazaar he. yah faijabad janapad kii badi bazaar main se ek he. suchithtaganj bazaar main bank of baroda, state bank of india, en:central bank of india, Sahara Insurance kii shakhaayen hai. yahan kii juberganj bazaar main prayek buudhvaar ko vishaal bhains bazaar lagata he.
 isamen bhens ke atirikt gaay bail ityadi bhi kray vikray hetu laaye jate hai.
 suchithtaganj bazaar ko thakur shiv darshan sihan ne basaaya tha. suchithtaganj kaa naam apane priy mitra suchit sihan ke naam rakh diya.
 thakur shiv darshan sihan ,dyodhi hindu sihan ke sammanit raaj parivaar ke sadsy the. nauaan kuan se sohaval rod unke ke naam par darj he.
