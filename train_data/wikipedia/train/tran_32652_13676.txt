 kalpana priyadarshanee ek bhartiya film abhinetri thi, jo dakshin bhartiya filmon main mukhya rup se malayalam or tamil filmon main apane kaam ke liye prasiddh thi. unhone dakshin bhartiya bhashaaon main 300 se bhi adhik filmon main abhinay kiya he. unhone ek bal kalakaar ke rup main apana kariyar shuru kiya 1970 ke dashak se. 60wein rashtriya film awords main, unhone thanichala nanjan main unke pradarshan ke liye sarvshereshth sahaayak abhinetri kaa puraskaar jeeta. 
 kalpana kaa janm manch kalakaar chavara ve. pi. nair or vijayalakshmi ke hua tha. abhinetri kalranjini or urvashi unki bahane hai. unhone 1998 main malayalam film nirdeshak anil kumaar se shaadi kii or 2012 main talaak ho gaya. 
 kalpana ne ek bal kalakaar ke rup main apana kariyar shuru kiya vidarbha motkul film se. unhone tamil main apni pehali film chinna vedu kii. unki or anya yaadgaar filmon main shaamil hai saathi leelavati or kalaivedu thi. kalpana ne film kudumbakodati main ek gaanaa bhi gaaya tha.
 kalpana ne opiri film kii shooting ke liye hyderabad gai thi. 25 janvari 2016 ko, vah apane hotel ke kamare main behosh paaya gai. unhen turat hospital lejaaya gaya par unki raste main hi mrityu ho gai. 
 kalpana ne malayalam tivi channel par kai dharawahik or saakshaatkaar kii mejabani kii he
