 higroscopy kisi padaarth kii apane chaaron ore ke vaataavaran se jal ke anuon ko aakarshith karne kii kshamta he. yah kriya yaa to avasorana dwaara yaa adhishoshan dwaara hoti he.
 higroscopy padaarthon main shakkar, shahad, glissrol, ithenul, methenul, salphurik amla, iodine, anek chloride tathaa hydroxide, lavan aadi hai.
 zinc chloride, calcium chloride tathaa potacium hydroxide evam sodium hydroxide or anek vibhinn lavan itane hydroscopy hote hai ki ve khud ke dwaara avashoshit kiye gayi jal main ghul bhi jate hai. is gun ko diliquicense kehete hai. salphurik amla na keval ucch sandrata par higroscopy hota he balki iskaa vilyan 10 Vol-% yaa isse kam sandratha par bhi higroscopy hota he.
