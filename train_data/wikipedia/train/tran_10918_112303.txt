 piranha yah dakshin america main pai jane vaali ek machhlee kii prajati he. ye taaje pani kii machhlee he. jisaki visheshta unke daanton kaa tej or bada hona he. venezuela main inhen caribes kehete hai. yah saamanytah jhund main shikaar karti hai. inkaa ye naam paragway kii gurani bhashaa se angrezi, portugeez or anya bhashaaon main aayaa he. ye bahut aadamkhor machhlee maani jaati he. inke daant bahut tej or jabade bahut taqatvar hote hai. piranha kii lambaai saamanytah 14 se 26 cm tak hoti he. jabaki piranha kii kuch prajatiyan 43 cm tak note kii gayi he.
