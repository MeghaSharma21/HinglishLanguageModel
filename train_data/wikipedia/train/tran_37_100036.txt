 fani majumdaar
 kaajal 1965 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. is chalchitr ke nirmaataa pannalaal maheshwari the, tathaa nirdeshak ke taur par ram maheshwari ne apana yogadan diya tha. is chalchitr ke sitare the -minaa kumari, dharmendra, raaj kumaar, padmini, helen, durga khote, tun tun, mahmud or mumtaz. is chalchitr ke liye sangeet diya tha ravi ne. gulshan nanda ke upanyaas "madhavi " par aadhaarit is chalchitr ke liye fani majumdaar or kidaar sharma ne samvaad lekhan kiya. yah chalchitr box offis par "hit" rahi.yah film gulshan nanda ke upanyaas madhavi par aadhaarit he.
