 nishaan singh bharat ke liye qurbaan hone vaale vir sapoot the jinhone san 1857 se swatantraaa ke pratham sangram main apni veerta kaa parichay diya tha. nishaan singh kii smriti main gaanv main ek vidhyalay va ek pustkaalay sanchalit ho rahaa he.
 bihar ke shivsagar prakhand ke baddi gaanv ke rahane vaale jameendaar raghuvar dayaal singh ke putr nishaan singh sainya sanchaalan main maahir the. 1857 ke sipaahi vidroh main nishaan singh ne sainikon kaa netritv kiya tha. aara jail kii phatak tod bandiyon ko mukt karaane ke baad banda, kalpi kii ladaai main bhi angrejon ke daant khattey kar die. us samay lakhanu ke nawab ne unke adamya saahas ko dekhte hue 1230 rupee ke puraskaar ke saath azamgarh ke shashan kaa farmaan bhi jari kar diya. azamgarh kile par dhaava bol angrejon ko parrast kiya. bihar main babu kunwar singh ke daahine haath maane jane vaale babu nishaan singh ne kadam-kadam par unka saath diya.
 azamgarh main safalataa ke baad babu kunwar singh ne nishaan singh or do hajaar sainikon ke saath jagadishpur kii taraf prasthaan kiya. 26 april 1858 ko kunwar singh ke nidhan ke baad nishaan singh khud ko kamjor mahasoos karne lage. angrejon dwaara gaanv kii sampatti jabt kiye jane ke baad ve dumrkhaar ke sameep jangal kii ek gupha main rahane lage. jise aaj nishaan singh maan ke naam se janaa jaataa he. aane-jane vaale sabhi logon se ve angrejon ke viruddh ladaai jari rakhane kaa aahvaan karte the.
 paanch june 1858 ko sasaram levi ke dipti suparitendent captain nolan dwaara unhen giraftaar kiya gaya. 6 june ko sasaram laa afisar commanding karnal starten ke sipurd kiya gaya. jahaan court marshall dwaara mukadmaa chalaane or giraftaar ke 36 ghante ke andar unhen top se udaane kaa aadesh diya gaya. 7 june 1858 ko, subah gaurakshani muhalle main top ke munh par baandhakar angrejon ne unhen uda diya. court marshall ke dauraan unhone angrejon ke viruddh ladaai jari rakhane kaa aahvaan kiya.
