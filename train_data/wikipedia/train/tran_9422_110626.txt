 do varnon ke mail se hone vaale vikaar ko sandhi kehete hai. is milawat ko samajhakar varnon ko alag karte hue padon ko alag-alag kar dena sandhi-viched he. hindi bhashaa main sandhi dwaara sanyukt shabd likhane kaa saamaanya chalan nahin he. par sanskrit main iske bina kaam nahin chalata he. sanskrit ke tatsam shabd grahan kar lene ke kaaran sanskrit vyakaran ke sandhi ke niyamon ko hindi vyakaran main bhi grahan kar liya gaya he. shabd rachana main sandhiyaan usee prakaar sahaayak he jaise upsarg, pratyay, samaas aadi.
 yahan varnakram se sandhi tathaa uske viched sangrahit kiye gaye hai. saath hi sandhi kaa prakaar bhi nirdeshit he.
