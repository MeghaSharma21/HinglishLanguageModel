 ammisaduka kii shukr patalika, shukr ke khagoleey prekshan ke abhilekh ko sanderbhit karata he jo pratham sahasrabdi isa purv se anginat ankan chaak patalikaaon ke rup main sangrakshit hui chali aa rahi he. mana gaya he ki yah khagoleey abhilekh pehali baar samraat ammisaduka ke shasankaal ke dauraan sankalit kiye gaye jo hammurabi ke baad ke chauthe shaasha the. is prakaar, is paath ke mul shaayad madhya satrahavin shatabdi isa purv ke aasapaas ke liye dinaankit hone chahie.
 abhilekh ne shukr ke udai ke samayon tathaa suryoday va suryaast se pehle yaa baad kshitij par apni pehali or aakhri drishyata ko chandra tithiyon ke rup main darj kiya. ye prekshan 21 saal kii avadhi ke liye darj kiye gaye he.
