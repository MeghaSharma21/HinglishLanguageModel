 shivani bhatnagar ek prasiddh patrkaar thim.
 shivani bhatnagar murder cese main dilli hai court ne purv ipies aar ke sharma samet teen aropiyon ko bari kar diya. aar ke sharma ke saath hi shribhagwan das or satyaprakash ko bhi bari kiya gaya he. lekin court ne pradeep raay kii sajaa barkaraar rakhi he. in teenon ko nichali adaalat ne doshi mana tha. or umr kaid kii sajaa sunai thi. jiske khilaaf doshiyon ne hoicourt main apil kii thi. bataaden ki 23 janvari 1999 ko patrkaar shivani bhatnagar kii hatya kar di gai thi. police ke mutabik purv ipies aar . ke . sharma kii shivani se najdiki thi. baad main vah unase peechha chhudaana chahta tha or isi kaaran usne anya aropiyon ke saath milakar shivani kii hatya karvaa di. is maamle main doshiyon kii apil par sunwaayee ke baad hai court ne pichley saal 21 disambar ko faisla surakshit rakh liya tha.
