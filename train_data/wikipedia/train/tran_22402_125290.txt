 sarungbam bimala kumari devi ek bhartiya chikitsak hai or mukhya chikitsa adhikari ke taur par imphal main jo ki bhartiya rajya manipur ke pashchimi kshetramen maujuud he, main kaam karti hai. manipur rajya chikitsa seva main vah 1979 se seva kar rahi hai or zyaadaatar kaam unhone gramin kshetron main kiya he. bharat ke pradhanmantri,narendra modi, ke do yatraaon ke dauraan khaadya suraksha karyalayrajya unke netritv main tha. devi, ko 2014 main ambedkar antarrashtriya puraskaar, se sammanit kiya gaya or 2015 main padam shri, jo ki chauthaa sarvocch bhartiya naagarik sammaan he, se nawaaza gaya.
