 kendriya krishi praudyogiki santhaan ) bhopal main sthit ek krishi-shiksha evam anusandhan tathaa vikaas kaa santhaan he. yah bhartiya krishi anusandhan parishad kaa ek swytt santhaan he. kendriya krishi abhiyantriki santhaan kaa uddeshy krishi abhiyantriki se sambandhit anusandhan kaary karnaa he. 
 isaki sthapana 15 farvari 1976 ko hui thi. yah ek sau pachaas acre jameen main yah failaa shodh kendra kisaanon ke liye ucch stareey krishi praudyogiki evam anusandhan ke bare main vibhinn shaikshanik kaarykram evam prashikshan kii jaankaree pradaan karata he. armbh main iskaa kaaryakshetra krishi mashine, kataai ke baad kii praudyogiki tathaa krishi main urja aadi kshetron main tha. kintu baad main iske kaaryakshetra kaa vistaar karke krishi-audyogiki, instrumentation, sinchai, tathaa drenez ejiniyari aadi ko bhi sammilit kar liya gaya.
