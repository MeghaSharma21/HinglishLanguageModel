 david charles haan jise kabhi-kabhi radioective boy scout yaa nulier boy scout bhi kaha jaataa he, ek american tha jisane 1994 ke dauraan 17 saal kii umr main ek gharelu breader riector banane kaa prayaas kiya. yah boy scout of america main ek scout tha, isane apane experement apane pichhawaade bani jhopadi main kiye. haalanki iskaa riector kabhi bhi critical maas par nahin pahunchaa. kuch samay baad police ko bhi is baat kii jaankaree ho gayi, iske ghar ko paryaavaran sangrakshan agency dwaara saafe kiya gaya or isaki lab ko nasht kar diya gaya.
