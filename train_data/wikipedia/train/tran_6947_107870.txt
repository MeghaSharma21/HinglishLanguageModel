 dow bashir badr ko urdu kaa vah shaayar mana jaataa he jisane kamyaabi kii bulandiyon ko fateh kar bahut lambi duuri tak logon kii dilon kii dhadakanon ko apni shaayari main utaara he. sahitya or naatak akedmi main kiye gayi yogadano ke liye unhen 1999 main padmashri se sammanit kiya gaya he.
 inkaa puraa naam saiyad mohammad bashir he. bhopal se tallukaat rakhane vaale bashir badr kaa janm kanpur main hua tha. aaj ke mashhoor shaayar or geetkaar nusrat badr inke suputr hai.
 dow bashir badr 56 saal se hindi or urdu main desh ke sabase mashhoor shaayar hai. duniya ke do darjan se zyada mulkon main mushaayare main shirkat kar chuke hai. bashir badr aam aadmi ke shaayar hai. zindagi kii aam baton ko behad kubsuurati or salike se apni gazalon main kah janaa bashir badr sahab kii khaasiyat he. unhone urdu ghazal ko ek nayaa lahaza diya. yahi vajah he ki unhone shrota or pathakon ke dilon main apni khaas jagah banaai he.
