 berint sagar, arctic mahaasaagar kaa ek bhaag he, tathaa norway or rus ke uttar main sthit he. madhya yug main ise maraman sagar ke naam se janaa jaataa tha, sagar kaa vartmaan naam dach navik wilem berint ke naam par rakhaa gaya he. is sagar kii ausat gaharaai 230 me or adhiktam gaharaai 450 me he. novaya zemlya berint sagar ko kaara sagar se pruthak karti he. berint sagar kshetra main jeevashm eendhan urja sansaadhan prachur maatra main uplabdh hai.
