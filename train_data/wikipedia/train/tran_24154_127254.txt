 bharat, aadhikarik bharat ganraajya ek dakshin asian desh he. yah 29 rajyon or 7 kendra shasit pradeshon se milakar bana he. sabhi bhartiya apni swayan kii sarkaar rakhte hai or kendrashasit pradesh kendra sarkaar ke adhikaar kshetra main aate hai. adhiktar anya deshon kii tarah bharat main bhi rashtriya pratik paye jate hai. rashtriya pratikon ke atirikt sabhi bhartiya rajya or kendra shasit pradesh apnekhud kii rajya mohar or pratik rakhte hai jisamen rajya pashu, pakshi, ped, phool aadi shaamil hai. bharat ke sabhi rajya pashuon kii suchi niche di gayi he.
