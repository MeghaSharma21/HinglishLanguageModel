 vansda rashtriya udyaan bharat ke gujarat rajya ke navsari zile main sthit ek rashtriya udyaan he jo san 1979 main sthapit kiya gaya tha. rashtriya udyaan sthapit hone se pehle yah kshetra vansda ke maharaja kaa niji ilaaka tha. isi kaaran se is udyaan kaa naam bhi vansda rashtriya udyaan padaa.
 yah udyaan dakshini gujarat main navsari zile kii purvi seemaa main sthit he. yah dakshin main valsad zile ke jangalon or purv main daang ke jangalon donon ke saath ek nirantar van kshetra kaa nirmaan karata he.
 udyaan rashtriya rajmarg-8 ke paas sthit he or lagbhag 24 varg kimi failaa hua he. haalanki yah udyaan chota he, lekin yah bahut ghanaa he or kuch ilaakon main to puure din dhoop zamin nahin chhu paati he. yahan jeev or vanaspati main kaafii vividhta dekhi ja sakati he.yahaan kaa bhu-bhaag kuch hisson main samtal he or kuch main oobad-khabad. yahan kaa pani ambika nadi ke zariye navsari ke nikat samudr main jaataa he. udyaan dakshin-pashchim main rajasav vibhaag dwaara viksit kshetra se sata he or purvottar main ambika ke saath. park ke chaaron ore kaa kshetra pashchimi ghat, jise sahyadri bhi kehete hai, kii uttari or pashchimi seemaa he.
 yahan 2,000 mi.me. ausatan salanaa varsha hoti he, jisaki vajah se is udyaan main kaafii hariyaali he. yahan ke kuch bhaag to itane ghaney hai ki vahaan par din main bhi andhera rahata he. udyaan kaa adhikansh kshetra nami vaala parnpaati van he lekin kuch ilaakon main sookhe parnpaati vriksh bhi hai. is udyaan main vanaspati kii 450 prajatiyaan hai jisamen se 443 phool dene vaali prajatiyaan hai. jeevon kii yahan kai prajatiyaan hai jinamein pramukh hai tendua, lakadabaggha, chital, chousinga, kakad ityadi.
