 himanshu shrivastav ek sahityakar, upanyaaskaar the, jinaki lagbhag 50 upanyaas or kahani sangrah prakashit hui hai.
 himanshu shrivastav hindi ke un saubhagyashaalee lekhakon main se ek hai, jinhone saahitiyik raajnneeti ke dal se apane ko sarvatha bachaakar rakhaa or rachanadharmita ke kshetra main anekashah kirtiman sthapit kiye. udhaaharan ke liye yah nibasancoch kaha ja saktaa he ki inke ek upanyaas ‘lohe ke pankh’ ke kathan, varnan vishadta or anubhav-sansar ko hindi kaa koi anya upanyaaskaar ab tak chhu nahin sakaa; yon pranayam bahuton ne kiye. himanshu shrivastav bihar ke saaran jilantargat haraaji gram main san 1934 main janame or san 55-56 tak saahitiyik chal-kapat nahin, balki apni pratibha ke kaaran sabhi dhaaraon ke samikshakon or lekhakon ke liye avismaraneey kathakaar ban gaye.ab tak bees se adhik upanyaas, dedh sau kahaniyaan or teen naatak prakashit ho chuke hai. pratham shrenee ke radio naatakkaar ke rup main sweekrut-sthapit. murdhanya samalochak or sahityakar dow ramkumar verma ke shabdon main—‘‘himanshu shrivastav ke upanyaason ne hindi upanyaas ko ganga jaisi udaattata pradaan kii he.’’
