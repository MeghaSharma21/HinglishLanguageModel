 mukteshwar mandir bhubaneshwar ke kurd zile main sthit he. mukteshwar mandir do mandiron kaa samooh he: parameshwar mandir tathaa mukteshwar mandir. mukteshwar mandir bhagwaan shiv ko samarpit he or yah mandir ek choti pahari par bana hua he is mandir tak pahunchane ke liye lagbhag 100 seedhiyaan chadhani padati hai. yahan bhagwaan shiv ke saath brahma, vishnu, parvati, hanuman or nandi ji bhi virajman hai. mandir ke baahar languron kaa jamaavada laga rahata he.
 parameshwar mandir tathaa mukteshwar mandir kii sthapana 970 i. ke aasapaas hui thi. parameshwar mandir abhi surakshit avasthaa main he. yah mandir is kshetra ke puraane mandiron main sabase aakarshak he. isamen aakarshak chitrakaari bhi kii gai he. ek chitra main ek narttaki or ek sangitajna ko bahut achche dhang se darshaya gaya he. is mandir ke garbhagrih main ek shivlinga he. yah shivlinga apane baad ke lingraj mandir ke shivlinga kii apeksha zyada chamkila he.
 mukteshwar mandir main nagar style or kaling vaastukala kaa adbhoot mail dekha ja saktaa he. mukteshwar mandir main naqkaashi kaa behtareen kaam kiya gaya he. is mandir main kii gai chitrakaari kaaphi acchhee avasthaa main he. ek chitra main krushkaay saadhuon tathaa daudte bandaron ke samooh ko darshaya gaya he. ek anya chitra main panchatantr kii kahani ko darshaya gaya he. is mandir ke darvaaze arq style main bane hue hai. is mandir ke khambhe par bhi naqkaashi kii gai he. is mandir kaa torana magaramchh ke sir jaise aakaar kaa bana hua he. is mandir ke daayin taraf ek chota sa kuan he ise marichi kunda ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he.
