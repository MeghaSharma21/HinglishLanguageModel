 thailand ke mahaan buddha yaa thailand ke vishaal buddha thailand main sabase oonchi evam duniya main nauvi sabase oonchi pratimaa he. yah pratimaa mahaminh shakyamuni visejaichanr ke naam se bhi jaani jaati he.
 yah buddha pratimaa thailand ke ang thong praant main wat mang buddha vihaar main sthit he, yah murti 92 meter oonchi or 63 meter chaudee he. is pratimaa kaa nirmaan 1990 main shuru kiya gaya tha or 2008 main vah puraa ho gaya he. is pratimaa par sone ke paint kaa aavaran he or yah kancreet se bani he.
