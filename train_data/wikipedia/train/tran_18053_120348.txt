 swasth bharat yatra bharat chodo andolan ke 75 varsh puure hone ke avasar par gandhi smriti evam darshan samiti ke maargdarshan main armbh kii gai 20 hajaar kilometre kii jansandeshaatkam yatra he. swasth bharat nyaas ke chairman ashatosh kumaar singh ke netritv main 'swasth balika - swasth samaaj' kaa sandesh lekar nikali is yatra kaa armbh nai dilli se mukhtaar abbaas nakvi dwaara hari jhandi dikhaa kar kiya gaya. is yatra ko gandhi smriti env darshan samiti, samvaad media, raajakmal prakaashan, nastiwa aspataal, medicare aspataal, spandan, jaldhara, help and hope sahit anya kai gaiyrsarkari sansthaaon kaa samarthan praapt he.
 yah yatra ab tak 16 hajaar kilometre kii duri tay karte hue 26 rajyon se hokar guzari he tathaa 50 hajaar gaanvon ko chuaa he. is dauraan skulon tathaa mahavidhyalayon kii ek lakh se adhik balikaon se sidhaa samvaad sthapit kiya he.
