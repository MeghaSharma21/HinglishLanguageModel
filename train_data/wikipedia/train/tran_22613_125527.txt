 sarhad-e-broanil yaa sarhad-e-vaakhaan, jise kabhi-kabhi sirf sarhad bhi kaha jaataa he, purvottaree afghanistan ke badakhshaan praant ke vaakhaan galiyare kshetra main barogil darre ke charnon main sthit ek gaanv he. yah hindu kush parvaton main 3,400 meter kii oonchaai par vaakhaan nadi ke kinare basaa hua he or yahan ke adhiktar nivasee vaakhi maatrubhaashi hai. yah ek kachi sadak dwaara ishakashim basti se or phir aage afghanistan ke anya bhaagon se judaa hua he. dakshin main barogil darre ko paar karke pakistan ke chitral zile kii mastuj vaadi he. gaanv ke irdagird 12,000 foot kii ausat oonchaai par sthit ek aasaan dhalaan vaala unchaa maidani ilaaka he jise 'dasht-e-barogil' kaha jaataa he or jiskaa prayog sthaneey nivasee maveshi charaane ke liye karte hai.
