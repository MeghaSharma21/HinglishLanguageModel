 
 
 vienna anterrashtriya vimaanashetra , shwechat main rajdhani vienna se 18 kimi dakshin purv main sthit vimaanashetra he. yah austria kaa sabase bada evam vyastatam vimaanashetra he. is vimaanashetra ko praayah shchechat naam se janaa jaataa he, jis kshetra main yah sthit he. yah vimaanashetra bowing 747, airbas A340 evam airbas A380 jaise bade vimaanakshetron ke avataran kii kshamta he. yahaan austrian airlines evam isaki sahabhaagi companiyon kaa hub bhi he.
 varsh 2011 main vimaanashetra ne kul 21,106,292 yatri evam 246,157 vimaan yatayaat vahan kiye jo pichley varsh 2010 kii apeksha kramshah 7.2% evam 0.0% vruddhi he.
