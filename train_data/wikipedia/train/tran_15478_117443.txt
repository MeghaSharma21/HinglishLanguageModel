 vardhaman mahavir khulaa vishvavidhyalay kota rajasthan kaa khulaa vishvavidhyalay he. isaki sthapana 23 july 1987 ko hui thi. is vishvavidhyalay kaa paathyakram vishesh rup se manviki, vaanijya, pustkaalay vigyaan tathaa soochna vigyaan par kendrit he.
 is vishvavidhyalay kaa lakshya parmparik shiksha se vanchit logon ko shikshit karnaa he. is vishvavidhyalay ke chah kendra hai jo puure rajasthan main faile hue hai. yah vishvavidhyalay lagbhag das hajaar logon ko shikshit karata he.
