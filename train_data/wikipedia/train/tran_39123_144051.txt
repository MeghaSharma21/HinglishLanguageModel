 jura pahadiyon kaa ek drishy
 jura parvatamala europe ke madhya bhaag main alps parvaton se uttar main sthit ek parvatamala he. yah parvat lagbhag france or switzraland kii seemaa ke saath-saath chalate hai. in parvaton ke naam par switzraland ke jura canton, france ke jura vibhaag, chandramaa par sthit montes jura naamak parvatamala, or bhuvaigyanik samay-maan ke jurasic kaal kaa naam rakhaa gaya he.
