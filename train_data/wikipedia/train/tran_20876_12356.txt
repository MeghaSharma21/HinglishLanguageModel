 pier iti thioder ruso barbijan style kaa france kaa chitrakaar tha. 
 thioder ruso kaa janm paris me ek darji ke parivaar main hua. drishy chitera charles remand or guilalethier ke maargdarshan main usne solah varsh kii umr main hi kalashiksha puri kar lee. france main jagah jagah ke prakritik drishyon ko chitrit karne ke adhyayan se usne parishrampurvak apni style kaa nirmaan kiya. san 1830 main usne moulik chitrastyli ke kalakaaron ke kashton main hissaa bantaya. roodhiwaadee chitrakaaron ne paris kalapradarshnee main iske bhaag bhaag lene kaa virodh kiya. san 1831 or 1834 main uski saadhaaran kritiyon ko pradarshnee main rakhaa gaya tha lekin san 1836 main 'laa deskaite de vaache' naamak mahaan kalakriti pradarshnee ke liye asweekrut kar di gai. is apmaan ke kaaran usne pradarshan main san 1848 tak bhaag lenaa asweekaar kar diya. 
 apane hi desh main kalakshetra se baahar rahakar ruso ne jo kalanirmiti kii vah bahut unchi sabit hui. 'di chestnut avenue', 'di marsh in di lands', 'hor frast' aadi in dinon kii kritiyaan hai. san 1851 kii pradarshnee main uskaa sarvottam chitra 'di fij ov di forest' pradarshit hry. isi sheershak ke samaan doosraa ek chitra veles collection main rakhaa he. ruso abtak baribizan main kabhi kabhi aakar rahata tha. lekin ab usne jangal ke isi gaanv main apana nivasasthaan banaaya. san 1855 main ruso ke bees saal main banaae gaye asweekrut chitra ikatthe kar ek pradarshnee yojit kiye gaye. phir bhi uskaa sangharsh chalata rahaa or swasthya girtaa gaya. san 1867 kii pradarshnee kaa vah fin art jeuri main adhyaksh chunaa gaya.
 ruso ke chitra hameshaa hi gambhir rahe, iskaa kaaran uskaa udasin jivan bhi ho saktaa he. kai jalarang kii kritiyaan, rekhachitran or syaahi se bani kritiyaan dekhne par koi bhi jaan saktaa he ki vah hameshaa hi nae anusandhanon yaa anveshanon ke peeche laga rahaa. veles or loweri main uski kritiyon kaa acha sangrah he. victoria museum main bhi kuch laghukritiyaan rakhi hai.
