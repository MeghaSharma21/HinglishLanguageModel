 lautoka prashanth mahaasaagar ke fiji desh kaa doosraa sabase bada nagar he. yah viti levu dweep ke pashchimottaree bhaag main or nandi se 24 kilometre uttar main sthit he or fiji main pravesh ke liye suva ke baad doosraa bandargah he. prashasnic rup se yah fiji ke pashchimi vibhaag ke ba praant kaa bhaag he. yah fiji ke ganna upaj kshetra ke madhya main sthit he isliye ise aksar sugar city ke naam se bhi pukaara jaataa he. 2007 kii janaganana ke anusaar, 16 varg kilometre ke kshetra main faile is shahar kii jansankhya 52,220 he. lautoka main bhartiya mul ke logon kii baari sankhya he.
