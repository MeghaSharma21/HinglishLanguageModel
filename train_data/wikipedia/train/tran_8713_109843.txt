 sant bakhnaa ji, gayak sant the. ve sant dadu dayaal ke baawan pradhan shishyon main se ek the. padon kii rachana karte or gum-gum kar tathaa sadhu santon kii sangat main baithkar gaate. ve grihasth sant the. sant dadu se unka bada gaharaa lagaav tha. dadu kii mrityu hone par bakhnaa ji ne jo pad gaaya tha, usase pataa chalata he ki unke man main guru ke liye kitni shraddha thi- ‘bichhadya ram sanehi re, mhare man pachhataao ye hi re. bilakhi sakhi saheli re, jyon jan bin nagar veli re. bakhnaa bahut bisure re, darasan ke kaaran jhure re.’
 ve rajasthan ke narayana nagar ke rahane vaale the. ve musalmaan the lekin aise sansaarik bandhan puri tarah bhula chuke the. dadu kii shishy parampara main rajjab, bajid, bakhnaa ji - sabhi ne sampradayik sadbhaav kaa prachaar kiya. bakhnaa ji kehete the ki hamen apana jivan ‘ram’ kii chetana se jagrit kar lenaa chahie. ramanaam kaa gungan karne main jo samay bitta he, vahi sarthak hota he, shesh vyarth he.
 unka kahana tha ki dhan kii seva, ahankaar or vansh swamipan - ek hi maansikta ke do paksh hai- 
 ‘bakhnaa ham to kahenge, ris karo mat koi. 
 maaya aru swami pani, doi-doi baat na hoi..’ 
 sant bakhnaa aatm jyoti jagaane kii baat par bal dete the. yadi ham sachhee sadhana karen to paramatma swayan aakar hamase milenge- 
 ‘dhundhai deep patang nai, to vakhanaan virad lai jayee. 
 deepak maahai joti whai, to ghanaan miliga ai.’ 
 kehete hai ajmer jate hue samraat jahangir narayana main tehre the. usne bakhnaa ji kii pariksha lene ke liye kaaji or panditon se poocha ki paramatma ne yah sristi kis samaru rachi?
 sant bakhana ji ne kaha- 
 ‘jihu viria yah sab hua, so ham kiya vichaar. 
 bakhnaa variyaan khushi kii, karata sirajanhaar.’ 
 ve kehete the ki sadhu santon kii vaani ko apnaanaa chahie, kyonki vahi samaaj ko dhokar nirmal banaati he. ‘vaani barase sabad huhaav. canaras bhari-bhari hariras bhav.’
