 inhone apane test cricket kaa pehla match 1998 main dakshin africa cricket team ke khilaaf khelaa tha or antim test match 2009 main australia cricket team ke khilaaf khelaa gaya tha ,jabaki ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket kii shuruyaat 1999 main pakistan cricket team ke khilaaf kii thi or antim match westeindies cricket team ke khilaaf khelaa tha. flintoff ne apane test karier main kul 79 maichon main 3845 ran banaae the or vanade main 141 maichon main 3394 ran banaae jabaki test main test main 226 wicket or vanade main 168 wicket chatkaaye the.
