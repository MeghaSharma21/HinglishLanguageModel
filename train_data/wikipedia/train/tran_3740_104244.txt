 pramathanath bose suprasiddh bhartiya vaigyanik or bhugarbhavid.
 inkaa janm 12 may 1855 i. ko bengal ke nadiya jile main gaipur naamak sthaan par hua tha. aapaki ucch shiksha landan vishvavidhyalay main hui, jahaan se aapane bhuvigyaan main bi. es.si. kii degree praapt kii. san 1880 main aapaki niyukti bhuvaigyanik surveykshan vibhaag main assistant suparintendent ke pad par hui. apane kaarykaal main aapane desh ke bhinn bhinn bhaagon main surveykshan kar bahut se khanij nikshepon kaa pataa lagaaya. bhusurvekshan vibhaag main kaam karne ke saath aap mayurbhanj riyasat ke bhi bhuvid the. mayurbhanj ke prasiddh louh parvat gorumahisani kii khoj kaa shrey aap hi ko he. aap hi ke kaaran tata aayran or steel company kii sthapana jamshedpur main hui.
 san 1903 main avkaash praapt karne ke baad aap ranchi main rahane lage. yahan jivan paryant aapane saarvajanik seva kii, evam saamaajik, dharmik or shaikshanik kshetron main mahatvapurn kaam kiya. vriddhavastha main bhi aap yuvakon ke samaan parishram kiya karte the. aapkaa jivan bahut sada tha tathaa aap apane sheel swabhaav ke kaaran bade lokapriy the. mrityuparyant aapkaa swasthya bahut acha rahaa. 27 april 1934 i. ko ranchi main hi aapkaa swargvaas ho gaya.
