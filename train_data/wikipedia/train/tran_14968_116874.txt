 dalit sira rajasthan or gujarat kaa ek meetha pakwaan yaa mithai he. yah pake hue gehun ke saath taiyaar kiya jaataa he, jise sirf dalia bhi kaha jaataa he.
 dalia kaa sira main nimna saamagree kii zarurat padati he, pake hue gehun, gud, makkhan, pani, doodh, ilaychi powder, chote nariyal ke tukade.
 dalia kaa sira banane main ek kadhai main ghi daalkar usamen dalia ko bhoonna padataa he or tab tak bhuna jaataa he jab tak uskaa rang sunaharaa na ho jaae. phir usamen pani daalkar aanch aane kaa intjaar karnaa padataa he. iske baad isamen gud yaa chini daal di jaati he. zyada swaadisht ke liye ilaychi aurnariyal ke tukade bhi mila sakate he.
