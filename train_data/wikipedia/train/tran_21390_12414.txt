 kham tibet ke teen parmparik praanton main se ek he, jinhe tibeti bhashaa main tibet ke teen 'cholka' kaha jaataa he. anya do praant amdo or eu-tsang hai. yah pahari shrunkhalaaon or tang ghaatiyon kaa kshetra he. yahan ke log tibeti main kham-paa kehalaate hai or lhasa ke tibetiyon se alag ruparang, veshbhusha or bolne kaa lahaza rakhte hai. aitihaasik rup se kham 'chushi gangadruk' ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa tha, jiskaa matlab 'chaar nadiyaan or chah parvatamalas' he. tibet par janavaadee ganatantr china kaa qabza hone ke baad uski sarkaar ne tibet kaa prashasnic vibhaajan kar diya or kham kaa bhushetra ab sichuaan or kuch had tak unnan praanton main sammilit he. 1954 main kham ke logon ne china ke kilaaf khulaa vidroh kar diya, jo 'khampa vidroh' ke naam se janaa jaataa he. unhonen bahari duniya se madad maangi lekin koi sahaayta na milane par yah vidroh china kii sarkaar dwaara kuchlaa gaya.
