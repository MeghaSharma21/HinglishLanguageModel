 yah suchi adhuri he; aap iskaa vistaar karke sahaayta kar sakate hai. 
 yah ek suchi he bien de interes cultural sthalon kii jo kaseres praant, spain main maujuud hai:
 kaseres kaseres praant kii rajdhani he. yah spain main maujuud ek actremdura swayatt samudaay kaa hissaa he. 2013 kii janaganana se pataa chalaa he ki kaseres kii jansankhya 96,000 ke lagbhag he. yahan kii nagar palika kaa kshetra lagbhag 1,750.33 km2 he. kshetra ke hisaab se yah janaskhya spain main sarvaadhik he. is divaar-band shahar ko unesco ne vishwa viraasat kaa sthal ghoshit kiya he.
