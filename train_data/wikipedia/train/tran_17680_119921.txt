 yamin ud daula nawab saadat ali khan avadh ke shaasha nawab shujauddula ke duusare bete the. saadat ali khan apane sautele bhai, asafuddaula, ke baad avadh ke takht par 1798 par baithe.
 saadat ali khan kii tajposhi 21 janvari 1798 ko bibyapur mahal, lakhanu main sar jon shor dwaara kii gai. unhone 1801 main angrezon ko aadha avadh de diya.
 unka 1805 main sar gore ouseli dwaara nirmit dilakusha kothi naamak ek mahal bhi tha.
 nawab saadat ali khan 1814 main mare or unhen apni patni 'khurshid zaadi' ke saath judwaan qaisarbaag ke makbare main dafanaaya gaya.
