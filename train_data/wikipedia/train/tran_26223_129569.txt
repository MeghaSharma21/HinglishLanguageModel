 varsh 2008 main bhartiya sansad main ghatit ek ghatana jisamen manmohan singh sarkaar dwaara vishwaas mat hasil karne kii bahes ke dauraan bhaajapa ke teen saansadon ne sansad main ek karod rupe ke noton kii gaddiyon sansad main dikhaai thi. in saansadon kaa aarope tha ki manmohan sarkaar ne samaajawaadee party ke amar singh ke madhyam se unke mat ko kharidne kii koshish kii thi.
 2011 main ek janhit yachika par sunwaayee karte hue sarvocch nyaayalay ke nirdeshon par dilli police is maamle par phir se sakriy hui or 6 sitambar 2011 ko amar singh tathaa bhaajapa ke do saansad tihaad jail bhej die gaye.
