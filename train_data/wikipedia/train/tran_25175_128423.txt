 hindi kavita kii parampara bahut lambi he. kuch vidbaan sarhapaad ko hindi kaa pehla kavi maanate hai. sarhapaad or unke samavarti va parvarti sidhon ne dohon or padon ke rup main apni sfoot rachanaaen prastut kin. rasokal tak aate-aate pracheen hindi kaa rup sthir ho chukaa tha. apbhransh or shuruaati hindi paraspar ghuli-mili dikhaai detii hai. dhire-dhire hindi main parishkar hota rahaa or apbhransh bhashaa ke patal se lupt ho gai.
