 rudradaman kaa girnaar shilaalekh pashchimi chhatrap naresh rudradaman dwaara likhvaya gaya shilaalekh he. yah shilaalekh girnaar parvaton par he jo junagarh ke nikat sthit he. yah 130-se 150 i ke madhya likhaa gaya tha. junagarh shilaon par ashok ke 14 shilaalekh tathaa skandagupt ke shilaalekh bhi he.
 rudradaaman kaa girnaar shilaalekh ucch koti ke sanskrit gadhy kaa swaroop prakat karata he jisamen subandhu, dandi or baan kii gadhyshailee kaa purvarup dekha ja saktaa he. isaki bhashaa saral or pravahpurn he. isamen deergh samaason kaa bhi prayog dekhte banataa he. alankrita style, nadaatmakataa isaki anya visheshtaayen he.
 is shilaalekh main ek bahut bade sarovar ke nirmaan kii baat kahi he. is kshetra main pani kii kami he. yah kaary bhi janhit ke liye prashansaneey prayaas he.
