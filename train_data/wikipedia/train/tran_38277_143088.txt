 dow sarala birla ek bhartiya shikshavid thim jo birla parivaar dwaara samarthit 45 shikshan sansthaanon kii santhapika. ve swatantraaa senaanee brijlaal biani kii beti thim jo ki agrani udyogpati ji di birla kii beti or arbapati kumaar mangalam birla kii daadi thi. april 1941 main unhone basant kumaar birla se shaadi kii. unka ek betaa aditya vikram birla or do betiyaan, jayshri mohataa or manjushri khetaan thim.
