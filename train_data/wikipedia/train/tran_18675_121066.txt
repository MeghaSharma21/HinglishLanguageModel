 chaturangini - pracheen bhartiya sangathit sena. sena ke chaar ang- hasti, ashv, rath, padaati maane jate hai or jis sena main ye chaaron hai, vah chaturangini kehalaati he. chaturangbal shabd bhi itihaspuraanon main miltaa he. is vishay main saamaanya niyam yah he ki prayek rath ke saath 10 gaj, prayek gaj ke saath 10 ashv, prayek ashv ke saath 10 padaati rakshak ke rup main rahete the, is prakaar sena praaia: chaturangini hi hoti thi.
 sena kii sabase choti tukdi 'patti' kehalaati he, jisamen ek gaj, ek rath, teen ashv, paanch padaati hote the. aisi teen pattiyaan senamukh kehalaati thim. is prakaar teen teen guna kar yathakram gulm, gana, vahini, prutna, chamu or anikini kaa sanghathan kiya jaataa tha. 10 anikini ek akshuhinee ke baraabar hoti thi. tadnusaar ek akshuhinee main 21870 gaj, 21970 rath, 65610 ashv or 109350 padaati hote the. kul yoga 218700 hota tha. kehete hai, kurukshetra ke yuddh main aisi 18 akshuhinee sena ladi thi. akshuhinee kaa yah pariman mahabharat main ullikhit he. mahabharat main main sena pariman kii jo gananaa he, usase is gananaa main kuch vilakshantaa he. shantiparv 59/41-42 main 'ashtaang sena' kaa ullekh he, usamen bhi pratham chaar yahi chaturangini sena he.
