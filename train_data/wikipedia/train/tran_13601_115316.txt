 pithauragarh tehsil bharat ke uttarakhand rajya main pithauragarh janapad main ek tehsil he. pithauragarh janapad ke dakshin-purvi bhaag main sthit is tehsil ke mukhyalay pithauragarh nagar main sthit hai. iske purv main nepal, pashchim main gangolihot tehsil, uttar main didihat tehsil tathaa dakshin main champavat janapad kii lohaghat tehsil he. tehsil ke adhikaar kshetra main kul 324 gaanv aate hai, or 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar isaki jansankhya 1,66,801 he.
