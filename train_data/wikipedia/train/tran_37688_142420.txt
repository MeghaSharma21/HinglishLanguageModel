 
 grupar haddidaar machhliyon ke perceformez gana ke seranidaaye kul ke epinefelinae upkul ke sadsy hote hai.
 "grupar" shabd kisi agyaat dakshin america adivasee bhashaa se utpann hua he or portugali bhashaa se hota hua vishwa kii anya bhashaaon main pahunchaa he. grupar ko philipinoz ke luzone kshetra main "lapu-lapu" or visaaya va mindanaao main "pugapo" kehete hai. newzealand kii maori bhashaa main ise "hapuku". madhya purv main ise "hammur" kehete he or faras kii khadi main ise bahut khaaya jaataa he.
 gruparon kaa sharir bada or munh chaudaa hota he. yah na to tezi se tairte hai or na hi lambi duriyon tak jate hai. inaki lambaai 1 meter se adhik or vazan 100 kilogram se zyaada ho saktaa he. yah anya machhliyaan, octopus or crusteshian khate hai, jinhen ve chabane kii bajaae niglanaa pasand karte hai. apane bade munh or chlom se vah tezi se badi maatra main pani apni ore khinch sakate hai or isi ko vah apane grass ko apni taraf khinchne ke liye prayog karte hai.
