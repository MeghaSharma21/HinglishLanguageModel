 tubalite kabir khan dwaara nirdeshit ek bhartiya drama film he, jiske nirmaataa kabir khan or salmaan khan hai. salmaan khan tathaa jhu jhu abhinit is film kii kahani 1962 ke bharat-china yuddh ke samay kii he. salmaan khan ne ek bhartiya vyakti kii bhoomika nibhaai he, jise china kii ek ladki se pyaar ho jaataa he.
 lakshmana singh bisht ek bholaa or acha insaan, parantu maansik taur se ayogy, 1962 china-bhartiya yuddh ke dauraan kumaun regiment main karyrat bhai bharat kii talash karne ke liye nikal padataa he.
 nirdeshak ko kannada film tublite ke nirmaataaon se ek naa aapatti pramaan patr praapt hua. july 2016 main ladakh main filmaane kaa pehla kaarykram shuru hua. chini abhinetri jhu jhu ko agrani bhoomika ke liye antim rup diya gaya tha. unhone 8 agast 2016 ko shooting shuru kii. pehla kaarykram ek maheene tak chale or agast 2016 ke madhya main puraa hua.
 film ke sangeet kii rachana preetam ne kii he.
