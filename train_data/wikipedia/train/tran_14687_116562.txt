 raharaas sahib sikhon dwaara shaam ke samay kii jane vaali prarthana he. is prarthana ko paanch guruon guru nanak dev ji, guru amardaas ji, guru ramdaas ji, guru arjan dev ji or guru govind singh ji ne banaaya he. mul prarthana jise sikhon ke pehle guru, nanak dev ji ne likhaa va bolaa tha main anya guruon ne apni panktiyaan jodi. har pankti ishwar ke vibhinn vichaaron va pahaluon par prakaash daalati he, va sarvashaktimaan kii aradhana karti he.rahiraas din ke ant main gaayi jati he. iskaa uddeshy din ke kaary khatm karne ke baad gayak main ek nai urza bharne ke liye hoti he. iskaa uddeshy ishwar kii prarthana kar ke shaareerik kamajori, thakaan, garibi, jameen-jaaydaad, niraashaa, asafalataa jaise vichaaron se chhutkaaraa paakar swayan main ek nai urja kaa sanchaar karnaa hota he. kehete hai ki isamen baiyanti chaupai guru govind singh ji dwaara gaayi hui unki vyaktigat prarthana he jo prakruti ke jal tatv se sambandhit he.
