 bhopal sambhag madhya bharat ke madhya pradesh rajya main ek prashasnic bhugolic ikai he. bhopal is sambhag kaa prashasnic mukhyalay he. vartmaan main, is sambhag main 5 jile hai, bhopal, raisen, rajgad, sihoroor vidisha.
 1956 main madhyapradesh main vilay se pehle bhopal, sihor or raisen jile purv bhopal rajya kaa hissaa the, or vidisha purv madhya bharat rajya kaa hissaa tha.
