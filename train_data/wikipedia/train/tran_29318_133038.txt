 yah pracheen bharat main teesri se paanchavin sadi tak shashan karne vaale gupt rajvansh kaa raja tha. inaki rajdhani patliputra thi jo vartmaan samay main patna ke rup main bihar kii rajdhani he. yah kumaar gupt kaa bada putr evam prasiddh gupt saas skandagupt kaa bada bhai tha. 
 yah hindi sahitya ke prasiddh naatakkaar jaishankar prasad dwaara likhe gaye lokapriya naatak skandagupt kaa pradhanpaatr he. usamen iskaa chithran ek bheeru, vilaasi or clive raja ke rup main hua he. yah apni stri dhruvaswamini ko shaq raja ko bhent swaroop dekar sandhi kar lene ke prastaav ko bhi sweekaar kar letaa he.
