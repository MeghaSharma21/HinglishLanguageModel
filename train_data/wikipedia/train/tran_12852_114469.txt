  aay ek khapat or bachat he jisko avasar nirdisht samay seemaa ke bheetar praapt kiya jaataa he jo aam taur par maudrik sandarbh main vyakt kiya jaataa he. sabhi mazdooree, vetan, laabh, bhugtan, kiraye aadi aay ghar-parivaar, desh ko chalaane main jiskaa prayog hota he. vyapaar aadi main iskaa mahatv he, dhan kaa niveshkar arjit sampati jisamen laabh kamaaya jaataa he usko aay kaha jaataa he. 
 prati vyakti aay kaa upyog kisi ek desh ke logon kii sampatti kaa anumaan lagane ke liye kiya jaataa he, kisi anya desh kii tulana main. aamtaur par yah kisi sarvamaanya antarrashtriya mudra jaise euro yaa dollar main mapa jaataa he.
