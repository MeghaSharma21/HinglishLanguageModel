 sanyukt rajya kii naagarikta ek darja hai, jisase vishesh adhikaar, kartavya or laabh aprihaary hote hai. naagarikta ko adhikaron kaa hone kaa adhikaar samajha jaataa hai, kyonki, yah uttarvarti adhikaron ke samooh, jaise ki, sanyukt rajya main rahane or kaam karne kaa adhikaar, or sangheey sahaayta praapt karne kaa adhikaar, kaa aadhaar-sthambh hai.
