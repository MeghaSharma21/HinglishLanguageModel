 tafseer : yah ek arabi shabd he. aam taur par quran ke vyakhya ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he. tafseer ke lekhak ko "mufassir" kehete hai. tafseer, aksar quran ko acchhee tarah samajhane ke liye istemaal kiya jaataa he. matlab, chand, vichaar, taatpary, arth, hetu, vagaira ko samajhane ke liye tafseer kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. alag alag vidwaanon ne alag alag tafseer likhi he, magar moolaarth ek hi paaya jaataa he.
