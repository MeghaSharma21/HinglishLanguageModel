 baltoro muzataag karakoram parvatamala kii ek upashrinkhalaa he. vishwa kii duusari sabase oonchi chotii ke2 isi shrinkhalaa main hi sthit he. prashasnic rup se yah paak-adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-blatistan kshetra main or china dwaara niyantrit shinjiang praant main aata he. bharat lagbhag is puure kshetra ko apana bhaag maanata he.
 yah baltoro parvatamala ke un parvaton kii suchi he, jo 7,200 meter se unche hai or 500 meter se adhik kii sthalakritik udagrata rakhte hai. is se kam udagrata rakhane vaale shikhar swatantra parvat nahin maane jate balki aasapaas ke anya parvaton ke hi shikhar maane jate hai .
