 sambandhit kwantum sankhya:
 sanyukt:
 sambharik prachakran kan kaa ek naij gun he. yah prabal anyonya kriya se sambandhit kwantem sankhya he. wo kan jinpar lagane vaala prabal bal samaan ho lekin aavesh kaa maan bhinn ho kaa samaan kan kii bhinn avasthaa ke rup main adhyayan kiya ja saktaa he. iske liye unhen unki aavesh avasthaa ke sangat bhinn sambharik prachakran avasthaa paribhaashit kii ja sakati he.
