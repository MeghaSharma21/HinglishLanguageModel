 kupolli venkatappagauda putappa  ek kannada lekhak evam kavi the, jinhen 20veen shatabdi ke mahanatam kannada kavi kii oopaadhi di jaati he. ye kannada bhashaa main gyanpith sammaan paane vaale saat vyaktiyon main pratham the. putappa ne sabhi saahitiyik kaary upnaam 'kuvimpu' se kiye hai. unako sahitya evam shiksha ke kshetra main san 1958 main padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. inke dwaara rachit ek mahakavy shriramayana darshanam ke liye unhen san 1955 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
