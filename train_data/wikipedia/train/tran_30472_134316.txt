 badi dur se aaye hai ek bhartiya hasya dharawahik he, jo sab tivi par prasaarit hota he. yah kaarykram 09 june 2014 se armbh hua tha. yah ek bahari duniya ke parivaar kii kahani he jo apane laapata bachche ko dhundne ke liye prithvi par aata he.
 yah kahani ek pargrahi parivaar kii he jo apane bachche kii khoj main prithvi par aate he or yahan aakar ve kaaphi pareshaan ho jate he, kyonki unki or prithvi vaasiyon kii kerani main bahut antar he. unnat shakti se susajjit yah pargrahi parivaar hamaari sabhyataa ko apanaate hue maanviya rup dhaaran karata he. yaheen se kala evam hasya kii shuruuaat hoti he. yah kaarykram bharat kaa pehla pargrahi hasya kaarykram he.
