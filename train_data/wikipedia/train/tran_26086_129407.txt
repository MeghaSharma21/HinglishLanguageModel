 angin acurek ek turk abhinayta he jise antarrashtriya emi puraskaar ke liye namit kiya ja chukaa he. acurek ne turki kii kai pramukh filmon or television shrinkhalaaon main kaam kar prasiddhi pai he. bharat main angin kii prasiddhi unke television dharawahik phaatmagul ke kaaran he jiskaa prasaaran zee zindagi naamak tivi channel par kiya jaataa he.
 angin acurek kaa janm 12 oktober, 1981 ko turki kii rajdhani ankara main hua tha. angin ke pita ek sarkari adhikari or mata ek gruhani hai. angin kaa ek chota bhai bhi he. san 2002 main, angin acurek ne metropolitan vishvavidhyalay se snaatak kii oopaadhi "bhashavigyaan or itihaas," main visheshgyata ke saath praapt kii he. chaatr jivan se hi angin ko theatre kaa shauk tha or wo k i naatako ke manchan main sahabhaagi tha. snaatak hone ke do saal ke baad, angin ne yuva pratibhaaon ke liye ek tivi sho pratiyogita turkiye'nin yildizlari “Turkiye’nin Yildizlari” main bhaag liya. is pratiyogita main angin ne "sarvshereshth abhinayta" kaa puraskaar jeeta. is pratiyogita ke baad angin acurek ko pehali baar ek tivi shrinkhalaa yabanchi daamaat main ek sahaayak bhoomika mili.
 san 2006 main, angin acurek ko unki pehali film kadar main kaam karne kaa prastaav mila. is film kii bhoomika ke liye unhen "sabase honhaar abhinayta" kaa puraskaar praapt hua.2007 main angin acurek ek aitihaasik dharawahik karylan Karaylan main nazar aaye jo francisi kabze ke itihaas se sambandhit tha. shrinkhalaa bir bulut olsan Bir Bulut Olsam main angin ek maansik rup se asantulit premi kii bhoomika nibhaate dikhe. san 2010-2011, main aaye dharawahik phaatmagulun suju ne main angin kii nibhaai romantic bhoomika kerim ne unhen bahut prasiddhi dilaai, is dharawahik main unki nayika berin saat thim.
