 
 cansas nadi sanyukt rajya america ke cansas rajya se hokar bahane vaali ek nadi he. ise kau ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. yah nadi misori nadi kii jal nikasi kaa sabase dakshinpashchimi bhaag bhi he, jo swayan bhi mississippi nadi kii jal nikasi kaa sabase uttarpashchimi bhaag he. 
 is nadi kaa naam kabhi is kshetra main rahane vaale kanza logon ke naam par padaa he or phir baad main is nadi ke naam par cansas rajya kaa naam padaa.
 nadi ghati kii ausat chaudaai 4.2 kimi tak he or sarvaadhik chaudaai vaimego or rausaville ke bich he jahaan par ye 6.4 kimi chaudee he or phir isaki chaudaai eudora se neeche ke sthanon par ghatakar 1.6 kimi tak rah jaati he. nadi kaa adhiktar bhaag baadh niyantran ke liye bandhikrut he, lekin kainsaas nadi saamanytah mukt rup se bahati he or iske maarg par bahut kam avarohak hai, jisame mod band or ek nimna prabhaav vaala panbizli band sammilit hai.
  parvat  maidan dweep
