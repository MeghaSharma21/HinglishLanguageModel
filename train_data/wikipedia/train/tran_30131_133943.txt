 hont yaa onth manushya tathaa kai anya jantuon ke munh kaa bahari dikhne vaala bhaag hota he. hont komal, lacheele tathaa chalyman hote hai or aahaar grahan chhidr kaa dwaar hote hai. iske alaava vah dhwani kaa uccharan karne main madad bhi karte hai jisaki vajah se manushya gale se nikali dhwani ko vaartalap main parivartit kar paane main sakasham ho sakaa he. manushyon main hont sparsh samvedi ang hota tathaa purush tathaa nari ke antarang samay main kaamuktha badhaane kaa kaam bhi karata he.
 hont do bhaagon main vibhajit hota he-upree hont or nichalaa hont. vigyaan kii bhashaa main inako kramshah lebium supirius oris tathaa lebium infirius oris bhi kaha jaataa he. jis hisse main hont twachaa ke saath milte hai us hisse ko vermilion border kehete he. usee prakaar honton kii laal khaal ko vermilion zone kehalaata he. yahi vermilion zone munh ke andar kii shleshmi jhilli or sharir ke upar kii twachaa ke bich kaa parivartan kshetra he. honton main na to bal hote hai or na hi paseene kii granthiyaan. isliye unhen paseene tathaa shaareerik tel kii suraksha nahin mil paati jisase vah apni upree sataha ko chikna rakh saken, taapmaana niyantrit kar saken tathaa rogaanuon se batch saken. isi karanvash hont jaldi suukh jate hai or cat-fat jate hai.
 hamaare jyotish shaastra bhi hont ko ahmiyat di gai he. jyotishi vyakti ke honton ko dekhakar uske vyaktitv ke bare main jaan lete hai.
