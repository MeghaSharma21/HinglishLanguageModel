 republican party se bhi ise janaa jaataa he) sanyukt rajya america kii do sabase badi raajnneeti partiyon main se ek he, duusari democratic party he. 1854 main daasatv-virdohi kaaryakartaaon dwaara sthapit kii gai republican party ne desh kii raajnneeti par 1860 se 1932 tak kii zyaadaatar avadhi main apana varchasv rakhaa tha. america ke 44 main se 18 raashtrapati republican party se bane hai. sabase naveenatam samay main republican party se bane raashtrapati jorge walker bush the, jinka kaarykaal 2001 se 2009 tak tha.
 nivartmaan samay main party kii nitijyata america roodhiwaadita ko pratibimbit karti he. republican party kii america roodhiwaadita puri tarah se udaarvaad kii raajnneetik vichaardhaara kii asweekruti par aadhaarit nahin hai, chunki america roodhiwaadita ke kai siddhant shastreey udaarvaad par hi aadhaarit hai. iske vipareet republican party kii roodhiwaadita mukhyatah shastreey siddhanton par aadhaarit he jo mukhya raajnneetik pratidwandvi democratic party ke aadhunik udaarvaad ke viruddh he. democratic party kii mukhya vichaardhaara samakaleen samay main america udaarvaad par aadhaarit he.
 varsh 2010 main chuni gai 112 ve congress main republican party ke paas house of ripregentative main siton kaa bahumat he tathaa seenet main yah alpamat main he. party ke paas desh ke kul 50 rajyon main se bahumat main rajyapal hai tathaa rajya vidhayikaon main bhi bahumat he.
 2012 main chune gai 113 wein congress main bhi republican party ke paas house of ripregentative main siton kaa bahumat he tathaa seenet main yah alpamat main he. saath hi party ke paas desh ke kul 50 rajyon main se bahumat main rajyapal hai tathaa rajya vidhayikaon main bhi bahumat he.
