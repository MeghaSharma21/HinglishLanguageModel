 siddham yaa south asia inscriplens database dakshin asia, madhya asia or dakshin-purv asia se praapt shilalekhon kaa muktarup se uplabdh strot he jo british sangrahalay, british pustkaalay or skool off oriental and african studies main rakhe hue hai. isaki sthapana european research counsil ne kiya tha. siddham kaa mukhya dhyaan bindu chauthi, paanchavin or chhathi shatabdi ke sanskrit ke puralekh hai kintu isamen sabhi kaalon ke puralekhon kaa samavesh kiya ja saktaa he. anumaan he ki keval dakshin asia se hi praapt aitihaasik puralekhon kii sankhya 90 hajaar se adhik he. agle charan main siddham kaa vistaar tamil, kannada, prakrut, pracheen tibeti, burmi bhashaa, pu bhashaa or mon bhashaa ko bhi sammilit karne kaa lakshya he.
