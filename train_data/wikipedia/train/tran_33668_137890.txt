 virendra thakran bhartiya pahalwaan hai jinhone 1992 main kaali main vishwa kushti championships main kaansya padak jeeta tha. 1995 main kamanwealth championships main unhone rajat padak jeeta tha . dakshin asian championships main unhen swarna padak praapt hua. gramin kshetron main hone vaale kushti muqablon main ve bahut lokapriy hai or log unhen dhiraj pahalwaan ke naam se pukaarte hai.
 virendra kaa janam gurugram ke nikat jhadsa gaanv main shri bharat singh thakran ke ghar san 1970 main hua. inhone san 1991 se lekar 1997 tak islampur gaanv main guga navami par hone vaale dangal main hameshaa kushtiya jiti thi.
 gaanv ke hansaram pahalwaan or inke pita bharat singh main acchhee dosti thi. hansaram ne unke kushti prem ko dekhte hue unako swayan guru hanuman ke akhaade main choda. ye bhi guru hanuman ke priy shishyon main se ek the. baad main inhone bhartiya rail main apni nokari kii shuruyaat kii. ye bhartiya rail vibhaag ke utkrishth pahalawaanon main gine jate the. san 1995 main bharat main yojit saf khelo main swarna padak jeeta tha. 1992 main inhone vishwa championships main kaansya padak jeeta tha 1991 main junior world championships main dhiraj bronj jeet chuke hai. 1995 main inhone kaamanwealth kushti championsheep main rajat padak jeet kar bharat kaa naam roshan kiya tha. inaki sagi bahan shrimati preetam sivaach bhartiya mahila hoki team kii captain or desh kii pratibhavan khiladiyon main ek rahi huyi he. kul milakar is thakran parivaar ne desh ke liye honhaar khiladi paidaa kiye hai.
