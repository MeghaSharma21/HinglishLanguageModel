 islam garh jalalpur jattaan kaa ek nagar he. yah shahar ke dakshin main sthit he. ye ek pracheen shahar he jise 300 ipu main raja chandragupta maury ne banwaaya tha. sthaneey itihaaskaaron kii maanata he ki islam garh main chandragupta maury ne ek qile kaa nirmaan kiya tha. qile kii jagah kaa asal naam to maaloom nahin magar samay ke saath ye qila islam garh qila ke naam se prasiddh ho gaya. ye qila aurangzeb, ahamed shah abdali, maharaja ranjit singh or in kii senaaon kaa taksaal rahaa he. keval qile ke kuch karaab avshesh hi aaj maujuud he. 1832 main islam garh qila lahore ke maharaja ranjit singh kaa taksaal tha.
