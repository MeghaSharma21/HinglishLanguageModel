 arijit singh janm 25 april 1987) ek bhartiya parshv gayak hai. inkaa janm pashchim bengal ke murshidaabad main hua tha. inhone apane karier kii shuruyaat 2005 main ek vaastavik kaarykram fame gurukul se kii thi. inkaa naam jab prasiddh hua jab inhone 2013 main aashiqui 2 film main tum hi ho gaanaa gaaya tha. is kaaran inako 59waan filmafere puraskaar main sabase acha purush gayak chunaa gaya tha. inke alaava inhone kil dil main bhi aisa hi gaanaa gaaya tha jo sajade naam tha. janvari 2016 main 61waan filmafere puraskaar inkaa suraj duba he gaanaa jo roy film main gaaya tha inhen sabase acha gaanaa chunaa gaya. inhen varsh 2018 main sun mere humsafar gane ke liye bhi film fair award diya gaya he.
