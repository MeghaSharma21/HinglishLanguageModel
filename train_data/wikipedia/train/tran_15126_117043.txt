 samul mathew karran england cricket team ke ek cricket khilaadi he, jo saare or england ke liye khelte he. karran ek baayen haath se ballebaaji or baayen haath se hi madhyam-tej gati se gendbaaji karte hai.
 karran kaa janm zimbabwe ke purv kevin karran ke ghar northmpton main hua tha. ye england ke antarrashtriya cricket khilaadi tom karran or ben karran ke bhai hai. inhone springvail house, saint jorge college, haraare or wellington college, barkshaayar main padhai kii he.
 karran under-15, under-17, or dwitiya ilevan str par saare kaa pratinidhitiv kar chuke he. 2014 ke satr ke dauraan unhone saare championships premier divijan main vebridge kaa pratinidhitiv kiya tha. purv cricket cricket khilaadi alek stewart ne unhen "17 varshiya sabase achche cricketer" ke rup main varnit kiya tha.
 sam karran ko trans-tasman trikoniya siries 2018 kii siries main england cricket team main pehali baar mauka diya gaya tha lekin isamen unhen khilne kaa mauka nahin diya gaya tha. is trikoniya shrinkhalaa main england ke alaava australia or newzealand bhi shaamil thi. iske baad inhen pakistan ke england daure par shaamil kiya gaya tha kyonki ben stokes ke mansapeshiyon main khinchaav aa gaya tha. iske baad inhen duusare match main padaarpan karne kaa mauka diya gaya tha. karran ne 1 june 2018 ko test cricket main padaarpan kiya he.
 24 june 2018 ko, inhone australia cricket team ke khilaaf ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket karier kii shuruyaat kii.
