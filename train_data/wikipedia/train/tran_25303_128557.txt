 ugta bharat ek hindi samachaarpatr he. iskaa prarambh uttar pradesh ke noida shahar se 17 july 2010 ko kiya gaya tha. is patr ko do tarah se prakashit kiya jaataa he, ek saptahik ke rup main jo ki prayek saptaah ke veeravaar ko prakashit hota he or ek dainik patr ke rup main. yah antarjaal par i-paper tathaa online vaibsite ke taur par bhi uplabdh he. 
 rakesh kumaar arya is patr ke sansthaapak evam pradhan sampadak hai. is patr ko varsh 2010 main saptahik patr ke rup main shuru kiya gaya tha evam isaki apaar safalataa ko dekhte huye ise varsh 2015 se dainik patr ke rup main bhi prakashit kiya ja rahaa he.
 yah samachar patr uttar pradesh ke noida shahar main prakashit hota he.
