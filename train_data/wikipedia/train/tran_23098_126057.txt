 punjabi bhashaa aandolan ) pakistan ke punjab praant main shuru hua ek aandolan he jiskaa uddeshy punjabi bhashaa, kala, samskruti or sahitya ko punerjeevit karnaa he. pakistan main punjabiyon kii sankhya sabase adhik he phir bhi punjabi ko sarkari manyata nahin he. urdu ko rashtrabhasha bana diya gaya he jabaki urdu pakistan ke bahut hi kam logon kii maatrubhaasha he. punjabi bhashaa aandolan, punjabi rashtrawaad par aadhaarit he.
