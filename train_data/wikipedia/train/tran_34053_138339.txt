 tezaab 1988 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. ection romani is film main anil kapur or maadhuri dikshit mukhya bhoomika main he. film ne chaar filmphere puraskaar jite the. geet "ek do teen" se maadhuri bahut lokapriy hui. film 1988 kii sabase safal hindi film bhi thi. isse maadhuri kii safalataa kii shuruyaat bhi hui.
 mahesh deshmukh desh kii sashastr balon main shaamil hua kyonki vah eemaandaaree se or deshbhakt se desh kii seva kar sake. apane college ke dinon main uske saathi sahpaathee mohini ke saath jhagada hua. apane doston or khud ke bich ek shart ke baad vah mohini ke saath pyaar main pad jaataa he, jo baad main mahasoos karti he ki vah vastav main uske saath pyaar main pad gaya he or vah bhi sahanubhuti detii he. mohini ke pita sharaabi he or unki ichha he ki mohini nrity or veshyavriti main chali jaae he taaki vah apni dainik khuraaq praapt kar sake. mohini mahesh ke saath sharan lene kaa prayaas karti he, haalanki, mahesh ko is had tak uljhan main daal diya jaataa he ki vah giraftaar, aaropee sabit ho jaataa he or use bombay shahar se tadeepaar rahane kii sajaa sunai jaati he. jab mahesh lautataa he, to vah ab eemaandaar or deshbhakt mahesh nahin he, balki munna naamak ek gangster he, jisko kai puraane hisaab chuktaa karne hai.
 sabhi geet javed akhtar dwaara likhit; sara sangeet lakshmikant-pyarelal dwaara rachit.
 geet ek do teen ko film baagi 2 ke liye punah nirmit kiya gaya jise shreya ghoshaal dwaara gaaya gaya.
 film superhit rahi thi or 1988 kii vyavsaayik rup se safal film thi. "ek do teen" geet ke liye bhi ye film prakhyat he.
