 ganit main 1,2,3,... ityadi sankhyaon ko prakritik sankhyaayein kehete hai. ye sankhyaayein vastuon ko ginaney athwa kram main rakhane ke liye prayukt hoti hai.
 prakritik sankhyaon ke jo gunaswabhaav bhajyata se sambandhit hai.
 
 
 
 
 unka adhyayan sankhya siddhant main hota hai. udhaaharan: abhajya sankhyaon kaa bantan. vibhaajan praganana ityadi gananaa tathaa krameekaran sambandhi samasyaon kaa adhyayan kramchay-sanchay main kiya jaataa he.
 kuch lekhak shunya ko bhi prakritik sankhyaon ke samucchy main ginate hai, lekin adhiktar lekhak keval 1, 2, 3, ... ityadi dhan sankhyaon ko prakritik sankhyaayein bataate hai.
