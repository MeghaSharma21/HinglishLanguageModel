 anges deaton ko alfred nobel kii purnyasmriti main diya jane vaala arthshaastra kaa nobel puraskaar oktober 12 ,2015 ko pradaan kiya gaya. unhen yah puraskaar khapat, garibi, or kalyaan ke apane vishleshan ke liye pradaan kiya gaya. 
 deaton kaa janm united kingdom main edinburgh, scotland main oktober 19 ,1945 ko hua. praarambhik shiksha fets college main huyi. inhone cambridge vishvavidhyalay se snaatak, snatakottar evam piech.di. kii oopaadhiyaan praapt kii. varsh 1976 main yah bristol vishvavidhyalay main arthshaastra ke professor bane. yahaan inhone 1983 tak kaary kiya. vartmaan main yah america main prinston vishvavidhyalay ke liye kaary karte hai. 
 inke do bachche hai jinka janm 1970 or 1971 main hua inhane eni cese, aarthik anusandhan ke rashtriya beuro main ek shodh sahyogi se shaadi kii thi.
