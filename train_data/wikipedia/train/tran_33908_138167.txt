 uranimottar tatv yaa parauranium tatv yaa transuranik tatva ve rasinic tatva hai, jinka parmaanu bhaar 92 se adhik hota he. 92, uranium kaa parmaanu bhaar he. atah ye sabhi aavart sarini main uranium ke baad aate hai. inamen se koi bhi tatva sthir nahin hota he, va radiodharmee kshay ke uparaant anya chote tattvon main badal jate hai.
 aavart sarini ko dekhne se gyaat hoga ki prakruti main paae janevaale tatwon main uranium sabase baari he or isaki parmaanu sankhya 92 he, parantu kuch aise manushyanirmit tatv bhi hai jinaki parmaanu sankhya 92 se adhik he. in tatwon ko ham uranimottar tatv, yaa parauranium tatv, kehete hai. ye saare tatv asthir tathaa radioactive gun ke hai. inaki khoj tatwantaran kriyaaon dwaara hui or ye uranium tatv se nirmit kiye gaye. rasinic gunon main inamen bahut samanta he, jisase inhen actenide shrenee main rakhaa jaataa he.
 uranimottar tatwon main plutonium kaa mahatvapurn sthaan he. iskaa aarambh main hi upyog parmaanu bam main ho chukaa tha or 1950 i0 se purv hi iskaa utpaadan bhi adhik maatra main ho chukaa tha. isse ucch parmaanu sankhya vaale tatv adhik asthir hote tatwon ke samasthanik itane asthir hai ki unke rasinic prayog rasinic gun viral mrudaaon, yaa lanthenid tatwon se milte-julate hai. yadi bhavishya main 104, yaa isse adhik parmaanu sankhya ke tatwon kaa nirmaan sambhav ho sakaa, to unke gun inase bhinn honge. ve kramash: chauthe, paanchaven, chathe aadi samoohon ke tatwon ke samaan honge.
