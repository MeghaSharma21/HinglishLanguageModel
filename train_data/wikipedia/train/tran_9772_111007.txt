 ambalika mahabharat main kashiraj kii putree bataai gayi hai. ambalika kii do badi bahane thim, amba tathaa ambika. amba, ambika or ambalika kaa swayamvar hone vaala tha. unke swayamvar main jaakar akele hi bhishma ne vahaan aaye samast raajaaon ko parrast kar diya or teenon kanyaon kaa haran karke hastinapur le aaye jahaan unhone teenon bahanon ko satyavati ke saamne prastut kiya taaki unka vivaah hastinapur ke raja or satyavati ke putr vichitraveery ke saath sampann ho jaye. ambika or ambalika vichitraveery kii patniyaan baniin. lekin vichitraveery kii akaal mrityu ke kaaran vah donon niqusantan rah gayin. bhishma ne pehle hi brahmacharya vrath kii shapath le rakhi thi or ab donon putron, chitrangad tathaa vichitraveery kii akaal mrityu ke kaaran kuruvansh kaa vansh khatre main tha. aise main satyavati ne apane sabase bade putr veda vyas ko yaad kiya or niyog kii vidhi se ambika or ambalika kaa garbhaadhan karavaaya.satyavati ne ambalika ko nirdesh diya ki vah ambika kii tarah apni ankhen band naa kare. jab veda vyas ambalika ke sammukh prastut huye to ambalika lajja ke kaaran pili pad gai or isi kaaran jab uski kokh se pandu kaa janm hua to vah janm se hi pilia rog se grast tha. 
 ga: vichitraveery ke nidhan ke baad vyas se dhritarashtra or pandu kaa janm hua. c: kunti ko vivaah se pehle karn paidaa hua. da: pandav pandu ke putr the. lekin devataaon ke var prabhaav se kunti or maadri ko ye putr utpann hue. yama dharmraj se yudhisthir,vaayu se bhim,indra se arjuna,maadri ko aswani devataaon se judawaan bachche nakul, sahadev hue. t: duryodhana or uske sau bhai ek baar paidaa hue. na: pandavon ko draupadi se paanch putr utpann hue. unako upapandav kehete the. : ** yudhisthir se prativindhyas, bhim se shritasom, arjuna se shritakarm, nakul se shatanik, sahadev se shritsen kaa janm hua.
