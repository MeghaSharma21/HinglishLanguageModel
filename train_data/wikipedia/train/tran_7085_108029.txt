 vidyut ke kisi strot se sampark main aane ke kaaran twachaa, mansapeshiyon athwa bal se hokar paryaapt vidyut dhara pravaahit ho jaati he to ise vidyut sparshaghat kehete hai. yah jaanboojhkar kiya gaya ho saktaa he yaa durghatanavash ho saktaa he. kintu praayah 'sparshaghat' se aashay sharir ke kisi ang se avaanchit dhara-pravaah se hi liya jaataa he.
 vidyut sparshaghat se twachaa jal sakati he, aadmi behosh ho saktaa he, yaa mrityu ho sakati he.
 1. aag, bijli or pani kabhi-kabhi bahut khatarnaak ho sakate hai. atah bijli kaa surakshit prayog karen.
 2. bijli pole tathaa ste vir me apane jaanavar na baadhen.
 3. bijli ke taar ke paas kande sukhaane ke liye lehe kaa taar na baandhe.
 4. bijli line ke niche bas trali khadi karke saamaan na utaare.
 5. bijli line ke neeche yaa nikat makaan, khalihaan na banaaye tathaa ped na ugaaye.
 6. katiyaa lagaakar bijli kaa prayog na kare. arth taar/arthig se bijli na jalaaye.
 7. I.S.I. vaayaruing materiel evam upkarnon kaa prayog kare.
 8. vidyut vaayaruing main arth likege protective divaais lagaayen, purna suraksha paayen.
 9. vidyut durghatana kii suchana vidyut suraksha vibhaag ko turant den.
 10. apane vidyut adhishthanon/vidyut upkarnon kii niyamanusaar vidyut suraksha vibhaag dwaara jaanch karavaayen.
 11. bijli ke taron se chhedchhad na karen.
 12. aag kii sthiti main bijli turant band karen.
 13. bijli kii aag par pani kadaapi na daalen.
 14. bijli se durghatnagrast vyakti ko mrit na samajhen turant upachaar karen.
 15. vidyut vaayaruing/adhishthapan kaa kaary rajkeey licensadhari vidyut thekedaaron se karaayen.
 16. bijli mistre vidyut suraksha vibhaag dwaara yojit pariksha dekar viruman permit praapt kar sakate hai.
 17. viruman permit dhari mistre se hi bijli kaa kaary karaayen.
 18. switch ko phej ke taar main hi lagavaayen. neutral main kadaapi na lagaayen.
