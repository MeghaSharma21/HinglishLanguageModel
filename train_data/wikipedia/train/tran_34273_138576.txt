 wiliam web elice cap, rugby union vishwa cap ke vijeta ko di jaati he. is cap kaa naam wiliam web elice ke naam par he, jo ek lokapriy myth ke anusaar prasiddh rugby skool ke chaatr the or ek footbal match ke dauraan gend ko uthaa kar rugby khel kaa avishkaar kiya.
 trophy 38 centimetre unchi or 4.5 kilo vajan he, or chaandi se bani he. yah cap pehali baar newzealand ne 1987 rugby union vishwa cap jeet kar praapt kiya tha. cap ko "bill" upnaam se bhi bulaayaa jaataa he.
 do aadhikarik web elice cap rahe hai, ek cap landan kii kairington or company dwaara banaai gai ek 1906 trophy he, duusari ek 1986 pratikruti he.
 jon candal-karcenter, england ke purv khilaadi or pehle rugby union vishwa cap ke aayojak or bob veigail, england ke purv khilaadi or antarrashtriya rugby board ke sachiv, gerard and company kaa daura kiya, jiske nideshak richard jarvis ne unhen yah cap dikhaaya. jiske baad yah cap pehle vishwa cap me upyog ke liye chunaa gaya. trophy kaa anurakshit prayek khel ke baad sunar thomas lyat karte he. ise jald hi "wiliam web elice cap" naam diya gaya. cap chaar deshon dwaara jeeta gaya he; newjiland, australia, dakshin africa or england.
 cap ke vartmaan dharak newjiland he, jise ve 2011 vishwa cap ke final main france ko 8-7 se haraane ke baad jeeta. cap har vishwa cap toornament se pehle daure par chalaa jaataa he, vishwa cap ke final main bhaag lene vaale sabhi rashtron ke liye.
