 sar philomi teleto  tuvaalu ke ek rajaneta hai. unhen 15 april 2005 se 19 march 2010 ke bich, tuvaalu kii rani, elizabeth dwitiya dwaara, tuvaalu ke governor-general yani maharajyapal ke pad par niyukt kiya gaya tha. is kaal ke dauraan ve maharani ke pratinidhi ke rup main, unki anupasthiti ke dauraan shaasha ke kartavyon kaa nirvaah karte the.
