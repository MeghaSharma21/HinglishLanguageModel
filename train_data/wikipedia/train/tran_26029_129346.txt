 siris yaa series hamaare saura mandal ke kshudragrah ghere main sthit ek bouna grah he. iskaa vyas lagbhag 950 kimi he or yah apane swayan ke gurutvkarshak khichaav se gol akar paa chukaa he. yah kshudragrah ghere kii sab se badi vastu he or isamen us puure ghere kii lakhon khagoleey vastuon ke puure sammilit dravyaman kaa lagbhag ek-tihaai siris main nihit he. mana jaataa he ke siris barf or pathar kaa bana hua he. adhiktar vaigyanik anumaan lagate hai ke isaki sataha barfili or andar kaa kendriya bhaag patthariilaa he. aisa bhi sambhav he ke pathar or barf ke bich ek pani kii moti tah ho.
