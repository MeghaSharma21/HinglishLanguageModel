 martin luther se bhramit na hon. yah lekh dow martin luther king, junior ke bare main he jo alag he
 dow martin luther king, junior america ke ek padri, aandolankaaree evam africa-america naagarik adhikaron ke sangharsh ke pramukh netaa the. unhen america kaa gandhi bhi kaha jaataa he. unke prayatnon se america main naagarik adhikaron ke kshetra main pragati hui; isaliye unhen aaj maanav adhikaron ke pratik ke rup main bhi dekha jaataa he. do charchon ne unako sant ke rup main bhi manyata pradaan kii he.d.r martin ka americy rashtra me bahut bada yogdaan raha hai
 san 1959 main unhone bharat kii yatra kii. dow king ne akhabaaron main kai aalekh likhe. 'strid toverd freedom' tathaa 'vhai ve can note wet' unki likhi do pustaken hai. san 1957 main unhone south chrishchiyan leadership conference kii sthapana kii. dow king kii priy ukti thi- 'ham vah nahin hai, jo hamen hona chahie or ham vah nahin hai, jo hone vaale hai, lekin khuda kaa shukr he ki ham vah bhi nahin hai, jo ham the.' 4 april 1968 ko goli markar unki hatya kar di gai.
