 jab sharir kaa taap saamaanya se adhik ho jaye to us dasha ko fever yaa bukaar kehete he. yah rog nahin balki ek lakshan he jo bataata he ki sharir kaa taap niyantrit karne vaali pranali ne sharir kaa vaanchit taap 1-2 degree celsius badhaa diya he. manushya ke sharir kaa saamaanya taapmaana 37°celsius yaa 98.6° fahrenheit hota he. jab sharir kaa taapmaana is saamaanya str se upar ho jaataa he to yah sthiti fever yaa bukhaar kehalaati he. fever koi rog nahin he. yah keval rog kaa ek lakshan he. kisi bhi prakaar ke sankraman kii yah sharir dwaara di gai pratikriya he. badhata hua fever rog kii gambhirtha ke str kii ore sanket karata he.
 nimnlikhit rog fever kaa kaaran ho sakate he-
 saadhaaran fever main sharir kaa taapmaana 37.5 di.se. yaa 100 fahrenheit se adhikhona, sirdard, thand laganaa, jodon main dard, bhukh main kami, kabj hona yaa bhukh kam hona evam thakaan hona pramukh lakshan hai.
 iske upachaar hetu saral upaay paalan karen:rogi ko achche hawaadaar kamare main rakhana chaiye. use bahut saare drav padaarth pine ko den. svachh evam mulaayam vastra pahanaauun, paryaapt vishram ati aavashyak he. yadi fever 39.5 degree se. yaa 103.0 fahrenheit se adhik ho yaa phir 48 ghanton se adhik samay ho gaya ho to doctor se paraamarsh len.
 iske alaava rogi ko khub sara svachh evam ublaa hua pani pilaaein, sharir ko paryaapt calories dene ke liye, glucose, aarogyavardhak peya, falon kaa ras aadi lene kii salaah di jaati he. aasaani se pachnewala khaanaa jaise chaaval kii kanji, sabudane kii kanji, jau kaa pani aadi dena chaiye. doodh, roti evam dableroti, maans, andey, makkhan, dahi evam tel main pakaaye gayi khaadya padaarth na den.
