 brahmos 2 rus kii enpio mashinostroyenia or bharat kii raksha anusandhan evam vikaas sanghathan dwaara vartmaan main sanyukt rup se vikasadhin ek hypersonic krooj missile he. jo ek saath brahmos airospes privet limited dwaara gathit he. yah brahmos krooj missile shrinkhalaa kii duusari krooj missile he. brahmos-2 kii range 290 kilometre or mek 7 kii gati hone kii sambhaavna he. udaan ke krooj charan ke dauraan missile ek scramjet airbrasting jet ingine kaa prayog karegi. missile kii utpaadan laagat or bhautik aayam sahit anya vivaran, abhi tak prakashit nahin kiye gaye hai. is missile kii 2020 tak pareekshan ke liye taiyaar hone kii ummid he.
 brahmos-2 kii yojanabaddh parichaalan seemaa 290 kilometre tak simit kar di gai he kyonki rus missile praudyogiki niyantran vyavastha kaa ek hastaksharkarta sadsy desh he, jo rus ko 300 kilometre ke upar simaaon vaali mixilon ko viksit karne main anya deshon kii madad se roktaa he. haalanki, ab bharat ek missile praudyogiki niyantran vyavastha hastaksharkarta sadsy desh ban gaya he. isaki sheersh gati vartmaan brahmos 1 kii doguni hogi. or yah duniya main sabase tej krooj missile hogi. brahmos 2 ko mek 5 kii gaati se paar karne ke liye rus ek vishesh or gupt eendhan sutara viksit kar rahaa he.
 oktober 2011 tak missile ke kai roopon kaa dizin puraa kiya gaya tha. or 2012 main pareekshan shuru hone vaale the. chauthi peedi ke bahuuddeshy roosi nausena vidhvansak ko bhi brahmos 2 se las karne kii sambhaavna he.
 brahmos airospes ne bharat ke purv raashtrapati i pi je abdul kalam ke sammaan main missile ko brahmos-2 kaa naam diya he.
