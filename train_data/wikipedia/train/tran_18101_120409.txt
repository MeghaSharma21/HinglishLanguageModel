 barbados rashtriya cricket team barbados cricket association dwaara yojit barbados kii rashtriya cricket team he. barbados westeindies cricket board kaa sadsy he, jo antarrashtriya cricket parishad ke apane adhikaar main sadsy he, or barbadian westeindies cricket team ke liye antarrashtriya str par khelte hai.
 barbados kisi bhi antarrashtriya pratiyogitaaon main bhaag nahin letaa he, balki caribbean main antar-kshetreey pratiyogitaaon main, jaise peshewar cricket lig . team frenchiji naam barbados pride ke tahat peshewar cricket lig main pratispardhaa karti he.
 sabase pramukh barbadian cricketron main jorge chalanger, joel garnor, gordon greenidge, wes haul, desmund heness, conrad hunt, mailkam marshall, gairi sobers, clide valkot, everton veeks or frank warel shaamil hai.
