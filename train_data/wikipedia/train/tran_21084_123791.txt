 suman sahay ek bhartiya saamaajik kaaryakarta tathaa jin abhiyaan ke sansthaapak hai. 
 sahay ne 1975 main bhartiya krishi anusandhan santhaan se piechdi praapt kii .] unhone kramshah alberta vishvavidhyalay, chicago vishvavidhyalay, or hidelburg vishvavidhyalay main kaam kiya, jahaan unhone maanav anuvanshiki main apana adhivaas praapt kiya.. vah gaiyr sarkari sanghathan jin abhiyaan ke nideshak hai. sahay ne 40 se adhik lekh prakashit kiye hai, adhiktar anuvanshik rup se sanshodhit jeevon se sambandhit nitigat muddon par he.
