 vaadya sangeet aisi sangeet rachana hoti he jisamen bol or gayan nahin hote hai. aise sangeet kii rachana purna rup se vaadya yantra par kii jaati he. aisi rachana sangeet rachayita swayan apane kisi pradarshan ke liye kar saktaa he. vaadya sangeet kii parampara pashchimi samskruti or bharat main bahut pracheen he. bharat main bahut pehle samay se veena ke upyog se vaadya sangeet kii rachana kii jaati thi.
