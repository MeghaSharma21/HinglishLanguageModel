 anuprayukt yantriki bhautik vigyaanon kii kii vah shakha he jisamen yantriki ke vyaavhaarik upyogon kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. anuprayukt yantriki bhautik vigyaan kii shakha yantriki kaa vyaavhaarik anuprayog he. anuprayukt yantriki me vishleshan mul rup se gatij evam sthatij vishleshan kaa samagam he. abhiyantrik yantriki kisi pind par bahari bal lagaaye jane par di jane vaali pratikriya kaa ganitiya samikarno ke madhyam se nirikshan karata he. anuprayukt yantriki nikaayon ke kuch udhaaharan taral ke pravaah, bahari bal ke aavedan se ek thos kaa fracture, yaa dhwani ke pratikriya swarup kaan kaa kampan shaamil hai. anuprayukt yantriki ke adhyankarta ko angreji main mechanician kaha jaataa he. jaisaa kii naam pradarshit karata he anuprayukt yantriki, yantriki vigyaan ke siddhanto kaa, praayogic takaniki kshetra me, ke bich kii khaayi ko bharta he. anuprayukt yantriki injeenring ke kayi saare shakhao me mulbhut rup se prayukt hota he. sar isek newton ko siddhanthik yantriki kaa pita mana jaataa he usee prakaar timoshanko ko anuprayukt yantriki kaa pita mana jaataa he.
