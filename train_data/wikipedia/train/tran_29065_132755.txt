 islam newzealand main ek apekshaakrit nayaa or chota dharmik samudaay he.
 2006 kii janaganana main 36,072 se adhik logon ne apane aapako is panth kaa anuyaayi bataaya.
 islam kaa newjiland main aagaman 1870 main muslim chini sone ke aagaman ke saath aarambh hua. bharat se muslim apravasi or purvi europe ke chote numberon ko jaldi 1900s se 1960 ke dashak tak based. bade paimaane par muslim apravas 1990 ke dashak main vibhinn yuddh se apravasiyon or sharnaarthiyon ke aagaman-deshon faad ke saath shuru hua.
 pehla muslim sanghathan ne newjiland muslim sangh, 1950 main panjikrut kiya gaya. pehla islamic centre oakland shahar main 1959 main sthapit kiya gaya tha. pehali masjid 1979 se 1980 ke upar bana hua tha.
 raashtra ko vyapak muslim samudaay sanghathan, sangh islamic sanghon newjiland ke, 1979 main panjikrut kiya gaya. pehali nirvaachit rashtriya adhyaksh Mazhar Krasniqi, ek albenien Kosova mul rup se kiya gaya. 2002 main newjiland sarkaar ek queens seva padak se sammanit kiya Krasniqi.
 1982 or 1999 Marhum shaikh khalid hafeez ke bich varisht dharmik netaa the. hafeez bharat main paidaa hua tha or saudi arab main shikshit.
