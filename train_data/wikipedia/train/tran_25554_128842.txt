 masechusets, sanyukt rajya america kaa ek rajya he.
 masachusets purvottar sanyukt rajya america ke new england kshetra main ek rajya he. yah pashchim main neuyork se uttar main road island or connectikat dakshin main, varmont or new hampshire seemaant he. masachusets bhoomi kshetra dwaara 7 waan sabase chota rajya he, lekin 14 veen sabase adhik aabaadi vaala or 3 sabase ghani 50 rajyon ke basaa. yah chah new england rajyon main se sabase adhik aabaadi vaala he or prati vyakti desh kii chhathi sabase oonchi sakal gharelu utpaada he. purv main greater boston kshetra or pashchim main springfield mahanagareey kshetra: rajya ke liye do alag-alag mahanagareey kshetra hai. lagbhag 'masachusets aabaadi kaa do tihaai vartmaan main greater boston main rahati he.
 masachusets america itihaas main ek aitihaasik, mahatvapurn saanskrithik or vanijyik bhoomika nibhaai he. plymouth tirthayatri, meflower ke yatriyon dwaara 1620 main sthapit kiya gaya new england main pehali colony, kaa sthal tha. 1636 main sthapit kiya gaya harvard vishvavidhyalay, sanyukt rajya america main ucch shiksha kii sabase puraani sanstha he.
 18 veen sadi main, boston andolan ke liye "liberty kii paalna" ke rup main janaa jane laga. shahar america kranti ke liye centre tha. jisaki vajah se sanyukt rajya america great britain se aazaadee mili.
 19 veen sadi main, basketbaal or wollyboul kaa olympic khel kramash: springfield or Holyoke ke pashchimi masachusets shaharon main avishkaar kiya gaya.
