 
 samudrafeni sepida jeevvaigyanik gana ke samudri praani hote hai. octopus, squid or nautilas ke saath samudrfeniyaan sheershapad jeevvaigyanik varg ke sadsy hai. samudrfeniyon kii yah visheshta he ki unka shankh unke sharir ke baahar hone ki bajaaye unke sharir ke andar hota he. airagonite se bana yah bhitari shankh khokhla hota he or samudrafeni ke sharir ko dhaancha pradaan karne ke saath-saath isamen gas bharkar samudrafeni sahajata se samudr main upar-neeche kii gahraiyon main jane main sakasham he.
 samudrfeniyon kii aankh kaa bhitari kaala bhaag W-aakaar kaa hota he. octopason kii tarah inaki bhi aath bhujaayen hoti hai or do lamben tentekal bhujaayen hoti hai jinase yah apane grass ko pakad sakate hai. inke mastishk or vyavhaar se yah anumaan lagaaya gaya he ki yah sabase budhimaan aksheruki praaniyon main se ek hai.
