 
 2016-17 sanfoil 3-de cap ke ek pratham shrenee cricket dakshin africa main ho rahe 6 oktober 2016 se pratiyogita 9 april 2017 tak he. pratiyogita teraa dakshin africa prantiy tiimon or namibia ke bich khelaa jaataa he. apane samakaksh, sanfoil shrinkhalaa ke vipareet, maichon ke bajaay lambaai chaar main se teen din hai. toornament 2016-17 siese prantiy ek din kii chunauti he, jo ek hi tiimon suvidhaayen saath samanaantar main khelaa ja rahaa he.
