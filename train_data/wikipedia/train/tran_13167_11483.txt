 vasim sajaad ek pakistani vakil evam kanoonee shikshak hai jinhone do asatat samayon july 1993 se navambar 1993 or 1996 se 1997 tak pakistan ke kaaryvahak raashtrapati kaa padbhaar sambhaala. wo disambar 1988 se oktober 1999 tak pakistan kii seenet ke adhyaksh bhi rahe.
 wasim sajaad, assi ke dashak main raajnneeti main aae or pehle seenet ke sadsy or phir unniis sau thaasi main adhyaksh bane or unniis sau taraanve main gulam ishak khan ke istife ke baad antarim rup se raashtrapati bane. unhone raashtrapati ke niyamit chunaav main bhi hissaa liya lekin faruk legaari ne unhen haraaya. vah unniis sau stanwe mohammad rafique tarad ke chayan se pehle ek baar phir antarim adhyaksh bane.vartmaan main wo pakistani muslim lig ke sadsy ke rup main pakistani seenet main vipaksh ke netaa hai.
