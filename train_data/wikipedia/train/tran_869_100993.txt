 microprocesar ek aisa digital electronic yukti he jisamen lakhon transistoron ko ekikrut paripath ke rup main prayog kar taiyaar kiya jaataa he. isse computer ke kendriya prakraman ikai kii tarah bhi kaam liya jaataa he. integrated circuit ke avishkaar se hi aage chalakar microprocesar ke nirmaan kaa rasta khulaa tha. microprocesar ke astitv main aane ke purv sipiu alag-alag electronic avyavon ko jodakar banaae jate the yaa phir laghustareey ekikaran vaale paripathon se. sabase pehla microprocesar 1970 main bana tha. tab iskaa prayog electronic pariklakon main binary coded decimal kii gananaa karne ke liye kiya gaya tha. baad main 4 va 8 bit microprocesar kaa upyog terminles, printer or automation divaais main kiya gaya tha.
