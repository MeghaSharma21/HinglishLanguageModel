 shri rameshwar gahira guru sanskrit mahavidhyalay, chhattisgarh ke jashpur jile ke samarbar main sthit ek sanskrit mahavidhyalay he. isaki sthapana 01 july 1956 ko rameshwar gahira dwaara kii gayi thi. yah mahavidhyalay 'sanathan sant samaaj gahira' dwaara sanchalit he. yah mahavidhyalay bagichaa tehsil m bagichaa se batauli-ambikapur maarg par bimada se 4 ki.me. uttar main gram samarbar main sthit he. yah mahavidhyalay san 1985 tak sampoornanand sanskrit vishvavidhyalay varanasi se sambaddh rahaa, iske pashchaat san 1985 se 2000 tak avibhajit madhya pradesh ke avadhesh pratap singh vishvavidhyalay reva se sambaddh rahaa. vartmaan main yah mahavidhyalay ko pan ravishankar shukla vishvavidhyalay raipur se sthai sambaddhta/ manyata praapt he.
 mahavidhyalay ko 1998 se niyamit anudaan praapt ho rahaa he. chhatisgarh kaa yah ekmaatr praachya sanskrit mahavidhyalay he jahaan gurukul parampara ke anusaar lagbhag 90 pratishat adivasee/vishesh pichhadi janajati /pichde varg ke chaatr/chatraayen nickshulk shiksha grahan kar rahe hai.
