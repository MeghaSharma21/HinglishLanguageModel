 horeshio nelson  britain kii nausena ke ek prasiddh sipahasaalar the jinhonen france ke samraat nepolian bonapart ke kilaaf hue yudhon main jeet paane ke liye bahut khyaati praapt kari. unhen ek preranaatmak faujee netaa ke rup main janaa jaataa tha or unki yuddh-paristhitiyon ko jaldi se bhaanpakar shatru ko chaunkaane vaali chaal chalane ke kaushal ke liye yaad kiya jaataa he. ve bahut dafaa ladiyon main ghayal hue, jisamen unhen ek baazuu or ek aankh bhi khoni padi.
 nelson kii bahut-si jeeton main se san 1805 ke trafalgar ke yuddh main baari jeet ke liye shrey miltaa he. isamen unhone british nausena ke 27 jahaazon vaale daste se francisi-spani mishrit nausena ke 33 jahaazon vaale bede se muqaabala kiya, jisamen britain kaa ek bhi jahaaz nahin duba jabaki dushman ke 22 jahaaz dhwast kiye gaye. isi muthbhed main unhen gahari chot lagi jis se unka dehanth ho gaya. is vijay kii smriti main landan ke trafalgar chowk main nelson kalam naam kaa ek stambh khadaa kiya gaya saath hi edinburgh ke kalton pahari par nelson manument naamak ek smarak bhi khadaa kiya he.
