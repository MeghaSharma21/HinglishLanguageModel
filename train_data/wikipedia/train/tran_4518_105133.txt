 gajiabad mahanagar kii meyar upachunaav main bhaajapa pratyaashee aashu verma ne jeet hasil kar lee he. unhone apane nikatatam pratidwandvi sapa ke sudhan ravat ko haraaya he, vahiin congress party tisare sthaan par rahi. bhaajapa pratyaashee aashu verma ne 45228 voton se jeet hasil kii he. bhaajapa ke aashu verma ko 115879 mat to sapa pratyaashee sudhan ravat ko 70651 vot mili hai, jabaki congress ummidavaar ko 23317 vot mili hai.
