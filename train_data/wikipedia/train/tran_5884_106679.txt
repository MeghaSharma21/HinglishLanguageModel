  rabindra kelkar  konkani sahitya ke sabase majboot stambh the. 85 varshiya is mahaan hasti ko varsh 2006 kaa gyanpith puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya. unki pramukh rachanaaon main aamchi bhaas konkanich, 'bahubhashik bharatant bhashane samajashaastra' shaamil hai. 
 rabindra kelkar kaa janm 7 march 1925 main dakshin goa ke kokulim kshetra main hua. konkani, hindi or marathi main unki 32 se adhik moulik pustaken prakashit ho chuki he. vah swatantraaa sangram or goa ke mukti sangram se jude rahe. vah aadhunik konkani andolan ke praneta the or konkani bhashaa mandal kii sthapana main unki ahm bhoomika rahi. kelkar ko gyanpith puraskaar ke alaava 1976 main sahitya academy puraskaar, 2008 main padmabhushan pradaan kiya gaya tha or 2007 main unhen sahitya academy kaa faillo chunaa gaya tha.
