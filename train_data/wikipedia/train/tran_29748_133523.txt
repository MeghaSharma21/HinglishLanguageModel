 srilanka cricket team november or disambar 2017 main bharat kaa daura karegi. mul kaarykram main farvari 2018 se shuru hone vaale teen test, paanch ek divasiy antarrashtriya or ek twanti-20 antarrashtriya match kaa daura kiya gaya tha.
 march 2017 main, srilanka cricket ne 2017-18 nidaas trophy ke liye, srilanka, bharat or bangladesh kii visheshta simit-over trikoniya shrinkhalaa toornament kii tarikhon kii ghoshnaa kii. yah march 2018 main yojit hone vaali he. eselsi ke adhyaksh thelanga sumathipala ne kaha ki is series main nirdhaarit simit-overon match ke kuch match ab nidaas trophy main khele jaaenge. is daure ke sanshodhit kaarykram main ab teen test, teen vanade or teen tii20i hai. test series se pehle, do divasiy daure kaa aayojan bharat board ke adhyaksh ilevan or srilanka ke bich khelaa jayega.
 agast 2017 main, bhartiya cricket control board ke kaaryakari sachiv amitabh choudhary ne bataaya ki test kolkata, nagpur or dilli main khelaa jayega. ekadivasiy maichon main dharmashala, mohali or vishakhapatnam honge or teen tii20i match katak, indore or mumbai main honge. iske alaava, choudhary ne kaha ki srilanka ke unnat aagaman kaa doosraa kaaran bharat or pakistan ke bich prastaavit shrinkhalaa ko radd karnaa tha. oktober 2017 main, viraat kohli ko bharat ke daure ke liye captain ghoshit kiya gaya tha, lekin varsh ke aakhir main dakshin africa ke bharat daure kii taiyaari main shrinkhalaa ke duusare chamahi ke liye vishram kii sambhaavna he.
