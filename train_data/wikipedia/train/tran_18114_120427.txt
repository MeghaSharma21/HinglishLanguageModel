 toofaan hayaan jise philippines main toofaan yolanda ke naam se janaa jaataa he, 195 mile prati ghante kii ucchtam hawa gati ke saath ab tak kaa sabase taqatvar ushnkaribandheey chakravaat mana ja rahaa he. 
 2013 main prashanth mahaasaagar main aae paanchave mahachakravaat hayaan kaa janm 2 navambar ko pashchimi prashanth mahaasaagar main sthit ponpe ke purv-dakshinpurv main ek kam dabaav ke kshetra ke rup main hua. aam taur par pashchim kii ore bad rahe is updrav kaa vikaas tezi se hua or agle din ke savere tak yah ek ushnkaribandheey avdaab ban gaya. somvaar ko hayaan ke vietnam pahunchaa jise dekhte hue 6 lakh logon ko surakshit jagah visthapit kar diya gaya. china main hai alert jari kar diya gaya he or saikadon udaanen bhi radd kar di gai hai.
 toofaan ne philippines, vishesh kar samar dweep or lete main baari tabaahi machaai. philippines ke shahar tacloban main kam se kam 10,000 logon ke maare jane kii ashanka he.vietnam main hayaan se bhuskhalan kii ghatnaayen hui hai. haalanki kisi bhi tarah ke janmaal ke nuksaan kii khabaren nahin ai hai.
