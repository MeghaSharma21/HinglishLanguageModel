 connectikat purvottar sanyukt rajya ke new england kshetra main dakshini rajya he. connectikat kii seemaa purv main road island, masachusets uttar main, pashchim main neuyork or dakshin main long island sound se he. isaki rajdhani hartford he. rajya kaa naam connectikat nadi ke naam par rakhaa gaya he, jo ki ek pramukh america nadi he jo rajya ko lagbhag do bhaagon main baantati he. 
 connectikat sanyukt rajya main kshetra ke hisaab se teesraa sabase chota rajya he. aabaadi main 29waan or jansankhya ghanatv ke maamle main yah rajya chauthaa sthaan rakhata he. yah sanyukt rajya kii sangheey sarkaar ke vikaas main prabhaavshaalee tha. dakshini or pashchimi connectikat new york mahanagareey kshetra kaa hissaa he; jise tri state aria ke rup main vyapak rup se janaa jaataa he. 
 connectikat main pehle basane vaale europea log dach the. prarambh main connectikat kaa aadha kshetra dach colony new netherland kaa hissaa tha. 1630 ke dashak main england dwaara pehali badi bastiyon kii sthapana kii gai. masachusets be kollani ne connectikat colony kii sthapana kii; yah colony un teraa coloniyon main se ek thi jisane america kranti main british shashan ke khilaaf vidroh kiya tha. 2010 kii janaganana ke anusaar sanyukt rajya america main, connectikat kii ucchtam prati vyakti aay he, maanav vikaas suchakank he or saath hi sarvaadhik ausat gharelu aay he.
