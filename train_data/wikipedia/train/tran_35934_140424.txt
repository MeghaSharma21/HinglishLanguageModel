 matochin jaldmarumadhya yaa matochin shaar, novaya zemlya ke severni or yuzani dwipon ke bich sthit ek jaldmarumadhya yaa jalasanyoji he jiskaa kshetrafal lagbhag 323 vargakimi he. yah berint sagar ko kaara sagar se jodta he. isaki lambaai lagbhag 100 kilometre he or iske sankirnatam bhaag main isaki chaudaai lagbhag 600 meter he. varsh ke adhikansh samay yah barf se dhakaa rahata he. iske kinaaron par machhlee pakadaney kii bastiyaan hai. 
 yahi wo sthal he, jahaan par 1963 se lekar 1990 tak, rus ne 39 bhumigat parmaanu pareekshan, surangon or kupakon kii ek vishaal sarani main kiye gayi the. 2000 ke baad se, rus ne is pareekshan sthal ko pun: sakriy karne ke liye in puraani surangon ke vistaar ke liye nirmaan kaary shuru kiya he or kai naye pareekshan kiye hai.
