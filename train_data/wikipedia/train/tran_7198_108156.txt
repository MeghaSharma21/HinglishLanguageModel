 shaakib khan bangladeshi abhinayta hai. unka mul naam masood rana he. khan ne sohanur rahamaan sohan kii annath bhaalobasha se filmon main padaarpan kiya lekin isse unhen paryaapt khyaati nahin mili. kuch varshon baad khan ne apane aap ko bangladesh film itihaas ke sabase safal or sabase adhik bhugtan praapt karne vaale abhinaytaon main se ek ke rup main sthapit kiya. unhone bangladeshi cinema main apane abhinay se anek uplabdhiyaan praap kii. unhen film bhalobasalei ghar badha jaay naa or khodaar pare maa meril pratham puraskaar ke saat namakan mili jisamen se paanch jite tathaa do rashtriya film puraskaar jite.
 shaakib khan dhaka, bangladesh main ek muslim parivaar main 28 march 1979 ko hua tha. unke pita kaa kaam aksar apni schuli shiksha dinon ke dauraan ek se duusare shahar se sthanantarit karne ke liye parivaar kii aavashyaktaa he. apane bachapan main vah ek doctor yaa ejeeniyar banane ke liye sapana dekha. unhone kaha ki ek ujjwal chaatr tha or madhyamik skool certificate or hair secondari skool certificate kii pariksha main shandaar parinaam tha.
 1999 main shaakib khan filmon shaashvat prem aayaa. vartmaan main vah film kalakaar sangh ke adhyaksh hai or bhi vibhinn saamaajik saanskrithik gatividhiyon ke saath kaam karnaa. unhone yah bhi praan power energy drink ke brands ambesder he. or asian dwaidh toun.
 shaakib khan kii film kalakaar sangh ke adhyaksh or bhi vibhinn saamaajik gatividhiyon ke saath kaam karnaa. 2013 main vah apane hi production house esake films kaa shubharambh. 2011 ke baad se vah bangladesh filmi kalakaar association ke adhyaksh hai. iske alaava unki film karier se, khan laal chikan namit dhaka main ek restaraan shrinkhalaa kaa maalik he.
 and 1 march 2013
