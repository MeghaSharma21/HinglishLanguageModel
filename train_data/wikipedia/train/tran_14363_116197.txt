 bolivia main garbhpaat vartmaan main avaidh he lekin yadi mahila ke swasthya ke liye hanikaarak ho athwa yadi balaatkaar kaa maamla ho to garbhpaat kiya ja saktaa he. yah niti 1973 se lagu he.
 us mahila ke liye jo garbhpaat kii anumati detii he sajaa ek se teen varsh karawas he or garbhpaat karne vaale ke liye ek se chah varsh kii hawalat kii sajaa ho sakati he. yadi koi mahila swayan kaa garbhpaat karti he to use sahmati ke liye milane vaali sajaa ke samaan hi sajaa milti he.
 bahraal, yah vastav main ek garbhvatee mahila ke liye chunauti he ki wo kanoonee or surakshit garbhpaat karvaa sake, chaahe unka balaatkaar hua ho.
