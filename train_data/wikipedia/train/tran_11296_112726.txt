 murshidaabad bharat ke pashchim bengal kaa ek pramukh shahar evam loksabhaa kshetra he.
 british kaal ke dauraan murshidaabad bengal presidensi main ek jila tha. mughal kaal main yah shahar bengal kii rajdhani rahaa. yah shahar bhagirathi-hughali nadi ke tat par basaa he. 
 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaara, murshidaabad kii jansankha 36,894 he. jis main purush 51% or stri 49% hai. is kii aabaadi main 70% muslim samudaay ke hai. murshidaabad kii aksharasyata 66% he, jis main purush 71%, or stri 61% hai. murshidaabad kii jansankhya main 12% bhaag 6 saal se kam aayu ke bachche hai.
