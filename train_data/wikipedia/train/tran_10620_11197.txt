 narhad gaanv jhunjhunu jile main sthit heverhar main pir baba kii dargah he,narhar par muslimon va nehra jato kaa raaj rah chukaa he,
 ab is gaanv main ranva va dhayal do jat gotro kaa aadhiky he,yah jat bahulya gaanv he. narhad kasbe main sthit pavitra hajib shakeerbar shah kii dargah kaumi ekta kii jivant misaal he. is dargah kii sabase badi visheshta he ki yahaan sabhi dharmon ke logon ko apni-apni dharmik paddati se puja archana karne kaa adhikaar he. kaumi ekta ke pratik ke rup main hi yahaan pracheen kaal se shrikrishna janmashtami ke avasar par vishaal mela lagata he. jisamen desh ke vibhinn hisson se hinduon ke saath musalmaan bhi puri shraddha se shaamil hote hai.
