 stotraratnavali vibhinn devi-devataaon ke stotron kaa ek sangrah he. isamen ganesh, shiv, shakti, vishnu, ram, krishna evam surya aadi pramukh devataaon ke prasiddh stotron kaa sangrah kiya gaya he. ant main prakeern stotron main devataaon ke praatah samaran tathaa kuch gyaanaprad aadhyatmik stotra bhi diye gayi hai.
 yah granth kaaphi pracheen he tathaa iske mul sankalankarta kaa naam gyaat nahin he. yah vibhinn prakashnon se prakashit hota he jinamein gitapras, gorakhpur bhi shaamil he. gitapras, gorakhpur dwaara prakashit stotraratnavali kaa naveenatam sanskaran saatvaan he jo sanwat 2065 main prakashit hua. iskaa icesbiton kramaak 81-293-1355-3 he.
