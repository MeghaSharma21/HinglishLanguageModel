 poonch nagar poonch jile main sthit ek nagar parishad he, jo ki bhartiya rajya jammu or kashmir main sthit he. is nagar kaa ullekh mahabharat main miltaa he tathaa chini yatri huentsang ne bhi is nagar kaa ullekh kiya he.
 poonch nagar 33°46′N 74°06′E / 33.77°N 74.1°E / 33.77; 74.1 nirdeshaank par sthit he. is nagar kii samudr tal se ausat oonchaai 981 meter he. pir punjaal pahariyaan poonch ghati se lekar kashmir ghati tak faili hai. is nagar main chaar tahseelein hai: haveli, mendhar, surankot or mandi.
 is nagar kii jalwaayu atyant thandi he. garmiyaan choti va sukhadaayi hoti he. garmi kaa taapmaana saamanytah 31 degree celsius se adhik nahin hota. pashchimi vikshobh ke kaaran thandi bahut adhik hoti he. disambar ke maheene main barfabaari hoti he. ausat vaarshik varsha 1087 millimetre hoti he.
 2011 kii jansankhya ke anusaar poonch kii aabaadi 40,987 he. purushon kii 55% tathaa mahilaon kii 45% he. yahan kii sakshartaa dar 79% he.
 poonch kai aitihaasik ghatnaon kaa gavaah rahaa he. jab sikandar mahaan ne 329 isa purv main raja poras par akraman kiya tha, tab yah kshetra dravbheeshan naam se janaa jaataa tha. chhathi shatabdi main prasiddh chini yatri huentsang bhi is kshetra se gujara tha. 850 isvi ke aasapaas is kshetra main raja nar ne raaj kiya tha jo ki multah ghoda vyapaaree tha. mahmud gaznavi ne poonch par 1020 isvi main akraman kiya tha. rajtarangini ke anusaar, poonch ke raja trilochan paal se mahmud gaznavi kii bheeshan ladaai hui thi.
 1596 main mogul samraat jahangir ne sirajuddin ko yahan kaa raja banaaya. 1819 isvi se 1850 isvi tak poonch khalsa darbar ke antargat rahaa, tab yahan sikhon ne raaj kiya. aazaadee ke samay yah hissaa raja hari singh ke kabje main tha.
