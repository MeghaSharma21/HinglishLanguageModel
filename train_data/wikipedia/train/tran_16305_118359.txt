 lusaane kii sandhi switzraland ke lusaane nagar main 24 july 1923 ko kiya gaya ek shaanti samjhauta tha. iske parinamswaroop turki, british samrajya, french ganraajya, italy rajtantr, japan samrajya, greece rajtantr, romania rajtantr tathaa sarv-croat-slovin rajya ke bich pratham vishwayuddh ke armbh ke samay se chalaa aa rahaa yuddh aupchaarik rup se samaapt ho gaya. yah sevres kii sandhi ke tut jane ke baad shaanti kii disha main kiya gaya doosraa prayaas tha.
 sevres kii sandhi kii sharten turki ke sultan dwaara sweekaar kii gayi lekin mustafa kamaal pasha dwaara chalaari ja rahi ek samanaantar sarkaar dwaara sweekrut nahin huin. ve sevanivrutt hokar ankara chale gaye or ek pratipakshi sarkaar banaari tathaa ek vishaal sena kaa sanghathan bhi kiya. unaniyon dwaara mustafa kamaal ko haraane ke lagaataar prayaas asafal hue or badi sankhya main unani maare gayi. bache-khuche unani asia miner se nishkasit kar diye gayi. sevres kii sandhi ko lagu karanevaala vahaan kori nahin tha. francisi va italvi sainik vahaan se waapas bula liye gaye the. choti si birtish sena apane padaavon par rah gari thi or is par akraman karne ke bajaay mustafa kamaal ne sandhivartaayen kin jisase lusaane kii sandhi huri.
 sandhi ne turki ganraajya kii swatantraaa ke liye avasar pradaan kiya. iske alaava turki main jaateey unani alpsankhyak varg kii suraksha va khaskar yunaan main jaateey turki musalmaan alpsankhyak varg kii suraksha ke liye bhi avasar pradaan kiya. turki kii unani jansankhya kii ek badi taadaad kii adla-badali yunaan kii turki jansankhya ke saath huri. sandhi ne yunaan, bulgaria, va turki kii seemayen parisimit kar deen, cyprus, iraq va syria par sabhi turki daavon ko aupchaarik rup se maan liya. sandhi ne nae turki ganraajya ko mrit otoman samrajya ke uttaradhikari rajya ke rup main antarrashtriya manyata bhi dilaari.
