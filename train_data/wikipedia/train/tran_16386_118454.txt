 abhyuday narendra kohli dwaara rachit upanyaas he.
 abhyuday kaa arth 'sansaarik soukhya tathaa samruddhi kii prapti'. maharishi kanad ne dharm kii paribhaasha main abhyuday kii siddhi ko bhi pariganit kiya he . bhartiya dharm kii udaar bhaavana ke anusaar dharm keval moksh kii siddhi kaa hi upaay nahin, pratyut aihik sukh tathaa unnati kaa bhi saadhan he. isliye vaidic dharm main abhyuday kaal main shraadh kaa vidhaan vihit he. raghunandan bhattacharya ne abhyuday shraadh ko do prakaar kaa mana he : bhoot jo putrajanmadi ke samay hota he or bhavishyat jo vivaahadi ke avasar par hota he. saransh yah he ki vaidic dharm keval paralok kii hi shiksha nahin detaa, pratyut vah is lok ko bhi vyavhaar kii siddhi ke liye kisi bhi tarah upekshniya nahin maanata.
