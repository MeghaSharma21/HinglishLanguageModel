 kavy se ras kis prakaar utpann hota he, yah kavyashastra kaa shaashvat prashn rahaa he. sanskrit kavyashastra ke adyacharya bharat muni ne apane vikhyath rasasutra main ras nishpatti par vichaar karte hue likhaa- 
 bhatta lollat ne nishpatti kaa arth utpattivaad se liya he tathaa sanyog shabd ke teen arth nikaale hai. sthaayee bhav ke saath utpaada- utpaadak bhav sambandh, anubhav ke saath gamy-gamak bhav sambandh, tathaa sanchari bhaavon ke saath poshya-poshak sambandh. 
 acharya shankuk ne nishpatti kaa arth anumiti se liya he tathaa sanyog kaa arth liya he anumapya-anumaapak bhav sambandh. 
 bhatta nayak ne nishpatti se bhukti kaa arth grahan kiya he tathaa sanyog kaa bhav ke liye bhojya-bhojak sambandh mana he. 
 acharya abhinavgupt ne nishpatti kaa arth abhivyaktvaad se lekar sanyog kaa arth vyangy-vyanjak bhav sambandh ke rup main liya he.
