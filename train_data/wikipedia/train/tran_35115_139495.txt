 geneva sammelnon main chaar sandhiyaan or teen atirikt protocol shaamil hai jo yuddh ke maanviya upachaar ke liye antarrashtriya kanoon ke maanakon ko sthapit karte hai. ekvachan shabd geneva sammelan, dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke baad baatcheet ke jariye 1949 ke samjhauton ko darshata he, jisamen pehali teen sandhiyon kii sharton ko adhyatan kiya gaya or chauthi sandhi jodi gayi. chauthe geneva sammelan ke anuchhedon main bade paimaane par kaidiyon ke yudhkalin buniyadi adhikaron ko paribhaashit kiya gaya; yuddh kshetra main or aasapaas ke kshetra main nagarikon or ghayalon ke liye suraksha sthapit kiye jane kii vyavastha di. 1949 kii sandhiyon kaa 194 deshon ne purnatah yaa apwaadon ke saath anumodan kiya.
