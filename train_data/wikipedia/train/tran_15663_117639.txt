 sura ad-dhuha quran kaa 93waan sur he. isamen 11 aayatein hai.
 kuch gyaataaon main matbhed he, phir bhi yah sura mohammad ko prakat kiya hua dwitiya sura mana jaataa he. pratham sura al-alak ke praapt hone ke uparaant ek shant antaraal tha, jisamen koi vaartalap nahin hua. isse naye bane paigambar ko andeh hua ki kahin unhone ishwar ko naaraaz to nahin kar diya, ki ve itani der se koi bhi sandesh nahin de rahe hai. is sura ne us shanti ko bhang karte hue, mohammad ko vishwaas dilaayaa ki samay ke saath - saath sab kuch samaj main aata jaayegaa. praatah kaal kaa chitra is sura kaa pratham shabd he or ise mohammad ke ishwar ke paigambar hone ke "pratham diwas" ko chihnit karata he. saath hi saath jivan ke naye dhang kii shuruyaat kaa sanket karata he, jo ki islam banegaa. is sura ke baad, mohammad kii mrityu paryant jibraeel kaa prakaton, quran ke shabdon ke saath, niyamit rup se hota rahaa. 
 vishay vastu, lambaai, style evam quran main apane niyojan ke kaaran; yah sura praayah sura al-inshirah ke saath yugal main aata he. inako samakaleen prakatit mana jaataa he.
