 bharhut bharat ke madhya pradesh rajya main satna jile main sthit ek sthal he. yah sthaan buddha stupa or kalakritiyon ke liye prasiddh he. yahan kaa stupa sambhavtah ashok dwaara teesri shatabdi isapurv nirmit kiya gaya tha. shri kaningham ne sarvapratham 1873 i. main is sthal kaa pataa lagaaya tha.
  bharhut murtikaka main sadaiv harit stariye chattan ka prayog kiya jata hai.
