 mitrapaksh shaktiyaan yaa elide shaktiyaan un deshon kaa gut tha jinhonen dwitiya vishwayuddh main britain, sanyukt rajya america, soviet sangh or china kaa saath diya or aksh shaktiyon ke kilaaf lade. bhinn mitrapaksh desh dwitiya vishwayuddh kii muthbhed main yaa to isliye shaamil hue kyonki unpar aksh desh yaa deshon ne akraman kar diya, yaa unhen apane upar akraman hone kaa dar tha, yaa phir unhen chintaa thi ki aksh shaktiyaan agar jeet gayi to puri duniya par haaavee ho jaayengi.
 1 sitambar 1939 main yuddh kii shuraat main frans, poland or united kingdom hi mitrapaksh main the. jald hi britain ke kuch adheen desh - australia, canada, new zealand or dakshin africa bhi is gut main sammilit ho gaye. 1941 ke baad mitrapaksh kaa netritv britain]], america or soviet sangh ne milakar kiya. bharat, belgium, yunaan, mexico, checkoslovakia, norge, nederlandz, ithiopia or brazil main mitrapaksh main the. 1945 main jaakar mitrapaksh shaktiyon kii jeet hone par aksh shaktiyon kaa gut khatm ho gaya.
