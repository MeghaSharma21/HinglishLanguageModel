 idgah masjid pashchimi janavaadee ganatantr china ke shinjiyang praant ke kashgar shahar main sthit ek masjid he. yah china kii sabase badi masjid he, jisamen 10,000 se 20,000 namaazi samaa sakate hai. ramadan ke dauraan yahan kabhi-kabhi 1 lakh logon tak kaa jamaavada ho jaataa he. iskaa nirmaan san 1442 main shaqasiz mirza ne karavaaya, jo us samay kashgar ke shaasha the. yahan pehle se san 996 se bane hue kuch hisse the jinhen masjid main shaamil kar liya gaya.
 idgah masjid 16,800 varg meter ke kshetrafal par faili hui he. masjid ke mukh or anya bhaagon main pile rang kii tilein lagi hui hai or samay-samay par imaarat kii marammat kii ja chuki he. masjid kaa adhyayan karne vaale visheshgyon ke anusaar is masjid kii style madhya asia or madhya purv se zyaada milti he or china kii anya masjidon se kam. saamne ke aangan main ped lage hue hai or vahaan pani se bharaa ek hauz he jahaan anuyaayi masjid ke mukhya bhaag main daakhil hone se purv vuzu karte hai .
