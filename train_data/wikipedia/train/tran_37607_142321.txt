 cartosat 2bi surya tulyakaali kaksha main bharat dwaara chhoda gaya ek prithvi avlokan upgrah he or yah cartosat upgrahon kii shrinkhalaa kaa chauthaa upgrah he. bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan dwaara bhartiya remote sencing upgrah shrinkhalaa ke antargat nirmit 17waan upgrah he. is upgrah ko 116 kilo algerian upgrah, canada or switzraland se ek-ek naino upgrah or ek stusat pico satellite ke saath 12 july 2010 ko sriharikota main satish dhawan antariksh centre ke pratham laanch pad se dhruviya upgrah launch whical rocket dwaara launch kiya gaya tha.
 upgrah vidyutchumbakeey spectrum ke drishy kshetra main kaale or safed chitra lene main sakasham ek panecromatic camera lekar gaya he. atyadhik chanchal cartosat-2bi ko 26 degree tak ghumaya ja saktaa he.
 yah satish dhawan antariksh centre ke pratham pad se 12 july 2010 ko 03:52 yu.tii.si par launch kiya gaya tha. ise anya videshi upgraho ke saath launch kiya gaya tha.
 launch ke baad upgrah ne apni pehali tasviir uttar pradesh ke elahabaad kii bheji.
