 uchakuduk madhya asia ke uzbekistan desh ke navoi praant kaa ek shahar he. bhugolic nirdeshaankon ke hisaab se yah nagar 42°9'24 uttar or 63°33'20 purv main sthit he. san 2007 main isaki aabaadi 27,000 se thodi zyaada anumaanit kii gai thi. yah shahar kizil kum registan ke bich main padataa he. soviet sangh ke jamaane main yah ek 'band shahar' tha kyonki yahan se soviet sangh ke parmaanu hathiyaaron ke liye urenium kii khaane chalaai jaati thi. uzbek bhashaa main 'uchakuduk' shabd kaa arth 'teen kuen' hota he.
 uchakuduk shahar se sambandhit ek ghatana 10 july 1985 ko ghati jab yahan eroflot udaan sankhya 7425 kaa vimaan durghatnagrast hua. is haadase main 200 logon kii jane gai. uchakuduk yahan se ubhare 'yalla' naam ke ek sangeet gut ke liye bhi mashhoor he jo 1970 or 1980 ke dashakon main puure soviet sangh or kuch anya purv europea deshon main prasiddh hua. inkaa sabase mashhoor gaanaa 'uchakuduk - tri colodatsa' tha.
