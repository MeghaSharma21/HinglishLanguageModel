 saumyajit ghosh ek bhartiya silligudi ,pashchim bengal ke teble tennis kii khilaadi he. ye sabase chote khilaadi the jinhone 2012 greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main qwalifai kiya tha jo ki landan main yojit kiya gaya tha.
 ghosh kaa parivaar ek madhyam shrenee kaa he jo pashchim bengal ke silligudi main rahete hai.inke pita hari shankar ghosh jo ki sthaneey mahanagar palika main karyrat he or mata gruhani he.
