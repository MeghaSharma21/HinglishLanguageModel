 bombai 1995 kii tamil romani natya film he. iskaa nirdesan maniratnam ne kiya he or mukhya kirdaar aravind swami or manisha koirala ne nibhaae he. sangeet diya he e aar rahamaan ne or hindi geet mehboob dwaara likhe gaye hai. yah film bharat ke disambar 1992 se janvari 1993 kii avadhi ke dauraan hui ghatnaon par kendrit he. vishesh rup se ayodhya ke babari masjid ko lekar vivaad, phir uskaa todna, mumbai main sampradayik tanaav hona or antat bombai ke dange hona. 
 shekhar mishra narayan or shaila bano pyaar main hai or shaadi karnaa chaahate hai. ekmaatr samasya yah he ki shekhar hindu he or shaila muslim he. yah vivaah unke sambandhit pariwaron ke liye ek samasya he. natijatan, yuva joda bombai shahar ko saath bhaag jaataa he. phir 6 disambar, 1992 ko ayodhya main babari masjid ko tod diya gaya - jiske parinamswaroop har jagah dharmik dange hone lage - jisamen bombai bhi shaamil tha. shekhar or shaila ab judawaan, camel bashir or kabir narayan ke mata-pita hai. dangon ke dauraan, shekhar ke pita or shaila ke mata-pita ghrinaa ko dafnane kaa faisla karte hai or apane bacchon se milane aate hai. jab ve apane vyaktigat matbhedon ko sulajhane kii prakriya main the tabhi aag lagati he or parivaar ko alag kar detii he. shaila ke mata-pita or shekhar ke pita tatkaal maare jate hai or kabir or camel kaa koi ata-pataa nahin hota.
 hindi gaanon kii suchi:
 sabhi geet mehboob dwaara likhit; sara sangeet e aar rahamaan dwaara rachit.
 yah film maniratnam kii roza or dil se ke saath ek shrinkhalaa ke rup main lee jaati he.
 film donon aalochanatmak or aarthik taur par safal rahi. isi naam se film ko hindi or telugu bhashaa main dab kiya gaya. e aar rahamaan dwaara diya gaya sangeet bahut hi saraahaa gaya or kai puraskaar unhone praapt kiye. maniratnam ke nirdesan kii bhi saraahna hui.
