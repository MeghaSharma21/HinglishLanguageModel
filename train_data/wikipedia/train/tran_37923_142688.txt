 haijong bhashaa
 hindu, dyaoism,
 kachaari, bodo, 
 haijong log uttar-purvi bharat or bengal ke janajaatiy log hote hai. ye meghalaya kii chauthi sabase bady jati hai.haijong log purvottar bharat ke pashchim bengal or bangladesh main faile hue hai. adhikansh haijong log bharat main hi based hue hai. vartmaan main inaki sankhya 1,50,000 bharat main or 50,000 bangladesh main he. haijong mukhya rup se chaaval kisaan hote hai. inaki bharat main sthiti ek anusuchit janajati kii he.
 
 haijong logon ke mukhya paanch vansh milte hai. inhen haijong bhashaa main nikani kehete hai. samaan vansh main vivaah varjit hote hai. vansh se vansh kii sanskriuti va reeti-rivaajon main thode thode antar evam bhinnata hoti hai. ye nikani is prakaar se hai:
 haijong hindu hi hote hai or hindu sanskaron evam reeti-rivaajon kaa paalan karte hai. inke hindookaran kaa samay gyaat nahin he. haijong logon main jeevwad me manyata hoti he. inke hindu-purv kaal ke rivaajon ke bare main kuch adhik gyaat nahin he.
