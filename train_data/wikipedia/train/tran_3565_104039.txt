 england main pakistan cricket team 2016
 pakistan kii rashtriya cricket team ke vartmaan main ek chaar test maichon kii shrinkhalaa, ek paanch maichon kii ek divasiy antarrashtriya kii shrinkhalaa ke liye 3 july 7 sitambar 2016 ko england or ireland kaa daura kar rahe hai or ek se ek twanti -20 international ne england ke khilaaf cricket team ke saath hoga. unhone yah bhi test siries main test shrinkhalaa ke dauraan wastershire ke khilaaf do divasiy match or do vanade maiches vanade siries se pehle ireland ke khilaaf karne se pehle summerset or sasex ke khilaaf do teen divasiy maiches khelenge. england ke ficchars ek ank aadhaarit scoring pranali he, jo may 2016 main srilanka ke khilaaf shrinkhalaa main pesh kiya gaya tha kaa hissaa banegi.
