 van mahotsav bharat sarkaar dwaara vriksharopan ko protsaahan dene ke liye prati varsh july ke pratham saptaah main yojit kiya jane vaala ek mahotsav he. yah 1960 ke dashak main paryaavaran sangrakshan or prakritik parivesh ke prati samvedansheeltaa ko abhivyakt karne vaala ek andolan tha. tatkaaleen krishi mantri kanhaiyalaal maniklal munshi ne iskaa sutarapaat kiya tha.
