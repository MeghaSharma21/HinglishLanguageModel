 vidyut chaap bhatthi main vidyut chaap dwaara utpann ooshmaa kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. chaap do electrodon ke bich utpann kii jaati he, athwa electrode evam dhan ke bich. 
 in bhatthiyon main pratirodh bhatthiyon kii apeksha adhik ooshmaa utpann ja sakati he. ye bhatthiyaan mukhyatya louhik dhaatuon, athwa unki mishradhaatuon ko pighalaane ke liye kaam main aati hai. inkaa sambharan kam voltata tathaa adhik dhara kaa hota he. at:, ise saamaanya sambharan se vishesh parinamitra dwaara praapt kiya jaataa he. electrode, saamanyat:, carbon ke hote hai, parantu bahut si bhatthiyon main upyukt dhaatu ke bhi bane hote hai, jo chaap utpann hone par dhire dhire swayan bhi upyukt ho jate hai. dhara pravaahit hone par chaap dwaara, electrode ke sire dhire-dhire kshat ho jate hai. is prakaar chaap kii lambaai bad jaati he or chaap bujh bhi ja sakati hai. at:, in bhatthiyon main electrodon ko dhire dhire aage badhaane kii vyavastha bhi karti he.
