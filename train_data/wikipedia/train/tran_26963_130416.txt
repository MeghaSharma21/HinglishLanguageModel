 seemaa chaowkee, seemaa post, seemaa avlokan post yaa biopi ek chaowkee he jo apni seemaa par ek samprabhu rajya dwaara banaai jaati he, aamtaur apane padosi desh se satee seemaa par nigaraanee rakhane ke liye inaki shrinkhalaa ko ek niyamit antaraal par rakhaa jaataa he. is tarah kii chaukiyon par seemaa rakshakon jawan tainaat rahete hai va gashti dal pratidin gasht lagate hai. ye chowkiyaan apane mukhyalayon ke sadaiv sampark main rahati hai taaki jisase jaankaree saajha kii ja sake va apatkaal ke dauraan madad bhi pahunchaai ja sake.
