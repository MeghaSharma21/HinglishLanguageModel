 avanti pracheen bharat ke 16 mahajanapadon main se ek tha. yah janapad pashchimi or madhya malwa ke kshetra main basaa hua tha jiske do bhaag the. uttari avanti jisaki rajdhani ujjayini thi tathaa dakshini avanti jisaki rajdhani mahishmati thi. in donon kshetro ke bich netravati nadi bahati thi. ujjayini kii pahchaan madhyapradesh ke vartmaan ujjain nagar se kii ja sakati hai. mahavir swami tathaa gautam buddha ke samakaleen yaha ke shaasha chandpradyot tha. iske pramukh nagar kurargarh, makkargarh ev sudarshanpur the.
