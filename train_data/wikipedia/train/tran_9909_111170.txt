 tarapur bihar ke munger jile kaa ek prakhand he. yah 15 farvari 1932 ko hue bheeshan narsanhaar ke liye prasiddh he.
 15 farvari 1932 ko dopaher saikadon aazaadee ke diwane munger jila ke tarapur thane par tirangaa lahraane nikal pade. un amar senaniyon ne haathon main rashtriya jhanda or hothon par 'vande maataram', 'bharat mata kii jai' ke naaron kii gunj liye hanste-hanste goliyaan khai thi. bhartiya swatantraaa sangram ke sabase bade golicond main deshbhakt pehle se lathi-goli khaane ko taiyaar ho kar ghar se nikle the. 50 se adhik sapooton kii shahadat ke baad sthaneey thana bhawan par tirangaa laharaaya.
 tarapur shahid diwas bharat main prayek varsh 15 farvari ko manaaya jaataa he jisamen 15 farvari 1932 ko bihar rajya ke munger ke tarapur golicond main shaheedon ko shranddhajali di jaati he. aazaadee ke baad se har saal 15 farvari ko tarapur diwas manaaya jaataa he. bharat maa ke vir beton ke upar angrej collector i oli evam espi dablyoo flag ke netritv main goliyaan daagi gai thim. goli chal rahi thi lekin koi bhaag nahin rahaa tha, log date hue the. is golicond ke baad congress ne prastaav paarit kar har saal desh main 15 farvari ko tarapur diwas manaane kaa nirnay liya tha. yah jaliyanvaala baag se bhi bada hatyakand tha.
