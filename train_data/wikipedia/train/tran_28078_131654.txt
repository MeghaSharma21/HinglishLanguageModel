 robin hud angreji lokkathaon kaa ek bahishkrut hero tha. vah ek achook tirandaaj va kushal talwarbaj tha jise aaj bhi uske apane saathi "meri mens" ke saath milakar amiron kii sampatti ko lut kar garibon main baant dene ke liye janaa jaataa he. paramparaagat rup se, robin hud or uske aadamiyon ko hare kapadey pahane hue chitrit kiya jaataa he.
 robin hud madhyakaal main ek lokapriy vyakti ke rup main prasiddh ho chukaa tha or aadhunik sahitya, filmon evam television main lokapriy bana rahaa. shuruaati sutron ke anusaar robin hud ek saamaanya aadmi tha jo peshey se ek kisaan tha par usko adhikansh samay ek aise amir ke rup main varnit kiya gaya jise ek beyimaan sheriff ne jaan boojhkar uski jameen se bedakhal karke samaaj se bahishkrut kar diya tha.
 lokapriy samskritiyon main robin hud or uske saathi, jinhen "meri main" kaha jaataa tha, nottinghamshair ke sherwood jangalon main rahete the jahaan shuruuaati kathaon kii sarvaadhik ghatnaayen ghati. is prakaar robin hud kii sabase pehali chaar panktiyon kii kavita 15vi shatabdi main darj kii gai jisaki shuruyaat main kaha gaya he kii: "robin hood in sherevud stod" . haalaanki baaki abtak sanjoyi gai shuruaati kavitaaon or puraane sroton se yah chitra ubhar kar aata he ki robin hud shaayad barnsdel kii ilaake se tha jo ab dakshin yorkshaire main sthit he.
 anya paramparaon main robin ke vaastavik ghar kii jagah yorkshaire ke bheetar or uske baahar, donon sthanon par bataai gai he jiske karan asamanjas kii sthiti utpann hoti he.
