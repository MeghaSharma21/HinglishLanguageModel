 parsaram maderna
 parsaram maderna rajasthan se bhartiya rashtriya congress ke netaa the. ve rajasthan ke jodhpur jile ke lakshmana nagar gram ke nivasee the. unke putr mahipal maderna bhi raajnneetijna hai.
 kisaan maseehaa va rajasthan kii raajnneeti ke louh purush kahe jane vaale shri parsaram maderna kaa janm jodhpur jile ke falaudi main srilakshmananagar main 23 july 1926 ko hua. unki praarambhik shiksha unke gaanv main hui or baad main lakhanu vishvavidhyalay se unhonen snakottar va el0el0bi0 kii padhai purna kii. 16 farvari 2014 ko lambi bimaari ke baad jaipur ke esemes aspataal main unka nidhan ho gaya.
 shiksha samaapt karne ke pashchaat rajasthan vaapasi par unhonen marwar kisaan sabha ke madhyam se baldev ram miradha ke saath kaary kiya or uske baad rajasthan kisaan sabha ke liye.
