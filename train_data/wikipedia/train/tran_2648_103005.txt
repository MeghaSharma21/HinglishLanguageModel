 guptasar gurudwara muktsar jila main chaitanya ke sameep sthit he. is jagah kii sthapana guru govind singh ne kii thi. guru govind singh ji is jagah par apane sainikon ko unki tankhwaah baanta karte the. ek din ek sainik ne paise lene se manaa kar diya or kaha ki vah keval unase aadhyaamik gyaan kii prapti karnaa chahta hai. sainik dwaara yah shabd kahe jane par guru ji ne kuch paise gupt sthaan main rakh die. iske baad se is jagah ko guptasar ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
