 tapadipt lamp yaa incandicent lamp ko bolchaal main balb kehete hai. yah tapadipti ke dwaara prakaash utpann karata he. garam hone ke kaaran prakaash kaa utsarjan, tapadipti kehalaata he. isamen ek patala philament hota he jisase hokar jab dhara bahati he tab yah garam hokar prakaash dene lagata he. philament ko kaanch ke balb ke andar isaliye rakhaa jaataa he taaki ati tapt philament tak vayumandaleey oxygen na pahunch paye or is tarah kriya karke philament ko kamjor na kar sake.
 uddipt deepak vibhinn aakaar, voltata or shakti ke banaaye jate hai. 1.5 volt se lekar 300 volt ke balb aasaani se uplabdh hai. isi tarah 1 wat se lekar hajaaron wat ke balb banate hai. uddept balbon kii visheshta he ki inke nirmaan kaa kharch bahut kam hota he; bina kisi atirikt taam-jhaam ke inhen sidhe uchit voltata se jodakar chalaaya jaataa he; ye asi or disi dono se kaam karte hai. in kaarnon se praaia: gharon main yahi lamp upyog kiya jaataa rahaa he. kintu isaki prakaashik dakshata kam he arthath samaan prakaash paidaa karne ke liye yah apekshaakrit adhik bijli letaa he. is kaaran dhire-dhire anya prakaash sroton kaa prachlabadh rahaa he or iskaa prachlan kam ho rahaa he.
