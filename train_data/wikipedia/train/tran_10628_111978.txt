 purv africa rift ek sakriya bhuvaigyanik rift he jo purv africa main bad rahi he. ise pehle great rift ghati kaa bhaag mana jaataa tha lekin bahut se bhuvaigyanik ise ab ek alag rift ghati maanate hai. yah africa plate ke tuutakar do bhaagon main bantane kii jari prakriya ke kaaran ban gai he.
 purv africa rift ghati ke kshetra main kai mrit va moorchhit jwalamukhi sthit hai, jinamein prasiddh kilimanjaro parvat bhi shaamil he. mahaan africa jhilein bhi isi rift ghati me sthit hai.
