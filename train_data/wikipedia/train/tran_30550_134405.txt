 sudhir tailang bhartiya kartoonist the.
 tailang kaa janm bhartiya rajya rajasthan ke bikaner main hua. tailang ne apana pehla cartoob 1970 main banaaya or 1982 main elstrated vikali of india,mumbai se apana karier armbh kiya. varsh 1983 main unhone dilli main navabharat times main kaam karnaa armbh kiya. unhone iske atirikt hindustan times, indian express or da times of india ke liye bhi cartoon banaaye. unhone apana antim kaary asian age ke liye kiya. shiksha evam sahitya ke kshetra main unhen 2004 kaa padam shri puraskaar mila.
 unhone haal hi main "no, prime ministar" sheershak se cartoon pustak bhi jari kii thi. is pustak main bharat ke purv pradhanmantri manmohan singh kuch cartoon shaamil hai. unka mastishk cancer se 6 farvari 2016 ko nidhan ho gaya.
