 chintamani ghosh bharat ke mahaan printer evam indian press, prayag ke swami the. unhone san 1884 main is press kii sthapana kii thi jisase hindi sahitya kii hajaaron pustaken print huin. chintamani ghosh ne pratham sarvshereshth masik 'saraswati' dwaara or hindi ke anek granthon ko chhapakar hindi sahitya kii jitani seva kii he, utani seva hindi bhashaa bhashi kisi prakaashak ne shaayad hi kii hogi.
 chintamani ghosh ne 1884 main indian press kii sthapana kii or 1899 main nagari prachaarini sabha se prastaav kiya ki sabha ek sachitr masik patrika ke sampadan kaa bhaar le jise ve prakashit karenge. nagari prachaarini sabha ne iskaa anumodan to kar diya kintu sampadan kaa bhaar lene main apni asamarthta jataai. ant main sampadan kaa bhaar ek samiti ko saunpane par sahmati bani. is samiti main paanch log the. ve the babu shyaam sundar das, babu radhakrishna das, babu karthik prasad, babu jagannatha das or kishorilaal goswami. or is tarah 'saraswati' kii yojana ko antim rup mila.
 janvari 1900 main iskaa prakaashan prarambh hua. praveshank ke mukhaprishth par paanch chitra the-sabase upar veenavadini saraswati kaa chitra tha. upar bain ore surdaas or dayee ore tulsidas tathaa neeche bain ore raja shiv prasad sitarehind or babu harishchandra ke chitra the. patrika ke naam ke neeche likhaa rahata tha-kaashi nagari prachaarini sabha ke anumodan se pratishthit. patrika ke prakaashak chintamani babu ne patrika kaa niti vaktavy is tarah ghoshit kiya tha-
