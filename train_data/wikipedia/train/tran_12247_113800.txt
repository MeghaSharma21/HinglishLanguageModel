 bristol scout ek sada, ekal seat, rotary ingine vaala biplen tha jo kii multah naagarik daud ke liye banaaya gaya tha va baad me jiskaa kii prayog halake ladaaku va tohi vimaan ke rup main hua. ise british or colonial aroplen company ke chitrankarta frunk barnwell ne banaaya tha. is vimaan ne pehali udaan 23 farvari 1914 ko bhari.
 san 1914 se 1916 tak aise kareeb 374 vimaan shahi udan corps, shahi nausena kii vaayu seva va australian udan corps ke liye banaae gaye. iskaa upyog in senya sanghathano dwaara pratham vishwayuddh main german samrajya ke khilaaf kiya gaya.
