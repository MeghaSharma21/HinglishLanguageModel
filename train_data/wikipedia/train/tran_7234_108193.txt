 dolotsav mukhyat: vaishnav sampradaay ke mandiron main manaaya janevaala pramukh utsav he jisamen shrikrishna kii murti hindole main rakhkar upavanadi main utsavarth le jate hai. yon to samast bharat main is utsav kaa prachlan he kintu vrundavan tathaa bengal main yah vishesh samaaroh se manaaya jaat he. bengal main ise 'dolayatra' kehete hai.
 aajkal yah utsav pratipada se yukt phalgun shukla poornima tathaa caitra shukla dwadashi ko manaaya jaataa he. jaisaa naam se hi spasht he, isamen dol yaa hindol kii pramukhataa he. shri gopalbhatta goswami virachit 'shri haribhakti vilaas' naamak nibandhgranth ke anusaar caitra shukla dwadashi ko vaishnavon ko aamantrit kar geet-vaadya-sankirthan-sahit vividh upachaaron se bhagwaan shri krishna yaa vishnu kaa pujan karke pranampurvak unhen dolarudh karake jhulaane kaa vidhaan he. is prakaar din vyateet hone par vaishnavon sahit raatri jaagran kare. caitra shukla tritiya tathaa uttaraphalguni se yukt phalguni poornima ko bhi yah utsav karnaa chahie.
 padmapurana ke pataal khand main bhi dolotsav yaa dolayatra kaa vishad varnan he:
 ukt puranic varnan se yah spasht he ki yah utsav ati pracheen kaal se praaia: samast bharat main prachlit tha. pehle yah utsav phalgun tathaa caitra maas main anek dinon tak hota tha. kalantar main yah sankshipt hota gaya. ab yah keval do dinon kaa rah gaya he- phalgun shukla poornima tathaa caitra shukla dwadashi. is utsav ko vasantotsav kaa ang mana ja saktaa he. padmapurana, garud puraana, dolayatratatv tathaa sriharibhaktivilas main is utsav ke vishad vivechan tathaa matamtantar uplabdh hai.
 thirumala main bhi dolotsav yaa 'dolseva' kii jaati he.
