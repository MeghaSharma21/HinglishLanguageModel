 sun yaat-sen china ke krantikari netaa tathaa chini ganatantr ke pratham raashtrapati evam janmdata the. peshey se ve chikitsak the. chini ganatantr main unhen 'rashtrapita' kaha jaataa he jabaki chini janavaadee ganatantr main unhen 'loktantric kranti kaa agradoot' kaha jaataa he. king rajvansh ko ukhaad phenkane main unki mahati bhoomika thi. 1912 main jab chini ganatantr bana to unhen asthaayee raashtrapati niyukt kiya gaya.
