 husainivaala bhartiya punjab ke ferozepur jile kaa ek gaanv he. yah pakistan kii seemaa ke nikat satluj nadi ke kinare sthit he. iske saamne nadi ke duusare kinare par pakistan kaa 'genda singh vaala' naamak gaanv he. isi gaanv main 23 march 1931 ko shahid bhagat singh, raajaguru or sukhdev kaa antim sanskaar kiya gaya tha. yaheen par unke saathi batukeshwar dutt kaa bhi 1965 main antim sanskaar kiya gaya tha. in shaheedon kii smriti main yahan rashtriya shahid smarak banaaya gaya he. bhagat singh kii maan vidyavati kaa antim sanskaar bhi yaheen kiya gaya tha.
