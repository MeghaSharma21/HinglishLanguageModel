 mahatma anand swami saraswati arya samaaj ke netaa evam 'arya gajet' tathaa 'milaap' aadi patron ke sampadak the. ve avibhajit punjab kii patrakarita ke pitamah tathaa sangharkshil sanyasi the. unka mul naam 'khushhalchand khursand' tha.
 mahatma anand swami saraswati kaa janm 15 oktober 1882 ko punjab ke jalalpur jatta gaanv main hua tha. aryasamaj se prabhavit hokar unhone yuvavastha main hi apana jivan vaidic dharm ke prachaar-prasar ke liye samarpit karne kaa sankalp liya. lahore main mahatma hansraj ji ke saannnidhya main unhone 'arya gajet' saptahik ke sampadakeey vibhaag main pravesh kar patrakarita kshetra main padaarpan kiya. rashtriya jaagran tathaa vaidic dharm ke prachaar ke uddeshy se unhone 13 april 1923 ko baisakhi parv par dainik urdu 'milaap' kaa prakaashan shuru kiya. us samay ve khushhalchand 'khursand' ke naam se jane jate the. 
 san 1921 main malabar main jab mopla musalamaanon ne hinduon par amanviya atyaachaar dhaae tab ve arya yuvakon ko saath lekar malabar pahunche tathaa atyaachaariyon ke viruddh chetana jagaai. san 1939 main hyderabad ke nizaam ne jab hinduon kaa utpeedan kiya to ve satyagrahi jattha lekar hyderabad pahunche tathaa saat maheene tak nizaam kii jail main rahakar sangharsh kiya. sindh main jab satyarth prakaash par pratibandh lagaaya gaya tab unhone sindh pahunchakar sangharsh kiya. san 1949 main ve sanyas grahan kar 'mahatma-anand swami saraswati' ban gaye. unhone kenya, uganda tathaa tanzania aadi kaa bhraman kar vaidic dharm kaa prachaar kiya.
 unhone puure sansar ko gayatri mantra kii anuthi shakti se parichit karaane main safalataa praapt kii. 24 oktober 1977 ko ve brahmalin ho gaye.
