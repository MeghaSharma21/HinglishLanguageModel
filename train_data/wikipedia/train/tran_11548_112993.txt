 ongi bhashaa-parivaar, jise dakshin andmani bhashaa-parivaar bhi kehete hai ek chota-sa bhashaa-parivaar he jisaki sirf do sadsy bhashaayen hai: ongi bhashaa or jarva bhashaa. yah donon andaman dweepasmooh ke dakshini hisse main boli jaati hai or bahut hi kam maatrubhaashiyon kii vajah se sankatagrast maani jaati hai. 1997 main ongi bhashaa ke keval 96 or jarva bhashaa ke keval 200 vaktaa jivit the. is bhashaa-parivaar kii jangil naamak ek teesri sadsy bhashaa bhi thi lekin vah 1895 or 1920 ke bich main vilupt ho gai thi.
