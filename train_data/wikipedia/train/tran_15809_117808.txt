 fredrick bastiyaat kaa janm 1801 main beyon main hua tha or 1850 main rome main unka nidhan hua tha. jab ve nau saal ke the, unke mata-pita kaa nidhan hua. tatpashchaat unke bhavy mata-pita ne unka paalan-posan kiya. unka parivaar ek chote se kasbe mugron se taalluk rakhata tha, jahaan unhone apni jindagi kaa adhikansh hissaa guzaara or jahaan par unki ek pratimaa bhi sthapit he. mugron, beyon ke uttar-purv main ek francisi hisse 'les lendes' main sthit he. unhone apni jindagi kaa baad kaa hissaa paris main 'les jarnal des ikanomistes' ke sampadak or 1848 main sansad sadsy ke taur par guzaara.
 ek arthshaastri ke taur par unhen suspasht dimag or virodhiyon ko ukhaad dene vaali vyangyatmak style hasil thi. unhone apane vakt main aarthik vigyaan ko upabhoktaaon, yani logon kii soch ke aadhaar par viksit karke ek nai disha di thi. vah len-dein or nijee pasand kii aazaadee ke mukhar or kabhi na thakne vaale jaanbaaj the, jo kaarobaar main kisi bhi tarah kii baadha yaa anudaan ke sakht khilaaf the. aaj 150 saal baad bhi unke dwaara kiye gaye kaam kii taazgi or auchity barkaraar he. sansthaaon or samaajon ke gathan ko lekar unki kai bhavishyavaaniyan khari sabit hui hai.
 ek darshanik ke taur par, ve aadhunik kaal ke kai udaarvaadiyon ke purvajon kii tarah the, unhone vyaktigat aazaadee or javabdehi par kai aadharbhoot nitiyaan taiyaar kar di thim.
 ek sthaneey jaj, ke taur par ve kushalta or samanta ke agradoot the.
 ek raajnneetijna ke taur par, ve sarkaar ke chote aakaar ke pakshdhar the or janata kii jeb se badhate anwarat kharchon ke khilaaf ladate rahete the. unhone upniveshwaad ke vistaar or gulaami pratha kii bhi mukhar aalochana kii thi. ve satta ke vikendrikaran, saansad rahete mantri banane or sansad main sarkari adhikariyon kii sankhya ko simit karne ke pakshdhar the. vah raajnneeti main mahilaon kii zyada bhagidaari ke bhi pakshdhar the.
