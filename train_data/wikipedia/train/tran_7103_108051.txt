 ramasahaaydaas hindi ke reetimukt kavi the. ye 'bhagat' naam se prasiddh the or isi naam se kavitaayen bhi likhte the. 
 ramasahaaydaas varanasi ke choubepur ke rahanevaale asthana kysth the. bhavanidas inke pita or chintamani inke guru the. kashinaresh maharaj uditnarayan singh hi inke ashrayadata the. inke janmkaal ke vishay main koi soochna nahin milti, kintu 'shivsingh saroj' main inkaa upasthitikaal sanwat 1901 vikrami diya gaya he. inkaa kavykaal praaia: san. 1861 vi. se 1881 vi. tak mana jaataa he. ye param bhakt or prakruti se bade vinamr the.
 inaki rachanaaon ke vishay main 'shivsingh saroj' se pingalagranth 'vruttatarangini', 'mishrabandhu-vinod' se 'ramasatasai' or ramanaresh treepathi krit 'kavita kaumudi' se 'shringarasatsai', 'vruttatarangini', 'kakehara' 'ramasaptsati' evam 'vanibhushan' kii rachana milti he. 'ramasatasai', 'ramasaptsati' or 'shringarasatsai' teenon ek hi rachana ke teen naam gyaat hote hai. naam ke aadhaar par anumaan he ki 'vanibhushan', alankaranirupak granth rahaa hoga, jo anuplabdh he. ramchandra shukla ne 'kakehara' ko kavi kii antim kriti mana he. yah jaaysi ke akharavat kii paddati par rachi gai dharm-niti pradhan rachana he. pingalanirupak kriti 'vruttatarangini' kii prati nagariprachaarini sabha, varanasi main surakshit he.
 kavi kii prakhyati kaa kaaran uski mahatvapurn rachana 'ramasatasai' hi he. isaki anuthi bhavvyanjana or apurv bhashakaushal ke naate ramasahaaydaas kaa naam hindi ke reetimukt kavyakaron ke saath aadar se liya jaataa he.
