 chadiumpusha krishna pilla- malayalam ke ek suprasiddh kavi the. ve malayalam svachhntavaad kavyamandal ke sarvaadhik lokapriy kavi the. unki kavyakala kii sabase badi visheshta sangitatmakataa he.
 krishna pilla kaa janm madhya kerala main itappalli naamak sthaan main hua tha. 20 varsh kii avasthaa ke purv hi ve samast kerala main lokapriy ho gaye. unki kavita kaa pramukh vishay prem ke katu anubhav se utpann bhavuktapurna sangharsh he. unhone dambh evam vishwasghaat ke viruddh abhiyaan kiya jo paryaapt maatra main faile hue the. unke geetkavy or badi badi kavitaayen 30 se adhik sankalnon main prakashit hui hai.
 'vaashpanjali', 'mohini', 'sankalp kanti', 'patunn pishachu', 'hemantchandrika', 'tilottama', 'manaswini' unke kuch sankalnon main se hai. unki atyadhik sarvapriya pustak 'ramanan' he jo shokpradhan abhinav graamy kavy he. yah ek saathi kavi itappalli raghavan pilla ke du:khant prem ke sambandh main likhaa gaya he jabaki usne tarun avasthaa main hi aatmahatya kar lee thi. 'rakt pushpandall' unki pragatisheel evam krantikari kavitaaon kaa sankalan he. krishnapilla europea bhashaaon evam hindi ke romantic kaavyon se adhik prabhavit hue. unka drushtikon yaa adarsh avichal nahin tha. unhen jivan kii vishmataaon kaa nishchit bodh nahin tha. apane manobhaavon kii abhivyakti ke upar kathor bauddhik niyantran kaa abhaav evam svachhand rachanaaon ne unki kavitaaon ko shaktiheen evam feekaa bana diya he. kintu nishkapatata, manohar sangeetamyata, komalkant padaavalee evam abhivyaktiyon kii sugamawa se padhanevaali saamaanya janata ke madhya unhen priy bana diya.
