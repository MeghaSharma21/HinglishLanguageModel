 shri chakra ek yantra he jiskaa prayog shri vidya main hota he. ise 'shri yantra', 'nav chakra' or 'mahameru' bhi kehete hai. yah sabhi yantro main shiromani he or ise 'yantraraj' kaha jaataa he. vastutah yah ek ek jatil jyamitiya akruti he. is yantra kii adhishthatri devi bhagwati tripur sundari hai. shri yantra kii sthapana or puja se lakshmi kii prapti hoti he. navaratri, dhanteras ke din shriyantra kaa pujan karne se lakshmi ji prasann hoti hai.
 shri yantra ke kendra main ek bindu he. is bindu ke chaaron ore 9 antargrathit tribhuj hai jo navshakti ke pratik hai. in nau tribhujon ke antavgrathit hone se kul 43 laghu tribhuj banate hai.
