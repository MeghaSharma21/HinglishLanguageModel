 shikari 2000 kii en chandra dwaara nirdeshit hindi bhashaa kii thriller film he. isamen govinda, karishma kapur, tabu, kiran kumaar, johny liver, sushamaa seth, shweta menon or nirmal pandey mukhya kalakaar hai. govinda ne nakaaratmak bhoomika nibhaai.
 virendra dakshin africa ke kep toun main ek vyapaarik tikun he. uski bahan rajeshwari, patni suman or uski maa uske ekmaatr rishtedaar hai. virendra kaa vaivaahik jivan vivaad main he, kyonki suman ke saath kabhi bhi koi sambandh nahin rahaa he. ek din, virendra mahendra pratap singh ke rup main vyapaarik duniya main uskaa pratidwandvi aa jaataa he. mahendra ek bhartiya masala tikun he jo dakshin africa main apane vyapaar kaa vistaar karnaa chahta he.
 virendra shuruyaat main mahendra kii vistaar yojanaon se satark rahata he, lekin baad main uske pravesh ko wo apane vyavsaay kaa vistaar karne ke liye ek upkaran ke rup main dekhtaa he. virendra se anjaan, mahendra vastav main om shrivastav naamak ek chipa hua vyakti hai. tikun banane main om kaa bada agenda he. mahendra or virendra ne ek sauda kiya, jis par virendra ko jashn manaane ke liye jangal main ek ghar main aamantrit kiya jaataa he. 
 sabhi geet aadesh shrivastav dwaara sangitabaddh.
