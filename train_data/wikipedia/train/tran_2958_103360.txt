 himbhanjak yaa barfbhanjak aise samudri jahaaz yaa nauka ko kehete hai jo barngrast pani main yatayaat karne kii kshamta rakhata ho. kisi jahaaz ko himbhanjak samajha jane ke liye usamen teen gun zaruri hai: uskaa dhaancha aam jalayanon se majboot hona chahie, uskaa aakaar aage se barf hataane ke liye anukool hona chahie or usamen barf se dhake pani main jor se barf dhakelkar aage nikalne kii kshamta hoti chahie.
 barngrast pani se guzarane ke liye himbhanjak gati se sakht-jami barf par apane sharir se prahaar karata he. is se barf tut jaati he lekin nauka ke aage barf ke tukdon kaa jamaavada jahaaz ko dhime kar saktaa he, isliye jahaaz kaa aakaar kuch aisa hota he ki tuuti barf uske dai-bai taraf yaa phir nauka ke neeche jane ke liye vivash ho jaati he or aage kaa rasta khulata jaataa he. jahaaz kaa nodak nauka ke baahar hota he isliye barf ke tukdon se lagaataar takaraane se use kshati pahunch sakati he. isliye himbhanjakon ke nodak majboot banaae jate hai or unka nirmaan kuch aisa hota he ki agar unke pankhe tut jaae to bich-yatra main bhi jahaaz ke karmchaaree unhen badalakar nae pankhe laga sakate hai. prithvi ke dhruviya ilaakon main aksar keval himbhanjak hi salaamati se yatayaat kar sakate hai.
