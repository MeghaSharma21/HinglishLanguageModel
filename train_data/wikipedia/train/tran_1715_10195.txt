 olympic main lebanon kii pehali upasthiti 1936 main thi jab berlin main greeshmakaleen olympic main "adhikariyon ke pratinidhimandal" ne bhaag liya tha. 1947 main lebanon kii olympic samiti ke gathan ke baad, lebanon ne 1948 main olympic khelon main pehali baar bhaag liya, or tab se athleates ko ek greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main sabhi ke liye muqabla karne ke liye bheja he. 1956 ke khelon ke bahishkaar ke liye lebanon main teen deshon main se ek tha, jo suvej sankat main british or francisi bhagidaari ke virodh main tha.lebanon ne 1948 ke baad se sabase sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main bhaag liya he, keval 1994 or 1998 ke sheetakaaleen khelon main nahin hai.
 lebanon ne singapore main 1 youth olympic khelon 2010 main bhaag liya.
 lebanani athleton ne kul chaar padak jite, teen greeko-roman kushti main or ek bharottolan main.
 lebanon kii rashtriya olympic samiti 1947 main banaai gai thi or 1948 main antarrashtriya olympic samiti dwaara manyata praapt thi.
