 antarrashtriya mudra kosh yaa antarrashtriya mudra kosh ek antarrashtriya sanstha he, jo apane sadsy deshon kii vaishvik aarthik sthiti par nazar rakhane kaa kaam karti he. yah apane sadsy deshon ko aarthik or takneekee sahaayta pradaan karti he. yah sanghathan anterrashtriya vinimay daron ko sthir rakhane ke saath-saath vikaas ko sugam karne main sahaayta karata he. iskaa mukhyalay washington di si, sanyukt rajya main he. is sanghathan ke prabandh nideshak domnik strans he. imef kii vishesh mudra esadiar he. antarrashtriya vyapaar or vitt ke liye kuch deshon kii mudra kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he, ise esadiar kehete hai. esadiar main euro, pound, yen or dollar hai. imef kii sthapana 1944 main kii gai thi. vibhinn deshon kii sarkaar ke 45 pratinidhiyon ne america ke britain woods main baithak kar antarrashtriya aarthik samjhaute kii ruparekha taiyaar kii thi. 27 disambar, 1945 ko 29 deshon ke samjhaute par hastakshar karne ke baad imef kii sthapana hui. 
 imef ke kul 186 sadsy desh hai. 29 june 2009 ko kosovo ganraajya 186wein desh ke rup main shaamil hua tha. imef kaa uddeshy aarthik sthirataa surakshit karnaa, aarthik pragati ko badhaava dena, garibi kam karnaa, rojgaar ko badhaava dena or antarrashtriya vyapaar suvidhajanak bananaa he. sadsy deshon kii sankhya badhane ke saath vaishvik arth-vyavastha main imef kaa kaary kaaphi badha he. koi bhi desh imef kii sadsyta ke liye aavedan kar saktaa he. pehle yah aavedan imef ke karypalak board dwaara vichaaradhin bheji jaati he. iske baad kaaryakari board, board of garvaness ko uski sanstuti ke liye bhejtaa he. vahaan sweekrut hone par sadsyta mil jaati he.
