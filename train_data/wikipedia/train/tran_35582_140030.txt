 girija 1950 or 1960 ke dashak kii kii ek praarambhik telugu film abhinetri or haasyabhinetri hai. unka janm 3 march 1938 ko andhra pradesh ke kankipu main hua tha. unki pehali film kastari siverao kii paramanandy shishulay katha thi. unhone kai filmon main abhinay kiya jo ki rilangi venkataramiyah ke saath thi or prasiddh hit kamedian jodi ban gai. unki beti salima ek abhinetri he jo mukhyatah mukhya malayalam filmon main kaam karti he.
