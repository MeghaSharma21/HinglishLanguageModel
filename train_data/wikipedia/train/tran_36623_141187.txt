 naa aana is desh lado ek hindi dharawahik he. yah dharawahik sabase pehle colors par prasaarit hua tha, jiske baad yah dharawahik raaj tivi par prasaarit hua. is dharawahik ko tamil nadu main bahut pasand kiya gaya. iske har abhinaytaon ke abhinay ko saraahaa gaya. san 2017 main isane colors par apane sijan 2 lado - virpur kii mardaani ke saath televijan par vaapasi kii par san 2018 main yah sho band ho gaya.
 isaki katha beti bachaao abhiyaan se sambandhit he. jisame ammaji kaa abhinay jabardast he. dharawahik main ye darshaya jaataa he kii virpur main betiyon kii janm ke baad hi hatya kar di jaati he, isaliye vahaan koi beti nahin he. phir ek ladki aati he or beti hatya ke is mudde kaa jam kar virodh karti he, or logon kii ghatiya soch ko badal detii he.
