 1991-92 wills trophy 17 oktober or 25 oktober 1991 ke bich sharajah main yojit trikoniya ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket toornament tha. isamen pakistan, west indies or bharat kii rashtriya cricket tiimon ko shaamil kiya gaya tha. 25 oktober ko final main bharat ko haraakar pakistan ne toornament jeet liya. toornament ke labhaarthiyon main vaasim raja or pakistan ke mahmud hossain, si ke naidu or bharat ke i e es prasanna or westeindies si ji greenidge the.
 pehle toornament haarane ke baavjood purv toornament ke pasandida pakistan ne jeet hasil kii or unki sthiti asahaay rahi jab tak westeindies tisare main teen gendon par do ran nahin bana sake. 
 vinod kambli or javagal shrinath ne bharat ke liye apni oriai kii shuruyaat kii
 final
 bharat ke sanjay manjrekar ne player of da siries award jeeta.
