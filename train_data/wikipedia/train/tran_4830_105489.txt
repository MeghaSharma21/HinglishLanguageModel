 vitt adhinium bharat kaa mahatvapurn adhinium he. is adhinium ke dwaara bharat sarkaar prayek vitt varsh ke armbh main vittiya prastaav rakhti he. yah adhinium bharat ke sabhi rajyon evam kendra shashit pradeshon par bhi lagu hota he.
 prayek varsh vittamantri dwaara sansad main aay-vyay kaa lekha-jokha prastut kiya jaataa he. bhaag A main sarkaar ke vittiya kshetra main prastaavit nitiyon ko darshaya jaataa he. bhaag B main prastaavit kar bajet kaa varnan rahata he. ise lagu karne ke liye sansad main vitt vidheyak prastut kiya jaataa he. jab ise sansad se sweekruti mil jaati he or raashtrapati kii sahmati praapt ho jaati he tab yah 'vitt adhinium' ban jaataa he.
