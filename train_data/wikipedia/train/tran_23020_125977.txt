 kuch radioskriy nabhikon se utsarjit hone vaale ucch-urja tathaa ucch-veg vaale electron yaa positronon ko beta kan kehete hai. ye ek prakaar ke inkaree vikiran hai. inhen 'beta kiran' bhi kehete hai. radioskriy nabhik se beta kanon kaa nikalnaa 'beta-kshay' kaha jaataa he. beta kanon ko greek-varna beta dwaara nirupit kiya jaataa he. beetaa-kshay do prakaar kaa hota he, β− tathaa β+, jisamen kramshah electron or positron nikalate hai.
