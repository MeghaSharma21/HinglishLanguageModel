 soons yaa dalfinas taramandal khagoleey gole ke uttari bhaag main dikhne vaala ek taramandal he jo khagoleey madhyarekha ke kaafii sameep padataa he. yah antarrashtriya khagoleey sangh dwaara jari kii gai 88 taramandalon kii suchi main shaamil he or duusari shatabdi isvi main tolmi ne jin 48 taramandalon kii suchi banaai thi unamem bhi shaamil tha.
 soons taramandal ko angrezi main "dalfinas constlation" kaha jaataa he. soons usee jaleey standhari praani kaa hindi naam he jise angrezi main "dolphin" kehete hai.
 soons taramandal main 5 mukhya taare hai, haalanki vaise isamen 19 taron ko bair naam die ja chuke hai. inamen se 5 ke ird-gird gair-saureey grah parikrama karte hue paae gaye hai. is taramandal ke mukhya taare or anya vaastuen is prakaar hai -
