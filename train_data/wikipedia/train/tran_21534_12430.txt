 dwaitvaad do bhaagon main athwa do bhinn roopon vaali sthiti ko nirupit karne vaala ek shabd he. is shabd ke arth main vishyavaar bhinnata aa sakati he. darshan athwa dharm main iskaa arth puja archana se liya jaataa he jiske anusaar prarthana karne vaala or sunne vaala do alag rup hai. in donon kii mishrit rachana ko dwaitvaad kaha jaataa he.
