 dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke pashchaat jalwaayu parivartan ko lekar vaishvik str par charchaayen prarambh huin. 1972 me sweden kii rajdhani stoffhome main pehla sammelan yojit kiya gaya. tay hua ki prayek desh jalwaayu parivartan se nipatane ke liye gharelu niyam banayega. is aashay kii pushti hetu 1972 main hi sanyukt raashtra paryaavaran kaarykram kaa gathan kiya gaya tathaa nairobi ko iskaa mukhyalay banaaya gaya.
 stoffhome sammelan ke 20 varsh pashchaat brazil ke rio di generio main sambaddh rashtron ke pratinidhi ekatrit hue tathaa jalwaayu parivartan sambandhit kaaryayojna ke bhavishya kii disha par punah charchaa aarambh bhi.is sammelan ko rio sammelan, stoffhome 20, 92 abhismay, tathaa agenda 21 aadi naamon se bhi janaa jaataa he. rio main yah tay kiya gaya ki sadsy raashtra prayek varsh ek sammelan main ekatrit honge tathaa jalwaayu sambandhit chintaaon or kaaryayojanaon par charchaa karengen. is sammelan ko conference of parties naam diya gaya. 1995 main pehla coup sammelan yojit kiya gaya. 1995 se 2011 tak kul 17 conference of parties yojit kiye ja chuke hai.
