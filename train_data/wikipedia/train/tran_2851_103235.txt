 marty akhtisaari finland ke purv raashtrapati ne kosovo ke bhavishya par hui vaartaon main sanyukt raashtra ke vishesh doot kii bhoomika nibhaai or varsh 2005 main indonesia ke aache praant ko lekar hue samjhaute main bhi vah madhyasth rahe. unhen 2008 ke nobel shanti puraskaar die jane kaa nirnay liya gaya he. norway kii paanch sadasyon vaali puraskaar samiti ne is saal 197 ummidavaaron main se unke naam kaa chayan kiya. akhtisaari ko yah puraskaar 10 disambar ko oslo main diya jayega. 10 disambar ko hi 1896 main is puraskaar ke sansthaapak sweden ke anveshak elfred nobel kaa nidhan hua tha.
