 milling kattar kaatane vaale aujaar hai jo praayah milling mashiinon main tathaa kuch anya masini aujaaron main prayukt hote hai. apni gati ke dwaara ve padaarth ko katkar hataate hai. 
 aadhunik milling kattar gol chakrakaar aari kaa hi parishkrut rup he, jo swayan ghumkar dhire-dhire thodi thodi dhaatu ko khurchakar katata he. vicitra akritivali vastuon ko chirane kaa kaam, jo anya aariyon se nahin kiya ja saktaa, use milling kattar se karte hai. milling kattar aaj anek prakaar ke banaae gaye hai jinke daanton kii rachana bhinn bhinn prakaar kii hoti he.
