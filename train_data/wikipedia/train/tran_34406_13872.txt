 saat rang ke sapane 1998 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. priyadarshan dwaara nirdeshit is film main juhi chawla, aravind swami, farida zalaal, anupam kher, satish shah, or tinnu anand mukhya kalakaar hai. yah nirdeshak kii 1994 kii ek malayalam film kii rimek he jisamen mohanlal or shobhana mukhya kalakaar the.
 yashoda kii jabardastee ek maansik rup se biimaar vyakti se shaadi kara di jaataa he. wo baad main ek bachche ko janm detii he or us bachche ko akele chhod kar hameshaa ke liye chali jaati he. uske is tarah akele chhod kar chale jane se uskaa bhai, bhanu gusse main rahata he or iskaa badla lene kii bhi sochataa he. apane is gusse ko baahar nikaalane ke liye har saal wo apane naukar or achche dost, mahipal ko bail gaadi ke pratiyogita main bhaag lene or apni bahan ke sasuraal waalon ko haraane kii baat karata he. mahipal har saal jitate rahata he.
 bhanu chalis ke paas pahunchne ke baad bhi shaadi nahin kiye rahata he or apane gaanv main rahane vaali ek ladki ko lubhaane kii koshish karte rahata he. ek din bhanu or mahipal, apane bail gaadi main jate rahete hai or tabhi unhen baldev or uski bahan, jaalimaa milte hai. bhanu ko jaalimaa se pyaar ho jaataa he, vahiin mahipal un donon ko pasand nahin karata or bich raste se hi bhagaanaa chaahate rahata he.
 sabhi geet samir dwaara likhit; sara sangeet nadeem-shravan dwaara rachit.
