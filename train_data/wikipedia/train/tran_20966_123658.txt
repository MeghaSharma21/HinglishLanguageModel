 baadaam ke tel ko british farmacopia main 'olium emigdali' kehete hai. yah baadaam kii giri se praapt hota he. giri ko kolhu main perkar, athwa vilaayakon dwaara, tel ko alag karte hai. tel kii maatra mithe baadaam main 45% se 55% or kaduve baadaam main 35% se 44% ho sakati he.
 baadaam kaa tel ashushkaneey sthir tel he. yah halake pile rang kaa hota he. isaki gandh vishesh prakaar kii hoti he. nishkarshan dwaara praapt tel kuch maile rang kaa hota he. is tel ke vishisht gun is prakaar hai :
 yah jal main aviley, alcohol main alp viley or ether, chlorofarm tathaa benzin main sahaj viley he. isamen mukhyat: olyik, linoleik ke atirikt, santrupt amlon main miristic or pamitic amla kuch rahete hai. sookshm mashiinon ke liye snehak tel ke nirmaan, oshadhiyon, chehre kii kriimon tathaa biscuit yaa anya mithaiyon ke banane main yah prayukt hota he.
 kadue baadaam se sthir tel ke atirikt 0.5% se 7% tak vashpashil tel bhi praapt hota he. sthir tel nikaal lene par jo avshisht ansh batch jaataa he uskaa pani ke saath sampreshan karte hai. avshisht ansh main emigdalin naamak glucoseida rahata he or usamen ek enzim imalsin rahata he. jal kii upasthiti main imalsin emigdalin kaa vighatan kar glucose, benzaldihide or hydrocynic amla mukt karata he. is prakaar se praapt utpaada ke aswan se vashpashil tel praapt hota he, jisamen banjaldihide or hydrocynic amla donon rahete hai. asut ko chooney or pheras sulphate ke saath upachaarit karne se hydrocynic amla nikaala ja saktaa he. banjaldihide ke kaaran asut main vishesh gandh hoti he. is gandh ke kaaran hi sangandh tel ke rup main iskaa vyavhaar hota he.
