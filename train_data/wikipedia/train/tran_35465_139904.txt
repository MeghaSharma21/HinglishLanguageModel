 marshall maikluhaan ek canadai professor, darshanik or bauddhik the. unka kaam media adhyayan ke mukhya stambhon main se ek ke rup main dekha jaataa he, saath hi vigyaapan or televijan udyogon main vyaavhaarik tareeke se upyog main bhi aata he. ve vaakyansh "global village" ke janak hai.
 herbert marshall maikluhaan kaa janm 21 july 1911 ko admonton, alberta, main hua tha. elsi naomi ke bete or herbert arnest maikluhaan donon hi canada main paidaa hue the. uskaa bhai mauris do saal baad paidaa hua tha. "marshall" unki mamia daadi kaa sarnem tha. unki maan ek baptist skool kii shikshika thim jo baad main ek abhinetri ban gai. unke pita methodist the or admonton main unka ek real estate kaa vyavsaay tha. vishwa yuddh pratham main vifal hone par vyapaar bhi vifal ho gaya or maikluhaan ke pita ne unhen canadai sena main bhartee kara diya. ek saal kii seva ke baad, unhone influenza qaraar kiya or canada main bane rahe, seemaa rekhaon se dur. 1915 main sena se apane nirvahan ke baad, maikluhaan parivaar ko lekar vineepeg, manitoba main chale gaye, jahaan marshall bada hua or skool gaya, 1928 main manitoba vishvavidhyalay main daakhila lene se pehle vah kelvin technical skool main pard rahe the.
