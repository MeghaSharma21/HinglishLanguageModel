 vidyut aaveshon ke maujuudagi or bahaav se jude bhautik parighatnaon ke samucchy ko vidyut kaha jaataa he. vidyut se anek jaani-maani ghatnaayen judi he jaise ki tadit, sthitik vidyut, vidyutchumbakeey preran, tathaa vidyut dhara. iske atirikt, vidyut ke dwaara hi vaidyutchumbakeey tarango kaa srijan evam prapti sambhav hota he?
 vidyut ke saath chumbakatv judi hui ghatana he. vidyut aavesh vaidyutchumbakeey kshetra paidaa karte hai. vidyut kshetra main rakhe vidyut aaveshon par bal lagata he.
 samast vidyut kaa aadhaar electron hai. electranon ke hastaanantaran ke kaaran hi koi vastu aaweshit hoti he. aavesh kii gati hi vidyut dhara he. vidyut ke anek prabhaav hai jaise chumbakeey kshetra, ooshmaa, rasinic prabhaav aadi.
 jab vidyut or chumbakatv kaa ek saath adhyayan kiya jaataa he to ise vidyut chumbakatv kehete hai. vidyut ko anekon prakaar se paribhaashit kiya ja saktaa he kintu saral shabdon main kaha jaye to vidyut aavesh kii upasthiti tathaa bahaav ke parinamswaroop utpann us saamaanya avasthaa ko vidyut kehete hai jisamen anekon kaaryon ko sampann karne kii kshamta hoti he. vidyut chal athwa achal electron yaa proton se sambaddh ek bhautik ghatana he. kisi chaalak main vidyut aaveshon ke bahaav se utpann urja ko vidyut kehete hai.
 isa se lagbhag 600 varsh purv yunaan nivasee theliz is baat se parichit the ki kuch vastuen ragadne ke pashchaat halaki vastuon ko aakarshith karti hai. iskaa ullekh thiafrastus ne 321 i.pus. main tathaa plini ne san 70 main kiya tha. is aakarshan shakti kaa adhyayan 16 veen shatabdi main wiliam gilbert dwaara hua tathaa unhone ise 'electric' kaha. aadhunik shabd 'electron' kaa upyeg unani bhashaa main ambar ke liye kiya jaataa he. 'electricity' shabd kaa upyog san 1650 main walter sharlton ne kiya. isi samay rabart byal ne pataa lagaaya ki aaweshit vastuen halki vastuon ko shunya main bhi aakarshith karti hai, arthant vidyut ke prabhaav ke liye hawa kaa madhyam hona aavashyak nahin he. san 1729 main steefan grey ne apane prayogon ke aadhaar par kaha ki yah aakarshan shakti kisi vastu ke ek bhaag se duusare bhaag ko sanchaarit kii ja sakati he. aisi vastuon ko desagulirs ne 'chaalak' kaha. sabhi prakaar kii dhaatuen is shrenee main aati hai. ve vastuen jinamein is shakti ko sanchaarit nahin kiya ja saktaa, vidyutarodhi kehalaati hai. is shrenee main ambar, mom, sukhi hawa, sookhaa kaanch, rabar, lakh ityadi hai.
 vastuon kii ragad ke kaaran vidyut do prakaar kii hoti he, ghanaatmak evam rinatmak. pehle inke kramash: kachabh tathaa rejini naam prachlit the. san 1737 main duphe ne bataaya ki sajaateey aavesh ek duusare ko pratikarshith karte hai tathaa vijaateey aakarshith karte hai. 1745 main clist ne cumin main, macenbrook ne liden main, tathaa wiliam watasan ne landan main kaha ki vidyut kaa sanchay bhi kiya ja saktaa he, inke prayogon tathaa vichaaron ne prasiddh sanchayak liden jaar ko janm diya. lagbhag isi samay vidyut ko paryaapt maatra main praapt karne ke praytn bhi jari the tathaa vibhinn prakaar ke vidyut yantron kaa avishkaar hua. wilia vatsan kaa vichaar tha ki vidyut ek prakaar kaa pratyasth taral hoti he jo prayek vastu main vidyaman hoti he. aveshvihin vastuon main yah saadhaaran maatra main hoti he at: iskaa nirikshan nahin kiya ja saktaa. watasan ke taral siddhant ke anusaar vidyut ek vastu se duusari vastu main chali jaati he. amareeki vaigyanik tathaa raajnneetijna benjamin franklin ne is siddhant kaa samarthan kar, vistaar kiya. franklin ne kaha ki vidyut na to utpann kii ja sakati he, na nasht hi. franklin ne liden jaar kaa adhyayan kar uski kriya ko samajhane kii cheshtaa kii. par franklin kaa sabase prasiddh evam mahatvapurn vah prayog tha, sisamen unhone siddh kiya ki meghon se meghgarjan ke samay vidyut tathaa saadhaaran vidyut ke gun samaan hai. unhone yah bhi kaha ki vidyut ke kan ek duusare par bal daalate hai.
 franklin ke pashchaat apinus ne in vichaaron ko liya tathaa iskaa abhas diya ki do vastuon kaa bal unke bich kii duuri badhaane par ghat jaataa he. is siddhant kaa vistaar jajef pristli tathaa henry cavendish ne kiya. phir kulom ne khoj kii ki do aaveshon ke bich kaa bal, unke bich kii duuri ke varg ke vyutakramanupati tathaa aaveshon ke gunanfal ke samaanupati hota he. vidyut kaa yah mul niyam ab bhi 'kulom kaa balanium' kaha jaataa he. san 1837 main maaikel farade ne kinhin do aaweshit vastuon ke bich ke vidyut bal par madhyam ke prabhaav kaa adhyayan kiya tathaa pataa lagaaya ki yadi madhyam hawa ke sthaan par koi or vidyutarodhi ho to vidyut bal ghat jaataa he, vidyut rodhi ke is gun ko unhone 'vishisht parvaidyutta' athwa paraavidhyut kaha. unhone apane barf ke baratanwale prasiddh prayog dwaara darshaya ki yadi kisi aaweshit chaalak kaa ek baratan main laayaa jaae, to baratan ke andar kii ore vijaateey aavesh prerit hota he tathaa baahar kii ore sajaateey awesh. farade ne paraavidhyut kaa gahan adhyayan kiya tathaa unke vibhinn prabhaavon ko samajhane ke liye vidyut bal rekhaon kaa vichaar upasthit kiya tathaa aaweshit vastuon ke bich ke khaali sthaan ko 'kshetra' kaha. farade ke kshetra siddhant ko ganit kii sahaayta se gouse ne aage badhaayaa.
 aththarahavin shatabdi ke antim varshon main aaveshon ke chalan ke sambandh main kai prayog tathaa siddhant prakaash main aane lage the. san 1780 main italy ke ljugi gailawani ne mechak ke upar vidyut pravaah ke kai prayog kiye. san 1800 main volta ne tanu amla athwa lavan vilyan se bhigi hui do asmaan dhaatuon main vidyut prabhaav paae tathaa unase vidyut dhara praapt kii. is vidyut pravaah ko kai guna karne ke liye unhone aisi kai asmaan dhaatuon ke jodon ko lekar ek punj banaaya jise voltiy punj kehete hai. volta dwaara in prayogon ke anusaar vidyuddhara praapt karne ke liye 'voltiy cell' kii rachana hui. usee varsh england main niklson tathaa karlile ne is baat kaa pataa lagaaya ki yadi pani main vidyut dhara pravaahit kii jaae to pani ke hydrogen tathaa oxigen main apghaton ko jaataa he. aise apghaton ko vaidyut apghaton kehete hai. crukashaik ne pataa lagaaya ki vilyan ke dhaatulavan bhi isi prakaar apaghatit kiye ja sakate hai. iske pashchaat farade ne is kriya kaa niyamit adhyayan kiya tathaa 'farade ke niyamon' kii sthapana kii. in niyamon athwa inase sambandhit prayogon ke aadhaar par vidyut dhara utpaadan karanevaale vibhinn prakaar ke cell tathaa sanchayakon kii rachana kii gai he.
 san 1820 main hens chrishchiyan arsted ne khoj kiya ki ek taar main pravaahit vidyut dhara ke saath usase sambandhit ek chumbakeey kshetra bhi hota he. is mahatvapurn khoj ko kipro tathaa sawar ne or ampiore ne ganit evam prayogon kii sahaayta se aage badhaayaa. ampiore ne yah dikhaaya ki do samaantar taron main vidyut dhara kii disha samaan hone par aakarshan tathaa vipareet hone par pratikarshan hota he. arsted ke siddhanton ko farade ne viksit kiya tathaa vidyut-chumbakeey preran ke niyamon kii sthapana kii. preran kaa adhyayan baad main niman tathaa webar ne bhi kiya parantu prarena sambandhi vichaaron kaa mahatvapurn upyog clark maxwell ne san 1851 main kiya tathaa 'maxwell samikarnon' kii sthapana kar vidyucchumbakeey siddhanton ko ganit kii sahaayta se ek sulajhaa hua rup diya. aadhunik bhautikee main in samikarnon kaa vishesh sthaan he.
 san 1822 main jebec ne dekha ki yadi ek paripath main do asmaan dhaatuon ko jod diya jaae or ek jod ko garam kiya jaae to paripath main vidyut pravaahit hoti he. aisi vidyut ko 'ooshmaa vidyut' kehete hai.
 san 1826 main george simon om ne prasiddh 'om ke niyam' kii sthapana kii. san 1841 main jul ne vidyut ke ooshmaa prabhaav kaa adhyayan kiya tathaa batalaaya ki kisi cell kii rasinic urja, jo paripath main dhara pravaahit karti he, us paripath main utpaadit ooshmaa urja ke baraabar hoti he. helm holtz, wiliam tomson, kelvin, lorden, aadi ne vidyut urja sambandhi anya siddhanton kaa vikaas kiya. san 1848 main kirkhhaaf ne vidyuddhara sambandhi niyamon ko prastut kiya. san 1851 main lard kelvin ne ooshmaa vidyut kaa ooshmaagatiki ke siddhaton dwaara vishleshan kiya. san 1855 main maxwell dwaara vidyut tathaa prakoshtarang sambandhi vichaaron kii neenv padi. san 1884 main jon henry paniting ne vidyut chumbakeey kshetra main urja pravaah kaa adhyayan kiya. san 1886 main hinrikh herts kii sahaayta se maxwell ke siddhanton ko praayogic samarthan mila. iske pashchaat vidyucchumbakeey tarangon ke vishay main kai vaigyanikon kaa dhyaan aakarshith hua. marconi ne san 1896 main inkaa prayog sandesh bhejne main kiya. isi samay ke lagbhag bharat ke jagadishchandra basu, ne ucch aavritiwali vidyucchambakeey tarangon kaa janan kiya tathaa inke gunon ko prakaash ke siddhanton se samajhane kii cheshtaa kii. iske pashchaat is vishay kii paryaapt pragati hui jise falswaroop radio, televijan tathaa 'electronic' kaa kshetra viksit hua.
 herts ke anya prayogon ne 'prakashavidyut' kii bhi khoj kii jisko instin ne kwantum siddhanton dwaara san 1905 main samajhaayaa. san 1895 main tathaa usee samay ke lagbhag bhautikee ke kshetra main ek krantikari andolan aayaa. san 1895 main rantagen ne 'eksre' kaa, 1896 main bekrel ne ne radioskriyata kaa tathaa 1897 main sar je.je. tomson ne 'electron' kaa avishkaar kiya. tamsan ne gaison main se vidyudvisarjan ke vishay kaa bhi adhyayan kiya. is vishay main inase pehle, aarambh main gislar, plakar, hitorph, goldstin aadi ne kaary kiya tha.
 baad ke vaigyanikon main se pramukh hai je.es. townsend tathaa unke saathi. san 1902 main richardsan ne 'tapayanic' vishay kii neenv daali. 'tapayan dhara' ke siddhant par radio valve tathaa electronic ke anya vaalvon kii rachana hui he. beesvin shatabdi main ek ke pashchaat ek mahatvapurn khojon kaa taanta bandh gaya jinke parinaam swaroop aaj ke hydrogen bam, sangalan urja, 'sputanik' tathaa anya upgrah ityadi. inke panpane main kisi na kisi rup main vidyut ke siddhanton kaa upyog hua he.
