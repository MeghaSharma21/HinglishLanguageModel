 apvaah yaa dharatleey apvaah jal kii vah maatra he jo prithvi kii sataha par gurutvkarshan ke prabhaav main dhaal kaa anusaran karte hue jaldharaon, saritaaon, naalon or nadiyon ke rup main pravaahit hota he. kisi nadi basin yaa kisi bhi bhugolic ikai kaa apvaah us ikai main hone vaale varshan main se nisyandan, vashpikaran, mrudaa-jal-dhaaran ityadi dwaara hone vaale kshay ko gata kar nikaala ja saktaa he. saritaaon or nadiyon kaa apvaah unke basin ke aakaar par bhi nirbhar karata he.
 apvaah kaa maapan or iske pratirupon kaa adhyayan kisi bhugolic kshetra ke jal tantra yaa jal chakra ko samajhane ke liye atyant aavashyak he. isi liye jal sansadhano ke kshetreey athwa vaishvik adhyayan main apvaah kaa mahatvapurn sthaan hota he.
 bahata hua jal kai prakaar ke sthalroopon kaa nirmaan bhi karata he jinhen jaleey sthalaroop kehete hai. inamen pramukh hai ve-aakaar kii ghati, jalprapaat, kshiprika, baadh maidan, visarp, delta ityadi.
 nirdhaarit jalmargon kaa anusaran karata hua bahata jal inke jaal dwaara jo tantra banaata he use apvaah tantra kehete hai. is apvaah tantra kaa jyamitiya vinyas yah bataata he ki yah kis prakaar kaa apvaah tantra he yaa iskaa apvaah praturup kya he.kisi kshetra kaa apvaah tantra us kshetra kii sthalakriti or jalwaayu par nirbhar hota he
