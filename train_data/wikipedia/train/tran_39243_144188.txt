 laghu tarangaradio hotin hai 3 MHz evam 30 MHz kii aavritiyon ke madhya. inhen purv kaal main aisa kaha jane laga, kyonki in aavritiyon se judi tarang dairghy, us samay prayog hone vaali deergh tarang se choti hoti thim. ek vaikalpik naam he ucchavriti radio. aisa isliye hota tha kyonki, aavriti evam tarang dairghy, vilom anupaat main hote hai.
