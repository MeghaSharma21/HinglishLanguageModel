 vaiyaktik upyog kii vasteen, arjeet aay tathaa bachat saath hi utpaadan ke katipay saadhan jo vyaktigat rup se istemaal kiye jate hai, vaiyaktik sampatti kehalaate hai. vaiyaktik sampatti kii prakruti nijee sampatti se multah bhinn hoti he. nijee sampatti manushya dwaara manushya ke shoshan ke, dusron ke shram ke falon ko hadapney ke saadhan ke rup main kaam karti he parantu vaiyaktik sampatti kii manyata kaa arth uski aseemit sanvruddhi nahin he. samaajwaad ke antargat anarjit aay kii prapti ke uddeshy se vaiyaktik sampatti ke durupyog kii sambhaavna bani rahati he. parantu samyawad ke antargat vaiyaktik sampatti kii sankalpana kaa koi arth nahin rah jayega, kyonki vaiyaktik aavashyaktaaon kii purti mukhyatya saamaajik koshon se hogi.
