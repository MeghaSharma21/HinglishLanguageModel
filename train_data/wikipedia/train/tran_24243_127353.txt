 burhanuddin rabbaani  afghanistan ke 1992 se 1996 tak or 2001 me duusare kaarykaal ke raashtrapati the.
 rabbaani jamiyat - e islamic afghanistan ke netaa the. unhone united islamic front ke raajnneetik pramukh ke rup main bhi kaam kiya. yah vibhinn raajnneetik dalo kaa ek gathbandhan tha, jo afghanistan main taliban shashan khilaaf ladaa. ve 1992-1996 se raashtrapati the, jab unhe taliban adhigrahan kii vajah se kabul shahar chhodane ke liye majaboor kiya gaya. unki sarkaar ko kai deshon or sanyukt raashtra dwaara manyata praapt thi.
 20 sitambar, 2011 ko ek aatmghaatee hamalavar ne kabul main purv raashtrapati ke ghar main pravesh kar rabbaani hatya kar di.
