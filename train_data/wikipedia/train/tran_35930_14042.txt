 shrinath singh dvivedi yug ke hindi sahityakar hai. inhone "saraswati" kaa bhi sampadan kiya tha. unka janm 1901 i0 main elahabaad jile ke manpur gram main hua tha. inhone swatantraaa aandolan main bhaag liya tha| hindi sahitya sammelan ke indore adhiveshan main ve mahatma gandhi dwaara sammelan ke prabandh mantri niyukt kiye gayi, baad main ve hindi sahitya sammelan ke sabhapati bhi hue| ve taatkaaleen sanyukt praant sarkaar dwaara gathit samachar patr udyog jaanch samiti ke sadsy bhi the| 
 unki pramukh rachanaayen hai: uljhan, kshamaa, ekakini yaa akeli stri, prem pariksha, jaagran, prajamandal, ek or anek, apahruta aadi aapaki prasiddh kritiyaan hai. inaki kahaniyon main ,jo ki padosi tathaa anya kahaniyaan, patheyika evam nayantara aadi sangrahon main sangrahit hai, tatkaaleen gramin janajivan kii jhalak dekhi ja sakati he|
 inhone shishu, baalasakhaa, balbodh evam didi, hal, aadi masik patrikaon kaa anek varshon tak sampadan kiya. shrinath singh ne dainik deshbandhu tathaa deshdoot saptahik samachaarpatron kaa bhi sampaadan kiya | bacchon ke liye shrinath singh ne anek lokapriy evam saral, subodh kavitaaon kii rachana kii jo ki inke dwaara rachit pustakon or uparokt patron main maujuud hai. apane samay main thakur shrinath singh ke naam se bhi jane jate the. hindi ke us samay ke kai sahityakaron dwaara samay samay par unhen likhe gayi anek patr nehru museum nai dilli main sangrakshit hai.inaki bal upyogi rachanaayen hai - bal kavitawali, khelghar, balbharati, pipehari, gubbaaraa, mithitanen, | garunakanya, sunehri nadi kaa devtaa, paridesh kii saiyr, avishkaron kii kathayen, inke upanyaas kavi or krantikari va somnath tathaa bacchon ke liye likhit avishkaron kii kathayen jo ki shrinath singh ke naam se likhi gai he, digital librari of india main hai. unki kai rachanaaon ko internet archive main dekha ja saktaa he ||
