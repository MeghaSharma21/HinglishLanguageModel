 shikari-hans bhujaa hamaari gallexi kii ek sarpil bhujaa he. yah lagbhag 3,500 prakaash varsh chaudee or 10,000 prakaash varsh lambi he, lekin akashganga ke mahaan aakaar ke hisaab se ek choti baazuu samajhi jaati he. prithvi, hamaara suraj or hamaara puraa saura mandal isamen sthit he, isliye is bhujaa ko "sthaneey bhujaa" bhi kaha jaataa he. kabhi-kabhi ise "shikari shaakh" bhi kehete hai.
 is baazuu kaa aupchaarik naam shikari-hans bhujaa isliye padaa kyonki aasmaan main dekhne par iske taare adhiktar shikari taramandal or hans taramandal ke kshetron main nazar aate hai. shikari-hans bhujaa akashganga kii do anya baazuon ke bich sthit he: karaina-dhanu bhujaa or yayati bhujaa . hamaara saura mandal shikari-hans bhujaa ke bich main he or kshirmarg ke centre se lagbhag 26,000 prakaash varsh kii duuri par he.
 angrezi main "shikari-hans bhujaa" ko "orin-signs arm" yaa "orin spor" kaha jaataa he. "orin" latini bhashaa main "shikari" or "signs" us bhashaa main "hans" ke liye shabd hai.
