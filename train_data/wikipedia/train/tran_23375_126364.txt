 bharat main gunvatttapurna kanoonee shiksha ke badhate mahatv ke saath vibhinn rashtriya kanoon vidyalayon ko mahatvapurn sthaan praapt hua he. is kram main chaatro kii aankakshaon kaa puraa karne ke liye kendra sarkaar ne pahal karte hue rajyon ke saath rashtriya mahatv ke vidhi vidhyalay / vishvavidhyalay ko sthapit kiya he. vartmaan main 16 rashtriya str vidhi vidhyalay/vishvavidhyalay gunvatttapurna kanoonee shiksha ke rup main samekit snaatak or snaattakottar paathyakram kii peshkash deshbhar ke chaatro ko de rahe hai.
 ye vidhi santhaan in paathyakramon ke alaava enjio, sarkari adhikariyon prashaskon, sthaneey sarkari pratinidhiyon or peshewaron kanoonjna ke saath bor concil ke pratinidhiyon, anya prashaskon or peshewaron ke liye bhi vibhinn paathyakram yojit karte hai.
