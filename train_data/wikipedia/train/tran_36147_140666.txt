 
 hazarat moulana sufi moofti azaanagachhi sahib  ), ek mahaan bhartiya sufi sant the. ye pashchim bengal ke bagmari main ek islamic sufi gaiyr sarkari sanghathan, hakkaani anjuman ke sthapakake rup main kaaphi parichit hai. ye islam dharm ke dusre khalipha hazrat umr ke 36 wein baad kii peedi se sambandhit hai.
 sufim ek tarika he, jo ek vyakti ke aadhyatmik vikaas main madad karata he or prayek vyakti ke madhyam se, kul pranali main anushaasan laane main madad karata he. hazarat moulana azaanagachhi aise sufi the, jinhone 1920 ke dashak or 1930 ke shuruaati dinon main british bengal main sufism kaa prasar kiya. unhone ek tarika viksit kiya, jiskaa naam tarika i jamiya |
