 shri rup goswami, vrundavan main chaitanya mahaprabhu dwaara bheje gaye chah shangoswami main se ek the. ve kavi, guru or darshanik the. ve sanathan goswami ke bhai the. 
 inkaa janm 1493 i ko hua tha. inhone 22 varsh kii aayu main grihasthashram tyaag kar diya tha. baad ke 51 varsh ye braj main hi rahe. inhone shri sanathan goswami se diksha lee thi. inhen shuddh bhakthi seva main maharath praapt thi, atev inhen bhakthi-rasacharya kaha jaataa he. ye gaurang ke ati premi the. ye apane agraj shri sanathan goswami sahit nawab hossain shah ke darbar ke ucch padon kaa tyaag kar gaurang ke bhakthi sankirthan main ho liye the. inhen ke dwaara chaitanya ne apni bhakthi-shiksha tathaa sabhi granthon ke aavashyak saar kaa prachaar-prasar kiya. mahaprabhu ke bhakton main se in donon bhaaiyon ko unke pradhan kaha jaataa tha. san 1564 i ko 73 varsh kii aayu main inhone param dham ko prasthaan kiya.
 shri rup goswami samast goswamiyon ke nayak the. unhone bhaktikaryakalapon kaa nirdesh karne ke liye hame upadeshamrita pradaan kiya jisase bhaktagan uskaa paal kar saken. jis prakaar shrichaitanya mahaprabhu apane peeche shikshashtak naamak aath shloke chhod gayi the usee prakaar shri rup goswami bhi shriupadeshamrita pradaan kiya jisase ham shuddh bhakt ban saken samast aadhyatmik kaaryon main manushya kaa pehla kartavya apane man tathaa indriyon ko vash main karnaa he. man tathaa indriyon ko vash main kiye bina koi bhi vyakti aadhyatmik jivan main pragati nahin kar saktaa. prayek vyakti is bhautik jagat main rajo tathaa tamo gunon main nimagn he. use shri rupagoswami ke upadeshon par chalate hue apane aapako shuddh satv gun ke pad tak uthanaa chaiye. tabhi aage kii unnati sambadhi prayek aadhyatmik rahasy prakat ho jaayegaa
