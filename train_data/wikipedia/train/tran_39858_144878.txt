 ramesh babu kaa janm chennai, tamilnadu main pramukh telugu cinema abhinayta krishna or shrimati indira devi ko 13 oktober 1965 main hua. unki teen choti bahanen or ek chote bhai hai unki bahanen padmavati, abhinetri-nirmaataa manjula ghatmanani or priyadarshanee hai. unke bhai, mahesh babu telugu cinema main ek pramukh abhinayta hai.
 1977 main, ramesh babu ne 12 saal kii umr main ek bal abhinayta ke rup main filmon main apane pita kii film manushulu chessena dongal ke saath kaam kiya. do saal ke baad, 14 varsh kii umr main, unhen film nida main ek mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai gai, jiskaa nirdesan dasri narayan raav ne kiya tha. film main unke chaar varshiya bhai, mahesh babu ko ek choti bhoomika main bhi mila. uske baad, unhone abhinay se ek sankshipt antaraal liya 1987 main, unhone ve. madhusudan raav dwaara nirdeshit film samraat ke saath ek pramukh abhinayta ke rup main vaapasi kii. is film main sonam ek mahatvapurn bhoomika main ramesh or sharada ke saath joda gaya tha. 1988 main, unhen jaandhiyaala kii chinni krishnudu, e kodendaraami reddy ke bazaar roudi or kaliyuga karandu or muguru kodukuli chaaro main apane pita dwaara nirdeshit filmon main dekha gaya tha. muguru kodukula kaa bhi unke pita or bhai ne unke saath abhinay kiya. bazaar roudi ek blockbster ban gai or ramesh ko ek ekal hero ke rup main apni pehali safalataa de rahi thi. 1989 main, unhen dashari narayan raav ke blike tiger or ve madhusudan raav ke krishna gaadi abbai main dekha gaya tha. 1990 main, vah ke murali mohan raav ke ayadham or es ravichandra kii kaliyuga abhimanyudu main dekha gaya tha. 1991 main, vah apane pita kii bhoomika main naa il naa swargram main bhi dikhaai die. 1993 main, unhone ek baar phir se masa kodala film ke liye dasri narayan raav ke saath milakar kaam kiya or aman ke saath anna chelelu main bhi dekha gaya. 1994 main, film pichaa thoranam main ek agrani bhoomika main unhen pichhali baar dekha gaya tha. yah film adhauthi sabhaskar dwaara nirdeshit thi or rambha abhinay ke saath ramesh the. 1997 main, unhen en shankar ke muthbhed main ek sahaayak bhoomika main dekha gaya tha.
