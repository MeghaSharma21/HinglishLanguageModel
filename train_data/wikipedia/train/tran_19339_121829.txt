 richard samuel atonbaro aise pratibhashali angrej abhinayta, nirdeshak va nirmaataa hai jinhen academy puraskaar, bafta or teen baar golden globe puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya he. un kii gandhi ko saal 1983 main do shreniyon main auskar puraskaar mila tha. etenbera ko sarvshereshth nirdeshak kaa aword mila tha jabaki gandhi film ko sarvshereshth film kaa. atonbaro ne puraskaar grahan karne ke baad kaha, "sahi myanon main iske zariye aapane gandhi ko sammanit kiya he. vah lakhon logon ke liye preranarot the."
