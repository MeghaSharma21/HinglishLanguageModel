 prambanan java main ek vishaal hindu mandir-parisar he. iskaa nirmaan 850 main hua. yah unesco vishwa dharohar sthal he or lokapriy paryaton sthal or tirthasthan bhi he.
 isamen teen pramukh mandir shiv, vishnu or brahma kii hai. shiv mandir main teen or murtiyon hai- durga, ganesh or agastya kii. shiv, brahma, vishnu ke vaahan nandi, hans or garuda ke bhi mandir hai.
 durga kii murti ko loro jongrang bhi kehete hai. yah mandir durga ke is naam loro jongrang se bhi vikhyath he. bahut kaal tak yah mandir khandahar tha. punarotthana ke pashchaat yahaan ke java dweep ke kai parivaar waapas hindu dharm ko laut aaye hai.
