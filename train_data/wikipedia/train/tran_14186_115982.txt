 kisi ek bhashaa yaa anek bhashaaon ko likhane ke liye prayukt maanak pratikon ke krambaddh samooh ko varnmaala kehete hai. udhaaharan ke liye devanagari kii varnmaala main a aa i i u uu ri ri lri lri e i o ou an ah k kha ga gha ng. c chha ja jha na. t tha da dha na. t tha da dha na. pa pha ba bha m. ya r l va. sha sha sa ha ko 'devanagari varnmaala' kehete hai or a b c d ... z ko roman varnmaala kehete hai. 
 varnmaala is manyata par aadhaarit he ki varna, bhashaa main aane vaali mul dhvaniyon kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai. ye dhvaniyaan yaa to un aksharon ke vartmaan uccharan par aadhaarit hoti hai yaa phir aitihaasik uccharan par. kintu varnmaala ke alaava likhane ke anya tareeke bhi hai jaise shabd-chihn, silabari aadi. shabd-chihnan main prayek lipi chihn puure-ke-puure shabd, roopim yaa simantic ikai ko nirupit karata he. isi tarah silabari main prayek lipi chihn kisi akshar ) ko nirupit karata he. 
 anya vidhiyon main bhavchitron kaa istemaal hota he yaa phir chihn shabdaanshon ko darshaate hai. isi tarah, pracheen misri bhashaa ek chitralipi thi jisamen kisi varnmaala kaa prayog nahin hota tha kyonki uski lipi kaa har chihn ek shabd yaa avadharana darshata tha.
 prayek varnmaala main do prakaar ke varna hote hai swar varna tathaa vyanjan varna. vyanjanon ke saath swar lagane ke bhinn tareeqon ke aadhaar par varnmaalaon ko teen virgon main baanta jaataa hai:
 'varnmaala' ko angrezi main 'elfaabat' kehete hai. arabi, farsi, kurdi or madhya purv kii anya bhashaaon main ise 'alif-bei' yaa sirf 'alif-be' kehete hai .
