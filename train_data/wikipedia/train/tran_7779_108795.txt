 armeik ek semitic bhashaa he jo madhyapurv or uske uttari-kendriya bhaag main pichley 3000 saalon se boli ja rahi he. yah kai pracheen yahudee tathaa isaai granthon kii bhashaa he or mana jaataa he ki isa messiah kii maatrubhaasha aramike hi thi.
 yah bhashaa mritpraaya ho gai he. samraat ashok ke kai shilalekhon main aramike lipi kaa prayog hua he.
 armeik, semetic athwa sami bhashaa parivaar ke uttar pashchim bhaag kii ek prasiddh bhashaa he. armeik mul rup se philstin evam syria ke un pravaasiyon kii bhashaa thi or uttar main badhkar "aaram' arthath pahari pradesh main jaakar bas gaye. armeik kii hebrew se bahut adhik samanta he. armeik ke pracheen abhilekh damishq ke nikat i.pus. chhati shatabdi ke aasapaas ke milte hai.
 armeik kii mukhya do shakhaaen hai : purvi armeik, pashchimi armeik. 
 purvi armeik kii mukhya upbhaashaayen hai. : babylonian, beian, harnian evam siriak. siriak ko christini armeik bhi kehete hai kyonki is armeik main isaayiyon kaa dharmik sahitya likhaa gaya he. swayan isa bhi purvi armeik bolte the. purvi armeik kii uparyukt samast bhashaaen upbhaashaayen praaia: samaapt ho chuki hai. isaki kuch aadhunik upbhaashaon kaa prayog mesopotemia ke kuch bhaagon main hota he.
 pashchimi armeik i.pus. chauthi shatabdi se isa kii sawatin shatabdi tak pashchimi asia evam misr kii mukhya evam sampark bhashaa thi. pashchimi armeik kii mukhya upbhashaayen hai : pracheen armeik, bibly armeik, philstini armeik tathaa cemeriton armeik. pashchimi armeik main yahudiyon kii anek dharmik rachanaayen hai. pashchimi armeik kii uparyukt upbhaashaayen ek prakaar samaapt ho chali hai. isaki parvarti jivit upbhasha kaa prayog lebanom ke chote se bhaag main hota he.
