 aravali parvat shrunkhalaaon se ghira aitihaasik shahar bundi rajasthan ke hadoti anchal main sthit he. kaha jaataa he ki meena jati ke kisi 'bundi' naamak sardaar ne is shahar ko basaaya tha. sansthaapak ke naam par hi iskaa naam 'bundi' padaa.
 baad main hada shaasha raav deva ne boonda ko haraakar bundi par apana adhikaar kar liya phir ise apni rajdhani banaaya. san 1947 main bharat ke swatantra hone tak bundi ek swatantra riyasat ke rup main kaayam rahaa.
