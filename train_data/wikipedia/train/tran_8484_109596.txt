 vishnu puraana ke anusaar yah prithvi saat dwipon main banty hui he. ye saaton dweep chaaron ore se saat samudron se ghire hai. ye sabhi dweep ek ke baad ek duusare ko ghere hue bane hai or inhen ghere hue saaton samudr hai. dadhi kaa sagar kronchadweep ko ghere hue he. is sagar ko shakadweep ghere hue he.
