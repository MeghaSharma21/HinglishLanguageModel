 online kray, electronic vaanijya kaa ek vishesh rup he jisamen upabhokta vastu yaa sevaaon ko vikreta se sidhe internet ke madhyam se kharid saktaa he.
 haalaanki online kharidari saral or suvidhajanak lagati he, par ise kai baar sarkarein kar lagaakar or mahangaa kar detii hai. sitambar 2016 se uttar pradesh sarkaar online kharidari par 5% pravesh kar lagaaya he. iske peeche rajya sarkaar ko lagbhag 600 karod rupee salanaa rajasav laabh hone kaa anumaan lagaaya gaya he.
