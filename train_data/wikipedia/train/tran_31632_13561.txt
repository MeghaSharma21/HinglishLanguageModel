 sahchar poonjeewaad yaa suhrid poonjeewaad yaa kroni capitalism arthvyavastha kii us avasthaa kaa suchak he jahaan vyapaar-vaanijya main safalataa vyapaaree or sarkari adhikariyon ke aapasi sambandh se tay hone lagati he. iske tahat sarkari tantra dwaara vyapaariyon-udyogpatiyon ko kanoonee sweekruti ke aavanton main paksh lekar, unhen sarkari anudaan dekar, kar sambandhi sahuliyatein dekar tathaa anya aarthik aniyamittaaon ke zariye laabh pahunchaaya jaataa he. saarvajanik maanas patal par sahchar poonjeewaad ek pad ke taur par asian vittiya sankat kii vyakhya ke dauraan saamne aayaa.
