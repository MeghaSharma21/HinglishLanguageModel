 haihay puraanon main varnit bharat kaa ek pracheen rajvansh tha. harivansh puraana ke anusaar haihay, sahatrajit kaa pautra tathaa yadu kaa praputra tha. shrimadbhagwat mahapurana ke navam skandh main is vansh ke adhipati ke rup main arjuna kaa ullekh kiya gaya he.puraanon main haihay vansh kaa itihaas chandradev kii taeesvi peedi main utpann vitihotra ke samay tak paaya jaataa he. shrimadbhagwat ke anusaar brahma kii barahavi peedi main haihay kaa janm hua. harivansh puraana ke anusaar gyarahavi peedi main haihay teen bhai the jinamein haihay sabase chote bhai the. haihay ke shesh do bhai-mahahay evam venuhay the jinhone apane-apane naye vansho kii parampara sthapit kii.
