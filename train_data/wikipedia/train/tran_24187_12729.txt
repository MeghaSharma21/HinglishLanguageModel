 lagum ek prakaar kaa paudha yaa fal he jo fabasie yaa laguminose parivaar se sambandhit he. lagum kaa fal ek saadhaaran sa sookhaa fal he jo ek capel par viksit hota he or saamanytah do bhaagon main banta hota he. is prakaar ke falon ke liye ek anya naam he "pod", lekin yah naam anya prakaar ke falon ke liye bhi prayukt kiya jaataa he, jaise vanilla. kuchh prasiddh lagum hai, alphalapha, tipatiyaa ghaas, matar, been, masoor, meskuit, corob or moongfalee.
