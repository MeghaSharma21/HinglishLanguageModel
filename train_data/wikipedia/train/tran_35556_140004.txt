 mumbai samachar bharat main prakashit hone vaala gujarati bhashaa kaa asia ke sab se puraane vartmaanapatron main se ek or gujarati kaa pratham samachar patr he. iskaa mukhyalay mumbai main he. isvisan 1822 main iske prakaashan kii sharuaat hui thi. ahmedabad, vadodara, bangalore or nayi dilli main isaki shakhaayen hai. ye bharat sarkaar ke samachaarpatron ke panjiyak karyaalay dwaara arenai kramank se panjikrut he.
 1 may 1960 ko bhashaa ke aadhaar par gujarat or maharashtra rajyon ke vibhaajan se purv ye dono pradesh brihad mumbai rajya main shaamil the. gujarati or marathi dono bhashaa bolne vaale log mumbai main the. tab bharat kii azaadi se bhi purv ke kaal main 1 july 1822 ke din gujarati bhashaa main mumbai samachar dainik ke prakaashan kii sharuaat hui thi. yah asia kaa sab se purana or gujarati bhashaa kaa pratham akhbaar he. parsi parivaar ne iske prakaashan kii sharuaat kii thi. ye samachaarpatr 1832 tak ek saptahik, 1855 tak dwi-saptahik or uske baad dainik ke rup main prakashit ho rahaa he. iske sansthaapak parsi vidwaan fardoon ji ne 1822 main pratham deshi mudranalay kii sthapana kii thi or 1814 main gujarati panchang kaa isamen mudran karke prakashit kiya gaya tha. 1822 se bombe samachar kaa mudran bhi isi mudranalay main sharu kiya gaya.
 bharat ke mukti sangram main is akhbaar ne ahm bhoomika nibhaai he. mahatma gandhi, jawaharlal nehru, vallabh bhai patel ityadi swatantry senaniyon ne is akhbaar ko udrutt kiya he. gujarati bhashaa main patrakarita kii niv mumbai samachar ne rakhi. sthapana ke samay se hi sampaadan niti sansanikhej samachar dene kii nahin kintu ek nishpaksh or imaanadaar tareeke se ghatnaon ko prastut karne kii rahi he. prarambh se ajtak iskaa sanchaalan kama parivaar ke alag-alag vyaktiyon ke haathon main rahaa hai. aaj bhi ye samachaarpatr kaa prakaashan aadhunik mudranalay dwaara rangarang prarup main hota he.
