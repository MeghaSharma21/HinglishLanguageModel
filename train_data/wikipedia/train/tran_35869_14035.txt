 sayed mohammad jonpuri  ne makka main hijri varsh 901 main swayan ko imam mahadi ghoshit kiya. iske kaaran mahadavia main unhe adarpurvak dekha jaataa he. unka janm jonpur, uttarapradesh, bharat hua tha tathaa unhone puure bharat, arabi prayadweep or pracheen kuraasaan kaa bhraman kiya tha, jahaan 63 varsh kii aayu main faraah, afghanistan main unka nidhan ho gaya.
 jonpuri ne apane parivaar or anuyaayion ke ek chote dal ke saath jonpur choda. kai sthanon ko badalane or mahadavia ke nae saathiyon ke saath is dal ne faraah, afghanistan main pravesh kiya.
 53 varsh kii aayu main jonpuri ne hajj kii yatra ke liye makka kaa daura kiya. yaheen par 1496, main kaaba kaa tawaaf karne ke pashchaat jonpuri ne daawa kiya ki vahi bhavishyavaanee vaale mahadi hai or jo koi unhen maanata he vah momin he.
 jonpuri kii baton ko makka ke islamic vidwaanon andekha kiya. makka main lagbhag saat yaa aath maheene rahane ke baad jonpuri bharat laute jahaan unhone swayan ko ahmedabad main mahadi ghoshit kiya. iske baad unhone yahi daawa badhli main kiya jo patan, gujarat ke nikat he.
