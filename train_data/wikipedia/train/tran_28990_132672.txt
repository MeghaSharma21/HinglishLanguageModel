 kshobhi antra vikaar yaa iritable boul syndrome aanton kaa rog he jisamen pet main dard, bechaini va mel-nikaas main pareshaani aadi hote hai. ise spastic colon, iritable colon, mukas koiltis jaise naamon se bhi janaa jaataa he. yah aanton ko kharaab to nahin karata lekin uske sanket dene lagata he. purushon kii tulana main mahilaayein is beemaari se adhik prabhavit hoti hai.
 is rog kaa kaaran gyaat nahin he. kabj yaa atisar kii shikaayat ho sakati he yaa kabj ke baad atisar or uske baad kabj jaisi sthiti bhi dekhne ko milti he. 
 isaki koi chikitsa bhi nahin he. kintu kuch upachaar avashya hai jinke dwaara lakshanon se chhutkaaraa dilaane kii koshish kii jaati he, jaise bhojan main parivartan, dawa tathaa manovaigyanik salaah aadi.
