 nimi, mithila ke pratham raja the. ve manu ke pautra tathaa ikshvaku ke putr the.
 nimi ne vasishtha ko ruthvik bananaa chaha par vasishtha khaali nahin the. at: dusron ke acharyatv main yajna karana aarambh kiya. vasishtha ne isse cruddh hokar nimi ke deh kaa paat hone kaa shaap de diya. is deh ke manthan se janak kii utpatti hui or nimi swayan videh hokar praaniyon kii palkon main nivas karne lage. sanskrit main 'nimi' kaa arth 'palak' hota he.
