 adil mansoori ek prasiddh kavi, naatakkaar, or sulekhak tha. unhone kai bhashaaon, arthath, gujarati, hindi or urdu main likhaa.
 adil mansuri,janm samay naam farid mohammad gulam nabi mansuri, 18 may 1936 ko ahmedabad main hua tha. unhone premchand rychand training college, ahmedabad se apni praathmik shiksha puri kii or jiel niu english skool, ahmedabad or mahanagar hiskool, karachi se apni madhyamik shiksha puri kii. unhone kai vyavsaayon par apana haath ajamai. unhone karachi main or apane purvajon ke kapade kii dukaan par or baad main ahmedabad main kapaas or kapade ke vyavsaay main kaam kiya. unhone angreji topic or gujarati angana patrikaon ke saath patrkaar ke rup main kaam kiya. 1972 main vigyaapan agency shilpi kaa copiriter tha. baad main unhone bharat chhod diya or sanyukt rajya america chalaa gaya. 6 november 2008 ko sanyukt rajya america, new jersey main unki maut ho gai.
 unki ghazal kii prayogtmak roopon main dilchaspee thi. valank, pagarv or sataat unke ghazal sangrah hai. unhone kai anya roopon main kavita likhi he, lekin unhen mukhyatah apane gajalon ke liye janaa jaataa he. unki ghazal urdu ghazal se prabhavit hai. unhone gujarati, hindi or urdu main gajalen likhi or ek bhashaa ke shabdon kaa duusari main khul car istemaal kiya.
 haath pag bandhayela che or je nathi te unke absard ekanki naatakon ke sangrah hai.
