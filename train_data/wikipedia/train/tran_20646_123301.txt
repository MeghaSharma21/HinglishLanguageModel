 rafiq shaadaani ek bhartiya kavi the jo avadhi, hindi va urdu bhashaaon main likhte the. ve apane vyangy or katuphaas ke liye jane jate hai. madhya uttar pradesh kii lok-boli kaa prayog karne vaale rafiq shaadaani apane rajnatik vyangay ke liye bhi jane jate hai. unhone vyangy kii 13 pustaken likhi.
 rafiq shaadaani kaa parivaar uttar pradesh ke faizabad zile ke mumtaznagar gaanv se tha, lekin unke pita, imamuddin burma main tambaaku, tel va itr ke vyapaaree the. march 1934 main rafiq shaadaani kaa janm burma main hi hua. 1962 ke baad unhone apni vyangy shaayari mushaairon main padhani armbh kari. baharich main hui vaahan durghatana main 9 farvari 2010 main unak dehanth ho gaya.
