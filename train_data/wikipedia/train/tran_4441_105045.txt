 prakaash varsh lambaai kii maapan ikai he. yah lagbhag 950 kharab kilometre kii hoti he. yahaan ek trillian 1012 ke rup main liya jaataa he. anterrashtriya khagoleey sangh ke anusaar, prakaash varsh vah duuri he, jo prakaash dwaara nirvaat main, ek varsh main puri kii jaati he. yah lambaai maapne kii ek ikai he jise mukhyat: lambi duriyon yatha do nakshatron bich kii duuri yaa isi prakaar kii anya khagoleey duriyon ko maapne main prayog kiya jaataa he.
 ek prakaash varsh baraabar hota he:
 uparokt aankde julian varsh puure 365.25 diwason ke baraabar hota he, jaisaa ki IAU dwaara paribhaashit he.
 prakaash varsh kaa prayog praayah taron kii dooriyaan naapane hetu hota he. isaki adhimaanya ikai he parsac. parsac kii paribhaasha anusaar vah duuri he jo, jitani duuri par ek vastu digbhed ke ek arxecind ke baraabar hilti prathit hoti he, jab prekshak ek khagoleey ikai apni dashti rekha ke abhilamb chalata he. yah lagbhag 3.26 prakaash-varshon ke baraabar hota he. parsac ko anya ikaayiyon kii tulana main adhik saralata se paryvekshan aankado se milan or vyutpann kiya ja saktaa he. vaise vaigyanik varg main prakaash varsh hi adhik prachlit he.
