 saanchaa:gyanasandook korian naam
 hanja, chini varnon ke liye prayukt hone vaala korian shabd he. yah un shabdon ke liye prayukt hota he jo chini varnon se liye gaye hai or jo korian uccharan ke saath bole jate he. hanja mel yaa hanja-io ve shabd hai jo hanja ke saath likhe jate hai. hanmun kaa arth shastreey chini lekhan se he, lekin hanja ko kabhi-kabhaar hanmun ke liye bhi prayukt kiya jaataa he. hanja main bahut parivartan nahin aayaa he isliye bahut se chini varna vaise hi hai jaise kii parmparik chini varna. keval kuch hi hanja varna korea ke liye anupam hai.
 haalanki dashak 1440 main, dhwani par aadhaarit korian varnmaala kaa avishkaar, raja sejong mahaan ne kiya tha. sarvapratham iskaa bahut upyog nahin hua. tathaapi, praarambhik 19veen or 20veen sadi ke dauraan iskaa upyog hanja se adjik tha or yah ab korea kii aadhikarik bhashaa he.
 par tab tak, lagbhag har koi hanja likhnaa or padhanaa jaanata tha, isliye adhikansh pracheen korian pustaken hanja main likhi hui hai. jo vidhyaarthee korian itihaas kaa adhyan karte hai ve hja bhi sikhte hai taaki aitihaasik dastavezon ko padhaa ja sake. korea main aaj bhi bachche hanja sikhte hai or bahut se korian shabdon kii jade hanja main hi hai.
