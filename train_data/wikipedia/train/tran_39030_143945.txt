 rura :uttar pradesh rajya ke kanpur dehaat jile main adarsh nagar panchayat he. is nagar se hokar uttar madhya railway line guzarati he tathaa nagar ke uttari kinare nichali ganga nahar bahati he. bhatauli is nagar se mila hua he jise bhatauli -rura kehete hai. is nagar se akbarpur kii duuri 15 kilometre he jahaan kanpur dehaat kaa jila mukhyalay he. yah nagar road dwaara derapur ,jhinjhak or mahanagar kanpur judaa hua he. 
 rura railway stesan kanpur dehaat jile kaa pramukh stesan he. yah purv main kanpur ,mughalsarai ,patna, haavda aadi nagaron se tathaa pashchim main itawa ,agra ,dilli ,meerut aadi nagaro se judaa hua he. yahaan kai fast evam super fast express gaadiyaan ruukti hai 
 yah nagar rajkeey parivahan seva dwaara mahanagar kanpur ,akbarpur ,jhinjhak ,rasulabad,bilhor ,derapur,shivali aadi sthanon se judaa hua he.
