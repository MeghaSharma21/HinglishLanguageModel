 marcides banj W212 purv W211 model kii jagah bechi gai nauveen peedi kii i-class car he. CLK-class coop or cabriolate kaa ek pratisthaapan, C207 sambandhit i-class coop kaa chasis code he. nai i class kii bikri europe main march main or america main july 2009 main shuru kii gai. 
 W212 or C207 i-class kii digine canceptphasination dizin adhyayan se prerit hai. bahari style ke kuch tatwon ko bhi purvavarti W204 C-class or W221 S-class se liya gaya he. pichley pahiye ke ubhare mehrab 1950 kii aitihaasik marcides model "panton" kii phender flaurs kii yaad taaja karaati hai. 
 i-class salon kaa anaavaran uttari america antarrashtriya auto sho 2009 main kiya gaya. nayaa salon europe main bikri ke liye march 2009 main taiyaar ho gaya jabaki 2010 automobile model varsh tha. nai i-class main chaalak thakaan sansuchan, gali-prasthaan chetavanee or yatayaat sanket pahchaan sahit kai nai suraksha praudyogic suvidhaayen hai. isamen kai aisi or suvidhaayen bhi shaamil hai jo iske pradarshan, dakshata or aaraam main sudhaar karti hai jaise ki "direct control" anukuli nilamban, sakriy siten or gatiman radiator luvers jo vaahan kii vayugatiki ko unnat karti hai. maanak marcides-banj kii suraksha or suvidhaayen jaise ki electronic sthirataa program, airbag or saath hi swat: jalwaayu niyantran shaamil hai.
 i-class coop kaa anaavaran geneva motor sho 2009 main kiya gaya or isaki bikri may 2009 main shuru kii gai. C207 i-class coop W212 motor se sambandhit he, iske lagbhag 60 pratishat yantric sanghatak ek jaise hai. i-class coop main bhi kai prakaar kii nai or maanak suvidhaayen maujuud hai.
 estate sanskaran kaa anaavaran francfort motor sho 2009 main kiya gaya or 5 agast 2009 ko isaki bikri shuru kii gai. shuruaati model main chaar-silendar-sanchalit E 220 CDI blue efficienci or E 250 CDI blue efficienci, V6-sanchalit E 350 CDI blue efficienci or E 350 CGI blue efficienci or V8-sanchalit E 500 shaamil hai. america model kii bikri model varsh 2011 ke liye june 2010 main shuru ho jaaegi.
 A207 cabriolate kaa anaavaran 11 janvari 2010 ko uttari america international auto sho, datroit, michigan, sanyukt rajya america main kiya gaya tha. isaki bikri europe main 27 march 2010 ko or sanyukt rajya america main may 2010 ko shuru kii gai.
 sanyukt rajya america main model E350 or E550 sanskaran uplabdh hai. cabriolate ke america sanskaran main airskarf pranali or nai aircap windshield spoilar system, cabin main ksheen draft or hawa ke shor ko kam karne ke liye vikshepit hawa kii suvidha he.
 australia main, 1.8 litre 4 silendar E250 CGI kaa ek model saath hi saath 2.2 litre 4 silendar dijal ingine jise E250 CDI ke naam se parichit model bhi uplabdh he. E250 model par airskarf or aircap ke vikalp bhi hai.

AMG C63 kii tarah hi, nai E63 ko nai vyapak front eksal, atyadhik rup se sanshodhit suspension or adhik vishisht body panelon ke saath pehle se adhik bade paimaane par sanshodhit kiya gaya. 
 vaikalpik AMG pradarshan package main halke 19 inch AMG mishrit dhaatu ke pahiyon ke saath 255/35R19 saamne or 285/30R19 pichley tair, majboot suspension, limited-slip diphrencial, vishesh thri-spoke stearing wheel or electronic top speed limetre 300 kimi/ghanta se 250 kimi/ghanta tak kii programing shaamil kii gai.
