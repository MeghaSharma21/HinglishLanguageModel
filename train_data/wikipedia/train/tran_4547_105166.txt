 jab twachaa main khokhli sui chubhokar pichakaari kii sahaayta se sharir ke bheetar koi drav pravisht karaayaa jaataa he to use sui lagaana kehete hai. sui se pravisht karaaye gayi drav kaa asar keval sthaneey na hokar us puri pranali main asar karata he. 
 vastut: sui lagane kii kai vidhiyaan hai. jaise intradermal, sabakutanius, mansapeshiyon main, nason yaa shiraaon main ityadi.
