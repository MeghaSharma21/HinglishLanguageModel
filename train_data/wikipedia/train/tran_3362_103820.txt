 shri jeev goswami, vrundavan main chaitanya mahaprabhu dwaara bheje gaye chah shangoswami main se ek the. unki gananaa gaudeey sampradaay ke sabase mahaan darshanikon evam santon main hoti he. unhone bhakthi yoga, vaishnav veydant aadi par anekon granthon kii rachana kii. ve do mahaan santon rup goswami evam sanathan goswami ke bhattije the. 
 inkaa janm shri vallabh anupam ke yahaan 1533 i0 (tad.1455 shaq. bhadrapada shukla dwadashi ko hua tha. shri sanathan goswami, shri rup goswami tathaa shri vallabh, ye teenon bhai nawab hossain shah ke darbar main ucch padasin the. in teenon main se ek ke hi santan thi, shri jeev goswami. badshaah kii sevaaon ke badale in logon ko acha bhugtan hota tha, jiske kaaran inkaa jivan atyant sukhmay tha. or inke ekmaatr putr ke liye kisi vastu kii kami na thi. shri jeev ke chehre par suvarna abha thi, inaki ankhen camel ke samaan thim, va inke ang-pratyang main chamak nikalati thi.
 shri gaur sudarshan ke ramkeli aane par shri jeev ko unke darshan hue, jabaki tab jeev ek chote balak hi the. tab mahaprabhu ne inke bare main bhavishyavaanee kii ki yah balak bhavishya main gaudeey sampradaay kaa prachaarak va guru banegaa. baad main inke pita va chaachaaon ne mahaprabhu ke sevarth inako parivaar main chhodkar prasthaan kiya. 
 inhen jab bhi unki yaa gaurang ke charnon kii smriti hoti thi, ye moorchhit ho jate the. baad main logon ke suzav par ye navadweep main mayapur pahunche va shrinivas pandit ke yahaan, nityanand prabhu se bhent kii. phir ve donon shachi mata se bhi mili. phir nity ji ke aadeshaanusaar inhone kaashi ko prasthaan kiya. vahaan shri rup goswami ne inhen shrimadbhagvat kaa paath karaayaa. or antatah ye vrundavan pahunche. vahaan inhone ek mandir bhi banwaaya.
 1540 shaq shukla tritiya ko 85 varsh kii aayu main inhone deh tyaagi.
