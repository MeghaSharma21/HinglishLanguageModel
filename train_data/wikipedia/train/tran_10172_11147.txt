 lakshmikutti bhartiya rajya kerala ke tiruvanantpuram main kalar jangalon main rahane vaali ek adivasee mahila hai jinhen parmparik dawaaiyon ke kshetra main saraahneey kaary ke liye bharat ke chauthe sarvocch sammaan padam shri se sammanit kiya gaya. lakshmikutti parmparik dawaaiyon par kaam karne vaali prasiddh vishaarogysadhak hai. unhen kerala sarkaar kii taraf se 1995 main nattu vaidya ratna puraskaar bhi mila tha. wo parmparik dawaaiyon par pichley 50 varsh se kaam kar rahi hai.
