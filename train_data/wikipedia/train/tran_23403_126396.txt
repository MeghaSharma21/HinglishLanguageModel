 shri guru teg bahadur ji kaa yah gurudwara mirzapur jile sthit varanasi ke dakshin se 40 kilometre kii duuri par ahrud gaanv main sthit he. yah gurudwara nauven sikh guru, teg bahadur ko samarpit he. yah gurudwara, gurudwara baag sahib ke naam se prasiddh he. mana jaataa he ki 1666 main varanasi kii yatra ke dauraan guru ji is jagah par aae the. is gurudware main ek vargakar haul or kai chote-chote kamren hai. gurudware kii imaarat behad khubasurat he. gurudware ke thik peeche ek chota sa bagichaa sthit he. 1742 main prakashit pavitra guru granth sahib kii hastlipi aaj bhi yahaan sangrakshit he. iske atirikt, gurudwara baag sahib main haath se likhi hui pothee, jisapar guru govind singh ke hastakshar hue hai, maujuud he. yah pothee logon ke saamne keval guru teg bahadur or guru govind singh kii jainti par hi pradarshit kii jaati he.
