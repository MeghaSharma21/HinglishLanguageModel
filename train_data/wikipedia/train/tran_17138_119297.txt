 turki, tab otoman samrajya ne pehle 1908 main olympic khelon main bhaag liya or tab se athleates ko greeshmakaleen olympic khelon ke adhikansh sanskarnon main pratispardhaa karne ke liye bheja he. turki ne sabase sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main bhi bhaag liya he kyonki 1936 main unki pehali upasthiti thi.
 turki athleton ne kul 91 padak jite hai, jo 39 swarna, 24 rajat or 28 kaansya padak main vibhajit he. kushti main turki ne sabase zyada padak jeeta.
 turki ke liye rashtriya olympic samiti, turki rashtriya olympic samiti 1908 main banaai gai thi or 1911 main manyata praapt thi.
