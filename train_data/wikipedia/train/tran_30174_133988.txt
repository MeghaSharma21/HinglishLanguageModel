 advait veydant veydant kii ek shakha.ahan brahmasmiadvait vedant yah bharat main pratipaadit darshan kii kai vichaardhaaraon main se ek he, jiske aadi shankaracharya puraskarta the. bharat main parabrahm ke swaroop ke bare main kai vichaardhaaraein hain. jisamen dwait, advait yaa keoladvait, vishishtadvait, shuddhadvait, dwaitadvait jaisi kai saidhantik vichaardhaaraein hai. jis acharya ne jis rup main brahm ko janaa uskaa varnan kiya. itani vichaardhaaraein hone par bhi sabhi yah maanate he ki bhagwaan hi is sristi kaa niyantaa he. advait vichaardhaara ke sansthaapak shankaracharya hai, jise shankaradvait yaa keoladvait bhi kaha jaataa he. shankaracharya maanate hain ki sansar main brahm hi satya he. baaki sab mithyaa he . jeev keval agyaan ke kaaran hi brahm ko nahin jaan paata jabaki brahm to uske hi andar virajman he. unhone apane brahmasutra main "ahan brahmasmi" aisa kahkar advait siddhant bataaya he. vallabhacharya apane shuddhadvait darshan main brahm, jeev or jagat, teenon ko satya maanate hai, jise vedon, upanishdon, brahmasutra, gita tathaa shrimadbhagwat dwaara unhone siddh kiya he. advait siddhant charachar sristi main bhi vyaapt he. jab pair main kaanta chubhta he tab aakhon se pani aata he or haath kaanta nikaalaneke liye jaataa he. ye advait kaa ek uttam udhaaharan he.
 shankaracharya kaa ‘ecobrahm, dwitiyao naasti’ mat tha. sristi se pehle parambrahm vidyaman the. brahm sat or sristi jagat asat he. shankaracharya ke mat se brahm nirgun, niskriy, sat-asat, kaary-kaaran se alag indriatit he. brahm ankhon se nahin dekha ja saktaa, man se nahin janaa ja saktaa, vah gyaata nahin he or na gyey hi he, gyaan or kriya ke bhi ateet he. maaya ke kaaran jeev ‘ahan brahm’ kaa gyaan nahin kar paata. aatma vishuddh gyaan swaroop niskriy or anant he, jeev ko yah gyaan nahin rahata.
