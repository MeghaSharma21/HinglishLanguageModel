 bandol, bharat ke rajya madhyapradesh ke antargat siwani jila se 16 kilometre uttar disha kii ore rashtriya rajmarg kramank 7 jabalpur road par sthit hain is gaanv kaa bhogolia kshetrafal 706.95 hectare he yah ek bady gram panchayat hain. ise bandol yaa bandol naam se bhi janaa jaataa he.bandol gram panchayat main audyogic ikai ke rup main pashu aahaar sanyantr he jo sanchi dugdh sheet kendra ke saath yahan sthapit kiya gaya he. 1 kilometre kii duuri par shrivani jal shodhan plant yahan sthapit he.bandol kaa yah sampurna kshetra sarvaadhik stone kressure hone ke kaaran zyada janaa jaataa he isaki gittiyaan aas-paas ke jile khaskar balaghat jile main zyada pahuchaayi jaati hain.gaanv kii saptaahik bazaar kaa din guuruuvaar hain. bandol gaanv se 2 kilometre pashchim disha kii ore vinganga nadi pravaahit hoti he.
 bandol gram main vishwa prasiddh mata katyayani kaa mandir he. navaratri ke samay shashthee puja ke din kunvaari kanyaon dwaara puja karne kaa vishesh mahatv he. manyata ke anusaar kunvaari kanya dwaara shashti puja vidhi vidhaan se karne par manabhaavan var kii prapti hoti he.
 yah siwani jila kaa ek maatr pashu aahaar sanyantr he, jo sanchi dugdh sheet kendra ke saath yahan sthapit he.
 yah pratikshalay hair secondari skool ke sameep bakhari road par panchaayat dwaara nirmaan kiya gaya hain.
 shaskeey praathmik shaala madhyam hindi/ angreji gaanv main pehla prathamik skool san 1922 main kholaa gaya jise aaj shaskeey praathmik shaala ke naam se janaa jaataa hain jo aaj bhi sanchalit ho rahaa hain. yah sabase purana sarkari skool hain.
 yah ucchtar vidhyalay hone se gaanv baccho ke saath saath aas-paas ke gaanv ke bachche bhi yah adhyayan karne aate hain.
