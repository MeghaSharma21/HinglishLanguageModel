 aparajita windos vista, windos 7 tathaa windos sarwar 2008 main prayukt ek opentip unicode hindi font he. yah sarvapratham windos vista main aayaa tha.
 aparajita sundar banaavat vaala font he jo ki graphics kaaryon hetu upyukt he haalaanki yah chote font aakaar main spasht nahin dikhataa. yah bade font aakaar main sheershak aadi likhane hetu upyukt he.
 aparajita main devanagari ke kuch sanyuktakshron ke varnkhand nahin hai jaise shri, k aadi jis kaaran yah sanskrit taxt ke pradarshan evam mudran hetu upyukt nahin.
