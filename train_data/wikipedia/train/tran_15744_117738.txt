 sthanang sutara jain granth he jo pratham gyaarah aagmon main se ek he. isamen prayukt bhashaa ardhamagadhi he. iske mul sutron ko tikaa ke bina samajhnaa bahut kathin he. isaliye 11veen shatabdi main abhayadevsuri ji ne sthanang sutara par sanskrit main ek paribhaashik granth kii rachana kii. yah granth jain tatvamimasaa kaa 'ang-granth' he. kintu isamen ganit ke bahut se vishay, vidhiyaan or sankriyaayein di gayi hai.
 sthanang sutara main ganit ke un vishyon kii suchi vidyaman he jo isa kii duusari shatabdi se bhartiya samaaj main adhyayan kii jaatin thi.
