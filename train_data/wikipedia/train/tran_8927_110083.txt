 avantisundari katha sanskrit sahitya ke gadhykavy ke antargat ek mahatvapurn kathaprabandh he. vidwaanon ne ise acharya dandi kii kriti mana he or inaki teesri rachana ke rup main isi prabandh ko manyata di he. dandi ke kavyadarsh kii tikaa janghal ne ise dandi kii rachana kaha he.
 dandi ke aavirbhaavkaal kii sambhaavna vidwaanon ne 500 i. se 800 i. ke bich kii he. pracheen granthon kii khoj main avantisundari katha kii ek apurna prati uplabdh hui thi. em. aar. kavi naamak ek vidvan ne iskaa sampadan karke san 1924 i. main ise prakashit karavaaya or pusht pramaanon ke aadhaar par ise dandi kii rachana bataaya. iskaa kathanak kavikalpit he; jaisaa kathaprabandh ke liye aavashyak he. iskaa kathanak dandi ke dashakumarcharit kii bhaanti hi he. rajkumaron or avantisundari nayika kii katha ke vyaaj se isamen tatkaaleen samaaj kaa yathatathy chithran uplabdh hota he. gadhyshailee kii drushti se yah katha prabandh ek mahatvapurn kriti he or sanskrit gadhykavy kii style ke vikascram main ek nishchit sopan ke rup main mana jaataa he.
