 dharm kaa arth hota he, dhaaran, arthath jise dhaaran kiya ja sake, dharm ,karm pradhan he. gunon ko jo pradarshit kare vah dharm he. dharm ko gun bhi kah sakate hai. yahan ullekhneey he ki dharm shabd main gun arth keval maanav se sambandhit nahin. padaarth ke liye bhi dharm shabd prayukt hota he yatha pani kaa dharm he bahna, agni kaa dharm he prakaash, ushma dena or sampark main aane vaali vastu ko jalaana. vyapaktaa ke drushtikon se dharm ko gun kahana sajiv, nirjeev donon ke arth main nitant hi upyukt he. dharm saarvbhaumik hota he. padaarth ho yaa maanav puri prithvi ke kisi bhi kone main baithe maanav yaa padaarth kaa dharm ek hi hota he. uske desh, rang rup kii koi baadha nahin he. dharm saarvkalik hota he yani ki prayek kaal main yug main dharm kaa swaroop vahi rahata he. dharm kabhi badalata nahin he. udhaaharan ke liye pani, agni aadi padaarth kaa dharm sristi nirmaan se aaj paryant samaan he. dharm or sampradaay main moolbhoot antar he. dharm kaa arth jab gun or jivan main dhaaran karne yogya hota he to vah prayek maanav ke liye samaan hona chahie. jab padaarth kaa dharm saarvbhaumik he to maanav jati ke liye bhi to isaki saarvbhaumikta honi chahie. atah maanav ke sandarbh main dharm kii baat karen to vah keval maanav dharm he. hindu, muslim, isai, jain yaa buddha aadi dharm na hokar sampradaay yaa samudaay maatr hai. “sampradaay” ek parampara ke maanane waalon kaa samooh he.
  bhartiya samskruti or darshan kii pramukh sankalpana he. 'dharm' shabd kaa pashchimi bhashaaon main koi tulya shabd paanaa bahut kathin he. saadhaaran shabdon main dharm ke bahut se arth hai jinamein se kuch ye hai- kartavya, ahimsa, nyaay, sadacharan, sad-gun aadi.
 sanathan dharm main chaar purusharth sweekaar kiye gayi hai jinamein dharm pramukh he. teen anya purusharth ye hai- arth, kaam or moksh.
 gautam rishi kehete hai - 'yato abhyudaynishreyas siddhih sa dharm.' 
 manu ne maanav dharm ke das lakshan bataaye hai:
 jo apane anukool na ho vaisaa vyavhaar duusare ke saath nahin karnaa chaiye - yah dharm kii kasuti he. 
 uparokt maanav dharm kaa paalan karne vaala hindu samudaay vishwa ke sabhi bade samudaayon main sabase purana he. yah samudaay pehle arya naam se sambodhit kiya jaataa tha. is samudaay kii kai manyataayen vedon par aadhaarit he, yah samudaay apane andar kai alag alag upasna paddatiyaan, mat, sampradaay ko maanane waalon ko samete hue hai. ye duniya kaa teesraa sabase bada samudaay he, par iske zyaadaatar log bharat main hai or vishwa kaa sabase adhik hinduon kaa pratishat nepal main he. haalaanki is samudaay ke logon dwaara kai devi-devataaon kii puja kii jaati he, lekin asal main ye akeshwarwadi dharm he.
 is samudaay ke log swayan ko sanathan dharm athwa vaidic dharm maanane vaala kehete hai. indonesia main is dharm kaa aupchaarik naam "hindu aagam" he. hindu keval ek sampradaay hi nahin he apitu jivan jeene kii ek paddati he. logon kaa maananaa he ki "hinsaayam dooyate yaa sa hindu" arthath jo apane man vachan karm se hinsa se dur rahe vah hindu he.
 vatsyayan ne dharm or adharm kii tulana karke dharm ko spasht kiya he. vatsyayan maanate hai ki maanav ke liye dharm mansa, vaachaa, karmana hota he. yah keval kriya yaa karmon se sambandhit nahin he balki dharm chintan or vaani se bhi sambandhit he.
