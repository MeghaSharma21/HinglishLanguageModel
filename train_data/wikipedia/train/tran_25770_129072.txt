 vidyadhar chandel vansh kaa sabase pratapi raja tha jisane khajuraho kaa kandaria mahadev mandir banwaaya tha. musalmaan lekhak usko 'chandra' evam 'vidaa' naam se pukaarte hai. vah apane dada ke saamaan vir or kusal shaasha tha. ali ebn ul-atahar ke anusaar vidyadhar ke rajya seemaa kii seemaa bharat main sabase badi thi.
 uske samay chandelon ne kalchuri or parmaron par vijay pai or 1019 tathaa 1022 main mahmud kaa muqabla kiya. chandel rajya kii seemaa vistrut ho gai thi. vidyadhar ke baad chandel rajya kii kirti or shakti ghatane lagi.
