 sachindra prasad bose  ek swatantraaa senaanee or shri surendranath bannerjee ke anuyaayi the.ve brahm samaaj ke udaaravaadi netaa, shri krishna kumaar mitra ke damaad the. 
 vah ripan college, kalakatta ke chauthe varsh ke chaatr the jab 4 november 1905 ko, unhone ardblyoo bengal sarkaar ke tatkaaleen mukhya sachiv, aradbalu kayeel dwaara jari kiye gaye paripatr ke virodh main paripatr-virdohi socity kaa gathan kiya. kayeel ke us paripatr main bengal sarkaar ke magistriton or colectoron ko raajnneeti main shaamil chaatro ke khilaaf bahut sakht kadam uthaane ke nirdesh diye gaye the. unhone kaha ki iske sachiv bane or krishna kumaar mitra iske adhyaksh bane.
 unhone kolkata dhvaj naam se prasiddh dhvaj ko banaaya tha or 7 agast 1906 ko kalakatta ke parsi bagan chowk par fahraaya tha. 1908 main, unhen giraftaar kiya gaya or rawalpindi jail bhej diya gaya. baad main, unhone "vyavsaa o vaanijya" naam kii ek patrika ke sampadak ke rup main kaam kiya.
