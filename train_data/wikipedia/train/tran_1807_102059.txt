 bhartiya samskruti main rajarshi aise raja ko kehete hai jo rishi bhi ho. duusare shabdon main, vah rishi jo rajvansh yaa kshatriya kul kaa ho. jaise,— rajarshi vishwamitra.
 rishi saat prakaar ke maane gaye hai-
 ek pad aise saat rishiyon kaa mana gaya he jo kalpant pralayon main vedon ko rakshit rakhte hai. bhinn bhinn manavataron main saptarshi ke antargat bhinn bhinn rishi maane gayi hai. jaise, is vaiwaswath manwantar ke saptarshi ye hai—kashyap, atri, vashishtha, vishwamitra, gautam, jamadagni or bharadwaj. swyambhuv manwantar ke— marichi, atri, angira, pulasthya, pulah, kratu or vashishtha.
