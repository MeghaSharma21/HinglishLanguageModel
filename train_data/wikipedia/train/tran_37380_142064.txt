 vijay or sani do dost hai, jo ek duusare ke bahut kareeb hai. ve college main ek saath padhate hai or yahaan tak ki ek hi ladki, priya se pyaar karte hai. sani samrudh or prabhaavshaalee he or ise niyantrit karnaa or haaavee hona pasand he. vijay ek garib parivaar se aata he. sani ek kathputli ke rup main vijay kaa upyog kar college ko niyantrit karnaa chahta he, jo sani ko pasand nahin aata he. natijatan, sani ke pita, jo college ke hisse ke maalik hai, unke or sani kii sthiti par jor dene ke liye kadam uthaate hai. saath hi saath college adhikariyon par dabaav daalate hai. duusari taraf vijay ko sani ko dhokhaa dene ke liye sabak sikhaane kaa faisla karte hai. lekin sani apane tareeke se badla lenaa chahta he.
