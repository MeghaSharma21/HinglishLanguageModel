 jwalamukheey cretar zamin main ek gol aakaar kaa gaddha hota he jo kisi jwalamukhi ke fatne se ban jaataa he. aam taur se is gadde kaa farsh samtal hota he or usamen ek chhed se pighale pathar, gas or anya jwalamukheey padaarth ugalate hai. kai dafaa jwalamukhi ke andar lava se bhari hui gupha khaali ho jane se uski chat baith jaati he or ek cretar-numa gaddha bana detii he, par yah cretar nahin balki ek jwalamukheey kund yaa "caldera" kehalaata he.
 angrezi main jwalamukheey cretar ko "wolkainic cretar" kehete hai.
