 sigma sagitteri jiske bair namakan main bhi yahi naam darj he, akaash main dhanu taramandal kaa doosraa sab se roshan tara he or prithvi se dikhne vaale taron main se 52vaan sab se roshan tara he. yah hamase 228 prakaash-varsh kii duuri par sthit he or prithvi se iskaa ausat saapeksh kantimaan 2.06 he.
 sigma sagitteri ko angrezi main "nanaki" bhi kaha jaataa he jo asyrian yaa babylonian bhashaaon se liya gaya he or jiskaa arth "dharti kaa rajkumaar" tha. yah naam itihaaskaaron ko khudaai karne par babylonian puraatan sthalon par mila or taare ke varnan se andaaza lagaaya gaya ke yah sigma sagitteri ko babylonion dwaara diya gaya naam hi rahaa hoga. bhartiya samskruti main zeta sagitteri or sigma sagitteri ke do taare uttarasadha nakshatra banaate hai.
 sigma sagitteri kaa mukhya tara ek nile-safed rang vaala B2.5 V shrenee kaa tara he. isaki andruni chamak hamaare suraj kii 3300 guna he. iskaa vyas hamaare suraj ke vyas kaa 5 guna or iskaa dravyaman suraj ke dravyaman kaa lagbhag 7 guna he. sambhav he ki iske saath ek saathi tara bhi ho jiske saath yah ek dwitara mandal ho lekin vaigyanikon ko yah pakkaa gyaat nahin he.
 akaash main sigma sagitteri suryapath ke bahut paas dikhataa he or kabhi-kabhi chandramaa ke peeche chup ho jaataa he. bahut hi lambe antaraalon ke baad kabhi-kabhaar yah saura mandal ke kisi grah ke peeche bhi chup jaataa he. 17 navambar 1981 kii raat ko yah shukr ke peeche chupa tha. yah mangal ke peeche bhi chup saktaa he lekin pichhali dafaa yah aaj se sainkadon saal purv san 423 isvi kii 3 sitambar kii tithi ko mangal dwaara chupaaya gaya tha.
