 lee liyanji, janm 26 april 1963), yaa jinhen unke anya naam jet lee se janaa jaataa he, ek film abhinayta, film nirmaataa, chini marshall artist, vushu chapian va antarrashtriya sitare he jo bejing main janme the or jinhone chini rashtriyata 2009 main tyaag di thi.
 teen varshon tak vu bin main prashikshan ke baad lee ne apni pehali rashtri championships bejing vushu team ke liye jiti. 17 kii umr main vushu se nivritti ke baad ve china main shaolin temple film se shuruyaat karke ek lokapriy abhinayta ban gaye. unhone aage chalakar kai safal marshall arts filmon main abhinay kiya, jiname mukhya rup se vans apon a time in china shrunkhlaa shaamil he jisamen unhone lokkatha ke nayak vong phei-hang kii bhoomika nibhaai thi.
 lee kaa hollywood filmon main pehla kirdaar leethal vepan 4 main ek nakaaratmak bhoomika ke rup main tha parantu unka mukhya paatr ke rup main kirdaar romeo must daai main tha. iske baad unhone kai hollywood filmon main kaary kiya jiname kis of da dragon or anlishd shaamil he. unhone da forbydan kingdom main jaiki chaiyn ke saath, da ekpendables main sylvester stalon ke saath or da mummy: tomb of da dragon emperor main brendan freizer ke saath abhinay kiya he. ve hong kong kii film osian hewan main bhi nazar aae jiskaa lekhan va nirdesan kaksu xiaolu ne kiya he.
