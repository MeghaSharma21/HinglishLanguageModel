 wiliam bouman amareeki sena main sharirkriya vaigyanik the jo 'jathar kriyavigyaan ke janak' kahe jate hai. inhone maanav ke pachanatantr par bahut mahatvapurn kaary kiya tha.
 inkaa janm krishak parivaar main hua tha. yah kushagrabuddhi balak aage chalakar prasiddh vaigyanik hua. chikitsa vigyaan kii shiksha inhone vaiyaktik rup se ek chikitsak se pai or varmaant rajya kii tritiy medical sosayity se chikitsavriti kaa license praapt kiya. baad main ye amareeki sena main sarjan ke pad par niyukt ho gaye.
 sharirarachana or uske kaary se sambandhit anek baaten un dinon agyaat thim. boumeinn ne anusandhan kiya or bataaya ki amashay ke pachak ras kya kaary karte hai or kab tak kin avasthaaon main yah ras nahin banataa. bouman ne pachan ke rasinic rup kii sutaraman sthapana kii. in kaaryon kii unke shodhaprabandh experiments and abzaraveshans main vistaar se charchaa he. sharir kriya vigyaan main bouman kaa anudaan mahatvapurn he. inhone prayog or avlokan ko nai disha pradaan kii.
