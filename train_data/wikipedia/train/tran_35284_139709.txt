 captain pushpendra kumaar garg naukayan main ek bhartiya khilaadi he. inkaa janm uttar pradesh ke aligarh jile ke saasni main hua tha. inhone bhartiya nausena main shaamil hone se pehle sainik skool, lakhanu or rashtriya raksha academy, kharkwasla main adhyayan kiya. inhen 1993-94 main rajeev gandhi khel ratna puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha.
