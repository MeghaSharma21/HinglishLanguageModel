 maala sinha hindi filmon kii ek abhinetri hai. ve nepali-bhartiya hai tathaa unhone hindi ke alaava bangala or nepali filmon main bhi kaam kiya he. ve apni pratibha or sundarata donon ke liye jaani jaati hai. ve 1950 ke dashak se armbh karke 1970 ke dashak tak hindi filmon kii pramukh abhinetri thim. unhone sau se adhik filmon main kaary kiya he jinamein se pramukh hai- pyaas, dhul ke phool, anpadh, dil tera divaana .
