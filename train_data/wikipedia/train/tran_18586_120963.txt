 hin grah paripatr ek vaigyanik patrika he jo har poornima par hin grah kendra dwaara prakashit kii jaati he. isamen khagolashastreey samiksha, hin grahon va dhoomaketuon kii parikrama kakshaon va panchang se sambandhit jaankaree, or kuch prakritik upgrahon se sambandhit jaankaree bhi sammilit hoti he. naye khoje gayi kshudragrah, dhoomaketu va anya hin grah yaa unki kakshaon ke bare me gyaat nai jaankaree bhi isamen shaamil kii jaati he.
