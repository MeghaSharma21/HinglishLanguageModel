 yoga ke sandarbh main naadee vah maarg he jisase hokar sharir kii urja pravaahit hoti he. yoga main yah mana jaataa he ki nadiyaan sharir main sthit naadeechakron ko jodatiin he.
 kai yoga granth 10 naadiyon ko pramukh maanate hai. inamen bhi teen kaa ullekh baar-baar miltaa he - ida, pingala or sushumna. ye teenon merudand se jude hai. iske alaave gandhari - bain aankh se, hastijihva dahini aankh se, pusha daahine kaan se, yashaswini baane kaan se, alambusha mukh se, kuhu jananaangon se tathaa shankhini guda se judi hoti he. anya upanishad 14-19 mukhya naadiyon kaa varnan karte hai. 
 ida rinatmak urja kaa vaah karti he. shiv swaroday, ida dwaara utpaadit urja ko chandramaa ke sadrishy maanata he atah ise chandranadi bhi kaha jaataa he. isaki prakruti sheethal, vishramdaayak or chitt ko antarmukhi karanevaali maani jaati he. iskaa udgam muladhar chakra mana jaataa he - jo merudand ke sabase neeche sthit he.
 pingala dhanatmak urja kaa sanchaar karti he. isko suryanadi bhi kaha jaataa he. yah sharir main josh, shramashakti kaa vahan karti he or chetana ko bahirmukhi banaati he. 
 pingala kaa udgam muladhar ke daahine bhaag se hota he jabaki pingala kaa baayein bhaag se.
