 shtetin poland kaa mukhya bandargah he. yah order nadi ke baayein kinare par shaigesin kii khadi se 27.2 kilometre or baltic sagar se 64 kilometre andar sthit he. yah pomarenia kii pracheen rajdhani tha.
 shahar kot ke bheetar saint jon or saint james ke prasiddh girjaghar vidyaman the, jo dwitiya vishwayuddh main nasht kar die gaye. mahan fredrick dwaara nirmit shaharkot 1874 main tod diya gaya, lekin uske vishaal dwaara abhi bhi vidyaman hai. pracheen nagar kaa lagbhag 80 pratishat or naveen nagar kaa lagbhag 50 pratishat bhaag dwitiya vishwayuddh main nasht ho gaya tha. yah nagar san 1648 se 1720 tak sweden ke adhikaar main rahaa, san 1720 se 1945 tak prashia kaa bhaag rahaa tathaa 1945 i. ko potsdam sandhi ke baad yah poland main mil gaya. tabhi se nagar ke punarnirmaan evam naveen vikaas kaa kaary teji se niojit dhang par ho rahaa he. yah loha engeeniri, vastra, rasayan, cement, sabun tel, kaagaj or chini udyog kaa centre he. yahan se chini, sharaab, khadyann, aalu evam aate kaa niryaat kiya jaataa he tathaa loha, soda, potash, kahavaa, kapaas, makka evam lakdi kaa aayaat kiya jaataa he.
