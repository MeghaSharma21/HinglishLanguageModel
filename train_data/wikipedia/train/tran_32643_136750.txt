 ogtai khan mongol samrajya ke sansthaapak changez khan or uski mukhya patni borte kaa teesraa putr tha or puure samrajya kaa doosraa kagaan tha. usne apane pita ke iran, china or madhya asia ko mongol samrajya ke adheen karne ke abhiyaan main bahut hissaa liya. use ek qaabil or sahasi sipahasaalar mana jaataa he. kaha jaataa he ki ogtai apane pita kaa sab se priy betaa tha. usamen kisi bhi bahes main logon kaa mat jeet lene kii kshamta thi. bade kad-but or shaktishaalee vyaktitv vaala ogtai hansmukh or budhimaan bhi mana jaataa tha.
