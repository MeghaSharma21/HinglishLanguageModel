 lui da broai ek francisi bhautikee vaigyanik or nobel puraskaar vijeta the. unhone 1924 main saare padaarthon ke tarang-kan dvirup hone kaa daawa kiya tha or uske liye ganit viksit kiya tha. yah bhavishyavaanee aage chalakar prayogon main siddh ho gayi. inke naam ko bhartiya upmahadweep main aksar "lui di broggli" uccharit kiya jaataa he, jo vastav main sahi uccharan nahin he.
 looyee victor da broggli kaa janm france ke diep nagar main hua tha. inhone 1901 itho main paris vishvavidhyalay se itihaas ke snaatak tathaa 1913 itho main vigyaan ke snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii.
 san 1924 main broggli ne doctor kii oopaadhi ke liye apana shodhaprabandh upasthit kiya, jiskaa sheershak tha 'kwantum siddhant sambandhi gaveshanaayen'. 1929 itho main broggli ko nobel puraskaar mila or usee varsh francisi vaigyanik academy ne inhen pvankare padak pradhan kiya. 1933 itho main ye us academy ke sabhasad bhi nirvaachit ho gaye or 1942 itho main uske sthaayee mantri bhi niyukt ho gaye. san 1928 main pvankare institute main saidhantik bhautikee ke pradhanadhyaapak niyukt hue. inhen ke prayaas se yah sanstha samakaleen bhautik siddhanton ke adhyayan ke liye ek centre ban gai. broggli ne parmanvik kanikaon tathaa prakaash-vigyaan par mahatvapurn vaigyanik pustaken prakashit kii hai. san 1945 main francisi sarkaar ne parmanik urja ke ucch ayog kii sthapana kii or broggli ko uskaa takneekee paramarshdaata niyukt kiya. saahitiyik kaary ke kaaran 1945 itho main ye francisi academy ke sadsy nirvaachit hue. ye francisi lekhaksang ke sammanit sabhapati rahe or 1952 itho main inhen vaigyanik lekhan kii utkrushtataa ke liye kaling pratishthan dwaara pradutt pratham puraskaar mila tha.
 prayek gatisheel kan ke saath inhone ek aanushanbik tarang kii kalpana kii. jin kanon kaa dravyaman prekshangrahya parinaam kaa hota he or jinka adhyayan chirpratishthit yantriki se kiya jaataa he unamem se kanikaon ke gunon kaa hi lagbhag purna praadhanya rahata he, kintu parmanustareey kanikaon main tarangeey gun pramukh ho jate hai. in siddhanton kaa praayogic satyaapan bel telephone kii prayogshaalaon main kuch amareeki bhautik vaigyanikon ne kiya. electron or proton ke sadrush parmanvik kanikaon main unki aanushanbik tarangon ke kaaran prakaash or eks kirnon ke samaan hi vivartan kii ghatnaon kaa praayogic adhyayan kiya. baad main inhen vichaaron kaa vyaavhaarik upyog chumbakeey lenson ke vikaas main hua, jinpar electron sookshmdarshi aadhaarit he.
