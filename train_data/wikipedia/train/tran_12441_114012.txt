 chumphone thailand kaa ek praant he. yah dakshini thailand kshetra main malay prayadweep ke kra thalsandhi bhaag main sthit he or isaki pashchimi seemaa burma ke tanintharyi mandal ke saath satee hui he. donon desh kii yahan kii seemaa par tenacerim pahadiyaan ek prakritik sarhad ke rup main vistrut hai. yah purvi disha main thailand kii khadi ke saath tatavarti he.
 "chumphone" kii namotpatti par matbhed he. sambhav he ki yah gooler vriksh ke thai bhashaa ke naam "maduea chumphone" par rakhaa gaya ho, kyonki yah is kshetra main baari maatra main paye jate hai. yah bhi sambhav he ki yah thai shabd "chumanumporn" par padaa ho jiskaa arth "balon yaa shaktiyon kaa jamaavada" he.
