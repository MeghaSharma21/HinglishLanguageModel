 mumtaz mahal arjumand bano begum kaa adhik prachlit naam he. inkaa janm april 1593 main agra main hua tha. inke pita abdul hasan asaf khan ek farsi sajjan the jo noorjehan ke bhai the. noorjehan baad main samraat jahangir kii begum baniin. 19 varsh kii umr main arjumand kaa nikaah shahjahan se 10 may, 1612 ko hua. arjumand shahjahan kii teesri patni thi par sheeghra hi vah unki sabase pasandida patni ban gayi. unka nidhan burhanpur main 17 june, 1631 ko 14veen santan, beti gauharaara begum ko janm dete vakt hua. unako agra main taj mahal main dafnaaya gaya.
