 dev franco ek america film or televijan abhinayta he. unhone apane abhinay kii shuruyaat superbed or charlie sti cloud jaisi film main choti bhoomika adaa kar kii. aage unhen ek kamedi shrinkhalaa "scrubs" ke nauven sijan main abhinay karne kaa mauka mila. unki pehali safal film, 21 jamp street thi, jisamen franco ne ek sahaayak abhinayta kii bhoomika adaa kii thi. 
 franco dwaara mukhya abhinit filmon main friete nite, nau you si me or isaki agali kadi, nau you si me 2, warm bodys, nebers or isaki agali kadi nebers 2, nerve, da little house or da dizaster artist aadi shaamil he.
 
 2012 main franco abhinetri ellison bri se mili. agast 2015 main, jode ne sagaai kar lee. 13 march, 2017, ko jode ne pushti kii he ki ve ek nijee samaaroh main shaadi kar chuke he.
