 daphala bharat kii ek pramukh janajati hai. == nivas kshetra == arunachal pradesh
 daphala jati ke log bangni bhi kehalaate hai, purvi bhutan or arunachal pradesh ke janajaatiy log he.
 ye log chini-tibeti parivaar kii tibeti-burmi bhashaa bolte hai.daphala apana bharan-poshan jhum kheti, shikaar or machhlee markar karte hai.ye 900 se 1,800 meter kii oonchaai par balliyon par bane makaanon main rahete hai.vansh kaa nirdhaaran paitruk aadhaar par kiya jaataa he, jo 60 yaa 70 logon kaa ek kutumb hota he.yah kutumb ek lambey ghar main ek saath rahata he, jisamen vibhajit khand nahin hote, lekin prayek danpati ke parivaar ke liye ek alag chulhaa hota he.is paramparaagat pitrugrih ke alaava koi aupchaarik saamaajik sanghathan athwa gram sarkaar nahin hoti.inke dharm main prakruti se judi pavitra atmaaon main vishwaas sammilit he.
