 makbara kevi 27 misr main raajaaon kii ghati main sthit he. is makbare kaa daura jon wilkinsons ne kiya tha, lekin prashanth lutheran vishvavidhyalay ke donald pi. rayaan dwaara bhi 1990 tak puri tarah se khoj nahin gaya tha. makbara avyavasthit he, or iske kabje yaa nivaasiyon ke bare main kuch bhi spost jaankaree nahin pataa he.
