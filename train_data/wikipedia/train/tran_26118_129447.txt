 mishra brahmanon main aaspad yaa upnaam he. sab gunon or karmon main nipun tathaa sabamein mili rahane ke kaaran is up naam ko angeekrut kiya gaya.
 mishra upnaam ke brahmanon main nimna gotra hote hai 
 vatsa mishra kaa gotra he vatsa kaa arth bachhda putr shishy aadi hota he vatsa gotra gaanaa payaasi aadi mishra brahmano kaa gotra hota he .
 katyayan gotreey brahmanon kii ginati shatkulon main hoti he. shatkulon main katyayan, upamanyu, bharadwaj, kashyap, shandilya or sankrut aate hai. in gotron ke brahman kuleen kehalaate hai. baijegaanv, suthiyaayein, maanjhagaanv, ankin, gwalmaidan, badarka ke brahman shreshth katyayan gotreey mishra hai.jaise pratap narayan mishra baijegaanv ke mishra. 
 kashyap gotreey mishron main lakshmana, nagara, shahabaad aadi hai.
 is gotra main pramukhataa se parashuram ke mishra vidyaman hai . shandilya gotreey brahmanon ke anya upnaam tiwari, treepathi, dikshit tathaa chakravarti bhi hote hai . shandilya rishi chandravansheey shrikrishna vasudev ke kulguru kehalaate hai .
 is gotra main shivadutt miraanv, sahataavan kesharimau, brindavan lalpura aadi ke brahman aate hai. 
 is gotra ke antargat vir jajmau, banavaari chachenda, prajapati itawa tathaa krishni kushikpur ke kanyakubj brahman aate hai.     
 is gautra ke antargat mithila bahaman aate he. is gautra ke logo kaa upnaam mishra hota he.ye bahyan bihar ke madhubani jile, darbhanga,benipati,raika aadi aasapaas ke kshetro me rahete he.
