 narathivaat thailand kaa ek praant he. yah dakshini thailand kshetra main malay prayadweep main sthit he. dakshin main isaki seemayen malaysia ke kelanthan rajya se lagati hai or purv main yah thailand kii khadi se tatavarti he.
 "narathivaat" sanskrit ke "nar + adhivaas" kaa thai bhashaa rup he. malay bhashaa main ise "menara" bulaayaa jaataa tha jo arabi bhashaa ke minar shabd se utpann he or malaysia main ab bhi ise is naam se bulaayaa jaataa he.
 narathivaat thailand ke un keval chaar praanton main se ek he jisamen muslim samudaay bahusankhyak he. sthaneey aabaadi 82% muslim or 17.9% buddha dharmi he. lagbhag 80.4% log apni maatrubhaasha ke rup main pattani malay bhashaa bolte hai. saanskrithik va jaateey rup se narathivaat ke malay malaysia ke kelanthan malayon se sambandhit hai. adhiktar log krishi or matsyodyog main lage hue hai.
