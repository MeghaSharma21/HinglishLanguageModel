 dhanna bhagat ek rahasywaadee kavi or jiskaa teen bhajan aadi granth main maujuud hai ek vaishnav bhakt the.dhanna mul rup se dhani dhori rajasthan ke tonk jile main tehsil ke paas ke gaanv main bharat jat parivaar main paidaa hua the.dhanna kaa janm lekin apane naam kaa varsh prakat nahin he jaisaa kabir yaa ravidaas ke lekhan main maiks arthur hal karata he. 'beej buvaai bina anaaj kaise dhanna' mira bai gane main apane naam kaa jald se jald ullekh he.unhone ramnuj se shiksha praapt kii thi.
