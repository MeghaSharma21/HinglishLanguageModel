 boson :- ve kan jo bose-oinstin saankhyikee kaa paalan karte he or jinaki prachakran hoti he, boson kehalaate he. moolbhoot balo ko sanjokar rakhane vaale sabhi urja vaahak kan boson hote he. ve sanyojit kan jiname fermion kii sankhya sam hoti he, boson kehalaate he, udhaaharan - meson. kisi bhi parmaanu kaa nabhik fermion he athwa boson, yah is baat par nirbhar karata he ki usamen maujuud proton va neutron kaa yoga sam he athwa visham.
 sheet helium, jisaki shyanta shunya hoti he, kaa vicitra vyavhaar hota he ki yah apane main aarpaar aa ja saktaa he. iskaa yah vyavhaar bosonic gun ke kaaran hota he, chunki iskaa nabhik boson hota he or poli eksclusan siddhanth kaa paalan karne baadhya nahin hota islie yah apane main aarpaar guzar saktaa he.
 bhautik shaastra main do prakaar ke anu maane jate hai - boson or fermian. iname se boson satyendra nath basu ke naam par hi hai.
