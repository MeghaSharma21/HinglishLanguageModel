 dashavathar mandir, uttar pradesh ke lalitpur jile ke devgarh main sthit he. yah bhagwaan vishnu ko samarpit he. aisa mana jaataa he ki yah gupt kaal main bana tha. yahan aap bhagwaan vishnu ke das avatar ke darshan kar sakate hai or yahi nahin mandir ke dwaar par ganga or yamuna deviyon kii nakkashi kii gayi he. pattee par vaishnav puranic kathaon kii nakkashi bhi kii gayi he.
