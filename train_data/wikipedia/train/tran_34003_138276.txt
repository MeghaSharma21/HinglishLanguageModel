 shalaka, akshakriya yaa dyutakarm bharat kaa bahut pracheen vyasan he. vaidic kaal main or uske baad mahabharat ke yug main bahut prachlit tha. yah aksh yaa shalaka se khelaa jaataa tha. in shalakaon par ank likhe hote the jinaki gananaa ko daawein kaha jaataa tha. inase khelte samay kabhi-kabhi apsaraon kaa aavahan kiya jaataa tha jo apane prabhaav se anukool daawein laati thim. daavon par choti yaa badi dhanrashiyaan lagaai jaati thim. pandavon ne to draupadi ko bhi daawein par laga diya tha. khelanevaale pakshon ko apni vijay kii badi chintaa rahati thi or jab kushalta se kaam nahin banataa dikhta tha to mantra kaa prayog kiya jaataa tha. isliye shalakaon ko abhimantrit karke khel khelaa jaataa tha.
