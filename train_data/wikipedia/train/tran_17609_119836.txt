 kiran bhatnagar janm -February 8, 1951 bhartiya rangakarmi, lekhak or nirdeshak. neshnal skool of drama main adhyayan. bhartiya natya sangh se judy thi. vartmaan mai sangeet naatak academy main upasachiv. 
 kiran bhatnagar dwaara sampadit je. en. kaushal ke sansmaranon kaa sangrah dard aayaa tha dabe paanv main je. en. kaushal ke 28 lekhon or rang-prasangon ke sansmaranon kaa sanchayan he. unke madhyam se bhartiya visheshkar hindi rangamanch kaa chalis varsh kaa itihaas ek bhinn rup main hamaare saamne prastut hua he.
