 chamkila daanav tara mahadaanav taron or daanav taron ke bich kii shrenee kaa tara hota he. yah aise daanav taare hote hai jinamein chamak bahut zyaada hoti he lekin yah itane bade nahin hote ke inhen mahadaanav taron kaa darja diya jaae. yarkis varnakram shreneekaran main isaki chamak kii shrenee "II" hoti he. vashchik taramandal kaa sargas naam kaa tara aise ek chamkile daanav kaa udahaaran he.
 "chamkile daanav taare" ko angrezi main "brit jaaent star" kaha jaataa he.
