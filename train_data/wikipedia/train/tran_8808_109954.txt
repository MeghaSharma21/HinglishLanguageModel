 bhartiya aam chunaav, 2014 ke liye vibhinn sansthaaon dwaara surveykshan karaae ja rahe hai jisase bharat ke matdaan ke mijaaz kaa pataa chalata he. inhi chunaav purv surveykshanon ko is lekh main shaamil kiya ja rahaa he. sabhi chunaav purv surveykshan janvari 2013 se lekar ab tak ke hai.
 bharat main chunaav purv surveykshan vivaadit ho saktaa he. in aaropon ko main pakshpatpurna herpher main shaamil he. 
 chunaav purv surveykshan paddati main baari sudhaar hua he. siesds naamak sanstha kaa surveykshan abtak 16 maukon par bilkul sahi, 7 maukon par lagbhag sahi tathaa 4 maukon par galat sabit hue hai.
