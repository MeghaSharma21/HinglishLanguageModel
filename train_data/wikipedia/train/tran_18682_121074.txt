 interval, varsh 2000 main star tivi par prasaarit el dharawahik tha, jiske mukhya paatr the, om puri, aamir bashir, arundhati, joy sengupta or uttara bokar. isaki nirmaataa thi theatre abhinaytri, aparajita krishna, tathaa nirdeshak jogi dayaal, gulaal kriplani or swayan aparajita thim.
 iskaa pehla prasaaran 4 janvari 2000 ko hua tha. yah ek saptahik dharawahik tha or har mangalvaar kii raat 9:30 main star plus channel par prasaarit hua karata tha. yah star plus kaa ek prime time dharawahik tha. yah dharawahik un kuch main se ek he, jisamen om puri ji ne swayan abhinay kiya tha. iske kul 52 episod the.
 ek nayak ke rup main ek virdohi nayak ko pradarshit karne vaala yah dharawahik sabase pehle dharawahikon main se ek tha. isaki kahani srivi namak paatr ke jivan ke chaaron ore gumti he, jise om puri ne nibhaai he. paatr srivi ek chote se shahar main ek librarian tha, lekin vah ek aadhunik guru ban jaataa he.
 dharawahik main, srivi ek aisa vyakti he jo abhi bhi apane ateet se baahar rah rahaa he unke manovaigyanik samasyaayein hai, jinamein dukhad pravritiyon shaamil hai. shri ve sheersh par apni vruddhi ke dauraan sabhi vipakshon ko kuchal detii he yah sab uske jivan ke pyaar ke saath nahin hone ke kaaran uske dard ke kaaran hota he, ek aisi ghatana jo serial ke samay se pehle saat varsh pehle hui thi. shri ve ne sabhi ko or sab kuch par haaavee karne kii koshish kii taaki dard ko bhuulane ke liye usne us mahila ke haathon sahaae jo chura liye or baad main uske dil ko tod diya. uskaa andhera prerana use upar kii taraf badhata he or vah bahut shaktishaalee ho jaataa he vah rajanetaaon, udyogpatiyon or mashhoor hastiyon ke liye ek shakti dalal main badalata he. vah ek kathputli he jo kuch bahut hi khatarnaak pille ke taar khinchataa he.
 is sho ko apni anuthi kahani or om puri or anya kalakaaron dwaara abhinay kii ucch gunvattta kii vajah se acchhee samiksha mili.
