 kanagai parvat madhya mongolia main sthit ek parvat shrunkhlaa he. yah mongolia kii rajdhani ulaan bator se lagbhag 400 kimi pashchim par sthit he. kanagai ke pahaad altaai parvaton se purv main or sayan parvaton se dakshin kii taraf hai.
 500 kimi tak chalane vaali kanagai shrunkhlaa kaa sabase unchaa pahaad lagbhag 4,000 meter lambaa otagontenger he. in pahaado se orphon, selenge, idar or zawkhaan nadiyaan nikalati hai or isi kshetra main orog or boon jhilein bhi sthit hai. pashchim main yah parvat khatm hote hai or mahaan jhil droni shuru hoti he.
 'kanagai' main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
