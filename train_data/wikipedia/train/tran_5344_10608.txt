 thanjavur tamilnadu kaa ek pramukh saanskrithik centre rahaa he. is sangrahalay main pallav, chol, pandya or nayak kaaleen pashaan pratimaaon kaa sangrah he. ek anya ddeergha main thanjore kii glass paintings pradarshit kii gai hai. lakdi par banaai gai in tasveeron main rang-sangyojan dekhte hi banataa he. yah sangrahalay apane kaansya shilp ke sangrah ke liye prasiddh he.
