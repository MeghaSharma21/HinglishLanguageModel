 compojit padaarth do yaa adhik padaarthon se nirmit padaarth hai jinke avyavon kii bhautik evam rasinic gun bahut bhinn hote hai. inke ye avyav compojit padaarth ke andar sthool str par bhi alag-alag hi bane rahete hai. vishesh baat yah he ki compojit padaarth main ve gun praapt ho jate hai jo ki uske ghatak padaarthon main nahin hote. is visheshta ke kaaran aise padaarth banaaye ja sakate hai jinamein koi vishisht gun ho . apni isi visheshta ke kaaran compojit padaarth udyogon main bahutayat main prayog kiye jane lage hai. kintu compojit padaarthon ke gunon kii vyakhya karnaa saral nahin he.
