 rajasthan kii nadiyaan
 - chambal envam maahi ko chhodkar rajasthan kii anya koi nadi baramasi nahin hai.- chambal nadi ko rajasthan kii kamdhenu bhi kaha jaataa hai.- chambal nadi rajasthan envam madhya pradesh kii seemaa banaati hai. - bhencerodgarh ke nikat chambal nadi chulia jalprapaat kaa nirmaan karti hai. - purnatah rajasthan main bahane vaali nadi banaas hai. - banganga nadi ke kinare pracheen bairth sabhyataa viksit hui thi. - bingod ke sameep banaas,menal envam bedch nadiyon kaa sangam triveni kehalaata hai. - chambal envam luni nadi ke pracheen naam kramshah charmanvati envam lavanavati hai. - luni nadi kaa jal balotra tak meetha envam baad main khaaraa hai. - maahi nadi ko adivaasiyon kii ganga,kanthal kii ganga,bangad kii ganga or dakshin rajasthan kii swarna rekha bhi kaha jaataa hai. - maahi nadi kark rekha ko do baar paar karti hai. - maahi nadi duungarpur envam bansawada kii seemaa banaati hai. - maahi,som envam jaakham ke sangam sthal par magh maah kii poornima ko meley kaa aayojan kiya jaataa hai jo adivaasiyon ke kumbh ke naam se prasiddh hai. - kalibanga sabhyataa ghagghar nadi ke kinare viksit hui thi. - ghagghar nadi ko mrit nadi ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa hai. - kaankani nadi ko sthaneey bhashaa main masurdi nadi ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa hai. - nimbark sampradaay kii pramukh peeth salemabad rupangarh nadi ke kinare sthit hai. - bikaner envam churu jile main koi nadi pravaahit nahin hoti hai. - adhik varsha ke kaaran ghagghar nadi kaa pravaah pakistan ke fort abbaas tak chalaa jaataa hai. - gujarat kii mukhya nadi sabarmati udaipur jile se nikalati hai. - bedch nadi ko pracheen kaal main aid nadi ke naam se janaa jaataa tha.
