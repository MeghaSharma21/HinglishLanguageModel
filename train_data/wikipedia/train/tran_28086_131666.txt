 perumal murugan tamil main likhane vaala bhartiya lekhak, vidwaan or saahitiyik itihaaskaar he. inke ab tak chaar upanyaas, teen kahani sangrah or teen kavy-sangrah prakashit hue hai. vah namakal main sarkari arts kaalaj main ek tamil professor hai.
 janvari 2015 main unhonen ne hinduvadi sanghathanon ke virodh ke kaaran likhane kaa kaam chhod diya. unhonen ne fasebuk kii apni waal par likhaa he, "lekhak perumal murugan nahin rahe, vah paramatma nahin is liye vah phir likhnaa shuru nahin karenge, abhi sirf ek adhyaaapak pi. murugan jivit rahenge."
