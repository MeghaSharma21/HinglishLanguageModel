 gulam vansh  madhyakaalin bharat kaa ek rajvansh tha. is vansh kaa pehla shaasha qutubuddin aibak tha jise mohammad gauri ne prithviraj chouhan ko haraane ke baad niyukt kiya tha. is vansh ne dilli kii satta par 1206-1290 isvi tak raaj kiya.
 is vansh ke shaasha yaa sansthaapak ghulaam the na ki raja. is liye ise rajvansh kii bajaay sirf vansh kaha jaataa he.
 isane dilli kii satta par kareeb 84 varshon tak raaj kiya tathaa bharat main islamic shashan kii neenv daali. isse purv kisi bhi muslim shaasha ne bharat main lambey samay tak prabhutv kaayam nahin kiya tha. isi samay changez khan ke netritv main bharat ke uttar pashchimi kshetra par mongolon kaa akraman bhi hua.
