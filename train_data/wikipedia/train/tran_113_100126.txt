 kanji swami aadhyatmik jain guru the. wo unhone jain dharmavalambiyon main swadhyay ko pratishthit kiya.
 unka janm ek sthandwasi jain ke rup main hua. unhone sthandwasi sant ke rup main jivan shuru kiya. ve 1932 main acharya kundakund ke pandit todermal or samaysar ke kaaryon se bahut prabhavit hue. uske baad unhone in digambar jain siddhanton ko apane darshan main apanaaya. unke samarthakon kaa samooh baad main ek up-samudaay ke rup main ubhara jo digambar jain siddhanton kaa anusaran karte hai.
