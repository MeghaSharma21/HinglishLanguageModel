 nabha riyasat, angreji raaj ke samay bharat ke punjab main sthit ek riyasat thi jisaki rajdhani nabha thi. 1947 main bharat ke swatantra hone ke pashchaat sahaayak sandhi samaapt ho gayi or kuch dinon tak nabha riyasat swatantra thi. kuch dinon ke pashchaat iske shaasha ne bharat main vilay ke prapatron par hastakshar kar diye. kuch dinon baad yah punjab rajya main mila lee gayi.
