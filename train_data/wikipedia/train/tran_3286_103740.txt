 iske dwaara aap apana database fil bana sakate hai. yah "font-and" ke rup main kaary karata he or microsoft escuel sarwar ke jaise "back-and" ke rup main bhi. kuch anya anuprayog jaise vijuval basic, eespi.net aadi bhi microsoft acces ke database praaroop kaa upyog karte hai. yah microsoft ke anya anuprayogon ke saath bhi kaam karata he, jaise excel, outluk, verde, powerpoint aadi.
