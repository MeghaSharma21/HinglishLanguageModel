 nangpa laa himalay kaa ek darra he jo sadiyon se tibet or nepal ke bich vyapaarik kendra kii tarah rahaa he. yah mount everest se tees kilometre pashchim main avasthit he. san 2006 main 75 tibeti logon kaa ek jattha jab bhaag kar nepal aa rahaa tha to chini cho oyu post se unpar goliyaan chalaai gai jisamen ek 17 varshiya buddha bhikkhani kii maut ho gai. aarambh main chini adhikariyon ne is maamle ko dabaane kaa praytn kiya. lekin isi ghatana ko ek european photographer ne filmaaya or baad main prakashit kar diya. photographer himalay main chadhaai kar rahaa tha.
