 parmaanu ghadi ek prakaar kii ghadi he jo electromagnetic spectrum kii microwave, optical yaa ultrawilet rigen main electron tranjishan frequency kaa prayog time keeping ke standard element ke rup main karti he. parmaanu ghadiyaan gyaat sabase sateek samay or aavriti maanak hai, or inhen antarrashtriya samay vitaran sevaaon ke liye praathmik maanakon ke rup main upyog kiya jaataa he, taaki televijan prasaaran kii lahar aavriti ko niyantrit kiya ja sake or vaishvik nevigation satellite system jaise jeepies main bhi iskaa prayog kiya jaataa he.
