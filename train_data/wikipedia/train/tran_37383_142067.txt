 JSTOR, jise jestor uccharit kiya jaataa he, san 1995 main sthapit ek digital dastauez kosh he. shuru main isamen adhyayan patrikaon ke puraane anko kaa digitalkaran karke rakhaa jaataa tha lekin ab isamen pustakon, nai kritiyon or patrikaon ke naye anko kii pratiyaan bhi milti hai. isamen lagbhag 2,000 patrikaon kii puri saamagree khoji or padhi ja sakati he. lagbhag 160 deshon kii 8,000 se zyaada sansthaaon ke sadsy jestor par saamagree dekhte-padhate hai. isaki adhiktar saamagree praapt karne ke liye niyamit shulk dena padataa he, haalaanki 2012 ke baad se kuch mudradhikaar-mukt saamagree kisi bhi pathak ko panjikaran karne ke uparant muft uplabdh kari jaati he.
 'JSTOR' kaa naam 'Journal Storage' ko sikodkar gadha gaya tha.
