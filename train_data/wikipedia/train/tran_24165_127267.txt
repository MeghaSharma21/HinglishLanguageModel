 rog or sambandhit swasthya samasyaon kaa antarrashtriya sankhyikee vargeekaran ne 10 vaa sansodhan prastut kiya jisamen rogo ke lakshan, sikaayat, saamaajik paristhitiyon tathaa bahya karkon ko koding kiya. yah shrenee vibhaajan vishwa swasthya sanghathan ke anuroop kiya gaya. ICD-10 par kaary 1983 main prarambh kiya gaya jo 1992 se samaapt hua.
