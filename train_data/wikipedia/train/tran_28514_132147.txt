 ganganagar rajasthan kaa ek jila he. sriganganagar shahar ganganagar jila kaa mukhyalay he. yah rajasthan pradesh kaa sabase uttari janapad he jiske uttar main fazilka evam hanumangarh, dakshin main bikaner tathaa churu, tathaa pashchim main pakistan he. pehle yah bikaner rajya kaa ek bhaag tha. 
 ganganagar janapad kaa pramukh prashaskeey centre tathaa vikasshil nagar bhi he. iskaa namakaran bikaner ke maharaj gangassingh ke naam par hua he. yah jile ke sarvaadhik samunnat tathaa sinchit krishikshetra main sthit hone ke kaaran pramukh vyapaarik mandi tathaa yatayaat centre ho gaya he. sriganganagar ko rajasthan kaa ann kaa katoraa bhi kaha jaataa he | yahan janapadiya prashasnic karyaalayon tathaa nyaayalayon ke atirikt kai snaatak mahavidhyalay tathaa anya saanskrithik santhaan hai. yah nagar purnataya 20veen shatabdi kii dein he. praarambhik dashabdiyon main yah agyaat gram rahaa. lekin gang-nahar sinchai pariyojna dwaara kshetra main krishi kaa vikaas hone ke kaaran isaki jansankhya adhik bad gai he. yahan 1945 main chini kaa kaarkaana kholaa gaya. yahan ek audyogic santhaan kii bhi sthapana hui he. sriganganagar main shri buddhajohad gurudwara va laila-majanu kii majaar pramukh darshneey sthal hai |
 shri ganganagar kii jalwaayu bahut garm he. garmi main taapmaana 52 celsius pahunchataa he or sheetakaaleen main -2 celsius ke aasapaas gir jaataa he. ausat vaarshik varsha keval 20 semi hoti he. varsha kii maatra, jalwaayu tathaa jalpurti kii drushti se yah janapad rajasthan ke retile evam shushk kshetra main padataa he is sampurna kshetra main jal kaa dharatleey pravaah nahin ke baraabar he. sampurna janapad brihad balukamay maidan he. ekmaatr nadi ghaggar he jiskaa pravaah hanumangarh ke paas hi ret main samaptpraaya ho jaataa he. janvari kaa adhiktam taap 20.70 sein. tathaa nimnatam 2.40 sein. rahata he. greeshm ke june maheene main adhiktam taap 43.0 sein. tak ho jaataa he kintu ganganagar main 50.0 sein. tak kii sambhaavna rahati he. jile main ausat vaarshik varsha 8.580 sein. hoti he or varsha ke kul din 15.2 hai. adhikansh varsha june-july-agast maheenon main ho jaati he. rajasthan main sarvaadhik ret ke tufaan ganganagar jile main hi aate hai. ole kabhi-kabhaar, dashabdiyon main ekaadh baar, padate hon. 
 jile kii retili bhoomi main jalpurti karne par utpaadan shakti bahut adhik ho jaati he. ghaggar-ghati kii matiyaar bhoomi tathaa varsha ritu main bhar jane vaale talab tathaa jhiilon ke tal main prapya matiyaar domat gehun evam chane kii fasalon ke liye prasiddh hai. na keval bhakhda-nangal-yojana ke jal dwaara, pratyut hanumangarh se viksit vishaal rajasthan-nahar-pariyojna dwaara, jo vishwa main apane dhang kii sarvaadhik lambii nahar he, janapad kaa sarvageen vikaas kiya ja rahaa he. jile kaa gang-nahar-upniveshkshetra bharat kaa sabase kora kshetra he jahaan sarvaadhik tractor prayukt ho rahe hai. sinchai kii vruddhi ke saath krishi ke yantric sadhano kaa adhik upyog hota ja rahaa he. janapad main sthit suratgarh farm asia mahadesh kaa vrihattam suniyojit 30,670 acre kaa farm he jisamen yantric krishi hoti he. yah krishikshetra prayogshaala sadrush he jisamen shushk pradesh ke upyukt krishi kaa vikaas karne, samunnat beej utpann karne, pashuon kii naslein samunnat karne aadi ke prayog kiye ja rahe hai. at: janapad kii krishivyavastha jeevikaayaapan krishi kii sthiti se nikalakar vaanijya krishi kii ore teevr gati se agrasar ho rahi he. khadyannon ke atirikt ganna evam kapaas kaa utpaadan bad rahaa he. krishi padaarthon par aadhaarit udyog-dhandhe punap rahe hai. ganganagar main chini kaa kaarkaana tathaa audyogic santhaan, hanumangarh main urvarak kaarkaana, risinghnagar main audyogic, pravidhak tathaa shaikshanik santhaan aadi janapad kii vikasshiltaa ke suchak hai. bhakhda-nangal-yojana dwaara janapad ke pramukh sthanon ko bijli praapt ho rahi he.
 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar ganganagar shahar kii kul jansankhya 2,10,788 he; or ganganagar zile kii kul jansankhya 17,88,427 he. yah rajasthan kaa sarvaadhik sikh aabaadi vaala jila he. jila mukhyalay se punjab kii seemaa mahaj paanch kilometre dur he. jile kii shrikaranpur vidhansabha kshetra sikh bahul he.
