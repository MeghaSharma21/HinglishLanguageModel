 gaumukh kaa arth hota he gaay kaa mukh arthath apane sharir ko gaumukh ke samaan bana lene ke kaaran hi is asana ko goumukhasana kaha jaataa he. goumukhasana teen shabdon kii sandhi se bana he - gau + mukh + asana.
 dandasana main baith kar baayen pair ko mode kar edi ko daaen nitamb ke paas rakhe yaa edi par baith bhi sakate hai. daaen pair ko mode kar baayen pair ke upar is prakaar rakhen ki donon ghutane ek-duusare se sparsh karte hon. daaen haath ko upar uthaa kar peeth kii ore moden or baayen haath ko peeth ke peeche se lekar daaen haath ko pakaden. gardan or qamar sidhi rakhen. ek ore se karne ke baad duusari ore se bhi isi tarah karen.
 yoga kii paathshaala main aap 1 yaa 2 minute tak savasana kaa abhyaas kar sakate hai.
 gardan dard, qamar dard vaale log ise na kare.
