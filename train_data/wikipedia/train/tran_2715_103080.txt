 kundalini yoga yaa lay yoga kaa arth he - chitra milan me leen ho janaa, praan sanchaar karnaa, brahm ke gyaan main leen hona. kundalini yoga par tantra sampradaay or shakt sampradaay kaa adhik prabhaav rahaa he. 
 chitt kaa apane swaroop villin hona yaa chitt kii niruddh avasthaa layayog ke antargat aata he. sadhak ke chitt main jab chalate, baithte, sote or bhojan karte samay har samay braham kaa dhyaan rahe - isi ko layayog kehete hai. yogatvopanishad main is prakaar varnan he-
  yah yoga sambandhit lekh apni praarambhik avasthaa main he, yani ki ek aadhaar he. aap ise badhaakar vikipedia kii madad kar sakate he.
