 maan mandir ghat varanasi main sthit ek ganga ghat he. is ghat ko jaipur ke maharaja jaisingh dwitiya ne 1770 main banwaaya tha. isamen nakkashi araja lidaar alankrita jharokhe bane hai. iske saath hi unhone varanasi main yantra mantra vedhashala bhi banavaayi thi jo dilli, jaipur, ujjain, mathura ke sang paanchavin khagolashastreey vedhashala he. is ghat ke uttari ore ek sundar balcony he, jo someshwar ling ko arghya dene ke liye banavaayi gai thi.
