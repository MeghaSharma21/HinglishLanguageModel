 nyaayawad pracheen chini itihaas ke jhou rajvansh ke jhagadate rajyon ke kaal ke sau vichaardhaaraon naamak daur main prachlit ek vichaardhaara thi. yah saamaajik or rajnatik vyavastha par kendrit thi or jivan ke lakshya or dharmik maamalon ke saath iskaa zyaada sarokaar nahin tha. shuru main is vichaardhaara ko ullekith chin rajya ke rajaneta shang yang ne kiya. ise aage sab se adhik viksit haan feizi or lee si ne kiya. inke anusaar insaan kii mul prakruti swaarthee he or ise kabhi badla nahin ja saktaa. isliye samaaj ko suvyavasthit rakhane ke liye kade kaanoonon kii aavashyaktaa he jinhen sakhti se lagu kiya jaae. nyaaywaadiyon ke liye raashtra or rajya kaa hit nagarikon ke hit se badhkar he. isliye zaruri he ki rajya sampann or shaktishaalee ho bhale nagarikon ko kitna hi mushkil jivan hi kyon na jeena pade.
