 nathan maaikel line  australia ke cricket khilaadi he. ye australian team kii ore se of-spinner hote hue sabase zyada wicket lene vaale khilaadi he. inase pehle sabase zyada wicket lene kaa record hag trumbal he jinhone 2015 tak 141 wicket liye the. inhen kai upanaamon se janaa jaataa he.
 nathan line ne apane test cricket karier shuruyaat 31 agast 2011 ko srilanka ke khilaaf kii thi. line kaa ab tak test cricket maichon main sabase acha pradarshan 50 ran dekar 8 wicket lenaa rahaa he. jabaki inhone vanade cricket karier kii shuruyaat 8 march 2012 ko srilanka ke khilaaf hi kii thi lekin inhone ab tak 5 saalon main maatr 13 vanade match hi khele he. vanade main inkaa sabase acha pradarshan 44 ran dekar 4 wicket lenaa rahaa he.
