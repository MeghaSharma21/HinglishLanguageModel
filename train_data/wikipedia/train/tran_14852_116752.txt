 qayamat shabd kaa saral hindi main arth pralay hota he. arthath qayamat saamanytah us sthiti ko kaha jaataa he jab prithvi par ek baar jivan samaapt ho jaayegaa. iske alag-alag dharmon or samskritiyon ke bhinn-bhinn vichaar hai. is tarah kii bahut bhavishyavaaniyan bhi samay-samay par kii jaati hai. iskaa koi vaigyanik pramaan nahin he. 
 muslim dharm ke maanane waalon ke anusaar, is sakal charachar jagat kaa ek na ek din ant hona he, usee ant hone vaale din ko yom al-qiyama kehete hai.
