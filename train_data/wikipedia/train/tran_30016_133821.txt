 ek prakaar kaa babul. kathakikar. sonakikar. yah china, bharat, pakistan, nepal, shri lanka, bhutan, myanmar main paaya jaataa he.
 vishesh—iskaa ped bahut bada hota he or praaia; samast bharat main se paaya jaataa he lekin uttar pradesh ke khayr shahar me ye adhik maatra me paaya jaataa he. iske hir kii lakdi bhure rang kii hoti hai, ghunti nahin or ghar tathaa kheti ke aujaar banane ke kaam main aati he. babul kii tarah isamen bhi ek prakaar kaa gond nikalataa he or bade kaam kaa hota he. 
 is vriksh kii lakdi ke tukdon ko ubalkar nikaala or jamaaya hua ras jo paan main chooney ke saath lagaakar khaaya jaataa he, khayr yaa kattha kehalaata he.
