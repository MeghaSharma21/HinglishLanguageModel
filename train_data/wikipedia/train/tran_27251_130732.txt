 aakhaal praant turkmenistan kii ek vilaayat he. yah us desh ke dakshin-madhya main sthit he or copet daag parvaton ke saath isaki sarahadein iran or afghanistan se lagati hai. is praant kaa kshetrafal 97,160 kimi2 he or san 2005 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 9,39,700 anumaanit kii gai thi. aakhaal praant kii rajdhani aneu shahar he jo rashtriya rajdhani ashqabad ke paas hi dakshin-purv main sthit he.
 san 2000 main aakhaal praant main turkmenistan ke 14% log rahete the. deshbhar ke krishi utpaadan kaa 23% mulya or audyogic utpaadan kaa 31% mulya is praant se aata tha. yahan krishi ke liye sinchai mashhoor karakum nahar se hoti he jo aamu dariyaa se pani laati he or is praant ke purv se pashchim tak bahati he. dakshin main afghanistan se uttar kii ore bahane vaali tejen nadi bhi praant ke dakshin-purvi bhaag ko pani pradaan karti he or is nadi kaa pani tejen shahar ke dakshin main do bade sarovaron se guzarataa he. aakhaal praant san 1881 ke geok tepe yuddh ke liye prasiddh he, jis sthal par ab ek nai masjid khadi he. yah cove ata namak gufa main dharti-tal ke neeche sthit baharden naamak jhil ke liye bhi mashhoor he.
