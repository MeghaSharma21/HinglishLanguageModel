 shaantiparv mahabharat kaa 12vaan parv he. shaantiparv main dharm, darshan, rajaniti or aadhyatm gyaan kaa vishad nirupan kiya gaya he. 
 iske antargat 3 upparv hai-
 isamen 365 adhyay hai. shaanti parv main yuddh kii samaapti par yudhisthir kaa shokakul hokar pashchaatap karnaa, shrikrishna sahit sabhi logon dwaara unhen samajhaana, yudhisthir kaa nagar pravesh or rajbhishek, sabake saath pitamah bhishma ke paas janaa, bhishma ke dwaara shrikrishna kii stuti, bhishma dwaara yudhisthir ke prashnon kaa uttar tathaa unhen rajdharm, aapaddharm or mokshadharm kaa upadesh karnaa aadi varnit he. mokshaparv main sristi kaa rahasy tathaa aadhyatm gyaan kaa vishesh nirupan he. shaanti parv main mankgita, “parasharagita” tathaa “hansagita” bhi hai.
