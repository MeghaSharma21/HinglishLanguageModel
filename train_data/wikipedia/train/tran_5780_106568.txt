 rotnest dweep, jise sthaneey nuungaar log vadjemap kehete hai, pashchimi australia ke tat se aage hind mahaasaagar main sthit ek dweep he. yah sankshipt or anaupchaarik rup se rotto bhi kehalaata he. rotnest dweep freementle se pashchim main 18 kimi kii samudri duuri par he. 19 varg kimi kaa yah dweep ek sangrakshit kshetra he or yahan kuoca naamak dhaniprani kii aabaadi pai jaati he, jo bahut hi kam anya sthanon par milane vaala praani he. yah ek paryatak aakarshan he or perth se yahan ke liye dainik navi sevaayen chalati he. anumaan he ki har varsh lagbhag 5,00,000 sailaani ise dekhne aate hai. lagbhag 100 log sthai rup se dweep par rahete hai. dweep par koi bhi motor vaahan le janaa manaa he.
