 duarte barbosa europe kaa ek prasiddh lekhak tha jisane dakshin bharat main vyapaar or samaaj kaa ek vistrut vivaran likhaa. duarte kaa janm 1480 i. main hua tathaa. uski mrityu 1521 main hui thi. duarte ke likhane ke kaaran 1600 i. ke baad bharat main aane vaale dach ,angrej or francisi yatriyon kii sankhya badhane lagi thi.
