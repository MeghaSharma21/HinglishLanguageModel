 barauni, netra palak ke chor par uge bal ko kehete hai. barauniyaan bahari padaarthon jaise ki dhul-mitti ko ankhon main jane se roak kar ankhon kii raksha karti hai saath hi yah sparsh ke prati behad samvedansheel hoti he or kisi bhi bahari vastu jaise ki koi keet yaa tinkaa aadi ke prati vyakti ko sachet karti hai taaki wo apni ankhen turant band kar sake. aksar hindi main agyanswaroop barauni ke liye palak shabd kaa prayog hota he jo sahi nahin he.
