 onegaa jhil pashchimottaree rus main carelia ganatantr, leningrad oblast va vologda oblast ke kshetron main sthit ek mithe pani kii jhil he. laadoga jhil ke baad yah europe kii duusari sabase badi jhil he. navik yatayaat ke liye baltic sagar ko shwet sagar se jodne vaala nahar-maarg onegaa jhil or iske dakshin-pashchim main sthit laadoga jhil se hote hue nikalataa he.
 is jhil main kishi dweep sthit he, jo apni lakdi kii imaaraton ke liye prasiddh he. yah jagah-jagah se laai hui kaath se sainkadon varshon ke kaal main banaai gai thim.
