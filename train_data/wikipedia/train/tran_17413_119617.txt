 purvashadha nakshatra mandal kaa 20vaan nakshatra he. yah dhanurashi main aata he. is nakshatra kaa swami shuk he to raashi swami shukr. nakshatra swami kii sarvaadhik dasha 20 varsh kii hoti he. iske baad surya va chandra 16 varsh kii dasha rahati he.
 is nakshatra main jitna bhi yogya varsh hota he, vah lagbhag bachapan se lekar yuvavastha tak yahi dasha chandra ke anusaar rahati he. inke praarambhik jeevankaal main shukr, chandra, surya kaa vishesh mahatv rahegaa. isi dasha-antaradasha main padhai, vivaad, sarvice aadi ke yoga banenge. inkaa janm lagn main shubh hokar baithna ati uttam faldaayi rahegaa.
 shukr kala, dhan, saundarya prashaadhan, beautisian, chikitsa, ejeeniyar, electronic, kamvasana aadi kaa kaarak he. vahiin raashi guru mahatvakanksha, eemaandaaree, dayaa bhav, raajnneetik, prashasnic sanghathan aadi kaa kaarak he. shukr yadi swarashi yaa ucch kaa ho to aise jataka sundar, kalakaar aadi hote hai vahiin guru swarashi yaa ucch kaa ho to ye apane kaaryakshetra main pragati ke saath eemaandaar hote hai. inamen kala ke saath sadgun bhi hote hai.
 mesh lagn ho va shukr nakshatra swami saptam main ho to aise jataka kii stri yaa pati dhani hoga yaa dhan kii kabhi kami nahin rahegi. shukr is lagn main ucch kaa ho to baahar se laabh mili, videsh main rahakar dhan paae.
 dwitiya bhav main swarashi vrishabh kaa ho to aise jataka ko apni vaani se dhan mili. swar uttam ho gayan ke kshetra main safalataa paae. shukr chaturth main dasham main bhi shubh faldaayi hoga. vrishabh lagn main shukr lagn, ekadash, dasham, navam main shubh fal degaa, vahiin raashi swami guru, tritiy, ekadash, chaturth saptam main shubhafaladaayi hoga. shukr chaturth main ho to daanpaty sukh nahin miltaa, lekin aisa jataka janata ke bich prasiddh hota he.
