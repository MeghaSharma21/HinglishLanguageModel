 jaslin dhamijaa ek anbhavi bhartiya kapadaa kala itihaaskaar, shilp visheshgy or sanyukt raashtra ke purv kaaryakarta hai. vah apane hathkargha or hastkala udyog par shodh ke liye jani jaati he. vah minesota vishvavidhyalay main jivit saanskrithik paramparaon kii professor rahe chuki he. unhone vastron par bharat kii pavitra textiles sahit kai kitaaben likhi hai. 
 dhamija uttar pashchimi simavarti praant main abbatabad main bady hui. miranda house, dilli vishvavidhyalay se snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii. 1954 main unhone bharat sarkaar main samskruti or shilp punarutthanavaadee kamaladevi chattopadhyaay ke saath apane kariyar kii shuruyaat kii. 1960 ke dashak main, usne bharat ke hastshilp board ke saath kaam kiya. 
 hahli me vah neshnal institute of fishan technology, nai dilli main kaam karti he. jahaan vah bhartiya kapadaa or veshbhusha kaa itihaas padhaati he.
