 qashkaadariyaa praant madhya asia main sthit uzbekistan desh kaa ek vilaayaat he jo us desh ke dakshini kshetra main sthit he. yah qashkaadariyaa nadi ke jalsambhar kshetra main or pamir parvaton kii pashchimi dhalaanon main sthit he. praant kaa kul kshetrafal 28,400 varg kimi he or 2007 main isaki anumaanit aabaadi 20,67,000 thi. is soobe ke 70% se adhik log gramin ilaaqon main rahete hai. qashkaadariyaa praant kii rajdhani qarshi shahar he.
 'qashkaadariyaa' main 'qa' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'k' se miltaa-julata lekin zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'qeemat' or 'qurabaani' 'qa' se miltaa he.
