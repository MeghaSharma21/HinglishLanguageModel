 shabdaawalee vigyaan, paribhaashik shabdon tathaa unke upyog ke adhyayan kii vidya he. paribhaashik shabd un shabdon, samasik-shabdon yaa bahu-shabdic-abhivyaktiyon ko kehete hai jinka upyog vishisht sandarbh main vishisht arth rakhata he. ye 'vishisht arth' un shabdon ke 'saamaanya upyog ke arth' se bahut alag ho sakate hai. udhaaharan ke liye saadhaaran arth main 'kaary' kaa matlab koi bhi kaam karnaa -jaise khaanaa khaanaa, hansnaa, chalanaa, padhanaa aadi he, kintu bhautikee main bal or us bal ke kaaran uski disha main hue visthaapan ke gunanfal ko kaary kehete hai.
 shabdaawalee vigyaan vah vidha he jo paribhaashik shabdon ke vikaas tathaa anya pahaluon kaa adhyayan karti he. kintu dhyaan dene yogya he ki shabdaawalee vigyaan, koshrachana se bhinn he kyonki shabdaawalee vigyaan main avadharanaon tathaa avadharanaon ke samudaay kaa bhi adhyayan sammilit he.
