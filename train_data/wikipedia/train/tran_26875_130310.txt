 purv qazakhstan praant madhya asia ke qazakhstan desh kaa ek praant he. isaki rajdhani oscamen naam kaa shahar he, jise ust-camenogorsk bhi kaha jaataa he. is praant kii uttari seemayen rus se or purvi seemayen janavaadee ganatantr china ke shinjiyang praant se lagati hai. iskaa sudur purv chor mongolia se keval 50 kimi dur he lekin bich main china or rus kaa zaraa se hissaa qazakhstan or mongolia ke bich aa jaataa he.
 praant ke purvi hisse main altaai parvaton kaa kuch bhaag aata he lekin pashchimi hisson main stapi kshetra kaa maidan vistrut he. praant ke uttar main semey shahar he jise pehle semipalatinsk kaha jaataa tha. yahan soviet sangh ke jamaane main us desh ke parmaanu hathiyaar aazamaae jate the. 1949 se 1989 ke bich yahan 456 parmaanu visphot kiye gaye.
 san 2009 kii janaganana ke hisaab se is praant ke 55% log kazakh samudaay ke or 41% log roosi samudaay ke the. inke alaava yahan ukraini or anya samudaayon ke log bhi rahete hai. rajdhani oscamen main roosi or ukraini logon kii taadaad kazakh logon se zyada he.
