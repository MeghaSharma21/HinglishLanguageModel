 david viley  ek antarrashtriya str ke cricket khilaadi he jo england kii rashtriya cricket team ke liye khelte hai.
 inhone apane ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket karier kii shuruyaat ireland team ke khilaaf 23 june 2015 ko kii thi jabaki tinti-tinti cricket karier kii shuruyaat 07 sitambar 2014 ko newjiland ke khilaaf khelkar kii thi.
 viley ballebaaji ke alaava gendbaaji bhi karte hai ye baanyein haath se tej gati se gendbaaji karte hai. is kaaran viley england cricket team ke ek harfanmaulaa khilaadi kehalaate hai.
