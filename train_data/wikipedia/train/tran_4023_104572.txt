 kuze gune ek turki televijan dharawahik he jisaki patakatha age yorench or malak genzolu ne likhi he. kuze tekinolu, istambul ke ek baker kaa ek sakht or vidrohi betaa he, jo chaar saal baad jail se waapas us aparaadh kii sazaa kaat kar aata he jo asal main usne nahin kiya tha. kuze ne apane bade bhai gune ko bachaane ke liye uskaa gunaah apane sar le liya tha. 
 dharawahik kaa prasaaran turki main channel kanal di par 7 sitambar, 2011 ko shuru hua tha. bharat main iskaa prasaaran zindagi channel par 19 disambar 2016 se shuru hua he. iskaa prasaaran roj raat 10 baje kiya jaataa he.
