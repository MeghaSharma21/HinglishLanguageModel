 aakarnar, jiskaa bair naam "alfaa aridani" he, srothasvini taramandal kaa sab se roshan tara he. yah prithvi se dikhne vaale taron main se nauva sab se roshan tara bhi he. aakarnar bahut garam he or isliye iskaa rang nila he. haalaanki yah suraj kii hi tarah kaa ek mukhya anukram tara he, phir bhi isaki chamak suraj kii 3,000 guna he. taron ke shreneekaran ke hisaab se ise B3 kii shrenee di jaati he. yah prithvi se 144 prakaash-varshon kii duuri par he.
 aakarnar kii ek sift yah he kii yah tara bahut tezi se ghurnan kar rahaa he ke iskaa gol akar pichak gaya he or iske madhyarekha kii chaudaai iske aksh kii lambaai se 56% zyaada he. puri akashganga main jitne taron kaa adhyayan hua he yah un saaron main se sab se pichakaa hua tara he.
