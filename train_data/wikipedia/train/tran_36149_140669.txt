 satyapal malik bhartiya raajnneetijna tathaa vartmaan main jammu or kashmir ke rajyapal hai. 30 sitambar 2017 se 21 agast tak bihar rajya ke rajyapal rahe. isse pehle aligarh seat se 1989 se 1991 tak janata dal kii taraf se saansad rahe. 1996 main samaajawaadee party kii taraf se phir chunaav lade lekin haar gaye. meerut ke ek college se unhone padhai kii he. bhaajapa ke rashtriya upaadhyaksh bhi rah chuke hai. 21 agast 2018 ko jammu or kashmir ke rajyapal niyukt kiye gaye.
