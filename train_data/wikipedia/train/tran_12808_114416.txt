 yamuna drutagami maarg ek drutagami maarg he jo 165 kilometre lambaa he. vaise to yamuna drutagami maarg ko banane kaa elaan saal 2001 main kiya gaya tha. lekin sarkaar badali or badalane ke saath hi saal 2003 main is par kaam band ho gaya. saal 2007 main ise dobaaraa shuru kiya gaya. lekin ab yamuna drutagami maarg industrial deveopment athurity ne is hive ko hari jhandi de di he. dilli agra maarg par bad rahe parivahan dabaav ko is drutagami maarg se kam kiya gaya he. saath hi is drutagami maarg ko nae international airport yani taj aviation hub se bhi joda gaya he. 
 is yamuna drutagami maarg par halke vaahanon ke liye kam se kam gati seemaa 100 kilometre prati ghanta he. jabaki baari vaahanon ko 60 kilometre prati ghanta kii raftaar he yani isse kam speed par chalane vaale vaahanon kii pravesh yamuna drutagami maarg par nahin hoga. tej raftaar se chalane ke kaaran yahan sabase zyada sadak haadase hote he. 
 express ve ko 6 se 8 len kiya ja saktaa he. kancreet se bane is yamuna drutagami maarg par 7 interchange, 35 anderpas, ek rail over brij, ek bada pul or 45 chote pul, 68 trek crossing hai. toll ekatra karne ke liye is drutagami maarg par 3 mukhya toll plaza or interchange loop par 7 toll plaza banaae gaye hai.
 yaha tino toll plaza pe graahak suvidha kend he jahaan aap ja kar apana fast tag bana sakate he. praathmik chikitsa centre bhi he jahaan aap free main praathmik chikitsa le sakate he. sabhi toll plaza se 500 meter kii duuri pe jan suvidha centre he jahaan aap apane khaane kii vasteen kharid sakate he auki kuch samay ke liye aaraam kar sakate he. or yahan free main shauchalay or mutralay kii bhi suvidha he.
