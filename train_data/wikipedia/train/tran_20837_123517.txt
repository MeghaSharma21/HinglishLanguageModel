 narayan baapuji udagikar sanskrit sahitya ke anveshak ke rup main prasiddh the. 
 em.e. tak shiksha praapt kar lene ke ananthar puna ke sarkari hiskool main adhyaaapak ke rup main aapane sarvapratham apana saarvajanik jivan aarambh kiya. uske baad deccan college ke pustkaalay vibhaag main bhi aapane kuch samay tak kaary kiya. 1916 se 1918 tak bhandarkar oriental research institute ke avaitnaik sachiv tathaa 1918 se 1919 tak hastlikhit granthsangrah ke curator ke rup main aap rahe. ukt sanstha se 'mahabharat' ke shuddh sanskaran kii yojana jab bani, aap pradhan sampadak ke rup main 1919 se 1924 tak kaam karte rahe. viraatparv kaa prakaashan aapke hi suyogy sampadakatv main hua. raa.go. bhandarkar ke nibandhon ke chaar bhaag aapane hi sampadit kiye the. itihaas or sanskrit ke vidhyaarthiyon tathaa anveshakon ke liye is pustak kii upadeyata mahatvapurn he. prasiddh german vikshan richard gaaven kii bhagavadgita kii prastaavna kaa aapane angreji anuwaad kiya tha. iske atirikt praakrit mahakavy 'gaudwaho' kaa sanshodhit sanskaran aapane hi antyat parishram se taiyaar kiya.
 sa. 1927 main aap lakve se aakraant ho gaye or kuch hi varshon baad 1931 main aapaki mrityu hui.
