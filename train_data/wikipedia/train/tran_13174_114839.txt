 
 
 uttar pradesh rajya rajmarg 26 bharat ke uttar pradesh rajya kaa ek rajya rajmarg he. 402.03 kilometre lambaa yah rajmarg pilibhit se shuru hokar basti tak jaataa he. ise pilibhit-lakhimpur-baharich-basti maarg bhi kaha jaataa he. yah pilibhit, shahjahanpur, lakhimpur kheri, baharich, shravasti, balrampur, siddharthanagar, or basti jilon se hokar gujarata he.
