 mahashakti ek desh he jo purna vishwa main apana prabhaav failaa saktaa he, or jo budhimaan tarah se aise karata he. mahashaktiyaan ke paas sainik or arthshaastreey shakti hoti he, iske alaava rajanayik prabhaav or "namr" shakti bhi honi chahie. in shaktiyaan se chote shaktiyaan ko mahashaktiyaan kii bhavanaayen kaa aadar karte he.
 agar pure vishwa main keval ek mahashakti hoti he, to us desh ko paramashkti kaha jaataa he.
