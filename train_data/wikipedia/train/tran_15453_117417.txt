 sacriyit carbon yaa sacriyit kathkoyala carbon kaa vah rup he jisamen chote-chote kam aaytan ke khaali sthaan hote hai. bahut adhik maatra main sookshm randhron kii upasthiti ke kaaran sakriy carbon ke kisi tukade kaa prisht kshetrafal apekshaakrit bahut adhik hota he. udhaaharan ke liye, keval 1 gram sacriyit carbon kaa prishth kshetrafal 500 varg me se bhi adhik ho saktaa he. is kaaran kuch rasinic kriyaaon ke liye sacriyit koyala bahut hi upyukt hota he. 
 sacriyit carbon praayah charcol se praapt kiya jaataa he kintu ab adhikar ucch-randhrata yukt biochaar se praapt kiya jaataa he. vishwa swasthya sanghathan kii atyaavashyak dawaaon kii suchi main sacriyit carbon bhi sammilit he.
