 thirumala bharat ke rajya andhrapradesh ke jila chitur kaa ek pahari ton he, jo tirupati rural mandal or tirupati revenue kshetra main aata he. is pahari gaanv main thirumala venkateshwar mandir he, jo shri venkateshwara kaa mandir he.
 thirumala do shabdein se bana he. thiru or mala. thiru kaa arth shre, pavitra or lakshmi ke hai, or mala kaa arth malay, pahad yaa parvat ke hai. arthath thirumala kaa arth, shri parvat, pavitra parvat yaa lakshmi parvat ke hai.
