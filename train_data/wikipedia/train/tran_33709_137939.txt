 rashtriya vittiya prabandhan santhaan, bharat sarkaar ke vitt mantralay kii ek swaayat sanstha he. is sanstha ko kendriya mantrimandal ne manjuuri de di thi. mantralay dwaara kiye gaye prastaav ke aadhaar par 1993 main is sanstha ko sthapit kiya gaya tha. kendriya mantrimandal ne pariklpana kii thi ki raa. vi. pra. san., sangh lok seva ayog dwaara niyukt adhikariyon ke liye ek prashikshan santhaan ke rup main shuru hoga jo kii vaarshik civil seva pariksha ke madhyam se sangh lok seva ayog or vibhinn varisht or sheersh prabandhan lekha or vitt main kaary karne ke liye jimmedaar padon main sevaaon ke liye aavantit bharat sarkaar ke liye kaam karte hai.
