 kisi padaarth kii us parat ko tanufilm yaa patali parat, kehete hai jisaki motaai 1 nanometer ke kuch bhaag se lekar kuch micron tak ho. tanufilm kaa sanshleshan ek moolbhoot prakram he or anek kaamon ke liye upyog main laayaa jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye, gharon main upyog main aane vaale darpan ke peeche dhaatu kii ek patali parat hoti he jo prakaash kaa paravartan karti he .
