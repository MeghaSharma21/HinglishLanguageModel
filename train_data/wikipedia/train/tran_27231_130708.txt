 bhartiya badminton lig bhartiya badminton sangh dwaara shuru kii gayi frenchiji aadhaarit badminton pratiyogita he. is pratiyogita kaa pehla sanskaran 14 se 31 agast 2013 tak bharat main khelaa jaayegaa. isamen bhartiya khilaadiyon ke saath-saath videshi khilaadi bhi shaamil honge. pehle ibiel ke liye khilaadiyon kii nilaamee 22 july 2013 ko dilli main yojit kii gai thi. is lig main 6 timen hissaa lengi. in chah frenchiji tiimon main hyderabad hotshots, banga beats, krish dilli smashars, avadh varirs, pune pistons or mumbai masters shaamil hai. 
 biai ne sabhi sheersh badminton khilaadiyon ke liye is lig main bhaag lenaa anivaary kar diya gaya he. 
 pehle sizan kii nilaamee, jo kii pehle 30 june 2013 ko anusuchit kii gayi thi, pehle 19 july 2013 tak or fir 22 july 2013 tak ke liye taal di gayi. antat: 22 july ko dilli main nilaamee puri hui. 
 har team main chaar videshi or chah bhartiya khilaadi shaamil hai. timen kuch is prakaar hai -
