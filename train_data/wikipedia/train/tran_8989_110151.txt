 apkendriya bal :Centrifugal Force) vah bal hota he jiske kaaran kisi gatisheel vastu main, centre se dur bhaagane kii pravriti hoti he. yah wo abhaasi bal hota he jo abhikendriya bal ke samaan tathaa vipareet disha main kaary karata he. kriim sepperator tathaa centriphyugal dryer apkendriya bal ke siddhant par kaary karte hai. newton yantriki main apkendri bal ek jadtviya bal he jo vrittiya gati karti hui vastuon par gati ke path ke kendra se dur trigya kii disha main lagata hua prathit hota he. vastav main yah ek kalpit bal he, vaastavik nahin.
 aitihaasik drushti se kabhi-kabhi abhikendri bal kii pratikriya ko apkendri bal kaha jaataa rahaa he.
