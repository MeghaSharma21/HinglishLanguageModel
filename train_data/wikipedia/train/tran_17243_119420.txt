 laghu jalavidyut kaa arth he sthaneey samudaay or sthaneey udyogon ke upyog ko puraa karne laayak chote str ke jalavidyut sanyantra. laghujalavidyut kshetreey vidyut grid ko vidyut supplai bhi kar sakate hai. vaise to laghu jalavidyut kii koi bahut paribhaasha nahin he, kintu mote taur par 1 megawatt se 20 megawatt tak ke sanyatra is shrenee main rakhe jate hai. 
 laghu jalavidyut ko doordaraj ke un sthanon par bhi sthapit kiya ja saktaa he jinako mukhya grid se bijli dena bahut kharchila ho.
