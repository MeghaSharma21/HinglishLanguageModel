 2014 rashtramandal khel glassgo, scotland, main 23 july se 3 agast 2014 ke madhya yojit hua. yah olympic va asiad khelon ke baad teesraa sabase badi bahu-khel spardhaa he.
 yah scotland main hone vaala ab tak kaa sabase bada bahu-khel aayojan rahaa jisame 4,560 khilaadi 17 vibhinn khelon main bhaag liya. scotland main isse purv edinbura main 1970 or 1986 ke rashtramandal khel yojit kar chukaa he.
 in khelon ne sanghathan, logon kii upastithi tathaa utsaah ke kaaran kaaphi prashansa pai. rashtramandal khel sangh ke pramukh mike hupar ne in khelon kii prashansa karte hue ab tak itihaas kaa asaadhaaran khel qaraar diya. scotland kii dharti par teesri baar yojit hue in khelon main united kingdom ke grih deshon england, wales tathaa mejabaan scotland ne sabase adhik padak paa kar sarvshereshth pradarshan kiya. england ne 1986 ke khelon ke baad padak talika main sheersh sthaan hasil kiya, jo ki scotland main hi yojit hue the. kiribati ne rashtramandal khel ke itihaas main apana pehla padak jeeta, jo ki bharottolan kii purush 105 kigra spardhaa main swarna tha.
 scotland pehla aisa desh tha jisane 2004 main in khelon ko yojit karne kaa vichaar kiya. sitambar 2004 main glassgo boli ke liye scotland kii taraf se pratibhaagi shahar bana. scottish excicutive ne 16 agast 2005 ko tatkaaleen pratham mantri jaik maconnel ke saath, jinhen united kingdom sarkaar tathaa sabhi mukhya scottish partiyon kaa samarthan praapt tha, ne apana in khelon ko yojit karne kaa apana uddeshy ghoshit kiya.
 march 2006 main boli kii prakriya prarambh hui. glassgo ne apni boli melbern main 2006 rashtramandal khel ke dauraan aadhikarik rup se nigeriayin rajdhani abuja or canadai shahar helifax ke saath rashtramandal khel mahasang ko saunpi. oktober 2006 main matdaan pratinidhiyon ne suvidhaayen dekhne ke liye glassgo shahar kaa daura kiya. 16 janvari 2007 ko glassgo kii boli safal ghoshit kii gai. helifax ne baad main 8 march 2007 ko apni boli waapas le lee.
 mejabaan shahar kaa antim nirnay 9 navambar 2007 ko colombo, srilanka main rashtramandal khel mahasang ke sabha main liya gaya, jahaan 71 rashtrakul deshon ke sadsy maujuud the. in sabhi sadasyon ne gupt matdaan dwaara apana pasandida shahar chunaa. matdaan kaa parinaam is prakaar he
 is toornament main 71 rashtramandal deshon ke lagbhag 4,500 khilaadi bhaag le rahe hai. 7 oktober 2013 ko gambia ne in khelon main bhaag lene se manaa kar diya.
 is rashtramandal khel main kul 17 khelon main 261 padak spardhaaen hui. is baar record 22 para-khelon kii spardhaaen paanch vibhinn khelon main yojit hongi. 2010 rashtramandal khel main shaamil teerandaji va tennis ko is toornament se hataa diya gaya he tathaa triathlan or judo ko joda gaya he. kuch 2010 main shaamil spardhaon ko bhi hataa diya gaya he jiname athletics kii walking spardhaa, sincranaise tairaaki, greeko-roman kushti shaamil he. saath hi nishanebaaji kii padak spardhaaen bhi 2010 main 44 ke mukaable 19 kar di gayin hai. cycling main parvatarohi biking bhi jodi gai he.
