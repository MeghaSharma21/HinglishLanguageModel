 
 uch yaa uch shareef pakistan ke punjab praant ke dakshin main bahawalpur zile main sthit ek aitihaasik shahar he.
 is kshetra main kam-se-kam 500 isapurv se hinduon kii bastiyaan thi. kaha jaataa he ki jab 325 isapurv main sikandar mahaan ne punjab par qabza kar liya to usne sindhu nadi or chenab nadi ke sangam-sthal par apane naam kaa ek shahar banane kaa aadesh diya. is shahar ko 'alakshendria en indo potamo' naam se sthapit kiya gaya or yahi shahar aage jaakar uch shahar bana. samay ke saath chenab or sindhu nadiyon ne apana rukh kuch badla or ab unka sangam yahan se 100 kimi dur mithnkot shahar ke paas padataa he.
 iske bahut baad san 712 isvi main jab arab senaayen islam aane ke baad fail rahi thim to mohammad bin qaasim ne is shahar par qabza kar liya or yahan islamic prabhaav badhane laga. is sthaan par 13veen sadi main gori samrajya kaa bhi qabza ho gaya or uske patan ke baad yah dilli sultanate kaa hissaa bana. in sabhi kaalon main bahut se muslim sant-fakir yahan aa based or shahar ke naam ke peeche 'shareef' lagaaya jane laga . 'uch shareef' kaa matlab 'unchaa pavitra shahar' he. in santon main bibi javindi, jalaal-ud-deen 'surkh-posh' bukaari or behaal haleem shaamil the, jinke madhbare ab is shahar main sthit hai.
