 kupid pracheen roman dharm ke devataaon main se ek hai. vah kaamdev kii tarah prem or kaam-vaasana ke devtaa hai. unke samatuly pracheen unani dharm ke devtaa the iros. unka ek or latini naam he, amor. ye roman devtaa jupiter or devi venus ke putr maane jate hai. unako ek pankh lage bachche kii tarah chitrit kiya jaataa he, haath main dhanush hota he jisase ye baan chhodkar manushyon main prem bhav jaagrit karte hai. kaha jaataa he ki kupid kaa baan lagane par manushya jisase bhi sabase pehle miltaa he usase atyadhik prem karne lagata he. inhen kahaniyon main atyadhik chanchal, chapal or rasik varnit kiya jaataa he. inke tunir main do tarah ke baan hote hai - svarnim nok vaale baan jinase prem jaagrit hota he or sise kii nok vaale baan jinase ghrinaa utpann hoti he. kai chitron main inke tiron kii nok ko hridayakar bhi dikhaate hai. vailemtine diwas ke maukey par khaas taur par kupid ke chitra dekhne ko milte hai.
