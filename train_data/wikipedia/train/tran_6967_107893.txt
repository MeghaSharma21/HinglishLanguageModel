 paudhon main paraag kan kaa nar-bhaag se maadaa-bhaag par sthantharan paragan kehalaata he. paraagan ke uparant nishechan kii kriya hoti he or prajnan kaa kaary aage badhata he.
 paragan kya he?
 jab kisi pushp kaa paragakan nikaalkar kisi duusare pushp yaa phir kisi duusare paudhe ke pushp tak pahunchataa he, to is kriya ko paragan kehete hai
 paragan kii kriya do vidhiyon dwaara hoti he:-
 1 svaparagan
 2 parparagan
 1 svaparagan :- jab paragan kii kriya kisi pushp ke paragakosh se paragakan nikaalkar usee paudhe ke pushp par parata he to vah svaparagan kehalaata he.
