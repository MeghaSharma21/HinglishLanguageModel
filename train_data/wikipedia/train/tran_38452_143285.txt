 maatr hindi cartoon masik patrika he. iskaa prakaashan 5 disambar 1996 ko raipur, chhattisgarh se prarambh hua. yuva cartoonist tryambak sharma ne is patrika kii neenv rakhi.
 cartoon vaach ne shankars vikali ke band hone ke baad upje shunya ko samaapt kiya or aaj yah patrika sampoorn bharat ke cartooniston kaa mukh-patr ban gai he. yah patrika gum ho chuke cartooniston ko charchaa main laane ke saath-saath nae cartooniston ko bhi manch pradaan karti he. cartoon vaach dbaara gat varsh main landan ke nehru centre main do saptaah kii cartoon pradarshnee yojit kii thi jise kaaphi saraahaa gaya.
 cartoon vaach prativarsh desh ke ek prasiddh cartoonist ko 'lifetime achievement award' pradaan karti he. ab tak yah puraskaar aar ke lakshmana, sudhir tailang, aabid surati, praan, rajendra dhodapakar, ech em sudan, suresh saavant, shyaam jagota, ajit naiynan, kaak, hossain jameen, bi.ve.pandurangarao or jagajeet singh rana ko diya ja chukaa he. yah puraskaar cartoon vaach dbaara yojit cartoon utsav kaarykram ke dauraan pradaan kiya jaataa he. cartoon vaach prativarsh nae cartooniston ko mauka dene or nai pratibha ko talashne ke liye akhilbhartiya or rajya stareey pratiyogitaayen bhi yojit karti he. is patrika ke prarambh se cartoonist aabid surati, bi el wahi, ve ve panduranga raav, dhanesh divakar jude rahe hai. varttman main isamen kirtish bhatta, rajesh dube, pankaj goswami, prabhakar jhalke, nirmish thakur, em ke shiri, bi hari, gopal krishna, vins, suresh saavant, devanshu vatsa, abhishek tiwari, ismail lahari, shyaam jagota, chandrashekhar hada sahit bharat ke sabhi pradeshon ke cartooniston ke cartoon hote hai.
 cartoon vaach ko limca book of world records main shaamil kiya gaya he. yah desh kii ek maatr masik cartoon patrika he jo hindi or angreji dono bhashaaon main prakashit hoti he.
 cartoonist dhanesh divakar
