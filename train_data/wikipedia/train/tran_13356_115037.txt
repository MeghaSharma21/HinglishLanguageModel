 wiliam vilson scottish mul ke 19veen shatabdi ke patrkaar, tairaaki prashikshak evam coach, tathaa pratiyogi tairaaki kii vaigyanik takneekon ke yogadanakarta the. 1883 main unhone di swimming instractor prakashit kii, jo ki stroke kii dakshata, suraksha tathaa prashikshan kii aadhunik avadharanaon ko paribhaashit karne vaali pehali pustakon main se ek thi.
 1877 main wilson ne ek samuhik jaleey gend kii khel ke niyam banaae, jise unhone jaleey footbal kaa naam diya. 1885 main great britain ke tairaaki sangh ne is khel ko manyata di, jise ab water polo kaha jaataa he, tathaa wilson kii niyamavali kaa vistaar kaarte hue niyamon kii sthapana kii. 
 1891 main wilson ne jivan raksha abhyaason par kai vistrut tathaa sodaaharan lekh prakashit kiye, tathaa sthaneey tairaaki sanghon ko jivan rakshak takneekon main kushalta ke liye puraskrit kiya. wilson kii vidhiyaan ek pustika ke rup main vitarit huin, tathaa unke yogadan ko manyata dene ke liye unhen royal lif-seaving socity kaa pratham lif governor nirvaachit kiya gaya tha.
