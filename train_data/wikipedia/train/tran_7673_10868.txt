 berite ek khanij he jisamen barium sulphate paaya jaataa he. iskaa rang safed yaa laal, chamak kachopam, kathorta 3-3.5 tathaa aapekshik ghanatv 4.5 hota he.yah khanij orthorobik samudaay main crystalikrut hota he. andhra pradesh ke kadappa jile kii mangampet khan iske liye prashidd he.
 berite se safed varnak taiyaar kiya jaataa he. tel ke kune khodate samay gas ko rokane ke liye berite kaa prayog hota he. isse anya rasayanak taiyaar kiye jate hai, jinka upyog anek kaamon main hota he.
 yah khanij adhiktar chooney kii shilaon main dhariyon main miltaa he. dhatvik nikshepon ke saath bhi yah khanij paaya jaataa he. england main westmorland county kii sise kii khadaan se berite kaa ek sau pound bhaar kaa ek crystal uplabdh hua tha. bharat ke andhra pradesh berite kaa sabase bada utpaadak he. lagbhag 90 pratishat berite yahan ke karnul or kadapa jilon se praapt hota he. berite ke anya mahatvapurn nikshep rajasthan main alvar ke nikat hai.
