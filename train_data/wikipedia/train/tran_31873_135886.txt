 suniti chaudhuri bharat kii ek krantikari nari thim. swatantraaa sangram main bahut kam avasthaa main bada karnama karke dikhaane vaali suniti us samay ke prasiddh deepali sangh kii sadasya thim. aazaadee kii ladai main suniti chaudhuri kaa karnama sunakar log aaj bhi daanton tale angulii dabaa lete hai. 
 24 disambar 1931, tripura
 fajunnisa balika vidhyalay kii do chaatraon kumari shanti ghosh or kumari suniti choudhary ne magistrate bi ji steevenson se milane kii anumati maangi | kaaran poochne par unhone uttar diya kii wo ladakiyon kii tairaaki pratiyogita ke sandarbh main unase kuch baat karnaa chaahati hai. magistrate ke kamare main panhuchate hi unhone goli chalaa di. un viranganaon kaa nishaanaa achook tha, steevenson vahiin mar gaya. donon vir balaayein giraftaar kar lee gayin. 27 farvari 1932 ko unhen ajanm kaala pani kaa dand hua. sattaavanee kranti ke baad yah pehali ghatana thi jisame kisi mahila ne raajnneetik hatya kii thi. 
 jahaan is veertapurna kaary kii saraahna honi chahie thi vahin gandhivaad or ahimsa ke nashe main chur sardaar ballab bhai patel ne kaha ki ye donon ladkiyaan bhartiya naariyon ke liye kalank swaroop hai. mahaan krantikari manmathanath gupt ji likhte hai -" itihaas hi batayega ki ye ladkiyaan bharat ke itihaas kaa kalank nahin hai | haan is prakaar kaa bayaan avashya kalank tha |"
