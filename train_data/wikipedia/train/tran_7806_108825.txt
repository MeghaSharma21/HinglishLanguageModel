 gopal baalkrishna kolhatkar bharat ke rasayanashastri the. 
 unka janm satara jile ke ek chote se gaanv jaakhan main hua tha. gaanv kii praathmik paathshaala main shiksha paakar mumbai ke maratha hai skool main or baad main saint jevirs kalege main bhautikee or rasayan kaa adhyayan kiya. rasayan kii em. e. pariksha main aapako swarnapadak mila. tadnantar bangalore ke bhartiya vigyaan santhaan se anusandhan kaary dwaara e. ai. ai. es-si kaa diploma praapt kiya.
 1907 i. main puna ke fergyusan kalege ke aaajeevan sadsy banakar rasayan ke praadhyaapak niyukt hue or 1947 i. tak kalege se sambhddha rahe. fergyusan kalege kii rasayanashala ko nirmit tathaa susajjit karne kaa shrey aapako he. aisi susajjit rasayanashala puna vishvavidhyalay se sambhddha kalejon main se kisi main nahin he. bombai vishvavidhyalay ke sinet or ecademic kausil ke aap sadsy rahe. aapke anek chaatr rasayan ke adhyaapan or anusandhan kaary main khyaati paa chuke hai. aapane angreji main rasayan kii kuch paathya pustaken bhi likhi hai. 9 navambar 1955 i. ko aapkaa dehavsaan hua.
