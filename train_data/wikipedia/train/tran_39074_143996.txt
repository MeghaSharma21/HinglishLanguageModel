 maanak se panjikrut hone ke liye do prakaar ke lekha-pareekshanon: ek bahari pramann nikaay dwaara lekha-pareekshan or is prakriya ke liye prashikshit aatanrik karmchaariyon dwaara lekha-pareekshan, kii aavashyaktaa he. samiksha or moolyankan kii ek nirantar prakriya hi iskaa lakshya he jiske tahat - yah satyapit kiya jaataa he ki system usee tarah kaam kar rahaa he jis tarah se ise karnaa chahie, pataa lagaaya jaataa he ki isamen kahaan sudhaar ho saktaa he or pahachaani gai samasyaon ko sahi yaa rokthaam kiya jaataa he. aatanrik lekha-pareekshakon ke liye ise gunakari mana jaataa he jab ve apane saamaanya prabandhan rekha se baahar lekha-pareekshan karte hai, jisase ki unke nirnay main swatantraaa kii ek degree laai ja sake.
 1994 ke maanak ke tahat, lekha-pareekshan kii prakriya ko paryaapt rup se "anupaalan lekha-pareekshan" kaa pradarshan karke sambodhit kiya ja saktaa tha:
 2000 maanak ek alag drushtikon kaa upyog karata he. lekha-pareekshakon se jokhim, sthiti or mahatv par prakaash daalkar "anupaalan" ko kanthsth karne kii keval lekha-pareekshan ke paar jane kii ummid kii jaati he. iskaa matlab yah he ki unase yahi ummid kii jaati he ki aupchaarik rup se kya nirdhaarit he, keval isi kaa paalan karne ke bajaay, kya prabhaavshaalee he, is par adhik gaur karen. pichley maanak se iske antar ko is prakaar samajhaayaa ja saktaa he:
 lekha-pareekshan ke liye ISO 19011 kaa maanak anya prabandhan pranaaliyon, jaise - EMS, FSMS ityadi, ke atirikt ISO 9001 main bhi lagu hota he.
 ISO 9001 maanak samanyikrut or amoort he. iske hisson kii vyakhya saavdhaanee se kii jaani chahie jisase iske ek vishesh sanghathan ke bheetar hone kaa arth nikal sake. software kaa vikaas karnaa, paneer taiyaar karne yaa paraamarsh seva pradaan karne jaisaa nahin he; phir bhi ISO 9001 ke disha-nirdeshon ko inamen se prayek main lagu kiya ja saktaa he kyonki ve vyavsaay prabandhan ke disha-nirdesh hai. vibhinn sanghathanonâ€”police vibhaag, peshewar footbal team or nagar parishad â€”ne ISO 9001:2000 kii pranaaliyon ko safaltaapurvak karyanvit kiya he.
 samay ke saath, vibhinn udyog kshetron ne apane disha-nirdeshon kii vyakhyaaon ko apane vipanan-sthal ke bheetar manakikrut karne kii kaamana kii he. yah aanshik rup se yah sunishchit karne ke liye he ki ISO ke sanskarnon kii apni vishisht aavashyaktaaen hai, balki yah bhi koshish or sunishchit karnaa he ki adhik upyukt prashikshit or anbhavi lekha-pareekshakon ko unka moolyankan karne ke liye bheja jaataa he.
 saamanytah ISO 9000 kii prabhaavashiltaa par bahes kaa centre nimnlikhit prashn hai:
 ise vyapak rup se sweekaar kiya gaya he ki uchit gunvattta prabandhan aksar nivesh, share vipanan, bikri vruddhi, bikri margin, pratispardhi laabh or mukademebaaji ke parihaar par ek sakaaratmak prabhaav daalkar vyavsaay main sudhaar laata he. vade, or barns ke anusaar ISO 9000:2000 ke gunvattta siddhant bhi bahut achche hai, jo kehete hai "ISO 9000 ke disha-nirdesh gunvattta prabandhan pranaaliyon ke liye ek vyapak model pradaan karte hai jo kisi bhi company ko pratispardhi bana sakate hai." barns loyad ke register quality esurance ke ek surveykshan kaa bhi uddhrut dete hai jisane sanket diya ki ISO 9000 ne shuddh laabh main badhottaree kii, tathaa vah ek or surveykshan kaa uddhrut prastut karte hai jise deloit-tach ne kiya tha jisane khabar di ki panjikaran kii laagat ko teen saalon main vasula gaya. providence bijnes news ke anusaar ISO kaa karyanvayan nimnlikhit laabh pradaan karata he:
 haalanki, 800 spanish companiyon ke ek vyapak sankhyikeey adhyayan se pataa chalaa ki ISO 9000 panjikaran apane aap main hi thodaa-bahut sudhaar laata he kyonki isamen ruchi lene vaali companiyon ne aam taur par pehle se hi gunvattta prabandhan ko kuch is tarah kii patibaddhta kii he or panjikaran se pehle bas aise hi uskaa pradarshan kar rahi thi.
 ISO 9001 kii ek aam aalochana panjikaran ke liye aavashyak dhanrashi, samay or kagaji kaarywahi he. barns ke anusaar, "virodhiyon kaa daawa he ki yah keval pralekhan ke liye he. samarthakon kaa maananaa he ki agar ek company ne apni gunvattta pranaaliyon ko pralekhith kiya he, to adhikansh kagaji kaarywahi pehle se hi puri ho chuki he." 
 ISO 9001 kisi bhi tarah se is baat kaa sanket nahin he ki isaki pramanit pranaaliyon ke istemaal se utpaadit utpaada kisi bhi tarah se achche hai. ek company karaab gunvattta vaale utpaada kaa utpaadan karne kaa iraadaa kar saktaa he or lagaataar evam uchit pralekhan ke saath is par ISO 9001 kaa mohar laga saktaa he. ceddan ke anusaar, ISO 9001 samaj or sudhaar ke bajaay vinirdesan, niyantran or karypranaliyon ko badhaava detaa he. vade kaa tark he ki ISO 9000 ek dishanirdesh ke rup main prabhavi he, lekin ise ek maanak ke rup main badhaava dene par "yah companiyon kii soch ko gumraah karne main madad karata he ki pramann kaa matlab behatar gunvattta he, ... jisase sanghathan ko apane khud ke gunvattta maanakon ko sthapit karne kii aavashyaktaa hoti he." savistaar ke taur par, vade kaa tark he ki ISO 9001 ke vinirdeshnon par bharosa karne se ek safal gunvattta pranali kii guarantee nahin milti he.
 antarrashtriya str par manyata praapt hone par bhi zyaadaatar america upabhokta ISO 9000 ke bare main jaanate nahin hai or yah unke liye koi mahatv rakhti he. pramanit karne or uske baad pramaan patr kaa rakrakhaav karne kii atirikt laagat ko uchit nahin tehraaya ja saktaa he agar utpaada ke antim upyogkartaaon ko ISO 9000 kii aavashyaktaa nahin he. vastav main is laagat se ek company ko ek gaiyr ISO 9000 pramanit company ke khilaaf pratispardhaa karte samay ek pratispardhaatmak nuksaan kaa saamana karnaa pad saktaa he. 
 is maanak ko khaskar vifaltaa ke khatre ke rup main dekha jaataa he jab ek company kii ruchi gunvattta se pehle pramann main ho. vastav main pramann aksar gunvattta main sudhaar lene kii ichha ke bajaay graahak ke sanvidatmak aavashyaktaaon par aadhaarit hote hai. "yadi aap keval diwaar par pramaan patr dekhnaa chaahate hai, to ho saktaa he, aap ek aisi kagaji pranali kaa nirmaan karenge jiske liye aapako zyada kuch karne kii jarurat nahin he jis tarah se aap apane vyavsaay ko chalaanaa chaahate ho," ISO ke roger frost ne kaha. ek swatantra lekha pareekshak dwaara pramann ko aksar ek samasya kshetra ke rup main dekha jaataa he or barns ke anusaar "paraamarsh sevaayen badhaane kaa ek madhyam ban gaya he." vastav main, ISO khud salaah detaa he ki ISO 9001 ko pramann ke bina, keval gunvattta ke laabh ke liye jise hasil kiya ja saktaa he, lagu kiya ja saktaa he.
 ek or suchit samasya kai pramann nikaayon ke bich kii pratispardhaa he, jo ek form kii gunvattta pranali ke parichaalan main dekhe gaye dosha ke ek sahaj drushtikon tak le jaataa he.
 abrahamsan kii daleel thi ki fashnabal prabandhan samvaad, jaise - gunvattta ke daayre, main ghanti kii vakrata ke rup main ek jivan-chakra kaa anusaran karne kii pravriti hoti he jo shaayad ek prabandhan sanak kaa sanket he.
