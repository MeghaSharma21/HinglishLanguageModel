 ankhon ke apvartan dosha se matlab he - 'aakhon dwaara prakaash ko retina ke upar focus na kar paanaa balki retina ke pehle yaa baad main focus karnaa'. iske kaaran chizon ko dekhne main pareshaani hoti he. apvartan dosha se grasit drushti visham drushti kehalaati he.
 visham drushti nimna prakaar kii hoti he :
 deergh drushti - yah us prakaar kii visham drushti he jisamen netra kaa mukhya aksh laghu ho jaataa he, athwa netra kii apvartan shakti ksheen hoti he. at: samaantar prakashakirane retina ke parshv main sangamit ho jaati hai.
 nikat drushti - yah us prakaar kii visham drushti he jisamen netra kaa mukhya aksh deergh ho jaataa he, athwa netra kii apvartan shakti adhik ho jaati he. at: samaantar prakashakirane retina ke samaksh sangamit ho jaati hai.
 drushti vaishamay - yah us prakaar kii visham drushti he jisamen netra ke vrittakaron main prakaash kaa apvartan bhinn bhinn hota he.
 drushtivaishamy do prakaar kaa hota he :
  niyamit 
  aniyamit 
 aniyamit drushtivaishamy moulik dosha ke kaaran hota he, jaise kiretons, athwa praapt dasha, jaise cornea kii apaardarshakta.
  saadhaaran deergh drushti drushtivaishamy, yogic deergh drushti drushtivaishamy, saadhaaran nikat drushti drushtivaishamy, yogic nikat drushti drushtivaishamy tathaa mishrit drushtivaishamy, jisamen ek vrittakar deergh drushtivaishamy, jisamen ek vrittakar deergh drushti evam anya nikat drushti hoti he.
