 bhartiya rail main rikt padon kii bhartee railway bhartee bordon ke madhyam se kii jaati he. is samay puure bharat main kul 21 railway bhartee board kriyasheel hai.
 yah railway bhartee board samay samay par vistaarit hui nitiyon ke aadhaar par nimnlikhit uddeshyon ke liye gathit hua he.
 1. vidyaman railway bhartee bords ke kaam ke boj ko samekit bananaa.
 2. bhartee prakriya main saralata laana.
 3. bhartee agency ko duurastha ilaake main rahane vaale ummidavaaron ke kareeb laana.
 4. akhil bhartiya str par bhartee karnaa.
