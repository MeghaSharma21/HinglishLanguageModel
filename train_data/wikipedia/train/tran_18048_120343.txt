 jhagadate rajyon kaa kaal pracheen china ke purvi jhou rajvansh kaal ke dusre bhaag ko kehete hai, jo louh yug main lagbhag 475 isapurv se 221 isapurv tak chalaa. purvi jhou raajkaal main is se pehle basant or sharad kaal aayaa tha. jhagadate rajyon ke kaal ke baad 221 isapurv main chin rajvansh kaa kaal aayaa jinhonen china ko phir se ek vyavastha main sangathit kiya. dhyaan rakhane yogya baat he ki purvi jhou kaal main vaise to jhou samraat ko sarvocch kaha jaataa tha, lekin yah sirf naam maatr hi tha - sari shaktiyaan vastav main bhinn rajyon ke raajaaon-jagiradaaron ke paas thim.
 'jhagadate rajyon ke kaal' kaa naam haan rajvansh ke dauraan likhe gaye 'jhagadate rajyon kaa abhilekh' naamak itihaas-granth se liya gaya he. is baat par vivaad he ki 'basant or sharad kaal' kis samay khatm hua or 'jhagadate rajyon kaa kaal' kab shuru hua, lekin bahut se itihaaskaar jin naamak rajya ke vahaan kii teen shaktishaalee pariwaron ke bich ke vibhaajan ko is kaal kii aarambhik ghatana maanate hai or yah 403 isapurv main hua tha.
