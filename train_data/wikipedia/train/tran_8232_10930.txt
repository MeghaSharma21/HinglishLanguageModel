 dow jaiprakash narayan bharat ke rajaneta, samaaj-sudhakarak, tivi tippaneekar tathaa stambhakaar hai. ve lok satta ke adhyaksh hai tathaa vartmaan main andhra pradesh vidhaan sabha ke sadsy hai. iske purv ve bhartiya prashasnic seva main bhi naukarshah the. ve bharat main kai chunaav sudhaar lagu karwaane main safal rahe hai. unhone lokapal vidheyak kaa masauda bhi banaaya he.
 jaiprakash ne 1990 ke dashak main bhartiya prashasnic seva se istipha dekar loksatta kaa gathan kiya tha. iske jariye ve loktantric sudhaar ke liye andolan chalaanaa chaahate the. loksatta ko unhone 2009 ke chunaav se pehle raajnneetik party bana diya or vidhansabha chunaav main hyderabad ke kuketapalli vidhansabha kshetra se unhone jeet hasil kii.
