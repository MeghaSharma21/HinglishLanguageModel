 pandit gaurishankar hirachand ojha, bharat ke prasiddh itihaaskaar the. 
 unhone raajaputaane ke itihaas ke lekhan ke liye vistrut thospurn peethika bana kar ek agradoot kii bhaanti itihaas kaa pranayan kiya, kai prashnon ke uttar diye, kai prasang kaayam kiye. unhone nirantar khoj va gambhir adhyayan kebaad rajnatik ghatnavali kii sunishchit parampara kaayam kar tatkaaleen aadarshon ke anusaar itihaas lekhan kaa prayaas kiya. kai avidit tithiyon ko udghaatit kiya tathaa ashuddh tithiyon kaa shuddhikaran kiya. anek trutit vanshawaliyon ko shuddh kiya tathaa anekanek aitihaasik lupt kadiyon ko joda. sthaneey sroton kaa farsi aadi sroton se sahi talmail baitha kar ghatnaon ko parakhane kaa suytn kiya. is prakaar rajasthan ke itihaas sambandhi vaigyanik dhang se anusandhan karne vaale ve ekmaatr vidwaan the.
