 prakashvaidyut cell ek ardhchaalak yukti he jis par prakaash yaa anya vidyutchumbakeey vikiran padane se uskaa koi vaidyut gun badal jaati he yaa jo vidyut vibhav paidaa karata he yaa prakaash aadi paidaa karata he. apane is gun ke kaaran yah ek tarah se prakaash-sansuchak kaa kaam kar saktaa he. prakaashachalakeey cell prakaash utsarji cell evam prakashwoltiy cell iske udhaaharan hai.
