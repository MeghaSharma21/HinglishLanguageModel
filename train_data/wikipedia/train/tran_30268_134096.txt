 ghatigaanv bharat ke madhya pradesh ke gwalior zile ke gwalior tehsil main sthit shahar he. yah gwalior se 35 kimi dur sthit he, jo ghatigaanv ke zila or up-jila mukhyalay donon he. 2009 ke aankadon ke anusaar, ghatigaanv bhi ek gram panchayat he.
 shahar kaa kul bhugolic kshetra 1860.1 hectare he. ghatigaanv kii kul aabaadi 5,641 logon kii he. ghatigaanv main lagbhag 1,088 ghar hai. gwalior, ghatigaanv ke najdiki mahanagar he jo lagbhag 35 kimi dur he.janaganana 2011 kii jaankaree ke anusaar ghatigaanv kaa sthaan code 453771 he.
 yahan se e ech-46 haive gujarata hai.
