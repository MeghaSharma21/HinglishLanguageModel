 lav yaa louh arthath kan yaa loha, tamil: இலவன், malay: tilavi, indonesian: laav, khmeri: jupalaks, lao: phra lao, thai: phra lop, telegu: లవుడు) apane judawaan bhrata kush ke saath hindu bhagwaan ram evam unki bharya sita ke putr the. aitihaasik tathyon ke anusaar ye lavpuri ke sansthaapak the, jo vartmaan main pakistan sthit shahar lahore he. lahore ke kile main inkaa ek mandir bhi bana hua he. dakshahin-purv asian desh laos evam thai nagar lobpuri, donon hi unke naam par rakhe gaye sthaan hai.
 ye donon judawaan bhai louh or kush apane pita ram ke samaan hi yashasvi hue or inhone kramshah lahore evam kasur nagaron kii sthapana kii thi.
 pakistan main punjab ke lahore ke shahi kile ke bhitar lav kaa ek mandir bhi sthit he. yah mandir alamgiri daravaaje ke nikat sthit he, jahaan lahore kile kaa purana karagaar hua karata tha.
