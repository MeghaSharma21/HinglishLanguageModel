 chipko aandolan ke praneta sundarlal bahuguna kaa janm 9 janvari san 1927 ko devon kii bhoomi uttarakhand ke silyara naamak sthaan par hua. praathmik shiksha ke baad ve lahore chale gaye or vahiin se bi.e. kiye.
 san 1949 main miraben va thakkar bappa ke sampark main aane ke baad ye dalit varg ke vidhyaarthiyon ke utthan ke liye prayasarat ho gaye tathaa unke liye tehri main thakkar bappa hostal kii sthapana bhi kiye. daliton ko mandir pravesh kaa adhikaar dilaane ke liye unhone aandolan ched diya.
 apni patni shrimati vimala nautial ke sahyog se inhone silyara main hi 'parvatiy navajivan mandal' kii sthapana bhi kii. san 1971 main sharaab kii dukaanon ko kholane se rokane ke liye sundarlal bahuguna ne solah din tak anshan kiya. chipko aandolan ke kaaran ve vishvabhar main vrikshamitr ke naam se prasiddh ho gaye.
 bahuguna ke 'chipko aandolan' kaa ghoshvakya he-
 sundarlal bahuguna ke anusaar pedo ko kaatane kii apeksha unhen lagaana ati mahatvapurn he. bahuguna ke kaaryon se prabhavit hokar america kii friend of nechar naamak sanstha ne 1980 main inako puraskrit bhi kiya. iske alaava unhen kai saare puraskaron se sammanit kiya gaya.
 paryaavaran ko sthai sampati maananevaala yah mahapurush aaj 'paryaavaran gandhi' ban gaya he.
