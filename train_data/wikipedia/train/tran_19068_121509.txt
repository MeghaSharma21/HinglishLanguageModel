 kumaaran aashaan  ek vishwa vikhyath malayalam kavi or darshanik the. unako aashaan ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. vah ek kavi hone ke saath-saath samaaj-sudhaarak, tathaa patrkaar the. kumaaran aashaan kaa janm 12 april 1873 ko tiruvananthapuram ke nikat kayikkara gaanv main hua tha. 16 januvari 1924 ko unka nidhan ho gaya.
 
 nirupakon kehete hai ki- " kumaran aashan kaa veen poou malayalam main svachhndathawad andolan kii praarambhik rachanaaon main sarvaadhik mahatvapurn he. malayalam kaa svachhndathawad aashan kii kavitaaon ke rup main pallavit or pushpit hua. nalini, lila, chintavishtyaay sita, chandalabhikshuki, prarodanam, duravastha, karunaa ityadi inaki mukhya rachanaayen hai. aashan jis kavy prapanch ko anaavrutt karne main safal hue vah gambhir darshaniktaa, jeevandarshan kaa adamya kautuhal or teevr bhavvibhorta se bhasvar he. aashan hi vah kavi the jinhone shringaar ko saamaanya dharaatal se swargik vishuddhta tak pahunchaaya. aadhyatmik prem kii sudar kalpana ne unki kavita ko prabhapurit kiya he."
