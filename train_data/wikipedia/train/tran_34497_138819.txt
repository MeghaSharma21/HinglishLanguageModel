 vidhishastra yaa nyaayashastra, vidhi kaa siddhanth, adhyayan va darshan hai. isamen samast vidhik siddhanth sammilit hai, jo kanoon banaate hai. vidhishastra ke vidwaan, jinhen jurist yaa vidhik siddhantavaadi bhi kaha jaataa hai, vidhi, vidhik karann, vidhik pranali or vidhik sansthaaon ke prakruti kii gahari samaj praapt kar paane kii aashaa rakhte hai.
 vidhi ke vigyaan ke rup main vidhishastra, vidhi ke bare main ek vishesh tarah kii khojbin he. saadhaaran arth main samast vaidhanik siddhant vidhishastra main antarnihit hai. vidhishastra "jurisprudence" arthath Juris = vidhaan, Prudence = gyaan. is arth main kanoon kii sari pustaken vidhishastra kii pustaken hai. is prasang main kanoon kaa ekmaatr arth hota he desh kaa saadhaaran kanoon, jo un niyamon se sarvatha pruthak he, jinhen kanoon se saadrishy rahane ke kaaran kanoon kaa naam diya jaataa he. yadi ham vigyaan shabd kaa prayog iske adhik se adhik vyapak rup main kare jisamen bauddhik anusandhan ke kisi bhi vishay kaa gyaan ho jaae to ham kah sakate hai ki vidhishastra desh ke saadhaaran kanoon kaa vigyaan he.
 pracheenatam bhartiya vidhishastra dharamshastra ke anek paathon main maujuud hai, jaise kii bodhayan kaa dharamshastra.
 vidhi main maanav ko bhaavnaatmak sambal pradaan karne va prakruti dwaara pradutt sukh-suvidhaon ke samyak upbhog hetu samaan adhikaar he.vidhi dwaara maanviya hit main sangrakshit yah vidha hi "prakritik vidhi" kahi jaati he.

 vidhishastra teen shakhaon main vibhakt he-
 vaidhanik abhidarshan kaa uddeshy he kisi prastaavit vidhi kii pranali ke tathy ko, chaahe vah vartmaan ho athwa bhootkaal main iskaa astitv rahaa ho, upasthit karnaa. vaidhanik itihaas kaa uddeshy he us aitihaasik prakriya ko upasthit karnaa jisase koi kanoon pranali viksit hui he yaa hui thi. vidhinirmaan ke siddhant kaa uddeshy he kanoon ko upasthit karnaa-vah kanoon nahin jo vartmaan he yaa bhootkaal main tha, balki vah kanoon jo desh, kaal, paatr ke anusaar hona uchit he. vidhishastra ko kisi vaidhanik pranali ke vartmaan yaa bhoot se apeksha nahin he, yah iske adarshamay bhavishya se sambhddha he.
 vidhishastra siddhanth ke teen ang hote hai- vishleshnatmak vidhishastra, aitihaasik vidhishastra, evam naitik vidhishastra.
 vishleshnatmak shakha main krambaddh vaidhanik siddhant ke darshanik athwa saamaanya vichaar hote hai; aitihaasik shakha main vaidhanik itihaas kaa darshanik athwa saamaanya bhaag hota he; naitik shakha main vidhannirmaan ke darshanik siddhant rahete hai. kintu ye teenon shakhaayen paraspar sambhddha hai. at: inhen ek duusare se pruthak kar inpar vichaar nahin kar sakate.
 dekhen, vishleshnatmak vidhishastra
 vishleshnatmak vidhishastra kaa uddeshy hota he - 'vidhaan ke moulik siddhanton kaa vishleshan'. inke aitihaasik udgam, vikaas, naitik bhav athwa manyata par is prasang main vichaar aavashyak hota he. iske antargat nimnlikhit vishay aate hai-
 mulat: vidhaan ke saadhaaran siddhanton ke udgam evam unke vikaas se sambhddha he. jin sroton se desh kaa saadhaaran vidhaan prabhavit hota he, ve bhi isaki seemaa ke antargat he. anya shabdon main, yah vidhaan ke mul siddhant evam unki paddati kii bhaavana kaa itihaas he.
 naitik vidhishastra, vidhaan kii vivechna naitik ganbhiry evam isaki purnataa kii drushti se karata he. kanoon kii pranali ke bauddhik tatv athwa iske aitihaasik vikaas se ise koi prayojan nahin he. vidhaan ke uddeshy evam kis seemaa tak tathaa kis rup main isaki purti hoti he, yahi iskaa vishay he. saadhaarant: iskaa lakshya evam uddeshy kisi raajnneetik ferme ke antargat rajya kii bhautik shakti dwaara nyaay kaa paalan karne main he. at: naitik vidhishastra yah dekhtaa he ki nyaay ke siddhant kaa vidhaan se kahan tak sambandh he. yah naitik evam vaidhanik darshan kaa milanbindu he. apane saamaanya rup main nyaay, naitikta athwa naitik darshan se sambhddha he. apane vishesh rup main nyaay, desh ke kanoon kii antim shrunkhlaa ke rup main vaidhanik darshan kii us shakha se sambhddha he, jise naitik vidhishastra kehete hai. isaki paridhi ke antargat saamaanya: nimnlikhit vishay aate hai-

 sanathan bhartiya vidhaan main vidhishastra dharamshastra par aadhaarit he. "dharm" kii paribhaasha nimnlikhit rup main kii gai he-
 arthath veda, smriti, sadachaar evam suniti dharm ke udgam hai. "dharm" vyapak shabd he. dharmik, naitik, saamaajik evam vaidhanik drushti se yah manushya ke kartavya evam daayitv kii samshti he. dharmik evam dharm nirpeksh bhaavana ke bich vibhaajan rekha sthapit nahin kii ja sakati, kyonki kitne hi vishay aise hai jo dharmik evam sansaarik donon hai.
 bharat kaa sanathan dharm raja athwa shaasha ke aadesh par aadhaarit nahin he. isaki manyata isi main antarnihit he. smritikaron or unke purvajon ne kaha he ki "dharm bhagwaan kii dein he. yah raajaaon kaa raja he. isse adhik shaktishaalee doosraa koi nahin. isaki sahaayta se shaktiheen bhi shaktishaalee se apana adhikaar le sakate hai. raja nyaay kaa nirmaataa nahin, keval iskaa paalak he."
