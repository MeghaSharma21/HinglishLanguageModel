 mor yaa pevo taramandal khagoleey gole ke dakshini bhaag main dikhne vaala ek taramandal he. isamen kuch mukhya taron ko lakiron se jodakar ek kaalpanik mor kii akruti banaai ja sakati he. "pevo" latini bhashaa main "mor" ke liye shabd he. isaki paribhaasha aupchaarik rup se san 1612 yaa 1613 main patras plankiyas naamak dach khagolashastri ne kii thi haalanki iske naam kaa prayog 1597-1598 se hi shuru ho chukaa tha.
 mor taramandal ko angreji main "pevo constlation" bulaayaa jaataa he lekin kabhi-kabhi "peakock constlation" bhi kehete hai, kyonki "peakock" angreji main "mor" ke liye shabd hota he. farsi main is "taoos surat-e-falki" kehete hai. "taoos" farsi main "mor" ko kehete hai, udhaaharan ke liye vishwa-prasiddh "mor singhaasana" ko kabhi-kabhi bharat main bhi "takht-e-taoos" kaha jaataa he. "surat-e-falki" kaa arth "falak" main taron se banane vaali "surat". unani main bhi ise "taoos asterismos" kaha jaataa he. unani main "aster" taare ko kaha jaataa he.
 mor taramandal main 7 mukhya taare hai, haalanki vaise isamen 24 taron ko bair naam die ja chuke hai. inamen se 5 ke ird-gird gair-saureey grah parikrama karte hue paae gaye hai. is taramandal ke mukhya taare or anya vaastuen is prakaar hai -
