 mukhya anukram yaa main sikwans ek taron kii shrenee he. hazaron-lakhon taron ke adhyayan ke baad dekha gaya he ke bahut se chote aakaar ke taron main taare ke rang or uski nirpeksh kantimaan main gaharaa sambandh hota he. in taron kii chamak jitani zyaada ho ve utane hi nile nazar aate hai or chamak jitani kam ho ve utane hi laal nazar aate hai. aise taron ko mukhya anukram taare yaa boune taare kaha jaataa he.
