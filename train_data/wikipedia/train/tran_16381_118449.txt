 laal masjid islamabad, pakistan ke rajdhani main sthit ek masjid he. ek mahilaayein ke liye ek dharmeey vidhyalay, jamiya hafsaa madrasah or ek purush madrasah, masjid se sanyukt he.
 laal masjid kaa nirmaan 1965 main kiya gaya or iskaa namakaran isaki laal deevaaron or aantarik bhaag ke kaaran kiya gaya. rajdhani vikaas praadhikaran ke anusaar laal masjid pakistan kii sabase pracheen masjidon main se ek he. moulana mohammad abdullah ko iskaa pratham imam niyukt kiya gaya. 1998 main moulana mohammad abdullah kii hatya kar di gayi jiske baad abdul azeez gaazi or abdul raashid gaazi ne masjid main padbhaar sambhaal liya or yahan kattarpanthi shikshan or sarkaar ko khule virodh kaa ek kendra banane kaa kaary kiya.
