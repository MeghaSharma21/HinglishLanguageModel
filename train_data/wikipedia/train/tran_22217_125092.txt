 saan chrisatobal de laa laguna tenreef dweep samooh ke uttari bhaag main santa cruge ke tenreef praant main ek shahar he. ye canary dweepasmooh spain main sthit he. ye shahar aabaadi kii ore se is dweepasmooh kaa teesraa sabase bada shahar he. 
 yahan laa laguna vishvavidhyalay he jo 30,000 chaatro ke liye shiksha kaa ek strot he. laa laguna ko kenry dweepasmooh kii saanskrithik rajdhani bhi kaha jaataa he. 2010 main ek surveykshan ke anusaar, is shahar ko ek behatar pratishtha ke liye sarvshereshth shahar or puure spain main teesraa, gizon or marbela ke baad, sthaan hasil kiya. ise 1999 main unesco ne vishwa dharohar main shaamil kiya. 
 1494 i. main yahan par aguaar kii ladaai hui thi. alphonso lugo dwaara is shahar kii niv 1496 or 1497 ke dauraan rakhi gayi. aguaar kii ladaai ke baad ye is dweepasmooh ke rajdhani ban gaya. 1701 main laa laguna vishvavidhyalay kii niv rakhi gayi. aarthiktaa or jansankhiya kii kami ke kaaran 1723 i. main santa cruge de telepher ko isaki rajdhani banaaya gaya. ye tab se hi isaki rajdhani he. 2 dasambar 1999 ko ise vishwa dharohar main shaamil kiya gia.
 isse pehle is shahar ko aguaar bulaayaa gaya tha. iskaa yah naam iske mul nivaasiyon ne rakhaa tha, jo kii gunches the. yah naam ab bhi sahitya main prayog kiya jaataa he. baad main ise saan chrisatobal de laa laguna kaha jane laga. thode samen baad ise saan chrisatobal laa laguna kaha jane laga. aaj kal ise sirf laa laguna hi kaha jaataa he.
