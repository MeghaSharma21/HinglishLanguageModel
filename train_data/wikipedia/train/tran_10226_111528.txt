 talvya vyanjan wo vyanjan hote hai jinke uccharan main jeebh ke pichley bhaag ko taalu se sangharsh karnaa padataa he.c varg ke samast akshar isi nisarg ke hai or inhen sparsh sangharshi kaa bhi abhidhaan dete hai.jaise ki : "c" "chha" "ja" "jha" "na". samaran rahe ki bindu vaale akshar bhi isi shrenee se sambandhit hai.jaise ki "ji" kaa uccharan "tch" ke samaan hoga, thik vaise hi "ji' kii dhwani "tchh" jaisi hogi. or ye donon akshar videshi bhashaa kii dhvaniyon ko darshane ke liye prayukt hote hai.saath hi saath dhyaan dene kii aavashyaktaa he ki "za" yaa "ji" talvya dhvaniyaan nahin hai balki yah "ooshm dhvaniyaan" maani jaati hai.
