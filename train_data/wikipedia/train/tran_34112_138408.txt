 bodhisattva prasiddh itihaaskaar di di kaushambi dwaara rachit ek marathi naatak he. yah granth prathamatya 1945 main prakashit hua tha. isaki prastaavna dattatreya baalkrishna kalelkar ne likhi thi. is naatak main chaar ank hai.
 'rajkumaar siddharth ne vruddh, rogi, mrit or sanyasi ko dekhakar raat main grihatyag kiya tha' is baat ko kaalpanik yaa prakshipt maanakar kosambi ji ne tripitak sammat ghatana ko is naatak ke madhyam se atyant rochak tareeke se prastut kiya. anya granthon main bhi unhone shaakyon va coliyon ke rajyon ke bich bahane vaali rohinee nadi ke jal se upasthit vivaad ko siddharth kumaar ke ghar chhodane kaa aitihaasik kaaran man or use anek pramaanon se siddh bhi kiya. kintu tab bhi swayan ke dwaara upasthaapit pramanpurna tathy apurna hi lagane lage, to tripitak ke hi pramaanon se paripusht is ghatana ko or adhik spashtataa se upasthaapit karne ke liye unhone ‘bodhisattva’ namak chaar ankon kaa chota sa naatak likh diya. is naatak main unhone tripitak sammat siddharthabhinishkaraman kii karneebhoot ghatana ko atyant rochakata se upasthaapit kiya.
 dow prafull gadpal ne marathi bhashaa se is naatak kaa sanskrit main anuwaad kiya, jo rashtrisanskritsanthanam se 2013 main prakashit hua.
