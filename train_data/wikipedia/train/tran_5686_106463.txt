 rashainshastra main tri-bandh do parmaanuon ke madhya ke us aabandh ko kehete hai jisamen 2 electron vaale sah-sanyoji aabandh kii tulana main 6 electron bhaag lete hai. sabase saamaanya tri-bandh, elkinon main do carbon-parmaanuon ke madhya paye jate hai. iske atirikt anya tri-bandh vaale yogicon main cynide or iso-cynide hai. kuch dwiparmanvik anu jaise dwi-nitrogen or carbon monoaksaide main bhi tri-bandh paye jate hai. rasinic sutara main tri-bandh ko darshane ke liye donon anuon ke madhya teen samaantar rekhaon banaayi jaati hai.
 tri-bandh, tulya ekal-bandh or dwi-bandh se chote or adhik majboot hote hai jinaki bandh koti teen hoti he.
