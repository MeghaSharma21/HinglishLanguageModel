 vidyut kosh vidyut urja kaa strot he jise rasinic urja se praapt kiya jaataa he. vaidyut abhiyantriki evam electranix main do yaa do se adhik vidyutrasinic selon ke sangyojan ko vidyut kosh kehete hai. ye rasinic urja bhandarit karte hai evam is urja ko vidyut urja ke rup main uplabdh karte hai.
 san 1800 main alesandro volta dwaara sabse pehle bydaree kaa avishkaar hua. aajkal adhikansh gharelu evam audyogic upyogon ke liye vidyut kosh hi vidyut urja kaa pramukh saadhan he. san 2005 ke ek anumaan ke anusaar vishwa bhar main vidyut kosh kii bikri lagbhag 48 billian america dollars ke baraabar hota he.
