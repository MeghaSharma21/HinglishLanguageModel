 kush ayodhya ke raja ram or sita ke putr tathaa lav ke judwa bhai the.
 kush va lav ne ram ke ashwamegh ghode ko pakad kar ram ko yuddh ke liye chunauti de daali thi.ayodhya ke sabhi veeron ko chote se balak ne haraakar yah siddh kar diya tha; shakti kaa gurur khatarnaak hota he.kushvaha vansh ke vaahak kush ne apni maan sita ko nyaay dilaane ke liye ayodhya raja sah pita se bhari sabha main samvaad kiya or maan sita ko pavitra or satya savit kiya. maan sita ne apane rajya ko kush ke haath main saunp diya or khud dharti maan ke garbh main chali gayi. tabhi se kush ko stri nyaay karta mana jane laga. sita ke garbh main jane se ram vyathit ho gayi or apana rajya kush or anya bhaaiyon ko soup gaye. kush kii aane vaali vanshaj kushvaha kahalaayi.
 
 yah vansh magadh main sthapit tha.
