 shin rajvansh pracheen china kaa ek rajvansh tha jisane china main 9 isvi se 23 isvi tak ke chote kaal main raaj kiya. ise rajvansh to kaha jaataa he lekin isamen samraat sirf ek hi tha. yah haan rajvansh ke kaal ke bich main aayaa. is se pehle ke haan kaal ko 'pashchimi haan rajvansh' kaha jaataa he or iske baad ke haan kaal ko 'purvi haan rajvansh' kaha jaataa he.
 sheen rajvansh kaa iklauta samraat wang mang tha. pashchimi haan kii rajmata, maharani wang jhengajun wang parivaar se ai thi or uskaa vivaah haan rajpariwaar main hua tha. wang mang usee kaa bhaanja tha or wang parivaar kaa tha. usne is sambandh kaa faaydaa uthaaya or singhaasana hadap liya. san 9 isvi ko usne swayan ko samraat ghoshit kar diya. vaise to vah ek chatur vidwaan tha lekin rajya vyavasthit rakhana uski kshamta se baahar tha. ek kisaanon kaa vidroh bhadak gaya or vidrohiyon ne 23 isvi main rajdhani changaan par gheraa daal diya. is jhadap main vah maaraa gaya or uskaa shin rajvansh vahin samaapt ho gaya. haan rajvansh phir se singhaasana par behaal ho gaya.
