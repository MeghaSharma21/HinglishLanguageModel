 kim jong un uttari korea ke sarvocch netaa hai. ve kim jong il ke putr hai. or kim sung kaa pota he. isane 28 disambar 2011 ko apane aapako taanashaah bana liya or uski aadhikarik ghoshnaa bhi kar di.
 kim ke praarambhik jivan se judi sabhi jaankaree keval uttar korea ke taanashaah dwaara yaa phir un logon ke dwaara hi mili he, jo use videshon main dekhen hai. jaise ki switzaraland ke vidhyalay main kim ke dwaara padhane kii ghatana. uttar korea ke vibhaag vaale kim ke janm kii taarikh 8 janvari 1982 he, bataate hai. jabaki dakshin korea ke aadhikarik vibhaag ise or baad main he bolta he. denis rodman ne kaha ki uskaa janm 8 janvari 1983 ko hua tha or ve kim se sitambar 2013 ko mili the. yah apane teen bhaaiyon main se doosraa he.
 japani samachar-patr main sabase pehle prakashit hue ek soochna ke anusaar kim switzaraland ke hi ek vidhyalay main padhai kiya tha. vah us vidhyalay main "chol-pak" yaa "pak-chol" naam se 1993 se lekar 1998 tak tha. jo ek sharmile ladke va basketball premi ke rup main vahaan padhai kar rahaa tha.
