 pradhanmantri ek aisa rajaneta hota he jo ki sarkaar kii kaaryakarini shakha kaa sanchaalan karata he. saamanytah, pradhanmantri apane desh kii sansad kaa sadsy bhi hota he.
 bharat main pradhanmantri yaa anya koi mantri chah maah tak bina sansad sadsy rahete hue bhi pad par bane rah sakate hai lekin unhe chah maheene ke andar sansad ke kisi bhi sadan kaa sadsy bananaa padega. agar pradhanmantri yaa mantri is avadhi main sansad ke sadsy banane main vifal rahete hai to unhe tyaagptr dena padega. lekin iskaa yah arth kadaapi nahin ki har baar chha: maah ke liye aap sadan ke sadsy na rahete hue bhi mantri pad par asin rahe. is sambandh main ucchtam nyaalay kaa nirnay atyant mahatvapurn he. bharat ke vartmaan pradhanmantri varsh 2014 main nirvaachit shri narendra modi hai jo bhartiya janata party ke sadsy tathaa varanasi se saansad hai.
