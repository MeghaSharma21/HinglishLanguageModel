 is parivaar kii bhashaaen austrailia mahadweep ke sabhi pradeshon main mulnivaasiyon dwaara boli jaati hai or ek hi strot se nikali hai. ye ant main pratyay jodanevaali, yogtmak, ashlisht prakruti kii hai, is kaaran kuch log inhen dravid bhashaaon se sambhddha samajhate the. is parivaar kii tasmenia bhashaa ab samaapt ho chuki he. anya bhashaayen bhi junglee jatiyon kii hai. samast austrailia mahadweep kii janansakhya main ye mulnivaasee keval 50-60 hajaar rah gaye hai.
 in bhashaaon main mahapraan vyanjanon ko chhodkar kavarg, tavarg or pavarg ke teen-teen vyanjan hai. chaaron atasth bhi hai. swaron main i, i, u, uu, e, e, o, o vidyaman hai. ekvachan, dwivachan or bahuvachan kaa prayog hota he. kahin-kahin trivachan bhi he. kriya kii prakriya jatil he jisamen sarvnaam jud jaataa he. sangya kii kartri, karm, sampradaan, sambandh, apadan aadi vibhaktiyaan bhi hai.
