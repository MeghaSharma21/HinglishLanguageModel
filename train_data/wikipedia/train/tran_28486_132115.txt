 iareneses-1eff bhartiya kshetreey nauvahan upgrah pranali ke saat upgrahon kii shrinkhalaa main chatha upgrah he. isse pehle iareneses-1e, iareneses-1bi, iareneses-1si, iareneses-1di or iareneses-1i antariksh main prakshepit kiye ja chuke hai. yah upgrah bhartiya kshetra ke liye nauvahan sevaayen pradaan karne ke liye saat upgrahon ke nakshatra kaa hissaa he. yah upgrah bhu-samakalik kaksha main sthapit kiya gaya he. ise pieselvi-exel si32 se 10 march 2016 ko 16:01 iasti par choda gaya. 
 is upgrah main do tarah ke pelod hai. nauvahan pelod upyogkartaaon ko disha suchak nauvahan sanket bhejega or reging pelod main si-band transponder he jo upgrah ke kaaryakshetra kaa sateek nirdhaaran karne main sahaayak he. iskaa jeevankaal 12 saal he.
