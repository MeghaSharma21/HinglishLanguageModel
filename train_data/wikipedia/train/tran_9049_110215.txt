 medical nyaayashastra vigyaan or chikitsa kaa ek shetra he jisamen in donon ke istemaal se kanoonni samasyaon ko sulajhane kaa prayaas kiya jaataa he. is shetra main karyrat mukhyatah doctor evam medical shiksha se jude vaigyanik hote hai, jinka daayitv mrityu kaa kaaran evam usase sambandhit anya prashnon ke bare main maranottar pariksha ke baad raay dene kaa hota he. 
 aadhunik chikitsa kanoon ke dwaara banaaya gaya kshetra he jo kii rajya ke dwaara niyantrit kiya jaataa he. iske nyaay pranali main prayog se mrityu se jude aparaadh, balaatkaar ke badhati ghatnaayen or pitrutv se jude maamalon kii jaanch kii jaati he. kanoon se sambandhit anya maamle jaise ki vivaadaaspad pitrutv, balaatkaar sunishchit karnaa, aprakritik sharirik sambandh, ghaav ke kaaran tay karnaa, vishaktan aadi main bhi inaki mahatpurna bhoomika hoti he| 
 medical nyaayashastra apradhi kii maansik sthiti pataa lagane main madad karte hai. isse yah bhi pataa chalata he kii yah vyakti kisi parikshan pranali ke liye swasth he yaa nahin. is kshetra kii madad se yah bhi pataa lagaaya jaataa he kii mrityu ko kitna samay ho gaya he or mrityu kaa kaaran kya he agar kisi vajah se kaaran spasht na ho to. is kshetra ke dwaara un sabhi choton kaa bhi pataa lag jaataa he jinako bahut samay purv sharir par anubhav kiya gaya ho. yah kshetra maut kaa pramaan patr dilwane main bhi sahaayak he kyonki iske antargat vigyaanan or kanoon donon kaa hi prayog kiya jaataa he.
