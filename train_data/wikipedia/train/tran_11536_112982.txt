 santiago de kompostela bada girjaghar vishwa viraasat sthaan santiago de komposatela main sthit ek bada girjaghar he. yahan sant james ko dafnaaya gaya tha jo isa messiah ke prachaarkon main se ek the. 
 katha ke anusaar prachaarak sant james isaai mat ko ibrani peninasula main lekar aae. san 44 main jerusalem main unka sar kalam kar diya gaya. unke sharir ko gaalisia, spain main laayaa gaya. 3ri sadi main unki kabr kii beparwahee hone lagi.katha ke anusaar san 814 main pelagius ne unki kabr ko phir se dhundha jab usne raat ke samay aasmaan main ajeeb-o-garib raushnee dekhi.
