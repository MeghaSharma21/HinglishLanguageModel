 bhaj govindam 5veen shatabdi i. pus. kii ek lokapriy hindu bhakthi rachana he, jo sanskrit main likhi gayi he, tathaa ise likhane kaa shrey aadi shankaracharya ko he. yah rachana yah rekhaankit karti he ki prabhu ke prati bhakthi saamaanya aadhyatm kaa ek ati mahatvapurn bhaag he, jiskaa ki bhakthi yoga va bhakthi andolan dwaara samarthan kiya gaya he. 
 yah kaaljayi rachana is baat kii smarika he ki aadi shankar jinhen gyanmarg kaa mahaan purodha mana jaataa he ve bhi bhakthi ko samaan lakshya tak pahunchaane vaali bataakar prashansa karne main nahin hichkichaye. tathaa jaisaa ki chakravarti rajagopalaachari ne apane bhashya main likhaa he, jab budhimatta paripakv hoti he to hriday main jamkar jaden jamaa leti he, vah pragna ban jaati he. jab vah pragna jivan ke saath sanyukt hoti he tathaa kriya kaa rup le leti he tab bhakthi ban jaati he. gyaan jo paripakv hota he bhakthi kehalaata he. yadi yah bhakthi main nahin badalata to nirupayogi he.
