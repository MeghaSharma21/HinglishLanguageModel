 kisi yogic ke sambhavan ke samay uske 1 mol ke nirmaan main hone vaala purna ooshmaa parivartan us yogic kaa maanak sambhavan purnoshma yaa maanak sambhavan ooshmaa kehalaata he. yogic kaa nirmaan usamen maujuud tattvon se hona chaiye, tathaa sabhi padaarth apane maanak avasthaa main tathaa 1 vayumandaleey daab par hone chaiye. maanak sambhavan purnoshma ko ΔHfO yaa ΔfHO se darshaya jaataa he. is nirupan main aane vaala theta yah dikhaata he ki prakriya maanak sthitiyon main diye hue taap par hui he. maanak avasthaaen nimnlikhit hai-
 udhaaharan ke liye, carbon daai oxide kii maanak sambhavan purnoshma, nimnlikhit abhikriya kii purnoshma ke baraabar hogi. 
 sabhi tatv apane maanak avasthaa main likhe jate hai, tathaa 1 mol utpaada kaa nirmaan hota he. yah sabhi sambhavan purnoshmaon ke liye satya he.
