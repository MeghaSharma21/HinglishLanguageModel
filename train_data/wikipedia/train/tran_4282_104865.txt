 bikaner se 36 mile dur dakshin main basaa yah gaanv aitihaasik drushti se mahatvapurn he. yahaan raav bika ke tisare chachaa udha rinmalot ke do putron panchayan tathaa sanga kii devliyaan hai, jo kramash: 1511 or 1524 i0 kii hai. anumaanath: panchayan ne yah gaanv basaaya tha or usee ke naam se isaki prasiddhi he.
