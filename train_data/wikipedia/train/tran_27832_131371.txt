 orlando nite club golibari 12 june 2016 ko hua tha. jisamen 19 varshiya umr matin naamak aatankwaadi ne america ke pulse nite club main 50 logon kii goli maar kar hatya kar di. yah club orlando, florida main sthit ek samalangik sabhasthal he. sanyukt rajya america ke itihaas main yah sabase bheeshan golibari he, tathaa 9/11 ke uparant 15 saalon main yahan sabase bada aatanki hamalaa he.
 umr matin afkhaan mul kaa america naagarik tha. uske paas bandook kaa license tha, tathaa wo democratic party kaa panjikrut sadsy tha. 2015 main umr matin ke pita siddiki matin ne taliban kaa samarthan kiya tha, evam taliban ke viruddh pakistani sena kii kaarywahi ke kadi aalochana kii thi.
 hamale se pehle 911 apatkaaleen dhoorbhaash number par matin ne apane aap ko islamic rajya kaa sadsy bataaya tha. raatri ke 2 baje matin ne club ke andar kai logon ko maar daalaa evam baaki ko bandhak bana liya. pulicekarmi jaldi hi ghatnasthal par pahunch gaye, parantu bandhakon kii suraksha hetu unhone club ke andar pravesh nahin kiya. subah paanch baje unhone bandhakon ko chhudaane kaa nirnay le liya. jaise hi ve club ke andar pahunche, matin ne or golibari shuru kar di. police ne use maar daalaa, tathaa uttarajive bandhakon ko chhudaa liya. orlando ke mahayor ke anusaar ab tak 50 logon kii maut ke pushti kii ja chuki he, evam 53 log ghayal hai.
