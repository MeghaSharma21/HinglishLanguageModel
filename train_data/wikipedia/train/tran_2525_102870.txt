 hindun air force stesan kaa hawaii drishy
 hindun air force stesan  bhi kaha jaataa he ,ek bhartiya vaayu sena kaa vimaanashetra he jo bhartiya rajya uttar pradesh ke ghaziabad zile main sthit he. is vimaanashetra kii sataha kancreet or asphalt se bani hui he. yah vimaanashetra hawaii base ke hisaab se puure asia main sabase bada or saath hi vishwa kaa aathvaan sabase bada air force stesan he. hindun air force stesan 14 kilometre failaa hua he jabaki 2 kilometre golai main failaa hua he. 
 yah air force stesan har saal 8 oktober ko air force diwas manaata he.
