 annami pahadiyaan dakshinpurvi asia ke hindchin kshetra ke purvi bhaag main ek 1,100 kilometre tak chalane vaali parvatamala he. yah laos, vietnam or purvottaree cambodia ke ek chote ilaake par vistrut he. laos ke siengkhuang praant main khadaa 2,598 meter unchaa phu bia is parvatamala kaa sabase unchaa shikhar he.
 annami pahadiyon main do bhoomiya parishetra aate hai. ek mausami ushnakshetreey van, jisamen garmiyon main varsha or sardiyon main shushki rahati he. doosraa annami parvat ardra van, jo swayan do parikshetron main vibhajit he: dakshini annami parvat ardra van or uttari annami parvat ardra van. in kshetron main kai aise praani rahete hai jo keval yahan yaa sameepi kshetron main hi milte hai, jaise ki annami khargosh, saola, duuk langur, gaur, chini pangolin or hindchini baagh.
