 sharada math saraswati kaa mandir he jise shri narayan guru ne kerala main varakala ke paas shivgiri naamak gaanv main banaaya tha. gurudev ne poornima ke avasar par, 1912 april main, shri sharada math main mata saraswati deviji kaa pratishtha kiya. sharada abhishek samiti ke adhyaksh ke rup main dow palpu apana karatvay nibhaayaa. mahakavi shri kumaaran aashaan sadsy ke sachiv the.
