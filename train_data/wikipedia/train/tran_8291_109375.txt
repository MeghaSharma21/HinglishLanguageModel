 sar fradrick wiliam harshal ek germany main paidaa hue british khagolashastri or sangeetkaar the. 19 varsh kii umr main ve germany chhodkar britain main aa based. unhone hi urenus grah kii khoj kii thi. yah doorabeen dwaara pahachaanaa gaya pehla grah tha. unhone iske atirikt urenus ke do upgrahon kii or shani ke do upgrahon kii bhi khoj kii. haalanki ve apni khagolashastriy gatividhiyon ke liye zyaada vikhyath hai, unhone apane jeevankaal main 24 sangeet kii tukadiyaan bhi likhin.
