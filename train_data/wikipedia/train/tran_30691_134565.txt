 june 2016 main, da avangers ke liye sangeet dene vaale alan sylvastry, ko infinity war or uski agali kadi main sangeet dene ke liye chunaa gaya. sylvastry ne kaha ki pehle prayek charitra kii theme ko film ke score main shaamil karne ke liye charchaaen huin, "lekin har koi ekamat tha ki is prayaas se vikarshan ke atirikt or kuch nahin hoga." silavestry ne film ke liye sangeet janvari 2018 main record karnaa shuru kiya, or march ant tak kaam puraa kar liya.
 is film ke sangeet par kaam karne ko sylvestri ne ek bilkul alag anubhav bataaya, or kaha ki yah unke "ab tak ke kiye gaye sabhi kaamon se bilkul alag tha." sylvastry ne prayek infinity stone par alag dhun banane se parhez kiya, jaisaa ki unhone captain america: da first avanger main tesaract ke saath kiya tha. sylvastry ke anusaar "infinity stones ke liye sangeet vastav main thanos kii pratikriya ke aasapaas banaaya gaya he. har baar jab use koi stone mila tha, vah pal hameshaa mahatvapurn or aksar bhaavnaatmak tha."
 sylvastry kaa puraa score orcestral he, lekin vah film main blike panther se ludwig gornson kii theme kaa upyog kiya gaya he. film main da spinners kaa "da rabarband main" geet bhi shaamil he, jo tab bajataa he, jab ki gargiens of da gallexi pehali baar dikhaai dete hai.
