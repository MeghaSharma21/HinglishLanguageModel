 nirdhoom powder chote agnyashastra main istemaal hone vaale pranodak he jiske sakriy avyav nitroglicyrin avam nitrocelulose hote he. yah nirdhoom powder single base powder hote he jab yah keval nitrocelulose ke prayog se banate he. or dabl base powder tab hote he jab yah nitroglicyrin avam nitrocelulose donon ke mishran se banate he. nitroglicyrin avam nitrocelulose ke alaava barium nitrate, potacium dikromet, grafite avam wesilin kaa prayog bhi kiya jaataa he.
