 moksh
 dhyaan yoga 
 
 hamaare janm se mrityu ke bich kii avadhi hi jivan kehalaati he. lekin hamaara janm kya hamaari ichha se hota he? nahin, yah to maatr nar or maadaa ke sambhog kaa parinaam hota he jo prakruti ke niyam ke antargat he. iske atirikt jivan kaa mukhya ang ek chetan tatva he jo jivan kii sabhi kriyaaon kaa sakshi hota he.
