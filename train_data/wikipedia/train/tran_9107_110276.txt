 ghaatak varsh 1996 main bani nirdeshak rajkumaar santoshi kii ection-drama aadhaarit hindi bhashaa kii film he. film kii mukhya bhoomikaaon main sani dyol, minakshi sheshadri, amrish puri evam danni denjongpa ne abhinay kiya he. abhinetri minakshi sheshadri kii yah unki antim film he. film kaa "aapthudu" naam se telegu bhashaa kaa punarsanskaran bhi nirmit hua jinamein abhinayta rajashekhar ne mukhya bhoomika kii thi.
 is film ki safalataa kaa adaja is baat se lagaaya ja saktaa he ki kareeb 4 crores ki laagat se bani is film ne baks aphis par kareeb 40 crores kaa shandaar karobaar kiya tha.
