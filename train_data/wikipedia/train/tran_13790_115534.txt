 1851gregory kaaldarshak kaa buudhvaar ko prarambh hone vaala ek aam varsh or julian kaaldarshak kaa somvaar ko prarambh hone vaala ek aam varsh tha, jo aam yug or ano domini padnaam kaa 1851 varsh, 2 sahasrabdi kaa 851 varsh, 19 shatabdi kaa 51 varsh, or 1850 dashak kaa 2  varsh tha. 1851 ke shuruyaat par, gregory kaaldarshak julian kaaldarshak se 12 din aage tha, jo 1923 tak sthaneey upyog main rahaa.
