 train tu pakistan yaa pakistan mail suprasiddh angreji upanyaaskaar khushvanth singh kaa 1956 main america ke grove press award se puruskrit upanyaas he. yah agast 1947 main bharat vibhaajan kii traasadee par kendrit he.
 ‘train tu pakistan’ main punjab ke ek kalpit gaanv ‘manu maajaraa’ kii kahani kahata he. yah gaanv bharat-paak seemaa ke qareeb hi sthit he yahan sadiyon se musalmaan or sikh mil-jul kar rah rahe hai.par desh ke vibhaajan ke saath hi sthitiyaan badal jaati hai or log ek-duusare ke koon ke pyaase ho jate hai. yah sara vrittant ek prem kahani kii prishthbhumi main he.
