 koyala aabantan ghotaalaa bharat main rajnatik bhrashtachar kaa ek nayaa maamla he jisamen niyantrak evam mahalekhapareekshak ne bharat sarkaar par aarope lagaaya he ki desh ke koyala bhandar manamaane tareeke se nijee evam sarkari abantit kar diye gayi jisase san 2004 se 2009 ke bich 10,67,000 karod kii haani hui. sansad main pesh kaig report main july 2004 se ab tak hue 142 koyala blaak aavanton se 1.86 lakh karod rupee ke nuksaan kaa anumaan lagaaya gaya he. report main kaha gaya ki 2004 se 2009 ke bich koyala khadaanon ke theke dene main aniyamitaayen barti gayi. behad sasti keemton par bagaiyr nilaamee ke khadaanon se koyala nikaalane ke theke nijee companiyon ko die gaye. isse sarkari khajaane ko 1.86 lakh karod rupee kaa anumaanit nuksaan hua he. bharat ke loktantrik kaal main pehali baar hua he ki kisi maamle main desh ke pradhanmantri par oongali uthaai gayi ho. bhaajapa kol block aavanton maamle main pradhanmantri manmohan singh ke istife kii maang kar rahi he.
