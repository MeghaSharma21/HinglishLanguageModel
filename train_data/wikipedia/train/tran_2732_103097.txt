 al joff, libya ke dakshinpurv main sthit jile kufr kaa rajdhani shahar he. shahar 24°13′N 23°18′E / 24.217°N 23.300°E / 24.217; 23.300 par sthit he or samudr tal se isaki ausat oonchaai 382.2 meter he. 1984 kii janaganana ke anusaar shahar kii jansankhya 17320 thi. al joff main barish na ke baraabar hoti he, varsh bhar kii ausat varsha sirf 1 inch hoti he. garmiyon kaa ausat taapmaana 37.8 °C se upar rahata he.
 shahar kufr basin ke sabase bade nachlistaan main sthit he. yah sahaara marusthal kaa sabase adhik sinchit marudyan he.
 2011 ke virodh pradarshnon ke baad is kshetra kaa niyantran kathith rup se muammar al-gaddaphi kii sarkaar ke haathon se nikal kar libya ke vipaksh ke haathon main chalaa gaya tha.
