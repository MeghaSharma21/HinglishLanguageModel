 hefei purvi china ke anhui praant kii rajdhani or sabase bada shahar he. yah ek upapraant kaa darja rakhane vaala ek nagar he. yah anhui praant ke kendriya bhaag main sthit he. san 2010 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 57,02,466 anumaanit kii gai thi jisamen se 33,52,076 iske shahari kshetra main rah rahe the.
 hefei main garmiyon main mausam garam or naam hota he. july main ausat taapmaana 28.1 °centegrade hota he, jabaki kabhi-kabhi yah 37 °centegrade yaa us se bhi zyada pahunch saktaa he. garmiyon main baarishein bhi padati hai. sardiyon main taapmaana shunya se neeche gir jaataa he or kabhi-kabhi zaraa-bahut barf girti he. janvari kaa ausat taapmaana 2.6 °centegrade rahata he. may or june ke maheenon main aasapaas ke dehati ilaaqon main kisaan apane kheton main agali fasal kii taiyaari karne ke liye aag laga dete hai jis se puure shahar par aksar ek mithi-gandh vaala dhuaan chaa jaataa he.
