 jini or juju ek bhartiya swarrakalpana vaala naatak he jo sab tivi par prasaarit hota he. yah 5 navambar 2012. se 6 june 2014 tak chalaa. iske 100 prakaran purna hone ke baad amritsar main is kaarykram ke puure dal ne dhamal machaaya tha.
 vikram khanna ek baar vimaan udaate hue ek jagah rukta he vahaan uski mulaakaat jini se hoti he. iske baad jini use juju kahati he or uske peeche pad jaati he. jini vikram se pyaar karne lagati he. lekin vikram usase dur bhaagate rahata he. jini ke bare main keval juju ke dost vela ko hi pataa rahata he. iske kai dinon ke baad zinjora naamak ek jin jini se shaadi karne ke liye aata he. vah juju ko kaid kar letaa he. lekin baad main use haraa dete hai. jab jini 2001 varsh kii ho jaati he. to jin lok se kai jin jini se shaadi karne ke liye aate hai. lekin jini kai kaaran bataa kar un logon ko manaa kar detii he. iske baad ant main jini or juju ko ek saath naachte hue dikhaaya jaataa he ki donon ek duusare se pyaar karte hai. iske baad yah samaapt ho jaataa he.
