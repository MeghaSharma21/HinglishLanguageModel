 yahan ke ham sikandar bhartiya hindi bhashaa main bana naatak tivi shrunkhla he jo 2011 se 2013 tak doordarshan neshnal par prasaarit hui thi. is dharawahik main tom alter, sakasham daayma, anu nain, dhruv raaj sharma, tanvi chaturvedi or punit kumaar kii mukhya bhoomika thi. dharawahik ko doordarshan ke liye dilip sood dwaara nirmit, nirdeshit or utpaadit kiya gaya tha. is dharawahik kii kahani or samvaad taaki imam or harish vyas ne likhe the.
