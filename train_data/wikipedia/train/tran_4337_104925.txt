 aravalli jila bharat desh main gujarat rajya ke 33 jile main se ek mahatvapurn jila he. jile kaa mukhyalay modasa he. aravalli kii pahari shrinkhalaa se jile kaa naam aravalli padaa he. 15 agast 2013 ko gujarat ke tatkalin mukhyamantri narendra modi ne sabarkantha jile kaa vibhaajan karke naye aravalli jile kii ghoshnaa kii thi.
 aravalli jila gujarat or rajasthan kii seemaa ko jodta he. jile kaa naam aravalli kii parvatamala se padaa he. ye aravalli kii parvatamala daanta or modasa ke bich main sthit he. gujarat sarkir dwaara 2013 main 7 naye jile kii rachana kii gai thi tab aravalli banaaskantha jile se alag hokar nayaa jila banaaya gaya tha. 2012 main gujarat vidhansabha ke chunaav ke baad bhartiya janati party kii sarkaar ne tatkalin mukhyamantri narendra modi dwaara aravalli ko nayaa jila banane kii kaarywahi kii gai thi.aravalli jile main 6 tehsil he. jile kaa kshetrafal 3308 he. 2011 kii janaganana anusaar aravalli kii jansankhya 90,8,797 he. 1000 purush ke saamne stri kii sankhya 946 he.
