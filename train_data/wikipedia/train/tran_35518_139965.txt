 yah bhartiya antariksh anusandhan centre ke dwaara 23 sitambar 2009 ko choda gaya doorasanvedi upgrah he. yah oshansat-1 kii jagah legaa. oshansat-2 samudr kaa mizaaj jaanane or mausam kii bhavishyavaanee karne main sahaayak he. isaki sabase badi khaasiyat yah he ki isamen lage upkarnon kii sahaayta se samud ke un ilaakon kii nishandehi kii ja sakati he, jahaan machhliyaan zyada maatra main milti hai. is drushti se yah machuaaron tathaa machhlee udyog ke liye bahut upyogi he.
