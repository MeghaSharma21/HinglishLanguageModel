 phulvaa khamkar ek bhartiya koreographer or nartak he, jo bollywood or marathi filmon main kaam karti he. vah bharat ke pehle nrity reality sho bugi vugi kii vijeta rahi or dance india dance super moms main 5 antim pratiyogi main se ek thi. unhone hindi or marathi filmon jaise happy new year, juli 2, natarang, khuli mulgi detaa kaa mulgi, or mitawa kii koreography kii he. unhone zee marathi ke ek nrity reality sho uka peksh ek bhi jeeta or dusre or tisare sijan kii vah nyaayadhish rahi. natrang se apsara aali ke geet ke liye unhen sarvshereshth koreography ke liye zee gaurav puraskaar mila 2010 main mila.
 phulvaa naam unke pita dwaara namit kiya gaya tha, jo ek marathi sahitya lekhak the. unhone balamohan vidyamandir dadar se apni shiksha praat kii. unhone vaanijya or arthshaastra ke ramanranjan aanandilal podra college se vaanijya main snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii. dadar main samarth vyam mandir se unhen jimnastic siikhaa tha. unhone kathak or samakaleen nrity main shiksha praat kii he. 
 natrang, popat, jhaapatalela, mitawa, aika dajiba, idiachi kalpana, santo aakaa, priyatama, sahpaathee jaise marathi filmein unhen nrity nrityvid ke rup main kam kiya. unhone bollywood film taal ke liye aishwarya raay ko prashikshit kiya tha. unhone film happy new year ke geet "manavaa laage" ke liye farah khan ke saath sahay ke rup main kaam kiya tha. unhone zee tivi ke reality sho dance india dance super moms pratiyogita main bhi bhaag liya, jahaan vah 5 antim pratiyogi main se ek thi. phulvaa soni tivi ke nrity sho bugi vugi, sijan 1 kii vijeta rahi. unhone amrit khanvilakar, atul kulkarni or sonaali kulkarni ko apni kala ko puraa karne ke liye nirdeshit kiya. bollywood or marathi filmon ke alaava, unhone kuch dakshin bhartiya filmon or punjabi filmon kii koreography bhi kii he. vah apana khud kaa nrity vidhyalay chalaa rahi he, jise naam "phulvaa skool of dance" he mumbai main.
