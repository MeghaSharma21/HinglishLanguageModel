 west flanders ] ; pashchimi flemish: West Vloandern; french: Flandre-Occidentale; german: Westflandern) belgium ke flemish kshetra kaa sabase pashchimi praant he. 1 janvari 2016 ke anusaar isaki jansankhya 11,81,828 he tathaa jansankhya ghanatv 380 varg kimi0 he. yah 3,125 varg kimi0 kshetra main failaa hua he. is kshetra kii nokke-heist nagarpalika ke ek gaanv ramscapel main kamil bulke kaa janm hua tha.
