 
 
 uttar pradesh rajya rajmarg 5 bharat ke uttar pradesh rajya kaa ek rajya rajmarg he. 351.34 kilometre lambaa yah rajmarg gram pipari ke sameep nepal-bharat seemaa se shuru hokar dudhi tak jaataa he. ise lumbini-dudhi maarg bhi kaha jaataa he. yah siddharthanagar, basti, ambedkar nagar, jonpur, sant ravidaas nagar, mirzapur or sonbhadra jilon se hokar gujarata he.
