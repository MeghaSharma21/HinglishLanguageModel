 krittivas ramayana main ahiravana vishrava rishi ke putr or ravan ke bhai the. wo rakshas the or gupt rup se ram or unke bhai lakshmana ko narg-lok main le gayi or vahaan par apni araghya mahamaya ke liye dono bhaaiyon kii bali dene ko taiyaar ho gayi. lekin hanuman ne ahiravana or unki sena ko markar inaki raksha kii.
