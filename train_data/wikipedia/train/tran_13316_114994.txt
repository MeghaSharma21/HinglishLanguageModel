 1991 europea cap final yugoslavia ke red star beograd ek penalty shoot out main france ke olympics marsile haraa diya jo 29 may 1991, par baari, italy, main stadio san nicola main yojit ek footbal match tha. red star apni pehali europea cap jeetne ke liye unke tantrika yojit rup main francisi paksh ke liye minial amoros kii yaad aati he, mahatvapurn sabit hui.
