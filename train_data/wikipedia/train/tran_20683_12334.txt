 bistoon pashchim iran main ek sthaan he jo apane aitihaasik mahatv ke liye vikhyath he. yah karamanshah se qareeb 30 kimi dur hamaadaan jane vaali sadak par upasthit he. yahan par daara pratham ke shilaalekh ke alaava harcules kii vishram kii avasthaa main prastarchavi, parthiyanon kii agni puja, ek saasaani pul, mongol akraman ke bhagnavshesh, ek satrahavin sadi main nirmit sarai or unnasavin sadi main banaai gai gheraabandi dekhi ja sakati he. yah sthaan irani itihaas ke sangrahalay kii tarah he.
