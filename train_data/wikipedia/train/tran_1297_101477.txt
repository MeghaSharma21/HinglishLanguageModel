 kulluk bhatta manusmriti ke suvikhyath tikaakar. inkaa janm bengal ke nandan gram main ek varendra brahman ke ghar hua tha. unke pita kaa naam divakar bhatta tha. manusmriti par inhone jo tikaa kii he uskaa naam manworth muktawali he. manusmriti unhone sankshep main kintu atyant subodh bhashaa main sandarbh sahit smriti kii vyakhya kii he. isamen unhone apane kisi vaiyaktik mat kaa pratipaadan nahin kiya he varan medhatithi or govindraj ke mat ke dwaara hi vivechna kii he. is tikaa ke atirikt smritisagar, shraadhsagar, vivadsagar or ashauchasagar, unke anya granth hai.
