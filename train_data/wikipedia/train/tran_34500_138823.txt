 duqm madhya-purv oman main sthit ek bandargah-nagar he. yah us desh ke al vusta praant main arab sagar ke kinare sthit he. duqm main tezi se vikaas ho rahaa he or isaki jansankhya san 2008 main 5,100 se badhkar 2010 main 11,200 ho gai. oman paryaton vikaas nigam ise ek paryatak sthal ke rup main viksit karnaa chaah rahaa he or uskaa dhyey shahar kii aabaadi ko 2020 tak badha kar 1,00,000 karnaa he.
 san 2018 main bharat or oman main ek samjhauta hua ki bharat duqm ke vikaas main nivesh va yogadan karegaa. iske antargat bharat ko is bandargah par apni nausena ke jahaaz laane kii suvidha bhi di gai.
