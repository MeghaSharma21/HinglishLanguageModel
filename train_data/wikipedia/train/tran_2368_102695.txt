 nepali jati vyavastha nepal main saamaajik starikaran kii paramparaagat vyavastha he. yah mukhyatah hindu dharm kii varnashram vyavastha se nikali he jisamen chaar mukhya saamaajik varg brahman, kshatreey, vaisya or shudra aate hai. jati vyavastha saamaajik samoohon ko stareekrut vyavastha main dikhaate hai jinhen jaat kehete hai. yah rivaaj paramparaagat rup se keval khas, madhesi, tathaa nevars logon main paayi jaati he. haalanki 18 veen sadi main nepal ke ekikaran ke saath nepal kii vibhinn bhartiya mul kii adivasee jatiyon or janajatiyon ko bahut had tak jati vyavastha main shaamil kiya jaataa rahaa he. akhil hindu saamaajik sanrachana main balaat shaamil karne ke baavjood aadim asmitamoolak samooh jati vyavastha main shaamil nahin hai. 
 khas samudaay kii jati vyavastha main teen samooh hai. inaki maatrubhaasha nepali he. sibies ne 2001 main khas samudaay main keval 9 samooh darj kiya.
