 rehana jabbaari iran main kufia vibhaag ke ek adhikari murataza abdul ali sarbandi kii hatyari mahila thim. wo varsh 2007 se oktober 2014 main faansee kii sajaa diye jane tak apane kathith hamalavar kii hatya ke aarope main karawas main thim. jabbaari ke anusaar unhone apni aatmaraksha ke liye hatya kii thi lekin yah adaalat main sabit nahin ho sakaa.
