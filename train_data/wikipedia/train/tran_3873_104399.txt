 digital forencik ek nyaayalayik vigyaan kii hi shakha he. jis me digital yantra se jaankaree nikaali jaati he jese kii mobile phone se yaa kisi computer se jo aparaadh se judaa hota he. digital forensik paryaay he computer forensik kaa. sabase pahela digital aparaadh san 1978 florida me hua tha. computer se jarori jaankaree mitaane ke aparaadh me. ese aparaadh badhate rahe jime aate pratilipyaadhikaar, sibar aparaadh or kamoddeepak chitra .digital forencik jaanch kaa mukhya focus ek aapraadhik gatividhi kaa uddeshy saboot thik karne ke liye he. jese kii -
 ek jaanch ke takneekee pahalu kai up shakhaon main baanta gaya he. jese kii computer forensik, network forencik, forencik data vishleshan or mobile yantra forencik. forencik prakriya me jabti, forencik imaging or digital media kaa vishleshan or ekatra sakshya main ek report kaa utpaadan shaamil hai.
 computer forensik
 computer forensik me jo computer me jaankaree jamaa he uskaa vishleshan karnaa yaa computer se judaa koi bhi hi aparaadh hua ho. computer kii chori hona yaa usame jaankaree kii chori hona computer forensik mai aata he.
 network forensik
 network forensik yaa sibar forensik jo aparaadh network yaa internet se kiya ho. itnaa hi nahi network se oro par nigaraanee bhi rakhi ja sakati he.
 forensik data vishleshan
 yah khojane ke liye or dhokhadhadi vittiya aparaadh se utpann gatividhiyon ke pattern kaa vishleshan karne ke uddeshy se sanrachit data kii parakh hoti he.
 mobile yantra forensik
 kisi bhi aparaadhik sthaan par paaya mobile se jaankaree praapt ho sakati he kii vah kiskaa mobile he or usmi kya jaankaree jamaa he. mobile se yah bhi pataa lagayaa ja saktaa he kii mobile se kiye hue sampark kaha kiye the or kab kis samay kiye the.
