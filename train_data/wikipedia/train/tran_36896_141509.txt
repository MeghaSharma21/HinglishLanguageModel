 thumari ek katha-sangrah he jiske rachaayita fanishwar nath renu hai. thumari naamak is katha sangrah main vividh prakaar kii kahaniyaan hai or thumari sheershak se koi kahani na hote hue bhi isiilie lekhak ne iskaa naam thumari namak gayan vidha ke naam par rakhaa he jisamen mishrit bhaavon or raagon kaa nirupan hota he, kyonki yah sangrah vividh prakaar or bhav vaali kahaniyon kaa sangrah he. 
 is katha sangrah kii sarvaadhik prasidd kahani teesri kasam he jis par shailendra ne ek film kaa nirmaan kiya ji isi teesri kasam naam se he or jisamen raaj kapur tathaa wahidaa rahamaan mukhya bhoomikaaon main hai.
 is sangrah kii ek anya kahani panchalite kaafii lokapriy rahi or yah kai jagah hindi kahani ke paathyakram kaa hissaa bhi he.
