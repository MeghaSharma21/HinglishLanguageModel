 
 2016 isisi vishwa cricket lig divijan paanch ek antarrashtriya cricket toornament ki may 2016 ke dauraan jersey main jagah le lee thi. yah isisi vishwa cricket lig ke 2012-18 chakra kaa hissaa banaaya he. jersey cricket board, nizeria cricket sangh ke saath hi anya bolidaata ko oktober 2015 kii mejabani ke adhikaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
 pratiyogita ke final main jersey 44 ran se haraane ke saath oman saint muktidaata main greenville cricket ground par khelaa gaya tha. donon timen 2016 divijan chaar toornament ke liye protsaahit kiya gaya.
 chah tiimon ke toornament ke liye kwalifai:
 march 2016 main surinam main apane kuch khilaadiyon kii paatrata ke bare main isisi kii jaanch padtaal ke kaaran toornament se hat gai. kam se kam chah khilaadiyon shrinkhalaa ke divijan chah toornament aadmi, govin singh or agrani wicket lene vaale gendbaaj, muneshwar patandin sahit unki paatrata ke sambandh main uthaae gaye prashnon, tha. vanuatu unki sthanapann ke rup main namit kiya gaya.
 
 
 
 22 may ko oman or nigeria ke bich sthirataa ke liye, sue redourn, khadey ampiron main se ek tha, jabaki jaclyn williams tisare ampire tha. yah pehali baar do mahila ampire isisi toornament main purushon ke match main ampiring kii gai he.
 vanuatu khelaa denmark or landan main merilabone cricket club ke khilaaf abhyaas match. jersey par jari karne ke baad, team bhi ek antim abhyaas match oman ke khilaaf toornament uchit shuru hone se pehle khelaa tha. guernase sasex, england main varrup maichon kii ek shrinkhalaa khelaa jaataa he, netherlands e ke khilaaf do ek paksh bhi shaamil he.
