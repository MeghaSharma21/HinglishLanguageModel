 muscat anterrashtriya vimaanashetra, jise siyab anterrashtriya vimaanashetra bhi kaha jaataa he, oman kaa sabase bada vimaanashetra he va muscat shahar se 32 ki.me duuri par sthit he. yah oman ke rashtriya vayuvahak seva oman air kaa pramukh hub bhi he. vartmaan main yahaan maatr ek hi terminal he, evam doosraa terminal 2014 tak purna ho jane kii aashaa he. naye terminal kii yatri kshamta 1.2 karod yatri prativarsh hogi.
 yah vimaanashetra 1973 main siyab anterrashtriya vimaanashetra ke naam se bayat el falaaj main sthit ek choti si airfield ke sthaan par bana tha.
