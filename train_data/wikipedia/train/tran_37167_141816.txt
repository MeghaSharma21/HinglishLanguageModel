 manushyon kii anuvanshiki main pitruvansh samooh di yaa waahe-diene hapalogrup D pitruvansh samooh he. is pitruvansh samooh ke sadsy purush zyaadaatar tibet, japan or andaman dwipon main paae jate hai. anumaan he ke jis purush se yah pitruvansh shuru hua vah aaj se 50,000 yaa 60,000 varsh pehle asia ke mahadweep main kahin rahata tha, lekin is se aage uske nivas sthaan kaa thik se anumaan nahin lag paaya he. yah pitruvansh samooh bahut pracheen mana jaataa he or andaaza he isaki utpatti manushyon ke africa chhodane ke jald baad hi ho gayi thi. asia ke baahar yah pitruvansh samooh kisi purush main kahin nahin paaya gaya he. yah purush swayan pitruvansh samooh dei kaa vanshaj tha or pitruvansh samooh di ko pitruvansh samooh dei kii ek shakha mana jaataa he.
 angrezi main "vansh samooh" ko "hapalogrup", "pitruvansh samooh" ko "waahe chromosome hapalogrup" or "matruvansh samooh" ko "amtidiene hapalogrup" kehete hai.
