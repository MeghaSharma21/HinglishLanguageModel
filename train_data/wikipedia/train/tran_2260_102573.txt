  ke rajya or sangh kshetra
 bharat ke rajyon kii yah suchi us pratishatanusaar jisamen 12-23 maheenon ke bacchon ko sabhi sujhavit tike die gaye. yah jaankaree enegechaes-3 se sankalit kii gai thi. rashtriya parivaar swasthya surveykshan vyapak-paimaane, bahu-dauriya surveykshan he jo anterrashtriya jansankhya vigyaan santhaan, mumbai dwaara karaayaa jaataa he jo parivaar kalyaan or swasthya mantralay, bharat sarkaar dwaara nirdisht he. enegechaes-3 11 oktober 2007 ko jari kiya gaya tha or puraa surveykshan is website par dekha ja saktaa he.
