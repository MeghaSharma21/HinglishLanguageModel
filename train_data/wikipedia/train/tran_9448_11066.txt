 yah judwa mandir apane khubasurat kalakaari ke liye prasiddh he. khubasurat gadhi hui murtiyan tathaa pathar par kii gai nakkashi, is mandir ko jaisalmer kii bahumoolya dharohar banaati he. nichalaamandir kunthunath ko samarpit he, jo ashtpad aadhaar par bana he. shantinath kii pratimaa mandir ke upree bhaag main sthit he. in mandiron kaa nirmaan jaisalmer kii chopra tathaa shankhwal pariwaron dwaara karaya gaya. mandir main lage shilaalekh ke anusaar in donon mandiron kaa nirmaan 1480 i.main hua tha. 1516 i. main mandiron ke kuch hisse main badlaav va anya nirmaan kaary kiye gaye.
 yah mandir shikhar se yukt he, is shikhar ke bhitari gumbadon main vaadya yantron ko bajaati hui va nrity karti hui apsaraon ko utkeern kiya gaya he. inke neeche gandharvo kii pratimaaen. mandir ke sabha mandap ke chaaron ore stambhon ke madhya sundar torana bane hai. gudh mandap main ek safed aakaar kii duusari kaale sangemarmar kii karyotsarg mudra main murtiyan pratishthit bani he, iske donon parshwo main 11-11 anya tirthakaron kii pratimaaen bani hai. is kaaran choubisi kii sangya di gai he. hinduon kii do pratimaaen dashavathar or lakshminarayan bhi mandir main sthapit hai.
 mandir ke anya bhaag main shaayad hi aisa koi pathar mili jisapar shilpkaar ne kuch-na-kuch na ukera ho. hathi, ghona, singh, bandar, phool, pattiyon se puraa mandir aachhadit he. naaliyaan bhi magaramchh ke mukh ke aakaar kii banaai gai hai. jaisalmer panchatirthi itihaas ke lekhak ne yahan utkeern kii gai kaamani sreeyon ke ang pratyang ke sajiv saundarya kaa varnan nimna prakaar kiya he. chaandi si gol gukhaakriti, ban vishaal bhujaayen, chaudaa lalaat, nagin si gunthi baalo kii laten, tirchhe nayan, tote kii chonch si naak, patale va sundar hont, bhare hue stan, patali sapaat pindli va aabhushan se sajaa puraa sharir aadi kii sukshmata or bhav-bhangima yukt pratimaaen vastu prabhavotpadak he. kala kii drushti se inako khajuraho, konark va dilwana main praapt pratimaaon ke samakaksh rakhaa ja saktaa he.
