 bhartiya rajya bihar main saharsa-samastipur railkhand ke bich dhamaaraa ghat stesan par 19 agast 2013 somvaar subah 8.30 baje rajyarani superfast train kii chapet main aakar 37 yatriyon kii maut ho gai, tathaa sau se adhik ghayal ho gaye. gussai bheed ne do trainon main aag laga di. khagadiya ke katyayani main sawan ke har somvaar par lagane vaale meley main ja rahe shraddhalu badi sankhya main dhamaaraa ghat aate hai. is stesan par teen line hai. subah do linon par samastipur-saharsa sawari gaadi va saharsa-samastipur paisanger train khadi thim. in sawari gadiyon se yatri utar kar bich kii rail patari ko paar kar rahe the ki tabhi tej gati se aa rahi rajyarani superfast train yatriyon se bhare railway trek ko chirati chali gai. bihar sarkaar ne mritak ke parijanon ko do-do lakh rupee muaavja dene kii ghoshnaa kii he.
