 go-air bharat kii kam keemat vaali vimaan seva he. may 2013 ke share gananaa ke anusaar yah bharat kii paanchavi sabase badi vimaanan seva he. is seva kaa prarambh navambar 2005 se shuru hua. yah 21 shaharon main din bhar kii 100 tathaa saptaah kii 750 udaanon dwaara gharelu vimaanan seva pradaan karata he. is par wadiya samooh kaa swaamitv he.
 go air india kii sthapana san 2005 main hui thi. isaki sthapana kaa shrey bharat ke agr ganya vyapaaree nasali wadiya ke bete jahangir wadiya ko jaataa he. is air lines par wadiya parivaar kaa puraa swatv he tathaa jahangir wadiya is ke managing diarector bhi hai. go air kaa pehla oparation navambar 2005 main airbas e 320 aircraft ke saath prarambh hua.april 2012 main airline apane market shares ke chalate hue chhathwein evam antim sthaan se bad kar paanchaven sthaan par pahunch gayi.
 bharat main go air kul 21 sthalon par dainik 100 tathaa saptaah kii lagbhag 750 vimaan udaanon ke dwaara seva pradaan karata he. bharat sarkaar ke kendriya vimaanan mantralay ke adeshon ke anusaar go air ko antarrashtriya udaanon kii sweekruti nahin mili he. bharat ke 16 rajyon evam centre shasit pradeshon main yah 21 sthanon par apni sevaayen pradaan kar rahaa he, jinke naam nimnavat hai –
 chaalan kaa kharch kam karne ke liye bede main ek niti apanaayi gayi tathaa cabel airbas e 320 – 200 air craft ko sammilit kiya gaya jisamen kii unki adhiktam yatri kshamta 180 thi. oktober 2013 ke aankadon kii maane to go air aise 15 vimaanon kaa parichaalan karata he tathaa bede kii ausat vimaan aayu 3.1 saal he.
