 indira gandhi rajbhasha puraskaar rajbhasha niti ke karyanvayan or hindi ke prayog ko aage badhaane main uplabdhiyon ke liye die jate hai. varsh 1986-87 main yah puraskaar yojana aarambh kii gai thi. iske antargat prayek varsh vibhinn mantralayon, vibhaagon, bainkon, vittiya sansthaanon, saarvajanik kshetra ke pratishthanon va shahar rajbhasha karyanvayan samitiyon ko rajbhasha niti ke anupaalan main uplabdhiyon ke liye puraskaar die jate hai. saath hi kend sarkaar, bank, vittiya sansthaanon, vishvavidhyalayon, prashikshan sansthaanon or kendriya sarkaar ke swytt nikaayon ko hindi main mul pustaken likhane vaale karyrat yaa sevanivrutt karmchaariyon ko nakad puraskaar die jate hai.
 aadhunik vigyaan, praudyogiki va samakaleen vishyon ke gyaan-vigyaan par mul pustak lekhan ke liye ‘rajeev gandhi rashtriya puraskaar yojana‘ bhi chalaai jaati he. is yojana main koi bhi bhartiya naagarik bhaag le saktaa he. kshetreey str par ‘kshetreey rajbhasha puraskaar‘ chalaae jate hai. ye puraskaar rajbhasha niti ke karyanvayan or hindi ke prayog ko aage badhaane main uplabdhiyon ke liye die jate hai. iske liye kshetreey/adhinsth karyaalayon, saarvajanik kshetra ke upakramon, shahar rajbhasha karyanvayan samitiyon, bank va centre sarkaar ke vittiya sansthaanon ko puraskaar die jate hai.
