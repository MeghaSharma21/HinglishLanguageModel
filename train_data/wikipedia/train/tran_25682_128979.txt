 shahbuddin sahab bharat ke ek aapraadhik raajnneetijna netaa hai. rashtriya janata dal ke pramukh netaaon main se ek hai, evam lalu prasad yadav ke kareebi maane jate hai. 30 agast 2017 ko, patna ucch nyaayalay ne sivaan hatya ke maamle main mohammad shahabuddin kii maut kii sajaa ko barkaraar rakhaa.
 shahabuddin kaa janm magadh main pratappur main hua. 1980 ke dashak main mahavidhyalay se hi inhone raajnneeti main pravesh kar liya. 1986 ke baad se in par anek aapraadhik maamle daayar hue. yah siwan kii seat se chaar baar sansad sadsy evam do baar vidhayak chune gaye. 2004 main inhone jail se chunaav ladaa or jeeta. police dwaara inke viruddh gavaah nahin pesh kar paane ke kaaran inhen bizzat bari kar diya gaya.
 2005 main shahabuddin ko dilli main giraftaar kar liya gaya. 2009 main inhen chunaav ladane se vanchit kar diya gaya. 11 sitambar 2016 ke din inhen jail se baahar nikaala gaya. 30 sitambar 2016 ko suparim court ne jamaanat radd kar di.
 mohammad shahabuddin ne hina sahab se shaadi kii he, jisamen do baar loksabhaa chunaav main bhaajapa ke omprakash yadav se haar gaye hai. hina ne sivaan ke college se grajuation kiya he. college main hina akeli aisi ladki thi jo burka pahan padhgaye aati thi. grajuation kii padhai ke baad hi hina kii shaadi shahabuddin se ho gai thi. hina sahab ko ek betaa osama or do beti hai- hira shahab or tasaneem shahaab. badi beti hera shahab hyderabad main dawa ke ek chaatr he. unka betaa osama england main elelbi kar rahaa he, jabaki sabase kam umr kii beti tasanem ne apni padhai puri kar lee he. unke bete, osama sahab gavaah hatya ke maamle main aaropee hai.
