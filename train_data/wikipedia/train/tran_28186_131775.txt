 roman lipi likhaawat kaa wo tarika he jisamen angrezi sahit pashchimi or madhya europe kii sari bhashaayen likhi jaati hai, jaise german, francisi, spanish, portugali, italvi, dach, norwegian, swedish, romanian, ityadi. ye baayen se daayen likhi or padhi jaati he.
 angrezi ke alaava lagbhag sari europea bhashaayen roman lipi ke kuch aksharon par atirikt chinh bhi prayukt karte hai.
 roman lipi ke kuch akshar
 asal main roman lipi latini bhashaa ke liye hi bani thi, yani ki latini kii apni lipi he. isaliye iskaa harek akshar lagbhag hameshaa ek hi uccharan detaa he . ati-pracheen roman lipi ye thi -
 roman lipi main likhi hui angrezi or uske uccharan main bahut zyaada gadabad ghotaalaa he. isaki vajah he :
 anya europea bhashaaon main likhaawat or uccharan main utana bhed nahin he or agar he bhi to uske achche kaase niyam kanun hote hai.
