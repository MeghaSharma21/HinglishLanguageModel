 
 antarctic kril antarctic mahadweep ke ird-gird dakshini mahaasaagar main pai jane vaali kril kii jati he. crusteshia jeevvaigyanik upsang ke yah nanhen praani samudr main ghaney jhund banaakar rahete hai - aksar in jhundon main ek ghan meter main 10,000 - 30,000 kril hote hai. antarctic kril 6 saal tak jivit rah saktaa he or yah 6 semi lambaai or 2 gram vazan tak pahunch saktaa he. kril sookshmjivi plavak khate hai or phir kai bade aakaar ke praani krilon ko khate hai. yadi jaiwabhaar kii drushti se dekha jaye to antarctic kril hamaare grah kii sabase vistrut jatiyon main se ek he. kul milakar sabhi antarctic kril kaa anumaanit jaiwabhaar 50 karod ton he.
