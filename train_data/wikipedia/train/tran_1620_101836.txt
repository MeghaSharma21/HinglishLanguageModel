 johaar ghati, jise milam ghati yaa goriganga ghati bhi kehete hai, bharat ke uttrakhand rajya main sthit ek prasiddh ghati he. gori nadi isi ghati se hokar bahati he. ek samay main is ghati se tibet ke liye mahatvapurn vyapaarik raste nikalate the. milam or martholi ghati main sthit pramukh gram hai. 
 johaar ghati ke 12 gaanv milam himand se nikalne vaali gori nadi ke kinare based hai. munasyari se 6 hajaar se 10 hajaar feet tak oonchai par based in gaanvon tak pahunchane ke liye 65 kimi kaa paidal safar tay karnaa padataa he.
