 digboi tel shodhanagar ko assam oil company limited dwaara 1901 main digboi main sthapit kiya gaya tha. yah asia kii sabase puraani or sabase lambe samay se parichaalit tel rifinary he. 1981 se indian oil korporation limited ne assam tel company limited kii rifinary or vipanan prabandhan apane haath main liya or ek alag vibhaag banaaya. yah vibhaag tel shodhan or vipanan donon abhiyaanon ke liye jimmedaar he. digboi tel shodhanagar main rifinary sthapit kshamta 0.50 mmatipie tha. rifinary kii shodhan kshamta main july, 1996 main rifinary ke aadhunikeekaran se 0.65 mmatipie kii vruddhi hui thi. 1999 main 1,70,000 tipie kshamta kaa ek nayaa koking unit chaalu kiya gaya. microchristelline mom kaa utpaadan badhaane ke liye ek nae salvent divaxing unit 2003 main sthapit or chaalu kiya gaya. rifinary bhi deazal kii gunvattta main sudhaar karne ke liye 2002 main hydrotreater-yuopi sthapit kiya he.
