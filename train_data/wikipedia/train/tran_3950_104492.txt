 hindi shiksha sangh dakshin africa dakshin africa kii ek hindisevi sanstha he. isaki sthapana 25 april san 1948 main vahaan kii arya pratinidhi sabha tathaa sanathan dharm sabha ke sammilit sabha main kii gayi thi. pandit nardev vidyalankar iske pratham adhyaksh the.
 17 oktober 1948 ko is nayi sanstha dwaara hindi seekhney ke liye ruchi paidaa karne ke liye hindi sahitya sammelan kii sthapana kii gai. is kaarykram main 35 paathshaalaon ne bhaag liya. jab unke dhyaan main yah aayaa ki bahut se hindi adhyaaapak paryaapt rup se prashikshit nahin he to unhone adhyapakon ke liye hindi prashikshan kii kakshaen shuru kii or pandit ji ne iske sangyojan main pramukh bhoomika adaa kii. ek gaiyr-sarkari sanstha ke rup main sangh ne ise chunauti ke rup main liya or hindi adhdhyan or adhyaapan kaa kaam sangathit rup se prarambh kiya.
