 bharat ke pradhanmantri kaa karyaalay se aashay bharat ke pradhanmantri ke sidhe neeche aane vaale najdiki adhikariyon or karmchaariyon ke samooh se he. pramukh sachiv iske sarvocch adhikari hai. samprati nripendra mishra pradhan sachiv hai. 1977 tak 'pradhanmantri karyaalay' ko 'pradhanmantri sachivalay' kaha jaataa tha jise morarji desai ke pradhanmantritvakaal main badalakar 'pradhanmantri karyaalay' kar diya gaya.
 pradhanmantri karyaalay, bharat sarkaar kaa ek bhaag he. yah sachivalay ke dakshini block main sthit he.
 piemo pradhanmantri ko sachivalayan sahaayta pradaan karata he. piemo bhrashtachar virdohi ikai or saarvajanik shikayaton se nipatane pankh shaamil hai.
 karyaalay pradhanmantri or mantri kuch chuninda adhikariyon gharon bharat bhartiya civil seva, jo uske saath kaam karne ke liye prabandhan or sarkaar or uske karyaalay kaa samanvay. unke karyaalay ke madhyam se pradhanmantri, swatantra prabhaar or rajyapalon or rajya sarkaar ke mantriyon ke mantri kendriya union cabinet ke sabhi mantriyon ke saath nirdeshaank.
 south block main pradhanmantri karyaalay, raashtrapati bhawan kii bhavyata andekhi yah he ek taraf cabinet sachivalay or duusare par bahari maamalon or raksha mantralayon ke bich sandwiched. 20 kamare piemo donon avasanrachanaatmak or raashtra kii mukhya kaaryakari janashakti samarthan pradaan karne ke liye susajjit he. hai takanic saamaan or atyaadhunik sanchaar upkarnon ke liye gharelu or antarrashtriya ghatnaon par najar rakhane ke liye sthapit kiya gaya.
