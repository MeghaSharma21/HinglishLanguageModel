 april yaa may 2019 main sattaraveen lok sabha kaa gathan karne ke liye aam chunaav bharat main hone vaale hai. haalanki, bijepi 2018 ke ant tak shuruaati chunaavon ke liye jane par vichaar kar rahi he
 loksabhaa ke 543 nirvaachit sadasyon ko ekal-sadasyeey nirvachan kshetron se pehle-purv-post-post matdaan dwaara chunaa jayega. bharat ke raashtrapati ek atirikt do sadasyon ko namankit karte hai.
 neeche kuch sansthaaon dwaara agle aam chunaavon se sambandhit mat-surveykshan prakashit kiye gayi hai.
