 shahar badi or sthaayee maanav basti hoti he. shahar main aam taur par awaas, parivahan, swatchta, bhoomi upyog or sanchaar ke liye vyapak system hota hai. aitihaasik rup se shaharvaasiyon kaa samgr rup se maanavtaa main chota sa anupaat rahaa he. lekin aaj do shatabdiyon se abhootpurv or teji se shaharikaran ke kaaran, kaha jaataa he ki aaj aadhi aabaadi shaharon main rah rahi hai. vartmaan main shahar aamtaur par bade mahanagareey kshetra or shahari kshetra ke centre hote hai. sabase aabaadi vaala uchit shahar shanghai he.
 ek shahar anya maanav bastiyon se apane apekshaakrit bade aakaar ke kaaran bhinn hota he. shahar akele aakaar se hi alag nahin he, balki yah ek bade raajnneetik sandarbh main bhoomika nibhaataa he. shahar apane aasapaas ke kshetron ke liye prashasnic, vanijyik, dharmik or saanskrithik kendron ke rup main seva detaa he. ek vishisht shahar main peshewar prashaasak, niyam-kaayade hote hai or sarkaar ke karmchaariyon ko khilaane ke liye karadhaan bhi. shahar shabd farsi se hindi bhashaa main aayaa he. purana sanskrit shabd nagar bhi upyog kiya jaataa visheshkar sarkari kaary main jaise ki nagar nigam.
