 chitra datta ek bhartiya bhautik vigyaani he, jo jaev soochna vigyaan or computesnal jeev vigyaan main kaam kiya he. vah pramukh vaigyanik hai strectural jeev vigyaan or jaev soochna vigyaan bhartiya rasayan vigyaan santhaan, kolkata me. 
 datta ne snaatak vigyaan kiya or snatakottar bhautik vigyaan me pashchim bengal main vishwabharati vishvavidhyalay se. unhone 1984 main kalakatta vishvavidhyalay se bhautikee vigyaan main piechdi praapt kii, jiske baad ve ek vaigyanik ke rup main bhartiya rasayan vigyaan santhaan main kaam shuru kar diya. 
 chitra datta ko 1992 main neshnal academy of sciensej ke phelo ke rup main chunaa gaya tha.
