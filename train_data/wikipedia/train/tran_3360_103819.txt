 bosnia or harzegovina ne athleates ko 1992 main pehali baar apane hi jhande ke tahat samar olympic khelon main bheja. bosnian athleton ne yugoslav dhvaj ke tahat pratispardhaa kii, jab tak ki us desh kaa vilag nahin tha.
 bosnia or harjegovina kii olympic samiti 1992 main banaai gai thi or 1993 main manyata praapt thi.
