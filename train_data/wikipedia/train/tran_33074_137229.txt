 sindhushri khullar niti ayog kii mukhya kaaryakari adhyaksh hai. isse pehle bharat ke yojana ayog main sachiv ke pad par rah chuki khullar kii niyukti pradhanmantri narendra modi dwaara 9 janvari 2015 ko nav-nirmit rashtriya bharat parivartan sanstha ke pehle mukhya kaaryakari adhikari ke rup main kii gayi. khullar 1975 batch kii iees adhikari hai or yojana ayog main inaki niyukti varsh 2012 main hui thi. isse pehle narendra modi ne 15 agast 2014 ko apane swatantraaa diwas ke bhaashan main hi yojana ayog kii jagah niti ayog banane kii ghoshnaa kii thi.
