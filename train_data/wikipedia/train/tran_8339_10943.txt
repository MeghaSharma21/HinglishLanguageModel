 bhuvigyaan main antarhimyugiya kaal, jise kabhi-kabhi keval antarhimyugiya bhi kaha jaataa he, do himayugon ke bich kaa vah samay hota he jisamen prithvi kaa ausat taapmaana himayugon se adhik hota he or himayugon main vistrut himchaadaren yaa to bahut sikud jaati hai yaa samaapt hi ho jaati hai. yah antarhimyugiya kaal hazaron varshon tak chalata he. is samay prithvi ek antarhimyugiya kaal main he jo nutanatam yug kehalaata he or aaj se 11,700 varsh pehle atyantnutan yug ke ant main armbh hua tha.
