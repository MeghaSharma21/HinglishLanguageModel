 ramvilas paswan bhartiya dalit raajnneeti ke pramukh netaaon main se ek hai. ve lok janashakti party ke adhyaksh evam rashtriya janatantric gathbandhan kii sarkaar main kendriya mantri bhi hai. ve solahavin loksabhaa main bihar ke hajipur loksabhaa kshetra kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai.
 paswan bihar ke khagariyaa jile ke shahrbanni gaanv se hai. vah ek anusuchit jati parivaar ke liye paidaa hua tha. unhone 1 9 60 ke dashak main rajkumaari devi se shaadi kii. 2014 main unhone khulaasa kiya ki loksabhaa namakan patron ko chunauti dene ke baad unhone 1 9 81 main unhen talaak de diya tha. unki pehali patni ushaa or aashaa se do betiyaan hai. 1 9 83 main, unhone amritsar se ek airhostess or punjabi hindu rina sharma se vivaah kiya. unke paas ek betaa or beti he. unke bete chiraag paswan ek abhinayta se bane rajaneta hai
