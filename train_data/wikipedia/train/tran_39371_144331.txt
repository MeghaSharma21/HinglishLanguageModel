 kwai nadi kshetra main tenacerim pahadiyaan
 tenacerim pahadiyaan burma, thailand va malaysia main uttar-dakshin disha main lagbhag 1700 kimi tak chalane vaali ek parvatamala he. haalanki inaki oonchaai adhik nahin he, phir bhi yah apane madhya or uttari bhaag main burma or thailand ke bich main ek prakritik seemaa banaati hai. inkaa naam burma ke tanintharyi mandal ke naam ke ek parivartit rup par padaa he. apane dakshini bhaag main yah kra thalsandhi main se nikalati hui singapore ke paas tak pahunch jaati he.
