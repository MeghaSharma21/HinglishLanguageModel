 chanpak athwa sonchampa, maganoliesii kul kaa paudha he. michilia varg ke paudhe dakshin-purvi asia main paae jate hai.
 chanpak kaa ped aath se lekar das meter tak unchaa hota he or iskaa phool sunahale rang kaa tathaa sugandhyukt hota he. ye paudhe bagichon main sugandh ke liye lagaae jate hai. champe kaa itr isi ke phulon se banaaya jaataa he. assam pradesh main chanpak kii pattiyaan resham ke keedon ko khilaai jaati hai or un keedon se 'champa resham' nikaalte hai.
