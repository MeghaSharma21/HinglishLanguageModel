 naukuchiatal bhimtal se 4 kilometre dakshin-purab samudr kii sataha se 1292 meter kii oonchaai par sthit he. nainital se is taal kii duuri 26.2 kimi he. yah 983 meter lambi, 693 meter chaudee or 40 meter gahari he.is nau kone vaale taal kii apni vishisht mahatta he. iske tede-medhe nau kone hai. is anchal ke logon kaa vishwaas he ki yadi koi vyakti ek hi drushti se is taal ke nau konon ko dekh le to use moksh prapti ho jaati he. parantu vaastaviktaa yah he ki saat se adhik kone ek baar main nahin dekhe ja sakate.
 is taal kii ek or visheshta yah he ki isamen videshon se aaye hue naanaa prakaar ke pakshi rahete hai. taal main camel ke phool khile rahete hai. is taal main machhliyon kaa shikaar bade achche bhang se hota he. 20-25 pund tak kii machhliyaan is taal main aasaani se mil jaati he. machhlee ke shikaar karne vaale or nauka vihaar shaukeenon kii yahan bheed lagi rahati he. is taal ke pani kaa rang gaharaa nila he. yah bhi aakarshan kaa ek mukhya kaaran he. paryatakon ke liye yahan par khaane or rahane kii suvidha he. dhoop or varsha se bachane ke liye bhi paryaapt vyavastha kii gayi he.
