 sadak yatayaat niyantran main vaahanon ko disha nirdesh dena, yadi kisi sthaan par marammat kaa kaary ho rahaa he to yatayaat ko anya disha main nirdeshit karnaa yaa koi durghatana ho jaati he yaa anya koi yatayaat se judi samasya hoti he. vah sabhi sadak yatayaat niyantran main shaamil he. isamen sisitivi kaa bhi upyog kiya jaataa he, jisase yatayaat main hone vaali har gatividhi par purna dhyaan diya ja sake.
 yatayaat niyantran baahar main ek prakaar kaa kaary he jo raat, din or har prakaar ke mausam main kiya jaataa he. is dauraan jivan sankatmay sthiti main rahata he kyonki kisi bhi vaahan se koi bhi badi durghatana ghatit ho sakati he. isamen sabase badi pareshaani yah he ki kai baar yatayaat niyantrak apni jimmedaari nibhaate hue ub jaataa he. jiske kaaran vah yatayaat main dhyaan kendrit nahin kar paata he. is kaaran kai baar bahut badi durghatana hoti rahati he.
