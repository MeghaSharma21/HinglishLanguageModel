 vallathol narayan menon kerala rajya se malayalam bhashaa ke bhartiya kavi the.ve malayalam sahitya main maanav ke maansik bhav ko kaalpaniktaa kaa paridhaan dekar sudar rup main prastut karne vaale mahaan kaviyon main se ek the. unhone 1909 main balmiki ramayana kaa malayalam bhashaa main anuwaad kiya. 1910 main "badhirvilapam" naamak vilaapkavy likhaa. iske baad unhone anek naatakeey bhavakavy likhe--ganpati, bandhansthanay aniruddhan, oru kattu, shishynum makanum, magdalan mari yam, achnum maknum kochusita ityadi. san 1924 ke baad rachit sahityamanjariyon main hi vallathol ke deshbhakti se otprot ve kavyasuman khile the jinhone unako rashtrakavi ke pad par asin kiya. anre gurunathan ityadi un bhavagiton main atyadhik lokapriy hai. jivan ke komal or kaant bhaavon ke saath vicharan karnaa vallathol ko priy tha. andhkaar main khadey hokar rone kii pravriti unamem nahin thi. yah satya he ki patit pushpon ko dekhakar unhone bhi aahen bhari hai, parantu unpar ansu bahaate rahane kii tulana main viksit sumanon ko dekhakar ahlad prakat karne kii pravriti hi unamem adhik hai. vallathol narayan menon ko sahitya evam shiksha kshetra main padam bhushan se 1954 main sammanit kiya gaya.
