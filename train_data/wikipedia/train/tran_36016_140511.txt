 chitradurg, karnataka ke chitradurg jile main sthit ek durg he. 
 chitradurg ke kile kaa itihaas sambhavtah 15veen shatabdi ke ant se 18veen shatabdi ke prarambh ke bich kaa he. is kile ko kisne banwaaya, iske koi pramanik aitihaasik shrot nahin hai. itihaaskaar is par ekamat hai ki us kalkhand main jo bhi us kshetra kaa adhipati rahaa usne is kile ko banavaane main yogadan diya, jisame sabase pehle rashtrakut, chalukya or hoysaal pramukh the. iske baad kuch samay tak nayak vansh kaa shashan rahaa par inkaa kshetra sirf chitradurg tak hi tha.
 is durg ke bheetar 18 mandir hai or kuch masjiden hai jo kii haidar ali ke atikaraman ke baad sambhavatya kuch mandiro ko tod kar banaayin gayi hongi. is durg main barsaati pani ke sangrakshan kii samuchit vyavastha thi taaki is kile main pani kii kami na ho. is kile ke liye vahaan ke tatkaaleen shaskaon kaa haidar ali se or uske baad anya raajaaon se kai baar yuddh hua tha, pehla 1760 main phir 1770 main.
