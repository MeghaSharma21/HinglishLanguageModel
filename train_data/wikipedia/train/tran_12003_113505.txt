 manipur main phalgun maas ke din yaosang naamak parv manaaya jaataa he jo holi se bahut miltaa julata he. yahan dhulendi vaale din ko 'pichakaari' kaha jaataa he. yaosang se abhipraay us nanheen-si jhonpadi se he jo poornima ke aparaa kaal main prayek nagar-gram main nadi athwa sarovar ke tat par banaai jaati he. isamen chaitanya mahaprabhu kii pratimaa sthapit kii jaati he or pujan ke baad is jhonpadi ko holi ke alaav kii bhanti jala diya jaataa he. is jhonpadi main lagane vaali saamagree 8 se 13 varsh tak ke bacchon dwaara paas pados se chura kar laane kii parampara he. yaosang kii raakh ko log apane mastak par lagaate hai or ghar le ja kar taviz banavaate hai. 'pichakaari' ke din rang-gulaal-abir se vaataavaran rangeen ho uthta he. bachche ghar-ghar ja kar chaaval sabziyaan ityadi ekatra karte hai. is saamagree se ek bade samuhik bhoj kaa aayojan hota he.
