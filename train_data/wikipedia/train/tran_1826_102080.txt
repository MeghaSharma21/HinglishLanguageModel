 fauzia azeem, jo aam taur par qndil baloch ke naam se prasiddh he, ek pakistani soshal media shaksiyat thim jo internet par video banaakar apni dainik dincharya or vibhinn vivaadaaspad muddon par charchaa karti thim. kim kardasian or punam pandeya jaisi shaksiyaton ke saath bhi inaki tulana kii gai. sabaa qamar qndil baloch kii bayopic 'baagi' main dikhi . 
 inaki shaadi ashiq hossain ke saath 2008 main hui thi or unka ek betaa bhi hua. 2010 main donon alag ho gaye.
 qndil apane bold andaaz or vivaadon main rahane kii vajah se aksar media main chain rahati thi is kaaran soshal media par inke folovers kii sankhya hazaron main thi. inhone kai baar purv cricketer or vipakshi netaa imran khan ke saath shaadi karne kii ichha jataai thi. inke dwaara post muslim dharm guru mufti abdul qavi ke saath apni vivaadaaspad tasviren bhi kaafii charchaa main rahi.baloch aksar cricket se jude bayanon ke liye charchaa main rahati thim. july 2016 main inaki do shaadiyon kii baat saamne aane se inkaa parivaar naaraaz tha.
 16 july 2016 ko uske multan main sthit ghar main inke bhai wasim ne inaki galaa ghont ke hatya kar di. shuruaati report main qndil ko goli maari jane kii baat kahi ja rahi thi. zila police pramukh azhar akram anusaar inaki maut galaa ghontne se hui. saath hi yah bhi kahan ki yah sammaan kii khaatir kii gai hatya kaa maamla lagata he. ve isaki jaanch kar rahe hai. police ne bataaya ki qndil ko apane hi parivaar se modelling chhodane or soshal media se hatane kii dhamkee mili thi. uskaa bhai uske fesabook post or video ko lekar usein dhamkee de rahaa tha.don kii khabar ke anusaar, teen saptaah pehle qndil ne grih mantri, fedral investigeshan athurity ke mahanideshak or islamabad ke varisht adheekshak ko patr likhakar suraksha pradaan karne ke liye kaha tha.
