 vikendrikaran athwa vikendrikaran kaaryon, shaktiyon, logon ko yaa chizon ko kendriya sthaan yaa praadhikari se hataakar punah vibhajit karne kii prakriya ko kehtexhh hai. jabaki kendrikaran mukhyatah sarkari kshetron main kiya jane vaala or vyapkar rup se adhyayan kiya jane vaala vishay he jiske samaan vikendrikaran ko saamaanya rup se paribhaashit nahin kiya ja saktaa. vikendrikaran kaa arth bhinn kshetron main, isko lagu karne ke tareekon ke anusaar bhinn ho saktaa he. vikendrikaran kii avadharana ko nijee vyavsaayon or sanghathanon main samooh gatiki or prabandhan vigyaan, raajnneeti vigyaan, vidhi, lok prashasan, arthshaastra or praudyogiki ke kshetron main lagu kiya jaataa he.
