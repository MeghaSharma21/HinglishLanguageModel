 mandla jila, bharat ke madhya pradesh rajya main satapura pahadiyon main sthit ek jila he. narmada nadi uttar-pashchim bahati hui is jile ko reva se alag karti he. narmada kii sahaayak banjaar nadi kii ghati main jile kaa sabase adhik upjau bhaag padataa he, jise 'haveli' kehete hai. haveli ke dakshin banjaar kii ghati jangalon se dhaki hui hai. sarvapramukh imarti ped saal hai. baans, tik or harad anya ullekhneey vriksh hai. nadiyon kii ghaatiyon main dhan, gehun or tilhan kii upaj hoti he. jangal main chita ke akhet ke liye yah ek prasiddh jila hai. lakh utpaadan, lakdi chirana, paan ugaana, pashupaalan, chataayee or rassiyon kaa nirmaan yahan ke logon ke udhyam hai. yahan ke 60 pratishat nivasee gaund janajati ke hen. yahan maiganej or dhaatu ke nikshep hai.
 mandla nagar, narmada nadi ke kinare jabalpur ke 45 mile dakshin-purv main mandla jile kaa prashasnic kendra he, jo teen ore se narmada dwaara ghira hua hai. phul dhaatu ke paatro ke liye vikhyath he. yah gond vansh kii rajdhani rah chukaa he. yahan kile or prasaad ke avshesh hai. desh kaa ekmaatr jeevashm rashtriya park mandla jile main sthit he.
