 jose di anchieta  ek spani mul ke isai dharmprachaarak or mishanri the jinaki karmbhumi brazil thi. vah saao poulo or rio di genero shahar ke sansthaapak the. vah ek agrani bhashavid, lekhak, doctor, vaastukar, ejeeniyar, maanavtaavaadi or kavi bhi the. vah pehle naatakkaar, pehle vyakaranvaadi or canary dweepasmooh main paidaa hue pehle kavi or brazil ke sahitya ke pita hai. 
 saan chrisatobal de laa laguna hua tena laguna tenreef main. 14 saal kii umr main vah ek baar vahaan coyambara vishvavidhyalay gaye, unhone sosayadi of jisas main pravesh kiya or, adhyayan ke varshon ke baad, brazil main ek mishanri ke rup main soochibaddh kiya gaya.
 unhone brazil ke swadeshi logon ko prashikshit or shikshit kiya or portugali nivaasiyon ke durupyog se unka bachaav kiya. 1597 main reritiba main unki mrityu ho gai.
 unhen 3 april, 2014 ko pope francis dwaara sant ghoshit kiya gaya tha. isliye, vah harmano pedro ke baad canary dwipon ke doosraa mul sant hai or brazil ke tisare sant bhi maane jate hai.
