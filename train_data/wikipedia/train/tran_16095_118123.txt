 richi beno  australia cricket team ke ek purv cricketer the jinhone 1964 tak antarrashtriya cricket khelaa tha. baad main inhone camentator kaa kirdaar bhi bakhubi nibhaayaa tha. inkaa janm new south wales main 1930 main hua jabaki inkaa nidhan sidney ,australia main 10 april 2015 ko hua tha.
 beno ek test cricket main harfanmaulaa khilaadi the.
 beno ne apana pehla test cricket match westeindies cricket team ke khilaaf 25 janvari 1952 main khelaa tha jabaki antim test match 12 farvari 1964 dakshin africa cricket team ke khilaaf khelaa tha. inhone apane test karier main 63 maichon main 248 wicket liye the jabaki kul 2201 ran bhi banaae the.
 beno ne apane karier main 16 baar ek pari main 5 wicket liye the jabaki inkaa sarvaadhik gendbaaji vishleshan 72 ran dekar 7 wicket tha.
