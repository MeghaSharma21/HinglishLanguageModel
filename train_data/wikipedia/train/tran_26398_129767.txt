 bharatendu vimal britain me based bhartiya mul ke hindi lekhak he. upanyaas `son machhlee' ke lekhak bharatendu vimal bi.bi.si. radio se jude hai. kamaleshwaraji ne is upanyaas ko gater ganga kii sangya dete hue is upanyaas kii bhoomika main isaki bhuri-bhuri prashansa kii he. bharatendu vimal ghazal evam kavita bhi likhte hai. mumbai ke film jagat se jude vimalji ko varsh 2003 ke liye apane upanyaas `son machhlee' ke liye padmanand sahitya sammaan praapt ho chukaa he. ve kisi bhi gutbazi yaa vaad-vivaad kaa hissaa nahin banate or kisi shant kone main aaraam se apana kaam karte rahete hai. vimalji britain bhar ke bahut se kavi sammelnon kaa kushal sanchaalan bhi kar chuke hai.
