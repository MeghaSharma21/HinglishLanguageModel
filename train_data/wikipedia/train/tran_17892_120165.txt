 pitruvansh purvajon kaa byora dene kii us pranali ko kehete hai jisamen pita or phir uski purush purvajon ko dekhte hue vansh khinchaa jaae. pitruvansheey vyavastha kisi us saamaajik pranali ko kehete hai jisamen koi vyakti paariwarik rup se apane pita or uske pitruvansh kaa bhaag mana jaataa he. aam taur se pitruvansh vyavastha main kul putron ke dwaara chalata he or parivaar kaa netritv us parivaar main paidaa hua sabase adhik umr kaa purush karata he. aise samaajon main sampatti or oopaadhiyaan bhi beton ko mila karti he.
 'pitruvansheey' ko angrezi main 'patriliniyal' kehete hai.
 bhartiya upmahadweep, iran or europe main vistrut hind-europea jatiyaan parmparik rup se apane samaajon main pitruvansheey vyavastha rakhaa karti thim.
