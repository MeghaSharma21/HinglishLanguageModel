 yah ayurveda main ek prakaar kii aushadhi he jise bahut se aushadhiy paudhon evam khanijon se banaaya gaya he.
 punarnava,bhoomi aanwala, moky, chitrak, aanwala, baheda, kutki, aambeej, nishoth, bilva, ajvayan, atis kadava, ghritkumari, muktashukti bhasma, kasis bhasma, louh bhasma, mandur bhasma 
 1. is vati ke seven se pet dard, mandagni,atisar, vibandh, ajeernata, aadi udar vikaron kaa upachaar kiya jaataa he.
 2. isse yakrut ke rog, jaise- pilia thik kiya jaataa he.
 3. raktaalpata tathaa jeern fever aadi yakrut vikaron main visheshat laabh hota he.
 4. is aushadhi kaa prayog kabz or dast donon stithi main kiya jaataa he.
 5. apach evam bhukh na lagane main bhi yah laabhakaari he.
 6. iskaa prayog anya aushadhiyon ke saath dama, jalodar, mutradhiky, vishaanujanit sankraman, shvasanishodh, krimirog, vran, bavasir aadi ke liye bhi kiya jaataa he.
