 dharatleey jal yaa satahi jal vah jaal he jo prithvi kii sataha par saritaaon, nadiyon, jhiilon, talabon or ardrabhumiyon ityadi main paaya jaataa he. ise samudri jal, bhoojal or vayumandaleey jal se alag samajha ja saktaa he. yah jal chakra kaa abhinn evam mahatvapurn hissaa he. 
 jal ko sansaadhan ke rup main dekha jaae to maanav upyog main aane vaala zyaadaatar jal dharatleey jal hi he. iskaa kaaran yah he ki dharatleey jal kaa zyaadaatar hissaa meetha jal he or maanav upyog yogya he. saath hi yah aasaani se uplabdh or dohan yogya bhi he. saath hi isamen manushya kii kriyaaon dwaara kaafii pradooshan bhi hua he.
