 koraasaan yaa kuraasaan iran ke uttar purv kaa ek kshetra he. isamen razvi koraasaan, uttar koraasaan or dakshin koraasaan praant aate hai. vrihattar koraasaan main afghanistan ke sate kshetron ke alaava tajikistan, turkmenistan or uzbekistan ke nikatavarti pradesh sammilit hai.
 mulat: iran ke purv aamu nadi ke dakshin or hindookush ke uttar sthit vistrut bhu bhaag kaa naam khurasaan tha. arab bhaugilikon ke kathananusaar iske purv main sistan or bharat, pashchim main ghujz kaa registan, uttar main vakshupradesh or dakshin evam dakshin-pashchim main iran kaa registan tha. kintu ab is naam kaa prayog atyant simit arth main hota he. yah iran ke us uttar-purvi praant kaa naam he jo uttar main roosi cospian pradesh main laga he. atrak nadi chaat tak isaki seemaa banaati he. iske purv main afghanistan, pashchim main astrabad, shahrud, semnaan damdhaan or yajd ke irani praant or dakshin main keraman he. vistaar main yah uttar dakshin 500 mile or purv pashchim 300 mile he. iskaa kshetrafal 25,000 vargamil he.
 is praant kaa adhikansh dharatleey bhaag pahari, marusthaleey yaa namakeen jhil kaa nichalaa gart he. dakshin main pahari bhaag kii oonchaai 11,000 feet se lekar 13,000 feet tak he.
 is pradesh main kuon tathaa bich bich main lupt ho janevaali nadiyon dwaara sinchit bahut se nachlistaan paae jate hai. atarek or kashaf kii upjau ghaatiyon main khadyann, kapaas, tambaakoo, chukundar tathaa falon kii kheti hoti he. yah praant kaser, pista, gond, kashthafal, kambal, khaal or neelmani ke liye prasiddh he. yahan par loha sisa, namak, sona, tanba or sphatik bhi paaya jaataa he.
 meshed is praant kii rajdhani he jo sadak dwaara anya pramukh nagaron se mili hui he. mulya kii drushti se niryaat kii vastuen kramash: kaaleen, chamada tathaa khaal, opium, imarti lakdi, kapaas kii chijen, silk or nil mani hai.
 vartmaan sthitivartmaan main yah khurasaan pradesh teen pradeshon main vibhakt kar diya gaya he.
