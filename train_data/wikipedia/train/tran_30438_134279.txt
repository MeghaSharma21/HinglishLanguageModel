 pyaar kaa saaya 1991 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. film main mukhya bhoomikon main rahul roy, shiba or amrutha singh hai.
 avinaash saxena apane chachaa or chachere bhai vimal ke saath rahata he. uske chachaa guzar jate hai. kyonki vah jaanate the ki vimal sharaabi or udaoo vyakti he, isliye wo sari sampatti use chhod jate hai. phir bhi, donon chachere bhai kaaphi acchhee tarah se rahete hai. jab avinaash gaanv ke apane gharon main se kisi ek main jaataa he, to use pataa chalaa ki uske naukar ram prasad ne uske kamare ko uski anumati ke bina ek yuva mahila gloria ko de diya he. gloria se milane ke baad ve donon ek duusare ke saath pyaar karne lagate hai or ek saadhaaran mandir samaaroh main shaadi karte hai. phir ve shahar laut aae, jahaan gloria, jo ab puja he vimal se milti he. phir ek der raat ko ghar lautate hue jode par hamalaa kiya jaataa he or avinaash kii hatya ho jaati he, jisase puja vidhwaa or vidhwans ho jaati he. phir kuch maheene baad, puja ko ek bhavishyavaktaa maaya gangadhami ne sampark kiya, jo use suchit karti he ki avinaash uske saath sampark main he or use santvanaa dena chahta he or saath hi use chetavanee detaa he ki jisane use maar daalaa vah use bhi maar degaa. puja iskaa uphaas karti he or iske bare main vimal ko bataati he. puja ko yah nahin pataa ki vimal khud hi avinaash ko maarane ke liye hatyare ko bhartee karne ke liye jimmedaar tha taaki vah sampatti or dhan ko ekmaatr jivit rishtedaar ke rup main praapt kar sake.
