 christmas island hind mahaasaagar main sthit austrailia ke kshetradhikaar main aane vaala mahaj 134 varg kimi kshetra main failaa ek dweep he. is desh kii jansankhya kariban 2,072 he, jo dweep ke rihaayshi uttari chor par rahete hai. dweep kii bhugolic rup se alag hone or maanviya hastakshep kam hone kii vajah se yahaan ucch stareey ped-paudhon main vividhta milti he, jo paryaavaranvid or vaigyanikon ke liye bahut kaam kii he.
