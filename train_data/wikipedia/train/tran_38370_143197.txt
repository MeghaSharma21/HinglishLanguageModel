 parvati boul bengal se ek boul lok gayika, sangitajna evam maukhik kathavachak hai. ye bharat kii agrani boul sangitajna bhi hai. boul guru, sanathan das boul, shashanko ghosh boul kii dekharekh main, ye bharat evam anterrashtriya str par bhi boul kaarykram 1995 se karti aa rahi hai. inkaa vivaah ravi gopaalan nair, jo ek jane maane pav kathakali dastaane vaali kathputlikaar hai, se hua. ab parvati 1997 se tiruvanantpuram, kerala main rahati hai. yahaan ve ekataraa boul sangeet kalari naamak boul sangeet vidhyalay bhi chalaati hai. sangeet ke alaava parvati painting, print mekking, naatak, nrity or chitron kii sahaayta se kissagoi kii vidha main bhi maharath rakhti hai.
 manch par parvati haath main iktaaraa, kandhe par duggi evam anya paaramparik vaadya yantra taange boul nrity karti dikhaayi detii hai. gardan main pushpamala evam pairon main khanakati paayal ke saath kumkum aalata lagaae gerui veshbhusha main ve bhakthi geet kaa pradarshan karti hai. ye boul nrity main praayah radha yaa krishna kii raasalila dikhaati hai, jinamein praayah radha banakar sufi sangeet par apana nrity prastut karti hai. boul nrity style main gayan, nrity or sankirthan sabhi ek saamanjasy banaaye hue ek hi samay main upkarnon ke prayog kii tarah kiya jaataa he. boul ke ke bare main kehete hai ki yah gati main dhyaan kii sadhana he.
 rachanaatmak, prem, bhakthi or shanti kii mahima bataati boul ko sangeet se adhik upyukt madhyam bataate hai. is aitihaasik lok parampara main praayah vishesh prakaaraki bhusha evam sangeet ke vaadyayantra hi hote hai. ye style bengali parivesh se nikal hi rahi he, tathaa chaitanya mahaprabhu se prerit he. boul main gurudev rabindranath tagore kii kavita or sangeet kaa kaaphi prabhaav dekha jaataa he.
