 minisha lamba ek bhartiya hindi film abhinetri he.
 minisha lamba sherwood hai skul main padhai kii or baad main chettinad vidyashram chennai main padhi. 2004 main unhone angrezi main snaatak degree dilli ke miranda house se puri kii. 
 lamba kii ichha patrkaar banane kii thi. dilli main apni degree karte vakt unhen alji, soni, cadabri, hajamola, airtel, sanasilk aadi ke vigyapanon main kaam karne ke prastaav mili parantu vah cadabri kaa vigyaapan tha jisane unhen prakaashjyot main laa khadaa kiya. unhen bollywood nirdeshak shoojit sirkaar ne apni film yahan ke liye nimantrit kiya. unhone ise sweekaar kar abhinay kshetra main yahan se kadam rakhaa. samikshakon ne unke abhinay ko kaafii saraahaa.
 2008 main unhone siddharth anand kii film bachanaa e hasino main bipasha basu, deepika padukone or ranbir kapur ke saath mukhya bhoomika adaa kii. is film ko yash raaj films ne nirmit kiya tha. unka aglaa mukhya kirdaar shyaam benegal kii film wel dan abba main tha jisaki canans film samaaroh main kaafii tarif kii gai. unhone korporate, rocky: da rebel, anthoni koun he, honeymoon trevells privet limited, anamika, shaury or das kahaniyaan main sah sah-adaakara kii bhoomika nibhaai. unhone maxim india ke liye chitra bhi nikalvaaya.
 lamba philhal shiris kundar kii joker main shreyas talapade ke saath bhoomika adaa karengi.
