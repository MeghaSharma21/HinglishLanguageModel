 tendu yaa akmol yaa svarnamr ek pile, naarangi yaa laal rang kaa meetha fal hota he. iskaa akar 0.5 se 4 inch tak kaa gola hota he. bharat main yah madhya pradesh or uttar pradesh main, khaaskar vindhyaachal kii pahadiyon main, paidaa kiya jaataa he. 
 tendu ko angrezi main "percimman" kehete hai or kabhi-kabhi "date-plums" bhi kehete hai kyonki bahut se logon ko iskaa swaad kajur or aalu-buchaare kaa mishran lagata he.
 bhartiya or america tendu ke ped lagbhag 15 meter tak ugte hai. japani tendu kaa ped 4-10 meter kaa hota he.
