 vahaan rahete hue usne portugal ore england ke raajnneetik sambandh drudh karne kaa prayaas kiya. 1704 i. main jab uske bhai portugal naresh pedaro dwitiya biimaar pade to vah portugal ke shashan kii sangrakshika banaai gai or uske is shasankaal main portugal ko spain ke viruddh anek safalataa mili. 31 disambar 1705 ko uski mrityu hui. marate samay usne apni sari arjeet sampatti apane bhai ko vasiyat kar di thi.
 catherine bahut baar garbhvatee huin lekin kabhi jivit uttaradhikari ko janm nahi de pain. catherine ne england main chaay pine kaa rivaaj shuru kiya. kaha jaataa he ki naye yark ke queens ilaake kaa naam inpar rakhaa gaya, lekin iske samarthan main aitihaasik strot nahin mili hai.
