 vaksh yaa chaati maanvon va anya kasheruki praaniyon kii sharir yojana main aage kii ore sthit gardan or udar ke bich kaa bhaag hota he. isamen vaksh guha, vaksh bhitti ke saath-saath kai mansapeshiyaan or hriday, fefde, shwaasanlee, grasanalee tathaa thimus granthi jaise mahatvapurn ang shaamil hai. pasli pinjar puure chaati kshetra kii raksha karata he. chaati par kai rogon kaa prabhaav ho saktaa he or chaati-peeda inamen se kai kaa mukhya sanket hoti he.
