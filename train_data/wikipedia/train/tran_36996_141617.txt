 umesh chandra banerjee bhartiya barrister evam bhartiya rashtriya congress ke pratham adhyaksh the. britain ke house of comons ke liye chunaav ladane vaale ve pratham bhartiya the . britani sansad main pravesh paane kii unhone do koshishen kii kintu asafal rahe.
 umesh chandra banerjee kaa janm 29 disambar 1844 ko kalakatta ke ek ucch madhyam varg ke kuleen brahman parivaar main hua. unke purvaj hughali jile ke bangada naamak gaanv se the. unke pita kalakatta ucch nyaayalay main nyaaywaadee the. 1859 main unka vivaah hemangini motilal ke saath hua. unhone 1862 dablyoopi atornez of kalakatta supreme court main lipic kii naukari armbh kii. is samay unhone kanoonee jaankariyaan praapt kii jo unke aage ke jivan main kaaphi sahaayak rahi. 1864 main unhen bombay ke aaraje jijabai ne chaatravrutti ke saath england bheja. 1868 main apni kolkata vaapasi par unhen sar charles paul, barrister-at-lau, kalakatta ucch nyaayalay main naukari mili. anya vakil jepi kennedy ne bhi unki ek vakil ke rup main kaaphi sahaayta kii. kuch hi samay main wo ucch nyaayalay ke jane-maane vakeelon main se ek ho gayi. wo kalakatta vishvavidhyalay ke chaatr evam iske vidhi sankaay ke adhyaksh bhi rahe or iske baad vidhaan parishad ke liye bhi chune gayi. wo kalakatta baar se 1901 main seva nivrutt huye. unki putree janaki banerjee ne nevanham college, cambridge uniwarsity main praakrit vigyaan, rashayan shaastra, pranishastra or karyiki kii shiksha praapt kii.
 unhone bhartiya rashtriya congress ke bombay main 1885 main huye pratham satr kii adhyakshata kii. yah satr 28 disambar se 31 disambar tak chalaa tha or 72 sadasyon ne isamen bhaag liya tha.
