 shashwatvaad buddha ke samay main prachlit vah darshanik siddhanth he jo maanata he ki aatma ek rup, chirantan or nity he, unka na to kabhi naash hota he or na kabhi usamen koi vikaar hota he. yah siddhanth uchchhedwad kaa viparyaay he. is prakaar ke vichaar buddha ke samay main anek panthon or sampradaayon main vyaapt tha. buddha ne nikaayon or aagmon main iskaa khandan kiya. 
 buddha kaa anityavad, shashwatvaad tathaa uchchhedwad kaa madhyam maarg he. 'prayek vastu he main, yah ek ekaantik mat he, prayek vastu nahin he, yah doosraa ekantik mat he. in donon hi ekantik maton ko chhodkar buddha ne madhyam maarg kaa upadesh diya.
