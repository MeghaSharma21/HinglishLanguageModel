 bhartiya gyanpith bharat main sahitya sambandhi gatividhiyon ke sanvardhan or sangrakshan ke liye karyrat sabase pramukh or pratishthit santhaan he. shrimati rama jain or shri sahushaanti prasad jain dwaara santhaapit yah santhaan saahitiyik pustaken prakashit karata he tathaa gyanpith puraskaar or murtidevi puraskaar naamak do puraskaar pradaan karata he, jo sahitya ke sarvocch puraskaron main se hai. isaki sthapana 1944 main hui.
