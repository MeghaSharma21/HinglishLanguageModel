 is airline kii sthapana 1998 main hui thi or may 1999 se iskaa sanchaalan shuru hua tha. iske chaar pramukh share dharak hai jiname se leonnelle guren bhi ek hai evam yah nijee niveshkon or brit air ke swaamitv main he.
