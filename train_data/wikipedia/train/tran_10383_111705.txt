 uttari vibhaag prashanth mahaasaagar main sthit fiji desh ke chaar prashasnic vibhaagon main se ek he. is vibhaag main fiji kaa doosraa sabase bade dweep, vanua levu kaa purna bhaag or kuch anya chote dweep sammilit hai. isamen desh ke teen praant - buaa, thakoundrove or mathauata - shaamil hai. lambaasa is vibhaag kii rajdhani he.
