 samasthanik ek hi tatv ke parmaanu jinaki parmaanu sankhya samaan hoti hai, parantu bhaar alag-alag hota he, unhen samasthanik kaha jaataa he. inamen prayek parmaanu main samaan protone hote hai. jabaki neutron kii sankhya alag alag rahati he. is kaaran parmaanu sankhya to samaan rahati he, lekin parmaanu kaa dravyaman alag alag ho jaataa he. samasthanik kaa arth "samaan sthaan" se he. aavart sarini main tatwon ko parmaanu sankhya ke aadhaar par alag alag rakhaa jaataa he, jabaki samasthanik main parmaanu sankhya ke samaan rahane ke kaaran unhen alag nahin kiya gaya he, is kaaran inhen samasthanik kaha jaataa he. 
 parmaanu ke nabhik ke bheetar protone kii sankhya ko parmaanu sankhya kaha jaataa he, jo bina aayan vaale parmaanu ke electron ke baraabar hote hai. prayek parmaanu sankhya kisi vishisht tatv kii pahchaan bataata he, lekin aisa samasthanik main nahin hota he. isamen kisi tatv ke parmaanu main neutron kii sankhya vistrut ho sakati he. protone or neutron kii sankhya us parmaanu kaa dravyaman sankhya hota he or prayek samasthanik main dravyaman sankhya alag alag hota he. 
 udhaaharan ke liye, carbon ke teen samasthanik carbon-12, carbon-13 or carbon-14 hai. inamen sabhi kaa dravyaman sankhya kramshah 12, 13 or 14 he. carbon main 6 parmaanu hota he, jiskaa matlab he ki carbon ke sabhi parmaanu main 6 protone hote hai or neutron kii sankhya kramshah 6, 7 or aath he.
 samasthanik ke hone kii baat sabase pehle 1913 main fredrick soddi ne bataai thi. unke radiodharmee kshay ke adhyayan se bataaya ki uranium or sisa ke madhya 40 alag alag radiodharmee tatv hai, jabaki aavart sarini main uranium se sisa tak maatr 11 tatwon ko hi rakhaa gaya he.
