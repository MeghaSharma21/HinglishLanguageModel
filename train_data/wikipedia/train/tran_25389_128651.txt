 pashto, jise pakhto yaa afgaani bhi kaha jaataa he, afghanistan or pakistan main rahane vaale pathan samudaay kii mukhya bhashaa he. yah kendriya or dakshini afghanistan or uttar-pashchimi pakistan ke kaibar-pakhtuunakva praant ke vaasi hai. bhugolic drushti se yah aamu dariyaa se dakshin or sindhu nadi se pashchim ke kshetron main rahete hai. pashto hind-irani bhashaa-parivaar kii irani upashakha kii sadsy he or irani bhashaaon main ise ek purvi irani bhashaa mana jaataa he. anumaan kiya jaataa he ki vishwa-bhar main qareeb 5-6 karod log pashto apni maatrubhaasha ke rup main bolte hai. afghanistan ke samvidhan ne dari ke saath-saath pashto ko bhi ek rajbhasha hone kaa darja diya he.
 pashto bhi urdu or sindhi ki tarah arabi-farsi lipi kaa prayog karti he, lekin usamen kuch badlaav laae gaye hai taaki 't' jaisi prachlit dhvaniyaan likhi ja saken. dhyaan rahe ki urdu ke liye bhi aise badlaav kiye gaye hai, lekin urdu main alag tarah se parivertikaran hua jis se pashto or urdu lipiyon main kuch antar he.
 pashto kii do mukhya upbhashaayen hai: uttari or dakshini. uttari pashto bolne vaale apni bhashaa ko "pakhto" bolte hai or dakshini pashto bolne vaale apni bhashaa ko 'pashto' bolte hai. iske mukhya vibhaajan ke alaava bhi alag-alag sthanon par shabdon ko alag lahazon main bolaa jaataa he. alag-alag sthanon kii upbhaashaon main kuch chune hue shabd is prakaar hai:
 jaisaa ki dekha ja saktaa he pashto ke bahut se shabd sanskrit yaa hindi se milte-julate hai. is vajah se bahut se bhashavaigyanikon ko pehle bhram ho gaya ki shaayad pashto bhi ek hind-arya bhashaa he, lekin vyakaran jaanchne se pataa laga ki yah ek irani bhashaa he jisapar hind-arya bhashaaon kaa gaharaa prabhaav padaa he. vaise bhi irani bhashaaon or hind-arya bhashaaon main bahut se sajaateey shabd hai jo rup or arth main milte hai.
 pashto bhashaa or hindi main kaafii samantaayein pai jaati he. aadhunik hindi or urdu par bhi pashto kaa prabhaav he. donon hindi or pashto main "na" paaya jaataa he haalanki urdu main ye maujuud nahi he.
