 ye mahasagariya basin ke sabase neeche bhaag hai or inaki tali ausat mahasagariya nital ke kaaphi neeche milti hai. inaki sthiti sarvatra na milakar yatra-tatra bikhare hue rup main milti hai.vastav main ye mahasagariya nital par sthit teevr dhaal vaale lambe, patale tathaa gahare avanaman ke kshetra hai. inaki utapatti mahasagariya tali main prathvi ke crust ke valan evam bhranshan ke parinamswaroop maani jaati hai. arthath inaki utapatti vivartanik kriyaaon se hui hai.
