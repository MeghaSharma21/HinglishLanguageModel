 clerance house yaa clarence house, landan main, westminster shahar ke da mal kshetra main sthit ek shahi awaas he. yah bhawan, saint james pales se sata hua he, or donon ek hi baagh main sthit hai. yah mahal 1953 se 2002 tak, 50 saal ke avadhi tak raaj mata elizabeth kaa nivas rahaa tha. us samay ke baad se yah bhawan rajkumaar charles, wales ke rajkumaar or camila, cornwall kii dches kaa aadhikarik nivas he. yah bhawan 2003 se 2011 tak, prince wiliam kaa bhi aadhikarik nivas tha, jab 2011 main unka vivaah ho gaya. saath hi prince harry bhi 2003 se 2012 tak yahan rahete the. is mahal ko har garmiyon main kareeb ek maheene ke liye kholaa jaataa he, aam taur par agast ke maheene main. ise 1970 se grad-1 ke bhawan kaa darj praapt he.
 is bhawan ko wales ke rajkumaar kaa nijee karyaalay hone kii vishesh rupase pahachaanaa jataahe, or aksar "clarence house "ko "wales ke rajkumaar ke nijee karyaalay" ke liye ek upalakshan ke rup main upyog kiya jaataa he. pehle, iske jagah, "saint james pales" ke naam kaa prayog kiya jaataa tha.
