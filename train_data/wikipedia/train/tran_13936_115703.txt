  santoor bharat ke sabase lokapriy vaadyayantra main se ek he jiskaa prayog shastreey sangeet se lekar har tarah ke sangeet main kiya jaataa he.
 santoor ek vaadya yantra he. santoor kaa bhartiya naam 'shatatantri veena' yani sau taron vaali veena he jise baad main farsi bhashaa se santoor naam mila.
 santoor kaa bhartiya naam tha shatatantri veena yani sau taron vaali veena jise baad main farsi bhashaa se santoor naam mila. yah apane aap main ek anokhaa vaadya he jo ki taar kaa saz hone ke baavjood lakdi kii choti chhadon se bajaayaa jaataa he. 
 yah mul rup se kashmir kaa lok vaadya he jise sufi sangeet main istemaal kiya jaataa tha. yah ek simit samudaay ke bich hi istemaal hota tha. keval vaadi-e-kashmir main iskaa chalan tha. baaki to jammu sahit or jagahon par log iske bare main jaanate hi nahin the. pandit sivakumar sharma ne ise lokapriyata kii paraakaashtha tak pahunchane main vishesh yogadan diya.
