 oun-zaaraa, ek pakistani dharawahik he jiskaa prasaaran pakistan ke e-plus antertenment par har guuruuvaar ko kiya jaataa tha. bharat main iskaa prasaaran zindagi par pratidin kiya jaataa tha. yah dharawahik zindagi tivi channel ke shuruaati kaaryakramon se ek tha. is dharawahik kii kahani ek shaadishuda jode ke aasapaas gumti he. yah pakistani lekhak fiza iftikhar ke upanyaas, 'hisar-e-mohabbat' par aadhaarit he or iskaa nirdesan haissam hossain dwaara kiya gaya he. pramukh bhoomikaaon ko maaya ali or osman khalid bat ne nibhaayaa he.
