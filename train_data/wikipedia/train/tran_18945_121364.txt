 banu tameem janajati yaa bani tamim arab kii mukhya janajatiyon main se ek he. vartmaan samay main is janajati ke vanshaj arab prayadweep or saudi arab jaise padosi deshon main based hai. unamem iraq, kuwait, katar, oman, bahrain or sanyukt arab emirate jaise desh shaamil hai.arabi main tamim shabd kaa arth majboot or thos he. iskaa arth purnataa bhi ho saktaa he. wahabiwad ke aagaman se pehle banu tamim ke bich islamic shiksha kaa bahut kam itihaas tha.
