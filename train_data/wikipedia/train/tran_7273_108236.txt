 chanpak bacchon kii ek hindi patrika he jo pakshik he.
 chanpak kaa pehla sanskaran 1968 main prakashit hua.
 yah patrika 8 bhashaaon main prakashit hoti he. yah patrika dilli press dwaara prakashit hoti he. chanpak ek bal patrika he, jisamen adhiktar kahaniyon ke paatr jaanavar hi hote hai. nai dilli ke jhandevalaan ilaake kii rani jhaansi rod par sthit dilli press kii is patrika main jaadui kahaniyon kii koi jagah nahin he. pari sansar se dur yah patrika jaanwaron ke saath-saath vigyaan ke vishay se sambandhit kahaniyon ko bhi chhapane main bhi ruchi rakhti he. 'chiku' naamak iskaa sthai cartoon stambh sabakaa pasandida paatr he. iske alaava isamen chanpak chekars naamak ek game bhi prakashit kiya jaataa he. dilli press ne jaani maani antariksh yatri kalpana chawla ko ek tii-shart bhent kii thi jis par chanpak likhaa tha. is tii-shart ko unki mrityu ke baad unke pati khud dilli press ko lautaane aae the.
