 
 2016-17 nashnal cricket lig bangladesh main yojit kiya ja rahaa he, nashnal cricket lig pratham shrenee cricket pratiyogita ke atharahawein sanskaran he. toornament 25 sitambar 2016 ko shuru kiya tha. khulna divijan gat champion hai.
 baari barish baarah ek drow ke alaava kisi anya parinaam main samaapt hone se keval do maichon ke saath match ke pehle teen round prabhavit. bangladesh cricket board ne ghoshnaa kii he ki sabhi maichon main 14 oktober 2016 par jagah lene ke liye nirdhaarit jab tak mausam main sudhaar deree ho jayega. haalanki, vahaan ke mausam main koi sudhaar nahin he or un maichon tha or bhavishya judanar aagaamee bangladesh premier lig kii vajah se sthagit kar diya gaya tha. toornament ko garibi rekha se neeche ke samaapan ke baad 20 disambar 2016 ko phir se shuru kar diya gaya.
