 farrukh sier ek mogul badshaah tha jisane 1713 se 1719 tak hindustan par hukumat kii. 
 uskaa puraa naam abbul mujaffaruddin mohammad shah farrukh sier tha. aalim akbar sani vaala, shaan padshahi bahr-ur-baar, tathaa shahide-mazloom uske shahi kitaabon ke naam hua karte the. 1715 i. main ek shishtmandal jaan suraman kii netritv main bharat aayaa. yah shishtmandal uttarvarti mogul shaasha farrukh sier kii darbar main 1717 i. main pahunchaa. us samay farrukh sier jaanaleva ghaav se peedit tha. is shishtmandal main hamiltan naamak doctor the jinhonen farrakhsier kaa ilaaj kiya tha.isse farrukh sier khush hua tathaa angrejon ko bharat main kahin bhi vyapaar karne kii anumati tathaa angrezon dwaara banaaai gaye sikke ko bharat main sabhi jagah manyata pradaan kar diya gaya. farrukh sier dwaara jari kiye gaye is ghoshnaa ko east india company kaa megna carta kaha jaataa he. megna carta kaa sarvapratham 1215 i. main britain main jaan-II ke dwaara hua tha.
