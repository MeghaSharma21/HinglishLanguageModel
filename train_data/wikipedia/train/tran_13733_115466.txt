 mitocondrial diene mitocondria main sthit diene he, eukeriotic cell ke cellular angon ke bheetar bhojan se rasinic urja main parivartit karke koshikaon use adenosine tryphosphate kii tarah upyog kar sakate hai. mitocondrial diene 37 jin lagbhag 16,569 aadhaar jode gaya he or paas code he ki oxidative phophorilikaran prakriya yaa cellular urja utpaadan main istemaal kiya utpaadon ke liye. mitocondrial diene ek eukeriotic cell main diene kaa keval ek chota sa hissaa he. sabase bahukoshikeey jeevon main, mtDNA ek paripatr, sahasanyojak band, dabl asahaay diene ke rup main yojit kiya jaataa he. lekin kai koshikeey bhi bahukoshiki or durlabh maamalon main mtDNA paaya jaataa he.
