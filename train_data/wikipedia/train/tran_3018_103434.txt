 hindi ke naye dhang ke gadhy-lekhakon main aapkaa naam vishesh rup se ullekhneey he. kai naye prakaar ke gadhy-madhyamon kaa bhi aapane prayog kiya he. 'gyanoday' ke ankon main prakashit 'jo ve swayan na kah paae' isi prakaar kii rachanaayen hai. vibhinn lekhakon ke sahyog se prastut dharawahik upanyaas 'gyaarah sapanom kaa desh' kii niyojna bhi aapane kii. sfoot vishyon par likhe gaye nibandhon ke sankalan 'kaagaz kii kishtiyaan' tathaa 'nae rang, nae dhang' sheershak se prakashit aapaki pramukh kritiyaan hai.
