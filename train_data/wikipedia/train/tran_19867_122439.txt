 china kaa sabase purana rajvansh he - shiya rajvansh. inkaa astitv ek lokkatha lagata tha par henon main puratathvik khudaai ke baad iske vajood kii satyata saamne ai. pratham pratyaksh rajvansh tha -shang rajvansh, jo purvi china main 18veen se 12 veen sadi isa purv pili nadi ke kinare bas gaye. 12veen sadi isa purv main pashchim se jhaau shaskaon ne inpar hamalaa kiya or inke kshetron par adhikaar kar liya. inhone 5veen sadi isa purv tak raaj kiya. iske baad china ke chote rajya aapasi sangharsh main bhid gaye. isa purv 221 main kin raajaaon ne china kaa pratham baar ekikaran kiya. inhone raja kaa karyaalay sthapit kiya or chini bhashaa kaa maanakeekaran kiya. isa purv 220 se 206 i. tak haan rajvansh ke shaskaon ne china par raaj kiya or china kii samskruti par apni amit chhap chodi. fih prabhaav ab tak vidyaman he. inhone resham maarg kii bhi sthapana rakhi. haanon ke patan ke baad china main phir se araajaktaa kaa maahaul chaa gaya. sui rajvansh ne 580 isvi main chaan kaa ekikaran kiya jiske kuch hi saalon baad is rajvansh kaa patan ho gaya.
