 frigat ek madhyam aakaar kii yudhpot hoti he jinhen tej gati or furati se disha badalane kii drushti se nirmit kiya jaataa he. inkaa prayog anya naukaaon va jahaazon ke saath chalakar unki raksha karne, unke liye saamaan or rasad laane-jane or tezi se kisi choti nauka ko pakadaney ke liye kiya jaataa he. yah swayan baari nausainik yuddh ke liye sakasham nahin hoti. frigat se chote aakaar ke jahaaz ko corgett kaha jaataa he.
