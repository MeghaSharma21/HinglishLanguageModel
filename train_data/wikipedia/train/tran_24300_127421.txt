 dehradun se 10 kimi kii duuri par musuri ke raste main yah ek sundar paryaton sthal he jo shivalik shrunkhlaa kii talahati main sthit he. malsi dear park ek chota sa chidyaghar he jahaan bacchon ke liye prakritik saundarya se ghira ek park bhi viksit kiya gaya he. sundar vaataavaran ke kaaran yahaan taazgi kaa ahsaas hota he jisase yah ek adarsh darshneey-sthal or picnic-spot ban chukaa he.
