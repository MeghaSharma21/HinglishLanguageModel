 crepe jhilmil banaavat ko reshmi kapadaa jo dekhne main ek ajeeb dhang kaa kadaa or salwat padaa jaan padataa he. yah kade reshmi soot se buna jaataa he. isaki do kismein prachlit hai- narm purvi athwa canton crepe or kadaa crepe. canton crepe dekhne main lahardaar dikhaai padataa he. iske bane kaa taar do sooton ko gond ke saath ulti disha main batakar kadaa taiyaar kiya jaataa he. bunate samay kapadaa ekadam chikna hota he. usamen kisi prakaar kii salwat nahin hoti. baad main jab ubalkar gond nikaal diya jaataa he, vah ekadam naram ho jaataa he or dhaage kii anthan dhili ho jaati he jisase kapade main salawatein pad jaati he jo is vastra kii visheshta maani jaati hai. chini or japani is prakaar kaa crepe taiyaar karne main nipun maane jate hai.
 kade crepe kii katai or bunai saamaanya hoti he. uskaa crepe swaroop bunai ke baad kii prakriya main nihit he. kintu isi kya prakriya he yah nirmaataa hi jaanate hai or ve use gopneey rakhte hai. is prakaar kaa crepe ek dhaage, do dhaage, teen dhaage yaa chaar dhaage kaa banataa he or praaia: kaale rang main taiyaar kiya jaataa he. england main yah asex, narwich, yaaramath, manchester or glassgo main banataa he. ab reshmi crepe kii nakal par sooti crepe bhi banane lage hai.
