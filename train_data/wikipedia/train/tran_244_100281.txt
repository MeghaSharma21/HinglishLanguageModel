 nobel puraskaar har varsh royal swedish academy of sciences, da swedish academy, da karolinska institute, evam da norwegian nobel kamity dwaara un logon or sansthaaon ko pradaan kii jaati he jinhone rasayanashastra, bhautikeeshastra, sahitya, shanti, evam aushadhivigyaan ke kshetra main adwitiya yogadan diya ho. nobel puraskaron kii sthapana alfred nobel ke vasietname ke anusaar 1895 main hui. vasietname ke mutabaki nobel puraskaron kaa prashaskeey kaary nobel foundation dekhegaa. arthshaastra ke kshetra main nobel puraskaron kii shuruyaat 1968 main sweden kii kendriya bank sweringe rixbank dwaara hui. yah puraskaar arthshaastra ke kshetra main adwitiya kaary karne vaale logon or sansthaaon ko har varsh diya jaataa he. prayek puraskaar ek alag samiti dwaara pradaan kiya jaataa he. da royal swedish academy of sciences bhautikee, arthshaastra or rasayanashastra main, karolinska institute aushadhi ke kshetra main, norwegian nobel samiti shanti ke kshetra main puraskaar pradaan karti he. prayek puraskaar vijeta ko ek medal, ek diploma, ek monetary eward pradaan kii jaati he.
 varsh 1901 main, pehle nobel puraskaar vijeta ko puraskaar ke rup main 150,782 swedish krone die gaye, jo 2007 ke mutabik 7,731,004 swedish krone ke baraabar he. varsh 2008 main, vijetaon ko puraskaar main 10,000,000 swedish krone kii raashi puraskaar main di gai puraskaar stoffhome main 10 disambar ko yojit ek samaaroh main pradaan kiya jaataa he. 10 disambar ko hi alfred nobel kaa nidhan hua tha. varsh 2008 tak 789 logon or 20 sansthaaon ko nobel puraskaar pradaan kiya ja chukaa he, isamen 62 arthshaastra main puraskaar paane vaale bhi shaamil hai. chaar nobel puraskaar vijetaon ko unke sarkaron dwaara puraskaar sweekaar karne kii anumati nahin mili. adolf hitler ne teen germanon, richard kuhan, adolf butennt, evam garhard domagk or soviet union kii sarkaar ne boris pasteranak ko puraskaar nahin lene ke liye dabaav daalaa. do nobel puraskaar vijetaon jyaan-paal sartra or lee dak tho ne puraskaar lene se inkaar kar diya. sartra ne kisi bhi aadhikarik sammaan ko lene se inkaar kiya to lee dak tho ne vietnam main kharaab sthiti ke kaaran puraskaar se inkaar kar diya. chah vijetaon ne ek se adhik baar nobel puraskaar jeeta. inamen, da international kamity of da red cross socity ne teen baar nobel shanti puraskaar jeeta, jo kisi anya se zyada he. 809 nobel vijataaon main 35 mahilaayein shaamil hai. pehali mahila nobel puraskaar vijeta meri curi thi, jinhone 1903 main bhautikee ke liye nobel puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya.. varsh 1940 or 1942 main dwitiya vishwayuddh ke kaaran puraskaar nahin die gaye.
