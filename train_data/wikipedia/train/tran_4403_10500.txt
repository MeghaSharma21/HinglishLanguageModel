 catholic uniwarsity of luven, luven shahar main sthit, belgium kaa sabase bada, sabase purana or sabase pramukh vishvavidhyalay mana jaataa he. luven vishvavidhyalay ko 1425 main brabent ke duk jon IV dwaara sthapit kiya gaya tha or pope martin V ke paapal bul dwaara anumodith kiya gaya tha. belgium ke sabase pramukh vishvavidhyalay ke rup main saikadon varshon tak iskaa vikaas hua. kamil bulke ne yahan se civil ejineering main biessi degree hasil kii thi.
