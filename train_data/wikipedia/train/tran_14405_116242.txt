 satish dhawan antariksh centre, bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan kaa prakshepan centre he. yah andhra pradesh ke sriharikota main sthit he, ise 'sriharikota range' yaa 'sriharikota laanching range' ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. 2002 main isro ke purv prabandhak or vaigyanik satish dhawan ke maranoparant unke sammaan main iskaa naam badla gaya. 
 kendriya mantrimandal ne 12 sitambar, 2013 ko satish dhawan antariksh kendra, sriharikota main prakshepan yaan kii asembli ke liye duusare bhawan ke nirmaan kii manjuuri di. is par 363.95 karod rupee kii anumaanit laagat aegi, jisamen saat karod rupee kaa kharch videshi mudra main hoga. is duusari building ke uplabdh ho jane se pieselvi or jieselvi kii prakshepan frequency badhegi. yah jieselvi emke-III ke ekikaran ke liye vartmaan whical asembli building ko atirikt suvidha muhaiyaa karaayegi. tisare prakshepan pad tathaa bhavishya main saamaanya yaan prakshepan ke liye bhi isse kaaphi suvidha milegi.
 is laanch pad se upgrah prakshepan yaan or sanvardhit upgrah prakshepan yaan ko laanch kiya gaya tha. yah vartmaan prakshepan sthal ke dakshini sire par sthit he. ise sevamukt kar diya gaya he. shuru main ise upgrah prakshepan yaan laanch karne ke liye banaaya gaya tha. lekin baad main ise sanvardhit upgrah prakshepan yaan prakshepan parisar ke rup main istemaal kiya gaya tha.
