 satluj yamuna link nahar athwa es.vaai.el. bharat main sutluj or yamuna nadiyon ko jodne ke liye ek prastaavit 214 kilometre lambii nahar pariyojna he. haalanki, is prastaav main kai baadhaaen hai, or vartmaan main bhartiya sarvocch nyaayalay main yah pariyojna lambit he. es.vaai.el. vastutah punjab or haryana rajyon ke bich nadi jal bantwaare ko sanderbhit karata he.
 is vivaad kii shuruuaat tab hui, jab 31 oktober 1966 ko punjab rajya ko punargathit kiya gaya or haryana rajya bana tha. vivaad kaa pramukh jad he- nadi jal kaa bantwaaraa. 1976 main tatkaaleen pradhanmantri indira gandhi ne is maamle main hastkshep kiya or faisla kiya ki donon rajya arthath prayek 3.5 million acre foot praapt karenge. janvari 2002 main supreme court ne pariyojna kii khudaai jari rakhane ke liye punjab sarkaar ko nirdesh diya. 2003 main punjab ne is daayitv se mukt hone kii sochiqui 2004 main punjab rajya vidhanmandal ne bhoomi ko dinotiphoi karne ke liye "punjab termination of agreement act-2004" paarit kiya. is sambandh main supreme court main ek presidential riference di gayi or march 2016 main supreme court ne is par sunwaayee aarambh kiya. 2016 main punjab vidhansabha main punah ek vidheyak paas kar kisaanon ko adhigrahit bhoomi waapas karne kii baat kii gai he. supreme court ne vidheyak par yathasthiti kaa aadesh diya he.
 nahar kaa kaary 85% tak puraa ho gaya he, or haryana sarkaar ne nahar ke apane hisse ke kaary ko lagbhag puraa kar liya he. haryana ne apane yahaan nahar ke 92 kimi ke kaary ko puraa kar liya he.
