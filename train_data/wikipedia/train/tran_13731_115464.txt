 nirdeshaank: 22°19′N 79°31′E / 22.32°N 79.51°E / 22.32; 79.51chaurai bharat desh main madhya pradesh rajya ke chhindwara jila kii ek tehsil, vidhansabha kshetra or nagarpalika hain. chaurai se chhindwara or siwani jila kii duuri 35 kilometre hachaurai vidhaan sabha kshetra me bichhua or chaand tehsil shaamil hain.yahan ke pramukh dharmik sthalo main 
 pramukh hain.
 san 1966 se chaurai main tulsi jayanti prativarsh bade dhoomdhaam se manaayi jaati he 21 dino tak ankhad ramayana ke paath ke pashchaat tulsi janyati ke din ramayana samaapan ke baad mata mandir se goswami tulsidas ji kii photo palki main rakhkar pure nagar main ghumaayi jaati he inke peeche jhankiyaan va shaila nrity karte hue log aage badhate hain. nagar kii tulsi jayanti jisaki khyaati dur dur tak he isame shaila nrity,jas pratiyogita or jhankiyaan aadi pramukh he jise dekhne log dur dur se aate hain sthaneey kalamanch main shaila nrity kaa aayojan hota he jisame aas-paas kshetro se aaye gramin bade utsaah se bhaag lete hain ore janpratinidhiyo ke dwaara puraskaar pate he saath hi chaurai main ganesh utsav or sharadiya navaratri bhi bade dhoomdhaam se manaayi jaati hai.
 yah ek pramukh khel maidan hain is stadium me mukhya rup se cricket or footvaal zyada khelaa jaataa he jisame harsaal trophy hoti he saath hi 15 agast or 26 janvari ko aayojan isi maidan par hota he jisame reli or vibhinn prakaar ke rangarang prastuti skool ke bachche dete hain.
