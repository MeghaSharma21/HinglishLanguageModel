 kubalai khan yaa 'khubilai khan' mongol samrajya kaa paanchava kagaan tha. usne 1260 se 1294 tak shashan kiya. vah purvi asia main yuan vansh kaa sansthaapak tha. uskaa rajya prashanth mahaasaagar se lekar ural tak or siberia se vartmaan afghanistan tak failaa hua tha jo vishwa ke rahane yogya kshetrafal kaa 20 pratishat he. kubalai khan mongol samrajya se sansthaapak changez khan kaa pota or uske sabase chote bete tolui khan kaa betaa tha. uski mata sorboogtani beki ne use or uske bhaaiyon ko bahut nipunata se paala or parvaarik paristhitiyon par aisa niyantran rakhaa ki kubalai mongol samrajya ke ek bade bhu-bhaag kaa shaasha ban sakaa.
