 pashchim germany 1952 or 1988 ke bich olympic khelon main bhaag liya. 1952 main - dwitiya vishwa yuddh or germany ke vibhaajan ke baad germany kii pehali olympic - purv germany kii ek puraani team thi, jisane purvi germany ko sahyog dene se inkaar kar diya tha, iske parinaam germany kii ore gine jate hai. isi tarah, 1956 or 1964 ke bich donon deshon ne germany dhvaj ke uniphid team ke tahat ekajute kiya. yah 1968 tak nahin tha ki ek purna pashchim german team kii upasthiti hui, or yah sabhi olympic main lekin 1980 greeshmakaleen olympic main bhaag liya - jaisaa ki 1980 ke greeshmakaleen olympic bahishkaar kaa paalan kiya gaya - 1988 tak. iske baad, 1990 main german punarmilan ne german team ko phir se sthapit kiya.
 pashchim germany ne ek avasar par khelon kii mejabani kii he.
