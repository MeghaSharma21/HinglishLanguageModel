 lord of da flise 
 lord of da flise wiliam golding dwaara likhit ek upanyaas he. yah sheershak bilazebab naamak dusht yahudee devtaa ke naam kaa shabdic anuwaad he jo philistin ke akrone shahar main puja jaataa tha. yah upanyaas 1954 main prakashit hua tha. 
 golding ne do nijee anubhavon par is upanyaas ko aadhaarit kiya 1. apane samay ke ladakon ke public skulon ke adhyaapan anubhav or 2. vditiya vishwa yuddh ke anubhav.
