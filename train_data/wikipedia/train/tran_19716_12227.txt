 jhalak dikhalaa ja ek bhartiya televijan vaastavik kaarykram he jiskaa praaroop britain ke bibisi van channel ke ek nrity kendrit kaarykram se liya gaya he.
 jhalak dikhalaa ja ke chathe satr kii mejabani kapil sharma evam manish paul ne kii. is satr main maadhuri dikshit, remo disuja tathaa karan johar ne nireekshak kii bhoomika nibhaai.pratiyogita main bhaag lene vaale ummidavaar—
 kaarykram kaa antim charan 14 sitambar 2013 ko prasaarit hua or drushti dhami evam salmaan usuf khan kii jodi vijeta rahi.
 jhalak dikhalaa ja kaa 7 waan satr 07 june 2014 ko aarambh hua. kaarykram kii mejabani ranvir shouri tathaa drushti dhami ne kii. lekin kaarykram ke chauthe saptaah main drushti dhami kii jagah manish paul ne le lee. remo disuja, maadhuri dikshit evam karan johar nireekshak kii bhoomika nibhaa rahe hai.

 jhalak dikhalaa ja reloded; ise pehle bhi host rah chuke manish paul dwaara host kiya gaya he. pichley sijan se is baar jaj ke rup main karan johar hi jhalak main dikhaai denge. shahid kapur,lauren gotlib or ganesh hegade is baar jhalak ke naye jaj hai.
