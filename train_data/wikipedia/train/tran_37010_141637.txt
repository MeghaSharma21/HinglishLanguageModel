 nayaki devi chalukya vansh kii maharani thi, jisane 1178 i. main mohammad gori ko parrast kiya tha. 
 virangana nayaki devi kandab ke mahamandaleshwar parmandi kii putree thi. inkaa vivaah gujarat ke maharaja ajaypal se hua tha. ajaypal siddhraj jaisingh ke pautra tathaa kumarpal ke putr the. angrakshak dwaara varsh 1176 main ajaypal kii hatya ke baad rajya kii bagdor maharani nayaki devi ke haath main aa gai thi, kyonki tab unke putr mulraj balya avasthaa main the.
 mohammad gori ko jab pataa chalaa ki gujarat par ek vidhwaa rani kaa shashan he to usne gujarat par akraman kar diya. purv soochna ke aadhaar par nayaki devi kii sena ne gujarat kii rajdhani patan se dur abu parvat kii talahati main kayaadaraa ke nikat pahunch kar ghori se yuddh kiya. is yuddh main ghori buree tarah se ghayal hua or use praan bachaa kar bhaagna padaa. iske baad ghori ne kabhi gujarat kii ore mud kar nahin dekha.
