 hifaazat 1987 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. is film main pehali baar maadhuri dikshit kii jodi anil kapur ke saath banaai gai thi. kadar khan ne sawad likhe hai or aar di burman dwaara sangeet diya gaya he.
 lakshmi ke teen mare hue bachche hone ke baad, uskaa pati, satyaprakash or uski maa darate hai ki vah or bachche paidaa nahin kar paaegi, or isliye parivaar ke naam ko aage badhaane ke liye koi bhi nahin hoga. isliye ve prastaav dete hai ki satyaprakash duusari baar shaadi kar le, jiske liye lakshmi sahmati detii he. phir wo rukmini se shaadi kar letaa he. is vivaah ke baad lakshmi garbhvatee ho jaati he or ek bete rajkumaar ko janm detii he, or uske turant baad rukmini bhi ek bete lakhan ko janm detii he. rajkumaar kaa apharan kar liya jaataa he, or wo unke jivan se gaayab ho jaataa he. uske baad, ek thekedaar kii hatya ke liye satyaprakash ko giraftaar kiya gaya, or jail kii sajaa sunai gai. rukmini or uskaa bhai buddhiram, ghar chalaane lagate hai or lakshmi par durvyavhaar karne lagate hai. saalon baad, ek yuva vyakti lakshmi or satyaprakash ke laapata betaa hone kaa daawa karata he, or vah asahaay lakshmi ke liye kuch sammaan laane kaa prayaas karata he. lekin yah lambey samay tak nahin tikta he. jab satyaprakash ko jail se reeha kiya jaataa he, to vah jawan aadmi ko apane saathi jail kaidi ke rup main pahachaanata he, or lakshmi ke sadame or niraashaa ke baavjood, use ghar chhodane ke liye kahata he. use pataa nahin he ki ab usne buddhiram ke liye use or lakshmi ko maarane ke liye darwaja khol diya he.
