 sajadaa tere pyaar main ek bhartiya television dharawahik he jiskaa prasaaran 2012 main shuru kiya gaya haau. yah aalia kii kahani bataata he jo ek college chaatra he jo apane jivan kaa makasad pyaar ke zarie paati he. uski deshbhakti kii tab pariksha hoti he jab vah dahshatwaadiyon se muqabla karti he.
 sajadaa tere pyaar main ek 21 varshiya yuva aadhunik muslim ladki aalia par aadhaarit he jo mumbai main rahati he. vah ek alhad ladki se paripakv jawan aurat main badal jaati he. aalia kaa pyaar byaaj, ranvir, ro agent he, bharat kii bahari khufia agency ke liye kaam kar rahaa he. ranvir aalia jasoos mahendra pratap ko pakadaney main madad ke liye poochta he. vah ek chauraahaa jahaan vah pyaar or kartavya ke bich chunane ke liye majaboor karne ke liye aata he.
