 gambia nadi vikaas sanghathan; Gambia River Basin Organistion: yah sanghathan gambia nadi ke sansadhano ke vikaas main samanvay sthapit karne ke uddeshy se chaar deshon ko ek manch par laata he. iskaa mukhyalay dakaar senegal main sthit he. sadsyta vaale desh gambia, gini, gini-bisaau or senegal hai.
 senegal or gambia ne 1978 main gambia nadi vikaas sanghathan kagathan kiya. gini or gini-bisaau kramshah 1981 or 1983 main iske sadsy bane. is sanghathan kaa mukhya uddeshy hai-
 purna athwa nirmaanadhinayojanaon main gambia nadi parpul kaa nirmaan or kyanga,geba evam coliba, koruval nadi ghaatiyon main krishivikas sammilit he. sinchai or jal- vidyut pariyojnaon par vishesh dhyaan diya ja rahaa he.
