 osman pratham yaa osman gaazi osman sultanate or osmani shahi khandaan ke sansthaapak the. unki sultanate kaa shasankaal chah sadiyon tak rahaa tha.
 osman ke walid arthugrul ne kaayi turkon ke saath mongolia kii sarazamin ko chhodkar anatolia main sthanantaran kiya. unhone vahaan kii seljuk turk rumi sultanate se vafadaari nibhaai, baad main is sultanate ne arthugrul ko apni emirate kaa nirmaan karne hetu anumati di. aage chalakar arthugrul ne padosi bikeentin rajyon par vijay prapthkar apni emirate kii simaaon kaa vistaar kiya.
 osman ne apane walid kaa sthaan lekar unki emirate kii simaaon kaa adhik vistaar kiya. samast islamic vishwa se aveshpurna muslim sainikon ne osman kii sena main shaamil hue taaki ve sab milakar nirbal bikeentin samrajya‎ par qabza paa sake. osman ke antim sainya abhiyaan bikeentin shahar bursa par tha.
