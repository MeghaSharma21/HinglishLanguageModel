 shishir kumaar ghosh bharat ke prasiddh rashtravaadi patrkaar, amrit bazaar patrika ke sansthaapak tathaa swatantraaa sangram senaanee the. ve 'india lig' ke sansthaapakon main se the. ve ek vaishnav the. unhone chaitanya mahaprabhu ke jeevancharit par 1897 main angreji main ek pustak kii rachana bhi kii thi. ve shrila bhakthivinod thakur ke mitra the bhi jinako unhone 'saptam goswami' kaha tha.
 shishir ghosh apane jivan ke adhikansh samay shantiniketan main rahe jahaan ve angreji ke praadhyaapak the. ve rabindranath thakur kii rachanaaon ke gyaata or rahasyavaad ke adhyeta the.
