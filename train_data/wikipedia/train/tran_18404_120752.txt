 di purandeshwari andhra pradesh se bhartiya raajnneetigya he. wo 2009 main hue aamchunaav main andhra pradesh ke vishakhapattanam chunaav kshetra se 15veen loksabhaa ke liye sadsy nirvaachit huin. isse pehle wo baaptla lok sabha kshetra kaa pratinidhitiv karti thim. purandeshwari 7 march 2014 ko congress chhodkar bhartiya janata party main shaamil hui.
 unhone sansad main vibhinn ahm bilon par charchaaon main bhaag liya, udahrnarth: gharelu hinsa vidheyak, hindu uttaradhikaar bill aadi. asian age patrika ne sansad main unke pradarshan kii saraahna karte hue unhen san 2004-05 kaa sarvshereshth saansad chunaa.
 unhone apni shiksha chennai ke pratishthit vidhyalay sacrade heart matriculation hyer secondary skool, carch park se grahan kii. unhone bie kii degree south indian adjukeshnal trust and women college, chennai se hasil kii. unhone ratnashastra kaa bhi course kiya he. baad main wo hyderabad main jem and jewelri institute main sthaanantarit ho gayin.
 wo paanch bhashaayen likh va pard sakati hai. iname angreji, telegu, tamil, hindi or francisi shaamil hai.   
 wo andhra pradesh ke purv mukhyamantri or telegu desham party ke sansthaapak en tii ramarao kii beti hai. unhone daggubati venkateshwara raav se vivaah kiya he, jo kendra sarkaar main purv mantri, purv cabinet sadsy or andhra pradesh vidhaan sabha ke vartmaan vidhayak he. ve prakasham jile kii parchur vidhaan sabha kshetra se chune gaye. vah telegu film abhinetri nandamuri balakrishna or nandamuri harikrishna kii bahan hai.
