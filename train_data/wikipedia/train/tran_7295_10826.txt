 anuvadak strotbhasha ke paath ko arthpurna rup se lakshyabhasha main anudit karata he. anuvadak ke dwaara kiye jane vaali ye prakriya anuwaad kaary kehalaati he. anuwaad kaa kaary antatogatva ek hi vyakti karata he . ekaaki anuwaad main to anuvadak akelaa hota hi he, sahyogaatmak anuwaad main bhi, antim bhaag main, sampaadan kaa kaam anuvadak ko akele karnaa hota he. atah anuvadak ke saath anek daayitv jud jate hai or kaary ke safal nishpaadan main usase anek apekshaayen rahati hai . bhashaa gyaan, vishay gyaan, abhivyakti kaushal, vyaktigat gun aadi kii drushti se anuvadak se hone vaali apekshaaon par vichaar karnaa hota he .
