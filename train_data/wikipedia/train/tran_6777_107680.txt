 zaalamaan dimshits belarus ke yanovi psorant vitibask kshetra main 1921 i. main paidaa hue the.
 zaalaman ne moscow institute of oriental studies se 1950 i. main apni shiksha puri kii thi.
 zaalaman ne hindi or urdu kii chah paathya pustaken likhi thi. iske ilaava unhone donon bhashaaon ke lokapriy kahawaton or shabdkoshon par kaafii kaam kiya he.
 1969 main zaalaman ne hindi kii ek paathya pustak likhi jo ki aaj ke vidhyaarthiyon ke liye bhi maargdarshan kaa kaam detii he.
 zaalaman kii mrityu ke das saal se bhi adhik samay ke baad 2001 main unki likhi "urdu grammer" kitaab prakashit hui.
 zaalaman ko bharat or rus donon hi sarkaron kii ore se puraskaron se sammanit kiya gaya he. bharat kii ore se unhen jawahar laal nehru puraskaar diya gaya tha.
 zaalaman kaa 1990 main dehant hua tha.
 2011 main bharat main rus ke rajdoot alexander kadakin ne prastaav diya ki zaalaman dimshitas ke janm din yani 25 may ko anaupchaarik roosi bharatvid diwas ke rup main manaanaa chahie.
