 nakhunon ke kavak dwaara sankramit hone ko nakhkvakta yaa nakhdadru kehete hai. yah nakhunon main sabase adhik hone vaala rog he. yah keval anguuthe main yaa iske alaava anya anguliyon kii nakhon main bhi ho saktaa he. kintu anguuthon kaa sankraman apekshaakrit adhik dekha jaataa he. 
 nakhkvakta kaa sankraman 6% se 8% vayask logon main paaya jaataa he.
