 ariari madagascar kii mudra he. 5 iriembilanja main samavibhajit yah mudra duniya ke do gaiyr dashamalav vaali mudraon main se ek he. ariari or iriembilanja shabd upnivesh se purv jari mudra se liye gaye hai, inamen se ariari chaandi kii mudra kaa naam tha. iriembilanja kaa shabdic arth 'ek loha vajan' he, jo ariari ke 1⁄5 bhaag ko puraane samay main kaha jaataa tha.
 ariari ko 1961 main jari kiya gaya. yah 5 franks ke baraabar tha. sikke or banknote donon hi franks or ariari main jari kiye gaye. ariari kii up-ikai iriembilanja, 1⁄5 ariari ke baraabar, ek frank ke baraabar thi. 1 janvari 2005 ko ariari ne aadhikaarit taur par frank kaa sthaan le liya.
