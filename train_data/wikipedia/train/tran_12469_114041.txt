 pepe mendhak ek antarjaal mim he. pepe ko aksar ek hare rang ke maanavarupi mendhak ke rup main chitrit kiya jaataa he.
 pracheen kaal se maanav sabhyataa main mendhak ko ishwar kii abhivyakti mana jaataa he. rugveda 7-103 main kaha gaya he - "gavaan manduka dadatah shatani sahasrasaave par tirant ayuh". pracheen misr main bhi mendhakarupi bhagwaan shri kek kii puja kii jaati thi. varttman kaal main shri mat furi ne 2005 main pepe ko rachakar mandu-archana kaa punarsthaapan kiya. 2008 main aarthik mandi ke samay pepe ne mispes, gaeyaa online or 4chaiyn jaise jaalasthalon par lokapriyata praapt kii. 2015 main tambler par yah vishwa ke sarvaadhik lokapriy meemon se ek ban gaya. isi varsh durlabh pepe chitron kii bikri or nilaamee i-bey or crecialist jaise jaalasthalon par hone lagi. pepe kii lokapriyata itane bad gayi ki newjiland sarkaar ke samaksh pepe vaale rashtriya dhvaj kaa prastaav rakhaa gaya.
 bhartiya angrezi samachar patr daily news and analysis ne pepe ko 2015 ke sabase mahaan meemon kii suchi main paanchave sthaan par shaamil kiya. trinidad kokila sushri niki minaj evam america-kokila sushri kaity peri jaisi prasiddh hastiyon ne apni bhaawanaaon ko prakat karne ke liye pepe kaa sahaara liya he. 2016 main sanyukt rajya america ke bhavi raashtrapati mahamahim shriman donald trump ne apane abhiyaan main pepe ko prayukt kiya. iske uttar main kutil hillari ne pepe ko gori shwet rashtrawaad kaa ek pratik qaraar de diya.
