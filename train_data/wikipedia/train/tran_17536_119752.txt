 pavuluri mallan bharat ke gyarahavin shatabdi ke ek ganitagya the. mahaviracharya dwaara sanskrit main rachit ganitsarasangrah kaa unhone 'saar sangrah ganitamu' naam se telegu kavita main anuwaad kiya. 
 pavuluri mallan rajraja narendra ke samakaleen the. unhone 'bhadraadri ram shatkamu' naamak ek anya granth kii bhi rachana kii. pavuluru gram aajkal prakasham jila ke parchuru mandal main he.
