 kilkil 1 . vishnupurana tathaa shrimadbhagwat puraana main kaliyugi raajaaon ke prasang main maunvanshi raajaaon ke ananthar ullikhit ek rajya or rajyavansh. vishnu puraana main inkaa naam 'cankil' diya gaya he . bhagwat main inaki rajdhani 'kilkila' kaa ullekh kiya gaya he jo inke namakaran kaa kaaran maani ja sakati he . bhagwat ke varnan se prathit hota he ki ye mulat: bharat ke baahar vahli ke raja the jinka aadhipaty bharat main bhi kisi yug main tha. bhaaudaji ke mat se ye ajanta gupha ke aasapaas bataaya jaataa he. yavan naam se inke ionianan greek hone kaa anumaan hota he. ye konkan main 980 isvi ke aasapaas shaasha rup main vartmaan the. andhra par rajya karanevaale is yavan vansh kaa utkarshkaal 576 i. se 900 i. tak mana jaataa he.
 2. bundelkhand se praapt anek shilalekhon main 'kilkila' naam kaa ullekh hua he or is pradesh main kilkila naam kii nadi bahati he. is kaaran kuch itihaaskaaron kaa mat he ki is pradesh kaa pracheen naam kilkila tha or abhilekhon main varnit kilkila nrip duusari teesri shatee i. main bundelkhand main rajya karte the. kuch log inhen nagvanshi anumaan karte hai tathaa unka sambandh bhaarshiv or vaakatak nareshon se jodate hai.
