 vaasit praant, jise arabi main muhaafazaat​ vaasit kehete hai, iraq kaa ek praant he. isaki rajdhani al-kut shahar he.
 arabi bhashaa main 'vaasit' kaa matlab 'bich' yaa 'madhya' hota he. vaasit dajala nadi main baghdad or basra ke mahatvapurn kshetron ke bich padataa he. yah kufa, basra or iran ke ahvaaz shahar ke bhi bich main padataa he.
 vaasit praant dakshini iraq main sthit he, jahaan shiya samudaay kaa jor he. is praant main bhi shiya muslim arab logon kii baari bahusankhya he.
