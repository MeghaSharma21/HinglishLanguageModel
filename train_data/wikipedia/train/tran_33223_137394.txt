 un sabhi anuon ko jaivanu kehete he jo kisi bhi jeev main paye jate hai. ye jatil carbonic anu hote hai. inkaa nirmaan sajivon ke sharir main hota he. ye sajivon ke sharir ke vikaas tathaa rakrakhaav ke liye aavashyak hote hai. carbohydrate, amino amla, protein, arene tathaa diene pramukh jaivanu hai. carbonic yogico kii tarah inkaa nirmaan carbon, hydrogen, nitrogen, oxigen se hota he, kabhi-kabhi phosphorus tathaa sulfur aadi tatv bhi alp maatra main ho sakate hai.
