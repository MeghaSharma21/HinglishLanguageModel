 bharat main rashtriya rajmargon kii suchi bhartiya rajmarg ke kshetra main ek vyapak suchi detaa he, dwaara anurakshit sadakon ke ek varg bhartiya rashtriya rajmarg praadhikaran. ye lambey mukhya main duuri roadways hai bharat or ke atyadhik upyog kaa matlab he ek parivahan bharat main. ve main ek mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaa bhartiya arthvyavastha. rashtriya rajmarg sankhya 2 laned, ke bare main 65,000 kimi kii ek kul, jinamein se 5,840 kimi badal saktaa he gathan main "swarna Chathuspatha" yaa svarnim chaturbhuj, ek pratishthit pariyojna rajg sarkaar dwaara shuru kii shri atal bihari vaajapeyi. jisamen se 4,885 kimi ausat rahe hai alag express rajmarg. main kuch or kshetron main yah 4 galiyon ko vistrut kar sakate hai viksit kiya he. bade shaharon ke kareeb, rajmargon bhi 8 len kaa ho saktaa he. bharat main rajmarg bharat main kul sadak network kaa lagbhag 2% he, lekin kul sadak yatayaat kaa lagbhag 40% kiya jaataa he. rashtriya vidheyak, 1995 rajmarg, nirmaan or bharat ke in dhamniyon ke rakrakhaav main nijee nivesh ke liye pradaan karata he. sabase lambey samay tak rashtriya rajmarg NH7 jo uttar ke varanasi se dakshin ke bhartiya mukhya bhoomi ke dakshini sabase bindu par kanyakumari, tamilnadu 2369 kimi kii duuri tay kii he or jabalpur, nagpur, hyderabad or bengalore jaise mahanagaron se gujarata he. kam se kam rashtriya rajmarg NH47A, jo ernakulam - cochin port ke liye ek 6 km khand he. bharat ko duniya kaa sabase ooncha drivable rajmarg main hone kaa gaurav praapt he manali ke leh ladakh, kashmir jodne ke liye.
