 bharat ke rajasthan rajya main jaipur nagar ke sameep sthit yah lavan jal kii jhil he. yah jhil samudr tal se 1,200 foot kii oonchaai par sthit he. jab yah bhari rahati he tab iskaa kshetrafal 90 varg mile rahata he. isamen teen nadiyaan aakar girti hai. is jhil se bade paimaane par namak kaa utpaadan kiya jaataa he. anumaan he ki aravali ke shisht or naaise ke garton main bharaa hua gaad hi namak kaa strot he. gaad main sthit vilayashil sodium yogic varsha ke jal main ghuskar nadiyon dwaara jhil main pahunchaata he or jal ke vaashpan ke pashchaat jhil main namak ke rup main rah jaataa he. 
 mahakavy mahabharat ke anusaar yah kshetra asur raaj vrishaparv ke samrajya kaa ek bhaag tha or yahan par asuron ke kulguru shukracharya nivas karte the. isi sthaan par shukracharya kii putree devayani kaa vivaah naresh yayati ke saath sampann hua tha. devayani ko samarpit ek mandir jhil ke paas sthit he.avedh borwell ke chalate tathaa paravaasi parindon ko surakshit rakhane ke liye naresh kadayaan dwaara janhit yachika supreme court main daayar ker di he. ek anya hindu manyata ke anusaar, shakambhari devi jo ki chouhan rajputon kii rakshak devi hai, ne yahaan sthit ek van ko bahumoolya dhaatuon ke ek maidan main parivartit kar diya tha. log is sampada ko lekar hone vaale sambhavit jhagadon ke lekar chintit ho gayi or ise ek vardaan ke sthaan par shraap samajhane lage. logon ne devi se apana vardaan waapas lene kii prarthana kii to devi ne sari chaandi ko namak main parivartit kar diya. yahan shakambhari devi ko samarpit ek mandir bhi upasthit he.
 sambar jhil puure bharat bhar main ek prasiddh sthaan he is jhil kii khaas baat ye hai ki yahan par namak utpaadan hota he yahan par meetha pani nahin he is jhil kii lambaai 11 meter skyr main he.
