  javed ahamed, 4 navambar 1974 ko uttar pradesh ke gorakhpur janapad me varisht patrkaar evam rashtra bhashaa ratna se sammanit qamar ahamed aaazaad ke vahaan janme. javed ahamed ne apane pita ke padchinhon par chalate hue sirf 12 saal kii umr main lekhan shuru kiya. or 16 varsh kii aayu main saptahik samachar patr "saransh samay" ke teen sanskaran ke sampadak rahe. phir swatantra rup bharat kii patr-patrikaon me likhte rahe or 1998 me mumbai ja kar film udyog se jud gayihindi film "vaisaa bhi hota he " kii patakatha likhi..is film kaa geet "allaah ke bande hans de" behad lokapriy he....lagbhag 200 vigyaapan filmein kiya or vibhinn tivi channelon se jude rahe.
