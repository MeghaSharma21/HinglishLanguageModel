 afraiya  yaduwanshi ahir jati kaa ek kul he. raajapootaana gajetir ke anusaar rewadi ke afriya ahir jati ke yadu vansh se hai. rewadi rajya par afriya kul ne hi shashan kiya he. rewadi ke raja raav nandaram isi gotra ke the.
 18veen shatabdi ke prarambh main, ahir senapati raav nandaram ne rewadi rajya kii sthapana kii thi. unhen rewadi ke aas paas 360 gaavon kii yah jaagir va choudhary kaa khitaab mughal samraat farrukhsiar se praapt huye the. us samay choudhary kaa khitaab sainya shakti kaa suchak hua karata tha. 
 afriya rajasthan rajya main alvar ke tijaara naamak sthaan se haryana ke rewadi kshetra main aakaar based the. raav tulaaraam singh va unke vanshajon kaa gotra afriya he.henry em iliyat ke vrutanton ke anusaar- sabhi praanton ke ahir apni mul utpatti mathura yaa uske pashchim ke kshetra se maanate hai. afriya ahir jati kaa ek mahatvapurn samudaay he.mughal shashan kaal main, afriya, koslia, va khosa rajshahi ahir vansh the jinka mogul darbar ke numaayindon se sidhaa sampark tha.raav nandaram rewadi ke yaduwanshi ahiron ke afriya parivaar main janme the, jinhone apni apaar sainya shakti va vistrut upjau jameen ke madhyam se apni prabhutaa siddh kii thi.
 dilli main afriya gotra ke 18 gaanv hai jinhe sureda satra khaanp bhi kaha jaataa he. kuch gaanv is prakaar hai- kheda dabar, jafarpur kalaan, khadkhadi nahar, khadkhadi jatmal, daulat poor, ghumanheda, kheraa, pandwala, jhulajhuli gaanv aadi.
