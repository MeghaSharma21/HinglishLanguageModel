 dugdh fever pashuon ko lagane vaala ek rog he jo aksar zyada doodh dene vaale pashuon ko byaane ke kuch ghante yaa dinon baad hota he. pashu ke sharir main calcium kii kami ke kaaran yah rog hota he. saamanytah ye rog gaayon main 5-10 varsh kii umr main adhik hota he. aamtaur par pehali byaant main ye rog nahin hota. 
 is rog ke lakshan byaane ke 1-3 din tak prakat hote he. pashu ko bechaini rahati he. mansapeshiyon main kamajori aa jane ke kaaran pashu chal phir nahi saktaa pichley pairon main akadan or aanshik lakva kii sthiti main pashu gir jaataa he. uske baad gardan ko ek taraf peeche kii ore mode kar baitha rahata he. sharir kaa taapmaana kam ho jaataa he.
