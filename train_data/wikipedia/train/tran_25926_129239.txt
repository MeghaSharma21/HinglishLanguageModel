 tulus france kaa ek pramukh nagar. france ke equyten basin main garon pradesh kaa yah prashaskeey nagar he jo paris se 709 kilometre dakshin-purv main sthit he. nagar ke utthan main isaki sthiti kaa vishesh haath rahaa he. nagar kaa vistaar ab nadi ke baayein tat par bhi ho gaya he, yadhyapi bayen tatavarti kshetra 13 meter nicha he or praaia: baadh peedit rahata he. baayein tat par nagar kaa ek chauthai bhaag hi he. nagar kii 1/5 jansankhya maatr 142 hectares par nivas karti he jo pracheen nagar kaa centre he. isi kshetra main pramukh vyapaarik or duusari sansthaaon ke karyaalay bhi hai.
 ullekhneey imaaraton main gauthik carch, roman girjaghar tathaa ajaayabaghar aadi mukhya hai. ajaayabaghar main madhyakaalin vaastukala evam shilpakala ke namoonay surakshit hai.
 nagar main vishvavidhyalay, chikitsa mahavidhyalay aadi shikshan sansthaayen tathaa anek audyogic santhaan hai. nagar samudr se dur he par railmargon or vayumargon dwaara paris, wordo, marsei, milan aadi mukhya mukhya nagaron se sambhddha he. audyogic utpaadanon main nitrogen urvarak, vayuyan, kaartoos, aadi pramukh hai. inke atirikt bune hue saamaan, juute, aataa, kaagaj aadi bhi nirmit hote hai. andey, doodh aadi mukhya niryaat vastuen hai.
