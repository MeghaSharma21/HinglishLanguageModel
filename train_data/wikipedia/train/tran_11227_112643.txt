 uttar pradesh main paryaton bharat bhar main suvikhyath he evam isaki pashchimi simaayen desh kii rajdhani nai dilli se lagi hui hai. uttar pradesh bhartiya evam videshi paryatako ke liye ek mahatvapurn sthaan he. is pradesh main kai aitihaasik evam dharmik sthal hai. uttar pradesh kii aabaadi bharat ke sabhi rajyon main sabase adhik he. bhugaulik rup se bhi uttar pradesh main vividhta dekhne ko milti he- uttar kii ore himalay parvat hai or dakshin main sindhu-ganga ke maidan hai. bharat kaa sabase lokapriy aitihaasik paryaton sthal taj mahal yahaan ke agra shahar main sthit he. varanasi, jo ki hinduon ke liye mahatvapurn dharmik sthal he jo isi pradesh main he.
 sukarakshetra soron janapad kasganj kaa ek pracheen tirthasthal he, joki satyugin he. yah bhagwaan varah kii mokshasthali he. bhagwaan varah ne yaheen devi prithvi ko pratham baar gita sunai thi. bhagwaan surya, chandra aadi devataaon va anek rishi, muniyon kii yah tapasthali rahi he. mahaprabhu vallabhacharya, chaitanya mahaprabhu, vitthalanath ji, gunsaai ji aadi kii yah sadhanasthali rahi he. ramcharitamanas ke rachanaakaar tulsidas, ashtchhaap ke jadiya kavi nandadaas va sadhvi ratnavali kaa janmayahan hua tha. chakratirtha, raghunatha ji mandir, rupatirth, somtirth, dwarikadhish mandir, nimeshwar mandir, parashuram mandir, tulsi smarak, ganpati aavahan akhaada, vaivaswatatirth, papamochanatirth, shakhotakatirth, adityatirth, griddhawat, chakreshwar mandir, shwetvarah mandir, shyamavarah mandir,shri vighn vinayak panchmukhi ganesh ji mandir, laddu vaale balaji mandir, someshwar mandir, roopeshwar mandir, bhooteshwar mandir, maanas mandir, bhairavnath mandir, suryakund, yogeshwar mandir, chandrakup, batukanath mandir, kachchapaprishthiya shriyantra, lehreshwar mandir, navadurga mandir, mahaprabhu vallabhacharya kii 23veen baithak, vitthalanathji va gunsaiji kii baithak, kapilmuni kii gupha, budhiganga aadi yahan ke pavitra darshneey sthal hai.
