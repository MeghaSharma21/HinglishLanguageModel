 aparigrah gaiyr-adhikaar kii bhaavana, gaiyr lobhi yaa gaiyr lobh kii avadharana he, jisamen adhikaraatmakataa se mukti pai jaati he.. yah vichaar mukhya rup se jain dharm tathaa hindu dharm ke raaj yoga kaa hissaa he. jain dharm ke anusaar "ahimsa or aparigrah jivan ke aadhaar hai". aparigrah kaa arth he koi bhi vastu sanchit naa karnaa.
 ahimsa ke baad, aparigrah jain dharm main doosraa sabase mahatvapurn gun he. aparigrah ek jain shravak ke paanch mul vraton main se ek he| jain muni ke 28 mul gunon main se yah ek he| yah jain vrath ek shravak kii ichchhaaon or sampatti ko simit karne ke siddhant he. jain dharm main sansaarik dhan sanchay ko laalach, irshyaa, swaarth or badhati vaasana ke ek sambhavit strot ke rup main mana jaataa he. jivit rahane ke liye paryaapt bhojan karnaa, dikhaave yaa ahankaar ke liye khaane se zyada mahaan mana jaataa he|
