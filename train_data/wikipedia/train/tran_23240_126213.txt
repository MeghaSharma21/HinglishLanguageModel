 DQDB distributed-kyuu duel bas ke liye ek sankshipt naam he. DQDB bahu abhigam network sanchaar ko dohari-bas vitarit qatari main ekikrut aayojan kaa upyog karne main sakasham he. DQDB sthaneey kshetra network yaa mahanagareey kshetra network ke liye pahunch pradaan karata he or ofline avasthaa conection unmukh data or aawaaj sanchaar ke rup main samakalik sanchaar data hastantaran kaa samarthan karata he.DQDB ek ucch shakti sancharan dar ke liye ek sanchaar protocol he gati jiskaa 34em.bi.pies se 155em.bi.pies ko jaataa he. DQDB IEEE 802.6 maanak he.
 DQDB' dohari bas network topology do chalati vipareet, ek dishaatmak ke hote hai kaa upyog karata he bas, jahaan:• sir-ant dwidishaatmak koshikaon utpann karata he. • host data sthalon ko dikhaata he or hastantaran karata he. • vitarit qatari "pehle-aao pehle-paao" vaali koshikaon prasaaran seva karte hai.
  
 DQDB main, networkke sabhi node networkke anya nodeske bare main jaanate hai. data sahi baske madhyam se failata he, jahaan ek bas bain ore nodes ke liye data bhejtaa he or duusari bas daayen nodes ke liye data bhejtaa he. vitarit qatari main, mejabaan counter, CD or RC rahete hai. prasaranke dermen data anurodh bit gaiyr vyast bas counter main guzar rahaa cell par set kiya jaataa he or mejabaan nirdhaarit karata he or aar si counter bad jaati he. isi tarah mejabaan ek khaali guzar rahaa cell ke liye RC counter or CD counter kam ho jaati he. jab donon RC or CD shunya ho jate hai, tab sandesh bheja ja saktaa he.
