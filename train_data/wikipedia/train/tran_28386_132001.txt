 sadhika randhaava jinhen keval sadhika ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he ek bhartiya abhinetri hai.
 sadhika ne apan kariyar 1997 main sawan kumaar taak kii film salama pe dil aa gaya main ayub khan ke abhinit film se kiya. sadhika haftaa vasuuli, kaabo, ab ke baras, pyaasa, 2 oktober kaash... aap hamaare hote, shikaar, bullate: ek dhamaakaa sahit 2005 tak vibhinn filmon main abhinya kiya. haal hi main, unhone ek film phir hai tauba se vaapasi kii koshish kii lekin yah film buree tarah se pit gayi.
 randhaava sikh army prishthbhumi se hai. unke pita unhen ek abhiyantaa bananaa chaahate the lekin unhen bollywood se prastaav aane lage jiske liye unke pita ne unhen sahaara diya. unki bahan jesi randhaava ek model hai.
