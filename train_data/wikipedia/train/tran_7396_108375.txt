 kumaunni bharat ke uttrakhand rajya ke antargat kumaun kshetra main boli jane vaali ek boli he. is boli ko hindi kii sahaayak pahari bhashaaon kii shrenee main rakhaa jaataa he. kumaunni bharat kii 325 manyata praapt bhashaaon main se ek he or 26,60,000 se adhik logon dwaara boli jaati he. uttrakhand ke nimnlikhit jilon - almoda, nainital, pithauragarh, bageshwar, champavat, udhamsingh nagar ke atirikt assam, bihar, dilli, madhya pradesh, maharashtra or punjab, tathaa himachal pradesh or nepal ke kuch kshetron main bhi boli jaati he.
 kumaunni boli ek arvachin boli he. lipibaddh na ho sakane ke kaaran aaj bhi jas kii tas aapasi vaartalap ke madhyam tak hi simit he. madhya peedi ke log kumaunni or hindi dono bhashaaon main samvaad karte hai. kumaunni, devanagari lipi main likhi jaati he. lipibaddh na ho sakane ke kaaran kumaunni bhashaa kaa koi sahitya uplabdh nahin he.
 kumaunni bhashaa yani boli, kumaanun kshetra main vibhinn rupantaranon main boli jaati he jaise:- 
 kumaanun kshetra main lagbhagag 20 prakaar kii boliyaan boli jaati hai jinamein se kuch is prakaar hai:- johari, maj kumaria, daanpuria, askoti, siraali, soryali, chugarakhyali, kamaiya, gangola, khasparjia, faldkoti, pachhaai, rauchabhaisi.
 kumaunni boli shanai-shanai lagbhag lupt hone ke kagaar par he. jiske kai kaaran he. palaayan, shaharikaran, lipibaddh na ho paanaa ityadi. kumaunni jaanane vaale lagbhag sabhi log hindi samaj sakate hai. hindi bhashaa ke is kshetra main badhate prabhaav ke kaaran yah bhashaa teji se lupt hone kii sthiti main pahunch chuki he. nagareey kshetron main bahut kam log yah bhashaa bolte hai or bahut se maamalon main yadi mata pita kumaunni yaa gadhwali jaanate bhi hai to unke bachche in bhashaaon ko nahin jaanate hai. bahut se anya maamalon main bachche kumaunni samaj to sakate hai lekin bol nahin sakate. bahut se kumaunni pariwaron main puraani do peedi ke log jab nai peedi ke logon se kumaunni main samvaad karte hai to unhen uttar hindi main miltaa he.
