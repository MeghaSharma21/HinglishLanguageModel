 adalwara kalam bhartiya rajya rajasthan ke savai madhopur jile kii chouth kaa barwada tehsil kaa ek gaanv he.
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar gaanv main 479 parivaar nivas karte hai jinaki jansankhya 1914 he isamen 979 purush or 935 mahilayen hai. yahan kii kul jansankhya kaa 24.67% logon kii sankhya 6 varsh se kam aayu he. adalwara kalam kii sakshartaa dar 56.13% he jo rajasthan kii ausat sakshartaa dar 66.11% se kam he. yahan purush sakshartaa dar 76.97% tathaa mahila sakshartaa dar 34.17 % he. bharat ke samvidhan ke panchayat raaj adhinium ke anusaar gaanv kaa mukhiya sarpanch hota he.
 
 adalwara ,savai madhopur gilen ,jila parishad ke tahat or tehsil va pachaanyat samiti chouth kaa barwada ke tahat aata he ,vidhansabha kshetra khandar ke adheen aata hai ,loksabhaa ke liye tonk-savai madhopur nirvachan kshetra ke tahat aata hai. adalwara main gram panchaayat hai.gram panchaayat ke chunaav bhi adalwara main niyamit hote rahe hai or log apane gharelu muddo ko lekar apane pratinidhi chunate rahe hai.
