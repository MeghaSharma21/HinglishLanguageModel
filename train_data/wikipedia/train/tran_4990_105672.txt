 pulind bhartiya upmahadweep ke madhya bhaag main sthit vindhyas parvaton ke kshetra main basane vaale ek pracheen qabeele or janajati kaa naam tha. san 269 isapurv se 231 isapurv tak samraat ashok dwaara shilaon par taraashe gaye adeshon main pulindon kaa, unki pulindnagar naamak rajdhani kaa or unke padosi qabeelon kaa zikr mila he. is se itihaaskaar yah andaaza lagate hai ki sambhawatah unki rajdhani bharat ke aadhunik madhya pradesh rajya ke jabalpur zile ke kshetra main rahi hogi. kuch vidwaanon kaa samajhnaa he ki vartmaan bundelkhand ilaake kaa naam "pulind" shabd kaa parivartit rup he, haalanki is tark par vivaad jari he.
 aitihaasik sroton main pulindon kaa vindhyas pradesh ke saath saafe sambandh dikhataa he, lekin unke kabeeley kii shakhaayen himalay kshetra or assam tak faili hui thim. himalay ke kshetra main unhen kiraat naamak janajati se sambandhit samajha jaataa tha.
