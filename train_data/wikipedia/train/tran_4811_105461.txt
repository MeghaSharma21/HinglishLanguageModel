 kriti kharbanda bhartiya film abhinetri he jo mukhya rup se kannada or telugu filmon main kaam karti he. ek model ke rup main karier kii shuruyaat ke baad unhone telugu film boni ke saath abhinay kii shuruyaat kii. iske baad vah kannada main sheersh abhinetri main se ek ban gai. unhone haal hi main tamil or hindi film udyog main apni shuruyaat kii he. film shaadi main zarur aana main rajkumaar raav ke saath kriti kharbanda najar ai. phir aage wo film 'guest in landan' main mukhya bhoomika main karthik aryan or unki patni ke kirdaar main dikhin.
 kriti kharbanda kaa janm nai dilli main aswani kharbanda or rajani kharbanda ke yahan hua tha. unki ek choti bahan ishita kharbanda or ek chote bhai jayavardhan kharbanda hai, jo paper plane productions ke sah-sansthaapak hai. 1990 ke prarambh main vah apane parivaar ke saath bengalooru chale gaye. baldwin girls hai skool main apane ucch vidhyalay ko puraa karne ke baad, vah shri bhagwaan mahavir jain college se snaatak hone se pehle bishap coton mahila chrishchiyan college main bhaag leti thi. vah gahanaa digining main ek diploma rakhti he.
 swayan ke anusaar, vah skool or college ke dauraan saanskrithik gatividhiyon main bahut sakriy thi. ek bachche ke rup main, vah kai vigyapanon main bhi pradarshit huin or usne skool / college main modelling jari rakhaa, jisamen kaha gaya ki vah "hameshaa tivi vigyapanon ko pasand karte the". unke college ke dinon ke dauraan unke pramukh modelling abhiyaan bhima jewelers, spire, or fere and lavali ke liye the. spire billboard par unki tasviir ne enarai nideshak raaj pippala kaa dhyaan aakarshith kiya jo apni film ke liye ek nayika kii talash main the or unhone anke abhinay kariyar ke liye maarg prashast kiya.
