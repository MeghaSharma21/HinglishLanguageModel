 malay dweepasmooh dakshinpurvi asia kii mukhyabhumi or australia ke bich main vistrut ek dweepasmooh he. hind mahaasaagar se lekar prashanth mahaasaagar tak faile hue is dweepasmooh main 25,000 dweep hai. kshetrafal ke aadhaar par yah duniya kaa sabase bada or dweep-sankhya ke aadhaar par vishwa kaa chauthaa sabase bada dweepasmooh he. bruneii, purvi malaysia, indonesia, purvi timore, singapore or philippino isamen aate hai. papua new gini isamen sammilit nahin kiya jaataa haalanki kuch paribhaashaon main nayaa gini dweep kaa pashchimi bhaag malay dweepasmooh main gina jaataa he.
