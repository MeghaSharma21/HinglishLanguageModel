 surinam ek rajtantr he. yahan raashtrapati sarkaar kaa adhyaksh hota he. bharat kii tarah yahan bhi bahudaliy raajnneeti prachlit he. raashtrapati kaa chunaav rashtriya dal do-tihaai bahumat se karti he. agar yah bahumat naa mili, to loksabhaa saadhaaran bahumat se raashtrapati kaa chunaav karti he. inkaa chunaav panchavarshiya hota he.
