 blue mountainz kaa ek drishy
 blue mountainz, arthath nile pahaad, carabian sagar main sthit jamaica dweep va desh kii sabase lambi parvatamala he. jamaica kaa sabase unchaa parvat, 2256 meter lambaa blue mountain parvat bhi isi parvatamala main shaamil he. yahan se dweep ke uttari va dakshini tat donon dikhte hai or saafe dinon main dur se cuba dweep kaa kinaaraa bhi nazar aata he. is parvatamala main vishwaprasiddh blue mountain coffee bhi ugti he, jo duniya kii sabase mahangi coffee maani jaati he.
