 bhalaai yaa kalyaan ek vyakti yaa samooh kii avasthaa ke liye ek saamaanya shabd hai. udaharnarth, unki saamaajik, aarthik, manovaigyanik, aadhyatmik yaa chikitsa avasthaa; koi ucch stareey bhalaai kaa matlab, kuch samaj main, vyakti yaa samooh kii sakaaratmak avasthaa se hota hai, jabaki kam bhalaai kaa arth nakaaratmak ghatnaon ke saath judaa hua hai.
