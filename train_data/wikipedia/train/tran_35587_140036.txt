 suhaaga-manakaa pareekshan anekon prakaar ke manakaa-pareekshanon main se ek he. yah pareekshan gunatmak acarbonic vishleshan kaa paramparaagat ang he jiske dwaara diye gayi namoonay main kuch dhaatuon kii upasthiti kaa anumaan hota he. san 1812 main ise berjilius ne sujhaayaa tha. suhaaga pareekshan ke baad sabase mahatvapurn manakaa-pareekshan microcosmik salt pareekshan he.
 jwaala pareekshan main upyog kiye jane vaale platinum yaa nicrom ke taar ke sire par ek chota sa gola bana liya jaataa he. is loop ko bunsan burner par tab tak garam karte hai jab tak ki laal na ho jaay. iske baad is loop ko suhaage ke churna main dubaa diya jaataa he. is par suhaage ke jo kan chipak jate hai unako jwaala ke sabase garam bhaag main garm karte hai. garam karne par suhaaga main maujuud crystalan jal vashpikrut hokar nikal jaataa he or uske nikalne par suhaaga 'phool' jaataa he or phir sikudakar ek rangahin paardarshee kaanch-jaisaa 'manakaa' banaata he. yah vastav main sodium metaborate tathaa boric enhydride kaa mishran he.
 ab is maanak ko nam kiya jaataa he . nam maanak ko vishleshan ke liye diye gayi namoonay main dubaaya jaataa he kintu dhyaan rakhte hai ki namoonay kii bahut kam maatra isse chipke.. ab manakaa or usase chipke namoonay ko jwaala ke nichle bhaag main garam karte hai. iske baad isko thanda hone dete hai or iske rang ko dhyanpurvak dekhte hai.
 tanba, loha, chromium, mangneej, cobalt or nikal ke lavenon ke apane vishisht rang ke manakaa dekhne ko milte hai. pareekshan hone ke baad manakaa ko iske galan taap tak garam karke pani ke baratan main dubaa diya jaataa he jisase manakaa samaapt ho jaataa he.
