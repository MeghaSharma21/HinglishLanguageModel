 tola bhartiya upmahadweep kaa ek parmparik vazan kaa maap he, jo aaj bhi zevar tolane ke liye johriyon dwaara prayog kiya jaataa he. aadhunik vazan ke hisaab se ek tola 11.6638038 gram ke baraabar he.
 parmparik bhartiya vazan is prakaar hai -
 yapurane bhartiya naap-taul :-8 khaskhas = 1 chaaval,
 8 chaaval = 1 ratti,
 8 ratti = 1 maasha,
 4 maasha =1 tank, 12 maasha = 1 tola,
 5 tola = 1 chhataank,
 4 chhataank = 20 tola yaa 1 pav,
 8 chhataank yaa 40 tola = 1 adsera,
 16 chhataank yaa 80 tola = 1 ser,
 5 ser = 1 paseri,
 8 paseri = 40 ser yaa 1 man,
 1 keji = 86 tola yaa 1 ser 6/5 chhataank,
 100 keji = 1 quintal yaa 2 man 27 5/2 ser.
