 doorshiksha parishad  nai dilli, bharat main sthit sanghathan he, jo mukt vishvavidhyalayon evam doorshiksha pranali ke samanvay evam vikaas ke liye tathaa unke maanakon kii sthapana ke liye uttaradaayi he. is parishad kii sthapana indira gandhi rashtriya mukt vishvavidhyalay adhinium ke antargat kii gai thi. iske vartmaan adhyaksh provi en rajasekharan pillai hai.
