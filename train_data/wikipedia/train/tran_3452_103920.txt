 dow padma bandopadhyaay bharat kii pehali mahila air vaais marshall hai. vah air comador ke pad par padonnat hone ke liye bhartiya vaayu sena kii pehali mahila adhikari or bharat ke airospes medical sosayadi kii pehali hai. vah uttari dhruv main vaigyanik anusandhan karne vaali pehali bhartiya mahila hai. unki ek or uplabdhi yah he ki vah bhi raksha seva staff college course puraa karne vaali pehali mahila adhikari hai. 1973 main unhen vishisht seva padak praapt hua or 1991 main unhen e epa ech e puraskaar praapt hua.
 dow padma bandopadhyaay ke anusaar "sena kii vardi un yuvatiyon ke liye nahin he jo mahaj romaanch ke liye is seva main aana chaahati hai. agar koi ladki maansik taur par taiyaar nahin hogi to yah savirs uske liye everest ko fatah karne se kahin zyada mushkil sabit ho sakati he. sena main kai mushkil halaat kaa saamana karnaa pad saktaa he. inase unhen ek aam sainik kii tarah nibatna hota he or mahila hone ke naate ve isse batch nahin saktin. vaise, kisi mahila adhikari ke aatmahatya karne se yah nahin mana janaa chahie ki sena mahilaon ko rakhane main sakasham nahin he. is sarvice main aanevaali kathinaaiyon ko chalange ke rup main bhi liya ja saktaa he or sajaa ke taur par bhi. yah sab mindset par nirbhar karata he."
