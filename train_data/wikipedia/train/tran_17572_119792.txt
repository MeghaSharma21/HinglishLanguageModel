 ketu vishwanath reddy telugu ke prasiddh sahityakar hai. andhra pradesh ke yerangutal main sthit rangaipuram main 10 july 1939 kaa janmen is lekhak kii 36 pustaken prakashit hui hai jinamein ketu vishwanath reddy kathalu naamak kahani-sangrah evam varalu bodhi naamak upanyaas prasiddh hai. inhone lagbhag 14 pustakon kaa sampadan evam 3 pustakon kaa angreji se telugu main anuwaad kiya he. inaki kahaniyaan vibhinn bhashaaon main anudit hui hai. 1991 main likhi gai inaki prasiddh kahani khadgalu-kaatuk pittalu 2007 main vijayraghav reddy dwaara hindi main anudit hui.inako kendriya sahitya academy puraskaar, shri pulupulla venkateshivaiyaa sahitya puraskaar, andhrapradesh sahitya academy puraskaar, venkataramaiyya swarnapadak evam bhartiya bhashaa parishad kaa rutambhraa puraskaar se sammanit kiya ja chukaa he. inhone thailand, singapore, britain kii shaikshanik yatraayen bhi kii he.
