 vaishvik nabhikeey urja saajhedaari centre bharat sarkaar ke parmaanu urja vibhaag, ke tatwadhan main chhathi anusandhan evam vikaas ikai he. yah centre haryana main bahadurgard main jasur khedi ke paas nirmaanadhin he. 
 yah centre rashtriya rajmarg sankhya 10 par rashtriya rajdhani -'nai dilli' hawaii adde se lagbhag 45 kimi kii duuri par he. nikatatam shahar bahadurgard he. centre ke liye bhoomi kaa adhigrahan kiya ja chukaa he or santhaan kaa master plan taiyaar kar liya gaya he. centre kaa ek parvahan karyaalay bahadurgard main sthapit kiya gaya he. centre ke pratham charan kaa nirmaan kaary jald hi shuru hone kii sambhaavna he.
 ji.si.en.i.pi. kaa lakshya parmaanu prasar ke khatre ko kam karte hue, ek surakshit or svachh tareeke se, parmaanu urja ke vistaar ko aage badhanaa he. yah centre prasar pratirodhi parmaanu pranaaliyon ke dizin, parmaanu suraksha, vikiran suraksha, parmaanu saamagree lakshan adhyayan or vikiran praudyogiki ke anuprayogon ke kshetra main aadhunik shiksha, prashikshan or anusandhan evam vikaas se sambandhit suvidhaayen pradaan karegaa. ji.si.en.i.pi. kii sthapana bhartiya or antarrashtriya vaigyanikon / antarrashtriya parmaanu urja agency ke visheshgyon or samayik muddon par icchhuk deshon dwaara shodh, prashikshan or antarrashtriya sangoshtee ke madhyam se parmaanu urja ke kshetra main sahyog hetu bharat kii ek pahal he. is centre main parmaanu pranaaliyon or suvidhaon main agrim shodh, adhyayan or prashikshan hetu paanch vidhyalay honge.
