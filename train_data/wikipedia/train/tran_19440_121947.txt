 yaccin yaa vorashigum paak-adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-balatistan kshetra ke gizar zile kii ek ghati he. prashasnic drushti se yah gizar zile kii ek tehsil bhi he. yaccin vaadi hindu kush parvaton main sthit he or yahan se purv main ishkoman vaadi pahunchne ke liye asambar darre naamak ek parvatiy darre se guzaranaa padataa he.
 yaccin ke adhiktar log burushsaki bolte hai, haalanki khovaar bolne waalon kaa alpsankhyak samudaay bhi yahan rahata he. sthaneey nivasee dharmik drushti se zyaadaatar shiya islam kii ismaili shakha ke anuyaayi hai.
 ishkoman jane vaale asambar darre ke alaava bhi kai darre yaccin ko anya sthanon se jodate hai. darkot darra yaccin se barogil darre kaa rasta detaa he jahaan se guzarakar aage kaibar-pakhtuunakva praant ke chitral kshetra or afghanistan ke vaakhaan galiyare ke maarg nikalate hai. thoi darra yaccin se chitral zile kii yarkun nadi ghati main sthit gazin basti kaa rasta pradaan karata he. itihaas main darkot darra aamu dariyaa kshetra se sindhu nadi kshetra tak aane-jane kaa sabase chota maarg hua karata tha. 19veen sadi main jab bhartiya upmahadweep par british raaj tha or madhya asia main roosi samrajya kaa jor tha to bharat ke angrez hukumraanon ko yah dar tha ki roosi sena kabhi yaccin kaa maarg lekar bharat par qabza karne kii koshish kar sakati he. isliye unhonen afghanistan ko vaakhaan kshetra par apana adhikaar jamaa lene ke liye uksaaya taaki roosi kshetra or bhartiya kshetra main sidhaa sampark na ho.
