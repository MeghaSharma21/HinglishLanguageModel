 vykom satyagrah, asprishyata kii kupratha ke viruddh travnakor main chalaaya gaya tha. iskaa uddeshy nimna jaateey esavaaon evam achhuton dwaara gandhi ji ke ahimsawadi tareeke se travnakor ke ek mandir ke nikat kii sadakon ke upyog ke bare main apane-apane adhikaron ko manvaanaa tha. sarvapratham hindu mandiron main pravesh tathaa saarvajanik sadakon par harijanon ke chalane ko lekar traavncor ke gram ‘vykom’ main andolan shuru hua.
 kerala main chhooaachhut kii jaden kaafii gahari jameen hui thim. yahan savarnon se avarnon ko 16 se 32 feet kii duuri banaaye rakhni hoti thi. avarnon main main ‘ethwa’ or ‘pulaiya’ achhut jatiyaan shaamil thim. 19veen sadi ke ant tak kerala main narayan guru, en. kumaaran, tii. ke. madhavan jaise buddhijiviyon ne chuaachhut ke viruddh aawaaj uthaai. 
 is aandolan kaa netritv esavaaon ke congressi netaa tii. ke. madhavan, ke. kelppan tathaa ke. pi. keshavamanon ne kiya. gram main sthit ek mandir main 30 march, 1924 ko kerala congressiyon ke ek dal ne, jisamen savarna or avarna donon sammilit the, mandir main pravesh kiya. mandir main pravesh kaa samachar failte hi savarnon ke sanghathan ‘nair sarvice socity’, ‘nair samajam’ va ‘kerala hindu sabha’ ne andolan kaa samarthan kiya. namboodariyon ke sanghathan ‘yogakshem’ ne bhi is andolan ko samarthan pradaan kiya. 30 march, 1924 ko ke.pi. keshavamanon ke netritv main satyagraiyon ne mandir ke pujaariyon tathaa traavncor kii sarkaar dwaara mandir main pravesh ko rokane ke liye lagaai gai baad ko paar kar mandir kii ore kooch kiya. sabhi satyagraiyon ko giraftaar kiya gaya. is satyagrah ke samarthan main puure desh se swayamsevak vykom pahunchne lage.
 march 1925 main gandhi ji kii madhyasthata main travnakor kii maharani se mandir main pravesh ke bare main andolankariyon se samjhauta hua.
