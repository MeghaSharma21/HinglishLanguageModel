 bolangir yaa balangir bhartiya rajya oreisha kaa ek jila he. balangir oreisha ke pashchimi bhaag ke pramukh vyapaarik nagaron main se ek he. yah shahar maharaajaaon ke kaal main sundar reeti se basaaya gaya tha. balangir sambalpur se 141 kilometre dur he or yahan abhi bhi puraane fishan ke sundar or shantipurna sthal vidyaman hai. balangir anek saanskrithik kaaryakramon ke aayojan kaa sthal rahaa he. soundaryapurna or aakarshak tatwon main ratna ke samaan jaadu jaise drishy ke liye gandhamardan ke pahari jharne or mahanadi ke parvatiy sthalon guzarane ke sthal pramukh hai. yahan varsh ke adhikansh samay main garm or shushk jalwaayu rahati he. yahan sardiyaan bhi kathin kintu sukhad hotin hai. isliye yahan sardiyon main aana acha hota he.
 harishankar- yah balangir se lagbhag 81 kimi kii duuri par sthit he. yah sthaan harishankar mandir ke liye prasiddh he jo gandhamardan parvat kii dakshini dhaal par avasthit he. gandhamardan parvat kaa yah sthaan aushadhiy paudhon ke liye prasiddh he.
 sonpur- sonpur kaa naam jaisaa pratibimbit karata he, is anupam shahar ke vibhinn bhaagon main puraane sone ke sikke paae jate the. yah mahanadi or tel nadi ke sangam ke madhya sthit basaaya gaya ek pracheen nagar he. yah balangir se 48 kilometre dur he or chaaron ore mandiron se ghira he.
 ranipur jharial- ye judwaan shahar hai jo balangir se 104 kilometre dur sthit hai. yah balangir jile ke titalgarh sab-divijan main hare-bhare paryaavaran ke madhya sthit he jo pracheen dharoharon se paripurna he. yah ek praathmik sakshya he ki yahan tirthaton karanevaalon ke dwaara kuch puraane mandiron kii khoj kii gai thi jis sthaan ko 'somtirth' kaha jaataa he. prasiddh 64 yogini mandir bhi yahan sthit he.
 patnagarh- patnagarh, patna rajya kii pracheen rajdhani tha, jahaan kuch anupam smarak hai. yah balangir se 38 kilometre dur sthit he.
