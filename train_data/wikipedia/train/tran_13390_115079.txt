 doorsanchaar ke kshetra me, ek sanchaar protocol yaa sanchaar navachaar aankada nirupan, sanket pranali, pramanikaran or truti sansuchan ke liye ek maanak niyamon kaa set he jinka prayog ek sanchaar channel par jaankaree bhejne ke liye aavashyak hota he. sanganak vigyaan me sanchaar protocol yaa protocol computaron ke bich soochna vinimay karne ke liye niyamon kaa ek set he. aasaan shabdon me yah vah bhashaa he jiske madhyam se do sanganak aapas me sampark sthapit karte hai.
