 main khilaadi tu anaadi 1994 kii hindi bhashaa kii ection kamedi film he. akshay kumaar, saf ali khan, rageshwari or shilpa shetty abhinit yah film saal kii sheersh 5 sabase zyada kamai karne vaali filmon main se ek ban gai or ise box offis par safalataa ghoshit kar diya gaya. iskaa sangeet bhi lokapriy hua tha or aaj bhi iske geet sune jate hai. 
 sabase sammanit inspectoron main se ek arjuna jogalkar drug dealer or gangster, goli dwaara maar diya jaataa he. ek cabre gayak or goli kii rakhel mona, goli ke khilaaf gavaahi dene ke liye taiyaar hoti he or arjuna kaa chota bhai inspector karan dwaara use police suraksha main liya jaataa he. uskaa lakshya kisi bhi anyaay se ladanaa or apane bhai kii hatya kaa badla lenaa he. jab goli ko mona ke aagaamee gavaahi ke bare main pataa lagata he jo uski asli pahchaan kaa pardaafash karegi. to vah use dhundta he or maar detaa he.
 deepak kumaar, jo ki ek romantic abhinayta hai, apni bhoomikaaon se ub chukaa he or romantic nayak ke rup main tip-cast hone ke kaaran niraash he. vah kuch alag karnaa chahta he jo uske sust or ubaau astitv main kuch badlaav or uttejnaa laae. apane neeras jivan ko badalane ke liye, vah nashe main dub padataa he or drive karata he. use police giraftaar kar leti he. yahi vah jagah he jahaan vah karan se miltaa he or uski dridhtaa, saahas or eemaandaaree se bahut prabhavit hota he. deepak uske vyavhaar kaa adhyayan karnaa chahta he taaki vah ise apni agali film ke liye prishthbhumi ke rup main istemaal kar sake.
 sabhi anu malik dwaara sangitabaddh.
