 sahityalahari 118 padon kii ek laghu rachana he. iske antim pad main surdaas kaa vanshavriksh diya he, jiske anusaar surdaas kaa naam 'surajdas' he or ve chandabaradaayi ke vanshaj siddh hote hai. ab ise prakshipt ansh mana gaya he ore shesh rachana purna pramanik maani gai he. 
 isamen ras, alankaar or nayika-bhed kaa pratipaadan kiya gaya he. is kriti kaa rachana-kaal swayan kavi ne de diya he jisase yah sanwat 1607 vikrami main rachit siddh hoti he. ras kii drushti se yah granth vishuddh shringaar kii koti main aata he.
