 china eastern airlines karporation limited china kii airline he, jiskaa mukhyalay china eastern airlines building of shanghai hongakio antarrashtriya hawaii adda changning jila, shanghai, main sthit he. yah china kii ek mahatvapurn he jo antarrashtriya ,gharelu evam pradeshik root par chalati he. yatri parivahan ke aadhaar par china eastern airlines, china kii duusari sabase badi airlines he. china eastern evam isaki anushanbee shanghai airlines 21 june 2011 ko ski team kaa 14va sadsy bane.
 2014 main china eastern airlines ne gharelu evam intarvashti udaanon ke dwaara 83.08 million yatriyon kaa parivahan kiya.
 china eastern airlines, kii sthapana 25 june 2008 main sieesi huadang prashasan ke dwaara kiya gaya tha. 1997 main china eastern airlines ne ghate main chalane vaali china general aviation kaa adhigrahan kiya. yah desh kii pehali airline company ban gayi jisane intarvashti bazaar main apane share utaare. 
 china kii sarkaar kaa china eastern airlines main ke mahatvapurn hissedari 61.64% he jabaki anya pramukh share dharak H shares A shares he. june 2006 main media main yah khabaren ai kii iske 20% shares videshi niveshkon ko bechaa jaayegaa. 
 state concil of china ke anumodan ke baad 2 sitambar 2007 main yah ghoshnaa kii gayi kii singapore air lines evam tisek holding sanyukt rup se china eastern airlines kaa ke shares kaa adhigrahan karenge. 2007 main niveshkon ne china eastern airlines main 24% hissedari kharidne kaa antim samjhauta kiya . 
 11 june 2009 ko yah ghoshnaa kii gayi kii china eastern airlines shanghai airlines ke saath vilay karegi. china eastern evam shanghai airlines ke bich vilay is ummid se kii gayi thi kii yah vilay shanghai sthit karriers ke bich atyadhik pratidwandita ko kam karegaa evam shanghai kii chhavi ko antarrashtriya vimaanan kaa centre banane main madad karegaa. farbari 2014 main yah vilay purna hua evam china eastern airlines, shanghai airlines kii purna swaamitv vaali company ban gayi. yadpi shanghai airlines ke brands evam poshak ko yathaavat rahane diya.
 2012, main china eastern airlines ko “golden ting award ” se capital market anual conference 2012, main sammanit kiya gaya evam isaki pahchaan 50 sabase keemtee cheneze brands main kii gayi. sitambar 9, 2014 china eastern ne nayaa logo evam nayaa paridhaan prastut kiya. 
 china eastern airlines kaa asia, uttar america evam australia ke ruts par mahatvapurn upasthiti he. yah udaanon kii sankhya main vruddhi karke gharelu bazaar kaa bhi phayda uthaanaa chalati he. 2007 main isane neuyork ke liye udaan prambh kiya, yah vimaan seva is airlines kii sabase lambi non-stop seva ban gayi.
 china eastern airlines pehali chinij vimaan company thi jisane airbas kharidne kaa order kiya. A320 siries is airlines kaa aadharastambh he ,evam iskaa mukhya upyog gharelu udaanon ke liye kiya jaataa he.
