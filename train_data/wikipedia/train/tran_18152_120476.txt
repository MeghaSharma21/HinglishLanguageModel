 pradarshan moolyankan ke bahut se tareeke avishkaarit he. prayek tarika kisi na kisi sanstha ke koi na koi uddeshy ke liye prabhavi he. yah tareeke koi bhi sanstha yaa karmchaaree ke zarurat ke aadhaar par khaarij yaa sweekaar kiye jane chahie. pradarshan moolyankan ke tareeko ko do shrenio me baanta gaya he-
 
 
 iske laabh he- prabandhaneey pad ke liye upyukt. iske nuksaan he- sabhi naukariyo ke liye upyukt nahin he, yogyata vetan kaa bhugtan lambii avadhi lakshyon ke bajaay alpakaalith lakshyon ke aadhaar par hota he.
