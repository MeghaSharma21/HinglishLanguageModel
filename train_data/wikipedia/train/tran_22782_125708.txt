 jimi carter ) ek america rajaneta hai jo 1976 se 1980 tak sanyukt rajya america ke 39wein raashtrapati rahe. raashtrapati banane se pehle ve sanyukt rajya america kii nausena main karyrat rahe, georgia main senetter rahe or georgia ke governor bhi rahe. raashtrapati kaarykaal ke baad ve maanav adhikaar sansthaaon evam paropkaaree sansthaaon ke saath jude rahe. unhen 2002 main nobel shaanti puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
 jimi carter kaa janm georgia ke plance naamak shahar main vaais clinics aspataal main hua tha jahaan unki maan lilian carter nurse thim. unke pita arl carter kaa khet tha jisamen ve kapaas evam moongfalee ugaaya karte the. 1926 main jimi kii choti bahan gloria, 1929 main routh or 1937 main chote bhai billy kaa janm hua. 1941 main jimi ne plance hai skool se dasvin kaksha paas kii. 1941 main jimi ne americas shahar main sthit georgia southwestern college main padhai shuru kii. 1942 main unhone yah college chhod georgia institute of takanology main daakhila liya. 1943 main unhen anapolis, marilland sthit yues neval academi main daakhila mil gaya or ve 1946 main vahaan se uteern hue. uteern hone ke pashchaat ve sanyukt rajya america kii nausena kii pehali pareekshanatmak pandubbi main karyrat hue.
 july 7, 1947 ko carter ne bahan routh kii saheli elanor rojalin smith se vivaah kar liya. 1947 main carter ke putr jon wiliam kaa janm hua, 1950 main james arl III kaa, 1952 main donal jeffri kaa or 1967 main putree emi lin kaa janm hua.
 1953 main jimmy ke pita arl kii mrityu par jimi apni patni ke saath georgia vaapis laut gaye, pita ke kheti ke kaarobaar ko sanbhaalane ke liye.
 1962 main carter ko georgia senet main chunaa gaya. 1966 main unhone georgia ke governor pad ke liye chunaav ladaa parantu jeet nahin paae. 1971 main ve phir georgia ke governor pad ke chunaav main khadey hue or jite.
 1977 main ve sanyukt rajya america ke 39ve raashtrapati bane. raashtrapati ke taur par inke kaarykaal main nimna mukhya ghatnaayen huin:
 ve 1980 main raashtrapati chunaav main punah khadey hue parantu haar gaye.
 raashtrapati kaarykaal samaapt hone ke baad carter ne maanav adhikaar sambandhit anek sansthaaon, evam anek paropkaaree sansthaaon ke saath kaam kiya he. 1982 main carter ne atlanta, georgia sthit emry vishvavidhyalay main carter presidential centre kii sthapana kii jo loktantr or maanav adhikaar sambandhit kaary karata he.
 carter ne raashtrapati kaarykaal ke pashchaat anek pustaken bhi likhi hai.
 carter ko 2002 main nobel shaanti puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
