 lasabela yaa las bela pakistan ke balochistan praant kaa ek tatavarti zila he. ise sindhi lahaze main las belo kehete hai. yah 30 june 1954 ko us samay ke kalaat vibhaag ke andar ek alag zile ke rup main gathit kiya gaya tha. is zile main 9 tahseelein or 21 sangheey councilâ€‹ aati hai.
 lasabela main baloch logon or sindhi logon kaa ek mishran rahata he. yahan par sindhiyon kaa jamot samudaay baari sankhya main he. jamot ek rajput samudaay he jo swayan ko sammaan rajvansh kaa vanshaj samajhataa he. lagbhag sabhi log muslim hai haalanki ek chota hindu samudaay bhi maujuud he. yahan ke sindhi ek 'lasi' naamak sindhi upbhasha bolte hai jo maanak sindhi bhashaa se thodi alag he.
 madhya purv or bhartiya upmahadweep ke bich yadi makraan kaa tateey rasta apanaaya jaae to lasabela usapar padataa he. sikandar mahaan jab pashchimottar bharat par qabza karne ke baad babilon laut rahaa tha to lasabela se guzara tha. 711 isvi main umayyad kilaafat kaa sipahasaalar mohammad bin qaasim bhi sindh par hamalaa karne ke liye lasabela se niklaa tha. bharat kii azaadi or vibhaajan se pehle las bela british raaj ke prashasan main ek alag riyasat hua karti thi jiskaa shaasha jamot samudaay kaa tha or 'jam' kii oopaadhi rakhata tha. swatantraaa ke baad 1954-1955 main iskaa pakistan main vilay kar diya gaya. aaj bhi lasabela se chand kilometre dur lagbhag sau qabron kaa ek samadhikshetra he jahaan lasabela ke puraane shahi pariwaron ke sadsy dafn hai.
