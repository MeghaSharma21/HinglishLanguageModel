 sarkaar dwaara sanchit nidhi se rakam nikasi ko manjuuri dilaane ke liye sansad main prastut vidheyak viniyog vidheyak kehalaata he.iskaa praavdhaan samvidhan ke anuchhed 114 main he. samvidhan ke anuchhed 266 main bharat kii sanchit nidhi gathit karne kaa praavdhaan he. bharat sarkaar kii sampurna aay sanchit nidhi main jamaa hota he or sampurna vyay bhi sanchit nidhi se hi hota he. sansad kii anumati se hi sanchit nidhi se dhan liya ja saktaa he.
