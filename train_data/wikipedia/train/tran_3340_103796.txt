 baari jal, hydrogen ke samasthanik duterium kaa oxide hai. isame 0.014 % saadhaaran jal hota hai. rasayan kii bhashaa main hydrogen oxide he. is ke ek anu main oxigen kaa ek parmaanu hydrogen ke do parmaanuon se sah sanyoji bandh se judaa rahata he. hydrogen ke teen samasthanik paye jate hai, anya do duterium or tritium kehalaate hai, jinka parmaanu bhaar kramshah 2 or 3 hota he. saamanyat: prakritik jal main jal ke aise anuon kii sankhya chaar karod das lakh or ek ke anupaat main hoti he jisame hydrogen kaa doosraa samasthanik paaya jaataa he. is prakaar ke jal ke anu ko D2O se nirupit kiya jaataa he. aisa jal jisame 99 pratishat se adhik anu D2O ke hote hai usko baari jal ke naam se janaa jaataa he, iskaa ghanatv saamaanya jal se adhik hota he.
 baari jal kaa vyavsaayik utpaadan mukhyatah rasinic vidhi se kiya jaataa he jisame gatij samasthanik prabhaav takanic kaa prayog hota he. baari jal kaa mukhya upyog nabhikeey sanyantron main hone vaali nabhikeey vighatan kriyaaon ke dauraan utpann neutron ko avashoshit karne ke liye mandak ke rup main hota he. jisase kii nabhikeey urja kaa niyantrit utpaadan or shantipurna upyog kiya ja sake. yahan baari jal ke sthaan par saadhaaran jal kaa bhi prayog kiya ja saktaa he lekin us paridrishy main sanyantra main uranium 235 kaa hi prayog kiya ja saktaa he, kyonki saadhaaran jal baari jal kii apeksha adhik neutron avashoshit kar letaa he.
