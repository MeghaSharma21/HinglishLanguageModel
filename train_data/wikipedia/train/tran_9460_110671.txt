 yoga siddhant ke anusaar prayek manushya ke merudand ke neeche ek urja sangrahit hoti he jiske jaagrath hone par swayangyaan kii prapti hoti he. iskaa zikr upanishdon or shakt vichaardhaara ke andar kai baar aayaa he. urja kaa yah rup ek saanp ke saadhe teen kundali markar baithe hue rup se milti he.
 iske anusaar dhyaan or asana karne se, yah urja meru ke neeche se hokar mastishk tak 7 chakron se hokar guzarati he.
