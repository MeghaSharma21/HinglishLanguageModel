 michigan vishaal jhilein ke ilaake par sanyukt rajya ke madhya pashchim kshetra main sthit rajya he. isaki rajdhani launsing he, or iskaa sabase bada shahar datroit he. michigan ekmaatr rajya he jisamen do prayadweep shaamil hai. michigan main duniya ke kisi bhi raajnneetik upkhand ke mukaable sabase bada mithe pani kaa kinaaraa he.
 17veen shatabdi main francisi khojakartaaon dwaara upniveshit hone se pehle michigan main kai mul america janajatiyaan nivas karti thi. 1762 main saptavarshiya yuddh main france kii haar ke baad yah kshetra british shashan ke adheen aayaa. jisane america krantikari yuddh main haar ke baad yah kshetra nae swatantra hue sanyukt rajya ko saunp diya gaya. ise 26wein rajya ke rup main 26 janvari 1837 ko sangh main bhartee kiya gaya.
 haalanki michigan ek vividh arthvyavastha viksit kiye hue hai, lekin ise vyapak rup se america motor vaahan udyog ke centre ke rup main janaa jaataa he. yah desh kii teen pramukh automobile companiyon kaa ghar he . 2016 ke anumaan ke mutabik rajya kii jansankhya 99,28,300 he. jisase iskaa rajyon main 10waan sthaan hua. 91% janata angrezi bolti hai jo kanoonee taur par sarkari bhashaa nahin he .
