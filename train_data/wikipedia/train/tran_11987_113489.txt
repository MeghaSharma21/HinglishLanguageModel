 moturi satyanarayan dakshin bharat main hindi prachaar aandolan ke sanghathak, hindi ke prachaar-prasar-vikaas ke yug-purush, mahatma gandhi se prabhavit evam gandhi-darshan evam jivan mulyon ke pratik, hindi ko rajbhasha ghoshit karaane tathaa hindi ke rajbhasha ke swaroop kaa nirdhaaran karaane vaale sadasyon main dakshin bharat ke sarvaadhik mahatvapurn vyaktiyon main se ek the. ve dakshin bharat hindi prachaar sabha, rashtrabhasha prachaar samiti, wardha tathaa kendriya hindi santhaan ke nirmaataa bhi hai.
 shri moturi satyanarayan kaa janm andhra pradesh ke krishna jile ke donpadu gram main hua tha.
 dakshin bharat hindi prachaar sabha ke prachaar sanghathak, andhra-prantiya shakha ke prabharee, madras kii kendra sabha ke pariksha mantri, prachaarmantri, pradhanmantri, rashtrabhasha prachaar samiti, wardha ke pratham mantri, bhartiya samvidhan sabha ke sadsy, rajyasabha ke manonith sadsy, kendriya hindi santhaan ke sanchaalan ke liye san 1961 main bharat sarkaar ke shiksha evam samaaj kalyaan mantralay dwaara sthapit ‘kendriya hindi shikshan mandal' ke pratham adhyaksh, rajya sabha ke duusari baar manonith sadsy, kendriya hindi shikshan mandal ke duusari baar adhyaksh . unhone vigyansanhita naamak ek granth kii rachana bhi kii. ve prayojanamulak hindi ke vichaar ke janak the.
 bharat sarkaar, anek vishvavidhyalayon, dakshin bharat kii hindi prachaar-prasar kii sansthaaon evam kendriya hindi santhaan dwaara sammanit.
 vishesh ullekhneey :-
 1. padam bhushan 1962 main

 4. unke sammaan main kendriya hindi santhaan dwaara prati varsh, bhartiya mul ke kisi vidwaan ko videshon main hindi prachaar-prasar main ullekhneey kaary ke liye, 'padmabhushan dow moturi satyanarayan puraskaar' se sammanit kiya jaataa he.
