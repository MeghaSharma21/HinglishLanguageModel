 khang yaa ness aamtaur par kuch standhari jeevon munh baahar nikle hue asaamaanya rup se lambey daant hote hai. walrus or junglee suar jaise praaniyon main yah bade hue radanak danta or, hathi or naravhal ke maamle main yah bade hue krintak danta hote hai.
 khang aam taur par or ghumaavdaar hai ore nukile hote he, haalanki naravhal kaa ekmaatr khang sidhaa hota he or isaki sanrachana sarpil hoti he. haathiyon ke maamle main khang ko hathi daant yaa gajadant kaha jaataa he or yah doosraa krintak hote hai.
