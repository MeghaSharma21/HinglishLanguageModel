 
 hawaldaar elumalai em bhartiya sena ke dakshini kaman ke madras regiment main hawaldaar ke pad par the. hawaldaar elumalai em jo kii tamilnadu pradesh ke nivasee the inhone 19 varsh bhartiya sena main apni duty kaa nirvahan kiya. 3 farvari 2016 ko siachin kii sonam post par himaskhalan ke dauraan ve veergati ko praapt hue. himaskhalan ke dauraan havaldaar elumalai em ne apni company commander ko radio set par barf main 35 foot neeche se sabase pehle soochna dene waalo main se ek the. inhone musiibat se samay sainyam kaayam rakhte hue sthiti par niyatran kiya. inaki adbhut saahas sujh boojh or samajhadaari se bachaav dal ne pahunch kar bachaav kaary ko puraa kiya.
 drudh karttavya nishthaa ,acolpaneey pahal ,karmthata or saraahneey bahaduree ke liye dakshini kaman alankaran samaaroh 2017 main hawaldaar elumalai em ko " sena padak " se sammmanit kiya gaya. maranoparant inke paripper main inaki patni shrimati i jamuna rani or do putr kavirasu or shri priyadarshan hai.
