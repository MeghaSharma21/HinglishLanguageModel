  peter selestin elampaseshri oefem, cap jammu-srinagar ke roman catholic suba ke duusare bishap the.
  unka janm 28 june 1 9 38 ko, bharat ke kerala, mutuchiri main hua tha. metric puraa karne ke baad unhone capchin main shaamil hokar 1 9 63 main apni pratigya kii.
  3 oktober 1 9 66 ko unhen pujaariyon miner capachyun mandali ke aadesh main pujari tehraaya gaya tha unhone kuch samay ke liye capchin ke agra mishan main kaam kiya. 1 9 80 main unhen uttar bharat main capchin ke superior niyukt kiya gaya tha. unhen pro-prefect apostolic ke rup main jammu-srinagar mishan ke liye bheja gaya tha. 1986 main ise banaaya gaya tha jab vah pehali baar suba ke vikaar niyukt kiya gaya tha. vah 1997 main prantiy pratinidhi ke rup main assam-meghalaya mishan ko sthanantarit kar diya gaya tha.
  3 april 1 99 8 ko elmpasseri, jammu-srinagar, bharat ke roman catholic suba ke duusare bishap kaa niyukt kiya gaya. unke bishap kaa kram 6 sitambar 1 99 8 ko tha or vah 2014 main sevanivrutt hue. vah bishap hippolyatas anthoni kunnkunal oefem, cap vah july 2012 main ek stroke kaa saamana karnaa padaa. bigadati swasthya ke kaaran unhone 2013 main istipha de diya. 3 disambar 2014 ko holi si ne unka istipha sweekaar kar liya tha.
 peter selestin ne 1 9 78 main rome ke medical college, rome, italy se misiology main doctoret praapt kii.
 27 may 2015 ko asisi ashram, bharanaangam, kerala main lambey samay tak beemaari or bade paimaane par dil kaa daura karne ke baad unka nidhan ho gaya. vah 77 saal kaa tha
