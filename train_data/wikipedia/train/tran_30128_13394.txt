 minar unchaa stambh-numa sthapaty hota he jo dekhne main kisi aam burj se adhik lambaa or khinchaa hua dikhataa he. saamanytah minar belanaakaar, lambe or upar pyaaz-numa mukut se susajjit hote hai. ve aasapaas kii imaaraton se adhik unche hote hai or aksar muslim masjidon ke saath lage hue paae jate hai.
 minaaron main aam-taur par ek nichalaa daravaaza hota he jis se minar main daakhil hua ja saktaa he, andar ek zeena hota he jis se upar tak chadha ja saktaa he or sabase upar khadey hone kii jagah hoti he. islamic pratha main is unche chabutare par khadey hokar mullah azaan lagaakar logon ko namaaz padhane ke liye bulaayaa karte hai, haalanki zaruri nahin he ki har minar kaa prayog isi prakaar ho. bhartiya upmahadweep, afghanistan or iran main minaaren gol yaa ashtabhuji hoti hai lekin uttari africa main chakor akar kii minaaren banane kii pratha he.
