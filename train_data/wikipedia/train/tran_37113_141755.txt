  vaayas of jehad afghanistan main sakriy sanghathan taliban kaa aadhikarik angreji antarjaal prishth he. isapar ve apni buniyadi nitiyon evam vichaardhaara se sambandhit lekh tathaa vaishvik paridrishy par apana drushtikon prastut karte hai.
 17 june 2012 ko taliban ne is par prakashit ek aalekh main bharat kii prashansa karte hue kaha ki afghanistan kii dharti kaa uske khilaaf istemaal karne kii ijaajat nahin di jaaegi.
