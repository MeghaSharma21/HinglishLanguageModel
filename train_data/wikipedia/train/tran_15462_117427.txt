 kirkbaron umeo shahir main ume nadi par sthit ek pul he. iskaa nirmaan 1973 main shuru hua or 26 sitambar 1975 main sampann hua. yah ume nadi kaa teesraa pul he. 1960 ke dashak se lekar 1970 tak yah bahis hoti rahi ki kya is pul ko giraje ke paas banaaya jaae yaan nahin. iske nirmaan dauraan ek kabristaan mila jiske kaaran puraatatv vigyaniyon dwaara khudwai karwaai gai.
 1930ke dashak main yah baat saafe ho gai thi ki umeo kaa eklota pul gamalaa baron bad rahi awazai kaa saamana nahin kar paayegaa. westerbaton main county board ne vaastukar o.ech.garaahnen ke aadesh die ki ek nae pul banane kii yojana banaai jaae. garaahnen ne 16 july 1936 ke bhin-bhin yojanayon kaa prastaav rakhaa. unamem se ek yojana umeo shahir ke giraje ke paas ostra kirkogaataan tak ek pul banane kii thi. isamen ek mukhya samasya yah thi ki is pul ke kuch hisse hilne vaale hone chahie the tanki iske niche se jahaaj guzar sake. aisi yojana bahut hi mahingi maani gai thi.
