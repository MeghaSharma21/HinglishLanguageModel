 pin ghati rashtriya udyaan bharat ke himachal pradesh ke spiti kshetra main sthit ek rashtriya udyaan he. yah lahaul or spiti jile main, spiti ghati ke thande registan main sthit he. yah 1987 main ek udyaan ghoshit kiya gaya tha.
 yah kshetra lahaul or spiti jile main, spiti ghati kii pin nadi tathaa uski pradhan sahaayak nadi parahio ke jalgrahan kshetra main padataa he. is udyaan kii oonchaai 3500 me. se lekar 6000 me. tak he. nikatatam shahar yaa kasbaa lahaul or spiti jile kaa kaaza he. 
 pin ghati rashtriya udyaan apni barf se ladi oonchaai vaale ilaakon or dhili dhalaanon ke kaaran himalay ke kai lubhtpraaya praaniyon, jaise himalay ibex, him tendua, bharal, oonee kharaha, tibeti bhediya or him murg sahit kai anya jaanwaron kaa prakritik vaas he. yahan kai prakaar kii vishisht vanaspati jaise ratanjot, salampanja, somalata. junglee gulab bhi yahan bahutayat main paaya jaataa he.
