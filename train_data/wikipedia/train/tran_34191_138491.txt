 oruzgan yaa urozgan afghanistan kaa ek praant he jo us desh ke madhya bhaag main sthit he. is praant kaa kshetrafal 22,696 varg kimi he or isaki aabaadi san 2006 main lagbhag 3.1 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. is praant kii rajdhani tareenkot shahar he. is praant main basane vaale adhiktar log pashtun hai or saanskrithik rup se yah praant kandahaar ke kshetra se miltaa-julata he.
 28 march 2004 main oruzgan praant ke un zilon ko alag karke daayakundi praant banaaya gaya tha jinamein hazaaraa samudaay ke log bahusankhyak the. isamen keval ek gizaab zila tha jisamen pashtun log bahusankhyak the, lekin 2006 main is zile ko daayakundi se hataakar waapas oruzgan main mila diya gaya.
