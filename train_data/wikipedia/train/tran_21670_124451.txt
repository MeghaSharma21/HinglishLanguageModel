 hugo varsh 2011 kii british-america-french bhashaa kii 3di aitihaasik evam romaanch-drama aadhaarit film he jiskaa nirdesan evam nirmaan martin scorces ne kiya tathaa lekhak jaan logan ne patakatha ko mul lekhak bryan selzanik ke graphic upanyaas da invension of hugo cabrett ke aadhaar par roopantaran karaayaa, yah 1930 ke dashak ke ek kishor ke bare main jo paris sthit ger montpernaise ke railway stesan par akelaa rahata tha. film ke sah-nirmaan main do anya log graham king kii jike films tathaa jaani dep kii infinitem nihil bhi sammilit hai, film main kai prakhyat adaakaaron ko cast kiya he jinamein ben kingsle, saicha bairaan kohen, esa battarfield, chloe gras maretz, re vinston, emily martimer, jud laa, hellen mike'krari evam christopher lee shaamil hai.
 maaikel pit, martin scorcese tathaa bryan selzanik chemiyo bhoomikaaon main.
