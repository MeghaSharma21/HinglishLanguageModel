 
 raashtrapati shashan
 aravind kejarivalaap
 dilli vidhaan sabha chunaav 2015, 7 farvari 2015 ko yojit kiya gaya or parinaam 10 farvari 2015 ko ghoshit kiya gaya. yah chunaav dilli ke 70 vidhansabha siton par ladaa gaya. 
 2013 ke dilli vidhansabha chunaavon main, bhartiya janata party ne 70 siton main se 32 jiti or sabase badi party ke rup main ubhari. haalanki ve bahumat se rah gaye or isliye sarkaar banane main asamarth the. dilli ke tatkaaleen leftinent governor najeeb jang ne sarkaar banane ke liye aam aadmi party ko, jo bhartiya janata party ke baad duusari sabase badi party hui, aamantrit kiya.
 28 disambar, 2013 main, aap ne bhartiya rashtriya congress se baahar se samarthan lene ke baad rajya sarkaar kaa gathan kiya. avlambee mukhyamantri shila dikshit ko haraane vaale aap ke netaa arvind kejariwal dilli ke 7 wein mukhyamantri bane. haalanki, 14 farvari 2014 ko arvind kejariwal ne apane pad se istipha de diya. isi kaaran dilli main lagbhag ek saal ke liye raashtrapati shashan bana rahaa. 12 janvari 2015 ko bharat ke nirvachan ayog ne rajya vidhansabha chunaav 7 farvari 2015 ko yojit kiye jane kii ghoshnaa kii.
 chunaav ayog ne 12 janvari 2015 ko chunaav ke kaarykram kii ghoshnaa kii.
 10 farvari 2015 ko matdaan kii ginati hui. aap ne 70 main se 67 siten jiti or bhaajapa ne sirf 3.
