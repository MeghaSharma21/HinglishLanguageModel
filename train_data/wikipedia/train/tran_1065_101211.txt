 richard ernst levi ek dakshin africa cricket khilaadi he. inhone srilanka main 2006 you-19 cricket vishwa cap main khelaa. ye vartmaan main pashchimi praant, kep cobraj or northemptnshir ke liye khelte he. inhone kep toun main viyanberg boys hai skool main bhaag liya or 2005 main cricket ke liye sammaan praapt kiya tha.
 2012 ke saal main, levi ne dakshin africa cricket team ke liye 13 antarrashtriya tii-20 match khele. hamiltan main cedon park main newzealand ke khilaaf apane duusare match main inhone naabad 117 ranon kii pari kheli thi or yahi unka ab tak kaa tii-20 main sabase bada score he. iske baad se hi ye antarrashtriya main jagah banane main prayaas kar rahe he lekin unhen jagah nahin mili.
