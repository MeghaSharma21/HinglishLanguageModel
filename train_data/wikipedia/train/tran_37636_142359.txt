 ajasu party ajasu party bharat sarkaar ke chunaav ayog ke dwaara nibandhit ek kshetriy rajanithik party he. iske kendriya adhyaksh sudesh kumaar mahto he. bharat nirvachan ayog nai dilli ke dwaara adhisuchana sankhya 56/2010/PPS-II taarikhah 17-10-2010 i. ke dwaara ajasu party ko jharkhand rajya ke liye kshetreey dal ke rup main soochibaddh karte hue chunaav chinh kelaa chhap abantit kiya. 2009 ke jharkhand vidhaan sabha chunaav main ajasu party ke taraf se paanch vidhayak chunaav jeet ke vidhansabha pahuunche. tathaa 2011 main hatiya vidhansabha main hue upachunaav main ajasu party ummidwar jeviem ke ummidwar ko haraate hue vidhansabha main party vidhayakon kii sankhya 6 pahunchi. jharkhand vidhaan sabha 2005 iske do sadsy the. iske kendriya adhyaksh sudesh kumaar mahto 2005 main bani arjuna munda sarkaar main sube ke grahamantri the. isi party ke chandraprakas choudhary bhi arjuna munda sarkaar main kebinet mantri the. 2014 ke vidhaan sabha chunaav main party ke 5 ummidwar chunaav jeet kar vidhansabha pahuunche. tathaa vartmaan main rajya ke raghubar das sarkaar main ajasu party ke vidhayak shri chandraprakas choudhary kebinet mantri hai.iskaa kendriya karyaalay purnima tower, dusraa talla, cameraa sankhya -2e, lake road, ranchi, jharkhand main sthit he.
