 abhiyantrikeey bhautikee yaa abhiyantrikeey vigyaan bhautikee, ganit or abhiyantriki ke sanyukt adhyayan se sambaddh he. yah vishesh rup se sanganak, parmaanu, vidyutiya evam yantric abhiyantriki se sambandhit he. mul rup se vaigyanik tareekon ko drushtikon main rakhte hue, yah shakha abhiyantriki main nae samaadhaanon kii rachana evam unka vikaas kar lagu karne kaa uddeshy rakhti he.
