 samrik paryvekshan yaa toh lenaa yaa riconicens yuddh se purv shatru kii sthiti yaa gati kii toh lagane ko kehete hai. sthalakriti paryvekshan main choti sainik tukdi yaa anya sahaayta ko lekar koi afasar sambandhit kshetra kii bhoomi yaa maarg kii banaavat, prakritik tathaa anya baadhaon ityadi kii jaanch karata he. yudhnithik toh pehle ghudsavaaron dwaara karai jaati thi, par ab yah kaary vayuyanon se liya jaataa he.
 samrik paryvekshan sabhi prakaar kii senaaon ke liye aavashyak hota he, chaahe yah swaraksha ke nimitt pehle hi ho athwa shatru se sampark hone par ho. aajkal ghudsavaaron kaa mukhya upyog isi kaary ke liye hota he. paidal sena ke saath isiilie ghudsavaaron kaa bhi ek dal rahata he. kabhi-kabhi sab prakaar kii, arthath paidal, ghudsavaar, topakhana aadi sammilit, ek badi sena dwaara paryvekshan is vichaar se karaayaa jaataa he ki shatru kii yuddh niti yaa chaal kaa pataa lag jaae, chaahe is kaary main ek khaasi jhadap hi ho jaae.
