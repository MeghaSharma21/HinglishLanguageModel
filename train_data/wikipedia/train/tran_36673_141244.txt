 megnas carlson norge ke shatranj khiladi hai va shatranj ke taatkaalik grandmaster vaa vishwa champion vaa vishwa ke pratham sthaan ke khilaadi hai. inaki ucchtam reating 2872, ajtak kii sarvocch hai.
 carlson kaa janm norway ke tonsburg main 30 nabambar 1990 ko hua tha. mahaj 10 varsh kii aayu se hi shatranj khilne lage the. 13 varsh kii aayu main grandmaster kii oopaadhi praapt kii. 
 9 navambar 2013 se 22 navambar 2013 tak bharat ke chennai main yojit vishwa shatranj championships main 5 baar ke vishwa champion vishvanathan anand ko haraakar carlson vishwa champion bane. is paratiyogita main kul 12 match hone the kintu carlson ne 10 maichon main hi nirnay apane paksh main kar liya. is pratiyogita main unka safar is prakaar rahaa.
