 sonagiri, gwalior se 60 kimi dur jila datia madhya pradesh main sthit he. yah sthaan mukhyatah jain tirth he. sonagiri main 57 mandir hai, jiname kramank 57vaa mandir mukhya he. is mandir me bhagwan chandraprabhu kii 11feet unchi murti sthapit he, tathaa yahan ek vishaal haul he jahaan dhyaan kiya jaataa he, or paas hi ek mahastambh sthapit he, jisaki oonchaai 43 feet he. 
 sonagiri me hi acharya shubhachandra tathaa rishi bhratrurihari ne nirvana paaya evam anek granthon kii rachana kii.
