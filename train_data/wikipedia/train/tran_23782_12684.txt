 mishra dhaatu nigam bharat main vishesh prakaar kii dhaatuen evam mishradhatuen banane kaa kaarkaana he. yah andhrapradesh ke hyderabad main sthit he. saarvajanik kshetra kaa yah upkram bharat ke nabhikeey, antariksh or prakshepastra kaaryakramon ke liye mahatvapurn upkarnon kaa utpaadan karata he. 
 'midhani ab duniya kaa sabase badhiya maraging ispaat taiyaar kar rahaa he. iskaa istemaal nabhikeey riektaron, eendhan sanvardhan centriphujon, prakshepastron or rocketon main hota he. isro kaa jieselvi rocket bhi isi dhaatu se lipta hota he.'
 midhani kii sthapana 1972 main hui thi. isaki sthapana kaa uddeshy desh ke ranneetik kaaryakramon ke liye utpaada taiyaar karnaa tha. aaj midhani na keval samvedansheel utpaadon ko bana rahi he, balki baari munafa bhi arjeet kar rahi he. yah company miniratn kii shrenee-1 main aati he. pichley 4 saal main company kaa munafa 6 guna ho chukaa he. vitt varsh 2008-09 main company kaa munafa 40 karod rupee tha.
