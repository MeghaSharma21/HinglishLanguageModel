 parma footbal club aamtaur par sirf parma ke rup main sanderbhit kiya jaataa he, parma main aadhaarit ek italvi peshewar footbal club he. yah disambar 1913 main verdi footbal club ke rup main sthapit hua tha, 1923 ke baad se, club stadio ennio tardini main apane gharelu match kheltaa he. 
 parma gharelu lig khitaab kabhi jeeta nahin par yah gharelu koppa italia jeeta he or yah europea morche par ek sheersh club he, yah club europea morche par do yuiede cap, ek europea super cap or ek yuiede cap vinurs cap jeeta he. europe main unke pradarshan ke kaaran italvi lig ke sthapit shaktiyon ke prabhutv ko ek dhamkee thi. haal hi main, parma ke vittiya pareshaaniyon ne club kii mahatvakankshaon ko simit kar diya he. club kii hameshaa se apane academy se yuva khilaadiyon ke vikaas kii niti rahi he.
