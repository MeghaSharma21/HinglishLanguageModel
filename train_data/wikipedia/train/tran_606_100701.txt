 rani sati, rajasthan kii ek virangana thim jinhen narayani devi yaa dadiji bhi kehete hai. inkaa jeevankaal ke bare main thik-thik pataa nahin he kintu 13veen se 17veen shatabdi ke bich inkaa jeevankaal mana jaataa he. apane pati ke mrityu ke pashchaat ve sati ho gayin thim. unke naam se rajasthan or anya sthanon par anek mandir banaae gaye hai jinamein se jhunjhanu kaa raani sati mandir atyant bhavy or prasiddh he.
