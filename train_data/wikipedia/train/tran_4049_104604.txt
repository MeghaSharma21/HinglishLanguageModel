 
  gulzarilal nanda bhartiya raajnneetijna the. unka janm sialkot, punjab, pakistan main hua tha. ve 1964 main pratham bhartiya pradhanmantri jawahar laal nehru kii mrityupashchaat bharat ke pradhanmantri bane. congress party ke prati samarpit gulzaari laal nanda pratham baar pandit jawaharlal nehru kii mrityu ke baad 1964 main kaaryvahak pradhanmantri banaae gaye. duusari baar laal bahadur shastri kii mrityu ke baad 1966 main yah kaaryvahak pradhanmantri bane. inkaa kaarykaal donon baar usee samay tak simit rahaa jab tak kii congress party ne apane nae netaa kaa chayan nahin kar liya.
 nandaji ke rup main vikhyath gulzaari laal nanda kaa janm 4 july 1898 ko sialkot main hua tha, jo ab pashchimi pakistan kaa hissaa he. inke pita kaa naam bulaaki ram nanda tathaa mata kaa naam shrimati ishwar devi nanda tha. nanda kii praathmik shiksha sialkot main hi sampann hui. iske baad unhone lahore ke 'forman chrishchiyan college' tathaa elahabaad vishvavidhyalay main adhyayan kiya. gulzaari laal nanda ne kala sankaay main snatakottar evam kaanoon kii snaatak oopaadhi praapt kii. inkaa vivaah 1916 main 18 varsh kii umr main hi lakshmi devi ke saath sampann ho gaya tha. inke parivaar main do putr or ek putree sammilit hue.
 gulzaari laal nanda ek rashtrabhakt vyakti the. is kaaran bharat ke swadhinata sangram main inkaa kaafii yogadan rahaa. nandaji kaa jivan armbh se hi raashtra ke prati samarpit tha. 1921 main unhone asahyog aandolan main bhaag liya. nandaji bahumukhi pratibha ke dhani the. unhone mumbai ke neshnal college main arthshaastra ke vyaakhyaata ke rup main apni sevaayen pradaan kin. ahmedabad kii textiles industry main yah lebar association ke sachiv bhi rahe or 1922 se 1946 tak kaa lambaa samay inhone is pad par guzaaraa. yah shramikon kii samasyaon ko lekar sadaiv jaagruk rahe or unka nidaan karne kaa prayaas karte rahe. 1932 main satyagrah aandolan ke dauraan or 1942-1944 main bharat chodo aandolan ke samay inhen jail bhi janaa padaa.
 nandaji mumbai kii vidhansabha main 1937 se 1939 tak or 1947 se 1950 tak vidhayak rahe. is dauraan unhone shram evam awaas mantralay kaa kaarybhaar mumbai sarkaar main rahete hue dekha. 1947 main 'indian neshnal trade union congress' kii sthapana hui or iskaa shrey nandaji ko jaataa he. mumbai sarkaar main rahane ke dauraan gulzaari laal nanda kii pratibha ko rekhaankit karne ke baad inhen congress alaaqamaan ne dilli bula liya. yah 1950-1951, 1952-1953 or 1960-1963 main bharat ke yojana ayog ke upaadhyaksh pad par rahe. aise main bharat kii panchavarshiya yojanaon main inkaa kaafii sahyog pandit jawaharlal nehru ko praapt hua. is dauraan unhone nimnavat prakaar se kendriya sarkaar ko sahyog pradaan kiya-
 gulzaari laal nanda kendriya mantrimandal main cabinet mantri rahe or swatantra mantralayon kaa kaarybhaar sambhaala.nandaji ne yojana mantralay kaa kaarybhaar sitambar 1951 se may 1952 tak nisthapurvak sambhaala.nandaji ne yojana ayog evam nadi ghati pariyojnaon kaa kaary may 1952 se june 1955 tak dekha.nandaji ne yojana, sinchai evam urja ke mantralayik kaaryon ko april 1957 se 1967 tak dekha.nandaji ne shram evam rojgaar mantralay kaa kaary march 1963 se janvari 1964 tak safaltaapurvak dekha.
 nandaji ne mantrimandal main varishthatam sahyogi hone ke kaaran do baar kaaryvahak pradhanmantri kaa daayitv sambhaala. inkaa pratham kaarykaal 27 may 1964 se 9 june 1964 tak rahaa, jab pandil jawahar laal nehru kaa nidhan hua tha. doosraa kaarykaal 11 janvari 1966 se 24 janvari 1966 tak rahaa, jab laal bahadur shastri kaa taashkand main dehant hua. nandaji pratham paanch aam chunaavon main loksabhaa ke sadsy nirvaachit hue.
 nandaji ne ek lekhak kii bhoomika adaa karte hue kai pustakon kii rachana kii. jinke naam is prakaar hai- sam aspects of khaadi, approach tu da second five year plan, guru tegbahadur, sant and sevier, history of edjastment in da ahmedabad textalls, for e maural revolushan tathaa sam basic concidration.
 gulzaarilal nanda ko desh kaa sarvocch sammaan bharat ratna or doosraa sarvshereshth naagarik sammaan padam vibhushan pradaan kiya gaya.
 nanda dderghaayu hue or 100 varsh kii avasthaa main inkaa nidhan 15 janvari 1998 ko hua. inhen ek svachh chhavi vaale gandhivadi rajaneta ke rup main sadaiv yaad rakhaa jayega.
