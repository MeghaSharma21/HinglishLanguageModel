 
 karanzidaaye haddidaar machhliyon ke perceformez gana kaa ek kul he jisamen jaik machhliyaan shaamil hai. is kul main pompano, jaik mekaral or scad machhliyaan bhi sammilit hai. yah samudri machhliyaan hai jo atlantic, prashanth or hind mahasaagaron main pai jaati hai. karanzidaaye kul kii machhliyaan tej tairne vaali parabhakshi jatiyaan hoti hai jo rifon ke upar or khule sagar main shikaar karti hai or kuch sagaratah main ret main chupe aksherukon ko khodakar nikaalati or khaati hai.
 kul kii adhiktar machhliyaan 25 - 100 centimetre tak lambi hoti hai. kul kii sabase badi machhlee, badi amberjaik, 2 meter tak lambi ho jaati he.
