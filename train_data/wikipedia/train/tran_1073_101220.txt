 ajitnath jain dharm ke 24 tirthakaro main se vartmaan avasarpini kaal ke dwitiya tirthankar he.ajitnath kaa janm ayodhya ke rajpariwaar main magh ke shukla paksh kii ashtami main hua tha. inke pita kaa naam jitshatru or mata kaa naam vijaya tha. ajitnath kaa chihn hathi tha.
 jain granthon ke anusaar dwitiya tirthankar, ajitnath kaa janm pratham tirthankar rishabhnath ke janm ke baad 50 lakh karod vargros pam or 12 lakh purv biit jane ke baad hua tha. unki oonchai 450 dhanush thi. unhone 18 lakh purv yuva avasthaa main vyateet kiye. unhone apane rajya par 53 lakh purv or 1 purvang tak shashan kiya . unhone 1 purvang kam 1 lakh purv kaal sanyam sadhana main vyateet kiya . bhagwaan ajitath kii kul aayu 72 lakh purv kii thi.
