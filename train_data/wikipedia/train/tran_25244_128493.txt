 
 vajrashalk yaa pangolin folidota gana kaa ek standhari praani he. iske sharir par keratin ke bane shalk numa sanrachana hoti he jisase yah anya praaniyon se apni raksha karata he. pangolin aise shalkon vaala akelaa gyaat standhari he. yah africa or asia main prakritik rup se paaya jaataa he. ise bharat main sallu saanp bhi kehete hai. inke nivas vaale van shighrata se kaate ja rahe hai or andhvishwasi prathaon ke kaaran inkaa aksar shikaar bhi kara jaataa he, jisaki vajah se pangolin kii sabhi jatiya ab sankatagrast maani jaati hai or un sab par vilupti kaa khatara mandara rahaa he.
 pangolin kii jihva cheenteeshoron kii tarah hoti he or is se vah cheentee va dimak khaane main sakasham hota he, or yahi uskaa mukhya aahaar he. isliye pangolin ko kabhi-kabhi shalkdaar cheenteekhor bhi kaha jaataa he.
