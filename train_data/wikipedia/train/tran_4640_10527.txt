 karak pakistan ke kaibar-pakhtuunakva praant kaa ek zila he. yah kohat zile ke dakshin main, bannu or lakki maravat zilon ke uttar main sthit he. peshawar se karachi jane vaale sindhu rajmarg ke raste main aane vaala yah zila kaibar-pakhtuunakva kii rajdhani peshawar se 123 kilometre kii duuri par he. kaha jaataa he ki karak pakistan kaa iklauta zila he jisamen keval ek hi pashtun qabeele ke log rahete hai or yah khatak kabila he.
 karak zile main san 1998 main 4,30,796 logon ki aabaadi thi. iskaa kshetrafal qareeb 3,372 varg kimi he. 1940 se lekar 1982 tak yah kohat zile kaa hissaa tha, lekin 1 july 1982 ko ise ek alag zile kaa darja de diya gaya. isaki rajdhani terry naamak shahar he or kisi jamaane main terry nawab kaa is puure zile par raaj hua karata tha. yahan ke log khatak qabeele ke barake upambeele ke pathan hai or is puure kshetra main pashto boli jaati he.
