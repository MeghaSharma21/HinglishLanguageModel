 sindhu ghati kii sabhyataa se sambandhit chote-chote sanketon ke samooh ko sindhu lipi kehete hai. ise sindhu-saraswati lipi or hadappa lipi bhi kehete hai. yah lipi sindhu sabhyataa ke samay paripakv rup dhaaran kar chuki thi. isko abhi tak samajha nahin ja paaya he usase sambandhit bhashaa agyaat he isaliye is lipi ko samajhane main vishesh kathinaayee aa rahi he.
 bharat main lekhan 3300 ipu kaa he. sabase pehle kii lipi saraswati lipi thi, uske pashchaat brahmi ai.
