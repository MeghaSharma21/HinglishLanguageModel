 preri prithvi ke samshitoshna kshetra main sthit vishaal ghaas ke maidaanon ko kaha jaataa he. inamen taapmaana greeshmaritu main madhyam or sheetarutu main thanda rahata he or madhyam matraaon main barf-barish padati he. yahan par vanaspati jivan ghaas, phoos or choti jhaadon ke rup main adhik or pedo ke rup main kam dekhne ko miltaa he. aise ghaasdaar maidaanon ko uttari america main "preri", uresia main "stap" yaa "stapi", dakshin america main "pampa" or dakshin africa main "vald" kaha jaataa he.
 uttar america kii preri sanyukt rajya america ke madhya bhaag, canada ke dakshin-madhya bhaag or mexico ke uttar-madhya bhaag main faili hui he. pichley himyug main failati himanion ne zamin par chalakar use ragadate hue mitti main bahut uthal-puthal kari. jab yah himaniyaan pighalin to apni barf main mishrit gard or padaarth kii ek tah chhod gai jis se is kshetra kii mitti ghaason ke liye bahut paushtik ban gai. pichley ek lakh saal se yahan par preri kii ghaason main kram-vikaas chal rahaa he. itani vistrut ghaas hone se in kshetron main charne vaale jaanwaron ke vishaal jhund bhi rahane lage, jaise kii america bison or bhinn prakaar ke hiran. in jaanwaron ke jhundon kaa shikaar karke bahut se mul america adivasee qabeele bhi ek samrudh shikari-faramar jeevani jeene main sakasham the. sookhe ke mausam main yahan bhayankar aag bhi failati rahati thi isliye in ghaason main aag ke baad swayan ko phir se failaane kii kshamta ban gai or kuch ke liye to niyamit rup se aag laganaa unke panpane ke liye zaruri bhi ho gaya. aag lagane se ped to maare jate hai, lekin ghaas phir bhi waapas aa jaati he. jab europian log america main aakar based to unhone bahut se preri kshetra ko krishi ke ilaaqon main badal diya.
