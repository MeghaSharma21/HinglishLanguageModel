 
 derveze yaa daravaaza turkmenistan ke aakhaal praant main sthit ek gaanv he. yah karakum registan ke madhya main rashtriya rajdhani ashgab se lagbhag 260 kimi dur sthit he. yahan rahane vaale zyaadaatar log turkmen samudaay ke ardh-kanaabadosh jeevani vyateet karne vaale theke qabeele ke sadsy hai. turkmen bhashaa main 'daravaaza' kaa vahi arth he jo hindi main hota he, yani 'dwaar'.
 derveze kaa kshetra zamin ke neeche maujuud prakritik gas ke bhandar ke liye prasiddh he. 1971 main, jab turkmenistan soviet sangh kaa hissaa tha, kuch soviet bhuvaigyanik yahan khudaai karte hue neeche ek gas se bhari hue badi gufa tak pahunch gaye. yahan pahonchte hi gupha kii chat gir gai or unka sara khudaai kaa saaman usamen ja gira. yahan, 40°15′10″N 58°26′22″E / 40.25264°N 58.43941°E / 40.25264; 58.43941 ke bhugolic nirdeshaank par, ek 70 meter bada chhidr khul gaya or usamen se zahreeli gas baahar ko uthne lagi. is zahreele risaav ko rokane ke liye unhone gas main aag laga di. unka anumaan tha ki gas kuch hi dinon main jalakar khatm ho jaaegi, lekin yah aag ajtak jal rahi he. sthaneey log is aag ugalate hue bade chhidr ko 'pataal kaa dwaar' kehete hai.
