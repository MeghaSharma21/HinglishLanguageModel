 doa-al-adal ek misr ke cartoonist hai, jo apane raajnneetik, saamaajik yaa dharmik arthon ke saath vyangypurna cartoon ke liye vikhyath hai. unhen misr ke sabase prasiddh mahila cartoonist ke rup main uddhrut kiya gaya he. vah vartmaan main al masori al yum akhbaar ke liye kaam karti he, jiske saath unhen kaaphi dhyaan mila he or vivaad paidaa hua he. 
 al-adal ne al dostor, rose al yusuf magazine or sabaa el kher magazine ke liye ek cartoonist ke rup main kaam kiya he, or usne katar el nada, ala-el deen or bassam ke liye sachitr karne kaa kaam kiya he. vah ab al masori al yum ke liye kaam karti he, jiske saath unhen kaaphi dhyaan mila he. 2009 main unhone karicchar puraskaar main pehali mahila praptakarta ban kar unhen jarnlistic distinction jeeta. 2016 main bibisi kii 100 mahilaon main se ek kaa namit kiya gaya tha.
 al-adal ne disambar 2012 main vivaad paidaa kar diya tha jab akhbaar al masori al yum ne uskaa cartoon prakashit kiya tha jisamen ek devdoot ne adam or eve ko suchit kiya tha ki ve sahi ummidavaar ke liye matdaan karne par adan garden main rah sakate the. haalanki, usne dharm ke raajnneetik viniyog kii aalochana karne kaa iraadaa kiya tha, parantu us par ishaninda karne ke liye jurmaana lagaaya gaya tha. usee varsh, us par ek cartoon, jisamen usne misr main islamvaadiyon kii or raajnneeti main unke prabhaav kii aalochana kii thi, ke liye abhiyojak general talat abdalla ne poochtacha kii thi. brazil ke cartoonist carlos latf ne unka bachaav kiya, jinhone al-adl ko ek bhaalaa kii tarah pencil ke saath ek islamvaadi ke khilaaf khud kaa bachaav karte hue chitrit kiya. farvari 2013 main, usne ek udhaas dikhne vaala aadmi ko ek mahila kii taangon ke bich ek laal phool ko kaatane ke liye, ek seedhi par chadhane or kanchi chalaane kaa chitra banaakar, mahila jananaang vikruti kii aalochana karne ke liye ek cartoon banaaya. raashtrapati mubarak ke mukademe kaa chithran karne vaale unke cartoon bhi lokapriy the
