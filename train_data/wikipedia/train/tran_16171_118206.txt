 udaipur ke shaasha ne jal ke mahatv ko samajhate hue kai baandhon or jalkundon kaa nirmaan karavaaya tha. ye kund tatkaaleen samay ke viksit ejineering kaa utkrisht namoona hai. pichhola jhil, doodh thali, govardhan sagar, kumari talab, rangsagar jhil, swarup sagar tathaa fateh sagar jhil yahaan kii saat pramukh jhilein hai. inhen samuhik rup se udaipur kii saat bahanon ke naam se janaa jaataa he. ye jhilein kai shatabdiyon se udaipur kii jeevanarekha hai. ye jhilein ek-duusaren se judi hui hai. ek jhil main pani adhik hone par uskaa pani apane aap duusare jhil main chalaa jaataa he.
