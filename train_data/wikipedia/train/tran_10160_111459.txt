 vijaynagar sahitya se aashay vijaynagar samrajya main rachit kannada, telegu sanskrit or tamil sahitya se he. yah kaal dakshin bharat ke saahitiyik itihaas kaa swarna kaal tha. is kaal main raajaaon ne vibhinn bhashaaon ke sahityakaron ko aashray diya jinhone jain, virshaiv or vaishnav sampradaay kii paramparaon ke granthon kii rachana kii. is kaal main saikadon granthon kii rachana hui jo bhartiya samskruti, dharm, jeevancharit, prabandh, sangeet, vyakaran, kavita, ayurveda aadi se sambandhit the.
