 brushrahit disi motor electronically commutated motors ) ek prakaar ke tulykaalik motor hai jisamen comutesan ke liye brush nahin hote balki yah kaary inwarter yaa swimming power supplai dwaara kiya jaataa he. is motor kaa roter par ek sthaayee chumbakeey field laga hota he tathaa stater par vinding hoti he jisko inwarter/swimming power supplai dwaara vidyut prayavarti dhara dete hai jo jaruri nahin ki sinaswydel ho. is motor ke andar ek censor bhi laga hota he jo roter ke dhruvon kii sthiti bataata he jisase is motor ko chalaane or control karne main sahaayta milti he.
