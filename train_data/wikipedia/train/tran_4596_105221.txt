 vidyut chumbakeey tarangen jinaki tarang dairghy ek meter se chott tathaa ek millimetre se bani hoti he, yaa aavriti 300 mega hertz se 300 giga hertz ke bich hoti he, unhen atisookshm tarang kehete hai.
 prakirnan, dhruvikaran, vivartan, apvartan, vilyan, samaveshan aadi, jo ki pyaayah pratyaksh prakaash ke gun hote hai, inamen bhi milte hai.
 atisookshm tarang main nimna aati hai :-
  300 GHz, ke upar vidyut chumbakeey vikiran kaa vilyan, prithvi ke vaataavaran dwaara itnaa adhik hota he ki, yah prabhavi rup se ekadam thos jaisaa ho jaataa he, va unhen nikalne nahin detaa, jab tak ki waapas paardarshee nahin ho jaataa, adhorakt yaa pratyaksh varnakram ke liye.
