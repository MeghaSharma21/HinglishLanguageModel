 chandramauli kumaar prasad, bhartiya press council ke adhyaksh hai. 23 may, 2018 ko centre sarkaar ne unhen lagaataar duusare kaarykaal ke liye bhartiya press parishad kaa adhyaksh manonith kiya he. isse purv ve november, 2014 main nyaayamurti markandeya katju ke sevanivrutt hone ke baad pehali baar bhartiya press parishad ke adhyaksh bane the. bharat main press ke maanakon ko banaae rakhane ke saath unamem sudhaar or iske swatantraaa ke sangrakshan ke uddeshy se pratham press ayog kii sifarish ke aadhaar par varsh 1966 main sansad dwaara bhartiya press parishad kii sthapana kii gai thi. yah parishad press council act, 1978 ke tahat kaary karti he. 
 is niyukti se pehle, unhone 8 farvari 2010 se 14 july 2014 tak bhartiya sarvocch nyaayalay ke nyaayadhish ke rup main apni sevaayen di hai.
