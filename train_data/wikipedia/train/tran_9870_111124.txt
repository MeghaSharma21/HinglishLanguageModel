 paurav jhelum nadi ke paas hi kaa rajya he.
 paurav par bahut raja-maharaja raaj karke gaye he par uname se mukhya the raja puru,raja bharat,raja paurav,raja bamani,raja poras,raja malayaketu va raja sidhaarth. paurav kaa nirmaan raja puru ne kiya tha.
 sikandar mahaan ke akraman ke samay paurav bahut hi sundar tha. paurav ko tab paurav raashtra kaha jaataa tha. paurav kaa mukhya rang nila tha jisase rajya ko nil sadan bhi kaha jaataa tha. yahan ke ghar nile rang ke the. sainik bhi neeli varadi pahante the. rajya kaa har vyakti nile kapade pahana karata tha. raja poras ke pita raja bamani ne sabhi rajyo se zyaada paurav raashtra par raaj kiya tha.
 paurav ke bahut naam hai parantu iske mukhya naam hai-paurav raashtra, paurav nagar, nil sadan tathaa pauravaas.
