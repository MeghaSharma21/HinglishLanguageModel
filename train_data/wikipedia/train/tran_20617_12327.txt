 pakistan ke samvidhan kaa satarahawon sanshodhan jise satrahavin sanshodhan 2003 e ke naam se janaa jaataa he, disambar 2003 main kii gai. yah sanshodhan lagbhag ek saal tak tatkaaleen raashtrapati general musharraf ke samarthakon or virodhiyon ke bich bahes bahes or kai maukon par talakhyon ke baad paarit kiya gaya tha. yah sanshodhan is lihaaj se behad mahatvapurn he ki samvidhan pakistan main vyapak rup se kai sanshodhan kiye gaye. in sanshodhano main kai raashtrapati pakistan ke pad se sambandhit thim or samvidhan pakistan main terahaven sanshodhan main kii gai lagbhag sanshodhan waapas le lee gayi. 
 samvidhan pakistan main satrahavin sampadit hi chaynit binduon aabhal main varnit hai.
