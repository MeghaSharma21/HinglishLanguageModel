 himchadar himani barf kaa ek bada samooh hota he jo dharti ko dhake or kam-se-kam 50,000 varg kimi kshetrafal par vistrut ho. tulana ke liye yah bharat ke punjab rajya ke kshetrafal ke lagbhag baraabar he. vartmaankaal main himchaadaren keval antarctic or greenland main milti hai, lekin pichley himyug main uttar america ke adhikansh bhaag par lorentide himchadar, uttari europe par visheli himchadar or dakshini dakshin america par patagonian himchadar faili hui thi.
 kshetrafal main himchaadaren himchattaanon or himanion se badi hoti hai. 50,000 varg kimi se kam aakaar kii himchadar ko barf kii topi kehete hai.
 antarctic ke mahadweep par sthit antarctic himchadar prithvi kaa sabase vishaal barf kaa samooh he. yah himchadar 1.4 karod varg kimi par faili hui he. tulana ke liye bharat kaa kul kshetrafal 12 lakh varg kimi se zaraa adhik he, yani antarctic himchadar bharat se 10 guna adhik kshetra par vistrut he. isamen 3 karod ghan kimi jal qaid he or yah vishwa ke kul mithe pani kaa navve pratishat he. agar yah puraa pigal jaye to prithvi ke samudron kii sataha 58 meter uth jaayegi.
 paar-antarctic parvatamala is himchadar ko purvi antarctic himchadar or pashchimi antarctic himchadar main vibhajit karti he, jinamein purvi himchadar aakaar main badi he. purvi antarctic himchadar to dharti par tiki he or yadi yah barf na bhi hoti to yah sthaan samudri-sataha se upar hota lekin pashchimi antarctic himchadar ke neeche kii zamin kahin-kahin par samudr-sataha se 2500 meter neeche he or bina barf ke yah samudr kaa farsh hota.
 greenland himchadar greenland ke 82% kshetrafal par vistrut he or agar yah puri tarah pighale to samudri-sataha ko 7.2 meter adhik unchaa kar degi. yah anumaan he ki bhumandaleey ooshmeekaran ke kaaran har varsh greenland himchadar kaa 239 ghan kimi pigal rahaa he.
