 khagolashastra main daanav tara aise taare ko bolte hai jiskaa aakaar or chamak donon us se bad ke ho jo uski sataha ke taapmaana ke aadhaar par mukhya anukram ke kisi taare ke hote. aise taare aam taur par suraj se 10 se 100 guna vyas main bade hote hai or chamak main 10 se 1000 guna zyaada roshan hote hai. apane taapmaana ke hisaab se aise daanav taare kai rangon main milte hai - laal, naarangi, nile, safed, vakairaah. mahadaanav taare or paramdanav taare in daanav taron se bhi bade or adhik roshan hote hai.
 "daanav taron" ko angrezi main "jayant star" kaha jaataa he.
