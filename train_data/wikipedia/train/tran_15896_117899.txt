 amoghvarsh nripatung yaa amoghvarsh pratham bharat ke rashtrakut vansh ke mahanatam shashak the. ve jain dharm ke anuyaayi the. itihaaskaaron ne unki shantipriyata evam udaaravaadi dharmik drushtikon ke liye unhen samraat ashok se tulana kii he. unke shasankaal main kai sanskrit evam kannada ke vidwano ko prashray mila jinamein mahaan ganitagya mahaviracharya kaa naam pramukh he.
 amoghvarsh rashtrakut raja jo sa. 814 i. main gaddi par baitha or 64 saal raaj karne ke baad sambhavat: 878 een. main mara. vah govind tritiy kaa putr tha. uske kishor hone ke kaaran pita ne mrityu ke samay karkaraj ko shashan kaa kaary sanbhaalane ko sahaayak niyukt kiya tha. kintu mantri or samant dhire-dhire vidrohi or ashishnu hote gaye. samrajya kaa gangavadi praant swatantra ho gaya or vengi ke chalukyaraj vijayaditya dwitiya ne akraman kar amoghvarsh ko gaddi se utaar tak diya. parantu amoghvarsh bhi saahas chhodanevaala vyakti na tha or karkaraj kii sahaayta se usne rashtrakooton kaa singhaasana phir swytt kar liya. rashtrakooton kii shakti phir bhi lauti nahin or unhen baar-baar chot khaani padi.
 amoghvarsh ke sanjan tamrapatr ke abhilekh se samakaleen bhartiya raajnneeti par paryaapt prakaash padataa he, yadhyapi usamen swayan uski vijayon kaa varnan atiranjit he. vastav main uske yuddh praaia: uske vipareet hi gaye the. amoghvarsh dharmik or vidyavyasnee tha, mahalakshmi kaa param bhakt. jainacharya ke upadesh se uski pravriti jain ho gai thi. 'kavirajmarg' or 'prashnottaramalika' kaa vah rachayita mana jaataa he. usee ne manyakhet rajdhani banaai thi. apane antim dinon main rajkaarya mantriyon or yuvaraj par chhod vah virakt rahane laga tha.
