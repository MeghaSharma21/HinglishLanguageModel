 lion girjaghar santa maaria de laa regala ko samarpit he. 1844i. main ise cultural intrust ghoshit he. ise pulkara leonina bhi kaha jaataa he. ye 13veen sadi kii ek sarvshereshth rachana he. ye gothic style main bana hua he. ise ustad vaastukar enrike ne banaaya tha. yah 16veen sadi main puri tarah ban kar taiyaar hui.
