 mullah abd-ul-kaadir badayuni ek farsi mul ke bhartiya itihaaskaar evam anuvadak rahe the. ye mughal kaal main bharat main sakriy the. ye muluk shah ke putr the.. bachapan main ye basaavar main rahe or shiksha sanbhal evam agra main lee. baad main ye badayu aa gayi or 1562 main main patiala ke rajkumaar hossain khan ke antargatt karyrat rahe. baad ke varshon main inhone sufiwaad kaa adhyayan kiya. akbar ne inhen apane darbaar main 1574 main islamdharm ke adhikari or salaahkaar rup main niyukt kiya tha, jahaan inhone apane jivan ke bahut se varsh vyateet kiye.
