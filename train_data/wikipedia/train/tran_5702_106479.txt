 tikshnjan oxide indhan jaise dijal yaa koyale ke dahan se pradooshit dhuen kaa mukhya avyav he. yah naam NO, NO2 tathaa anya kai gaison ko sammilit rup se diya jaataa he jo pradooshak hai or amleey varsha ko janm detii hai. yah petrol, dijal, koyale ko jalaane se utpann hoti he or bijli kadakane se samay aasmaan main bhi banati he. yah gas bacchon ko, sardiyon main saans kii bimariyon ke prati, samvedansheel banaati he.
 nox kaa sabase bada strot deazal kaa jalanaa he jo vaahanon ke ingine main vaayu kii adhikta main jalataa he. adhik taapmaana par hue dahan, jo ki ek dijal ingine main hota he, main vaayu main mauzood tikshnjan, saansavayu se pratikriya kar iskaa nirmaan karte hai.
