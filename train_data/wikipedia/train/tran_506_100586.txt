 saraswati gora ek bhartiya saamaajik kaaryakarta thi. kai saalon tak naastik centre ke netaa ke rup main ullekhneey kaam kiye hai, unhone asprishyata or jati vyavastha ke khilaaf prachaar kiya hai.
 unka janm 28 sitambar 1912 andhra pradesh main vijayangaram main hua tha. vah das saal kii umr main hi unki shaadi goparaju ramchandra raav ke saath hui. unki aatmakatha 'maai life vid gora' prakashit kii gai thi 2012 main. 19 agast 2006 ko vijaywada main fefdon ke sankraman ke karan unki mrityu ho gai.
