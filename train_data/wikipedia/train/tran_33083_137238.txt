 ulm germany kaa ek nagar he jo danyube nadi ke baayein kinare par swabian alps kii tarai main sthit he.
 danyube main is nagar ke kuch upar iler tathaa kuch neeche blue naamak nadiyaan aakar milti hai. danyube yahan jal yatayaat ke yogya he. falswaroop yah ek nadi-bandargah ke rup main pradhan vyavsaayik centre ho gaya he. yahan tak nekar tathaa rhin nadiyon se bhi yatayaat hota he. yah chamade or oon kaa pradhan bazaar he tathaa yahan par taar kii rassiyaan, sohaaga, rang, makkhan, jute, lakh, itr or cement taiyaar kiya jaataa he. sharaab banane, kapadaa bunane, loha tathaa pital galane kaa bhi kaary hota he. ulm apni milon or foji chhavnee ke kaaran bhi vikhyath he.
