 rover aise vaahan ko kehete hai jo kisi anya grah yaa khagoleey vastu par ghoomane-phirane kii kshamta rakhata ho. kuch rover robot hote hai or bina kisi vyakti kii maujuudagi ke chalate hai or kuch manushyon ko sthaan-se-sthaan le jane ke liye bane hote hai. aam taur par rover kisi grah par kisi anya yaan ke andar le jaae jate hai jo us grah kii sataha par utarataa ho. udahaaran ke liye navambar 2010 main mangal vigyaan prayogshaala yojana ke antargat ek "curiusity" naam kaa rover mangal grah ke liye ravaanaa kiya gaya.
 "rover" shabd kaa angrezi bhashaa main arth "ghummakad" yaa "ghoomane-phirane vaala" hota he. yah shabd manushyon, jaanwaron or vaahanon, sabhi sandarbhon main istemaal kiya jaataa he.
