 saghan chak :discs) yaa compact discs ek prakaashiya chak hoti he jisame aankade ankiy praaroop me sanchit rahete hai. mul rup se iskaa vikaas dhwani recording ke sanchay ke liye hua tha, lekin baad main iskaa prayog anya aankadon ke sanchay ke liye bhi kiya jane laga. shrrivy chak to oktober 1982 se vyavsaayik rup se uplabdh he. 2009 main bhi yah shrrivy aankadon ke bhautik bhandaaran kaa maanak madhyam bani hui hai. 
 sidi kaa maanak yaa prasiddh vyas 12 centimetre yaa 120 millimetre hota he. jo ki 80 minute kaa data yaa lagbhag 700 mega bite data rakhata he. or bhi anya tarah ki kam vyas ki bhi sidia aati he. mini sidi ke vibhinn 60 se 80 millimetre lekar vyas he.

 ek sidi mai data kaa ek hi sarpil trek hota he, jis par sara data hot he. jo ki discs ke andar se baahar ki ore chakkar daar hota he. yah sarpil trek hota he. sidi player sidi par dhakkon ke rup main sangrahit data ko khojane or padhane kaa kaam he. sidi player ke teen buniyadi ghatak hote hai:
 1) ek drive motor discs spins. is drive motor 200 or 500 rpm se trek ko padhati he. 
 2) ek lager or ek lens pranali: CD par dhyaan kendrit karne or padhane ke liye.
 3) ek trekking pranali lager : jisase lager beam sarpil trek kaa anusaran kar sake.
