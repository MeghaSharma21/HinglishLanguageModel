 anuroop abhiklitr yaa analog computer ek aisa analog vidyut paripath hota he jo anek samasyaon kaa samaadhaan karata he. udhaaharan ke liye yah kisi sanket kaa samakalan karke output degaa yaa kisi sanket kaa avkalan kar saktaa he aadi. inamen nivesh evam output sabhi satat char ke rup main hote hai. anuroop sanganak yantric, hydralik electranik yaa anya prakaar ke ho sakate hai. electranik anuroop abhiklitron ke nirmaan ke liye mukhya rup se aaparational pravardhak prayog kiye jate hai. ek baat dhyatby he ki anuroop abhiklitr sannikat samaadhaan detaa he jabaki ankiy abhiklitr bilkul sahi samaadhaan detaa he.
