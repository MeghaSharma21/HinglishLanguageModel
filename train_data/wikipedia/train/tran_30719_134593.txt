 puranic 16 mahajanapadon main se ek he. vartmaan main bundelkhand kaa ilaaka iske arntagat aata he. ganga or narmada ke bich ke kshetra kaa pracheen naam chedi tha. buddha granthon main jin solah mahajanapadon kaa ullekh he unamem yah bhi tha. kalichuri vansh ne bhi yahan rajya kiya. kisi samay shishupal yahan kaa prasiddh raja tha. uskaa vivaah rukmini se hone vaala tha ki shrikrishna ne rukmani kaa haran kar diya iske baad hi jab yudhishthar ke rajasuya yajna main shrikrishna ko pehla sthaan diya to shishupal ne unki ghor ninda kii. is par shrikrishna ne uskaa vadh kar daalaa. madhya pradesh kaa gwalior kshetra main vartmaan chanderi qasbaa hi pracheen kaal ke chedi rajya kii rajdhani bataaya jaataa he.
