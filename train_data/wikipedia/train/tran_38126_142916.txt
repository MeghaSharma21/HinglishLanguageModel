 samudriya electronic electraniki kii vah shakha he jisamen samudri vaataavaran ke anuroop kaam karne vaali electranik pranaaliyon ke dizin evam vikaas kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. chunki namakeen jal kii ek choti buund bhi kisi saamaanya electranik paripath ko bhrasht kar sakati he, isaliye samudri maahaul main kaam karne ke liye dizin kii jane vaali pranaliyaan yaa to jal-rodhi hotin hai yaa jalarakshit . samudri chartploter, kritrim-pilot, viechaef radio, minanveshi aadi samudri electranik pranaaliyon ke kuch udhaaharan hai.
