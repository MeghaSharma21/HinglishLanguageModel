 mukul bhatta kashmir ke yashasvi vidwaan evam siddh acharya kallat ke putr the. rajtarangini ke anusaar bhatta kallat kashmir naresh avantiverma ke shasankaal main vartmaan the. avantiverma kaa samay san 857-884 i maanya he at: mukul bhatta kaa samay naveen shatabdi kaa antim charan or dasvin kaa prarambh maanya hota he. 
 mukul bhatta ne 'abhidhaavritimaatrakaa' naam kaa granth likhaa he jisamen kul 15 karikas hai. in karikaon par vistrut vritti bhi mukul bhatta ne hi likhi he. is chote kintu mahatvapurn granth main vaacharth or lakshyarth kaa tathaa abhidha or lakshana maatr kaa nirupan kiya gaya he. yah nirupan vistrut evam samikshatmak he. abhinav bharti main bhi mukul bhatta kii karikas uddhrut hai. kavyprakash main bhi yatra tatra mukul bhatta ke vichaar purv paksh ke rup main uddhrut kiye gaye hai. udbhatacharya ke 'kaavyalankar sarasangrah' par 'laghuvritti' vyakhyaakaar pratiharenduraj yaa induraj ne apni vyakhya ke antim padya main apane acharya ke rup me mukul bhatta kaa gaurav ke saath ullekh kiya he. yah pratiharenduraj konkan nivasee tha. mukul bhatta dwaara rachit anya koi granth uplabdh nahin he, phir bhi is chote se granth 'abhidhaavritimaatrakaa' dwaara sahitya kshetra main unka naam ullekhneey evam samadarneey aacharyon main pariganit he.
