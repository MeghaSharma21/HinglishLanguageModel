 praudyogiki ke kshetra main us vyakti ko takaneekajna yaa takanisian kehete hai jo kisi takneekee kshetra ke kaaryon ko karne main siddhhast ho. takanisian ke paas takneekee siddhanton kaa acha vyaavhaarik gyaan hota he. anbhavi takaneekajna ke paas sambandhit kshetra kaa madhyam str kaa saiddhanthik gyaan hota he kintu unka vyaavhaarik gyaan uttam str kaa hota he. udhaaharan ke liye audio takanisian dhvaniki ke bare main utana gyaan nahin rakhte jitna gyaan dhvaniki ejeeniyar ke paas hota he, kintu ve dhwani upkarnon ke sanchaalan main ejeeniyar se adhik kushal hote hai.
