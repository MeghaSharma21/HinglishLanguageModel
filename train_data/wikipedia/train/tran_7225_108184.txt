 troy kaa yuddh - pracheen unani purakatha sangrah kii mukhya ghatana he.unaniyon ne troy nagar ko das saalon ke liye ghere main daal diya. homer kii eliad naamak rachana is yuddh ke keval 51 dinon ke nirnaayak drishyon kaa vivaran detii he. try kaa rajkumaar sparta kii rani hellen kaa apharan kar try nagar le gaya. is apmaan kaa badla lene ke liye hi greece ke sabhi raajaaon or veeron ne milakar try par akraman kiya. try se lautate samay unka jahaaj tufaan main fas gaya. vah bahut dinon tak idhar udhar bhatakta rahaa. iske baad apane desh lautaa.
