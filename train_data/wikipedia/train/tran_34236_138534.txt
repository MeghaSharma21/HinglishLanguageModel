 murvaas,vidisha jile kii tehsil lateri kaa ek gaanv he,tehsil ke andar ye sabase bada gaanv hai. isaki jansankhya 2011 ke anusaar 4102 he ye abhi panchayat ke hisaab se isaki jansankhya 6500 he. murvaas main sarkari karyaalayon main panchayat he is panchayat ke antargat sirf ek hi gaanv aata he. or murvaas main thana va bank bhi hai,or shaskeey skool bhi hai jisamen praathmik shalaays 2 ,madhyamik shalaays 1, evam ek u.maa.vidhyalay he. is gaanv main 4 aanganbadiyaan hai. yahan gaanv ke kuch pracheen jagahon ke naam pracheen mandir kataarmal,shriram janaki mandir,panaya kua, patapara, chaowkee hai, yahan ek madrasa bhi he.
