 abraj kudai, saudi arab ke makka nagar main ek yojanadhin hotel he jiske 2017 tak puraa ban jane kii sambhaavna he. nirmaan puraa ho jane par yah vishwa kaa sabase bada hotel hoga jo 45 tal unchaa hoga or isamen 10,000 kamare, 70 restraan, chat ke upar haleepad honge or isamen kul 12 imaaraten hongi. paanch tal keval saudi shahi parivaar ke upyog ke liye hi honge. arabian bisinis ke anusaar, das tower chaar-sitara awaas uplabdh karaayenge, jabaki do anya toweron main vishesh atithiyon ke liye paanch-sitara suvidhaayen uplabdh karai jaayengi. is pariyojna kii kul laagat 3.5 arab dollar anumaanit he, or yah kul 14 lakh varg meter main failaa hoga.
 landan-aadhaarit farm arin hospitality ko is hotel ke kamaron or aantarik saaj-sajja ke dizine kaa theka diya gaya he.
