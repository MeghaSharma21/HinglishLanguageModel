 kar ke swarg yaa 'tex hewan' un deshon ko kehete hai jahaan anya deshon kii apeksha bahut kam kar lagata he, yaa bilkul kar nahin lagata. aise deshon main kar ke alaava bhi bahut si gatividhiyaan chalatin hai. aise desh tex main kisi prakaar kii paardarshita nahin rakhte na hi kisi prakaar kii vittiya jaankaree ko saajha karte hai. ye desh un logon ke liye swarg hai, jo tex chori karke paise in deshon main jamaa kar dete hai.
 aise deshon main paise jamaa karne par ve paise jamaa karne vaale vyakti yaa sanstha ke bare main kuch bhi nahin poochhate. yahi kaaran he ki tex choron ke liye aise desh swarg jaise hote hai, jo apane desh se paise in deshon main kaledhan ke rup main jamaa kar dete hai.
 switzaraland hi nahin, asia main singapore, malaysia, honkong, morishas, maldive, dubai or bahrain, europe main austria, belgium, monako, aadi kar ke swarg hai.
