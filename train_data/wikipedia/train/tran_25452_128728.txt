 
 rajeev gandhi kii htya ke baad unka vikrut sharir hwai jahaas ke dwaara dilli ke palam hwai adde par laayaa gaya | waha se unka sharir shav-pariksha or sanghathan ke liye aal india institute of medical sains pahunchaaya gaya | unka rajkiy kriya karm 24 may 1991 ko huya tha| uskaa prasaran rashtriya or antar rashtriya str par hua|is me shaamil hone desh videsh ke kai netaa aaye | unka dah-sanskaar yamuna nadi ke tat par huya, thik usee sthaan par jahaan unki mata, bhai, dada or mahatma gandhi kaa dah-sanskaar hua tha | aaj us sthaan ko vir bhoomi ke naam se pahchaana jaataa he ||
 ke niyantran se ek vishesh jaanch ki sthapna ki| esaiti jaanch-padtaal ke anusar is me littey kaa haath tha, is aarope ko ucchtam nyaayalay ne bhi pakkaa kiya|| aage jaachan lagane par pataa chalaa kii isame diemke dal kaa bhi haath tha| report ke anusaar diemke dal ne littey ko sharansthaan diya tha or is se unka kaam bahut aasaan ho gaya|
 kmishn report ke anusaar san 1989 ko aam raajnneetik jhukaav kaa sthayeekaran hone par bharat me tamil logo ke aapraadhik or raashtra virdohi karmo ko roak ne ke bajaay unhe protsaahn kiya gaya | is report se yah bhi gyaan pasta he kii japhna me littey ke netaaon ke kabje me kuch sankethik shabdon main badale hue sandesh the jinka vinimay diemke dal ke centre sarkaar or rajya sarkaar ke bich hua tha | is sandesh ko baad me sanket-vaachan karne par jaan pada kii yah rajeev gandhi kii htya se sambandhit the | november 1998 me report ke lik hone par congress party ne pradhan mantri aaike gujral kaa sanyukt morcha sarkaar gira diya | congress party ne diemke dal par rajeev gandhi kii htya kaa aarope lagaaya or is sanyukt morcha sarkaar se nikaal dene kii maang ki | esaiti jaanch report, nyaayamurti verma report or jainakmishan report chaan - bin karne ke baad yah samaapt kiya ja saktaa he ki rajeev gandhi kii htya ek shanik maamla nahi tha balki yah bady samajhadaari se yojit kiya gaya tha or is me shaamil hone vaale littey se badhkar dusre strot the |
