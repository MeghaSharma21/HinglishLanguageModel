 raav guzaramal singh raav nandaram singh putr the tathaa chandravanshi ahir shaasha the. raav guzaramal ke bade bhai raav bal kishan 24 farvari 1739 ko karnal yuddh main nadir shah ke viruddh ladate huye veergati ko praapt hue, unke baad raav guzaramal raja bane. raav bal kishan ki veerta or bahaduree ko dekhte hue nadir shah ne raav raaj gharaana ko "raav bahadur" kaa khitaab nawaja. raav guzaramal ke samay par ahir parivaar kii shakti charam seemaa par thi. unki jaagir main rewadi, jhajjar, dadri, haansi, hisar, kanaud, va narnaul aadi pramukh nagar shaamil the. guravarah va gokul garh ke kile isi kaal kii dein he. gokul sikaa mudra kaa prachaalan isi kaal main kiya gaya. apane pita ke naam stupa va jalashay kaa bhi nirmaan gujaramal ne karavaaya tha. unhone meerut ke brahanpur va morna tathaa rewadi main ramgarh, jaitpur va srinagar gaavon kii sthapana kii thi.
 farrukhnagar kaa baloch raja va hathi singh kaa vanshaj ghasera kaa bahadur singh donon raav guzaramal ke kaatar shatru the. bahadur singh, bharatpur ke jat raja surajmal se alag hokar swatantra shashan kar rahaa tha. tab raav guzaramal ne surajmal ke saath milakar use muhthod jawaab diya. guzaramal kaa bahadur singh ke sasur nimrana ke todermal se bhi maitreepurn sambandh tha. san 1750 me, todermal ne raav gujaramal ko bahadur singh ke kehane par aamantrit kiya ore dhokhe se unka vadh kar diya.
