 simulink, bahudomen vaale gatij tantron ke modelling, simulation evam vishleshan karne ke liye ek vyapaarik software-aujaar he. yah da matheworks dwaara nirmit he. isaki mukhya visheshta yah he ki yah block aarekh par aadhaarit graphic interphes pradaan karata he jisase modelling evam simeshan ke liye kisi program ke likhane kii aavashyaktaa nahin padati or prayokta ko bahut suvidha hoti he. isamen gatij tantron main prayog aane vaale vibhinn avyavon ke liye block kii vyavastha he. isaki block librari ko apni aavashyaktaa ke anuroop dhaala ja saktaa he. 
 niyantran tantra evam aankik sanket prasanskaran main simulink kaa bahutayat main prayog hota he.
