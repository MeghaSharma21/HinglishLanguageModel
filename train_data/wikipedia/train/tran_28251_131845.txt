 shirshabhisuchak vah ank he, jo khopadi kii chaudaai ko lambaai se bhaag dene par praapt bhaag fal main 100 se guna karne par praapt hota he. 
 khopadi kii chaudaai kaanon ke thik upar maapi jaati he or lambaai bhrumadhya se lekar pashchakapaal ke udagr bindu tak maapi jaati he. shirshabhisuchak, yadi 75 se kam hota he, to sir yaa khopadi dirghshirsk, yadi 75 se 80 ke madhya hota he, to khopadi madhyashirsk tathaa yadi 80 yaa isse adhik hota he, to khopadi laghushirsk, kehalaati he. 
 sweden ke e.e. retsius naamak maanavshastri ne is ank kaa suzav diya tha. maanav kii vibhinn prajatiyon main vibhed karne main shirshabhisuchak bada upyogi siddh hua he. maanav jati main yah ank 60 se 100 tak paaya jaataa he. khojon se siddh ho gaya he ki shirshabhisuchak vaataavaran se bahut prabhavit hota he. at: ab is ank kaa upyog bahut kam kiya jaataa he. yah kapaly suchak se, jo keval kapaal kii maap se sambandh rakhata he, bhinn hota he.
