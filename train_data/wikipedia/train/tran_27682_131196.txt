 parichay – abul fazal kaa pura naam abul fazal ebn mubarak tha. iskaa sambandh arab ke hijaaji parivaar se tha. iskaa janm 14 janvari 1551 main hua tha. iske pita kaa naam shaike mubarak tha. abul fazal ne akbarnama evam aaine akabari jaise prasiddh pustak kii rachana kii. praarambhik jivan – abul fazal kaa pura parivaar deshantaravaas kar pehle hi sindh aa chukaa tha. phir hindustan ke rajasthan main ajmer ke paas nagaur main hameshaa ke liye bas gaya. iskaa janm agra main hua tha. abul fazal bachapan se hi kaaphi pratibhashali balak tha. uske pita shaikh mubarak ne uski shiksha kii acchhee vyavastha kii shidhra hi vah ek gudh or kushal sameekshak vidwaan kii khyaati arjeet kar lee. 20 varsh kii aayu main vah shikshak ban gaya. 1573 i. main uskaa pravesh akbar ke darbar main hua. vah asaadhaaran pratibha, satark nishtha or vafadari ke bal par akbar kaa chaheta ban gaya. vah shidhra akbar kaa vishwasi ban gaya or shidhra hi pradhanmantri ke ohade tak pahunch gaya. abul fazal kaa itihaas lekhan – vah ek mahaan rajaneta, rajanayik or sounya general hone ke saath–saath usne apni pahchaan ek lekhak ke rup ne bhi vah bhi itihaas lekhak ke rup main banaai. usne itihaas ke parat-dar-parat ko ujagar kaa logon ke saamne laane kaa prayaas kiya. khaas kar uskaa khyaati tab or bad jaati he jab usne akbarnama or aaine akabari kii rachana kii. usne bhartiya mughalakalin samaaj or sabhyataa ko is pustak ke madhyam se bade hi achche tareeke se varnan kiya he.
