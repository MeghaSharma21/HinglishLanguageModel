 fatima bhutto janm naam, fatima murtaza bhutto, ek pakistani lekhika or patrkaar he. ve pakistan ke purv raashtrapati or pradhanmantri zulfikaar ali bhutto kii potee or purv pradhanmantri benazir bhutto kii bhatiji hai.
 fatima bhutto may 1982 ko afghanistan kii rajdhani kabul main us vaqt paidaa hui, jab uske pita mir murtaza bhutto deshnikale kaa jivan bitaa rahe the. unki maan fauzia fasihuddin bhutto afgani videsh mantralay main ek adhikari kii beti thim.
 fatima bhutto shaayara or lekhika hai. pakistan, america or britain ke kai akhbaaron main kollam bhi likhti hai. 1997 main pandrah saal kii umr main unka pehla kavy-sangrah oxford uniwarsity press, pakistan se prakashit hua tha, jiskaa tital Whispers of the Desert he. 2006 main duusari kitaab 8 oktober 2005 ko kashmir or suba sarhad main aae zalazale ke vishay par or 8:50a.m. 8October 2005 prakashit hui. teesri kitaab Songs of Blood and Sword prakashnadhin he. unka pehla upanyaas, The Shadow of the Crescent Moon prakashit hua he, jisamen afghanistan kii sarhad ke paas vazeerastaan ke janajaatiy kshetra main rahane vaale teen bhaaiyon or do auraton ke sangharsh kii kahani he.
