 hdaramout praant yaa hazaramout praant yaman kaa ek praant he. yah desh kaa sabase bada praant he or aitihaasik 'hdaramout' kshetra kaa bhaag he jahaan prachinkaal main kai shaktishaalee yemeni rajya ubhare the. san 2004 main suqutra kaa dweep samooh bhi adan praant se hataakar hdaramout praant kaa bhaag bana diya gaya. hdaramout praant kii samskruti padosi oman desh ke zofaar praant se milti he. in donon kshetron ke logon ko 'hdaramuti' bulaayaa jaataa he or yah dakshini arabi bhashaa kii hdaramuti upbhasha bolte hai.
 'hdaramout' ko arabi lipi main 'حضرموت‎' likhaa jaataa he jiskaa antim akshar 'ض‎' bhartiya upmahadweep, afghanistan or iran main 'za' kaa uccharan rakhata he, jabaki arab deshon main iskaa uccharan 'da' yaa 'dha' se miltaa-julata kiya jaataa he. isliye is kshetra or praant ke naam ko - 'hdaramout', 'hadharamout' or 'hazaramout' - teenon uccharanon ke saath paaya jaataa he. thik yahi uccharan kaa antar 'ramadan' shabd main bhi dekha jaataa he jo 'ramadan' or 'ramaadaan' donon roopon main miltaa he.
