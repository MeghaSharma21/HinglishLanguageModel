 krishnadas payahari ramanandi sampradaay ke pramukh acharya or kavi the. inkaa samay solahavin shatee i. kaha jaataa he. ye brahman the or jaipur ke nikat 'galata' naamak sthaan par rahete the or keval doodh pite the or isi kaaran inkaa naam payahari arthath pay + aahaari padaa. ye ramanand ke shishy anantanand ke shishy the or amer ke raja prithviraj kii rani bala bai ke dikshaguru the. kaha jaataa he ki inhone kapalik sampradaay ke guru chaturnath ko shastrarth main parajeet kiya tha isse inhen mahant kaa pad praapt hua tha. ye sanskrit bhashaa ke pandit the or brajabhasha ke kavi the. brahmagita, premasatvanirup inke mukhya granth hai. inke brajabhasha ke anek pad praapt hote hai. 
 kehete hai ek samay payahari krishnadas kii gupha ke saamne baagh aayaa to aapane usko atithi jaan, nevata dekar aatithyadharm-pratipalanpurvak apana pal katkar diya. is prakaar ke prasiddh yash ko aap jag main praapt hue.
