 kevalanwai, nyaayadarshan main ek prakaar kaa vishesh anumaan he. yahan hetu, saadhy ke saath sarvada sattatmak rup se hi sambhddha rahata he. nyaayadarshan ke anusaar vyapti do prakaar se ho sakati he- anvayamukhen tathaa vyatirekmukhen. anvay kaa arth he- tatsattve tatsatta arthath kisi vastu ke hone par kisi vastu kii sthiti, jaise dhooma ke rahane par agni kii sthiti. vyatirek vyapti vahaan hoti he jahaan hetu tathaa saadhy kaa sambandh nishedhmukhen siddh hota he. kevalanwai anumaan keval pratham vyapti ke upar hi aadhaarit rahata he. yatha:
 gyey kaa arth he gyaan kaa vishay hona. . abhidhey kaa arth he abhidha kaa vishay hona arthath vah padaarth jise ham koi naam de sakate hai. jagat kaa yah niyam he ki gyanvishay hote hi padaarth kaa koi na koi naam avashyamev diya jaataa he. yah vyapti sattatmak rup se hi siddh kii ja sakati he, nishedhmukhen nahin, kyonki koi bhi aisa padaarth nahin he jisko naam na diya ja sake. arthath abhidheyabhaav ko ham gyeyabhav ke saath drushtaant ke abhaav main kathampi sambhddha nahin siddh kar sakate. isaliye uuparavaala nigaman keval anvayavyapti ke aadhaar par hi siddh kiya ja saktaa he. isiliye ye anumaan keplanwai kehalaata he.
