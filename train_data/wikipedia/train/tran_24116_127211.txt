 macidonia, aadhikarik rup se macidonia ganraajya dakshini europe ke madhya balcon prayadweep par sthit ek desh he. yah kabhi yugoslavia kaa bhaag hua karata tha, jisase 1991 main isane swatantraaa kii ghoshnaa kii. yah 1993 main sanyukt raashtra kaa sadsy bana, lekin yunaan ke saath naam ke vivaad kii vajah se ise purv yugoslav ganraajya macidonia ke asthai naam se pravesh diya gaya.
 yah ek landlock desh he, jisaki seemaa uttarpashchim main kosovo, uttar main srbija, purv main bulgaria, dakshin main greece or pashchim main albania se milti he. isaki rajdhani scopje he, anya shaharon main bitola, kumanova, prilip, oharid, veles, stip, cokani, gostivaar or strumika shaamil hai. yahaan 50 se zyada jhilein or 2000 meter se oonche 16 parvat hai. macidonia sanyukt raashtra or europe ke parishad kaa sadsy he. disambar 2005 se yah europea sangh main shaamil hone ke liye ummidavaar he, iske alaava nato sadsyta ke liye bhi aavedan diya hua he. yahaan kii macidonian bhashaa or albanian bhashaa mukhya- or rajbhashaayen hai.
