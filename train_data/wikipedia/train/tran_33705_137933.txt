 kanpur nagar zila bhartiya rajya uttar pradesh kaa ek jila he. iskaa mukhyalay kanpur he. yah zila kanpur mandal kaa hissaa he. 1977 main is jile se kanpur dehaat jila alag ho gaya. 1979 main punah in donon jilon kaa ekikaran hua or ek baar phir 1981 main donon jile alag ho gaye.
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar kanpur nagar jile kii aabaadi 4,572,951 he. yah ise bharat ke 640 jilon main 32vaa sthaan detaa he. is jile kaa ghanatv 1449 prati varg kimi he. 2001-2011 ke dauraan jansankhya main 9.72% kii vruddhi darj kii gayi. yahan kii linganupaat dar prati 1000 purashon par 852 mahilaon kii he. sakshartaa dar 81.31% he.
 audyogic drashtikon se dekha jaae to bharat main chennai ke baad chamade kaa sabase jaada utpaadan kanpur main hi hota he. yahi kaaran he ki kanpur nagar ko ledar city ke naam se janaa jaataa he, or uttar bharat kaa manchester bhi kaha jaataa he.
 yahan lagbhag 400 chamade kii tenries he tathaa 1000 gharelu ikaiyaan he. jaha mukhyatah bhais ke chamade kaa kaary hota he. yahan 1200 karod rupee kaa vaarshik saamagree chamada taiyaar kiya jaataa he. 6500 karod rupee ke chamade kaa vaarshik niryaat kiya jaataa he tathaa 1000 karod rupe kaa salanaa niryaat gharelu ikaiyon dwaara kiya jaataa he.
 yahan sabase jaada tenriyaan jajmau main he jo kii tenariyon kaa garh ke naam se jaananaa jaataa he. yahan kii tenariyo kaa ganda pani va kachra ganga nadi main choda jaataa he jo kii ganga nadi ke pani ko bahut ganda karata he. aane vaale samay main chamada udyog kii ore se 400 karod rupe kaa jalshodhak plant kaa nirmaan prastaavit he.
