 paraasaran do bhinn sandrata vaale gholon ke bich honevaali ek vishesh prakaar kii visaran kriya he jo ek ardhapargamya jhilli ke dwaara hoti he. isamen vilaayak ke anu kam sandrata vaale ghol se adhik sandrata vaale ghol kaa ore gati karte hai. yah ek bhautik kriya he jisamen gholk ke anu bina kisi bahya urja ke prayog ke ardhapargamya jhilli se hokar gati karte hai. viley ke anu gati nahin karte hai kyonki ve donon gholon ke alag karne vaali ardhapargamya jhilli ko paar nahin kar pate hai. paraasaran main urja mukt hoti he jiske prayog se ped-paudhon ke badhate jad chattaanon ko bhi tod detii hai.
 paraasaran- vah kriya jiske falswaroop ardhaparagamya jhilli se hokar vilaayak ke anu kam sandratha vaale ghol se adhik sandratha kii or jate he paraasaran kriya kehalaati he.
