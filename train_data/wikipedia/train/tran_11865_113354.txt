 shrimadbhagwata evam vishnu puraana ke anusaar brahma ji kii vanshavali ke raja ajamidh kii peedi main kuru raja ke naam se sampoorn kurukshetra janaa jaataa he. kuru ek atyant pratapi raja hue hai jo mahabharat main kauravon ke pitamah the, unhi ke naam se kuru pracheen bharat ke 16 mahajanapadon main se ek tha. iskaa kshetra aadhunik haryana ke ird-gird tha. isaki rajdhani sambhawatah hastinapur yaa indraprastha thi. kuru ke rajya kaa vistaar kahan tak rahaa hoga ye bharatiyon ke liye khoj kaa vishay he kintu vishwa itihaas main kuru Cyrus the Great pracheen vishwa ke sarvaadhik vishaal samrajya ko nae aayam dene vaala chakravarti raja tha jisane iran main maid samrajya ko apane hi sasur astyagas se hastantarit karte hue samrajya kaa nayaa naam ajamidh samrajya rakhaa tha. is samrajya kii simaayen bharat se mishra, libya or greek tak faili hui thi.
