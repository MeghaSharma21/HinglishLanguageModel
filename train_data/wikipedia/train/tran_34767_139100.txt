 muhalla yaa mohalla kisi nagar, qasbe, yaa gaanv main aise simaankit bhugolic kshetra ko kehete hai jahaan ke nivasee ek-duusare ke nikat rahete hai or dainik rup se ek-duusare ke sammukh hote hai. "samajashastriyon main molllon kii koi sarvasammt paribhaasha nahin he. sthaneey rup se muhalle vishesh bhugolic kshetra or kaaryaatmak drushti se samaajik rishton ke sanjalo ke samooh hote hai. muhalle aisi sthaneey ikai hote hai jahaan sammukh hokar log baatcheet or anya antahkriyaayein karte hai. yah ve nijee sthaan va mauqe hote hai jahaan nivasee saanjhe mulyon ko jari rakhte hai, yuvaaon ko saamaajik dhara se jodate hai or saamaajik niyantran banaae rakhte hai." aadhunik bharat ke kuch kshetron main muhalle ke liye angrezi kaa colony shabd bhi prayog hone laga he.
 samaajon main log aksar sabase adhik apane muhallon main hi ek-duusare ko prabhavit karte hai. isliye kai deshon or rajyon main kaanoon, shiksha, saamaajik kaary, vikaas, swasthya, vidyut, jal, kooda or anya sevaayen muhallon ke aadhaar par hi uplabdh banaai jaati hai. kuch kshetron main shaharon ko aupchaarik rup se muhallon main baanta jaataa he:
