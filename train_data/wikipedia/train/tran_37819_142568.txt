 imperial ikaiyaan britain main armbh hui ek maapan pranali he jo samay ke saath-saath un sabhi deshon main fail gai thi jahaan british samrajya kaa vistaar tha. isaki ikaiyon main foot, mile, pound or acre shaamil hai. inhen prayog karne vaale adhikansh desh is pranali ko aupchaarik rup se chhodkar metry paddati apana chuke hai, lekin anaupchaarik rup se isaki ikaiyon kaa prayog abhi bhi jari he. masalan bharat kaa sarkari lambaai naapane kaa maap meter va centimetre he lekin loksamskruti main abhi bhi logon kaa kad futon va inchon main bataaya jaataa he.
