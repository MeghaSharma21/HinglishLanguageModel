 kisi vaigyanik jigyaasa ke samaadhaan ke liye usase sambandhit kshetra main or adhik aankade ekatra karne ji aavashyaktaa hoti he. in aankadon kii prapti ke liye jo kuch kiya jaataa he use prayog kehete hai. prayog, vaigyanik vidhi kaa pramukh stambh he. prayog karnaa evam aankade praapt karnaa isaliye bhi jaruri he taaki siddhanth ke pratipaadan main kahin purvaagrah yaa pakshpaat aade na aa jaane.
 kisi kshetra ke gahan adhyayan evam gyaan ke liye prayog kaa bahut mahatv he. prakritik evam saamaajik dono hi vigyaanon main prayog kii mahati bhoomika he. vyaavhaarik samasyaon ke samaadhaan main, kam gyaat kshetron ke or adhik jaankaree prapti ke liye tathaa saiddhanthik manyataon kii jaanch ke liye prayog karne kii jarurat padati rahati he.
 kuch prayog isaliye nahin kiye ja sakate ki ve bahut mahange ho sakate hai, bahut bhayankar ho sakate hai yaa unhen karnaa naitik drushti se maanya nahin he.
