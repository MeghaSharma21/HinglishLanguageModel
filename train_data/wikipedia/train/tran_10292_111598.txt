 prashasnic rup se malaysia ek sangheey raashtra he, jo teraa rajyon or teen sangheey kshetron main sangathit he. malay bhashaa main rajyon ko "negery" kehete hai, jo sanskrit ke "nagar" shabd se utpann he. sangheey kshetron ko malay main "vilaayaah persecutuan" kehete hai jo arabi bhashaa ke "vilaayat" shabd se aayaa he. malaysia ke gyaarah rajya or do sangheey kshetra dakshinpurv asia kii mukhyabhumi main malay prayadweep par sthit he, jo kshetra pashchimi malaysia kehalaata he. baaqi ke do rajya or ek sangheey kshetra borneo dweep par sthit hai or yah samuhik rup se purvi malaysia kehalaate hai.
