 purananusaar ye saat nagar yaa tirth jo mokshadaayak kahe gayi hai. ve hai:-
 hindu dharm ke anusaar, ayodhyadi saaton puriyaan kaashi main varttman he, aisa puraanon kaa vachan he or isi aadhaar par kaashi main jin-jin sthanon main unki sansthiti he, vahaan unpuriyon kii yatra hoti he. yah yatra nity karne kaa vidhaan he, parantu, is yatra kii visheshta yah he ki isamen kis ritu main kis puri kii yatra karnaa chahie, iskaa bhi nirdesh he. brahamvaivarttapurana30 ke anusaar shankhodwar ke paas dwarka he. yahan kii yaktra varsha main, bindumadhav ke paas vishnukanchi he, vahaan kii yatra sharad ritu main, someshwar ke vivyakon main ramkund par ayodhya he jahaan rameshwar naam kaa shivlinga he. vahaan kii yatra greeshm ritu main, asisangam par gangadwar, arthath haridwar he, jahaan kii yatra shishir ritu main, vriddhakaal se krittivaseshwar tak ujjayini athwa avantika he, jahaan kii yatra hemanth ritu main, uttarark se uttar varna nadi tak mathura he, jahaan kii yatra vasantaritu main hoti he. kaashi or shivakanchi to kaashi main vyaapt hi he.
