 
 gyanpith puraskaar
 bhalchandra nemade bhartiya marathi lekhak, upanyaaskaar, kavi, sameekshak tathaa shikshavid hai. 1963 main keval 25 varsh kii aayu main prakashit 'kosla' naamak upanyaas se unhen apaar safalataa mili. san 1991 main unki tikaswayamvar is kriti ke liye unhen sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. unhen varsh 2014 kaa pratishthit gyanpith puraskaar diya jayega.shri nemade kii pramukh kritiyon main 'kosla' or 'hindu' upanyaas shaamil hai. unke sahitya main 'deshivaad' par bal diya gaya he. vah 60ke dashak ke laghu patrika andolan ke pramukh hastakshar the.
 praadhyaapak bhalchandra nemade marathi ke prasikad lekhak ve.sa. khandekar, vi.vaa. shirvarkar, win.da. karandikar ke baad yah puraskaar praapt karne vaale chauthe marathi lekhak hai. pro. bhalchandra nemade marathi sahitya main sarvasparshi tathaa sarvapratishthit naam he. upanyaas, kavita evam aalochana main unki viral khyaati he. shri nemaade marathi aalochana main 'desiwad' ke pravartak hai. 1963 main prakashit 'kosla' upanyaas ne marathi upanyaas lekhan main disha pravartan kaa kaam kiya. is upanyaas ne marathi gadhy lekhan ko pichhali aadhi shatabdi main lagaataar chetana or form ke str par udwelit kiya. unke anya upanyaas 'hindu' main sabhyataa vimarsh upasthit he. yah kriti kaal kii aavdharna kii vaigyanik drushti se bhashya karti he. gramin se aadhunik parisar tak kii saamaajik visangatiyon ko gaharaai se abhivyakt karne vaale shri nemaade marathi sahitya kii teen pidiyon ke sarvapriya lekhak hai.
