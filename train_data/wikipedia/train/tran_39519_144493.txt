 asaf ali; Asaf Ali: ek bhartiya swatantraaa senaanee or prasiddh bhartiya vakil the. vah bharat se sanyukt rajya america ke pehle rajdoot the. inhone odisha ke rajyapal ke rup main bhi kaary kiya tha asaf ali kaa janm 11 may 1888 isvi ko british bharat ke sihara uttar pradesh main hua tha.
 1914 main, bhartiya muslim samudaay par british samrajya par ek bada prabhaav padaa. asaf ali ne turki khilaafat paksh kaa samarthan kiya or pravi council se istipha de diya. unhone ise asahyog ke ek adhinium ke rup main dekha or disambar 1914 main bharat laut aaye. bharat lautne ke baad, asaf ali rashtravaadi andolan main ek andolankaaree rup se shaamil ho gaye.1935 main muslim rashtravaadi party ke sadsy ke rup main inhen kendriya vidhaan sabha ke liye chunaa gaya tha. vah muslim lig ke ummidavaar ke khilaaf congress ke ummidavaar ke rup main phir se nirvaachit hue the or inhen upaadhyaksh ke rup main chunaa gaya tha.
 1 9 28 main, inhone 21 varsh kii aruna aasaf ali se shaadi kii, . bharat chodo andolan, 1 9 42 ke dauraan mumbai main gowalia tanks maidan par bhartiya rashtriya congress dhvaj ko ugaahne ke liye unhen vyapak rup se yaad kiya jaataa he.
 asaf ali 2 sitambar 1946 se jawaharlal nehru kii adhyakshata main bharat sarkaar ke antarim sarkaar main railway or parivahan ke prabharee the. inhone farvari 1 9 47 se farvari 1949 se 1949 tak america ke bharat ke pehle rajdoot ke rup main kaary kiya.
 1 april 1953 isvi 64 varsh kii aayu main main switzraland main bharat ke rajdoot ke rup main seva karne ke dauraan, bern duutavas karyaalay main nidhan ho gaya tha. 1989 main, bharat dak ne unke sammaan main ek ek dak tikat jari kiya tha. unki patni aruna asaf ali ko bharat ke sarvocch naagarik puraskaar- bharat ratna se sammanit kiya gaya tha.
