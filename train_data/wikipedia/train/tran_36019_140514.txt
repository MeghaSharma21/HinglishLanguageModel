 pro ram prakaash ko 1949 main bharat se sarkaar dwaara marishas bulaayaa gaya tha. unke hindi, sanskrit, french or angreji gyaan ke samaksh sara marishas natmastak tha. unhone sarkari niti ko kaaryrup dekar schuli paathyakram main rakhaa. desh main pehali baar hindi chaatr adhyapakon kaa safal prayog kiya gaya. aaj unke dwaara sampadit naveen hindi paathya-pustak maala ko desh kii sarvottam praathmik kakshaon kii paathya-pustak mana jaataa he.
