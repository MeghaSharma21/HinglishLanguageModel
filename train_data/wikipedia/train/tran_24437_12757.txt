 pattee sanvahneey padpon kaa ek ang he. pattiyaan or tana sammilit rup se praroh banaate hai. 
 pattiyaan parvasandhiyon se nikalati hai. inke kaksh main ek kaksh-kalika hoti he. pattiyon main prakaash-sanshleshan kii mahatvapurn kriya hoti he. vashpotsargan kii kriya bhi pattiyon main hoti hai. saadharantah pattiyaan do prakaar kii hoti hai, saral pattee evam sanyukt pattee.
