 ajit paal singh bharat ke purv hockey khilaadi rahe hai. ajit centre half pojishan par khelte the. varsh 1975 ke hockey vishwa cap vijeta team ke captain rahe ajit paal ko bharat sarkaar ne 1992 main padmashri purushkaar se sammanit kiya. unhone 1968 se 1976 ke madhyam teen olympic main bharat kaa pratinidhitiv kiya, is main se do olympic main bharat ne kaansya padak praapt kiya. bhartiya olympic sangh ne 2012 main captain ajit paal singh ko landan olympic ke liye bhartiya dal kaa pramukh niyukt kiya gaya.
