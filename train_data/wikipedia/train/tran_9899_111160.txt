 kendriya sadak anusandhan santhaan kii sthapana ] kii ek pramukh rashtriya prayogshaala ke rup main 1948 main vaigyanik tathaa audyogic anusandhan parishad kii ek angabhoot prayogshaala ke rup main kii gai I santhaan kii sthapana kaa uddeshy sadakon tathaa hawaii pattiyon kaa abhikalp, nirmaan evam rakrakhaav, bade tathaa madhyam shaharon kii yatayaat tathaa parivahan aayojana, vibhinn kshetron main sadakon kaa prabandh, marginal saamagree kaa sudhaar, sadak nirmaan main audyogic apshisht kii upyogita, bhu skhalan niyantran, bhu sudhaar paryaavarniya pradooshan tathaa sadak yatayaat suraksha par anusandhan tathaa vikaas pariyojnaon par kaary karnaa he I santhaan bharat tathaa videshon main vibhinn upyogkarta sanghathanon ko takneekee tathaa paraamarsh sevaayen pradaan karata he I santhaan sadak tathaa ran-ve pariyojnaon ke kaary nishpadit karne ke liye 1962 se jan samooh tak anusandhan tathaa vikaas uplabdhiyon ke prachaar prasar ke liye rashtriya tathaa anterrashtriya prashikshan kaaryakramon/punargrahan shikshan paathyakramon ko yojit karne kaa samarthy rakhata he I mahamarg abhiyantriki ke kshetron main maanav sansaadhan kii kshamta banane ke liye santhaan ke paas rashtriya tathaa anterrashtriya prashikshan kaaryakramon ko yojit karne kii kshamta he I
 siesaiaar kendriya sadak anusandhan santhaan kii sthapana bharat sarkaar ke vaigyanik evam audyogic anusandhan parishad kii ek ghatak prayogshaala ke rup main san 1952 main dilli main kii gai. santhaan rajmarg or sadak parivahan praudyogiki ke pramukh kshetron main vyavsaay ko ucch gunvattta or vishwa str par sweekaarya anusandhan or paraamarsh sevaayen pradaan karata he. santhaan ke paanch anusandhan evam vikaas kshetra tathaa chaar avasanrachana takneekee sahaayta kshetra he. siesaiaar - siararai ek iesao 9001 praaanpatr praapt santhaan he.
 santhaan main anusandhan or vikaas kshetron main sadak or sadak parivahan ke sabhi pahaluon par anusandhan or vikaas kaary sampann kiye jate hai. inaki gatividhiyon ko paanch pramukh kshetron main samoohikrut kar sakate hai, jaise kutitom abhiyantriki or saamagree, bhootkneekee abhiyantriki, sethu or sanrachanaayen, yatayaat or parivahan yojana, sadak vikaas yojana evam prabandhan. santhaan pryojit or anubandh anusandhan kaary karata he or sabhi sarkari or nijee kshetra ke sanghathanon ke liye paraamarsh sevaayen pradaan karata he.
