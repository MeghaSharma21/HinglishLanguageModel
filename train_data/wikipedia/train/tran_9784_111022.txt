  jefriarchar 
 jefri howard archer, baaran archer off veston-soupar-mer angreji lekhak or purv raajnneetijna hai. lekhak banane ke pehale ve sansad ke sadsy the, lekin ek vittiya khottale ke vachah se unhen istipha dena pada, jo unhen lagbhag deewaala bana diya. baad main apane lokapriy kitabon ke royaltis se vah phir se dhani ban gayi or kancerwaytive party ke up adhykksh bhi ban gayi. baad main phir se unhen istipha dena padaa or unka rajnatik jivan us khatnaa se khatam ho gaya. unhe 2001 -2003 main jail bhi jaan pada. unke pustaken ab tak kam se kam do crores pacchaas lakh kappio duniya bhar main bik chuke hai.
 jefri hoverd archer kaa janm city lanton maternity aspatal main hua tha. archer ne apnebachapan ke mukhya bhaag vestan-soupar mer, somerset main bitaayaa. unako ek bada bhai hai, david brun. jab archer paidaa hua tab unke pita kaa umr tha 64. jab ve balak tha, unka ye sapana tha ki wo bristole rovers futbol club kaa captain bane. 1951 main, vah wellington school somarset ko ek chaatravrutti jeeta.. is samay unki maan lola, ne "ti kps" naamak ek stambh yogadan westen supar moer main sthaneey press ako diya he or or uske bete "tuppens" ke sahasik kary ke bare main likhi he, isi karan archer ne wellington skool main badmaashi ke shikaar banate he. archer skool chhodane ke baad, vah sena ke saath or pulice ke liye prashikshan sahit naukariyon main kuch maheenon ke liye kaam kiya. baad main archer shaareerik shiksha ke adhyaaapak banate he.
 archer ne teen saal oxphod yunivercity departmend four kantinuving atukeshan se shaikshanik yogyata praapt karne ke liye pathai kiya. e yogyata oxoford ke brasanos kaalaj se milte the, lekin unhone kabhi purv snaatak ke rup main vaho dakhil nhiin hue the. archer par yah aarope lagaaya gaya tha ki ve apane shaikshanik yogyata ke bare main galat jankaree di thi, jaise ki ek amareeki bodi building club ko apane pathai ke santhaan ke rup main pesh kiya. un par yah aarope bhi tha ki ve apane teen e-level ke bare main bhi aksar kahata tha, jo unako kabhi nahin mila tha. archer apane web sat par oxford ke principal ke bare main likhaa hai, lekin apni siksha na puraa hone ke bare main kuch nahin bataate hai.
 oxford main vah kayik abhyaas, jaise kii tej daudna or kudana, main maahir tha. parantu yah sposhat nahin tha ki agar vovishvavidhyalay ke pratinidhi ban sakate the, kyon ki ve kaalaj main daakhil nahin hue the. archer ne ek baar khel-kud pratiyogita main england kaa pratinidhi bane or safal bhi hue |archer ne oxpho charity ke liye, vishwa vikhyath beatiles ke sahaayta se dhan samaharan kiya tha. beatiles ne unka aamantran sweekaar kiya or brasnos kalaj gaya or vidhyaarthiyon ke saath tasviir khichvaaya| kalaj ke dinon main archer, meri torine vidn ko mili, jo baad main unke patni bani. meri oxford ke sennt ans kalaj main rasatantr ke vidyarthini thi. unak vivaah july 1966 main hue. meri baad main sourorj main visheshgya bani.
 oxford chhodne ke baad, unhone deshiy janamadin nyaas ke saath, charity ke liye dhan samaharan kaa kaam zaari rakhaa. deshiy janamadin nyaas ek aushadhik charity hai. unka rajniatik jivan kaa shruwaad bhi is samay hua, jab ve 1967-70 main gratr lantn kauncil main kansarvative kaunsilr kaa bhoomika nibhaayaa.ek sansdha jin main ve kaam kar rahe the, jinka naam hai yunatd naishans associyeshan, unke khilaf yah aarope lagaaya ki ve galat jaanakri dekar paise nikaal rahe hai. is bare main akhbaar main bhi khabar chhap gayi. is dauraan archar ne kancerwaytive party ke sansad sadsy hanfri berkeley ke khilaaf manahani kaa mukkadama darj kiya, jo teen saal ke baad adaalat ke baahar niptaya gaya. berkeley sansadeey chunaav main archar ke ummidavaari ke khilaaf the lekin archer hi ummidavaar bane.1969 main archer apane company, aaro anterpraces kaa sthapana kiya, jo dhan samaaharan tathaa jan-sampark ke liye kaam karanevaale compani tha. unhone meyafer main ek chitra dirdha bhi khulaa, jo aadhunik kala ke pradarshan karte the. is chitra dirdha main archar ne paise khoye or aakhir main unhone e bech diya.
 untiis varshiya archer 1969 main ek up-chunaav main laut nirvachan kshetra ke linkanshair se sansad ke sadsy chunaa gaya. yah pehle se hi kancervettive party ke hi the. laut niravachan kshetra main teen mukhya pradesh the - laut, clitorps or inmmingam. jab archer sansad ke sadsysansad ke sadsy the, ve hameshaa inmmingam kancerwaytive club jate the. unhone 1970 main kenneti memmoril test main bhaag liya, jo laut se scagness or waapas daudne yaa chalane kaa ek mukqabla the.
 sansad main unhone apane hi party ke khilaaf aawaaj udaate the. sansad main unhone apane hi party ke khilaaf kai baar aawaaj udaaye the. unhone vruddh janon ke liye doordarshan anugya muft banane ke liye kaam karte the. iske alaava ve sangrahalay par lagu kiye jane vaale keemat ke khilaaf the. archer mrutyu dand ke bhi khilaaf the, jisko ve bahut krur maanate the. kein and abel kaa drushya avishkaar 1985 main si bi es mein dikhaaya gaya, jis main peter stross or sam nil ne mukhya kirdaar nibhaaye. ek vittiya khotale ke vachah se unako apane pad se istipha dena pada|
 archer kaa pratham kitaab he, note e penni mor, note e penni les, jo pehle yu es e main prakashit hue the or baad main ingland main 1976 main prakashit hui. yah kitaab atyant safal hua tha. is kitaab kaa shabd avishkaar 1980 main bi bi si radio 4 main aayaa tha or iskaa drushya aavishkaar bi bi si main 1990 main prasarit hua. kein and abel archer kaa sab se safal kitaab hai. agle saal archer kaa ek or kitaab fest emang icwals likhaa jo chaar vyaktiyon ke bare main hai, jinka lakshya england ke pradhan mantri bananaa tha. jab wo jail main the tab unhone e prisun dairi or kai anya kitaaben bhi likhe the.
 jeffri archer par bhi chaar naataken likhaae ja chuke hai.
 2000 ke meyar ke chunaav ke liye archer ko ummidavaar chunaa gaya. iske liye unako do purv pradhan mantriyon ke sahaayta mili. ye do purv pradhan mantriyan the ladi tachar or jone meyajar| unke sahaayta ke baavjut archer chunaav main nahin lad sakaa, kyonki us vakt unke khilaaf kuch aarope uda| is aarope ke dauraan hue mukkadame main wo shikshit hue or chaar saal ke liye jail bheja gaya. 2003 main archer jail se chhot gaya. filhaal ab unako raajnneeti main waapas aane main koi dilchaspee nahin rahaa, aisa unka kahana hai.
