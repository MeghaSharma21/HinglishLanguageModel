 shraddha arya ek bhartiya abhinetri he.unka janm nai dilli,bharat.me 17 agast 1978 ko hua. or ab vah 30 varsh kii ho chuki he.
 shraddha arya ne doordarshan main apana pehla kaary varsh 2004 main ek gun khojane vaale kuch lokapriy dharawahik se shuru kiya tha. jiskaa naam he, indiyas besat sine staras ki khoj kii pratibhaagi rahi he.or main lakshmi tere angan kii,tumhaari paakhi,dream girl or kundli bhagya. jaisai dharawahiko ke alaava kai sari filmon main bhi kaary kiya he.
