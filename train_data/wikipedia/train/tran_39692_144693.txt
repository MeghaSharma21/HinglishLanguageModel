 osiyaan jodhpur-bikaner rajmarg kii duusari disha main registan main sthit he. is pracheen nagar-kshaitra kii yatra ke dauraan bich-bich main padate hue registani vistaar va chote-chote gaanv ateet ke laharaate hue bhu-bhaagon main le jate hai.
 osiyaan main sundar taraashe hue jain va brahmanon ke mandir he. inamen se sabase asaadhaaran hai aarambh kaa surya mandir, kaali mandir, sachchiyaya mata mandir,piplad mata mandir or bhagwaan mahavir kaa mukhya mandir he.osiyaan main shri guru jambheshwar bhagwaan kaa mandir bhi he. guru jambheshwar bhagwaan kaa mandir osiyaan railway stesan ke paas hai.
