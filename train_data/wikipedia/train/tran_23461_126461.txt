 desh ke paanchave kumbh ke rup me "rajim-kumbh" ko desh ke vishisht sadhu santo ne apni manyata pradaan kii he. yah is aitihaasik nagari rajim kaa saubhaahgya he. nawapaara nagar me maa durga ke anek mandir he. jiname latarrapaara bajrang chowk me sthit shri singhvaasini durga mandir uname se ek he. bataaya jaataa he ki latarrapaara me 70-80 varsh purana shri hanuman mandir ek chote se chabutre me sthit tha. san 1962 se nirantar latarrapaara me kwanr navaratri me saarvajanik durgotsav samiti kaa gathan kar maa durga kaa adamkad aakarshak pratimaa sthapit kii jaati he.
