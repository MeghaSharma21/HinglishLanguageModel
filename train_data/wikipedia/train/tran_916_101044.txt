 general raahil sharif navambar 2013 se pakistan ke 15wein senadhyaksh hai. 29 navambar 2013 ko pakistan sena ke 15wein sena pramukh ke rup main unhone padbhaar sambhala tathaa sevanivrutt ho rahe general ashfaaq parvaiz kayaani kaa sthaan liya. iske pehle ve inspector general ke pad par rahe hai. unhen pakistan ke nishaan-e-imtiyaaz va hilaal-e-imtiyaaz naamak sammaanon se nawaaza ja chukaa he. pakistani senadhyaksh ke rup main unhen chaar sitara padavi hasil he.
