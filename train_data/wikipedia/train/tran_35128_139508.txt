 jinaan janavaadee ganraajya china ke purvi bhaag main sthit shandong praant kii rajdhani he. is kshetra kii chini sabhyataa ke shuruyaat se hi ek ahm bhoomika rahi he or ab jinaan ek bada prashasnic, aarthik or yatayaat centre he. 2010 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 68,14,000 thi jisamen se 32,45,000 shahari naagarik or baaqi gramin kshetron ke nivasee the. china kii prashasan pranali main is shahar ko "up-prantiy nagar' kaa darja hasil he.
 'jinaan' kaa naam aitihaasik ji nadi se aayaa tha. yah shahar ke uttar main baha karti thi or 'jinaan' kaa matlab 'ji se dakshin' he. 19veen sadi ke bich main pili nadi ke rukh badalane se ji nadi gaayab ho gai. is nagar ko 'chashmon kaa shahar' bhi kaha jaataa he kyonki shahar ke bich main or ird-gird ke ilaaqon main bahut se pani ke chashme hai.
