 thar marusthal bharat ke uttarpashchim main tathaa pakistan ke dakshinpurv main sthitahai. yah adhikansh to rajasthan main sthit he parantu kuch bhaag haryana, punjab,gujarat or pakistan ke sindh or punjab praanton main bhi failaa he. aravali pahari ke pashchimi kinare par thar marusthal sthit he. yah marusthal baaluu ke tibbon se dhankaa hua ek tarangit maidan he.
 thar marusthal adbhut he. garmiyon main yahaan kii ret ubalati he. is marubhumi main saath degree celcius tak taapmaana ricard kiya gaya he. jabaki sardiyon main taapmaana shunya se neeche chalaa jaataa he. garamiyon main marusthal kii tej hawaaen ret ke tilon ko ek sthaan se duusare sthaan par le jaati hai or tilon ko nai akritiyaan pradaan karti hai.
 jan-jivan ke naam par marusthal main milon dur koi-koi gaanv miltaa he. pashupaalan yahaan kaa mukhya vyavsaay he. do-chaar saal main yahaan kabhi barish ho jaati he. keekar, tint,phogada, khejadi or rohida ke vriksh kahin-kahin dikhaai dete hai. indira nahar ke madhyam se kai kshetron main jal pahunchane kaa prayaas aaj bhi jari he. 
 marusthal main kai jahrile saanp, bichchhu or anya keede hote hai.
 rajasthan main maru samaaroh - farvari main poornamasi ke din padane vaala ek manohar samaaroh he. teen din tak chalane vaale is samaaroh main pradesh kii samrudh samskruti kaa pradarshan kiya jaataa he.
 prasiddh gaiyr va agni nartak is samaaroh kaa mukhya aakarshan hote he. pagadi baandhne va maru shri kii pratiyogitaayen samaaroh ke utsaah ko dugana kar detii he. sam baalu ke tilon kii yatra par samaapan hota he, vahaan oont kii sawari kaa anand uthaa sakate hai or poornamasi kii chandani raat main tilon kii suramya prishthbhumi main lok kalakaaron kaa utkrisht kaarykram hota he.
