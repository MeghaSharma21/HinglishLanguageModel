 big efem, ek rashtravyapee nijee efem radio stesan he. is radio stesan ke maalik prasidd bhartiya vyavsaayee anil ambani hai. yah radio stesan 92.7 megaherts efem band fricuency par prasaarit hota he. vartmaan main yah stesan 44 vibhinn shaharon main prasaarit hota he. yah ekmaatr aisa radio stesan he jo ki jammu or kashmir main bhi prasaarit hota he.
 1 july 2008 se big efem singapore main bhi apana prasaaran shuru kar diya. singapore main yah subah 5 baje se rat 8 baje tak har rose 96.3 megaherts par prasaarit hota he.
