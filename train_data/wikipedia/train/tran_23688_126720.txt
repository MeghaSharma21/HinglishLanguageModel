 1927 me santhaapit iberia spain kii rajkeey vimaan seva he madrid me sthit yah erlines apane mukhya aadhaar sthalon barjas erport or barsilona el praat erport se kai saare antarashtriya gantavyon ke liye udaane sanchalit karti he. 
 iberia riginal or iberia express ke saath milakar yah iberia samooh kaa nirmaan karti he. iberia samooh 39 se adhik deshon ke 90 se adhik gantavyon tak seva pradaan karata he. kai anya erlines ke saath code samjhaute ke aadhaar par yah 90 duusare gantavyon tak kii yatra suvidha bhi pradaan pradaan karata he.
 8 april 2010 ko yah soochna saarvajanik kii gayi ki british erlines ke saath iberia ke vilay ke liye samjhauta ho gaya or is vilay ke parinaam swaroop aamadani ke aadhaar par vishwa ke tisare sabase bade erlines samooh kaa nirmaan hoga.
 29 november 2011 ko donon erlines ke share dharakon ne is vilay ko manzuri de di. janvari 2011 me nayi sanyukt sanstha internshnal erlines group kii sthapana kii gayi haalaanki donon erlines ab bhi apane brands nem se hi sevaayen sanchalit karti hai. 
 is erlines kaa mukhya karyaalay em ve. 49 bijnes park madrid me sthit he 2013 me is erlines me kharchon me kami kii lihaaj se madrid ke salmenka district ke kampos welzaquz sthit apana purana mukhya karyaalay badal diya tha.
 3 april 2001 ko iberia kaa nijeekaran kar diya gaya or yah madrid ke ibics-35 stock index me shaamil kar lee gayi.
 iske pramukh share dharak the - kaja madrid– 23.45%, british erways 13.2%, es i pi ai– 5.2%, el corte inglas– 2.90%. 
 baad me british airways ne iberia me american erlines kii bakaayaa saajhedaari ko 13 million pound chukaa kar kharid liya. iske baad isaki iberia me saajhedaari 10% ke aasapaas pahunch gayi or ise
 iske board me do nideshak niyukt karne kaa adhikaar bhi praapt ho gaya.
 july 2008 me british airways or iberia ne paraspar vilay kii ghoshnaa kii, april 2010 me vilay ko antim rup diya gaya or july 2010 me european kamishan or united stats department of transportesan ne ise apni anumati pradaan kii or saath hi american erlines ke saath iske margon ke taratamya ko saadhane kaa kaam shuru kiya gaya. 6 aktubar 2010 se british airways, iberia or american erlines ke gathbandhan ne aupchaarik rup se kam shuru kar diya. is gathbandhan ke parinamswaroop british airways ko salanaa 330 million pound kii bachat hoti he saath hi iberia ke saath vilay ke kaaran or 230 million pound kii bachat kaa anumaan tha.
 vilay se purv british airways kii iberia me 13.5% kii hissedari thi. vilay ke baad ise internshnal erlines group me 55% kii bhaagedari mili or ke baaki hissedaaron ke paas 45% hissaa gaya. 
 iberia ke paas kam laagat vaali erlines uiling ke 9.49% hai. baaki ke 90.51 isaki mul company internshnal erlines group ke paas he. aisa is liye kiya gaya taaki internshnal erlines group ke paas 100% hissedari naa ho or baaki share iske bhagidaaro ke paas hi rahe. iske atirikt royal er marok me bhi iberia kii 0.95% bhagidaari he.
