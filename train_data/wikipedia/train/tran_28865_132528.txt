 jaimangla garh bihar kaa pramukh paryaton sthal or pakshi vihaar he. yah kaanvar jhil se ghira hua he, jahaan har saal desh-videsh se lakhon kii sankhya main pakshi aate hai. yahaan mata jaimangla kaa prasiddh mandir he. 
 jaimangla garh begusarai-hasanpur mukhya rajyamarg par sthit he. yah bihar ke begusarai jila mukhyalay se 21 kilometre or manzol se chaar kilometre dur he. jaimangla mata kaa mandir mukhya sadak ke kareeb ek kilometre dur he. mandir chaaron or se kaanvar jhil se ghira hua he. 
 aisi manyata he ki yah raaj jaimal kaa garh tha. philhal isaki aitihaasikta par shodh jari he.
 kaanvar jhil mithe pani kaa vishaal jhil he. jisaki lambaai kareeb 42 kilometre he. yahaan har saal lakhon pakshi vishwa ke vibhinn deshon se aate hai.
 yah vishwa ke sabase bade pakshi viharon main se ek he. yahaan pakshiyon ko maarna pratibandhit he.
 jaimangla garh paryatensthal ke rup main prasiddh he. ek janvari ko nae saal ke maukey par yahaan hajaaron log vanbhoj ke liye aate hai.
