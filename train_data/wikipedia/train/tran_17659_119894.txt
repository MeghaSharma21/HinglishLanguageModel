 rajasthani khaanaa vishesh rup se shaakaahaari bhojan hota he or yah apane swaad ke kaaran saare vishwa main prasiddh ho gaya he. apni bhugolic paristhitiyon ke kaaran paaramparik rajasthani khaane main besan, daal, matha, dahi, sookhe masaale, sookhe mewa, ghi, doodh kaa adhikadhik prayog hota he. hari subjiyon kii taatkaalik anuplabdhta ke kaaran paaramparik rajasthani khaane main inkaa prayog kam hi rahaa he.
 mukhyat: nimna rajasthani khaane adhik prachlit hai.
