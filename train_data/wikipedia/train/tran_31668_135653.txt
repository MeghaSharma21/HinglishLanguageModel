 amejan basin amejan nadi or uski sahaayak nadiyon dwaara nikaas vaala dakshin america kaa hissaa he. amejan jal nikasi basin main 6,915,000 km2 kshetra ko shaamil kiya gaya he yaa dakshin america mahadweep kaa lagbhag 40 pratishat. yah bolivia, brazil, colombia, ecuador, guyana, peru, surinam or venezuela ke deshon main sthit he.K
