 joseph lui lagranj france ke ganitagya the. inkaa janm 25 janvari 1736 i. ko turine main hua. 17 varsh kii alpaayu main ye rajkeey sainik academy main ganit ke professor niyukt hue. ganit evam khagol shaastra ko inaki dein apurv he. khagol shaastra main inhone "chandra-mukti-siddhant" tathaa "brihaspati ke chaar upgrah sambandhi siddhant" kii vyakhya par anek anveshan kiye. 1766 i. main ye berlin main ganit ke professor niyukt hue. taduparant lagranj ne samikarnon evam sankhyaon ke siddhanton par anek khojein kin or dwighaateey anirnit sameekaran kaa hal diya, tritiy varna ke saranikon kaa suchi stambh sambandhi anveshanon main khub prayog kiya or vicharan karalan kii sahaayta se kaalpanik-veg-siddhant se yantravigyaan kii sampurna paddatiyon kaa nigaman kiya. inke atirikt inhone sambhavyata, parimit antar, aaroh sitat bhinnon or dirghavrittiya samakalon par bhi anek anveshan kiye. inke prasiddh granth "mechaanik anaalithik", "lason syur na kalcyul de fonksyon", rejolusyo dejokvasyon newmeric or "nuvail methud pur rezudra lejacvasyon literaal paar l muaiyen da ceri" he. 10 april 1813 i. ko paris main inkaa dehanth ho gaya.
