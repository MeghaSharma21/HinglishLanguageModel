 krushansar yaa krishnasar bharat ke jammu va kashmir rajya ke gandarbal zile ke sonmarg qasbe ke paas sthit ek svachh parvatiy jhil he. 3,710 meter par sthit yah jhil prasiddh kishanganga nadi kaa sarvapratham strot he. isaki lambaai 0.95 kimi or chaudaai 0.6 kimi he.
 'krushansar' naam 'krishnasar' kaa vikrut rup he or iskaa arth 'krishna kaa sarovar' he.
 krushansar ek sundar parvatiy jhil he jiske peeche vishnu parvat khadaa hua he. isaki tulana aksar paas kii vishansar se kii jaati he kyonki jahaan krushansar kaa rang kuch haraa nazar aata he vahaan vishansar kaa bahut nila he. donon jhiilon main trout machhlee milti he. yah uttari europe kii nivasee he lekin ise kashmir kii kai nadi-jhiilon main laakar choda gaya tha taaki log inhen pakadaney-khaane kaa anand le saken. krushansar ke aasapaas marg faile hue hai jahaan charvaahe apni bhed-bakriyaan lekar aate hai. sardiyon main yahan baari barfabaari hoti he or jhil bhi jam jaati he. krushansar se ek dhara nikalakar vishansar main jaati he or us jhil se aage kishanganga nadi nikalati he.
