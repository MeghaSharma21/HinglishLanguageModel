 1972 main jal vigyaan main antarrashtriya snatakottar paathyakram ke prarambh ke saath jal vigyaan vibhaag astitv main aayaa. is vibhaag main chalaaye ja rahe pathykram vartmaan main bharat sarkaar tathaa unesco dwaara samposhit hai. ab tak 33 deshon ke 240 videshi prashikshu adhikariyon ko sammilit karte huye kul milakar 623 prashikshu adhikari snatakottar paathyakram main bhaag le chuke hai.
