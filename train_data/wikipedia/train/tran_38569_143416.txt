 naam 1986 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. mahesh bhatta dwaara nirdeshit or kumaar gaurav dwaara nirmit is film main nutan, sanjay dutt, kumaar gaurav, punam dhillon, amrutha singh or paresh rawal mukhya kalakaar hai. is film ko mahesh bhatta, paresh rawal or sanjay dutt ke kariyar main ek mile kaa pathar mana jaataa he. bhartiya box offis par naam blockbster sabit hui thi. isko iske sangeet visheshkar geet "chitthee ai he" ke liye bhi janaa jaataa he. 
 janaki kapur or uske do bete ravi or viki garib hai or apni jaruratein puure karne main bhi unhen kathinaayee kaa saamana karnaa padataa he. ravi ek jimmedaar betaa he jo apane bhai or maan donon ke liye eemaandaaree se paise kamata he. seemaa uski khuddari or usulon se prabhavit he or use pasand karti he. vahi viki aawaara kism kaa vyakti he jo jaldi paise kamaane ke liye jua jaise kai gaiyr-kanoonee kaam karata he. viki ko dubai ja kar kaam karnaa he kyonki usne kai logon se suna he ki vahaan unhone safal jindagi sthapit kii he. ravi paise ikatthe karke use dubai bhejne kaa bandobast karata he. vahaan viki ko pataa chalata he ki uske kagajaat nakli he or use nashili dawaaon kii taskari karakar vahaan bheja gaya he.
 koi or rasta na dikhne par wo vahaan wo rana ke liye taskari kaa kaam shuru kar detaa he. jisamen uskaa saath anichhik rup se rita bhi detii he. vahiin uske bhai or maan ke paas koi soochna na aa paane par wo log chintit hone lagate hai. unhen pataa chalata he ki wo hong cong main pahunch chukaa he. ravi use dhundne vahaan jaataa he. viki taskari ke liye pakadaa jaataa he or use sajaa ho jaati he. wo police se bhagkar rana kaa katl kar detaa he. ravi police se bachaakar viki ko desh se baahar nikaalane kii koshish karata he lekin ant main wo police dwaara golibari main maaraa jaataa he. bharat main rita viki ke bachche ko janm detii he lekin khud mar jaati he. ant main seemaa, ravi or uski maan viki ke bachche ke saath mandir main milte hai.
