 vishnu ramakrishna karkare hindu mahasabha ke ek samarpit kaaryakarta the. inhen gandhi-vadh ke maamle main aaajeevan karawas kii sajaa di gayi thi. 
 adaalat main jab gandhi-vadh kaa abhiyog chalaa to madanlaal paahwa ne usamen sweekaar kiya ki jo bhi log is shadyantr main shaamil the purv yojananusaar use keval bam phodkar sabha main gadbadi failaane kaa kaam karnaa tha, shesh kaary anya logon ke jimme tha. jab use choturam ne jane se roka to usne jaise bhi usase ban paaya apana kaam kar diya. us din kii yojana bhale hi asafal ho gayi ho parantu is baat kii jaankaree to sarkaar ko ho hi gayi thi ki gandhi kii hatya kabhi bhi koi kar saktaa he phir unki suraksha kii chintaa aakhirkaar kinhen kerani chaiye thi?
