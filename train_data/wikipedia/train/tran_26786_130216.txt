 hillari erhard daf ek amareeki abhinetri evam gayika hai. usqi bady bahan heli daf bhi ek gayika he. chote parde par lizi makegwayar ke rup me safalataa hasil karne ke baad usne kai filmo me abhinay kiya jiname pramukh he, chiper bai da dajan, da lizi makegwayar movie tathaa a sindrela story . usne 2005 main $ 1.5 karod kamaaya. daf ne paanp sangeet main khub shoharat hasil kii he tathaa uske elbum kii 1.3 crores se zyaada kii bikri hui he. usne apni kapadon kii collection, staf bai hilleri daf shuru kii he tathaa elizabeth ardan ke saath ek itr kii shuruuaat kii he. 2007 main uski aane vaali filmein hai vaanr eenk tathaa anupranit foodfite. daf kii naveenatam elbum "diganity" april 2007 main relies hui he. 
 hilleri kaa janm 28 sitambar 1987 ko huston texs main hua jahaan inke pita, ranbart erhard daf shrankhla dookaano ke maalik he tathaa inaki maan, susan colins ek gruhinee he. unki maan ke protsaahan se dono daf bahano ne abhinay kii class main bhaag liya jis se unhe kai sthaneey naatako main kaam mila.
