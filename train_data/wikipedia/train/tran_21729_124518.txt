 rupanjali shastri ek purv bhartiya mahila cricket khilaadi he. ye bharat kii rashtriya mahila cricket team ke liye 1990 ke dashak main test or ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket match khelaa karti thi. ye daahine haath se ballebaaji karti thim jabaki daahine hi haath se of brake gendbaaji karti thim. inhone apane jivan main kul ek maatr test or 12 ek divasiy antarrashtriya match team ke liye khele the.
