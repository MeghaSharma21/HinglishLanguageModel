 dweepiya kelti bhashaayen pashchimottar europe main kelti bhashaa-parivaar kii ek upashakha he jisaki bhashaayen britain va ireland ke dwipon par utpann hui thim. yah mukhyabhumi europe or anatolia main utpann hone vaali mahadwipiya kelti bhashaaon se bhinn thi. sabhi mahadwipiya kelti bhashaayen vilupt ho gai or vishwa main aaj boli jane vaali sabhi kelti bhashaayen keval dweepiya kelti shakha se hi hai. 5veen or 6thi shatabdi isvi main kuch dweepiya kelti bolne vaale pashchimottar france main ja based or vahaan ke britaini prayadweep va kuch anya kshetron main aaj bhi unke bolne vaale milte hai.
