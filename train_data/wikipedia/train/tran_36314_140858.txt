 koshikantarik paraseete aise paraseete ko kehete hai jo apane parposhi kii koshikaon ke bheetar badhane or prajnan karne kii kshamta rakhata ho. kai aise paraseete rogajanak bhi hote hai, masalan malaria karne vaala plasmodium.
 vikalpi koshikantarik paraseete ve hote hai jo kisi parposhi jeev kii koshikaon ke bheetar yaa baahar ke paryaavaran donon main rah sakate hai. iskaa udhaaharan yarsinia vansh ke kuch bacteria hai.
 avikalpi koshikantarik paraseete ve hote hai jo kisi parposhi jeev kii koshikaon ke bheetar hi rah sakate hai or yah koshikeey sansadhano par puri tarah nirbhar hai. iskaa udhaaharan clamedia vansh ke bacteria hai.
