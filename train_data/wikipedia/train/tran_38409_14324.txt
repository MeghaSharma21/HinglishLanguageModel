 avikalpi vayujeev aisa jeev hota he jise badhane va panpane ke liye oxigen kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. koshikeey shwasan se yah jeev carbohydrate or vasa jaisi samagriyon ko oxigen ke prayog dwaara chayapachay se urja main badalte hai. aisa viviya shwasan aahaar ko avayaviya shwasan yaa kinvan kii tulana main adinosin triphosphate ke rup main adhik urja main badal paata he. lekin isamen anivaary vayujeev ke sharir ko oxikaarak abhikriyaaon main hone vaali haniyon ko bhi jhelana padataa he.
