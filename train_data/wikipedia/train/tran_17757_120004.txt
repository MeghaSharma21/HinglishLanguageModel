 andreyes vicelius belgium ke, sharir vaigyanik, chikitsak, tathaa sharirarachana vigyaan par vishwa kii sabase prabhavakaari pustak 'di humeni korporis fabrica' ke rachayita the. weselius ko aadhunik maanav sharir rachana vigyaan kaa janak mana jaataa he. ve paidua vishvavidhyalay main praadhyaapak rahe tathaa baad main charles pancham ke rajchikitsak bhi rahe.
 vicelius ko sarvocch sharir vaigyanik kaha jaataa he. maanav sharir ko rachana par inke granth kii gananaa is vishay ke sarvotkrisht granthon main hoti he. isamen asthiyon or tantrikatantr ke varnan to utkrisht hai hi, par peshiyon ke varnan ke liye yah visheshkar prasiddh he. vicelius ne adhyaapan karte samay swayan vichchhedan kar, shareeravigyaan kii shiksha pranali main kranti laa di.
 andreyes vicelius kaa janm brasalz naamak nagar main hua tha. inhone luvain main silvian tathaa jihenez ganthar se shiksha pai thi.
 san 1537 main inhone irani chikitsak reziz ke ek granth kaa sampadan kiya or tab venice ke padua vishvavidhyalay se em.di. kii oopaadhi praapt kii. yaheen ye shalyachikitsa tathaa shareeravigyaan ke acharya niyukt hue. san 1538 main inhone kuch sudhaar kar, kintu gallen kii vichaar-paddati par aadhaarit, chah sharir-vigyaan-saraniyon kaa prakaashan kiya. san 1539 main inhone raktamoshan par ek lekh likhaa tathaa san 1541 main gallen ke teen granthon kaa sampadan kiya. san 1543 main inkaa 'fabrica' granth epitom ke saath prakashit hua. baad main ye samraat charles paanchaven tathaa unke uttaradhikari philip duusare ke chikitsak ke pad par rahe.
