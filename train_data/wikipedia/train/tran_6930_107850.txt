 kudankulam nabhikeey vidyut pariyojna bharat ke tamilnadu pradesh ke kudankulam main sthit he. iskaa nirmaan san 2002 main armbh hua or 13 july 2013 ko yah krantik hua. is pariyojna main 1000 megawatt vidyut kshamta vaale do unit hai. pehle unit ne 22 oktober 2013 se vidyut grid ko vidyut aapurti karnaa armbh kiya. is sanyantr kii do ikaayiyon kii mul laagat 13171 karod thi jo badhkar 17270 ho gayi.
 10 agast 2016 ko isaki duusari ikai ko krantik kiya gaya 
 kudankulam nabhikeey vidyut pariyojna main 1000 megawatt vidyut kshamta vaali do ikaiyaan hai.
 kekeenpipi ikai-1 bhartiya nabhakeey vidyut corporation li. kaa 20waan nabhakeey vidyut stesan he jo vidyut grid se joda ja chukaa he. ise oktober, 2013 main grid ke saath joda gaya tha or yah anishchit taur par vidyut kaa utpaadan kar rahaa he. is unit ke vidyut str ko parmaanu urja niamak parishad dwaara di gai anumati ke anusaar charanwar badhaayaa gaya or isane 07 june 2014 ko purna vidyut kshamta hasil kar lee. oktober 2013 ko grid ke saath jode jane ke baad se lekar 13 july 2014 tak isamen anishchit taur par vidyut kaa vaastavik rup se utpaadan lagbhag 2565 unit rahaa he.

 14 may 2014 ko hui garam pani risaav kii ghatana ke falswaroop enpisiiel ke teen kaamgaar garam pani se jalane ke kaaran ghayal hue or teen theka kaamgaar kekeenpipi unit-1 kii terbine building ke ek heater loop ke teen stareey garam pani inlate valve ke rakh-rakhaav ke dauraan jakhmi hue. yah ghatana trep main garam pani kii nikasi se hui. ghatana kii shuruuaati jaanch se pataa chalaa ki paanch sau mem aakaar ke valve ke fatne ke kaaran ekatrit garam pani failane se yah ghatana hui.
