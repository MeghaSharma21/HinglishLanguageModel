 
 kandisa bhartiya rock band, indian oshan kii teesri album he. saat geeton vaali yah album march 2000 main release kii gai thi, or isane indian oshan ko bharat ke sabase vaastavik or rachanaatmak bandon main se ek kii pahchaan dilaai, or yah aage chalakar kisi bhi bhartiya band kii sarvaadhik bikne vaali album bani.
 is album ke do geet, "maa reva" or "kandisa" rolling stone kii 2014 kii "25 sarvshereshth bhartiya rock geet" suchi main shaamil kiye gaye the.
 1997 main desert rain kii release ke baad indian oshan samooche bharat main gum-gum kar sadakon par pradarshan kar rahaa tha. 1998 main band ko times mujeec dwaara sin kar liya gaya. iske kuch samay baad indian oshan ne khajuraho main raashtrapati ke aar narayanan kii upasthiti main ek sho kiya, jise kaaphi saraahaa gaya. is sho tathaa isse pehle sadakon par gaaye unke geeton ko hi unhone kandisa album main prayog kiya.
 album kii recording mumbai ke western outdoor studio main hui thi. in geeton ko record karne main unhen kul 2 saptaah lage, or phir agle 5 din mix karne main. indian oshan ke sadasyon ke anusaar yah unki pehali 'vaastavik' studio album thi, kyonki isamen unhone pehali baar kisi mujeec produsar kii sahaayta lee thi.
 album kaa sheershak geet "kandisa" aramike-syrian bhashaa me he, jo kerala main saint thomas isaayiyon dwaara boli jaati he. iske atirikt "koun" geet main kashmiri bhashaa kaa prayog he, jinhen drumer amit kilom kii mata, indira kilom ne likhaa tha.
 sabhi geet indian oshan dwaara likhit hai.
