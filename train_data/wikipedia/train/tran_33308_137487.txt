 hathgola barood aadhaarit hathiyaar he jo chhodane ke thodi der baad fatataa he. mul rup se yuddh main ye hathgola phekane main daksh sainik-toli kaa yah kaam hota tha. kintu vartmaan samay main hathgole rifl se feke jate hai yaa isi kaam ke liye vishesh rup se dizin kiye hue 'grenade-lanchar' se feke jate hai.
