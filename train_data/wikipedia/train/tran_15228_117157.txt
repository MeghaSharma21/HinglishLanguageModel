 adam jon morrison ek america sevanivrutt peshewar basketbaal khilaadi. morrison khelaa jaataa he par teen saal ke liye vishvavidhyalay or ek mana jaataa tha ke sheersh college basketbaal khilaadiyon 2005-06 main he. vah tha ek final ke liye nismith or lakdi ke puraskaar. vah namit kiya gaya tha sah-saal ke khilaadi ke saath duk je je Redick dwaara sanyukt rajya america basketbaal riters association or jeeta 2006 shevarlyt player varsh puraskaar ke. vah ke bare main sochaa he ke rup main sabase acha khilaadi Gonzaga main purushon kii basketbaal ke itihaas. apane upnaam "safed mamba", use karne ke liye diya dwaara apane purv sherlot banbilav saathiyon.
