 gunakar mule  hindi or angreji main vigyaan-lekhan ko lokapriy banane vaale jane maane lekhak the. 
 maharashtra ke amravati jile ke sindhu bujurg gaanv main 3 janvari 1935 ko janmen marathi mul ke hone ke baavjood unhone elahabaad vishvavidhyalay se ganit main em.e. kiya or lekhan ke liye hindi va angreji bhashaaon ko madhyam banaaya. varshon darjeeling sthit rahul sangrahagar se sambhddha rahane ke uparaant 1971-72 ke daur main ve dilli aa gaye the or phir dilli hi unke jivan kaa antim padaav bani. yaheen unhone vivaah kiya, ghar basaaya or rahe. ek dilachasp tathy yah bhi he ki civil marij ke dauraan court ke sammukh mule ji ke pita kii bhoomika baba nagarjun ne nibhaai thi. 
 gunakar marathi bhashi the, par unhone pachaas saal se adhik samay tak hindi main lekhan kiya or unki kareeb 35 pustaken chhapin. ve rahul sankrutyaayan ke shishy the. unke parivaar main do betiyaan, ek putr tathaa patni hai. unhone hindi main kareeb teen hajaar lekh likhe or angareji main unke 250 se adhik lekh hai. ve ensiiarti ke paathya pustak sampadan mandal va neshnal book trust kii hindi prakaashan salaahkaar samiti ke sadsy rah chuke hai. 16 oktober 2009 ko miyasthinia gravis naamak neuro disorder ke kaaran mule kaa pandav nagar main dehavsaan ho gaya.
 gunakar mule ko hindi academy kaa sahityakar sammaan, kendriya hindi santhaan kaa atmaram puraskaar, bihar kaa karpuri thakur smriti sammaan mil chukaa he.
