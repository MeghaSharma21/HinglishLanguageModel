 hemlata luvanam ek bhartiya samaaj sudhaarak, lekhak or naastik the jinhone asprishyata or jati vyavastha ke khilaaf virodh kiya tha. vah apane pati lavvanam ke saath sanskaar kaa sah-sansthaapak bhi the.
 hemlata kaa janm 26 farvari 1932 ko british bharat ke madras presidency main vinukonda main hua tha, jo ab andhra pradesh ke guntur jile main he. vah telugu kavi gurum joshua or mirayama kii beti thim or saamaajik sudhaarak goparaju ramchandra raav or saraswati gora kii beti thim, jo naastik samaaj sudhaarak the or vijaywada main naastik centre ke sansthaapak the.
 hemlata samsar ke sansthaapak sachiv the, ek gaiyr-sarkari sanghathan jo maanavtawaad, saamaajik nyaay, maanav adhikaar or andhra pradesh rajya main saamaajik-aarthik vikaas ke liye kaam karti thi. 
 hemlata ne hyderabad main poti shriramulu telegu vishvavidhyalay se saamaajik kaary main doctoret kii oopaadhi praapt kii. neshnal book trust of india ne rashtriya jeevani shrinkhalaa main unki ek jeevani prakashit kii, jisamen vakulabharanam lalita or compli sundar ne likhaa tha. 2004 main unhone raamineni foundation puraskaar visesha pursaram jeeta. 2003 main unhone saamaajik kaary ke kshetra main unke yogadan ke liye andhra pradesh ke rajyapal surajeet singh barnaala se ek red and white brewery puraskaar praapt kiya.
