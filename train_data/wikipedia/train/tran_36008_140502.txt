 is puraana main paratpar brahm shiv ke kalyankari swaroop kaa tattvik vivechan, rahasy, mahima or upasna kaa vistrut varnan he. isamen inhen panchdevon main pradhan anadi siddh parameshwar ke rup main sweekaar kiya gaya he. shiv-mahima, lila-kathaon ke atirikt isamen puja-paddati, anek gyaanaprad aakhyaan or shikshaprad kathaon kaa sundar sangyojan he. isamen bhagwaan shiv ke bhavyatam vyaktitv kaa gungan kiya gaya he. shiv- jo swayambhu hai, shaashvat hai, sarvocch satta he, vishwa chetana hai or brahmand astitv ke aadhaar hai. sabhi puraanon main shiv puraana ko sarvaadhik mahatvapurn hone kaa darja praapt he. isamen bhagwaan shiv ke vividh roopon, avtaron, jyotirlingon, bhakton or bhakthi kaa vishad varnan kiya gaya he.
 'shiv puraana' kaa sambandh shaiva mat se he. is puraana main pramukh rup se shiv-bhakthi or shiv-mahima kaa prachaar-prasar kiya gaya he. praaia: sabhi puraanon main shiv ko tyaag, tapasya, vatsalya tathaa karunaa kii murti bataaya gaya he. kaha gaya he ki shiv sahaj hi prasann ho jane vaale evam manovanchit fal dene vaale hai. kintu 'shiv puraana' main shiv ke jivan charitra par prakaash daalate hue unke rahan-sahan, vivaah or unke putron kii utpatti ke vishay main vishesh rup se bataaya gaya he.

 ‘shivpurana’ ek pramukh tathaa suprasiddh puraana he, jisamen paratmpar parabrahm parameshwar ke ‘shiv’ swaroop kaa tattvik vivechan, rahasy, mahima evam upasna kaa suvistrut varnan he. bhagwaan shivamaatr puranic devtaa hi nahin, apitu ve panchdevon main pradhan, anadi siddh parameshwar hai evam nigamagam aadi sabhi shastron main mahimamandit mahadev hai. vedon ne is paramatatva ko avyakt, ajanma, sabakaa kaaran, vishwapanch kaa srashta, paalak evam sanhaarak kahkar unka gungan kiya he. shrutiyon ne sadaa shiv ko swayambhu, shant, prapanchatit, paratpar, paramatatva, ishwaron ke bhi param maheshwar kahkar stuti kii he. ‘shiv’ kaa arth hi he- ‘kalyanswaroop’ or ‘kalyanpradaata’. parambrahm ke is kalyaan rup kii upasna ucch koti ke sidhon, aatmakalyankami saadhakon evam sarsaadhaaran aastik janon-sabhi ke liye param mangalamay, param kalyankari, sarvasiddhidaayak or sarvashreyaskar he. shastron main ullekh miltaa he ki dev, danuj, rishi, maharishi, yogindra, munindra, siddh, gandharv hi nahin, apitu brahma-vishnu tak in mahadev kii upasna karte hai. is puraana ke anusaar yah puraana param uttam shaastra he. ise is bhootal par bhagwaan shiv kaa vaanmay swaroop samajhnaa chaiye or sab prakaar se iskaa seven karnaa chaiye. iskaa pathan or shravan sarvasaadhanrup he. isse shiv bhakthi paakar shreshthatam sthiti main pahunchaa hua manushya sheeghra hi shivpad ko praapt kar letaa he. isaliye sampoorn ytn karke manushyon ne is puraana ko padhane kii ichha kii he- athwa iske adhyayan ko abhisht saadhan mana he. isi tarah iskaa prempurvak shravan bhi sampoorn manovanchit falon ke denevaala he. bhagwaan shiv ke is puraana ko sunne se manushya sab paapon se mukt ho jaataa he tathaa is jivan main bade-bade utkrisht bhogon kaa upbhog karke ant main shivlok ko praapt kar letaa he. yah shivpurana naamak granth choubees hajaar shlokon se yukt he. saat samhitaon se yukt yah divya shivpurana parabrahm paramatma ke samaan virajman he or sabase utkrisht gati pradaan karne vaala he.
