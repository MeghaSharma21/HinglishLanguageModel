 bajrangbali bhartiya devtaa hanuman kaa hi ek naam he. hanuman naam se ek film bhi bani he jo bharat kii pehali anemated film he. isamen bal hanuman kii lilaon kaa varnan kiya gaya he.
 bajrangbali or hanuman naam kaise pade inke sambandh main vistaar se yah jaananaa rochak rahegaa ki sanskrit kaa ek shabd he hanuh jiskaa matlab hota he chibuk, thudi, thodi yaa jabada. yani hothon ke neeche kii haddi kaa ubhaar. isaki mul dhaatu he ghanu jiskaa matlab kathor he. sanskrit kii mul dhaatu ghanu se parvarti sanskrit main ga dhwani kaa lop ho gaya or iskaa rup bana hanuh. gauratlab he ki bhaktashiromani hanuman ke namakaran main bhi isi ghanu yaa hanuh kaa yoga rahaa he. puranakatha ke anusaar janm lete hi mahabali fal samajhakar surya ko khaane lapke. surya ko inaki pakad se chhudaane ke liye indra ne apane vajra se in par prahaar kiya jisase inkaa jabada yani hanu tedi ho gai. tabhi se inhen hanuman kehane lage. 
 inke bajrangbali naam ke peeche bhi vajra shabd kaa yogadan he. sanskrit main ek shabd he vajrah yaa vajram jiskaa arth he bijli, indra kaa shastr, hira athwa ispaat. isse hi hindi kaa vajra shabd bana he. indra ke paas jo vajra tha vah maharishi dadhichi kii hadidyon se bana tha. hanuman vanararaj kesari or anjani ke putr the. kesari ko rishi-muniyon ne atyant balashaali or sevaabhaavi santan hone kaa aashirwad diya. isiilie hanuman kaa sharir lohe ke samaan kathor tha. isiilie unhen vajrang kaha jane laga. atyant shaktishaalee hone se vajrang ke saath bali shabd judakar unka naam ho gaya vajrangbali jo bolchaal kii bhashaa main bana bajrangbali. inhen marut yani vaayu devtaa kaa putr bhi kaha jaataa he isliye inkaa ek naam maruti yani vaayu ke samaan vegvan bhi kaha jaataa he.
