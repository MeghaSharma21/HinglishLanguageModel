 udayaditya panvar, malwa kaa raja tha jisane jaisingh ke baad rajdhani se malwa par raaj kiya. chalukyon se sangharsh pehle se hi chal rahaa tha or uske aadhipaty se malwa abhi haal hi alag hua tha jab udayaditya lagbhag 1059 i. main gaddi par baitha. malwa kii shakti ko pun: sthapit karne kaa sankalp kar usne chalukyaraj karn par safal chadhaai kii. kuch log is karn ko chalukya na maanakar kalchuri lakshmikarn maanate hai. is sambandh main kuch nishchaypurvak nahin kaha ja saktaa. isamen andeh he ki udayaditya ne karn ko parrast kar diya. udayaditya kaa yah prayaas parmaron kaa antim prayaas tha or l. 1088 i. main uski mrityu ke baad parmaar vansh kii shakti uttarottar ksheen hoti gai. udayaditya kii abhilekhon main bhoj kaa 'bandhu' kaha gaya he. kuch aashcharya nahin jo vah parmaron kii duusari shakha kaa rahaa ho. udaipur or nagpur ke abhilkhon main iskaa ullekh raja bhoj ke uttaradhikari ke rup main hua he.
 udayaditya ke teen putr the .pratham lakshmanadevdwitiya narvarman tathaatrutiy jagadev panvarpita udayaditya ke raajkaal me hi lakshmanadev malwa ke dakhkhan kii uparajdhani nagardhan se is pradesh kaa subedaar tha. udayaditya kii mrityu 1086 isa me hooi. udayaditya ke mrityu pashchayaat dhar rajsinghaasana par narvarmandev kaa rajbhishek hua or usne malwa praant par 1133 tak raaj kiya.
