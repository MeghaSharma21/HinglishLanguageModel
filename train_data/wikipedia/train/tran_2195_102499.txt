 us bhitti ko dharak bhitti kehete hai jo aprakritik pravanta vaali mitti ko katkar girane se bachaane ke liye nirmit kii jaati hai. udhaaharan ke liye, kisi pahari sthaan par sadak ke kinare dharak bhitti banani padati he taaki sadak par mitti or pathar na girane paaen.
 yah bhitti kankreet, eent, pathar, aadi se banaayi jaati he. dharak bhitti kaa upyog anya kaaryon ke liye bhi kiya jaataa he, jaise basament kaa nirmaan, naaliyaan banane main aadi. pani aadi rokane ke liye bhi iskaa prayog hota he. in bhittiyon par kshaitij disha main bhi bal lagata he jo bhitti dwaara roki gai mitti kii prakruti par nirbhar karata he.atah dharak bhitti ke dizin main in kshaitij balon kii gananaa mahatvapurn he.
