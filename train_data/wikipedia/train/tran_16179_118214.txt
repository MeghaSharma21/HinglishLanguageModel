 aarthik swatantraaa kaa prayog aarthik evam nitigat charchaaon main bahudhaa hoti rahati he. jis prakaar swatantraaa kii anekon paribhaashaayen di jaatin he, usee tarah 'arthik aazaadee' kaa bhi koi sarvamaanya paribhaasha nahin he. kintu paramparaagat rup se mukt bazaar or nijee swaamitv par jor dene vaale udaaravaadi drushtikon 'aarthik aazaadee' kaha jaataa he. duusare chor par 'vrihad' vikalp pradaan karne vaali arthniti ko aarthik aazaadee kehete hai.
