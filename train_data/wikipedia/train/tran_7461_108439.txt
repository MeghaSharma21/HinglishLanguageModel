 avatar singh cheema pratham bhartiya the jo safalataa purvak mount everest fatah kiya. wo bhartiya sena ke do asafal prayaason ke pashchaat 1965 main mount everest par chadhane ke liye taiyaar kiye gayi tisare mishan kaa hissaa the. unhone 20 may 1965 ko safaltaapurvak vijay praapt kii. wo bhartiya sena main parachute palaton kaa hissaa the. baad main unhen karnal ke rup main pronnati mili.
 unhen unki uplabddhiyon ke liye arjuna puraskaar or padam shri se sammanit kiya gaya. wo rajasthan rajya ke sriganganagar jile ke nivasee the.
