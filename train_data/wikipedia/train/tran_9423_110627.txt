 rudki, bharat ke uttrakhand rajya main sthit ek nagar or nagarpalika parishad he. ise rudki chhavnee ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he or yah desh kii sabase puraani chhavniyon main se ek he or 1853 se bengal abhiyantriki samooh kaa mukhyalay he.
 yah nagar gang nahar ke tat par rashtriya rajmarg 58 par dehradun or dilli ke madhya sthit he.
 rudki kaa naam ruri/ rudi se padaa, jo badgujar rajput sardaar kii patni thi or pehle ise 'ruri kii' bhi likhaa jaataa tha. sthaneey bhashi gramvaasiyon kaa maananaa he kii ise iskaa naam "rorone kii" arthath ror kaa nivas se mila.
 18veen sadin main rudki solani nadi ke pashchimi tat par basaa gaanv tha. taatkaalik saharanpur jile ke panvar gurjaron kii langaura riyasat ke anrtgat aata tha. yah kshetra gujaron dwaara shasit hone ke kaaran 1857 i0 tak " gujarat" kehalaata rahaa, 1857 kii kranti main gurjaro va raanghado dwaara kiye gaye bhayankar vidroho va updravo ke kaaran angrejon ne 1857 kii kranti ke daman ke baad gurjaron ke prabhaav ko kam karne ke liye tamaam sarkari dastavejon va abhilekho main " saharanpur- gurjaratr" naam par pratibandh laga diya gaya or sirf ' saharanpur' naam darj karne ke aadesh jari kar die gaye. 
 yah 1813 i0 main langaura rajya ke raja ramadayal singh panvar kii mrityu ke baad british east india company ke adheen aa gaya.
 angrejon ne gurjaro kii ganga- yamuna ke doaab main lagaataar badhati ja rahi takat ko kamjor karne va saath hi ruhelaon ko apane niyantran main laane ke uddeshy se rudki gaanv main chhavnee kii sthapana kii. jiskaa upyog 1824 i0 main langaura riyasat ke talluka va gurjar kile kunja bahadurpur ke raja vijay singh panvar ke netritv main lade gaye pehle swatantraaa sangram va 1857 i0 main saharanpur ke gurjaron va ranghadon dwaara kiye gaye bhayankar vidroho ko kuchalane ke liye kiya gaya.
 april 1842 main angrej adhikari sar probe kotle ke netritv main ganganhar nirmaan hetu khudaai prarambh kii gai, tathaa ganganhar ke nirmaan kaary va rakrakhaav ke liye 1843 main nahar ke kinare canal workshop va Iron foundry kii sthapana kii gai.

 rudki ganganhar kaa nirmaan kaary 1854 main purna hua tathaa ise 08 april 1854 ko chaalu kar diya gaya jisase lagbhag 5000 ganvo kii lagbhag 767000 acre bhoomi kii sinchai kii jaati he tathaa uttarakhand rajya ke janapad haridwar ke pathari va mauhmmadpur ganvo me is par jal-vidyut grih banaakar bijli utpaadan bhi kiya ja rahaa he.
 rudki 29°52′N 77°53′E / 29.87°N 77.88°E / 29.87; 77.88. ke ankshashon par sthit he. isaki samudratal se oonchaai 268.9 meter he. yah rashtriya rajdhani dilli se 172 kimi uttar main ganga or yamuna nadiyon ke madhya, himalay kii talahati main sthit he. 9 november, 2000 ko uttrakhand rajya banane se purv yah uttar pradesh kaa ek bhaag tha.
 rudki bhugolic rup se kisi bhi bade jalashay se dur hone or himalay ke nikat hone ke kaaran rudki kaa mausam bahut charmi or asthir he. greeshm ritu march ke atikaal se armbh hoti he jo july tak rahati he or ausat taapmaana 280 se. rahata he. monsoon kaa mausam july se armbh hokar oktober tak rahata he or monsoon ke badalon kii himalay dwaara rikawat ke kaaran prachand varsha hoti he. monsoon ke baad kaa mausam oktober se armbh hota he or november ke ant tak jari rahata he, jab ausat taapmaana 210 se. se 150 se. tak tahata he. sheet ritu disambar main armbh hoti he, jab nunatam taapmaana jamaav bindu tak pahunch jaataa he jiskaa kaaran he himalay se aane vaali avarohan pawanen. kul vaarshik varsha 102 inch tak hoti he.
 rudki, uttar railway kshetra ke adheen aata he or desh ke pramukh nagaron se railmarg dwaara judaa hua he.
 nikatatam hawaii adda jollygrant he jo dehradun main he, lekin dilli sthit indira gandhi anterrashtriya adde ko variyata di jaati he.
 rudki ke nikat pramukh bade nagar hai dehradun, haridwar, rishikesh, saharanpur, muzaffarnagar, meerut, ambala or chandigarh hai. rashtriya rajmarg 58  or enech73 rudki se hokar jate hai.
 2001 kii janaganana ke aadhaar par, rudki kii jansankhya 2,52,784 he jisamen se purush 53% or mahilaayein 47% hai. rudki kii ausat sakshartaa sar 82% he, jo rashtriya ausat 64% se adhik he: purush sakshartaa 87% or mahila sakshartaa 81% he. 11% jansankhya 6 varsh se kam ayuvarg kii he. is nagar main hindu 61%, musalmaan 28%, sikh/punjabi 8%, jain 2.7% or isai 0.3% hai. 
 kul 2,52,784 kii jansankhya ke saath, yah uttrakhand main haridwar or haldwani ke baad teesri sabase badi nagarpalika parishad he.
 pramukh bhashaayen hai hindi, punjabi or urdu.
 desh kaa sabase purana praudyogiki santhaan bhi rudki me sthit he jise iaiti rudki ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
