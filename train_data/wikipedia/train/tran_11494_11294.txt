 gajipur city korporation, desh ke anya nagar-nikaayon ke saamaan hi bangladesh ke sthaneey prashasan niyamon ke antargat, gajipur nagar ke viniman hetu ek prashasnic rachana he, joki antytah bangladesh ke sthaneey prashasan evam gramin vikaas mantralay ke antargat he.
