 dach gilder, netherland kii bhootpurv rashtriya mudra thi. san 2002 main netherland ne bhi euro kshetra ke anya deshon ke saamaan hi europe kii ekal mudra euro ko apana liya. gilder 13 veen shatabdi se netherland kii rashtriya mudra thi. 
 netherland anteliz, main abhi bhi dach gilder kaa hi upyog hota he, haalanki yah mudra dach gilder se bhinn he.
 dach naam gulden ek madhyam dach visheshan he jiskaa arth he, 'swarna', jo is baat kaa sanket he kii multah dhaatu ke sikke sone se bane hote the. dach gilder ke liye upyog main laayaa jane vaala ƒ yaa FL, florioun ek or anya pracheen mudra se praapt kiya gaya tha.
 euro se dach gilder kii vinimay dar 2.2037 prati euro he, jo abhi bhi puraane len dein ke liye upyog main laayaa jaataa he.
 dach gilder ke sikkon or noton kii ek bahut anuthi visheshta he ki ve ek chauthai kii pranali par aadhaarit he, isliye 0.20, 2, 20, 200 ityadi ke sthaan par ƒ 0.25, ƒ 2.50, moolyavarg ke sikke or ƒ 25 or ƒ 250 moolyavarg ke nont uplabdh the.
 dach gilder kii ek or visheshta ƒ 5 sikaa he or yadi yah abhi bhi prachlan main hota or vaidhanik nivida hota to, yah niyamit rup se jari kiya jane vaala duniya kaa sarvocch moolyit sikaa hota jiskaa mulya 2.27 € he.
 euro ko apanaae jane ke samay nimnlikhit moolyavarg ke sikke sanchalan main the:- 
 sabhi sikkon ke agr bhaag par maharani kii chhavi hoti thi or duusari ore ek saadhaaran grid. gilder ke 1, 21/2 or 5 ke sikkon ke kinaaron par God zij met ons likhaa hua hota tha.
