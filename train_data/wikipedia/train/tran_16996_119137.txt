 iritriya purvottar africa main sthit ek desh he, jise aadhikarik taur par iritriya rajya ke naam se janaa jaataa he. iske pashchim main sudan, dakshin main ithiopia or dakshinpurv main jibooti sthit he. desh ke purv or uttar purv bhaag main saudi arab or yaman ke sidh main laal sagar kii vishaal tatarekha he. dhaalak dweepasmooh or hanish dweep samooh ke anek bhaag iritriya kaa hissaa hai. 1,18,000 varg kimi se chote is desh kii aabaadi kariban 5 lakh he. desh kii rajdhani asamaara he.
