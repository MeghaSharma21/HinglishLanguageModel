 kalita purvottar bharat ke assam rajya me paayi jane vaali ek jati he. yah jati sanyukt rup se swayan ko kshatriya varna ke antargat maanati he. bahut pehle 15ve-16ve shatabdi me kalita samrajya ke astitv ke aitihaasik pramaan uplabdh he.
 kalita jati kii utpatti pramaano ke sandigdh hone ke kaaran vivaadaaspad he. 'puranic paramparao ke anusaar kalita pavitra arya maane jate he. aaryon se utpatti kaa siddhanth vartmaan vyavsaayik jati ke udai se purv kalitao ke aagaman kaa sanket detaa he. kalita samanytah kshatriya varna ke antargat sammilit hote he va swayan ko "kullupt" kehete he, "kul" arthath jati va "lupt" arthath khoyaa hua, is sandarbh me yah kathan prachlit he ki parushram ne jab sampoorn prithvi ko kshatriya viheen karne kaa pran liya to unke bhay se kalita jo ki mul rup se kshatriya the bhaag khadey huye. parantu shabd shaastra ke anusaar yah katha uchit prathit nahi hoti he.
 assam me kalita jati padanukram me brahmano ke baad pratham sthaan par gine jate he. kuch dantakthao ke anusaar ve gaiyr vaidic arya he, jinhone assam me arya sabhyataa pratipaadit kii. sthaneey logo me sammilit hokar va swa-samskruti par sthaneey prabhaav ke baavjood bhi, uname arya sabhyataa kii paramparaaye kuch had tak aaj bhi vidyaman he.
 dow bies guha ke anusaar gujarat ke nagar brahmano va kamrup ke raja bhaskaravarman dwaara nidhanpur me bhu-daan me prayukt upanaamo me samanta paayi gayi he, jaise ki dutt, dhar,dev, nandi,sen va vasu aadi. atah inhe assam ke kalitao se mila ke dekha ja saktaa he. parantu nidhanpur tamrapatron kaa ullekh karte huye itihaashkar "fani deka" ke anusaar yah saare naam kaaysthon me prayukt hote he.
