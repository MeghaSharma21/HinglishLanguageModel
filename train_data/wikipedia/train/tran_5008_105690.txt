 ustad barkat ali khan punjab se sambandh rakhane vaale, patiala gharaane ke prakhyat shastreey sangeetkaar or gayak the. ve ustad ali baksh khan kasuri ke putr or ustad bade ghulaam ali sahab ke chote bhai or mubarak ali ke bade bhai the.
 ustad barkat ali khan 1905 ko kasoor, punjab, british bharat main janme the. unhen sangeet kii shiksha apane pita, ali baksh khan se mili. ve thumari daadaraa, ghazal or geet shaileyon main panditya rakhte the. unhen apni thumari or ghazal ke liye janaa jaataa tha. unki ghazal gayikee ke liye, 1950 ke dashak main unhone bharat or pakistan kaaphi prasiddhi praapt kii thi. unhone mashhoor gazalkar ustad ghulaam ali sahab ko bhi shiksha di thi.
 55 varsh kii choti aayu main hi, lahore, pakistan main, 19 june san 1963 main unki akasmaat mrityu ho gai thi.
