 hijaaz pahadiyaan yaa hejaz pahadiyaan saudi arab ke pashchimi hijaaz kshetra main us desh ke pashchimi tat ke paas sthit pahadiyon kii ek shrunkhlaa he. vastav main is tat kii barabari main do pahari shrunkhalaayen chalati hai - uttar main hijaaz pahadiyaan hai, bich main ek kam oonchaai kaa kshetra he or dakshin main phir aseer pahadiyaan hai. hijaaz ke pahaad purv main dhalaan rakhte hai jahaan se kabhi-kabhaar girane vaali barish bahakar aasapaas kii kuch vaadiyon main chashmen or kune main pani bharkar vahaan par nachlistaan bana detii he. hijaaz pahadiyon kii pashchimi taraf main dhalaan kii bajaae khaiyaan hai, jahaan oonchaai bahut jaldi girti he. pahadiyon or laal sagar ke bich kam jagah he, isliye tateey maidan yaa to bahut chota he yaa he hi nahin.
