 
 dugong ek madhyam aakaar kaa samudri standhari praani he jo vishwa ke kai bhaagon main samudri tateey kshetron ke jal main paaya jaataa he. yah sirenia jeevvaigyanik gana kaa sadsy he, jisamen chaar jeevvaigyanik jatiyaan pai jaati hai. dugong is gana ke adheen dugongidaaye naamak kul kii iklauti jivit jati he. is kul main stailer samudri gaay naamak ek anya jati bhi thi lekin uskaa itnaa shikaar kara gaya ki vah 18veen shatabdi main vilupt ho gai. sirenia gana main dugong ke alaava teen menati kii jatiyaan bhi hai.
 dugong kaa hazaron varshon se tel or maans ke liye shikaar kiya gaya he. yah ek lambi aayu kii jati he - ausatan dugong 70 varshon ke liye jivit rahete hai. inke prajnan kii gati dhimi he or inaki sankhya dhire-dhire hi badhati he. in kaarnon se inkaa sangrakshan aavashyak ho gaya he or yah ek asurakshit jati ghoshit kar di gai he. kai sthanon par inhen surakshit rakhane ke liye prayaas kare ja rahe hai, masalan bharat kii chilka jhil main.
