 bhartiya rashtriya raksha vishvavidhyalay ek prastaavit vishvavidhyalay he. 
 pradhanmantri dow manmohan singh ne 23 may 2013 ko binola, gudgaanv, haryana main bhartiya rashtriya raksha vishvidhyalay kii aadharshila rakhi. prastaavit bhartiya rashtriya raksha vishvidhyalay 200 acre se adhik bhoomi par sthit hoga. yah 2018 main puri tarah se kaary karnaa shuru karegaa.
 yah vishvavidhyalay purnroop se swytt sanstha hogi. ise sansad ke adhinium ke tahat gathit kiya jayega. raashtrapati vishvavidhyalay ke visitor honge tathaa raksha mantri iske chancelar rahenge.
