 dinar  iraq kii mudra he. yah iraq ke kendriya bank dwaara jari kiya jaataa he or 1,000 fills main samavibhajit he, haalanki mudrasphiti ne fills ko prachlan se baahar kar diya he.
 dinar 1923 main bhartiya rupye ke sthaan par jari kiya gaya, jo pratham vishwa yuddh ke baad british kabje ke dauraan aadhikarik mudra thi. dinar 1959 tak bina mulya parivartan ke british pound ke saath aanki jaati rahi, iske baad ek dinar = 2.8 dollar kii dar par sanyukt rajya america dollar ke saath iskaa akalan kiya jane laga tha.
