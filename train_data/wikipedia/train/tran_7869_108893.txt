 sili dweepasmooh britain ke dakshin-pashchimi chor par sthit cornwall kii nokh ke tat se samudr main kuch aage avasthit ek dweepasmooh he. yah england or united kingdom kaa dakshintam tathaa england kaa pashchimtam bindu he.
 is puure dweepasmooh kii kul aabaadi san 2011 ke aankade ke anusaar 2,203 he. yah cornwall kii rudhispad county kaa hissaa he. haalaanki 1880 se is dweep kii apni swam kaa ek sthaneey prashasan he, jise county council kaa darj praapt he. is dweep kii adhikansh bhoomi cornwall kii dachi ke swaamitv ke adheen aati he. arthvyavastha kaa lihaaz se, paryaton yahan kaa sabase ahm udyog he, is dweep ko bich resort main chottiyaan bitaane ausat samudr tat kaa tutf uthaane ke liye janaa jaataa he. paryaton ke alaava, krishi bhi sthaneey arthvyavastha kaa ek mahatvapurn hissaa he.
