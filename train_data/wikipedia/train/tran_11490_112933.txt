 wilhem maxmilion vunt germany ke chikitsak, darshanik, praadhyaapak the jinhen aadhunik manovigyaan kaa janak mana jaataa he. vunt ne manovigyaan ko vigyaan mana or unhone hi sabase pehle apane aap ko manovaigyanik kaha. ve 'praayogic manovigyaan ke janak' maane jate hai. san 1879 main lipjig vishvavidhyalay main manovigyaan kii pratham prayogshaala sthapit kii. iske baad manovigyaan ko adhyayan kii ek swatantra vidha mana jane laga.
