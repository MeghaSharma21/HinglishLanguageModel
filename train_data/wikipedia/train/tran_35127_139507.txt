 leftinent umr faiyaaz pare bhartiya sena ke ek adhikari the jinka may 2017 main lashkar-e-taiyaba or hijbul mujahiddeen ke aatankwaadiyon ne jammu or kashmir main apharan kar liya or hatya kar di.
 unhen 10 disambar 2016 ko ek leftinent ke rup main main bhartiya sena main kamishan mila or ve 2 batalian, raajapootaana rifles main sevarat the.
 leftinent umr faiyaaz pare kaa 9 may 2017 kii raat ko jammu or kashmir ke shopiyaan jile main unke rishtedaar ke ghar se apharan kar liya gaya. jis samay par unka apharan kiya gaya tab vah duty se chuttee par the or nihathe the. ve apane rishtedaar ke ghar main ek shaadi samaaroh main bhaag lene ke liye aae the. agali subah unka mrit sharir paaya gaya tha, jisase pataa chalaa ki unhen do baar goli maari gai. unki aayu keval taees saal thi. 
 unke parivaar main unke mata-pita or do bahanen hai. unke pita ek kisaan he.
 shahid leftinent umr fayaaj ke antim sanskaar ke samay unhen guard of honor diya gaya or unke antim sanskaar 400 logon ne main bhaag liya tha. 
 ek nihathe sena adhikari leftinent umr fayaaj kii hatya kii soshal media main bade paimaane par aalochana hui. 
 naishanal dephence academy main unka naam smriti kaksh main thik vahiin ankit kiya gaya he, jahaan paramveer chakra vijetaon ke naam ankit hai. shahidi praapt karne vaale ve naishanal dephence academy ke 299wein snaatak hai. bharat ke thalsenadhyaksh general bipin ravat ne unhen shradhanjali dete hue kashmir ke naujavaanon se unki hatya ke khilaaf ekajute hone or unki tarah shanti or tarakkee kii raah par chalane kii apil kii. pahalgam ke army skool kaa naam unke naam par rakhane kii ghoshnaa bhi kii gai he.
