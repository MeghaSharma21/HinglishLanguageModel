 Solid Modeling - thos vastuon ke praaroop banane ko thos pratiman kah sakate hai. iskaa kaam bhautik vastuon ke ganakeey samroop taiyaar karnaa he. iskaa prayog yantriki, anemation, vishleshan or utpaadan jaisi vidhaon main hota he. 
 iskaa uddeshy thos akritiyon ko computer par dikhaaya, rup or aakaar badalanaa, kaat sakna, jod karnaa ityadi jaise kaamon ke liye taiyaar karnaa he. iske model bana dene se isko ekadam sookshm jaalon dwaara aachhadit kar is par bal or gati ke prabhaav ke vishleshan bhi kiye jate hai. iske karne ke liye jin softver yaa takanic kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he unamem AutoCAD, ProEngineer, UG ke naam prasiddh hai. 
 
 iskaa naam jo Solid Modeling kaa arth darshata he - kisi bhautik vastu ko computer par dekhne laayak namoona banane se he. yah thoson ko dekh sakane ke aadharbhoot takanic ke liye prayukt hota he. yah thos vishleshan or computer graphix kii vidha se alag he. 
 prishthbhumi takanic ke taur par isamen sthaan ko yaa akaash ko chote-chote ghan aaytanon main baant diya jaataa he. jab aap koi vastu banaate hai to ye sunishchit kiya jaataa he ki koun kaa chota-ghan aapaki vastu kaa ang he or koun sa nahin. jo chota-ghan aapaki vastu kaa ang he use aapaki vastu kaa rang, chamak or gun ityadi kaa maan de diya jaataa he. iske baad use pradarshit karne yaa vishleshan karne main aasaani hoti he. in anu aaytanon ko Voxel kehete hai. 
 pradarshan ke bare main ye dhyaan rakhen ki kisi apaardarshee vastu ke upari sataha par jo hogi vahi aapako dikhegi, andar kii chiz nahin. agar aapako andar kii chijen dekhni he to thos ko kaatane jaisi kriya kii jaati he.
