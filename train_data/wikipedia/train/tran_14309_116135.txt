 vairagyashatakam bhartruhari ke teen prasiddh shatakon jinhen ki shatakatray kaha jaataa he, main se ek he. isamen vairaygy sambandhi sau shloke hai. shatakatray main anya do hai- shringarshtakam va nitishatakam.
 
 pani kii tarang or bulbule ke samaan is chanchal or nashwar jivan main praaniyon ko bhala sukh kahan mil saktaa he. vriddhavastha main jeern tathaa jhurriyon vaale angon se yukt hokar manushya nar ke samaan parade ke peeche chalaa jaataa he - bhogon ko bhogana samaapt nahin hota apitu vyakti hi samaapt ho jaataa he - "bhoga na bhukta, vayamev bhuktah". is prakaar bhritahari ne yahan sansar kii asarataa or vairaygy ke mahatva kaa pratipaadan kiya he. is shatak main kavy-pratibha or darshaniktaa kaa adbhut samanvay kiya gaya he. isamen sansaarik aakarshanon or bhogon ke prati udaseenta ke ubharate huye bhaavon kaa chithran dikhaayi detaa he. kavi kii to yahi kaamana he ki kisi punyamay arany main shiv-shiv kaa uccharan karte huye uskaa samay bitta jaye.
