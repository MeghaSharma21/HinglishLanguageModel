 khandaan 1979 main anil gaangulii dwaara nirdeshit, sibte hasan rizvi dwaara nirmit hindi film he. is paariwarik katha aadhaarit film ke pramukh kalakaar jitendra, sulakshana pandit va bindiya goswami, sangeetkaar khayyam or geetkaar nakhsh layalapuri he. 
 gaurishankar apni patni, do putr vikaas va ravi ke saath rahata he. vikaas dhani lakdi nanda se vivaah karata he or ravi college kii padhai kar rahaa he. ravi usha ke prati aakarshith hue use vivaah karnaa chahta he. idhar gaurishankar kii sevanivritti hone ko he or vikaas abhi berozegaar he. kuch din baad vikaas ko naukari kaa prastaav aata he jisaliye use 5000 jamaa karana he. nanda apane chachaa se rupaye udhaar liye naresh ko detii he, jisase use naukari milti he. udhar ravi ko apane maalik ke 5000 churaane kaa ilzaam main police giraftaar karti he. adaalat use teen maheene qaid kii sazaa detii he, jise sun gaurishankar kii mrityu hoti he. sazaa ke baad ghar aaye ravi sunata he ke usha kaa vivaah deenanath kaa putr rakesh se nishchay kiya gaya he. ravi bombay jaakar naukari paae apni maan ko paise bhejtaa rahata he. ravi va usha kii agali bhet ke baad hone vaale ghatnaon ko aage kii katha main darshaya gaya he.
