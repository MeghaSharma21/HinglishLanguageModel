 haroshio walpol angl raajnneetijna, kala itihaaskaar, evam sahityakar the. inhen 'horess walpol' bhi kaha jaataa he.
 walpol kaa janm 24 sitambar, 1717 ko landan main hua. inaki shiksha ital or kings kalege, kembridge main hui. yahan par inaki mitrataa bhavi sahityakar tamas grey, richard west, tamas stern henry, samur kan ke or george augustus silvin se hui. ye may, 1741 main sansad sadsy nirvaachit hue, kintu raajnneetik jivan kii safalataa santoshjanak na hone se inhone sansad se avkaash grahan kar liya. apane pita ke prabhaav se inhen sarkari naukari gai, jahaan par inhone 1745 se 1784 tak kaary kiya. 1747 main inhone tamas nadi ke kinare 'vila ov stebairi hill' kray kiya, or yahan par ek mudranalay sthapit kiya, jiskaa naam afisina arabbatiyana rakhaa. is mudranalay se inaki bahut si rachanaaon ke pratham sanskaran prakashit hue. yah aaajeevan gathiya ke rog se peedit rahe. 2 march, 1797 ko avivaahit hi inkaa dehant ho gaya.
 inaki pramukh rachanaayen is prakaar hai :
 concil ov aantraanto ek prakaar kaa romaanchakaari upanyaas he. historic dout oun di life and rain ov king richard di third inaki pehali kriti he, jisamen anusandhan or aitihaasik tathyon dwaara raja richard ke kukhyat charitra ko thik thik samajhaayaa gaya he. di misterius madar inkaa du:khant upanyaas he. anecdots ov painting in england angl chitrakala kii abhootpurv pustak he. ye apni dainiki main angl raajnneeti kii mukhya ghatnaayen likhaa karte the. unka inaki mrityu ke pashchaat sampadan hua. ye patron ke kushal lekhak the. inaki gananaa angl bhashaa ke swatantra patralekhkon main hoti he.
