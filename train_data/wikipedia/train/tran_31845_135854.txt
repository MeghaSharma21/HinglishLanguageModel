 Arun Thapa ek prasiddh nepali gayak or geetkaar he. 
 .arun thapa kaa janm kathmandu, nepal main 1 9 52 main hua tha. unke pita ganju prasad thapa the unke mata-pita nepal waapas chale gaye, jab arun teen maheene kaa tha. unhone kathmandu, nepal main adhyayan kiya or bada hua. unhone sabase pehle gaanaa baahal tole main ek skool main padhai kii, phir cheppatapati main jepi skool main. baad main unhone darjeeling main turnbul hai skool main padhai kii, jo 1 9 71 main kathmandu se skool chhodane kaa pramaan patr puraa karne ke liye waapas aa gaya. baad main unhone kala kii degree main intermediat ke liye prithvi narayan campus, pokhara main adhyayan kiya, lekin jald hi padhai chhod sangeet main apana keriar banane kii talash main nikal padaa.
 unhone pehale apane college main gaanaa shuru kiya, jahaan use ek ladki ke saath pyaar ho gaya. unke adhikansh gane unke rishte kii yaad main the. unhone kai gane record kiye lekin unke kariyar ko super hit geet jati maaya lei pani se shuru kiya man jaataa he  jahaan unhone saarvajanik sneh praapt kiya. ek lokapriy gayak hone se pehle, unhone nepal bank limited main kaam kiya tha. deep shreshth unke sabase achche dost the. ve hameshaa ek saath ghoomte rahenge. unhone pehali baar 1971 main radio nepal main apana geet .
 thapa lambey samay tak fefde or yakrut kii beemaari se peedit tha. 22 july 1999 ko unka nidhan ho gaya kathmandu main he.
 unhone shuru ke do sangeet album: arun or sabase acha ke arun. donon ke album bahut lokapriy the ke bich nepali sangeet shrotaaon. thapa jari rakhaa tha apane gayan karier ke 30 saal tak jari rakhaa
