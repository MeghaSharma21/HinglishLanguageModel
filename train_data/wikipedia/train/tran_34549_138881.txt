 shahada ek he or mohammad allaah ke bheje gaye preshit hai. yah elaan sookshm rup se is tarah he:
 har musalmaan is baat ko prakat karata he ki "allaah ek he, or mohammad, allaah ke rasul hai", yahi vishvas kaa mul dhatu or stambh he. 
 shahada gavaahi ko kehete hai, gnaan kosh main, nyaay kosh main bhi is shabd ko istemaal kiya jaataa he, jo ke gavaahi ke liye istemaal hota he, chaahe vah karja, hadise, buraai, yaa talaak ke vakt main ho. 
 islamic pad kosh main or kuran ke sandarbh main yah shabd "vishvas prakat" ke liye istemaal kiya jaataa he. shahada padhnaa yaa bolnaa, yaa prakat karnaa har muslim ke liye zaruri he. isi se imaan yaa vishvas sweekaar or prakat hota he.
 in chitron main, bahut saare dhvaj hai jin par "shahada" likhaa hua he. 
 bab-al-phutuh/bab al-nasr, fatimid kairo main "shahada" or ali vaali allaah kaa ijapha.
 najd kaa dhvaj
 saudi arab kaa dhvaj
 mankus kaa sone kaa dinar. abbasi dinaaron ki nakal . aaj bhi "mohammad, allaah ke preshit hai" likhaa hua milegaa.
 ebn tulun masjid kairo main, imam mustansir kaa kibala, shiya kalima likhaa hua.
