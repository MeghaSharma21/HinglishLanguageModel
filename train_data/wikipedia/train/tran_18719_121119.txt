 kachari ek junglee lata he jiske pattey kakadi ke patton jaise hote hai. isamen pile-pile phool lagate hai.iskaa fal sabji ke rup main prayog kiya jaataa he. iskaa upyog maance ke mridukaran ke liye bhi kiya jaataa he. kachari ko ayurveda main 'mrigakshi' kaha jaataa he or yah bigade hue zukam, pitt, kaph, kabj, parameh sahit kai rogon main behtareen dawa maani gai he.
