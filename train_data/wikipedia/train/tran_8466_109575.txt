 mrudaa main lavan kii maatra ko mrudaa lavanta kehete hai. mrudaa main namak kii maatra badhane kii kriya lavnikaran kehalaati he. namak, prakritik rup se mitti tathaa jal main paaya jaataa he. mrudaa kaa lavnikaran prakritik prakriya dwaara ho saktaa he jaise, khanij apkshay yaa samudr ke kramshah dur jane se. sinchai karne aadi kritrim kriyaaon se bhi mrudaa kii lavanta bad sakati he.
