  zollywood jharkhand kaa cinema he jo mul rup se jhaari bhashaa kii filmon kaa nirmaan karata he.iske alaava khortha bhashaa evam santhali main bhi filmein banati hai.
 pratham nagpuri chalchitr sona kar nagpur tha. jo 1994 main pradarshit hua jiskaa nirmaan dhananjay singh ne kiya. bhartiya cinema main jharkhand ke cinema ke aa jane ke baad kalakaaron ko adhik kaam milanaa prarambh ho gaya to jharkhand main bade bade film kaarykram hone lage. jharkhand main film nirmaan ko badhaane ke liye jharkhand cinema ko zollywood naam diya gaya. aaj 100 varsh ke bharatiya cinema ke itihaas main zollywood kaa apana mahatv he.
