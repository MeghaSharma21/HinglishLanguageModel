  koharaa yaa fog praayah thandi ardra hawa main banataa he or iske astitv main aane kii prakriya badalon jaisi hi hoti he. garm hawa kii apeksha thandi hawa adhik nami lene main sakasham hoti he or vaashpan ke dwaara yah nami grahan karti he. ye vah badal hota he jo bhoomi ke nikat banataa he. yani ek badal kaa vah bhaag jo bhoomi ke upar hawa main tehra hua ho koharaa nahin hota balki badal kaa vah bhaag jo upree bhoomi ke sampark main aata he, koharaa kehalaata he. iske atirikt koharaa kai anya tareekon se bhi banataa he. lekin adhikansh kohre do shreniyon, advection fog or radiation fog main badal jate hai. donon hi prakaar main koharaa aam hawa se adhik thanda mahasoos hota he. aisa usamen bhari hui nami ke kanon ke kaaran hota he.
 advection fog tab banataa he jab garm hawa kaa ek vishesh hissaa kisi nam pradesh ke upar pahunchataa he. kai baar koharaa kaaphi ghanaa bhi hota he jisase dur dekhne main pareshaani mahasoos hoti he. samudr kinare rahane vaale log advection fog se parichit hote hai. 
 radiation fog tab banataa he jab dharti kii upree parat thandi hoti he. aisa praayah shaam ke samay hota he. dharti kii upree parat thandi hone ke saath hi hawa bhi thandi ho jaati he, jis kaaran koharaa upajataa he. 
 koharaa kai pahari ghaatiyon main bhi chaataa he. vahaan upree garm hawa thandi hawa ko jameen ke nikat rakhti he. aisa koharaa praayah subah ke samay hota he. suraj nikalne ke baad thandi hawa garm hoti he or upar uthati he. iske baad se koharaa chantnaa shuru ho jaataa he.
