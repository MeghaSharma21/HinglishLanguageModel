 shanti yaa sukh kaa anubhav karnaa yaa bodh karnaa alokik gyaan praapt karne jaisaa he, yah tabhi sambhav he, jab aap purnatah swasth hon. acha swasthya praapt karne ke yun to kai tareeke hai, unamem se hi ek aasaan tarika he yogasana va pranayam karnaa.ham aapako padmasana se parichit karavaate hai-
 vidhi: jameen par baithkar baayein pair kii edi ko dayee jangha par is prakaar rakhte hai ki edi naabhi ke paas aa jaaen. iske baad daayen paanv ko uthaakar bain jangha par is prakaar rakhen ki donon ediyaan naabhi ke paas aapas main mil jaaen.
 merudand sahit qamar se upree bhaag ko purnataya sidhaa rakhen. dhyaan rahe ki donon ghutane jameen se uthne na paaen. tatpashchaat donon haathon kii hatheliyon ko god main rakhte hue sthir rahen. isko punah paanv badalakar bhi karnaa chahie. phir drushti ko nasagrabhag par sthir karke shant baith jaaen.
 samaran rahe ki dhyaan, samadhi aadi main baithne vaale asana main merudand, katibhaag or sir ko sidhaa rakhaa jaataa he or sthirtapurvak baithna hota he. dhyaan samadhi ke kaal main netra band kar lenaa chahie. aankhe deergh kaal tak khuli rahane se aankhon kii taralata nasht hokar unamem vikaar paidaa ho jane kii sambhaavna rahati he.
 yah asana paanvon kii vaataadi anek vyaadhiyon ko dur karata he. vishesh kar katibhaag tathaa taangon kii sandhi evam tatsambandhit nas-naadiyon ko lachak, drudh or sphurtiyukt banaata he. shwasan kriya ko sam rakhata he. indriya or man ko shant evam ekagra karata he.
