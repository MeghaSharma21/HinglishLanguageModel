 rashtriya dizin or utpaada vikaas kendra kii sthapana 1999 main bharat sarkaar ke vastra mantralay ke vikaas ayukta dwaara kii gai thi. rashtriya dizin or utpaada vikaas kendra board kaa pratinidhitiv pratishthit niryaatak, niti-nirdharak or vyapaar tathaa udyog ke pratishthit vyakti karte hai. bharat sarkaar ke vastra mantralay ke vikaas ayukta ensidipidi ke paden adhyaksh hote hai. yah kendra sadsy aadhaarit sanstha he jo pramukh taur par hastshilp kshetra kaa pratinidhitiv karti he. dizin/utpaada vikaas or praudyogiki ke kaaryakshetron main thos maal kshetra ke antar ko puraa karne ke uddeshy ke saath isaki shuruyaat kii gai thi. samoohon ke liye pan india aadhaar par dizin or utpaada vikaas gatividhiyon ko puraa karne ke liye yah kendra vikaas ayukta ke kareebi sanidhya main kaary karata he. is kendra ke tahat bahut saare rashtriya or antar-rashtriya diziner visheshikrut dizin/utpaada sevaaon kii sampurna shrenee pradaan karne ke liye karyrat he.
 sevaayen dizin or utpaada vikaas, pradarshnee dizin, graphic-dizin or package dizin jaise kai kshetron main yah kendra apni sevaayen pradaan karata he. iske atirikt ensidipidi satat aapurti prabandhan shrinkhalaa kaa srijan bhi karata he. ensidipidi main gunvattta or praudyogiki unnayan ke saath hi dizinron kii madad se diginon ko utpaadon main tabdeel bhi kiya jaataa he. dizin kendron, utpaada kendron/sankulon par dizin bank kii sthapana ke jariye ensidipidi dhanchaagat sahyog pradaan karata he. dizin or utpaada vikaas ko badhaava dene ke liye yah audyogic samoohon ko shuru se ant tak dizin sevaayen pradaan karata he. is kendra main bade paimaane par utpaadan ke liye moolbhoot kaaryon main mashinikaran ke liye takneekee sahaayta bhi uplabdh he.
