 zofaar muhafazah oman kaa ek muhafazah he.
 'zofaar' ko arabi lipi main 'ظفار‎' likhaa jaataa he jiskaa pehla akshar 'ظ‎' bhartiya upmahadweep, afghanistan or iran main 'za' kaa uccharan rakhata he, jabaki arab deshon main iskaa uccharan 'da' yaa 'dha' se miltaa-julata kiya jaataa he. isliye is kshetra or praant ke naam ko - 'zofaar', 'dophaar' or 'dhofaar' - teenon uccharanon ke saath paaya jaataa he.
