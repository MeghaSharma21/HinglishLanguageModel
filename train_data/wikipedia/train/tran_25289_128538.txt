 nishastrikarannishastreekaran vishwa shanti kaa ek pavitra saadhan he yah yudhon ko samaapt kar maanavtaa ki sachhee seva kaa maarg prashast karne kaa ek aandolan he maanav itihaas main lagbhag 14,500 chote bade yuddh lade gaye or do baar vishwayuddh kaa bhi saamana bhi karnaa padaa in yudhon main jan dhan ki haani or aanvik astron ke prayog ne sampurna maanav sabayata ke vinaash kaa khatra utpann kar diya is se sampurna vishwa main nishastreekaran ke prayaas jo pakadaney lage.nishastreekaran kaa arth hinsa ke samast bhautik or maanviya sadhano kaa unmoolan karnaa he. is kaarykram kaa uddeshy hathiyaaron kii hode roak kar aarthik vikaas ko sunishchit karnaa he.morgan thao ke anusaar "nishastreekaran se taatpary sahshtron kii hode ko samaapt karne se he"
 nishastreekaran multah do prakaaron kaa hota he. gunatmak or maatraatmak.
 aavashyaktaa:-
 varttman antarrashtriya raajnneetik paridrishy main nishastreekaran kaa mul prerak shastreekaran kii hode he. jisane sampurna vishwa ko aatankit kar rakhaa he. ek anumaan ke anusaar varttman main uplabdh parmaanu astron se lagbhag 30 baar puri prithvi ko nestnabut kiya ja saktaa he. nishastreekaran kaa anya prerak vishwa shanti kii sthapana kaa dhyey bhi he. -Atul sahu 67676
