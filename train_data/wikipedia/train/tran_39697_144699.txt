 paryaavaran adhinium 1986 ek vyapak vidhaan he isaki rup rekha kendriya sarkaar ke vibhinn kendriya or rajya praadhikaranon ke kriyakalapon ke samanvayan ke liye taiyaar kiya gaya he jinaki sthapana pichley kanoonon ke tahat kii gai he jaisaaki jal adhinium1974 or vaayu adhinium,1981. maanav paryaavaran kii raksha or sudhaar karne evam ped-paudhe or sampatti kaa chhodkar maanav jati ko aapadaa se bachaane ke liye ipie paarit kiya gaya, yah kendra sarkaar kaa paryaavarniya gunvattta kii raksha karne or sudhaarney, sabhi sroton se pradooshan niyantran kaa niyantran or kam karne or paryaavarniya aadhaar par kisi audyogic suvidha kii sthapana karnaa/sanchaalan karnaa nishedh yaa pratibandhit karne kaa adhikaar detaa he.
 ipie kii sambhaavna vyapak he paryaavaran kii paribhaasha main jal, vaayu, bhoomi or jal, vaayu, bhoomi or maanav jati ke or anya jivit jeev-jantu vanaspati, sookshm jeev evam sampatti ke bich maujuud paraspar sambandh shaamil he. kanoon aapadaa janak apshisht aapadaajanak apshisht prabandhan or prachaalanon sambandhi bhi niyam lagu karata he. adhinium main sanchaalkon, praadhikrut karne kii paristhitiyaan, niptaan sthalon kii sthitiyaan, aapadaajanak apshisht aayaat karne ke niyamon, durghatnaon kii soochna dena, packaging or labling apekshaayen or sambhavit sanchaalkon ke liye apil karne kii prakriya jinhen praadhikrut nahin kiya gaya he, ko bhi paribhaashit kiya gaya he. niyamon ke vinirmaan aapadaajanak or nashile rasayanon kaa bhandaran or aayaat sookshm jeevon, prajnan rup se ejineering jeevon yaa selon par lagu kiya gaya he.
