 nishaan-e-imtiyaaz pakistan rajya ke rajya kii sabhy sajaavat main se ek he. pakistan ke liye vishwa manyata yaa desh ke liye utkrisht seva kii disha main unki uplabdhiyon ke aadhaar par pakistan main kisi bhi naagarik ko yah sarvocch sammaan diya jaataa he. yah puraskaar pakistan ke nagarikon tak hi simit nahin he, jabaki yah ek naagarik puraskaar he, ise sainya karmiyon ko bhi sammanit kiya ja saktaa he or vardi par pahana ja saktaa he. nishan, sajaavat / aadesh ke rup main anuwaad karata he, ek atyadhik pratibandhit or pratishthit puraskaar he jo lagbhag swatantraaa ke raashtrapati padak or british samrajya ke aadesh ke baraabar he, or yah order of imtiyaaz kaa pehla shrenee puraskaar he. anya teen avarohi shreniyan hilaal-e-imtiyaaz, seetara-e-imtiyaaz or tamgha-e-imtiyaaz hai. aam taur par, ise pakistan main ucchtam puraskaar mana ja saktaa he kyonki ucch puraskaar nishan-e-pakistan ko keval rajyon ke videshi pramukhon se sammanit kiya jaataa he.
