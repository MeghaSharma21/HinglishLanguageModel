 motopa dur karnaa he to bhastrika pranayam karen 
 bhastrika pranayam
 bhastrika he dhaunkani. is pranayam main saans kii gati dhaunkani kii tarah ho jaati he. yani shwaas kii prakriya ko jaldi-jaldi karnaa hi bhastrika pranayam kehalaata he.
 vidhi: padmasana yaa phir sukhasana main baith jaaen. qamar, gardan, peeth evam ridh kii haddi ko sidhaa rakhte hue sharir ko bilkul sthir rakhen. iske baad bina sharir ko hilaae donon nasika chhidr se aawaaj karte hue shwaas bharen. karte hue hi shwaas ko baahar choden. ab tej gati se aawaaj lete hue saans bharen or baahar nikaalen. yahi kriya bhastrika pranayam kehalaati he. hamaare donon haath ghutane par gyaan mudra main rahenge or ankhen band rahengi. dhyaan rahe, shwaas lete or chhodte vakt hamaari lay naa toote.
 laabh va prabhaav: is pranayam ke abhyaas se motopa dur hota he. sharir ko pranavayu adhik maatr main milti he or carbon-daai-oxide sharir se baahar nikalati he. is pranayam se rakt kii safai hoti he. sharir ke sabhi angon tak rakt kaa sanchaar bhalee-bhaanti hota he. jathragni tej ho jaati he. dama, tivi or saanson ke rog dur ho jate hai. fefde ko bal miltaa he, snaayumandal sabal hota he. vaat, pitt or kaph ke dosha dur hote he. isse pachan santhaan, liver or kidney kii massage hoti he.
 saavdhaaniyaan: ucch raktchaap, hriday rogi, hernia, ulcer, mirgee stroke vaale or garbhvatee mahilaayein iskaa abhyaas naa karen.
 vishesh: abhyaas se pehle 2 gilaas jal avashya len. shuru-shuru main aaraam lekar abhyaas karen. zyada laabh uthaanaa ho to ise yoga guru ke saannnidhya main hi karen.
