 yama hai ham ek hasya dharawahik he jo sab tivi par prasaarit hota he. kaarykram main ek puranic kirdaar yamraj kii prithvi par jaadui or hasyaprad safar ko dikhaaya gaya he.
 isamen dikhaaya gaya he ki logon ke man main yamraj ke prati kya bhaavana he or apane prati logon kii avadharana badalane ke liye yamraj apane saathi chitragupta ke saath prithvi par aate he or logon ko apana kartavya samajhaate he. kaarykram main yamraj 21veen sadi kii dharti kaa bhraman karte hai.
 iske kuch samay ke baad vah baldev kapur ke ghar main aa jate hai. uski patni bindiya un donon ko apana bhai bana leti he. baldev un donon ke ghar main rahane se bilkul khush nahin hota he. kai baar yamraj or chitragupta ke kaaryon ke kaaran vah pareshaan ho jaataa he. lekin uskaa parivaar yamraj or chitragupta se pyaar karata he, is kaaran vah kuch nahin bol paata he.
