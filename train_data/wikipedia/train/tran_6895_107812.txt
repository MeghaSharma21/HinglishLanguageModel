 1 agast 2000 ko pahalgam main amarnath yatra par aatanki akraman hua. isamen 30 log maare gaye. pahalgam bharat ke jammu evam kashmir rajya ke anantnaag zile main he. shraddhalu salanaa amarnath yatra ko nikle the jab islamic aatankvaadeeyonne yah akraman kiya.iske baad, bharat ke pradhan mantri atal bihari vaajapeyi ne pahalgam kaa daura kiya or hatyaon ke liye lashkar-e-taiyaba ko doshi tehraaya.
