 comodor ek nausena rank he jo kai nausenaon main upyog kiya jaataa he jo nausena captain se varisht hota he, lekin rier admiral kanishtha hota he gaiyr-angrezi-bhashi raashtra aksar samakaksh ke rup main flotila admiral yaa counter admiral yaa varisht captain kaa pad upyog karte hai, haalanki counter admiral bhi rier admiral ke anuroop ho sakate hai.
 paramparaagat rup se, "comodor" ek samay main ek se adhik jahaajon kaa aadesh dene vaale kisi bhi adhikari ke liye rank athwa pad he. ek aadhikarik rank ke rup main, ek camodor aamtaur par jahaajon ke ek flitala yaa squadran ko ek bade task force yaa admiral dwaara kii gai nausena bede ke bhaag ke rup main niyantrit karata he.
