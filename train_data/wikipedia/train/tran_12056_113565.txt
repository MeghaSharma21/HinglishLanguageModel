 yri ganatantric sena yaa irish republican army / ireland kii mukti ke liye gathit krantikari sainikon kaa sanghathan tha. iskaa lakshya ireland ko britain se purnatah mukt karana tha. 'irish republican brotherhud' naamak sanghathan iskaa pitrusanghathan tha jisaki sthapana 25 navambar 1913 main kii gayi thi. 6 disambar 1921 main, jab angl-yri-sandhi hui or yudhviram lagu hua to, isamen vibhaajan ho gaya.
 san 1919 se july 1921 tak aa.ga.se. ne ireland main british sena ke viruddh chhapamar yuddh kiya. is yuddh kaa sabase bheeshan kaal 1920 ke navambar se lekar 1921 ke july tak tha.
