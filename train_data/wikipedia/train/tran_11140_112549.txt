 rashtriya shikshak shiksha parishad  bharat sarkaar kii ek sanstha he, jisaki sthapana rashtriya shikshak shiksha parishad adhinium, 1993 ke antargat 17 agast, 1995 main kii gai thi. iskaa uttardaayitv bhartiya shiksha pranali ke maanak, prakriyaayein evam dhaaraon kii sthapana evam nirikshan karnaa he.
 1973 ke purv rashtriya adhyaaapak shiksha parishad kii bhoomika adhyaaapak shiksha se sambandhit sabhi vishyo par kendriya or rajya sarkaro ke liye ek salaahkaar nikaay ke rup main thi. parishad kaa sachivalay rashtriya shekshik anushandhan tathaa prashikshan parishad, ke adhyaaapak shiksha vibhaag main sthit tha. shaikshanik kshetra main apane prashansaneey kaary ke babajood parishad, adhyaaapak shiksha main maanako ko banaaye rakhane tathaa ghatiya adhyaaapak shiksha sansthaanon kii barotari ko rokane ke apane anivaarye viniamak kaary nahin kar saki thi.
 rashtriya shiksha niti 1986 or uske adheen kaary yojana main adhyaaapak shiksha pranali ko sarvatha durust karne ke liye pehle upaay ke rup main sanvidhik darje or apekshit sansadhano se yukt rashtriya adhyaaapak shiksha prarishad kii kalpana kii gai thi. ek savidhik nikaay ke rup main rashtriya adhyaaapak shiksha prarishad adhinium 1993 ke adheen rashtriya adhyaaapak shiksha prarishad 17 agast 1995 se astitav main ai.
 rashtriya adhyaaapak shiksha prarishad kaa mul uddeshy samooche bharat main adhyaaapak shiksha pranali kaa niojit or samanvit vikaas karnaa, adhyaaapak shiksha pranali main mandandon or maanako kaa viniman tathaa unhe samuchit rup se banaaye rakhana or tatsambandhi vishay hai.
 rashtriya adhyaaapak shiksha parishad kaa mukhyalay dilli main he tathaa bhopal, bhubaneshwar, bangaleru tathaa jaipur main isaki kshetriy samitiyaan hai.
