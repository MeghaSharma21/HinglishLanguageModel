 hongasandra venkatarmaia sheshadri ek bhartiya lekhak va samajasevi the. unka janm bangalore main hua tha. bangalore vishvavidhyalay se rasayan shaastra main snatakottar oopaadhi lene ke baad ve rashtriya swayamsevak sangh ke siddhanton se prabhavit hue or apana puraa jivan sangh kii vichaardhaara ke sanvardhan hetu samarpit kar diya.
 sheshadri ji ne san 1946 main vataur ek prachaarak ke sangh kaa kaary karnataka se prarambh kiya. sangh kaary vistaar main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaate hue ve san 1987 main iske sarkarywah bane. unhen unki kriti torberalu par karnataka rajya sahitya academy se san 1982 main sammanit kiya gaya. unhone 'vikram', 'utthan', 'organizer' va paanchajnya sareekhe patr-patrikaon main lekh bhi likhe. unhone seetaram goyal va keaar malkani jaise diggaj lekhakon kii aalochana karke unhen 'organizer' sareekhe patron se baahar kaa rasta dikhaa diya. seetaram goyal ne unki pustak "The Tragic Story of Partition" kii saraahna kii. baad main purv pradhanmantri shri atal bihari vaajapeyi ne bhi unke is kaary kii prashansa kii. sheshadri ji sangh ke varishthatam or atyadhik sammanit netaaon main the jinhone hajaaron swayamsevakon ko prerit kiya. desh or deshavaasiyon kii seva karte hue unhone san 2005 main antim saans lee. unki antyeshthi main baari sankhya main log sammilit hue.
