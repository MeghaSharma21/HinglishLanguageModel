 vishwa dharohar samiti, un sthalon kaa chayan karti he jinhen unesco ke vishwa dharohar sthalon ke rup main soochibaddh kiya ja saktaa he. yah vishwa dharohar kanvension ke karyanvayan ke liye jimmedaar he, vishwa dharohar kosh ke istemaal ko paribhaashit karata he or raashtra dalon se anurodh par vittiya sahaayta kaa aavanton karti he. iskaa gathan 21 raashtra dalon se milakar hota he, jinka chunaav raashtra dalon kii mahasabha dwaara chaar saal ke kaarykaal ke liye hota he. 
 vishwa dharohar kanvension ke niyamanusaar, samiti ke sadsy raashtra kaa kaarykaal chah saal ke liye hota he, lekin kai raashtra dal swechha se keval chaar saal ke liye hi samiti ke sadsy bane rahanaa sweekaar karte hai taaki, duusare raashtra dalon ko bhi samiti kaa sadsy banane kaa mauka mil sake. udhaaharan ke liye, 15 veen mahasabha ke liye nirvaachit sabhi sadasyon ne swechha se apana kaarykaal chah varsh se gata kar chaar varsh karne kaa faisla kiya.
