 vinod raay bharat ke 11wein niyantrak evam mahalekhapareekshak the. is pad par ve 7 janvari 2008 se 22 may 2013 tak the. upie sarkaar dwaara kiye gayi lakhon karod rupee ke tuji spectrum ghotaalaa evam koyala ghotaalaa kii sansanikhej reporton ke kaaran ve charchaa main aaye the. samprati ve sanyukt raashtra ke bahari lekhaparikshakon ke adhyaksh hai. 
 ve dilli vishvavidhyalay ke hindu college se arthshaastra main em0e0 hai iske atirikt unhone harvard vishvavidhyalay se lok prashasan main snatakottar kii oopaadhi bhi praapt kar rakhi he. 1972 batch ke ai0e0es0 adhikari rahe vinod raay isse purv kai mahatvapurn padon par kaary kar chuke hai.
 jab pradhanmantri karyaalay ke rajya mantri ve0 narayansami ne saarvajanik rup se media ke saamne yah bayaan diya ki "sieji ko sarkari skiimon main ho rahe skaimon par apni tippanee dene kaa koi adhikaar hi nahin he, isse bharat ke niyantrak evam mahalekhaakaar kii kaaryastylee par prashnchinh lagata he." to vinod raay ko swayan apane bachaav main kahana padaa ki sieji kaa yah moolbhoot or naitik daayitv he ki vah sarkaar ke kaamkaaj main dakhal na dete hue bhi aarthik maamalon main paayi gayi aniyamitataayen use bataaye taaki samvidhan dwaara pradutt adhikaron kii raksha kii ja sake or sarkaar par niyantran bana rahe. yadi aisa nahin kiya gaya to yah desh kii janata ke saath vishwasghaat hoga..
