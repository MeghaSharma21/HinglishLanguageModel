 beghar yaa niraashrayata ek niyamit rup se nivas ke bina logon kii haalat he. beghar log adhiktar samay, niyamit rup se surakshit or paryaapt awaas banaae rakhane main asamarth hote hai yaa niyamit rup se tay or paryaapt raat ke samay nivas nahin juta paat. beghar kii kanoonee paribhaasha vibhinn deshon main, yaa ek hi desh yaa kshetra ke alag alag adhikaar kshetra alag nyaayalay ke bich badal jaataa he. beghar shabd ke antargat un sabhi vyaktiyon ko sammilit kiya ja saktaa he jinka raat ke samay praathmik nivas sthaan ek beghar aashray, ek varming centre, ek gharelu hinsa aashray, ek vaahan yaa phir gatte ke baxon, tent, tirpal yaa anya asthaayee sthiti main rahete hai. america sarkaar ke beghar ganan adhyayanon main aise vyaktiyon ko shaamil kiya gaya he jo ki kisi aise saarvajanik yaa nijee sthaan main sote hon jo kii manushya ke liye ek niyamit rup se awaas ke rup main istemaal ke liye 
 ek anumaan ke anusaar, san 2005 ke andar duniya bhar main 10 karod beghar vyakti the. akele britain main 80,000 se adhik beghar log hai or yah sankhya har saal bad rahi he. pashchimi deshon main, beghar logo ke bich bahut badi sankhya purushon kii he. isamen vishesh rup se kaaphi bada bhaag ekal purushon kaa he. 
 adhikansh desh beghar logon kii sahaayta ke liye vibhinn prakaar kii sevaayen pradaan karte hai. yah desh, sarkari vibhaagon yaa phir saamudayik sanghathano kii sahaayta se bhojan, aashray or kapade pradaan karte hai. in kaaryakramon ko sarkaar, daan sanghathanon, charchon or vyaktigat daataaon dwaara samarthit kiya jaataa he. kai shaharon main beghar logon ko rojgaar ke avasar uplabdh karaane ke liye sadak samachaarpatr, prakashnon ko sthapit kiya gaya he. kuch begharon ke paas naukariyaan hai, vahiin baaki aay ke liye anya tareeke apnaane padate hai. bheekh maangana yaa aarthik sahaayta lenaa ek vikalp he, lekin kai shaharon main yah teji se avaidh hota ja rahaa
