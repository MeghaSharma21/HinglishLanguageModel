 jonathan "johny" lee miller  ek angrez abhinayta hai. shuruaati dinon ke dauraan inhen sarvaadhik 1996 kii filmon trainspotting or havers main inaki bhoomikaaon ke liye janaa jaataa tha. miller varsh 2008 tak kam saarvajanik vikhyati ke saath filmon or rangamanch main lagaataar kaam karaten rahen, 2008 main inhone america tivi siries eli stone ke do satron main abhinay kiya iske baad hi yah broadway naatak after miss juli or bibisi nirmit emma main pramukh bhoomikaaon main nazar aae. 2010 main yah shotime shrinkhalaa dextor ke paanchaven satr ke mukhya pratipakshi jordan chess kii bhoomika main dikhen. 2011 main inhone benedict kambarbatch ke saath royal neshnal theatre main yojit frenkstin naatak main abhinay kiya. may 2011 main ghoshnaa kii gai thi ki yah tim bartan kii film dark shadows main roger colins kaa kirdaar nibhaayenge.
