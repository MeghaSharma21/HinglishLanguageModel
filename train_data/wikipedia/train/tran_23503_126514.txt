 devendra satyaarthee hindi, urdu or punjabi bhashaaon ke vidwaan tathaa sahityakar hai. unka mul naam devinder batta he. shri satyaarthee loakgeet adhyayan ke pranetaon me se rahe hai. unhone desh ke kone-kone kii yatra kar vahaan ke lokajeevan, geeton or paramparaon ko aatmsaat kiya or unhen pustakon or vaartaon main sangrahit kar diya jiske liye ve 'lokyatri' ke rup main jane jate hai.
 devendra satyaarthee ne lokgeeton kaa sangrah karne hetu desh ke vibhinn kshetren kii yatraayen kii thim tathaa in sthanon ke sansmaranon ko bhavatmak style main unhone likhaa he. "kya gori kya saanwali" tathaa "rekhaayen bol uthin" satyaarthee ke sansmaranon ke apane dhang ke sangrah hai.
 shri satyaarthee kaa janm 28 may 1908 ko patiala ke kai sau saal puraane bhadaud gram main janm. 1977 main bharat sarkaar dwaara padmashri se sammanit. 
 hindi main lokyaan 'dharti gaati he' kaa prakaashan hua 1948 main. 'bela foole aadhi raat'-1949, 'baajat aave dhol'-1950, 'chitron main lotiyaan'-1951 main prakashit huin.
 hindi main aapke sangrah hai : 'chattan se puch lo', 'chaay kaa rang', 'nae dhan se pehle', 'sadak nahin bandook' . upanyaason main 'rath ke pahie', 'kathputli,, 'doodh gachh', 'brahmaputra', 'katha kaho, urvashi' or 'teri kasam sutluj' pramukh hai. 
 aatmakathaatmak lekhan hindi main : 'chand-suraj ke veeran', 'nilyakshini' or 'safarnama pakistan' hai.
 niabandh va rekhachitr : 'hindiah ek yug, ek pratik' ,' rekhaaen bol uthin, 'kya gori kya saanwari', 'kala ke hastakshar' vividh mahatvapurn granthon kaa sampadan bhi aapane kiya.
