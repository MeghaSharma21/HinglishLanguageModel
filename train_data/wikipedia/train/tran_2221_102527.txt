 kantactor ek vidyut-niyantrit switch he jiske samparkon dwaara badi maatra kii vidyut shakti ko band yaa chaalu kiya jaataa he. contactor kii pramukh visheshtaayen jo ise riley se alag karti hai, ye hai: 
 riley kii bhanti ye bhi vidyut-chumbak ke siddhant par kaam karte hai. inamen bhi bahut kam vidyut shakti kaa prayog karte hue bahut adhik vidyut shakti ko joda/todaa jaataa he, lekin circuit braker ke vipareet kantactor short circuit dhara ko todane ke liye nahin hote.
