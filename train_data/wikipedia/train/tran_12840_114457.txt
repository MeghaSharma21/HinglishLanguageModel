 kuzadaar pakistan ke balochistan praant kaa ek zila he. yah parmparik rup se jhalawan kshetra kaa bhaag he or kalaat khaanat kaa hissaa tha. zile kii rajdhani kuzadaar shahar he, jo balochistan praant kaa doosraa sabase bada nagar he.
 'kuzadaar' shabd main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
 zila shushk or ardh-registani kshetra main sthit he. zile kii purvi seemayen sindh se or dakshini seemayen las bela zile se lagi hui hai. zile kii rajdhani kuzadaar sindh kii rajdhani karachi se 400 kimi dur or balochistan kii rajdhani va sabase bade shahar queta se 300 kimi kii duuri par basii hui he. pakistan se nikalakar iran or phir turki or yunaan jane vaala rajmarg isi zile se guzarataa he.
 balochistan kii sabase unchi pahadiyaan, jinaki shrunkhlaa shashaan pahadiyaan kehalaati he, kuzadaar zile main hi sthit hai.
