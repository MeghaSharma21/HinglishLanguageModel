 jet conect ke rup main sanchalit jetlite mumbai, bharat main aadhaarit ek vayuseva thi. ise pehle jet air wez conect ke naam se janaa jaataa tha, jet lit india limited kaa ek vyavsaayik naam he. yah mumbai main sthit ek vimaanan seva he jis par kii jet airways kaa malikana haq he. yah vimaan seva bharat ke sabhi metropol shaharon ko jodne ke liye niyamit udaan sevaayen pradaan karti he.
 is air line kii sthapana 20 sitambar 1991 ko hui thi tathaa isne 3 disambar 1993 ko sahaara airlines ke rup main, apni sevaayen pradaan kerani shuru kar di. isamen is samay tak do bowing 737 – 200 air crefton ko shaamil kiya gaya tha.
