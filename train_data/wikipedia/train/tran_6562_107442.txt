 bisalari multah ek italvi company he, jiskaa gathan phelis bisalari ne kiya tha. bharat main bottleband pani bechney kii soch inhen kii thi. iske baad bisalari ko mumbai main 1965 main kaanch kii botalon main do kismon main pesh kiya gaya - babli or still. parley ne 1969 main bisalari limited ko kharid liya or 'bisalari' brands naam ke antargat kaanch kii botalon main pani bechnaa shuru kiya. baad main parley ne pivisi gaiyr-vaapasi botalon kii taraf rukh kiya or antat piiti contenron ko apanaaya. 1995 main ramesh je chouhan ne bisalari ke sanchaalan kaa vistaar shuru kiya. 2003 main bisalari ne europe main apane udhyam kii ghoshnaa kii. uske baad ramesh je chouhan ne apana hissaa wakhaarikar and sans ko bech diya, lekin sabhi sanchaalan ramesh je chouhan ke adheen hi hai.
