 histor himand himalay parvat tantra ke karakoram shrenee main sthit 61 kimi lambaa himand he. vartmaan main yah pakistan adhikrut kashmir kaa hissaa he or ladakh ke balistaan kshetra main aata he. vastutah ye ilaaka ek sheet marusthal he. iske sahaayak ke rup main uttari dhaalon se utarane vaale ghati himand haramosh macif se aate hai or dakshini dhaalon se utarane vaale kanjut sar macif se.yah biafo himand se histor laa darre ke sameep 5,128 me kii oonchaai par miltaa he or donon sanyukt rup se vishwa kaa sabase bada himand tantra banaate hai. yah himand do pracheen parvatiy rajyon nagar or balistaan ko jodta he.histor himand ke agal-bagal parvatiy dhaalon kii tivrata or iske parshv morenon ke golashmon kii prakruti aisi he jo yahan trekking ko khaasaa mushkil banaati he. iske chaar sahaayak himandon ko paar karnaa sabase mushkil or khatarnaak kaam hota he.
 histor nadi jo hunja nadi kii sahaayak he isi himand ke pighalane se utpann jal se nikalati he.histor tak ladakh ke scardu se hokar pahunchaa ja saktaa he.
