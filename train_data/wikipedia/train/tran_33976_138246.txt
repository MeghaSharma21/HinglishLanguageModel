 panchasikh, sankhya darshan ke ek pradhan acharya the jinka varnan mahabharat ke shaantiparv main aayaa he. ye kapil kii shishyparampara main asuri ke shishy the. inkaa ulekh vamanpurana, kurmpurana or vayupurana tathaa tarpan vidhi main pratishthit acharya ke rup main hua he he. mahabharat main to inke vansh or jivan sambandhi kai ghatnaon kaa varnan miltaa he.
 sankhya sutron main inke mat kaa ullekh miltaa he. inako log 'dwitiya kapil' kehete hai.
 tarpan vidhi ke 'manushya tarpan' main panchasikh kaa bhi naam he-
 surendranath dasgupt apane 'a history of indian philosphy' main kehete hai ki mahabharat main panchasikh ne sankhya kaa jo vivaran diya he, usase charakasamhita kaa vivaran miltaa-julata he.
