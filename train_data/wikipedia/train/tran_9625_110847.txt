 ramakrishna mishan vivekananda vishvavidhyalay ramakrishna mishan dwaara sanchalit ek vishvavidhyalay he. bharat sarkaar ne ujisi adhinium 1956 kii dhara 3 ke antargat ise manit vishvavidhyalay ghoshit kiya he. iske chaar praangan hai jo belud, pashchim bengal, coimbatore, ranchi tathaa narendrapur main sthit hai.
 yah vishvavidhyalay vibhinn vishyon kii shiksha detaa he jinamein gramin evam janajati vikaas se lekar aapangata prabandhan tathaa vishesh shiksha, moolbhoot vigyaan, bhartiya samskruti tathaa aadhyatmik viraasat aadi sammilit hai.
