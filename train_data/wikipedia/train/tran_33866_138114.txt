 tripitak
 vinay pitak ek buddha granth he. yah un teen granthon main se ek he jo tripitak banaate he. is granth kaa pramukh vishay vihaar ke bhikshu, bhikshuni aadi he. vinay pitak kaa shabdic arth "anushaasan kii tokari" he. buddha dharm main bhikshu or bhikshuni ke rup me pravesh karne vaale shishy ke aacharan vyavasthit karne ke nimitt nirmit anushaasan ke niyamon ko vinay kehete he. atah vinay pitak vinay se sambandhit niyamon kaa vyavasthit sangrah he. 
 
 vinay pittak kaa 6 sanskaran purna rup se sangrakshit he. in main se 3 abhi bhi dharmik kaary ke liye prayojit he. 
 is ke saath saath hi vibhinn sanskaranon kaa vibhinn bhaag vibhinn bhashaaon main paaya jaataa he.
