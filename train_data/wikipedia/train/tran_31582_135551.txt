 anemi at da gates 2001 yuddh aadhaarit chalchitr he, jiskaa nirdesan jin-jaik anaud ne kiya he. jud lau, joseph finees or aid haris is film ke mukhya abhinayta hai. yah film dwitiya vishwa yuddh main hue 'stallingrad kii ladai' kii kehani par aadhaarit heis chalchitr kaa sheershak wiliam craig kii 1 9 73 ki akaalpanik pustak anemi at da gates: da battle of stallingrad se liya gaya he. yah pustak stallingrad kii ladai main 1 9 42 se 1 9 43 ke ghatnaon kaa varnan karti he. yah soviet nishanebaaz vasili jaitesev or unke german pratidwandi, major ervin konig ke bich bad rahe dwandvayuddh par adhrith he, jab ve donon yuddh ke dauraan ek duusare kaa peechha karte hai. is chalchitr ke kuch ansh ek anya pustak war of da rats se bhi liye gaye hai. mahadadwipiya europe main yah chalchitr stallingrad naam se jari kiya gaya tha or yah isi naam ke ek anya chalchitr se bilkul bhinn he.
