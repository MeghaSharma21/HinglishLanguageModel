 shiyaan ech-6 soviet sangh ke tupolev tu-16 do ingine vaale jet bombar kaa license nirmit sanskaran he. jo chini pipuls libration army air force ke liye banaaya gaya he.
 1958 main tu-16 kii delivery shuru hui. or shiaan airport industrial corporation ne 1950 ke dashak ke ant main china ne soviet sangh ke saath ek license utpaadan samjhaute par hastakshar kiye. pehla chini shiyaan ech-6 1959 main udaaya gaya tha. utpaadan shiyaan main sanyantr dwaara kiya gaya tha jahaan kam se kam 150 vimaan 1990 ke dashak main banaae gaye the. china kaa anumaan he ki vartmaan main 120 vimaanon ke aasapaas kaam kar rahe hai.
 iskaa naveenatam sanskaran ech-6ke he jo ek bahut badal hua sanskaran he jo air-launch cruge mixilon ko le jane main sakasham he. sanyukt rajya america ke raksha vibhaag ke mutabik, yah chini vaayu sena ko sateek maargdarshan vaali yupoton ke saath lambii duuri kii aakraamak vaayu kshamta degaa.
 pehla gharelu utpaadit shiyaan ech-6 ko 1968 main puraa kiya gaya tha or bambaari prashikshan ke sakshya america ke guptchar upgrahon dwaara ise 13 agast 1971 ko darj kiye gaye tha. agle varsh ke march tak, siie ne anumaan lagaaya tha ki chini vaayu sena main 32 oparation parichaalan kar rahe hai tathaa 19 abhi nirmaanadhin he. jinhe jald hi sena main shaamil kar liya jayega.
 shiyaan ech-6 kaa istemaal lop nur pareekshan sthal par nau parmaanu upkarnon ko chhodane ke liye kiya gaya tha. haalanki, belistic missile praudyogiki main vruddhi ke vikaas ke saath, shiyaan ech-6 kii peshkash kii gai parmaanu vitaran kshamtaayen mahatv main kam ai. siie ne 1976 main anumaan lagaaya tha ki shiyaan ech-6 ek dohari parmaanu/paaramparik bambaari kii bhoomika main aa gaya tha.
 china ne okinawa ke paas lambii duuri ke abhyaas karne ke liye ech-6 vimaan kaa istemaal kiya he, jisase japani vaayu self dephence force ko prerit kiya ja sake.
