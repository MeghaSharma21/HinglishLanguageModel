 yogeshwar dutt ek bhartiya kushti khilaadi hai. inhone 2012 greeshmakaleen olympix main kushti kii 60 kigra freestil spardhaa main kaansya padak jeeta tha. scotland ke glasgo main yojit kaamanwealth games 2014 main yogeshwar dutt ne 65 kilogram bhaar varg main freestil kushti main canadai pahalwaan ko haraakar bharat ke liye swarna padak jeeta. 2012 main bharat sarkaar dwaara inhen rajeev gandhi khel ratna kaa sammaan diya gaya.
