 scot laing maarval comics dwaara prakashit america comic pustakon main dikhaai dene vaala ek kaalpanik superhiro he. is charitra ko 1959 main david mishelin or jon bern ne hank pim ke baad duusare aint-main ke taur par banaaya tha. scot laing ke rup main yah sabase pehle da avangers #181 main, jabaki aint-main ke rup main yah pehali baar maarval premier #47 main dikhaai diya.
 scot laing ek apradhi or electronics visheshgy tha, jise stark international dwaara tab vruddh ho chuke hank pim se ant-main suut churaane kaa kaary mila. apni biimaar beti kii madad karne ke liye laing ne pim ke ghar main ghuskar uskaa suut chura liya. baad main uski pratibha dekhakar pim ne use hameshaa ke liye vah suut de diya jisase vah ant-main ban gaya. ant-main ke rup main laing avangers kaa sadsy rahaa, jab tak ki vah avangers dissembal kahani ke dauraan maaraa nahin gaya. saalon baad avangers: da children crossed mini shrinkhalaa main use phir punerjeevit kiya gaya.
 abhinayta paul red maarval cinemetic univers main scot laing kii bhoomika nibhaa rahe hai. laing ke rup main vah sarvapratham 2015 kii film ant-main main dikhe the. iske baad unhone captain america: civil war main yah bhoomika nibhaai, or vah hi aint-main and da wasp or avangers 4 main bhi apni bhoomika ko doharaaenge.
