 tritiy vishwa kii sankalpana sheet yuddh ke samay main aayi. un deshon ke samooh ko 'tritiy vishwa' kaha gaya jo na to nato ke saath the na hi soviet gut ke saath. sanyukt rajya america, pashchimi europe ke desh tathaa unke saathi deshon ko 'pratham vishwa' kehete the; soviet sangh, china, cuba tathaa unke sahyogiyon ko 'dwitiya vishwa' kehete the.
