 tata chikitsa kendra, bharat main kolkata sthit cancer ke shodh, nidaan or upachaar kaa ek atyaadhunik centre he.
 tata medical centre kaa udghaatan 16 may, 2011 ko ratan tata dwaara kiya gaya tha. yah visheshkar purvi evam purvottar bharat or bangladesh ke cancer rogiyon kii madad karne ke liye lakshith ek paropkaaree pahal he. is kendra kaa netritv iske nideshak dow memon chandi dwaara kiya jaataa he.
 tiemasi ek gunvatttapurna cancer-dekhbhaal facility he jo puri tarah se nahin lekin vishesh rup se kshetra ke garibon kii aavashyaktaaon ko puraa karata he.
 kolkata ke bahari ilaake rajarghat main sthit is santhaan tak kolkata shahar or shahar ke hawaii adde se aasaani se pahunchaa ja saktaa he. centre main jaldi hi premashray naamak ek aashray ghar hoga, jo marijon or unke rishtedaaron ke liye muft awaas evam bhojan pradaan karegaa.
