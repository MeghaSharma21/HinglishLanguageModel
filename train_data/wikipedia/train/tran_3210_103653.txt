 urja dakshata ek lakshya he, jo dainik jivan main or kaarkaane aadi main lagane vaale urja ko kam karne or puri tarah se sahi upyog karne kaa he. jisamen is prakaar se urja kaa upyog karnaa he ki jisase adhik urja vyarth na ho or taapmaana adhik na ho. usase vaataavaran kaa taapmaana bhi nahin badhegaa or urja kaa sahi upyog ho paaegaa.
