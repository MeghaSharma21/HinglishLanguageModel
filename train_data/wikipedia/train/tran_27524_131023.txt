 meghalaya sarkaar bhartiya rajya meghalaya evam uske sabhi 11 jilon kii sarvocch adhishasi praadhikari he. isaki pramukh shakhao main rajyapal ke netritv main karypalika, nyaaypalika evam vidhayika aate hai. 
 rajya kii vidhaan sabha main vartmaan main 60 sadsy hote hai. meghalaya rajya ke do pratinidhi lok sabha hetu nirvaachit hote hai, prayek ek shilong or ek tura nirvachan kshetra se. yahaan kaa ek pratinidhi rajya sabha main bhi jaataa he. iskaa kaarykaal 5 varsh kaa hota he.rajya ke srijan se hi yahaan guahati ucch nyaayalay kaa adhikaar kshetra rahaa he. 1974 se hi guahati ucch nyaayalay kii ek circuit bench yahaan sthapit he. march 2013 main meghalaya ucch nyaayalay ko guahati ucch nyaayalay se vilag kar diya gaya or ab rajya kaa apana ucch nyaayalay he.
 raashtra kii gramin janata ko sthaneey swa-sarkaar uplabdh karaane hetu bhartiya samvidhan main praayojan kiye gayi hai. inke anusaar panchayat raaj santhaan kii sthapana kii gayi he. purvottar rajyon ke bhinn reeti rivaajon evam manyataon ko dhyaan main rakhte hue, kshetra ke liye ek alag raajnneetik evam prashasnic dhaanche kaa nirmaan kiya gaya he kshetra kii kuch janajatiyon kii apni parmparik raajnneetik pranaliyaan hai. inke chalate yah mahasoos kiya gaya ki panchayat raaj pranali yahaan lagu kii jane se vivaad utpann karegi. gopinath bordoloi kii adhyakshata main bani ek upasmiti kii sifaarishon ko samvidhan main sanlagn kiya gaya. iske tahat meghalaya sahit purvottar ke kai gramin kshetron main swytt jila parishadon kaa gathan kar unka apana samvidhan lagu kiya gaya. meghalaya main aisi edisi parishaden nimna hai:
