 jalbhar, jalbhrit athwa jalabhra dharaatal kii sataha ke neeche chattaanon kaa ek aisa sanstar he jahaan bhoojal ekatrit hota he or manushya dwaara nalkupon se nikaalane yogya anukool dashaon main hota he. vaise to jal str ke neeche kii sari chattaanon main pani unke randhrakash main avashya upasthit hota he lekin yah jaruri nahin ki use maanav upyog ke liye nikaala bhi ja sake. jalbhre aisi chattaanon ke sanstar hai jinamein randhrakash bade hote hai jisase pani kii zyada maatra ikaitha ho sakati he tathaa saath hi inamen pargamyata zyada hoti he jisase pani kaa sancharan ek jagah se duusari jagah ko teji se hota he. 
 samanytay jalbhar ke liye ek or dasha kaa hona aavashyak he or vah he is ucch pargamya sanstar ke thik neeche ek apargamya jalrodhi shail sanstar kii upasthiti. atah jalbhar zyaadaatar aisi balua pathar chattaanon main paae jate hai jinke neeche shell yaa siltstone kii parat paayi jaati ho. 
 jalbhar ko is apargamya jalrodhi parat kii upasthiti ke aadhaar par prakaaron main baanta jaataa he - mukt jalbhar or sanrodhit jalbhar . sanrodhit jalbhar ve hai jinamein upar or neeche donon taraf jalrodhi sanstar paaya jaataa he or inke richarge kshetra duusare oonchaai vaale bhaagon main hote hai. inhi sanrodhit jalparon main utsrut coop bhi paae jate hai.
 jalbhar kii anya kai bhautik visheshtaayen hoti hai.jalbhar ke vargeekaran kaa ek or aadhaar he hidrolic conductivity kii disha. iske aadhaar par inhen sampraay or asampraay jalbhar main vibhajit kiya jaataa he.
 jalparon main jal kaa dhimaa kintu nirantar pravaah hota rahata he jise adhopravaah kehete he.
