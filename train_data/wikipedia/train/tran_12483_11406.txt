 reeta faria powell miss world kaa khitaab jeetne vaali pehali bhartiya or asian mul kii mahila hai. ve pehali bhartiya miss world hai, jinhone chikitsa shaastra main visheshgyata hasil kii.
 mumbai main 1945 main janmi reeta pehali bhartiya or asian mul kii mahila hai jinhone 1966 main miss world kaa tittle jeeta. ek saal modelling kii oonchaaiyon ko chune ke baad reeta ne doctorl kii padhai puri kii or is kshetra main apana karier banaaya. unhone mumbai sthit grant medical college or sar jamshedji jijabai group of hospital se em bi bi es main snaatak kiya. uske baad ve kings college evam aspataal, landan main ucch adhyayan hetu chali gai. 1971 main unhone david powell se shaadi kii or 1973 main donon dublin main rahane lage, jahaan unhone dokatary kii prectis karnaa shuru kiya. 
 unhone dobaaraa fishan kii duniya main 1998 main femina miss india kii jaj ke taur par kambek kiya. ve miss world pratiyogita main bhi bataur jaj shaamil ho chuki hai.
 unki shaadi 1971 main hui. vartmaan main ve dublin, ireland main apane endocrinologist pati david powelke saath rahati he. unke do bete or paanch pote-potiyaan hai.
