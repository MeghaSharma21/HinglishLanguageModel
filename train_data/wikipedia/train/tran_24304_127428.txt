 taqadir : yah shabd arabi he, iskaa mul shabd "qadra" he, arthath "bhagya". islam ke chh vishwaas sutron main se ek he "val qadri qaairihi", matlab allaah se pradaan kiye gayi bhagya par bhi vishwaas rakhana. isi ko taqadir kehete hai. sthool rup se "allaah se prasadit bhagya par vishwaas rakhana" or uskaa shukr baja laana. isi silsile ko aage badhaate huve yah bhi kaha gaya ki "mar kar uthne par bhi vishwaas karnaa".
