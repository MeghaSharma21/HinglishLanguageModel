 rashtriya i-shashan yojana ) bharat sarkaar dwaara armbh kii gayi ek yojana he jiskaa uddeshy bharat ke nagarikon ko sabhi sarkari sevaayen electranik madhyamon se uplabdh karana he. yah yojana bharat sarkaar ke electronic vibhaag, soochna praudyogiki vibhaag tathaa prashasnic sudhaar evam lok shikaayat dwaara vibhaag dwaara banaayi gayi thi.
