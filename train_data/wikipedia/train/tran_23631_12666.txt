 purva phalguni nakshatra 11vaan nakshatra he. yah surya kii singh kii raashi main aata he. iske chaaron charan singh main mo tii t naam akshar se charnanusaar aate hai. nakshatra swami shuk se isaki mitrataa nahin he. surya agnitatv pradhan he to shukr kala, saundarya, dhan kaa kaarak he. is nakshatra kii dasha chandra kii sthiti anusaar hoti he. yah sarvaadhik dasha 20 varsh vaala nakshatra he.
 yah 13 ansh 20 kala se shuru hokar 26 ansh 20 kala par samaapt hota he. singh raashi par nakshatra swami kaa hona us jataka ko sundar lavanyamay banayega. yadi aisa jataka kala ke kshetra main jaae to safalataa avashya milti he. aise jataka uttam kad, sammohak, madhur muskaan ke dhani hote hai. inke jivan par surya, shukr va jo bhi lagn main janma ho va shukr jis raashi par ho uske swami kaa bhi prabhaav padegaa.
 mesh, surya lagn ho va shukr saptam bhav main ho to aisa jataka sundar aakarshak chehre vaala, hothon va jaanghon par til hoga. is lagn main shukr kii sthiti dwitiya, dwadash, dasham, ekadash, chaturth bhav main thik rahegi. shukr mangal saath hua to aisa jataka kaamuk pravriti kaa ho saktaa he. shukr rahu ke saath ho to bigade swabhaav kaa vyasnee, vrishabh lagn ho to shukr kii sthiti lagn main, tritiy bhav main dasham, navam bhav main uttam faldaayi rahegi.
