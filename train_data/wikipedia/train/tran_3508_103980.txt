 paarad ek pracheen jati jo paras ke us pradesh main nivas karti thi jo cospian sagar ke dakshin ke pahaado ko paar karke padataa tha. iske haath main bahut dinon tak paras samrajya rahaa.
 mahabharat, manusmriti, brihatsamhita ityadi main paarad desh or paarad jati kaa ullekh miltaa he. yatha— 
 isi prakaar brihatsamhita main pashchim disha main basanevaali jatiyon main 'paarat' or unke desh kaa ullekh he— 
 puraane shilalekhon main 'parthav' rup miltaa hai jisase unani 'parthia' shabd bana he. europee vidwaanon ne 'pahlav' shabd ko isi 'parthiv' kaa apbhransh yaa roopantak maanakar pahlav or paarad ko ek hi tehraaya he. par sanskrit sahitya main ye donon jatiyaan bhinn likhi gai hai. manusmriti ke samaan mahabharat or brihatsamhita main bhi 'pahlav' 'paarad' se alag aayaa he. atah 'paarad 'kaa'pahlav' se koi sambandh nahin prathit hota. paras main pahlav shabd shashanvankhi samraaton ke samay se hi bhashaa or lipi ke arth main miltaa he. isse siddh hota he ki iskaa prayog adhik vyapak arth main parsiyon ko liye bhartiya granthon main hua he. kisi samay main paras ke sardaar 'pahalbaan' kehalaate the. sambhav he, isi shabd se 'pahlav' shabd bana ho. manusmriti main 'pardon' or 'pahlvon' aadi ko aadim kshatriya kaha he jo brahmanon ke adarshan se sanskarabhrasht hokar shudratv ko praapt ho gaye.
