 deepak puri moger bear ke sansthaapak, adhyaksh or prabandh nideshak hai. moger bear duniya kaa doosraa sabase bada optical storage media nirmaataa company hai.
 puri ne shuru main tel company iesesao ke saath junior kaaryakari ke rup main kaam kiya tha - 1962 main kolkata main, or baad main shalimaar paints ke saath. 1964 main, puri ne apni pehali company, kalakatta main dhaatu udyog, aluminium ke taron or furniture ke kaarobaar shuru kiya tha. do saal baad, unhone kaarobaar ko vinirmaan ke rup kaa rup de diya. kalakatta main shram muddon ke kaaran, unhone 1983 main nai dilli main sthanantarit karne kaa nirnay liya. jahaan unhone switzarland sthit moger bear ke saath moger bear india ke sanyukt upkram ko shuru kiya tha.
 puri ne varisht congress netaa or purv kendriya mantri kamalnath kii bahan nita puri se shaadi kii he or unka ek betaa ratul he. nita or ratul donon hi moger bear kaa hissaa hai.
