 mariyam uz-jamaani begum sahiba ek rajkumaari thi jo mogul badshaah jalaal uddin mohammad akbar se shaadi ke baad mallika-i-hindustan baniin. ve jaipur kii rajput amer riyasat ke raja bhaarmal kii sab se badi putree thi jise betaa samaan maanate the. unke garbh se sultanate ke valiahad or agle badshaah nuruddin jahangir paidaa hue.
 rajasthan ke antim swatantra rathod raja maldev dwaara apni mrityu se pehle, apane chote putr raav chandrasen ko marwar kii rajgaddi dene se uske putron main jhagada shuru ho gaya. rajgaddi kaa vaastavik uttaradhikari va maldev kaa jyeshth putr udaisih tha. pita dwajehara kiye gayi nirnay ke kaaran use mughal samraat akbar se yuddh kiya jisame usne akbar ko buree tarah se haraaya. mamata bano mogul badshaah jahangir kii maan thi.
 daasi hira kunvaari kaa vivaah akbar ke saath 6 farvari 1562 ko sambar, hindustan main hua. vah akbar kii teesri patni or uske teen pramukh malikaaon main se ek thi. akbar ke pehali malikaa ruqaaiyya begum niqusantan thi or uski duusari patni salima sultan uske sabase bharosemand sipahasaalar bairam khan kii vidhwaa thi. shaadi ke baad hira kunwari ko mariyam uz-jamaani begham sahiba kaa kitaab diya gaya.
