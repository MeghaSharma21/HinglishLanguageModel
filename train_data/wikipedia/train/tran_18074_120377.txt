 kama rus main bahane vaali ek pramukh nadi he. yah volga nadi kii baayein taraf kii sabase lambi sahaayak nadi he. vastav main milan bindu tak yah volga se bhi badi he.
 yah ural parvat ke pashchimi pradesh main molotove nagar ke pashchim se nikalati he. krmanusaar uttar, purv tathaa dakshin kii ore mudakar molotove pahunchati he. phir, 1,200 mile dakshin-pashchim bahakar kaazan shahar ke nikat volga main girti he. yahi sangam pracheen tartar rajya kaa centre tha. nahar dwaara kama kaa sambandh uttari dvina se ho jane ke kaaran ural pradesh se baltic sagar tak yatayaat kaa ek mahatvapurn maarg khul gaya he. garmiyon main molotove tak bade jalayaan aa sakate hai. molotove ke nikat kama ke jal se vidyut utpaadan bhi hota he.
