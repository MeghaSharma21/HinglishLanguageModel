 barishaal bangladesh kaa ek pramukh nagar he. yah nagar bangladesh ke dakshin-madhya bhaag main kirtankhola nadi ke tat par sthit he. yah barishaal jile tathaa barishaal prabhag kaa sabase bada nagar tathaa vahaan kaa mukhyalay he. yah avibhajit bharat kaa sabase purana nagarpalika evam nadi pattan he. barishaal nagarpalika 1876 main bani thi.
