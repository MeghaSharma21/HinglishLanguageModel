 nat uttar bharat main hindu dharm ko maanane vaali ek jati he jiske purush log praaia: baazigari yaa kalabazi or gane-bajaane kaa kaary karte hai tathaa unki striyaan naachne va gane kaa kaary karti hai. is jati ko bharat sarkaar ne samvidhan main anusuchit jati ke antargat shaamil kar liya he taaki samaaj ke andar unhen shiksha aadi ke vishesh adhikaar dekar aage badhaaya ja sake.
 nat shabd kaa ek arth nrity yaa naatak karnaa bhi he. sambhavat: is jati ke logon kii isi visheshta ke kaaran unhen samaaj main yah naam diya gaya hoga. kahin kahin inhen baazigar yaa kalabaaz bhi kehete hai. sharir ke ang-pratyang ko lacheela banaakar bhinn mudraon main pradarshit karte hue janata kaa manoranjan karnaa hi inkaa mukhya pesha he. inaki striyaan khubasurat hone ke saath saath haav-bhav pradarshan karke nrity va gayan main kaaphi praveen hoti hai.
 naton main pramukh rup se do upjatiyaan hai-bajaniya nat or brajvasi nat. bajaniya nat praaia: baazigari yaa kalabazi or gane-bajaane kaa kaary karte hai jabaki brajvasi naton main striyaan nartki ke rup main naachne-gane kaa kaary karti hai or unke purush yaa pati unke saath sajinde kaa kaary karte hai.
