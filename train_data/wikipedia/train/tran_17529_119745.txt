 khatwang shiv ke haath kaa ek aayudh. isamen dand ke upar pashu ke khur ke bich maanav kapaal laga hota he. yogi or sanyasi bhi is aayudh kaa upyog karte hai. lokajeevan main ise jaadu kii lakdi kaha jaataa he.
  suryavanshi raja vishwasah kaa putr. isane devdaanav yuddh ke samay swarg jaakar devataaon kii badi sahaayta kii thi. vaayu puraana main is raja kii badi mahima gaai gai he.
