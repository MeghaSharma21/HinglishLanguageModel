 sampathvaduge rohita silva ek purv srilankai cricket team ke khilaadi he, jinhone 1983 se 1988 tak 9 test or 20 vanade main khele the. ye ek baayen haath ke wicketiper or ballebaaj the or srilanka ke liye ballebaaji kii shuruyaat bhi kiya karte the.
 amaal silva ne prince of wales college, moratuva or saint peter college, colombo main apni shiksha purna kii thi.
 amaal silva ne apane antarrashtriya cricket karier kii shuruyaat 11 march 1983 ko ek test match khelkar kii thi or wo test match newzealand team ke khilaaf khelaa gaya tha.
 amaal silva ne apane cricket karier kii shuruyaat test match se kii thi. inhone apana pehla test cricket match 11 march 1983 ko newzealand team ke khilaaf kii thi. inhone apane test karier main maatr 9 match hi khele the jisamen inhone 25.21 kii ballebaaji ausat se 353 ran banaae the jisamen inke do shatak bhi shaamil he. inkaa test main sarvaadhik score 111 ran rahaa he. ye wicket-keeper bhi rah chuke hanto inhone wicket ke peeche or kshetrarakshan main 33 kaich or wicket ke peeche 1 stump bhi kiya tha.
 amaal silva ne apane ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket karier kii shuruyaat 03 navambar 1984 ko newzealand ke khilaaf kii thi or antim match 25 oktober 1985 ko pakistan ke khilaaf khelaa tha.
 inhone apane puure ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket karier main kul 20 match khele the jisamen 22.05 kii ballebaaji ausat se 441 ran banaae the. iske alaava inhone vanade cricket main shatak to nahin laga paye lekin 3 ardhashatak jarur lagaae the. silva ne wicket keeper ke taur par 17 kaich kiye the jabaki 3 stump out bhi karne kaa naam he.
 is prakaar amaal silva ne apane cricket jivan main kul 29 antarrashtriya test+vanade match khele he jisamen inhone 794 ran banaaye. jabaki wicketcaping main inke 50 kaich or 4 stump out bhi rahe he.
