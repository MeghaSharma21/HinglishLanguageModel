 maava yaa khoyaa doodh se bana ek thos padaarth he jisase mithaiyaan evam anya pakwaan banate hai. yah bhartiya mithaaiyon main atyadhik prayog kiya jane vaala padaarth he. yah doodh ko garam karke uske pani ko jalaakar banaaya jaataa he.
 ab ise utaar kar thanda kar len. thanda ho jane par yah daanedaar ho jaataa he tab aap isko istemaal main laa sakate hai.
