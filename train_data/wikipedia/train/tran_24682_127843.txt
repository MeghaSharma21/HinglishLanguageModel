 ladi shriram college  dilli vishvavidhyalay main mahilaon kaa mahavidhyalay he. yah bharat main udaar kala-shiksha ke liye pramukh sansthaanon main se ek mana jaataa he.
 isaki sthapana 1956 main sar shri ram ne apni patni phulan devi kii smriti main kii thi. isaki shuruyaat dariyaganj, nai dilli main ek college, 243 chaatro, 9 sikshakon, 4 sahyogi staff or adhyayan ke 3 alag paathyakram ke saath hui thi. sanstha kaa uddeshy mahilaon ko ucch shiksha pradaan karnaa tha.
 aaj, yah college dakshin dilli ke lajpat nagar zila main ek 15-acre parisar main sthit he or lagbhag 2000 vidhyaarthee, 150 se adhik shikshakon, prashasnic or staff or adhyayan ke 16 paathyakramon kaa samarthan rakhata he.
