 devonal fredrarik watmor ek purv anterrashtriya austrailian cricket khilaadi he or vartmaan samay main bangladesh cricket team ke coach he. apane chote anterrashtriya kariyar main unhone march 1979 se janvari 1980 tak 7 test match or 1 ekadivasiy match khelaa. first class cricket main unhone victoria ke liye 6,000 se zyada ran banaae.
 anterrashtriya cricket se 1988/89 main sanyaas lene ke baad ve cricket coach ban gaye. unhone srilanka kii cricket team ko do alag samay main coach kiya, jisame pehle samay main srilanka kii team ne 1996 cricket vishwa cap jeeta. un do antaraal ke madhya main unhone lankashaire kii team ko coach kiya. jahaan unki team ne 1998 or 1999 main naishanal lig or 1998 kii natwest trophy jiti.
 2003 se ve bangladesh cricket team ke coach he.
