 navgathit rajya jharkhand main jharkhand rajya kii swasthya, chikitsa, shiksha evam parivaar kalyaan socity ke tahat rajya aids niyantran sosayadi 9 may 2001 ko panjikrut kii gayi thi kiya gaya tha. aavashyaktaa ke anuroop takneekee or prabandhkeey karmiyon kaarykram ke adhik prabhavi prabandhan ke liye kai niyuktiyaan kii gayi hai or kii ja rahi hai. sachiv, swasthya evam parivaar kalyaan vibhaag, jharkhand sarkaar ke netritv main ek prashasnic samooh rajya aids niyantran socity suchalu rup se chalati he.
 jharkhand rajya aids niyantran socity ek sakriy sanghathan ke rup main kaam karte hue echivi/ aids ke rogiyon ke liye vibhinn sarkari evam swayamsevi sanghathanon ke sahyog se swarozgaar yojana lagu kii jisase echivi / aids ke rogi aatm-nirbhar ho saken.
 jharkhand rajya aids niyantran socity na keval echivi / aids balki inase jude nashile padaarthon ke seven kii dushparamper ke viruddh bhi agrasar he. aise hi ek prayaas main june 2012 main socity dwaara baridih sthit drops-in centre main jaagruk kaarykram chalaaya gaya. isamen logon ko nashe ke dushprabhav ke prati jaagruk kiya gaya. is dauraan chitrankan pratiyogita, naatak, jaadu, atyadi kaa aayojan kiya gaya.
