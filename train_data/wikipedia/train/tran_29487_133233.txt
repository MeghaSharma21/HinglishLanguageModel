 emi louis jackson ek angrez model va film abhinetri he. unhone 2008 main miss tin world va 2009 main miss liverpool jeeta. 2010 main ve miss england kii daud main thi parantu yah baazi jesica linley ne antim pheri main maar lee. unhone apane abhinay kariyar kii shuruyaat 2010 kii safal tamil film madrasapattinam se kii jiske liye unhen kai sammaanon se nawaaza gaya. unhone apane boliwood kariyar kii shuruyaat ek divaana tha se kii jise 17 farvari 2012 ko release kiya gaya.
 isle of main main janmi jackson alan or margureta jackson kii beti va alisia kii choti bahan he. unka parivaar woolton, liverpool main do saal baad waapas lautaa taaki unke pita apana media kaa kariyar punah sambhaal sake. jackson ne saint edwards college se apni padhai kii va aage chalakar siksth form se angrezi bhashaa va angrezi sahitya kii shiksha grahan kii. 
 miss tin liverpool va miss tin great britain kaa sondarya puraskaar jitne ke baad unhone miss tin world kaa puraskaar 2008 main jeeta. unhone bos model management ke saath saude par hastakshar kiye va puure europe main modeling kii.
 2009 main 17 varsh kii aayu main bhartiya film nirmaataaon ne unhen model esansi kii websit par dekha va audishan ke liye aamantrit kiya jiske parinaam swaroop unhen tamil film madrasapattinam main abhinayta arya ke saath mukhya bhoomika mili. film ko release ke baad badi safalataa hasil hui or samikshakon ne jackson ko unke abhinay ke liye kaaphi saraahaa. unhone baad main gautam menon kii film ek divaana tha main tak malayaali ise ladli kii bhoomika saakaari jo inkaa boliwood main pehla kadam tha.
