 mount abu vanyajeev abhayaarany mount abu kaa prasiddh paryatak sthal he. yahan mukhya rup se tendue, slothbear, wild bor, sambhars, chinkaara or langur paae jate hai. 288 varg kilometre main faile is abhayaarany kii sthapana 1960 main kii gai thi. yahan pakshiyon kii lagbhag 250 or paudhon kii 110 se zyada prajatiyaan dekhi ja sakati hai. pakshiyon main ruchi rakhane waalon ke liye upyukt jagah he.
