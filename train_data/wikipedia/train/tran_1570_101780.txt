 sirilik lipi purvi europe or madhya asia ke kshetra kii kai bhaashaon ko likhane main prayukt hoti he. ise azbuka bhi kehete hai, jo is lipi kii varnmaala ke shuruaati do aksharon ke puraane naamon ko milakar banaaya gaya he, jaise ki unani varnmaala ke do shuruaati aksharon - alfaa or beta - ko milakar alfabet yani varnmaala banataa he. is lipi ke varnon se jin bhaashaon ko likhaa jaataa he usamen roosi bhashaa pramukh he. soviet sangh ke purv sadsy tajikistan main farsi bhashaa kaa sthaneey rup bhi isi lipi main likhaa jaataa he. iske alaava bulgarian, serbian, kazakh, mysidonian, uzbek, ukraini tathaa mongolian bhashaa bhi mukhyatah isi lipi main likhi jaati he. is varnmaala ko europea sangh main aadhikarik manyata praapt he jahaan keval roman tathaa unani lipi hi anya aadhikarik lipiyaan hai. 
 hindi se milti-julati tajakistan or uzbekistan ke kuch bhaagon main boli jane vaali paraya bhashaa bhi sirilik lipi main likhi jaati he.
 san 1863 i. main do bhai kirill or mefodi ne puraani slav varnmaala kaa nirmaan kiya. yah puraani greek varnmaala ke aadhaar par banaai gai thi. puraani slav varnmaala main 43 varna the. sirilik main greek varnmaala ke sab varna sammilit hai. iske sivaa isamen yahudee, brahmi kii varnmaala ke bhi kuch varna hai.

 sirilik aksharon ke do rup hote hai - sidhe or itelic. yah donon or sambandhit dhvaniyaan neeche di gai hai. dhyaan rahe ki kuch bhashaaon main in ke alawa kuch anya akshar bhi sirilik main jode jate hai.
