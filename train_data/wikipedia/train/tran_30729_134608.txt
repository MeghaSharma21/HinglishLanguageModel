 sura al-fajr quran kaa 89waan sura he. isamen 30 aayatein hai. 
 yah sura vishwaas naa rakhane vaale logon ke vinaash ke bare main varnan karata he, jaise pracheen misr ke log or stambhon vaale eram ke log. jo log dhan-sampatti ko chaahate hai, evam garib evam anathon kii avhelnaa karte hai, yah unka bahishkaar karata he. antim aayat kahati he &mdash sachche tathaa imaan vaale logon ko jananath kaa vishwas dilaayaa jaataa he. ishwar isamen swayan kehete hai "meri jananath main aao".
