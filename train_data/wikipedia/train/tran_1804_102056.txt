  bharat praveshdwar
 bharat kii raajnneeti apane samvidhan ke dhaanche main kaam karti hai, kyonki bharat ek sangheey sansadeey, loktantric ganatantr hai, jahaan par raashtrapati desh kaa pramukh hota hai or pradhanmantri sarkaar kaa pramukh hota hai. bharat ek dwi-rajtantr kaa anusaran karata hai, arthath, kendra main ek kendriya satta vaali sarkaar or paridhi main rajya sarkarein. 
 samvidhan main vidhaan mandal ke dwisadaniyata kaa praavdhaan hai, jis main ek upree sadan jo bhartiya sangh ke rajya tathaa kendra-shasit pradesh kaa pratinidhitiv karata hai, or nichalaa sadan jo bhartiya janata kaa pratinidhitiv karata hai, sammilit hai.
 shashan evam satta sarkaar ke haath main hoti he. sanyukt vaidhanik bagdor sarkaar evam sansad ke dono sadanon, lok sabha evam rajya sabha ke haath main hoti he. nyaay mandal shaskeey evam vaidhanik, dono se swatantra hota he.
 samvidhan ke anusaar, bharat ek pradhan, samaajawaadee, dharm-nirpeksh, loktantric rajya he, jahaan par sarkaar janata ke dwaara chuni jaati he. america kii tarah, bharat main bhi sanyukt sarkaar hoti he, lekin bharat main kendra sarkaar rajya sarkaron kii tulana main adhik shaktishaalee he, jo ki britain kii sansadeey pranali par aadhaarit he. bahumat kii sthiti main na hone par sarkaar na bana paane kii dasha main athwa vishesh sanvaidhanik paristhiti ke antargat, kendra sarkaar rajya sarkaar ko nishkasit kar sakati he or sidhe sanyukt shashan lagu kar sakati he, jise raashtrapati shashan kaha jaataa he.
