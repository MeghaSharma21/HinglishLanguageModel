 ahirbudhnya samhita, pancharaatr sahitya kaa ek atyant mahatvapurn granth he. vishnubhakti kaa jo darshanik athwa vaicharik paksh he, usee kaa ek pracheen naam pancharaatr bhi he. paramtatv, mukti, bhukti, yoga tathaa vishay kaa vivechan hone ke kaaran is sahitya kaa yah namakaran kiya gaya he. naarad pancharaatr or is samhita main ukt namakaran kaa yahi arth batalaaya gaya he. pancharaatr sahitya kaa rachanakaal saamanyatya isapurs chaturth shatee se isottar chaturth shatee ke bich mana jaataa he. pancharaatr samhitaon kii sankhya lagbhag 215 batalaai jaati he, jinamein abtak lagbhag 16 samhitaon kaa prakaashan hua he. ahirbughnya samhita kaa prakaashan 1916 i. ke dauraan teen khandon main hua tha. isamen aath adhyay hai, jinamein gyaan, yoga, kriya, charya tathaa vaishnavon ke saamaanya achaarpaksh ke pramanik vivechan ke saath-saath vaishnav darshan ke aadhyatmik pramayon kii bhi pramanik vyakhya di gai he. anya anek samhitaon se isaki visheshta yah he ki isamen tantric granthon kii tarah hi tantric yoga kaa bhi sangopang vivechan kiya gaya he, yadhyapi bhakthi kii mahima yahan kam nahin he. isamen bhedabhedwaad kaa bhi paryaapt vyaakhyaan he. isi aadhaar par kuch vidvan ramanuj darshan kii bhoomika ke liye pancharaatr darshan ko mahatvapurn maanate hai.
