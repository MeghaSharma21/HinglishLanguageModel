 theva 17veen shatabdi main mogul kaal main rajasthan ke rajgharaanon ke sangrakshan main panpi ek bejod hastkala he. theva kii hastkala kala striyon ke liye sone minaakaari or paardarshee kaanch ke mail se nirmit aabhushan ke nirmaan se sambaddh he. iske ine gine shilpi-parivaar rajasthan ke keval pratapgarh jile main hi rahete hai. theva-aabhushanon ke nirmaan main vibhinn rangon ke shishon ko chaandi ke maheen taron se bane frem main daal kar us par sone kii baarik kalakritiyaan ukeri jaati he, jinhen kushal or daksh haath chote-chote aujaaron kii madad se banaate hai. yah aabhushan-nirmaan kala apni moulikta or kalaatmakataa ke kaaran vishwa kii un ini gini hastkalaon main se ek he - jo antarrashtriya bazaar or vipanan ke abhaav main jaldi vilupt ho sakati hai|
 "theva" naam kii utpatti is kala ke nirmaan kii do mukhya prakriyaaon 'tharana' or 'waada' se mil kar hui he. 
 is kala main pehle kaanch par sone kii bahut patali work yaa shit lagaakar us par baarik jaali banaai jaati he, jise tharana kaha jaataa he. duusare charan main kaanch ko kasne ke liye chaandi ke baarik taar se frem banaaya jaataa he, jise "wada" kaha jaataa he. tatpashchaat ise tej aag main tapaaya jaataa he. falswaroop shishe par sone kii kalakriti or khubasurat dizin ubhar kar ek nayab or laajawab kriti kaa aabhushan ban jaati he.
 kaanch par kanchan kaa karishma kahe jane vaali yah shilp kaanch par sunahari painting kii tarah he, jo chitrakaari kii neenv par janm leti he. is shilp kii rachana jatil, kathin tathaa shramsadhy to he hi, parantu sone par chitrankit shilp ko kaanch main samahit kar dene kaa kamaal in mutthi bhar kaarigaron ke hi bas kii baat he. shilp chithran ko 23 karet sone ke pattar par ‘taankal’ naamak ek vishesh kalam se ukera jaataa he or kushaltapurvak bahurangi kaanch par madh diya jaataa he. jab donon padaarth sona or kaanch paraspar jud kar ekmec ek jeev ho jate hai to sone main kaanch or kaanch main sona dikhalaai padataa he. isi ko theva kala kehete hai. kaanch kii jhagamag ko or adhik prabhaavshaalee tathaa umdaa banaaye rakhane ke liye ise ek khaas prakriya se guzarana padataa he jisase ki kaanch main samahit sone kaa kaary ubhaar dene lage or dekhne vaale ke man ko chhu le. is prakaar shilp kaa prayek namoona paardarshee kaanch kaa ho jaataa he or us par maanik, panna tathaa neelam jaisi prabhaa dikhalaai padati he. is kala ko ukerne main belgium se vishesh prakaar ke kaanch istemaal hote the, par aise kaanchon kii anuplabdhta se ab alag umdaa kaanch kaa istemaal hota hai.
 prarambh main theva kaa kaam laal, nile or hare rangon ke moolyawaan pattharon hira, panna aadi par hi hota tha, lekin ab yah kaam pile, gulabi or kaale rang ke kaanch ke ratnon par bhi hone laga he. pehle is kala se banaae jane vaale baks, plates, dish aadi par dharmik anukritiyaan or lokapriy lokkathaayen ukeri jaati thim, lekin ab yah kala aabhushan ke saath-saath pendents, year-ring, taai or sadiyon kii pin, kafalinks, photophreme aadi main bhi prachlit ho chali he. is kala ko kai nijee upakramon dwaara aadhunik fishan kii vividh diginon main dhalkar lokapriy banane ke prayaas kiye ja rahe hai.
 is kala ko jaanane vaale desh main ab gine chune parivaar hi bache hai. ye parivaar rajasthan ke navgathit pratapgarh jile main rahane vaale rajasoni gharaane ke hai, jinhen is anuthi kala ke liye kai antarrashtriya, rashtriya or rajyastareey puraskaar mil chuke hai. encikalopedia britanika ke "pi" upkhand main pratapgarh kii is vishisht aabhushan parampara kaa ullekh kiya gaya he. rajasthan kii is bejod aabhushan parampara ke sarkari sangrakshan ke prayaas aaj bhi aparyaapt hai |
 haalanki bharat sarkaar dwaara, theva kala kii pratinidhi-sanstha rajasthan theva kala santhaan pratapgarh ko is anuthi kala ke sangrakshan main vishesheekaran ke liye vastuon kaa bhugolic upadarshan adhinium, 1999 ke tahat jeographical indication sankhya kaa pramaan-patr pradaan kiya gaya he. ullekhneey he ki jeographical indication kisi utpaada ko uski sthaan vishesh main utpatti evam prachlan ke saath vishesh bhugolic gunvattta evam pahchaan ke liye diya jaataa he.
 theva kala ke liye raashtrapati puraskaar se sammanit or theva kala santhaan ke ek hastshilpi sadsy mahesh raaj soni ko vishwaas he ki ab bhugolic upadarshan sankhya milane ke baad yah kala apni bhugolic pahchaan ko acchhee tarah se kaayam rakhane main kaamyaab ho paayegi.
 is shilp kala par navambar 2002 main paanch rupee kaa dak tikit bhi jari hua he.
