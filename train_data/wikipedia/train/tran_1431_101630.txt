 ienes sahyadri bhartiya nausena kaa ek swadesh nirmit gaided missile stealth frigate pot he.yah shivalik shrenee ke fregates main se ek he.
 
 ienes sahyadri kai atyaadhunik hathiyaaron se las he. inamen lambii duuri kii anti ship misile, madhyam or kam duuri kii sataha se hawa main maar karne vaali misile or kai prakaar kii topein hai jo ise kisi bhi hamale se nipatane main sakasham banaati hai. isi ke saath ienes sahyadri main tainaat kai bhoomika nibhaane vaale do helicopter bhi isaki maarak kshamta main badhottaree karte hai. 21 july 2012 ko nausena main shaamil hue ienes sahyadri kaa netritv vartmaan main captain jyotin raina kar rahe hai or isamen vibhinn shakhaon main 25 adhikari or 255 nausainik karyrat hai.
