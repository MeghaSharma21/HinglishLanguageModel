 rane dacart ek francisi ganitagya, bhautikivigyaani, sharirkriyavigyaani tathaa darshanik the.
 unka janm 31 march 1596 i. ko hegg main hua tha. 21 varsh kii aayu main shiksha samaapt kar ye orange ke rajkumaar moris kii sena main bhartee ho gaye. yahan par praapt avkaash ko ye ganit ke adhyayan main vyateet kiya karte hai. inhone kai yudhon main bhi bhaag liya. sevoy ke duk ke saath hue yuddh main pradarshit veerta ke kaaran inkaa leftinent general kii oopaadhi pradaan kii gai, parantu inhone use sweekaar nahin kiya or paris main teen varsh tak shantipurvak darshanashastra kii sadhana karte rahe. prakruti ke bhedon kii ganit ke niyamon se tulana karne par inhone aashaa prakat kii ki donon ke rahasyon kaa gyaan ek hi prakaar se kiya ja saktaa he. is bhanti inhone tatvagyan main 'karteziyanvaad' kaa avishkaar kiya.
 ganit ko inaki sarvottam dein he vaishleshik jyamiti. 1637 i. main prakashit inke "diskur da laa metud' main jyamiti par bhi 106 prishth kaa ek niabandh tha. inhone sameekaran siddhant ke kuch niyamon kaa bhi avishkaar kiya, jinamein "chinhon kaa niyam' atyant prasiddh he. 11 farvari 1650 ko inaki mrityu ho gai.
