  abhimaan 1973 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he.
 subir kumaar ek peshewar gayak he jo samruddhi kii ore agrasar he. uma se milane tak uskaa shaadi karne kaa vichaar nahin tha. uma gaanv main durga mausi ke saath rahati he. usase shaadi kar bombay laa usase gayaki kaa pesha shuru karnaa chahta he. is bich uski gayaki dagamagaati he or uma kii ubhartee he or apane pati se bhi prasiddh gayak banati he. is par subir uma se dwesh karne lagata he or unke vaivaahik jivan main tanaav hota he. ek samvednaatmak mode par donon ke bichhadne ke baad uma kaa garbhpaat hota he. kya subir apane abhimaan va dwesh par vijay paata he?
 hrishikesh mukherjee ke is shreshthatam nirdesan main geet tere mere milan kii ye raina se ant main donon kaa bhaavnaatmak milan dikhaaya gaya he.
 sabhi geet majaruh sultanpuri dwaara likhit; sara sangeet sachin dev burman dwaara rachit.
