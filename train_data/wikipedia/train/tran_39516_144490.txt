 nimittopadaneshwarwad ke antargat ishwar ko is vishwa kaa nimitt or upadan kaaran mana jaataa he. iske alaava ishwar ko vishwathit evam vishwa main vyaapt donon hi mana jaataa he. is drushti se nimittopadaneshwarwad, ishwarwad ke samaan he or sarveshwarawad se alag he. kintu nimittopadaneshwarwad main ishwar ko ishwarwad kii tarah vyaktitvvaan nahin mana jaataa he balki isamen sarveshwarawad kii tarah ishwar ko vyaktitvarahit mana gaya he. jahaan sarveshwarawad ke anusaar 'sab kuch ishwar he', vahiin nimittopadaneshwarwad ke anusaar 'sab kuch ishwar main he' .
