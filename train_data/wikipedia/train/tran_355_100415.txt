 pugan yaa bagaan, myanmar ke mandle kshetra main sthit ek pracheen aitihaasik nagar he. pali bhashaa main iskaa naam 'arimddan' he. 9veen shatabdi se lekar 13veen shatabdi tak yah nagar pugan rajya kii rajdhani tha. 11veen se 13veen shatabdi ke samay jab yah rajya apane charmotkarsh par tha tab yahan 10 hajaar se adhik buddha mandir, pagoda or math nirmit kiye gayi jinamein se 2200 mandir ab bhi acchhee sthiti main vidyaman hai. 
 cambodia ke angakore wat nagar kii jo prasiddhi he, vahi myanmar ke pugan nagar kii he.
