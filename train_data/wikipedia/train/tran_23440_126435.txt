 kshar ek ayurvedic aushadhi he jiskaa varnan kai ayurvedic granthon main miltaa he. yah vibhinn rogon kii chikitsa main prayukt 
 kiya jaataa he. 'kshar' shabd 'kshar' se vyutpann hua he jiskaa arth he- pighalanaa yaa galanaa . acharya sushrut ne ise dosha ko nasht karne vaale padaarth ke rup main paribhaashit kiya he.
