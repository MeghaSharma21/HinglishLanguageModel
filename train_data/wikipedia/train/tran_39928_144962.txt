 siddharth kaak kashmiri bhartiya vruttchitr, doordarshan film nirmaataa evam prastota hai jinhen mukhyatah surabhi naamak doordarshan dharawahik ke nirmaataa or prastota ke rup main janaa jaataa he. uske baad, kaak ne ford foundation ke saath 'surabhi foundation' kii sthapana kii or saanskrithik kalakritiyon par kaam armbh kar diya.
 kaak kaa janm kashmir main hua or wo unka paalan-poshan dilli main hua. unki shiksha laurence skool, sanavar se armbh hui or uske baad unhone saint steefan college se snaatak kii shiksha purna kii.
