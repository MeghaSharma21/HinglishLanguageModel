 baidhnath bharat kaa sabase purana evam sabase lokapriy ayurvedic aushadhi nirmaataa samooh he. is company ke chyavanprash, dashamoolarishth, mahanarayan tel, chandra prabhaavati, mahayograj google aadi dawaaon kii ek khaas vishwasaneeyata he. vaidyanath kii khaas davaayen, kasamrita, beta eks, kabzhar to logon kii jubaan par he.
 1942 main bharat chodo andolan ke samay vaidyanath ne ek naaraa diya tha - 'desh kii mitti, desh kii hawa, desh kaa pani, desh kii dawa.' vaidyanath abhi puri duniya main failaa he. iskaa andaja isi baat se lagaaya ja saktaa he ki america main is company ke 33 dealer hai. vahaan dalas main vaidyanath kaa mukhyalay he. yah company europea desh italy, germany va belgium aadi main bhi niryaat kar rahi he.
 shri baidhnath ayurveda bhawan pra. limited]] kii sthapana varsh 1918 pan ramadayal joshi ne kiya tha. iskaa panjikrut karyaalay kolkata main he. yah company abhi 700 ayurvedic utpaadon kaa nirmaataa ban gai he. iske paas atyaadhunik factoriyaan hai or vahaan puri tarah vaigyanik paddatiyon se kaam hota he. dawa kii gunvattta hi is company kii pahchaan he. company ke paas bharat sarkaar dwaara sweekrut shodh centre he jahaan yogya vaigyanikon kii team he. ayurveda main vaidyanath kii vishwasaneeyata kaa yah paimaana he ki yahan se ayurveda par chhapane vaali pustak ayurvedic medical collejon ke paathyakram main shaamil kii jaati hai.
 iske ayurvedic saar sangrah main ayurvedic utpaadon kii sari vidhiyaan sankalit hai. yah bharat sarkaar dwaara sweekrut fermakopia kaa hissaa he. puure desh main vaidyanath ayurveda ke college, aspataal va dispenceriyaan hai.
