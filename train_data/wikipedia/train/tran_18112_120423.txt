 melanin ek prakritik rangadravya he ko prithvi ke adhiktar jeevon main paaya jaataa he, haalanki makadiyon jaise bhi ginati ke kuch jeev hai jinamein yah nahin paaya gaya he. jaanwaron main melanin tirocine naamak ek amino amla se banataa he. jeevon main melanin kaa sabase adhik paaya jane vaala rup 'eumelenin' kehalaata he or kaale-bhure rang kaa hota he. manushyon main yahi baalo, twachaa or aankhon ko rang detaa he. melanin kaa ek anya jeevvaigyanik rup 'feomelanin' he jo kuch-kuch laal hota he. yah laal baalo vaale or chiththidaar twachaa vaale vyaktiyon main rang detaa he. 
 yadi melanin kam ho to aankhon kaa rang haraa yaa nila, baalo kaa rang sunaharaa or twachaa kaa rang shwet ho jaataa he. melanin suraj kii kirnon main maujuud hanikaarak parabangnee se raksha karata he jo koshikaon ko nuksaan pahunchaata he jis se twachaa main jhurriyaan padane se lekar kark rog tak ho saktaa he. kam dhoop vaale sard kshetron main utpann hui maanav jatiyon main melanin kam hota he kyonki dhoop sharir main vitamin di banane ke liye aavashyak he. adhik dhoop vaale garam kshetron main utpann jatiyon main sharir kii raksha ke liye melanin adhik hota he. inamen sharir par padane vaala 99.9% parabangnee vikiran melanin dwaara roak diya jaataa he.
