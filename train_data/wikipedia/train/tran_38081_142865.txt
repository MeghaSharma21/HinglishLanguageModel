 antariksh main pehali baar jinniya naamak phool ugaaya gaya he.
 nasa dwaara 16 janvari 2016 ko jari soochna ke anusaar vaigyanikon ne antarrrashtriya spes stesan main ek phool ugane main safalataa praapt kii. yah america main paaya jane vaala jinniya naamak phool he.
 yah pehali baar hua he jab vaigyanikon ne prithvi se baahar koi phool khilaayaa he. shunya gurutvkarshan ke baavjood isaki paidaavaar kii gayi.
 isaki banaavat kaaphi had tak surajamukhi ke phool jaisi he jise khaane laayak bhi bataaya gaya he.
 nasa ke vaigyanik scot keli ne twit ke jariye jaankaree di ki jinniya phool america main ugaaya jaataa he tathaa ise salad ke rup main bhi khaaya ja saktaa he. antariksh vaigyanik is safal prayog ko antariksh vigyaan jagat main ek badi uplabdhi maan rahe hai.
 yah paudha spes centre main maujuud veji system ke madhyam se ugaaya gaya, jisamen laal, nile or hare rang kii elidi lite kaa prayog kiya jaataa he.
 iske saath hi ab antariksh main paudhon or kuch or jaruri baton par bhi gahan adhyayayan kiya ja sakegaa. isse pehle may 2014 main antarrrashtriya spes stesan main maujuud nasa kii lab main ek subjees ko safaltaapurvak ugaaya gaya tha.
