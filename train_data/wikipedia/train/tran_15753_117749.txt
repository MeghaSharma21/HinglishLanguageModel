 bhoomika chawla ek bhartiya abhinetri hai jo mukhyat dakshin bhartiya filmon main kaam karti he.
 bhoomika chawla kaa janm 21 agast, 1978 ko hua tha. unka janm kaa naam rachana or unhen pyaar se gudiya kah kar pukaara jaataa he.
