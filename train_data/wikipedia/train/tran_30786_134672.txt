 cordoba dakshini spain main cordoba praant kii rajdhani he. yah ek roman shahar tha,lekin aathvin shatabdi main muslim senaaon dwaara vijay ke baad upnivesh kiya gaya tha. jiske yah islamic emirate or cordoba khilaafat kii rajdhani rahaa tha.khalipha al hakam dwitiya ne kai chikitsa vidyalayon or vishvavidhyalayon ke alaava kai pustkaalay khole jo us samay maujuud the, jisase cordoba shiksha ke liye ek centre banaaya gaya. in sadiyon ke dauraan muslimon dwaara shasit samaaj kaa centre ban gaya, jisamen anya sabhi samoohon kaa dwitiya shrenee kaa darja tha. 1236 main isai senaaon dwaara rikanquista ke dauraan punah kabja kar liya gaya tha. aaj yah ek maamuli aakaar kaa aadhunik shahar he jansankhya anusaar 2011 kii jansankhya lagbhag 330,000 thi.. yah ke aitihaasik centre ko unesco kii vishwa viraasat sthal kaa naam diya gaya thamosam ke hisaab se cordoba main spain or europe main sabase oonchi garmiyon kaa taapmaana hai, july or agast main ausat ucch taapmaana 37 degree celsius ke aasapaas rahata he.
