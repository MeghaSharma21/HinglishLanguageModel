 puraniya yaa purniyan bihar rajya kaa zila he. iske uttar main arriya tathaa kishanganj jila, purv main pashchim bengal kaa dinajpur, pashchim main madhepura jila, dakshin main bhagalpur tathaa katihaar jila seemaa banaati he.
 iskaa kshetrafal 3229 varg kimi he. uttar kii ore dharaatal patharilaa tathaa purv kii ore nadiyon evam prakritik sroton se bane kabhi na sookhanevaale daldal evam pashchim kii ore retile ghaas ke maidan milte hai. ganga ke alaava kosi, mahananda tathaa panaar nadiyaan bahati hai. purv kii ore kahin-kahin uttam jalodh mitti milti he. varsha sheeghra prarambh hoti tathaa joro ke saath hoti he. vaarshik varsha kaa ausat 71 inch rahata he. krishi main dhan ke atirikt daalen, tilhan or tambaakoo bhi paidaa haata he. pashu chote tathaa kamjor haate hai. udyogon main mote rangeen kapade, baiylgaadiyon ke pahie, chataaiyaan tathaa jute ke saamaan banaae jate hai.
