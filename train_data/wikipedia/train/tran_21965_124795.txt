 naaraa, rajnatik, vanijyik, dharmik or anya sandarbhon main, kisi vichaar yaa uddeshy ko barambar abhivyakt karne ke liye prayukt ek yaadgaar adarsh-vaakya yaa sookhti he. angrezi main naare ke liye prayukt slogan shabd, scottish tathaa irish galic sluagh-ghairm ke angrezikrut shabd slogorn se vyutpann he. likhit or drishy se lekar alaape or asabhy tak, naaron main vividhta ho sakati he. aksar unki aasaan bayaanbazi prakruti, vistrut vivaranon kii koi gunjaaish nahin chhodti he or isliye sambhawatah ve abhisht shrotaaon ke liye prakshepan kii bajaay, ekikrut uddeshy kii saamaajik abhivyakti ke rup main adhik kaam karte hai.
 kulchihnon main, visheshtah scottish kulchihnon main naaron kaa istemaal usee tarah hota he, jaise ki adarsh-vaakya prayukt hote hai. jahaan adarsh-vaakyon ke kai alag mul ho sakate hai, vahiin naaron kaa udgam, rannaad yaa yuddh-ghosh ke rup main yaa unka pratinidhitiv karne ke liye hua mana jaataa he. ve aam taur par hathiyaaron ke aavaran par chotii ke upar pradarshit hote hai.
