 khorasm praant madhya asia main sthit uzbekistan desh kaa ek vilaayaat he jo us desh ke uttar-pashchimi kshetra main sthit he. yah amu dariyaa ke ant vaale ilaake main sthit he . praant kaa kul kshetrafal 6,300 varg kimi he or 2005 main isaki anumaanit aabaadi 12,00,000 thi. khorasm praant kii rajdhani urgench shahar he. yahan mohammad ibn musa al-khwaarizmi naamak mashhoor madhyakaalin ganitagya paidaa hue the jinhonen bhartiya ankon or dashamalav paddati par granth likhe the. in granthon kaa baad main latini bhashaa main anuwaad hua jis se yah bhartiya avishkaar europe main prachlit ho gaye.
 'khorasm' main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
