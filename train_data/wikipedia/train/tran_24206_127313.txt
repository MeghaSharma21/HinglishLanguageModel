 lakshminath bejbaruva aadhunik asamiya sahitya ke path-pradarshak kahe jate hai. kavita, naatak, galp, upanyaas, nibandh, ramyarachana, samalochna, prahasan, jeevani, aatmajivani, shishusahitya, itihaas adhyayan, saanvadikta aadi drushtiyon se bejbaruva kaa yogadandaan amuly he.
 unka "asamiya saahityer chaaneki" naamak sankalan vishesh prasiddh he. asamiya sahitya main unhone kahani tathaa lalit niabandh ke bich ke ek sahitya rup ko adhik prachlit kiya. bejbarua kii hasyaras kii rachanaaon ko kaaphi lokapriyata mili. isiilie use "rasraj" kii oopaadhi di gai.
 bejbaruva kaa saahitiyik jivan kolkata main shiksha prapti ke samay se hi armbh ho gaya tha.
