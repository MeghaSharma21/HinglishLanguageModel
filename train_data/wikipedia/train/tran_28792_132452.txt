 
 kufa iraq ke najaf praant main furaat nadi ke kinare sthit ek shahar he. yah iraq kii rajdhani baghdad se 170 kimi dakshin main or prantiy rajdhani najaf se 10 kimi purvottar main padataa he.
 samarra, kerbala, kaazimia or najaf ke saath kufa shiya islam ke paanch sabase mahatvapurn iraqi shaharon main gina jaataa he. yah islam ke pigambar mohammad ke damaad, ali ebn abu talib kii antim rajdhani thi. islam kii sabase pracheen masjidon main se ek, mahaan kufa masjid, isi shahar main sthit he.
