  raajnneeti praveshdwar
 aazarbaijan, cokeshas ke purvi bhaag main ek ganraajya he, purvi europe or asia ke madhya main basaa hua. bhugolic rup se yah asia kaa hi bhaag he. iske seemaant desh hai: armenia, georgia, rus, iran, turki or iskaa tateey bhaag caspian sagar se lagata hua he. yah 1991 tak bhootpurv soviet sangh kaa bhaag tha.
 azarbaijan ek dharmanirpeksh desh he or varsh 2001 se council kaa sadsy he. adhikansh jansankhya islam dharm kii anuyaayi he or yah desh islamic sammelan sangh kaa sadsy raashtra bhi he. yah desh dhire-dhire aupchaarik lekin sattavadi loktantr kii ore bad rahaa he.
 "azarbaijan" naam ke udgam ko lekar kai prakaar kii avadharanaayen he. sabase prachlit prameya yah he kii yah naam "atropaton" shabd se niklaa he. atropat farsi akaminoid rajvansh ke samay main ek kshatrap tha, jise sikandar mahaan ne akraman karke parrast kiya or atropaton ko swadhinata mili. us samay yah kshetra midia atropatia yaa atropatine ke naam se janaa jaataa tha.
 is naam kii mul utpatti kii jaden pracheen irani panth, parsi dharm main maani jaati hai. aavesta ke ek dastauez main is baat kaa ullekh he "âterepâtahe ashaonô fravashîm ýazamaide", pracheen farsi main jiskaa shabdic anuwaad he "pavitra ataare-pata ke fraavashi kii ham vandana karte hai". atropatno ne atropaton kshetra par shashan kiya. "atropaton" naam swayan ek pracheen-irani, sambhawatah midan, kaa unani dhvanyatmak yugma he, jiskaa arth he "pavitra agni dwaara rakshit".
 azarbaijan main praarambhik maanav bastiyon ke chihn pashaan yug ke baad ke dinon ke hai. 550 isapurv main acumenida rajvansh ne is kshetra par vijay praapt kii thi, jisase parsi dharm kaa udai hua or baad main yah kshetra sikandar mahaan ke samrajya kaa bhaag bana or baad main uske uttaradhikari, celiusida samrajya kaa. albanian cokeshan logon ne chauthi shatabadi isapurv main is kshetra main ek swatantra rajshahi kii sthapana kii, lekin 95-67 isapurv main tigaraneese 2 mahaan ne isapar adhikaar kar liya.
