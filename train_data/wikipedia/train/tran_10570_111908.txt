 sankhya siddhanth main kisi purnank q ko mapank n kaa dwighati avshesh kehete hai yadi nimnlikhit sambandh satya ho-
 jahaan x koi purnank he. iske vipareet yadi uparokt sambandh x ke kisi bhi maan ke liye satya na ho to q ko mapank n kaa dwighat anavshesh kehete hai.
 dwighat avshesh multah sankhya siddhanth kii modular ganit naamak shakha kii sankalpana thi. aajkal dwighat avasheshon kaa upyog dhwani ejiniyari, beej-lekhan tathaa bahut badi sankhyaon ke gunankhand nikaalane main hota he.
