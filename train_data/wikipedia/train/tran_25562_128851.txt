 
 bohrium ek rasinic tatv he, jise Bh ke chihn se dikhaaya jaataa he or iskaa parmaanu kramank 107 he. is tatv ko prayogshaala main banaaya ja saktaa he, lekin prakruti main kahin nahin miltaa he. iskaa sabase sthaayee samasthanik 270Bh he. isaki ardh-aayu lagbhag 61 second kii hoti he. apramanit 278Bh kii ardh-aayu 690 second kii hoti he, jo pehle vaale se kaaphi adhik samay he. 
 do samoohon ne kaha ki un logon ne ek nae tatv kaa khoj kiya he. bohrium ke hone kaa pramaan sabase pehle 1976 main roosi anusandhan dal ke euri oganessian ne diya tha.
