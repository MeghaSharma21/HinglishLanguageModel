 nicobari bharat ke nicobar dweepasmooh main rahane vaale austro-asian bhashaa-parivaar kii nicobari bhashaayen bolne vaala ek samudaay he.
 mul rup se nicobari sarvatmavaad ke anuyaayi the jisamen prakritik shaktiyon or purvajon ko devon ke rup main puja jaataa tha. bharat main british raaj ke kaal main inkaa isaikaran kiya gaya, lekin parmparik nicobari aasthaaen aaj bhi kai logon dwaara maanya hai. isaikaran ke prayaas europea deshon dwara san 1758 main armbh hue lekin vifal rahe. baad main isaikaran ke baad bhi samaaj main "tamiluana" or "meluana" kii bhoomikaaye jari raheen hai or alag-alag dwipon par aasthaaen alag gaharaai se jari hai. yah manyata he ki kuch vastuen saadhaaran hoti hai or kuch pavitra or pavitra vastuon ko chune yaa prayog karne main saavdhaanee baratani chaiye. masalan har gram kii ek saamaajik nauka hoti he jise pavitra mana jaataa he. anya naukaaon se machhlee pakadaney kaa va yatayaat kaa kaam liya jaataa he lekin is pavitra nauka ko keval samarohik prayogon main hi laayaa jaataa he. bacchon ko iske paas jane yaa isamen khilne kii manaahi he. car nicobar ke alaava anya dweep ke log choti manavnuma murtiyan rakhte hai jinhen bina haath dhoye choona manaa hota he.
 nicobari nar-naariyon kaa saamaajik str lagbhag baraabar hota he. nicobari samudaay kii mukhiya "rani" kii upadhi rakhti he. pehali rani kaa naam islone tha jinhonen 1941-42 kaal main nanakori dweep ke mewaalaal naamak tehseeldaar se vivaah kiya tha.
 logon kaa awaas dharti se khambon par uthe hue kutiron main hota he. raatri main inpar seedhi lagaakar chadha jaataa he or phir seedhi upar khinch lee jaati he. gaavon main yah kutir sthaan-sthaan par khadey kiye jate hai.
 nicobari samudaay alag-alag nicobari bhashaayen bolte hai. car bhashaa sarvaadhik bole jane vaali nicobari bhashaa he.
