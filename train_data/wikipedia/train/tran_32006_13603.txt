 kalpakkam tamilnadu kaa ek chota sa kasbaa he jo chenai ke 70 kimi dakshin main samudr ke kinare sthit he. yah bharat sarkaar ke parmaanu urja vibhaag ke madras parmaanu urja sanyantr tathaa indira gandhi parmaanu anusandhan kendra ke liye prasiddh he. iske alaava yah puratathvik mahatv ke mandiron ke liye bhi prasiddh he.
