 
 chadcharaan​, jise itihaas main chadcheran or aahangaaran​ ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa tha, madhya afghanistan ke gor praant kii rajdhani he. harirud ke dakshini kinare par basaa yah shahar 2,280 meter kii oonchaai par he.
 chadcharaan​ rajmarg dwaara apane se 380 kimi pashchim main sthit heraat se judaa hua he or rashtriya rajdhani kabul lagbhag utani hi duuri par purv main sthit he. sardiyon main bhari barfabaari se yah rajmarg aksar band ho jaataa he or garmiyon main bhi gaadi dwaara kabul se yahan pahunchne main ek puraa din lag jaataa he.
 'chadcharaan' main 'ga' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'ga' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'galati' or 'garib' shabdon ke 'ga' se miltaa he.
 mahmud gazanvi dwaara is kshetra main islam failaae jane se pehle yahan kai dharm ke log rahete the, jinamein hindu, buddha or parsi dharm ke anuyaayiyon ke saath-saath kuch yahudee bhi the. 12veen sadi main gazanaviyon ke patan ke baad yahan par gor ke sthaneey gori samrajya kaa bolbalaa rahaa. 13veen sadi main changez khan kii mongol sena ne yahan dhaava bolaa. iske baad yahan ilkhaani samrajya kaa raaj rahaa. 14veen sadi main taimur ne is ilaake par qabza kar liya. aage chalakar 16veen sadi main babar ne apane babarnama naamak vrutant main 1507 main kabul jate hue 'chadcheran​' se guzarane kaa bakhaan likhaa, jisamen usne ise heraat, gor or gazani ke bich kaa ek shahar bataaya.
 chadcharaan​ gor paant kaa sabase bada shahar he or yahan lagbhag 1,50,000 log rahete hai. inamen se 96% tajik samudaay ke, 2% pashtun samudaay ke or 2% hazaaraa samudaay ke hai.
