 ienes vajrakosh bhartiya nausena kaa naveenatam adhishthan he jo karnataka ke karwar main sthit he. raksha mantri shri manohar parrikar ne 9 sitambar 2015 ko ise raashtra ko samarpit kiya.
 ienes vajrakosh karwar main nausena kaa teesraa adhishthan he, jise raashtra ko samarpit kiye jane ke baad bhartiya nausena kii aakraamak or surakshatmak kshamtaaon main bahut vruddhi hogi. 
 nikat bhavishya main karwar pashchimi kaman ke antargat bhartiya nausena kaa ek pramukh aadhaar banane ja rahaa he. ienes vajrakosh main mahatvapurn nausena parisampattiyon jaise ki vishisht hathiyaaron or mixilon ke bhandaaran kii vishesh suvidhaayen va aavashyak sanrachanaayen maujuud hongi jinhen visheshgy apni nigaraanee main rakhenge taaki samrik aavashyaktaaon ko puraa kiya ja sake.
