 ramprasad niranjani hindi sahityakar the. ab tak kii khoj ke aadhaar par ye hindi ke pratham praudh gadhylekhak maane jate hai. aap patiala darbar main kathavachak the. patiala riyasat kii maharani deso ko sunaane ke liye hi aapane 'bhashaa yogawasishth' kaa parimarjeet khadi boli gadhy main pranayan kiya tha. yah aapaki ekmaatr rachana he. bich-bich main sanskrit ke shuddh tatsam shabd or hindi ke katipay puraane prayog bhi uplabdh hote hai.
