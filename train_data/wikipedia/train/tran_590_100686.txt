 hans dilli se prakashit hone vaali hindi kii ek katha masik patrika he jiskaa sampaadan rajendra yadav ne 1986 se 2013 tak puure 27 varsh kiya. 
 upanyaas samraat premchand dwaara sthapit or sampadit hans apane samay kii atyant mahatvapurn patrika rahi he. mahatma gandhi or kanhaiyalaal maanik laal munshi bhi do varsh tak hans ke sampadak mandal main shaamil rahe. munshi premchand kii mrityu ke baad hans kaa sampaadan unke putr kathakaar amritraay ne kiya. anek varshon tak hans kaa prakaashan band rahaa. baad main munshi premchand kii janmatithi ko hi san 1986 se akshar prakaashan ne kathakaar rajendra yadav ke sampaadan main is patrika ko ek katha masik ke rup main phir se prakashit karnaa prarambh kiya. vartmaan main hans ek saanskrithik, saahitiyik or vichaarsheel patrika ke rup main jaani jaati he.
