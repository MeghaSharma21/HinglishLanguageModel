 turkana jhil, jise pehle rudolf jhil bulaayaa jaataa tha, mahaan africa jhiilon main se ek he. yah kuch-kuch khaare pani kii jhil ghanfal ke hisaab se caspian sagar, isik-kul or vaan jhil ke baad duniya kii chauthi sabase badi khaari jhil he. aral sagar is se kabhi bada hua karata tha lekin adhiktar suukh jane ke kaaran ab is se chota he. turkana ek registan-jaise kshetra main sthit he or vishwa kii sabase badi sthai registani jhil bhi he. iskaa adhiktar bhaag kenya main he lekin sudur uttari chor ithiopia main padataa he.
 is jhil ke bich ke kendriya dweep par ek jivit jwalamukhi he jis se gas or ghuaan hameshaa risataa rahata he. jhil ke dakshini or purvi tat patthariile hai jabaki uttari or pashchimi taton par retile maidan or ret ke tile hai. dhoop main aasapaas kii dharti jald hi garam ho jaati he jabaki pani dhire-dhire hi garam hota he. zamin or pani main taapmaana antar se yahan tej hawaayen chalati hai or kabhi-kabhi achaanak aandhiyaan bhi aa jaati hai. maidani taton par magaramchh or patthariile kinaaron par saanp va bichchhu milte hai. paryatakon ko yahan kabhi-bhi bina sthaneey maargdarshakon ke akele na jane kii salaah di jaati he.
 turkana ke aasapaas ke ilaake main pracheen maanvon ke kai jeevashm milen hai or kai vidwaanon ke anusaar maanav jati kaa janm yaheen hua tha.
