 paraprakritik shabd un vyaktiyon, vastuon yaa ghatnaon ke liye prayukt hota he jise kuch log vaastavik maanate hai, lekin jo prakruti kaa bhaag nahin hote yaa saamaanya prakruti se pare hote he. "alokik" yaa "parloukik" shabd bhi iske liye prayukt hota he. chunki inke astitv ko siddh kar paanaa sambhav nahin hota, isliye aamtaur par log inke bare main asahmat rahete hai.
 vaigyanikon kaa maananaa he ki hamen paraprakritik ke sthaan par vishay par bolnaa chahie, kyonki ham yah siddh nahin kar sakate kii paraprakritik vishay vaastavik hai. kuch anya logon kaa maananaa he ki yadhyapi paraprakritik vishyon kaa vaigyanik pramaan nahin hota, lekin ye vaastavik hai. bahut se vishay jaise dharm, jaadu, chamatkaar, aatma, bhoot, devdoot, shaitan, dev or ishwar paraprakritik yaa parloukik hai.
 comic pustakon main, ek souparahiro yaa mahanayake vah vyakti hota he jiske paas paraprakritik yaa mahamanviya shaktiyaan hotin hai or vah logo ko khataron se bachaata he or apni pahchaan chhipaata he. inaki shaktiyon ko mahamanviya isliye kaha jaataa he kyonki prakritik rup se kisi bhi saamaanya maanav main aisi shaktiyaan sambhav nahin hai. udhaaharan ke liye souparaman, spidar-main, bitman ityadi.
