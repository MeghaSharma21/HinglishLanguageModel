 terenganoo malaysia kaa ek rajya he. yah prayadwipiya malaysia ke purvottaree tat par dakshin china sagar ke kinare sthit he. is rajya main kai dweep bhi aate hai jinamein redang dweep prasiddh he.
 malaysia main alag-alag rajyon ke puraane rajpariwaron ko sanvaidhanik darja mila hua he. terenganoo kaa sanvaidhanik adhyaksh sultan kehalaata he haalanki is pad ke saath koi vaastavik prashasnic adhikaar nahin aate. malaysia main kuch rajyon ko aupchaarik arabi bhashaa ke naam bhi diye jate hai or terenganoo kaa naam "terenganoo daar-ul-imaan" he, yani "selangore, imaan kaa ghar".
