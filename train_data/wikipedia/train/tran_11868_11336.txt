 malta ne 15 greeshmakaleen olympic khelon or 1 sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main hissaa liya he.
 landan 2012 olympic khelon ke rup main koi maltez pratiyogi ne olympic padak nahin jeeta he haalanki shooter wiliam chetakati 2004, 2008 or 2012 main apekshaakrit patale antar se dabl trep shooting final ko ganvaa diya tha. 2004 or 2008 main chetakati 6 wein ke liye judaa tha, lekin taai braker par haar gaye.elice pelegreen desh kaa pratinidhitiv karne vaala pehla sheetakaaleen olympian he, 2014 main sochi main sheetakaaleen olympic main.
 maltez olympic samiti kaa gathan 1928 main hua tha or 1936 main iosi dwaara manyata praapt thi.
