 shalk aise chote or khand ko kehete hai jo uske sharir kii twachaa se baahar uga hua ho or andar ke sharir va twachaa ko vaataavaran, shikaar yaa anya haani se surakshit rakhe. saanp or machhlee jaise praaniyon main shalk kaa dhakaav unke bhitari najuk sharir kii raksha karata he. titali jaise kuch praaniyon main yah pankhons kii raksha ke saath-saath unhen rang bhi detaa he.
 ek samudri chil ke panjo ke upree bhaag par shalk
 titliyon ke pankhons par maheen rangadaar shalk lage hote hai
 ek machhlee ke shalk
 do bhinn dianosor kii twachaa ke jeevashm jinamein shalk spasht dikh rahe hai
