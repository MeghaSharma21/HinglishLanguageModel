 phongphui, jise nila parvat bhi kehete hai, purvottar bharat kii lushai parvatamala kaa or mizoram rajya kaa sabase unchaa parvat he. yah 2,157 meter unchaa he or dakshinpurvi mizoram main burma kii sarhad ke paas sthit he. yah puraa kshetra phongphui rashtriya udyaan ke antargat ek sangrakshit kshetra he. phongphui mizoram ke longtlai zile main sthit he.
 parvat ke upar lagbhag 2 kimi kii samtal dharti he.
 phongphui ek pavitra parvat mana jaataa he. iskaa naam lai bhashaa se liya gaya he, jisamen 'fong' kaa matlab 'ghasbhumi' yaa 'marg' hota he or 'pui' kaa arth 'mahaan' he. kisi jamaane main phongphui ke puure kshetra par ghaas se bhare marg faile hue the, jis kaaran yah naam padaa. manyata thi ki parvat par 'sangau' naamak devtaa-raja kaa vaas he, jis karanvash pahaad ke charnon main sthit ek basti kaa naam sangau pad gaya. sangau raja ke putr kaa vivaah 'cherian' naamak rajpariwaar kii rajkumaari se hua or uske saath yahan ek hoolok gibbon kaa joda or ek chid kaa vriksh laayaa gaya. vartmaan kaal main pahaad ke upar jane vaale raste kaa pramukh praveshsthan 'farpak' kehalaata he, jiskaa arth 'keval chid' he.
