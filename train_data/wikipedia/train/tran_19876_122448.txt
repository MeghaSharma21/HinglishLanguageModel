 chandrashekhar kambaar ek kannada bhashaa ke kavi, naatakkaar evam loksahityakar hai. unhone kannada bhashaa main filmon kaa nirdesan bhi kiya he or ve hampi main kannada vishvavidhyalay ke sansthaapak kulapati bhi rahe hai. unke ullekhneey saahitiyik yogadan ke alok main unhen 2010 ke liye gyanpith puraskaar pradaan karne kii ghoshnaa kii gai he.
 inke dwaara rachit ek naatak sirisampige ke liye unhen san 1991 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
