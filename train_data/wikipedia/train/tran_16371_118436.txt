 kaman mishmi yaa miju mishmi, tibet or arunachal pradesh ke mishmi logon kii teen janajatiyon main se ek hai. bharat main is samudaay ke sadsy anjo or lohit zilon main sthit hai. miju gut kaa daawa he ki ve burma ke kachin kshetra se aae hai. ve tibeti-burmi bhashaa-parivaar kii midzu shakha kii bhashaayen bolte hai.
