 bangaroo lakshmana bhartiya janata party se ek bhartiya raajnneetijna the. wo varsh 2000 se 2001 tak bhaajapa adhyaksh evam 1999 se 2000 tak bharat sarkaar main rail rajya mantri rahe. unhen farji raksha saudon main lipt paye jane ke baad chaar varsh karawas kii sajaa mili.
 bangaroo lakshmana kaa 1 march 2014 ko lambii beemaari ke baad nidhan ho gaya. unhone antim saans sikanderabad sthit yashoda aspataal main lee.
