 nagari lipi se hi devanagari, nandinagari aadi lipiyon kaa vikaas hua he. iskaa pehle praakrit or sanskrit bhashaa ko likhane main upyog kiya jaataa tha. kai baar 'nagari lipi' kaa arth 'devanagari lipi' bhi lagaaya jaataa he. 
 nagari lipi kaa vikaas brahmi lipi se hua he. kuch anusandhanon se pataa chalaa ki nagari lipi kaa vikaas pracheen bharat main pehali se chauthi shatabdi main gujarat main hua tha. saatvin shatabdi main yah lipi aamtaur par upyog kii jaati thi or kai shatabdiyon ke pashchaat iske sthaan par devanagari or nandinagari kaa upyog hone laga.
