 bangladesh ke mukhya nyaayadhish kaa pad, bangladesh sarvocch nyaayik pad he. is pad par virajman hone vaale pehle padaadhikari nyaayamurti ab saadaat mohammeh khan sayam the, joki 16 disambar 1972 se november 1975 tak is pad par rahe the. tatpashchaat, janvari 2015 tak is pad par kul 21 log virajman ho chuke hai. vartmaan mukhya nyaayadhish surendra kumaar sinha is pad par 17 janvari 2015 virajman hai. ve hindu dharm ke anuyaayi hai, tathaa bishnupriy manipuri samudaay se aate hai, tathaa ve bangladesh main kisi bhi alpsankhyak jaateey samoohon se niyukt pehali mukhya nyaayadhish he. nyaayamurti bhaavani prasad sinha bhi ek hi samudaay se hai. nyaayamurti mahauday nazaman aara sultanaa is pad ko sushobhit karne vaali pehali mahila nyaayadhish thim, or madam justise krishna debanath bangladesh kii pehali mahila hindu nyaayadhish he. vartmaan main supreme court main chah mahila nyaayadhishon rahe hai. 
 bangladesh main sainya shashanke ke dauraan, 1973 se 1975 ke bich, sarvocch nyaayalay ko dwibhajit kar, do bhinn, ucch nyaayalay va sarvocch nyaayalay sthapit kiye gaye the. is bich, nyaayamurti ruhul islam ucch nyaayalay ke mukhya nyaayadhish the, or saiyad mahmud hossain sarvocch nyaayalay ke mukhya nyaayadhish the. nimna talika bangladesh ke saare mukhya nyaayadhishon ko suchit karti he.
