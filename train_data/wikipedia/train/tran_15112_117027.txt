 checkoslovakia madhya europe main sthit ek desh hua karata tha jo oktober 1918 se 1992 tak astitv main rahaa. dwitiya vishwayuddh ke dauraan 1939 se 1945 ke ek antaraal main iskaa jabardastee germany main vilay kar diya gaya isliye vaastaviktaa main yah desh us jamaane main astitv main nahin tha, haalanki aupchaarik rup se mitrapaksh shaktiyaan tab bhi ise manyata detii raheen. 1945 main soviet sangh ne iske ek purvi hisse ko checkoslovakia se alag karke apane kshetra kaa bhaag bana liya. sheet yuddh kaal main checkoslovakia par samyawad shashan rahaa or yah desh soviet sangh ke netritv main gathit warsaw sandhi ke mitrapaksh main shaamil tha. soviet sangh ke tuutane par 1990 main yahan bhi samyawad khatm ho gaya. dhire-dhire desh ke do mukhya samudaayon - check or slowe - ke bich tanaav badhata rahaa or lagane laga ki ve ek raashtra main milakar nahin rah paayenge. 1992 main raishumaari kii gai or janata ne desh ko baantne kaa faisalaa chunaa. 1 janvari 1993 ko desh bina kisi hinsa ke do alag rashtron main baant gaya jinhen check ganatantr or slovakia ke naamon se janaa jaataa he. vishwa main anya deshon ke hue vibhaajanon kii tulana main yah bantwaaraa itane komal or shantipurvak dhang se hue ki is ghatana ko itihaaskaar or sameekshak kabhi-kabhi 'makhmali talaaq' kehete hai.
