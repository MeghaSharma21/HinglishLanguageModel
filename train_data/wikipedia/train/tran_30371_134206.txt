 2010 indian premier lig indian premier lig kaa teesraa sanskaran tha. jiskaa kartadharta bhartiya cricket niyantran board tha. yah pratiyogita bharat main hi yojit kii gai thi. yah pratiyogita 12 march se 25 april 2010 tak gaya tha. yah pehla aisa toornament tha jise utyub par live dikhaaya tha. is sanskaran ke aakhri chaar match 3di televijan screen par dikhaae gaye the.
 is sanskaran main kul 60 match khele gaye the jiskaa final match mumbai ke di vaay patil stadium main 25 april ko khelaa gaya tha. match chennai super kings or mumbai indians ke bich khelaa gaya tha. match main chennai super kings ne toss jitkar pehle ballebaaji karte hue 20 over main kul 5 wicket khokar 168 ran banaae the jawaab main mumbai indians ne 20 over main 9 wicket khokar 148 ran hi bana pai thi ,main of da match suresh raina ko diya gaya tha or main of da toornament sachin tendulkar ko diya gaya tha.
