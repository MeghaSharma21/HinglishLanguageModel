 piloda athwa pilauda bhartiya rajya rajasthan ke savai madhopur jile kii gangapore city tahsil kaa ek gaanv he. yah gaanv jile ek bade gaanvon main se ek he. gaanv kii kul jansankhya 6,038 se adhik he jisamen adhiktar janajatiyaan hai. gaanv main 2,000 se adhik ghar hai.
 yah gaanv bhi rajasthan ke anya adivasee gaanvon ke samaan he. yah gaanv sadak maarg evam railmarg se acchhee tarah se judaa hua he. is gaanv kaa brodges railway stesan shri mahaviraji or gangapore railway stesan ke madhya sthit he. gaanv shri lahakod devi ke tirthasthal ke rup main prasiddh he. iske paas kaa pavitra talab, mukhyatah april maah main durgashtami or shri ram navami ke din shraddhaluon kii bheed ke saath dekha ja saktaa he. is din yahan sthaneey mela lagata he jiskaa aayojan antar-gramin restling, kushti, oont daud, ghud daud, bail daud, tairaaki or cycling ke rup main kiya jaataa he.
