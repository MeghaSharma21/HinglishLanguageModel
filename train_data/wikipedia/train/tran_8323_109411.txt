 garty theresa kori check-amareeki jaev vaigyani thi jo kii nobel inam jeetne vaali duniya kii teesri or america kii pehali aurat thi. chikitsa ke kshetra main nobel inam jeetne vaali vah pehali aurat thi. uskaa janm praha main hua tha. tabhi ke samay main striyon ko vigyaan ke kshetra se dur hi rakhaa jaataa tha or shiksha kshetra main unako bahut kam avasar milte the. kori ne phir ek chikitsa college main daakhila liya jahaan pe vah karl ferdinand kori se mili, jisane grajuation puri karne ke uparaant 1920 main gerti se vivaah kiya.
