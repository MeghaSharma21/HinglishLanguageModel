 kanhori gaanv bharat desh ke haryana rajya main jila rewadi kaa ek madhyam aakaar kaa simavarti gaanv he.
 jhajjar jile kaa silani gaanv iskaa purvaj gaanv mana jaataa he. vaise to yah gaanv jat bahulya he parantu iske atirikt anya jatiyon main chamaar, dhanak, kumhaar, nai, khaati, brahman va valmiki yahan nivas karte hai. jat jati main chaahaar, kinha tathaa lamba gotra maujuud hai.
 yah gaanv rewadi va jhajjar ke bich gaanv guravda se 4 kilometre dur uttar-pashchim main sthit he. iskaa nikatatam railway stesan palhavas he. is gaanv main moolbhoot suvidhaon main ucchtar madhyamik vidhyalay, praathmik chikitsaalay, anganvadi, mandir, chaoupal, sinchai nahar, jalghar aadi uplabdh hai. 
 gaanv ke log mukhyatah kheti baadi karte hai. iske atirikt yahan ke yuva police, sena aadi main janaa pasand karte hai. yahan kii kabddi kii team puure pradesh main prasiddh he.
 is gaanv main prayek varsh caitra maas ke shukla paksh kii ekadshi ko baba bhairu ji kaa mela lagata he. is din baba bhairu ji ko tel chadhaakar puja kii jaati he. is avasar par kushti pratiyogita kaa bhi aayojan kiya jaataa he jisamen dur dur se pahalwaan aakar apana loha manavaate hai.
