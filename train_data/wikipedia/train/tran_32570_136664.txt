 yato dharmah tato jayah ek sanskrit shloke he. yah bharat ke sarvocch nyaayalay kaa dhyey vaakya he. yah mahabharat main kul gyaarah baar aata he or iskaa matlab he "jahaan dharm he vahaan jai he."
 is dhyeyvakya kaa arth mahabharat ke us shloke se aata he jab kurukshetra ke yuddh main arjuna yudhisthir ke akarmanyata ko dur kar rahen hai. wo kehete hai, "vijay sadaa dharm ke paksh main rahati he, evam jahaan shrikrishna hai vahaan vijay he". gandhari bhi apane putron ke mrityu ke baad samaan udgaar kahati he.
