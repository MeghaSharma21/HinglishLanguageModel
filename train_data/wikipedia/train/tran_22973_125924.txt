 nicholai petrovich ek roosi anveshak, vyapaaree or jahaaz captain tha. iske prayaason se san 1812-42 tak roosi samrajya ne uttari america mahadweep ke pashchimi tat par apane upnivesh banaae. alaska ko baad main sanra.america ne rus se kareed liya. wo rus ke pehle vishwa parikrama karne vaale jahaaj kaa captain tha . 
 wo prasiddh siberian vyapaaree grigori shelliov kaa 1794 main sahyogi bana or baad main uskaa damaad. iske baad rus ke nae raja paul ne use roosi america company kaa pehla commander banaaya gaya. wo aaj ke california tak roosi samrajya ko le gaya, lekin 1840 aate aate america zamin par rus kaa prabhutv or utsaah donon khatm ho gaye.
