 bikol prayadweep dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh ke luzone dweep par sthit ek prayadweep he. yah dweep ke dakshinpurvi bhaag main sthit he or kezone praant ise luzone ke atirikt kshetra se jodta he lekin iskaa anya bhaag puri tarah samudr se ghira hua he. prashasnic drushti se yah bikol kshetra kaa hissaa he.
