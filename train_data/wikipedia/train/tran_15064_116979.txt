  vikhyath vaidic tathaa puranic asur : vaidic sambar parvatanivasee das tha jisane 'vrutra' kii tarah akaash main nabbe, ninyanbe yaa sau durgon kaa nirmaan kiya tha . apane ko devtaa maan lene par indra ne maruton or ashviniyon kii sahaayta se tathaa divodas ke anurodh par iskaa vadh kar diya evam samast durg nasht kar daale.
 puranetihaas granthon main yah kashyap or danu ke putron main se ek tha or daanav hote hue bhi param gyani tathaa raajnneetijna tha. vritrasur se hue yuddh main is devpidak asur kaa vadh bhi indra ke haathon hua.
  kashyap danputra ek anya daanav jo kansa kaa anuyaayi tha. krishnaputra pradhyumna ke haathon maare jane kii akashwani sunakar isane unako maarna chaha. ant main yah pradhyumna ke haathon maaraa gaya .
  is naam ke anya anek daanvon main hiranyaksh kaa putr, tripur nagari kaa bali paksheeya asur yoddha aadi ullekhneey hai.
