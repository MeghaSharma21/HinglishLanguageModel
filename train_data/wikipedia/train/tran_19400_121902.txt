 gaidaar prasiddh roosi lekhak jinaki kahaniyaan soviet bacchon main bahut prasiddh thim.
 gaidaar 14 varsh kii aayu main laal sena main swayamsevak banakar aae. 17 varsh kii aayu main regiment ke commander hue. aswasthta ke kaaran 1924 main sena se chuttee mili or saahitiyik kaary prarambh kiya. mahan deshbhaktipurna yuddh ke samay gaidaar morchon par gaye vahiin phasiston ne unhen maar daalaa. 
 gaidaar ne kishoropayogi sahitya ko badi dein di. inke anek upanyaas or kahaniyaan hai, jinamein mukhya skool, doorvarti desh, sainik rahasy, nila pyaalaa, chuk or gek, timur or uskaa dal hai. in kritiyon main maitri, saahas tathaa deshbhakti kii bhavanaaen paripurna hai jinke kaaran ye rachanaayen ati lokapriy hai. inke aadhaar par anek filmein bhi bani hai. anek bhashaaon main, jinamein hindi bhi sammilit he, gaidaar kii kritiyaan anudit hai.
