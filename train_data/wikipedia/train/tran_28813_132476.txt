 daabit baari jal riactor ) vah power riactor he jisamen vimandak tathaa shitalk ke rup main baari jal kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. isamen eendhan ke rup main prakritik uranium kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. isamen baari jal ko saamaanya daab ke bajaay ucch daab par rakhaa jaataa he taaki bina ubaale hi jal ko adhik taap tak le jaaya ja sake. 
 yadhyapi baari jal, saadhaaran jal kii apeksha adhik mahangaa padataa he, kintu iske upyog se ek laabh yah hota he ki isamen prakritik uranium se hi kaam chal jaataa he, arthath bina sanvardhit uranium ke bhi kaam chal jaataa he.
