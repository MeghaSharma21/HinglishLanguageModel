 khagol vigyaani athwa khagol shastri ve vaigyanik adhyayankarta hai ji akashiya pindon, unki gatiyon or antariksh main maujuud vividh prakaar kii chizon kii khoj or adhyayan kaa kaary karte hai. pashchimi samskruti main galileo naamak khagol vigyaani ko aadhunik khagolashastra kaa pita mana jaataa he. haalaanki kuch log yah naam copernicus ko dete hai.
