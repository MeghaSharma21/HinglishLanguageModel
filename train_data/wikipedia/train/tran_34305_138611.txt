 islamic sahitya islam ke drushtikon se athwa islamic pariprekshyon ke bare main likhaa gaya sahitya he. yah kisi bhi bhashaa main likhaa gaya ho saktaa he. suruaati adhyayanon main arabi or farsi main islam ke bare main likhaa gaya sahitya hi islamic sahitya mana jaataa tha, baad main ise vistrut avadharana ke rup main dekha gaya or gaiyr-muslim lekhakon dwaara likhaa gaya sahitya bhi isamen shaamil kiya jaataa he. iske liye ek shabd "adab" bhi he, haalaanki ab is shabd kaa prayog "sahitya" ke liye hone laga he. haal main islamic sahitya ke upanyasikaran par bhi adhyayan hue hai. or rashtrawaad jaisi raajnneetik avadharanaon ke saath sangatta ke binduon par bhi adhyayan hua he.
