 thanjore balasaraswati ek bhartiya nartak he jo bharatnatyam, shastreey nrity ke liye jaani jaati he. 1957 main unhen padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya or 1977 main padam vibhushan, bharat sarkaar dwaara die gaye tisare or duusare ucchtam naagarik sammaan se sammanit kiya. 1981 main unhen bhartiya fine arts socity, chennai ke sangeeta kalasikhamani puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
 balasaraswati kaa janm 13 may 1918 madras presidency, british bharat hua tha. unke purvaj pammmal ek sangeetkaar or nartak the. unki daadi, vina dhanmal beesvin shatabdi kii ek sabase prabhaavshaalee sangeetkaar maani jaati he. unki maa, jayammal ek gayak thim. unhone 1934 main kalakatta main pehali baar dakshin bharat ke baahar apni paramparaagat style kaa pehla pradarshan karne vaali pehali mahila thi.
 unhone bharat main kai naatak puraskaar praapt kiye, jisamen sangeet naatak academy se raashtrapati kaa puraskaar shaamil tha. pratishthit rashtriya seva ke liye bharat sarkaar se padmavibhushan, madras sangeet academy se sangeeta kalanidhi, sangeetkaaron ke liye dakshin bharat kaa sarvocch puraskaar .
