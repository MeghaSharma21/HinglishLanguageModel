 saanchaa:Infobox Finnish municipality
 helsinki finland kii rajdhani or sabase bada shahar he. ise nilnagar yaa blue city bhi kaha jaataa he.
 sant nicholas carch yahan kaa sabase bada girijaghar he. is girijaghar ke bare main kaha jaataa he ki yahan aap jo bhi maangenge aapaki muraad jarur puri hogi. agar aap sansar kii sabase mahangi shaadi karnaa chaahate hai to chale aaen is girijaghar main 750,000 euro lekar. asal main yahi wo girijaghar he jahaan sabase mahangi yaadgaar shaadi hoti he.
 coppatari bazaar machhliyon kaa sabase bada bazaar he. yahan har tarah kii machhliyaan milti he. samasya yah he ki yahan kaa har bazaar bahut manhagaa he, agar aap kharidari karnaa chaahate he to apni jeb bharkar hi vahaan jaaen.
 yahan par ek bahut bada chidyaghar he. yah ek dweep par sthit he, isliye yahan aane ke liye aapako naav se janaa padegaa. yahan aane kaa anubhav bahut mjedaar he. yahan aapako duniya bhar ke kai pakshiyon ke darshan ho jaayenge. yahan jaanwaron ke rahane ke liye itane sundar ghar bane he ki yakeen hi nahin hota ki yahan jaanavar rahete hai.
 lina maiki park yahan kaa sabase bada udyaan he. yah bahut hi sundar he. yah udyaan dekhte hue aap samudr ke bare main, isame rahane vaale praaniyon ke bare main bahut hi gaharaai se samaj paayenge. yaheen aap shark, dolphin, whale aadi machhliyon ko dekh paayenge.
