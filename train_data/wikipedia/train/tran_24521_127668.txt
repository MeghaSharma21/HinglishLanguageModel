 nanga parvat duniya kii nauvi oonchi chotii he. is kii oonchaai 8,125 meter yaa 26,658 foot he. ise duniya kaa "qaatil pahaad" bhi kaha jate he kyonki isapar chadhane vaale bahut se logon kii jane ja chuki hai. beesvi sadi ke pehle hisse main aath hazaar meter se oonche pahaado main is ek pahaad par sab se zyada mautein hui hai. nanga parbat pakistan dwaara niyantrit gilgit-blatistan ke kshetra main aata he, jise bharat apana hissaa bhi maanata he.
 nanga parbat himalay parvat shrunkhlaa ke sudur pashchimi bhaag main sthit he or aath hazaar meter se oonche pahaado main se sab se pashchimi he. yah sindhu nadi se zaraa dakshin main or astor ghati kii pashchimi seemaa par khadaa hua he.
 nanga parbat ke do bade mukh hai jinpar chadhane vaale is parvat par chadhane kii raah dhundte hai. dakshin main iskaa "rupal mukh" he jo kii pahaad ke charnon se 4,600 meter kii sidhi diwaar he. uttar main iskaa "rakhiot mukh" he jo sindhu nadi kii ghati se 7,000 meter kii chadhaai he. rakhiot mukh kii chadhaai rupal mukh se vaise zyaada to he lekin uski dhalaan rupal kii diwaar se zyaada aasaan he. rupal mukh ko kuch log vishwa kii sab se oonchi pahari diwaar kehete hai. nanga parbat ke pashchim main ek teesraa mukh he jise "diamir mukh" kaha jaataa he.
