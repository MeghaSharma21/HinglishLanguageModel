 tigava madhya pradesh ke katni jile kaa ek gaanv he jahaan ek pracheen puratathvik sthal he jisamen 36 hindu mandiron ke bhagnavshesh vidyaman hai. inamen se pracheen kankali devi mandir abhi bhi achchi dasha main he. yah mandir lagbhag 400-425 i kaa he.
 is gaanv ko 'tigavaan' bhi kehete hai. yah gaanv katni or jabalpur ke bich main bahuriband se 4 kimi kii duuri par he.
