 shravan yaa altere, jiskaa bair naam "alfaa akvilla" he, garud taramandal kaa sab se roshan tara he. yah prithvi se dikhne vaale sab se roshan taron main se barahawaan sab se roshan tara he. yah ek A shrenee kaa mukhya anukram tara he. shravan bahut tezi se ghurnan karata he - isaki madhya rekha par iske ghurnan kii raftaar 286 kilometre prati sekkind he, jis vajah se iskaa gol akar dhruvon par pichak gaya he. shravan prithvi se lagbhag 16.8 prakaash varsh kii duuri par hai.
 shravan ko angrezi main "altere" kehete hai. yah naam arabi bhashaa ke "النسر الطائر‎" vaakyansh se liya gaya he jiskaa matlab he "udati hui chil".
 shravan kaa vyas suraj ke vyas kaa 1.63 - 2.03 guna he or iskaa dravyaman saura dravyaman kaa 1.79 guna he. isaki chamak suraj se 10.6 guna he.
