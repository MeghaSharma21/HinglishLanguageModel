 tripura adivasee kshetra swashaasi jila parishad, tripura rajya ke tripuri bahul ilaakon ko prashashit karne vaala swashasit nikaay he. isaki sabha va parishad, agartala se 26 kimi dur khumulung namak nagar main he.
 sansad dwaara, tripura adivasee kshetra swashaasi jila parishad adhinium ko, sthaneey tripuri janajatiyon dwaara kiye gaye anekon, loktantrik shashan sthapit karne kii maang ke liye andolan kiye jane ke baad, varsh 1979 main samvidhan kii 6thi anusuchee main die gaye praavdhaanon ke antargat paarit kiya gaya tha. ek swashasit zila parishad sthapit karne ke peeche mul lakshya sthaneey adivasee janon ko swasan shashan karne hetu shasakt karnaa, yahan ke samaaj ke pichde logon kaa chautarafa vikaas, tathaa sthaneey, sabhyataa, samskruti va paramparaon kaa sangrakshan tha. baharhaal, 1979 main paarit adhinium ke antargat, is parishad kii sthapana 18 janvari 1982 main hua, tathaa, tatpashchaat, 6thi anusuchee ke praavdhaanon ke anusaar, evam samvidhan ke 49wein sanshodhan dwaara, 1 april 1985 ko iskaa unnayan kiya gaya.
 titedisi kaa kul kshetrafal 7,132.56 varg-kimi he. joki tripura rajya ke kul kshetrafal kaa lagbhag 68% he.
 zila parishad ke antargat lagbhag 70% bhoomi, pahari wanon se dhanka he. jabaki sabhi sada kheti yogya bhoomi titedisi ke daayre se baahar hai.
 rajya kii 36,73,917 kii kul aabaadi main anusuchit janajati kii kul jansankhya 8,53,920 he. inamen se 6,79,720 jansankhya titedisi kshetra ke bheetar he.
 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar tripura kii kul aabaadi 31,91,168 thi.
 titedisi ko ek parishad dwaara sanchalit kiya jaataa he jisamen 30 sadsy hai. 30 sadasyon main se, 28 sadasyon ko vayask matadhikaar ke jariye chunaa jaataa he jabaki tripura ke rajyapal dwaara 2 sadasyon ko namit kiya jaataa he. 28 nirvaachit siton main se 25 anusuchit janajatiyon ke liye arakshit hai.
 titedisi ke paas ek niyamit prashasnic sanrachana he. kaaryakari samiti 9+1=10 sadasyon se bana he or mukhya kaaryakari adhikari prashasan kaa pramukh he.
