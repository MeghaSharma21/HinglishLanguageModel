 kathakaar, patrkaar, naatakkaar or kavi euri palikof kaa janm 1954 main masco main hua. 1976 main unhone masco pradesh adhyaapan santhaan se em.e. kiya or phir vahiin se piech.di. kii. iske baad ve ek skool main adhyaapan karne lage. baad main 'mascovski litaraatar' naamak saahitiyik patr main sampadak rahe. ab san 2001 se euri palikopha 'litratuuranaya gajyeta' naamak ek bade saptahik ke sampadak hai.
 euri palikof kii rachanaaon kaa pehla prakaashan 1974 main 'mascovski kamasamolits' samachar patr main hua. tab se ve is patr main lagaataar likhte rahe hai. 1980 main palikof kii pehali kitaab prakashit hui jo ek kavita-sangrah tha or jiskaa sheershak tha - 'bramya pribitya' . lekin euri palikof kathakaar ke rup main charchit hue. 1985 main 'unst' patrika main 'kshetreey str kii apattakaaleen sthiti' naamak lambii kahani chhapane ke baad rus main unki charchaa hone lagi. 'unst' or 'maskwa' naamak saahitiyik patrikoon main inaki rachanaayen lagaataar prakashit hoti hai.
 inaki prasiddh gadhy rachanaayen hai- 'aadesh se pehle sau din', 'apafigey', 'kostya gumnakof kaa paris', 'demgaradok', 'gire huon kaa aasmaan', 'maine bhaagane kii sochi' or 'afrodita kaa bayen stan' aadi. euri palikof ke lekhon ke sangrahon ke naam hai- 'jhooth ke samrajya se jhooth ke lokarajya tak' or 'pornokrasi'. inaki rachanaaon kaa anuwaad vishwa kii tamaam bhashaaon main hua he. inaki rachanaaon ke aadhaar par anek naatakon kaa manchan or filmon kaa nirmaan ho chukaa he.
 euri palikof antarrashtriya sahitya kosh ke adhyaksh hai. ve roosi pen centre ke sadsy hai or dastoivski kosh kii kaaryakari parishad ke sadsy bhi hai. 1990 se 2001 tak ve roosi lekhak sangh ke sanchaalan mandal ke sadsy rahe. ve swatantra lekhak sangh ke sansthaapak sadsy hai. euri palikopha rus ke raashtrapati dwaara sanchalit samskruti kala parishad or naagarik samaaj va maanavadhikaar sansthaanon ke vikaas main sahyog dene vaali parishad ke sadsy hai. unhen anek saahitiyik puraskaar bhi mil chuke hai.
