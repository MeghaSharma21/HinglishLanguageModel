 mashin gan ek aisi bandook he jo kii swachaalit rup se ek ke baad kai golia kai so prati minute kii raftaar se ek saath daag sakati he. inhen mul rup se sab mashin gan bhi kaha jaataa he. yah yaa to kisi stand ke uppar lagaake ke va us ke sahyog se prayog main lee jaati he yaa inke halake prakaar sidhe haath main lekar prayog main liye jate he. iske lagaataar goli chalaane ke do tareeke he. kuch mashin gan sidhe piston kaa prayog karti he va aajkal zyaadaatar gas se swachaalit piston kaa.
 pratham va dwitiya vishwa yuddh main apni barbar maarane kii shakti kii vajah se puure vishwa kii senaaon main yah kaaphi prachilit hui. kai vaigyanik ise pichhele 100 saalo main hue sabase mahatvapurn avishkaaro main se ek maanate he.
