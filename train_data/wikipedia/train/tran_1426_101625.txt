 ibb​ praant yaman kaa ek praant he.
 yaman kii rajdhani sana ke baad yah ibb desh kaa doosraa sabase ghani aabaadi vaala kshetra he. isaki rajdhani bhi ibb naamak shahar he jo yemeni pahari kshetra ke purvi ant par sthit he jahaan achaanak zamin kii oonchaai tihamah tatavarti kshetra or taise​ shahar kii taraf girti he. is praant main kaafii varsha girane se dharti bahut upjau he or krishi zoron se chalati he. yahan genhu, jau, til or jowar ke alaava 'kaat' kii paidaavaar bhi hoti he. kaat ek paudha he jiske pattey chabane se halkaa nasha chadhataa he or yah puure dakshini arabi prayadweep main chabaaya jaataa he.
 ibb praant ke log badi taadaad main duusare deshon main kaam karne jate hai or lakhon kii sankhya main saudi arab or america main vyavsaayon main lage hue hai.
