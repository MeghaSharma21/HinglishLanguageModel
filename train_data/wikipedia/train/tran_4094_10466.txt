 parkaar yaa sarsinas khagoleey gole ke dakshini bhaag main sthit ek chota-sa taramandal he. isaki paribhaasha 18ve sadi main kii gai thi. antarrashtriya khagoleey sangh dwaara jari kii gai 88 taramandalon kii suchi main bhi yah shaamil he.
 latini bhashaa main "sarsinas" kaa arth "compass" hota he, yani vah yantra jis se kaagaz par vrutt banaae jate hai or jise hindi main "parkaar" bulaayaa jaataa he.
 parkaar taramandal main 9 taaren hai jinhen bair naam die ja chuke hai, jinamein se agast 2011 tak ek ke ird-gird gair-saureey grah parikrama karata hua paaya gaya tha. is taramandal ke sab se roshan taare, alfaa sarsinaaye or betaa sarsinaaye, donon dwitare hai.
