 us padaarth yaa yogic ko abhikarmak kehete hai jo kisi tantra main rasinic abhikriya utpann karne ke liye daalaa yaa milaayaa jaataa he. us padaarth ko bhi abhikarmak kahenge jise yah jaanchane ke liye milaayaa jaataa he ki koi abhikriya hoti he yaa nahin. is tarah ke kuch vaishleshik abhikarmak hai - fehling kaa abhikarmak, milon kaa abhikarmak tathaa tolin kaa abhikarmak .
