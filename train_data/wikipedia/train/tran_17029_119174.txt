 samudri toofaan gustav 2008 ke atlantic samudri tufaani mausam kaa saatvaan ushnkaribandheey chakravaat, teesraa samudri toofaan tathaa doosraa bada samudri tufaan he. iskaa nirmaan 25 agast 2008 kii subar port o prince, hiti se hua tha par dopaher hote hote yah ushnkaribandheey chakravaat or 26 kii subah tak yah samudri toofaan kaa rup le chukaa tha. usee din hiti nagar jackmel main isse prabhavit hokar bhuskhalan hua or 31 agast tak carabian main isse marane waalon kii sankhya 88 ho gai. 1 sitambar kii subah 9:30 baje CDT gustav kaa kendra sanyukt rajya america ke aasapaas tha. ise duusari shrenee ke samudri toofaan kaa darja diya gaya he.
