 saiyad sibte razi bhartiya rashtriya congress party ke ek rajanyik hai. ve rajya sabha ke teen baar sadsy rahe. baad main unhen jharkhand or assam kaa rajyapal bhi banaaya gaya.
 sibte razi kaa janm uttar pradesh ke janapad raibareli main 7 march 1939 ko hua. unki maan kaa naam razia begum or baap kaa naam saiyad viraasat hossain tha. unhone husainabad hair secondari skool se dasvin karne ke baad ek shiya college main pravesh liya. vahaan ve chaatr netaa ban gayi. padhai ke saath-saath jebkharch nikaalane ke liye ve do-do hotalon ke accounts bhi dekhte the. baad main unhone lakhanu vishvavidhyalay se bi0com0 kiya.
 sibte razi ne 1969 main you0pi0 kii youth congress jwine kar lee or 1971 main ve iske adhyaksh bana diye gayi. 1973 tak ve youth congress ke adhyaksh rahe. 1980 se 1985 tak rajya sabha sadsy ke atirikt 1980 se 1984 tak uttar pradesh congress cameti ke mahasachiv bhi rahe. unhen congress party dwaara duusari baar 1988 se 1992 tak tathaa teesri baar 1992 se 1998 tak rajya sabha kaa sadsy banaaya gaya. 
 march 2005 main, jab ve jharkhand ke rajyapal the, unhone sarkaar main endie ke sadasyon kii sankhya ko nazar andaaz karte hue jharkhand mukti morcha ke shibu sorain ko sarkaar banane kaa nyotaa dekar vivaadaaspad bhoomika nibhaai. isaki shikaayat milte hi tatkaaleen raashtrapati e.pi.je. abdul kalam ne hastkshep kiya or sibte razi ke nirnay ko ulatate hue endie ke arjuna munda ko 13 march 2005 ko unheen rajyapal dwaara mukhyamantri pad kii shapath dilaayi.
