 sant nicholas shatabdiyon se madhya purv tathaa samast europe main atyant lokapriy sant the, jo bacchon, kumariyon, mallahon tathaa bahut se nagaron ke sangrakshak maane jate hai. 
 sant nicholas kaa parv 6 disambar ko padataa he. germany, switzraland, holland aadi main us parv main bacchon ko uphaar milte hai or unhen vishwaas dilaayaa jaataa he ki sant nicholas ye uphaar dene aate hai. america tathaa englad main usee rivaaj ko chrismas ke avasar par rakhaa gaya he. vahaan kaa santa clos athwa fadar chrismas mulat: sant nicholas hi he. unke vishay main prachlit adhikansh dantakthaon kaa koi aitihaasik aadhaar nahin he. itnaa hi gyaat hota he ki vah chaturth shatabdi i. main asia miner ke mira naamak nagar ke vishap the jo paroapkaar ke kaaryon ke kaaran vikhyath the. unka avshesh 11veen shatabdi main italy ke baari naamak nagar main laayaa gaya tha jo vahaan ke sant nicholas naamak mahamandir main surakshit he.
