 hoshang shah  malwa kshetra kaa aupchaarik rup se niyukt pratham islamic raja tha. ise hushang shah gori ke naam se bhi prasiddh is raja kaa malwa kaa raja ghoshit hone se purv alp khan naam prachlit tha. alp khan ke pita dilawar khan dilli ke sultan firoz shah tughlaq ke darbar se sambhddha rahaa tha. dilawar khan ko tughlqon main se ek, firuz dwaara malwa kaa rajyapal niyukt kiya gaya tha. 1401 main usne swayan ko dilli sultanate se mukt swytt raja ghoshit kar liya tha. atah vah mandu main 1401 main malwa ke pratham raja ke rup main aayaa tha. kintu usne swayan ko vahaan kaa raja ghoshit nahin kiya tha.
