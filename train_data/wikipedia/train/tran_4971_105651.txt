 suryaprajapti sutara jain dharm kaa ek granth he. shwetambar jain sampradaay isaki shikshaaon kaa anusaran karata he. isko surya pannati bhi kehete hai. vastutah yah upang aagam he.
 yah granth surya evam grahon ke bare main he. isamen in sabakaa vrihad varnan he. surya evam grahon kii gati evam sthiti kii gananaa ke liye aavashyak ganit kaa isamen vistaar se varnan he.
