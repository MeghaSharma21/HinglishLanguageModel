 british sangrahalay, jo britain kii rajdhani landan main sthit he, duniya ke sab se mahaan maanviya itihaas or sabhyataa ke sangrahalayon main se ek mana jaataa he. iske sthai sangrah main 80 lakh se adhik vastuen hai jo har mahadweep se laai gai hai or manushya jati kii shuruyaat se ajtak kii samskruti kii jhalken dikhaatin hai. is sangrahalay kii sthapana san 1753 main hens slone ke vyaktigat sangrah ke saath hui thi. 15 janvari 1759 ko iske darvaaze aam janata ke liye khule or agali dhai sadiyon main vishvabhar main british samrajya ke failaav ke saath-saath yahan dilachasp vastuon kaa ekatrikaran zoron par rahaa. is sangrahalay main kuch vastuon par vivaad he, jaise ki elgin sangemarmar kii shilpawastuen jinhen yunaan vapaas maangata rahaa he.
