 ek gan diod, jise transfard electron divaais ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he, ucch aavriti electronics main upyog kiye gaye nakaaratmak pratirodh ke saath diod kaa ek rup he, do terminal niskriy ardhchaalak electronic ghatak. yah bhautikavid je bi. gun dwaara 1 9 62 main khoja gaya "gan prabhaav" par aadhaarit he. iskaa sabase bada upyog electronic osillator main he, microwave utpann karne ke liye, jaise ki radar speed bandooken, microwave riley data link transmeter, or swachaalit darwaja kholane vaale.
 iskaa aantarik nirmaan anya diodon ke vipareet he, isamen keval en-dipid ardhchaalak padaarth hote hai, jabaki zyaadaatar diod main donon pi or en-dopad kshetra shaamil hote hai. isliye yah keval ek disha main nahin aati he or anya diod kii tarah vartmaan main sudhaar nahin kar sakati he, yahi vajah he ki kuch strot diod shabd kaa prayog nahin karte hai, lekin ted pasand karte hai. gan diod main, teen kshetra maujuud hai: unamem se do prayek terminal par baari en-dip kiye gaye hai, jisamen halke dhang se en-dided saamagree kii ek patali parat hoti he. jab ek voltage ko divaais par lagu kiya jaataa he, to patali madhyam parat main vidyut dhaal sabase bada hoga. yadi voltage bad jaataa he, to parat ke madhyam se vartmaan main pehali vruddhi hogi, lekin ant main, ucch kshetreey mulyon par, madhya parat ke pravaahkeey gun badal jate hai, isaki pratirohakata badhati ja rahi he, or vartmaan main giravat aati he. iskaa matlab he ki ek gan diod main isaki vartmaan-voltage visheshta vakr main nakaaratmak antar pratirodh kaa kshetra he, jisamen lagu voltage kii vruddhi vartmaan main kami kaa kaaran banati he. yah property ise radio aavriti emplifaire ke rup main kaary karne, asthir or asthir hone or disi voltage ke saath purvagrahit hone par ise badhanaa detii he.
