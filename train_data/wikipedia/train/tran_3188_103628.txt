 uttari samaar dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh ke visaaya dweep dal ke purvi visaaya prashasnic kshetra main ek praant he. yah samaar dweep ke uttari bhaag par vistrut he or dweep ke do anya praant - samaar va purvi samaar - is se dakshin main sthit hai. uttar main saan barnaradino jalsandhi ke paar sorasogon praant he. purv main philipino sagar tathaa pashchim main samaar sagar he.
