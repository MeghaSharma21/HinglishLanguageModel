 gul bardhan  bhopal, madhya pradesh, bharat sthit koreographer or theatre vyaktitv thi. vah bhartiya pipuls theatre association ke saath judy hui thi. vah little ballat mandali kii sah-sansthaapak bhi thi. little ballat mandali 1952 main bombai main gathit ek nrity company thi. 1954 main apane pati kii mrityu ke baad, gul bardhan little ballat mandali company kii adhyakshata kii. baad main ise "rang shri little ballat mandali" kaa naam diya gaya or jisane vibhinn deshon main apana pradarshan kiya. 
 unhone 2010 main sangeet naatak academy puraskaar or padam shri sahit kai puraskaar praapt kiye.
