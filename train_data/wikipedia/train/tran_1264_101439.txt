 mouno pul  great britain main abhi tak bachaa ekmaatr madhyayugin gadhwala nadi kaa pul he. pul ke get tower abhi bhi apni jagah khadey hai. pul monmouth, wales, main mouno nadi pe bana hua he or yahan se mouno or vei nadi kaa sangam kul 500 meter dur he. nivartmaan samay main yah keval paidal yatriyon ke liye he va united kingdom ke anusuchit pracheen smarak ke saath-saath grad pratham soochibaddh sthal bhi he.
 maujuuda pul terahavin sadi ke ant main ban kar hua tha, paaramparik rup se 1272 main, haalanki is tithi kaa samarthan karne ke liye koi dastaveji sakshya uplabdh nahin he. is pul ne apane se pehle kii bani lakdi kii sanrachana ko pratisthapit kiya tha. 
 pathar kaa pul puraane laal balua pattharon se bana he jisamen teen mehrab he. pul kaa get-house, jise mouno get-house kha jaataa he, jo mouno ko apni vishisht or ullekhneey dikhaawat detaa he, terahavin sadi ke ant main yaa chaudaavin sadi ke shuruyaat main joda gaya tha, pul banane ke kuch varsh pashchaat.
