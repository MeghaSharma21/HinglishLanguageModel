 pratapaditya yeshor  ke raja tathaa bhuswami the. unhone mughalon ke viruddh yuddh kiya. apane charmotkarsh ke samay unka rajya vartmaan pashchim bengal ke nadiya, uttar choubees pargana or dakshin choubees pargana jilon sahit vartmaan bangladesh main uttar main kushtiya jile se lekar purv main badisal, sundarban tathaa dakshin main bengal kii khadi tak failaa hua tha.
