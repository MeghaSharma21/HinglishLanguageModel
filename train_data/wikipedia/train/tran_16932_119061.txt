 dow yashavanth singh parmaar bharat ke rajaneta evam swatantraaa-sangram senaanee the. vah himachal pradesh ke pratham mukhyamantri the. unka himachal pradesh ko astitv main laane or vikaas kii aadharshila rakhane main mahatvapurn yogadan rahaa he. lagbhag 3 dashakon tak kushal prashaasak ke rup main jan-jan kii bhaawanaaon samvednaaon ko samajhate hue unhone pragati path par agrasar hote hue himachal pradesh ke vikaas ke liye nai dishaaen prastut kii. unka sara jivan pradesh kii janata ke liye samarpit rahaa ve umr bhar garibon or zaruratmandon kii sahaayta karte rahe. 
 sirmaur jila ke chanalag gaanv main 4 agast 1906 ko janme dow parmaar kaa jivan sangharkshil vyakti kaa jivan rahaa. unhone 1928 main bi0e0 anars kiya; lakhanu se em0e0 or el0el0bi0 tathaa 1944 main samajashaastra main pi ech di kii. 1929-30 main ve thiosophical sosayadi ke sadsy rahe. unhone sirmaur riyasat main 11 varshon tak sab jaj or magistret ke baad jila or satr nyaayadhish ke rup main apni sevaaye di.
 ve naukari kii parvaah naa karte hue suket satyagrah prajamandal se jude! unke hi prayaason se yah satyagrah safal hua. 1943 se 46 tak ve sirmaur associyeshan ke sachiv, 1946 se 47 tak himachal hill state kanusil ke pradhan, 1947 se 48 tak sadsy aal india pipulas confres tathaa pradhan prajamandal sirmaur sanchaalak suket aandolan se jude rahe. dow parmaar ke prayaason se hi 15 april 1948 ko 30 siyaasaton ke vilay ke baad himachal pradesh ban paaya or 25 janvari 1971 ko is pradesh ko purna rajya kaa darja mila.
 1948 se 52 sadsy sachiv himachal pradesh chif adwajri council, 1948 se 64 adhyaksh himachal congress cameti, 1952 se 56 tak himachal pradesh ke mukhyamantri ; 1957 saansad bane or 1963 se 24 janvari 1977 tak himachal ke mukhyamantri pad par kaary karte rahe kiya! dow parmaar ne paaliondary in da himalayaj, himachal paaliondary itas shape and states, himachal pradesh cese for statehud or himachal pradesh aria and laguegij naamak shodh aadhaarit pustake bhi likhi. dow parmaar 2 may 1981 ko swarg sidhaar gaye.
