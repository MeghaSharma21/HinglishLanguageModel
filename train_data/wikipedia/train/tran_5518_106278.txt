 chanhudado sindhu ghati sabhyataa ke nagareey jhukar charan se sambandhit ek puraatatv sthal he. yah kshetra pakistan ke sindh praant ke mohenjodaro se 130 kilometre dakshin main sthit he. yahan par 4000 se 1700 se isha purv main basaa hua mana jaataa he or is sthaan ko indragop manakon ke nirmaan sthal ke rup main janaa jaataa he.
 chanhudado kii pehali baar khudaai march 1930 main enji majumdaar ne karwaai or uske baad 1935-36 main america skool of indic and iranian tathaa musium of fine arts, boston ke dal ne arnest jon henry maike ke netritv main karwaai.
