 is pyaar ko kya naam duun? star plus kaa ek ati lokapriy kaarykram tha jo 06 june 2011 se 30 navambar 2012 tak prasaarit hua tha. kaarykram kii lokapriyata ko dekhte hue kaarykram kaa dwitiya satr is pyaar ko kya naam duun?-ek baar phir nae paatro evam kahani ke saath star plus par prasaarit kiya gaya.
 yah kahani arnav or khushi kii ek prem kahani he, jisamen khushi lakhanu main rahane vaali ek ladki he jo pyaar, rishte, or vishwaas kii shakti main vishwaas rakhti he. arnav ek vyavsaayee rahata he. vah ek duusare se ko bilkul pasand nahin karte rahete hai, lekin baad main un donon ko majaburi main shaadi kerani padati he. baad main jab un logon ko lagata he ki vah donon ek duusare se pyaar karte hai tab bhi vah is baat ko sweekaar nahin karte or ant main jaakar vah is baat ko maan lete hai.
