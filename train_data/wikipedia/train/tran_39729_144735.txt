 electronic main dvimanitr ek ankiy paripath he jiskaa nirgam do sthai avasthaaon main main se kisi ek main bana rahata he main kuch na kiya jaay). ise 'ltch' bhi kehete hai. is paripath ek yaa adhik nivesh hote hai jin par sanket kaa uchit parivartan karke nirgam ke avasthaa ko badla ja saktaa he. iske ek ek yaa do nirgam hote hai. 
 dvimanitr kai prakaar ke hote hai or aankik electronic kii moolbhoot nirmaan-ikai hai. ye aankada bhandaaran ke avyav taiyaar karne ke liye prayukt hote hai. ye 'anukramik tark' kii shrenee main aate hai. inkaa upyog spandon ko ginaney ke liye tathaa alag-alag samay par pahunchane vaale nivesh sanketon ko samakalik banane ke liye kiya jaataa he.
 pratham dvimanitr paripath kaa avishkaar san 1918 main hua tha jo nirvaat nalee kaa upyog karke banaaya gaya tha. us samay uskaa naam ekles-jordan trigar paripath tha. baad main transistor se dvimanitr bane or aajkal ekikrut paripath ke rup main uplabdh hai.
