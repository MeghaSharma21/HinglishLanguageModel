 oman arabi prayadweep ke purv-dakshin main sthit ek desh he jise aadhikarik rup se sultanate uman naam se jaanate hai. yah saudi arab ke purv or dakshin kii disha main arab sagar kii seemaa se laga he. sanyukt arab emirate iske uttar main sthit he.
 oman kii kul jansankhya 25 lakh ke aasapaas he or yahan baahar se aakar rahane waalon kii sankhya kaafii he. lagbhag puri jansankhya muslim he jisamen ibaadiyon kii sankhya sabase adhik he. iske america or britain ke saath gahare kootnithik sambandh hai.
 sumeri sabhyataa ke ek lekh ke anusaar ise magan naam se janaa jaataa tha. oman naam er arabi jati par padaa jo yaman ke uman kshetra se aae the. isapurv chhathi sadi se lekar saatvin sadi ke madhya tak yahan iran ke teen vanshon kaa shashan rahaa - hakhaamani, parthian or saasaani. saatvin sadi main mohammad sahab ke jeevankaal main hi oman main islam kaa aagaman ho gaya tha. san 1508-1648 tak yahan par portugaliyon ke upnivesh the jo vasco da gamma dwaara bharat kii khoj kiye jane ke baad samudri raaston par niyantran ke liye banaae gaye the. portugal par spain ke adhikaar ho jane ke baad portugaliyon ko waapas janaa padaa. iske baad omanion ne purvi africa tateey pradeshon se bhi portugaliyon ko maar bhagaaya.
 oman main 5 pradesh or 4 shaskeey prakhand hai - 
  bharat · iran · maldive · oman · pakistan · somalia · yaman
