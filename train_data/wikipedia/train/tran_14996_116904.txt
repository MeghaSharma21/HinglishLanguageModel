 durga mandir kaashi ke puraatan mandir mki ski ek he. is mandir kaa ullikh " kaashi khand" mki bhi miltaa he. yah mandir varanasi cant se lagbhag 5 kimi kii duuri par he. laal pattharon se bane ati bhavy is mandir ke ek taraf "durga kunda" he. is kunda ko baad main nagar palika ne phuhaare main badal diya, jo apni mul sundarata ko kho chukaa he. is mandir main maan durga "yantra" rup main virajmaan he. is mandir main baba bhaironath, lakshiji, saraswatiji, evam mata kaali kii muti rup main alag se mandir he. yahan mangalik kaay evam mundan ityadi main maan ke dashan kly liye aati he. mandir ke andar havan kunda he, jahaan roj havan hote hai. kuch log yahan tantra puja bhi karte hai. sawan mahine main ek maah kaa bahut manamohak mela lagata he.durga mandir ke najadik anand park he, jahaan par arya samaaj kaa pratham sastrarth kaashi ke vidwaanon ke saath hua tha. 
 kaha jaataa he kii is mandir kaa nirmaan 18 ve shatabdi main bengal kii rani bhavani ne karavaaya tha kaha jaataa he kii yah 20 sa yantra sthapit he yah par 12 paae vishaal mandap bana hua he. dekha jaay to kai vajah se yah mandir prasiddh he
