 suruud-e-milli tajikistan kaa raashtragan he. iske bol gulnazar kaldigav ne likhe the or iskaa sangeet suleman yudacov ne banaaya tha. tajikistan kabhi soviet sangh kaa hissaa hua karata tha or jo us samay tajikistan kaa raashtragan tha usee kaa sangeet nae raashtragan main bhi istemaal kiya gaya.
 tajiki bhashaa farsi kii ek upbhasha he or uske bahut se shabd hindibhashiyon ko samaj aa sakate hai. is maamle main kuch samajhane le liye sahaayak tipanni neeche di gayi he.
 tajikistan ke raashtragan ke shabdon main kai tatv hai jo bina anuwaad kiye bhi hindibhashiyon dwaara samajhe ja sakate hai, jis se ve sidhe mul tajiki bhashaa main hi is raashtragan ko anubhav kar paane main sakasham hai -
