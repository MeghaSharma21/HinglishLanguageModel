 jal surveykshan yaa hydrographic survey sagar kaa vaigyanik maanchitr hota he, jisamen sagar kii gaharaai, uski akruti, uskaa tal, usamen kis disha se or kitni dharaaen bahati hai, kaa gyaan hota he. iske saath hi usamen aane vaale jwaron kaa samay or pariman bhi pataa lagaaya jaataa he. jhiilon or nadiyon kaa jal surveykshan keval us sthiti main kiya jaataa he jab unamem jahaaj chalate hon. isse abhiyantriki or nauvahan ke kaam main sahaayta milti he. jal surveykshan main pani kii vartmaan maatr or rigervyr ke ke bare main jaankaree milti he. 
 jal surveykshan sagar kii tah main hone vaale dainik parivartanon ke or iske andar ke rahasyon ke gyaan hetu kiya jaataa he. is surveykshan kii sahaayta se samudr main maujuud khanijon, dhaatuon, gas aadi ke bhandar pataa lagane main madad milti he. saath-saath hi samudr ke bheetar cabel, pipeline bichaane, dreging jaise kaaryo ke liye usamen lagaataar hone vaale parivartanon ke bare main jaankaree karnaa behad aavashyak ho jaataa he. bharat main pehali baar nausainik jal surveykshan vibhaag, jo marine survey of india ke niyantran main tha, kii sthapana 1770 main east india company ne kii thi. 1874 main captain dunds tailor ne mereen survey of india ko kolkata main sthapit kiya. 1947 main bharat kii swatantraaa uparant is vibhaag ko mereen survey of india ke antargatt kar diya gaya. iske baad ise 1954 main dehradun main sthapit kiya gaya or iskaa naam nausainik jal surveykshan karyaalay kar diya gaya. iske baad main 1996 main iskaa naam neshnal hydrographic offis kar diya gaya. neshnal hydrographic survey march 1999 main iesao 9002 kaa str pradaan kiya gaya.
