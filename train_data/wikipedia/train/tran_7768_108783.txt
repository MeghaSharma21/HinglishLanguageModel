 24 disambar 1906 kii shaam canadai vaigyanik reginald fecenden ne jab apana voyalin bajaayaa or atlantic mahaasaagar main tair rahe tamaam jahaajon ke radio operataron ne us sangeet ko apane radio set par suna, vah duniya main radio prasaaran kii shuruyaat thi. 
 isse pehle jagadish chandra basu ne bharat main tathaa gulyelmo marconi ne san 1900 main england se america betaar sandesh bhejkar vyaktigat radio sandesh bhejne kii shuruyaat kar di thi, par ek se adhik vyaktiyon ko ek saath sandesh bhejne yaa broadcasting kii shuruyaat 1906 main fecenden ke saath hui. lee da forest or charles herold jaise logon ne iske baad radio prasaaran ke prayog karne shuru kiye. tab tak radio kaa prayog sirf nausena tak hi simit tha. 1917 main pratham vishwa yuddh kii shuruyaat ke baad kisi bhi gaiyr fauzi ke liye radio kaa prayog nishiddh kar diya gaya.
