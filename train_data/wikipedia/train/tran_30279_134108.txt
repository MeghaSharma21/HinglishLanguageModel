 amhari yaa amharik uttar-madhya ithiopia main amhara dwaara boli jane vaali bhashaa he, jise ithiopia sangheey loktantric ganraajya main aadhikarik kaamkaaji bhashaa kaa darja diya gaya he. arabi ke baad, yah duniya kii duusari sabase zyaada boli jane wali sami bhashaa he. ithiopia ke alaava misr, israel or sweden main basane vaale 27 lakh log ise bolchaal main prayukt karte hai.
