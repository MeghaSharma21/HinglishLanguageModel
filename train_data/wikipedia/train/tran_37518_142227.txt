 ishakajade 2012 kii romantic thriller film he. film kaa lekhan or nirdesan habib faisal dwaara kiya gaya he. aditya chopra ne yash raaj films ke tahat iskaa utpaadan kiya he. navdith sitaarein arjuna kapur or pariniti chopra ne film main mukhya kirdaar adaa kiye hai.
 chouhan or kureshi do raajnneetik parivaar he jiskaa pratidwandvita or aapasi nafrat pidiyon se chal rahi he. parama chouhan parivaar se he or joya kureshi se jab donon main pyaar ho jaataa he to kahani main kai pahalu ujagar hote hai.
