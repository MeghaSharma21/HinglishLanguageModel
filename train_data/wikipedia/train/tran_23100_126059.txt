 
 buddha dharm ke mahayan sampradaay ke anusaar amitabh buddha vartmaan jagat ke abhibhavak tathaa adhishwar buddha kaa naam he. 'amitabh' mukhya buddha hai jinaki mahayan kii japan or china main prachlit 'pavitra bhoomi' shakha main manyata praapt he. inhen tibet main prachlit vajrayan shakha main bhi mana jaataa he. dharmik granthon ke anusaar amitabh buddha apane anginat pichley janmon main 'dharmkar' naamak bodhisattva the jinhonen itane achche kaam kiye kii inke punyon kii poonji aseem he. 'amitabh' shabd 'amit' or 'aabh/abha' ko jodakar bana he or iskaa arth he 'anant prakaash'.
 is sampradaay kaa yah mantavya he ki swayambhu adibuddha kii dhyanshkti kii paanch kriyaaon ke dwaara paanch dhyani budhon kii utpati hoti he. unheen main anyatam dhyani buddha amitabh hai. anya dhyani budhon ke naam hai- berochan, akshobhya, ratnasambhav tathaa amoghsiddhi. adibuddha ke samaan inke bhi mandir nepal main uplabdh hai. baudhon ke anusaar teen jagat to nasht ho chuke hai or aajkal chaturth jagat chal rahaa he. amitabh hi is vartmaan jagat ke vishisht buddha hai jo iske adhipati tathaa vijeta maane gaye hai.
 'amitabh' kaa shabdic arth he 'anant prakaash se sampann dev' . unke dwaara adhishthit swarglok pashchim main mana jaataa he jise 'sukhavati' ke naam se pukaarte hai. us swarg main sukh kii anant satta vidyaman he. us lok ke jeev hindu devon ke samaan saundarya tathaa saukhyapurna hote hai. vahaan pradhanthya bodhisatvon kaa hi nivas he, tathaapi katipay arhaton kii bhi satta vahaan maani jaati he. vahaan ke jeev amitabh ke saamne camel se utpann hote hai. ve bhagavan buddha ke prabhabhasur sharir kaa swat: apane netron se darshan karte hai. sukhavati anshwar lok nahin he, kyonki vahaan ke nivasee jeev agrim janm main buddharup se utpann hote hai. is prakaar amitabh kaa swarg keval bhogabhumi hi nahin he, pratyut vah ek anandadaayak shikshankendra he jahaan jeev apane paapon kaa praayshchit kar apane aapako sadgunsmpann banaata he.
 japan main amitabh japani naam 'amido' se vikhyath hai. purvokt swarg kaa varnanparak sanskrit granth 'sukhavati vyuh' naam se prasiddh he jiske do sanskaran aajkal milte hai. brihat sanskaran ke chini bhashaa main baarah anuwaad milte hai jinamein sabase pracheen anuwaad 147-186 i. ke bich kiya gaya tha. laghu sanskaran kaa anuwaad kumarjiv ne chini bhashaa main paanchavi shatabdi main kiya tha or hwentsang ne saptam shatabdi main. isse is granth kii prakhyati kaa purna parichay miltaa he.
