 suryvansh,arkavansh puraanon ke anusaar ek pracheen bhartiya vansh he jisaki utpatti surya dev se maani gayi he. ye brahman evam rajput hote hen. isi vansh parampara main se jain evam buddha panth kaa prardubhav hua he.
 puraanon, visheshtah vishnu puraana, valmiki rachit ramayana or vyas rachit mahabharat sabhi main is vansh kaa vivaran miltaa he. 
 kalidas ke raghuvansham main bhi is vansh ke kuch naam ullikhit hai.
