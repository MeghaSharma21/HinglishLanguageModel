 majja yaa meru asthiyon ke andar bharaa hua ek mulaayam utak hota he. vayaskon main badi asthiyon main majja rakt koshikaayen nirmaan karne main sahaayak hota he. isamen kul sharir bhaar kaa 4% samahit rahata he, yani lagbhag 2.6 ki.gra, vayaskon main.
 asthimajja goode ke samaan mridu utak he jo sab asthiyon ke spanji bhaag ke avkaashon main, lambii asthion kii madhyanalika kii guha main or bade aakaar kii hewarsi nalikaaon main paaya jaataa he. bhinn-bhinn asthiyon main or anu ke anusaar uske sanghatan main antar hota he. majja do prakaar kii hoti he - pili or laal.
 pili majja kaa aadhaar tautav utak hota he jisamen raktavahikaaye or koshikaaye pai jaati hai jinamein adhikansh vasakoshikaaye hoti hai. kuch laal majja ke samaan koshikaaye milti hai.
 laal majja kaa aadhaar sanyoji utak hota he jiske dhaanche ke jaal main rajataragi tantu or usase sambandhit jeevanubhakshi koshikaaye tathaa kai prakaar kii raktakanikas or unke purvagami rup, kuch vasakoshikaaye tathaa kuch lymph parv hote hai.
