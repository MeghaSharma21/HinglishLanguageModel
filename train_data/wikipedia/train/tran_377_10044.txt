 vigaan dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh ke ilokos kshetra ke ilokos sur praant main sthit ek shahar he. yah us praant kii rajdhani bhi he or luzone dweep ke pashchimi tat par dakshin china sagar ke kinare sthit he. philipinoz main 19veen shatabdi main spain kaa upniveshi raaj tha jo baad main sanyukt rajya america dwaara khaded diya gaya. vigaan philipinoz ke un bahut kam shaharon main se ek he jahaan ab bhi spani vaastukala milti he.
