 mulya chukaaye bina shram karaane kii pratha ko begaar kehete hai. isamen shramikon kii ichha ke bina kaam liya jaataa he. saamanti, samrajyavaadee or afasarshahi praayah samaaj ke kamazor logon se begaar karwaati he. britishkaleen bharat main to yah aam baat thi. kintu swatantra bharat main bhi is tarah kii ghatnaon kaa sarvatha abhaav nahin he. yah pratha angarejon ke khilaaf aam janata ke asantosh kii ek badi vajah thi.
