 mafia italy ke sicili ke apradhi tatv the. inhen 'kosa nostra' bhi kaha jaataa he. aisa mana jaataa he ki unnisvin shatabdi ke uttarardh main main ye sicili main ye khub fal-phool rahe the. vastut: yah apradhi samoohon kaa shithil sangh hota he jinamein samaan santhnik dhaancha evam samaan 'aachaar samhita' hoti he. prayek samooh ko 'parivaar', 'claan' yaa 'koska' ke naam se janaa jaataa he or kisi ek kshetra main ek 'parivaar' kii samprabhutaa rahati he jahaan ye svachhnd hokar apni gatividhiyaan chalaate hai.
