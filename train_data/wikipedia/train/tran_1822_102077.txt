 mahapurana kaa uttarardh 'uttarpurana kehalaata he. yah jinsen ke pattshishy gunabhadracharya kii praudh rachana he. isamen lagbhag 9,500 shloke hai jinamein 24 main se 23 tirthakaron tathaa anya shalakapurushon ke charitra kavyariti main varnit hai. mahapurana ke purvaaddh, adipurana kii apeksha vistaar main yah ni:sadeh bahut hi nune he, parantu kala kii drushti se yah puraana adipurana kaa ek upyukt poorak mana ja saktaa he.
 uttarpurana kii samaaptitithi kaa puraa parichay nahin miltaa, parantu isaki samaapti shaq san. 820 se pehle avashya ho gai hogi, kyonki gunbhadra ke shishy loksen ke kathananusaar ukt sanwat main is granth kaa poojamahotsav nishpann kiya gaya tha. vidwaanon kaa anumaan he ki mahapurana kaa yah poojamahotsav loksen ne apane guru ke swargavaasi hone par kiya hoga. gunbhadra bade hi vineet tathaa gurubhakt the. kavyakala main ve apane poojya gurudev ke suyogy shishy the. uttarpurana kii kathaon main jeevandhar kii katha badi prasiddh he jiskaa varnan anek kaviyon ne sanskrit or tamil main kavyarup main kiya he.
