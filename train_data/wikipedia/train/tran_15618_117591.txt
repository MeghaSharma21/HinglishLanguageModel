 jeevanaparyant sankalpapurvak aahaar kaa tyaag karke dhyansth mudra main asin hone ko pryopaveshan kaha he. bhagwat puraana main ullekh he ki pandavavanshi raja parakshit ne ganga kinare anshan vrath sweekaar kiya or samast sang chhodkar ve shrikrishna ke charnon main leen ho gaye. vaayu puraana ke anusaar indra dwaara use shishyon kii hatya kiye jane par sukarma ne bhi pryopaveshan vrath sweekaar kiya tha.
