 chandraprabha vati ek ayurvedic aushadhi he. yah cholestrol kam karne main laabhadaayak hoti he. 
 "chandraprabha vati " ayurveda shaastra kaa ek aisa adbhut evam gunakari yoga he jo aaj ke yug main stri-purush donon varg ke liye, kisi bhi aayu main upyogi evam laabhakaari siddh hota he. "ras tantrasaar v siddh prayog sangrah " tathaa "ayurveda-sarasangrah " naamak suprasiddh ayurvedic granthon main chandraprabha vati kii bahut prashansa kii gayi he or vaidya jagat bhi chandraprabha vati ko bahut gunakari v vishwasaneey yoga maanata he.
