 sheetakaaleen tufaan sardiyon main hone vaala ek tufaan he. yah barf yaa ole ke saath varsha ke rup main hota he. yah nimna taapmaana main mausam ke hone se hota he. yah anya mausam main bhi yadi jameen kaa taapmaana bahut kam ho, to bhi is prakaar mausam ho saktaa he. aisa hi ek tufaan garmi ke mausam main america main 1816 main aayaa tha. saath hi 2013 main 10semi kaa barf kii chadar jamaa diya tha or 10mimi kaa hawa main barf ke saath hawa kaa taapmaana ko bhi thanda kar diya tha.
