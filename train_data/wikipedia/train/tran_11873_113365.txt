 hindu silhara rajvansh vartmaan mumbai kshetra par 810 se 1240 i. ke lagbhag shashan karata tha. inaki teen shakhaaen thim:- 
 yah rajvansh dackhin ke pathaar par rajya karte rashtrakut rajvansh ke adheen jagirdaar or samant hua karte the. yah 8veen -10veen shatabdi kii baat he. govind dwitiya, rashtrakut shaasha ne uttari konkan kaa rajya kapardin pratham, silhara vansh ke sansthaapak ko 800 i. main diya. tabase uttari konkan ko kapardi dweep yaa kavdidweep kaha jane laga. is rajya kii rajdhani puri hui, jo vartmaan rajapur yaa raigarh he. is vansh ko tagar-puradhishwar kii oopaadhi mili, jiskaa arth tha tagar kaa swami. 1343 ke lagbhag, salset dweep or puraa dweepasmooh muzaffar vansh ko chalaa gaya. 
 silhara vansh, dakshnin maharashtra jo kolhapur main the, in teenon main se ek the. is vansh kii sthapana rashtrakut vansh ke patan ke baad huyi thi.
