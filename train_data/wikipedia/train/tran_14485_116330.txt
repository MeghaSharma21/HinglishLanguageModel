 quark ek praathmik kan he tathaa yah padaarth kaa mul ghatak he. quark ekajute hokar sammishra kan hedron banaate he, parmaanu nabhik ke mukhya avyav proton va neutron inamen se sarvaadhik sthir hai. naiysargik ghatana rang bandhan ke kaaran, quark naa kabhi sidhe prekshit hua yaa ekant main paaya gaya; ve keval hedronon ke bheetar paye ja sakate he, jaise ki beriwanon or mesonon ke rup main. 
 quark ke anek aantarik gun he, jiname vidyut aavesh, dravyaman, rang aavesh or spin sammilit he. kan bhautikee ke maanak model main quark ekmaatr praathmik kan he jo sabhi chaar moolbhoot antahkriya yaa moulik balon ko mahasoos karata he, saath hi yah maatr gyaat kan he jiskaa vidyut aavesh praathmik aavesh kaa purnank gunanfal nahin he.
 quark ke chah prakaar he, jo jane jate he flavor se: ap, down, strinj, charm, top or bottm. ap va down quark ke dravyaman sabhi quarko main sabase kam he. apekshaakrit baari quark kanika kshay kii prakriya ke madhyam se tivrata se ap va down quark main badal jate hai. kanika kshay, ek ucch dravya avasthaa kaa ek nimna dravya avasthaa main parivartan he. is vajah se, ap va down quark aam taur par sthir hote he or brahmad main sabase aam hai, vahiin strinj, charm, bottm or top quark keval ucch urja takkaron main utpann kiye ja sakate he. har quark flavor ke pratikan hote he jinke pariman to quark ke baraabar hote he parantu chinh vipareet rakhte he tathaa yah antiquark ke rup main jane jate he.
 quark model swatantra rup se bhautikavidon mare gail-man or jorge wig dwaara 1964 main prastaavit kiya gaya tha. quark hedronon ke ang ke rup main pesh kiye gaye the. 1968 main stenford raikhik twarak centre par prayog hone tak unke bhautik astitv ke bahut kam pramaan the.twarak prayogon ne sabhi chah flavoron ke liye pramaan pradaan kiye. top quark sabase ant main fermilab par 1995 main khoja gaya. 
 quark moolbhoot kan main se ek he, jisase padaarth banataa he. proton va neutron inhi moolbhoot kano se bane hote he. quark ko unke flavor se janaa jaataa he or yah chh: prakaar ke hote hai:-ap quark, charm quark, taap quark, down quark, strinj quark or batom quark. inke pratik chinh kramash: u, c, t, d, s or b he. pehle teen prakaar ke quark ap-tip quark tathaa shesh down-tip quark kehalaate hai. ap or down quark kaa dravyaman sabhi quarko main sabase kam hota he. baari dravyaman vaale quark, dravyaman ke kshay ke kaaran teji se ap va down quark main parivartit hote hai, kyonki ap va down quark saadharantaya sthaayee hote hai or brahmand main sabase adhik paye jate hai. quark kaa pratikan {anti partical} anti-quark kehalaata he, prayek prakaar ke quark ke pratikan hote he, jo kramash: anti-ap quark---- anti-batom quark kehalaate he.
 quark akele nahin paye jate, varan hamensha samooh main paye jate he. quark se milakar banane vaale avyav hedron {hadrons} kehalaate he. teen quark ke sanyonjan se beryon {baryons}, teen anti-quark ke sangyojan se anti-beryon tathaa ek quark evam ek anti-quark ke sangyojan se meson banate he. do ap quark evam ek down quark ke sangyojan se proton tathaa ek ap quark evam do down quark ke sangyojan se neutron banate he. beryaan, mesaan, proton, neutron, newclian {ek proton + ek neutron} or parmaanu nabhik, yah sabhi hedron kehalaate he kyonki sabhi quark se milakar bane hote he. quark kaa sankhyikeey vyavhaar fermion hota he.
 quark main kai prakaar ke gun hote he, jaise vidyut aavesh, rang aavesh, bhrami yaa prachakran {spin} or dravyaman. pratikan main bhi yah sabhi gun paye jate he, parantu vipareet hote he.
 ap-tip quark kaa vidyut aavesh +2/3 or down-tip quark kaa -1/3 hota he. inke pratikan kaa aavesh vipareet kramash: -2/3 or +1/3 hota he. quark ke sangyojan se banane vaale kano kaa vidyut aavesh, usamen paye jane vaale quarko ke kul vidyut aavesho ke yoga ke baraabar hota he. jaise:- proton kaa vidyut aavesh, do ap-quark or ek down-quark ke vidyut aavesho ke kul yoga ke baraabar arthath 1 hota he. isi tarah neutron kaa vidyut aavesh shunya hota he.
 mukhya lekh : durbal antahkriya
 kan bhautikee kii chaar moolbhoot antahkriya on main se ek, keval durbal antahkriya ke madhyam se ek flavor kaa quark kisi anya flavor ke quark main tabdeel ho saktaa he. ek W boson ko avashoshit yaa utsarjit karke kisi bhi ap-prakaar quark ko kisi bhi down-prakaar quark main parivartit kar sakate hai tathaa isi tarah thik iske vipareet. yah flavor parivartan tantra beta kshay kii radiodharmee prakriya kaa kaaran banataa he, jisamen ek neutron kaa ek proton, ek electron or ek electron antinutrino main "vibhaajan" ho jaataa he . yah tab hota he jab kisi neutron main koi ek down quark kaa abhaasi W⁻ boson utsarjak dwaara ap quark main kshay ho jaataa he or neutron ko ek proton main badal detaa he. W⁻ boson phir ek electron or ek electron antinutrino main tut jaataa he. 
 kwantum chromodynamiks ke anusaar, quark janmajaat rang aavesh naamak gundharm rakhte he. teen tarah ke rang aavesh hote he- haraa, laal, or nila. isi tarah teen prati rang aavesh hote he-megenta, syaan or pilaa. har quark ek rang vahan karte he, jabaki prayek antiquark prati rang vahan karte he. rang aavesh keval quark or gluon main hote he. dhyaan rahe, rang aavesh kaa drishy prakaash ke rang varnakram se koi sambandh nahin he.
 teen rangon ke bhinn-bhinn sanyojanon ke saath quarkon ke aaveshon ke madhya aakarshan or pratikarshan kii pranali prabal antahkriya kehalaati he, jisaki madhyasthata gluon naamak bal vaahak kan dwaara hoti he; isaki neeche main vistaar se charchaa hui he. vah siddhant jo prabal antahkriyaaon ko bataata he kwantum chromodynamiks kehalaata he. A quark, which will have a single color value, can form a bound system with an antiquark carrying the corresponding anticolor. The result of two attracting quarks will be color neutrality: a quark with color charge ξ plus an antiquark with color charge −ξ will result in a color charge of 0 and the formation of a meson. This is analogous to the additive color model in basic optics. Similarly, the combination of three quarks, each with different color charges, or three antiquarks, each with anticolor charges, will result in the same "white" color charge and the formation of a baryon or antibaryon.
