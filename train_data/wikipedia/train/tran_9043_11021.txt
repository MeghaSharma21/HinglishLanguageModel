 bipin chandra aadhunik bharat ke itihaas ke itihaaskaar the. professor bipin chandra bharat ke swatantraaa sangharsh or aadhunik itihaas lekhan parampara main marxvaadi chintan dhara ke itihaaskaar the. 
 bipin chandra kaa janm 1928 ko kangra, himachal pradesh main hua tha.
 bipin chandra jawaharlal nehru vishvavidhyalay main saamaajik vigyaan santhaan ke aitihaasik adhyayan centre main professor ameritas the. vartmaan main ye neshnal book trust ke adhyaksh the. inhone aadhunik bharat ke itihaas par kai pustaken likhi jinamein se pramukh pustaken nimna hai:- 
 lambii beemaari ke chalate 30 agast 2014 ko bipin chandra kii unke awaas par gudgaanv main mrityu ho gayi.
