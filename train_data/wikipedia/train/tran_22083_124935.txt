 swami satyanand saraswati, sanyasi, yoga guru or aadhyatmik guru the. unhone antararashtriya yoga fellowship tathaa bihar yoga vidhyalay kii sthapana kii. unhone 80 se bhi adhik pustakon kii rachana kii jisamen se 'asana pranayam mudrabandh' naamak pustak vishwaprasiddh he.
 swami satyanand kaa janm 23 disambar 1923 ko uttrakhand ke almoda main margshirsh kii poornima ko hua tha. unke pitaaji british shashan main police adhikari the tathaa unki maan nepal ke rajgaraane kii thim. apane mata-pita kii eklauti santan satyanand ne bachapan se hi dharm or aadhyatm main gahari dilchaspee dikhaanaa prarambh kar diya tha. unhen apane aadhyatmik guru kii prabal talash thi or vah iske liye kaaphi ghumaa karte the.
 18 varsh main unhone ghar chhod diya, 19 varsh kii aayu main unhen apane guru shivanand saraswati ke darshan hue. 1947 main unhen guru ne paramhans sanyas main dikshit kiya. unhone 12 saal guru kii seva kii. uske baad ve parivrajak ke rup main ghoomte rahe - bharat, afghanistan, burma, nepal evam srilanka main. is dauraan unhone yogic takneekon ko pratipaadit tathaa parishkrut kiya.
 yoga kii ati pracheen paddati ko vigyaan ke sammat or iske pariprekshya main prastut karne kii aavashyaktaa kaa anubhav hone par unhone 1956 main antarrashtriya yoga mitra mandal tathaa 1963 main bihar yoga vidhyalay kii sthapana kii. iske baad videshon or swadesh main kai yogic bhraman tathaa pravachan kiye. 1988 main sab tyaag kar sanyas le liya. 1989 main unhen rikhia aakar aasapaas ke logon kii madad karne kaa divine aadesh aayaa. yahan unhone ekaantavaas karte hue ucch vaidic sadhanaayen kin. 5 disambar 2009 kii madhyaraatri main apane shishyon kii upasthiti main ve maha samadhi main leen ho gaye.
