 saajan 1991 main bani hindi bhashaa kii premakahani film he jiskaa nirdesan laurence disuza dwaara kiya gaya he or salmaan khan, sanjay dutt or maadhuri dikshit isamen mukhya bhoomikaaon main he. reelij hone ke baad film superhit sabit hui thi or iskaa sangeet bhi khub saraahaa gaya tha.
 aman ek garib, viklang anaath he jo apane bachapan ke dauraan se amir akaash verma se mitraapurna he. akaash ke mata-pita ne use apanaaya or use apana parivaar kaa naam diya or akaash or aman bhaaiyon ke rup main bade ho gaye. jabaki akaash ek ladkibaaz hai, aman kalam naam "sagar" ke tahat kavitaaon ko likhata he. kavitaaon ko prakashit kiya jaataa he or aman bahut lokapriy ho jaataa he. uske prashansakon main se ek puja saxena ke naam se ek yuva mahila he jo sagar se chitti dwaara sampark main he. akaash puja se miltaa he or uske saath pyaar main pad jaataa he. jab aman ko yah pataa chal jaataa he to vah akaash ko sagar ke rup main pesh aane ke liye kahata he, jo vah karata he or puja or akaash ek-duusare se pyaar karne lagate hai. dil toote aman jaanata he ki yadhyapi mahilaayein uski kavita pasand kar sakati hai, phir bhi ve khule taur par baahar nahin aa jaaengi. jab ve uski viklaangata dekhengi to use pyaar nahin karengi. ant main, akaash ko pataa laga ki aman vastav main sagar he or puja se pyaar karata he, isliye vah aman ke liye apane pyaar kaa tyaag karne kaa faisla karata he.
 is film ke sabhi gane bahut lokapriy hue. iske sangeet ke liye sangeetkaar nadeem shravan ko filmafere kaa sarvshereshth sangeetkaar kaa award bhi mila tha.
 
 sabhi geet samir dwaara likhit; sara sangeet nadeem-shravan dwaara rachit.
 film box offis par superhit sabit hui or 1991 kii sabase zyada kamai karne vaali film thi.
