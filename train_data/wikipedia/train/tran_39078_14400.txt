 paryaavas vikhandan kisi jeev ke paryaavas kshetra main pratikul paristhiti ke upakshetra ban jane se uske kai alag-thalag khand ban jane kii prakriya ko kehete hai. yah khand ek-duusare se jude nahin hote or jeev na to in khandon ke bich sahajata se aa-ja saktaa he, na anya khandon main rahane vaale apni jeevvaigyanik jati ke anya jeevon ke saath prajnan kar saktaa he or na hi sabhi khandon ke prakritik sansadhano kaa prayog karne main sakasham hota he. 
 is vikhandan se jeevon kii aabaadi bhi vikhandit ho jaati he. falswaroop jeevon kii sankhya kam ho jaati he. kuch paristhitiyon main vah jati vilupti kii kaagaar par aa jaati he. yadi yah prakriya dhire-dhire shatabdiyon yaa hazaron varshon main bhuvaigyanik kaarnon se ho to ek jati ke vibhaajan se nai jati bhi utpann ho sakati he. aadhunik kaal main maanviya hastkshep se hue paryaavas vikhandan ke kaaran vanya jivan ko baari haani pahaunchi he.
