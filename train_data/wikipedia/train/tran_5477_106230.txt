 someshwar dwitiya, someshwar pratham kaa sabase bada putr tathaa pashchimi chalukya rajvansh kaa shaasha tha. apane pita baad vah gaddi par baitha. apane pita ke shasankaal main vah gadag ke aasapaas ke kshetron kaa prashasan dekhtaa tha. 
 apane shasankaal main vah apane chote bhai vikramaditya shashth se lagaataar chunauti paata rahaa jo atyant mahatvakankshi tha. antatah vikramaditya ne 1076 i main use parajeet kar shashan apane haath main le liya.
