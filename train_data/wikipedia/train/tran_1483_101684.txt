 eni jaafaree ek pakistani abhinetri or model he.
 eni jaafaree ham tivi ke kaarykram aseer zaadi main ek pramukh bhoomika main dikhaai di thi. 2013 main vah humayun said ke utpaadan main main hum shahid afridi main film kii. eni jaafaree bhi anemated shrinkhalaa main mukhya kirdaar jiya aawaaj uthaai he, Burka Avenger'burka avenzar. vah vartmaan main osman khalid bat ke saath baaluu maahi ke liye shooting kar rahi he.
