 kubernath raay hindi lalit nibandh parampara ke mahatvapurn hastakshar, saanskrithik nibandhkaar or bhartiya aarsh-chintan ke gandhamadan the. unki ginati acharya hajariprasad dvivedi or vidyanivas mishra jaise khyatilabdh nibandhkaaron ke saath kii jaati he. 
 kubernath raay kaa janm 26 march 1933 ko uttar pradesh ke gajipur jile ke matasaan gram main hua.unke pitaaji kaa naam swa baikuntha narayan raay evam mataji kaa naam swa lakshmi raay tha. unhone matsaan, malsaan, quins kalege, varanasi, kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay, varanasi or kalakatta vishvavidhyalay se shiksha praapt kii.unki patni kaa naam maharani devi tha. apane sevakaal ke armbh main unhone vikram vidhyalay kolkata main adhyaapan kiya uske baad ve nalbari, assam main angreji ke praadhyaapak or sahjanand mahavidhyalay, gajipur, uttar pradesh main praachaarya rahe.
 shri raay kaa nidhan 5 june 1996 ko hua ko unke paitrik gaanv matsaa main hua.
