 darkhan mongolia kaa ek shahar he. yah darkhan-ool praant kii rajdhani he or isaki sthapana san 1961 main rashtriya rajdhani, ulaan bator, main bheed kam karne ke liye hui thi. mongol bhashaa main darkhan kaa arth 'lohaar' hota he. is nagar kaa nirmaan soviet sangh kii baari saahayata ke saath kiya gaya tha or iske dhyey mongolia ke uttari bhaag main audyogic kendra bananaa tha. aaj bhi yah nagar rus ke sameep hone ke kaaran ek chote roosi samudaay kaa ghar he.
