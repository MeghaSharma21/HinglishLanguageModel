 purvi samay mandal ) sanyukt rajya america ke satraa purvi rajyon, purvi canada ke kuch hisson or mexico ke qvinatanaa rou rajya, madhya america main panama or caribbian dwipon main istemaal hone vaala samay mandal he.
 wo kshetra jo purvi maanak samay kaa istemaal karte hai utisi se paanch ghante peeche hote hai. 
 purvi divalok samay, jab basant ritu or garmiyon main divalok bachat samay kaa istemaal karte hai tab utisi se chaar ghante peeche hote hai.
 1966 ke america ekasmaan samay kanoon ke anusaar divalok bachat samay kii shruwaat april ke antim ravivaar se honi thi auraktubar ke antim ravivaar tak chalani thi. 1987 main is kanoon main paribvartan karke deebas kii shruwaat april ke pehle ravivaar se kar di gai.
 canada ke nimnlikhit praant or kshetra purvi samay mandal kaa hissaa hai.
 columbia jila or satraa anya rajya purvi samay mandal main sthit hai.
 
 
 
 
 chha: rajya madhya or purvi samay mandalon main bante hue hai.
 rajdhani washington disi or neuyork jaise bade va raajnneetik tathaa aarthik rup se mahatvapurn shaharon ke is samay mandal main hone kii vajah se america main kisi bhi ghatana ke varnan ke liye is samay ko bahut istemaal kiya jaataa he.
 bahamas, keman dweepasmooh or haiti aadhikarik taur par sardiyon main purvi maanak samay or garmiyon main purvi divalok bachat samay kaa istemaal karte hai. cuba bhi aksar aisa hi karata he. turk or kaikas dweepasmooh bhi pehle isi samay mandal kaa istemaal karte the lekin 2015 main unhone atlantic samay mandal apana liya.
 madhya america main panama bhi purvi maanak samay utisi-05:00 kaa puure varsh istemaal karata he.
