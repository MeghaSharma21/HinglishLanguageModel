 prasruth ardhvivruth agraswar ek prakaar kii swar dhwani hoti he jiskaa prayog kuch bhashaaon ko bolne main hota he. anterrashtriya dhvanyatmak varnmaala main ise É› ke chinh dwaara pradarshit kara jaataa he. hindi main ise "ji" ke varna ke prayog se likhaa jaataa he.
 yah swar uccharan main 'e' or 'i' ke bich kaa swar he. udhaaharan ke liye, angrezi ke teen shabdon ko dekha ja saktaa he:
 hindi shabdon main bhi 'ji' kii dhwani istemaal hoti he lekin ise prathanusaar likhaa nahin jaataa. hindi main aksar yadi 'ha' se pehle 'a' kii dhwani ho to maanak rup se use 'ji' uccharit kiya jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye:
 yadi inhen 'ji' kii bajaae anya dhwani se bolaa jaae to use kshetreey lahaza mana jaataa he:
 angrezi ke mul ke shabdon main 'ji' kii dhwani kaa uccharan 'e' karne se galatzhamiyaan ho sakati he. masalan "du you haev a pen" ko galati se "du you haev a pen" kehane se prashan kaa puraa arth hi badal jaataa he.
 'ji' or 'i' ke prayog hindi or marathi main bhinn hote hai. marathi main 'i' kaa uccharan 'ai' se miltaa sanyukt swar hota he. udhaaharan ke liye marathi main 'bail' ko 'bail' or 'ainak' ko 'ainak' se miltaa hua padhaa jaataa he. thik aisa hi purvi hindi kshetron main hota he. marathi main 'ji' kaa prayog maanak hindi ke 'i' swar ke baraabar hota he.
