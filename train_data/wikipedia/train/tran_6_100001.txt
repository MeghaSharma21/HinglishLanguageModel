 harold pinter nobel puraskaar vijeta or mashhoor britani naatakkaar the. unka 25 disambar ko 78 varsh kii umr main nidhan ho gaya. ve liver cancer se peedit the.
 harold pinter kaa janm landan main ek yahudee tailor ke ghar 10 oktober 1930 ko hua tha. unka pehla naatak tha 'da room' jo unhone 1957 main likhaa tha. unhen 2005 main sahitya ke nobel puraskaar se nawaaza gaya tha. unhone 30 se zyaada naatak likhe jinamein da keretekar, da home kaming or da damb waiter shaamil he. unhone kai filmon or tivi naatakon ke liye patakatha lekhan bhi kiya he jisamen da french leftinent vuman shaamil he.
 harold pinter ko na sirf unke sahitya ke liye balki apane raajnneetik vichaaron ko khulkar prakat karne ke liye janaa jaataa rahaa he. iraq ke kilaaf ladaai chhedane kaa unhone jamkar virodh kiya tha. 
 harold pinter ko kai puraskaar mili jinamein shakespeare puraskaar, sahitya kaa european puraskaar, david kohen british sahitya puraskaar, lorence olivier award, lijen de oner, moliere de oner lifetime achievement aadi shaamil hai. unhen 18 vishvavidhyalayon ne maanad oopaadhiyaan pradaan kin.
 iraq yuddh ke virodh main likhi kavitaaon ke liye unhen wilfred oven puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. unka yah kavita sangrah waar san 2003 main prakashit hua tha. pinter kaa raajnneeti se moh jagajaahir tha. unhone srbija par nato kii bambaari ke virodh main vaktavy diya tha.
 nobel puraskaar sweekaar karte hue unhone kaha tha, "main pichley pachaas varshon se naatak likhata rahaa hoon or raajnneetik rup se bhi sakriy rahaa hoon, lekin main nahin jaanata ki uskaa is puraskaar se kitna sambandh he."
