 kalaadhungi uttrakhand rajya ke nainital janapad main sthit ek nagar he. yah nagar haldwani-ramnagar tathaa bajpur-nainital sadakon ke chauraahey par haldwani se 26 kilometre, nainital se 30 kilometre tathaa bajpur se 19 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. kalaadhungi nainital janapad kii 8 tahseelon main se ek bhi he.kalaadhungi gym corbett kaa sheetakaaleen awaas bhi tha. ab unke kalaadhungi sthit bangale ko museum bana diya gaya he. uttarakhand ke prasiddh shikari or baad main gym corbett vanya jeev abhyaarany kii sthapana ke himachati gym corbett ne apni pustakon "kumaun ke aadamkhor" " rudraprayag kaa aadamkhor tendua" "kahani jangal kii" "maai india" aadi pustakon main kalaadhungi kaa vivaran diya he.unke vivaranon main likhaa he ki kalaadhungi parvatiy dhaalon ke ant main sthit gaanv tha. jo ki nainital kii kathor sardiyon se bachane kaa upyukt sthaan tha, kyonki yahaan kii jalwaayu tarai ke ilaakon kii bhaanti ushn thi. kheti hetu paryaapt jameen thi. chaaron ore ghaney jangal the. jo vividh vanyajeevon se bhare the. jahaan aksar shikari apane bhojan va manoranjan hetu shikaar karne jate the. kabhi kabhi ek mile se bhi paas koi baagh yaa tendua vicharan karata paaya jaataa tha. jo sthaneey nivaasiyon ko koi haani pahunchaae bina phir jangal main bhaag jate the.
