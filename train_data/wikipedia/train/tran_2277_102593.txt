  rajsamand jhil conkaroli tathaa rajsamand shaharon ke bich sthit he. is jhil kii sthakapana 17veen shatabdeey main mewar ke maharana rajsingh ne kii thi. is jhil kaa nirmaan gomti, kelva tathaa tali nadiyon par dam banaakar kiya gaya he. conkaroli main jhil ke tat par dwarkadhish krishna kaa mandir he. yahaan jane ke liye udaipur se sidhi bas seva he.
  yah bharat kaa sabase bada kritrim jhil he. yah jhil 88 varg kilometre kshetra main failaa hua he. maharana jaisingh ne is jhil kaa nirmaan 17veen shatabdi main gomti nadi par dam banaakar kiya tha. iske tatbandh par marble kaa ek smarak tathaa bhagwaan shiv kaa ek mandir he. is jhil ke duusari taraf rajpariwaar ke logon ke garmiyon main rahane ke liye mahal bane hue hai. is jhil main saat dweep hai. yah jhil ke chaaron taraf pahariyaan hai. pahadiyon par do mahal bane hue hai. inamen se ek hawa mahal tathaa doosraa ruthi rani kaa mahal he. yahaan ek jaisamand vanyajiv abhyaaran bhi he.
