 brisalakon chid ek prakaar kaa chid he jo vishwa main kisi bhi anya saadhaaran praani se adhik aayu jeeta he. mana jaataa he ki yah qareeb 5,000 saalon tak ji saktaa he. isaki teen jatiyaan gyaat hai, jo sabhi uttar america ke pashchimi ilaaqon main milti hai:
 brisalakon chid pahaado par 1,700 se 3,400 meter kii oonchaaiyon par vriksh rekha se zaraa neeche miltaa he. yah chote samoohon main ugte hai. in sthanon par thand, shushk zamin, tej hawaaon or varsh main achche mausam kii choti avadhi kii vajah se bahut dhire ugte hai. jahaan aam chid kii patali tiliyaan jaldi hi sookhkar jhad jaati hai vahaan brisalakon kii tiliyaan chalis saalon tak ped par ugti rah sakati hai. inase ped kii antim tahaniyon ke ant par yah paanch-paanch ke samooh main ug rahi tiliyaan unhen bottle saafe karne vaale brushon kaa rup de detii hai.
 in pedo kii lakdi behad ghani or sakht hoti he jis se keede, fngus or anya hanikaarak jeev isapar aasaani se hamalaa nahin kar sakate. in pedo kii lambi umr kaa ek raaj unki lakdi kii sakhti bhi he. yadi ped mar jaae yaa uski koi shaakh tut jaae to vah aam pedo kii lakdi kii tarah sadati nahin balki pathar kii tarah hawa, pani va barf se ajeeb-ajeeb shaklein le leti he. mara hua brisalakon chid kaa ped bhi dashakon tak apni jadon par khadaa rah saktaa he.
