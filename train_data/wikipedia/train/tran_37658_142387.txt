 str samvedak  kisi bhi taral padaarth, anya draveekrut thos padaarth, draveekrut sahit thos, gaad, daanedaar saamagree, or powder jin kii upree muktsatah hoti he unka str naapta he. 
 jis padaarth ko naapnaa hota he vah ek container yaa saanche main hona jaruri he yaa phir apane prakritik form main hona chahie . str do tarah se maape ja sakate hai - nirantar yaa bindu ho saktaa he. ek nirdhaarit seemaa ke bheetar nirantar str ko str sensor maapta he or ek nishchit jagah main padaarth kii sahi maatra bataata he, jabaki bindu str sensor se keval ye sanket miltaa he ki padaarth ek nidhrit bindu ke upar yaa neeche he. aam taur par bindu sensor ye bataata he ki padaarth kaa str ek bindu se jarurat se zyada kitna ucch yaa kam hai.
