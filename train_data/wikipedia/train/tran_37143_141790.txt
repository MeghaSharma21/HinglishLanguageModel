 lakshmi poruri-madan main ek sevanivrutt peshewar tennis khilaadi or aadhunik yug main dablutie tour par peshewar tennis khilne ke liye pehali bhartiya-america mahila he. 
 poriri central california main badi hui, jahaan ek bahut hi kam umr se, vah ek tennis kauthuk ke rup main jane jaati thi. 1986 main, unhone final main monica seles ko haraa kar orange boul jeeta. 15 saal kii umr main, unhone apana pehla yues open khelaa, jahaan vah duusare round main katerina malewa se haar gayi. vah ek purna athlettic chaatravrutti par 1990 se 1994 tak stenford uniwarsity main rahi thi, jahaan vah chaar baar ol american athlete, varsh 1994 ke player of da year, or desh main sheersh rank vaali mahilaon ke colejiet tennis khilaadi thi. 
 stenford se snaatak hone ke baad, poruri ne kai saalon tak peshewar rup se tennis khelaa. peshewar tennis se avkaash grahan karne par, poruri ne boston, eme main ek saal ke liye angreji padhai. poruri phir canada main macgil vishvavidhyalay main gayi, jahaan unhone emb praapt kii. poruri ne 2004 main california main lautne se do saal pehle wal street par kaam kiya tha. 
 2015 tak, vah apane pati, ajay madan, or beti ke saath austin, texas main rahati he, jo ek korporate or pratibhuti vakil he.
