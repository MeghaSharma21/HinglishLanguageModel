 lakdi, hathidaant, pathar aadi ko garh chhilkar alankrita karne yaa murti banane ko utkeernan yaa nakkashi karnaa kehete hai. yahan kashtha utkeernan par pravidhik drushtikon se vichaar kiya gaya he. 
 utkeernan ke liye lakdi ko saavdhaanee se sookhne dena chahie. ek reeti yah he ki nai lakdi ko behate pani main daal diya jaae, jisamen uskaa sab ras bah jaae hawaadaar jagah main chhod dena kaaphi hota he. shisham, baanjh or lakadiyon par sookshm utkeernan kiya ja saktaa he. motaa kaam praaia: sabhi lakadiyon par ho saktaa he. utkeernan ke liye choti badi anek prakaar kii chapati or gol rukhaaniyon tathaa chhuriyon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. kaam ko pakadaney ke liye baak bhi ho to suvidha hoti he. kaath kii ek mungari bhi chahie. kone antare main lakdi ko chikna karne ke liye tedi reti bhi chahie. baarik kaam main rukhaani ko thonka nahin jaataa. keval ek haath kii gadori se dabaaya jaataa he or duusare haath kii anguliyon se uske agr ko niyantrit kiya jaataa he. utkeernan sikh saktaa he. nausikhue ke liye das baarah aujaar paryaapt honge. utkeernan ke liye bane yantron ko badhiya ispaat kaa hona chahie or unhen chura tej karne kii silli par tej kare antim dhar chamade kii chamoti par ragadkar chadhaani chahie. atikshn yantron se kaam schachh nahin banataa or lakdi ke fatne yaa tuutane kaa dar rahaata he. gol rukhaaniyon ko natodaar prishth kii ore se tej karne ke liye belanaakaar silliyaan milti hai yaa saadhaaran silliyaan bhi ghiskar waisi banaai ja sakati hai.
 yon to thodaa bahut utkeernan sabhi jagah hota he, panratu kashmir kii bani akhrot kii lakdi kii utkeern vastuen badi sundar hoti hai. china or japan ke mandiron main kaashthotkirnan ke ascharyajanak sookshm or sundar udhaaharan milte hai.
