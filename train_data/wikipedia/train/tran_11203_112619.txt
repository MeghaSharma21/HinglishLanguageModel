 newada sanyukt rajya america ke pashchimi or dakshin-pashchimi kshetra main sthit rajya he. nevada 7waan sabase vyapak kshetra vaala or 34waan sabase adhik aabaadi vaala amareeki rajya he. nevada kaaphi had tak registan or ardh-shushk he, jisamen se adhiktar ilaaka great basin ke bheetar he. great basin ke dakshin main sthit kshetra mohavi marusthal ke bheetar hai. 
 newada kaa pataa lagane vaale pehle europea log spanish the. yah kshetra new spain naamak spani colony kaa hissaa ban gaya or 1821 main swatantraaa praapt karne ke baad mexico kaa hissaa ban gaya. mexican-america yuddh main apni jeet ke baad sanyukt rajya america ne 1848 main is kshetra par kabja kar liya. nevada 31 oktober 1864 ko 36waan rajya bana. 1940 main newada kii aabaadi keval 1,10,000 se thodi hi adhik thi. haalanki jua vaid hone ke kaaran nevada 20veen shatabdi main ek pramukh paryaton sthal main badal gaya. 
 2016 ke anumaan ke mutabik rajya kii jansankhya 29,40,058 he. rajya kaa sabsaa bada shahar las vegas he.
