 melpatur narayan bhattatiri  bhartiya ganitagya. ve achyut pisharti ke tritiy shishy the. ve ganitagya vaiyaakaran the. 'prakriya sarvaswam' unki sabase mahatvapurn kriti he. yah kriti panini kii sutratmak reeti se vyakhya karti he. kintu unki 'narayanium' naamak kriti sabase prasiddh he jisamen guruvayurppan kii stuti he. vartmaan samay main bhi iskaa guuruvayur mandir main paath kiya jaataa he.
