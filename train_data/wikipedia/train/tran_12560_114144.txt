Tukol ke liye janaa jaataa tha apane vidwaanon ke kaam par jain dharm, shiksha or nyaaypalika. vah nyaayadhish tha kii ucch nyaayalay ke mysore. vah adhyakshata main mysore vetan ayog . unhone yah bhi seva ke rup main vaais chancelar ke bangalore vishvavidhyalay. unke yogadan ke liye nyaaypalika or pustakon par jain dharm, yoga, dhyaan or rahasyavaad main jain dharm, anuwaad ke saman Suttam or vibhinn prakashnon) ullekhneey hai. 
 Tukol main paidaa hua tha gudur gaanv ke Hungund tehsil, bagalkot jilahai. vah ek chaatr tha ke karnataka college, Dharwar or ferguson college, pune.
