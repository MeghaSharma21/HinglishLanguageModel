 khihaa ek bhartiya pakshi he. ise sanskrit main priyanvad kehete hai. 
 is pakshi ke do spasht pradeshik bhed he. ek to lalamunhi khihaa jo himalay main gadhwal se sikkim tak praaia: 2 se 7 hajaar foot kii oonchaai par paae jate hai. kabhi kada ye 10 hajaar foot tak kii oonchaai par bhi dekhe jate hai. is varg kaa khihaa 11 inch kaa hota he. isaki chonch tedi hoti he, upree punch or daine kaa ghira hua bhaag kaahi bhura hota he or nichalaa bhaag akharoti tathaa safed hota he. thudee or gale par dhoomil bhure rang kii dhaariyaan hoti he. yah jhaadiyon main nivas karata he or pahadiyon par hi adhikansh jivan vyateet karata he. isaki boli madhur hoti he. nar pakshi kii boli duhari hoti he. duusari boli pehali boli ke tatkaal baad dhvanit hoti he. yadi maadaa uske nikat hoti he to duusari dhwani ke baad apni madhur dhwani se tatkaal uttar detii he. yah pakshi duusare pakshi kii boliyon kaa bhi pratyuttar dete hai. yah pakshi naachata bhi he. gubareela, kentua, keede aadi iske mukhya bhojan hai.
 duusari jati kaa khihaa mukhyat: dakshin bharat ke parvaton main paaya jaataa he. kintu abu kii pahadiyon, madhya pradesh or khandesh ke aasapaas bhi ye dekhe jate he. yah lalamunhi khihaa se kuch chota hota he. iskaa upree bhaag bhura or maathaa kaala hota he. aankh ke upar ek safed pattee si hoti he, jiske upar kaali kaali kinari hoti he. chaati or pet par ek patali kaali bhuri lakeer hoti he. chonch pili hoti he. isaki boli saamanyat: karkash hoti he par kabhi kabhi yah madhur siti bhi bajaata he. log siti bajaakar apane nikat rakhane kaa prayaas karte hai. yah apni lambii talwarnuma chonch se phulon kaa ras chusata he. vaise, keede makode bhi iske bhojan hai.
