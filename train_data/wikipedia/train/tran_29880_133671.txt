 imam ahamed raza khan fazile barelvi kaa janm 10 shavwaal 1672 hijri mutabik 14 june 1856 ko bareli main hua. aapke purvaj qandahar ke pathan the jo mughalon ke samay main hindustan aaye thea. imam ahamed raza khan fazile barelvi ke maanane vaale unhen aalahazrat ke naam se yaad karte hai. aalaa hazarat bahut bade mufti, aalim, hafiz, lekhak, shaayar, dharmaguru, bhashavid, yugaparivartak, tathaa samaaj sudhaarak the. aalaa hazarat imam ahamed raza khan kadri 14 veen shatabdi ke navajivandaata the. jinhen us samay ke prasiddh arab vidwaanon ne yah upadhi di. unhone bhartiya upmahadweep ke musalamaanon ke dilon main allaah taaala va mohammad rasoolallaah sallahu taaala alaihi vasallam ke prati prem bhar kar or mohammad rasoolallaah sallahu taaala alaihi vasallam kii sunnaton ko jivit kar ke islam ko failaayaa aapke walid sahab ne 13 varsh kii choti si aayu main ahamed raza ko mufti ghoshit kar diya. unhone 72 se adhik vibhinn vishyon par 1000 se adhik kitaaben likhin jin main tafseer haddees unki ek pramukh pustak jis kaa naam addaulatul makkia he jis ko unhone keval 8 ghanton main bina kisi sandarbh granthon ke madad se haram-e-makka main likhaa. unki ek pramukh granth fatawa rajavia is sadi ke islamic kanoon kaa acha udhaaharan he jo 13 vibhaagon main vitarit he. kuran kareem kaa urdu anuwaad bhi kiya jise kanjul imaan naam se janaa jaataa he aalaa hazarat ne paigambar hazrat mohammad sallaho alaihi vasallam kii shaan ko ghataane waalo ko koran or hadis kii madad se munh tod jawaab diya ! aapaki hi khandaan se maftie aazamen hind, hujjatul islam, tajushashari, jaise paay ke bujurg is duniya main aay
