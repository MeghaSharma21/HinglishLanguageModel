 neli kim fartado ek kenadian gayika-geetkaar, record nirmaataa va abhinetri he. fartado victoria, british colombia, canada main pali badi he.
 fartado ko prasiddhi apane pehle album voa, neli! va uske ekal geet "ai em like a bird" se mili jisane unhen 2001 kaa varsh ke ekal geet kaa juno puraskaar or 2002 kaa sarvshereshth mahila pop vocal parformans kaa grami puraskaar jitavaaya. isamen do or antarrashtriya ekal geet the: "turn of da lite" or "shit on da radio ". apni beti nevis ke janm ke baad unhone apana doosraa studio album fokalor release kiya. yah vyapaarik drushti se america main kam safal rahaa. isamen teen antarrashtriya ekal geet the: "powerles ", "tri" or "forka".
 2006 kii garmiyon main unhone aapkaa teesraa studio album loose release kiya. yah abtak kaa unka vishvabhar main sabase safal album he. isamen pratham kramank ke hit geet "promicious", "minitor", "se it rhit" or "ol da good things " shaamil he. tin varshon ke viraam ke baad sitambar 2009 main unhone apana spanish album me plan apane pehle spanish geet "manos al aire" ke saath release kiya. isamen "mias" or "bajo otra luz" baaki geet the. me plan ke liye neli ko sarvshereshth mahila pop vocal album ke latin grami puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. 26 oktober ko ek rimix album me plan remixes release kiya gaya. ek maah baad 12 navambar 2010 ko fartado ne apana pehla sarvshereshth hit geeton kaa album da best of neli fartado release kiya.
