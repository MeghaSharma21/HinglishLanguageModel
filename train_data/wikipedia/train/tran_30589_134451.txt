 
 hongar yaa shark ek prishthvanshi samudri jal main rahane vaala praani he. iskaa sharir bahut lambaa hota he jo shalkon se dhakaa rahata he. in shalkon ko plaqid kehete hai. twachaa chikani hoti he. twachaa ke neeche vasa kii moti parat hoti he. iske sharir main haddi kii jagah upasthi pai jaati he. sharir naukaakaar hota he. iskaa nichalaa jabada upree jabade se chota hota he. atah iskaa munh saamne na hokar neeche kii ore hota he jisamen tej daant hote hai. yah ek maansaahaari praani he. shark ke sharir main dekhne ke liye ek jodi ankhen, tairne ke liye paanch jode pakhane or shwans lene ke liye paanch jode chlom hote hai.
