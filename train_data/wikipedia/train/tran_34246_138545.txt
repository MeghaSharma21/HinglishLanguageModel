 athens main 1896 greeshmakaleen olympic main aadhunik olympic andolan ke janm ke baad se prayek greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main jimnastics kii ghatnaon kaa chunaav hua. 32 saal ke liye, keval purushon ko pratispardhaa karne kii anumati thi. amsterdam main 1928 greeshmakaleen olympic ke shuru hone se, mahilaon ko kalatmak jimnastic kii ghatnaon main bhi pratispardhaa karne kii anumati di gai thi. layaaddh jimnastic kii ghatnaayen los angeles main 1984 greeshmakaleen olympic main pesh kii gayi, or sidney main 2000 greeshmakaleen olympic main trempolin kii ghatnaon ko joda gaya.
 
 kalatmak jimnastic main pratispardhaa karne vaale raashtra, or prayek olympic main laae jane vaale jimnast kii sankhya, neeche dikhaae gaye hai.
