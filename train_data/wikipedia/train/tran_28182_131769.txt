 mandsaur jila puratathvik or aitihaasik drushti se bahut hi mahatvapurn he. shivana nadi ke kinare sthit bhagwaan pashupatinath mandir ke kaaran yah desh bhar main prasiddh he. yah pradesh kaa ausat kshetrafal vaala jila he. jo 142 kimi uttar se dakshin or 124 kimi purv se pashchim kii ore failaa hua he. iskaa kul kshetrafal 5521 varg kimi he. san 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar jile kii jansankhya 1183274 he. jile main anusuchit jati ke 212262 or anusuchit janajati ke 37526 log nivas karte hai. ise nepal ke kathmandu sthit bhagwaan pashupatinath ke samaan hi mana gaya he. yahaan maalavi boli boli jaati he. jo rajasthani or hindi kii mishrit bhashaa he. yahaan opium kaa utpaadan vishwa main sabase adhik hota he. is kaaran bhi yah jila puure vishwa main janaa jaataa he. slate-pancil udyog jile kaa mahatvapurn udyog he. mandsaur mapra ke uttar main sthit he jo ujjain kamishnar sambhag ke antargat aata he. yah jila 230’45’50’’ akshansh uttar or 250’2’55’’ akshansh uttar saamanantar tathaa 740 42’30’’ purv or 750’50’20’’ purv ke madhya sthit he. yah jila rajasthan ke chaar jilon pashchim or uttar main chittorgarh, uttar main bhilwara, uttar-purv main kota or purv main jhalawad ke saath hi dakshin main madhya pradesh ke ratlam jile kii seemaa se laga hua he. yah jila upsanbhaagon or 6 tahseelon main banta hua he. upsambhageeya mukhyalay mandsaur, malhargarh, sitamau or garoth tathaa tehsil mandsaur evam malhargarh pashchim main sthit he. jabaki suvasra, bhagpura, garoth or sitamau poorviy bhaag main sthit hai. 
 tapamanah jile kaa taapmaana farvari ke baad se hi badhanaa shuru ho jaataa he. may main saamanytah sabase adhik garmi rahati he. din kaa ausat adhiktam taapmaana 39.80 degree celsius tathaa nunatam taapmaana 25.40 degree se. rahata he. greeshm main din main garm or dhul bhari hawaaen chalati hai. june main monsoon se purv taapmaana 45 di.se. tak pahunch jaataa he. janvari mausam kaa sabase sard maheenaa hota he, jisamen adhiktam taapmaana 35 di.se. tathaa nunatam taapmaana 9.30 di.se. tak rahata he.
