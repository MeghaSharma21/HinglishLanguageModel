 eni monsan ek angreji vanaspatishastri or paudhon or keedon ke sangrahakartta thi. unka janm 1726 main darlington, england main hua tha. vah henry wain kii beti or charles dwitiya kii potee thi. 1746 main, unhone cregiehaul ke charles hope-vere se shaadi kii or uske do bete the. phir baad main 1757 main, unhone linkanshair ke karnal jorge monson se shaadi kii chunki uske nae pati kaa karier bhartiya sena ke saath tha, usne apana adhikansh samay kalakatta main bitaayaa, jahaan vah anglo-bhartiya samaaj main pramukh ban gai. 
 1760 main, vah pehle se hi ek "ullekhneey mahila vanaspatishastri" ke rup main vanaspati samudaay ke liye acchhee tarah se jaani jaati thi. 
 18 farvari 1776 ko kalakatta main unki mrityu ho gai.
