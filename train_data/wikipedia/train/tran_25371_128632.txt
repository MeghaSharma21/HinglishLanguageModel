 bharat dakshin asia main sthit bhartiya upmahadweep kaa sabase bada desh he. purna rup se uttari golardh main sthit bharat, bhugolic drushti se vishwa main saatvaan sabase bada or jansankhya ke drushtikon se doosraa sabase bada desh he. bharat ke pashchim main pakistan, uttar-purv main china, nepal or bhutan, purv main bangladesh or myanmar sthit hai. hind mahaasaagar main iske dakshin pashchim main maldive, dakshin main srilanka or dakshin-purv main indonesia se bharat kii saamudrik seemaa lagati he. iske uttar kii bhautik seemaa himalay parvat se or dakshin main hind mahaasaagar se lagi hui he. purv main bengal kii khadi he tathaa pashchim main arab sagar hai.
 pracheen sindhu ghati sabhyataa, vyapaar margon or bade-bade samrajyon kaa vikaas-sthaan rahe bhartiya upmahadweep ko iske saanskrithik or aarthik safalataa ke lambey itihaas ke liye janaa jaataa rahaa he. chaar pramukh sampradaayon: hindu, buddha, jain or sikh dharmon kaa yahaan udai hua, parsi, yahudee, isai, or muslim dharm pratham sahasrabdi main yahaan pahuche or yahaan kii vividh samskruti ko nayaa rup diya. kramik vijayon ke parinamswaroop british east india company ne 18veen or 19veen sadi main bharat ke zyadatar hisson ko apane rajya main mila liya. 1857 ke vifal vidroh ke baad bharat ke prashasan kaa bhaar british sarkaar ne apane upar le liya. british bharat ke rup main british samrajya ke pramukh ang bharat ne mahatma gandhi ke netritv main ek lambe or mukhya rup se ahimsak swatantrata sangram ke baad 15 agast 1947 ko azaadi pai. 1950 main lagu hue naye samvidhan main ise saarvajanik vayask matadhikaar ke aadhaar par sthapit sanvaidhanik loktantric ganraajya ghoshit kar diya gaya or unaited kingdom kii tarz par westminster style kii sansadeey sarkaar sthapit kii gayi. ek sangheey raashtra, bharat ko 29 rajyon or 7 sangh shasit pradeshon main gathit kiya gaya he. lambe samay tak samaajawaadee aarthik nitiyon kaa paalan karne ke baad 1991 ke pashchaat bharat ne udaarikaran or vaishvikaran kii nayi nitiyon ke aadhaar par sarthak aarthik or saamaajik pragati kii he.
 2017 main bhartiya arthvyavastha maanak mulyon ke aadhaar par vishwa kaa chatha sabase bada or kray-shakti samta ke aadhaar par vishwa kii teesri sabase bady arthvyavastha he. 33 lakh varg kilometre kshetrafal ke saath bharat bhugolic kshetrafal ke aadhaar par vishwa kaa saatvaan sabase bada raashtra he. 1991 ke bazaar-aadhaarit sudhaaron ke baad bharat vishwa kii sabase tej viksit hoti badi arth-vyavasthaaon main se ek ho gaya he or ise ek nav-audyogikrut raashtra mana jaataa he. parantu bharat ke saamne abhi bhi garibi, bhrashtachar, kuposhan, aparyaapt saarvajanik swasthya-seva or aatankwaad kii chunautiyaan hai. aaj bharat ek vividh, bahubhashi, or bahu-jaateey samaaj he or bhartiya sena ek kshetreey shakti he.
 bharat ke do aadhikarik naam hai- hindi main bharat or angrezi main india . india naam kii utpatti sindhu nadi ke angreji naam "inds" se hui he. bharat naam, ek pracheen hindu samraat bharat jo ki manu ke vanshaj rishabhdev ke sabase bade bete the or jinaki katha shrimadbhagwat mahapurana main he, ke naam se liya gaya he. bharat shabd kaa matlab he aantarik prakaash yaa videk-rupi prakaash main leen. ek teesraa naam hindustan bhi he jiskaa arth hind kii bhoomi, yah naam visheshkar arab/iran main prachlit hua. iskaa samakaleen upyog kam or praayah uttari bharat ke liye hota he. iske atirikt bharatvarsh ko vaidic kaal se aryaavart "jambudweep" or "ajanabhadesh" ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa rahaa he. bahut pehle bharat kaa ek munhabola naam 'sone kii chidiya' bhi prachlit tha.
 bharat kaa rashtriya chihn sarnath sthit ashok stambh kii anukriti he jo sarnath ke sangrahalay main surakshit he. bharat sarkaar ne yah chihn 26 janvari 1950 ko apanaaya. usamen keval teen singh dikhaai padate hai, chauthaa singh drushtigochar nahin he. rashtriya chihn ke neeche devanagari lipi main 'satyamev jayate' ankit he.
 bharat ke rashtriya jhande main teen samaantar ytaakar pattiyaan hai. upar kii pattee kesaria rang kii, madhya kii pattee safed rang kii tathaa neeche kii pattee gahare hare rang kii he. jhande kii lambaai chaudaai kaa anupaat 3:2 kaa he. safed pattee par charkhe kii jagah sarnath ke singh stambh vaale dharmachakr anukriti ashok chakra he jiskaa rang gaharaa nila he. chakra kaa vyas lagbhag safed pattee ke chaudaai jitna he or usamen 24 are hai.rashtrabhasha: hindikavi rabindranath thakur dwaara likhit 'jan-gana-man' ke pratham ansh ko bharat ke rashtriya gaan ke rup main 24 janvari 1950 i. ko apanaaya gaya. saath-saath yah bhi nirnay kiya gaya ki bankimchandra chatarji dwaara likhit 'vande maataram' ko bhi 'jan-gana-man' ke samaan hi darja diya jayega, kyonki swatantraaa sangram main 'vande maataram' gaan janata kaa preranarot tha.
 bharat sarkaar ne desh bhar ke liye rashtriya panchang ke rup main shaq sanwat ko apanaaya he. iskaa pratham maas 'chait' he or varsh saamanyat: 365 din kaa he. is panchang ke din sthaayee rup se angreji panchang ke maas dinon ke anuroop baithte hai. sarkari kaaryo ke liye gregory calendar ke saath-saath rashtriya panchang kaa bhi prayog kiya jaataa he.
 pracheen hindu manyataon ke anusaar bharat ko ek sanathan raashtra mana jaataa he kyonki yah maanav-sabhyataa kaa pehla raashtra tha. shrimadbhagwat ke pancham skandh main bharat raashtra kii sthapana kaa varnan aata he. bhartiya darshan ke anusaar sristi utpatti ke pashchaat brahma ke maanas putr swayambhu manu ne vyavastha sambhaali. inke do putr, priyavrat or uttanpad the. uttanpad bhakt dhruv ke pita the. inhen priyavrat ke das putr the. teen putr balykaal se hi virakt the. is kaaran priyavrat ne prithvi ko saat bhaagon main vibhakt kar ek-ek bhaag prayek putr ko saunp diya. inhen main se ek the aagnidhra jinhen jambudweep kaa shashan kaary saunpa gaya. vriddhavastha main aagnidhra ne apane nau putron ko jambudweep ke vibhinn nau sthanon kaa shashan daayitv saunpa. in nau putron main sabase bade the naabhi jinhen himavarsh kaa bhu-bhaag mila. inhone himavarsh ko swayan ke naam ajanabh se jod kar ajanabhavarsh prachaarit kiya. yah himavarsh yaa ajanabhavarsh hi pracheen bharat desh tha. raja naabhi ke putr the rishabh. rishabhdev ke sau putron main bharat jyeshtha evam sabase gunwan the. rishabhdev ne vanprastha lene par unhen rajpaat saunp diya. pehle bharatvarsh kaa naam rishabhdev ke pita nabhiraj ke naam par ajanabhavarsh prasiddh tha. bharat ke naam se hi log ajanabhakhand ko bharatvarsh kehane lage.
 pashaan yug bheemabetkar madhya pradesh kii guphaayen bharat main maanav jivan kaa pracheenatam pramaan hai. pratham sthai bastiyon ne 9000 varsh purv swarup liya. yahi aage chal kar sindhu ghati sabhyataa main viksit hui, jo 2600 isa purv or 1900 isa purv ke madhya apane charam par thi.lagbhag 1600 isa purv arya bharat aae or unhone uttar bhartiya kshetron main vaidic sabhyataa kaa sutarapaat kiya. is sabhyataa ke strot veda or puraana hai. kintu arya-akraman-siddhant abhi tak vivadaspad he. lokmaanya bal gangadhar tilak sahit kuch vidwaanon kii manyata yah he ki arya bharatvarsh ke hi sthaayee nivasee rahe hai tathaa vaidic itihaas kareeb 75,000 varsh pracheen he. isi samay dakshin bharat main dravid sabhyataa kaa vikaas hota rahaa. donon jatiyon ne ek duusare kii khoobiyon ko apanaate hue bharat main ek mishrit-samskruti kaa nirmaan kiya.
 500 isvi purv kly baad kai swatantra rajya ban gaye. bharat ke praarambhik rajwanshon main uttar bharat kaa maury rajvansh ullekhneey he jiske pratapi samraat ashok kaa vishwa itihaas main vishesh sthaan he. 180 isvi ke armbh se madhya asia se kai akraman hue, jinke parinamswaroop uttar bhartiya upmahadweep main unani, shaq, parthi or antat kushaan rajvansh sthapit hue. teesri shatabdi ke aage kaa samay jab bharat par gupt vansh kaa shashan tha, bharat kaa "svarnim kaal" kahalaaya." dakshin bharat main bhinn-bhinn kaal-khandon main kai rajvansh chalukya, cher, chol, pallav tathaa pandya rahe. isa ke aas-paas sangam-sahitya apane charam par tha, jisamen tamil bhashaa kaa parivardhan hua. satvahanon or chalukyon ne madhya bharat main apana varchasv sthapit kiya. vigyaan, kala, sahitya, ganit, khagolashastra, pracheen praudyogiki, dharm, tathaa darshan inhen raajaaon ke shasankaal main fale-foole.
 12veen shatabdi ke prarambh main, bharat par islamic akramanon ke pashchaat, uttari va kendriya bharat kaa adhikansh bhaag dilli sultanate ke shasnadhin ho gaya; or baad main, adhikansh upmahadweep mughal vansh ke adheen. dakshin bharat main vijaynagar samrajya shaktishaalee niklaa. haalaanki, visheshtah tulanaatmak rup se, sangrakshit dakshin main anek rajya shesh rahe, athwa astitv main aaye. mughalon ke sankshipt adhikaar ke baad satrahavin sadi main dakshin or madhya bharat main maraathon kaa utkarsh hua. uttar pashchim main sikhon kii shakti main vruddhi hui.
 17veen shatabdi ke madhyakaal main portugal, dach, france, britain sahit anek europea deshon, jo bharat se vyapaar karne ke icchhuk the, unhone desh kii aatanrik shaskeey araajaktaa kaa phayda uthaaya angrej duusare deshon se vyapaar ke icchhuk logon ko rokane main safal rahe or 1840 tak lagbhag sampurna desh par shashan karne main safal hue. 1857 main british east india company ke viruddh asafal vidroh, jo bhartiya swatantrata ke pratham sangram se bhi janaa jaataa he, ke baad bharat kaa adhikansh bhaag sidhe angreji shashan ke prashasnic niyantran main aa gaya.
 beesvi sadi ke prarambh main aadhunik shiksha ke prasar or vishwapatal par badalati raajnneetik paristhitiyon ke chalate bharat main ek bauddhik aandolan kaa sutarapaat hua jisane saamaajik or raajnneetik staron par anek parivartanon evam aandolanon kii niv rakhi. 1885 main bhartiya rashtriya congress kii sthapana ne swatantrata aandolan ko ek gatiman swaroop diya. beesvin shatabdi ke prarambh main lambe samay tak swatantraaa prapti ke liye vishaal ahimsawadi sangharsh chalaa, jiskaa netritv mahatma gandhi, jo aadhikarik rup se aadhunik bharat ke 'rashtrapita' ke rup main sambodhit kiye jate hai, isi sadi main bharat ke saamaajik aandolan, jo saamaajik swatantryata prapti ke liye bhi vishaal ahimsawadi evam krantivaadee sangharsh chalaa, jiskaa netritv dow babasaheb ambedkar ne kiya, jo ‘aadhunik bharat ke nirmaataa’, ‘samvidhan nirmaataa' evam ‘daliton ke masiha’ ke rup main sambodhit kiye jate he. iske saath-saath chandrashekhar aaazaad, sardaar bhagat singh, sukhdev, raajaguru, netaji subhash chandra bose, vir saavarkar aadi ke netritv me chale krantikari sangharsh ke falswarup 15 agast, 1947 bharat ne angreji shashan se purnatah swatantraaa praapt kii. taduparant 26 janvari, 1950 ko bharat ek ganraajya bana.
 ek bahujaateey tathaa bahudharmik raashtra hone ke kaaran bharat ko samay-samay par sampradayik tathaa jaateey vidwesh kaa shikaar hona padaa he. kshetreey asantosh tathaa vidroh bhi haalaanki desh ke alag-alag hisson main hote rahe hai, par isaki dharmanirpekshata tathaa janatantrikta, keval 1975-77 ko chhod, jab tatkaaleen pradhanmantri indira gandhi ne apatkaal kii ghoshnaa kar di thi, akshunn rahi he.
 bharat ke padosi rashtron ke saath ansulajhe seemaa vivaad hai. iske kaaran ise chote paimaanon par yuddh kaa bhi saamana karnaa padaa he. 1962 main china ke saath, tathaa 1947, 1965, 1971 evam 1999 main pakistan ke saath ladaaiyaan ho chuki hai.
 bharat gutnirpeksh aandolan tathaa sanyukt raashtra sangh ke sansthaapak sadsy deshon main se ek he.
 1974 main bharat ne apana pehla parmaanu pareekshan kiya tha jiske baad 1998 main 5 or pareekshan kiye gayi. 1990 ke dashak main kiye gayi aarthik sudharikaran kii badaulat aaj desh sabase tezi se vikasshil rashtron kii suchi main aa gaya he.

 bharat kaa samvidhan bharat ko ek samprabhu, samaajawaadee, dharmanirpeksh, loktantrik ganraajya ghoshit karata he. bharat ek loktantric ganraajya he, jisaki dwisadanaatmak sansad westominster style kii sansadeey pranali dwaara sanchalit he. bharat kaa prashasan sangheey dhaanche ke antargat chalaaya jaataa he, jiske anusaar rashtriya str par centre sarkaar or rajya str par rajya sarkarein hai. centre or rajya sarkaron ke bich shaktiyon kaa bantwaaraa samvidhan main di gai ruparekha ke aadhaar par hota he. vartmaan main bharat main 29 rajya or 7 centre-shasit pradesh hai. centre shasit pradeshon main, sthaneey prashasan ko rajyon kii tulana main kam shaktiyaan praapt hoti hai. bharat kaa sarkari dhaanchaa, jisamen centre rajyon kii tulana main zyaada shashakt he, use aamtaur par ardh-sangheey kaha jaataa rahaa he, par 1990 ke dashak ke rajnatik, aarthik or saamaajik badlaavon ke kaaran isaki ruparekha dhire-dhire or adhik sangheey hoti ja rahi he.
 iske shashan main teen mukhya ang hai: nyaaypalika, karypalika or vyavasthapika.
 vyavasthapika sansad ko kehete hai, jiske do sadan hai – ucchsadan rajyasabha, athwa rajyaparishad or nimnasadan loksabhaa. rajyasabha main 245 sadsy hote hai jabaki loksabhaa main 545. rajyasabha ek sthai sadan he or iske sadasyon kaa chunaav, apratyaksh vidhi se 6 varshon ke liye hota he. rajyasabha ke zyaadaatar sadasyon kaa chayan rajyon kii vidhansabhaon ke sadasyon dwaara kiya jaataa he, or har duusare saal rajya sabha ke ek tihaai sadsy padmukt ho jate hai. loksabhaa ke 543 sadasyon kaa chunaav pratyaksh vidhi se, 5 varshon kii avadhi ke liye aam chunaavon ke madhyam se kiya jaataa he jinamein 18 varsh se adhik umr ke sabhi bhartiya naagarik matdaan kar sakate hai. iske ilaava 2 sadasyon ko raashtrapati anglo-indian samudaay main se namit kar sakati he, agar yah samudaay sansad main paryaapt rup se pratinidhitiv naa paa sakaa ho.
 karypalika ke teen ang hai – raashtrapati, uparashtrapati or mantrimandal. raashtrapati, jo raashtra kaa pramukh he, kii bhoomika adhiktar aanushthanik hi he. uske dayitvon main samvidhan kaa abhivyaktikaran, prastaavit kanoonon par apni sahmati dena or adhyadesh jari karnaa pramukh hai. vah bhartiya senaaon kaa mukhya senapati bhi he. raashtrapati or uparashtrapati ko ek apratyaksh matdaan vidhi dwaara 5 varshon ke liye chunaa jaataa he. pradhanmantri sarkaar kaa pramukh he or karypalika kii sari shaktiyaan usee ke paas hoti hai. iskaa chunaav rajnatik partiyon yaa gathbandhan ke dwaara pratyaksh vidhi se sansad main bahumat praapt karne par hota he. bahumat bane rahane kii sthiti main iskaa kaarykaal 5 varshon kaa hota he. samvidhan main kisi up-pradhanmantri kaa praavdhaan nahin he par samay-samay par isamen pherbadal hota rahaa he. mantrimandal kaa pramukh pradhanmantri hota he. mantrimandal ke prayek mantri ko sansad kaa sadsy hona anivaary he. karypalika sansad ko uttaradaayi hoti he, or pradhanmantri or unka mantrimandal lok sabha main bahumat ke samarthan ke aadhaar par hi apane karyaalay main bane rah sakate hai.
 bharat kii swatantra nyaaypalika kaa dhaanchaa tristareey he, jisamen sarvocch nyaayalay, jiske pradhan pradhan nyaayadhish he; 24 ucch nyaayalay or bahut sari nichali adaalatein hai. sarvocch nyaayalay ko apane mul nyaayadhikaar, or ucch nyaayalayon ke upar apiliy nyaayadhikaar ke maamalon, dono ko dekhne kaa adhikaar he. sarvocch nyaayalay ke mul nyayadhikaar main moulik adhikaron ke hanan ke ilaava rajyon or centre, or do yaa do se adhik rajyon ke bich ke vivaad aate hai. sarvocch nyaayalay ko rajya or kendriya kanoonon ko asanvaidhanik tehraane ke adhikaar he. bharat main 24 ucch nyaayalayon ke adhikaar or uttardaayitv sarvocch nyaayalay kii apeksha simit hai. samvidhan ne nyaaypalika ko vistrut adhikaar diye hai, jinamein samvidhan kii antim vyakhya karne kaa adhikaar bhi sammilit he.
 bharat vishwa kaa sabase bada loktantr he. bahudaleey pranali vaale is sansadeey ganraajya main chha: manyata-praapt rashtriya partiyaan, or 40 se bhi zyaada kshetreey partiyaan hai. bhartiya janata party, jisaki nitiyon ko kendriya-dakshinpanthi yaa ruudhivaadee mana jaataa he, ke netritv main centre main sarkaar he jiske pradhanmantri shri narendra modi hai. anya partiyon main sabase bady bhartiya rashtriya congress he, jise bhartiya raajnneeti main centre-vampanthi or udaar mana jaataa he. 2004 se 2014 tak centre main manmohan singh kii gathbandhan sarkaar kaa sabase bada hissaa congress party kaa tha. 1950 me ganraajya ke ghoshit hone se 1980 ke dashak ke ant tak congress kaa sansad main nirantar bahumat rahaa. par tab se rajnatik patal par bhaajapa or congress ko anya partiyon ke saath satta baantni paddy he. 1989 ke baad se kshetreey partiyon ke udai ne centre main gathbandhan sarkaron ke naye daur kii shuruyaat kii he.
 ganraajya ke pehle teen chunaavon main jawaharlal nehru ke netritv main congress ne aasaan jeet pai. 1964 main nehru kii mrityu ke baad laal bahadur shastri kuch samay ke liye pradhanmantri bane, or 1966 main unki khud kii maut ke baad indira gandhi pradhanmantri baniin. 1967 or 1971 ke chunaavon main jeetne ke baad 1977 ke chunaavon main unhen haar kaa saamana karnaa pada. 1975 main pradhanmantri rahete hue unhone rashtriya aapatkaal kii ghoshnaa kar di thi. is ghoshnaa or isse upji aam naaraazgi ke kaaran 1977 ke chunaavon main navgathit janata party ne congress ko haraa diya or purv main congress ke sadsy or nehru ke kebinet main mantri rahe morarji desai ke netritv main nai sarkaar bani. yah sarkaar sirf teen saal chali, or 1980 main hue chunaavon main jitkar indira gandhi phir se pradhanmantri baniin. 1984 main indira gandhi kii hatya ke baad unke bete rajeev gandhi congress ke netaa or pradhanmantri bane. 1984 ke chunaavon main jabardast jeet ke baad 1989 main navgathit janata dal ke netritv vaale rashtriya morcha ne vam morcha ke bahari samarthan se sarkaar banaai, jo keval do saal chali. 1991 ke chunaavon main kisi party ko bahumat nahin mila, parantu congress sabase bady party bani, or pi ve narsinha raav ke netritv main alpamat sarkaar bani jo apana kaarykaal puraa karne main safal rahi.
 1996 ke chunaavon ke baad do saal tak rajnatik uthal puthal kaa vakt rahaa, jisamen kai gathbandhan sarkarein ai or gai. 1996 main bhaajapa ne keval 13 din ke liye sarkaar banaai, jo samarthan naa milane ke kaaran gir gai. uske baad do sanyukt morche kii sarkarein ai jo kuch lambey vakt tak chali. ye sarkarein congress ke bahari samarthan se bani thim. 1998 ke chunaavon ke baad bhaajapa ek safal gathbandhan banane main safal rahi. bhaajapa ke atal bihari vajapeyi ke netritv main rashtriya janatantric gathbandhan naam ke is gathbandhan kii sarkaar pehali aisi sarkaar bani jisane apana paanch saal kaa kaarykaal puraa kiy. 2004 ke chunaavon main bhi kisi party ko bahumat nahin mila, par congress sabase bady party banake ubhari, or isane sanyukt pragatisheel gathbandhan ke naam se nayaa gathbandhan banaaya. is gathbandhan ne vampanthi or gaiyr-bhaajapa saansadon ke sahyog se manmohan sinh ke netritv main paanch saal tak shashan chalaaya. 2009 ke chunaavon main upie or adhik siten jeeta jiske kaaran yah samyavaadee dalon ke bahari sahyog ke bina hi sarkaar banane main kaamyaab rahaa. isi saal manmohan sinh jawaharlal nehru ke bad aise pehle pradhanmantri bane jinhe do lagaataar kaarykaal ke liye pradhanmantri banane kaa avasar praapt hua. 2014 ke chunaavon main 1984 ke baad pehali baar kisi rajnatik party ko bahumat praapt hua, or bhaajapa ne gujarat ke purv mukhyamantri narendra modi ke netritv main sarkaar banaai.

 1947 main apni swatantraaa ke baad, bharat ke adhikansh deshon ke saath sauhardpurn sambandh banaae rakhaa he. 1950 ke dashak main, bharat ne purajor rup se africa or asia main europea upniveshon kii swatantraaa kaa samarthan kiya or gut nirpeksh andolan main ek agrani kii bhoomika nibhaai. 1980 ke dashak main bharat do padosi deshon ke nimantran par, sena ke dwaara sankshipt sainya hastkshep kiya, ek srilanka me or dusraa maldive main. bharat ke padosi pakistan ke saath ek tanaav bharaa sambandh he or donon deshon ke bich chaar baar yuddh hua he, 1947, 1965, 1971 or 1999main. kashmir vivaad in yudhon kaa pramukh kaaran tha. 1962 ke bharat - china yuddh or pakistan ke saath 1965 ke yuddh ke baad bharat or soviet sangh ke saath sainya sambandhon me kaaphi badhotaree hui. 1960 ke dashak ke ant main soviet sangh bharat kaa sabase bada hathiyaar aapurtikarta ke rup main ubhari thi.
 rus ke saath samrik sambandhon ke alaava, bharat kaa isroyal or france ke saath vistrut raksha sambandh hai. haal ke varshon main, bharat ne kshetreey sahyog or vishwa vyapaar sanghathan ke liye ek dakshin asian association main prabhaavshaalee bhoomika nibhaai he. bharat ne 100,000 sainya or police karmiyon ko chaar mahadwipon bhar main sanyukt raashtra ke pantis shanti abhiyaanon main seva pradaan kii he. bharat ne vibhinn bahupaksheeya manchon, sabase khaskar purvi asia ke shikhar baithak or ji-8 5 main ek sakriy bhagidaari nibhaai he. aarthik kshetra main bharat kaa dakshin america, asia, or africa ke vikasshil deshon ke saath ghanishtha sambandh he.
 bharat main kai saare parv manaae jate hai, jisamen 26 janvari ko ganatantr diwas, 15 agast ko swatantraaa diwas, or 2 oktober ko gandhi jayanti puure desh main manaai jaati he. iske alaava anya parv rajyon ke anusaar hote hai.
