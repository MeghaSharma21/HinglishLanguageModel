 bihar rajya power holding company limited, bharat ke bihar rajya main karyrat ek rajya-swaamitv vaali vidyut viniman board he. iskaa naam pehle 'bihar rajya bijli board' tha. bihar rajya bijli board, 1958 main vidyut adhinium, 1948 ke tahat ek sanvidhik nigam ke rup main sthapit kiya gaya tha. navambar 2012 tak, biesaibi ke lagbhag 1700 adhikari or 14,850 karmchaaree hai. vidyut janan kii kshamta keval 530 megawatt kii he. biesaibi ko 2 agast, 2011 ko aprabhavi banaaya gaya tha. power finance corporation, biesaibi ke punargathan ke liye mukhya salaahkaar the.
 biesaibi ko 5 bhaagon main baanta gaya he- 
 17 april 2018 ko, mukhyamantri nitish kumaar kii adhyakshata main bihar rajya cabinet ne barauni thermal power stesan, kanti thermal power stesan or nabinagar supercritical thermal power project ko antipisi limited ko saunpane kii manjuuri de di. bihar state power jenereshan company limited ke ejeeniyar or karmiyon kii seva bijli company kii sancharan or vitaran kshetra ke liye le lee jaayegi. jenereshan company band nahin hogi. yah ab hidel or solar urja utpaadan ikaayiyon par kendrit hogi. prastaavit lakhisarai ke kajraa or bhagalpur ke pirpanti saura urjaghar antipisi ke hawaale hoga. baxur ke chausa bijlighar ke liye satluj jal vidyut nigam se samjhauta kiya gaya, chausa bijlighar ke liye emoyu kii avadhi 17 janvari, 2020 tak ke liye badha di gai. 
 mukhya lekh: barauni thermal power stesan
 unit kramank 1, 2 or 3 main 15 emdblyoo prayek or 50 megawatt ke unit kramank 4 or 5 ko sevanivrutt kiya jaataa he kyonki ve bahut puraane hai.
 110 megawatt kii unit 6 or 7 naveekaran or bharat heavi electricals dwaara anurakshan ke rakrakhaav ke adheen he, jo mul rup se in donon ikaayiyon ke upkarnon kii aapurti kii thi. rashtriya sam vikaas yojana ke tahat bharat heavi electricals limited ke dwaara kareeb chah sau karod rupee kii laagat se barauni thermal kii 110 megawatt kshamta vaali chhathi or saatvin ikai kaa aadhunikeekaran va navinikaran kiya ja rahaa he. varsh 2006 main band pade barauni thermal main saatvin ikai kaa varsh 2010 main tathaa chhathi ikai kaa jirnoddhar kaary varsh 2012 main shuru kiya gaya tha. abhi tak maatr saatvin ikai kaa kaary kisi tarah puraa kiya gaya he. barauni new ekstansion project kaa kaam varsh 2011 main shuru hua tha. vistaar yojana main 250 megawatt kshamta kaa do unit aathvi or nauveen kaa nirmaan kaary bharat heavi electricals limited ke dwaara varsh 2014 main puraa kiya janaa tha.
 is sanyantr main 500 megawatt kii nai kshamta badhaane kii yojana he. march 2013 main, centre sarkaar ne baruni thermal power plant ko koyala linkage pradaan karne kaa aashwaasan diya.
 mukhya lekh: kanti thermal power stesan
 kanti thermal power stesan bhi jorge farnandis thermal power plant stesan ke rup main janaa jaataa he. bihar ke bhartiya rajya kii rajdhani, patna se 90 kimi dur kaanti mujffarpur main sthit he. yah kanti bijli utpaada nigam limited dwaara sanchalit he, jo antipisi or biesaibi patna ke bich ek sanyukt udhyam he. antipisi ke 64.57% or biesaibi 35.43% ke saath sanyukt udhyam company ke bahutansh share hai. sanyantr 2003 ke baad se kaaryaatmak nahin rahaa he, haalanki 2013 ke ant tak teen safal pareekshan chalaane ne bijli ke vanijyik utpaadan kaa maarg prashast kiya he. november 2013 main nitish kumaar ne kaha ki kanti main ek or 500 emdblyoo bijli sanyantr sthapit kiya jayega. 195 megawatt kii pehali ikai biechiel dwaara march 2015 main 2x195 megawatt ke sanyantr main chaalu kii gai thi. 13 june 2016 ko 2 X 195 megawatt kii duusari ikai chaalu kii gai thi. mujffarpur thermal power stesan kii maujuuda kshamta 610 megawatt he.
 mukhya lekh: antipisi baadh
 antipisi baadh patna jile main rashtriya rajmarg -31 par baarah sab-divijan ke purv main keval chaar kilometre purv main sthit he. is pariyojna ko mega power project naam diya gaya he, or iskaa swaamitv bhartiya urja company neshnal thermal power corporation he.
 biechiel dwaara nirmit 1,320 megawatt baar charan -2 parichaalan chaalu he jabaki roosi form technopromaxport dwaara 1,980 megawatt baar charan -1 kaa nirmaan kiya ja rahaa he.
 mukhya lekh: antipisi aurangabad
 nabinagar super thermal power project bihar ke aurangabad jile ke nabinagar taluk main maajiyaan or ankora gaanvon main sthit ek koyala aadhaarit thermal power plant he. ise 1989 main bihar ke tatkaaleen mukhyamantri satyendra narayan sinha ne sankalpit kiya tha, jinhone prastaav sthapit karne kaa prastaav bheja tha. bihar ke aurangabad jile ke nabinagar main bharat ke tatkaaleen pradhan mantri rajeev gandhi ke liye antipisi kii super thermal power project; lekin pariyojna kamjor ho gai kyonki nimnlikhit rajya sarkarein iskaa paalan karne main naakaam raheen. 2007 main, manmohan singh kii sarkaar ne aakhirkaar manjuuri kaa tikat lagaaya.
 power plant kaa swaamitv nabinagar power jenerating company he - shuruyaat main antipisi limited or bihar state power holding company limited ke bich 50:50 sanyukt udhyam. nabinagar sanyantr main 4380 megawatt kii kshamta hogi. shuruyaat main pariyojna kii peedi kii kshamta 3960 megawatt thi, lekin 2016 main utpaadan kshamta 4380 megawatt tak badi thi.
 mukhya lekh: antipisi kahalgamv
 kahalgamv main antipisi ke super thermal power plant kaa kaam 1985 main shuru hua tha. march 1992 main 210 megawatt kshamtawali pehali unit kaa sanchaalan shuru hua. dhire-dhire isaki kshamta main izaafaa hota gaya. sanyantr kii kul sthapit kshamta 2340 megawatt he. plant main bijli utpaadan ke liye rojaanaa 35 hazaar se 50 hazaar ton koyale kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he, jisaki aapurti jharkhand sthit rajmahal kol mine se kii jaati he. plant se har saal kareeb 65 lakh ton fli aish nikalataa he. fli aish main silica, alumina, paara or aayran hote hai.
