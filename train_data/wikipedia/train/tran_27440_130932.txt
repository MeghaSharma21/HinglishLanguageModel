 dilli ajukeshan socity dilli main karyrat ek panjikrut saamaajik sanstha he jo puraani dilli kshetra main shaikshik rup se pichde alpsankhyak samudaay ke utthan ke liye kaam kar rahi he. sanstha ko 1951 main doctor zakir hossain ke netritv main sthapit kiya gaya tha. vartmaan main najeeb jang, kendriya vishvavidhyalay jamiya milia islamia ke kulapati iske adhyaksh hai.
 bharat ke vibhaajan ke baad is sanstha kii sthapana prasiddh shikshavidon or lokopkaaree sajjanon ne kii - jinamein doctor jakir hossain, pro mohammad mujeeb, mirza shahbaaj beg, em em zadi, mir mushtak ahamed, shafiq ur rahamaan kidawai, mufti atik ur rahamaan, doctor salaamat ullah, hakim abdul hamid or mirza mahmud beg shaamil the. iskaa gathan puraane anglo arabi college or skool ko naveen tathaa sthaayee rup pradaan karne ke liye va dilli ke musalamaanon kii gauravshaalee viraasat madrasa gaji ud deen ko sangrakshit karne ke liye hua.
