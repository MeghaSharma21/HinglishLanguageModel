 isisi champiens trophy 2013, 6 se 23 june 2013 ke madhya england or wales main prayojit ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket-pratiyogita thi. sabhi match teen nagaron main yojit kiye jaayenge : landan, birmingham or cardiff . isisi champiens trophy 2013 vijeta ko $20 lakh puraskaar raashi praapt hogi, jo pratiyogita kii shuruuaat tak sabase badi raashi he. bharat ne final main england ko 5 ran se haraa kar ye pratiyogita jeet lee. yah saatvin or antim isisi champiens trophy he jaisaa ki yah 2017 main isisi vishwa test se pratisthapit kii jaani he. tathaapi janvari 2014 main, isisi ne pushti kii agali champions trophy toornament 2017 main hogi or prastaavit vishwa test championships ko radd kar diya gaya he.
 isisi champiens trophy 2013 aath tiimon ke madhya kheli jane vaali pratiyogita he jo do samoohon main variyata or vibhakt kii gai he. prayek team apane samooh main prayek team ke saath khelegi. ankon ke aadhaar par prayek samooh se do timen semi-final main pahunchengi.
