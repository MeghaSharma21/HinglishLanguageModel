  dilli vishwavidhalay chihn
 acharya narendra dev mahavidhyalay dilli vishvavidhyalay kaa sanghatak he, jiskaa naam aadhunik bharat ke mahaan shikshashasthri acharya narendra dev ke naam par rakhaa gaya he. dilli sarkaar dwaara 1991 main sthapit evam purna vittiya sahaayta praapt yah dilli vishvavidhyalay kaa ekmaatr aisa mahavidhyalay he jahaan snaatak str par mukhya rup se vigyaan vishyon kii shiksha di jaati he. yah mahavidhyalay apane pratibhavan, anushasanpriya evam saamaajik kartavya ke prati sajag rahane vaale vidhyaarthiyon ke liye janaa jaataa he.
 is mahavidhyalay main vigyaan snaatak ke anek vishuddh evam anuprayukt vishyon kii shiksha di jaati he. vigyaan snaatak  ke vishay hai - bhautik vigyaan, rasayan vigyaan, electraniki vigyaan, vanaspati vigyaan, praani vigyaan, sanganak, evam ganit. 
 vigyaan snaatak paathyakram ke vishay hai - bhautik vigyaan, jivan vigyaan, anuprayukt bhautikee, anuprayuktt jivan vigyaan dilli vishvavidhyalay ke isi mahavidhyalay main pehali baar jaev chikitsa vigyaan main snaatak kii shiksha di jaani armbh hui. yah ekmaatr mahavidhyalay he jahaan par anuprayukt jivan vigyaan ke antargat reshmi keedon ke utpaadan kii shiksha di jaati he. vigyaan vishyon ke atirikt yahan par vaanijya snaatak bhi uplabdh he.
