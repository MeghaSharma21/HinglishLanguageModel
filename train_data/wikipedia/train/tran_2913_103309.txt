 kunchan nambiar malayalam ke aarmabhik kavi, vyangakaar the. 
 inkaa janm madhya kerala ke kochisthaan main hua tha. anpalappush ke devnarayan naamak shaasha ke sangrakshan main vahiin bas gaye or baad main travnakor ke shaasha maartand verma ke darbar main pravisht hue. apane yauvankaal ke praarambhik dinon main kunchan nambiar ne bhinn shaileyon main kavyrachana kii. unki praarambhik kavitaaon main bhagavatddoot, bhagwatam, nalcharitam or chanakyasutram ke naam ginaae ja sakate hai. vah naveen evam adbhat malayalam kavyastylee 'tukkalppatte' ke pravarttak hai. tukkal drut gati se prachaalit karanevaala nrity he jisamen nartak swayan padhyabaddh kahaniyon kaa gayan karat he. nambiar ne lagbhag saath tukkal kavitaayen likhi hai jinamein kalyansongadhikm, karttvirarjunvijaym, kiraatam sabhapraveshm, tripuradahanam, hariniswayamvaram, rugminiswayamvaram, pradoshmahatmyam, syamantakam evam ghoshyatra sammilit hai. nambiar ne apni tukkal kavitaaon main namr kavy-style kaa vikaas kiya he jisane samaaj ke sabhi virgon ko aakrisht kiya he or jiske dwaara unhone samaaj kii vaastaviktaaon ko pratibimbit karne kaa praytn kiya he. yadhyapi unhone itihaas evam puraana se hi apni kavitaaon ke liye kahaniyon kaa chunaav kiya he tathaapi samakaleen saamaajik jivan ke prasag main hi unhen chitrit kiya he. hasya evam vyang ke bhi ve mahaan kavi maane ja sakate hai. unhone malayalam main ahamkaari saamanton, bhrasht karmchaariyon, lobhi or striparayan brahmanon ore tuchh nairon ityadi samast shrenee ke logon par vyang kiya he. unki anek padoktiyaan sarsaadhaaran main yathesht prachlit hai or unhone lokoktiyon kaa sthaan grahan kar liya he.
