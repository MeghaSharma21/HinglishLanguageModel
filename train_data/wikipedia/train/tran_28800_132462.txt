 tripitak
 abhidhammpitak ek buddha granth he. yah un teen granthon main se ek he jo tripitak banaate he. is granth main vishleshanyukt deshna or dharmik vyakhya sangrahit he.
 parampara main kaha gaya he ki buddha ne apane gyaan ke turant baad abhimamma ko sochaa tha, phir kuch saal baad devataaon ko sikhaaya. baad main buddha ne ise sariputta ko doharaaya, jisane ise apane shishyon ko saunp diya. yah parampara paariwaar main bhi spasht he, vinay pitaka ke bahut der se, jo buddha kii prashansa kii ek antim kavita main ullekh karti he ki is sabase achche jeev, sher ne teen pitaakon ko sikhaaya. haalanki, vidwaan aamtaur par buddha kii mrityu ke baad 100 se 200 saal baad teesri shatabdi isa purv ke aasapaas kuch samay paidaa karne ke liye abhimamma kaary karte hai. isliye, saat abhimamma kaaryon kaa aamtaur par vidwaanon dwaara daawa kiya jaataa he ki buddha ke shabdon kaa pratinidhitiv na karen, balki shishyon or vidwaanon ke un logon kaa pratinidhitiv na karen. rupert gethin ne haalanki kaha ki abhimmaa paddati ke mahatvapurn tatv shaayad buddha ke jeevankaal main waapas aa jaaenge. e ke worder or peter harvey donon ne mataka ke liye shuruaati tithiyon kaa suzav diya jis par abibimmaa kitaaben aadhaarit hai. abhidam ne sutta, ke vistaar ke rup main shuruyaat kii lekin baad main swatantra siddhant viksit kiye. canan ke aakhri bade vibhaajan ke rup main, abhimamma pitak ke paas ek chacord itihaas tha. ise mahasanghika skool  or kai anya skulon dwaara cannical ke rup main sweekaar nahin kiya gaya tha. abhyamma pitak ke bheetar ek anya skool main khudaaka nikayaa kaa adhikansh hissaa shaamil tha. iske alaava, abhimmaa kaa paali sanskaran sakhti se therawada sangrah he, or anya buddha vidyalayon dwaara manyata praapt abhimamma kaaryon ke saath bahut kam he. vibhinn praarambhik vidyalayon ke vibhinn abhimmaa darshanon par siddhant par koi samjhauta nahin he or 'vibhajit buddha dharm'  kii avadhi se sambandhit he. paali canan ke shuruaati granthon main abhimamma pitak ke granthon kaa koi ullekh nahin he. pehali buddha parishad kii kuch reporton main abhimmaa kaa bhi ullekh nahin kiya gaya he, jo vinay ke granthon or paanch nickes yaa chaar aagaama ke astitv kaa ullekh karte hai, haalanki yah dhyaan diya ja saktaa he ki aadarneey pehali parishad main hone se pehle abhyamma main sabase pehle buddha ke paas paarit kiya gaya tha . anya khaaton main abhimamma shaamil he. sarvastiv vidhyalay ke abhyarth pitt ke vipareet theravaddi abhimamma pitak main, aupchaarik siddhantankan anupasthit he, or dharmon kii aupchaarik sthiti kaa sawaal ek mahatvapurn mudda bana hua he. sabhaav kii dharna kaa upyog keval thrawadin granthon main kiya jaataa he. kshanikta kaa siddhant bhi therawada vichaaron ke liye ek der se joda gaya he. yah keval baudhdhaghasa ke samay prakat hota he
 iskaa vistaar is prakaar he-
