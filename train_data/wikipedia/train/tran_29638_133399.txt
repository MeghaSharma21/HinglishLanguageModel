 kisi grah ko surya kii parikrama karne se jo bal praapt hota he. is prakaar jo bal praapt hota he. use cheshtaa bal kehete he. 
 surya ke paas grah apni kaksha main ghoomte hue sadaiv margi hote he. parantu prithvi se ve kabhi kabhi vakri prathit hote he. cheshtha bal nikaalane ke liye cheshtaa kendra nikaala jaataa he. cheshtaa kendra main 3 jamaa karne ke baad cheshtaa bal praapt hota he.
