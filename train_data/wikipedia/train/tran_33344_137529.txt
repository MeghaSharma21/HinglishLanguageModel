 sankisha athwa sankisa bharatvarsh ke uttar pradesh rajya ke farrukhabad jile ke pakhnaa railway stesan se saat mile dur kaali nadi ke tat par, buddha-dharm sthaan he, iskaa pracheen naam sankashya he, kehete hai buddha bhagwaan swarg se utar kar yaheen par aaye the, jan bhi ise apana tirth sthaan maanate he, terahaven tirthankar vimalnathji kaa yah kevalgyaan sthaan mana jaataa he, vartmaan sankisha ek tile par basaa chota sa gaanv he, tila bahut dur tak faila hua he, or kila kehalaata he, kile ke bheetar eenton ke dher par bisahari devi kaa mandir he, paas hi ashok stambh kaa sheersh he, jis par hathi kii murti nirmit he, atataiyon ne hathi kii sound ko tod daalaa he.
 sankisa uttar pradesh ke farrukhabad ke nikat sthit aadhunik sankis gram se samikrut kiya jaataa he haalanki bhugolic rup se yah janapad eta main aata he. kaningham ne apni kriti 'da enshant jiography of india' main sankisa kaa vistaar se varnan kiya he. sankisa kaa ullekh mahabharat main kiya gaya he
 us samay yah nagar paanchal kii rajdhani kampilya se adhik dur nahin tha. mahajanapad yug main sankisa paanchal janapad kaa prasiddh nagar tha. buddha anushruti ke anusaar yah vahi sthaan he, jahaan buddha, indra evam brahma sahit swarna athwa ratna kii sidhiyon se trayastrunsa swarg se prithvi par aaye the. is prakaar gautam buddha ke samay main bhi yah ek khyaati praapt nagar tha.
 mahatma buddha kaa aagamanisi nagar main mahatma buddha ne apane priy shishy anand ke kehane par yahan aaye va sangh main striyon kii pravrujya par lagaayi gayi roak ko todaa tha or bhikshuni utpalavarna ko diksha dekar buddha sangh kaa dwaar striyon ke liye khol diya gaya tha. buddha granthon main is nagar kii gananaa us samay ke bees pramukh nagaron main kii gayi he. prachinkaal main yah nagar nishchay hi kaafii bada rahaa hoga, kyonki isaki nagar bhitti ke avshesh, jo aaj bhi hai, lagbhag chaar mile kii paridhi main hai. chini yatri fahyaan paanchavin shatabdi ke pehle dashak main yahan mathura se chalakar aayaa tha or yahan se kanyakubj, shravasti aadi sthanon par gaya tha. usne sankisa kaa ullekh seng-kyi-shi naam se kiya he. usne yahan hinayan or mahayan sampradaayon ke ek hazaar bhikshuon ko dekha tha. kaningham ko yahan se skandagupt kaa ek chaandi kaa sikaa mila tha.
 vishaal 'singh' pratimasatvin shatabdi main yuvanachwang ne yahan 70 foot oonchaai stambh dekha tha, jise samraat ashok ne banwaaya tha. us samay bhi itnaa chamakdaar tha ki jal se bhigaa-sa jaan padataa tha. stambh ke sheersh par vishaal singh pratimaa thi. usne apane vivaran main is vicitra tathy kaa ullekh kiya he ki yahan ke vishaal math ke sameep nivas karne vaale brahmanon kii sankhya kai hazaar thi.
