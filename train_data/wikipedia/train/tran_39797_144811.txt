 gujarat rajya ke dakshini bhaag main jwariya shakti paidaa karne tathaa pine, sinchai or audyogic uddeshyon ke liye taaja pani kii ek vishaal jalashay kii sthapana ke liye khambhaat kii khadi ke paar kalpasar pariyojna antargat ek baandh ke nirmaan kii pariklpana kii gai he. is band ke upar ek das len sadak kaa link bhi banaaya jayega jo saurashtra or dakshin gujarat ke bich kii duuri ko bahut kam kar degaa.
 rajya sarkaar ne ek vijyapti main kaha he ki is pariyojna par 55,000 karod rupee kharch hoga or iske san 2020 tak puraa hone kii sambhaavna he. is taaje pani kii vishaal jalashay kii jal bhandaaran kshamta 16,791 million cubic meter hogi. khambhaat kii khadi ke paar 35 kimi lanbaa yah baandh bhavnagar main ghogha or bharuch jile ke hansot ko aapas main jodega jisase in donon kii bich kii duri 225 kimi kam ho jaayegi | is par 5880 megawatt kshamta kaa ek jwariya vidyut utpaadan ghar sthapit hoga.
