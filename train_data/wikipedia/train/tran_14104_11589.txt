 bharat bhushan hindi ke kavi evam sukumaar geetkaar the.
 bharat bhushan kaa janm uttarapradesh ke meerut main hua tha. inhone hindi main snatakottar shiksha arjeet kii or praadhyaapan ko jeevikavriti ke rup main apanaaya. ek shikshak ke taur par kariyar kii shuruyaat karne vaale bharat bhushan baad main kavy kii duniya main aae or chaa gaye. unki saikadon kavitaaon va geeton main sabase charchit 'ram kii jalsamadhi' rahi. unhone teen kavy sangrah likhe. pehla sagar ke sip varsh 1958 main, doosraa ye asangati varsh 1993 main or teesraa mere chuninda geet varsh 2008 main prakashit hua. varsh 1946 se manch se judne vaale bharat bhushan mrityu se kuch maheenon purv tak manch se geeton kii rasdhaar bahaate rahe. unhone mahadevi verma, ramdhaari singh 'dinakar' jaise kavi-sahityakaron ke saath bhi manch saajha kiya. dilli main 27 farvari 2011 ko yojit kavi sammelan main unhone antim baar bhaag liya tha.
 inkaa geet sangrah 'sagar ke sip' he. iske atirikt tamaam kavitaayen patr-patrikaon main prakashit hoti rahati hai. 'ram kii jal samadhi' inaki bahucharchit kavita he.
