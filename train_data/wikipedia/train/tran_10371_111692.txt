 shalibhadra suri ek kavi the jo 'bharteshwar bahubali raas' naamak granth ke rachayita hai. is rachana ke do sanskaran milte hai. pehla praachya vidya mandir baroda se prakashit kiya gaya he tathaa doosraa "raas' or rasaanwayi kavy' main prakashit hua he. kriti main rachanakaal san. 1231 vi. diya hua he. isaki chand sankhya 203 he. isamen jain tirthankar rishabhdev ke putron bharteshwar or bahubali main rajgaddi ke liye hue sangharsh kaa varnan he.
