 chaand ek prasiddh hindi patrika thi jiskaa prakaashan san 1923 main elahabaad se hua. iske sampadak nand gopal singh sahagal, mahadevi verma, nand kishor tiwari rahe. kuch dinon tak iskaa sampadan munshi navjadik laal ne kiya tha. is patrika main nari jivan se sambandhit charchaa adhik rahati thi. ‘chaand’ kaa marwani ank apane samay main bahucharchit rahaa. saahitiyik hote hue bhi is main samaaj sudhaar kii pravriti balwati rahi. iskaa ek visheshank ‘phaansi’ naam se bhi main prakashit hua tha jo anek deshbhakton ke liye prerana kaa strot bana.
