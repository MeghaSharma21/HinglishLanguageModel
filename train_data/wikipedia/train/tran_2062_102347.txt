 mahaan satyanarayan or suryanarayan dwaara nirmit tathaa esarmanathan dwaara nirdeshit 1983 kii ection naatak hindi film he. film main amitabh bachchan teen abhinay bhoomikaaon main hai or unke saath wahidaa rahamaan, praveen bobi, zeenat aman, ashok kumaar, amjad khan, kadar khan, aruna irani, sujeet kumaar tathaa shakti kapur hai. film kaa sangeet arady burman ne diya he.
 vikram singh hanikaarak peya padaarthon ke rup main fansaaya gaya vyakti he jise uske vyapaar sahyogi rajan ne fansaaya. jail se chhutate hi vah rajan ko maar detaa he or apane purv vakil amit ko fansaata he. amitabh ko bhaagane ke liye majaboor kiya jaataa he jabaki uski patni do judwa bacchon guru or shankar ko janm detii he. durbhagyavash, use uske ek putr guru se dur kar diya jaataa he or unka putr banjh jode dwaara chura liya jaataa he. apane pati ke saath bhaagate huye tathaa apane ek putr ko khone ke baad janaki shankar ko sambhalati he. amit sochataa he ki uski patni janaki kaa nidhan ho chukaa he atah vah punah ek saamaanya jivan vyapan karne ke liye rana ranvir naam se apni dattak putree manju ke saath rahata he. janaki duusare nagar main shankar ke saath rahati he jo ek pulish adheekshak ban jaataa he. guru kaa paalan-poshan banjh dampati ne kiya jo baad main ek naatak abhinayta ban jaataa he or rita kaa peechha karata he. manju ko shankar se pyaar ho jaataa he, jabaki use is baat kii jaankaree nahin thi ki wo rana ranvir kaa khoyaa hua putr he. ant main sabhi mil jate hai tathaa punah ek khushhaal parivaar ban jaataa he.
