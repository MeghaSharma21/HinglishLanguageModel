 puning mandir jise vastutah vishaal buddha mandir kaha jaataa he, buddha chengde hebai praant, china main mandir parisar he. yah king rajvansh main kwananong samraat ke shasankaal ke dauraan 1755 main banaaya gaya tha. yah chengde mountain resort ke paas he or putuo xongcheng mandir ke samaan prasiddh he, puning mandir chengde ke "aath bahari mandiron" main se ek he.
 pinging mandir kaa saame math ke baad, tibet main pavitra lamaavadi sthal lhasa main potola pales ke baad kaa putuo xongcheng mandir kaa chithran kiya gaya tha. saamne kaa mandir chini style main banaaya gaya tha, haalanki mandir parisar main chini or tibeti sthapaty style kaa prayog kiya gaya hai. puning mandir main bodhisattva awalokiteshwar kii duniya kii sabase oonchi lakdi kii murti he., isliye ise aksar vishaal buddha mandir naamak upnaam diya jaataa he. mandir ke jatil vaishishtay is prakaar hai - sabhamandap, mandap, mrudang minar or ghanti minar.
 
 18 veen shatabdi ke baad se, king rajvansh ke dauraan, uttar pashchimi aadhunik china se failaay hue hai. yah murti paanch prakaar kii lakdi se banaai gai he, jisamen deodaar, saru, alm, sanobar or linden shaamil he.
 1994 tak, chengde mountain resort or chengde ke aath bahari mandir ko sanyukt raashtra shiksha, vigyaan or saanskrithik sanghathan vishwa dharohar sthal ke rup main sthapit kiya gaya tha. aaj puning mandir paryatak-aakarshan or sthaneey utsavon kii ek jagah he.
