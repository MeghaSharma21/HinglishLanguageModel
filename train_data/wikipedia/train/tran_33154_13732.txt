 shri rashtriya rajput karani sena 2006 main sthapit ek rajput jati sanghathan he. yah jaipur, rajasthan, bharat main aadhaarit he. ye samooh kathith "rashtriya ekta" kii himaayat karte hai or jati-kendrit sakaaratmak bhedbhaav or "bhrashtachar" kaa virodh karte hai. satish bhadauria is sanghathan ke agua he.
 samooh sanjay lila bhansaali dwaara nirmit va nirdeshit film padmavati kaa virodh kar rahe hai kyonki inke anusaar film main itihaas se chhedchhad kii gai he. samooh ne bhansaali or abhinetri deepika padukon ke viruddh hinsak krity karne kii dhamkee di he.
