 baadh besalt aise bhayanak jwalamukheey visphot yaa visphoton kii shrunkhlaa kaa nateeja hota he jo kisi samudri farsh yaa gharti ke vistrut kshetra par besalt lava failaa de, yani vahaan lava kii baadh failaakar use jamane par besalt kii chattaanon se dhak de. bahut hi bade baadh besalt ke pradeshon ko udbhedan kaha jaataa he, jiskaa ek mahatvapurn udhaaharan bharat kaa dakkan udbhedan he. prithvi ke pichley 25 karod varshon ke itihaas main baadh besalt kii gyaarah ghatnaayen raheen hai, jinhone vishwa main kai pathaar or parvatamalas nirmit kii hai. hamaare grah ke itihaas kii paanch mahavilupti ghatnaon ke peeche bhi asi baadh besalt ghatnaon ke hone kaa sandeh he or sambhav he ki prithvi par kshudragrah prahaar se bhi baadh besalt ghatnaayen biit sakati hai.
