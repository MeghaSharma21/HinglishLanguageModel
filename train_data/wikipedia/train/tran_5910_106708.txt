 lans nayak albert ekka ek bhartiya sainik the jo bharat-pakistan yuddh 1971 main hili kii ladaai main veergati ko praapt ho gaye the. inhen maranoparant paramveer chakra se sammanit kiya gaya tha.
 albert ekka kaa janm 27 disambar, 1942 ko jharkhand ke gumla jila ke dumri blaak ke jari gaanv main hua tha. unke pita kaa naam julius ekka, maan kaa naam mariyam ekka or patni kaa naam balamdin ekka tha. unhone praarambhik shiksha si si skool patratoli se kii thi or madhyamik pariksha bhikhmpur middle skool se paas kii thi. inkaa janm sthal jari gaanv channpur tehsil main padane vaala ek adivasee kshetra he jo jharkhand rajya kaa hissaa he. albert kii dili ichha bhartiya sena main jane kii thi, jo disambar 1962 ko puri hui. 
 unhone sena main bihar regiment se apana kaary shuru kiya. baad main jab 14 gards kaa gathan hua, tab albert apane kuch saathiyon ke saath vahaan sthanantarit kar kiye gaye. albert ek achche yoddha to the hi, yah hockey ke bhi achche khilaadi the. bharat-pakistan yuddh 1971 main albert ekka veerta, shaury or sainik hunar kaa pradarshan karte hue apane ikai ke sainikon kii raksha kii thi. is abhiyaan ke samay ve kaaphi ghayal ho gayi or 3 disambar 1971 main is duniya se vidaa ho gaye. bharat sarkaar ne inke balidaan ko dekhte hue maranoparant sainikon ko diye jane vaale ucchtam sammaan paramveer chakra se sammanit kiya tha.
