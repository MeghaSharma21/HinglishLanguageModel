 asian olympic parishad asia main khelon kii sarvocch sanstha he or asia ke 45 deshon kii rashtriya olympic samitiyaan isaki sadsy he. iske vartmaan adhyaksh shaikh fahd al-sabaa hai. parishad ke andar sabase puraani rashtriya olympic samiti japan kii he jise anterrashtriya olympic samiti kii manyata 1912 main mili thi, jabaki purvi timore kii rashtriya olympic samiti sabase nai he, jo 2003 main isaki sadsy bani. e. o. pa kaa mukhyalay kuwait nagar, kuwait main sthit he.
 nimnlikhit desh asian olympic samiti ke sadsy hai. koshtak main us desh kaa ai o si kuta or kab us desh kii rashtriya olympic samiti astitv main ai diya gaya he. yadi nirmaan varsh ke baad us desh kii raa. o. sa. ko manyata mili to yah bhi diya gaya he.
