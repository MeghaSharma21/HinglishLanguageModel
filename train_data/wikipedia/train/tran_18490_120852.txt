 lipulekh laa yaa lipulekh darra himalay kaa ek pahari darra he jo bharat ke uttarakhand rajya ke kumaun kshetra ko tibet ke taklakot shahar se jodta he. yah prachinkaal se vyapaariyon or tirthayatriyon dwaara bharat or tibet ke bich aane-jane ke liye prayog kiya ja rahaa he. yah darra bharat se kailash parvat va maansarovar jane vaale yatriyon dwaara vishesh rup se istemaal hota he.
 dhyaan den ki 'laa' shabd tibeti bhashaa main 'darre' kaa arth rakhata he. qaayade se is darre ko 'lipulekh darra' yaa 'lipulekh laa' kahana chaiye. ise 'lipulekh laa darra' kahana 'darre' shabd ko dohraane jaisaa he, lekin yah prayog phir bhi prachlit he.
