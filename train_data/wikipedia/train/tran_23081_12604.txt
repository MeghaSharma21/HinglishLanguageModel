 thanka chitrakala bhartiya, nepali tathaa tibeti samskruti kii anukaam misaal he. is ke madhyam se tibeti dharm, samskruti evam darshanik mulyon ko abhivyakt kiya jaataa rahaa he. is kaa nirmaan saamanyat: sooti vastra ke dhule hue katal car kiya jaataa he. 
 mahayan or bajrayaan buddha dharm mai thanka kaa bahut bada sthaan he. yah chitrakala bina kohi bhi gumba yaa anya dharmik sthal adhura hota he. 
 thanka nirmaan ke liye ek vastra kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. vastra kai madhya bhaag mai pramukh dev/devi yaa guru kaa chitra hota he or unke charon or un se sambandhit karyou ko darshaya jaataa he.
