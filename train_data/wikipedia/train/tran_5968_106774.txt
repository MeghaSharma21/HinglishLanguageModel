 angika ek bhashaa he jo bihar ke purvi, uttari va dakshini bhaagon,jharkhand ke uttar purvi bhaagon or pan. bengal ke pashchhim bhaagon main boli jaati he jisamen godda, sahibganj, pakur, dumka, devgarh, koderma, giridih jaise jile sammilit hai. yah bhashaa bihar ke bhi purvi bhaag main boli jaati he jisamen bhagalpur, munger, khagadiya, begusarai, purniya, katihaar, arriya aadi sammilit hai. yah nepal ke tarai bhaag main bhi boli jaati he. angika bhartiya arya bhashaa he. 
 angika hind-europea bhashaa-parivaar parivaar ke andar aati he. ye hind arya upashakha ke antargat vargeekrut he. hind-arya bhashaayen wo bhashaayen hai jo sanskrit se utpann hui hai. urdu, kashmiri, bengali, oriya, punjabi, romani, marathi, maithili, nepali jaisi bhashaayen bhi hind-arya bhashaayen hai.
 angika shabd ang se bana he. angapradesh main boli jane vaali bhashaa ko angika naam diya gaya.angika ko log chhechhaa ke naam se bhi jaanate hai.
 angika bhashaa main nirmit pehali film khagadiya vaali bhauji 2007 ko bihar main pradarshit kiya gaya. sunil chaila bihari ne nai angika film banane ke bare main soch rahen hai. wo jab baankaa aaye the to unhone is vishay par dainik jaagran se baatcheet kii thi. unhon ne kaha tha kii logo ke samaksh angika ke ashleel chalchitr or sangeet parosa ja rahaa he jisase angika kii chhavi karaab ho rahi he. unhone ashleel sangeet banane waalon par nishaanaa saadhaa tha. ek nai film ang putr april 2010 ko pradarshit hui thi, jasme angika ke lok geet main maahir sunil chaila bihari ji ne mukhya bhoomika nibhaayaa tha.
