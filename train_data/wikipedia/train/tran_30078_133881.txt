 tarabai modak kaa janm mumbai main hua tha. unhone 1914 main mumbai vishvavidhyalay se snaatak kiya. unki shaadi shri modak se hue jo ek vakil the or baad main unhen 1921 main unase talaak le liya. unhone rajkot main mahila college ke principal ke rup main kaam kiya. purvaskuli shiksha main unke kaam ke liye unhen 1962 main padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. vah bhartiya rashtriya congress ke sadsy the. unke jivan par aadhaarit ek naatak banaaya gaya,'ghar teghancha hava' ratnakar matkaari dwaara nirmit kiya gaya tha.
