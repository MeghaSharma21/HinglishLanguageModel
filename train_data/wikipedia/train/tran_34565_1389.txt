 hormuz jaldmarumadhya pashchim asia kii ek pramukh jalsandhi he jo iran ke dakshin main faras kii khadi ko oman kii khadi se alag karata he. iske dakshin main sanyukt arab emirate or oman kaa musandam naamak bahikshetra hai. tel ke niryaat kii drushti se yah jaldamru bahut mahatvapurn he kyonki iraq, qatar tathaa iran jaise deshon kaa tel niryaat yaheen se hota he. apane sabase kam chaude sthaan par iske donon taton main 39 kilometre kii duuri he.
