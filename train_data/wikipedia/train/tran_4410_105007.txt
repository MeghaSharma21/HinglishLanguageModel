 mela hindi bhashaa kii ek film he jo 1948 main pradarshit hui.
 mohan or manju kii shaadi tay hoti he. mohan shahar main gahane kharidne jaataa he to use chor peet daalate hai. mohan aspataal main hota he, peeche se gaanv main panchayat faisla karti he ki manju kii shaadi kisi or se kar di jaae. mohan vaapis pahunchataa he to manju vidhwaa ho chuki hoti he or apane pati ke bacchon kii dekhbhaal kar rahi hoti he.
 dilip kumar, nargis
