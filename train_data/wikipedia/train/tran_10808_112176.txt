 mangal baaki grahon kii bhaanti kundalee ke baarah bhaavon main se kisi ek bhav main sthit hota he. baarah bhaavon main se kuch bhav aise hai jahaan mangal kii sthiti ko mangali dosha ke rup main liya jaataa he.
 kundalee main jab lagn bhav, chaturth bhav, saptam bhav, ashtum bhav or dwadash bhav main mangal sthit hota he tab kundalee main mangal dosha mana jaataa he. saptam bhav se ham daampaty jivan kaa vichaar karte hai. ashtum bhav se daampaty jivan ke mangali sukh ko dekha jaataa he. mangal lagn main sthit hone se saptam bhav or ashtum bhav donon bhaavon ko drushti detaa he. chaturth bhav main mangal ke sthit hone se saptam bhav par mangal kii chaturth purna drushti padati he. dwadash bhav main yadi mangal sthit he tab ashtum drushti se saptam bhav ko dekhtaa he.
 iske atirikt sukhi daampaty jivan ke liye ham paanch baton kaa vichaar karte hai -
 jyotish main in paanchon baton kaa pratinidhitiv lagn, chaturth, saptam, ashtum tathaa dwadash bhav karte hai. isiilie in paanchon bhaavon main mangal kii sthiti ko mangali dosha kaa naam diya gaya he. 
 vivaah bhav main mangal evam anya grahon kii yuti
 kundali main mangalik yoga kii aanaline jaanch
 mangali hona abhishaap nahin!
