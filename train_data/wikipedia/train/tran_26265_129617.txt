 javed ali ek bhartiya parshvagayak he jo san 2000 se hindi filmon main gane ga rahe hai. inhone 2007 main lokapriy gane ek din teri yaadon main jo ki naqaab film kaa gaanaa tha tathaa 2008 main bani jod akbar kaa gaanaa jashn-i-baharan bhi kaaphi lokapriy hua tha. ye hindi ke alaava bengali ,oriya ,tamil ,telegu bhashaa main bhi parshvagayak he. javed ali 2011 main zee tivi par chalane vaale sa re ga maa paa little champs main jaj bhi rah chuke he. haal hi main inhone vazeer film main maula gaanaa gaaya he. 
 javed ali kaa janm dilli main 1982 main hua tha inke pita prasiddh qavalli ke gayak he. ye bachapan se hi gayan main nipun the.
