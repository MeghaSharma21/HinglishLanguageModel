 darkest ower 2017 kii ek british yuddh naatak film he, jise jo rhite dwaara nirdeshit or anthony macarton dwaara likhaa gaya he. is film main abhinayta geri oldman ne winston churchill kaa kirdaar nibhaayaa he, or pradhanmantri ke apane shuruaati dinon kaa chithran he, jis samay naji germany ne puure pashchimi europe par kabza kiye hue tha, or dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke dauraan united kingdom ko parajeet karne kii dhamkee de rahaa tha, jisase sarkaar ke ucchtam str par, joki hitler ke saath shanti sandhi karnaa chaahate the or churchill, jinhone inkaar kar diya ke bich takaraav hua. film main christin scot thomas, lily james, ben mendelosan, stephen dilne or ronaald pikup ne bhi abhinay kiya hai.
 is film kaa pradarshan teluraide film mahotsav evam toronto antarrashtriya film mahotsav main kiya gaya tha. 22 navambar 2017 ko sankshipt pradarshan ke baad film ko sanyukt rajya america main 22 disambar ko purna rup se pradarshit kiya gaya, or united kingdom main 12 janvari ko pradarshit kiya gaya. 90wein academy puraskaar main ise sarvshereshth film sahit ise chah namakan praapt hue he. or 71wein british academy film puraskaar main ise 9 namakan praapt hue, jisamen do puraskaar jite gayi.
