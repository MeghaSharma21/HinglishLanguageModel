 tu stage tu orbot  prakshepan yaan ek antariksh yaan hota he, jisamen do alag charnon ke dwaara adeshiy kakshiya veg ko praapt karne ke liye lagaataar pranodan pradaan kiya jaataa he. yah teen charan ke kaksha lanchar or ekal charan ke liye kaksha lanchar kaa madhyavarti he.
 liftof main pehla charan vaahan ko tej karne ke liye jimmedaar hota he. kuch bindu par doosraa charan pehle charan se khisak kar alag ho jaataa he or apni shakti se kaksha ke liye udaan jari rakhata he.
 ekal charan kii tulana main aisi pranali kaa yah laabh he ki antariksh yaan ke puure dravyaman ko kaksha main nahin le jaaya jaataa. yah laagat, sanrachana or ingine ke aakaar ko kam rakhane main sahaayta karata he.
 teen yaa adhik charnon ke sthaan par isamen ek phayda yah he ki isamen jatilta, laagat or asafalataa kaa jokhim ko kam rahata he.
