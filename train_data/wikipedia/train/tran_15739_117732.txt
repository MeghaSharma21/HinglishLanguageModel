 swayansiddha bharat sarkaar ke mahila or bal vikaas mantralay dwaara pryojit or 2000-01 main prarambh mahilaon ko saamaajik, aarthik, raajnneetik or kanoonee shashaktikaran kii samanvit yojana he. iske antargat mahilaon ke swayansahayta samoohon ko choti bachaton hetu protsaahit kiya jaataa he. pramukh uddeshy mahilaon kaa vittiya samaveshan he. yojana shat pratishat centre dwaara pryojit he. 2001 se ab tak 70 hajaar se adhik mahila samoohon ke bank khate khole gayi. yah yojana 31 march 2008 ko samaapt kar di gayi he.
