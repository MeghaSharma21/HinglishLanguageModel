 da canan of medicine ek 14-khand arabi chikitsa vishwakosh he, jo farsi muslim vaigyanikon evam chikitsak ebn sina dwaara banaaya gaya tha. ise san 1025 main purna kiya gaya tha. yah arabi bhashaa main likhaa he. yah unke niji anubhav, madhya kaaleen islamic aushadhiyon, unani aushadhgyon ke lekhk gallen ke lekhon par aadhaariy tha. iske saath hi yah bhartiya chikitsakon sushrut evam charak va pracheen arabi va farsi aushadhi gyaan par bhi aadhaarit tha. canan ko aushadhi itihaas main atimahatvapurn pustak mana jaataa he.
