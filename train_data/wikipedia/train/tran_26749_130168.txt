 charan ek jati he ko sindh, rajasthan or gujarat main nivas karti he.
 sadasyon ke jati mana jaataa he paramatma ke dwaara samaaj ke ek bade varg he. jati kii mahila ke bahut acha laga rahe hai ke rup main maan devi dwaara anya pramukh samudaayon ke is kshetra sahit rajputon.
 Charan samaaj par aadhaarit he, likhaa vanshavalihai. ek charan par vichaar karenge sabhi anya Charans baraabar ke rup main yahaan tak ki agar ve ek duusare ko jaanate nahin he or moulik alag aarthik yaa bhugolic sthiti.
 anil chandra banerjee, ek itihaas ke professor, ne kaha ki 
 apane khaane or pine kii aadaton ke un sadrush rajputon. Charans istemaal kaa anand karne ke liye kii khapat opium or sharaab pine ke liye sharaab, prathaon jo bhi kar rahe hai ke bich lokapriy rajputon ke is kshetra.Charans khaane ko nahin he maance ke liye gaay or pakad, jo un logon main kya bolnaa upeksha. gaayon kaa sammaan kar rahe hai kii tarah. ek pati or patni nahin hoga doodh pine se ek hi gaay, doodh yaa gande dwaara apane counter bhaag he. doodh pine se ek maan kaa pratik he ki jo log aisa mana jaataa ho janaa chahie ke rup main bhai bahan hai. isse pehle 1947 main bhartiya swatantraaa, ek balidaan ke ek purush bhens ke gathan kaa ek pramukh hissaa kaa utsav navaratrihai. is tarah ke samarohon main aksar istemaal kiya ja karne ke liye kii adhyakshata Charan aurat he. 
 ek puri style sahitya ke rup main janaa jaataa he charan sahitya he. ke Dingal bhashaa or sahitya ke bade paimaane par maujuud hone ke kaaran is jati he. Zaverchand Meghani bitaate hai Charani sahitya main teraa upasheliyon:
 anya vargeekaran ke Charani sahitya hai Khyatas, Vartas or Vatas, raso, Veli - Veli krishna Rukman ri, doha-chand .
