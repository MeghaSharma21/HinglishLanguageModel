 gandki nadi, jise badi gandak yaa keval gandak bhi kaha jaataa he, bihar or nepal main bahane vaali ek nadi kaa naam he. is nadi ko nepal main saligrami yaa saalagrami or maidaanon me narayani or saptgandki kehete hai. greece ke bhugolbettaaon kii condochets tathaa mahakavyon main ullikhit sadanira bhi yahi he. 
 gandki himalay se nikalakar dakshin-pashchim bahati hui bharat main pravesh karti he. triveni parvat ke pehle isamen ek sahaayak nadi trishulganga milti he. yah nadi kaaphi dur tak uttar pradesh tathaa bihar rajyon ke bich seemaa nirdhaarit karti he. isaki seemaa par uttar pradesh kaa keval gorakhpur jila padataa he. bihar main yah champaran, saaran or mujffarpur jilon se hokar bahati hui 192 mile ke maarg ke baad patna ke sanmukh ganga main par mil jaati he. is nadi kii kul lambaai lagbhag 1310 kilometre he.
 vigalit him dwaara varsh bhar pani milte rahane se yah sadaawahi bani rahati he. varsha ritu main isaki baadh sameepvarti maidaanon ko khatre main daal detii he kyonki us samay iskaa paat 2-3 mile chaudaa ho jaataa he. baadh se bachane ke liye iske kinare band banaae gaye hai. yah nadi maarg-parivartan ke liye bhi prasiddh he. is nadi dwaara nepal tathaa gorakhpur ke jangalon se lakdi ke latthon kaa tairta hua gattha nichle bhaagon main laayaa jaataa he or usee maarg se anaaj or chini bheji jaati he. triveni tathaa saaran jile kii naharen isse nikaali gai hai jinase champaran or saaran jile main sinchai hoti he.
