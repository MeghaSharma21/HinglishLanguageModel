 maderid kaa saan andres girjaghar madrid, spain main sthit ek carch he. ise bien de interes cultural kii shrenee main 1995 main shaamil kiya gaya tha. 
 yah girjaghar musalamaanon ke qabze ke samay se ek purv kaa girjaghar or usee sthaan par sthit he.
 purv girjaghar ko saan isidor labrador or unki patni santa maria de laa kaabeza jinhone ise banaaya tha, akasar yahan jate-jate the.
 saan isidor labrador, jinka ghar paas hi tha, marnoparant apane ghar par ek chota girjaghar banane kaa aadesh diya tha. is chote girjaghar kaa nirmaan 1657 shuru hua tha jabaki saan isidor labrador ko 1622 ko ek dharmik sant ke rup main manyata di gai thi.
 iske alaava is girjaghar kaa punarnirmaan 1663 or 1669 main, or baad main 1783 or 1789 main kiya gaya tha. shuru-shuru main nirmaan ek barok style main hoje de villarreal dwaara kiya gaya tha. iske baad kaa nirmaan pedaro de laa torre or juaan de lobera dwaara liya gaya tha.
 girjaghar ke andar kii chitrakala or sajaavat ko spain kii grih yuddh se bahut had tak nuqsaan hua he.
