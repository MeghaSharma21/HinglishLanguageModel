 jaevzilli kisi jeev ke sharir main kisi ansh ko dhakne vaali yaa do anshon ko alag karne vaali ardhapargamya jhilli hoti he. jaevzilliyaan aksar lipid dwiparat kii bani hoti hai jisamen kuch protein sthai yaa asthai rup se jude yaa dhanse hue hote hai or jinka prayog jhilli ke paar sanchaar or rasayanon va yanon kii awajahi ke liye kiya jaataa he.
