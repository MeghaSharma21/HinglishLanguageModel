 jo ingine he ooshmaa ko yantric urja main badalte hai unhen ooshmaa ingine kehete hai. ye ingine ooshmaa ke ucch taap se nimna taap par pravaahit hone ke gun kaa upyog karke banaae jate hai. iske vipareet ve ingine jo yantric urja kii sahaayta se ooshmaa ko adhik taap se kam taap par le jate hai unhen 'ooshmaa pump' yaa 'prasheetak' kehete hai.
 ucch taap se nimna taap par ooshmaa kisi taral ke sahare sthaanantarit hoti he. ooshmaa mashine praayah kisi ooshmaa chakra main kaam karti hai. isiilie inhen sambandhit ooshmaagatikeey chakra ke naam se jaataa jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye karna chakra par kaam karne vaala ooshmaa ingine 'karna ingine' kehalaata he.
 ooshmaa ingine anya injanon se is maamle main bhinn hai ki inaki adhiktam dakshata karna ke prameya se nirdhaarit hoti he jo apekshaakrit bahut kam hoti he. kintu phir bhi ooshmaa ingine sarvaadhik prachlit ingine he kyonki lagbhag sabhi prakaar kii urjaaon ko ooshmaa main bahut aasaani se badla ja saktaa he or ooshmaa ingine dwaara is ooshmaa ko yantric urja main.
