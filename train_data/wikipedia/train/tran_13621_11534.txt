 shulparni, ilex jinas kaa saamaanya naam he. yah donon golardho ke ushn tathaa shitoshna katibandhon main paaya jaataa he. yah apni sundar pattiyon evam aakarshak laal beriyon ke kaaran lagaaya jaataa he. is vansh ke vriksh yaa kshup parnpaati, yaa sadahrith hote hai. parnpaati spishiz adhikanshat: kshup hote hai, jabaki sadahrith spishiz ke vriksh chote, yaa madhyam oonchaai ke hote hen. vriksh kii oonchaai praaia: 40 se 50 foot tathaa kabhi kabhi 100 foot tak hoti he. shulparni ke padap ekalingi yaa ubhayalingi hote hai.
 isaki pattiyaan praaia: areey, pushp hare tathaa laal, kaali yaa kabhi kabhi pili beriyaan hoti he, jo sheet ritu paryant daaliyon par rahati hai. is vriksh kii lakdi, kathor, baari, safed tathaa sundar gathan vaali hoti he, jiskaa upyog maapne ke paimaane ko banane tathaa fernituron main astr dene ke liye hota he. bagichon main baad lagane ke liye shulparni kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. patjhad yaa vasant ritu sadahrith spishiz lagane kaa sarvottam samay he. shulparni ke lagbhag 300 spishij gyaat hai.
