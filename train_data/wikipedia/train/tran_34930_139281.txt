 kalaat khaanat british raaj ke kaal main ek riyasat thi jo san 1666 se 14 aktubar 1955 tak astitv main rahi. bharat va pakistan kii swatantraaa ke baad yah agast 1947 se lekar march 1948 tak ek swatantra raashtra ke rup main tha, jiske baad iskaa pakistan main vilay kar diya gaya. kalaat shahar is rajya kii rajdhani thi. iske shaasha kii oopaadhi khan thi isaliye is riyasat ko aupchaarik rup se khaanat kaha jaataa tha.
 kalaat ke shaasha 1739 tak vali kii oopaadhi rakhte the lekin uske baad khan kahalaane lage.
