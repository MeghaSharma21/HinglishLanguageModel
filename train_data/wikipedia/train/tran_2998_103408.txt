 bal gopal kare dhamal ek bhartiya televijan dharawahik he jo 22 disambar 2014 se big magic par prasaarit hota he. satyajit sharma dharawahik main mukhya bhoomika main he.
 dharawahik kii kahani gopal sharmaji ke ird-gird gumti rahati he jiske saamaanya or neeras jivan main natkhat bal gopal ke aane se kai parivartan hote he. bhagwaan krishna kaa bal rup sirf professor gopal sharma ko hi dikhataa he. vah unase baaten karte hai, madad karte hai or unka dil bhi bahalaate hai. agal-bagal khadey log ye dekhakar chaunk jate hai ki aakhir professor sahab ko hua kya he? kya unhen khud se baaten karne kii beemaari ho gai he? sab gopal das par hansate hai or sochane par majaboor ho jate hai ki aakhir maajaraa kya he?
