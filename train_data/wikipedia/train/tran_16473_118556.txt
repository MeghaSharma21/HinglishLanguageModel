 oirat mongol logon kaa sabase pashchimtam samudaay he jo pashchimi mongolia ke altaai parvat kshetra main vaas karte hai. kai qabeelon ke ekikaran se bani yah jati madhya asia ke purvi bhaag main utpann hui thi lekin ab inkaa sabase bada gut rus ke kalmikia ganatantr main miltaa he jahaan inhen kalmik log kaha jaataa he. kalmiki 17veen sadi ke shuruuaati daur main zungaaria se roosi samrajya ke dakshin-purvi europi bhaag main aa based the.
 aitihaasik rup se oiraton ke chaar pramukh qabeele hai: zungaar, tornaut​, dorbet or khoshut​. inke anya qabeelon main khoid​, bayid, myangad, zakhchin, baatud​, barga or darkad shaamil hai. duniya bhar main lagbhag 5 lakh oirat hai, jinamein se 2 lakh mongolia main, 1.75 lakh rus main or 1.4 lakh china main rahete hai.
