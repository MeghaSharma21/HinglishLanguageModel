 bhaskar pratham bharat ke saatvin shatabdi ke ganitagya the. sambhawatah unhone hi sabase pehle sankhyaon ko hindu dashamik paddati main likhnaa armbh kiya. unhone aryabhatta kii kritiyon par tikaa likhi or usee sandarbh main jya ya kaa parimey maan bataaya jo anany evam atyant ullekhneey he. aryabhatiy par unhone san 629 main aryabhatibhashya naamak tikaa likhi jo sanskrit gadhy main likhi ganit evam khagolashastra kii pratham pustak he. aryabhat kii paripati main hi unhone mahabhaskareey evam laghubhaskareey naamak do khagolashastreey granth bhi likhe.
 mahabhaskareey main aath adhyay hai. saatven adhyay ke shloke 17, 18 or 19 main unhone sin x kaa sannikat maan nikaalane kaa nimnlikhit sutara diya he-
 is sutara ko unhone aryabhatta dwaara diya hua bataaya he. is sutara se praapt jya ya ke maanon kaa aapekshik truti 1.9% se kam he. mahabhaskareey ke kuch bhaagon kaa baad main arabi main anuwaad hua.
