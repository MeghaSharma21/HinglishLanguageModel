 bydaree kaa itihaas vidyutrasinic selon ke vikaas kaa itihaas he. badariyon ke vikaas se hi vidyut kaa audyogic upyog aarabh hua. unnisvin shatabdi ke antim dinon tak batariyaan hi vidyut urja ke mukhya strot thim kyonki tab tak vidyut janitr kaa avishkaar nahin hua tha.
 bydaree kii praudyogiki main lagaataar vikaas hua jinke bina telegraph, telephone, portable computer, mobile phone, vidyut kaaron aadi kaa vikaas nahin ho saktaa tha.
 san 1749 benjamin franklin ne 'bydaree' shabd kaa istemaal kiya tha. apane vidyut-sambandhi prayogon ke liye ve jin 'linkd capacitars' kaa prayog karte the, unako unhone 'bydaree' kii sangya di.
