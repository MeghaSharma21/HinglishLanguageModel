 mahamahopadhyaay harprasad shastri  bharat ke ek shikshashasthri, sanskrit ke vidwaan, bharatvid tathaa bangla sahitya ke itihaaskaar the. unka mul naam 'harprasad bhattacharya' tha. ve charyaapad kii khoj ke liye prasiddh hai, jo bangala sahitya kaa sabase pracheen udhaaharan he.
 harprasad shastri britishkaleen bengal ke khulanaa jila ke kumira gram main janme the. tab unka aadi nivas uttar choubees pargana jila ke nayhati main tha. gaanv ke vidhyalay main praathmik shiksha arjan ke pashchaat ve kolkaata sanskrit kalejiet skul tathaa presidensi kalege main adhyayan kiya. kolkaata main ve apane bade bhai nandakumar nyaayachanchur bandhu tathaa bisisht samaaj sanskaarak or pandit ishwarchandra vidyasagar ke saath rahe. varsh 1871 main ve praveshika pariksha uteern hue. 1873 saal main ve 'first arts pariksha' uteern kiye. 1876 saal main bi e degree arjan kiye. 1877 saal main sanskrit main sammanik hue. baad main em.e. pariksha paas karke unhone 'shastri' kii oopaadhi praapt kii. is pariksha main harprasad hi pratham shrenee se uteern ekmaatr chaatr the.
