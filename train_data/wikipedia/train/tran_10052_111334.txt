 radhika apte ek bhartiya abhinetri hai. in kaa janm pune main 7 sitambar 1985 ko hua tha. yah hindi, bengali, marathi, telugu or malayalam bhashaa main kai filmein kar chukin hai.
 inhone apane abhinay kii shuruyaat ek bollywood film vaah life ho to aisi main ek chote se kirdaar ko nibhaa kar kii. jiske pashchaat unhone anya bhashaa ke filmon main kaary karnaa shuru kiya. yah shriram raghavan dwaara nirdeshit badlapur main bhi kaary kar chukin hai.
