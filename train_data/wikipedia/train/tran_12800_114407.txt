 islom abdug'anievich karimov  uzbekistan ke pratham raashtrapati the. is pad par ve 1991 se mrityu paryant 2016 tak rahe.
 karimov kaa janm samarkand main hua tathaa 1941 se 1945 tak ve anathalay main bhi rahe. unhone arthshaastra va ejineering kii padhai kii thi.31 agast 1991 ko unhone uzbekistan ko swatantra raashtra ghoshit kiya or raashtrapati pad kaa chunaav 86% mat prapthkar jeeta. haalaanki vipaksh or antarrashtriya samoohon dwaara unpar dhaandhli ke aarope lagaae gaye. 29 agast 2016 ko tabiyat bigadane ke baad unhen gahan chikitsa main rakhane kii khabar ai. 2 sitambar ko samachar agenciyon ne khabar di ki unki mrityu ho gai he. usee din sarkaar ne bhi khabar kii pushti kar di.
