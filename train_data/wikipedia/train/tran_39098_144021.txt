 manovigyaan ke sandarbh main shilgun siddhant yaa visheshak siddhanth maanav vyaktitv ke adhyayan kaa ek tarika he. shilgun siddhantkaar mukhyatah shilgunon ke maapan ko mahatv dete hai. vyavhaar, vichaar, tathaa bhaavana ke suspasht atternon ko shilgun kehete hai. shilgun siddhanth kaa maananaa he ki vyaktiyon ke shilgun kaaphi lambi avadhi main bhi bahut seemaa tak aparivartit rahete hai, ye alag-alag vyaktiyon main alag-alag hote hai.
