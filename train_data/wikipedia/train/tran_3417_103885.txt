 choudhary mohammad sarwar ko 2013 main pradhanmantri pakistan navaj sharif ne punjab cagorenar chanaa.teen baar ] british sansad ke sadsy chune gaye, or 13 saal glassgo centre lebar party dwaara pratinidhitiv kiya. british sansad ke pehle muslim sadsy hai. unke baad unke bete anas sarwar is seat se nirvaachit hue. rajyapal manonith hone ke baad choudhary mohammad sarwar ne daawa kiya ki unhone british naagarikta chhod di he .
 choudhary sarwar britain main thok kariyana, banaam united thok kaa kaarobaar chalaate hai jisase unhone lakhon kamaaye hai. navaj sharif ko jab musharraf ne apdasth karke kaid kiya, to choudhary sarwar britain pratinidhi ke rup main mari main navaj sharif se mulaakaat kii. nirvaasan ke jamaane main navaj sharif or choudhary sarwar ke sambandh main vruddhi hui. satta main waapas aakar unke vyaktigat sambandhon ke kaaran sharif ne unhen pakistan ke sabase bade praant ke rajyapal se sammanit kiya.
 29 janvari, 2015 ko unhone rajyapal punjab ke pad se istipha de diya.
