 kundawapar nalanda jile ke ekangarsarai prakhand mukhyalay se purab 2 kam rashtriya rajmarg 110 par basaa hua ek kasbaa he, jisaki aabaadi 6 hajaar he.
 kundawapar main ek talab he jise log kumhaini pokhar ke naam se pukaarte hai. is 2500 saal puraane kumhaini pokhar kaa nirmaan guptavansh ne karavaaya tha. is pokhare main abhi bhi shunga kaaleen ite milti he. is pokhar main haal main hi buddha bhagwan kii murti nikali he.
 is gaav main ek goraaya bhagwan ke naam se buddha bhagwan kii murti ko gaav baale puja karte he, kundawapar ke purv main ek tila he jise log dhibra pokher kehete hai. vahaan par shunga kaal ke bhawan kaa nirmaan dikhataa he.
