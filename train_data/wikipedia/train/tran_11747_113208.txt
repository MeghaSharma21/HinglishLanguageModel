 baluchistan pakistan kaa pashchimi praant he. baluchistan naam kaa kshetra bada he or yah iran tathaa afghanistan ke sate hue kshetron main banta hua he. yahaan kii rajdhani queta he. yahan ke logon kii pramukh bhashaa balooch yaa baluchi ke naam se jaani jaati he. 1944 main baluchistan ke swatantraaa kaa vichaar general mani ke vichaar main aayaa tha par 1947 main british ishaare par ise pakistan main shaamil kar liya gaya. 1970 ke dashak main ek balooch rashtrawaad kaa udai hua jisamen baluchistan ko pakistan se swatantra karne kii maang uthi.
 yah pradesh pakistan ke sabase kam aabaad ilaakon main se ek he.
 iske purvi kinare par sindhu ghati sabhyataa kaa udbhav hua. kuch vidwaanon kaa maananaa he ki sindhu ghati sabhyataa ke mul log balooch hi the. par iske sakshya naganya hai. sindhu ghati kii lipi ko na padhe jane ke kaaran sanshay ab tak bana hua he. par sindhu sabhyataa ke avshesh aaj ke baluchistan main kam hi paae jate hai.
 baluchi logon kaa mana he ki unka mul nivas syria ke ilaake main the or unka mul semetic he. aaj kaa dakshini baluchistan iran ke kamran praant kaa hissaa tha jabaki uttar purvi bhaag sistan kaa ang. san 652 main muslim khaliph umr ne kamran par akraman ke aadesh die or yah islamic kilaafat kaa ang ban gaya. par umr ne apana samrajya kamran tak hi simit rakhaa. ali ke khilaafat main puraa baluchistan, sindhu nadi ke pashchimi chor tak, khilaafat ke tahat aa gaya. is samay ek or vidroh bhi hua tha.san 663 main hue vidroh main kalaat rashidun khilaafat ke haath se nikal gaya. baad main ummayadon ne isapar kabja kar liya. iske baad yah mughal hastkshep kaa bhi vishay rahaa par ant main british shashan main shaamil ho gaya. 1944 main ise swatantra karne kaa vichaar bhi angrezon ke man main aayaa tha par 1947 main yah swatantra pakistan kaa ang ban gaya.
 sattar ke dashak main yahan pakistani shashan ke khilaaf mukti abhiyaan bhi chalaa tha jise kuchal diya gaya.
