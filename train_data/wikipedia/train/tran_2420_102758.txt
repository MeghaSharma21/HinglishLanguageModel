 raatri drushti kam prakaash vaali paristhitiyon main dekhne kii kshamta hoti he. kai praaniyon kii aankhon ke drushti patal main shalaka koshikaaye unhen raatri drushti pradaan karti hai. kritrim yantron se is kshamta ko badhaayaa ja saktaa he. kuch praaniyon main "tapitum lusidam" naamak ang raatri drushti ko adhik shaktishaalee banaata he lekin maanvon main is ang kaa abhaav he.
