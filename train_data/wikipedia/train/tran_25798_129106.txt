 shivadin ram joshi ek bhartiya kavi the. unke sahitya main savaiya, manahar, matagyand, kundali chand ore kavitt kaa prayog tathaa dhamal, bhajan ore gajalon kaa samavesh he. padyatmak rachanaaon kaa vishay gyaan, vairaygy, prem, prakruti chithran, prarthana or upadesh ke saath-saath samaaj main vyaapt kuritiyon, paakhand, bhrashtachar evam kaal chintan unke sahitya kaa mukhya centre rahe hai. unke sahitya kii bhashaa braj mishrit hindi he. kahin kahin rajasthani, urdu or farsi shabdon ke alaava angreji ke shabdon kaa bhi prayog hua he. joshi kaa adhikansh sahitya aprakashit he.
 unke pita kaa naam surajbhaan tathaa mata kaa naam lakshmi tha. das varsh kii umr main unhone lekhan kaary shuru kar diya tha.
 chand lahar, anubhavlahar or krishna sudama charitra unki prakashit kritiyaan he. akashwani jaipur tathaa ajmer se unki rachanaaon kaa prasaaran bhi hua he. rajasthani bhashaa ke sahityakar gordhan singh shekhavat dwaara sampadit shekhavati ke sahityakar va raghunatha prasad tiwari 'umang' ke granth khandela kshetra kaa saanskrithikvaibhav main bhi joshi kaa varnan he. 27 july 2006 ko unki mrityu ho gai.
