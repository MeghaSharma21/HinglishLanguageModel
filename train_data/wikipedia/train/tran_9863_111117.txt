 herohito athwa shoova samraat, japan ke 124ve samraat the. unka raajkaal 25 disambar 1926 se lekar 1989 main unki mrityu tak tha. haalanki japan ke baahar wo apane vyaktigat naam herohito se jane jate hai, japan main aaj wo keval apane maranoparant naam shoova se jane jate hai. shoova shabd unke raajkaal kii avadhi kaa naam tha, jise unki mrityu ke baad unka khud kaa naam bana diya gaya.
