 vataseri parameshwar nambudiri  bharat ke keraly ganit sampradaay se sambandhit ek mahaan ganitagya evam khagolashastri the. 
 parameshwar vishwa ke pratham ganitagya hai jinhone sabase pehle us vrutt kii trigya bataayi jiske andar nirmit chakree chaturbhuj kii bhujaayen di huin hai. parameshwar ke anusaar, yadi chakree chaturbhuj kii bhujaayen a, b, c, tathaa d, hon to uske parivritt kii trigya R nimnlikhit vyanjak se di jaayegi-
 yahi sutara 1782 main, 350 varsh baad huliyyar ne diya tha.
