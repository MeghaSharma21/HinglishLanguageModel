 udghaatan 1896 ke greeshmakaleen olympic main sweden ne pehle olympic khelon main bhaag liya, or athleates ko har game main pratispardhaa karne ke baad se ek apwaad ke saath bheja he, kareeb 1904 greeshmakaleen olympic main bhaag liya. sweden ne sabhi olympic khelon main do padakon, 1896 khelon or 1904 games ke alaava padak arjeet kiye hai. keval ek anya raashtra ne 1908 se prayek olympic khel main padak arjeet kiya he, sweden ke padosi desh finland.
 sweden ne 1912 greeshmakaleen olympic stoffhome main ek avasar par khelon kii mejabani kii. sangrodh ke kaarnon se melborn main 1956 ke greeshmakaleen olympic ke ghudsavaar aayojan stoffhome main yojit kiye gaye the.
 swedish athlete ne greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main kul 494 padak jite, or sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main 144 anya.
 antarrashtriya olympic samiti ne swedish adhikari or khel prashikshak victor blike ko iske mul sadasyon main se ek ke rup main shaamil kiya tha. swedish olympic samiti kii sthapana or 1913 main manyata praapt thi.

 *is talika main chah padak shaamil hai - teen swarna, do rajat or ek kaansya - 1908 or 1920 ke aankade skating samarohon main sammanit kiya gaya.
