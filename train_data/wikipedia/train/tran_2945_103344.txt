 badhirta yaa bahraapan ek aam beemaari he. is vikaar kii sthiti main sunne kii shakti kam ho jaati he. iske saatah hi saath vyakti kii saamaajik va maansik pareshaaniyaan bhi bad jaati hai. 
 jab koi vyakti bolta he, to vah dhwani tarangon ke dwaara hawa main ek kampan paidaa karata he. yah kampan kaan ke parde evam sunne se sambandhit teen haddiyon - melius, inkas evam stepis ke dwaara aantarik kaan main pahunchataa he or sunne kii nas dwaara aantarik kaan se mastishk main sampreshit hota he. is kaaran hamen dhwani kaa ahsaas hota he. yadi kisi kaaran se dhwani kii in tarangon main avrodh paidaa ho jaae, to bahraapan ho jayega. yadi avrodh kaan ke parde yaa sunne kii haddiyon tak simit rahata he to ise concective defnes kehete hai. yadi avrodh kaan ke aantarik bhaag main yaa sunne se sambandhit nas main he, to ise censori neural defnes kehete hai.
