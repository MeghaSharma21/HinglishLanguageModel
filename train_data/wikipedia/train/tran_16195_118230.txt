 niki ink ek america bahurashtriya company he jo khel se jude saamaan dizin kaa vikaas karti he or duniya bhar main bechati he. 
 niki disambar 2005 se bharat kii rashtriya cricket team kii praayojak company he or bhartiya cricket control board ne yah qaraar paanch saal ke liye or badha diya he jo ab bhi jari he. niki company bhartiya cricket team ke liye kit kii aapurti karti rahegi. company ne yah nahin bataaya ki yah qaraar kitne rupye kaa he lekin samajha jaataa he ki yah kareeb 270 karod rupye kaa hoga. pehle yah qaraar 200 karod kaa tha.
 
 team india kii kit kaa 2014 main pehali baar shat pratishat punarnavinikaran paliester kapade se banaai gai he jo halkaa or kaaphi lacheela he. isamen adhik pasinaa sokhne kii kshamta bhi he. niki ne yahaan bayaan jari karke bataaya ki isamen aaraam or thandak ke liye ventilation kii bhi behatar vyavastha kii gai he. team india kii yah kit or anya saamaan 20 sitambar se niki ke store par uplabdh rahegaa.
