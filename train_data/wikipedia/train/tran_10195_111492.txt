 rajat jayanti kaa prayog pachchisvin jayanti athwa pachchisvin varshaganth ke liye kiya jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye yadi bharat desh 15 agast 1947 ko swatantra hua to 15 agast 1972 ko swatantraaa prapti kii rajat jayanti hogi. dhyaan dene kii baat ye bhi he ki isi udhaaharan me 15 agast 1972 bharat kaa chhbbeesvaan swatantraaa diwas hoga. chunki jayanti ghatana ke ek saal baad se prarambh hoti he isaliye ye ghatana kii vaastavik sankhya se ek kam chalati he. angreji bhashaa me rajat jayanti ke liye silver jubilee shabd kaa prayog hota he.
 ghatana kii choubeesvin varshaganth ke din se lekar rajat jayanti tak chalane vaale ek varsh ko rajat jayanti varsh kaha jaataa he. adhiktar samooh yaa sanghathan jo kisi ghatana kaa rajat jayanti varsh manaa rahe hote hai, wo is puure varsh koi na koi aayojan athwa samaaroh karte rahete hai jo ki ant me rajat jayanti ke din sampann hote hai.ghatana kii choubeesvin varshaganth ke din se lekar rajat jayanti tak chalane vaale ek varsh ko rajat jayanti varsh kaha jaataa he. adhiktar samooh yaa sanghathan jo kisi ghatana kaa rajat jayanti varsh manaa rahe hote hai, wo is puure varsh koi na koi aayojan athwa samaaroh karte rahete hai jo ki ant me rajat jayanti ke din sampann hote hai.
