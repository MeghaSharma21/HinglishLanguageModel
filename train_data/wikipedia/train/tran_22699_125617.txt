 besabaal ek balle evam gend se khele janaa vaala amareeki khel he.
 besabaal 1846 main england main sabase pehle khelaa gaya tha, lekin isko vaastavik rup kuch parivartan ke saath uttari america ne diya. 19veen shatabdi ke ant main yah sanyukt rajya america kaa neshnal game ban gaya. besabaal tab uttari america, central or dakshin america, carebia or east asia main khub khelaa jaataa tha. besabaal ko dhire-dhire olympic main bhi sthaan mil gaya. yah samar olympic main khelaa jaataa he.
 isamen ek vishesh prakaar ke bat or ball kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. bat rod kii tarah hota he or ball ko isse hit kiya jaataa he. yah khel do tiimon ke bich khelaa jaataa he. ek team main 9 khilaadi hote hai. ek team batting karti he or duusari fielding. bat, rod kii tarah hone se ball ko hit karnaa mushkil hota he. bowler bina kisi tappe yaa jameen par ball ko lagaae sidhaa bitman kii taraf phenkata he. bitman ball ko hit karke apane balle ko vahiin chhodkar duusare chor par bhaagta he. besabaal khilne kii pich diamond aakaar kii hoti he, jiske chaar base hote hai. ballebaaj ball ko hit karke nan.-2 base main bhaagta he, yani vah ek baar main jitne base cover karegaa usko utane hi ran milenge. yah romaanchak game khilne main jitna mjedaar he, utana dekhne main bhi.
