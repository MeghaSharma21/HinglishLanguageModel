 
 deodaar ek sidhe tane vaala unchaa shankudhari ped he, jiske pattey lambey or kuch golai liye hote hai tathaa jisaki lakdi majboot kintu halki or sugandhit hoti he. inke shanku kaa aakaar sanobar se kaaphi miltaa-julata hota he. inkaa moolsthaan pashchimi himalay ke parvaton tathaa bumadhyasagariya kshetra main he, . yah imaaraton main kaam aati he. yah pashchimi himalay, purvi afghanistan, uttari pakistan, uttar-madhya bharat ke himachal pradesh, uttarakhand, jammu evam kashmir tathaa dakshin-pashchimi tibet evam pashchimi nepal main 1500-3200 meter kii oonchai par paaya jaataa he. yah ek shankudhari vriksh hota he, jisaki oonchai 40-50 me. tak or kabhi-kabhaar 60 me. tak hoti he. iske tane 2 meter tak or khaas vrikshon main 3 meter tak ke hote hai. isaki kuch prajatiyon ko snigdhdaaru or kashthadaru ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. snigdh deodara kii lakdi or tel dawa banane ke kaam main bhi aate hai. iske anya naamon main deodara prasiddh he. yah nichle pahari kshetron main paaya jaataa he. 
 pahari samskruti kaa abhinn ang deodaar kaa vriksh sadaa se kaviyon tathaa lekhakon kaa prerana strot rahaa he. deodaar ke pattey hare rang ke or kuch laali liye hue hote he. deodaar tikhaa tej swaad or karkash suganndh vaala hota he. isaki taasir garm hoti he is kaaran adhik maatra main upyog fefdon ke liye hanikaarak hota he. deodaar ke dosha ko kathira or baadaam kaa tel nasht karata he. isaki tulana adhakh se kii ja sakati he. ise anek dosha ko nasht karanevaala kaha gaya he. yah sujan ko pachaata he, sardi se utpann hone vaali pidaa ko shant karata he, pathari ko todta he or isaki lakdi ke gunagune kaadhe main baithne se guda ke sabhi prakaar ke ghaav nasht ho jate he.
