 malviya rashtriya takneekee santhaan, jaipur, rajasthan kii sthapana 1963 main kii gai thi or 26 june 2002 ko ise malviya rashtriya praudyogiki santhaan kaa darja diya gaya. 
 yah santhaan nau avar snaatak pathyakram tathaa 10 purnakalik evam paanch anshkalik snatakottar pathyakramon kaa sanchaalan karata he. yah santhaan civil ejiniyari, rasayan ejiniyari, vidyut ejiniyari, electronic tathaa sanchaar ejiniyari, soochna praudyogiki, abhiyantriki ejiniyari tathaa dhaatukarmee ejiniyari main chaar varshiya avar snaatak pathyakramon or ek paanch varshiya bi. arq. pathyakram kaa sanchaalan karata he. yah santhaan 10 vishyon main teen semester purnakalik or paanch semester anshkalik snatakottar degree kaaryakramon kaa sanchaalan karata he. yah santhaan electraniki evam computer ejiniyari main janashakti vikaas hetu vishwa bank, swiss vikaas korporation tathaa bharat sarkaar dwaara vittposhit impact naamak pariyojna ko lagu kar rahaa he.
