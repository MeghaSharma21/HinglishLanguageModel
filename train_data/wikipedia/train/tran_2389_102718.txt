 nabinagar super thermal power project bihar ke aurangabad jile ke nabinagar taluk main maajiyaan or ankoraha gaanvon main sthit ek koyala aadhaarit thermal power plant he. ise 1989 main bihar ke tatkaaleen mukhyamantri satyendra narayan sinha ne sankalpit kiya tha, jinhone sthapit karne kaa prastaav bheja tha. bihar ke aurangabad jile ke nabinagar main bharat ke tatkaaleen pradhan mantri rajeev gandhi ke liye antipisi kii super thermal power project; lekin pariyojna kamjor ho gai kyonki nimnlikhit rajya sarkarein iskaa paalan karne main naakaam raheen. 2007 main, manmohan singh kii sarkaar ne aakhirkaar manjuuri kaa tikat lagaaya.
 power plant kaa swaamitv nabinagar power jenerating company he - shuruyaat main antipisi limited or bihar state power holding company limited ke bich 50:50 sanyukt udhyam. nabinagar sanyantr main 4380 megawatt kii kshamta hogi. yah desh kii teesri sabase badi pariyojna hogi. vartmaan main desh kii sabase badi bijli pariyojna vindhyachal maha taap vidyut grih jabaki duusari badi pariyojna balaar main he. shuruyaat main pariyojna kii peedi kii kshamta 3960 megawatt thi, lekin 2016 main utpaadan kshamta 4380 megawatt tak badi thi. yah super thermal power project 3,200 acre main failaa hua he, jisamen township ke liye 144 acre bhoomi or rail galiyare ke nirmaan ke liye 63 acre jameen shaamil he. 17 april 2018 ko, mukhyamantri nitish kumaar kii adhyakshata main bihar rajya cabinet ne nabinagar power janarating company ko rashtriya thermal power corporation ko saunpane kii manjuuri de di. 15 may 2018 ko, bihar sarkaar ne thermal plant ko 33-varshiya patte ke liye rashtriya thermal power corporation ko saunpane ke liye samjhauta gyaapan par hastakshar kiye. nabinagar sanyantr ke unit -1 ke liye, bihar ko sanyantr se utpann bijli kaa 71% milegaa, jabaki upi ko 8%, sikkim 1% or pashchim bengal 9% milegaa.
