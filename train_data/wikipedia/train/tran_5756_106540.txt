 mariz, janm naam abbaas abdul ali vaasi gujarati bhashaa ke kavi the. ve mukhtah anaki gazalon ke liye jane jate hai, saamaanya rup se ve gujarat ke gaalib ko kaha jaataa he. unke praarambhik varshon main usne apni padhaaiyaan ko chhodkar juute kaa kaarkaanaa main kaam karne lage. kavita main ruchi rakhte hue, usne patrakarita main apni padhai prarambh kii thi. unki kavitaayen ne keval unki mrityu ke pashchaat prasiddhi ko praapt kii thi.
