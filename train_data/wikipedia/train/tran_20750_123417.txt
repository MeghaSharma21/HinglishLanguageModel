 faanoos : arthath "diya", "deep", "charaag" ityadi. is faanoos ko puraane daur main bhi istemaal kiya jaataa tha. aaj ise ''faanoos-e-ramadan'' yaa ''ramadan lantern'' kaha jaataa he.
 faanoos shabd, puraani misar kii parampara se liya gaya he. iske baraabar kaa greek shabd "candal" he to hindi kaa shabd deep he. is faanoos ko ummid or andhere main ujaale kaa pratik maan jaataa he. 
 fanos paaramparik upyog main gharon kii sajaavat ke liye rahaa. is kaa itihaas fatimi khilaafat ke daur tak jaataa he. shuruuaat main is faanoos yaa lantern ko khaliph al-muizz lidinillaah ke kairo aane ke mauqe par jo ramadan kaa maheenaa bhi tha, unke swaagat ke liye istemaal kiya gaya. laqin yah faanoos aaj sabhi muslim deshon main upyog hone laga he. 
 aa
 aaj faanoos kaa upyog duniya bhar ke log karne lage hai. khaas taur par asian deshon main iskaa vistrut rup se upyog hone laga he. inkaa istemaal gharon, hotalon, malls, or digar pradeshon main dekha jasakta he. inhe kai dijainon main, rangon main or rup main banaaya jaataa he. inke banane main dhaatu or kaanch kaa prayog karte hai.
