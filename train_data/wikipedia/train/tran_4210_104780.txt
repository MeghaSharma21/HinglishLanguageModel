 
 khet yaa kshetra singh  mewar samrajya ke shaasha the. inke pita kaa naam rana hammir singh tha jabaki inke uttaradhikaar rana lakha hue inhone ajmer or mandalgarh main shashan kiya tha.
 rana kshetra singh jinhone 1364 isvi se 1382 isvi tak mewar rajya shashan kiya tha kshetra singh rana hammir singh kii santaan the. inhone ajmer or mandalgarh par shashan kiya tha jabaki mandsaur or chappan par bhi raaj kaaj kiya tha. unka nidhan ek yuddh ke dauraan 1682 main ho gaya tha iske baad inke uttaradhikari inke hi putr rana lakha hue the.
