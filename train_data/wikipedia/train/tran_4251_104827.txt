  patharghata upjila, bangladesh kaa ek upazila he, joki bangladesh main tritiy str kaa prashasnic anchal hota he . yah barishaal vibhaag ke barguna zile kaa ek upjila he, jisamen, zila sadar samet, kul 6 upazile hai, or mukhyalay barguna sadar upazila he. yah bangladesh kii rajdhani dhaka se dakshin kii disha main avasthit he. yah mukhyatah gramin kshetra he, or adhikansh aabaadi graamy ilaakon main rahati he. 
 yahan kii aadhikarik str kii bhashaayen bangla or angrezi he. tathaa bangladesh ke kisi bhi anya kshetra kii tarah hi, yahan kii bhi pramukh maukhik bhashaa or maatrubhaasha bangla he. bengali ke alaava angrezi bhashaa bhi kai logon dwaara jaani or samajhi jaati he, jabaki saanskrithik or aitihaasik nikatataa tathaa bhashaai samanta ke kaaran, kai log, simit maatra hindustani bhi samajhane main sakasham hai. yahan kaa bahusankhyak dharm, islam he, jabaki pramukh alpsankhyak dharm, hindu dharm he. barishaal vibhaag main, janasankhyikeek rup se, islam ke anuyai, aabaadi ke ausatan 88.1% he, jabaki shesh jansankhya pramukhtah hindu dharm kii anuyai he. yah mukhyatah gramin kshetra he, or adhikansh aabaadi graamy ilaakon main rahati he.
 patharghata upjila bangladesh ke dakshini bhaag main, barishaal vibhaag ke barguna jile main sthit he.
