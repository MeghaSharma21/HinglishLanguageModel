 electron, lepton parivaar ke pratham peedhee kaa sadsy he, jo ki gurutvkarshan, vidyut chumbakatv evam durbal prabhaav sabhi main bhoomika nibhaataa he. electron kan evam tarang dono tarah ke vyavhaar pradarshit karata he. beta-kshay ke rup main yah kan jaisaa vyavhaar karata he, jabaki yang kaa dabl slit prayog main iskaa kiran jaisaa vyavhaar siddh hua. chunki iskaa sankhyikeey vyavhaar fermion hota he or yah poli eksclusan sidhdant kaa paalan karata he.
 iris bhautikavid jorge jonstone stony ne 1894 main electron naam kaa suzav diya tha. vidyudanu kii kan ke rup main pahchaan 1897 main je je thomson or unki vilayati bhautikavid dal ne kii thi.
 kai bhautikeey ghatnaayen jaise-vidhyut, chumbakatv, ushma chaalakata main vidyudanu kii ahm bhoomika hoti he. jab vidyudanu twarit hota he to yah photon ke rup menurja kaa avasorana yaa utsarjan karata he.proton va neutron ke saath milakar yahparmaanu kaa nirmaan karata he.parmaanu ke kul dravyaman main vidyudanu kaa hissaa kam se kam 0.06 pratishat hota he. vidyudanu or proton ke bich lagane vaale kulamb bal ke kaaran vidyudanu parmaanu se bandhaa hota he. do yaa do se adhik parmaanuon ke vidyudanuon ke aapasi aadan-pradaan yaa saajhedaari ke kaaran rasinic bandha banate hai.
