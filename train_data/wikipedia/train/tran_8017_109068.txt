 bharati dakshin bharat kii ek pramukh abhinetri hai. inhone kannada, tamil, telegu, hindi or malayalam cinema main kaam kiya he. 1967 kii film meherbaan se unhone hindi cinema main sahaayak abhinetri ke rup main pravesh kiya tha. lav in bangalur cinema ke dwaara kannada cinema pravesh karanevaali bharati baad main tamil, telegu, hindi or malayalam bhashaaon ke 150 se zyada filmo main kaam kiya he. purab or pashchim, mastaana, ham tum or wo, sab kaa saathi, kunvaara baap or uttar dakshin inke pramukh hindi filme hai. kannada main dow rajkumaar ke saath sandhyarag, bangarad manushya, doorad bett jaise prasiddh filmo main kaam kiya he. dow rajkumaar, akkineni nageshwar raav, shivaji ganeshan, dilip kumaar, en.tii.ramrao, manoj kumaar or vinod khanna jaise abhinaytaonke saath kaam kiya he.
