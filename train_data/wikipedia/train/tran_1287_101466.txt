 1 farvari 1905 ko italy ke tiwoli main janme prasiddh america bhautik vaigyanik jinhen 1959 main oven chemberlain ke saath pratiprotron kii khoj ke liye bhautikee kaa nobel puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya. 
 unka janm 1 farvari 1905 ko italy ke tiwoli main hua tha. ve anti protein, statin or technetium par kiye apane anusandhanon ke liye vikhyath hue. unhone anek prasiddh sansthaaon, jaise ki los ellamos rashtriya prayogshaala, california vishvavidhyalay, barkale, palermo ke vishvavidhyalay, rome laa Sapienza ke vishvavidhyalay, rome laa Sapienza ke alma meter vishvavidhyalay, aadi main kaam kiya. 22 april 1989 ko 84 varsh kii avasthaa main unka nidhan ho gaya.
 unhen 1959 main bhautikee kaa nebel puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya.
