 unhone america or kenya main ucchshiksha arjeet kii. 1970 ke dashak main maathe ne green belt andolan naamak gaiyr sarkari sanghathan kii niv daalkar poudharopan, paryaavaran sangrakshan or mahilaon ke adhikaron kii ore dhyaan diya. 2004 main "satat vikaas, loktantr or shanti ke liye ke liye apane yogadan" kii vajah se nobel shanti puraskaar praapt karne vaali pehali africa mahila or pehali paryaavaranvid bani. varsh 2005 main inhen jawahar laal nehru puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
 ve 2002 main saansad bani or kenya kii sarkaar main mantri bhi raheen. 26 sitambar 2011 ko nairobi main unka nidhan ho gaya.
