 sierra intertenment
 prototip ek video game he jiskaa vikaas radical intertenment dwaara va prakaashan activijan dwaara kiya gaya he. is game ko uttari america main 9 june 2009 or europe main 12 june ko release kiya gaya tha.
 yah game new york shahar main rachaa gaya he jahaan ek mahamari blacklite puure manhaton main fail rahaa he. isse prabhavit log badal kar shaitan ban jate hai jo bhinn bhinn prakaar ke hote hai or jinka makasad duusare aam logo ko maarna yaa prabhavit karnaa ban jaataa he. in sab ke bich khel kaa mukhya paatr elex marsar he jo ek shaktishaalee rup badal sakane vaala insaan he jise apane bhootkaal kii koi yaardaash nahin he.
