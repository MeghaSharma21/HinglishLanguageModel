 jalchikitsa anek rogon kii chikitsa karne kii ek nishchit paddati he, jisamen sheethal tathaa ushn jal kaa bahyabhyantar prayog sarvshereshth aushadhi hoti he or upachaararth prayukt anya sabhi oshadhiyaan praaia: hanikar samajhi jaati hai.
 jalopchaar 1829 i se prachlit he. iskaa shrey silisa ke vincent prisnits naamak ek kisaan ko he, jisane sarvapratham iskaa vyavhaar prachlit kiya. baad main anek doctoron ne aantjvar, atijvar ityadi main sheetakaari snaan bada upyogi paaya. ab iskaa prayog adhik vyapak ho gaya he.
 jalchikitsa main jal kaa prayog nimnlikhit vidhiyon dwaara kiya jaataa he :
 1. ekang tathaa sarvang ke liye sheethal tathaa ushn aaveshton . ardravastraveshton chikitsa vyavsaay kaa ek mahatv kaa ang ho gaya he.
 2. ushn vaayu tathaa bashpasnan - turkish bath ushnavayusnaan kaa uttam udhaaharan he. david urgunhrt ne paurvatya deshon se lautne par england main isko khub prachlit kiya. ab turkish bath ek swatantra sarvamaanya saarvajanik pratha hi ban gai he.
 3. sheethal or ushn jal kaa sarvang snaan.
 4. sheethal yaa ushn jal se paad, kati, sheersh, merudand aadi, ekangsnan.
 5. ardra tathaa shushk patbandhan or compress .
 6. sheethal tathaa ushn senk evam pultice 
 7. prakshalan - isamen 15 degree -21 degree sein. taap kaa pani haathon se sharir par lagaaya jaataa he.
 8. asek - isamen rogi tub main baitha yaa khadaa rahata he or uske sarvang yaa ekang par baaltee se pani daalaa jaataa he.
 9. douse - isamen pipe ke dwaara sharir par pani choda jaataa he.
 10. jalpan - isamen pine ke liye sheethal yaa ushn jal diya jaataa he.
