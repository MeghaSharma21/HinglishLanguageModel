 
 wibrionasy gram-rinatmak bacteria kaa ek jeevvaigyanik kul he, jo proteobacteria sangh ke gammaproteobacteria varg ke wibrionalis gana kaa ekmaatr sadsy he. yah mithe or khaare jal main rahete hai or is kul kii bahut si sadsy jatiyaan rogajanak hai, jinamein haiza utpann karne vaali wibrio coliraye shaamil hai. adhikansh jeevadeeptiman bacteria isi kul ke sadsy hai or aksar gahare jal main rahane vaale praaniyon ke sahjivi hote hai.
