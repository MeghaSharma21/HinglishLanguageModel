 manushyon kii anuvanshiki main pitruvansh samooh this yaa waahe-diene hapalogrup S ek pitruvansh samooh he. yah pitruvansh swayan pitruvansh samooh amaunopines se utpann hui ek shakha he. is pitruvansh ke purush zyaadaatar oshiania ke melanesia kshetra main basate hai, lekin kuch-kuch indonesia ke nazadiiki hisson main bhi paae jate hai. mana jaataa he ke mul pitruvansh samooh this jis purush ke saath armbh hua vah aaj se 28,000-41,000 saal pehle nayaa gini yaa uske ird-gird ke kisi ilaake kaa rahane vaala tha.
 angrezi main "vansh samooh" ko "hapalogrup", "pitruvansh samooh" ko "waahe chromosome hapalogrup" or "matruvansh samooh" ko "amtidiene hapalogrup" kehete hai.
