 kumaun abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay yaa kumanu enginring college bharat ke uttrakhand rajya ke almoda jile main sthit ek abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay he, jiskaa ekmaatr uddeshy rajya main praudyogikeey shiksha pradaan karnaa he. is mahavidhyalay kii sthapana 1991 main ki gai thi or pichley 19 varshon kii yatra main isane bahut se badlaav dekhen hai. yah santhaan uttrakhand sarkaar dwaara purnatah vitt-poshit he or iskaa prabandhan rajya sarkaar ke praudyogiki mantri kii adhyakshata main adhyaksh board karata he. is santhaan kaa pradhanacharya adhyaksh board kaa sadsy sachiv he. yah mahavidhyalay, rajya sarkaar dwaara paudi ke govind ballab pant abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay ke saath milakar rajya kaa prastaavit rashtriya praudyogiki santhaan he.
