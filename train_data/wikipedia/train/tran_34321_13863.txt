 diyaara ek sthaneey shabdaawalee he jo bhojpuri bhashi kshetra main nadi ke baadh maidan main sthit ek sthalaroop ke liye istemaal hoti he. vastutah yah vishesh paristhitiyon main nirmit baadh kaa maidan hi he kintu ganga or sarayu nadiyon ke baadh kaa maidan inke visarpo ke kaaran nadi ke mul paat se kaaphi dur tak vistrut hota he or akruti main ardhavritakar yaa ardhachandrakaar hota he. nadiyon ke ke kinare yah upjau kshetra he jo adhik prabal baadh aane par dub jaataa he. aamtaur par diyaara kaa kuch kshetra salanaa barh kaa shikaar hota he. doaab kshetra ke viparit diyaara kshetra chota or tapunuma hota he. ganga tathaa isaki sahaayak nadiyon main kai diyaara kshetra milte hai jo viral jansankhya kaa pradesh he.
