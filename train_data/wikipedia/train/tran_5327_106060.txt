 samiti log madhya purv se utpann hui un maanav jatiyon ko kaha jaataa he jo kisi sami bhashaa ko apni maatrubhaasha ke rup main bolte hai. in bhashaaon main ibrani bhashaa, arabi bhashaa, akkadi bhashaa, aramai bhashaa, giiz bhashaa, phonishian bhashaa or amharik bhashaa shaamil hai. aadhunik kaal main samiti logon ke mukhya samudaay arab log or yahudee log hai. duniya bhar main samiti logon kii aabaadi 35 karod anumaanit kii gai he jisamen se 30 karod arab, 3.8 karod ithiopia ke habashi log, 40-50 lakh mizrahi yahudee log, 42 lakh asyrian log or 10 lakh malta ke dweep ke log hai.
