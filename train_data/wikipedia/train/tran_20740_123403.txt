 jyotsna milan hindi kii kavyitri evam kathakaar thim. ve striyon ke sanghathan 'seva' ke masik mukhpatr 'anusuya' kii sampadak bhi thim. unhen mahilaon ke utthan tathaa unase jude samvedansheel mudadon ko apni lekhnee ke madhyam se abhivyakt karne main maharath hasil thi. ve varisht sahityakar padmashri rameshchandra shah kii patni thim. 
 ‘ghar nahin’ : ‘a astu kaa’ or ‘khandhar or anya kahaniyaan’ ityadi unki pramukh kritiyaan hai. vibhinn patr-patrikaon main niyamit rup se unki rachanaaen prakashit va prashansit hui he. saath hin ve anek maanad sammaanon se sammanit bhi huin hai. unka dinaank 5 may 2014 ko raat lagbhag aath baje nidhan ho gaya. 
 'ham savita' shri ila r. bhatta kii 'stri-chintan kii pustak' kaa unhone anuwaad evam sampadan kiya. saath hin ila bahan bhatta ke aitihaasik upanyaas 'lorie yuddh' kaa anuwaad or gujarati ke niranjan bhagat, suresh joshi, laal shankar thaakar, gulam mohammad shaikh, priyakanth maniar evam pawan kumaar jain kii kavitaaon kaa bhi unhone anuwaad kiya. varsh 1985-86 ke liye madhyapradesh sarkaar kii muktibodh fellowship evam maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantralay kii senior fellowship upanyaas lekhan ke liye. ramanujam tathaa dharwarkar dwaara sampadit 'di oxford anthology of indian poitry', arlin joid dwaara sampadit 'in der on wois' evam 1998 kii shreshth kahaniyaan ke gujarati sankalan main unki kahani sankalit he.
