  richand gandinar, baran kennesi  ek british raajnneetijna the. unhen 7 may 1965-30 april 1969 ke bich, maharani elizabeth dwitiya dwaara, australia ke governor-general yani maharajyapal ke pad par niyukt kiya gaya tha. is kaal ke dauraan ve, maharani ke pratinidhi ke rup main, unki anupasthiti ke dauraan shaasha ke kartavyon kaa nirvaah karte the. iske alaava, apane vyavsaayik jivan ke dauraan, unhone british samrajya kii seva main, vishwa bhar main vistrut vibhinn british upniveshon main, anya anek mahatvapurn va varchasvpurn padon par apni seva di thi.
