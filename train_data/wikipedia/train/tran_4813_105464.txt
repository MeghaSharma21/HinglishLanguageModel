 shalya kriya ke liye us sthiti kaa nirmaan karnaa jisase kii vyakti ko dard or ahsaas na ho. yah kai prakaar se kiya ja saktaa he. kisi vyakti ko puri tarah se behosh karke yaa kisi hisse ko chetanashunya karke. puri tarah se behosh karne ko general anaesthesia or kisi hisse ko chetanashunya karne ko local yaa rizanal anaesthesia kaha jaataa he. is avasthaa ko pradaan karne vaale chikitsak ko murchak, behoshee kaa doctor kehete hai.
