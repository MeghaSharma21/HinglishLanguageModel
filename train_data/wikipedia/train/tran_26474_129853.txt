 naresh bedi ek bhartiya film nirmaataa he, jo vanya-jivan se sambandhit film nirmaan ke liye jane jate hai. unhen vruttchitr, anemation or laghufilmon ke liye yojit mumbai antarrashtriya film mahotsav-2016, ke udghaatan samaaroh main ve shantaram life time achievement puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya. unhen unke prakruti tathaa vanya jivan par filmakan ke liye isse purv wild screen award, prithvi ratna puraskaar, da hwel award jaise anekon puraskaar diye ja chuke hai. inaki ek anya film ‘ganga ghadiyal’ ne wild screen red pandas puraskaar, jise green askar kaha jaataa he, jeeta he. yah puraskaar jeetne vaale ve pratham asian hai. unhone apane filmon main durlabh vanya jeev kshanon ko filmaaya he. inke filmon ko bi.bi.si. neshnal jeographic, discovery or canal plus jaise pratishthit channelon par prasaarit kiya gaya he. unka janm uttarakhand ke haridwar main hua or ve vahiin par paley bade. jangalon tathaa jantuon ke prakritik awaas kaa inhone najadik se adhyayan kiya he.
