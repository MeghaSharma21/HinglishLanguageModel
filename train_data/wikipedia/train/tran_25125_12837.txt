 shakkaan-ho ek paramparaagat japani maapan pranali he. iskaa naam shakkaan-ho do shabdon se bana he:- shaku - lambaai kii ek ikai or kaan -bhaar maap kii ikai.
 yah pranali multah chini he. isaki ikaiyaan shang vansh ke kaal main 13veen shatee i.pus. main baniin thim. yah 10veen shatee i.pus. main sthaayee rup se kaam main ayin or japan, korea or dakshin purv asia tak failin. tang vansh kii ikaiyaan japan main 701 main aadhikarik rup se apana lee gayin. vartmaan shaku maapan, tab se ab tak shaayad hi kuch badla ho. kai taiwani maapan ikaiyaan shakkaan-ho pranali se vyutpann hai.
 1924 main, shakkaan-ho pranali kaa sthaan metric pranali ne le liya or puraatan ikaayiyon kaa prayog 31 march 1966 se aadhikarik rup se nishedh kar diya gaya. phir bhi aaj kai sthanon par yah prayog me aati he. japani badhaigiri or kheti-baadi main inkaa prayog saamaanya he. japani auzaar sun or bu ke hi maap se ab bhi banaaye jate hai. bhoomi ab bhi tsubo ke naap se hi bikati he. san 2005 main japani logon ko apane makaanon kaa kshetrafal varg meter yaa tsubo, kisi main bhi dene ko kah gaya tha.
 shakkaan-ho ke bhi kai sanskaran hai. nimnlikhit sarini inamen se ek saamanytah prayog ho rahaa, edo kaal kaa maapan dikhaati he. san 1891 main adhikansh ikaayiyon ko metric paribhaasha di gayi thi.
