 shankar keshav kanetkar marathi ke suprasiddh kavi. 
 kanetkar kaa janm 28 oktober 1893 i. ko fatyapur, rahimatpur, jila satara main hua. aapane em.e. kii pariksha uteern kii or lekhankaarya main jute gaye. 'abhaagi camel', 'ambarai', 'kanchanganga', 'chandralekha' tathaa 'aniket' ityadi aapke prakashit kavyagranth hai. 'natyachata' naamak ek aalochanatmak granth kaa bhi aapane pranayan kiya he. 4 disambar 1973 i. ko 80 varsh kii aayu main aapkaa dehavsaan ho gaya.
