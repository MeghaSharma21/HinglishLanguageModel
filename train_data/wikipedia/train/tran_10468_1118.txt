 
 sonkutta yaa vanajukkur kutton ke kul kaa junglee praani he jo dakshin asia or dakshin purv asia main paaya jaataa he. yah apni prajati kaa iklauta jivit praani he jo ki kutton se dantavali or stnagron main alag he. ab yah antarrashtriya sanghathan dwaara viluptpraaya prajati ghoshit ho gai he kyonki inke awasaiya kshetra main kami, shikaar kii kami, anya shikariyon se spardhaa or shaayad gharelu yaa junglee kutton se beemaari sarne ke kaaran inaki sankhya tezi se ghat rahi he.yah ek nihaayat saamaajik praani he jo ki bade kutumbon main rahata he jo ki aksar shikaar ke liye chote tukdon main bant jate hai. africa junglee kutton kii bhaanti or anya kutton ke vipareet dhol shikaar ke baad apane shavakon ko pehle khaane detaa he. haalaanki dhol manushyon se darataa he, lekin inke jhund bade or khatarnaak praaniyon, jaise junglee shookar, junglee bhainsa tathaa baagh par bhi akraman karne se nahin hichkichaate hai.
 chunki dhol shabd sirf angrezi bhashaa main hi bolaa jaataa he atah is shabd kii vyutpatti kaa anumaan lagaana kathin he, lekin aisa anumaan he ki yah shabd kannada bhashaa ke tola shabd se udghrut hua he jiskaa arth he bhediya. iske anya paryaay hai junglee kutta, laal bhediya, chennai, laal kutta, or pahari bhediya. 
 desi bhashaa main dhol ke kai naam hai masalan laal rakshas, junglee rakshas himalay ke ilaakon main isko bhausa, bhansa or buansu ke naamon se bulaayaa jaataa he.
