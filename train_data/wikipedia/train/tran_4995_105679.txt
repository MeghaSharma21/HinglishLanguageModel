 jain dharm main shravak shabd kaa prayog grihasth ke liye kiya gaya hai. shravak ahimsa aadi vraton ko sampurna rup se sweekaar karne main asamarth hota hai kintu tyagavritiyukt, grihasth maryaada main hi rahakar apni tyagavritti ke anusaar in vraton ko alpaansh main sweekaar karata he. shravak shabd kaa mul 'shravan' shabd main hai, arthath, vah jo sunata hai. 
 upaask, anuvrati, deshvirat, saagaar aadi shravak ke paryaayi shabd hai. jain granth, tatwarth sutara ke anusaar :
 shravak ke chha: aavyashak bataaye gaye he
