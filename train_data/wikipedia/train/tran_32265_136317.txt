 pranodan kisi vastu ko gati dene ke liye lagaaye gayi bal kaa utpaadan karne ke saadhan ko kehete hai. kisi bhi pranodan pranali main yantric shakti banane kaa strot or phir is shakti ko dhakelne ke liye bal main parivartit karne kaa koi pranodak aavashyak hota he. praudyogic pranaaliyon main yantric shakti strot ko aksar ingine yaa motor kaha jaataa he. phir is shakti ko pahiyon va dhuree, nodak yaa tezi se peeche kii ore gas yaa anya saamagree phenkane vaale raaket dwaara dhakelne ke bal main parivartit kar ke gati praapt kii jaati he. jaivik pranodan pranaaliyon main koi praani apni mansapeshiyon ko shakti strot or pankhons, finon yaa taangon ko pranodak banaata he.
