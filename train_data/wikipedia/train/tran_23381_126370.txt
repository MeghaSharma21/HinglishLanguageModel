 vatting for a vija naamak bhimrao ambedkar ne angreji main apni aatmakatha likhi thi. isaki prishth sankhya 20 evam lekhan kaa san 1935-36 hai. vatting for a vija ke sampadak pro francej dablyoo pritchet ne aantarik saakshyon ke aadhaar par anumaan lagaaya he ki yah 1935 yaa 1936 kii rachana he. isamen ambedkar dwaara unke hastlekh main taiyaar kii gai asprishyata ke saath apane anubhavon se sambandhit yaaden shaamil hai. ‘vatting for a vija’kaa pehla prakaashan pipuls ajukeshan sosayadi dwaara 1990 main hua tha. isaki pandulipi bhi vahiin uplabdh he. ise america ke colombia vishvavidhyalay main pathyapustak ke taur par padhaayaa jaataa he. angreji se iskaa hindi anuwaad savita pathak ne kiya he.
