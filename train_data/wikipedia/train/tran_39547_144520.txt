 yah vishwa kii ek pramukh jalsandhi hai. isaki lambaai 805 kimi yaa 500 mile he. iskaa naam malakka ke sultanate par padaa he. yah jalsandhi adhik gaharaa nahin jiske kaaran adhik bade jahaaj yahan se nahin ja sakate. lekin prashanth mahaasaagar or hindmahaasaagar ke bich ke jal-maarg main sthit hone ke kaaran iskaa bahut mahatv he. solahavin sadi main portugaliyon ne is mahatvapurn maarg par kabza karne kaa abhiyaan chalaaya tha. satrahavin sadi main dachon ne ise portugalio se chin liya or 80 saalon ke baad ise britishon ko ek sandhi ke tahat de diya.
 indonesia-malaysiamalay prayadweep or indonesian dweep sumatra ke bich sthit he ! yah hind mahaasaagar tathaa prashanth mahaasaagar ko jodti he.
 yah jal sandhi hind mahaasaagar or prashanth mahaasaagar jodatii he. yah donon mahasaagaron kaa milan bindu he. yah bhugolic vibhaagon ko saanskrithik rup se jodatii he.
 is jalmaarg se asia ke liye tel jaataa he tathaa indolaysian coffee kaa vyapaar pramukh he.
