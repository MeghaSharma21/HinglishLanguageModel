 bandar melaka yaa melaka shahar yaa malakka shahar malaysia ke melaka rajya kii rajdhani he or malakka jalsandhi par sthit ek bandargah he. isaki sthapana 14veen shatabdi main singapore ke antim raja, parameshwar, ne kii thi jinke purv rajya singapore par majaapahit samrajya ne san 1398 main akraman kiya tha. iske baad raja parameshwar ne malay prayadweep par aakar san 1402 main ek nae shahar va rajya kii sthapana kari jo aaj kaa bandar melaka he. dwitiya vishwayuddh main isapar japaniyon kaa adhikaar ban gaya or 1951 main yah naye swatantra hue malay gantantr kaa bhaag ban gaya. pracheen kaal se hi bharat tathaa china se iskaa vyapaarik sambandh he par isaki atyadhik vruddhi angrejon ke aane ke baad hi hui.
 naveen bandar melaka main ab bhi portugali or holland vaasiyon ke pracheen bhawanon ke dhvansaavashesh milte hai. yah purvi asia kaa sabase mahatvuparn tathaa bada audyogic centre he. iske prishthpradesh main bumadhyarekhiy saghan sadabahaar van paae jate hai. tateey bhaagon main pashchim kii ore mangrove jati ke vriksh adhik paae jate hai. yahan kaa mukhya udhyam krishi he. yahan ke nivasee, rabar, dhan, nariyal, ananas tathaa garam masaalon kii kheti karte hai. is bandargah se rabar, nariyal, chaaval tathaa garam masaalon kaa niryaat hota he.
 sumatra tathaa malay prayadweep ko ek duusare se alag karanevaala ek jaldmarumadhya he jo dakshin china sagar tathaa hind mahaasaagar ko aapas main milaataa he. is jalsandhi kii lambaai 500 mile tathaa chaudaai 25 mile se 100 mile tak he. iske dakshin-purvi chor par sthit ek chote dweep par singapore sthit he. is jalsandhi ke dwaara sansar kaa sabase adhik maal aata jaataa he.
