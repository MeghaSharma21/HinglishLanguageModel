 maanjhi bharatvarsh kii yah ek adikalin machuaaraa jati he. maanjhi jati mulat: hindu dharm se sambandhit he. yah akhetak arthath shikari jati ke he. inkaa ullekh mahabharat evam ramayana jaise bharat ke pracheen dharmik granthon main miltaa he. yah jati prachinkaal se jal jangal or zamin par ashrith he. varnavyavasthanusaar yah jati shudra shrenee ke antargat aati he. chuki yah jati mukhyat: jal se sambandhit vyavsaay kar apana jeevanyaapan karte chale aae hai. isi liye maanjhi, "machuaaraa"\ "mallaah" \"kevat" \"majhavaar" kii hi kai up-jatiyon main se ek he. bharat sarkaar ke dwaara kiye gaye 1961 ke janaganana main spasht rup se maanjhi or majhavaar ko ek duusare kaa paryaayavachi mana he. at: in tathyon se yah spost he kii maanjhi, mallaah or majhavaar yah tino hi moolyat: ek hi jati hai, desh ke alag-alag jagah rahane va alag sthaneey boli ke kaaran hi inhen or bhi alag-alag naamon se janaa jaataa he.
