 vadodara vimaanashetra yaa civil vimaanashetra haarani gujarat ke baroda shahar ke kendra se uttar-purv disha main sthit ek nagar vimaanashetra he. yah baroda ke upnagar haarani main sthit he. 
 ab tak antardeshiy rahaa baroda vimaanashetra vartmaan main nirmaanadhin terminal ke purna hokar prachaalan main aate hi anterrashtriya vimaanashetra ban jaayegaa. abhi yahaan kaa antardeshiy terminal apni chat par bane gumbadon evam apni mishrit gujarati evam vaast shaileyon ke kaaran anya vimaanashetra bhawanon se bhinn he. yah terminal abhi chota he evam badi sankhya main yatri maatra vahan nahin kar paata he. 
 terminal kii kshamta vruddhi karne evam ise anterrashtriya vimaanashetra banane hetu yahaan ek nayaa ekikrut terminal bhawan nirmaan karne kaa nirnay liya gaya tha. naye terminal bhawan ke liye abhiklpana karne hetu ek anterrashtriya vaastu abhiklpana pratiyogita yojit kii gayi thi. parinamswaroop sanyukt rajya aadhaarit bharat ke ek consortium jenslar, frederick shwartz architects and kriyative group dwaara yah pratiyogita jiti gayi evam naye terminal bhawan ke roopankan kaa theka mila.
