 shri kolayat ji yaa kolayat rajasthan ke shahar yah bikaner se lagbhag 30 mile dakshin-pashchim main isi naamake railway stesan ke nikat basaa isi naam kii tehsil kaa mukhyalay he. yahaan isi naam se prasiddh ek talab he, jiske kinare kapil muni kaa ashram mana jaataa he. prativarsh karthik poornima ke din yahaan mela lagata he jisamen log kapil muni ke ashram ke darshanath aate hai. paas hi dhuni nath kaa ek anya mandir he. pushkar ke samaan yahaan ke talab ke kinare bahut se ghat or mandir bane hue hai, jo saghan pipal ke vrikshon kii sheethal chaaya se aachhadit hai. kolayat ji main bikaner kaa sabase acha ekikrut township kapilmuni vatika jahaan par aadhunik jivan style kii sabhi suvidhaayen uplabdh hai kapil muni vatika ke andar park club house jaisi sabhi aadhunik suvidhaayen uplabdh he yahaan par pine kaa shuddh pani or drenez kii acchhee vyavastha he yahaan par gramin kshetra nirmaan sangh kaa pradeshkaryaalay bhi he yahaan par kai dharmashalas evam dev mandir bhi vidyaman hai.shri
