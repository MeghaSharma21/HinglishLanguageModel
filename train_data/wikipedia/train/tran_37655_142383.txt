 abhinn shyaam gupta bharat ke purush badminton khilaadi he. vartmaan main yah elahabaad shahar main rahete he or yah ekal main ek rashtriya champion he. gupta ne purushon ke ekal main 2004 greeshmakaleen olympic main badminton khelaa, dakshin korea ke park tai-sang se 32 ke daur main haar gaye the.
 gupta ko bhartiya badminton main unke yogadan ke liye arjuna puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
 abhinn elahabaad vishvavidhyalay se bi com paas hai. ve vartmaan rup se indian oil korporation limited, soobedaar terminal, elahabaad main up prabandhak hai.
 abhinn 2004 kaa limca book record rakhte hai. ve bharat ke pehle shatler hai jo sabhi shreniyon main rashtriya champion rahe hai - mini, sab-junior, junior or senior .
