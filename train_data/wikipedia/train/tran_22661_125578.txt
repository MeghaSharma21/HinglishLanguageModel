  saiyad zafar ali shah ek pakistani rajaneta or pakistan kii qaumi asembli ke purv sadsy the. ve 17/10/1993 se 1996/05/11 ke liye pakistan kii qaumi asembli ke upaadhyaksh niyukt kiye gaye the. upaadhyaksh hone ke naate, ve sabhadhyaksh, kii anupasthiti main, unke ke kaaryon kaa nirvaah karte the.
