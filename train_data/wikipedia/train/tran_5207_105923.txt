 rajasthan praachyavidya pratishthan rajasthan sarkaar dwaara sthapit ek santhaan he jo rajasthani samskruti evam viraasat ko sangrakshit rakhane evam uski unnati karne ke uddeshy se sthapit kiya he. isaki sthapana 1954 main muni jinvijay ke maargdarshan main kii gayi thi. muni jinvijay royal aciatic sosayadi ke sadsy the. bharat ke raashtrapati dow rajendra prasad ne 1955 main isaki aadharshila rakhi. 14 sitambar 1958 ko iskaa udghaatan hua. iskaa mukhyalay jodhpur main he.
