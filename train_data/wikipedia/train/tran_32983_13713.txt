 drishy pradooshan ek prakaar kii pareshaani he, jo pradooshan ke kaaran utpann hoti he. drishy pradooshan manushyon ke dekhne vaale kshetron main nakaaratmak badlaav karne par hote hai. jaise hare bhare ped paudhon ko kaat dena, mobile aadi ke tower laga dena. bijli ke khambe, sadak aadi sthanon main bikhare kachare aadi is shrenee main aate hai. yah ek tarah ke banaavat ke kaaran bhi hota he, jise bina paryaavaran aadi ko dekhe hi bana diya jaataa he. jaise kisi sthaan par keval imaarat, makaan aadi kaa hona.
 saarvajanik sthanon main jab sarkaar yaa uski dekh rekh karne waalon kaa niyantrak nahin hota or vyapaaree adhik laabh kamaane ke liye is tarah ke nirmaan karata he. yaa phir kisi rikt sthaan kaa upyog apane udyog yaa anya kisi imaarat aadi ko banane hetu karata he. iske alaava bina kisi yojana yaa paryaavaran aadi ko andekha kar ke banaai gai imaarat, makaan aadi bhi drishy pradooshan ke kaarak hote hai.
