 tum bin 2 2016 main bani bhartiya hindi film he, jiskaa lekhan or nirdesan anubhav sinha ne kiya he. iskaa nirmaan sinha or bhushan kumaar ne tii-siries ke binar tale kiya he. ye 2001 kii "tum bin" film shrunkhlaa kii agali film he. jise cinemagharon main 18 navambar 2016 se dikhaaya gaya. 
 is film ke nirmaan kii aadhikarik ghoshnaa march 2016 main kii gai or photography april 2016 se shuru hui. is film kaa zyaadaatar hissaa scotland main shoot hua he.
