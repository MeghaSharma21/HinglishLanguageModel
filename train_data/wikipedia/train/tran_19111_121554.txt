 mahatma gandhi maarg yaa mudrika maarg yaa ring road dilli ke do ring margon main se ek he. doosraa he dow headgavar maarg yaa bahari ring maarg . donon kii lambaai milakar 87 kimi he.
 40 ki. me. lambaa mahatma gandhi maarg praayah signal free he, arthath kuch kshetra chhodkar isapar koi trephic signal nahin hai. is puure maarg par donon ore teen len sadak he, jo ki chaar len main badali ja rahi he. is sadak par dheron pul yaa flai over hai. 
 kuch hi samay pehle is maarg par robotic sadak safai pranali kaa prayog shuru hua he. is maarg par dilli parivahan nigam mudrika naam kii bas seva chalaati he.
 is maarg par bahut se pramukh bas stop padate hai. ye maarg main aane vaale pramukh sthanon ke liye bane hai.
 is maarg ko signal free banane hetu is maarg par fli overon kii bahutayat he. jinamein se mukhya hai:-
 atyant tej yatayaat hone se maarg ko paar karne main paidal yatriyon ko kathinaayee naa aaye va yatayaat bhi baadhit na ho, iske liye anek andarpas yaa subway banaae gaye hai.
