 
 asherikia proteobacteria sangh ke gammaproteobacteria varg ke anterobacteriasi kul kaa ek vansh he. yah ek antarbijanu na banane vaala, chadi-akruti kaa vikalpi avayujeev bacteria he, jo sabhi anya proteobacteria kii bhaanti gram-rinatmak he. asherikia kii kuch jatiyaan niyattapi praaniyon ke jathrantra kshetra main rahati hai or un praaniyon ko vitamin ke pradaan karti hai. kuch anya jatiyaan rogajanak hai.
