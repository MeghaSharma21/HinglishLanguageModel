 yah hindi tathaa urdu mul ke angreji shabdon kii suchi he. kai hindittha urdu samakaksh shabd sanskrit se nikle hai; dekhen sanskrit mul ke angrezi shabdon kii suchi. kai anya farsi bhashaa mul ke hai. kuch baad vaale arabi tathaa turki mul ke hai. kai maamalon main shabd angreji bhashaa main kai raaston se aaye hai jisase antatah vibhinn arth, vartanee tathaa uccharan ho gayi hai jaisaa ki europea mul ke shabdon ke saath hua he. kai shabd angreji main british raaj ke dauraan aaye jab kai log hindi tathaa urdu ko hindustani kii kism maanate the. in upniveshkal ke udhaar aaye huye shabdon ko, praayah anglo indian kaha jaataa he.
