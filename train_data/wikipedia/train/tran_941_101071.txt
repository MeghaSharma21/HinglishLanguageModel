 risat-2 bharat kaa ek kritrim upgrah he. 'risat', radar imaging satellite kaa laghurup he. yah sabhi mausamon main surakshatmak-nigaraanee karne vaala upgrah he. ise bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan ne 20 april ko sriharikota se prakshepit kiya. risat-do kisi bhi mausam main prithvi kii tasviir khinchne main sakasham he or baadh evam bhuskhalan jaisi prakritik aapadaaon ke bare main bhi pataa lagane main upyogi he. 
 risat-2 main sithentic epperchar radar laga he jo purv main prakshepit bharat ke sudur samvedan upgrahon se alag he. yah signal ke liye kai antina se las he, jisase spasht tasviren khinchi ja sakati hai. esar kaa nirmaan israel kii airospes industries ne kiya he, jo raksha kshamtaaon se yukt he. esar din-raat kisi bhi mausam or badal vaale mausam main bhi tasviir khinch saktaa he. isse pehle bhartiya upgrahon main aisi kshamta nahin thi.
