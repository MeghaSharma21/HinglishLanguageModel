 madhya jurysik yug jurysik kalp ke teen bhuvaigyanik yugon kii shrunkhlaa kaa doosraa yug tha. yah aaj se lagbhag 17.4 karod varsh purv purv jurysik yug kii samaapti ke saath armbh hua or iskaa ant aaj se lagbhag 16.3 karod varsh purv uttar jurysik yug ke armbh hone par hua. madhya jurysik yug main panjiya adhimahadweep loreshia or gondvanaa ke do khandon main tuutane laga or atlantic mahaasaagar astitv main aa gaya.
