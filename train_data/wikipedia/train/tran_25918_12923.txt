 gulshan baavaraa ke naam se mashhoor film geetkaar gulshan mehta ne hindi film udyog ke 49 varsh ke seva kaal main 250 geet likhe. avibhajit bharat shaikhupura main janme gulshan mehta ko baavaraa kaa upnaam film vitarak shantibhai patel ne diya tha. baad main puraa film udyog unhen isi naam se pukaarane laga. filmon main kaam shuru karne ke pehle baavaraa bhartiya rail main kaam karte the. film udyog main unhen pehla geet likhane kaa avasar 1959 main film chandrasena main mila tha.
 unka hit geet film 'satta bazaar' ke liye 'chaandi ke chand tukde ke liye' tha. unhone kalyanji-aanandji ke sangeet nirdesan main 69 geet likhe. jabaki, aar di burman ke saath 150 geet likhe the. unhone film 'sanam teri kasam', 'agar tum na hote', 'satte pe satta', 'yah vaada rahaa', 'haath kii safai' or 'rafu chakkar' ko apane geeton se sajaayaa tha. baavaraa ko film 'upkaar' main 'mere desh kii dharti' or film 'zanjeer' main 'yaari he imaan meraa' geet ke liye film fere puraskaar mila tha. pichley saat saal se vah board of indian parfarming rhite sosayadi ke nideshak pad par karyrat the. 
 7 agast ko gulshan baavaraa kaa nidhan mumbai ke palihill sthit nivas main lambii beemaari ke baad dil kaa daura padane ke baad nidhan ho gaya. unke icchaanusaar unke mritdeh ko jeje aspataal ko daan diya jayega.
