 mohammad ali johar:, jinhen moulana mohammad ali johar ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he, ek bhartiya muslim netaa,kaaryakarta, vidwaan, patrkaar or kavi the. vartmaan main mohammad ali ke sammaan main rampur jile main mohammad ali johar vishvavidhyalay samarpit he.
 mohammad ali kaa janm 1878 main rampur main, british bharat rampur riyasat main main hua tha.. unke pita, abdul ali khan, jab ve paanch varsh ke the, tab mrityu ho gai thi. unke bhai moulana shaukat ali the, jo khilaafat andolan ke netaa bane. apane pita kii mrityu ke baavjood, johar ne daarul ulum, aligarh muslim vishvavidhyalay main bhaag liya or 1898 main, linkan college, oxford main aadhunik itihaas kaa adhyayan kiya.bharat lautne par, unhone rampur rajya ke shiksha nideshak ke rup main kaary kiya or baad main baroda naagarik seva main shaamil ho gaye. vah ek british lekhak or times, landan, da manchester gardian or da objarwar jaise pramukh samachar patron main lekh likhte rahe the. or ek doordarshee raajnneetik netaa ke vaktaa the. unhone kalakatta main 1911 main angreji saptahik da commered kaa shubharambh kiya. yah teji se parisancharan or prabhaav arjeet kiya. vah 1912 main dilli chale aae or vahaan unhone 1913 main ek urdu bhashaa ke dainik samachar patr hamdard kaa shubharambh kiya. johar ne 1902 main amajadi bano begum se shaadi kii. begum sakriy rup se rashtriya or khilaafat andolan main shaamil thim..johar ne aligarh muslim vishvavidhyalay kaa vistaar karne ke liye kadi mehnat kii, jise muhammadan anglo-oriental college ke naam se janaa jaataa tha or 1920 main jamiya milia islamia ke sah-sansthaapakon main se ek the.
