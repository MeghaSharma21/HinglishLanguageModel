 qaza yaa qadaa kuch arab deshon main dwitiya str ke prashasnic vibhaag hote hai jinka darja lagbhag bharat ke zilon ke baraabar hota he. udhaaharan ke liye iraq ke praanton ko qazaon main baanta jaataa he. iskaa naam usamaani samrajay ke jamaane main padaa tha - qaza us kshetra ko kaha jaataa tha jo kisi qaazi kii nigaraanee main rakhaa gaya ho.
 'qaza' ko arabi lipi main 'قضاء‎‎' likhaa jaataa he jisamen 'ض‎' kaa akshar bhartiya upmahadweep, afghanistan or iran main 'za' kaa uccharan rakhata he, jabaki arab deshon main iskaa uccharan 'da' yaa 'dha' se miltaa-julata kiya jaataa he. isliye is vibhaag ke naam ko - 'qaza' or 'qadaa' - donon uccharanon ke saath paaya jaataa he.
