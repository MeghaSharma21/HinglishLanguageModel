 sanjay rashtriya udyaan bharat ke madhya pradesh rajya main sidhi jile main sthit ek rashtriya udyaan he. is udyaan kaa vistaar 466.657 varg kimi kaa he or yah sanjay-dubri tiger reserve ke antargat padataa he.
 rashtriya udyaan zyaadaatar saal jangalon se bana he. udyaan baagh, tendua, chital, sambhar, junglee boar, nilgai, chinkaara, civet, saahi, goh or pakshiyon ke teen sau nau prajatiyaan hone kaa daawa karata he. sabase aakarshak pakshiyon main golden hoded oriel, racket poonch drongo, bhartiya pitt ruphus-treepi, lesar adjutent, laal sir vaala giddh, sanras giddh, bhartiya safed poonch vaala giddh, misr kaa giddh, nitzars or kai anya prajatiyaan hai.
 sanjay rashtriya udyaan ko san 1981 main madhya pradesh main sthapit kiya gaya tha. san 2000 main madhya pradesh ke vibhaajan ke baad iskaa ek bada bhaag chhattisgarh rajya ke paas chalaa gaya.
 is udyaan main mukhyatah saal ke van hai. baagh, tendua, chital, sambhar, junglee suar, chinkaara, nilgai, sehi, goh ityadi yahan ke mukhya aakarshan hai. iske alaava is udyaan main pakshiyon kii 309 prajatiyaan pai jaati hai.
