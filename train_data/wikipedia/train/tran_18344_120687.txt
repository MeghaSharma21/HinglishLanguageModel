 yah lekh jaadugar jaadu vaale manushya ke vishay main he. yadi aap jaadugar film ke bare main jaananaa chaahate hai to jaadugar par jaaen.
 jaadugar kaa tatpary us vyakti se hota he jise jaadu kaa gyaan ho or uskaa upyog kar saktaa ho. aisa gyaan rakhane or upyog karne vaali aurat ko jaaduugarani yaa bhaanumati kehete hai. dusht jaaduugaraniyon ko dian yaa chudail bhi kaha jaataa he. zyaadaatar log jaaduugaron or jaaduugaraniyon ko myth or kaalpanik maanate hai. aadhunik yug main chote-mote jaadu-jaise khel dikhaane vaale ko bhi jaadugar kehete hai. je. ke. rolling rachit kaalpanik upanyaas shrunkhlaa harry potter kaa mukhya kirdaar harry pottershyad 21 veen sadi kaa sabase mashhoor jaadugar he.
