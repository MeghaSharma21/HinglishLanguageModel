 "pavitra upavan " yah ek paryaavarniya sankalpana he | ise angreji bhashaa main  secred grove thi marathi bhashaa main devarai namase janaa jaataa he | bharatmen tathaa videsh main bhi is upavan kii sankalpana he | videshmen ise "carch forest" namase janaa jaataa he| 
 is upavan kii visheshta yah he kii yah upavan jis gaanvake kareeb hota hi us gaanvake log use sambhalate he | shaskeey vyavastha kaa is prakriyaamein sahbhaag nahin hota| yah upavan kisi devataaka nivasasthaan manajata he or uski pavitrataa sanbhali jaati he |
 pashaan yugake kaalase yah pavitra sankalpana samajamen sweekrut kii gai he aisi manyata he | khetike liye jab ped kaatna shuru hua usee kaalmein ped bachaneke liye is sankalpana kii swikar kiya gaya hoga aisi sambhaavna he | 
 inke alaava devarhati, devarkand, siddervanam, orans yah naam bhi prasiddh he | 
 yah upavan bhagavanako samarpit hetu log is upavan main pedki kataai nahi karte| yahan ugnevaale har ek patta, paudha, phool isape bhagwaan kaa adhikaar hota he aisa mana jaataa he | isi kaaran prakrutike sangrakshan ke mahatvapurn kaaryamen suvidha hoti he | is upavan ko vibhinn prajati ke pashu pakshi kitzepana nivasasthaan banaate he | vibhinn prakaarake jadibuti bhi in upawanomen paayi jaati he | isse upavanki prakritik mahatta dhyanamen aati he |
