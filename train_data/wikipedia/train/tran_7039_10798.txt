 rashtriya fishan technology santhaan bharat main fishan dizin, prabandhan or takneekee ke kshetra main ek pramukh santhaan hai. varsh 1986 main bharat sarkaar ke vastra mantralay ke tatwadhan main rashtriya fishan technology santhaan kii ek sheersh nikaay ke rup main sthapana kii gai thi. bharat kii sansad dwaara paarit nift adhinium 2006 ke madhyam se nift ko anya pratishthit sansthaanon kii bhaanti fishan praudyogiki main shiksha ke vikaas or anusandhan ke liye sanvidhik darja pradaan kiya gaya he jisase yah santhaan apane vidhyaarthiyon ko degree or anya shaikshik pramaan patr pradaan kar sakegaa.
 is santhaan ne bhartiya fishan udyog ko vishwa ke utkrisht nipun dizinron, prabandhan or vinirmaan praudyogiki kii jaankaree di he. is santhaan ne shiksha praapt karne kaa vaataavaran taiyaar kiya he isse naveenta, sanrachanaatmak or shreshthata praapt karne main protsaahan miltaa he. nift ek bahushiksha pradaan karne kaa bahuaiami santhaan he, jo nirantar pathpradarshak kii bhoomika adaa karata he.
 is santhaan ne apane baarah kendron jo nai dilli, bangalore, chennai, gandhinagar, hyderabad, kolkata mumbai, raibareli, bhopal, kannur, patna or shillong main sthit hai ke madhyam se fishan shiksha ko puure desh main pahunchane kaa agragami kaary kiya he. nift ke patna, bhopal, shillong, kannur sthit kendron ne july 2008 se apane kaary aarambh kar die hai. nift ne apane in kendron ke madhyam se fishan shiksha, anusandhan or prashikshan ke liye ek samaan manch uplabdh karavaaya he. nift ne bharat se baahar bhi apane pankh failaaye hai or moricious main ek kendra kii sthapana kii he.
