 theocritas unani graamyagiton kaa kavi. 
 tiyokritas siracuj main utpann hua. kuch log use cos main janamaa maanate hai. tritiy shatabdi itho puchho ke uttarardh main jivit tha. misr pahunchne par tolemi philadeleis ne uskaa bahut sammaan kiya. theocritas ne uske sammaan main kuch kavitaayen likhin. siracuj lautne par hiron dwitiya se uski mitrataa hui. uske dwaara rachit graamyagiton main ab pratikshaptansh bahut miltaa he. aisa mana jaataa he ki unani sahitya main graamyagiton kii madhurata kaa samavesh theocritas ne kiya, yadhyapi unani dorian logon main is sahitya ke tatv vidyaman the. theocritas ne apane geeton main sicily ke janajivan ko uski gaharaai main paithkar chitrit kiya he. un geeton main kavi ne kahin madhur hasya bikheraa he to kahin vah jivan ke katu yatharth se shokavihval hokar royaa bhi he. dori bhashaa kii gramin boli main apana sahitya rachate hue usne style or bhav-saundarya ko jivit rakhaa he. vargil or tennisan usase bahut prabhavit jaan padate hai. minek vardsaworth or fretshe ne theocritas kii kavitaaon kaa sampadan kiya he. chapman or kalvarali ne unke angreji anuwaad prastut kiye hai.
