 rambrant haramanazun faan rain ek prasiddh dach chitrakaar the. unhen europea kala itihaas main sabase mahatvapurn chitrakaaron main se ek samaran kiya jaataa he. dach swarna yug ke dauraan unke kalatmak yogadan nazar main aae the, jab dach swarna yug chitrakaari behad urvar or naveenta-yaachak tha.
 javaani main hi chitrakaar ke taur par safalataa hasil karne ke baad, rambrant apane baad ke saalon main kaafii vyaktigat traasadee or vittiya kathinaaiyon ko bardaasht karnaa padaa. phir bhi unki nakkashi evam chitrakaari unke jeevankaal ke dauraan bahut lokapriy thi, ek kalakaar ke taur par unki pratishtha unchi badhati rahi or bees saal tak unhone anek mahatvapurn dach chitrakaaron ko sikhia tha.
 rambrant kii sabase badi rachanatmik praptiyon main khaas taur par unke dwaara banaae samakaleen ke chitra, swaychitr or bybal se drishychitr shaamil hai. unhone swaychitr, ek adwitiya or antarang jeevani kaa nirmaan karte the jisamen kalakaar ne ghamand ke bina or atyant gambhirtha se apane aap kaa surveykshan kiya.
