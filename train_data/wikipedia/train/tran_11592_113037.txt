 maanav dwaara mangal kaa upniveshan, atkal or gambhir adhyayan kaa ek centre bindu he, kyonki uski satahi paristhitiyaan or jal kii uplabdhta yakeenan mangal grah ko soramandal main prithvi ke alaava anya sabase adhik mehmaananwaaj grah banaata he| chandramaa ko maanav upniveshan ke liye pehle sthaan ke rup main prastaavit kiya gaya he, lekin mangal grah ke paas ek patala vayumandal he, jo ise maanav or anya jaivik jivan kii mejabani ke liye samarthy kshamta detaa he | sambhavit upniveshan sthalon ke rup main, mangal or chaand donon ke saath neeche gahare gurutveey kupon main utarane ke saath saath laagat or jokhim kaa nuksaan bhi juda he, jo kshudragrahon ko soramandal main maanav ke praarambhik vistaar ke liye ek or vikalp bana saktaa he| 
 sanrachana, aakaar or satahi gurutvkarshan ke maamle main prithvi apane "judwa grah" shukr ke kaaphi kareeb he, lekin vichaar jab upniveshan ke liye ho tab mangal sarvaadhik sammohak jagah he. prithvi or mangal ke bich nimna samanthaayen he -
 budh ke atyant garm or thande taapmaana, shukr kii satahi garm bhatthi, yaa bahari grahon or unke upgrahon kii tusharajaniki thand ko dekhe to, mangal kii sataha par sthitiyaan kisi anya gyaat grah yaa chaand kii sataha kii tulana main vaasayogyata ke zyada kareeb he. keval shukr ke badalon ke sheershavasyogyata ke maamle main mangal kii tulana main prithvi ke zyada kareeb hai.
