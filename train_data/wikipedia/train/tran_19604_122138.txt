 kadri sampradaay soofiyon kaa ek sampradaay he. iskaa naam abdul qaadir jilani ke naam par rakhaa gaya he jo gilaan ke rahane vaale the. yah sampradaay islam ke multatv par aadhaarit he.
 yah sampradaay arabi bolne vaale deshon ke saath-saath turki, indonesia, afghanistan, bharat, bangladesh, pakistan, balcon, rus, falastin, israel, chini janavaadee ganraajya, purvi africa or pashchimi africa main prasiddh he.
