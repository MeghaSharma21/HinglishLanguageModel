 latikaa sarkaar ek bhartiya nariwadi, saamaajik kaaryakarta, shikshak or vakil thim. vah mahila adhyayan or bharat main mahilaon ke adhikaar ke liye kaam karti he. vah mahila vikaas adhyayan centre, dilli kii sansthaapak sadsy thi, jo 1980 main sthapit hui thi. vah cambridge vishvavidhyalay se snaatak hone vaali pehali bhartiya mahila thim..
 unka janm 4 janvari 1923 main hua tha. vah pashchim bengal main ek kuleen parivaar main paidaa hui thi. unke pita bhi vakil the. sarkaar ne newham college, cambridge main kanoon kaa adhyayan kiya or cambridge vishvavidhyalay se snaatak hone vaali pehali bhartiya mahila bani. phir vahi se hi unhone piech di kii degree praapt kii.
 unhone 1953 main vidhi sankaay, dilli vishvavidhyalay main padhaanaa shuru kiya vah pehali mahila vyaakhyaata thim. 1971 main, vah bharat main mahilaon kii sthiti par samiti ke sadsy bani. 1980 main, ve veena majumdaar dwaara sthapit mahila vikaas adhyayan, dilli ke sansthaapak sadsy bani. 
 23 farvari, 2013 ko unki mrityu hui.
