 jagannatha choudhary ek bhartiya rajaneta the. vah baliya jile ke pramukh congressi netaaon main se ek rahe hai. choudhary uttar pradesh kii duusari vidhansabha sabha main vidhayak rahe. 1957 uttar pradesh vidhaan sabha chunaav main inhone uttar pradesh ke baliya jile ke 215 - sikandarpur vidhaan sabha nirvachan kshetra se congress kii ore se chunaav main bhaag liya. iske baad 1984 ke loksabhaa chunaav me chandrasekhar ko haraakar vah baliya se saansad bane.
 jagannatha choudhary kaa janm baliya janapad ke kathonda gaanv ke ek madhyam vargeey kisaan parivaar main ramalagan choudhary ke chote putr ke rup main 1 janvari 1925 ko hua. vah jameen se jude kshetra ke sarpanch ke rup main apane jivan kii shuruyaat kiye. vah sarpanch ke baad kshetra kii janata ne 57 main baliya janapad ke sikandarpur vidhansabha se vidhayak chune gaye. duusari baar bhi chunaav main sikandarpur kii janata ne apana pratinidhi chunaa. iske baad pun: chilkahar kii janata ne do baar apana vidhayak banaaya. ek baar choudhary ne upra vidhaan parishad ke sadsy evam upra hartico ke adhyaksh ke rup main sarkaar main seva karne kaa mauka mila. baliya loksabhaa se 1984 main chunaav jitkar apni upasthiti darj karai. vah ek maatr janpratinidhi rahe jinhone sarkaar main sinchai evam krishi mantri rahete hue kshetra main naharon kaa jaal bichaaya. loksabhaa kii rahanumai ke darmiyaan shiksha, doordarshan, vidyutikaran, krishi-vigyaan centre, overbrij va rail line ke doharikaran ke beej vapan sambandhi ghaneron kaary gavaahi de rahe he. varsh ke ant or naye varsh ke prarambh milan bindu 31 disambar 1999 kii raat ko brahmalok ko padhaar gaye.
