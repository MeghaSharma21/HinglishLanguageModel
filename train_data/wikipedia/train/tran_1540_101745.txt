 romablon dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh kaa ek dweepasmooh he. yah us desh kii prashasnic pranali main praant kaa darja rakhata he or mimaropa naamak prashasnic kshetra kaa hissaa he. tablas dweep or sibuyan dweep iske sabase bada dweep hai or unke alaava praant main anya kai chote dweep bhi shaamil hai. romablon apane sangemarmar ke khanan ke liye puure desh main mashhoor he or apane sundar baluutaton va anya paryaton sthalon ke liye bhi prasiddh he.
