 haldighati itihaas main maharana pratap or akbar ke bich hue yuddh ke liye prasiddh he. yah rajasthan main eklingaji se 18 kilometre kii duuri par he. yah aravali parvat shrunkhlaa main ek darra he. yah rajsamand or paali jilon ko jodta he. yah udaipur se 40 kimi kii duuri par he. iskaa naam 'haldighati' isaliye padaa kyonki yahan kii mitti haldi jaisi pili he.ise raati ghati bhi kehete hai.
 haldighati kaa yuddh 18 june 1576 i. ko hua tha. is yuddh main maharana pratap vijay hue the. isi yuddh main maharana pratap kaa prasiddh ghoda chetak maaraa gaya tha. ab yahaan ek sangrahalay he. is sangrahalay main haldidhaghati ke yuddh ke maidan kaa ek model rakhaa gaya he. iske alaava yahaan maharana pratap se sambandhit vastuon ko rakhaa gaya he. pravesh shulke: 20 ru. samay: subah 8 baje se shaam 8 baje tak. sabhi din khulaa hua.
