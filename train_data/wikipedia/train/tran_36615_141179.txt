 leyate dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh ke visaaya dweep dal ke purvi visaaya prashasnic kshetra main ek dweep he. yah samaar dweep ke pados main dakshinpaschim disha main sthit he or prashasnic rup se do praanton main vibhajit he: leyate praant or dakshini leyate praant. prashasnic taur par paas kaa panaaon dweep bhi dakshini leyate praant kaa hissaa he. leyate dweep paas ke samaar dweep se saan huaniko jalsandhi dwaara banta hua he lekin yatayaat ke liye isapar ek 2.16 kilometre lambaa samudri pul banaaya gaya he. leyate ke purvottar main biliraan dweep he jo 1992 tak leyate praant kaa bhaag tha lekin us varsh ke baad ek alag praant bana diya gaya.
