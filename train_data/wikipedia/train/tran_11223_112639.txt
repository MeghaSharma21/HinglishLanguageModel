 bhashaa udyog un kaaryakalapon ko kehete hai jo bhashaaon ke computer dwaara prasanskaran se sambandhit upkarnon evam utpaadon ke dizin, utpaadan evam vipanan aadi se sambandh rakhte hai. bhashaa udyog, soochna praudyogiki ke udyog kaa ek bhaag he. isamen software praudyogiki, bhashaa vigyaan, kritrim buddhi evam interphes dizin aadi kii mahatvapurn bhoomika he. 
 bhashaa udyog ke andar aane vaali pramukh sevaayen nimnlikhit hai-
 anuwaad evam interpritesan kaa kaam anadi kaal se hota aa rahaa he. kintu antarjaal ke pradurbhaav ke baad aadhunik bhashaa udyog bahut teji se badha he. bahut bade text ko bahut alp samay main bhi anekon bhashaaon main badla ja saktaa he. yah bhashaa udyog kii bahut badi uplabdhi he. is samay bhinn-bhinn aakaar ke bhashaa-udyog companiyaan astitv main hai kintu koi aisi company nahinhai jiskaa puure vishwa main dabadabaa ho.
