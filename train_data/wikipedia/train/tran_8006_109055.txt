 firoz varun gandhi ek bhartiya raajnneetijna hai. wo bhartiya sansad ke nimna sadan loksabhaa ke sultanpur se sadsy hai. wo bhartiya janata party kii rashtriya kaaryakarini ke sadsy or party ke itihaas main sabase yuva rashtriya mahasachiv hai. isse pehle wo 15veen loksabhaa main pilibhit se saansad rah chuke hai. varun gandhi ko march 2013 main rajnath singh kii team main bhi chunaa gaya. unhen rashtriya mahasachiv chunaa gaya or wo party ke itihaas main sabase yuva mahasachiv hai.
 varun gandhi kaa janm 13 march 1980 ko nai dilli main hua. unke mata pita kaa naam menka gandhi or sanjay gandhi he. wo purv pradhanmantri indira gandhi ke pautra hai. unke pita kii maut ek vimaan durghatana main june 1980 main hui. unke pita ke nidhan ke samay varun gandhi kii aayu maatr 3 maah thi.
 varun gandhi ne 1999 main pilibhit nirvachan kshetra main apni maa ke liye prachaar kaary armbh kiya. unhone apni maa ke saath 2004 main bhaajapa kii sadsyta praapt kii. isse pehle wo rashtriya janatantric gathbandhan kii sadsy thim.
