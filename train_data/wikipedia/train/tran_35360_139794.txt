 yangun myanmar desh kii purana rajdhani he. iskaa purana naam rangoon tha. . bahadur shah zafar yaheen dafan hai. aaazaad hind fouj jiske sarvocch commander netaji subhaashachandr bose the us fouj kaa mukhyalay yaheen tha.
 rangoon dakshini verma ke madhyavarti bhaag main, rangoon nadi ke kinare, martabaan kii khadi tathaa irawadi nadi ke muhane se 30 kimi uttar, sagartal se keval 20 foot kii oonchaai par sthit he. yah burma kii rajdhani, sabase bada nagar tathaa pramukh bandargah he. yahan ausat vaarshik varsha 100 inch hoti he. sameepvarti kshetra main dhan kii krishi adhik hoti he. bandargah se chaaval, tik tathaa anya lakadiyaan, khaalen, petroleum se nirmit padaarth tathaa chaandi, sisa, jasta, taanbe kii vastuon kaa niryaat hota he. vayumarg, nadimarg tathaa railmarg yatayaat ke pramukh saadhan hai. vidyut santhaan, reshmi evam oonee kapade, lakdi chiraai kaa kaam, railway ke saamaan, jalayanirmaan tathaa matsay udyog main kaaphi unnati ho gai he. yahan par sabhi aadhunik vastuen jaise bade bade hotel, cinemaghar, bhandar, pagoda, girjaghar, park, vanaspatik udyaan, ajaayabaghar tathaa vishvavidhyalay aadi hai. yahan kii sabase pramukh imaarat shwedragon pagoda he, jo sagartal se 168 foot kii oonchaai par bana he. yah pagoda 368 foot unchaa, 900 foot lanbaa tathaa 685 foot chaudaa he tathaa iske upar sone kii panni chadhi hui he. nagar ko yuddh tathaa jwalamukhi se kaaphi haani uthaani padi he.
