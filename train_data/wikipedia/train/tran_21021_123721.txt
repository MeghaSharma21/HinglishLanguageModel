 gunnar gunnersan danish bhashaa ke upanyaaskaar, naatakkaar, kavi tathaa kahani lekhak the.
 inkaa janm iceland main 1889 i. main ek saadhaaran krishak parivaar main hua tha. 18 varsh kii aayu main lekhak banane kii balwati ichha lekar denmark gaye or kathin sangharsh ke baad danish bhashaa ke achche lekhak ke rup main sthaan banane main safal hue. 1939 i. main iceland laut aae. inaki rachanaaon main iceland kii saamaanya janata ke du:kha sukh kaa bada hi sundar chithran hua he. inamen apane desh ke prati anuraag he or vahaan ke rahanewaalon ke prati sanman kaa bhav. charitron ke aantarik bhaavon kaa bada sookshm adhyayan inaki rachanaaon main miltaa he. inaki mukhya rachanaayen hai- ‘da story ov da borg femily’ ; ‘sworn bros’ ; ‘seven days darkeness’ ; ‘da carch on da mountain’ .
