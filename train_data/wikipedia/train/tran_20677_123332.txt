 sapos bhartiya rajya chhattisgarh ke mahasamunda zile kaa ek gaanv he. ise green gaanv kaa darja diya gaya he. is gaanv main nity subah bharat kaa raashtragan gaaya jaataa he, jiske liye yah prasiddh he 
  sapos gaanv main 922 kii aabaadi he, jinamein se 447 purush hai jabaki janaganana 2011 ke anusaar 475 mahilaayein hai.sapos gaanv main 0-6 varsh kii umr ke bacchon kii aabaadi 136 he jo gaanv kii kul aabaadi kaa 14.75% he. sapos gaanv kaa ausat ling anupaat 1063 he jo chhattisgarh rajya ausat 991 se adhik he.janaganana ke anusaar sapos kaa bal ling anupaat 1159 he, chhattisgarh ke ausat 969se adhik he.chhattisgarh kii tulana main sapos gaanv main ucch sakshartaa dar he. 2011 main, chhattisgarh ke sakshartaa dar 70.28% kii tulana main sapos gaanv kii sakshartaa dar 71.76% he. sapos main purush sakshartaa 82.5 he.
