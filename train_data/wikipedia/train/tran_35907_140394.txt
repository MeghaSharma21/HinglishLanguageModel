 perth scorchars australian gharelu twanti-20 cricket team hai jo big bash lig main perth kaa pratinidhitiv karti hai. 
 scorchars bibiel itihaas main sabase safal team hai, teen championships jeet rahe hai or do baar daudane vaale dhaavak hai. ve bibiel 02 main brisben hit dwaara apane duusare final main haar gaye the. iske baad unhone agle do championships jite, lig ke itihaas main uplabdhi hasil karne vaali pehali team ban gai. ye jeet hobert hericans or sidney sixrs ke khilaaf canberra ke manuka ovel main ek antim gend thriller main ain.
 miki arthur ko mul rup se coach niyukt kiya gaya tha, lekin australian cricket team ke coach niyukt kiye jane ke baad 2011-12 ke satr kii shuruyaat se pehle chhod diya gaya tha. unhen apane purv sahaayak, lachalan stevens dwaara pratisthapit kiya gaya tha. justin langer ne november 2012 main stevens ko badal diya.
 scorchars ke paas alice vilaani dwaara yojit mahila big bash lig main bhi ek paksh he or lisa keitaly dwaara prashikshit kiya gaya he.
