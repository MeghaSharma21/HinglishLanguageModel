 jogiya janubi pattee, gorakhpur -mokama rashtriyakrut maarg par gorakhpur se 75 kilometre purv, fajilnagar kasbe ke kareeb basaa ek bada gaanv he jo kushinagar janapad main padataa he. is gaanv main ek ati pracheen khandit murti he jo jogir baba ke naam se prasiddh he. yahaan ek mandir ke avshesh hai jo chhathi-saatvin sadi kaa jaan padataa he. jogir baba ke naam se is gaanv kaa naam jogiya padaa he. yah gaanv lokrang kaaryakramon kii vajah se bhi haal-philhal charchaa main aayaa he. yahaan prativarsh desh ke saikadon hindi ke sahityakar/ lok kalakaar jutate hai or do dinon tak apni prastutiyon ko jeevant banaate hai. yah gaanv kathakaar subhash chandra kushvaha kaa paitruk gaanv bhi he.
