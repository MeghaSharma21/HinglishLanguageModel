 rashid jahaan, bharat se urdu kii ek pragatisheel lekhika, kathakaar or upanyaaskaar thim, jinhone mahilaon dwaara likhit urdu sahitya ke ek nae yug kii shuruyaat kii. ve peshey se ek chikitsak thim. 
 unka janm 5 agast 1905 ko uttar pradesh ke aligarh shahar main hua. unki maan kaa naam wahid shahjahan begum or pita kaa naam shaikh abdullah tha. unke pita aligarh ke mashhoor shikshavid or lekhak tathaa aligarh mahila college ke sansthaapakon main se ek the, jinhen sahitya evam shiksha ke kshetra main san 1964 main bharat sarkaar dwaara, padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. raashid kii praarambhik shiksha aligarh main hui. tatpashchaat ve lakhanu ke iza bela thobaron college se inter karne ke baad dilli ke lord harding medical college se varsh 1934 main doctor banakar nikaleen. varsh 1934 main unki shaadi mahmud aljaafara ke saath hui jo urdu ke lekhak hone ke saath amritsar ke islamia college main principal the. vahiin dow rashid jahaan kii mulaakaat faiz ahamed faiz se hui or ve sakriy rup se pragatisheel andolan se jud gayi.
 rashid pehali baar charchaa main tab ai jab unki kahaniyon or naatakon kaa sangrah ‘angaare’ 1932 main prakashit hua, kyonki unki kahaniyon or naatakon main tatkaaleen samaaj main prachlit muslim kattarpanth tathaa yon naitikta ko gahari chunauti di gai thi. jald hi yah british shashan dwaara pratibandhit bhi ho gai. rashid jahaan bhartiya communist party or pragatisheel lekhak sangh kii sakriy kaaryakarta thim. 
 rashid kaa nidhan 29 july 1952, ko moscow, rus main hua.
