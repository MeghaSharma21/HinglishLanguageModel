 mahatrikon mandir, uttar pradaash ke mirzapur shahar main sthit he. yah mandir vindhyaachal railway stesan ke dakshin disha kii ore sthit he. devi kaali or sankat mochan ke darshan karne ke baad bhakt apni parikrama sant karnagiri baawali ke darshan karke puri karte hai. kalikhoh ke aas-paas anya kai mandir jaise anand bhairav, siddhanath bhairav, kapaal bhairav or bhairav aadi sthit he. vindhyaachal mandir or parikrama puri karne ke pashchaat man ko behad sukun praapt hota he. yah puri yatra maha trikon ke naam se prasiddh he.
 kaha jaataa he ki maha trikon kii parikrama karne se bhakton kii ichha puri hoti he. mandir sthit vindhyavashni devi ke darshan karne ke pashchaat bhakt sankat mochan mandir jate hai. is mandir ko kalikhoh ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he.
