 chitra nike ek bhartiya shikshavid, lekhak or saamaajik kaaryakarta he. vah bhartiya shiksha santhaan ke adhyaksh or bharat ke yojana ayog ke visheshgy sadsy bhi he. vah maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantralay dwaara sthapit gaiyr-aupchaarik shiksha samiti kii adhyaksh or rashtriya sakshartaa mishan ke sadsy thi. bharat sarkaar ne 1986 main padam shri ke chauthe ucchtam bhartiya naagarik sammaan se sammanit kiya.
 
 chitra nike kaa janm 15 july 1918 ko maharashtra ke pashchimi bhartiya rajya pune main hua tha. kala main snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii mumbai vishvavidhyalay se. unka kareer bhugangarh, kolhapur jile main gramin santhaan main shuru hua jahaan unhone harijanon ke bich shaikshanik shiviron kaa aayojan kiya, mahilaon kii mahila sanghon banane or bacchon ke decare centre or swasthya clinics kii sthapana kii.
