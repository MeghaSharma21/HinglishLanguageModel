 manavnuma yaa hominid, jinhen mahavanar bhi kehete hai, ek aise praani parivaar kaa naam he jinamein copi parivaar kii ve sari naslein shaamil hai jo manushya hon yaa manushya jaisi hon. inamen manushya, chimpanzi, gorilla or orangutan ke vansh aate hai. kuch aisi bhi manavnuma naslein hai jo vilupt ho chuki hai, jaise kii australopithiks.
 "manavnuma" ko angrezi main "hominid" kehete hai.
