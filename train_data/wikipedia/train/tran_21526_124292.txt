 chhandogya upanishad samavedic chaandogya brahman kaa aupnishadik bhaag he jo pracheenatam das upanishdon main navam evam sabase brihadaakaar he. iske aath prapathkon main prayek main ek adhyay he.
 brahmagyaan ke liye prasiddh chhandogya upanishad kii parampara main a. 8.15 ke anusaar iskaa pravachan brahma ne prajapati ko, prajapati ne manu ko or manu ne apane putron ko kiya jinase iskaa jagat main vistaar hua. yah nirupan bahudhaa brahmavidon ne sanvadatmak rup main kiya. shwethaketu or uddalak, shwethaketu or pravaahan jabli, satyakam jabal or haridrumat gautam, kaamalayan upkosal or satyakam jabal, aupmanyavadi or ashwapati kaikey, naarad or sanatkumar, indra or prajapati ke sanvadatmak nirupan udhaaharan suchak hai.
 sanyas pradhan is upanishad kaa vishay 8-7-15main ullikhit indra ko die gaye prajapati ke upadeshaanusaar, apaap, jaraa-mrityu-shokarahit, visidhits, pipasarahit, satyakam, sathyasankalp aatma kii khoj tathaa samyak gyaan he.
