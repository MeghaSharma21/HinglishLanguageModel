 congou nadi, jo zire nadi bhi kehalaati he, africa kii ek pramukh nadi he. 4,700 kilometre kii duuri tay karne vaali yah nadi pashchim madhya africa kii sabase vishaal or nil nadi ke baad africa kii sabase lambi nadi he. congou nadi vishwa kii samast nadiyon main, dakshin america kii amenzan nadi ke baad, duusari sabase adhik jalpravaah vaali nadi he. lambaai main yah duniya kii nauvi sabase lambi nadi he or purv africa rift kii pahadiyon-patharon main apane strot se lekar atlantic mahaasaagar main vilay tak 4,700 kimi kaa faaslaa tay karti he. apane maarg main congou nadi do baar bumadhya rekha paar karti he. is vishaal nadi kaa jalsambhar kshetra bhi vishaal he or isamen lagbhag 40 lakh varg kimi aate hai, jo ki puure africa mahadweep kaa 13% kshetrafal he.
 isaki sampurna lambaai 2,900 mile he. iskaa pravaahakshetra 14,25,000 varg mile he. nadi apane muhane par saat mile chaudaa rupadhaaran kar samudr main girti he. yah samudr main prati second 20 lakh ghan foot keechad yukt pani giraati he. yah nadi madhya africa ke 4,650 foot kii oonchaai se nikalati he uttari rhodesia main chambezi taduparant lua pula naam se vikhyath he. yah nadi 200 foot kii oonchaai se girakar stanley jalprapaat kaa srijan karti he. iske pashchaat yah bahut badi nadi kaa rup dhaaran kar leti he jo 980 mile chandrakaar rup main bahati hui bumadhya rekha ko do baar aar-paar karti he. isaki sahaayak nadiyon main kasaai tathaa umbaagi vishesh ullekhneey hai. is nadi main 4,000 laghu dweep hai. isamen choti-choti vashpachaalit naukaaye bhi chalaai jaati hai. iskaa nichalaa jalpravaah 28 sthalon par vighatit hokar jalashakti utpaadak sthanon kaa srijan karata he. yahan par shikaar khilne yogya bhayankar junglee jaanavar paae jate hai kyonki isanadi kaa adhikansh maarg ghaney tathaa abhedya jangalon se ghira hua he. isamen saikadon jatiyon kii machhliyaan milti hai tathaa tateey pradesh main durlabh keede-makodon kii prapti hoti he.
