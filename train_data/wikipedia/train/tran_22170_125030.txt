 fradrick naaman sansthaapan udaaravaadi raajnneeti kaa ek german sansthaapan he jo jo mukt loktantrik dal se sambandhit he. is sansthaapan kii sthapana 1958 main jamarn sangheey ganraajya ke pratham raashtrapati thiodor hius ne kii thi or yah vaiyaktik swatantrata or udaarvaad ko protsaahit karata he.
 yah sansthaapan protestant dharamshastri, fradrick naaman ke siddhanton kaa paalan karata he. pichhali sadi ke armbh main, naaman ek agrani german mukt vichaarak or raajnneetijna the. unhone kritasankalpapurvak naagarik shiksha kaa samarthan kiya. naaman kaa maananaa tha ki ek kaaryshil loktantr ke liye shikshit or rajnatik drushti se avgat nagarikon kaa hona aavashyak he. unke anusaar, rajnatik bhagidaari or tatpashchaat loktantr ke liye naagarik shiksha purvakankshit he. is prakaar, yah sansthaapan sangathit udaarvaad kaa ek abhikarta he. yah naagarik shiksha, anterrashtriya raajnneetik samvaad, or raajnneetik paraamarsh ke madhyam se ise badhaava detaa he. is sansthaapan ke europe, asia, america, or africa main bahut se karyaalay he. iske germany ke mukt loktantik dal or libral interanshnal se bhi nikat ke sambandh hai.
