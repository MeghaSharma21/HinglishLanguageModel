 dhvaj yaa jhandaa athwa pataakaa saamanytah kapade kaa bana ek sanket he jo praaia: ek dand ke sheersh par lagaaya jaataa he. yah praaia: kisi desh, sena, sanstha athwa samooh kaa pratik hota he. sabhi rashtron ke apane adwitiya jhande hai jo us desh ke sanket ke rup main prayog kiye jate hai. bharat kaa rashtriya dhvaj tirangaa he jo kesaria, safed or hare rang kii teen pattiyon or bich kii safed pattee par ashok chakra ke chihnankan ke saath vyavahrut hota he.
