 liem swee king ek bhootpurv indonesian badminton khilaadi hai jo 1970 se madhya-1980 ke dashak tak sakriy rahe. unhone ol england open badminton pratiyogita 1978, 1979 or 1981 main teen baar jiti. morten froast, haan jiaan or luaan jiaan or prakaash padukon, ke samatuly vah apane samay ke shirshatam purush badminton khilaadiyon main ek gine jate the. purush ekal va yugal donon main hi wo apane samay ke sheersh purush badminton khilaadi maane jate the. unhen unke ati teevr gati vaale kudakar maare gayi smash shot ke liye bhi janaa jaataa tha.
 liem kaa janm 28 farvari 1956 ko kudus, kendriya java main hua. unke mata-pita thian pus or oi si moi china ke phujian praant ke nivasee the. unke 7 bahane thim. unhone apni praathmik shiksha sekholah tiongoha main puri kii or 1965 main esady negery demaan II chale gayi. 1974 main unhone apana naam badalakar guntur kar liya.
 liem ne navambar 1972 main 15 varsh kii umr main hi kendriya java badminton pratiyogita jeet lee thi jab wo apane pehle jakarta badminton open main bhaag le rahe the. yah unka pehla antarrashtriya khitaab tha. teen saal baad unhone moenadi cap, kendriya java governor cap jeeta. 1973 main unhone tegal, kendriya java main yojit hui garuna cap badminton vishwa pratiyogita jiti. 
 jab wo varisht hai skool main the tab shruwati 1974 main unhone rashtriya pratiyogita jiti. usee varsh unhone pehali baar ol england open badminton pratiyogita main hissaa liya. 1976 or 1977 main wo upavijeta rahe jabaki 1978, 1979 or 1981 main unhone yah pratishthit khitaab apane naam kiya. 1980 or 1983 kii vishwa pratiyogitaaon main wo apane hi hamavatanon ke khilaaf upavijeta rahe. 1976, 1979 or 1984 main hue thomas cap me apni vijeta indonesian team kaa wo ahm hissaa rahe. 
 unhone badminton se 1988 main sanyas le liya or ab jakarta main ek swasth centre chalaate hai.
 liem ko 2002 main badminton haul of fame kii suchi main shaamil kiya gaya. indonesia main unke jivan par ek film "king" ban chuki he.
