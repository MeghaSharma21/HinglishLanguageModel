 abhyantar sambandh ek antarvaiyaktik sambandh he jisamen sharirik yaa bhaavnaatmak antarangta shaamil hoti he. sharirik antarangta kii visheshtaon main mitrataa, afalaatooni sneh, romani sneh yaa maanav sambhog ho sakate hai. haalaanki "abhyantar sambandh" ke prayog main aam taur se "yon sambandh" shaamil hota he, iskaa mangalabhashit prayog kisi aise sambandh ke liye hi hota he to sambhog par hi aadhaarit ho.
 abhyantar sambandh samast rup se maanav anubhav par ek kendriya bhoomika nibhaate hai. maanav swabhaav main aam taur se kisi se judana or pyaar karnaa he, jo saadhaaran rup se abhyantar sambandh ke daayre main santusht hota he. is sambandh main kisi ko chaahane yaa pyaar karne, romani sneh, sharirik yaa yon aakarshan, yon sambandh, yaa sadasyon ke bich bhaavnaatmak or nijee sahaayta he. abhyantar sambandh ek jalkram banane main sahaayak ho saktaa he jisase majboot bhaavnaatmak lagaav sambhav he.
