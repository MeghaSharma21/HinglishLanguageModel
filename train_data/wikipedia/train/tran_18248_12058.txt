 meetha jal athwa taaja jal prakritik rup se prithvi par paaya jane vaala vah pani he jo samudri or samudratati lagunon ke namak mishrit jal se alag he. ise aise jal ke rup main paribhaashit kiya jaataa he jisamen 0.5 bhaag prati hajaar se kam lavan ghule hue hon.
 yah prithvi par kai roopon main paaya jaataa he jaise him tapiyon ke rup main dhruviya kshetron main yaa oonche parvaton par, nadiyon main pravaahit dharatleey jal, jameen ke neeche sthit bhugarbhik jal, mitti main uplabdh nami ke rup main mrudaa jal, jhiilon talabon or pokharon main sthit jal ityadi ke rup main.
 vastutah meetha jal yaa taaja jal shabd samudri khaare jal se alag sara jal he jo prithvi par paaya jaataa he.yah peya jal kaa samanaarthi nahin he or peya jal iskaa ek hissaa maatr he. vastav main prithvi par uplabdh mithe jal kaa kaafii hissaa pine yogya nahin he kyonki usamen rasinic athwa jaivik dooshan paaya jaataa he.
