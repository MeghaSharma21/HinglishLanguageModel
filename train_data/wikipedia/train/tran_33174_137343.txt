 spani armda spain kii 130 jahaajon vaali ek nausena thi jise agast 1588 main england ko jeetne ke liye ravaanaa kiya gaya tha. medina sidonia ke duk iske commander the. iskaa uddeshy england kii rani elizabeth pratham ko sattachyut karnaa tha. kintu yah armda parajeet hua or is jeet se england kii nausena kii dhak jam gayi.
