 electronic yuddh ) se taatpary aisi kaarywahi se he jisamen vidyutchumbakeey spectrum kaa upyog kiya gaya ho. electronic yuddh ke antargat disht urja ke dwaara spectrum kaa niyantran, shatru par akraman karnaa, yaa spectrum ke madhyam se kisi akraman kaa pratirodhit karnaa bhi shaamil he. electronic yuddh kaa uddeshy virdohi ko vidyutchumbakeey spectrum ke aasaani se upyog karne evam uske laabhon se vanchit rakhana he. yah yuddh hawa se, samudr se, thal se, yaa antariksh se ladaa ja saktaa he. iskaa lakshya maanav, sanchaar vyavastha, radar aadi hote hai.
