 potacium nitrate ek rasinic yogic he. iskaa anasutra KNO3 he. yah ek ynic lavan he. potacium nitrate 'shora' naamak khanij ke rup miltaa he or nitrogen kaa prakritik thos strot he. nitrogen se yukt bahut se yogicon ko samuhik rup se 'shora' kehete hai; potacium nitrate unamem se ek he. 
 potacium nitrate kaa upyog mukhyatah urvarak, rocket ke nodak, tathaa pataakhon main hota he. potacium nitrate, barood ke teen ghatakon main se ek he. madhyayug se hi ise khaadya sangrakshan ke liye upyog kiya jaataa rahaa he.
