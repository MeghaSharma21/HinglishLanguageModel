 jaitrasingh chouhan rajasthan ke ek rajput raja the. unhe ranthambhore samrajya kaa ek mahaan shaasha mana jaataa he. ranthambhore samrajya main maharaja jaitrasingh kaa 1249 isvi ke lagbhag rajarohan hua tha. inhone ranthambhore samrajya par 32 saal tak shashan kiya or ranthambhore samrajya kaa vistaar kiya. inke putr kaa naam hammirdev chouhan tha jinhone apane pita jaitrasingh kii yaad main ranthambor durg main 32 khambho kii chhatari kaa nirmaan karavaaya tha.
