 khagolashastra main krantivrutt yaa suryapath yaa acliptic akaash ke khagoleey gole par vah maarg he jise zamin par baithe kisi darshak ke drushtikon se suraj varsh bhar main letaa he.
 aam bhashaa main agar yah kalpana kii jaae ke prithvi ek kaalpanik gole se ghiri hui he or suraj usapar sthit ek roshni he, to agar salbhar ke liye koi har rose dopaher ke barah baje suraj khagoleey gole par jahaan sthit he vahaan ek kaalpanik bindu bana de or phir in 365 binduon ko jod de or us rekha ko donon taraf badhaakar kshitij kii ore le jaae to use krantivrutt mil jayega. krantivrutt khagoleey gole par bana hua ek kaalpanik mahavritt hota he. kyonki apane kaksha main suraj kii parikrama karti hui prithvi kaa aksh 23.4Â° ke kone par he isliye yahi kone krantivrutt or khagoleey madhya rekha main bhi he.
 "krantivrutt" ko angrezi main "acliptic" or arabi-farsi main "dayera al-baruj" kehete hai. "khagoleey madhya rekha" ko angrezi main "salastial equater" kehete hai. "khagoleey gole" ko angrezi main "salastial sfair", farsi main "kara-e-aasmaan" or bengali main "kha-golok" kehete hai.
 vishuv ke dinon main, jo 21 march or 23 sitambar ko aate hai, baarah baje suraj thik prithvi kii bumadhya rekha ke thik upar hota he. kyonki khagoleey madhya rekha kii paribhaasha yahi he ke vah prithvi ke bhu madhya rekha ke upar hoti he, isliye in krantivrutt or khagoleey madhya rekha in do sthanon main ek dusre ko katati hai. sankranti ke dinon main suraj prithvi kii bumadhya rekha se sab se adhik duuri par hota he, jo yahi krantivrutt kii bhi khagoleey madhya rekha se charam duriyon ke do din hai.
