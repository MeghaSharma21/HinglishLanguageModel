 pranapir badshaah kii kabr ek 14 veen sadi kaa makbara he, jo ki safed shiv mandir kii saamagree nirmit he. yah bharat ke haryana rajya main, hisar shahar ke mahavir stadium ke sameep sthit he.
 yah makbara government college hisar ke parisar ke bheetar panchayat bhawan kii bagal main sthit he. paas me mahavir stadium he.
 yah kabr baba pranpir badshaah kii he. ve sher behalol yaa dana sher ke aadhyatmik guru the. sher behalol ne pehle se hi bataaya tha ki gayasuddin tululaq kaa ek din dilli sultanate kaa sultan banegaa. 
 kabr ko ek unche manch par banaaya gaya he. ise ek nishchit oonchai tak safed kankar pathar block kaa upyog kar nirmit kiya gaya he. jo ki sambhavta muslim akramanakariyon nasht kiye gayi kisi ek hindu mandir se liye gayi hoge. kabr kaa sheersh lakhuaari eenton kaa upyog kar banaaya gaya he, sabhi chaar disho me dhanushakar dwaar he.  sheersh kaa gumbad ek ashtakoniya aadhaar par sthit he. vahaan iske andar kisi kii majaar hone ke koi saboot nahin he. 
 kabr main he kii jeern-sheern haalat main he, kuch maamuli marammat kaa kaam  bhartiya puraatatv surveykshan ke dwaara liya gaya he. 
 Hisar in Haryana
