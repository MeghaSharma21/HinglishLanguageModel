 abisidi 2 ek nrity par aadhaarit bhartiya bollywood film he. iskaa nirdesan remo disuja or nirmaan siddharth roy kapur ne kiya he. isamen mukhya kirdaar main varun dhawan, shraddha kapur or prabhu deva hai. iske alaava sahaayak kirdaar main lauren gotlib, raghav juyal or dharmesh yelande hai. yah film 2013 main bani film abisidi kaa doosraa bhaag he. yah film 19 june 2015 ko cinema gharon main pradarshit hogi.
 yah kahani vishnu, vini or suresh kii he jo vasai main rahete hai or vishwa ke hip hop dance championships naamak nrity kii pratiyogita main bhaag lete hai. suresh or vini apane nrity ke shikshak vishnu ko sammaan dilaane ke liye is pratiyogita ko jeetna chaahate hai. iske liye ve sabhi las vegas naamak shahar ke liye nikal jate hai or is pratiyogita main bhaag lene or jeetne tak kaa safar is film main dikhaaya gaya he.
 is film ko 19 june 2015 ko walt dijani studios moshan pikchar dwaara pradarshit kiya jayega.
 
 is film ke gane sachin-jigar dwaara banaae gaye hai. iske bol mayur puri dwaara likhe gaye hai. iske gane 22 may 2015 ko pradarshit hue.
