 sudhan paak-adhikrut kashmir ke poonch, sudhanoti, baag or kotli zilon main rahane vaale ek samudaay kaa naam he. poonch zile main rawalakot or sudhanoti zila vishesh rup se inaki mathrubhumi he. haalanki aadhunik yug main yah dharm se muslim hai, aitihaasik rup se yah hindu muhayaal samudaay se sambandhit hai. yah punjabi kii upbhashaayen bolte hai. 1960-80 kaal main is samudaay ke bahut se log britain main ja based the.
