 suprasiddh vyapaarik santhaan bharsar ke sanchaalak mandal ke tatkaaleen sadsy henry ashtum kaaleen british sarkaar ke aarthik salaahkaar, maharani elizabeth ke pratham mudraniryanta tathaa british royal exange ke adi sansthaapak sar thomas gresham is vishisht aarthik siddhant ke udbhaavak maane jate hai. yadhyapi yah siddhant unase bahut pracheen he, phir bhi tatkaaleen maudrik sthiti ke aadhikarik gambhir adhyayan evam sookshm vishleshan ke dwaara inhone apane is mat kii sarvapratham sthapana kii isaliye unke naam par yah siddhant prachlit hua. sar thomas gresham ke shabdon ke is siddhant kaa hindi roopaantar is prakaar he : 
 is siddhant kaa vartmaan sanshodhit swaroop nimnlikhit he : 
 saamanytah ek dhatuman main kam ghise sikke, dwidhatu evam bahu dhatuman main dhatvik drushti se apekshaakrit moolyavaan, kagaji maan se parivarty mudra or dhatvik evam kagaji sahamaan main bazaar kii drushti se aantarik yaa dhatvik drushti se moolyavaan tathaa samamoolya kii hote hue bhi naveen tathaa kalatmak mudra acchhee samajhi jaati he.
 acchhee mudra sangrah ke liye upyukt hone, dhaatu ke rup main vikray dwaara vishesh labharjan ke nimitt deshavidesh main chorbazari ke liye adhik upyukt hone tathaa buree mudra kii buraaiyon ke kaaran apane paas na rakhane kii manovaigyanik pravrikti ke kaaran apane mul kaary krayvikray ke saadhan main prayukt hone kii apeksha uparyukt kaaryon ke liye prachlan se baahar kar di jaati he.
 is siddhant ke prayog kii seemaa kaa nirdhaaran mudra kii maang, mudra ke prati vishwaas, maudrik vidhaan tathaa saakh vyavastha dwaara hota he. in drushtiyon se yadi mudra kii purti maang se adhik na ho, buree mudra itani buree na ho gai ho ki us par se janata kaa vishwaas hi uth gaya ho tathaa uskaa prachlan vidhansammat hote hue bhi agrahya ho gaya ho or jab prachlan main koi bhi mudra pramanik nahin rahati yaa ek aseemit or anya mudaaen simit vidhigrahya hoti hai tathaa saakh vyavastha yadi aisi rahati he ki kisi mudra ke prachlan se baahar jane par moolyastar prabhavit nahi hota tathaa mudrabazar kaa suvyavasthit niyantran rahata he to yah siddhant lagu nahin ho paata.
