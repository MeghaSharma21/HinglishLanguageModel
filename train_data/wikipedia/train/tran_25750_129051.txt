 golmud, jise chini log kabhi-kabhi 'girmu' uccharit karte hai, pashchimi china ke chingei praant main ek shahar or zile kaa naam he. yah chingei praant kii doosraa sab se bada nagar or tibet ke pathaar par sthit teesraa sab se bada nagar he. san 1999 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi lagbhag 2 lakh thi or is zile kaa kshetrafal lagbhag 124,500 varg kimi he. is shahar kaa naam mongol bhashaa se liya gaya he jisaki pashchimi upbhasha main iskaa arth 'nadiyaan' hota he. golmud kunlun parvaton ke kaafii paas he or yah pahaad shahar ke dakshin main padate hai. uttar main chaai erhaan naam kii ek khaari jhil sthit he jo paryatakon ke liye dilachasp maani jaati he.
 golmud kaa mausam shushk or thanda he. sardiyaan lambi hoti hai or janvari main ausat taapmaana −9.1 °centegrade hota he. itani sardi ke baavjood sookhaa vaataavaran hone se barf kam hi girti he. garmiyon main july ke maheene main ausat taapmaana 17.9 °centegrade hota he. garmiyon main 28 dinon ke kaal main har varsh milakar 42 millimetre tak kii chhutput barish hoti he. puure china main yah kshetra sab se zyaada dhoop paane vaala mana jaataa he or har varsh yahan lagbhag 3,100 ghanton kii dhoop dekhi jaati he.
 san 1999 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi lagbhag 2 lakh thi, jinamein se lagbhag 90% chini nasal ke log the.
