 prishthbhumi - 1499 main aruba ko spain dwaara khoja gaya tha lekin 1636 main netherland dwaara adhigrahit karaliya gaya. is dweep kii arthvyavastha main teen mukhya udyogon kaa bolbalaa rahaa he. 19 veen sadi main sone kii khudaai or 1924 main tel shodhak kadhakaane khulne se desh main samruddhi ai| 20 veen sadi ke antim dashakon main yahan paryaton udyog main bhi bahut oochaal aayaa he. aruba 1986 main netherland anteliz se vilag hokar, netherland rajshahi ke aadhin ek swytt sadsy bana. 1990 main purna swatantraaa kii or uthe andolan ko aruba ke anurodh par roak diya gaya.
