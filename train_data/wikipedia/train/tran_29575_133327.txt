 honkong anterrashtriya vimaanashetra  honkong kaa pramukh vimaanashetra he. sthaneey log ise chep laap coke airport bhi kehete hai kyonki ye chep laap coke dweep par bana hua he. isse purv prayog hota vimaanashetra kaai taak airport tha, jo ab band ho chukaa he. 
 yah vimaanashetra prachaalan hetu 1998 main kaai taak airport ke sthaan par chaalu hua tha. iske saath hi yahaan menland china evam asia ke shesh bhaagon ke 40 gantavyon hetu mahatvapurn trans-shipment kendra, yatri-kendra bhi he. isi vimaanashetra kii imaarat vishwa ke sabase bade yatri terminal bhawanon main se ek he jo 24 ghante prachaalan ke liye khuli rahati he. 1998 main naye bhawan ke prachaalan aarambh ke samay yah vishwa main sabase bada tha. yah vimaanashetra airport athurity honkong dwaara sanchalit he or ye cathe paisefic, dragon air, honkong airlines, honkong express ke liye praathmik kendra he.
