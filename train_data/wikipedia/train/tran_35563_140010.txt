 nandamuri tarak ramarao yaa en.tii. rama raav filmi abhinayta, nirdeshak, nirmaataa evam rajaneta the. unhone telegu desham party kii sthapana kii or andhra pradesh ke mukhyamantri rahe. unhen antiaar ke upnaam se praaia: janaa jaataa he.
 rajya ko congress ke aadhipaty dur karne ke liye en.tii. rama raav ne varsh 1982 main telegu desham party kii sthapana kii. jab en.tii. rama raav ne rajnatik pari kii shuruuaat kii us samay vah ek lokapriy abhinayta the. varsh 1983 main sarvasammati se en.tii. rama raav ko telegu desham vidhayak dal kaa netaa chaynit kiya gaya, jisamen das cabinet mantri or paanch rajya mantri the. en.tii. rama raav andhra-pradesh ke dasven mukhyamantri the. 1983 se 1994 ke bich vah teen baar is pad ke liye chune ja chuke the.
