 jignesh mewaani gujarat, ek bhartiya raajnneetijna he. vah vadgam nirvachan kshetra main gujarat vidhaan sabha ke ek sadsy he. unhone ek saamaajik kaaryakarta or vakil ke rup main kaam kiya he. unhone 2016 main gujarat main bhartiya jati vargeekaran main 'neech jatiyon' ke rup main maane jate daliton ke hiton ke liye netritv kiya tha.
 mewaani 11 disambar, 1982 main ahmedabad, gujarat.main paidaa hua tha.unka parivaar miu jo faijabad jila main ek gaanv he, kaa mul nivasee he. unhone apni schuli padhaiahmadabad main swaastik vidhyalay or vishwa vidhyalay madhimk shaala main kii. unhone ech ke arts college, ahmedabad se angreji sahitya main kala snaatak 2003 main kii. 2004 main,unhone patrakarita or jan sanchaar main diploma kii. 2004 se 2007 tadbhiyaan, gujarati patrika main ek reporter ke rup main seva kii. 2013 main, unhone di. tii. lau college, ahmedabad se kanoon main snaatak praapt kii..
 gujarat ke saurashtra ilaake ke una gaanv main dalit logon par hamalaa hone ke baad, gaay sangrakshan samooh ke sadasyon ne daawa kiya ki gujarat bhar main virodh pradarshan hua. jignesh mevani ne ahmedabad se una tak dalit asmita yatra kii thi, jo 15 agast 2016 ko khatm hua tha or dalit mahilaon sahit kuch 20,000 daliton ne is main bhaag liya tha, jinhone goron ke shav hataane ke apane paaramparik naukariyon ko chhodane kii pratigya lee thi. unhone daliton ke utthan ke liye bhoomi kii maang kii.
