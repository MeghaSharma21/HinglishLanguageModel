 tara devdhar bharat se ek mahila badminton khilaadi he. vah bharat ke cricket lig di. bi. devdhar kii beti hai.
 tara devdhar ne 1942 main mahilaon kii dubles main apni bahan sundar devdhar ke saath apana pehla rashtriya khitaab jeeta tha. 1942 or 1954 ke bich devdhar bahanon, suman, sundar or tara ne bhartiya rashtriya badminton champion pratiyogita par dabadabaa banaaya. 
 vah ek majboot tennis khilaadi bhi thim. visconsin-medisson vishvavidhyalay main adhyayan karte hue, yues tennis championships main unhen videshi tennis khilaadiyon ke bich paanchvaan sthaan mila tha.
