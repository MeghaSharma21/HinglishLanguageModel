 pratyaagmani pump ek prakaar kii dhanatmak-visthaapan pump he jisamen piston pump, plumsr pump, or diafram pump aadi shaamil hai. iskaa upyog vahaan hota he jahaan apekshaakrit kam maatra main drav ko pump karnaa ho tathaa kaaryakari daab bahut adhik ho. pratyaagmani pampon ke jis chambar main drav ko rokkar rakhaa jaataa he, vah achal silinder ke rup main hota he jisamen piston yaa plumsr sthit hota he.
