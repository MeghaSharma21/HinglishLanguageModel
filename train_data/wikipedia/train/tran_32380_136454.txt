 katar arab prayadweep ke uttar purvi tat par sthit ek chota prayadweep he. iske dakshin main jahaan saudi arab he, vahiin shesh teenon ore faras kii khadi he. ek tel samrudh raashtra ke rup main katar duniya kaa doosraa samrudh desh he. san 1783 main kuwait ke al khalif vansh ne yahaan shashan karnaa prarambh kiya. tatpashchaat yah turki ke adheen rahaa. pratham vishwayuddh ke baad yah britain ke sangrakshan main rahaa. 1971 main swatantraaa milane ke baad 1972 main khalipha bin hamad kaa shashan prarambh hua.
 mana jaataa he ki katar naam aaj ke jubaara naamak shahar ke pracheen naam "kataraa" se utpann hua he, jo pracheen samay main kshetra kaa mahatvapurn bandargah or shahar tha. "kataraa" shabd potolmi dwaara banaae gaye arab prayadweep ke maanchitr par pehali baar najar aayaa tha.
