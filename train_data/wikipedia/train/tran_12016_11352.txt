 bhul chook leni deni, ek vaakyansh he, jiskaa upyog kisi anubandhatmak dastauez jaise ki ek mulya-suchi, dar-suchi or visheshtaon se sambandhit koi galat yaa adhuri jaankaree, pradaan karne kii sthiti main, vidhik dayitvon ko kam karne ke ek prayaas ke rup main kiya jaataa he. un sthitiyon main jahaan koi jaankaree apekshaakrit teji se parivartit hoti he main, ise aksar ek disclemur ke rup main lagu kiya jaataa he. kanoonee drushti se, yah ek aisa bayaan he jiske anusaar, koi vishesh soochna puri tarah se vishwasaneey nahin he or samay ke saath badal sakati he. 
 bhul chook leni deni kaa sabase adhik prayog united kingdom, australia or ireland ganraajya main hota he, saath hi ise bharat, dakshin africa or canada main bhi prayog kiya jaataa he. vishwa ke anya deshon main iskaa prayog itnaa aam nahin he.
