 aparaadh anveshan ek anuprayukt vigyaan he jisamen tathyon kaa adhyayan evam vishleshan kiya jaataa he taaki apradhi kii pahchaan kii ja sake. isamen talaashi lenaa, saakshaatkaar lenaa, poochtacha karnaa, sakshya ekatra karnaa, aadi sammilit he. aadhunik aparaadh anveshan main nyaayalayik vigyaan naamak vaigyanik takanic kaa praayah upyog kiya jaataa he.
 aparaadh se peedit log athwa uske pratyakshdarshi, police ke paas kisi bhi prakaar ke aparaadh ke sambandh me ja sakate he. kuch prakaar ke aparaadh, jinhe sangyeya aparaadh kehete he, main police kaa turant pariwaadee kii shikaayat darj karnaa aavashyak he. ise pratham soochna report kehete he. yadi peedit ne FIR main kisi kaa naam diya he yaa police ko yah prathit ho ki kisi ne koi aparaadh karavaaya he, to vah unase sawaal kar sakate hai.
