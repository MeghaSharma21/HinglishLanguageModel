 madhavadas jagannathi pehle shri madhavendrapuri ke shishy the par shri gaurang kaa aavirbhaav hone par yah unke anugat ho gaye. shrimadhavendrapuri san0 1548 main aprakat hue at: inkaa janm san0 1530 ke lagbhag hua hoga. 
 yah pad banaakar gayan karte hue yatra karte rahete the or jagannatha puri adhik jate the, jisase yah 'jagannathi' bhi kahe jane lage. ye kanyakubj brahman the. ye virakt bhakt sanyasi tathaa sanskrit ke vidwaan the or brajabhasha padon ke sivaa inhone sanskrit main kai granth prastut kiye hai par ve sab praaia: aprapya he. brajabhasha kii kuch choti choti rachanaayen, jaise dhyaan lila, madalsa aakhyaan, paratit parichha aadi praapt hai. yah 16veen shatee ke ant tak vidyaman the.
