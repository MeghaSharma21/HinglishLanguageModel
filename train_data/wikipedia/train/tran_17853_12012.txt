 
 mohammad ebn jakaaria raji, faras ke prasiddh hakim the . inkaa janm tehran ke paas raaj naamak nagar main hua tha. inke janm tathaa mrityu kaa yatharth samay anishchit he. ye faras kii khadi par sthit basra nagar main bas gaye the.
 arabi main chikitsashastra par pustak likhanewaale hakimon main inhen agraganya samajha jaataa he. inhone lagbhag 200 pustaken likhin, jinamein chikitsashastra kii anya pustakon ke sivaay is vishay kaa ek saarvbhaum kosh tathaa ganit, jyotish, dharm or darshanashastra par bhi pustaken thim. apni ek pustak main inhone sarvapratham chechak ko masoorika se bhinn rog bataaya.
 inaki arabi kii pustakon kaa anuwaad latin bhashaa main kiya gaya or is prakaar inke dwaara sanchit gyaan kaa europe main prasar hua.
