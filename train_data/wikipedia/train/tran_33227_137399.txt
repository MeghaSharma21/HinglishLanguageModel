 khagol vigyaan main, yuti-viyuti, ek gurutvkarshan pranali main teen khagoleey pindon kaa ek saral rekhiy vinyas he. saral shabdon main, jab surya, prithvi or chandramaa ek sidh main hote hai to yah sthiti yuti-viyuti kehalaati he. yuti-viyuti sthiti main chandramaa yaa to surya ke saath sangyojan main hota he yaa surya se vimukh hota he. surya grahan or chandra grahan yuti-viyuti ke samay par hota he.
