 acharya pushpadanth sagar jain dharm ke ek pramukh digambar sant hai. unhone kshullak diksha acharya vidyasagar se grahan kii. unki digambar diksha acharya vimal sagar ke haathon sampann hui. pushpagiri tirth kaa nirmaan kaary acharya pushpadanth sagar kii prerana se ho rahaa he.
 muni tarun sagar, muni pulak sagar, muni pragna sagar, muni arun sagar, muni saurabh sagar or muni pranaam sagar unke pramukh shishy hai.
