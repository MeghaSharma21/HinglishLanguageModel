 tour di france vishwa kii sabase lokapriy or pratishthit cycle pratiyogita he. prativarsh july maah main iskaa aayojan hota he. 1903 main isaki shuruyaat hui thi or pratham or dwitiya vishwa yudho ke dauraan iskaa aayojan nahin hua tha. yah pratiyogita france or uske padosi rashtro se ho kar guzarati he. lambii duuri kii is pratiyogita ko pure hone main lagbhag 3 saptaah kaa samay lagata he. sabase kam kul samay lene vaala khiladi pratiyogita kaa vijeta hota he.
