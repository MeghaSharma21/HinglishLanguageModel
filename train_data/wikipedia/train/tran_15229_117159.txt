 foundation twachaa ke rang vaala ek soundarya prashaadhan he jo chehre par lagaaya jaataa he. foundation lagane se twachaa kaa rang ek hi jaisaa dikhaai detaa he. isse mechap ke liye ek aadhaar taiyaar ho jaataa he. isse chehre ke daag, dhabbe evam nishaan aadi chhip jate hai. iske alaava twachaa ke mul varna ko badalane ke liye bhi yah lagaaya jaataa he.
 twachaa ke rang se ek shed gaharaa foundation le len. ye aksar stick, taral tathaa kriim ke rup main miltaa he. ise gardan, kaan, chehre or haathon ke khule hue hisson par acchhee tarah failaakar laga len. maathe, naak, gaalon, thodi or gardan par yah nishaanon kii tarah laga len or phir geele spanj se acchhee tarah failaa len. agar twachaa zyada rukhi he to foundension main thodaa sa moshcharizer mila len. agar twachaa taileey ho to iske andar 1-2 boond pani yaa gulabjal mila sakate hai.
