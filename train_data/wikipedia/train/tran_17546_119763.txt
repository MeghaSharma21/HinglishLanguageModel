 negros oxidental yaa pashchimi negros dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh kaa ek praant he. yah negros dweep ke pashchimottaree ardh-bhaag main sthit he. praant ke pashchimottar main panaay khadi or gimaras jalsandhi ke paar gimaras kaa dweep-praant tathaa panaay dweep par iloilo praant sthit hai. dakshinpurv main negros oriental praant sthit he. philipinoz kaa aadhe se adhik chini utpaadan negros oxidental praant main hi hota he.
