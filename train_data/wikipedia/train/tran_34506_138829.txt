 balkh afghanistan kaa ek praant he jo us desh ke uttari bhaag main sthit he. balkh kii rajdhani prasiddh mazaar-e-shareef shahar he. iskaa kshetrafal 17,249 varg kimi he or isaki aabaadi san 2006 main lagbhag 11.2 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. is praant kaa naam isi naam ke ek shahar par padaa he jo rajdhani se 25 kilometre pashchim kii ore he or atharahavin sadi tak shashan kaa kendra tha. is kshetra ko hind-irani purv ke aaryon ke aagaman kaa pramukh kendra mana jaataa he jahaan wo qareeb 2000 isapurv main aae honge. isa ke do sadi purv main yah ilaaka aitihaasik bactria samskruti ke kshetra kaa kendra tha or isi ke naam ko yawanon ne badalakar bectra rakhaa tha.
 'balkh' kaa naam sanskrit main 'vahlika' kaha jaataa tha or iskaa zikr valmiki kii ramayana main bhi miltaa he. 'balkh' shabd main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
