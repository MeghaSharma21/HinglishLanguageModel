 gini, aadhikarik taur par gini ganraajya, pashchim africa main sthit ek desh he, jise purv main french gini ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. arddh chandrakaar aakaar kaa yah desh purv se dakshin kii ore failaa hua he, jahaan isaki pashchimi seemaa andh mahaasaagar se milti he. andh mahaasaagar ke alaava gini-bisaau bhi desh ke pashchim main sthit he. andruni hisson main uttari seemaa par senegal or uttar or uttar-purv main mali sthit he. dakshin purv kii ore kot da' ivor he, dakshin main liberia or dakshin pashchim main siyera leone sthit hai. desh ke madhya se bahane vaali nizer nadi pani ke saath-saath jal parivahan kii suvidha muhaiyyaa karaati he.
 konakri desh kaa sabase bada shahar or rajdhani hone ke saath-saath rashtriya sarkaar kaa mukhyalay bhi he. desh ko kabhi-kabhi padosi desh gini-bissau se alag bataane ke liye gini-konakri bhi kaha jaataa he. gini main choubees jaateey samoohon kaa vaas he, jinamein se teen sabase bade or sabase prabhavi samooh phula, mandinakaa or susu hai. 
 isaki rajbhasha he francisi bhashaa. isaki mukhyabhashaayen hai mandingo, phula tathaa susu.
