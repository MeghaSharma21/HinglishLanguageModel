 
 ramban bharat ke jammu or kashmir rajya main ek nagar or zila he. yah mahaan pir punjaal parvatamala main sthit he or rajya ke jammu vibhaag kaa hissaa he. banihaal isi zile main aata he or kashmir ghati main pravesh karne se pehle antim padaav he. yahan se rail or jawahar sadak surang banane se pehle bhi paas ke banihaal darre se yatayaat kashmir jaaya karata tha.
 ramban main kashmiri bhashaa kii ek upbhasha, rambani boli jaati he - haalanki isamen gahare dogri bhashaa ke prabhaav bhi dekhe jate hai. sthaneey aabaadi lagbhag 70% muslim or 30% hindu he.
 ramban zile kii ausat oonchaai samudratal se 1,156 meter upar he. is zile kii simaaon main patnitop kaa hill-stesan dakshintam bhaag main aata he. purvi chor par assar, pashchimi par gool or uttari par banihaal sthit he. bhushetra bahut pahari he. isaki seemayen reasi, udhampur, doda, anantnag or kulgaam zilon se lagati hai.
