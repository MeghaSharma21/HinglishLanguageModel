 1976 europea cap final 12 may 1976 par hamepaden park, glassgo, main yojit ek footbal match tha, jis main france ke bern munich ko pashchim germany ke bern munich 1-0 haar diy.
 
 match glassgo, pehle se hi saint atienne pratiyogita ke dauraan sthaneey team rangeron ko haraaya dekha tha ki ek shahar main hamepaden park main jagah le lee. les verts, bern munich ke khilaaf lagaataar teesri baar europea cap jeetne kii ummid kar rahaa tha ki ek team khel rahe the. 
 gend apane hi half main gend jeeta he or ek 50 meter ekal ran par chalaa gaya jo barnd durnberger se thik pass kaam karne ke baad net ke peeche khojane ke gard mular ke saath shuru hua. lekin unke prayaas hungary referi karolya palotai dwaara ofside ke liye referi dwaara asweekrut kiya gaya tha, televijan dhimi gati ke laabh ke saath samiksha kii acchhee tarah se jab bhi kathor samajha saktaa he ki ek nirnay.57 wein minute main, franj bekanbawar osvaldo piyaja dwaara gherne kii koshish kii or referi sirf ardhvrutt kii bain bees meter se german team ke liye ek free kik de diya tha jo gard mular, ko paarit kar diya.franj bekanbawar lakshya ke bain ore main aadhe se adhik ran banaae hai, jo apane adhikaar par roth ke liye gend ko ittala de di.
 match ke ant main, ve badakismat rahaa tha ki laga, lekin unke samarthakon badhai un the kyonki saint atienne ke khilaadiyon, ro rahe the or france main unki vaapasi hui thi vir, ve haar gaye the, bhale hi.
