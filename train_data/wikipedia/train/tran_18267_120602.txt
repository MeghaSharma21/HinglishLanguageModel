 jagamohini sampradaay, purvi bengal kaa ek sampradaay. iskaa naam jagmohan goswami ke naam par padaa jo iske pravartak maane jate hai. is sampradaay ke log nirgun upaask hai. guru kii puja inaki upasna kaa mukhya ang he. iske do bhed- gruhi or udasin hai. kintu iskaa koi dharmagranth uplabdh nahin he.
