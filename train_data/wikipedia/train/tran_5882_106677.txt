 karakum registan madhya asia main sthit ek registan he. turkmenistan desh kaa 70% ilaaka isi registan ke kshetra main aata he. 'karakum' shabd kaa arth 'kaali ret' hota he. yahan aabaadi bahut kam ghani he or ausatan har 6.5 varg kimi main ek vyakti miltaa he. yahan barish ausat rup se 10 varshon main ek dafaa girti he. yah vishwa ke sab se bade retile registaanon main se ek he.
 kul milakar karakum kaa kshetrafal 3,50,000 varg kimi he. turkmenistan ke desh kaa adhiktar kshetra isi registan main aata he. karakum caspian sagar se purv main or aral sagar ke dakshin main sthit he. uttar-purv main aamu dariyaa or kizil kum registan hai. aadhunik yug main aral sagar ke sikudne se us jhil kaa bahut sa hissaa is registan kii chapet main aa gaya he or kuch log is bhaag ko 'aral karakum' kehane lage hai. yah lagbhag 40,000 varg kimi par vistrut he. aral ke sookhe hue farsh par krishi main istemaal hone vaale bahut se kitnashak padaarth mili hue the jo sinchai kii naharon se bahakar yahan aa gaye the. ab yah ek maheen dhul main mili hue hai or hawa ke saath udakar hazaron mile dur tak pradooshan le jate hai. aral karakum se ude kitmar padaarth antarctic main penguinon ke koon main paae ja chuke hai. yahan kii dhul greenland kii himanion main, norve ke wanon main or belarus ke kheton main bhi paae ja chuki he.
 karakum ilaake ke andar bolshoi parvat shrunkhlaa aati he jisamen pashaan yug ke manushya baseron ke avshesh mili hai. aadhunik yug main yahan duniya kii duusari sab se lambi sinchai nahar, jise karakum nahar kehete hai, banaai gai thi. yah 1,375 kimi lambi he lekin vaqt ke saath-saath isamen se pani jagah-jagah se chooney laga he, jis se kai choti jhilein ban gayi hai. inkaa pani registan ke neeche ke namak ko upar le aayaa he.
