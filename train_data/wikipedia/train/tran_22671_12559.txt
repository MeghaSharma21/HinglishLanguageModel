 indra sen shri aravind ke ek pramukh shishy the. inhone shri aravind ke siddhanton kaa purna manovigyaan shabd ke rup main sarvapratham prayog kiya. 
 indra sen eme, elelbi, piechdi shri aurbindo or maa, manovaigyanik, lekhak, or shikshak, or ek shaikshanik anushaasan ke rup main intigral manovigyaan ke sansthaapak ke bhakt the. sen kaa janm kalakatta ke ek bengali hindu parivaar main punjab ke jhelum gilemein hua tha, lekin dilli main bada hua jab unka parivaar vahaan chale gaye. ek yuva umr se vah aadhyatmik khoj main ruchi rakhte the. unhone dilli vishvavidhyalay main darshan or manovigyaan donon main ek master kii degree puri kii. apni padhai ko aage badhaane ke liye, unhone germany main fraberg vishvavidhyalay main namankit kiya, or manovigyaan main piechdi praapt kii. unhone martin hidagar ke vyaakhyaan main bhaag liya or koinegsburg vishvavidhyalay main bhartiya darshanashastra or sanskrit ko padhaayaa. is samay, unke mukhya hit hegel ke darshan the, or jang kaa manovigyaan.
