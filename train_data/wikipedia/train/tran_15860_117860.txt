 sindur ek laal yaa naarangi rang kaa soundarya prashaadhan hota he jise bhartiya upmahadweep main mahilaaye prayog karti hai. hindu, buddha, jain or kuch anya samudaayon main vivaahit striyaan ise apni maang main pahenti hai. iskaa prayog bindiyon main bhi hota he. rasaynic drushti se sindur aksar paare yaa sise ke rasayanon kaa bana hota he, isliye vishaiylla hota he or iske prayog main saavdhaanee baratane ko or is padaarth ko bacchon se dur rakhane ko kaha jaataa he.
