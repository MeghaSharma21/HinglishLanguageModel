 sita samahit sthal mandir sant ravidaas nagar jila main sthit he. yah mandir ilahabaad or varanasi ke madhya sthit jangiganj bazaar se 11 kilometre ganga ke kinare sthit he. manyata he ki is sthaan par maan sita se apane aap ko dharti main samahit kar liya tha. yahan par hanumanji kii 110 feet unchi murti he jise vishwa kii sabase badi hanuman ji kii murti hone kaa gaurav praapt he. swami jitendranand ji ke aseem prayaas se or shri prakaash narayan punj kii madad se ye sthaan paryatak sthal ke rup main ubhar kar aayaa he.
