 aadhikarik jalsthal
 bhartiya saanskrithik sambandh parishad bharat sarkaar kaa swatantra sanghathan he jisaki sthapana 1950 main hui thi. is sanstha kaa mukhyalay aajaa bhawan, nai dilli main he. is sanghathan ke kshetreey karyaalay bangalore, kalakatta, chandigarh, chennai, jakarta, moscow, berlin, kairo, landan, taashkand, almati, johansburg, durban, port of spain or colombo main hai. yah sanstha bharat kii samskruti or shiksha ke vikaas ke anek kaaryon main sanlagn he.
 bharat tathaa anya deshon ke bich saanskrithik sambandh or parsparik samaj sthapit, punarujjivit or unhen sudrudh karne ke praathmik uddeshy ke saath bhartiya saanskrithik sambandh parishad kii sthapana kii gai thi. sanstha kii niyamavali ke anusaar iske uddeshy nimna hai
 parishad ke pramukh kriyalap nimnlikhit hai-
 parishad ke kriyalap isaki shaasi parishad dwaara nirdhaarit or anumodith kiye jate hai.
 parishad ko iske adhyaksh or sanvidhik nikaayon arthath shaasi nikaay, saadhaaran sabha or vitt samiti dwaara maargdarshan diya jaataa he. in nikaayon main sabhi kshetron ke visheshgy hote hai, inamen sansad ke pratinidhi, saanskrithik pandit, shikshavid, sarkari pratinidhi, lalit evam pratimaa kala ke kshetra ke pratishthit kalakaar, vaigyanik, takneekee or anusandhan santhaan sahit vishvavidhyalay/sansthaaen tathaa parishad ke uddeshyon main ruchi rakhane vaale sanghathanon ke pratinidhi shaamil hai. ye nikaay parishad ke kriyakalapon ko nitigat disha pradaan karte hai. mahatvapurn pahaluon par vichaar ke liye samay-samay par in nikaayon kii baithak hoti hai. inamen bajet, karrwai yojana ityadi shaamil hai.
