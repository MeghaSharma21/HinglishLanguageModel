 kokila windos vista, windos 7 tathaa windos sarwar 2008 main prayukt ek opentip unicode hindi font he. yah sarvapratham windos vista main aayaa tha. yah mukhyatah dastavejon main hindi taxt ke pradarshan hetu banaaya gaya he.
 kokila sundar banaavat vaala font he jo ki graphics kaaryon hetu upyukt he haalaanki yah bahut chote font aakaar main spasht nahin dikhataa. yah saamaanya evam bade font sies main mudran hetu upyukt he. yah kuch had tak chanakya font jaisaa dikhataa he.
 kokila main devanagari ke kuch sanyuktakshron ke varnkhand nahin hai jaise shri, k aadi jis kaaran yah sanskrit taxt ke pradarshan evam mudran hetu upyukt nahin.
