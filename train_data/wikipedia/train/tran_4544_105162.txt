 azarbaijani, azeri yaa torki azarbaijan or pashchimottaree iran main azarbaijani logon dwaara boli jane vaali ek turki bhashaa he. yah turki bhashaa-parivaar kii oguz shakha kii ek bhashaa he or turki, qashakaai or turkmen bhashaa se kaafii samantaayein rakhti he. vishwa bhar main azeri bolne waalon kii sankhya 2.5 se 3.5 karod anumaanit kii gai he.
