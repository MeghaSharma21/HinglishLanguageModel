 rana rimal mewar ke rajput raja the. ve rana kumbha ke putr the. unhone udai singh ko parrast karke rajgaddi praapt kii kyonki udai singh apane pita kaa hanta tha.
 unke shashan ke aarmabhik dinon mem hi malwa ke shaasha ghiyaas shah ne chittod par akraman kiya kintu use safalataa nahin mili. iske sheeghra baad giyas shah ke senapati jafar khan ne mewar par akraman kiya kintu vah bhi mandalgarh or khayrabad main parajeet hua. rimal ne raav jodha kii beti shringaradevi se vivaah karke rathoron se shatruta samaapt kar di. rimal ne risingh toda or ajmer par punah adhikaar kar liya. unhone mewar ko bhi shaktishaalee banaaya tathaa chittod ke ekanath ji mandir kaa punarnirmaan karaayaa. inke putr kunwar prithviraj,jaimal, tathaa      rana sanga main rana sanga uttaradhikari hue |
