 vrihat avsaadee manoveekshipti yaa vrihat avsaadee rog ) ek maansik rog he jisamen bahut adhik avsaad hota he jisase vyakti ke aatm sammaan main kami aati he or vah saamaanya ruchi kii gatividhiyon main bhi anand nahin aata. yah saamaanya avsaad se alag he or isamen vyakti ek prakaar se aksham banane vaala manorog he jo vyakti ke parivaar, kaary, skool, sone evam khaane kii aadaton tathaa saamaanya swasthya ko buree tarah prabhavit karata he. isse grast bahut se log aatmahatya kar lete hai.
