 nutan, hindi cinema kii prasiddh abhinetri thim. nutan ke soundarya ko miss india puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. vah is puraskaar ko paane vaali pehali mahila thim. nutan ne 50 se bhi adhik filmon main kaam kiya or unhe bahut se anya puraskaron ke alaava 6 baar filmafere puraskaar mila . 
 nutan kaa janm 4 june 1936 ko ek padhe-likhe or sabhrant parivaar main hua tha. inaki mata kaa naam shrimati shobhana samarth or pita kaa naam shri kumarasen samarth tha. nutan ne apane filmi jivan kii shuruyaat 1950 main kii thi jab vah skool main hi padhati thim. 
 11 aktubar 1959 ko nutan ne leftinent commander rajnish bahal se vivaah kar liya. nutan ke putr mohanish bahal bhi hindi filmon main abhinay karte hai. nutan kii bahan tanuja or bhatiji kaajol bhi hindi cinema kii prasiddh abhinetriyon main shaamil hai. nutan ke abhinay ko hindi film uddog ke sabhi bade naamon ne saraahaa he or vah aaj bhi bahut si adaakaaraon kaa adarsh bani hui hai.
 nutan kaa dehanth farvari 1991 ko cancer ke kaaran hua.
