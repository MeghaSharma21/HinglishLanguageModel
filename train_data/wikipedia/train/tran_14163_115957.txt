 hamaas philistini sunni musalamaanon kii ek sashastr sanstha he jo philistin rashtriya praadhikaran kii mukhya party he. hamaas kaa gathan 1987 main misr tathaa falstin ke musalamaanon ne milakar kiya tha jiskaa uddheshy kshetra main israeli prashasan ke sthaan par islamic shashan kii sthapana kerani thi. hamaas kaa prabhaav gaza pattee main adhik he. iske sashastr vibhaag kaa gathan 1992 main hua tha. 1993 main kiye gaye pehle aatmghaatee hamale ke baad se lekar 2005 tak hamaas ne israeli kshetron main kai aatmghaatee hamale kiye. 2005 main hamaas ne hinsa se apane aap ko alag kiya or 2006 main iske baad 2006 se gaza se israeli kshetron main rocket hamalon kaa silsilaa aarambh hua jiske liye hamaas ko jimmevaar mana jaataa he. san 2008 ke ant main israyal dwaara gaza pattee main hamaas ke khilaaf kii gai sainya karrwai main koi 1300 log maare gaye the. is abhiyaan kaa uddeshy israeli kshetron main rocket hamale rokna tha. par hamaas ne israyal par aam nagarikon ko maarane kaa aarope lagaaya.
 hamaas shabd arabi bhashaa ke harakat al-mukavama al-islamia kaa sankshep he par arabi main hi hamaas shabd kaa matlab "utsaah" hota he.
