 jog prapaat karnataka main sharavati nadi par he. yah chaar chote-chote prapaton - raja, raaket, rorer or daam blaachein - se milakar bana he. iskaa jal 250 meter kii oonchaai se girakar bada sundar drishy upasthit karata he. iskaa ek anya naam jerasppa bhi he.
 gerasppa karnataka tathaa maharashtra rajyon kii seemaa par shivamoga jile ke pradhan centre se 95 kimi dur sthit he. shivamoga se prapaat tak motor maarg he, jo manoram jangalon se hokar gaya he. raste main chaar vishramgrih he.
 yahan chaar prapaat hai. ye prapaat shiravati naamak nadi ke oonchaai se girane ke kaaran banate hai. pratham prapaat me, jise raja kehete hen, jal 829 foot kii oonchaai se 132 foot gahare kunda main girtaa he. darshak upar se is atal gaddhe main dekh sakate hai. dwitiya prapaat main fenil jal kaa teevr pravaah ghumaavdaar maarg se hota hua ek guha main pahunchataa he, jahaan se vah raja prapaat ke kataav main gir jaataa he. teesraa prapaat kuch dakshin hatakar he. isamen se jal kii dhara phen ke rup main, jatke se, nirantar nikalati rahati he or aatishbaajee ke agnibaan kii bhanti rang-birange chamkile binduon main bikharakar neeche girti he. iske bhi dakshin chaturth prapaat kii feete samaan pani kii chaadaron kaa kram he, jo shila kii dhalwaan sataha se neeche girti hai. is prapaat kaa sabase sundar drishy karnataka kii ore se dikhaai padataa he. jahaan pani girtaa he vahaan tak pahunchne kaa maarg kathin he, kintu vahaan tak pahunche bina prapaat kii shobhaa kaa puraa anand nahin mil saktaa.
 garmi ke dinon main is prapaat kaa jal ksheen ho jaataa he or varsha main jal kii adhikta ke kaaran gadhde kaa samast kshetra ghaney abhedya kuhare se dhakaa rahata he. is sthaan par maharashtra tathaa karnataka donon rajyon dwaara jalashakti se vidyut utpaadan ke bade bade sanyantr sthapit kiye gaye hai.
