 pankaj kapur bharat ke janemaane naatakkaar tathaa tivi va film abhinayta hai. ve anekon tivi dharawahikon tathaa filmon main abhinay kar chuke hai.
 doordarshan par 80 ke dashak main prasaarit jasusi dharawahik karamchand main nibhaai sheershak bhoomika shaayad unki sabase mashhoor bhoomikaaon main se hai. unki anya saraahneey filmon main ek doctor kii maut tathaa vishaal bharadwaj nirdeshit "maqbool" shaamil hai. askar award vijeta richard atenbaro nirdeshit or 1982 main nirmit film gandhi main pankaj ne pyarelal kii sankshipt bhoomika adaa kii thi par film ke hindi sanskaran main gandhi kaa kirdaar nibhaa rahe ben kingsle kii dabing karne kaa unhen avasar mila.
 pankaj bollywood ke prasiddh yuva abhinayta shahid kapur ke pita hai jo abhinetri va nrutyaangna nilimaa azeem se unke pehle vivaah kii santan hai. pankaj ne doosraa vivaah abhinetri supriya pathak se kiya.
