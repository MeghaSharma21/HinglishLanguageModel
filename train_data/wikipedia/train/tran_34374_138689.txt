 rashtriya phacist party 23 farvari 1919 ko italy main benito musolini dwaara sthapit raajnneetik party. pratham vishwayuddh main parajeet italy main is party kii ugr rashtravaadi kaaryakramon ko sheeghra hi lokapriyata or safalataa milane lagi. chunaav main bahumat na praapt hone par bhi musolini ne italy kii satta par adhikaar kar liya. san 1922 se 1934 tak italy kii satta is party ke hanth thi. vartmaan main yahi ekmaatr dal he jiske pun:sanghathan karne par italy main sanvaidhanik rup se pratibandh he.
