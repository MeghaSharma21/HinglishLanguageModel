 aparnaa datta gupta ek vaigyanik he. vah hyderabad vishvavidhyalay me pashuvigyaan vibhaag main padhaati hai. unhone keetanuon ke sharirik vigyaan ke kshetra main anusandhan kiya he. yah adhyayan kiton ko dhyaan main rakhte hue, unke niyantran par mahatvapurn prabhaav daalata hai. 
 aparnaa ne pi ech di banaras hindu vishvavidhyalay se kiya. vah ai en es e, en es ai, or ai e es si kii ek felo hai.
