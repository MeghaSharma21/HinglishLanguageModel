 akendrik yaa prokeriot aise jeev ko kaha jaataa he jisaki koshikaon main jhilliyon main band kendrak jaise koi asaral dhaanche na hon. inke vipareet sukendrik yaa koshikaon main ek jhilli se ghira hua kendrak hota he jiske andar anuvanshik saamaan hota he. adhiktar akendrik jeev eckoshikeey hote hai haalaanki kuch ke jeevankram main kabhi-kabhi ek bahukoshikeey antaraal bhi aata he.
 unani bhashaa main 'pro' kaa matlab ' pehle' or 'kerri' kaa matlab 'beej' yaa 'gari' hota he. yani prokeriot koshikaaye kendrak ke banane se pehle kii koshikaaye hai.
