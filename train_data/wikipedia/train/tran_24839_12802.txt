 baghirua, uttar pradesh ke mainpuri jile main bhogaanv tehsil main sthit ek bada gaanv he, jisamen 437 parivaar nivas karte hai. yah gaanv mainpuri loksabhaa va kishni vidhaan sabha kshetra ke antargat aata he.
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar baghirua kii jansankhya 2748 he, jisamen 1503 purush va 1245 mahilaayein hai. shunya se 6 varsh tak kii umr ke bacchon kii sankhya 381 he, jo ki gaanv kii kul jansankhya kaa 13.86% he. jansankhya kaa maatr 4.15% anusuchit jati varg he va vartmaan main gaanv main koi bhi anusuchit janajati kaa vyakti nahin rahata he.
 baghirua main prati 1000 purushon ke saapeksh 828 mahilaayein hai, jo ki uttar pradesh rajya ke ausat 912 se kaaphi kam he.
 uttar pradesh kii ausat sakshartaa dar 68.88% se kaaphi adhik baghirua kii ausat sakshartaa dar 80.35% he. purush sakshartaa 89.77% va mahila sakshartaa 68.88% he. 
 bhartiya sanvaidhanik vyavastha va panchayat raaj adhinium ke praavdhaanon ke tahat baghirua sarpanch dwaara prashasit hota he. baghirua uttar pradesh sarkaar kii da ram manohar lohia sakal gram vikaas yojana ke tahat vikaas hetu "lohia gram" sheershak se soochibaddh rahaa he.
