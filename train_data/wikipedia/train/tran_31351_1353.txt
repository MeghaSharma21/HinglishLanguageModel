 nighantu shabd kaa arth namsangrah he. 'ni' upsargak 'ghati' dhaatu se 'mrigavyadayasch' una. 1.38 sutara se ku pratyay karne par nighantu shabd vyutpann hota he. 
 sanskrit kaa pracheen shabdkosh he. isamen vaidic sahitya main praapt shabdon kaa apurv sangrah he. vaidic samhitaon main se chunakar yahan par shabd ekatra kiye gaye hai. yah sambhavat: sansar ke kosh sahitya kii sarvapratham rachana he. vaidic sahitya ke vishisht shabdon kaa sangrah bahut hi suvyavasthit rup se isamen kiya gaya he. 
 pracheen kaal main sambhavat: is nighantu kii tarah ke kuch anya nighantu bhi rahe honge, kintu abhi tak unke astitv kaa kuch pramaan uplabdh nahin ho paaya he. is nirghatu ke rachayita ke vishay main yadhyapi vidwaanon ke anek purv paksh hai tathaapi vishisht evam pracheen vidwaanon ke dwaara yah pramanit ho chukaa he ki iske rachayita yask hai. iskaa prarambh "go" shabd se hota he or samaapti "devpatni" shabd se dekhi jaati he.
 nighantu ke paanch adhyay hai. prarambh ke teen adhyyon main "naghantuk" shabdon kaa sangrah he. chaturth adhyay main "negam" shabd or pancham adhyay main "daivat" shabd ekatrit kiye gaye hai. prayek adhyay main vibhagadyotak khand hai. pehle teen adhyyon main sajaateey shabdon kaa chayan kiya gaya he. yah niyam chaturth or pancham adhyyon main nahin he.
