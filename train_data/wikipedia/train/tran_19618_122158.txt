 dow sampoornanand kushal tathaa nirbhik rajaneta evam sarvatomukhi pratibhavale sahityakar evam adhyaaapak the. ve uttar pradesh ke mukhyamantri rahe.
 sampoornanand kaa janm varanasi main 1 janvari san 1890 ko ek kysth parivaar main hua. vahiin ke queens kalege se bi.es.si. kii pariksha uteern kar prayag chale gaye or vahaan se el.tii. kii oopaadhi praapt kii. iske baad aap prem mahavidhyalay tathaa baad main duunger kalege main pradhanadhyaapak ke pad par niyukt hue. desh kii pukaar par aapane yah naukari chhod di or phir kaashi ke sukhyat deshbhakt babu shivaprasad gupt ke aamantran par gyanmandal sanstha main kaam karne lage. yaheen rahakar aapane "antarrashtriya vidhaan" likhi or "maryaada" kaa sampadanabhaar bhi sambhaal liya. iske baad jab is sanstha se "today" naamak angreji dainik bhi nikaalane kaa nishchay kiya gaya to iskaa sampadan bhi aapako hi saunpa gaya jise aapane badi yogyata ke saath sampann kiya.
 shri sampoornanand main shuru se hi rashtraseva kii lagan thi or aap mahatma gandhi dwaara sanchalit swadhinata sangram main hissaa lene ko aatur rahete the. isi se sarkari vidyalayon kaa bahishkaar kar aae hue vidhyaarthiyon ko rashtriya shiksha pradaan karne ke uddeshy se sthapit kaashi viapeeth main sevakaarya ke liye jab aapako aamantrit kiya gaya to aapane saharsha use sweekaar kar liya. vahaan adhyaapan kaary karte hue aapane kai baar satyagrah andolan main hissaa liya or jail gaye. san 1926 main aap pratham baar congress kii ore se khadey hokar vidhansabha ke sadsy nirvaachit hue. san 1937 main congress mantrimandal kii sthapana hone par shikshaamantri pyarelal sharma ke tyaagptr de dene par aap uttar pradesh ke shikshaamantri bane or apni adbhut kaaryakshmata evam kushalta kaa parichay diya. aapane grih, arth tathaa soochna vibhaag ke mantri ke rup main bhi kaary kiya. san 1955 main shri govindvallabh pant ke kendriya mantrimandal main sammilit ho jane ke baad do baar aap uttar pradesh ke mukhya mantri niyukt hue. san 1962 main aap rajasthan ke rajyapal banaae gaye jahaan se san 1967 main aapane avkaash grahan kiya.
 shri sampoornanand bhartiya samskruti evam bhartiyata ke anany samarthak the. yoga or darshan unke priy vishay the. ve niyamit rup se pujapath or sandhya karte the tathaa maathe par tilak lagaate the. raajnneeti main ve samaajwaad ke anuyaayi the kintu unka samaajwaad uske videshi praturup se bhinn bharat kii paristhitiyon evam bhartiya vichaarparampara ke anuroop tha. hindi tathaa sanskrit se unhen vishesh prem tha par ve angreji ke atirikt urdu, farsi ke bhi achche gyaata tathaa bhautikee, jyotish or darshan shaastra ke bhi pandit the. vibhinn vishyon kii prabhoot pustaken ve nirantar padhate rahete the or apni maanas manjusha main jin amuly gyanaratnon kaa sangrah kiya karte the, lokhit ke liye unke dwaara unka daan or utsarg bhi hota rahata tha. hindi main vaigyanik upanyaas unhone hi sarvapratham likhaa. is prakaar unhone adhyayan, manan se jo kuch bhi ikattha kiya uskaa bahulansh "aadana hi visagarth sataan vaarimuchamiv" is ukti ke anusaar apni praudh lekhnee dwaara janata main pun: vitarit kar diya. aapaki kuch pramukh hindi rachanaayen ye hai : antarashtri vidhaan, samaajwaad, chidvilas, ganesh, jyotirvinod, kuch smritiyaan, kuch sfoot vichaar, hindu dev parivaar kaa vikaas, grahanakshatra. inke atirikt samayik patron main aapane jo bahusankhyak lekh likhe ve bhi hindi sahitya kii amuly nidhi hai. inke kuch sangrah prakashit bhi ho chuke hai.
 uttar pradesh main unmukt karagaar kaa adbhut prayog aapane prarambh kiya jo yathesht rup se safal hua. nainital main vedhashala sthapit karaane kaa shrey bhi aapako hi he. varanasey sanskrit vishvavidhyalay or uttar pradesh sarakar dwaara sanchalit hind samiti kii sthapana main aapkaa mahatvapurn yogadan rahaa he. ye donon sansthaayen aapaki utkrisht sanskritnishta evam hind prem ke adwitiya smarak hai. kala ke kshetra main lakhanu ke maris mujeec college ko aapane vishvavidhyalay str kaa bana diya. kalakaaron or sahityakaron ko shaskeey anudaan dene kaa aarambh desh main pratham baar aapane hi kiya. vriddhavastha kii pension bhi aapane aarambh kii. aapako desh ke anek vishvavidhyalayon ne "doctor" kii sammanit oopaadhi se vibhooshit kiya tha. hindi sahitya sammelan kii sarvocch oopaadhi "sahityavachaspati" bhi aapako mili thi tathaa hindi sahitya kaa sarvocch puraskaar "manglaprasad puraskaar" bhi aap praapt kar chuke the.
 aapkaa nidhan 10 janvari 1969 ko varanasi main hua.
 purv mukhyamantri, uttar pradesh
