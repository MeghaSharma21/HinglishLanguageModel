 puraatatvashaastra or maanavshaastra main lambaaghar aise ek kamare vaale ghar ko kaha jaataa he jisaki chaudaai kam ho lekin lambaai uske mukaable main bahut zyada ho. asia, europe or uttar america ke kai samudaayon main aise lambeghar banane kaa rivaaj he. aksar aise ghar lakdi, sarkandon yaa bans ke bane hote hai. kai manushya samaajon main dekha gaya he ke samaaj ke shuruyaat main kisi bhi anya maqaan banane se pehle lambeghar banaaye jate hai. inkaa prayog samudaay ke mukhya vyaktiyon kii baithkon se lekar pariwaron ke rahane ke liye ho saktaa he. misaal ke liye uttar america ke irkwoi mul america adivasee samudaay ke har lambeghar main lagbhag 20 parivaar ikatthe rahete the. har ghar kii mukhiya us ghar kii sab se buzurg aurat hoti thi.
