 rathschild vansh ek vishwaprasiddh vyapaarik vansh he jisane 1760 ke dashak main bank kaa kaarobaar sthapit kiya jo kaaphi phala-phula. germany ke francfort nivasee meyar amscale rothschild se yah vansh bankinc ke kshetra main sthapit hua. amscale rothschild ne apanepanch putron ke saath kaam karte hue ek intaraashtriya banking parivaar kii neenv daali. is parivaar ne us samay ke sabase shaktishaalee pariwaron ko bhi peeche chhod diya. vartmaan me is parivaar ke paas kitni sampatti he yah aapasi vivaad ke kaaran tay nahi ho paaya he.
