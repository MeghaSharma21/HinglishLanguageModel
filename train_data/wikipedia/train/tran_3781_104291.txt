 ankan lipi, kunifarm lipi yaa keelakshar vishwa main likhane kii pracheenatam vidhiyon main se ek he. chhathi-saatvin sadi i.pus. se lagbhag ek hajaar varshon tak iran main kisi-na-kisi rup main iskaa prachlan rahaa. pracheen farsi yaa avastai ke alaava madhyayugi farsi yaa irani bhi isamen likhi jaati thi. sikandar ke akraman ke samay ke prasiddh badshaah daara ke anek abhilekh evam prasiddh shilaalekh isi lipi main ankit he. inhen 'daara ke keelakshar lekh' bhi kehete hai. 
 is lipi kaa prayog sabase pehle 30veen sadi isapurv main sumer sabhyataa main ubhara or isaki kuch purvaj lekhan vidhiyaan ke bhavchitra bhi milen hai. samay ke saath-saath yah chitralipi saral hoti gai or chitron se hatakar swaron ko darshane lagi. jahaan kaansya yug ke shuru main koi 1000 keelakshar chihn the yah kaansya yug ke ant tak ghatakar keval 400 rah gaye. samay ke saath-saath phonishian lipi zyaada prachlit ho gai or keelakshar lipi khatm ho gai.
 kunifarm lipi yaa keelakshar namakaran aadhunik he. ise pressipolitain bhi kehete hai. yah ardh-varnatmak lipi thi. isamen 41 varna the jinamein 4 paramaavashyak evam 37 dhvanyatmak sanket the.
 is lipi kaa vikaas mesopotamia evam vebelonia kii pracheen sabhy jatiyon ne kiya tha. bhashabhivyakti chitron dwaara hoti thi. ye chitra mesopotamia main keelon se naram eenton par ankit kiye jate the. tirchhi-sidhi rekhaayen khinchne main saralata hoti thi, kintu golakaar chitrankan main kathinaayee. sam desh ke logon ne inhen se aksharatmak lipi kaa vikaas kiya jisase aaj kii arabi lipi viksit hui. mesopotamia or sam se hi iranavaalon ne ise liya. katipay strot is lipi ko finish lipi se viksit maanate hai. daara pratham ke khudwaaye keelaksharon ke 400 shabdon main pracheen farsi ke rup surakshit hai.
