 arundhati kirakire  ek purv bhartiya mahila cricket khilaadi he.
 kirakire bhartiya mahila team ke liye 2000 se 2005 tak test or ek divasiy antarrashtriya praroopon main khelaa karti thi. ye mukhya rup se ballebaaji ke liye jaani jaati he jabaki ek wicket-keeper kii bhi bhoomika nibhaati thim.
