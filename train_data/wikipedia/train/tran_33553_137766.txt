 pracheen kuraasaan yaa pracheen koraasaan madhya asia kaa ek aitihaasik kshetra tha jisamen aadhunik afghanistan, turkmenistan, uzbekistan, tajikistan or purvi iran ke bahut se bhaag shaamil the. isamen kabhi-kabhi sonda or aamu-paar kshetra shaamil kiye jate the. dhyaan dijiye ki aadhunik iran main ek 'koraasaan praant' he jo is aitihaasik kuraasaan ilaake kaa keval ek bhaag he.
 madhya farsi main 'khur' kaa matlab 'suraj' or 'asaan' yaa 'ayaan' kaa matlab 'aana' hota he. 'kuraasaan' kaa matlab he 'vah jagah jahaan se suraj aata ho' yani 'purvi zamin'. yah naam isliye padaa kyonki kuraasaan kshetra iran se purv main he.
 khurasaan iran ke us uttar-purvi praant kaa namahai, jo uttar menrusi cospianpradesh se sata hua he. atrakanadi chaat takisaki bhugolinseema kaa nirdhaaritkarti he. iskepurv main afghanistan, pashchimme astrabad, shahrud, semnaan,damdhaan or yajd keirani praant auradakshin main keraman he. isprakaar iskaa kshetrafal25,000 vargamilhai. khurasaan kaa adhikanshdharathaleey bhagpahadi,marusthaliya namakinjhil kaa nichalaa gartahe. dakshin mempahari bhaagki oonchaai 11,000se lekar 13,000 tak he. 
 khurasaan main kuontthaa bich-bich main luptaho janevaali nadiyon dwaara sinchitabahut se nachlistaan paae jatehain. atarek or kashafaki upjaughaatiyon main khadyann, kapaas ,tambaakoo, chukandar tathaa falonki kheti hoti he.yah praant kesar, pista, gond,kashthafal, kambal, khaal auraneelamani aadi keliye prasiddh he. yahan par loha ,sisa, namak, sona,tanba or sphatikabhi paaya jaataa he.
 'meshed' khurasaan praantki rajdhani he.yah sadak dwaara anya pramukhanagaron se judi he.mulya kii drushti seniryaat kii vastuenkramash: kaaleen,chamada tathaa khaal,opium,imarti lakdi,kapaaski chijen,silk auraneelamani hai.
