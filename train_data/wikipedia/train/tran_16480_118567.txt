 maharajaadhiraj sar raaj rajeshwar savai shri tukojirao tritiy holkar terahaven bahadoor GCIE indore ke holkar rajvansh ke maharaja the jo shivajirao holkar ke putr or uttaradhikari the. wo 31 janvari 1903 ko raja bane. unki mata akhand sobhagyawati shrimant maharani sita bai sahib holkar thim. unhone apni shiksha daali college, indore or isisi dehradun se purna kii.
