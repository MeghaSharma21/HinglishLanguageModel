 raahi saranobat ek bhartiya mahila nishanebaaj hai, jinhone 5 april 2013 ko korea ke changwan main chal rahe iesaef vishwa cap kii 25 meter pistal spardhaa ke final main sthaneey nishanebaaj keyonge kim ko 8-6 se haraakar swarna padak bharat ke naam kiya. wein vishwa cap main swarna padak jeetne vaali pehali bhartiya pistal nishanebaaj hai.
 raahi ne america main 2011 iesaef vishwa cap main kaansya padak jeeta tha. kaamanwealth 2010 main ek gold or ek silver medal jeetne vaaleen raahi ne iesaef world cap, 2011 main bronj jitkar olympic kaa tikat liya.
 landan olympix 2012 main raahi bharat kii sabase kam umr kii nishanebaaj thim.
 2014 kaamanwealth games main 25 meter air pistal main raahi saranobat ne behtareen pradarshan karte hue gold medal jeeta.tathaa 2014 ke asian khelon main kaansya padak hasil kiya.
 2018 asian khelon main raahi ne nishanebaaji kii 25 meter pistal spardhaa main khel record ke saath bharat ko swarna padak dilvaaya. is prakaar se vah asian khelon main nishanebaaji main swarna padak praapt karne vaali pehali mahila khilaadi baniin.
 raahi saranobat ne shooting kii taiyaari kolhapur ke sambhaji raaje shooting centre main kii.
 raahi ne apana pehla medal youth kaamanwealth games main jeeta tha.
