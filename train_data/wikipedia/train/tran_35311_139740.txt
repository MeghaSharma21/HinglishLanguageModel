 qattara droni uttarpashchimi misr ke matruh rajya main libayai registan main sthit ek ragistani droni he. is droni main africa kaa doosraa sab se nichalaa jamini kshetra aata he, jo samudri sataha se 133 meter neeche he. is droni kaa kshetrafal lagbhag 19,500 varg kimi he, yani bharat ke mizoram rajya se thodaa chota. qattara droni ke bich ke sabase nichle bhaag main ek namak kaa maidan he. iske 20 kimi pashchim par qattara se choti ek or droni main siva nachlistaan he. is droni main keval ek sthai aabaadi vaala qasbaa he, jiskaa naam qara nachlistaan he or jisamen barbar jati ke lagbhag 300 log rahete hai.
