 varna-vyavastha hindu dharm main saamaajik vibhaajan kaa ek aadhaar he. hindu dharm-granthon ke anusaar samaaj ko chaar varnon main vibhajit kiya gaya he- brahman, kshatriya, vaisya or shudra. isame brahman varna ko sabase shreshth mana gaya he. haalaaki shudra varna ko baaki sab varna kii seva karne kaa praavdhaan in dharm grantho me kiya gaya he.
 suttanipaat
 
 
 mrituraj kshatriya, brahman, vaisya,shudra, chandaal or bhangi- kisi ko kobhi nahin chodta, sabko kuchal daalata he.
 
 
 ve sabhi jo surat he, vinamr ho satkarmon main lage he, sudant he, aatmasanyam kaa jivan jite he, ve sabhi parinivruth he, chaahe ve kshatriya ho, brahman ho, vaisya ho, shudra ho, chandal ho yaa bhangi ho
 vidwaanon kaa mat he ki aarambh main yah vibhaajan karm aadhaarit tha lekin baad main yah janmadhaarit ho gaya. vartmaan main hindu samaaj main isi kaa viksit rup jati-vyavastha ke rup main dekha ja saktaa he. jati ek antarvivaahi samooh he. 1901 kii jati aadhaarit janaganana ke anusaar bharat main do hajaar se adhik jatiyaan nivas karti hai.
