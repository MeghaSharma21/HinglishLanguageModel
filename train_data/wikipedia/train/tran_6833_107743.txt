 
 vande maataram bhartiya sangeetkaar e. aar. rahamaan kii studio elbum he. yah sabase adhik bikne vaali bhartiya gaiyr-filmi album he. ise 9 disambar 1997 main colombia records dwaara jari kiya gaya tha. album bharat kii aazaadee kii swarna jayanti varshganth ke avasar par jari kiya gaya tha or bharat ke logon ke bich deshbhakti garv or rashtriya ekta kii bhaavana ko main bharne main isane mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai thi.
