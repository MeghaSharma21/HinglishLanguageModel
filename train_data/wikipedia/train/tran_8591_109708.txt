 maanavjit singh sandhu  ek bhartiya punjabi nishanebaaj he. jo visheshtah trape shooting ke liye jane jate hai inhen 2006 main rajeev gandhi khel ratna bhi diya gaya tathaa 1998 main arjuna puraskaar se nawaaza gaya.
 sandhu ne laurence skool sanavar main shiksha grahan kii thi.
 ye punjab ke firozpur zile ke ratta kheda gaanv se nivas karte hai inke pita kaa naam gurbir singh he.
 inhone 2006 main iesaef world shooting championships main swarna padak jeeta tha. saath hi inhone 1998 or 2002 ke asian khelon main chaar rajat padak bhi jite the.
 1998 ke rashtramandal khelon main bhi inhone swarna padak jeeta tha or 2006 ke khelon main kaansya padak jeeta.
 maanavjit singh sandhu ne 2016 greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main bhi qwalifai kiya. jo ki brazil ke rio di generio main yojit kiya gaya.
