 2 karod 
 marathi bharat ke maharashtra praant main boli janevaali sabase mukhya bhashaa he. bhashaai parivaar ke str par yah ek arya bhashaa he jiskaa vikaas sanskrit se apbhransh tak kaa safar puraa hone ke baad aarambh hua. marathi bharat kii pramukh bhashaaon main se ek he. yah maharashtra or goa main rajyabhasha he tathaa pashchim bharat kii sah-rajyabhasha hai. maatrubhaashiyon ki sankhya ke aadhaar par marathi vishwa main pandrahawein or bharat main chauthe sthaan par he. ise bolne waalon kii kul sankhya lagbhag 9 karod he. yah bhashaa 900 isvi se prachlan main he or yah bhi hindi ke samaan sanskrit aadhaarit bhashaa he.
 marathi bhashaa bharat ke atirikt morishas or israel main bhi marathi mul ke logon dwaara boli jaati he. inke atirikt anya desh bhi hai jahaan marathi mul ke log nivas karte he or ve is bhashaa kaa upyog karte he jinamein pramukh hai - america, sanyukt arab emirate, dakshin africa, singapore, germany, sanyukt rajshahi, australia,west eendis or newzealand.
 bharat me marathi mukhyatah maharashtra main boli jaati he. iske atirikt yah goa, karnataka, gujarat, andhra pradesh, madhya pradesh, tamil nadu or chhattisgarh main boli jaati he. kendra shasit pradeshon main yah daman or div or dadar or nagar haveli main boli jaati he. jinamein maharashtra or goa kii yah rajyabhasha he.
