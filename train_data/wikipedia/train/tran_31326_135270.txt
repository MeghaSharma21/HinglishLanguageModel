   yah lekh yaa iske kuch prabhag tatasth naa hokar ekapaksheeya ho sakate hai. kripaya aise anshon ke liye sandarbh uplabdh karaayen yaa inhen hataa den.
 yah siddhant vedistan naamak hindu rashtravaadi lahuurashtra ke samraat harshavardhan ne apane 4 varsho kii kade anesandhan ke baad sthapit kiya. yah siddhanth angrejo or anya bharat virdohi itihaaskaaro kaa bhanda phodne kaa daawa karata he.
 is siddhanth ke anusaar sindhu nadi sabhyataa ke tathaa kathith dravid logo or uttar bharat ke tathaa kathith aaryo me koi fark nahin he. is ke anusaar bhartiya sabhyataa ek he jo kai nadiyo ke sahare se panpi. sindhu nadi ghati ilaake me zyaadaatar anaaj saraswati nadi ilaake se aata tha. jab 5000 varsh pehle saraswati sutluj ke bahaav me parivartan aane ke kaaran vilupt ho gai tab sindhu-saraswati ilaake me khadya samsya utpann hui. zyaadaatar saraswati ilaake ke log dakshin bharat me pravaas karte hai. is kaa mukhya kaaran yah tha ki saraswati nadi ilaake ke logo ko pataa tha ki pashchim ke taraf pravaas kaa koi phayda nahin, ve jaanate the kii sindhu nadi ilaake kaa bhi koi badiya haal nahin he. or ve dakshin bharat me bare me sindhu nadi ke pashchim kii dunaya se zyada jaanate the. yah log aaj ke tathaa kathith dravid hai. sindhu nadi ilaake me paridrishy kuch alag tha. yah ek vyapaarik kendra tha. yahan par jivan bahut hi viksit tha. yahan par rahane vaale logo ko pashchim asia kii duniya ke bare me dakshin bharat se zyada jaankaree thi.
