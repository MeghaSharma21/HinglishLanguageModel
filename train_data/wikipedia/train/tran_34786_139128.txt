 
 gandhak yaa salfar ek rasinic tatv he jisaki parmaanu sankhya 16 hoti he. iskaa sanket S hota he tathaa parmanubhaar 32.1. yah aavart sarini main oxigen ke group main aata he. yah jaev padaarthon main vibhinn roopon main vidyaman rahata he.
 prakruti main sulfur mukt tathaa sanyukt donon avasthaa main miltaa he. mukt avasthaa main yah jwalamukhi kshetron, mukhyatah italy ke sicily tathaa japan main paaya jaataa he. america ke luciana tathaa texas rajyon main puthvi kii sataha ke 250 meter neeche yah prachur maatra main miltaa he.
 sanyukt avasthaa main yah dhaatuon ke sulphid tathaa sulphate ke rup main miltaa he - 
 yah lahasun, pyaaj, anda jaise jaivik padaarthon main bhi paaya jaataa he.
