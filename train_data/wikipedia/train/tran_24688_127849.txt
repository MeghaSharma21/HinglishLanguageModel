 astitvvaad ek aisi vichaardhaara he jisamen astitv ko tatv se upar samajha jaataa he. iske anusaar maanav apane paryaavaran kii nirjeev vastuon se aatmbodh kaa uttardaayitv purnataya swayan letaa he. atah use apane paryaavaran main tatvagyan kii apeksha nahin hoti.
 1940 va 1950 ke dashak main astitvvaad puure europe main ek vichaarkranti ke rup main ubhara. europe bhar ke darshanik va vichaarkon ne is andolan main apana yogadan diya he. inamen jyaan-paal sartra, albert kaamu va ingmaar bergaman pramukh hai.
 kalantar main astitvvaad kii do dharaaen ho gai. 
 astitvvaadi vichaar yaa pratyay kii apeksha vyakti ke astitv ko adhik mahatva dete hai. inke anusaar saare vichaar yaa siddhant vyakti kii chintna ke hi parinaam hai. pehle chintan karne vaala maanav yaa vyakti astitv main aayaa, atah vyakti astitv hi pramukh he, jabaki vichaar yaa siddhant gaun. unke vichaar se har vyakti ko apana siddhant swayan khojna yaa bananaa chahie, dusron ke dwaara pratipaadit yaa nirmit siddhanton ko sweekaar karnaa uske liye aavashyak nahin. isi drushtikon ke kaaran inke liye sabhi paramparaagat, saamaajik, naitik, shastreey evam vaigyanik siddhant amaanya yaa avyaavhaarik siddh ho jate hai. unka maananaa he ki yadi ham dukh evam mrityu kii anivaaryta ko sweekaar kar len to bhay kahan rah jaataa he.
 astitvaadi ke anusaar dukh or avsaad ko jivan ke anivaary evam kamya tattvon ke rup main sweekaar chahie. paristhitiyon ko sweekaar karnaa yaa na karnaa vyakti kii hi ichha par nirbhar he. inke anusaar vyakti ko apni sthiti kaa bodh du:kha yaa traas kii sthiti main hi hota he, atah us sthiti kaa swaagat karne ke liye prastut rahanaa chahie. dastaevsky ne kaha tha- ‘‘yadi ishwar ke astitv ko mitaa den to phir sab kuch sambhav he.’’
 victor i frankal ek lekhak or manochikitsak bhi handi vah vienna chikitsa vidhyalay ke vishvavidhyalay main manorog or neurology ke praadhyaapak rahedi unki mrityu san 1977 main huiqui unke kareeb batis kitabe chabis bhashaaon main anuvaadit kiye ja chuke handi vishwa yuddh II ke samay unhone apane teen saal oschvitz, dachaau or kai alag ekaagratha shivi main bitaayeti
 unhone apane pustak “manus search for mining”, main logotherapy naam ke chikitsa ke baaren main vistaar main bataaya hai or yahi nahi isi pustak main unhone mrityu shivi se astitvvaad tak ke yatra ke vishay main apane anubhav ke bahut si jhalkiyaan di haii logotherapy ko apana mul vishay samajhakar hi unhone astitvvaad ko paribhaashit karnaa chaha or ve kahi had tak is main safal bhi rahedi logos ek greek shabd hai jiskaa shuddh rup hai ‘arthmai logotherapy ek manushya ke astitv v manushya ke arth kii khoj par dhaarit handi logotherapy ke aadhaar par jivan main arth ke khoj kaa prayaas ek manushya kaa sabase pratham prerak bal handi isi kaaran frankal ne ucch mahatva jivan ke arth ko diya hai naa ki anand kobhi 
 manushya ke arth ke khoj kii dhara main hataasha yaa niraashaa kaa bhi bahut bada bhaag hota handi logotherapy ne ise astitv kuntha kaa naam diya handi ‘astitv’ shabd ko teen tareeke se upyog main daalaa ja saktaa handi astitv jo apane aap main arth hai yani manushya ke jivan kaa aadharani astitv kaa arth, vyaktigat astitv main thos arth ke khoj kaa prayasi is hataasha kaa parinaam neurosis bhi ho saktaa handi victor i frankal apane marijon ke takalif dwaara ek bahut bade prashn ko uthaate hai jo aaj ke jivan main sabaki durbalata kaa kaaran ban chuki hai, vah hai zindagi main arthhinata kaa pravasdai aisi takalif adhiktar un logo main dekhi gayi jo mrityu shivi se batch niklequi in logo main zindagi ko jeene ki utsukata hi nahin rahiqui unke zehan main khokhlaapan hi rahaa kyunki ve aatanrik akelepan se guzarate handi is khoklepan yaah akelepan ko jisase har insaan jhujhata hai use victor i frankal ne astitv vaakum kaa naam diyani
 astitv vaakum ek aisi ghatana hai jo beesvi sadi main bahut hi vikhyath hui handi astitv vacum ki shruwaat udaasi se hoti handi isi avasar par frankal ‘ravivaar neurosis’ ke baaren main spasht karte handi yah ek tareeke kaa avsaad hai jisame vyakti apane jivan main santosh kii kami ko mahasoos karata hai or iskaa ehsaas use tab hota hai jab vah hafte bhar ke bhaag-dod ke baad apane jivan ke akelepan ko mahasoos karata handi frankal ke hisaab se sharabipan or bal aparaadh ke kaarnon ko tab tak samajha nahin ja saktaa jab tak usase dabe hue astitv vacum ko manyata nahin miltishi yahi kaaran vruddh logo or pensioner ke stithiyon main bhi lagu hoti handi yah astitv vacum alag alag bhesh dhaaran karte hai or jivan ke bhinn bhinn pahaluon main manushya ke samaksh prastut hoti handi inamen se kuch shakti kii ichha yaa dhan kii ichha ho sakati hai jo arth ke khoj main manushya ke raah ko dhundhala kar detii handi yah astitv kuntha kaa hi parinaam hai jisase yon libdo bhi aniyantrat ho jaati handi
 logotherapy kii koshish hameshaa yahi rahati hai ki marijon ko puri tarah se apane kartavya ke taraf modaa jaekai taaki vah yah samaj sake ki ve kiske liye, kis liye or kyon kartavya se bandhe hue handi frankal kaa yah kahana hai ki jivan kaa vaastavik arth swayan ke maanas main nahin balki sansar main handi usee tarah maanav ke astitva kaa asli uddeshy aatm parichay se nahin hotaryi uskaa saar aatm shreshtataa se praapt hoti handi frankal is baat ko nyaay siddh karne ke liye tark yah laate hai ki aatm parichay kabhi bhi jivan kaa aadhaar nahin ban saktaa kyunki maanav jitna use paane kaa praytn karegaa vah utana usko khoyegaqui
 sansar ko swayan ki abhivyakti kabhi nahin samajhnaa chahie, naa hi sansar ko ek maatr saadhan yaah aatm parichay ko praapt karne kaa upaychiisse yahi aashay spasht hota hai ki jivan kaa arth sthir nahin he, par iskaa koi chor bhi nahin handi logotherapy ke antargat ham jivan ke arth teen tareeke se dhund sakate hai: karm ke madhyam se; kisi ke yaah kisi vaastu ke mulya ko anubhav karke; pidaa sekhi pehle tareeke ko purna karnaa atyant laabhadaayak haii dusre or tisare tareeko main punah vistaar ki aavashyaktaa handidusraa tarika jisase jivan main arth kaa pravesh hota hai wo anubhavon se hi praapt hota hai, yah shiksha hamen prakruti se ho sakati hai yaa phir samskruti se yaa phir kisi ko anubhav karke bhi yani prem ke dwarai prem hi vah ek dhaal hai jisase manushya ke antaratmik vyaktitv ko sinchaa ja saktaa handi koi bhi kisi manushya ko tab tak nahin jaan saktaa yaa samaj saktaa jab tak prem bhav ki utpatti nahin hotiji prem ko aadhyatmik rup ke anusaar agar daalaa jaae to usase vah dusre manushya ke mahatvapurn lakshan ko dekh saktaa hai, vahi manushya jisase vah prem karata hoi isse bhi adhik vah apane samarthy ki pushti ho jaati he jo aatm parichay ke raah par bahut bada kadam haii isse donon vyakti apane samarthy ko pahchaan jate handi
 teesraa tarika jo manushya ko apane jivan ke arth ko khojane main sahaayta karata hai vah he peedaashi manushya jab bhi asahaay yaa aisi paristhiti se guzarataa he jiskaa parinaam unke haath main na ho jaise: koi laailaaj beemaari, usee vakt ek insaan ko apni pahchaan ko bodh karnaa kaa bharpoor mauka miltaa he, arthath ksht ko jhelkar jivan kaa sabase bada arth paane kaa avasar miltaa handi isamen ksht ki taraf vyakti kaa nazariyaa bhi peeda ko jhelane ki takat detaa haii
