 dhyaan yoga 
 
 na tato na mata na bandhurn daata na putro na putree na bhrityo na bharta.na jaaya na vidya na vrittirmamaev gatistvan gatistvan tvameka bhavani .1.
 bhavabdhavpaare mahadukhabhiruh papaat prakaami pralobhi pramattah.kusansarpashaprabaddhah sadaahan gatistvan gatistvan tvameka bhavani .2.
 na janaami daanan na c dhyanayogan na janaami tantran na c stotramantram.na janaami puujaan na c nyasayogam gatistvan gatistvan tvameka bhavani .3.
 na janaami punyan na janaami tirthan na janaami muktin layan vaa kadachit.na janaami bhaktin vratan vaapi maatargatistvan gatistvan tvameka bhavani .4.
 kukarmi kusangi kubuddhih kudasah kulachaarheenah kadacharlinah.kudrishtih kuvakyprabandhah sadahm gatistvan gatistvan tvameka bhavani .5.
 prajeshn rameshan maheshan sureshan dineshan nishitheshwaran vaa kadachit.na janaami chanyat sadaahan sharanye gatistvan gatistvan tvameka bhavani .6.
 vivaade vishaade pramaade pravaase jaale chaanale parvate shatrumadhye.aranye sharanye sadaa maa prapaahi gatistvan gatistvan tvameka bhavani .7.
 anatho daridro jararogayukto mahakshinadinah sadaa jadyavaktra.vipattau pravishtah pranshtah sadahm gatistvan gatistvan tvameka bhavani .8.
 iti shrimachhdkaracharyakrutan bhavanyashtakan sampoornam.aacharya sukhendra ji maharaj
