 bhartiya lipiyon ko braille paddati se nirupit karne ke liye viksit ekasmaan vyavastha ko bharti braille kehete hai. san 1951 ke aasapaas bhartiya lipiyon ko likhane kii ekikrut vyavastha sweekrut kar lee gayi jo srilanka, nepal or bangladesh ne bhi sweekaar kar liya he.
 bharti braille 6 dot par aadhaarit he. yah bharat ke sabhi lipiyon ke varnon ke liye, jahaan tak sambhav hua he, samaan braille sanket diya gaya he.
