 baijnath uttrakhand rajya ke bageshwar janapad main gomti nadi ke kinare ek chota sa nagar he. yah apane pracheen mandiron ke liye vikhyath he, jinhen bhartiya puraatatv surveykshan dwaara uttrakhand main rashtriya mahatv ke smarakon ke rup main manyata praapt he. baijnath un chaar sthanon main se ek he, jinhen bharat sarkaar kii swadesh darshan yojana ke tahat 'shiv heritage circuit' se joda janaa he. 
 baijnath ko prachinkaal main "kartikeypur" ke naam se janaa jaataa tha, or tab yah katyuri rajvansh ke shaskaon kii rajdhani thi. katyuri raja tab gadhwal, kumaun tathaa doti kshetron tak raaj karte the.
 is kshetra ke sabase puraane avasheshon main karvirpur yaa kabirpur naamak ek shahar shaamil he. is shahar ke khandhiron kaa prayog karke hi katyuri raja narsingh dev ne apni rajdhani yahan basaai thi. 7veen se 13veen shatabdi tak baijnath katyuri rajvansh kii rajdhani thi, or tab ise kartikeypur kaha jaataa tha.
 nepali akramanakari cranchaldev ne 1191 main baijnath par akraman kar katyuri raajaaon ko parajeet kar diya. is akraman se kamjor hua katyuri rajya 13veen shatabdi tak 8 alag alag riyasaton main vighatit ho gaya. vighatan ke baad bhi 1565 tak baijnath main katyuri rajvansh ke mul vanshajon kaa hi shashan rahaa, or unhen baijnath katyur kaha jane laga. 1565 main almoda ke raja baalo kalyaan chand ne baijnath par kabza kar liya or use apane rajya main hi mila liya.
 1791 main kaali nadi ke purv kii ore apane rajya kaa vistaar karte hue gorkha raajaaon ne almoda par akraman kiya, or sampoorn kumaun rajya par adhikaar praapt kar liya. east india company ne 1814 ke angl-nepal yuddh main gorkhaon ko haraa diya, jiske baad 1816 main sugauli sandhi ke anusaar yah angrejon ko praapt hua. :594 
 1901 main baijnath 148 kii aabaadi vaala ek chota sa gaanv tha. 
 baijnath bageshwar janapad main 29°55′N 79°37′E / 29.92°N 79.62°E / 29.92; 79.62 par janapad mukhyalay ke 20 kimi uttar main sthit he. samudr tal se isaki ausat oonchai 1,130 meter he. baijnath kumaun himalay main sthit katyur ghati main gomti nadi ke tat par basaa he.
 2007-2008 main mandir parisar ke paas ek kritrim jhil kii ghoshnaa kii gayi thi. is jhil kaa udghaatan 14 janvari 2016 ko uttrakhand ke tatkaaleen mukhyamantri, harish ravat ne kiya. jhil main machhliyaan bahutayat main hai. haalanki machhlee pakadaney par sakhti se pratibandh he, parantu jhil main ye ek pramukh paryatak aakarshan haior paryatakon machhliyon ko chanaa khilaate hue dekha ja saktaa he. paas hi sthit garud kshetra ke sabase puraane bazaaron main he.
 pantnagar main sthit pantnagar vimaanashetra baijnath se nikatatam hawaii adda he, jabaki kathgodam railway stesan nikatatam railway stesan he. baijnath bageshwar-gvaldaam or almoda-gopeshwar sadakon ke tirahe par sthit he. yah uttarakhand parivahan nigam kii 'kumaun darshan' bas seva dwaara haldwani, bhimtal, almoda or ranikhet se judaa hua he. 
 tanakpur se bageshwar tak ek rail patari prastaavit he, jiske ban jane ke baad 2020 se yah kshetra or adhik paryatakon ko aakarshith kar paayegaa.
