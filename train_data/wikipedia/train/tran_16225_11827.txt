 samuel richardsan kii gananaa angreji upanyaas ke nirmaataaon main kii jaati he. 
 saadhaaran pratibha ke is lekhak kaa sahityakshetra main pravesh aksmaat hi hua. lagbhag pachaas varsh kii avasthaa tak inkaa puraa samay apane nijee mudran vyavsaay ke safal sanchaalan main hi laga. isi bich do pustakavikritaon ne inase patron kii style main ek upadeshaatmak granth likhane kaa aagrah kiya jisase sarsaadhaaran, visheshtya mahilaon ko, jivan kii vibhinn paristhitiyon main uchit aacharan kii shiksha mil sake. is prakaar 'femilior letters on emportent akegens' ke prakaashan se inke saahitiyik jivan kaa prarambh hua. phir to inhone pamela, clarisa harlo tathaa sar charles gradisson naamak teen bade bade upanyaas likhe jinke kaaran inhen england ke baahar europe main bhi khyaati mili.
 richardsan ke sabhi upanyaas patron kii style main hai. patron ke madhyam se ve apane paatro ke bhaavon kaa sookshm manovaigyanik adhyayan prastut karte hai. angreji upanyaas apane praarambhik yug main ghatnapradhan hi tha. charitrachitran kii drushti se use ek lambii manzil tay kerani thi. richardsan ne pehali baar usamen charitrik vivechan kaa samavesh kiya. visheshtya nari hriday ke paraspar virdohi bhaavon kaa unke upanyaason main gudh adhyayan miltaa he.
 richardsan ke upanyaason kii duusari visheshta unka naitik uddeshy he. upanyaas unke liye keval manoranjan kaa saadhan na hokar pathakon kaa dharm tathaa naitikta main shikshit karne kaa madhyam tha. 'pamela' kii rachana, jaisaa lekhak ne upanyaas ke mukh prishth par hi spasht kar diya he, isi uddeshy se kii gai. naitikta kaa yah aagrah sambhavat: puritain prabhaav tathaa charles dwitiya ke samay ke natya sahitya main ashleelata ke viruddh pratikriya ke kaaran aayaa.
 richardsan main ek duusare prakaar kii pratikriya bhi dekhne ko milti he. jahaan 18veen shatabdi main sahitya tathaa jivan ke vyapak kshetra main buddhi paksh ko hi pradhanta di jaati thi, unhone apane upanyaason main bhav paksh par jor diya.
