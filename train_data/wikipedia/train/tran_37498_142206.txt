 vahan kshamta athwa jansankhya vahan kshamta kisi bhugolic kshetra ke paaritantr main kisi jeevadharee prajati kii us adhiktam jansankhya ke rup main paribhaashit kii jaati he jise us paaritantr ke sansaadhan poshan pradaan kar sakate hon. spasht he ki vahan kshamta se adhik jansankhya vruddhi us paaritantr par dabaav daalegi or atyadhik vruddhi us tantra ke vifal hojaane kaa kaaran bhi ban sakati he.
 paaritantr kii vahan kshamta se adhik jansankhya vruddhi ho janaa jansankhya utkshep kaha jaataa he.
 yah ek paristhitikeey sankalpana he jiskaa vartmaan maanav janaadhiky ko prithvi ke paryaavaran rupi paaritantr ke sansadhano or sevaaon kii seemaa se jodakar dekha ja rahaa he. maanav janaadhiky ek aisi sthiti he jab maanav jansankhya kisi kshetra kii paristhitikeey vahan kshamta se adhik ho jaae. yah shabdaawalee puri maanav jati kii jansankhya or vaishvik paryaavaran kii vahan kshamta ke bich sambandhon ko vyakt karne ke liye bhi istemaal hoti he.
 vidwaanon kaa maananaa he ki manushya jansankhya vruddhi dwaara prithvi ke paryaavaran par dabaav daal rahaa he kyonki maanav jansankhya pehle hi adhiktam vahan kshamta kaa str paar kar chuki he.
