 jwalamukhi shanku, jwalamukhi se sambandhit saralatam sanrachanaon main se ek hai. inkaa nirmaan jwalamukheey udgaar ke samay nikaas nalika se nikle utsarg ke nikaas nalee ke chaaron ore shankvakar rup main jamane se hota he, jabaki is shanku kaa madhya bhaag ek gart ke rup main viksit hota he. udgaar ke dauraan baahar nikle tukdon kii prakruti or aakaar ke anusaar jwalamukhi shanku vibhinn prakaaron main viksit hote hai.
