 sherpa ek maanav jati he jinka mukhya nivas nepal or tibet ke himalayan kshetron tathaa uske aasapaas ke ilaakon main he. tibeti bhashaa main shar kaa arth hota he purab tathaa paa pratyay log ke arth ko vyakt karata he; atah sherpa kaa shabdic arth hota he purab ke log. ye log pichley 200 varshon main purvi tibet se aakar nepal ke in ilaakon main bas gaye. nepal ke pahari kshetron ke log sherpa striyon ko sherpani kehete hai.
 parvatarohan main siddhhast hone kii inaki pratibha ke kaaran nepal main parvatarohiyon ke guide tathaa saamaan dhone ke kaaryon main in sherpaon kii seva lee jaati he. is kaaran, aajkal nepali parvatarohi guide ko saamaanya rup se sherpa kaha jane laga he bhale hi wo sherpa samudaay ke hon yaa naa hon.
 in logon kii bhashaa sherpa bhashaa he. yah bhashaa tibbat kii puraani or paaramparik bhashaa se 65% milti he ore ye log buddha dharm maanate he.
