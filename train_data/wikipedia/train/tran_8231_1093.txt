 emil jaaya hungariyai mul ke ek australian peshewar pahalwaan the jo apane akhaade ke naam king kong se behatar jane jate the.
 king kong ne vishwa bhar main kushtiyaan ladi. bharat main unka muqabla daara singh se hua jahaan unhe paraajay haath lagi.
 san 1956 me apni patni eli se vivahoparaant ve austrailia me hi bas gaye the. san 1970 main singapore main ek kushti mukaable ke liye jate hue sadak durghatana main unki mrityu ho gai.
