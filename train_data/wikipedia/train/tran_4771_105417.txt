 2011 pakistan bhucamp 19 janvari 2011 ko raat 1:53 par pakistan main aayaa shaktishaalee bhucamp tha. dakshin pashchimi pakistan main richter paimaane par 7.4 kii tivrata vaale is bhucamp kaa kendra pakistan kaa baluchistan praant tha. sanyukt rajya bhugarbha surveykshan ke anusaar is bhucamp kaa kendra dharaatal se 84 kilometre neeche tha jis kaaran itani adhik tivrata laa sakane vaale is bhucamp se jan-dhan kii baari kshati nahin hui.
 is bhucamp ke jatke padosi deshon or kuch duuri par sthit deshon tak main mahasoos kiye gaye jaise bharat, afghanistan, iran, oman, sanyukt arab emirate or bahrain. mersali parimaap par is bhucamp kii tivrata vibhinn sthanon par is prakaar thi: islamabad main mep 4, karachi main mep 4, muscat mep mep 4, dilli main mep 4, kabul main mep 3, dubai main mep 3 or abu dhabi main mep 3.
 is bhucamp se pakistan ke dalbandin main 200 mitti ke gharon ke dhahane ke samachar hai. queta main 2 mahilaayein bhucamp ke kaaran hridayaghat se mar gayi. queta bhucamp-kendra se lagbhag 330 kimi uttarpashchim main sthit he, jahaan tivrata mep 4 thi.
 is bhucamp ke teevr jatke uttar bharat main bhi mahasoos kiye gaye. is bhucamp kii tivrata richter paimaane par dilli main 4.2 maapi gai. bharat ke dilli, jaipur, barmer, ganganagar or jaisalmer ke atirikt rajasthan, punjab or haryana ke kai bhaagon main bhucamp ke jatke mahasoos kiye gaye.
 is bhucamp se bharat main kisi bhi prakaar kii kshati nahin hui.
