 tiryak akraman ek sainya yukti he jisamen akramanakari senanayake kisi chune hue sthaan par apni adhikansh shakti jhonk detaa he or shesh shakti ko shesh sthanon par lagaataa taaki shatrusena ko roka ja sake. aisa karke kamjor yaa samaan shakti vaala senanayake ek yaa kuch sthanon par shatru sena par baari pad jaataa he. iske baad senanayake shatru ko purnatah parajeet karne kaa praytn karata he. is vidhi kii manyata he ki apni shakti ko kenrit karke hi safalataa praapt kii ja sakati he, shakti ko sab jagah samaan rup se baantne se aap kamjor bane rahete hai.
 prasha ke fredrick dwitiya kii sena, akraman kii is yukti kaa prayog karne ke liye prasiddh thi.
