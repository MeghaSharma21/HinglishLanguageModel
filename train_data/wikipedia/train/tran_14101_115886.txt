 doodh talai jhil jo ki ek jhil yaa chota talab he jo bhartiya rajya rajasthan ke udaipur zile main sthit he yah maharana fateh singh ke nivas sthaan shiv pales or pichhola jhil ke nikat sthit he. yah jhil maharana pratap hawaii adda se lagbhag 24 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he jabaki udaipur city railway stesan or udaipur city bas depo se maatr 4 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he.
 is jhil tak pahunchne ke liye baahar se kai trainen aati rahati he jo udaipur city railway stesan par rukati he jabaki maharana pratap hawaii adda bhi he to log hawaii jahaaj dwaara bhi pahonchte hai.
 maaniky laal verma udyaan jo ke naam se janaa jaataa he jo pichhola jhil or doodh talai jhil kii shobhaa badhaate hai. iskaa nirmaan udaipur nagar parishad ne san 1995 main karavaaya tha. yahaan udyaan tak pahunchne tak sidhiyaan bhi banaai gayi he taaki saralata se pahunchaa ja sake saath hi sabase upar karani mata kaa mandir bhi he jisamen karani mata kii murti bhi he.
 inke alaava yahaan ek chota udyaan park bhi he jo pandit deen dayaal udyaan park naam se janaa jaataa he isaki dekh rekh urban improvement trust ,udaipur karata he.
 yahan par ek rassi kaa maarg bhi he jo pandit deen dayaal udyaan park ke sheersh bhaag par sthit he yah lagbhag 500 meter ooncha he. yah mono cabel kaa banaaya gaya jisapar mahaj 4 minute kii sawari kii jaati he or puure shahar ko upar se achche se dekh sakate hai.
