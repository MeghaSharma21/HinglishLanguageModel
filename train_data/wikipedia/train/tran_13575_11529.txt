 abtabad zila pakistan ke kaibar-pakhtuunakva rajya ke hazaaraa kshetra main sthit ek zila he. is zile kaa kshetrafal 1,969 varg kimi he or isaki rajdhani abtabad shahar he. 1998 main isaki aabaadi 8,81,000 logon kii thi. is zile kii uttari taraf mansehara zila, pashchimi taraf haripur zila, dakshini taraf pakistani punjab kaa rawalpindi zila or purvi taraf pakistan-niyantrit kashmir kaa muzaffarabad zila padataa he. baaqi hazaaraa kii tarah abtabad zila bhi pahari ilaaka he.
 abtabad zila do tahseelon or ek shahari ilaake main banta hua he - abtabad tehsil, haweliyaan tehsil or navanshahar shahari ilaaka.
 kaibar-pakhtuunakva rajya ke purvi hisson main punjabi bhashaa kii hindko upbhasha boli jaati he or yahi abtabad zile ke logon main bhi dekha jaataa he. yahan pashto bolne vaale bhi kai log rahete hai.
 abtabad zile ke bich main nathiya gali naam kaa prasiddh hill-stesan he. punjab ke maidaanon main garmi se bachane ke liye yahan bahut taadaad main log aate hai kyonki uttari punjab se nathiya gali pahunchnaa bahut aasaan he. 9,777 foot oonchi miranjaani or 9,100 foot oonchi mukeshpuri yahan kii do mashhoor pahadiyaan hai.
