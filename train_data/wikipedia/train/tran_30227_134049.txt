 tatta pani yaa tattapani azaad kashmir ke poonch zile main ek shahar he. yahan gandhak-yukt garam pani ke chashmen hai, jinpar shahar kaa naam padaa he . tatta pani poonch nadi ke kinare 2,237 foot kii oonchaai par basaa hua he. yah kotli se 26 kimi, hajira se 29 kimi or rawalakot se 45 kimi dur he. dhyaan den ki bhartiya upmahadweep ke himalay-vaale kai bhaagon main isi "tattapani" naam ke kai or nagar-gram-muhalle bhi hai or lagbhag in sabhi main garam pani ke chashmen hote hai, masalan himachal pradesh or kaibar-pakhtuunakva donon main is naam ke sthaan hai.
 shahar ke adhiktar log jat or rajput gotron se hai. lagbhag sabhi muslim hai.
