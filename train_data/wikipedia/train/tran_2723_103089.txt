 akelas thos un thos padaarthon ko kehete hai jinke andar paryaapt duuri tak parmaanuon kaa koi sunishchit vinyas nahin hota. 
 akelas thos garam karne par kramash naram ho jate hai or phir dhire-dhire unki shyanta itani kam ho jaati he ki ve chalya banakar drav main parivartit ho jate hai. in padaarthon kaa koi nishchit galnaank nahin hota. ye padaarth thik-thik thos kii paribhaasha ke antargat nahin aate. isliye inako atyadhik shyanta vaale atishitlit drav bhi kaha jaataa he. kaanch, mom, vasa, alkatraa aadi akelas thos main se hai.
 adhikansh thos amarfas rup main paye jate hai yaa unhen amorfas rup main banaaya ja saktaa he.
