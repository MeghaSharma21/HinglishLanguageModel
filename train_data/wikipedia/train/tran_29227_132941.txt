 kudremukh rashtriya udyaan bharat ke karnataka rajya main chikmagalore jile main sthit ek rashtriya udyaan he. yah bharat main pashchimi ghat kaa nam ushnkaribandheey sadabahaar van ke antargat doosraa sabase bada vanyajeev sangrakshit kshetra he. yah udyaan 600.32 varg kimi main failaa hua he. ise 1987 main rashtriya udyaan ghoshit kiya gaya tha.
 yah rashtriya udyaan chikmagalore se 95 kimi dakshin-pashchim main sthit he or 600.32 varg kimi main failaa hua he. isaki ausatan oonchaai samudr sataha se 1894.3 me. he. haal hi main ise baagh aarakshan banane par bhi vichaar chal rahaa he.
