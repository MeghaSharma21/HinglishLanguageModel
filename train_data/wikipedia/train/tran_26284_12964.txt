 1860 isvi main mahaan roosi lekhak evan turgnev dwaara rachit upanyaas 'pita or putr' vartmaan paripekshya main bhi utana hi praasangik he, jitna us daur main tha. turgenev ne is upanyaas ko us daur main likhaa tha, jab rus main taanashaah jaar nikolai pratham ke shashan kaa ant hua tha or vahaan saamaajik badlaav angadaayiyaan le rahi thim. har daur kii tarah is daur main bhi nai peedi or puraani peedi ke bich ek antar dekhne ko miltaa he. nai peedi main hameshaa ek nai soch or nae vichaaron kaa prardubhav hota he or puraani peedi uskaa virodh yah kahkar karti he ki yah nitigat nahin he, is upanyaas ke mul main bhi yahi vichaar nihit he. saath hi pita or putr ke rishte kaa jo varnan miltaa he vah vaaki dil ko chune vaala he. upanyaas ke paatro ke madhyam se unnisvin sadi ke shuruyaat main rus kii saamaajik sthiti kisi thi, is par baariki se prakaash daalaa gaya he. bajarov, arkadi nikolai petrovich, pavel petrovich jaise alag-alag vichaaron vaale paatro ke jariye is baat ko spasht karne kaa prayaas kiya gaya he ki jaar ke shashan ke baad logon ko kin-kin vaicharik or saamaajik sanghashon kaa saamana karnaa padaa he. in sab ke bich vibhinn ghatnaon ke jariye lekhak ne kisaanon kii sthitiyon par bhi prakaash daalaa he. upanyaas ke kendra main bajarov he, jo rus ke us daur kii saamaajik or maansik soch ke bilkul ulat he. vah khud ko nihalist maanata he, uskaa akkadpan hi uski sabase badi khaasiyat he.
