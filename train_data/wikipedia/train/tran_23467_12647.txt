 khaadya utpaadan kii drushti se lagaayi gayi pedo evam jhaadiyon ke samooh ko falodyan kehete hai. inamen ve ped hote hai jo fal yaa mewa utpann karte hai jinako bechakar kamai kii ja sakati he.
 kai baar falaudyan ek bade udyaan kaa ek hissaa hote hai, jahaan ve na sirf udyaan ko saundarya pradaan karte hai apitu falon ke utpaadan se paalak ko aarthik laabh bhi hota he.
