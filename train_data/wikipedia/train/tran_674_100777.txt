 jwaala yaa lau dahan kaa dikhaai dene vaala gasiy avyav he. yah atyadhik ooshmakshepi dahan kriyaaon main dikhataa he - jabaki kai oxikaran kriyaayein bina jwaala ke lapat ke hi ho jaati he. aag kaa rang taapmaana, dahan ke avyavon kaa anupaat or eendhan par nirbhar karata he. nila, laal or pile rang saamaanya hai.
