 kanstentan 1 ek roman samraat tha. vah kanstentan thi great yaa saint constentan aadi naam se bhi jane jate hai. unka shashan 306 i se 337 i tak tha. roman sena adhikari fluvius constentinas or unki patni helnaa kanstentan 1 ke mata-pita the. sisar unka pita bana. constentan ko east bheja gaya or vahaan vah sainya ke ucch pad ko sambhaalane laga tha. 305 main constentan pashchim ke varisht samraat bana. 306 i main apane pita kii mrity ke baad constentan aborakam ke samraat bana. samraat ke rup main kanstentan ne samrajya ko majboot karne ke liye kai prashasnic, vittiya or saamaajik kaary kiye. vah kai yudhon main vijayi huin. shashan kaa punargathan kiya gaya. nayaa sone kaa sikaa pesh kiya gaya tha. unhone ne kabeeley ke khilaaf roman simaaon par safal abhiyaan chalaaya. roman samrajya ke itihaas main ek alag yug kaa ullekhan kiya gaya tha. unhone ek nayaa shahi nivas kaa nirmaan kiya. kanstentan ko aitihaasik rup main "pratham isai samraat" kaha jaataa he.
