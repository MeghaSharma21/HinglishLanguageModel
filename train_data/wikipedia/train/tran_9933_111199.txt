 sammaan rajvansh yaa sammaa rajvansh 1335 isvi se 1520 isvi kaal main sindh tathaa balochistan va punjab ke kuch hisson main raaj karne vaala ek rajput vansh tha. unki rajdhani thaththa shahar tha. ve swayan ko shrikrishna kaa vanshaj bataate the. is vansh kii sthapana jam unad​ ne kari jo shrikrishna ke baad kii 95veen peedi ke the. baad main in raajaaon ne islam main dharm-parivartan kar liya. unhone sindh main kuch mahaan puraatan-sthal chode hai jinamein thaththa kaa shahi samadhi-kshetra shaamil he. is rajvansh ke baad sindh main argaun​ rajvansh kaa qabza ho gaya. sammaaon kii ek shakha 'chudasma' naamak rajput samooh he jo aadhunik gujarat or sindh main basaa hua he.
