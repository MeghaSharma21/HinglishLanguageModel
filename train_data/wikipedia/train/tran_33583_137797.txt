 shughni bhashaa ek pamiri bhashaa-parivaar kii boli he jo madhya asia main tajikistan ke kuhistoni-badakhshaan swashasit praant or afghanistan ke badakhshaan praant main boli jaati he.
 rushani bhashaa, jo adhiktar tajikistan main boli jaati he, shughni kii hi ek upbhasha he. bartaangi, khulafi or roshoravi bhi aksar shughni kii upbhashaayen kehalaati hai lekin vastav main bartaangi or khulafi is se bahut alag bhashaayen hai.
 shughni or saricoli bhashaa donon anya purvi irani bhashaaon se milti-julati hai, lekin donon main kuch aise shabd bhi hai jo vaakhi, pashto or avastai jaisi purvi irani bhashaaon main nahin milte.
 uparalikhit main -
 yah bhi dhyaan den ki irani bhashaayen or hindi va sanskrit jaisi hind-arya bhashaayen sabhi hind-irani bhashaa-parivaar kii sambandhi bhashaayen hai, isliye inamen aapas main bahut se milte-julate sajaateey shabd hote hai
