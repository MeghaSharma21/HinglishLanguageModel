 gurunath vidyanidhi sanskrit ke vidwaan the. unka janm vartmaan bangladesh ke dhaka jile ke vikrampur gaanv main hua tha. unhone kai granthon kii rachana kii jo sanskrit vidwaanon evam shodharthiyon ke liye bahut mahatvapurn hai. mugdhvodh vyakaran, mitralabh, amarkosh, sahitya darpan, tathaa chand manjari aadi unki prasiddh kritiyaan hai.
