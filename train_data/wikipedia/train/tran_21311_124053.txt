 pratihaar kala style madhyakaalin murti style he, is style kii pratimaaon main gupt kaal kii anek visheshtaayen samahit hai. pratihaar kala style kii pratimaaen madhyakaalin style kii anya kala shaileyon kii tulana main sabase adhik prabhaavshaalee evam sundar hai. is style kii pratimaaon ke mukh par prasannataa kaa bhav pradarshit hua he.in pratimaaon ke sharir kii sudaulata par vishesh dhyaan diya gaya he tathaa alankaranon kaa kam prayog hua he. uttar-pradesh ke jila jhansi sthit barua sagar kaa vishaal mandir pratihaar kala style kaa utkrisht udhaaharan he. is mandir ke pravesh dwaar par anek sundar murtiyan ukeri gayi hai. is mandir kii kalakritiyon ko dekhne se spasht hota he ki us samay ke kalakaar murtiyon ke ang-pratyang kii sudaulata ke prati atyant sajag the tathaa inke nirmaan main siddhhast the.
