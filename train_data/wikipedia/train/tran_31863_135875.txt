 hero nan 1 1997 kii david dhawan dwaara nirdeshit hindi bhashaa kii film he. isamen govinda or karishma kapur pramukh bhoomikaaon main he. film adhikanshtah rishikesh mukherjee dwaara nirdeshit kamedi film bawarchi dwaara prerit he. ise telugu main nandamuri baalkrishna ko lekar gopipinti alladu ke rup main punarnirmit kiya gaya tha.
 rajesh malhotra ek dhani vyapaaree dhanraj malhotra kaa putr he. vah apane ghar par khush nahin he kyonki uske pita use apana jivan man-mutabik jeene nahin dete hai. vah apane ghar se nikalataa he or europe pahunchataa he. minaa deenanath kii potee he or usne europe main adhyayan karne ke liye chaatravrutti hasil kii he. vah apni chachee shanno ke saath europe yatra karti he. rajesh or minaa milte hai or pyaar main padate hai. dhanraj malhotra apane bete kii talash main sahaayak sharma ke saath europe pahonchte hai or unhen pataa chalata he ki uske bete ko pyaar ho gaya he. ve bharat lautate hai taaki rajesh or minaa shaadi kar saken.
 deenanath ke ghar main ek samasya he. ve ek sanyukt parivaar hai or haal hi main naukar babu bhaag gaya he. ve ab ek nae naukar kii talash main hai. rajesh ne mahasoos kiya ki uske pita ne deenanath ke saath baithak main gadabadi kii he. isaliye vah raju naam ke ek naukar ke rup main deenanath ke ghar main kaam karata he. us ghar main har kisi ko kuch samasya he, jo raju apane buddhi ke madhyam se hal karata he. ghar main he deenanath ke bade bete vidya nath, deenanath ke duusare bete jivan, chota betaa pappi, badi beti, shanno or choti potee, dimple.
