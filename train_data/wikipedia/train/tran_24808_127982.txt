 humayunnama kii lekhika gulbadan begum thi. is pustak main babar or humayun kii itihaas kaa vanan miltaa he. humayun kii jeevani kaa naam humayunama he jo unki bahan gulbadan begham ne likhi he. isamen humayun ko kaaphi vinamr swabhaav kaa bataaya gaya he or is jeevani ke tareeke se unhone humayun ko krodhit or uksaane kii koshish bhi kii he.
