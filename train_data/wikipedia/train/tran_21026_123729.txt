 garbh kaal yaa hamal avadhi jantu jagat kii maadaa kaa garbh dhaaran se lekar shishu ko janm dene kii avadhi ko kehete hai. yah avadhi har praani ke liye alag-alag hoti he.
 adhikansh prajatiyon ke liye, janm se pehle bhrun kitna badhata he vah garbh kaal kii lambaai nirdhaarit karata he. choti prajatiyon kaa garbh kaal saamaanya rup se bade jaanwaron kii tulana main choti avadhi kaa hota he. udhaaharan ke liye, ek hathi kaa garbh kaal 645 din kaa hota he, jabaki ek billi kaa garbh kaal saamaanya rup se 58-65 din kaa hota he. haalanki, sabhi jatiyon ke liye bhrun kaa badhanaa garbh kaal kii lambaai ko nirdhaarit nahin karata he. jo jatiyaan prajnan kaal main hi prajnan karte hai, saadharantay: saal ke usee samay bachche janate hai jab bhojan kii prachurtaa ho.vibhinn anya kaarak bhi garbh kii avadhi nirdhaarit karne main karyrat ho sakate hai. manushyon main, nar bhrun saamaanya rup se maadaa bhrun se kai din adhik garbh main rahete hai or yadi garbh main judwa yaa adhik bachche hon to garbh kaal kii avadhi ghat sakati he. nasl bhi garbh kaal ko gata badha sakati he. kutton main garbh kaal or kam shaavak paidaa hone ke bich ek sakaaratmak sambandh hota he.
