 sangemaramar sar karakoram parvatamala kii pashchimtam upashrenee, batura muzataag, kaa ek pahaad he. prashasnic rup se yah paak-adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-blatistan kshetra main aata he jisapar bharat apni samprabhutaa kaa daawa karata he.
 parvat ke upree bhaag main ek pili dhari he jiske prabhaav se parvat kaa vah hissaa sangemarmar jaisaa prathit hota he. isi kaaran parvat kaa yah naam padaa.
