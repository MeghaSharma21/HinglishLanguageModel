 norge kuferiket norye, ni-norwegian: Kongeriket Noreg kuferiket nureg) europe mahadweep main sthit ek desh he. isaki rajdhani he oslo . isaki mukhya- or rajbhasha he norwegian bhashaa. norway ek rajshahi he. iske kshetradhicar me scandenevian prayadweep kaa pashchimi hissaa, yaan maayen or swalbard tathaa boove naam ke archetik dweep samooh aate hai.
 norway kaa kul kshetrafal 3 lakh 85 hajaar dau sau baawan varg kilometre he or jansankhya lagbhag pachaas lakh. yah europe me nuntam jansankhya ghanatva vaale desho me dwitiya sthaan pe aata he. desh kii simaae purv me sweden se lagati he or uttar me kuch kshetra kii simaae finland or rus se lagati hai. norge kaa raja harald paanchavaa he or uski pradhanmantri jens stoltemberg he.
 norge main 17 may kaa din rashtriya diwas ke rup main manaaya jaataa he. is din 1814 isvi main norge kaa samvidhan bana tha. desh ke sansad ko storeting kaha jaataa he jiske sadsy har chaar saal baad chune jate hai.
