 udan rahit pakshi vah pakshi hote hai jiname udne kii kshamta kaa abhaav hota he or yah pakshi bajaay udne ke apni chalane yaa tairne kii yogyata par nirbhar hote hai or mana jaataa he ki inkaa vikaas inke udaan main sakasham purvajon se hua he. vartmaan main udan rahit pakshiyon kii lagbhag chalis prajatiyon astitv main hai or inkaa sabase achche gyaat udhaaharan he, shaturamurg, kasowri, emu, riya, kiwi or penguin. kuch log yah maanate hai ki udan rahit pakshiyon kaa vikaas shikariyon ke abhaav main dwipon par hua he.
