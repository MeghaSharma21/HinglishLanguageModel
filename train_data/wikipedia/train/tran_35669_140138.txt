 thallett yaa thallett aster, thelic amla ke aster hai or mukhya rup se abhighatyakon ke rup main istemaal kiye jate hai. inhen mukhya rup se polivinil chloride ko naram karne ke liye istemaal kiya jaataa he. sanyukt rajya america, canada or europea sangh main kai utpaadon se thaleton ko swasthya sambandhi kaarnon ke kaaran charanbaddh tareeke se baahar kiya ja rahaa he. 2010 tak bazaar main thallett abhighatyakon yukt utpaadon kaa varchasv tha; kintu kanoonee praavdhaanon or paryaavaran ke prati bad rahi jaagrukta ke kaaran ab vibhinn utpaadakon ko gaiyr thallett abhighatyakon kaa upyog karne ke liye majaboor hona padaa he.
 wo plastic jisamen inhen milaayaa jaataa he ke saath kisi prakaar kaa koi sahasanyojak bandha na hone ke kaaran thaleton ko aasaani se paryaavaran main mukt kiya ja saktaa he, jaise jaise plastic purana hota he or tuutata he thaleton kaa paryaavaran main hone vaala risaav bhi badhata jaataa he. log aamtaur par thallett ke sampark main aate hai or zyaadaatar americanyon ke mutr main kai thaleton ke metabolites paye gayi hai. kyonki thallett abhighatyak rasaynic rup se pivisi se jude nahin rahete is liye yah ris kar yaa udvashpit hokar aasaani se pani or bhojan yaa vaataavaran main ghul sakate hai or is prakaar koi bhi vyakti inke pratyaksh athwa apratyaksh pradooshan kaa shikaar ban saktaa he.
