 umavia shaasha islam ke mukhiya, yani caliph, san 661 se san 750 tak rahe. pratham chaar caliphaaon ke baad ve satta main aae or iske baad kilaafat vanshanugat ho gai. unke shashan kaal main islamic sena ko sainik safalataa bahut mili or ve uttari africa hote hue spain tak pahunch gaye. isi kaal main muslimon ne madhya asia sahit sindh par bhi adhikaar kar liya tha. multah makka ke rahane vaale umayyon ne apni rajdhani damishq main banaai. san 750 main chale ek parivartan aandolan ke baad abbasi khalifaaon ne inako haraa diya or khud shaasha ban baithe. haalanki umavian vansh main se ek - abd ur rahamaan or uskaa ek unani das - bachakar africa hote hue spain pahunch gaya or cordoba main apni kilaafat sthapit kii jo gyarahavin sadi tak rahi.
 islam dharm main inako sansaarikta ke qareeb or islam ke sandeshon se dur vilasitapurna jivan vyateet karne vaale shaasha ke rup main dekha jaataa he. inke kilaaf chauthe caliph ali ke putr hossain ne yajeed ke annayay ke khilaf aawaj uthayi par unhen ek yuddh main jaan ganvaani padi. ali or hossain ke samarthakon ko shiya sampradaay kaha gaya. ye vansh islam ke itihaas main ek mahatvapurn sthaan rakhata he kyonki yaheen se shiya-sunni matbhed bade the.
