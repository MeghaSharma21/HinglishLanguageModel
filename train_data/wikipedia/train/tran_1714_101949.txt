 dainik hindustan hindi kaa dainik samachar patr he. yah 1932 main shuru hua tha. iskaa udghaatan mahatma gandhi ne kiya tha.
 1942 kaa bharat chodo aandolan chhidane par `hindustan' lagbhag 6 maah tak band rahaa. yah sensorship ke virodh main tha. ek agralekh par 6 hajaar rupee kii jamaanat maangi gai. desh ke swadhin hone tak `hindustan kaa mukhya rashtriya aandolan ko badhaava dena tha. ise mahatma gandhi va congress kaa anuyaayi patr mana jaataa tha. gandhi-subhash patr vyavhaar ko hindustan' se avikal rup se prakashit kiya.
 hindustan' main krantikari yashpal kii kahani kai saptaah tak rochak dar se prakashit hui. rajasthan main rajshahi ke viruddh andolanon ke samachar is patr main pramukhataa se prakashit hote rahe. hyderabad satyagrah kaa purna `hindustan' ne samarthan kiya.
 devdas gandhi ke maargdarshan main is patr ne ucch aadarshon ko apane saath rakhaa or patrakarita kii swasth paramparaayen sthapitki. gandhiji ke prarthana pravachan pan jawaharlal nehru va sardaar vallabh bhai patel ke bhaashan avikal rup se `hindustan' main chhapate rahe. dainik `hindustan' kaa patna se bhi sanskaran prakashit ho rahaa he.
 yah samachar patr uttar bharat main prakashit hota hai. yah dilli, lakhanu, kanpur, varanasi, patna, bhagalpur, ranchi, dehradun tathaa chandigarh se ek saath prakashit hota he.
 isaki pathak sankhya 94 lakh he.
