 aapitas hamaare saura mandal ke chathe grah shani kaa teesraa sab se bada upgrah he. aapitas saura mandal ke saare upgrahon main se gyarahavaan sab se bada upgrah he. isaki khoj 1671 main italy ke khagolashastri jiovanni kisini ne kii thi. aapitas is baat ke liye mashhoor he ke uske ek bhaag kaa rang kaafii halkaa he or dusre bhaag kaa rang bahut hi gadha he. is upgrah ke thik madhya rekha main ek uthi hui chattan dekhi gayi he jo is chaand ke aadhe hisse tak chalati he. aapitas kaa ghanatv kaafii kam he or vaigyanik anumaan lagate hai ke isamen sirf 20% pathar he or baaqi sab pani kii barf he.
 aapitas ko angrezi main "Iapetus" likhaa jaataa he. aapitas pracheen unani dharmik kathaon kaa ek paatr tha.
 aapitas kaa vyas lagbhag 1,490 kimi he. tulana ke liye prithvi ke chandramaa kaa vyas lagbhag 3,470 kimi he, yani aapitas se lagbhag dhai guna. iskaa aakaar ek dhruvon se pichake hue gole kii tarah he or bumadhya rekha main thodaa sa motaa he. isi vajah se kuch khagolashastri iske aakaar ko akhrot jaisaa bataate hai.
 17ve sadi main hi jiovanni kisini ne sahi andaaza laga liya tha ke aapitas kaa ek rukh gora he or ek kaala. aapitas ke gadhe rang vaale kshetra ko ab "kisini rajio" ke naam se bulaayaa jaataa he. mana jaataa he ke jo padaarth is ilaake ko us kaa gadha rang dete hai ve dhoomaketuon yaa anya kisi bahari vastuon ke zariye is upgrah par aa gire the. mana jaataa he ke in padaarthon main carbon ke kuch rasayan or hadrogen cynide maujuud hai.
