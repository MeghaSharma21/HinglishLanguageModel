 hazaron khwaahishen aisi hindi-angreji bhashaa main bani, raajnneetik evam prem aadhaarit bhartiya film hai jiskaa nirdesan tathaa nirmaan sudhir mishra ne varsh 2003 main kiya tha, magar pradarshit 2005 main hui.
 film ke baikadrops main bhartiya apatkalin samay ko darshaya gaya hai, jise film main 1970 ke dashak ke teen mahatvakankshi yuvaaon dwaara bayaan kiya gaya he, jab bharat ke andruni saamaajik evam raajnneetik taur vyapak paimaane par badlaavo rahen the. film kaa sheershak mashhoor urdu shaayar mirza gaalib kii likhi shaayari se liya gaya gaya he.
 film ko 6 maah tak vibhinn 12 film utsavon main pradarshit kiya gaya jinamein turki, esthonia, river tu river, berlin, edinburgh, washington, goa, bite da mango, kamanwealth, india, daalas, evam pacific rim aadi shaamil rahen.
 yah chalchitr ek adarshavaad or vaastaviktaa ke bich ke sangharsh ko prastut karata he. is chalchitr main vikram ek gandhivaadi congress ke netaa kaa putr hote he or siddharth ek nyaayadhish ke putr hote hai. donon ek hi college main padhte he or donon ko gita se prem hot he. siddharth baad main naksalwadi aandolan ko samarthan karne ke liye bihar main chalaa jaataa he. vikram raajnneetik partiyon or vyapaaree sanghathanon kii tikadambaaji main sahyog se aarthik rup se safal hota hai. 
 yah ek hindi cinema ke chalchitr hokar bhi is chalchitr kaa zyaadaatar samvaad angreji bhashaa main he.
