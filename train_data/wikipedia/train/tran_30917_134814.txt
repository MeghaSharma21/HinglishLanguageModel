 jalock kashmir kaa shaasha tha. uskaa ullekh rajtarangini main hua he. jalock kii pahchaan ke vishay main mataiky nahin he. jalock, shaayad koi kushaan shaasha ho jiskaa naam galat rup main prayukt hota aayaa ho. kintu vartmaan sthiti main jalock ke vishay main nishchaypurvak kuch nahin kaha ja saktaa.
 aadhunik vidvan use ashok kaa putr maanate hai. sambhavat: ashok ke rajyakaal main jalock kashmir kaa rajyapal rahaa hoga. ananthar jalock ne swayan ko vahaan kaa swatantra shaasha ghoshit kiya hoga. kalhan use mlecchon kaa sanhaarkartta evam sampurna dharitri kaa vijeta bataate hai. kintu saadharantaya kanyakubj tak ke pradeshon par hi uske akraman aitihaasik jaan padate hai. uttar bharat main uske samrajyavistaar kii seemaa kaa nirdhaaran sambhav nahin he. 
 usne kashmir main varnashram vyavastha sthapit kii. bauddhavadisamoohjit shaiva guru ke prabhaav se vah vijayeshvar evam bhutesh kaa upaask hua hoga. rajtarangini se vidit hota he ki aarambh main vah bauddhavirohi tha, par baad main uski aasthaa buddha dharm main bhi hui or usne kirti ashram vihaar kii sthapana karai.
