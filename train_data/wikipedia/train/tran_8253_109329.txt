 
 seranidaaye haddidaar machhliyon ke perceformez gana kaa ek vistrut kul he. is kul main 65 vanshon ke antargat lagbhag 450 machhlee jatiyaan shreneekrut hai. isamen samudri bas or grupar machhliyaan bhi shaamil hai. seranidaaye kul kii adhikansh machhliyaan chote aakaar kii hai, lekin mahaan grupar vishwa kii sabase badi haddidaar machhliyon main se ek he: yah 2.7 meter tak lambi or 400 kilogram tak baari ho jaati he. seranidaaye kul kii machhliyaan vishvabhar ke ooshnakaribandheey or uposhnkatibandheey sagaron main pai jaati hai.
