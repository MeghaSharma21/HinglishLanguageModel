 swami adbhutanand shriramkrishna ke sanyasi shishyon main se ek hain. unka sanyas purv naam tha rakhturam. bihar ke chhapra jile main unka janm hua. ramakrishna bhakt mandali main ve latu maharaj ke naam se parichit hain.
 adbhutanand kaa janm bihar ke chhapra jila main ek gariv parivaar main hua. paanch varsh kii umr main apane matapita ke dehant ke baad ve anaath ho gayi. karm santhaan ke liye ve kolkata pahunche or ramakrishna ke gruhi bhakt ramachandra dutt ke parivaar main naukar ke rup main kaam karne lage.
 laathu apane maalik ramachandra ke saath 1880 main pehali baar dakshineshwar aae. iske baad ve shriramkrishna ke sevak ban gayi. shriramkrishna ke nikat aanebaale sanyasi shishyon main latu pratham the.
 shriramkrishna ke gale kii beemaari hone ke baad unhen shyampukur or vaad main kashipur laayaa gaya. latu apane guru kii seva ke saath saath sadhana main lage rahete the.
 shriramkrishna ke mrityu ke baad ve sharada devi ke saath tirth darshan karne chale gayi. kolkata lotane ke baad ve sanyas grahan kiye or varahanagar math main rahane lage. 1895 i. main ve bhubaneshwar or puri bhraman karne gayi.swami vivekananda ke dehanth ke baad ve balram mandir main 1912 tak rahen.
 latu maharaj ne apana antim jivan kaashi main vyateet kiya. 28 april 1920 main unka dehanth hua.
