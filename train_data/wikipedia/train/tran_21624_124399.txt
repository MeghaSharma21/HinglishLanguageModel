 pehla samvidhan sanshodhan adhinium, 1951
 is savindhan sansodhan adhinium dwaara anuchhed 15,19,85,87,174,176,314,342,374,376 main sansodhan kiye gaye tathaa do naye anuchhed 31k tathaa 31kha jode gayi or naunvi anusuchee ko bhi savindhan main jode gayi.31k main jameendaari pratha ke unmoolan ko vaidhaniktaa pradaan kii gayi 31kha main naunvi anusuchee main shaamil kiye gaye adhiniyamon kii vaidhinakta ko nyaayalay main chunauti nahi di ja sakati.anuchhed 19 main sansodhan karke lok vyavastha, videshi rajyo se maitri sambandh tathaa aparaadh ke uddipan ke aadhaar par vaak evam abhivyakti kii swatantraaa par roak lagaayi gayi he.anuchhed 15 ko jodakar samaajik tathaa shaikshanik drushti se pichde huye logo ke sambandh main vishesh kanoon nirmaan hetu rajya ko adhikaar diya gaya he.
