 jamaana divaana 1995 main bani hindi bhashaa kii hasya premakahani film he. isko ji pi sippi ne nirmit kiya or ramesh sippi ne iskaa nirdesan kiya. shahrukh khan or ravina tandon mukhya bhoomika main hai or jithendra or shatrughna sinha kramshah unke pita bane hai. nadeem-shravan dwaara sangeet rachit he. yah film asafal rahi thi or ramesh sippi kii antim nirdeshit film he.
 sundar kii dusht chaalon kaa shikaar hone se pehle suraj or lala achche dost the. ab lala kaa maananaa he ki uski patni mar chuki he or suraj iske liye jimmedaar he. ve kattar dushman ban jate hai or giroh yudhon ke madhyam se shahar main araajaktaa paidaa karte hai. sahaayak police ayukta ke paas 2 aapraadhik manovaigyanik, kedy or shalini he. vah lala kii sundar or suruchipurna beti priya ko suraj ke jeevant or utsaahi putr rahul ke kareeb laane kii sajeesh bunate hai, taaki donon pakshon ko ek saath laane kii ummid kii ja sake.
 sabhi geet samir dwaara likhit; sara sangeet nadeem-shravan dwaara rachit.
