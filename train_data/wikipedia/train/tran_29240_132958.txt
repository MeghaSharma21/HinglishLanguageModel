 lava yaa lova dakshinpurvi asia main basane vaala ek samudaay he jo mukhya rup se uttari thailand main rahata he. unki bhashaa bhi "lava" kehalaati he or burma va china ke unnan praant main boli jane vaali blong or vaa bhashaaon se sambandhit ek austro-asian bhashaa he. is samudaay kii do mukhya shakhaayen hai: pashchimi lava log mai hong son praant ke dakshini bhaag main or purvi lava log chiung may praant main rahete hai. thailand ke lopburee praant kaa naam aitihaasik rup se isi samudaay par padaa he.
