 baga, bharat ke goa rajya ke bardez main sthit samudr-tateey kasbaa he. yah kasbaa kaalangute ke nyaayakshetra main he jo do kilometre dakshin main he. baga apane samudratat or baga nadika ke liye lokapriy he or yahan prati varsh hazaron paryatak aate hai.
 baga samudr-tat uttari goa kaa ek lokapriy paryatak sthal he. baga kasbaa sinquarim, candolim se prarambh hokar kaalangute se hokar jane vaale samudr-tat par sthit he jo baga tak sannihit he. is samudr-tat par jhonpadiyon or matsay-grahan naukaaon kii panktiyaan hoti hai, or ucch jowar bhaate ke samay tat sankara ho jaataa he. baga samudr-tat parasailing, jal-kridaaon or dollaphin paribhraman ke liye bhi prasiddh he.
