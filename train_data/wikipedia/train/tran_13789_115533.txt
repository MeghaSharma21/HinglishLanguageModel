 phantasy katha lekhak sergey lucyanenka kaa janm 1968 main hua. unhone alma-ata medical institute main doctorl kii padhai kii or phir maansik rog chikitsa kaa vishesh prashikshan praapt kiya. ve alma-ata se prakashit hone vaali phantasy patrika 'lok' ke sahaayak sampadak rahe. sergey lucyanenka kii pehali phantasy katha 'narushenie' 1987 main alma-ata kii 'zarya' patrika main prakashit hui. pehali pustak 'aatmni son' 1992 main prakashit hui.
 1993 se sergey lucyanenka ne lekhan ko apana mukhya pesha bana liya. sergey lucyanenka ke anusaar unki pramukh rachanaayen hai- 'atomi sapana', 'patjhar ke aagatuk', 'thande tat' or 'spectrum' . 'nachnoy dazor' - sergey lucyanenka kaa behad charchit or lokapriy upanyaas rahaa. phir inhone 'din ke prahari' upanyaas main usee vishay ko aage badhaayaa. lucyanenka kii rachanaaon par rus main bahut se serial bane hai. lekhan kii apni style ko sergey lucyanenka 'krur karravaayiyon kii phantasy' yaa 'raste kii phantasy' kehete hai. 
 sergey lucyanenka kii rachanaayen vishwa kii 20 se adhik bhashaaon main anudit ho chuki hai. inaki pachchis lakh kitaaben prativarsh bikati hai. inaki rachanaaon kaa anuwaad sabase pehle bharat main kiya gaya tha. tab 1991 main 'sputanik kavest' patrika ne inaki kahani 'van main chipa neech dushman' prakashit kii thi. sergey lucyanenka ko bahut se saahitiyik puraskaar mil chuke hai.
