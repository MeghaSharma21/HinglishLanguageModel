 madhu koda jharkhand ke bhootpurv mukhyamantri hai. unhen is navnirmit rajya ke paanchaven mukhyamantri ke rup main 18 sitambar 2006 ko dilaai gayi thi. shri koda ke mantrimandal main aath mantri hai evam ve bharat ke kisi bhi praant main nirdaleey ummidwar ke rup main mukhyamantri banane vaale pehle mukhyamantri hai. 
 madhu koda ke rajnatik jivan kii shuruyaat aal jharkhand stodend union ke ek kaaryakarta ke rup main hui thi baad main ve rashtriya swayamsevak sangh ke bhi sadsy bane. 2000 ke jharkhand vidhansabha ke chunaavon main ve bhaajapa ummidwar ke rup main jagannathpur vidhansabha seat se chaynit hue. babulal marandi ke netritv main banane vaali sarkaar main ve panchayat raaj mantri bane or baad main ve 2003 main arjuna munda kii sarkaar banane ke baad bhi isi pad par kabiz rahi. 
 2005 kii vidhansabha chunaavon main unhen bhaajapa dwaara ummidwar banane se manaa kar diya gaya. iske baad ve ek nirdaleey ke rup main usee vidhaan sabha seat se chune gayi. khandit janadesh ke kaaran ve bhaajapa ke netritv main bananevaali arjuna munda kii sarkaar kaa unhone baahar se samarthan kiya or unhen khan evam bhuvaigyanik maamalon kaa mantri banaaya gaya. 
 sitambar 2006 main shri koda or anya teen nirdaleey vidhayakon ne shri munda kii sarkaar se apana samarthan waapas le liya jisase sarkaar alpamat main aa gayi. baad main vipaksh sanyukt pragatisheel gathbandhan ne unhen mukhyamantri ke rup main sweekaar kar apni sarkaar banaayi jisamen jhamumo, rajd, united goans democratic party, rashtravaadi congress party, farward blaak, 3 nirdaleey vidhayak shaamil the. jisamen congress baahar se samarthan kar rahi he.
