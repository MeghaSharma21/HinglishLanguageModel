 himdhara kisi himchadar ke aise kshetra ko kehete hai jisamen barf apane aasapaas kii barf se adhik tezi se bah rahi ho. yah ek vishesh prakaar kii himani hoti he. himdharaayen antarctic main bahut dikhti hai jahaan ve puri barf kaa 10% pratishat he. ve 50 kimi chaudee, 2 kimi gahari or sainkadon milon tak chalane vaali ho sakati hai. samajha jaataa he ki antarctic kii himchaadaron se sagar main bah jaani vaali adhikansh barf inhen himdharaon main bahati he.
