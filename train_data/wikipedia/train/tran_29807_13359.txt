 anglo-francas di petait veneri kutto kii ek madhya aakaar kii nasal he jise ke shikaar ke liye jhund main centahound ke tarah istemaal kiya jataa he. yah un anglo french hound nasalo main se ek he jinhen kii french centahound or english foxound ke milan se prajnan karaayaa gaya tha. inke naam petait veneri kaa yah matlab nahin ke yah aakar main nasal ke taur pe chote hote par iskaa matlab he ki inhen chote shikaar aadi khelo main istemaal kiya jaataa he.
