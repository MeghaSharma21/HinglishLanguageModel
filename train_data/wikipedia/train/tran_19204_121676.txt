 kakadi zaayad kii ek pramukh fasal he. isko sanskrit main 'karkati' tathaa marbari bhashaa main walam kakri kaha jaataa he. yah "kukurbetacy" vansh ke antargat aati he. 
 vishwaas kiya jaataa he ki kakadi kii utpatti bharat se hui. isaki kheti kii reeti bilkul taroi ke samaan he, keval uske bone ke samay main antar he. yadi bhoomi purvi jilon main ho, jahaan sheet ritu adhik kadi nahin hoti, to oktober ke madhya main beej boe ja sakate hai, nahin to ise janvari main bona chahie. aise sthanon main jahaan sardi adhik padati hai, ise farvari or march ke maheenon main lagaana chahie. isaki fasal balui dumat bhoomiyon se acchhee hoti he. is fasal kii sinchai saptaah main do baar kerani chahie. kakadi main sabase acchhee sugandh garam shushk jalwaayu main aati he. isamen do mukhya jatiyaan hoti hai - ek main halake hare rang ke fal hote hai tathaa duusari main gahare hare rang ke. inamen pehali ko hi log pasand karte hai. graahakon kii pasand ke anusaar falon kii chunaai tarunavastha main athwa iske baad kerani chahie. isaki madhya upaj lagbhag 75 man prati acre he.
