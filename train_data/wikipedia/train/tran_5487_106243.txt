 28 april, 2007 ke anusarrot: 
 sanjay manjrekar ek purv bhartiya cricket khilaadi he. manjrekar apane samay main bhartiya team ke liye test tathaa ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket match khelaa karte the.
 sanjay vijay manjrekar kaa janm 16 july 1965 ko mangalore main hua. sanjay manjrekar 1952 se 1965 ke bich bharat ke liye 55 test match khilne vaale vijay manjrekar ke bete hai.
