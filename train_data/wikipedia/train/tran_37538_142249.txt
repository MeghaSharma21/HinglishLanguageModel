 torii kyonobu japan kaa rangamanchi chitrakaar. 
 uskaa janm tokio main hua. isane rangamanchi chitrakaaron kii ek shaleen parampara kaa prarambh kiya. iskaa guru bhi rangamanchi sinbordon kaa chitrakaar tha. kyonobu ne aarambh main granth chithran kaa kaam kiya kintu sheeghra hi vah abhinaytachitron ke nirmaan nishnaat mana jane laga. us kaal main abhinaytaon ke chitra banane kii reeti chal padi thi jise kyonobu ne apni praveenta dwaara sampann kiya. mard abhinayta hi nari paatro ke abhinay bhi karte the. kyonobu donon ke vividh prakaaron kii ascharyajanak safalataa se abhivyakti karata tha. unke vastron par vah cheri kii kaliyon, vibhinn kusumon, vijanon, pakshiyon aadi kaa adbhut roopayan karata tha. uske chitrafalak kaa adhikansh bhaag pradhan aalekhya, abhinayta, abhinetri kii akruti se hi bharaa hota, bas pratikat: ek vriksh athwa uski tahani us akruti ke saath khachit hoti. rangamanchi chitron ke atirikt shrungpradhan chitron ke album bhi is chitrakaar ne tatkaaleen parampara main prastut kiye.
