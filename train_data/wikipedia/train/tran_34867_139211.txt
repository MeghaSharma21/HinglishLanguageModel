 jansankhya 2011 ke anusaar kul aabaadi main 3.2% log shahari ilaakon main rahete hai jabaki gramin ilaakon main 96.8% log rahete hai. shahari kshetron main ausat sakshartaa dar 77.6% he jabaki gramin kshetron main yah 61.1% he. iske alaava sapotara tehsil main shahari kshetron kaa ling anupaat 849 he jabaki gramin kshetron main 852 he.
 sapotara tehsil main 0-6 saal kii umr ke bacchon kii jansankhya 33690 he jo ki kul aabaadi kaa 16% he. 18149 purush bachche or 15541 mahila bachche 0-6 saal ke bich hai. is prakaar 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar sapotara tehsil kaa bal ling anupaat 856 he jo sapotara tehsil kii ausat ling anupaat se adhik he.
 sapotara tehsil kii kul sakshartaa dar 61.66% he. sapotara tehsil main purush sakshartaa dar 63.91% he or mahila sakshartaa dar 37.37% he.

 ramathara kila sapotara 
 ye kila rajasthan ke sapotara main sthit he. ye kila bharatpur or savai madhopur main ranthambhore tiger rigerv main keoladev ghana pakshi abhayaarany ke bich sthit he. kelaa devi vanyajeev abhayaarany is kile se maatr 15 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he yah kila jhil ke kinare or gaanv ke manoram drishy ke liye prasidd he.
 ye kila 1645 main raja bhopal ko unke pita kii taraf se jaagir main mila tha jo yahan ke raja the. us samay thakur brijendra raaj paal or unke parivaar dwaara is kile kaa prabandhan kiya jaataa tha.
 is kile ke andar do mandir hai jisamen se ek bhagwaan ganesh to doosraa bhagwaan shiv ko samarpit he. yahan sthit shiv mandir main bhagwaan shiv kii ek sangemarmar se bani vishaal murti he jo 18 veen shatabdi kii vaastukala ko darshaati he. is kile kii yatra karne par yahan aane vaale paryatak sundar kheton, daang ke pathaar kalisil jhil ke manoram drishyon ko bhi dekh sakate hai. bharatpur pakshi abhayaarany yahan se najadik hone ke kaaran yahan aap sudur desh se udhakar aane vaali chidiyon ko bhi dekh sakate hai.
 
 
 
 amargarh kila sapotara 
 amargarh kila 250 saal purana kila he jo yahan amargarh gaanv ke paas sthit ek pahaad kii chotii par sthit he. is kile kaa naam raja amar mel ke naam par rakhaa gaya jinhone is kile kaa nirmaan karaayaa tha. dhan ke khet or prachur maatra main hariyaali is kile kii shobhaa main chaar chaand lagaati hai. chaaron taraf se jangalon or pahaado se ghira hone ke kaaran yahan aane vaale paryatak ko ek behtareen drishy dekhne ko miltaa he.
 sapotara ke aasapaas ke sabase najdiki railway stesan
 sapotara ke nikatatam railway stesan gangapore shahar he jo lagbhag 19.5 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. nimnlikhit talika main anya railway stesan se isaki duuri dikhaai detii he.
 sapotara kaa sabase nikatatam hawaii adda 
 
 2011 ke survey ke anusaar sapotara tehsil main kul aabaadi main 97,030 kaary gatividhiyaan main lage hue the. 68.9% shramik apane kaam ko mukhya kaary ke rup main bataate hai jabaki 31.1% 6 maheene se kam samay ke liye aajeevika pradaan karne vaali seemaant gatividhi main shaamil the. mukhya kaary main lage 97,030 shramikon main, 47,957 kisaan the jabaki 5,360 krishi majdoor the.
 sapotara main dilli-mumbai rail maarg par narayanpur tatwaaraa pramukh stesan hai. narayanpur tatwaaraa par kai trainen uplabdh hai jo ko uttar bharat ke kai mahatvapurn sthanon or bharat ke baaki hisson se sapotara ko jodatii hai. narayanpur tatwaaraa stesan mumbai or dilli ke bich pramukh vidyutikrut railway maarg par padataa he. narayanpur tatwaaraa railway stesan ke alaava, anya railway steshnon ke nikat nikatataa main gangapore city railway stesan, nimoda railway stesan or lalpur umari railway stesan hai.
 shahar se nai dilli, bombai, lakhanu, kanpur, jamuttvi, amritsar, ludhiana, jalandhar, haridwar, dehradun, jaipur, chandigarh, kalka or shri mata vaishno devi katra ke liye trainen hai.
