 agni ke dwaara devataaon ko pahunchane vaale som ke liye havan kunda kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. havan kunda ko vedi ke anusaar banaaya jaataa he. ek vedi kaa havan kunda rojaanaa ke bhojan ko pakaane ke kaam main liya jaataa he. dwi vedi kaa havan kunda saadhaaran brahman ke dwaara apni dainik havnaatmak kriya ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he. trivedic havan kunda ko sat raj or tam tino prakaar kii aakankshaon kii purti ke liye kiya jaataa he. chaturvedic havan kunda ko dharm arth kaam or moksh kii prapti ke nimitt prayog kiya jaataa he. havan kunda main vedi kaa aakaar vargakar hota he or sidhiyon ke rup main kaary yojana ke anusaar chaudai or oonchai main banaaya jaataa he.
