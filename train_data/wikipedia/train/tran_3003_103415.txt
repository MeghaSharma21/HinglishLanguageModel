 soni sori chhattisgarh ke dantewada jile main sthit jabeli gaanv kii ek adivasee vidhyalay shikshika hai.san 2011 main police dwaara yah iljaam lagaaya gaya ki ve naksaliyon ko suchanaaen uplabdh karwaati hai. soni sori ko dilli main giraftaar kar liya gaya or chhattisgarh police ko saunp diya gaya. court main peshi se pehle inhen do din kii police hiraasat main rakhaa gaya, jahaan chhattisgarh police dwaara unhen nanga kiya gaya or bijli ke jatke lagaae gaye. third degree tarchar kii vajah se court main pehle din peshi ke dauraan ve chal bhi nahin paa raheen thim. baad main supreme court dwaara swatantra jaanch karwaae jane par unke yonang main teen pathar paae gaye. soni sori ko court main peshi ke baad jail bhej diya gaya. dhai saal kii yatnapurna kaid ke baad supreme court ne unhen sthai jamaanat pradaan kar di. chhattisgarh police dwaara lagaae gaye aath aaropon main saat jhuthe nikle jabaki ek main unhen jamaanat mil gai.
 vartmaan main soni sori chhattisgarh ke adivaasiyon par ho rahe krur hamalon kaa virodh kar adivaasiyon ko nyaay dilaane kii disha main kaam kar rahi hai. bastar main farji muthbhedon, adivaasiyon mahilaon ke saath surakshakarmiyon dwaara balaatkaar samet tamaam adivasee utpeeda ke muddon ko soni sori ne rashtriya str par uthaaya he, jisase vah chhattisgarh police or prashasan ke nishane par hai.
 pichley do dashakon se bastar ke adivasee, police or naksaliyon ke bich pis rahe hai. kabhi police dwaara to kabhi naksaliyon dwaara adivaasiyon ko maarane kii khabaren aksar prakaash main aati rahati hai. bhay ke kaaran hajaaro adivasee bastar chhodie par majaboor ho gaye hai. bastar ek yudhakshetra ban gaya he, jahaan police or naksaliyon kii goliyaan chalati hai or marataa he sirf adivasee.
 bastar main adivaasiyon ke liye jo bhi kuch karata he, vah police or prashasan ke nishane par aa jaataa he. shaayad yaheen kaaran he ki soni sori bhi nishane par hai.
