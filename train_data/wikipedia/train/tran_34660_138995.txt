 lutishia ek madhyam aakaar kaa kshudragrah he. yah mangal or brihaspati grahon ke bich mukhya kshudragrah patte main surya kii parikrama karata he. yah dheledaar kshudragrah lagbhag 96 kimi vyas kaa he. yah purnatah golakaar nahin he, lutishia kaa vyas ek disha main 132 kimi he, jabaki anya disha main keval 76 kimi he. europea antariksh yaan roseta july 2010 main lutishia ke paas se gujara or kshudragrah ko sabase pehle achche se dekha.
