 poonch zila azaad kashmir kaa ek zila he. paak-adhikrut kashmir do bhaagon main vibhajit he: azaad kashmir or gilgit-blatistan. poonch zila inamen se azaad kashmir naamak hisse main aata he. iskaa pramukh nagar rawala kot he. bharat ise apana bhaag maanata he or bharat ke jammu va kashmir rajya main bhi ek poonch zila he. paak-adhikrut kashmir ke poonch kshetra main sudhan or awan samudaay baari sankhya main hai.
