 vairengte bharat ke mizoram rajya ke kolasib jile main sthit ek kasbaa he. yah mizoram kii rajdhani izol se 130 kimi kii duuri par sthit he.
 bharat kii janaganana 2011 ke anusaar vairengte kii jansankhya 10,544 he jisame 5649 purush or 4905 mahilaayein hai.
 yahaan 6 varsh se kam umr ke bacchon kii sankhya 1441 he, jo ki 13.65% jansankhya kaa nirmaan karte hai. linganupaat dar 868 he jo ki mizoram rajya ke ausat 976 se kam he. bacchon main linganupaat dar 1013 he jo ki rajya kii dar se 970 se kaaphi adhik he. sakshartaa dar 94.73% he jo ki rajya dar 91.33% se adhik he. purush sakshartaa dar 95.24% he tathaa mahila sakshartaa dar 94.14% he. 
 2011 janaganana ke anusaar, yahan par 83.51% isaai, 10.44% hindu, 5.44% muslim tathaa 0.61% anya hai, jisame mukhyatah buddha or sikh hai.
 pratighaat vidroh tathaa jangal sangharsh vidhyalay vairengte, mizoram sthit bhartiya sena ke ek prashikshan evam shodh santhaan he jahaan aparamparaagat yuddh, visheshtah pratighaat vidroh tathaa gorilla sangharsh kaa prashikshan diya jaataa he.
