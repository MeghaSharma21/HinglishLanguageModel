 pulikat jhil tamilnadu ke tat par 60 kilometre lambi or 5 se 15 kilometre chaudee ek jhil he. yah ek chhichhli anup he. is jhil kii ausat gaharaai 18 meter he. yah samudr se baaluu kii bhitti dwaara alag hone se bani he.
 satish dhawan antariksh kendra isi jhil ke nikat shri harikota dweep par sthit haishree harikota dweep main avsaadee nikshep ke kai str milte hai jinhen samudri laharon ne bichaaya he.
 pulikat jhil 13.33° se 13.66° N or 80.23° to 80.25°E, ke bich sthit he jabaki agar iske suukh chuke hisson ko bhi shaamil kiya jaay to uttar main iskaa vistaar 14.0°N. tak he. iskaa lagbhag 84% hissaa andhra pradesh main or 16% hissaa tamilnadu rajya main aata he. 
 jhil kaa kshetrafal jowar ke dauraan 450 varg kilometre or bhaate ke dauraan 250 varg kilometre ke aasapaas rahata he. isaki kul lambaai 60 kilometre or chaudaai lagbhag 0.2 kilometre se 17.5 kilometre ke bich he.
 jalwaayu kii drushti se yah jhil ushnkaribandheey monsooni jalwaayu ke antargat aati he jahaan taapmaana lagbhag 15 °se. se 45 °se. ke madhya rahata he.
 haal main hue kuch surveykshanon main pulikat jhil ke sankatagrast hone kaa daawa kiya gaya he or iske liye maanviya gatividhiyon ko jimmevaar tehraaya gaya he. ek surveykshan ke mutabik is jhil kaa kul satahi kshetrafal pehle 450 varg feet hua karata tha jo ab ghatakar maatr 350 varg feet hai rah gaya he. isse is jhil ke bashindon or yahan aane vaale hajaaron pravasi pakshiyon ke astitv par sankat utpann ho gaya he. yahi nahin is jhil kii gaharaai main kami or lavanta main vruddhi bhi darj kii gayi he.
