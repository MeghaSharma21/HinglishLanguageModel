 dilli sultanate ke khilji vansh kaa shaasha. qutub shah ne san 1316 se 1320 tak dilli main shashan kiya. khusro khan naamak iske ek vishwaas-paatr ne isaki hatya karke sinhasan par kabja kiya. qutubuddin mubaaraq kilji 19 april 1316 i. ko dilli ke singhaasana par baitha tha. usne 1316 i. se 1320 i. tak rajya kiya. gaddi par baithne ke baad hi usne devgiri ke raja harpal dev par chadhaai kii or yuddh main use parrast kiya tathaa baad main use bandi banaakar uski khaal udhadva di. apane shashan kaal main usne gujarat ke vidroh kaa bhi daman kiya tha. apni in vijayon ke kaaran hi uskaa dimag phir gaya. vah apana samay sura tathaa sundariyon main bitaane laga.uski in vijayon ke atirikt anya kisi bhi vijay kaa varnan nahin miltaa he. qutubuddin mubaaraq kilji ne apane sainikon ko chah maah kaa agrim vetan diya tha. viddhanon evam mahatvapurn vyaktiyon kii chhini gayi jaagiren unhen waapas kar deen. allaudin kilji kii kathor dand vyavastha evam bazaar niyantran aadi vyavastha ko usne samaapt kar diya tha. qutubuddin mubaaraq kilji ko nagn stri-purushon kii sangat pasand thi. apni isi sangat ke kaaran kabhi-kabhi vah rajya darbar main stri kaa vastra pahan kar aa jaataa tha. 'barani' ke anusaar mubarak kabhi-kabhi nagn hokar darbariyon ke bich dauda karata tha. usne ‘al imam’, ‘ul imam’ evam ‘kilaafat-ullah’ kii oopaadhiyaan dhaaran kii thim. usne kilaafat ke prati bhakthi ko hataakar apane ko ‘islam dharm kaa sarvocch pradhan’ or ‘swarna tathaa prithvi ke adhipati kaa 'caliph ghoshit kiya tha. saath hi usne ‘alvasik villah’ kii dharm kii pradhan oopaadhi bhi dhaaran dhaaran kii thi.
 mubaaraq ke vazeer nasiruddin khusron khan ne 15 april 1320 i. ko uski hatya kar swayan dilli kii gaddi ko hathiyaa liya.
