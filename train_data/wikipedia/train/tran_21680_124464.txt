 uttar africa africa ke mahadweep kaa uttari bhaag he. iske or up-sahaara africa ke darmayaan sahaara kaa vishaal registan aata he jo uttar africa or baaqi africa main itnaa bada faaslaa bana detaa he ke uttar africa kii jatiyaan, bhashaayen, reeti-rivaaj, mausam or jaanavar baaqi africa ke kaafii bhinn hai. sanyukt raashtra kii paribhaasha ke anusaar uttar africa main saat raashtra aate hai: morocco, algeria, tunishia, libya, misr, sudan or pashchimi sahaara. is bhubhag main kuch chote-se spain-niyantrit uttar africa kshetra bhi aate hai. uttar africa main arabi, barbari bhashaayen or kuch anya bhashaayen boli jaati hai. morocco, algeria, tunishia, libya or mauritania ke samooh ko "magarib" bhi kaha jaataa he, jiskaa arth arabi main "pashchimi" he.
 is ilaake ke uttari bhaag main atlas parvat shrunkhlaa morocco, uttari algeria or tunishia main sthit hai. in pahaado main kahin-kahin van or jhaad hai or anya sthanon par zamin patthariilii or banjar he. inamen kahin-kahin sardiyon ke mausam main barf bhi padati he or chote-mote nadi-naale pravaah karte hai. thodaa sa dakshin main or purv kii disha main yah pahaad khatm ho jate hai or ghaas or jhaad ke khule faile hue maidan hai, jo or dakshin kii or jaakar sahaara registan kii ret main samaapt ho jate hai. uttari africa kaa 75% bhaag yahi sahaara registan he. sahaara kaa bahut sa hissaa darasal ek pathaar par sthit he or isamen bahut si chattaane or sookhe pahaad sthit hai. purv kii or misr main nil nadi kii ghati main kaafii hariyaali he or misr kii adhikansh aabaadi isi nadi ke paas rahati he. sahaara ke registan main bhi kahin-kahin par nachlistaan milte hai. puure uttari africa main samudr ke kinare kaa ilaaka thodaa-bahut hariyaala he or vahaan shahar-qasbe based hue hai.
