 barfi! ne vibhinn shreniyon main namakan or puraskaar praapt kiye jo pramukhtah swa-abhigyaan se lekar iske chhayankan, nirdesan, patakatha, sangeet or paatr abhinay tak he. film 85wein academy awards ke liye videshi bhashaa film ke liye bharat kii aadhikarik pravishti ke rup main chaynit hui. film ko 58wein filmafere awards ke liye teraa namakan praapt kiye jinamein se sarvshereshth film, kapur ko sarvshereshth abhinayta, preetam ko sarvshereshth sangeet nirdeshak sahit 7 main jeet darj kii. 'barfi!' ne 19wein screen awards main taees namakan batore jinamein kapur ko sarvshereshth abhinayta, basu ko sarvshereshth nirdeshak or kapur-chopra ko jodi nambar van sahit nau puraskaar jite. 14 wein ji sine awards main, 'barfi!' ko nau namakan mili jinamein se sarvshereshth film, basu ko sarvshereshth nirdeshak or chopra ko sarvshereshth abhinetri sahit aath uda liye.
