 aagaa khan pratham · aagaa khan dwitiyaaga khan tritiy · aagaa khan chaturth
 ali shah, aagaa khan dwitiya, mul naam ali shah,47waan imam, shiyaon ke nijri ismaili mat ke aadhyatmik netaa. ye aagaa khan pratham ke jyeshtha putr the, shiyaon ke nijaari ismaili sampradaay ke imam yaa aadhyatmik netaa ke rup main pita ke uttaradhikari the. imam ke apane chote se kaarykaal main unhone apane samudaay kii dasha sudhaarney ke prayaas kiye.
