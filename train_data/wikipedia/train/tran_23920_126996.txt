 pashu-rati athwa pashu-anurakti jaanwaron par yon nirdhaaran ko samahit karne vaali apkaamuktha ko kaha jaataa he. manushya or gaiyr manushya jaanwaron ke madhya hone vaale antar-prajateey yon gatividhi ko pashugaman kehete hai. in shabdon ko saamanytah ek duusare ke sthaan par prayukt kiya jaataa he lekin kuch shodhakarta inamen aakarshan or kaary ke rup main vibhed karte hai.
 yadhyapi vibhinn deshon main pashugaman vaidhanik rup se avaidh he tathaa pashugaman ko jaanwaron ke saath durvyavhaar niyamon ke antargat avaidh mana jaataa he tathaa ye aparaadh kii shrenee main aata he.
