 cola ati-gahan vedhan chhidr, cola prayadweep par soviet sangh kii ek vaigyanik vedhan pariyojna ke antargat prithvi kii parpati par kiya gaya us samay kaa sabase gaharaa vedhan tha. pariyojna kii shuruyaat 24 may 1970 ko uralmaash-4E tathaa baad main uralmaash-15000 shrinkhalaa kii vedhan rigon kaa upyog karte hue kii gayi. ek kendriya chhed se shuru karke uske chaaron ore uski shakhaon ke rup main kai chhidr vedhe gayi. 1989 main sabase gahare chhed esji-3 kii gaharaai 12262 meter  tak pahunch gayi or yah prithvi par maanav dwaara vedhit ab tak kaa sabase gaharaa chhidr tha. 
 isaki yah khyaati agle do dashak tak bani rahi jab tak ki 2008 main katar ke al shaheen telkup jisaki gaharaai 12,289 meter thi or phir 2011 main roosi dweep sakhalin main apatati vedhan dwaara nirmit 12,345 meter gaharaai ke sakhalin-I odoptu o pi-11 coop ne ise pachhad nahin diya.
