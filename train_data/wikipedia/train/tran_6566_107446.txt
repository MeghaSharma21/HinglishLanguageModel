 nasika yaa naas kuch praaniyon kii naak ke ant main sharir se baahar khulne vaali do naliyon main se ek ko kehete hai. pakshiyon or stanadhariyon main nasikaon main unhen dhaancha pradaan karne vaali haddiyaan yaa upaasthiyaan hoti hai, or nasikaaye andar liye jane vaale shwaas ko garam karti hai or baahar jane vaale shwaas se nami hataakar uskaa jal sharir se khoe jane se rokatii hai. machhliyaan apane naak se shwaas nahin letin, haalanki unamem bhi do chote chhidr hote hai jinka prayog soonghney ke liye kiya jaataa he.
