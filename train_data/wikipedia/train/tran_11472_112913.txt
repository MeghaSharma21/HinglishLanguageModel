 photodod ek ardhchaalak yukti he jo ko vidyut urja main badalati he. duusare shabdon main, yadi photodod kisi lod se juda he or is par prakaash aaptit hota he, to isamen vidyut dhara bahane lagati he. saura cell bhi ek photodod he jiskaa upyog prakaash se vidyut urja praapt karne ke liye kiya jaataa he. photodod kaa upyog prakaash sansuchak kii tarah bhi kiya ja saktaa he.
