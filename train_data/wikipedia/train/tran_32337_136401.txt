 osman tariq khwaaja  pakistani ek australian cricket khilaadi he.
 khwaja ko 2010-11main ashes shrinkhalaa main 17 sadasyeey team main chunaa gaya tha. shrinkhalaa ke tisare match main riki ponting ke anguli main chot lagane ke kaaran khwaaja kaa naam aage aayaa tha. 03janvari 2011ko ye australia ke 419wein khilaadi bane the. inke alaava osman khwaja pehle aise khilaadi he jo pakistan ke evam muslim he jo australia ke liye khelte hai. 31 janvari 2016 ko inhone bharat ke khilaaf inhone apane tinti-tinti main apane karier kii shuruyaat kii.
