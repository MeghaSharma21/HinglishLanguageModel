 fusion sangeet, sangeet kii do yaa do se adhik shaileyon ko milakar praapt huya sangeet he. fusion angreji bhashaa kaa shabd he jiskaa arth hota he vilay yaa mail, chunki is prakaar ke sangeet kaa pradurbhaav pashchimi deshon main hua or vahaan se yah bharat aayaa isiilie hindi main bhi ise iske angreji naam arthath fusion se janaa jaataa he. fusion style ke sangeet kii mukhya visheshtaon main vaadan ke samay main isaki gati or taal main hua badlaav or kabhi kabhi lambey samay tak chalane vaale sangeet ke wo chote chote ansh hai jiname se prayek kii apni apni gati or taal hoti he. pashchim main "fusion" shabd ko yadi akele istemaal kiya jaae to iskaa arth aksar jaiz fusion hota he.
