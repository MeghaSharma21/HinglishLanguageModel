 kashipur bharat ke uttrakhand rajya ke udham singh nagar janapad main sthit ek mahatvapurn puranic evam audyogic shahar he. varsh 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar is nagar kii kul jansankhya 1,21,623 he, jabaki kashipur tehsil kii kul jansankhya 2,83,136 he. is prakaar, jansankhya kii drushti se kashipur kumaun main teesraa or uttrakhand main chatha sabase bada nagar he. udham singh nagar janapad ke pashchimi bhaag main sthit yah nagar bharat kii rajdhani, nai dilli se lagbhag 240 kilometre dur uttar-purv main, or uttrakhand kii antarim rajdhani, dehradun se lagbhag 200 kilometre dakshin-purv main sthit he.
