 upyukt takneekee, samuchit takanic, madhyamik takneekee yaa samyak takanic us takneekee ko kehete hai jo us parivesh ke paryaavaran, samskruti or aarthik sthiti ke anukool ho, jahaan vah prayog kii jaani he. is arth main samuchit takanic ke liye kam sansadhano kii jarurat padati he; isko apnaane kaa kharch kam aata he tathaa iskaa paryaavaran par dushprabhav kam hota he. 
 british arthshaastri fritz sumekar' upyukt takneekee kii avadharana ke praneta hai. unki pustak small ij butiphul - iknamiks aij iff pipal matterd main takanic or uske doorgaami prabhaavon par gambheer vichaar kiya gaya he.
