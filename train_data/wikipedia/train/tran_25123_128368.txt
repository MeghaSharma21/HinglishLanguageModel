 pramod ranjan choudhary ek bengali krantikari the. unhone angrez shashan ke viruddh awaz uthaai thi.
 pramod kaa janm chittagong ke kelishhar main hua jo ab bangladesh main he. unke pita kaa naam shri ishan chandra choudhary tha. 1920 main shiksha ke dauraan vah anushilan samiti se jud gaye jo ki ek krantikari sanghathan ke rup main prasiddh he. 1921 main vah asahyog aandolan se bhi jud gaye.
 1925 main choudhary ko dakshineshwar bam cond main sandigdh ke rup main pakadaa gaya tha or kadi sazaa sunai gai. alipur jail main jahaan vah band the, choudhary ne may 28, 1927 ko bhoopendra nath chatarji ko maar daalaa jo police ke dipti kamisionar the. isi ke kaaran use sitambar 28, 1927 ko phaansi de di gai.
