 britani rajasatta ke adhinsth jo ardh-swatantra sattayen thim unhen swytt upnivesh yaa dominian  kaha jaataa tha. ye britani samrajya ke bhaag the. sabase pehla dominian canadian confedreshan tha jiskaa nirmaan 1867 main kiya gaya tha. canada, austrailia, newjiland, newfoundland, dakshin africa, or irish free state aadi dominian hai. 1940 ke dashak main bhartiya upmahadweep bhi dominian the. 1926 kaa balfor ghoshnaa ke anusaar dominian kii yah paribhaasha thi- 'britani samrajya ke adheen swytt samudaay' . 1931 kaa westoministar vidhaan ne dominiyanon ke purna vidhaayi swatantry kii bhi ghoshnaa kii. 
 kintu iske purv kaal main dominian kaa arth bhinn tha. 1535 se 1801 tak wales kii rajnatik sthiti ko bhi 'dominian' kaha gaya he. isi tarah 1686 se 1689 ke bich new england ko bhi dominian kaha gaya tha.
