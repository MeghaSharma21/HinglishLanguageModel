 pandurang shastri athawale, bharat ke darshanik, aadhyatmik guru tathaa samaaj sudhaarak the. unako praaia: dadaji ke naam se janaa jaataa he jiskaa marathi main arth 'bade bhai sahab' hota he. unhone san 1954 main swadhyay aandolan chalaaya or swadhyay parivaar kii sthapana kii. swadhyay aandolan shrimadbhagavadgita par aadhaarit aatm-gyaan kaa andolan he jo bharat ke ek lakh se adhik gaavon main failaa hua he or iske koi lakho sadsy hai. dadaji gita evam upanishdon par apane pravachan ke liye prasiddh the. 
 unhen san 1997 main dharm ke kshetra main unnati ke liye templton puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. san 1999 main unhen saamudayik netritv ke liye maghasse puraskaar se bhi sammanit kiya gaya. usee varsh bharat sarkaar ne unhen padmavibhushan se sammanit kiya.
