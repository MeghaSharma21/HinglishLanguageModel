 surah al kaaria
 surah al kaaria makka main nazil hui. yah quran kii 101 number kii sur: he, lekin nazil hone ke hisaab se iskaa number 30 he. al kaaria kaa matlab "aapadaa" yaa "qayamat" he . kaaria shabd kaar se bana he jiskaa matlab he "prahaar" yaa "maarna" yani kaaria matlab ek ke baad ek prahaar se aane vaali awaz, jise is sur: main khadkhadaane vaali awaz kaha gaya he . is surah main logon ko bahut badi aapadaa yani qayamat ke din ke bare main bataaya gaya he. saath hi is baat ke bare main bhi aagaah kiya gaya he ki is din sirf nek amal karne vaale log hi is aapadaa se batch paayenge.
 surah al kaaria kaa arth 
 بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
 shuru karata hu allaah ke naam se jo bahut zyada meharbaan or nihaayat raham karne vaala he
 الْقَارِعَةُ
 101:1 khadkhadaane vaali 
 مَا الْقَارِعَةُ
 101:2 khadkhadaane vaali kya he?
 وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ مَا الْقَارِعَةُ
 101:3 or tuune kya janaa khadkhadaane vaali kya he?
 يَوْمَ يَكُونُ النَّاسُ كَالْفَرَاشِ الْمَبْثُوثِ
 101:4 jis din log aise honge, jaise bikhare hue patange 
 وَتَكُونُ الْجِبَالُ كَالْعِهْنِ الْمَنفُوشِ
 101:5 or pahaad aise ho jaayenge jaise ki dhuni hui rang birangi oon 
 فَأَمَّا مَن ثَقُلَتْ مَوَازِينُهُ
 101:6 to jiske aamaal ke vazan baari niklenge
 فَهُوَ فِي عِيشَةٍ رَّاضِيَةٍ
 101:7 wo dil pasand aish main hoga 
 وَأَمَّا مَنْ خَفَّتْ مَوَازِينُهُ
 101:8 or jis ke vazan halke niklenge
 فَأُمُّهُ هَاوِيَةٌ
 101:9 uskaa thikaana to havia he
 وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ مَا هِيَهْ
 101:10 or tum kya samajhate ho ki yah havia kya he 
 نَارٌ حَامِيَةٌ
 101:11 ye dahakati hui aag he
