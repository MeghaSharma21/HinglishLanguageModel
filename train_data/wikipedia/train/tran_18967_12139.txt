 kshamaavni yaa "kshamaa diwas" jain dharm ke anuyaayiyon dwaara manaaya jane vaala ek parv he. digambar ise aswin krishna maas kii ekam ko manaate hai. shwetambar ise apane 8 divasiy paryushan parv ke ant main manaate he. is parv par sabase apane bhuulon kii kshamaa yachana kii jaati he. ise kshamavani, kshamavani or kshamaa parv bhi kehete he.
 is din jain sabhi jeevon se kshamaa maangate he or kshamaa karte hai.
