 ramswaroop chaturvedi hindi sahitya ke un samikshakon main se the jo mukhyatah bhashaa kii srujanaatmakataa ko kendra main rakhkar samiksha karm main pravrutt hue the.
 unka janm 6 may 1931 ko hua tha. unhone agra se 1946 main hiskool kiya, kanpur ke christ carch college se bi.e. kii degree lee or 1950 main elahabaad chale gayi. san 1954 main elahabaad vishvavidhyalay ke hindi vibhaag main pravaktaa pad par niyukt hue or professor ke pad se sevanivrutt hue. 24 july 2003 ko 72 varsh kii aayu main unka nidhan hua. ek sameekshak ke rup main unhone hindi sahitya ko kai granth diye. 'hindi sahitya or samvednaa kaa vikaas' naamak kriti ke liye unhen 1996 kaa vyas sammaan mila.
