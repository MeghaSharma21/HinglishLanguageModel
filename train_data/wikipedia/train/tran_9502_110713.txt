 sind bharat ke madhya pradesh va uttar pradesh main bahane vaali ek nadi he. isaki lambaai 350 kimi he. madhya pradesh main yah uttar purv disha main bahati he or jagamanpur ke paas uttar pradesh main pravisht hoti he or yahan se 15 kimi uttar main yah yamuna nadi se mil jaati he.
 yah vidisha jile ke nainvacas gram main sthit taal se nikalati he jo samudr dal se 1,780 foot kii oonchaai par sthit he. parvati, nan evam mahour isaki pramukh sahaayak nadiyaan hai. is nadi main puure varsh jal rahata he. varsha ritu main isamen bhayankar baadh aati he. chattani kinaaron ke kaaran yah nadi sinchai ke upyukt nahin he.
