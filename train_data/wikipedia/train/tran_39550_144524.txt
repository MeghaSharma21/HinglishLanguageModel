 veniyar furniture ke kaary main liya jane vaali ek lakdi arthath plywood jaisaa hota he. iskaa motopa 3 mem hota he tathaa yah zyaadaatar 4 foot vaai 8 foot kii shit main miltaa he. veniyar kai tarah ke aate he tathaa kaaphi mahange bhi aate hai. veniyar ko plywood par lagaaya jaataa he tathaa is par keel ko sthaayee rup se nahin maaraa jaataa he. veniyar ko sookhe paper se ghisa jaataa he isse yah kaaphi aakarshak dikhataa he. inke alaava veniyar par lemination bhi kiya jaataa he isse yah kaaphi chamakdaar ho jaataa he.
