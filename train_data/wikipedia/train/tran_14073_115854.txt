 rashid masood ek bhartiya saansad hai jo bharat ke ucchtam nyaayalay dwaara apradhi ghoshit kiye gayi hai. masood 1990 or 1991 ke bich centre kii vishwanath singh sarkaar main swasth rajya mantri the. kendriya pool se desh bhar ke medical collejon main daakhile ke liye tripura ko aavantit embibies siton par dhokhadhadi se ayogy ummidavaaron ko namit karne ke maamle main masood ko doshi tehraaya gaya tha.
 wo bhartiya rashtriya congress ke sadsy ke rup main bhartiya raajnneetijna or uttar pradesh ke saharanpur lok sabha nirvachan kshetra se purv lok sabha sadsy hai. wo rajyasabha sadsy bhi the. wo 10 agast 2007 ko uparashtrapati pad chunaav ke liye sanyukt rashtriya pragatisheel gathbandhan ke ummidavaar bhi the or 75 maton ke saath tisare sthaan par rahe.
 masood aise pehle janpratinidhi ban gaye hai, jinaki rajya sabha sadsyta adaalat se doshi tehraane ke baad gai he.
