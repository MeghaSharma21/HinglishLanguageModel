 prasoon joshi hindi kavi, lekhak, patakatha lekhak or bhartiya cinema ke geetkaar hai. ve vigyaapan jagat kii gatividhiyon se bhi jude hai or anterrashtriya vigyaapan company 'mican irikson' main kaaryakari adhyaksh hai. film ‘taare zamin par’ ke gane ‘maa...’ ke liye unhen 'rashtriya puraskaar' bhi mil chukaa he. ab sensor board ke chairman hai
 prasoon kaa janm uttarakhand ke almoda zile ke danya gaanv main 16 sitambar 1968 ko hua tha. unke pita kaa naam devendra kumaar joshi or mata kaa naam sushamaa joshi he. unka bachapan evam unki praarambhik shiksha tehri, gopeshwar, rudraprayag, chamoli evam narendranagar main hui, jahaan unhone em.essi.or uske baad em.bi.e. kii padhai kii.
 unki teen pustaken prakashit hui he. dilli 6’, ‘taare zamin par’, ‘rang de basanti’, ‘ham tum’ or ‘phna’ jaisi filmon ke liye kai superhit gane likhe hai. film ‘lajja’, ‘ankhen’, ‘kyonki’ main sangeet diya he. ‘thanda matlab coca cola’ evam ‘barbar shop-e ja bal kataa laa’ jaise prachlit vigyapanon ke kaaran unhe anterrashtriya manyata mili.
