 satsang kaa arth bhartiya darshan main he "param satya" kii sangati, guru kii sangati, yaa vyaktiyon kii aisi sabha kii sangati jo satya sunati he, satya kii baat karti he or satya ko aatmsaat karti he. isamen vishisht baat he ki isamen pracheen granthon ko suna yaa padhaa jaataa he, us par baat kii jaati he, uske arth par charchaa kii jaati he, un shabdon ke strot ko aatmsaat kiya jaataa he, dhyaan kiya jaataa he or unke arth ko apane dainandin jivan main utaara jaataa he. samakaleen paashchaatya satsang karaane vaale guru- jo aksar advait vedant parampara ke hai - kai baar paramparaagat purvi gyaan ko aadhunik manovigyaan kii paddatiyon ke saath milaate hai.
