 
 aiquifisi bacteria kaa ek vividh jeevvaigyanik sangh he jisamen keval ek varg sammilit he, jiskaa naam bhi aiquifisi he. is sandh va varg kii sadsy jatiyaan kathin paryaavarniya paristhitiyon main rahati hai. yah gram-rinatmak, antarbijanu na banane vaale chadi-akruti ke bacteria hote hai. aiquifisi swaposhi bhi hote hai or vaayu kii carbon dioxide se carbon khinchne main sakasham hote hai. is sangh ko pani ke chashmon, talabon, sagaron or mahasaagaron main paaya gaya he.
