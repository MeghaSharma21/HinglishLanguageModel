 nira yadav bharat ke jharkhand rajya kii koderma seat se bhartiya janata party ke vidhayak hai. 2014 ke chunaavon main ve rashtriya janata dal ke ummidavaar annapurna devi ko 13525 voton ke antar se haraakar nirvaachit hue. da nira yadav abhi jharkhand sarkaar main schuli shiksha evam sakshartaa vibhaag tathaa ucch evam takneekee shiksha va kaushal vikaas vibhaag kii mantri hai.
