 gwaal kavi reetikaal ke reetigranthkaar kavi hai. reetikaal ke ve antim acharya hai. inke likhe chaudaa granth milte hai. inamen prasiddh kritiyaan hai rasikananda, saahityanand, rasrang, alankaar bhrambhanjan, dooshandarpan tathaa prastaar prakaash. gwaal kavi kaa mahatva reetikaar acharya kii drushti se bahut he. unhone 'dooshan darpan' main hindi kaviyon kii kavitaaon ke udhaaharan sankalit karke unka vistaar se dosha vivechan kiya he. gwaal kavi se pehle dosha vivechna ko itnaa adhik mahatva kisi kavi ne nahin diya tha. gwaal kavi kaa sambandh mathura se rahaa he. ghumkkad hone ke kaaran inhen solah bhashaaon kaa abhyaas tha.
