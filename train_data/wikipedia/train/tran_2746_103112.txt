 mukhabhigam bhi maithun kaa ek tarika he jise praaia: sambhog se purv yonangon ko mukh, jeebh, hont ke prayog se uttejit kiya jaataa he. ise aam bolchaal kii bhashaa main mukh maithun kehete hai. yah saamanyat: stri kii yoni main purush ling ke pravesh karne se purv tak hi simit rahati he. stri-purush donon ke purnataya uttejit ho jane ke baad to sara kaam ling hi karata he.
 aajkal samalangikta ke kaaran is prakaar kaa maithun prachlit he. kamasutra ke praneta vatsyayan ne is prakaar ke maithun ko prakaaraantar se kukrity kaha he or hey bataaya he.
