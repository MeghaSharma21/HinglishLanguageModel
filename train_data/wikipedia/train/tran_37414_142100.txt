 siatal america ke washington rajya kaa ek pramukh shahar he. yah washington rajya kaa sabase bada shahar hone ke saath-saath vahaan kaa pramukh bandargah bhi he. yah prashanth mahaasaagar tathaa lake voushington ke bich sthit he. canada kii seemaa yahan se keval 160 kilometre dur he. april 2009 main yahan kii aabaadi lagbhag 61700 thi.
 pike place market yahan kii badi mashhoor sabji mandi he. parytak evam nivasee roj fal, subjiyan, phool, machhlee aadi khraridanen yahan hajaaron kii taadaad main aate hai.
 maanav yahan kam-se-kam 4000 rvashon se basaa hua he. goron kaa aagaman san 1851 main shuru hua. arthrar denni tathaa unke saathiyon ne sabase pahli basti basaai jiskaa naam new york-olkai rakhaa gaya. san 1853 main duwamish tathaa suvamish kabeelon ke sardaar sialah ko sammanit karne ke liye basti kaa naam siatal rakhaa gaya.
