 henry shashtham 1422 se 1461 evam baad main 1470 se 1471 tak pun england ke raja tathaa 1422 se 1453 tak frans ke vivaadit raja rahe. henry pancham ke ekmaatr bachche jo unke uttaradhikari ke rup main apane pita kii mrityu ke baad maatr 9 maah kii aayu main sihansan par baithe tathaa apane dada 'charles chaturth' kii mrityu ke thode hi samay baad france ke uttaradhikari bhi bane.
