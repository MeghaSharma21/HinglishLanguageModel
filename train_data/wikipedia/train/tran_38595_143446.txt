 somdev suri 10veen shatabdi ke ek jain sant the. unka janm sambhavtah bengal main hua tha kintu kaary kshetra dakshin bharat rahaa. 
 unke dwaara rachit nitivakyamritam niti kaa prasiddh granth he. iske alaava unhone "upaskadyayan" naamak ek granth rachaa jo digambar jain samrdaay kaa pramukh granth he.
