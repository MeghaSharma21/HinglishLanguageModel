 tarang dairghy: 1000 m to 100 m 
 madhyam aavriti  ve radio aavriti hoti hai, jo 300 kHz se 3000 kHz ke madhya hoti hai. is pattee ke ek bhaag ko madhyam tarang AM prasaaran pattee bhi kehete hai. ise hectometre pattee yaa tarang bhi kaha jaataa he, kyonki isaki tarangon kii dairghy das se ek hecto meter hota he. .
 nirdisht sanketak jo ki hawaii evam sagariya nauvahan hetu prayog hota he, 190 kHz to 435 kHz, kii pattee prayog karata he. 
 500 kHz kai varshon tak vipatti evam aapatti kaaleen aavriti ke rup main prayog kii gai he. 510 se 530 kHz. ke bich kai NDB hai.
