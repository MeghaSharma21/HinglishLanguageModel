 ramajna prashn tulsidas kii rachana he jo shubh-ashubh vichaar ke liye rachi gayi he. yah vichaar unhone ram-katha kii sahaayta se prastut kiya he. yah rachana doha style main rachit he. saat-saat saptakon ke saat sargon ke liye pustak kholane par jo doha miltaa he uske pehle ram-katha kaa koi ek prasang aata he or baad main shubh-ashubh fal charchaa aati he.
