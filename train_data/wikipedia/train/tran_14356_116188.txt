 pili chhatari vaali ladki, udai prakaash kaa ek hindi kahani yah ek stri vimarsh he. isamen ek yuvak va ek yuvati ke bich prem kii kahani pesh kii gai he, jiske raste main jati vyavastha, saamaajik mulya or upabhokta samskruti rukaawatein hai. lekin yah kahani usase kahin pare tak saamaajik-raajnneetik vimarsh main dakhlandaazi karti he. rahul ke dil main pyaar aata he or yah pyaar vidroh nahin, lekin do staron par bhitarghaat karata he. pehali baat ki rahul kaa mardwaadi rukh, jiskaa parichay hamen shuru main hi uske kamare main maadhuri dikshit ke poster ke prakaran se miltaa he, tut-tuutakar chur ho jaataa he. baad main donon kaa pyaar manuaadi manyataon par aadhaarit or azaadi ke baad bharat kii upabhokta samskruti main panpi manyataon ko andar se todane lagata he. ant main palaayan- or yah palaayan bhi bhitarghaat he, samaaj main prachlit manyataon ko spasht rup se thukraana he. kahani ke aarambh main bollywood he, ant bhi bollywood kii tarah sukhaant he. lekin samooche vimarsh main bollywood ke vipareet spasht taatpary he.isamen vartmaan main jo jatigat saamaajik antar virodh or jo ek duraav jaisi bhaavana bah rahi he uskaa bakhubi chithran kiya gaya he lekin lekhak kii maansikta kya he iskaa parichay hamen praapt nahin hota. lekhak ne yah kaary apane shudhi pathakon par chhod diya he.
