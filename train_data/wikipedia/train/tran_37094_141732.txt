 kaayantaran ek jeevvaigyanik prakriya he jisamen kisi jaanavar ke paidaa hone ke yaa andey se nikalne ke baad koshikaon kii badhautari se uske shaareerik dhaanche main kam samay main bade parivartan aa jate hai. udhaaharan ke liye rengne vaali illi kayantaran karke udne vaali titali ban jaati he. isi tarah se mendhak kaa baccha ek nanhee machhlee jaisaa lagata he lekin kuch hi dinon main puri tarah parivartit hokar mendhak ban jaataa he.
