 amarushtakam sanskrit kaa ek geetikavy he. iske rachayita amru yaa amaruuk hai. iskaa rachana kaal naveen shatee mana jaataa he. isamen 100 shloke hai jo atyant shringarpurn evam manoram hai.
 naam se yah shatak he, parantu iske padyon kii sankhya ek sau se kahin adhik he. sookhtisangrahon main amaruuk ke naam se nirdisht padyon ko milakar samast shlokon kii sankhya 163 he. is shatak kii prasiddhi kaa kuch parichay isaki vipul tikaaon se lag saktaa he. iske upar das vyakhyaaon kii rachana vibhinn shatabdiyon main kii gai jinamein arjuna varmdev kii 'rasik sanjivani' apni vidvatta tathaa maarmikata ke liye prasiddh he. aanandavardhan kii sammati main amaruuk ke muktak itane saras tathaa bhavpurn hai ki alpakaay hone par bhi ve prabandhkavy kii samta rakhte hai. sanskrit ke alankarikon ne dhvanikavy ke udhaaharan ke liye iske bahut se padya uddhrut kar inaki saahitiyik sushamaa kaa parichay diya he. 
 amaruuk shabdakavi nahin hai, pratyut rasakavi hai jinka mukhya lakshya kavy main ras kaa prachur unmesh he. amarushtak ke padya shringaar ras se purna hai tathaa prem ke jite jaagate chatakile chitra khinchne main vishesh samarth hai. premi or premicaon kii vibhinn avasthaaon main vidyaman shringaari manovritiyon kaa ativ sookshm or manovaigyanik vishleshan in saras shlokon kii pradhan vishistataa he. kahin pati ko pardesh jane kii taiyaari karte dekhakar kamini kii hridayvihvalata kaa chitra he, to kahin pati ke aagaman kaa samachar sunakar sundari kii harsh se chhalkati hui aakhon or viksit smit kaa ruchik chithran hai. hindi ke mahakavi bihari tathaa padmakar ne amaruuk ke anek padyon kaa saras anuwaad prastut kiya he.
