 balmoral kasal, aberdinshir ke royal deesaid main sthit ek haveli-numa bhawan he. yah aberdinshir, scotland main krathy namak gaanv ke nikat sthit he. yah belator se 6.2 mile pashchim main avasthit he. yah maahal san 1852 se hi british shahi parivaar ke shahi nivaason main se ek rahaa he, jab rajkumaar albert, maharani victoria ke pati, dwaara praathmik qila samet is puri sampada ko nijee taur par kharid gaya tha. yah mahal, rajpariwaar kii nijee sampatti he, buckingham palace jaise anya mehalon kii tarah rajmukut kii sampatti nahin he.
 is sampada kii kharid kii turant baad aisa abhas kiya gaya kii, takaaleen haveli, rajpariwaar ke awaas-yogya aakaar kii nahin thi. atah, varttman kasal ke nirmaan ko sheeghra hi aadhat kiya gaya. is nirmaankarya ke vaastukar, aberdeen ke wiliam smith the, haalaanki, unki praathmik naqshe main albert ne kuch parivartan bhi faraamosh kiye the. yah mahal scots baronian vaastustyli kii behtareen misaal he, or ise historic scotland dwaara "A"-suchit bhawanon kii shrenee main gina jaataa he. naveenatam mahal par nirmaankarya, 1856 main purna hua, tathaa, kuch samay pashchaat, puraane mahal ko dhwast kar diya gaya.
 is mahal main, rajpariwaar ke aagaamee sadasyon dwaara anek sudhaar va parivartan or yogadan jode gaye. aaj is aapadaa kul 50,000 ekar ke kshetrafal kii bhoomi par failaa hua he. yah ek kaaryshil estate he, or isamen, mahal ke alaava, khet, or jangalon samet patharile viraane or daldal bhi hai. saath hi isamen prabandhit va poshit, ghode, maweshiyaan or hiranon ko bhi paal jaataa he.
