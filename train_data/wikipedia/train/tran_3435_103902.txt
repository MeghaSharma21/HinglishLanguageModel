 peshawar ucch nyaayalay, kaibar pakhtuunakva praant ke sarvocch nyaayik sanstha he. yah prantiy rajdhani peshawar main sthit he. yah civil or aapraadhik maamalon main praant kii sarvocch apilay adaalat he, evam kaibar pachhatuunakva ke saare jila nyaayalay or satr nyaayalay iske adhikarashetra ke antargat aate hai.
 19veen shatabdi ke antim dinon main bharat ke vysarai lord karjan ne khaybar pakhtoonkhwaa praant ko gathit karne ke suzav ko paarit kiya tha, jis kaa prastaav bharat ke tatkaaleen rajya sachiv lord jorge hamiltan ne 20 disambar san 1900 main rakhaa tha.
 is baat ko adhikarik taur par 9 november 1901 ko sthapit kiya gaya. saath hi ish baat kii jarurat samajhi gai kii is praant ke liye ek nyaayik ayukta ko niyukt kiya jaae. khaybar pakhtoonkhwaa vidhi evam nyaay prastaavna sankhya 8 1901 ke tahat maharajyapal evam parishad ,ne bharat sarkaar adhinium, 1854 ke tahat kaibar pakhtoonkhwaa praant main nyaayik sansthaanon kii sthapana kaa aadesh diya or isi pariyojna ke tahat is adaalat kii bhi sthapana kii gai. 
 1973 kaa samvidhan, yah praavdhaan detaa he ki peshawar ucch nyaayalay ke do nyaayachaukiyaan abtabad or diai khan main sthapit kii jaaen. samvidhan kaa anuchhed 199, ucch nyaayalayon kii nyaayik adhikaar-seemaa ko vistaar se ankit karata he. 1973 ke samvidhan main die gaye praavdhaan paraspar 1956 ke samvidhan main die gaye praavdhaanon ke baraabar hi thea, jise vistaar main, 1962 ke samvidhan main diya gaya tha.
