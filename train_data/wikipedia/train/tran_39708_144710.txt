 vaidic dharmapar ek shrenee kaa bhaag
 moksh
 dhyaan yoga 
 
 naag stotra naag devataao ke nau avtaro ko sambodhan karne ke uddeshy se rachit he, is stotra main vibhinn naag devataao ke naam ke saath stuti kar bhakt naag devo ko prasann karata he, kyonki yahi wo nimnlikhit naago ke naam he jo is prithvi ke bhaar ko apane mani par grahan kiye hue he. isaliye ye hamaara param kartavya he kii ham naag devtaa ko is stotra ke madhyam se unka dhanyavad kare.
 naag stotra kuch is prakaar se he :
 ananthan vasuki shesh padmanabhan c kambalam.
 shadkhpal dhartaraashtra takshakan kaliyan tathaa..
 etani nav namani naganaan c mahatmanaam.
 siankale pathennityam praatah kaale visheshtah..
 tasme vishabhyan naasti sarvatra vijayin bhavet.
 naag stotra kaa bhavarth yahi he kii jo is stotra kaa pathan-pathan pratidin karata he wo jis kshetra main jaataa he use vijay praapt hoti he or uske saare manokamana purn hote hai.
