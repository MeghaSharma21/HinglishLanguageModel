 balmaa 1993 kii hindi film he. iskaa nirdesan laurence disuza ne kiya he or mukhya bhoomikaaon main avinaash vadhavan, aaysha julka, said jafari or anjana mumtaz hai. film to koi khaas nahin chali lekin iskaa sangeet lokapriy rahaa he. 
 vishaal ek yuva college se snaatak he jo apni chikitsakeey rup se aswasth maa ke saath ek garib jivan style jeeta he. naukari talashne ke uske sabhi prayaas anushansa kii kami ke kaaran safal nahin rahe hai. phir ek din dainik samachar patr ko padhate samay usne madhu naam kii ek amir ladki ke liye aavashyak pati ke liye ek vigyaapan notice kiya. ek sifarish ko lekar sahi mauka dhundte hue vah turant aavedan karata he. wo ummidavaaron main se ek he jise shortlist kiya jaataa he or saakshaatkaar ke liye chunaa jaataa he. baad main phir vivaah hota he. yah vivaah he jo vishaal or madhu ke jivan ko hameshaa ke liye badal degaa.
 sabhi geet samir dwaara likhit; sara sangeet nadeem-shravan dwaara rachit.
