 fagawada ek nagar he or yah haal hi main yahme kapoorthala jile nagar nigam ban gaya he jo kii punjab praant ke madhya bhaag main - sthit he . is nagar ko antarrashtriya str par manyata praapt he, kyonki ek badi sankhya main enarai isi nagar se he. yahan bahutayat main rajput barsar, jat jati nivas karte hai. yah jalandhar rajasav prabhag ke antargat aata he. 
 fagawada main ghoomane ke liye shiv mandir pakkaa baag, baba balak nath mandir, panj mandiri mandir, gita bhawan mandir, gurudwara sukhchain sahib, gurudwara chhivin patshahi, shri swami shankar nath parvat math, mata mansa devi hadibad, kheda masjid jaise dharmik sthaan hai. dharmik sthanon ke alaava hargobind nagar choupati ilaake bahut prasiddh hai, jinamein bade brandon ke outlet or showrooms hai.
