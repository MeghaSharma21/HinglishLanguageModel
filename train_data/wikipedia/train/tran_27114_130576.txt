 kshobhamandal yaa troposphear prithvi ke vayumandal kaa sabase nichalaa hissaa he. isi parat main ardrata, jalakan, dhulkan, vayudhundh tathaa sabhi mausami ghatnaayen hoti hai. yah prithvi kii vaayu kaa sabase ghanaa bhaag he or puure vayumandal ke dravyaman kaa 80% hissaa isamen maujuud he. bumadhya rekha par isaki gaharaai 20 kimi he jo dhruvon par ghatakar sirf 7 kimi hi rah jaati he. kshobhamandal kii ausat oonchaai 10 se 12 keemi he. vayumandal main iske upar kii parat ko samtapamandal yaa stratosphear kehete hai. in donon paraton ke bich kii rekha kaa naam tropopauz he. is mandal kaa taapmaana 15℃ se -56℃ tak hota he. oonchaai ke saath isame vayudab va taapmaana main kami hoti he
 dharaatal se upar jaane pye prati 1,000 feet kii uchaai par taapmaana main 3.6 degree fahrenheit kii giravat aati jaati hai.
