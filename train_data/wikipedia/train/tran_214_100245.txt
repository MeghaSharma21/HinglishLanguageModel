 rashtriya rajnatik gathbandhan
  raajnneeti praveshdwar
 bangladesh main raajnneeti samvidhan, main die gaye sansadeey, pratinidhitiv vaadi loktantric, ganatantric pranali ke antargat hoti he jiske anusaar: raashtrapati bangladesh ke rashtradhyaksh evam bangladesh ke pradhanmantri, sarkaar evam ek bahudaleey janatantric pranali ke pramukh hote hai. kaaryakari shaktiyaan, bangladesh kii sarkaar ke adhikarashetra ke antargat aati hai, evam vidhai shaktiyaan sarkaar or sansad donon par nyochaavar kii gai hai. iske alaava, bangladesh main ek swatantra shreneebaddh nyaaypalika bhi he, jiske shikhar par bangladesh kii sarvocch nyaayalay he. bangladesh ke samvidhan ko san 1972 main likhaa gaya tha or tab se lekar aaj tak isamen kul 16 sanshodhan kiye gaye hai.
