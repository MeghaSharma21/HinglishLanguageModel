 soha ali khan hindi filmon kii ek abhinetri hai. ve prasiddh cricket khilaadi mansoor ali khan pataudi evam hindi filmon kii mashhoor abhinetri sharmila tagore kii beti hai. abhinayta saif ali khan inke bhai hai.
 soha ali khan kaa janm 4 oktober 1978 ko pataudi main hua tha. 2006 main unohane rang de basanti film main kaam kiya jis main unka naam sonia tha or jis ke baad unako best supportin actress kii kaaran ipha award or best sopporting actress ke liye giffa award diya gaya. soha ali khan ke pita mansoor ali khan pataudi or unke dadaji iftikhar ali khan pataudi bhartiya cricket team ke captain the. or unke mata sharmila tagore or bhai saf ali khan yah donon filmi duniya ke abhinayta he or unki bahan sabaa ali khan jewelri diziner he.
 unohane apni skool kii padhai nayi dely kii " The British School " se kii or Balliol College main dakhla liya, master degree praapt kii jo ke London School of Economics and Political Science. film jagat main aane se pehle soha ali khan "ford foundation" or "sitibank" main kaam karti thi. actor kunal khemu or soha ali khan ne behad nijee samaaroh main byaah rachaaya.
 soha ali khan kii pehali film 2004 main ai jis kaa naam he dil maange mor, jis main unke saath shahid kapur, aaysha takia or tulip joshi ne kaam kiya. unohane ek bengali film main kaam kiya jis kaa naam tha "antar mahal" jo ke 2005 main ai thi or "rang de basanti" 2006 main ai. uske baad unki jo film ai uskaa naam tha "tum mili" jis main unohane imran hashmi ke saat kaam kiya.
