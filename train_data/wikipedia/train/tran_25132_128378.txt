 nartki yah ek kaansya murti he jo mohenjodaro se praapt hui thi jo sindhu ghati kii sabhyataa kaa ek sthal he. mana jaataa he kii ise 2500 isapurv ke aasapaas banaaya gaya hoga. mohenjodaro ke ek ghar se ek or kaansya murti praapt hui thi jo lagbhag isi kii tarah he. bhirdana main ek mitti ke bartan par bhi nartki kaa bhittichitra mila he. is murti kii unchai 10.5 centimetre he. yah mohenjodaro ke 'ech aar kshetra' main 1926 main arnest maike ko praapt hui thi. yadhyapi yah murti nrity mudra main nahin he phir bhi 'nartki' isaliye kaha gaya kyonki isaki sajaavat se lagata he ki iskaa vyavsaay nrity hoga. iske sharir par vastra nahin hai kintu iske ek haath main upar tak chudiyaan hai.
