 web brouzar ek prakaar kaa software hota he, jo kii vishwavyaapee web yaa sthaneey sarwar par uplabdh lekh, chhaviyon, chal-chhitron, sangeet or anya jaankariyon ityadi ko dekhne tathaa anya internet suvidhaon ke prayog karne main prayukt hota he. web prishth ech.tii.em.el. naamak computer bhashaa main likhe jate he, tathaa web broujar un ech.tii.em.el. prishthon ko upabhokta ke computer par darshata he. vyaktigat computaron par prayog hone vaale kuch mukhya web broujar hai internet explorer, mojila fyrafox,safari, opera, flock or google krom, ityadi he jabaki web brousero ke smartphone sanskaran ech.tii.em.el. prishthon ko upabhokta ke mobile par darshane me sahaayta karte he
 prayek computer ek prachaalan tantra kaa samarthan karata he, kisi ke system main windos, to kisi main lines yaa unics hota he. prayek vyakti or company apni aavashyaktaaon ke anusaar prachaalan tantra sthapit karte hai. prayek prachaalan tantra kii programing alag hoti he or prakaarya bhi alag hote hai. internet ke aarambhik kaal main prachaalan tantra kaa alag-alag hona ek badi samasya thi. alag prachaalan tantra hone ke kaaran ek prachaalan tantra ko duusare se sanchaar ke liye samasyaayein aane lageen. is daur main aisi bhashaa kii atyaavashyaktaa mahasoos kii jane lagi, jo sabhi prachaalan tantron ke liye samaan ho. aise main soochna ke aadan-pradaan ke liye sarvamaanya programmen bhashaa achtmal ai. isaki programing or prakaarya aisa banaaya gaya, jo web broujar ko samaj main aae. prayek webabroujar achtmal programmen bhashaa ko samajhataa he. aarambh ke dinon ke kai broujar sirf achtiemal kaa samarthan saport karte the, lekin vartmaan main broujar achtmal jaisi duusari programmen bhashaaon jaise ki exchatimal, aadi ko bhi ko saport karne lage.
 san 1991 main tim burner lee ne kai takneekon ke sanyukt prayog se milakar web broujar kii neenv rakhi thi. is web broujar kaa naam world wide web rakhaa gaya tha, jise laghunaam main dablyu.dablyu.dablyu bhi kehete hai. prishthko yuarel ke rup main loket kiya jaataa he or yahi you.aar.el web pate ke taur par janaa jaataa he. is web pate kaa aarambh angrezi ke akshar-samooh ech tii tii pi se hota he. kai broujar achtitipi ke alaava duusare yuarel tip or unke protocol jaise gobar, apatpi aadi ko saport karte hai.
 internet explorer microsoft kaa web brouzar he. haal hi main iskaa launch kiya gaya nayaa sanskaran ai.i-8 he. microsoft ke anusaar yah ab tak kaa sarvottam brouzar he. nayaa aaii puraane sanskarnon se 40% teji se khulata he. yah pannon ko teji se rendor karata he or video bhi teji se chalaata he. google ke anusaar bhi yah broujar firefox or krom donon se tej he. isamen do aisi suvidhaayen jodi gai hai jisase prayoktaaon ko kaaphi suraksha milti he. ek he kros site fishing, yani ki aaii8 webbpannon par rakhi gai hanikaarak script kii pahchaan kar letaa he or aise pannon ko kholta nahi he, jisase prayoktaaon ke computer main aisi script sthapit nahi ho paati. duusari suvidha he clic-hijekking, kai baar prayoktaaon ko koi batan dikhaaya jaataa he jise dabaane par nayaa panna khulegaa aisa bataaya jaataa he, parantu vah vastav main hijacing script hoti he jisase koi hanikaarak sakript computer main sthapit ho jaati he. aaii8 aisi kisi bhi script ko roak detaa he.
 aaii8 ke teb penal main bhi badlaav kiye gaye hai. ab ek hi prakaar kii citen paas paas khulti he or ek hi samooh kii citon kii teb kaa rang bhi ek jaisaa hota he jisase prayoktaaon ko teb pannon ko pahchaanane main aasaani rahati he. iske alaava aaii 8 main ek vishesh suvidha he exicrator. kisi bhi webpej ke kisi bhi shabd ko chunane karne par ek nile rang kaa batan miltaa he jisamen kai link hote hai jaise ki google mep main dhunde, viki par dekhen aadi. isse prayokta kaa samay bachataa he.
 firefox duniya main teji se lokapriy ho rahaa mukt strot web brouzar he. mozillaa firefox ke anusaar uske naveenatam sanskaran firefox 3 kii gati sarvaadhik tej he. isamen nayaa java engine lagaaya gaya he or yah brouzar jimail jaisi site ko dugani teji se kholta he. firefox 3 me ek nai suvidha jodi gai he - van clic site info, jisase prayokta maatr ek batan dabaakar kisi bhi site kii sampoorn jaankaree praapt kar sakate hai. firefox kaa aavaran bhi behatar banaaya gaya he. kisi bhi site par satrarambh karne par ab soochna baar aati he jo kam jagah gherti he. firefox kii downlod suvidha bhi bejod he. prayokta apane downlod ko bich main hi roak sakate hai or pun: vahiin se shuru kar sakate hai. downlod kii gai filon tak pahunchnaa bhi aasaan he.
 safari appal company kaa web brouzar he. appal ne haal hi main safari kaa nayaa sanskaran safari 4 laanch kiya he. is broujar main nayaa nitro engine lagaaya gaya he. appal ke anusaar yah brouzar sabase tej he. vaise isamen suraksha kii drushti se koi nayaa feechar joda nahin gaya he. lekin fishing or melaware suraksha sambandhit puraane sabhi feechar isamen pehle hi uplabdh he. safari kaa teb system ab sabase upar laga diya gaya he. iske alaava top site suvidha manavaanchit citen saralatam tareeke se kholane detii he. safari kii ek nayi suvidha he cover flo. yah suvidha pichhali baar surf kii gai citon kii jaankariyaan or privyuu pradaan karata he. cover flo citon ko usee kram me samyojit karata he jis kram me ve surf kii gai thi.
 krom google kaa web brouzar he. haal hi main krom kaa beta 2 sanskaran jari kiya gaya he. krom kaa nayaa sanskaran puraane sanskaran kii toolna main 30 se 40% tak adhik tej he. krom google kaa utpaada hone ke kaaran hanikaarak webbpannon kii pahchaan aasaani se kar paata he. google team lagaataar aise pannon kii khoj karti rahati he. jab bhi upayokta aise kisi pannon ko surf karne kii koshish karte hai krom aisa karne se roak detaa he. krom kaa teb system anoothaa he. sabhi tebon ko khinch kar alag brouzar kaa rup bhi diya ja saktaa he or shortkat ke rup main desktop par saheja bhi ja saktaa he. krom kii tebing system anya broujaron se ekadam alag he. krom kii anokhi suvidha he speed dial. kisi bhi nai teb ko kholane par pichhali baar surf kiye gaye webbpannon ke 9 thambanel dikhaai dene lagate hai saath hi nae buchamark kii kiye gaye pannon kii suchi bhi.
 flock ek web brouzar he, jo mozila firefox kuta-aadhaar par viksit he. ye saamaajik netwarking evam web 2.0 kii suvidhaon ke liye khaas viksit kiya gaya he. flock kaa sanskaran 2.5 aadhikarik rup se 19 may 2009 ko launch kiya gaya tha. ye nickshulk downlod hetu uplabdh he. isapar microsoft windos, mike oes eks or linex plataforms par samarthan uplabdh he.
