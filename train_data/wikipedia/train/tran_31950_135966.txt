 kumari radha  ek prasiddh hindi or magahi kavi thi. 
 kumari radha kaa janm 7 sitambar 1936 ko jivachpur gaanv, supaul bihar main hua tha. vah 5 bacchon ke parivaar main duusari beti thi unke pita, dow lakshmi prasad or unki maa, indira devi hai. us ne apni praathmik shiksha gaanv se kii or us ne 1966 main patna vishvavidhyalay se bie kii. baad main us ne 1968 main masters, eme puri kii. unhone apane chaatr jivan se sahitya, kala, saamaajik or raajnneetik kaam main ruchi leni shuru kar di thi.
 pad se sevanivrutt hokar, vah shram rojgaar vibhaag se vibhageeya patrika "shramik" kii sahaayak sampadak he.
