 siddhidaas mahaju nepalbhasha ke mahakavi he. unhe nepalbhasha punarjagran kaa chaar stambh mai ek ke rup mai bhi lete he. nepalbhasha mai aadhunik kavita evam aadhunik katha lekhan ke sruvaat mai inkaa bada haat tha. unhaunne ramayana ko nepalbhasha mai anuwaad kiya tha.
 inkaa janm ness 987 yanglaga dutiya de din huwa tha. inkaa pita kaa naam lakshminarayan mahaju va mata kaa naam harshalakshmi mahaju tha. veh kwachhen nani, kel tvaah, kathmadonmai rahete the. inkaa vivaah gangadevi/ hakunani se huwa tha. inkaa dehant ness 1050 kachlaaga balachare me huwa tha.
 inkaa kriti is prakaar he-
 in ke vaani "bhashaa mvantsa jati mvaai" nepalbhasha sahitya kaa ek bahut prabhaavshaalee vaakya he. kisi bhi jati, prajati yaa sabhyataa ke astitv ke liye bhashaa aprihaary he. is vaani ke anusaar sabhyataa ke vikaas ke maapan ke liye sabhyataa kaa bhashaa sahitya ko maapna aprihaary he.
