 pratapsingh barath bharat ke swatantraaa sangram ke ek krantivir the.
 unka janm rajasthan ke udaipur main 24 may 1893 main hua tha.ve krantivir thaa.kesari singh barath ke putr the. praarambhik shiksha kota, ajmer or jaipur main hui. krantikari master ameerchand se prerana lekar desh ko swatantra karwaane main jute gaye. 
 ve rasbihari bose kaa anusaran karte hue krantikari aandolan main sammilit hue. raas singh bihari bose kaa pratap par bahut vishwaas tha. 23 disambar 1912 ko lord hardings par bam phenkane kii yojana main ve bhi sammilit the. unhen banaras kand ke sandarbh main giraftaar kiya gaya or san 1916 main 5 varsh ke sashram karawas kii sajaa hui. bareli ke kendriya karagaar main unhen amanviya yatnaayen di gayin taaki apane sahyogiyon kaa naam unase pataa kiya ja sake kintu unhone kisi kaa naam nahin liya. 24may 1918 ko jail main hi angrejon kii kathor yaatnaaon se ve shahid ho gayi.bareli jail main charles cleveland ne inhen ghor yatnaaen di ore kaha - "tumhaari maan roti he " to is vir ne jabaab diya - " main apni maan ko chup karaane ke liye hajaaron maano ko nahin rulaa saktaa. " or kisi bhi saathi kaa naam nahin bataaya.
