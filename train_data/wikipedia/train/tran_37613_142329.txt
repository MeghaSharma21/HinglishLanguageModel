 bhautik niyatank us bhautik raashi ko kehete hai jiske bare main aisa vishwaas kiya jaataa he ki vah raashi prakruti main saarvatrik he tathaa samay ke saath aparivartansheel yaa niyat he. bhautik niyatank, ganitiya niyatank se is maamle main bhinn hai ki ganitiya niyatank sankhyatmak drushti se to niyat hote hai kintu unka kisi maapan se sambandh nahin hota.
 vigyaan main bahut se bhautik niyatank hai jinamein se pramukh hai - shunya main prakaash kaa veg c, gurutvkarshan niyatank G, plank niyatank h, nirvaat kaa vidyut niyatank ε0, tathaa electron kaa aavesh e. 
 vistrut jaankaree ke liye moolbhoot bhautik niyatank dekhen.
 Using dimensional analysis, it is possible combine fundamental physical constants to produce basic units of measurement. Depending on the choice and arrangement of constants used, the resulting natural units may have physical meaning. For example, Planck units use   c, G, ℏ, ε 0   {\displaystyle c,G,\hbar ,\varepsilon _{0}} and   k  {\displaystyle k} to derive constants relevant to unified theories, including quantum gravity.
