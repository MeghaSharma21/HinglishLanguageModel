 bheemabetkar bharat ke madhya pradesh rajya ke raisen jile main sthit ek purapashanik awasaiya sthal he. aisa mana jaataa he ki yah sthaan mahabharat ke charitra bhim se sambandhit he evam isi se iskaa naam bhimbetka padaa.ye guphaayen madhya bharat ke pathaar ke dakshini kinare par sthit vindhyaachal kii pahadiyon ke nichle chor par hai.iske dakshin main satapura kii pahadiyaan armbh ho jaati hai
