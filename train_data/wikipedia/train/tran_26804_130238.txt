 burma, jo myanmar bhi kehalaata he, 21 prashasnic ikaiyon main banta hua he, jo is prakaar hai:
 burma kaa san 2008 kaa samvidhan desh ke rajyon, mandalon or swytt ikaiyon ko paribhaashit karata he.
 yah donon sabase unche darje ke vibhaag maane jate hai. rajyon main alpsankhyakon kii bahutayat hoti he jabaki mandalon main bahusankhyak bamaa log adhik hai. is tathy ke alaava rajya or mandal main koi vishesh prashasnic antar nahin hota. har rajya yaa mandal kaa ek mukhyamantri, anya mantrigan or ek mahanyaywaadee hota he.
 yah donon mandalon yaa rajyon ke bheetar hote hai. inkaa prashasan das yaa us se adhik sadasyon vaala ek pramukh parishad karata he, jisamen se kuch jan-matdaan dwaara or kuch sena kii ichha se niyukt hote hai.
