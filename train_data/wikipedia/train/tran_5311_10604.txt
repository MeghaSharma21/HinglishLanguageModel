 maah laaka bai, janm chanda bibi, or kabhi-kabhi maah laaka chanda kaha jaataa tha, hyderabad main sthit ek bhartiya 18 veen sadi kirdu kavi, veshya or paropkaaree thi. 1824 main, vah apane kaam, gulzaar-e-mahalaaka namit urdu ghazal ke ek sankalan ke maranoparant prakashit hone se pehali asi mahila kavi ban gai jis kaa dewan prakashit hua ho. vah ek aise samay main hui thi jab dakhini atyadhik farsinishth sankraman ban rahaa tha. us ke saahitiyik yogadan dakshini bharat main aise bhashaai parivartanon par antardrushti pradaan karte hai. vah dakkan kii ek prabhavi mahila tawife thi; nizaam, hyderabad ke shaasha, unhen omarah, or adaalat main nikat sahyogi ke rup main niyukt kiya. 2010 main, sanyukt rajya america kii sangheey sarkaar dwaara daan kii gai dhanrashi kaa upyog karke uske makbare vaale hyderabad main uskaa smarak behaal kiya gaya tha.
 maha lakka bai kaa janm 7 april 1768 ko aurangabad main aaj ke maharashtra main chanda bibi ke rup main hua tha. unki maa raaj kunwar - raajapootaana se thi. or pita bahadur khan the, jisane mughal samraat mohammad shah ke adaalat main ek mansabadar ke rup main kaary kiya tha. khan dilli se hyderabad deccan chale gaye jahaan unhone raaj kunwar se mulaakaat kii or usase shaadi kii. :107 chand bibi ko kunwar kii bebas bahan mehtab maa ne apanaaya tha, jo hyderabad ke nizaam ke pradhan mantri nawab rukan ud daula ke lagbhag ek niyamit rup se patni thi.
