 1988 europea cap final main netherland ke piesvi ointhowan or portugal ke benfika ke bich khelaa ek footbal match tha. piesvi atirikt samay ke baad ek drow ke baad penltiz par 6-5 jeeta. match 25 may 1988 par, shtutgart main nekar stadium, main khelaa gaya tha.
 piesvi ke liye, is jeet dach cap, dach championships or europea cap ke ek tihara surakshit. piesvi team ke paanch ki garmiyon main germany main yuiefee euro 1988 jeetne ja rahaa tha ki dach team kaa hissaa the.
