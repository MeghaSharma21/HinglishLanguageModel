 
 
 uttar pradesh rajya rajmarg 13 bharat ke uttar pradesh rajya kaa ek rajya rajmarg he. 228.20 kilometre lambaa yah rajmarg baharich se shuru hokar baandaa tak jaataa he. ise 	baharich-barabanki-haidargarh-fatehpur-baandaa maarg bhi kaha jaataa he. yah baharich, barabanki, raibareli, fatehpur or banda jilon se hokar gujarata he.
