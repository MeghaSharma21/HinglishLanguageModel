 kisi padaarth kii tanan samarthy yaa tanaav pushti  us padaarth ke pratibal-vikruti vakr ke mahattam bindu hota he tathaa yah sanket detaa he ki kis pratibal ke baad gardan bananaa armbh hoga. iskaa maan pareekshan ke liye lee gayi padaarth ke namoonay ke aakaar par nirbhar nahin karata. 
 sanrachanaon tathaa yantric yuktiyon main prayukt ejiniyari padaarthon ke liye pratyasthata gunank tathaa ksharan pratirodh ke saath-saath tanaav-pushti atyant mahatv kii raashi he. mishradhaatuon, compojit padaarthon, siramics, plasticon, kashtha, kankreet aadi ke liye iske maan diye jate hai.
 tanaav pushti kii teen paribhaashaayen hai-
  paraabhav samarthy jis pratibal par padaarth kii vikruti pratyasth se apratyasth me badalane lagati he jisase padaarth main sthaayee vikruti utpann ho jaati he.
  adhiktam samarthy yah vah pratibal he jo pratibal-vikruti graph main adhiktam pratibal kii suchak hoti he. 
  vibhanjak samarthy yah vah pratibal-vikruti vakr ke us bindu par pratibal kaa maan he jis par vastu tut jaati he. 
 kuch padaarthon ke saamaanya tanaav pushti ke maan neeche kii sarini main diya gaya he-
