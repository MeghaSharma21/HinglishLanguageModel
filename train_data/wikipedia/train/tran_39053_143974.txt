 ubies vishwa kii ek pramukh weetiya company he jo bharat main "swiss bank" ke naam se kukhyat he. iskaa mukhyalay switzraland ke jurik or basel main he. yah sansar kii vyaktigat smapda ke prabandhan kii sabase badi company he. aisa vishwaas kiya jaataa he ki vishwa ke bade-bade bhrasht rajaneta or tex-chor is bank main apana dhan jamaa karte hai. ubies "unian bank of switzraland" kaa sankshipt rup he jo ki isaki purv-sanstha kaa naam he. san 1998 main iskaa vilay swiss bank karporation main ho jane ke baad ab yah naam sarthak nahin rahaa.
