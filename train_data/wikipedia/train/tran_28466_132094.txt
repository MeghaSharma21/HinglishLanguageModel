 indira gandhi antararashtriya khel stadium, uttrakhand rajya ke haldwani nagar main sthit ek nirmaanadhin bahuuddeshiy stadium he. bharat kii pratham mahila pradhanmantri shrimati indira gandhi ke naam par sthapit is stadium kaa nirmaan 2015-16 main 38wein rashtriya khelon ke aayojan ke liye karavaaya gaya tha. 25,000 logon kii kshamta vaale is stadium kaa udghaatan 18 disambar 2016 ko uttrakhand ke tatkaaleen mukhyamantri, harish ravat ne kiya tha. yah stadium 70 acre kshetra main failaa hua he or isamen cricket or footbal ke maidan, 800 meter daud ke liye ek trek, ek hockey field, badminton court, lon tennis court, boxing ring or swimming pool bhi hai.
 yah stadium haldwani nagar ke gaulapar kshetra main gaula nadi ke tat par sthit he. yah haldwani bas stesan se lagbhag 3 kimi jabaki railway stesan se 2.5 kimi kii duuri par he.
 7 navambar 2014 ko uttrakhand sarkaar ne hyderabad kii en si si limited ke saath samjhauta gyaapan par hastakshar kiye, jiske anusaar en si si limited ko 30.20 hectare bhoomi par 18 maheenon main stadium kaa nirmaan karnaa tha. us samay is pariyojna ko 225 karod rupaye aavantit kiye gaye the. haldwani stadium kii neenv 9 november 2014 ko uttrakhand ke tatkaaleen mukhyamantri mukhyamantri harish ravat dwaara rakhi gai thi. 18 disambar 2016 ko unhone is stadium complex ke antargat antarrashtriya cricket ground kaa locarpan kiya. may 2017 tak stadium ke antargat athlete stadium taiyaar nahin hua tha.
 athletics, basketball, valiball or hockey ko chhodkar pratiyogita ke anya sabhi khel haldwani stadium main yojit kiya janaa prastavit he.
 stadium kii kshamta 25,000 darshakon kii he.
