 ginni maahi ek punjabi loakgeet, rap or hip-hop gayika hai jo bharat ke jalandhar, punjab se he. unke geet fain baba sahib di or danger chamaar prasiddh hai. maahi kaa mul naam gurkanwal cor hai. vah bhim geeton ke liye prasiddh hai.
 ginni maahi kaa janm rakesh chandra maahi or paramjeet cor ke ghar, punjab ke jalandhar, abdpura main hua tha. vah chamaar yaa ravidaas samudaay ke parivaar se sambandhit he. unhone hans raaj mahila mahavidhyalay main adhyayan kiya he.
 ginni maahi ne 11 saal kii umr se gayan shuru kar diya tha. ginni ne ek hazaar se adhik stage sho or gayan kaaryakramon main hissaa chukaa hai. vah babasahab ambedkar ko apana adarsh maanati hai. ginni apane har geet main ek sandesh dena chaahati he, vah apni awaz se hi logon ko saamaajik pichhadepan se baahar nikaalane ke liye prayaas karti hai or ab tak apane lakshya main safal bhi rahi he.
