 binaaka gitamala bhartiya filmi sangeet kaa sabase pehla count down kaarykram tha radio par| 1950 or 1960 ke dashak film, film sangeet or radio ke dashak the| film or radio ke alaava koi or vishesh saadhan nahin tha un dinon manoranjan kaa| lokapriy filmi geeton par aadhaarit ek kaarykram prasaarit hota tha un dinon radio ceylone se - binaaka gitamala| filmi geeton se sambandhit sabase lokapriy kaarykram tha ye us samay kaa| har buudhvaar ko raat 8 baje se 9 baje tak binaaka gitamala sunne ke liye log radio se chipak jaaya karte the| melodius dhunon or madhur kanthswaron kaa sangam shrotaaon ko puure ek ghante tak bhav vibhor banaaye rakhata tha.
 binaaka gitamala kaa pehla kaarykram san 1952 ke antim saptaah main prasaarit hua tha. mahal, nagin, udan khatola jaise filmon, jinaki atmaaon main keval madhur sangeet basataa tha, kaa jamaana tha wo| madhur geeton ke saath udghoshak amin sayaani ke vishesh andaaj ne moh liya shrotaaon ko or apane pehle hi prasaaran se binaaka gitamala sarvaadhik lokapriy kaarykram ban gaya radio kaa| san 1952-53 tak keval 7 gane prasaarit kiye jate the is kaarykram main or geeton ko lokapriyata kaa darja bhi nahin diya jaataa tha. san 1954 se binaaka gitamala count down kaarykram ban gaya or gaanon ko reating diya jane laga| us san ke pehle kaarykram main talat mahmud kaa gaaya geet ‘jaayen to jaayen kahan…..’ sheersh geet tha.
 lokapriyata hi mukhya aadhaar hota tha gaanon ke reating kaa| geeton ke record ke bikri ke aankade ekatrit kiye jate the lokapriyata ke nirdhaaran ke liye or shrotaaon ke mat bhi liye jate the| parantu baad main yah pataa chalane par ki kai gaanon ke liye shrotagan farji naamon ke anek mataptr bhej dete hai, voting kii paddati ko samaapt kar diya gaya|
 binaaka gitamala ke naam ko do baar badla gaya, pehle sibaka gitamala or baad main kolgate gitamala ke rup main| baad main sibaka gitamala kaa prasaaran radio vividh bharti se bhi kiya jane laga par vividh bharti main iskaa naam sibaka sangitamala hua karata tha.koi bhi gaanaa 25 baar bajane ke baad ritair kar diya jaataa tha, gaanaa bajane ke paaydaano kii sankhya ko jod kar uski reating vaarshik aadhaar par tay kii jaati thi tathaa disambar ke aakhri buudhvaar ko binaaka gitamala sartaaj geet bajataa tha.
