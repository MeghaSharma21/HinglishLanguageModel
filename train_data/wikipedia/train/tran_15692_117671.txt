 makka ek pramukh khaadya fasal hai, jo mote anaajo kii shrenee main aata he. ise bhutte kii shakl main bhi khaaya jaataa he.
 bharat ke adhikansh maidani bhaagon se lekar 2700 meter unchaai vaale pahari kshetron tak makka safaltaapurvak ugaaya jaataa he. ise sabhi prakaar kii mittiyon main ugaaya ja saktaa he tathaa balui, domat mitti makka kii kheti ke liye behatar samajhi jaati he. makka ek aisa khadyann he jo mote anaaj kii shrenee main aata to he parantu isaki paidaavaar pichley dashak main bharat main ek mahatvapurn fasal ke rup main mode le chuki he kyonki yah fasal sabhi mote va pramukh khaddano kii badhottaree dar main sabase agrani he. aaj jab gehun or dhan me upaj badhanaa kathin hota ja rahaa he, makka paidaavaar ke naye maanak prastut kar rahi he jo is samay badhkar 5.98 tak pahunch chukaa he.
 yah fasal bharat kii bhoomi par 1600 i0 ke ant main hi paidaa karnaa shuru kii gai or aaj bharat sansar ke pramukh utpaadak deshon main shaamil he. jitani prakaar kii makka bharat main utpann kii jaati he, shaayad hi kisi anya desh main utani prakaar kii makka utpaadit kii ja rahi he. haan yah baat or he ki bharat makka ke upyogo me kaaphi pichhada hua he. jabaki america main yah ek purnataya audyagik fasal ke rup main utpaadit kii jaati he or isse vividh audyagik padaarth banaaai jate he. bharat main makka kaa mahatva ek keval khadyann kii fasal ke rup me janaa jaataa he. sayukt rajya america me makka kaa adhiktam upyog starch banane ke liye kiya jaataa he.
 bharat main makka kii kheti jin rajyon main vyapak rup se kii jaati he ve hai - andhra pradesh, bihar, karnataka, rajasthan, uttar pradesh ityadi. iname se rajasthan main makka kaa sarvaadhik kshetrafal he va andhra main sarvaadhik utpaadan hota he. parantu makka kaa mahatv jammu kashmir, himachal, purvottar rajyon, madhya pradesh, chhattisgarh, maharashtra, gujarat va jharkhand main bhi kaaphi adhik he.
