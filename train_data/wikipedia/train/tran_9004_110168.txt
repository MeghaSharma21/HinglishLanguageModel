 raghav sachhar ek bhartiya gayak hai, inke pita aar. ke. sachhar or mata ushaa sachhar hai. vah teen bacchon main sabase chote hai. unhonen chaar saal kii umr se hi apana pehla vaadyayantra, hormonica, bajaanaa prarambh kar diya tha. tab se har saal unke mata-pita ne unhen ek vaadyayantra hi uphaar main dena prarambh kar diya. sangeet premiyon ke parivaar main janme raghav ko sangeet main bahut kam umr se hi dilchaspee thi. unhen 1994 main dilli main yojit pepsy kornukopia pratiyogita main sarvshereshth drum vadak kaa puraskaar jeeta, iske alaava unhen bits pilaani main sarvshereshth kii-board vadak kaa puraskaar bhi mila. unhonen apane mitron ke saath milakar ek band canzona kaa gathan kiya or uske pashchaat varsh 2000 main ve monaash conservetary of mujeec se sangeet kii shiksha lene ke liye melbourn, australia chale gaye.
 is samay ke dauraan raghav ne dakshin purv asia main taiwan, dakshin korea, honkong, singapore or malaysia jaise deshon kii yatra kii. bharat yatra ke dauraan unhone ekal rup se sangeet-sabhaayen kin or viva or shubha mudgal ke saath bhi kaary kiya, iske alaava unhonen chote geet evam filmon kaa shravy-bhaag bhi record kiye. unka yash kaaphi badha jab monaash main academik utkrushtataa ke liye unhen varsh 2000 evam 2001 main golden kii puraskaar evam arnest puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. raghav ab 32 se adhik upkarnon ko bajaate hai. unke nae album ke sheershak geet, charming luteraa ko bharat bhar main safalataa mili.unka nayaa kaary, jo ve xing ke saath mil ke kar rahe hai,  jai jagadish - rimix kii rachana he, jisamen unhonen 9 vaadya-yantra bajaaye hai.
