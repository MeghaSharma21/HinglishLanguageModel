 madhur kapila ek upanyaaskaar, patrkaar, or hindi sahitya evam kala kii sameekshak hai.
 madhur kapila jalandhar, punjab main paidaa hooyi thi. vah aajkal chandigad main rahati hai.
 madhur kapila 1977 se ek swatantra patrkaar or kala sameekshak hai. dainik tribune, dinmaan, punjab kesari, jansatta, hindi hindustan samachar or vibhinn saahitiyik patrikaon jaise hans, vartmaan sahitya, vagarth, nayaa gyanodae, dastak, iravati, harigandha, jagriti or kai or. inamen inaki laghu kathaayen prakashit huin. dainik tribune main saahitiyik kollam "kala kshetreya" – ek saptahik kala or sahitya kollam. dainik tribune main upanyaas saatvaan swar dharawahik ke rup main prakashit. 
 vah teen dashakon se chandigarh sangeet naatak academy kii sadsy hai. unki kahaniyaan punjab vishvavidhyalay chandigarh or kurukshetra vishvavidhyalay main kai piechdi or emphil shodh kaa vishay rahi hai.
 madhur kapila kii pehali kahani 1960 main vir pratap main prakashit kii gayi thi. us samay se unke teen upanyaas prakashit hue – bhatke raahi, saatvaan swar or saamne kaa aasmaan; or teen laghu kahani sangrah – bichon bich, tab shaayad or ek muqadama or.
 2011 main madhur kapila ko hindi sahitya main utkrisht yogadan ke liye chandigarh sahitya academy puraskaar prastut kiya gaya.
