 pech nadi purvi afghanistan main sthit ek nadi he. yah nuristaan praant ke madhya bhaag main hindu kush parvaton kii barf or himanion se shuru hokar dakshin-dakshinpurv disha main kunar praant main pravesh karti he or prantiya rajdhani asadabad ke paas kunar nadi main vilay ho jaati he. nadi kii bahut si bai or dai upanadiyaan hai jo kai vaadiyaan banati hai jinamein chapa daraa, vegal, korangal, vatapur or sharaya shaamil hai.
 pech nadi or uski upadiyon kii ghaatiyon main pashto bolne vaale pashtun log bahusankhyak hai or yahan unka saafi qabeela basaa hua he. iske alaava yahan nuristaani log bhi rahete hai or ashkunu bhashaa bhi boli jaati he, jo ek nuristaani bhashaa he.
