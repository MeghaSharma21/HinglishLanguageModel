 casimir phunk poland ke jeevarsayanajna the.
 inkaa janm warsaw main 23 farvari, 1884 i. ko hua. inhone switzraland ke bern vishvavidhyalay, paris ke paister institute or berlin vishvavidhyalay main shiksha praapt kii. jeevarsayanajna ke rup main inhone aspataalon main kaary kiya. ye san 1915 main america gaye or inhone vahaan kii kai anusandhanshaon main vibhinn padon par kaary kiya.
 vitamin kaa anveshan or uski upyogita ko siddh karne ke kaaran inhen prasiddhi mili. inhone pratham vishwayuddh main adrenelin yogic kaa vyapaarik str par utpaadan kiya tathaa machhlee ke tel se vyapaarik str par vitamin nikaalane kii vidhi nikaali. 1917 se 1923 i. tak ye ech.e. metz anusandhanshala ke nirdeshak or neuyark main colombia ke kaay-shalya-chikitsa college main pravaktaa rahe. 1936 i. main sanyukt rajya vitamin karporation ke salaahkaar pad par niyukt hue. 1947 i. main inhone neuyork main phunk foundation chikitsa anusandhan kii sthapana kii.
