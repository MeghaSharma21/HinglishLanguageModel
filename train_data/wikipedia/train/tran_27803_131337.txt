  raajnneeti praveshdwar
 islamic ganraajya pakistan main paanch ucch nyaayalay hai, jinamein se chaar prayek praant ke mukhyalay main sthit hai. pakistan ne paanchaven nyaayalay sangheey rajdhani kshetra ke liye paarit kiya he jo islamabad main sthit he. paanchaven nyaayalay kii yojana lahore prantiy nyaayalay ne roak laga di thi, or is faisle ko pakistan kii nyaayalay ne 24 disambar 2007 par roak laga di. 
 ucch nyaayalayon ko niyuktiyon kii prakriya, ucchtam nyaayalayon main niyuktiyon ke samaan he. 18 wein samvidhan sanshodhan karne se pehle, ucch nyaayalay niyuktiyaan supreme court kii niyukti kii tarah hi aalochanaon kaa saamana karnaa padaa. vartmaan niyuktiyon supreme court main niyukti ke rup main ek hi tareeke se kiya jaataa he. lekin bajaay 4 ucchtam nyaayalay ke nyaayadhishon, 4 sabase varisht ucch nyaayalay ke nyaayadhishon, prantiy kanoon mantri or prantiy baar council ke ek sadsy, nyaayik ayog main baithne ke ucch nyaayalay ke nyaayadhishon kii niyukti ke bare main faisla karte hai.
