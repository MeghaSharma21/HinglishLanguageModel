 pratidravya 
 parishunyan kisi vastu ke sarvanaash athwa purnatah samaapt hone kii avasthaa kaa naam he. sanskritnisht shabd parishunyan kaa shabdic arth param rup se shunya hona.
 bhautik vigyaan main, yah shabd saamanytah apaparmanvik kanon kii unke pratikanon se hone vaali takkar main ghatit ghatana kii prakriya ko nirupit karne ke liye prayukt kiya jaataa he. iskaa ek udhaaharan chitra main dikhaaya gaya he jahaan ek electron apane pratikan positron ke saath parishunyan karke ek photone yugma detaa he.
