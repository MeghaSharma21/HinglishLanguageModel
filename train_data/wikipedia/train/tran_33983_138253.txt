 kaala junalgala purvajagat kaa ek junalgala he jo asia ke garam kshetron main bhartiya upmahadweep, china, indonishya, australia. ityadi main paaya jaataa he. aamtaur par yah sthaneey he lekin kuch uttar main rahane vaale kaale junalgale choti duriyon par pravaas karte hai.
 yah iksobraekas naamak junangalon ke jeevvaigyanik vansh kii sabase bade aakaar kii jati he or 58 semi lambi ho sakati he. yah apni lambi gardan or lambi pili chonch se pahachaani jaati he. iske shishu gadhe kaaki rang ke hote hai lekin bade hokar inkaa upree bhaag lagbhag puraa kaala ho jaataa he. gardan dai-bai ore se pili hoti he or dhad kaa nichalaa hissaa bhuri lakero vaala shwet hota he.
 kaale junalgale apane ande sarkandon vaale daldali kshetron main dete hai jahaan ve sarkandon ke gucchhon, jhaadiyon yaa vrikshon par ghonsle banaate hai. maadaa har varsh teen se paanch ande detii he. zamin par yah chhupakar bina adhik dhwani kare chalate hai isle kam dekhe jate hai lekin udate hue inkaa upar-se-kaala neeche-se-shwet sharir aasaani se pahachaanaa jaataa he. ve kiton, machhliyon or ubhaycharon ko aahaar banaate hai.
