 pragash teen kashmiri muslim ladakiyon dwaara banaaya gaya ek aadhunik sangeet-samooh tha jiskaa gathan disambar 2012 main hua tha. in pratibhavan ladakiyon ke is band ko kaaphi prasiddhi mili lekin thode samay pashchaat hi in par islam ke niyam todane ke aarope main aatankwaadiyon se dhamkiyaan milane lagi. inke viruddh srinagar ke mufti ne fatwa bhi jari kiya, jiske parinamswaroop unhone ise band karne kaa nirnay le liya.
