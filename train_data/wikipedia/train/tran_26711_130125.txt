 amarah dakshinpurvi iraq kaa ek shahar he or us desh ke maysaan praant kii rajdhani he. baghdad se dakshin main sthit yah shahar dajala nadi ke kinare basaa hua he or iran kii sarhad se lagbhag 50 kimi dur he. yahan ke adhiktar log shiya muslim hai. iske aasapaas kaa kshetra ek krishi-pradhan ilaaka he or yah apni buni hui vastuon ke liye or chaandi ke kaam ke liye janaa jaataa he. isaki sthapana 1860 ke dashak main osmani samrajya kii ek faujee chhavnee ke rup main hui thi.
