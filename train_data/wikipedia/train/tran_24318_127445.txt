 phool waalon kii saiyr matlab "phuulavaalon kaa juluus", dilli ke phool vikritaon dwaara ek vaarshik utsav he. yah teen divasiy tyohaar he, mahrauli or dilli ke kshetra main barsaat ke mausam ke thik baad, aam taur se sitambar ke maheene main yojit kiya jaataa he. yah tyohaar dilli kii mishrit samskruti ko udhaaharan ke rup main dekha jaataa he, jo shahar main sampradayik ekta kaa maahaul banane main sahaayta karata he, or aaj bhi tyohaar ko samaan rup se hindu or musalmaan donon ke dwaara manaaya jaataa he.
 is dharmanirpeksh tyohaar main shehanai waalon or nartkiyon ke netritv main ek juluus, or bade pushp pankhe chalaanaa shaamil hai.
