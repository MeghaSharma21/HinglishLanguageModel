 yogeshwari kaa arth durga hota he. yogijan isaki upasna karte hai. yogeshwari devi kaa varnan matsay puraana main miltaa he. 
 dushton ke sanhaar ke liye atyant tikshn khadg uske haath main rahata he or rudraksh kii maala vah dhaaran karti he. unki jihva lambii he, tikshn dadh he, bhayankar mukh he, kesh unke upar ko uthe hue hai. kanth main vah narkapaal or asthiyon kii maala pahenti hai. unke vam hast main rakt or maance se bharaa hua khappar he or dakshin hast main vah barchhi dhaaran kiye hai. unke teen netra hai. unka udar andar ko ghusaa hua he.
