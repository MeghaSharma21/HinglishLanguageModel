 mukhya aksh ek dirghavritt kaa sabase lanbaa vyas he, ek rekha jo centre or donon nabhiyon se hokar guzarati he, iske chor aakaar ke sarvaadhik duuri ke bindu he. mukhya aksh kaa aadha arddh-mukhya aksh he or is tarah naabhi se hokar centre se dirghavritt ke kinare tak jaati he; anivaary rup se, yah kaksha ke sabase duurastha binduon se lee gai, kaksha kii trigya kii ek maap he. vrutt ke vishesh maamle main, arddh-mukhya aksh ek trigya he. ek tarah se soche to arddh-mukhya aksh ek dirghavritt kii sabase lambi trigya he.
