 mahamad pratham, mahamad chalbi, kirishji, osmania samrajya ke sultan or duusare sansthaapak the. ve baikeid pratham or daulat khatun ke putr the.
 ankare ke yuddh main baikeid yildaram kii amir taimur ke haathon paraajay or osmania samrajya ke tukdon main vibhajit hone ke baad mahamad pratham ne adarna main takht sambhala or rajdhani bursa se adarna sthanantarit karke samrajya ko samaapt hone se bachaaya or vidrohon ko khatm karne ke saath-saath albania samet kai ilaaqon par vijay hasil karke apane samrajya main sammilit kiye.
 mahamad pratham kii umr nidhan ke vaqt keval 40 saal thi or unhone sirf 8 saal tak shashan kiya. unke mazaar bursa kii hari masjid main sthit he.
