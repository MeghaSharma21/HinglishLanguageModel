  talakona bharat ke andhra pradesh pradesh ke chitoor jile main sthit ek jalprapaat he. is jal prapaat kii oonchaai 270 foot he.
 chitoor jile ke yerrawaripalem mandal ke nerabelu gaanv main sthit he. 
 yah shri venkateshwar abhayaarany main sthit he, jo tirupati-thirumla ke qareeb he. yah ek van praant main sthit he. yahaan ke romantic drishy manamoh lete hai. paryaatkon kaa tantaa laga rahata he. yahaan ek mandir bhi he jisko siddheshwar swami mandir bhi kaha jaataa he.
 yahaan ke jangalon main gilahariyaan, hiran, chita, sambhar ityadi jaanavar hai. kai jadi bootiyon kaa bhi gehvaara he. fal phool, paude, ped se bharaa jangal he, jo ek abhayaarany he. yahaan chandan ke vriksh bhi kaafii maatra main hai. aushadhi paude or vriksh bhi hai. 
 talakona kii jangalon main "taani coster", "stachitarpheta jamecensis"
 orchid
 rustic titali
 mallroom
 pili ghasi titali
