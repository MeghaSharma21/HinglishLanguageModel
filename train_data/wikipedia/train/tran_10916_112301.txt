 aushadhiy mitti kaa prayog pragaitihaasik kaal se chalaa aa rahaa he. vishwa ke vibhinn bhaagon ke adivasee ab bhi mitti kaa bahutayat main upyog karte hai.
 chikitsa ke liye kai prakaar kii mittiyaan prayog kii jaatin hai. ye praayah sharir ke baahar hi upyog main laayi jaati hai . kintu kuch aantarik upyog bhi kiye jate hai. chikitsa ke liye prayukt pramukh mitti kii kismein hai - bentonite tathaa multani mitti
