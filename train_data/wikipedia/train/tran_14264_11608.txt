 hasan ali khan pune ke ek mashhoor ghoda vyapaaree hai, jinhen sambhawatah bharat ke sabase bade tex chori or hawala ke cese main giraftaar kiya gaya. iskar vibhaag ke anusaar hasan ali or uske sahyogiyon se 71 hajaar 845 karod rupee tex bakaayaa tha, jo ki us saal ke puure bharat ke health bajet or salanaa vasule jane vaale sarvice tex se zyada tha.
 pravartan nideshaalay kii poochtacha main hasan ali ne khulaasa kiya ki uske acount main jamaa hajaaron karod rupye main se ek bada hissaa desh ke kai bade netaaon or naukarshahon kaa he. in bade netaaon main maharashtra ke teen purv mukhyamantri bhi shaamil hai. idi ne in mukhyamantriyon or netaaon ke naam kaa khulaasa nahin kiya. uske sahyogi kashinath tapuria ne khulaasa kiya ki 2005-2006 ke bank statement ke anusaar khan ke khate main do arab dollar jamaa the.
 sarkari vakil ne adaalat main kaha ki hasan ali ke antarrashtriya hathiyaar vyavsaayee adnan khashogi se sambandh hai. idi dwaara daayar aaropapatr ke anusaar, khan khashogi ke liye kaam kar rahaa tha. khashogi ko switsraland main 'avaanchit vyakti' qaraar de diya gaya tha. isliye khan kaa ubies khate kaa istemaal hathiyaar taskar khashogi ke dhan ko jamaa karne ke liye kiya jaataa tha.
