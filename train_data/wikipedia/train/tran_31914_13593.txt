 bijganit, bhaskar dwitiya kii rachana he. isamen bijganit kii charchaa he. yah siddhantashiromani kaa dwitiya bhaag he. anya bhaag hai - leelavati, grahaganit tathaa goladhyay.
 is granth main bhaskaracharya ne anirdharya dwighat samikarnon ke hal kii chakravaal vidhi di he. yah vidhi jayadev kii vidhi kaa bhi parishkrut rup he. jayadev ne brahmagupta dwaara di gayi anirdharya dwighat samikarnon ke hal kii vidhi kaa samanyikaran kiya tha.
 yah vishwa kii pehali pustak he jisamen spasht ullekh he ki dhanatmak sankhyaon ke do vargamul hote hai.
 isamen baarah adhyay hai.
 is granth me nimnlikhit upavishay hai-
