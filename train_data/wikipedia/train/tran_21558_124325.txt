 pramaatra yantriki main pramaatra uljhaav us sthiti ko kehate hai jab do vastuon ke ek-duusare par koi bhautik prabhaav padane ke baad unako alag karne par bhi un donon kii paristhiti main ek pramaatra sambandh rahata he. yadi ek vastu ke kisi kwantem gun ko mapa jaae to duusari vastu usee kshan us se vipareet gun dhaaran kar leti he. masalan agar do vidyudanuon main pramaatra uljhaav paidaa kar ke unhen ek-duusare se milo dur kar diya jaae or phir unamem se ek kaa spin mapa jaae to duusare kaa spin usee kshan uskaa vipareet ho jayega. vigyaan yah sabit kar chukaa he ke kisi vidyudanu main tab tak spin pakkaa nahin hota jab tak mapa jaae. iskaa arth yah hua ke kisi agyaat prakriya ke zariye duusare vidyudanu ko gyaat ho jaataa he ke pehle kaa spin jaanchaa gaya he.
 angrezi main "pramaatra yantriki" ko "kwantem mekanix" or "pramaatra uljhaav" ko "kwantem antangalament" kehete hai.
 haalaanki pramaatra uljhaav kaa siddhant sabase pehle albert oinstin ne kuch anya vaigyanikon ke saath milakar hi niklaa tha, phir bhi in vastuon ke bich main aise jaadooi se lagane vaale sampark ko vah kabhi bhi sweekaar nahin kar paae or is siddhant ko galat sabit karne kii koshish main jute rahe. ve gusse se pramaatra uljhaav ko "dur se ho rahi bhutiya harakat" kaa naam dete the. unhone apane saapekshata siddhant main daawa kiya tha ke koi bhi chiz prakaash kii gati se tej nahin chal sakati or is kshanik sampark ko vah maan nahin paae. unka prayaas asafal rahaa or vaigyanik prayogon ne pramaatra uljhaav ke siddhant ko sahi sabit kar diya he. dhyaan rahe ke is siddhant ke zariye koi vastu ek sthaan se duusare sthaan nahin bheji ja sakati or na hi koi sandesh bheja ja saktaa he.
