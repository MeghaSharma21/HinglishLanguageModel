 uttaripura ,ek gaanv he jo bharat main uttar pradesh praant ke kanpur jile kii tehsil bilhor main sthit he. uttaripura kii jansankhya 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar 2354 aanki gayi thi. yahan kii pramukh bhashaa hindi he. yah kshetra kanpur shahar or jile se 42 kilometre kii duuri pe sthit he.
 yah kshetra laiya ke vyapaar ke liye pure bharat varsh main prasidd he. varsh ke sabhi maheenon main yahan hameshaa laiya kaa vyapaar chalata rahata he. yahan ke kisaano kii mukhya fasal aalu he. kisaan ko aalu rakhane ke liye 2 sheet-grih uplabdh hai. is gaanv main vartmaan main uttaripura chaowkee bhi sthit he. yah gaanv jiti road ke kinare sthit he. is gaanv ke paas main dudwa ,makrand nivaada, maan nivaada or bhi kai gaanv sthit he. is gaanv main rahen vaale bhartiya janata party ke yuva netaa avinaash shukla ne joki ek sanghathan "hindu kranti dal" ke pradesh adhyaksh hai bataaya kii yah gaanv adhik maatra main failaa hua he yahan pe chunaav main do gaanv milakar vot daalate hai uttari or chetpurva. bhartiya janata party ke netaa avinaash shukla ne bataaya he kii is gaanv kii pragati din pratidin chal rahi he jiske liye gaanv ke sabhi nivasee purna rup se saraahneey hai.
 vikash kaa dar din pratidin behatar ho rahaa he or sakshartaa kaa str bhi badhata ja rahaa he |
