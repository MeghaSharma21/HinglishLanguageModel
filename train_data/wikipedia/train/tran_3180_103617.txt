 sudas bharat main ek pracheen raja the jinka ullekh rugveda main miltaa he. ve dasharajna yuddh ke vijeta the jiskaa varnan rugveda 7:18, 7:33 or 7:83:4-8 main he. unka jeevankaal alag-alag sroton main 1700 isapurv se lekar 1100 isapurv ke bich ke kisi samay main anumaanit kiya jaataa he.
 'sudas' ke naam ke arth par kuch vivaad he. vaidic sanskrit main iske do sambhav matlab hai:
 sudas hind-aaryon ke 'bharat' naamak samudaay ke 'tritsu' naamak qabeele ke naresh the. ve divodas ke putr the, jo swayan srunjay ke putr the. srunjay ke pita kaa naam devvat tha. dasharajna yuddh ke baad bharat samudaay kaa hind-arya logon main bolbalaa ho gaya or aage chalakar yahi puure desh kaa naam bhi pad gaya. us kaal main rajnatik vyavastha ganatantric qabeelon se parivartit hokar raajaaon par kendrit bhi ho rahi thi. dasharajna yuddh main vijayi bharat qabeela raja-pratha par aadhaarit tha jabaki unke virodh main khadey 10 qabeele lagbhag sabhi loktantric the.
