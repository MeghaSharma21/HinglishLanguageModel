 8 november gregory calendar ke anusaar varsh kaa 312wa din he. saal me abhi or 53 din baaki he.
 1895 bijli ke saath prayog karte samay wilhem rantgan ne eks re kii khoj kii.
 1917 valdameer lenin, liyo trotski or joseph stallin ko 1917 kii oktober kranti ke baad pipuls kamisar adhikrut kiya. 
 1923- bear haul kranti. munich main adolf hitler ne german sarkaar ko apdasth karne kii naajiyon kii ek koshish kaa netritv kiya. senaaon ne naajiyon ke is prayaas ke asafal kar diya.
 1933 mandi ke baad america raashtrapati frenclin roosevelt ne 40 lakh logon kaa rojgaar dene ke liye civil works administration kaa gathan kiya.
 1960 jon epa kennedy sangharmpurn mukaable main richard niksan ko bahut kam antar se haraa kar america ke 35 wein raashtrapati bane.
 2002 iraq main nishaakaran ke liye sanyukt raashtra sangh ne prastaav paarit kiya. jisamen tatkaaleen iraqi raashtrapati saddaam hossain ko kaha gaya ki ve hathiyaar khatm karen yaa mushkilon kaa saamana karen.
 2003 iraq ke saath yuddh main das hajaar se zyada america sainikon ne iraq ke shahar faluja kii gheraabandi kii.
