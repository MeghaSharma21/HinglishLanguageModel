 bhabhi ji ghar par he! ek bhartiya dharawahik he. iskaa prasaaran and tivi par 2 march 2015 se somvaar se shuukravaar raat 10:30 baje se hua.
 yah kahani kanpur ke do padosi pariwaron kii he. donon hi parivaar ke pati ko duusare kii patni se pyaar ho jaataa he or vah unase milane ke liye bahaane talash karte rahete hai. vah sabhi galat tareeqe sabit ho jaataa hai or vah un sabhi main faste rahete hai.
 har episod main isaki alag kahani hoti hai jisame manmohan tiwari kachche baniyine ke vyapaaree he, jabaki vibhuti mishra berozegaar pati he jinaki patni anitha mishra grouming claces lekar ghar chalati he. manmohan tiwari kii patni ek gharelu mahila he jo ghar ke kaaryo main lagi rahati he.
