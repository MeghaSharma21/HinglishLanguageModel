 santiago de compostela khudamukhatyar sanghathan gaalisia kii rajdhani he. is shahir kii shuruyaat sant jemz kii samaadh ke saath shuru hua jo ab ek bada girjaghar he. 1985 main is shahir ke puraane kasbe ko unesco dwaara vishwa viraasat sthaan ghoshit kiya gaya.
 yah shahir chauthi sadi main ek roman kabristaan tha. 
 2012 main is shahir kii jansankhya 95,671 thi.
 santiago de compostela kii garmiyaan thandi or sukhi hoti hai or sardiyaan thandi or geeli hoti hai.
