 manushyon kii anuvanshiki main matruvansh samooh aar yaa mitocandria-diene hapalogrup R ek matruvansh samooh he. yah matruvansh samooh or isaki badi upashakhaayen uresia, oshiania or mahamerica main faili hui hai.
 anumaan he ke jis stri se yah matruvansh shuru hua vah aaj se lagbhag 66,000 varsh pehle bhartiya upmahadweep kii nivasee thi.
 dhyaan den ke kabhi-kabhi matruvanshon or pitruvanshon ke naam milte-julate hote hai, lekin yah keval ek ittefaq hi he - inkaa aapas main koi sambandh nahin he.
 angrezi main "vansh samooh" ko "hapalogrup", "pitruvansh samooh" ko "waahe chromosome hapalogrup" or "matruvansh samooh" ko "amtidiene hapalogrup" kehete hai.
