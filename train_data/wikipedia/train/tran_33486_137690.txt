 hypercementosis daanton kii ek agyatahetuk,gaiyr navtpadit sthiti he jo 1 yaa 1 se adhik daanton kii jado par cementem ke jamaa hone se hoti he. daanton ke sire par cementem kii moti parat chadh jane se daant saadhaaran daanton se bade dikhaai dete hai. 
 isse daanton main ek ajeeb sa ubhar aa jaataa he jo kii daanton ko nishkrut karne main bhi asuvidha detaa he. iske ho jane se daanton ke apical foramen main rakt kii aapurti nahin ho pati jiske kaaran pulp gal jaataa he. 
 hypercementosis bahut se kaaran se hota he, uname se kuch nimna he:
 hypercementosis hone se daanton main asuvidhajanak sansani hoti he jo kii baad main danta ke dard main parivartit ho jaati he.
