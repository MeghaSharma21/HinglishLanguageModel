 perth australia ke rajya pashchimi australia kii rajdhani or sabase adhik jansankhya vaala shahar he. aabaadi ke hisaab se yah australia kaa chauthaa sabase bada shahar he. isaki sthapana 12 june 1829 ko capton james sterling ne kii thi. perth kii vartmaan jansankhya 1.74 million he.
 mahanagareey kshetra, australian dakshin pashchim divijan ke hind mahaasaagar tathaa ek kam tateey kataav jise darling range ke rup main janaa jaataa he, ke madhya main stith he. perth shahar ke kendriya vyapaar jile evam vibhinn upnagar hans nadi ke kinare stith hai. vyapaar or prashasan centre or sampannataa main lagaataar vruddhi hone se perth ko "city of lite" ke sammaan se nawaja gaya he. san 2011 main " di iconomist " kii suchi main perth ko aanthva sthaan praapt hua.
