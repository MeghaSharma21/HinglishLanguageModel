 pune ke laal mahal pune, bharat main sthit sabase prasiddh smarakon main se ek he. varsh 1630 isvi, shivaji ke pita shahji bhosle main apni patni jijabai or bete ke liye laal mahal kii sthapana kii. vah apni pehali kile par kabja kar liya jab tak shivaji kai varshon ke liye yahaan ruke the.mul laal mahal khandhar main gir gai or vartmaan laal mahal mul ke punarnirmaan he or pune shahar ke centre main sthit he. apni pehali patni ke saath shivaji kii shaadi, maharani Saibai laal mahal main jagah le lee.
