 padmaja nidu bhartiya raajnneetijna sarojini naidu kii suputree thim. unhone apni maan kii tarah bharat ke hiton ke liye khud ko samarpit kar diya tha. keval ikkis varsh kii umr main ve bharat kii rashtriya raajnneeti main pravesh kar kai thim, jab unhen bhartiya rashtriya congress kii hyderabad ikai kaa sanyukt sansthaapak banaaya gaya. unhone videshi saamaanon ke bahishkaar karne or khaadi ko apnaane hetu logon ko prerit karne kaa sandesh diya or samarpit abhiyaan main shaamil hui. ve 1942 main bharat chodo andolan main bhaag lene ke kaaran jail bhi gai. swatantrata ke pashchaat ve pashchim bengal kii rajyapal baniin. unhone aadhi sadi se bhi zyada saarvajanik jivan jiya, is dauraan ve red cross se bhi judin or 1971 se 1972 tak ve isaki adhyaksh bhi raheen.
 unke naam par darjeeling main padmaja naidu himalayan praani udyaan he.
