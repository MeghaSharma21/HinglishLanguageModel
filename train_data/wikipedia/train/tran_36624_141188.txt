 kwantum sankhyikee tathaa sankhyikeey bhautikee main avilaganeey kanon kaa sanchay keval do vivikt urja pravasthaon main rah rakata he. isamen se ek kaa naam bose-ainstin sankhyikee he. lager tathaa gharshanhin atitaral hilium ke vyavhaar isi sankhyikee ke parinaam hai. is vyavhaar kaa siddhanth 1924-25 main satyendra nath basu or albert oinstin ne viksit kiya tha. 'avilaganeey kanon' se matlab un kanon se he jinaki urja avasthaaen bilkul samaan hon.
 yah sankhyikee unhi kanon par lagu hoti he jo jo pauli ke apvarjan siddhant ke anusaar nahin chalate, arthath anekon kan ek saath ek hi 'kwantum state' main rah sakate hai. aise kanon kaa chakran kaa maan purnank hota he tathaa unhen boson kehete hai. 
 yah sankhyikee 1920 main satyendranath bose dwaara pratipaadit kii gayi thi or photaanon ke sankhyikeey vyavhaar ko bataane ke liye thi. ise san 1924 main oinstin ne samanyikrut kiya jo kanon par bhi lagu hoti he.
 sankhyikeey rup se, ooshmaagtiya samya kii dasha main, Ei urja vaale kanon kii sankhya ni nimnlikhit sambandh ke anusaar hogi-

 adhik taapmaana par kwantem prabhaav adrushya hone lagata he or tab bose-oinstin sankhyikee, maxwell-boltman sankhyikee kii taraf agrasar hone lagati he. kintu kam taap par donon sankhyikee alag-alag rahati hai.
