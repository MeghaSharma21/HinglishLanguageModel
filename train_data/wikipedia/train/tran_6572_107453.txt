 bastariya batalian bharat ke kendriya rigerv police bal kii ek batalian he. is batalian ko naksaliyon se ladane ke liye banaaya gaya he jisamen mukhya rup se adivasee yuva shaamil he. is batalian ke liye bijapur, dantewada, narayanpur or sukama jile se jawan kii bhartee hui he. bastaaria batalian main kul 543 jawan hai, jisamen 189 mahilaayein shaamil hai.
