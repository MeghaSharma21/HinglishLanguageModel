 bihar anjuman kii sthapana shakeel ahamed, nayyar ahsan aadi diggajo ne san 11 march 1999 ko dubai, sanyukt arab imaraat main daali thi. jo san 1986 se abu dhabi me sakriy anjuman islamia, bihar kaa ek vistaar he. iskaa uddeshy muslim samaaj vishesh rup se bihar or jharkhand ke pichhade musalamaanon ke kalyaan ke liye sampadaaon kaa ekikaran karnaa evam sahaayta me haath bataana he. yah ek laabh rahit sanstha he jiskaa kisi bhi raajnneetik or dharmik sanstha se koi sambandh nahi he. anjuman purna rup se sthaneey kanoon ke anukaran ke liye vachanabaddh he.
