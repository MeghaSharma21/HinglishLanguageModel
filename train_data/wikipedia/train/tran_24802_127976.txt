 maharashtra rajya yaa marathi vyanjan yaa marathi khaanaa bhartiya rajya maharashtra ke marathi logon kaa vyanjan he. yahan par bhojan main kai prakaar ke vishisht vyanjan banaaye jate hai. paramparaagat rup se, maharashtriyon ne apane bhojan ko dusron kii tulana main adhik drudh mana he.
 maharashtra ke vyanjan main halke or masaaledaar vyanjan shaamil hai. gehun, chaaval, jowar, bajra, subjiyan, masoor or fal aahaar shaamil hai . moongfalee or kaashew aksar subjiyon ke saath parose jate hai. aarthik paristhitiyon or samskruti kii vajah se maance kaa paaramparik rup se upyog kiya jaataa he.
 mumbai, pune or anya jaise metropolitan shaharon main shahari aabaadi videshon ke anya hisson se prabhavit hue he. udhaaharan ke liye, udupi vyanjan, idli or dosa, saath hi chinis or pashchimi vyanjan, jo aaj gharelu khaanon main or restaraan main kaaphi lokapriy hue hai.
 vishisht rup se maharashtra ke khaanon main urad ke modak, aalu patton kii sabji or thali peeth shaamil hai .
