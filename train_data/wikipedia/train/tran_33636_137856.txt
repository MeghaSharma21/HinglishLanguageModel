 magistor degree athwa magistor oopaadhi  ucch shiksha main kaam aane vaali ek academy oopaadhi he.
 magistor oopaadhi europe main madhyakaalin vishvavidhyalayon main armbh hui or prarambh main doctoret ke samaan hoti thi; haalanki doctoret kii oopaadhi mul rup se dharamshastra, vidhi evam aushadhi main milti thi jabaki magistor kii oopaadhi kala ke vishay main milti thi. kuch deshon main, yah oopaadhi aaj tak bhi apane mul rup main chalati he jabaki anya deshon main magistor thodi nimna str kii oopaadhi ban gayi he, kuch sthanon par yah master kii oopaadhi ke samaanaantar chal rahi he.
