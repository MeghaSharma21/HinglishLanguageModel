 bruneii dollar, 1967 se bruneii sultanate kii mudra he. ise saamanytah dollar chihn $ yaa vaikalpik rup se anya dollar mudraon se alag karne ke liye B$ ke saath darshaya jaataa he. bruneii dollar athurity monatory bruneii darusallaam ke dwaara jari kiya jaataa he.
 1967 ke mudra vinimay adhinium ke antargat yah singapore dollar se badla ja saktaa he. jis prakaar se prathagat nivida ke liye bruneii dollar singapore main maanya he; usee prakaar singapore dollar bruneii main bhi bhugtan ke liye maanya he.
