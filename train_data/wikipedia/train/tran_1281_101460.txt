 najivaad, german taanashaah adolf hitler kii vichaar dhara thi. yah vichaardhaara sarkaar or aam jan ke bich ek naye se rishte ke paksh main thi. is ke anusaar sarkaar kii har yojana main pahal ho parantu phir vah yojana janata-samaaj kii bhaagidaari se chale. kattar german rashtrawaad, deshprem, videshi virdohi, arya or german hit is vichaar dhara ke mul ang he. naji yahudiyon se sakht nafrat karte thea or europe or germany main har buraai ke liye unhen hi doshi maanate the. naaziyon ne centre main apni sarkaar banate hi germany main hitler kii taanaashaahee sthapic kii or phir yahudiyon ke germany main din bhar gayi. dwitiya vishwa yuddh main yahudiyon ke qatle-aam ke peeche bhi naaziyon kaa hi haath tha.
