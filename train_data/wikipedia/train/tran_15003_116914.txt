 bharat ke mahaan ganitagya bhaskar pratham ne apane 'mahabhaskareey' naamak granth main trikoshmetiya falan jya ya kaa maan nikaalane kaa ek parimey vyanjak diya he. yah pataa nahin he ki bhaskar ne yah sanniktan sutara kaise nikaala hoga. kintu ganit ke anekon itihaaskaaron ne apane-apane anumaan lagaaye hai ki bhaskar ne yah sutara kis prakaar nikaala hoga. yah sutara sundar evam sahaj he tathaa iske dwaara Sin x kaa paryaapt shuddh maan praapt hota he.
 mahabhaskareey main aath adhyay hai. saatven adhyay ke shloke 17, 18 or 19 main unhone sin x kaa sannikat maan nikaalane kaa nimnlikhit sutara diya he-
 is sutara ko unhone aryabhatta dwaara diya hua bataaya he. is sutara se praapt jya ya ke maanon kaa aapekshik truti 1.9% se kam he.
