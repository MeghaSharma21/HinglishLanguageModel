 adhipadap ve paudhe hote hai, jo aashray ke liye vrikshon par nirbhar hote hai lekin paraseete nahin hote. ye vrikshon ke tane, shakhaaen, daraaron, cotaron, chaal aadi main upasthit mitti main upaj jate hai va usee main apni jaden chipka kar rakhte hai. kai kismon main to viviya jaden bhi paayi jaati hai jinamein velamen utak hote hai. ye utak vaayu se bhi nami avashoshit kar lete hai.
 ye paudhe usee vriksh se nami evam poshan khinchate hai. iske alaava varsha, vaayu yaa aasapaas ekatrit jaev malbe se bhi poshan lete hai. ye adhipadap poshan chakra kaa bhaag hote hai or jis kaa bhaag hote hai, us paristhitiki kii vividhta evam biomas, donon main hi yogadan dete hai. ye kai prajatiyon ke liye khaadya kaa mahatvapurn strot bhi hote hai. vrikshon ke visheshkar puraane bhaagon par adhik adhipadap paye jate hai.
