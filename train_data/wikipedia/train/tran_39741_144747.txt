 shishumaar yaa dreko taramandal khagoleey gole ke uttari bhaag main dikhne vaala ek taramandal he, jiske taare prithvi ke uttari golardh main rahane vaale bahut se sthanon par paridhruviya hai . yah antarrashtriya khagoleey sangh dwaara jari kii gai 88 taramandalon kii suchi main shaamil he or duusari shatabdi isvi main tolmi ne jin 48 taramandalon kii suchi banaai thi unamem bhi shaamil tha.
 shishumaar bhartiya upmahadweep kii pracheen sabhyataa main ek magaramchh jaise jalawaasi jaanavar kaa naam tha or is taramandal ko pracheen khagolashastri aisi hi akruti main dekha karte the. pashchim main ise ek andahe ke rup main dekha jaataa tha or iskaa angrezi naam "dreko" bhi unani bhashaa ke "dracon" se liya gaya he.
 shishumaar taramandal main 14 mukhya taare hai, haalanki vaise isamen 76 taron ko bair naam die ja chuke hai. inamen se 9 ke ird-gird gair-saureey grah parikrama karte hue paae gaye hai. is taramandal ke mukhya taare or anya vaastuen is prakaar hai -
