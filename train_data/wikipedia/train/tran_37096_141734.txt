 kunind bharat kaa ek prakhyat pracheen jansamooh jiskaa pehali or chauthi shatee i. ke bich apana mahatvapurn ganraajya tha. mahabharat main iskaa ullekh paishaach, anbashth or barbar naamak parvatiy jatiyon ke saath hua he or kaha gaya he ki ve shailod nadi ke donon taton par nivas karte the. unka pradesh kaaphi vistrut tha or unke kai sau kul the. unhone yudhisthir ko rajasuya yajna ke samay pipilika suvarna bhent kiya tha. kunidon kaa ullekh ramayana or puraanon main bhi hua he. varahamihir ke kathananusaar ke uttarpurv ke nivasee the. unhone inkaa ullekh kashmir, kulut or sarindh ke saath kiya he. taalami ne kii inaki charchaa kii he. uske kathananusaar ye log vipasha, shatadru, yamuna or ganga nadiyon ke udgam pradesh main rahete the. is prakaar saahitiyik sutron ke anusaar ye log himalay ke punjab or uttarapradesh se sate nichle hisse main rahete the. sambhavat: kumayun or gadhwal kaa kshetra inke adhikaar main tha.
 in logon ke ganraajya ke jo sikke mili hai unase gyaat hota he ki ve log apana shashan bhagavan chithreshwar ke naam par karte the. chithreshwar shiv: kaa mandir kumaun main chitrashila naamak sthaan main aaj bhi vidyaman he. aisa bhi jaan padataa he ki is ganatantreey rajya ne rajtantr kaa rup dhaaran kar liya tha. sikkon par amoghbhuti naamak maharaj kaa ullekh miltaa he.
