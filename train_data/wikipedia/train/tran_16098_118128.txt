 johar puraane samay main bharat main rajput striyon dwaara kii jane vaali kriya thi. jab yuddh main haar nishchit ho jaati thi to purush mrityuparyant yuddh hetu taiyaar hokar veergati praapt karne nikal jate the tathaa striyaan johar kar leti thim arthath johar kunda main aag lagaakar khud bhi usamen kud jaati thi. johar kar lene kaa kaaran yuddh main haar hone par shatru raja dwaara haran kiye jane kaa bhay hota tha.
 johar kriya main rajput striyaan johar kunda ko aag lagaakar usamen swayan kaa balidaan kar detii thi. johar kriya kii sabase adhik ghatnayen bharat par mughal aadi bahari akramanakariyon ke samay huyi. ye muslim akramanakari hamalaa kar haraane ke pashchaat striyon ko lut kar unka sheelbhang karte the. isaliye striyaan haar nishchit hone par johar le leti thi. itihaas main johar ke anek udhaaharan milte hai.
 hindi ke ojasvi kavi shyaamanarayan pandeya ne apana prasiddh mahakavy johar chittor kii maharani padmini ke virangana charitra ko chitrit karne ke uddeshy ko lekar hi likhaa tha..rajasthan kii johar parampara par aadhaarit unka yah mahakavy hindi jagat main kaaphi charchit rahaa.
