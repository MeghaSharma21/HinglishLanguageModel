 sanyukt rajya america main raashtrapati or uparashtrapati kaa chunaav apratyaksh nirvachan paddati ke tahat hota he jisamen naagarik gupt matdaan dekar yues chunavi college ke sadasyon kaa chunaav karte hai. is matdaan se nirvaachit sabhi sadsy milakar raashtrapati or uparashtrapati kaa sidhaa chunaav karte hai. raashtrapati chunaav prayek chaar varsh ke interval par chunaav diwas ke din hote hai jo 2 navambar se 8 navambar ke madhya aane vaala mangalvaar kaa din hota he. pichhlaa raashtrapati chunaav 2012 kaa raashtrapati chunaav tha or aagaamee 2016 kaa raashtrapati chunaav he.
 america samvidhan ke anuchhed do main chunavi college sahit america chunaav paddati ko varnit kiya gaya he. yah samvidhan nirmaataaon ke congress ke madhyam se raashtrapati chunaav chaahane vaale or lokapriy raashtrapati chunaav ke vichaarkon ke madhya samjhauta tha.
 america samvidhan praathmik str par dal kaa pratinidhi chunaa jaataa he. is praathmik chunaav prakriya kaa koi likhit nirdesh nahin he. atah raajnneetik dalon dwaara apane kshetriy pratinidhiyon ko niyukt karne ke liye is chunaav main bhaag lete hai. praathmik chunaav main chune gayi dal pratinidhi duusare daur main raajnneetik dal kaa hissaa banate hai or apni party ke raashtrapati pad ke ummidavaar kaa chunaav karte hai. is daur main namakan kii prakriya bhi hoti he. tisare daur main chunaav prachaar or televijan par vibhinn ummidavaaron kii bahes hoti he.
 navambar maah kii taarikh 2 se 8 ke madhya aane vaala mangalvaar kaa din chunaav diwas ke rup main mana jaataa he. is din aam naagarik apane pratinidhi kaa chunaav karte hai. is chunaav main chune gayi sabhi sadsy chunavi college ke sadsy hote hai. inaki kul sankhya 538 hoti he.
 chune gayi sabhi sadsy sidhe raashtrapati kaa chunaav karte hai. is matdaan main raashtrapati chune jane ke liye kam se kam 270 mat milanaa aavashyak he.
