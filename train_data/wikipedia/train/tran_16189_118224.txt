 resha kisi prakritik yaa kritrim padaarthon ke bane patale tantu ko kehete hai. yah oon, kapaas, kagaz, pedo kii chaal, poliester or kai anya samagriyon ke ho sakate hai. aam taur par patale tantu ko hi resha kaha jaataa he. mote tantuon ko aksar 'rajju' kaha jaataa he. maanviya prayog main kai prakaar ke reshon ko bunakar chizein banaai jaati he, udhaaharan ke liye vastra.
