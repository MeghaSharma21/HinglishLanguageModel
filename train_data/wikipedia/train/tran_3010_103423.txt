 nokke-heist belgium ke pashchim flanders praant main sthit nagarpalika he. nagarpalika main heist-aan-zee, nokke, duinburgen, ramscapel or westcapel shahar shaamil hai. 1 janvari 2006 ko, nokke-heist kii kul jansankhya 34,063 thi. kul kshetrafal 56.44 kimi² he jo 603 prati vyakti prati jansankhya ghanatv prati kimi² detaa he. nokke-heist netherland ke saath belgium kii seemaa par uttar sagar ke kinare sthit he. yah belgium ke ek prasiddh or adhik samrudh samudr tateey sairgah main se ek he.is nagarpalika ke ek gaanv ramscapel main kamil bulke kaa janm hua tha.
