 ganit main, 'sameekaran ke hal' kaa arth he, aisi sankhyaayein, falan yaa samucchy khojna jo us diye gayi sameekaran ko santusht karte hai. jab ham sameekaran ke hal kii baat karte hai to usamen aaye hue ek yaa adhik char raashiyon ko 'agyaat' mana gaya hota he. 
 udhaaharan ke liye, sameekaran 4.x3 + x2 - 36 = 0 main char x agyaat raashi he or x= 2 iskaa ek hal he.
 sameekaran kaa hal vaishleshik vidhi se nikaala ja saktaa he yaa sankhyatmak vishleshan vidhiyon dwaara. kuch simit samikarnon kaa hi hal nikaalane kii vaishleshik vidhiyaan gyaat hai. kintu sankhyatmak vidhiyon dwaara sabhi samikarnon kaa hal sambhav he.
