  rashtriya sabhabhutan kii do sadaneey sansad kaa yah janata dwaara nirvaachit nimna sadan he. bhutan kii sansad kaa gathan rashtriya sabha ,rashtriya parishad or bhutan ke raja se milakar hota he. yah bhutani sansad kaa shakti shaali sadan he. 
 varsh 1953 main bhutan ke tisare naresh jigme dorji wangchuck ne pratham rashtriya sabha kii sthapana kii thi. is sabha kaa gathan ek ghoshnaa ke aadhaar par kiya gaya isamen yah kaha gaya ki is sabha main janata or dev log ke pratinidhi honge. yahaan dev logon se taatpary he " visisth log ". bhutan naresh kii yah ghoshnaa ek likhit dastavej ke rup main thi jise aage chalakar punakha dzong main namagyal tse tshongkhang par sweekrut hua. baad main yahi dastavej rashtriya sabha ke sanchaalan kaa aadhaar bana. 
 varsh 2013 ke nirvachan main pi di pi ko 32 or pi di tii 15 siten praapt huin.vartmaan rashtriya sabha main kul 47 sadsy hai.
