 barkhaa dutt ek bhartiya tivi patrkaar hai.
 dutt kaa janm nai dilli main air india ke ek adhikari es pi dutt or prabhaa dutt, jo hindustan times ke saath ek prasiddh patrkaar the. dutt apni patrakarita kaushal ko apni maa, bharat main mahila patrakaron ke bich ek agrani karne kaa shrey detaa he. unki choti bahan bahaar dutt sienen ibien ke liye kaam kar rahe ek televijan patrkaar bhi hai. vah khud ko agyeyavaadee kehete hai jo dharm ko khaarij karte hai. vah samaan aachaar samhita kii avadharana kaa samarthan karti he. barkhaa ne tripal taalak or muslim pitrusatta ke khilaaf apni raay vyakt kii he.
 armbh main unhen bahut khyaati mili kintu haal main hi radia tepe maamle main unka naam aane se badnaam ho gayin or jagah-jagah unka bhrasht patrkaar ke rup main virodh ho rahaa he.
