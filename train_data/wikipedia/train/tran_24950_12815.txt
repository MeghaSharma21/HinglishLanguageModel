 naina chotii yaa china chotii bhartiya rajya uttrakhand ke paryaton sthal or pramukh nagar nainital ke sameep ek 2,600+ meter unchi parvat chotii he. naina peak ke naam se mashhoor yah parvat chotii yahan kaa ek pramukh paryaton aakarshan he. saafe mausam main yahan se himalay kii kai himachhadit chotiyon kaa nazaaraa kiya ja saktaa he or nainital shahar kaa vihangavlokan kiya ja saktaa he. chotii tak pahunchne ke liye paidal yatra kii ja sakati he athwa ghode par chadhakar jaaya ja saktaa he. yah rasta burans, deodaar or cyprus ke ghaney wanon ke bich se hokar jaataa he.
 yah parvat chotii bhugolic rup se madhya himalay kaa hissaa he or nainital se lagbhag paanch-chah kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. chotii kii bhugolic avasthiti 29.40 uttar akshansh or 79.44 purv deshaantar par he or yah 2,600+ meter he.
