 chandrakumar agarwal  asamiya ke prasiddh lekhak, kavi or patrkaar the. ve 'jonaki' kaal ke agradoot the asamiya sahitya main unhen pratimaar khonicor kaha jaataa he. ve jonaki patrika ke pratham sampadak tathaa vittaposhak the. ve asamiya bhashaa unnati saadhini sabha ke sansthaapak bhi the. lakshminath bejbaruva, hemchandra goswami tathaa chandrakumar agarwal ko asami sahitya kii trimurti kaha jaataa he. chandrakumar agarwal ke bhai anand chandra agarwal bhi lekhak evam kavi the. prasiddh kavi, naatakkaar, asamiya filmkaar jyoti prasad agarwal unke bhattije the.
