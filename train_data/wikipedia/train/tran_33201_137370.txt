 vishwa shastreey tamil sammelan 2010, , tamil vidwaanon, shodhakartaaon, kaviyon tathaa khyatilabdh janon kaa ek anterrashtriya sammelan he. yah 23 june se 27 june 2010 tak coimbatore main sampann hua.
 bharat tathaa anya deshon se 400 se adhik patrakaron ne is sammelan main bhaag liya. 71.25 lakh ru. kii laagat se ek 12000 varg foot kaa media kendra sthapit kiya gaya. isamen telephone va fax conection, 75 internet sambhddha computer, tathaa tivi evam band-paripath camere lagaaye gayi. 
 sammelan kaa udghaatan bharat kii raashtrapati pratibha patil dwaara kiya gaya. finnish tamil vidvan asko parpolo ko kalagnar em. karunanidhi shastreey tamil puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
 vishay geet "semmoriyaan tamiz moguiyam" tamilnadu ke mukhyamantri em. karunanidhi dwaara lekhnibaddh kiya gaya he, tathaa sangeet e. aar. rahamaan dwaara diya gaya he.
