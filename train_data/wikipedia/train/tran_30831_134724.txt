 Atri main se ek he, or sabase adhik rugveda main iskaa ullekh he.
 rugveda ke paanchaven mandal ko unke sammaan main atri mandla kaha jaataa he, or isamen assi or saat bhajan unke or unke vanshaj ke liye jimmedaar hai.
 atri kaa puraanon or hindu mahakavy jaise ramayana or mahabharat main bhi ullekh kiya gaya he. atri gotra sabase zyada yadavon main paaya jaataa he . 
 Life 
 atri saat mahaan rishi yaa saptarshi main se ek he, jisamen marichi, angiras, pulaha, kratu, pulsty or vashishtha shaamil hai. vaidic yug ke puranic kathaon ke anusaar rishi atri kaa ansuya devi se vivaah hua tha. unke teen putr the, dattatreya, durvasas or soma. divine lekha ke anusaar, vah saat saanpathion main se antim he or mana jaataa he ki vah jeebh se utpann hua he. atri kii patni anusuya thi, jinhen saat mahila patharaavon main se ek mana jaataa he. jab divine awaz se tapasya karne kaa nirdesh diya jaataa he, to atri turant sahamat ho gaya or gambhir tapasya kii. unki bhakthi or prarthanaaon se prasannataa, hindu trayee, arthath, brahma, vishnu or shiv unke saamne prakat hue or unhen vardaan diya. usne sabhi teenon ko uske paas janm lene kii maang kii puranic kathaon kaa ek or sanskaran bataata he ki ansuya, uski shuddhta kii shaktiyon ke dwaara, teen devataaon ko bachaaya or badale main, unka janm bacchon ke rup main hua tha. brahma kaa janm chandra ke rup main hua, vishnu ko dattatreya ke rup main or shiv ke kuch hisse main durvasa ke rup main paidaa hua tha. atri ke bare main ullekh vibhinn shastron main paaya jaataa he, jisamen rugaved main ullekhneey astitv he. vah kai yugon se bhi judaa hua he, ramayana ke dauraan treta yug main ullekhneey astitv he, jab vah or anusuya ne ram or unki patni sita ko salaah di thi. is jodi ko bhi ganga nadi ko dharti par laane ke liye jimmedaar tehraaya gaya he, jiskaa ullekh shiv puraana main paaya jaataa he. 
 rugveda kaa drashtaa 
 vah rugveda kaa paanchvaan mandal kaa drashtaa he. atri ke kai putr or shishyon ne rugaved or anya vaidic granthon ke sankalan main yogadan diya he. mandal 5 main 87 bhajan shaamil hai, mukhya rup se agni or indra, lekin viswasdev, maaruts, judawaan-devtaa mitra-varuna or acins ke liye. do bhajan ushaaon or savitri ke liye. is pustak ke adhikansh bhajan atri kabeeley sangeetkaaron ko diya jaataa he, jinhen atres kaha jaataa he. rugveda ke ye bhajan bhartiya upmahadweep ke uttar-pashchimi kshetra main banaaye gayi the, jo ki lagbhag 1500 -1200 bisii. 
 rugveda ke atri bhajan unke sangeet sambandhi sanrachana ke liye mahatvapurn hai or paheliyon ke rup main aadhyatmik vichaaron ko darshane ke liye bhi mahatvapurn hai. in bhajanon main sanskrit bhashaa kii lacheelepan kaa upyog karne vaale lexical, vaakyavinyas, rupavachak or kriya naatak shaamil hai. atri mandal main rugveda kaa bhajan 5.44 vidwaanon dwaara mana jaataa he jaise ki geldonor rugveda main sabase kathin paheli bhajan he. chand, rupakon ke madhyam se prakritik ghatana kii apni suruchipurna prastuti ke liye bhi janaa jaataa he, jaise bhajan 5.80 main ek hansmukh mahila ke rup main kavy rup se prarambh kiya gaya. 
 jabaki paanchavi mandal ko atri or uske sahyogiyon ke liye jimmedaar mana jaataa he, rishi atri ko anya mandal main 10.137.4 ke rup main rugveda kii kai anya chhandon kaa ullekh yaa shrey diya gaya he. 
 ramayaan main adhyay
 ramayana main, ram, sita or lakshmana unke ashram main atri or anasuya kaa daura karte hai. atri kii jhopadi ko chitrakoot main varnit kiya gaya he, divya sangeet or geeton ke saath ek jhil ke paas, pani, phulon se bharaa hua, kai "kraine, machuaaron, floating kachhue, hans, bedu or gulabi hans" ke saath. 
 puraanon main ek hi rishi ke alag alag samay par hone kii jo ghatnaayen hai, ve vastav main kisi ek vyakti nahin balki ek rishi parampara ko vyakt karti hai. jis tarah se aadi shankaracharya ke sabhi uttaradhikariyon ko shankaracharya kaha jaataa he, usee tarah se kisi mahaan rishi ke uttaradhikari ko unka hi swarup mana jaataa tha. mahaan rishi kisi ashram ke kulapati hote the. us ashram ko us rishi kaa ashram kaha jaataa tha. us rishi ke brahmaleen ho jane ke baad bhi us ashram ko us rishi ke naam se hi janaa jaataa tha. or jansadharan us ashram ke kulapati ko sansthaapak rishi ke naam se hi jaanati thi. kai maamalon main koi vishesh naam ek oopaadhi thi. jaise sava karod gayatri jap kaa purashcharan karne vaala vashishtha mana jaataa tha. vyas naam ke kul 28 rishi hue jo antim rishi krishna dwaipayan vyas kahe jate hai.]
 atriyon naam ke kai santon kaa ullekh vibhinn madhyakaalin yug puraanon main kiya gaya he. atri main us puranic kinvadantiyaan vividh or asangat hai. yah spasht nahin he ki yadi ye ek hi vyakti, yaa alag rishiyon ke samaan he, jinka naam ek hi he.

 dakshin bharat main vaicharas ek mahatvapurn samudaay bana rahe hai, or ve apane vaidic viraasat kaa paalan karte hai. 
 brahma ke manasaputra atri ne rishi kardam kii putree anusuya se vivaah kiya tha. anusuya kii mata kaa naam devhooti tha. atri-danpati kii tapasya or tridevon kii prasannataa ke falswaroop vishnu ke ansh se mahayogi dattatreya, brahma ke ansh se chandramaa tathaa shankar ke ansh se mahamuni durvasa, maharishi atri evam devi anusuya ke putr rup main aavirbhoot hue. inke brahmavadini naam kii kanya bhi thi.
 atri putr chandramaa ne brihaspati kii patni tara se sansarg kiya jisase use budh naam kaa putr utpann hua, jo baad main kshatriyon ke chandravansh kaa pravartak hua. is vansh ke raja khud ko chandravanshi kehete the. chunki chandra atri rishi kii santan the isliye chandravanshi aatreya bhi hue. brahmanon main ek upnaam hota he aatreya arthath atri se sambandhit yaa atri kii santan.
 chandravansh ke pratham raja kaa naam bhi som mana jaataa he jiskaa prayag par shashan tha. atri se chandramaa, chandramaa se budh, budh se pururava, pururava se aayu, aayu se nahush, nahush se yeti, yayati, sanyaati, ayeti, viati or kriti naamak chah mahabal-vikramashali putr hue.
 nahush ke bade putr yeti the, jo sanyasi ho gaye isliye unke dusre putr yayati raja hue. yayati ke putron se hi samast vansh chale. yayati ke 5 putr the. devayani se yadu or turvasu tathaa sharmishtha se druhmu, anu evam puru hue. yadu se yadav, turvasu se yavan, drahuyu se bhoj, anu se malachh or puru se paurav vansh kii sthapana hui.
 atri hinduon ke ek mahaan rishi hai. vanvaas kaal main shriram tathaa mata sita ne atri ashram kaa bhraman kiya tha. inaki patni anusuya ne sita ko pativrata dharm kaa upadesh diya.
 atri parampara ke tirth sthalon main chitrakoot, rameswaram, minakshi amman, tirupati balaji or hinglaj mata mandir aadi hai.
