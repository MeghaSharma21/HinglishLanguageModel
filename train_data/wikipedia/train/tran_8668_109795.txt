 parinukayan ek aise dastauez ko kehete hai jisamen kisi nauka ke captain ko kisi sagar ke chor ke saath lage hue bandargahon or tateey sthalakritiyon kaa duriyon ke maapan ke saath vrutant mili. puraane yugon main aise parinukyanon ke prayog se navi captainon ko naukayan main sahaayta milti thi. itihaas main aise kai prasiddh parinukayan pustaken milti hai.
