 tariq fatah, janm 20, 1949), pakistani mul ke canadai lekhak, prasarak evam secular udaaravaadi kaaryakarta hai. cheging a miraj : da trygic illuton of ain islamic state unki prasiddh kriti he.
 tariq fatah islamic atvaad ke kilaaf bolne or ek udaaravaadi islam ke paksh ko badhaava dene ke liye prasiddh hai. tariq fatah dakshin asia or vishesh rup se kattarpanthi bhartiya or pakistani musalamaanon kii algaavvaadi samskruti ke viruddh bhi bolte hai, tathaa ve samalangik vyaktiyon ke saamaan adhikaron or hiton ke bhi pakshdhar hai. saath hi ve balochistan main maanavadhikaar ke hanan or pakistan dwaara balochistan main kiye ja rahe zyadatiyon ke vishay par bhi bolte or likhte hai, or 'azaad balochistan' ke pakshdhar ke rup main bhi jane jate hai. unhone canadai muslim congress kaa sthapana kiya or ve islam kaa udaaravaadi or pragatisheel rup ke samarthak hai. ve zee news ke fatah kaa fatwa kaarykram main islam par lagaataar apana najariya pesh karte aa rahe hai.
 pakistani vyavasthapika tathaa islamic kattarpanth, evam islamic itihaas or kuch paramparaon ke vishay main bolne ke kaaran, unke vichaar, aksar bahes or vivaad kaa paatr rahete hai.
 unka janm karachi main hua lekin ve punjabi mul ke hai. unhone karachi vishvavidhyalay se jeev-rasayan main shiksha praapt kii. canada main apana ghar basaane se pehle unka parivaar soudi arab main kuch saal rahaa tha.
 unka drudh mat he ki pakistan vishwa main aatankwaad kaa praayojak he.
