 chaat bharat me vishesh taur par uttar bharat me khaaye jane vaala ek swaadisht naashtaa he. chaat, hindi shabd chaat se liya gaya he jiskaa arth hota he swaad lenaa. bharat me chaat mukhya rup se sadak kinare khadey thelon par bechi jaati he or amuman har umr kaa vyakti ise maze lekar khaataa he.
 chaat me mukhya rup se aalu tikki, gol gappe, paapadi, bhalle, seve puri, daal ke laddu, raaj kachaudee, lachha tokari, tale hue aalu aadi aate hai. 
 iske atirikt kate hue falon ko bhi chaat ke rup me khaaya jaataa he.
 chaat banane me mukhya rup se aalu, besan, daal, dahi, masaalon, tamaato, pyaaj or chatnee aadi kaa prayog hota he.
 paapadi chaat paapadi chaat banane kii vidhi- chaat itihaas se paapadi, uble aalu,tamaato,pyaaj, matar, dahi or dhaniye kii chatnee ko milakar banaaya jaataa he.iske baad ise chaat masala or seve se sajaayaa jaataa he. iskaa swaad khat, meetha or tikhaa hota he.
