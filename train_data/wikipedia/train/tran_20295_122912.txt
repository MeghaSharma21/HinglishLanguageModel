 srijan myth sansar ke nirmaan or logon ke usamen basane ke pratikatmak varnan ko kehete hai. haalaanki myth shabd saamaanya upyog main aksar juthi yaa kaalpanik kahaniyon ko sanderbhit karata he, par aupchaarik rup se iskaa matlab jhooth nahin hota he. aam taur par samskritiyaan apane srijan mithkon ko satya maanati hai.
 srijan mithkon main aam taur par bahut chizein samaan hoti hai. ye sabhi dharmon main paye jate hai or inhen aksar pavitra mana jaataa he. in kathaon ke paatr aksar devtaa, insaan or bolne vaale jaanavar hote hai. ye paatr aksar aasaani se rup badal sakate hai. ye kathaayen aksar dhundhale yaa avishisht ateet main ghatati hai. srijan myth un sawaalon kaa vyaakhyaan karte hai jo unamem vishwaas karne vaale samaaj ke liye sabase mahatvapurn hon. in se hamen us samaaj ke vaishvik drushtikon or sansar main apni jagah ke dhaanche kaa pataa chalata he.
 srijan myth maukhik rup main viksit hote hai. isliye inke ek se zyada sanskaran hote hai. srijan myth sabhi maanav samskritiyon main paae jate hai, ye myth ke sabase saamaanya praroop hai.
