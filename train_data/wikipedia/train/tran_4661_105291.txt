 pracheen-vishwa ke saat aashcharya yaa vishwa ke saat aashcharya, pracheen ucch vaastukala ke namoonay hai. yah suchi helenic logo me bahut prasikad thi. parantu yah suchi sirf bumadhyasaagar ke aas-paas kii imaaraton ko dhyaan main rakhkar banaayi gayi thi. yadhyapi vartmaan swaroop kii yah suchi, punarjagran kaal tak sthir nahin thi, pehali baar pehali-duusari shatabdi isa purv aisi suchi saamne aayi thi. mul suchi ne umr ke madhyam se asankhya sanskarnon ko prerit kiya, aksar saat pravishtiyon ko soochibaddh karte the. mul saat aashcharyon main se, keval ek-giza kaa mahaan pyramid, pracheen chamatkaaron main se sabase purana or apekshaakrit upasthit he. rods kaa calasas, sikandariyaa kaa prakashastambh, mousolas kaa makbara, artamis kaa mandir or olympia main jiyas kii murti sabhi nasht ho chuki he. babylon ke jhulate upavan kaa sthaan or buniyadi satya agyaat he, or atakalein hai ki shaayad yah astitv main rahaa hi nahin hoga.
 suchi main keval bumadhyasagariya or madhya purvi kshetron ke murtikla or sthapaty smarakon ko shaamil kiya gaya tha, jisamen greek ke liye gyaat duniya ko shaamil kiya gaya tha. isliye, is kshetra se dur sthit sthalon ko suchi main nahin joda gaya tha.
 helenistic lekhakon dwaara likhe kavitaaon kaa bhi is aashcharya suchi main shaamil sthanon par baari prabhaav padaa tha. saat pravishtiyon main se paanch, unanion kii kala or vaastukala main uplabdhiyon kaa namuna he .
