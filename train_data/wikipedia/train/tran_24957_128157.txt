 rajaraaj chol 1 dakshin bharat ke chol samrajya ke mahaan chol samraat the jinhone 985 se 1014 tak raaj kiya. unke shashan main cholon ne dakshin main srilanka tathaa uttar main kaling tak samrajya failaayaa. rajraj chol ne kai nausainya abhiyaan bhi chalaaye, jiske falswaroop malabar tat, maldive tathaa srilanka ko aadhipaty main liya gaya.
 rajraj chol ne hinduon ke vishaltam mandiron main se ek,thanjore ke brihadishwar mandir kaa nirmaan karaayaa. unhone san 1000 main bhu-surveykshan kii bheeshan pariyojna shuru karai jisase desh ko valnadu ikaayiyon main punarsangathit karne main madad mili.
 chol vansh kaa doosraa mahaan shaasha kotutung tritiya tha
 nauvi shadi mai chholau kaa udai hua. inkaa rajya tungbhadra tak failaa hua tha. chhole rajaao ne shaktishali nausaina kaa vikaas kiya. is vansh kii sthapana vijayalay ne kii. rajraj chol ne shashipadsekhar kii oopaadhi dhaaran kii thi.
