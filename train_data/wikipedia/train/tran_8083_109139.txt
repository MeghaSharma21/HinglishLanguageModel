 kordoba kaa roman pul guadalaquivir nadi ke paar pehali shatabdi isa purv main kordoba, andalsia, dakshini spain main banaaya gaya tha. yah aitihaasik pul chote-se sanskshit ilaaka sotos de laa albolafia kaa bhaag ban chukaa he.
 is pul ko shaayad rome ke logon dwaara pehali shatabdi isa purv main ek lakdi ke pul kii jagah par banaaya gaya tha. yah vartmaan main, islamic punarnirmaan ke baad, 16 kii tulana main ek kam arched, or 247 meter kii kul lambaai he or chaudaai lagbhag 9 meter ke saath khadaa he. 
 augusta kaa rasta jo rome ko kadiz jodta tha or shaayad vahaan se guzarataa bhi tha. islamic shashan ke shuru main muslim governor al-samh ebn malik al-kaulani ne farmaan jari kiya ki ek nayaa pul roman nirmaan ke khandaron par banaaya janaa chahie. madhya yug main, kalahora minar or puerta del puento kramshah pul ke dakshini or uttari chor par banaaya gaya tha. baad kaa nirmaan ab ek 16 veen sadi kaa punarnirmaan he. pul ko khangala or apane vartmaan aakaar ko vistaar kiya gaya tha. mehrab shahar ke drishyon haaavee he or yah prasiddh muslim vaastukala ko darshaati he. 17 veen sadi main saint rafeel kii ek murti barnabe gomez del rio dwaara banaakar pul ke madhya main rakh di gai thi. is pul kp bade paimaane par 2006 main behaal kiya gaya tha.
