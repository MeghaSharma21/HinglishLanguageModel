 11 april 2018 ko algerian sainya bimaan durghatnagrast ho gaya tha. vimaan main 257 yatri the isamen yatri sahit 10 chaalak dal ke sadsy bhi maare gaye jiname adhiktar sainya karmi the or unke parivaar ke sadsy the. vimaan boufrik airbase se udaan bharne ke kuch samay baad aag lag gayi jis sthaan par aag lagi thi vah rajdhani algeriars se 25 kilometre dur dakshin pashchim main hai.
 algeria ke raksha mantralay ne bataaya kii rus dwaara nirmit iel-76 vimaan tindown ke liya udaan par tha vimaan main savaar sabhi 276 yatri or chaalak dal ke 10 sadasyon kii maut ho gayi..
