 ashok chihn bharat kaa rajkeey pratik he. isko sarnath main mili ashok laat se liya gaya he. mul rup isamen chaar sher hai jo chaaron dishaao kii ore munh kiye khadey hai. iske neeche ek gol aadhaar he jis par ek hathi ke ek daudta ghoda, ek sand or ek singh bane hai. ye golakaar aadhaar khile hue ulte latake camel ke rup main he. har pashu ke bich main ek dharm chakra bana hua he. raashtra ke pratik main jise 26 janvari 1950 main bharat sarkaar dwaara apanaaya gaya tha keval teen singh dikhaai dete hai or chauthaa chipa hua he, dikhaai nahin detaa he. chakra centre main dikhaai detaa he, sand dahini ore or ghoda baayin ore or anya chakra kii bahari rekha bilkul daahine or bai chor par. ghanti ke aakaar kaa camel chod diya jaataa he. pratik ke neeche satyamev jayate devanagari lipi main ankit he. shabd satyamev jayate shabd mundakopanishad se liye gaye hai, jiskaa arth he keval sachai kii vijay hoti he.
