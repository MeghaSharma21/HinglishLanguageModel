 shining janavaadee ganraajya china ke pashchimi bhaag main sthit chingei praant kii rajdhani or sabase bada shahar he. yah tibet ke pathaar par sthit sabhi nagaron main se bhi sabase bada he. yah china kii prashasan pranali ke anusaar ek upaprantiy shahar kaa darja rakhata he. san 2010 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 22,08,708 anumaanit kii gai thi, jisamen se 11,98,304 shahari ilaaqon main rahete the. aitihaasik rup se yah shahar resham maarg par ek mahatvapurn padaav tha or china or tibet ke darmiyaan oon, lakdi or namak ke vyapaar ke liye bhi ahm tha. yahan kumbum tibeti buddha math or 600 saal puraani dongguan masjid sthit hai.
 yahan garmiyon main taapmaana zyaada nahin badhata or yah garmiyon main apane achche mausam ke liye prasiddh he. july main ausat taapmaana 17.3 °centegrade rahata he. sardiyon main janvari main ausat taapmaana −7.4 °centegrade hota he. yahan baarishein may se sitambar tak hoti hai.
