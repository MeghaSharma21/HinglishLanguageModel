 menka gandhi bharat kii prasiddh rajnetri evam pashu-adhikaaravaadi hai. purv main ve patrkaar bhi rah chuki hai. kintu bharat kii mahila pradhan mantri indira gandhi ke chote putr swa sanjay gandhi kii patni ke rup main ve adhik vikhyath hai. unhone anekon pustakon kii rachana kii he tathaa unke lekh vibhinn patr-patrikaon main praayah aate rahete hai. ve vartmaan main bharat kii mahila evam bal vikaas mantri hai. 
 menka gandhi kaa janm dilli main hua tha. unki shiksha larens skool, sanwar tathaa tadoparant ladi shriram kalege, nayi dilli main hui. unhone tatkaaleen pradhan mantri shrimati indira gandhi ke chote bete sanjay ke saath vivaah kiya. ek aakasmik durghatana main sanjay gandhi ke dehant ke baad ve san 1982 main raajnneeti main aayin.
 shrimati menka gandhi ek jaani-maani paryaavaranvaadi kaaryakarta hai. bharat main pashu-adhikaron ke prashn ko mukhyadhara main laane kaa shrey menka gandhi ko hi jaataa he. san 1992 main unhone pipal for animals naamak ek gair-sarkari sanghathan armbh kiya jo puure bharat main aashray chalaata he.
