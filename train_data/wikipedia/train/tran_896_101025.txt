 vasai mumbai main vasai-viraar kaa ek kshetra he. 
 vasai ek aitihaasik shahar he. pehle vasai kaa basin naam tha. baad me vasai padaa. yah nagar pehle porchiyugal logon ke adhin tha. vasai viraar mahanagar palika he jo maharashtra rajasav me dusraa sthaan rakhti he. mumbai ka ek behtareen shahar he. sab suvidhaon yukt shahar he. ageucation hub he. palghar jila, konkan divijan me aata he. mumbai se yah 50 kilometre dur he. yahan kii bhashaa marathi, angreji, hindi he. sarkari kaam kaaj marathi va angreji me hota he. vasai railway jankshan he. yahan se hindustan ke har kone se trainon kaa avaagaman he. MIDC me mumbai kaa nan0 1 shahar he. vasai viraar mahanagar palika kii jansankhya lagbhag 1.50 lakh he. hariyaali zyada hone ke kaaran ise harith vasai bhi kehete hai.
