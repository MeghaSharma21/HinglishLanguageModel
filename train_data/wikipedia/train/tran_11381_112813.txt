 da imperial gajetir aaf india bhartiya britani samrajya dwaara taiyaar kiya gaya bhugolic kosh he jo vartmaan main ek sandarbh kaary ke rup main kaam main liya jaataa he. angreji raaj main 19ve shatabdi ke ant or 20veen shatabdi ke aarmabhik do dashakon main gajetir taiyaar huai the. san 1881 main imperial gajetir aaf india kaa prakaashan hua. isaki ruparekha wiliam wilson hunter ne san 1869 main hi armbh kar di thi. yah 9 valum main prakashit hua. iskaa doosraa sanskaran san 1885-87 main prakashit hua, jisamen 14 valum the. hunter bharat main gajetir ke janmdata kahe jate he. aapaki mrityu ke baad imperial gajetir aaf india kaa nayaa sanskaran 26 valum main prakashit hua. isi ke saath provins series ke 19 provins gajetirs tathaa district series ke gajet bhi prakashit hui.
 usne glassgo, paris tathaa baan main shiksha praapt kar 1862 i. main 'indian civil sarvice' main pravesh kiya or bengal main niyukt hua. usamen dhara pravaah likhane kii adbhut shakti thi.
 hanter ne 'gramin bengal kaa krmanusaar itihaas' likhakar ek rajaneta ke rup main acha naam kamaaya. chaar saal baad 'bharat kii anarya bhashaaon kaa tulanaatmak kosh' prakashit karke apane paanditya kaa bhi parichay diya. bharat ke sankhyikeey surveykshan kaa prabandh kiya or 1875-1877 i. main 'bengal kaa sankhyikeey vivaran' 20 khandon main prakashit kiya.
