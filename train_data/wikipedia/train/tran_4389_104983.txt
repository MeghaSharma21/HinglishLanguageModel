 shripati mishra uttar pradesh ke bhootpurv mukhyamantri the. vah bhartiya rashtriya congress ke ek netaa the. ve purv main vidhaan sabha adhyaksh bhi the.unke pita pratishthit rajkeey vaidya pandit ram prasad mishra the. unka janm 20 janvari, 1924 ko sultanpur main hua tha. unhone varanasi tathaa lakhanu vishvavidhyalayon main padhai kii va em0e0, el-el0bi0 kii degree praapt kii. unka vivaah shrimati rajkumaari mishra se 1941 main hua. unke teen putr tathaa ek putree thim. vyavsaayik rup se ve krishi evam vakaalat karte the. march 1962 main ve pehali baar vidhaan sabha ke sadsy ke rup main nirvaachit hue. march 1967 main ve chauthi vidhaan sabha ke sadsy bane. 19 june 1967 se 14 april 1968 tak ve uttar pradesh vidhaan sabha ke upaadhyaksh rahe.
 1969 main ve lok sabha ke sadsy bane. 18 farvari 1970 se 01 oktober 1970 tak ve choudhary charan singh kii sarkaar main mantri rahe va unke paas khaadya evam rasad, rajasav abhaav, samaaj kalyaan, harijan sahaayak, shiksha, khelkud, shram, sahaayta evam punarvaas kaa kaarybhaar tha. 18 oktober 1970 se 04 april 1971 tak ve tribhuvan narayan singh sarkaar ke mantrimandal main shiksha evam pravidhik shiksha mantri rahe. 1970 se 1976 tak ve vidhaan parishad ke sadsy rahe. 1976 main ve rajya yojana ayog ke upaadhyaksh bane. june 1980 main ve aathvin vidhaan sabha ke sadsy bane. 07 july 1980 se 18 july 1982 tak ve uttar pradesh vidhaan sabha ke adhyaksh rahe. 19 july 1982 se 02 agast 1984 tak ve uttar pradesh ke mukhyamantri rahe. 1985 main ve aathvin lok sabha ke sadsy bane. 07 disambar 2002 ko unka nidhan ho gaya.
