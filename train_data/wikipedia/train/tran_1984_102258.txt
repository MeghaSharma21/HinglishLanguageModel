 mumbai ke sarvaadhik pracheen dharmasthalon main se ek he yahan kaa mahalakshmi mandir. samudr ke kinare bi. desai maarg par sthit yah mandir atyant sundar, aakarshak or lakhon logon kii aasthaa kaa pramukh centre he.
 maan lakshmi ko dhan kii devi mana gaya he. mahalakshmi kii puja ghar or kaarobaar main sukh or samruddhi laane ke liye kii jaati he. mahalakshmi mandir ke mukhya dwaar par sundar nakkashi kii gai he. mandir parisar main vibhinn devi-devataaon kii aakarshak pratimaaen sthapit hai.photo gailaree dekhne ke liye clic karen
 mandir kaa itihaas atyant rochak he. angrejon ne jab mahalakshmi kshetra ko varli kshetra se jodne ke liye brich candi maarg ko banane kii yojana banaai tab samudr kii tufaani laharon ke chalate puri yojana khatai main padati prathit hui.
 us samay devi lakshmi ek thekedaar raamji shivaji ke swapn main prakat huin or unhen samudr tal se deviyon kii teen pratimaaen nikaalkar mandir main sthapit karne kaa aadesh diya. raamji ne aisa hi kiya or brich candi maarg kaa nirmaan safaltaapurvak sampann hua.
 mandir ke garbhagrih main mahalakshmi, mahakali evam mahasaraswati teenon deviyon kii pratimaaen ek saath vidyaman hai. teenon pratimaaon ko sone evam motiyon ke aabhushanon se susajjit kiya gaya he. yahan aane vaale har bhakt kaa yah drudh vishwaas hota he ki mata unki har ichha jarur puri karengi.
 
 mumbai bharat kii vyavsaayik rajdhani he tathaa desh ke prayek bhaag se acchhee tarah se rail, road or vayumarg se judi hui he.
 Mumbai Central Station se texi le or wallking dis 20min hai.
