 amtivi lokapriy 24-ghante ke sangeet or yuva manoranjan channel kaa italvi bhashi sanskaran he. 24 ghante ke italvi bhashi amtivi ke prakshepan se pehle, italy main amtivi darshakon ne ke pan-european sanskaran praapt kiya. europe main amtivi kaa kshetriyakaran march 1997 main german bhashi ke prakshepan ke saath, ek hi varsh ke july main euke branded channel ke baad hua. sitambar 1997 main, jab iskaa prasaaran prasaaran dijai tivi rete e ke tivi kaaryakramon main darj kiya gaya tha, to italvi channel aadhikarik rup se landan main launch kiya gaya tha.
