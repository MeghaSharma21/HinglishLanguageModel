 qutubpur datana, ravaa rajput kaa gaanv he jo ki budhaanaa tehsil, muzaffarnagar jila, uttar pradesh se 8 kimi purv main stith he. gaanv kaa daakghar 3 kimi dur ek kurthal naamak gaanv main padataa he. datana gaanv budhaanaa tehsil ke antargat padataa he.gaanv ke nirdeshaank 29.2321482 u. evam 77.5177181 pus. hai. hindon nadi gaanv ki seemaa ko uttar ki disha main choote hue bahati he. gaanv main ek praathmik vidhyalay he. isi vidhyalay ke peeche stith ek 8veen tak shiksha pradaan karne ke liye nayaa vidhyalay kholaa gaya he. shivaji kaa mandir bhi praathmik vidhyalay ke paas main hi he. kheti yogya jameen ko mukhyatah 2 hisso main vibhajit kiya gaya he, khaddar evam bangar jo nadi se kramshah paas or dur hai. haalanki ganna hi gaanv ki vanijyik fasal he parantu aaj kal popler kii kheti bhi bahutayat main ho rahi he. gaanv ke purv main nagava or ataali, pashchim main mahalajana, uttar main riyavali evam nangla naamak gaanv stith hai. bas, jugad or tanga gaanv main aane jane ke mukhya saadhan hai. dilli se gaanv tak pahuchane ke liye badot or meerut se hote hue sadak maarg he.
