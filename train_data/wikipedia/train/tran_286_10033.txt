 jagar baandh ek prakritik baandh he jo rajasthan rajya ke hindun main jagar gaanv ke paas sthit he  sinchai or pani kii aapurti ke uddeshy ke liye 1957 main puraa kiya gaya.
 jagar baandh hindun city, rajasthan ke jagar gram main sthit he. jagar baandh picnic or saiyr ke liye ek shandaar jagah he. saptahanth ke dauraan, aagatuk sundar vaataavaran or thandi hawa kaa anand lene ke liai yahan samoohon main aate hai. yah ek saarvajanik sthaan he. yah pahadiyon main he. aravali range main sthit ek hill stesan kii tarah ek lokapriy sthaan he. yah apane oonchai, nadiyon, jhiilon or jangalon ke liye priy he. yah ek baandh he, is aadhaar phisalane pahari chadhaai, ucch pahadiyon or talabon kaa anand le sakate hai. ham kah sakate hai ki yah hindun city ke sabase prasiddh picnic sthal he.
