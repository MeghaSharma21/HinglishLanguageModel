 jagarnott angreji bhashaa main prayukt hone vaala ek shabd he jiskaa prayog ajey ke rup main sandarbhit shabdic yaa rupak shakti kii vyakhya ke liye hota he. yah praayah kisi badi mashin yaa kisi team yaa ikatthe kaary karne vaale logon ke samooh ke liye yaa kisi karishmai netaa dwaara chalaaye ja rahe ubhar rahe raajnneetik aandolan ke liye prayukt hota he. iskaa sambandh praayah kuchle jane athwa bhautik haani se joda jaataa he.
 yah shabd sanskrit shabd jagannatha se bana he.jagannatha jo ki bharat ke pracheen vaidic dharmagranthon main varnit bhagwaan krishna ke anek naamon main se ek he. bharat ke sarvaadhik prasiddh mandiron main se ek jagannatha mandir puri, urisa main he. is mandir main salanaa rath yatra mahotsav hota he jisamen bhagwaan jagannatha, devi subhadra tathaa bhagwaan balbhadra kii murtiyon ko rath main baitaakar yatra par le jaaya jaataa he.
 14veen shatabdi kii ek prasiddh kitaab en:The Travels of Sir John Mandeville en:apocryphally main kuch hinduon ko ek dharmik balidaan ke rup main bataaya gaya he jo ki swayan ko is vishaal rath ke pahiyon ke neeche arpit kar kuchle jaakar mrityu ko praapt hote hai. is daawey ke aadhaar par british upnivesh ne yah daawa pravartit kiya ki krishna ke hindu bhakt siraphire hathdharmi hai jo moksh praapt karne ke liye swayan ko in rathon ke pahiyon ke neeche phenk dete hai. jabaki kai anya sutron ne sujhaayaa ki jo kuch mrity huyi ve bheed tathaa baicheni ke kaaran durghatanavash huyin.yah badi lorie ke liye ek british slang bhi he.
 is shabd kaa prayog victorian kaal main sharaab kii avadharana ke sambandh main bhi hua he. udhaaharan ke liye iskaa prayog Robert Louis Stevenson kii en:Dr. Jekyll and Mr. Hyde main Hyde ke liye hua he.
