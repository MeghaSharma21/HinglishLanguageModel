 
 2016-17 pro-50 championships zimbabwe main ek list e cricket toornament he. pratiyogita vartmaan main 10 farvari se agast 2017 tak chal rahaa he. yah mul rup se may main shuru karne ke liye nirdhaarit kiya gaya tha, lekin zimbabwe ke ek divasiy antarrashtriya afghanistan ke khilaaf shrinkhalaa ke liye taiyaari main farvari ke liye aage laayaa gaya tha. kholane judanar logaan cap, jiske liye baad main pratiyogita main punarnirdhaarit kiya gaya main anusuchit judanar badal diya. logan cap ke samaapan ke baad, pro-50 championships may 2017 main der se shuru hui.
 metabeleland taskars ne toornament jeetne ke liye ek khel diya.
