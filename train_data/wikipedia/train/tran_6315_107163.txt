 lakshmana jhula rishikesh main ganga nadi par bana ek pul he.
 puraatan kathananusaar bhagwaan shriram ke anuj lakshmana ne isi sthaan par jute kii rassiyon ke sahare nadi ko paar kiya tha. swami vishudanand kii prerana se kalakatta ke seth surajmal jhuhanubala ne yah pul san 1889 main lohe ke majboot taron se banwaaya, isse purv jute kii rassiyon kaa hi pul tha evam rasson ke is pul par logon ko chhinke main bithakar khinchaa jaataa tha. lekin lohe ke taron se bana yah pul bhi 1924 kii baadh main bah gaya. iske baad majboot evam aakarshak pul banaaya gaya.
 is pul ke pashchimi kinare par bhagwaan lakshmana kaa mandir he jabaki iske duusari ore shriram kaa mandir he. kaha jaataa he ki shriram swayan is sundar sthal par padhaare the. pul ko paar kar bain ore paidal rasta badrinath ko tathaa daayin ore swargashram ko jaataa he. kedarkhand main is pul ke neeche indrakund kaa vivaran he, jo ab pratyaksh nahin he.
