 munj yaa 'sarapat' ek ghaas he jo 10-15 foot unchi hoti he. isaki pattiyaan patali evam lambi hoti hai jinke kinare chote-chote kanten hote hai. isaki chappar banaayi jaati he. iske phool safed hote hai. yah praaia: nadiyon ke kinare balui jameen par adhik ugti he. isko log kheton kii med par bhi lagaate hai.
