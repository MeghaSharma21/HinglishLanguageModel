 moira keli ek abhinetri hai. 1990 ke tivi series twin picks ke pricel main lara flin boil kii jagah, fine walk vid me ke saath, donna hayward kii bhoomika ke liye ve sabase prasiddh hai. 1992 main unhen kate mosele kii bhoomika ke liye bhi janaa jaataa hai. simba ke pride film main simba ke pyaar naala kii aawaaj bhi di thi. unhone chaplin main hetty keli or ona o'nil kii bhoomika bhi nibhaai thi. 
 keli kaa janm kuyins, new york main hua tha, vah irish apravasiyo kii beti thi. unke pita peter ek prashikshit vilan vadak tathaa mata, eni ek nurse hai. keli chah bacchon main se teesraa baccha he or vah ronkoncoma main pali badi hai, vah ek catholic ke rup main badi hui hai. keli ne conetcot senior hai skool bohemia se padhai kii hai, or 1986 main long island se snaatak kiya hai. baad main marimount manhaton college se aage kii padhai kii. 1984 main hai skool ke dauraan ani main ek chota sa role mila tha. 
 keli ne ek kishor hatyare ke rup main lav, lise and murder ek tivi film main shuruyaat main kaam kiya tha. vah da boy hu craid bich, hai-life, or billy bathget kii filmon main choti bhoomikaayen bhi nibhaai, uske baad donna hayward kaa role bhi nibhaayaa, jisase unhen bahut prasiddhi mili. unhone onors, little odesa, da taai date bainds or dangeras beauty ke saath filmon main pradarshit hui he. apane swatantra filmi kariyar main, keli ne sakriy kaaryakarta dorothi de in antertaning angels: da dorothi de story or glen chlose main da sefti of objects main bhi abhinay kiya tha. 
 2000 main keli ne texas ke ek vyapaaree steve heavit se shaadi kii. uke 2 bachche bhi hai.
