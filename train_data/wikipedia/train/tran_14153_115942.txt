 nepalirajbanshimaithili
 jhapa jilla nepal ke purvanchal vikaas kshetra ke mechi anchal main sthit ek atyaadhik urvar evam ghanaa vasti sameta hua jilla hai. is jilla kaa kshetrafal 1606 berg kimi or jansankhya 8 lakh se zyada hai. is jilla ke purv or dakshin main bharat, uttar main ilaam, pashchim main morang jilla hai. maanav bikaas suchakank main jhapa jilla tisare sthanme he.ish zilleme 7 nagarpalika or 37 ga bi sa he.jillame 7 nirvachan kshetra he.
