 pradipt batti yaa pradipt nalika yaa floricent lamp ek 'gas-discharge batti' he jisamen paare ke vaashp ko iksite karne ke liye vidyut vibhav kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. yah samaan maatra main prakaash paidaa karne ke liye saadhaaran balb kii tulana main kam bijli khaataa he. kintu inka aakaar bada hota he, in par shuruyaat main adhik paisa kharch karnaa padataa he tathaa inamen paara mercury kii ek sookshm maatra bhi hoti he jo paryaavaran ko nuksaan pahunchaati he.
