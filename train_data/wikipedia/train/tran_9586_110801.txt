 aadhikarik mat un maton, vishwaason, aasthaaon or shikshaaon kaa vidhivat sangrah hota he jise kisi manyata, dharm, samaaj yaa anya saamaajik vyavastha main aadhikarik mana jaataa he. yah aavashyak nahin he ki us saamaajik vyavastha se baahar ke log kisi aadhikarik mat ko satya maane, or aksar do dharmik sansthaanon ke aadhikarik mat ek-duusare se vipareet bhi ho sakate hai.
