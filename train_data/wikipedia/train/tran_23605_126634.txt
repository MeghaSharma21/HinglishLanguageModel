 dinesh trivedi gujarati danpati heeralaal or urmila kii sabase choti santan hai, jo bharat vibhaajan ke samay karachi, pakistan se aae the, jahaan trivedi ke sabhi bhai-bahan paidaa hue the. dilli aane se pehle unke mata-pita kai jagahon par bhatakate rahe, dilli main trivedi kaa janm hua. unke pita kolkata kii ek company hindustan construction company main kaam karne lage.
 kolkata ke saint jevirs kalege se unhone vaanijya main snaatak kiya. isse pehle unhone himachal pradesh ke bording skulon main shiksha pai. snaatak main degree lene ke baad wo bees hajaar rupee karj liye or austin sthit arlington kaa taxs vishvavidhyalay se emb puraa kiya. bhartiya vayusena ke vimaanon ko udaane kii ichha lekar unhone poilat kii training lee. unhone sitar bajaane kii bhi shiksha lee or shastreey sangeet kaa anand liya. pune ke film and televijan institute of india main abhinay kaa prashikshan lene ke liye aavedan bhi diya, lekin unhen laga ki ye ek agambhir kaam he, isliye unhone abhinayta banane kaa vichaar chhod diya. ramakrishna mishan ke ek vigyaapan main swami vivekanand kaa chitra dekhakar unhone sanyasi banane kaa faisla kar liya. lekin parivaar or chicago main ek swami kii salaah ke baad unhone sanyasi banane kaa vichaar chhod diya.
