 aaryasptshati sanskrit main muktak geeti-kavitaaon vaali ek kriti he. yah govardhanacharya kii rachana maani jaati he. isamen 700 aryaayein sangrahit honi chahie parantu vibhinn sanskarnon main aryaaon kii sankhya 760 tak pahunch gai he. at: yah kahana kathin he ki uplabdh aaryasptshati kshepakon se rahit he.
 madhyayug main yah sangrah kaaphi lokapriy tha or uski aryaaon kii chaaya lekar bahut si footkar rachanaayen likhi gayi. bihari kavi kii "satsai" is sangrah se bahut prabhavit he. aaryasptshati main hi yah ullekh miltaa he ki jo shringarras kii dhara praakrit main hi uplabdh thi usko sanskrit me avatarit karne ke liye yah prayaas kiya gaya he. yahan sanket haal kii "gathasptshati" kii ore he. haal ne praakrit gaathaon main shringarparak rachanaayen nibaddh kii he. govardhan ne inhi gaathaon ko apni aryaaon kaa adarsh banaaya. praakrit kaa gaathachand, sanskrit ke aryaalandon ke adhik nikat he at: govardhan ne aryaachand ko hi rachana ke liye chunaa. keval chand main hi nahin apitu bhavchitran main bhi govardhan haal kaa bahudhaa anukaran karte hai. parantu isse yah nahin samajhnaa chahie ki aaryasptshati, gathasptshati kaa sanskrit anuwaad maatr he. jab bhi govardhan kisi gaatha ke bhav ko vyakt karnaa chaahate hai, ve usamen apni moulik pratibha ke pradarshan se nahin chookte. at: aaryaspshati gathasptshati se sthoolrup main prabhavit hote hue bhi apane aapmen mailic he.
 shringaar kii abhivyakti ke liye govardhan ko acharya mana jaataa he. inako rachanaaon main shringaar kaa uddaam rup khulkar aayaa he. kahin-kahin to nagn chithran apni nagnata ke kaaran rasabhas utpann kar dete hai. ek jagah to govardhan ne prem main shav ke chumban kii bhi baat kahi he. parantu abhivyakti kii tivrata, alankarisanyojna tathaa vyanjana kii gambhirtha ke kaaran govardhanacharya kii gananaa satkaviyon main kii ja sakati he.
