 isisi vanade championships main ek divasiy antarrashtriya vanade cricket antarrashtriya cricket parishad dwaara chalaae pratiyogita he. main he ki yah bas ek ranking yojana niyamit vanade match samay par madha he pratiyogita kaalpanik he. har vanade match ke baad donon tiimon ko ek ganitiya sutara ke aadhaar par ank praapt shaamil kiya gaya. prayek team ke ank kul dwaara maichon kii unki kul sankhya ek darza dene ke liye khelaa baanta gaya he, or sabhi tiimon reating ke kram main ek talika main krambaddh hai.
 saadrishy dwaara cricket ballebaaji ausat, ek vanade match jeetne ke liye ank, hameshaa team kii reating se adhik kar rahe reating badhati ja rahi he, or ek vanade match haarane ke liye ank hameshaa reating se kam nahin hai, reating ko kam karne. ucch or nimna darja tiimon ke bich ek match taiyaar kii ucch darja team kii keemat par kam rated team ko phayda hoga. ek 'ausat' he ki team ke rup main aksar ke rup main yah kho detaa majboot or kamjor tiimon main se ek mishran khelte samay jeetta 100 kii reating karnaa chahie tha.
