 lauhavidhyut kuch padaarthon ke khud hi swayan main vidyut dhruvon ko bana lene ke swabhaav ko kehete hai. masalan barium titanet main khud hi rinatmak or ghanaatmak vidyut dhruv ban jate hai jinsein us padaarth main sthai rup se vidyut dwidhruv aghurna aa jaataa he. lauhavidhyut padaarth par bahari vidyut kshetra lagane se yah dhruvikaran hataaya ja saktaa he.
 padaarthon main lauhavidhyut swabhaav dikhne se pehle vaigyanik louhachumbakatv dekh chuke the jisamen louhachumbaki padaarth apane-aap hi uttar or dakshini chumbakeey dhruv bana lete hai. louhachumbaki padaarth lohe yaa usee jaise dhaatu ke hote hai. jab vaigyanikon ne aisi hi chiz kuch anya padaarthon main dekhi to use lauhavidhyut bulaane lage haalaanki adhiktar lauhavidhyut padaarthon kaa lohe se kuch bhi lenaa-dena nahin hota.
