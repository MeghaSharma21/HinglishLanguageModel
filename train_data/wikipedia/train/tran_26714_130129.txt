 sanskrit yadhyapi bharat kii sabase pracheen bhashaa maani jaati he. is kaaran bharat main sanskrit hona swaabhaavik he. sanskrit bhashaa ko devvani bhi kaha jaataa he. bharat main adikalon se sanskrit bhashaa chali aa rahi he.
 sanskrit bhashaa mukhyatah bharat kii hi bhashaa he is kaaran bharat main sanskrit ke shaikshanik sansthaanon kii koi kami nahin he is vazah vibhinn rajyon main santhaan khole gaye hai. jo kuch is prakaar he - sarkari sanskrit vishvavidhyalay ,varanasi ,jagadguru ramanandacharya rajasthan sanskrit vishvavidhyalay tathaa rashtriya sanskrit santhaan ityadi sanskrit ke saadhan he.
