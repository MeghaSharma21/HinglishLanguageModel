 nihaal singh vaala moga jila, punjab, bharat main ek nagar he. yah moga jile main ek pramukh tehsil bhi he. yah nagar guru gobind singh maarg par padataa he. nikatatam shaharon main moga, barnaala, bathinda, faridakot or ludhiana hai. 34 gaanv nihaal singh vaala tehsil ke antargat aate hai. 
 nagar kii aabaadi lagbhag 5000 he or yah ek nagar samiti he. 
 nagar main pramukh skool - royal kanvent skool, green vaili convent skool, kamala nehru skool, sarkari hai orpraathmik skool hai. najadik ke gaanv manuke main iamti college, or patto hira singh main sarkari college he.
