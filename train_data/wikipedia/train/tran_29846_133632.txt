 guge pashchimi tibet kaa ek pracheen rajya tha jo 10veen shatabdi isvi ke baad apane charam par pahunchaa. apane sabase bade vistaar main iskaa raaj bharat ke kuch ilaaqon tak pahunch gaya, jinamein himachal pradesh kaa spiti kshetra or upree kinnaur zila, tathaa jammu va kashmir kii zanskaar ghati shaamil the. iskaa kendra tibet ke ngaari vibhaag ke aadhunik zanda zile main tha. isaki rajdhani tsaprang thi, jiske khandhar avshesh aaj bhi khadey hai.
