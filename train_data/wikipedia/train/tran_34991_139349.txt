 saanskrithik-aitihaasik manovigyaan, manovigyaan kii ek shakha he jiskaa vikaas liv vaigotsaki, alexandra luria tathaa unke mandal dwaara 1920 or 1930 ke dhashkon ke madhya pratut kiya gaya. yadhyapi vaigotsaki ke lekhan main 'saanskrithik-aitihaasik manovigyaan' kahin bhi nahin aata, kintu unke mandal tathaa anuyaayiyon ne isi naam se unke siddhanth ko aage badhaayaa. vaigotsaki-luria pariyojna kaa lakshya ek 'naye manovigyaan' kii sthapana karnaa tha jo man, mastishk or samskruti ke apruthkkaraniya ekta ko kii bhoomika ko samajha sake.
