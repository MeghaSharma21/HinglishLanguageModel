 oke janu 2017 kii ek bhartiya romaanchak natya film he jiskaa nirdesan shaad ali ne kiya he jabaki film ke nirmaataa karan johar he tathaa dharma productions ke binar tale film pradarshit hogi. film main shraddha kapur or aditya roy kapur mukhya kirdaar he. yah film maani ratnam par aadhaarit he jo ki ek tamil film oke kanmani film kii punahnirmit he.
 29 farvari 2016 ko film kaa pehla drishy pradarshit kiya gaya tha. jabaki yah film 13 janvari 2017 ko cinemagharon main pradarshit hui.
