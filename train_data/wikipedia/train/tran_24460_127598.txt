 companiyaan apane kamarchaariyon ko phone, car yaa altie, altisi jaisi yatraaon ke liye suvidhaayen detii hai. inke badale un par jo tex lagaaya jaataa he, use frinz benefit tex kehete hai. lekin adhiktar udyog, sanghathan athwa companiyaan epabiti kaa boj kamarchaariyon par hi daal detii hai or ise unki tankhwaah main se kaata jaataa he. companiyaan epabiti ko tex kii dohari maar maanati hai, kyonki ve aay par bhi tex detii hai or sahuliyaton par bhi.
