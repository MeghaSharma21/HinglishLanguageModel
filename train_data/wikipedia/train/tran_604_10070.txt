 charam udagr shikhar, yaa sankshipt main keval charam un shikharon kaa ek vargeekaran he jinaki sthalakritik udagrata 1,500 meter yaa usase adhik he. vishwa main aisi lagbhag 1,524 chotiyon hai. udagrata ke anusaar vishwa ke pehle teen sabase prasiddh charam udagr shikhar hai, everest parvat, akankagua or mckinale parvat. matterhorn or ijar jaisi kuch prasiddh chotiyon, charam udagr shikhar nahin hai kyonki ve unche pahaado se darron ke dwaara judi hai or isliye inhen paryaapt sthalakritik udagrata hasil nahin he.
