 compost ek prakaar kii khaad he jo jaivik padaarthon ke apghaton evam punahchakran se praapt kii jaati he. yah jaev krishi kaa mukhya ghatak he. compost banane kaa sabase saral tarika he - nam jaev padaarthon kaa dher banaakar kuch kaal tak pratiksha karnaa taaki iskaa vighatan ho jaay. vighatan main kuch saptaah yaa maheene lagate hai. uske baad vah humas main badal jaataa he. compost banane kii aadhunik vidhi kai charnon main purna hoti he or prayek charan main jal, vaayu evam carbon tathaa nitrogen se samrudh padaarthon ko bade nape-tule dhang se daalaa jaataa he.
