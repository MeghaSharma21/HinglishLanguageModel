 granada dakshini pashchimi spain ke aandalusia swytt samudaay main sthit ek praant he. granada praant kii rajdhani bhi granada naam kaa hi ek shahar he. is praant kaa kshetrafal 12,635 varg kimi he, yani bharat ke nagaland rajya se thodaa chota. granada praant kii aabaadi 876,184 he jinamein se 30% isaki rajdhani main rahete hai. spain ke muslim raaj kaal ke dauraan granada main "al humraa" naam kaa ek mahal or qila bana tha jo vishwa-prasiddh he or jise dekhne duniya bhar se sailaani aate hai.
