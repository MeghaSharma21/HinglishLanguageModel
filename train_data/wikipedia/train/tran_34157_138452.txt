 gharelu praudyogiki se aashay ghar or ghar ke andra upyog aane vaali saamagree se sambandhit praudyogiki se he. gharelu praudyogiki ke kai paksh hai. ek taraf refricrator, dhulaai mashin aadi grihopayogi saamaan he to duusari taraf grih praudyogiki anuprayukt vigyaan kaa upyog karke kisi lakshya ko praapt karne kii koshish karata he. 
 aajkal ghar main anekon praudyogiki kaa prayog kiya ja rahaa he. unamem se kuch kii suchi neeche di gayi he-.
