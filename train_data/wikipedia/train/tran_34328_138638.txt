 english cricket team ne farvari, march or april 2006 ke dauraan bharat kaa daura kiya. english cricket team pakistan ke khilaaf apane vinaashkari spel se pehle unhen isisi test championships main duusare sthaan par le jane vaale form ko banaae rakhane ke icchhuk tha, or jisane australia main ghar par 2005 ashes siries jeetne main madad kii. yah lakshya saamaanya pet kii shikayaton se kaaphi had tak rukaa hua tha, jo lagbhag hameshaa bhartiya daur ke bhartiya dauron main english team ko kuchalane ke liye or captain maaikel won kii chot kii ek punrariti hoti he; swing bowler simon jones or ashley gaills kii anupasthiti, jo oparation ke liye daure ko yaad nahin karti thi. iske saath hi, stand-in captain marcus trescothique "vyaktigat kaarnon" ke liye ghar chale gaye, aage nahin bolnaa chaahate the, andrew flintoff ko chhodkar, jo apane bete ke janm se chook gaye, pehali baar captain kaa khitaab lene ke liye captain do taraf se antarrashtriya cricketron: england kii ore se elester kook or monti panesar ke saath-saath shantkumaran srisanth, piyush chawla or munaf patel.
 bharat ne isisi test championships main duusare sthaan par pahunchane kii ummid kii shrinkhalaa shuru kii, jisamen england ne unhen haal main baahar kar diya.
 teen test maichon or saat ek divasiy antarrashtriya aayojan kiye gaye the. barish ke kaaran ek ekadivasiy baahar dhoyaa gaya. test shrinkhalaa 1-1 se drow kii gai jabaki bharat ne ekadivasiy shrinkhalaa 5-1 jiti.
