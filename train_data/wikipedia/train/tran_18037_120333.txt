 liyu moldova kii mudra he. romanian liyu kii tarah moldovan liyu 100 bani main samavibhajit he. mudra kaa naam romania se janma he, jiskaa arth he sher.
 varsh 1918 or 1940 or 1941 se 1944 ke bich, jab moldova romania kaa hissaa tha, moldova ke purvi hisse main romanian liyu kaa istemaal kiya jaataa tha. soviet sangh ke vighatan or swatantra moldova ganraajya ke gathan ke baad 29 navambar 1993 ko moldovan liyu jari kiya gaya. isane ek liyu = 1000 coupon kii dar par puraane coupon mudra kaa sthaan liya. 
 moldova se alag hue gaiyr manyata praapt transnistria main transnistria ruble kaa chalan he.
 sikkon ke rup main 1993 main aluminium se bane 1, 5, 10 or 25 bani, aluminium-tamba se bane 50 bani or jasta yukt lohe ke 1 or 5 liyu ke sikke jari kiye gaye. lekin baad main prachlan se hataa liye gaye.
 moldovan banknote kii do shrinkhlaayein jari kii gayi. pehali shrinkhalaa alp samay ke liye hi prachlan main rahi, jisamen keval 1, 5 or 10 liyu ke note shaamil the.
