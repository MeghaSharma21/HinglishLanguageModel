 mathan bharat ke jharkhand praant kaa ek pramukh shahar he. jo kidanbad jile me aata he. yaha par damodar vaili corporation pariyojna ke tahat baraakar nadi par mathan baandh kaa nirmaan kiya gaya he. is baandh ke nirmaan ke peeche uddeshy tha, barh kii vibhishika ko kam karnaa, jalavidyut paidaa karnaa, nahar ke jariye dur-durtak krishi bhumi tak pani pahuchana.
 
 darshneey sthal
 baandh ke paas pahari ke neeche bhumigat jal vidyut paidaa kii jaati he. baandh ke paas hi sundar park kaa nirmaan kiya gaya he. dhanbad or aasapaas kekshetron kaa pramukh picnic spot he. paas hi kaali mata kaa ek bada or pracheen mandir he, jise maa kalyaneshwari kaa mandir bhi kaha jaataa he.
 dur-dur se log mata ke darshan, mundan va shaadi-vivaah ke liye aate he. 
 
 
  jharkhand se sambandhit yah lekh apni praarambhik avasthaa main he, yani ki ek aadhaar he. aap ise badhaakar vikipedia kii madad kar sakate he.
