 ugrashrav sauti yaa ugrashravah puranic kaal ke bhaat the jinhone mahabharat,bhagavat puraana,harivansh, or padam puraana kaa vimochan kiya tha or unka vyakhaan multah naimisharany main rishi-muniyon ke saamne kiya tha. jab rishi vaishampayan ne janamejay ko bharat se lekar kurukshetra yuddh tak kuru vansh kaa sara vrittant sunaaya, us samay sauti vahiin maujuud the. unhone bhi yah mahakavy suna or naimisharany main jaakar saare rishi samooh, jo shaunak rishi ke ashram main ekatrit huye the, ko sunaaya.
