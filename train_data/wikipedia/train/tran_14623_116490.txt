 marindooke dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh kaa ek dweep he. prashasnic drushti se yah mimaropa prashasnic kshetra kaa bhaag he or praant kaa darja rakhata he. iske uttar main tayabaas khadi he or dakshin main sibuyan sagar. marindooke mindoro dweep se purv main, romablon dweepasmooh se uttar main or kezone praant ke bondok prayadweep se pashchim main he.
