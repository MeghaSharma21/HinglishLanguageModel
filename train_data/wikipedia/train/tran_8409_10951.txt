 okhotsk sagar prashanth mahaasaagar ke uttarpashchimi bhaag kaa ek samudr he. yah purv main kamachatkaa prayadweep, dakshinpurv main kuril dweepasmooh, dakshin main japan ke hokkaido dweep, pashchim main rus ke sakhalin dweep or pashchimottar main siberia ke tateey ilaake se ghira hua he. iskaa naam rus ke okhotsk shahar par padaa he jo sudur purv main rus kaa pehla qasbaa tha.
 okhotsk sagar kaa kshetrafal 15,83,000 varg kimi he or isaki ausat gaharaai 2,818 foot he. isaki sabase gahari khai 11,063 foot kii gaharaai tak jaati he. sakhalin dweep ke uttar or dakshin main yah japani sagar se judaa hua he. sardiyon main yahan itani thand ho jaati he ki isapar bhimkaay barf kii silliyaan banakar tairti rahati hai, jo aate-jate jahaazon ke liye bahut badi rukaawatein paidaa kar detii hai. japan ke hokkaido dweep ke alaava iske chaaron taraf roosi kshetra he, isliye is samudr ko bhi roosi-niyantrit kshetra hi mana jaataa he.
