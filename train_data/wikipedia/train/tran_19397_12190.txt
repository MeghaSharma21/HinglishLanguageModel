 hindi ke prasiddh sahityakar pandit hrishikesh chaturvedi kaa janm 22 disambar 1907 ko tadnusaar pus krishna 3 samvat 1964 ko hua. pandit chaturvedi ke sampurna krititv kaa prakaashan "hrishikesh rachanawali " naam se pustak ke rup main hua.
 chaturvedi ji parmparik dev vandana ke sanskaron se jude the. ve us sanskaar se apane kavita-kaal ke udai se ant tak jude rahe.
 san 1928 se ve chamatkaarpurn kala -paksheeya kavita se jude. is kshetra main ve itnaa aage bade ki aise lekhan vaale kisi bhi kavi ko unhone peeche chhod diya - saras chitrakavy, vilom kavy ," shriramkrishnakavy ", yamak shlesh yukt saras prabandh kavy "shriramkrishnayan " tathaa anek chamatkaarpurn padya panktiyaan likhin.
 san 1936 hindi main halawaad kaa shor machaa or khumaari ke liye umr khayyam kaa bataaya rasta sujhane laga tab chaturvedi ji ne bhartiya samskruti ke anukool bhaang kii khumaari ko thik tehraaya. bhaang or bhangdi par unhone 101 rubaayiyaan " vijay vatika " pustak main likhi jo shuddh hasya kii rachana he
 chaturvedi ji kii kavita kaa chauthaa sopan he- hasya vyayang. san 1947 main vibhaajan kii vibheeshikaa ne uname paine vyayang likhane kii samvedansheeltaa jaagrath kii or unhone samaaj sudhar ke liye hasya vyayang yukt kavita likhi jo kavi sammelnon ke manch se bahut lokapriy hui.
 unhone sanskrit, brajabhasha, khadi boli, urdu, angreji main bhi rachana kii.
 unhone aastik pathak ke or shrota ke liye " srisatyanarayan vrath katha " tathaa "durga saptshati " ke anuwaad bhi aisi bhashaa main kiye jise jan saadhaaran samaj sake. gita or meghdoot ke bhi unhone anuwaad kiye.
