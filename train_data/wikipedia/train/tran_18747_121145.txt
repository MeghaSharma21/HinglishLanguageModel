 bimal raay  hindi filmon kaa ek prasiddh film nirdeshak the.
 bimal royবিমল রায়12 july 1909 ko janmenshapur, dhaka jile, purvi bengal or assam, british bharat mar gaya 8 janvari 1966 mumbai, maharashtra, bharatbaio nirmaataa or nirdeshkapati Manobina roybaccherinki bhattacharyayasodhara rockprajita sinhajoy bimal roybimal roy  ek bhartiya film nirdeshak the. unhone vishesh rup se do bigha jameen, parinita, Biraj bahu, madhumati, sujatha, bandini or kii tarah apane yathartwaadee or samaajawaadee filmon ke liye vikhyath he, use hindi cinema kaa ek mahatvapurn nideshak bana rahi he. italvi nav-yathartwaadee cinema se prerit hokar, vah vittorio di sika kii cycle chor dekhne ke baad do bigha zamin banaaya he. unka kaam vishesh rup se apni mise en drishy jisamen vah yatharthwaad chitrit karne ke liye karyrat hai ke liye janaa jaataa he. unhone ek jeeta gyaarah filmphere puraskaar, do rashtriya film puraskaar, or kaan film mahotsav ke antarrashtriya puraskaar sahit apane karier ke dauraan, puraskaron kii sankhya. madhumati 1958 main 9 filmphere puraskaar, ek ricard 37 saal ke liye yojit jeeta.
