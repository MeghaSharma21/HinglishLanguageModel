 grih-niti yaa gharelu niti ve prashasnic nirnay hai jo raashtra kii seemaa ke andar ke muddon se sambandhit hote hai. yah videsh niti se bilkul alag niti he. grih-niti ke antargat bahut se kshetra aate hai jinamein pramukh hai- vyapaar, shiksha, urja, swasthya seva, kanoon, dhan-sampatti evam kar, prakritik sansaadhan, saamaajik kalyaan, vyaktigat adhikaar tathaa swatantrata aadi.
