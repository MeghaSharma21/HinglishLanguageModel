 sanskrit or pali sahitya main anbashth jati tathaa ambashth desh kaa ullekh anek sthalon par miltaa he. inke atirikt sikandar ke itihaas se sambandhit katipay greek or roman lekhakon kii rachanaaon main bhi anbashth jati kaa varnan hua he. diodoras, kurtiyas, justin tathaa talemi ne vibhinn uccharanon ke saath is shabd kaa prayog kiya he. prarambh main anbashth jati yudhopjivi thi. sikandar ke samay uskaa ek ganatantr tha or vah chenab ke dakshini tat par nivas karti thi. aage chalakar ambashthon ne sambhavat chikitsashastra ko apana liya, jiskaa parigyaan hamen manusmriti se hota he.
