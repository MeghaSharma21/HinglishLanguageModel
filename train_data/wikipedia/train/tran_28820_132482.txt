 castella de aguada, jise bandra kile ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he, mumbai main bandra kshetra main sthit ek durg he. castella, portugali shabd castelo kaa apbhransh rup he. iskaa sahi naam castelo de aguada hona chaiye, kintu iske nirmaataaon dwaara ise forte de bandora kaha gaya tha. yahan bandra dweep kii bhoomi ke ant par sthit he.1640 main portugaliyon ne ise pehre kii minar ke uddeshy se banaaya tha, kyonki yahaan se mahim kii khadi, arabi samudr or mahim ke dakshin dweep par acchhee najar rakhi ja sakati he. 1661 main iskaa samrik mahatv or bad gaya jab portugaliyon ne british samraat jorge dwitiya ko dahej swaroop tatkaaleen bombai ke saat dweep saunp die the.
 durg ke naam se gyaat hota he ki vah sthaan jahaan taaja pani ek fawwaare ke rup main uplabdh tha . yah oon portugali jahaajon ke liye tha jo praarambhik avadhi main taton ke liye upasthit rahete the. samudri tat se 24 meter kii oonchai par bana yah durg kai staron par basaa hua he. bollywood filmon main castella de aguada kai filmo main dikhaaya gaya he jise dil chahta he or buddha mil gaya.
