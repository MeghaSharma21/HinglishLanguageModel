 jin do laa phantan  france ke bal-kathakaar evam kavi the.
 chato thiari main san 1621 main janme laa phantan ne dharamshastra tathaa kanoon kii shiksha grahan kii or kuch samay baad ve paris chale gaye. vahaan unhone gramin kavyasamvaad, virakavy, gathakavy, geetikavita aadi likhnaa prarambh kiya. 1665 main unki kahaniyaan prakashit hui. san 1668 se 1694 ke bich unhone panchatantr ke dhang kii kalpit kathaon kii 12 pustaken prakashit karai. san 1695 main uski mrityu ho gai.
 apane prakrutiprem, manovaigyanik antardrushti tathaa ahladakaari hasya vinod ke kaaran usne in kalpit akhyayikaon ke rup rang main nayaa vistaar kiya. naitik upadeshak ke rup main vah praaia: nirashaavadi tha kintu manushya kii kamajoriyon kii jaankaree ke baavjood usamen katutaa nahin aa pai. sukh prapti ke icchhuk logon ko usne ekantavaas tathaa prakrutisampark main rahane kii salaah di he. vah us samay kaa mahaan kalakaar tha jab saubhaahgya se chaudaaven looyee ke shasankaal main france main sukhyat lekhakon kii acchhee jamaat vidyaman thi.
