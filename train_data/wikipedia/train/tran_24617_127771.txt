 pankhudi pathak samaajawaadee party kii rastria pravaktaa ke pad par niyukt thi |
 samaajawaadee party ke adhyaksh akhilesh yadav dwaara pravaktaa ke pad par niyukt kiya gaya tha. 
 pankhudi ne 2017 ko party chhod diya. 
 inkaa janm 1992 main hua tha.
 - dilli main rahane vaali pankhudi non political becoground se hai.unke pita jesi pathak or maa aarti pathak doctor hai, jo nijee practice karte hai. dilli uniwarsity kii lau student pankhudi pathak hai. unka ek chota bhai chiraag pathak he.
 pankhudi lambey samay se samaajawaadee party kii chaatr sabha se judi rahi hai.
 2010 main hansraj college ke chunaav main unhone joint secretary pad kaa chunaav jeeta. us samay inaki umr lagbhag 18 saal thi. unhone 2 se 3 saal tak party kii taraf se pratyaashiyon ko chaatrasangh kaa chunaav bhi ladaaya.
 pankhudi samaajawaadee party ke saath akhilesh yadav or unki wif dimple yadav se kaaphi prabhavit hai. samaajawaadee party main vah 2010 main party se judin hai.
