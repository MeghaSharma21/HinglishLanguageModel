 magnetron adhik shakti kii sookshmatarangen paidaa karne vaali ek nirvaat nalika he. isamen electronon kii dhara par chumbakeey kshetra kii sankriya se sookshmatarangen utpann kii jaatin hai. aajkal inkaa upyog microwave oven, evam radar ke vibhinn roopon main prayukt hoti he.
 aadhunik samay main prayukt magnetron vastav main 'anunaadi kotor magnetron' he. yah apane purvavarti magnetron tub kaa parivartit rup he jiskaa vikaas san 1940 main randal evam boot ne kiya. is avishkaar ke kaaran centimetre-band radar kaa nirmaan sambhav hua .
