 
 ratanjot, jise rang-e-badshaah bhi kehete hai, borajinasie jeevvaigyanik kul kaa ek phooldaar paudha he. iske nile rang ke phool hote hai.
 isaki jad se ek maheen laal rangane vaala padaarth miltaa he jiskaa prayog khaane or pine kii chizon ko laal rangane ke liye hota he, masalan bharat ke rogan josh vyanjan kii tari kaa laal rang aksar is se banaaya jaataa he. iske alaava davaaiyon, telon, sharaab, ityaad main bhi iske laal rang kaa istemaal hota he. kapade rangane ke liye bhi iskaa prayog hota he. vastav main 'ratanjot' naam is paudhe kii jad se nikalne vaale rang kaa he lekin kabhi-kabhi puure paudhe ko bhi isi naam se pukaara jaataa he. aadhunik kaal main E103 ke namakan vaala khaadya rang, jise alcanin bhi kehete hai, isi se banataa he.
 dhyaan den ki kabhi-kabhi jatropha ko bhi ratanjot kaha jaataa he.
