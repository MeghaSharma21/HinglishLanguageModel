 giza misr main teesraa sabase bada shahar he. yah nil nadi ke pashchimi tat par kendriya kahiraa se 20 ki.me. dakshin-pashchim main sthit he. 
 giza giza pathaar ke sthaan ke rup main sabase prasiddh he - vishwa ke kai prabhaavshaalee pracheen smarakon kaa sthaan, jisamen mahaan sphinks, mahaan giza ke pyramid or kai anya bade pyramid or mandir sammilit hai.
 ek samay main giza kaa mahaan pyramid "praim meridian" - jiske aadhaar par deshaantar nirdhaarit karte hai - ke sthaan ke liye prastaavit kiya gaya tha.
