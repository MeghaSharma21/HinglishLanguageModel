 1990 ke isisi trophy ek simit overon ke cricket 4 june or 23 june 1990 ke bich netherland main yojit toornament tha. yah chauthi isisi trophy toornament kaa manchan kiya ja karne ke liye pehali baar england ke baahar yojit kiya gaya tha, or. yah bhi ek sheershak praayojak ke liye sabase pehle isisi trophy pratiyogita thi, aadhikarik taur par unibind isisi trophy ke rup main janaa ja rahaa he. pichley trophy main ke rup main, match 60 over main ek paksh par or safed kapade or laal gendon ke saath khelaa gaya. zimbabwe lagaataar teesri baar ke liye pratiyogita jeet lee, duusari baar final main netherland kii dhadkan or bhi ve har khel toornament main khelaa jeet he, or ke rup main vijetaon ko 1992 vishwa cap ke liye kwalifai kiya. kuch hi samay baad trophy kaa manchan kiya gaya tha, zimbabwe isisi ke purna sadsy kaa darja ke liye protsaahit kisi bhi maamle main the.
 satraa tiimon ke toornament main chunaav ladaa. samay main antarrashtriya cricket parishad ke sabhi unniis sahyogi sadsy, japan or pashchim africa ke saath hi logon ko ek team bhejne ke liye nahin toornament main pratispardhaa karne ke liye paatr the.
