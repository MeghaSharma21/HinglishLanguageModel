 jayadeep ahlavat ek bhartiya abhinayta hai. unhone bollywood main akshay kumaar nirmit film aakrosh se abhinay kii shuruuaat kii, yaddapi unka sabase mahatvapurn abhinay anuraag kashyap kii film gangs of vasepur main shahid ke rup main rahaa.
 unka janm rohtak, haryana ke gaanv kharkada ke ek jat parivaar main hua or unhone abhinay ke kshetra main apni snaatak kii shiksha bhartiya film or television santhaan se 2008 main purna kii.
 jayadeep ahlavat ne apane snaatak ke baad abhinay ke kshetra main kadam rakhaa. unhone pratham abhinay priyadarshan kii film khatta mittha main khalanaayak ke rup main kiya. usee varsh wo ajay devgan ke saath film aakrosh main najar aaye. uske baad unhone kai bade binar kii filmein bhi kii jaise anuraag kashyap kii gangs of vasepur or camel hasan kii vishvarupam .
