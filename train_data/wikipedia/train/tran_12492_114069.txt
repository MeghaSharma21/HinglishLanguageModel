 gatik parisar kisi char raashi ke adhiktam evam nunatam maanon ke anupaat ko kehete hai jo kisi yukti, upkaran, yantra yaa ang dwaara grahan kiye ja sakate hai. ise yaa to anupaat ke rup main abhivyakt kiya jaataa he yaa anupaat ke laghuganak dwaara abhivyakt karte hai. udhaaharan ke liye maanav ke kaan evam netra kii gatik parisar bahut adhik hoti he . 'gatik parisar' kaa upyog dhwani, sangeet, electronic, maapan, photography aadi main hota he. adhik gatik parisar vaale yantra, upkaran or yuktiyaan acchhee maani jaatin he.
 yadi koi bahumapi kam se kam 1 milivolt or adhik se adhik 1000 volt kaa maapan kar saktaa he to kahenge ki isaki gatik paraas 1000000 yaa 120 dibi he. yadi koi electranik tula kam se kam 1 gram tathaa adhik se adhik 10 kilo taul sakati he to uskaa gatik parisar = 10000 = 80 dibi hua. is tula kii vibhedan kshamta 1 gram he. koi duusari tula 1 milligram nunatam se lekar 1 gram adhiktam taul saktaa he to uski vibhedan kshamta behatar he kintu uski gatik parisar kam he .
 arakhiyata, santrupti evam anya kaarnon se vibhinn yuktiyon gatik parisar badhanaa chunauteepurn kaary he. gatik parisar badhaane ke liye vibhinn kshetron main alag-alag tareeke apanaae jate hai.
