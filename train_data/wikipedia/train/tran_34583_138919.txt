 dadagiri balaprayog se yaa dhamkaakar kisi anya yaa anyon kaa apmaan karne, kaam nikalwane, manovaigyanik rup se haaavee hone yaa apana prabhutv jamaane ke prayaas ko kehete hai. yah durvyavhaar aksar baar-baar doharaaya jane vaala or dadagiri karne vaale ke liye aadat ban jane vaala hota he. dadagiri anya prakaar kii jhadpon se bhinn he kyonki isamen dadagiri karne vaala apane shaareerik yaa saamaajik bal ko duusare paksh se kahin adhik samajhataa he or swayan ko kisi bhi prakaar kii haani se surakshit mahasoos karata he. dadagiri kaa vyavhaar aksar kanoonee aparaadh kii shrenee main aata he.
