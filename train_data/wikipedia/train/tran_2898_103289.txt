 swami sumedhanand saraswati rajasthan ke sikar jile main sthit vaidic ashram piparali ke ek sant hai. sikar lok sabha nirvachan kshetra se 16veen loksabhaa main bhaajapa saansad hai.
 sumedhanand kaa janm san 1952 main ko rohtak, haryana ke ek dhaaka parivaar main hua. unke pita kaa naam mayaram arya he. unke janm pramaan patr ke anusaar unka janm haryana ke rohtak sthit baland gaanv main ek jat parivaar main hua. unhone 1964 main grihatyag kar aadhyatmik granthon kaa adhyayan armbh kiya. iske baad wo arya samaaj kii seva main lag gayi. wo vaidic ashram piparali ke adhyaksh hai. wo sarvadeshik arya pratinidhi sabha, nai dilli kii sanchaalan samiti ke adhyaksh bhi hai. wo pichley kai varshon se "gau sarankshan" or "shiksha sanskaar" abhiyaan chalaa rahe hai.
 sumedhanand saraswati ne chunavi dangal main pehali baar 16veen lok sabha chunaav ke liye 2014 main chunaav ladaa. unhen yoga guru baba ramdev ke anurodh par bhaajapa ne sikar lok sabha kshetra kaa pratyaashee banaaya. isamen unki sampati maatr 34 hajaar teen sau gyaarah rupee thi. yah sohalavi loksabhaa ke saansadon main sabase kam sampati vaale saansad hai. 2014 ke aam chunaav main rajasthan se jeetne vaale saansadon main sumedhanand saraswati aayu ke hisaab se karnal sonaaram or chotu ram choudhary ke baad tisare sabase bade saansad hai. rajasthan main unke alaava do anya sanyasi bhi mahant chandnath or arjunpuri bhi chunavi maidan main the.
