 jat sikh yaa punjabi bhashaa main jatt sikh sikh dharm main yaqiin rakhane vaale jat jati ke samudaay ko pukaara jaataa hai. yah log bharat ke rajya punjab, uttari rajasthan, haryana, dilli ke alaava bharat bhar main har jagah nivas karte hai thodi thodi sakhya main. jatt sikh purvi pakistan main bhi bahutayat sankhya main he. punjabi daliton ke baad jat sikh bhartiya punjab kii sabase badi aabaadi he.
 jat sikh se saat jatt sikh filhal ke samay canada kii sansad main sadsy hai. jaaton main sikh jaaton ne jis tarah se taraqqi kii wo kaabile taareef hai. jatt sikh pankhad andhvishwaas se kose dur sachai ke maarg par chalane vaali mahaan jat qaom he. bhagat singh,hari singh nalava, maharaja ranjit singh, banda singh bahadur bairangi, kartaar singh sarabha, udham singh, ityadi bahut se aise mahapurush rahe hai jo jatt sikh samudaay se hai.
