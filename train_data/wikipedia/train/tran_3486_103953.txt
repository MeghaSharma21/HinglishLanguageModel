 fasal yaa sasy kisi samay-chakra ke anusaar vanaspatiyon yaa vrikshon par maanvon va paaltu pashuon ke upbhog ke liye ugaakar kaati yaa todi jane vaali paidaavaar ko kehete hai. masalan genhu kii fasal tab taiyaar hoti he jab uske dane pakakar pile se ho jaaen or us samay kisi khet main ug rahe samast genhu ke paudhon ko kaat liya jaataa he or unke kanon ko alag kar diya jaataa he. aam kii fasal main kisi baag ke pedo par aam pakane lagate hai or, bina pedo ko nuksaan pahunchaae, falon ko todakar ekatrit kiya jaataa he.
 jab se krishi kaa avishkaar hua he bahut se maanvon ke jeevankram main fasalon kaa bada mahatv rahaa he. udhaaharan ke liye uttar bharat, pakistan va nepal main rabi kii fasal or kareef kii fasal do badi ghatnaayen hai jo badi had tak in kshetron ke gramin jivan ko nirdhaarit karti hai. isi tarah anya jagahon ke sthaneey mausam, dharti, vanaspati va jal par aadhaarit fasalen vahaan ke jivan-kramon par gaharaa prabhaav rakhti hai.
 bhartiya fasalon kaa vargeekaran bhinn-bhinn aadhaaron par kiya ja saktaa he. neeche kuch aadhaaron par bhartiya fasalon kaa vargeekaran diya gaya he.
