 vismaran se taatpary samaran kii vifaltaa se he. jab vyakti apane bhootkaal ke anubhavon ko chetan main laane main asafal ho jaataa he, tab use vismriti kehete hai. jis prakaar se jivan ko upyogi tathaa sukhi banane ke liye smriti aavashyak he, usee prakaar hamaare jivan main vismriti kii bhi upyogita tathaa mahatv he.
