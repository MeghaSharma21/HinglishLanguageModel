 chintan avadharanaon, sankalpanaaon, nirnyon tathaa siddhanto aadi main vastugat jagat ko paravartit karne vaali sankriya he jo vibhinn samasyaon ke samaadhaan se judi hui he. chintan vishesh rup se sangathit bhootadravya-mastishk- kii ucchtam upaj he. chintan kaa sambandh keval jaivik vikascram se hi nahin apitu saamaajik vikaas se bhi he. chintan kaa udbhav logon ke utpaadan kaaryaklap kii prakriya ke dauraan hota he or vah yatharth kaa vyavahrut paravartan sunishchit karata he. apane vishisht mul, kriyalap ke dhang or parinaamon kii drushti se uskaa swaroop saamaajik hota he. isaki pushti is baat main he ki chintan shram tathaa vaani ke kaaryaklap se, jo keval maanav samaaj kii abhilakshankataayen hai, avichchhedya rup main judaa hua he. isi kaaran manushya kaa chintan vaani ke saath ghanishtha rakhte hue moort rup grahan karata he or uskaa parinaam bhashaa ke rup main vyakt hota he.
