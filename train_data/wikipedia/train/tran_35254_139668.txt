 paridhi sharma bhartiya televijan kii ek vikhyath abhinetri he. inhone apane abhinay jivan kii shuruyaat varsh 2010 main dharawahik tere mere sapane se kii. baad main ve dharawahik ruk janaa nahin main bhi chuni gai. vartmaan main, vah aitihaasik dharawahik jodha akbar main rajkumaari jodhabai kii mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaa rahi he.
 inkaa janm 15 may 1987 ko indore, madhya pradesh ke baagh kshetra main hua tha. inhone rakrakhaav evam anusandhan kendra se bibie kii padhai puri kii. paridhi sharma kii shaadi tanmay saxena se hui he. donon ek-duusare se ek raktadan shivi main hui thi. vahiin pehali najar main donon main prem ho gaya tha. kuch saal ek-duusare se ke saath ghoomane-phirane ke baad inhone shaadi kar lee.
