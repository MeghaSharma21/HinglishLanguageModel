 zaak laakaan ek francisi manovishleshak or manovikaravigyaani the jinhen "froid ke baad sabase vivaadaaspad manovishleshak" kaha jaataa he. 1953 se 1981 tak paris main vaarshik seminar dene ke dwaara laakaan ne 1960 or 1970ve sadi ke kai mukhya francisi buddhijivion ko prabhavit kiya, khaas taur pe unhen jo uttar-sanrachnawad se jude hue hai. uttar-sanrachnawad, samalochanatmak siddhant, bhashavigyaan, 20ve sadi kaa faansisi darshan, film siddhant or naidanik manovishleshan par unke vichaaron kaa mahatvapooran prabhaav he.
