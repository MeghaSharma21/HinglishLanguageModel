 berlin samiti 1915 ke baad baad se bhartiya swatantraaa samiti, 1914 main germany main bhartiya chaatro or desh main rahane vaale raajnneetik kaaryakartaaon dwaara pratham vishwa yuddh ke dauraan germany main gathit ek sanghathan tha. samiti kaa uddeshy bhartiya swatantraaa ko badhaava dena tha. prarambh main ise berlin-bhartiya samiti kaha jaataa tha, sanghathan kaa naam badalakar 1915 main bhartiya swatantraaa samiti rakhaa gaya, or hindu-german shadyantra kaa ek abhinn hissaa ban gaya tha. samiti ke prasiddh sadasyon main virendranath chattopadhyaay, chempakaraman pillai or abinaash bhattacharya shaamil the.
 kai bharatiyon, vishesh rup se shyaamaji krishna verma ne 1905 main england main india house kaa gathan kiya tha. yah sanghathan, dadabhai nauroji, lala lajpat raay, madam bhikaaji kama or in jaise anya bhartiya diggajon ke samarthan ke saath bhartiya chaatro ko chaatravrutti kii peshkash, rashtravaadi kaary kaa padonnat, or upnivesh virdohi raay or vichaaron ke liye ek pramukh manch tha. krishna verma dwaara prakashit da indian sosiologist, ek prashikad aupniveshik virdohi patrika thi. india house se jude pramukh bhartiya rashtravaadiyon main damodar saavarkar, virendranath chattopadhyaay or har dayaal shaamil the.
 british sarkaar ne apane kaam kii prakruti or da indian sosiologist ke uttejit swar ke kaaran india house par najar rakhana chaalu kar diya, jisamen kai baar british aupniveshik adhikariyon kii hatya kaa karne kaa suzav diya jaataa tha. 1909 main, india house ke saath nikatataa se jude madanlaal dhingra ne bharat ke videsh sachiv ke raajnneetik edisi wiliam hat karzan baili kii goli markar hatya kar di. hatya ke baad, india house ko teji se dabaa diya gaya or krishna verma samet iske kai pramukh netaaon ko europe bhaagane ke liye majaboor hona padaa. kuch, virendranath chattopadhyaay sahit, germany chale gaye, jabaki kai netaa paris chale gaye.
