 vigyaan kii pragati se shikshapranali main bhi naveen vichaardhaaraon kaa janm hua he. isamen pariksha sambandhi parivartan ullekhneey he. vaigyanikon kii dharna rahi he ki likhitapariksha dwaara ham parikshaarthi ke un gunon ko napate hai jinhen naapnaa hamaara dhyey hota he. iske atirikt is pariksha main pareekshak kii nijee bhavanaayen ang pradaan karne main vishesh kaary karti hai. in donon se raksha karne ke liye yah uchit samajha gaya ki vishaynishth pariksha hi parikshaarthi ke moolyankan main sahaayak ho sakegi. is vichaardhaara ke falswaroop america main i. el. tharnadike ne sarvapratham awapti-pariksha ke paksh main 1904 main ek pustak likhi. uske pashchaat bhinn-bhinn deshon ke shikshavidon ne bhi apane desh kaa prachaar kiya. 
 un logon kaa vichaar he ki pramanit pariksha ke liye awaptipareeksha ek mukhya saadhan he. is prakaar kii kuch parikshaayen adhyay ke dwaara apane vishay ke gyaan ko naapane ke liye banaai jaati hai tathaa kuch vishaynishth parikshaayen pramanikrut kii jaati hai or unke dwaara ek kshetra ke parikshaarthiyon kii yogyata tulanaatmak rup se aasaani se naapi ja sakati he. awaptipareeksha banane ke pehle pareekshak ko yah swayan samaj lenaa chahie ki vah kis vastu ko naapnaa chahta he. use yah bhi jaan lenaa he ki awaptipareeksha parikshaarthi ke arjeet gyaan ko hi naapti he. awaptipareeksha banane main item ke chunaav main vishesh dhyaan dena chahie. inhen ke upar us pariksha kii manyata nirbhar karti he. kis tarah ke item hone chahie, iskaa gyaan "shaikshik sankhyashastra" se purna parichay hone par hi ho saktaa he. aajkal hamaare desh main is disha main kaary ho rahaa he or ol india concil for secondary ajukeshan ne videshi visheshgyon dwaara adhyapakon ke prashikshan ke liye suvidhaayen di hai.
