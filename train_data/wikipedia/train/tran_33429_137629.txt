 hindustani shastreey sangeet bhartiya shastreey sangeet ke do pramukh style main se ek he. duusari pramukh style he - karnataka sangeet.
 yah ek parampric udvikasi he jisane 11veen or 12veen shatabdi main muslim sabhyataa ke prasar ne bhartiya sangeet kii disha ko nayaa aayam diya. yah disha professor lalit kishor singh ke anusaar unani paaythagoras ke gram va arabi farsi gram ke anuroop aadhunik bilaval thaath kii sthapana maani ja sakati he. isse purv kaaphi thaath shuddh mail tha. kintu shuddh mail ke atirikt uttar bhartiya sangeet main arabi-farsi athwa anya videshi sangeet kaa koi doosraa prabhaav nahin padaa. "madhyakaalin musalmaan gayakon or nayakon ne bhartiya sanskaron ko banaae rakhaa." 
 rajdurbar sangeet ke pramukh sangrakshak bane or jahaan anek shaskaon ne pracheen bhartiya sangeet kii samrudh parampara ko protsaahan diya vahiin apni aavashyaktaa or ruchi ke anusaar unhone isamen anek parivartan bhi kiye. hindustani sangeet keval uttar bharat kaa hi nahin. bangladesh or pakistan kaa bhi shastreey sangeet he.
 yah parampar he ki jo utkrisht kalakaar ek darje ki uplabdhi hasil ki ho unhe mul rup se hindu vidwaan kalakaaro ko bhav muslim vidwaan kalakaaro ko ke khitaab se nawaja jaat he. 
 hindustani sagit kaa ek or pahalu bhi he jisme sufi jamane ki dharmik alhadagi thi, jisme ustad kolkaar git ki rachano ko ishwar ko yaad karne ke liye kiy karte the.12 ve sadi ke daur ke ant me hindustani shasreey sageet se hi dakshini karnataka sangeet vibhajit hua.
