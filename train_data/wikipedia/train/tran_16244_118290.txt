 aagaa khan saanskrithik trust aagaa khan dawalpment network ke adheen ek sanstha he. ye sanstha muslim samaaj kii imaaraton va samudaayon ke bhautik, saamaajik, saanskrithik evam aarthik sudhaar hetu karyrat he.
 is sanstha kii sthapana 1988 main hui va panjikaran geneva main hua tha. yah ek niji gaiyr sampradayik paropkaaree sanstha thi. yah sanstha aagaa khan dawalpment network kaa ek abhinn ang he, jisaki sthapana aagaa khan chaturth ne kii thi.
