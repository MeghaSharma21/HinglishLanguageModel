 nanpur ek teji se ubharta ek ausat shahar he, yah 22.26 ansh akshansh evam 80. 10 deshaansh par, mandla jile main sthit he, swatantraaa purv se is shahar main jabaki railgadiyaan apane nerogese rup se puure bharat main dauda karti thim tab nanpur teen bhinn dishaao evam jilon se aane-jane vaali gadiyon kaa jankshan tha, hai to aaj bhi lekin ab is jankshan main wo baat nahin he jo british kaal main thi.
