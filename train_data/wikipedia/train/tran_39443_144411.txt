 yah kasbaa sanra america ke texas rajya ke arlington nagar ke saath doharaadar he. bad konigshofan main ek manoranjan park he jo arlington nagar ke sammaan me "arlington" namit he. vahiin, duusari ore arlington main bhi varsh 2006 main ek jalakrida park kholaa gaya jiskaa naam bad konigshofan ke sammaan main "bad konigshofan family equatika centre" rakhaa gaya.
