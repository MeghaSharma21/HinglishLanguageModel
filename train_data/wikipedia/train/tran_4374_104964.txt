 yah sahaara marusthal se uttari purv disha main chalane vaali garm tathaa shushk hawa hai. harmaton ke aane se africa kaa ushn pashchimi tat suhaavna ho jaataa he. isi prabhaav ke kaaran gini tat par is hawa ko doctor vaayu ke naam se janaa jaataa hai kyonki yah vaayu is kshetra ke nivaasiyon ko ardra mausam se raahat dilaati hai. is prakaar kii pawan ko uttari america main blike roller va faras kii khadi ke uttar-purv main shaamaal kaha jaataa he.
