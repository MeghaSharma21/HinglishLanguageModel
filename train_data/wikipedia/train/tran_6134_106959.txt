 samvidhan smarak rajdhani ashgab main sthit, turkmenistan ke samvidhan ko samarpit ek sangemarmar yukt smarak he. 185 meter ooncha yah smarak, turkmenistan kii duusari sabase oonchi maanav-nirmit sanrachana he. yah ashgabat kii arqbil aivenue par sthit he. varsh 2008 se 2011 ke bich paulimex naamak ek turkian nirmaan company ne iski nirmaan pariyojna ko puraa kiya tha. ashgab tivi tower ke banane se pehle yah desh kii sabase oonchi imaarat thi. 
 is imaarat kii neenv ko arqbil rajmarg kii uttar kii ore may 2008 main rakhi gai thi. iskaa nirmaan paulimex naamak ek turkian nijee nirmaan company dwaara kiya gaya tha, is pariyojna kaa anubandhan mulya €4,50,00,000. is smarak kaa aadhikarik uddhatan samaaroh kaa aayojan, 18 may 2011 ko turkmenistan ke raashtrapati kii upasthiti main kiya gaya tha.
 yah smarak ek 185 meter oonche vargakar anuprast kaat vaale minar evam oguz khan ke sitare ke aakaar kaa ek nichle aadhaar se sanrachit he. smarak ke minar ke chaaron-pakshon ko turkmen geliam kaaleen kii vanavaton se sajaayaa gaya he or iske shikhar par ek vardhaman chand he jiske mukh par paanch sitare hai. minar ke nilchala aadhaar ko 27 meter oonche teen-stariy oguzakhaan ke sitare ke aakaar kaa banaaya gaya he, jiske chaaro ore seedhia or kritrim jharne hai. parisar ke andar ek sangrahalay, ek conference haul, ekasammelan kaksh, pustkaalay, uphaar kii dukaan or coffeeki dukaan he.
