 apik ek broujar he. isaki khaas baat yah he ki isamen kai feechar bharat ko centre main rakhkar banaae gaye hai.
 ise bengaluru kii hiden software riflex company ne viksit kiya he. vishvabhar main prayog kiya ja sakane vaala yah broujar bharat kaa pehla internet broujar hoga. bhartiya ejiniyaron kii ek team dwaara banaaya gaya is apik broujar ko mojila platform ke aadhaar par taiyaar kiya gaya he. 
 apik ko taiyaar karne vaasli company hiden riflex ke anusaar is broujar ko vistrut rup dene se pehle iske sankshipt sanskaran kaa pareekshan kiya gaya tha jo ki puri tarah se safal rahaa he. apik broujar par 1500 se zyada theme maujuud hai. broujar ke said baar par kai tarah kii aplication kii ek list he jis par clic karte hi koi bhi aplication chunaa ja saktaa he. masalan is broujar se tivetor, fasebuk or anya soshal networkig site par bhi log in kiya ja saktaa he.
 yahi nahin ise kul 12 bhartiya bhashaaon ke antahnirmit dhvanyatmak/budhimaan sampadak he. saath hi is broujar main tamaam letest khabaron, live tivi channel, video, stock kii jaankaree, live cricket score, nae mujeec album ke liye bhi cidbaar banaaya gaya he.
 ipic broujar me security ke liye bhi khaas intjaam hai. iske saath hi antiveyras system judaa hua he. pehle bhartiya broujar ko taiyaar karne vaali company hiden riflex ko bhartiya mul ke amareeki ejeeniyar alok bharadwaj dwaara varsh 2007 main sthapit kiya gaya tha.
