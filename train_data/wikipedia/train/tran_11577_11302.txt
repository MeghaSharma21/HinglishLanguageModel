 kuch mishradhatuen, jo saralata se nimna taap par hi pigal jaati hai, galaneey dhaatu kahi jaati hai. aisi mishradhaatuon main saadharantaya bismuth, bang, sis, cadmium yaa paara rahete hai. vang, sis athwa inase praapt mishradhaatuon main vishesh anupaat main vismath milaane se galnaank kam ho jaataa he. aisi kuch tritiyak dhaatuon kaa galnaank pani ke ubalane ke taap se bhi kam he.
 newton kii dhaatu, jisamen 50% vismath ke saath 31.25 % sis tathaa 18.75% vang rahata he, 98 se. par pighalati he. inhen dhaatuon se praapt rose darset tathaa lichenberg kii dhaatuen bhi kam taap par galaneey hai. inamen 50 bhaag bismuth ke saath vividh maatra main vang or sis rahete hai. inamen cadmium milaane par or bhi kam taap par pighalanevaali chaturtha dhaatuen praapt hoti hai. sara milaane se bhi galnaank kam ho jaataa he.
 bud kii dhaatu main jo 71 sein. par pighalta he, 50 bhaag bismuth, 25 bhaag sis, 12.5 bhaag cadmium or 12.5 bhaag vang rahete hai. inhen chaaron dhaatuon se lipovitz dhaatu bhi praapt hoti he.
 ye mishradhatuen byler ke surakshadat, swachalit chhidkaav karne vaale tathaa agni se bachaav ke anya upkarnon main prayukt hoti hai. taap kii nishchit seemaa se unchaa hone par in dhaatuon se nirmit daat gal jate hai. jaise aag lagane par, athwa vishesh ooncha taap hone par pani ke nal main lage aise daat ke galane se pani kaa pravaah swayan hi aarambh ho jaataa he. anya mahatvapurn kaaryo main, jaise vidyut kaa fus, shoulder tathaa gaspravaah ke roak banane or patali nalee ko modane main bhi in dhaatuon kaa upyog hota he. parevaali aisi mishra dhaatuen sharir ke vibhinn angon ke saanche banane main kaam aati hai.
