 prakaash jaavdekar bhartiya janata party ke netaa or bharat sarkaar ke maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantralay ke padasin kendriya mantri hai.
 inhen 2008 main maharashtra se sansad ke ek sadsy ke rup main rajyasabha ke liye nirvaachit kiya gaya tha, or 2014 main madhya pradesh se phir se nirvaachit kiya gaya.
 2014 bhartiya aam chunaav main bhartiya janata party kii jeet ke baad inhen pradhanmantri narendra modi dwaara paryaavaran, van or jalwaayu parivartan ke liye rajya mantri niyukt kiya gaya tha.
