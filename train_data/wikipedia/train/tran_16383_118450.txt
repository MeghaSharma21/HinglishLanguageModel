 sant mikel del faai ek cinobetic benedictine ashram he. yah bikes, katalonia, spain main sthit he. is gyarahavin shatabdi kii imaarat ko 1988 main bien de interes cultural ke rup ghoshit kiya gaya tha. .
 yah ashram ek purnatah surakshit prakritik vaataavaran main sthit he jise patharilii pahari chotiyaan jinhen singels berti ke naam se katalan ke tat-purv pahari main kaha jaataa he. 
 yahan kaa girjaghar ek romenesco style kaa dwaar rakhata he jo arth-golaaidaar maharaab se banataa he. yahan ke cholemon ke upar ped kii akritiyaan bani hai. girjaghar ke farsh ke upar do puraane girjagharon ke mukhya pathar spasht rup se dikhaai dete hai. ek taraf ke chote girjaghar main to qabaren hai. unamem se ek 13 veen sadi kii bataai jaati he or samajha jaataa he ki guillem, arl osona or ramon berenge pratham kii he jisane apane adhikaron kaa tyaag karte hue sant migal ne sanyaas grahan kiya tha. duusari qabr bataai jaati he ki andaru arabizu kii ho sakati he, jo navaron main sanyaas liye hue the parantu ashram ko vastuen laakar diya karata tha. ek purana hawaadaar ghar he jo 15 shatabdi se sambdhit he. kai varshon tak use ek aspataal ke taur par istemaal kiya gaya tha magar yah apni puraani banaavat ke anuroop hi he.
