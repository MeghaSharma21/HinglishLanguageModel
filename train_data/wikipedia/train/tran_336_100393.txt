 vedamata gayatri mandir mata gayatri devi kaa mandir he. gayatri maan kaa chhavi sthapit hone ke kaaran gayatri parivaar ke aacharyon dwaara is mandir kaa namakaran kiya gaya he, gayatri mandir ek prasiddh mandir he. gayatri mandir shri rampravesh kushvaha dwaara sthapit kiya gaya he. yah mandir kushinagar, uttar pradesh ke vigyaan main ek lambey samay se sthit he. gayatri mandir ke antargat vibhinn prakaar ke saanskrithik or saamaajik gatividhiyaan yojit hoti hai or shahar ke saanskrithik centre ke rup main kaary karata he. prabandh samiti veda mata gayatri mandir ke dwaara prayek varsh nau kund gayatri mahayajna evam adarsh vivaah samaaroh kaa aayojan kiya jaataa he.garib var-kanya jodo kii samuhik vivaah yajna ke purnahuti ke din sampann karaayaa jaataa he ek samruddhi sampann parivaar ki kanyaon ke samaan hi gayatri mandir main bhi uchit or jivan upyogi vastu ke saath aashirwad dekar ke kanyaon kii vidaai kiya jaataa he.
