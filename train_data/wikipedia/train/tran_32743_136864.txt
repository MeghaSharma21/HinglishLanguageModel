 chitrasen kurukshetra ke yuddh main kaurav sena kaa ek yoddha tha or uskaa vadh satyaki dwaara kiya gaya. chitrarasen angadesh ke raja karn kaa putr tha. karn kii duusari patni supriya ke chaar putron main sabase bada putr tha. mahabharat main yah bhi ulekh he ki paan dake agayatvas ke doran arjuna ne apn naam chhitrasen rekha.
