 baldirai, sultanpur jile kii tehsil he. amethi jila banane tak maatr jo ek blaak tha. yah kai gaanvon se ghira he, iske najadik main paara ek mukhya sthaan he jisaki aabaadi hind-muslim donon se milakar bani he, dharmik sauhaard ke liye visheshit he. lakhanu or baldirai ke bich kii duuri 120 kilometre he.aam bolchaal kii bhashaa avadhi he, hindi or urdu bhi boli jaati he.
