 kachin rajya burma kaa uttartam rajya he. iske purvi or uttari sarahadein china se milti hai, or iske pashchimottar main bharat sthit he. prantiya rajdhani myitcheena ke alaava bhaamo or putao yahan ke mukhya shahar hai. kachin rajya kaa 5889 meter unchaa khaakabo raji parvat bharat-burma kii seemaa par sthit he or burma kaa sabase unchaa parvat he. dakshinpurvi asia kii sabase badi jhiilon main se ek, induji jhil, bhi isi praant main padati he.
 kachin rajya ke adhiktar nivasee jingpo samudaay ke hai or jingpo bhashaa bolte hai. yah adhiktar isai dharm ke anuyai hai, haalanki inamen se bahut buddha dharm va sarvatmavaad main bhi manyata rakhte hai.
 burma ke samvidhananusaar 24 sitambar 1947 i. ko myitcheena evam bhaamo zilon ko milakar kachin rajya kaa nirmaan kiya gaya. kachin kaa kshetrafal lagbhag 15,500 vargamil he. yah rajya uttari burma main naga evam patkai pahadiyon ke purv tathaa salvin nadi ke pashchim main sthit he. iravati tathaa isaki sahaayak chadwin nadiyaan is rajya ke uttari bhaag se nikalakar dakshin kii ore bahati hai. is chhin-bhinn pahari evam pathari kshetra main ghaney jangal hai. purvi bhaag main kochani pahadiyaan uttar-dakshini faili hui hai. bhaamon tathaa myitcheena is rajya ke pramukh nagar hai. bhaamon chini seemaa se 20 mile kii duuri par sthit burma-china vyapaar kaa mukhya centre he. myitcheena rail dwaara mandley or rangoon se sambhddha he. yahan se 'ledo maarg' assam ko jaataa he. dhan evam makka is rajya kii mukhya upaj he. iske atirikt kapaas, tambaku, opium, matar, tihahan evam subjiyan bhi ugaai jaati hai. yah kshetra nirmaan kashtha ke liye prasiddh he jo nadiyon dwaara bahaakar mandley evam ragun ke kaarkaanon main pahunchaaya jaataa he. iravati tathaa anya nadiyon kii ghaatiyon main sona paaya jaataa he.
