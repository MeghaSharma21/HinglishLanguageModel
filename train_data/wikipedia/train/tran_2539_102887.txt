 maudood ahamed
 maudood ahamed, ek bangladeshi rajaneta the, evam 27 march 1988 se 12 agast 1989 ke bich ve, bangladesh ke pradhanmantri the. iske alaava bhi ve kai padon par rah chuke hai: uppradhanmantri, uparashtrapati vidhi, nyaay evam sansadeey kaary mantri . saath hi ve noakhali se chah baar saansad chune gaye the.
