 asmita marva ek bhartiya fishan diziner hai, jinka moolyankan fishan patrika vog dwaara sheersh 9 evam ubharate antarrashtriya dizinron ke liye kiya gaya tha. asmita marva paaramparik chizon ko vaishvik chizon ke saath milaane main vishwaas rakhti hai. unke kalamkaari ke jeevant avatar ne fishan kii duniya main toofaan laa diya tha. 
 marva ne 1990 ke dashak main hyderabad main kapade dizin karne shuru kar die the. vah hyderabad se telugu film udyog main jane vaali pehali diziner hai. kai abhinaytaon jaise nagarjun, baalkrishna, priti jinta, anjala jhaveri, shreya, mahesh babu, tabboo, amisha, charmi, or anoushka ne unke dwaara taiyaar paridhaan kai filmon main pahane hai. unamem se kuch filmein hai-manmadhudu, santhosham, premante idera, arjuna, attadu, pokiri or gharshana ityadi.
 2008 main unhone lackme india fishan wyk main bhaag liya tha.
