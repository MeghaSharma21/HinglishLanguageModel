 bushara ansaari karachi se sambandh rakhane vaali pakistani telivision kii jaani-pahachaani abhinetri, mezbaan or nirmaataa hai. 
 vah pakistan ke suprasiddh patrkaar ahamed bashir kii beti hai. bashari ne uski praarambhik shiksha lahore ke ladi griphin skool se or intermidiet lahore kollege faar viman univarsity se hasil kii or 1977 main vaqaar unnisa kollege rawalpindi se grajuet kii degree hasil kii. unhone 11 june 1978 ko telivision nirmaataa iqbaal ansaari se shaadi kii. bashari pehali baar unheen ke ek drama main namoodaar huin or uske baad unke shreshth draamon kaa silsilaa shuru ho gaya, or unhone hasya abhinay ke saath-saath gambhir abhinay main bhi apana loha manvaaya. abhinay ke ilaava pashari ne gulukaari or modelling bhi kii he. unhen unke shreshth kaary kii manyata main pakistan sarkaar kii ore se 1989 main 'pride aaf paraformans' se nawaaza gaya tha.
