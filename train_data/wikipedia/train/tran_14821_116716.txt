 saltoro parvatamala yaa saltoro muzataag jammu or kashmir main sthit karakoram parvatamala kii ek upashrenee he. yah karakoram ke hriday main siachin himani se dakshinpaschim main sthit he, jo prithvi ke dhruviya kshetron ke baahar sthit sabase badi do himanion main se ek he . saltoro parvatamala paak-adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-blatistan kshetra kii seemaa par sthit he, jahaan parvatamala kii pashchimi taraf saltoro ghati sthit he.
 yah saltoro parvatamala ke un parvaton kii suchi he, jo 7,200 meter se unche hai or 500 meter se adhik kii sthalakritik udagrata rakhte hai. is se kam udagrata rakhane vaale shikhar swatantra parvat nahin maane jate balki aasapaas ke anya parvaton ke hi shikhar maane jate hai .
