 kargil vijay diwas swatantra bharat ke liye ek mahatvapurn diwas he. ise har saal 26 july ko manaaya jaataa he. kargil yuddh lagbhag 60 dinon tak chalaa or 26 july ko uskaa ant hua. isamen bharat kii vijay hui. is din kargil yuddh main shahid hue javaanon ke sammaan hetu manaaya jaataa he.
 1971 ke bharat-paak yuddh ke baad bhi kai sainya sangharsh hota rahaa. donon deshon dwaara parmaanu pareekshan ke kaaran tanaav or bad gaya tha. sthiti ko shant karne ke liye donon deshon ne farvari 1999 main lahore main ghoshnaa patr par hastakshar kiye. jisamen kashmir mudde ko dwipaksheeya vaarta dwaara shantipurna dhang se hal karne kaa vaada kiya gaya tha. lekin pakistan ne apane sainikon or ardh-sainik balon ko chhipaakar niyantran rekha ke paar bhejne laga or is ghuspaith kaa naam "oparation badr" rakhaa tha. iskaa mukhya uddeshy kashmir or ladakh ke bich kii kadi ko todna or bhartiya sena ko siachin glacier se hataanaa tha. pakistan yah bhi maanata he ki is kshetra main kisi bhi prakaar ke tanaav se kashmir mudde ko antarrashtriya mudda banane main madad milegi. 
 prarambh main ise ghuspaith maan liya tha or daawa kiya gaya ki inhen kuch hi dinon main baahar kar diya jayega. lekin niyantran rekha main khoj ke baad or in ghuspaithiyon ke niojit ranneeti main antar kaa pataa chalane ke baad bhartiya sena ko ahsaas ho gaya ki hamale kii yojana bahut bade paimaane par kiya gaya he. iske baad bharat sarkaar ne oparation vijay naam se 2,00,000 sainikon ko bheja. yah yuddh aadhikarik rup se 26 july 1999 ko samaapt hua. is yuddh ke dauraan 527 sainikon ne apane jivan kaa balidaan diya.
