 bhartiya dhvaj samhita bhartiya dhvaj ko fahraane va prayog karne ke bare main diye gaye nirdesh hai. is samhita kaa aavirbhaav 2002 main kiya gaya tha. bharat kaa rashtriya jhanda, bharat ke logon kii aashaaon or aakankshaon kaa praturup he. yah rashtriya gaurav kaa pratik he. sabhi ke maargdarshan or hit ke liye bhartiya dhvaj samhita-2002 main sabhi niyamon, rivaajon, aupchaariktaaon or nirdeshon ko ek saath laane kaa prayaas kiya gaya he. dhvaj samhita-bharat ke sthaan par bhartiya dhvaj samhita-2002 ko 26 janvari 2002 se lagu kiya gaya he.
 bhartiya naagarik ab raat main bhi rashtriya dhvaj tirangaa fahraa sakate hai. iske liye shart hogi ki jhande kaa pole vastav main lanbaa ho or jhanda khud bhi chamake. grih mantralay ne udyogpati saansad naveen jindal dwaara is sambandh main rakhe gayi prastaav ke baad yah faisla kiya. isse pehle jindal ne har naagarik ke moolbhoot adhikaar ke taur par tirangaa fahraane ke lihaaj se adalati ladaai jiti thi. congress netaa jindal ko diye gayi sandesh main mantralay ne kaha ki prastaav kii padtaal kii gayi he or kai sthanon par din or raat main rashtriya dhvaj ko fahraane ke liye jhande ke bade pole lagane par koi aapatti nahin he. jindal ne june 2009 main mantralay ko diye gayi prastaav main bade aakaar ke rashtriya dhvaj ko smarakon ke polon par raat main bhi fahraaye jane kii anumati maangi thi. jindal ne kaha tha ki bharat kii jhanda samhita ke aadhaar par rashtriya dhvaj jahaan tak sambhav he suryoday se suryaast ke bich fahraaya janaa chahie, lekin duniyabar main yah saamaanya he ki bade rashtriya dhvaj 100 foot yaa isse unche pole par smarakon par din or raat fahraaye gayi hote hai.
