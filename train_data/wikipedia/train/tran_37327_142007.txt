 basal dav yaa basal kii phakhta ek ullekhneey dak tikat he jise swiss kenton basal dwaara 1 july 1845, ko jari kiya gaya tha or iskaa ankit mulya 2 1/2-rapan tha. ek ekmaatr dak tikat he jise basal ne jari kiya he. 1 janvari 1849 ko deshvyapee dak seva kii sthapana se pehle switzraland kaa prayek canton apni dak seva ke liye jimmedaar tha, saath hi is kaaran desh bhar ke vibhinn cantno main dak seva kii vibhinn daren prachlit thim. basal ke alaava keval jurikh or geneva hi dak tikat jari karne vaale anya canton the.
 tikat vaastukar, milshio beri dwaara dizin basal dav dak tikat par ek ubhari hui phakhta, bani hoti thi jo apni chonch me ek patr dabaaye hoti thi jis par "STADT POST BASEL" ankit rahata tha. tikat ko kaale, nile or gahare laal rang se chapa jaataa tha or yah duniya kii pehali teen rang kii dak tikat thi. 30 sitambar 1854 ke baad kaa iskaa upyog vaid nahin rahaa lekin us samay tak 41,480 tikaten chhaapi ja chuki thim..
 is dak tikat kii kai nakli pratiyaan banaai gayi hai.
