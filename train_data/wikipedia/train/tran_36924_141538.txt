 vidhyaarthee vah vyakti hota he jo koi cheez sikh rahaa hota he. vidhyaarthee do shabdon se bana hota he - "vidya" + "arthi" jiskaa arth hota he 'vidya chaahane vaala'. vidhyaarthee kisi bhi ayuvarg kaa ho saktaa he balak, kishor, yuva, yaa vayask. lekin mahatvapurn baat yah he ki vah kuch sikh rahaa hona chahie.
