 1993 yuiefee champions lig final munich, germany main olympia stadium par may 1993 26 par khelaa francisi club marsile or italvi club milan, ke bich ek footbal match tha.
 kot da'evoar janme marsile rakshak basil boli marsile unki pehali europea cap khitaab dene ke liye 43 wein minute main khel kaa ekmaatr gol. yah ek francisi team european cap jeeta tha ki pehali baar tha or koi francisi paksh ke baad se trophy jeet liya he.
 club or unke adhyaksh barnard tapy baad main french gharelu lig ke 1992-93 sizan ke dauraan match phixing cond main shaamil kiya gaya he ke liye paaya jayega, jisaki vajah se ve divijan 2 main chalaa gaya or agle mausam ke liye europea footbal main bhaag lene se pratibandhit kar diya. ghotaale keval french lig maichon main prabhavit ke rup main, 1993 ke yuiefee champion ke rup main marsile kii sthiti prabhavit nahin tha.
