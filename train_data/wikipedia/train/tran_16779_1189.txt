 kala, samskruti kii vahika he. bhartiya samskruti ke vividh ayaamon main vyaapt maanviya evam rasaatmak tatv uske kala-roopon main prakat hue hai. kala kaa praan he rasaatmakataa. ras athwa anand athwa aswaady hamen sthool se chetan satta tak ekarup kar detaa he. maanviya sambandhon or sthitiyon kii vividh bhavalilaon or uske madhyam se chetana ko kala ujaagaar karti he. astu chetana kaa mul ‘ras’ he. vahi aswaady evam anand he, jise kala udghaatit karti he. bhartiya kala jahaan ek ore vaigyanik or takneekee aadhaar rakhti he, vahiin duusari ore bhav evam ras ko sadaiv praanatatvan banaakar rakhti he. bhartiya kala ko jaanane ke liye upved, shaastra, puraana or puraatatva or pracheen sahitya kaa sahaara lenaa padataa he.
