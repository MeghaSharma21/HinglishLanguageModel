 praveen bhatta bhartiya film chhayakaar, nirdeshak or lekhak hai. yah kai baar mahesh bhatta ke filmon main kaary kar chuke hai. inhone yah kaary 1960 main kemramen ke rup main shuru kiya. 1962 main apane pita vijay bhatta kii film ko nirdeshit kiya. isi ke baad 1965 main unki agali film main chhayakaar bane. iske baad lahu ke do rang film 1979 main kiya or usee ke baad se yah mahesh bhatta or vikram bhatta ke kai filmon main kaary kar rahe hai.
