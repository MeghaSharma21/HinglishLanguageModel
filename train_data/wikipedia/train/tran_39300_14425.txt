 koshyakutauli tehsil bharat ke uttarakhand rajya main nainital janapad kii ek tehsil he. nainital janapad ke uttari bhaag main sthit is tehsil ke mukhyalay koshyakutauli kasbe main sthit hai. iske purv tathaa dakshin main nainital, pashchim main almoda janapad kii ranikhet tehsil, uttar main vetalghat, or almoda janapad kii almoda tehsil he. 
 tehsil ke adhikaar kshetra main kul 118 gaanv aate hai, or 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar isaki jansankhya 31,557 he.
