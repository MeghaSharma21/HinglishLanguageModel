 silicon ; pratik : Si) ek rasinic tatv he. yah prithvi par oxigen ke baad sabase adhik paaya jane vaala tatv he. silicon ke yogic electronic avyav, sabun, shishe evam computer chips main istemaal kiye jate hai. silicon kii khoj 1824 main sweden ke rasayanashastri jones jakab bajralius ne kii thi. aavart saarini main ise 14wein sthaan par rakhaa gaya he. 
 silicon yogicon, jaise silicon karbaide ko unki anokhi visheshtaon ke liye istemaal kiya jaataa he. kathorta main yah tatv heere kii barabari karata he. jab silicon ko anya tatwon ke saath milaayaa jaataa he to us yogic ko silicket kehete hai. silikets ko anek audyogic kaaryo ke liye istemaal main laayaa jaataa he. inaki anya rasinic yogicon ke saath kriya karai jaati he taaki yah apane silicon tatv alag karen yaa anya tatwon ke saath vibhinn kaaryon ke liye kriya kar saken. bekri ke non-stick upkaran, bijli utpaadon ke shield silicon se banate hai. 
 silicon kaa electronics kshetra main adhikadhik prayog hone ke kaaran america kii computer jagat ke centre ko silicon vaili kaa naam diya gaya he.
