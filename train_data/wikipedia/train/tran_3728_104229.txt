 nimkathana bhartiya rajya rajasthan ke sikar jile kii ek nagarpalika kshetra he. yah sikar kii aath tahsilon main se ek he. neem kaa thana sikar jile main vidhansabha kshetra he 2011 ke anusaar isaki kul jansankhya3,99,991 thi 4 oktober 1995 ko nimkathana main purna surya grahan dikhaai diya. iske adhyayan hetu vishwa ke vaigyaniko dwaara yahan shodh centre banaaya gaya. nagar ke najadik hi ganeshvar sabhyataa he. yah ek teji se viksit hota hua shahar he yahaan kii sakshartaa 80% he yah shahar shiksha ke kshetra main bhi bahut agraniy he audyogic kshetra adhik viksit hone ke kaaran yah kshetra rojgaar ke avasar bhi adhik pradaan karata he yahaan par rajasthan ke sabase zyada tirth sthaan maujuud hai tathaa yahaan kaa ganeshvar taamar kaaleen sabhyataa kaa ek hissaa rahaa he neem kaa thana kaa naam yahaan par nivas karne vaali nimbark sampradaay se padaa nimbark sampradaay ek naga saadhuon kaa sampradaay tha yah shahar chaaron ore se aravali kii pahadiyon se ghira hua he yahaan par bharat sarkaar dwaara uttar pashchim coridor maujuud he rajasav aay ke maamle main udaipur ke baad rajasthan kaa doosraa sabase bada shahar he yah shahar jila banane ke sabhi maapdandon par khara utarataa he haiveek
 nimkathana uttari akshansh 27.738 evam purvi deshaantar 75.783 par sthit he. isaki maasat se ausat oonchaai 480 meter he.
 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar nimkathana kii kul jansankhya 2,10,000 he. purush hissaa 53% evam mahilaayein 47% hai. nimkathana kii sakshartaa dar 67% he jo rashtriya ausat 59.5% se kuch adhik he jisamen purush sakshartaa 77% evam mahila sakshartaa 56% he. nimkathana main kul jansankhya kaa 16% log 6 varsh se kam aayu ke bachche hai. nimkathana nagar kii jansankhya 29,500 he.
 saati or sota naamak nadiyaan nimkathana se shuru hoti hai or kotputli kii taraf bahati he.kantali nadi nimkathana kshetra kii pramukh nadi he jo khandela kii pahadiyo se nikal kar jhunjhunu main pravesh kar samaapt ho jaati he.
