 yah aadhunik nevigation upkarnon se las he. iskaa istemaal shahari or gramin kshetron main aasaani se kiya ja saktaa he. iske pichley hisse kaa aakaar chota hone se ise chote hawaii addon par bhi land yaa tech of kiya ja saktaa he.isse bahut hi kam dhwani pradooshan hota he. saath hi yah naveenatam aadhunik paryaavaran aavashyaktaaon ko puraa karata he. is helicopter main riplencable transport modul laga hua he, jisase kam samay main yah apni kaaryakshmata badalane main sakasham he.
