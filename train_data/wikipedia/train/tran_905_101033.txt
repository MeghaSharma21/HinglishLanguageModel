 wembali stadium jise kai baar keval wembali bhi kaha jaataa he wembali, landan main sthit footbal stadium he. isaki kshamta 90,000 he. yah 2007 main kholaa gaya tha or pichley purana wembali stadium kii site par banaaya gaya tha. stadium main aam taur par pramukh footbal maichon jaise egge cap final, lig cap final or england rashtriya footbaal team ke gharelu maichon kii mejabani hoti he. yah europe kaa doosraa sabase bada stadium he, keval barsilona ke camp nou ke peeche. 
 2012 ke landan olympic khelon main, stadium ne footbal maichon kii mejabani kii. haalaanki, yah ek footbal stadium he lekin yahan bahut saare anya khel bhi yojit hote hai. yahan rugby lig maichon kaa aayojan bhi hua he or 2007 ke baad se prativarsh isane challene cap ke final maichon kii mejabani kii he. stadium ne yahan tak ki rugby union ke maichon kii mejabani kii he. 2007 ke baad se, prativarsh stadium enegel ke 1 niyamit satr maichon kii mejabani kii he.
