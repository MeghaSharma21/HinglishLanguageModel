 nelson mandela anterrashtriya diwas prati varsh 18 july ko sanyukt raashtra dwaara shaanti ke liye nobel puraskaar vijeta purv dakshin africa raashtrapati nelson mandela ke janm diwas kii yaadgaar ke rup main manaaya jaataa he. iskaa nirnay 18 july 2010 ko, jab mandela 92 varsh ke hue tab se prati varsh manaane ke liye liya gaya tha. sanyukt raashtra mahasabha ke adhyaksh ali treki ne bataaya ki yah nirnay ek aise mahaan vyakti ko sammanit karne ke liye liya gaya jisane aam logon kii bhalaai ke liye na sirf kaam kiya apitu uski keemat bhi chukaayi. mandela ne apane jivan kii sabase zyada umr qaid main bitaayi. qaid ke dauraan ve adhikansh samay kep toun ke kinare based kukhyat roben dweep bandigrih main rahe. unke 91wein janm diwas par sanyukt raashtra mahasachiv baan kii moon ne kaha tha - "mandela sanyukt raashtra main ucch aadarshon ke pratik hai. mandela ko yah sammaan shaanti sthapana, rangabhed unmoolan, maanavadhikaron kii raksha or langik samanta kii sthapana ke liye kiye gayi unke satat prayaason ke liye diya ja rahaa he."
