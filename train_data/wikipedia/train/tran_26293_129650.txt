 ramvilas sharma ke rachana sansar ko teen pramukh bhaagon main baanta ja saktaa he- saahitiyik, aitihaasik tathaa aalochanatmak.
 itihaas kii samasyaon se jujhna maano unki pehali pratigya ho. ve bhartiya itihaas kii har samasya kaa nidaan khojane main jute rahe. unhone jab yah kaha ki arya bharat ke mul nivasee hai, tab iskaa virodh hua tha. unhone kaha ki arya pashchim asia yaa kisi duusare sthaan se bharat main nahin aae hai, balki sach yah he ki ve bharat se pashchim asia kii ore gaye hai. ve likhte hai - duusari sahastrabdi isa purv bade-bade jan abhiyaanon kii sahastrabdi he. isi dauraan bhartiya aaryon ke dal iraq se lekar turki tak fail jate hai. ve apni bhashaa or samskruti kii chhap sarvatra chhodte jate hai. poonjeewaadi itihaaskaaron ne ulti ganga bahaai he. jo yug aaryon ke bahirgaman kaa he, use ve bharat main unke pravesh kaa yug kehete hai. iske saath hi ve yah prayaas karte hai ki pashchim asia ke vartmaan nivaasiyon kii aankhon se unki pracheen samskruti kaa vah paksh ojhal rahe, jiskaa sambandh bharat se he. sabase pehle swayan bharatvasiyon ko yah sambandh samajhnaa he, phir use apane padosiyon ko samajhaana he.
