 enrico fermi italian bhautik vigyaani evam nobel puraskaar vijeta the. 
 fermi kaa janm 29 sitambar 1901 ko rome shahar main hua. shiksha-diksha gatingen evam liden main hui tathaa taduparant rome main bhautikee ke praadhyaapak niyukt hue.
 inhone baari tatwon ke nabhikon ko todane ke sambandh main mahatvapurn shodh kaary kiya tathaa san 1934 main, neutron kii bambaari dwaara baari tatwon ke nabhikon ko todane main safalataa praapt kii. is prakaar fermi ne tatwantaran karne main mahatvapurn kaary kiya. kritrim radio active padaarthon kaa srijan karne ke uplakshya main, san 1938 main, inhen novel puraskaar praapt hua.
 ye san 1939 main colombia vishvavidhyalay main bhautikee ke praadhyaapak niyukt hue. san 1942 main inhen pratham parmaanu bhatti banane main safalataa mili. nabhikeey vigyaan main aapkaa yogadan chirsmaraneey rahegaa.
