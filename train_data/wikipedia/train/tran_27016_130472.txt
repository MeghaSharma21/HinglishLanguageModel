 mohankheda madhyapradesh ke dhar gilen main sthit ek kasbaa he. yahan shwetambar jain samaaj kaa ek aisa mahatirth viksit hua he, jo desh or duniya main khyath ho chukaa he. shrimad vijay rajendra surishwaraji maharaj sahab kii yah tirth nagari maanavseva kaa bhi tirth ban chuki he. guru saptami par har saal yahan shradghaluon kaa sailaab umadta he. lakhon shradghaalu poojya gurudev kaa jaighosh kar apane kalyaan kaa maarg praapt karte hai.
