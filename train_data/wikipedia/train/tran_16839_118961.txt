 chandrakanta hindi kii jaani maani lekhika he. unke lekhan kaa prarambh 1966 se hua jab unki pehali kahani 'khun ke reshe' kalpana main prakashit hui. aath saal baad unka pehla kahani sangrah 'salaakhon ke peeche' prakashit hua. iske che saal baad unka pehla upanyaas 'arthantar' prakashit hua. ve do sau se adhik kahaniyon kii rachana kar chuki hai. unki kahaniyon ke 13 sangrah, 7 upanyaas or 1 kavita sangrah prakashit ho chuke hai. inamen 'poshnul kii vaapasi', 'elaan gali zindaa he', 'yahan vitasta bahati he' tathaa 'apane apane konark' kaaphi charchit rahe. kashmir kii kesari aabhohawaa main pali badi chandrakanta kii kahaniyon main vahaan kii sabhyataa or samskruti kaa andaaj sahaj samaaya hua he. ve vahaan ke lok or lok samskruti kii gahari pahchaan rakhti hai or unki lekhnee ise marmbhedi safalataa ke saath vyakt karti he. 
 aaj ke daur kii suprasiddh katha lekhika chandrakanta kaa 2001 main prakashit upanyaas 'katha satisar', kashmir ke vrihattar itihaas ke bebaak chithran ke liye janaa jaataa he. iske liye chandrakanta ko 2005 main birla foundation ke vyas sammaan se vibhooshit kiya gaya he. unki rachanaaon ke vishwa kii anek bhashaaon main anuwaad ho chuke hai.
