 gaza pattee ek philistaani kshetra he. yah israyal ke dakshin-pashchim main ek 45 kilometre lambi tathaa 6-12 kilometre chaudee pattee ke rup main hai. iskaa dakshini chor misr se judaa hua he par baaki dishaao se yah israyal dwaara ghira he. ya haan tak ki isaki pashchimi seemaa jo bumadhyasaagar ko khulti he, par bhi israeli jalsena kaa niyantran he. yahan arab musalmaan rahete hai jinaki aabaadi koi 20 lakh ke aasapaas he.
 gaza shahar yahan kii mukhya aabaadi he.
