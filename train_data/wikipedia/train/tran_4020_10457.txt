 mahadaanav tara ek atyaadhik dravyaman or chamak vaala tara hota he. yarkis varnakram shreneekaran main isaki chamak kii shrenee "Ia" or "Ib" he. inase bade taare brahmand main mutthi-bhar hi hai or ve paramdanav taare kehalaate hai.
 "mahadaanav taare" ko angrezi main "superjaayent star" kaha jaataa he.
 mahadaanav taron kaa dravyaman 10-70 Mâ˜‰ or unka ardhavyas 30-500 Râ˜‰ hota he. kuch mahadanavon kaa ardhavyas suraj se hazaar guna zyaada bhi hota he. mahadaanvon kii chamak suraj kii chamak se 30,000 se lakhon guna zyaada hoti he. mahadaanav taare har varnakram shrenee main milte hai - isamen shikari taramandal main sthit 'O' shrenee kaa nila mahadaanav rajanya bhi shaamil he or 'M' shrenee kaa laal mahadaanav aadra bhi.
 mahadanavon kaa nirpeksh kantimaan -5 se -12 magnitude ke kshetra main hota he . bahut adhik dravyaman hone ke kaaran in taron main gurutvkarshan kaa dabaav bhayankar hota he or nabhikeey sanlayan bahut tezi se chalata he, jis se inkaa hydrogen indhan boune taron ke muqaabale main bahut jald khap jaataa he. jahaan hamaare suraj ko 10 arab saal kii aayu mili he vahaan mahadaanav taare kuch lakh saal hi jite hai.
