 corporal jyoti prakaash niraala maranoparant shanti kaal ke sarvocch sainya sammaan, ashok chakra ke praptakarta the. vah bhartiya vaayu sena ke garud commando force ke sadsy the. bhartiya vaayu sena kii ore se suhas biswas or rakesh sharma ke baad is sammaan ko praapt karne vaale vah tisare vyakti the.
 niraala kaa janm 15 november 1986 ko bihar ke rohtas jile ke badildih gaanv main tejanarayan singh tathaa malti devi ke ghar hua tha. unki chaar bahanen bhi thi. 2010 main unki shaadi aurangabad kii sushamaa nand se hui thi. vah apni patni or putree jigyaasa ke saath chandigarh main rahete the.
 2005 main jyoti prakaash vayusena main bhartee hue the. unki ikai ko 13 rashtriya rifles ke liye niyukt kiya gaya tha, or oparation rakshak ke tahat vah jammu or kashmir main tainaat the. 18 november 2017 ko search oparation ke dauraan chandragarh gaanv main javaanon ne ek ghar ko gher liya. tabhi usamen chhipe aatankiyon ne firing shuru kar di thi. niraala ke paas ek lite mashinagan thi. encounter ke dauraan goli lagane ke baavjood unhone do aatankwaadiyon ko maar giraaya tha. is encounter main aatanki sanghathan lashkar-e-taiyaba ke kul 6 aatankwaadi maare gaye the.
 26 janvari 2016 ko bharat ke raashtrapati ram nath kovind dwaara unhen maranoparant ashok chakra se sammanit kiya gaya. iske atirikt unke paitruk gaanv badiladih ke sameep ek smritidwar tathaa gaanv ke praathmik vidhyalay parisar main unki adamkad pratimaa bhi lagaai gayi he.
