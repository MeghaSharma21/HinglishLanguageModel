 santhal pargana bharat ke jharkhand rajya kii prashasnic ikaayiyon main se ek he. yah jharkhand kii ek kamishnari he jiskaa mukhyalay dumka main he. is ikai main jharkhand ke chah jile - godda, devgarh, dumka, jamtada, sahibganj or pakur shaamil hai. british rajmen pehle santhal pargana naam se hi sanyukt bihar main ek jila hua karata tha jise 1855 main britishon ne jila ghoshit kiya tha or yah bengal presidency kaa hissaa hua karata tha. santhal pargana do shabdon santhal - jo ek adivasee samudaay he or pargana - jiskaa arth pranta yaa rajya hota he - se bana he. santhal pargana ke sabhi chah jilon main santhal adivaasiyon kii bahutayat he jo austro aciatic bhashaa parivaar kii santhali] or bhartiya arya bhashaa parivaar kii angika bhashaa kaa prayog karte hai. british raaj ke dauraan adivaasiyon dwaara yahan kai vidroh hue the jisamen tilka maanjhi, birsa munda, or siddhu murmu ityadi jaise adivaasiyon ne kaaphi pramukh bhoomika nibhaai thi.
