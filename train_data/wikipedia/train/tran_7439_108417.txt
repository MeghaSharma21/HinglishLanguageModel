 shiyaan vaai-20 ek bada sainya parivahan vimaan he. is pariyojna ko shiyaan vimaan audyogic nigam dwaara viksit kiya ja rahaa he or ise aadhikarik taur par 2006 main launch kiya gaya tha. vimaan kaa aadhikarik kodenam kunpeng he. jo pracheen china kii puranic pakshi par rakhaa gaya hai yah pakshi hajaaron kilometre tak ud saktaa tha. haalanki, chini vimaanan udyog ke bheetar hi, vimaan ko aamtaur par apane upnaam chabbi girl naam dwaara janaa jaataa he kyonki isame china main bane anya chini vimaanon kii tulana main vyapak fuzlage he.
