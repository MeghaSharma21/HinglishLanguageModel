 pithauragarh bhartiya rajya uttrakhand kaa ek jila he. yah kshetra 2,750 varg meter main failaa hua he. 2011 ke jansankhya gananaa ke anusaar yahan kul 4,85,993 log rahete hai. jile kaa mukhyalay pithauragarh he. yahan aadhikarik rup se or shiksha ke liye hindi bhashaa kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. iske alaava yahan adhik sankhya main kumaunni bhashaa bolne vaale log rahete hai. 24 farvari 1960 ko pithauragarh kii 30 pattiyaan or almore kii do pattiyon ko milakar pithauragarh jile kaa gathan kiya gaya tha.
 pithauragarh main greeshm ritu ke samay garmi rahati he or sharad ritu ke samay thand rahati he. varsh main sabase adhik thand disambar or janvari main maah ke dauraan hoti he. iske upar ke sthanon main barfbaari bhi hote rahati he. taapmaana march ke madhya se badhanaa shuru ho jaataa he or june ke madhya tak rahata he. yahan vaarshik varsha 36.7 semi hoti he.
 jile main 12 tahseelein hai: dharchula, bangapani, munasyari, thal, bedinag, ganaigangoli, gangolihot, devlathal, kanalichhina, didihat, tejam or pithauragarh.
