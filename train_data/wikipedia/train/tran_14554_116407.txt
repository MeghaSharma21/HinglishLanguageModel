 
 rajasthan ke jhunjhunuun jile ke luna gaanv main 18 july 1927 ko janmen mehadi hasan kaa parivaar sangeetkaaron kaa parivaar rahaa he. mehadi hasan ke anusaar kalavant gharaane main ve unase pehle kii 15 peediyaan bhi sangeet se hi judi hui thim. sangeet kii aarambhik shiksha unhone apane pita ustad azim khan or chachaa ustad ismil khan se lee. donon hi dhrupad ke achche jaankaar the. bharat-paak bantwaare ke baad unka parivaar pakistan chalaa gaya. vahaan unhone kuch dinon tak ek cycle dukaan main kaam kii or baad main motor mechainic kaa bhi kaam unhone kiya. lekin sangeet ko lekar jo junun unke man main tha, vah kam nahin hua.
 1950 kaa daur ustad barkat ali, begum akhtar, mukhtaar begum jaison kaa tha, jisamen mehadi hasan ke liye apni jagah bana paanaa saral nahin tha. ek gayak ke taur par unhen pehali baar 1957 main radio pakistan main bataur thumari gayak pahchaan mili. uske baad mehadi hasan ne mud kar nahin dekha. phir to filmi geeton or gajalon kii duniya main wo chaa gayi.
 1957 se 1999 tak sakriy rahe mehadi hasan ne gale ke cancer ke baad pichley 12 saalon se gaanaa lagbhag chhod diya tha. unki antim ricarding 2010 main sarahadein naam se aayi, jisamen farahat shahzad kii likhi "tera milanaa bahut acha lage he" kii ricarding unhone 2009 main pakistan main kii or us treks ko sunakar 2010 main lata mangeshkar ne apni ricarding mumbai main kii. is tarah yah yugal album taiyaar hua.
 mehadi hasan ko gayaki ke liye duniya bhar main kai sammaan mili. hajaaron gazalen unhone gaain, jinke hajaaron album duniya ke alag-alag deshon main jari huye. pichley 40 saal se bhi adhik samay se goonjati shahenshaah-e-ghazal kii aawaaj kii viraasat ab bachi hui he.
