 yatharthwaad beesvin shatee ke tisare dashak ke aasapaas se hindi sahitya main pai jane vaali ek vishesh vichaardhaara thi. iske mul main kuch saamaajik parivartanon kaa haath tha. jo parivartan hua uske mulkaaran the rashtriya swadhinata sangram, communist aandolan, vaigyanik kshetra main vaishvik str par hue visphot, vishwa sahitya main hue parivartanon kaa parichay or arya samaaj aadi saamaajik aandolan. yathartwaadee lekhakon ne samaaj ke nimnavarg ke logon ke dukhad jivan kaa chithran kiya. unke upanyaason ke nayak the gariv kisaan, bhikhmange, bhangi, rickshaw chaalak mazdoor, bhaarvahee shramik or dalit. iske pehle kahani sahitya main aise upekshit varg ko koi sthaan nahin tha.
