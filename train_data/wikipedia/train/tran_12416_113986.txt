 madhya pradesh ke chhindwara jile ke patalkot main sthit he, adivasee sangrahalay. yah sangrahalay 20 april 1954 ko kholaa gaya tha or 1975 main ise "rajya sangrahalay" kaa darja mila. 8 sitambar 1997 ko adivasee sangrahalay kaa naam badalakar "shri badal bhoi shaskeey adivasee sangrahalay" kar diya gaya. yah saare janajaatiy sangrahalayon main sabase purana he. isamen 14 kaksh, 3 galiyare or 2 khule galiyare hai. 
 isamen avibhajit madhyapradesh kii lagbhag 46 janajatiyon kii jivan style, saanskrithik dharohar, pratik chihnon or kala shilp ko pradarshit kiya gaya he jinamein mukhute, agni prajvalan ke saadhan, devi devataaon kii murtiyon, mritak stambh, krishi upkaran, paintings, astr-shastr, poshaaken, kanghiyaan, juute, khadau, ghaas ke sunahare aabhushan, vibhinn janajatiyon ke nrityon ke model, mitti ke baratan, vastra nirmaan, fossil, topi, dholak, khudaai se praapt prastar murtiyon, pashaan yug ke bhitti chitra, nrity prashaadhan, vaivaahik mukut aadi shaamil hai. inaki kul sankhya 2,200 he. sangrahalay main vishesh janajatiyon ko pruthak keson main model, chart, paintings va maanchitron ke madhyam se pradarshit kiya gaya he.
