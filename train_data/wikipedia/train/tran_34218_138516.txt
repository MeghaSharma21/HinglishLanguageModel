 aaj ham jise mumbai kehete hai, mul rup se saat alag alag samudri dwipon se mil kar bani he. paanch shatabdiyon ke lambey antaraal ke dauraan in saat dwipon ko vibhinn bhoomi-sudhaar pariyojnaon ke dwaara ek duusare se jod kar aaj ke mumbai shahar or aitihaasik puraani mumbai kii rachana kii gayi. mul dweepasmooh main nimnlikhit dweep shaamil the: 
 aasapaas ke trombe or sailset dwipon kaa vilay kar greater mumbai kii rachana kii gayi. 
 shesh dweep hai: 
 1970 main sarvocch nyaayalay dwaara tatirekha kii suraksha or machuaaron dwaara iske upyog ke liye nishedhagnaon kii ek shrinkhalaa sthapit kii. in nishedhon ke saath saath 1990 ke dashak gathit tateey niamak kshetra or badhati paryaavaran chintaaon ke kaaran mumbai main bhoomi-sudhaar pariyojnaon kii sankhya or paimaane main ullekhneey kami ai he.
