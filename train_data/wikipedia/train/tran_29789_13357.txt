 ibrahim lodhi dilli sultanate kaa antim sultan tha. vah afgani tha. usne bharat par 1517-1526 tak rajya kiya or phir mughalon dwaara parajeet hua, jinhone ek nayaa vansh sthapit kiya, jis vansh ne yahan teen shatabdiyon tak rajya kiya.
 ibrahim ko apane pita sikandar lodhi ke maranoparant gaddi mili. parantu uski shaskeey yogyataayen apane pita samaan nahin thim. use anek vidrohon kaa saamana karnaa padaa.
 rana sanga ne apana samrajya pashchim uttar pradesh tak prasar kiya or agra par hamale kii dhamkee di. purv main bhi vidroh shuru ho gaya. ibrahim ne puraane evam varisht sena commandaron ko apane vafaadaar naye waalon se badal kar darbar ke navabon ko bhi naakhush kar diya tha. tab use apane log hi daraane dhamkaane lage the. or antat afgani darbariyon ne babar ko kabul se bharat par akraman karne ke liye aamantrit kiya.
 ibrahim kii mrityu panipat ke pratham yuddh main ho gayi. babar ke ucch koti ke sainik evam apane lodhi sainikon kaa alag hona uske patan kaa kaaran bana, haalanki uski sena kaaphi bady thi.
 ibrahim lodi ne rajya kaa vibhaajan karke apane bhai 'jalaal khaan' ko jonpur kaa shaasha niyukt kiya, parantu baad main jonpur ko apane rajya main mila liya. singhaasana par baithne ke uparant ibrahim ne ajam humayun sherwani ko gwalior par akraman karne hetu bheja. vahaan ke tatkaaleen shaasha vikramjit singh ne ibrahim kii adheenta sweekaar kar lee. mewar ke shaasha rana saangaa ke viruddh ibrahim lodi kaa abhiyaan asafal rahaa. khatauli ke yuddh main ibrahim lodi rana saangaa se haar gaya. is yuddh main rana saangaa ne apana baanya haath kho diya. rana saangaa ne chanderi par adhikaar kar liya.
 mewar evam ibrahim lodi ke bich jhagade kaa mukhya kaaran malwa par adhikaar ko lekar tha. ibrahim ke bhai jalaal khaan ne jonpur ko apane adhikaar main kar liya tha. usne kalapi main ‘jilaluddin’ kii oopaadhi ke saath apana rajbhishek karavaaya tha. ibrahim lodi ne lohani, formuli evam lodi jatiyon ke daman kaa purna prayaas kiya, jisase shaktishaalee sardaar asantusht ho gayi.
 ibrahim ke asantusht saradaaron main punjab kaa shaasha ‘daulat khaan lodi’ evam ibrahim lodi ke chachaa ‘alam khaan’ ne kabul ke taimur vanshi shaasha babar ko bharat par akraman ke liye nimantran diya. babar ne yah nimantran sweekaar kar liya or vah bharat aayaa. 21 april 1526 ko panipat ke maidan main ibrahim lodi or babar ke madhya hue bhayanak sangharsh main lodi kii buree tarah haar hui or uski hatya kar di gai. ibrahim lodi kii sabase badi durbalata uskaa hathi swabhaav tha. uske samay kii pramukh visheshta uskaa apane saradaaron se sangharsh tha. ibrahim kii mrityu ke saath hi dilli sultanate samaapt ho gai or babar ne bharat main ek naveen vansh ‘mogul vansh’ kii sthapana kii.
 farishta ke anusaar - “vah mrityuparyant ladaa or ek sainik bhanti maaraa gaya.”niamtullah kaa vichaar he ki - “sultan ibrahim lodi ke atirikt bharat kaa koi anya sultan yuddh sthal main nahin maaraa gaya.”
