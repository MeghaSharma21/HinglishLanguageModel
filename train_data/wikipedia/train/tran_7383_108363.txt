 interstailor varsh 2014 kii apik vigyaan-fantasy aadhaarit film he jisaki sahalekhan, sah-nirmaan, or nirdesan christopher nolan ne kiya he. film main mathew mike'kanf, ana haithve, jesica chestin, bill ervin, ellen berstin tathaa maaikel ken ne mukhya adaayagi kii he. film kii kahani anusaar, antariksh yatriyon kaa ek dal ek varmahal ke jarie sabhi maanvon ke nae ghar kii khoj hetu yatra par ravaanaa hote hai. 
 christopher evam jonathan nolan ne saath milakar patakatha par kaary kiya he, joki mul rup se jonathan ko yah vichaar 2007 main aayaa tha. nolan ne yah film aapni patni yema thomas ke saath aapni prodkashan company sinakapi or linda obs ke linda obs prodkashan saath sanyukt rup se nirmaan kiya he. caltech ke bhautik siddhant kip tharn, se prerit hokar jinpar yah film bani he, ve iske akjecutive nirmaataa or iske vaigyanik salaahkaar kii bhi bhoomika main hai. iske baad, unhone is par kitaab bhi likh bhi daali thi, da science of interestailor . film ke pramukh nirmaan main paramount pickchars warner brass, evam ligendry pickchars jaise sah-nirmaataa bhi jude hai. 
 dharti ke manushyon ke laayak na rahane or maanav jati ke astitv ko bachaae rakhane kii khatir nae grah kii talash interstailor he. kharaab mausam kii vajah se dharti par ann sankat paidaa ho gaya he. maanavjaati ko bachaane kaa puraa jimmaa kuch antarikshayatriyon par he jo varmahole se guzarate hue maanav ke liye nae ghar ko khojenge. is abhiyaan kaa hissaa he nasa kaa purv payalat kupar, or uske saath eni haithve bhi is abhiyaan par hai. yah film ek lanbaa safar he, jisamen rishton or halaat kii jang saamne najar aati he. ek hatakar or anjaan duniya jisase hamaara abhi tak vaasta nahin padaa he.
