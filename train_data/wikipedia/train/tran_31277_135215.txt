 pragatisheel janatantrik morcha bharat ke uttrakhand rajya main ek raajnneetik morcha he. isaki sthapana varsh 2014 main hui thi jisamen rajya kii do pramukh kshetreey shaktiyaan, bahujan samaaj party, uttrakhand kranti dal evam nirdaleey vidhayak sammilit hai. vartmaan main prajmo bhartiya rashtriya congress ke netritv vaale sanyukt pragatisheel gathbandhan kaa ang he.
 prajmo kii sthapana 2014 main uttrakhand main congress ke netritv vaali sarkaar main parivartan se thik pehle hui thi, jis samay vijay bahuguna ke sthaan par harish ravat ko rajya kaa nayaa mukhyamantri banaaya gaya tha. us samay uttrakhand vidhansabha main teen sadasyon vaali bahujan samaaj party, ek sadsy vaale uttrakhand kranti dal evam teen nirdaleey vidhayakon dwaara vidhansabha main, jahaan kisi bhi raajnneetik dal ko bahumat praapt na hone ke chalate utpann huyi apni atyant mahatvapurn sthiti ko samuhik rup majboot banane ke uddeshy se ek morcha banaaya gaya. aisi sthiti main congress tathaa bhartiya janata party donon kii hi sarkaar gathan ke liye prajmo par nirbharta bani rahi. purv main morche ke gathan se pehle iske sadasyon kaa samarthan swatantra rup se vijay bahuguna ke netritv vaali congress sarkaar ko praapt tha. morche ke gathan ke pashchaat bhi iske sadasyon kaa samarthan aapasi samjhaute ke baad harish ravat ke netritv vaali congress ko jari rahaa jiske falswaroop prajmo ke sadasyon ko rajya sarkaar main 5 cabinet mantripad haath lage.
 vartmaan uttrakhand vidhaan sabha main prajmo ke ekmaatr sadsy dhanolti se nirdaleey vidhayak preetam singh panvaar hai.
