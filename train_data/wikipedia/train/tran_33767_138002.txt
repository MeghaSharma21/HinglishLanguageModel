 duwari subbarao kaa janm 11 agast 1949 ko hua. ve ek bhartiya arthshaastri, kendriya banker hai. ve 5 sitambar 2008 se 4 sitambar 2013 bhartiya reserve bank ke 22wein governor rahe.
 shri subbarao 1972 batch ke bhartiya prashasnic seva ke andhra pradesh cader ke adhikari hai.
 shri subbarao andhra pradesh ke pashchim godavari jile ke eluru sthaan se hai. unki shiksha sainik vidhyalay se hui. 1969 main unhone bhartiya prodyogiki santhaan, khadgpur se bhautikee main B. Sc. Hons puraa kiya. baad main unhone bhartiya prodyogiki santhaan, kanpur se bhautikee main M.Sc. kii upadhi praapt kii. 1972 main unhone bhartiya prashasnic seva main safalataa arjeet kii.
 1978 main, unhone ohayo state univarsity se arthshaastra main master kii degree hasil kii. baad main unhone andhra vishvavidhyalay se arthshaastra main PhD kii upadhi lee.
 shri subbarao ne jab bhartiya reserve bank ke governor kaa padbhaar sambhala, vah samay 1930 kii mandi ke baad, vaishvik arthvyavastha kaa sabase bhayaavah samay tha.
