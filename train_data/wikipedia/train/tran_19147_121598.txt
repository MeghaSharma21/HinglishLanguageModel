 crystalata kisi thos padaarth main dhaanche kii suvyavastha ke maap ko kehete hai. crystalon main parmaanu yaa anu ek niyat va aavarti kram main sajjit hote hai. crystalata kaashtha kaa padaarth kii kathorta, ghanatv, paardarshita or visaran ke gunon par baari prabhaav padataa he.
 gas main anuon va parmaanuon ke sthaan bilkul aniyamit or betaratib hote hai isliye uski crystalata shunya bataai jaati he. drav or kaanch jaise aniyat thos ek madhyamik avasthaa samajhi jaati he, jinamein kuch anuon va parmaanuon ke paimaane par to suvyavastha dikhti he lekin us se adhik par avyavstha. do yaa us se adhik bane mishraatuon main ucch crystalata to hoti he lekin unamem kai swatantra adhik crystalata vaale alag-alag kshetron ke kan ek duusare se jude hote hai. sabase adhik doshahin crystal ardhchaalak electronic main banaae jane vaale silicon bul hote hai jisamen puraa-kaa-puraa bul bina kisi kan-seemaa rakhane vaala ek hi crystal hota he.
 kaanch-mritikashilp or kuch polimar main crystal or aniyat bhaag mili hue hote hai or unamem crystalata maap unke aaytan yaa bhaar kaa vah pratishat hota he ko crystalia ho. kisi padaarth kii crystalata ko eks-kiran crystaliki or raman varnakrameeyata dwaara mapa ja saktaa he, lekin iske liye calorimiti takneekein bhi aksar prayog hoti hai.
