 kik ek ection feechar film he is film ke nirdeshak tathaa nirmaataa sajid nadiadwala hai. is film ke mukhya abhinayta salmaan khan, jaclyn furnandis, navazuddin siddiqui tathaa randeep hooda hai. yah 2009 main ai telugu film 'kik' kaa punarnirmaan he. film 25 july 2014 ko release hui. kik ne pehle din main 28 karod rupe kama liye.
 film bharat main har samay kii duusari sabase oonchi kamai karne vaali bollywood film ban gaye saatven sarvaadhik kamai karne vaali videshi bazaaron or duniya bhar main teesraa sarvaadhik kamai karne vaali bollywood film main shaamil ho chuki he. 
 salmaan khan kii film kik sau karod ke club main shaamil ho gai he. film ne pehle saptaah main 164.09 karod rupe kaa collection kiya tha. 350 karod se zyaada kamai karne ke baad kik sarvaadhik kamai karne vaali bhartiya filmon kii suchi main shaamil ho gai he.
 is movie ke andar salmaan khan bacchon ko ilaaj kar aata he
 film kaa sangeet himesh reshmia or meet brose anjaan dwaara rachaa gaya he.
