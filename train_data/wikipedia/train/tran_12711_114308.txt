 rameswaram hinduon kaa ek pavitra tirth he. yah tamil nadu ke ramnathpuram jile main sthit he. yah tirth hinduon ke chaar dhaamon main se ek he. iske alaava yahaan sthapit shivlinga baarah dwadash jyotirlingon main se ek mana jaataa he. bharat ke uttar me kaashi kii jo manyata he, vahi dakshin main rameshwaram kii he. rameswaram chennai se lagbhag sava chaar sau mile dakshin-purv main he. yah hind mahaasaagar or bengal kii khadi se chaaron ore se ghira hua ek sundar shankh aakaar dweep he. bahut pehle yah dweep bharat kii mukhya bhoomi ke saath judaa hua tha, parantu baad main sagar kii laharon ne is milaane vaali kadi ko kaat daalaa, jisase vah chaaron ore pani se ghirakar tapu ban gaya. yahaan bhagwaan ram ne lanka par chadhaai karne se purv ek pattharon ke sethu kaa nirmaan karavaaya tha, jisapar chadhakar vanar sena lanka pahunchi va vahaan vijay pai. baad main ram ne vibhishana ke anurodh par dhanushkoti naamak sthaan par yah sethu tod diya tha. aaj bhi is 30 mile lambey aadi-sethu ke avshesh sagar main dikhaai dete hai. yahaan ke mandir ke tisare praakaar kaa galiyara vishwa kaa sabase lanbaa galiyara he.

 rameshwaram se dakshin main kanyakumari naamak prasiddh tirth he. ratnakar kahalaanevaali bengal kii khadi yaheen par hind mahaasaagar se milti he. rameshwaram or sethu bahut pracheen he. parantu ramnath kaa mandir utana purana nahin he. dakshin ke kuch or mandir dedh-do hajaar saal pehle ke bane he, jabaki ramnath ke mandir ko bane abhi kul aath sau varsh se bhi kam hue he. is mandir ke bahut se bhaag pachaas-saath saal pehle ke he.
 rameswaram kaa galiyara vishwa kaa sabase lanbaa galiyara he. yah uttar-dakshinmein 197 me. evam purv-pashchim 133 me. he. iske parcote kii chaudaai 6 me. tath oonchai 9 me. he. mandir ke praveshdwar kaa gopuram 38.4 me. ooncha he. yah mandir lagbhag 6 hectare main bana hua he.
 mandir main vishalakshi ji ke garbh-grih ke nikat hi nau jyotirling hai, jo lankapati vibhishana dwaara sthapit bataae jate hai. ramnath ke mandir main jo tamrapat he, unase pataa chalata he ki 1173 isvi main srilanka ke raja parakram bahu ne mul ling vaale garbhagrih kaa nirmaan karavaaya tha. us mandir main akele shivlinga kii sthapana kii gai thi. devi kii murti nahin rakhi gai thi, is kaaran vah ni:sangeshwar kaa mandir kahalaaya. yahi mul mandir aage chalakar vartmaan dasha ko pahunchaa he.

 satrahavin shatabdi main dalvaay setupati ne purvi gopuram aarambh kiya. 18veen shatabdi main ravivijay setupati ne devi-devataaon ke shayan-grih va ek mandap banwaaya. baad main muttu ramling setupati ne bahari parcote kaa nirmaan karavaaya. 1897 â€“ 1904 ke bich madhya devakottai se ek parivaar ne 126 feet ooncha nau dwaar sahit poorvigopuram nirmaan karavaaya. isi parivaar ne 1907-1925 main garbh-grih kii marammat karwaai. baad main inhone 1947 main mahakumbhabhishek bhi karavaaya.
 rameshwaram kaa mandir bhartiya nirmaan-kala or shilpakala kaa ek sundar namoona he. iske pravesh-dwaar chalis feet ooncha he. praakaar main or mandir ke andar saikadau vishaal khambhein he, jo dekhne main ek-jaise lagate he ; parantu paas jaakar jaraa baariki se dekha jaay to maaloom hoga ki har khambhe par bel-boote kii alag-alag kaarigari he.
 ramnath kii murti ke chaaron or parikrama karne ke liye teen praakaar bane hue he. inamen teesraa praakaar sau saal pehle puraa hua. is praakaar kii lambaai chaar sau foot se adhik he. donon or paanch foot ooncha or kareeb aath foot chaudaa chabutara bana hua he. chabutaron ke ek ore pathar ke bade-bade khambho kii lambi katare khadi he. praakaar ke ek sire par khadey hokar dekhne par aisa lagata he maaro saikadon torana-dwaar kaa swaagat karne ke liye banaae gayi he. in khambhon kii adbhut kaarigari dekhakar videshi bhi dang rah jate he. yahaan kaa galiyara vishwa kaa sabase lanbaa galiyara he.
 ramnath ke mandir ke chaaron or dur tak koi pahaad nahin he, jahaan se pathar aasaani se laaye ja saken. gandhamadan parvat to nammaatr kaa he. yah vastav main ek tila he or usamen se ek vishaal mandir ke liye jaruri pathar nahin nikal sakate. rameshwaram ke mandir main jo kai lakh ton ke pathar lage he, ve sab bahut dur-dur se naavon main ladakar laaye gayi he. ramnath ji ke mandir ke bhitari bhaag main ek tarah kaa chikna kaala pathar laga he. kehete he, ye sab pathar lanka se laaye gayi the.
 rameshwaram ke vishaal mandir ko banavaane or uski raksha karne main ramnathpuram naamak choti riyasat ke raajaaon kaa bada haath rahaa. ab to yah riyasat tamil nadu rajya main mil gai hai. ramnathpuram ke rajbhavan main ek purana kaala pathar rakhaa hua he. kaha jaataa he, yah pathar ram ne kevatraj ko rajtilak ke samay uske chihn ke rup main diya tha. rameshwaram kii yatra karne vaale log is kaale pathar ko dekhne ke liye ramnathpuram jate he. ramnathpuram rameshwaram se lagbhag taintis mile dur he.

 rameshwaram ke vikhyath mandir kii sthapana ke baaren main yah rochak kahani kahi jaati he. sitaaji ko chhudaane ke liye ram ne lanka par chadhaai kii thi. unhone ladaai ke bina sitaaji ko chhudwane kaa bahut praytn kiya, par jab ram safalataa na mili to vivash hokar unhone yuddh kiya. is yuddh main ravan or uske sab saathi rakshas maare gayi. ravan bhi maaraa gaya; or antatah sitaaji ko mukt karakar shriram waapas laute. is yuddh hetu ram ko vanar sena sahit sagar paar karnaa tha, jo atyadhik kathin kaary tha. 
 ravan bhi saadhaaran rakshas nahin tha. vah pulasthya maharishi kaa naati tha. chaaron vedon kaa jaananevaala tha or tha shivaji kaa bada bhakt. is kaaran ram ko use maarane ke baad bada khed hua. brahma-hatya kaa paap unhen lag gaya. is paap ko dhone ke liye unhone rameshwaram main shivlinga kii sthapana karne kaa nishchay kiya. yah nishchay karne ke baad shriram ne hanuman ko ajna di ki kaashi jaakar vahaan se ek shivlinga le aao. hanuman pawan-sut the. bade veg se akaash maarg se chal pade. lekin shivlinga kii sthapana kii niyat ghadi paas aa gai. hanuman kaa kahin pataa na tha. jab sitaaji ne dekha ki hanuman ke lautne me der ho rahi he, to unhone samudr ke kinare ke ret ko mutthi main baandhakar ek shivlinga bana diya. yah dekhakar ram bahut prasann hue or niyam samay par isi shivlinga kii sthapana kar di. chote aakaar kaa sahi shivlinga ramnath kehalaata he.
