 antarrashtriya sambandhon main kshetreey shakti us rajya ko mana jaataa he jo duniya ke kisi bhugolic kshetra vishesh ke bheetar adwitiya shakti or prabhaav rakhata he. jaise bharat dakshin asia main ek kshetreey shakti hai.
 kshetreey shaktiyaan ek kshetreey bhubhag main dhruviyata kaa nirmaan karti hai. aamtaur par, kshetreey shaktiyaan kshetra vishesh main mahatvapurn kshamtaayen rakhti hai, lekin vaishvik str par ye kshamtaayen utana mahatv nahin rakhti he.raajnneetik anusandhan ke liye europea sahaayta sangh ne kshetreey shakti ko is prakaar paribhaashit kiya he: "ek rajya jo ek bhugolic rup se paribhaashit kshetra se sambandhit ho, aarthik or sainya drushti se is kshetra main dabadabaa rakhata ho, jo is kshetra main varchasv rupi prabhaav or vishwa str par mahatvapurn prabhaav rakhata ho, jo shakti ke sansadhano kaa upyog karne ke liye taiyaar ho or uske padosi deshon dwaara use kshetreey netaa ke rup main manyata praapt ho yaa phir kshetreey netaa sweekaar kiya gaya ho.
