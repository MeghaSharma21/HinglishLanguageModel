 jis rocket kaa motor propelent eendhanon ko do alag-alag avasthaaon main upyog karata he use sankar rocket yaa 'hybrid rocket' kehete hai. eendhan kii ye do avasthaaon main pehali thos avasthaa hoti he or duusari yaa to gas avasthaa yaa drav avasthaa. sankar rocket kaa concept kam se kam 75 varsh purana he.
