 bharat main dhaatukarm kaa itihaas pragaitihaasik kaal se armbh hokar aadhunik kaal tak jari he.
 pracheen bharat main loha ispaat kaa puraa ullekh he. kuch pracheen smarak jaise nai dilli kaa prasiddh louh stambh yaa konark main surya mandir main prayog kiya gaya thos beam main paryaapt sakshya miltaa he jo pracheen bhartiya dhaatu vigyaan kaa praudyogikeey utkarsh dikhaata he.
 bharat main lohe kaa prayog pracheen yug kii ore le jaataa he. vaidic saahitiyik strot jaise rugaved, atharvaveda, puraana, mahakavy main shaanti or yuddh main lohe ke gaare main ullekh kiya gaya he. ek adhyayan ke anusaar loha bharat main adikalin laghu suvidhaon main 3000 varshon se adhik samay se nirmit hota rahaa he.
