 nirmala jain hindi sahitya kii adhyaaapak, aalochak or charchit aatmakatha jamaane main ham kii lekhika he.
 nirmala jain kaa janm 28 oktober 1932 main dilli ke ek vyapaaree parivaar main hua. bachapan main hi uske pita kii mrityu ho gai thi. us ne dilli main hi shiksha puri kii or varshon katthak guru achchhan maharaj se nrity kii shiksha praapt kii. usne dilli vishvavidhyalay se em.e., piech.di. or di. lit. kii oopaadhiyaan praapt kin. adhyaapan ke kshetra main nirmala kii pehali niyukti ladi shriram college main hui. 14 saal tak us college main rahane ke baad vah dilli uniwarsity main rahi. sevanivrutt hone ke baad bhi, ve vishesh aamantrit rup se das varsh tak adhyaapan karti raheen.
