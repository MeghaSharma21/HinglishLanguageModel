 bhopal, madhya pradesh
  divakar verma hindi sahityakar tathaa navageetkaar the.
 25 disambar 1941 ko sukarakshetra soron, uttar pradesh kii paavan dharaa par janme divakar verma apatkaal main misaabandi rahe. san 1974 main bhartiya majdoor sangh se jude. 1979 main bhartiya majdoor sangh ke pradesh mantri tathaa baad main pradesh upaadhyaksh rahe. 
 1998 se unhone sahitya srijan shuru kiya. 2002 main ve sahitya parishad se judkar rashtriya mantri bane. 01 may 2014 ko bhopal, madhya pradesh main cancer ke kaaran unka nidhan ho gaya. verma ji apane nidhan ke samay tak niraala srijan peeth, bhopal ke nideshak the. 
 divakar verma kii ab tak kul 8 pustaken prakashit ho chuki hai. unke 4 geet sangrah, 1 ghazal sangrah, 1 naatak, 1 doha sangrah tathaa 1 mukt chand kavita sangrah.
