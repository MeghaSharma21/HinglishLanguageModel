 kulcha ek uttar bhartiya roti kii kism he. bharat ke saath-saath ye pakistan main bhi lokapriy hai. praayah ise chhole ke sang khaaya jaataa he. yah maida ko khamir uthaa kar aave main banaaya jaataa he. 
 kulcha mukhyatah ek punjabi vyanjan he, jo punjab se udgam hua he. amritsar kaa khaas kulcha amritsari kulcha kehalaata hai. maida ko dahi ke saath mel kar khamir uthaaya jaataa he. uske baad usamen uble masaaledaar aalu or kati pyaaj aadi bhar kar bharwaan kulche banaaye jate hai. inhen sunahare rang kaa hone tak aave yaa tandoor main pakaaya jaataa he. uske baad iske upar makkhan laga kar chhole ke saath paroste hai. ye amritsari kulche hote hai. bina bhare hue sade kulche banate hai. 
 kulche kaa udgam iran main mana jaataa he. iske lakhanu ke vyanjan visheshgyon ne prayog kar antaran banaaye hai. inamen kulcha nahari bhi ek he. iske visheshgy karigar haji jubair ahamed ke anusaar kulcha avadhi vyanjanon main shaamil khaas roti he, jiskaa saath nahari bina adhuraa he. lakhanu ke gilaami kulche yani do bhaag vaale kulche unke pardada ne taiyaar kiye. kulche rich diet main aate hai or aisa mana jaataa he, ki ki acchhee khuraaq vaala insaan bhi teen se adhik nahin kha saktaa he. kulche garm khaane main hi majaa he yani tandoor se nikle or parose jaayen.
