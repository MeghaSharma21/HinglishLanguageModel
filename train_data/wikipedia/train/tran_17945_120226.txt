 pratapsingh chhatrapati shahu dwitiya ke putr the.
 pratapsingh pita ke mrityu ke baad satara ke singhaasana par baitha . vah sachharitra, udaar, budhimhan, vidyapremi tathaa dharmik vritti kaa vyakti tha. satara kii satta to pahile hi shoonyapraay ho chuki thi. peshwa bajirao dwitiya ne pratapsingh ko basauta ke ekaaki kile main rakh, use or bhi aapng bana diya tha. angl maratha yuddh main ashta main bajirao kii paraajay ke baad vah angarejon ke adhikaar main aa gaya. angarejon ne use chota sa rajya de pun: satara main singhasanacin kiya . praaia: teen saal vah grant ke abhibhaavkatv main rahaa. 1822 main use purna shasnadhikaar saunpe gaye. 1839 main, rajyadroh ke abhiyog par padchyut kar, use banaras nishkasit kar diya gaya, jahaan 1847 main dayaneey paristhiti main uski mrityu ho gai.
