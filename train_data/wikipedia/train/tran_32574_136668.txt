 doob yaa durva ek ghaas he jo jameen par pasarti he. hindu sanskaron evam karmkandon main iskaa upyog kiya jaataa he. marbari bhashaa main ise dhro kaha jaataa hain.
 shaayad hi koi aisa insaan hoga jo doob ko nahin jaanata hoga. haan yah alag baat he ki har kshetron main tathaa bhashaaon main yah alag alag naamon se janaa jaataa he. hindi main ise doob, dubada, sanskrit main durva, sahastraveery, anant, bhargavi, shataparva, shatavalli, marathi main pageri durva, kaali durva, gujarati main dholadhro, niladhro, angreji main cochagrass, kriping sinoden, bengali main nil durva, sada durva aadi naamon se janaa jaataa he. iske aadhyatmik mahatvanusaar prayek puja main doob ko anivaary rup se prayog main laayaa jaataa he.
 iske aushadhiy gunon ke anusaar doob tridosh ko harne vaali ek aisi aushadhi he jo vaat kaph pitt ke samast vikaron ko nasht karte hue vaat-kaph or pitt ko sam karti he. doob seven ke saath yadi kapaal bhaati kii kriya kaa niyamit yogic abhyaas kiya jaye to sharir ke bheetar ke tridosh ko niyantrit kar detaa he, yah dah shaamak, raktadosh, murchha, atisar, arsh, rakt pitt, pradar, garbhasrav, garbhpaat, yon rogon, mutrakruchh ityadi main vishesh laabhakaari he. yah kaantivardhak, rakt stanbhak, udar rog, pilia ityadi main apana chamatkaari prabhaav dikhaata he. shwet durva visheshtah vaman, kaph, pitt, dah, amatisar, rakt pitt, evam kaas aadi vikaron main vishesh rup se prayojaneey he. seven kii drushti se doob kii jad kaa 2 chammach paste ek cap pani main milakar pinaa chahie.laan ke rup main bhi doob ghaas kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. ghar ke aage khaali jagah main ise laga kar sundarataa dekhi ja sakati he.
