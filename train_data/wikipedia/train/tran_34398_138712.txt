 ismail samaani tajikistan kaa sabase unchaa parvat he. jab tajikistan roosi samrajya or phir soviet sangh kaa hissaa tha, jab yah parvat un deshon kaa bhi sabase unchaa pahaad hua karata tha. yah puure madhya asia or puri pamir parvatamala kaa bhi sabase buland parvat he. 
 iskaa naam samaani rajvansh ke purvaj ismail samaani par rakhaa gaya he. soviet sangh ke jamaane main 1933 main iskaa naam pehle soviet taanashaah joseph stallin ke sammaan main stallin parvat or phir, 1962 main, samyavaadee vichaardhaara ke sammaan main komunism parvat rakh diya gaya. soviet sangh ke tuutane or tajikistan ke azaad hone ke baad 1998 main iskaa naam parivartit karke 'ismail samaani parvat' kar diya gaya.
