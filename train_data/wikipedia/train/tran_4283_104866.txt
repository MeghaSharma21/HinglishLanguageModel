 vanaspatik janan ek prakaar kaa alangik janan he jo vanaspatiyon main hota he. is janan prakriya main bina beej yaa bijaanu ke hi nayi vanaspati paidaa hoti he. vanaspatik janan prakritik rup se bhi hota he or udyanvaigyanikon dwaara prerit bhi ho saktaa he.
 vanaspatik janan main koi vanaspatik bhaag, nae ped kii utpatti karata he or janak paudhe sa alag hokar nayaa jivan prarambh karata he. iske do prakaar, ek prakritik or doosraa kritrim, hai. 
 prakritik vanaspatik janan nimnlikhit prakaar kaa hota he:
 samudbhavan  koshika main ek taraf yaa chaaron taraf anek pravaradh nikalakar matra koshika se alag hokar swatantra rup se pravardhan kar koshikaon kii shrunkhlaa banaate hai. iskaa udhaaharan yeast he. ek duusare prakaar ke samudbhavan ko jima samudbhavan kehete hai, jisamen paitruk pind ke kisi nikle bhaag se kaliyaan nikalakar usee ke saath lipati rahati hai, yaa alag ho jaati hai. aisa janan kaai, liverwart or pravaal dendrophilia main dekha jaataa he.
 bhustari yaa ranor  main jo paudhe sidhe khadey nahin ho sakate ve jameen par rengte hue badhate hai, unke upar ke bhaag par valkal patr rahete hai, jinke konon main kaliyaan rahati hai. kaliyon ke bich sa patali jhakadaa jaden nikalakar jameen ke andar chali jaati hai or is prakaar nae paudhe taiyaar hote hai. doob ghaas iskaa udhaaharan he.
 sakar bhustari se miltaa julata he. antar yah he ki sakar main jameen ke andar tanon par valkal patr hote hai or unke konon kii kaliyon se shakhaayen nikalakar hawa main chali jaati hai. prayek shakha ke tal se jhakadaa jaden nikalakar jameen ke andar ghus jaati hai. pudinaa iskaa udhaaharan he.
  bhustarika yaa afaset bhi bhustari kii tarah failati he, par yah bhustari se choti or moti hoti he tathaa thodi dur hi rengkar tane ke ant main ek nayaa paudha utpann karti he.
  patrakand yaa balbil main akshakoniya kaliyaan hoti hai, jo adhik maatra main khaadya padaarth ekatrit ho jane se moti ho jaati hai or jameen par girane par nae paudhe ko janm detii hai. lahasun, pushpakram, banaalu yaa jameenkand, ananas ityadi iske udhaaharan hai.
  prakand yaa risom ke upar valkal patr or neeche jhakadaa jaden hoti hai. patr ke konon kii kaliyon se ankur nikalakar hawa main chale jate hai. jaden pramukh risom se alag hokar vanshavistaar karti hai. iske udhaaharan adrakh, haldi or fern hai.
  ghanakand yaa karm ke udhaaharan ghuiyaan or banda hai. inamen neeche ek phula hua tana rahata he jise mandal kehete hai. iske upar valkalapatr kaa aavaran hota he. inako kone main kaliyaan rahati hai, jinase anukool mausam par ankur nikalakar upar chalaa jaataa he or neeche se jaden nikalakar prithvi ke andar chali jaati hai. is prakar nae paudhe utpann hote rahete hai.
  balb ghanakand sa hi hota he, par iskaa mandal apekshaya chota hota he or upar raseeli moti phaankiyaan hoti hai. andar kii pattee ke kone main kali rahati he, jo anukool mausam par nae tane ko janm detii he. pyaaj iskaa udhaaharan hai.
  kand yaa tuber valkalapatron ke konon main kand lagata he. kand kaa tana phula hua rahata he. isamen khaadya sanchit rahata he. aalu iskaa acha udhaaharan he. aalu par kaliyaan yaa aankhe hoti hai. prayek aankh ek paudha utpann karti he.
 jadon dwaara vanaspatik utpaadan main satawar, dalia or shakarkand kii jaden kand utpann karti hai, in kandon se phir nae paudhe utpann hote hai.
  pattiyon dwaara utpaadan main kuch paudhon ke pattey nae paudhe utpann karte hai. inhen patrakalika kehete hai. pathar kuchi, begonia, parnavrunt tathaa callankoi iske udhaaharan hai. kuch fern main bhi isi reeti se janan hota he.
 kuch paudhon kaa janan kritrim reeti se bhi hota he. kuch paudhe tanon kii kataran se, kuch paudhe kalam baandhane se or kuch daab kalam se nae paudhon ko utpann karte hai.
 kritrim vanaspatik janan se paudhe kii jatigat shuddhta banaai rakhi ja sakati he, jo beej dwaara utpann paudhe se nishchit nahin hoti or janan praaia: nishchit hota he. aise janan ke liye khaadya padaarth paryaapt rahanaa chahie. iske abhaav main janan langik yaa alangik ho saktaa he.
