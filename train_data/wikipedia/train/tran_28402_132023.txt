 sammishra vishleshan jise saamanytah sammishra charon ke falnon kaa siddhanth bhi kaha jaataa he ganitiya vishleshan kii ek shakha he jisamen sammishra sankhyaon ke falnon kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. yah bijiy jyamiti, sankhya siddhanth, vyaavhaarik ganit sahit ganit kii vibhinn shakhaon main upyogi he tathaa isi prakaar taral gatiki, ushmagatiki, yantric abhiyantriki or vidyut abhiyantriki sahit bhautik vigyaan main bhi upyogi he.
 ek sammishra falan vah kehalaata he jisamen swatantra char or ashrith char donon hi sammishra sankhyaayein ho. adhik sankshep main sammishra falan vah falan he jisamen praant or parisar donon sammishra tal ke upasamucchy hon.
 ek sammishra falan ke liye, swatantra char or ashrith char donon ko vaastavik va kaalpanik bhaagon main vibhakt kiya ja sakat he:
 anya shabdon main, falan f ke ghatak
 ko vaastavik charon x or y.ke vaastavik-maan falnon ke rup main likhaa ja saktaa he.
