 purapashan kaal praugaetihaasik yug kaa vah samay he jab maanav ne pathar ke aujaar bananaa sabase pehle armbh kiya. yah kaal aadhunik kaal se 25-20 lakh saal purv se lekar 12,000 saal purv tak mana jaataa he. is dauraan maanav itihaas kaa 99% vikaas hua. is kaal ke baad madhyapashan yug kaa prarambh hua jab maanav ne kheti karnaa shuru kiya tha. 
 bharat main purapashan kaal ke avshesh tamil nadu ke kuranul, karnataka ke hunsngi, odisha ke kuliana, rajasthan ke deedvanake shringi talab ke nikat or madhya pradesh ke bheemabetkar main milte hai. in avashesho kii sankhya madhyapashan kaal ke praapt avashesho se bahut kam he.
