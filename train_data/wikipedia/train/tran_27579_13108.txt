 baijnath mandir baijnath main sthit nagar style main bana hindu mandir he. yah 1204 isvi main ahuka or manyuka naamak do sthaneey vyapaariyon ne banwaaya tha. yah vaidyanath ke rup main bhagwaan shiv ko samarpit he. shilalekhon ke anusaar vartmaan baijnath mandir ke nirmaan se purv bhagwaan shiv ke mandir kaa astitv tha. mandir ke garbhagrih main shivlinga he. baharali deevaaron par anekon chitron kii nakkashi hui he. 
 mandir ke mukhya kaksh main shila-falak chattan par nakkashit do lambey shilaalekh hai. ye shilaalekh sharada lipi main sanskrit or takari lipi main sthaneey boli pahari kaa upyog karke likhe gaye hai. ye shilaalekh bhartiya rashtriya panchang varsh 1126 main ahuka or manyuka naamak do mandir nirmaataa vyapaariyon kaa vivaran dete hai, iske alaava isamen bhagwaan shiv kii prashansa, mandir nirmaan ke samay ke shaasha raja jai chandra kaa naam, vaastukaron ke naamon kii suchi or daata vyapaariyon ke naam bhi shaamil hai. anya shilaalekh main kangra jile ke purana naam - nagarkot kaa ullekh he.
 mandir kii deevaaron par kai pratimaaen banaai gai hai. inamen se kuch murtiyan evam pratimaayein vartmaan mandir se pehle bani hui hai. mandir main yah murtiyan evam pratimaaen hai: bhagwaan ganesh, bhagwaan harihar, kalyansundar or bhagwaan shiv dwaara asur andhak kii haar.
