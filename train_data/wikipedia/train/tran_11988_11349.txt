 iova sanyukt rajya america ke madhya-pashchim main sthit ek rajya he jise kabhi-kabhi america kaa dil bhi kaha jaataa he. yah purv main mississippi nadi or pashchim main mizori nadi or big siokes nadi ke kinare sthit he. iova kaa naam american indian janajati ive, jo europea logon ke aagaman ke samay is kshetra ke nivasee the, ke naam par aadhaarit he. yah rajya french shashan ke samay uskaa hissaa bhi rahaa. baad main jab ise loiciana ne kharid liya tab yahan ke rahane waalon ne kheti-baadi vaali arthvyavastha ke saath isaki neenv daali. iova aksar duniya main khaadya saamagree kii rajdhani bhi kahi jaati he, par yah yaad rakhana chahie kii isaki arthvyavastha, samskruti or paridrishy kaaphi bhinn bhinn he.
 madhya 20vi sadi main iova kii krishi aadhaarit arthvyavastha main badlaav aaye or yah utpaada nirmaan, prasanskaran, vittiya sevaaon, jaev praudyogiki, hari urja aadhaarit arthvyavastha main tabadil ho gayi. iova rahane ke hisaab se puure desh main sabase surakshit rajya mana jaataa he. di mayin rajya kaa sabase bada shahar bhi he or isaki rajdhani bhi. 2016 main isaki aabaadi 31,34,693 anumaanit kii gai he. yah ise sanyukt rajya main 30waan sabase adhik aabaadi vaala rajya banaata he.
