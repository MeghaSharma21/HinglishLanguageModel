 
 koparnisium, jiskaa rasinic pratik Cn he, ek rasinic tatv he. iskaa parmaanu kramank 112 he. koparnisium prakruti main nahin paaya jaataa or yah keval prayogshaala main kritrim rup se nirmit kiya gaya he. iskaa nabhik bahut asthai he jiske karanvash yah atyant radiodharmee he or isaki ardhayu kaal keval 29 sekkind he. yah sabase pehle 1996 main banaaya gaya tha or kul milakar san 2015 tak koparnisium ke keval 75 parmaanu dekhe gayi the. yah yah aavart sarini ke di khand main aata he or ek paractenide tatv he.
