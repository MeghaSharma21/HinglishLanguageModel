 bashar al assasaad syria kaa netaa he 1 9 wein or vartmaan raashtrapati, syria, 17 july, 2000 se karyaalay rakhti he. vah syria ke sashastr balon ke commander-in-chif hai, sattarudh arab socialist party party ke mahasachiv or syria main party kii shakha ke kshetreey sachiv hai. vah hefez al asad kaa betaa he, jo 1971 se 2000 tak syria ke raashtrapati the.
