 angrej sarkaar dwaara bharat main san 1878 main deshi press adhinium paarit kiya gaya taaki bhartiya bhashaaon ke patr-patrikaon par or kadaa niyantran rakhaa ja sake. us samay lord littan bharat kaa governor general or visarai tha. is adhinium main patr-patrikaon main aisi saamagree chhapane par kadi karvai kaa praavdhaan tha jisase janata main british shashan ke viruddh asantosh panpane kii sambhaavna ho. vastutah yah kanoon bhashaai samachar-patron ko dabaane ke liye laayaa gaya tha. deshi press adhinium paarit hone ke agle din hi kolkata se bangala main prakashit amrit bazaar patrika ne apane ko 'angreji dainik' patr bana diya. iske sampadak shishir kumaar ghosh the.
 is adhinium ke antargat saikadon deshi patr-patrikas zapt kar lee gayin. preson main taale daal die gaye. swadeshvaasiyon main rashtriya chetana jaagrit karne ke liye sahityakar bankimachandra chattopadhyaay ‘anand math’ kaa vandemaataram lekar aae jo british shaskaon ke coup kaa kaaran bana. musalamaanon ko uksaakar ‘aanandamath’ kii dheron pratiyaan jala di gayi .
 varnakular press act bahut badnaam hua tathaa england main satta parivartan ke baad 1881 main radd kar diya gaya or 1867 vaale puraane kanoon ko hi jari rakhaa gaya.
