 da lucky van america lekhak nicholas sparks dwaara likhit upanyaas he jise 30 sitambar 2008 main release kiya gaya tha. isaki kahani ek america marine logan thibolt par kendrit he jise iraq main ek muskuraati hui yuva mahila kaa chitra miltaa he. apane iraq ke tin dauron ke dauraan vah us chitra ko apane achche naseeb kii kunjee ke taur par le jaataa he. apane dauron main vah kai naseeb-purna ghatnaon se guzarataa he or antat waapas lautne par us mahila ko dhanyavad karne ke liye khojataa he. is upanyaas ko 2012 main scot hiks dwaara isi naam kii film main parivartit kiya gaya he.
