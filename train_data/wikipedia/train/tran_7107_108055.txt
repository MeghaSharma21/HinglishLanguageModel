 aayudh bhandar, jise aayudh depo, aayudh aapurti bindu, gola barood niptaan kshetra, yaa aayudh damp aadi kai prakaar se namit kiya jaataa he, ek sainya bhandaran suvidha he jisamen gola barood or visphotakon kaa bhandaran kiya jaataa he.
 jinda gola barood or visphotakon kaa bhandaaran swaabhaavik rup se khatarnaak he. yahan utraai, packing, or gola barood ke hastantaran main durghatnaon kii sambhaavna rahati he. karmiyon yaa paas ke gola barood ko nuksaan se bachane ke liye khaas dhyaan rakhaa jaataa he.
 gahan nivarak upayon ke baavjood, duniya bhar ke gola barood depo, gaiyr-ladaaku aag or visphot se peedit hai. haalanki yah ek durlabh ghatana he, par jab yah hota he tab iskaa parinaam vinaashkari hi hota he. gola barood depo main apni citon main se ek main bhi maamuli visphot hone par, ek saath aasapaas ke naagarik ilaakon or imaaraton ko turant khaali kara apatkaaleen nikasi kii jaati he. is prakaar, sangrahit gola barood ko apane aap main ek surakshit duuri se agnishman ke bahut hi simit prayaas ke saath, kai dinon yaa hafton ke liye puri swayan visphot hone liye chhod diya jaataa he.
