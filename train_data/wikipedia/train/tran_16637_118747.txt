 swatantrata ke baad bharat ne aarthik vikaas ke liye samaajawaadee aarthik nitiyon kaa anusaran kiya. kai kshetron main to bharat sarkaar kaa ekmaatr adhikaar tha. swatantrata ke uparant teen dashak tak bharat kii prati vyakti aay keval 1% prati varsh kii dar se badi. 80 ke dashak ke madhya se bharat ne apane bazaar ko dhire-dhire kholana armbh kiya or aarthik udaarvaad ke maarg par chal niklaa. 1991 ke pashchaat or bhi adhik moolbhoot aarthik sudhaar hue. 2000 ke baad aarthik sudhaaron ko or gati di gayi or ab bharat mukt bazaar arthvyavastha kii disha main bahut aage nikal gaya he.
 1991 ke aarthik udaarikaran ke baad bharat kii jidipi kii vruddhi dar main teji aayi.
 bharat ke vibhinn bhaagon kii vruddhi dar main bahut asamanta he. varsh 2015-16 main, andhra pradesh kii vruddhi dar, bihar kii or madhya pradesh kii rahi jabaki maharashtra kii, urisa or punjab thi.
