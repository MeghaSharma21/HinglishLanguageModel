 deepa malik, shotput evam jevalin thro ke saath-saath tairaaki evam motor raceling se judi ek viklang bhartiya khilaadi hai jinhone 2016 paralampic main shotput main rajat padak jitkar itihaas rachaa. 30 kii umr main teen tumor surgeries or sharir kaa nichalaa hissaa sunn ho jane ke baavjood unhone na keval shotput evam jwaleen thro main rashtriya antarrashtriya pratiyogita main padak jite hai, balki tairaaki evam motor raceling main bhi kai spardhaon main hissaa liya he. unhone bharat kii rashtriya pratiyogitaaon main 33 swarna tathaa 4 rajat padak praapt kiye hai. ve bharat kii ek aisi pehali mahila he jise himalay car raily main aamantrit kiya gaya. varsh 2008 tathaa 2009 main unhone yamuna nadi main tairaaki tathaa special bike sawari main bhaag lekar do baar limca book of world ricard main apana naam darj karaayaa. yahi nahin, san 2007 main unhone taiwan tathaa 2008 main berlin main javelin thro tathaa tairaaki main bhaag lekar rajat evam kaansya padak praapt kiya. komanwealth games kii team main bhi ve chaynit kii gai. paralampic khelon main unki ullekhneey uplabdhiyon ke kaaran unhe bharat sarkaar ne arjuna puraskaar pradaan kiya. 
 rio paralimpic khel- 2016 main deepa malik ne shot-put main rajat padak jeeta, deepa ne 4.61 meter tak gola fenka or duusare sthaan par raheen. paralimpic khelon main medal jeetne vaali deepa pehali bhartiya mahila ban gai hai.
