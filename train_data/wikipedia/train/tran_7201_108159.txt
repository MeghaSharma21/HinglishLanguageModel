 tandi united footbal club tandi shahar main sthit he, jo scotland ke ek peshewar footbal club he. 1909 main gathan, mul rup se tandi hibernian ke rup main, club 1923 main vartmaan naam main badal.. united ko bhay yaa tangarin ke upnaam se bulaay jaataa he.
 gharelu str par, club ke ek avasar par scottish premier divijan jeeta he or scottish cap main do baar . unka ghar stadium tannadis park he, jahaan ve 1909 ke baad se nirantar khelaa rahe hai.
