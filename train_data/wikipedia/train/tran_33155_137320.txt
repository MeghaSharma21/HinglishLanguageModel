 marach gomke jaipal singh munda ashvini kumaar pankaj dwaara likhit bharat main adivasee raajnneeti ke sansthaapak pairokaar, jharkhand andolan ke sabase bade netaa or bhartiya hockey ke pehle captain jaipal singh munda kii hindi main prakashit pehali jeevani he. 2015 main prakashit yah jeevani jaipal singh munda ke raajnneetik yogadan or unke jivan ke vividh pahaluon ko tathyagat rup se saamne rakhti he.
 koun the jaipal singh munda? inkaa bhartiya swatantraaa or naye bharat ke nirmaan main raajnneetik-bauddhik yogadan kya tha? ve jis adivasee samaaj kaa pratinidhitiv kar rahe the, uski aakankshaayen kya thim? is bare main aazaadee ke sattar saal baad bhi itihaas chup he. ek taraf gandhi-nehru, jinna, ambedkar sahit anke swatantraaa senaniyon par sainkadon kitaaben hai, naatak or filmein hai or abhi bhi in sab par har din ek nayi pustak likhi ja rahi he, jaipal singh munda par ek bhi kitaab nahin he. ve koun-se kaaran hai jinaki vajah se 1928 kii olympic hockey main captaini karte hue pehla swarna padak dilaane vaale, desh ke liye isies kaa tyaag karne vaale or adivaasiyon ke is sabase bade netaa kii is kadar upeksha kii gayi? kyon jharkhand rajya kii sarkaron dwaara bhi alag jharkhand rajya ke is praneta ko yaad nahin kiya ja rahaa he? yah pustak inhen sab sawaalon kaa vistaar se or pramanik dhang se jawaab detii he.
 jaipal singh munda kaa vaastavik naam ishvardaas jaipal singh he. jinhen aadar se jharkhand ke adivasee unhen ‘marach gomke’ kehete hai. yah pustak bataati he ki jharkhand alag rajya kaa sapana bhale aanshik rup se unki mrityu ke 30 saal baad puraa hua lekin jaipal singh munda ne jis adivasee darshan or raajnneeti ko, jharkhand andolan ko apane vaktavyon, santhnik kaushal or ranneetiyon se bhartiya raajnneeti or samaaj main sthapit kiya, vah bhartiya itihaas or raajnneeti main apratim he. ek hockey khilaadi ke rup main rashtriya or antarrashtriya hockey main unke abhootpurv yogadan par bhi yah pustak bakhubi prakaash daalati he.
