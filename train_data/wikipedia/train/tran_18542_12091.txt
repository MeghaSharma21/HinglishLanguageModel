 nivkh yaa nivkhi yaa gilyak ek maanav samudaay he jo rus ke sudur-purv main sakhalin dweep or khaabarowsk kroy main amur nadi ke sagar se mil jane vaale kshetra main rahata he. yah is kshetra ke pracheenatam nivasee maane jate hai. prachinkaal main yah garmiyon main samudr-taton ke paar or sardiyon main taton se hatakar nadi-jharanon ke paas machuaaron or shikariyon kaa jivan vyateet karte the. san 2002 kii roosi janaganana main inaki anumaanit aabaadi keval 5,287 gini gai thi. inaki bhashaa bilkul pruthak he or iskaa kisi bhi anya gyaat bhashaa se sambandh nahin, haalanki is bhashaa ke andar chaar gyaat upbhashaayen hai.
 'nivkh' main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
 mana jaataa he ki nivkhon ke purvaj siberia main bykele jhil se purv main aaj se 10,000 saal se bhi pehle pahunche. nivkhon ke baad is kshetra main bahut si anya jatiyaan ai jinase nivkhon ko bahut haani hui. pehle yahan tungusi log aae, jinke ek manchhu-naamak samudaay ne nivkhon se lagaan lenaa shuru kar diya. 19veen sadi tak is ilaake main roosi samrajya kaa qabza ho gaya or roosi kozaakon ne nivkh zameenon ko apana banaakar nivkhon ko ek chota-sa nirdhan alpsankhyak samudaay bana choda. aadhunik yug main bahut se nivkhon ne roosi rahan-sahan or khan-paan apana liya he. dharmik drushti se adhiktar nivkh ojha pratha main vishvas rakhte hai haalanki kuch ne roosi isai dharm apana liya he.
