 amrit kaa shabdic arth 'amarta' he. bhartiya granthon main yah amartv pradaan karne vaale rasayan ke arth main prayukt hota he. yah shabd sabase pehle rugveda main aayaa he jahaan yah som ke vibhinn paryaayon main se ek he. vyutpatti kii drushti se yah unani bhashaa ke 'ambrosia' se sambandhit he tathaa samaan arth vaala he.
