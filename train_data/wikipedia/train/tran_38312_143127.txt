 isha foundation tamilnadu ek aadhyatmik sanghathan he jise aadhyatmik guru sadguru jaggi vasudev ne 1992 main netritv kiya. yah sanghathan ke kareeb 20 lakh swayamsevakon, yoga, paryaavaran or saamaajik kaary ke kshetra main sakriya he. inke mukhya sthaan coimbatore ke paas isha yoga centre main or sanyukt rajya america ke isha institute of inner sciences he. isha foundation aarthik or sanyukt raashtra ke saamaajik parishad main salaahkaar sthiti paa chuki he.
 isha foundation isha yoga naam se vibhinn yoga kaarykram pradaan karata he. isha kaa arth niraakaar paramatma he. isha yoga kaa praarambhik kaarykram he, innar engeering . isme dhyaan, pranayam or shambhavi mahamudra sikhaaya jaataa he. yah kaarykram companiyon main or 1998 main gel main aparaadhiyon ke liye bhi sanchalit kii jaati he. bhartiya hockey team ke liye ek yoga course bhi 1996 main yojit kiya gaya. 1997 main sanyukt rajya america main yoga ke kaaryakramon kaa aayojan shuru kiya. 
 green hands pariyojna isha foundation kii paryaavaran sambandhi prastaav he. puure tamilnadu main lagabag 11 karod ped ropit karnaa, pariyojna kaa ghoshit lakshya he. ab tak green hands pariyojna ke antargat tamilnadu or puduccheri main 1800 se adhik samudaayon main, 20 lakh se adhik logon dwaara 82 lakh paudhe ke ropan kaa aayojan kiya he. is sanghathan ne 17 oktober 2006 ko tamilnadu ke 27 jilon main ek saath 8.52 lakh paudhe ropakar ginis vishwa record banaaya tha. paryaavaran suraksha ke liye kiye gaye iske mahatvapurn kaaryon ke liye ise varsh 2008 kaa indira gandhi paryaavaran puraskaar diya gaya.
 gramin kayakalp kaary or seva ek abhiyaan he jiskaa mukhya uddeshy he gramin janata ke swasthya or jindagi main sudhaar laana.
