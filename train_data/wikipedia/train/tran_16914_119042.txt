 chen liyangue  china kii samaajawaadee dal ke netaa hai. unka janm ningabo, jejiyang pradesh main hua tha. ve china ke purv raashtrapati jiyang zemin ke qareebi the or kaha jaataa he ki unhone shanghai kii garmati arthvyavastha par qaabu paane ke liye kiye gaye vartmaan raashtrapati hu jintao ke nirdeshon ko andekha kar diya. chen liyangue ko satta ke durupyog or ghusakori ke liye doshi paaya gaya he. pichley ek dashak main bhrashtachar ke aarope main sazaa paane vaale ve sabase varisht chini adhikari hai.
