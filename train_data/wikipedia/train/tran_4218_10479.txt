 bharti bhawan ek shaikshanik prakaashan santhaan he jisaki shuruyaat patna main san 1943 main hui thi. is samooh ne vidyalayon or pratiyogitaaon ke liye kai pustaken chhaapi hai. aaj iske karyaalay patna ke alaave dilli, ranchi, bengalooru jaisi jagahon par hai. prasiddh profesar ech si verma kii 'concepts of physics' is prakaashan kii sabase prasiddh pustakon main se ek he. inaki pusken angrezi or hindi main hoti hai. pandit totaaraam sanadhy kii pustak fiji dweep main mere ikkish varsh bharti bhawan dwaara prakashit karakar bhawan kii khyaati ko badhaaya. jinka janm 1876 main hirangaanv firozabad main hua tha.
