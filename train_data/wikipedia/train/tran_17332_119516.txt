 2003 2003 africa-asian khel, jinhen aadhikarik rup se pratham africa-asian khelon ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he or anaupchaarik rup se adhishthapan africa-asian khelon ke naam se, ek anterrashtriya bahu-kreedaa khel pratiyogita he jisaki mezbaani 24 oktober se 1 navambar 2003 ke bich hyderabad, bharat main kii gai thi. hyderabad main yojit yah ab tak kii sabase bady khel pratiyogita he or bharat main yojit bhi, jo 2010 rashtramandal khelon ke baad duusare sthaan par he. kul 96 deshon se 2,000 se bhi adhik khiladiyon ne in khelon main bhaag liya tha. aath kridaaon main kul 129 khel pratiyogitaayen thim.
 96 deshon dwaara bhaag liye jane ke pashchaat bhi in khelon kii gunjaaish kam thi jisamen kul milakar keval aath khel the. in khelon main maatr ek vishwa kirtiman tutaa or 37 deshon ne kam se kam ek padak jeeta tha.
 pratham africa-asian khelon main asia se 43 or africa se 53 desh in khelon main bhaag lene aae the. ye desh nimnlikhit hai:
