 acharya sudharmaswami bhagwaan mahavir ke paanchave ganadhar the vartmaan main sabhi jain acharya va sadhu unke niyamon kaa samaan rup se paalan karte hai. inkaa janm 607 isa purv hua tha tathaa inhen 515 isa purv main kevalgyaan praapt hua evam 507 isa purv main 100 varsh kii aayu main inkaa nirvana hua.jinhone gautam ganadhar ke nirvana ke pashchaat baarah barsho tak jain dharm kii acharya parampara kaa nirvaah kiya.
