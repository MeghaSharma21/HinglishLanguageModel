 saas bina sasuraal ek soni par prasaarit hone vaala dharawahik hai. yah 18 oktober 2010 se 6 sitambar 2012 tak chalaa. yah arun ranjankar dwaara nirdeshit or vipul shah va sanjeev sharma dwaara nirmit dharawahik he. 
 yah kahani ek vidhyalay kii shikshika tanya or chaturvedi parivaar ke tisare putr tej kii he. jo ek duusare se pyaar karte rahete he. lekin chaturvedi parivaar main saaton log purush rahete he, jo kisi bhi mahila ke ghar main na rahane ke paksh main rahete he. tanya or tej ke vivaah hetu kai samay baad jaakar sabhi sahamat ho jate hai.
