 jantar mantar, "yantra mantra" kaa apbhransh rup he. savai jaisingh ne aisi vedhashalaon kaa nirmaan jaipur, ujjain, mathura, dilli or varanasi main bhi kiya tha. pehali vedhashala 1724 main dilli main bani. iske 10 varsh baad jaipur main jantar mantar kaa nirmaan hua. iske 15 varsh baad mathura, ujjain or banaras main bhi aisi hi vedhashalaayein khadi kii gayi.
