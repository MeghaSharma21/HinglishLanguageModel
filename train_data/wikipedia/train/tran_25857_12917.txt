 indonesian indonesia kii ekmaatr aadhikarik or rashtriya bhashaa he. yah malay bhashaa kii ek maanak prayukti par aadhaarit he. malay ek austronishian bhashaa he jo bahubhashiya indonesian dweepasmooh par sampark bhashaa ke rup main shatabdiyon se vistrut rahi he. indonesia vishwa kaa chauthi sabase adhik jansankhya vaala desh he or is kaaran se indonesian vishwa kii sarvaadhik boli jane vaali bhashaaon main se ek he. ise bolne vaale adhikansh log iske saath-saath aksar ek kshetreey bhashaa yaa sthaneey upbhasha satati bhi bolte hai.
 malay kii jis upbhasha par indonesian aadhaarit he use "riyau malay" bhi kehete hai. apane naam ke baavjood vastav main yah riyau dweepasmooh praant kii malay se adhik malakka sultanate kii shastreey malay bhashaa par aadhaarit he. ise 1928 indonesia yuva shapath main ekikrut bhashaa kii ghoshnaa ke baad, pehali baar 1945 main indonesia ke swatantraaa kii ghoshnaa ke saath aadhikarik bhashaa ke rup main ghoshit kiya gaya.
