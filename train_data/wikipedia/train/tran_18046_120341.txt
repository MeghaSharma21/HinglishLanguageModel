 kaaraqalpaq uzbekistan main basane vaali ek turki jati he. yah samudaay amu dariyaa ke antim bhaag main or aral sagar ke dakshini kinare par rahata he. vishwa bhar main inaki jansankhya lagbhag 6.5 lakh anumaanit kii jaati he, jinamein se 5 lakh uzbekistan ke kaaraqalpaustan swashasit praant main rahete hai. is jati kaa naam turki bhashaa ke do shabdon ko jodakar bana he: 'kaara' or 'kalpaq' yani . haalanki in logon kaa ilaaka uzbekistan main aata he, inaki samskruti vastav main kaazaakh samudaay se zyaada milti he. kaaraqalpaq log adhiktar sunni islam kii hanfi shakha ke anuyaayi hote hai.
 
 'kaaraqalpaq' main 'qa' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'k' se miltaa-julata lekin thodaa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'qeemat' or 'qareeb' ke 'qa' se miltaa he.
