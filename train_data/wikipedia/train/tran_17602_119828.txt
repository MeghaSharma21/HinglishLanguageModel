 1968 greeshmakaleen olympic, aadhikarik taur par XIX olympiad ke khelon ke rup main janaa jaataa he, oktober 1968 main mexico city, mexico main yojit ek antarrashtriya multi-sport kaarykram tha.
 yah latin america main pehali baar olympic khelon kaa aayojan kiya gaya tha or pehle spanish bhashi desh main yojit hone vaala pehla olympic khel tha. ve paramparaagat cander trek ke bajaay trek or field event ke liye ol-class trek kaa upyog karne vaale pehle khelon the.
 melborn main 1956 khel or tokyo main 1964 ke khel ke baad, 1968 ke khelon ko varsh kii antim timahi main tisare sthaan par rakhaa gaya tha. mexican chaatr andolan 1968 main ek saath hua or olympic khelon ko sarkaar ke daman se sahasambandhit kiya gaya.
