 jasoos vijay bharat main janata ke bich echivi/aids ke bare main jaagrukta failaane ke liye doordarshan or rashtriya aids niyantran sanghathan ke sahyog se bibisi world sarvice trust dwaara utpaadit ek bhartiya jasusi rahasy tivi shrinkhalaa he. iskaa june 2002 main didi neshnal par premier hua tha. sitambar 2006 main iske ant tak kul teen satr prasaarit kiye gaye.
 yah shrinkhalaa vijay par kendrit thi jo ek nijee jasoos tha or isamen gauri jo uski sahaayak thi wo baad main unki patni ban gaya thi. shrinkhalaa ko choti shrinkhalaa main vibhajit kiya gaya tha. jisamen prayek choti shrinkhalaa aamtaur par ek maheene tak lambi thi or prayek choti shrinkhalaa main vijay ek maamla hal kiya karata tha. prayek episod ke ant main, om puri ne gramin ilaakon main jaakar or aam logon se baat karke yaa darshakon ke patron kaa jawaab dekar echivi/aids ke mudde ko sambodhit kiya. darshakon ko unhen likhane ke liye aamantrit kiya gaya tha taaki ve vijay se pehle apradhi ko pakadakar patr main unka naam likh kar bhej saken. sarvshereshth uttaron ke liye puraskaar the. jinamein darshakon ko dharawahik main shaamil hone kaa mauka miltaa tha.
