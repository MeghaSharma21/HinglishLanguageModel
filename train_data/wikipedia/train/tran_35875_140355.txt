 pushkar bhaan kashmir se radio abhinayta evam patakatha lekhak the.
 unhone som nath sadhi ke saath teen naatak - grand reharsal, chapat or nev nosh likhe evam in teenon main pramukh bhoomika nibhaai.
 1952 main, pushkar ne akashwani ke srinagar channel main kalakaar evam naatakkaar ke rup main jude or 1985 main varisht nirmaataa ke pad se sevanivruth hue. bhaan ne kashimir filmon manziraat or shaayar-e-kashmir mehjoor main abhinay kiya. unhone bollywood abhinayta raaj kapur ke saath bhi abhinay kiya.
 unhen machamaa ke liye 1974 main padmashri evam 1976 main sahitya academy puraskaar diya gaya.
