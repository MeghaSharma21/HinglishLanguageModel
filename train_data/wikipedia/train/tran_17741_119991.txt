 viki par kai lekh jo pareekshak ke str par surakshit kiye jate he keval pareekshako kii anumati ke baad hi apadet hote he tab tak kisi anamak sadsy dwaara sampadit hone ke vabjood hamen purana sanskaran hi dikhaayi detaa he. mukhyatah ab kisi bhi prishth ko ardh surakshit yaa purna surakshit karne kii bajaay pareekshako ke str par set kar sakate he. isse aisa hoga ki koi anya sadsy is str par surakshit pej ko sampadit kar paayegaa. parantu uske dwaara kiye gayi parivartan kachche honge vah tabhi sweekaarya honge jab pareekshak unhe jaanch lenge matlab sweekaar kar lenge. isse yah phayda hoga ki jis prakaar koi avishisht sadsy purna surakshit prishth ko sampadit nahin kar paata parantu pareekshako ke str par set karne par us sadsy ko us prishth par sampadan karne kaa adhikaar milegaa or uske dwaara kiye gayi parivartan vaale prishth ko tab tak sweekaar nahin kiya jaayegaa jab tak koi prabandhak yaa pareekshak use sweekaar na kare. swatah parakshit sadasyon ke sampadan swatah hi jaachan ho jaayenge aashaa he is samasya se purna surakshit hone vaale prishthon ko kisi anamak sadsy dwaara sampadit na kar paane vaali samasya dur ho jaayegi. iske saath kai mahatvapurn prishtho ko barbarta evam utpaat se bachaaya ja sakegaa.
