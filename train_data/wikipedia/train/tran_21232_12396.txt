 aakasmikwaad darshanik mat ; ghatnaon ke akaaran ghatit hone kaa siddhant. yunaan ke mahaan darshanik plato ne iskaa pratipaadan kiya. simavishesh tak arestu bhi iske samarthak the. sansar kii gatividhi ke sanchaalan main anek aakasmik sanyogon kaa vishesh mahatv he. at: it mat ko aakasmikwaad kaha gaya. paashchaatya deshon main vaigyanik vivechan kaa praadhanya hone par is vichaardhaara kii manyata nahin rahi. uttarkalin unani darshanikon ne bhi "vidhi" or "kaaran" ko pradhanta dekar aakasmikwaad ke siddhant ko asweekaar kiya.
 buddha dharm ke vyapak prasar ke purv bharat main aakasmikwaad kii darshanik manyata "yadrichhavaad" ke rup main thi. brahmad kii sanrachana or sanchaalan "aakasmikta" tathaa "akaarnatv" ko kaaran mana gaya. sankhya darshan main sookshm, agyaat or aakasmik tatv ko kaary kaa prerak bataaya gaya. bhartiya darshan main "aakasmikta" kii "swechha" tathaa "anavaratata" ke rup main bhi manyata rahi he.
 "aakasmikwaad" spashtat: maanata he ki sristi kii sabhi ghatnaayen tathaa samast kaary akaaran or sanyogvash sampann ho rahe hai. is mat ke aalochakon kaa kathan he ki "kaaran" kaa sookshm swaroop gyaat na hone par use bhramavash "aakasmik" or "sanyogbaddh" kahana yuktisangat nahin he. apane gyaan, kalpana or sadhano ke simit or asamarth hone ke kaaran hi hamen kaary, ghatana athwa rachana ke "kaaran" kaa bodh nahin ho paata or is sthiti ko "aakasmik" kah diya jaataa he. samprati "aakasmikwaad" vaigyanik chintanavidhi ke kaaran maanya nahin he.
 nitishastreey chintan main "aakasmikwaad" is tathy kaa pratipaadan karata he ki maansik parivartan aakasmik or akaaran bhi hote hai, tathaa purvanishchit kaarnon evam prerak tatwon ke abhaav main bhi swechhaya sanchalit maansik vyapaar swat: gatisheel rahete hai; chitrakala main "aakasmikwaad" prakaash ke aakasmik prabhaavon ke vivechan se sambandhit hai.
