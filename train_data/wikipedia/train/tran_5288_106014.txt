 bangladesh cricket team vartmaan main dehradun main afghanistan cricket team ke khilaaf teen twanti-20 antarrashtriya khilne ke liye bharat kaa daura kar rahi he. mul rup se match ek divasiy antarrashtriya ke rup main khelaa jane vaala tha, lekin bangladesh cricket board 2020 isisi vishwa tinti 20 toornament kii taiyaari main tii20i main phiksture badalane ke liye sahamat ho gaya. may 2018 main, afghanistan cricket board ne pushti kii ki sabhi teen maichon rajeev gandhi antarrashtriya cricket stadium main khele jaaenge. yah jagah par khele jane vaale pehle antarrashtriya cricket match honge. tii20i ke aage, bangladesh ne afghanistan e ke khilaaf ek abhyaas match khelaa.
 is shrinkhalaa se pehle, afghanistan or bangladesh ne 2014 isisi vishwa tinti 20 toornament ke samooh charan main ek baar pehle ek-duusare ko khelaa tha, bangladesh ne nau wicket se jeet hasil kii thi.
