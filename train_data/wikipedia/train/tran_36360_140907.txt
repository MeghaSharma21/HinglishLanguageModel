 diophants naamak unani ganitagya ne, jo sambhavat: isa ke pashchaat teesri shatabdi main rahaa, bahut se bahupadiy anirdharya samikarnon kaa adhyayan kiya tathaa purnankon main unke halon ko gyaat kiya. kintu aadhunik tathyon ke prakaash main ab isamen koi sandesh nahin rah gaya he ki diophants se bahut pehle hi bhartiya ganitagyon ne is kshetra main atyant mahatvapurn kaary kar liya tha.
 diophanteey samasyaon main samikarnon kii sankhya charon kii sankhya se kam hoti he . in samikarnon main charon ke gunank purnank hote hai or charon kaa aisa purnank maan praapt karnaa hota he jo in samikarnon ko santusht karen. isliye un sabhi samikarnon kaa naam diophanteey sameekaran pad gaya jinamein charon ke gunank purnank hote hai. 
 diophanteey samasyaon ke ganitiya adhyayan ko aajkal diophanteey vishleshan kehete hai. itihaas main harek diophanteey sameekaran ek bujuval kii tarah prayog kii jaati rahi hai kintu diophanteey samikarnon kaa saamaanya siddhanth beesvin shatee kii ek badi ganitiya uplabdhi maani jaati he. 
 diophanteey vishleshan main praaia: nimnlikhit prashnon par vichaar kiya jaataa he-
 800 isapurv se 500 isapurv rachit shulbasutron main diophanteey samikarnon kaa purnank hal miltaa he. lagbhag 800 isapurv main boudhayan ne yugapat diophanteey samikarnon kaa hal do dhanatmak purnaakon kaa set ke rup main praapt kiya tha. unhone chaar agyaat raashiyon se yukt yugapat diophanteey samikarnon kaa hal nikaalane kii bhi koshish kii thi. 600 isapurv main aapstamb ne 5 agyaat raashiyon vaale diophanteey samikarnon kaa hal karne kii koshish kii thi.
 ati pracheen kaal se hi bhartiya ganitagya   a x + b y = c  {\displaystyle ax+by=c} prakaar ke samikarnon kaa purnank hal khojane kii disha main karyrat rahe hai. yadhyapi aajkal inhen 'diophanty sameekaran' kaha jaataa he kintu diophants se bahut pehle shulbasutron main aisi ganitiya samasyaon kii vishad charchaa he. shulbasutron kaa kaal 800 isapurv yaa usase bhi pehle he.

 'kuttak' kaa shabdic arth 'kootane vaala' he. kuttak vidhi vastav main ek punaravarti vidhi he. vartmaan samay main bhaskar dwaara 621 i main vyakhya sahit di gayi is vidhi ko 'aryabhat kii kalanvidhi' kehete hai jo pratham ghaat vaale diophanty samikarnon ke hal kii maanak vidhi ban gayi he.
