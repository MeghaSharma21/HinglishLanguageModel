 mahilaon kii ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket mahila cricket ke simit overon ke rup he. maiches 50 over, mens game ke baraabar ke liye nirdhaarit kar rahe hai. mahilaon ke pehle vanade pehali mahila vishwa cap jo england main yojit kiya gaya tha ke hisse ke rup main, 1973 main khelaa gaya. pehle vanade dekha mejabaan team ek antarrashtriya ekadash ko haraaya.
 2006 main, isisi ne ghoshnaa kii ki keval sheersh 10 wein sthaan par pakshon test or vanade kaa darja hoga. 2011 mahila cricket vishwa cap kwalifire ke dauraan netherland sheersh 6 sthaan pe khatm nahin kii haisiyat se apane ek din kaa darja kho diya he. ek din kii sthiti ke saath sheersh 4 tiimon ke is kwalifing toornament main bhaag lene ke liye aavashyak nahin kar rahe the, is toornament main sheersh 6 sheersh 10 samgr sthanon pe gathan kiya he. bangladesh das deshon main jo vartmaan main ek din kaa darja diya he main se ek ke rup main netherland badal diya. deshon kii jo vartmaan main ek din kaa darja diya he is prakaar hai:
 nimnlikhit timen bhi vanade nahin khelaa he, lekin vartmaan main, vanade sthiti nahin he, haalanki ve bhavishya main us sthiti hasil karne ke liye arhata praapt kar sakate.
 anya tiimon jo ek baar vanade kaa darja diya tha ke liye bhi kar rahe hai, lekin ab is tarah ke khel khelte hai. teen hi 1973 mahila vishwa cap main prakat hota he. chaar purv vanade timen hai:
