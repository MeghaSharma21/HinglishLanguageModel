 dablyoo ium kak vedhashala sanyukt rajya america ke hawaii rajya main sthit ek do-doorabeenon vaali khagoleey vedhashala he. hawaii prashanth mahaasaagar main sthit ek dwipon vaala rajya he or kak vedhashala uske ek dweep par sthit mouna kea naamak pahari ke shikhar ke sameep lagbhag 4,145 meter kii oonchaai par sthit he. yah vishwa kii sabase badi khagoleey doorabeenon main se ek he. apane dur-daraaj va unche sthaan, bade prakaashiki upkarnon or nai takneekon vaale yantron ke kaaran yah antariksh anusandhan ke liye ek bahut mahatvapurn vedhashala maani jaati he.
 is vedhashala ko sthapit karne ke liye san 1985 main howard bi kak ne apane pita ke samaran main banaai "dablyoo ium kak nidhi" se 7 karod america dollar kaa chanda diya tha. inhen ke naam par vedhashala kaa naam rakhaa gaya. 'kak' shabd main 'ji' swar ke uccharan par dhyaan den - yah 'e' or 'i' ke uccharan se alag he or in teenon kii tulana angrezi ke 'pan', 'pen' or 'pen' se dekhi ja sakati he.
