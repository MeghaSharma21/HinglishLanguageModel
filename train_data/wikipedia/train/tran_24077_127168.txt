 haddi ke koyale kaa upyog pramukh rup se rangon or gandhon ko dur karne ke liye hota he. ek samay anek deshon main safed chini ke praapt karne ke liye iskaa upyog hota tha.
 koyala kathor haddiyon se banaaya jaataa he. bahut dinon se rakhi yaa gaadi haddiyon se acha koyala nahin banataa. koyala banane main haddiyon ko tukade tukdekar, bhaap or vilaayak se nishkarshithkar tathaa haddi ko bhanbhak main rakhkar dhire dhire garam karte hai. isamen kuch gaisen, kuch haddi tel, kuch alkatraa or kuch ammonia praapt hota he. haddi kaa lagbhag 60 pratishat koyale ke rup main praapt hota he. haddi ke koyale main nimnlikhit padaarth rahete hai :
 koyale kaa rang halkaa kaala or koyale kii raakh safed yaa malai ke rang kii hoti he. koyala drudh or sardhraan hota he. kuch dinon ke upyog ke baad koyale kii sakriyata nasht ho jaati he, par usko punajirvta kiya ja saktaa he. peeche yah niskriy ho jaataa he or khaad ke liye prayukt hota he. isamen calosium phosphate rahane ke kaaran yah bahumoolya khaad he.
