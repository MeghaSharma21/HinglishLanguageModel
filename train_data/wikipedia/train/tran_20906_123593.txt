 hazarat ibraham ishwar ke aadesh se mesopotemia ke upar tathaa haran naamak shaharon ko chhodkar kanan or misr chale gaye. bible main ibraham kaa jo vrittant miltaa he, uski rachana lagbhag 900 i.pus. main anek paramparaon ke aadhaar par hui thi. isamen samskruti or reeti-rivaajon kaa jo varnan he vah hammurabi se bahut kuch miltaa-julata he. ibrano tathaa hammurabi ke bahut se kanoon ek jaise hai. aadhunik khudaai dwaara hammurabi kaa acha parichay praapt hua he.
 sari bible main ibraham kaa mahatv sweekrut he-
  ye ek ishvar ke maarg chalate the. bible ke anusaar ishwar ne unako kanaan desh dilaane kii pratigya kii thi. inke saath ishwar kaa jo vyaakhyaan hua tha uski smriti main yahudee khatna karte hai.hazrat mohammad,hazrat musa or hazrat isa ibraham ke sabase mahan vanshajon main se hai.
  ibraham ko ishwar kaa mitra kaha gaya he. ishwar ke aadesh par ye apane ekmaatr putr hazarat ismail kii qurbaani karne ke liye taiyaar the. vastut: abraham un samast logon ke aadhyatmik pita maane jate hai, jo ek ishwar par aasthaa rakhte hai.
 islam dharm ke granth quran ke anusaar, hazarat ibrahim ek bahut bade paigambar hai . kuran main ibrahim ke naam se ek adhyay bhi he jise "surah-ibrahim" kehete hai.
