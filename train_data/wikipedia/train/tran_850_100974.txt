 
 arun jetali bhartiya janata party ke pramukh netaa hai. ve vartmaan samay main bharat ke vitt mantri hai. ve rajg ke shashan main kendriya nyaay mantri ke saath-saath kai bade pad par asin the.
 unka janm maharaj kishan jetali or ratan prabhaa jetali ke ghar main hua. unke pita ek vakil hai, unhone apni vidyalayan shiksha saint jevirs skool, nai dilli se 1957-69 main purna kii. unhone apni 1973 main shri ram college of commers, nai dilli se commers main snaatak kii. unhone 1977 main dilli vishvavidhyalay ke vidhi sankaay se vidhi kii degree praapt kii. chaatr ke rup main apane karier ke dauraan, unhone academik or paathyakram ke atirikt gatividhiyon donon main utkrisht pradarshan ke vibhinn sammaanon ko praapt kiya hai. wo 1974 main dilli vishvavidhyalay ke chaatr sanghathan ke adhyaksh bhi rahe. 
 jetali ne 24 may 1982 ko sangeeta jetali se vivaah kar liya. unke do bachche, putr rohan or putree sonaali hai.
