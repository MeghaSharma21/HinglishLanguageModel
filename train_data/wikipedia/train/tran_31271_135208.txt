 aarazu laurence disuza dwaara nirdeshit 1999 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. isamen maadhuri dikshit, akshay kumaar, saf ali khan, amrish puri, paresh rawal or rima lagu ne mukhya kirdaar nibhaae hai. 
 dayashankar landan, britain main rahane vaale ek amir enarai hai. unki ekmaatr beti puja unki ankhon kaa tara he. unka jivan puja or unki patni parvati ke aasapaas gumti he. dayashankar apane mrit mitra ke bete amar kaa khyaal bhi rakhte hai. vah hameshaa yah chahta he ki unke god liya hua putr amar or unki beti puja bade hokar ek-duusare se shaadi karen. haalanki, puja ek payalat vijay se pyaar main pad gai he. vijay se vivaah ke iraade se puja ke pita bahut niraash hai.
 sangeet kii rachana anu malik dwaara kii gai he or bol anand bakshi ke hai.
