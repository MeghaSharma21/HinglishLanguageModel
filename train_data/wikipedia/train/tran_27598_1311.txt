 santander bada girjaghar spain main santander shahar main sthit he. ise gothic style main banaaya gaya he. haalanki isamen baad main sudhaar hota rahaa he. 
 ye ek itihaasik girjaghar he. ise 8veen sadi main ek pahari sero de somorrostro pe banaaya gaya. is se pahile yahan ek roman kasbaa ports vikatoriye luilbrigencym maujuud tha. iske neeche kii manzil 12veen sadi main banaai gayi thi. iske ab kaa vartmaan mukhauta alphonso aathven ne taiyaar kiya tha. isaki upar kii manzil 12veen se 14veen manzil ke dauraan banaai gai. isamen ek gothic math bhi maujuud he. 1941i. main spani grah yudh main is girjaghar kaa bahut nuksaan hua.
