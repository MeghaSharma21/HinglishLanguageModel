 khaadya yogaj vah padaarth hote hai jo khaadya vastuon main suvaas, rup, rang, gandh yaa anya kisi gun ko surakshit rakhane yaa badhaane ke liye daale jate hai. kuch yogaj khaane main shatabdiyon se daale ja rahe hai, jaise ki sirka, namak, chini, ityadi. aadhunik kaal main sulfur dioxide daalkar khaane ko apghaton se bachaaya jaataa he.
