 nizaam sugar factory "nizaam deccan shugars limited " ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he. yah chini kaarkaana bharat ke telugana, nizamabad jile ke bodhan shahar main sthit he.
 yah kaarkaana jila mukhyalay nizamabad se 25 kilometre dur sthit he, or ek samay main asia kaa sabase bada chini kaarkaana hone ke liye janaa jaataa he.
 nizaam sugar factory kii sthapana 1937 main hyderabad praant ke nizaam mir osman ali khan ne kiya tha. yah lagbhag 15,000 acre jameen par sthapit kiya gaya tha or is sthaan kaa naam "shakkar nagar" rakhaa gaya tha. nizaam kaal ke dauraan kaarkaana sabase pramukh niyokta tha.
 nijams shashan ke baad, kaarkaane ghate main chalanaa shuru kar diya or vartmaan sarkaar apni pichhali mahima ko banaae rakhane kii koshish kar rahi he.
