 hero saikil
 om prakaash munjaal, hero cycle ke sevanivrutt adhyaksh or hero group ke sah-sansthaapak the. unhone varsh 1956 main ‘hero group’ company ke gathan ke saath hi bharat kii pehali cycle kaa nirmaan karne vaali ikai kii shuruuaat kii thi, jo varsh 1980 ke daur main duniya main sabase zyada cycle kii nirmaataa company ban gai. vishwa ke sabase bade cycle nirmaataa ke taur par varsh 1986 main hero cycle kaa naam ‘ginis book of world record’ main bhi darj hua.ve apane kaam ke prati behad eemaandaar the. ve apane graahakon ko kabhi bhi niraash nahin karte the. ek baar jab unki company ke kaaryakarta hadtaal par the to unhone khud hi factory main kaam karnaa shuru kar diya. ise dekhte hue kaaryakartaaon ne hadtaal band kar waapas kaam main lag gaye the.
