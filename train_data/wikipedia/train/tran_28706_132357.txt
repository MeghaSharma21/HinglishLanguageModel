 sahil yaa sahel pattee africa ke pashchim se purv tak faila ek kshetra he jo sahaara ke registan ko dakshin ke ghaas ke maidaano se pruthak karata he. sahel pattee kii lambaai 3,862 kilometre he or yah atlantic mahaasaagar se lekar laal sagar tak faili hui he. is pattee kii chodai kuch sau kilometre se lekar hajaaron kilometre tak he. sahel pattee senegal, moritania, mali, burkina phaso, nizer, nyeegeria, chaad, sudan or iritria main faili hui he.
