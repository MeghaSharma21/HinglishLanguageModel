 kshatrium ongabi thoranisbi devi ek bhartiya shastreey nartak or lekhak he, jo manipuri ke bhartiya shastreey nrity rup main visheshgyata he. unhen 2003 main bharat sarkaar dwaara padam shri, chauthe ucchtam bhartiya naagarik puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha. 
 unka 3 november 1946 ko bharat ke manipur main ek chote se gaanv singhjaimi sapam leikai main paidaa hui. unhone 6 varsh kii aayu main manch pradarshan shuru kar diya tha. unhone bharat or canada, pashchim germany, landan, dubai or sanyukt rajya america jaise anya deshon main kai kala tyoharon main pradarshan kiya he. 
 devi maharaja okadrajeet singh se royal bage ke praptakarta hai or manipur sarkaar se swarna padak praapt kiya he. manipur rajya kala academy ne 1977 main unhen vaarshik puraskaar se sammanit kiya. 1980 main sangeet naatak academy ne ek puraskaar se sammanit kiya. 1991 main manipur sarkaar se unhen pramaan patr kaa sammaan mila. unhen 2003 main bharat sarkaar dwaara padam shri, chauthe ucchtam bhartiya naagarik puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha.
