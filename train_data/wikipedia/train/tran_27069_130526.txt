 acharya dharsen pratham shatabdi ke digambar sadhu the. 
 acharya dharsen main, pehali sadi main do acharya, acharya pushpadanth or acharya bhootbali ko aagam granth shatkhandgam lipibaddh karne hetu nirdeshit kiya. is aagam ke tirthankar mahavir karta he. do acharya likhaa tha, tad ke patton par, shatkhandgam ke bich - sabase purana gyaat digambar jain granthon. yah tha ke bare main 683 varsh ke baad nirvana ke mahavir he.
