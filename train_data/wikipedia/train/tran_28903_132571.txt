 
 hawaizada ek bhartiya bollywood film he, jiskaa nirdesan vibhu puri ne kiya he. iskaa nirmaan reliance intertenment ke saath vishaal gurmani or rajesh banga ne kiya he. is film main mukhya kirdaar main ayushmaan khurana, pallavi sharadaor mithun chakravarti hai. yah film shivkar baapuji talapade ke avishkaar par aadhaarit he or cinemagharon main 30 janvari 2015 ko pradarshit hogi.
 yah kahani ek bharat main rahane vaale ladke kii he, jo udana chahta he or safal bhi ho jaataa he.
 yah kahani bhartiya avishkaarak shivkar baapuji talapade ke avishkaar par aadhaarit he. jisamen vah bharat kaa pehle vimaan kaa avishkaar karte hai. bombai main 1895 kii is ghatana kaa jab vibhu puri ko pataa chalaa to unhone is par ek film banane kii sochi. jisamen mukhya kirdaar main ayushmaan khurana ko liya.
