 yah lekh uttrakhand kii mandakini naamak nadi par he. anya mandakini lekhon ke liye dekhen mandakini
 mandakini nadi alakananda nadi kii ek sahaayak nadi he. is nadi kaa uddgam sthaan uttrakhand main kedarnath ke nikat he. mandakini kaa strot kedarnath ke nikat charabadi himand he. sonprayag main yah nadi vasukiganga nadi dwaara jalposhit hoti he. rudraprayag main mandakini nadi alakananda nadi main mil jaati he. uske baad alakananda nadi vahaan se bahati hui devprayag kii ore badhati he, jahaan bah bhagirathi nadi se milakar ganga nadi kaa nirmaan karti he.
 upsarg "mand" kaa arth he "shithil" or "dhimaa" or isliye mandakini kaa arth hua "vah jo shithilataa se bahe".
 guptkashi kasbe ke nikat mandakini nadi
 rudraprayag kii ore bahati mandakini jahaan vah alakananda main mil jaati he
 shrimadbhagwat main mandakini kaa ullekh moksh-pradaayini nadiyon main se ek ke rup main hua he.
