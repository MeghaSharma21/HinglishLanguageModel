 veena hindi kii sabase puraani patrika he jo anwarat prakashit ho rahi he. yah shri madhyabhaarat hindi sahitya samiti, indaur dwaara prakashit kii jaati he.
 rashtrabhasha hindi evam sahitya ke mulyon ko samaaj tak pahunchaane ke uddeshy se samiti ne san 1927 main veena patrika kaa prakaashan aarambh kiya tha. tab se ab tak veena nirantar prakashit ho rahi he. ye shrey bharat main sirf veena ko hi praapt hua he. pandit ambikaprasad treepathi ke sampaadan main veena kaa pehla ank oktober 1927 main prakashit hua.
 veena ko prarambh se hi desh ke anek shalaka-purushon, chintakon evam murdhanya sahityakaron kaa sakriy sahyog praapt hota rahaa he. mahaamanaa madanamohan malviya, mahatma gandhi, dorajendra prasadji, kaaka kalelkar, bharataratna dobhagvandas aadi mahapurushon evam desh ke prakhyat chintakon jaise jaishankar prasad, niraala, mahadevi, harivansh raay bachchan, baalkrishnasharma naveen, subhadrakumari chouhan, maakhanlaal chaturvedi, acharya ramachandra shukla, dongendra, dogulab raay, premachandji, agyeya, vrindavanlaal verma, rajkumaar verma, doshivamangala singh suman aadi kii lekhnee kaa prasad veena ko miltaa rahaa he. purv pradhanmantri shri atalbihari vaajapeyi ji bhi yuvavastha main veena ke lekhak rahe hai.
 veena samakaleen or sambhaavnashil lekhakon kii rachanaaon ko samaan rup se prakashit karti he. veena ke shodhaparak lekh vidhyaarthiyon ke liye kaaphi upyogi sabit hote hai. tatkaaleen raashtrapati doshankardayal sharma ne ek samaaroh main kaha tha ki shodh or saahitiyik patrika ke rup main veena kaa puure desh main vishisht sthaan he.veena kaa sampaadan abhi tak solah manishiyon ne kiya he. pan.ambika prasad treepathi veena ke pehle sampadak the. varttman main dow vinayak pandeya yah daayitv nibhaa rahe he. vibhinn avasaron par veena ke 30 visheshank prakashit ho chuke he.
