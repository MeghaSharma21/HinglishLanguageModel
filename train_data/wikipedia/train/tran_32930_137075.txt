 bharat sarkaar ne san 1968 main ucch shiksha ke madhyam parivartan par bal diya tha. is rashtriya shiksha niti ke antargat bhartiya bhashaaon main ucch shiksha ke vidhyaarthiyon ke liye vibhinn vishyon kii pustakon ke lekhan, prakaashan ke saath-saath unka vipanan bhi kiya janaa tha. isi sandarbh main hindi pradeshon main granth acadamiyon kaa gathan kiya gaya.
 san 1970 main ek swaitshasi sanstha ke rup main madhyapradesh hindi granth academy kii sthapana kii gai thi. san 2000 main madhyapradesh kaa ek hissaa alag hokar chhattisgarh ke rup main saamne aayaa. nae bane rajya chhattisgarh main janvari 2006 main vidhiwat rup se chhattisgarh rajya hindi granth academy kii sthapana kii gai, jiskaa panjiyan 06 june 2006 ko hua. san 2000 main teen nae bane rajyon chhattisgarh, jharkhand or uttaranchal main chhattisgarh pehla rajya he jahaan hindi granth academy kii sthapana kii gai.
 academy ke adhyaksh evam upaadhyaksh kaa pad paden he. adhyaksh mananeey ucch shiksha mantri evam upaadhyaksh mananeey schuli shiksha mantri hote hai. academy kaa sampurna kaary sanchaalak ke adheen hota he. vidhiwat rup se astitv main aane ke baad chhattisgarh rajya hindi granth academy kaa pratham sanchaalak varisht patrkaar, lekhak shri ramesh naiyar ko banaaya gaya.
