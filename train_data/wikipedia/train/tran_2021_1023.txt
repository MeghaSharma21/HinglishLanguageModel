 kutaisi georgia kii vidhaayi rajdhani tathaa teesraa sabase adhik jansankhya vaala nagar he. yah tiblicy se 221 kimi dur rioni nadi ke tat par sthit he tathaa pashchimi kshetra imereti kii rajdhani he. yah aitehasik rup se georgia ke pramukh nagaron main se ek he tathaa madhyayug main pehle georgia rajshahi tathaa baad main imereti rajshahi kii rajdhani rahaa he. 26 may 2012 ko georgian raashtrapati mikhail sakashwili ne yahan desh ke naye sansad bhawan kaa udghaatan kiya, tabase yah nagar desh kii vidhaayi rajdhani ke rup main janaa jane laga.
