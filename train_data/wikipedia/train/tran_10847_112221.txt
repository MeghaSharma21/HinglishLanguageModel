 lesley clodias maidani hockey ke prasiddh bhartiya khilaadi the. bharat sarkaar ne inhen 1971 main padmashri purushkaar se sammanit kiya. 
 lesley clodias kaa janm bilaspur, chhattisgarh main 25 march 1927 ko hua. angloindian samudaay ke clodias kii ruchi khelon main bachapan se hi thi. clodias kaa nidhan 85 varsh kii aayu main kolkata ke ek nijee aspataal main 20 disambar 2013 ko ho gaya.
 clodias pehle bengal nagpur railway team ke liye footbal khelte the. 1946 main unhen beton cap ke liye bengal nagpur hockey team main jagah di gayi. iske baad unhone footbal chhod ke hockey par hi dhyaan dena shuru kiya. 1948 ke landan olympic, 1952 ke helsinki olympic or 1956 ke melbern olympic main swarna padak jeetne vaali team ke sadsy rahe clodias ne varsh 1960 ke rome olympic main bhartiya team kii captaini kii, rome olympic main bharat ne rajat padak praapt kiya. 
 clodias or udham singh kaa naam ginis book of world ricards main darj he. in donon ne hockey main olympic ke sarvaadhik padak praapt kiye hai. 
 2012 ke landan olympic khelon ke samay teen bharatiyon khiladiyon ke naam par vahaan kii metro stesan ke naam rakhe gaye jiname bhartiya hockey ke jaadugar major dhyanchand, rup singh or clodias ke naam the.
