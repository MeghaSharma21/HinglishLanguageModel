 abadway maharashtra main ratnagiri jile ke mandangarh tehsil main ek chota gaanv he. yah gaav mandangarh taluka se 17 kilometre dur hai. yah bhimrao ambedkar evam unke purvajon kaa mul gaanv hai, isliye yahan pune kii ashok sarvangin vikaas sanstha ne ashok stambh evam sheelaalekh khada kar use 'sphurtibhumi' naam diya hai. is gaanv ko saasand amar saabale ne unke vikaas yojana main shaamil kiya hai.
 2011 kii bhartiya janaganana ke anusaar ambadway gaanv sambandhi jaankaree.
