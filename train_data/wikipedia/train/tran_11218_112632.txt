 africa rashtriya congress ) dakshin africa kii shashan karne vaali party he. yah congress of south african trade unions ) evam south african communist party ke sang tripart gathbandhan main he. yah april 1994 se hi gaiyr-rangabhed loktantr sarkaar ke sthapit hone ke saath hi shasan kar rahi he. apane ko baayein baaju kii anushaasit shakti kehalaati he. yah sakaa mul rup 8 janvari 1912 main bloimfountain main sthapit hua tha.
