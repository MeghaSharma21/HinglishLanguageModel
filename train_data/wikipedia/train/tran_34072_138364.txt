 anekaantwaad jain dharm ke sabase mahatvapurn or moolbhoot siddhanton main se ek he. mute taur par yah vichaaron kii bahulata kaa siddhanth he. anekavant kii manyata he ki bhinn-bhinn konon se dekhne par satya or vaastaviktaa bhi alag-alag samaj aati he. atah ek hi drushtikon se dekhne par purna satya nahin janaa ja saktaa.
 anekaantwaad ke anusaar prayek vastu main anant virdohi yugal ek saath rahete ho. ek samay main ek hi dharm abhivyakti kaa vishay banataa he. pradhan dharm vyakt hota he or shesh gaun hone se avyakt rah jate hai. vastu ke kisi ek dharm ke saapeksh grahan va pratipaadan kii prakriya he'nay'. sanmati prakaran main nayawad or uske vibhinn pakshon kaa vistaar se vichaar kiya gaya he. vastubodh kii do mahatvapurn drushtiyaan hai-dravyaarthik or paryaayaarthik. dravyaarthik drushti vastu ke saamaanya ansh kaa grahan karti he jabaki paryaayaarthik drushti vastu ke vishesh angon kaa grahan karti he. bhagwati aadi pracheen aagmon main dravyaarthik or paryaayaarthik in donon drushtiyon kaa ullekh miltaa he.
 prayek vastu anant dharmon kaa samavaay he. vastu main anant virdohi yugal ek saath rahete hai. ek hi vastu main vastutv ke nishpadak paraspar virdohi yugalon kaa prakaashan karnaa hi anekaant he. nityatv-anityatv, vaachyatv-avachyatv, ekatv-anekatv aadi virdohi dharm paraspar saapeksh rahakar hi apane astitv ko surakshit rakh sakate hai.
