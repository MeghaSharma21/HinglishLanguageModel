 bharti foundation, bharti interprijej naamak prasiddh bhartiya vyapaarik samooh kaa lokopkaarik kaary karne vaali sanstha he. yah san 2000 main sthapit kiya gaya tha. iskaa uddeshy vanchit bacchon evam yuvaavon ko apana kshamta saakaar karne main sahaayta karnaa he. 
 bharti foundation ne 500 praathmik evam 50 ucch madhyamik satya bharti skool ke madhyamon se lagbhag 2 lakh bacchon ke madhya shiksha kaa alakh jagaane kaa bidaa uthaaya he. in skulon kii sthapana tathaa iske parisanchaalan va prabandhan ke liye bharti samooh lagbhag 200 karod rupe dhanrashi uplabdh kara rahaa he. skulon kii sthapana main saarvajanik-nijee-bhagidaari swaroop ko ankikrut kiya gaya he. yah bahut hi anokhaa or desh main kisi nijee kshetra kaarobaar samooh dwaara aarambh kiya gaya ek vishaal shiksha abhiyaan ban chukaa he, visheshtaur par gramin bharat ke liye. isaki sabase pramukh baat he ki is shiksha abhiyaan kaa dayera keval ek rajya vishesh na hokar 5 rajyon main he. ye vidhyalay punjab, haryana, rajasthan, uttar pradesh or tamilnadu main sthit he. varsh 2006 main aarambh hua satya bharti skool kaarykram ek vyapaak shaikshanik abhiyaan he, jo ab aise samay main jor pakadaney laga he jab bharat main bal shiksha par sarkaar bahut hi gambhir he gai he.
