 dakshin sudan ganraajya kaa kul chihn july 2011 main swatantraaa ke baad apanaaya gaya tha. swatantraaa se purv ke, dakshin sudan sudan kaa ek swytt kshetra tha. 
 kul chihn ke dizin ko april 2011 main dakshini sudan ke swytt sarkaar kii cabinet ne manjuuri di thi. iske pehle may 2011 main dakshini sudan vidhansabha dwaara is chinh samarthan kiya gaya tha.
