 raja mehadi ali khan ek bhartiya kavi, lekhak or geetkaar the. 
 mehadi ali khan kaa janm avibhajit bharat ke jhelum main hua tha. bachapan main hi unhone apane pita ko kho diya tha or kuch dinon ke baad apni maan hebe sahiba ko bhi. allamaa sar mohammad ikbaal ne hebe sahiba ko ek agrani urdu shaayara ke rup main shumaar kiya he. raja mehadi ko pehali baar es mukherjee ne 1946 main bani apni film do bhai main geet likhane kaa mauka diya. is film ke do gane kramash: 'meraa sundar sapana' 'or' 'yaad karoge' sarvaadhik hit hue. 1947 main hue sampradayik dangon ke baavjood, raja mehadi or unki patni tahiraa ne navgathit pakistan kii ore palaayan na karte hue bharat main rahane kaa faisla kiya. 1948 main dilip kumaar or kamini kaushal abhinit film shahid main likhe unke deshbhakti geeton kramash: "vatan kii raah main" or "todo-todo bachche" kii khub dhooma rahi. 29 july 1966 ko unka nidhan ho gaya.
