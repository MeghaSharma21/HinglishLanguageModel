 nikolay rerikh roosi chitrakaar, lekhak, puraatatvavid, thiosophist, darshanik the. ve hypnosis evam anya aadhyatmik kriyaaon se prabhavit the tathaa unki kalakritiyon main uski chhap bhi dikhti he.
 ve nau oktober, 1874 ko saint petersburg main paidaa hue, kintu vah lambey samay tak bharat main rahe. inhone rus, europe, madhya asia, mongolia, tibet, china, japan or bharat kii yatraaen kin. 1928 se vah himalay ke sammukh aae. iske anupam saundarya se vah itane prabhavit hue ki inhone apane jivan ke bees varsh kullu ghati main vyateet kiye. 73 varsh ke apane jivan kaal main inhone vigyaan or darshan ke kshetra main apaar gyaan praapt kiya, lekin pramukh rup se yah amar chitrakaar ke rup main prasiddh hue. inke sammaan main america main 1929 main 29 manjila vishaal bhawan banwaaya gaya. yahaan inaki chitrakaariyaan sangrahit hai. kullu ghati ke ek gaanv naggar main roric sangrahalay banaaya gaya he. 13 disambar 1947 ko inkaa nidhan hua. yah maharishi ke naam se prasiddh the.
 bahumukhi pratibha ke dhani roric na keval ek mahaan chitrakaar hi the balki puratatvvetta, kavi, lekhak, darshanik or shikshavid the. ve himalay main ek institute sthapit karnaa chaahate the. is uddeshy se unhone raja mandi se 1928 main ’’haul estant naggar‘‘ kharida.
