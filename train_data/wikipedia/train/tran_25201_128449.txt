 telugana ke jile : dakshin bharat main telugana rajya 31 jilon main vibhajit he. telugana rajya main ek 'jila' ek prashasnic bhugolic ikai he, jiskaa netritv jila colector, bhartiya prashasnic seva se sambandhit adhikari he.
 2016 main telugana ke nae jile banaae gaeshetra ke sandarbh main, bhadradri kothagudam zila 7,483 kimi 2 ke kshetra main sabase bada jila he or hyderabad jila 217 kimi 2 se sabase chota he. hyderabad jile 3943323 kii aabaadi vaala sabase adhik aabaadi vaala jila he or komaram bhim jila sab se kam jansankhya 515,805 vaala jila he.
