 raashtrapati baraak obama ke shashan main america adhik aakraamak rahaa 2016 main obama ne vibhinn deshon main 26,171 bam giraae gaye. yah aankada purv raashtrapati george dablue bush ke kaarykaal se 130 fisad adhik rahaa jisamen har teen ghannte main teen bam giraae gayi.
 sabase adhik hamale pakistan, afghanistan, libya, yaman, somalia, iraq or syria main kiye gaye. in deshon main grih yudhd ke halaat he pakistan yaman libya somalia main saat saal main america hamalo main 600 naagarik maare gaye.
 obama kaarykaal ke dauraan america senaao ne drone hamalo main visheshgyata hasil kii bush kaarykaal kii tulana main varsh 2016 main 10 fisad ijapha darj kiya gaya.
 world trade centre par hue aantki hamalo ke baad america sansad ne kuch prastaav paas kiye jisase aantak mitaane ke liye america kisi bhi desh par aavashyak sainya bal kaa istemaal kar saktaa he lekin visheshgy ise niyamo kaa ullandhan maan rahe hai. unke mutabik jin deshon par america ne 2016 main hamale kiye unka 2001 ke aantki hamalo se kuch lenaa dena dena nahin he. iske baad bhi america saina 138 deshon main maujuud he.
