 maharaja 1998 main bani hindi bhashaa kii alokik romaanchakaari film he. anil sharma dwaara nirdeshit is film main govinda or manisha koirala mukhya bhoomikaaon main hai. yah apane rajya ko waapas paane ke liye alokik shaktiyon vaale rajkumaar ke sangharsh ke bare main he. anya kalakaaron main rajbabbar, saleem ghosh, shakti kapur, aruna irani or kulbhushan kharbanda shaamil hai.
 apni mrityu se darate hue ranbir singh ne kohinoor ko jab vah ek baccha tha maarane kaa faisla kiya. haalanki vah kisi tarah batch gaya. 20 varshon ke baad kohinoor rajya main apane sahi sthaan kaa daawa karne ke liye laut aayaa. usne padaarth or jaanwaron par unnat shaktiyaan viksit kii hai, jiskaa vah ranbir or uske sahyogiyon dwaara apharan kii gai apni dai maan, ameenabi ko mukt karne ke liye upyog karata he. kohinoor ko kai pareekshanon ko paarit karnaa hoga. unamem se ek he televijan samvaadadaata style maathur dwaara phayda uthaae janaa. vah daawa karti he ki vah usase pyaar karti he; or bhukhe aadam-khor sheron se ladata he.
 sabhi nadeem-shravan dwaara sangitabaddh.
