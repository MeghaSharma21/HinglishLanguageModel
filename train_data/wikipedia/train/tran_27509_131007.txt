 tanaras khan is gharaane ke pravartak maane jate hai. tanaras khan kii taan bahut mashhoor thi. inhone taanon kaa bahut abhyaas kiya tha. inke putr umrao khan hue jinhone gharaane ko aage chalaaya. umrao khan ke baad dilli gharaana adhik unnati nahin kar sakaa or ab to yah gharaana lagbhag samaapt sa hi ho gaya he.
 sangeet shri- en.si.i.aar.tii.
