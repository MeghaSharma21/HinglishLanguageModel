 mohi kaa yuddh yaa saayo nadi kaa yuddh mongol samrajya or hungary rajya ke bich 11 april 1241 ko ladaa gaya ek yuddh tha jo europe par mongol hamale kaa ek charan tha. yah purvottar hungary main saayo nadi ke dakshinpaschim main sthit mohi shahar ke paas ladaa gaya. is yuddh main mongolon kii purna vijay hui or unhone hungary ko dhwast kar diya. hungary ke lagbhag aadhe basti-gaanv nasht kar die gaye or hungarian logon kii 15-20% aabaadi iske pashchaat maari gai.
