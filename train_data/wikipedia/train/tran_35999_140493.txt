 kishanganga nadi kashmir kshetra kii ek nadi he. iskaa naam pakistan main badalakar neelam nadi kar diya gaya he.
 kishanganga nadi jammu va kashmir rajya ke sonmarg shahar ke paas sthit kishansar jhil se shuru hoti he or uttar ko chalati he jahaan badoab gaanv ke paas draas se aane vaali ek upanadi isamen mil jaati he. phir yah kuch dur tak niyantran rekha ke saath-saath chalakar gurez ke paas paak-adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-blatistan kshetra main daakhil ho jaati he. vahaan se pashchim kii taraf bahakar yah muzzafarabad ke uttar main jhelum nadi main ja milti he. iske kul 245 kimi ke maarg main se 50 kimi bhartiya niyantran vaale ilaake main aata he or shesh 195 kimi paak-adhikrut kashmir main.
 kishanganga vaadi, jise pakistan neelam vaadi kahata he, ek 250 kimi lambi hari-bhari himalay kii god main sthit tang ghati he. yah muzzafarabad​ se hokar athmuqaam tak jaati he. yahan hindu va buddha dharmon kaa aitihaasik shiksha santhaan, sharada peeth, sthit he. yah purv kshetra saraswati devi se sambandhit mana jaataa tha or ise puraane jamaane main 'sharadadesh' bhi kaha jaataa tha.
 neelam vaadi main pravesh karne ke do raste hai. ek to muzzafarabad​ se aata he or doosraa kaibar-pakhtuunakva ke mansehara zile ke kaayaan​ shahar se.
