 alan reeve musk ek dakshin africa-canadai-america diggaj vyapaaree, niveshak, ejeeniyar, or avishkaarak he. alan spesex ke sansthaapak, siio or mukhya diziner; testla company ke sah-sansthaapak, siio or utpaada ke vaastukar; openeai ke sah-adhyaksh; neuralink ke sansthaapak or siio or da boring company ke sansthaapak hai. iske alaava ve solarsity ke sah-sansthaapak or purv adhyaksh, zip2 ke sah-sansthaapak or eks.com ke sansthaapak hai, joki baad main confinity ke saath vilay ho gaya or use nayaa naam pepel mila.
 disambar 2016 main, alan ko fors patrika kii duniya ke sabase shaktishaalee logon kii suchi main 21vaan sthaan praapt hua. janvari 2018 tak, alan kii kul sampatti 20.9 arab america dollar he, or forbs dwaara duniya ke 53wein sabase amir vyakti ke rup main soochibaddh kiya gaya he.
 alan ne kaha he ki solarsity, testla or spesex kaa lakshya, vishwa or maanavtaa ko badalane ke liye unke drushtikon ke chaaron ore ghumta hai. unke lakshyon main sandharniya urja utpaadan or upyog ke madhyam se bhumandaleey ooshmeekaran ko kam karnaa or mangal grah par maanav basti kii sthapana ke dwaara "maanav vilupt hone ke khatre" ko kam karnaa shaamil he.
 apane mukhya vyavsaayik vyavsaayon ke atirikt, unhone ek ucch gati parivahan pranali kii kalpana kii jise hiparalup ke rup main janaa jaataa he, or usne bijli ke pankhe ke pranodan ke dwaara supersonic jet vimaanon kaa urdhwadhar udaan bharna or utarana prakriya ko prastaavit kiya he, jise musk electric jet ke rup main janaa jaataa he.
