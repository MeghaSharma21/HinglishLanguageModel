 apane upar padane vaale kisi sataha ke prakaash yaa anya vidyutchumbakeey vikiran ko pratibimbit karne kii shakti kii maap ko prakaashanupaat yaa dhavalata kehete hai. agar koi vastu apane upar padane vaale prakaash ko puri tarah waapas chamakaa detii he to uskaa albido 1.0 yaa pratishat main 100% kaha jaataa he. khagolashastra main aksar khagoleey vastuon kaa albido jaanchaa jaataa he. prithvi kaa albido 30 se 35% ke bich main he. prithvi ke vayumandal ke badal bahut roshni waapas chamakaa dete hai. agar badal na hote to prithvi kaa albido kam hota.
 nayi giri barf kaa albido bahut unchaa hota he jabaki koyale kaa albido sirf 0.04 hota he.
 albido shabd ko angrezi main "albedo" likhaa jaataa he. iskaa mul latini bhashaa kaa "albus" shabd he, jiskaa arth "safed" he.
