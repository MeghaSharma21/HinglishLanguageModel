 kedarnath singh, hindi ke suprasiddh kavi va sahityakar the. ve agyeya dwaara sampadit teesraa saptak ke kavi rahe. bhartiya gyanpith dwaara unhen varsh 2013 kaa 49waan gyanpith puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya tha. ve yah puraskaar paane vaale hindi ke 10wein lekhak the.
 kedarnath singh kaa janm 7 july 1934 i ko uttar pradesh ke baliya jile ke chakiya gaanv main hua tha. unhone kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay se 1956 i main hindi main eme or 1964 main pi-ech di kii oopaadhi praapt kii. unka nidhan 19 march 2018 ko dilli main upachaar ke dauraan hua.unhone jawaharlal nehru vishvavidhyalay main bhartiya bhashaa centre main bataur acharya or adhyaksh kaam kiya tha.
 kedarnath singh kii pramukh kavy kritiyaan ‘jameen pak rahi he', ‘yahaan se dekho’, ‘uttar kabir’, ‘talstoy or cycle’ or ‘baagh’ hai. unki pramukh gadhy kritiyaan ‘kalpana or chhayavaad’, ‘aadhunik hindi kavita main bimbvidhan’ or ‘mere samay ke shabd’ hai.
 1989 main unki kriti ‘akaal main saras’ ko sahitya academy puraskaar mila tha. iske alaava unhen vyas sammaan, madhya pradesh kaa maithilisharan gupt sammaan, uttar pradesh kaa bharat-bharti sammaan, bihar kaa dinakar sammaan tathaa kerala kaa kumaar aashaan sammaan mila tha. varsh 2013 main unhen pratishthit gyanpith puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha. is puraskaar se sammanit hone vaale vah hindi ke 10wein sahityakar the.
