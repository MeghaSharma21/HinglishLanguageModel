 ross 128 bi prithvi ke aakaar kaa ek bahirgrah he jo ross 128 naamak laal boune taare ke vaasayogy kshetra main parikrama kar rahaa he. yah ek patthariilaa grah he jo prithvi se 11 prakashavarsh kii duuri par sthit he. proxima centori bi ke baad yah prithvi kaa doosraa sabase se sameepi bahirgrah he. yah prithvi se 35% adhik dravyaman rakhata he or prithvi kii tulana main apane taare kii 38% adhik roshni praapt karata he. khagolvaigyanikon kaa anumaan he ki yadi iskaa koi vayumandal he to sambhav he ki isaki sataha par drav-avasthaa main jal bhi ho saktaa he.
