 vidroh yaa baghawat aagyaakaarita yaa aadesh kaa inkaar he. yah ek sthapit praadhikaran ke aadesh ke khilaaf khule pratirodh ko sanderbhit karata he. vidrohi yaa baagi vah vyakti he jo vidroh yaa vidrohi gatividhiyon main hissaa letaa he. koi vidroh kisi utpeedan kii sthiti or asweekruti kii bhaavana se utpann hota he. phir is sthiti ke liye jimmedaar praadhikari kaa na paalan karne dwaara swayan prakat hota he.
