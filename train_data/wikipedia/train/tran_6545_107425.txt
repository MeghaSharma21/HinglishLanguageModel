 'jya', 'kotigya' tathaa 'utkramajya' naamak teen trikoshmetiya falan bhartiya khagolashastriyon evam ganitagyon dwaara pratipaadit kiye gayi the. vartmaan samay main praapt granthon main sabase pehle ye suryasiddhanth main milte hai. vastutah ye vrutt ke chaanp ke falan hai na ki kone ke falan. kintu aaj hame gyaat he ki jya or kojya kaa vartmaan samay ke sine or cosine se bahut najadik kaa sambandh he. or vastav main sine or cosine naamak in vartmaan falnon ke naam sanskrit ke jya or kojya se hi vyutpann hue hai.
 'jeeva' or 'jya' samanaarthi hai. 'jeeva' kaa mul arth he - 'dhanush kii dori'
