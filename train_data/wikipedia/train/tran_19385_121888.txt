 andhra pradesh vidhaan sabha bharat ke rajya andhra pradesh kaa sanrachana vidhaan sabha kaa nimna sadan he. 2014 tak andhra pradesh kii vidhaan sabha main 175 vidhayak shaamil hai, jinaki avadhi 5 varsh he. vidhansabha kii sanrachana dwisadaneey he or 3 satron arthath bajet, monsoon or sheetakaaleen satr yojit kiye jate hai. andhra pradesh ke vidhaan sabha ne 2016 ke rajya bajet satron se amravati main antarim vidhaayi vidhansabha bhawan main manthan shuru kiya. nai imaarat main unnat he swachaalit bhaashan anuwaad kii suvidha, swat: vot recording system. 
 yah andhra pradesh ke shahar vijaybada ke nikat rajdhani amravati main he.
