 sar sandford fleming, ek british canadai ejeeniyar or avishkaarak the. great britain ke scotland, main janme or paley bade fleming ne 18 varsh kii umr main aupniveshik canada main pravaas kiya. unhone duniya bhar main maanak samay kshetron kaa prastaav diya, canada ke pehle dak tikat ko dizin kiya, surveykshan or nakshaa banane ke kshetra main vishaal kaary kiya, antaraupniveshik railway tathaa canada prashanth railway ke adhiktar hisse kaa ejiniyari kaary kiya or canada kii royal socity ke sansthaapak sadsy or royal canadian institute of toronto jo ki ek vigyaan sanghathan he, ke sansthaapak the.
