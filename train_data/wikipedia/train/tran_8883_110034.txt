 is lekh main ullekhneey audyogic vipadaaon kii suchi di jaayegi. audyogic vipadaa se aashay audyogic companiyon dwaara paidaa kii gayi vipadaaon se he. ye vipadaayen durghatanavash ho sakati hai yaa suraksha kii avhelnaa se ho sakate hai yaa ayogyata se ghatit ho saktin hai. audyogic vipadaaon main jan-dhan kii bahut adhik haani hoti he.
