 tulabugha, talubugha yaa telubugha sunaharaa urdu naamak mongol khaanat kaa san 1287 isvi se lekar 1291 isvi tak khan tha. vah taratu kaa putr or sunahare urdu samrajya ke sansthaapak batu khan kaa pota tha. 1257 main usne mashhoor mongol sipahasaalar burundai ke netritv main lithuania par hamale main bhaag liya. 1284-85 main usne nogai khan ke saath hungary par duusare akraman kaa or 1287 main poland par tisare akraman kaa netritv kiya. 1287 main nogai khan kii madad se vah sunahare urdu kaa khan bana lekin 4 saal baad nogai khan ke hi kaaran use hataakar tokhta ko khan banaaya gaya. tokhta ne 1290 yaa 1291 main use marava diya.
