 cerikunda, gambia kaa sabase vishaal nagar he, jo rajdhani banjul ke dakshin-pashchim main sthit he. san 2006 main is nagar kii jansankhya 3,35,733 thi. yadhyapi banjul rajdhani he lekin chunki vah ek dweep par basaa hua he or isliye uskaa adhik failaav nahin ho saktaa, isliye cerikunda kaa hi failaav ho rahaa he.
 cerikunda apane bazaar, resham kapaas vriksh or kushti akhaade ke liye janaa jaataa he. nagar ke upnagareey kshetra hai kanifing, latrikunda, sukuta or landan konaa or yah kasbaa samudr kinare ke bakau, faajaraa or kotu ke prashray-sthalon tak fail rahaa he.
