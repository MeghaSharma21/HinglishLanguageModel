 sela darra bharat ke arunachal pradesh rajya main tawang or west kameng jile ke madhya avasthit ek ucch tungta vaala pahari darra he. isaki oonchaai 4,170 meter he or yah tibeti buddha shahar tawang ko dirang or guwahati.se jodta he. is darre se hokar hi tawang shesh bharat se ek mukhya sadak ke madhyam se judaa hua he. is darre ke aas-paas vanaspatiyaan alp maatra main ugti hai tathaa yah kshetra aamtaur se varsh bhar barf se dhanka hota he. is darre ke shikhar ke najadik sthit sela jhil is kshetra main sthit lagbhag 101 pavitra tibeti buddha dharm ke jhiilon main se ek he.
 bharat sarkaar ne 2018-19 ke bajet main sabhi mausam main parivahan kii suvidha se yukt sela darra surang ke nirmaan hetu vittaposhan kii ghoshnaa kii.
 sela darra tibeti buddha dharm kaa ek pavitra sthal he. baudhon kaa maananaa he ki yahan aas-paas main 101 pavitra jhilein hai.
 ek anushruti ke anusaar 1962 ke bharat-china yuddh ke dauraan jaswant singh ravat naamak bhartiya sena ke ek vir jawan ne is darre ke najadik chini sainikon ke khilaaf akele yuddh kiya tha. jaswant singh ravat ko unke saahas evam kartavya ke prati samarpan ke liye maranoparant maha vir chakra se sammanit kiya gaya tha.
