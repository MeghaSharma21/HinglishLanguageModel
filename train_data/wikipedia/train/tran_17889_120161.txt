 aksharpalli sankhya-lekhan kii ek paddati he jo purv-aadhunik kaal tak bharat main pandulipiyon ke pannon kii prushtasankhya darshane ke liye prayukt hoti thi.
 is pranali ke utpatti ke vishay main kuch bhi gyaat nahin he. aisa anumaan kiya jaataa he ki isaki utpatti brahmi kii sankhya paddati se hua hoga. is paddati kaa upyog jain granthon main bahutayat main hua he jo 16veen shatabdi tak dekhne ko milti he. nepal main bhi yah pranali bahut samay tak prachlan main rahi. kerala aadi main yah pranali 19veen shatabdi tak dekhne ko milti he.
 udhaaharan nimnlikhit sarini main kuch udhaaharan diye gayi hai. dhyaan rahe ki sankhyaon ke liye prayukt sabhi shabd nahi diye gayi hai.
