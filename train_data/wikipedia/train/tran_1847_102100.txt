 prashanth plate ek bhugolic plate he jiske upar prashanth mahaasaagar kaa adhikansh hissaa sthit hai. iskaa kshetrafal 10.3 karod varg kilometre he or yah prithvi kaa sabase bada bhugolic plate he. is plate kii seemaa uttar main uttar america plate se, purv main nazaka, huaan de fooka or cocaos plate se, dakshin main antarctic plate se or pashchim main philipino sagar or hind-australian plate se milti he.
