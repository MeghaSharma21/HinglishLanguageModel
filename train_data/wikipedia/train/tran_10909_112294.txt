 ke. bi. tilak hindi filmon ke saath hi telegu or marathi pilmon ke prasiddh nirmaataa-nirdeshak or bhartiya swatantraaa senaanee the. 
 unka janm 14 janvari 1926 main hua tha. unki koi santan nahin thi. unhone apane bhattije ko god liya tha. 23 sitambar 2010 ko hyderabad ke ek aspataal main 84 varsh kii avasthaa main unka nidhan ho gaya.
 unhone hindi ke alaava telugu, tamil or marathi main bhi anek yaadgaar chalchitr banaaya. 
 ve pragatisheel vichaaron ke dhani the. apane chalchitron main unhone saamaajik samasyaon kaa prabhavi chithran kiya he.
 unhen andhra pradesh sarkaar ne bi.en. reddy aword se sammanit kiya tha.
