 20 navambar 2016 ko kanpur se 100 kilometre dur pukhraayaan main ravivaar tadake qareeb teen baje patna-indore express ke 14 dibbe patari se utarane ke kaaran yah bada haadasa hua. ye train indore se patna ja rahi thi. haadase main 153 logon ke marane kii pushti hui he. vahiin 250 se zyada ke ghayal hone kii bhi khabar he.
 haadasa ravivaar subah 3 bajkar 10 minute par hua. train indore se patna kii ore ja rahi thi. jaise hi indore-patna express jab pukhrayaan stesan se guzarate hue kanpur ke liye nikali hi thi, ki kuch kshan baad dalel nagar crossing ke paas train ke chaudaa dibbe patari se utar gaye. rajya kii bason ke dwaara ghayalon ko aspataal le jaaya gaya, vahiin mritkon ko postmortem house pahunchaaya gaya. doctors or paramedical kii timen bhi ghatnasthal par raahat kaary ke liye pahaunchin.
 anek log dibbon main fase hue the. gas kattar kii madad se dibbon ko katkar fanse hue logon ko nikaalane kii koshishen kii gayin. raahat va bachaav kaaryon main andiaref or prashasan kii timen lageen. sena kii team bhi bachaav kaary ke liye ghatnasthal pahaunchin.
 kanpur rail haadase ke kaaran kai trainen radd va diawart kar di gayin. 
 haadase main maare gaye logon main se 7 kii madhyapradesh ke nagarikon ke taur par pahchaan hui.
