 suvidhapurvak ek chit or sthir hokar baithne ko asana kaha jaataa he. asana kaa shabdic arth he - baithna, baithne kaa aadhaar, baithne kii vishesh prakriya aadi.
 paatanjal yogadarshan main vivrutt ashtaangyog main asana kaa sthaan tritiy evam gorakshnathadi dwaara pravartit shadangayog main pratham he. chitt kii sthirataa, sharir evam uske angon kii dridhtaa or kayik sukh ke liye is kriya kaa vidhaan miltaa he. 
 vibhinn granthon main asana ke lakshan hai - ucch swasthya kii prapti, sharir ke angon kii dridhtaa, pranayamadi uttarvarti sadhankramon main sahaayta, sthirataa, sukh daayitv aadi. pantajali ne sthirataa or sukh ko lakshanon ke rup main mana he. prayatnshathily or paramatma main man lagane se isaki siddhi batalaai gai he. iske siddh hone par dwandvon kaa prabhaav sharir par nahin padataa. kintu patanjali ne asana ke bhedon kaa ullekh nahin kiya. unke vyaakhyaataaon ne anek bhedon kaa ullekh kiya he. in asana kaa varnan lagbhag sabhi bhartiya saadhnaatmak sahitya main miltaa he.
