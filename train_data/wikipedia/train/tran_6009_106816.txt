 united jihadi council, jisko mutahida jihad council bhi kehete hai, 1994 main bana charampanthi jihadi sanghathan he. is sanghathan ke pramukh 60 saal ke mohammad yousuf shah urf saiyad salahuddin hai, jo jammu or kashmir ke sabase bade or puraane charampanthi sanghathan hizbul mujahidin ke bhi mukhiya hai. is sanghathan ko kashmir main bhartiya shashan ke khilaaf lard rahe vibhinn sashastr ugravadi samoohon ko ekajute karne or un ke prayaason ko kendrit karne ke liye banaaya gaya tha. isse hathiyaar, gola barood, prachaar saamagree or sanchaar jaise sansadhano ke vitaran ko or adhik suvyavasthit bana diya. isse vibhinn jihadi samoohon kaa samanvay or unke sansadhano ko bhartiya prashasit kashmir ke andar sainya mahatv ke thikaanon par jaankaree ekatra karne, yojana ke sanchaalan or strikkarne ke liye pool karnaa bhi aasaan bana diya he.
