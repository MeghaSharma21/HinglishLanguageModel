 si ve raaman pillai malayalam ke mahaan upanyaaskaar, naatakkaar tathaa patrkaar the. unhen praayah 'si ve' kaha jaataa he. malayalam main ve sabase mahaan aitihaasik upanyaaskaar hue hai. si ve kaa janm tiruvananthapuram main hua tha. 
 unke upanyaason kii prishthbhumi 18veen shatabdi kii ghatnaon kii shrunkhalaayen hai jinke dwaara tiruvitankur rajya kaa nirmaan evam sansthaapan hua. martand verma upanyaas main ramantapi or martandaverma ke bich uttaradhikari ke kalah kii kahani kaa varnan he. dharmaraaja upanyaas kartiktirunal ramvarmraj ke shasankaal kii raajnneetik evam sainik ghatnaon ke upar aadhaarit he. ramrajbahadur upanyaas tipu sultan ke tiruvitankur par kiye gaye akraman kii prishthbhumi par taiyaar kiya gaya he. in sabhi upanyaason ke kathanak vistrut hai. kerala ke itihaas kii gatividhiyon main unki antardrushti or sajiv paatro ke chithran kii yogyata ne pathakon ke netron ke samaksh tatkaaleen ghatnaon kaa jeeta-jaagta chitra upasthit kar diya he. unki style main saralata kii kami he. unhone bahut se vyangamay prahasan bhi likhe hai.
