 icelandic iceland kii bhashaa he, jiskaa sambandh uttar germanik bhashaa se he. iske nikatatam sambandhi ferose or kuch norwegian boliyaan jaise telemark boli or sognamal hai. bahut se pashchim europea bhashaaon main swar parivartan str ko kam kar diya he, vishesh rup main sangyavibhakti ke sambandh main, lekin icelandic bhashaa ne latin yaa zyada nikat rup se puraani norwegian or puraani angrezi, kii tarah vyakaran sambandhi ekarupata banaakar rakhi he.
 isa kii 8veen shatabdi ke aasapaas pracheen scandenevian bhashaa kii uttari shakha do upashakhaon- purvi upashakha evam pashchimi upashakha-main vibhajit ho gai. is purvi upashakha se icelandic evam narvian bhashaayen viksit hui. aanrabh main icelandic evam narvian bhashaaon main koi bhinnata nahin thi. naveen shatabdi ke aasapaas norway ke nivaasiyon ne jaakar iceland ko basaaya. prakritik paristhitiyon ke kaaran iceland ke nivaasiyon kaa norway nivaasiyon se itnaa drudh sambandh nahin rahaa. falswaroop iceland kii bhashaa swatantra rup se viksit ho gai.
 saahitiyik samruddhi kii drushti se icelandic bhashaa kaa vishesh mahatv he. visheshkar 12veen se 14veen shatabdi tak kaa samay is bhashaa ke sahitya kii unnati kaa kaal he. unke virakaavyon kaa vishwasahitya main mahatvapurn sthaan he.
 is bhashaa par latin evam anya german bhashaaon kaa paryaapt prabhaav he.
