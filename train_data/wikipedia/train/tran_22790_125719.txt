 yah vaayu samast sharir main dhoomati he. isi vaayu ke prabhaav se ras, rakt tathaa anya jeevanopayogi tatv saare sharir main behate rahete hai. sharir ke samast kaaryaklap or kaary karane kii cheshtaayen bina vyaan vaayu ke sampann nahin ho sakati hai. jab yah kupit hoti he to samast sharir ke rog paidaa karti he.
