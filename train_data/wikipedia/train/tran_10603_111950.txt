 vigyaan or praudyogiki ke kshetra main germany kii uplabdhiyaan ullekhneey hai. anusandhan tathaa vikaas germany kii arthvyavastha kaa pramukh ang he. bhautikee, ganit, rasayan vigyaan tathaa ejiniyari aadi vigyaan ke vividh vidhaon main germany ke logon ne atyant mahatvapurn anusandhan kiye hai. 20veen shadi ke adhikansh kaal main vigyaan main germany ko anya deshon kii apeksha adhik nobel purskaar mili hai.
 is desh ke udyog, vishvavidhyalay tathaa anusandhan-santhaan vaigyanik anusandhan ko badhaava dete hai.
