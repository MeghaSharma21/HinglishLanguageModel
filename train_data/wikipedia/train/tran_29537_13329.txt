 blf dweep bharat ke andaman va nicobar dweepasmooh ke andaman dweepasmooh bhaag kaa ek dweep he. yah 1.14 varg kimi kshetrafal kaa ek chota dweep he. yah ek prakritik sangrakshit kshetra he. yah pashchim main spike dweep or dakshin main dakshin andaman dweep se keval samudri pani kii choti si naharon se alag he.
 blf dweep par kabhi biihat andmani samudaay ke sadsy rahaa karte the. mana jaataa he ki lagbhag 40,000 varshon tak inkaa kisi bhi anya maanviya samudaay se sameepi sampark nahin tha jis karanvash inke shariron main saadhaaran rogon se ladane kii kshamta kabhi utpann nahin hui. aadhunik kaal main, samay ke saath-saath, unki sankhya tezi se girti hui vilupti kii kaagaar par aa pahaunchi or 1961 main keval 19 jivit the. is samudaay ko bachaane ke liye street dweep par unke nivas kaa prabandh kiya gaya, jo is dweep se adhik bada he or 1969 main biihat andmanion ko vahaan le jaaya gaya. san 1987 main blf dweep ko ek prakritik sangrakshit kshetra ghoshit kar diya gaya.
