 gulyelmo marconi italy kaa anveshak tha jisane lambi duuri tak radio sanchaar ke kshetra main agrani bhoomika nibhaai. usne ek niyam diya jise 'marconi niyam' kehete hai. usne radio telegraph kaa vikaas bhi kiya. 
 marconi kaa janm italy ke boleen nagar main 25 april 1874 itho ko hua tha. aapaki shiksha diksha ghar par hi nijee taur par hui thi. sh vidhyaarthee jivan main hi aapane is baat ko bhaanp liya tha ki harts dwaara utpann kii gai vidyutchumbakeey tanrago kii madad se dur tak sandesh bheja ja saktaa he. phir to mrityu paryant aap isi kshetra main nirantar anusandhan karte rahe. radio telegraphy ko vyaavhaarik rup dene kaa shrey marconi ko hi praapt he. san 1895 main marconi ne apane ghar ke bageeche main hi radio telegraphy ke praarambhik prayogo kaa sutarapaat kiya. sheeghra hi bina kisi taar aadi kaa sahaara liye hi aap ek mile kii duuri tak radio sanket bhejne main safal hue. agle varsh aap england gayi or vahaan aapane radio telegraphy kaa sarvapratham patent praapt kiya. yahan ek pradarshan main aapane 9 mile kii duuri par radio sanket bheja. san 1899 me aapane english channel ke aar-paar 85 mile ke faasale par radio sanket bheja. aap radio transmeter or graahak yantra main sudhaar kar 12 disambar,1901 ko aitlantic mahaasaagar ke aar-paar 1,800 mile kii duuri par radio sanket bhejne main safal hue. aapaki khojon ke falswaroop hi radio yantra itane janaapayogi ban sake. in avishkaron ke upalaksh main aapako 1909 main nobel puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya. england ke badshaah tathaa rus ke zaar ne bhi marconi ko vishesh sammaan pradaan kiye. merconi kii mrityu 1937 itho main hui.
