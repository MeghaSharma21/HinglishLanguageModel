 krishakwaad ek saamaajik or rajnatik darshanik drushtikon he jisamen gramin samaaj ko nagareey samaaj se unchaa tehraaya jaataa he. is vichaardhaara ke anusaar swatantra krishak kisi aay lene vaali vyavsaayee naukar se adhik mahaan he or krishi kii jivan-paddati dwaara hi adarsh saamaajik mulyon kii prapti ho sakati he. yah gaanv ke saral jivan par jor detii he or shaharon kii halchal or sankul jeevani kii ninda.
