 balkash jhil madhya asia ke qaazakhstan desh main sthit ek vishaal jhil he. yah asia kii sabase badi jhiilon main se ek he. iskaa kul kshetrafal 16,400 kimi2 he, lekin isamen pani daalane vaali nadiyon se sinchai ke liye pani khichne kii vajah se iskaa aakaar ghat rahaa he. bich main ek dharti kii oongali ise do hisson main baantati he - pashchimi bhaag kaa pani meetha he jabaki purvi bhaag kaa pani khaaraa he. purvi bhaag pashchimi bhaag se adhik gaharaa he. iskaa sabase gaharaa sthaan keval 26 meter gaharaa he.
 balaash jhil ek band jalsambhar main sthit he jisamen saat nadiyon kaa pani aata he, jisamen sabase pramukh ili nadi he. jhil ke kinare bahut se shahar based hue hai jinamein sabase bade kaa naam bhi balaash shahar he. isamen 66,000 nivasee rahete hai.
 balkash jhil purvi kazakhstan main ek vishaal ardhachandrakaar khaare pani kii jhil he. isaki sagartal se oonchaai 900 foot he. ili, ask or lepsa aadi nadiyaan isamen girti hai, kintu is jhil se koi nadi nikalati nahin. yah registani bhaag main sthit he. iskaa purv tateey bhaag khaari mitti kaa pradesh he. iske taton par machhliyaan pakadi jaati hai. uttari kinare par taanben kii khaane hai, evam balkash nagar main tanba galane kaa kaam bhi hota he.
