 ila rugveda main 'ann' kii adhishthatri' maani gai hai, yadhyapi sayan ke anusaar unhen pruthivi kii adhishthatri maananaa adhik upyukt he. vaidic vaaiumay main ila ko manu ko maarg dikhalaanevaali evam pruthivi par yajna kaa vidhiwat niyaman karanevaali kaha gaya he. ila ke naam par hi jambudweep ke navakhandon main ek khand 'ilaavruth varsh' kehalaata he. mahabharat tathaa puraanon kii parampara main ila ko budh kii patni evam pururava kii mata kaha gaya he.
