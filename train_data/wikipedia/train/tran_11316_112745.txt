  india mark dwitiya  ek maanav sanchalit handpump he jise 50 meter yaa usase kam gaharaai se pani uthaane ke liye banaaya gaya he. yah pump duniya main sabase vyapak rup se sanchalit he. is pump kaa nirmaan varsh 1970 main vikasshil deshon tathaa gramin kshetron main pani nikalne ke liye kiya gaya tha.
 is pump kaa dizin bharat sarkaar ne unicef or vishwa swasthya sanghathan ke sahyog se banaaya tha. yah pump sholapur pump par aadhaarit he, jise bhartiya maikenic ne dizin kiya tha. unicef ke visheshgyon ne kai vikalpon ke adhyayan ke baad ise sabase tikaoo paaya tha. iskaa vyavsaayik utpaadan varsh 1977-78 main prarambh hua. varsh 1987 main iskaa vaarshik utpaadan 2,00,000 tha. iskaa niryaat asia ,africa or latin amareeki deshon ko kiya jaataa he. bharat main is pump kii vaarshik khapat 50,000 he.
 pehle is pump ke borwell main india mark dwitiya pump lagane main lohe ke pipe stemaal hote the jo jang lagane ke kaaran kam tikaoo the ab unke sthaan par pi ve si ke pipe stemaal hote hai jo tikaoo or saste hai.
