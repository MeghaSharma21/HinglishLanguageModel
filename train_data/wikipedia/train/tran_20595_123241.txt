 hyenan sahaayta·soochna janavaadee ganatantr china kaa sabase chota praant he. yah dakshin-purvi china main dakshini china sagar main sthit ek dweep he. puraane jamaane main yah guangdong praant kaa hissaa hua karata tha lekin 1988 main ise qareeb 200 anya chote se dwipon ke saath ek nae hyenan praant main gathit kiya gaya. san 2010 kii janaganana main hyenan kii aabaadi 86,71,518 thi. haalanki isamen 200 ke aasapaas dweep hai, is praant ke kul 33,920 varg kimi kaa 97% hyenan ke mukhya dweep main he. tulana ke liye bharat ke kerala rajya kaa kshetrafal 38,863 varg kimi he.
 china kii sarkaar ke anusaar is dweep se sudur dakshin main sthit spratli dweep-samooh or paracel dweep-samooh isi praant kaa hissaa he, lekin anya desh in dwipon ko china kaa hissaa nahin maanate. hyenan dweep par haan chini logon se bhinn ek lee naamak lok-jati rahati he jo is dweep ke mul nivasee maane jate hai. hyenan kii rajdhani hykou shahar he, jo dweep kaa sabase bada nagar bhi he.
 hyenan kaa mausam nam or garm he. janvari-farvari main ausat taapmaana 16 se 21 centegrade tak gir saktaa he lekin us se kam nahin jaataa. july-agast main yah 25 se 29 centegrade jaataa he. dweep kaa uttari bhaag zyaada garm hota he or garmiyon ke 20 sabase garm dinon main kabhi-kabhi yahan adhiktam taapmaana 35 centegrade yaa us se bhi upar chalaa jaataa he. janvari-farvari main uttari bhaag main koharaa padataa he jabaki purvi bhaag main garmiyon main jabardast chakrapati toofaan aate hai jinase baadh kii pareshaani bhi hoti he.
