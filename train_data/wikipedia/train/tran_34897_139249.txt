 urja evam sansaadhan santhaan, jo aamtaur par terry ke naam se janaa jaataa he, 1974 main sthapit, urja, paryaavaran or tikaoo vikaas ke kshetron main anusandhan gatividhiyon par kendrit nai dilli main aadhaarit shodh santhaan he.
 terry ek swatantra, laabh ke liye nahin, urja anusandhan santhaan he jo paryaavaran or sthai vikaas par kendrit, prakritik sansadhano ke kushal or tikaoo upyog karne ke liye samarpit he.
 terry kaa mul mithapur, gujarat ke ek doordaraj ke shahar main nihit he jahaan ek tata ejeeniyar, darbari seth, apane kaarkaane me alwanikaran par urja kii baari maatra main kharch ke bare main chintit tha.unhone prakritik sansadhano or urja kii kami kii kami se nipatane ke liye ek shodh santhaan banane kaa prastaav rakhaa.jearady tata, tata samooh ke adhyaksh, ne is vichaar ko pasand kiya or prastaav sweekaar kar liya.terry 3.5 crores rupee kii maamuli rakam ke saath shuru kiya gaya.tatkaaleen pradhanmantri indira gandhi ke nimantran par, terry ne 1974 main dilli main tata urja anusandhan santhaan ke rup main panjikrin kiya.samay ke saath gatividhiyon kaa dayera badhne par, iskaa naam badalakar 2003 main urja or sansaadhan santhaan kar diya gaya.
 terry ne mumbai main bombai house, tata gharaane ke mukhyalay main kaary shuru kiya tha. 1984 main, yah dilli sthanantarit ho gaya.ek ke baad ek se duusare aadhaar ghumaane ke baad jisame india international centre shaamil he, terry india habitate centre main le jaaya gaya.aaj terry kii bharat or videsh donon main kai kendron ke saath ek vaishvik upasthiti he.
 vibhinn paryaavaran or urja ke muddon se sambandhit vishyon se anusandhan peshewaron ke saath terry me lagbhag 900 karmchaaree hai. santhaan ke mahanideshak rajendra laalkrishna pachori 2007 ke nobel puraskaar sammanit jalwaayu parivartan par antar sarkari panel ke bhi adhyaksh the.terry kii kaaryakari nideshak dow leena shrivastav ko france ganraajya ke pradhan mantri dwaara sammanit kiya gaya or unhe 2007 me oredre neshnal du merite diya gaya.
 terry ek pramukh bhartiya gaiyr sarkari sanghathan, urja or paryaavaran kii shaileyon main anusandhan or vishleshan kaa aayojan karne vaala ek vaishvik santhaan he, ek vishvavidhyalay he jo sookshm jeevon se vaishvik jalwaayu parivartan or bich main sab kuch karne ke liye simaaon ke saath kaam karata he.apane astitv ke 30 varshon main, terry ne 2600 se adhik pariyojnaayen puri kii he.
 terry me koi 20 vibhaag he jisame sabase mahatvapurn he:
 terry vishvavidhyalay 19 agast 1998 ko sthapit kiya gaya tha or ek vishvavidhyalay ke rup main vishvavidhyalay anudaan ayog dwaara 1999 main manyata di gai.1998 main terry vistrut adhyayan skool ke rup main sthapit, sanstha ko baad main terry vishvavidhyalay naam diya gaya tha.terry vishvavidhyalay bharat main apni tarah kaa pehla santhaan he jo sthaayee vikaas ke liye paryaavaran, urja or prakritik vigyaan ke adhyayan ke liye samarpit he.
