 sanchaar preshak kaa praptakarta ko soochna bhejne kii prakriya he jisame jaankaree pahunchane ke liye aise madhyam kaa prayog kiya jaataa he jisase sampreshit soochna preshak or praptakarta donon samaj saken yah ek aisi prakriya he jis ke dwaara praani vibhinn madhyamon ke dwaara soochna kaa aadan pradaan kar sakate hai sanchaar kii maang he ki sabhi paksh ek samaan bhashaa kaa bodh kar saken jis kaa aadan pradaan hua ho, shravanik madhyam hai boli, gaan or kabhi kabhi awaz kaa swar evam gaiyr maukhik, shaareerik madhyam jaise kii shaareerik haav bhav, sanket boli, sam bhashaa, sparsh, netra sampark athwa lekhan kaa prayog sanchaar kii paribhaasha he - ek aisi kriya jis ke dwaara arth kaa nirupan evam sampreshan sanjhi samaj paidaa karne kaa prayaas main kiya ja sake is kriya main ankhya kushaltaaon ke rangpatal kii aavashyaktaa he antah vyaktigat or antar vyaktigat prakraman, sun avlokan, bol, poochtacha, vishleshan or moolyankanin prakriyaaon kaa upyog vikasaatmak he or jivan ke sabhi kshetron ke liye sthanantarit he : ghar, skool, saamudayik, kaam or pare.sanchaar ke dbaara hi sahyog or pushtikaran hote hansanchaaran vibhinn madhyamon dbaara sandesh bhejne kii abhivyakti he chaahe vah maukhik athwa amaukhik ho, jab tak koi vicharoddeepak vichaar sanchaarit ho bhav kriya ityadi
 sanchaar kai staron par, kai alag alag tareekon se hota he or adhiktam praaniyon ke liye, saath hi kuch mashiinon ke liye bhi .yadi samast nahin to adhiktam adhyayan ke kshetra sanchaar karne ke liye dhyaan ke ek hisse ko samarpit karte hai, isliye jab sanchaar ke bare main baat kii jaae to yah jaananaa aavashyak he ki sanchaar ke kis pahalu ke bare main baat ho rahi he. sanchaar kii paribhaashaayen shrenee vyapak hai, kuch pahachaanati hai ki pashu aapas main or manushyon se samvaad kar sakate hai or kuch simit hai evam keval maanvon ko hi maanav pratikatmak baatcheet ke maapdandon ke bheetar shaamil karte hai
