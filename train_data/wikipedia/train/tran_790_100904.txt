 
 antonio guteres portugali raajnneetijna or kootneetijna hai. guteres 1995 se 2000 tak portugal ke pradhanmantri rahe. guteres ne sanyukt raashtra ke sharnaarthi sanghathan main bhi das saal tak kaary kiya he. suraksha parishad ke matdaan main unhen spasht bahumat hasil hua. isi ke baad hi unka naam agle mahasachiv ke rup main ghoshit kiya gaya. guteres sanyukt raashtra main nawein mahasachiv hai.
 inkaa janm portugal kii rajdhani lisbon main 30 april 1949 ko hua tha. prestigius lisu de camoes naam ke madhyamik vidhyalay main inaki praarambhik shiksha hui. varsh 1971 main inhonen lisbon vishvavidhyalay se snaatak oopaadhi praapt kii. iske baad inhone assistant professor ke rup main shikshan kaary prarambh kiya.
