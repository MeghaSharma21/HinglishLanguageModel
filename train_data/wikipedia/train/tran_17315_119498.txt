 jolly elelbi subhash kapur dwaara nirdeshit bollywood kii kanuni naatak hasya film he. film main mukhya bhoomika arshad varsi, boman irani or amrutha raav kii he. film kaa natya trailor 8 janvari 2013 ko jari kiya gaya. film 15 march 2013 ko jari kii gayi. film kii kahani vakil jagadish tyaagi urf jolly ke ird-gird gumti rahati he. 
 april 2014 main 61wein rashtriya film puraskaron main jolly elelbi ko sarvshereshth hindi film kaa puraskaar mila.
 
 jagadish tyaagi urf jolly ek sangharshrat vakil he jo apane kshetra main ek bada naam bananaa chahta he. jolly meerut se dilli aata he or ek aise cese kaa hissaa ban jaataa he jise bharat kaa ek prasiddh or manhagaa vakil tejinder raajapaal lard rahaa he. raajapaal kaa muvakkil rahul dewan ek hit and ran cese main fansaa he jisamen uski gaadi ne footpath par soe kuch logon ko kuchal diya tha. raajapaal ne apane client ko bachaane ke liye police ke saath milakar sabooton ke saath chhedhkhani kii or sabit kar diya ki rahul nirdosh he. raajapaal ke bahes karne kii adaa se jolly kaaphi prabhavit hota he lekin use samaj aa jaataa he ki anyaay hua he. jolly cese phir se khulwaane ke liye jaj sunderlaal treepathi ke court main janhit yachika daayar karata he.
 film kii kahani 1999 ke sanjeev nanda ke hit-and-en cese kii ghatana se prerit he.
 
 jolly elelbi kaa sangeet krishna ne taiyaar kiya he. bappi lahidi dwaara taiyaar pehla geet 13 farvari 2013 ko jari kiya.
