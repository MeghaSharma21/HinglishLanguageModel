 bharat main bharat ke logon ke hit ke prashnon ko uthaane ke liye jan-aandolan ke rup main kai rashtravaadi aandolan chalaaye gayi. in aandolanon main logon ko swayan kaarywahi karne ke liye utsahit kiya jaataa tha yaa nivedan kiya jaataa tha. yadhyapi in aandolanon ke kaaran pratyaksh rup se bharat ko swatantrata nahin mili, kintu inase bharat ke logon main rashtrawaad bhi bhaavana bhar gayi. anek logon ne sarkari kaary choda, skool, kaarkaane or sevaayen chodi.
