 
 gopal raay aam aadmi party ke ek saamaajik kaaryakarta or rajaneta hai. vah aam aadmi party kii rashtriya kaaryakarini ke sadasyon main se ek he. gopal raay kaa janm 10 mai 1975 ko gram gobaridih, nikat sipaah ibrahimabad bazaar, thana madhuban, jila mau main ek kisaan parivaar main hua. aapane grih janapad se hi praarambhik shiksha, primary paathshaala gobaridih, medil shiksha, kisaan laghu madhyamik vidhyalay murarpur, hariskool va intermediat kii shiksha, tarun inter kalege kunda kuchari se praapt kii.
 18 janvari 1999 ko inako gardan main goli maari gayi. goli ridh kii haddi aakar funs gayi. vah jinda to bache magar gardan ke neeche kaa hissaa puri tarah niskriy ho gaya.lakhanu medical kalege main maheenon chale ilaaj ke pashchaat sudhaar kii dhimi gati ko dekhakar doctoron ne inhen ghar lejaakar eksrasij karaane kii salaah di. inhone 9 agast 2009 se 23 agast 2009 tak teesraa swadhinata andolan ke binar tale sansad bhawan ke paas dilli main jantar-mantar par 15 dinon tak anshan kiya. iske pashchaat bhrashtachar-mukt bharat ke liye prarambh hue jan lokapal aandolan main sakriy.
