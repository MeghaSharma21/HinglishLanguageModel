 najeeb mahfuz ek misri lekhak va sahityakar the jinhone san 1988 main sahitya ke liye nobel puraskaar jeeta. taufiq al-hakim ke saath, unhen 20ve shatabdi ke duusare bhaag main ubharne vaale samakaleen astitvvaadi arabi lekhakon ke sarvapratham gut main mana jaataa he. apane 70 saal ke lekhan main unhone 34 upanyaas, 350 laghukathaayen, dariinon filmon kii patakathaayen or paanch naatak likhe. unki kai kritiyon ko misri va videshi filmon main dhaala ja chukaa he.
