 aaj bhi khare hai talab, prasiddh paryaavarnavid anupam mishra dwaara likhit hindi pustak he. yah 1993 main prakashit hui thi. yah pustak paramparaagat talabon evam jal-prabandhan se sambandhit he tathaa aath varsh ke gahan kshetra-anusandhan ke pashchaat likhi gayi thi. bharat main bejod sundar talabon kii kisi bhavy parampara thi, pustak uskaa puraa darshan karaati he. talab banane kii vidhiyon ke saath-saath anupam ji kii lekhnee un gumnaam nayakon ko bhi andhere konon se found laati he, jo vikaas ke nae paimaanon ke kaaran bisraa die gaye hai. 
 anupam ji ne apni pustakon ko copirite-mukt rakhaa he. isi kaaran “aaj bhi khare hai talab” pustak kaa ab tak braille lipi sahit 19 bhashaaon main anuwaad kiya ja chukaa he. saamaajik pustakon main mahatma gandhi kii pustak “maay experiments wyeth truth” ke baad keval yahi ek pustak braille lipi main uplabdh he.
