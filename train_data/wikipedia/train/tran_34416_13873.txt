 engri bards ek america angrezi film he. iskaa nirmaan jon kohen or kethrin vriden ne kiya he.
 finnish ke video game banane vaali company rovio ne ek samachar main bataaya kii vah soni ke saath mil kar is par ek film banane vaali he. iske liye marwel main kaary karne vaale purv nirmaataa david micel se bhi baat kii he. yah pehle irun main or thor jaise kirdaar ke liye film kaa nirmaan kar chuke hai. iske bare main studio ne aadhikarik rup se 29 sitambar 2014 main bataaya. iske baad 1 oktober ko is film kii chhavi dikhaai gai.
