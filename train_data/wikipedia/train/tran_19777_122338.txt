 akram siddhanth, ganit kii ek shakha he jo un gatik nikaayon par apaana dhyaan kendrit karti he jinka vyavhaar unke aarmabhik dasha par bahut adhik nirbhar karata he. vastav main aise bahut se nikaay hai jinamein avyavashtha hi avyavashtha dikhti he kintu ve random nikaay nahin hote balki atyadhik araikhik akrami nikaay hote hai.
