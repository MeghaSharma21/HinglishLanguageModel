 lava tub ek prakritik rup se bani hui pipe, tub yaa surang kii akruti kaa dhaancha hota he jo lava bahaav se ban jaataa he. yah kisi lava nahar ki sataha par thande hue lava ke jamkar thos ho jane ke baad uske neeche lagaataar lava behate rahane se nirmit hota he. lava tuben sakriya ho sakati hai, yani kisi jwalamukhi ke visphoton ke samay us se nikal rahe lava ko bahane kaa sthaan detii hai yaa mrit ho sakati hai, yani lava bahaav bahut kaal se samaapt ho chukaa he or ve thandi hokar ek lambi gufa-jaisi surangen ban gai hai.
 vishwa ke kai sthanon par mrit lava tubon main log saiyr karne jate hai or ve paryatak sthalon ke rup main jaani jaati hai.
