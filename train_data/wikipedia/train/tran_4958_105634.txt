 chitrakoot zila aids niyantran socity uttar pradesh ke zila chitrakoot main aids rokthaam kaa prayaas karti he.
 chitrakoot zila aids niyantran socity kii bhoomika
 chitrakoot zila aids niyantran socity ek sakriy bhoomika nibhaa rahi he. socity dwaara samay-samay jaagrukta kaarykram yojit hote rahe hai or isi shrinkhalaa main vishwa aids diwas 2011 par swasthya vibhaag sahit anek sansthaanon main kaarykram yojit kiye gaye. jila aids niyantran socity ke tatwadhan main siaisi se mahila jila chikitsaalay tak aids jaagrukta raily nikaali gai. jila chikitsaalay main sangoshtee yojit kii gai. jila chikitsaalay main aids vishay par sangoshtee kaa aayojan kiya gaya.
