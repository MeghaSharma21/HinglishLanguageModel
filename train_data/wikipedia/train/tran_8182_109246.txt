 shab-e-qadra muslim samudaay dwaara ramadan ke pavitra maheene kii ek raat ko kehete hai. us raaj visheshta muslim manyata ke anusaar quran kii aayaton kaa prithvi par jibareel naam ke farishte ke zarie paigambar mohammad par nazil hona shuru hua tha. yah raaj aam taur se 27 ramadan maani jaati he jisamen musalmaan jaagate hai or apane paapon ke liye allaah se kshamaa maangate hai.
