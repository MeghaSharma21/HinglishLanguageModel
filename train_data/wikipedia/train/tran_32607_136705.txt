  congress party main ve apni spashtvaadita ke liye vishesh rup se jane jate hai. 1989 main ve varanasi se lok sabha ke saansad chune gayi or bharat sarkaar ke vitt mantralay main mantri bhi rahe.
 vartmaan samay main ve congress kaaryakarini ke vishisht aamantrit sadsy hai. 
 anil shastri kaa janm lakhanu main laal bahadur shastri or lalita shastri ke yahan hua. unhone dilli ke saint columbia skool or saint stephen college se shiksha grahan kii. baad main unhone astris bijiness skool you0ke0 se aidvance management program kaa course bhi kiya.
 unka vivaah manju shrivastav se 24 farvari 1973 ko hua jinase unke teen bete hai - adarsh, lagan or mudit. do bete gudgaanv main sarvice karte hai tathaa sabase chota mumbai main ek retail shop kaa kaam dekhtaa he. 
 swayan anil shastri ne bhi snaatak karne ke baad puure satraa saal ek corporate offis main kaam kiya. vahaan se tyaagptr dekar ve raajnneeti main aa gayi..ek baar congress ke tikat par loksabhaa kaa chunaav jeeta or do saal tak mantri bhi rahe. is samay ve apane parivaar sahit noida main sthaayee rup se rah rahe hai.
 prabandhan ke kshetra main ucchtar shiksha pradaan karne hetu unki ahm bhoomika rahi he. anil shastri apane pita ke naam par simit sadhano se shuru kiye gayi bahadur shastri institute of management dilli ke chairman tathaa bahadur shastri institute of management and technology bareli ke adhyaksh hai. iske atirikt bahadur shastri neshnal memorial trust ke trustiyon main bhi unka naam shaamil hai. yah trust kai saamaajik va saanskrithik gatibidhiyon ko sanchalit karata he.
