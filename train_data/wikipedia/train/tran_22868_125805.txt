 nadi jodo pariyojna ek bade paimaane par prastaavit civil ejineering pariyojna he, jiskaa uddeshy bhartiya nadiyon ko jalaashyon or naharon ke madhyam se aapas main jodna he. jisase bharat ke kuch hisson main lagaataar baadh yaa pani kii kami kii samasya ko dur kiya ja saktaa he.
 is pariyojna ko teen bhaagon main vibhajit kiya gaya he: uttar himalayan nadi jodo ghatak; dakshini prayadwipiya ghatak or 2005 se shuru, antarraji nadi jodo ghatak. pariyojna ko jal sansaadhan mantralay ke antargat bharat ke rashtriya jal vikaas praadhikaran dwaara prabandhit kiya ja rahaa he. endabludie ne himalayan ghatak kii 14 pariyojnaon, prayadwipiya ghatak kii 16 pariyojnaayen, or 37 antarraji nadi jodne pariyojnaon par adhyayan kar report taiyaar kiya he.
 bharat main ausat varsha kareeb 4,000 arab ghan meter he, lekin bharat kii adhikansh varsha 4 maheene kii avadhi - june se sitambar ke bich hoti he. halaaki, desh bhar main varshan samaan nahin he, purv or uttari main adhik barish hoti he, jabaki pashchim or dakshin main kam barish hoti he. bharat main kai baar atirikt monsoon or baadh ke varsh, or iske baad ausat yaa der se monsoon ke saath sookhaa ki sthiti bhi dekhne ko milti he. is bhugolic or samay ke vicharan main prakritik pani kii uplabdhta banaam sinchai, peya or audyogic gatividhi hetu jal kii vaarshik maang ke bich, maang-aapurti kii ek bady khai bani hui he, joki bharat kii badhati aabaadi ke saath or bigadate ja rahi he.
