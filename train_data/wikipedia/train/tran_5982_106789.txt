 pisi kii bhautik suraksha kaa vichaar karnaa, suraksha kaa pehla charan he. bhautik suraksha kaa rakrakhaav bajet or sthanvishesh par nirbhar karata he. computer ko bhautik suraksha pradaan karanevaali kuch padhdatiyaan hai:
 BIOS password kii setting karne se anadhikrut yuzars dwaara system kaa durupyog or ribooting nahin kiya ja saktaa. yah ek nimna stareey suraksha pradaan karata he kyonki koi bhi batterij ko discnect kar ke utpaadakon ke difolt passwords ke saath BIOS ko axes kar saktaa he. parantu anadhikrut yuzars ke dwaara cese ko kholkar BIOS ko axes karne main thodaa samay lagata he jo utpaati hastkshep ke chinh chhod jaataa he.
