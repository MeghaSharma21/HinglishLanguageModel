 sanganak vigyaan ke sandarbh main mridu sanganan kaa arth sanganan kii drushti se kathin evam jatil samasyaon kaa aisa hal dena jo purnatah thik nahin ho kintu saral ho. mridu sanganan kaa pradarsh maanav mastishk ke kaary karne ke dhang se miltaa-julata he. 
 'mridu sanganan' kaa aabirbhaav 1990 ke baad hua. iske purv ke sanganan kaa drushtikon purnatah thik-thik samaadhaan dene kaa tha jo keval saral samasyaon ke liye hi sambhav ho paata tha. kintu jeevigyaan, ayurvigyaan, maanavshaastra, prabandhan evam isi tarah ke anekanek kshetron main aane vaali samasyaon ke liye inamen koi samaadhaan nahin tha. mridu sanganan kaa uddeshy aise hi jatil, aspasht, anishchit, aanshik satyata vaali samasyaon kaa kam mulya main motaa kintu upyogi hal pradaan karata he.
 faji logic, neural computing, genetic computing, probeblistic rijning paraspar virdohi nahin hai balki poorak hai. udhaaharan ke liye aajkal 'neurophaji pranali' naamak paddati bahut prachlit ho rahi he.
