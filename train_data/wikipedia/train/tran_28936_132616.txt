 
 chawlon ke kheton main panpane vaale machharon se . yah machhar japani enciphelitis virus se sankramit ho jate hai . japani enciphelitis virus se sankramit machharon ke kaatane se hota he. japani encephelitis virus se sankrant paaltu boar or junglee pakshiyon ke kaatane par machhar sankrant ho jate he. iske baad sankrant machhar poshan ke dauraan japani encephelitis virus kaatane par maanav or jaanwaron main jate hai. japani encephelitis virus paaltu boar or junglee pakshiyon ke rakt pranali main parivardhit hote hai. japani encephelitis ke virus kaa sankraman ek vyakti se duusare vyakti main nahin hota he. udaarahan ke liye aapako yah virus kisi us vyakti ko chune yaa chuumane se nahin aa saktaa jise yah rog he yaa kisi swasthya seva karmchaaree se jisane kisi is prakaar ke rogi kaa upachaar kiya ho. keval paaltu boar or junglee pakshi hi japani encephelitis virus failaa sakate hai.
 sir dard ke saath bukhaar ko chhodkar halke sankraman main or koi pratyaksh lakshan nahin hota he. gambhir prakaar ke sankraman main sirdard, tej bukhaar, gardan main akadan, ghabrahat, coma main chale janaa, kanpakanpin, kabhi-kabhi anthan or mastishk niskriy, pakshaaghaat hota he. japani encephelitis kii sanchayi kaalvadhi saamanytah 5 se 15 din hoti he. isakimrityudar 0.3 se 60 pratishat tak he.
 isaki koi vishesh chikitsa nahin he. gahan sahaayak chikitsa kii jaati he. yah rog alag alag deshon main alag alag samay par hota he.kshetra vishesh ke hisaab se gramin kshetron main rahane vaale, vahaan pratiniyukt sakriy duty vaale sainik or gramin kshetron main ghoomane waalon ko yah beemaari adhik hoti he. shahari kshetron main japani encephelitis saamanytah nahin hota he. japani encephelitis ke liye bharat main niskriy moosak medha vyutpann japani encephelitis tikaa uplabdh he.
