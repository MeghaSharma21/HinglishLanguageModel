 bhartiya bhashaa kendra kii shuruuaat 29 oktober 1974 ko hui. is kendra main hindi, urdu, tamil, hindi anuwaad ke adhyayan kii vyavastha he. nikat bhavishya main bangala, marathi, asamiya aadi bhashaaon kaa adhyayan armbh hone kii ummid he. kendra kii academik samskruti ke adarsh katha samraat premchand hai. unki bhashaa or sahitya ko aadhaar banane ke kaaran hi yah desh kaa sambhavat: akelaa kendra he jahaan hindi or urdu kii padhai saath-saath hoti he. premchand ke isi mahatv ko dhyaan main rakhte hue kendra ne is baar se har saal premchand ke janm diwas 31 july ko ‘premchand memorial lacture’ yojit kar apana academik satr shuru karne kaa faisalaa liya he. is kram main pehla vyaakhyaan urdu ke prakhyat aalochak pro mohammad hasan dwaara 31 july 2008 ko diya gaya. samaaroh kii adhyakshata hindi ke prakhyat aalochak pro namvar singh ne kii. ullekhneey he ki pro hasan or pro namvar singh bhartiya bhashaa kendra kii aadharshila rakhane waalon main se hai.
