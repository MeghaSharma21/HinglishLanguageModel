 dharaan nepal ke kosi jile kaa ek pramukh nagar he. purvi nepal ke pahar or taraai ke bich sthit yah nagar shiksha tathaa swasthya kaa pramukh kendra he. yahan ke vi pi koirala shwasth bigyaan pratisthan, purvanchal ingeniyarine kyampus, khadhya pravidhi kyampus, mahendra kyampus jaise bahut se ucch shiksha santhaan ke saath saath bahut se aspataal bhi yaanha pe he yah sahar nepal ke pramukh mahendra rajmarg se bis keelometre uttar ki duri pe he. dhankuta va paahadi kshetra jane vaale pramukh rajmarg isi sahar se hokar jaataa he.
