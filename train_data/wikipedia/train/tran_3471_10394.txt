 ramsevak shankar ek raajnneetijna hai or 1988-1990 ke dauraan surinam ke raashtrapati rahe. sattarudh sainya shashan dwaara nae samvidhan apanaae jane ke baad 1987 main hue sansadeey chunaavon ke baad rashtriya vidhansabha dwaara unhen raashtrapati chunaa gaya. ramsevak shankar bhootpurv krishi mantri the or ramadat misier ke baad surinam ke raashtrapati bane.
 1989 main ve maroun yaa bush nigro lohon ko kshamaadaan karne ke liye sahamat hue jisase is baat kii sambhaavna thi kii ve varshawanon main vicharan karte hue apane hathiyaar rakh denge. boujersi or rashtriya loktantric dal ne is samjhaute kaa yah kehete hue virodh kiya kii yah samvidhan kaa ulanghan he or yah kadam ek swatantra sainya shakti ko vaid karne vaala hoga.
 1990 main boujersi dwaara kiye gaye ek raktahin sainya takhtapalat ke baad, ek sena samarthit sarkaar sthpit kii gai or johan kraag ko nayaa raashtrapati banaaya gaya.
