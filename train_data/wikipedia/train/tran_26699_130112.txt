 takaar afghanistan kaa ek praant he jo us desh ke uttar-pashchim main sthit he. is praant kaa kshetrafal 12,333 varg kimi he or isaki aabaadi san 2009 main lagbhag 8.9 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. is praant kii rajdhani taloqaan shahar he. yahan ke adhiktar log farsi bolne vaale tajik or uzbek bhashaa bolne vaale uzbek log hai. yah praant apane namak ke kaanon ke liye mashhoor he jahaan se khodakar hazaron saalon se namak nikaala ja rahaa he.
 'takaar' main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
