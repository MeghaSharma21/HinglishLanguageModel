 bharat main sanskrit tathaa anya bhashaaon main anek mahakavyon kii rachana hui he. bharat ke mahakavyon main valmiki ramayana, vyas dwaipayan rachit mahabharat, tulsidasarchit ramcharitamanas, aadi granth parmukh hai.
 hindi main prithviraj raso, padmavat, ramcharita maanas, ramchandrika, saket, saket sant, kamayani, gandhi parayan, daityavansh, haldighati, kurushetra, aryaavart, noorjahan, subhaashachandr bose, vivek shri, bhagatsingh aadi 12 mahakavy, kunal, vardhaman ne udaatt parampara ko gatimayata pradaan kii he.
 samay, ruchi tathaa kriyasheeltaa ke abhaav ke baad bhi hindi sahitya main mahakavy lekhan kii parampara gati tathaa vistaar paa rahi he. is dashak ke mahakavyon main dow kabra kii 3 kritiyon ke atirikt purushottam shriram charit va shrikrishna charit, swayan dharitri hi thi, vaigyanik aadhyatm va prakruti or purush, devayani, sutaputra va mahamatya, ratnaja va bhoomijaa, veeravar tathya tope, virangana durgavati, kshatraani durgavati, dadhichi, maanav, khajuraho aadi ne mahakavy prasaad ko jeevantta pradaan kii he. 
 hindi mahakavy srijan kii parampara main mahakavy ke 3 tatv kathavstu, nayak tathaa ras maanya hai. kathavstu ke 3 ang vistaar, vishalata va chand vaividhya, nayak ke 3 ang gun dhirodattata, shaleenta, praasangikta tathaa ras ke 3 gun bhashaa-style, alankaar tathaa bhavanubhav hai.
