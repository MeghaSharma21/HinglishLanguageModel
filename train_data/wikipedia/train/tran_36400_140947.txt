 arthath sabhi sambandhiyon, jaanwaron, sadhu, devi-devataaon, rahgeeron, braman, raja, chor-chakaar, bhikhaari aadi sabhi 36 komon ke liye anaaj maangata he or bache anaaj se gharavaale kaam chalaate hai.
