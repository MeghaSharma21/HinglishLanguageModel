 bani hassaan arabi mul kaa ek samooh he, jo maakil arab janajatiyon kii chaar up-janajatiyon main se ek he, jo 11 veen shatabdi main bani hilaal or banu sulem janajatiyon ke saath magareb main aae the..
 17 veen shatabdi ke chaar boba yuddh ke baad benny hasan or anya yoddha arab janajatiyon ne kshetra ke sanjaja barbar janajatiyon par haaavee rahi. natijatan, arab samskruti or bhashaa par haaavee ho gai, or barbar janajatiyon main kuch arbakaran hua. arabi ke bani hasan boli ko is kshetra main istemaal kiya gaya or abhi bhi hasania arabi ke rup main boli jaati he.
