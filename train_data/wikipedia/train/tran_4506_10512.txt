 guthlidaar fal aise fal ko kehete hai jisamen bahari chhilake or goode ke andar ek sakht guthli ho jiske andar fal kaa beej ho. coffee, aam, baadaam, aadu, aalubaaloo, pista, khubaani or aalu-bukhaaraa kuch guthli vaale fal hai.
 "guthlidaar falon" ko angrezi main "drup" kehete hai.
 guthliyaan aksar aise falon main hoti hai jo bahut mithe hon or jinke rang bahut ronaki or aakarshak hon. jaanavar inhen aksar khate hai or yaa to guthli fank dete hai yaa nigal jate hai. nigali hui guthliyaan jaanavar ke fal hazam kar lene ke baad unki beet main kahin dur gir jaati hai. is se ped apni santanon ko dur-dur tak failaa saktaa he.
