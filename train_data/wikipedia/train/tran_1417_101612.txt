 karaina khagoleey gole ke dakshini bhaag main dikhne vaala ek taramandal he. yah kabhi argo nauka taramandal main shaamil hota tha lekin ab vah taramandal teen hisson main baant diya gaya he. raat ke aasmaan kaa doosraa sab se roshan tara, agasti, isamen shaamil he.
 angrezi main karaina taramandal ko "karaina constlation" likhaa jaataa he. karaina latini bhashaa kaa lafz he or iskaa arth he "kishti kaa penda ". farsi main ise "shahtakhta" or arabi main ise "al-qaayada" kaha jaataa he - in donon kaa arth "aadhaar" hi hota he.
 ratri ke akaash main akashganga kaa failaav is taramandal se guzarataa he isliye isamen bahut se khule taraguchh dekhe ja sakate hai. har saal akaash main karaina ke kshetra main eta carinids naam kii ulkapindon kii bauchhaarein dekhi ja sakati hai jo sab se zyaada janvari 21 ke aasapaas hoti hai.
