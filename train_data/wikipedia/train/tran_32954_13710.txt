 savitru, rugveda main varnit saura devtaa hai. gayatri mantra kaa sambandh savitru se hi mana jaataa he. savita shabd kii nishpatti 'su' dhaatu se hui he jiskaa arth he - utpann karnaa, gati dena tathaa prerana dena. savitru dev kaa surya devtaa se bahut samya he.
 savita kaa swaroop alokamay tathaa svarnim he. isiilie ise swarnanetra, swarnahast, swarnapaad, evam swarna jivy kii sangya di gai he. uskaa rath swarna kii abha se yukt he jise doya adhik laal ghode khinchate he, is rath par baithkar vah sampoorn vishwa main bhraman karata he. ise asur naam se bhi sambodhit kiya jaataa he. pradosh tathaa pratyush donon se iskaa sambandh he. harikesh, ayohanu, ampanpaat, karmkratu, satyasoonu, sumrilik, sunith aadi iske visheshan hai.
 rugveda ke 11 sookhton main savitru kaa naam aata he. iske atirikt anekon mantron main savitru kaa naam aata he. kul milakar 170 baar savitru kaa naam aayaa he. udai ke purv surya ko savitru kehete hai, jabaki udai ke pashchaat 'surya'.
