 drakshakrishi angoor se sambandhit udyaan vigyaan kii shakha he. isamen angooron kii boai, poshan, dekh-rekh or fal utpaadan kaa adhyayan kara jaataa he. isamen drakshashetra kaa prabandhan bhi shaamil he, jisamen rogon va nashijivon kaa rokthaam, urvarak kaa prayog, sinchai, falon kaa vikaas, fal todane ke samay kaa nirdhaaran or sardiyon main shaakhon kii kataai sammilit hai. haalaa ke utpaadan main yah vigyaan bahut mahatvapurn he.
