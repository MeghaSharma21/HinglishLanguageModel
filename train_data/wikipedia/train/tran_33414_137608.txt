 1984 ke khelon ke baad se greeshmakaleen olympic main sincranaise tairaaki kaa ek aayojan rahaa he vartmaan olympic kaarykram yugal or team kii ghatnaon main pratispardhaa he, lekin pichley khelon main, ek ekal kaarykram bhi tha. sanyukt rajya america, canada or japan paaramparik rup se is khel main sabase majboot raashtra hai, jo 1984 se 1996 tak olympic padak jeet rahe hai, lekin rus ne haal hi main varchasv sthapit kiya he, 2000 or 2016 ke bich har ghatana jeetne ke liye.
