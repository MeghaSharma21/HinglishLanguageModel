 main in blike 3' 2012 main bani america 3 di kaalpanik vigyaan par aadhaarit hasya film he jisame vil smith or tommy lee jones mukhya bhoomika main he. yah film 2002 main bani main in blike II kaa aglaa bhaag he jise 25 mai 2012 ko pichhali film ke das varshon baad va pehali film main in blike se pandraa varshon baad release kiya gaya he. film main josh brolin, emma thompsan. elis ive or jemene klement anya bhoomikaaon main he or inke saath bairi sonanfild nirdeshak va steven spilberg nirmaataa ke rup main punah laute he.
 antarabrahmand gunehagaar boris da animal chandramaa par sthit lunarmax jail se bhaag nikalataa he or samay main waapas ja kar easant ke ko maarane kii koshish karata he jisane 26 july 1969 ko uskaa ek haath uda kar use giraftaar kiya tha. new york shahar main hui ek antarikshayan kii durghatana va ek chini restaraan main hui hathapayee se ke yah samaj jaataa he kii boris bhaag niklaa he or is baat par pachhataata he kii usne use pehle kii kyon nahin maar diya. jab vah apane ghar lautataa he to uske saare naamo nishaan mit jate he. easant je akelaa insaan he jise ke ab bhi yaad he haalanki main in blike ke mukhyalay main kisi ko bhi ke yaad nahin.
 easant o jo zed kii mrityu ke pashchyaat emibi kii sachiv he, je ke kathanon se yah nishkarsh nikaalati he kii kii samay main kuch gadabed hui he. donon ko pataa chalata he kii boris 1969 ke samay main bhaag niklaa he or usne ke kii hatya kar di jisase prithvi par uski jaati ne hamalaa kar diya kyonki prithvi kaa suraksha kavach archnet ke ne 1969 main chaalu kiya tha, jo uski mrityu ke karan sthapit nahin ho paaya. ek dukaan ke maalik jeffri price kii madad se je chrisler building se kud kar samay yatra karata he. uske paar keval 24 ghante he taaki vah boris ko roak sake or vah ke kii hatya ke ek din pehle pahunch jaataa he. tab agent ja ko pataa chalata he kii uske pita ek captain the.
