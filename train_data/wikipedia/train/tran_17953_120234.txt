 seti pratham navin samrajya ke unnisven vansh kaa doosraa firo tha or rameces pratham kaa putr tha or rameces mahaan kaa pita tha. uske shashan kaal par kai itihaaskaaron kii alag alag raay he. tolamac kaal ke itihaaskaar manetho ne seti ko 55 varsh kaa shashan kaal diya he jabaki aaj ke kai itihaaskaar 9 varsh or 11 varsh ko sahi maanate hai. madhyadhara ke itihaaskaaron ke anusaar usne misr pe 11 varsh tak raaj kiya.
 'seti' kaa arth he "devtaa set kaa" jo yah sanket karata he ki vah set kaa poojak tha.seti kaa asli naam tha "seti merenpitaah" jiskaa arth tha "devtaa set kaa upaask ,devtaa pitaah kaa priy".
 atharahawein vansh ke patan ke saath hi misr kaa patan hona shur ho gaya tha, tab atharahave vansh ke antim firo horemheb ne apane amaatya rameces pratham ko apana uttaradhikari niyukt kiya. rameces pratham ne keval 17 maheene hi raaj kiya or phir seti ne raaj kiya. seti ne kai yuddh lade or misr ko dubaara shaktishaalee banaaya. usne hittite samrajya se misr ke khoye hue praant apane adheen kiye or kadesh par vijay bhi pai. seti ke antim dinon main kadesh ke saath anya kuch praant dubaara hittite samrajya ke adheen ho gaye jise ek or baar phir seti ke putr rameces dwitiya ne misr ke adheen kiya. ek safal or shaktishaalee shaasha hone ke baavjood seti pratham gumnami main rahaa kuki uske putr rameces dwitiya kii prasiddhi usase kai zyada thi.
