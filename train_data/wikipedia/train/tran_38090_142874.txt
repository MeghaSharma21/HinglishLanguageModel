 vidyut chumbakeey vikiran shunya evam anya madhyamon se swayan-prasaarit tarang hoti he. ise prakaash bhi kaha jaataa he kintu vastav main prakaash, vidyutchumbakeey vikiran kaa ek chota sa bhaag he. drushya prakaash, eks-kiran, gamma-kiran, radio tarange aadi sabhi vidyutchumbakeey tarange hai.
 yah ek anuprastha tarang he. 
 vidyutchumbakeey vikiran kaa vargeekaran aavriti ke aadhaar par hota he ; kyonki aavruti ke aadhaar par inke kuch gun prabhavit hote hai. aavruti ke aadhaar par nimna prakaar ke varg hote hai :-
