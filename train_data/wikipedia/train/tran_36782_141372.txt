 di si avanti ek aagaamee bhartiya sports car he. yah bharat main nirmit pehali purna swadeshi sports car he jise auto expo 2012 main pehali baar amitabh bachchan dwaara anavarit kiya gaya tha. ise bharat ke jane maane diziner dilip chhabdiya ke di si dizine ne banaaya he. iskaa naam tin tin comics kii paatr stadbekar avanti ke naam par rakhaa gaya he. italian bhashaa main iskaa arth he "aage badhnaa". iskaa uddeshy bharat main aisi sports kaaron kaa nirmaan karnaa he jinhen janata vaazib daamon par kharid sake.yah ab bharat kii sadako par 2015 me utaari gayi.
 avanti kaa ingine ford dwaara nirmit aadhunik ichoboost tantragyan se bana he. yah 2.0 litre kshamta kaa DOHC chaar silendar vaala ingine he jisamen direct injection, har silendar par chaar volve ke saath veriable volve timming va terbo charging maujuud he. iskaa power output 261bhp he or yah 366Nm kaa tork utpann karata he. aage chalakar isamen honda kaa V6 ingine bhi uplabdh karaayaa jayega jo 394bhp kaa power output pradaan karata he. ingine euro-V pradooshan maapdandon par khara utarataa he or thode badlaav ke baad euro-VI ke kaabil banaaya ja saktaa he.
 isamen chah gear vaala maanviya gearbox he or technology ke shaukeen logon ke liye 6 speed dual klach gearbox bhi diya gaya he. yah shunya se 100 kilometre/ghante kii raftaar saat second me chhu sakati he jiskaa shrey iske halake vazan ko jaataa he. isaki ucchtam gati seemaa 250 kilometre/ghante tak he. iske saath hi car main abs va airbags pehle se hi die gaye he.
 bharat kii pehali sports car avanti kaa anavaran 24 sitambar 2015 ko kiya gaya. isaki keemat 35.93 lakh he.
