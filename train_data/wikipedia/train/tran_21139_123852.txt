 junius richard jayavardhane, 4 farvari, 1978 se 2 janvari, 1989 tak srilanka ke raashtrapati the.
 ju.ri. jayavardhane 1940 se ceylone kii raajnneeti main sakriy hue. ve sanyukt rashtriya dal ) ke ek sansthaapak sadsy the or 1973 main ve yuanpi ke pramukh bane. july 1977 main yuanpi ne chunaav jeeta or jayavardhane desh ke pradhanmantri chune gaye.
 apane karyaalay kii avadhi ke dauraan dashakon se jari tamil alpsankhyakon ke saath jari sangharsh he or tej ho gaya kyonki unhone bhi srilankai sarkaar kii usee niti ko jari rakhaa jisamen samvidhan main tamilon ko barbari kaa adhikaar die jane ke praavdhaanon ke pashchaat bhi unke saath bhedbhaav kiya jaataa rahaa. vibhinn tamil dalon ko jayavardhane ne ek hi sur main pruthakatavaadi ghoshit kar diya jisase unke kaarykaal ke dauraan tamil asantosh main or vruddhi hui. saath hi saath unhone sabhi tamil dalon ko shatru ghoshit karke unki maangon ko taak par rakh diya. iskaa parinaam yah hua kii tamil asantosh ko sashastr sangharsh kaa aarra lenaa padaa or srilanka main ek raktranjit grihayuddh armbh ho gaya.
 raashtrapati jayavardhane ne america raashtrapati ronaald reagan or america logon ko hathi kaa ek baccha bhent swaroop diya.
 1978 main jayavardhane main sanvaidhanik sudhaar jari rakhe or srilanka ko raashtrapati ganatantr banaaya. tab jayavardhane desh ke raashtrapati bane or swayan ko "rashtrapita" ghoshit kiya. unhone apne kareebi ranasinghe ko sarkaar kaa pramukh banaaya.
 jayavardhane kii mrityu 90 varsh kii aayu main 1996 main prakritik kaarnon se hui.
