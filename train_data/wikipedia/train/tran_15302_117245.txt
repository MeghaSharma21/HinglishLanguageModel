 id gaah yaa idgah : dakshin asia main upyogit islaami samskruti kaa ek shabd he. id ul-fitr or id al-azaha ke parvon ke avasar par, gaanv ke baahar, samuhik prarthanaaon ke liye upyog kiye jane vaala sthal yaa maidan. khaas taur par ramadan or baqar id ke mauqon par yahaan namaaz padhi jaati he, jise id kii namaaz bhi kaha jaataa he. saadhaaran taur par jahaan rozaana paanch vaqt kii namaaz padhi jaati he us sthal ko masajid kehete hai 
 islaami parampara ke anusaar yah mana jaataa he ki hazarat mohammad ne id kii namaaz adaa kii thi, is liye is namaaz ko id gaah par adaa karnaa sunnat mana jaataa he.
