 moth ek prakaar kaa dalhan hota he. isse daal milti he. yah calcium, phosphorus, carbohydrate va vitaminon se yukt tathaa krimi naashak va fever naashak hoti he. 
 moth, moong kii tarah kaa ek prakaar kaa motaa ann he jo vanamoong bhi kaha jaataa he. yah praayah saare bharat main hota he. isaki boai greeshm ritu ke ant yaa varsha ke aarambh main or kataai kharif kii fasal ke saath jaade ke aarambh main hoti he. yah bahut hi saadhaaran koti kii bhoomi main bhi bahut acchhee tarah hota he or praayah bajre ke saath boyaa jaataa he. adhik varsha se yah kharaab ho jaataa he. isaki faliyon main jo dane nikalate hai, unki daal banati he. yah daal saadhaaran daalon kii bhanti khai jaati he or mandagni athwa fever main pathya kii bhanti bhi di jaati he. vaidyak main ise garam, kastyli, madhur, sheethal, malrodhak, pathya, ruchikari, halaki, baadi, krimijanak, tathaa raktpitta, kaph, vaav, gudkil, vayugole, fever, dah or kshayrog kii naashak mana he. isaki jad maadak or vifailli hoti he.
