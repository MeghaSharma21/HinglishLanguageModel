 ek hiran ek servide parivaar kaa ruminant stanpayi jantu he.
 hiran Cervidae parivaar ke sadsy hai. ek maadaa hiran ek harini kaha jaataa he. ek purush ek hiran kaha jaataa he. hiran kii prajatiyon main se kai kismein hai. hiran duniya bhar ke kai mahadwipon par paae jate hai. yah standhari prajatiyon aamtaur par van nivas main rah paaya he. baks karne ke liye sing he karte hai. do sing kaa adhikari nahin karte hai. hiran bhi khuron ke adhikari.
