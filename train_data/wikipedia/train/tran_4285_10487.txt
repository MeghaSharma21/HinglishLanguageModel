 cryogenic ingine-7.5 ek 'cryogenic ingine he. jise bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan ne jieselvi mark-2 ke upree charan ke liye viksit kiya gaya he. yah ingine cryogenic upree charan pariyojna ke tahat viksit kiya gaya he. iskaa prayog rus kaa cryogenic ingine kevidi-1 ko swadeshi cryogenic ingine se badalane kaa tha. jieselvi mark-1 main rus ke cryogenic ingine kevidi-1 kaa prayog hota tha. lekin jieselvi mark-2 sanskaran main swadeshi cryogenic ingine kaa prayog kiya gaya.
