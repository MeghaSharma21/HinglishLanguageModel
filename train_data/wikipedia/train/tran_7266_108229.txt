 hanoot singh rathod bhartiya sena ke bhootpurv leftinent general the. 1971 ke bangladesh mukti sangram main unki bhoomika ke liye unhen mahavir chakra pradaan kiya gaya tha. 
 hanoot singh kii aguwai main puna horse regiment ne varsh 1965 tathaa 1971 ke bharat-paak yuddh main pakistan ke 48 tanks nasht kar die the jiske baad paak sena ke saamne haar sweekaar karne ke atirikt koi vikalp nahin bachaa.
 le. general hanoot singh kaa janm 6 july 1933 ko le. karnal arjuna singh ke ghar hua tha. vah desh ke purv videsh evam rakshaamantri jaswant singh ke chachere bhai the. dehradun ke karnan brown skool se padhai puri karne ke baad vah 1949 main endie main daakhil hue. yahaan vah sacond leftinent pad par niyukt hue. iske baad vah seedhi dar seedhi tarakkee karte rahe.
 swatantra bharat ke 12 pramukh generala main shaamil or bhartiya faaj main general hanoot ke naam se vikhyath general singh antim samay main dehradun main rah rahe the. ve yahaan zyaadaatar samay aadhyatmik sadhana main leen rahe.
