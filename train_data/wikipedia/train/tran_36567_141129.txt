 namakan prakriya :
 hindu dharm ke anusaar srishtikarta brahma hote hai. inaki aayu inhen ke sau varshon ke baraabar hoti he. inaki aayu 1000 mahayugon ke baraabar hoti he. vishnu puraana ke anusaar kaal-gananaa vibhaag, vishnu puraana bhaag 1, tatiya adhyay ke anusaar:
 
 brahma kaa ek diwas 10,000 bhaagon main banta hota he, jise charan kehete hai: 
 
 yah chakra aise doharaata rahata he, ki brahma ke ek diwas main 1000 mahayug ho jate hai
