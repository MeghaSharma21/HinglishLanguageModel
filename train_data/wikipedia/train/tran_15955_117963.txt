 bihar rajya ke begusarai jile kaa ek gaanv he. yah begusarai jila mukhyalay se pantis kilometre uttar-purv main sthit he. 
 iske purab main shashan, pashchim main dihi, uttar main coraiya or dakshin main dharmpur gaanv sthit he.
 sujanpur kii aabaadi kareeb das hajaar he.
 sujanpur kii arthvyavastha mul rup se krishi aadhaarit he. is gaanv ke zyaadaatar purush duusare rajyon main rahete hai or vahaan mazdooree karte hai.
