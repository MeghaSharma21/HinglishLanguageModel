 banavaarilal sharma ek prasiddh gandhivaadi, jane-maane ganitagya, purv professor evam vibhaagadhyaksh - ganit, elahabaad vishvavidhyalay tathaa aazaadee bachaao andolan ke rashtriya sanyojak the. da0 sharma kaa janm bharat ke uttar pradesh rajya ke jila agra main tundla ke paas banthal gaanv main hua tha. unki praathmik shiksha gaanv main tathaa aage kii shiksha elahabaad main hui. da0 sharma ne paris univarsity se doctorat kii oopaadhi grahan kii or anterrashtriya ganit parishad ke 6 varsh tak adhyaksh bhi rahe. ve aazaadee bachaao aandolan ke prakhar vaktaa the, jo desh bhar main bhraman karte the or vibhinn aarthik-saamaajik vishyon par bhaashan dete the. iske atirikt ve aam logon evam vidhyaarthiyon ke saath milakar bahurashtri companiyon ke viruddh aandolan evam virodh pradarshan bhi karte the. unhone apane jeevankaal main swatantraaa kii raksha hetu 19 maah jail main bhi gujaarey or anek mahatvapurn pustaken bhi likhin. pro banavaari laal sharma nai aazaadee udghosh naamak patrika ke sampadak the. nai aazaadee udghosh patrika desh main bahurashtriya companiyon ke khilaaf aandolan kii sarvaadhik prasar vaali patrika he jise hindi ke alaave angreji main bhi prakashit kiya jaataa he. pro sharma ne bahurashtriya upniveshwaad ke khilaaf duniyaan main chal rahe aandolanon ke kai sahitya kaa bhi anuwaad kiya he. unke pasand kii pustakon main global kansperesi, hnen karporation rules da world aadi pramukh hai. pro sharma ne in pustakon kaa hindi main anuwaad bhi kiya or aaj yah pustaken deshbhar main aandolankariyon kii pasandida pustakon main shumaar he. pratyakshdarshi bataate hai ki antim samay main pro sharma ke antim vaakya the- ‘‘dhokhebaajon se saavdhaan rahanaa, desh ke swabhimaan kii raksha apane praanon se bhi zyada jaruri hai.’’ unka nidhan 26 sitambar, 2012 ko chandigarh main hua tha.
