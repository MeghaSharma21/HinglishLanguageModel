 aise electranik pravardhak ko shravy pravardhak yaa audio emplifire kehete hai jo kam shakti ke shravy sanketon kaa pravardhan kar saken. shravy-aavriti shakti pravardhak vah electronic pravardhak he jo kam shakti ke shravy aavriti vaale vidyut sanketon ko pravardhit karke unako itnaa shaktishaalee bana de ki ve laudspikar ko chalaa saken. un sanketon ko shravy sanket kehete hai jinaki aavriti 20 hertz se lekar 20 hajaar hertz ke bich hoti he. is seemaa ke bheetar kii aavritiyon vaale sanket hi maanav karn ko sunai padate hai, isse kam yaa adhik ke nahin. 
 shravy pravardhakon kaa nivesh sanket kuch sau microwat ke str kaa hota he jabaki output das, sau yaa hajaar wat ke str kaa ho saktaa he. shravy pravardhak, radio, tivi, telephone, cellphone aadi ke aavashyak ang he. 
 shravy pravardhak kaa sabase adhik upyog dhwani sanketon ko pravardhit karne ke liye hota he. kintu iske bahut se anya upyog bhi hai, jaise- sanket sansuchan, maapan evam niyantran, swachaalan, sudur niyantran, enlog computary aadi. 
 shravy pravardhak praayah do charnon se bane hote hai- 
 kabhi-kabhi ek hi charan main uparokt donon kaary kar liye jate hai.
 shravy pravardhak kaa nirgam charan kai prakaar se vargeekrut kiya ja saktaa he-
