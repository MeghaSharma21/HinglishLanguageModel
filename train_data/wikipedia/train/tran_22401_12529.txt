 paderuna railway stesan gorakhpur-thave line par sthit ek railway stesan he. yah bhartiya rajya, uttar pradesh ke paderuna nagar main sthit he or gorakhpur railway stesan ke 77 kimi uttar-purv main he. yah kushinagar se 20 kimi dur he, jo baudhon ke liye ek antarrashtriya paryatak or dharmik sthal he.
 paderuna railway stesan samudr tal se 89 meter kii oonchai par he or iskaa stesan code "POU" he.kai vishesh trainen paderuna se guzarati he, jinamein pramukh hai: 05008 haavda-ramnagar, 05115 chhapra-anand vihaar vaaya tamukhi road; paderuna; gorakhpur jan or 05717 katihaar-jalandhar express vaaya paderuna; captainganj jan. aagaamee rail bajet main or bhi kai trainon kii ghoshnaa kiye jane kii ummid he. kushinagar kaa jila mukhyalay hone ke kaaran paderuna railway stesan ek mahatvapurn railway stesan he.
 bengal or uttar pashchim railway ne 1907 main sivaan se captainganj ke liye 79 mile lambii or 1,000 mimi1,000 mi.me. chaudee meter gej line kaa nirmaan kiya.
 is line ke captainganj-thave bhaag ko 2011 main 1676 main mimi1,676 mimi chaudee broad gej line main parivartit kiya gaya.
 paderuna railway stesan se har din lagbhag 18,000 yatri guzarate he.
