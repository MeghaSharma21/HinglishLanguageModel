 rasayan vigyaan main uddeshypurvak ek yaa kram se kai rasinic abhikriyaayein karakar ek yaa anek utpaada banane kii kriya ko rasinic sanshleshan kehete hai. 
 rasinic sanshleshan samyak prakaar se chune hue kuch gyaat abhikarmakon yaa abhikaarakon se armbh kii jaati he. iske uparant inhen bhinn-bhinn prakaar kii abhikriyaaon se gujaarte hue antim utpaada tak le jaaya jaataa he.
 sanshleshan ke kuch aise bhi tareeke hai jinamein abhikarmak k se utpaada kha banane se bilkul hi bhinn ranneeti apanaayi jaati he. jaise cascad abhikriyaayein, bahuavayavi abhikriyaayein, doordarshisdrush sanshleshan aadi.
 carbonic sanshleshan, rasinic sanshleshan kii ek sabase mahatvapurn shakha he jo carbonic yogicon ke sanshleshan se sambandhit he.
