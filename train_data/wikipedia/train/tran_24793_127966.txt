 ram singh bharat ke ek dharmik netaa, samajasudharak tathaa swatantraaa senaanee the. ve pehle netaa the jinhone asahyog or britani vastuon tathaa sevaaon ke bahishkaar ko angrejon ke viruddh ek rajnatik hathiyaar ke rup main prayog kiya. 
 ram singh namdhari sikh the. unhone 12 april 1857 ko safed rang kaa swatantrata kaa dhvaj fahraakar angrejon ke viruddh vidroh kaa shankhanaad kar diya tha. 
 ram singh kaa janm punjab ke ludhiana jile ke bhaini ryaan gaanv main hua tha.
