 welcome back ek aane vaali bhartiya kamedi film he. iskaa nirdesan anis baazmi ne kiya he va nirmanfiroz nadiadwala or epa em khan swiss antertenment privet limited dwaara kiya gaya he. ye 2007 kii film welcome kaa sikval he.
 pehle isaki release disambar 2014 main thi lekin baad main release date ko aage badha kar 29 may 2015 kar diya gaya. lekin phir bhi yah afvaah he ki ab ye 17 july 2015 ko release hogi.
 actor naanaa patekar, paresh rawal, va anil kapur is part main bhi unhi rolls main dikhaai denge jinamein pehle the. lekin is baar lead role main akshay kumaar ke sthaan par jon abraham hai par ve kisi or bhoomika main dikhaai denge. katrina caff jinhone pichhali film main femail lead role kiya tha is baar unhen sonakshi sinha ne riples kar diya par sheeghra hi sonakshi ne ye film date na milane kii samasya ke kaaran chhod di. sonakshi ke baad asin ko bhi is role ke liye chunaa gaya par date kii samasya ke kaaran asin ne bhi film chhod di or iske baad shruti hasan ne inhen riples kiya.
 sara lauren is film main ek item sang karengi.
 film kaa sangeet anu malik, anand raaj anand, meet bros anjan dwaara banaaya gaya he. anu malik ne ek intervyuu main kaha ki hal hi main unhone iske 2 gane jinka inhone nirmaan kara he ve filmaaye hai. kuch samay pehle die gaye intervyuu ke anusaar is film kaa ek gaanaa anu malik ne bhi gaaya he.
