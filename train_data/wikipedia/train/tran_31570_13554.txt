 pilaa samraat yaa huangdi pracheen china kaa ek prasiddh or puranic samraat tha jise china main bahut aadar se yaad kiya jaataa he. chini mithy-kathaon ke hisaab se usne china par 2697-2597 isapurv yaa 2696-2598 isapurv ke kaal main raaj kiya. huangdi teen adhipatiyon or paanch samraaton main se ek the. unhen chini sabhyataa kaa sansthaapak mana jaataa he or bahut si chizon kaa shrey diya jaataa he. kuch kathaon main ve china kii kendriya sarkaar ke nirmaataa the, brahmand ke shaasha the or pataal-lok ke swami the. unhen kabhi-kabhi sabhi chini nasl ke logon kaa purvaj bhi kaha jaataa he.
