 boron samooh aavart sarini ke 13wein stambh ke rasinic tatwon kaa ek samooh he. is samooh main boron, aluminium, gallium, indium, thallium or ununtrium shaamil hai. inamen boron ek upadhatu gina jaataa he jabaki aluminium, gallium, indium or thallium sankramanoparant dhaatuen hai. ununtrium prayogshaala main banaaya gaya ek kritrim tatv he. iskaa nabhik bahut asthai he jiske karanvash yah atyant radiodharmee he or isaki ardhayu kaal keval 20 sekkind he.
