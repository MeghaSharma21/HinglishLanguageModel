 kapisa afghanistan ke 34 praanton main se ek he. yah desh ke uttar-purvi kshetra main sthit he. kapisa kii rajdhani mahmud-e-raaqi naamak shahar he. is praant kii aabaadi 4,06,200 he or iskaa kshetrafal 1,842 varg kimi he.
 kapisa kaa sab se pehla zikr sanskrit ke prasiddh vaiyaakaran panini kii likhaaiyon main paanchavi shatabdi isapurv se miltaa he. unhone kapishi naamak nagar kii charchaa kii or kaha kii yah kapisa rajya kii rajdhani he. unhone ek kapishyan naamak madiraa kaa bhi ullekh kiya jo kapisa se aati thi. is kshetra ke hind-yavan rajyon ke apollodotas pratham or eukratidi raajaaon ke sikkon par bhi kapisa kaa "kavishiye" naam se ullekh paaya gaya he. mahabharat main is nagar main das rakhane kii pratha kaa bhi varnan aata he. yahan ke angoor or madiraa kaa varnan kai pracheen bhartiya granthon main miltaa he. yahan par puraatan vastuon kii khoj main kai shishe ke bane madiraa ke basana or machhali ke rup main bane pyaale mili hai.
 yahan ke lagbhag 4 lakh logon main se 80% tajik hai, jo ek prakaar kii farsi bhashaa bolte hai. 8% pashto bolne vaale pashtun log hai. 10% pashai bhashaa bolne vaale log hai. hazaaraa or nuristaani samudaay ke log bhi yahan basate hai.
 kapisa ke adhiktar log krishi main jute hue hai. kesar yahan kii ek pramukh paidaavaar he. har nagar-basti main ek saptahik mela lagata he jisamen vyapaar bhi hota he or samjik mail-milaap bhi. is praant main al-beruni vishvidhyalay bhi sthit he.
