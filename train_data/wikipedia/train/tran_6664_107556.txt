 ganit, prabandhan vigyaan, arthshaastra, byoinformctics or computer vigyaan main gatik kramadesan jatil samasyaon ko saral charnon main todakar hal karne ke liye ek vidhi he. ise 'gatik ishtatameekaran' bhi kehete hai. yah un samasyaon par lagu he jo apni tarah kii choti samasyaon ke ativyapan or ishtatam taraf ko pradarshit karti he. jab lagu hoti he, yah vidhi sahaj tareekon se bhi kam samay leti he.
 gatik kramadesan main ek badi samasya ko sabase pehle choti-choti upsamasyaon ke rup main badla jaataa he. iske baad in saral samasyaon ko keval ek baar hal kiya jaataa he tathaa inke halon ko sangruhit kar liya jaataa he. is kaam ke liye smriti-aadhaarit data-strecchar kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. agali baar jab bhi vahi upsamasya saamne aati he to usko punah hal karne ke bajaay uske sangruhit hal ko le liya jaataa he. is prakaar kuch smriti kaa atirikt upyog karke gananaa main lagane vaale samay kii bachat kii jaati he.
