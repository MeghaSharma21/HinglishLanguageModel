 prayogshaala ek aisi suvidha ko kehete hai, jo vaigyanik anusandhan, prayog evam maapan ke liye aavashyak maahaul pradaan karata he.
 prayogshaala main suraksha ati aavashyak he. rasayan athwa bhautikee main kai prakaar ke aise prayog hote hai jisamen aag lagane yaa rasinic abhikriya aadi hone kaa khatra bana rahata he. is kaaran sabhi prayogshaala main aag bujhane kaa sabhi saamaan pehle se hota he. iske alaava prayogshaala main pravesh se purv hi yah bataaya jaataa he ki yahan kya or kaise karnaa he. iske saath hi sabhi prakaar ke saavdhaanee se judi baaten bataai jaati hai.
