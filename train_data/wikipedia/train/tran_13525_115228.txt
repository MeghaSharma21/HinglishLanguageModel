 ant:karan kaa taatpary us maansik shakti se he jisase vyakti uchit or anuchit kaa nirnay karata he. saamanyat logon kii yah dharna hoti he ki vyakti kaa antakaran kisi kaary ke auchity or anauchity kaa nirnay karne main usee prakaar sahaayta kar saktaa he jaise uske karn sunne main, athwa netra dekhne main sahaayta karte hai. vyakti main antahkaran kaa nirmaan uske naitik niyamon ke aadhaar par hota he. antakaran vyakti kii aatma kaa vah kriyaatmak siddhant mana ja saktaa he jisaki sahaayta se vyakti dwandvon kii upasthiti main kisi nirnay par pahunchataa he. abhigyaan shakuntalam main kalidas kehete hai:
 antashchetana shabd angreji ke 'inner kanshasness' kaa paryaayavachi he. kabhi-kabhi yah sahaj gyaan yaa prabhaa ke arth main bhi prayukt hota he. sant jone yaa gandhi ji praayah apni bhitari aawaaj yaa aatma kii aawaaj kaa hawala dete the. kai rahasyavaadiyon main yah antashchetana adhik viksit hoti he. parantu sarsaadhaaran main bhi man kii ankhen to hoti hi hai. yahi manushya kaa niti aniti se pare sadasadvivek kehalaata he. darshanikon kaa ek sampradaay yah maanata he ki jeev swabhaavatah 'shiv' he or is kaaran kisi ashikshit yaa asanskrit kahalaane vaale vyakti main bhi achche-bure ko pahchaanane kii antashchetana pashu se adhik vidyaman rahati he. bhautikwaadi antashchetana ko janmatah upasthit jaivik gun nahin maanate balki sabhyataa ke itihaas se utpann, chetana kaa bahya aavaran maanate hai; jaise frid use super igo kahata he. arvind ke darshan main yah shabd ubharkar aayaa he. yadi bhautik jad jagat or maanvi chaitanya ke bheetar ek si vikaas rekha khojani ho, yaa munmay main chinmay banane kii sambhaavnaayen hon to is antashchetana kaa kisi na kisi rup main purv astitv manushya main maananaa hi hoga. yoga isi ko atmik unnati bhi kahata he. yogi arvind kii paribhaasha main yahi chaityas purush yaa 'cychik beeing' kaha gaya he.
