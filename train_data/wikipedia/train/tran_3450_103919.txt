 kisi tantra ke output yaa avasthaa-charon ko aavashyaktaanusaar banaaye rakhane ke liye jo kuch atirikt vyavastha kii jaati he use niyantran pranali kehete hai. jis mul tantra ke output ko niyantrit karnaa hota he, use sanyantr yaa niyantrit tantra kehete hai. niyantran pranali, sanyantr ke output yaa state-charon ke maan par lagaataar najar rakhti he tathaa sanyantr ke input ko is prakaar badalati rahati he ki output vaisaa hi bane rahen yaa vaise hi badalen jaisaa vaanchit ho. 
 band loop kaa transafar falan nimnlikhit hota he-
 parshv chitra ko dekhen. chitra ke anusaar ham likh sakate hai ki,
 isi prakaar niyantran pranali analog yaa digital ho sakati he. rekhiy yaa arekhiy ho sakati he.
 sopani niyantran pranali main ek se adhik loop hote hai. isamen niyantran char ke niyantran ke liye na keval ise hi mapa jaataa he balki kisi anya char ko bhi mapa jaataa he. niyantran char ko sidhe niyantrit karne ke bajaay bahari loop, bhitari loop ke liye set-point pradaan karata he jo prakram ke madhyam se antatah praathmik char ko niyantrit karata he.
 sopani niyantran chunane kaa mukhya kaaran yah hota he ki isse niyantran karne main kathin tantra do bhaagon main bant jaataa he or mukhya disterbens ko gherte hue ek dwitiyak niyantran loop bana diya jaataa he. is prakaar dwitiyak loop adhikansh disterbens ko hataa detaa he atah mukhya loop ko keval choti disterbans ko hi niyantrit karnaa hota he. 'sheeghra soochna safalataa kaa aadhaar he' - yahi sopani niyantran kaa bhi aadhaar he.
 sopani niyantran vahaan sabase adhik laabhadaayak hota he jahaan bhitari band loop sabhi disterbens ko or duusare order ke deree ko samet le taaki praathmik loop main keval 'bada lag' hi shaamil ho.
