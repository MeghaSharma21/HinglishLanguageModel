 banbhatta kii aatmakatha acharya hajaari prasad dvivedi rachit ek aitihaasik hindi upanyaas he. isamen teen pramukh paatr hai- banbhatta, bhattini tathaa nipunika. is pustak kaa pratham prakaashan varsh 1946 main raajakmal prakaashan ne kiya tha. iskaa naveen prakaashan 1 sitambar 2010 ko kiya gaya tha. yah upanyaas acharya hajariprasad dvivedi kii vipul rachana-samarthy kaa rahasy unke vishad shastreey gyaan main nahin, balki us paardarshee jivan-drushti main nihit he, jo yug kaa nahin yug-yug kaa satya dekhati he. unki pratibha ne itihaas kaa upyog ‘teesri aankh’ ke rup main kiya he or ateetkaaleen chetana-pravaah ko vartmaan jeevanadhaara se jod paane main vah ascharyajanak rup se safal hui he. banbhatta kii aatmakatha apni samast aupnyasik sanrachana or bhangima main katha-kriti hote hue bhi mahakavyatv kii garima se purna he. 
 is upanyaas main dvivedi ji ne pracheen kavi banbhatta ke bikhare jivan-sutron ko badi kalaatmakataa se goonthkar ek aisi kathabhumi nirmit kii he jo jivan-satyon se rasmay saakshaatkaar karaati he. iskaa kathanayake kora bhavuk kavi nahin varan karmnirat or sangharkshil jivan-yoddha he. uske liye ‘sharir keval bhaar nahin, mitti kaa dhela nahin’, balki ‘usase bada’ he or uske man main aryavartt ke uddhaar kaa nimitt banane kii teevr bechaini he. ‘apane ko nickshesh bhav se de dene’ main jivan kii sarthakata dekhne vaali niuniya or ‘sabakuch bhul jane kii sadhana’ main leen mahadevi bhattini ke prati uskaa prem jab ucchata kaa varan kar letaa he to yahi goonj ant main rah jaati he-
 'banbhatta kii aatmakatha' harshkaleen sabhyataa evam samskruti kaa jivant dastavej he. aitihaasik upanyaaskaar ko ateet main bhi pravesh karnaa padataa he. ateet main pravisht ho kar hi itihaas ko vartmaan sandarbho ke saath jod paata he. isi judaav ke madhyam se aitihaasik upanyaaskaar ateet ko chitrit karata he.
