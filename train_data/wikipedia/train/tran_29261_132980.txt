 divalok bachat samay yaa greeshmasmay kuch deshon kii us pratha ko kehete hai jahaan garmiyon ke mausam main subah jaldi hone vaali raushnee kaa laabh uthaane ke liye greeshm ritu main ghadiyon ko aage kar diya jaataa he. aamtaur par deebas main har varsh main nirdhaarit shuruuaati or antim tithiyaan tay karke prashasnic aadesh dwaara ghadiyon ko ek ghanta aage chalaaya jaataa he. is se puure desh kii dincharya lagbhag ek ghante pehle shuru hoti he or ek ghante pehle khatm hoti he, yani raat ko battiyaan ityadi ek ghanta kam prayog hoti hai or urja kii bachat hoti he.
