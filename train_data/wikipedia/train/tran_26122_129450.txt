 akbar khan, 1989 main viklang vyaktiyon ke kalyaan ke liye bharat ke rashtriya puraskaar ke ek praptakarta hai.
 akbar khan kaa janm 16 agast 1962 ko rajasthan, bharat ke gaanv bangasar ke muslim parivaar main hua.
 khan ne 1987 main dow rana ruknuddin ke saath shaadi kii. unke do bete, nabahat khan or noman khan hai.
 dow rana ruknuddin ne apni piechdi aligarh muslim vishvavidhyalay kii.
