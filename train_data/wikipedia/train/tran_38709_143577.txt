 bhashavigyaan main bhashaa prayukti kisi bhashaa kii vah bhashika hoti he jo kisi vishesh saamaajik paristhiti, saamaajik varg yaa lakshya ke liye prayog ho. masalan aupchaarik sthitiyon main hindi main "aap kal aaiye" yaa "tu kal aana" ke prayog ko hi sahi vyakaran samajha jaataa he, jabaki do kishor mitron ke bich "tu kal aayiyo" kaa prayog bhi ek bhinn bhashaa prayukti ke rup main aksar hota he. isi tarah america angrezi main "aae am going tu tok tu maae fadar" sabhy samaaj main prayog hone vaali aupchaarik bhashaa prayukti he jabaki "aem gonna tok tu maae old main" thik vahiin arth rakhane vaali anaupchaarik prayukti he.
