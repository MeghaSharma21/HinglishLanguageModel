 hardik himanshu pandya, jo ki bhartiya cricket kaa ek ubharta sitara he, kaa janm 11 oktober 1993 main surat, gujarat main hua tha, ek harfanmaulaa cricket khilaadi hai. ye bhartiya cricket team, baroda cricket team tathaa mumbai indiens ke liye khelte hai. pandya daayen haath ke madhyam tej gendbaaj tathaa daayen haath ke madhyakram ballebaaj hai. inhone apane karier kii shuruyaat 26 janvari 2016 ko tinti tinti ke saath australia ke khilaaf kii thi. gauratlab he ki champion trophy 2017 i. ke bharat paak final match main pandya ne apni ballebaaji ke dam par paak gendbaajon ko pareshaani main daal diya tha or hardik kii is jabardast pari kaa hindustan ne "hardik" swaagat kiya.
