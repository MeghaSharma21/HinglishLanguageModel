 prabhu joshi, hindi kavi, lekhak, kathakaar evam vichaarak hai.
 12 disambar 1950 devas ke ranva gaanv main.
 jeevigyaan main snaatak tathaa rasayan vigyaan main snatakottar ke uparaant angrezi sahitya main bhi pratham shrenee main em.e.. angrezi kii kavita strectural grammer par vishesh adhyayan.
 pehali kahani 1973 main dharmayug main prakashit. 'kis haath se', 'prabhu joshi kii lambii kahaniyaan' tathaa uttam purush' katha sangrah prakashit. nai duniya ke sampadakeey tathaa fichar prishthon kaa paanch varsh tak sampadan. patr-patrikaon main hindi tathaa angrezi main kahaniyon, lekhon kaa prakaashan. chitrakaari bachapan se. jalarang main vishesh ruchi.
 lincystone tathaa harbart main austrailia ke trinale main chitra pradarshit. gailaree for celifornia kaa jalarang hetu thomas moraan award. twanti first senchari gailaree, neuyark ke top sevanty main shaamil. bharat bhawan kaa chitrakala tathaa m. pra. sahitya parishad kaa katha-kahani ke liye akhil bhartiya sammaan. sahitya ke liye m. pra. samskruti vibhaag dwaara gajanan madhav muktibodh fellowship.
 berlin main sampann jansanchaar ke antarrashtriya spardhaa main after aal haau long radio kaarykram ko juri kaa vishesh puraskaar dhoomil, muktibodh, salvador daali, picasso, kumaar gandharva tathaa ustad amir khan par kendrit radio kaaryakramon ko akashwani ke rashtriya puraskaar. 'impact of electranik media on trybal sosayadi' vishay par kiye gaye adhyayan ko 'adiens research wing' kaa rashtriya puraskaar.
 indore doordarshan main kaarykram nishpadak.
