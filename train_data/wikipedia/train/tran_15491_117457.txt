 gori rajvansh yaa gori silsilaa, jo apane-aap ko shansabani rajvansh bulaayaa karte the, ek madhyakaalin rajvansh tha jisane iran, afghanistan, pashchimottar bharat, kuraasaan or aadhunik pashchimi china ke shinjiyang kshetra ke kai bhaagon par 1148-1215 isvi kaal main raaj kiya. yah rajvansh gazanvi rajvansh ke patan ke baad uthaa tha. yah rajvansh afghanistan ke gor praant main kendrit tha or itihaaskaaron kaa maananaa he ki iskaa rajpariwaar tajik mul kaa tha.
 gori rajvansh kii sarvapratham rajdhani gor praant kaa firophakoh shahar tha lekin baad main heraat ban gaya. iske alaava gazani or lahore ko bhi rajdhaniyon kii tarah istemaal kiya jaataa tha, visheshkar sardiyon main. dilli kaa prasidd qutub minar isi vansh ke qutub-ud-deen aibak kaa banwaaya hua he, jisane dilli sultanate kii sthapana bhi kii. is rajvansh ke patan ke baad iran main khwaaresm​ shah rajvansh or uttar bharat main dilli sultanate ke ghulaam rajvansh ne isaki jagah lee.
 madhya 12veen sadi se pehle gori sardaar 150 saalon tak gazanaviyon or saljuqon ke adheen rahe. is kaal ke ant tak gazanvi swayan saljuqon ke adheen ho chuke the. 
 1148-1149 main qutub-ud-deen naamak ek sthaneey gori netaa kisi paariwarik jhagade ke baad sharan lene jab gazanaa aayaa to gazanvi shaasha baharaam shah ne use zehar dekar maar daalaa. badalen main ek duusare gori sardaar alaa-ud-deen hossain ne gazanaa par hamalaa kara or use 7 dinon tak lutaa or jalaakar raakh kar daalaa. iske baad use 'jahansos' ke naam se janaa jane laga, jiskaa matlab 'jahaan/vishwa main aag lagane vaala' hota he. iske saath hi gazanvi samrajya khatm hone laga.
 gazanaviyon ne saljuqon kii sahaayta se gazani par dobaaraa niyantran kar liya lekin jald hi use kuch oguz​ turkmaani jhundon ko kho baithe jo swayan purvottar disha se apni zaminein kaara-kitaan khaanat ko khokar yahan aa dhamke the. 1152 main alaa-ud-deen jahansos ne saljuqon ko kar dene se manaa kar diya or firophakoh se faujen badhaai lekin saljuqon ke shaasha, sultan ahamed sanger, ne use haraa diya. sultan sanger ne alaa-ud-deen ko kuch der bandi banaakar rakhaa lekin phir reeha kar diya. 1161 main alaa-ud-deen jahansos kaa dehanth ho gaya or uskaa betaa saif-ud-deen gori shaasha bana. apane marane se pehle alaa-ud-deen jahansos ne apane do bhatijon - shahaab-ud-deen or giyaas-ud-deen - ko qaid kar rakhaa tha lekin saif-ud-deen ne unhen reeha kar diya.
 saljuqon ne jab is kshetra par qabza kiya tha jo unhone saif-ud-deen kii patni ke zevar bhi le liye the. gaddi grahan karne ke baad ek din saif-ud-deen ne kisi sthaneey sardaar ko yah zevar pahane dekh liya or taish main aakar use maar daalaa. jab mritak ke bhai ko kuch mahino baad mauqaa mila to usne saif-ud-deen ko badale main bhaalaa marakar maar daalaa. is tarah saif-ud-deen kaa shasankaal keval ek varsh ke aasapaas hi rahaa. giyaas-ud-deen nayaa shaasha bana or uske chote bhai shahaab-ud-deen ne uskaa rajya vistaar karne main uski bahut vafadaari se madad kari. shahaab-ud-deen ne pehle gazanaa par qabza kiya, phir 1175 main multan or uch par or phir 1186 main lahore par. jab uskaa bhai 1202 main mara to shahaab-ud-deen mohammad gori sultan ban gaya.
 1206 main aadhunik pakistan ke jhelum kshetra main nadi ke kinare mohammad gori ko khokhar naamak rajput qabeele ke logon ne apane upar hue hamalon kaa badla lene ke liye maar daalaa. mohammad gori kaa koi betaa nahin tha or uski maut ke baad uske samrajya ke bhartiya kshetra par uske preeya ghulaam qutub-ud-deen aibak ne dilli sultanate sthapit karke uskaa vistaar karnaa shuru kar diya. uske afghanistan va anya ilaaqon par goriyon kaa niyantran na batch sakaa or khwarezmi samrajya ne unpar qabza kar liya. gazanaa or gor kam mahatvapurn ho gaye or dilli ab kshetreey islamic samrajya kaa centre ban gaya. itihaaskaar san 1215 ke baad gori samrajya ko puri tarah visthapit maanate hai.
