 mailcome marshall brijtoun, barbados se westeindies cricket team ke khilaadi the jinhen cricket itihaas ke sabase achche tej gendbaajon main se ek mana jaataa he. vah 1970-80 ke dashak kii ajey westeindies team ke tej gendbaaj the. vah 80 ke dashak ke sabase safal gendbaaj the or us avadhi main westeindies kii safalataa ke mukhya bindu the. mailcome kaa county championships main hampshire kii taraf se bhi safal cricket kariyar rahaa. 
 mailkam ne 1978-79 main bharat ke khilaaf apni pehali test series main 3 match main maatr 3 wicket liye the. uske baad unhen 1980 main hi dobaaraa khilne kaa mauka mila. westeindies team main joel garnor, endi roberts, colin croft, maaikel holding jaise safal gendbaaj maujuud the. is kaaran unhen aniyamit rup se khilne kaa hi mauka miltaa tha. 1982 tak unhone 12 test khelkar 31.88 kii ausat se 34 wicket liye the. yah aankade saadhaaran the. 1982 main unhone 22 maichon kii championships main hampshire ke liye 134 wicket liye. uske baad unhen 1983 ke bhartiya team ke westeindies daure par team main chun liya gaya. unhone is shrinkhalaa main 21 wicket liye. phir westeindies ke bharat daure par unhone 5 test main 33 wicket liye. westeindies ne test shrinkhalaa 3-0 se or vanade 5-0 se jiti thi. phir australia ke khilaaf gharelu maidaanon par unhone 21 wicket liye or 1984 main england main england ke viruddh 24 wicket liye. vahaan hedingle main kshetrarakshan karte hue unke baayein haath kaa angootha tut gaya tha. vah phir bhi ballebaaji karne aae or itane samay tak tike rahe ki laire gomes ne apana shatak puraa kar liya. unhone phir gendbaaji karke 53 ran dekar 7 wicket bhi liye. ise unke sarvshereshth pradarshnon main se ek ke rup main gina jaataa he. 
 garnor or holding ke sanyas lene ke baad 1988 tak courtney walsh or kirtli ambros unke anya mahatvapurn gendbaaj saathi ban chuke the. is kaaran unka kaary bhaar unke puure kariyar main kam hi tha. phir bhi 1983 se 1991 kii avadhi main unke naam 69 maichon main 342 wicket he, jo ki kisi bhi gendbaaj ke muqaabale sabase zyada he. is avadhi main westeindies tej gendbaajon dwaara liye gaye vikaton main se unhone 31.37 fisadi wicket swayan liye. 1982-83 se lekar 1985-86 tak in teen saalon main ve sabase achche gendbaaj the. us avadhi main unhone lagaataar saat shrinkhalaaon main 21 yaa usase adhik wicket liye, jisamen se antim paanch main unki gendbaazi ausat 20 se kam rahi. jite hue test maichon main mailcome kii gendbaaji ausat sirf muthaiya muralidharan ko chhodkar sabhi gendbaajon se kam he.
 kul milakar 1977/78 se 1995/96 tak mailcome ne 408 pratham shrenee match khele the. jisamen unhone 19.10 kii ausat se 1651 wicket liye. mailcome hampshire county cricket club kii taraf se 1979 se 1993 tak khelte the. unhone county championships main 800 se adhik wicket liye or hampshire ko list e cricket kii pratiyogita benson or hajes cap ko jeetne main bhi madad kii. 1978 se 1991 tak chale apane antarrashtriya kariyar ke 81 test maichon main unhone 20.94 kii ausat se 376 wicket liye. 1998 main courtney walsh ne jab tak mailcome se adhik wicket nahin liye the, tab tak vah westeindies ke sabase safal gendbaaj the. mailcome nichle kram ke kushal ballebaaj bhi the or unhone test cricket main 10 ardh shatak bhi lagaae. mailcome ek divasiy cricket main kam safal rahe. unhone 136 ek divasiy antarrashtriya khele the, jisamen unhone 26.96 kii ausat se 157 wicket liye.
