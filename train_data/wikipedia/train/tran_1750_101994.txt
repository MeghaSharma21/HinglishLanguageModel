 nagda madhya pradesh main ujjain se lagbhag 30 mile uttar-pashchim main, pashchim railway ke mumbai-dilli maarg par, chambal nadi ke tat par sthit he. yah eklingaji se kuch pehle sthit he. nagda kaa pracheen shahar kabhi rawal nagadityae kii rajdhani thi. vartmaan main yah ek chota sa gaanv he. yah gaanv 11veen shatabdi main bane 'saas-bahu' mandir ke liye prasiddh he. is mandir kaa mul naam 'sahastrahabahu' tha joki yah naam vikrut hokar saas-bahu ho gaya he. yah ek chota sa mandir he. lekin mandir kii vaastustyli kaaphi aakarshak he.
 malwa ke parmaar nareshon ke abhilekhon main nagda kaa pracheen naam nagarad miltaa he.nagda par kiye gayi utkhanan main praarambhik louh samskruti ke pramaan mili hai.nagda se das prakaar ke loh upkaran mili hai. jinamein dudhari, kataar, kulhadi kaa moonth, chammach, chimati, kulhadi, challa, banagra, chaaku or hansiya ullekhneey hai.nagda or eran ke utkhannon evam anya sthalon kii khudaai ke aadhaar par us puraane mat ko auchitypurna nahin mana gaya he, jinamein in pura sthalon par tamrapashanik samskruti kii parisamapti ke tatkaal baad aitihaasik yug kii samskruti kaa prarambh mana jaataa he.ab yah tathy santhaapit hua he ki in pura sthalon par bhi, jahaan par samskruti ke satatya kii baat kahi gayi thi, aise pramaan milte hai, jinase yah spasht ho jaataa he ki tamrapashanik samskruti kii parisamapti or praarambhik aitihaasik yugin samskruti ke bich main anek varshon kaa antaraal rahaa hoga.
