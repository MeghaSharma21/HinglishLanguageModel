 asamaa al-ashad; Asma al-Assad syria ke raashtrapati bashar al ashad kii patni or vartmaan syria kii pratham mahila asamaa al-asad kaa janm 11 agast 1 9 75 ko landan main cromwel aspataal ke cardiologist favaaz akhrs or unki patni sahaar akhtar se hua tha, jo ek sevanivrutt rajanyik the jo landan main syrian duutavas main pratham sachiv ke rup main kaary karte the. asamaa ke mata-pita sunni musalmaan or syrian mul ke hai, jinka homes shahar se sambandh hai. vah acton, landan main badi hui, jahaan vah twaford carch of england hai skool or baad main ek nijee ladakiyon kaa skool, queen college, landongai. unhone 1996 main kingscolege landan se computer science main pratham shrenee ke bachaler of science degree or francisi sahitya mendiploma ke saath snaatak kiya. vah angreji bhashaa angreji, arabi, french or spanish bolti he.
