 alastair nathan cook  ek angreji cricketer he jo asex county cricket club ke liye kheltaa he, or pehle england ke liye sabhi antarrashtriya praroopon main. england test or van de international tiimon ke purv captain, unke paas kai angreji or antarrashtriya record hai. unhen england ke liye khilne vaale mahanatam ballebaajon main se ek mana jaataa he, or aadhunik yug ke sabase shandaar ballebaajon main se ek he. cook har samay paanchvaan sabase zyada test ran scorer he. 
 cook england ke sabase zyada prabhavit khilaadi hai or unhone 5 9 test or 69 ekadivasiy maichon main angreji record main team kaa netritv kiya he. vah england ke test maichon main agrani ran-scorer hai, or sabase kam umr ke khilaadi 12,000 test ran ko puraa karne ke liye hai. cook ne england ke liye 33 test shatak record kiye hai or 50 test jeet main bhaag lene vaale pehle england khilaadi hai. ek baayen haath ke udghaatan ballebaaj, vah aam taur par pehali parchi par khele jate hai.
 cook ne asex kii academy ke liye khelaa or 2003 main pehali baar ilevan ke liye apni shuruyaat kii. unhone 2006 main england kii kai yuva tiimon main 2006 main test said tak pahunchane tak khelaa. isibi neshnal academy, cook ke saath westeindies main daure ke dauraan marcus trescothique ke liye aakhri minute ke pratisthaapan ke rup main bharat main england kii rashtriya team ke liye bulaayaa gaya tha or 21 saal kii umr main ek shatabdi ke saath shuru hua tha. unhone apane pehle varsh main 1,000 ran banaae or bharat, pakistan, westeindies or bangladesh ke khilaaf apane pehle test maichon main sadiyon banaaye. cook ne england main 200 9 kii ashes shrinkhalaa jeetne or 2010-11 main ashes ko banaae rakhane main 2010 main test captain ke rup main deputizing or phir oriai captain purnakalik lene ke baad ek mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai.
 2 9 agast 2012 ko saathi salaami ballebaaj andrew strans kii sevanivritti ke baad unhen test team ke captain niyukt kiya gaya. cook ne 1 9 84-85 se bharat main apni pehali test shrinkhalaa jeetne ke liye england kaa netritv kiya. daure ke dauraan vah apane pehle paanch test maichon main shatak lagane vaale pehle captain bane. 30 may 2015 ko, cook england ke liye test maichon main agrani ran-scorer bane, graham gooch se aage. bangladesh or bharat ke england ke 2016 daure ke baad, vah test captain ke rup main neeche utar gaye. 2011 main cook ko embii niyukt kiya gaya tha  or 2016 main sibii cricket ke liye sevaaon ke liye. pakistan ke khilaaf pehle test ke dauraan 24 may 2018 ko, cook ne 153 ke saath lagaataar test maichon kii sabase adhik sankhya main bhaag lene ke liye alan border ke record ko baraabar kiya, hedingle main duusare test main ek hafte baad ise paar kar gaya. 3 sitambar 2018 ko, cook ne ghoshnaa kii ki unke baarah saal kaa antarrashtriya kariyar 11 sitambar 2018 ko bharat ke khilaaf shrinkhalaa ke samaapan par samaapt hoga.
