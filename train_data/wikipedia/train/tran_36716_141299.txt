 bhartiya cricket team vartmaan samay main 21 july se 16 sitambar 2011 ke bich england kaa daura kar rahi he. is daure kii test match shrinkhalaa kaa naam pataudi trophy 2011 rakhaa gaya he. is daure main ek tinti-tinti antarrashtriya match, paanch ek divasiy antarrashtriya or chaar test maichon ke saath-saath angreji countiyon ke viruddh match hai. lords main khelaa gaya pehla test match 2000vaa test tha. tisare test main england kii jeet ne unhen vishwa ranking main number ek kaa sthaan diya.
 bharat ne apane daure ke pehle match ke liye apane kaaphi test khilaadiyon ko vishram kiya: mahendra singh dhoni, eshanth sharma, praveen kumaar or harbhajan singh sabhi team se bahaar the.
 is match main haar ke saath hi bharat isisi ranking main tisare sthaan par khisak gaya he, jabaki england ab vishwa kii nambar ek team ban gayi he.
