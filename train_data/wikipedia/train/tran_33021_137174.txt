 khamaaj thaat bhartiya sangeet paddati ke das thaaton main se ek hai. yah ek raag he. yah sampurna shadav he. iskaa vaadi swar gandhar or sanvaadi nishad he. aaroh main rishabh varjit he. nishad shuddh, avaroh komal or anya sabhi swar shuddh lagate hai. yah raag shringarpradhan he. iske gane kaa samay raatri kaa dwitiya pahar bataaya gaya he.
