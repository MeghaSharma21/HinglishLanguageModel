 banking carmik chayan santhaan ) bharat kii ek swatantra sanstha he jo anya sansthaaon ko karmachariyon ke chayan, bhartee evam moolyankan main sahyog pradaan karti he. yah 1975 main armbh hui thi. 1984 main yah swatantra sanstha bani. 
 san 2011 se is santhaan ne bhartiya bainkon ke liye adhikari tathaa clerkon kii niyukti hetu samaan likhit pariksha armbh kiya. jo bhi saarvajanik kshetra ke 20 bainkon tathaa kshetreey gramin bainkon main bhartee hona chahta he use seedblyoo dena hi padegaa.
