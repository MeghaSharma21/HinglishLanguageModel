 andaman or nicobar dweepasmooh bharat kaa ek kendra shasit pradesh he. ye bengal kii khadi ke dakshin main hind mahaasaagar main sthit he. andaman or nicobar dweep samooh lagbhag 572 chote bade dwipon se milakar bana he jinamein se sirf kuch hi dwipon par log rahete hai. yahan kii rajdhani port blair he.
 bharat kaa yah kendra shasit pradesh hind mahaasaagar main sthit he or bhugolic drushti se dakshin purv asia kaa hissaa he. yah indonesia ke acheh ke uttar main 150 kimi par sthit he tathaa andaman sagar ise thailand or myanmar se alag karata he. do pramukh dweepasmoohon se milakar bane is dweepasmooh ko 10° u akshansh pruthak karti he, jiske uttar main andaman dweep samooh or dakshin main nicobar dweep samooh sthit hai. is dweepasmooh ke purv main andaman sagar or pashchim main bengal kii khadi sthit he.
 dweepasmooh kii rajdhani port blair ek andamani shahar he. 2001 kii bharat kii janaganana ke anusaar yahan kii jansankhya 356152 he. puure kshetra kaa kul bhoomi kshetra lagbhag 6496 kimi² yaa 2508 varg mile he.
 andaman shabd malay bhashaa ke shabd haanduman se aayaa he jo hindu devtaa hanuman ke naam kaa parivartit rup he. nicobar shabd bhi isi bhashaa se liya gaya he jiskaa arth hota he nagn logon kii bhoomi. hind mahaasaagar main basaa nirmal or shant andaman paryatakon ke man ko aseem anand kii anubhuti karaata bharat kaa ek lokapriy dweep samooh he. andaman apane anchal main moonga bhitti, saaf-svachh sagar tat, puraani smritiyon se jude khandahar or anek prakaar kii durlabh vanaspatiyaan sanjoe hai. sundarata main ek se badhkar ek yahaan kul 572 dweep hai. andaman kaa lagbhag 86 pratishat kshetrafal jangalon se dhakaa hua he. samudri jivan, itihaas or jalakridaaon main ruchi rakhane vaale paryatakon ko yah dweep bahut raas aata he.
 is dweep samooh par 17 veen sadi main maraathon dwaara adhikaar kiya gaya tha. iske baad is par angrejon kaa shashan ho gaya or baad main duusare vishwayuddh ke dauraan japan dwaara is par adhikaar kar liya gaya. kuch samay ke liye yah dweep netaji subhaashachandr bose kii azaad hind fouj ke adheen bhi rahaa tha. bahut kam logon ko hi pataa hoga ki desh main kahin bhi pehali baar port blair main hi tirangaa fahraaya gaya tha. yahaan netaji subhash chandra bose ne 30 disambar 1943 ko union jaik utaar kar tirangaa jhanda fahraaya tha. isli andaman nicobar prashasan kii taraf se 30 disambar ko har saal ek bhavy kaarykram manaane kii shuruuaat kii gai he. general lokanathan bhi yahan ke governor rahe the. 1947 main british sarkaar se mukti ke baad yah bharat kaa kendra shasit pradesh bana.
 british shashan dwaara is sthaan kaa upyog swadhinata andolan main damankaaree nitiyon ke tahat krantikariyon ko bharat se alag rakhane ke liye kiya jaataa tha. isi kaaran yah sthaan andolankariyon ke bich kaala pani ke naam se kukhyat tha. kaid ke liye port blair main ek alag karagaar, cellular jail kaa nirmaan kiya gaya tha jo british india ke liye siberia kii samaan tha.
 26 disambar 2004 ko tsunami laharon ke kahar se is dweep par 6000 se zyada log maare gayi.

 hare-bhare vrikshon se ghira yah samudratat ek manoram sthaan he. yahaan samudr main dubkee lagaakar pani ke neeche kii duniya kaa avlokan kiya ja saktaa he. yahaan se suryaast kaa adbhut najaara kaaphi aakarshak prathit hota he. yah bich apni prakritik khubsuurati ke liye lokapriy he.

 yahaan bharat kaa ekmaatr sakriy jwalamukhi he. yah dweep lagbhag 3 kilometre main failaa he. yahaan kaa jwalamukhi 28 may 2005 main fata tha. tab se ab tak isse lava nikal rahaa he.
 uttari andaman dweep main sthit prakruti premiyon ko bahut pasand aata he. yah sthaan apane santaron, chawlon or samudri jivan ke liye prasiddh he. yahaan kii sedal peak aasapaas ke dwipon se sabase ooncha point he jo 732 meter ooncha he. andaman kii ekmaatr nadi kalpong yahaan se bahati he.
 yahaan kisi jamaane main gulam bharat se laae gaye bandiyon ko port blair ke paas viper dweep par utaara jaataa tha. ab yah dweep ek picnic sthal ke rup main viksit ho chukaa he. yahaan ke toote-phute faansee ke fande nirmam ateet ke sakshi banakar khadey hai.yaheen par sher ali ko bhi faansee di gai thi, jisane 1872 main bharat ke governor general lord mayo kii hatya kii thi.
 yahaan ke svachh nirmal pani kaa saundarya sailaaniyon kaa man moh letaa he. in dwipon main kai baar tairti hui dolphin machhliyon ke jhund dekhe ja sakate hai. shishe kii tarah saaf pani ke neeche jaleey paudhe va rangeen machhliyon ko tairte dekhakar paryatak apni bahari duniya ko aksar bhul jate hai.
