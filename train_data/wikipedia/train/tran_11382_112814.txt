 jayantiya hills bhartiya rajya meghalaya kaa ek jila he. jile kaa mukhyalay jovai he. 22 farvari 1972 ko jaintiya hill jila srujit kiya gaya tha. iskaa kul bhugolic kshetrafal 3,819 varg kilometre he or bharat kii janaganana 2011 ke anusaar yahaan kii jansankhya 2,95,692 he. yahaan kaa jila mukhyalay jovai main sthit he. jaintiya hills jila rajya main koyale kaa sabase bada utpaadak jila he. jile bhar main koyale kii khaane dikhaai detii hai. iske alaava yahaan choonepatthar kaa khanan bhi vruddhi par he kyonki cement udyog yahaan joro par he or usamen choonepatthar kii oonchi maang he. haal ke kuch varshon main hi ek bade jile ko do chote jilon: pashchim jayatiya evam purvi jaintiya hills main baant diya gaya tha
