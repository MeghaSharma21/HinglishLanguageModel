 mandana rajasthan kii lok kala he. ise vishesh avasaron par mahilaayein zamin athwa diwaar par banaati hai. mandana, mandan shabd se liya gaya he jiskaa arth he sajja. mandane ko vibhinn parvon, mukhya utsavon tathaa rituon ke aadhaar par vargeekrut kiya ja saktaa he. ise akritiyon ke vibhinn aakaar ke aadhaar par bhi baanta ja saktaa he. udhaaharan-swarup chaukaa, mandane kii chaturbhuj akruti he jiskaa samruddhi ke utsavon main vishesh mahatv he jabaki tribhuj, vrutt evam swaastik lagbhag sabhi parvon yaa utsavon main banaae jate he. kuch akritiyon main aanevaale parv kaa nirupan evam us dauraan padane vaale parvon ko bhi banaaya jaataa he. mandana kii paaramparik akritiyon main jyamitiya evam pushp akritiyon ko liya gaya he. in akritiyon main tribhuj, chaturbhuj, vrutt, swaastik, shatranj pat kaa aadhaar, kai sidhi rekhaayen, tarang kii akruti aadi mukhya he. inamen se kuch akritiyaan hadappa kii sabhyataa ke kaal main bhi milti he. pushp akritiyaan zyaadaatar saamaajik evam dharmik vishwaas tathaa jaadu-tone se judi hui hai jabaki jyamitiya akritiyaan tantra-mantra evam tantric rahasy se. kuch jyamitiya akritiyon ke rup kisi devi yaa devtaa se jude hote hai jaise ki shashtakon jo do tribhuj ke jodakar banaaya jaataa he, devi lakshmi ko chihnit karata he. maadne kii yah akruti kaaphi prasiddh he tathaa ise visheshat: deepawali tathaa sardiyon main fasal kataai ke samay utsav main banaaya jaataa he. inke alaava bahut sari akritiyaan kisi mukhya akruti ke chaaron ore banaai jaati he. ye akritiyaan saamaajik evam dharmik parampara par aadhaarit hoti he jisamen varshon puraane reeti-rivaaj tathaa vibhinn parvon main mahilaon kii drushti ko pradarshit karti he.
