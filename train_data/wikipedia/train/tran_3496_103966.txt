 hugo puraskaar har saal us varsh main chhapi sarvshereshth vigyaan kathaon ke liye diya jaataa he. iskaa naam hugo garnsbek kii smriti main rakhaa gaya tha jinhonen "adbhut kahaniyaan" naam kii vigyaan katha patrika shuru kari thi. yah inaam vishwa vigyaan katha sanghathan diya karti he or sab se pehle hugo puraskaar san 1953 main diya gaya tha.
