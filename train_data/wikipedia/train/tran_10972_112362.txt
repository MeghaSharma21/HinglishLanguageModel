 mahishur rahamaan
 mahishur rahamaan, bangladesh ke ek varisht mantri the, jinpar railmarg, sadakon or rajmargon kaa prabhaar tha. pad va aude kii haisiyat se ve 29 june 1978 se 12 march 1979 ke bich pradhanmantri kii haisiyat rakhte the. ve bangladesh nesanalist party kii sthapana main nirnaayak the. bangladesh ke pradhanmantri ke pad ko, shaikh mujiburrahman kii hatya ke karanvash 15 agast 1975 se barkhaast kar diya gaya tha.
 jiyaur rahamaan ke raashtrapati banane ke pashchaat, ek mantritviya vyavastha ko punarsthapit kiya gaya, or june 1978 se unhone bataur mukhya mantri/varisht mantri kaary kiya. unako pradhanmantri banane kii baat thi, parantu 12 march 1979 ko unki akaal mrityu ke kaaran shah ajijur rahamaan ko 15 april 1979 ko bangladesh kaa pradhanmantri bana diya gaya.
