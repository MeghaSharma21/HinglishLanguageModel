 grasani shoth yaa grasanyarti vyaadhi main grasanika, mridutal tathaa tundivaadi kii shleshm kala main shoth ho jaataa he.
 yah rog praaia: sheet lag jane ke kaaran utpann hota he. kabhi-kabhi uttejk padaarthon ke vaashp se, yaa garam uttejk padaarth ke prayog se bhi, rog kii avasthaa utpann ho jaati he.
 choti chechak, masoorika, yakshma, upadansh aadi rogon main bhi grasanishoth ke lakshan paae jate hai.
 atyadhik dhumrapaan, chirkaalin madhpan, rajakan ityadi se bhi rog ho jane kii ashanka rahati he.
 grasanika raktayukt ho jaati evam sooj jaati he. rogi kaa kanth shukpurna, taalu main pidaa, khaansi tathaa swar baari ho jaataa he ki kanth main koi vastu fasi hui he. bhojan nigalane main ksht hota he.
