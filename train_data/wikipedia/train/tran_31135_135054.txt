 dhanurveda ek upved he. iske antargat dhanurvidya yaa sainya vigyaan aata he. duusare shabdon main, dhanurveda, bhartiya sainya vigyaan kaa doosraa naam he.
 shastron ke anusaar chaar veda hai or isi tarah chaar upved hai. in upavedon main pehla ayurveda he. doosraa shilp veda he. teesraa gandharva veda or chauthaa dhanurveda he. is dhanurveda main dhanurvidya kaa sara rahasy maujuud he. ye alag baat he ki ab ye dhanurveda apane mul swarup main kahin nahin he.
 vaishampayan dwaara rachit 'nitiprakash' yaa 'nitiprakashika' naamak granth main dhanurveda ke bare main jaankaree he. yah granth madras main dow aaport dwaara 1882 main sampadit kiya gaya. dhanurveda ke alaava is granth main rajdharmopadesh, khadgotpatti, mukthayudhanirupan, senanayan, sanyprayog evam rajvyapaar par aath adhyyon main takshashila main vaishampayan dwaara janamejay ko diya gaya shikshan he. is granth main rajshastra ke pravartakon kaa ullekh he.
