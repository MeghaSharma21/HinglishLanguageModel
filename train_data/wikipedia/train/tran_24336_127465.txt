 chumbakeey pratishtambh, chumbakeey paripathon ke vishleshan main prayukt ek concept he. jis prakaar vidyut paripath main voltata ko pratirodh se vibhajit karne par dhara praapt hoti he, usee prakaar chumbakeey paripath main chumbakeey vaahak bal ko pratishtambh se bhaag dene par chumbakeey flux praapt hota he. lekin pratishtambh main koi urja nasht nahin hoti jaise pratirodh main hoti he. balki isamen chumbakeey urja bhandarit kii jaati he. jis prakaar kisi bindu se duusare bindu tak jane ke liye dhara kam pratirodh kaa maarg pasand karti he, usee prakaar chumbakeey flux bhi kam pratishtambh ke maarg se janaa pasand karata he. 
 pratishtambh kii ikai invars henry he. kisi path kaa pratishtambh nimnlikhit sutara se nikaala jaataa he-
