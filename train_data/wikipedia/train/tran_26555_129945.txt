 bandung indonesia ke pashchim java praant kaa sabase bada shahar or rajdhani he. iske mahanagar kshetra main san 2015 main 84,95,928 nivasee the, jiske aadhaar par yah indonesia kaa doosraa sabase bada mahanagar kshetra tha. kendriya shahar kii aabaadi 25 lakh he jiske anusaar yah desh kaa teesraa sabase bada shahar he.
 bandung java dweep par rashtriya rajdhani jakarta se 140 kilometre dakshinpurv main samudratal se 768 meter kii oonchaai par basaa hua he. apni oonchaai ke kaaran yahan kaa taapmaana salbhar anya indonesian shaharon se thanda rahata he. yah ek nadi droni main jwalamukheey pahadiyon se ghira hua he. san 2018 ke asian khelon ke mezbaan nagaron main bandung bhi ek he.
