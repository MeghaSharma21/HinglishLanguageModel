 rasinic hathiyaar nishedh sanghathan arthath oganizeshan for prohibishan of chemical vepans sanyukt raashtra sangh samarthit ek sanstha he jo duniya bhar main rasinic hathiyaaron ko nasht karne or unki rokthaam ke liye kaam karti he. vartmaan main yah sanstha syria main hathiyaaron ko khatm kiye jane kaa kaam dekh rahi he. nobel shanti puraskaar samiti ne 2013 kaa shanti puraskaar is sanstha ko diya he.
 
 sanghathan kii mul sanghathanaatmak sanrachana or gatividhiyaan chemical vepans kanvension main varnit kiye gayi hai . isaki mukhya nikaay lagbhag prativarsh hone vaale sammelan hai jisamen sabhi sadsy raashtra bhaag le sakate hai or samaan matadhikaar rakhte hai. aam taur par sammelan main sabhi deshon kaa pratinidhitiv sanghathan kaa ek sthaayee pratinidhi dwaara kiya jaataa he, jo adhikansatah ek rajdoot bhi hota he. sammelan main, sanghathan or parmparik se jude sabhi vishyon par nirnay liya jaataa he.
 sanghathan kaa netritv mahanideshak karte hai, jinaki niyukti sidhe sammelan main kiya jaataa he. mahanideshkon kii ek suchi yahan di gai he.
 pratham mahanideshak kaa dwitiya kaarykaal keval ek varsh kaa tha, uske baad sadsy deshon ke avishvaas ke aadhaar par unhen padmukt kar diya gaya.
