 chhavi pandey ek bhartiya televijan abhinetri, film abhinetri tathaa gayak he jo star plus ke dharawahik teri meri lav story main smita ke kirdaar se jaani jaati he. 
 chhavi pandey ne 2008 main colors tivi ke kaarykram indiaj got tallent main bhaag liya tha. iske baad unhone bidesiya naamak ek bhojpuri film main bhi kaam kiya. star plus ke dharawahik teri meri lav story se unhen vishesh khyaati mili. vartmaan main vah zee tivi ke dharawahik bandhan main riya kii bhoomika nibhaa rahi he.
