 patan bharat ke gujarat pradesh kaa jila evam jila-mukhyalay he. yah ek pracheen nagar he jisaki sthapana 745 i main vanraj chhavda ne kii thi. raja ne iskaa naam 'anhilpur patan' yaa 'anhilwaad patan' rakhaa tha. yah madhyakaal main gujarat kii rajdhani hua karata tha. is nagar main bahut se aitihaas sthal hai jinamein hindu evam jain mandir, rani kii vaav aadi prasiddh hai.
 patan kaa pracheen naam 'anhilpur' he. pracheen kaal main ise musalamaanon ne khandhar bana diya tha, unheen khandhiron par pun: naveen patan ne pragati kii he. maharaj bhim kii rani udhyamati kaa banwaaya bhawan khandhar avasthaa main ab bhi vidyaman he. nagar ke dakshin main ek prasiddh khan sarovar he. ek jain mandir main vanraaja kii murti bhi darshneey he. naveen patan maratha logon ke prayaas kaa fal he. yah saraswati nadi se deb kimi kii duuri par he. jain mandiron kii sankhya yahan ek sau se bhi adhik he, par ye vishesh kalatmak nahin hai. khaadi ke vyavsaay main idhar kaaphi unnati hui he.
