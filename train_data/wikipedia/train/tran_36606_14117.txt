 osmania general aspataal bharat ke sabase puraane aspataalon main se ek he, hyderabad main afazalganj main sthit he. hyderabad ke antim nizaam, mir osman ali khan ne isaki sthapana kii thi or unhi ke naam par iskaa naam rakhaa gaya he. yah aspataal telugana sarkaar dwaara chalaaya jaataa he, or yah rajya ke sabase bade aspataalon main se ek he. osmania medical college, jo osmania general aspataal se judaa hua he, kii sthapana 1846 main hui thi or yah duniya ke sabase puraane chikitsa shaikshik sansthaanon main se ek he.
 durbhagyavash aazaadee ke baad yah aspataal bhrasht adhikariyon kaa ek pramukh udhaaharan ban gaya he or pehle str banaakar nahin rakh sakaa.
