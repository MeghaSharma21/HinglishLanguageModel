 '''itihaas mujhe doshamukt karegaa''' fidel castro kaa 16 oktober 1953 ko diye gaye chaar-ghante ke bhaashan kaa antim vaakya or anuvarthi sheershak he. castro ne yah bhaashan 'monkada baiyrakon' par huye hamale ke baad isamen unka naam aane par nyaayalay main apane bachaav main kahe the. yadhyapi castro ke shabdon kaa koi bhi abhilekh nahin banaaya gaya, unhone baad main 26 july ke unke andolan ke ghoshnaa patr ko prakashit karan ke liye taiyaar kiya tha.
