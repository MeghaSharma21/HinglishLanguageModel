 gailapagos dweepasmooh kaa dweep dakshin plaza santakruge dweep ke purvi tat se kuch duuri par sthit ek chota sa dweep he, iskaa kshetrafal 0.13 varg kilometre or adhiktam oonchai 23 meter he. dakshin plaza kaa naam ecuador ke purv raashtrapati general leonidas plaza ke sammaan main rakhaa gaya he.
 dakshin plaza dweep kaa nirmaan samudr ke neeche hue jwalamukhi visphot se upar uthte lava se hua he. apane chote aakaar ke baavjood yah prajatiyon kii ek badi sankhya kaa ghar he or apni asaadhaaran vanaspati ke liye prasiddh he. yahi kaaran he ki yah dweep aagantukon ke bich bahut lokapriy he. dakshin plaza kii vanaspati main openshia cactus or sesuvium paudhe shaamil hai jo lava sanrachanaon ke upar bichhe ek laal kaaleen ke samaan prathit hote hai.
 iske khadi dhalaanon par vibhinn pakshiyon ko dekha ja saktaa he jinamein, laal chonch vaali rekhiy pakshi ke ghonsle or ababil-puchh gal shaamil hai. lekin sabase adhik aanandadaayak anubhav to in khadi chattaanon ke shikhar par khadaa hokar neeche ke drishy ko niharna or in chattaanon ke aadhaar par chahalkadami karnaa he. sesuvium ke paudhe mausam ke anusaar apni rangath badalte hai, jahaan barsaat ke mausam main yah gahare hare rang ke hote hai vahiin sookhe ke mausam main inkaa rang badalakar naarangi or bangnee ho jaataa he. dweep par ek badi basti sthaliy gohon kii bhi he.
