 vishwa kii sabase choti kaan kii mashin nainoplag viksit kiya gaya he. 
 shodhakartaaon kii ek antarrashtriya team ne vishwa kii sabase choti kaan kii mashin viksit kii. iskaa naam ‘nainoplag’ rakhaa gaya. jo bahere logon ko sunne main sahaayta pradaan karti he. angrezi akhbaar times of india dwaara disambar 2014 ke chauthe saptaah main is khabar ko prakashit kiya gaya. 
 nainoplag kii pariklpana sabase pehle nevena jivik ne kii. uske baad audyogic diziner jong lee, audio abhiyantaa malden stawri or vidyut abhiyantaa zoran marinovi dwaara nishpaadan ke liye prayog main laayaa gaya. 
 nainoplag itani choti he ki isko aasaani se dekha yaa khoja nahin ja saktaa. is nainoplag kii digining kaa vittaposhan indigogo dwaara kii gai. indigogo vittaposhan karne vaala ek manch he. 
 is divaais kaa vikaas america kii photonix andure nanobateri dwaara kiya gaya ko naino takanic ke dwaara kiya gaya. ke kshetra main pragati ke dwaara vishesh rup se america kii photonix andure nanobateri dwaara hi sambhav banaaya gaya he jo ki ret ke ek dane ke aakaar ke samaan he or richarging ke bina chah din tak kaam karne ke liye sakasham he.
