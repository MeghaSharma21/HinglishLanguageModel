 mullaperiyar baandh yaa mulliperiyar baandh bhartiya rajya kerala sthit ek rajgiri, gurutveey baandh he. yah samudr tal se 881 me kii oonchaai par pashchimi ghat kii ilaychi pahadiyaan ke madhya thekkadi, idukki jila main sthit he. iskaa nirmaan 1887 se 1895 ke madhya jon peniquik dwaara madras presidency ke purvi kshetron ko jal pradaan karne ke ek samjhaute ke tahat hua tha. isaki aadhaar se 53.6 me oonchaai tathaa lambaai 365.7 me he. thekkadi sthit periyar rashtriya udyaan is baandh ke jalashay ke chaaron ore sthit he. baandh kerala main sthit he, magar iskaa sanchaalan tamilnadu dwaara kiya jaataa he, jabaki periyar nadi kaa jalgrahan kshetra 5398 km2 he jisame se keval 114 km2 tamilnadu rajya main padataa he. mullaperiyar baandh kaa hi sampoorn jalgrahan kshetra kerala main hi padataa he. kuch reporton ke anusaar 21 navambar 2014 ko baandh kaa jalastr 142 feet pahunch gaya jo 35 saalon kaa sabase adhik jalastr tha. jalashay ne phir se 15 agast 2018 ko 142 feet kaa adhiktam jalastr chhu liya jo 2018 kerala baadh kaa pramukh kaaran bana.
