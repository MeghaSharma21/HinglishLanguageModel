 blatistantan rajnatik rup se vivaadit kshetra he jo karakoram ke dakshin tathaa sindhu nadi ke uttar main sthit he. yah atyant parvatiy kshetra he or is kshetra kii ausat oonchaai 3350 meter se adhik he. ise 'laghu tibet' bhi kehete hai. iskaa kshetrafal lagbhag 27,400 kimi2 he. is kshetra main balati bhashaa boli jaati he jo tibeti lipi main likhi jaati he. is kshetra ke adhikansh log musalmaan hai. skardu tathaa kargil yahan ke mukhya kasbe hai.
 is kshetra kaa adhikansh bhaag pakistan ke adheen he or kuch bhaag bharat ke adheen . yah kshetra sindhu nadi kii ghaatiyon se bana he. blatistan ke dakshin-purv main bharat or pakistan ke bich kii niyantran rekha he.
