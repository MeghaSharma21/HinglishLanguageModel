 bhartiya kanoon britani kanoon par aadhaarit he. angrezo ne ise pehali baar apane shasankaal ke dauraan lagu kiya. angrezo dwaara lagu kiye kai adhinium or adhyadesh aaj bhi prabhaavasheel hai.
 bhartiya savindhan ke lekhan ke dauraan isamen ireland, sanyukt raashtra america, britain or france ke kanoonon ko samahit kiya gaya tha. bhartiya kanoon sanyukt raashtra ke maanavadhikaar or vaataavaran sanmbadhi dishanirdeshon ke anuroop he. isamen kuch antarrashtriya kanoonon, jaise boudhik adhikaron aadi, ko bharat main lagu kiya gaya he.
 bhartiya naagarik kanoon ek jatil kanoon he jisamen prayek dharm-vishesh ke apane kanoon hai. adhikansh rajyon main vivaah or talaak ke liye panjikaran aavashyak nahin he. hindu, musalmaan, isai, sikh va anya dharmon ke apane kanoon hai. iskaa akelaa apwaad goa rajya he jahaan portugali samaan naagarik samhita prabhavi he or sabhi dharmon ke liye vivaah, talaak or god lene sambandhi ek jaise kanoon hai.
