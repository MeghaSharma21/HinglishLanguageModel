 dow reba som ek bhartiya academik, itihaaskaar, lekhak or shastreey gayak he. vah kolkata main 'saanskrithik sambandhon ke liye bhartiya parishad' rabindranath tagore centre ke nideshak hai.
 reba som rabindrasangit kii ek prashikshit gayak bhi he. unki compact discs, rabindranath tagore ke chaynit geeton main unke angreji anuwaad or tagore ke geeton ke lipyantaran shaamil hai.
 1971 main videsh seva adhikari himachal som se vivaahit hone ke baad reba ne duniya bhar main posting main yatra kii he, jisamen brazil, denmark, pakistan, america, bangladesh, canada, laos, italy shaamil hai. unki rome pichhali posting thi jahaan unke pati bharat ke rajdoot the. unke do bete vishnu som hai, jo bharat ke agrani televijan patrkaar or samachar ankar or new york sthit ek nivesh banker abhishek som hai.
