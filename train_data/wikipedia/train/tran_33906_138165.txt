 madhupatr yaa stevia suryamukhi kul kaa ek padap he. 
 iskaa vanaspatik naam stavia rebodiana he or yah esteresi kul kaa sadsy he. yah purvottar paragway, america, brazil, japan, korea, taiwan or dakshin-purv asia main paaya jaataa he. stavia se ribiana naam kaa shunya-calories sweetner taiyaar kiya jaataa he jisamen mukhya rup se staviole glicosaida hota he. stavia main flavinoid or tarpin bhi prachur maatra main paae jate hai. iske anya rasinic ghatakon main staviosida shaamil he jo duniya kaa sabase meetha prakritik sweetner he. yah chini se 250 guna or sukroj se 300 guna zyada meetha hota he. mukhya rup se stavia kii pattiyon kaa istemaal hota he. yah daanton ko sadane se bachaata he. daanton par jamane vaali parat ko kam karata he. yah ek aisa sweetner he jo daanton ke liye phydemand hota he. stavia maansik sajagata badhaata he, thakaan mitaatha he, pachan kriya main sahaayak hota he or raktadab ko thik karata he. stavia kaa istemaal herbal utpaadon ko swaadisht or khushboodaar banane main bhi kiya jaataa he.
