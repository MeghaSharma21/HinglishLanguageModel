 sandeep lamichaane ek nepali cricket khilaadi hai, jo ki nepal kii rashtriya cricket team ke liye khelte hai. vah mukhyatah leg brake gendbaaj hai.
 sandeep lamichaane kaa janm nepal ke syanja jile main hua tha. bachapan main sachin tendulkar va shen worne unke adarsh the.
 unhone ne 16 april 2016 ko namibia ke viruddh 2015-17 isisi vishwa cricket lig se list e cricket se padaarpan kiya. apane list e padaarpan se purv ve 2016 under 19 cricket vishwa cap ke liye nepal under-19 cricket team main chune gayi the. unhone is pratiyogita ke duusare hi match main ireland under 19 cricket team ke viruddh 32wein over main lorson takar, adam denisan tathaa fiachara takar ko out karke hetric lee. ve under-19 cricket vishwa cap ke itihaas main hetric lene vaale paanchaven khilaadi bane. is match main unhone 27 ran 5 wicket liye jo ki pratiyogita kaa sarvshereshth gendbaaji pradarshan tha tathaa is pradarshan ke liye unhen main of da match kaa puraskaar diya gaya. ve is pratiyogita main 17.07 kii ausat tathaa 21.9 ke strike rate ke saath 14 wicket lekar duusare sarvaadhik wicket lene vaale khilaadi bane. sitambar 2016 main unhen australia cricket team ke purv captain maaikel clark kaa patr mila, jisame unhen sidney sthit western saherbs distric cricket club se khilne kaa nyauta mila.
 janvari 2018 main unhen 2018 indian premier lig kii nilaamee main unhen rakhaa gaya jahaan unka aadhaar mulya 20 lakh bhartiya rupe tha. unhen dilli dairedevills ne aadhaar mulya par kharid liya or ve ipel main khilne vaale pehle nepali khilaadi bane. 
 isi maheene ve isisi vishwa cricket lig divijan do 2018 ke liye nepali cricket team main chune gayi. lamichaane ko is pratiyogita main player of da toornament kaa puraskaar mila kyonki unhone 6 maichon main sarvaadhik 17 wicket liye the.
 march 2018 main unhone carabian premier lig main khilne ke liye saint kits and nevis patriots club ke saath $5000 kaa anubandh kiya.
