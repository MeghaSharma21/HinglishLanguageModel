 westeindies cricket team sitambar 2017 main ireland kaa daura karne ke liye ek divasiy antarrashtriya match khilne ke liye nirdhaarit he. yah ireland kaa pehla oriai match hoga kyonki ve june 2017 main ek purna sadsy paksh ban gaye the. jab team 2015 cricket vishwa cap ke samooh charan main mili, to tiimon ne pichley match kaa saamana kiya. haalanki, barish or ek geela outfield ke kaaran bold gend ke bina match ko bulaayaa gaya tha. westeindies ko ab 2018 cricket vishwa cap kwalifir se bachane ke liye sitambar main england ke khilaaf paanch vanade khilne ke baad aparajit rahane kii jarurat he, or 2019 cricket vishwa cap ke liye sidhe arhata praapt karne ke liye.
