 toofaan yaa aandhi prithvi yaa kisi anya grah ke vayumandal main uttejnaa kii sthiti ko kehete hai jo aksar sakht mausam ke saath aati he. isamen tej hawaayen, ole girnaa, baari barish, baari barfabaari, badalon kaa chamakna or bijli kaa chamakna jaise mausami gatividhiyaan dikhti hai. aamtaur par toofaan aane se saadhaaran jivan par bura asar padataa he. yatayaat or anya dainik kriyaaon ke alaava, baadh aane, bijli girane or himpat se jaan va maal kii haani bhi ho sakati he. registan jaise shushk kshetron main retile toofaan or samudron main unchi laharon jaisi khataranaak sthitiyaan bhi paidaa ho sakati hai. iske vipareet barish va himpat se kuch ilaaqon main sookhe kii samasya main madad bhi mil sakati he. mausam-vaigyanik anumaan lagaate hai ki har saal prithvi par lagbhag 1.6 karod garaj-chamak vaale toofaan aate hai.
