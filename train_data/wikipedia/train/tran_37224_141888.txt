 khalihaan, jise baadaa bhi bolte hai, aise sthaan yaa kaksh ko kehete hai jisamen pashu-maveshi, kheti ke auzaar or kati hui fasal ko rakhaa jaataa he. aam taur par khalihaan barish, barf, dhoop or aandhi se suraksha ke liye kisi kachi yaa pakki chat se dhake hue hote hai. dhyaan den ki kuch sandarbhon main khalihaan or baadaa keval us makaan ko hi nahin balki uske ird-gird kii bina dhaki hui zamin ko bhi kehete hai jahaan fasal or hal vagairah pade rahete hai.
 khalihaanon ko angrezi main "barn" or farsi main "ambaar" yaa "kiraman" kehete hai.
 khalihaanon kii banaavat kshetra par nirbhar karti he. inhen pathar, mitti, ghaas or lakdi sabhi se banaaya jaataa he. aadhunik yug main khalihaanon kaa dhaancha banane ke liye kabhi-kabhi lohe kaa bhi prayog hota he. jin jagahon par khalihaan ghaas yaa lakdi ke bane hote hai vahaan hameshaa aag kaa dar rahata he, kyonki us se andar rakhi hui fasal nasht ho jaati he or kisaan ke jaanavar bhi maare ja sakate hai.
 bhartiya upmahadweep kii lok-samskruti main khalihaan kii chhavi kaa bahut mahatv he. agar kisi ke jivan main bahut dhan-daulat he to kaha jaataa he ke uske "ambaar bhare hue hai", jo us krishi-jeevani kii taraf ishaara he jisamen anaaj or jaanavar se bhare khalihaan hi dhan-sampati kaa sanket the. sher-o-shaayari main dil kii tulana aksar ek kiraman se kii jaati he or premika kii nazar kii tulana bijli yaa ek angaare se jo use jalaakar raakh kar detii he or vyakti ko barabaad kar detii he. udahaaran ke liye -
