 divine gyaan, ilham athwa shruti dharamshastra tathaa pratyayvaadee dharmik darshan kii aadharbhoot avadharana he. ilham rahasyamay prabodhan kii sankriya main alokik yatharth ke atindriya sangyaan kii abhivyakti he. ilham kii charchaa mukhyatah sami dharm ke pavitragranthon main kii gayi he. samasamyik dharamshastra yah daawa karte hue ki ilham tarkbuddhi ke vipareet nahin he, is vichaar ko aadhunik rup dene kaa prayaas karata he. aisi manyata he ki, samakaleen dharmik dhaaraon main ilham ke pratyay ke kaaran ishwarwad kii darshanik pairvi main atarkbuddhivaad kii bhoomika me vruddhi ho rahi he.
