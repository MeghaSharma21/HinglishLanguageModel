 alampur bhartiya rajya telugana ke jogalba gadhwal jile kaa ek shahar he. yah ek dharmik mahatv kaa shahar he jo pavitra maani jane vaali nadiyon tungabhadra or krishna ke sangam par sthit he or ise dakshin kaashi kii sangya di jaati he tathaa ise prasiddh shaiva tirth shrisalam kaa pashchimi dwaara bhi kaha jaataa he. 
 skanda puraana main alampur mandir kii pavitrataa kaa ullekh kiya gaya he. alampur main pramukh devataaon main brahmeshwar or jogulamba hai. yah nalmala pahadiyon se ghira hua he. alampur tungabhadra nadi ke baayen kinare par sthit he.
