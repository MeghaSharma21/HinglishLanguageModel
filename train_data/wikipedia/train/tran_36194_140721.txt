 spring temple buddha, vairochan buddha kii ek sabase vishalkay buddha pratimaa he, jo ki henon ke jaokun kasbe main, china main sthit he. yah fodushaan sinik aria main, neshnal frive nambar 311 main sthit he.
 yah buddha murti 128 meter unchi he jisamen ki 20 meter unchaa camel-singhaasana bhi shaamil he, tathaa yah sansar kii sabase unchi pratimaa he. yadi iske 25 meter unche aadhaar/bhawan ko bhi gin liya jaye to isaki kul oonchaai 153 meter ho jaati he. oktober 2008 kii sthiti ke anusaar iske aadhaar ko nai shakl di ja chunki he or ab isaki oonchaai 208 meter ho gai he. is pratimaa ke neeche buddha math - vihaar he.
 spring temple buddha ke nirmaan kii yojana kii ghoshnaa afghanistan main taliban dwaara bamiyaan ke buddha pratimaaon ke dhwans ke turant baad kii gayi thi. china ne afghanistan main buddha dharohar ke yojanabaddh vinaash kii bhartsana kii thi.
