 antarrashtriya prakruti sangrakshan sangh, prakritik sansadhano ke sangrakshan ke liye samarpit ek antarrashtriya sanghathan he. sanghathan kaa ghoshit lakshya, vishwa kii sabase vikat paryaavaran or vikaas sambandhi chunautiyon ke liye vyaavhaarik samaadhaan khojane main sahaayta karnaa he. sangh vishwa ke vibhinn sangrakshan sanghathanon ke network se praapt jaankaree ke aadhaar par "laal suchi" prakashit karata he, jo vishwa main sabase adhik sankatagrast prajatiyon ko darshaati he.
