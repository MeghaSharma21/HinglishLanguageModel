 jitin prasad ke pita jitendra prasad bharat ke tatkaaleen pradhanmantri rajeev gandhi, pi.ve.narsimha raav ke rajanithik salaahkaar ,uttar pradesh congress cameti ke adhyaksh tathaa bhartiya rashtriya congress ke upaadhyaksh rah chuke hai. jitin prasad ne apni praarambhik shiksha doon public skool tathaa snaatak main dilli vishvivadyalay se bi.com tathaa antarrashtriya prabandhan santhaan se MBA kiya he.
  sarvapratham jitin prasad ne san 2001 main bhartiya yuva congress main sachiv bane,san 2004 main apane grih loksabhaa seat, shahjahanpur se 14 veen loksabhaa chunaav main kismat aajamaayi tathaa vijay bhi praapt hui.pehali baar jitin prasad san 2008 main  kendriya rajya ispaat mantri niyukt kiya gaya.
 uske baad san 2009 main jitin prasad  15 veen loksabhaa chunaav loksabhaa dhaurhara se lade va 184,509 voton se vijayi bhi hue.jitin prasad 2009- 18 janvari 2001 tak sadak parivahan evam rajmarg mantralay,19 janvari 2011- 28 oktober 2012 tak petroleum evam prakritik gas mantralay tathaa 28 oktober 2012 - may 2014 tak maanav sanshaadhan evam vikaas mantralay, upie sarkaar main kendriya rajyamantri rahen hai.jitin prasad shahjahanpur ,lakhimpur tathaa sitapur main kaaphi lokapriy netaa hai.jitin prasad ji ko uttar pradesh main shantipriy va vikasvaadi raajnneeti ke liye janaa jaataa he.
