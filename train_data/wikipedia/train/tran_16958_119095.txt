 bhartiya shailakartit sthapaty vishwa main paye jane vaale anya shailakartit sthapatyon kii tulana main bahut adhik vividhta liye hue or sankhya main bahut adhik he. shailakartit sthapaty, kathor prakritik shailon ko kaat-katkar banaaye gayi mandiron, bhawanon aadi ko kehete hai. bhartiya upmahadweep main paye jane vaale shailakartit sthapaty mukhyatah dharmik sthapaty he.
