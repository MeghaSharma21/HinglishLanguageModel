 fesial twachaa kaa soundarya badhaane kii ek prakriya he jisamen kai upachaar sammilit hai, jinamein pramukh hai- vaashp dena, chehre kii mask, loshan, kreemen, pil, maalis aadi.
 == phase pack ==facial karne ke baad chehre par phase pack lagaaya jaataa he. aisa karne se chehre kii twachaa main nikhaar aata he. phase pack munh or ankhon ko chhodkar baaki puure chehre par lagaaya jaataa he. chehre par chamak laane ke liye ghar kaa hi saamaan lekar phase pack banaaya jaataa he.
 1. thande pani yaa gulabjal main rooyee ke 2 tukade lekar ankhon par rakh len. aisa karne se ankhon ko aaraam miltaa he.
 2. phase pack ko puure chehre par 10 minute ke liye lagaakar rakh len. phir iske sookhne par geeli ruiya spanj se chehre ko saaf kar len.
 3. 1 chammach multani mitti, aadha chammach shahad or aadha chammach neembu ke ras ko gulabjal main milakar chehre par laga len. isko aadhe ghante sookhne ke baad gunagune pani se dho len.
 4. subah 1 gilaas gunagune pani main neembu kaa ras nichodkar pine se sharir ke saare jahrile padaarth baahar nikal jate hai.
 5. bhojan main taileey padaartho kaa seven kam karnaa chahie or salad or falon ke ras ko lenaa chahie.
