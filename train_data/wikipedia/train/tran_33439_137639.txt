 dong yen chaao, cham kaal kaa ek shilaalekh he jo dakshin-purvi asia ke itihaas kaa ek mahatvapurn dastauez he. yah madhya vietnam main 1936 main khoja gaya or ye puraani malaya bhashaa main likhaa he. isamen koi tithi ankit to nahin he par ise 11veen sadi kaa mana ja rahaa he, jo malay bhashaa ke aaj ke mukhya pradeshon - sumatra or malaysia - ke prachinatam puralekhon se bhi 300 saal purana he.
