 taoopo jwalamukheey kshetra new zealand ke uttar dweep main sthit ek bahut hi sakriya jwalamukheey kshetra he. iskaa naam taoopo jhil par padaa he jo yahan ke sabase bade jwalamukh-kund main sthit ek jhil he. yah jwalamukheey kshetra V kaa aakaar rakhata he or har varsh 8 millimetre adhik bad jaataa he. isamen ruapehu, nangarehoi or whakaari jaise kai prasiddh jwalamukhi khadey hue hai.
