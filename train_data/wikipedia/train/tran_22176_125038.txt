 aiwan-e-sadar pakistan ke raashtrapati kaa aadhikarik nivas va pradhan karyaalay he. yah pakistan kii rajdhani islamabad main aadhaarit he. yah purvottar islamabad main constichution avenue par sansad bhawan or sachivalay ke madhya main avasthit he. iske parisar main mukhya bhawan ke alaava raashtrapati kii adhikarini kii rihaishi colony bhi he.
 1988 main aiwan-e sadar ke puraa hone ke samay raashtrapati the gulam ishaak khan. raashtrapati parvej musharraf ne apane apane kaarykaal ke dauraan, apane nivas ke rup main is bhawan kaa istemaal nahin kiya, kyonki ve apane raashtrapatitv ke dauraan, senadhyaksh bhi the, atah unka aadhikarik nivas, rawalpindi kaa army house tha. pakistan ke purv raashtrapati asif ali jaradaari ne apane udghaatan se pehle din par aiwan-e sadar main le jaaya gaya he.
