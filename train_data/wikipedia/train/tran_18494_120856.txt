 bushara ijaz pakistan kii prasiddh lekhika he. 
 vah syra, galpkar or rosenamaa 'nai baat' kii kollam nigaar he. bushara ijaz pakistani punjabi sahitya main mahatvapurn yogadan diye hai. jisane pichley thode samay main hi purvi punjabi ke samikshakon kaa dhyaan khinchaa he. vah mul rup se urdu bhashaa kii lekhika he. parantu purvi or pashchimi punjab ke adaan-pradaan dwaara uplabdh maukon ne use urdu se punjabi main aane hetu prerit kiya. bushara ijaz kaa janm 18 june 1959 ko kot fazal, zila sargodha, pakistani punjab main hua hai. saatvin jamaat main padhate 12 saal kii umr main uskaa vivaah choudhary izaaj ahamed se ho gaya. bacchon kii padhai ke liye vah lahore aa gai. usne apni padhai bhi shuru kar di. vah bi.e. tak talim hasil kii.
 bushara ijaz ne apana sahityak safar 1989-90 se shuru kiya. likhane kii shuruuaat usne urdu zabaan main kii thi. ijaz kii zindagi kii ahm ghatana choti umr main shaadi kaa ho janaa or maan se dur hona tha. yah akelaapan usko shaayari kii dargaha tak le gaya. vah kahati he, "maan kii mohabbat uski lekhnee kaa mukhya mudda he".
