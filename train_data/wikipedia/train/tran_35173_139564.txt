  mir barkat ali khan mukaram jahaan asaf jahaan aathvin, siddiki beyaphendi, kam aupchaarik rup se "mukaram jahaan" ke rup main janaa jaataa he, 1967 main apane dada kii mrityu par sheershak hyderabad ke nizaam banaaya gaya.
 unke mahaan raajnneeti rustam-e-dauran, arustu-e-jaman, waal maamluk, asaf jahaan aathvin, mujffar ul-maamlik, nizaam ul-mulk, nizaam ud-daula, nawab mir barakat 'ali khan siddiki bikendi bahadur, sipaah salaar, fath jang, hyderabad or beraar ke nizaam.
 vah aaj bhi neeche likhe ke maalik he:
 apane dada kii tarah, 1980 ke dashak tak mukaram bharat main sabase amir vyakti the. haalanki, 1990 ke dashak main unki adhikansh pitru sampattiyaan bharat sarkaar dwaara chin liya gaya.
