 madhavi saradesai ek bhartiya academik thi, jo konkani saahitiyik jarnal jaag ke sampadak thi or lekhika bhi, jo mukhya rup se goa main konkani bhashaa main kaam karti thi. vah goa vishvavidhyalay ke konkani vibhaag kii pramukh thi. inke dwaara rachit ek niabandh-sangrah manthan ke liye unhen san 2014 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
 unhone angreji main piechdi kii degree praapt kii konkani par lexical prabhaavon kii tulanaatmak bhashavaigyanik or saanskrithik adhyayan par. saradesai ne apni praathmik shiksha ko konkani madhyam se kiya tha or chaugule mahavidhyalay, madagaanv se angreji or darshan main snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii. cancer ke saath ek nishchit ladaai ke baad unka 22 disambar, 2014 ko nidhan ho gaya.
