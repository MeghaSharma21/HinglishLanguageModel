 18agast 1945 ke baad kaa subhaashachandr bose kaa jivan/mrityu aaj tak ansulajhaa rahasy bana hua he. 18 agast 1945 ko unke atibhaarit japani vimaan durghatnagrast hua tha. yah durghatana japan adhikrut formosa main hui thi. usamen netaji mrityu ke pash se surakshit batch gayi the yaa nahin, iske bare main kuch bhi spasht nahin he. haalanki, unke adhiktar samarthak, mukhyatah bengal main, is samay ko tathyaheen or mrityu ke kaaran nahin hona bataakar asweekaar karte hai. unke nidhan ke kuch hi ghanton main shadyantrkaari siddhanth prakat hone lage or tatpashchaat lambi avadhi tak jari rahi evam bose ke bare main samrik mithkon ko astitvamay banaate rahe. swatantraaa ke baad bharat sarkaar jaanch ke liye teen baar ayog gathit kiye jinamein se do ne vimaan durghatana main maut kii pushti kii lekin tisare ke anusaar bose ne apane aap ko nakli maut di. yah report ko sarkaar ne bina kisi kaaran ke asweekrut kar di.
