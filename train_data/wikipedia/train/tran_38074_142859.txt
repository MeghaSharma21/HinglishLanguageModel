 bharat china ke baad vishwa kaa doosraa sabase bady jansankhya vaala desh he. bharat kii vibhinnataaon se bhari janata main bhashaa, jati or dharm, saamaajik or raajnneetik sanghathan ke mukhya shatru hai. mumbai, dilli, kolkata or chennai, bharat ke sabase bane mahanagar hai. 
 bharat main 64.8 pratishat sakshartaa he jisame se 75.3 % purush or 53.7% striyaan sakshar he. ling anupaat kii drushti se bharat main prayek 1000 purushon ke peeche maatr 933 mahilayen he. kaary bhagidaari dar 39.1% he. purushon ke liye yah dar 51.7% or striyon ke liye 25.6% he. bharat kii 1000 jansankhya main 22.32 janmon ke saath babbti jansankhya ke aadhe log 22.66 varsh se kam aayu ke hai. 
 yadhyapi bharat kii 80.5 pratishat jansankhya hindu he, 13.4 pratishat jansankhya ke saath bharat vishwa main musalamaanon kii sankhya main bhi indonesia or pakistan ke baad tisare sthaan par he. anya dharmavalambiyon main isai, sikh, buddha, jain, ayyavali, yahudee, parsi, ahmadi or bahaai aadi sammilit hai. 
 bharat chaar mukhya bhashaa sutron, indo-european, draviniyan, sino-tibeton or ausatro-agiatic kaa bhi strot he. bharat kaa samvidhan kul 23 bhashaaon ko manyata detaa he. hindi or angreji kendriya sarkaar dwaara sarkari kaamkaaj ke liye upyog kii jaati he. sanskrit, tamil, kannada or telegu jaisi ati pracheen bhashaaain bharat main hi janmi hai. kul milakar bharat main 1652 se bhi adhik bhashaaain evam boliyaan boli jaatin hai.
