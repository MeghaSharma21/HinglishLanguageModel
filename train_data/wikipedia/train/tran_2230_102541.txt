 sonpur mela
 melon main bhartiya samskruti kii jhalak pai jaati he. in melon main saamaajikta, samskruti aadi kaa adwitiya sammilan hota he. madhyapradesh main 1,400 sthanon par meley lagate hai. ujjain jile main sarvaadhik 227 meley or hoshangabad jile main nunatam 13 meley yojit hote hai. march, april or may main sabase zyada meley lagate hai, iskaa kaaran ye ho saktaa he ki is samay kisaanon ke paas kam kaam hota he. june,july, agast ore sitambar main nahin ke baraabar meley lagate hai. is samay kisaan sabase adhik vyast hote hai or barish kaa mausam bhi hota he. madhyapradesh ke mukhya meley nimnanusaar he:-
