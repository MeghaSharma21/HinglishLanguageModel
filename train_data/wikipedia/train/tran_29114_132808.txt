 kremian khaanat ek khaanat thi jisapar san 1441 se 1783 isvi tak kremian tartar logon kaa raaj rahaa tha. iske khan shaasha toqa taimur ke vanshaj the jo swayan jochi khan kaa tehraavaan putr or mongol samrajya ke sansthaapak changez khan kaa pota tha. sunaharaa urdu naamak khaanat ke ant hone par jitani bhi tartar khaanatein ubharin unamem se kremian khaanat hi sabase dderghaayu thi. iskaa raaj aadhunik ukraine ke kremia prayadweep or uske kuch padosi ilaaqon par tha. jab turki se ubharkar is puure kshetra main usamaani samrajya shaktishaalee bana to kremian khaanat uske adheen ek rajya ban gaya or uske dwaara surakshit rakhaa jane laga. usamaani sultan kremian kaanon ke saath adheen raajaaon jaisaa bartaav kam or dost jaisaa bartaav zyaada karte the. 18veen sadi main roosi samrajya ke ubharne par isaki zameenon par rus ne qabza kar liya or khaanat kaa ant ho gaya.
