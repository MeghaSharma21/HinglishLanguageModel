 vietnami lipi vietnami bhashaa ko likhane ke liye prayukt aadhunik lipi he. yah latin lipi par aadhaarit he, or vishesh rup se portugali varnmaala par. isamen kuch diagraph tathaa axent chihn jodakar yah lipi bani he jisamen chaar kaa prayog atirikt dhwani ke liye kiya jaataa he jabaki anya paanch kaa upyog prayek shabd kaa 'tone' bataane ke liye kiya jaataa he. vietnami varnmaala apni diatrix kii adhikta ke kaaran aasaani se pahachaani jaati he.
 vietnami varnmaala main 29 varna he.
 inke alaava nimnlikhit 10 digraph/trigraph bhi prayog kiye jate hai-
 latin varnmaala ke ye varna - "F", "J", "W" or "Z" vietnami bhashaa ke lekhan main upyog nahin hote balki videshi shabdon ke lekhan main hi inkaa istemaal hota he.
 vietnami lekhan main vartanee or uccharan main sidhaa sambandh nahin he balki jatil sambandh he. kuch sthitiyon main ek hi varna anya dhvaniyon ko nirupit karata he; duusari taraf do yaa adhik varna ek hi dhwani ke liye prayukt hote ham.
