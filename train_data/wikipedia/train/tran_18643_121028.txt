 qaraar patr kisi ke dwaara dalal ke sahyog se kiye gaye len-dein kii pushti karata he. yah kraykartta or uske dalal ke bich vyapaar niptaare ke sandarbh main prabhavi rishte kii sthapana karata he. iske dwaara daavon, vivaadon yaa antar sambandhi shikayaton ke niptaare main sahaayta milti he. dalal ke viruddh shikaayat karne ke liye pehali shart qaraar patr kaa hona he. qaraar patr main sujhaaye gaye kram main adhikrut adhikari dwaara hastakshar tathaa usamen len-dein sambandhi sabhi jaankariyaan honi chahie. isse yah bhi jaankaree hoti he ki kis tithi ko kharidari kii gai. isaki ek copy client ko di jaati he or duusari brokar ko. kisi vivaad, clem ke niptaare ke samay qaraar patr kaaphi sahaayak hota he. isse yah sabit hota he ki sheyaron kaa aadan-pradaan enesai or registerd sab-brokar ke dwaara hua he. isamen sebi kaa kharidari karne vaale sadsy ko jari kiya gaya registration number hona chahie. kharidari kii jaankaree, order number, kharidari number, samay, security naam, rate, brokerej, setlement number aadi kii jaankaree honi chahie.
