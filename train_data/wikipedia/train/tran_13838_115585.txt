 swatantraaa aadhunik kaal kaa pramukh rajnatik darshan he. yah us dasha kaa bodh karaati he jisamen koi raashtra, desh yaa rajya dwaara apni ichha ke anusaar kaary karne par kisi duusare vyakti/ samaaj/ desh kaa kisi prakaar kaa pratibandh yaa manaahi nahin hoti.arthath swatantra desh/ raashtra/ rajya ke sadsy swashaasan se shasit hote hai. swatantraaa kaa vilom shabd 'paratantraa' he. jaruri nahin ki 'swatantraaa' kaa arth 'aazaadee' bhi ho.
