 trishanku kaa arth he niraadhaar lataka hua.
 trishanku ikshvaku vansh kaa ek raja tha jise rishi vishwamitra ne sasharir swarg bheja tha. devraj indra ne use swarg se waapas prithvi kii ore dhakel diya. neeche girte huye trishanku ko rishi vishwamitra ne bich main hi lataka kar uske liye swarg kaa nirmaan kiya tathaa vah apane swarg ke saath aaj bhi vaastavik swarg or prithvi ke bich lataka hua he. isi kaaran se niraadhaar latakane kaa bhav pradarshit karne ke liye trishanku shabd kaa prayog hota he.
