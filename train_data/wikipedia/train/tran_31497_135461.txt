 dioni hamaare saura mandal ke chathe grah shani kaa chauthaa sab se bada upgrah he. puure saura mandal main yah 15vaa sab se bada upgrah he or apane se chote saare upgrahon ke mili hue dravyaman se bada he. vaise to yah adhiktar pani kii barf kaa bana he, lekin titan or ansaoods ke baad shani kaa teesraa sab se ghanatv vaala upgrah he, jis se yah anumaan lagaaya jaataa he ke isaki banavaat main aadhe se thodaa kam hissaa patthariilaa he. jis disha main yah parikrama karata he, us taraf ke rukh par ulkapindon ke takaraav se bane gaye kaafii prahaar cretar hai, jabaki duusare rukh par chamakati hui barf kii chattaanon ke jaale bichhe hue hai.
 dioni kaa vyas lagbhag 1,122 kimi he. tulana ke liye prithvi ke chandramaa kaa vyas lagbhag 3,470 kimi he, yani qareeb dioni se tiguna.
 dioni ko angrezi main "Dione" likhaa jaataa he. dioni pracheen unani dharmik kathaon kaa ek bhimkaay paatr tha.
