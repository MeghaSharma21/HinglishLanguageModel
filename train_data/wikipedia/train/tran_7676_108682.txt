 nirdeshaank: 22°46′N 73°43′E / 22.77°N 73.72°E / 22.77; 73.72
 godhra bharat ke gujarat praant kaa ek shahar he. 2001 bharat kii janaganana ke rup main, godhra 121,852 kii aabaadi thi.
 godhra kaa naam sahasaa tab saamne aayaa jab vahaan 27 farvari 2002 ko railway stesan par sabarmati train ke es-6 coach main bheed dwaara aag lagaae jane ke baad 59 karsevakon kii maut ho gai. iske parinamswaroop puure gujarat main sampradayik dange hona shuru ho gayi.
 27 farvari 2002 : godhra railway stesan ke paas sabarmati train ke es-6 coach main muslimon dwaara aag lagaae jane ke baad 59 karsevakon hinduo kii maut ho gai. is maamle main 1500 logon ke khilaaf praathmiki darj kii gai.
 28 farvari 2002 : gujarat ke kai ilaakon main danga bhadkaa jisamen 1200 se adhik log maare gaye.
 03 march 2002 : godhra train jalaane ke maamle main giraftaar kiye gaye logon ke khilaaf aatankwaad nirodhak adhyadesh lagaaya gaya.
 06 march 2002 : gujarat sarkaar ne kamishan of inquairi act ke tahat godhra cond or uske baad hui ghatnaon kii jaanch ke liye ek ayog kii niyukti kii.
 09 march 2002 : police ne sabhi aropiyon ke khilaaf bhadasn kii dhara 120-bi lagaaya.
 25 march 2002 : centre sarkaar ke dabaav kii vajah se sabhi aropiyon par se pota hataaya gaya.
 18 farvari 2003 : gujarat main bhaajapa sarkaar ke dobaaraa chune jane par aropiyon ke khilaaf phir se aatankwaad nirodhak kanoon laga diya gaya.
 21 november : ucchtam nyaayalay ne godhra train jalaae jane ke maamle samet dange se jude sabhi maamalon kii nyaayik sunwaayee par roak lagaai.
 04 sitambar 2004 : rajd netaa lalu prasad yadav ke railmantri rahane ke dauraan kedriya mantrimandal ke faisle ke aadhaar par ucchtam nyaayalay ke nyaayadhish yusi banerjee kii adhyakshata vaali ek samiti kaa gathan kiya gaya. is samiti ko ghatana ke kuch pahaluon kii jaanch kaa kaam saunpa gaya.
 21 sitambar : navgathit samprag sarkaar ne pota kanoon ko khatm kar diya or aropiyon ke khilaaf pota aaropon kii samiksha kaa faisla kiya.
 17 janvari 2005 : yusi banerjee samiti ne apni praarambhik report main bataaya ki es-6 main lagi aag ek ‘durghatana’ thi or is baat kii ashanka ko khaarij kiya ki aag bahari tatwon dwaara lagaai gai thi.
 16 may : pota samiksha samiti ne apni raay di ki aropiyon par pota ke tahat aarope nahin lagaae jaaen.
 13 oktober 2006 : gujarat ucch nyaayalay ne vyavastha di ki yusi banerjee samiti kaa gathan ‘avaidh’ or ‘asanvaidhanik’ he kyonki nanaavati-shah ayog pehle hi dange se jude sabhi maamle kii jaanch kar rahaa he. usne yah bhi kaha ki banerjee kii jaanch ke parinaam ‘amaanya’ hai.
 26 march 2008 : ucchtam nyaayalay ne godhra train main lagi aag or godhra ke baad hue dangon se jude aath maamalon kii jaanch ke liye vishesh jaanch ayog banaaya.
 18 sitambar : nanaavati ayog ne godhra cond kii jaanch saunpi or kaha ki yah purv niojit shadyantr tha or es6 coach ko bheed ne petrol daalkar jalaayaa.
 12 farvari 2009 : ucch nyaayalay ne pota samiksha samiti ke is faisle kii pushti kii ki kanoon ko is maamle main nahin lagu kiya ja saktaa he.
 20 farvari : godhra cond ke peediton ke rishtedaar ne aropiyon par se pota kanoon hataae jane ke ucch nyaayalay ke faisle ko ucchtam nyaayalay main chunauti di. is maamle par sunwaayee abhi bhi lambit he.
 01 may : ucchtam nyaayalay ne godhra maamle kii sunwaayee par se pratibandh hataaya or sibiai ke purv nideshak aarake raghavan kii adhyakshata vaale vishesh jaanch dal ne godhra cond or dange se jude aath anya maamalon kii jaanch main teji ai.
 01 june : godhra train cond kii sunwaayee ahmedabad ke sabarmati kendriya jail ke andar shuru hui.
 06 may 2010 : ucchtam nyaayalay sunwaayee adaalat ko godhra train cond samet gujarat ke dangon se jude nau samvedansheel maamalon main faisla sunaane se roka.
 28 sitambar : sunwaayee puri hui lekin sheersh adaalat dwaara roak lagaae jane ke kaaran faisla nahin sunaaya gaya.
 18 janvari 2011 : ucchtam nyaayalay ne faisla sunaane par se pratibandh hataaya.
 22 farvari : vishesh adaalat ne godhra cond main 31 logon ko doshi paaya, jabaki 63 anya ko bari kiya.
 1 march 2011: vishesh adaalat ne godhra cond main 11 ko faansee, 20 ko umrakaid kii sajaa sunai.
