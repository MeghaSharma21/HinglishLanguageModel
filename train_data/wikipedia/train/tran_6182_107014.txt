 vikaspedia bharat sarkaar dwaara shuru kii gayi ek website he jo vibhinn prakaar kii suchnaayen pradaan karti he. yah farvari 2014 main armbh kii gayi thi. is portal kaa vikaas 'bharat vikaas praveshdwar-ek rashtriya pahal' ke ek bhaag ke rup main saamaajik vikaas ke kaaryakshetron kii suchanaaen/ jaankariyaan or soochna evam praudyogiki par aadhaarit utpaada va sevaayen dene ke liye kiya gaya he. bharat vikaas praveshdwar, bharat sarkaar ke sanchaar evam soochna praudyogiki mantralay ke electronic or soochna praudyogiki vibhaag, kii ek pahal or pragat sanganan vikaas centre, hyderabad ke dwaara karyanvit he.
 yah websthal hindi, angreji, asamiya, marathi, bangla, telegu, gujarati, kannada, malayalam, tamil, marathi aadi 23 bhashaaon main he. vikaspedia main 6 alag-alag vishay hai, krishi, swasthya, shiksha, samaaj kalyaan, urja, or i-shashan.
