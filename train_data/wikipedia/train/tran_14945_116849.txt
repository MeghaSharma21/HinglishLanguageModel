 ek antar-akashgangiy tara aisa tara he jo kisi akashganga kaa hissaa nahin he. aise taare vigyaan main kaafii vivaad kaa vishay rahe hai or yah mana jaataa he ki ve do akashgangaon ke takaraane ke parinamswarup tayar hote hai.
 ek aam dharana kii taaren keval akashgangaon main paye jate hai, jhuthi siddh hui jab 1970 main antar-akashgangiy taron kii khoj hui. aise taare sabase pehle akashgangaon ke virgo cluster main paae gaye jahaan ab tin sau crores aise sitare dunde ja chuke he.
 aaj tak in taaro kii utpatti ek raaj hi bani hui he par aam taur par yah mana jaataa he ki do yaa adhik akashgangaon ke takaraane par kuch taare brahmad main fek die jate hai.
