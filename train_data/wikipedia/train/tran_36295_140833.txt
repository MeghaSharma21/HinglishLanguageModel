 angreji 'id' kaa tulya hindi.idan janmajaat prakruti kaa hota he tathaa isamen mukhya rup se vyakti kii mul vasanaye, pravritiyaan tathaa damit icchaayen aati hai. idan kisi bhi tarah kaa tanaav nahin sah saktaa he or bina kisi baadha yaa intjaar ke tatkaal anand, sukh va santushti praapt karnaa chahta he. yah purnatah achetan main kaam karata he.
 manovigyaan main ahan athwa "main", athwa "swa" kaa arth maanav kii un samast shareerik tathaa maansik shaktiyon se he jinke kaaran vah "par" arthath "anya" se bhinn hota he. manovishleshan main munashya kii ve shaktiyaan jo usko yatharthata ke anusaar vyavhaar karne ke liye prarit karti hai. manovaigyanikon kaa vichaar he ki "aham" or "par" kaa bodh tathaa vikaas saath-saath hota he.
 angreji 'super-igo' kaa tulya hindi.paraahan saamaajik manyataon, sanskaron va aadarshon se sambandhit hota he tathaa maanviya,saamaajik or rashtriya hit main vyakti ko tyaag va balidaan hetu tatpar karata he. paraham purnatah chetan se nirdhaarit hota he.
