 2016-2017 ke antarrashtriya cricket sizan sitambar 2016 se april 2017 tak kaa tha. is avadhi ke dauraan 41 test maichon, 87 ekadivasiy antarrashtriya, 43 twanti-20 antarrashtriya, 4 pratham shrenee maichon, 16 list e maichon, 41 mahila vanade international or 15 mahila tii-20 international khelaa gaya is sijan main hui 41 test maichon main se 3 din / raat test maichon kaa match rahaa. sizan pakistan ke saath shuru hua test cricket ranking, australia ne ekadivasiy ranking kii aguai kii, newjiland ne twanti -20 ranking kii aguwai kii or australia kii mahilaon ne mahila ranking kii aguwai kii.
 purushon kaa purna sadsy cricket newjiland ke bharat daure ke saath shuru hua. bharat ne test shrinkhalaa main newjiland par 3-0 kii jeet ke saath bharat ko pehle test ranking main sthanantarit kiya. bharat ke 500 wein test match main shrinkhalaa ke pehle test ke saath is daure main bhartiya cricket main kai mile kaa pathar bhi dikhaai die, shrinkhalaa kaa doosraa test bharat main bharat kaa 250 waan test rahaa, or bharat kaa 900 waan vanade pehla oriai tha. is sizan main ullekhneey hilite main bangladesh kii pehali test jeet england par 1-1, or dakshin africa ke australia main australia ke khilaaf lagaataar tisare test siries kii jeet se bani. haalanki, dakshin africa ke australia daure ke vivaad se peedit hone ke kaaran antarrashtriya cricket council ne gend par chhedchhad ke saath dakshin africa ke captain faf du plasis kaa aarope lagaaya. newjiland ne 1985 se pakistan ke khilaaf apni pehali test series jeet lee he. bharat ne 2008 ke baad pehali baar anthony di melo trophy ke liye chunaav lade england ke khilaaf test siries jiti. australia main yojit chapel-hadlee trophy ke dasven sanskaran, australia ne 3-0 se jeeta tha, or newjiland main yojit gyarahawein sanskaran, newjiland ne 2-0 se jeeta tha. bangladesh ne apana pehla test match bharat main khelaa, jisane 208 ran se jeet hasil kii. ek antarrashtriya toornament tha, zimbabwe main trikoniya shrinkhalaa thi jisamen srilanka or westeindies bhi shaamil the, jo srilanka ne jeeta tha. dakshin africa ke khilaaf twanti-20 shrinkhalaa 2-1 se jeeta jab srilanka kii pehali shrinkhalaa dakshin africa ke khilaaf kisi bhi praaroop main hui. dakshin africa ne baarah baar lagaataar ekadivasiy jeet darj kii jisamen ghar par australia or srilanka ke safedi shaamil the. sizan kii antim test shrinkhalaa main pakistan ne westeindies main apni pehali test shrinkhalaa jiti thi.
 purushon ke sahyogi or sahabaddh cricket main, intercantinental cap ke teen maichon or vishwa cricket lig championships ke chah maichon kaa aayojan hona tha. in maichon ke parinaam ab tak papua new gini ko vishwa cricket lig championships main pehali jagah or intercantinental cap main chauthe sthaan par dekhne ko mila he. atirikt antarrashtriya tour or toornament bhi sheersh sahyogiyon or sahyogi deshon main shaamil the: yuai banaam oman, honkong banaam papua new gini, afghanistan banaam ireland, 2017 desert tii 20 chalange, or 2016-17 sanyukt arab emirate tri-raashtra shrinkhalaa. afghanistan ne bangladesh ke khilaaf teen maichon kii shrinkhalaa bhi kheli, or ireland ne dakshin africa or australia ke khilaaf ekadivasiy match khele, donon ko ganvaa diya. iske alaava, 2019 cricket vishwa cap kii yogyata prakriya main ek or kadam sanyukt rajya america or oman ko vishwa cricket lig divijan 3 main padonnat kiye jane ke saath puraa kiya gaya tha. 
 is sizan main 2014-16 isisi mahila championships kaa samaapan hua, jisamen antim saat shrinkhalaa kii avadhi is avadhi main nirdhaarit kii gai thi. in maichon ke samaapan par, australia, england, newjiland or westeindies toornament kii sheersh chaar timen the, or isliye 2017 mahila cricket vishwa cap ke liye sidhi yogyata praapt kii. bangladesh, ireland, zimbabwe, thailand, papua new gini or scotland ke saath, neeche chaar tiimon ko mahila cricket vishwa cap kwalifire 2017 main rakhaa gaya he. kuch vivaad tha, haalanki, jaisaa ki bharat or pakistan ke bich kii shrinkhalaa is samay kii avadhi main nirdhaarit hona tha, lekin shrinkhalaa aage nahin badi thi. natijatan, isisi takneekee samiti ne yah faisla sunaaya ki bharat kii mahila team ne sabhi maichon ko jabt kar liya he, pakistan ko die jane vaale ank, prabhavi rup se bharat ko neeche chaar main bhej diya. is sijan ke dauraan 2016 mahila twanti-20 asia cap bhi khelaa gaya tha, bharat ne lagaataar chatha khitaab jeeta tha. bharat or bangladesh ke bich toornament ke salaami ballebaaj ne itihaas ko dekha, bangladesh ko 54 ranon tak pahunchaaya gaya, jo ki mahila twanti -20 international main sabase kam he. haalanki, yah record keval chaar dinon tak khadaa tha, jaisaa ki baad main toornament main tha, bangladesh ne 44 ran par pakistan ko out kar diya tha.
