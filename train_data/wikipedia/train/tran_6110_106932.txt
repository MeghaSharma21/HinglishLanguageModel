 aadim-hind-europea bhashaa, jise proto-indo-european bhashaa bhi kaha jaataa he, bhashavaigyanikon dwaara puure hind-europea bhashaa-parivaar kii sabhi bhashaaon kii ekmaatr pracheen janani bhashaa maani jaati he. mana jaataa he ke ise pracheen kaal main aadim-hind-europea log bolaa karte the, lekin yah bhashaa hazaron varsh purv hi puri tarah se lupt ho chuki thi. bahut si hind-europea bhashaaon ke sajaateey shabdon kii ek-dusre se tulana ke baad bhashavaigyanikon ne is lupt bhashaa kaa punarnirmaan kiya he jis se is ke shabdon kaa anumaan lagaaya ja saktaa he.
 bhashavaigyanik anumaan lagaate hai ke aadim-hind-europea bhashaa lagbhag 3700 ipu tak boli jaati rahi or phir uskaa vibhinn bhashaaon main khandan hone laga. is tithi ke bare main vidwaanon main hazaar varsh idhar-udhar tak kaa aapasi matbhed he. is baat par bhi kai avadharanaayen hai ke is bhashaa ko bolne vaale kahan rahete the, lekin bahut se pashchimi vidvan kurgan avadharana main vishwaas rakhte hai. kurgan avadharana ke anusaar is bhashaa ko bolne vaale aadim-hind-europea log purvi europe or pashchimi asia ke kuch hisson main faile hue pontic-caspian stapi ke kshetra main rahete the. aadhunik yug main wiliam jones pehle vidvan the jinhone pracheen sanskrit, pracheen unani, pracheen farsi or latin bhashaaon main samantaayein dekhakar yah daawa kiya tha ke ye sari ek hi aadim-hind-europea bhashaa se upji bhashaayen hai. beesvi shatabdi kii shuruyaat tak bhashavaigyanikon ke parishram se aadim-hind-europea bhashaa kaa chithran kaafii had tak kiya ja chukaa tha, jisame chote-mote sudhaar lagaataar hote rahe hai.
