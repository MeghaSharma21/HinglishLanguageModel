 pandit jagat narayan mullah apane samay main uttar pradesh ke prasiddh vakil or sarkari abhiyojak the. 'mullah' unka upnaam tha. wein 3 varsh tak lakhanu vishvavidhyalay ke upkulapati bhi rahe. rishte main jagat narayan mullah pandit jawahar laal nehru ke saale the.
 jagat narayan mullah ke pita pandit kaali sahay mullah uttar pradesh main sarkari seva main the. isiilie jagat narayan kii shiksha uttar pradesh main hi hui. unhone agra vishvavidhyalay se kaanoon kii pariksha uteern kii or lakhanu main vakaalat karne lage. sheeghra hi unki gananaa prasiddh vakeelon main hone lagi. apane samay ke pramukh vyaktiyon, jaise-pandit motilal nehru, babu ganga prasad verma, si. vaai. chintamani, bishan narayan dar aadi se unke ghanishtha sambandh the.
 1916 i. kii lakhanu congress kii swaagat-samiti ke adhyaksh vahi the. lagbhag 15 varshon tak lakhanu nagarpalika ke adhyaksh rahe. maantegue chamsford sudhaaron ke baad uttar pradesh concil ke sadsy nirvaachit hue or pradesh ke swytt shashan vibhaag ke mantri bane. jagat narayan mullah 3 varsh tak ve lakhanu vishvavidhyalay ke upkulapati bhi rahe.hunter cameti ke sadsy
 jab bharat main jaliyanvaala baag ke hatyakand sahit daman kaa nayaa daur shuru ho gaya tha or in ghatnaon kii jaanch ke liye sarkaar ne jo ‘hunter cameti’ gathit kii thi, uske teen bhartiya sadasyon main ek jagat narayan mullah bhi the. in teenon ne cameti kii report main apni asahmati darj kii thi.
