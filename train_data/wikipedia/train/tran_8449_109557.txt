 
 bhartiya ganda, jise ek sing vaala ganda bhi kehete hai, vishwa kaa chauthaa sabase bada jalchar jeev he. aaj yah jeev apane awasaiya kshetra ke ghat jane se sankatagrast ho gaya he. yah purvottar bharat ke assam or nepal kii tarai ke kuch sangrakshit ilaakon main paaya jaataa he jahaan isaki sankhya himalay kii talahati main nadiyon vaale vanyakshetron tak simit he. 
 itihaas main bhartiya ganda bhartiya upmahadweep ke sampoorn uttari ilaake main paaya jaataa tha jise sindhu-ganga-brahmaputra kaa maidan kehete hai. yah sindhu, ganga tathaa brahmaputra nadiyon ke maidani kshetron main, pakistan se lekar bhartiya-burma sarhad tak paaya jaataa tha or iske awasaiya kshetra main nepal, aaj kaa bangladesh or bhutan bhi shaamil the. aisa mana jaataa he ki yah burma, dakshini china tathaa indochina main bhi vicharan karata ho lekin yah siddh nahin ho paaya he. yah jati san 1600 tak uttar-pashchimi bharat or pakistan main aasaani se dekhi ja sakati thi, lekin iske turant baad is ilaake se vilupt ho gai. apane anya awasaiya kshetron main bhi yah san 1600 se 1900 tak tezi se ghate or beesvin sadi kii shuruyaat main yah viluptata kii kagaar main khadaa tha.
 ek anumaan ke mutabik aaj junglee halaat main keval 3000 se kuch adhik bhartiya gande bache hai jisamen se lagbhag 2000 to keval bharat ke assam main hi paye jate hai.
