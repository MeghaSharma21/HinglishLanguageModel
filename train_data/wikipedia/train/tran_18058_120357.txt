 vrindavandaas thakur bangala bhashaa main chaitanya bhagwat naamak granth ke rachayita hai. yah granth chaitanya mahaprabhu kaa jeevancharit he. yah bangala bhashaa kaa aadi kavy granth mana jaataa he.
 inke pita kumarhad nivasee baikunthanatha thakur the. navadweep main san. 1582 main inkaa janm hua. kuch din ke baad mata ke saath yah kumarhad laut gaye, jahaan inaki mata kaa bhi sharirant ho gaya. inhone 'chaitanya mangal' granth likhaa he, jo baad main chaitanya bhagwat naam se prasiddh hua. krishnadas kaviraj ne apane granth chaitanyacharitamrita main isaki badi prashansa kii he or kavi karnpur ne inhen veda vyas kaa avatar kaha he. antim avasthaa main ye vrundavan gaye.
 inaki anya rachanaayen hai shrinityanand charitamrita, anandalahari, tatvasaar, tatvavilas, bhakthiquintamani aadi.
