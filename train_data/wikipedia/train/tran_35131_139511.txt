 prakaash valve kii kriya ko praapt karne ke do buniyadi siddhant hai. pehla, lakshya tak pahunchne vaale prakaash kaa iske maarg se vikshepan karke or doosraa prakaash ko avaruddh karke .
 avaruddh vidhi kaa prayog drav crystal pradarshi screen, video prakshepak or pasch-prakshepan television main hota he.
