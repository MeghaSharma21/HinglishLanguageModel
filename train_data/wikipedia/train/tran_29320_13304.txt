 coke borok yaa tipracak bhashaa yaa tripuri bhashaa bharat ke tripura rajya ke tripuri samudaay or bangladesh ke kuch padosi kshetron main boli jane vaali bhashaa he. 'coke' kaa arth 'bhashaa' or 'borok' kaa arth 'log' hota he. yahan borok shabd se arth tripuri log he. coke borok tibeti-burmi bhashaa-parivaar kii sadsy he or assam main boli jane vaali bodo bhashaa or dimasa bhashaa kii nikat sambandhi he. coke borok ko kabhi-kabhi tripuri bhashaa bhi kah diya jaataa he.
 coke borok bhashaa main ginati' ko lekhamung kehete hai. mukhya sankhyaon ke liye coke borok main prayukt shabd neeche die gayi hai.
