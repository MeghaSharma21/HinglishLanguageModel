 hariharpur, jonpur, uttar pradesh, bharat
 hariharpur gaanv, uttar pradesh ke jonpur jile kaa kaaphi pichhadaa hua gaanv he. yahan kii kul aabaadi 3500 he. gaanv main ke log aaj bhi prayaapt shauchalay nahin he. is gaanv ko bharat kaa sabase sundar gaanv banane kaa sankalp patrkaar vijay yadav ne liya he. iske liye vah jila adhikari se lekar desh ke pradhanmantri tak se sampark kar rahe he. hariharpur ko adarsh gaanv hi nahin balki smart village banane kaa sapana he.
