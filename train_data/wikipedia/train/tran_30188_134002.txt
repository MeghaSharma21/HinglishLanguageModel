 antarrashtriya parmaanu urja abhikaran ek swytt vishwa sanstha he, jiskaa uddeshy vishwa main parmaanu urja kaa shantipurna upyog sunishchit karnaa he. yah parmaanu urja ke sainya upyog ko kisi bhi prakaar rokane main prayasarat rahati he. is sanstha kaa gathan 29 july, 1957 ko hua tha. iskaa mukhyalay vienna, austria main he. sanstha ne 1986 main rus ke cheranobal main hui nabhikeey durghatana ke baad apane nabhikeey suraksha kaarykram ko vistaar diya he. vartmaan main iske mahasachiv misr mul ke mohammad albaradei hai. albaradei ko sanyukt rup se 2005 kaa shanti nobel puraskaar diya gaya. iske sabase pehle mahasachiv dablyoo sterling kol the. ieie board ke 35 sadsy deshon main se 26 nabhikeey aapurtikarta samooh sadsy desh hai. 
 ieie sidhe sidhe sanyukt raashtra sangh ke adheen nahin he, lekin yah sanyukt raashtra mahasabha or suraksha parishad ko apni report detii he. is sanstha ke mukhyat: teen ang hai-
 board of garvanurs main sadasyon kii sankhya 35 hoti he, jinamein se 13 sadsy pichley board se liye jate hai, jabaki shesh 22 sadasyon kaa chunaav saamaanya sammelan dwaara hota he. board of garvanurs kaa mukhya kaary ieie kii nitiyon kaa nirdhaaran karnaa he.
 sanstha apane bajet kaa prastaav general conferes ke saamne rakhti he. iske alaava ise mahasachiv kaa bhi chunaav karnaa hota he. general conference kii prayek varsh sitambar maah main baithak hoti he, jisamen board of garvanurs dwaara prastaavit bajet or kaaryo kii sahmati pradaan kii jaati he. sachivalay ke adhyaksh mahasachiv hote hai. yah general conference or board of garvanurs dwaara laae gaye prastaavon ko kaaryrup main laane ke liye uttaradaayi hota he. is sanstha ke teen mukhya kaam hai-
