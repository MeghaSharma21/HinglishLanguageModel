 cricket ke khel main gend phenkane kii kriya yaa kala ko gendbaazi kaha jaataa he.
 gendbaazi kai prakaar se kii jaati he.
 iname se tej gendbaazi or phiraki gendbaazi iske do mul prakar he. teesraa prakaar madhyam tej gendbaaji he. yah do tareekon se kii sakati he. daahine haath se or baayen haath se. gendbaaji kaa mukhya udadeshy ballebaaj ko out karnaa or use ran banane se rokna hota he.
