 kaay-chikitsa ayurveda main varnit ashtaang chikitsa kaa ek vibhaag he. aadhunik arth main yah angreji ke general medicine kaa samanaarthi he. chikitsa main kaay-chikitsa or shalyachikitsa naamak do mukhya bhaag hai. 
 chikitsa ke drushtikon se ayurveda ko aath angon main vargeekrut kiya gaya he. ise "ashtaang ayurveda" kehete hai.
 1- shalya
 2- shaalakya
 3- kaay chikitsa
 4- bhoot vidya
 5- comarbhrity
 6- agad tantra
 7- rasayan
 8- bajeekaran
