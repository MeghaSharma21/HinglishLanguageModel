 tang rajya pracheen china ke jhou rajvansh ke antim bhaag main basant or sharad kaal or jhagadate rajyon ke kaal ke dauraan ek riyasat thi. yah 1046 isapurv se 414 isapurv tak chalaa. yah lu rajya ke adheen tha or aadhunik shandong praant ke dakshin bhaag main sthit tha. iskaa kshetra ab tangzou naamak shahari-zile main aata he. tang rajya ko aage chalakar yue rajya ne hadapkar apane kshetra main mila liya tha. bahut se chini log jinka paariwarik naam 'tang' hota he isi kshetra se hote hai. tang rajya prasiddh chini darshanik moji kii janmabhumi bhi thi.
