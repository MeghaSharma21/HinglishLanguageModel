 korku bharat ke madhya pradesh va maharashtra rajyon main boli jane vaali austro-asian bhashaa-parivaar kii munda shakha kii ek bhashaa he. ise korku samudaay ke log bolte hai, jo bhartiya samvidhan ke tahat ek anusuchit janatati hai. korkubhashiyon ke aasapaas gondi bhashaa bolne vaale basate hai, jinaki sankhya adhik he. aitihaasik rup se korku logon kaa nihaali bhashaa bolne waalon ke saath sambandh rahaa he, jo korku bolne waalon ke gaavon ke bheetar hi vishesh hisson main rahete hai.
 "koro" kaa arth "manushya" hota he or "kor-ku" kaa arth "manushyon vaali ".
