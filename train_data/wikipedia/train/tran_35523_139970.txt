 anokhelaal mishra prasiddh tabalavadak the. ve sangeet jagat main 'naa dhin dhin naa' ke jaadugar ke naam se prasiddh the. unka sambandh banaras gharaane se tha.
 pan. anokhelaal ji kaa janm san 1914 i. main bharat ke uttar pradesh ke gram-tajpur, tehsil-sakaldiha, jila-chandauli, main hua tha. inke pita pan budhu prasad mishra ji ek prasiddh saarangi vadak the. jab pan anokhelaal ji maatr dhai varsh ke the tabhi inke pitaaji tathaa maatr 6 varsh kii avasthaa main inaki mataji kaa swargvaas gaya tha. inkaa paalan-poshan inaki daadi shrimati janaki devi ne kiya. shrimati janaki devi ji inako lekar banaras aa gai tathaa inako banaras gharaane ke table ke prakand vidwaan pan bhairav prasad mishra ji se tabalaa vaadan kii shiksha dilwane lagi.
 inkaa bachapan atyant ksht main beetaa tha. guru pan bhairav prasad mishra ji ke sanidhya main inhone kathor parishram kiya tathaa sote-jogate har samay keval table ke prati hi samarpit ho gayi. pan bhairav prasad mishra ji se inhone 15 varshon tak banaras gharaane ke table kii vidhivat shiksha praapt kii. sachhee lagan va athak parishram dwaara 8-10 ghante pratidin ke abhyaas ko aapane dhire-dhire abhyaas badhaate hue 18 ghante tak kar diya. is kathin abhyaas ke kaaran inako shaareerik ksht bhi uthaanaa padaa, parantu ye apni sadhana main nirantar nimagn rahe. inke samaan abhyaas karnaa, kisi bhi kalakaar ke liye atyant kathin va asaadhya he. inhone pan dauji mishra ke saath gayan tathaa pan mohan laal ji va prasiddh nrutyaangna sivakunwar bai ke kathak ke saath pratidin ghanton abhyaas karte the. maatr solah varsh kii avasthaa main hi swatantra vaadan tathaa sangati donon main hi aap praveen ho gayi the.
