 adityavardhan thaneshwar ke pushyabhuti vansh kaa raja env kanth ke rajvansh ke pratishthata narvardhan kaa pautra tha. adityavardhan ne magadharaj damodar gupt kii putree mahasena gupta se vivaah kiya jisase vardhanon kii maryaada badi. adityavardhan ke sambandh main isse adhik kuch pataa nahin. uske baad uskaa putr prabhakarvardhan thaneshwar kaa raja hua. vidwaanon kaa anumaan he ki adityavardhan ne chhathi shatabdi ke ant main raaj kiya tha.
