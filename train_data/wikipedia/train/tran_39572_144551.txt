 dehradun diary janvari 2013 main pradarshit hindi rahasyamay film he. film sammaan hatya jaise jwalanth mudde par aadhaarit he.
 priti thakur iees afasar family ke anshul sharma se pyaar karti he. priti ke pita ek raajnneetik party ke diggaj netaa hai or inhen kisi bhi surat main priti or anshul kaa aapas main milanaa julna pasand nahin he. iske baavjood priti ghar waalon kii parvaah kiye bina anshul se milti he. priti kaa bhai vishaal ek din anshul kii hatya kar detaa he. anshul kii maa apane bete ko insaaf dilaane ke liye kanoonee ladaai ladati he, lekin thakur family ke raajnneetik dabadabe ke chalate insaaf kii ladaai lambii hoti jaati he. kanoonee ladaai ko lagaataar aage talataa dekh anshul kaa bhai akaash apni maa ke saath insaaf kii ladaai main shaamil ho jaataa he.
 film main nimna kalakaar apane abhinay kaa pradarshan karte hai.
