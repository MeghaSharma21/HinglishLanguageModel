 zanskaar bharat ke jammu or kashmir rajya ke purvi bhaag main kargil zile main sthit ek bhugolic va saanskrithik kshetra he. prashasnic drushti se yah ek tehsil kaa darja rakhata he or padum isaki rajdhani he. yah kshetra zanskaar nadi kii do shakhaon ke saath-saath basaa hua he.
 zanskaar kshetra sameep ke laddakh kshetra se zanskaar parvatamala dwaara vibhajit he. yah parvatamala 6000 meter kii ausat oonchaai rakhti he or iskaa purvi bhaag ek pathaar ke lakshanon vaala ilaaka he jo ki rupashu kehalaata he. zanskaar parvatamala himachal pradesh main bhi jari rahati he jahaan yah kinnaur kshetra ko spiti ghati se alag karti he.
 zanskaar or iske saath sata hua laddakh kaa kuch bhaag kabhi guge rajya kaa bhaag hua karata tha jo ki pashchimi tibet par bhi vistrut tha. zanskaar kaa naam laddakhi lahaze main "zangskaar" uccharit kara jaataa he, haalanki sthaneey zanskari uccharan main yah "zanhaar" bolaa jaataa he. tibeti bhashaa main tambe ko "zangs" kaha jaataa he or zanskaar kshetra main bhi tamba miltaa he. kuch strot yah anumaan lagaate hai ki naam kaa utpatti isi se hui or "zanskaar" kaa mul arth "shwet tamba" yaa "tambe kaa tara" hua karata tha.
 april 2006 main sthaneey swasthya-sambandhi janaganana main zanskaar kii aabaadi 13,849 thi, jisamen se lagbhag 95% tibeti buddha dharm ke anuyaayi the. 5% kaa ek chota alpsankhyak samudaay sunni muslim tha. jaateey rup se zanskari log changpa, daard or mon logon kaa mishran hai. rajdhani padum kii jansankhya lagbhag 500 he or yah hi zanskaar kaa sabase bada shahar he.
