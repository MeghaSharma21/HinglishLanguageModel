 gas liter vah yukti he jisase gas ke chuulhe jalaaye jate hai. yah un gas chulhon ko jwalit karne ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he jinamein swachaalit jwalan pranali nahin hoti. gas liter pijo-vidyut-prabhaav ke aadhaar par kaam karata he. isamen ek pijo-electric crystal par ek hathaudaa tej gati se takaraata he jisase lagbhag 800 volt paidaa hota he. is voltata ke kaaran ek chinagaari paidaa hoti he jo gas ko jala detii he.
