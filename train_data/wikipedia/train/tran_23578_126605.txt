 tokyo stock exange sankshep main tosho  yaa tii es i/tii vaay o ek stock exange he jo tokyo, japan main sthit he. apni soochibaddh companiyon ke kul bazaar puunjikaran ke aadhaar par yah asia kaa sabase bada va duniya kaa teesraa sabase bada stock exange he. april 2015 main 2,292 soochibaddh companiyon ke saath iskaa sanyukt bazaar puunjikaran 4.09 kharab america dollar tha.july 2012 main osaka securities exange ke saath vilay kii yojana ko japan fere trade kamishan dwaara anumodith kiya gaya jiske parinamswaroop ikai, japan exange samooh, 1 janvari, 2013 ko astitv main ai.
 tokyo stock exange 15 may, 1878 ko tokyo kabushiki torihikijo ke rup main tatkaaleen vitt mantri okuma shigenobu or poonjivadi pakshakaar shibusava eichi ke nirdesh par sthapit kiya gaya tha. 1 june, 1878 ko isamen trading shuru hui.
 1943 main das anya pramukh shaharon ke share bazaaron ke saath sanyukt karke ek ekal japani stock exange banaaya gaya. kintu nagasaki ke visphot ke baad sanyukt exange band kar ke punargathit kiya gaya.
 16 may, 1949 ko nae pratibhuti vinimay adhinium ke antargat apane vartmaan naam se tokyo stock exange punah prarambh hua.
