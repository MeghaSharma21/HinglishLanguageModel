 electra manogranthi manovishleshanvaadiyon kii ek dharna he jo pita or putree ke bich yunakarshan se sambandhit he. is dharna ke anusaar jaise putr swabhaavat: maan kii ore aakarshith hota he vaise hi putree kaa pita kii ore samvegatmak tathaa kaamuk prakaar kaa aakarshan rahata he. 
 frid ne sarvapratham greek myth 'idipas' ke aadhaar par idipas manogranthi tathaa electra manogranthi kii pariklpana prastut kii thi or bataaya tha ki jis ladki kaa pita kii ore kamatmak rukh athwa rujhaan hota he, vah nishchit hi electra manogranthi se peedit rahati he. aisi ladki kaa vaivaahik jivan yaa to puri tarah asafal ho jaataa he athwa du:khapurna rahata he kyonki avchetan man se apane pati main pita ke gun, lakshan aadi khojati he or unke na milane par hataash ho jaati he. parantu jaiviki tathaa kayikee sambandhi aadhunik anusandhanon se friid kii ukt pariklpana ko siddh nahin kiya ja sata he.
