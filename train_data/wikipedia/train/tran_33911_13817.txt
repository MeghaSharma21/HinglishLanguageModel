 rajanya yaa rijal, jiskaa bair naam "betaa oryonis" he, kalpurush taramandal kaa sab se roshan tara he. yah prithvi se dikhne vaale taron main se chatha sab se roshan tara bhi he. isaki chamak 0.18 magnitude par maapi gayi he. yah prithvi se 700-900 prakaash varsh kii duuri par he. rajanya ek nila mahadaanav tara he jo hamaare suraj ke dravyaman se 17 guna dravyaman he. isaki andruni chamak hamaare suraj kii chamak kii 85,000 guna he.
 rajanya ko angrezi main "rijal" kehete he, jo arabi bhashaa ke mul naam "ribil al-jabbaar" se aayaa he, jiskaa arth he "bhimkaay aadmi kaa bayen paaun".
 vaigyanikon ko san 1831 main pataa chalaa ke rajanya ek doharaa tara he, jinke naam "rajanya e" or "rajanya bi" rakhe gaye. in donon main 2,200 khagoleey ikaayiyon kii duuri he or, jahaan tak gyaat he in donon kaa aapas kaa gurutvkarshak sambandh bahut kamazor he yaa he hi nahin. "rajanya bi" kii roshni "rajanya e" se itani zyaada feeki he ke maamuli doorabeenon se yah alag nazar hi nahin aata. baad main yah bhi gyaat hua ke vastav main "rajanya bi" swayan ek dwitara he jiske donon taare apane dravyaman kendra ke ird-gird har 9.8 dinon main ek parikrama puri kar lete hai. in donon kii shrenee B9V he or donon mukhya anukram taare hai . inamen se bada vaala tara suraj kaa 2.5 guna dravyaman rakhata he or doosraa vaala 1.9 guna dravyaman rakhata he.
