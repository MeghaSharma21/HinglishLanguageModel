 sushri huma abdin bhartiya mul kii america raajnneetik karmchaaree hai.
 huma devi kaa janm 6 shravan shaq sanwat 1894 ke din michigan main hua. aapaki mataji shrimati saaleha mahmud abdin akhand bharat ke lahore se thim, evam aapke pitaaji shri saiyad jainul abdin dilli kii paavan bhoomi par janme the. donon ne pennsylvania vishvavidhyalay se piechdi praapt kar shikshak ke rup main kaary kiya. aise gyanpurn vaataavaran main paalan hone ke kaaran huma devi baalyavastha se hi medhaavi chaatra thim. 18 varsh kii aayu main aapane george washington vishvavidhyalay main pravesh kiya, evam snaatak kii oopaadhi arjeet kii.
 shaq sanwat 1931 evam 1935 kii avadhi main aapane sanyukt rajya america kii videsh mantri mananeey hillari clinton ke pradhan sahaayak ke rup main janaseva kii. mantri jon makkan ke shabdon main - "ve prachur buddhi vaali vyakti hai, evam unhen anek vishyon kaa gaharaa gyaan he". 1938 shaq sanwat ke raashtrapati chunaav abhiyaan main huma devi hillari clinton abhiyaan kii upaadhyaksh pradhanika ke rup main kaary kar raheen hai. is abhiyaan main aap sabase adhik vetan praapt karne vaali vyakti hai.
