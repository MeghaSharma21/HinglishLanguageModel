 romulo gaalegas venezuela ke upanyaaskaar evam rajaneta the. 1948 main 9 maah kii alpavadhi ke liye ve venezuela ke pratham raashtrapati the jise bina bhrashtachar ke chunaa gaya tha. 
 inkaa janm 1884 i. main hua tha. inke upanyaason main hamen unke desh benezuela ke gramin jivan kaa bada hi rochak or yatharth chitra miltaa he. vahaan ke saamaajik jivan kii visheshtaon ke saath hi saath ye prakritik vaataavaran ko bhi sajiv rup main prastut karte hai. pathak upanyaas ke kuch charitron or unki kahani se hi parichit nahin hota balki use benezuela desh evam vahaan ke saamaajik jivan kii puri jhaanki mil jaati he.
 romulo gaalegas ke upanyaason kii ek or visheshta he. unamem hamen sabhyataa or barbarta ke bich much rahe sangharsh kaa bhi chitra miltaa he. aadhuniktaa ke vyapak prabhaav ke kaaran kis prakaar jivan ke puraane taur badal rahe hai or naveen mulyon kii sweekruti kitne teevr virodh ke baad ho paati he yah unke upanyaason me bade hi maarmik dhang se dikhaaya gaya he. praarambhik rachanaaon main manovaigyanik drushti se asaadhaaran charitron ke adhyayan main inaki vishesh ruchi kaa abhas miltaa he lekin baad kii rachanaaon main jivan ko yatharth rup main prastut karne kaa prayaas he. dona barbara, jo san 1929 main metrid se prakashit hua, inhen antarrashtriya khyaati praapt hui. inaki anya mukhya rachanaayen is prakaar hai : ‘laa trepadora’ ; ‘cantaclaro’ ; pobar nigro .
