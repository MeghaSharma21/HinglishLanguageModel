 patan  matika ke sambandh main 24 sthitiyon se sambandhit he: "achche dhamm ko jad kii sthiti se achche dham se sambandhit he", vivaranon or uttaron kii sankhya ke saath. is pathar paath main kai kaaran or prabhaav shaamil hai siddhant vivaran vistaar, seemaa or unki disha ke unmoolan param prakruti par nirbhar karata he.
