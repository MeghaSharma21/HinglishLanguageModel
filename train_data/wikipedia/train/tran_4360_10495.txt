 kolaba yaa kulaba mumbai kaa dakshini kshetra he. 16veen sadi ke portugali samrajya ke dauraan is dweep ko candil ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. baad main 17veen sadi main britani adhikaar kshetra main aane ke kaaran ise kshetra ko 'colio' ke naam se janaa jane laga.
 vartmaan main getwe of india ke paas main kala decco style kaa reagal theatre he. vahaan paas main leopold kaife or tajmahal pales and tower hottle hai. iske dakshini bhaag main navy nagar sthit he. yahan paas main sabase puraani imaarat ke rup main afakhaan carch he. navy nagar ke madhya main bharat kii nami vaigyanik sansthaanon main se ek tata moolbhoot anusandhan santhaan sthit he. kolaba main ratan tata sahit kai nami hastiyaan rahati hai.
