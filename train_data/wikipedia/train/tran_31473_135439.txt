 metalika ek amareeki los angelis, california kaa heavi metal band he. inke sangeet main uttejktaa, raftaar, vaadya is tarah bhare he kii unke chalate yah "chaar bade" thrash metal ke sansthapako main se ek hone kaa kitaab rakhata he jiname slair, megadeth or enthraiks bhi shaamil he. inaki sthapana 1981 main hui jab james hetafield ne lard ularich dwaara sthaneey akhbaar main prakashit ek drumer kaa vigyaapan dekha.
