 aichi prifekchar, japan kaa ek prifekchar he jo chubu kshetra ke tokai kshetra main sthit he. isaki rajdhani nagoya he. yah chukyo mahanagareey kshetra kaa mukhyakshetra he.
 mul rup se yah kshetra teen prantiya bhaagon main vibhakt tha ovari, mikava or ho. nousama kaal ke baad, mikava or ho kaa ekikaran kar diya gaya. 1871 main, haan vyavastha ke unmoolan ke baad, ovari ko, chita prayadweep ko chhodkar, nagoya prifekchar main sansthagat kiya gaya, jabaki mikava ko chita prayadweep se milakar, nukata prifekchar banaaya gaya. april 1872 main, nagoya prifekchar ko aichi prifekchar namit kiya gaya or usee varsh 27 navambar ko nukata prifekchar se sansakt kiya gaya.
 2001 kii sthiti tak, aichi prifekchar kii jansankhya main 50.03% purush or 49.97% mahilaayein thim. 1,39,540 nivasee videshi the jo jansankhya kaa lagbhag 2% he.
