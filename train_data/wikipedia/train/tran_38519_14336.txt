 pithauragarh se 54 kilometre kii duuri par sthit escot kasturi mribhayaarany ek lokapriy paryatak aakarshan he. yah dharchula main sthit he.escot kasturi mribhayaarany kii sthapana kasturi hiran ke sangrakshan ke liye kii gai thi. kasturi hiran ke alaava,park main anya jaanavar tendua, junglee billi, udbilav,kankad,chotecingon vaala barasingha,goraal(ghume hue singon vaale barasinghe,safedbhaalu,himalayan chitah,kasturi mrug,himalayan kaala bhaaluu,or bharladi dekhne ko milte hai.is abhayaarany main barf main paaya jane vaale murga, teetar tathaa europeanteetar sahit kai pakshi dekhe ja sakate hai. kumaun main varsh 1986 main bana yarbhayaarany samudr tal se 5412 foot kii oonchai par sthit he. abhayaarany kenikat kai mandir hai.
