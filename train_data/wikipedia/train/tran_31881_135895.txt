 kapothak ek pakshi he, jo kabutaron kaa nikat sambandhi he. yah pandki, phakhta, panduk or siroti ke naam se bhi prasiddh he. 
 kapothak 12 inch tak lambey, bhole bhaale pakshi hote hai. inaki prakruti, swabhaav tathaa anya baaten kapoton se milti julati hoti hai. kapot kii tarah ye bhi anaaj or beej aadi se apana pet bharte hai or inhen kii bhanti inkaa anda dene kaa samay bhi saal main do baar aata he. tab maadaa apane machaan-numa, titare bitare ghonsle main do safed andey detii he.
 vaise to isaki kai jatiyaan saare sansar main faili hui hai, parantu unamem nimnlikhit vishesh prasiddh hai :
 1. dhawar -yah kad main sab kapotakon se bada or raakh ke rang kaa hota he jiske gale main kaala kantha sa rahata he.
 2. kalhak -yah dhawar se kuch chota or bhure rang kaa hota he. iske upree bhaag par kaali chitiyaan or chihn pade rahete hai.
 3. chitarokha -yah kalhak se kuch chota, parantu sabase sundar hota he. iske agle upree kaale bhaag main safed bindiyaan or pichley bhure bhaag main katthi chitiyaan padi rahati hai.
 4. tuutarun -yah uparyukt teenon kapotakon se chota hota he. iskaa upree bhaag bhura or chaati se neeche kaa bhaag safed rahata he. gale par kaali pattee rahati he jisapar safed bindiyaan rahati hai.
 5. eentkohari -iskaa rang eent jaisaa or kad sabase chota hota he. punch ke neeche kaa bhaag safed or gale main kaala kantha rahata he.
 6. stock dav -yah dhawar se kuch chota hota he, parantu rang usase kuch gadha hota he. iske gale main dhawar kii tarah kantha nahin rahata. isaki maadaa pedo ke cotaron main andey detii he.
 7. chollard yaa barbari dav -yah uttari america kaa prasiddh kapothak he jiske sharir kaa rang chandan ke samaan or gale main kaala kantha rahata he.
 8. shail kapothak -inase hamaare paaltu kabutar utpann kiye gaye hai.
 9. vipali kapothak - yah chote kad kaa hota he.
