 manushyon kii anuvanshiki main pitruvansh samooh pi yaa waahe-diene hapalogrup P ek pitruvansh samooh he. yah pitruvansh swayan pitruvansh samooh amaunopines se utpann hui ek shakha he. is pitruvansh ke purush bharat main kuch samudaayon main hi milte hai - manipur ke 30% muslim purush or gond janajati kii madia shakha ke 25% purush iske sadsy hai. europe main kroeshia ke hwaar dweep par bhi kuch purush iske vanshaj hai. chaahe pitruvansh samooh pi ke sidhe vanshaj kam hon, lekin is se utpann cu or aar upashakhaon ke vanshaj europe, madhya asia, dakshin asia, uttar america or dakshin america par karodon kii sankhya main dur-dur tak faile hue hai. anumaan he ke jis purush se pitruvansh samooh pi shuru hua vah aaj se lagbhag 27,000-41,000 varsh pehle jivit tha or madhya asia yaa siberia main rahata tha.
 angrezi main "vansh samooh" ko "hapalogrup", "pitruvansh samooh" ko "waahe chromosome hapalogrup" or "matruvansh samooh" ko "amtidiene hapalogrup" kehete hai.
