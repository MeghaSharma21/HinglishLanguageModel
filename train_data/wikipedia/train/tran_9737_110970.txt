 kanhoji ongre, jinhen 18ve sadi i main maratha samrajya kii nausena ke sarvapratham sipahasaalar the. unhen sarkel ongre bhi kaha jaataa he. "sarkel" kaa arth bhi nausenadhyaksh hota he. unhone aaajeevan hind mahaasaagar main british, portugali or dach nausainik gatividhiyon ke viruddh ladaai ladi. unke pita tanhoji ongre bhi chhatrapati shivaji kii fuj main nayak the or kanhoji ongre kaa bachapan se hi maratha fuj ke saath sambandh rahaa. unhone maratha nausena ko ek nae str par pahunchaaya or kai sthanon par maratha nausainik adde sthapit kiye, jinamein andaman dweep, vijayadurg, aadi shaamil the. ve aaajeevan aparajit rahe.
