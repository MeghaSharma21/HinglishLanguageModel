 laptev sagar, arctic mahaasaagar kaa ek uthlaa seemaant samudr he. yah siberia ke purvi tat, taimir prayadweep, severnaya zemlya or nayaa siberian dweep samooh ke bich sthit he. isaki uttari seemaa arctic kep se nirdeshaank 79°N or 139°E se guzarati he or enici antareep par ant hoti he. kaara sagar iske pashchim main jabaki purvi siberian sagar iske purv me sthit he.
 is samudr kaa naam do roosi anveshakon dimitri laptev or khariton laptev ke naam par rakhaa gaya he, isse pehle ise kai anya naamon se janaa jaataa tha or antim prachlit naam nordenskjold sagar tha jise swedish anveshak adolf erik nordenskyould ke naam par rakhaa gaya tha. is samudr kii visheshtaon main isaki bheeshan jalwaayu paristhitiyaan pramukh hai kyonki iskaa taapmaana varsh main nau maheenon se zyada samay tak 0° celsius yaa usase neeche rahata he or agast se sitambar ke bich ke samay ko chhodkar yah jamaa rahata he. isaki anya visheshtaon main iske pani kii kam lavanta, vanaspati, jeevon or maanav jansankhya kii durlabhta or isaki kam gaharaai he.
 laptev sagar ke taton par hajaaron saalon se eukagir, eveni or evanki naamak mul nivasee rahete aaye hai, jinka praathmik vyavsaay machhlee pakadana, shikaar or rendier paalan he. is kshetra kaa roosi anveshan 17 veen shatabdi main shuru huya. roosiyon ne isaki shuruyaat dakshin se kii jahaan kai badi nadiyaan samudr main visarjeet hoti thim in nadiyon main pramukh thim, lenaa nadi, khatanga nadi, anabar nadi, olenyoke nadi or omoloy nadi. samudr main kai dweep samooh upasthit hai, jinamein se kai par maimath naamak pracheen vishaal hathi ke acchhee tarah se sangrakshit avshesh milte hai. kshetra main mukhya maanviya gatividhiyon main uttari sagar maarg ke bheetar khanan or naugaman shaamil hai, machhlee pakadana or shikaar sthaneey str par kiya jaataa he or iskaa koi vyavsaayik mahatv nahin he. ilaake kii sabase badi basaavat or bandargah tiksi he.
