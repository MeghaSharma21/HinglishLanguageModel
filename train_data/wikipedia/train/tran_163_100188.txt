 is prakaar austric parivaar ke mukhya do varg hai- 
  austronesian, austro-aciatic. 
 austronesian athwa malay-poli-nesian varg kii bhashaayen prashanth mahaasaagar ke dwipon main faili hui hai. in bhashaaon ke bhi kai samooh hai, : indonesian, malanesian, mecronesian evam polisian. austronesian varg ke vivechan main neugini evam austrailia kii kuch mul bhashaaon kaa bhi ullekh kiya jaataa he kyonki in bhashaaon main kuch visheshtaayen austronesian varg kii hai.
 austro-aciatic varg kii bhashaayen madhyabhaarat ke chota nagpur pradesh se lekar anaam tak faili hui hai. isaki mukhya teen shakhaayen hai :
  munda, manakhmer, anami.
 munda bhashaaon kaa kshetra mukhya rup se bharat he. iske do bhaag hai. ek to himalay kii taraiwala bhaag jisaki seemaa shimla kii pahadiyon tak he tathaa doosraa madhyabhaarat kaa chota nagpurvaala bhaag. is shakha kii mukhya upbhashaayen hai : santhali, mundaari, kanavari, khadiya, ho evam shawar. munda bhashaaon kaa bhartiya bhashaaon par paryaapt prabhaav he.
 manakhmer shakha kii bhashaayen, vartmaan samay main mukhya rup se syam, burma or bharat main boli jaati hai. is shakha kii do mukhya bhashaayen hai-maan evam khmer. maan kaa kshetra burma kii maratabaan khadi kaa tatavarti bhaag he. yah kisi samay badi samruddhi saahitiyik bhashaa thi. maan ke shilaalekh 11veen shatabdi ke aasapaas ke hai. khmer kaa kshetra burma evam syam he. khmer bhashaa ke shilaalekh 7veen shatabdi ke aasapaas ke hai. bharat ke assam pradesh kii khaasi pahadiyon par boli janevaali "khaasi" athwa "khasia" isi shakha se sambandh rakhti he. nicobar kii "nicobari" evam burma ke wanon main boli janevaali "palong" aadi bhashaaon kaa sambandh bhi is shakha se he.
 anami anaam pradesh kii bhashaa he jo mukhya rup se hindchin ke purvi kiraane ke bhaagon main boli jaati he. yah ek prakaar se mishrit bhashaa he, jisamen kuch visheshtaayen manakhmer shakha kii evam kuch visheshtaayen chini bhashaa kii hai. isliye kuch log isaki gananaa is parivaar main na kar chini parivaar main karte hai.
 ek hi parivaar kii hone par bhi is parivaar kii bhashaaon main paryaapt bhinnata he. yon mukhya rup se bhashaayen shlisht yogtmak bhashaayen hai kintu saath hi kuch bhashaaon main ayogaatmak bhashaaon ke lakshan bhi dikhaai padate hai.
