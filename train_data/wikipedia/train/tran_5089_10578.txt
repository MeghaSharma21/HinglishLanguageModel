 dhaniram 'chaatruk' aadhunik punjabi kavita ke sansthaapak maane jate hai. aapaki kritiyaan pracheen or arvachin punjabi kavy kii kadi hai. aap hi sarvapratham vidvan hai, jinhen sahityaseva ke upalaksh main 75veen varshaganth par 'abhinandan granth' samarpit karke sammanit kiya gaya.
 chaatruk ji kii rachanaayen ye hai : 
 antim rachana san 1954 main urdu lipi main prakashit hui thi. shesh sabhi gurumukhi lipi or punjabi bhashaa main hai. muhaavareydaar theth punjabi kaa hi inhone vyavhaar kiya he. inaki praarambhik kavitaaon par to aadhyatmik or puranic vichaardhaara kii gahari chhap thi. kintu kalantar main inaki kavypratibha yatharthwaad kii ore unmukh hui. inke yatharthwaad main vaigyanik sujh boojh ke sthaan par nijee anubhavon kaa prabalya he. yatharthwaad ke naam par ve samaaj kaa nagn ghinauna chitra prastut nahin karte, varan niyantrit drushtikon rakhkar shikshaprad sandesh bhi dete hai. unki kavitaaon main sufiwaad ke darshan bhi hote hai, par saidhantik vivechan kii abhilaashaa ke abhaav ke kaaran ve sthal goodhatigudh nahin ho paae hai. dharmik kshetra main ve samanvayvaadee prathit hote hai. unke halake phulke geeton main vyaktigat prem kii abhivyakti bhi hui hai, kintu usamen laaj or shame kii simaaon kaa bandhan vidyaman he. swadhin bharat kii samasyaayen, desh or samaaj main unnat or avanat paksh 'sufikhana' main bhalee prakaar chitrit hue hai. vishyon kii bahuvidhta or chhandvaividhya inaki kavyagat visheshtaayen hai.
