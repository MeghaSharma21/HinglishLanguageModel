 sandip tomars ek bhartiya purush pahalwaan hai, jo purushon kii 55 kigra varg pratispardhaa main bhaag lete hai. inhone 24 april 2016 ko purushon ke 57 kilogram vajan ke saath olympic toornament main jane hetu apana sthaan surakshit kar liya. inhone mongolia main toornament ke antim din ek kaansya padak jeet kar yah sthaan praapt kiya. is padak ko praapt karne ke liye inhone ukraini pahalwaan andry yatsenko ko 11-0 se haraaya tha. isi ke saath yah chauthe pahalwaan ban gaye jo olympic main bhaag le rahaa he.
