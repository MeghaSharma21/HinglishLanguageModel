 badminton ne 1992 greeshmakaleen olympic main pehali baar shuruyaat kii thi or 6 olympiadon main iskaa chunaav hua he. 63 alag-alag rashtron olympic badminton pratiyogitaaon main upasthit hue hai, jinamein se sabhi 19 baar 6 baar pradarshit hue hai. yah badminton world federation dwaara shasit he.
 neeche 2016 greeshmakaleen olympic ke baad shrenee or deshon ke aadhaar par dikhaae gaye swarna padak vijeta hai. china greeshmakaleen olympic main sabase safal raashtra rahaa he. china hi ekmaatr aisa desh he, jo 2012 ke olympic khelon main kiye gaye padakon ke shatout ko hasil karne main safal rahe.
 saahasik greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main badminton ke samgr vijeta kaa matlab he.
 
 nimnlikhit deshon ne badminton pratiyogita main bhaag liya he. talika main sankhya us varsh ke olympic main bheje gaye pratiyogiyon kii sankhya darshaati he.
