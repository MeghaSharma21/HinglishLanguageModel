 tanhun jila nepal ke pashchimanchal vikaas kshetra ke gandki anchal main sthit ek atyaadhik urvar evam ghanaa vasti vaali jila hai. is jila kii kshetrafal 1546 berg kimi or jansankhya karive 4 lakh hai. is jila ke purv main chitwan or gorkha uttar main kaski or lamjung pashchim main syanja dakshin main palpa or navalparasi jilaayein hai. is jile kaa kendra damali he, ja maadi nadi ke tatpar avasthit he.
 isi jile ke chundi ramgha gram main 1814 july 10 main nepali bhashaa ke adikavi bhanubhakt acharyaka janm hry tha.
