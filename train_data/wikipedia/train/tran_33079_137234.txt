 savai maan singh dwitiya kushwaha vansh se sambandhit jaipur ke antim shaasha the. unhone 1922 se lekar rajya ke bharat main vilay tak shashan kiya. iske baad unhone 1949 se lekar 1956 tak rajasthan ke rajpramukh ke rup main kaary sambhala. baad ke saalon main inhone spain main bharat ke rajdoot ke rup main kaary kiya. maharaja savai maan singh dwitiya ko achche khilaadi hone ke saath-saath polo khel main khaasi shoharat hasil thi.
