 atisha pratap singh kuchipudi nartki hai. atisha ne 15 may 2017 ko rang pravesham vishaywastu par kuchipudi nrity kaa manchan kamaani auditorium, dilli main kiya. atisha sitambar 2017 main landan fishan wyk ke udghaatan or samaapan samaaroh main ek nrity pradarshan karne ke liye nirdhaarit he.
 atisha kendriya mantri or bihar ke saaran se loksabhaa saansad rajeev pratap rudi kii kishori beti hai. aatisha saal 2014 ke loksabhaa chunaav ke dauraan rudi ke program main najar aati thim. uski maan neelam pratap hai, jo haal tak indian airlines kii ek sahaayak company elliance air main inflite kii pramukh ke rup main karyrat thim.
