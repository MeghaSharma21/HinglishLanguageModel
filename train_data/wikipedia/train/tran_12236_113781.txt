 tii e saraswati ammaa bharat ke kerala rajya main janmi ganitagya thim. unhone bharat ke pracheen evam madhyakaalin jyamiti par vishesh kaary kiya.
 tekkathymynkothaklam saraswati ammaa ne madras vishvavidhyalay main viraghavan ke saath kaam kiya or phir ranchi or dhanbad main shikshan kiya. unke shodh ke aadhaar par hi pracheen or madhyakaalin bharat main jyamiti par pehla pramanik or vyapak adhyayan prakashit hua. unhone radhacharan gupt ke ‘pracheen or madhyakaalin bharat main trikonmiti’ naamak shodh prabandh kaa paryvekshan bhi kiya.
 kerala ganitiya sangh ne 2002 main apane vaarshik sammelan main professor tii e saraswati ammaa memorial naamak ek niyamit vyaakhyaan shuru kiya. michio yano ke shabdon me ,saraswati ammaa kii pustak 'pracheen or madhyayugin bharat main jyamiti' ne "bhartiya jyamiti ke adhyayan ke liye ek drudh neenv sthapit kii."
