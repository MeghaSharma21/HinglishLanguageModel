 ongi, andamani bhashaonki ongan shakha me vargeekrut
 sthaneey sampradaay, vivaran agyaat
 duusare andamani mul nivaasiyon me shaamil he jarva
 ongi, ek andamani adivasee janajaatiy samooh he jo bengal kii khadi main sthit andaman dweep samooh ke mul nivasee hai. inhen kai baar negrito ke rup main bhi vargeekrut kiya jaataa hai. purv main ve chota andaman dweep or paas ke dwipon me faile hue the, tathaa inhone kuch kshetra or shivi ruteland dweep or dakshin andaman dweep ke dakshini chor par sthapit kiye the. yah arddh khanaabadosh jati bhojan ke liye puri tarah shikaar or jamaa karne par nirbhar thi.
 ongi jansankhya me bhayankar giravat aayi he, jahaan yah 1901 me 672 thi wo ghat kar sirf 100 rah gayi he bache hue log litl andaman par do arakshit shiviron tak hi simit hai. ongi jansankhya main giravat kaa ek pramukh kaaran baahar kii duniya ke saath unke sampark dwaara unke khanpan kii aadaton main aayaa parivartan he.. 1901 main, jahaan 672 the; vahiin 1911, main 631 ; 1921, main 346; 1931, main 250 ; 1941 main, agyaat ; 1951 main 150(bharat kii swatantraaa ke kareeb.
