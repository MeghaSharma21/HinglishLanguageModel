 malaka yaa malakka malaysia kaa ek rajya he. yah malay prayadweep ke dakshini chor par malakka jalsandhi ke tat par sthit he, jiske paar indonesia kaa sumatra dweep he. melaka malay itihaas kaa sabase purana sultanate tha, lekin san 1511 main isapar portugaliyon ne kabza kar liya or sultanate ko samaapt kar diya. aadhunik kaal main yahan ab "yang di-peratua negery" naamak adhikari rajyapal kaa pad dhaaran karata he.
 yah malaya prayadweep ke pashchimi samudratat par 1664 varg kimi main faile hue malaka pradesh kii rajdhani tathaa bandargah he. yah ek ati pracheen europea basti he. kaha jaataa he, malaya ke raja ne san 1403 main is nagar kii sthapana kii thi. dwitiya vishwayuddh ke samay yah japaniyon ke adheen rahaa evam 1951 itho main swatantra hua or malaya ganatantr kaa ek bhaag ho gaya. 
 pracheen kaal se hi bharat tathaa china se iskaa vyapaarik sambandh he par isaki atyadhik vruddhi angrejon ke aane ke baad hi hui. naveen malaka main ab bhi portugali or holland vaasiyon ke pracheen bhawanon ke dhvansaavashesh milte hai. yah purvi asia kaa sabase mahatvuparn tathaa bada audyogic centre he. iske prishthpradesh main bumadhyarekhiy saghan sadabahaar van paae jate hai. tateey bhaagon main pashchim kii ore mangrove jati ke vriksh adhik paae jate hai. yahan kaa mukhya udhyam krishi he. yahan ke nivasee, rabar, dhan, nariyal, ananas tathaa garam masaalon kii kheti karte hai. is bandargah se rabar, nariyal, chaaval tathaa garam masaalon kaa niryaat hota he.
 malaka jaldmarumadhya,sumatra tathaa malaya prayadweep ko ek duusare se alag karanevaala ek jaldmarumadhya he jo dakshini china sagar tathaa hind mahaasaagar ko aapas main milaataa he. is jalsandhi kii lambaai 500 mile tathaa chaudaai 25 mile se 100 mile tak he. iske dakshin-purvi chor par sthit ek chote dweep par singapore sthit he. is jalsandhi ke dwaara sansar kaa sabase adhik maal aata jaataa he.
