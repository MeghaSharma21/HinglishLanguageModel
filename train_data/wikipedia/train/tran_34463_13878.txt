 bal seetaram mardhekar marathi bhashaa ke vikhyath sahityakar hai. inke dwaara soundaryashastra ke adhyayan par rachit ek kitaab soundarya ani sahitya ke liye unhen san 1956 main sahitya academy puraskaar ke marathi-bhashaa shrenee se maranoparant sammanit kiya gaya.
 mardhekar kii kavitaaon par likhe aalochana mardhekaranchi kavita : swaroop ani sandarbh ke liye vijaya rajadhyaksh ko bhi 1993 me marathi kaa sahitya academy puraskaar mila hai.
