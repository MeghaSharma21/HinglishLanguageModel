 aahaareey khanij ve khanij hote hai, jo aahaar ke sang sharir ko milte hai evam poshan karne main sahayk hote hai.sharir ke liye paanch mahatvapurn tatva calcium, magnesium, fasphoras, potashium or sodium atyaavashvak hote hai. inke alaava anya mahatvapurn kintu sookshm maatrik tatv hai, chromium, tamba, iodine, loha, maiganej or jasta. iske atirikt selenium bhi achche swasthya banaaye rakhane main ek upyogi he. anya sookshm maatrik tattvon main salfar, nikal, cobalt, fureen, anxigen, carbon, hydrogen or nitrogen bhi hamaare shaareerik swasthya ke liye aavashyak hai. ye sab milakar aahaareey khanij kehalaate hai. ye swasthya ke liye utane hi aavashyak hai, jitne vitamin ityadi. louh rakt ke liye or calcium hadidyon ke liye sampoorak ke rup main garbhavastha main mahatvapurn he. iodine kii kami galgand or mandabuddhi, tathaa magnesium kii kami cancer kaa kaaran ban sakati he. maiganej or chromium kaa bhi hriday-rog se sambadh he. saamaanya rakt-sharkara ke staron ko banaae rakhane ke liye chromium kii aavashyaktaa he. pachan-tantra main jaste kii kami se ganjaapan, bhukh na laganaa or yon-dushkriya ke parinaam ho sakate he.

 aadi lavan hote he. inke chloride salphet evam fasphet aadi yogic bhi sharir ke vikaas ke liye upyogi hote he.
 inke strot he- anaaj, sabhi maance, machhlee, andey, puri, safed aate kii roti, nariyal, pista, akhrot, vanaspati ghi, chocolate, mithaiyaan, sharaab, doodh chhilka sahit daale, chaay, kanfi, matar, aloobookhaara, chini maida aadi.
