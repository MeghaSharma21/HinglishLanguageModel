 virpur gaanv bharat ganraajya ke gujarat rajya me mahisagar zille me basaa hua ek chota gaanv he. ahmedabad se kareeb 118 keelometre kii duri par basaa hua he .
 gaanv lagbhag 400 saal purana mana jaataa he. ek bithak he, jise "pirmagali pahad" naamak choti pahari ke paas ek jagah he jahaan sant khwaja mehmood daryai dulha baithe or virpur se 2 kilometre dharmik shikshaayen deen.
 virpur main ek masjid bhi he, jise darava ke naam se janaa jaataa he, khwaja mehmood dariyaan dulha kii makbara. khwaja "ursh" kii har jayanti par bahut saare utsaah ke saath manaaya jaataa he, samaan rup se sabhi dharmon ke logon dwaara. iske alaava "dadi ammaa" kii makbara he jahaan ek pathar maujuud he jise aadhyatmik netaa dwaara aashirwad diya gaya he. jab koi pathar par baithta he, to vah swayan hi ghumta he or kuch aadhyatmik shakti ke rup main aashirwad pradaan karata he.
 mahisagar nadi gaanv se kareeb 10 kilometre bahati he or iske saath hi dharmik pratikatmakataa bhi he. yah ek baar ek devi ko shradhanjali arpit karne vaale yatriyon ke liye tirthayatra kii site thi.
