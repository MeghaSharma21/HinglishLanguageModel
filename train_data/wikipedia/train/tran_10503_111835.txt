 kanon ke aadhaar par mitti ko kai virgon main baanta ja saktaa he. mitti ke kisi namoonay main upasthit vibhinn khanij-kanon ke aakaar ke aadhaar banaakar yah vargeekaran kiya jaataa he. mitti ke mukhya ghatak hai- maheen pise hue shail-kan, mrittika, jaivik padaarth aadi. 
 yah mitti jalwaayu tathaa vanaspati pradeshon ke anusaar paayi jaati he jise do mukhya bhaagon main vibhakt kiya jaataa he- padalfer or pedonkaal. padalfer main aluminium tathaa loha kii maatra adhik hoti he or yah van pradeshon tathaa lambi ghaason vaale pradeshon main viksit hoti he. vanapradesh kii padalfer mittiyon main padjaal, padzalik or letrit mukhya hai. lambi ghaas vaale pradeshon kii pramukh mittiyaan preyari mitti or laal tathaa pili mitti hai. pedokaal main calcium kii pradhanta hoti he. isamen teen pramukh mrudaa samooh sammilit hai- charanojam, bhuri stapi tathaa marusthaleey mitti. padalfer tathaa pedokaal ke atirikt tundra mitti bhi katibandheey mitti he jo atyadhik nimna taapmaana ke kaaran bahut kam viksit ho paati he.
 katibandheey mittiyon ke bich-bich main bikhare roopon ke paayi jane vaali mitti ko katibandhantarik mitti kehete hai. iske upar mukhyatah mul chattaanon, apkshay, vashpikaran, jal risaav aadi kaa prabhaav hota. chuunaa pathar se nirmit rendjina mitti, lava se utpann regad mitti iske udhaaharan hai. 
 is mrudaavarg par mitti-nirmaan kii kisi prakriya vishesh kaa prabhaav nahin hota he tathaa unamem vibhinn sanstaron kaa vikaas nahin ho paata he. jalodh, himodh tathaa vaiudh yaa loes isi prakaar kii mittiyaan hai jo sthanantarit hote rahane ke kaaran praayah purna viksit nahin ho paati hai.
