 kulturavaven umeo kaa ek samskruthik centre he. iskaa udghaatan 21 navambar 2014 ko kiya jayega, jis varsh ke liye umeo ko yurap kii do samskruthik rajdhaniyon main se ek chunaa gaya he. isamen shahar kii kai samskruthik sansthaaen shaamil kii jaayengi or kai nai sansthaaen bhi kholi jaayengi. 
 yah imaarat kendri umeo main storagataan or ume nadi ke bich sthit he. yah imaarat 2011-2014 ke bich shahiri vikaas pariyojna ke tahat banaai gai he.
 kulturavaven kii do imaaraten hai jo teesri manzil par ek ho jaatin hai.
 iskaa puraa kshetra 24,000 varg meter he or iske andruni dizine ko chhodke baaki imaarat ke kharche kaa anumaan 70 karod krobor lagaaya gaya he. iske vaastukar wit architekchar or snohaita hai.
