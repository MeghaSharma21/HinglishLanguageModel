 iravati karve bharat kii shikshashasthri, lekhika evam nrivaigyanik thim.
 iravati karve kaa janm burma main hua tha kintu ve pune main padhi-likhin or badi huin. unhone san 1928 main mumbai vishvavidhyalay se samajashaastra main master degree praapt kii. iske pashchaat 1030 main berlin vishvavidhyalay se nrivigyaan main piechdi kii oopaadhi lee. ve pune ke deccan college ke samajashaastra tathaa nrivigyaan vibhaag kii vibhaagadhyaksh raheen. unhone san 1947 main dilli main hue bhartiya vigyaan congress ke nrivigyaan prabhag kii adhyakshata kii. unhone academik evam anya vishyon par marathi va angreji donon bhashaaon main bahut adhik likhaa. 'ugant' ke liye unako sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
