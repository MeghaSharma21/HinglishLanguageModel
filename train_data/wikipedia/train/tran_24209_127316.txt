 praudyogiki evam abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay, udaipur, udaipur, rajasthan, bharat main sthit ek saarvajanik abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay he. yah desh ke sabase puraane, prasiddh or pratishthit abhiyantriki sansthaanon main se he. yah maharana pratap krishi evam praudyogiki vishvavidhyalay kaa ek ghatak college he.
 praudyogiki evam abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay rajasthan sarkaar dwaara krishi abhiyantriki main snaatak paathyakram ke saath sthapit kiya gaya tha. yah armbh main rajasthan ke krishi mahavidhyalay parisar main tha or 1968 main apane vartmaan vishvavidhyalay parisar sthanantarit hua. 1975-76 main udaipur politechnic ki shaikshanik or bhautik suvidha ko bhi rajya sarkaar dwaara mahavidhyalay main sthanantarit kar diya gaya. rajya main audyogic or maanav sansaadhan vikaas ko dekhte hue atirikt degree kaaryakramon ko bhi joda gaya. sabhi degree paathyakram eisitii se anumodith hai. anya paathyakram bhi uplabdh hai.
 yah mahavidhyalay udaipur nagar railway stesan or bas terminal se 7 kimi or rana pratap nagar railway stesan se 2 kimi kii duuri par sthit he.
 mahavidhyalay main nimnlikhit vibhaag hai:
 mahavidhyalay main nimnlikhit pramukh suvidhaayen hai: puri tarah se susajjit prayogshalas, aadhunik pustkaalay, cafeteria, chikitsa suvidha, shaikshanik/prashasnic block, khel kii suvidha, kaaryshaalaayein, chatravaas kii suvidha, ityadi.
 college ke chaar vidhyaarthee chatravaas or ek vaigyanik chatravaas hai jo ki adhyayan ke liye gharelu maahaul or upyukt vaataavaran uplabdh karaane vaale hai. sabhi chatravaason main shuddh pani cooler, tivi, gijar or anya manoranjan ke saadhan uplabdh hai. chatravaas ke chaatro ko din-raat internet kii suvidha bhi pradaan ki gayi he.
