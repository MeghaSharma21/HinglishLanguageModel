 yah abhayaarany 76° 35’- 76° 50’ i deshaantar or 10° 20’ – 10° 26’ en akshansh ke bich sthit he. yah palakkad shahar se 135 kimi or tamilnadu ke purv main annamalai vanya jeev abhayaarany ke nikat sthit he. uttar main isaki seemaa nemaara van prabhag, dakshin main vejachal van prabhag or pashchim main chalakuri van prabhag se milti he. abhayaarany kaa bhuvigyaan hornbland, biotite, shail or crenokite se yukt he. 
 inaki oonchai 300 meter or 1438 meter ke bich he. thuthampara main abhayaarany kii uttari seemaa par nelliumpathi paahdiyon se annamalai paahdiyon tak 600 meter kii oonchai he. abhayaarany kii pramukh chotiyaan dakshin seemaa par krimala, uttar main pandaravarai, purvi seemaa main kuchchimudi, vangoli malaya pashchimi seemaa main pullaippadam hai. abhayaarany main teen manushya nirmit jalashay hai: parambikulam, thunakdavu or peruvaripallam or inkaa sammalit kshetra 20.66 varg kilometre he. thuvair jharne inamen se kisi ek jalashay main jaakar khaali hote hai. yahaan par 7 mukhya ghaatiyaan or parambikulam, thekkedi or sholayar naamak 3 badi nadiyaan hai. is kshetra main karapaara or kurarakutti nadiyaan bhi bahati hai. 3-di sthalakritik maanchitr dekhen.
 jangal main purv anumati ke saath trekking kii ja sakati he. sampark karen: eco care centre, parambikulam vanyajeev abhayaarany, anappedy, thunakadavu, polachi, palakkad, kerala - 661 678.phoneh 04253 – 245025 jalashay main naukavihar kiya ja saktaa he. yahaan par thunakadavu gaanv ke paas hi asia kaa sabase bada kennemer saugan kaa ped bhi he.
 parmbikullam ke mukhyalay thunakadavu main arakshit van kshetra main ek vriksh-grih bhi he jise pehle se arakshit karnaa padataa he. thunakadavu thillikali or ilathod main bane rajya van vibhaag ke rest house aaramdaayak rahan-sahan pradaan karte hai.
 tamilnadu ke polachi se sadak dwaara parambikulam pahunchaa ja saktaa he. palakkad se polachi 45 kilometre kii duuri par he, phir polachi se parambikulam 65 kimi kii duuri par he. nikatatam railway stesan polachi main he or nikatatam hawaldda palakkad se 120 kimi par coimbatore, tamilnadu main he.
 abhayaarany main vividh prakaar ke jeev badi maatra main paae jate hai inamen shaamil hai: stanadhariyon kii 39 prajatiyaan, amphybia kii 16 prajatiyaan, pakshiyon kii 268 prajatiyaan, machhlee kii 47 prajatiyaan, 1049 prajatiyon ke keede-makodon or titilyon kii 124 prajatiyaan. 
 abhayaarany main mukhyatah sagaun, rosewood, chandan or, neem jaise kai kism ke ped maujuud hai. yahaan tak ki ab tak kaa sabase purana sagaun kaa ped kenimara bhi yaheen hai. yah lagbhag 450 saal purana he or avishwasaneey rup se isaki paridhi 6.8 meter or oonchai 49.5 meter he. ise bharat sarkaar kaa mahavriksh puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya he.
 april 2007 main parambikulam vanyajeev abhayaarany or nelliumpathi jangalon ke aasapaas ke kuch hisson main lagi aag ke kaaran sainkadon acre vanya ilaake or vriksh nasht ho gaya. aag berozegaar agnishman karmchaariyon or shahad ikattha karne waalon ne lagaai thi. 
 aag kaa ek kaaran purvi kshetra main monsoon purv barish kaa abhaav hona bhi rahaa. is kshetra main janvari, farvari, march or april ke dauraan barish hoti he. is varsh, janvari main yahan keval 4 mimi barish hui or uske baad dobaaraa barish nahi hui. garmiyon main nelliumpathi ko abhootpurv sookhe kaa saamana karnaa padaa. april main adhikttam taapmaana 34o celsius or nunatam taapmaana 26o tak pahunch gaya.
