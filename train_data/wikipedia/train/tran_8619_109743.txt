 company, saajhedaari yaa asthaayee sanghathan ke rup main shuru kiye gayi us udhyam yaa naye vyavsaay ko starop company yaa starop kehete hai jo ek duhraane yogya or scalabel vyapaar model kii khoj ke liye armbh kiya jaataa he. 
 in companiyon, aam taur par nae banaae gaye, ek prakriya main navachaar ke vikaas, manyata or lakshith bazaaron ke liye shodh kar rahe hai. dot-kaam companiyon ke ek mahaan sankhya kii sthapana kii thi, jab shabd antarrashtriya str par dot-kaam bulbule ke dauraan lokapriy ho gaya. is prishthbhumi ke vajah se, kai starop keval tech companiyon kiya ja karne ke liye vichaar he, lekin yah hameshaa sach nahin he: adhik ucch mahatvakanksha, naveenta, scalebility or vikaas ke saath kya starop kaa saar he. 'sanghathan ek duhraane yogya or scalabel vyapaar model ke liye khoj karne ke liye gathan kiya.' is maamle main, kriya 'khoj' badi he, antar karne ke liye iraadaa he yani atyadhik moolyawaan, chote vyavsaayon, jaise ek restaraan main ek paripakv bazaar parichaalan se starop . baad ek acchhee tarah se gyaat maujuuda vyapaar ranneeti he, jabaki ek agyaat ek starop kii padtaal yaa vidyaman ko baadhit karne ke liye abhinav vyapaar model bazaar main, amazon, oober yaa google ke maamle main lagu karata he. rikt or dorf main joden ki starop badi companiyon kii nahin chote sanskarnon rahe hai: ek starop ek asthaayee sanghathan utpaada/feet ke liye ek bazaar khoj karne ke liye dizine kiya gaya he or ek vyapaar model he, jabaki iske vipareet, ek badi company ek sthaayee sanghathan he ki pehle se hi ek utpaada/bazaar feet hasil kiya he or ek acchhee tarah se paribhaashit, nishpadit karne ke liye dizine kiya gaya he puri tarah se pushti, acchhee tarah se pareekshan kiya, siddh kar diya he, satyapit, sthir, spasht, sanyukt raashtra aspasht, duhraane yogya or scalabel vyapaar mod.
 starop companiyon ke sabhi roopon or aakaaron main aa sakate hai. mahatvapurn kaaryon main se kuch surakshit pramukh kaushal or anusandhan kaa sanchaalan or ek pehali baar kam se kam vyavhaarya utpaada maanya he, kaa akalan or vichaaron yaa aage or gahari samaj sthapit karne ke liye avasaron ke alaava vyapaar avadharanaon kaa vikaas karne ke liye vichaaron yaa vyapaar avadharanaon ke saath hi par unke vanijyik kshamta kaa nirmaan karne main sakasham hona karne ke liye sansaadhan ke liye ek sah sansthaapak team kaa nirmaan kar rahe hai. sansthaapak team kii patibaddhta kii pushti karne ke liye, unke yogadan or swaamitv adhikaron or starop ke liye starop ke liye utpann kiya ja rahaa he bauddhik gunon par kabja karne ke liye sah sansthaapakon sahamat hai or ek shairdharakon samjhaute jaldi par nishpadit karne ke liye leni chahie. star ke liye vyavsaay model aam taur par ek neeche-upar yaa sheersh neeche drushtikon ke madhyam se paaya jaataa he. ek company ke rup main yah gujarata he vibhinn mile ke pathar, ek starop nahin rahegaa may is tarah ke rup main ek ipo main saarvajanik rup se kaarobaar hota ja rahaa he, yaa ke rup main astitv ko band karke ek ek vilay yaa adhigrahan ke madhyam se swatantra ikai. companiyaan bhi vifal ho sakati or puri tarah sanchalit karne ke liye nahin rahegaa. or jahaan starop paidaa hua he or ho janaa, starop paristhitiki tantra kii paripakvata ke aakaar kii maatra or starop kii safalataa par spasht prabhaav padataa he. niveshak aam taur par un nae unke majboot sah-sansthaapak team, jokhim/inaam profil or scalebility dwaara pratishthit companiyon ke liye sabase aakarshith kar rahe hai. ki he, ve nichle bootstrtping laagat, ucch jokhim or adhik sambhavit vaapasi par nivesh he. safal starop ek sthapit vyapaar, arth main he ki ve simit nivesh puunji, shram yaa bhoomi ke saath teji se badhane kii kshamta se adhik scalabel aam taur par kar rahe hai. samay aksar sabase mahatvapurn kaarak sabase badi starop safaltaaon ke liye kiya gaya he. jabaki ek hi samay main yah master karne ke liye sabase kathin chizon main se ek hone ke liye kai dharawahik udhyamiyon or niveshkon dwaara kii pahchaan kii he. starop vitt poshan ke liye kai adwitiya vikalp muthbhed.angil niveshak or udhyam puunji companiyon starop companiyon ke sanchaalan, shuru ek equity hissedari ke liye beej dhan kaa aadan-pradaan madad kar sakate hai. vyavhaar main haalanki, kai starop shuru main khud ke sansthaapakon dwaara vitt poshit hai. factoring ek anya vikalp he, haalanki starop ke liye adwitiya nahin he. anya dhan ke avasaron chrovd-funding, udhaaharan ke liye equity chrovd-fundig ke vibhinn roopon main shaamil hai. duniya bhar main shuru ap main udai junun aadhaarit arthvyavastha ke prarambh hone ke pehle charan ke rup main kai dwaara dekha gaya he.
 starop kaarysthal, jo jameen se unke vyapaar paane ke liye jaruri he main dakshata ko badhaava dene ke liye kuch maamalon main ek aaramdaayak drushtikon kaa upyog. 1960 ke adhyayan main, duglas mcgrager dand or puraskaar kaarysthal main ekarupata ke liye aavashyak nahin he ki, kyonki kuch logon ko protsaahan ke bina kaam ke liye prerana ke saath paidaa hote hai par jor diya. tanaav ke is hataane kaaryakartaaon or shodhakartaaon ne kaam paryaavaran ke aasapaas unhen, or haath main, kaam par or adhik par kam dhyaan kendrit karne ke liye unhen bahut kuch ke liye unki company ko praapt karne kii kshamta de rahi anumati detaa he. is samskruti aaj ujjwal man starop driving par hasil karnaa lakshya badi companiyon ko shaamil karne ke liye viksit kiya gaya he. google, anya companiyon ke bich, kharidi banane starop or unke kaaryakartaaon kaa maananaa sahi ghar par apane karyaalayon main bhi de unhen laane ke kaam ko apane kute ko pragati kii he. samskruti starop kaarysthal, yaa ek company ek starop se shramikon ko kaam par rakhane ke liye sabhi parivartanon ko peeche karne ke liye isi tarah kaam karte hai, mukhya lakshya banane ke logon ko sahaj rup main sambhav ke rup main lag rahaa he taaki ve karyaalay main sarvshereshth pradarshan ho saktaa he. kuch companiyon ke bhi kaise bade ve heneken maamle main haal hi main he ke rup main ek vishesh rup se janasankhyikeey, par kabja karne ke liye kar rahe hai ko chhipaane kii koshish karo.
 ek company ke mulya isaki praudyogiki par aadhaarit he, yah aksar bauddhik sampada ke sangrakshan ke liye unke vichaar praapt karne ke liye vyapaar maalikon ke liye bhi utana hi mahatvapurn he. arthshaastri kaa anumaan upar america ke mulya kaa 75% saarvajanik companiyaan ab aadhaarit he ki apane bauddhik sampada par samachar patrika . aksar, ek chota sa starop company ke mulya kaa 100% apni bauddhik sampada par aadhaarit he. is prakaar, yah unki bauddhik puunji kii suraksha ke rup main sambhav ke rup main jaldi ke liye ek dhwani ranneeti viksit karne ke liye praudyogiki-unmukh starop companiyon ke liye mahatvapurn he. starop companiyon, vishesh rup se un judi nai takanic ke saath, kabhi kabhi unke rachanakaaron or niveshkon ke liye bahut bada ritern kaa utpaadan-google, jinke rachanakaaron bane arbapatiyon unke stock swaamitv or vikalp ke madhyam se is tarah kii ek taaja udhaaharan he. tathaapi, starop companiyon kii asafalataa kii dar bahut adhik he. 
 vifaltaa ke liye ek aam kaaran yah he ki starop companiyon, nivesh kaa yaa apane staff kaa bhugtan karne ke liye kaaphi laabhadaayak hota ja rahaa se pehle unke agle daur kii suraksha ke bina dhan se baahar chalaa sakate hai. jab aisa hota he, yah karmchaariyon vetan ke bina chhod sakate hai. kabhi kabhi in companiyon dwaara anya companiyon, agar ve vyavhaarya ho samajha rahe hai, lekin baar baar ve kho aay ke liye kaam kiya samay sambhalanaa karne ke liye bahut thodaa sahaara ke saath karmchaariyon chhod kharidi kar rahe hai. yadhyapi starop sabhi prakaar ke vyavsaayon kii, or puri duniya main banaae gaye hai, kuch sthanon or vyavsaayik kshetron ke starop companiyon ke saath vishesh rup se jude rahe hai. internet bulbule der se 1990 ke dashak ke kuch internet kaa upyog, dusron ke sevaayen pradaan karne ke liye internet kaa upyog pradaan karne ke liye praudyogiki kii bikri kii badi sankhya he internet starop companiyon, ke saath judaa hua tha. is starop gatividhi ke adhikansh main sabase acchhee tarah se gyaat starop paristhitiki tantra - silicon vaili, uttari california starop company gatividhi kaa ucch str ke liye prasiddh ke ek kshetra main sthit tha.
