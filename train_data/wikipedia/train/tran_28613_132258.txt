 niveshak un vyakti yaa sansthaaon ko kaha jaataa he, jo kisi yojana main apana dhan nivesh karte hai. niveshak kai prakaar ke hote hai, jaise vyaktigat niveshak, saamaajik sansthaaen or videshi sansthagat niveshak.
 sankhya ke anusaar dekhen, to yah samooh shairdharakon kaa sabase bada bhaag hota he. saarvajanik nirgam ke sandarbh main, vyaktigat niveshkon ko do bhaagon main baanta ja saktaa he. pehle vah jo adhiktam ek lakh rupye ke share ke liye aavedan kar sakate hai or duusare vah jo ek lakh yaa usase adhik mulya ke sheyaron ke liye aavedan kar sakate hai. in niveshkon ko echenai kaha jaataa he. ipo main footkar niveshkon kaa hissaa 35 or echenai kaa 25 pratishat hota he.
 ye kai logon dwaara aapas main milakar banaai gai sansthaaen hoti hai, lekin ye sansthaaen apane banaae gaye niyam kanoonon ke tahat hi share bazaar main nivesh kar sakati hai.
 ye ve sansthaaen hoti he jinaki rachana bharat main nivesh karne hetu videsh main kii gai he. bharat main nivesh karne ke liye in sansthaaon ko sebi ke saath apana panjikaran videshi sansthagat niveshak ke rup main karnaa hota he. sebi ke niyamon ke mutabik is tarah kii sansthaaen kisi bhartiya company ke ipo ke kul mulya ke das pratishat se zyada par nivesh nahin kar saktin.
 vittiya sansthaaon ke antargat bank, beemaa companiyaan, pension fund aadi ke liye dhan lagane vaali sansthaaen hoti hai. niveshkon ke sandarbh main kahen, to praathmik or dwitiyak bazaar ke ye sabase bade niveshak hote hai.
