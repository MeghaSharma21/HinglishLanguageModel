 makhdoom allaudin ali ahamed sabir; Makhdoom Alauddin Ali Ahmed Sabir: jinhen allaudin sabir kaliyari yaa ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he, 13 veen shatabdi main ek pramukh dakshin asian sufi sant the. vah baba farid, ke uttaradhikari or chishti aadesh ke sabariya shakha main sabase pehle the. inaki dargah haridwar ke nikat kaliyar gaanv main he.
