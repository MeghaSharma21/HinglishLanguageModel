 kurta yaa kurtaa ek paaramparik bhartiya poshaak he. yah ek lambi kameez kii tarah hota he jo ghutanon tak lanbaa hota he or pyjama ke saath pahana jaataa he jo yaa to kurte ke rang se miltaa julata hota he yaa safed hota he. kurte ko aam prayog or khaas maukon dono ke liye istemaal kiya jaataa he haalanki khaas maukon par pahane jane vaala kurta aam taur se zyada fashneble hote hai. kurte sooti, oonee or reshmi sabhi tarah ke samagriyon main milte hai. bharat or padosi deshon bangladesh or pakistan main bhi kurte kii parampara rahi he. kurte ke kai parivardhit rup hai jinamein sherwani, pathaani suut ityadi pramukh. auraten bhi kurton kaa istemaal karti hai kintu inhen aamtaur par kurti or anya naamon se pukaara jaataa he.
