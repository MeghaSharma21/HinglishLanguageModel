 yah sahaara marusthal main bhumadhya sagar kii ore chalane vaali garm hawa hai. sahaara marusthal se italy main pravaahit hone vaali siraanko hawa baaluu ke kanon se yukt hoti hai, tathaa sagar se nami dhaaran karne ke baad jab italy main varsha karti hai to in baaluu ke kanon ke kaaran varsha kii bunde laal ho jaati hai. is prakaar kii varsha ko italy main rakt kii varsha kehate hai.
