 akatobe praant madhya asia ke qaazakhstan desh kaa ek praant he. isaki rajdhani bhi akatobe naam kaa shahar hi he. is praant se ilek nadi nikalati he jo ural nadi kii ek mahatvapurn upanadi he. is praant main tel kii khaane hai or unase praant ko acchhee aamadani ho jaati he. soviet sangh ke jamaane main yahan kii aalia moldagulova naamak stri soviet laal sena main ek mashhoor nishanebaaz thi or praant main jagah-jagah uske naam ke smarak hai.
 kazakh bhashaa main 'aq tobe ' kaa matlab 'safed pahaad' hota he. isamen bindu vaale 'qa' ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bindu-rahit 'k' ke uccharan se alag hota he or 'qareeb' or 'qeemat' jaise shabdon ke 'qa' jaisaa hota he.
 san 2009 kii janaganana ke hisaab se is praant ke 79% log kazakh samudaay ke the. inke alaava yahan roosi, tartar, ukraini, german, korian, yahudee, armenian, chechen or moldovi log bhi rahete hai.
