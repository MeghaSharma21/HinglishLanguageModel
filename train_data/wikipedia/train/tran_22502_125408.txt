 kyoto vishvavidhyalay, yaa kyodai japan ke kyoto nagar main sthit ek rashtriya vishvavidhyalay he. yah japan kaa doosraa sabase pracheen vishvavidhyalay he tathaa asia ke sarvshereshth vishvavidhyalayon main se ek he. yah vishvavidhyalay vishwastareey anusandhankartaaon, nobel puraskaar vijetaon, do field madelvijetaon tathaa ek gouse puraskaar praptakartaaon ke liye prasiddh he.
