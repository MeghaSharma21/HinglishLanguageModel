 musafirkhaana bhartiya rajya uttar pradesh ke amethi jile main sthit ek kasbaa va nagar panchayat kshetra he, purv main yah sultanpur jile kii ek mahatvapurn tehsil thi. 'musafirkhaana' shabd kaa arth he sarai yaa dharmashala. musafirkhaana uttar pradesh ke amethi jile main sthit' he. yah lakhanu-amethi road par sthit he gauriganj or amethi sadak maarg madhyam se musafirkhaana pahunchaa ja saktaa hai. nikatatam railway stesan musafir khaanaa railway stesan he. iske atirikt amethi, gauriganj,nihalgarh evam sultanpur nikatavarti railway stesan hai. nikatatam hawaii adda amausi he. bhugolic drushti se, shahar 26.37 ° uttari akshansh or 81.8 ° purvi deshaantar main sthit he. samudr tal se isaki oonchai 102 meter he.
 musafirkhaana main sthit he 26°22′N 81°48′E / 26.37°N 81.8°E / 26.37; 81.8. yah 102 ke ek ausat oonchai he meter .
 
 2001 ke anusaar bharat kii janaganana, musafirkhaana 7373 kii aabaadi thi. purushon aabaadi ke 53% or mahilaon 47% kaa gathan. musafirkhaana 66%, 59.5% ke rashtriya ausat se adhik kii ek ausat sakshartaa dar he: purush sakshartaa 75% he or mahila sakshartaa he 57%. musafirkhaana main, jansankhya kaa 15% umr ke 6 varsh se kam he.
 musafirkhaana ek railway stesan he, jo varanasi-lakhanu rail maarg par padataa. musafirkhaana kii bahut acchhee connactivity kii rajdhani lakhanu bas yaa train dwaara yani upar se he.
