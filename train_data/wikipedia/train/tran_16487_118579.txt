 rus ne kai avasaron par aadhunik olympic khelon main hissaa liya he, lekin apane itihaas main vibhinn rashtron ke rup main. roosi samrajya ke rup main, raashtra pehle 1900 khelon main bhaag liya, or 1908 or 1912 main phir se laut aayaa. 1917 main roosi kranti ke baad, or 1922 main soviet sangh kii baad kii sthapana ke baad, yah 30 saal kaa hoga jab tak ki roosi athleton ne olympic main ek baar phir se bhaag liya, jaisaa ki 1 9 52 ke greeshmakaleen olympic main soviet sangh ke rup main. 1991 main soviet sangh ke vighatan ke baad, rus ne 1992 main uniphid team ke hisse ke rup main bhaag liya, or ant main 1994 sheetakaaleen olympic main rus ke rup main ek baar phir se laut aayaa.
 roosi olympic samiti 1991 main banaai gai thi or 1993 main manyata praapt thi. soviet sangh ne moscow main 1980 greeshmakaleen olympic kii mejabani kii, or rus ne sochi main 2014 ke sheetakaaleen olympic kaa aayojan kiya.
 chah akramaniyon main roosi athleton ne greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main kul 451 padak or sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main 124 or anya 12 padon par jeet hasil kii he. haal ke baarah khelon main rus ke 575 kul padak, jisamen 200 swarna padak shaamil hai, sanyukt rajya america ke baad duusare sthaan par hai.
 soviet sangh or roosi samrajya ke sabhi greeshmakaleen or sheetakaaleen olympic padak rus dwaara viraasat main mila, lekin roosi sangh ke padak kii sankhya ke saath milakar nahin mila.
 rus ne ek avasar par khelon kii mejabani kii he.
