 frank edward wooli angrej peshewar cricketer the. vah harfanmaulaa ke rup main khelte the. apane tees se adhik varshon ke pratham shrenee kariyar main unhone kent county cricket club kii taraf se khelte hue 978 maichon main 58,959 ran banaae jo ki keval jaik hobbs se kam he. 1,000 se adhik pratham shrenee kaich lene vaale wo akele gaiyr-wicketiper he. unhone 2,000 se upar wicket bhi liye. vah england ke liye 1909 se 1934 tak khele jisamen unhone 64 maichon main 3,283 ran banaae or 83 wicket liye.
