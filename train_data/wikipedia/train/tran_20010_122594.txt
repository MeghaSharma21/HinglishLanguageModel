 pound jibraltar kii mudra he. iskaa britain pound sterling ke saath samamoolya par vinimay kiya ja saktaa he.
 1898 tak, jibraltar main mudra kii sthiti jatil thi, real vyavastha main british, spanish or jibralterian sikke mili-jule the. 1898 main british pound ko adhikrut mudra kaa darja diya gaya. 1927 ke baad se jibraltar apane banknote, 1988 ke baad se apane sikke jari kar rahaa he.
