 vaat, pitt, kaph in teenon ko dosha kehete hai. in teenon ko dhaatu bhi kaha jaataa he. dhaatu isaliye kaha jaataa he kyonki ye sharir ko dhaaran karte hai. chunki tridosh, dhaatu or mel ko dooshit karte hai, isi kaaran se inako ‘dosha’ kehete hai.
 ayurveda sahitya sharir ke nirmaan main dosha, dhaatu mel ko pradhan mana he or kaha gaya he ki 'dosha dhaatu mel mulan hi shariram'. ayurveda kaa prayojan sharir main sthit in dosha, dhaatu evam malon ko samya avasthaa main rakhana jisase swasth vyakti kaa swasthya bana rahe evam dosha dhaatu malon kii asamaanya avasthaa hone par utpann vikruti yaa rog kii chikitsa karnaa he.
 vaat, pitt, kaph - ye teen shaareerik dosha maane gayi hai. ye dosha asaamaanya aahaar-vihaar se vikrut yaa dooshit ho jate he isliye ise 'dosha' kaha jaataa he. sharirgat anya dhaatu aadi tatv inhe dosha ke dwaara dooshit hota he. in tino dosha ko sharir kaa stambh kaha jaataa he. inke praakrit avasthaa evam sam maatra hi sharir ko swasth rakhata he, yadi inkaa kshay yaa vruddhi hoti he, to sharir main vikruti yaa rog utpann ho jaati he.
 arthath sharir rakt aadi dhaatu se nirmit hota he evam mel sharir ko stambh kii tarah samhaale huye hai. dosha, dhaatu, mel prakritik rup se rahakar uchit aahaar-vihaar karne waal sharir dhaaran karte he. sharir kii kshay, vruddhi, sharirgat avyavo dravyon kii vikruti, aarogyata-anaarogyata, in dosha dhaatu malon par hi aadhaarit he yadhyapi sharir ke liye dosha dhaatu mel teenon pradhan dravya he phir bhi shaareerik kriya ke liye vaataadi dosha ke adhik kriyasheel hone se sharir main dosha varg kii pradhanta rahati he.
 teenon dosha main sarvapratham vaat dosha hi viruddh aahaar-vihaar se prakupit hota he or anya dosha evam dhaatu ko dooshit kar rog utpann karata he. vaat dosha praakrit rup se praaniyon kaa praan mana gaya he. ayurveda shaastra main sharir rachana, kriya evam vikrutiyon kaa varnan evam bhed or chikitsa vyavastha dosha ke anusaar hi kiya jaataa he.
