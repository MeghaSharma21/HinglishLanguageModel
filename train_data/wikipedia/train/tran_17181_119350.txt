 fatahganj kaa makbara paanch manjila he or dilli main sthit apni samakaleen sabhi imaaraton main sabase ucch koti kaa he. khubsuurati ke maamle main yah humanyu ke makbare se bhi sundar he. yah bharatpur road ke najadik, railway line ke paar purv disha main sthit hai. yahaan tak pahunchane ke liye railway line ke upar ek pul bhi banaaya gaya he. yah makbara ek bageeche ke bich main sthit he or isamen ek skool bhi he. jo surakshakarmi is makbare kii dekhbhaal karata he vah ise kai baar 9 baje se pehle bhi khol detaa he. fatahganj kaa makbara dekhne ke baad rickshaw se moti dungri pahunchaa ja saktaa he. moti dungri kaa nirmaan 1882 main hua tha. yah alvar ke shahi pariwaron kaa awaas tha. yah 1928 tak shahi pariwaron kaa awaas rahaa. maharaja jaisingh ne ise tudwakar iske sthaan par isse bhi khubasurat imaarat banavaane kaa faisla kiya. is imaarat ke liye unhone europe se vishesh saamaan mangaya tha lekin durbhagyavash jis jahaaj main saamaan aa rahaa tha vah dub gaya. jahaaj dubne ke saath hi maharaj jaisingh ne is imaarat ko banavaane kaa iraadaa chod diya. imaarat nahin banane kaa ek phayda yah hua ki ab paryatak is pahari par berok-tok chadh sakate hai or shahar ke sundar dushya kaa anand le sakate hai.
