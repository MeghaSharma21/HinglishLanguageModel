 purab se utpann pashchimi sabhyataa, jon em hobsan dwaara san 2004 main likhit pustak he jisamen is aitihaasik siddhanth ke viruddh tark diya gaya he ki pashchim kaa udai san 1492 ke baad 'kunwari maan' se hua. is pustak main yah darshane kaa safal praytn kiya gaya he ki pashchimi kaa udai vastutah uske purvi deshon ke saath antalakriya ke kaaran hua jo pashchim kii tulana main saamaajik evam praudyogikeey drushti se adhik unnat the.
