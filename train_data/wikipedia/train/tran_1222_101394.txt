 tadeepaar 1993 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. iskaa nirdesan mahesh bhatta ne kiya or mukhya bhoomika main unki beti puja bhatta or mithun chakravarti hai. vishesh upasthiti main juhi chawla bhi he. yah film ausat pradarshan hi kar pai lekin iske geet superhit rahe hai.
 hinsak takaraav main hone ke baad, shankar ko police ne giraftaar kar liya, adaalat main pesh kiya jahaan use shahar kii simaaon ke baahar rahane kii sajaa sunai gai . use upasthiti ke uddeshy se khud ko nikatatam police stesan main pesh hote rahanaa he. shankar ek dost, kadak ke saath rahata he, or ek raat sankat main ek mahila ke paas aata he. vah use kuch gundon se bachaata he, use ghar le jaataa he or pataa lagaataa he ki uskaa naam namakeen he. iske baad, unhen yah bhi pataa chalaa ki vah karodpati mohinadevi jaisi dikhti hai, jo gaayab ho gai he. shankar ne namakeen se mohinadevi kaa pratirupan karne ke liye kaha, uski kuch sampattiyon ko hadapney ke liye, taaki teenon amir jeevanstyli ji saken. namakeen aisa karne ke liye sahamat hai, lekin sawaal yah he ki mohinadevi kahan he, or namakeen kaa kya hoga jab wo mil jaaegi?
 sabhi geet samir dwaara likhit; sara sangeet nadeem-shravan dwaara rachit.
