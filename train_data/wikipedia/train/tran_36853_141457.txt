 shipaki laa yaa shipaki darra himalay kaa ek pramukh darra hai. yah bharat ke himachal pradesh ke kinnaur zile ko tibet ke ngaari vibhaag ke zanda zile se jodta he. sutluj nadi is darre ke paas hi ek tang ghati se guzarakar tibet se bharat main daakhil hoti he.
 dhyaan den ki 'laa' shabd tibeti bhashaa main 'darre' kaa arth rakhata he. qaayade se is darre ko 'shipaki darra' yaa 'shipaki laa' kahana chaiye. ise 'shipaki laa darra' kahana 'darre' shabd ko dohraane jaisaa he, lekin yah prayog phir bhi prachlit he.
