 vishwa islamic mishan soofivaad se prerit sunni musalamaanon kaa intaraashtriya sanghathan he. isaki sthapana 1972 main shah ahamed nooraani siddiki, saiyad mahruf hossain shah, shah aarif kadri naushahi or allamaa arshadul kadri dwaara islamic pavitra shahar makka main hui thi. world islamic mishan kaa vikaas vishwa ke kai deshon ke musalamaanon ko islamic shiksha pradaan karne ke liye hua he. sanghathan kaa karyaalay england ke manchester shahar main he tathaa yah vishwa ke kam se kam 24 deshon main sakriy he.
