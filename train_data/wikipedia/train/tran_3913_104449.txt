 sindh, varsh 1936 se 1947 tak britani bharat kaa ek praant tha. 1947 baad se 1955 tak yah pakistan kaa praant rahaa. britani kaal main sindh praant ke antargat kherpur riyasat ko chhodkar sindh ke sabhi bhaag aate the or isaki rajdhani karachi thi. pakistan banane par karachi, pakistan kii rajdhani ban gaya.
