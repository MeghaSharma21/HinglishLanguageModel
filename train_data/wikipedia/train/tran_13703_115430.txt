 sronchan gampo, ke baad se 617-698 i0 ke samay main tibeti samraat the. lichchhabi shaasha anshuvarma ke baad beti bhrikuti ke saath nepal ke sambandhon ke saath bandhe. sronchan gampo kaa shasankaal ke dauraan sambhot lipi keavishkaar ke baad kiya gaya he karne ke liye bharat or nepal ke baad buddha paath karne ke liye es kaa tibeti bhashaa main anuwaad or lipyantaran karne ke liye karrwai bhi shuru ho jayega sronchan gampo teen tibeti dharmraj se pahela dharmraj mana jaataa he.
