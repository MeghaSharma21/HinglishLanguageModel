 pashchim papua dakshinpurv asia ke indonesia desh kaa ek kshetra he jo nayaa gini dweep ke pashchimi hisse par vistrut he. is dweep kaa purvi bhaag papua new gini naamak ek bhinn raashtra he. yah pashchim papua or papua naamak praanton main vibhajit he. yahan ke adhiktar log papuai, malanishian or austronishian hai. is kshetra main isai bahusankhyak hai or uske baad muslimon kii sankhya he. bhinn samudaayon ke bich indonesian bhashaa kaa prayog hota he. praant kaa adhiktar bhaag ghaney jangalon se dhakaa he jahaan kai janajatiyaan rahati hai.
