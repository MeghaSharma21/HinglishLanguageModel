 2007 amareeki open tennis pratiyogita kaa aayojan abhi neuyork shahar main billy jin king neshnal tennis centre main hua. 
 purush ekal main pichley teen baar ke vijeta switzaraland ke roger federr kaa muqaabala srbija ke novak jokovich se hua. federr ne is mukaable main 7-6, 7-6, 6-4 se vijay praapt kar chauthi baar yah khitaab jeet liya. saath hi unhone 12vaan grand slam jitkar roy emresan ke 12 grand slam jeetne ke ricard kii barabari bhi kar lee. ab keval peet sempraas hi unase aage hai jinhone 14 grand slam khitaab jite hai. 
 vishwa kii sheersh variyata praapt khilaadi belgium kii justin henin ne yah kitaab rus kii swetlana kuzanetasova ko 6-1, 6-4 se haraakar jeet liya.
