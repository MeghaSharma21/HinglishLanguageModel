 coffee vid karan ek vaartalap kaarykram he jo star world india par pradarshit hota he. yah kaarykram mashhoor chalchitr nirmaataa vah nirdeshak karan johar dwaara mezbaan kiya jaataa he. is kaarykram ne 3 sizan purna kiye hai. philhal is kaarykram kaa chauthaa sizan chal rahaa he. is kaarykram main mezbaan karan johar jane-maane film hastiyon ko aamantrit karte hai or unke saath unki filmon vah nijee zindagi ke bare main charchaa karte hai. is karykram kaa chauthaa sizan 1 disambar 2013 ko armbh hua.
 yah kaarykram apane taaze or parihaas yukt tatv ke kaaran yuva darshak ko aakarshith karata he. yah film hastiyon ke khulaase va unke sahyogiyon or sahakarmiyon par vichaar ke kaaran prasiddh he. is kaarykram main kai khulaase va vivaad hote hai jiske kaaran yah bahut tii.aar.pi batorta he.is sho kaa aarambh 2004 main hua or is sho ne bahut safalataa praapt kii. is sho kaa pehala prakaran 19 navambar 2004 ko hua or iskaa pehala sizan 27 may 2005 ko khatm hua. yah sho bich main tham diya gaya tha kyonki karan johar ko apni film Kabhi Alvida Na Kehna kii shooting kerani thi. chaar saal ke uparaant or kai pun: pradarshan ke baad yah sho tivi par phir se 7 navambar 2010 ko lautaa. is sho ke tisare sizan kaa ant 17 april 2011 ko hua. is sho ke chauthe sizan kaa pratham pradarshan 1 disambar 2013 ko hua.
 'laai-o-meter' ek aisa yantra tha jiske dwaara karan johar kaarykram main aae hue filmi hastiyon ke jhuuthon ko pakad lete the jo ve kaarykram ke dauraan bolte the. is yantra kaa upyog keval pehale sizan main hi hua tha.
 is bhaag main karan johar hastiyon se anek sawaal poochhate hai jinke jawaab mehmaanon ko jald se jald dene padate hai. praayah vah aise prashn puchhatein hai jinamein mehmaanon ko do yaa teen vikalpon ke bich chunaav kakaran padataa he yaa prashn ke sidhe jawaab dene padate hai. karan johar zyaadaatar apane mehmaanon par bal daalate hai unke diye gaye vikalpon main se ek kaa chunaav kakaran ke liye.
 kabhi kabhi kaarykram main ek bhaag aata he jo sho main aae hue mehmaan ko darshakon kii vichaar-dhara ko un tak pahunchane main madad karata he. ve yaa to aam janata yaa bollywood ke anya hastiyon se aa sakati hai. kuch apisods main yah bhaag sho ke ant main aata he or kuch main sho ke dauraan. kai apisods main yah bhaag dikhaaya hi nahin gaya he.
 yah bhaag is kaarykram ke chauthe sizan se aarambh hua he. is bhaag main karan johar apane mehmaanon se kai tarah ke sawaal poochhate hai. mehmaanon ko ek yantra diya jaataa he jisko bajaakar ve un prashnon kaa jawaab de sakate hai. jo sabase pehale yantra ko bajaata he use jawaab dene kaa mauka miltaa he, galat jawaab dene par prashn dusre mehmaan ko de diya jaataa he. jis filmi hasti ke sabase zyaada ank hote hai, use ek vishisht inaam miltaa he. yah bhaag coffee vid kakaran ke chauthe sizan se aarambh hua he.
 coffee vid kakaran par janaa saare film hastiyon kaa sapana rehta he, parantu yah hastiyaan kisi na kisi vivaad kaa hissaa zarur banate hai. nimnlikhit kuch aise vivaad diye gayi hai jo naa hi vivaad kathan kehane se surkhiyon main aaye hai parantu jinke bare main ve aise kathan kehete hai unhen bhi kukhyati dilavaate hai.
 
 is kaarykram kaa pehla sizan bahut safal rahaa tathaa yah vah pehla angrezi kaarykram tha jise bharat main bahut ucch retings mili the. jis episod main abhisek bacchn or priti zinta aae the, vah episod sabase charchit vah prasiddh apisod ban gaya tha pehle sizan kaa.doosraa sizan 11 farvari 2007 main aarambh hua.uske baad coffee vid kakaran chaar saal ke antar ke baad STAR World India par phir se darshaya gaya.
 is kaarykram kaa pehla sizan 19 navambar 2004 ko aarambh hua tha.
