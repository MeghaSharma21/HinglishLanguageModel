 misr main raajaaon kii ghati main sthit makbara kevi 15 kaa upyog unnisvin rajvansh ke firaun seti dwitiya ke dafan ke liye kiya gaya tha. raajaaon kii ghati ke mukhya bhaag se dakshin-pashchim main chalane vaali badi ke sir par makbare ko ek urdhwadhar chattan sire ke aadhaar par kholaa gaya tha. yah uttar-se-dakshin-purv dhuree ke saath chalata he, jisamen ek chota pravesh galiyara hota he jiske baad teen galiyare segment hote hai jo ek acchhee tarah se kamare main samaapt hote hai, jisamen kuen kii kami hoti he, jise kabhi kholaa nahin jaataa tha. iske baad yah ek chaar-stambh vaale haul or galiyare kaa ek or khinchaav se judata he jise ek dafan kaksh main parivartit kiya gaya tha..
 kaksh kii deevaaron or chat ko plaster se dhanka hua tha or anubis jacals or devataaon kii do panktiyon ke saath chitrit kiya gaya tha, jo raa or ossieris ke anuyaayiyon kaa pratinidhitiv karte the, jinhen mummy jaisi aankadon kii nichali pankti par rakhaa jaataa he. chat kii lambaai ke saath pankh vaali devi nat dikhaai detii he. or ba ke ba kaa pratinidhitiv kya ho saktaa he uske sir se upar chitrit kiya gaya he. painting paaramparik misr ke litni ri, amudat or gates kii pustak se khinche gaye paaramparik chithran hai.. acchhee tarah se kamare main diwaar painting adhik asaamaanya hai; ve raja ko kai alag-alag abhivyaktiyon main dikhaate hai, udhaaharan ke liye ek panther ke peeche yaa ek papeeras skif par. chitron main dikhaae gaye vasteen tutankhamun kii makbare main paae gaye khojon main dikhaai detii hai..
