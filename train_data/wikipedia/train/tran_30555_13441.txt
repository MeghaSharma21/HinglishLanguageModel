 almati anterrashtriya vimaanashetra  kazakhstan kaa sabase bada vimaanashetra he. yah desh ke sabase bade shahar evam vanijyik rajdhani almati se lagbhag 18 kilometre dur sthit he. almati anterrashtriya vimaanashetra kazakhstan ke lagbhag aadhe yatri sankhya evam 68% cargo parivahan sampann karata he. varsh 2011 main vimaanashetra ne 3,665,538 yatri, 1,830,297 aagaman yatri, evam 1,835,241 prasthaan yatriyon sahit avaagaman kiye.
