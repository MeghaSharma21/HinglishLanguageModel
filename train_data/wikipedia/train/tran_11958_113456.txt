 paal-udaan yaa gliding aisi manoranjan kriya va khel he jisamen bina kisi motor yaa anya kritrim urja kii khapat se chalane vaale saadhan kaa prayog kare kisi vimaan ko apane pankhons kaa paal ke rup main istemaal kar ke use prakritik vaayu bahaav se bhoomi se upar hawa main udate hue rakhaa jaataa he. aise vimaanon ko glyder yaa palvimaan kehete hai. kai praani or vanaspati-ansh bhi is siddhant ke prayog se sthaan-se-sthaan tak vaayu main yatayaat karte hai. paal-udaan main kaushal hawa ke upar uthte pravahon ko khojane-pahchaanane or phir vimaan ko unke upar le jaakar uthavane main maharath ko samajha jaataa he. anukool sthitiyon main nipun vimaanchaalak halake vimaanon ko paal-udaan dwaara sainkadon kilometre tak bina kisi kritrim urja ke le ja sakate hai or vicitra paristhitiyon main 1000 kimi tak kii aisi udaane dekhi gai hai.
