 dari yaa dari farsi afghanistan main prachlit aadhunik farsi kaa ek rup he. pashto ke saath-saath yah afghanistan kii do sanvaidhanik rajbhashaon main se ek he. yah afghanistan ke lagbhag 50% logon kii maatrubhaasha he or desh kii sabase adhik boli jane vaali bhashaa bhi he. afghanistan main alag-alag bhashaayen bolne vaale samudaayon ke bich main bhi iskaa sampark bhashaa ke rup main istemaal hota he. irani farsi or dari farsi bolne vaale ek dusre ko aasaani se samaj pate hai or inamen sirf lahaze or kuch shabdon kaa antar he. bahut se bhashavaigyanikon ke anusaar dari main puraani farsi ke bahut se aise tatv surakshit hai jo aadhunik irani farsi main kho gayi hai.
 dari main shabdon kaa uccharan aksar irani farsi se alag or hindi-urdu se miltaa-julata hota he -
 inamen dari kaa uccharan pracheen farsi jaisaa mana jaataa he or irani farsi kaa badla hua.
