 bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan, rudki bharat kaa ek saarvajanik ejiniyari vishvavidhyalay he. yah uttrakhand rajya ke rudki main sthit he. pehle iskaa naam 'rudki vishvavidhyalay' tathaa isse bhi pehle iskaa naam 'thomson college of civil ejineering' tha. isaki sthapana multah 1847 main hui thi. san 1949 main isko vishvavidhyalay kaa darja diya gaya. san 2001 main ise bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan ke rup main parivartit kar diya gaya.
 apane roopantaran ke baad se bhartiya praadyogiki santhaan rudki ne desh ko janashakti tathaa gyaan uplabdh karaane tathaa anusandhan kaary karne me pramukh bhoomika adaa kii he. yah santhaan vishwa ke sarvottam prodyogiki sansthano me apana sthaan rakhata he. isane prodyogiki vikaas ke sabhi kshetron main apana yogadan diya he. vigyaan, prodyogiki va ejineering shiksha tathaa anusandhan ke kshetra me ise dhara nirdharak bhi mana jaataa he. octubar 1996 main yah santhaan apane astitv ke 150 varsh purna kar chukaa he. 21 sitambar 2001 ko bharat sarkaar ne ek adhyadesh jari karke is santhaan ko desh kaa saatvaan bhartiya prodyogiki santhaan ghoshit kiya. ai. ai. tii. rudki ko raashtra kaa ek mahatvapurn banane ke liye yah adhyadesh ab sansad ke ek adhinium me parivartit ho chukaa he.
 vastukala evam ejineering ke 10 vishyon main snaatak paathyakram sanchalit kiye ja rahe he ; snatakottar, prayukt vigyaan va vastukala tathaa niyojan vishyon ke 55 paathyakramo kii suvidha uplabdh he. santhaan ke sabhi vibhaagon va anusandhan kendron main shodhakarya kii bhi suvidhaayen he. 
 santhaan main samast bharat ke vibhinn kendron par yojit sanyukt pravesh pariksha ke madhyam se bi tech. va bi. arq. paathyakramon main chaatro ko pravesh diya jaataa he.
