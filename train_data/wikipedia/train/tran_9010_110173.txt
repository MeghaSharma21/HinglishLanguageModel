 karn parv ke antargat koi bhi upparv nahin he or adhyyon kii sankhya 79 he.
 is parv main dronacharya kii mrityu ke pashchaat kaurav senapati ke pad par karn kaa abhishek, karn ke senapatitv main kaurav sena dwaara bheeshan yuddh, pandavon ke parakram, shalya dwaara karn kaa sarathi bananaa, arjuna dwaara kaurav sena kaa bheeshan sanhaar, karn or arjuna kaa yuddh, karn ke rath ke pahiye kaa prithvi main dhansana, arjuna dwaara karnavadh, kauravon kaa shok, shalya dwaara duryodhana ko santvana dena aadi varnit he.
