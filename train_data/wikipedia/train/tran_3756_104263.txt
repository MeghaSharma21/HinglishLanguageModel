 chandrasekhar aaazaad krishi evam praudyogic vishvavidhyalay kanpur, bharat main uttar pradesh rajya ke kanpur jile main sthit ek krishi vishvavidhyalay he. iskaa namakaran kanpur ke prasiddh krantikari chandrashekhar azaad ke naam par kiya gaya he. isamen paanch sankaay hai. krishi tathaa grih vigyaan ke sankaay kanpur main hai tathaa krishi abhiyantriki or takanic kaa sankaay, matsay sankaay, or dugdh sankaay itawa main he. yah vishvavidhyalay uttar pradesh ke 29 jilon main krishi samudaayon kii aavashyaktaaon kii dekhbhaal karata he.
