 mahatma gandhi chitrakoot gramoday vishvavidhyalay madhya pradesh ke satna jile main mandakini nadi ke kinare chitrakoot main sthit he. isaki sthapana kaa mukhya uddeshy mahatma gandhi ke gramin vikaas ke swapn ko saakaar kar gram swarajya kii sthapana he. at: samyak takanic kii shiksha or iskaa prasar karnaa iskaa pramukh lakshya he. isaki sthapana 12 farvari san 1991 ko mahashivaratri ke din madhyapradesh sarkaar ke adhinium ke dwaara hui.
