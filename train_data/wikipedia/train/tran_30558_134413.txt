 laghu chitrakaari bhartiya shastreey parampara ke anusaar banaai gai chitrakaari shilp he. “samarangan sutaradhar” naamak vaastushastra main iskaa vistrut rup se ullekh miltaa he. is shilp kii kalakritiyaan sainkadon varshon ke baad bhi ab tak itani naveen lagati hai maano ye kuch varsh purv hi chitrit kii gai hon. 
 kaagaj par laghu chitrakaari karne ke liye sabase pehle aadhaar rang banaaya jaataa he. iske liye kaagaj par khadiya mitti ke chaar se paanch str chadhaane kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. iske baad chitrakaari main rangon ko samtal karne ke liye dabaav ke saath use ragada jaataa he. prishthbhumi banane ke baad poshaak kii ore dhyaan diya jaataa he. ant main aabhushanon or chehre sahit anya angon kii rachana kii jaati he. puri kalakriti nirmit ho jane ke baad unamem rang bhare jate hai. aabhushanon main swarna or chaandi ke rangon ko badi kushaltapurvak bharaa jaataa helaghu chitrakaari banane main pracheen samay se hi prakritik rangon kaa hi prayog kiya jaataa tha. is chitrakaari main vibhinn prakritik pattharon kaa bhi prayog kiya jaataa tha yatha moonga, lajvart, haldi aadi.
