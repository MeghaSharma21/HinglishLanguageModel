 sharmindagi ek bhaavnaatmak sthiti he jisamen aamtaur asuvidha yaa bechaini kaa anubhav hota he jab koi saamaajik rup se asweekaarya yaa galat samajha janaa vaala kaary yaa sthiti koi doosraa vyakti dekh len yaa prakat ho jaae. aam taur par isamen sammaan yaa garima ke nuksaan kii kuch dharna shaamil he, lekin sharmindagi kaa str or prakaar sthiti par nirbhar karata he. sharmindagi kuch had tak shame kii tarah he, sivaay iske ki sharmindagi aamtaur par ek aise krity ke kaaran hone kaa arth he jo naitik rup se galat naa hote hue saamaajik rup se galat he. 
 nijee maamalon yaa vyaktigat trutiyon yaa durghatnaon par avaanchit dhyaan ke kaaran sharmindagi vyaktigat ho sakati he. vyaktigat kaaryon se sharmindagi ke kuch kaaran, jaise jhooth bolte yaa galati karte pakade janaa. kai samskritiyon main, nagn yaa undervier main dekha janaa sharmindagi kaa ek vishesh rup se tanaavpurn rup he. vyaktigat sharmindagi un logon ke kaaryon se bhi ho sakati he jo sharaminda vyakti ko saamaajik rup se ajeeb paristhiti main daalate hai - jaise mata-pita bacchon kii bachapan kii tasveeron ko mitron ko dikhaate hai, kisi ke dwaara kisi kii upasthiti yaa vyavhaar ke bare main apmanjanak tippanee karnaa, kisi anya vyakti dwaara asweekaar kiya janaa, dhyaan kaa paatr bananaa, yaa yahan tak ki kisi or kii sharmindagi bhi dekhte samay. 
 kabhi-kabhi sharaminda vyakti muskaan yaa hansee ke saath sharmindagi ko chupaane kii koshish karata he. sthiti kii anumaanit gambhirtha ke aadhaar par krodh kii bhaavana bhi ho sakati he, khaskar agar vyakti sochataa he ki koi anya vyakti jaanboojhkar sharmindagi paidaa kar rahaa he.
