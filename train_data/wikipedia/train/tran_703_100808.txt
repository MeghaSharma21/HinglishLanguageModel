 aizol zila bharati rajya mizoram ke aath zilon main se ek he. is zile ke uttar main kolasib zila, pashchim main mamit zila, dakshin main serchhip zila or purv main champhai zila avasthit he. zile kaa kshetrafal 3,577 varg kilometre he. is zile kaa mukhyalay aizol he jo ki mizoram kii rajdhani bhi he. bharat kii janaganana 2011 ke anusaar yah zila mizoram kaa sabase adhik jansankhya vaala zila he. 
 zile kaa naam iske mukhyalay aizol nagar ke naam par rakhaa gaya he. mizo bhashaa main ai kaa arth haldi tathaa zol kaa arth maidan se he. atah iskaa arth haldi ke maidaanon se he.
 bharat kii janaganana 2011 anusaar aizol zile kii jansankhya 400,309 he, jo ki lagbhag bruneii ke baraabar he. is prakaar se jansankhya ke anusaar is zile kaa bharat ke 640 zilon main 557vaan sthaan he. zile kaa jansankhya ghanatv 112 prayek varg kilometre main nivasee he. 2001-11 ke dauraan jansankhya vruddhi dar 24.07% he. is zile kaa linganupaat dar 1009 mahila prati 1000 purush tathaa sakshartaa dar 97.89% he.
 yahan par 94.71% isaai, 3.31% hindu, 1.31% muslim, 0.39% buddha, 0.13% anya, 0.03% sikh tathaa 0.02% jain dharm ke anuyaayi hai. iske atirikt 0.1% logon ne apana dharm kaa ullekh nahin kiya he.
