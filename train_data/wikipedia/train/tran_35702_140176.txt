 kendriya mantrimandal, bharat ganraajya main kaaryakari adhikaar kaa prayog karata hai. is main varisht mantri or kanishtha mantri sammilit hote hai, jinka netritv pradhanmantri karte hai. kendriya mantrimandal naamak ek choti kaaryakari nikaay, bharat main sarvocch nirnay lene kii sanstha hai. keval pradhanmantri or cabinet mantri hi cabinet ke sadsy hote hai. bharat main sabase varisht civil sevak, cabinet sachiv, cabinet sachivalay kaa netritv karte hai, tathaa mantriyon kii parishad ko prashasnic sahaayta pradaan karte hai. rajya mantriyon ko apane kaam main cabinet mantriyon kii sahaayta ke saath kaam saunpa gaya hai.yah suchi bharat ke kendriya mantriyon kii suchi he jise narendra modi ke netritv main solahavin loksabhaa ke gathan ke baad 2014 main chunaa gaya he.
 is suchi main janvari 2015 main parivartan kiya gaya he.
 samuhik rup se mantriyon kii parishad bhartiya sansad ke nichle sadan ko javabdeh hai. bhartiya samvidhan ke anusaar, mantriparishad main mantriyon kii kul sankhya lok sabha ke sadasyon kii kul sankhya ke 15% se adhik nahin honi chahie. har mantri ko sansad kaa sadsy hona aavashyak hai; jo mantri sansad ke nichle athwa upree sadan kaa lagaataar 6 maheenon tak sadsy nahin, usase turant uskaa mantri pad chin liya jayega.
