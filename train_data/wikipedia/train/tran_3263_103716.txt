 kisi prakaar kii urja ko bhavishya main upyog karne ke uddeshy se sanchit karnaa urja bhandaran kehalaata he. urja ko sanchit karne vaali yuktiyon ko urja sanchayak kehete hai. urja vividh roopon main rah sakati he, jaise vikiran, rasinic urja, gurutveey sthitij urja, vaidyut sthitij urja, vaidyut urja, ooshmiya urja, gupt ooshmaa tathaa gatij urja. kuch prakaar kii urjaaon ko bhandarit karnaa apekshaakrit saral tathaa kam kharchila kaary he jabaki anya ko bhandarit karnaa apekshaakrit kathin. isaliye urja bhandaran ke liye urja ko us rup main badal liya jaataa he jisamen bhandarit karnaa aasaan ho or jisase aasaani se duusare rup main urja ko parivartit kiya ja sake. baari maatra main urja ke bhandaran ke liye pampit jal sabase upyukt he. isi liye vishwa kii sampoorn sanchit urja kaa 99% urja isi rup main sanchit kii jaati he.kuch prakaar kii urja bhandaran kam samay ke liye hi sambhav hota he jabaki kuch prakaar ke urja bhandaran uski apeksha bahut adhik samay ke liye bhi kiya ja saktaa he.
