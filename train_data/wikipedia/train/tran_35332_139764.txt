 mexilari sinus yaa urdhvahanu vayuviver maanav sharir kii khopadi main hawa bhari hui guha hoti hai jo hamaare sir ko halkaapan va saans vaali hawa kaa nami yukt karte hai. jab kabhi sinus kaa sankraman ho jaataa he to ye sirdard kaa bhi kaaran banate hai. parantu saare sirdard kaa kaaran sinus hi nahin hote, kai baar rogi ko sinus ke dard kii bajaay adhakapari yaa tanaav vaala sirdard ho saktaa he. adhakapari sirdard se chehre kii nasen prabhavit ho jaati hai. jo ki maathe, gaalon or jabade ko jaati hai. isaki vajah se sinus ke paas dard hota he. jab naak va sinus kaa sankraman hota he to lakshan ankhon par, maathe par va gaalon par baaripan mahasoos hota he. kai baar to naak band, thakaan, sardi ke saath bukhaar, chehre par sujan va naak se pilaa yaa hare rang kaa resha bhi girtaa he. ise sinocitis kehete hai. yadhyapi kai baar yah mahatvapurn baat hoti he ki kai sirdard to lambey samay se chale aa rahe hai, nazle ke kaaran hote hai. isliye jab kabhi migraine kaa ilaj kare to sinus sirdard ko jarur dhyaan rakhen.
 vastav main sinus ke sankraman hone par sinus kii jhilli main sujan aa jaati he. sujan ke kaaran hawa kii jagah sinus main mawaad yaa balgam aadi bhar jaataa he, jisase sinus band ho jate hai. is vajah se maathe par, gaalon va upar ke jabaade main dard hone lagata he or sinus sirdard kaa kaaran banate hai. sirdard aage jhukne va letne se bad jaataa he. is sirdard ko kam karne ke liye sinus kii sujan ko kam karke mawaad va balgam ko nikaala jaataa he. sinus sirdard ko kam karne ke nimr upaay hai, jaise:
 sinus sirdard agar ukt saavdhaaniyon aadi se thik na ho to doctor se jaanch karwaani chahie taaki sirdard kaa sadaa ke liye ilaj ho sake or agar sirdard aadi kaa kaaran migraine ho to uskaa davaaiyaan aadi lekar ilaj karwaanaa chahie.
 eks-re, city scan va allergy test aadi karwaakar yadi koi naak kii haddi evam sinus kii beemaari aati he to us marij ko doorabeen kaa oparation avashya karwaanaa chahie.
