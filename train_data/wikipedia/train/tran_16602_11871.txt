 arya vansh aitihaasik rup se 19veen shatabdi ke ant or 20veen shatabdi ke armbh main pashchimi sabhyataa main aryavanshi kaaphi prabhaavshaalee log hua karte the. mana jaataa he ki is vichaar se yah vyutpann he ki hind europee bhashaa ke bolne vaale mul logon or unke vanshajon ne vishisht jati yaa vrihad shwet nasl kii sthapana kii. aisa mana jaataa he ki kabhi-kabhi aryan jati se hi aryavad astitv main aayaa.
 armbh main ise maatr bhashaai aadhaar par janaa jaataa tha lekin naji or nav-naji main jatvaad kii utpatti ke baad saidhantik rup se iskaa prayog jaadu-tona or shwet pratishtha ko sthapit karne ke liye kiya jane laga.
 aryan shabd kii utpatti sanskrit bhashaa ke arya shabd se hui he, vishuddh sanskrit main iskaa arth "sammanneey, aadarneey or uttam hota he".
 18veen shatabdi main jo log puraani hind europee bhashaa ke jaanakar the unke purvaj bhartiya or irani mul ke log the. aryan shabd kaa prayog na keval bhartiya or irani logon ke liye kiya jaataa tha balki iskaa istemaal hind europee mul ke logon ke liye bhi kiya jaataa tha, inamen albanian, kurd, armenian, unani, laathini or german bhi shaamil the. baad main baltic, selts or slavik bhi isi samooh main gine jane lage. iske sambandh main ye tark diya gaya ki in sabhi bhashaaon kaa mul strot ek hi he - jise aaj portugali or europea ke naam se janaa jaataa he, darasal ye wo bhashaaen hai jinke bolne waalon ke purvaj europea, irani aryaayi the. ye portugali-european ke sajaateey samooh or unke vanshajon ko aryaayi kaha jaataa he.
 19veen or 20veen shatabdi main iskaa prachlan aam tha. isaki misaal 1920 main prakashit ech.ji. wales kii kitaab da outline of history main dekha ja saktaa he. ie pustak main unki uplabdhiyon kaa ullekh karte hue wo likhte hai ki "jab sargun dwitiya or sardanapalus asyria par shashan tha" or "wo syria, misr or babylonia se yuddh kar rahe the". jaise, wales ne suzav diya he ki aaryon ne antat "sampoorn pracheen vishwa, yahudee, igian or misr ko samaan rup se apane adhikaar main kiya". 1944 main rand meconley dwaara taiyaar vishwa maanchitr main aryaavanshiyon ko maanavjaati ke das sarvshereshth samoohon main se ek mana gaya he. skanddinavian mul ke nasl virdohi ichaswatantrywadee upanyaskaar paul anderson, jo ki vigyaan galp ke lekhak the, ne apane vibhinn laghu kathaon, upanyaason, laghu upanyaason main baar-baar hind europee logon ke liye aryaayi shabd kaa paryaayavachi shabd, ke rup main istemaal kiya he. unhone aryan ko ek aisi shikari chidiya kaha he jo inhen duusare grahon par ja kar vaahan apana kabja karne ko prerit karti he or anya grah pranali main upnivesh nivasaneey grah or nae upniveshwaad grah ke daur main unhen agrani udhmi ke taur par pesh karne ke liye protsaahit karti he.
 aryan shabd kaa prayog aam taur par bhartiya va europea yaa kuch had tak bhartiya va iraniyon ke liye kiya jaataa he, haalanki kai jaankaar is paribhaasha ko saamagree aadhaarit puraane vidwata main aaj bekaar or raajnneetik rup se galat samajhate hai lekin puraani kitabon main aksar iskaa zikr dekhne ko mil jaataa he, jaise ki 1989 main lekhak colin renfriu dwaara sintific american jisamen "aryan" shabd kaa prayog parmparik bhartiya–europea logon ke liye kiya he.
 19veen shatabdi main maanav-jati ke vigyaan ko kuch vidwaanon dwaara vaigyanik naslvaadi pravriti ke pratik ke taur par dikhalaayaa jaataa he, "arya vanshiyon" ko bhaaropee jati ke up-samooh ke rup main dekha jaataa he jo mul rup se hind europee bhashaa bhashaa ke bolne vaale the, jinke vanshaj portugali, hind europee the, jo europe, asian rus, anglo-america, dakshini dakshin america, dakshini africa, australia mahadweep, newjiland, armenia, greater iran or uttari bharat, or dakshini asia ke nepal, srilanka or maldweep main rahete the.
 19ve or 20ve shatabdi ke armbh main aryan shabd ko "portugali-hind europee or unke vanshaj ko sanderbhit kiya jaataa tha". maiks mular ko aise pehle lekhak ke rup main aksar pahachaanaa jaataa tha jisane english main aryan "jati" shabd kaa prayog kiya. 1861 main die gaye apane lectures on da science of language main usne aryan shabd kaa istemaal "res of pipul" ke rup main kiya, yani logon kii jati ke taur par kiya. us vakt jati shabd kaa prayog kabeelon yaa sajaateey samooh ke liye kiya jaataa tha.
 jab mular ke kathan ke anusaar aryan logon ko maanav-jati kaa ek vishesh samooh kaha jane laga to usne ye saafe kiya kii uskaa arth vanshaj se he na kii vishesh nasl se, usne kaha ki bhashaa-shaastra or maanav-vigyaan kaa mishran ek khatarnaak siddhant he. usne kaha ki "bhashaa vigyaan or maanav-vigyaan bahut zyada bhinn nahin ho sakate... lekin main jarur doharaana chahta hum jo maine pahave bahut baar kaha he ki aryan khun ko lambaa sheersh vaala kahana galat he." usne apane virodh ko 1888 main likhe apane lekh biography of vards and da home of da aaryaas main bhi doharaaya.
 mular kaa ye jaateey maanav shaastra ke vikaas kaa jawaab darasal arthur de gobiniu ke liye tha jo ye maanata tha ki hind europee sarvshereshth jati kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai. baad ke kai lekhak jaise francisi lekhak Vacher de Lapouge, ne apni kitaab L'Aryen, main ye tark detaa he ki is visisth jati ko shirishya suchi kaa prayog kar or anya sanketakon se unke shaareerik banaavat se bhi pahachaanaa ja saktaa he. unhone tark diya ki lambey sar vaale europea uttari europe main paae jate the jo aam taur par "brachiosephalik" vaale logon par shashan kiya karte the. .
 shwet nasl vaale aryanon kaa vibhaajan vishuddh rup se ek bhashaayi prayaas he na ki maanavvigyaan kaa armbh bindu, maanav-shaastra kaa vaastavik yaa prakrirthik vibhaajan kaa centre-bindu nordic, alpine or bumadhyasagariya jabaki bhashaai etbaar se ye wo samooh tha jiskaa sambandh asian or africa bhashiyon se tha. haalanki baad main kuch maanav vigyanio or puraatatv mahiron ne "aryan" ke is bhashaayi vargeekaran ko chhetriyata se sambhddha kar diya, vishesh rup se uttari europe se.
 19ve shatabdi main is mat ko kaaphi prasiddhi mili. 19ve shatabdi ke madhya main kaha jane laga ki aaryon ki utpatti dakshin-pashchimi bhaag, jise aaj rus ke naam se janaa jaataa he, main hui. lekin 19ve shatabdi ke ant tak aryan mul kaa tark bhaag ko chunauti milane lagi, jisamen aryan kaa mul pracheen germany yaa skandinavia kaa hone kaa vichaar tha, nayaa tark aryano kaa sambandh germany se sabit karne laga yaa phir un deshon main jahaan arya-kul ke avshesh milte hai. aaryon ke german mul ko, puratatvvetta gustaaf kojina ne pramukhataa se badhaava diya or daawa kiya ki proto-indo-european log niolithik germany ke corded ware samskruti ke samaan the. 20ve shatabdi main buddhajiviyon ne is tark kaa jamkar prachaar-prasar kiya, ye "corded-noradics" kii avadharana karleton es. kun ki 1939 main prakashit kitaab da reces of europe main dekhi ja sakati he.
 is siddhant or pariklpana ko duusare maanav-shastriyon ne chunauti di. germany ke rudolph varcho sukhi khopadiyon ke adhyan kaa siddhant diya, usne nordic yani uttari europe se aryano ke sambandh ke rahasy kaa zordaar khandan kiya, 1885 main carlsruhe main yojit puratatvavidon ki congress main varcho ke sahaayak joseph kolmaan ne kaha ki europea chaahe wo england ke hon yaa german ke yaa phir french or spanish hon sabhi mishrit jatiyon se hai, iske atirikt usne ghoshnaa ki ki kapaal - vigyaan ke anusaar koi bhi jati kisi se sarvshereshth nahin he.
 varcho ke is siddhant ne kai vivaadon ko janm diya. hoston stewart chemberlen jo ki aaryon ki shreshthata kaa kaayal tha, joseph kollmann ke tarkon kaa vistaar se jawaab diya. haalanki "aryan jati" kii kalpana kaaphi mashhoor thi, vishesh kar germany main, lekin kuch log aise bhi the jo iske sakht khilaaf the, otto shreder, rudolf von jhering or robert hartman ne varcho ke siddhanton kaa puraa samarthan kiya, robert ne to maanav-shaastra se "aryan" shabd ke istemaal par hi paabandi lagane ki maang kar daali.
