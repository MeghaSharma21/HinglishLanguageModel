 
 mitrak yaa betaa santori, jiskaa bair naam β Centauri yaa β Cen he or jise hadar ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he, naraturang taramandal kaa doosraa sab se roshan tara he. yah prithvi se dikhne vaale taron main se dasva sab se roshan tara bhi he. taron ke shreneekaran ke hisaab se ise "B1 III" kii shrenee di jaati he.
 san 1935 main jone voot naamak khagolashastri ne kulasa kiya ke mitrak vastav main ek doharaa tara he . baad main pataa chalaa ke in donon taron main se mukhya tara vastav main swayan dwitara he, yani ke kul milakar yah teen taron kaa jhund he.
