 ibola virus 
 yah vartmaan main ek gambhir beemaari kaa rup dhaaran kar chukaa he. is beemaari main sharir main nason se khun baahar aana shuru ho jaataa he, jisase andruni raktastrav prarambh ho jaataa he. yah ek atyant ghaatak rog he. isamen 90% rogiyon kii mrityu ho jaati he.
 is rog kii pahchaan sarvapratham san 1976 main ibola nadi ke paas sthit ek gaanv main kii gai thi. isi kaaran iskaa naam ibola pada.ibola ek aisa rog he jo marij ke sampark main aane se failata he. .marij ke paseene,marij ke khun yaa,shwaas se bachakar rahen..iske chapet main aakar typhoid,cholera,bukhaar,or mansapeshiyon main dard hota he. .bal jhadane lagate hai. .nashon se manshapeshiyon main khun utar aata he. .isse bachaav karne ke liye khud ko satark rakhana kii upaay he. .ibola kaa naam kaagon kii ek sahaayak nadi ibola ke upar padaa he. .or virus sabase pehle africa main paaya gaya tha. .ibola ke marijon kii 50 se 80 fisadi maut record kii gai he.
 yah rog paseene or laar se failata he. sankramit khun or mel ke sidhe sampark main aane se bhi yah failata he. iske atirikt, yon sambandh or ibola se sankramit shav ko thik tarah se vyavasthit na karne se bhi yah rog ho saktaa he. yah sankramak rog he.
 iske lakshan hai- ulti-dast, bukhaar, sirdard, raktastrav, ankhen laal hona or gale main kaff. aksar iske lakshan prakat hone main teen saptaah tak kaa samay lag jaataa he.
 is rog main rogi kii twachaa galane lagati he. yahan tak ki haath-pair se lekar puraa sharir gal jaataa he. aise rogi se dur rah kar hi is rog se bachaa ja saktaa he.
 abhi is beemaari kaa koi ilaj nahin he. iske liye koi dawa nahin banaai ja saki he. iskaa koi anti-virus bhi nahin he. iske liye tikaa viksit karne ke prayaas jari hai; haalanki abhi tak aisa koi tikaa maujuud nahin he.
