 sopperon, hungary kaa ek nagar he jo pashchim main austria se lagati hui seemaa ke nikat sthit he, alps kii talahati main, vienna se 60 kimi or budapest se 220 kimi. is nagar ke log apane desh or grihnagar ke prati deshbhakti ke liye jane jate hai. ek prasiddh smarak 'nishtha kaa dwaar' yahan ke logon kaa apane desh ke prati vishwaas kaa pratik he or 1921 main banaaya gaya tha.
