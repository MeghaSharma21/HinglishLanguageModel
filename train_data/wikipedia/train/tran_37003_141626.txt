 nirdeshaank: 32°10′N 75°25′E / 32.17°N 75.42°E / 32.17; 75.42pathankot bharat ke punjab rajya kaa ek shahar he. 2011 main yah pathankot jile kii rajdhani ban gaya. 1849 se pehle yah nurpur kii rajdhani tha.
 samrik drushti se pathankot bharat ke sarvaadhik mahatvapurn thikaanon main se ek he. yahaan par vayusena stesan, sena gola-barood depo evam do bakhtarband brigade evam bakhtarband ikaiyaan hai. janvari 2016 main pathankot vayusena stesan par aatankwaadi hamalaa hua.
 yah taparweare ke baratanon ke utpaadan ke liye bhi prasiddh he.
