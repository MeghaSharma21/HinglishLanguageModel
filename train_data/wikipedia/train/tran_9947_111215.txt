 gogunda rajasthan kaa ek kasbaa or tehsil mukhyalay he. yah udaipur se 35 kimi uttar-pashchim main sthit he. yah aravali kii pahadiyon par unchai main sthit he. yaheen par san 1527 main maharana pratap kaa rajbhishek hua tha. pehle gogunda riyasat thi. badi riyasaton me iskaa ullekh miltaa he. maharana pratap singh ne apni rajdhani banaaya tha. yahan se pure mewar kaa rajya sambhalate the.
