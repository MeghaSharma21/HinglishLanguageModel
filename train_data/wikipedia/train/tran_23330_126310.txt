 upgrah nauvahan pranali kritrim upgrahon kii ek pranali ko kehete hai jo vishwa main sarvatra bhu-spesial pojishning pradaan karne main samarth ho. is pranali kii sahaayta se chote electranik resiver apni sthiti kii atyant yatharthata se gananaa kar lete hai. ye electranik resiver vartmaan sthaneey samay kii gananaa bhi kar lete hai.
