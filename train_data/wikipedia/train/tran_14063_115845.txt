 jalanikaas, sadak tathaa sanlagn kshetra ke satahi tathaa bhumigat phaltu jal ko dur le janaa he. sadakon ke dirghajivan tathaa unke yatayaat kii suvidha ko banaae rakhane ke liye jalanikaas kii samuchit vyavastha atyaavashyak he.
 jalanikaas ke sambandh main teen baaten aavashyak he: 
 mukhya maarg ke jalanikaas ke liye puliya, pul tathaa upasetu hote hai. inkaa kaary prakritik stroton main behate pani, yaa sadak par, yaa sadak ke aasapaas ekatrit pani ko nikaalnaa hota he. puliyon or pulon se pani sadak ke neeche se nikalataa he, kintu upasetu se pani sadak ke prishth par bhi bah saktaa he. isse upasetu jal main dub saktaa he. par aisi sthiti kuch mahatvaheen sadakon par hi yaa aisi sadakon par jahaan yatayata bahut kam he, kuch seemaa tak bardaasht kii ja sakati he.
 sadakon ke prishthiya jal ke achche nikaas ke liye sadaken aisi banaai jaati hai ki unamem alp ubhaar ho, taaki pani kinare kii naaliyon main bahakar nikal jaae. yadi sadak parshvik dhaalu jameen par ho, to uski unchi dhaal par ek duusari nikaas naalee banaakar nikatatam puliya yaa pul se mila di jaati he, taaki pani usase nikal jaay. inhen jalroke naalee kehete hai.
 bhumigat jal ke nikaas kaa yah uddeshy hota he ki sadakon kii mitti apekshaya shushk dasha main bani rahe. jalanikaas ke liye sadak ke neeche sachhidr naaliyaan baithai jaati hai, jisase adhobhumi jal kii sataha ko nicha karne main sahaayta milti he. duusari reeti main sadak kii bagal main gahari naaliyaan khodi jaati hai. inhen phir geele pathar se bhar dete hai. sadak ke neeche kaa adhobhumi jal in gahari naaliyon main riskar jaataa or phir dur bah jaataa he.
