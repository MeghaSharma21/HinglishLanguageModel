 madelin davis ek america clasik mahila bluez gayika thi, joki 1920 ke dashak main recording kalakaar ke rup main sakriy thi. unke sabase prasiddh sangeet trek rahe hai "cocola bluej" or "itz red hot". vah behatar gyaat recording kalakaaron, jiseki maa rene, basi smith, clara smith, mosel alderson, victoria spive, sippi wales, or bartha "chippi" hill kii samakaleen thi. sangeet ke alaava unke jivan ke bare main kam hi gyaat he.
 davis ne apni das recording chicago main paramount record ke liye kii thi. unka pehla satr 1927 main hua. unhone apane sangeet album "harri sandown bluej" or "climbing mountain bluej usee varsh sitambar main record kiye evam do or gane november main record kiye. 
 oktober 1928 main, davis ne apana antim recording satr apane saathiyon georgia tom dorese joki piano vadak evam tampa joki guitar vadak the, unke samarthan se purna kiya. unhone chaar geet record kiye: "gold tooth mama bluej," "deth bel bluej," "tu blike bed," or "itz red hot". 
  "varid down vid bluez" ke do vaikalpik sanskarnon evam "harri sandown bluej" "climbing mountain bluej", "land ladies phutsteps," "winter bluez" or "cocola bluej" unki sankalan album femail bluej singrs main shaamil the, bhaag 5: si/di/i, joki 1997 main jari dastauez record he.
