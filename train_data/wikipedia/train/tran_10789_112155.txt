 mahaprasthanik parv main koi upparv nahin or maatr 3 adhyay hai.
 is parv main draupadi sahit pandavon kaa mahaprasthaan varnit he. vrishnivanshiyon kaa shraadh karke, prajajanon kii anumati lekar draupadi ke saath yudhisthir aadi pandav mahaprasthaan karte hai, kintu yudhisthir ke atirikt sabakaa dehpaat maarg main hi ho jaataa he. indra or dharm se yudhisthir kii baatcheet hoti he or yudhisthir ko sasharir swarg miltaa he.
