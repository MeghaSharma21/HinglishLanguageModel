 brahm samaaj bharat kaa ek saamaajik dharmik andolan he, jisane bengal ke punarjagran yug ko prabhavit kiya. iske pravartak, raja rammohan raay, apane samay ke vishisht samaaj sudhaarak the. 1828 main brahm samaaj ko raja rammohan or dwarkanath tagore ne sthapit kiya tha. iskaa ek uddeshy bhinn bhinn dharmik aasthaaon main banti hui janata ko ek jute karnaa tathaa samaaj main faili kuritiyon ko dur karnaa he. samaaj kaa vaastavik arth hota sam+aaj.
 san 1815 main rajaraam mohan raay ne "athmiya sabha" kii sthapana kii. wo 1828 main brahm samaaj ke naam se janaa gaya. devendranath thakur ne use aage badhaayaa. baad main keshav chandra sen  jude. un donon ke bich matbhed ke kaaran keshav chandra sen ne "bharatvarshiya brahmsamaj" naam kii sanstha kii sthapana kii.
 raja rammohan raay dharmik satya ko khojane ke pyaas main prerit ho kar udaar man main sabhi mahatvapurn dharm ke shaastra samooh adhyayan kiya. is prakaar se vah sanskrit bhashaa main hindu dharm shastron, jaise ki veda, adhyayan karne ke saath, vah arabi bhashaa main kuraran or hebrew bhashaa or greek bhashaa main bible paath kiya he.
