 pravaali, balui bhitti yaa balua bhitti kisi pravaal bhitti kii sataha par nirmit ek chota, kam oonchai vaala retila dweep hota he. pravaliyaan prashanth, atlantic or hind mahasaagaron ke ushnkaribandheey paryaavaran vaale sampurna kshetron main paayi jaati hai, jahaan ye hajaaron logon ko rahane or krishi ke liye yogya bhoomi uplabdh karaati hai. inke aasapaas kaa shail paristhitiki tantra bhi dweep nivaasiyon ko bhojan or nirmaan saamagree uplabdh karaata he.
