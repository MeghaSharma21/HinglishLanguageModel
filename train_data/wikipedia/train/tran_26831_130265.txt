 vishnu sadashiv kokaja vishwa hindu parishad ke nae antarrashtriya adhyaksh hai. san 2003 se 2008 tak ve himachal pradesh ke rajyapal the. unhone bharat vikaas parishad ke adhyaksh ke rup main bhi seva di he.
 vishnu sadashiv kokeje kaa janm madhya pradesh main 6 sitambar 1 9 3 9 main hua tha. unhone indore se elelbi puraa karne ke baad 1964 main kanoon kaa abhyaas karnaa shuru kiya. unhen 28 july 1990 ko madhya pradesh ucch nyaayalay kaa nyaayadhish niyukt kiya gaya tha. unhone 2001 main 11 maheenon ke liye rajasthan ucch nyaayalay ke kaaryakari mukhya nyaayadhish ke rup main kaary kiya or sitambar 2002 main bharat ke sarvocch nyaayalay ke varisht adhivaktaa ke rup main namit kiya gaya tha. 
 ve 8 may 2003 ko himachal pradesh ke rajyapal bane, or 1 9 july 2008 tak is pad par karyrat the.
