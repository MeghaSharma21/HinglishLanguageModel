 rashtri sanskrit santhaan nai dilli main sthit ek shaikshanik santhaan he. yah bharat sarkaar dwaara purnatah vittposhit manit vishvavidhyalay he. bharat sarkaar ne sanskrit ayog kii anushansa ke aadhaar par sanskrit ke vikaas tathaa prachaar-prasar hetu sanskrit sambaddh kendra sarkaar kii nitiyon evam kaaryakramon ke kriyanvayan ke uddeshy se 15 oktober 1970 ko ek swytt sanghathan ke rup main isaki sthapana kii. 7 may 2002 ko maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantralay, bharat sarkaar ne ise bahuparisareey manit vishvavidhyalay ke rup main ghoshit kiya.
 rashtri sanskrit santhaan ke sanstha ke bahirnium main ghoshit uddeshy is prakaar he-
 santhaan kii sthapana ke uddeshy parmparik sanskrit vidya va shodh kaa prachaar, vikaas va protsaahan he or unka paalan karte hueh-
 kendriya maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantri santhaan kaa kuladhyaksh hota he. prabandhanparishad santhaan kii sarvshereshth niti-nirdharak parishad he. rashtri sanskrit santhaan kaa kulapati joprabandhan parishad kaa adhyaksh hota he, santhaan kaa mukhya shaikshanik evam prashasnic adhikari he.
 rashtri sanskrit santhaan nimnlikhit kaaryakramon ke kriyanvayan hetu kritasankalp he.
 rashtri sanskrit santhaan nimnlikhit kaaryo ke liye sadaa prayatnsheel he--
 santhaan ke angabhoot parisaron main santhaan dwaara nirmit paathyakram ke aadhaar par prak-shastri se lekar acharya str kaa shikshan pradaan kiya jaataa he. santhaan dwaara sanchalit or santhaan se sambaddh sanskrit sansthaayen bhi ukt paathyakram ke anusaar adhyaapan-kaary sampann karti hai.
 parisaron main shikshan abhyaas par bal dete hue ek shaikshanik satr ke shikshak-prashikshan paathyakram kaa sanchaalan kiya jaataa he jisase sanskrit main shiksha acharya evam shiksha shastri oopaadhi pradaan kii jaati he.

 santhaan akhil bhartiya str par anaupchaarik sanskrit shikshan kendron ke madhyam se kramik sanskrit swadhyay saamagree jise diksha paathyakram kehete he, kaa sanchaalan karata he.

 santhaan shastreey granthon ke vishisht adhyayan evam shikshan hetu vishishtadhyayan kaarykram kaa aayojan karata he.
 santhaan bhashaa sanskrit shikshan kii mudrit evam electranik saamagree kaa nirmaan evam uskaa prachaar-prasar bhi karata he.

 apane parisaron tathaa sambaddh sansthaaon main parmparik paddati se sanskrit shikshan hetu prathama se acharya tak sabhi kakshaon ke paathyakram kaa nirdhaaran karata he.
 santhaan parmparik paddati se sanskrit shikshan hetu desh ke vividh praanton main apane parisaron kii sthapana, adhigrahan va sanchaalan karata he. vartmaan main santhaan ke nai dilli sthit mukhyalay ke atirikt gyaarah angabhoot parisar he. santhaan ke antargat abhi tak nimnlikhit parisar desh ke vibhinn rajyon main sanskrit vidya ke kendra ke rup me kaam kar rahe he.

 santhaan sanskrit bhashaa or sahitya ke sanvardhan tathaa prachaar hetu maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantralay, bharat sarkaar dwaara pravartit nimnlikhit vividh yojanaon kaa karyanvayan karata haih –
 is yojana ke antargat sanskrit ke vikhyath seva-nivrutt vidwaanon ko santhaan ke parisaron tathaa anya sanskrit sansthaaon main do varsh kii avadhi ke liye niyukt kiya jaataa he. ve shikshakon or chaatro ko sanskrit vaanmay ke vibhinn shastron/vidya visheshon main gahan shikshan pradaan karte hai.
 is yojana ke adheen santhaan lekhakon or prakashkon se sanskrit granthon kaa kray karke sanskrit sansthaaon ko nickshulk vitarit karata he.
 aitihaasik siddhanton ke aadhaar par 1500 i.pus. se 1900 i. tak kii avadhi kaa ek ati vyapak sanskrit shabdkosh dekan kalege, puna ke dwaara taiyaar kiya ja rahaa he jise vittiya sahaayta di jaati he.
 is yojana ke antargat chune hue sanghathanon ko jyotish, karm-kand, puralipi-shaastra, suchi-nirmaan, pandulipi vigyaan, sanskrit ashulipi or tankan aadi vishyon main kaaryshaalaaon ke aayojan hetu tathaa vyavsaayik prashikshan dene ke liye vittiya sahaayta pradaan kii jaati he.
 is yojana ke antargat desh ke vibhinn bhaagon main 23 sansthaayen santhaan ke adheen chal rahi he. in sansthaaon ko vyay kii aavarti madon par 95% tathaa anavarti madon par 75% anudaan diya jaataa he.
 santhaan bharat ke raashtrapati dwaara prativarsh sammanit sanskrit ke 15, praakrit kaa 1, arabi ke 3 or farsi ke 3 prakhyat vidwaanon main se prayek ko 50,000/- rupe kii vaarshik maandey raashi pradaan karti he. yuva sanskrit vidwaan ke sammanarth prativarsh ek lakh rupe kaa maharishi baadaraayan puraskaar armbh kiya gaya.
 santhaan prayek varsh desh ke vibhinn bhaagon main aath shastreey vishyon par akhil bhartiya vakspardhaa yojit karata he jisase parmparik sanskrit chaatro ko shastreey sanskrit bhashaa main aashu bhaashan main protsaahit kiya ja sake. shlokantyakshari, samasya-purti tathaa shalaka pratiyogitaaon kaa aayojan bhi kiya jaataa he.
 is yojana ke antargat chaynit sanghathanon ko sanskrit shikshakon ke vetan, chaatro kii chaatravrutti, pustkaalay anudaan or sanstha ke bhawan-nirmaan ke rup main vittiya sahaayta di jaati he.
 santhaan anterrashtriya samitiyon ke sahyog se anterrashtriya str par sanskrit sammelnon kaa aayojan karata he. is sandarbh main ullekhneey he ki santhaan ne pratham vishwa sanskrit sammelan kaa aayojan nai dilli main varsh 1972 main, pancham vishwa sanskrit sammelan kaa aayojan varanasi main varsh 1981 main or dasham vishwa sanskrit sammelan kaa aayojan bangalore main 3-9 janvari 1997 ko kiya.
 rashtri sanskrit santhaan ke mukhyalay main prashasan anubhag niyamon, viniyamon or prakriyaanusaar aantarik vyavastha kaary karata he. yah santhaan ke vibhinn angeebhoot parisaron ko adhik prabhavi or kaarykushal banane hetu aavashyak sthaapan sahaayta pradaan karata he. yah anubhag pramukh rup se saamaanya prashasan, sthapana sambandhi kaary, seva or bhoomi aapurti or bhawan adhigrahan, nae parisaron kii sthapana or prabandhan parishad tathaa vitt, samiti kii baithkon ke sanchaalan aadi kaary karata he.
 vitt vibhaag kaa samast kaary upanideshak kii dekharekh main kiya jaataa he. santhaan-mukhyalay sthit vitt vibhaag kaa kaary aay-vyay kaa vivaran taiyaar karnaa, anudaan kii prapti, mukhyalay evam uske antargat parisaron ke paksh main unka vitaran, vittiya vyavastha or vaarshik lekha-vivaran taiyaar karnaa vitt-vibhaag se hi santhaan evam parisaron ke adhikariyon evam karmchaariyon kii bhavishya-nidhi ko surakshit rakhane tathaa yatha samay vitaran kii vyavastha kii jaati he. mantralay evam santhaan kii yojana ke asweekrut paathyakramon ke chaatr-chaatraon kii chaatravrutti-raashi kaa vitaran-kaary, mantralay kii shastrachudamani ityadi katipay yojanaayen, sammanit sanskrit panditon ke liye maandey raashi kaa bhugtan tathaa vibhinn shodh sansthaanon evam sammaan se sammanit vidvajjanon ke liye dey vaarshik raashi kaa vitaran bhi santhaan ke vitt vibhaag se hi jaataa he.
 is vibhaag ke mahatvapurn uttardaayitv haih -
 is anubhag kaa mukhya daayitv, vibhinn parikshaon kaa aayojan tathaa santhaan ke pariksha patron kaa moolyankan santhaan ke dwaara prathama se acharya, shikshashasthri tathaa vidyavaridhi jaisi vibhinn parikshaon kaa sanchaalan kiya jaataa he. in parikshaon main angeebhoot parisaron tathaa sambaddh sansthaaon ke chaatr pravesh pate he. ye pariksha shaikshik parishad dwaara nirdhaarit maargdarshi siddhanton or is uddeshy se nirdhaarit paathyakramon ke anusaar sanchalit kii jaati he.
 is vibhaag dwaara sanskrit ke prachaar ke liye mantralay ke vibhinn yojanaon kaa kriyanvayan kiya jaataa he-
 is yojana ke antargat sanghathan/sansthaaon ko sanskrit ke prachaar prasar ke liye kiye ja rahe kriyakalapon or unka vistaar karne yaa nai yojanaayen banane ke liye vittiya sahaayta di jaati he.
 is yojana ke anragat akhil bhartiya str pap aath saastkeey vishyon me vakspardhaon kaa auyojan kiya jaataa he. saath hi antyaakshari evam samsyapurti spardhaaen thabhi yojit kii jaati he, jinamein desh k sabhi praanton se chaatr bhaag lete he.
 sanskrit bhashaa me uplabdh vibhinn shastron main shikshakon tathaa shodh chaatro ke vishesh maargdarshan ke liye viyapeethon evam anya sanskrit sansthaaon main suprasiddh seva nivrutt sanskrit vidwaanon ko is yojana ke antargat do varsh ke liye niyukt kiya jaataa he.
 is yojana ke antargat sanskrit chaatro ke liye jyotish, karmkand, puralipishastra, pandulipi vigyaan, sanskrit ashulipi-tankan aadi kaa alpakaalik vyavsaayik prashikshan sanchalit karne ke liye sanskrit sansthaaon ko aarthik anudaan diya jaataa he.
