 isik kul purvi kirgizstan ke uttar tiyaan shaan parvaton main sthit ek band jalsambhar vaali jhil he. yah vishwa kii dasvin sabase badi jhil he or caspian sagar ke baad duniya kii duusari sab se badi khaari jhil he. kirgiz bhashaa main iske naam kaa arth 'garam jhil' he kyonki barf se dhake pahaado se ghire hone ke baavjood bhi iskaa pani barf main nahin jam jaataa. isik kul kaa pani khaaraa hone ke kaaran hi yah kabhi nahin jamata . kirgizstan kii sarkaar ne yahan ek surakshit prakritik udyaan bana diya he. itihaaskaaron ko yahan khudaai karte hue ek 2,500 varsh purana shahar mila he or is par anusandhan jari he.
 isik kul 182 kimi lambi he or isaki sab se adhik chaudaai ek sthaan par 60 kimi tak pahunch jaati he. iskaa kul kshetrafal 6,236 varg kimi he. agar pahari jhilein dekhi jaaen to dakshin america ke bolivia desh kii titikaka jhil ke baad yah duusari sab se badi parvatiy jhil he. isaki gaharaai 668 meter tak jaati he or yah pahaado main 1,607 meter kii oonchai par sthit he. kul milakar 118 nadiyaan or jharne aakar is nadi main vilay ho jate hai. bahut se band jalsambharon kii tarah iskaa pani bhi kaafii khaaraa he or iskaa khaaraapan 0.6% mapa gaya he. yah samudr ke ausat khaarepan se to kam he lekin kisi jhil ke liye bahut he.
