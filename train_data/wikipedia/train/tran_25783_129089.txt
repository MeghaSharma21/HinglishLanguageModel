 parabangnee kiran ek prakaar kaa vidyut chumbakeey vikiran hai, jinaki tarang dairghy pratyaksh prakaash se choti ho evam komal eks kiran se adhik ho. inaki aisa isliye kaha jaataa he, kyonki, inkaa varnakram liye hota he vidyut chumbakeey tarang jinaki aavriti maanav dwaara darshan yogya bangnee varna se upar hoti hai.para kaa matlab hota he ki is se adhik arthath begni se adhik aavriti kii tarang. 
 inaki khoj is prekshan se bahut kuch jubii hui hai, ki rajat nirey lavan dhoop packne par kaale padd jate hai. 1801 main johnn wilham reter ne ek vishisht prekshan kiya, ki bangnee prakaash ke pare apratyaksh kirane, rajat nirey ke lavan main bhiige kaagaj ko kaala kar deteen he. usne unhen di-oxidiging kirane kaha jisase ki unki rasyneey kriyaaon par bal diya ja sake saath hi inhen varnakram ke duusare sire par upasthit ooshm kirnon se pruthak pahachaanaa ja sake. kalantar main ek saral shabd rasinic kirane prayog hua. jo ki unnisvin shatabdi tak chalaa, jab jaakar donon ke hi naam badale or parabangneeevam adhorakt' kehalaae.
 naam bangnee se pare yani parabangnee isliye pana kyonki ye pratyaksh prakaash kii sarvaadhik aavriti evam nunatam tarang dairghy vaale bangnee se bhi adhik aavriti vaali, saath hi ; kam tarang dairghy vaali hotin hai. inhen angreji main ultra woilet reyz kaha jaataa he.
