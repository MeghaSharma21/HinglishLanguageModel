 jansankhya vruddhi kisi bhi kshetra main logon kii sankhya badhane ko kaha jaataa he. puure duniya main manushya kii jansankhya har saal lagbhag 8.3 karod yaa 1.1% kii dar se badhati ja rahi he. varsh 1800 ko puure vishwa kii jansankhya lagbhag ek arab thi, jo 2017 tak bad kar 7.6 arab ho gai he. aage bhi isaki sankhya main badhaav kii hi ummid he or ye andaja lagaaya gaya he ki 2030 ke madhya tak ye aabaadi 8.6 arab ho jaaegi or 2050 tak 9.8 arab tak ho jaaegi. 2100 tak isaki aabaadi 11.2 arab tak ho sakati he.
 18veen sadi ke dauraan jab audyogic kranti shuru hui to pashchimi deshon main bahut hi teji se jansankhya vruddhi shuru ho gai thi. is jansankhya vruddhi ke kaaran ko jaanane ke liye british vaigyanik thomas mccayon ne khoj shuru kiya tha. usne is khoj se praapt jaankaree kaa prakaashan modern rise of popyulation main kiya. 
 united nesan ke anusaar 1990 or 2010 ke madhya vishwa kii jansankhya main 30% yaa 1.6 kharab manushyon kii vruddhi hui he. is vruddhi main sabase aage bharat or china hai. pratishat ke hisaab se yah sankhya sabase zyada sanyukt arab emirate main 315% or katar main 271% he.
