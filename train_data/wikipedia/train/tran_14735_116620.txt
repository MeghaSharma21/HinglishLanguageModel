 koyal yaa kokil 'kukku kul' kaa pakshi he, jiskaa vaigyanik naam 'eudinamis scolopekas' he. nar koyal neelaapan liye kaala hota he, to maadaa teetar kii tarah dhabbedaar chitakabari hoti he. nar koyal hi gaata he. uski ankhen laal va pankh peeche kii ore lambey hote hai. need parjivita is kul ke pakshiyon kii vishesh nemat he yani ye apana ghosla nahin banaati. ye duusare pakshiyon visheshkar kauon ke ghonsle ke andon ko gira kar apana anda usamen rakh detii he.
