 hafiz mohammad abdul kareem, ke rup main "munshi" main janaa jaataa he, maharani victoria, jo uske shasankaal ke antim pandrah varshon ke dauraan use sneh praapt ek bhartiya muslim parichar tha.kareem jhansi british bharat main, ek aspataal sahaayak ke bete ke paas paidaa hua tha. 1887 main victoria ke swarna jayanti varsh, kareem do rani ko naukar banane ke chaynit bharatiyon main se ek tha. victoria use ek mahaan sauda kii tarah aayaa or use "munshi", hindi - urdu aksar "clerk" yaa "shikshak" ke rup main anuvaadit shabd kaa sheershak diya he. victoria use use bhartiya sachiv niyukt kiya he, use sammaan ke saath bauchhaar or uske liye bharat main ek bhoomi anudaan praapt.
 kareem or rani ke bich kareebi rishta royal gharelu bheetar gharshan ke netritv ke anya sadasyon swayan use behatar mahasoos kiya. rani uske saath uski yatra par kareem lene par jor diya, jo uske or uske aane ke bich bahes kaa kaaran bana. 1901 main victoria kii maut ke baad uske uttaradhikari, edward saptam, bharat kareem laute or jabti or victoria ke saath munshi patrachaar ke vinaash kaa aadesh diya. kareem baad main agra ke paas chupchaap rahete the, sampatti he ki victoria uske liye vyavastha kii thi par 46 varsh kii aayu main apni mrityu tak.
