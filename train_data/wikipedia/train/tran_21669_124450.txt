 bhartiya ucch adhyayan santhaan, shimla kii sthapana varsh 1965 main socity panjikaran adhinium, 1860 ke tahat kii gai thi or yah raashtrapati nivas, shimla main sthit he] yah santhaan jivan tathaa vichaar sambandhi moulik vishyon evam samasyaon ke bare main ni:shulk evam srujanaatmak anveshan ke liye ek awasaiya kendra he.
 is santhaan ke kaary is prakaar hai :-
 patrikayein:
 'samarhil' ek academik samiksha patrika hai jiskaa prakaashan varsh mein doc baar kiya jaataa hai is patrika kly ek bhaag mein manviki or samaajik vigyaan par naveenatam prakashnein par samiksha karnaa mukhya udayshya he. pratishthit viddhanthan kly likh or buddhijivion kly saath kiyaa gayi saakshaatkaar saamaanya pathakein kii ruchi kly street hai.
 'stdies in humenitez and seashal sinsez':
  studies in humenitiz and seashal saises patrika varsh mein doc baar prakashit kii jaati hai iskaa ek ank mein ek vishrush vishay par likh prakashit kiyaa jataa he. iskaa doosarii ank mein manviki or saamaajik vigyaan kly viddhanthan kly likh prakashit kiyaa jataa he tathaa isames vishayein par kiyaa gayi shodh kii naveenatam jaankaree samahit hoti hai. santhaan kly paas is samay tak 300 ski adhik prakashnein mein adhyaaatation ddhara prastut monograph, santhaan mein aaypjit sangkshthiyaan kii sampadit kaarywahi tathaa vishisht atithiyaan kly bhaashan, evam agunthuk tak viddhanthan tathaa adhyaaatation ddhara prastut likh ityadi hai. santhaan kly prakashnein kaa drush vidrush kly shoadhjagat mein yogadan ullekhneey he. manoj kumaar kishangarh ke dwaara prakashit kiya gaya
