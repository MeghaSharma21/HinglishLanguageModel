 miranda arun grah ke paanch gol akar vaale bade prakritik upgrahon main se sab se chota or arun kii sab se qareebi parikrama karte hua upgrah he. dhyaan rahe ke is se chote arun ke ird-gird 20 se adhik anya upgrah bhi hai, lekin ve gol kii bajaae besange akar ke hai. isaki khoj 16 farvari 1948 main jarard kyper ne doorabeen se kii thi. miranda kii paas se tasviren keval wayager dwitiya yaan ke janvari 1986 main arun ke paas se guzarane par leen gayi thim. miranda kii sataha dekhne main oobad-khabad lagati he or vaigyanikon kaa maananaa he ke vah adhiktar jame hue pani kii barf kii bani hui he. yah bhi soch he ke miranda ke andar pathar bhi maujuud hai. is chandramaa kii gahari khaaiyon ko dekhakar lagata he ke ateet main isapar bahut bukamp aate the jis vajah se isaki zamin itani tuuti or tedi-medhi he.
