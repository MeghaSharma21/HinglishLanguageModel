 padam vibhushan sammaan bharat sarkaar dwaara diya jane vaala doosraa ucch naagarik sammaan he, jo desh ke liye asainik kshetron main bahumoolya yogadan ke liye diya jaataa he. yah sammaan bharat ke raashtrapati dwaara diya jaataa he. is sammaan kii sthapana 2 janvari 1954 main kii gayi thi. bharat ratna ke baad yah doosraa pratishthit sammaan he. padam vibhushan ke baad teesraa naagarik sammaan padam bhushan he. yah sammaan kisi bhi kshetra main vishisht or ullekhneey seva ke liye pradaan kiya jaataa he. isamen sarkari karmchaariyon dwaara kii gai sevaayen bhi shaamil hai.
