 yom al-qiyama yaa yom ad-deen : islam main chha: vishwaason main aakhri punerjeevan kaa din he, isi ko yom al-qiyama he. is kii koi prayek taarikh nahin bataai gayi laqin kuch chizon ko zarur suchit kiya gaya. quran main iskaa vistrut rup se prastaav kiya gaya he. 
 quran main sura al-kiyamh main is kaa zikr he. or hadison main bhi iskaa zikr vistaar rup se kiya gaya he. is din ko faisale kaa din bhi kaha jaataa he. 
 quran main "aakhri faisalaa" yani qiamat ke din kaa prastaav kai jagahon or aayaton main kiya gaya he. mul rup se yah baaten jaananaa aavashyak he.
