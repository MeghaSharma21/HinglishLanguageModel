 purvanchal vikaas kshetra nepal kaa ek praant he jo nepal ke paanch vikaas kshetron main se ek vikaas kshetra he. yah nepal ke sabase purvi bhaag he. is ke purv main bharat kaa sikkim tathaa pashchim bengal kaa darjeeling tathaa pashchim main nepal kaa madhyamanchal vikaas kshetra tathaa uttar main china kaa tibet tathaa dakshin main bharat kaa bihar sthit he. purvanchal kaa mukhyalay dhankuta main sthit he. purvanchal main 3 anchal tathaa 16 gilen hai.
