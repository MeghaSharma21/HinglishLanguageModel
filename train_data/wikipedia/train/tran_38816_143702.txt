 rabbaa main kya karun amrit sagar chopra dwaara nirdeshit or moti sagar dwaara nirmit 2013 kii bollywood hasya vidha film he. akaash chopra, arshad varsi, paresh rawal, tahiraa kochhar or riya sen mukhya abhinay bhoomikaaon main hai.
 isaki kahani do bhaaiyon kii he jinka vivaah aam bhartiya tarike se dilli main hota he.
 film do bhaaiyon kii kahani he. arshad varsi akaash ke chote bhai hai. jo jald hi shaadi ke bandhan main bandhane vaala he. shaadi se pehle akaash ko arshad varsi or uske teen mamaa milakar khushhaal shaadishuda jindagi ke gur sikhaate hai.
