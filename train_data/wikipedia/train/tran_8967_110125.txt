 goa mukti sangram, goa mukti aandolan yaa goa mukti sangharsh san 1961 main bhartiya sashastr sena dwaara kiya gaya ek abhiyaan tha jiske parinaam swaroop goa ko portugal ke aadhipaty se mukt karakar bharat main mila liya gaya. isamen vayusena, jalsena evam thalsena - teenon ne bhaag liya. yah sangharsh 36 ghante se adhik samay tak chalaa. isko "aaparishan vijay" kaa kootnaam diya gaya tha.
