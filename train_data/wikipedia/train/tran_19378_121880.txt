 nidhi raazdaan ek bhartiya patrkaar va television vyaktitv hai. vah enditivi 24*7 ke sho left, rhit or centre kii praathmik ankar hai. yah kaarykram aam taur par har shaam nayi dilli ke ek studio se live prasaarit hota he jon ki samaacharon ko or usase judi bahes kii charchaa karata he. 1999 se apane kariyar kii shuruyaat karne vaali nidhi raazdaan ne apane kariyar main kai samachar kaaryakramon ko cover kiya hai or kai shrankhalaao kii mejabani kii hai, va kai baar news seen se live reporting kii hai. enditivi 24*7 ek angreji bhashaa televijan network he jo bharat main samachar or vartmaan maamalon ko lekar aata he, jo kii nai dilli televijan limited ke swaamitv main he. 
 nidhi raazdaan maharaj krishna raazdaan kii beti hai, jon ki samachar agency ke mukhya sampadak hai. vah badgaam, kashmir se he. unhone apije skool, shaikh sarai, nai dilli se padhai kii. unhone ladi shriram college for vimen se snaatak kii oopaadhi praapt kii or baad main bhartiya sanchaar santhaan, dilli main radio or tivi patrakarita main snatakottar diploma kiya. 2005 main nidhi raazdaan ne neelesh mishra se shaadi kii. 2007 main dow saal baad unka talaak ho gaya.
