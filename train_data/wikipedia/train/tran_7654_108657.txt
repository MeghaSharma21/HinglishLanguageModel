 saanchaa:Rivers of Kerala
 thodupuzayar nadi kerala ke muwattupuzayar nadi ke ek shakha he or kareeb 50 kii me dur he. yah mulamattaman ke aasapaas ke jaise ilappalli jharna, vagaman, kudaiattur, aadi pahadiyon se udbhav hota he or mulamattaman power house se nikalati pani triveni sangman main totupuryaar nadi main milti he, jis kaaran se saal bhar is nadi pani se bharaa hua rahata he. is nadi totupuzathodupuza shahar ke bich main se bahati he or muwattupuzayar nadi main ja milti he.
 khaas baat yah he ki, is nadi ke naam se hi totupura shahar kaa naam bhi ban gaya. malayalam jubaan main 'totu' matlab jharnaa or 'puza' kaa matlab nadi hota he yani ek jharnaa nadi ke rup main badal gaya or ek matlab aisa hota he, totu matlab chhukar puza matlab nadi yani nadi ko chhukar rahanevaale ek shahar he totupura
