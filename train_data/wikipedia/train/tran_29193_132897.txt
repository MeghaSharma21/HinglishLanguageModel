 vikalpi avayujeev aisa jeev hota he jo oxigen kii upasthiti main adinosin triphosphate banane ke liye uskaa viviya shwasan dwaara prayog kar letaa he lekin oxigen na hone par bhi avayaviya shwasan yaa kinvan dwaara jeene va panpane main sakasham he. iske vipareet avikalpi vayujeev bina oxigen ke mar jate hai or avikalpi avayujeev oxigen kii upasthiti main hanigrast hote hai yaa mar jate hai. stallilococas naamak bacteria vikalpi avayujeevon kaa udhaaharan hai.
