 muraad pratham 1362 se 1389 tak osman samrajya ke shaasha the. ve aurakhaan pratham or valida nilofar khatun ke bete the.
 unhone baazantini shahar aadrianopal par fatah hasil kar iskaa naam adarna rakhaa. muraad pratham ne is shahar ko rajdhani ghoshit kar europe main osman samrajya kaa vistaar kiya. unhone balcon prayadweep ke adhikansh kshetra par qabza kiya or baazantini samrajya ko shradhanjali dene ke liye majaboor kiya. muraad pratham pehle shaasha the jinhone osmani riyasat ko pehali baar osman samrajya main parivartit kiya. unhone 1383 main sultan kaa kitaab hasil kiya or samrajya ke sarkaar ke liye dewan pranali ko sthapit kiya. unhone samrajya ko do rajya, anatolia or rumelia main vibhajit kiya.
