 munshi premchand kii rachana-drushti vibhinn sahitya roopon main pravrutt hui. bahumukhi pratibha sampann premchand ne upanyaas, kahani, naatak, samiksha, lekh, sampadakeey, sansmaran aadi anek vidhaon main sahitya kii sristi kii. pramukhatya unki khyaati kathakaar ke taur par hui or apane jivan kaal main hi ve ‘upanyaas samraat’ kii oopaadhi se sammanit hue. 
 unhone kul 15 upanyaas, 300 se kuch adhik kahaniyaan, 3 naatak, 10 anuwaad, 7 bal-pustaken tathaa hajaaron prishthon ke lekh, sampadakeey, bhaashan, bhoomika, patr aadi kii rachana kii.
 premchand dwaara likhe gaye upanyaason kii suchi. 
 munshi ji ne teen naatak bhi likhe kintu naatak ke kshetra main premchand ko koi khaas safalataa nahin mili. ye naatak vastutah sanvadatmak upanyaas hi ban gaye hai.
