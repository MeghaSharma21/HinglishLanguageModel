 laalan, laalan sai, laalan shah, laalan faqir yaa mahatma laalan bengali baul sant, faqir, geetkaar, samaaj sudhaarak tathaa chintak the.
 bengali samskruti main vah dharmik sahansheeltaa ke gaazi ban gaye jinke geeton ne rabindranath tagore, kaazi nazarul islam, or alen ginsburg samet anek kavi, samaaj sudhaarak or chintakon ko prerana di or prabhavit kiya - kyonki vah "jaati dharm ke sabhi bhinnataaon ko radd karte the" - zindagi dauraan or marane uparant bhi vah chokhi charchaa kaa vishya hai. unke pairokaar zyaadaatar bangladesh or pashchim bengal ke nivasee hai. unhone kushtiya railway stesan se taqariban 2 ki0me dur cheuria main 'laalan akharah' naam kii sanstha sthapit kii. unako baul sangeet ke sansthaapak ko bhi mana jaataa he.
