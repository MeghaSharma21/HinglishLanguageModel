 someshwar pratham prasiddh |chalukyaraj jaisingh dwitiya jagadekamall kaa putr jo 1042 i. main singhaasana par baitha. 
 pita kaa samrudh rajya praapt kar usne digvijay karne kaa nishchay kiya. chol or parmaar donon uske shatru the. pehle vah parmaron kii ore badha. raja bhoj dhara or mandu chhod ujjain bhaaga or someshwar donon nagaron ko luutata ujjain ja chadha. ujjain kii bhi vahi gati hui, yadhyapi bhoj sena taiyaar kar phir lautaa or usne khoe hue praant lautaa liye. kuch dinon baad jab ahnilwad ke bhim or kalchuri lakshmikarn se sangharsh ke bich bhoj mar gaya tab uske uttaradhikari jaisingh ne someshwar se sahaayta maangi. someshwar ne use malwa kii gaddi par baitha diya or swayan cholon se ja bhida. 1052 i. main krishna or panchganga ke sangam par koppam ke prasiddh yuddh main cholon ko parrast kiya. bilhan ke 'vikramakadevacharit' ke anusaar to someshwar ek baar chol shakti ke centre ranchi tak ja pahunchaa. someshwar ne dakshin or nikat ke rajkulon se safal loha lekar ab apana ruukh uttar kii ore kiya. madhyabhaarat main chandelon or kushwahon ko raudata vah ganga jamuna ke dwab kii ore badha or kannaujraj ne darkar kandaraon main sharan lee. uski shakti is prakaar badhati dekh lakshmikarn kalchuri ne uski raah roki, par use harkar maidan chhodana padaa. isi bich someshwar ke bete vikramaditya ne mithila, magadh, ang, bang or goud ko rond daalaa. tab kahin kamrup pahunchne par vahaan ke raja ratnapal ne chalukyon kii baag roki or someshwar koshal kii raah ghar lautaa. hyderabad main kalyani naam kaa nagar usee kaa basaaya hua pracheen kalyaan he jise usne apni rajdhani banaaya tha. 1068 i. main biimaar padane par jab someshwar ne apane bachane kii aashaa na dekhi tab vah tungabhadra main swechha se duubakar mar gaya.
