 rashtriya takneekee santhaan, raipur, chhattisgarh ko dinaank 1 disambar 2005 se kendriya sarkaar ke purnat: vittposhit santhaan ke rup main adhigrahit kiya gaya or rashtriya praudyogiki santhaan kaa darja pradaan kiya gaya. vartmaan main santhaan avar snaatak str par 12 pathyakram or 6 snatakottar pathyakram sanchalit kar rahaa he. santhaan main duurastha shiksha pradaan karne kii suvidha he. santhaan main 82 prayogshalas hai jo bahut badi hai. college main ek balika chatravaas sahit 6 chatravaas hai. is santhaan main ek susajjit pustkaalay hai.
