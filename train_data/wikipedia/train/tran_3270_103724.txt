 akshar bharti ek swayamsevi sanstha he. isaki sthapana april 2007 may 5-15 varsh ke aayu ke vanchit varg ke bachein tak shiksha pahunchane ke mishan se is sanstha kaa sthapana kiya gaya. iske liye dur-daraaj ke gaanvo me pustkaalay kii sthapana kii he. 2020 tak shashakt bharat ke nirmaan ke lakshya ko paane me yah sanstha apana rachanaatmak sahyog de rahi he. puskalay kaa sthapana is vichaar se kiya gaya ki jab use padhne kii zarurat padhe to wo pard saken.
