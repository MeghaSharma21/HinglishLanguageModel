 aarti saaha bharat tathaa asia kii pehali mahila english channel paar karne vaali prasiddh tairaak thim.
 
 kalakatta,pashchim bengal} kii mul nivasee aarti ne chaar saal kii umr se hi tairaaki shuru kar di thi. unka puraa naam aarti saaha 'gupta' he. sachin naag ne unki is pratibha ko pahachaanaa or use taraashne kaa kaary shuru kiya. 1949 main aarti ne akhil bhartiya ricard sahit rajyastareey tairaaki pratiyogitaaon ko jeeta. unhone 1952 main helsinki olympic main bhi bhaag liya.
 bhartiya purush tairaak mihir sen se prerit hokar unhone english channel paar karne kii koshish kii or 29 sitambar 1959 ko ve asia se aisa karne vaali pratham mahila tairaak ban gayi. unhone 42 mile kii yah duuri 16 ghante 20 minute main taiy kii.
 unhen 1960 main padmashri se sammanit kiya gaya. 
 pilia ke kaaran 23 agast 1994 ko unki mrityu ho gai.
