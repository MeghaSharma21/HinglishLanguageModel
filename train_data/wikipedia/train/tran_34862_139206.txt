 agni sakshi 1996 main bani hindi bhashaa kii naatak film he. iskaa nirdesan partho ghosh ne kiya or mukhya bhoomika main jaiki shroff, naanaa patekar or manisha koirala hai. is film ke aas paas hi yaarana or daraar jari hui thi jo kramshah maadhuri dikshit or juhi chawla dwaara abhinit thim. ye teenon julia rabarts abhinit sleeping vid da anemi par aadhaarit thim or agni sakshi saphaltam sabit hui thi. naanaa patekar ne is film ke liye 1997 main sarvshereshth sahaayak abhinayta ke liye rashtriya film puraskaar praapt kiya tha.
 suraj kapur ek akelaa or amir yuva vyakti he. ek din vah shivangi ke miltaa he or donon ek duusare ke saath pyaar main padate hai. iske turant baad donon shaadishuda vivaahit jivan kaa anand lene ke liye vivaah kar lete hai or bas jate hai. ek raat main bhojan karte samay, jode ko ek purush vishwanath sampark karata he, jo daawa karata he ki shivangi uski patni madhu he. suraj or shivangi chaunk gaye or unhen chhodane ke liye kaha, jo vah karata he. lekin agle din yah purush, police or adaalaton kii sahaayta se sabit karata he ki shivangi vastav main uski patni madhu, he. suraj ne vishwaas karne se inkaar kar diya or shahar ko apni patni ke saath chhodane kaa faisla kiya. lekin yah joda kai anya avasaron par vishwanath se miltaa he jahaan uskaa ravaiya aakraamak ban gaya. vah maanati he ki vah vastav main madhu he. madhu vishwanath ke nirantar durvyavhaar or manamaani se tang aa gai thi. madhu ke pita ne use bataaya ki vah abhi bhi jawan he or uske aage puraa jivan he. use ek nai jagah main ek nai pahchaan ke saath apane jivan ko phir se shuru karnaa chahie. aisa vah karti he. uski kahani sunne ke baad suraj ne use bataaya ki vah abhi bhi usase pyaar karata he. vah uske jivan ke baaki hisson ke liye vishwanath ko jail bhejne kii yojana banaata he.
