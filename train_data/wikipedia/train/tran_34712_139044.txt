 gokharu yaa 'gokshur' bhoomi par failane vaala chota prasaranshil kshup he jo ki asadha or shravan maas me praaia har prakaar kii jameen yaa khaali jameen par ug jaataa he. patr khandit or phool pile rang ke aate hai, fal kantak yukt hote hai, bazaar me gokharu ke naam se iske beej milte hai. uttar bharat me, haryana, rajasthan me yah bahut miltaa he.
 isamen chane ke aakaar ke kade or cantile fal lagate hai. ye fal oshidhi ke kaam main aate hai or vaidyak main inhen sheethal, madhur, pusht, rasayan, deepan or kaash, vaayu, arsh or brananashak kaha he. yah fal bada or chota do prakaar kaa hota he. kahin kahin garib log iske bijon kaa aataa banaakar khate hai.
 yah shitaviry, mutravirechak, bastishodhak, agnideepak, vrishya, tathaa pushtikaarak hota he. vibhinn vikaro me vaidyavarg dwaara isko prayog kiya jaataa he. mutrakruchh, sojaak, ashmari, bastishoth, vrikkavikaar, prameh, napunsakata, ovarian rog, veery kshinta me iskaa prayog kiya jaataa he.
 gokharu xigophilii kul ke antargat tribulas terrestris naamak ek prasar vanaspati he, jo bharat main balui yaa patharilii jameen main praaia: sarvatra pai jaati he. ise chota gokharu yaa gudkhul or gokshur bhi kehete hai. iske sanyukt patr main 5-7 jode chane ke samaan patrak, patrakonon main ekaaki pile pushp or kantedaar gaal fal hote hai.
 ayurveda ke 'dashamul' naamak das vanaushadhiyon ke prasiddh gana main ek yah bhi he or iske mul kaa athwa fal kaa chikitsa main upyog hota he. yah madhur, snehak, mutravirechaneey, bajikar tathaa thothahar hone ke kaaran mutrakruchir, ashmari, prameh, napunskatv, sujak, vastishoth tathaa vaatarogon main laabhadaayak mana jaataa he.
 tilkul kii pedalium murex naamak ek duusari vanaspati he, jo 'bada gokharu' ke naam se prasiddh he. iske falon kaa bhi praaia: chote gokharu kii tarah hi prayog hota he. iske fal chaar kaanton se yukt or aakaar main pyramida shanku jaise hote hai. yah dakshin bharat, visheshat: samudratat, gujarat, kathiyawad tathaa konkan aadi main hota he.
