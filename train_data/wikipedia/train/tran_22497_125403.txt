 umeo dizine sanstha umeo vishvavidhyalay main ek sanstha he. yah 1989 main khuli thi.umeo dizine sanstha umeo vishvavidhyalay ke mukhya campus or umeo shahir ke centre ke bich main sthit he.
 umeo dizine sanstha aisa iklota scandinavian skool he jisko bijansavil magazine ne duniya ke 60 sabh se achche dizine skool main se ek rakhaa he, or isko duniya ko 18 sabh se achche skulon main se ek bhi kaha gaya he. 2011 main isko red daat sanstha dwaara "europa or america" kshetra kaa doosraa sabh se acha dizine skool mana gaya or 2012 main isane pratham sthaan praapt kiya.
