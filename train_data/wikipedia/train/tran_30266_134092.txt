 televijan kaarykram  kisi vigyaapan, trailor yaa darshakon ke liye aakarshan ke hetu na hone vaali saamagree ke kisi anya khand ke alaava, over-da-air, cabel televijan yaa internet televijan par prasaarit karne ke liye sambandhit prastutiyon kii ek shrunkhlaa hai. shaayad hi kabhi yah ekal prastuti ho sakati he, jise tivi program kaha jaataa he.
 kisi televijan kaarykram ke simit sankhyaon ke prakarnon ko ek miniceris yaa serial yaa simit shrunkhlaa kaha ja saktaa he.
 kisi ek-baar prasaaran ko "vishesh" yaa khaaskar euke main ek "vishesh prakaran" kaha ja saktaa hai.
