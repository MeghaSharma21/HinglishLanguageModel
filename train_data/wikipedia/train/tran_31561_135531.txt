 jharkhand ke kuch sthanon main jeevashm ke kuch ansh un kalakritiyon kii ore ishaara karte hai jisase yah pataa chalata he ki chotanagpur kshetra main homo irekatas se homo sipiens jati main badlaav ko darshata he. yahan pathar or anya upkaran, sabhyataaon ke praarambhik varshon se 3000 se adhik varsh pehle ke hai. 6 yaa 7 veen shatabdi i.pus. ke -- mahakavy mahabharat yug ke " kikat " pradesh kaa ullekh rugveda main he jo parasnath kii pahadiyon main giridih jile main, jharkhand main he.
 yahan kaa samrudh, sabhy astitv, maanav samaaj or unke saanskrithik tareeke, gufaaon main jivit rahane ke tareeke, smarak, chattani kala main aashrayon ke rahasy jaananaa to abhi tak shesh he.
 kya diwaar kii gupha par dianasor he ? yaa phir ek vishaal hathi kaa peechha shuruaati daur main purushon dwaara kiya ja rahaa he ? kya ek pragaitihaasik vriksh lakhon saal se pathar main sthir he ? haan, jharkhand ke kuch bhaagon main gupha chitra, 'pathar kii kala' or 'shailavarnana' or bhuvaigyanik samay bitne kaa bhi sanket he. pracheen sabhyataa main hadappa kii maujuudagi kaa bhi pramaan he.
 jharkhand ke kai jilon main is tarah ke sait or avshesh hai.
