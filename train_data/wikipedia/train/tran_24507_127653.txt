 ve din biit gayi hai jab mahilaayein keval ghar kii rrasoi main payi jaati thim. aajkal mahilaayein keval purushon se kandhe se kandhaa milakar hi nahin chhalti hai balkee kaii kshetron main unase acha pradarshan bhi kararahi hai. chaahe vyapaar ho, sena yaa fishan dizining ho, prayek or lagbhag har kshetra main pratibhashali mahilaon ko ham ab paa sakate hai.
 ve ek bhartiya-amriki diziner or udyami hai, jinka janm 27 farvari, 1965 main, naii dilli main hua
