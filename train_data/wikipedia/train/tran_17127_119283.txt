 magha yaa ragulus​, jiskaa bair naam "alfaa leyonis" he, singh taramandal kaa sab se roshan tara he. yah prithvi se dikhne vaale taron main se baisva sab se roshan tara he. magha hamaare saura mandal se lagbhag 77.5 prakaash-varsh dur he. vastav main magha ek tara nahin balki do dwitaron kaa mandal he, yani kul milakar chaar taare hai.
 magha taare ko angrezi main "ragulus​" kehete hai jo mul rup se latini bhashaa kaa shabd he or jiskaa arth he "chota raja" yaa "rajkumaar". arabi bhashaa main ise "qalb al-asad" kehete hai, jiskaa arth he "singh kaa dil".
 magha do dwitaron kaa mandal he. pehle dwitare ko magha "e" kaha jaataa he. iskaa mukhya tara B7V shrenee kaa ek nila-safed mukhya anukram tara he. iske ird-gird parikrama karata saathi tara thik se kabhi dekha nahin gaya lekin uske bare main anumaan he ke yah suraj ke dravyaman ke 0.3 guna dravyaman vaala safed bouna tara he. donon taare apane saanjhe dravyaman centre kii har 40 dinon main parikrama puri kar lete hai. mukhya tara itani tezi se ghurnan kar rahaa he ke uskaa gol akar bahut pichak chukaa he. agar iske ghoomane kii gati vartmaan gati se sirf 16% zyaada hoti to is taare ke tut kar hisse ban jate.
 pehle dwitare se 4,200 khagoleey ikaiyon kii duuri par doosraa dwitara he. iskaa ek tara magha "bi' kehalaata he or K2V shrenee kaa tara he, jabaki doosraa magha "si" kehalaata he or M4V shrenee kaa tara he. donon apane saanjhe dravyaman centre kii ek parikrama puri karne main 2,000 saal lagate hai.
