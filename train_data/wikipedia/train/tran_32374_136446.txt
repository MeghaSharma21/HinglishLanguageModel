 smita patil hindi filmon kii ek abhinetri hai.bhartiya sandarbh main smita patil ek sakriy nariwadi hone ke atirikt mumbai ke mahila centre kii sadsy bhi thim. ve mahilaon ke muddon par puri tarah se vachanabaddh thim or iske saath hi unhone un filmo me kaam karne ko praathamikta di jo paramparaagat bhartiya samaaj me shahari madhyavarg kii mahilaon kii pragati unki kaamuktha tathaa saamaajik parivartan kaa saamana kar rahi mahilaon ke sapanom kii abhivyakti kar saken.
 pune main janmi smita ke pita shivajirao patil maharashtra sarkaar me mantri or mata ek saamaajik kaaryakarta thim. unki aarambhik shiksha marathi madhyam ke ek skool se hui thi . unka camere se pehla saamana tivi samachar vaachak ke rup hua tha. hameshaa se thodi vidrohi rahi smita kii bady ankhon or saanwale saundarya ne pehali nazar me hi sabhi kaa dhyaan apni ore khinch liya .
 kalantar me smita patil ke prem sambandh raaj babbar se ho gayi jinaki pariniti antantah vivaah me hui. raaj babbar jo pehle se hi vivaahit the or unhone smita se shaadi karne ke liye apni pehali patni ko chhoda tha.
