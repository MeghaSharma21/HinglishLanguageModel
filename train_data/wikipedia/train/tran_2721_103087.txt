 pakistan ke mukhya nirvachan ayukta, pakistan kii samvidhan dwaara sthapit kiya gaya ek sanvaidhanik pad he, ve pakistan ke nirvachan ayog ke adhyaksh va niyukt padaadhikari hote hai. nirvachan ayog pakistan kii vah sanvaidhanik santhaan he jise pakistan main rashtriya evam prantiy str par swatantra or nishpaksh chunaav yojit karne kaa prabhaar he.
 san 1973 ke purv is pad par keval prashasnic sevaaon ke adhikariyon ko hi niyukt kiya jaataa tha or yah niyukti keval pakistan ke raashtrapati dwaara kii jaati thi, parantu san 1973 ke samvidhan main, jisamen purv samvidhanon ke mukaable, anek mahatvapurn parivartan kiye gaye the, ke parvartan ke baad is pad par niyukti ko keval nyaaypalika par sankuchit kar diya gaya. 1973 kaa samvidhan is baat ko anivaary karata he kii mukhya nirvachan ayukta keval nyaayik shakha se hi niyukt kiya jayega. atah maujuuda sanvaidhanik praavdhaanon ke anusaar keval varisht nyaayadhish hi is pad par niyukt hone ke liye yogya hai. mukhya nirvachan ayukta kii niyukti va kaarykaal shapath, samvidhan yaa raashtrapati dwaara kiya jaataa he.
 pakistan ke nirvachan ayog kii sthapana pakistan ke samvidhan dwaara kiya gaya tha. ise 1956 main sthapit kiya gaya tha.nirvachan ayog, mukhya nirvachan ayukta samet chaaron praanton se niyukti kiye gaye sadasyon se gathit hota he. mukhya nirvachan ayukta kaa kaarykaal 3 varsh hota he, jis bich unhen kaarykaal va vittiya suraksha pradaan kii jaati he. mukhya nirvachan ayukta va anya ayuktaon ko pakistan ke raashtrapati dwaara niyukti kiya jaataa he.
