 pratyaksh varnakram yaa drushya varnakram vidyut chumbakeey varnakram kaa ek bhaag he, jo maanviya chakshuon ko dikhaai detaa he. is shrenee kii vidyut chumbakeey tarangon ko prakaash kehete hai. ek adarsh maanvi chakshu vaayu main dekhati he 380 nanometer se 750 nanometer tarangadarghy kii prakaash ko dekh sakati he.. iske anusaar jal main or anya madhyamon main yah us madhyam ke apvartan gunank ke gunak main drishyata ghat jaati he. aavriti ke anusaar, yah 400-790 teraa hertz ke baraabar kii pattee main panta he. aankh dwaara dekhe gaye prakaash kii adhiktam samvedansheeltaa 555 nm hoti he . vaise varnakram main ve sabhi rang nahin hote jo ki maanvi aankh yaa mastishk dekh yaa pahchaan saktaa he jaise bhura, gulabi yaa rani anupasthit hai. yah isliye kyonki ye mishrit tarang dairghy se banate hai, khaskar laal ke chaaya. 
 
 indradhanush ke parichit varna jo ki pratyaksh varnakram main aate hai, main ve sabhi varna sammilit hai, jo ki pratyaksh prakaash kii ekal aavriti dwaara dikhaai dete hai, yani ki shuddh varnakramik yaa ek rangeey / monochromatic varna. 
 varna pradarshak laal, haraa, tathanila varna milaate hai, varnakram ke barabari main. is drushtaant main patali laal, hari va neeli pattiyaan upar prayog kiye rang ko nirmit karne main lage in rangon kaa mishran.
