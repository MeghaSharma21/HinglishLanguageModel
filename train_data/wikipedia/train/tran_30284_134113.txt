  celucus sikandar ke sabase yogya senapatiyon main se ek tha jo uski mrityu ke baad bharat ke vijit kshetron par uskaa uttaradhikari bana. vah sikandar dwaara jeeta hua bhu-bhaag praapt karne ke liye utsuk tha. is uddeshy se 305 i. pus. usne bharat par punah chadhaai kii. chandragupta ne pashchimottar bharat ke unani shaasha celukas nicketer ko parajeet kar aria, arakosia, jedrosia peropanisadai ke bhu-bhaag ko adhikrut kar vishaal maury samrajya kii sthapana kii. celukas ne apni putree helen kaa vivaah chandragupta se kar diya. usne megasthnij ko rajdoot ke rup main chandragupta maury ke darbar main niyukt kiya.
