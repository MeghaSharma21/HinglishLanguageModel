 langot purushon dwaara pahana jane vaala ek ant:vastra he. yah purush jananaang ko dhakkar evam dabaakar rakhane main sahaayta karata he. bharat main iskaa upyog pehle bahut hota tha. aajkal bhi brahmacharee, sadhu-sant, akhaada ladane vaale pahalwaan aadi iskaa upyog karte hai. 
 langot kisi bhi rang kaa ho saktaa he kintu laal langot vishesh rup se lokapriy he. yah puri tarah se khulaa hua trikonakar kapade kaa bana hota he.
