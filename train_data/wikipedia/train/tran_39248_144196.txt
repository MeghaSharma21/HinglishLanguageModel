 sarkashia ek pradesh tathaa aitihaasik desh he. yah uttari kakeshas kshetra main krishna sagar ke uttari-purvi kinare par sthit he. yah kakesian logon kaa pracheen desh he.
 madhya yug main sarkashiyavasi kakeshas parvaton main rahete the. 10veen se 13veen shatabdi tak sarkashia georgia ke shashan ke antargat rahaa, phir kai shatabdiyon tak sarkashia swatantra rahaa. san 1829 i. main sarkashia par rus kaa purna shashan kaayam hua. falswaroop lagbhag paanch lakh sarkashiyavasi turki or balgeria chale gaye or ab keval 93,000 sarkashiyavasi rah gaye hai. is kshetra main nivas karanevaali hi anya jatiyon main roosi or cokeshian jatiyaan hai.
 ucch varg ke sarkashiyavasi musalmaan hai. sarkashiyavasi striyaan sundarataa ke liye prasiddh hai or ek samay baadshahon ke haram ke liye inaki badi chaah thi.
