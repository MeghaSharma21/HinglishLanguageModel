 aak battak ke samaan, chota, samudriya, tittibh varg kaa pakshi he. iskaa sharir gatha hua, pankh chote or sankare, 12 se 18 paron kii choti naap tathaa sharir ke pichley bhaag main aapas main jhilli se jude, kul teen angulionwale, pair hote hai. pairon kii sthiti sharir ke pichley bhaag main hone ke kaaran aak bhoomi par sidhe hokar chalata he. saadhaarant: iske sharir ke upree bhaag kaa rang kaala or nichle kaa shwet hota he. yah andh mahaasaagar tathaa prashanth mahasaagaron ke uttari bhaagon or dhruv mahasaagaron main paaya jaataa he.
 aak anek jatiyon ke hote hai. inkaa nivas andh tathaa prashanth mahasaagaron ke uttari bhaagon or dhruv mahasaagaron me simit he. varsh ke adhik bhaag ko ye tat ke pasavaale samudr main bitaate hai. keval sheet ritu main ye dakshin kii ore chale jate hai. inkaa bhojan mukhyat: machhlee tathaa kathini varg ke jeev, jaise kekade zinga, mahachingt ityadi hote hai. inhen ye jal main ghota markar pakadte hai. tapuon or samudratati pahadiyon main ye santanotpati ke liye bas jate hai. inaki praaia: sab jatiyaan ghonslaa nahin banaatin tathaa ek jati ko chhodkar baaki sab jatiyon ke aak varsh main keval ek anda dete hai. andey se baahar nikalne par bachche kaale roendaar paron se dhake rahete hai. samudr main to aak maun rahete hai, par santanotpati ke liye based upniveshon main ye vicitra prakaar ke swar nikaalte hai.
 bhimkaay aak 30 inch lanbaa hota he. paron ke liye andhadhundh shikaar kiye jane ke kaaran isaki jati 19veen sadi main lupt ho gai.
