 pakatiya afghanistan kaa ek praant he jo us desh ke purv main sthit he. is praant kaa kshetrafal 6,432 varg kimi he or isaki aabaadi san 2002 main lagbhag 4.15 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. is praant kii rajdhani gardez shahar he. is praant kii sarahadein pakistan se lagati hai. yahan ke lagbhag 90% log pashtun hai or lagbhag 10% farsi bolne vaale tajik log hai.
 'pakatiya' ko pehle 'pashatiya' or kuch vidwaanon kaa maananaa he kii pashto shabd isi se utpann hua he. kisi jamaane main pakatiya, khost or pakatika praant ek hi soobe ke bhaag hua karte the or in teenon ko ab 'loya pakatiya' bulaayaa jaataa he.
