 narak, tiryanch, maanush or dev lok main bante sansar ke vyavasthaapak aath karm hai. anant gyaan, darshan, sukh or veery ke punjeebhoot is aatma kii shraddha, gyaan, aadi kaa jis prakaar gyanavarani, darshanavarani karm virup karte hai, usee prakaar chatha karm vividh shariron kaa nirmaan karaata he or aatma ke anek naam rakhata he. namakarm ke pratham bhed kaa naam gati he. yat: gati namakarm jeevon ko anek rangabhumiyon par chalaata he atev ise gati kehete hai . 
 gati namakarm ke mukhya bhed narak, tiryanch, manushya or dev ye chaar hai. 
 1. jo sharir, nivas, kaal or aatmrup se na swayan prasann rahen or na dusron ko prasann rahane den unhen naaraki kehete hai. inke lok ko narak kehete he. 
 2- jinke man -vachan-kaary ruju na hon, jo aaharadi sangyaao ke adheen hon, agyani hon atev paaplin hon unhen tiryach kehete hai. 
 3- jo man se bhala bura sochen, kushal viveki hon, sabase adhik man kaa upyog karte hon tathaa manushya kii parampara main hon unhen manushya kehete hai. 
 4. jo anima, laghima, aadi aath riddhiyon ke kaaran anand se vihaar karte hon, bhale bhaavon main magn rahete hon tathaa jinke sharir sadaiv satej, kantimay or veeryavan rahete hon unhen dev kehete hai.
