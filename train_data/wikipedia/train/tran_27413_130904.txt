 teble nambar 21 aditya dutt dwaara nirdeshit evam iroj international dwaara nirmit 2013 kii bollywood hindi ection rahasyamay film he. is film kaa namakaran bhartiya samvidhan ke anuchhed 21 ke liye kiya gaya jisamen jivan suraksha evam swatantraaa kaa ullekh he. film main mukhya bhoomika main paresh rawal, rajeev khandelwal or tina desai hai.
 vivaan or siya agasti jhagadon ko samaapt karne ke liye ek duusare se milte hai. yah jodi ek lucky drow main fiji ganraajya ke sundar dweep kii yatra jitate hai; jisamem chhuttiyaan purnatah sukhad hotel main rahanaa or achche ratrikaleen bhojan ke saath pryojit hai.
 jari hote hi teble nambar 21 ne tikat khidki par dhimi shuruyaat di. saptaahant tak isane ₹1.5 karod kii kamai kii, jabaki chaar dn kii kul kamai lagbhag ₹6.75 karod rahi. film kii pratham saptaah kii kul kamai ₹9.5 karod rahi or do saptaah kii kul kamai ₹11.5 karod rahi.
