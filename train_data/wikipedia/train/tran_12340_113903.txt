 dilip singh judev chhattisgarh ke raajnneetijna evam atal bihari vaajapeyi ke netritv vaali bhartiya janata party abhinit rajg sarkaar main ve van evam paryaavaran rajyamantri the. ve jashpur ke rajpariwaar se sambandhit the tathaa madhya bharat ke janajaatiy logon main atyant lokapriy the . ve bilaspur se loksabhaa saansad the.
 judev kii prasiddhi unke dwaara kiye gayi rashtravaadi kaaryon ke liye hai. unhone 'gharvaapasi' naamak abhiyaan chalaaya jisko baari samarthan mila. is abhiyaan ke antargat hajaaron logon ko punah hindu dharm main dikshit kiya gaya jo isai mishnariyon ke prabhaav main aakar pehle isai ban gayi the.
 judev ke upar varsh 2003 main chhattisgarh main khanan adhikaron ke badale ghoose lene kaa aarope laga. yah aarope sanday newspaper dwaara lagaaya gaya tha jinhen ghoose prakaran kaa ek video praapt hua tha. haalanki unhone in aaropon kaa khandan kiya tathaa, isai mishnariyon or congress party khaaskar ajit jogi par nishaanaa sadhate hue, ise swayan ko bhrashtachar main phasaane vaala ek shadyantra qaraar diya. judev ne 17 navambar 2003 ko apane mantri pad se tyaagptr de diya.
 14 agast 2013 ko vrikk or yakrut main sankraman kii vajah se unka nidhan ho gaya.
