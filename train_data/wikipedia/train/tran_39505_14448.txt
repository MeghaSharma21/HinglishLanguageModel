 rajbhar
 bhar bharat kaa ek jati samudaay he, jise navik yaa mallahon kii ek upjati ke rup main bhi sanderbhit kiya jaataa he. inhe rajbhar bhi kehete hai.
 
 madhyakaalin bharat main inke swayan ke chote chote kabeeley sthapit the. purvi uttar pradesh main inke chote chote rajya bhi the jahaan se uttar-madhya kaal main inhe rajputon va mughalon ne khaded kar visthapit kar diya tha. antim bhar raja ko jonpur ke sultan ibrahim shah sharki ne maaraa tha.
 bhar mukhya rup se chote kisaanon kaa ek samudaay he jo apni aamadani main badhottaree hetu mazdooree bhi karte hai. samanytah bhar jati ke logon kaa bhoomi swaamitv kam he. yah hindu dharm ko maanate hai tathaa inkaa saanskrithik aacharan avadh kshetra ke anya hindu samudaayon jaisaa hi he. yah log avadhi va bhojpuri bhashaa bolte hai. bhar samudaay azamgarh, mau, jonpur, gajipur, gonda, gorakhpur, varanasi, ambedkar nagar va faizabad jilon main paaya jaataa he.
 bhar jati bharat kii aarakshan vyavastha ke tahat ati pichhadaa varg ke rup main vargeekrut he. 2013 main, uttar pradesh main bhar va anya 16 ati pichde samudaayon ko "anya pichhadaa varg" suchi ne nikaal kar "anusuchit jati" varg main shaamil karne kaa prastaav diya gaya, jo kii 2014 ke chunaavon ke liye mahatvapurn mudda bhi ho saktaa tha.
 bhar un 17 jatiyon main se hai jinhe uttar pradesh kii samaajawaadee sarkaar ne "anusuchit jati" main sammilit kiye jane kaa prastaav diya tha jise vot bank raajnneeti maanate huye adalati roak ke baad bharat sarkaar ne sthagit kar diya he.
