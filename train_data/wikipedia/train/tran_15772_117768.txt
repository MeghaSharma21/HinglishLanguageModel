 kisi bhi vyapaaree yaa sanstha ke liye yah sambhav nahin he ki vah saare len-dein vyavhaaron ko yaad rakh sake. isaliye unhen yaad rakhane ke liye dainiki taiyaar kiya jaataa he. isamen lenden hote hi pravishti kar lee jaati he. atah prayek saudon yaa vyavhaar ko kramvaar likhane ki kriya ko praarambhik lekha kaha jaataa he. ise 'rojnaamachaa' bhi kehete hai. isamen suvidha ke liye kuch sahaayak pustake bhi rakhi jaati he. jaise kray pustak vikray pustak, rokad pustak tathaa mukhya jarnal aadi.
 udhaaharan ke liye - nagar panchayat main dinaank 15.4.2008 ko karyaalay ke liy 1000.00 rupaye nagad dekar furniture kharida. yah ek sauda he jiskaa jarnal book main praarambhik lekha nimnanusaar hoga -
 chunki jarnal lekhe taiyaar karne kii praarambhik pustak he, isaliye ise mahatvapurn sthaan praapt he. aajkal sidhe lager evam sahaayak bahiyaan taiyaar karnaa prachlan main hone se abhilekh kaa mahatv vyapaarik pratishthanon main ab dhire-dhire kam ho rahaa he. sthaneey nikaayon main yadi dwi pravishti lekha apanaai jaati he to usamen jarnal kaa bahut mahatv rahegaa kyonki ve sabhi vyavhaar jisamen mudra kaa vaastavik lenden nahin hua he. samaayojan pravishtiyaan aadi jarnal ke madhyam se hi lekhaabaddh kii jaayegi.
