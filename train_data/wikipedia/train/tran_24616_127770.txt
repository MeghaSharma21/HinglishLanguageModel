 toba tech singh sadat hasan manto dwaara likhi gai or 1955 main prakashit hui ek prasiddh laghu katha he. yah bharat ke vibhaajan ke samay lahore ke ek pagaliane ke paaglon par aadhaarit he or samikshakon ne is katha ko pichley 50 saalon se saraahte hue bharat-pakistan sambandhon par ek "shaktishaalee tanz" bataaya he.
 1947 main vibhaajan ke samay bharat or pakistan kii sarkaron ne ek dusre ke hindu-sikh or muslim paaglon kii adla-badali karne par samjhauta kiya. lahore ke pagaliane main bishan singh naam kaa ek sikh pagal tha jo toba tech singh naamak shahar kaa nivasee tha. use police ke daste ke saath bharat ravaanaa kar diya gaya, lekin jab use pataa chalaa ki toba tech singh bharat main na jaakar bantwaare main pakistan kii taraf pad gaya he to usne jane se inkaar kar diya. kahani ke ant main bishan singh donon deshon ke bich kii sarhad main contili taron ke bich leta, mara yaa marataa hua darshaya gaya. katha kaa aakhri jumala he:
 kahani main jab bhi mukhya paatr bishan singh uttejit yaa gussa hota he to punjabi, hindi-urdu or angrezi ko milajulakar badbadaane yaa chillane lagata he. uski baaten vaise to bematlab hoti hai lekin unamem bharat or pakistan donon ke liye dabi hui si gaaliyaan bhi dikhti hai. udhaaharan ke liye:
 vibhaajan kii 60veen saalagirah par pakistani naatak-mandali ajoka ne is katha par aadhaarit naatak bharat main rachaaya. isapar samaacharon main hui ek tippanee ke anusaar yah 'donon deshon ke bich ke maamalon par ek tabasra he, jisamen cametiyon, upkametiyon or mantreey-str ke vaartalapon ko hi sabhi masalon kaa hal bataaya jaataa he'.
