 tark-vitark chetana dwaara chizon ko samajhane, tathyon ko sthapit va pramanit karne, tark kaa prayog karne or vyavhaaron tathaa vishwaason ke kaaran samajhane va unhen badalane kii prakriya ko kehete hai. kai maanviya kaary va adhyayan - jinamein darshanashastra, vigyaan, bhashaa, ganit or kala shaamil hai - tark-vitark kii shakti par nirbhar hai or yah sakshmata aksar maanvon ko prithvi ke anya praaniyon se bhinn karne vaala ek mahatvapurn gun mana jaataa he.
