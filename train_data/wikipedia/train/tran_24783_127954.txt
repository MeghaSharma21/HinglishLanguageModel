 ghengha ek rog he jisame galaa phool jaataa he. yah sharir main iodine ke kii kami ke kaaran hota he. iodine kii kami ke kaaran thyrid granthi main sujan aa jaati he. yah rog bahudhaa un kshetron ke logon ko hota he jahaan pani main iodine nahin hota. iodine kii kami kii purti ke liye praaia: iodinoyus namak kaa upyog karne kii salaah di jaati he.
