 general satyavant mallannah shrinagesh, jinhen saamanytah es em shrinagesh ke naam se janaa jaataa he, bhartiya thalsena ke tritiy thalsenadhyaksh the jinka is pad par kaarykaal 14 may 1955 se 7 may 1957 tak rahaa. uske baad 1957 se 1962 tak unhen bhartiya prashasnic college, hyderabad, hyderabad state ke mananeey pradhan ke rup main niyukt kiya gaya.
 seva nivritti ke pashchaat unhone 14 oktober 1959 se 12 navambar 1960 tak or punah 13 janvari 1961 se 7 sitambar 1962 assam ke rajyapal kaa padbhaar grahan kiya. 8 sitambar 1962 se 4 may 1964 tak wo andhra pradesh ke rajyapal rahe evam 4 may 1964 se 2 april 1965 tak ve mysore ke rajyapal rahe.
