 vishukkani us jhaanki-darshan ko kehete hai, jiskaa darshan vishu ke din praat:kaal sarvapratham kiya jaataa he. aisa vishwaas he ki vishukkani kaa prabhaav varsh bhar rahata he. vishu kii purv sandhya ko 'kani' darshan kii saamagree ikatthi karke sajaa di jaati he. ek kaanse ke degchi yaa anya kisi bartan main chaaval, nayaa kaphda, kakadi, kaccha aam, paan kaa patta, supaari, katahal, aaina, amaltas ke phool aadi sajaa kar rakh die jate hai. is bartan ke paas ek lambaa deepak jalaakar rakhaa jaataa he. praatah kaal parivaar kaa koi bujurg vyakti ek-ek karke parivaar ke sadasyon kii ankhen moond 'vishukkani' tak le aakar ankhen khulavaate hai. uparyukt 'kani' kaa darshan karaane ke baad ghar ke bujurg parivaar ke sabhi sadasyon ko 'canittam' yaa bhent main kuch rupee dete hai. is avasar par daavat bhi di jaati he. uttari kerala main vishu ke din aatishbaajee kaa aayojan bhi hota he.
