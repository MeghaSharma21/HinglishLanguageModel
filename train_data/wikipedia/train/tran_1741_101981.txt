 cueba yah brazil ke rajya mato groso kii rajdhani he. yah dakshin america mahadweep ke sateek centre main sthit he. nagar brazilian swarna hode ke dauraan, 1719 main sthapit kiya gaya tha, yah 1818 ke baad se rajya kii rajdhani he. nagar maveshi paalan or krishi kshetra ke liye ek vyapaarik centre he. nagar main naav parivahan kaa ek mahatvapurn saadhan bane hue hai.
