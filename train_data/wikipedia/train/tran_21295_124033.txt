 samir raichaudhuri'  bangla sahitya ke ek pramukh kavi, aalochak, kahanelekhak evam darshanik hai. bhukhi peedhee aandolan ke pratham menifesto ghoshnakaareeyon main vah pradhan the. hawa 49 sahityapatr ke vah sampadak rahe hai. unhonen angreji bhashaa main bhi kavita evam kahaniyon ke sankalan sampadn kiye hai. unke likhe khulaja simsim kahani sankalan ko adhunantik kaha gaya he.
