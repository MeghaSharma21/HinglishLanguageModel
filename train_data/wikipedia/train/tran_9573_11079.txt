 firefox oes smartphone tathaa tablet computaron ke liye ek linaks-aadhaarit muktastrot prachaalan tantra he. yah gaiyr laabh sanghathan mozila dwaara viksit kiya ja rahaa he, jise mozila firefox ke nirmaan ke liye behatar janaa jaataa he.
 yah is prakaar dizin kiya gaya he ki achtmal five anuprayog, javascript tathaa mukt web epiai kaa upyog karte hue smartphone or tablett ke hardware se sidhe baat kar sakate hai. is prachaalan tantra kaa pradarshan andrid-campatible smartphonon evam raspberi par kiya ja chukaa he.
