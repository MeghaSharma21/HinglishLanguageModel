 newcastle united footbal club newcastle apon tin, england, main sthit ek english premier lig footbal club hai.
 is club kii sthapana 1892 main do sthaneey klbon, newcastle east and or newcastle west and ke vilay se hui.itihaas ke anusaar in klbon ne 4 pratham shrenee or 6 egge cap khitaab jite hai, jisame 1969 kaa inter-citis fares cap or 2006 kaa yuiefee intertoto cap shaamil he.
 east and or west and klbon ke ekikaran ke baad, newcastle united tab tak east and kii laal vardi main hi kheltaa rahaa jab tak ki 1894 main unhone rang ke kaaran praayah hone vaale takaraav se bachane ke liye kaali safed dhariyon vaali shart ke saath kaale rang kii choti pant or mojey kii chinhatmak vardi ko apana liya.
 newcastle kii apane padosi sanderland se bahut puraani dushmani he jiske saath ve 1898 se tin-vier darbi ke liye sangharsh karte chale aa rahe hai.
 ye club footbal lig championships ke vartmaan vijeta hai.
 is club kii sthapana disambar 1892 main do sthaneey tiimon, newcastle east and or newcastle west and ke vilay se hui thi jo purv main naurdarn lig main ek duusare kii pratispardhi thim lekin west and dwaara aarthik sankaton kaa saamana kiye jane par unhone vilay kaa nirnay le liya. is saude main west and ke stadium saint james 'park kii leez bhi shaamil thi or nae club ke naam ke liye anekon suzav die gaye jceme newcastle rangers or newcastle city bhi shaamil the, haalanki unhone newcastle united naam ko chunaa.
 newcastle united 1900 ke dashak main bhi lig championships jeetta rahaa or yah teen baar 1905, 1907 or 1909 main vijeta bana. cap pratispardhaon main club kii jeet kaa silsilaa chalata rahaa or vah 7 varshon main 5 egge cap ke antim charnon tak pahunchaa, club 1905, 1906, 1908, 1910 or 1911 ke antim charnon main shaamil hua. haalanki club in sabhi final charnon main se ek hi jeet sakaa, yah jeet 1910 ke final main bransale ke vipareet thi jo gudison park main khelaa gaya ek riple tha. haalanki is kaal ke dauraan bhi ek vishesh niraashajanak baat yah thi ki team 1908-09 ke satr main apni apane sabase badi virdohi team sanderland se 9-1 se haar gayi, sanderland ab bhi is nattejey ko apni vijay ke kirtiman ke rup main dekhtaa he.
 team 1924 main egge cap ke final main pahunch gayi, yah tab tak wemble stadium ke itihaas main khelaa gaya doosraa hi final tha. ve eston vila ko haraane main safal rahe the or is prakaar club ke itihaas main unhone duusari egge cap trophy uske naam kar di thi.
 1927 main newcastle chauthi baar lig kaa vijeta bana or yah aaj tak lig main unki jeet kaa aakhri mauka tha. is kaal ke pramukh khilaadiyon main captain hughi galechar, nil haris, sten simor or frank hadspeth the.
 1950 ke dashak main newcastle ne 5 varsh kii avadhi main teen baar egge cap trophy jiti. 1951 main unhone blackpul ko 2-1 se haraaya, iske ek varsh baad hi arsenal ko 1-0 se or 1955 main unhone manchester city ko 3-1 se haraaya. newcastle ko ab ek ucch darza mil chukaa tha or iske khiladiyon ko bhi; vishesh rup se 'vor jaiki' milberne or bobi 'dabler' mishael ko. is team ke anya khilaadiyon main frank briken, ivor brodis, len white or welshmen ivor elchurch the.
 puraane sangharshwan yoddha jo harvey, jinhone yuddh ke baad anekon safal pariyon ke dauraan team kaa netritv kiya tha, ve newcasal ko punerjeevit karne ke liye laut aaye the. team ke punarnirmaan ke liye unhone sten simor ke saath haath milaayaa or 1965 main unhone dwitiya shrenee kii championships jeetne main safalataa mili. iske baad newcastle ekaisi team ban gayi thi jo bahut hi asthir or purvanumaan se pare thi or jo sadaiv hi acchhee se acchhee team ko haraane main samarth thi lekin ise kabhi bhi thik se apni shakti kaa anubhav nahin ho paaya.
 harvey kii team 1968 main pehali baar europe ke liy chaynit hui or agle hi varsh usne inter-citis fares cap jeet kar sabko achambhit kar diya, isamen unhone final main hungary ke ujpast ke viruddh jabardast jeet hasil karne ke pehle apni jeet ke raste main sporting libson, feyanurd, real zaragoza or rangers ko haraaya tha. newcastle ke paas ek bharosemand team thi or 9wein number par ek lokapriy gol arjeet karne vaale khilaadi ko rakhane kii newcastle kii parampara ab bhi chalati rahi kyonki welshaman wine davis ek prasidd khilaadi the.
 europea vijay ke baad ke varshon main harvey pratibhashali manoranjankartaaon kii ek shrankhla lekar aaye jisane newcastle samooh ko bahut khush kar diya. jon tuder, jimi smith, tony green, terry hibit or visheshtah striker melcom mekadonald jaise sabhi khilaadi apane samarthakon ke pasandida khilaadi ban gaye. melcom mcdonald ko upnaam 'supermack' diya gaya tha, vah newcastle ke sabase lokapriy khilaadiyon main se ek the or aaj tak bhi samarthak unka bahut sammaan karte hai. ve ek bahut hi kushal gol karne vaale khilaadi the jinhone 1974 or 1976 main egge cap main liverpool ke viruddh or lig cap main manchester city ke viruddh do baar united kii team kaa wemble tak netritv kiya tha lekin prayek avasar par newcastle trophy ko punah tinside main waapas laane main asafal rahaa tha. iske sthaan par ek choti santvanaa yah rahi thi ki 1974 or 1975 main texaco cap main team ko ek ke baad ek safalataa mili thi.
 1980 ke dashak kii shuruyaat se newcastle kaa naatakeey rup se patan ho gaya tha or ab vah dwitiya shrenee par pahunch gaye the. harvey ke sthaan par swami ke rup main gaurden lee aa gayi the phir bahut zaldi unke sthaan par bhi richard dinnis aa gaye or richard denis ke baad bill mcgary aa gaye. lekin vah arthur kaux the jo peter beardsle, kris vaidal, terry macdermout or england ke purv captain kevin kigan, joki team kaa aadhaar the, jaise khilaadiyon ke saath newcastle ko punah pratham shrenee main lekar aaye. 1989 main team kaa pradarshan punah girane se purv tak newcastle jaik charlton, vili macphol or gym smith jaise prabandhakon tathaa paul gescoign jaise khilaadiyon sahit kaaphi samay tak sarvocch shikhar par rahi thi.
 baad main, kigan oswaldo ardels ke sthaan par 1992 main ek laghukaalin sanvida par manager ke rup main tinside laut aaye, unhone pehle yah daawa kiya tha ki maatr yahi ek aisa kaam he jo unhen footbal main waapas aane ke liye prerit kar saktaa he. newcastle team dwitiya shrenee ke galat chor par sangharsh kar rahi thi; sar jon haul sab kuch kar rahe the sivaay club par niyantran sthapit karne ke or newcastle ko iske itihaas main pehali baar tritiy shrenee par pahunchane se bachaane ke liye unhen ek chote se jaadu kii aavashyaktaa thi. apane hi kshetra main portsmouth or bahya kshetron main licester city ke viruddh donon lig game ke final charan main jeetne se unka bachanaa sunishchit ho gaya, licester se unki jeet antim kshan main kiye gaye gol se hui thi, haalanki jais baad main spasht ho gaya, yadi newcastle licester se parajeet ho jaataa to bhi vah batch jaataa.
 1992-93 kaa satr club ke bhagya ke lihaaj se ek naatakeey mode lekar aayaa. grimsbai toun ke viruddh apane hi kshetra main 1-0 se haarane se purv team ne apane pehle 11 lig game main jeet hasil kii or unka yah pradarshan english lig ke 13 lagaataar jeet darj karne se sirf do game peeche rah gaya. sanyogvash grimes bai main 4 may 1993 ko aakraamak style main footbal khelte hue newcastle 2-0 jeet ke saath pratham shrenee kaa vijeta ban gaya or ise premier lig ke liye tarakkee mil gayi.
 kigan ke samay main, newcastle kii jeet kaa silsilaa aage badhata rahaa, 1993-94 ke satr main unhone apni aakraamak style or tritiy sthaan praapt kar sabko prabhavit kiya, yah ucch shikhar par unki vaapasi kaa pehla satr tha. kigan ke aakraamak khel ke siddhant ke kaaran hi newcastle ko ski television ke dwaara "da intertanners" kaa khitaab mila. yah khitaab liverpool ke vipareet 3-0 se darj kii gayi jeet ke madhyam se or bhi surkiyon main aa gaya. iske agle satr main newcastle ne apane sheersh score karne vaale khilaadi andrew kol kaa sauda menchester united ke saath kar liya or is satr ke pratham ardhansh main adhikansh samay tak suchi main aage rahane ke baad bhi ant main unhen chhathvaan sthaan mila. isi satr main club kii yuiefee cap se europea pratispardhaa main vaapasi bhi hui thi haalanki ve bahari gol ke falswaroop duusare round main baahar ho gaye the.
 1995-96 main sthanantarit puunji dwaara anya khilaadiyon ke saath david ginola or les ferdinand ko anubandhit karke newcastle ne apana punarnirmaan kiya. us satr main club premier lig jeetne ke kaaphi sameep pahunch gaya tha or ek samay to vah apane nikatatam virdohi manchester united se 12 ank aage bhi ho gaya tha, lekin antat use haar kaa saamana karnaa padaa. us satr kaa ek match vishesh rup se alag rahaa, yah 3 april 1996 ko hua liverpool ko 4-3 se haraane vaala match tha; april 2003 main is match ko premier lig ke dasven satr ke award samaaroh main "match of da dicade" kaa naam diya gaya. 30 july 1996 ko khitaab naa mil paane kii niraashaa tab kuch had tak kam ho gayi jab club ne ellen shieror ko us samay ke vishwa stareey paarishramik, 15 million pound par anubandhit kiya. 1996-97 ke satr main ek baar phir newcastle sambhavit vijeta manchester united ko saint james park main 5-0 se haraane ke baavjood bhi duusare sthaan par hi rahaa. is satr main club bhi yuiefee cap ke quarter final main pahunch gaya tha.
 janvari 1997 kii shuruyaat main kigan ke chaunkaa dene vaale istife se samarthakon ke bich bahut gahan khinnata or bhram kii sthiti paidaa ho gayi, haalanki kaiyon ko yah vishwaas tha ki isaki shuruuaat to 1995/96 ke satr main khitaabi jeet se baahar hone ke baad se hi ho gayi thi. keni delgish unke uttaradhikari ke rup main aaye or lig main doosraa sthaan paane main club kaa netritv kiya, unhone kigan dwaara banaaye gayi team main koi parivartan nahin kiya. haalanki unka saubhaahgya zaldi hi khilaadiyon ke ghayal hone se hui kshati or 1997-98 satr ke bazaari sthanantaran ke kaaran palat gaya or saath hi saath nae khilaadi jinhen anubandhit kiya gaya tha, ve un khilaadiyon kii kahin se barabari nahin kar paa rahe the jo team se baahar the, is prakaar club lig main 11wein sthaan par rahaa or egge cap kaa final jeet paane main asafal rahaa. 1998-99 ke satr kii shuruyaat main hi delgish chale gaye or iske baad rud gulit ne team kii kaman sanbhali. khiladiyon ko anubandhit karne ke baad club ek baar punah egge cap ke final main pahunch gaya lekin jeetne main asafal rahaa. sheeghra hi agle satr main gulit kaa team ke saath or adhyaksh fredy shefrd ke saath matbhed ho gaya jiske falswaroop prabandhak ko saarvajanik rup se maafi mangani padi or unhone yah kaha ki captain ellen shieror ke saath unki koi anban nahin hui he. gulit ne satr 1999/2000 main 8 match purv club ko chhod diya.
 england ke purv captain bobi robasan ko nae prabandhak ke rup main niyukt kiya gaya. kaman sanbhaalane ke baad unka pehla gharelu match vishesh rup se yaadgaar or prabhaavshaalee tha; jisame newcastle ne sheffield vencede par 8-0 se jeet hasil kii thi, yah aaj tak club kii gharelu jeet kaa ek kirtiman he. is tarah ke achche pradarshan ne robasan ke pehle satr main premier lig main club kii upasthiti sunishchit karvaa di, haalanki agle satr main team europe ke liye chaynit nahin ho saki. 2001 -02 ke satr main haalanki kuch chunautiyaan saamne aayin or chauthe sthaan par samapti ke baad newcastle ko champion lig ke liye aharta mil gayi. robasan ke khilaadi jis aakraamak style main khelte the uske kaaran robasan kii team or kigan ke intertanners ke bich tulana kii jane lagi jisase antat tinside kii vardi me intertanners ke hi tag ko vaapasi mil gayi. is satr kii surkiyon main manchester united ke vipareet khelaa gaya 4-3 match, arsenal ke vipareet 3-1 kii jeet or leeds united tathaa darbi country main kii gayi shandaar vaapasi aadi shaamil the. agle satr main, robasan ne lig main teesraa sthaan praapt karne main or champion lig ke samooh staron main team kaa netritv kiya lekin nokout str tak nahin pahunch sake. haalanki 2003/04 kaa satr itnaa yaadgaar nahin rahaa kyonki club qualifing round main hi champion lig se baahar ho jane ke kaaran is satr ke liye yuiefee cap par nirbhar ho gaya or semifinal tak prabhaavshaalee pradarshan ke baavjood bhi ve cap jeetne main safal nahin ho sake or premear lig main paanchave sthaan par samaapti kii. board or kuch khilaadiyon ke saath matbhed or champion lig ke liye chaynit na ho paane ke kaaran robasan ko unke pad se hataa diya gaya.
 greim suunes ko robasan ke sthaan par laayaa gaya. robasan ke darje ko dekhte hue inaki niyukti samarthakon ke bich ek prashn ban gayi thi or apni niyukti par lagbhag 10 naabad maichon kii lakeer ke baad bhi 2004/05 va 2005/06 donon hi satron main khilaadiyon dwaara oonche sthanantaran shulk kii maang or 17 million pound ke record paarishramik par micel oven ke anubandh ke kaaran team ke saath hue kai matbhedon se yah anubhav kiya gaya ki team abhi bhi aavashyak maanak str par nahin pahunch saki he or 2 farvari 2006 ko unhen pad se hataa diya gaya. shuruyaat main youth academy ke nideshak glen roder ne asthaayee taur par suunes kaa sthaan liya. unke netritv main khele gaye pehle match main ellen shieror ne jeki milbern ko club ke sarvaadhik gol karne vaale khilaadi ke sthaan se hataa kar swayan unka sthaan le liya. maatr saadhe teen mahino main hi apane netritv dwaara team ko 15wein sthaan se 7wein sthaan par laa dene ke falswaroop roder ko ant main team kaa purnakalik prabandhak bana diya gaya. yogadan or puraskaron se anugrihit hokar shieror ne 2005-06 ke satr ke ant main avkaash le liya, unhone ab tak club ke liye kul 206 gol kiye the. 2005-06 ke satr kii acchhee samaapti ke baad bhi 2006-07 ke satr main roder kaa bhagya atyant shighrata ke saath palat gaya, aisa is ghatana ke kaaran hua kyonki unhone youth academy ke khilaadiyon par premear lig or europe main pratispardhaa karne ke liye bharosa kar liya tha or ise aaj tak club ke itihaas main varisht khilaadiyon kii sabase buree kshati ke rup main dekha jaataa he, unhone parsparik sahmati dwaara 6 may 2007 ko club chhod diya.
 sam alrdise ko 15 may ko roder ke sthaan par manager ke rup main niyukt kiya gaya or baad main yah adhyaksh ke rup main fredy shefrd dwaara kiya gaya antim kaary ban gaya, jinhone 7 june ko anichhaapurvak apane antim share club main mike eshle ko bech die or unka sthaan nae adhyaksh kris mort ne le liya.
 is satr main ek aashajanak shuruyaat karne ke baavjood bhi newcastle bottm club darbi county se haar gaya or shuruaati qatari se prashansakon ke pasandida khilaadiyon kii anupasthiti ke kaaran 8 maheene se bhi kam samay tak kaman sanbhaalane ke baad aapasi sahmati ke aadhaar par 9 janvari 2008 ko alrdise ko club chhodkar janaa padaa.
 16 janvari ko yah tay ho gaya ki kevin kigan prabandhak ke rup main newcastle main sansanikhej vaapasi karne vaale hai, unhen 8 janvari 1997 se club ko chode hue ab 11 varsh or 8 din ho chuke the. ve newcastle ke anekon prashansakon kii pramukh pasand the. janvari 2008 main hui anya niyuktiyon main denis vaais ne footbal nideshak kaa pad sambhala or saath hi saath tony gimenez upaadhyaksh banakar tathaa jeff vatere takneekee samanvayak banakar aaye. iske peeche yah vichaar tha ki ek mahadwipiya style kii kaary sanrachana viksit kii jaye jo kigan ke samarthan main kaary kare. vaais or vatere ko is saude ko karne ke liye jiminez ko bulaane se pehle aarambhik moolyankan karnaa tha. iske atirikt april 2008 main david williamson ko parichaalan nideshak ke rup main niyukt kiya gaya. june main mort ne adhyaksh pad chhodane kaa nirnay kar liya or unke sthaan par derek llembias aa gaye jo eshle ke puraane saathi the.
 shuruyaat main kigan kii vaapasi se vaisaa prabhaav nahin padaa jaisaa ki apekshit tha kyonki khele gaye 8 maichon main se ek main bhi club ko jeet nahin mili jisase ki ve egge cap se baahar ho gaye or isse unhen maamuli bahishkaar kaa saamana karnaa padaa, lekin newcastle arsenal, manchester united or liverpool sareekhon ke saath khel rahaa tha. satr ke bache hue 8 maichon main club kaa bhagya bilkul palat gaya or club ne premier lig main apana sthaan surakshit karne ke liye paryaapt maichon main jeet hasil kar lee or sammanjanak rup se 12wein sthaan par samaapti kii. jab satr samaapti kii ore badhane laga tab kigan saarvajanik rup se yah kahkar board kii aalochana karne lage ki vah unhen aavashyak aarthik sahaayta nahin de rahaa he jisase ki vah club ko sheersh 4 main sthaan dilaa saken. unke aaropon ke kaaran swami mike eshle ko lekar vivaad hone laga joki pehle hi stock market main kai million kaa ghaataa hone kii khabaron se sangharsh kar rahe the.
 2008-09 satr main board or kigan ke madhya teen din tak chali baatcheet ke baad greeshm sthantaran khidki kai saptaah tak band rahi, phir kul 232 dinon tak kaman sanbhaalane ke baad 4 sitambar ko kigan ne yah kehete hue apane pad se istipha de diya ki yadi unhen is baat par niyantran rakhane kaa adhikaar nahin he ki club kise anubandhit karegaa to vah ek prabandhak ke rup main kaary kar paane main asamarth hai. pad chhodte samay unhone kaha ki "yah meraa vichaar he ki ek prabandhak ke paas prabandhan kaa adhikaar hona chahie or kisi bhi club ko kasi bhi prabandhak par aise khilaadiyon ko nahin thopanaa chahie jinhen vah nahin chahta ho." saint james park ke maidan ke baahar board ke virodh main bahut bade str par virodh pradarshan kiya gaya, vishesh rup se footbalwis ke maalik or nideshak eshle ke viruddh or agali lig ke gharelu pratispardhaa main 13 sitambar ko hal city kaa virodh kiya gaya.
 5 sitambar 2008 ko maalik mike eshle or prabandh nideshak derek llambias ko lig prabandhakon ke sanghathan dwaara chetavanee di gayi thi ki club kii kaman sanbhaalane vaale agle prabandhak ko santusht rakhen jisase ki is prakaar kii ghatnaon ko dobaaraa hone se or club kii chhavi ko karaab hone se roka ja sake. club ke board ne is chetavanee ke virodh main yah kehete hue palatwaar kiya ki jab kigan ne janvari main punah padbhaar sambhala tha to vah is sanrachana se waakif the, lekin leme ke pramukh prabandhak richard bevan ne yah kehete hue kigan kaa ghor samarthan kiya tha ki kigan or board ke madhya ek sanvida sahmati kaa khandan kiya gaya he. disambar 2008 main yah suchit kiya gaya ki kigan or mike eshle ke bich ek kanoonee jhagada sharu ho rahaa he, jisame kigan ki ore se yah daawa kiya ja rahaa he ki club main unke kaarykaal ke dauraan sanvida kaa ulanghan hua he or eshle yah daawa kar rahe hai ki unki saarvajanik chhavi ko kshati pahunchi he.
 iskaa pataa lagane par agle din ek vistrut aadhikarik kathan main eshle ne club ke maujuuda halaaton, ve aarthik simaaen jinke antargat unka kaarykaal chal rahaa tha or club ko sthir bhavishya pradaan karne ke liye unhone vahaan par jo bhi badlaav kiye the, kaa vivaran dete hue yah ghoshnaa kii ki vah club ko bechnaa chaahate hai. iske saath hi unhone yah bhi spasht kar diya ki unke kathan ko kigan par akraman ke rup main nahin dekha jaye or yah bhi ki ve newcastle united ke "ab bhi prashansak" hai or unhone "paisa banane ke uddeshy se newcastle ko nahin kharida tha", bhavishya main hone vaale maichon kii suraksha ke maddenzar, vah ab "aage club ko aarthik sahyaat de paane ke liye taiyaar nahin hai".
 24 sitambar ko club ne pratispardhi match ke dauraan apni nunatam upasthiti darj kii jo 20,577 thi.yah 1993 main ucch shikhar par rahane se ab tak ke bich kii nunetan upasthiti thi or pichhali nune upasthitiyon se 4000 kam thi. haalanki 20,577 kii bheed ab bhi 32 tiimon vaali ek pari main sheersh se chhathwein sthaan par thi, aisi pari jisame ki manchester united, arsenal, liverpool, chelsia or eston vila sareekhee timen shaamil thim. yah bheed totenham hostpur kii lig cap ke liye ekatrit hui thi, jise durbhagyapurvak newcastle 2-1 se haar gaya. us samay, totenham 2 ankon ke saath premear lig main sabase antim sthaan par tha, jabaki newcastle swayan bhi 4 ankon ke saath neeche se duusare sthaan par tha.
 ek ascharyajanak gatividhi ke antargat purv vimbledon prabandhak jo kinier ne 26 sitambar 2008 ko ek 10 maah kii shuruaati sanvida par newcastle kii kaman sambhaal lee. iske peeche yah vichaar tha ki vah tab tak club kaa prabandhan sanbhaalenge jab tak ki maalik eshle ise bech nahin dete. oktober main kinier ke anubandh kii samaysima 1 varsh ke liye badha di gayi or iske baad 28 navambar ko unhen satr ke aakhir tak club kaa prabandhan dekhne ke liye sthaayee sthaan de diya gaya.
 disambar 2008 ke ant main eshle ne yah ghoshnaa kii ki vah club ke liye ek upyukt khariddaar talash kar paane main asamarth rahe isliye ab vah club ko bechney kaa prastaav bazaar se hataa rahe hai. unhone yah bhi ghoshnaa kii ki ab bhi club ke prati unke iraade nek hai or vah lagaataar club kii unnati ke liye raste talashte rahenge.
 is baat kii jaankaree mili ki eshle ne sthaayee rup se club kaa prabandhak banane ke liye kinier se mukt baatcheet kii. kinier ko die gaye is prastaav par prashansakon ke madhya kaaphi vivaad hua kyonki jahaan kuch team ke aatm vishwaas or manobal main aaye vishaal sudhaaron or vikhyath khilaadiyon jaise kevin nolan, rain tailor or peter lovancrends ke anubandhon ko sweekaar kar rahe the, vahiin duusari ore anya prashansak isliye naaraaz the kyonki ab eshle kinier ko unhi sab sharton kaa prastaav de rahe the jinke liye kevin kigan ne apane jate samay maang kii thi.haalanki farvari 2009 main hryday sambandhi samasyaon ke kaaran kinier ko aspataal main bhartee karaayaa gaya tha or iske baad farvari main unhen oparation karwaanaa padaa tha. swasthyalabh kii chhuttiyon ke dauraan kinier ne swayan ko prabandhkeey kartavyon se alag kar liya tha.
 1 april 2009 ko hui anya ascharyajanak gatividhi ke antargat club ke purv captain ellen shieror ne apni match of da de prastutakarta kii bhoomika se vishram le liya or club ke antarim prabandhak ban gaye, yah club ko bahishkaar se nikaal le jane kaa ek prayaas tha. iske sheeghra baad hi denis vais bhi chale gaye or iske saath hi club ne yah ghoshan kii ki unke sthaan par koi nayaa footbal nideshak nahin aayega. shieror kii niyukti ke baad bhi shieror ke netritv main newcastle sirf ek hi game jeet sakaa jo saint james park main midlesburg ke upar lee gayi 3-1 kii jeet thi, iske atirikt club ke 2 match dra hue or usne 5 match haare. teenon north east premear lig timen, newcastle, sanderland or midlesburg ko bahishkaar kii sambhaavna kaa saamana karnaa padaa or unke paas maatr ek hi match tha jo 24 may 2009 ko hona tha, isse newcastle kii premier lig kii chhavi ko pichley 16 varshon main pehali baar khatra mahasoos hua. newcastle united kaa boro ke saath bahishkaar kar diya gaya jab ve vila park main apane purv midfilder demian daf dwaara kiye gaye gol ke baad eston vila ke vipareet 1-0 se haar gaye.

 jimi laurence ke paas newcastle ke sarvaadhik maichon main khilne kaa kirtiman he, unhone 1904 se 1921 ke bich ek golkeeper ke rup main 496 pratham-team match khele hai. purv captain or left back frank hudspeth duusare sthaan par hai, jo 472 maichon main khele hai or 37 avasaron par score kiya he.
 ellen shieror 1996 se 2006 ke bich khele gaye sabhi maichon main 206 gol karne ke saath club ke sheersh golscorer hai, unhone yah sthaan farvari 2006 main jaiki milbern kii 200 teli ko peeche karke praapt kiya he. milbern ke paas yah kirtiman 1957 se tha, vishwa yuddh II ke dauraan unke dwaara score kiye gaye 38 yudhkalin lig gol isamen shaamil nahin kiye gaye hai. unke asaadhaaran saajhedaar len white, 153 gol ke saath newcastle ke tisare sheersh scorer hai. albert stebbins newcastle ke ab tak ke sheersh scorer ho sakate the lekin unke gol is ginati main shaamil nahin kiye gaye hai kyonki uname se adhikansh vishwa yuddh II ke dauraan banaaye gayi the.
 club ke itihaas main prati match kii dar se sabase safal striker hughi galechar hai - jinhone apane 174 outing main 143 gol ke saath 82 pratishat kii strike dar praapt kii.
 newcastle kii sabase badi jeet apane gharelu match main 13-0 kii thi jo usne prabhag 2 main newport county ke vipareet 5 oktober 1946 ko jeeta tha.
 newcastle kii sarvaadhik gharelu upasthiti 68,386 he, yah kirtiman 3 sitambar 1930 ko chelsia ke vipareet khele gaye match main darj kiya gaya tha.chelsia ke dwaara galechar ke anubandh se prashansakon kaa gussa bhadak uthaa or hajaaron kii sankhya main jeeyordi janata apane nayak kaa swaagat karne ke liye upasthit hui. maidan ke sarvaadhik sankhya main darshakon kii upasthiti ke atirikt hajaaron or darshak maidan ke baahar ekatra the. vartmaan main sent james park kii kshamta 52,387 he, isliye isaki sambhaavna bhi bahut hi kam he ki in kirtimanon ko nikat bhavishya main dhwast kiya ja saktaa he. premier lig main ab tak darj kii gayi sarvaadhik upasthiti 52,327 he jo 28 agast 2005 ko manchester ke viruddh khele gaye ek match main darj kii gayi thi. newcastle ne championships match kii upasthiti kaa bhi kirtiman darj kiya he, jisame 2009-10 satr main 24 april 2010 ko ipaswitch toun ke viruddh club ke final gharelu match main 52,181 darshak upasthit hue the.
 newcastle united ke paas bhi champiens lig kaa ek kirtiman he, yah kirtiman champiens lig ke itihaas main ab tak ekmaatr aisi team hone kaa he jo samooh str ke apane pehle teen match haarane ke baad bhi agle str ke liye chaynit ho gayi. yah adbhut kirtiman 2002/03 ke satr main hasil kiya gaya tha jab newcastle dynamo kiv 2-0, feyenurd 0-1 or juvents 2-0 se haarane ke baad bhi juvents 1-0, dynamo kiv 2-1 or feyenurd 2-3 ke vipareet dwitiya samooh str par pahunch gaya tha.

 nimnlikhit prabandhakon main sabhi ne kam se kam ek trophy newcastle united ke charge main jiti thi:
 6 sitambar 1895 ko hui sthapana se lekar 1997 tak, newcastle united kaa swaamitv or parichaalan sheyaron ke aadhaar par ek privet limited company ke rup main tha.
 april 1997 main, premier lig ke bich shuru hue nae chalanon ke anusaar, sar jon haul kii adhyakshata main club ko public limited company ke rup main stock exange main jari kar diya gaya, iske peeche ek bade sporting club newcastle ko aarthik sahaayta dene kaa uddeshy bataaya gaya. adhikansh share haul parivaar yaa unke vyavsaayik saajhedaar fredy shefrd ke paas chale jane ke kaaran yah prastaav adhik safal nahin rahaa.
 1997 main, sar jon haul adhyaksh pad se hat gaye or ek gaiyr prabandhkari nideshak bane rahe, adhyaksh ke rup main unka sthaan fredy shefrd ne liya, ab jon ke putr duglas haul or beti ellison antonopolas hi board main haul parivaar ke pratinidhi the. ek saarvajanik ninda ke baad zaldi hi shefrd or duglas haul ne istipha de diya or 1998 main 10 maheenon baad punah waapas aa gaye.
 1998 ke ant main, media samooh antiel 10 million pound main club kii 6.3 pratishat daavedaari kharidne ke baad puri tarah se club par adhikaar karne ke mansoobey bana rahaa tha. baad main april 1999 main campatishan kamishan ne ruport murdoch, biskibi ke maalik, dwaara manchester ke prastaavit niyantran ko roak dene ke baad, iskaa niyantran bhi sthagit kar diya gaya.
 2007 main ek ascharyajanak gatividhi ke antargat vyavsaayee mike eshle ne ek holding company, saint james holdings ke dwaara club ke 41 pratishat share kharid liye, yah duglas or haul kii sanyukt daavedaari kaa sauda tha, spashtatah yah sab shefrd kii jaankaree se baahar tha joki us samay biimaar the or aspataal main bhartee the. stock market ke niyamon ke niyamon ke tahat, is kharidari ke liye eshle ke dwaara usee mulya yaa bade mulya par anya shairdharakon ko niyantran kaa aupchaarik prastaav jari karnaa aavashyak tha, jisame shefrd, prashansak or sansthagat niveshak shaamil the. aage aane vaale saptahon main, eshle ko or bhi adhik share mil jane par shefrd antat prastaav ke samaapt hone ke purv apni 28 pratishat kii daavedaari bechney ko taiyaar ho gaye jisase iskaa gaiyr soochikaran ho gaya, haalanki anya partiyon dwaara pichley samparkon ko di gayi kii pratikraya ke uttar main shefrd ne saarvajanik rup se yahi kaha ki ise "khareednaa asambhav he" or yah sanket die ki ve iske vipaksh main prastaav bhi laa sakate hai. newcastle united pielsi ke share 18 july 2007 ko subah 8 baje landan stock exange se gaiyr soocheekrut ho gaye, iske saath hi agle maheene shefrd or anya nideshkon ne club chhod diya.
 jabaki mike eshle, holding company or is prakaar club ke bhi akele maalik hai, tab bhi unhone swayan ko club ke nideshak board main koi aadhikarik pad nahin diya he, pehle unhone vishram kii avasthaa main kris mort ko adhyakshata di or 2008/09 satr ke prarambh ke samay derek llembias ko di.
 kigan ke achambhit karne vaale istife par prashansakon ke virodh ke baad 14 sitambar 2008 ko eshle ne yah ghoshnaa kii ki vah club ko bechney kaa prastaav jari karne vaale hai, unhone kaha ki "maine aapaki baat suni he. aap log chaahate hai ki mai chalaa jaaun. ab mai yahi karne kaa prayaas kar rahaa hum. unhone yah bhi kaha ki unhone club ko kharidne main or karjon ko nipataane main kul 244 million pound kharch kiye the. nirikshankartaaon dwaara yah aankalan kiya gaya ki yah ek aisa sauda he jo kai kharidaaron ko aakarshith karegaa isliye sambhawatah vah kul 300 million pound se adhik main club ko nahin bech paayenge.
 eshle ke kathan ke kuch hi samay baad yah soochna mili ki 200 million pound kii record keemat par menchester city ko kharidne se purv abudhabi united samooh ne enuefasi or manchester city ko anubandhit kiya he, yah ghoshnaa 1 sitambar ko kii gayi thi. 28 disambar 2008 ko eshle ne bechney kaa prastaav waapas le liya.
 31 may 2009 ko ai media soochna main yah bataaya gaya ki eshle phir se club ko bechney kaa prayaas kar rahe hai. 7 june 2009 ko eshle ne is baat kii pushti kii ki club 100 million pound main bechney ke liye prastaavit he or unhone sambhavit kharidaaron se kaha ki ki ve apane prastaavon ke saath unhen email karen. 12 agast 2009 tak koi bhi prastaav nahin milane par eshle apni chhuttiyon se laut aaye or club main ek or varsh rahane kii sambhaavna dekhne lage.
 5 agast 2008 ke rup main
 vartmaan main club ke pramukh praayojak naurdarn rock bank or khel sambandhi saamaan banane vaali company puma he. mike eshle sareekhe maalikon dwaara club ke sambandh un pratyaksh khel sambandhi khudara shrinkhalaaon se bhi ho gaya tha jinaki sthapana un logon ne hi kii thi.
 naurdarn rock saude kii ghoshnaa april 2003 main hui thi or yah kaha gaya tha ki yah 2004/05 satr ke ant tak prabhavi rahegi. april 2004 main ise 2009/10 tak ke satr ke liye badha diya gaya. baad main farvari 2008 main rin sankat ke dauraan naurdarn rock kaa prabhavi rup se rashtrikaran kar diya gaya, isko thik se spasht karne ke liye yah kaha ja saktaa he ki, bank of england se liye gaye qarz se tulana karne par or 2008 kii pehali chamahin main 585 million pound ke nuksaan ke baad, is saude kii keemat club ke liye 25 million pound thi.
 adidas kit kaa sauda 2009/10 satr main samaapt ho gaya.
 club ne 1980 tak shart ke praayojakon ko pravesh nahin diya tha. isse pehle club ke bryker scottish & newcastle se puraane sambandh the, jo saint james park ke milbern stand ke thik vipareet sthit tin bryuari ke maalik the. 1980 or 90 ke dashak ke dauraan club kii pattee main unke bear brands mike ivance or newcastle brown ele ke chinh ko sthaan diya gaya. 2000 main antiel main parivartan ke saath prayojan sauda samaapt hone ke saath or 2004 main tin bryuari band hone par ye sambandh prabhavi rup se 1 july 2007 ko samaapt ho gaye, yah eshle dwaara niyantran liye jane ke samay kii baat he jab club ne 3 million pound main carling ke saath sauda kiya tha, haalanki newcastle brown ele kii upasthiti ab bhi maidan ke kuch hisson main thi or may 2008 tak club kii website main club factofil pej par galloget stand kaa naam newcastle brown stand hi tha.
 18 janvari 2010 ko naurdorn rock ne yah ghoshnaa kii ki unhone newcastle united ke saath ek nayaa 4 varshiya prayojan sauda kiya he jisaki keemat 1.5 million pound se lekar 10 million pound ke adhiktam sambhavit mulya ke bich thi, yah 2010/11 satr se shuru ho rahi thi or prayojan kii purna 4 varsh kii avadhi main premear lig main khilne par nirbhar thi. isamen do varshon ke baad ek samiksha bindu bhi tha.
 10 janvari 2010 ko newcastle united footbal club ne puma ke saath nae saude kii ghoshnaa kii jiske falswaroop puma nayaa aadhikarik aapurtikarta or newcastle united ke liye utpaadon kii pratikruti kaa aadhikari license praapt aapurtikarta ban gaya. nae saude ke anusaar puma 2010/11 se shuru hone vaale satr se lekar do varshon tak team kit, pratikruti kit or prashikshan upkarnon kii aapurti karegaa.
