 paal yaa baadbaan pawan kii shakti dwaara kisi vaahan ko jal, him yaa dharti par aage dhakelne ke ek saadhan ko kehete hai. aamtaur par paal kapade yaa anya kisi saamagree se bani ek sataha hoti he jo vaahan main jade hue mastul kahalaane vaale ek sakht khambe ke saath lagi hui hoti he. jab paal par vaiu pravaah kaa prahaar hota he to paal phool-sa jaataa he or aage ko dhakela jaataa he. yah bal mastul dwaara yaan ko prasaarit hota he. kuch paalon kaa aakaar aisa hota he jinpar pawan ek upar kii ore uthaane vaala bal bhi prastut karti he. is se bhi vaahan ko aage dhakelne main asaani hoti he. paal or patang donon main pawan ke bal kaa prayog vastuon ko gati dene ke liye hota he.
