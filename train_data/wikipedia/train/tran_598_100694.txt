 australian cricket team ne 9 oktober se 10 november 2008 tak bharat kaa daura kiya or border-gavaskar trophy ke liye chaar test maichon main khele. duusare test match ke dauraan sachin tendulkar ne test cricket main 12,000 ran banane vaale pehle vyakti bane, bryan lara ke 11,953 ran kaa record todaa. sachin ne kaha, "jis din unhone record hasil kiya tha, us din" yah nishchit rup se mere karier ke 19 varshon main sabase badi uplabdhi he ". duusare test match main bharat kii 320 ran kii jeet australia ke khilaaf ranon ke mukaable unki sabase badi jeet thi, jo melbern main 1977 main hui 222 ranon kii jeet thi or ranon ke maamle main unki sabase badi jeet thi. tisare test match kii pehali pari main, gautam gambhir or ve ve es lakshmana ek test pari main doharaa shatak banane vaale pehle bhartiya khilaadi bane. is shrinkhalaa main do bhartiya cricketron - anil kumble or saurav ganguli ke antim test bhi dekhe gaye.
