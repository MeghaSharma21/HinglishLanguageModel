 kuwait pashchim asia main sthit ek samprabhu arab emirate he, jisaki seemaa uttar main saudi arab or uttar or pashchim main iraq se milti he. uttar se lekar dakshin tak kii adhiktam duuri 200 kimi or purv se lekar pashchim tak kii duuri 170 kimi he. kuwait ek arabi shabd he, jiskaa arth he 'pani ke kareeb ek mahal'. kariban 30 lakh kii jansankhya vaale is sanvaidhanik rajshahi vaale desh main sansadeey vyavastha vaali sarkaar he. kuwait nagar desh kii aarthik or raajnneetik rajdhani he. kuwait main anek dweep bhi shaamil hai, jisamen iraq kii seemaa se laga bumiyan sabase bada he.
 1990 main kuwait par iraq ne akraman kar kabja jamaa liya tha. saat maheene kaa iraqi kabja sanyukt rajya america nit sena dwaara sidhe akraman ke baad khatm hua tha. lautati hui iraqi sena ne kariban 750 tel ke kunon ko khaak main mila diya, jo ek badi aarthik or payaarvaran traasadee ke rup main parinit hui. yuddh main kuwait kii aadharbhoot sanrachana buree tarah se kshatigrast hui, jise punah sudhaarna padaa.
