 uttrakhand vidhansabha chunaav, 2002 bharat ke uttrakhand rajya main hua pehla vidhansabha chunaav tha. jab uttrakhand 9 navambar 2000 ko bharat ke sattaiswein rajya ke rup main astitv main aayaa tha tab rajya main bhartiya janata party kii antarim sarkaar banaai gai. 2002 main hue chunaavon se rajya main pehali rajya sarkaar kaa gathan hua tha jo janata dwaara chuni gai thi. is chunaav ke liye rajya main kul 6,753 matdaan-kendra banaae gaye the or chunaav tithi 14 farvari 2002 thi.
 in vidhansabha chunaav main kul 927 pratyaashee maidan main the jinamein se kul 70 pratyaashee nirvaachit hue . in chunaavon kaa parinaam is prakaar rahaa:
 is chunaav main bhartiya rashtriya congress 36 siten jitkar sabase bade dal ke rup main ubhari or rajya main pehali sarkaar congress kii bani.
