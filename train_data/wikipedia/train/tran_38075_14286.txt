 camp dharti purvi antarctic kaa ek patala sa bhubhag he. yah camp tat se bhitari bhoomi kshetra he jo ek phank ke rup main dakshini dhruv tak jaataa he. rekhaansh ke hisaab se yah 56° 25' purv or 59° 34' purv ke bich kaa kshetra he. camp dharti ke purv main mike robertson dharti or pashchim main anderbi dharti he. aygarden dweep samooh camp kshetra main aata he.
