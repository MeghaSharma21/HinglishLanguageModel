 ganit, sankhyikee evam ganitiya vigyaanon main us raashi ko prachal kehete hai jo falnon evam charon ko ek ubhaynisht char kii sahaayta se paraspar jodatii he. praaia: t ko prachal ke rup main prayog kiya jaataa he. prachal ke prayog se charon ke sambandh saral tareeke se abhivyakt karne kii suvidha praapt ho jaati he. duusare shabdon main, prachal ke prayog ke bina raashiyon kaa aapasi sambandh ek sameekaran kii sahaayta se bataanaa bahut kathin, asambhav yaa jatil hota he.
 dhyatavya he ki prachal shabd kaa prayog alag-alag sandarbhon main bhinn-bhinn prakaar se kiya jaataa he.
