 lipi yaa lekhan pranali kaa arth hota he kisi bhi bhashaa kii likhaawat yaa likhane kaa dhang. dhvaniyon ko likhane ke liye jin chihnon kaa prayog kiya jaataa he, vahi lipi kehalaati he. lipi or bhashaa do alag alag chizein hoti hai. bhashaa wo chiz hoti he jo boli jaati he, likhane ko to use kisi bhi lipi main likh sakate hai. kisi ek bhashaa ko uski saamaanya lipi se duusari lipi main likhnaa, is tarah ki vaastavik anuwaad na hua ho, ise lipyantaran kehete hai.
 
 
 
 yadhyapi sansar bhar main prayog ho rahi bhashaaon kii sankhya ab bhi hajaaron main he, tathaapi is samay in bhashaaon ko likhane ke liye keval lagbhag do darjan lipiyon kaa hi prayog ho rahaa he. or bhi gaharaai main jane par pataa chalata he ki sansar main keval teen prakaar kii hi mul lipiyaan he- 
 ye tino lipiyaan teen alag-alag kshetron main viksit huin jo parvaton evam marusthalon dwaara ek-duusare se alag-alag sthit hai.
 isamen swar apane puure akshar kaa rup liye vyanjan ke baad aate hai.
 isaki harek ikai main agar ek yaa adhik vyanjan hota he or usapar swar kii maatra kaa chihn lagaaya jaataa he. agar ikai main vyanjan nahin hota to swar kaa puraa chihn likhaa jaataa he.
 ye sarlikrut chitra hote hai.
