 mudrarakshas ke naam se suprasiddh subhash chandra gupta kii chintak evam patrkaar ,sampradayikta virdohi lekhan evam dalit sahitya chintak ke rup main vikhyath varisht sahityakar mudrarakshas ke 12 upanyaas, 3 vyangy sangrah evam 10 se zyada naatakon kii pustaken prakashit hui. kai naatakon kaa inhone nirdesan bhi kiya. vibhinn patr-patrikaon main aalochana sambandhi niyamit lekhan karte rahe. 'gyanoday' naam kii patrika kaa inhone sampaadan bhi kiya. lambii beemaari ke baad 83 varsh kii umr main 13 june 2016 ko lakhanu main nidhan hua.
