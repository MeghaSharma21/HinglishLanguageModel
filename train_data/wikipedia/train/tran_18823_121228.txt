 kori james anderson newzealand ke ek cricket khilaadi hai. wo christchurch se hai or uttar jila cricket team kii or se khelte hai. unhone 16 june 2013 ko antararashtriya ekadivasiy cricket main england ke viruddh padaarpan karte hue newzealand ke liye ek divasiy antarrashtriya cap praapt kii. unhone 1 janvari 2014 ko westeindies ke kilaaf queenstown main khelte hue 17 varsh puraane sabase tej shatak purna karne ke shahid afaridi ke kirtiman ko pachhadte hue apane naam kiya. unhone maatr 36 gendon main shatak purna kiya.
