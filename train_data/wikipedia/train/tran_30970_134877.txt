 hansasandesh, veydantadeshik dwaara 13ve shatabdi main rachit sanskrit prem kavy he. yah 110 padyon kaa ek chota kavy he jisamen hans ke madhyam se ram lanka sthit apni priya sita ko sandesh bhejte hai. yah kavy ek sandesh kavy he or isamen kalidas ke meghdoot kii style kaa anukaran kiya gaya he. vaishnav logon ke liye yah bahut mahatvapurn granth he.
