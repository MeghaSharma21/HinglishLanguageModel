 pratim dasgupta ek bhartiya patrkaar, film sameekshak, patakatha lekhak, film nirdeshak hai. wo da telegraph samachar patr ke liye bollywood filmon kii samikshaayen likhte hai. unki lekhak-nirdeshak ke rup main pratham film paanch adhyay he jo priyanshu chatarji or diya mirza abhinit he.
 pratim dasgupta kaa janm 1981 main kolkata main hua or apni shiksha south point skool se kii. wo saint jevirs college, kolkata se jan sampark or film adhyayan main maanad praapt hai.
