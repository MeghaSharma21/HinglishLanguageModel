 lucasfilm limated ek amareeki film nirmaataa company he jise jyorj lucas dwaara 1971 main sthapit kiya gaya tha. yah san francisco, california main sthit he. lucas is company ke chairmen or sieo he or mishelin chaauu isaki adhyaksh va sioo he.
 company mukhya taur par star wars filmon ke nirmaan ke liye jaani jaati he or isane kai anya box offis hit filmon kaa bhi nirmaan kiya he jiname indiana jones film shrankhla or american graphity shaamil he. yah kai nai filmon kii takneekein khojane va sthapit karne ke liye agraniy he jiname special effect, dhwani, computer anemation shaamil he.
