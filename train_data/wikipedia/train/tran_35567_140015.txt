 
 2016-17 logaan cap ek pratham shrenee cricket vartmaan main disambar 2016 or may 2017 ke bich zimbabwe main yojit kiya ja rahaa pratiyogita he. toornament ek duusare ko chaar baar khel rahe prayek team ke saath baarah round bhar main khelaa jayega. toornament kii shuruyaat he, kyonki sajo chunautiyon main se sthagit kar diya gaya kholane ke saath ek saptaah baad kii tulana main mul kii yojana banaai jagah lene ke liye punarnirdhaarit mail khaataa he.
 mid west rhinoj or metabeleland taskars ke bich udghaatan sthirataa khilaadiyon dwaara hadtaal ke saath teen din kii shuruyaat main baadhit kiya gaya tha. virodh, gharelu khilaadiyon ne kiya jaisaa ki ve november main bhugtan nahin kiya gaya tha. metabeleland taskars match kii duusari pari jabt kar liya he, or isliye mid west rhinoj match jeet liya.
