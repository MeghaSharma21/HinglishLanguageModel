 avasthaa aarekh yaa 'sthiti aarekh' yaa 'state diagram' aankik electronic, sanganak vigyaan evam sambandhit kshetron main prayukt ek mahatvapurn aarekh he jo kisi logical pranali kii ek avasthaa se duusari avasthaa main jane kii sharton aadi ko pradarshit karti he. iske liye jaruri he ki sambandhit pranali simit avasthaaon vaali ho. avasthaa aarekh ke kai rup hai or unamem thodaa-bahut antar hota he.
 praayah avasthaa aarekh se navaparichiton ko state diagram or flo-chart main bhram ho jaataa he. neeche ke chitra main donon kii tulana di gayi he. state mashin kii kriyaayein kinhin spasht ghatnaon ke kaaran hoti hai jabaki flo-chart main ek kaam samaapt hone par doosraa kaam swatah armbh ho jaataa he.
 swaroop kii drushti se bhi donon main antar he. avasthaa aarekh main 'chaanp' prasanskaran yaa transishan ko nirupit karte hai jabaki flo-charton main prasanskaran khaanon se sambandhit he. jab tak koi ghatana nahin hoti tab tak state mashin niskriy bani rahati he jabaki kisi 'node' main sthit hone par flo-chart apane kaam main vyast rahati he.
