 aisa kaha ja rahaa he ki subhash chandra bose ki antim gyaat yatra or vimaan durghatana main unki mrityu ke baad netaji ke saath jo moolyawaan vastuen milin thi, uskaa aaazaad hind ke logon ne durupyog kiya. aaazaad hind fouj ke kosh ko us samay saat lakh dollar ke baraabar aanka gaya tha. aaazaad hind fouj ke kosh ke gaban ke bare main sabase pehle anuj dhar ne san 2012 main chhapi 'indiaj biguest cover-ap' naamak apni pustak main vistaar se likhaa tha.
 varsh 2016 main saarvajinak kiye gaye netaa ji se jude dastavejon se is baat kii pushti hui he ki aaazaad hind fouj kaa khajaana churaayaa gaya tha. san 1951 se 1955 ke dauraan bharat or japan ke bich hui baatcheet se yah bhi pataa chalata he ki bharat kii tatkaaleen nehru sarkaar ko is kosh ke gaban ke bare main pataa tha. naishanal archive kii gopneey filon se pataa chalata he ki sarkari adhikariyon ko netaa ji ke do purv sahyogiyon par shaq tha. unamem se ek sahyogi ko sammaan diya gaya or nehru kii panchavarshiya yojana kaarykram kaa prachaar salaahkaar banaaya gaya. 
 21 may, 1951 ko tokio mishan ke mukhya adhikari ke. ke. chitoor ne rashtramandal maamalon ke sachiv bi. en. bhattacharya ko subhash chandra bose ke do sahyogiyon- propeganda ministar es. e. ayyar or tokio main aaazaad hind fouj ke mukhyadhikari munga ramaamurti ke bare main andeh jaahir karte hue likhaa tha-
