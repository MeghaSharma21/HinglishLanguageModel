 'mansehara' kaa naam mogul samraat akbar ke mashhoor sipahsalaar raja maan singh par rakhaa gaya tha.
 mansehara se pashchim main afghanistan aana-janaa aasaan he isliye us desh main grihayuddh hone par bahut se afakhaan sharnaarthi yahan aa based. yah zila ek nadi-jharanon, jhiilon or parvaton se bharpoor kshetra he or sailaaniyon main bahut lokapriya he. isaki sabase badi nadi kunhaar nadi he . is zile kii kaayaan vaadi main teen mashhoor jhilein hai - luluser jhil, dudipatsar jhil or saif-ul-muluk jhil. teenon jhilein barf-chhidke pahaado or hari ghaas se ghiri hui hai. saif-ul-muluk ke bare main ek premakatha mashhoor he ki yah ek rajkumaar or uski pari premika ke milane kaa sthaan hua karti thi.
 mansehara zile main teen tahseelein hai - mansehara tehsil, balakot tehsil or ogi tehsil. iske alaava yahan do praant-prashasit qabaili kshetra bhi hua karte the - tor gar or upree tanaval. san 2011 main tor gar ko ek alag zile kaa darja mil gaya or tanaval main bhi ek alag zila banaae jane ke liye sangharsh chal rahaa he. tanaval ilaake main tanoli naamak ek qabeelai log rahete hai jinka rahan-sahan pashtunon se miltaa he lekin jinaki ek alag jaateey pahchaan bhi he.
