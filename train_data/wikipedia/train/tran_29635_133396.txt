 dinesh kumaar mishra urf deenanath mishra, kehuniya hai skool ke purv pradhanadhyaapak he. ve ek shikshavid, utkrisht vaktaa or ek aasthavan vichaarak he. shri mishra ne behatar shiksha or prabandh main bahut mahatv purna bhoomika nibhaai he. ve bahumukhi pratibha ke dhani jane-maane shiksha shastri he or shiksha ke kshetra main satat yogadan karte he. unki manyata he ki yadi sahi tareeke se shiksha di jaye to samaaj kii anek buraaiyon ko mitaayaa ja saktaa he. unki saamaajik rachanaayen pramukh dainik patron main bhi prakashit hoti hai.shri mishra pashchimi champaran, bihar ke mahatvapurn raajnneetik parivaar ke sadsy he jo kehuniya gaanv ke nivasee hai. ve shri krishna kumaar mishra ke bhai tathaa shri gauri shankar pandeya ke damaad hai. abhi haal ke packs chunaav main ve kehuniya roari packs ke adhyaksh nirvaachit hue hai.
