 janendra kii chhathin aupnyasik kriti 'vivart' kaa prakaashan san 1953 main hua. prarambh main yah upanyaas 'saptahik hindustan' main dharawahik rup se prakashit hua. is upanyaas ke kathanak kaa centre jiten kaa charitra he. uski saamaanya paariwarik sthiti se katha kaa vyaavhaarik aarambh hota he. uski asaadhaaran prasiddhi aadi bataakar lekhak katha-vikaas kaa bhavi maarg kholta he. bhuvanmohini ke kathanak main pravesh se usamen gati aati he parantu jab bhuvanmohini jiten se vivaah na karke nareshchandra kii patni ban jaati he tab katha kii samasya kaa ant ho jaataa he. uskaa asafal prem use krantikari dal main sammilit ho jane kii prerana detaa he. chaar varsh ke baad jiten kaa aana, sharan paanaa, bhuvanmohini ke gahane chura kar bhaagna, uske dalwaalon kaa bhuvanmohini ko pakad le janaa, jiten kaa police ko samarpan aadi naatakeeytapurna ghatnaayen kramshah ghatit hone lagati hai. uskaa ant bhi inhen ke jaal main bandhakar aakasmik rup se hota he or pathak ke hryday par koi prabhaav nahin daal paata.
