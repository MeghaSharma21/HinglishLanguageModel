 vikisukti vickemedia foundation dwaara sanchalit pariyojnaon main se viki-aadhaarit parivaar kaa ek sadsy he. yah bhi mediaviki software par hi chalata he. yah pariyojna danniel olston kii pariklpana par aadhaarit evam bryan vibar dwaara karyanvit is pariyojna kaa lakshya vishisht vyaktiyon, pustakon, filmon evam lokoktiyon se praapt ek vrahat uddhran sangrah jutanaa tathaa unke liye byorevar sandarbh sahit jaankaree tathaa ropan soochna ekatrit karnaa. haalanki kai online uddhran sangrah pehle hi uplabdh hai, kintu vikisukti un kuch vishesh main se ek he jo apane upayoktaaon ko yogadan karne kii suvidha bhi uplabdh karaata he. vikisukti ke prishth ullekhneey vyaktittvon ke vikipedia par bane lekhon se antarkadiyon dwaara jude hue hai.
 mul pariyojna purnataya angrezi main aarambh kii gayi thi, july 2004 main ek anya sanskaran dwaara atirikt bhashaaon ko bhi isamen sammilit karnaa aarambh kiya gaya.
 
 july 2004 main lagbhag 70 upadomen aarambh kii gayin. 28 navambar 2010 ke aankadon ke anusaa, 25 sanskaran hue, prayek main 1000 se adhik lekh the. sabase bada vikisukti 22,600 lekhon sahit polish vikisukti tha, jiske baad angrezi, italvi, german, portugali evam roosi pariyojnaayen thim jinamein 5000 se adhik lekh the. iske baad slowe, balgarian, bosnian, turki, slovenian evam francisi rahe. 53 bhashaai sanskarnon main 100 yaa adhik lekh the.
