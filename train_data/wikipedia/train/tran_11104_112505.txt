 robinsan aar66 ek halke vajan vaala helicopter he jise robinsan helicopter company dwaara dizin or nirmit kiya gaya he. isaki ghoshnaa 2007 main kii gayi thi or yah 2011 se purna rup se bananaa shuru hua.
 yah ek 4 se 5 seat vaala ek halkaa helicopter he. isamen rolls royas aaraar300 terbine ingine laga he jise vishesh taur par is ke liye dizin kiya or banaaya gaya he.
 iskaa airfrem robinsan aar44 kaa majboot rup he. iske mukhya pankhe ki pranali ko kuch aalochakon dwaara truti yukt mana jaataa he.
 rolls royas araaar300 ingine, joki 300 ashwashakti adhiktam, 270 ashwashakti paanch minute or 224 ashwashakti lagaataar utpann karata he. prarambh main, tarabine main 2,000 ghante kaa tibio hoga - airfrem ke aavashyak overhaal samay se 200 ghante kam, jo baad main badhaayaa jaayegaa.
 isamen fadek nahi he jis kaaran galat start se ingine main dhamaakaa ho saktaa he. yah samasya kai or terbine ingine vaale helicopteron main ho sakati he.
