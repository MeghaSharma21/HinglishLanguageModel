 bharat or australian cricket team ke bich chaar test maichon kii shrinkhalaa 23 farvari se 29 march tak rakhi gayi jiskaa pehla muqabla 23 farvari ko pune main khelaa gaya tha.bhartiya cricket control board ne oktober 2016 main daure kii tarikhon kii pushti kii thi. 
 duusare test ke dauraan mishael marsh ko kandhe kii chot kaa saamana karnaa padaa or vah shesh shrinkhalaa se baahar ho gaya. marcus estonis ko unke pratisthaapan ke rup main namit kiya gaya tha. kandhe kii chot ke kaaran shrinkhalaa ke antim do maichon ke liye hardik pandya ko team chhod di gai thi. mishael stark ko pair kii chot ke saath pichley do test maichon se baahar kar diya gaya tha. pat camins ko unke pratisthaapan ke rup main namit kiya gaya tha. chauthe test se pehle, shreyas ayyar ko viraat kohli ke cover ke liye bhartiya team main shaamil kiya gaya, jo kandhe kii chot se peedit tha.
