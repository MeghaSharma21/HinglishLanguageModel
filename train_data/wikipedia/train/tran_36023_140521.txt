 mangalsutra premchand dwaara rachit upanyaas he. yah apurna he.
 mangalsutra' main ek saahitiyik ke jivan kii samasya gayi he. isi drushti se yah upanyaas premuchandra ke anya upanyaason se bhinn he. iske chaar adhyyon main dev sahitya-sadhana main apana jivan vyateet karte hai. unhen kuch vyasan bhi lage hue hai. in donon kaarnon se unka bhautik jivan to sukhi nahin hota. haan, unhen khyaati avashya praapt hoti he. unke do putr, vakil santkumar or madhukumaar hai. jyeshtha putr santkumar jivan main sukh or aishrary chahta he or pita kii jeevandarshan kaa samarthan nahin karata. chota putr unke vichaaron or ardashon se sahamat he. vah bhi pita kii bhanti adarshavaadi he. premuchandra ne devkumar ko jivan ke sangharshon ke falswaroop swanirdhaarit adarsh se vichlit hota hua sa chitrit kiya he. bhavishya main kya hota, iskaa anumaan maatr premuchandra kii pichhali kritiyon ke aadhaar par kiya ja saktaa he. devkumar kii ek putree pankaja bhi he, jiskaa vivaah ho jaataa he .
