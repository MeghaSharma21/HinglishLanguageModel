 jajis logings  whitecross street, monmouth, monmouthshire, wales, main sthit atharahavin sadi kii imaarat he. isaki utpatti 16 veen sadi ke shuruyaat main hui thi va varsh 1756 ke aasapaas yah 'lebar in wain' naamak sarai ban gai. yah 1835 se pehle monmouth ke nyayadhishon kaa asthaayee awaas ban gaya va 1870 ke dashak main royal monmouthshire royal ejiniers kii bhojanshala. 1926 main imaarat ek motor geraj kii taur pe prayog main laane lagi, tathaa gadiyaan footpath par thik hoti thim. phir se nijee ghar banane se pehle yah ek samay conservative club bhi tha. nivartmaan samay main yah ek nijee makaan he jisamen peeche kii taraf ek ghudsal bhi he. yah sayukt rajshahi kii grad dwitiya soochibaddh imaaraton main se ek he va iske saath-saath monmouth heritage trail ke choubees sthalon main se ek bhi.
