 jan jagriti santhaan ek labhanirpeksh sanghathan he. iskaa mukhyalay majhagavaan main he jo kii madhya pradesh ke satna jile main sthit he. 
 iskaa mukhya uddeshy logo main saamaajik, raajnneetik evam aarthik jaagrukta laana he. vartmaan main is sanghathan ke adhyaksh shri mukesh kumaar agarwal evam sachiv shri pankaj kapur he. vartmaan main isamen kareeb 50 sakriy kaaryakarta he. is sanghathan ke madhyam se is kshetra main 2 varsho se pratibha khoj pariksha kaa aayojan karaayaa ja rahaa he or tisare varsh bhi is pariksha kaa aayojan 23 janvari 2011 ko karaayaa gaya he.
