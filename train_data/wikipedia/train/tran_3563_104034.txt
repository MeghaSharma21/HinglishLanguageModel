 olym kerala kaa ek pramukh tyohaar he. olym kerala kaa ek parv he. olym kaa utsav sitambar main raja mahabali ke swaagat main prati varsh yojit kiya jaataa he jo das dinon tak chalata he. utsav trikkakara kerala ke ek maatr vaaman mandir se prarambh hota he. olym main prayek ghar ke aangan main phulon kii pankhudiyon se sundar sundar rangolia daali jaati hai. yuvatiyaan un rangoliyon ke chaaron taraf vrutt banaakar ullas purvak nrity karti hai. is pukalam kaa praarambhik swarup pehle to chota hota he parantu har roj isamen ek or vrutt phulon kaa badha diya jaataa he. is tarah badhate badhate dasven din yah pukalam vrahat aakaar dhaaran kar letaa he. is pukalam ke bich trikkakarppan, raja mahabali tathaa uske ang rakshakon kii pratishtha hoti he jo kachi mitti se banaayin jaati he. olym main noka daud jaise khelon kaa aayojan bhi hota he.
 olym ek sampoornataa se bharaa hua tyohaar he jo sabhi ke gharon ko kushahaali se bhar detaa he.
