 karmveer ek hindi patrika thi. patrakarita ke pitru purush madhavrao sapre kii prerana se iskaa pratham prakaashan 17 janvari 1920 ko jabalpur se hua. iske pratham sampadak maakhanlaal chaturvedi the. navambar 1922 tak yah jabalpur se nikalati thi kintu baad main khandawa se prakashit hui.
