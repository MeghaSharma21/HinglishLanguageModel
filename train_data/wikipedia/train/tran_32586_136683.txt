 vriksh vicharan jaanwaron ke vrikshon par chadhane yaa chadhakar vriksh-se-vriksh ghoomane ko kehete hai. kai praani pedo par aahaar praapt karne yaa chupaane, van-farsh par gum rahe parabhakshiyon se bachane, oonchaai se van ke bade kshetra par grass yaa parabhakshiyon par drushti daalane yaa prajnan main apni santan athwa andon ko surakshit rakhane ke liye chadh jate hai. kuch jaanavar apana puraa yaa adhikansh jivan hi pedo par vyateet karne vaale vrikshavasi hote hai.
