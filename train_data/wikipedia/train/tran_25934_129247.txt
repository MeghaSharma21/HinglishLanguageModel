 durrani samrajya ek pashtun samrajya tha jo afghanistan par kendrit tha or purvottaree iran, pakistan or pashchimottaree bharat par vistrut tha. is 1747 main kandahaar main ahamed shah durrani ne sthapit kiya tha jo abdali kabeeley kaa sardaar tha or iran ke nadir shah kii fuj main ek sipahsalaar tha. 1773 main ahamed shah kii mrityu ke baad rajya uske putron or phir putron ne chalaaya jinhone rajdhani ko kabul sthanantarit kiya or peshawar ko apni sheetakaaleen rajdhani banaaya. ahamed shah durrani ne apana samrajya pashchim main iran ke mashaad shahar se purv main dilli tak or uttar main aamu dariyaa se dakshin main arab sagar tak failaa diya or use kabhi-kabhi aadhunik afghanistan kaa rashtrapita mana jaataa he.
