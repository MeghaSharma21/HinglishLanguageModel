 
 sanyukt rajya bhugarbha surveykshan america sarkaar kii vaigyanik sanstha he. usgs ke vaigyanik america dharti, prakritik sansaadhan or prakritik aapadaaon ityadi ke bare main shodhakarya karte hai. is sanstha kii chaar pramukh vigyaan shakhaaen he jo hai: bhugol, jeev vigyaan, bhugarbhavidya or jalavigyaan. usgs ek anusandhan sanstha he or isapar koi viniamak daayitv nahin he.
 yah sanyukt rajya kaa ekmaatr aantarik vaigyanik vibhaag he. usgs main lagbhag 10,000 log karyrat hai or iskaa mukhyalay reston main sthit he or anya pramukh karyaalay denwar or menlo park main bhi sthit hai.
 usgs kaa bhucamp soochna kendra golden main sthit he or yah vibhaag vishvabhar main bhukampon kii sthiti or tivrata kaa pataa lagane kaa kaam karata he.
  parvat  maidan dweep
