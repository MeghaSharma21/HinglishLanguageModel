 unesco-iechai, jal shiksha ke liye santhaan he. isaki sthapana 2003 main hui thi. yah nederland ke dalfat, main sthit ha tathaa unesco ke sabhi sadsy rajyon ke swaamitv main he. isko unesco tathaa niderland sarkaar dwaara saanjhe tor par "unesco shrenee ek santhaan" ke rup main sthapit kiya gaya ha. yah santhaan duniya kaa jal shiksha ke kshetra main sabase bada santhaan ha tathaa sanyukt raashtra pranali main yahi ek aisa santhaan ha jise adhikrut em es si degri dene kaa gorv praapt ha.
 1957 main shuru kiye gaye kaary, jab ai ech i ne pehali baar vikasshil deshon main karyrat peshewaron ko jal yantriki main snatakottar diploma diya tha, ko unesco-ai ech i jari kiye hue ha.
 unesco-ai ech i ke kaary nimna prakaar se prabhashit han:
 1957 main apni sthapana ke baad se ai ech i, jis naam se yah pehle janaa jaataa tha, 160 deshon, sabhi lagbhag vikasshil/parivartan, kaa pratinidhatv karne vaale 14000 se bhi adhik peshewaron ko snatakottar shiksha de chukaa ha. yah 75 se bhi adhik ummidavaaron ko pi ech di kii oopaadhi se sammanit kar chukaa ha or duniyabar main anek anusandhan tathaa kshamta nirmaan pariyojnaon ko kaarynvit kar chukaa ha.
