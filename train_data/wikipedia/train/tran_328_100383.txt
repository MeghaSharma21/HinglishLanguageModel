 sonpur jankshan railway stesan, stesan code SEE, bhartiya rajya bihar ke saaran jile main sthit ek railway stesan he. yah railway ke uttar purvi sambhag main padataa hai evam sonpur shahar evam patna ke aas paas rahane vaale log is stesan kaa zyaadaatar prayog karte hai. yah shahar ghaghara or ganga nadi ke paas sthit he.
 is anubhag ko apadet karne kii aavashyaktaa he haal ke ghatnaon yaa nai uplabdh jaankaree ko pratibimbit karne ke liye kripaya is lekh ko apadet karen 2011 main, sonpur jankshan ko â‚¹ 20 million kii laagat se stesan parisar main or aasapaas ke vyapak margon or hariyaali ke saath ek nayaa rup diya gaya tha. pichley kuch saalon se sonpur or hajipur ke bich 7 kilometre kii patariyon par doharikaran par kaam kiya gaya he or yah 2012 ke ant tak puraa ho jayega. 
 2009 main, bharat ke sabase lambey road-kam-rail pul kaa nirmaan, digha-sonpur rail-sah-sadak pul, kareeb ganga ke kinare bainkon par chal rahaa tha, patna se pehleja ghat tak pahunchane ke liye. pul agast 2015 ke ant tak puraa kiya gaya tha. yah 4.55 kilometre lanbaa he or isliye bharat main sabase lambey samay tak sadak sah rail rail or duniya main sabase lambey samay tak ek he.
 sonpur jankshan main 4 platform hai platform pul over pul ke saath jude hue hai. 24 coach trainon ko samyojit karne ke liye platform kii lambaai 2011 main badi thi. uplabdh pramukh suvidhaayen hai pratiksha kaksh, computarikrut aarakshan suvidha, vaahan parking. vaahanon ko stesan parisar main pravesh karne kii anumati he stesan main astidi / aiesadi / pisio telephone booth, shauchalay, chaay kii stall or pustak stall bhi he. yatri aarakshan pranali, booking counter or anarakshit tikat system ko air condishning suvidha pradaan kii jaati he.
 oktober 2016 main, railway ne sonapur railway stesan par ek happines jankshan kaa udghaatan kiya, jisase trainon ke liye pratiksha karne vaale yatriyon ko kitaaben or kai anya manoranjan upkaran uplabdh karaae gaye.
