 ganit kii vaastavik vishleshan ke rup main pahachaani jane vaali shakha main riman samakalan kisi falan kaa kisi interval main paribhaashit pratham nishchit paribhaasha he. yah paribhaasha bernahard riman ne di thi. vibhinn phalnom or praayogic anuprayogon ke liye riman samakalan kalan kii moolbhoot prameya athwa sankhyatmak samakalan ke sanniktan dwaara gyaat kiya ja saktaa he.
 riman samakalan vibhinn saiddhanthik uddeshyon ke liye anupyukt he.

 interval ke upar evam falan f se banane vaale aarekh ke neeche kaa samtal kshetra he . ham S kaa kshetrafal gyaat karnaa chaahate hai. hamane ek baar iskaa maapan kiya or kshetrafal ko nimnlikhit rup main nirupit karen:
 riman samakalan kaa mul vichaar S kaa kshetrafal gyaat karne ke liye saralatam sannikatano kaa upyog karnaa he. achche se acha sanniktan lene se hamaara arth he ki seemaant maan main vakr ke antargat S ke kshetrafal ke puri tarah se samaan he.
 dhyaan rahe ki yahan f dhanatmak athwa rinatmak ho saktaa he, S kii paribhaasha ko sanshodhit kiya jaataa he jisase samakalan kaa maan aarekh ke andar aane vaale chihnit kshetrafal ke anuroop ho arthath x-aksh ke upar kaa kshetrafal x-aksh ke neeche neeche ke kshetrafal main se ghataane ke tulya ho.
