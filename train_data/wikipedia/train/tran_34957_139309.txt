 yah lekh italy kii rajdhani evam pracheen nagar 'rome' ke bare main he. isi naam ke anya nagar sanyukt rajya america main bhi he. stanadhariyon kii twachaa par paae jane vaale komal bal ke liye bal dekhen. iskaa paryaayavachi shabd royaan yaa roaan he.
 rome ; angrezi: Rome) italy desh kii rajdhani he. 
 sthiti : 41°55' u.a. tathaa 12°28' pus.de.. vatican nagar ko milakar yah roman catholic dharm kaa centre bhi he. nagar kii sthiti italy prayadweep ke madhya main, pashchimi tat par, tiber nadi ke kinare, nadi ke muhane se 17 mile uttar-purv main he.
 aisa vishwaas kiya jaataa he ki rome nagar kii neenv 'vargakar rome' ke rup main palatin pahari par romulas ke dwaara daali gai thi. iskaa vistaar anya pahadiyon par, evam nadi ke donon ore, baad main hua. rome kii ek visheshta pahari dhaalon par iske chittkarshak udyanon evam girjagharon kii upasthiti he, jinka drishy bada hi ramnik he. nagar main lagbhag 300 girjaghar kai pustkaalay, ajaayabaghar aadi hai. vishvavidhyalay bhawan, europe kaa sundartam aspataal, pales ov justise, aadi anya prasiddh bhawan hai. rail ke dibbe, tramkaar, krushiyantra, shalyachikitsa sambandhi yantra, kaagaj, rasinic padaarth, nakli resham, sabun, kalapradarshan ke saamaan, jaise furniture, kaanch, gahanaa evam chamade ke samaan aadi taiyaar karne ke kaarkaane vahaan hai. nagar paryaton kaa centre evam krishi upajon or oon kaa bazaar bhi he.
