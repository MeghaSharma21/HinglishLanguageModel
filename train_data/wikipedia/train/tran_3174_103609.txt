 elex ek african tota tha, jo ki teevr buddhishalee tha. 
 vaigyanik irine ne apane tote kaa naam bhi apane pareekshan yani Avian Learning EXperiment se lekar ALEX rakhaa.
 iske baad alex kaa prashikshan shuru hua. alex kaa prashikshan aam prashikshanon se ekadam alag tha. alex ko vastuon, uske rang, aakaar, padaarth aadi ke liye prashikshit kiya jaataa tha. do vyakti alex ke saamne khadey hote or donon main ek vyakti apane saathi se sawaal poochta or saamne vaala jawaab detaa. yah sab alex ke saamne hota taaki wo dekh sake ki kya ho rahaa he. udhaaharan ke taur par ek vyakti apni jeb se pile rang kii plastic kii chaabi nikaalata or saamne vaale vyakti se poochta," What is this?" ab uskaa mitra kahata, "Key." yah baar baar doharaaya jaataa. phir sawaal badal kar poocha jaataa, "What color?" ab saamne vaala jawaab detaa,"Yellow". is prakaar se dhire dhire elex bhi samajhane lag gaya ki chaabi kya hoti he or vibhinn rang kaise hote hai.
