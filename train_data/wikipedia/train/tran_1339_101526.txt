 sainya abhyaas yaa yuddh kreedaa main sainya karrwai ke liye prashikshan dene ke uddeshy se sainya sansadhano kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. isamen bina vaastavik yuddh kaa samaanaa kiye hi yaa to yuddh ke prabhaavon kaa pataa lagaaya jaataa he yaa ranneetiyon kii pariksha kii jaati he. isse yah bhi sunishchit hota he ki sena yuddh ke liye taiyaar he.
