 ramchand pakistan ek pakistan kii urdu bhashaa main banane vaali film he. yah ek drame ke rup main he or film kaa nirdeshak mahrin jabbaar he jabaki nirmaataa javed jabbaar he. is film main nandita das, raashid faruq, faazil hossain, maaria vaasti or nauman ejaz ahm kirdaar nibhaae the. yah film ek bachche kii sachhee kahani par aadhaarit he. yah film bharat main bhi jari hui thi.
 pakistan main rahan vaala hindu dalit shankar apane aath saal ke bete ramchand ke saath anjaan se hind-paak sarhad ko parkar hindustan kii zamin par aa jaataa he. donon deshon ke bich tanaav vaale mahaul ke kaaran shankar or ramchand ko seemaa suraksha bal dwaara giraftaar kar liya jaataa he. bete ramchand or pita shankar ko bhartiya qaidakaane main bandi bana liya jaataa he. shankar kii patni champa apane aap ko akelaa paakar zindagi se jujhane lagati he. apni maan ke bina ramchand bahut duhkhi rahane lagata he.
