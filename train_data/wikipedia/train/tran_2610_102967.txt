 
  arabi: أبو بكر البغدادي)‎
 abu bakr al bagdadi kaa janm 1971 main iraq ke samarra nagar main hua.ali badari samarrai, abu dua, doctor ibrahim, al karrar or abu bakr alabagdadi aadi kai iske upnaam hai. yah piechdi hai. iske pita kaa naam albu badari hai jo salfi takfiri vichaardhaara ko maanate hai. abu bakr bagdadi kii shruwaat dharm ke prachaar saath hi prashikshan se hua. lekin jehadi vichaardhaara kii ore adhik jhukaawarakhane ke kaaran vah iraq ke diyaala or samarra main jehadi prishthibhumi ke kendron main se ek kendra ke rup main ubhraa.
 mana jaataa he, al bagdadi samarra ke nikat iraq main 1971 main paidaa hua hai. usne baghdad ke islamic vigyaan vishvavidhyalay se islamic vigyaan main master kii degree or piechdi arjeet kiya. jehadi websiton kaa daawa he ki vah pratishthit parivaar kaa padhaa-likhaa imam he. vah 2003 main iraq main america ghuspaith ke baad baagi guton ke saath america fouj se ladaa tha. vah pakadaa bhi gaya or 2005-09 ke dauraan use dahnini iraq main merica ke banaae jail camp bukaa main rakhaa gaya tha. or 2010 main iraq ke alqaida kaa netaa ban gaya.
 islamic state of iraq 
 kuch samachar patron ke mutabik duniya kaa khatarnaak aatanki sanghathan islamic state ke mukhya arthath pramukh abu bakr al bagdadi ke maare jane kii kai khabaren chhaapi he. sarvapratham aatanki sanghathan se jude amaak news agency ne daawa kiya he ki syria main america netritv vaale gathbandhan ke hawaii hamale main uski maut ho gai he haalanki isse pehle bhi inke maare jane khabaren prakashit hui thi lekin unhen afvaahen hi bataaya gaya tha. isse pehle isi saal 18 march 2016 ko bhi aisi kai khabaren ai thi kii bagdadi kii maut ho chuki he.
 inke alaava turki or iran kii media ne bhi uski maut kaa daawa thoka he. lekin abhi tak purna rup se pushti nahin hua he ki vastav main abu bakr al bagdadi maaraa gaya ki nahin.
