 sushant singh rajput bhartiya tivi or film abhinayta hai. sarvapratham unhonen kis desh main he meraa dil naamak dharawahik main kaam kiya par unako pahchaan ekta kapur ke dharawahik pavitra rishta se mili. iske baad unhen filmo ke prastaav milanaa shuru hue. film kaay po che! main wo mukhya abhinayta the or unke abhinay kii taareef bhi hui. iske baad wo shuddh desi romance main vaani kapur or pariniti chopra ke saath dikhe. film safal rahi or sushant kaa filmi kariyar paravaan chad gaya. sushant kii aane vaali film 'chanda mamaa dur ke' he.
