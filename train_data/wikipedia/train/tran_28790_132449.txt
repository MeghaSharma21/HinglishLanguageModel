 6 july 1946 ko janme jorge walker bush america ke 43wein raashtrapati the. unhone apana padbhaar 20 janvari san 2001 ko grahan kiya tha. 20 janvari, 2009 ko unhone damocratic party ke nav nirvaachit baraak obama ko satta saunp di. bush ko san 2004 ke raashtrapati ke chunaav main chaar varshon ke liye dobaaraa chun liya gaya tha. 
 raajnneeti main pravesh karne se pehle shri bush ek vyapaaree the. tel or gas kaa utpaadan karne vaali kai companiyon se ve jude rahe the or 1989 se 1998 tak texs rangers besabaal club ke sah maalikon main se ek the. ve san 1995 se san 2000 tak texs rajya ke rajyapal bhi rah chuke hai. unke parivaar ke sabhi sadsy raajnneeti main kaaphi ghanishtha rup se jude huye hai. shri bush ke pita jorge herbat walker bush swayan america ke bhootpurv raashtrapati hai. shri bush ke bade bhai jeb bush florida ke vartmaan rajyapal hai.
