 parivartaneey chaal drive or variable-speed drive ) kaa aashay aise avyav se he jo kisi mashin ke ghoomane ke chaal ko aavashyaktaanusaar badalane evam kisi ek chalpar niyantrit karne aadi ke kaam aata he. mashiinon kii chaal ke niyantran se urja kii bachat hoti he evam inase bane utpaada kii gunvattta behatar hoti he.
 parivartaneey chaal drive purnatah yantric ho sakati he, vidyutchumbakeey ho sakati he, hydralik ho sakati he, yaa electranik ho sakati he. aajkal power electranik parivartansheel chaal drive bahutayat se prayog kii ja rahi he. asi motaron ke liye prayukt parivartaneey aavriti drive, parivartansheel chaal drive kaa ek rup he.
