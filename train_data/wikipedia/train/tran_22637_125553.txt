 tumba shilp- bastar, chhattisgarh kii janajatiyon ke dwaara lowki par garm lohe ke chaaku se kiye gayi paaramparik chitrankan ko tumba shilp kehete he. 
 bharat main krishi ke baad sabase zyada aay kala jagat se praapt hoti he. bharat mai lagbhag 86 lakh gaanv he, jiske har gaanv mai koi-naa-koi shilp prectis kii jaati he, iname se tumba shilp chhattisgarh rajya ke bastar jile ke kai ganvo main adivasee logon dwaara prectis kii jaati he, jo unki aam jindagi main ahm bhumika nibhaati he. bastar kii vanavaasi samskruti main tumba unki jivan yatra kaa humsafar he. aisa mana jaataa he ki tumba jo ek prakaar kii sabji yaa fal hota he, kaa janm arfika main hua tha. pehle lowki kaa istemaal khaane se zyada bartan ke rup main kiya jaataa tha. yah laben aakaar ke saath hi kaddu kii tarah gol aakaar kaa bhi hota he. bady sies kii lowki ko ped par lagbhag ek saal tak pakkane athwa sukhaane ke liye chod diya jaataa he, is dauraan lowki ko chheda nahi jaataa, at: baad mai use todkar uski dhumil upari parat ko lohe ke chaaku kii sahaayta se chhil diya jaataa he or andar kii mulaayam saamagree ko mude hue chaaku kii sahaayta se baahar nikaal diya jaataa he, kyonki yah bahut patali hoti he jo hataane kii prakriya ke dauraan nasht ho sakati he. iske baad ise dhup mai sukhane ke liye rakh diya jaataa he, sukhane par vibhinn prakaar kii parmparik aakrittiyaan ore pattern lowki kii sataha par garm louhe ke chaaku kii sahaayta se taiyaar kiye jate he. ye log kabhi bhi lowki ke prakritik aakaar ko nahi badalte he. parantu ye log lowki par garm lohe ke chaaku se paaramparik chitrankan kar use ek adbhut kalakriti me badal dete he. lowki kii sataha par dizin kaa kaam puraa ho jane ke baad lowki ko naram sabun evam pani se dho kar dhoop me sookhaa diya jaataa he, sookhne ke baad vex panlish kii jaati he jisase kalakriti chamak uthati he.
