 hiku mul rup se japani kavita he. "hiku kaa janm japani samskruti kii parampara, japani janamanas or soundarya chetana main hua or vahiin palaa he. hiku main anek vichaar-dharaayen milti hai- jaise buddha-dharm chini darshan or praachya-samskruti. yah bhi kaha ja saktaa he ki ek "hiku" main in sab vichaar-dhaaraon kii jhaanki mil jaati he yaa "hiku" in sabakaa darpan he."hiku ko kavy vidha ke rup main basho ne pratishtha pradaan kii. hiku matsuo basho ke haathon sanbarkar 17 veen shatabdi main jivan ke darshan se jud kar japani kavita kii yugadhara ke rup main prasphutit hua. aaj hiku japani sahitya kii simaaon ko laanghakar vishwa sahitya kii nidhi ban chukaa he.
