 jab kiye jane vaale kaamon ko baar-baar baad main karne ke liye choda jaataa he to us vyavhaar ko dirghsutrata yaa 'kaam taalna' kehete hai. manovaigyanikon kaa vichaar he ki dirghsutrata, kaam ko shuru karne yaa use samaapt karne yaa nirnay lene se judi hui chintaa se ladane kaa ek tarika he. kisi vyavhaar ko dirghsutrata kehane ke liye usamen teen visheshtaayen honi chaiye - yah vyavhaar utpaadanvirohi ho ; anaavashyak ho or deree karne vaala ho.
