 khartoum africa ke sudan kii rajdhani he. yah uttar se victoria jhil se aati shwet nil evam pashchim main ithiopia se aati blue nil ke sangam par sthit he. donon nadiyon ke sangam ko al-morgan kehete hai. mukhya nadi nil apni yatra yahaan se uttaravarti hokar misr or antatah bumadhya sagar ko karti he. 
 nil nadi dwaara teen bhaagon main vibhajit hone se khartoum shahar ek trikoniya mahanagar banataa he, jisaki anumaanit jansankhya das lakh se adhik he. ye teen bhaag uttari khartoum evam omadarman se pashchim main sethuon dwaara jude hai.
