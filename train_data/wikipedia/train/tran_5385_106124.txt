 golodanaya stapi, jise uzbek bhashaa main mirzachol yaa mirzachol saharaa kehete hai, uzbekistan main sir dariyaa ke bahine kinare par laga hua 10,000 varg kimi kaa ek loyas maidan he. yah ek shushk stapi kshetra he jo 19veen sadi tak registani ilaaka hua karata tha lekin 1950 or 1960 ke dashakon main soviet sangh kii sarkaar dwaara nahar sinchai viksit karne se uzbekistan main kapaas or ann kaa ek mukhya strot ban chukaa he. sirdariyaa praant ke guliston or yangiyer shahar golodanaya stapi ke mukhya aabaadi ke centre hai.
 'golodanaya stapi' shabd kaa roosi bhashaa main matlab 'bhukhi stapi' he. yah naam isliye rakhaa gaya kyonki yah puraa ilaaka shushk or sookhe se prabhavit rahata tha. iskaa doosraa naam 'mirzachol' uzbek bhashaa ke do shabdon se bana he - 'mirza' or 'chol' - or iskaa arth 'registan kaa kinaaraa' he. dhyaan den ki bhartiya upmahadweep main bhi pakistani punjab praant main 'cholistaan' naam kaa registan he jo thar marusthal kaa hissaa he.
 golodanaya stapi uttar main tajikistan kii sarhad par sthit aitihaasik faraghna vaadi ke mukh se lekar purv main sirdariyaa praant tak or pashchim main jizaakh praant ke uttari bhaag tak vistrut he. dakshin main tiyaan shaan parvaton kii turkistan shrunkhlaa isaki seemaa he. bhugolic nazariye se yah kizil kum registan kaa ek dakshin-purvi failaav mana jaataa he. july main yahan kaa ausat taapmaana 28 centegrade or janvari main âˆ’2 centegrade hota he.
 yah kshetra ek vishaal maidani ilaaka he or iske sameep se sir dariyaa guzarataa he isliye roosi samrajya ke jamaane se hi 1870 ke baad yahan sinchai par jor diya gaya. aashaa thi ki agar yah ilaaka upjau ban jaae to bahut aarthik laabh hoga. yahan kii zamin namakeen thi, jisamen fasal ugaana bahut mushkil hota he. is vajah se ise upjau banane main or bhi pani lagata he kyonki pehle mitti main se namak dhulkar bahaane kii zaroorat hoti he. is kshetra se teen mukhya nahare nikaalane ke baad yahan kii lagbhag 5,00,000 hectares dharti par krishi hoti he.
