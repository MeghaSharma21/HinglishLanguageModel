 burmese, ek kaale rang kaa aaarasiempi polis seva kii ghodi thi jise royal canadian mounted police ne maharani elizabeth ko uphaar swaroop diya tha. maharani ko yah ghodi bahut priy thi or unhone 1969 se 1986 tak lagaataar 18 varshon tak truping da color samarohon main isaki sawari kii.
 burmese kaa janm royal canadian mounted police ke walsh kila, sascatchevan main sthit pashu form main hua tha. use otawa main prashikshit kiya gaya va 1969 main maharani ko tab bhent kiya gaya jab police bal ke jawan euke main yojit royal windsar horse sho pratiyogita main bhaag lene aaye the.
 maharani burmese kii hi sawari kar raheen thim jab marcus sargent ne 1981 main unke janmdiwas parade main truping da color samaaroh main jate hue unpar 6 goliyaan chalaayi thim. ghodi goliyon ke chalane se ugr ho gayi lekin maharani ne use sambhaal liya tha.
 burmese antim baar janata ke saamne 1986 ke truping da color samaaroh ke dauraan saamne ai or uske baad wo ritair ho gayi. uske jagah phir kisi or ghode ne nahin lee kyonki rani uske baad kisi nae jangi ghode ko prashikshan dene kii bajaae khule ekke main chalate hue dur machaan se parade kii nigaraanee karti thim. 
 burmese ko windsar kile ke charaagaah main 1990 tak rakhaa gaya jab uski mrityu ho gayi. rani ne sascatchevan, reagan, canada main burmese par sawari karte hue banaai gayi apni ek kaanse kii murti kaa 2005 main anaavaran kiya.
 burmese or maharani ko bhent kiye gayi ek anya ghode centanniel ko aaarasiempi ke kaaryakarta sarjent fred rasamusen ne prashikshit kiya tha.
 aar si em pi ke ek pravaktaa ke anusaar burmese naam aadhunik burma vaasiyon ke naam par he. burma vaasiyon ko angreji main burmese kehete hai. haalaanki aisa naam die jane ke peeche ke kaaran kaa zikr nahin kiya gaya he.
