 tosham haryana praant kaa ek shahar he.
 
 
 tosham ek dharm nagari he. yah mandir or pahaado ke kaaran ek alag pahchaan rakhata he. yahan baba mugipa ji kaa mandir he.
 tosham kaa itihaas bada hi sundar he. bataaya jaataa he ki pandav bhi yahan aaye the. mana jaataa he ki pahaad par 21 kund hai. yah pahaad haryana kaa sabase bada or unchaa he. ab tosham ko adarsh gaav bana diya gaya ha. yaha se kuch hi dur khanak naam kaa ek gaav ha. vah bhi kaaphi pahad ha. ore baba mugipa bhagwaan kaa mandir ha. khanak me bahot se karessur he. jiname rody, reti, aadi milti he. yah se dilhi ore lagbhag sampurna haryana me bheji jaati he. tosham kaa rajanitik tor par bhi kaaphi mahatvapoorn sthaan he.
