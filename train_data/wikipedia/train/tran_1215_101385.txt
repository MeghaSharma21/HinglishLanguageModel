 uttari maale ke ek chote se dweep vihamanafushi par sthit kurumba maldive ek 4.5 sitara hotel resort he. universal resorts dwaara prabandhit kurumba maldive, maldive kaa pehla resort he. iskaa vartmaan dizine vaastukar mohammad shafiq ne kiya tha.
 yah resort 1972 me kholaa gaya tha or sthaneey maldivi nivasee mohammad umr maniku or husen aafeef iske sansthaapakon me shaamil the. shuru me is resort kurumba maldive - kurumba kaa sthaneey dahaevi bhashaa me shabdic arth nariyal hota he- me 30 kamare the jinka nirmaan sthaneey taur par praapt utpaado se kiya gaya tha. isaki deevaaren muunga pathar or chat tad ke chappad ke bane the.
 yah resort hawaii adde se sirf 10 minute kii duuri par he or maldive me utarane kii kuch minute ke bheetar hi isaki suvidhaon kaa anand le sakate hai.yah resort kurumba bich se kuch hi duuri par bana he or yahan se bananaa rif dive site bhi kaafii nazadik he.phul moon bich or kuda bandos bich bhi kuch hi kilo meter kii duuri par hai.
 vartmaan me is resort me 180 vaataanukoolit kamare hai jiname frij or minibaar jaisi suvidhaayen uplabdh he. is resort kii khaas baat yah he ki iske sabhi kamaron me balakani bani hui he jahaan se samudr kaa nazaaraa saaf dikhataa he. puure resort me internet or vaai faai kii suvidha muft me uplabdh he.
 is resort me kanference kii suvidha ke liye 5608 varg meter me bana hua ek kanference centre bhi he. aagantukon kii suvidha ke liye hawaii adde tak aane-jane ke liye shatal seva bhi uplabdh karai jaati he.  
 is resort ko mili puraskaar or sammaanon kii suchi kaafii lambii he. isame se kuch is prakaar hai :-
 kurumba maldive edvokating da ritis of children naam kii ek gaiyr-sarkari, bina munaafe vaali sanstha ko pryojit karata he jo ki maldive me bacchon ke adhikaron ko badhaava detii he.
