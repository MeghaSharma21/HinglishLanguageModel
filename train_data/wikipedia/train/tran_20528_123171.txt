 
 kapler-69 prithvi se 2,700 prakaash-varsh kii duuri par akaash main hans taramandal ke kshetra main sthit ek hamaare suraj ke jaisaa G-shrenee kaa mukhya anukram tara he. 18 april 2013 ko vaigyanikon ne iske ird-gird do grahon kii maujuudagi gyaat hone kii ghoshnaa kari. shuru main unka anumaan tha ki inamen se ek 'kapler-69si' dwaara namankit grah is grahiy mandal ke vaasayogy kshetra main sthit he lekin baad main yah spasht hua ki yah vaas-yogya kshetra se andar he. iskaa arth tha ki yah hamaare saura mandal ke shukr jaisi paristhitiyaan rakhata hoga or yahan jivan kaa punap paanaa bahut kathin he.
 kapler-69 G-shrenee kaa tara he. iskaa dravyaman hamaare suraj kaa 81% or vyas hamaare suraj ke vyas kaa 93% he. iskaa satahi taapmaana 5638 calvin he jo hamaare suraj ke 5778 calvin ke satahi taapmaana ke aasapaas hi he. prithvi se isaki chamak lagbhag +13.7 magnitude maapi gai he jo itani dhundhlee he ki bina doorabeen ke nahin dekhi ja sakati.
