 4G, chauthi peedi kaa sanchhipt rup he, jo mobile phone wirelas seva kii chauthi peedi he. isse pichhali 2ji or 3ji peediyaan thim. thri ji takanic main uplabdh orthogonal frequency divijan multiple acces kii sahaayta se vartmaan network kii suvidha ko or behatar banaaya ja sakegaa. four-ji yani chauthi janareshan arthath chauthi peedi puri tarah se ypi aadhaarit seva hogi. isamen dhwani, paath or multimedia ko samaan gati se bheja or praapt kiya ja sakegaa.
 thri ji mobile main ve sab suvidhaayen uplabdh hai jo vartmaan mobile takanic ke liye aavashyak hai, four ji kii gati 100 embipies hogi jo thri ji ke mukaable 50 guna adhik hogi. thri ji wireles network main 384 kebipies se 2 embipies kii gati se hi data bheja ja saktaa tha. in laabh ke saath saath hi is takanic kii keemat bhi thri ji ke mukaable kaaphi kam hogi. thri ji ke mukaable 4ji kaa data rate adhik he yani data kaa sthanantaran tej gati se kiya ja sakegaa. thri ji takanic jahaan wide aria network par kaam karti he, vahaan 4ji local aria network or base stesan wide aria network par kaam karti he.
