 marxvaadi vichaardhaara kaa sahitya main pragativaad ke rup main udai hua. yah samaaj ko shoshak or shoshit ke rup main dekhtaa he. pragativaadee shoshak varg ke khilaaf shoshit varg main chetana laane tathaa use sangathit kar shoshan mukt samaaj kii sthapana kii koshishon kaa samarthan karata he. yah poonjeewaad, samantvaad, dharmik sansthaaon ko shoshak ke rup main chinhit kar unhen ukhaad phenkane kii baat karata he.
 hindi sahitya main pragativaad kaa aarambh 1936 se mana ja saktaa he. isi varsh lakhanu main pragatisheel lekhak sangh kaa pehla sammelan hua jisaki adhyakshata premchand ne kii. iske baad sahitya kii vibhinn vidhaon main marxvaadi vichaardhaara se prabhavit rachanaayen hui. pragativaadee dhara ke sahityakaron main nagarjun, kedarnath agarwal, ramvilas sharma, namvar singh, aadi pramukh hai.
