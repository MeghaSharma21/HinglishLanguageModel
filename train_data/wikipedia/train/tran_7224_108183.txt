 goguryeo ek pracheen korian rajya tha jo aadhunik korea, dakshini manchuriya or rus ke premorski kroy praant ke dakshini bhaag par failaa hua tha. yah 37 isapurv se 668 isvi tak astitv main rahaa. bekaje or silla ke saath yah 'pracheen korea ke teen rajyon' main se ek tha. korian prayadweep main rajnatik prabhaav ke saath-saath goguryeo apane pados ke china or japan ke kshetron main bhi dabil detaa tha.
