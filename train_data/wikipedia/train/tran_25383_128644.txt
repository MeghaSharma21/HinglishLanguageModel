 paul betni ek british abhinayta hai, jinhen maarval cinemetic univers kii filmon main jarvis tathaa vijan kii bhoomika nibhaane ke liye janaa jaataa he. betni ko sabase pehle 2000 kii british film gangster nan. 1 tathaa 2001 kii bryan helegeland dwaara nirdeshit film aa nits tail se pahchaan mili, or uske baad unhone vibhinn prakaar kii filmon main kaam kiya, jinamein a beautiphul mind, master and commander: da for said of da world, dogvile, wimbledon tathaa da vinci code pramukh hai.
 betni ko kai puraskaron ke liye namankit kiya ja chukaa he, jinamein sarvshereshth sahaayak abhinayta kaa bft puraskaar, tathaa 'outstnding parformens bai a cast in a moshan pikchar' kaa screen actors gild puraskaar pramukh hai. 2003 se vah america abhinetri jenifer conly se vivaahit hai, tathaa unke 2 bachche hai. unki sabase safal filmein da avangers, avangers: age of ultron, aayran main 3, captain america: civil war tathaa da vinci code hai.
