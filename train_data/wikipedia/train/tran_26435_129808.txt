 anwar masood ek vikhyath pakistani kavi hai jo adhiktar vyangy kavita likhtein hai, haalanki unki or bhi vishyon par rachanaayen hai. ve punjabi, urdu or farsi bhashaaon main likhte hai.
 anwar masood kaa janm 8 navambar 1935 ko punjab praant ke gujarat shahar main hua . unke janm ke baad, 1941 main, unka parivaar lahore main ja basaa or unhone vahaan apni praathmik padhai puri kii. 1947 main ve phir gujarat waapas aa gaye or vahaan ke public skool main shesh vidhyalay kii kakshaen purna kin. uske uparant unhone gujarat main hi sthit zamindara kalege se bie uteern kii or paas hi ke shahar kunja main sthit sarkari islamia haai skool main padhaanaa armbh kar diya. kuch samay baad unhone 1961 main lahore ke oriental kalege se farsi bhashaa kaa eme swarna-padak ke saath puraa kiya. 1962 se 1966 tak ve pakistan ke vibhinn kalejon main padhaate rahe hai. ve apni punjabi kii vyangy kavitaaon ke liye jane jate hai jo kii bharat-paak seemaa ke donon taraf ke punjab main atyant lokapriy huin hai.
