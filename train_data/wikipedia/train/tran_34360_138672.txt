 albert i richardsan tha ek ghadisaz eshton-under-lin se, lankashair, jisane pehla banaaya vyaavhaarik tisamide.
 unhone dizine kiye pehla vyaavhaarik tisamide jo ek alarm ghadi, ek bhaavana deepak, or ek dhone kettle par aadhaarit tha. unhone dizine bech diya ek bandook banaanevaala frank clark ko jo birmingham se tha, jisane 1902 main dizine ko ekasv kiya or naam diya "ek upkaran jo ek chaay yaa coffee kaa cap swachaalit bana sakate hai."
