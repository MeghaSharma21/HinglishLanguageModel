 subhash kapur ek bhartiya film nirdeshak or film nirmaataa he. inke alaava ye pehle ek raajnneetik patrkaar bhi the. ye jolly elelbi or funs gaye re obama jaisi filmon ke liye jane jate hai.
 subhash kapur be apni hindi sahitya main master of arts kii padhai karne ke baad 1990 main raajnneetik patrkaar ke taur par apana karier shuru kiya. baad inhone kuch commercial laghu filmein bhi banaai or 2006 main dilli se mumbai main aa gaye.
