 asthi vigyaan, asthiyon yaa haddiyon ke vaigyanik adhyayan ko kehete he. yah nrivigyaan or puraatatv kii ek upashakha he. asthi vigyaan ke antargat haddiyon kaa vistrut adhyayan kiya jaataa he jisamen, asthi sanrachana, kankaleey tatv, daant, akruti vigyaan, asthiyon ke kaary, rog, vikrutivigyaan, asthikaran kii prakriya, haddiyon kaa pratirodh or kathorta shaamil hai. aksar jaev saanskrithik sandarbhon main vaigyanikon dwaara asthi vigyaan kaa prayog, maanav avasheshon kii aayu, ling, mrityu, vruddhi or vikaas ke nirdhaaran ke liye kiya jaataa he.
