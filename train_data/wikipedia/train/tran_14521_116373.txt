 juel uraanv bharat ke vartmaan janajaatiy maamle ke kendriya mantri hai. juel uraanv kaa janm 22 march 1961 ko odisha ke sundargarh main hua tha. juel ne 16veen loksabhaa main sundargarh sansadeey kshetra se biju janata dal ke dilip tirki ko kadi shikast di.
 sundargarh sansadeey kshetra se lagaataar teen baar 12veen, 13veen or 14veen loksabhaa main chunaav jeetne vaale juel pichley chunaav main congress pratyaashee va purv mukhyamantri hemanand se parajeet ho gaye the.
 juel bharat sarkaar main purv cabinet mantri bhi rah chuke hai. vah bhartiya janata party ke upaadhyaksh hai. vah oreisha main bhaajapa ke anbhavi netaaon main se ek he. vah oreisha main bhaajapa ke adhyaksh bhi rah chuke hai.
