 jamaal hindi ke kavi the.
 'shivsinghsaroj' main inhen jamaluddin, pihaani nivasee or san. 1625 main upasthit kaha gaya he. acharya ramchandra shukla ne jamaal ko musalmaan kavi or unka rachana-kaal sanwat 1627 anumaanath: mana he. vishwanathprasad mishra ne inke vishay main ek dantakatha kaa ullekh kiya he jo unhone prasiddh kavi dinadayal giri ke prashishy chunnilaal se suni thi. uske anusaar jamaal sukavi abdurrahim khaankhaana ke putr the. vilaas main duube rahane ke kaaran jamaal ant:poor se baahar bahut kam hi nikalate the. pita rahim ko yah bura lagata tha. putr ko bhoga se dur khinchne or usamen kavyrachanashkti jagaane ke liye rahim ne pratidin rangmahal ke dwaar par ek kuta doha likhavane kaa upaay kiya. jamaal nity us dohe ko padhate, der tak uskaa abhipray samajhate or pratyuttar main ek anya doha usee dwaar par ankit kar dete the. prashnottar rup main is dohakan kaa shubh parinaam yah hua ki jamaal bhoga se bhagkar kavyrachana main lag gaye. is kapolkatha se itnaa pataa lag jaataa he ki jamaal samrat akbar ke samay main avashya vidyaman the.
 ab tak jamaal ke paune chaar sau ke lagbhag footkar dohe or katipay chappay hi praapt ho sake hai. vaise 'jamaal pachisi' or 'bhaktamaal kii tippanee' inke do or granth kahe jate hai. inhone pramukh rup se kuta dohon kii hi rachana kii he jinka pradhan vishay shringaar he. inaki sampurna rachanaayen prem, niti or krishna-katha se sambandhit hai. inhone 'chitrakavy' kii rachana main vishisht prakaar kii vichitrataa dikhaai he. bhavvyanjana kii sahaj maarmikata, shabdakrida kii nipunata or kuta kavyrachana kii praveenta inamen thi.
