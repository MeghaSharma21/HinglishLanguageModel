 bharat kii janaganana 2011, janaganana ayukta si. chandramauli dwaara raashtra ko samarpit bharat kii 15veen rashtriya janaganana he, jo 1 may 2010 ko armbh hui thi. bharat main janaganana 1872 se kii jaati rahi he or yah pehali baar he jab biometric jaankaree ekatrit kii gai. janaganana ko do charnon main puraa kiya gaya. antim jari prativedan ke anusaar, bharat kii jansankhya 2001-2011 dashak ke dauraan 18,14,55,986 se badhkar 1,21,08,54,977 ho gai he or, bharat ne jansankhya ke maamle main apane duusare sthaan ko banaae rakhaa he. is dauraan desh kii sakshartaa dar bhi 64.83% se badhkar 74.04% ho gai he.
 2011 kii janaganana ke liye kul 27 lakh adhikariyon ne 7,000 nagaron/kasbon or 6,00,000 gaanvon ke pariwaron ke yahan padhaar kar aankade jutaaye jisamen logon ko ling, dharm, shiksha-str or vyavsaay ityadi main vargeekrut kiya gaya. is kaam main kul 22 arab rupye kharch kiye gaye. prati das varshon main honi vaali is janaganana main desh ke vishaal aakaar or saanskrithik vividhta ke atirikt bhi bahut si chunautiyaan bhi hoti hai.
 janaganana main kisi vyakti kii jati se sambandhit soochna kaa samavesh, sattarudh gathbandhan ke kai bade netaaon jaise ki lalu prasad yadav, sharad yadav, mulaayam singh yadav or mayawati jaise netaaon kii joardaar maang par kiya gaya. isi maang kaa samarthan vipakshi partiyon jaise ki bhartiya janata party, akali dal, shivasena or anna dravid munetra kashgam dalon dwaara bhi kiya gaya. jati sambandhi soochna kaa samavesh pichhali baar british raaj ke dauraan hui 1931 kii janaganana main kiya gaya tha. shuruaati janagananaaon ke dauraan, log aksar samaaj main khud ko unche tabake kaa dikhaane ke liye apni jati ko badha chadha kar pesh kiya karte the, par is baar lagata he ki log sarkari laabh paane ke ummid main apni jati ko nimna bataane kii cheshtaa karen.
 swatantra bharat main jati-gananaa kaa sirf ek udhaaharan miltaa he. kerala main 1968 main i.em.es. nambudiripad kii communist sarkaar ke dwaara vibhinn nichali jatiyon ke saamaajik or aarthik pichhadepan ke akalan ke liye jati-gananaa kii gayi thi. is janaganana ko 1968 kaa saamaajik-aarthik surveykshan kaha gaya tha or iske parinaam kerala ke 1971 ke rajptr main prakashit kiye gaye the.
 is janaganana main teen prashnawaliyaan thim, makanasoocheekaran, rashtriya jansankhya register or parivaar-ikaiyaan.
 makanasoocheekaran anusuchee main 35 prashn the.
 parivaar anusuchee main 29 prashn hai.
 janaganana se anantim aankadon ko 31 march 2011 ko jari kiya gaya. sampoorn report ke varsh 2012 main jari kiye jane kii ummid he. jansankhya kaa kul ling anupaat 2011 main prayek 1,000 purushon ke liye 943 mahilaon kii he. bharat main transgender kii aadhikarik sankhya 4.9 lakh he.
 hinduon kii jansankhya 79.8% he. musalamaanon kii jansankhya 14.2% he jo kii pichley dasak 11% thi. agast 2011 main bharat kii janaganana ke aankadon ko jari kiya gaya tha. isamen pataa chalaa he ki 2,870,000 logon ne apni pratikriya main koi dharm nahin bataaya, desh kii jansankhya kaa lagbhag 0.27%. haalanki, sankhya main naastik, tarksangatvaad or un logon ko shaamil kiya gaya jo ucch shakti main vishwaas karte the. "anya" vikalp naaballig yaa adivasee dharmon ke saath-saath naastik or agyeyavaad ke liye bhi tha.
 hindi bharat ke uttari hisson main sabase vyapak boli jane vaali bhashaa he. bhartiya janaganana "hindi" kii vyapak vividhta ke rup main "hindi" kii vyapak sambhav paribhaasha leti he. 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar, 43.63% bhartiya logon ne hindi ko apni mul bhashaa yaa maatrubhaasha ghoshit kar diya he. bhashaa data 26 june 2018 ko jari kiya gaya tha. bhili / bhilodi 1.04 karod vaktaaon ke saath sabase zyada boli jane vaali anusuchit bhashaa thi, iske baad gondi 29 lakh vaktaaon ke saath thim. 2011 kii janaganana main bharat kii aabaadi kaa 96.71% 22 anusuchit bhashaaon main se ek apni maatrubhaasha ke rup main bolta he.dwibhashivaad or tribhaashivaad par 2011 kii janaganana report, jo praathamikta ke kram main do bhashaaon par data pradaan karti he jisamen ek vyakti maatrubhaasha ke alaava anya main kushal he, sitambar 2018 main jari kiya gaya tha. bharat main dwibhashi vaktaaon kii sankhya 31.49 karod he, jo 2011 main jansankhya kaa 26% he.
