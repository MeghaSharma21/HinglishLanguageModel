 footbal club steo bucuresti bucharesti main sthit ek romanian peshewar footbal club he. ve europea pratiyogitaaon main ke rup main acchhee tarah romanian lig main sabase safal romanian footbal team hai. ve final 1986 europea cap main vijayi hone ke naate, europea cap jeetne vaale pehle purvi europea team ban gai. steo 1989 europea cap ke final main phir se khelaa. 
 club aitihaasik romanian army sports club ke rup main janaa jaataa he. footbal vibhaag 1998 main, tathaapi, alag. us pal main, sena ke liye apane hi sidhaa link rashtriya raksha mantralay kii sampatti thi, jo apane puraane ghar jameen thi.
 steo desh ke sheersh lig main apane sabhi itihaas kharch kiya gaya he or keval paanch baar ke liye chathe sthaan neeche samaapt ho gaya he. prarambh main, club romanian tirange ke rang main khelaa - pile or laal, nile, - lekin pile jald hi iske mahatv kho diya he or team ke laal or nile rang ke saath jud gaya. haal hi main, dur kit phir se ekikrut pile rang ke liye shuru kar diya he.
 club, dinaamo bucuresti saath ek lambey samay se pratidwandvita he, donon ke bich maichon main aamtaur par "anant darbi" yaa "romanian darbi" ke rup main bheja ja rahaa he.
