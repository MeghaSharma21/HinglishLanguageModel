 sweekaarpatr arya samaaj ke sansthaapak swami dayanand saraswati dwaara rachit ek laghu pustika he. yah multah rajkeey mudra patr par likhit ek vasietname kii tarah he jisamen unhone apane deh tyaagane ke baad apana kaam aage badhaane ke liye paropkaarini sabha kaa varnan kiya he.
 is patrak main 13 sakshiyon ke aarambh main hi hastakshar he. iske baad paropkaarini sabha ke 23 prastaavit padaadhikariyon va sabhaasadon ke naam hai. inamen pramukh hai mahadev govind rande tathaa lahore, danapur, bombay, puna, farrukhabad va kanpur ke aryasamaajon ke tatkaaleen padaadhikari. tatkaaleen udaipur naresh sajjansingh ji paropkaarini sabha ke pradhan niyukt kiye gaye.
 yah pustika swami dayanand ke dehavsaan ke kuch samay purv hi prakashit hui thi. isamen unhone paropkaarini sabha ke kaaryakalapon ke alaava yah bhi likhaa he ki unka antim sanskaar vedokt reeti se kaise kiya jaae.
 ullekhneey he ki paropkaarini sabha ke niyamon main yah bhi ullekh he ki yadi sabha main koi jhagada ho to aapas main sulah karen kintu kachhari na jaaen to kachhari na jaaen, kintu yadi na sulajhe to tatkaaleen raja se salaah lee jaae. mukhyatah angrezon kii banaai kachhari dwaara hastkshep se bachane ke liye aisa kiya gaya hoga. antim niyam main yah bhi likhaa he ki yadi aavashyaktaa pade to sabhasad in niyamon main baad main parivartan bhi laa sakate hai.
