 sangheey shasit kabyli ilaaka pakistan kaa arddh swytt praapt kabyli kshetra tha. yah 1947 se 2018 tak astitv main rahaa. iskaa vilay kaibar pakhtuunakva main kar diya gaya. is kshetra main lagbhag sabhi pathan hai, jo khaybar pakhtunakhvaa or uttari baluchistan ke padosi praanton main bhi rahete hai or seemaa paar afghanistan main faile hue hai. ve zyaadaatar musalmaan hai. 2001 main sanyukt rajya america main 9/11 ke hamalon ke baad, janajaatiy kshetra ugravad or aatankwaad kaa ek pramukh ilaaka rahaa he. 2001 se pakistan sena ne taliban ke khilaaf 10 abhiyaan shuru kiye, jisamen uttar wajiristaan main oparation zarb-e-azb shaamil he.
