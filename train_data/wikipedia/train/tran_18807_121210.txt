 barkatullah vishvavidhyalay madhyapradesh kaa ek pramukh vishvavidhyalay he, jo bhopal main sthit he. bhopal ke swatantraaa senaanee pro barkatullah ke naam par is vishvavidhyalay kaa namakaran kiya gaya tha. rajya sarkaar dwaara vittposhit is vishvavidhyalay se bhopal ke atirikt saat anya jilon sihor, vidisha, raisen, harda, hoshangabad, rajgad or betul ke college sambhddha hai. isaki sthapana 1970 main kii gai thi or iskaa namakaran 1988 main kiya gaya.
