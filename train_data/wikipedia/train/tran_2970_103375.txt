 avadhi hindi kshetra kii ek upbhasha he. yah uttar pradesh me avadhi kshetra lakhanu, hardoi, sitapur, lakhimpur, faijabad, pratapgarh, sultanpur, ilahabaad tathaa fatehpur, meerajapur, jonpur aadi kuch anya jilon main bhi boli jaati he. avadhi bhashaa kii kahavate uttar pradesh ke avadh kshetra ke gramin lok jivan main atyadhik prachlit hai. 
 kahawat aam bolchaal main istemaal hone vaale us vaakyansh ko kehete hai, jiskaa sambandh kisi na kisi kahani yaa puranic kathao se judaa hua hota he. kahin kahin ise muhaavara athwa lokokti ke rup main bhi jaanate hai. praayah ye kahavate ek bhashaa ke kahawaton ko anya bhashaaon ke dwaara mul yaa badale huye rup main apana bhi liya jaataa he.
