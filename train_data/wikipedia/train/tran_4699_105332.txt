 mairi scladowka curi  vikhyath bhautikavid or rasayanashastri thi. meri ne radium kii khoj kii thi. vigyaan kii do shakhaon main nobel puraskaar se sammanit hone vaali vah pehali vaigyanik hai. vaigyanik maa kii donon betiyon ne bhi nobel puraskaar praapt kiya. bady beti irine ko 1935 main rasayan vigyaan main nobel puraskaar praapt hua to choti beti eve ko 1965 main shanti ke liye nobel puraskaar mila.
 meri kaa janm poland ke warsaw nagar main hua tha. mahila hone ke kaaran tatkaaleen warsaw main unhen simit shiksha kii hi anumati thi. isliye unhen chup-chhupaakar ucch shiksha praapt kerani padi. baad main badi bahan kii aarthik sahaayta kii badaulat vah bhautikee or ganit kii padhai ke liye paris ain. unhone frans main doctoret puraa karne vaali pehali mahila hone kaa gaurav paaya. unhen parisvishvavidhyalay main profesar banane vaali pehali mahila hone kaa gaurav bhi mila. yaheen unki mulaaqaat piyare curi se hui jo unke pati bane. is vaigyanik dampatti ne 1898 main polonium kii mahatvapurn khoj kii. kuch hi maheene baad unhone radium kii khoj bhi kii. chikitsa vigyaan or rogon ke upachaar main yah ek mahatvapurn krantikari khoj sabit hui. 1903 main meri curi ne pi-ech.di. puri kar lee. isi varsh is dampatti ko radioactivity kii khoj ke liye bhautikee kaa nobel puraskaar mila. 1911 main unhen rasayan vigyaan ke kshetra main radium ke shuddhikaran ke liye rasayanashastra kaa nobel puraskaar bhi mila. vigyaan kii do shakhaon main nobel puraskaar se sammanit hone vaali vah pehali vaigyanik hai. vaigyanik maa kii donon betiyon ne bhi nobel puraskaar praapt kiya. bady beti irine ko 1935 main rasayan vigyaan main nobel puraskaar praapt hua to choti beti eve ko 1965 main shanti ke liye nobel puraskaar mila.
