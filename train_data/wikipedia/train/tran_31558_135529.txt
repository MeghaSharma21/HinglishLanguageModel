 chandigarh air force stesan bhartiya vayusena kaa ek ati mahatvapurn base he. pakistan va china donon kii simaaon se lagbhag baraabar duuri main sthit air force ke is stesan main "12 wing" or "3 base repere depo" hai.
 
 12 wing, eeff vayusena main bahut mahatvapurn transport base he. uttari himalayan kshetra ke liye to ye ek jivan rekha he. 12 wing suraksha kii drushti se ek behad samvedansheel ilaake main mausam kii chunauteepurn sthitiyon ke bich kaary karata he. aise mushkil halaaton main ye wing uttari himalayan kshetra main sabhi oparations kaa kushaltapurvak sanchaalan karata he. 
 3 biardi eeff arthath 3 base repere depo, air force helicopteron kii repering or inke rakh-rakhaav kaa kaary karti he jinamein emai-8, emai-17, emai 17 1ve or emai-17 ve5 shaamil hai.
