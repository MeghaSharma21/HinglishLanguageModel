 saanchaa:History of South AsiaBArawa ganatantr kaa itihaas 26 janvari 1950 ko shuru hota he.
 raashtra ko dharmik hinsa, jatvaad, naksalwad, aatankwaad or visheshkar jammu or kashmir tathaa uttarpurvi bharat main, kshetreey algaavvaadi virdrohon kaa saamana karnaa padaa.
 bharat ek parmaanu-hathiyaar sampann rajya he; isane pehla parmaanu pareekshan 1974 main kiya, va 1998 main baad main or paanch pareekshan kiyen. 1950 ke dashak ke lekar 1980 ke dashak tak, bharat ne samaajawaadee-prerit nitiyon kaa anusaran kiya. arthvyavastha license raaj, sangrakshanvaad or saarvajic swaamitv se bandhi hui thi, jiskaa parinamswaroop bhrashtachar or dhimaa aarthik vikaas the. 1991 main shuru huen nav-udaar aarthik sudhaaron ne bharat ko vishwa main teesri sabase badi or sabase tej viksit hoti arthvyavastha main parivartit kar diya. aaj, bharat, vaishvik maamalon main ek pramukh awaz ke saath, ek pramukh vishwa shakti hai or sanyukt raashtra suraksha parishad main sthaayee seat hetu prayasarat hai. anek arthshaastriyon, sainya vishleshkon or buddhijiviyon kii yah apeksha hai ki bharat nikat bhavishya main ek mahashakti banegaa.
