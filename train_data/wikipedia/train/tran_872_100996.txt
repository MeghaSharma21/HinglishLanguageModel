 chandigarh rajya aids niyantran socity centre-shasit kshetra chandigarh main aids niyantran, jaagran tathaa rakt bainkon kii dekharekh kaa kaam karti he. chandigarh main 10.54 lakh kii aabaadi he. jansankhya kaa ghanatv prati skavair kilometre 9252 vyakti he. jansankhya main ling-anupaat prati hajaar purushon main 818 mahilaon kaa he. shahari kshetron main rahanewaalon kii jansankhya kaa pratishat 89.8% he or gramin aabaadi 10.2% he. kshetra main sakshartaa kii dar 81.9% he. chandigarh main yuvaaon main echivi prasar 2003 main 0.5% tha parantu 2006 main kami ai he or tab se yah 0.25% bana rahaa he.
 bhartiya udyog parisangh ne abhinetri gul panaag ko 2004 main apane aids jaagarukta abhiyaan kaa ' sadbhaavna rajdoot ' niyukt kiya he. parisangh kii vijyapti ke anusaar is pariyojna ko chandigarh rajya aids niyantran socity evam swasthya mantralay ke sahyog se lagu kiya gaya tha. is pariyojna ke tahat chandigarh ke logon visheshkar audyogic shramikon ko aids sambandhi vishyon par jaagruk kiya gaya tha. siaiai is abhiyaan ko puure uttar kshetra main chalaaya gaya tha.
 vishwa aids diwas par chandigarh main rakt ekatra karne ke liye ek mobile vine seva uplabdh kii gai he jiske saath hi rajya aids niyantran socity do divasiy sammelan yojit karke logon ko is bare main jaagruk karti he. in sammelnon ke pehle din rakt suraksha, jaanapadik rog vigyaan or nigaraanee, sahaayta, dekhbhaal, upachaar or saamaajik- medico kanoonee muddon se sambandhit muddon par vichaar- vimarsh kiya jaataa he.
 rashtriya aids niyantran sanghathan ne chandigarh ko blad transfusion sevaaon ke liye sarvshereshth bataaya he. is shahar ko yahaan ke svaichhik raktadan abhiyaanon ke liye janaa jaataa he. shahar main chaar licensadhari blad bank hai, jo svaichhik daan dwaara 80 pratishat rakt sangrahit karte hai. chandigarh main pratidin 115 unit rakt sangrahit kiya jaataa he.
