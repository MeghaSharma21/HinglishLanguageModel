 shilavart yaa pathar kaa gola aise puraatatv sthal ko kehete hai jisamen kai pattharon ko khadaa karke zamin par ek bade gole kaa aakaar banaaya gaya ho. aise pathar ke chakra vishwa main kai jagahon par milte hai or alag-alag kaarnon se banaae gaye the. inamen se adhiktar pashaan yug main bane the haalanki kuch kaansya yug va anya kaalon main bhi khadey kiye gaye the. udhaahrnavash bharat ke nagpur shahar ke paas junapani shilavart 1000 isapurv ke kaal main khadey kiye gaye the. aksar shilavarton main maanav shavon ko dafanaaya jaataa tha, masalan maharashtra ke wardha zile main pashaan yug main dafanaae gaye 1500 logon ke liye bane 5 shilavart milen hai.
