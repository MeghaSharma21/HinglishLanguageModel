 qaafiristaan aaj ke paak-afakhaan kshetra main sthit ek kshetra he jise 1896 main afghanistan ke shaasha abdir-rahamaan khan ne islam main parivartit kar diya tha. iskaa naam badalakar nuristaan kar diya gaya tha.
 kaafiristaan afghanistan kaa ek praant he jiske uttar main badaashaan kaa pradesh, uttar-purv main chitral tathaa dakshin-purv main kunar kii ghati he. san 1885-86 se pehle is parvatiy pradesh ke bare main bahut kam gyaan tha. kaafir logon kaa yah desh 1859 i. tak purna rup se swatantra rahaa. iske pashchaat kabul ke amir abdurrahman ne is bhaag ko apane adhikaar main kar liya tathaa yahan ke nivaasiyon ko islam dharm kaa samarthak bana liya.
 desh main vibhinn sankri ghaatiyaan hai jo unchi parivartit chattaanon se bani hui parvatashreniyon dwaara alag-alag kar di gai he. puraa pradesh bahut hi unchaa nicha he. maidan yaa samtal kshetra nammaatr ke liye bhi nahin hai. sara parvatiy kshetra jangalon se dhakaa hua he. dhaalon par charaagaah hai. yahan par fal tathaa tarkariyaan paidaa kii jaati hai. nadiyon main paryaapt machhliyaan pai jaati hai. ghaatiyon main fal, phool tathaa ann paidaa kiye jate hai.
