 vaakhi purvi irani bhashaa-parivaar kii pamiri upashakha kii ek bhashaa he. ise purvottaree afghanistan ke vaakhaan kshetra or use se lage china, pakistan or tajikistan ke ilaakon main bolaa jaataa he. ise bolne waalon kii sankhya lagbhag 30,000 he.
 haalanki vaakhi kaa naam vaakhaan kshetra main basane vaale vaakhi logon par padaa he, lekin ve swayan apni bhashaa ko 'khik zic' bulaate hai. vaakhi log apane samudaay ko 'khik' bulaate hai or 'khik zic' kaa matlab 'khik bhashaa' he. 'vaakhi' or 'khik' shabdon main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
