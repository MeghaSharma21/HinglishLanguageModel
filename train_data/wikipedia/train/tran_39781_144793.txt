 prashanth samay kshetra samanvit saarvatrik samay se aath ghante ghataakar maanak samay dekhne par milti he. is kshetra main maanak samay greenvich vedhashala ke 120 wein madhyahn pashchim saura samay par aadhaarit he. america or canada main ise saamanytah prashanth samay kshetra kehete hai. lekin kabhi kabhi ise prashanth maanak samay bhi kehete hai.
 varsh 2006 main iske sthaneey samay ko subah ke roshni ke samay april ke pehle ravivaar ko 02:00 se 03:00 badal gaya. jo oktober ke antim ravivaar ko waapas 02:00 se 01:00 main aa jaataa he.
 2005 kii urja niti adhinium ke kaaran america main yah 2007 main prabhaav main aayaa.
