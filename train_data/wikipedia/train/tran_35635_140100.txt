 kathmandu vishvavidhyalay, nepal kaa swytt, nijee vishvavidhyalay he. yah nepal kaa teesraa sabase purana vishvavidhyalay he or nepal ke kabhreplanchok jila ke dhulikhel main sthit he. dhulikhel, kathmandu se 30 kimi purv main sthit he. isaki sthapana 1991 main hui thi. is vishvavidhyalay ke praangan dhulikhel, patan, or bhaktpur main hai. isamen manviki, vyavasthaapan, vaanijya, vigyaan, chikitsashastra, ingenring aadi kii shikshan hota he. samprati kathmandu vishvavidhyalay main 15,000 vidhyaarthee adhyayanrat hai.
