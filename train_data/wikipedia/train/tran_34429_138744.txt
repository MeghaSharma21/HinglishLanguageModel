 harsimrat cor badal
 harsimrat cor badal  bharat kii ek raajnneetijna hai or vartmaan main bharat sarkaar ke antargat khaadya prasanskaran udyog mantri hai. ve bhatinda lok sabha nirvachan kshetra se varsh 2009 se lagaataar 15 veen or 16veen loksabhaa kii saansad hai. ve shiromani akali dal kii sadsy or punjab ke up mukhyamantri sukhbir singh badal kii patni hai. 
 punjab kshetra main ghat rahe ling anupaat ke viruddh chalaae ja rahe nanhee chhaan abhiyaan ke liye inkaa ullekh kiya jaataa he.
