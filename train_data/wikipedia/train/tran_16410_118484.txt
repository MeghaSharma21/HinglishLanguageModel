 rohit khatter
 samir uddin
 kalakandi ek bhartiya blike kamedi film he. yah akshat verma dwaara likhit or nirdeshit he. isamen saf ali khan or sobhitha dhulipala ne abhinay kiya he. yah film rohit khatter or aashi dua dwaara nirmit he. is film ko 12 janvari 2018 relies kiya gaya tha.
 kalakandi kaa sangeet samir uddin or shaashvat sachdev dwaara diya gaya he jabaki bol anvita dutt or akshay verma dwaara likhe gaye he. album kii pehali geet "swagpur kaa choudhary" he, jo kii 23 disambar 2017 ko jari kii gai thi. doosraa gaanaa "kaala dorya" he. album ko zee mujeec company dwaara 27 disambar 2017 ko release kiya gaya.
