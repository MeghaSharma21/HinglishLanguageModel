 pawan hans helicopters limited bharat kii ek helicopter seva he. iske prachaalan juhu vimaanashetra, viley parley, mumbai-pashchim se niyantrit hote hai. ye company oengisi ko ofshor sthalon par sevaayen dene ke alaava ye kai rajya sarkaron ko, visheshkar purvottar bharat main sevaayen pradaan karata he. iske alaava ye saarvajanik santhaan jammu evam kashmir rajya main tirthasthan vaishno devi ke liye katra se upar tak kii seva bhi detaa he.
 company 1985 main astitv main ai. is samay isamen bharat sarkaar kii 51% tathaa tel evam prakritik gas ayog kii 49% shairdharita he. isaki kii sthapana sagar main tel kii khoj; tateey tel kshetra main helicopter sahaayta sevaayen uplabdh karaane, pahari tathaa durgam kshetron main parichaalan karaane tathaa yatayaat or paryaton ko badhaava dene hetu charter udaane uplabdh karaane ke mul uddeshy se kii gai thi.
