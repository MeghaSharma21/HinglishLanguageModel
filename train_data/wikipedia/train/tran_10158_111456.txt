 4chaiyn ek antarjaal-sthit vichaar-vimarsh manch, jo vishwa ke sabase lokapriy jaalasthalon se ek he. yahan par charchaa angl bhashaa main hoti he, haalanki vishvabhar ke buddhijivi yahan ekatrit hote hai. karmchaariyon ke sivaay yahan koi panjikrut sadsy nahin he - 4chaiyn par buddhijivi praayah agyaat naam se apane vichaar vyakt karte hai. yah vishwa ke sabase adhik araajaktaavaadi or prabhaavshaalee jaalasthalon main se ek he.
 4chaiyn kii sthapana vi. sa. 2060 main kii gayi thi. iske mul sansthaapak shri christopher pool aajkal google ke karmchaaree hai, jabaki iske vartmaan swami shri hiroyuki nishimura hai. multah: 4chaiyn par japani samskruti ke premi maanga evam anime jaise vishyon par charchaa karte the. parantu samay ke saath-saath yahan par anya vishyon main ruchi rakhane vaale buddhijivi bhi ekatrit hone lage. aaj 4chaiyn par bhanti-bhanti ke samudaay hai: itihaas, kala, khel, ganit, chalchitr, parivahan, prakruti, praudyogiki, manviki, yatra, raajnneeti, vigyaan, vyapaar, shastr, sangeet, samskruti, samachar, sahitya, swasthya aadi.
 4chaiyn antarjaal sakriyatawad ek pramukh centre he. vi. sa. 2073 ke sanyukt rajya raashtrapati chunaav ke samay 4chaiyn vijeta shri donald trump ke samarthakon kaa yah ek pramukh manch tha. vishwa-vikhyath sangh anonemas kii utpatti yaheen par hui thi. lolcat jaise kai mim bhi yaheen se udbhoot hue. 4chaiyn ke buddhijiviyon ne kai anya achche kaaryon main bhi apana yogadan diya he, jaise ki aparaadhiyon ko pakadaney main prashasan kii sahaayta, vruddh sanakon ke janmdiwas samaaroh kaa aayojan aadi.
