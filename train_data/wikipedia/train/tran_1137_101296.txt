 angami japo 1904 se 1990 tak nagaland rashtrawaad ke netaa the.unhone nagaland kii aam janata se desh or rajya ke prati chal rahe sainya kaarywahi ko sainya vidroh kaa naam diya or ise samaapt khatm karne main khaasi pahal kii tha. shuruyaat ke charnon main inhone shanti sthapit karne ke liye alag-alag asamiya, gairo, kaasi, lushai, mikiras,aborsh,ityadi se mili. 
 ve purvi pakistan chale gaye the jo ab bangladesh ban chukaa he. vahaan se disambar 1956 ke lagbhag landan chale gaye the.nagaland nashnal council kaa ve vahaan se bhi andolan ko chalaate rahe. unka nidhan 1990 ko landan main hi ho gaya. unke 11 bachche the.
