 mithuben hormasji petit bhartiya mahila swatantraaa kaaryakartaaon kii ek agrani sainani thi, jinhone mahatma gandhi ke dandi march main bhi bhaag liya tha.
 mithuben petit kaa janm 11 april 1892 ko bombai ke ek samrudh parsi parivaar main hua tha, unke pita sar dinshow maaneji petit the jo ek prasiddh udyogpati or baronet the.
 yuva petit apni mausi se prabhavit thi jo gandhi kii anuyaayi, or rashtriya stri sabha kii sachiv thi. kasturba gandhi or sarojini naidu ke saath petit ne dandi march main ek pramukh bhoomika nibhaai thi, kasturba gandhi ke saath sabarmati se march shuru hone ke baad, 6 april 1930 ko sarojini naidu ke saath milakar unhone dandi main pehali baar namak uthaakar kanoon todaa or unhone 9 april 1930 ko bhimrad main pun: ulanghan doharaaya. yatra bhartiya swatantraaa andolan kii sabase mahatvapurn ghatana main se ek he. us samay jab mahilaon ko peeche rakhaa jaataa tha, petit un teen mahilaon main se ek thi jinhone yatra main ek mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai or namak par kar ke khilaaf naagarik avagya kii. petit ne 1928 ke bardoli satyagrah main bhaag liya jo british raaj ke khilaaf "koi kar nahin" abhiyaan tha, jahaan unhone sardaar patel ke maargdarshan main kaam kiya tha. petit bharat main sharaab virdohi andolan main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai thi or mahatma gandhi ke saath samay bitaate hue unhen gujarat main anusuchit janajatiyon ke saath sharaab ke mudde kii vyakhya karti thi.
 petit ne maroli main ek ashram sthapit kiya jise kasturba vanaat shaala kaha jaataa he, jisamen pariwaron se vanchit adivasee, harijan bacchon ko aatmanirbhar banane ke liye fisher lok katai, carding, bunai, deyari kheti, chamade ke kaam or silaai main diploma course padhaayaa jaataa he. unhone maansik rup se biimaar marijon ke ilaaj ke liye usee naam kaa ek aspataal bhi kholaa tha.
 16 july 1973 ko unki mrityu ho gai.
