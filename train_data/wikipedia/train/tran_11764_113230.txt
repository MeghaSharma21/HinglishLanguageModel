 sari bhartiya stri kaa mukhya paridhaan he. yah shaayad vishwa kii sabase lambii or puraane paridhaanon main se gina jaataa he. yah lagbhag 5 se 6 gaj lambi bina sili hue kapade kaa tukdaa hota he jo blouse yaa cholii or saaya ke upar lapetakar pahana jaataa he.
 sari pahanane ke kai tareeke hai jo bhugolic sthiti or paaramparik mulyon or ruchiyon par nirbhar karata he. alag-alag style kii saadiyon main kanjivaram sari, banarsi sari, patola sari or hacoba mukhya hai. madhya pradesh kii chanderi, maheshwari, madhubani chapai, assam kii moonga rasham, urisa kii bomakai, rajasthan kii bandhej, gujarat kii gathoda, pataula, bihar kii tasar, kaatha, chhattisgadhi kosa rasham, dilli kii rashami saadiyaan, jharkhandi kosa rasham, maharashtra kii pathani, tamilnadu kii kanjivaram, banarsi saadiyaan, uttar pradesh kii tanchi, jamdani, jamvar evam pashchim bengal kii baaluuchhari evam kantha tangail aadi prasiddh saadiyaan hai.
