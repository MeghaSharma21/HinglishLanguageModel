 gendoli rajasthan ke bundi jila ke paas main basaa hua ek chota gaanv he joki jile se 40 kilometre dur he aravali parvatamala ke najadik sthit he
 bundi ke vir itihaas main gendoli kaa naam bhi pramukh hai. suryamall mishran dwaara likhi gayi "rang main bhang " se is gaanv kaa itihaas juda hua he. pracheen kal main bundi ke rajya hone ke samay gendoli kaa naam gananapur tha. yah par itihaas main yah mithkon ke anusaar bundi kii rajdhani hua karti thi. yah par itihaas se jodne vaali kai kadiyaan hamako dekhne ko milti he . sankshipt main, yah kaa itihaas bahut khaas hone kii vajah se ise bahut kam hi log jan paae he or kuch log mithkeey kahanio ke aadhaar par gaanv kaa itihaas bataate he .
 Kanchan Dhamyaha gendoli or pholai ke bich main sthit he!
 yah pracheen jain mandir taqariban 500 saal purana hai. is pracheen mandir ke andar jain samudaay ke bhagwan kii murti virajman he . saath hi yah par pracheen kaal kii puraani bhittichitra kala ke bhi 3 4 udahrahan he . mithkon ke anusaar yah puraa mandir keval ek hi khambhe par tikaa hua he, tathaa is mahel ke niche kai galiyare he jo kii ise kae anjan jagahon se jodate he. jain samudaay ke drushtikon se yah mandir kaaphi pavitra he .
 yah mukhya sadak RJSH29 se lagbhag 3 kilometre dur sthit he . pracheen manyato ke anusaar yahan par 36 karod devi devataao kii pratimaaye virajman thi jiname se ab prakarthik ghatnao kii vajah se lagbhag 50-60 hi baaki rah gayi he. is pavitra sthaan main shri shiv shankar bholenath kaa shivlinga vidyaman he jo kii lagbhag 5000 saal pracheen he . shivlinga varttman main bhi surakshit sthiti main he,or anya murtiyan jirnoddhar sthiti main hai.
 ye bhi pracheenatam mandiron main se ek he tathaa yah par shraddhalu badi dur dur se aate he . yah mandir bhi pahadiyon ke talahati main basaa hua he.
 yah bhi mukhya bazaar main hi sthit he. mandir bahut sundar he.
