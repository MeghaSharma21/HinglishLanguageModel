 bhilai ispaat sanyantr ) bharat ke chhattisgarh ke bhilai main sthit ispaat kaarkaana he. yah bharat kaa pehla ispaat utpaadak sanyantr he tathaa mukhyatah relon kaa utpaadan karata he. 
 is sanyantr kii sthapana soviet sangh kii sahaayta se 1955 main hui thi.
 is kaarkaane kii sthapana duusari panchavarshiya yojana ke antargat kii gai thi.
 das baar desh kaa sarvshereshth ekikrut ispaat kaarkaane ke liye pradhanmantri trophy praapt yah kaarkaana raashtra main rail kii patariyon or baari ispaat platon kaa ekmaatr nirmaataa tathaa sanrachanaon kaa pramukh utpaadak he. desh main 260 meter kii rail kii sabase lambi patariyon ke ekmaatr saplir, is kaarkaane kii vaarshik utpaadan kshamta 31 lakh 53 hajaar ton vikrey ispaat kii he. yah kaarkaana vir rod tathaa marchent utpaada jaise vishesh saamaan bhi taiyaar kar rahaa he. bhilai ispaat kaarkaana iesao 9001:2000 gunvattta prabandhan pranali se panjikrut he. atah iske sabhi vikrey ispaat iesao kii paridhi main aate hai. 
 bhilai ke kaarkaane, isaki basti or dalli khaanon ko paryaavaran prabandhan pranali se sambandhit iesao 14001 bhi praapt he. yah desh kaa aisa ekmaatr ispaat kaarkaana he jise in sabhi kshetron main praaanpatr mila he. kaarkaane ko saamaajik uttardaayitv nibhaane ke liye ese: 8000 praaanpatr or vyavsaayik swasthya tathaa suraksha ke liye oechaesees-18001praaanpatr bhi praapt he. in anterrashtriya str par maanya praaanpatron ke kaaran bhilai ke utpaadon kaa mahatv or bhi bad jaataa he tathaa ispaat udyog main isaki gananaa sarvshereshth sanghathanon main kii jaati he. bhilai ko anek rashtriya puraskaron se sammanit kiya gaya he tathaa ise lagaataar teen varsh siaiai-itisi sastenibility puraskaar praapt hua he.
