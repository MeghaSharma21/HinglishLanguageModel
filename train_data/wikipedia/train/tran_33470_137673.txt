 2011 varjeenia bhucamp sanyukt rajya america ke sudur purvi rajya varjeenia main 23 agast 2011 ko aayaa 5.8 kii tivrata kaa bhucamp tha jo sthaneey samyaanusaar dopaher main 1 bajkar 51 minute or chalis sekhind par aayaa tha. usgs ke anusaar, is bhucamp kaa kendra varjeenia rajya kii rajdhani richmand se 61 kimi uttarpurv main mineraal main tha. bhucamp ke 12 ghanton ke pashchaat chaar pashchaatvarti jatke aae the jinaki tivrata kramshah 2.8, 2.2, 4.2 or 3.4 thi.
 yah bhucamp anya america rajyon jaise maisaachusits, georgia or ilinoy or canadian praant ontario main bhi mahasoos kiya gaya tha. neuyork nagar, washington or tornto main bahut si unchi imaaraten khaali kara lee gayi.
 is bhucamp ke kaaran uttar enna nabhikeey urja sanyantra ke do riector bhi swatah band ho gaye the.
