 chiporesun yaa chapursan paak-adhikrut kashmir ke ke gilgit-balatistan kshetra ke hunzaa-nagar zile kii gozaal tehsil main sthit ek vaadi he. is ghati main aath gaanv hai, jinamein adhiktar vaakhi bhashaa bolne vaale ismaili shiya rahete hai. yah ilaaka irshaad darre dwaara afghanistan ke vaakhaan galiyare se judata he or china kii sarhad ke bhi kaafii sameep he.
 chiporesun vaadi main daakhil hote hi pehla gaanv yazarich he. uske baad raminj aata he. agle teen gaavan - aminabad, rahimabad or nurabad - samuhik rup se ikatthe kirmin kehalaate hai. inke baad kill aata he, jo vaadi main bahane vaali nadi ke dono kinaaron par vistrut he. phir reshit or sher-e-sabz gaanv aate hai. inke baad ispanj or shuthmarg or phir ant main zud kun aata he, jo 3,500 meter kii oonchaai par basaa hua he.
