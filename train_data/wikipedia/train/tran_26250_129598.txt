 rugveda kii 21 shakhaon main se aashvalyan anyatam shakha he jiskaa ullekh 'charanvyuuh' main kiya gaya he. is shakha ke anusaar na to aaj riksanhita hi uplabdh he or na koi brahman hi, parantu kaveendracharya kii granthsuchee main ullikhit hone se in granthon ke astitv kaa spasht pramaan miltaa he.
 is shakha ke samgr kalpasutra hi aaj uplabdh hai- aashvalyan shrautasutra, grihmasutra or dharmasutra. aashvalyan shrautasutra main 12 adhyay hai jinamein hota ke dwaara pratipaadya vishyon kii ore vishesh lakshya kar yaagon kaa anushthan vihit he. isamen puronuvakya, yajya tathaa tatat shastron ke anushthan prakaar, unke desh, kaal or karta kaa vidhaan, swar-pratigar-neunkh-praayshchitt aadi kaa vidhaan vishesh rup se varnit he. narsingh ke putr gargy narayan dwaara kii gai is shrautasutra kii vyakhya nitant prakhyat he.
 aashvalyangriysutra main gruhm karm or shodash sanskaron kaa varnan kiya gaya he. rigvediyon kii grihyavidhi ke liye yahi grihmasutra vishesh lokrapy tathaa prasiddh he. isaki vyapaktaa kaa kuch parichay isaki vipul vyakhya-sampatti se bhi lagata he. iske prakhyat tikagranthon main mukhya ye hai: 
 aashvalyangriy anek granthkaaron ke naam se prasiddh hai. aise granthkaaron main kumaril swami, raghunatha dikshit tathaa gopal mukhya hai. is grihyasutra ke prayog, paddati tathaa parishisht ke vishay main bhi anek granthon kaa samay samay par nirmaan kiya gaya he. kumaril kii grihyakarika main aashvalyangriy kii narayanavruti tathaa jayantswami kaa nirdesh uplabdh hota he. 'aashvalyansmriti' ke bhi abhi tak hastlekh hi uplabdh hai. yah 11 adhyyon main vibhakt or lagbhag 2,000 padyonvala granth he jiske uddhran hemaadri tathaa madhavacharya ne apane granthon main die hai.
