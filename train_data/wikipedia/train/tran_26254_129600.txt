 nimna daab kshetra yaa kam daab kaa kshetra us jagah ko kehete hai, jahaan vaayu mandal kaa daab aas paas ke kshetra se kam ho jaataa he. jab aasapaas ke kshetra main daab adhik hota he, to vahaan kii hawa us nimna daab ke kshetra main pravesh karti he. iske saath kai baar badal bhi aa jate hai, jo varsha ke mukhya kaaran banate hai. iske alaava hawa ghoomane lagata he or chakravaat bhi ban jaataa he.
 jab vaayu mandal main nimna daab kaa kshetra ban jaataa he or aas paas ucch daab kaa kshetra hota he, to vah ek duusare ke jagah main aane ke liye koshish karte hai. nimna daab ke kshetra main hawa bahut kam hoti he or ucch daab ke kshetra main vaayu kam jagah main hi bahut maatra main hoti he. is kaaran ucch daab ke kshetra se hawa teji se nimna daab ke kshetra main pravesh karti he.
