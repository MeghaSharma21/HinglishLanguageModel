 dharmnath agrahari bihar rajya se swatantraaa sangram senaanee thea. 1942 ke bharat chodo andolan ke dauraan tatkaaleen chhapra gilen ke british dushmano ke chakke chhudaane vaale bhartiya ranbankur me se aap ek thea. dharmnath agrahari ko 1942 main mahatma gandhi ke bharat chodo andolan me sakriy sahabhaagita hetu janaa gaya. aapke pita kaa naam swargeey sarayu prasad tha tathaa aap bihar praant ke sivaan me channpur, mahavir chowk ke nivasee thea. dinaak 3 oktober 2008 ko bharat maan ke vir sapoot swatantraaa senaanee dharmnath agrahari kaa dehanth channpur ke hi ek nijee nursing home me hua. unka antim sanskaar sarayu nadi ke tat par kiya gaya.
