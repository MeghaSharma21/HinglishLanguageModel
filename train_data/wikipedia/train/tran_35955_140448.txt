 maniben vallabhbhai patel ne bharat kii swatantra ke liye apana sampoorn jivan samarpit kar diya. parantu swatantra bharat main apane vriddhavasthakaal main unke paas dhan, maan, aavashyakvastuon kaa abhaav tha. bharat kii swantrata ke liye jitne aandolan lohpurush ne kiye hai, un sab main se adhiktam aandolanon main maniben kaa mahadyogdan rahaa he. satyagrahon main kathorparishram ke pashchaat karagaar main bhi unhone karawas kii kathorpeeda sahi hai. rashtraseva ke liye samarpit wo mahila avivaahita rah kar aaajeevan bharat ke hit ke liye chintan karti rahi.
 1903 varsh ke 'april'-maas kii teesri dinaank par gujaratraajya ke kheda jile main maniben kaa janm hua. unke pita sardaar vallabhbhai patel bharat ke mahan netaaon main or mahan deshbhakton main se ek the. unke matrushri kaa naam jhaverba tha. maniben jab saat varshiya thi, tab unke matrushri nidhan ho gaya. maniben kaa ek anuj bhi tha. atah uske poshan kaa daayitv balykaal se hi maniben ke upar aa padaa.
 parivaar ke sadsy sardaar vallabhbhai patel ko bahut samajhaay ki, ve punarvivaah kar len. parantu drudhman vaale sardaar vallabhbhai patel baale, “main vimaata kaa dukh apane bacchon ke upar daalana nahin chahta”. uske baad aaajeevan maat kaa or pita kaa daayitv sardaar vallabhbhai patel ne vahan kiya. jhaverba kii mrityu ke ekvarsh baad hi sardaar vallabhbhai patel ko padhne ke liye videsh janaa pad gaya. atah unhone apane bacchon ko apane agraj viththalbhai ke paas bhej diya. tab viththalbhai mumbai main nivas karte the. mumbai main quin meri vidhyalay main maniben kaa abhyaas armbh hua. parantu mumbai-mahanagar ke vaataavaran main maniben aswasth rahati thi. vaidyon ke aushadh dene ke baad bhi unke swasthya main koi parivartan na hua. pita ke viyog se unki sthiti aisi ho gai he, ye bhi ek kaaran tha.
