 gujarat vidhaan sabha chunaav 2002 • 2007 • 2012 
 bharat ke pradhan mantri lok sabha chunaav, 2014 • shapathgrahan
 vaishvik yogadan 
 bharat 
 ---
 
 antarrashtriya saura gathbandhan,  saura urja par aadhaarit 121 deshon kaa ek sahyog sanghathan he jiskaa shubharambh bharat va france dwaara 30 november 2015 ko paris main kiya gaya. yah bharat ke pradhanmantri narendra modi dwaara kii gai pahal kaa parinaam he jisaki ghoshnaa unnahone sarvapratham landan ke vembly stadium main apane udbodhan ke dauraan kii thi. 
 yah sanghathan kark va makar rekha ke bich sthit rashtron ko ek manch par laaegaa. aise rashtron main dhoop kii uplabdhta bahulata main he. is sanghathan main ye sabhi desh saura urja ke kshetra main milakar kaam karenge. is prayaas ko vaishvik str par urja paridrishy main ek bade badlaav ke rup main dekha ja rahaa he. 
 is sanghathan kaa antarim sachivalay rashtriya saura urja santhaan, gualpahadi, gudgaanv main banaaya gaya he. iskaa udghaatan 25 janvari 2016 ko. mukhyalay ke nirmaan hetu bharat sarkaar ne rashtriya saura urja santhaan campus ke andar paanch acre jameen aavantit kii he.
 25 janvari 2016 ko bharat ke pradhanmantri shri narendra modi or france ke raashtrapati shri francwa oland ne sanyukt rup se antarrashtriya saura gathbandhan mukhyalay kii aadharshila rakhi.
 2016 ke marakkesh jalwaayu sammelan main 15 november 2016 ko is sandhi kaa aadhikarik praaroop prastut kiya gaya or pehle din 15 deshon ne is par hastakshar kiye. ye desh the- bangladesh, brazil, burkina phaso, cambodia, congou, dominican republic, ithiopia, gini republic, madagascar, mali, nauru, nizer, tanzania tathaa tuvaalu.
