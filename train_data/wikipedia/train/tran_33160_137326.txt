 poli vinil chloride ek acristalia tapasughattay padaarth he. kathor padaarth he. ooshmaa tathaa rasinic padaarthon kaa is par prabhaav nahin padataa he. poliethlin or polipropeilin ke baad yah teesraa sarvaadhik utpaadit plastic he. nirmaan kaaryon main pivisi kaa upyog hota he. pivisi se pipe, cabel insulation, farsh par bichaane kii chadar, daravaaje aadi banaae jate hai. polivinil chloride kaa nirmaan monomer vinil chloride ke bahulikaran dwaara kiya jaataa he.
 shuddh pivisi multah safed, kathor or bhangur thos hota he jisamen sughatyakari milakar use naram va lacheela banaaya jaataa he. sughatyakari ke rup main thalats kaa prayog sabase adhik hota he. naram polivinil chloride kaa upyog vastra tathaa gadde aadi banane ke liye bhi kiya jaataa he.
