 nutan savera rashtriya hindi saptahik hindi patrakarita ke kshetra main janaa-mana naam he. nutan savera kii pramukh visheshta yah he ki isane hindi kii garima ko kaayam rakhte hue khabaron or mahatvapurn muddon ko saral bhashaa main prastut kiya he, taaki adhik se adhik pathakon tak pahunchaa ja sake. samaaj or vyavastha main faile hue kadachaar, bhrashtachar, gair zimmedaari or kuraj par prahaar karte hue nutan savera apane kaalmon main rashtriya uplabdhiyon tathaa usamen yogadan karanevaale vyaktiyon or pravritiyon ko aage badhkar ujagar karata he.nutan savera ne 1993 main hindi patrakarita ke kshetra main aagaaz kiya. ardhashti se adhik ke anbhavi varisht patrkaar tathaa sampadak nandakishor nautial ke sampadan main nutan savera hindi patrakarita kii nayi oonchaaiyaan chhu rahaa he. nandakishor nautial isse pehle 30 varshon tak hindi blitz ke sampadak rah chuke hai. is samay vah maharashtra rajya hindi sahitya academy ke karyaadhyaksh hai. shri badrinath-kedarnath mandir samiti trust ke bhi adhyaksh rah chuke hai. unke sampadan main nutan savera desh or samaaj kii seva main agrani bhoomika nibhaa rahaa he.aaj nutan savera naamak vah paudha vatvriksh kaa rup dhaaran kar chukaa he jisaki shakhaaen maharashtra, gujarat, uttarakhand, uttar pradesh, rajasthan, himachal pradesh, punjab, haryana, chhattisgarh, urisa, bihar, jharkhand, madhya pradesh sahit samast hindustan main faili hai. nutan savera akhbaar kaa mukhya karyaalay mumbai main sthit he.
