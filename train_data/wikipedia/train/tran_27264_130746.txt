 aal india anna dravid munetra kadgam tamil nadu or puducheri, bharat kaa ek raajnneetik dal he. isaki sthapana san 1972 main purv abhinayta va raajnneetijna em ji ramachandran ne kii thi jab wo dramuk se alag ho gayi the.
 1989 se is dal kii netaa jayalalita hai. dal ne tamilnadu main chah baar sarkaar banaai he or 2011 se tamil nadu main sarkaar isi dal kii he.
