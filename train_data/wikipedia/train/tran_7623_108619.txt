 monte-carlo vidhiyaan computer-kalan vidhiyon ke us samooh ko kehete hai jo kisi samasya ke sankhyatmak parinaam praapt karne ke liye yadrichhik pratichayan kaa upyog karata he. is vidhi kaa mul mantra yah he ki yadrutchhata kaa upyog karte hue un samasyaon k bhi hl nikaal sakate hai jo siddhantatah sunirdharya hai. mante carlo vidhiyaan praayah hi bhautik evam ganitiya samasyaon ke hal ke liye upyog main laayin jaatin hai. ye us samay sarvaadhik upyogi hotin hai jab anya vidhiyon kaa upyog nahin kiya ja sake. ye vidhiyaan mukhyatah teen prakaar kii samasyaon ke hal ke liye prayukt hotin hai- ishtatameekaran, sankhyatmak samakalan, tathaa prayikta vitaran diye hone par dra nikaalnaa.
