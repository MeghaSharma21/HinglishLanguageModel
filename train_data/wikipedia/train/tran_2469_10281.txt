 darbishaire county cricket club england or wales ke 18 pratham shrenee ke county club main se ek he. yah bahut puraane samay se he. isamen simit over main team ko darbishaire phelcon kaha jaataa tha. yah pehle darbishaire scorpian ke naam se 2005 tak janaa jaataa tha, uske baad yah phentum ke naam se 2010 tak janaa jane laga.
 isaki sthapana varsh 1870 main kiya gaya tha. yahan pehla match varsh 1871 main khelaa gaya tha. varsh 1871 se 1887 tak gaiyr-aadhikarik sroton ke anusaar yah pratham shrenee kaa county club tha. lekin iske bahut bure khilne ke kaaran or bahut saare sattebaajon ke kaaran yah kai samay tak khel nahin paaya. varsh 1895 main county pratiyogita main ise bulaava milane ke baad hi ise khilne kaa mauka mila. ise aadhikarik rup se pratham shrenee ke team varsh 1895 main merilabon cricket club ne mana tha.
