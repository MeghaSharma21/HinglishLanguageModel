 mahanagareey kshetra ek vishaal jansankhya centre or uske padosi kshetra ko milakar hota he. isamen ekadam nikatataa se jude shahar evam anya prabhavit kshetra bhi gine ja sakate hai. isamen sabase bade shahar ke naam se hi is kshetra ke naam ko janaa jaataa he. 
 bharat main, janaganana ayog ne mahanagareey kshetra ko paribhaashit is prakaar se kiya he. yah wo kshetra hote hai,. jinaki jansankhya 40 lakh se adhik hoti he. inamen mumbai, dilli, chennai, kolkata, bengalooru or hyderabad-- ye chah shahar vartmaan main aate hai. in shaharon ke nivaasiyon ko ucch ghar kiraaya bhatta adhikrut he. yah aankade maatr shahari kshetra par lagu hote hai.
