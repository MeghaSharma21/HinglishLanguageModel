 kath upanishad yaa kathopanishad, ek krishna yajurvedic upanishad he. 
 kathopanishad krishna yajurvedic shakha ke antargat ek upanishad he. yah upanishad sanskrit bhashaa main likhit he. iske rachiyata vaidic kaal ke rishiyon ko mana jaataa he parantu mukhyat vedavyas ji ko kai upanishdon kaa lekhak mana jaataa he.
 is upanishad kaa shantipth nimna he:
 yah upanishad aatm-vishyak aakhyayika se armbh hoti he . pramukh rup se yama nachiketa ke prashn pratiprashn ke rup main he . vajashrava loukik kirti kii ichha se vishvajit yag kaa anushthan karte hai . yaajak apni samgr sampatti kaa daan kar de yah is yajna kii pramukh vidhi he . is vidhi kaa anusaran karte hue usne apni sari sampatti daan kar di . vah nirdhan tha isliye uske paas kuch gaayen pitodak jagdhatrin dugdhdoha nirindriya or durbal thim . pita un gaayon ko yadi daan karte hai to nishchay hi punya nahin praapt hoga, jisase kiya ja rahaa yajna vifal na ho vaisaa mujhe karnaa chahie yah sochakar uskaa putr nachiketa apane pita se, ‘mujhe kise doge’ aisa do teen baar poonchhata he . tab krodhit hokar pita ne 'yama ko duungaa' aisa bolaa .
