 vah pashu athwa vanaspati vasa jiskaa prayog khaane ke liye kiya jaataa he aahaar vasa kehalaati he. kisi tel ke vipareet, kamare ke taapmaana par aahaar vasa ardhthos avasthaa main rahati he. yahan yah baat dhyaan dene yogya he ki vasa shabd kaa prayog amuman tel or aahaar vasa dono ke liye hi kiya jaataa he. aahaar vasa ke udhaaharan hai makkhan, ghi, charbi or margerine. 
 san 1827 main wiliam prout ne vasa ko protein or sharkara ke saath santulit aahaar ke ek mahatvapurn poshak tatv ke rup main manyata kaa pradaan kii thi.
