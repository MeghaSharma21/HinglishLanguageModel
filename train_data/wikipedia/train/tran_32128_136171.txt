 bamsef anusuchit jati, anusuchit janajati, pichhade va in samudaayon ke dharmantarit alpsankhyakon ke karmchaariyon kaa akhil bhartiya sanghathan he. jo vyavastha bharat main vyaapt gairbarabari ko dur kar barabari sthapit karnaa chaahati he.
 isaki sthapana 1973 main manyavar kanshiram or di.ke. kharpade ne kii. kanshiram ne daliton ko ektabaddh kar, atyaacharon kaa pratirodh karne tathaa unhen samaaj main nyyochit sthaan banane ke liye joardaar dhang se prerit kiya. aajkal yah sanghathan 'mulnivaasee bahujan sangh' ke naam se janaa jaataa he. bich main kamjor pads gai bamsef ko babasahab ambedkar ke janm din 14 april ko san 1978 main punah shashakt banane kaa prayaas kiya gaya. bamsef se kanshiram ne dilli, maharashtra, madhya pradesh, punjab, haryana or uttar pradesh main dalit karmchaariyon kaa sanghathan majboot banaaya. iske pashchaat 6 disambar ko dies 4 kii sthapana kii. kanshiram ne naaraa diya ‘thakur, brahman, baniyaa chhod, baaki sab hai di es 4’. isi kram main san 1984 main bahujan samaaj party kii sthapana kii gai. babari masjid dhwans ke baad san 1993 main vidhaan sabha ke chunaav main basapa or sapa gathbandhan ne 67 siten jeetin. yah bhartiya raajnneeti ke itihaas main kisi dalit bahulya dal kii jiti gayi sarvaadhik siten thim. pichley dinon bamsef va bahujan samaaj party ke bich matbhinnata dikhaai di.
 bamsef brahmanwad ko apana mukhya shatru maanate hue mahatma jyotirav govindrav phule or babasahab ambedkar ko apana adarsh purush maanati he. bamsef ke anusaar samanthavirohi shaktiyon se ladne ke liye saamaajik badlaav ke andolan main sakriy bhagidaari aavashyak he. bamsef kaa vishwaas he ki sattadhari jatiyon or shoshit jatiyon ke spasht vibhaajan ko samajhate hue hamen bamsef ke antargat shoshit jatiyon ko sangathit kar shoshan kaa pratikaar karnaa hoga.
