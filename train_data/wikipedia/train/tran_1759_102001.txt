 faruk sheikh ek bhartiya abhinayta, samaaj-sevi or ek televijan prastota the. unhen 70 or 80 ke dashak kii filmon main abhinay ke kaaran prasiddhi mili. wo saamanytah ek kala cinema main apane kaary ke liye prasiddh the jise samanaantar cinema bhi kaha jaataa he. unhone satyajit raay or rishikesh mukherjee ke nirdesan main bhi kaam kiya.

 kaa janm mumbai ke ek vakil mustafaa shaikh or farida shaikh ke ek musalmaan parivaar main jo bodaili kasbe ke nikat nasbadi gram ke nikat badodi gujarat ke amaroli main hua. unke parivaar vaale zamindaar the or unka paalan poshan shandaar parivesh main hua. wo apane ghar ke paanch baccho main sabase bade the.
 wo saint mairi skool, mumbai main padhane gayi or baad main saint jevirs college, mumbai gayi. unhone kanun kii padhai siddharth college of lau main purna kii.
 28 disambar 2013 ko tadake dubai main dil kaa daura padane se unka nidhan ho gaya. wo dubai chhuttiyaan manaane gayi the.
