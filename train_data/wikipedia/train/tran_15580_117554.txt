 koran shareef main mahilaon se sambandhit vibhinn mahatvapurn paatr or kahaniyaan maujuud hai. islam kii naitik shiksha main mahilaayein ek charchit vishay hai. koran main kuch mahilaon kaa stvan kiya gaya he, jabaki dusron ko apane aniatik kaaryon ke liye ninda kii gayi he. marayam vaahid mahila he jiske puure naam kaa zikr koran main miltaa he. baaqi ke naam anya taur-tareeqon se ullikhit hai. koran main mahilaon kaa ullekh kuch packmbaron or saradaaron kii maataaon yaa patniyon or pratinidhi ke rup main hai.
 hawwa, koran main anya sabhi mahilaon kii tarah ke alaava) naam se ullikhit nahin he. haalanki baad kii hadison main unhen hawwa ke rup main jaani jaati he. ve kul teen surahon main ullikhit hai, do surahon main aadam kii "saathi" or "bivi" ke rup main ullikhit, jabaki aakhri ek surah main sirf aadam kaa ullekh miltaa he.
 koran main hawwa kii bhoomika aadam ke saathi ke rup main he. allaah ne aadam ko aagaah kar diya tha ki iblis unke or hawwa kaa dushman he.
