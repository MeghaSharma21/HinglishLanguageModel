 alexander dweep, jo alexander pratham dweep antarctic mahadweep kaa sabase bada ] he. yah pashchimi antarctic ke antarctic prayadweep ke palmer dharti kshetra main belingshousan sagar main sthit he. dweep or antarctic prayadweep ke bich main margarit khadi or jorge shashtham khadi aati he. jorge shashtham khadi main jorge shashtham himchattan sadaa tiki rahane se vaastv main alexander dweep mukhyabhumi ke prayadweep se hameshaa himchattan dwaara judaa rahata he. yah dweep uttar-dakshin disha main 390 kimi lambaa he. apane uttari bhaag main yah 80 kimi or dakshini bhaag main 240 kimi chaudaa he. uttari canada ke devon dweep ke baad, alexander dweep vishwa kaa doosraa sabase vishaal dweep he jahaan koi maanav-vaasi nahin.
