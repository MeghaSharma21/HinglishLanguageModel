 
 †adepiformisalimriformis† = vilupt
 strepsirayini yaa gili-naak narwanar narwanar gana kaa ek claide he jisamen sabhi limarrupi narwanar shaamil hai, jaise ki madagascar ke limar, africa ke galego or poto, or bharat va dakshinpurvi asia ke loris.
 strepsirayini narwanar gana ke duusare pramukh claide, heplorini se kaafii bhinn hote hai. inkaa mastishk chota hota he. inaki naaken geeli rahati hai, jab kii heplorini naaken baahar se adhiktar shushk hoti hai. inaki soonghney kii shakti bahut prakhar hoti he or aankhon main ek pratibimbi vyavastha ke kaaran yah raat main dekh paane main adhik sakasham hote hai . inamen vitamin si bana sakane vaala prakinv hota he or ve yah aavashyak rasayan swayan bana lete hai, jabaki heplorini claide ke praaniyon main yah nahin rahaa jis karanvash unhen vitamin-si yukt bhojan khaane kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. kai strepsirayini praaniyon ke jabadon ke saamne ke nichle daant kanghee jaisi vyavastha main hote hai jinase ve apane bal sanvaar sakate hai. vartmaan main astitv rakhane vaali adhiktar jatiyaan nishachari hai.
