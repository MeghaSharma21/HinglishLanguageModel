 rajasthan ke prasiddh tabalaa vadak. 
 ve bikaner ke damamiyaan mohalla main rahete the. 85 varsh kii avasthaa main 9 disambar 2009, buudhvaar kii subah ko unka nidhan ho gaya. unhen bikaner ke jassuget ke baahar sthit bade kabristaan main supurde khaak kiya gaya. unke janaaje kii namaaj masjid padmamiyaan ke imam mo. moinudin ne padhai. 
 unhone gulam ali khan, padmashri allaah jilai bai aadi kai prasiddh gayak kalakaaron ke saath sangat kii thi. 
 unhen sangeet naatak academy, jodhpur dwaara kala purodha kii oopaadhi pradaan kii gai thi.
