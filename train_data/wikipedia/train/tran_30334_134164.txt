 maharaj singh college, saharanpur, saharanpur uttar pradesh kaa ek pramukh snatakottar mahavidhyalay he jiskaa yah naam iske sansthaapak babu maharaj singh, edwocket ke naam par diya gaya he jinhone varsh 1957 main saharanpur main is college kii sthapana kii. vigyaan vishay kii vibhinn shakhaon - yatha - bhautikee, rasayanashastra, jantu vigyaan, jeev vigyaan, padap vigyaan aadi kii snatakottar kakshaon ke atirikt yahaan par hindi, angrezi, raajnneeti shaastra aadi vishay kii bhi kakshayen chalati hai. choudhary charan singh vishvavidhyalay meerut ke aadhin chal rahe maharaj singh college, saharanpur main lagbhag 4000 chaatr evam chaatrayen adhyayanrat hai.
