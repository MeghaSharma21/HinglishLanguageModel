 vriksh khanitr yaa 'tree sped' ek vishesh mashin he jo laghu evam madhyam aakaar ke vrikshon ko khodakar kisi duusare sthaan par ropane main sahaayak hoti he. yah mashin vriksh kii jad ke aapapaas kii mitti ko bachaate hue, vriksh ko jad samet khodkar uthaa leti he or jahaan us vriksh ko ropana hota he vahaan tak saavdhaneepurvak pahunchaa bhi detii he. in mashiinon main kai blade hote hai jo vriksh kii jad ko gher lete hai, khudaai karte hai, mitti or jadon sahit ped ko kasakar pakad lete hai or saavdhaneepurvak uthaakar anyatra pahunchaa dete hai.
