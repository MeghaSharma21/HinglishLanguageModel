 da cross saint thomas squaire, overmonnau, monmouth, wales main chauraahey ke bich saint thomas da marter ke vipareet va monnau pul ke pashchim main sthit he. madhyayugin kaal kii yah vaastu, jise overmonnau kros bhi kaha jaataa he, 1888 main punah nirmit kiya gaya tha or duusari shrenee kii imaarat ke rup main 15 agast 1974 main pramanit kiya gaya.
 kuch unnisvi sadi ke sroton ke anusaar mukhya kros 1039 main upasthit tha. saint thomas squaire ko ek bazaar samajha jaataa tha par baad main monnau pul ke banane se, jisane is gaav ko overmonnau se jod diya, yah bazaar band ho gaya. 1 april 1764 ko ek 18 varshiya mahila ko apni malkin ko zehar dekar maarane ke aarope main jivit jalaane kii sazaa sunai gai or is sazaa ko kros ke paas hi amal main laayaa gaya.
 jon speed ke naqshe main kros ko apni vartmaan jagah par dikhaaya gaya he jo 1611 main chitrit kiya gaya tha parantu 19vi sadi ke ant tak keval pathar kaa aadhaar hi batch paaya tha jo green dragon sarai ke maarg ke nikat tha. 1888 main charles henry-chrompton-roberts ne, jo drybrij house se the, is kros kii punarnirmiti karne kaa zimmaa uthaaya.
