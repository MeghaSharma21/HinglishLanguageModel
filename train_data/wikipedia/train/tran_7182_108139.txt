 dominiki sangh roman catholic sampradaay kaa ek upasampradaay he. isaki sthapana sant dominic ne kii thi.
 spainnivaasee upadeshak sant dominiki elbigensus naamak sampradaay kaa virodh karne ke liye bahut samay tak dakshin france kaa daura karte rahe or vahiin unhone tuluus naamak nagar main san 1215 i. main ek dharmasangh kii sthapana kii jo baad main dominiki sangh ke naam se vikhyath hua.
 upadesh dena tathaa janata main dharmashiksha kaa prachaar karnaa dominiki dharmasandhiyon kaa prarambh hi se pradhan kaary rahaa, us kriyasheeltaa ko safal banane ke liye sangh se vyatigat dhyaan, prarthana tathaa swadhyay ko mahatv diya jaataa tha. yah sangh sheeghra hi samast europe main fail gaya. 13veen sha.i. main hi paris, oxford, padua, colon aadi mukhya shikshakendron main uske mathon kii sthapana hui or vahaan ke vishvavidhyalayon ke bahut se praadhyaapak tathaa chaatr is sangh ke sadsy bane.
 parvarti shatabdiyon main dominiki faras, bharat, china, uttari africa aadi mishan kshetron main bhi dharmprachaar karne gaye. carch ke itihaas main tathaa vishesh rup se isai dharmavigyaan tathaa darshan ke itihaas main is sangh kaa atyant mahatvapurn sthaan he. sant tomas ekwines sabase prasiddh dominiki hai. unhone arestu ke darshan ko aadhaar banaakar samast isai dharmasiddhanton kaa vishad pratipaadan kiya he. unki rachanaaon kaa parishilan aajkal prayek roman katholic purohit ke liye anivaary he.
 is sangh ke sadsy darshan, bible aadi ke vishay main anek vaigyanik patrikaon ke sampadan dwaara pashchimi deshon ke buddhijiviyon ke liye dharmik samasyaon kaa samaadhaan karte hai.
