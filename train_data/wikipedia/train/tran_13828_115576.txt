 kamachatkaa kroy rus ke siberia kshetra ke sudur purv main sthit ek 'kroy' kaa darja rakhane vaala sangheey khand he. pehle yah do prashasnic khandon main banta hua tha - kaamchatkaa oblast or koryak swashasit okrug. in donon main raishumaari karne ke baad janata ke faisale se 1 july 2007 ko inkaa vilay karke kamachatkaa kroy banaaya gaya. isaki rajdhani petropavlovsk-kamchaatskeey naam kaa shahar he.
 san 2007 main kii gai ek janaganana ke anusaar isaki aabaadi 3,21,764 thi. is kroy kaa kshetrafal 4,72,300 varg kilometre he, yani lagbhag uttar pradesh, bihar or jharkhand ko milakar kshetra ke baraabar. bhugolic rup se kamachatkaa kroy kaa bada hissaa kamachatkaa prayadweep main aata he.
