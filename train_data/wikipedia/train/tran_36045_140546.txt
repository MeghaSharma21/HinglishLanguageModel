 aadhunik pantathlon ek vishesh rup se aadhunik khelon, bairan piyare di kurtin ke sansthaapak dwaara greeshmakaleen olympic khelon ke liye banaai gai ek sports pratiyogita he, or pehali baar 1912 main ladaa gaya tha. cobertin pracheen olympic khelon main pentathalon ghatana se prerit tha, jo us samay adarsh sainik ke kaushal ke baad taiyaar kiya gaya tha.
 aadhunik pentathalon ne 19veen shatabdi ke kaivalari sipaahi ke dushmano ke peeche anubhav kaa ujagar kiya: use ek aparichit ghode kii sawari karnaa chahie, pistaul or talwaar ke saath ladaai karnaa, tairnaa or chalaane.isliye, aadhunik pentathalon ghatana main paanch khel vishyon shaamil hai:
 aadhunik pantathalon olympic kaarykram par lagaataar 1912 se rahaa he. 2000 ke khelon tak mahila kaa aayojan shaamil nahin tha.
