 jhaan viktar paunsley francisi ganitagya evam ejeeniyar the. ganitagya ke rup main unka sabase ullekhneey kaary prakshepiy jyamiti par tha.
 inkaa janm 1 july 1788 i. ko mas main hua tha. nepolian ke roosi akraman ke samay ye bandi bana liye gaye the. bandigrih main inhone 'trete de propriyete projektiv de figyur' pustak kii rachana kii, jo 1814 i. main inke france lautne ke aath varsh pashchaat prakashit hui. isamen inhone un akritiyon ke gunon kaa varnan kiya he, jo kendriya pratyaalekh ke pashchaat aparivartit rahati hai. iske atirikt paunsley ne jyamitiya saattatya ke siddhant kaa sutara rup main varnan kiya or falswaroop un binduon evam rekhaon par vichaar kiya, jo anant par lupt athwa kaalpanik ho jaati hai. ye adarsh bindu evam rekhaayen jyamiti ko vishleshan kii apurv dein hai. inhone koniya vyutkram kii vidhi kaa bhi avishkaar kiya or inase 'dwait avasthaa ke siddhant' kaa nigaman kiya. 22 disambar 1867 i. ko paris main inkaa dehanth ho gaya.
