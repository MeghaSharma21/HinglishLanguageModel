 parmanand shrivastav hindi ke pratishthit sahityakar the. unki gananaa hindi ke sheersh aalochakon main hoti he. gorakhpur vishvavidhyalay main premachand peeth kii sthapana main unka vishesh yogadan rahaa. kai pustakon ke lekhan ke atirikt unhone hindi bhashaa kii saahitiyik patrika aalochana kaa sampaadan bhi kiya tha.
 aalochana ke kshetra main ullekhneey yogadan ke liye unhen vyas sammaan or bharat bharti puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya. lambi beemaari ke baad unka gorakhpur main nidhan ho gaya.
 10 farvari 1935 ko gorakhpur jile ke baansagaanv main janme parmanand shrivastav ne apni praarambhik shiksha paitruk gaanv se purna karne ke uparant saint andrj college gorakhpur se snaatak, parasnatak va doctrate kiya or usee college main pravaktaa ho gayi. baad main ve gorakhpur vishvavidhyalay chale gayi or hindi vibhaag ke acharya pad se sevanivrutt hue. gorakhpur vishvavidhyalay main premachand peeth kii sthapana main unka vishesh yogadan rahaa.
 parmanand shrivastav ne hindi kii saahitiyik patrika "aalochana" kaa sampaadan bhi kiya tha. unki gananaa hindi ke sheersh aalochakon main hoti he. varsh 2006 main unhen aalochana ke kshetra main ullekhneey yogadan ke liye unhen ke ke birla foundation dilli tathaa uttar pradesh hindi santhaan lakhanu se do-do puraskaar mili.
