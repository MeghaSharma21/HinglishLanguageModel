 hunaan janavaadee ganraajya china ke dakshin-madhya bhaag main sthit ek praant he. hunaan kaa arth 'jhil se dakshin' hota he, jo is praant kii dongting jhil se dakshin kii sthiti par padaa he. hunaan kii rajdhani changasha shahar he. kyonki shiang nadi is praant kii ek pramukh nadi he isliye is praant ko chini bhavchitron main sankshipt rup se 'shiang' likhaa jaataa he. yah prachinkaal main shaktishaalee chu rajya kaa hissaa tha. hunaan kaa kshetrafal 2,11,800 varg kimi he, yani bharat ke jammu va kashmir rajya se zaraa kam. san 2010 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 6,56,83,722 thi, yani bharat ke jharkhand rajya se zaraa kam. 
 hunaan kaa mausam garam or nam mana jaataa he. sardiyon main barf kabhi-kabhaar hi padati he. mausam main garmi or nami ke kaaran khaanaa jaldi karaab ho jaataa he, isliye hunani khaanaa apane mirch-masaalon ke liye prasiddh he jo ise adhik der tak surakshit rakhte hai. praant main haan chini log bahusankhya hai, haalanki yahan miao, tujiyaa, dong, yaao, bai, hui, jhuang or anya jatiyon ke samudaay bhi rahete hai. janavaadee ganatantr china ke sansthaapak maao xedong kaa janmasthan, shaaoshan shahar, bhi hunaan praant main sthit he.
