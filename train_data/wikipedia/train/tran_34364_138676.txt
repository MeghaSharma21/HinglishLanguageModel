 pancheshwar band uttarakhand rajya main bharat-nepal seemaa par banane vaala bahuuddeshiy bijli pariyojna he. jo uttarakhand ke champavat jile main ban rahaa he. yah pariyojna kaali nadi par banaai ja rahi he.yah pariyojna bharat or nepal kii samuhik pariyojna he jisame bharat kaa share adhik rahegaa.is pariyojna kaa kaary 2018 me suru hona prastaavit he jo 2026 tak puraa ho jayega tathaa 2028 tak isse vidhut utpaadan suru ho jayega.
 is pariyojna main do dame banane prastaavit hai.1. pancheswar pariyojna 2.roopaligad dame 
 yah bharat me banane ke pashchaat sabase bada dam hoga jisaki oonchai 315 meter prastaavit he.yah ek imbankment dam he.isaki utpaadan chhamata 6480 megawatt prastaavit he.
 yah pancheswar dam se 25 km kii duuri par roopaligad ore kaali nadi ke sangam par banegaa jisaki utpaadan chhamata 240 megawatt hogi.
 is prakaar pancheswar dam kii kul utpaadan chhamata 6720 megawatt ho jaaegi.
