 nakkash kii devi - gomti dham bharat desh ke rajasthan rajya ke karauli jila ke prasiddh nagar hindun main sthit ek prasiddh vikhyath mandir he.nakkash kii devi - gomti dham ko hindun city kaa hriday kaha jaataa he. yah hindun city ke madhya main sthit he. yah durga ke ek rup nakkash kii devi kaa mandir he. kaha jaataa he ki jab sant shri gomti das ji maharaj yahan par aae the to unhen raatri ko kaila mata ne swapn main apane ek pipal ke neeche dave hone kii soochna di. agle hi din vahaan khudaai karne par mata kii do chamatkaari murtiyon mili jinhen vahiin sthapit kar mata kaa mandir banwaaya. mandir ke peeche kii taraf param poojya brahmaleen shri shri 1008 gomti das ji maharaj kaa vishalkay mandir unke shishyon dwaara banaaya gaya. jisamen unki samadhi bhi sthit he. yahan par chamatkaari shiv parivaar, panchmukhi hanumanji kii pratimaa, ram mandir, yamraj ji aadi ke mandir sthit hai. yahan par ek vatika sthit he. ise gomti dham ke naam janaa jaataa he. iske ek taraf vishalkay talab jalsen sthit he.
