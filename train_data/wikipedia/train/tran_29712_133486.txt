 raja naharsingh mahal 18veen sadi kaa pracheen mahal he evam apane vaastukala ke liye prasiddh he. yah haryana rajya ke faridabad shahar ke ballabhagarh kshetra main sthit he tathaa iskaa nirmaan jat raja naharsingh ke uttaradhikariyon dwaara kiya gaya tha. is mahal kaa nirmaan kaary 1850 main puraa hua tha. ise ballabhagarh kila mahal ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he or dakshin dilli se 15 kimi kii duuri par sthit he. yahaan ke jat raja naharsingh ne bhartiya swatantrata sangram ne mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai thi. mahal ke mandap or aangan atyant sundar hai evam isaki jhuki hui mehraaben or sundar rup se saje kamare sthaneey itihaas kii jhalak dikhalaate hai. vartmaan main yah ek viraasat sampatti he. mahal ke chaaron ore kai shahari kendra hai. yah rajasi mahal baari sankhya main paryatakon ko aakarshith karata he.
