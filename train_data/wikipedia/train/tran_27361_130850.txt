 bhuwan chandra khanduri jinhen major general bi. si. khanduri ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he, bharat ke uttrakhand rajya kii teesri vidhansabha ke sadsy hai. wo rajnatik dal bhartiya janata party ke sadsy ke rup main uttrakhand vidhansabha kii dhoomakot seat kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai. vah 8 march 2007 se 27 june, 2009 tak uttrakhand rajya ke mukhyamantri the. 11 sitambar, 2011 ko tatkaaleen mukhyamantri ramesh pokharial nishank ke istife ke baad ve waapas uttrakhand ke mukhyamantri bane.
 shri khanduri kaa janm 1934 main dehradun main hua tathaa shiksha elahabaad vishvavidhyalay, sainya abhiyantriki mahavidhyalay pune, instituute of engeers, nai dilli or raksha prabandh santhaan sikandarabad main shiksha praapt kii.
 unhone 1954 se 1990 tak bhartiya sena kii kor of injeeniurs main seva kii. bhartiya sena main vishisht seva ke liye unhe 1982 main raashtrapati dwaara ati vishisht seva medal pradaan kiya gaya.
 sevanivritti ke uparant ve raajnneeti main aae or 1991 tathaa baad ke chunaavon main uttrakhand ke gadhwal kshetra se loksabhaa ke liye nirvaachit hue.
 mananeey atal bihari vaajapeyi ji ke netritv vaali sarkaar main ve sadak, parivahan evam rajmarg maamalon ke rajya mantri rah chuke hai. 2003 main unhe cabinet mantri banaaya gaya, jis par vah may 2004 main rashtriya banaaya gaya, jis par vah may 2004 me rashtriya prajatantric gathbandhan sarkaar ke satta parivartan tak rahe. vah bhartiya janata party ke varisht netaa he. mantri ke rup main unhone bhartiya janata party ke pratishthat vikaas pariyojna ko or tatparta se dakshata rashtriya rajmarg se lagu kiya.
