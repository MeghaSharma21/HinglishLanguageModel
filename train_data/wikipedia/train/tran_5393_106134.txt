 preetam rani sivaach kaa janm 2 oktober 1974 ko gudgaanv ke paas ke gaanv jharsa main hua tha. vah bhartiya mahila hockey team kii purv captain hai or unhone 9 varsh kii umr main hi hockey khelnaa shuru kar diya tha. unhone apni hockey ko pitiai master tara chand ke maargdarshan main jharsa gaanv ke skool main viksit kiya tha. master tara chand or pramukh master raghavendra singh yadav ne hockey ke sarvshereshth khilaadi banane main unki madad kii thi. 2008 main, unhen olympic qualifire ke liye team main shaamil hone ke liye bulaayaa gaya tha taaki unke anubhav se atirikt dhan laa saken. or unki team ko olympic ke liye arhata praapt hone ke baad, sivaach ne ek saakshaatkaar main kaha, kii "ham utane bure bhi nahin the jitne parinaam main dikhaae gaye. vah aakhri baar 2002 ke kaamanwealth games main swarna padak jitate samay hockey khelte hue dikhi. ab vah vishwa cap or rashtramandal khelon ke liye bhartiya mahila hockey team ke saath coach hai. or ab vah academi chalaa rahi he jahaan vah ek coach banane ke liye prashikshan detii he.
