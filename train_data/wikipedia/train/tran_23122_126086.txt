 bandi kii duvidha khel siddhant main ek prakaar kii paristhiti kaa ek prasiddh udhaaharan he jo yah darshata he ki log ek-duusare par bharosa na karne ke kaaran kabhi-kabhi aapas main tab bhi sahyog nahin kar pate jab sahyog karne se donon kaa hi spasht laabh ho rahaa ho or asahyog se donon kii spasht haani ho rahi ho.
 kisi chori karne ke ilzaam par police do vyaktiyon ko giraftaar kar leti he lekin uske paas inke khilaaf koi saboot nahin he. unhen ek duusare se alag karke un donon se police alag-alag kahati he ki -
 iskaa saar ek talika main aise likhaa ja saktaa he:
 is khel main, agar yah lage kii doosraa bandi swaarthee he to swayan bhi kudagarz jo janaa chahie or dhokhaa de dena chahie, varanaa khud ko hi 1 varsh kii jail ho sakati he. lekin agar donon bandi hi yah karne lage to donon ko hi 3 maheene kii jail hogi. is se bhale to vah donon hi chup rahakar the, kyonki tab unhen keval 1 maheene kii jail hoti. yah udhaaharan darshata he ki haalanki donon ko aapasi sahyog karne main apane swaarth ke anusaar bhi nijee laabh he phir bhi vah ek-duusare par bharosa na hone ke kaaran asahyog karne par majaboor ho jate hai.
