 thodi yaa thudi manushyon ke chehre main hont ke neeche vaali haddi ko kehete hai. yah chehre kaa sabase nichalaa bhaag hota he. manushyon ko bolne ke liye honton ko hilaanaa padataa he or uske liye snaayuon kii aavashyaktaa padati he. hothon ke yah snaayu thodi kii haddi se lage hote hai jisase manushya ko hont hilaane main suvidha hoti he. isi kaaran se thodi viksit huyi or ham, yani aaj ke maanav, apane aadim purvajon se chehre kii rup-rekha main thodi ke maamle main kuch bhinn hai.
