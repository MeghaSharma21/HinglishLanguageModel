 sasta sahitya mandal, bharat kaa ek pramukh pustak prakaashak sanstha he. is sanstha kii sthapana san 1925 main mahatma gandhi kii prerana se hui jisamen jamanalaal bajaj evam ghanashyamdaas birla kii pramukh bhoomika thi. yah ek dharmarth sanstha he jo bina laabh kamaaye kaam karti he. iskaa mukhya uddeshy kam mulya par ucchstareey sahitya kaa prakaashan karnaa he. mandal ne ab tak lagbhag 2000 se adhik pustakon kaa prakaashan kiya he. armbh main iske kaaryon kaa kendra ajmer tha kintu 1934 main yah dilli aa gaya tha. is prakaashan ne jo sabase pehali pustak chhaapi vah gandhiji dwaara rachit "dakshin africa main satyagrah kaa itihaas" thi.
 mandal dwaara prakashit pustaken anekanek vishyon - itihaas, arthshaastra, nitishastra, samajashaastra, mahapurushon kii jivaniyaan aadi hoti hai. bahut si pustaken gandhiji evam gandhi-sahitya se sambandhit hoti hai. iske kai prakashnon ko bharat ke kendriya sarkaar yaa rajya sarkaron se puraskaar bhi mil chuke hai.
 sasta sahitya mandal koi saadhaaran prakaashak nahin he. isaki sthapana ke peeche vahi tatv sakriy the jinhone aage chalakar azaadi kii ladaai main bad-chadhakar hissaa liya. gandhiji ke anany anuyaayi or unke paanchaven putr ke naam se vikhyath seth jamanalaal bajaj iske mukhya prerak the or ghanashyamdaas birla, haribhaau upaadhyaay, viyogi hari aadi iske sakriy sahyogi the. 
 sasta sahitya mandal ne hindi kii stareey kintu atyant sasti pustaken chhapakar hindi sahitya evam hindibhashi janata kaa upkaar kiya he. isane anya bhashaaon kaa uttam sahitya bhi anuwaad karke hindi main uplabdh karaayaa he. iske is kaary kii bhuri-bhuri prashansa hui he.
