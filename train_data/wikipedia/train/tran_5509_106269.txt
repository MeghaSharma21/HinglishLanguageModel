 methodism protestant isiyat se sambandhit dharmik aandolan he. yah anekanek naamon vaali sansthaaon ke madhyam se chalaaya ja rahaa he. inkaa daawa he ki iske lagbhag 7 karod anuyai hai. methadist vishwa bhar main failaa hua he. britain ke atirikt vah pradhanthya canada, dakshin africa tathaa austrailia main failaa hua he.
 ek anglican padri jaan wesli ke netritv main methodism kaa pravarttan hua tha. unhone san 1729 i0 main apane bhai charles tathaa kuch anya saathiyon ke saath oxfard ke vidhyaarthiyon ke liye 'holi club' naamak sanstha banaai. is club ke sadsy ekatra hokar bible padhate, upwas karte, janata ko upadesh dete or bimaaron tathaa kaidiyon se bhent karne jate the. logo ne uphaas main 'holi club' ke sadasyon kaa naam methadist rakhaa tha. kintu wesli ne swany usee naam ko apanaaya. pranrabh me ve anglican girjagharon main pravachan kiya karte thea. kintu unka sudhaar andolan badhata gaya or methodist sosayadi ke rup main anglican carch se alag ho gaya. 18veen shatabdi ke uttarardh main methadist ko america main badi safalataa mili. aaj kal vahaan kaa carch mahatvapurn ban gaya he.
