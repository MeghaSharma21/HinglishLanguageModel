 pilkhuva hapur kaa ek kasbaa he.pilkhuva qasbaa pehle ghaziabad jile kaa bhaag tha magar san 2011 main tatkaaleen basapa sarkaar ke kaarykaal main ise hapur jile main shaamil kar diya gaya. pilkhuva G.T. road par dilli se 45 kilometre purv main sthit he yahan kii aabaadi ek lakh se adhik he iske purv main hapur, pashchim main ghaziabad, uttar main modinagar v dacchhin main bulandshahr sthit he. yah nagar dilli-muradabad railway line par sthit he v jila mukhyalay se 10 kilometre kii duuri par he vartmaan main pilkhuva dhaulana vidhaan sabha v ghaziabad loksabhaa kshetra kaa hissaa he
 itihaas -- pilkhuva qasbaa san 1700 isvi main yahan rahane vaali muslim chhipi birdari ke purvajon dwaara basaaya gaya, san 1700 isvi main kalanur se aakar based natthu singh rana ke do putr kalyaan singh va nihaal singh pilkhuva main kankhali jhil ke kinare aabaad hue, in do bhaaiyon kii santane chapai kaa kaam karne kii vajah se chhipi kehalaati he. kuch samay ke baad lala ganga sahay naam ke ek vyakti yahan aakar bas gaye or inaki santan bhi yahan badi taadaad main rahati he, phir aahista- aahista anya log bhi aate gaye or kaaravaan banataa gaya.
 pehle yahan kii aabaadi kankhali jhil ke aas paas hi hua karti thi or mohalla gadhi ek alag basti thi magar badhate badhate do-teen gaanv rampura, pabla, jathpura or mohalla gadhi bhi pilkhuva main hi samaa gaye ! aaj ke samay main pilkhuva kii aabaadi N.H.-24 ke donon taraf babbti ja rahi he.
 pilkhuva bharat kii hathkargha or chaape hue kapadey kii sabase badi mandiyon main se ek he, pilkhuva main khaas taur se kapadey kii bunai, chapai, rangai, dhulaai, silaai aadi kaa hi kaarobaar hota he or yahan kii zyaadaatar aabaadi isi kaarobaar se judy he I pilkhuva kaa bana hua kapada bharat ke kone kone main bheje jaataa he, yahan kii hand blaak print kii chaadaren bharat ke bahaar export bhi kii jaati hai,
 pilkhuva pehle nagar palika hua karti thi magar ab ek nagar parishad he, is vaqt pilkhuva nagar parishad main 25 ward hai, pilkhuva nagar parishad rajmarg N.H.-24 par sthit ek shandaar imaarat main maujuud he jo ki nagar parishad kii hi sampatti he,
 pilkhuva main desh kii badi badi transport companiyaan maujuud hai, iname zyaadaatar ke karyaalay railway road par sthit hai haalaanki hapur pilkhuva vikaas praadhikaran dwaara N.H.-24 par transport nagar banaaya janaa bhi prastaavit he magar abhi us par kaam chal rahaa he, filhal sabhi transport companiyaan shahar main maujuud hai.
 pilkhuva ke aas paas bahut se gaanv sthit he or in gaanvon ke adhiktar log kheti baadi hi karte hai. iname kuch pramukh gaanv hai ------- sikhida, haval, madapur, kherpur khayrabad, dahpa, kamalpur, azampur, partapur, mirapur, jathpura, shamali, atarauli, mukimpur, duhari, chhijaarsi,kalaunda, kheda, galnd.
