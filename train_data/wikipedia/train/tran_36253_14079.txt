 konkana sen sharma, kaa janm 3 disambar 1979 ko hua tha, vah ek bhartiya abhinetri hai. vah film nirmaataa or abhinetri aparnaa sen kii beti hai. sharma mukhyatya bhartiya kalagrihon or swatantra filmon main dikhti hai or apane kshetra main unki uplabdhiyon ne unhen samakaleen samanaantar cinema main ek utkrisht abhinetri ke rup main sthapit kar diya he.
 inhone film indira se ek bal kalakaar ke rup main shuruyaat kii thi, konkana ne ek vayask abhinetri ke rup main shuruyaat bengali film ek je aache kanya se kii. pehali baar angreji film mistar and mises ayyar dwaara logon kaa dhyaan unki ore gaya, is film kaa nirdesan unki maan ne kiya tha or is film main apane abhinay ke liye unhen sarvshereshth abhinetri kaa rashtriya film puraskaar bhi mila. inhen naatakeey film pej 3 ke dwaara darshakon ke bich vyapak pahchaan mili or tab se vah kai filmon main abhinay kar chuki hai, jiname se adhikansh ke liye unhen vyavsaayik safalataa se adhik aalochanatmak prasansha mili. unhen film omkara or life in e metro ke liye lagaataar do baar sarvshereshth sahaayak abhinetri kaa filmphere puraskaar mila. omkara main apane abhinay ke liye unhen duusari baar sarvshereshth sahaayak abhinetri kii shrenee main rashtriya puraskaar mila.
 konkana, mukul sharma or aparnaa sen kii beti hai. unki ek badi bahan, kamalini chatterjee, bhi hai. konkana ke naanaa, chidanand dasgupta, ek film aalochak, vidwaan, professor, lekhak or kalakatta film socity ke sahasansthaapak hai. unki swargeey daadi, supriya dasgupta, prasidd aadhunik bengali kavi jibananand das kii chacheri bahan hai.
 konkana ke paas dilli ke saint stephen college se angreji main degree he, jo unhen 2001 main mili thi. vah modern hai skool for girls, kalakatta or kalakatta internashnal skool kii chaatra thim.
 konkana ne film indira se ek bal kalakaar ke rup main shuruyaat kii thi. san 2000 main unhone vayask abhinetri ke rup main bengali film ek je aache kanya se shuruyaat kii, jisame unhone ne ek nakaaratmak charitra nibhaayaa tha. iske baad unhone rituparno ghosh kii bahuprashansit film titali main mithun chakraborti or apni maan aparnaa sen ke vipareet ek bhoomika nibhaai.
 jab unhone apni maan dwaara nirdeshit angreji film mistar and mises ayyar main kaam kiya to vah ek bahucharchit chahare ke rup main jaani jane lageen. is film ne multiplexon main bahut acha kaam kiya or aalochanatmak drushti se bhi bahut safal rahi. ek tamil gruhani ke rup main konkana kaa abhinay is bhashaa ke uccharan main unki praveenta kii bahut prashansa huyi or iske liye unhen sarvshereshth abhinetri kaa rashtriya film puraskaar bhi mila. baad main filmphere ke 2010 ke sanskaran main "top 80 ikonic parformenses" main unke abhinay ko bhi sthaan mila.

 iske baad unki agali rashtriya film puraskaar jeetne vaali film pej 3 aayi. ek chatur patrkaar kii bhoomika ke liye unki prashansa kii gayi or vah cinema jane vaale darshakon ke liye ek parichit cheharaa ban gayin.
 konkana ko mira nair kii hollywood film da nemsek main sheersh bhoomika kaa prastaav mila tha, lekin film 15 park avenu ke saath tarikhon ke takaraane ke kaaran vah is film ko sweekaar nahin kar sakin. lekin unhone 15 park avenu main ek maansik rup se biimaar mahila kii bhoomika or omkara main ek madhyavay gramin mahila kii prashansaneey bhoomika dwaara isaki bharpai kar lee. omkara ke liye unhen filmphere kii ore se sarvshereshth sahaayak abhinetri or rashtriya film puraskaar kii ore se bhi sarvshereshth sahaayak abhinetri kaa puraskaar mila. unki agali film Deadline: Sirf 24 Ghante ko ausat pratikriya mili. 2006 main, konkana ne kaala ghoda film samaaroh ke liye ek 18 minute lambi bengali laghu film namkorone ke dwaara nirdesan kii pahal kii.
 iske baad konkana ne rituparno ghosh ke bengali kala film dosar main abhinay kiya, jiskaa pratham pradarshan kai antarrashtriya film samarohon main kiya gaya. is film ke liye unhen mahindra indo-american art counsil film samaaroh main sarvshereshth abhinetri kaa puraskaar diya gaya.
 2007 main aayi unki pehali film madhur bhandarkar ke saath unka doosraa sahakaarya tha, ek vidha film jiskaa naam trephic signal tha, jisame unhone galiyon main rahane vaali ek veshya kii bhoomika nibhaai thi.2007 main aayi unki duusari film anuraag basu kii life in e... metro thi, ise acchhee samiksha mili thi or bhartiya box offis par bhi iskaa pradarshan acha tha. is film ne mumbai ke alag alag logon ke jivan kaa chithran kiya tha or konkana kii bhoomika ek jawan or asurakshit mahila kii thi jiske liye unhen filmphere puraskaar bhi mila.
 2007 ke aakhir main, konkana ne yashraj film binar ke antargat do filmon main abhinay kiya. unhone is donon filmon ke prati apane utsaah kaa ullekh kiya kyunki yah pehali aisi filmein thim jisame unhen gane ke bol par apane hont chalaane the. pehali film, laagaa chunari main daag, jo ki pradeep sarkaar dwaara nirdeshit thi, main unhone banaras kii ek chote shahar kii jawan ladki chutki kii bhoomika kii thi, jisame vah rani mukherjee ke saath thim. bharat main yah film aalochanatmak or vyavsaayik drushti se asafal rahi, haalaanki unke kaam kii prashansa kii gayi. duusari film aajaa nach lai thi, jiskaa prachaar vyapak str par maadhuri dikshit kii vaapasi ke film ke rup main kiya gaya tha. is film ne bhi acha kaam nahin kiya. sienen- ibn ke rajeev masand ne is film main unke abhinay kaa ullekh karte hue kaha ki "...adbhud se kam nahin he. unki sabase badi shakti yah he ki vah bevakoof ban jane ke dar se ghabraati nahin hai or vah is baat kii bhi chintaa nahin karti ki log unpar hasenge. isi ke parinamswaroop aajaa nach lai main unka abhinay bebaak or swaabhaavik he.
 2008 main sensharma ne dil kabddi main abhinay kiya. unhone ek laghu film main bhi kaam kiya. jiskaa nirdesan mira nair ne at naamak film pariyojna ke liye kiya tha or ise cinemagharon main jari karne se pehle 2008 ke kai film samarohon main dikhaaya gaya tha.
 2009 main vah ek kam bajet kii angreji film da president ij kaming main dikhaai padin, iskaa nirdesan kunal roy kapur ne kiya tha. film kii shuruyaat sakaaratmak aalochana ke saath huyi. samiksha konkana ke paksh main di gayi thi. da times of india se nikhaat kaazmi ne likhaa,"abhinay kii drushti se, vah tanavagrast or jatilta grast sushri konkana hai jo prashansa le udati hai or jab film apane desipan kii sabhy khilli udaati he tab vah bhi saath main hansatii hai."
 iske baad konkana ne joya akhtar kii film lack bai chance main farhaan akhtar ke vipareet kaam kiya. jari hone ke baad, film ko aalochakon kii or se atyant sakaaratmak pratikriya mili or saath hi unke abhinay ko bhi, lekin box offis par film ko pratyaashit safalataa nahin mili. konkana kii haalia pradarshit film 2009 main ayaan mukherjee kii romani hasya film vek ap cid he, jisame unhone ranbir kapur ke saath kaam kiya he. jari hone par film ko har taraf se sakaaratmak pratikriya mili or unke abhinay kii atyadhik prashansa kii gayi. bollywood hangaamaa ke taran adarsh ne likhaa," konkana moolbhoot rup se swaabhaavik hai or sabase acchhee baat yah he ki, unka abhinay prayasarhit he." yah is avishwasaneey adaakara kii taraf se ek or aakarshak pradarshan he. da new yark times ne likhaa,"sushri sharma ne aisha jaise charitra ko vishisht bana diya he: jo ek swatantra shahari ladki he, jiskaa sapana karier or prem donon ko paanaa he." unka dwaara nibhaai gayi bhoomika aisha ek sookshm bhedi rachana he- mahatvakankshi, samvedi, vishwasaneey- or apane nirdesan kii pahal main mistar mukherjee ke dwaara konkana ko film main swachhandata dene kaa nirnay bilkul uchit he."
 2010 main konkana ne ashvini dheer kii hasya film atithi tum kab jaaoge main ajay devgan or paresh rawal ke vipareet kaam kiya. unki haalia pradarshit film, niraj pathak kii rhite yaan raang he jisame unhone ek vakil kii bhoomika kii he.
 unhone rituparno ghosh kii hasya film sanglass or vinay shukla kii mirch kaa filmakan bhi puraa kar liya he. sitambar 2009 tak, konkana aparnaa sen kii iti mrinalini main sheersh bhoomika kar rahi thim, jo june 2010 main pradarshit hone vaali thi. vah amitabh verma kii anaam film main ranvir shouri ke vipareet pratik babbar or vidya baalan ke saath kaam karengi, yah film sabyasachi dwaara nirdeshit pehali or basu chatterjee kii duusari film hogi. konkana ko aditya chopra kii agali filmamumbai main arjuna rampal, shahid kapur, rani mukherjee or esha dyol ke saath liya gaya tha, jo ki mumbai ke vishwa prasidd 2008 aatankwaadi hamale par aadhaarit he.
 june 2009 main, konkana ne pehali baar atul kumaar ke da blue mag main rajat kapur, vinay pathak, ranvir shouri or shiba chaddha ke saath manch par abhinay kiya. 2010 main is naatak ne desh main or videsh main daura kiya.
 konkana abhinayta ranvir shouri ke saath prem sambandh main hai. july 2008 main, aparnaa sen ne is tathy kii pushti kii kii unki beti ranvir shouri ke prati vachanabaddh hai.
 2005 main, konkana ko radiff ke "bollywood sheersh mahila kalakaar" main 11waan sthaan praapt hua. baad main 2006 main unhen 9waan sthaan praapt hua.
