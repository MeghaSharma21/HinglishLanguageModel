 hasan ruhani, jinka janm ke samay naam hasan feridon tha, ek irani raajnneetijna, vaqeel, vidwaan va kootneetijna hai. inhen june 2013 main iran kaa aglaa raashtrapati chunaa gaya. 1999 se ve iran kii mahiron kii sabha, 1991 se iran kii prashasnic nigaraanee parishad, 1989 se sarvocch rashtriya suraksha parishad or 1992 se kootneeti anusandhan kendra ke sadsy rahe hai.
 7 may 2013 ko ruhani ne iran ke raashtrapati chunaavon main apane aap ko ek ummidavaar ke rup main darj karavaaya. unhone kaha ki agar ve chune gaye to ek 'naagarik adhikaron kaa ghoshnaptr' taiyaar karenge, desh kii aarthik sthiti majboot karenge or pashchimi deshon ke saath sambandhon main sudhaar laane kii koshish karenge. 15 june ko unhone mohammad bager galibaaf or chaar anya ummidavaaron ko haraa kar chunaav jeet liya. krmanusaar ab unki 3 agast 2013 main raashtrapati pad grahan karne kii taiyaari he.
