 selangore malaysia kaa ek rajya he. yah malay prayadweep ke pashchimi tat par sthit he. selangore kaa kshetra kuala lampur or putrajay ke sangheey kshetron ko ghere hue he, jo kabhi isi rajya kaa hissaa hua karte the. sakal gharelu utpaada ke hisaab se selangore kii arthvyavastha puure malaysia kii arthvyavastha kii 23% he or yah anya kisi bhi rajya se adhik he. is rajya kii jansankhya bhi kisi anya rajya se adhik he or jivan str sabase unchaa he.
 malaysia main alag-alag rajyon ke puraane rajpariwaron ko sanvaidhanik darja mila hua he. selangore kaa sanvaidhanik adhyaksh sultan kehalaata he haalanki is pad ke saath koi vaastavik prashasnic adhikaar nahin aate. malaysia main kuch rajyon ko aupchaarik arabi bhashaa ke naam bhi diye jate hai or selangore kaa naam "selangore daar-ul-ehsaan" he, yani "selangore, ehsaanon kaa ghar".
