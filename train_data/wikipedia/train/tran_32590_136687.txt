 ganit main anupaat samaan prakaar kii do sankhyaon ke bich sambandh ko kehete hai. praayah ise "a sambandh b" yaa a:b kehete hai. udhaaharan ke liye yadi do pedo kii unchaaiyon kaa anupaat 3:5 he to iskaa arth yah he ki yadi pehle ped kii oonchai 3 meter he to duusare kii oonchai 5 meter hogi. athwa pehle kii unchaai 9 meter ho to duusare kii 15 meter hogi.
