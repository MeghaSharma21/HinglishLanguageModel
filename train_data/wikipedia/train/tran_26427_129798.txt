 battagram, jise battagram bhi uccharit kiya jaataa he, pakistan ke kaibar-pakhtuunakva praant kaa ek zila he. yah kohistaan zile ke dakshin main, manasehara zile ke uttar main or shangala zile ke purv main sthit he. isaki sarahadein tor gar​ naamak zile se bhi lagati hai jo 28 janvari 2011 tak qabaili kshetra hua karata tha.
 'battagram' sanskrit ke 'bhatta gram' kaa ek rup he or is zile ke naam kaa matlab 'brahman gaanv' he. is zile main bahut se anya gaanv-qasabon ke naam hindu jatiyon or sanskrit shabdon par pade hai, masalan chohan, jesole bazaar, ajmera, chappar​gram or baniyaan .
 battagram zile main san 1998 main 3,07,278 logon ki aabaadi thi. iskaa kshetrafal qareeb 1,301 varg kimi he. 1993 tak yah mansehara zile kaa hissaa tha, lekin july 1993 main ise ek alag zile kaa darja de diya gaya. isaki rajdhani bhi battagram naam kaa shahar hi he. is puure kshetra main pashto or hindko boli jaati he. battagram zile kii do tahseelein hai - allaai tehsil or battagram tehsil. oktober 2005 ke jabardast zalazale kaa is kshetra par baari asar padaa tha lekin raahat kaaryon se halaat main bahut sudhaar ho paaya he. is kshetra main kuch degree college bhi hai.
