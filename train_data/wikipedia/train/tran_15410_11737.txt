 pastivirus flaviviride parivaar main vishaanuon kaa ek jinas he. jo vishaanuain pastivirus ke jinas main hai vah stanadhariyon, bovide parivaar ke sadsy or suide parivaar ko sankramit karte hai. vartmaan main is jinas main chaar jati hai bovain viral dast virus 1 ke prakaar jati ko samet. is jinas se sambandhit rog: rakthasravi syndrome, garbhpaat, ghaatak shlashmik rog. 
 chaa, golakaar, vyas main lagbhag 50 enem. praudh virian main capcid protein ke alaava teen virus-incoded jhilli protein bhi shaamil hai. jinom rekhik or gaiyr-khandon, lambaai main lagbhag 12 kebi he.
 pastivirus vishaanuon main sakaaratmak-bhaavana arene ki ek lard he. vah 12.5 kilobase lambi he. kabhi-kabhi virian main jaanavar ke jinom ke anubhag maujuud hote hai jo kii pratilipi kiye gaye hai, haalanki yah aam taur par nahin hota. jinom ke tisare ant par koi paali-e nahin he. iskaa matlab he in vishaanuon main koi post-transcriptional ashodhan nahin hai or saral arene jinom hai. jinom main arene donon sanrachanaatmak or gaiyr-sanrachanaatmak kaa kutalekhan karne shaamil he. pastivirus kaa aanvik jeev vigyaan or maanav hepasivirson main kaaphi samantaayein or visheshtaayen hai. jinom sanghathan or anuwaad ranneeti dono peedi ke liye atyadhik samaan hai. bividivi ke liye baar-baar nonhomologs arene punarsangyojan aayojan anuvanshik rup se alag vishaanuon kii upasthiti hoti he jo mezbaan ke liye jaanaleva hai. 
 pastivirus austrailia main bade paimaane par he, khaskar pashu main. kuch vayask maveshi is rog se mukt hai jabaki kuch jivan bhar iske vaahak hai. pastivirus ke tike maujuud hai or tike kaa sahi lard dena chahie. yah tikaa pratirohak kshamta banaae rakhane ke liye saamaanya taur par dena chahie.
