 yajyayag main yajmaan ko shrautakarm karaanevaala vyaktivishesh rutvij kehalaata he. rutvijon kii sankhya main karmo ke anusaar paryaapt bhinnata he. agnihotri ke ghar par praat: or siankal home karanevaala ruthvij ek hi hota he, parantu darsh ishti main tathaa paurnmaas ishti main chaar rutvij hote hai jinke naam hai - adhvaryu, hota, brahma or aagnidhra. chaturmasya yag main in chaaron ke atirikt ""pratiprasthaata"" adhik hota he or pashuyag main ""maitravarun"" naamak chatha rutvij bhi hota he. agnyadhan main purvokt chaar rutvijon ke saath "udgaata"" naamak paanchavaan rutvij bhi hota he. agnishthom aadi somyag main 16 rutvij hote hai. ""sadsy"" naamak 17vaan rutvij somyag main pratyaksh bhaag na lekar "sad" naamak mandap main baitha rahata he. aashvalyan grihyasutra main rutvij ke chunane kii vidhi di gai he jinamein rutvij ke sarvangpurn, shashakt or tarun hone kaa spasht aagrah he. yah to hui shrautakarm kii baat. smart yagyon main bhi havan karne tathaa shantividhan ke liye rutvij chunaa jaataa he. is prakaar vaidic yagyon kaa nishpaadan rutvijon kii vidya, buddhi tathaa karmanishtha kaa sammilit fal hota he.
