 lipid dwiparat yaa phospholipid dwiparat lipid anuon kii do paraton kii bani ek jhilli hoti he. yah lagbhag sabhi jeevon kii koshikaon ko chadar kii tarah lapete rahati he. iskaa kaam hanikaarak rasyanon ko baahar rakhana or laabhadaayak rasyanon ko bheetar laana hota he. is dweeparat ke chhidr keval kuch nanometer chaude hi hote hai. yah dhruviya hoti he yani ] ko baahar rakhane or kanon ko unke vidyutiya lakshanon ke aadhaar par andar-baahar jane kii anumati detii he. koshika ke alaava koshika ke andar ke kai anshon ko bhi unki apni lipid dwiparat ne dhakaa hua hota he.
 lipid yogic aksar ubhaysanvedi hote hai, yani unka ek bhaag jalsnehi or ek bhaag jalavirohi hota he. jab unhen kisi jaleey vaataavaran main daalaa jaae to vah do parat kii chaadaron main vyavasthit ho jate hai, jisamen. jalsnehi sir baahar kii taraf or jalavirohi dumen andar kii ore hoti hai. yah dweeparat sharir main kai sthanon par prayog hoti he, masalan koshikaon kaa bahari khol isi se bani jhilli kaa nirmit hota he.
