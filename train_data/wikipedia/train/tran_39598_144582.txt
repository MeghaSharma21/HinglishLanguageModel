 malto yaa pahadiya purv bharat ke bihar, jharkhand, pashchim bengal va urisa rajyon or bangladesh ke kuch chote kshetron main boli jane vaali ek uttari dravid bhashaa he. isaki kumarbag pahadiya or sauria pahadiya naamak do upbhashaayen hai jinhen kuch bhashavaigyanik do sambandhit lekin swatantra bhinn bhashaayen samajhate hai. kumarbag pahadiya jharkhand or pashchim bengal tathaa urisa ke kuch simit kshetron main, jabaki sauria pahadiya bihar or pashchim bengal main tathaa bangladesh ke simit ilaaqon main boli jaati hai. in donon ke shabdon main 80% kii saamanta maapi gai he. malto bharat kii sabase uttari dravid bhashaa he.
