 kunduz afghanistan kaa ek praant he jo us desh ke uttar-purv main sthit he. is praant kaa kshetrafal 8,040 varg kimi he or isaki aabaadi san 2009 main lagbhag 8.2 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. is praant kii rajdhani kunduz shahar he. is praant main tajik or pashtun logon kii bahutayat he, lekin yahan bahut uzbek log bhi rahete hai. isaki uttari seemaa tajikistan se lagati he. kunduz nadi yahan kaa ek mahatvapurn jal-strot he or yah amu dariyaa kii ek upanadi he. yahi amu dariyaa kunduz praant or uske uttar main sthit tajikistan ke bich kii antarrashtriya seemaa bhi he. kunduz praant pashchim main mazaar-e-shareef tathaa dakshin main kabul se sadak dwaara judaa hua he. iske alaava yah tajikistan se bhi sadak dwaara sanyukt he.
 kunduz praant main 7 zile he. san 2001 main taliban ke patan ke samay yah afghanistan kaa sabase aakhri shahar tha jisane samarpan kiya. hazaron pakistani sainik, karmchaariyon tathaa kai al-qaayada or taliban ke sadasyon ko pakistani vaayu sena dwaara surakshit tareeke le uthaa liya gaya tha. is ghatana ko shaitan kaa utthan kii sangya mili thi.
