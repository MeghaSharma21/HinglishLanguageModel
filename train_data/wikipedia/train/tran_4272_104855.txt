 janet yellen america kii ek suprasiddh arthshaastri hai jinhen america raashtrapati baraak obama ne oktober 2013 main fedral rigerv ke board of governor ke adhyaksh ke taur par namankit kiya he. abhi is pad par ben barnanke karyrat hai, jinka kaarykaal 31 janvari 2014 ko samaapt ho rahaa he. vartmaan main janet is sanstha ke upaadhyakshon main se ek hai. 
 is avasar par obama ne unhen america ke 'sabase mahatvapurn arthshaastriyon or niti nirmaataaon' main se ek bataaya. abhi seenet dwaara is namakan kaa anumodan baaki he. yadi aisa ho jaataa he to vah pramukh banker kaa pad grahan karne vaali desh kii pehali mahila hongi.
 isse pehle ve fedral rigerv of san francisco kii president va mukhya kaaryakari adhikari bhi rah chuki hai. raashtrapati bill clinton ke samay main ve white house kii aarthik salaahkaar samiti kii pramukh thim. unhone california vishvavidhyalay tathaa barkale ke haas skool of bijnes main adhyaapan kaary bhi kiya he.
