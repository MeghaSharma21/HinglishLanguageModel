 ahirwada madhya bharat yaa aadhunik madhya pradesh main parvati or betwa nadiyon ke bich sthit ek aitihaasik kshetra he. ahirwada rajya bhilsa or jhaansi shaharon ke bich sthit tha.
 aabhir aksar saurashtra ke kshatrap shilaalekh main ullekith hote rahe hai. puraanon or brihadsanhita ke anusaar samudragupta kaal main aabhir dakshini kshetra main shaasha the. jo dhire-dhire bharat ke vibhinn bhaagon main fail gaye. baad kii taarikh main inhone madhya pradesh main ahirwada par kabja kar liya. samvath 918 ke jodhpur shilaalekh se spasht he ki rajasthan main bhi unka kabja tha. shilaalekh ke anusaar aabhir apane hinsak aacharan kii vajah se, apane padosiyon ke liye ek aatank the.
 aurangzeb ke shasankaal main, ahirwada khinchi vansh ke raja dhiraj singh ke shashan ke adheen aa gaya, jo zyaadaatar samay ke liye ahir vidroh ko dabaane or vyavastha behaal karne main vyast rahe.
 puuranamal 1714-1716 ke dauraan malwa kshetra ke ek ahir shaasha the.
 1714 main, jaipur ke raja jaisingh, malwa main vikaar dabaane main safal ho gaye. afgaan dangaiyon ne ahir netaa puuranamal kii madad se sironj par niyantran sthapit kar liya. ahir desh apane netaa puranmal ke netratv main angrez sarkaar se vidroh ke liye khadaa ho gaya, unhone sironj se kalabag ke liye sadakon ko band kar diya or ranod or indore ke apane gadhon se sarkaar ko pareshaan karnaa jari rakhaa. raja jai singh april 1715 main phir sironj pahunche or afgaan sena ko parajeet kiya. parantu jai singh dwaara sthapit yah shanti zyada din tak nahin tik paayi or november 1715 main puuranamal ahir ne malpur main nae sire se lut-paat shuru kar di. rohila, girussia, bhil, ahir va anya hindu rajshahi malwa main chaaron ore se vidroh hetu khadey ho gaye va sthiti ko niyantrit karne kii sarkaar kii har koshish ko naakaam kar diya.
