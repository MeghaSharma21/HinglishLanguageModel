 kos minar kaa prayog sher shah suri ke jamaane main sadakon ko niyamit antaraal par chinhit karne ke liye kiya jaataa tha.. grand trunk road ke kinare har kos par minaaren banavaai gai thim. adhiktar inhen 1556-1707 ke bich banaaya gaya tha. kai minaaren aaj bhi surakshit hai tathaa inhen dilli-ambala rajmarg par dekha ja saktaa he. puraatatv vibhaag ke anusaar bharat ke haryana rajya main kul 49 kos minaare he jinamein faridabad main 17, sonipat main 7, panipat main 5, karnal main 10, kurukshetra or ambala main 9 evam rohtak main 1 minar hai. aajkal inhen surakshit smarak ghoshit kiya gaya he tathaa puraatatv vibhaag inaki dekharekh karata he.
