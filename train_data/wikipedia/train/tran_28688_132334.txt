 club atletico de madrid, S.A.D., saamanytah atletico de madrid, yaa atletico ke rup main janaa, l lig main khelte hai jo madrid main sthit ek spanish footbal club he. atletico 1996 main ek dabl sahit nau avasaron or das par kopa del re, par laa liga jeet liya he, europe main, ve 1962 main europea cap vinurs cap jeeta 1974 main europea cap upavijeta rahe the, 1975 main intercantinental cap jeeta, 2010 main or 2012 main europa lig jeeta or 2010 or 2012 main UEFA super cap jeeta. 
 ve vicent kalderon stadium main apane ghar ke khel khilne, jo vartmaan main 54,960 darshakon ke liye upar rakhti he. 2015 main, atletico 70,000 kii kshamta he, jo hoga estadio laa peinet ke apane nae ghar, ko sthanantarit karne ke kaaran kar rahe hai.
 atletico madrid ke prashansakon ke aitihaasik ganraajya kaa bachaav kiya he or spain ke andar club se judaa hua he. apane itihaas ke dauraan, club puraane jamaane gadde ke rup main ek hi rang kii ja rahi apni pehali team dhariyon ke kaaran los cholchhoneros, sahit upnaam kii ek sankhya se gyaat kiya gaya he. club real madrid ke saath aitihaasik pratidwandvita he.
