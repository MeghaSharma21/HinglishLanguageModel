 viki loves monuments: viki smarakon ko pyaar karata he sitambar ke maheene ke dauraan yojit ek vaarshik antarrashtriya photographic pratiyogita he, jo duniya bhar main sthaneey vickemedia sahyogiyon kii sahaayta se vikipedia samudaay ke sadasyon dwaara duniya bhar main yojit kii jaati he. pratibhaagi apane kshetra main sthaneey aitihaasik smarakon or viraasat sthalon kii tasviren lete hai, or unhen vickemedia comons par aplod karte hai. ghatana kaa uddeshy bhaag lene vaale deshon kii viraasat sthalon ko hilite karnaa he taaki logon ko in smarakon kii tasviren capchar karne ke liye protsaahit kiya ja sake or unhen ek muft license ke tahat rakhaa ja sake, jise na keval vikipedia main balki har jagah har kisi ke dwaara phir se upyog kiya ja saktaa he.
 pehla viki smarak pratiyogita 2010 main netherland main payalat pariyojna ke rup main yojit kiya gaya tha. agle varsh yah europe ke anya deshon main fail gaya or ginis book of records ke anusaar, viki loves smarakon ke 2011 sanskaran ne sabase badi photography pratiyogita ke liye vishwa record tod diya. 2012 main, kul 35 bhaag lene vaale deshon ke saath pratispardhaa europe se aage badha di gai thi. viki ke dauraan 2012 smarakon ko pyaar karata he, aitihaasik smarakon kii 350,000 se adhik tasviren 15,000 se adhik pratibhaagiyon dwaara aplod kii gai thim. 2013 main, viki pyaar smarak pratiyogita antarctic samet chah mahadwipon main yojit kii gai thi or duniya bhar ke pachaas se adhik deshon kii aadhikarik bhagidaari thi. dablulem kaa 2016 sanskaran unesco dwaara samarthit tha or 43 deshon ke 10,700 pratiyogiyon ko dekha jinhone 277,000 photo jamaa kiye..
