 prem kumaar dhumal himachal pradesh ke purv mukhyamantri he. ve rajnatik dal bhartiya janata party ke sadsy ke rup main himachal pradesh vidhansabha kii hamirpur seat kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai.
 prem kumaar dhumal 10 april 1944 ko gaanv samirpur jila hamirpur main paidaa hue. inaki praarambhik shiksha middle skool bhagawada main hui or metric diavi hai skool toni devi, jila hamirpur se kii. 1970 main inhone doaba kalege jalandhar main eme english main sarvocch sthaan praapt kiya. inhone punjab, vishvavidhyalay main pravaktaa ke rup main kaary kiya. tatpashchaat doaba kalege jalandhar chale gaye. naukari karte hue inhone elelbi kiya. 1984 main inhone sansadeey chunaavon main bhaag liya or parajeet hue, kintu 1989 main vijayi hue. 1991 main punah hamirpur kii loksabhaa seat se vijayi hue tathaa himachal pradesh kii bijepi ikai ke adhyaksh bane. 1996 ke loksabhaa chunaavon main haar kaa saamana karnaa padaa. phir 1998 ke vidhansabha chunaavon main bamsan kshetra se jitkar pradesh main bhaajapa-hivinka gathbandhan ke march 1998 se march 2003 tak mukhyamantri rahe. disambar 2007 se disambar 2012 tak vah mukhyamantri pad par asin rahe.
