 phisalane ke nishaan yaa skid nishaan vah nishaan he jab koi thos vastu jo duusare ke khilaaf chaalen. jaise kii koi gaadi. 
 skid sadakon par tair kii vajah se nishaan hote hai jab ek vaahan pahiyaa rolling band ho jaataa he or slide yaa sadak kii sataha par gumti he.skid ank adhiktam or nunatam vaahan kii gati ek prabhaav yaa ghatana se pehle pataa lagane ke liye vishleshan kiya ja saktaa he. phisalana kaale barf yaa sadak par dijal jamaa par bhi ho saktaa he or nishaan bhi nahin aate he.
 skid nishaan "twaran ke nishaan" twaran se hone vaale ko kaha jaataa he jaataa he. ingine tair hastantarit kar sakate hai or adhik se adhik shakti pradaan karata he; "braking ke nishaan, "agar brake taala" or kaaran slide karne ke liye tair; yaa "raste se hatana ke nishaan", yadi tair bagal main slide. har ek visheshta upasthiti he, or ek anbhavi durghatana nirmaataa yaa forencik ejeeniyar aksar nirdhaarit kar sakate hai kya vaahan ke nishaan tair dwaara choda pareekshan karke kar rahaa tha.
 car durghatana main, skid ank zyada ek rabad ek kaagaj par rabar ke tukade chhodane kii tarah he ki, dwaara rabar sadak par jamaa kiya ja rahaa vajah se hai.skid nishaan bhi jab ek car achaanak ek phislan sataha par twaran, yaa ek hard kone letaa he ke bare main aa sakate hai.skid nishaan vahaan bhi gathan kiya ja saktaa he, jahaan ek seedhi achaanak phisal jaataa he or upyogkarta jameen par gir jaataa he.
 is tarah ke nishaan vaahan durghatana punarnirmaan ke liye mahatvapurn sakshya hai. skid nishaan kii lambaai aamtaur par baariki se brake lagaana ke pal main vaahan kii gati se sambandhit he, isliye nishaan ko maapne ke mul gati ke ek anumaan arjeet karata he.
