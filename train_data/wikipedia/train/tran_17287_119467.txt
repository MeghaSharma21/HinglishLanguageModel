 bernwell, england ke northhamptonshir county ke purv northhamptonshir jila, main sthit ek gaanv he. yah gaanv, landan se 78 mile uttar, or peterbaro se 14 mile dakshin-pashchim kii ore sthit he. nene naamak nadi iske uttar se guzarati he. nikatatam nagar ondal he, joki 2 mile uttar kii or sthit he.
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar bernwell main kul 362 nivasi the. jinaki ausat umr 40.54 varsh darj kii gayi thi. 16 yaa puraane, 62 ke 279 logon kii ekal, 165 shaadi kar rahe the, or 52 talakshuda yaa vidhwaa. 191 nivaasiyon, aarthik rup se sakriy the unhen or 6 anya karmchaariyon berozegaron kii 185. vahaan 5 khaali gharon, 150 nivasee the or 3 chuttee gharon yaa duusare ke gharon main the.
 bernwell minar glocaster ke duk kaa aitihaasik purv nivas he. yah bernwell gaanv ke nikat sthit he. is estate main aitihaasik bernwell kasal bhi sthit he. jise 1266 main baranger lee moen ne banwaaya tha. moen ne is makaan ko raimase abi ko 1276 main bech diya tha. ise 1536 tak is abi ne rakhaa tha, jiske baad ise raaj ko saunp diya gaya. elizabethan kaal kaa minar ghar, yahan kaa mukhya nivasashala ban gaya, jabaki baaki ke saare rihaishi kamare or kile ke anya sari imaarton ko 1704 main gira diya gaya tha.
