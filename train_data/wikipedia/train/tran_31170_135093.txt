 
 kaazigund, bharat ke jammu or kashmir rajya ke anantnag zile main ek nagar he. jammu se srinagar ke mukhya maarg main pir punjaal parvatamala ko banihaal darra dwaara paar kara jaataa he, jisamen banihaal jammu vibhaag kaa antim or kaazigund kashmir vibhaag kaa pehla padaav he. isliye ise kashmir ghati kaa praveshdwar bhi kaha jaataa he.
 kaazigund ke paas kai prasiddh pani ke chashmen hai, jinhen kashmiri bhashaa main "naag" kaha jaataa he. puure zile main asankhya chashmen hone ke kaaran hi iskaa naam "anantnag" padaa he. shahar se 10 kimi dur verinag he, jo vitasta nadi kaa strot he. lagbhag 3 kimi dur panjath naag he.
 san 1956 main kholi gai 2.85 kimi lambi jawahar surang pir punjaal shrunkhla ko chirakar banihaal ko kaazigund se jodatii he or kashmir vaadi or desh ke anya bhaagon ke bich ke sadak yatayaat kii mukhya sutara he. kaazigund se uttar kii ore srinagar tak rail chalati he jisaki din main chaar baar seva he. ab banihaal-kaazigund ke bich ek 11 kimi lambi pir punjaal rail surang dwaara banihaal tak bhi rail chalati he, jis se in donon ke bich kaa rasta keval 17 kimi rah gaya he. banihaal se dakshin kii ore railmarg badhaane kaa kaary jari he, jo puraa hone par desh ke kisi bhi bhaag se kashmir ghati ke kisi bhi bhaag tak rail seva sakasham kar degaa.
