 stambhan dosha yaa napunsakata ek prakaar kaa yon apavikas he. yah sambhog ke dauraan shishn ke uttejit na hone yaa use banaae na rakh sakane ke kaaran paidaa hui yon niskriyata kii sthiti he. iske mukhya jaivik kaarnon main hriday or tantrikatantr sambandhi bimariyaan, madhumeh, samvednaamandak padaarthon ke dushprabhav aadi shaamil hai. maansik napunsakata shaareerik kamiyon kii vajah se nahin balki maansik vichaaron or anubhavon ke kaaran paidaa hoti he.
