 juvents footbal club jise saamanytah maatr juvents or bolchaal kii bhashaa main juve kaha jaataa he, italy main peadmont naamak kshetra ke turine shahar main sthit ek peshewar italvi footbal club he. yah club desh main apni tarah kaa teesraa sabase purana footbal club he. 
 turine ke yuva chaatro ke ek samooh dwaara sport club juvents ke rup main 1897 main sthapit yah club samay ke saath desh kii samskruti or italywaad kaa pratik ban chukaa he jiskaa pramukh kaaran he safaltaaon kii ek lambi parampara, jinka italvi samaaj mahatvapurn prabhaav padaa tathaa club ke samarthakon kii saamaajik-aarthik prishthbhumi va vaicharik raajnneeti. 
 yadi club ke prashansakon kii sankhya aadhaar par dekha jaye to yah kisi bhi anya italvi footbal club se bhi bada he or saath hi vishwa str par bhi sabase bade clubs main se ek he. juvents kaa prashansak-aadhaar desh bhar main vyapak he or mukhya rup se un videshi kshetron main bhi he jahaan italvi apravasiyon kii ek mahatvapurn upasthiti he.
 juvents aitihaasik rup se italvi footbal main bhi or vishwa str par sabase mahatvapurn puraskaron ke vijeta ke rup main bhi, kuch sabase safal clubs main se ek he. international federation of footbal statisticks and history, jo fifa dwaara manyata praapt ek sanghathan he, ke dwaara 2009 main prakashit saarvkalik ranking ke mutabik, juvents 20 veen sadi ke dauraan italy kaa sarvshereshth club or europe main doosraa sabase acha pradarshan karne vaala club tha.
