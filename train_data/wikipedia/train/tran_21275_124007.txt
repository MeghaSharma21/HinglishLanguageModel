 da ravij kollam ek paanch sitara hotel he, jo bharat ke kollam shahar main ashtamudi jhil ke tat par he. iskaa swaamitv ravij hotels and resorts company ke paas he.is hotel kaa udghaatan bollywood abhinayta shahrukh khan or malayalam abhinayta mohan laal dwaara 19 agast 2011 ko kiya gaya.
 is hotel ke sabase paas padane vaala hawaii adda, trivendrum hawaii adda he jo is hotel se 70 kilo meter kii duuri par he.kochi doosraa sabase najdiki airport he jo yah se 170 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he.
 is hotel ke aas paas kai paryatak sthal he. iname sunset boat cruge, amritpuri, suspension brij evam kai aas paas kai jharne hai. shahar main hin laumakadavu baikwators, sasthamkotta lake evam kollam bich jaise paryatak sthal he.
