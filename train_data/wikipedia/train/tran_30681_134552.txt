 jyeshtha yaa antaras, jiskaa bair naam "alfaa scorpaye" he, vashchik taramandal kaa sab se roshan tara he. yah prithvi se dikhne vaale solahava sab se roshan tara he. jyeshtha samay ke saath apni chamak kam-zyaada karne vaala ek parivarti tara he jisaki ausat chamak +1.09 magnitude he. yah prithvi se lagbhag 600 prakaash varsh kii duuri par hai.
 jyeshtha ek laal mahadaanav tara he. iskaa vyas suraj ke vyas kaa 800 guna he - agar ise uthaakar hamaare saura mandal ke bich suraj kii jagah daal diya jaae to prithvi or mangal tak ke grah iske andar aa jaaen. iskaa dravyaman suraj ke dravyaman kaa 15 se 18 guna he. isaki bhayankar chamak suraj se 65,000 guna he.
 jyeshtha ke nazadik uskaa ek saathi tara he or yah do milakar ek dwitara mandal main gurutvkarshan se bandhe hue hai. yah saathi tara ek nila garm B2.5 shrenee kaa tara he. isaki chamak mukhya jyeshtha taare kii sirf 1/370 guna he or maamuli doorabeen se ise dekhnaa mushkil he kyonki mukhya taare kii roshni main yah chhip jaataa he. jab yah dikhataa bhi he to thodaa hare rang kaa prathit ho saktaa he, haalaanki vaigyanik maanate hai ke yah paas ke mukhya taare kii bhayankar roshni kaa nateeja he.
