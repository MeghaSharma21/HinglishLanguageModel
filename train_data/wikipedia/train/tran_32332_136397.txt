 vaayu sena padak ek bhartiya sainya sammaan he, jise saamanytah shanti kaal main ullekhneey seva ke liye diya jaataa he. haalanki, yah yuddh kaal main bhi diya gaya he kintu vir chakra ke samaan sankhya main nahin. yah sammaan maranoparant bhi diya jaataa he. vaayu sena padak kii sthapana 17 june 1 9 60 ko bharat ke raashtrapati dwaara kii thi or 1 9 61 se sammaan die jane lage. pichley ek dashak main ye sammaan 2 vibhaagon main ,veerta or samarpan ke liye diya jaataa rahaa hai.
 agrabhag : ek chaturbhuj -sashastr rajat sitara, jo camel ke phulon ke aakaar kaa he. centre main rashtriya pratik. ek sidhi baar jhulati pattica. prishthbhag : hindi main "vaayu sena medal" yaa "vaayu sena padak" pankhons ke prasar ke saath ek himalay eagle.riban: 30 mimi, grey or naarangi-bhagava ke 2 mimi vikarn kii pattee.
 is sammaan ko behatar dhang se samajhane ke liye bhartiya vaayu sena ke squadran leader harchand singh gil ko praapt duusare sammaan ke shabd : ::
 oktober 1 9 53 main, assam rifles or ek civil prashasnic dal ke ek samgr stambh par sashastr tagin logo dwaara hamalaa kiya gaya tha or kuch adhikariyon or javaanon kii hatya kar di gai thi. squadran leader eches gil sainikon ke saath hawaii adde main dakota vayuyan kii tukdi main the. do choti uplabdh landing ground par hawaii jahaaz ke carmikon or aapurti kaa kaam karnaa mushkil tha. kathinaaiyon ke baavjood squadran leader gil ne utsaah, aseem urja or saahas ke saath utarane kaa bada faisla kiya., jo asambhav dikhaai detaa tha, vah vaastaviktaa ban gai. unhone sarvocch kram kaa saahas or samarpan dikhaaya.
 squadran-leader eches gil kaa janm 25 march 1 9 25 ko mulampur, punjab, bharat main hua tha.
