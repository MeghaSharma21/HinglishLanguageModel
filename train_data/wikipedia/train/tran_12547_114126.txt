 charm kathputli jaisaa ki naam se hi spasht he pracheen samay main dakshin bharat main yah chalchitr kii tarah janasamudaay ko pradarshit kii jaati thi. isamen mukhyat: ramayana, mahabharat aadi dharmik prasangon kaa chithran darshaya jaataa tha. charm kathputli charmpatr par banaai jaati he tathaa ek jhine parade ke peeche se deepak / balb kii roshni dwaara darshaya jaataa he. in charm kathputliyon main vibhinn manobhaavan rang kare jate hai jo ki darshakon kaa man moh lete hai. is chitrakala ke kalakaar bahut hi manoranjak rup se gaakar va inhen nachaakar pradarshan karte hai.
