 faijabad main sthit uttar pradesh rajya aids niyantran socity se judy faijabad zila aids niyantran socity apane aap main ek niskriy sarkari sanghathan ke rup main kaam kar rahi he. isaki misaal isi saal 2012 main vishwa swasthya sanghathan ne raktadan ke liye jaagrukta paidaa karne ke liye 2012 main ek maah-bhar abhiyaan chalaane kaa nirdesh se pataa chalati he. yah abhiyaan may 2012 se 14 july 2012 tak chalaaya janaa tha. faijabad jile main yah jimmedaari jila aids niyantran socity kii thi ki vah sarvaadhik baar raktadan karne waalon ko soochibaddh kar sammanit kare tathaa panfalet, poster, walritting, goshtee, raily aadi ke madhyam se janajagran kare. baavjood iske, is sambandh kaaryayojna nahin banaai gai. isi se socity kii is babat gambhirtha ko samajha ja saktaa he.
