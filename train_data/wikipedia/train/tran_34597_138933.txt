 sankalp ek ashaaskeey sanstha he. isaki sthapana san 1986 me un chaatro ko protsaahit karne ke liye kiya gaya, jo prashasnic sevyon kii taiyaari karte he. sanstha koching, training or jaankaree aise chaatro ko detaa he. sanstha maanati he, ki jamini str par sarkari nitiyon ko paalan karne kii jimmedaari prashasnic adhikariyon par hoti he. isliye yah pariklpana kii gayi ki aise praashanik adhikari taiyaar hon jo ki raashtra ke liye samarpit hon, eemaandaar, jimmedaar evam saamaajik-vachanabaddhata puraa karne vaalen ho - isi lakshya ko praapt karne ke liye sankalp kii sthapana kii gai thi.
