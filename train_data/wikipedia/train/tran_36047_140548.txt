 antahkshepi sanchan ek nirmaan prakriya he jo thermoplastic padaarthon tathaa thermosetting padaarthon se vibhinn vastuen banane main prayukt hoti he.
 is prakriya main padaarth ko ek garm naal main le jaaya jaataa he, mishrit kiya jaataa he or saanchaa kotor main balpurvak ghusaa diya jaataa he. yahan thanda hokar kathor ho jaataa he or kotor kaa aakaar grahan kar letaa he.
 jab kisi utpaada kii dizin puri ho jaati he to saanche kii dizin karne vaale kisi dhaatu se mold kaa nirmaan karte hai. antahkshepi sanchan kaa upyog bahutayat main hota he or is prakriya dwaara chote-se-chota avyav se lekar kaaron kii sampoorn sharir tak banaaya jaataa he. ye upyog main laane vaali sabase saral va sasti moulding he. ye do prakaar kii hoti he.1--- hand operated injection moulding machine{hastchalit ant:kshepi mashin}2---machine operated injection moulding machine{mashin chaalit ant:kshepi mashin}
