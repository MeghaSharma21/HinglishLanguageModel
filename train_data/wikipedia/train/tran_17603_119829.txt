 pradhanmantri gramin vikaas adhyetavriti yojana grih mantralay ne desh ke 60 jilon kii pahchaan vampanthi charampanth se prabhavit jilon ke rup main kii he. bharat sarkaar ne in jilon main ek vishesh kaarykram chalaaya he jise samekit kaary yojana kaa naam diya gaya he. sabhi samekit kaary yojana jilon main jila adheekshak kii madad karne ke liye yuva peshewaron kii tainaati hetu 13 sitambar,2011 ko tatkaaleen kendriya gramin vikaas mantri jairam ramesh ne pradhanmantri ke gramin vikaas felos naamak yojana kii ghoshnaa kii thi.mishan mul rup se pradhanmantri gramin vikaas adhyetavriti praapt log vikaas ko sugam banane vaali ek yojana ke rup main kaary karenge, ve samekit kaary yojana ke jilon ke colectoron tathaa unke sahakarmiyon ko madad denge or unhen paristhitiyon kaa aavashyak vishleshan pradaan karenge ki unase kaise nipta jaae' felos sakriy rup se ek jila kaarykram kaa sanchaalan karenge, jisamen nimnaankit teen mahatvapurn ranneetiyaan shaamil hongi 'sabhi niojit gatividhiyon tathaa uchit bajet prakriya ke sanchaalan dwaara programing hetu jila sansaadhan base ko majboot karnaa.ativanchit samudaayon tak sevaaon kii pahunch sthapit karne ke liye vaikalpik tareekon ko khojkar is pranali ko susthaapit tathaa sudrudh bananaa.aisi prakriyaaon ko chaalu karnaa jo is vidhi main shaamil kiye gaye parivartanon ko badhaava detii ho.yah purna rup se sahaayak kaaryon ke ek samooh dwaara puraa kiya jayega, jaise jila tathaa block str ke adhikariyon kii kshamta kaa nirmaan karnaa, jile main saamaajik lamabandi prakriya ko aage badhanaa, khaskar yuvaaon ke bich; jamini samarthan hasil karnaa tathaa panchayaton ke saath majboot sambandh bananaa'
