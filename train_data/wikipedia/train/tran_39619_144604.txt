  sanaavad madhyapradesh rajya kaa ek pramukh shahar he. san 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar isaki jansankhya 20,000 thi. inamen purushon or mahilaon kii aabaadi kramshah 48% or 52% thi. yahan kii sakshartaa 82% thi jisamen purush sakshartaa 86% or mahila sakshartaa 72% thi. yahan kii jansankhya main 6 varsh se kam umr ke bacchon kaa hissaa 14% tha. indore - khandwa sadak par sthit yah shahar apane politechnic mahavidhyalay ke liye prasiddh he. is shahar main diploma kii government politechnic college, vaanijya or vigyaan ke liye ek sarkari degree college, vigyaan ke ek nijee college bhi hai. sanaavad kaa purana naam "gulashnabad" he. jile main shahar kii raajnneetik sthiti majboot he.isaki sthaneey arthvyavastha mukhya rup se krishi or vyapaar par aadhaarit he. aasapaas ke kshetra kii mukhya fasal gehun, kapaas or soyabean hai. yah narmada nadi ke nikat, indore se 70 kilometre or khargon or badvaah se 10 kilometre jo tehsil se 60 kilometre dur sthit he. yah shahar mela kaa shahar he. yahaan kaa piramphir evam sheetala mata mela bahut prasiddh he. yahan madhya - pradesh kii sabase choti bas stand chal rahi he.nahar kaam kii vajah se nirmaan kii kor ke kaaran shahar khargon jile main apni chhap bana rahi he. paanch jain mandir ke ek kul rahe hai. unamem se digambar jain Samavsaran mandir bharat bhar main aath prasiddh jain mandiron main se ek mana jaataa he. yahaan kii oonchi pahari par piramphir tekaari naamak prasiddh dargah he or usee pahari ke neeche mandir sthit he. yah shahar madhyapradesh, ke pramukh shaharon indore, khandwa aadi se acchhee tarah se sadak or rail dwaara judaa hua he.
