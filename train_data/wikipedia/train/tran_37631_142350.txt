 ayurvedaprakash, ayurveda rasshastra kaa prasiddh granth he. iske rachayita madhav upaadhyaay hai jo saurashtra ke nivasee the. unhone varanasi jaakar is vidya kaa adhyayan kiya tha. is granth kaa rachanakaal 16veen-17veen shatabdi ke baad kaa he kyonki isamen bhavaprakash kaa ullekh he. yah granth isaliye mahatvapurn he kyonki 16veen-17ve shatabdi tak jitani bhi saamagree ekatra ho sakati thi vah isamen ekatra kii gayi he or iske baad is kshetra main koi vishesh yogadan nahin hua he.
