 radha balakrishnan ek bhartiya bhautik vigyaani hai. vah mathematical sciensej santhaan, chennai main kaam karti hai jo bhautikee main gaiyr-rekhiy gatishiltaa or anuprayogon se sambandhit he. balakrishnan ne dilli vishvavidhyalay se bhautikee main biessi kii or 1965 main emessi kii degree paprapt kii or brandeise vishvavidhyalay se piechdi kii.
