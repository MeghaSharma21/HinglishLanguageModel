 jorge 1 great britain or ireland kaa maharaja tha. unka janm hanover main hua tha.vah brunsvik-lunberg ke shaasha kaa pehla putr tha. 54 kii umr main england pahunchaa tha.raani
 eni kii mratyu ke baad unhone british singhaasana chadhaa.george eni kaa nikatatam rishtedaar tha. unka rajya kaal main ekadhipaty bandha hone laga.
 jorge kaa janm 28 may 1660 ko hanover main hua tha. unke mata- pita ernast augustus or sophia the.1661 main unka bhai fredrick augustus kaa janm hua. donon bhaiyon kaa paalan-poshan ek saath hua.unki mata paryaton main thi. waapas lautakar unhone 4 bete or ek beti ko bhi janm diya.1675 main george kaa chachaa kii mrityu hui. unka anya do chaachaaon kii shaadi ho chuki thi.jorge ko viraasat milane kii sambhaavna kam thi.unke pita unhen shikaar karne le jaaya karata tha. 15 kii aayu main ernast augustus ne apane putr ko yuddh kii shiksha dene ki uddeshy se jorge ko jang main le gayi the.lekin 1679 main jorge kaa ek chachaa kii apratyaashit mrityu hui. unka koi putr nahin tha or jorge colonberg-goatingan kaa shaasha bana.
