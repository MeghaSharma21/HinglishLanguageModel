 risinghnagar rajasthan main ganganagar jile main sthit ek shahar or nagar palika he. yah 252 meter kii ausat oonchai par sthit he.
 yah shahar pehle panvar samudaay ke bahumat ke kaaran panvarsar naam se janaa jaataa tha, lekin baad main raja ganga singh ke bete risingh ke naam par shahar kaa naam risinghnagar kar diya gaya. raja ganga singh ke duusare putr; karan singh, anup singh, vijay singh, gajsingh ke naam par bhi nikat ke shaharon ke naam kramshah karanpur, anupgarh, vijaynagar, gajsinghpur rakhe gaye the. risinghnagar kaa railway stesan bharat ke liye pakistan se rail line kaa pehla stop tha. yahaan swatantraaa senaanee amar shahid birbal singh dhalia kaa smarak he jinhone angrejon ke virodh main tirangaa laharaaya tha, or phir goli khaakar shahid hue the.
 2001 ke anusaar bharat kii janaganana ke anusaar risinghnagar kii jansankhya 27,707 he. kul jansankhya main purush 54% or mahilaayein 46% he. risinghnagar kii ausat sakshartaa dar 60% he. jisamen purush sakshartaa 74% he, or mahila sakshartaa he 60% he. risinghnagar main, kul jansankhya ke 15% kii umr 6 varsh se kam he. 
 nagar main 65% hindu, 33% sikh or 2% anya hai. hinduon or sikhon main jat samudaay ke hindu or sikh jansankhya kaa 40% he. anya pramukh samudaayon maajabhi, nayak, meghavaal, agarwal, kumavat, maheshwari, kumhaar, sunar or suthar hai.
