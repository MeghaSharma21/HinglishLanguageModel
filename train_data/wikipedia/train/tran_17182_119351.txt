 robin alan thik america-canadai gayak-geetkaar, sangeetkaar or abhinayta hai. ye abhinayta alan thik or abhinetri gloria loring ke putr hai. inaki patni abhinetri paula paton hai, jinke saath thik ke 16 varsh kii umr se hi prempurna sambandh the. dampati kaa ek putr he jiskaa naam julian fugo thik he.
