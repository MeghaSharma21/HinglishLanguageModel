 superkamputer 'param kanchanjanga' kaa sikkim enaiti main shubharambh hua he.
 17 april 2016 ko sikkim ke rajyapal shrinivas patil ne aupchaarik rup se param kanchanjanga naam ke superkamputer kaa rashtriya praudyogiki santhaan sikkim main shubharambh kiya. iskaa naam kanchanjanga parvat ke naam par rakhaa gaya he. yah parvat duniya kaa teesraa sabase unchaa parvat he.
 31 rashtriya praudyogiki sansthaanon main uplabdh sabhi superkamputer ke mukaable param kanjanjanga sabase shaktishaalee or tej he.
 ise pune kii centre for deveopment of advanced computing or enaiti sikkim ne mil kar banaaya he. is super computer ko banane main kul teen karod rupye kharch hue hai.
 superkamputer ek aisa computer hota he jisamen saamaanya– uddeshy vaale computer kii tulana main ucch– str kii sangananan kshamta hoti he. superkamputer kaa parformens million instrections par second kii bajaay floating– point oparations prati second main mapa jaataa he. 2015 tak aise superkamputer the jo quadrillian FLOPS tak parform kar sakate the. superkamputer 1960 ke dashak main astitv main aae. shuruyaat main or praathmik rup se kai dashakon tak ise control data oparation par semor cre, cre research or anuvarthi companiyaan apane naam yaa monogram ke saath banaati thim.
 june 2013 main shuruyaat ke baad se china kii tiyaan–2 superkamputer vishwa kaa sabase tej superkamputer ban gaya. yah 33.86 peta FLOPS yaa 33.86 quadrilliens of FLOPS kaa pradarshan karata he.
