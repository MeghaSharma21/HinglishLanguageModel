 aluminium or tamra kii mishradhatuen, jinamein tamra kii maatra adhik ho, aluminium kans kehalaati hai. inaki visheshtaayen hai ucch dridhtaa, vidhi aakaaron main nirmit kiye jane kii kshamta, kshay tathaa klanti ke prati ucch pratirodhshakti, sundar svarnim rang or ushma upachaar se dhaatu kaa kadaa or naram ho sakna. 
 dhalaai karte samay simavarti daanon ke chaaron or alumina kii ek kathor or chimadi parat jam jaati he, jisase dhaatu baahar se bheetar tak ek samaan nahin rah jaati. is kathinaayee se bachane ke liye ghariya ke pende se pighali hui dhaatu upar chadhaai jaati he. is kriya main talachat ko rokane ke liye vishesh prakaar kii chalani kaa upyog kiya jaataa he or pighali dhaatu main halchal rokane ke liye use mand gati se bheetar daalate hai. welding sambandhi kathinaaiyaan ab dur kar di gai hai. aluminium kans main bhatthi kii gandhakmay gas, samudrajal or tanu amla ke prati pratirodhshakti hoti he. isliye iskaa upyog bartan banane main kiya jaataa he.
 saadhaarant: teen prakaar kii mishradhaatuon kaa prayog hota he :
 neeche kii sarini main sarvaadhik prayukt maanak almunium kans kii sanrachanaayen di gayin hai. isamen diye gayi pratishat bhaar ke anusaar hai. shesh bhaag tamra he jo sarini main darshaya nahin gaya he.
