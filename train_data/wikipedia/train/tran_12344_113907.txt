 wang yaping ek chini sena payalat or antariksh yatri he. unka janm 27 janvari 1980 main hua tha. vaing sienese dwaara namit kii gai duusari mahila antariksh yatri thi. vaing pipuls libration army vaayu sena main ek captain he. vah 2012 ke antariksh mishan shenazu 9 ke ummidavaar thi, haalanki unhen pehle chini mahila antariksh yatri ke aitihaasik mishan ke liye chunaa gaya tha.wang shenazu 9 ke liye byaap kru ke sadsy ke rup main antariksh yatri bani. wang june 2013 main duusari chini mahila antariksh yatri bani shenazu 10 ke antariksh yaan kru ke sadsy ke rup main. wang yaping, vostok 6 kii 50veen varshganth par antariksh main hone vaali do mahilaon main se ek thi. us samay antariksh main vah velentina terreshkova ke saath maujuud thi. tiyaangong -1 par savaar hone par, vaing ne vaigyanik prayogon kaa aayojan kiya or chini chaatro ko live televijan prasaaran dwaara bhautik vigyaan bhi padhaayaa.
