 film kaa sangeet vicky prasad, maanas-shikhar or sachet-parampara dwaara rachit he or geet siddharth-garima dwaara likhit hai.
 microsoft ke maalik bill gates ne saal 2017 kaa vishleshan karte hue toilet: ek prem katha kaa zikr kiya he or film kii tarif kii he.
 is film ne pehle din bharat main 13.10 karod ekatrit kiye. yah film akshay kumaar kii 9 veen sabase badi udghaatan film ban gai. film ne bharat main 132 karod rupee kii kamai kii, or akshay kumaar kii ucchtam kamai vaali film ke rup main ubhara.
