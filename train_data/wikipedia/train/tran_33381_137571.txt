 nepal me bahut pahile se ayurveda paddati upyog me tha. baidh or paramparaagat chikitsak gaanvghar or shahro me swasthya seva pahuchaate the. unalogo kii aushadhi ke shrot nepal ke himaal se taraai tak milanevaale jadibuti hi hote the. aadhunik chikitsa paddati kii shuruwat rana pradhanmantri jangvahadur rana kii belaayat yatra ke baad darvaar ke andar shuru huwa lekin nepal main aadhunik chikitsa sanstha ke rup main rana pradhanmantri vir samser ke kaal me kathamandu main san 1889 me sthapit vir aspataal hi he. tatpashchaat chandra samaser ke kaal me sthapit trichandra sainik aspataal he. haal main nepal ke hospital samanyatya ayurveda, prakritik chikitsa tathaa aadhunik chikitsa karke sarkari seva vidyaman he.
 nepal me nepali sena, nepali sainik vimaan seva, nepal sasastr prahari bal, nepal prahari, nepal sasastr vanarakshak tathaa rashtriya anusandhan vibhaag nepal lagaayat sassatra, tathaa guptchar suraksha nikaay rahehai.dakshin asia main nepal kii sena paanchavin sabase badi he or visheshkar vishwa yudhon ke dauraan, apane gorkha itihaas ke liye ullekhneey rahi he. gorkha sena ko sabase adhik baar victoria cross diya gaya he.
 bharat ke uttar main basaa nepal rangon se bharpoor ek khubasurat desh he. yahaan vah sab kuch he jisaki tamanna ek aam sailaani ko hoti he. devataaon kaa ghar kahe jane vaale nepal vividhaataaon se purna he. iskaa andaja isi baat se lagaaya ja saktaa he ki jahaan ek ore yahaan barf se dhakin pahadiyaan hai, vahiin duusari ore tirthasthan he. romaanchak khelon ke shaukeen yahaan river rafting, rock climing, jangal safari or skiing kaa bhi majaa le sakate hai.
 lumbini mahatma buddha kii janm sthali he. yah uttar pradesh kii uttari seemaa ke nikat vartmaan nepal main sthit he. unesco tathaa vishwa ke sabhi buddha sampradaay ke anusaar yah sthaan nepal ke kapilwastu main he jahaan par unesco kaa aadhikarik smarak lagaayat sabhi buddha dharm ke samprayayon ne apane samskruti anusaar ke mandir, gumba, bihar aadi nirmaan kiya he. is sthaan par samraat ashok dwaara sthapit ashok stambh par brahmi lipi main praakrit bhashaa main buddha kaa janm sthaan hone kaa varnan kiya hua shilapatr avasthit he.
 janakapur nepal kaa prasiddh dharmik sthal he, jahaan sita mata kaa janm huwa tha. ye nagar pracheen kaal main mithila kii rajdhani mana jaataa he. yahan par prasiddh raja janak the jo sita mata ke pita the. kaha jaataa he ki sita kaa janm mitti ke ghade se hua tha. yah shahar bhagwaan ram kii sasuraal ke rup main vikhyath he.
 muktinath vaishnav sampradaay ke pramukh mandiron main se ek he. yah tirthasthan shaligram bhagwaan ke liye prasiddh he. shaligram darasal ek pavitra pathar hota he jisko hindu dharm main poojaneey mana jaataa he. yah mukhya rup se nepal kii ore pravaahit hone vaali kaali gandki nadi main paaya jaataa he. jis kshetra main muktinath sthit hai usko muktikshetra' ke naam se janaa jaataa hai. hindu dharmik manyataon ke anusaar yah vah kshetra he, jahaan logon ko mukti yaa moksh praapt hota he. muktinath kii yatra kaaphi mushkil he. phir bhi hindu dharmavalambee badi sankhya main yahaan tirthaton ke liye aate hai. yatra ke dauraan himalay parvat ke ek bade hisse ko langhna hota he. yah hindu dharm ke duurastha tirthasthanon main se ek he.
 kathmandu shahar se 29 kilometre uttar-pashchim main chhuttiyaan bitaane kii khubasurat jagah kakani sthit he. yahaan se himalay kaa khubasurat najaara dekhte hi banataa he. kakani se ganosh himal, gaurishankar 7134 me., chuba bhaamar 6109 me., manslu 8163 me., himalchuli 7893 me., annapurna 8091 me. samet anek parvat chotiyon ko kareeb se dekha ja saktaa he.
 samudr tal se 4360 me. kii oonchai par sthit gosai kund jhil nepal ke pramukh tirthasthalon main se ek he. kathmandu se 132 kilometre dur dhunche se gosarai kunda pahunchnaa sabase sahi vikalp he. uttar main pahaad or dakshin main vishaal jhil isaki sundarataa main chaar chand lagaate hai. yahaan or bhi nau prasiddh jhilein hai. jaise saraswati bharav, saurya or ganosh kunda aadi.
 yah pracheen nagar kathmandu se 30 kilometre purv arnico rajmarg kathmandu-kodaari rajmarg ke ek ore basaa he. yahaan se purv main cayarelung or pashchim main himalchuli shrinkhalaaon ke khubasurat drishyon kaa anand uthaaya ja saktaa he.
 bhagwaan pashupatinath kaa yah khubasurat mandir kathmandu se kareeb 5 kilometre uttar-purv main sthit he. baqigamati nadi ke kinare is mandir ke saath or bhi mandir bane hue hai. vishmavaprasiddh mahakavy "mahabharat " jo maharishi vedavyasadwara 5500 isa purv bharatvarsh me hua. ucime kuntipuna dharmraj yudhishtir, arjuna, bhim, nakul, sahadev tathaa draupadi jab swargarohan kar rahe the tab ve jis vishaal parvat shrikhala se gayi use " mahabharat parvat shrikhala " tathaa jaha par kailasnath aadiyogi mahadev ji ne jyotirling ke rup me prakat huye wo sthaan " shri pashupatinath jyotirling mandir " ke naam se janaa jaataa he. "pashupatinath jyotirling devsthan" ke bare main mana jaataa he ki yah nepal main hinduon kaa sabase pramukh or pavitra tirthasthal he. bhagwaan shiv ko samarpit yah mandir prativarsh hajaaron deshi-videshi shraddhaluon ko apni ore khinchataa he. golf course or hawaii adde ke paas bane is mandir ko bhagwaan kaa nivas sthaan mana jaataa he.
 royal chitwan rashtriya udyaan desh kii prakritik sampada kaa khajaana he. 932 varg kilometre main failaa yah udyaan dakshin- madhya nepal main sthit he. 1973 main ise nepal ke pratham rashtriya udyaan kaa darja hasil hua. isaki adbhut paristhitiki ko dekhte hue unesco ne 1984 main ise vishwa dharohar kaa darja diya.
 is mandir ke bare main kaha jaataa he ki yah kathmandu ghati kaa sabase purana vishnu mandir he. mul rup se is mandir kaa nirmaan chauthi shatabdi ke aas-paas hua tha. vartmaan pagoda style main bana yah mandir 1702 main pun: banaaya gaya jab aag ke kaaran yah nasht ho gaya tha. yah mandir ghati ke purvi ore pahaad kii chotii par bhaktpur se chaar kilometre uttar main khubasurat or shantipurna sthaan par sthit he. yah mandir unesco vishwa dharohar suchi kaa hissaa he. 2072 baishakh 12 kaa bhucamp ne is mandirka kuch sanrachana vigad gaya he.
 bhaktpur ke darbar squayar kaa nirmaan 16veen or 17veen shatabdi main hua tha. iske andar ek shahi mahal darbar or paaramparik nevad, pagoda style main bane bahut saare mandir hai. swarna dwaar, jo darbar squayar kaa pravesh dwaar he, kaaphi aakarshak he. ise dekhakar andar kii khubsuurati kaa sahaj hi andaja lagaaya ja saktaa he. yah jagah bhi unesco kii vishwa dharohar kaa hissaa he.
 unesco kii aath saanskrithik vishwa dharoharon main se ek kathmandu darbar pracheen mandiron, mehalon or galiyon kaa samooh he. yah rajdhani kii saamaajik, dharmik or shahari jindagi kaa mukhya centre he.
 khubsuurati kii misaal swarna dwaar nepal kii shaan he. beshkeemtee pattharon se saje is daravaaje kaa dharmik or aitihaasik drushti se bahut mahatv he. shahi andaaj main bane is dwaar ke upar devi kaali or garud kii pratimaayein lagi hai. yah mana jaataa he ki swarna dwaar swarg kii do apsaraayein hai. iskaa vaastushilp or sundarataa paryatakon ko mantrmugdh kar detii he. tathaa manamohak sundar drishy paryatakon ke liye bahut hi mahatvapurn jagah he.
 kathmandu ghati ke madhya main sthit bodhanath stupa tibeti samskruti kaa centre he. 1959 main china ke hamale ke baad yahaan badi sankhya main tibetiyon ne sharan lee or yah sthaan tibeti bauddhadharm kaa pramukh centre ban gaya. bodhanath nepal kaa sabase bada stupa he. iskaa nirmaan 14veen shatabdi ke aas-paas hua tha, jab mughalon ne akraman kiya.
