 prangaar-14, 14C, yaa radioprangaar, prangaar kaa ek radiodharmee samasthanik he. prangaar 14 kii khoj 27 farvari, 1940 main martin camen or sam ruben ne california vishvavidhyalay vikiran prayogshaala, barkale main kii thi. jab prangaar kaa ansh prithvi main dab jaataa he tab prangaar-14 kaa radiodharmita ke kaaran hraas hota rahata he. par prangaar ke duusare samasthakanikon kaa vayumandal se sampark vichche1da or prangaar-dwi-oshid na banane ke kaaran unke aapas ke anupaat main antar ho jaataa he. prithvi main dabe prangaar main uske samasthanikon kaa anupaat jaanakar uske dabane kii aayu kaa pataa lagbhag shatabdi main kar sakate hai. haalanki iske astitva kaa sanket 1934 main frenz curi ne diya tha. iske anvik naabhi main 6 protone or 8 neutron hote hai.
