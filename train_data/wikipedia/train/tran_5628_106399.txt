 aryabhatta bharat kaa pehla upgrah he, jise isi naam ke mahaan bhartiya khagolashastri ke naam par namit kiya gaya he.yah soviet sangh dwaara 19 april 1975 ko cosmos - 3em prakshepan vaahan dwaara kasputin yaar se prakshepit kiya gaya tha.
 yah bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan dwaara nirmaan or antariksh main upgrah sanchaalan main anubhav praapt karne hetu banaaya gaya tha.96.3 minute kaksha 50.7 kii degree ke jhukaav par 619 kimi kii bhu - duurastha or 563 kimi kii bhu - sameepak kaksha main sthapit kiya gaya tha.yah eks - re, khagol vigyaan or saura bhautikee main prayogon ke sanchaalan ke liye banaaya gaya tha.antariksh yaan 1.4 meter vyas kaa ek chhbbees tarafa bahubhuj tha.sabhi chehre saura koshikaon ke saath cover hai.ek bhartiya banaavat ke transafarmar ki vifaltaa kii vajah se kaksha main 4 dinon ke baad prayog ruuk gaye. antariksh yaan se sabhi sanket aaparishan ke 5 dinon ke baad kho gaye the.upgrah ne 11 farvari 1992 par prithvi ke vayumandal main pun: pravesh kiya.upgrah kii chhavi 1976 or 1997 ke bich bhartiya rupaya do paison ke reverse par dikhaai diya.
