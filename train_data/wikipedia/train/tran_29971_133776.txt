 nauven asian khel 19 navambar se 4 disambar, 1982 tak dilli, bharat main yojit kiye gaye the. dilli main duusari baar in khelon kaa aayojan kiya gaya tha or isse purv 1951 ke abhishekaatmak asian khel bhi yahan yojit kiye gaye the. nai dilli, bainkak ke baad aisa doosraa nagar bana jisane in khelon kii ek se adhik baar mezbaani kii ho.
 dilli ke asian khel pratham asiad the jo asian olympic parishad ke sangrakshan main yojit ho rahe the. asian khel sangh, jiske nyaayadhikaar main pratham aath asian khel yojit hue the, ko bhang kar aope banaaya gaya.
 asia ke 33 deshon se kul 4,595 khilaadiyon ne in khelon main bhaag liya. in khelon main prathamopravesh khel ghudsawaari, golf, handball, naukayan or mahila hockey the.
 in khelon se hi china kaa padak talika main prabhutv dikhaai dene laga. japan ne isse pehle ke khelon main sarvaadhik padak jite the. china ne japan ko padak talika main sarvocch pad se apdast kar khelon kii duniya main apni upasthiti darj kara di.
 9wein asian khelon kii taiyaari main hi bharat main rangeen television badi shaan ke saath laayaa gaya, kyonki in khelon kaa rangarang prasaaran kiya janaa tha.
 in khelon kaa shubhankar appu naamak ek shishu hathi tha. vaastavik jivan main "kuttinarayanan" naamak yah hathi ek durghatana main apni taang tudwa baitha jab vah ek septic tank main gir gaya or jiske kaaran atatah uski mrityu ho gai. kuttinarayanan 14 may, 2005 ko mar gaya.
 1986 ke agle asian khelon or 1988 ke 24wein greeshmakaleen olympic khelon ke mezbaan dakshin korea ne 406-sadasyeey pratinidhimandal ke saath in khelon main bhaag liya, jisamen ek paryvekshan dal bhi tha jo suvidhaon, prabandhan or pratiyogitaaon ke bare main jaankaree ekatrit karne ke liye aayaa tha.
