 yogesh goud jinhen keval yogesh naam se shrey diya gaya he bhartiya lekhak or geetkaar hai. vishesh rup se unhen anand ke "kahin dur jab deen dhal jaay" or "jindagi kisi he paheli" jaise geeton ke liye sabase acchhee tarah se janaa jaataa he.
 yogesh mul rup se lakhanu se hai. unke kaary main sammaan ke liye unhen dadasahab phalke puraskaar ke alaava yash bharti puraskaar bhi mil chukaa he. inhone kai hajaar filmon ke geet likhe hai jisamen mili, manzil, choti si baat, rajnigandha pramukh rahi hai.
