 sankatmochan mahabali hanuman yah ek bhartiya puranic style par aadhaarit ek television kaarykram he jo 04 may 2015 ko raat 8:00 soni television par shuru hoga. isamen mukhya kirdaar ke rup main nirbhay vadhava hanuman ji kaa kirdaar nibhaa rahe hai.
 bhagwaan hanuman ke jivan, baadhaon ke vinaashkari kaa anusaran karata he, or apane bachapan se jab tak ki bhagwaan ram se mulaakaat kii or apane dharmabhimani shishy banane kii yatra kaa pataa lagaaya.
