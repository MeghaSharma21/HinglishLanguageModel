 sebstian bieniek ek german nirdeshak, kalakaar, photographer or lekhak he. 
 bieniek kaa janm sijarnovasi, poland main 24 april 1975 ko hua tha. unka bachapan poland main bitaa. 13 saal kii umr se pehle vah or uske parivaar ne germany main pravaas kiya. kala main unki gahari abhiruchi rahi he, vishesh rup se chitrakala me. unhone maatr 20 varsh kii umr main chitrakala kii kai pradarshaniyon main bhaag liya. unhoneberlin vishvavidhyalay ke brownshvik college of arts se kaala main snatakottar kiya. uske baad unhone 2002 main german film or televijan academy film nirmaan main diploma karne ke pashchaat kai filmon kaa nirmaan kiya or realphek ke naam se ek pustak bhi likhi.
