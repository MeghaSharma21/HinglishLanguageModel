 uttar pradesh ke lalitpur main sthit mahavir swami abhyaaran kii sthapana 1977 i. main hui thi. yah abhyaaran paanch varg kilometre ke kshetra main failaa hua he. yahaan pakshiyon ke kai prajatiyaan dekhi ja sakati he. kai jaanavar jaise tendua, nilgai, sambhar, neeli bail, langur or bandar aadi bhi dekhe ja sakate hai. yah ghoomane ke liye sabase sahi samay navambar se april he. iske atirikt yahaan vanya vibhaag dwaara rahane ke liye rest house kii suvidha bhi uplabdh he.
