 albertviley, france main 1992 ke sheetakaaleen khelon ke baad sheetakaaleen olympic main laghu trek speed skating kaa aayojan kiya gaya he. isse pehle, yah 1988 ke khelon main ek pradarshan khel tha. 1988 ke pradarshan pratiyogita ke parinaam aadhikarik olympic aankadon main shaamil nahin kiye gaye hai. khel asia or uttari america, arthath dakshin korea, china, canada or sanyukt rajya america kii tiimon kaa varchasv he. un chaar deshon ne 1992 se 120 main se 104 swarna padak jite hai. dakshin korea 1992 ke baad se 19 swarna padak sahit 37 padak ke saath padak padak kaa netritv karata he. sheetakaaleen olympic main dakshin korea jeetne vaali aath padak main sab kuch short-trek speed skating se aayaa tha. isi tarah, china ke 44 sheetakaaleen olympic ke 24 padak khel se hai.
 2010 ke sheetakaaleen olympic main, latvia ke haralds silvs donon hi short trek or lambii patakatha speed skating main bhaag lene ke liye olympic itihaas main pehali athlete ban gayi, or ek hi din do alag-alag vishyon main pratispardhaa karne vaale pehle. 2014 sheetakaaleen olympic main, victor ahan 500 meter pratiyogita jitkar sabhi chaar olympic swarna padak jeetne vaala pehla short trek speedskar bane. unhone 2014 main rus ke pratinidhitiv main 3 swarna padak jite, or 2006 main 3 dakshin korea kaa pratinidhitiv kiya.
