 denmark ne pehle 1896 ke udghaatan ke olympic khelon main bhaag liya, or tab se athleates ne har greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main bhaag lene ke liye, 1904 ke adhik khelon ke liye chhodkar chhod diya. 1948 se denmark ne kai baar sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main bhaag liya he, jisamen 1988 ke baad se har game shaamil he.
 denmark ke athleton ne kul 179 padak jite hai, jo sheersh padak banane vaale khel ke rup main naukayan he. 1998 ke sheetakaaleen olympic main karling main denmark ne sheetakaaleen khelon main keval ek padak jeeta he.
 1905 main denmark ke liye rashtriya olympic samiti banaai gai thi.
