 krishna sahasranam stotra main solah kalaon se purna bhagwaan shrikrishna ke ek hajaar naam shlokabaddh rup se varnit hai. inke ek hajaar naamon se sambandhit is stotra kaa paath karne se manushya ko kin-kin falon kii prapti hoti he, iskaa adbhut varnan is sahasranam stotra main praapt hota he. is prithvi par solah kalaon se sampann hone vaale keval ekmatrshrikrishna hi the, jo bhagwaan vishnu ke ansh se vaiwaswath manvantar ke atthaiswein dwapar main devaki ke garbh main utpann hue.
 is sahastranam stotra kaa paath karne vaale vyakti samast prakaar ke praayshchiton se mukt rahete hai. unki khyaati sarvatra vyaapt rahati he tathaa ve trayrin se bhi mukt rahete hai. jo punya shivlinga kii hajaaron pratishtha karne se praapt hota he, vahi punya keval shrikrishna sahasranam stotra kaa paath karne se sadhak coprapt hota he. apane jivan ke ant main vah sadhak krishna ke yayujya ko praapt karata he.
