 saiyad mujffar hossain barney bharat ke prashasnic adhikari, tripura, nagaland, haryana evam himachal pradesh ke purv rajyapal tathaa bharat ke rashtriya alpsankhyak ayog ke adhyaksh the.
 barney uttar pradesh ke bareli mahavidhyalay ke purv chaatr the. wo bhartiya prashasnic seva ke udisha cader ke adhikari the jahaan unki niyukti rajya ke mukhya sachiv ke pad par hui thi. 1981 or 1984 main unhone nagaland, tripura or manipur ke rajyapal kaa padbhaar grahan kiya tathaa 1987 se 1988 tak himachal pradesh evam haryana ke rajyapal rahe. wo 1988 se 1992 tak chaturth evam pancham alpsankhyak ayog ke adhyaksh bhi rahe. wo dilli sthit jamiya milia islamia vishvavidhyalay ke 1990 se 1995 tak kuladhipati bhi rahe. 7 farvari 2014 ko 90 varsh kii aayu main vriddhavastha ke kaaran unka nidhan ho gaya.
 barney ne iqbaal: poit - petriot of india naamak pustak kii rachana bhi kii.
