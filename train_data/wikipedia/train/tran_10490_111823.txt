 panorma british pravasi shasit pradesh jibraltar main prakashit hone vaala dainik akhbaar he. samachaarpatr kaa prakaashan disambar 1975 se ek saptahik akhbaar ke rup main shuru hua tha. varsh 2002 se yah dainik samachaarpatr ban gaya. akhbaar kii apni website bhi he, jiske anusaar panorma sampoorn jibraltar main aisa pehla akhbaar he jo world wide vaib par bhi uplabdh he. vebasite 1997 main sthapit hui thi tathaa nivartmaan samay main iske do domen hai: gibraltarpanorama.com or panorama.gi. website par uplabdh abhilekhaagaar main agast 2005 se vartmaan samay tak ke online sanskaran uplabdh hai.
