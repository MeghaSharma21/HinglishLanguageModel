 rangarez priyadarshan dwaara nirdeshit ek hindi film he. isamen jaiki bhaganani, priya anand pramukh bhunika main tathaa sahaayak bhoomika main raajapaal yadav evam amitosh nagpal hai. rangarez tamil cinema kii nadodial naamak film kaa rimek he, pehle hi telegu, malayalam evam kannada main rimek kii ja chuki he. is film kaa nirmaan vaasu bhaganani ne kiya tha or isko 21 march 2013 ko vimochit kiya gaya tha.
 isi film main vishwa prasiddh dakshin korian gane gangnum style ko desi roopaantar ke rup main purnadhi8car ke saath jaiki bhaganani par filmaaya gaya he.
