 england se ek cricket team merilabone cricket club dwaara yojit karne ke liye 3 janvari se 24 farvari 1964 bharat kaa daura kiya tha. ve khele paanch test gharelu bhartiya klbon ke khilaaf duusare match ke saath-saath bhartiya cricket team ke khilaaf match. test maichon main paksh "england" ke rup main janaa jaataa tha; anya maichon main yah rup main "emsisi" main janaa jaataa tha.
