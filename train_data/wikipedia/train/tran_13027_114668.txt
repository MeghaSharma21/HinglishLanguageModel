 munsha singh dukhi british samrajya se bharat kii aazaadee ke liye ladaai main ek rashtriya krantikari or kavi the. bah prasiddh gadar party main the or tisare lahore shadyantr cese ke tahat unhen umrakaid kii sazaa di gai thi.
 munsha singh kaa janm british punjab ke jalandhar jile ke, jandiyaala manjaki main1 july 1890 ko hua tha. unhone anaupchaarik shiksha praapt kii, or angreji, hindi, urdu, bangla kaa ek acha gyaan praapt kar liya tha.
