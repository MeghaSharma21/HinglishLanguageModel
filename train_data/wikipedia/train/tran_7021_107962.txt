 polinisian log prashanth mahaasaagar ke kai dwipon par mul-rup se basane vaale samudayon kaa samooh he jo polinisian bhashaayen bolte hai, jo oshiyani bhashaa-parivaar kii ek shakha he. is maanav samooh main tonga, samoa, niuye, hawaii, new zealand, tahiti, easter dweep or anya prashanth mahasagariya dweep-kshetron ke log aate hai. bhashaa, samskruti or anuvanshiki drushti se polinisian log sunda dwipon, taiwan or madagascar ke mul-nivaasiyon se sambandhit hai jo sabhi austronishian bhashaayen bolte hai. vishwa main lagbhag 20 lakh polinisian log hai.
