 goleey nirdeshaank paddati teen ayaamon vaale dik main prayog hone vaali aisi nirdeshaank paddati hoti he jisamen us dik main maujuud kisi bhi bindu kaa sthaan teen ankon se nirdhaarit ho jaataa he:
 zaahir he ki aisi paddati main pehle se hi kisi mul centre, shirobindu disha or mul samtal kaa chunaav kar lenaa aavashyak he. is chunaav ke baad har bindu kaa sthaan in teenon ankon - - ke aadhaar par batalaaya ja saktaa he. kabhi-kabhi 'dhruviya kone' ke sthaan par 'utkarsh yaa oonchaai kone' kaa prayog hota he jo mul samtal se oonchaai maapta he.
 bhugol main jagahon kaa ankan karne vaali akshansh-rekhaansh pranali ek goleey nirdeshaank pranali kaa udhaaharan he. isamen:
 is vyavastha main kisi sthaan kii:
 dhyaan rahe ki apane aakaar or ghurnan kii vajah se bumadhya rekha kaa nirdhaaran to prakruti dwaara tay tha, lekin donon dhruvon ke bich chalane vaali madhyahn rekha kahin bhi ho sakati thi. yah keval ek aitihaasik ittefaq he ki uskaa chunaav england se nikalne ke liye kiya gaya or vishvabhar main sweekrut ho gaya.
