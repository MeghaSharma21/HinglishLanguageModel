 camp david samjhauta israel ke pradhanmantri menchem tathaa misr ke raashtrapati anwar sadaat dwaara hastakshrit ek samjhauta he jis par 17 sitambar 1978 ko hastakshar hue the. 17 din tak camp david main chali gupt baatcheet ke baad yah samjhauta hua tha. is samjhaute par hwite house main hastakshar hue the or jimi carter iske sakshi the. is samjhaute ke do fremawork the.
 isi samjhaute duusare fremawork ke aadhaar par 1979 main misr-israel shanti sandhi hui jiske liye saadaat or begin ko 1978 kaa shaanti kaa nobel puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya. is samjhaute ke pratham fremawork kaa sambandhfilistini kshetron se tha or yah philistin ke kisi bhi pratinidhi kii anupasthiti main hi likhaa gaya tha. is fremawork kaa koi prabhaav nahin padaa or sanyukt rashtrasang ne isaki bhartsana bhi kii.
