 upasaala sweden kaa ek shahar he jo upsaala lain naamak pradesh kii rajdhani bhi he. upasaala nagar mahor jhil kii jal-yatayaat-yogya ek shakha ke tat par, jiskaa naam faris nadi he, stoffhome nagar se 41 mile uttar kii ore sthit he. is nagar kaa faris nadi tathaa mahor jhil kii jalpranali dwaara stoffhome se sidhaa sambandh he. aadhunik nag us pracheen upasaala se sambhddha he jo aadhunik nagar se praaia: do mile uttar kii ore basaa hua tha. nagar kaa yah pracheen bhaag nadi ke pashchimi kinare kii dhaal par sthit he. is upasaala nagar kaa varnan naveen shatabdi ke lekhon main miltaa he; us samay ke logon ke swarnajtit mandir ke liye yah vikhyath tha. yahan sweden ke girjagharon ke ekmaatr pradhan dharmacharya kaa nivas sthaan he. san 1702 i. main vinaashkari agni dwaara nagar ke adhikansh bhaag nasht ho gaye the.
 upasaala pradesh kaa kshetrafal 5,252 varg kimi he. yah sweden ke madhya-purv main stoffhome se dakshin main sata hua he. isaki tateey seemaa baltic sagar tathaa bothenia kii khadi dwaara prakshalit hoti rahati he. yah pradesh khanij padaarthon kii drushti se dhani he. yahan kii adhikansh jansankhya krishi karne, jangal kaatane, matsay udyog tathaa louh udyog main sanlagn he.
