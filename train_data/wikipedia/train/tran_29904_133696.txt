 dyushpita yaa dyushpitru pracheen hindu dharm ke ek devtaa hai jinka ullekh rugveda main miltaa he. unke naam kaa arth 'akaash pita' he or unki sangini 'prithvi mata' bataai gai hai. rugveda, agni, indra or ushas inhen kii santati he. ve aaryon ke sabase pracheen devataaon main se ek maane jate hai.
 rugveda ke 1:89:4, 1:90:7, 1:164:33, 1:191:6 or 4:1:10 shlokon main dyushpitru kaa ullekh he. iske alaava rugveda 1:89:4 main 'pitru dyush' or 'mata prithvi' kaa zikr saath-saath hota he. haalanki anya devo kaa ullekh inase adhik baar hota he, phir bhi anya pramukh devataaon kaa pita hone ke naate inaki ahmiyat bahut he. udhaaharan ke liye rugveda 4:17:4 main inhen indra kaa pita bataaya gaya he:
 kai vidwaanon kii drushti main dyoush pita kaa naam va unki bhoomika se saafe he ki ve vahi devtaa hai jinhen pracheen yunaan main 'zeeus' or pracheen rome main 'jupiter' kaha jaataa tha. mana jaataa he ki ve vahi dev hai jinhen aadim-hind-europea log 'dyeus' ke naam se puujate the.
