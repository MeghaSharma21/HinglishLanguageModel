 leonid ilich brikenev, soviet sangh kii communist party kii kendriya samiti ke bhootpurv mahasachiv the or san 1964 se san 1982 main apni mrityu tak soviet sangh ke pramukh shaasha the. unka atharah saal kaa shasankaal joseph stallin ke alaava kisi bhi anya soviet shaasha se lambaa tha. is kaal main unhone ek ore soviet sangh ko sainya rup se bahut shaktishaalee banaaya or uskaa vishwa-str par prabhaav bahut badhaayaa, lekin duusari ore soviet sangh kii aarthik sthiti kaafii manda ho gayi. is aarthik kamazori ko soviet sangh ke san 1991 main khatm hokar tut jane kaa ek mukhya kaaran bataaya jaataa he.
 'brikenev' main 'ji' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'jha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan angrezi ke 'televithan' shabd ke 'ji' se miltaa he.
