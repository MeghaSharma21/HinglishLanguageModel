 
 1960gregory kaaldarshak kaa shuukravaar ko prarambh hone vaala ek adhivarsh jo aam yug or ano domini padnaam kaa 1960 varsh, 2 sahasrabdi kaa 960 varsh, 20 shatabdi kaa 60 varsh, or 1960 dashak kaa 1  varsh tha.
 1960 gregory calendar kaa ek saadhaaran varsh he. ise africa kaa varsh bhi kaha jaataa he, kyonki is saal kai pramukh ghatnaayen ghati ― visheshkar satraa africa rashtron kii swatantraaa ― jisase vishwa kaa dhyaan is mahadweep par gaya or pan-africavaad kii bhavanaayen or gahari hui.
