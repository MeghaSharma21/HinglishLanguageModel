 pyaar ko ho jane do ek bhartiya hindi dharawahik he. jiskaa nirmaan ekta kapur or shobhaa kapur ne kiya he. iskaa prasaaran soni par 12 oktober 2015 se shuru hone vaala he. isamen mukhya kirdaar main iqbaal khan or mona singh hai.
 yah kahani ishan or preet kii he. jinke teen bachche trusha, niti or cid hai. ishan or preet apane parivaar ko hameshaa ek karke rakhte hai. lekin unki zimmedaari ke kaaran unka pyaar lupt ho jaataa he. unka parivaar unke pyaar ko phir se laane kii koshish karata he.
