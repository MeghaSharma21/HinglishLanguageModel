 udaipur, bharat ke tripura rajya kaa ek kasbaa or nagar parishad he. yah nagar tripur sundari mandir ke liye prasiddh he. yah nagar gomti jile kaa mukhyalay bhi he. yah tripura kaa teesraa sabase bada nagar he. 
 udaipur, tripura kii rajdhani agartala se lagbhag 55 kimi duuri par sthit he. yahan anekon kritrim jhilein hai, jaise- dhanisagar, bijay sagar, mahadev dighi, jagannatha dighi, amar sagar aadi.
