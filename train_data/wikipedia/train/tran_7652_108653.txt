 britani angreji sahityakar howard jaikbasan dwaara likhit hasya upanyaas jise 2010 ke main bukar puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha. jaikbasan kaa yah upanyaas schuli dinon ke do doston or teacher ke bich unke rishton ko rochak tareeke se ujagar karata he.
 upanyaas kaa mukhya paatr purv radio patrkaar julian tresalav he jo yahudee na hone ke baavjood khud ko yahudee kii tarah viksit karata he. yahudee jivan style apnaane kaa kaam vah bachapan ke apane do yahudee doston kii sangat main karata he. is puure ghatnakram ko upanyaas main bade hi rochak dhang se pesh kiya gaya he.
 is pustak ko paanch jajon kii jeuri ne 3-2 ke bahumat se bukar ke le chunaa. jeuri ke pramukh angreji lekhak andrew moshan ne kaha ki da phincolor queshchan bahut hi shreshth upanyaas he. isamen vishaywastu ko behad halke phulke andaaj main or mazaakia lahaze se ukera gaya he. isamen lekhak kii dimagi kalabaji main khub dekhne ko milti he.
