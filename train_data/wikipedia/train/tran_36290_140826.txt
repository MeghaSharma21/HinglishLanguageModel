 uchhaliya bharat ke maharashtra rajya kii ek jati he. is jati ke logon ko 'bhamata' yaa 'ganthchor' bhi kehete hai. ye maharashtri-mishrit telegu bolte hai. isi se kuch vidvan inako mul telegu praant maanate hai. lekin inke reeti-rivaaj, manyataon or shaareerik banaavat se samajashastri is nishkarsh par pahunche hai ki ye uttar bharat se aae hai or rajputon kii santan hai. baraar, gujarat or pashchim bharat main bhi uchhaliya jati ke log kaaphi sankhya main milte hai. anek rup banaae ye saikadon kos tak logon ko thagte ghoomte hai. send lagane yaa daaka daalane se ye dur rahete hai kyonki aisa karne par inhen jatibahishkrut kar diya jaataa he. santanotpatti par ye satbai kii puja karte hai or mundan sanskaar ke samay inke yahan jatibhoj dene kaa vidhaan he. balvivaah kaa prachlan he or vivaah ke samay varapaksh kii ore se kanyapaksh ko kuch rupye die jate hai. mritkon ko jalaayaa jaataa he.
