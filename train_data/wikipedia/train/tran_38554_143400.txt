 
 rasinic durghatana
 kisi ek yaa adhik sankat paidaa karne vaale padaarthon ke avaanchit rup se nikalne ko rasinic durghatana kehete hai. isse maanav swasthya ko yaa paryaavaran ko kshati ho sakati he. rasayanon se aag lag sakati he, visphot ho sakate hai, vishakt padaarth hawa yaa jal main mishrit ho sakate hai, jisase log beemaari, chot, mrityu aadi ho sakate hai.
 rasinic apaat sthiti us samay upasthit hoti he, jab kisi durghatana athwa akraman ke dauraan, jahrile rasayan-hawa main chhod die jaane, bhojan main mil jaaen athwa pani main chhod die jaaen. ye rasayan-gas, drav athwa thos rup main ho sakate hai. ye rasayan logon tathaa paryaavaran ke liye nuksaandeh ho sakate hai. ye rasayan us sthiti main logon ko nuksaane pahunchaa sakate hai, jab ye saans ke saath sharir main chale jaaen, twachaa ke madhyam se sokh liye jaaen athwa nigal liye jaaen. 
 rasinic apaat sthiti kii nimnlikhit lakshanon ke madhyam se pahchaan karen 
 pakshiyon, machhliyon athwa chote jaanwaron kaa adhik biimaar hona athwa maranaa bhi rasinic apaat sthiti kaa sanket ho saktaa he. 
 yadi logon par kuch nishchit rasayan lag jate hai, to adhikari unka ‘avishaktan‘ kara sakate hai. isamen kapade utaarna tathaa twachaa se rasayan hataane ke liye snaan shaamil ho saktaa he. aisa hasthan yogya snaan ikai main karaayaa ja saktaa he.
