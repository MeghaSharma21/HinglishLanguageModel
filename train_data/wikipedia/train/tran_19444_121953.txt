 dushyant singh bharat kii sohalavi lok sabha ke nirvaachit sadsy hai. wo bhartiya janata party naamak rajnatik dal ke sadsy ke rup main rajasthan se jhalawad-baaraa loksabhaa kshetra kaa netritv karte hai. isse pehle wo pandrahavin lok sabha ke sadsy bhi rah chuke hai.
 dushyant singh kaa janm 11 sitambar 1973 ko mumbai, maharashtra main hua. unki maan rajasthan kii vartmaan mukhyamantri vasundhara raaje hai or unke pita kaa naam hemanth singh he. unhone apni schuli shiksha doon vidhyalay se purna kii. uske baad wo saint steffens college, dilli; johnson or wales vishvavidhyalay, providence, aarai or IHTTI skool of management, naukatel, switzaraland gayi. unki patni kaa naam niharika raaje he or unke ek putr or putree he.
