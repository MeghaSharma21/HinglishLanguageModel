 chenanee-nashari surang jise patnitop surang ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he, bhartiya rajya jammu or kashmir ke rashtriya rajmarg sankhya 44 par sthit ek sadak surang he. iskaa kaary varsh 2011 main armbh hua tathaa uddhaghatan 2 april 2017 ko kiya gaya.
 yah bharat kii sabase lambii sadak surang he jisaki lambaai 9.28 kimi he. surang banane par mul anumaanit laagat ₹ 2,520 karod thi lekin parivardhit karne main kul ₹ 3,720 karod kharch huye. mukhya surang kaa vyas 13 meter he, jabaki samanaantar nikasi surang kaa vyas 6 meter he. mukhya or nikasi surangon main 29 sthanon par paar maarg banaaye gayi hai jo har 300 meter kii duuri par sthiti hai. yah desh kii pehali purna rup se ekikrut surang pranali vaali surang he.
 surang kii sahaayta se jammu or srinagar ke madhya duuri 30.11 kimi rah gayi or yatra samay main do ghante kii katautee ho gayi. patnitop par sardiyon main barfbaari or himaskhalan ke kaaran rashtriya rajmarg 44 par baadha utpann hoti thi tathaa prayek sheetkaal main kai baar vaahanon kii lambi qatari ke kaaran bhi baadha utpann hoti thi - kai baar kai dinon tak qatari main rahanaa padataa tha. surang patnitop, kud or batot ko upmargon se jodatii he jisase rashtriya rajmarg 44 par sardiyon main trekkic jam kii samasya ko kam kiya he.
 surang nichle himalay paraas main sthiti he jisaki oonchaai 1,200 meter he. surang kaa dakshini praveshdwar 33°02′47″N 75°16′45″E / 33.0463°N 75.2793°E / 33.0463; 75.2793 par sthit he or uttari praveshdwar kaa nirdeshaank 33°07′43″N 75°17′34″E / 33.1285°N 75.2928°E / 33.1285; 75.2928 he. surang kii khudaai chenanee kasbe se lagbhag 2 kimi duuri se armbh hui jo patnitop se dakshin main sthit he.
