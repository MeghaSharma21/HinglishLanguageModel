 
 africa nakharahit udbilav, jise grout udbilav or antareep udbilav bhi kehete hai, duniya kaa duusari sabase badi mithe-pani main rahane vaali jati he. yah up-sahaara africa ke savana or jangal ilaaqon main nadi-jhiilon ke paas milte hai. inaki sabase badi pahchaan inke bina nakhon vaale or adhuri jaali-vaale paanv hote hai. yah apni nakhun rahit lekin sparsh-sakasham ungliyon se pani main or pattharon ke neeche kenkade, mendhak or anya chote jeev khaane ke liye khojate hai.
 africa nakharahit udbilav kii khaal moti or mulaayam or pet par lagbhag reshmi hoti he. inkaa rang gaharaa kaaki hota he or chahare par safed nishaan hote hai jo neeche gale or seene tak jate hai. inke paanvon main paanch ungliyaan hoti hai lekin oongali-vipareet ho sakane vaale anguuthe nahin hote. in ungliyon main nakhun nahin hote sivaay peeche ke paanvon kii duusari, teesri or chauthi ungliyon main. dum milakar inaki lambaai 113-163 semi ke bich hoti he jiskaa lagbhag ek-tihaai unki dum main aata he. inkaa vazan 12 se 21 kilogram ke bich hota he.
 basant kii shuruyaat main maadaaon ke 2 se 5 bachche hote hai. nar or maadaa disambar main barishon ke dauraan byaahte hai lekin phir alag hokar jivan vyateet karte hai. bacchon kaa paalan keval maadaaen hi karti hai. maadaaon kii garbhavastha lagbhag do maheene chaalati he.
