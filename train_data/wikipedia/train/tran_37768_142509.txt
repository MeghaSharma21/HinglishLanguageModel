 architekchar or sanrachana ejiniyari main trace ek aisi sanrachana hoti he jo ek yaa ek se adhik trikoniya ikaayiyon se milakar bani hoti he. ye ikaiyaan saral avyavon se bani hotin he or inke sire paraspar 'node' par jude hote hai. sabhi vahyabal or pratikriyaayein inhi nodon par lagate he or yahan se avyavon main tensil yaa compressive bal ke rup main prakat hote hai.
 'ekataleey trace' vah trace hoti he jiske sabhi avyav or node dwibemiya samtal main hote he jabaki 'spes trace' ke andar member or nodes tribim ke andar hote he.
 trace ke andar plane memberz hote he jo kii jointo ke dwaara ek duusare se jude hote he. trace kaa aakar trikoniya isliye hota he kyukki es aakar ke andar sabase jayda sthirataa hoti he.
 es trace ke andar vertical member compressant ko sehate he jabaki horizantal member ko.
