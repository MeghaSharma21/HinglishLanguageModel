 dushanbe madhya asia ke desh tajikistan kii rajdhani or sabase bada shahar he. isaki aabaadi varsh 2008 main 6,79,400 anumaanit kii gai thi. shahar ko 1929 tak dyushanbe ke naam se janaa jaataa tha or 1929 se 1961 tak iskaa naam stalinabad rahaa.
 tajiki or farsi donon bhashaaon main 'dushanbe' kaa matlab somvaar hota he. dushanbe ek gaanv hua karata tha jahaan har somvaar ko ek bazaar laga karata tha, jo samay ke saath-saath vistrut hokar ek bada nagar ban gaya.
 varsh 2006 ke anumaan ke anusaar, dushanbe ke naagarik 83.4% tajik, 9.1% uzbek, 5.1% roosi or 2.4% anya samudaayon ke the. 1926 se lekar 2010 tak yahan kii aabaadi bahut tezi se badi he jiskaa anumaan is talika se lagaaya ja saktaa he.
