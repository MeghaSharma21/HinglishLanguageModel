 gyanendriyaan manushya ke ve ang he jo dekhne, sunne, mahasoos karne, swaad-taap-rang adi kaa pataa lagaate hai. maanav sharir main twachaa, aankh, kaan, naak or jiva aadi paanch prakaar kii gyanendriyaan hoti he. twachaa mahasoos karne kaa, aankhe dekhne kaa, kaan sunne kaa, naak gandh kaa pataa lagane kaa or jihva swaad ko parakhane kaa kaam karti he.
