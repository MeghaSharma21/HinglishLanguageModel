 padaarth kii avasthaayen vah vishisht rup hai, jo koi padaarth dhaaran yaa grahan kar saktaa he. aitihaasik sandarbh me, in avasthaaon kaa antar padaarth ke samgr gunon ke aadhaar par kiya jaataa tha. thos vah avasthaa he jisamen padaarth ek nishchit aakaar or aaytan grahan karata he, drav avasthaa main padaarth kaa aaytan to nishchit hota he par aakaar yah us paatr kaa grahan karata he jisame ise rakhaa jaataa he, gas ke maamle main naa to koi nishchit aakaar naa hi koi nishchit aaytan hota he or padaarth fail kar uplabdh aaytan ko grahan kar letaa he.
