 umayyad parivaar yaa umayyad vansh, jiskaa naam umayya ebn abd shams se niklaa he. umayya ke mahaan-pote muawiyah ne 661 isvi main umayyaad khilaafat kii sthapana kii, or apni rajdhani damishq, syria ko sthanantarit kar di. muaviyah kii shakha jo muaviyah kii thi, vah 684 main muavia dwitiya ke padonnati ke baad shakti kaa abhyaas karne ke liye band ho gai thi. or khilaafat ko marwanid shakha main sthanantarit kar diya gaya tha, jo ki khilaafat par shashan karnaa jari rakhaa tha or 750 main abbasi khalifo dwaara hatya umayyad khalipha kar di jaati thi.. jiske baad abd al-rahamaan namit umayad khalipha ne narsanhaar se bhaag kar iberian prayadweep main ek amir rup shashan kiya, jahaan unhone cordoba shahar se ek swatantra emirate kaa shashan kiya. unke vansh ne cordoba ke emirate par shashan karnaa jari rakhaa jo 11 veen shatabdi tak rahaa,
