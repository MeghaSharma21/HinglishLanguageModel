 kuccha kii khadi gujarat rajya ke uttar-pashchim kshetra main kuccha jila or jamnagar jile ke madhya sthit he. is khadi ke pashchim main arab sagar sthit he. vaise yah khadi arab sagar kaa hi ek hissaa he. gujarat kii pashchimi seemaa main 1600 ki. me. lanbaa samudr tat he jo bharat ke anya sabhi rajyon ke samudr taton kii apeksha sabase lanbaa he. yah samudr tat kuccha kii khadi or khambhaat kii khadi se milakar bana he.
