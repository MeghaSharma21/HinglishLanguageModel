 – europea sangh – belgium 
 brussels ; dach : Brussel, ), jise adhikrut taur par brusselles kaa kshetra yaa brusselles-rajdhani kshetra kaha jaataa he, belgium desh kii rajdhani, va europea sangh kii maani hui rajdhani he. yah 19 mahanagar palikaon ke saath belgium kaa sabase bada shahar he.
 brusselles kii sthapana 10vi-shatabdi ke kila shahar ke rup main hui thi jise charlimgane ke ek vanshaj ne sthapit kiya tha va jisame das lakh se adhik vaasi the. dwitiya vishwayuddh ke ant ke pashchyaat brusselles antarrashtriya raajnneeti kaa mukhya kendra bindu rahaa he. yahan europea sangh kii mukhya imarato ke saath-saath uttari atlantic sandhi sanstha kaa mukhyalay bhi he.
