 vidyut aghat se jaanboojhkar kii gai mrityu yaa durghatanavash hui mrityu ko vidyunmrityu yaa vidyut mrityu kehete hai. 'elctrocution' shabd kii tab saamne aayaa tha jab 1890 main 'vidyut kursi' kaa upyog hua tha.
 vidyunmrityu mrityudand dene kii vidhi he, jiskaa upyog pehali baar neuyork main 6 agast 1890 i. ko hua tha. mana jaataa he ki is vidhi main mrityu bina ksht ke tatkaal hoti he. isamen mukhyatah main 2,300 volt, ekal pravastha, 60 cycle prayavarti dhara kaa ek preran voltata niyantrak or swaparinmitra hota he. saath hi aavashyak switch or meter hote hai.
 yah sanyantr vidyunmrityu kursi ko, jisapar dandit vyakti ko baithaaya jaataa he, 2,000 volt kii dhara pradaan karata he or uske seene, bhujaaon, uru sandhi, takhne or pindli ke bich ke patale bhaag ko patte se surakshit rup se band diya jaataa he. uske sir ke liye tech kii vyavastha hoti he or chehre par nakaab daali jaati he. nam, spanjrekhit or samuchit rup se dhale electrodon ko sir or ek pair kii pindli par patt dwaara kasakar band dete hai. prarambh main 2,000 volt dhara kaa aghat diya jaataa he or phir ise turant ghataakar 500 volt kar diya jaataa he. 30 second ke antar par do minute tak dhara ko ghataya badhaayaa jaataa he. is bich chaar se aath ampiore tak kii dhara pravaahit kii jaati he. switch khol die jate hai or aadhikarik doctor sharir kii pariksha lekar use kanuunan mrit qaraar detaa he.
 vidyunmrityu ke dauraan vyakti tatkshan nishchet ho jaataa he, at: marane kii kriya bina ksht ke puri hoti he. dhara ke pratham sampark main hi parisancharan or shwasan band ho jate hai. der tak dhara ke anuprayog se jaev kriyaaon kaa sthaayee apavinyas ho jaataa he or unamem punarujjiwan kii koi sambhaavna nahin rah jaati. mrityu ke kuch minute baad tak merudand or pair par bandhe electrode ke nikat 120 degree fahrenheit se 128 degree fahrenheit tak, yaa isse bhi adhik taap paaya jaataa he.
