 ferdinand won richathophen ek pramukh bhugolvetta tha. uskaa janm pashchimi germany ke silesia kshetra main may 5, 1833 ko huwa. vah shahi parivaar ke sadasyon main se tha. vah german bhashaa ke atirikt english evam french kaa bhi gyaata tha. usne brusselles env berlin main bhuvigyaan, jeev vigyaan env bhugol kii ucch shiksha praapt kii. esa kaha jaataa he ki richathofan bhu-vigyaanik kii bhaanti china gaya or vahaan se 1872 me mana huwa bhugolvetta banakar lautaa.
