 swachaalit vihimikaran ek takanic he jo refricrator yaa freezer ke vaashpitra ke ek nishchit samay ke baad himikrut karti rahati he. is takanic kaa prayog karne vaali yuktiyaan praayah tushaar-mukt, tusharahin aadi naamon se jaanee jaatin hai.
 swatah vihimikaran ke liye ek timer kaa prayog kiya jaataa he jo ek nishchit samyaanterval ke baad evaporator quayal ko garam karne lagata he taaki iske upar jami barf pigal jaay. barf pighalane se bana jal ek nalee ke se hokar peeche ek jagah ikattha hota he. defrost karne vaala heater 15 minute se lekar aadhe ghante tak chaalu rahata he. defrost heater 350 W se lekar 600 W tak ke hote hai. garam hote hote jab evaporator quayal kaa taap 5 Â°C se adhik ho jaataa he to is par laga ek bimetailic switch heater kii vidyut ko kaat detaa he or is prakaar defrost kaa kaam band ho jaataa he. vidyut paripath kii rachana aisi hoti he ki jab defrost heater chaalu ho to compressor band hoga or jab compressure chaalu ho to defrost timer chaalu rahegaa. donon ek saath kabhi chaalu nahin ho sakate.
