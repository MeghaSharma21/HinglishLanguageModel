 askot uttarakhand ke pithauragarh jile main sthit ek kasbaa he. yah kumaun mandal main aata he. askot prachinkaal main isi naam ke ek rajya kii rajdhani hua karata tha. yah didihat tehsil or kanalichhina vikaskhand kaa hissaa he.
 askot yahan sthit ek vanya jeev abhyaarany ke liye prasiddh he, jisaki sthapana 1986 main kasturi mrug ke sangrakshan hetu kii gayi thi. yah tanakpur-tawaghat rashtriya rajmarg par pithauragarh or dharchula ke madhya sthit he, or parmparik kailash-maansarovar yatra maarg par ek mukhya padaav he.
