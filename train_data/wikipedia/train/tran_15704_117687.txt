 jagadekamall tritiy, pashchimi bharat ke pratichy chalukya rajvansh kaa raja tha.
 1146 se 1181 i. tak ke kaal main kalyani par kalchuri logon kaa adhikaar rahaa. kintu 1163 main tel dwitiya kii mrityu ke baad bhi chalukyon ne apana daawa nahin choda. jagadekamall tritiy isi samay hua. uske abhilekhon kii tithi 1164 se 1183 tak he. kadachit vah tel tritiy kaa putr tha. sambhavat: paristhiti ke anukool vah kabhi kalchuri naresh kaa aadhipaty sweekaar karata tha or kabhi swatantra shaasha ke rup main rajya karata tha. uske abhilekh chitaldurg, bellari or duusare jilon se praapt hue hai. ek abhilekh main to use kalyaan se rajya karata hua kaha gaya he. vijay pandya uskaa samant tha.
