 protestant isai dharm kii ek shakha he. iskaa udai solahavin shatabdi main protestant sudhaarwadi aandolan ke falswaroop hua. yah dharm roman catholic dharm kaa ghor virdohi he. isaki pramukh manyata yah he ki dharamshastra hi udghaatit satya kaa asli strot he na ki paramparaayen aadi. protestant ke vishay main yah praaia: sunne main aata he ki vah asankhya sampradaayon main vibhakt he kintu vastav main samast protestant ke 94 pratishat paanch hi sampradaayon main sammilit hai, arthath: lutheran, calovinist, anglican, baptist or methodist.
 16veen shatabdi ke prarambh main luther ke vidroh ke falswaroop protestant shakha kaa pradurbhaav hua tha. luther ke anuyaayi lutheran kehalaate hai; protestant dharmavalambiyon main unki sankhya sarvaadhik he.
 johan calovin france ke nivasi the. san 1532 i. main protestant banakar vah switzraland main bas gaye jahaan unhone luther ke siddhanton ke vikaas tathaa protestant dharm ke sanghathan ke kaary main asaadhaaran pratibha pradarshit kii. bible ke purvardh ko apekshaakrit adhik mahatv dene ke atirikt unki shiksha kii sabase badi visheshta he, unka purvavidhan naamak siddhant. is siddhant ke anusaar ishwar ne anadi kaal se manushyon ko do virgon main vibhakt kiya he, ek varg mukti paata he or doosraa narak jaataa he. calovin ke anuyaayi calovinist kehalaate hai, ve vishesh rup se switzraland, hungary, checkoslovakia, scotland, france tathaa america main paae jate hai, unki sankhya lagbhag paanch karod he. ye sab samudaay ek world presvitareey ellins ke sadsy hai, jiskaa centre genova main he.
 henry saptam ke rajyakaal main england kaa isai carch rome se alag hokar carch ov england or baad main anglican carch kahalaane laga. anglican rajdharm ke virodh main 16veen shatabdi main puritanvaad tathaa congragationelism kaa pradurbhaav hua.
 17veen shatee ke madhya main george fox ne "socity ov frends" kii sthapana kii thi, jo queckers ke naam se vikhyath he. ve log paurohitya tathaa puja kaa koi anushthan nahin maanate or apni prarthanasabhaon main maun rahakar abhyantar jyoti ke pradurbhaav kii pratiksha karte hai. england main atyaachaar sahakar ve america main bas gaye. aajkal unki sankhya do lakh se kuch kam he.
 san 1830 i. main yusuf smith ne america main "carch ov jisas christ ov di lattar des" kii sthapana kii. us sampradaay main smith dwaara rachit "book ov moraman" bible ke baraabar mana jaataa he, isse iske anuyaayi mormans kehalaate hai. ve madiraa, tambaakoo, kaaphi tathaa chaay se parhez karte hai. prarambh main ve bahuvivaah bhi maanate the kintu baad main unhone us pratha ko band kar diya. yang ke netritv main unhone oota state kaa basaaya jisaki rajdhani salt city is sampradaay kaa mukhya centre he. mormans kii kul sankhya lagbhag atharah lakh he.
 meri baker eddy ne isa ko ek aadhyatmik chikitsak ke rup main dekha. unka mukhya siddhant yah he ki paap tathaa beemaari hamaari indriyon kii maaya hi he, jise maansik chikitsa dwaara dur kiya ja saktaa he. unhone christian science naamak sampradaay kii sthapana kii jiskaa america main aajkal bhi kaaphi prabhaav he.
 pentcostal naamak anek sampradaay 20veen shatabdi main prarambh hue hai. kul milakar unki sadsyta lagbhag ek karod bataai jaati he. pentakost parv ke naam par un sampradaayon kaa naam rakhaa gaya he. bhavukta tathaa pavitra aatma ke varadaanon kaa mahatv un sampradaayon kii pradhan visheshta he.
 1526 i. main pavitra samrajya kii sabha kii baithak spear main hui, isamen germany ke shaasha catholic or luutherwaadi do dalon main vibhakt ho gayi the. 1529 i. main spear main hi duusari sabha hui. isamen samraat charles pancham ne catholic dharm kaa prabal samarthan kiya or naye dharmasudhar andolan ke viruddh kai kathor nirdesh paarit kiye. is sabha ke is ekapaksheeya nirnyon kaa sudhaarwadi shaskaon or samarthakon ne virodh kiya. is virodh or pratwaad ke kaaran is dharm sudhaar andolan kaa naam protestant padaa. 1530 i. main protestant dharm ke siddhanton ko nirdisht eki krit rup diya gaya. isamen luther ke siddhant sammilit kar liye gaye. europe main yah protestant dharm kaa udai tha. 
 samraat charles pratham ne germany main agsvarg main ek sabha yojit kii or usamen protestanton ko apane siddhanton ko prastut karne kii ajna di. faltah protestanton ne apane ekikrut siddhanton ko ek dastavej ke rup prastut kiya. is dastavej ko "amsvarg kii sweekruti" kehete hai, parantu charles pancham ne ise amaanya kar diya or naveen sudhaarwadi dharm ke daman kaa nishchay kiya. iskaa saamana karne ke liye luutherwaadi german raajaaon se 1531 i. main smalakaldic lig naamak surakshatmak sangh banaaya. ab samraat charles pancham ne protestanton kaa samuhik naash karne kaa nirnay liya. faltah germany main grih yuddh prarambh ho gaya. ise smalkaden kaa yuddh kehete hai. par kuch samay baad is grih yuddh se trast hokar samraat charles ke uttaradhikari ferdinand ne 1555 i. main agsvarg kii sandhi kar lee. isaki dharaayen adholikhit thim -
 sant jon of di kros spain ke ek mahaan darvesh or kavi the. unka janm spain ke fantiveros naamak sthaan par hua tha. san 1563 main ye carmelite order ke monk bane.
