 dushyant kumaar tyaagi ek hindi kavi or gazalkaar the. 
 dushyant kumaar kaa janm uttar pradesh main bijanour janapad kii tehsil nazibabad ke gram raajpur navada main hua tha. jis samay dushyant kumaar ne sahitya kii duniya main apane kadam rakhe us samay bhopal ke do pragatisheel shaayaron taj bhopali tathaa qaif bhopali kaa gazalon kii duniya par raaj tha. hindi main bhi us samay agyeya tathaa gajanan madhav muktibodh kii kathin kavitaaon kaa bolbalaa tha. us samay aam aadmi ke liye nagarjun tathaa dhoomil jaise kuch kavi hi batch gaye the. is samay sirf 42 varsh ke jivan main dushyant kumaar ne apaar khyaati arjeet kii.
 nida fazli unke bare main likhte hai 
 "dushyant kii nazar unke yug kii nai peedi ke gusse or naaraazgi se saji bani he. yah gussa or naaraazgi us anyaay or raajnneeti ke kukarmo ke kilaaf nae tewaron kii awaz thi, jo samaaj main madhyavargeey jhuthepan kii jagah pichde varg kii mehnat or dayaa kii numaanandagi karti he. "
