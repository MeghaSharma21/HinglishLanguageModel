 kisi raashi ke pariman ko nikatatam das ke ghaat ke rup main abhivyakti ko us raashi ke pariman kii koti kehete hai. is prakaar kehete hai ki dharti ke dravyaman ke pariman kii koti 1022 ton he jabaki surya ke dravyaman ke pariman kii koti 1027 ton he. 
 mahatta ke kramon kaa prayog praayah bahut lagbhag ke aankalnon/tulanaaon hetu kiya jaataa he. yadi do sankhyaayein ek mahatta ke kram ke antar par hai, to ek sankhya duusari ke lagbhag das guna ke baraabar he. ese hi yadi ve do kyamon kaa antar dikhaati hai, to vah sau guna he. ek hi kram ke do nambaron main ek hi paimaane par sthit hote hai. bani sankhya choti sankhya kaa adhiktam das guna ho sakati he. yahi kaaran he sarthak sankhyaon ke peeche. jo raashi chhodh yaa purnankit kii jaati he, vah kul raashi ke mahatta se kuch kram choti hoti he, atev tuchh he.
