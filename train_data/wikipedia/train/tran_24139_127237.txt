 jagadish singh khehar
 justise jagadish singh khehar vartmaan main 44wein bharat ke mukhya nyaayadhish hai. 04 janvari 2017 ko pradhan nyaayadhish ke pad kii shapath grahan kii. ve sikh samudaay se ucchtam nyaayalay ke pratham nyaayadhish hai.
 varsh 1974 main chandigarh ke rajkeey college se vigyaan main snaatak oopaadhi praapt kii. varsh 1977 main punjab vishvavidhyalay se vidhi main snaatak or 1979 main el el em kii oopaadhi praapt kii. vishvavidhyalay main achche pradarshan ke liye aap ko swarna padak bhi mila.
