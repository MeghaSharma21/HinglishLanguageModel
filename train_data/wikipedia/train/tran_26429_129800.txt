 sudani pound sudan kii mudra he. sudani sikke main keval arabi naam hote hai, jabaki bank note main arabi or angreji bhashaa hoti he. sudan main pehle pahal misri pound jari kiya gaya. mohammad ebn abdulla or abdullahi ebn mohammad dwaara jari kiye gaye sikke misri mudra ke saath-saath chalate rahe. 1956 main sudan dwaara swayan kii mudra jari kiye jane ke saath hi misri pound kaa chalan band hua. pound sau quirs se samavibhajit he. mehadi or khaliph ke samay quirs 40 paara se samavibhajit tha. 1916 se misri quirs 10 milim se samavibhajit tha, jo 1956 main sudan main lagu kiya gaya. 1992 main pound kaa sthaan dinar ne le liya. uttari sudan main jahaan dinar prachlan main hai, vahiin dakshini sudan main pound main hi sauda hota he, jabaki rumbek or yei kshetron main kiniai shilling kaa istemaal hota he. sarkaar or sudan pipuls libration movement ke bich hue samjhaute ke tahat janvari 2007 main doosraa pound jari kiya gaya, jo july 2007 tak ekmaatr vaidhanik mudra ban gaya. isane 1 pound = 100 dinar ke vinimay mulya par dinar kaa or 1 pound = 1000 pound pound kaa sthaan liya.
