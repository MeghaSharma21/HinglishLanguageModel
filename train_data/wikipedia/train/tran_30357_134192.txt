 puranic kalkram se taatpary hindu dharmagranthon main varnit hindu itihaas hi nahin apitu maanavmaatr ke itihaas se he. puranic kalanukram ke anusaar do sabase mahatvapurn tithiyaan hai, mahabharat yuddh tathaa kaliyug kaa armbh. mahabharat ke yuddh kaa kaal 3138 isapurv tathaa kaliyug kaa armbh 3102 isapurv. puranic kalkram ke anusaar vaidic kaal kaa vartmaan maanya samay se pehle tha.
