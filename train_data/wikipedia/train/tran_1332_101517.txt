 pandit sanjeev abhyankar  mewati gharaana ke ek hindustani shastreey sangeet gayak hai. 1999 main unhone apane hindi film godmadar ke geet suno re bhaila main sarvshereshth purush parshvagayak ke liye rashtriya film puraskaar jeeta. or shastreey kala ke kshetra main nirantar utkrushtataa ke liye madhya pradesh sarkaar se kumaar gandharva rashtriya puraskaar 2008 main bhi jeeta he.
