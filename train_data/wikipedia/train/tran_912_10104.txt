 adhidevataa yaa adhidevi aise devtaa yaa devi ko kehete hai. jo kisi sthaan, parivaar, gaanv, shahar, vyakti, raashtra, vyavsaay yaa anya chiz kaa rakshak hone ke liye vishesh manyata rakhata ho. udhaaharan ke liye hindu dharm main gyaan kii adhidevi saraswati hai.
 angrezi main adhidevataa yaa adhidevi ko 'tutailories' kaha jaataa he.
 hindu dharm main kai prakaar ke adhidev hua karte hai. gaanvon ke adhidevon ko 'gram devtaa' bhi kaha jaataa he. pariwaron ke adhidevon ko 'kuldevataa' or 'ishtadevataa' kaha jaataa he. kisi praant yaa samudaay ke devtaa ko 'lokadevataa' kaha jaataa he.
 pracheen rome kii sabhyataa main adhidev hua karte the. masalan lanuvium shahar kii kuladevi juno thim.
