 east indian railway company, baad main jiskaa naam badalakar east indian railway kar diya gaya, ko bharat ke uttar or purvi bhaagon main railway kii shuruyaat karne kaa shrey jaataa he, jabaki great indian peninsula railway, south indian railway, central india railway or north-western railway naamak companiyaan bharat ke anya bhaagon main railway kaa sanchalit karti thim. company ko 1 june 1845 ko landan main chalis lakh pound kii puunji ke saath sthapit kiya gaya tha.
