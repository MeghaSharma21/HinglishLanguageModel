 radhika menon bhartiya nauvahan nigam main tainaat ek bhartiya tanker captain hai. 7 july, 2016 ko antarrashtriya samudri sanghathan ne sagar main asaadhaaran bahaduree ke liye unhen sammanit karne kaa nirnay liya. unhen yah puraskaar samudr main durgamma naamak machhlee pakadaney kii naav main savaar saat machuaaron ke jaan bachaane ke liye diya gaya. unhone machuaaron kii jaan tab bachaai jab vah bhartiya nauvahan nigam ke sampurna swaraj naamak tel tanker par niyukt thim. yah puraskaar praapt karne vaali wo duniya kii pehali mahila hai.
