 buddha-dalit andolan yaa navabuddha andolan yah hindu dharm kii varnashram vyavastha main sabase neeche ke paaydaan par rakhe gaye logon dwaara apni saamaajik sthiti main parivartan va maanavadhikaar dilaane ke liye bisbin sadi main bhartiya netaa dow bhimrao ambedkar dwaara chalaaya gaya andolan he. ise bhartiya netaa dow bhimrao ambedkar ne daliton ke utthan ke liye ise chalaae tha. ambedkar maanate the ki daliton kaa hindu dharm ke bheetar rahakar saamaajik utthan sambhav nahin ho saktaa he, unhone dharm ke rup main vah vichaardhaara apamaani chahie jo unhen swatantry, samanta va bandhutav kii shiksha de. buddha vichaardhaara se prerit hokar unhone 14 oktober 1956 i. ko apane kareeb 10,00,000 anuyaayiyon ke saath nagpur main buddha dharm sweekaar kiya. unhone apane samarthakon ko 22 buddha pratigyaaon kaa anusaran karne kii salaah di. is andolan ko srilanka buddha bhikshuon kaa bharpoor samarthan mila.
