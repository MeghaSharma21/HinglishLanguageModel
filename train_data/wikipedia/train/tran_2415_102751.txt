 sookshmatarangen wo vidyutchumbakeey tarangen hai jinaki tarangadairghy 1 meter se lekar 1 millimetre ke bich ho. duusare shabdon main, inaki aavruti 300 MHz se lekar 300 GHz bich hoti he. yah paribhaasha vyapak rup se donon para ucch aavruti or atyadhik ucch aavruti  ko shaamil karti he. bhinn-bhinn strot, vibhinn simaaon kaa upyog karte hai. 
 microwave spectrum main praayah 1 GHz se 1,000 GHz kii tarangen aati hai kintu kuch paribhaashaon main 1 gigahertz se kam aavritiyon ko bhi microwave kah diya jaataa he. microwave ke adhikansh anuprayog 1 se 40 GHz.ke bich kaam karte hai.
