 sadhana ek prasiddh bhartiya sine tarika thi. hari shivadaasaani jo abhinetri babita ke pita hai, unke pita ke bhai hai.
 sadhana apane mata pita kii ekmaatr santan thim or 1947 me desh ke bantwaare ke baad unka parivaar karachi chhodkar mumbai aa gaya. sadhana kaa naam unke pita me apni pasandida abhinetri sadhana bose ke naam par rakhaa tha. unki maan ne unhen aath varsh kii umr tak ghar par hi padhaaya tha.
 tajjub he ki is honhaar abhinetri ko filmphere or dada sahab phalke award nahin mila.
