 bhamashah card rajasthan kii mukhyamantri vasundhara raaje ne rajasthan main bhamashah yojana kii nae sire se shuruuaat kar di he. bhamashah yojana ke tahat mahilaon ko kya-kya suvidhaayen di jaati hai. is yojana ke tahat rajya ke dedh karod pariwaron kii mahilaon kaa bank khaataa kholaa jayega, jisame mahilaon ko bank khaataa khulwaane ke liye bhamashah card ke liye apana panjikaran karana jaruri he.
