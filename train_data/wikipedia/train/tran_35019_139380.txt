 kunwar suresh singh avadh kii ek ek jane maane kalakankar rajgaraane se taalluk rakhte the. kalakankar naresh raja avadhesh singh ke chote bhai the. ve ek sahitya or kala premi bhi tha. ve sumitranandan pant or harivansh raay bachchan jaise bade kaviyon ke nearesth rahe. inhi ke aagrah par kavi sumitranandan pant ji kalakankar aakar bas gaye the, jisaki nishaani 'nakshatra' aaj bhi kalakankar main maujuud hai. kunwar suresh ji "pant ji or kalakankar" evam "yaadon ke jharokhe" naamak pustake bhi likhi hai.
 san 1938 i. main kaviver sumitranandan pant ne kunwar suresh singh ke aarthik sahyog se nae saamaajik-raajnneetik, aarthik spandanon se yukt 'roopabh' naamak patr ke sampadan karne kaa nirnay liya tha.
 kunwar suresh singh bharat ke swatantrata sangram ke aandolan bhi bhi adhik sakriya the. ve rashtrapita mahatma gandhi ke karibiyon main se ek the. balki baapu ne suresh ji ko aandolanon main maargdarshan hetu kai patr bhi likhe.
 uttarakhand sthit "sumitranandan pant saahitiyik vithika" naamak sangrahalay main kalakankar ke kunwar suresh singh or harivansh raay bachchan se kiye gayi unke patr vyavhaar kii pratilipiyaan yahaan maujuud hai.
