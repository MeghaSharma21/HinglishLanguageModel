  raajnneeti praveshdwar
 british rajpariwaar, british samprabhu ke parivaar ke sabase kareebi sadasyon ke samooh ko kahajata he. haalaanki, britain main aisa koi drudh niyam yaa vidhaan nahin he, jo yah suniyojit karata ho kii kin vyaktiyon ko is vishesh samooh main rakhaa jaae, naahi koi aisa vidhaan he jo rajpariwaar ko vistrut rup se paribhaashit karata ho. baharhaal, aam taur par un vyaktiyon ko jinpar hiz/har magesti yaa hiz/har royal hyness kaa sambodhan rakhte hai, ko aam taur par rajpariwaar kaa sadsy mana jaataa he. is maapdand ke aadhaar par raaj parivaar main, adhirat, unke/unki sahachaari, purv samprabhu kii vidhwaa/vidhurgan, wales ke rajkumaar ke jyeshthatam putr ke santan, tathaa purv shaasha ke putron kii dharmapatniyaan yaa unki vidhwaayen or unke purush-rekha ke pautra shaamil honge.
 vibhinn rashtramandal pradeshon ke sandarbh main, isi vishesh samooh ke liye bhinn shabdaawalee kaa upyog bhi kiya ja saktaa he, udhaaharan ke taur par, australia main is samooh ko australian rajpariwaar kaha jaataa he.
 rajpariwaar ke kai sadasyon ke naam par aadhikarik nivas bhi aavantit kiye jate he, jo inkaa aadhikarik nivas hota he, tathaa, kai sadasyon ko nijee adhikarini bhi pradaan kii jaati he. rajpariwaar ke nijee staff ke vetan kaa bhugtan, maharani ke kosh se kiya jaataa he. rajpariwaar ke adhikansh sadsy kisi-na-kisi prakaar se, windsor gharaane se sambandh rakhte hai. rajpariwaar ke varisht sadsy, aam taur par, kulanaam nahin rakajte hai.
