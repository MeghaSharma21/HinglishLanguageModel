 coptic isai; coptic orthodox carch misr kaa mukhya isai carch he. carch main aasthaa rakhane vaale adhiktar log misr main rahete hai, lekin isamen aasthaa rakhane vaale lagbhag 10 lakh log misr ke baahar bhi hai. is carch main aasthaa rakhane vaale maanate hai ki unka carch 50 isvi kaa he. ise us samay banaaya gaya tha jab dharm prachaarak mark misr aae the. carch ke pramukh ko pope kaha jaataa he or unhen prachaarak mark kaa uttaradhikari manajata he.
 misr kii aabaadi kaa sabase bada dharmik alpsankhyak samuh he. coptico ne apane itihaas main utpeedan kaa habala diya he jabaki human ritis watch ne haal hi ke varsho main coptic isaayiyo ke viruddh badhati dharmik ashishnutaa or sampradayik hisa kaa ullekh kiya he.2011 se 2017 tak aantki hamale or sampradayik dango main sau se adhik misr main coptico kii hatya kar di gai he.keval ek praant miniya main 2011 or 2017 main coptico par sampradayik hamalo main sabase zyada hatyae kii gai he isi ke saath coptic isai mahilao or ladakiyo ke apharan or agava bhi gambhir samasya rahi he.
