 ve rama raav sikkim ke purv rajyapal the jo varsh 2002 se 2007 tak sikkim ke rajyapal rahe. unhone varsh 1956 main jan sangh se judate huye raajnneeti main kadam rakhaa or bhartiya janata party ke rashtriya str ke netaa ban gayi. wo 1966, 1972, 1978 or 1984 main, kul chaar baar hyderabad grajuyat nirvachan kshetra se andhra pradesh rajya vidhaan parishad ke liye chune gayi.
 virama raav kaa janm 12 disambar 1935 ko andhra pradesh ke krishna jile ke machhlipattanam ke nikat ek telugu brahman parivaar main hua. wo paishe se vakil the or unhone andhra pradesh ucch nyaayalay main vakaalat kaa kaary bhi kiya.
 oktober 2002 main bharat ke raashtrapati ne unhen sikkim ke rajyapal ke rup main niyukt kiya.
 apollo aspataal hyderabad main 17 janvari 2016 ko unka nidhan ho gaya.
