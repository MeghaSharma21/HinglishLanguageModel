 shri somnath sanskrit vishvavidhyalay /ˈʃhrsoʊmənɑːthəvɪshvəvɪdjɑːləjə/) gujaratraajya ke saurashtra vibhaag main gir-somnath jile main sthit ek vishvavidhyalay he. wo gujarat rajya kaa ekmaatr sanskrit vishvavidhyalay he. “purnataa gauravaay” ye vishvavidhyalasy kaa dhyeysutra he. is vishvavidhyalay kaa vyap sampoorn gujaratraajya main he. atah vah sampoorn rajya main kahin bhi sanshodhan kendra or shaikshanik kendra kaa prasthapan kar saktaa he. sampoorn rajya main pantis se adhik mahavidhyalay is vishvavidyal ke angabhoot hai.
