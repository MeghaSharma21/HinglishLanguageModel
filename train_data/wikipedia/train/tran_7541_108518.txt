 pushp ke sabase bahari chakra ko bahyadalpunj kaha jaataa he. yah pushp kaa sabase neeche evam baahar kaa bhaag he. isamen pattiyon ke samaan hare rang ke kai bahya dal yaa ankhudi paae jate hai. jis samay phool kali kii avasthaa main rahata he, bahya dal use dhankkar rakhata he tathaa uski raksha karti he.
