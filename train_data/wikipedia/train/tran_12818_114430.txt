 mithilakshar lipi athwa mithilakshra kaa prayog log bharat ke uttar bihar evam nepal ke tarai kshetra kii maithili bhashaa ko likhane ke liye karte hai. ise 'maithili lipi' or 'tirhuta' bhi kaha jaataa he. is lipi kaa pracheenatam namoona darbhanga jila ke kusheshwarasthaan ke nikat tilkeshwarsthaan ke shiv mandir main he. is mandir main purvi videh praakrit main likhaa he ki mandir 'kattika sudi' shake 125 main bana tha. is mandir kii lipi or aadhunik tirhuta lipi main bahut kam antar he. 
 kintu 20veen shatabdi main kramash: adhikansh maithili ke logon ne maithili likhane ke liye devanagari kaa prayog karnaa armbh kar diya. kintu ab bhi kuch parmparik brahman 'paata' bhejne ke liye iskaa prayog karte hai. san 2003 isvi main is lipi ke liye font kaa vikaas kiya gaya tha. 
 yah lipi bangala lipi se milti-julati he kintu usase thodi-bahut bhinn he. yah padhane main bangala lipi kii apeksha kathin he.
