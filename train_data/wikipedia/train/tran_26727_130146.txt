 california praudyogiki santhaan, sanyukt rajya ke california main sthit ek takneekee shiksha kaa vishwaprasiddh santhaan he. isamen har saal 2100 chaatr namankit hote hai. iske saat shaikshanik vibhaagon main lagbhag 300 peshewar sankaay kaam karte hai. vibhaagon main jeevigyaan, rasayan vigyaan, ejineering, jyological vigyaan or saamaajik vigyaan shaamil hai.
