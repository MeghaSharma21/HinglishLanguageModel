 rajju karshan sethu, sethuon kaa ek prakaar hota he. isamen ek yaa adhik stambh hote hai,, jo stambh ispaat rajjuon dwaara sethu kii sataha kaa bhaar sambhalate hai. 
 iske teen mukhya up-bhed hote hai:-
 isamen sabhi cabel samanaantar hote hai, va stambh main vibhinn duriyon par jude hote hai. itani hi duriyon par ye cabel sadak par jude hote hai.
 isamen sabhi cabel stambh par sabase upar ek hi sthaan se jude hote hai. isamen cabel kii vaanchit lambaai harp aakaar se kahin adhik hoti he.
 isamen sabhi cabel urdhwadhar hote hai. ye neeche sadak se jude hote hai, va upar ek stambh se duusare stambh ko jode hue ek anya mote cabel se jude hote hai.
