 bhumigat cabile dharti ke sataha se neeche gadi hui chalati hai. inase vidyut shakti ko ek sthaan se duusare sthaan tak le jaaya jaataa he yaa ye sanchaar ke sanketon ko ek sthaan se duusare sthaan tak le jaatin hai. ye shiropari linon kii vikalp hai. praayah shiropari linon ko hataakar unke sthaan par bhumigat cabile bichaaye jate hai. jis kshetra main bahut se unche-unche bhawan hon vahaan shiropari line nahin lagaai ja saktin atah aisi sthiti main bhumigat cabel hi ekmaatr vikalp bachataa he. 
 bhumigat cabile, dharti ke andar chhipe hote hai, atah soundarya kii drushti se uttam hai. inase logon ko shaareerik kshati kii ashanka bhi bahut kam rahati he. inhen lagane kaa kharch adhik aata he kintu ye adhik din tak bina marammat ke kaam karte hai.
