 paryaa uzbekistan or tajikistan ke kuch bhaagon main boli jane vaali madhya hind-arya bhashaa he. yah hindi kii ek upbhasha he. is bhashaa kii visheshtaon ke adhyayan evam unke dastavejikaran par soviet bhashavaigyanik ai em oranski ne bahut mahatvapurn kaary kiya he.
 yah bhashaa brajabhasha, hariyaanvi tathaa rajasthani boliyon par aadhaarit he tathaa uzbek, tajik evam roosi bhashaaon se prabhavit he. vishwa bhar main iske lagbhag 2500 bolne vaale hai.
