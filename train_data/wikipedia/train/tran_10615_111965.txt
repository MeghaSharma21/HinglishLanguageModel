 crystal disuja bhartiya hindi abhinetri hai. yah ek hazaron main meri bahna he main jeevika viren singh kaa kirdaar nibhaa chukin hai. iske baad inhone ek nai pahchaan main sakshi kaa kirdaar nibhaayaa. yah vartmaan main zee tivi ke brahmarakshas - jaag uthaa shaitan naamak dharawahik main raina kaa kirdaar nibhaa rahi hai.
 crystal ne apane abhinay kaa safar 2007 main "kahe naa kahe" naamak dharawahik se shuru kiya tha. iske baad "kis desh main he meraa dil", "kasturi", "kya dil main he" or "baat hamaari pakki" he main bhi kaam kiya. 2011 main "ek hajaaron main meri bahna he" main abhinay ke kaaran yah kaaphi jane jaani lageen. isamen yah jeevika kaa ek mukhya kirdaar nibhaa raheen thim.
