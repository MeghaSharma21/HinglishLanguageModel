 vishwa hindu parishad ek hindu sanghathan he, jo rashtriya swayamsevak sangh yani "aresas" kii ek anushangik shakha he. ise viechpi or vihip ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. vihip kaa chinh bargad kaa ped he or iskaa naaraa, "dharmo rakshati rakshit:" yani jo dharm kii raksha karata he, dharm uski raksha karata he.america khufia agency siie ne vishwa hindu parishad or bajrang dal ko dharmik ugravadi sanghathan bataaya he,jiskaa ki sampoorn desh main teevr virodh chal rahaa he. siie dwaara jari world fact book main vihip or bajrang dal ko 'raajnneetik dawaab samooh' kii shrenee main shaamil kiya gaya hamel 
 vishwa hindu parishad kii sthapana 1964 main hui. iske sansthaapakon main swami chinmayanand, eses apte, master tara hind the. pehali baar 21 may 1964 main mumbai ke sandipani sadhnashala main ek sammelan hua. sammelan aresas sarasanghachaalak madhav sadashiv golwalkar ne bulaai thi. is sammelan main hindu, sikh, jain or buddha ke kai pratinidhi maujuud the. sammelan main golwalkar ne kaha ki bharat ke sabhi matabalanviyon ko ekajute hone kii jarurat he. unhone kaha ki hindu hindoostaniyon ke liye prayukt hone vaala shabd he or yah dharmon se upar he.
