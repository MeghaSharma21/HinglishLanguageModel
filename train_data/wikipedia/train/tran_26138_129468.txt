 2006 yuiefee champions lig final buudhvaar, 17 may 2006 ko saint denis, paris, france, main sted di france main england kii arsenal or spain ke barsilona ke bich ek sangh footbal match tha. yah europe ke praathmik cap pratiyogita, yuiefee champions lig ke 2005-06 satr ke antim match tha. barsilona ve 1992 main ek baar pratiyogita jeet lee or anya teen final main haar gaya tha, apane paanchaven final main dikhaai de rahe the. arsenal ke liye pehali baar final main dikhaai de rahaa he or aisa karne main final main suvidha ke liye landan se pehla club ban gaya.
