 yayati yaa percius taramandal khagoleey gole ke uttari bhaag main dikhne vaala ek taramandal he. duusari shatabdi isvi main tolmi ne jin 48 taramandalon kii suchi banaai thi yah unamem se ek he or antarrashtriya khagoleey sangh dwaara jari kii gai 88 taramandalon kii suchi main bhi yah shaamil he. yayati taramandal main algol naam kaa mashhoor parivarti tara sthit he. vaarshik percid ulkapindon kii bauchhaar bhi akaash ke isi kshetra main hoti he.
 yayati taramandal main chah mukhya taare hai, haalanki vaise isamen 65 taron ko bair naam die ja chuke hai. inamen se chah ke ird-gird gair-saureey grah parikrama karte hue paae gaye hai. is taramandal ke mukhya taare or anya vaastuen is prakaar hai -
