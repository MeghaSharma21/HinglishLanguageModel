 palaavan, jise kabhi-kabhi palawan or palaavan bhi kaha jaataa he, dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh kaa ek dweep he. yah us desh kii prashasnic pranali main praant kaa darja rakhata he or mimaropa naamak prashasnic kshetra kaa hissaa he. apane kshetrafal ke hisaab se palaavan desh kaa sabase bada praant he. yah purv main mindoro dweep se kuch duuri par armbh hota he or iskaa doosraa chor borneo ke dweep se kuch pehle sthit he. palaavan ke pashchimottar main dakshin china sagar or dakshinpurv main sulu sagar sthit he.
