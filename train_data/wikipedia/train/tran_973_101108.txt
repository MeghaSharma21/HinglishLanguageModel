 madhyapragjivi mahakalp yaa mesoproterosoik mahakalp prithvi ke bhuvaigyanik itihaas main ek mahakalp tha, jo aaj se 160 karod varsh pehle armbh hua or 100 karod varsh pehle ant hua. is ke baad main nutanpragjivi mahakalp or is se pehle purapragjivi mahakalp aayaa. nutanpragjivi, madhyapragjivi or purapragjivi mahakalp tino milakar pragjivi eon ke teen bhaag hai.
