 barnarado bartoluchi ek italvi film nirdeshak or patakatha lekhak hai. unki pramukh filmein hai - da confermist, last tango in paris, da last inparor, da celtering ski, steeling beauty, da drimers or little buddha. san 2011 main film nirmaan main unke yogadan ko dekhte hue kans film samaaroh main unhen palm di ore puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. charchit patakatha lekhika clara paplo unki patni hai.
 barnarado bartoluchi kaa janm italy ke emilia romagna praant ke parma shahar main hua tha. unke pita ek kavi, itihaaskaar or film sameekshak the. ek susanskrit parivaar main laalan-paalan kaa asar ye hua ki bartoluchi ne maatr 15 varsh kii aayu main lekhan kaary shuru kar diya. bartoluchi ke pita ne mashhoor italvi filmkaar piyare paolo pasolini ke pehle upanyaas ke prakaashan main madad kii thi jiske badale main pasolini ne bartoluchi ko apni film acaton main apana varisht sahaayak nirdeshak ke rup main kaam karne kaa mauka diya.
 bartoluchi apane pita kii tarah ek kavi or lekhak bananaa chaahate the. unhone rome vishvavidhyalay ke aadhunik sahitya vibhaag main daakhila le liya lekin isi bich bartoluchi ko pasolini kii film main bataur sahaayak nirdeshak kaary karne kaa mauka mil gaya jiske baad bartoluchi ne snaatak kii shiksha adhuri chhod di. bartoluchi ne 1962 main maatr 22 saal kii aayu main apni pehali feechar film kaa nirdesan kiya. san 1972 main bartoluchi kii film da last tango in paris se ek bada vivaad khadaa ho gaya. is film main marlen brando or maaria shinder ne pramukh bhoomikaayen nibhaai thim. is film ke ek rape seen ko lekar bartoluchi par tamaam aarope lage, mukademe hue or sajaa bhi hui.
 1976 main bartoluchi ne 1900 naam se ek film kaa nirmaan kiya or bhavy style main film nirmaan kii ek nai style ko janm diya. lekin bartoluchi ko asli prasiddhi mili film last inparor se. is film main china ke aakhri badshaah asin goro pui kaa jivan vrittant filmaaya gaya he. bartoluchi ko is film ke liye auskar puraskaar mila.
