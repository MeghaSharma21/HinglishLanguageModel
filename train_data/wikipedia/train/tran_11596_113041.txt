 computer vigyaan main un kalanvidhiyon ko anukraman kalanvidhi kehete hai jo kisi suchi ke avyavon kaa mul kram badalakar kisi anya kramvishesh main vyavasthit karne ke kaam aatin hai. jaise 2, 7, 4, 5, 6 ko anukramit karne par 2, 4, 5, 6, 7 milte hai. do prakaar ke kram sabase adhik prayukt hote hai - sankyatmak kram tathaa koshakram . computer prograamon main anukraman kaa sambhavtah sabase adhik upyog hota he. isliye dakshatapurvak anukraman karne vaale algoridm bahut mahatv rakhte hai. kuch anya kalanvidhiyaan tabhi kaary kar sakati hai jab pehle aank. don ko kisi kram main vyavasthit kiya gaya ho. udhaaharan ke liye khojane or vilay karne vaale algoridm thik se tabhi kaam karenge jab unka upyog kisi anukramit suchi par kiya jaay. 
 computary ke armbh se hi anukraman ke prashn par bahut adhik anusandhan hota aayaa he. yadhyapi anukraman kaa kaary bahut hi saral kaary he kintu jab kisi vrihad suchi ko dakshatapurvak anukramit karnaa ho tab yah ek jatil samasya ban jaati he.
