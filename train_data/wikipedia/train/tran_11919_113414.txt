 asset management company ek aisi company he jo apane graahakon ke pool kiye gaye fundon ko pratibhutiyon jaise muchl fund, equity main nivesh karne main sahaayta karti he jo ghoshit vittiya uddeshyon se mail khate ho. asset management company niveshkon ko adhik vividhta or nivesh vikalp pradaan karti hai, jo unke paas hote hai. eemasi mutual fund, hej fund or pension yojanaon kaa prabandhan karte hai, or ye companiyaan apane graahakon ko seva shulk yaa kamishan charge karke aay kamaati hai.
 aam taur par, asset management company ko kharid-paksh form mana jaataa he. yah keval is tathy ko sanderbhit karata he ki ve apane graahakon ko paisa nivesh karne yaa pratibhutiyon ko kharidne main madad karte hai. ve nirnay lete hai ki in-house research or data analytics ke aadhaar par kya khareednaa he, lekin ve vikray-paksh fermon se bhi saarvajanik siforishein lete hai.
