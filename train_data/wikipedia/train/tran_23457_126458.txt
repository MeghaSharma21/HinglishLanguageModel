 shimer ebn jiljushan yaa shimer banu kilaab janajati se jiljushan kaa putr tha, jo arab ke haujinini qaseed janajatiyon main se ek tha. hazarat abbaas ebn ali kii maa umm ul-banin bhi banu kilaab janajati se thim. islam main shimer kii ek atyachari pratishtha he. vah yajid pratham ke prati nishtha kaa bhugtan karne or ebn jiyaad kii umayyaad sena main shaamil hone se pehle kharij netaa tha. vah us vyakti ke rup main janaa jaataa he jisane karbala kii ladaai main islamic paigambar hazarat mohammad sahaab ke navaase hazarat hossain ebn ali kii hatya kar di thi.
 shiya paramparaon se sambandhit he ki shimer ko antat hazarat al-mukhtaar taakaphi ke sainikon ne maar daalaa tha, jinhone hazarat hossain or unke parivaar ke hatyaron par badla lene kii kaamana kii thi. shimer sharir ke tukade tukade kar diya the or junglee kutton dwaara nuchabaaya gaya tha.
