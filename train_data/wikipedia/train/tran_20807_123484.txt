 ekataraa athwa iktaaraa bhartiya sangeet kaa lokapriy tantvaadya yantra he jiskaa prayog bhajan yaa sugam sangeet main kiya jaataa he. isamen do taar lage hote hai. bharat, bangladesh, pakistan or misr ke paaramparik sangeet main iskaa prayog hota he. 
 iktaaraa multah bhartiya kaviyon tathaa ghumkkad gavaiyon dwaara prayog kiya jaataa tha. nichalaa gunjayman yantra ve lowki se banaate the, or upar kaa galaa banane ke liye bans kaa prayog karte the.
 ekataraa vadak yantra ko gale par pakadta he, or phir tarjani anguli dwaara taar ko bajaata he. gale ke donon hisson ko dabaane se taar dhilaa padataa he, is prakaar isaki pich kam ho jaati he. gardan ke har had tak chhidr ke saath tone kaa swarupan hi ekthare ko apni vishisht dhwani detaa he. yah sanket dene ke liye koi nishaan yaa maap nahin hai ki kis dabaav se kya dabaav paidaa hoga, isliye kaan dwaara samyojit kiya jaataa he.
