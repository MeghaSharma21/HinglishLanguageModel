 nepal ke mahottaree sadarmukaam jaleshwar se 27 kilometre uttar-pashchim sonamai sthaan main dashahra ke avasar par lagane vaale meley ko bhoot mela kehete hai.sonamai main bhoot bhagane kaa mela 200 saal pehle se chalata aa rahaa he, yah logon kaa anumaan rahaa he. ghatsthapana se suru hokar navami main samaapan hone vaala yah mela din bhar puja or manoranjan ke saath saptami or ashtami tak utkarshpurn hota he. bhootpret se mukt hone ke liye raat hone ke baad hi log mandir parisar me jate hai. din bhar pujapath main vyast puujaari raatbhar jaagran karte hue bhoot meley kaa netritv karte he. bhoot hataane or santaan sukh praapt karne kaa avasar ke rup main mana janevaala sonamai mela manokanksha puraa karata he aisa janvishwaas he.
