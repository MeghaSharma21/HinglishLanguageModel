 pandit lakhmi chand hariyaanvi bhashaa ke ek prasiddh kavi va lok kalakaar the. hariyaanvi raagani va sang main unke ullekhneey yogadan ke kaaran unhen "surya-kavi" kaha jaataa he. unhen "haryana kaa shakespeare" bhi kaha jaataa he. unka janm ] jile ke jetty kalam gaanv main hua tha tathaa maatr 42 varsh kii aayu main hi ve chal based. unke naam par sahitya ke kshetra main kai puraskaar die jate hai. 
 unke dwaara rachit kuch pramukh sang hai : nal-damayanti, mirabai, satyawan savitri, seth tara chand, puuran bhagat va shashi lakadahaara aadi. unke suputr pandit tuleram ne unki parampara ko aage badhaayaa. aajkal unke pautra vishnu unki is parampara kaa haryana, rajasthan va uttar pradesh ke dur-daraaj ke gaanvon tak prasar karne het prayasarat hai.
