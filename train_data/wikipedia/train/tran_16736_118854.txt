 un electronic pariklitron ko vaigyanik pariklitr kehete hai jo vigyaan, ejiniyari tathaa ganit kii gananaaon ke liye prayukt hote hai. 
 saadhaaran pariklitron main keval jod, ghataana, guna, bhaag aadi saadhaaran ganitiya kaary kii suvidha hoti he jabaki vaigyanik pariklitron main inke atirikt trikoshmetiya, laghuganakeey, ghaat, sankhyaon kaa vaigyanik nirupan, aadi kii kriyaayein bhi kii ja saktin hai. vaigyanik pariklitron ke aane se slide rule kaa prayog lagbhag puri tarah samaapt ho gaya. 
 ab ucch shiksha tathaa anya ucch kaaryon main vaigyanik pariklitr kaa sthaan graphy pariklitron ne le liya he. ye pariklitr vaigyanik pariklitr kaa sara kaam karte hi hai, ye aankadon yaa kisi falan ko graph ke rup main bhi pradarshit kar sakate hai. inamen program karne tathaa prograamon ko bhandarit karne kii bhi suvidha aa gayi he.
 pehla vaigyanik pariklitr 1968 main jari kiya gaya tha. yah nirdeshyogy havelett-pacurd HP-9100A tha. HP-9100 shrinkhalaa puri tarah se asatat transistor tark se nirmit hui thi, jisamen koi ekikrut circuit nahin the. havelett-packard kaa HP-35 1 farvari, 1972 ko pesh kiya gaya tha. yah duniya kaa pehla suvahya vaigyanik pariklitr tha.
