 duladev mandir bharat ke madhya pradesh rajya ke khajuraho sthaan main bana ek hindu mandir he. yah multah shiv mandir he. isko kuch itihaaskaar kunwarnath mandir bhi kehete hai. iskaa nirmaankaal lagbhag san 1000 i. he. mandir kaa aakaar 69 n 40' he. yah mandir pratimaa vargeekaran kii drushti se mahatvapurn mandir he. nirandhaar prasaad prakruti kaa yah mandir apni neenv yojana main samanvit prakruti kaa he. mandir sundar pratimaaon se susajjit he. isamen ganga kii chaturbhuj pratimaa atyant hi sundar bhang se ankit kii gai he. yah pratimaa itani aakarshak evam prabhavotpadak he ki lagata he ki yah apane aadhaar se pruthak hokar akaash main udne kaa prayaas kar rahi he. mandir kii bhitari bahari bhaag main anek pratimaaen ankit kii gai he, jinaki bhavabhangimaayen saundaryamayi, darshneey tathaa uddipak he. naariyon, apsaraon evam mithun kii pratimaaen, is tarah ankit kii gai he ki sab apane astitv ke liye sajag he. bhrasht mithun moh bhang bhi karte hai, phir apni visheshta se chaunkaa bhi dete hai.
 is mandir ke pattharon par "vasal' naamak kalakaar kaa naam ankit kiya hua mila he. mandir ke vitan golakaar tathaa stambh alankrita hai or nrity karti pratimaa evam sundar evam aakarshik hai. mandir ke garbhagrih main shivlinga yoni- vedika par sthapit kiya gaya he. mandir ke baahar murtiyan teen pattiyon par ankit kii gai hai. yahan hathi, ghode, yoddha or saamaanya jivan ke anekanek drishy prastut kiye gaye hai. apsaraon ko is tarah vyavasthit kiya gaya he ki ve swatantra, svachhand evam nirbaadh jivan kaa pratik maaloom padati he.
 is mandir kii visheshta yah he ki apsara todon main apsaaon ko do- do, teen- teen kii toli main darshaya gaya he. mandir, manndap, mahamandap tathaa mukhmandap yukt he. mukhmandap bhaag main ganesh or virbhadra kii pratimaaen apni rashikaon main is prakaar ankit kii gai he ki jhaank rahi dikhti he. yahan kii vidyadhar or apsaraayen gatisheel hai. parantu saamanytah pratimaaon par alankaran kaa bhaar adhik dikhaai detaa he. pratimaaon main se kuch pratimaaon kii kalaatmakataa darshneey evam saraahneey he. ashtavasu magarmukhi he. yama tathaa naritti kii kesh sajja paramparaon se pruthak pankhaakaar he.
 mandir kii jagati 5' unchi he. jagati ko sundar evam darshneey banaaya gaya he. jangha par pratimaaon kii panktiyaan sthapit kii gai hai. prastar par patrak sajja bhi he. devi- devtaa, digpal tathaa apsaraayen chazza par madhya pankti dev tathaa maanav yuglon evam mithun se sajaayaa gaya he. bhadron ke chazzon par rathikaaye hai. vahaan dev pratimaaen hai. dakshini bhadra ke kaksh- kuta par guru- shishy kii pratimaa ankit kii gai he.
 shikhar saptarath mul manjari yukt he. yah bhoomi amlkon se susajjit kiya gaya he. uru: shringon main se do saptarath ek panch rath prakruti kaa he. shikhar ke pratirathon par shring hai, kinare kii nandikaon par do- do shring hai tathaa prayek karanrath par teen- teen shring hai. shring sam aakaar ke hai. antaraal bhaag kaa purvi mukh kaa ugrabhag surakshit he. jisapar naun rathikaaye banaai gai hai, jinpar neeche se upar kii ore udgamon kii chaar panktiyaan hai. aath rathikaaon par shiv- parvati kii pratimaaen ankit kii gai hai. stambh shakha par teen rathikaaye hai, jinpar shiv pratimaaen ankit kii gai hai, jo parampara ke anusaar brahma or vishnu se ghiri hai. bhoot- nayak pratimaa shiv pratimaaon ke neeche hai, jabaki vishranti bhaag main navagraha pratimaaen khadi mudra main hai. is stambh shakha par jal deviyaan tribhangi mudraon main he. magar or kachua bhi yahan sundar prakaar se ankit kiya gaya he. mukh pratimaaen ganga- yamuna kii pratik maani jaati he. shaiva pratihaar pratimaaon main ek pratimaa mahakaal bhi he, jo khappar yukt he.
 mandir ke bahari bhaag kii rathikaaon main dakshini mukh par nrity mudra main chah bhujaa yukt bhairav, baarah bhujayukta shiv tathaa ek anya rathika main trimukhi dash bhujayukta shiv pratimaa he. isaki diwaar par baarah bhujaa yukt nataraj, chaturbhuj, harihar, uttari mukh par baarah bhujaa yukt shiv, asht bhujayukta vishnu, dash bhujaa yukt choumunda, chaturbhuj vishnu gajendramoksh rup main tathaa shiv parvati yugma mudra main he. pashchimi mukh par chaturbhuj nagn naruti, varun ke atirikt vrishabhmukhi vasu kii do pratimaaen hai. uttari mukh par vaayu kii bhagn pratimaa ke atirikt vrishabhmukhi vasu kii teen tathaa chaturbhuj kuber evam ishan kii ek- ek pratimaa ankit kii gai he.
