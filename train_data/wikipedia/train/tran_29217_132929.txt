 voltata niyantrak vah electronic yukti he jo swat: voltata ko ek nishchit maan ke paas banaakar rakhti he. yah prayavarti voltata ke niyantran ke liye banaayi ja sakati he yaa disht voltata ke liye. isamen vidyutyantric yuktiyaan lagi hotin hai; yaa ye aadhunik ardhchaalak yuktiyon evam kuch pasive yuktiyon ke yoga se banaayi ja sakati he.
 iske dwaara niyantrit voltata milane se jo yuktiyaan chalati hai ve kharaab nahin hotin evam apana kaam bhi niyat tareeke se karti hai. udhaaharan ke liye jin gaanvon main supplai voltage bahut ghatata-badhata rahata he vahaan tivi chalaane ke liye uske saath 220 volt kaa voltata-niyantrak bhi lagaaya jaataa he.
