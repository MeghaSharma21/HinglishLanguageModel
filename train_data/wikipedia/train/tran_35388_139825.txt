 sangyanatmak vigyaan yaa bodh vigyaan mastishk evam uski prakriyaaon kaa anataravishayi vaijjyanik adhyayan he. yah sangyaan kii prakruti or uske kaaryon kii khojbin karata he. sangyanatmak vaigyanik buddhi or vyavhaar kaa adhyayan karte hai jisamen focus is baat par rahata he ki tantrika tantra kis prakaar soochnaaon kaa kis prakaar nirupan karata he, kaise unka prasanskaran karata he or kaise unako roopaantarit karata he. bodh vigyaani ke liye mahatv ke kuch vishay ye hai- bhashaa, avgam, smriti, dhyaan, tarkna, tathaa samveg . in vishyon ko samajhane ke liye bodh vigyaani bhashavigyaan, manovigyaan, kritrim buddhi, darshan, tantrika vigyaan tathaa nrivigyaan aadi kaa sahaara letaa he.
