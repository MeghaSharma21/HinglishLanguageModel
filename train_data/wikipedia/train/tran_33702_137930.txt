 swayamvar pracheen kaal se prachlit ek hindu parampara he jisame kanya swayan apana var chunati thi or usase uskaa vivaah hota tha.
 is baat ke pramaan hai ki vaidic kaal main yah pratha samaaj ke chaaron varnon main prachlit or vivaah kaa praaroop tha. ramayana or mahabharat kaal main bhi yah pratha rajanya varg main prachlit thi, parantu iskaa rup kuch sankuchit ho gaya tha. rajanya kanya pati kaa varan swayamvar main karti thi parantu yah samaaj dwaara manyata pradaan karne ke hetu thi. kanya ko pati ke varan main swatantraaa na thi. pita kii sharton ke anusaar purna yogyata praapt vyakti hi chunaa ja saktaa tha. purvamadhyakaal main bhi is pratha ke prachlit rahane ke pramaan mili hai, jaisaa sanyogita ke swayamvar se spasht he.
