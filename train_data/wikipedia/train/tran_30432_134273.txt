 eastern institute for integratid larning in management uniwarsity yaa iilem vishvavidhyalay, sikkim ke jorethang main sthit vishvavidhyalay he. isaki sthapana sikkim rajya vidhaan adhinium sankhya 4, 2006, sikkim sarkaar ke tahat hui he. yah vishvavidhyalay anudaan ayog dwaara may, 2008 se puri tarah manyata or sweekruti praapt he. iilem vishvavidhyalay bharat kaa pehla vishvavidhyalay he jise eukeees dwaara pratishthit iesao 9001:14001 praaanpatr pradaan kiya gaya he.
 iilem vishvavidhyalay kaa prayojak nikaay, ‘eastern institute for integratid larning in management –kolkata’ he jisaki sthapana vividh programs ke madhyam se bharat main behtareen tathaa karier unnmukhi shiksha pradaan karne ke uddeshy 1995 main kii gai thi.
 apane sthapana kaal se hi bharat sarkaar kii akhil bhartiya takneekee shiksha parishad ne iilem ke vyapaar prabandhan main snatakottar diploma ko sweekruti de di thi. santhaan ne vishwa bharti, jo 1951 se kendriya vishvavidhyalay he, ke madhyam se emb program prarambh kar die the. iilem ko vishwa bharti ke sanghatak mahavidhyalay kaa darja praapt hua. vishwa bharti ne apane board of studies main iilem vishvavidhyalay ke pratinidhiyon ko shaamil kiya tha. iilem ke vibhinn factoti sadasyon dwaara pariksha patr taiyaar kiye gaye the or unki jaanch bhi kii gai thi.
 iilem institute, kolkata ko aktubar 2007 main bijness india dwaara ‘e’ grad pradaan kiya gaya he. is santhaan ne sabhi sheersh udyogon or karporate ke saath samjhauta kiya he or shat-pratishat placement kaa ricard iske naam he.
