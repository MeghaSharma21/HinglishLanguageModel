 kautilia arthshaastra se hamen antpal naamak rajkarmchaariyon kaa pataa chalata he jo seemaant ke rakshak hote the or jinka vetan kumaar, paur, vyaavhaarik, mantri tathaa rashtrapal ke baraabar hota tha.
 ashok ke samay antpal hi antamahamaatr kahalaane lage. guptapal main antpal gopta kahalaane lage the. malvicagnimitra naatak main virasen tathaa ek anya antpal kaa ullekh hua he. virasen, narmada ke kinare sthit antpal durg kaa adhipati tha. antapalon kaa kaary mahatvapurn tha; greek karmchaaree 'strategus' se in padaadhikariyon kii tulana karnaa sahaj he. antpal shabd saadharantaya seemaant pradesh ke shaasha yaa governor ko nirdisht karata he. yah shaasha sainik, asainik donon hi prakaar kaa hota tha.
