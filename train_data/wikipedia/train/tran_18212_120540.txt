 parsac lambaai kii khagoleey ikai he. yah 30 trillian kilometre ke lagbhag hoti he. parsac kaa prayog khagolashastra main hota he. isaki lambaai trikoshmetiya digbhed par aadhaarit he, jo ki sitaron ke bich duuri naapane kaa pracheen tarika he.
 iskaa naam digbhed ke angreji naam paralex yaa "parallax or second of arc" yani arcsacind, se bana he. ek parsac pathvi se kisi khagoleey pind kii duuri hoti he, jab vah pind ek arcsacind ke digbhed kone par hota he. parsac kii vaastavik lambaai lagbhag 30.86 petametre, 3.262 prakaash-varsh yaa 1.918×1013 miles ke baraabar hoti he
