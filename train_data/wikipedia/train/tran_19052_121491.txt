 89 wein academy puraskaar samaaroh, moshan pikchar arts and sciensej dwaara 2016 kii sarvshereshth filmon kaa sammaan karne ke liye, 26 farvari, 2017 ko los angeles, california main dolbi theatre main yojit kiya gaya tha. samaaroh ke dauraan, AMPAS academy puraskaar 24 shreniyon main prastut kiya gaya. nirmaataa maaikel di luka or jenifer tod, tathaa glen veis dwaara nirdeshit is samaaroh kaa tivi prasaaran abisi dwaara kiya gaya. hasya abhinayta jimi kimail ne is samaaroh kaa sanchaalan kiya. samaaroh main ek rochak vaakya bhi hua, jab galat ghoshnaa ke kaaran sarvashreth film kaa puraskaar moonlite kii jagah laa laa land ko mil gaya, halaaki baad main galati main sudhar kar liya gaya.
 89wein academy puraskaar ke pratyaashiyon kii ghoshnaa, 24 janvari, 2017 ko academy kii vaishvik live stream ke madhyam se kii gai. laa laa land ek saath chaudaa namakan ke saath record banane main kaamyaab rahaa.. araival or moonlite aath namakan ke saath duusare sthaan par hai.
 vijetaon ko pehle soochibaddh kiya gaya hai.
