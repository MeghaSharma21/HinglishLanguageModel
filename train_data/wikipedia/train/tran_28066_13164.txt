 zoramthanga bhartiya rajya mizoram ke purv mukhyamantri hai jo is pad par 4 disambar 1998 se disambar 2008 tak rahe. unki party mizo neshnal frant 2008 ke chunaavon main bhartiya rashtriya congress se chunaav haar gai. is haar ke baad unhone madan mohan lakhera ko 8 disambar 2008 ko tyaag patr de diya or teen din baad agle mukhyamantri ke kaarybhaar grahan karte hi kaarybhaar samaapt hua.
 pehle mizo neshnal frant ek pratibandhit sanghathan tha or gupt tareeke se ek alag desh ke liye sakriy tha. inhen halaat main 1966 main paas haa ek snaatak baagi ban gaya tha. zoramthanga ko ran bang ilaake ke sachiv ke pad kii peshkash kii gai or unhone teen varsh tak ye jimmedaari sanbhali. 1969 main mizo neshnal frant ke saare cader tatkaaleen purvi pakistan chale gaye. baad main laldenga ne zoramthanga ko apana sachiv bana liya jis pad par ve saat varshon tak bane rahe. 1979 main ve mizo neshnal frant ke upaadhyaksh ban gaye. vidroh ke dauraan sena ne zoramthanga ko giraftaar kar liya. 1990 main hui laldenga kii mrityu tak zoramthanga unke sabase bharosemand sahyogi the atah mizo neshnal frant ke adhyaksh bane. 25 varshon ke sangharsh ke baad mizo neshnal frant ne 30 june 1986 ko aitihaasik mizo shanti samjhaute par hastakshar kiya or 1987 main mizoram ko purna rajya kaa darja mil gaya. is samjhaute ke baad zoramthanga raajnneeti main aaye or apane pehle hi chunaav main chanfai se pehali baar vidhayak bane. baagi se mukhyamantri bane laldenga ke mantrimandal main unhen vitt or shiksha mantralay kii jimmedaari di gai. 1998 ke vidhansabha chunaav se thik pehle zoramthanga ne mizoram pipuls conference se gathjod kar liya. is siyaasi gathjod ne rajya vidhansabha kii 40 siton main se 33 jeet lee thim or wo pehali baar mizoram ke mukhyamantri bane. 2003 ke chunaav main unhone akele hi chunaav ladaa or rajya main jeet darj kii. 2008 ke chunaav main unki party keval 3 vidhayakon tak simit ho gai. zoramthanga ne apni chanfaai seat ko bhi ganvaa diya jise ve 1987 se jitate aa rahe the.
