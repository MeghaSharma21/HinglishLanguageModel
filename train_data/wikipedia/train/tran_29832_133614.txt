 mona ghosh shetty ek bhartiya aawaaj dabing kalakaar, gayak or nirdeshak hai. vah purv aawaaj dabing kalakaar lila roy ghosh kii beti hai jo apane kshetra main bahut anbhavi thim. vah andheri, mumbai main sthit sound and vijan india kii adhyaksh hai. iske pehle lila roy ghosh isaki sansthaapak or adhyachh thim jinka 2012 main nidhan ho gaya. tab se pehle vah sansthaapak thim or mona ke saath milakar adhyaksh ke rup main studio chalaati thim. wo hindi, bangla, angreji, marathi or urdu main baat kar sakati hai.
 vah kishor, yuva vayask or madhyam aayu varg ke mahila paatro ke liye dabing karne main maahir hai. 15 se adhik varshon tak unhone kai pramukh mahilaon kii bhoomika ke liye hindi main dab kiya he jaise : camerun diaaz, angelina joli, ket bakinsle, karston danst, hel beri, jho saldana, druyu bairemor, catherine jita-jones, jaclyn furnandis, katrina caff, deepika padukon. vah abhi bhi un abhinetriyon ke liye pramukh manyata praapt hindi dabing or aawaaj kalakaar he. shetty tivi vigyapanon ke liye bhi dabing karti hai jaisaaki unhone ek bhartiya vimaan seva ke ek vigyaapan ke liye kiya he. unhone haal hi main yashraj studio main nargis fakhri ke 2011 kii film "rockstar" ke liye dabing kii.
