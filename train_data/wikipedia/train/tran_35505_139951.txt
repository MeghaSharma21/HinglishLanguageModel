 
 mayawati bhartiya raajnneetijna evam bahujan samaaj party kii rashtriya adhyaksha he, jo bhartiya samaaj ke sabase kamjor virgon - bahujanon yaa anusuchit jatiyon or anusuchit janajatiyon, anya pichhadaa varg or dharmik alpsankhyakon ke jivan main sudhaar ke liye saamaajik parivartan ke ek manch par kendrit he. dalit raajnneeti kii purodha bhartiya raajnneeti main apana dakhal rakhane vaali is dalit mahila ne chaar baar uttar pradesh ke mukhyamantri pad kii bagdor sanbhali.
 mayawati kaa janm 15 janvari 1956 ko shrimati sucheta kriplani aspataal, nai dilli main ek hindu jatav parivaar main hua tha. unke pita, prabhu das, badalpur, gautam buddha nagar main ek daakghar karmchaaree the. parivaar ke putron ko nijee skulon main bheja gaya tha, jabaki betiyaan "kam-pradarshan vaali sarkari vidyalayon" main gai thim. baad main unke pita bhartiya dak-taar vibhaag ke anubhag pradhan ke pad se seva nivrutt hue.mayawati ke 6 bhai evam 2 bahanen hai. mayawati ke purvaj gram badalpur jila gautamabuddha nagar, uttar pradesh ke rahane vaale the. log sammaan main inhen bahan ji kah kar pukaarte hai. unke parivaar kaa sambandh dalit upjati se he. 
 prarambh aap ne je je colony dilli ke ek skool main shikshan kaary kiya. kisi samay unhone bhartiya prashasnic seva kii parikshaon ke liye adhyayan bhi kiya tha 
 san 1977 main kanshiram ke sampark main aane ke baad unhone ek purna kaalik raajnneetijna banane kaa nirnay le liya. kanshiram ke sangrakshan ke antargat ve us samay unki kor team kaa hissaa raheen, jab san 1984 main basapa kii sthapana hui thi.
 uttar pradesh kii raajnneeti main mayawati ek majboot stambh he. aap ke dwaara uttar pradesh main mukhyamantri pad kaa kaary kaal nimnavat rahaa
