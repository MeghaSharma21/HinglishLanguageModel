 semra bujurg bhartiya rajya bihar ke sivaan jile ke nautan vikaskhand main sthit ek madhyam aakaar kaa gaanv he. yah gram panchayat bhi he jiskaa pramukh sarpanch hota he.
 varsh 2011 kii janaganana ke aankadon ke anusaar is gaanv main kul 201 pariwaron main 1633 log nivas kar rahe the jinamein purushon kii sankhya 862 or mahilaon kii sankhya 771 thi. is prakaar, gaanv main linganupaat 918 mahila prati hajaar purush tha. gaanv kii 75.6% jansankhya sakshar thi jo bihar rajya kii ausat sakshartaa dar 61.8% kii tulana main behatar he. purush sakshartaa 87.05% or mahila sakshartaa 63.05% darj kii gayi.
 chah varsh se kam aayu sanvarg kii kul jansankhya 260 thi jo jo kul jansankhya kaa 15.92% he tathaa is aayu varg main linganupaat 935 tha jo kul jansankhya ke linganupaat se behatar he.
