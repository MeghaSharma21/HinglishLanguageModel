 kaag vrikshon ke tanon main bahyatvacha sthaan par avasthit mrit koshikaon ke bane utakon kaa motaa str hota he. inke kaaran saamanyat: hawa or pani ped ke bheetar nahin ja sakate. praaia: sabhi vrikshon main kaag paaya jaataa he, parantu kuch vrikshon ke tanon par kaag prachur maatra main banataa he, jaise tvaksha-banju main. inamen se samay-samay par yah vyapaar ke liye nikaala jaataa he. yah paudha fogesi kul kaa sadsy he. tvaksha-vanju ke vriksh 30 se 40 foot tak unche hote hai. ye dakshini europe tathaa africa ke uttari samudri taton ke deshaj hai. 15 se 20 varshiya vrikshon se kaag nikalne lagata he. june se agast tak yah kaary sampann hota he. bhoomi se kuch upar or phir shakhaon ke kuch neeche tane ke chaaron ore gaddha kaat diya jaataa he. iske baad kaag ko in donon kate bhaagon ke bich main se lambii pattiyon ke rup main nikaal liya jaataa he.
 kaag purnataya koshikaon se bana rahata he. prakritik kaag ke ek ghan inch main lagbhag 20,00,00,000 sookshm, vaayu se bhari mrit koshikaaye rahati hai. kaag kaa aapekshik gurutv keval lagbhag 0.25 hota he. kaag kii utpalavakta, sampidyata, pratyaastha, vaayu or pani kii apraveshyata, ucch gharshan-gunank, nune ushma-chaalakata aadi gun isaki vishisht rachana ke falswaroop hote hai.
 19veen shatabdi ke lagbhag ant tak kaag botalon ke dato, pluvon, utplavon, topon or juuton ke talle banane ke kaam aata tha. iske pashchaat iskaa upyog anek anya aavashyak kaaryon main bhi hone laga, jaise achaalak kaag daftiyon dwaara sheet godaamon ke banane main tathaa motaron ke gascat or khaane pine kii vastuon ko pack karne ke liye.
