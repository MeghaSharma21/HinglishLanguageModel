 jo vastu hamaari aankhon se dikhaai na de, vah adrishy kehalaati he. 
 adrishy hone kaa bhav adrishyata kehalaata he. adrishyata us vastu kii sthiti he jise dekha nahin ja saktaa he. shabd kaa prayog aksar fantasy / vigyaan katha main kiya jaataa he, jahaan vastuon ko jaadui yaa takneekee madhyamon se nahin dekha ja saktaa he; haalanki, iske prabhaav vaastavik duniya main vishesh rup se bhautikee or avadharanatmak manovigyaan virgon main bhi pradarshit kiye ja sakate hai. 
 chunki padaarthon ko apni satahon ko pratibimbit karne or darshakon kii ankhon ko maarane vaale strot se dikhaai dene vaale spectrum main prakaash dwaara dekha ja saktaa he, isliye adrishyata kaa sabase prakritik rup ek aisa vastu he jo na to prakaash ko pratibimbit karata he or na hi avashoshit karata he . ise paardarshita ke rup main janaa jaataa he, or kai swaabhaavik rup se hone vaali samagriyon main dekha jaataa he 
 adrishyata kii dharna kai optical or drishy karkon par nirbhar karti he. udhaaharan ke liye, adrishyata paryvekshak or / yaa upyog kiye gaye upkarnon kii ankhon par nirbhar karti he. is prakaar kisi vastu ko kisi vyakti, pashu, upkaran ityadi ke rup main vargeekrut kiya ja saktaa he. sensorial dharna par shodh main yah dikhaaya gaya he ki adrishyata chakron main dekhi jaati he. 
 adrishyata ko aksar chhadam rup kaa sarvocch rup mana jaataa he, kyonki yah darshakon ko kisi bhi prakaar ke mahatvapurn sanket, drishy prabhaav, yaa maanav ankhon ke liye ja sakane vaale vidyut chumbakeey spectrum kii aavritiyon ko prakat nahin karata he, balki radio, infrared yaa parabangnee tarang dairdhya.
