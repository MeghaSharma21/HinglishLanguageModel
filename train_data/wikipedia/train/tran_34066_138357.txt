 lakshmi chandra jain bharat ke gandhivadi arthshaastri the. bharat main aarthik niyojan ke pichley pachaas varshon ke idihas par dow jain kii baarik najar thi. wo yojana ayog ke sadsy rahe or dakshin africa main bharat ke rajdoot bhi. wo maanate the ki hamaari pramukh samasya naukarshahi par nirbharta he. unke mutabik gandhi yah bhalee bhaanti samajhate the ki janata kii bhagidaari ke bina koi kaam nahin kiya janaa chahie or janata kii bhagidaari ke bina koi kaam safal bhi nahin ho saktaa, lekin aazaadee ke baad ham yah buniyadi baat bhul gaye.
