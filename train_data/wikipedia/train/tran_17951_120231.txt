 madhyasthata ek vaikalpik vivaad samaadhaan prakram he jisamen pakshakar kisi tisare vyakti ke hastkshep ke madhyam se tathaa nyaayalay kaa sahaara liye bina apane vivaadon kaa niptaan karavaate hai. yah aisi vidhi he jisamen vivaad kisi namit vyakti ke saamne rakhaa jaataa he jo donon pakshon ko sunne ke pashchaat ardh-nyaayik tareeke se masale kaa nirnay karata hai. udaahranarth 'panch' yaa 'panchayat' ko koi vivaad sanderbhit karnaa madhyastham kaa ek rup he. saamanyat:, vivadakari paksh apana maamla kisi madhyastham nyaayaadhikaran ko sanderbhit karte hai tathaa nyaayaadhikaran dwaara liya gaya nirnay 'award' kehalaata he. madhyastham kaa prayog mukhyat: vyapaar kshetron main kiya jaataa he jaise nirmaan pariyojnaayen, nauvahan tathaa sanvahan, patent, kaarobaar chihn tathaa brands, vittiya sevaayen jinamein banking tathaa beemaa shaamil he, videshi sahyog, bhagidaari vivaad ityadi.
 samaadhaan samadhankarta kii sahaayta se pakshakaron dwaara vivaadon ke sauhardpurn niptaan kii prakriya he. yah madhyastham se is arth main bhinn he ki madhyastham main award tisare pakshakaar yaa madhyastham nyaaydhikaran kaa nirnay he jabaki samaadhaan ke maamle main nirnay pakshakaron kaa hota he jise samadhankarta kii madhyastham se liya jaataa he.
 vivaad samaadhaan kii aisi vidhiyaan kanoonee mukademe kii tulana main kai prakaar se laabhprad hai :- 
 madhyasth ke kaary is prakaar he:-
