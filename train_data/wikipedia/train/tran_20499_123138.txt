 bowing bi-17 flaaing fortress ek chaar-ingine vaala baari bamavarshak vimaan tha, jise sanyukt rajya america ne dwitiya vishwa yuddh main istemaal kiya tha. yah vimaan sanyukt rajya america vaayu sena kor ke liye bowing company ne 1930 ke dashak main viksit kiya gaya tha.
 8 agast 1934 ke din air kor ne martin bi-10 hamalavar vimaan kii jagah lene vaale ek nae bahu-ingine hamalavar ke liye nivida amntrit kii. 200 hamalaavaron ke nirmaan ke is anubandh ko jeetne ke liye bowing kaa muqabla duglas or martin jaisi companiyon ke saath tha. prototip durghatnagrast hone kii vajah se bowing ko anubandh naa mila sakaa, lekin air kor ko bowing kaa dizin itnaa pasand aayaa ki unhone akalan ke liye 13 or adhik bi-17 vimanon kaa order de diya. 1938 main laanch ke baad bi-17 ke dizin ko or adhik viksit kiya gaya.,bi-17 kaa prayog mukhyatah dwitiya vishwa yuddh abhiyaan ke dauraan german audyogic or sainya thikaanon ke khilaaf dindahaade bambaari main kiya gaya. kuch had tak bi-17 kaa prayog prashanth mahaasaagar ke yuddh main japani shipping or hawaii addon ke khilaaf chaape maarane main bhi kiya gaya.
 america ne bi-17 kaa prayog ek ranneetik hathiyaar ke rup main kiya. yah ek aisa shaktishaalee, unchi udaan bharne vaala, lambii duuri kaa bamavarshak tha, jo khud ko bachaane main or ladaai main vyapak kshati ke baavjood ghar lautne main sakasham tha. dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke dauraan bi-17 ne kisi bhi anya america vimaan se zyada bam giraae. america vimaanon dwaara germany par giraae 1,500,000 metric ton bamon main se 640,000 ton bi-17 ne giraae the. bhayanak yuddh main bhi nuksaan se bache rahane ke bi-17 ke kisson or tasveeron ko kaafii prachaar mila, jis vajah se is vimaan kii bhavy chhavi or adhik bad gai.
