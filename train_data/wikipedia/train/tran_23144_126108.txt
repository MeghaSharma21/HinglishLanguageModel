 acharya ramamurti treepathi or acharya ramamurti alag-alag vyakti hai; bhramit na hon.
 acharya ramamurti treepathi janm - 4 janvari 1929 nidhan- 30 march 2009) shiksha: kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay se em.e., pi-ech.di.; saahityacharya, sahityaratna. kavyashastra evam darshan ke prakand pandit.
 hindi evam sanskrit ke vidwaan evam samalochak the. ve sagar vishvavidhyalay main praadhyaapak rahe; vikram vishvavidhyalay main hindi ke vibhaagadhyaksh rahe tathaa kai vishvavidhyalayon ke atithi shikshak bhi rahe. ve shabd shakti evam ras vichaar ke apratim vyakhyaakaar the. 
 acharya ramamurti treepathi kaa janm varanasi ke nivin kala gaanv main 4 janvari san 1929 ko hua tha. unhone em e, piechdi evam di lit kii oopaadhi kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay se lee thi. shrimath, kaashi ne unhen jagadguru ramanandacharya puraskaar se sammanit kiya tha. bharat kaa aisa pehla math he jo har varsh sanskrit-hindi sahitya ke ek vidwaan ko ek lakh rupaya dekar sammanit karata he.
