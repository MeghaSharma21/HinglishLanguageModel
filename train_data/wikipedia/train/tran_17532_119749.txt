 roman dharm pracheen rome nagar or italy desh kaa sabase mukhya- or rajdharm tha. roman dharm sami dharm bilkul nahin tha. wo ek bhaaropee dharm tha. ye ek murtipujak or bahudevataavaadi dharm tha. isamen ek adrishy ishwar kii avadharana nahin thi. isai dharm ke rajdharm banane ke baad isaayiyon ne isapar pratibandh laga diya. iske baad ye lupt ho gaya.
 is dharm ke kai devataaon ke samtulya devtaa pracheen unani dharm main, germanik dharm or farsi dharm main milte hai.
 is dharm main kai devtaa the : jupiter, bakcus, apollo, kupid, mars, maracury, pluto, saturn, vulcon, nepchun, mithras, ityadi . roman devataaon main baad ke roman samraat bhi shaamil the.
 pramukh deviyaan thim juno, minerva, siris, flora, fortuna, dyna, venus, ityadi.
 puja mukhyatah pashubali dwaara hoti thi . is dharm main kuch khaas aadhyatmikta nahin thi.
