 paraseete krimi sankraman ko nematod sankraman bhi kehete hai. ye sankraman helliminthiasis jaisaa sankraman hota he, jo nematod failam ke jeevon dwaara hota he. nimatod paraseete hote hai. paraseete vah keetanu he jo vyakti main pravesh karke baahar yaa bheetar jud jaati he or saare poshak tatv ko chus leti he. kuch paraseete arthath krimi antat kamjor padakar vyakti main beemaari failaate hai. krimi lambey, aavaranheen or bina haddi vaale hote hai. inke bachche andey yaa krimi kosh se dimbhak  ke rup main badhate hue twachaa, mansapeshiyaan, fefda yaa aant ke us utak main krimi ke rup badhate jate hai jise ve sankramit karte hai.
 is rog main rogi ko khaane hetu taral padaarth hi den. iske alaava puraa aaraam mili. parivaar ke sabhi sadasyon kaa pareekshan or upachaar karana shreshth hoga. upachaar puraa hone tak antahvastr, kapade, chadar aadi ko garm pani se dhonaa chaiye. haath dhote rahanaa, bina pakaaya va kaccha aahaar na lenaa, fal va subjiyon ko acchhee tarah dhonaa or pani ko ubaal kar pinaa chaiye.
