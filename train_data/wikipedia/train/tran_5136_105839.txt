 bharat ke chennai main sthit pawan urja praudyogiki kendra bharat sarkaar ke aparmparik urja strot mantralay ke adheen swytt anusandhan evam vikaas sanstha he. yah santhaan pawan urja ke kshetra main anya anusandhan evam vikaas sansthaanon ke sahyog se pawan tarabine kii up-pranaaliyon tathaa ghatakon ke vikaas main naveen uplabdhiyon par dhyaan degaa. 
 pawan urja praudyogiki kendra ke kriyakalapon ko in paanch saamaanya kshetron main vargeekrut kiya gaya he:
 vartmaan pawan tarabine sansthapanon ke kaary nishpaadan main sudhaar laate hue unnat pawan tarabine praudyogiki ke kshetra main anusandhan karnaa hi anusandhan evam vikaas vibhaag ke pramukh kriyalap hai.
 anusandhan evam vikaas vibhaag ne vartmaan pawan tarabine sansthapanon ke kshetra main dhyaan dete hue pawan kheton ke grid sambandhit anveshan, adhiktam urja prapti hetu blade ke anukool kone tathaa pawan tarabinon ke giyar dibbon kii asafalataaon ke vishleshan main kaary karte hai.
