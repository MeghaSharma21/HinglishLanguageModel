 pashuon main punchhkatwa rog yaa deganala rog mukhyatah bhens jati ke pashuon main hota he. vaise go-vansh ke pashu bhi is sankraman ke shikaar hote hai. is beemaari kaa nishchit kaaran abhi tak gyaat nahin he parantu deganala ek faphund se paidaa hone vaala rog he. is beemaari main pashuon ke kaan, punch evam khur sookhne lagate he or antat sad kar gir jate he. pashu bhojan karnaa band kar detaa he evam dinon din kamjor hote jaataa he. kuch vaigyanikon kaa yah mat he ki yah beemaari sookshm poshak tatwon kii kami ke vajah se haata he. adhik nami yukt puaal yaa bhusa khilaane se yah rog aamtaur par jaanwaron main hota he.
