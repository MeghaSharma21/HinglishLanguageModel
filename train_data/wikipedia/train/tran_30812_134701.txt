 najam sethi, ek varisht evam mashhoor pakistani patrkaar hai. ve 27 march 2013 se 7 june 2013 tak pakistan ke praant punjab ke purv kaaryvahak mukhyamantri bhi the. ve ek kaaphi mashhoor evam puraskrit patrkaar hone ke saath hi vivaadaaspad hasti bhi hai. ve ek patrkaar, sampadak, sameekshak tathaa ek patrakaari vyaktitv bhi hai. ve lahore-aadhaarit raajnneetik saptahik, da friday times ke mukhya sampadak he, evam puvatah daily times or daily aajkal jaise akhabaaron ke sampadak bhi rah chuke hai. ve pakistan ke samachar channel jio tivi par aapas kii baat naamak ek saadhaaran gyaan evam raajnneetik tippanikari kaarykram chalate hai. saath hi, ve vanguard books naamak ek prakaashan evam pustak vekreta chen ke maalik bhi hai. unhen pakistani raajnneeti par apane bebaank bol or sambandhit aalochanaon or patrikarita ke liye janaa jaataa he.
 varsh 1999 main, unhen, pakistan kii iesai dwaara, british broadcasting korporation ko diya gaye sarkari bhrashtachar sambandhit apane ek intervyuu ke liye giraftaar kar liya gaya tha, or kareeb ek maheene tak hiraasat main rakhaa gaya tha.
 1999 main unhen international press freedom award se bhi nawaz gaya tha, or 2009 main unako world association of neuspapers dwaara golden pen of freedom se puraskrit kiya gaya tha.
 26 march 2013 ko unhen shaasha evam vipakshi dalon kii sweekruti se punjab ke antarim kaaryvahak mukhyamantri ke pad ke liye namankit kiya gaya tha. unhone 27 march ko shapath dilaai gayi, or ve is pad par 7 june 2013 tak the.
