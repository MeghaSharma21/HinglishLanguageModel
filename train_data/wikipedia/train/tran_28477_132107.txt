 changdu je-20 ek single seat, do jet engine, sabhi mausam vaala paanchavin peedi kaa stealth ladaaku vimaan he. jise china kii changdu airospes corporation dwaara chini vaayu sena ke liye viksit kiya gaya he. changdu je-20 ne apni pehali udaan 11 janvari 2011 ko kii thi, lekin vimaan ko aadhikarik taur par 2016 main china antarrashtriya vimaanan evam airospes pradarshnee par prakashit kiya gaya tha. march 2017 main, chini media ne bataaya ki vimaan ne vaayu sena ke bheetar simit seva ke saath praarambhik parichaalan kshamta charan main pravesh kiya he.
 changdu je-20 ko 1990 ke je-XX kaarykram ke tahat banaaya gaya he. changdu je-20 ko sateek atake kshamta ke saath hawaii varishthata ladaaku vimaan banane ke liye banaaya gaya he. sitambar 2017 main, changdu je-20 ne aadhikarik taur par chini vaayu sena main pravesh kiya, vah vishwa main paanchvaan peedi ke stealth ladaaku vimaanon main teesraa or asia main sabase pehla oparational ladaaku vimaan ban gaya. changdu je-20 gharelu chini tahang ingine se las kiya jayega.
 je-xx kaarykram 1990 ke dashak ke ant main shuru hua tha. changdu airospes corporation ne chini vaayu sena ke 2008 kii pratiyogita main shenyang airospes corporation ko haraakar namit project 718 ko jeet tha. jiske antargat changdu je-20 ko banaaya janaa tha.
 2009 main, chini vaayu sena ke ek varisht adhikari ne bataaya ki 2019 tak seva main shaamil kii taarikh ke saath 2010-11 main isaki pehali udaan kii ummid he.
 22 disambar 2010 ko, pehle je-20 prototip kaa changdu aircraft dizin santhaan ke baahar hai speed taxing test kiya gaya. teen maheene baad, pehle je-20 prototip ne chengdu main apni pehali udaan kii.
 2014 main, je-20 prototip main kai badlaav kiye gaye hai, jisamen nae lo-said effect or stealth coating shaamil hai, saath hi dobaaraa se dizine lambavat stabligers bhi shaamil hai. vishleshak ne dhyaan diya ki bahu-bhoomika ke liye kuch upkarnon or upkarnon ko prastut kiya gaya he jaise ki sateek-maargdarshika ke liye ekikrut lakshyikaran pod, or chah atirikt niskriy avarakt sensor jo vimaan ke chaaron ore dekhne main madad karte hai.
 disambar 2015 main, sainya paryvekshak dwaara je-20 kaa praarambhik utpaadan sanskaran dekha gaya tha.
 oktober 2017 ko, chini rajya media kii report he ki je -20 ke dizin ko antim rup diya gaya he, or vah bade paimaane par utpaadan ke liye taiyaar he or saath hi ise yuddh ke liye bhi taiyaar kiya ja rahaa he.
