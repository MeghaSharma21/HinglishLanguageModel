 martand verma, kerala kaa sahityakar si ve raman pillai kaa 1981 main prakashit hua ek malayalam upanyaas he. raja rama verma ke antim shasankaal se martand verma kaa rajbhishek tak venat kaa itihaas aakhyaan karnaa ek atishyoktipurna katha rup main hi is upanyaas prastut kiya he. kollvarsh 901-906 samay main hua is kahani kaa sheershak paatr ko singhaasana vaaris kaa sthaan se hataane ke liye padmanabhan tampi or ettuvittil pillon ne litayaa dushkarm yojanaon se sangrakshit karnaa anantapadmanabhan, mankoyikkal kuruppu or subhadr logon se jute he yah katha. 
 malayalam sahitay kaa pehla aitihaasik upanyas hokar yah sahityak rachana prastut anubhag main aitihaasik aakhyayika naamak ek shakha aarambh kiya. tiruvitankur itihaas dharmraja, ram raja bahadur naamak upanayaason main jari rahata he. is teen upanyaason ko siviute charitrakhyayikkal naam se prastut sahitay main kaha jate hai.
 yah upanyaas malayalam sahitya main romaanchak kahani or itihaas kaa ek utkrisht mishrit kriti mana jaataa he.
