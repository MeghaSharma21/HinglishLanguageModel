 dagistaan uttari cocks kshetra main sthit rus kaa ek rajya he. bhashaa or jaati ke nazariye se is pradesh main bahut vividhta he. yahan zyaadaatar kocksi, altaai or irani bhashaayen boli jaati hai. yahan kii sab se badi jatiyaan avar, dargin, kumik, lazgi or laac hai. haalanki yahan ke keval 4. 7% log roosi hai, phir bhi roosi yahan kii rajbhasha he. samay-samay par is ilaake main samudaayon ke bich tanaav kaa vaataavaran or jhadpen hoti rahati hai or aatankwaad ke bhi haadase hote rahete hai.
 iskaa sarkari naam "dagistaan ganraajya" he. ganraajya, rus ke avarohi kram main duusare str ke vibhaag hote hai. dagistaan kii rajdhani makhaachaqalaa he.
 dagistaan kaa kshetrafal 50,300 varg kimi he, jo kii bharat ke punjab rajya ke lagbhag baraabar he.
 2002 main dagistaan kii aabaadi 2,576,531 thi, jisamen se 42.8% log shahari kshetron main or 57.2% gramin ilaakon main rahete the. bhinn jatiyon ke jansankhya aankade beete varshon ke saath badalte gaye hai or kuch is prakaar hai -
