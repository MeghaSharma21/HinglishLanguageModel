 natasha devanshu vatsa dwaara rachit ek comic paatr he jo maithili bhashaa kii pehali chitrakatha shrinkhalaa he. yah chitrakatha 17 july, 1999 ko astitv main ai par maithili main bal patrikaon kii kami ke kaaran sitambar dwitiya 1999 main hindi kii bal patrika balahans main pehali baar prakashit hui. uske baad se natasha chitrakatha hindi-angrezi-maithili ke vibhinn patr-patrikaon, i-patrikaon main prakashit hoti rahi he.
 natasha 10 varsh kii ek choti-si chulabuli ladki he, jo apni sharaarton or hajir javaabi se pathakon ko gudgudati he. kabhi vah dusron ko murkh banaati he to kabhi swayan hi murkh ban jaati he. natasha ke sahyogi paatr romi, dadaji, dezi or bhondumal hai. romi, natasha kaa dost he, dadaji skool main shikshak hai, daiji pados main rahane vaali didi he or bhondumal natasha ke yahan naukari karte hai. idhar *shruti prakaashan ne natasha chitrakatha ko maithili bhashaa main kitaab ke rup main prakashit kiya he. sheeghra hi iskaa hindi or angreji sanskaran bazaar main uplabdh ho sakegaa. yah chitrakatha *Bird Features par bhi uplabdh he.
