 tata steel arthath tisco ke naam se jane jane vaali yah bharat kii pramukh ispaat company he. jamshedpur sthit is kaarkaane kii sthapana 1907 main kii gayi thi. yah duniya kii paanchavi sabase bady ispaat company he jisaki vaarshik utpaadan kshamta 28 million ton he. yah farchune 500 companiyon main bhi shumaar he jisamen iskaa sthaan 315 waan he. company kaa mukhyalay mumbai main sthit he. yah brihatar tata samooh kii ek agrani company he. tata steel bharat kii sabase zyada munafa kamaane vaali niji kshetra kii duusari bady company bhi he jisaki sakal vaarshik aay 1,32,110 crores rupee he jisamen 31 march 2008 ko samaapt hue varsh main shuddh laabh 12,350 crores rupee darj kiya gaya tha.
 company kaa mukhya plant jamshedpur, jharkhand main sthit he halaaki haal ke adhigrahano ke baad isane bahurashtriya company kaa rup hasil kar liya he jiskaa kaam kai deshon main hota he. varsh 2000 main ise duniya main sabase kam laagat main ispaat banane vaali company kaa khitaab bhi hasil hua. 2005 main ise duniya main sarvshereshth ispaat banane kaa khitaab bhi mila tha world steel dianemics. company mumbai stock exange ke saath saath neshnal stock exange main bhi suchit he evam varsh 2007 ke aankado ke anusaar isamen lagbhag 82,700 karmchaaree karyrat hai.
