 khaanat turki-mongol samskruti main kisi aise rajya ko kehete hai jisapar kisi khan kii oopaadhi vaale shaasha kaa rajya ho. isaki tulana hindi ke 'sultanate' shabd se kii ja sakati he jo kisi aise rajya ko kehete hai jahaan 'sultan' kaa rajya ho. isi tarah 'badshahath' main 'badshaah' kaa, 'riyasat' main kisi 'rais' kaa or 'emirate' main kisi 'amir' kaa raaj hota he. aadhunik turki bhashaa main 'khaanat' ko 'kamanlik' kehete hai.
 'khaanat' main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
