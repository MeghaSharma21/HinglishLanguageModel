 
 vilaayat khan kaa janm 1928 main gauripur main ek sangitajna parivaar main hua tha. pita, prakhyat sitar vadak ustad inayat hossain khaan, kii jaldi maut ke baad unhone apane naanaa or mamaa se sitar bajaanaa siikhaa. aath varsh kii umr main pehali baar unke sitarvaadan kii recording hui. unhone paanch dashakon se bhi adhik samay tak apane sitar kaa jaadu bikheraa. ustad vilaayat khaan kii pichhali kai pushtein sitar se judi raheen or unke pita inayat hossain khaan se pehle ustad imadad hossain khaan bhi jane-maane sitarvadak rahe the. ustad vilaayat khaan ke donon bete, sujaat hossain khaan or hidaayat khaan bhi tathaa unke bhai imrat hossain khaan or bhattije rais khaan bhi jane maane sitar vadak hai.
 ve sambhawatah bharat ke pehle sangeetkaar the jinhone bharat kii azaadi ke baad england jaakar sangeet pesh kiya tha. vilaayat khaan ek saal main aath maheene videsh main bitaayaa karte the or neujersey unka doosraa ghar ban chukaa tha. vilaayat khaan ne sitar vaadan kii apni alag style, gayaki style, viksit kii thi jisamen shrotaaon par gayan kaa ahsaas hota tha. unki kala ke sammaan main raashtrapati fakhruddin ali ahamed ne unhen aafatab-e-sitar kaa sammaan diya tha or ye sammaan paanevaale ve ekmaatr sitarvadak the. lekin ustad vilaayat khan ne 1964 main padmashri or 1968 main padmavibhushan sammaan ye kehete hue thukraa die the ki bharat sarkaar ne hindustani shastreey sangeet main unke yogadan kaa samuchit sammaan nahin kiya. unke parivaar main unki do patniyaan, do bete or do betiyaan bhi hai.
  13 march 2004 ko unka dehanth ho gaya. unhen fefde kaa cancer tha jiske ilaaj ke liye ve jaslok aspataal main bhartee the. unka adhiktar jivan kolkata main beetaa or unka antim sanskaar bhi unhen unke pita kii qabr ke sameep dafana kar kiya gaya.
