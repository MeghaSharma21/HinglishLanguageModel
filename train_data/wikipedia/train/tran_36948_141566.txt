 german romantika yaa german masoorika atyant sookshm vishaanu dwaara hota he jiskaa naam rubella he. varsh ke purvardh main prakope adhik hota he. dane nikalne ke purv atyat sankramak hota he. udbhavan kaal 17-18 dinon kaa hota he. jatiltaaon kii sambhaavna kam hoti he. ek akraman jeevanaparyant rog pratishodhak shakti utpann kar detaa he. saadhaaranta bade bachche tathaa kishor hi is rog ke shikaar hote hai.
 pratham 24 ghanton main hi dane nikal aate hai tathaa sampurna sharir par fail jate hai. chehre tathaa gardan se prarambh hokar, ye dane vaksh, peeth, haath or pair par failte hai tathaa raktavarna ke hote hai. ye 72 ghanton main samaapt ho jate hai. kaan ke peeche, sir ke pichley hisse tathaa gale main lasika granthiyaan badi ho jaati he tathaa sparsh se dard hota he. yadi garbhvatee nari ko yah rog garbhadhaaran ke pratham kuch saptahon ke bheetar hota he, to 50 pratishat sambhaavna he ki bachche ke hriday, aankh, kaan yaa mastishk kii banaavat main kuch dosha aa jayega. rog kii rokthaam yaa chikitsa ke liye uchit oshadhi nahin he.
