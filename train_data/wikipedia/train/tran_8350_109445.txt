 cheenk vah kriya he jisamen fefdon se hawa naak or muh ke raste atyadhik teji se baahar nikaali jaati he. yah ek ardh-swytt kriya he. 
 cheenk aamtaur par tab aati he jab hamaari naak ke andar kii jhilli, kisi bahari padaarth ke ghus jane se khujalati he. naak se turant hamaare mastishk ko sandesh pahunchataa he or vah sharir kii mansapeshiyon ko aadesh detaa he ki is padaarth ko baahar nikaalen. cheenk jaisi maamuli si kriya main kitni mansapeshiyaan kaam karti hai....pet, chaati, diaphram, vaktantu, gale ke peeche or yahaan tak ki ankhon kii bhi. ye sab milakar kaam karti hai or padaarth baahar nikaal diya jaataa he. kabhi-kabhi ek cheenk se kaam nahin chalata to kai chhinke aati hai. jab hamen zukaam hota he tab chhinken isliye aati hai kyonki zukaam kii vajah se hamaari naak ke bheetar kii jhilli main sujan aa jaati he or usase kujalaahat hoti he.
