 nirbhaya fund dushkarm ke peediton/uttarjiviyon ke raahat or punarvaas kii yojana ke nirmaan kii punariksha kii gai he, kyonki peedit ke muaavje vaala bhaag dand prakriya samhita kii dhara 375 ke praavdhaan dwaara adhigrahit kar liya gaya he, jisamen kaha gaya he ki prayek rajya sarkaar, kendra sarkaar ke samanvay se dushkarm sahit aparaadh kii peedita ko muaavje ke uddeshy se nidhiyaan uplabdh karaane kii yojana taiyaar karegi. ab tak 20 rajyon or saat sangh shasit pradeshon ne peedit muaavja yojana taiyaar kii he. yah jaankaree mahila or bal vikaas mantralay main rajya mantri shrimati krishna tirath ne loksabhaa main aaj ek prashn ke likhit uttar main di. 
 unhone bataaya ki mahila or bal vikaas mantralay dushkarm kii peediton tathaa kathin paristhitiyon main jivan yaapan kar rahi mahilaon ke punarvaas ke liye swadhar or alpavas grih yojana bhi chalaa rahaa he.
