 rambagh mahal rajasthan kii rajdhani jaipur main sthit ek mahal he lekin vartmaan main yah ek hotel he. yah pehle jaipur ke maharaja kaa ghar tha.
 is mahal main pehla kaksh san 1835 main "garden house" banaaya tha jo ki rajkumaar ram singh 2 ke liye banaaya tha. kai samay pashchaat yahan ke maharaja savai madhosingh ne 1887 main ek bada "shahi shikaar kaksh" naamak kaksh banaaya tha. 20veen shatabdi main rambagh mahal kaa "samul sweenton jaimub" ne kuch dizaaine banaakar vistaar kiya tha.uske pashchaat maharaja savai maansingh 2 ne isko apana rajkeey mahal bana diya tha.bharat kii swatantraaa prapti se purv ise angrejon ne rajkeey bhawan bana diya tha. 1957 main yah ghoshnaa kii gai ki ise ek lagzri hotel bana di jaye, kuch samay pashchaat ise hotel ke naam se janaa jane laga.
