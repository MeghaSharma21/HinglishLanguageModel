 mrityuj kaathinya, mrityu kaa teesraa charan he jo mrityu ke pahchaanane jane yogya lakshanon main se ek he. yah mrityu ke uparant peshiyon main aane vaale rasinic parivartanon ke kaaran hota he jiske kaaran shav ke haath-pair akadane lagate he. ho jate hai.) mrityuj kaathinya mrityu ke 4 ghante baad hi ho saktaa he.
 kathorta sabase pehle sir main dikhaai detii he phir thode thode samay main puure sharir main dikhaae dene lag jaataa he. puure sharir main kathorta aane ke liye 12 ghante letaa he, sharir main 12 ghante rukta he or 12 ghante main sharir se nikal jaataa he. agar sharir main akadan sir se pehale kisi or ang main dikhaai de to us paristhiti ko 'shav kaa anthan' kehete he.
 kathorta ke kshan kii shuruyaat vyakti ke umr, ling, shaareerik sthiti, or mansapeshiyon kaa nirmaan se prabhavit he. kathorta ke kshan shishu or bahut chote baccho main nahin dikhaai detaa he. kathorta ke kshan kai kaarnon se prabhavit hota he. iskaa ek kaaran mukhya karan parivesh kaa taapmaana, jab taapmaana zyaada hota he to kathorta ke kshan kii shuruyaat sheeghra hoti he. or jab taapmaana kam yaa thanda hota he to kathorta ke kshan kii shuruyaat der se hoti he.
