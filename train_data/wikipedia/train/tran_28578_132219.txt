 gopi gadhaa or gapshap bhartiya hindi hasya dharawahik he, jiskaa prasaaran sab tivi par 16 june 2012 se 4 agast 2012 tak har shanivaar raat 10 baje hota tha. iskaa nirmaan bhi sab tivi ne hi kiya he. jisamen anup upaadhyaay or gopi bhalla mukhya kirdaar main hai. is dharawahik main exiaar or chidiya ghar naamak do alag alag dharawahik se gopi exiaar se or gada prasad chidiya ghar se usee kirdaar or rup main aate hai, jis rup main vah us kirdaar ko nibhaa rahe hai. is dharawahik main alag alag hasya drishy dikhaaye jate hai.
