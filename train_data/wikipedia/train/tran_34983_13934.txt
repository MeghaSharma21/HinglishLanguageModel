 nimmo yaa nimmu bharat ke jammu or kashmir rajya ke laddakh kshetra ke leh tehsil main sthit ek basti he. yahan zanskaar nadi kaa sindhu nadi main vilay hota he. nimmu main sardi or garmi ke mausam main baari taapmaana parivartan hota he: greeshm ritu main +40 °centegrade or sheet ritu main −29 °centegrade. is kathor vaataavaran or sthaneey shushkta ke kaaran yahan vanaspati na ke baraabar hote hai.
