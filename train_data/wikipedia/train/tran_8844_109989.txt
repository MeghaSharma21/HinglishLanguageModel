 lomash ramakatha ke vaktaaon main se ek maharishi the. sharir par roen adhik hone se inhen yah naam mila tha. katha he ki sau varshon tak kamalpushpon se inhone shiv ji kii puja kii thi, isi se inhen yah vardaan mila tha ki kalpant hone par inke sharir kaa keval ek bal jhadaa karegaa. ye sadaa tirthaton kiya karte the or bade dharmatma the. tirthaton ke samay yudhisthir ne inase anek aakhyaan sune the. inhone durdam raja ko devi bhagwat kii katha paanch baar sunai thi jisase ravat naamak putr kii prapti hui thi. inhone narmada snaan kaa nirdesh kar pishaachayoni main pravisht gandharvakanyaon aadi kaa uddhaar kiya tha. inke likhe ye do granth bataae jate hai - lomashasanhita tathaa lomashashiksha. inke naam par ek 'lomash ramayana' bhi praapt he.
