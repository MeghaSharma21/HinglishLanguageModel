 bharat ke rajya madhyapradesh ke antargat siwani jila se 29 kilometre dur chandauri kala sthit he is gaanv kaa bhogolia kshetrafal 898.83 hectare hain.chandauri kala ek adarsh gram panchaayat hain. yah panchaayat chandaurikala or chandauri khurd se milakar bani he yah shaskeey praathmik shaala va shaskeey up chikitsaalay va panchayat karyaalay he. machagora band kii vaayi tat nahar is gaanv main nirmit bade nahar pul ke upar se bahati hui bakhaari gayi hain.is panchaayat ke aasapaas kaa kshetra bandol police thana ke antargat aata he junglee jaanavaro main hiran atyaadhik hone ke kaaran yahan aasaani se dekhe ja sakate hain van vibhaag bandol ke kshetradhikaar main yah sampurna kshetra aata hain. 
 chandori khurd gram panchayat chandori kala se judaa hua he or isi ke antargat aata he is gaanv kaa bhogolia kshetrafal 558.6 hectare he 2011 tak is gaanv kii jansankhya 479 hain. yah gaanv chandauri kala se 2 kilometre kii duuri par he yahan ki saptaahik bazaar kaa din shanivaar hain yahan aasapaas ke gaanv jisame sagar,chandaurikala, nakatiyaa,chhingavaar aadi jagah se log bazaar karne aate hain.
