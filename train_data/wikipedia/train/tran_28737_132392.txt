 maryaada patrika kaa pehla ank navambar san 1910 i0 main krishnakanth malviya ne 'abhyuday' karyaalay prayag se ise prakashit hua tha. iske pratham ank kaa pratham lekh 'maryaada' sheershak se purushottams tandon ne likhaa. 10 varshon tak is patrika ko prayag se nikaalane ke baad krishnakanth malviya ne iskaa prakaashan gyanmandal kaashi ko saunp diya. san 1921 i0 se shri shivaprasad gupt kas sanchaalan main or sampoornanand ji ke sampadakatv main "maryaada" gyanmandal se prakashit hui. asahyog aandolan main unke jail chale jane par dhanpat raay premachand sthanapann sampadak hue. patrika kaa vaarshik mulya 5 rupye tathaa ek prati kaa 2 aana tha. iskaa aakaar 10 * 7 tha. maryaada apane samay kii sarvshereshth masik patrika thi. premachand kii aarmabhik kahaniyaan isamen prakashit huin. san 1923 i0 main yah patrika anivaary kaarnon se band ho gai. iskaa antim ank pravasi visheshank ke rup main banarsidas chaturvedi ke sampaadan main niklaa, jo apni vishisht lekh saamagree ke kaaran aitihaasik mahatva rakhata he.
