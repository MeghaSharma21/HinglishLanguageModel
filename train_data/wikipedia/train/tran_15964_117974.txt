 chaar saral rekhaon se ghiri band akruti ko chaturbhuj kehete hai. eucalidian samtal jyamiti main, chaturbhuj ek bahubhuj he jisamen chaar kinare or chaar sheersh hote hai.
 "chaturbhuj " shabd kii utpatti do latin shabdon 'quadri', jiskaa arth he 'chaar kaa ek sanskaran', or 'latus', jiskaa arth he "bhujaa", se hui he.
 chaturbhuj saral yaa jatil hote hai. saral chaturbhuj uttal yaa avatal hote hai.
 ek saadhaaran chaturbhuj ABCD ke aantarik konon kaa yoga 360° hota he, arthath-
   ∠ A + ∠ B + ∠ C + ∠ D = 360 ∘  .  {\displaystyle \angle A+\angle B+\angle C+\angle D=360^{\circ }.} 
 yah aantarik kone yoga sutara × 180° dwaara praapt hota he. 
 koi bhi chaturbhuj jo swa-pratichchhedi nahin he, ek saadhaaran chaturbhuj hota he.
 uttal chaturbhuj
 ek uttal chaturbhuj main, sabhi aantarik kone 180° se kam hote hai or donon vikarn chaturbhuj ke andar sthit hote hai.
 aniyamit chaturbhuj : koi bhi bhujaayen samanaantar nahin hoti he.
 samalamb chaturbhuj : sammukh bhujaaon kaa kam se kam ek yugma samanaantar hota he. samaantar chaturbhuj ek samalamb chaturbhuj hota he.
 samdvibahu samalamb chaturbhuj : sammukh bhujaaon kaa kam se kam ek yugma samanaantar hota he or aadhaar kone maap main baraabar hote hai. vaikalpik paribhaasha ke anusaar, yah samaan lambaai ke vikarnon vaala samalamb chaturbhuj hota he.
 samaantar chaturbhuj : samanaantar bhujaaon ke do yugmon vaala ek chaturbhuj. aisa chaturbhuj, jisamen sammukh bhujaayen baraabar; sammukh kone baraabar; yaa vikarn ek duusare ko samdvibhajit karte hai. samachatubhuj samaantar chaturbhuj hote hai.
 samachatubhuj : sabhi chaaron bhujaayen samaan hoti hai or donon vikarn ek duusare ko lamb-samdvibhajit karte hai. varg, ek samachatubhuj hota he.
 aayat : sammukh bhujaayen samaan hoti hai, chaaron kone samakon hote hai, vikarn ek duusare ko samdvibhajit karte hai or lambaai main baraabar hote hai. varg, ek aayat hota he.
 varg : sabhi chaaron bhujaayen samaan hoti hai, or chaaron kone samakon hote hai. sammukh bhujaayen samanaantar hoti hai, vikarn ek duusare ko samdvibhajit karte hai or samaan lambaai ke hote hai. ek chaturbhuj ek varg hoga yadi vah ek samachatubhuj bhi ho or ek aayat bhi.
 patangakaar chaturbhuj : asann bhujaaon ke do yugma baraabar lambaai ke hote hai. taatpary yah he ki iskaa ek vikarn, chaturbhuj ko do sarvangasam tribhujon main vibhajit karata he, or isliye samaan bhujaaon ke do yugmo ke bich ke kone baraabar hote hai. iske donon vikarn ek duusare ke lambavat hote hai.
 sparshi chaturbhuj : chaaron bhujaayen ek antahvrutt kii sparsh rekhaaen hoti hai. ek uttal chaturbhuj, ek chakree chaturbhuj hoga yadi isaki sammukh bhujaaon kaa yoga samaan ho.
 chakree chaturbhuj : chaturbhuj ke chaaron sheersh ek parivritt par sthit hote hai. ek uttal chaturbhuj, chakree hoga yadi uske sammukh konon kaa yoga 180° ho.
 avatal chaturbhuj
 ek avatal chaturbhuj main, ek aantarik kone 180° se bada hota he or donon vikarnon main se ek vikarn, chaturbhuj ke baahar sthit hota he.
 aisa chaturbhuj, jo swayan ko pratichchhed kare, jatil chaturbhuj kehalaata he.
 vikarn : chaturbhuj ke do vikarn hote hai, jo vipareet shirshon ko jodate hai.
 dvimadhyikaaye : ek uttal chaturbhuj main do dvimadhyikaaye hoti hai jo vipareet bhujaaon ke madhya binduon ko jodatii hai. ye donon, chaturbhuj ke cendrak par pratichchhed karti hai.
 ek uttal chaturbhuj ABCD jisamen bhujaayen a = AB, b = BC, c = CD and d = DA hai, kaa kshetrafal gyaat karne ke liye vibhinn saamaanya sutara hai.

 sadish sutara
 kisi chaturbhuj ABCD kaa kshetrafal, sadishon kaa prayog karke praapt kiya ja saktaa he. mana sadish AC or BD kramshah A se C or B se D tak ke vikarn hai. tab chaturbhuj kaa kshetrafal nimna hoga:
   K =  1 2   |  A C × B D  |,  {\displaystyle K={\tfrac {1}{2}}|\mathbf {AC} \times \mathbf {BD} |,}

   K =  1 2   |  x 1  y 2  − x 2  y 1  | .  {\displaystyle K={\tfrac {1}{2}}|x_{1}y_{2}-x_{2}y_{1}|.}
