 isisi mahila cricket vishwa cap kwalifire ek antarrashtriya cricket toornament ki mahila cricket vishwa cap ke liye yogyata prakriya ke antim kadam ke rup main kaary karata he.
 mahila vishwa cap pehali baar 1973 main yojit kiya gaya tha, or pehle saat sanskarnon bhaag lene ke liye puri tarah se nimantran dwaara nirdhaarit, antarrashtriya mahila cricket parishad ke vivek par jari kiya gaya tha. ek yogyata toornament pehali baar he, jo netherland dwaara yojit kiya gaya tha or ireland se jeeta  2003 main yojit kiya gaya tha. do baad toornament yojit kiya gaya he - 2008 main dakshin africa dwaara yojit or pakistan jeeta, or 2011 main bangladesh kii mejabani main westeindies jeeta. 2017 toornament farvari 2017 main srilanka main yojit kiya jayega.
 udghaatan samaaroh idbloosisi dwaara yojit or idbloosisi trophy ke rup main branded kiya gaya tha. haalanki, idbloosisi 2005 main antarrashtriya cricket parishad dwaara sammilit kiya gaya tha, or sabhi anya sanskarnon vishwa cap kwalifire ke rup main bas janaa jaataa rahaa he. tiimon or kwalifing sthanon kii sankhya prayek toornament main vividh he - 2003 main, chah tiimon ke do kwalifing spot ke liye pratispardhaa he, jabaki agle sanskaran main aath tiimon ko do kwalifing spot chunaav ladaa. 2011 ghatana ko dekha das tiimon ke teen kwalifing sthanon ke liye pratispardhaa he, or agle toornament, 2017 main, das tiimon or chaar kwalifing sthanon kii suvidha hogi.
