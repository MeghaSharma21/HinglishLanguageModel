 Mula sangh ek pracheen jain mathvaasi aadesh. Mula kaa shabdic arth he "root" yaa mul aadesh.
 Mula-sangha kiya gaya he mukhya digambar jain ke aadesh. aaj digambar jain parampara kaa paryaay ban gaya he Mula sangha. mahaan acharya kundakund swami ke saath judaa hua he Mula sangha. sabase purana gyaat ullekh ke Mula sangha se he vigyaapan 430.
 Mula sangh main vibhajit kiya gaya tha kuch shakhaon. ke anusaar Shrutavatara ke acharya Indranandi or Nitisar ke bhattarak Indranandi, acharya Arhadbali kaa aayojan kiya tha kii ek parishad jain bhikshuon, or diya tha naam ke liye alag-alag samoohon main he. chaar pramukh samoohon the:
 ke bhattarak ke shravanbelagola or mudbidri karne ke liye sambandhit Deshiya gana, Bhattaraka ke Humbaj ke antargat aata he Balatkara gana.
 kai Digambara aadesh main uttar bharat ke the kashtha sangh. vahaan the kuch maamuli matbhed main prathaon ke bich Mula sangha or Kashtha sangha.
