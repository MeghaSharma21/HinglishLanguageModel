 preetam pyaare or wo sab tivi par prasaarit hone vaala ek hasya dharawahik he. yah dharawahik 3 march 2014 se shuru hua or 1 agast 2014 ko samaapt hua. yah us samay somvaar se shuukravaar raat 7:30 ko detaa tha.
 yah kahani preetam or pyaare ke bhuto ko pakadaney par aadhaarit he. jise hasya rup dene ke liye isamen kisi bhi bhoot ko yah donon nahin pakad pate hai or in donon kii galatiyon ke kaaran galati se bhoot pakad main aa jate he. isamen daara koyala mukhya khalanaayak pisaach he. jab dharawahik ke shuruyaat main yah preetam or pyaare ke parivaar ko apane mahal main fasaa letaa he par preetam or pyaare kii galati ke kaaran un atmaaon ko vahaan se bhaagna padataa he. iske pashchaat daara koyala un logo ko maarane kaa prayaas karte rahata he. preetam or pyaare ke daara koyala ko haraane ke pashchaat tiku kilwala ke kehane par ve donon bhootnashaka ban jate hai. iske pashchaat prati din ve nae atmaaon se ladate hai or galati ke kaaran pakad lete hai. yah ant tak isi prakaar ke pratidin ke kahani ke anusaar chalata rahata he.
