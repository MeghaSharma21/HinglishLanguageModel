 krishnaswami kasturirangan rajyasabha ke saansad evam prasiddh bhartiya antariksh vaigyanik hai. inhen bharat sarkaar ne 1992 main vigyaan ke kshetra main unke yogadan ke liye padam bhushan se sammanit kiya tha. ye karnataka se hai evam vartmaan main bhartiya yojana ayog ke sadsy ke rup main apni sevaayen de rahe hai.
 dow kasturirangan ne isro evam antariksh ayog ke adhyaksh or antariksh vibhaag main bharat sarkaar ke sachiv ke rup main 9 saal tak bhartiya antariksh kaarykram kaa nirdesan kiya. isse pehle jab ve isro ke upgrah centre ke nideshak the, tab unki dekharekh main bhartiya rashtriya upgrah, bhartiya doorasanvedi upgrah tathaa anya kai vaigyanik upgrah viksit kiye gaye. vah bharat ke pehle prayogtmak prithvi avlokan upgrahon, ke liye pariyojna nideshak the.
 unke netritv main bharat ke pratishthit prakshepan vaahan - dhruviya upgrah prakshepan yaan or bhusthir upgrah prakshepan yaan kaa safal prakshepan evam sanchaalan hua. unki aguai main bharat ne chandrayaan-1 kaa safal prakshepan kiya, jise mile ke pathar ke rup main dekha jaataa he. unke in prayaason ne pramukh taur par antariksh kaarykram chalaane vaale gine-chune deshon kii suchi main bharat ko bhi daal diya he.
