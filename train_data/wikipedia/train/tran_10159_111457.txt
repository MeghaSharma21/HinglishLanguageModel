 govinddas chakravartti bangla ke prasiddh kavi the.
 ye boraculi gram nivasee bhakt or padakarta the. ye shrinivas acharya ke shishy the. govinddas kaviraj inke samasamyik evam gurubhai the. govinddas chakravartti kii nishchit janmatithi agyaat he. inkaa rachanakaal govinddas kaviraj ke hi aasapaas he. bhakthi ratnakar granth main inke bare main kaha gaya he ki ye shrinivas acharya ke atipriy shishy the evam gital-vaadya-vidya main nipun bhaktimurti the. vaishnavadas evam uddhavdas ne apane ek ek pad main inkaa ullekh kiya he. inke kuch hi pad praapt hai.
