 vishwa balshram diwas 12 june ko manaaya jaataa he.
 bharat main balshram kii samasya dashakon se prachlit he. bharat sarkaar ne balshram kii samasya ko samaapt kadam uthaae hai. bhartiya samvidhan ke anuchhed 23 khatarnaak udyogon main bacchon ke rojgaar par pratibandh lagaataa he.
 bharat kii centre sarkaar ne 1986 main balshram nishedh or niyaman adhinium paarit kar diya. is adhinium ke anusaar balshram takneekee salaahkaar samiti niyukt ki gai. is samiti kii sifarish ke anusaar, khatarnaak udyogon main bacchon kii niyukti nishiddh he.
 1987 main, rashtriya balshram niti banaai gai thi.
