 amin sayaani bharat ke ek mashhoor radio sanchaalak yaa anounser hai. yah sirf bharat main nahin balki asia main khyati pai. inkaa mashhoor kaarykram "binaaka gitamala" radio silon se prasaarit hota tha. inaki nakal kar aaj bhi kai radio sanchaalak khud ko sanvaarane main kaamyab huve hai. inke baat karne kii style "bhaaiyo or bahano" kaafii saraahi gai. inhon ne 54,000 radio kaarykram kiye or 19,000 spots yaa jingles kiye. inkaa radio safar 1951 main shuru huwa tha.
