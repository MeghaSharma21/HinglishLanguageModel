 
 khakasia ganatantr ganatantr kaa darja rakhane vaala rus kaa ek rajya he. yah dakshin-madhya siberia kshetra main sthit he. isaki rajdhani abakan naamak shahar he. is ganatantr kaa naam khkaas naamak turki jati par padaa he or yahan roosi bhashaa ke saath-saath khkaas bhashaa ko bhi sarkari manyata praapt he.
 khaakaas swashasit pradesh jisaki sthapana 1930 main kii gai thi. yah madhya siberia main crasnayarsk pradesh ke aage uttarpurv sthit 23,973 vargamil kaa bhubhag he. camerova or oriyo ke swashasit pradesh iske dakshin ore camerova pradesh pashchim main hai. isamen yenise nadi kii sahayika abakan nadi bahati he or aage chal kar bhinusinsk kathe se guzarati hui isaki purvi seemaa nirdhaarit karti he. is pradesh kaa 60 pratishat bhubhag tega vanya pradesh he. lakdi nadi dwaara bahaakar abakan pahunchaaya jaataa he vahaan anek aara milen hai. kaanthe ke bheetar bhend or dugdh pashupaalan hota he. pehle bhinusinsk ke kaanthe main ghode aadi pashu swatantra vicharan kiya karte the. kintu ab vahaan gehun, jau or jayee kii kheti hoti he. yahan ke 52 pratishat turk-mongol sankar jati ke khaakasi log hai jo kisi samay ghumantu the.
 yah ilaaka prachinkaal main kirghiz logon kii mathrubhumi thi lekin 13veen sadi main mongol samrajya dwaara haraae jane ke baad adhiktar kirghiz log madhya asia main aadhunik kirghizstan ke kshetra main ja based. jo kirghiz nahin gaye vahin aadhunik khakasi logon ke purvaj the. 17veen sadi main roosi samrajya is kshetra main fail gaya or soviet sangh banane ke baad yah ilaaka usamen shaamil ho gaya. yahan bahut se roosi log aa based or ab keval 12% aabaadi khkaas nasl kii he.
 khakasia main koyale or anya khanijon kii khaane hai or saath-saath yahan ke wanon se lakdi kaa utpaadan bhi bahut hota he. yahi is ganatantr ke mukhya udyog hai.
