 ucchavach dharaatal kii oonchaai-nichaai se banane vaale praturup yaa pattern ko kehete hai. kshetreey str par ucchavach bhu-akritik pradeshon ke rup main vyakt hota he or chote str par yah ek sthalaroop yaa sthalroopon ke ek sanyukt samooh kaa pratinidhitiv karata he. 
 prithvi par teen prakaar ke ucchavach paye jate hai- 
 1- pratham shrenee ucchavach:- iske antargat mahadweep evam mahasagariya basin ko shaamil kiya jaataa he.
 2- dwitiya shrenee ke ucchavach:- parvat, pathaar, maidan tathaa jhil aadi dwitiya shrenee ke ucchavach hai.
 3- tritiy shrenee ucchavach:- sarita, sagariya jal, bhumigat jal, pawan, himand aadi ke kaaran utpann sthalakritiyon ko tritiy shrenee ucchavach kehete hai.
 bhu- akruti vigyaan ke vishyakshetra ke antargat uparyukt teen prakaar ke sthalroopon ko shaamil kiya jaataa he.
 kisi kshetra main apradan kii prakriya dwaara prabhavit ho sakane vaali uplabdh oonchaai ko sthaneey ucchavach kehete hai.
