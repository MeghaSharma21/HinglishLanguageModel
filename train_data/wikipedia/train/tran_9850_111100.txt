 frank burundi kii prachlit mudra he, jise sau centtimes se vibhajit kiya ja saktaa he. haalanki, burundi ke gathan ke baad se aaj tak sikke jari nahin kiye gaye hai. frank kaa burundi kii mudra ke rup main prachlan 1916 main sharu hua, jab belgium ne german ke aadhipaty ko samaapt kar apana aadhipaty kaayam karte hue german east african rupi ke sthaan par belgium congou frank jari kiya. yah mudra 1960 main rwanda or burundi frank jari karne tak prachlit rahi. 1964 main burundi ne swayan ke frank jari kiye. varsh 2009 ke ant tak frank ke sthaan par nayaa east african shilong burundi ke saath anya chaar purv africa deshon kii ek kaaman karency ke rup main jari karne kii yojana he.
