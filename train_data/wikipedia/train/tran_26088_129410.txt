 cadavarin pashu utakon kii apghaton dwaara utpaadit durgandhyukt diamin yogic he. cadavarin ek zaharila padaarth he, jiskaa sutara NH25NH2 he. cadavarin ko 1,5-pantandimin or pentamithaylindimin bhi kaha jaataa he. 
 cadavarin 1884 main berlin ke vaigyanik ludwig briger ne varnit kiya tha.
 cadavarin amino acid lysine kii dicarbokshilation kaa utpaada he. lysine kon sodium bicarbonate kii ek choti raashi main milakar garm karne par aasaani se ghar par bhi banaaya ja saktaa he. utpaadit gas ek glass container jo barf ke pani se ghira hua he main janaa chahie. garm karne ki prakriya ek glass container main honi chahie kyonki dhaatu prakriya ko dooshit kar sakati he. 
 aisa jaruri nahin ki cadavarin mrit sharir ke apghaton se hi utpaadit ho, yah choti maatr main jivit vyakti dwaara bhi utpaadit kiya jaataa he. yah mutr kii vishisht badboo ke liye aanshik rup se jimmedaar he.
 cadavarin kaa ooncha str lysine chayapachay main dosha ke saath kuch rogiyon ke mutr main paaya gaya he. aamtaur par bacterial vaginosis ke saath jude gandh kon cadavarin se joda gaya he. 
 cadavarin badi maatra main vishakt he.
