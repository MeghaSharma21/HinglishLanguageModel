 aadhunik palamira: Modern Palmyra,  madhya syria main ek shahar he, prashasnic rup se homes praant kaa hissaa he. yah syria ke registan ke madhya main damishq ke purvottar kshetra main 215 kilometre or phuraat nadi ke 180 kilometre dakshin-pashchim main sthit ek nachlistaan main sthit he. unesco kii vishwa dharohar sthal pracheen palamira ke khandhar aadhunik shahar se 500 meter dakshin pashchim sthit hai. apekshaakrit pruthak, sabase nikatatam ilaakon main purv main araq, uttar-purv main al-sukhnah, pashchim main tiyas or dakshin-pashchim main al-kauryathan shaamil hai.palamira tadmur jile kaa prashasnic centre or tadmur upjila he syria kendriya sankhyikee sankhyikee ke anusaar, shahar kii jansankhya 51323 thi or 2004 kii janaganana main 55,062 kii aabaadi vaali up-jansankhya thi. tadmur nivaasiyon kaa 1838 main sunni musalmaan hone kaa record kiya gaya tha. syrian grihayudhd yuddh ke dauraan, desh kii anya hisson se aantarik visthapit sharnaarthiyon kii aabaadi ke kaaran shahar kii aabaadi main kaaphi vruddhi hui he.
