 madhyavyavta yaa metamarizam kuch jeevon kii shaareerik yojana main shaareerik khandon kii ek lambaai se susajjit shrunkhlaa ko kehete hai. yah khand ek-duusare se milte hai lekin bilkul ek-jaise nahin hote kyonki unamem se kuch ke vishesh kaary hote hai. praaniyon main in madhyavay khandon ko somite, madhyavayavi yaa metamar kaha jaataa he. vanaspatiyon main inhen metamer yaa phytomar kehete hai.
