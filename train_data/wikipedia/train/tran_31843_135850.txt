 nirdeshaank: 23°49′N 86°28′E / 23.81°N 86.46°E / 23.81; 86.46sarayadhela jharkhand ke dhanbad jile ke antargat aanevaala ek shahari kasbaa he, jo atyant teji se vikaas kar rahaa he. jile ke nami shikshan santhaan, kai sarkari companio kii rihaayshi ilaaka or karyaalay, chote-mote dukaano ke saath big bazaar, megamart jaisi bady retail kshetra, kai mandir saath bank aadi is kshetra me khule he.
 2001 ke anusaar India census, sarayadhela kii kul jansankhya 24,183, jisame purusho kaa pratishat 53% tathaa mahilao kaa pratishat 47% tha. varsh 1991 se 2001 me jansankhya vrudhdhi dar 3.97 pratishat salaana thi. sarayadhela kaa ghnatv 14931 log prati kimi he, jabaki iskaa kshetrafal 1.62 varg kimi he. purusho kii sakshartaa 84% he or mahila sakshartaa kaa dar 72% he. sarayadhela main 6 varsh se kam umr waalo kii jansankhya ke 10% thi.
 dhanbad railway stesan se 5 kimi kii duri par sthit he. neshnal haive sankhya 32 iske bich-bich se guzarati he. is sadak ko dhanbad kaa mukhya maarg bhi kaha jaataa he.
