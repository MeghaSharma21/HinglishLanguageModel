 
 gizar​ paak-adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-balatistan kshetra kaa pashchimtam zila he. isaki rajdhani gaahkooch shahar he. yahan kai jatiyaan rahati hai or teen mukhya bhashaayen boli jaati hai - khovaar, shina or burushsaki. inke alaava is zile ke ishkoman kshetra main kuch vaakhi or tajik bolne vaale bhi yahan rahete hai. kuch gujjar log bhi is jile main based hue hai.
 chitral zile kii khovaar bhashaa main 'geraz​' kaa matlab 'sharnaarthi' hota he. chitral kaa mehtar jab bhi kuch logon par atyaachaar karata tha, ve gupis kii taraf bhagkar sharan lete the. chitral or gupis ke bich based hue logon ko samay ke saath 'gerzi' kaha jane laga. 1970-80 kaal main jab yahan zila banaaya gaya to uskaa naam isi ke vikrut rup 'gizar​' par rakh diya gaya. 'gizar​' main bindu-vaale 'ga' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bindu-rahit 'ga' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'galati' or 'garib' shabdon ke 'ga' se miltaa he.
 gizar​ zile kii sabhi mukhya vaadiyon main ismaili shiya logon kii bahusankhya he or sunni log alpsankhyak hai. yahan ismaili shiya aabaadi kaa 87% hai or sunni 13% hai.
 gizar​ zile main chaar tahseelein aati hai -
 gizar​ zila bhurajanatik drushti se bahut hi mahatvapurn jagah sthit he. iske uttar main afghanistan kaa vaakhaan galiyara he jisaki choti si chaudaai ke paar tajikistan aata he. agar paak-adhikrut kashmir bharat ke niyantran main hota to yahi vah ilaaka he jahaan bharat kii seemayen sidhi afghanistan ko chhootin. purvottar main gizar zila china dwaara niyantrit shinjiyang kshetra ko choota he. pashchim main isaki seemayen pakistan ke kaibar​-pakhtuunakva praant ke chitral zile se lagati hai.
