 koj prameya yaa koj kaa siddhant nobel puraskaar vijeta arthshaastri ronaald koj dwaara pratipaadit, bahyata va paryaavaran kii samasya ke samaadhaan se sambandhit, siddhant he jisaki manyatanusaar bazaar yantra ke dwaara bahyata ko sudhara ja saktaa he. yah siddhant arthvyavastha ke kanoonee pakshon se sambandhit he. saamanyatya yah pratipaadit kiya jaataa he ki bahyata kii samasya bazaar yantra ke kriyalan ko asafal banaati he, kyonki bahyata utpaadan ke dauraan srujit vah laagat he jise vastu kii maudrik laagat kii gananaa main nahin rakhaa ja saktaa. aisa tabhi ho saktaa he jabaki, sampatti adhikaar paribhaashit ho tathaa len-dein vyavhaar sambandhit koi laagat nahin ho, bahyata srujit karne vaale log tathaa usase peedit log aantarik rup se paraspar vyaktigat samjhauta kar len. is prakaar sansadhano kaa kushal aavanton hoga, donon pakshon ke bich sampatti adhikaar kaa bantwaaraa keval aay ke bantwaare ko prabhavit karegaa, samgr aay vitaran ko prabhavit nahin karegaa. aisi sthiti main kushalta ko upar uthaane ke liye na to sarkari niyantran kii aavashyaktaa hogi or na hi kararopan kii. dhyatavya he ki koj prameya tabhi lagu hoga jabaki aarthik vyavhaar kii laagat shunya ho. yah bheed ke sambandh main lagu nahin hoga jahaan pradooshan karne vaale tathaa usase prabhavit hone vaale bahut adhik sankhya main hon tathaa unke pahchaan karne kii laagat bahut adhik ho.
