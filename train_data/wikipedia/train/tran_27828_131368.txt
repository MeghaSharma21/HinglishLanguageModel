 shreeringpattam kii gheraabandi british east india company or mysore samrajya ke bich chauthaa anglo-mysore yuddh kaa antim takaraav tha. angrejon ke hyderabad ke sahyogi nizaam ke saath angrejon ne seringpattam main kile kii deevaaron kaa ulanghan karne or garh par hamalaa karne ke baad nirnaayak jeet hasil kii. mysore ke shaasha tipu sultan ladaai main maare gaye the.. jeet ke baad angrejon ne wodeyar vansh ko singhaasana main behaal kar diya, lekin rajya ke apratyaksh niyantran ko barkaraar rakhaa.
 yuddh main april or may 1799 ke maheenon main shreeringpattam ke aasapaas muthbhedon kii ek shrinkhalaa shaamil thi, british east india company or unke sahyogiyon kii sanyukt senaaon ke bich, 50,000 se adhik sainikon kii sankhya or mysore samrajya ke, tipu sultan dwaara shasit, 30,000 sainik sankhya thi. chauthaa anglo-mysore yuddh yuddh main tipu sultan kii haar or maut ke saath khatm ho gaya tha.
 jab chauthaa anglo-mysore yuddh tut gaya, to angrejon ne general jorge haris ke tahat do bade stambh ekatra kiye. pehle 26,000 se adhik british east india company ke sainik shaamil the, jinamein se 4,000 europea the jabaki shesh sthaneey bhartiya sipaahi the. doosraa stambh hyderabad ke nizaam dwaara pradaan kiya gaya tha, or isamen das batalian or 16,000 se adhik ghudsavaar shaamil the. saath main, sahyogi bal 50,000 se adhik sainikon kii sankhya main gina gaya. tipu kii sena teesraa anglo-mysore yuddh se samaapt ho gai thi or iske parinamswaroop aadhe rajya kaa nuksaan hua tha.
