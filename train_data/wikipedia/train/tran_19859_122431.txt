 buddha dharm main 'paripurnataa' yaa kuch gunon kaa charmonnayan kii sthiti ko parmita yaa parmi kaha gaya he. buddha dharm main in gunon kaa vikaas pavitrataa kii prapti, karm ko pavitra karne aadi ke liye kii jaati he taaki sadhak anavaruddh jivan jite hue bhi gyaan kii prapti kar sake. 'parmita' shabd 'param' se vyutpann he. 
  chah parmita : mahayan granthon main chah parmita kii gananaa milti he-
 dashabhumikasutra main nimnlikhit chaar parmita bhi ginayi gayin hai-
  das parmita : therwaad granthon main das parmita varnit hai-
