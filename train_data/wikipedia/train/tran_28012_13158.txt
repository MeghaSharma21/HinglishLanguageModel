 tharra uttar bharat or pakistan main ganne yaa genhu se banane vaali ek desi sharaab he jo aksar gair-kanoonee dhang se bina kisi panjikaran yaa anya prakaar kii sarkari anumati ke banaai jaati he. anaupchaarik rup se tharre ko 'kachi sharaab' or 'kutta maar' jaise naamon se bhi bulaayaa jaataa he or aam taur par isaki gandh kashay or swaad kadava yaa munh jalaane vaala hota he. takneekee rup se ganne ke ras se bani sharaab ko ram kaha jaataa he isliye zyaadaatar tharra bhi ek prakaar kii ram hi hota he.
 kyonki yah sharaab bina sarkari niyantran ke chhupakar banaai jaati he isliye iskaa seven karne waalon ko isaki swatchta or shuddhta kaa koi gyaan nahin hota. yadi isamen sharaab se milte-julate methanol jaise zahreele drav maujuud hon to yah pine vaale ko andha kar sakati he yaa jaanaleva bhi ho sakati he. kam khataranaak ashuddhiyon se samay ke saath-saath yah pine waalon kaa yakrut bhi karaab kar sakati he.
