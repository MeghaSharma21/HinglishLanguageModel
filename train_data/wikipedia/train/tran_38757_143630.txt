 
 gandhi 1982 main bani lokapriy bhartiya hasti mohandas karamchand gandhi ke vaastavik jeevani par aadhaarit film he. film kaa nirdesan richard atonbaro dwaara kiya gaya he or isamen ben kingsle gandhi kii bhoomika main he. is film ke liye donon ko academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha. film ko sarvshereshth film ke academy puraskaar ke saath aath anya academy puraskaar bhi praapt hue. yah bhartiya or united kingdom kii film nirmaataa companiyon dwaara banaai gai sanjha film thi. iskaa premier nai dilli main 30 navambar 1982 ko hua tha.
 shooting 26 november 1980 ko shuru hui or 10 may 1981 ko samaapt hui. bihar ke koilvar pul ke paas kuch drishyon ko shooting di gai. antim sanskaar drishy main 300,000 se adhik extra kaa istemaal kiya gaya tha, gineez world records ke anusaar kisi bhi film ke liye sabase adhik.
