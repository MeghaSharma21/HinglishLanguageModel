 khent lij or shelt nadiyon ke muhane par, brusselles se 31 mile uttar-pashchim main sthit nagar . yah belgium ke landers rajya kii rajdhani he. isaki sthapana 600 i. main is praant ke pratham count dwaara hui. dhire dhire prasiddhi praapt karata hua, yah nagar 1400 i. main unnati ke shikhar par pahunch gaya. is kaal se lekar 400 varsh tak khent kaa itihaas hinsa, viplav or nirantar yuddh kaa rahaa he or isi kaal main spain, france or austria ke adheen rahete hue isane apni aarthik or audyogic prabhutaa kho di; lekin 19veen shatabdi ke prarambh main yantric bunai dwaara isane pun: apni audyogic khyaati praapt kar lee.
 1812 i. main britain or netherland ke bich khent kii sandhi hui. counts kaa kila, gothij girjaghar, taunhaal tathaa sant baao ko bada girjaghar pracheen vaastukala ke smarak hai. yah linen or sooti vasr ke liye sansar prasiddh he. belgium kaa do tihaai linen yahan buna jaataa he. yah phulon ke liye bhi vikhyath he. yahan vishvavidhyalay, sangrahalay, krishi santhaan, royal conservetary or royal academy ov fine arts hai.
