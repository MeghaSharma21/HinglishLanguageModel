 nabhikeey pandubbi vah pandubbi he jisko chalaane ke liye aavashyak urja us par lage ek nabhikeey riector se milti he. paramparaagat pandubbiyon kii tulana main nabhikiya pandubbiyaan kai maamalon main shreshth hotin hai. udhaaharan ke liye, nabhikiya pandubbiyon ko kaary karne ke liye vaayu kii aavashyaktaa nahin hoti, atah inhen hawa lene ke liye baar-baar sataha par aane kii bhi aavashyaktaa nahin hoti.
