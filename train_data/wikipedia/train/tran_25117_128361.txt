 rukhsana kausar, bhartiya rajya jammu or kashmir ke kashmir ke rajouri jile ke antargat sthit kalsiyaan gaanv kii ek bahadur ladki he. usne 27 sitambar 2009 kii raat ko apane ghar main ghuse lashkar-e-taiyaba ke ek sheersh pakistani aatankwaadi ko maar giraaya, tathaa ek anya ko ghayal kar diya tha. usne or uske bhai bahanon ne milakar aatankwaadiyon se jamkar takkar lee thi. rukhsana ne ek aatankwaadi kii rifl chhinakar use ghatnasthal par hi dher kar diya tha. is ghatana main rukhsana ke mata-pita raashida or nur hossain ghayal ho gaye the. 27 sitambar kii is ghatana se pehle rukhsana ne kabhi bandook nahin pakadi thi, lekin us din usne bahaduree kii misaal kaayam kii. bharat kii raashtrapati pratibha patil tathaa grahamantri pi chidambaram ne bhi uske bahaduree kii tarif kii.
