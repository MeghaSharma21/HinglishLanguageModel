 jaevsansaadhan tathaa satat vikaas santhaan, jise laghurup main ai bi es di kehete hai, imphal, manipur main sthit hai. iskaa uddeshy is kshetra ke saamaajik-aarthik vikaas ke liye jaevpraudyogikeey hastkshepon ke madhyam jaevsansadhano kaa vikaas tathaa unka satat prayog karnaa he. iskaa lakshyabharat-burma jaevsansaadhan sthal ke antargat aanevaale bhartiya kshetra main jaevsansadhano kaa vaigyanik prabandhan karnaa he.
 bharat sarkaar ke kendriya mantrimandal ne 16 janvari, 2001 ko yojit apni baithak main imphal, manipur main jaev praudyogiki vibhaag, bharat sarkaar ke ek swaitshasi santhaan ke rup main jaevsansaadhan tathaa satat vikaas santhaan kii sthapana ko anumodan diya tha. jaevsansaadhan tathaa satat vikaas santhaan ko 26 april, 2001 ko manipur sosayadi panjikaran adhinium, 1989 main panjikrut karaayaa gaya.
