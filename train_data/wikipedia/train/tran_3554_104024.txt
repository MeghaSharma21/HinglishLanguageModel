 kalisto hamaare saura mandal ke paanchave grah brihaspati kaa doosraa sab se bada upgrah he or yah puure saura mandal kaa teesraa sab se bada chandramaa he . iskaa vyas lagbhag 4,820 kimi he, jo budh grah kaa 99% he lekin budh se kaafii ghanatv hone ke kaaran iskaa dravyaman budh kaa keval ek-tihaai he.
 kalisto ko angrezi main "Callisto" likhaa jaataa he. kalisto pracheen unani dharmik kathaon main ek tarah kii apsara thi.
 kalisto lagbhag baraabar maatra ke pathar or pani kii barf kaa bana hua he. mumkin he ke iske centre main ek patthariilaa gola ho. yah bhi sambhav he ke sataha se qareeb 100 kimi kii gaharaai par ek pani kaa mahaasaagar ho. kalisto kii sataha gadhe rang kii he or usapar bahut se prahaar cretar nazar aate hai. vaigyanik anumaan lagate hai ke yah sataha arabon varshon pehle bani thi or tab se barqaraar he. kalisto par ek "valhala" naam kaa vishaal prahaar cretar mashhoor he jika vyas lagbhag 3,800 kimi he.
 vaise to brihaspati se bahut hi bhayankar vikiran paidaa hoti he, jis se uske aasapaas kabhi bhi manushyon kaa rah paanaa mushkil lagata he . lekin brihaspati ke ird-gird parikrama karte kalisto kii kaksha kuch aisi he ke kalisto par vikiran kam he. isliye vaigyanik maanate hai ke bhavishya main yadi maanav bahari saura mandal main failenge to kalisto unke liye ek mahatvapurn adda ho saktaa he.
 kalisto kaa ek bahut hi patala vayumandal he jisamen adhiktar carbon dioxide or bahut kam maatra main aanvik oxygen maujuud he.
 sandarbh truti: "lower-alpha" naamak sandarbh-samooh ke liye tag maujuud hai, parantu samooh ke liye koi tag nahin mila. yah bhi sambhav he ki koi samaapti tag gaayab he.
