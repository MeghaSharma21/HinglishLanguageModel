 jugad bharat main un vastuon yaa aujaaron ko kehete hai jo aasaani se aasapaas uplabdh saamaanon kaa upyog karke banaayi jaati hai. aam logon ko gyaat saral vidhiyon ke upyog se kaam bana lenaa bhi 'jugad' kehalaata he. arthath maanak vidhi se hatakar alag vidhi se koi kaam kiya jaay yaa koi saamaan banaaya jaay to us vidhi yaa saamaan ko 'jugad' kehete hai.
 jab 'maanak sansadhano' kaa abhaav ho yaa ve bahut mahagein hon to 'kaamchalaoo dhang' se kuch karke kaam nikaal lene ko bhi jugad kehete hai. 
 bharat ke kai bhaagon main motorcykil yaa pumping set ingine ke prayog se bani sasti gaadi 'jugad' ke naam se jaani jaati he. isamen dijal ingine kaa prayog kar ek kaamchalaoo gaadi banaai jaati he, jo gaanv-kasbon main maal dhone, savaariyon ke avaagaman ityadi main prayog kii jaati he.
 'jugad' shabd kaa prayog hindi ke alaava anya bhartiya bhashaaon main bhi hota he. bhartiya mul ke videshon main based log bhi is shabd kaa khub prayog karte hai.
