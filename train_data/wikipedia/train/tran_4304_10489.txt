 shankar netralay chennai kaa laabh-nirpeksh netra chikitsaalay he. iske naam main 'shankar' shabd aadi shankaracharya ko suchit karata he. is chikitsaalay main puure bharat se tathaa vishwa bhar se log ankhon se sambandhit samasyaon kii chikitsa ke liye aate hai.
 is netralay main 1000 karmchaaree karyrat hai. isamen pratidin 1200 rogiyon kaa ilaaj hota he or 100 shalyakarm pratidin hoti he. iskaa vaarshik rajasav lagbhag 100 million america dollar he.
 shankar netralay ne anekon prasiddh netra-chikitsak paidaa kiye hai jinamein mumbai ke aditya jyoti netra aspataal ke sansthaapak dow es natarajan bhi shaamil hai.
