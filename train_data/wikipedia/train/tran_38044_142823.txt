 mari praant turkmenistan kii ek vilaayat he jo us desh ke dakshin-purv main sthit he. isaki sarhad afghanistan se lagati hai. is praant kaa kshetrafal 87,150 kimi2 he or san 2005 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 14,80,400 anumaanit kii gai thi. mari praant kii rajdhani kaa naam bhi mari shahar he, jise pehle marv bulaayaa jaataa tha. is praant kii aabaadi kaa ghanatv lagbhag 15 vyakti prati varg kimi he, lekin adhik viksit nachlistaan ilaaqon main yah 150-200 vyakti prati varg kimi tak pahunch jaataa he.
 san 2000 main turkmenistan ke 23% log mari praant main basate the. desh kaa 26% krishi utpaadan or 21% audyogic utpaadan isi praant se aata he. mukhya udyogon main prakritik gas, vidyut paidaavaar, vastra or kapade, qaaleen, rasayan or khanpan kii vastuen shaamil hai. 2001 main desh kii 74% bijli isi ek praant main banaai jaati thi. yahan krishi karakum nahar or afghanistan se uttar aane vaali muranaab nadi ke pani par nirbhar he. praant kaa uttari hissaa registani he lekin dakshini bhaag main ghaas or ret kaa mila-jula ilaaka he. purana marv ek pracheen shahar he or resham maarg par sthit pracheen shaharon main yah sabase acchhee haalat main he. praant kii rajdhani mari shahar karakum nahar or muranaab nadi ke milan kshetra par sthit he.
