 ramcharita upaadhyaay hindi kavi evam sahityakar the. 
 shri ramcharita upaadhyaay kaa janm san 1872 main uttar pradesh ke gajipur jile main hua tha. prarambh main ye brajabhasha main kavita karte the. acharya mahaviraprasad dvivedi ji ke protsaahan se inhone khadi boli main rachana prarambh kii or inaki rachanaayen 'saraswati' tathaa hindi kii anya patrapatrikaon main prakashit hone lageen. yah rashtriya jaagran kaa yug tha. inhone 'bharat bhakthi', 'bhavy bharat' tathaa 'raashtra bharti' jaisi uganuroop rachanaayen karke rashtriya jaagran main yogadan diya.
 inhone 'ramcharita chintamani' naamak prabandh kavy kii bhi rachana kii. yug kii chetanana se spandit hokar ram ke lokottar rup kaa chithran na karke maanviya rup kii pratishtha kii. is prakaar is kavy ke purani paatr ateet kaal ke praani na rahakar aadhunik vichaardhaara or vicasonmukh jivan se otprot hai. inhone sookhti evam niti ke padya bhi likhe, jinka sangrah 'sookhti muktawali' naamak pustak main hua he. inhone mahabharat kii katha ke aadhaar par ek mahilopayogi upanyaas 'devi draupadi' kii bhi rachana kii. apni bahumukhi sahityaseva ke kaaran dvivedi yug ke sahityakaron main inkaa vishisht sahityaseva ke kaaran dvivedi yug ke sahityakaron main inkaa vishisht sthaan he.
