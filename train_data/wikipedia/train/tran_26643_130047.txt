 shrimati renuka choudhary ek bhartiya rajaneta hai, ve congress kii ore se chaudaavin loksabhaa main khammam sansadeey seat se pratinidhitiv karti hai. samprag sarkaar banane ke baad se sarkaar main kendriya mahila evam bal vikaas mantri kaa padbhaar sambhala.
 shrimati ne audyogic manovigyaan main eme kii degree hasil kii he. shrimati choudhary kaa raajnneeti main pravesh telegu desham party ke manch se 1984 main hua jiske saath ve 1998 tak raheen. 1998 main unhone congress kii sadsyta hasil kii. ve 1986 se 1992 tak lagaataar do baar rajyasabha kii sadsy manonith huin. is dauraan ve telegu desham party kii chif vip raheen or terahavin or chaudaavin loksabhaa main lagaataar do baar khammam seat kaa pratinidhitiv kiya. samaaj seva ke kshetra main bhi sakriy rahane vaali renuka choudhary 1999 main pehali baar loksabhaa ke liye chuni gai thim. 2004 main ve duusari baar loksabhaa ke liye chuni gai thim. ve mahilaon or bacchon se judi samasyaon par lekhan bhi karti hai.
