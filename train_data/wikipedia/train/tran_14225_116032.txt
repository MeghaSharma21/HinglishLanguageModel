 garhwa bharat main jharkhand praant kaa ek jila he. garhwa jile kaa nirmaan palamu ke aath prakhando ko milakar 1 april 1991 ko kiya gaya jo palamu ke dakshin pashchim hisse main sthit the. garhwa ke uttar main son nadi bahati he jo ise chhattisgarh se alag karti he, purv main palamu he or pashchim main chhattisgarh kaa sarguja jila tathaa uttar pradesh kaa sonbhadra jila he. garhwa ke prakhandon main garhwa sahit- meril, ranka, bhanderiya, majiyaanw, nagar-untaari, bhavanathpur evam dhuraki shaamil the. baad main inhen prakhandon main se chah or naye prakhand srujit kiye gaye jinamein dandai, chinia, ramana, ramakanda evam kandi shaamil the. 
 garhwa rail evam sadak dwaara bharat ke baaki hisson se sugam sampark main he. jharkhand main ranchievam anya sthanon se garhwa ke liye sidhi bas seva uplabdh he. iske alaava bihar evam chhattisgarh ke bhi kuch anya sthanon se yahaan ke liye bas suvidha he. is jile se hokar koi rashtriya rajmarg nahin guzarati lekin 210 kilometre rajya ucch path evam 96 kilometre lambaai kii jila sadak is shahar ke vibhinn hisson ko jodti he. jinamein garhwa murisemer road jo nagar utari hote hue jharkhand ko uttar pradesh ke banaras evam elahabaad kshetron se jodta he, tathaa rehala godaramana road jo garhwa ko nav nirmit pradesh chhattisgarh se jodta he. railgadee se yahaan pahunchane ke liye sabase najdiki stesan mirzapur he. yah jila abhi bhi vaayu maarg se nahin juda he.
