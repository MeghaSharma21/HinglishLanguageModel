 helicopter ek vimaan he, jise ek yaa adhik kshaitij roter ke dwaara upar kii disha main nodith kiya jaataa he. prayek roter main do yaa adhik pankhudiyaan hoti hai. helicopteron ko roter-wing vayuyan kii shrenee main vargeekrut kiya gaya he, jisase ki inhen jude-pankh vayuyan se pruthak kiya ja sake. yah shabd angrezi helicopter se vyutpann he, jo french bhashaa ke shabd hélicoptère se niklaa he, jise gustav de ponton daaimecourt ne 1861 main srujit kiya tha. yah bhi unani bhashaa ke shabd helix/helik- se bana he, arthath "kundaleedaar" yaa "mudata hua" evam pteron = "pankh".
 roter pranali yaa roter, helicopter kaa ek ghumta hua bhaag hota he, jo use ek upari bal detaa he. yah pranali kshaitij bhi lag sakati he, kyonki mukhya roter urdhwadhar bal yaa lift detaa he. yaa ise urdhwadhar bhi lagaaya ja saktaa he, puchh roter kii tarah. yahaan yah kshaitij bal detaa he, tork prabhaav kii pratikriya ko rokane ke liye. roter main ek ek mast yaa dand, ek chakranay  hub or roter pankhudiyaan yaa blades hote hai!
