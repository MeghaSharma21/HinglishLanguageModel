 canada ke ek parivaar ke aborjini script likhane ke liye istemaal kiya abugidas aborjini canada ke kai bhashaaon ke Algonquian, Inuit, or ke pariwaron ke Athabaskan bhashaa he.
 vartmaan main yah likhane ke liye istemaal kiya kri ke sabhi boliyon se Naskapi ke rocky parvat sahit purvi kri, james khadi kri, daldali kri samtal maidan ko or kri . ye likhane ke liye bhi istemaal kiya jaataa inukitut main canada ke purvi arctic ; yahaan hai ve sah adhikari ke saath latin varnmaala ke kshetra main Nunavut . unhone kshetra ke liye upyog kiya jaataa he canada ke anya bade Algonquian bhashaa, Ojibwe main pashchimi canada, saath hi blakefut ke liye, jahaan ve hai aprachlit he. in bhashaaon ke aage Athabaskan ko pashchim, syllabics kaa upyog kiya jaataa he par ek ank likhane ke liye yaa kisi anya Dakelh, Chipewyan, Slavey, Tli Cho, Tasttine . Syllabics main prayog kiya jaataa he kabhi kabhi sanyukt rajya america dwaara samudaayon straddle he ki seemaa he, lekin mukhya hai canada ke ek ghatana he.
 yah script james ivance ke dwaara banaaya gaya tha, se prerana ke saath devanagari pitman ashulipi lipiyon .
