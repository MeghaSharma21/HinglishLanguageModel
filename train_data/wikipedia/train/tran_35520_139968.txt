 tihamah yaa tihama arabi prayadweep kaa laal sagar ke saath lagane vaala ek baariq tatavarti kshetra he jo aadhunik saudi arab or yaman ke deshon main aata he. bade arth main tihamah aqaaba kii khadi se lekar bab al-mandeb jaldamru tak vistrut he lekin aksar yah naam keval us kshetra ke dakshin aadhe bhaag ko lagaaya jaataa he, jo jaddah se shuru hokar dakshin main aseer praant or yaman ke pashchim main aata he.
 tihamah ke adhik bhaag main ret ke tile or maidan faile hue hai. iskaa adhiktar bhaag shushk he haalanki kahin-kahin par nachlistaan bhi milte hai. yaman ke al-hudaidah, mokaa or zabeed tathaa saudi arab ke jazan, al-qunfudah​, bariq or al-ligh​ is kshetra main sthit kuch mahatvapurn shahar hai. tihamah kaa adhiktar tat aane vaali naukaaon ke liye khataranaak he or isapar bahut kam bandargahen hai .
 aadhunik kaal main tihamah ko teen hisson main vibhajit kiya jaataa he jinka naam unase purv main sthit andruni kshetron par padaa he. sabase uttar main hijaaz se pashchim main tihaamat al-hijaaz he, us se dakshin main aseer kshetra se laga hua tihaamat aseer he or sabase neeche tihaamat al-yaman he. madhya or dakshini kshetra arabi prayadweep ke tatavarti koharaa registan kaa hissaa he.
 tihamah duniya ke sabase garm kshetron main se ek he. garmiyon main al-hudaidah kaa taapmaana 43 centegrade kaa ho saktaa he or dopaher ke 12 baje yahan nami kaa str 40-60% ke aasapaas rahata he. baahar se aaye log aise mausam main bahut ghutan mahasoos karte hai. 'tihamah' shabd kaa arth hi 'garam dharti' hota he. is kshetra ke kuch bhaagon main monsoon saal main do baar barish laata he - march-april main madhyam barish or phir july se sitambar main adhik barish.
