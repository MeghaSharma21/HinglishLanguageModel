 'nimbark' kaa arth he- neem par surya. mathura main sthit dhruv tile par nimbark sampradaay kaa pracheen mandir bataaya jaataa he. is sampradaay ke sansthaapak bhaskaracharya ek sanyasi the. is sampradaay kaa siddhanth 'dwaitadvaitvaad' kehalaata he. isi ko 'bhedabhedwaad' bhi kaha jaataa he. bhedabhed siddhanth ke aacharyon main audhulomi, ashmarathy, bhatri prapanch, bhaskar or yadav ke naam aate hai. is pracheen siddhanth ko 'dwaitadvait' ke naam se pun: sthapit karne kaa shrey nimbarkacharya ko jaataa he. unhone 'veydant parijaat-saurabh', veydant-kamdhenu, rahasy shodasi, prapann kalpavalli or krishna stotra naamak granthon kii rachana bhi kii thi. veydant parijaat saurabh brahmasutra par nimbarkacharya dwaara likhi gai tikaa he. isamen veydant sutron kii sakshipt vyakhya dwaara dwaitadvaithav siddhanth kaa pratipaadan kiya gaya he.
