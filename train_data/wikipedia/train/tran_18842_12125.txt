 balbhadra kunwar ek nepali swatantraaa senaanee evam general the. ve nepal ke rashtriya nayak hai. ve angrej-nepal yuddh main apane mahaan sevaaon ke liye vikhyath hai. ve shahi nepali sena ke ek capten the tathaa 1814 ke nalpani ke yuddh main senanayake ke rup main unako prasiddhi mili. nalpani, dehradun ke paas sthit he.
 unka janm vartmaan kabhreplanchok jilake bhanvarkot main pita sardaar chandraveer kunwar or mata ambika devi ke tisre putrako rupama hua. unka dadaji sardaar jaikrishna kunwar prasiddh sardaar ramakrishna kunwarake bhaai the. unke mamaa nepalke pradhanmantri evam sarvocch shaasha bhimsen thapa the. thapa vanshake saath sambandhi hone se kunwar kshatri nepalke kaaji pariwaronmen sammilit hue.
