 los angeles antararashtriya vimaanapttan golibari, 2013 prashanth samay katibaddh ke anusaar navambar 1, 2013 ko subah 9:20 baje ghatit ghatana he jisamen los angeles antararashtriya vimaanapttan ke jaanch bindu kshetra terminal 3 par ek bandukadhari ne golibari armbh kar di jisamen parivahan suraksha prashasan ke ek karmchaaree kii maut sahit kai log ghayal ho gayi.
 paul anthony ciyansia ne hawaii adde ke terminal 3 par pravesh kiya or arddh-swachaalit raifal ko baste se baahar nikaala, evam golibari shuru kar di. wo parivahan suraksha prashasan ke jaanch kshetra main ghus gaya or golibari chaalu rakhi. terminal par fyring karne ke baad bandookdhaari suraksha jaanch vaale kshetra main gaya or vahaan bhi usne firing jari rakhi.
