 purujit kuntibhoj naamak desh kaa raja tha. ye sthaan aaj ke madhya pradesh main he. vah kunti kaa bhai tha or pandavon kaa mamaa. kurukshetra ke yuddh main purujit ne pandavon kii ore se yuddh kiya or apane putron ke saath kai kaurav yoddhaon ko parrast kiya.
 teenon bhaaiyon, purujit, dhristaketu or vrihadkshatra kaa vadh chaudaaven din ke yuddh main dronacharya ke haathon hua. anya kaikey bandhu apane anya bhaaiyon dwaara maare gaye jo kaurav sena kii ore se yuddh kar rahe the.
