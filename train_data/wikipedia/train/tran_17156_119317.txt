 albarto moravia italy ke agrani upanyaaskaar the. inkaa janm 28 november 1907 ko rome main hua tha. inaki sarvaadhik charchit rachana 'da confermist' thi, jis ke aadhaar par 1970 main ek film bhi bani. inhone apni rachanaaon main prem vihin vaasana ko dikhaaya he. italy ke saamaajik jivan kaa utkrisht srijan karne kaa shrey inhen praapt he. jiske kaaran vishwa sahitya jagat main inhen ek vishesh sthaan diya jaataa he. 'a sik bvoy winter' inkaa pehla katha-sangrah tha. 1953 main unhen merjoti puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
