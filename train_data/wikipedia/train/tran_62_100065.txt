  abdurrahman avahtt bhashaa ke praarambhik mahakaviyon main gine jate hai. inkaa aavirbhaav apbhransh kaal ke parvarti yug main hua.
 inke pita kaa naam mir hossain or yah multan ke nivasee the.
 sandesh raasak/raso unki prasiddh kriti he jise 1207 i ke lagbhag kaa bataaya jaataa he. yah ek dootkavy he. hindi sahitya main prakruti varnan kaa samavesh karne vaale praathmik kaviyon main inaki gananaa kii jaati he.
 yah avahtt bhashaa ke pratham musalmaan kavi maane jate hai.
