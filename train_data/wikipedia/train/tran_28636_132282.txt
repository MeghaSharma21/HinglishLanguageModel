 nyaayamurti gyaan sudhaa mishra  supreme court main nyaayadhish hai. ve jharkhand ucch nyaayalay kii pehali mahila mukhya nyaayadhish rah chuki hai. unke pita nyaayamurti satish chandra mishra patna ucch nyaayalay ke ek purv mukhya nyaayadhish the. ve 16 march 1994 ko patna ucch nyaayalay kii nyaayadhish niyukt hui. unhone jharkhand ucch nyaayalay main 13 july 2008 ko mukhya nyaayadhish ke rup main yogadan diya. iske baad inkaa tabadalaa rajasthan ucch nyaayalay kar diya gaya. inhone rajasthan ucch nyaayalay main 14 varshon tak nyaayadhish ke rup main kaam kiya. 30 april 2010 ko 4 varshon ke liye supreme court kii nyaayadhish ke rup main inaki niyukti hui.
 ek nyaayamurti ke rup main ye samay kii pabandi ko mahatv dene ke liye mashhoor he.
