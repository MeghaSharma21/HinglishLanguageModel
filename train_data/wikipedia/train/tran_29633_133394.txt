 bhai kaanh singh nabha unnisvin sadi ke ek sikh vidwaan, koshkar or lekhak the jo apane rache vishwagyanakoshiya granth, 'mahaan kosh' ke liye jane jate hai. unke likhe granth mahaan kosh ko sikhamat, punjabi zabaan or viraasat kaa vishwagyanakosh kaa darja hasil he. unhone singh sabha lahar main bhi mahatvapurn yogadan diye.
