 khyath itihaas-sambandhi sahitya he jiske lekhan kaa prachlan bharat ke un kshetron main tha jise aajkal rajasthan ke naam se janaa jaataa he. arthath khyath aitihaasik dastavej hai. kuch prasiddh khyath nimnlikhit hai-
 ‘khyath’ shabd multah sanskrit kaa shabd he. ‘khya’ dhaatu main ‘kt’ pratyay judane se ‘khyath’ shabd bana he, jiskaa arth he 'bhootkaal kii ghatnaon kaa varnan' yaa 'bhootkaal ko gyaat karnaa'. khyatkaaron ne ‘khyath’ shabd kaa prayog 'itihaas' ke rup main hi kiya tha. luijipiyo tessitori sarvapratham evam sarvaadhik khyaton ko prakaash main laane vaale vyakti the. tessitori kaa surveykshan na sirf bhashaa vigyaan kii drushti se apitu sahitya main itihaas ke nirdhaaran kii drushti se bhi shreshth tha.
