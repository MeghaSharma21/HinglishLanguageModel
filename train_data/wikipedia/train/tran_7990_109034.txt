 bharat vyas hindi filmon ke prasiddh geetkaar the. inkaa janm 6 janvari 1918 ko bikaner me hua tha jati se pushkarana brahman the. mul rup se churu ke the.bachapan se hi iname kavi pratibha dikhne lagi thi. unhone 17-18 varsh kii umr tak lekhan shuru kar diya tha. churu se metric karne ke baad ve kalakatta chale gaye. unka likhaa pehla geet tha - aao vero hilmil gaae vande maataram . unke dwaara raamu channa naamak naatak bhi likhaa gaya.
 1942 ke baad ve bombay aa gaye unhone kuch filmno bhi bhoomika nibhaai lekin prasiddhi geet lekhan se mili unki mrityu 1982 me hui thi. unke likhe pramukh geet he - do aankhe baarah haath, navrang, buund jo ban gai moti filmon ke geet
