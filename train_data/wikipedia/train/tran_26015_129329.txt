 maariana dweepasmooh prashanth mahaasaagar ke pashchimi bhaag main sthit ek ardhachandra aakaar kaa dweepasmooh he jiske dweep 15 samudri jwalamukhiyon ke ve shikhar hai jo samudratal se upar ubhar aae hai. inamen se adhiktar jwalamukhi supt hai. yah dweepasmooh japan se purv-dakshinpurv, hawaii se pashchim-dakshinpaschim, nayaa gini se uttar or philipinoz se purv main hai or philipino sagar kii purvi seemaa paribhaashit karte hai. yah micronishia kshetra kaa bhaag maane jate hai. is puure dweepasmooh par sanyukt rajya america kaa adhikaar he or prashasnic rup se ise do bhaagon main baanta gaya he: uttari maariana dweep or dweepasmooh ke dakshini ant par guam kshetra. tiniyan or cypen uttari maariana dwipon ke mukhya dweep hai or aitihaasik avasheshon se pataa chalata he ki tiniyan puure dweepasmooh kaa pehla dweep tha jahaan maanvon kaa deraa dala.
