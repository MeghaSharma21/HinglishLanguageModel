 bhaskar-I or II upgrah bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan dwaara nirmit do upgrah the jinhone bharat ke pehle kam kaksha prithvi avlokan upgrah shrunkhlaa kaa gathan kiya. unhone telemetary, samudr vigyaan or jal vigyaan par aankade jutaaye hai.
 bhaskar - 1, jiskaa vajan 444 kilogram tha ko, kasputin yaar se 7 june 1979 ko intercosmos prakshepan yaan par se prakshepit kiya gaya. yah bhu - sameepak 394 kilometre or 399 kilometre kii apoji kaksha main 50.7 degree ke jhukaav par rakhaa gaya tha. upgrah ke shaamil
 upgrah ne samudr or bhoomi kii sataha kaa data pradaan kiya he. haalaanki, baad main camere kharaab ho gayi. grih vyavastha telemetary 17 farvari 1993 par punahpravesh tak praapt kii gai thi.
 bhaskar - 2, jo 436 kilogram vajani tha ko, 20 navambar 1981 main kasputin yaar se prakshepit kiya gaya tha. yah bhartiya up mahadweep kii 300 televijan chhaviyon kii prapti ke baad parichaalit ghoshit kiya gaya tha. grih vyavastha telemetary 1991 tak praapt kii gai thi. isane 30 navambar 1991 par kaksha main punah pravesh kiya. yah 368 kilometre kii bhu - sameepak or 372 kilometre kii apoji kaksha main 50.7 degree ke jhukaav par sthapit tha.
