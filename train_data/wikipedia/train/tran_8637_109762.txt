 pipalkot nepal ke dailekh jile kii ek gaanv vikaas samiti he. yah karnali nadi ke kinare basaa he.
 san 2001 kii nepal kii rashtriya janaganana ke anusaar yahan kaa jansankhya 3,931 he jisame purush kaa pratishat 59.90 va mahilaon kaa 40.10 he. is gaanv main kul 619 ghar he.
 yah ga.vi.sa. visan2038 main sinhasin ga.vi.sa.se alag hua tha. is ga.vi.sa.kaa simana is prakaar he- purv main kaalikot jila jila, pashchim main sinhasin, uttar main karnali nadi tathaa achhaam jila jila, dakshin membahakot ga.vi.sa..
 yahan par bramhan, thakuri, magar, khas kshatri, dalit aadi jaatiyon kaa vaas he. yahan ke adhikansh log hindu dharm maanate hai. yahan mukhya rup se dashahra, bhaiya dooj, deepawali, haritalika teej, chaitrashtami aadi tyohaar manaaye jate hai. in tyohaaron par jagah-jagah par meley lagate hai. yahan kii mukhya bhashaa nepali he.
 yahan kaa pramukh vyapaarik kendra khidki jiula va kitu he. yah ga.vi.sa.surkhet-jumlaa sadak khand se judaa he. yahaan ke adhiktar log krishi se jude hai.
