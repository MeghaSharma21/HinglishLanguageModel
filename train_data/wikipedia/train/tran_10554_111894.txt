 shaikshik sudhaar kaa saral arth yah he - 'vyaktiyon ko soochna yaa gyaan pradaan karne kii vidhi main parivartan karnaa'. hameshaa se hi shiksha kaa arth, shiksha kii vidhiyaan, shiksha-saamagree aadi par bahes hoti rahi he jiske parinamswaroop shiksha main aavashyak parivartan kiye jate rahe hai.
