 nehru smarak sangrahalay evam pustkaalay ) nayi dilli sthit ek sangrahalay evam pustkaalay he. iskaa lakshya bharat ke swatantraaa sangram sanjona tathaa uskaa punarnirmaan akarna he. yah teen murti bhawan ke praangan main sthit he. isaki sthapana 1964 main jawaharlal nehru ke dehant ke uparant kiya gaya. yah bharat sarkaar ke samskruti mantralay ke antargat ek swytt sanstha he.
 multah yah bharat ke pratham pradhanmantri jawaharlal nehru kaa sarkari awaas tha. iske chaar skandh hai- smarak sangrahalay, aadhunik bharat se sambandhit pustkaalay, samasamyik adhyayan kendra or nehru taramandal.
