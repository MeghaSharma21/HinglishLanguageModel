 majaathal hasarang vanyajeev vihaar himachal pradesh ke solan va shimla jile main sthit he. yah vihaar sutluj ke tat par sthit he. is vihaar main himalay kshetra ke kaale bhaaluu, junglee billi, bhartiya vanya bhaaluu, langur, sambar, hiran, leopard, chital, resus aadi jeev surakshit hai. is vihaar main keval ok ke vriksh paae jate hai. yah vanya vihaar kshetra 3,938.86 hectare kshetra main failaa hua he. 
 is vihaar kii samudratal se nunatam oonchai 900 meter tathaa adhiktam oonchai 1966 meter he. is vihaar kshetra main ausat vaarshik varsha 1040 millimetre ke lagbhag hoti he. is kshetra kaa taapmaana saamanytah nunatam ek degree centegrade tathaa adhiktam 29 degree centegrade rahata he. is vanyajeev vihaar ko sangrakshan pradaan karne ke liye pehali baar 1962 main tathaa duusari baar 1974 main adhishuchet kiya gaya.
