 rituparna sen sengupta bharat main pichley dashak se bangla cinema kii pramukh abhinetri hai or vartmaan main samikshakon dwaara saraahi gai hindi filmon main bhoomika nibhaa rahi hai.
 sengupta kaa janm 7 navambar 1971 main kalakatta main hua tha. vah bachapan se hi kala main sakriy thim or chitrangashu naamak chitrakaari vidhyalay main chitrakaari, nrity, gayan or hastshilp kii shiksha leti thim. unhone mount carmel skool main apni schuli shiksha praapt kii or baad main ladi breabourne college main itihaas se snaatak kii oopaadhi hasil kii. unhone kalakatta vishvavidhyalay se kala main snatakottar kaa adhyayan shuru kiya lekin abhinetri ke rup main apane carrier par dhyaan dene ke liye unhen apni padhai ko baadhit karnaa padaa.
 sengupta ne apane bachapan ke saathi sanjay chakravarti se kolkata main 13 disambar 1999 main shaadi kii jo ki mobiaps ke sansthaapak or siio hai. ve thalasemia se peedit bacchon ke kalyaan main sakriy rup se shaamil hai. unhen outdoor khel bhi pasand he or samay bitaane ke liye badminton bhi khelti hai.
