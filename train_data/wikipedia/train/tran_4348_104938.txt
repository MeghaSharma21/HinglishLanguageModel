 vashchik yaa scorpiyo taramandal raashichakr kaa ek taramandal he. puraani khagolashastriy pustakon main ise aksar ek bichchhu ke rup main darshaya jaataa tha. akaash main iske pashchim main tula taramandal hota he or iske purv main dhanu taramandal. yah ek bada taramandal he jo khagoleey gole ke dakshini bhaag main akashganga ke bich main sthit he.
 vashchik taramandal main 15 mukhya taare hai, haalanki vaise isamen 47 gyaat taare sthit hai jinako bair naam die ja chuke hai. vaigyanikon ko san 2010 tak inamen se 13 taron ke ird-gird 26 grah parikrama karte hue paa liye the. is taramandal main bahut se roshan taare he, jaise kii jyeshtha, graphiyas, mul tara, jubaha, sargas, vamairah.
