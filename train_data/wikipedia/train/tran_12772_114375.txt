 dasharajna yuddh yaa das raajaaon kaa yuddh ek yuddh tha jiskaa ullekh rugveda ke saatven mandal main 7:18, 7:33 or 7:83:4-8 main miltaa he. is yuddh main ek taraf puru naamak arya qabeela or unka mitrapaksh samudaay tha, jinke salaahkaar rishi vishwamitra the. duusari ore bharat naamak samudaay tha, jiskaa netritv tritsu naamak qabeele ke raja sudas kar rahen the or jinke prerak rishi vashishtha the. is yuddh main sudas ke bharaton kii vijay hui or uttar bhartiya upmahadweep ken arya logon par unka adhikaar ban gaya. aage chalakar puure desh kaa naam hi 'bharat' pad gaya. kai itihaaskaaron ke anusaar yah varnan ek vaastavik yuddh par hi aadhaarit ho saktaa he.
