 ranjit singh kii samadhi punjab ke sikh sardaar maharaja ranjit singh san 1839 ke marane kii yaadgaar he. yah qila lahore or baadashahi masjid ke nazadik sthit he. unke bete khadg singh ne yah us jagah par banani shuru kii thi jahaan ranjit singh ko marane ke baad main jalaayaa gaya tha. baad main unke duje bete dalip singh ne isko san 1848 main puraa kiya.
