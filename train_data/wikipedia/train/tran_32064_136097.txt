 cartoonist yusuf munna 1995 se anek patr-patrikao or news portal ke liye cartoon bana rahe he. uttar pradesh ke gorakhpur main janme yusuf ne dainik 'swatantra chetana' se apane karier kii shuruyaat kii. 1999 main ve nai dilli aa gaye. yahan ve 'kaanti' saptahik samachar patr main upasampadak pad par kaam karte hue cartoon nirmaan ke kshetra main lagaataar sakriy he. ab tak unke cartoon masik patrika antar pravaah, india next, muslim india, hindi dainik awam-i-hind, dainik siyasat, news portal www.twocircles.net aadi sthano par prakashit ho chuke he. vartmaan main ve The Milligazette or news portal www.indiatomorrow.net ke liye cartoon bana rahe he. 2016 main yusuf munna ko 'best cartoonist' award se sammanit kiya gaya.
