 itrans, devanagari sahit bharat kii anek lipiyon main likhe paath ko roman lipi main lipyantaran kii ek paddati he. aajkal anek computer softwaron ke uplabdh hone se itrans main lipyantaran kaa kaary atyant saral, tej or masini ho gaya he. iskaa vikaas avinaash chopde ne kiya. itrans kaa naveentam sanskaran 5.30 he jo 2001 ke july main aayaa tha. isi sanskaran ke baad itrans ko sthir kar diya gaya he.
 kuch udhaaharan:
 mukhyatah urdu yaa devanagari ke liye.
 windos axpi yaa windos 2000 prayog karne waalon ke liye hindi likhane kaa sab se suvidhajanak tarika he microsoft kaa indic ikhmai. apane computer par itrans main hindi main likhnaa chaalu karne ke liye iskaa prayog karen. is vidhi se itrans chaalu karne ke liye aapako windos kii installer sidi kii zarurat pad sakati he. yadi aapke computer par C:\I386 naam kaa foulder he to installer sidi kii jagah is foulder kaa path de den. itrans ke liye zaruri sabhi sanchikaaye isamen hote hai. yadi sidi nahi he to phir hindi tulkit aajamaayen.
