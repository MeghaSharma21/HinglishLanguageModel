 pak arun grah kaa ek upgrah he. 1985 tak pak vaigyanikon ke liye agyaat tha or iske astitv kaa tab pataa chalaa jab arun ke paas se guzarate hue wayager dwitiya yaan kii khinchi gayi tasveeron main yah nazar aayaa. pak kaa akar lagbhag gole jaisaa he lekin puraa gola nahin he. isapar antariksh se gire hue ulkapindon kii vajah se bahut se bade gadhde hai.
 pak kaa akar gol-jaisaa par thodaa besanga he. iskaa ausat vyas lagbhag 162 kimi he, jo arun ke paanch bade upgrahon se kaafii chota he. arun ke paanch bade upgrah titania, oberon, firial, ambrial or miranda hai, jinamein se sab se chota miranda he jiskaa vyas lagbhag 471 kimi he. prithvi ke chandramaa kaa vyas lagbhag 3,474 kimi he, yani kii hamaara chandramaa pak se lagbhag ikkis guna bada he.
