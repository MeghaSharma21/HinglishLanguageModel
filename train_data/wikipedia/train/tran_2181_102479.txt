 pope benedict XVI jinka asli naam yusaf aalios ratsinger he, catholic samudaay ke 265wein pope chune gayi hai. 16 april 1927 ko germany main janme pope benedict ko 24 april 2005 ko pontiff ke rup main chunaa jayega. pichley 275 saalon main chune gaye ye pope sabase umrdaar hai, is samay benedict kii umr 80 varsh he.
