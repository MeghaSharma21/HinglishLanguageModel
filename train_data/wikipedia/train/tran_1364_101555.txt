 gleez 581 prithvi se 22 prakaash varsh dur tula taramandal main sthit M3V shrenee ek laal bouna tara he. vaigyanikon kaa anumaan he ke iske ird-gird kam-se-kam chaar grah parikrama kar rahen hai, jinke naam gleez 581 i, gleez 581 bi, gleez 581 si or gleez 581 ji hai. sambhav he ke is grahiy mandal main do or gleez 581 di or gleez 581 fif naam ke grah hon lekin iskaa abhi pakkaa pataa nahin chal paaya he. 2010 main yah paaya gaya ke "gleez 581 ji" is taare ke vaasayogy kshetra ke bilkul bich main tha, lekin 2011 main yah ghoshnaa kii gayi ke "gleez 581 di" par prithvi ke jaise jivan hone kii sambhaavna he. pruthvi jaise jivan ko saamanytah taral jal or bhojan ke rup me urja shrot kii pramukh aavashyaktaa hoti he.
 is taare ne tab media kaa dhyaan aakarshith kiya jab san 2007 me iske vaasayogy kshetra ke najadik gleez 581 si grah mila. utsaah tab bahut bad gaya jab sitambar 2010 me iske tara mandal me gleez 581 ji grah mila, jo kii iske vaasayogy kshetra ke ekadam bijo-bich tha or taral pani or jivan kii sabhi zarurato ke liye vahaan sabase jyada upyukt tha.
