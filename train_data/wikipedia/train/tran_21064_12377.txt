 jiangsu janavaadee ganraajya china ke purvi tat par sthit ek praant he. iskaa naam 'jiang' or 'su' ko jodakar banaaya gaya tha. china ke sabhi praanton main se jiangsu main sabase ghani aabaadi he. jiangsu kii rajdhani nanjing shahar he. is praant kaa kshetrafal 1,02,600 varg kimi he, yani bharat ke bihar rajya se zaraa zyaada. san 2010 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 7,86,59,903 thi, yani bharat ke andhra pradesh rajya se zaraa kam. jiangsu kaa pile sagar se 1,000 kimi se bhi lambaa kinaaraa lagata he or praant ke dakshini bhaag se yangtse nadi guzarati he.
 1978 main shuru hue chini aarthik sudhaaron ke baad jiangsu main jabardast tarakqi hui he. yahan kii ausat prati vyakti aay china ke kisi bhi anya praant se zyaada he haalanki praant ke uttari hisse dakshin kii tulana main kaafii pichad gaye hai. is praant se duniya bhar main alectronic saamaan, rasayan or vastra niryaat hote hai or china main sabase zyaada videshi poonji bhi yaheen niveshit he.
