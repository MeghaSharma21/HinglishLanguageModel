 isi naam se prasiddh ek bhartiya krantikari ke bare main jaanane ke liye yahaan jaany - sukhdev
 mahabharat ke pashchaat ke kuru vansh ke raja.
 yah pracheen bharat ke suryvansh-chandravansh ke prasiddh raja the. bhartiya itihaas ati pracheen he. puranic vanshavali adhastat di gayi he. yah vanshavali krit yug se dwapar ke ant tak kii he. neeche likhi suchiyaan manu se armbh hoti hai or bhagwaan krishna kii peedhee par samaapt hoti hai. puri vanshavali jo puraanon main uplabdh he, nand vansh tak kii he.
 ram kii peedhee 65 he jabaki krishna kii 94. isse unke bich kii avadhi kaa anumaan bataaya ja saktaa he. in pidhiyon kaa jitna sambhav tha utana samakraman kiya gaya he. bharat ke pracheen saptarshi panchang ke anusaar yah kalkram 6676 ipu se armbh hota he.
 us kaal ke vibhinn arya raajaaon ke bare main in vanshavaliyon se bahut gyaan miltaa he.
