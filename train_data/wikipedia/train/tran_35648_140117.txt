 sattu ek prakaar kaa deshaj vyanjan he, jo bhune hue jau or chane ko pis kar banaaya jaataa he. bihar main yah kaaphi lokapriy he or kai roopon main prayukt hota he. saamanytah yah churna ke rup main rahata he jise pani main ghol kar yaa anya roopon main khaaya athwa piya jaataa he. sattu ke sookhe tathaa ghol donon hi roopon ko 'sattu' kehete hai.
 sattu sirf chane kaa hi nahin jau kaa bhi banaaya jaataa he. jau ke sattu ke liye bhi uparyukt prakriya hi jau ke saath kii jaati he.
