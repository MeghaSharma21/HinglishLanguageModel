 di en e jivit koshikaon ke gunasutron main paae jane vaale tantunuma anu ko di-oxyribonucleic amla yaa di en e kehete hai. isamen anuvaanshik kuta nibaddh rahata he. di en e anu kii sanrachana ghumaavdaar seedhi kii tarah hoti he.
 diene kii ek anu chaar alag-alag raas vastuon se bana he jinhen newcliotide kehete he. har newcliotide ek nitrogen yukt vastu he. in chaar newcliotidone ko adenine, guanin, thymin or cytosin kaha jaataa he. in newcliotidone se yukt dioxyribose naam kaa ek shakkar bhi paaya jaataa he. in newcliotidone ko ek phosphate kii anu jodatii he. newcliotidone ke sambandh ke anusaar ek koshika ke liye avashya proteinon kii nirmaan hota he. atah di in e har ek jivit koshika ke liye anivaary he. 
 diene aamtaur par chromosome ke rup main hota he. ek koshika main gunasutron ke set apane jinom kaa nirmaan karata he; maanav jinom 46 gunasutron kii vyavastha main diene ke lagbhag 3 arab aadhaar jode he. jin main anuvanshik jaankaree ke prasaaran kii poorak aadhaar bandhna ke madhyam se hasil kii he. udhaaharan ke liye, ek koshika ek jin main jaankaree kaa upyog karata he jab pratilekhan main, diene anukram diene or sahi arene newcliotidon ke bich aakarshan ke madhyam se ek poorak shahi sena anukram main nakal he. aamtaur par, yah arene kii nakal to shahi sena newcliotidon ke bich ek hi baatcheet par nirbhar karata he jo anuwaad naamak prakriya main ek milan protein anukram banane ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he. vaikalpik bhaanumati main ek koshika bas ek prakriya bulaayaa diene pratikruti main apane anuvanshik jaankaree copy kar sakate hai.
 di en e kii rupachitra kii khoj angreji vaigyanik james watson or francis crik ke dwaara san 1953 main kiya gaya tha. is khoj ke liye unhen san 1962 main nobel puraskaar sammanit kiya gaya.
