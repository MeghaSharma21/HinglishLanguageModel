 dhanu yaa segiterius taramandal raashichakr kaa ek taramandal he jisamen hamaari gallexi, kshirmarg, kaa kendriya hissaa aata he. puraani khagolashastriy pustakon main ise aksar ek dhanurdhari kinnar ke rup main darshaya jaataa tha. dhanu taramandal main hamaari akashganga ke kendra main sthit dhanu e naamak khagoleey radio strot he jo dhanu e* naamak vishalkay kaale chhidr kaa ghar mana jaataa he.
 dhanu taramandal main baarah mukhya taare hai, haalanki vaise isamen dariinon taare sthit hai. is taramandal ke kuch taare milakar iske andar ek chaaydani kii akruti banaate hai. kyonki is taramandal main hamaari gallexi, akashganga, kaa centre bhi padataa he isliye isamen bahut si niharikaayen bhi dikhti hai jinamein se ek massier 55 naam kaa tarasamuh kaa prakaash prabal he.
