 satha pratha, rajasthan me prachlit he, isame do parivaar kii nari ko samaaj ke banghan me jhakda jaataa he. jisase tang aakar nari aatm hatya bhi kar leti he. satha pratha me do parivaar me sahjatiy vivaah hota he. yani kii unki beti kaa vivaah apane ghar me or apni bahan kaa vivaah unke ghar me ! isko vivaah na kah kar ek prakaar kaa sauda yaa satha kaha jaataa he1 satha pratha me apni beti ko sasuraal me tang kiya jaataa he vaisaa kaa vaisaa unki beti ko bhi tang kiya jaataa he. natijaa ye ki koi ek talaak detii he to dusri bhi teen mahine ke garbh ke saath pihar me baith jaati he or apane maan baap ke saath milakar bhuwarn hatya tak kar detii he. kabhi kabhi pihar me baithi beti apane pati ke viyog me aatm hatya bhi kar leti he. satha pratha me dono parivaar kii betia rishto me zyada jakadan dekh kar apana maansik santulan bigaad detii he.
