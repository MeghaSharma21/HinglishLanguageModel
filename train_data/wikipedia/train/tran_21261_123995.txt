 guddu rangila subhash kapur dwaara nirdeshit aagaamee ek bhartiya kamedi film he. is film main arshad varsi, amit saadh or ronit roy mukhya kirdaar nibhaaenge. film ko fox star studios ne prastut kiya or mangalamurti films ke dwaara nirmit he. 
 uttari bharat ke dhul bhare ilaakon par aadhaarit film guddu rangila do bhaaiyon ke bare main ek kahani he. ye donon din main orcestra singrs ke rup main va raat main khabari ke rup main kaam karte hai jo shahar main sabase amir logon ke bare main jake local gundon ko bataate he or apana kamishan praapt karte hai. or is prakaar kamai karte rahete hai bina apane haath kaale kiye. sage bhai hote hue bhi ye donon ek duusare se bilkul alag hai. or yahi ye donon alag alag raah par chal padate hai or isko ye film bataati he.
