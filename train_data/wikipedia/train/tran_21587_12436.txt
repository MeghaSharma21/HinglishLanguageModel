 raikhik bijganit ganit kii ek shakha he jo sadish akaash tathaa un aakaashon ke bich raikhik pratichitran se sambandhit he.
 raikhik bijganit kaa armbh anekon agyaat raashiyon vaale yugapat samikarnon ke hal se hua. aise sameekaran praayah matrix or sadishon kaa upyog karke nirupit kiye jate hai. 
 shuddh ganit or anuprayukt ganit- donon main hi raikhik bijganit kii kendriya bhoomika he. calculas or raikhik ganit ke sammilit prayog se raikhik avakal sameekaran hal kiye jate hai. raikhik bijganit kii takneekein vaishleshik jyamiti, ejiniyari, bhautikee, prakritik vigyaan, sanganak vigyaan, computar anemation or saamaajik vigyaan main prayukt hoti hai. raikhik bijganit ke siddhanth or vidhiyaan atyant viksit hai. isi liye araikhik ganitiya modelon ko bhi kabhi-kabhi sannikat raikhik modelon se nirupit kar diya jaataa he jisase unhen hal karne main suvidha ho jaati he.
