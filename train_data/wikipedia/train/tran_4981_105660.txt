 ghaas-phoos se bani choti jhopadi. saadhuon ke rahane kaa sthaan.
 angareji shabd cottage ke arth main chota makaan jise dhanwaan log nagar ke baahar baag bageeche main, yaa greeshmakaal main parvatiy sthalon par, thode din ke vishram evam manoranjan ke hetu, banavaate hai. isaki rachana main saadhaaran makaanon ke nirmaan ke praaia: sabhi siddhant tathaa niyam lagu hote hai. antar keval itnaa hi he ki thode sthaan main sabhi suvidhaayen pradaan karne kaa prayaas kiya jaataa he. ejeeniyar athwa vaastuvid kii kushalta kaa yahi maapdand he. saadhaarant: iske kamare ausat makaan se chote hote hai or baramadon aadi kii vyavastha nahin hoti. baithak tathaa bhojan kaa sthaan ek hi kamare main hota he. thodi saajasja se hi kaam chal jaae, is aashay se aalmaariyaan or angithiyaan bhi praaia: deevaaron main bana di jaati he.
 kam vyay ke vichaar se kutiyaa ke nirmaan main kursii tathaa makaan kii uun chaai apekshaakrit kam rakhi jaati he. adhikansh kutiyaan ek manzil kii hi hone se bahudhaa chat bhi dhaalu, khapril, tin kii chadar athwa slate ityadi kii banaai jaati he.
 kutiyaa ke nirmaan main yah dhyaan rakhaa jaataa he ki kamare chote or aaraamdeh hon or uskaa nivasee bheetar baithe hi adhik se adhik prakritik drishy kaa avlokan kar sake.
