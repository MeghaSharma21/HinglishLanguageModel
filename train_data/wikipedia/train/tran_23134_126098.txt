 dabl kaa meetha ek lokapriy hyderaabadi vyanjan he jo mughlai khaane se he. ise ghar par bananaa bahut hi aasaan he or ise banane ke liye thodi hi saamagree chahie jaise ki bread, doodh, kriim or chini. isamen kesar or ilaychi kaa upyog bhi acha swaad or sugandh ke liye hua he. is racippi main bread ke tukdo ko kurkura hone tak ghi main tala jaataa he or phir unhen kesar or ilaychi vaali chaashni main dubokar upar se rabadi daali jaati he.
