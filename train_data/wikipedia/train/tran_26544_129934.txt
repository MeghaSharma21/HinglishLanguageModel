 nivesh yaa viniyog kaa saamaanya aashay aise vyayon se he jo utpaadan kshamta main vruddhi laayen. yah taatkaalik upbhog vyay yaa aise vyayon sambandhit nahin he jo utpaadan ke dauraan samaapt ho jaae. nivesh shabd kaa kai milte julate arthon main arthshaastra, vitt tathaa vyapaar-prabandhan aadi kshetron main prayog kiya jaataa he. yah pad bachat karne or upbhog main katautee yaa deree ke sandarbh main prayukt hota he. nivesh ke udhaaharan hai- kisi bank main puunji jamaa karnaa, yaa parisampatti kharidne jaise kaary jo bhavishya main laabh paane kii drushti se kiye jate hai. saamanytah ise kisi varsh main poonji stock main hone vaali vruddhi ke rup main paribhaashit karte hai. sanchit nivesh yaa viniyog hi poonji he.
