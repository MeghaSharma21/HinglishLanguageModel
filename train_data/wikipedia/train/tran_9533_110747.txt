 olympics lyon francisi uccharan: ; aamtaur par bas lyon, yaa ol ke rup main sanderbhit, lyon main sthit ek french footbal club he. yah france ke sarvocch footbal prabhag main kheltaa he, ligue 1. kai samarthakon or khel ke itihaaskaaron ke anusaar, club 1899 main lyon olympics universitaire ke rup main gathan kiya gaya tha, lekin rashtriya str par 1950 main ek club ke rup main sthapit kiya gaya tha. club ke sabase safal avadhi 21 veen sadi main he. club 2002 main apni pehali ligue 1 championships jiti or saat lagaataar khitaab kii ek rashtriya ricard tod stric shuru kiya.
 olympics lyon, lyon main 40,500 seat sted di gerland main apane gharelu match kheltaa he. club vartmaan lyon shahar ke upnagareey main ek nae stadium banane rahaa he. club ke rang, safed laal or nile hai. olympics lyon france main sabase lokapriy klbon main se ek he. desh kii aabaadi kaa 11% club kaa samarthan karte hai, is sankhya paris saint-germaine ke baraabar he or olympics di marsile ke peeche he.
