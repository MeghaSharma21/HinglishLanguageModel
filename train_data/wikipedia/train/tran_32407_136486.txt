 dire dawa ithiopia kaa doosraa sabase bada shahar he . ithiopia kii prashasnic pranali main dire dawa or addis ababa us desh ke do aupchaarik rup se namankit adhikrut nagar bhi hai. 
 dire dawa ithiopia ke purvi bhaag main dechaatu nadi ke kinare basaa hua he. yah nadi saal main kuch maheene sukhi hoti he lekin kabhi-kabhi isamen baadh aane se shahar main logon kii mrityu bhi ho chuki hai. dechaatu nadi nagar ko do bhaagon main baantati he jo ithiopia kii prashasnic pranali main do alag voradaon kaa darja rakhte hai. nadi ke purv or dakshin main 'megala' naamak purana shahar he or nadi ke uttar or pashchim main 'kezira' naamak nayaa shahar he.
 dire dawa vyapaarik or aarthik drushti se mahatvapurn he or desh kaa ekmaatr railmarg ise purv main sthit jibooti se jodta he. yah kabhi pashchim main rajdhani addis ababa tak jaaya karata tha lekin vahaan tak patari bichhi hone ke baavjood addis ababa se dire dawa ke raste par ab rail seva uplabdh nahin he. is shahar kii sthapana bhi 1902 main tab hui jab yahan rail pahaunchi. dire dawa ek audyogic kendra he or yahan kai mahatvapurn bazaar va mandiyaan hai. shahar se 40 kimi kii duuri par haramaya vishvavidhyalay he jo 1954 main sthpit hua tha or ithiopia ke sabase vishvavidhyalayon main se ek he.
