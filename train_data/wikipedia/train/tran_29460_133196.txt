 kavi, kathakaar or nibandhkaar alexandar ilichevski kaa janm 1970 main azarbaijan ke sumgit shahar main hua. 1987 main unhone masco rajkeey vishvavidhyalay ke antargat karyrat a. kalmagoraf bhautik-ganit vidhyalay kii shiksha samaapt karke masco ke bhautikee-takneekee santhaan ke saamaanya va anuprayukt bhautikee sankaay main pravesh le liya. saidhantik bhautikee main visheshgyata ke saath alexandar ilachevski ne 1993 main em.essi. kiya. iske baad 1991 se 1999 tak ve isroyal or california main vaigyanik rahe.
 anek saahitiyik patrikaon or internet patrikaon main alexandar ilichevsaki kii rachanaayen lagaataar prakashit hoti rahi hai. unke ab tak prakashit kavita-sangrahon main 'ghatana', 'adrushti', 'volga, tamba or sheesha' pramukh hai. kathakritiyon main 'aay-petri', 'clen kii bottle', 'maatis', 'gushmullah' or 'chunaa pathar kaa gayan' ullekhneey kritiyaan hai. alexandar ilichevski ne hi lokapriy vaigyanik internet-portal - 'ju taknolaujee' kii bhi pariklpana kii he.
 alexandar ilichevski ko ab tak anek puraskaar mil chuke hai jinamein patrika 'novi mir' kaa puraskaar, shreshth kahani ke liye euri kazakf puraskaar, upanyaas 'maatis' ke liye roosi bukar puraskaar aadi pramukh hai. ilachevski kaa naam san 2006 main bunin puraskaar ke liye antim suchi main shaamil tha or 2006 main hi unka 'aay-petri' naamak kahani-sangrah 'balshaya kniga' naamak rashtriya saahitiyik puraskaar kii daud main ant tak shaamil rahaa. alexandar ilichevski masco main rahete hai.
