 {{Infobox file format| name = em.pi-4 | icon = | logo =| extension = .mp4| mime = video/mp4, audio/mp4, application/mp4| type code = mpg4 | uniform type = | magic = | owner = ISO| genre = media container| container for = audio, video, paath | contained by = | extended from = QuickTime .mov evam empiiji-4 part 12| extended to = | standard = ISO/IEC 14496-14}}em.pi.i.ji 4 ISO/IEC 14496-14:2003, digital dhwani sanket sampeedan takanic hoti he. yah main empi3 se kuch zyada alag nahin he, lekin empi3 kii tulana main yah zyada jatil hota he. yadi saat baar di bolnaa he, to audio formet main ise di7 ke rup main likh diya jaataa he. isse audio sanchika sampeedit ho jaati he. audio sanchika kii tulana main video sanchika ko compress karnaa mushkil kaam hota he. isamen vishesh prakaar ke code tantra kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he. compressant or diconpressant ke jariye chitron ko frequency domen main sthanantarit kiya jaataa he. ise kodak kaha jaataa he. empi4 player saadhaaran tareeke se kaam karata he.
 empi4 kaa sabase bada phayda he isaki bhandaran kshamta. udhaaharan ke taur par ek puri hindi film kam se kam do sidi main aati he. yadi film kii sanchika ko empi4 main badla jaae, to ek empi4 main kam se kam chaar-paanch film aa sakati he. kai maamalon main jahaan empi4 takanic laabhadaayak he, to is takanic ke kai nuksaan bhi hai. empi 4 kii vajah se piresi kii samasya badi he. in sanchikaon ko downlod karne main samay kaaphi lagata he. saath hi inke bich-bich main rukne kii samasya bhi aati he.
 jaise empithri sanchika kisi mujeec yaa audio sanchika kii gunvattta ko prabhavit kiye bina sampeedit kar banaai jaati hai. thik usee tarah empi4 video sanchika ko sampeedit kar banaai jaati he. yah nai takanic video sanchika ko itnaa gata detii he ki unhen store karnaa behad aasaan ho jaataa he. audio-video ke liye banane vaali baari-bharkam sanchika sampeedit hokar empi4 ke rup main aati he.
