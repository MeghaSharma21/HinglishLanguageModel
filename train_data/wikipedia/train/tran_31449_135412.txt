 maciye vastuen un khagoleey vastuon ko kaha jaataa he jinhen francisi khagolashastri sharl maciye ne san 1771 main prakashit apni suchi main sammilit kiya tha. sharl maciye dhoomaketuon kaa adhyayan karte the or aisi vastuon se pareshaan the jo dhoomaketu nahin thi lekin dhoomaketuon jaisi lagati thim. unhone apane sahaayak pyer meshai kii sahaayta se aisi vastuon kii suchi banaai.
 angrezi main "maciye vastuon" ko "maciye obocts" kaha jaataa he. francisi or angrezi main "sharl maciye" kaa naam "Charles Messier" likhaa jaataa he lekin sahi uccharan "sharl maciye" hi he.
