 shimla se lagbhag 84 kilometre dur, shimla-rohdu maarg par pabbar nadi ke daahine kinare par dhan ke kheton ke bich mata hatakoti ke prasiddh pracheen mandir ke liye janaa jaataa he. 
 lagbhag 1370 meter kii unchai par basaa pabbar nadi ke kinare hatakoti main mahisasur rmadini kaa puraatan mandir he. jisamen vaastukala, shilpakala ke utkrishth namoonon ke saakshaat darshan hote hai. kehete hai ki yah mandir 10veen shatabdi ke aas-paas bana he. isamen mahisasur rmadini kii do meter oonchi kaansya kii pratimaa he torana sahit he. iske saath hi shiv mandir he jahaan pathar par bana pracheen shivlinga he. dwaar ko kalatmak pattharon se susajjit kiya gaya he. chat lakdi se rnimit he, jis par devi devataaon kii anukritiyon banaai gai hai. mandir ke garbhagrih main lakshmi, vishnu, durga, ganesh aadi kii pratimaayein hai. iske atirikt yahaan mandir ke praangan main devataaon kii choti-choti murtiyon hai. bataaya jaataa he ki inkaa nirmaan pandavon ne karavaaya tha.
