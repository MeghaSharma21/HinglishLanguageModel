 sarpadhari yaa ofiukas taramandal prithvi ke uttari bhaag se nazar aane vaala ek bada taramandal he. duusari shatabdi isvi main tolmi ne jin 48 taramandalon kii suchi banaai thi yah unamem se ek he or antarrashtriya khagoleey sangh dwaara jari kii gai 88 taramandalon kii suchi main bhi yah shaamil he. akaash main yah sarp taramandal ke do hisson - "sarp sir" or "sarp dum" - ke bich main sthit hota he. puraani khagolashastriy pustakon main ise aksar ek sarp ko bich se uthaate hue ek aadmi kii tarah darshaya jaataa tha.
 sarpadhari taramandal main das mukhya taare hai. inamen se sab se zyaada chamkile taare alfaa ofiuki tara or eta ofiuki hai. barnrd kaa tara, jo hamaare saura mandal ke kaaphi nikat he, isi taramandal main sthit he.
