 nebraska sanyukt rajya kaa rajya he jo great plance or madhya-pashchimi kshetra main sthit he. rajya ke uttar main south dekota, purv main iova or misori nadi ke kinare par dakshin-purv main mizori he. saath hi isaki seemaa dakshin main kensas, dakshin pashchim main colorado or pashchim main vioming se bhi lagati he. 
 1762 main saptavarshiya yuddh ke dauraan france ne louiciana kshetra spain ko saunp diya. is kshetra se france kii vaapasi ke baad mississippi nadi par britain or spain prabhutv ke liye pratispardhaa karne lage. apni swatantraaa ke baad sanyukt rajya america ne louiciana kharid ke anusaar is ilaake par daawa karnaa shuru kar diya. 1854 main america ne ek adhinium ke tahat is ilaake par apana adhikaar kar liya. phir sangheey sarkaar dwaara di ja rahi ni: shulk bhoomi par daawa karne ke liye kai hajaaron aabaadakaar nebraska main pahunchein. basane kii is pehali lahar ne kshetra ko rajya banane ke aavedan karne ke liye paryaapt aabaadi di. 1867 main yah 37wein rajya ban gaya. 
 2016 ke anumaan ke mutabik isaki aabaadi 19,07,116 he. jisase iskaa sabhi rajyon main 37waan sthaan hua. kshetra ke hisaab se ye 16waan sabase bada rajya he. angrezi sarkari bhashaa he.
