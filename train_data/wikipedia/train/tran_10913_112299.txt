 nanakshahi jantari yaa nanakshahi panchang ek saura panchang he jise shiromani gurudwara prabandhak cameti ne sikh dharm se sambandhit ghatnaon kii tithiyaan darshane ke liye sweekaar kiya tha. iskaa upyog 1998 se ho rahaa he. iske pehle sikhon ke parvon ke liye shaq panchang hi upyog kiya jaataa tha. ise paal singh pureval ne dizin kiya tha.
 iskaa armbh guru nanak dev ke janmdin se kiya gaya tha. is jantari ke anusaar nav varsh kaa pratham din 14 march ko padataa he.
