 yadi kisi rin yaa anya vittiya utpaada par chakrabruddhi byaaj ek varsh ke bajaay kisi anya avadhi main joda jaataa he, to prabhavi byaaj dar vah raashi he jo ek varsh main prati saikadaa judati he. udhaaharan ke liye, kisi jamaa raashi par 1% masik chakrabruddhi byaaj miltaa he to 1 varsh main 12.683% pratishat byaaj milegaa. atah is sthiti main prabhavi byaaj dar 12.683% vaarshik he, na ki 1x12=12% vaarshik.
