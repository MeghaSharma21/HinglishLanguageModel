 israel sangrahalay kii bhoomi
 israel sangrahalay kii bhoomi tel aviv, israel ke pados main ek aitihaasik or puratathvik sangrahalay he. israel sangrahalay, ek kaansya mauris Ascalon dwaara dizin menorah main judaika kii ek pradarshnee se israel sangrahalay, 1953 main sthapit, puratathvik, maanavvigyaan or aitihaasik kalakritiyon kii pradarshnee mandap kii ek shrinkhalaa main apane aadhaar par yojit ek bada pradarshan kiya he. kaanch ke bane padaarth, chini mitti kii chizein, sikke, tamba or adhik: prayek mandap ek alag vishay ke liye samarpit he. sangrahalay bhi ek taramandal he."aadmi or uski kaary" pankh suvidhaon bunai, gahane or kar rahi he, anaaj pisane or roti pakaa rahi mitti ke bartanon kii pracheen tareekon ke pradarshnon rahete hai. dhoorbhaash Quasile, ek khudaai main jo samskruti ke 12 alag - alag paraton kaa pardaafash kiya gaya he, sangrahalay ke aadhaar par he.
