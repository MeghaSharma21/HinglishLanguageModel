 bharattiya videsh vyayapar santhaan ) kii sthapana bharat sarkaar dwaara 1963 main ek swashaasi sanstha ke rup main kii gai thi. isaki sthapana ke peeche videsh vyapaar prabandhan ko vyavsaayik rup dene kaa udddeshy tha. iske alaava maanviya sansaadhaanon ke vikaas, aankado ke sankalan, vishleshan va vitaran va anusandhan ke madhyam se niryaat ko badhaava dena tha. santhaan apni bhoomika nimna rup main drushtigochar karata he-
 ift gat varshon ke dauraan dabblutio, vishwav bank, ankatad, vaanijya va udyog mantralay, bharat sarkaar jaise sanghathanon ke saath nae-nae anusandhan adhyayan sampann kiye hai. santhaan dwaara apane prabandhan vikaas kaaryakramon ke madhyam se 30 deshon main 40,000 se adhik vyavsayikkon ko international bijness or vyapaar niti ke kshetra main prashikshan pradaan kiya gaya he.
