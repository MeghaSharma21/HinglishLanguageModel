 bhim main ek bhartiya raja jo aaj ke gujarat ke kuch hisson par shashan kiya tha. unhone Chaulukya vansh ke ek rajput raja the. 1022-1064 sii purvavarti Durlabharaja uttaradhikari karn pati Udayamati ank Mularaja, kshahemraaja or karn rajvansh Chaulukya pita nagaraj bhim main bharat bhim IBhima IBhima main sthit he main bhim ke shasankaal ke dauraan jari kiye gaye shilaalekh ke dhabbe kaa pataa lagaaein praarambhik varshon bhim ke shasankaal ke gazanvi samrajya ke shaasha mahmud ne somnath mandir ko barkhaast kar diya se ek akraman ko dekha. bhim ne apni rajdhani ko chhod diya or is akraman ke dauraan Kanthkot main sharan le lee. mahmud ke chale jane ke baad, vah apni shakti baraamad kiye gaye or sabhi pradeshon vah viraasat main mila tha banaae rakhaa. unhone Arbuda main apane jagirdaar dwaara ek ne vidroh ko kuchalane, or asafal Naddula Chahamana rajya par akraman karne kii koshish kii. unke shasankaal ke ant main, vah kalchuri raja lakshmi-karn ke saath gathbandhan kiya he, or parmaar raja bhoj ke patan main ek mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai. dilwara mandir or Modhera surya mandir kaa jald se jald bhim ke shasankaal ke dauraan banaaya gaya tha. rani kii vaav kaa nirmaan kaary bhi apni rani Udayamati ko jimmedaar tehraaya he.
