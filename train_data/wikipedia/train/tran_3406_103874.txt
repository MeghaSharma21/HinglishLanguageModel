 prakshepit kar upyog main laayaa jane vaala astr. iskaa prayog dur sthit lakshya ko bedhane ke liye kiya jaataa he. isaki sahaayta se visphotakon ko hajaaron kilometre dur ke lakshya tak pahunchaaya ja saktaa he. is prakaar sudur sthit dushman ke thikaane bhi kuch hi samay main nasht kiye ja sakate hai. prakshepastra rasinic visphotakon se lekar parmaanu bam tak kaa vahan or prayog kar saktaa he. 
 gati niyantran ke aadhaar par prakshepastra do prakaar kaa hota he. 
 maarak kshamta ke aadhaar par prakshepastra chaar prakaar ke hote hai. 
 isaki maarak kshamta kuch sau kilometre se hajaar kilometre tak hoti he.
 isaki maarak kshamta ek hajaar se teen hajaar kilometretak kii hoti he.
 isaki kshamta teen se saadhe paanch hajaar keelometre tak kii hoti he. 
 isaki kshamta saadhe paanch hajaar kilometre se bhi adhik kaa hota he.
 upyog paddati ke aadhaar par prakshepastron ko nimna virgon main baanta jaataa he.
