 hariyaanvi cinema uttar bharat ke rajya haryana kaa cinema he jo hariyaanvi bhashaa main filmon ko prastut karata he.
 hariyaanvi bhashaa kii sarvapratham film ke bare main adhikarik rup se spasht nahin ho paaya he. kai chalchitr visheshgyon ke alag alag mat hai koi pratham hariyaanvi film dharti bataata he to kuch ke anusaar harphul jat jhulani vaala tathaa kuch veeraa shera ko hariyaanvi kii pratham film bataate hai. parantu hariyaanvi kii pehali sabase kamai karne vaali sabase badi hit film 1984 main release hui chandraval thi. iske baad hariyaanvi filmon kii barh si aa gai. jat, lado basanti, gulabo, lumbardaar, phaguna ayu re, jat haryane kaa, jatni jaisi kai filmein release huin. aaj hariyaanvi cinema ek nae daur main he.
