 keky ek pracheen rajya tha, jo avibhajit punjab se uttar pashchim disha main gandha or vyas nadi ke basaa tha. yahaan ke adhikansh nivasee keky janapad ke kshatreey the. atah kaikeyas kehalaate the. kaikey log praayah madra desh, ushinar desh yaa shivi pradesh ke logon ke sambandh main rahete the or sabhi sanyukt rup se vahika desh main aate the. ye varnan panini ke anusaar he. keky desh kaa ullekh ramayana main bhi aata he. raja dasharath kii sabase choti rani kaikeyi or uski daasi manthara keky desh kii hi thi. 
 bahut se puraanon main kaikey vaasiyon ko gandharva, yavan, shaq, parad, bahli, kamboj, dardaas, barbar, chini, tushaar, pahalav aadi kii ginati main joda gaya he. inhen idichy ke log kaha gaya he. udichy yani uttarapath kii uttari mandal.. kekaiy ne vartmaan jhelum, shahpur or gujarat ke kshetron main nivas kiya tha.
 keky punjab main gandhar kaa purvavarti pradesh arthath aajkal ke rawalpindi peshawar ke aasapaas ke pradesh kaa pracheen naam. ikshvakuvanshi raja dasharath kii rani kaikeyi yaheen kii rajakanya thim. keky rajya kii rajdhani rajgrih thi. is rajgrih kaa sameekaran aadhunik jalalpur se kiya jaataa he. ramayana main is nagar kaa ek doosraa naam 'giribraj' kaha gaya he. upanishdon main is pradesh ke vikhyath shaasha ashwapati kaa ullekh miltaa he. panini kii ashtaadhi main ise rajaadhishthit janapad kaha gaya he. mahabharat ke samay yahan dhristaketu naamak raja rajya karata tha. brahmad puraana ke anusaar keky nivasee anarya the kintu jain sahitya main unhen arya kaha gaya he.
