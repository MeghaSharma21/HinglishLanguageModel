 jyotish singh ke mat se likhit dravyagun ayurveda kaa mul vishay he. iske antargat aushadhiy padpon - unki pahchaan, gun tathaa upachaaratmak upyogon kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. ise ayurveda kaa 'materia medica' kah sakate hai. is vigyaan main bheshajagun vigyaan, bheshaj-abhigyaan tathaa padpon ke chikitsiya upyog shaamil he. isaki aath shakhaaen hai.
 'dravyagun' do shabdon se milakar bana he - 'dravya' tathaa 'gun' . dravya ke antargat jivit or nirjeev dono vastuen aa jaati hai.
 charak kaa kahana he ki kuch bhi aisa nahin he jo 'aushadhi' na ho. ayurveda kaa mat he ki kisi aushadhi kaa prabhaav uske kisi ek ghatak ke akele ke prabhaav se praayah bhinn hota he. ayurveda main vanaspatiyon ke gunagunon ko paanch bhaagon main bhanta gaya he- ras, gun, veery, vipaak or shakti . aushadhiyon ke kaary or prabhaav ko jaanane ke liye unke ras, gun, veery, vipaak, or shakti kaa gyaan ati aavashyak he.
 ye bhi kaha gaya he ki-
 ayurveda main 600 se bhi adhik aushadhiy padpon ko aushadh ke rup main upyog main laayaa jaataa he. inhen akele yaa dusron saath milakar rogon se mukti paaya jaataa he. aushadhiy padap alag-alag tarah ke krishi-jalwaayiya kshetron main paidaa hote hai. vartmaan samay main aushadhiy padpon ko 'prakritik aushadh' ke rup main prayog karne kaa chalan badha he. is kaaran is vishay kaa mahatv or bhi bad gaya he.
