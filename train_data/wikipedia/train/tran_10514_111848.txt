 sita mata vanyajeev abhayaarany ek vanyajeev abhayaarany he jo dakshin purvi bhaag main sthit pratapgarh jila ,rajasthan ,bharat main sthit he. 2 navambar 1979 ko rajasthan adhisuchana sankhya epa sarkaar dwaara ek sangrakshit van kshetra ke rup main ghoshit kar diya gaya. yahan bahut ghaney jangal he is vikasshil zile kii kul bhoomi kshetra lagbhag 40% he, jo 422.95 varg kilometre he. yah pratapgarh zile kaa mukhya aakarshan he. yahan kii bhoomi kyonki teen alag alag sanrachanaon ke sangam kii lahardaar he - malwa pathaar ,vindhyachal hills or aravali shrikhla.
