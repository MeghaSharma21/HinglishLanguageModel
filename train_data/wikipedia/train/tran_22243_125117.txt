 maidanarting bhartiya rajya ke meghalaya main purvi khaasi hills jile main ek janaganana shahar he. 
 bharat kii 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar, madanarting kii jansankhya 16,700 thi. jisamen purushon kaa bhaag 51% or mahilaon kii jansankhya 49% thi. yahaan kii ausat sakshartaa dar 76% he jo rashtriya ausat 59,5% kii tulana main kahin adhik he. isamen se purush sakshartaa 80% he, or mahila sakshartaa 72% he . maidanarting main 13% jansankhya 6 varsh se kam umr kii he.
