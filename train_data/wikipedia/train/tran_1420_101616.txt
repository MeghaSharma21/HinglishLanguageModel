 faraah afghanistan kaa ek praant he jo us desh ke pashchimi bhaag main sthit he. is praant kaa kshetrafal 48,471 varg kimi he or isaki aabaadi san 2006 main lagbhag 9. 3 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. yah iran kii sarhad se laga hua bahut hi halaki aabaadi vaala ilaaka he. is praant kii rajdhani faraah naam kaa hi shahar he. is praant main basane vaale adhiktar log pashtun hai. faraah main bahut se pracheen kilon ke khandhhar milte hai. inamen ek mitti kii eenton kaa bana qila jiskaa naam 'kaafir qalaa' prasiddh he, jiske bare main kaha jaataa he ki ise sikandar mahaan ne banaaya tha.
