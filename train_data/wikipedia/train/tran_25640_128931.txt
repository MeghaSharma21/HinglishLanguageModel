 bunai vastra bunai kii vah vidhi he jisamen taana evam bana naamak do bhinn tathaa paraspar lambavat dhaagon kaa samooh ko aapas main goonthkar vastra banaaya jaataa he. bunai ke alaava bhi kai vidhiyon se vastra banaaye jate hai, jaise- niting, les bananaa, felting, tathaa breading yaa plating . taana vah he jo lambaai kii disha main lagata he tathaa bana vah he jo chaudhaai kii disha main lagata he. taana or bana ko vibhinn tareekon se paraspar guntha ja saktaa he. vastra ke gundharm isse hi nirdhaarit hote hai ki taana or bana ko kis prakaar paraspar buna gaya he.
 vastra praayah kargha par buna jaataa he. kargha taanon ko apni jagah par banaae rakhata he or baanon ko unamem se hokar buna jaataa he. taana or bana ke upyog se bunai kaa kaam kargha ke atirikt duusari vidhiyon dwaara bhi kiya ja saktaa he, jaise- mej par bunai, back-strap, aadi.
 bunai se nirmit adhikansh vastra mukhyatya teen prakaar kii bunai main se kisi ek vidhi se banate hai-sada bunai, satin bunai tathaa twil .
