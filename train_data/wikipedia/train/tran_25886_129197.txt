 rajpath ko 1947 purv kings ve kaha jaataa tha| yah pashchim main raashtrapati bhawan se vijay chowk hokar purv main india get hokar dhyanchand rashtriya stadium (neshnal stadium tak jaataa he| iske donon ore ghaas lake sundar maidan aachhadit hai va ek ek jhil saath saath chalati he, jo ki isaki sundarata main chaar chaand lagaati he. yahan maarg pashchim main raisina kii pahari par chadh kar bharat ke raashtrapati ke awaas raashtrapati bhawan jaati he, jiske dono ore prashasnic kendra yaa sachivalay uttari khand north block va dakshini khand south block hai. 
 north block va south block ko kendriya sachivalay bhi kehete hai. north block main vitt mantralay va grih mantralay ke karyaalay hai. south block main videsh mantralay va raksha mantralay ke karyaalay hai. inke saath hi kuch anya karyaalay bhi sthapit hai.
 
 yahan rajpath kaa pratham chowk he. yahan beating of retreat samaaroh 29 janvari ko prayek varsh yojit hota he.
 yahan rajpath kaa va dilli kaa sabase bada va pramukh chauraahaa he.
