 martin shongavar germany kaa nakkashikar tathaa chitrakaar tha. uske banaaye chitra tathaa uski khyaati dur-dur tak pahaunchi thi. italy main vah 'bel martino' tathaa martino di anversa ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. 
 martin kaa pita sunar tha, par apane putr ko kala kii disha main prerit karne main usne vishesh utsaah dikhaaya. kolmaar ke ek bade hi mashhoor 'ingraving skool' main uskaa daakhila kara diya gaya, jahaan se kitne hi vishisht kalakaar banakar nikal chuke the. flanders ke samakaleen kalakaaron, visheshkar rogar vaan der weden kii kalatechnic or chitranmaili kaa uske krititv par vishesh prabhaav padaa. kolmaar kii saint martin carch kii bhavy vedika par 'virgin or balak christ' kii lahalhaae khile pushpon ke madhya ek badi hi bhavy akruti usne ankit kii. chitrakala se adhik vah nakkashi main daksh tha. usne anek aisi sundar kalakritiyaan prastut kin jinaki na sirf germany main balki italy, englad, france or spain main bhi khub bikri hui. uske vishay hameshaa dharmik or bhavanaparak hote the. taanbe par uske 113 chitrankan uplabdh hai jinpar uskaa naam bhi khuda he. mrityu tathaa isa-maan ke rajarohan sambandhi chitramala ke atirikt manoragon ke nidarshan main rekhaon kii susanyojna, pratipaadya vishay ko sookshmata se aankane tathaa saghan evam sundar akritiyon ke nirmaan main uski vishesh moulikta drushtigat hoti he.
