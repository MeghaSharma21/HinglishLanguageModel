 dakshin asia main urs, aamtaur par kisi sufi sant kii punyatithi par uski dargah par vaarshik rup se yojit kiye jane vaale utsav ko kehete hai. dakshin asian sufi sant mukhya rup se chishtiya kahe jate hai or unhen parameshwar kaa premi samajha jaataa he. kisi sufi sant kii mrityu ko visaal kaha jaataa he jiskaa arth premiyon kaa milaap hota he or unki barsi ko shaadi kii saalagirah ke rup main manaaya jaataa he.
 urs ke anushthanon ko amuman dargah ke sangrakshakon dwaara nibhaayaa jaataa he or inamen aamtaur par dharmik sangeet jaise ki qawwali kaa gayan shaamil hota he. ajmer main dargah sharif main moinuddin chishti ke urs par har saal 400000 se adhik shraddhalu shirkat karte hai.
