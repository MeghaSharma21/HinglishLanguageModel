 nirdeshaank: 31°34′56″N 74°19′35″E / 31.5822°N 74.3264°E / 31.5822; 74.3264 
 dilli darwaja puraane diwaar se ghire pakistan ke lahore shahar ke chah shesh daravaajon main se ek pravesh dwaar he. dilli daravaaje evam nearesth shahi hammam kaa punaroddhar 2015 main aagaa khan cultural sarvice pakistan dwaara karavaaya gaya he.
 dilli daravaaje kaa nirmaan mughal kaal main hua tha evam yah puraane lahore shahar ke 13 main se shesh bache chah praveshdwaron main se ek he. is daravaaje kaa naam dilli par rakhaa gaya he, kyonki yah purvamukhi hone ke kaaran dilli shahar kii ore rukh karata he. mughal kaal main yah dwaar lahore shahar ke mukhya praveshdwar ke rup main prayog kiya jaataa tha. iske dwaar prayek din sany band kar diye jate the. is parisar se lagi 17veen shatabdi kii kai aitihaasik imaaraten hai jaise vazeer khan masjid, shahi hammam evam haweliyaan.
