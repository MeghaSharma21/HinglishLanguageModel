 
 gadhaa ashv parivaar kaa pashu he. kai deshon main ise bojha dhone ke kaam laayaa jaataa he. gadhe ko adhiktar dhobi or kumhaar varg ke logon dwaara paala jaataa he.yah jeev gajab kaa sahan sheel  praani he.iske chehre kaa bhav ek sa bana rahata he. hryrrsh visaad donon main ek sa dikhaai detaa he.
 gadhaa ghode kii prajati kii ek upjati 'acins varg' kaa pashu he. is varg ke anek pashu hai par 'gadhe' se abhipraay is varg ke us pashu se samajha jaataa he jise log paalate hai or saamaan dhone kaa kaam lete he. yah aakaar main ghode se chota hota he, kaan lambey hote hai, punch kaa aakaar or rang ghode se sarvatha bhinn hota he. yah pashu apni mand buddhi or hathilepan ke liye prakhyat he. bharatvarsh main iskaa pracheenatam ullekh vaidic sahitya main miltaa he . aajkal iskaa prayog mukhyat: dhobiyon dwaara kapadon ko ghat se laane or le jane ke liye hota he.
 kuch samskritiyon main kisi murkh vyakti ko bhi gadhaa kaha jaataa he. gadhe ke bare main bahut kahavatein bhi prachlit hai, jaise : "dhobi kaa gadhaa, na ghar kaa, na ghat kaa", "gadhe ke sir se sing kii tarah gaayab hona". gadhe kii dulatti prasiddh he.
