 shikohabad shahar kii sthapana mughal shahjade daara shikoh ne kii thi. ye prarambh me mainpuri jile kii tehsil  thi . tatkaaleen mukhyamantri narayan dutt tiwari ne ise 5 farvari 1989 ko navnirmit jile firozabad main ise shaamil kar diya.
 shikohabad shahar uttar pradesh rajya me avasthit he. shikohabad shahar ke madhya me sirsa nadi bahati he. shikohabad shahar jila mukhyalay se 12 kilometre duuri par sthit he. agra shahar se 62 kilometre dur sthit he.
 shikohabad shahar rashtriya rajmarg -2 par sthit he. shikohabad depo se bas kisi bhi shahar ke liye mil jaati he. shikohabad se mainpuri or eta ke liye bas bhi chalati he.
 shikohabad jankshan railway stesan se agra, farukhabad, dilli, kolkata, meerut, elahabaad shahar ke liye railgadee dwaara pahunchaa ja saktaa he.
 shikohabad shahar me chaar pramukh mahavidhyalay hai:
 is shahar me 6 pramukh inter kalege he. lagbhag 50 se adhik english medium skool hai.
 shikohabad ko shiksha nagari ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. is shahar main purv mukhyamantri mulaayam singh yadav ne shiksha praapt kii. yahan mainpuri, eta or firozabad ke vidhyaarthee shiksha praapt karte hai . shikohabad shahar main ek nijee vishvavidhyalay bhi he.
 yah shahar kaanch udyog ke liye prasiddh tha. yahan kaa udyog kharaab sthiti main he. industrial park kii sthapana sarkari daftaron or kaagaz main hi hui he or vastav main nahin he.
