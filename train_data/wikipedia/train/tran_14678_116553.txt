 falcon haivi aanshik rup se pun: prayojya haivi lift launch vaahan he jise spesex dwaara dizine or nirmit kiya gaya he. yah falcon 9 ke aadhaar par banaaya gaya he. isaki prithvi kii nichali kaksha me bhaar le jane kii kshamta 63,800 kilogram hai. falcon haivi saturn 5, energy or en1 ke baad duniya kaa chauthaa sabase badi kshamta vaala rocket he or 2018 main sanchalit sabase shaktishaalee rocket banaaya gaya tha.
 spesex ne 6 farvari, 2018 ko 3:45 baje iesti par falcon heavi kaa pehla prakshepan kiya. rocket spesex ke sansthaapak alan musk kii ek testla rodster car ko ek dami pelod ke rup main lekar gaya tha..
 falcon haivi ko manushyon ko antariksh main le jane ke liye dizin kiya gaya tha jisamen chandramaa or mangal aadi ke mishan shaamil the. haalanki farvari 2018 tak, yah pramanit nahin he ki chaalak dal ke mishan ke liye iskaa istemaal kiya jayega yaa nahin. iske bajaay yah rocket bade upgrahon yaa antariksh yano ko launch karne ke liye samarpit kiya jayega.
 spesex ne agle falcon haivi launch kii ummid pehali safal udaan ke baad teen se chah maheene baad kii hai.
