 malti bedekar  ek bhartiya marathi lekhika thim. vah marathi sahitya main pehali pramukh nariwadi lekhika ke rup main jaani jaati hai. balutai khare unke maayke kaa naam tha. ve anantrao or indirabai khare kii beti thi. unki 1938 main vishram bedekar se mulaakaat hui or shaadi bhi, tatpashchaat unhone apana naam malti vishram bedekar rakh liya.
