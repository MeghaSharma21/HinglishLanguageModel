 dilli kaa sabase purana jain mandir laal kila or chandani chowk ke saamne sthit he. iskaa nirmaan 1526 main hua tha. vartmaan main isaki imaarat laal pattharon kii bani he. isliye yah laal mandir ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. yahaan kai mandir hai lekin sabase pramukh mandir bhagwaan mahavir kaa he jo jain dharm ke 24wein tirthankar the. yahaan jain dharm ke pratham tirthankar bhagwaan adinath kii pratimaa bhi sthapit he. jain dharm ke anuyaayiyon ke bich yah sthaan bahut lokapriy he. yahaan kaa shant vaataavaran logon kaa apni ore khinchataa he.
 mughal samrajya main mandiron ke shikhar banane kii anumati nahin thi. isliye is mandir kaa koi aupchaarik shikhar nahin tha. baad main swatantraaa prapti uparaant is mandir kaa punaroddhar hua.
