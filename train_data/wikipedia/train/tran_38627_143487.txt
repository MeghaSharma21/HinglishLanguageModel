 sulayman parvat yaa koh-e-sulayman, jinhen kesai parvat bhi kaha jaataa he, dakshinpurvi afghanistan or pashchimi pakistan ke balochistan praant ke uttar bhaag main sthit ek pramukh parvat shrunkhlaa he. afghanistan main yah zabul, loya pakatiya or kandahaar kshetron main vistrut hai. sulayaman parvat iran ke pathaar kaa purvi chor hai or bhugolic rup se use bhartiya upmahadweep se vibhajit karte hai. is shrunkhlaa ke sabase prasiddh shikhar balochistan main sthit 3,487 meter unchaa takhtâ€‹-e-sulayman, 3,444 meter unchaa kesai gar or queta ke paas sthit 3,578 meter unchaa zarathun gar hai.
 sulayman parvaton se uttar main hindu kush ke unche or shushk ilaake hai jahaan adhiktar zamin 2,000 meter se adhik oonchaai par he. in parvaton or is pathaar se hind mahaasaagar se aane vaali naam hawaayen yaheen tak pahunch paati hai or is se aage madhya or dakshini afghanistan kaa ilaaka kushk he. iske vipareet sulayman parvaton se purv or dakshin kaa ilaake main sindhu nadi kaa nadimukh he jahaan aksar baadhen aati hai or har jagah junglee jhaad-ghaas faili hui he. sulayman shrunkhlaa se kshetra kii do mukhya nadiyaan nikalati hai - dori nadi or gomel nadi.
 kripaya dhyaan den ki pashto main 'gar' kaa matlab 'parvat' hota he, jisamen bindu vaale 'ga' kaa uccharan bindu-rahit 'ga' se zaraa alag he or 'garib' or 'gaayab' jaisaa kiya jaataa he. isliye 'zarathun gar' kaa matlab 'zarathun parvat' he. 'gar' kaa bahuwachi 'garuna' he or 'sulayman parvatamala' ko pashto main 'da sulayman garuna' kehete hai.
