 jyamiti main kisi bahubhuj kaa parivritt aisa vrutt hota he jo us bahubhuj ke har sheersh se guzarataa ho. is vrutt ke kendra ko parikendra or trigya ko paritrijya kehete hai. prayek bahubhuj aisa nahin hota ki uske liye ek parigat vrutt banaaya ja sake or jiske liye parigat vrutt sambhav hota he use vrittiya bahubhuj kaha jaataa he. saare sam-saral bahubhuj, tribhuj or aayat vrittiya hote hai.
