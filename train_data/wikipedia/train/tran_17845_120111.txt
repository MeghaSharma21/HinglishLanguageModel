 paanch rajvansh or das rajshahiyaan china ke itihaas main san 907 isvi se 979 isvi tak chalane vaala ek daur tha. yah tang rajvansh ke patan ke baad shuru hua or song rajvansh ke ubharne par khatm hua. is kaal main china ke uttar main ek-ke-baad-ek paanch rajvansh satta main aaye. china bhar main or khaaskar dakshini china main, 12 se adhik swatantra rajya sthapit ho gaye. inamen se itihaas main 10 rajyon kaa varnan adhik hota he, isliye yah kaal 'paanch rajvansh or das rajshahiyon' ke naam se janaa jaataa he. isi kaal main manchuriya-mongolia kshetra main kitaani logon kaa liyaao rajvansh bhi sthapit hua.
 uttari china main raaj karne vaale paanch rajvansh or unke satta kaal yah the:
 das rajshahiyaan is prakaar thim: vu, min, chu, dakshini haan, purvakaleen shu, uttarkalin shu, jingnan, dakshini tang or uttari haan .
 tang samrajya ke antim dinon main shahi darbar ne jiedushi  naamak kshetreey sainik rajyapalon ke adhikaar badha die. isi dauraan huang chaao naamak ek netaa ne tang sarkaar ke viruddh vidroh yojit kiya jis se samrajya bahut hi kamazor pad gaya or rajyapal lagbhag puri tarah azaad ho gaye. isi se paanch rajwanshon or das rajshahiyon kaa kaal shuru hua. is kaal main uttari china main bahut bhayankar jhagade jari rahe. dakshini china main zyaada sthirataa thi lekin vahaan bhi yuddh hote rahe. san 960 main uttari song rajvansh sthapit hua or usne china ko phir se ek sutara main bandhane kii thaanee. ek-ek karke usne rajyon par qabza jamaaya or san 978 tak puure china ko apane adheen kar liya.
