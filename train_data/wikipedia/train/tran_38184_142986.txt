 
 raagi yaa madua africa or asia ke sookhe kshetron main ugaaya jane vaala ek motaa ann he. yah ek varsh main pak kar taiyaar ho jaataa he. yah mul rup se ithiopia ke ucch ilaakon kaa paudha he jise bharat main koi chaar hajaar saal pehle laayaa gaya. unche ilaakon main anukulit hone main yah kaaphi samarth he. himalay main yah 2,300 meter kii oonchai tak ugaaya jaataa he.
 ise aksar tilhan or nizer seed yaa fir daalon ke saath ugaaya jaataa he. yadhyapi aankade thik thik to uplabdh nahin he lekin fir bhi yah fasal vishwa bhar main 38,000 varg kilometre main boi jaati he.
 ek baar pak kar taiyaar ho jane par iskaa bhandaran behad surakshit hota he. is par kisi prakaar ke keet yaa fafoond hamalaa nahin karte. is gun ke kaaran nirdhan kisaanon hetu yah ek acha vikalp mana jaataa he.
 is anaaj main amino amla methonin paaya jaataa he, jo ki starch kii pradhanta vaale bhojya padaarthon main nahi paaya jaataa. prati 100 gram ke hisaab se iskaa vibhaajan is prakaar kiya jaataa he-
 bharat main karnataka or andhra pradesh main raagi kaa sabase adhik upbhog hota he. isse moti dabl roti, dosa or roti banati he. is se raagi muddi banati he jiske liye raagi aate ko pani main ubala jaataa he, jab pani gaadha ho jaataa he to ise gol akruti kar ghi laga kar sambhar ke saath khaaya jaataa he. vietnam me ise bachche ke janm ke samay aurato ko dawa ke rup me diya jaataa he. isse madiraa bhi banati he.
