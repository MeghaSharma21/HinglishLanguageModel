 paritaarkeey chakra kisi taare ke ird-gird parikrama kar rahe dhul, gas, shishugrahon, kshudragrahon va anya padaarthon ke bane chakra ko kehete hai. is chakra kaa aakaar toras-numa, challe-numa yaa roti-numa hota he. navjaat taron main is chakra main avyavasthit saamagree hoti he jis se aage chalakar shishugrah aadi janm sakate hai, jabaki kuch umr vaale taaro main is chakra me shishugrah hote hai jinase grah ban sakate hai.
