 aaazaad hind yaa aarzi huqumat-e-azaad hind ek bhartiya antajkalin sarkaar thi jise singapore main san 1943 main sthapit kiya gaya tha. yah aksh shaktiyo kii syta se bharat ko swadhinata ke liye ladhane vaale bhartiya rashtravaadeeyon dwaara banaaya gaya tha jiskaa netritv subhash chandra bose kar rahe the. germany se ek 'you bott' se dakshin asia aae, phir vahaan se japan gayi. japan se wein singapore aaye jahaan aaazaad hind kii aasthai sarkaar kii niv rakhi gayi. japani samrajya kii sainik, aarthik or naitik syta se yah sarkaar thiki rahi or japan ke 14 agast 1945 main aatmasamarpan karne ke baavjoot azaad hind ne haar nahin maani or yuddh jari rakhaa. azaad hind mitra deshon se ladhane vaala aakhri shakti tha. subhash chandra bose kii mrityu ke daawey ke baad hi is kii sena, azaad hind fuj ko paraajay mili.
