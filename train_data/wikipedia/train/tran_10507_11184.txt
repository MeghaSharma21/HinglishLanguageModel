 
 lake valesa
 
 lake valesa  poland ke rajaneta, trade-union sanghatankarta tathaa maanavadhikaar kaaryakarta hai. ve solidayrity naamak trade-union ke sah-sansthaapak the. yah union soviet block kii pratham swatantra trade-union thi. unhen san 1983 main shaanti kaa nobel puraskaar diya gaya. ve san 1990 se 1995 tak poland ke raashtrapati rahe.
