 sangitaratnakar shorgadev dwaara rachit sangitshastreey granth he. yah bharat ke sabase mahatvapurn sangitshastreey granthon main se he jo hindustani sangeet tathaa karnataka sangeet dono dwaara samaadrut he. ise 'saptaadhyaayi' bhi kehete hai kyonki isamen saat adhyay hai. bharatmuni ke naatyashaastra ke baad sangeet ratnakar aitihaasik drushti se sabase mahatvapurn granth mana jaataa he. barahavin sadi ke purvarddh main likhe saat adhyyon vaale is granth main sangeet va nrity kaa vistaar se varnan he.
 shorgadev yadav raja 'sinhan' ke rajdrabari the. sinhan kii rajdhani daulatabad ke nikat devgiri thi. is granth ke kai bhashya hue hai jinamein singhbhoopal dwaara rachit 'sangitasudhakar' tathaa kallinath dwaara rachit 'kalanidhi' pramukh hai.
 is granth ke pratham chah adhyay - swargatadhyay, ragavivekaadhyay, prakirnakadhyay, prabandhadhyay, taladhyay tathaa vaadyadhyaay sangeet or vaadyayantron ke bare main hai. iskaa antim adhyay 'nartanadhyay' he jo nrity ke bare main he.
 sangeet ratnakar main kai taalon kaa ullekh he. is granth se pataa chalata he ki pracheen bhartiya paaramparik sangeet main ab badlaav aane shuru ho chuke the va sangeet pehle se udaar hone laga tha. 1000veen sadi ke ant tak, us samay prachlit sangeet ke swaroop ko 'prabandh' kaha jane laga. prabandh do prakaar ke hua karte the - nibaddh prabandh va anibaddh prabandh. nibaddh prabandh ko taal kii paridhi main rahakar gaaya jaataa tha jabaki anibaddh prabandh bina kisi taal ke bandhan ke, mukt rup main gaaya jaataa tha. prabandh kaa ek acha udhaaharan he jayadev rachit geet govind.
 sangitaratnakar main 7 adhyay hai jinamein geet, vaadya or nrity ke vibhinn pakshon kaa varnan he.
 sangitaratnakar main kramchay-sanchay kaa bahut adhik upyog hua he.
