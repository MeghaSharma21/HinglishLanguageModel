 tantrika vigyaan tantrika tantra ke vaigyanik adhyayan ko kehete hai. parmparik rup se yah jeevigyaan kii shakha mana jaataa tha lekin ab rasayan shaastra, sangyaan shaastra, computer vigyaan, abhiyantriki, bhashavigyaan, ganit, ayurvigyaan, anuvanshiki or anya sambandhit vishyon ke antarvishyak sahyog dwaara paribhaashit he. tantrika jeevigyaan or tantrika vigyaan ko aksar ek hi arth vaala mana jaataa he haalaanki yah sambhav he ki bhavishya main jeevon se baahar bhi tantrika vyavastha banaai ja sake or us sandarbh main in donon naamon main antar hoga.
