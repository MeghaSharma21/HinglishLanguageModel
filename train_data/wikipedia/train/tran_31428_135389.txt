 spairo 2: ryptos revange plastesan video game consol ke liye ek video game he. yah doosraa khel he spairo vah dragon shrinkhalaa. pehali khel rahaa tha spairo da dragon jo waapas 1998 main jari kiya gaya tha.
 shrinkhalaa, spairo ke nayak menevalar kii bhoomi main rakhaa gaya he, bajaay dragon sansaaron, jahaan pichley kist sthapit kiya gaya tha. dianasor - sawari jaadugar, ryptos ke rup main janaa jaataa he, evalar kii duniya par akraman kiya he or apane jaadu ke saath apane nagarikon par kahar tej thi. khilaadi, spairo ke rup main evalar kii duniya ke madhyam se yatra or ryptos dwaara kiya nuksaan kii bharpai karnaa chahie.
