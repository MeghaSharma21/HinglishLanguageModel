 mangal grah ke 2 pracruthik upgrah he fobus or dymus. in upgraho ko mangal ke dwaara pakde hue kshudragrah mana jaataa he. in upgraho ki khoj san 1877 me asaf haul ne ki thi. usne inkaa naam unani puranic kathao ke paatr fobus or dymus jo ki yudh ke devtaa ares ke putr the ke naam par rakhaa. fobus or dymus dono shabdo kaa arth bhay he.
