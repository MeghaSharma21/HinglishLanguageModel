 rasinic sutara kisi rasinic yogic ko is prakaar nirupit karata he jisase pataa chalata he ki vah yogic kin-kin tatwon ke kitne-kitne parmaanuon se milakar bana he. saamaanya prayog main praayah anusutra ke liye bhi 'rasinic sutara' kaa hi prayog kar diya jaataa he.
 udhaaharan : mithen kaa anusutra pra.u4 he jo ingit karata he ki mithen kaa anu carbon evam hydrogen ke parmaanuon se milakar bana he tathaa mithen ke ek anu main carbon kaa ek parmaanu va hydrogen ke chaar parmaanu hote hai. parantu is sutara se yah pataa nahin chalata ki carbon kaa ek parmaanu va hydrogen ke ye chaar parmaanu kis prakaar vyavasthit hai. arthath ye ek hi samtal main hai yaa tribim main hai; inke bandhon ke bich kitna kone he; bandhon kii lambaai kitni he, aadi kaa anusutra se kuch bhi gyaan nahin hota.
 kai prakaar ke rasinic sutara upyog kiye jate hai jinaki alag-alag upyogita he or alag-alag jatilta bhi. badhate hue jatilta ke kram main ye hai - anubhavik sutara, anusutra, sanrachana sutara.
