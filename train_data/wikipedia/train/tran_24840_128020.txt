 songkhla thailand kaa ek praant he. yah dakshini thailand kshetra main malay prayadweep main sthit he. dakshin main isaki seemayen malaysia ke peralis or kedah rajyon se lagati hai or yah purv main thailand kii khadi se tatavarti he.
 songkhla ke lagbhag teen-chauthai log thai yaa thai-chini mul ke buddha dharmi log hai. yahan ek sam-sam naamak samudaay bhi he jiske log muslim or thai bhashi hai.
