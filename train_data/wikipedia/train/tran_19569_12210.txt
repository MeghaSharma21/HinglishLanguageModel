 shitoshna katibandh yaa samshitoshna katibandh ooshnakaribandh or sheet katibandh ke bich kaa kshetra kehalaata he. is kshetra kii visheshta yah he ki yahan garmi or sardi ke mausam ke taapmaana main adhik antar nahin hota. lekin yahan ke kuch kshetron main, jaise madhya asia or madhya uttari america, jo samudr se kaafii dur hai, taapmaana main kaafii parivartan hota he or in ilaakon main mahadwipiya jalwaayu paaya jaataa he.samshitoshna katibandheey mausam ooshnakaribandh ke kuch ilaakon main bhi paaya ja saktaa he, khaastaur par ooshnakaribandh ke pahari ilaakon main, jaise andies parvat shrunkhlaa. uttari samshitoshna katibandh uttari golarddh main kark rekha se arctic rekha tak tathaa dakshini samshitoshna katibandh dakshini golarddh main makar rekha se antarctic rekha tak kaa kshetra hota he. vishwa kii bahut badi jansankhya samshitoshna katibandh main— khaastaur par uttari samshitoshna katibandh main— rahati he kyonki is ilaake main bhoomi kii bahutayat he.
