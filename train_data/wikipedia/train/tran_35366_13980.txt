 mohammad ali razaai iran ke duusare raashtrapati the jinhonen 2 se 30 agast tak raashtrapati ke ohade par kaam kiya. is se pehle ve abol-hasan bani-sadr ke neeche pradhan mantri rah chuke the. 30 agast 1981 ko unpar ek jaanaleva hamalaa hua jisamen ve or unke pradhan mantri mohammad-jawaad bahonor donon maare gaye. june 1981 main banisadra ko raashtrapati-pad se bedagal kar diya gaya tha jis se deshbhar main rosh failaa. is maahaul main mojaahedin-e-khalq naamak sanghathan ne apane hazaron samarthakon ke saath ek vidroh yojit kiya. kam-se-kam 100 rajnatik netaa is vidroh main maare gaye, jinamein razaai shaamil the.
