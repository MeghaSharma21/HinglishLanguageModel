 champaner-pavagadh puratathvik udyaan ek unesco vishwa dharohar sthal he, jo ki bharat main sthit he. ise is suchi main san 2004 main sammilit kiya gaya tha. yahaan vrahat str par utkhanit puratathvik, aitihaasik evam jivit sanskaithik dharohar sampatti kii bahutayat he, jo ki ek prabhaavshaalee bhukhand main simati hui he.
 isamen pragaitihaasik chacolithik sthal, ek pracheen hindu rajya kii rajdhani kaa ek mahal va kila va solahavin shatabdi ke gujarat pradesh kii rajdhani ke avshesh hai. yahaan anya padaankon sahit, kile, prasaad, dharmik imaaraten, awasaiya ahaate, krishi chihn va jal aapurti nirmaan kaary ke aathvin shatabdi se lekar chaudaavin shatabdi tak ke anek sthal hai. 
 pavagadh pahari ke shikhar par bana kalika mata mandir, ati paavan sthal mana jaataa he. yahaan varsh paryant badi sankhya main shraddhalu aate hai. yah sthal ekmaatr purna evam aparivartit islamic mughal-purv nagar he.
 is shahar par vaastukar karan grover dwaara bahut dhyaan diya gaya he, jinhone kaaphi samay va prayaas kiye hai, is shahar ko punarsthapit va sudhaar karne hetu. saath hi is bhartiya viraasat ke punarsthaapan main kaaphi yogadan bhi diya he.
 champaner-pavagadh puraatatv park main anekon smarak sthit he jiname se 38 smarakon ko bhartiya puraatatv surveykshan ke daayre main liya gaya hai. surveykshan main dharohar kaa sangrakshan tathaa paryaton ko badhaava dene ke praytn kiye ja rahe hai saath hi saath paryaavaran ke sangrakshan kaa kaary bhi kiya ja rahaa he.
