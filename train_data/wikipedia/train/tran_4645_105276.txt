 dakshin qazakhstan praant madhya asia ke qazakhstan desh kaa ek praant he. isaki rajdhani shimkent naam kaa shahar he. is praant kii dakshini sarhad uzbekistan se lagati he or uzbekistan kii rajdhani tashkent iske bahut paas he. madhya asia kii prasiddh nadi sir dariyaa is praant se hote hue aral sagar kii taraf guzarati he. yahan se turkmenistan ke turkmenabt shahar se rus ke omsk shahar tak jane vaali tel kii ek mahatvapurn pipe-line bhi nikalati he. shimkent main kachche khanijon se tel, sisa or jasta ke kai kaarakaane hai.
 dakshin qazakhstan praant main desh ke kisi bhi anya praant se adhik ghani aabaadi he. isaki vajah yahan kaa anukool mausam, behatar sinchai kaa bandobast or tashkent, samarkand or bukhaaraa jaise pracheen madhya asian shaharon kii nazadiiki he. is praant kii aabaadi bhi tezi se bad rahi he, kyonki sthaneey kazakh or uzbek pariwaron main 5 se 8 bachche hona aam baat he or upar se uttari uzbekistan se bhi bahut se log yahan aakar vyavsaay dhundte hai.
 qazakhstan ke sabhi anya praanton main roosi jati ke log aabaadi kaa sabase bada yaa doosraa sabase bada samudaay hai lekin dakshin qazakhstan akelaa aisa praant he jahaan aisa nahin he. yahan kaa sabase bada samudaay kazakh he or 2009 kii janaganana main ve puri aabaadi ke 72% the. doosraa sabase bada samudaay uzbek hai or ve aabaadi ke 18% the. roosi, tajik, korian or kurd log bhi yahan rahete hai. soviet sangh ke khatm ho jane ke baad bahut se roosi log is praant ko chhodkar rus chale gaye hai.
