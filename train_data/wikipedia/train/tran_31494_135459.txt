 anaam 1992 kii ramesh modi dwaara nirdeshit hindi bhashaa kii thriller film he. isamen armaan kohli or aaysha julka mukhya bhoomikaaon main hai. yah film veda prakaash sharma ke upanyaas vidhwaa kaa pati par aadhaarit he.
 bombai ke police inspector angre ko ek phiet car kii chori kii jaanch karne kaa kaary saunpa gaya he. kathith apradhi ko aspataal main bhartee karaayaa gaya he, jo smriti haani kaa daawa kar rahaa he or apni nurse kaa galaa ghontne kaa prayaas kar chukaa he. phir ek amir udyogpati yah bataata he ki yah jawan aadmi uskaa betaa sikandar he or use uske saath ghar le jaataa he. vah har samay uske saath rahane ke liye ek saathi, roopesh kii vyavastha karata he. angre is maamle ko sangyaan main liye hue he lekin jab use inspector ke.ke dewan or pi.si. yadav dwaara adhishuchet kiya jaataa he to vah adhik pareshaan hota he. vah bataate he ki sikandar vastav main puna sthit shaatir chor johny ke. disuja he, jisane apni patni, jenis, saath hi apane saathi, roopesh ko maar diya ho saktaa he or bombai waapas ja rahaa he. baad main angre ko ek or rahasy kaa saamana karnaa padaa jab apradhi phir se baahar aata he- is baar akaash kii pahchaan ke saath, ek yuva mahila meghna kaa mangetar. jabaki angre apradhi kii vaastavik pahchaan ko jaanane ke liye drudh hai, meghna nishchit he ki vah ek dhongi he kyonki use yakeen he ki akaash maaraa gaya tha. sawaal bana hua he: yah apradhi koun he or uski vaastavik pahchaan vastav main kya he?
