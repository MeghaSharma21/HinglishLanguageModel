 aryasaty kii sankalpana buddha darshan ke mul siddhant he. ise sanskrit main 'chatvaari aryasatyani' or pali main 'chattari ariyascchani' kehete hai
 kii shrenee kaa hissaa
 aryasaty chaar hai-
 praani janm bhar vibhinn du:khon kii shrunkhlaa main padaa rahata he, yah du:kha aryasaty he. sansar ke vishyon ke prati jo trishna he vahi samuday aryasaty he. jo praani trishna ke saath marataa he, vah uski prerana se phir bhi janm grahan karata he. isliye trishna kii samuday aryasaty kehete hai. trishna kaa ashesh prahaan kar dena nirodh aryasaty he. trishna ke na rahane se na to sansar kii vastuon ke kaaran koi du:kha hota he or na maranoparaant uskaa punarjanm hota he. bujh gaye pradeep kii tarah uskaa nirvana ho jaataa he. or, is nirodh kii prapti kaa maarg aryasaty - aashtangik maarg he. iske aath ang hai-samyak drushti, samyak sankalp, samyak vachan, samyak karm, samyak aajeevika, samyak vyaayaam, samyak smriti or samyak samadhi. is aryamarg ko siddh kar vah mukt ho jaataa he.
