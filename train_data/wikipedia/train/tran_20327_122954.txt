 kanchi ke donon bhaagon ko avpaat thappe se gadhakar banaaya jaataa he. iske liye jo ispaat kaam main aata he, vah ustare ke ispaat se ghatiya hota he. garh jane ke baad donon bhaagon ko kathor ispaat ke pench dwaara do prakaar se lagaaya jaataa he. pratham vidhi main kanchi ke donon fal ek duusare kii ore jhuke rahete hai, jisase kaatanevaali dhaaron kii sameeptha bani rahe.
 angutha or angulii phasaakar sugamawa se kaary karne ke liye kanchi ke fal ke donon siron par dhanushakar akruti ghatvardhya dhalaai ke dwaara banaai jaati he ore baad main ispaat kaa fal in akritiyon main laga diya jaataa he. aluminium kii dhanushakar akritiyaan bhi thappa dhalaai dwaara taiyaar kar fal main lagaai jaati hai. aisi kainchiyan dekhne main sundar or kaam main halki hoti hai. bal kaatane, kapadaa kaatane, kaseeda tathaa salama lagane, bagwani tathaa shalyachikits aadi vibhinn kaaryon ke liye vibhinn akritiyon kii kainchiyan banaai jaati hai.
