 landan main bhartiya ucchayog united kingdom main bharat kaa rajanyik mishan he. yah bush house, citibank imaarat or australian ucchayog ke bich oldwich main india house main sthit he. uske saamne landan sakool of iconomics or kings college landan hai. is imaarat kaa prastaav bhartiya ucchayukta sar atul chatarji ne rakhaa tha or isaki abhiklpana sar herbert baker ne kii thi. imaarat 1930 main taiyaar hui or iskaa udghaatan usee varsh 8 july ko samraat jorge pancham ne kiya. imaarat kii bahari deevaaron par kai alankritiyaan hai jo bharat ke vibhinn rajyon ko darshaati hai. yahan 1991 main jon major ne jawaharlal nehru kii ardhapratimaa kaa bhi anaavaran kiya tha. 1981 se yah ek grad II listid imaarat he.
