 jo lare deen ke het bharat ke prasiddh hindi upanyaaskaar or lekhak surendra mohan pathak kaa agast 2014 main harper colinsprakashan dwaara chapa gaya upanyaas he. vargeekaran kii drushti se yah rahasy-romaanch kii shrenee main aanevaala upanyaas he. pehali baar kisi hindi upanyaas kii pre-publicing booking kaa tamgaa is upanyaas ke naam darj ho chukaa he.
 yah pathak ke vimal siries kaa 42vaan upanyaas he jisaki kahani dilli or mumbai ke parivesh main set he.
