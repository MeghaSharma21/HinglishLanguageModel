 dafodil norshisus vansh kaa pushp he. yah safed se pile tak anek rangon kaa hota he. isaki pattiyaan lambii or patali hoti hai.
 defodil va naragis ek hi parivaar ke mand-madhur sugandh vaale paudhe hai. naragis kii safed paanch pankhudiyon ke bich pilaa sugandhit pyaalaa khulaa hota he va paanch phool ek saath ek tahani par chakrakaar rup se khilte hai. defodil kaa phool pyaalaa aakaar main bada hota he. kai baar puraa phool sunaharaa pilaa yaa shwet hota he.
