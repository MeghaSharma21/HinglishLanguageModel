 chandal bharat main vyaktiyon kaa ek aisa varg he, jise saamanyat: jati se baahar tathaa achhut mana jaataa he. yah ek pracheen antyaj, neech or barbar jati he. ise shmashaan paal, dom, antavaasi, thap, shmashaan karmi, antyaj, chandaalani, pukkash, gavaashan, chuda, diwakirti, maatang, shwapach aadi naamon se bhi pukaara jaataa he.bhagwaan gautamabuddha ne suttanipaat main chandal kaa jikar kiya he.
 khattiye brahmane vesse, sudde chandalpukku se.
 na ki chi parivajjeti, sabbamevabhimaddati..
 mrituraj kshatriya, brahman, vaisya,shudra or chandaal - kisi ko kobhi nahin chodta, sabko kuchal daalata he.

 "mrituraj kshatriya, brahman, vaisya,shudra or chandaal - kisi ko kobhi nahi chodta, sabko kuchal daalata he."
 pracheen vidhi samhita "manu smriti" ke anusaar, is varg kaa udai ek brahman mahila or ek shudra purush ke milaap se hua tha. aadhunik samay main bengal main krishak, machuaare or navikon kii jati vishesh ke liye is shabd kaa upyog kiya jaataa he, vah adhiktar namshudra kehalaate hai. kuch vidwaanon kaa mat he ki namshudra kii utpatti bihar kii rajmahal pahadiyon main nivas karne vaali ek aadim janajati se hui he.
 chandal shabd kaa upyog premchand ne apni kahani nairashya main is prakaar kiya he, ""chandal kahin kaa! uske kaaran mere sainkadon rupee par pani phir gaya." hindi samaaj kii lokoktiyon tathaa kahabaton main "chandal chaukadi" kaa prayog kaaphi hota he.
