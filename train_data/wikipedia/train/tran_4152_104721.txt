 aath kaa samooh hi ashtak kehalaata he, jaise gangastak jisamen ganga kii stuti aath shlokon main kii gai he.- 
 yatha gangashtaka pathati ya: prayat: prabhaate,valmikina virachatin sukhdan manushya:"
 jo manushya prabhaat samay main valmiki muni dwaara rachit gangastak praem purvak padhta he, vah sukhi or prem purvak rahata he,
 is prakaar anek devataaon kii ashtak stutiyaan hai.
