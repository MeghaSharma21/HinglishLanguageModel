 kakori prithvi se karodon mile dur mangal grah par sthit ek cretar kaa naam he. iskaa vyas 29.7 kilometre he or yah mangal grah ke akshansh 41.8 va deshaantar 29.9 par sthit he. san 1976 main iskaa namakaran bharat ke ek aitihaasik shahar kakori ke naam par kiya gaya tha.
 lakhanu se lagbhag bees mile dur kakori railway stesan ke paas 9 agast 1925 ko keval das bhartiya krantikariyon ne ramprasad 'bismil' ke netritv main british raaj ke dauraan sarkari khajaana lut liya tha. samraat banaam ramprasad 'bismil' va anya krantikariyon par chalaaye gayi is aitihaasik mukaddame ko kakori kand ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
 krantikariyon dwaara sthapit hindustan republican association naamak party ke liye fand ekatra karne main in yuvakon ko acchhee khaasi kathinaayee ho rahi thi. angrezon ke dar se koi inhen chandaa detaa na tha or yuvakon ke apane gharon kii mali haalat acchhee na thi. aakhirkaar inhone ireland ke krantikariyon kaa tarika apanaayatehue or party ke liye paisa ugaahne kii niyat se pehali dakaiti 25 disambar 1924 kii raat ko bamrauli main daali jiskaa netritv bismil ne kiya tha..
 bamrauli, bichpuri va dwarkapur main daali gayi dakaitiyon main kuch khaas rakam haath na aane ke baad hi party ne sarkari khajaana luutane kii yojana banaayi. kakori stesan ke paas hui is dakaiti main yadhyapi krantikariyon ko 10000 rupayon se kam raashi hi haath lagi thi parantu british sarkaar ne mukademen main 10 lakh rupee kharch kiye. party ke 40 sadasyon ko puure hindustan se giraftaar kiya gaya. sarkaar kaa takhta palatney kii sajeesh main 4 ko phaansi va 16 anya krantikariyon ko kathor karawas sajaa di gayi.
 kakori kii ghatana koi maamuli ghatana na thi. itihaaskaaron ke anusaar is ghatana ne bhartiya swatantrata sangram kii dasha or disha donon hi badal deen. 1927 main bismil ashfaq, roshan va rajendra kii phaansi ke bees baras baad hi hindustan azaad ho gaya.
