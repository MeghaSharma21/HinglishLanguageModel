 charas 1976 kii bollywood film he jiskaa ramanand sagar ne nirmaan or nirdesan kiya. film main mukhya bhoomika main dharmendra or hema malini sahit ajit, aruna irani, amjad khan, asraani, sujith kumaar, keshto mukherjee or tom alter hai. is film main sangeet lakshmikant-pyarelal ne diya.
 suraj apane parivaar ke saath africa raashtra uganda main rahata he jabaki bharat main parivaar kii sampatti or kaarobaar kii dekhbhaal unka kaalicharan naam kaa ek karmchaaree karata he. general idi amin dwaara desh main shuru kii gai raajnneetik kranti ke kaaran suraj ko apane parivaar ke saath uganda chhodane ke liye majaboor hona padataa he. kaalicharan ko yah dar sataane lagata he ki unhen pataa chal jayega ki usne unka paisa drugs taskari main lagaaya hua he or is karanvash vah unke ghar main aag laga detaa he. suraj ke pita kii is aag main mrityu ho jaati he or uski bahan ko agava kar liya jaataa he. suraj dwaara kaalicharan kii taskari gatividhiyon ko rokane ke liye police use bhartee kar leti he or is prayojan ke kaaran vah europe jaataa he jahaan uski mulaakaat sudhaa se hoti he. sudhaa ek khubasurat nartki he jise kaalicharan drugs taskari main sahaayta karne ke liye blackmel karata he. suraj kaa mishan use pehle rome or fir macau le jaataa he jahaan uski punah bhet sudhaa se hoti he. sthaneey police kii madad se suraj or bhartiya narkotics vibhaag kaalicharan ke zyaadaatar aadamiyon ko pakadaney main kaamyaab ho jate hai parantu kaalicharan sudhaa ko bandhi bana kar apane bhumigat adde main chhip jaataa he. aage kii pathkatha yah darshaati he ki kaise suraj kaalicharan ke chungal se sudhaa ko mukt karaata he.
 film main sangeet kii rachana lakshmikant-pyarelal ne kii or geet likhe anand bakshi ne.
