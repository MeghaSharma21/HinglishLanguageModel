 ahmedabad se 140 kilometre dur sthit taranga hills ko jain mandiron ke liye janaa jaataa he. is pahari ko jain siddh kshetra kaha jaataa he. pahari par 5 digambar or 5 shwetambar mandir bane hue hai. mana jaataa he ki in pahadiyon ke shikhar par anek santon ne moksh praapt kiya tha. 12veen shatabdi main yahaan shwetambar solanki raja kumarpal ne bhagwaan ajeetnath ke sammaan main yahaan ek khubasurat mandir kaa nirmaan karavaaya tha.
