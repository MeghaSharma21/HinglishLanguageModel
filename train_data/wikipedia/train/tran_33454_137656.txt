 sarkari i-bazaar  bharat sarkaar dwaara sachivon ke samooh kii siforeeshon ke aadhaar par, sarkari i-bazaar kaa gathan kiya he jahaan se vibhinn sarkari vibhaagon dwaara aavashyak saamaanya upyog ke saamaanon or sevaaon kii online kharid kii ja sake.
 sarkari i-bazaar ke gathan kaa lakshya saarvajanik kharid main paardarshita, dakshata or gati ko badhanaa he. yah sarkari upabhoktaaon kii suvidha ke liye i-boli prakriya, reverse i-nilaamee or maang ekatrikaran ke upkaran pradaan karata he or sarkari kharch ke liye sarvottam mulya praapt karata he. bharat sarkaar niyam-1961 main sudhaar karte hue dinaank 8 disambar 2017 kii adhisuchana ke tahat rashtriya saarvajanik kharid portal-sarkaar i-bazaar kaa vikaas, sanchaalan or rakrakhaav ko sammilit kiya he. sarkari upyogkartaaon ko sarkari i-bazaar ke madhyam se kharidariyon ke liye adhikrut kiya gaya he or vitt mantralay dwaara saamaanya vittiya niyam-2017 main ek nayaa niyam sankhya 149 jodakar anivaary bana diya gaya he.
