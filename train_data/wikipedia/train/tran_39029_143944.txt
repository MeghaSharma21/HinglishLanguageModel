 samajashaastra, paddati or vishay vastu, donon ke maamle main ek vistrut vishay he. paramparaagat rup se isaki kendriyata saamaajik str-vinyas, saamaajik sambandh, saamaajik sampark, dharm, samajashaastra|samskruti]] or vichlan par rahi he, tathaa iske drushtikon main gunatmak or maatraatmak shodh takanic, donon kaa samavesh he. chunki adhikanshtah manushya jo kuch bhi karata he vah saamaajik sanrachana yaa saamaajik gatividhi kii shrenee ke arntagat sateek baithta he, samajashaastra ne apana dhyaan dhire-dhire anya vishyon jaise- chikitsa, sainya or dand sanghathan, jan-sampark or yahaan tak ki vaigyanik gyaan ke nirmaan main saamaajik gatividhiyon kii bhoomika par kendrit kiya he. saamaajik vaigyanik paddatiyon kii seemaa kaa bhi vyapak rup se vistaar hua he. 20veen shatabdi ke madhya ke bhashaai or saanskrithik parivartanon ne tezi se saamaaj ke adhyayan main bhashya vishyak or vyaakhyaatmak drushtikon ko utpann kiya. iske vipareet, haal ke dashakon ne naye ganitiya rup se kathor paddatiyon kaa udai dekha he, jaise saamaajik network vishleshan.

 sanrachana evam saadhan saamaajik siddhant main ek sthaayee bahes kaa mudda he: "kya saamaajik sanrachanaayen athwa maanav saadhan kisi vyakti ke vyavhaar kaa nirdhaaran karata he?" is sandarbh main 'saadhan', vyaktiyon ke swatantra rup se kaary karne or mukt chunaav karne kii kshamta ingit karata he, jabaki 'sanrachana' vyaktiyon kii pasand or kaaryon ko simit athwa prabhavit karne vaale karkon ko nirdisht karti he . sanrachana athwa saadhan kii pradhanta par charchaa, saamaajik satta-mimasaa ke mul marm se sambandhit hai . uttar aadhunik kaaleen aalochakon kaa saamaajik vigyaan kii vyapak pariyojna ke saath mail-milaap kaa ek prayaas, khaas kar britain main, vivechanaatmak yatharthwaad kaa vikaas rahaa he. raay bhaskar jaise vivechanaatmak yathartwaadiyon ke liye, paaramparik pratyakshvaad, vigyaan ko yani ki khud sanrachana or saadhan ko hi sambhav karne vaale, sattamulak halaaton ke samaadhaan main naakaami kii vajah se 'gyaan tarkdosh' karata he. atyadhik sanrachanaatmak yaa sadhanparak vichaar ke prati avishvaas kaa ek or saamaanya parinaam bahuaiami siddhant, vishesh rup se talcott parcens kaa kriya siddhant or anthoni giddens kaa sanrachanaatmakataa kaa siddhant kaa vikaas rahaa he. anya sakalywaadee siddhanton main shaamil hai, piyare bordeo kii gathan kii avadharana or alfred shutz ke kaam main bhi jivan-prapanch kaa drishyprapanchvaad kaa vichaar.
 saamaajik pratyakshvaad ke para-saidhantik aalochanaon ke baavjood, sankhyikeey maatraatmak tareeke bahut hi zyaada vyavhaar main rahete hai. maaikel buraavoy ne saarvajanik samajashaastra kii tulana, kathor aachaar-vyavhaar par jor dete hue, shaikshanik yaa vyavsaayik samajashaastra ke saath kii he, jo vyapak rup se anya saamaajik/rajnatik vaigyanikon or darshanikon ke bich sanlap se sambandh rakhata he.
 saanskrithik samajashaastra main shabdon, kalakritiyon or pratiko kaa vivechanaatmak vishleshan shaamil he, jo saamaajik jivan ke roopon ke saath anyonya kriya karata he, chaahe up samskruti ke antargat ho athwa bade paimaane par samaajon ke saath.cimail ke liye, samskruti kaa taatpary he "bahya sadhano ke madhyam se vyaktiyon kaa sanvardhan karnaa, jo itihaas ke kram main vastunishth banaae gaye hai. theodor adorno or walter benjamin jaise francfort skool ke siddhantkaron ke liye swayan samskruti, ek aitihaasik bhautikatavaadivishleshan kaa prachlit vishay tha. saanskrithik shiksha ke shikshan main saamaajik jaanch-padtaal ke ek saamaanya vishay ke rup main, 1964 main england ke birmingham vishvavidhyalay main sthapit ek anusandhan centre, samakaleen saanskrithik adhyayan centre main shuru hua. richard hogart, stuart haul or remand williams jaise birmingham skool ke vidwaanon ne vigat nav-marxvaadi siddhant main parilakshith 'utpaadak' or upabhoktaaon' ke bich nirbhik vibhaajan par prashn karte hue, saanskrithik granthon or janotpadit utpaadon kaa kis prakaar istemaal hota he, isaki parsparikta par jor diya. saanskrithik shiksha, apni vishay-vastu ko saanskrithik prathaon or satta ke saath unke sambandhon ke sandarbh main jaanch karti he. udhaaharan ke liye, up-samskruti kaa ek adhyayan, yuvaaon kii saamaajik prathaon par vichaar karegaa, kyonki ve shaasha varg se sambandhit hai.
 'vichlan' kriya yaa vyavhaar kaa varnan karti he, jo saanskrithik aadarshon sahit aupchaarik rup se lagu-niyamon tathaa saamaajik mandandon kaa anaupchaarik ulanghan karti he. samajashastriyon ko yah adhyayan karne kii dheel di gai he ki kaise ye maandand nirmit hue; kaise ve samay ke saath badalte hai; or kaise ve lagu hote hai. vichlan ke samajashaastra main anek prameya shaamil hai, jo saamaajik vyavhaar ke uchit rup se samajhane main madad dene ke liye, saamaajik vichlan ke antargat nihit pravritiyon or swaroop ko sateek taur par varnit karnaa chaahate hai. vipathagami vyavhaar ko varnit karne vaale teen spasht saamaajik shreniyan hai: sanrachanaatmak kriyavaad; pratikatmak anyonyakriyavaad; or virdohi siddhant
 aarthik samajashaastra, aarthik drishy prapanch kaa samajashastreey vishleshan he; samaaj main aarthik sanrachanaon tathaa sansthaaon kii bhoomika, tathaa aarthik sanrachanaon or sansthaaon ke swaroop par samaaj kaa prabhaav.poonjeevaad or aadhuniktaa ke bich sambandh ek pramukh mudda he. marks ke aitihaasik bhautikwaad ne yah darshane kii koshish kii ki kis prakaar aarthik balon kaa samaaj ke dhaanche par moulik prabhaav he. maiks webar ne bhi, haalanki kuch kam nirdharak taur par, saamaajik samaj ke liye aarthik prakriyaaon ko mahatvapurn mana.jorge cimail, vishesh rup se apane philaasphi of mani main, aarthik samajashaastra ke praarambhik vikaas main mahatvapurn rahe, jis prakaar emile darkhim apni da divizan of lebar in socity jaisi rachanaaon se.aarthik samajashaastra aksar saamaajik-aarthiki kaa paryaay hota he. tathaapi, kai maamalon main, saamaajik-arthshaastri, vishisht aarthik parivartanon ke saamaajik prabhaav par dhyaan kendrit karte hai, jaise ki factory kaa band hona, bazaar main herapheri, antarrashtriya vyapaar sandhiyon par hastakshar, nae prakritik gas viniman ityadi.
 paryaavaran sambandhi samajashaastra, saamaajik-paryaavarniya parsparik sambandhon kaa saamaajik adhyayan he, jo paryaavaran sambandhi samasyaon ke saamaajik karkon, un samasyaon kaa samaaj par prabhaav, tathaa unke samaadhaan ke prayaas par jor detaa he. iske alaava, saamaajik prakriyaaon par yathesht dhyaan diya jaataa he, jinaki vajah se katipay pariveshgat paristhitiyaan, saamaajik taur par paribhaashit samasyaayein ban jaati hai. 
 shiksha kaa samajashaastra, shikshan sansthaanon dwaara saamaajik dhaanchon, anubhavon or anya parinaamon ko nirdhaarit karne ke taur-tareeqon kaa adhyayan he. yah vishesh rup se ucch, agrani, vayask or satat shiksha sahit aadhunik audyogic samaaj kii schuli shiksha pranali se sambandhit he. 
 parivaar kaa samajashaastra, vibhinn saidhantik drushtikonon ke zarie parivaar ekak, vishesh rup se mul parivaar or uski apni alag langik bhoomikaaon ke aadhunik aitihaasik utthan kii jaanch karata he. parivaar, praarambhik or purv-vishvavidhyalayan shaikshik paathyakramon kaa ek lokapriy vishay he.

 gyaan kaa samajashaastra, maanviya vichaaron or saamaajik sandarbh ke bich sambandhon kaa, jisamen uskaa udai hua he or samaajon main prachlit vichaaron ke prabhaav kaa adhyayan karata he. yah shabd pehali baar 1920 ke dashak main vyapak rup se prayukt hua, jab kai german-bhashi siddhantkaron ne bade paimaane par is bare main likhaa, inamen sabase ullekhneey maiks shiller or karl minhem hai. 20veen sadi ke madhya ke varshon main prakaaryawad ke prabhutv ke saath, gyaan kaa samajashaastra, samajashastreey vichaaron kii mukhyadhara kii paridhi par hi bana rahaa. 1960 ke dashak main ise vyapak rup se punah pariklpit kiya gaya tathaa peter el.burgers evam thomas lacoman dwaara da soshal construction of riyaltee main vishesh taur par rozmarra kii zindagi par or bhi nikat se lagu kiya gaya, tathaa or yah abhi bhi maanav samaaj se gunatmak samaj ke saath nipatane vaale tareekon ke centre main he .mishael phokalt ke "puratathvik" or "vanshavali" adhyayan kaaphi samakaleen prabhaav ke hai.
 kaanoon kaa samajashaastra, samajashaastra kii up-shakha or kanoonee shiksha ke kshetrantargat abhigam, donon ko sanderbhit karata he. kaanoon kaa samajashastreey adhyayan vividhtapurna he, jo samaaj ke anya pahaluon jaise ki kanoonee sansthaaen, siddhant or anya saamaajik ghatnaayen or inke vipareet prabhaavon kaa kaanoon ke saath parsparik sampark kaa pareekshan karata he. uske anusandhan ke katipay kshetron main kanoonee sansthaaon ke saamaajik vikaas, kanoonee muddon ke saamaajik nirmaan or saamaajik parivartan ke saath kaanoon se sambandh shaamil hai. kaanoon kaa samajashaastra nyaayashastra, kaanoon kaa aarthik vishleshan, aparaadh vigyaan jaise adhik vishisht vishay kshetron ke aar-paar jaataa he. kaanoon aupchaarik he or isliye 'maanak' ke samaan nahin he. iske vipareet, vichlan kaa samajashaastra, saamaanya se aupchaarik or anaupchaarik donon vichalnon, yatha aparaadh or vichlan ke saanskrithik roopon, donon kaa pareekshan karata he.
 saanskrithik adhyayan ke samaan hi, media adhyayan ek alag vishay he, jo samajashaastra or anya saamaajik-vigyaan tathaa manviki, vishesh rup se saahitiyik aalochana or vivechanaatmak siddhant kaa sammilan chahta he. haalanki utpaadan prakriya yaa suruchipurna swaroopon kii aalochana kii chhot samajashastriyon ko nahin he, arthath saamaajik ghatakon kaa vishleshan, jaise ki vaicharik prabhaav or darshakon kii pratikriya, saamaajik siddhant or paddati se hi panape hai. is prakaar 'media kaa samajashaastra' swatah ek up-vishay nahin he, balki media ek saamaanya or aksar ati-aavashyak vishay he.
 sainya samajashaastra kaa lakshya, sainya kaa ek sanghathan ke bajaay saamaajik samooh ke rup main vyavasthit adhyayan karnaa he. yah ek bahut hi vishisht up-kshetra he, jo sainikon se sambandhit maamalon kii ek alag samooh ke rup main, aajeevika or yuddh main jivit rahane se jude saajha hiton par aadhaarit, badhyakaari samuhik kaaryon kii, naagarik samaaj ke antargat adhik nishchit or parimit uddeshyon or mulyon sahit jaanch karata he. sainya samajashaastra, naagarik-sainya sambandhon or anya samoohon yaa sarkari agenciyon ke bich parsparik kriyaaon se bhi sambandhit he. inhen bhi dekhen: aatankwaad kaa samajashaastra. shaamil vishay hai:
 raajnneetik samajashaastra, satta or vyaktitv ke pratichhedan, saamaajik sanrachana or raajnneeti kaa adhyayan he. yah antahvishay he, jahaan raajnneeti vigyaan or samajashaastra ek duusare ke vipareet rahete hai. yahaan vishay samaajon ke raajnneetik maahaul ko samajhane ke liye, sarkari or aarthik sanghathanon kii pranali ke vishleshan hetu, tulanaatmak itihaas kaa upyog karata he. itihaas or saamaajik aankadon kii tulana or vishleshan ke baad, raajnneetik rujhaan or swaroop ubhar kar saamne aate hai. raajnneetik samajashaastra ke sansthaapak maiks webar, moise austrogorski or robert michels the.

 shahari samajashaastra main saamaajik jivan or mahanagareey kshetra main maanviya sambandhon kaa vishleshan bhi shaamil he. yah ek maanak kaa adhyayan he, jisamen sanrachanaon, prakriyaayon, parivartan or shahari kshetra kii samasyaon kii jaankaree dene kaa prayaas kiya jaataa he or aisa kar aayojana or niti nirmaan ke liye shakti pradaan kii jaati he. samajashaastra ke adhikansh kshetron kii tarah, shahari samajashastri sankhyikee vishleshan, nirikshan, saamaajik siddhant, saakshaatkaar or anya tareekon kaa upyog, kai vishyon jaise palaayan or janasankhyikee pravritiyon, arthshaastra, garibi, vanshanugat sambandh, aarthik rujhaan ityadi ke adhyayan ke liye kiya jaataa he. audyogic kranti ke baad jorge cimail jaise siddhantkaron ne da metropolis and mental life main shaharikaran kii prakriya or prabhavit saamaajik algaav or gumnami par dhyaan kendrit kiya. 1920 or 1930 ke dashak main chicago skool ne shahari samajashaastra main pratikatmak parsparik sambaddhta ko kshetra main anusandhan kii ek vidhi ke rup main upyog main laakar ek vishesh kaam kiya he. gramin samajashaastra, iske vipareet, gaiyr saamaajik jivan mahanagareey kshetron ke adhyayan se jude samajashaastra kaa ek kshetra he.
 saamaajik shodh vidhiyon ko do vyapak shreniyon main vibhajit kiya ja saktaa he:
 jabaki kai pahaluon main kaaphi had tak bhinn hote hue, gunatmak or maatraatmak, donon drushtikonon main siddhant or aankadon ke bich vyavasthit anyonya-kriya shaamil he. vidhi kaa chunaav kaaphi had tak is baat par nirbhar karata he ki shodhakarta kya khoj rahaa he. udhaaharan ke liye, ek puri aabaadi ke sankhyikeey samanyikaran kaa khaakaa khinchne se judaa shodhakarta, ek pratinidhi namoona jansankhya ko ek surveykshan prashnawali vitarit kar saktaa he. iske vipareet, ek shodhakarta, jo kisi vyakti ke saamaajik kaaryon ke purna prasang ko samajhnaa chahta he, nrivanshavigyaan aadhaarit pratibhaagi avlokan yaa mukt saakshaatkaar chun saktaa he. aam taur par adhyayan ek 'bahu-ranneeti' dizin ke hisse ke rup main maatraatmak or gunatmak vidhiyon ko mila dete hai. udhaaharan ke liye, ek sankhyikeey swaroop yaa ek lakshith namoona hasil karne ke liye maatraatmak adhyayan kiya ja saktaa he or phir ek saadhan kii apni pratikriya ko nirdhaarit karne ke liye gunatmak saakshaatkaar ke saath sanyukt kiya ja saktaa he.
 jaisaa ki adhikansh vishyon ke maamalon main he, aksar samajashastri vishesh anusandhan takneekon ke samarthan shiviron main vibhajit kiye gaye hai. ye vivaad saamaajik siddhant ke aitihaasik kor se sambandhit hai .
 shodh vidhiyon kii nimnlikhit suchi na to anany he or naa hi vistrut he : 
 saamaajik anusandhan, arthshaastriyon,rajaneta|shikshavidon, yojanakaaron, kaanoon nirmaataaon, prashaskon, vikaskon, dhanadhy vyavsaayiyon, prabandhakon, gaiyr-sarkari sanghathanon or laabh nirpeksh sanghathanon, saamaajik kaaryakartaaon, saarvajanik nitiyon ke nirmaan tathaa saamaanya rup se saamaajik muddon ko hal karne main ruchi rakhane vaale logon ko jaankaree detaa he.
 maaikel braavo ne saarvajanik samajashaastra, vyaavhaarik anuprayogon se spasht rup se jude pahalu or academik samajashaastra, jo peshewar or chaatro ke bich saidhantik bahes ke liye mote taur par sambandhit he, ke bich antar ko darshaya he.
 samajashaastra vibhinn vishyon ke saath atichhaadan karata he, jo samaaj kaa adhyayan karte hai; "samajashaastra" or "saamaajik vigyaan" anaupchaarik rup se paryaayavachi hai. nrivigyaan, arthshaastra, darshanashastra, raajnneeti vigyaan or manovigyaan ne samajashaastra ko prabhavit kiya he or isse prabhavit bhi hue hai; chunki ye kshetra ek hi itihaas or samayik ruchi ko saajha karte hai.
 saamaajik manovigyaan kaa vishisht kshetra saamaajik or manovaigyanik hiton ke kai raaston se ubhar kar aayaa he; yah kshetra aage chal kar saamaajik yaa manovaigyanik bal ke aadhaar par pahachaanaa gaya he. vivechanaatmak siddhant, samajashaastra or saahitiyik siddhanton kii sansruti se prabhavit he.
 saamaajik jaiviki, is baat kaa adhyayan he ki kaise saamaajik vyavhaar or sanghathan, vikaas or anya jaivik prakriyaaon dwaara prabhavit hue hai. yah kshetra samajashaastra ko anya kai vigyaan se mishrit karata he jaise nrivigyaan, jeev vigyaan, praani shaastra va anya. saamaajik jaiviki ne, samajikaran or paryaavarniya karkon ke bajaay jin abhivyakti par bahut adhik dhyaan dene ke kaaran, saamaajik academy ke bheetar vivaad ko utpann kiya he .
