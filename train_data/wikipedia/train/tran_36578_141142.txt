 
 blid trugopan yaa  fizant kul kaa pakshi he jo trogopan prajati kaa sabase bada pakshi he. 
 ise assam main hurhuria yaa saan surai, naga bhashaa main agha, aogho, gnu, kuki bhashaa main chintho tathaa tibeti main bop kehete hai. 
 yah pakshi bhutan se lekar bharat ke purvottar praant arunachal pradesh, nagaland, mizoram, manipur se lekar uttareey myanmar, dakshin-purvi tibet tathaa pashchimottar yunaan main choti or chhitari huyi aabaadi main paaya jaataa he. haal ke anveshan se pataa chalaa he ki yah bharat main durlabh he haalanki nagaland or arunachal pradesh ke kuch sthanon main yah sthaneey rup se aam paaya jaataa he. 
 is pakshi kii aabaadi kaa anumaan 2,500 se 9,999 tak lagaaya gaya he or yah nirantar ghatati ja rahi he.
