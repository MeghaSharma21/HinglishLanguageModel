 anshu jamasempa ek bhartiya parvatarohi or duniya kii pehali mahila he, jinhone sizan main mount everest ke shikhar par do baar chadhaai kii hai. yah ek aurat dwaara sabase oonchi shikhar kii sabase tej dabl chadhaai bhi he. 
 yah 32 varshiya parvatarohi arunachal pradesh ke pashchimi kameng jile ke bomdila se hai. vivaah se pehle vah sonitpur jile ke gohpur main rahati thi or unka naam deepika kalita tha. uske pati, sering wang sabhi arunachal parvatarohan or saahasik khel association ke adhyaksh hai. unki 2 betiyaan bhi hai. 14 wein dalai lamas ke aashirwad lene ke baad unhone 2 april, 2017 ko guwahati se everest chadhaai abhiyaan shuru kiya tha. unhone everest base camp main anukulan ke liye 38 din kaa samay liya or 4 april ko unki mukhya yatra shuru kii 16 may ko 9:15 baje, 17 anya parvatarohiyon ke saath, unhone pahaad par chadhakar or bhartiya rashtriya dhvaj ko fahraaya. 19 may ko unhone ek nepali parvatarohi fari sherpa ke saath duusare kathin daure kii shuruyaat kii. lagbhag 10 baje tak unhone lambi paidal yatra bina kisi rukaawat ke saath kii or ant main 21 may, 2017 ko 7:45 baje sheersh par pahunchi. 
 itihaas banane ke liye, arunachal pradesh sarkaar ne tenzing norge rashtriya saahasik puraskaar ke liye unka naam sujhaayaa he. anshu jamasempa "savachh bharat abhiyaan" main brands ambesder bani thi. haal hi main unhen purvottar paryaton ke brands ambesder ke rup main chunaa gaya hai.
