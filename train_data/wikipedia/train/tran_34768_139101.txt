 raji 2018 kii hindi film he. iskaa nirmaan karan johar ne kiya he or mukhya bhoomika main aalia bhatta or vicky kaushal he. yah harinder sikaa ke upanyaas colling sahamat par banaayi gayi he. film kii kahani 1971 bharat paak yuddh ke dauraan kii he. sahamat naam kii ladki ko bharat kii taraf se jasusi ke liye pakistan bheja jaataa he. 
 yah film desh ke liye jasusi karne vaali bahadur ladki kii kahani he, sanyog se is ladki kaa naam bhi 'sahamat' he. nirmaataa karan johar ne harinder sikaa ke angreji upanyaas 'sahamat kaling' par yah film aadhaarit he jiskaa kalkhand bharat or pakistan vah yuddh he jo san 1971 main bangla desh kii azaadi ke liye ladaa gaya tha. 
 kashmir kaa ek deshbhakt parivaar apni najuk si beti ko jasusi ke liye bhej detaa he, pakistan ke ek senadhikari ke parivaar main, unki bahu banaakar. pramukh bhoomika main hindustani jasoos ke rup main aalia bhatta ne abhinay ke nae pratimaan sthapit kiye hai. anya bhoomikaaon main vicky kaushal, jayadeep ahlavat, rajit kapur, shishir sharma or nikhil bakshi ne jabardast kaam kiya he. 
 puri film kaa kathanak pakistan kaa he, jabaki shooting bharat main hi hui he kuch drishy pakistan-bharat seemaa kshetra ke bhi hai . 
 firstpost ke anna em em veeticad ne kaha ki aalia bhatta meghna gulzaar ke dil se rokane, dil kii dhadkan vaali jasusi naatak main shandaar he or film ko 5 main se 4.5 star die.
 tanu vaids manu riterns ke baad, bharat main 100 karod rupee se zyada sakane ke liye abhinetri lead dwaara sanchalit duusari film ban gai. is film ne bharat main 115 karod rupee kamaaye, jo alia bhatta kii sabase zyada kamai karne vaali film ke rup main ubhara, badrinaath kii dulhaniya se aage.
