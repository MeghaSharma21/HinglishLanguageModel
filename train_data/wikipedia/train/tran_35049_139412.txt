 thakurdwara uttar pradesh ke muradabad jile kii tehsil he. ramganga kii kai sahaayak nadiyaan yahan bahati hai. yah muradabad nagar se 49 kimi dur uttari seemaa par sthit he. yahan tehsil kii kachhari, aspataal tathaa vidhyalay hai. yahan saptaah main 3 din bazaar lagata he. 
 yahan sooti kapadey bunane evam rangane kaa kaary hota he.
 yahaan ke vidhayak nawab jaan khan hai jo kunwar sarvesh ke saansad banane ke uparant huye chunaav main sapa party se khadey hokar jiti he. inkaa nivas sthal vatika pilesh colony nikat sagar public skool kasipur road thakurdwara.
