 forencik anemeshan jisamen ghatnaon yaa durghatnaon kii audio-vijual punarnirmaan sahaayta jaanchakartaaon ke liye banaai gai hai, nyaayalayik vigyaan kii ek shakha he. forencik anemeshan purna gati computer graphics kaa upyog ek ghatana vishram karne ke liye he, jaise kii motor durghatana, ek imaarat, ek hamale, yaa ek yantric upkaran ke kaamkaaj ke patan. forencik anemeshan kaa istemaal pehali baar conners ve. sanyukt rajya america, donon pakshon ne 2 agast 1985 ko delta flite 191 kii durghatana ke ek maamle main computer ri-kritiyon or anemation kaa istemaal kiya tha.ek amareeki aapraadhik mukademe main computer anemeshan kaa pehla soochna upyog james mishael 1991 marine county, sie hatya ke mukademe main tha.
