 
 bargad bahuvarshiya vishaal vriksh he. yah ek sthaliy dwibijapatri env sapushpak vriksh he. iskaa tana sidhaa env kathor hota he. isaki shakhaon se jade nikalakar hawa main latakati hai tathaa badhate hue jameen ke andar ghus jaati hai env stambh ban jaati hai. in jadon ko baroh yaa praap jad kehete hai. iskaa fal chota golakaar env laal rang kaa hota he. iske andar beej paaya jaataa he. iskaa beej bahut chota hota he kintu iskaa ped bahut vishaal hota he. isaki pattee chaudee, env lagbhag andakar hoti he. isaki pattee, shakhaon env kalikaon ko todane se doodh jaisaa ras nikalataa he jise letex kaha jaataa he.
 hindu dharm main vat vriksh kii bahut mahatta he. brahma, vishnu, mahesh kii trimurti kii tarah hi vat,pipal va neem ko mana jaataa he, atahev bargad ko brahma samaan mana jaataa he. anek vrath va tyohaaron main vatvriksh kii puja kii jaati he.
