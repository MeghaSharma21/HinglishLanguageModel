 mahabharat main angadesh kaa varnan miltaa he jiske anusaar yah bharat ke purvi bhaag main sthit tha. mahabharat main surya devtaa kaa putr karn angadesh kaa raja tha.
 angadesh pehle raja jaraasandh kaa das tha kyoki ang kaa koi raja nahi tha. jab duryodhana ne karn ko raja banaaya to log kehane lage ki angadesh kaa raja bananaa matlab mrityu ke mukh main janaa he. karn angadesh gaya va yah janaa ki log isliye yah kahe he kyoki angadesh ko jaraasandh kaa dar tha. to karn ne jaraasandh ko haraaya va devtaa kuber kii sahaayta se angadesh ko dhanwaan banaaya. pehle angadesh me dhan, kapadaa, ghar, bhojan, sena va bahut vastuon kii kami thi lekin kuber ke vardaan ke baad angadesh me dhan, kapadaa, ghar, bhojan va do akshuhinee sena kaa nirmaan hua arthath angadesh ko dhanwaan banane me karn kaa haath tha.
