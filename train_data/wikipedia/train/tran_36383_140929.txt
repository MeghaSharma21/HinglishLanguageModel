 yarlung tsangpo tibet ke pashchimi bhaag main kailash parvat or maansarovar jhil se dakshinpurv main sthit tamlung tso se utpann hone vaali ek nadi he. yah dakshin tibet ghati or yarlung tsangpo mahaan ghati banaati hui dakshin kii ore rukh kar ke bharat ke arunachal pradesh rajya main pravesh kar jaati he jahaan ise siyang nadi ke naam se janaa jaataa he. yahi nadi aage chalakar or chaudee ho jaati he or bharat kii prasiddh brahmaputra nadi banati he. tibet main iske dwaara banaai gai yarlung tsangpo mahaan ghati vishwa kii sabase gahari or sabase badi tang ghati kehalaati he. pracheen tibet kii samskruti ke kai mahatvapurn kendra isi nadi ke kinare based hue the.
