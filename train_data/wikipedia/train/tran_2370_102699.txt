 saraa yaa sarahapa yaa siddh sarahapa hindi ke pratham kavi maane jate hai. unako buddha dharm kii vajrayan or sahajayan shakha kaa pravartak tathaa aadi siddh mana jaataa he. unka mul naam ‘rahulbhadra’ tha or unke ‘sarojvajra’, ‘sharoruhavajr’, ‘padam‘ tathaa ‘padmavajr’ naam bhi milte hai. ve paalshaasak dharmpal ke samakaleen the.
 unke janm-sthaan ko lekar vivaad he. ek tibeti janashruti ke aadhaar par unka janm-sthaan urisa bataaya gaya he. ek janashruti saharsa jile ke panchgachhiya gram ko bhi unka janm-sthaan bataati he.
 sarahapa dwaara rachit dohakosh main prayukt lipi vartmaan samay kii kathi, ang lipi, asamiya lipi or bangla lipi or oriya lipi se bahut kuch milti he. rahul sankrutyaayan ke anusaar, sarhapaad sabase pracheen siddh yaa siddhacharya the tathaa ve oriya, angika or hindi ke 'aadi kavi' hai. rahul sankrutyaayan ke anusaar sarhapaad, haribhadra naamak buddha darshanik ke shishy the jo swayan shaantarakshit ke shishy the. chunki tibeti aitihaasik sroton se yah sugyaat he ki shaantarakshit kaa jeevankaal 8veen shatabdi kaa madhyakaal tha, tathaa haribhadra paal raja dharmpal ke samakaleen the, atah prathit hota he ki sarhapaad kaa jeevankaal 8veen shatabdi kaa uttarardh yaa 9veen shatabdi kaa purvardh rahaa hoga.
