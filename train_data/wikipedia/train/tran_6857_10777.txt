 taiyuan janavaadee ganraajya china ke uttari bhaag main sthit shanshi praant kaa sabase bada nagar or rajdhani he. 2010 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 42,01,591 thi jisamen se 32,12,500 shahari naagarik or baaqi gramin kshetron ke nivasee the. 'taiyuan' kaa matlab 'mahaan maidan' hota he or iskaa naam fen nadi ke pahaado se utarane ke baad bane maidan par padaa he. yah ek audyogic shahar samajha jaataa he.
