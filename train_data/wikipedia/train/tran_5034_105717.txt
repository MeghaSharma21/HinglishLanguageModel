 neeli chhatari vaale ek bhartiya televijan dharawahik he jo zee tivi par prasaarit hota he. iske nirmaataa ashvini dheer he. 
 dharawahik main ek aam aadmi bhagavaandas kii kahani he. vah ghar main apane pita, bivi, bacchon or offis main bos kii daant sunata rahata he.udhar, bos bhi bhagavaandas kii kaaryasheli ko lekar aae din khari-khoti sunaate hai. dharawahik main shivaay naamak paatr ko bhagwaan shiv bataaya gaya he. shivaay se bhagwaan das kii mulaakaat bos dwaara sunaae gaye taanon ke baad hoti he. offis main bos ke taane sunne ke baad vah gusse main ek ped par uski tasviir taangkar pathar martha he. us dauraan uski mulaakaat bhagwaan shankar se hoti he, jo use pathar maarane se rokte hai. bhagwaan ko apane saamne paakar bhagwaan das hairaan ho jaataa he or vahiin se bhagwaan das or bhole shankar ke bich dosti kaa safar shuru hota he. ve sidhe taur par uski madad to nahin karte par musiibat ke samay use rasta jarur dikhaate hai.
