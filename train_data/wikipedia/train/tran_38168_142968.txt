 swanvigyaan main, saath-saath aane vaale swanon ke yugma ko dwiswan kehete hai. iskaa upyog ek swan se duusare swan main sankraman ko record karne ke sandarbh main kiya jaataa he.
 nimnaankit chitra main P1, P2, aadi ek ke baad ek aane vaale swan hai. inke sangat D1-2, D2-3, aadi dwiswan hai.
 yadi kisi bhashaa main swanon kii sankhya P he to ganitiya siddhanth se nikaala ja saktaa he ki kul sambhav dviswanon kii sankhya P2 hogi. kintu dviswanon kii kul sankhya is saiddhanthik sankhya P2 se kaaphi kam hoti he kyonki sabhi bhashaaon main is baat kaa pratibandh hota he ki kis swan ke baad koun swan aa saktaa he or koun nahin.
 spani bhashaa main lagbhag 800 dwiswan hai jabaki german bhashaa main lagbhag 2,500. 
 dviswanon kaa mahatv vaak sanshleshan kii drushti se he. pehle se record kiye gayi dviswanon ko jodakar jo dhwani praapt hoti he vah us dhwani kii apeksha kaaphi acchhee hoti he jo keval swanon ko jodakar nirmit kii jaati he. embrola pariyojna dviswanon ke sanshleshan par hi aadhaarit he.
