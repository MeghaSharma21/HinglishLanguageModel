 dadhi vayask nar manushya ke munh main gaal, thodi or gale main baalo kii upaj ko kehete hai. vaise to dadhi keval vayask nar manushya ke hi hoti he lekin kabhi-kabhi hormone kii gadabadi ke kaaran yah bacchon or mahilaon ke munh main bhi ug aati he. dadhi shabd kii utapatti dadh shabd se huyi he jiskaa arth he pichhlaa jabada. kyonki bal dadh ke baahar yani gaal par ugte hai isliye ise dadhi kaha jaataa he.
