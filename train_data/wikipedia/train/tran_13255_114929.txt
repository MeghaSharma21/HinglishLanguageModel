  sharnaarthiyon ke liye nonsen antarrashtriya karyaalay kaa gathan 1930 main norge ke maanavtaavaadi rajanyik fridchof nonsen ke nidhan ke baad hua. nonsen raashtra sangh ke antargat maanviya saamaajik kaary main jude the. pratham vishwa yuddh ke baad unhone sambandhit visthapit peediton ke liye kaam kiya or 1922 main is kaary ke liye nobel shanti puraskaar jeeta. nonsen ke jivit kaal main ye sanghathan ek beuro ke rup main kaam karata tha. 1930 main dil ke daure se mrityu ke baad raashtra sangh ne ye kaary jari rakhane ke liye sanghathan kaa nirmaan kiya. is karyaalay kaa mukhya daftar geneva, switzraland main tha. 
 sanghathan ko turant germany, austria, checkoslovakia ke sharnaarthiyon ke liye kaary karnaa pada. par sanghathan ke paas zyada paisa nahin tha. san 1938 main sanghathan ko nobel shanti puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. isi saal sanghathan kaa vighatan bhi ho gaya.
