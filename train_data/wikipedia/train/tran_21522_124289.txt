 ghatshila jharkhand main jamshedpur ke paas sthit ek chota shahar he jo apane uranium, tanba or anya khanijon kii khan ke liye prasiddh he. yaheen paas main bhartiya uranium nigam kaa kaarkaana sthit he jo puure desh ke uranium kii jarurat puri karata he.
 bharat kii janaganana 2001 ke anusaar ghatshila kii jansankhya 37,850 he jisame 53% purush or 47% mahilaayein hai. ghatshila kii ausat sakshartaa 73% he jo bharat ke rashtriya sakshartaa - 59.5% se kaaphi adhik he. sakshar jansankhya main purush sakshartaa 79% tathaa mahila sakshartaa 65% he. ghatshila kii jansankhya kaa 11% chha: varsh se kam aayu ke bacchon kaa he.
