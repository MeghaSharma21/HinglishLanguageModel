 gurmeet ram rahim singh insaan haryana ke sirsa main sthit aadhyatmik sanstha deraa sachcha sauda ke pramukh the. deraa sachcha sauda kii sthapana 1948 main shah mastaana ji dwaara kii gai thi. gurmeet is sanstha ke tisare pramukh hai. inke kaarykaal main deraa kaa abhootpurv prachaar prasar hua or iske anuyaayiyon kii sankhya main kai guna vruddhi hui. gurmeet ram rahim singh ke netritv main deraa sachcha sauda main kai sakaaratmak kaary kiye gaye, nae nae prayog kiye gaye, vahiin ve hameshaa vivaadon main bhi bane rahe. vivaadon kii parinti 25 agast 2017 ko ek yon shoshan maamle main adaalat dwaara inhen doshi qaraar die jane ke rup main hui. is maamle main ram rahim ko 20 saal ke sashram karawas va 65 lakh rupaye jurmaane kii sajaa hui.
 gurmeet ram rahim singh siddhu mul ke punjabi jat he. swayan gurmeet ram rahim singh ke anusaar uskaa janm 15 agast 1967 ko mata naseeb cor or pita maghar singh ke yahan gurusar modiya gaanv main hua tha. yah parivaar deraa sachcha sauda kaa bhakt tha.
 deraa ke dwitiya pramukh shah satnam ji ne gurmeet singh ko apana uttaradhikari ghoshit kiya. sirsa ke deraa sachcha sauda kii kaman ram rahim ne 90 ke dashak main sanbhali thi. gaddi sanbhaalane ke baad ve bhi paramparanusaar satsang, pravachan aadi dene lage. inke netritv main dere ke bhakton kii sankhya main kaaphi vruddhi hui. aamtaur par vyaktigat aadhyatm par kendrit is sanstha ko inhone saamaajik rup se sakriy banaaya. inke dwaara kai prakaar ke sakaaratmak samjik kaary kiye gaye, yatha:
 ram rahim kaa pehla mujeec album 'hive lav charger' naam se 2014 main aayaa tha. gurmeet ram rahim singh ne 2015 main filmon main pravesh kiya tha. 5 filmon kaa bhi nirmaan kiya.
