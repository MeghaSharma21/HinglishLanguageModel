 babul kaa minar ek aakasmik myth he jo tora kii utpatti kii kitaab main vibhinn bhashaaon ke mul ko samajhane ke liye thi. kahani ke anusaar, mahaan pralay ke baad, ek hi bhashaa bolne vaali pidiyon kii sanyukt manushya-jaati purv se pravaas karke shinaar kii zamin aa gai. vahaan vah ek shahar or burj banane ke liye sahamat ho gaye; yah dekhakar parameshwar ne unki boli gadabada di, jisase vah ab ek-duusare ko samaj na kar saken or unako duniya bhar main bikher diya.
