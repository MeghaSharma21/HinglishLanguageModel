 alaay vaadi madhya asia ke kirgizstan desh ke osh praant ke dakshini bhaag main purv se pashchim disha main vistrut ek chaudee or shushk vaadi he. yah ghati purv-pashchim disha main 180 kimi lambi or uttar-dakshin disha main 40 kimi chaudee he or isaki ausat oonchaai 2500-3000 meter he. ghati ke uttar main alaay parvat hai jinaki dhalaane faraghna vaadi par ant hoti hai. alaay vaadi ke dakshin main alaay-paar parvat hai jo tajikistan ke saath lagi sarhad par sthit hai. purv main tounmurun darra he jahaan se china kii sarhad par sthit irkeshtam naam kii aakhri kirghiz basti he.
 alaay vaadi kii paristhitiyaan yahan basane waalon ke liye kaafii kathin hai. yahan se guzare ek yatri ne is vaadi kaa byora dete hue kaha ki 'bina naukariyon ke, kathor sardiyon or kheti ke liye karaab haalat main yahan jivan bahut mushkil he or yahan ke adhiktar purush anya jagahon par kaam dhundne ja chuke hai'.
