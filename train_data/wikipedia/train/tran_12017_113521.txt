 rajaraam tritiy, bhosle vansh ke kolhapur ke maharaj the. ve apane pita maharaj shahu ke baad ve pad par aaye or 1922 se 1940 tak rajgaddi par virajman rahe. ek udaar shaasha, vah apane rajya main daliton or shoshit jatiyon ke utthan main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaate the. unhone kolhapur ucch nyaayalay, aadhunik awaas vikaas, ek adhyatan jal-aapurti pranali, muft praathmik shiksha or ucchtar str kii mahila shiksha kii sthapana kii. unhen ek beti thi. vah ek dur ke rishte, shivaji saatvin dwaara safal hue the.
