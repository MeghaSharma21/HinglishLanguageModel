 
 mahaan chol mandir chol shaskaon dwaara dakshin bharat main banawaaye gayi the. yah mandir hai:
 bahadeshwar mandir ko unesco dwaara 1987 main vishwa dharohar sthal ghoshit kiya gaya tha. airavateshwar mandir, darasuram ko san2004 main is suchi main joda gaya tha. is sthal ko ab "mahaan jivit chol mandir" naam diya gaya he.
 
 brihadeshwar athwa brihadishwar mandir tamilnadu ke thanjore main sthit ek hindu mandir he jo 11veen sadi ke armbh main banaaya gaya tha. ise tamil bhashaa main brihadishwar ke naam se janaa jaataa he. iskaa nirmaan 1003-1010 i. ke bich chol shaasha rajaraaj chol 1 ne karavaaya tha. unke naam par ise rajrajeshwar mandir kaa naam bhi diya jaataa he. yah apane samay ke vishwa ke vishaltam sanrachanaon main gina jaataa tha. iske teraa manzile bhawan kii oonchai lagbhag 66 meter he. mandir bhagwaan shiv kii aradhana ko samarpit he.yah kala kii prayek shakha - vaastukala, pashaan va tamra main shilpankan, pratimaa vigyaan, chitrankan, nrity, sangeet, aabhushan evam utkeernkala kaa bhandar he. yah mandir utkeern sanskrit va tamil puralekh sulekhon kaa utkrisht udhaaharan hai. is mandir ke nirmaan kala kii ek visheshta yah he ki iske gumbad kii parchaai prithvi par nahin padati. shikhar par swarnakalash sthit he. jis pashaan par yah kalash sthit he, anumaanath: uskaa bhaar 2200 man he or yah ek hi pashaan se bana he. mandir main sthapit vishaal, bhavy shivlinga ko dekhne par unka vrihadeshwar naam sarvatha upyukt prathit hota he.mandir main pravesh karne par gopuram ke bheetar ek chaukor mandap he. vahaan chabutare par nandi ji virajman hai. nandi ji kii yah pratimaa 6 meter lambii, 2.6 meter chaudee tathaa 3.7 meter oonchi he. bharatvarsh main ek hi pathar se nirmit nandi ji kii yah duusari sarvaadhik vishaal pratimaa he. thanjore main anya darshneey mandir hai- tiruvorirur, gangapondcholpuram tathaa darasuram.
