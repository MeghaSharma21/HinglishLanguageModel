 musalmaan kaa matlab vah vyakti he jo islam main vishwaas rakhata ho. haalaanki musalamaanon ke aasthaa ke anusaar islam ishwar kaa dharm he or dharm hazarat mohammad se pehle maujuud tha or jo log allaah ke dharm kaa paalan karte rahe vah musalmaan hai. jaise quran ke anusaar hazarat ibrahim alaihissalam bhi musalmaan the. magar aajkal musalmaan kaa matlab use liya jaataa he jo hazarat mohammad laae hue deen kaa paalan karata ho or vishwaas rakhata ho. madhyakaalin muslim itihaaskaaron ne bharat ko hind athwa hindustan kaha he.
 musalmaan yah sweekaar karte hai ki allaah akelaa paravaradigaar he or hazarat mohammad allaah ke preshit hai. is tareeke se koi bhi vyakti islam main jaataa he. jab hazarat mohammad ko allaah kaa rasul maan le to us par vaazib ho jaataa he ki unki har baat par vishwaas rakhe or amal karne kii koshish kare. jaise unhone kaha ki mere baad koi nabi nahin or ve parameshwar ke antim rasul hai to is baat par vishwaas rakhana islam ke ekadhikaar main he. islam ke mukhya dharm jin par musalamaanon ke kisi samudaay main koi matbhed nahin, nimnlikhit hai
 uparokt baton par vishwaas karne ko musalmaan kehete hai. unke alaava anya matbhed feroii or raajnneetik hai.
