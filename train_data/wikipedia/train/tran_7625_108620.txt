 joseph furye francisi ganitagya the. unka janm oksaiyr main hua. aath varsh kii umr main hi ye anaath ho gaye the, parantu saubhagyavash apane hitaishiyon kii sahaayta se inhen ek sainik skool main pravesh mil gaya, jahaan inhone ganit ke adhyayan main aashaatheet safalataa praapt kii or sheeghra hi ek sainik skool main ganit ke professor ho gaye. france kii kranti main inhone sakriy bhaag liya or misr par akraman main bhi nepolian ke saath gaye. taduparant inhone pindon main taap ke vistaar par safal shoth kiye, jinka varnan inaki prasiddh pustak 'laa theori anaalithik da laa shaller' main he.ganitiya bhautik shaastra ke nirdhaarit seemaant ke maanvaale nirmeyon ke hal kii aadhunik vidhiyon ke liye, yah mul pustak he. isamen prasiddh 'phurye shrenee' kaa bhi varnan he. 'sankhyatmak sameekaran ke vishleshan' par bhi inhone mahatvapurn shodh kiye.
