 vaak baadha kisi manushya kii aisi sthiti ko kehete hai jisamen use aasaani se saadhaaran tarah se baatcheet karne main kathinaayee pesh aae. isamen haklaana, tutlana or anya prakaar kii vakbadhaayen shaamil hai. dhyaan den ki mahaz kisi ke bolne ke alag lahaze ko vakbadha nahin kaha ja saktaa. yadi koi vakbadha purna rup se yaa kaafii had tak kisi ko bolne main asamarth kare to use 'goongaapan' kaha jaataa he. vakbadhaayen maanvon main aam pai jaati hai - unke hone par bhi log safal va sukhi jivan ji sakate hai or bahut se upachaar bhi uplabdh hai. masalan adhyayan se paaya gaya he ki america ke 1% log, yani 30 lakh america, haklaate hai.
