 
 riginal super-50 2016-17 riginal super-50, westeindies cricket board ke deshon ke liye gharelu simit overon ke cricket pratiyogita ke 43 wein sanskaran he. toornament antigua or barbuda main yojit kiya ja rahaa he.
 das tiimon kii pratiyogita main bhaag le rahe hai - westeindies ke gharelu cricket ke chah niyamit tiimon, do vikaas dal westeindies kii under 19 cricket team or angreji kii ore kent county cricket club. kent toornament main khilne ke liye dablyooisibi se ek nimantran sweekaar kar liya he or yah pehali baar he ki kisi bhi angreji county team ek videshi gharelu toornament main hissaa liya tha. nimantran westeindies ke purv captain jimi adams kaa prabhaav he, jo sitambar 2016 tak, county ke pramukh coach ke liye kiya gaya tha, aanshik rup se kii vajah se tha.
