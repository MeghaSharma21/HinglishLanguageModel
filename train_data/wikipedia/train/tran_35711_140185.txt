 norman arnest borlog nobel puraskaar vijeta ek america krishivigyaani the, jinhen harith kranti kaa pita mana jaataa he. borlog un paanch logon main se ek hai, jinhen nobel shanti puraskaar, swatantraaa kaa raashtrapati padak or congress ke gold medal pradaan kiya gaya tha. iske alaava unhen bharat kaa doosraa sarvocch naagarik sammaan padam vibhushan pradaan kiya gaya tha. borlog kii khojon se duniya ke karodon logon kii jivan bachi he.
 unke naveen prayogon ne anaaj kii samasya se jujh rahe bharat sahit anek vikasshil deshon main harith kranti kaa pravartan karne main mahatvapurn yogadan kiya. 
 inhone 1970 ke dashak main mexico main bimariyon se lard sakane vaali gehun kii ek nai kism viksit kii thi. iske peeche unki yah samaj thi ki agar paudhe kii lambaai kam kar di jaae, to isse bachi hui urja uske bijon yani daanon main lagegi, jisase dana zyada badhegaa, lihaaja kul fasal utpaadan badhegaa. borlog ne chota daanav kahalaane vaale is kism ke beej or urvarak vibhinn deshon ko bheja, jinase yahaan kii kheti kaa puraa nakshaa hi badal gaya. unke kitnashak va rasinic khaadon ke atyadhik istemaal or jameen se zyada pani sokhne vaali fasalon vaale prayog kii paryaavaranvaadiyon ne kadi aalochana kii. ve duniya ko bhukhmaree se nijaat dilaane ke liye jin sanvardhit fasal ke paksh main bhi rahe. unka mat tha ki bhukh se marane kii bajaay jiem anaaj khaakar mar janaa kahin zyada acha he. paryaavaranvaadiyon ke aitraaj kaa bhi jawaab unhone yah kahkar diya ki agar kam jameen se zyada upaj lee jaati he, to isse prakruti kaa sangrakshan hi hota he.
