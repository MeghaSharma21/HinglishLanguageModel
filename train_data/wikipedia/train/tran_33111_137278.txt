 kysth paathshaala, elahabaad kaa ek purana evam pratishthit vidhyalay he. ganesh shankar 'vidhyaarthee', harivanshrai bachchan aadi yahan ke vidhyaarthee the. 
 isaki sthapana san 1873 main avadh ke prasiddh vakil munshi kaliprasad kulbhaskar dwaara hui thi. shri hanuman prasad is paathshaala ke pratham adhyaksh the. armbh main yah hai skool tha jo san 1895 main yah intermidiet college ban gaya. iske aarmabhik pradhanacharyon main ek the ramanand chatarji, jo baad main bengali punarjagran kii patrika "modern rivyuu" ke sansthaapak bane bane. 'vande maataram' yahan kii prarthana thi.
