 jisat-11 ek bhartiya sanchaar upgrah he. jise bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan dwaara viksit tathaa bhartiya rashtriya upgrah dwaara sanchalit kiya jaayegaa. yah upgrah desh main unnat doorsanchaar or dit sevaayen pradaan karegaa. jisat-11 puure desh ke liye prati second 10 gigabite kii gati se data sanchaarit kar saktaa he. iskaa prakshepan arian 5 raaket se 2017 ko guyana antariksh centre, france se hona he.
