  brijabhoomi shiv kumaar dwaara banaaya gaya ek brajabhasha chalchitr he jo varsh 1982 main pradarshit hua tha. yah brajabhasha kaa sarvapratham chalchitr he jisamen mukhya bhoomika main shiv kumaar,raja bundela, alka noopur, or bharti acharekar the. is chalchitr ke saath hi brajabhasha chalchitrapat kaa aarambh hua. yah chalchitr sadabahaar rahaa.
