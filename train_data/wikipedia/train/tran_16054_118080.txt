 pani kaa rang alag-alag pariveshon main bhinn bhinn hota he jisamen pani maujuud hota he. apekshaakrit kam maatra main pani berang dikhataa he. shuddh pani maamuli nila rang hota he jo motaai badhate badhate gaharaa nila ho jaataa he. pani kaa nila rang ek aantarik gun he jo ki chaynaatmak avasorana or safed roshni ke bikharane ke kaaran hota he. visarjeet tatv yaa nilambit ashuddhiyaan pani ko ek alag rang de sakate hai.
