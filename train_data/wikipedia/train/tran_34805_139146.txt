 chikan pox vericella zoster virus ke sankraman se hone vaali beemaari he. yah bahut hi sankramak hoti he or sankramit nisruth padaarthon ko saans ke saath andar le jane se failati he. isamen bukhaar aa jaataa he or sharir par dadora ho jaataa he, jisamen tej khujali hoti he. iske alaava twachaa main bacterial sankraman, nimonia or mastishk main sujan bhi chikanpox ke lakshan hai. bacchon or yuvaaon main is beemaari kaa khatra sabase zyada rahata he. chikanpox chune se bahut teji se failata he. chikitsak salaah dete hai ki chikan pox ke nivaaran ke liye 12 se 15 maheenon kii umr ke bich bacchon ko chikan pox kaa tikaa, or 4 se 6 varsh kii umr ke bich dusaraa tikaa lagava lenaa chaiye.yah tikaa chikan pox ke halke sankraman ko rokane ke liye 70 se 80 pratishat asardaar hota he or gambhir rup se sankraman ko rokane ke liye 95 pratishat asardaar hota he. isiilie haalanki kuch bacchon ne tikaa lagava liya hota hai phir bhi uname is rog se grasit hone ke lakshan saumy hote hai, banibast un bacchon ke jinhone yah tikaa nahin lagavaaya hota he.
