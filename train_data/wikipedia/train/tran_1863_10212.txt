 pandit ganpati sharma ek aryasamaji vidwaan the jo shastrarth kii bhartiya parampara ko aage badhaane ke liye prasiddh hai. 
 pan. ganpati sharma kaa janm 1873 i. main churu nagar nagar main hua tha. inke pita pan. bhaniram vaidya parashar gotreey pari brahman the inaki shiksha kaashi or kanpur aadi sthanon par hui. 22 varsh kii aayu tak aapane sanskrit vyakaran or darshanon kaa adhyayan kiya.. 
 shiksha ke uparaant paitruk sthaan churu lautakar ye maharishi dayanand ke shishy va rajasthan main vaidic dharm ke mahaan prachaarak shri kaluram ji joshi ke prabhaav se aryasamaji bane. aryasamaji banakar inhone vaidic dharm va samskruti kaa prachaar kaa kaary armbh kar diya.
 ishwar ke prati atoot shraddha or vishwaas pandit ganpati sharma ke jivan kaa aadhaar he.
