 
 prince andrew, york ke duk british shahi parivaar ke ek sadsy he. ve maharani elizabeth dwitiya or rajkumaar philip, edinburgh ke duk kaa doosraa betaa he. 1986 se unhen york ke duk kii upadi se janaa jaataa he, or ve united kingdom ke singhaasana par uttaradhikaar ke kram main chathe sthaan par he.
 prince andrew kaa janm buckingham pales, landan main hua tha. unhone hithardown priperetary skool, barkshaayar, england or gordonstown, scotland se apni praathmik shiksha lee. prince andru ne uniwarsity main padhane ke bajaay britania royal neval college main janaa pasand kiya. nausena main unhone fockland yuddh main seva kii he, or apane karier ko jari rakhaa, 1999 main ve ek commander or 2001 main maanad captain ban gaye.
 1986 main, prince andrew ne prince of wales ke polo prabandhak, ronaald ferguson, kii choti beti, sara ferguson se shaadi kii. is shaadi se unhen do betiyaan huin:rajkumaari biatris or rajkumaari ujini. andrew or sara 1992 main alag ho gaye, or may 1996 main unhone talaak de diya. prince andrew shahi parivaar ke ek sakriy sadsy he.
