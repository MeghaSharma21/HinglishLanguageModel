 apghaton ek prakaar kii kriya he, jisamen tatv alag alag sookshm bhaagon main vibhajit ho jate hai. jeevon main yah kriya unke mrityu ke pashchaat shuru ho jaati he. isamen mahatvapurn yogadan sookshm jeevon kaa hota he, jo kisi bhi jeev ke laash ko dhire dhire bahut hi chote tukdon main vibhajit kar use paristhitik tantra kaa hissaa bana lete hai.
