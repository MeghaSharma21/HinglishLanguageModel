 manushya ke taalu ke donon ore baadaam ke aakaar kii do granthiyaan hoti he, jinhen ham galgutica, tundika yaa tonsil kehete hai. in grathiyon ke rog ko galguticashoth kehete hai.
 yah rog pusjanak jeevanuon ke upsarg, pradhanat: malagolanu se hota he. shaareerik rog-pratirodhshakti kii durbalata, adhik parishram, dooshit vaataavaran main nivas tathaa dooshit jal evam dooshit doodh ke vyavhaar se galguticashoth ke lakshan utpann ho jate hai. ritu parivartan ke samay sheet lag jane se bhi raag ho jane kaa bhay rahata he.
 is rog main galguticaay badi evam raktavarna dikhalaai detii hai. shoth kii avasthaa main fever, kanth main vednaa, mukh main thook adhik aana, khaansi, shirishul, bhojan nigalane main ksht, shwasan durgadhit aadi lakshan upasthit rahete hai.
 galgutikaaon ke prishth par pitvarna ke peeb ke dhabbe dikhalaai dete hai. yadi rog kaa uchit upachaar nahin kiya jaataa to galguticoon kii yah avasthaa sthaayee ho jaati he or thode thode samay ke antar par ye ksht dene lagati hai.
 ugr avasthaa main sulpha aushadhon kaa upyog karne se laabh hota he. potacium paramanganet ke tanu vilyan, yaa lavanjal kaa garaara karnaa chahie. dirghasthayee avasthaa main shalyakarm dwaara galgutikaaon ko nikalava dena chahie.
