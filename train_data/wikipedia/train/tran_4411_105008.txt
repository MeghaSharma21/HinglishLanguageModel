 jin rasinic abhikriyaaon main carbonic yogic bhaag lete hai unhen carbonic abhikriyaayein kehete hai. dahan sabase puraani carbonic abhikriya he. isi tarah vasa kaa sabunikaran karke sabun bananaa bhi pracheen carbonic abhikriya he. aadhunik carbonic rasayan kaa janm vohalar dwaara 1828 main uria ke nirmaan se hua. bahut se maanav-nirmit rasayanon jaise aushadhi, plastic, bhojan main milaaye jane vaale padaarth, vastra aadi carbonic abhikriyaaon par hi nirbhar hai.
 carbonic rasayan main mukhya rup se abhikriyaaon ke naam unke avishkaarak ke naam par rakhi jaati he.
