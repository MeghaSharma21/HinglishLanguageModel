 chandel abhilekhon main purvavarti naresh vaakpati ke putr jaishakti kaa ullekh aata he. yah 'jejjak' or 'jeja' ke naam se bhi prasiddh tha. iskaa rajyakaal sambhavat: 9veen shatabdi ke tritiy charan main tha.
 vah swatantra shaasha nahin tha kintu us kaal kii raajnneetik avyavashtha kaa laabh uthaakar isane apni shakti ko drudh kiya. praaia: vidvan ise pratihaaron kaa samant batalaate hai. abhilekhon main kabhi kabhi chandel raajaaon kii talika jaishakti ke naam se hi prarambh hoti he. kadachit usee ke samay main pehali baar vartmaan khajuraho ke sameep kii bhoomi ek pruthak bhukti ke rup main sangathit hui or jaishakti ke naam par hi vah 'jejac bhukti' kahalaai. usne apni putree natta kaa vivaah kalchuri naresh cokkall pratham ke saath kiya tha jo sambhavat: uski raajnneetik mahatvakankshaon se prerit tha. abhilekhon main uske naam ke saath uske anuj vijayashakti kaa naam bhi sambhddha rahata tha jo baad main singhaasana kaa adhikari hua.
