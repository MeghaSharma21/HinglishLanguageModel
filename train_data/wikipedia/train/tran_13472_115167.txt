 aatishbaazi main prayukt pataake evam anya yuktiyaan manoranjan evam khushi manaane ke liye prayukt kam shakti ke visphotak hote hai. aatishbaajee main prayukt yuktiyaan bhinn-bhinn prakaar kii hotin hai. ye multah chaar tarah ke prabhaav utpann karatin hai : tej dhwani, prakaash, dhunaa tathaa tairta hua padaarth.
