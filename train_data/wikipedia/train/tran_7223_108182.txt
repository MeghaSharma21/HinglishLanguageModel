 bei nadi yaa bei jiang dakshini china kii ek nadi he jo prasiddh moti nadi kii uttari upanadi he. chini bhashaa main 'bei' kaa matlab 'uttari' or 'jiang' kaa matlab 'nadi' hota he. yah uttari guangdong praant main ek 63 kimi lambi nadi he jo moti nadimukh mandal main pahunchakar apana pani dakshin china sagar main bhej detii he. moti nadi kii anya do mukhya upanadiyaan shi nadi or dong nadi hai.
