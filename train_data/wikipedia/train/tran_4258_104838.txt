 ailexandria kaa roshnighar sampaadan pracheen vishwa kaa saat aashcharyo main se ek he. is roshnighar kaa nirmaan isa se 3 shatabdi purv misr ke dwip feros par ek pratik chihn ke rup main karavaaya gaya tha. vibhinn sotro ke anusaar isaki unchai 115 meter se 135 meter ke bich rahi hogi.
