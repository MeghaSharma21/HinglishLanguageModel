 dilli-multan rajmarg bharat kii rajdhani dilli ko pakistan ke punjab praant main sthit multan shahar se jodne vaala ek rajmarg he. iskaa nirmaan dilli sultanate ke samraat sher shah suri ne multan, dilli or unke bich ke kshetron main vyaper or yatayaat behatar karne ke liye karavaaya tha. har chand mile par chowkiyaan sthit thim jahaan ghode taiyaar rahete the, jinhen ek sthaan se duusare sthaan tak sandesh or dak jaldi pahunchavaane ke liye prayog kiya jaataa tha. is vyavastha se dilli raaj darbar se aadesh or sandesh multan tak kuch dinon main hi pahunch jaaya karte the.
 pakistan main is sadak kaa abhi bhi yahi naam he, lekin bharat main yah naam kam prayog hota he. pakistani taraf sange-milon par punjab yatayaat vibhaag yahi naam prayog karata he. pakistani punjab main dilli-multan maarg paakpattan, aarifavaala, gaggu, buurevaala or vihadi se guzarataa he.
