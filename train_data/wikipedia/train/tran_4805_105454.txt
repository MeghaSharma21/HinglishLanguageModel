 gurudutt singh bhupati, amethi ke raja the. ye bandhul gotreey suryavanshi kushvaha kshatriya the. inke pita raja himmatbahadoor singh swayan kavi evam kaviyon ke ashrayadata the. is vansh ke praaia: sabhi naresh vidvan the or guniyon kaa yathochit sammaan karne main ruchi rakhte the.
 hindi ke poshan main yah rajvansh sadaa agraganya rahaa he. is darbar main hindi ke labdhpratishth kavi malik mohammad jaaysi, sukhdev mishra, kalidas trivedi, udaynath kavindra, dulah or savansh shukla ko sasamman aashray praapt tha. rajkaarya main atyant vyast rahete hue bhi gurudutt singh kavyanirmaan main dattachitt rahete the. ye nirbhik yoddha bhi the. avadh ke nawab saadat khan se anban ho jane par usne inkaa ramnagar kaa garh gher liya. uske sammukh markat karte hue ye baahar nikal gaye. kuch hi varshon main badi veerta se unhone pun: apane garh par adhikaar kar liya.
 sanwat 1791 main inhone 'bhupati satsai' kaa nirmaan kiya. arth evam bhav romaniyata kii drushti se satsai parampara main iskaa mahatvapurn sthaan he. 'bihari satsai' kii hode main bhupati ne isaki rachana kii he. kavi ke lokgyaan, shastragyan tathaa kavyagyaan kaa samanvit rup isamen parilakshith hota he. iske atirikt kanthabharan, sursaratnakar, rasadeep, rasaratnavali naamak granth bhi inke rache hue batalaaye jate hai. inke naam se sambanddh 'bhashaa bhagwat' vastut; inkaa granth nahin he. yah itawanivasee unaayon kysth lekharaj ke putr bhupati kavi kii rachana he. gurudutt singh bhupati kaa rachanakaal sanwat 1788 se 1799 tak he.
