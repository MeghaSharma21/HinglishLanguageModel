 montescue  france ke ek rajnatik vichaarak, nyayavid tathaa upanyaaskaar the. unhone shaktiyon ke pruthakkaran kaa siddhanth diya. ve france main gyanoday ke prabhavi or prakhyat pratinidhi maane jate hai.
 montescue kii itihaas, darshan, kanoon or raajnneeti main gahari ruchi thi jiske badaulat ve ek vyavsaayee, nyayavid, raajnneetijna, upanyaaskaar or raajnneetik vichaarak ke rup main vikhyath hai. unhen pracheen raajnneeti itihaas kaa acha gyaan tha jiskaa upyog karte hue unhone 1734 main roman logon ke utthan or patan par lekh likhe. rajnatik pragati ke adhyayan ke liye unhone sampurna europe kii yatra kii. unhone england main do varsh bitaye or jon lock or british samvidhan se kaaphi prabhavit rahe. unhone yahaan rah kar chaar niabandh likhe. unka 'da spirit of loz' naamak niabandh 1748 main chapa or sampurna europe main kaaphi charchit hua rahaa. uski vyakhya karte unhone vyavhaarya or swatantra rajtantr main satta vibhaajan kaa siddhant diya jiske liye ve aaj tak prasiddh hai.
