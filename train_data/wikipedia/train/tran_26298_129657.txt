 tuluv vijaynagar samrajya kaa teesraa rajvansh tha.
 tuluv rajvansh ek bhartiya rajvansh tha tathaa yah bharat kaa teesraa rajvansh tha inke samraaton ne vijaynagar samrajya par raaj kiya tha. tuluv rajvansh kii sthapana mul rup se tateey karnataka ke dakshini bhaagon par shashan karne vaale mukhiya banton dwaara kii gayi thi. inako tulu nadu ke naam se bhi bulaayaa jaataa he. kuch logon kaa maananaa ​​he ki ve tulu nadu ke tulu bhashi kshetra ke the or inaki maatrubhaasha pracheen tulu bhashaa thi kyonki vansh kaa naam "'tuluv'" praapt hua he.narsa nayak jo krishnadevaraay ke pita the or saath hi andhrapradesh ke chandragiri ke rajyapal bhi the. raja krishnadevaraay ne apni ek lokapriy pustak amuktamalyada main likhaa he ki yah ek telugudesh he or yahaan shakti suluv rajvansh ke baad ai he. tuluv rajvansh dakshin bharat ke vijaynagar samrajya ke sabase shaktishaalee rajvansh the. ye nagvanshi kshatriya vansh ke the.nagvanshi ke vanshaj nagaraj vasuki kii puja karte hai.
 nagaraj ek puranic kathaon ke anusaar saanp the. itihaas ke anusaar tuluv rajvansh kaa sabase shaktisaali or lokapriy raja krishnadevaraay the jinhone is vansh kaa bahut vikaas kiya tha.kuch logon kaa maananaa he ki yah kaal arthath rajvansh telegu sahitya kaa sunaharaa kaal mana jaataa he. in par kai telegu sanskrit ,kannada tathaa tamil kaviyon ne rachanaaen kii hai.
