 banaras main kedaar ghat ke paas kedareshwar mandir he. yah mandir 17veen shtabdi main aurangzeb ke kahar se batch gaya tha. isi ke sameep gauri kund he. isi ko aadi manikarnika yaa mul manikarnika kaha jaataa he.
 manikarnika ghat ke sameep vishnu charanpaduka he. ise marble se chinhit kiya gaya he. ise kaashi kaa pavithratam sthaan kaha jaataa he. anushruti he ki bhagwaan vishnu ne yahaan dhyaan lagaaya tha. isi ke sameep manikarnika kund he. mana jaataa he ki bhagwaan shiv kaa mani tathaa devi parvati kaa karnaphul is kund main gira tha. chakrapushkararni ek chaukor kund he. iske chaaro ore lohe kii railing bani hooi he. ise vishwa ko pehla kund mana jaataa he.
 yahaan kaa kaali bhairav mandir bhi prasiddh he. yah mandir goduliya chowk se 2 kilometre uttar-purv main toun haul ke paas sthit he. isamen bhagwaan shiv kii raudra murtti sthapit he. isi ke najadik bindu madhav ji kaa mandir he. yah mandir bhagwaan vishnu ko samarpit he.
