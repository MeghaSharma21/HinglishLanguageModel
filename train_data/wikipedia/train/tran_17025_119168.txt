 ativruddhi kaa matlab apane ghatak koshikaon ke badhane ke kaaran ek ang yaa utak ke aaytan main vruddhi ho jaati he.yah ativardhan se alag he, is main koshikaon lagbhag ek hi aakaar rahete hai lekin sankhya main vruddhi ho jaati he. ativruddhi or hyperplasia do alag prakriya he, ve aksar ek saath hote hai. jaise ki garbhavastha ke dauraan hormonal prerit prasar or garbhashay kii koshikaon kii vruddhi ke maamle main.
 utkendri atibruddhi ek prakaar he atibruddhi jahaan deevaaron or kaksh khokhla ang kaa vikaas se guzarate he jaha aakaar or maatra badha rahe hai. yah dil ke baayen ventrical ke liye vishesh rup se lagu kiya jaataa he.
