 rahali sagar jila mukhyalay ke dakshin-purv main kareeb 42 kimi kii duuri par sunar or dehar nadiyon ke sangam par sthit he. bundelkhand main pracheen mandir bahutayat main paae jate hai. rahali bhi iskaa apwaad nahin or iske aasapaas kai sundar mandir hai. yahaan se do kimi dur pangalpur main dhai sau saal purana pandrinath kaa mandir he. yahaan se kareeb 5 kimi dur tikhi main tiki toria kaa mandir he. yahaan vaastukala kii drushti se ekmaatr mahatvapurn vastu ek pracheen mandir ke chabutare kaa ek bhaag he.
