 uttam kumaar  ek bangla or hindi filmon kaa ek prasiddh abhinayta the.
 bangla filmon ke mahanayake uttam kumaar kaa janm hua tha 3 sitambar 1926 ko bhavanipur kolkata ke girish mukherjee road sthit apane pushtaini makaan main. kolkata ke hi south sabarban skool se schuli shiksha ke baad kolkata vishvavidhyalay ke goyenka college of commers and bijness administration main daakhila liya lekin ve apni padhai puri nahin kar paae or unhone kalakatta port trust main clerk kii naukari jwoin kar lee. abhinayta, nirmaataa, nirdeshak uttam kumaar kii bataur nayak pehali film thi nitin bose nirdeshit drushtidaan. suchithra sen ke saath unki jodi khub pasand kii gai. suchithra ke saath unki saptapadi, pauthe holo deree, haarano sur, chawa paava, bipasha, jivan trishna or sagarika jaisi filmein behad lokapriy raheen.
 bangla ke saath-saath unhone paanch hindi filmon main bhi abhinay kiya. 1967 main choti si mulaaqaat, 1975 main amaanush, 1 977 main anand ashram 1979 main qitaab or dooriyaan.
 unka asli naam tha arun kumaar chatterjee. kolkata main haajra anchal main unke naam par uttam theatre he tathaa taaliganj tramdipo ke samaksh unka vishaal stachu sadak ke chaunk par lagaaya gaya he. or ab vigat varsh 2009 main taaliganj metro stesan kaa namakaran mahanayake uttam kumaar ho gaya he.
 unke ekmaatr putr gautam kumaar chatarji ek vyavsaayee the. filmon main unki ruchi nahin thi lekin uttam ke pautra gaurav bangla filmon main abhinay kar rahe hai. unki khwaahish thi ki abhinay karte hue unka dam nikle or hua bhi aisa hi. 1980 main "o go bodhu shundari" kii shooting ke dauraan hridayaghat se unka nidhan ho gaya.
