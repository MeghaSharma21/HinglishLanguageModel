 do vastuon ke bich main ooshmaa ke pravaah main avrodh ko ooshmaa avarodhan kehete hai. ooshmaa ke rodhan ke liye vishesh rup se abhikalpit prakramon, vishesh aakar tathaa upyukt padaarthon kaa chunaav bahut jaruri he.
 do alag-alag taap vaali vastuon ke sidhe sampark main aane par unke bich ooshmaa kaa antaran avashya hoga. kintu in donon vastuon ke bich ooshmaarodhi padaarth pravisht kara dene se ooshmaa kaa pravaah pehle se kam hoga. kitna kam hoga yah is baat par nirbhar karata he ki ooshmaarodhi padaarth kii motaai kitni he, uski ooshmaa chaalakata kitni kam he aadi. isi prakaar vikiran dwaara ooshmaa ke pravaah ko kam karne ke liye kuch anya tareeke apanaae jate hai.
 kisi padaarth kii ooshmaa chaalakata jitani kam hoti he, vah utana hi acha ooshmaarodhi hota he. ooshmaa ejiniyari ke kshetra main ooshmaarodhi padaarth ke anya gun hai, ghanatv tathaa vishisht ooshmaa.
