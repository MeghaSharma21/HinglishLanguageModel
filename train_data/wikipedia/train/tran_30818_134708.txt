 dikshabhumi bharat main buddha dharm kaa ek pramukh centre he. yahaan buddha dharm kii punarutthan hua he. maharashtra rajya kii uparajdhani nagpur shahar main sthit is pavitra sthaan par bodhisattva dow bhimrao ambedkar ji ne 14 oktober 1956 ko pehle mahasthvir chandramni se buddha dhamm diksha lekar apane 5,00,000 se adhik anuyaayion ko buddha dharm kii diksha di thi. trisharan, panchasheel or apni 22 pratigyaayen dekar dow ambedkar ne hindu daliton kaa dharmaparivartan kiya. agle din phir 15 oktober ko 3,00,000 logon ko buddha dhamm kii diksha di or swayan bhi phir se dikshith hue. desh tathaa videsh se har saal yahaan 25 lakh se adhik ambedkarwaadi or buddha anuyaayi aate hai. har saal 14 oktober ko yahaan hajaaron kii sankhya main log buddha dharm paravartit hote rahete hai. yahaan 14 oktober 2015 main 50,000 dikshith hue hai. 14 oktober 2016 main 20,000 or 25 oktober 2016 ko manusmriti dahan diwas ke upalaksh main 5,000 obisi logon ne buddha dharm kii diksha lee he.
 maharashtra sarkaar ne dikshabhumi ko 'a' varg paryaton kshetra kaa darja diya he. nagpur shahar ke sabhi dharmik va paryaton kshetron main yah pehla sthal he, jise 'e' class kaa darja hasil hua he.
