 dakshini china sagar china ke dakshin main sthit ek seemaant sagar he. yah prashanth mahaasaagar kaa ek bhaag he, jo singapore se lekar taiwan kii khadi lagbhag 35,00,000 varg kimi main failaa hua he. paanch mahasaagaron ke baad yah vishwa ke sabase bade jalakshetron main se ek he. is sagar main bahut se chote-chote dweep hai, jinhen sanyukt rup se dweepasmooh kaha jaataa he. sagar or iske in dwipon par, iske tat se lagate vibhinn deshon kaa samprabhutaa kii daavedaari he. in daavedaariyon ko in deshon dwaara in dwipon ke liye prayukt hone vaale naamon main bhi dikhaai detii he.
