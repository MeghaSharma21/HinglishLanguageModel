 manushyon kii anuvanshiki main matruvansh samooh oaks yaa mitocandria-diene hapalogrup X ek matruvansh samooh he. yah matruvansh zyaadaatar europe, pashchimi asia or uttari africa main or kuch mul america adivasee samudaayon main paaya jaataa he. europe, madhya purv or uttari africa kii lagbhag 2% aabaadi is matruvansh kii vanshaj he.
 vaigyanikon ne andaaza lagaaya he ke jis stri ke saath is matruvansh kii shuruyaat hui thi vah aaj se 30,000 saal pehle iran-iraq ke paas kahin pashchimi asia main rahati thi. unke liye yah ek paheli he ke yah matruvansh uttari america main kaise ja pahunchaa jabaki isaki shuruyaat dur pashchimi asia main hui thi. isaki soch ab yah he ke is matruvansh kii vanshaj mahilaain madhya asia ke altaai parvat shrankhla ke ilaake se hoti hui pehle siberia or phir alaska ke zariye uttari america main daakhil huin. yaad rahe ke haalanki matruvansh bataane vaale mitocandria mardon or auraton donon main hoten hai, santanon ko yah keval apni maataaon se hi milte hai.
 angrezi main "vansh samooh" ko "hapalogrup", "pitruvansh samooh" ko "waahe chromosome hapalogrup" or "matruvansh samooh" ko "amtidiene hapalogrup" kehete hai.
