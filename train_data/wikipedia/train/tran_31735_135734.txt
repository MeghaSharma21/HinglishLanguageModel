 shah abdul hamid, ek bangladeshi rajaneta the. ve bangladesh avami lig se sambandhit the. unka janm 1900 ko rangappur jita ke gobindganj ke khalsee gaanv main hua tha. unke pita va mata kaa naam, kramshah haji abdul gafoor shah or rahima khatun tha. ve april 10, 1972 se may 1, 1972 tak bangladeshi sansad ke adhyaksh rahe the.
