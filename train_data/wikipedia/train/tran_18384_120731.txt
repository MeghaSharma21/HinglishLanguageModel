 maharashtra main buddha dharm rajya kaa ek bada dharm he. maharashtra bharat kaa sabase zyada buddha aabaadi vaala rajya he. buddha dharm maharashtra kii samskruti kaa ek mahatvapurn hissaa he. satvaahan kaal ke dauraan maharashtra main buddha dharm kaa prasar or prasar bahut bade paimaane par hua tha. naag logon ne dharm prasar ke liye apana jivan daav par lagaaya tha. hajaaron buddha guphaayen murtiyon banaai gai hai. siddhaon ke madhyam se naathon tak or naathon se warkari sampradaay tak buddha dharm failata gaya. saatvin shatabdi tak maharashtra main buddha dharm vyapak rup se prachlit tha.
 2011 main bhartiya janaganana ke anusaar, bharat main 84,42,972 buddha the or unamem sein sabase zyada 65,31,200 yani 77.36% buddha maharashtra rajya main the. janaganana ke anusaar, maharashtra main hindu dharm or islam ke baad buddha dharm maharashtra kaa teesraa sabase bada dharm he, jo maharashtra kii kul jansankhya kaa 6% he. bharat ke kul dharmaparavartit baudhon kii sankhya 73 lakh hai, unamem se lagbhag 90% maharashtra main hai. maharashtra main aabaadi main 12% vaala puraa mahaar samudaay buddha dharmavalambee hai, mahaar evam aupchaarik buddha in do buddha samoohon kii sankhya maharashtra main 1 karod se adhik hai. 1956 main, bhimrao ambedkar ne apane lakhon anuyaayiyon ko buddha dhamm kii diksha di thi. yah diksha samaaroh dikshabhumi nagpur main hua tha. maharashtra main vidarbha, marathavada evam kokan yahan ke dalit samaaj ne isame bade paimaane par bhaag liya. isi vajah se bharat main pramukh rup se maharashtra main baudhon kii sankhya adhik hui he.
 maharashtra main buddha dharm kaa prasar or prasar saatvahan kaal ke dauraan bade paimaane par hua he, jisamen naag logon kaa yogadan mahatvapurn he. jab pracheen vaastu or pracheen lekhan kii khoj kii gai, tab pataa chalaa kii saatvin shatabdi tak maharashtra main buddha dharm vyapak rup se prachlit tha. percy brown is vidwaan ke anusaar, bharat main murtiyon main se aadhi se adhik buddha murtiyon paayi jaati hai, isse maharashtra main buddha kaal kii lokapriyata dikhaai detii he. siddhaon ke madhyam se naathon tak or warkari sampradaay ke madhyam se buddha dharm failata gaya. saatvin shatabdi tak maharashtra main bade paimaane par buddha dharm kaa paalan kiya jaataa tha. uske baad musalmaan evam hindu shaskaon ke buddha prati nakaaratmak krition se buddha dharm ke anuyaayi kam hone lage, baudhon par hamale evam unka virodh hota rahaa. aadhunik bharat tak buddha dharm anuyaayi maharashtra main 1% se kam rah gayi.
 maharashtra main bhimrao ambedkar pramukh buddha netaa the, jinhone bharat main evam maharashtra main buddha dharm ko punarjivit karne main mahatvapurn yogadan diya. san 1950 ke dashak main ambedkar buddha dharm ke prati aakarshith hue or buddha bhikshuon va vidwaanon ke ek sammelan main bhaag lene ke liye srilanka gayi. 1954 main ambedkar ne myanmar kaa do baar daura kiya; duusari baar wo rangoon me tisare vishwa buddha faillowship ke sammelan main bhaag lene ke liye gayi. 1955 main unhone 'bhartiya buddha mahasabha' yaa 'buddhist socity of india' kii sthapana kii. unhone apane antim prasiddh granth, 'da buddha and hiz dhamm' ko 1956 main puraa kiya. yah unki mrityu ke pashchaat san 1957 main prakashit hua. 14 oktober 1956 ko nagpur shahar main dow bhimrao ambedkar ne khud or unke samarthakon ke liye ek aupchaarik saarvajanik dharmantaran samaaroh kaa aayojan kiya. pratham dow ambedkar ne apni patnimensavita ambedkar evam kuch sahyogiyon ke saath myanmar ke bhikshu mahasthvir chandramni dwaara paaramparik tareeke se triratn or panchasheel ko apanaate huye buddha dharm grahan kiya. iske baad unhone apane 5,00,000 anuyaayio ko triratn, panchasheel or 22 pratigyaayen dete hue navyan buddha dharm main parivartit kiya. ambedkar ne dusre din 15 oktober ko bhi vahaan apane 2 se 3 lakh anuyaayiyon ko buddha dhamm kii diksha di, yah ve anuyaayi the jo 14 aktubar ke samaaroh main nahin pahuch paye the yaa der se pahuche the. ambedkar ne nagpur kii "bhoomi" main kareeb 8 lakh logon buddha dharm kii "diksha" di, isliye yah bhoomi dikshabhumi naam se prasiddh hui. tisare din 16 oktober ko ambedkar chandrapur gayi or vahaan bhi unhone kareeb 3,00,000 apane anuyaayiyon ko buddha dhamm kii diksha di. ambedkar ne maharashtra main teen bade dharm parivartan samaaroh kiye, or keval teen din main ambedkar ne swayan 11 lakh se adhik logon ko buddha dharm main parivartit kar vishwa ke baudhon kii sankhya 11 lakh badhaa di or bharat main buddha dharm ko punarjivit kiya. iske baad ve nepal main chauthe vishwa buddha sammelan me bhaag lene ke liye kathmandu gayi. unhone apni antim pandulipi buddha or karl marks ko 2 disambar 1956 ko puraa kiya.
 san 2011 main, maharashtra main kul baudhon kii sankhya 1 karod se adhik hai, jo maharashtra kii aabaadi kaa 9 se 11% hissaa hai.
 san 1951 main, maharashtra main keval 2,489 buddha the, dow ambedkar ne 14 oktober 1956 ko kiye hue samuhik dharm parivartan ke baad san 1961 main yah baudhon sankhya 1,15,991% se badhakar 27,89,501 ho gai thi.
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar, bharat main baudhon kii sankhya 84,42,272 hai, or inamen se 65,31,200 buddha akele maharashtra rajya main hai. maharashtra main buddha dharmik samudaay bharat kaa sabase bada dharmaparivartit buddha samudaay he. vidarbha ke buldhaana, akola, washim, amravati, wardha, nagpur, bhandara, gondia, gadchiroli, chandrapur or yavatmal jilon main baudhon kaa ucchtam anupaat hai. in 11 jilon main 65 lakh baudhon main se lagbhag 30 lakh buddha hai. evam iname se aath jilon main unki aabaadi 12 se 15% he. akola main 18% baudhon kaa sabase bada anupaat he. gondia, gadchiroli or yavatmal jilon main anusuchit janajatiyon kii sankhya adhik he or baudhon kii sankhya 7 se 10% he. marathavada ke nanded, hingoli, parbhani, jaalnaa or aurangabad jilon main 12 lakh buddha hai. pehle ke teen jilon main unka hissaa 10% se adhik he, jabaki hingoli kii kul aabaadi 15% buddha he. thane, mumbai upnagar, mumbai, raigarh, pune, satara or ratnagiri in maharashtra pashchim ke jilon main 18 lakh se adhik buddha hai. iname se ratnagiri or mumbai upnagareey jila ke alaava anya jilon main buddha jansankhya 5% se kam he. mumbai upnagar or ratnagiri kii aabaadi kramash: 5% or 7% buddha hai.
 anusuchit jatiyon main buddha dharm teji se bad rahaa he. san 2001 main, bharat main 41.59 lakh buddha log anusuchit jati se the. 2011 main, yah aankada 38% badhkar 57.56 lakh ho gaya. desh main kul anusuchit jati ke baudhon main se 52,04,284 akel maharashtra rajya main hai. yah maharashtra kii kul buddha aabaadi kaa 79.68% hissaa he. jabaki maharashtra kii anusuchit jati kii kul 1,32,75,898 aabaadi hai or unamem 39.20% buddha he. maharashtra main buddha dharmavalambi anusuchit jatiyon kii aabaadi main 60% kii vruddhi hui he.
