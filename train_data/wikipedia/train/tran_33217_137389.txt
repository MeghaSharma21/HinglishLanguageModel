  stoffhome syndrome ek aisi sthiti hoti he jab aguva hone vaale vyakti ko apane apaherta ke saath sahanubhuti ho jaati he. ye kaid ke dauraan astitv ke liye ek ranneeti kaa rup mana jaat hai. 
 niles bejrot ne is shabd ko gadha jo kii ek swedish aparadhvigyaani or manochikitsak the. agast 1973 main yaan-erik olson ne stoffhome kii ek bank creditbancan ke chaar karmchaariyon ko bandhak ke rup main liya. stoffhome police se samjhauta vaarta ke dauraan unhone apane dost clark olofasan kii jail se rihaai maangi taaki wo uski madad kar sake. unhone bandhakon ko bank kii tijori main chah dinon tak kaid kar rakhaa tha. jab bandhakon ko reeha kar diya gaya, to unamem se kisi ne bhi adaalat main bandi banaanewaalon ke khilaaf gavaahi nahin di; or asal main baad main ve unhe milane jail bhi jate rahe.
