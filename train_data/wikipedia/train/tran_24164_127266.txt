 foodistaan ek televijan khaanaa game sho he. vah enditivi good times or jeeyo tivi dwaara utpaadan kiya jaataa he. sho ek pratiyogita main hindustan or pakistan ke sabase achche sheshas ek duusare ke kilaaf daalata he. apni vyaktigat vyanjanon samaan hai, lekin vahaan do vyanjanon ke bich kuch matbhedon ke hai. sho ne 26 episod prasaarit. samaapan manish mehrotra bharat se ​​or posta aagaa pakistan se ke bich main ladaa gaya tha, manish mehrotra hindustan se foodistaan jeet gaya.
 pratiyogiyon donon hindustan or pakistan kaa pratinidhitiv kiya or kuch prasiddh hotel samoohon se swaagat kiya.
