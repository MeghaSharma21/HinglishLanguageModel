 jon ronald raul tolkin angrezi bhashaa ke ek mashhoor lekhak or kahanikar the. wo ek roman catholoc british the. unke sabase prasiddh kaary unka upanyaas samooh he, jisamen da hobit, da lord of da rings or da silmerilon shaamil hai. hollywood kii ab tak kii sabase mashhoor filmon main se ek filmi shrunkhlaa da lord of da rings unheen ke upanyaas kaa filmakan he.
 tolkin germanik, anglo sackson or norse mithkon se bahut prabhavit the or wo british logon ke liye ek acchhee puranic kahani likhnaa chaahate the. apni javaani main wo pratham vishwayuddh main british sipaahi ban kar lade the or unako apane kai doston ke yuddh main maut kaa dukh sahanaa padaa tha. in baton kii jhalak tolkin ke kaaryon main bhi dikhti he. yuddh ke baad unhone ek vrahat kaalpanik kahani likhnaa shuru kiya, jisane sabase pehle da hobit naam ke upanyaas kaa rup liya. uskaa silsilaa da lord of da rings main jari rahaa. unki maut ke baad unke putr christopher tolkin ne silsilaa jari rakhte hue kuch anyupanyas bhi prakashit kiye.
