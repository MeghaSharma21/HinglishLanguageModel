 dajala nadi, jise tigris nadi bhi kehete hai, turki ke toros parvaton ke dakshinpurvi bhaag se nikalakar turki tathaa iraq main dakshin dakshin-purv kii ore 1,840 kimi tak bahane ke pashchaat furaat nadi main kurana naamak sthaan par milti he. is sangamasthal se ye donon nadiyaan shautil arab naam se 192 kimi dakshin purv main bahakar faras kii khadi main girti hai. dajala nadi apane upree bhaag main tivrgami he, at: ise yahan keval halaki naavon dwaara paar kiya jaataa he. iske tat par basra, baghdad or mosul pramukh nagar sthit hai.
