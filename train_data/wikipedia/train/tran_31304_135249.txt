 sukendrik yaa eukeriot ek jeev ko kaha jaataa he jisaki koshikaon main jhilliyon main band asaral dhaanche hon. sukendrik or akendrik koshikaon main sabase bada antar yah hota he ki sukendrik koshikaon main ek jhilli se ghira hua kendrak hota he jiske andar anuvanshik saamaan hota he. jeevigyaan main sukendrik koshikaon vaale jeevon ke texon ko 'sukendrik' yaa 'eukerioti' kehete hai.
 unani bhashaa main 'yu' kaa matlab 'acha' or 'kerri' kaa matlab 'beej' yaa 'gari' hota he. eukeriot koshikaon main ek spasht centre hota he isliye unhen 'acha beej' yaa 'eukeriot' kaha jaataa he. sanskrit or unani donon hind-europea bhashaayen hai, isliye unamem bahut se sajaateey shabd hai. yahi sajaatiyata 'yu-su' main he.
