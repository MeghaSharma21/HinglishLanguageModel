 muttahida qaumi movement pakistan kaa ek secular raajnneetik dal he. yah mukhaytah urdubhaashi mujahiron kaa dal he. vartmaan samay main yah dal sindh praant kaa doosraa sabase bada dal he jiske paas 130 main se 54 siten hai. yah pakistan kii neshnal asembli main chauthi sabase badi party he. 
 altaaf hossain ne 1978 main 'aal pakistan mujahir studants organijeshan' banaaya jisase 1984 main muzahir kaumi movement kaa janm hua. 1997 main is party ne apana naam badalakar 'muttahida qaumi movement' rakh liya. karachi main iskaa aadhaar bahut tagada he.
