 saajan chale sasuraal david dhawan dwaara nirdeshit 1996 main bani hindi bhashaa kii kamedi film he. govinda, karishma kapur, tabu, kadar khan or satish kowshik mukhya kalakaar hai. yah telugu film alari mogudu kii rimek he jisamen mohan babu or ramya krishnan the.
 shyamsundar sidhaa-sachcha gramin he jo sangeet main bahut ruchi rakhata he. vah shahar kii yatra karata he jahaan wo dakshin bhartiya tabalaa vadak muthuswami ke saath dost banataa he. tiipies casette company ke adhyaksh, khurana uski sangeet kshamtaaon se prabhavit hai or use ucch pad par padonnati karte hai. jab vah gaanv main apana karj chukaane ke liye waapas jaataa he, to use apni patni puja kii maut kii dukhad khabar milti he. vah baadh main mar gai he. uske baad vah khurana kii beti divya se shaadi karata he. jab khurana kaa dil kaa daura padataa he, shyamsundar ko aspataal main uski "anumaanit mrit" patni puja milti he. uske baad use apni donon patniyon ko bevakoof bananaa padataa he, yah sunishchit karne ke liye ki puja or divya ko pataa nahin chale ki usne donon se vivaah kiya he. bhale hi isamen do doharaa jivan shaamil ho.
 sangeet nadeem shravan dwaara or bol samir ke hai.
