 29 may 1453 ko osmani samrajya kii akramanakari senaaon dwaara baazantini samrajya kii rajdhani qustutuniya par adhikaar kar lene kii ghatana qustutuniya vijay kehalaati he. yah ek ati mahatvapurn ghatana thi. osmani sena kaa senapati 21 varshiya sultan mahamad dwitiya tha. yah vijay 7 saptaah kii gheraabandi ke baad mili thi.
 is ghatana ke falswaroop roman samrajya kaa ant ho gaya jo lagbhag 1500 varshon se chalaa aa rahaa tha. is ghatana se isaeevaad ko bhi dhakka pahunchaa or osmani senaaon ke europe main pravesh kaa maarg prashast ho gaya tha. is vijay ke baad osmani samrajya kii rajdhani edirn se hataakar qustutuniya laa di gayi. vijay ke pehle or baad main anekon unani evam anya buddhijivi qustutuniya chhodkar bhaag nikle. inamen se adhikansh italy ja pahunche jisase europea punarjagran ko bahut bal mila.
 yah ghatana madhyakaalin itihaas kii ek atyant mahatvapurn ghatana thi. kuch itihaaskaar ise madhyakaal kii samaapti kii ghatana maanate hai.
