 kasarcode, bhartiya rajya kerala kaa ek jila he.
 kshetrafal - 1992 varg kimi
 jansankhya - 12,04,078 
 itihasaunnisvin or chaudaavin shatabdi ke dauraan yahan aane vaale arabon logon ko harkiwilia kaha jaataa tha. portugal vyapaaree or jahaaj ke navik barbosa, jo 1514 main kumbhala gaye the, ne bataaya ki yahan se maldive ko chaaval niryaat kiya gaya tha. 1800 main, francis bukkanar attipparamba, kavvai ,nileshwar, baikal, chandragiri, ek vefer uski yatra vivaran main darj daura kiya. jab vijaynagar samrajya ne kasargod par hamalaa kiya, to yah yahan tha ki kolttiri vansh ne nileshwar dwaara shasit kiya. vijaynagar samrajya ke patan ke dauraan, prashasnic kaary ikkari naykkar dwaara kiya gaya tha. 1763 main, haidar ali ne ikkari naykkar ke mukhyalay bidanur par vijay praapt kii. tipu sultan ne baad main malabar ko jeet liya 1792 main shrirangapattanam kii sandhi ke anusaar, angrejon ne tulunad ko chhodkar anya kshetron par kabja kar liya tha or tipu kii mrityu britishon ne shasit kar di thi.
 bhashaayenkasargod ek bahubhashi kshetra he. kerala kii seemaa main sthit kasargod, saat alag-alag bhashaaon se adhik bolta he. yadhyapi ek aadhikarik bhashaa ke rup main malayalam istemaal kiya jaataa he. yahan logon kannada, tulu, konkani, baari, marathi, kor, tamil or hindi bolte hai. kasargod sanskaran kaa malayalam sanskaran samaan he is vishesh boli ko tulu or kannada sampradaayon ke prabhaav se banaaya gaya tha.
