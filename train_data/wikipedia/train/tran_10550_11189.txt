 nagar paak-adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-balatistan kshetra ke hunzaa-nagar zile main sthit ek shahar he. yah hunzaa nadi ke kinare 2,688 meter kii oonchaai par basaa hua he. china-dwaara niyantrit shinjiyang praant ko khunjaraab darre ke zariye pakistan se jodne vaala prasiddh karakoram rajmarg nagar se bhi guzarakar nikalataa he. yah jis vaadi main sthit he uskaa naam bhi nagar vaadi he or yah kisi jamaane main nagar riyasat kaa kshetra hua karata tha. nagar shahar ko vaadi se alag bataane ke liye kabhi-kabhi nagar khaas bhi kehete hai. yahan ke adhiktar log burushaski bolte hai haalanki sthaneey logon ko shina bhashaa bhi aati he. dharmik nazariye se nagar nivasee shiya hai.
