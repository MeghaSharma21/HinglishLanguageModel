 kamala prasad bisesar trininad evam tobago kii vartmaan pradhanmantri hai. ve bhartiya mul kii hai. ve trininad evam tobago kii saatvin kintu pratham mahila pradhanmantri hai. unhen 26 may san 2010 ko pradhanmantri niyukt kiya gaya. 
 ve 'united neshnal congress' kii netri hai. ve trininad evam tobago kii pratham etorni general, kaaryvahak pradhanmantri, vipaksh kii netaa bhi thi.
