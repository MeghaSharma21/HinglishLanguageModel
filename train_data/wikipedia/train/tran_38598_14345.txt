 avatar bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan kii maanavrahit ekcharniya punarprayojya antarikshayan ke kancept adhyayan kaa naam he. iskaa udaan bharna or utarana donon hi kshaitij disha main hoga. iskaa uddeshy kam laagat ke sainya evam vanijyik upgrah vimochan kii kshamta praapt karnaa he.
 2001 ke baad isamen aage koi adhyayan yaa vikaas nahin hue hai.
