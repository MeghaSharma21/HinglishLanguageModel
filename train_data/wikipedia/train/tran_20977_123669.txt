 prakritik sansadhano se paripurna hote hi bhi africa vishwa kaa sabase daridr or sabase aviksit bhu bhaag he. africa kaa itihaas bhayankar mahamariyon jaise malaria, ech. ai. ve, sainik vidroh, jaateey hinsa aadi ghatnaon se bharaa hua he jo iske vartmaan sthiti ke liye jimmedaar he. sanyukt raashtra dwaara san 2003 main prakashit maanav vikaas report main africa ke 25 deshon ne suchi main sabase nichalaa sthaan praapt kiya he.
 san 1995 se 2005 tak africa kii arthvyavastha main sudhaar aayaa or varsh 2005 ke liye yah ausatan 5 pratishat rahi. kuch desh jaise angola, sudan or equitarial gini jinhone apane petroleum bhandaron athwa petroleum vitaran pranali kaa vistaar kiya ne ausat se adhik vikaas dar darj kii. vigat kuch varshon main china ne africa deshon main kaaphi nivesh kiya he. san 2007 main chini upakramon ne africa deshon main kul 1 billian dollar kaa nivesh kiya.
