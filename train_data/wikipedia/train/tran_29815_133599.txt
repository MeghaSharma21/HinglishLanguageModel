 hindustani bhashaa hindi or urdu kaa ekikrut rup he. ye hindi or urdu, dono ke bolchaal kii bhashaa he. isamen sanskrit ke tatsam shabd or arabi-farsi ke udhaar liye gayi shabd, donon kam hote hai. yahi hindi or urdu kaa vah rup he jo bharat kii janata rozmarra ke jivan main upyog karti he or hindi cinema isi par aadhaarit he. ye hind europea bhashaa parivaar kii hind arya shakha main aati he. ye devanagari yaa farsi-arabi, kisi bhi lipi main likhi ja sakati he.
