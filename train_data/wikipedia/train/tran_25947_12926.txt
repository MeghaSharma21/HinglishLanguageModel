 tibeti-burmi bhashaa parivaar purvi asia, dakshin-purvi asia ke pahari ilaaqon or bhartiya upmahadweep main boli jane vaali lagbhag 400 bhashaaon kaa ek bhashaa-parivaar he. iskaa naam is parivaar kii do sab se zyaada boli jane vaali bhashaaon par rakhaa gaya he - tibeti bhashaa or burmi bhashaa . yah bhashaa-parivaar chini-tibeti bhashaa parivaar kii ek upashakha he, lekin chini bhashaa or in bhashaaon main bahut antar he.
