 universal motor yaa sarvavidhyut motor vah motor he jise ek-feji prayavarti dhara tathaa disht dhara donon se chalaaya ja saktaa he. yah vastav main series disi motor hoti he. ise asi commutator motor kii shrenee main rakhaa jaataa he. gharon main upyog main aane vaala mixr kaa motor universal motor hi hota he. iske atirikt railgadee kaa ingine khinchne ke liye universal motor kaa hi upyog kiya jaataa he kyonki inaki chaal ke saath balaghurna ke badalane kaa sambandh is kaam ke liye bahut upyukt he. yah motor kam chaal par bahut adhik balaghurna paidaa karata he jabaki chaal badhane par iske dwaara utpann kiya gaya balaghurna kramshah kam hota jaataa he.
