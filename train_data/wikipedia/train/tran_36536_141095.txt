 iesces code kii bank-waar suchi har us bank shakha main uplabdh he jo antar bank electronic fund transfar main bhaag leti he. bhartiya reserve bank kii websit par ai.epa.es code kii suchi va un bank shakhaon kii suchi uplabdh he jo neft/aar.tii.ji.es main bhaag leti hai. sabhi bainkon ko yah bhi suchit kiya gaya he ki ve apni graahakon ko jari checkbuk par apni bank shakha kaa ai.epa.es code chhaapen.
