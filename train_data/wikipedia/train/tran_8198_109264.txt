 janaa naa dil se dur bhartiya hindi dharawahik he, jiskaa prasaaran star plus par 9 may 2016 se 30 june 2017 tak hua. iskaa nirmaan beond drims naamak company ne kiya he. isamen mukhya kirdaar main vikram singh or shivani surve hai.
 vividha ajmer main rahane vaali ek ladki he, jiske pita kailash uske liye ek adarsh hai. kailash ne apane jivan main bahut se utaar chadhaav dekha he. use lagata he ki do logon kaa rishta tabhi ho saktaa he jab un donon samaan paise vaale hon.
 athrava ki chhavi kailash ke saamne bilkul bhi thik nahin rahati lekin vividha usase pyaar karne lagati he. adharva ko bhi usase pyaar ho jaataa he.
 yah kahani ajmer kii he or is dharawahik ke nirmaataa iske ek drishy main iske pushkar meley kii chhavi bhi dikhaanaa chaahate the. is drishy ko vaastavik dikhaane ke liye 100 se 150 pashuon ko laayaa gaya tha or puure jagah main rang-birangi chizon ko rakhaa gaya tha.
