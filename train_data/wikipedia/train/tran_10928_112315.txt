 iran ke is naqshe main zagros parvat shrunkhlaa pashchim main dekhi ja sakati he
 zagros parvat iran or iraq kii sabase badi parvatamala he. yah pashchimottaree iran se shuru hokar dakshinpurvi disha main 1,500 kimi chalakar hormuz jalsandhi main ant hoti he. irani pathaar ke pashchimi or dakshin-pashchimi bhaag par vistrut yah shrunkhlaa bhugolic rup se ureshian takhte or arabi takhte ke takaraav se bani thi. isaki sabase buland chotiyaan 4,409 meter unchaa koh-e-dena or 4,200 meter unchaa zard koh hai, haalanki alag-alag strot inaki unchaaiyaan alag-alag bataate hai.
