 khasm, hathyog sadhana kaa ek paribhaashik shabd jo kha + sam se bana he or iskaa mul arth he kha ke samaan. hathyog sadhana kaa uddeshy chitt ko saare sansaarik dharmon se muktakar use nirlypt bana dena tha. is sarvadharmashoonyata ko man kii shunyavastha kehete hai or shunya kaa pratik gagan he. at: parishuddh, sthir, nirmal chitt ko khasm kehete hai. siddhacharyon dwaara bodhichitt kii sadhana main man ko khasm swaroop dhaaran karne kaa upadesh diya gaya he. palatudaas aadi santon ne bhi isi arth main is shabd kaa prayog kiya he. is shabd kaa prayog kuch santon ne is arth ke atirikt pati or priyatam ke arth main kiya he; kintu vahaan iske mul main farsi shabd 'khasm' he.
