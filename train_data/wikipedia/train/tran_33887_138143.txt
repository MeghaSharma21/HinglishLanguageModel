  abdul wahaab khan ek pakistani rajaneta or pakistan kii qaumi asembli ke purv sadsy the. unhen 12 agast 1955 - 7 oktober 1958, ke bich pakistan kii qaumi asembli ke adhyaksh ke pad par niyukt kiya gaya tha. pakistan kii qaumi assembly ke adhyaksh kaa pad, pakistan kii samvidhan dwaara sthapit ek sanvaidhanik pad he, joki pakistan kii qaumi asembli ke sabhapati evam adhishthata hote hai.
