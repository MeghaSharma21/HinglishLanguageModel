 samooh 12 tatv aavart sarini ke rasinic tatwon kaa ek samooh he. yah aavart sarini ke di khand main aata he. is samooh main jasta, cadmium or paara shaamil hai. inke alaava, apane vidyudanu vinyas ke aadhaar par koparnisium bhi isi samooh kaa sadsy he, haalaanki yah keval ek prayogshaala main banaaya gaya paractenide kritrim tatv he. rontjainium kaa nabhik bahut asthai he jiske karanvash yah atyant radiodharmee he or isaki ardhayu kaal keval 29 sekkind he.
