 kaansya yug us kaal ko kehete hai jisamen manushya ne tambe tathaa uski range ke saath mishrit dhaatu kaanse kaa istemaal kiya. itihaas main yah yug pashaan yug tathaa louh yug ke bich main padataa he. pashaan yug main manushya kii kisi bhi dhaatu kaa khanan kar paane kii asamarthta thi. kaansya yug main lohe kii khoj nahin ho pai thi or louh yug main tamba, kansaa or lohe ke alaava manushya kuch anya thos dhaatuon kii khoj tathaa unka upyog bhi sikh gaya tha.kaansya yug kii visheshta yah he ki manushya shahari sabhyataaon main basane laga or isi kaaran se vishwa kii kai jagahon main puranic sabhyataaon kaa vikaas hua. is yug kii ek or khaas baat yah he ki vibhinn sabhyataaon main alag-alag lipeon kaa vikaas hua jinaki madad se aaj ke puraatatv shastriyon ko us yug ke bare main mahatvapurn tathy hasil hote hai. 
 sampoorn yug kii avadhi vishwa ke vibhinn kshetron main kaanse ko purnataya istemaal se sambandhit he, haalanki alag-alag jagahon main yah ghatana alag-alag daur main huyi.
