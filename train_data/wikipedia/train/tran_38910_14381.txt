 ai. en.ji vaisya bank li., ek bhartiya khudara bank tha. yah ai. en.ji samooh ke vaisya bank main 44% ansh lene ke uparaant oktober 2002 ko astitva main aayaa tha.
 1 april 2015 se iskaa vilay kotak mahindra bank main ho gaya.
 vaisya bank kii sthapana 1930 main bangaleru main hui thi. 1948 main yah anusuchit bank bana. 7 oktober 2002 ko ai. en.ji samooh ne bank main 44% ansh le liya. back kii bharat paryant 470 se adhik shakhaaen thim. iskaa mukhyalay bangaleru main sthit he. bank ne 2001 main jivan beemaa kaa vyapaar aarambh kiya tha. 1 april 2015 se iskaa vilay kotak mahindra bank main ho gaya.
