 bharat sarkaar dwaara antarrashtriya gandhi shanti puraskaar bharat ke rashtrapita mahatma gandhi ke naam par diya jane vaala vaarshik puraskaar he. gandhi ji ke shanti siddhanton ko shradhanjali swaroop, bharat sarkaar ne yah puraskaar 1995 main unke 125wein janm-diwas par aarambh kiya tha. yah vaarshik puraskaar un vyaktiyon yaa sansthaaon ko diya jaataa he, jinhone saamaajik, aarthik evam raajnneetik badlaavon ko ahimsa evam anya gandhivadi tareekon dwaara praapt kiya he. puraskaar main 1 karod rupee kii dhanrashi, prashastipatr or ek uttareey di jaati he. yah sabhi rashtron, jatiyon, ling ke logon ke liye khulaa he. pratham gandhi shanti puraskaar 1995 main tanzania ke pratham raashtrapati ke julius nairere ko pradaan kiya gaya tha. 2009 main yah puraskaar da childrens legal centre ko duniya bhar main bal maanavadhikaar ko badhaava dene ke liye diya gaya.
