 shila mehara ek bhartiya stri rog or prasuti visheshgy hai. vah mulchand aspataal, nai dilli ke stri rog or prasuti vibhaag kii nirdeshika hai. 1959 main ladi harding medical college se grajuation karne ke baad unhone DRCOG or MRCOG kii degree royal college of obstatarictions and gainicologistus, britain se kii. vah indian college of obstatarictions and gainicologistus main shodhakarta hai or bahut se puruskaar jaise ki- radha raman puraskaar or indian medical association se life time achievement puraskaar paa chuki hai. bharat sarkaar ne unhen chauthe sarvocch naagarik puraskaar padam shri se 1991 main sammanit kiya.
