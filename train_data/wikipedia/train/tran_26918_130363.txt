 uttar kiwu praant africa ke congou loktantrik ganraajya ke purvottaree hisse main sthit ek praant he. iskaa seemayen kiwu jhil se satee hui hai or usee jhil par iskaa or iske dakshin main sthit dakshin kiwu praant kaa naam padaa he. yah donon praant aitihaasik kiwu pradesh kaa hissaa hai jo purvi africa daraar kshetra kaa oonchaai vaala ilaaka he.
 uttar kiwu praant main virunga rashtriya udyaan sthit he jo pahari gorilla kaa nivas sthaan he.
 prantiya rajdhani goma main kiwu jhil kaa kinaaraa
 prarthana karte congoi isai
 sabziyaan ugaati striyaan
 praant kii prakruti kaa ek drishy
