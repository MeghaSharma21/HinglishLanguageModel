 dulhe raja 1998 kii harmesh malhotra dwaara nirdeshit hindi bhashaa kii hasya film he. isamen govinda, ravina tandon, kadar khan, johny liver, prem chopra or asraani hai.
 ke ke singhania pi ke divaani se ek mahangaa hotel "maharaja international" kharidte hai. baad main, singhania ko pataa chalata he ki hotel ke parisar ke andar ek dhaba ek raja naam ke aadmi dwaara chalaaya jaataa he jo hotel kii kamai ke liye nuksaandaayak he. raja ke dhaba ko hataane ke liye singhania dwaara upyog kii jane vaali har chaal vifal hoti he.
 singhania kii beti kiran rahul naam ke ek aadmi se pyaar karti he. rahul ko aarthik rup se ek sahyogi bishbar nath dwaara sahaayta di jaati he. usee samay, raja kiran ko pasand karne lagata he. 
 sabhi geet samir dwaara likhit; sara sangeet anand-milind dwaara rachit.
