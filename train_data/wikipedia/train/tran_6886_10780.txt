 
 khokali lagbhag 75 semi ooncha paudha hota he. iske pattey 3-8 semi lambey, andakaar athwa chaturkon-andakaar se hote hai. patton main praaia: teen shirayein hoti hai or unke kinare dantur hote hai, patton ko danthal patton se bhi lambey hote hai. phool chote chote hote hai tathaa patton ke kaksh main, spike jaise, sidhe gucchhon main lagate hai. maadaa pushp ke neeche ek tikona-sa sahapatr hota he. narpushp atyant chote hote hai, tathaa spike ke upree bhaag main lagate hai. fal royendaar hote hai tathaa sahapatron main dhake rahete hai.
 vaigyanik naam: akalifa eendika

 vyapaar kaary kaa naam akalifa vaigyanik naam par aadhaarit he.
 khokali kaa paudha bharat ke sabhi maidani bhaagon main paaya jaataa he. yah praaia: udyanon va kheton main tathaa sadakon va makaanon ke aasapaas ug aata he.
 khokali ke paudhon par jis samay phool aate hai, us samay unhen samoochaa ukhadkar sukha lete hai or aushadhi main prayog karte hai.is paudhe main ipeka jaise gun bataate hai. yah shwaas nalee kii sujan, shwasarog yaa dama, nimonia tathaa gathiya main upyogi he. isaki jad va pattey rochak hote hai. patton kaa ras vamankaaree hota he, arthath uske seven se kai ho jaati he. taaje patton ko pis kar phodon par bhi lagaate hai.
