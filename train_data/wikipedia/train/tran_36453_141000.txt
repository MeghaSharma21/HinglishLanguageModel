 oosar yaa banjar vah bhoomi he jisamen lavenon kii adhikta ho, . aisi bhoomi main kuch nahin athwa bahut kam utpaadan hota he.
 oosar sudhaar kii vidhi krambaddh charanbaddh tathaa samaybaddh pranali he is vidhi se bhoomi ko purna rup se thik kiya ja saktaa he. 
 - behatar jal prabandh evam jal nikaas kii samuchit vyavastha hetu surveykshan. 
 1.sthal unche sthaan par chunaa jaye. 
 2. jis sadasth ke khet main voring ho vah bakaayaadaar na ho. 
 3. ek boring kii duuri duusare se 200 me0 se kam na ho. 
 -yah kaary barsaat main yaa sitambar oktober main jab bhoomi nam rahati he to shuru kar deni chahie. 
 -med ke dharaatal kii chaudaai 90 semi oonchai 30 semi tathaa med kii upree sataha kii chau0 30 semi honi chahie. 
 -medbandi karte samay sinchai naalee or khet jal nikaas naalee kaa nirmaan do kheton kii medon ke bich kar dena chahie. 
 - bhoomi kii jutai varsha main yaa varsha ke baad sitambar oktober yaa farvari main karke chhod den jisase lavan bhoomi kii sataha par ekatra na ho. 
 -bhoomi kii jutai 2-3 baar 14-20 semi gahari kii jaye khet jutai se purv usrile ptch ko 2 semi kii sataha khurpy se khurchakar baahar naale main daal den. 
 -khet ko kam chaudee or lambi-2 kyaariyon main batkar kyaariyon kaa samatleekaran karnaa chahie. 
 -jal nikaas naalee kii taraf bahut halkaa sa dhaal dena chahie taaki khet kaa phaltu pani jal nikaas naalee dwaara bahaya jaye. 
 -mitti kii jaanch kara le aavashyak 50 pratishat jipsom kii maatra kaa pataa chal paata hai. 
 -khet kii dhaal tathaa nalakup ke sthaan ko dhyaan main rakhte hue sinchai tathaa khet naalee kaa nirmaan karnaa chahie. 
 -sinchai naalee bhoomi kii sataha se upar banaai jaye, jo aadhaar par 30 semi gahari tathaa sheersh par 120 semi ho. 
 -khet naalee bhoomi kii sataha se 30 banaai jaye, jo aadhaar par 30 semi gahari tathaa sheersh par 75-90 senmi ho. 
 yah 50 semi gahari aadhaar par 45 semi or sheersh par 145 semi or said slop 1:1 kaa hona chahie
 - samatleekaran karte samay khet main 5-6 me chaudee or lambi kyaariyaan bana len tathaa safed lavan ko 2 semi kii sataha khurpy se khurch kar baahar naale main daal den. 
 - phir kyaariyon main halkaa sa pani laga den chaar paanch din baad nikaal den jisase lavan leaching dwaara bhoomi ke neeche athwa pani dwaara baahar nikal jaayenge. 
 - samatleekaran kaa pataa lagane ke liye kyaariyon main halkaa pani laga den. tathaa halki jutai karke thik prakaar se samtal kar len. 
 - jipsom kaa prayog karte samay kyaariyaan nam ho. 
 - boriyon ko kyaariyon main samaan rup se failaa den. 
 - iske baad deshi hal yaa kaltieater kii sahaayta se bhoomi kii upree 7-8 semi kii sataha main jipsom mila den or phir halkaa pata lagaakar kyaariyon ko samtal kar len.
 - kyaariyon main jipsom milaane ke baad 10-15 semi pani bhar den or use 10 dinon tak leaching kriya hetu chhod den. 
 - 10 dinon tak kyaariyon main 10 semi pani khadaa rahanaa chahie. yadi khet main pani kam ho jaye to pani or bhar dena chahie. isliye jaruri he ki kyaariyon main duusare-tisare din pani bharte rahen. 
 - leaching kriya har haalat main 5 july tak puri ho jaye jisase 10 july tak dhan kii ropai kii ja saken. 
 - 10 dinon baad khet kaa lavanyukt pani khet naalee dwaara baahar nikaal den. 
 - leaching ke baad acha pani lagaakar dhan kii ropai, 5 semi pani bharkar oosar rodhi prajati kii 35-40 din aayu ke paudhe kii ropai kar den. 
  acarbonic:jipsom, pyrite, phosphogipsom, gandhak kaa amla
  carbonic padaarth:pressmud, oosar tod khaad, shira, dhan kaa puaav dhan kii bhusi, baaluu jalkumbhi, kaccha gobar or pual gobar or compost kii khaad, vargi compost, satyanashi kharpatavaari, aadi. 
  anya padaarth:jaivik sudhaar dhaicha, dhan, chukandar, paalak, ganna, deshi, babul aadi.
