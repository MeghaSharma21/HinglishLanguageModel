 vaasila sanyukt rajya america ke alaska rajya main sthit ek chota kasbaa he. yah alaska kaa chatha sabase bada nagar he. alaska ke sabase bade nagar ancorage se isaki duuri lagbhag 69 kimi he. 2010 kii america kii janaganana ke anusaar yahan kii kul jansankhya 7,831 he. vaasila kaa kshetrafal 32.1 varg kimi he jisamen se 30.3 varg kimi bhumikshetra or 1.8 varg kimi jalakshetra he. yahan kaa jansankhya ghanatv 258.4/kimi2 he.
 bart cottle yahan ke vartmaan mahayor hai.
 vaasila nagar parishad is nagar kii vidhayika he. yah kanoon banaati he or nitiyaan nirdhaarit karti he, sampatti kar kii dar tay karti he, or naagarik sevaaon ke liye vyayika or dhan ko sweekruti detii he. isamen chah sadsy hote hai, jo mukt rup se vaasila ke nivaasiyon dwaara teen varshon kii avadhi ke liye chune jate hai. mahayor kaa chunaav alag se kiya jaataa he. yadi kisi bhi mahayor pratyaashee ko kul daale gaye maton main se 40% se kam praapt hote hai to ek chunaav or karaayaa jaataa he.
