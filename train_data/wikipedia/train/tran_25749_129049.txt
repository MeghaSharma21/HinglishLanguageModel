 
 aspasht shabd
 huruf muqattʿaat shabd ") ek se paanch arabi aksharon kaa jod, jo koran kii 114 main se 29 surahon main bismillah ke baad main nirupit kiye gaye hai. saath hi in aksharon ko fawatih yaa "kholanewaala" bhi kehete hai.
 chaar surahen jo unke muqattaaat ke naam se hai, ta-ha, yaa-seen, saad or qaaf.
 arabi aksharon ke jod kaa mul mahatv agyaat he. tafasir kii vyakhya main in ke liye sankshipt rup main yaa to "khuda kaa naam yaa badai" yaa "sambandhit surahon kaa naam yaa un main antarnihit vastu" arth mana gaya he.
 muqattaaat in aayaat main hote hai 2–3, 7, 10–15, 19–20, 26–32, 36, 38, 40–46, 50 or 68. aksharon ko ek shabd kii tarah ek saath likhaa jaataa he, lekin prayek akshar kaa alag se bolnaa he.
