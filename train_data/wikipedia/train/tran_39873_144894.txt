 kitaab-ul-hind arabi main likhi gai al-beruni kii ek kriti he. isaki bhashaa saral or spasht he. yah ek vistrut granth he jisamen dharm or darshan ,tyoharon ,khagol-vigyaan ,reeti-rivaajon tathaa prathaon ,saamaajik jivan ,bhaar-taul tathaa maapan-vidhiyon ,murtikla, kanoon, maaptantr vigyaan aadi vishyon kaa vivechan kiya gaya he. 
 al-beruni ne apane granth kitaab-ul-hind ke prayek adhyay main ek vishisht style kaa prayog kiya he, jisamen armbh main ek prashn hota tha, phir sanskritvaadi paramparaon par aadhaarit varnan tha.
