 jon de 2013 main pradarshit ahishor solomon dwaara nirdeshit evam anjum rizvi, aatif e khan or ke asif dwaara nirmit ek hinsapradhan thriller he. isamen mukhya bhoomika nasiruddin shah, randeep hooda, vipin sharma,shernaz patel ne adaa kii he.
 film jon de se armbh hoti he jo ek behad eemaandaar or sidhdantvaadi bank prabandhak he. unki beti parl apane bwoyfriend kanishka ke saath ghoomane jaati he to ek durghatana main uski maut ho jaati he. do saal baad jon de ke bank main lut ho jaati he or bank luteron ke hamale main unki patni maaria ghayal hokar coma main chali jaati he. gautam ek police afasar he jiske anderworld don sikandar hayaat khan se behad nazadiiki sambandh hai. wo us don kii gang kaa mohara he lekin wo ab apni garlfriend tabassum habibi ke saath in sabase dur bhagkar shanti kii zindagi bitaana chahta he lekin aisa kar nahin paata. udhar jon de ko apni beti kii maut or patni par hue hamale main kuch ajeeb si saazish kii bu aati he or wo is maamle kii tah tak pahunchane kii koshish karata he. isi silsile main use aage pataa chalata he ki uski beti kii maut ke peeche kii vajah ek zamin ghotaalaa he jisase hayaat khan kaa gang judaa he. is bich paristhitiyaan kuch aisi ban jaati hai ki wo jon de or gautam ko aamane-saamne laa khadaa karti hai.
 
 jon de kaa sangeet kshitij taare evam strings ne diya he or geet said kadri ne taiyaar kiya he. iskaa sangeet 26 agast 2013 ko jari kiya gaya.
