 vellore kila tamil nadu rajya ke vellore ke centre main sthit he. kile main shri jalagandishwar mandir, ek masjid, carch, mutu mandapam, prasiddh vellore isai aspataal or rajya sarkaar sangrahalay bhi sthit hai. isi kile main tipu mahal bhi sthit he or manyata yah bhi he ki tipu sultan british yuddh ke samay apane parivaar ke saath kuch samay yahaan rahe the. british raaj ke dauraan vellore fort main kai shahi kaidiyon jaise candi ke antim raja vikram rajasinha or tipu sultan ke parivaar ke sadasyon ko rakhaa gaya tha. 1806 ke sipaahi vidroh kii agni sarvapratham vellore kile main hi bhadki thi. vellore fort ek vikhyath paryatak aakarshan he or rashtriya mahatv kii ek aitihaasik imaarat main gina jaataa he.
 kile kii bahari deevaaron kaa nirmaan vishaal grenite ke shilakhandon se hua, jisko chaaron ore se gahari khai ghere hue he. khai main bharne ke liye jal kii aapurti suryagunta jalashay dwaara kii jaati he or yah dohari deevaaron se drudh he. vartmaan main is kile kaa anurakshan bhartiya puraatatv surveykshan vibhaag ke adheen he.
