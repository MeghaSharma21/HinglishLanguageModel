 shaikh mohammad bin raashid al makatoom:, sanyukt arab emirate ke uparashtrapati or pradhan mantri tathaa, or dubai emirate ke shaasha.. 2006 main apane bhai shaikh makatoom kii mrityu ke baad, unhone april 2007 main sanyukt arab emirate sangheey sarkaar kii ranneeti ke saath sanyukt arab emirate kii sarkaar main sudhaar kiye hai. 2010 main unhone yuai banane ke uddeshy se sanyukt arab emirate drushti 2021 launch kiya tha. jo 2021 tak duniya ke sabase achche deshon main se ek he..
