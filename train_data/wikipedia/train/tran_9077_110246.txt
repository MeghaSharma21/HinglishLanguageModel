 bengal uttarpurvi dakshin asia main ek kshetra he. aaj bengal ek swadhin raashtra, bangladesh or bhartiya sangheey prajatantra kaa angabhoot rajya pashchim bengal ke bich main sahbhaaji he, yadhyapi pehle bengali rajya ke kuch kshetra ab padosi bhartiya rajya bihar, tripura or urisa main he. bengal main bahumat main bengali log rahete hai. inaki maatrubhaasha bangla he.
 pracheen kaal main bengal magadh tathaa ang pradeshon kaa ang tha. madhyakaal main dilli sultanate ke adheen rahaa tathaa baad main nababon ke haath chalaa gaya. bhartiya swatantraaa sangram ke dauraan bengal krantikari gatividhiyon kaa pramukh kendra rahaa tha.
