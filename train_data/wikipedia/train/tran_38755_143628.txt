 bhartiya chandan kaa sansar main sarvocch sthaan he. iskaa aarthik mahatv bhi he. yah ped mukhyat: karnataka ke jangalon main miltaa he tathaa bharat ke anya bhaagon main bhi kahin-kahin paaya jaataa he. bharat ke 600 se lekar 900 meter tak kuch unche sthal or malayadweep iske mul sthaan hai.
 is ped kii oonchaai 18 se lekar 20 meter tak hoti he. yah paropjivi ped, santelesi kul kaa santelum album linn he. vriksh kii ayuvruddhi ke saath hi saath uske tanon or jadon kii lakdi main sougandhik tel kaa ansh bhi badhane lagata he. isaki purna paripakvata main 8 se lekar 12varsh tak kaa samay lagata he. iske liye dhalwaan jameen, jal sokhanevaali upjau chikali mitti tathaa 500 se lekar 625 mimi. tak vaarshik varsha kii aavashyaktaa hoti he.
 tane kii naram lakdi tathaa jad ko jad, kunda, buraada, tathaa chhilka or chhilan main vibhakt karke bechaa jaataa he. isaki lakdi kaa upyog murtikla, tathaa saajasja ke saamaan banane main or anya utpaadanon kaa agarbatti, havan saamagree, tathaa sougandhik tej ke nirmaan main hota he. aswan dwaara sugandhit tel nikaala jaataa he. prayek varsh lagbhag 3,000 metry ton chandan kii lakdi se tel nikaala jaataa he. ek metry ton lakdi se 47 se lekar 50 kilogram tak chandan kaa tel praapt hota he. rasayanajna is tel ke sougandhik tatv ko sanshleshik reeti se praapt karne kaa prayaas kar rahe hai.
 chandan ke prasaaran main pakshi bhi sahaayak hai. bijon ke dwaara ropakar bhi ise ugaaya ja rahaa he. sandals spike naamak rahasypurna or sankramak vanaspatik rog is vriksh kaa shatru he. isse sankramit hone par pattiyaan anthkar choti ho jaati hai or vriksh vikrut ho jaataa he. is rog kii rokthaam ke sabhi praytn vifal hue hai.
