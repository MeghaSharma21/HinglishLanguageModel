 kshetreey pradhikaran newzealand kii dwitiya str ke sthaneey prashasnic ikaiyaan hoti hai. har prashasnic kshetra main kai kshetreey pradhikaran hote hai jinaki tulana bharat ke zilo se kii ja sakati he. newzealand main 67 kshetreey pradhikaran hai, jinamein 12 nagareey parishad, 53 zila parishad, ocland parishad or chaitham dweepasmooh parishad shaamil hai. dhyaan den ki jabaki bharat kaa ek zila puraa-kaa-puraa ek hi rajya main ho saktaa he, iske vipareet newzealand kaa ek kshetreey pradhikaran do alag-alag prashasnic kshetron main banta hua ho saktaa he.
