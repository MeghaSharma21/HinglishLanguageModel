 ardra bhartiya jyotish main ek nakshatra he. sanskrit bhashaa se aae is naam kaa arth hota he "nam". ardra se sambandhit hindu myth brihaspati kii duusari patni taraaka ke saath he. taraaka ek asur he jise brahma dwaara akhandaneeyata kaa vardaan mila hua he. ardra sataais nakshatron main se ek he. akashamandal main ardra nakshatra chata he. yah raahu kaa nakshatra he va mithun raashi main aata he. ardra nakshatra kaa swami raahu he va isaki dasha 18 varsh kii hoti hai, lekin mythnu raashi par 5 maah 12 din se 18 varsh tak chandra kii sthitinusaar dasha janm ke samay bhogana padati he.
 iske baad hi gyaan ke kaarak guru kii dasha lagati he, jo puure 16 varsh bhogana padati he. kisi bhi jataka kaa janm jis nakshatra main hota he. us nakshatra ke swami kaa prabhaav uske jivan par avashya dekha jaataa he. ardra nakshatra va mithun raashi par janmen jataka ko raahu va budh kaa jeevanabhar prabhaav rahegaa. vahiin guru kaa bhi mahatv uske jivan main dekhne ko milegaa.
 is nakshatra main janmen jataka chanchal swabhaav ke hansmukh, abhimaani, dukh paane vaale, bure vichaaron vaale vyasnee bhi hote hai. raahu kii sthitinusaar fal bhi miltaa he. mesh lagn ho raahu lagn main ardra nakshatra kaa ho to aisa jataka raajnneeti main parakramee, chatur chaalak apane virodhiyon ko parrast karne vaala, shatruhanta hota he. aisa jataka atyant kaamuk hota he. vrishabh tathaa mithun kaa raahu is lagn main vaani se chatur banayega, shatruhanta bhi hoga.
