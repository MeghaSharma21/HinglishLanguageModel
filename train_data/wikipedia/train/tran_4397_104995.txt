 nagnata yaa nivastrata kapade na pahanane ko kehete hai. isamen puri tarah yaa aadhe kapade na pahanane ko bhi isi shrenee main laayaa jaataa he. lagbhag sabhi deshon main yah aparaadh kii shrenee main aata he.
 saarvajanik sthal par nagn hona ek apmaan or shame kaa vishay he. lekin kuch europea deshon main is prakaar ke kaary logon dwaara kiye jate hai. is par kai europea deshon or germany aadi ne puri tarah se rokane ke prayaas kiye hai. europe main england main kuch had tak bina kapadon ke saarvajanik sthal jaise bich aadi par mahilaon. ko upar ke kapadon ko na pahanane yaa pahanane kaa adhikaar he. yah kanoon 1986 main banaaya gaya.
 phool-body scinar dwaara kisi vyakti ko bina kapade nikalwaaye uske adhiyaar aadi kii jaankaree lee jaati he. isse pehle jab is prakaar ke yantra nahin the. tab kisi kii jaanch ke liye stri ke liye anya stri jaanch adhikari or purush ke liye anya purush jaanch adhikari dwaara puri tarah jaanch kii jaati thi. iskaa upyog mukhya rup se hawaii addon main kiya jaataa he.
