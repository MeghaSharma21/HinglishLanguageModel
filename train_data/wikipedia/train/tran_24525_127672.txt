 
 ponthig, kapkot tehsil main bharat ke uttrakhand rajya ke antargat kumaun mandal ke bageshwar jile kaa ek gaanv he. yahan maan nanda bhagwati kaa bhavy mandir bhi he. yahan har saal maan ke darbar main puja kaa aayojan hota he. maan par bhakton kii aasthaa, vishwaas or shraddha ke kaaran yahan har varsh agast-sitambar main vishaal jan-samudaay umad padataa he, jise log pothing kaa mela naam se bhi jaanate hai.
