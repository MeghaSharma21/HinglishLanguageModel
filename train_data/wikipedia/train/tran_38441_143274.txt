 poongunnam shiv mandir
 poongunnam shiv mandir, dakshin bharat ke kerala rajya main trishur jile ke poongunnam main sthit he or mana jaataa he kii yah mandir 1000 saal se bhi adhik purana he. yah mandir kerala style vaastukala kaa ek adhbudh or utkrisht udhaaharan he.
