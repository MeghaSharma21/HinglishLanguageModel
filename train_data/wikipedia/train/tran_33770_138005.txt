 ruchi prajapati ko anya prajapatiyon ke samaan brahma ji ne apane maanas putr ke rup main rachaa tha. ruchi prajapati kaa vivaah swayambhuv manu kii pratham kanya aakuti ke saath hua tha. ruchi prajapati kii patni aakuti ke garbh se ek putr tathaa ek kanya kaa janm hua. ruchi prajapati ke putr ko swayambhuv manu ne le liya. ruchi prajapati kii kanya kaa naam dakshina tha. dakshina ke rup main aakuti ke garbh se swayan devi lakshmi avatarit huin thim. dakshina ke yuva hone par uskaa vivaah bhagwaan vishnu se kar diya gaya.
