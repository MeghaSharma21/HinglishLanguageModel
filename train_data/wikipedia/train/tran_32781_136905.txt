 mir farkhunda ali khan naseer-ud-dawala - asif jaah IV kaa janm 25 april 1794 ko, bidar main hua tha. vah mir akbar ali khan sikandar jaah, asif jaah tritiy ke sabase bade bete the. unhone apane pita kii mrityu ke baad usee varsh 23 may 1829 ko rajpad sambhala. 
 unhone 28 saal tak shashan kiya. anya nizaamon kii tarah, unka makbara makka masjid main he. 
 prakritik aapadaaon ne aasaf jaah chaturth ko bhi nahin choda; mahamari, baadh, chakravaat or sookhe niyamit antaraal par shashan kaal ko prabhavit karte rahe.
 unke kaal main varangal main ek nayi chhavnee kii sthapana hui. nae skool, masjid, mandir, girjaghar, pul or isi prakaar kii nirmaankari gatividhiyon kaa nayaa centre jald hi hyderabad ke nae shahar ke aasapaas sthapit hua.
