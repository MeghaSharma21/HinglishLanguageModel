 ke raghavan, jinhen mukhyatah raghavan master ke naam se janaa jaataa he, ek malayalam sangeet rachayita the. baburaj ke saath-saath raghavan ko aksar malayalam film sangeet ke punarodhar ke liye janaa jaataa he. ke raghavan ne malayalam sangeet ko pahachaanaa evam ek nai disha di. unhone lagbhag 400 malayalam cinema main gaanon kii rachana kii or malayalam film udyog main chaar dashakon tak sakriy rahe.
 raghavan kaa janm 2 disambar 1913 ko thalasseri main lokagayak em krishnan or narayani ke ghar main hua. unka nidhan 99 varsh kii aayu main 19 oktober 2013 ko thalasseri main hua.
 raghavan kaa janm bhartiya rajya kerala ke thalasseri naamak sthaan par 1913 main hua. unhone bachapan se hi shastreey sangeet kii shiksha lenaa armbh kar diya or wo footbal ke bhi achche khilaadi the.
 raghavan ne 400 se adhik filmon main sangeet diya, jinamein 'nirmalay', 'uttarayan', 'nagarmendi', 'unniarch', 'ramanan' or 'kallichellammaa' jaisi behtareen filmein bhi shaamil hai.
