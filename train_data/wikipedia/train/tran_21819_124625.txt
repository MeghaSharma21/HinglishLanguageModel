 relay maximam and minimum volts kitne ka hota ha
 riley ek vidyut switch yaa kunjee he jo ek duusare vidyut paripath ke dwaara kholi yaa band kii jaati he jo ki mukhya paripath se asambaddh hoti he. riley kii ek yaa ek se adhik kunjiyaan ek vidyut chumbak kii sahaayta se band yaa chaalu hoti hai. riley ko bhi ek samanyikrut vidyut pravardhak mana ja saktaa he kyonki kam shakti vaale paripath kii sahaayta se ek apekshaakrit adhik shakti vaale paripath ko niyantrit kiya jaataa he. kantactor bhi riley ke siddhant par hi kaam karata he kintu praaia: 15 ampir se adhik dhara vaale kantact ko band/chaalu karne ke liye prayukt hota he.
 riley vidyutchumbakeey yukti he jo nimnlikhit kaaryon ke liye prayog main laayi ja sakati he :
 prayek riley main ek chumbakeey paripath hota he jiskaa ek bada bhaag louhachumbakeey padaarth se bana hota he tathaa ek chota sa bhaag main flux hawa se hokar guzarane ko baadhya hoti he jise 'air-gap' kehete hai. jab riley ke kundalee main vidyut dhara pravaahit kii jaati he to is air-gap ke donon siron ke bich ek bal kaary karata he jo is gap ko kam karne kii koshish karata he. riley ke chumbakeey paripath kii rachana is prakaar kii gayi hoti he ki isame ek lachiledaar louhachumbakeey bhaag bi rahata he. riley kii kunadali ko dhara dene par yah lachiledaar bhaag khinch liya jaataa he. is bhaag se hi yantric link banaate hue 'chalyman sampark' hote hai jo ki band yaa khul jate hai.
