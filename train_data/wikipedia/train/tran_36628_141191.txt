 kentua khaad yaa varmicompost poshan padaarthon se bharpoor ek uttam jaev urvarak he. yah kentua aadi keedon ke dwaara vanaspatiyon evam bhojan ke kachare aadi ko vighatit karke banaai jaati he.
 varmi compost main badboo nahin hoti he or makkhi evam machhar nahin badhate he tathaa vaataavaran pradooshit nahin hota he. taapmaana niyantrit rahane se jeevanu kriyasheel tathaa sakriy rahete hai. varmi compost dedh se do maah ke andar taiyaar ho jaataa he. isamen 2.5 se 3% nitrogen, 1.5 se 2% sulfur tathaa 1.5 se 2% potash paaya jaataa he.
 kentua khaad kii visheshtaayen : is khaad main badboo nahin hoti he, tathaa makkhi, machhar bhi nahin badhate he jisase vaataavaran swasth rahata he. isse sookshm poshit tatwon ke saath-saath nitrogen 2 se 3 pratishat, phosphorus 1 se 2 pratishat, potash 1 se 2 pratishat miltaa he.
 is khaad ko taiyaar karne main prakriya sthapit ho jane ke baad ek se dedh maah kaa samay lagata he.prayek maah ek ton khaad praapt karne hetu 100 vargafoot aakaar kii nursery bed paryaapt hoti he.kechuna khaad kii keval 2 ton maatra prati hectare aavashyak he.
