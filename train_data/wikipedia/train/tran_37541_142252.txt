 dow kishorilaal gupt hindi sahityakar the. inhen 'vindh gaurav' se sammanit kiya gaya tha.
 kishorilaal gupt kaa janm uttar pradesh ke bhadohi main hua tha. ye pandit vishwanath prasad mishra ke pramukh shishyon main se ek the.
 'hindi sahitya kaa pratham itihaas' granth kaa sampaadan kishori laal gupt kii pramukh kriti he. yah hindi prachaarak santhaan varanasi se prakashit hui thi. gupt ji kii antim prakashit kriti sursagar kii tikaa chaar khandon main he.
