 foset comics foset prakaashan kaa ek prabhag tha, jo 1940 ke dashak main golden age ​​of comic books ke dauraan kai safal hasya pustak prakashkon main se ek tha. iskaa sabase lokapriy kirdaar captain maarval tha.
 foset dwaara prakashit anya paatro main captain video, hopelong casidy, ibees da invesible, bulatomen or bulatogirl, spai smashar, captain midnite, phantum eagle, mistar skarlet or pinki, minutes-main, commando yank or golden ero shaamil hai.
 1953 main foset prakaashan ne apana comic prabhag band kar diya, jiske falswaroop foset comics ke antargat comics kaa prakaashan samaapt ho gaya, iske sabhi charitron ke adhikaar haalaanki ab bhi foset comics ke paas hi the. 1970 ke dashak tak iske lagbhag sabhi charitron ke adhikaar bik chuke the, or phir 1980 main apane bache-khuche sabhi charitron ko disi ko saunpkar foset comics ko bhang kar diya gaya.
