 sinha pustkaalay patna kaa ek saarvajanik pustkaalay he. isaki sthapana 1924 main sachchidanand sinha ne kii thi. iskaa mul naam 'shrimati radhika sinha santhaan evam sachchidanand sinha pustkaalay' tha. 1955 main rajya sarkaar ne ise apane adhikaar main le liya.
 sinha pustkaalay kii librarian egg fazal bataati hai ki vartmaan samay main yahaan ek lakh 80 hajaar pustaken hai. vah kahati hai ki prayek varsh bihar sarkaar dwaara is librari ko 20 lakh rupee ke kareeb milte hai, jisamen se 75 hajaar rupee kitabon par or kareeb 36 hajaar rupee patrikaon par or shesh raashi vetnadi par kharch kiye jate hai. vah kehete hai ki yahaan pratidin 15 akhbaar or prayek maheene 27 patrikaayen aati hai.
