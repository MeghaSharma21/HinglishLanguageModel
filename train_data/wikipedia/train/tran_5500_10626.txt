 calories urja kii ikai he. yah maapan kii metry paddati kaa ang he or iske sangat es ai pranali main ab jul kaa prayog kiya jaataa he kintu bhojan main nihit urja tathaa kuch anya upyogon main ab bhi calories kaa hi prayog kiya jaataa he. 1000 calories ko 1 kilocalories kehete hai. sabase pehle professor nicholas clement ne 1824 main calories ko urja kii ikai ke rup main paribhaashit kiya. 1842 or 1867 ke madhya is shabd ko angreji evam french shabdkoshon main sammilit kiya gaya.
 1 gram jal kaa taap 1 degree celsius badhaane ke liye 1 calories ooshmaa kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. 1 calories lagbhag 4.2 jul ke baraabar hoti he.
