 madhya siberian pathaar uttar asia ke siberia ilaake main yenicey nadi or lenaa nadi ke bich main sthit ek pathaar he. iske alag-alag unchaaiyon vaale bhinn hisse hai. iskaa kul kshetrafal 35 lakh varg kimi he. iske uttar main pootorana pahaad hai or iske dakshin main purvi sayan parvat or bykaal parvat sthit hai. purv main yah pathaar yakuut ke maidan kii nichali bhoomi main badal jaataa he. agar puure siberia kaa kshetrafal dekha jaae to madhya siberian pathaar uskaa ek-tihaai hissaa he.
 is pathaar main choti garmiyaan or lambi bhayankar thand vaali sardiyaan hoti hai. iske adhikansh bhaag par konedhari vrikshon ke van faile hue hai. nichali tunguskaa nadi is kshetra kii pramukh nadi he. yahan bahut se khanij milte hai, jaise ki koyala, loha, sona, platinum, hira or prakritik gas. dakshin africa ke baad rus hi duniya kaa doosraa sabase bada platinum kaa utpaadak he or vishvabhar kii 30% platinum aapurti yaheen se aati he.
