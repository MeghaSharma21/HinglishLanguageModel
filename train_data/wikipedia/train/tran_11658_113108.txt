 bayalusima dakshin bharat ke karnataka rajya ke malnad kshetra ke purvi ore ke kshetra ko kaha jaataa he. yah failaa hua khulaa maidani kshetra he jisamen kuch tile mil jate hai. isamen dharwar, belgaum, gadag, haveri, bijapur, bagalkot, gulburg, raichur, coppal, bellari, chitra durg, devangiri, tumkur, hassan or mandya jile aate hai. yah naam sambhawatah kannada bhashaa se vyutpann he, jiskaa arth he samtal bhoomi yaa khuli bhoomi, kyonki yahaan se padosi kshetron kaa nirbaadh drishy sulabh hota he. isi kaaran se ise kai baar maidan bhi kaha jaataa he. bayalseema kii sthiti dackhin pathaar main malnad kshetra ke purvi ore pashchimi ghat ke purvi chor par he.
 bayalusima ke khanpan, boliyaan or sheliam bhinn sthanon par badalte rahete hai.
