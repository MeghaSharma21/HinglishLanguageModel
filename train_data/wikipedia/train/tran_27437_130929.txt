 tismar khan, 24 disambar 2010 ko pradarshit hone vaali ek bollywood film he, jiskaa nirdesan mai hoon naa or om shanti om jaisi safal filmon kii nirdeshak farah khan kar rahi hai. tismar khan main akshay kumaar, akshay khanna tathaa katrina caff kii mukhya bhoomikaayen he. film kaa nayak ek antarrashtriya thag he jo khud ko tismar khan yani chaalak samajhataa he.film kii patakatha shiris kundar ne likhi he, film main sangeet vishaal-shekhar kaa or geet javed akhtar ke hai. film kii patakatha shahrukh khan ko dhyaan main rakhkar likhi gayi thi, par kisi karanvash wo usame kaam nahin kar sake or yah bhoomika akshay kumaar kii jholii main chali gayi. aajkal film ko filmaaya ja rahaa he.
