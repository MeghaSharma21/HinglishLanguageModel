 bhartiya upmahadweep kii bhashaaon, granthon, itihaas, evam samskruti kaa adhyayan bharatvidya kehalaati he. yah asia adhyayan kaa ek bhaag he. ise bharat-adhyayan yaa dakshin-asin adhyayan bhi kaha jaataa he. 
 bharat vidya ke antargat bahut se kshetra aa jate hai jinaki takneekon ko dakshin asia ke upar lagu karte hue upyog kiya jaataa he. kuch kshetra is prakaar hai-
