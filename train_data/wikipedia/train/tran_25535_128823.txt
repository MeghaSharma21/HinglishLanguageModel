 bykaal parvat rus ke siberia kshetra ke dakshini bhaag main bykaal jhil ke uttarpashchimi chor par sthit ek parvat shrunkhlaa he. yah shrunkhlaa or purvi sayan parvat madhya siberian pathaar kii dakshini seemaa hai. siberia kaa ek pramukh dariyaa, lenaa nadi, bykaal pahaado main hi janm letaa he. bykaal jhil ke ird-gird ke pahari kshetra par ghaney van hai jinamein siberian sanobar, scoti chid or siberian prasaral jaise thande ilaaqon ke vriksh ugte hai. bykaal parvaton kaa sabase unchaa shikhar 2,572 meter unchaa cheraski parbat he.
