 ustad bade ghulaam ali kaan hindustani shastreey sangeet ke patiala gharaane ke gayak the. unki gananaa bharat ke mahanatam gayakon va sangitagnon main kii jaati he. inkaa janm lahore ke nikat kasoor naamak sthaan par pakistan main hua tha, par inhone apana jivan alag samayon par lahore, bombay, kolkata or hyderabad main vyateet kiya. prasiddh gajal gayak gulam ali inke shishy the. inkaa parivaar sangitagnon kaa parivaar tha. bade gulam ali khan kii sangeet kii duniya kaa prarambh saarangi vadak ke rup main hua baad main unhone apane pita ali baksh khan, chachaa kaale khan or baba scindia khan se sangeet ke gur siikhe. inke pita maharaja kashmir ke darbari gayak the or vah gharaana "kashmiri gharaana" kehalaata tha. jab ye log patiala jaakar rahane lage to yah gharaana "patiala gharaana" ke naam se janaa jane laga. apane sadhe hue kanth ke kaaran bade gulam ali khan ne bahut prasiddh pai. san 1919 ke lahore sangeet sammelan main bade gulam ali khan ne apni kala kaa pehali baar saarvajanik pradarshan kiya. iske kolkata or elahabaad ke sangeet sammelnon ne unhen deshvyapee khyaati dilaai. unhone apni behad sureeli or lochadar aawaaj tathaa abhinav style ke boote thumari ko ekadam naye andaaj main dhaala jisamen lok sangeet kii mithaas or taazgi donon maujuud thi. sangeet samikshakon ke anusaar ustad khan ne apane prayogdharmi sangeet kii badaulat thumari kii bol banaav style se pare jaakar usamen ek nayi taazgi bhar di. unki is style ko thumari ke punjab ang ke rup main janaa jaataa he. ve apane khyal gayan main dhrupad, gwalior gharaane or jaipur gharaane kii shaileyon kaa khubasurat sangyojan karte the. bade gulam ali khan ke munh se ek baar "radheshyaam bol" bhajan sunakar mahatma gandhi bahut prabhavit hue the. mughale ajam film main tansen paatr ke liye unhone hi apni awaz di thi. bharat sarkaar ne 1962 i. main unhen "padmabhushan" se sammanit kiya tha. 23 april 1968 i. ko bade gulam ali khan kaa dehavsaan ho gaya.
