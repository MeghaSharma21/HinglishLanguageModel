 heilonjiyang janavaadee ganraajya china ke sudur purvottar main sthit ek praant he jo aitihaasik manchuriya kshetra kaa bhaag he. heilonjiyang kaa matlab 'kaali andaha nadi' hota he, jo chini bhashaa main amur nadi kaa naam he. chini bhavchitron main iskaa sankshipt ekaakshari chihn '黑' he. is ilaake ko manchhu bhashaa main 'sahalion ula' kehete hai or mongol bhashaa main bhi iskaa naam kaaramoarin he. uttar or purv main is praant kii seemayen rus ko lagati hai. heilonjiyang praant kii rajdhani or sabase bada shahar harabin he. china ke sabase uttari or sabase purvi sthaan donon isi praant main padate hai. is shahar main bahut sardi padati he or yah sardiyon main yojit hone vaale barf-murtikla utsavon ke liye prasiddh he. is praant main manchhu, roosi, mongol or bahut se anya prabhaav nazar aate hai.
