 jain sant muni jinendra vijay madhya pradesh ke ratlam jile main paidaa hue. aapako ‘jalaj’ naam se bhi janaa jaataa he.
 jain kul main paidaa hone ke alaava bhi apni jain dharm main teevr jigyaasa ke kaaran baalyavastha main hi inhone grihasth jivan kaa tyaag kar jain diksha grahan kii or aatmakalyaan ke maarg par agrasar hue. diksha kaal main sanyam sadhana evam kathor jap-tap se gnanarjan ko pragatishil hue. apni kathor sadhana ke falswarup hi alp-aayu main jyotish vidya ke prakand gyaata ban inhone ‘jyotish samraat’ kii oopaadhi hasil kii.
 aatm kalyaan or gnanarjan ke saath hi samaaj kalyaan kii disha main bhi aap agrasar hai or saamaajik chetana or dharm prachaar ke uddeshy se pravachan or pustak rachana ke kshaitra main aap nirantar kriyasheel hai.aapke samajotthana ke prayaason ke parinaam swarup jain samaaj ne aap shri ko ‘malwa bhushan’ ki oopaadhi se nawaja he.
