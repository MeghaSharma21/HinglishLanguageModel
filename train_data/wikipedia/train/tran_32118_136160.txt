 super casets industries limited, bharat kii ek sangeet company he. iskaa sangeet billaa tii-series he. yah film nirmaataa evam vitarak company bhi he. baad main is company ne agarbatti or washing powder ke rup main anya upabhokta utpaadon ko nikaalnaa bhi armbh kiya.
 super casets, gopal casets & rajani industries tii-series ke bhaag hai.
 isaki sthapana gulshan kumaar ne kii or vartmaan main bhushan kumaar iskaa sanchaalan kar rahe hai. film nirmaan kaa kaary isane varsh 2001 main film tum bin se armbh kiya. iske dwaara jari kiya gaya pratham geet 1984 main lalu ram tha jiskaa sangeet rabindra jain ne diya tha.
 baad main isane 2009 tak tii-series brand ke adheen upabhokta electronics goods or audio-video system kaa nirmaan bhi kiya. iske baad main isane mobile phone handset bhi bazaar main utaare.
