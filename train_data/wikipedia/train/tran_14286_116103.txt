 uttamauja paanchal naresh drupad kaa putr or, dhridhyumna or draupadi kaa bhai. iske atirikt uske anya bhai the yudhmanyu or sathyajith or shikhandi.
 uttar vaidic parampara main jahaan srunjay panchalon ke saath sambhddha dikhalaae gaye hai, mahabharat main uttammauja ko paanchal tathaa srunjay donon hi kaha gaya he. mahabharat ke paatro main uttamauja ek parakramee raja tha jise 'yudhvishaarad' or 'veeryavan' kaha gaya he or jisane pandavon kii ore se yuddh kiya tha. dronacharya ke saath yuddh main vah veergati ko praapt hua.
