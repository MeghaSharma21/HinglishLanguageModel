 sialkot pakistan ke punjab praant kaa ek zila he. sialkot zile kii rajdhani sialkot shahar he. is zile kii chaar tahseelein hai - daska, pasarur, sambadiyaal or sialkot. yah zila pakistani punjab praant ke purvottar main sthit he. isaki seemayen pashchimottar main gujarat zile se, purvottar main bharat ke jammu zile se, dakshin purv main narowal zile seore dakshin-pashchim main gujaranvaala zile se lagatee hai.
 sialkot zile main san 2011 main 42,00,000 logon kii aabaadi thi. iskaa kshetrafal qareeb 3,016 varg kimi he. is zile kaa naam pracheen raja salivahan par padaa he or iskaa sambandh uske prasiddh putr raja rasaalu se bhi he jisapar punjabi lok-samskruti main kathaayen or gane hai. sialkot zile kaa pasarur shahar ek pracheen sthal he jisaki sthapana paras ram naamak ek brahman ne kii thi. pratham mogul samrajya samraat babar ne apane babarnama main bhi pasarur kaa zikr kiya he ki vah vahaan rukaa tha. is kshetra par samay-samay par pados ke jammu raajaaon kaa qabza hota rahata tha. mogul samrajya ke kamazor padane par yahan ranjit singh dev naamak ek dogra rajput raja kaa shashan ho gaya jisane is kshetra par afghanistan ke durrani samrajya ke failane par ahamed shah durrani ki adheenta sweekaar lee. sikh samrajya ke ubharne par yahan uskaa raaj aayaa. azaadi se thik pehle sialkot zile main muslimon ki bahutayat thi haalanki sialkot shahar main hindu bahut the. prasiddh kavi allamaa ikbaal, bhartiya abhinayta e ke hangal or prasiddh puraane hindi-film nayak rajendra kumaar sabhi sialkot main paidaa hue the.
