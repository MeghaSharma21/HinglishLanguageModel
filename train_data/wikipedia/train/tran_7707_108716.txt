 kra thalsandhi dakshinpurv asia main malay prayadweep kaa sabase kam chaudaai vaala bhaag he. iske purv ore thailand kii khadi he jabaki pashchimi taraf andaman sagar he. is thalsandhi kaa purvi bhaag thailand main aata he or pashchimi bhaag burma ke tanintharyi mandal kaa hissaa he. apane sabase tang bindu par kra thalsandhi kii chaudaai keval 44 kimi he. yadi yahan khodne se ek jalmaarg thailand kii khadi ko andaman sagar se jod de to purvi asia se pashchimi asia ke jal yatayaat ko neeche se malakka jalsandhi se jane kii aavashyaktaa nahin rahegi. lekin yah karnaa kathin he kyonki kra thalsandhi par tenacerim pahadiyaan naamak ek pahari shrunkhlaa khadi hui he.
