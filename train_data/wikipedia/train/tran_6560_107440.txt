 putorana pathaar yaa putorana pahaad rus ke siberia kshetra main sthit ek unchaa pathaar he. yah madhya siberian pathaar ke pashchimottaree chor par taimayir prayadweep se dakshin main sthit ek pahari ilaaka he. iskaa sabase unchaa pahaad 1,700 meter unchaa kamen parvat he. rus kaa bhugolic centre is pathaar par sthit vivi jhil main padataa he. yahan zamin ke neeche vishwa ke sabase bade nikal ke khanij bhandar hai. yahan aabaadi kam hone se prakruti kaa raaj he or duniya ke sabase bade rangifer jhund bhi yaheen paae jate hai.
