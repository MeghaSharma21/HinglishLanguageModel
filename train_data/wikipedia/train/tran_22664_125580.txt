 swarrakalpana kapolkalpit bramhand main aadhaarit kapolkalpana kii ek vidha he, jisamen aksar koi bhi sthaan, ghatnaayen, yaa log vaastavik duniya se sandarbh nahin rakhte hai. isaki jaden maukhik paramparaon main hai, jo baad main sahitya or naatak ban gayi. beesvin shatabdi se yah film, televijan, graphic upanyaas or video game sahit vibhinn media main vistaarit hui he. yah ek filmi vidha he jisamen saamanytah jaadu or paraprakritik parighatnaon ko kathanak ke praathmik avyav ke rup main dikhaaya jaataa he.
 swarrakalpana atkal kapolkalpana kii ek upavidha he. isamen vaigyanik or bhayankar themon ke abhaav se, yah kramtah vigyaan kapolkalpana or vibhats se alag he, bhale hi ye vidhaayen ek duusare kaa atichhaadan karti hai. lokapriy samskruti main, swarrakalpana mukhyatah madhyayugin rup kii hoti he. iske vyapak arthon main, haalanki, swarrakalpana main pracheen mithkon or kinvadantiyon se kai lekhakon, kalakaaron, film nirmaataaon, or sangeetkaaron dwaara kaaryon se lekar kai haalia or lokapriy kaary shaamil hai.
