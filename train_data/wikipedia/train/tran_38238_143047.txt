 ben mathew dacket  ek antarrashtriya str ke cricket khilaadi he jo ki england kii rashtriya cricket team ke liye khelte hai. ye gharelu cricket main northmptenshir county cricket team ke liye khelte he.
 ben dacket ek ballebaaji or wicket-keeper kii bhoomika nibhaate hai. inhone apane test cricket karier kii shuruyaat 20 oktober 2016 ko bangladesh cricket team ke khilaaf kii thi jabaki vanade kaa pehla match bhi bangladesh ke hi khilaaf 6 aktubar 2016 ko khelaa tha.
