 okaongo delta vastav main adwitiya or duniya main sabase
 bada antardeshiy delta he. 
 yah delta mukhya rup se botswana main sthit he. samudr main bahane ke bajaay yah kalahari marusthal ke ret main daldali delta banaati he.har varsh baadh aane par okaongo nadi kaa panikalahari ret ke lagunon or channelon kii bhulbhulaiya main15000 kimi se adhik falkar daldal bana detaa he. is jal ke failaav ne kai vividh paaritantron ko janm diya. 
 yahan kaa shandaar vanya jivan paryatakon ko bahut aakarshith karata he. unesco ne ise vishwa dharohar sthal ghoshit kiya he. africa main sabase zyada hathi yahi he. yahan safed or kaale gande vishisht he.
