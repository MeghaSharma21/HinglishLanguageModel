 lord duffrin bharat ke governor general rahe the. lord dafarin 1884 i. main lord ripan ke baad bharat kaa vysarai banakar aayaa. vah 1884 se 1888 i. tak bharat kaa visarai tathaa governor-general rahaa tha. saamaanya taur par uskaa shasankaal shantipurna tha, lekin tritiy burma yuddh usee ke kaarykaal main hua, jiske falswaroop uttari burma british bhartiya samrajya kaa ang ban gaya.
