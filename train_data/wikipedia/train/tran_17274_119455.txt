 rashtriya bhartiya ayurvigyaan sampada santhaan bharat kaa ek santhaan he jo ayurveda, yoga, unani, siddh, homeopathy, sova rigpa evam aadhunik chikitsa ke aitihaasik drushti se adhyayan evam pralekhan main ruchi rakhane vaale itihaaskaaron, vaigyanikon evam anya logon ke liye saamagree uplabdh karaata he. yah santhaan kendriya ayurvedic vigyaan anusandhan parishad, aayush vibhaag, swasthya evam parivaar kalyaan mantralay, bharat sarkaar ke prashaskeey niyantran ke adheen kaary kar rahaa he. dakshin-purvi asia kaa apni tarah kaa ek maatr yah santhaan gaddiannaram, dilasukhanagar, hyderabad main naveen bhawan main sthit he.
 is santhaan kaa naam pehle 'bhartiya ayurvigyaan itihaas santhaan' tha. pehle yah osmania medical college, hyderabad ke tritiy tal par sthit tha. yah dinaank 26-09-1956 se andhrapradesh sarkaar ke adheen unnat ayurvigyaan itihaas vibhaag ke rup main astitva main aayaa evam iske rashtriya evam anterrashtriya mahatva ke kaaran bharat sarkaar ko 14-02-1969 ko hastaantarit kiya gaya.
 ayurvigyaan itihaas par anusandhan saamagree ke apane vishaal sankalan ke kaaran yah vaishvik pahunch main he. santhaan ke paas ek chikitsiya-aitihaasik pustkaalay, sangrahalay or pralekhan vibhaag he. sanstha kaa pustkaalay 10,000 se adhik pustakon ke sankalan se vilakshan he, jisamen vibhinn bhashaaon main vaidyak itihaas or sambaddh vishyon par 15veen sadi se purv ke atidurlabh prakaashan sammilit he evam vaidyak sahyog ke kaary liye yah anusandhan sambandhit kendra he. isamen ayurvigyaan itihaas par ucchstar kii rashtriya or anterrashtriya patrikaon kaa iske armbh se purna sankalan he. yah santhaan en.tii.aar. university of health sainses, andhrapradesh dwaara manyata praapt piech.di. pradaan kar rahaa he.
