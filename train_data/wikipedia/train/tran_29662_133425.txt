 aariq boke mongol samrajya se sansthaapak changez khan kaa pota or uske sabase chote bete tolui khan kaa sabase chota putr tha. uski mata sorboogtani beki ne use or uske bhaaiyon ko bahut nipunata se paala. uskaa bada bhai mongake khan kuch arase ke liye samrajya kaa kagaan rahaa. jab 1259 main mongake kii mrityu ho he to uske donon bade bhai halaku khan or kubalai khan mongol grihabhumi se anupasthit the or aariq boke ne shashan kii bagdor sanbhali. 1260 main nae kagaan ke chunaav ke liye kubalai mongolia waapas aayaa to rajasambandhi do khemon main baant gaye - ek aariq boke kii tarafadaari karne laga or to doosraa kubalai khan kii. mongol grihayuddh chhid gaya, jisamen mongol samrajya kii puraani prathaon ke samarthakon ne aariq boke kaa saath diya jabaki manchuriya or uttar china ke rajkunwaron ne kubalai khan kaa. ant main aariq boke kii haar hui or usne kubalai khan ke aage hathiyaar daal die. kubalai khan ne bhai hone ke naate aariq boke kaa jivan to baksh diya lekin uske saathiyon ko maar daalaa. iske baad kubalai ne bina kisi rokatoke ke mongol samrajya ke paanchave kagaan ke rup main raaj kiya.
