 yaa mohammad, yaa ali, yaa ali madad, yaa hossain or yaa rasul dharmik naare hai jo islam ke anuyaayi ishvar se shakti yaa sahaayta maangane ke liye kehete hai. aksar yah kisi jokhim vaale yaa kathin kaam karne se bilkul pehle kahe jate hai. bhartiya upmahadweep main bahut se gair-muslim log, jaise kii hindu, bhi kabhi-kabhi "yaa khuda" jaise kisi vishesh dharm se sambandh na rakhe vaale naaron kaa prayog karte hai.
 "yaa" multah arabi kaa shabd he jiskaa arth sanskrit ke "he" or hindi-urdu ke "i" shabd se miltaa julata he.
