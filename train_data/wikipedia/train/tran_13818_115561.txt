 abhadra pradarshan kisi saarvajanik jagah par yaa saarvajanik rup se kisi vyakti dwaara sharir kaa kuch bhaag ko dikhaanaa, jo saamaajik rup se bhadra nahin hota abhadra pradarshan kehalaata he. is par bahut kam antar se anya deshon main badlaav he. lekin is par kanoon keval kuch hi deshon main bana he.
 iske alag-alag jagah par alag-alag maanak he. kai deshon main stri kaa koi bhi sharir kaa bhaag dikhaai nahin dena chahie. is kaaran use upar se neeche tak acchhee tarah se kapade pahanane hote hai. kuch deshon mukhyatah england or anya europea deshon main aisa nahin hai. vahaan kuch jagah par kai stri apane upar stan ko bhi nahin dhankati hai.
 iske maanak main mukhyatah stri dwaara apane kapade nikaalnaa yaa nikaalte hue uskaa pradarshan karnaa yaa apane guptang kaa pradarshan isamen shaamil he. isamen saamanytah stri dwaara apane bra ko kuch samay ke liye kholkar chuchuk ko dikhaanaa america main kai aise ghatnaon main shaamil he.
