 public provident fund athwa saarvajanik bhavishya nidhi ) bharat main bachat evam kar-bachat karne ke liye prayukt ek jamaa yojana he. bahut se log ise sevanivruti ke samay dhan prapti kaa saadhan bhi maanate hai. 
 pipief kaa khaataa kisi daakghar main, bhartiya state bank kii kisi shakha main, tathaa kuch anya rashtrikrut bainkon main khole ja sakate hai. isiisiai bank bharat kaa pehla nijee swaamitv vaala bank he jise pipief khaataa kholane kii anumati he.
 1 koi bhi vayask apane naam main yaa kisi naaballig ke naam main pipief khaataa khol saktaa he.
 2 khate kii avadhi 15 varsh hoti he jisaki samaapti par khate ko paanch-paanch varsh ke liye kitni bhi baar badhaayaa ja saktaa he. bade hue khate main bhi kar aadi ke sabhi laabh milte rahete hai.
 3 khate main prativarsh kam se kam 500 rupe jamaa karnaa anivaary he. adhiktam ek lakh pachaas hajaar rupaye prativarsh jamaa kiye ja sakate hai. yah raashi varsh main 12 yaa isse kam kishton main kabhi bhi jamaa kar sakate hai.
 4 kisi maheene kii 5 taarikh se us maheene kii antim taarikh ke bich jo nunatam raashi khate main hoti he usee par vyaaj miltaa he. vyaaj kii dar sarkaar samay-samay par nirdhaarit karti he.
 5 pipief main jamaa kii gayi raashi 80C ke antargat aamadani main se ghataayi jaati he. isi prakaar pipief se praapt mooldhan tathaa vyaaj iskar evam sampatti kar se mukt hota he.
 6 aavashyak hone par pipief khate main jamaa raashi se rin bhi liya ja saktaa he.
 7 saat varsh ke baad khate se aanshik nikasi bhi kii ja sakati he.
 8 pipief khate main kisi ko namit kiya ja saktaa he tathaa mul khataadhari kii mrityu kii dasha main namit vyakti ko is khate kaa maalik maan liya jaayegaa.
 9 khate ko bank se daakghar yaa daakghar se bank main sthaanantarit kiya ja saktaa he.
