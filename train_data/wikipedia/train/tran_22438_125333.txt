 durg vishvavidhyalay ke pratham kulapati ke rup main niyukt dow dikshit vartmaan main rusaa ke sadsy va government science college, durg ke purv praachaarya rah chuke hai. unki pehali niyukti sahaayak praadhyaapak ke taur par san 1971 main shaskeey kanya mahavidhyalay, bilaspur main hui iske baad june 1976 main inkaa sthanantaran science college durg main hua san 2000 se 2007 tak science college durg main praadhyaapak ke saath hi exam controlar kaa kaarybhaar bhi sambhala phir san 2007 se 2010 tak science college durg ke praachaarya rahe dow dikshit ucch shiksha mantri prem prakaash pandeya sahit pradesh ke kai nami hastiyon ke guru rahe hai.
