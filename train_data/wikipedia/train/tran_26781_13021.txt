 maun kaa arth andar or baahar se chup rahanaa he.
 aamtaur par ham ‘maun’ kaa arth honton kaa naa chalanaa mana jaataa he. yah bada simit arth he.
 kabir ne kaha he:
 kabiraa yah gat atpati, chatpat lakhi na jaae.jab man kii khatpat mite, adhar bhayaa tehraay.
 adhar matlab hont. hont vastav main tabhi thahrengen, tabhi shant honge, jab man kii khatpat mit jaayegi. hamaare hont bhi zyada isiilie chalate hai kyonki man ashaant he, or jab tak man ashaant he, tab tak hont chalem yaa na chalem koi antar nahin kyonki mul baat to man kii ashaanti he. wo bani hui hamel
 to kisi ko shabdaheen dekhakar ye mat samaj lenaa ki wo maun ho gaya he. wo bahut jor se chilla rahaa he, shabdaheen hokar chilla rahaa he. wo pagal hi he, bas uske shabd sunai nahin de rahe. wo bol rahaa he bas awaz nahin aa rahi. shabdaheenta ko, dhvaniheenta ko maun mat samaj lenaa.
 maun he- man kaa shant ho janaa arthath kalpanaaon kii vyarth udaan na bhare. aantarik maun main lagaataar shabd maujuud bhi rahen to bhi, maun bana hi rahata he. us maun main tum kuch bolte bhi raho to us maun par koi antar nahin padataa.
