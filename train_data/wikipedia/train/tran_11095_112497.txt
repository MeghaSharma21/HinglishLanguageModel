 oktober ski 1999 kaa ek chalchitr he, jiske nirmaataa he charles gordon or nirdeshak joi jaunston or mukhya bhumika main hai - jack gilanhaul, kris kupar or laura darn. yah chalchitr homer hikem ki sachhee kahani par aadhaarit he, jo ek koyale kii khaadaan main kaam karne vaale vyakti kaa betaa hota he jo soviet sangh dwaara pratham sputanik ke prakshepan ke baad rocket vigyaan main ruchi letaa he or antat nasa kaa ek vaigyanik banataa he.
