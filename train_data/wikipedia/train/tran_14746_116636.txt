 
 ananas saativus'
 ananas  ek khaadya ushnkaribandheey paudhe evam uske fal kaa saamaanya naam he haalanki takneekee drushti se dekhen, to ye anek falon kaa samooh vilay ho kar nikalataa he. yah multah paragway evam dakshini brazil kaa fal he. ananas ko taaja kaat kar bhi khaaya jaataa he or shire main sangrakshit kar yaa ras nikaal kar bhi seven kiya jaataa he. ise khaane ke uparaant mithe ke rup main salad ke rup main evam fruit-cocktail main maansaahaar ke vikalp ke rup main prayog bhi kiya jaataa he. mishtann rup main ye ucch str ke amleey swabhaav kaa hota he. ananas krishi kiya gaya bromelyakeai ekmaatr fal he. 
 ananas ke aushadhiy gun bhi bahut hote hai. ye sharir ke bhitari vishon ko baahar nikalataa he. isamen chlorine kii bharpoor maatra hoti he. saath hi pitt vikaron main vishesh rup se or pilia yani pandu rogon main laabhakaari he. ye gale evam mutr ke rogon main laabhadaayak he. iske alaava ye haddiyon ko majboot banaata he. ananas main prachur maatra main magnesium paaya jaataa he. yah sharir kii haddiyon ko majboot banane or sharir ko urja pradaan karne kaa kaam karata he. ek pyaalaa ananas ke ras-seven se din bhar ke liye aavashyak magnesium ke 75% kii purti hoti he. saath hi ye kai rogon main upyogi hota he. is fal main paaya jane vaala bromilen sardi or khaansee, sujan, gale main kharash or gathiya main laabhadaayak hota he. yah pachan main bhi upyogi hota he. ananas apane gunon ke kaaran netra-jyoti ke liye bhi upyogi hota he. din main teen baar is fal ko khaane se badhati umr ke saath ankhon kii roshni kam ho jane kaa khatra kam ho jaataa he. austrailia ke vaigyanikon ke shodhon ke anusaar yah cancer ke khatre ko bhi kam karata he. ye ucch antioxident kaa strot he va isamen vitamin si prachur maatra main paaya jaataa he. isse sharir kii pratirohak kshamta badhati he or saadhaaran thand se bhi suraksha milti he. isse sardi samet kai anya sankraman kaa khatra kam ho jaataa he. 
 san 2013 main puure vishwa main ananas kaa utpaadan 248 lakh ton tha. sabase adhik utpaadan costa rika main hua tha.
