 anterrashtriya vaayu yatayaat sangh ek anterrashtriya vayusevaon kaa udyog vyapaar samooh he, jiskaa mukhyalay montrial, canada main sthit he. yaheen par anterrashtriya nagar vimaanan sanghathan kaa mukhyalay bhi sthit he. is sanghathan kaa uddeshy he vayuseva udyog kii seva or badhaava dena. yah sangh 280 vayusevaon kaa pratinidhitiv karata he, jo anusuchit anterrashtriya vaayu yatayaat kaa 93% bhaag he. is sanghathan ke mahanideshak hai giovani bisignani. vartmaan main yah sanghathan 150 se adhik deshon main vyaapt he or iske vishwavyaapee 101 karyaalay hai.
 IATA kii sthapana april, 1945, main hawana, cuba main hui thi. yah anterrashtriya vaayu trephic sanghathan kaa uttaradhikari he, jisaki sthapana 1919 main da hegg main hui thi. isi varsh vishwa kii pratham anusuchit vimaan seva kaa aarambh hua tha. sthapana ke samay IATA main 31 rashtron se 57 sadsy the. inamen se adhikansh europe or uttari america se the. aaj iske 240 se dhik sadsy hai, jo ki vishwa ke 140 se adhik rashtron se hai.
