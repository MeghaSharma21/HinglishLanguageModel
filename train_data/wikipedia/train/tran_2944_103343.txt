 purulia jila bhartiya rajya pashchim bengal kaa ek prashaskeey jila he.iske uttar evam uttar-pashchim main jharkhand rajya ke dhanbad evam hazaribagh jile, pashchim main bokaro evam ranchi, dakshin main singhbhum tathaa purv main pashchimi bengal rajya kaa bankura jila sthit he. is jile kaa pramukh nagar purulia he. aadra, balrampur, raghunathpur, evam jhalda aadi is jile ke pramukh nagar hai. iske purvi bhaag main jalodh mitti milti he tathaa yah ek upjau bhaag he. dhan kii kheti jile main adhik kii jaati he.yapar main yahan chaaval kaa sthaan pramukh hai.
