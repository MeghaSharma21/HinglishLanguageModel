 firial arun grah kaa ek upgrah he. akar main yah arun kaa chauthaa sab se bada upgrah he. firial arun ke saare upgrahon main se sab se adhik chamakdaar he. arun ke anya bade chandramaaon kii tarah, firial bhi barf or pathar kaa bana hua he. isaki sataha barfili or andar kaa kendriya bhaag patthariilaa he. isaki sataha bahut oobad-khabad he or usapar oonche tile or gahari khaaiyaan donon dikhaai detii hai. wayager dwitiya yaan ke 1986 main arun ke paas se guzarane par firial kii sataha ke lagbhag 35% hisse ke nakshe banaae ja chuke hai. filhal firial kaa adhyayan karne ke liye koi or antariksh yaan bhejne kii koi yojana nahin he. haalanki arun ke saare bade upgrahon par bhukampon ke suraag milte hai, lekin aisa prathit hota he ke firial par aaye hue bukamp sab se tazaa hai.
 firial kaa akar gol he. iskaa ausat vyas lagbhag 1158 kimi he. iske muqaabale main prithvi ke chandramaa kaa vyas lagbhag 3,474 kimi he, yani kii firial kaa akar hamaare chandramaa ke ek-tihaai se zaraa chota he.
