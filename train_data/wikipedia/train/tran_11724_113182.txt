 peleosin yug, jise puranuthan yug bhi kehete hai, prithvi ke bhuvaigyanik itihaas kaa ek bhuvaigyanik yug he jo aaj se lagbhag 6.6 karod varsh pehle armbh hua or 5.6 karod varsh pehle tak chalaa. yah peliosin kalp or nutanjivi mahakalp kaa sarvapratham yug tha. is se pehle madhyajivi mahakalp ke antim kalp, chaakmay kalp, chal rahaa tha. peleosin yug ke ant hone par eosin yug armbh hua. peloyosea kalp or chaakmay kalp kii samay-seemaa par kriteshs-peliosin vilupti ghatana hui jisamen dianasor samet prithvi kii bahut-si jeev jatiyaan maari gai or stanadhariyon ko ubharne kaa avasar mila.
