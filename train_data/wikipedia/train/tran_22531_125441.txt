 gunatmak acarbonic vishleshan vishleshnatmak rasayan kii ek vidhi he jo acarbonic yogicon main upasthit mulkon kaa pataa lagane ke liye he. yah ek gaiyr-upkarniya vidhi he. upkarniya vishleshan ke vikaas ke kaaran is vidhi kaa vyaavhaarik mahatv nahin rah gaya he. kintu ab bhi yah pramukhataa se padhai/sikhaai jaati he. 
 iskaa mukhya dhyey lavenon ke jaleey vilyanon main upasthit yanon kaa pataa lagaana he. isaliye anya rup main maujuud padaarth ko pehle jaleey vilyan ke rup main laana jaruri ho saktaa he tabhi maanak vidhiyaan kaam main laayi ja sakati hai. iske baad us vilyan ko bhinn-bhinn abhikarmakon dwaara kriya karaayaa jaataa he jisase rang main parivartan, thos avakshep kaa nirmaan, yaa koi anya drushtigochar parivartan hota he jisase uchit nishkarsh nikaala jaataa he.
 uparokt vidhi shikshan ke liye prayukt hoti he. kintu vartmaan samay main parmaanu avasorana spectroscopy yaa ICP-emes kaa upyog kar ek hi upkaran dwaara, rasayan kii kam maatra kaa upyog karte hue, jaldi va sahi parinaam praapt kiya jaataa he.
 rasinic gunon ke aadhaar par praayah dhanayanon ko paanch samoohon main baanta jaataa he. har samooh ke liye ek-ek abhikarmak nirdhaarit he jiskaa upyog karke vilyan se use alag kiya jaataa he. dhyaan rahe ki abhikarmak ek nishchit kram main hi milaae jane chahie anyathaa kuch lavan agle samooh ke liye nirdhaarit abhikarmak ke dwaara bhi prakshepit ho jate hai jisase bhram kii sthiti paidaa ho jaaegi. ek samooh ko prakshipt kar lene ke baad kuch vishisht abhikriyaayein karakar pataa kiya jaataa he ki us samooh kaa koun sa tatv upasthit he.
