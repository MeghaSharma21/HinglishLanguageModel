 giddh shikari pakshiyon ke antargat aanevaale murdaakhor pakshi hai, jinhen griddha kul main ekatra kiya gaya he. ye sab pakshi do bhaagon main baante ja sakate hai. pehle bhaag main america ke condar, king valchar, californian valchar, turki bazard or amareeki blike valchar hote hai or duusare bhaag main africa or asia ke rajgriddha, kaala giddh, chamar giddh, bada giddh or gobar giddh mukhya hai.
 ye katthi or kaale rang ke baari kad ke pakshi hai, jinaki drushti bahut tej hoti he. shikari pakshiyon kii tarah inaki chonch bhi tedi or majboot hoti he, lekin inke panje or nakhun unke jaise tej or majboot nahin hote. ye jhundon main rahane vaale murdakher pakshi hai jinase koi bhi gandi or ghinauni cheez khaane se nahin bachati. ye pakshiyon ke mehtar hai jo safai jaisaa aavashyak kaam karke beemaari nahin failane dete.
 ye kisi unche ped par apana bhadda sa ghonslaa banaate hai, jisamen maadaa ek yaa do safed andey detii he.
 bharat main giddh kii jo prajatiyaan paayi jaati hai vah is prakaar hai:-
 yah jati aaj se kuch saal pehle apane puure kshetra main prachur maatra main paayi jaati thi. 1990 ke dashak main is jati kaa 97% se 99% patan ho gaya he. iskaa multah kaaran pashu dawaai dyclophinak he jo ki pashuon ke jodon ke dard ko mitaane main madad karti he. jab yah dawaai khaaya hua pashu mar jaataa he or usko marane se thodaa pehle yah dawaai di gai hoti he or usko bhartiya giddh khaataa he to uske gurde band ho jate hai or vah mar jaataa he. ab nai dawaai maloxicum meloxicam aa gai he or yah hamaare gidhon ke liye hanikaarak bhi nahin hai. jab is dawaai kaa utpaadan bad jaayegaa to saare pashu-paalak iskaa istemaal karenge or shaayad hamaare giddh batch jaayen.
