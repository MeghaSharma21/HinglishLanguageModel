 germany kii ekikrut team ne pashchim germany or purvi germany ke athleton kii sanyukt team ke rup main 1956, 1960, or 1964 ke sheetakaaleen or greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main bhaag liya. 1956 main team main tisare olympic nikaay, sarland olympic samiti se athlete shaamil the, jinhone 1960 main ek alag team ko bheja tha, lekin 1964 main german rashtriya olympic samiti main shaamil hone kii prakriya main tha. yah prakriya farvari 1957 main sarland kii FRG main pravesh ke baad puri hui thi.
