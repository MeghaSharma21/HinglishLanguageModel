 bhaajan ganit main vah kriya he jisase do sankhyaon kaa gunanfal or in sankhyaon main se ek ke die rahane par duusari gyaat kii jaati he. die hue gunanfal ko 'bhajya', di hui sankhya ko 'bhaajak' or abhisht sankhya ko 'bhagfal' kehete hai. 18 / 3 = 6 main bhajya = 18, bhaajak = 3, bhajanfal=6 he. spasht he ki yadi bhajya ya or bhaajak k dhan purna sankhyaayein hai, to bhagfal l tabhi purna sankhya hoga jab ya, k kaa samapvartak ho, kintu yadi ya do kramagat samaapvartyo k*r or k* ke bich main he to r ko bhagfal or ko shesh kehete hai. is bhaajan kriya ko 'sashesh bhaajan' kehete hai.
