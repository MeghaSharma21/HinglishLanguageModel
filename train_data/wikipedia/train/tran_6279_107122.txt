 yojit khel pratiyogita main, match phixing, khel phixing yaa daud phixing match kii us avasthaa ko kehete hai jinamein match ke niyamon kaa ulanghan karte hue parinaam pehle se hi nishchit ho jate hai.
 spot phixing, match phixing se bilkul alag he. spot phixing main aavashyak nahin ki vah khel ke parinaam ko prabhavit kare lekin match phixing main khilaadi sidhe-sidhe match ke nirnay ko prabhavit karte hai. koi khilaadi jaanboojhkar apni samarthy se kam pradarshan karata he taaki uskaa laabh virdohi team ko mili. jab match main ekadam baraabar kii takkar ho rahi ho, tab ek mahatvapurn khilaadi bhi nirnay ko palat saktaa he. anek baar phixing kii daldal main kai khilaadi ekasaath shaamil hote hai jo ‘talmail’ dikhaate hue match ke nattejey ko prabhavit karte hai.
