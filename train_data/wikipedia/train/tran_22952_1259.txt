 dakshin africa cricket team vartmaan main july or agast 2018 main do test, paanch ek divasiy antarrashtriya or ek twanti-20 antarrashtriya match khilne ke liye srilanka kaa daura kar rahi he. mul rup se, yah daura teen test maichon ke liye tha, lekin teesraa match gira diya gaya or vanade or tii20i phiksture dwaara pratisthapit kiya gaya. atirikt vanade phiksture kaa istemaal 2019 cricket vishwa cap ke liye taiyaari ke rup main kiya jayega.
 daure ke aage, srilanka cricket ne dinesh chandimal ko test said ke captain ke rup main namit kiya. haalanki, june 2018 main saint louisia main westeindies ke khilaaf duusare test ke dauraan kathith gend-chhedchhad main unki bhoomika ke liye chandimal ko anushaasanamak aaropon kaa saamana karnaa padaa. unki sunwaayee pehle test kii shuruuaat se do din pehle hone vaali he, or yadi pai jaati he, to unhen do maichon kaa pratibandh milegaa, suranga lackmal apane sthaan par team kaa netritv karne kii sambhaavna rakhte hai.
