 es/2004 en 1, neptune kaa ek chota sa chaand he. vyas main yah 11 mile he or apane swami grah kii parikrama mahaj ek prithvi diwas main karata he. july 2013 main isaki khoj ne neptune ke gyaat chaakar upgrahon ko badhaakar chaudaa kar diya. yah chandramaa itnaa mand he ki 1989 main vayejar 2 kii udan tak ise dekha nahin gaya tha. seti institute ke mark showalter ne ise 2004 se 2009 ke bich habal spes telescope dwaara khinchi sangrahit neptune tasveeron kaa vishleshan kar paaya he.
