 shaaririki, sharir yaa sharirarachana-vigyaan, jeev vigyaan or ayurvigyaan kii ek shakha he jiske antargat kisi jivit vastu kaa vichchhedan kar, uske ang pratyang kii rachana kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. achal main vanaspatijagat tathaa chal main pranijagat kaa samavesh hota he or vanaspati or praani ke sandarbh main ise kramash: padap shaaririki or jeev shaaririki kaha jaataa he. jab kisi vishesh praani athwa vanaspati kii sharirarachana kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he, tab ise vishesh shaaririki adhyayan kehete hai. jab kisi praani yaa vanaspati kii sharirarachana kii tulana kisi duusare praani athwa vanaspati kii sharirarachana se kii jaati he us sthiti main yah adhyayan tulanaatmak shaaririki kehalaata he. jab kisi praani ke angon kii rachana kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he, tab yah angik shaaririki kehalaati he.
 vyaavhaarik yaa loukik drushti se maanav sharirarachana kaa adhyayan atyant hi mahatvapurn he. ek chikitsak ko sharirarachana kaa adhyayan kai drushti se karnaa hota he, jaise rup, sthiti, aakaar evam anya rachanaaon se sambandh.
 aakaarikeey sharirarachana vigyaan kii drushti se maanavsharir ke bheetar angon kii utpatti ke kaarnon kaa gyaan, anveshan kaa vishay ban gaya he. is gyaan kii vruddhi ke liye bhrunvigyaan, jeevvikas vigyaan, jativikas vigyaan evam utak vigyaan kaa adhyayan aavashyak he.
 swasth maanav sharir kii rachana kaa adhyayan nimna bhaagon main kiya jaataa he:
 1. chikitsashastreey sharirarachana vigyaan, 
 2. shalyachikitsa sharirarachana vigyaan, 
 3. stri sharir vishesh rachana vigyaan, 
 4. dharatleey sharirarachana vigyaan, 
 5. sookshmdarsheey sharirarachana vigyaan tathaa 
 6. bhrun sharirarachana vigyaan .
 vikrut angon kii rachana ke gyaan ko vikrut sharirarachanavigyaan kehete hai.
 maanav kii vibhinn prajatiyon kii sharirarachana kaa jab tulanaatmak adhyayan kiya jaataa he, tab maanavvigyaan kaa sahaara liya jaataa he. aajkal sharirarachana kaa adhyayan sarvangi vidhi se kiya jaataa he.
 isa se 1,000 varsh purv maharishi sushrut ne shavchhed kar sharirarachana kaa paryaapt varnan kiya tha. dhire-dhire yah gyaan arab or yunaan hota hua europe main pahunchaa or vahaan par iskaa bahut vistaar evam unnati hui. shav kii sangraksha ke saadhan, sookshmdarshi, axre aadi ke uplabdh hone par sharirarachana vigyaan kaa adhyayan adhik sookshm evam vistrut ho gaya he. sharir rachana kii sabase choti ikai koshika he. bahut si koshikaaye milakar utak banate hai; ek yaa anek prakaar ke utakon se ang banate hai; kai ang milakar ek tantra banaate hai. sharir kai tantron kaa samooh he.
 sharir kaa nirmaan karanevaale jivit ekak ko koshika kehete hai. yah sookshmdarshi se dekhi ja sakati he. koshika ek svachh lasalase ras se, jise jeevadravya kehete hai, bhari rahati he. koshika ko chaaron ore se gheranevaali kala ko koshika membrane kehete hai. koshika ke centre main newclius rahata he, jo koshika par niyantran karata he. koshika ke jivit hone kaa lakshan yahi he ki usamen abhikriya, shakti, ekikaran shakti, vruddhi, visarjan shakti tathaa utpaadan shakti, upasthit rahe. sharir kaa swasthya koshikaon ke swasthya par nirbhar karata he. kaaryanusaar koshikaaye apana aakaar ityadi parivartit kar, bhinn bhinn virgon main vibhajit hoti hai, jaise tantrika koshika, asthi koshika, peshi koshika aadi.
 ek prakaar kii akruti evam kaary karanevaali koshikaaye milakar, ek vishesh prakaar ke utak kaa nirmaan karti hai. utak mukhyat: paanch prakaar ke hote hai : 
  upakalaa, 
  sanyoji utak, 
  scalerus utak, 
  peshi utak tathaa 
  tantrika utak.
 vistrut vivaran ke liye utak dekhiye.
 sharir kaa nirmaan nimnlikhit tantron dwaara hota he : 
  asthi tantra, 
  sandhi tantra, 
  peshi tantra, 
  rudhir parivahan tantra, 
  aashay tantra : 
  tantrika tantra tathaa 
  gyanendriya tantra.
 vistrut vivaran ke liye sharir ke tantra dekhiye.
 sharirshastra kii yah mahatvapurn shakha he or shalya chikitsa tathaa rog nidaan main atyant sahaayak hoti he. isi se gyaat hota he ki dahini dasvi parshuka ke cartillage ke neeche pittashay rahata he; yaa hriday kaa sheersh 5veen antarparshuka se sata, sharir kii madhya rekha se 9 semi. bain ore hota he; athwa bhagasthi, tuberkal se 1 semi. upar hoti he tathaa 1 semi. parshrv main bahya udari mudrika chhidr rahata he. sharir main sthit jahaan bindu twachaa par pahachaane ja sakate hai, vahaan se twachaa ke ant: sthit angon ko twachaa par khinchakar, us sthaan par kaatane par vahi ang hamen milanaa chahie.
 isi prakaar is shaastra ko adhyayan karne kii ek or vidhi he jisamen eks-re se sahaayta lete hai. ise radioligical anatomi kehete hai. asthiyon ke atirikt ab dhamniyon, vrikk, mutrashay aadi anek angon kii rachana tathaa sthiti kaa adhyayan isse karte hai. isse angon kii vaastavik rachana tathaa vikrut rachana donon kaa gyaan praapt hota he.
