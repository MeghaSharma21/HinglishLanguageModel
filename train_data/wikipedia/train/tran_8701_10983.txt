 nadira jahir babbar hindi film va naatakon main abhinay ke liye jaani jaati hai. unki apni ek natya sanstha he jiskaa naam ekajute he. ve hindi filmon ke abhinayta va rajaneta raaj babbar kii patni hai. raaj se unka ek putr arya va putree juhi hai.
 nadira ko 2001 main sangeet naatak academy puraskaar se nawaaza ja chukaa he. haal hi main unhone gurinder chadda kii film brid and prijudis main eshwarya raay kii maan kii bhoomika adaa kii thi.
