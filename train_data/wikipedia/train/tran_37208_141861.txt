 
 kasaava prithvi ke ushnkaribandheey or up-ushnkaribandheey kshetron main ugaaya jane vaala ek kshup he jisaki moti jad aalu kii tarah mand se yukt hoti he. chaaval or makke ke baad, maanav-aahaar main yah yah vishwa main teesraa sabase bada carbohydrate kaa strot he. kasaava mul-rup se dakshin america kaa vanaspati tha lekin ab vishwa-bhar ke garam kshetron main miltaa he. jab isko piskar powder yaa moti-aakaar ke kanon main banaaya jaay to yah tepyoka bhi kehalaata he. kasaava sookhe kii paristhiti main or kam-upjaau dharti par bhi ug sakane vaala paudha he.
 kasaava mithi or kadavi nasalon main miltaa he. anya kand-jadon kii tarah isamen bhi kuch vashaile padaarth upasthit hote he, jinaki maatra kadavi nasalon main mithi nasalon se kai adhik hoti he. khaane se pehle ise sahi prakaar se taiyaar karnaa aavashyak he varanaa isse synide vish-prabhaav ho saktaa he, jisase ghengha rog, gatibhang or lakva hone kii sambhaavna he. kuch kshetron main kisaan kadavi nasalon ko ugaana pasand karte hai kyonki nashijiv or pashu unhen khaanaa pasand nahin karte or unke fasalon kii chori bhi kam hoti he.
