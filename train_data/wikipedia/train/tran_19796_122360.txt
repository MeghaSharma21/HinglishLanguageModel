 brahmagupta kaa antarveshan sutara bhartiya ganitagya brahmagupta dwaara prastut ek antarveshan sutara he jo do ghaat vaale bahupad kaa upyog karata he. is antarveshan kaa varnan unke prasiddh granth khandakhadyak ke parishisht main aayaa he. yahi shloke brahmagupta dwaara isse pehle rachit 'dhyaan-grah-adhikaar' main bhi aata he kintu uski rachanakaal anishchit he.
