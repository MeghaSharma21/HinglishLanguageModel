 sesotho (sesotho, dakshini sotho, yaa dakshini sesotho mukhyatah dakshini africa main boli jane vaale vaali ek bantu bhashaa he, jo vahaan ke 11 aadhikarik bhashaaon main se ek he. iske alaava yah lisutu main bhi boli jaati he, jahaan kii yah rashtriya bhashaa he. yah ek mishrit bhashaa he, jisamen puure shabd ko banane ke liye kai upsargon or vichlan or lacheela niyamon kaa upyog kiya jaataa he.
