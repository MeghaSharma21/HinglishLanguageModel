 (bangla: কাজল দেবগন Kajol Debgon, kaajol hindi filmon kii ek prasiddh abhinetri hai jinka janm 5 agast 1974 ko hua tha. unki maan tanuja abhinetri thi or nani shobhana samarth bhi abhinetri thim. unki choti bahin tanisha bhi ab filmon main kaam kar rahi hai. unke pita kaa naam shomu mukherjee he. ve filmein banaate the. kaajol ne apana filmi safar film bekhudi se shuru kiya jisamen unke paatr kaa naam radhika tha. vah film to nahin chali par unki baad kii filmein bahut prasiddh huin. jaise ki baazigar or dilavaale dulhaniya le jaayenge. uhone apane sahakarmi or premi, ajay devgan se 24 farvari 1999 ko vivaah kiya tha. unki ek choti beti he jiskaa naam nysa he. unke naam kul 6 filmphere or unamem se 5 sarvshereshth abhinetri ke puraskaar hai jo kii ek kirtiman he. vah yah kirtiman apni mausi nutan ke saath saajha karti hai.
