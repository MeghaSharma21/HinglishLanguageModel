 goa sarkaar, ekasadaneey vyavastha vaali vidhayika he jiske antargat ek chalis sadasyon vaali vidhaan sabha aati he, jiskaa mukhiya mukhyamantri hota he. rajya ke rajyapal kii niyukti bharat ke raashtrapati dwaara kii jaati he. rajyapal kii bhoomika adhiktar samarohik hi hoti he, lekin yah tay karne main ki agali sarkaar koun banaayegaa yaa phir vidhayika ko nilambit karne kii sthiti main wo ek mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaataa he.
 apni azaadi se lekar 1990 tak goa par sthaayee sarkaron kaa shashan rahaa he par uske baad gathbandhan ke daur ke kaaran rajya main rajnatik asthirataa kaa maahaul he. goa ne 15 saal ke antaraal, yani 1990 se 2005 ke dauraan 14 sarkarein dekhin he. 2005 main rajyapal ne vidhaan sabha ko bhang karke rajya main raashtrapati shashan laga diya tha.
