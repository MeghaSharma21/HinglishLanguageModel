 kolvaale bharat ke goa rajya ke uttar goa jile main sthit ek janaganana nagar he.
 bharat kii 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar, kolvaale kii jansankhya 5,475 he. kul jansankhya main purush 55%, or mahilaayein 45% hai. yahan kii ausat sakshartaa dar 72% he jo rashtriya sakshartaa dar 59.5% se adhik he jisamen se purush sakshartaa dar 74% or mahila sakshartaa dar 69% he. 10% log 0-6 varsh ayuvarg ke hai.
