 banphool yaa vanphool aise phool ko kaha jaataa he jo bina kisi maanvi dekharekh ke swayan hi ugkar khil gaya ho. mausam, barish or dharti ke anusaar alag-alag sthanon par alag-alag jatiyon ke banphool milte hai. yah zaruri nahin he ki van main hi khilne vaale phool ko banphool kaha jaae - shahari kshetron main bhi ug rahe junglee phulon ke liye bhi yah naam istemaal hota he.
