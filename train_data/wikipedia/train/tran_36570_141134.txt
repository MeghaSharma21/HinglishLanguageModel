  virendra dayaal bharat sarkaar ne 1992 main prasha ke kshetra main padam bhushan se sammanit kiya tha. ye dilli se hai. virendra dayaal ek bhartiya civil sevak, rajanyik or ek purv bawarchi de cabinet sachiv general rah chuke hai. unhone nideshak ke karyaalay ke vishesh raajnneetik maamalon ke sanyukt raashtra ke rup main or jo bharat rajaneta ek purv mantri ke videsh, 2005 ke paul volkar samiti report main natwar singh, sahit kii ek sankhya ke khilaaf lagaaya aarope kii jaanch kii vishesh doot ke rup main seva kii he. vah ek purv bhartiya prashasnic seva adhikari or vivi 1956 ke dayaal rashtriya maanav adhikaar ayog bharat main sadsy ke rup main 1998 se 2006 ke liye do saalo ke liye baith gaya. yah apane kaarykaal ke dauraan vah dakshin asia main or kaaryakari sahaayak ke ucchayukta kii kshamta main vietnam ke naav log sankat main sharnaarthiyon main shaamil kiya gaya tha.
