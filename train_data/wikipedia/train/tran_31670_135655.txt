 hindu dharm main vishnu puraana ke anusaar prithvi kaa varnan is prakaar he.yah varnan shriparashar ji ne shri maitrey rishi se kyn tha. unke anusaar iskaa varnan sau varshon main bhi nahin ho saktaa he. yah keval ati sankshep varnan he.
 yah prithvi saat dwipon main banty hui he. ve dweep es prakaar se hai:-
 ye saaton dweep chaaron ore se kramshah khaare pani, ichuras, madiraa, ghrit, dadhi, dugdh or mithe jal ke saat samudron se ghire hai.ye sabhi dweep ek ke baad ek duusare ko ghere hue bane hai or inhen ghere hue saaton samudr hai. jambudweep in sab ke madhya main sthit he.
 is dweep ke swaami veeravar jyotishmaan the. inke saat putron : udbhid, venuman, vairath, lamban, dhruthi, prabhakar, kapil ke naam sangyanusaar hi iske saat bhaagon ke naam hai. madiraa sagar apane se duune vistaar vaale kush dweep se chaaron ore se ghira hua he. yahaan bhi saat parvat, saat mukhya nadiyaan or saat hi varsh hai. 
 vidrum, hemshaul, dhyutimaan, pushpavan, kusheshay, hari or mandarachal naamak saat parvat hai.
 dhutapapa, shiva, pavitra, sammati, vidyut, ambha or mahi naamak saat nadiyaan hai.
 udbhid, venuman, vairath, lamban, dhruthi, prabhakar, kapil naamak saat varsh hai.yahaan dami, shushmi, sneh or mandeh naamak chaar varna hai. yahaan kush kaa ati vishaal jhaad he. yah dweep apane hi baraabar ke ghi se bhare samudr se chaaron ore se ghira hua he.
