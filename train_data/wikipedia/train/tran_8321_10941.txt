 disha pataani ek bhartiya film abhinetri tathaa model he jo bollywood or telegu filmon main abhinay karti he. pataani kaa janm bareli ,bharat main hua tha. wese mul rup se disha uttarakhand ke tanakpur ke ambagh kii rahani vaali hai disha kii adaaen anya kalakaaron se alag hai. 
 disha kii bahin khushbu paatani sena main capton ke pad par tainaad hai inke pita jagadish paatani bareli main rahete hai yahan par hi disha kii padhai puri hui. pataani ne abhinay kii shuruyaat telegu film lophar se kii thi.jabaki inhone saal 2018 main tiger shroff ke saath baagi 2 main kaary kiya he.
