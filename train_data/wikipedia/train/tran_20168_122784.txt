 ienes camorta bhartiya nausena kaa stealth pandubbirodhi corgett yudhpot he jise 23 agast 2014 ko nausena main shaamil kiya gaya.
 ienes camorta bhartiya nausena ke nausena dizine nideshaalay dwaara pariyojna 28 ke antargat banaae ja rahe 4 stealth pandubbirodhi corgetts main se pehla he. isamen prayog kiye gaye lagbhag 90% sazosaman swadeshi he. is pot kaa nirmaan garden rich shiobilders and ejiniers limited ne kiya he.
 110 me0 lambaai tathaa 14 me0 kii chaudaai vaale is pot main do deazal ingine lage hai. isaki visthaapan kshamta 3500 ton he tathaa yah 25 note tak kii gati se chal saktaa he. isamen 15 adhikarigan evam 180 navikon ke rahane kii vyavastha he. 
 is pot main pandubbirodhi rocket tathaa torpeado tainaat kiye gaye hai. madhyam va ati-nikat aayudh pranali se bhi yah pot las he. swadesh main viksit revati radar is pot par laga he. yah pot pandubbirodhi yudhak se yukt helicopter ko le jane main bhi sakasham he.
