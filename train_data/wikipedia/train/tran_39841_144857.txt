 mathura kaa peda ek bhartiya mithai he jiskaa udbhav mathura shahar se hua he. maava yaa khoa se bani mithaiyaan uttar bharat me bahut lokapriy hai tathaa peda maave se banane vaali mithai he. bharat me mathura ke pede is kadar prasiddh hai ki yah shabd bhashaa me alankaar ke rup me prayukt hota he jaise ki "mathura kaa peda ou chhattisgarh kaa kheda". pede ke liye mathura ek trademark he. mathura bhraman ke dauraan mathura ke pede, mewa vati pede va niryaat quality ke pede aagantukon kaa dhyaan aakarshith karte hai.
 krishna janmabhumi mathura main peda lokapriy prasad he.. pede ko taaje maave, doodh, chini va ghi main suvaas hetu kalimirch churna milakar banaaya jaataa he. bharat main janmashtami kii chhuttiyaan pede ke swaad ke bina adhuri maani jaati hai. har saal janmashtami par pede banate hai jinase bhagwaan krishna ko bhoga lagaaya jaataa he tathaa vrath khandan pede se hi kiya jaataa he..
 mathura ke alaava anya bhartiya paryaton shaharon main bhi mathura ke pede, agra kaa petha va maner ke laddu paryatakon main khaase lokapriy vyanjan hai.
 bhartiya lok gayan main bhi mathura ke pedo kaa swaad dekhne ko miltaa he. "mathura ke pede mohe lave, khilaave ji......." sand pujan geet ke rup main ati lokapriy he.
