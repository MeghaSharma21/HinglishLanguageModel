 majalanic badal, hamaari mandakini se lagi hui aniyamit aakaar kii do padosi bauni akashgangaayen he. bada majalanic badal hamase 1,60,000 prakaash varsh dur he or chota majalanic badal hamase 1,80,000 prakaash varsh dur he. donon ko hi dakshini ardhgolardh me nangi ankhon se dekha ja saktaa he. inaki upasthiti ko pehali baar 1519 me portugali anveshak ferdinand majalan dwaara darj kiya gaya tha. baad me wein unheen par namit ho gayi.
 majalanic badal do besangi bauni gallexiyaan hai jo hamaari gallexi, akashganga, kii upgrah hai or hamaari sameepi gallexiyon ke sthaneey samooh ke sadsy hai. inhen prithvi ke akaash main sirf dakshini golardh se dekha ja saktaa he. in do gallexiyon ke naam hai -
 agar bina doorabeen ke prithvi ke dakshini golardh se majalanic badalon ko dekha jaae to yah akashganga ke bichade hue ansh lagate hai. bade or chote majalanic badalon ke bich lagbhag 75,000 prakaash-varsh kii duuri he. prithvi se bada majalanic badal 160,000 prakaash-varsh dur he jabaki chota majalanic badal 200,000 prakaash-varsh dur he. bade majalanic badal kaa aakaar lagbhag 14,000 prakaash-varsh he or chote vaale kaa keval 7,000 prakaash-varsh he. yah donon akashganga se kaafii chote hai .
 majalanic badalon kaa gaharaa adhyayan karne ke baad vaigyanik is nattejey par pahunche hai ke yah donon kabhi dundee sarpil gallexiyaan hua karti thim jinhen akashganga ke shaktishaalee gurutvkarshan se paidaa hue jowarbhata bal ne tod-marod ke besangi gallexiyaan bana daalaa he. hamaare kshirmarg par bhi in donon gallexiyon kaa asar padaa he or uskaa bahari sarpil aakaar thodaa sa tedha-medhaa ho gaya he. dekha gaya he ke in teenon gallexiyon ke bich hydrogen gas kii dharaayen bah rahi hai.
