 sanoullah amritasari ثناء اللہ امرتسری, Sanaullah Amritsari
 moulana sanaa allaah amritsari prasiddh vidwaan the jo amritsar main janme. ahale haddees the or apane panth ke prachaar ke liye sadaiv patibaddh rahe. kashmir se khandaani taluk tha, samachar patr ahale haddees shuru kiya tha, dharmik bahes karne main maahir the, unhen fateh kaadiyaan bhi kaha jaataa he, isai, hindu, arya samaaj, shiya or qadiyaniyon par unki pustaken kaaphi maqbool huin. satyarth prakaash ke jawaab main haq prakaash bajawab satyarth prakashor rangila rasul ke jawaab main 'muqaddas rasul bajawab rangila rasul' pustak prasiddh hai. arabi or urdu main koran kii tafseer likhi, phaliz ke rog main mrityu huyi.
