 todermal mogul badshaah akbar ke navratnon main se the. todermal kaa janm laharpur main hua tha parantu yah vivadaspad he ki yah kahan sthit tha.
 akbar ke samay se prarambh hui bhoomi paimaaish kaa aayojan todermal ke dwaara hi kiya gaya tha.
 bihar ke patna city ke dewan mohalle main, naujarghat sithat chitragupta mandir kaa purnirmaan, raja todermal tathaa unke naayab rahe kuvar kishor bahadur ne karwaakar, kasuti pathar kii chitragupta kii murti, hijri san 980 tadanusaar risvin san 1574 main sthapit karaari thi.
 janapad hardoi main uttar pradesh rajya ke rajasav adhikariyon ke liye banaaye gayi ekmaatr rajasav prashikshan santhaan kaa naam inke naam par raja todermal bhulekh prashikshan santhaan rakhaa gaya he jahaan iees, ipies, pisies, peepies ke alaava rajasav karmiyon ko bhulekh sambandhi prashikshan diya jaataa he.
