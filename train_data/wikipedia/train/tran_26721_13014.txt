 da. jitendra kumaar  bhartiya nefrologist evam pratyaropan chikitsa visheshgy evam shodhakarta hai. vah faridabad ke kyuarji health city haspital ke nefrology evam transplant medicin ke nideshak hai. unhone bharat main kidney rogon kii chikitsa ko badhaava dene main mahatvapurn yogadan diya he. kidney rogon kii rokthaam ke bare main jaagrukta kaayam karne ke liye sakriy hai. kidney rogon kii chikitsa ke kshetra main unke yogadan ke liye unhen anek rashtriya puraskaar praapt hue hai. 
 unka janm bihar ke nalanda jile ke harnaut thane ke antagart murhari gaanv main 15 march, 1969 ko hua. pitaaji niranjan prasad bihar sarkaar ke lokanirmaan vibhaag main adhikari ke rup main karyrat the. 
 da. jitendra kumaar ne teesri kaksha tak gaanv ke skool main padhai kii jabaki chauthi or paanchavi kaksha tak bihar ke bihar sharif ke standard english skool main padhai kii. uske baad 1979 main rashtriya pratibha pratiyogita ke jariye unka chayan mashhoor sainik skool, tilaiya main hua jahaan se unhone 12 veen tak padhai kii.
 unhone patna medical kalege se 1992 main embibies jabaki 1997 main medicin main medicin main kiya. 1997 main rashtriya pariksha board se dienbi kii oopaadhi hasil kii. varsh 2002 main lakhanu ke sanjay gandhi post graduate insticute aaf medical sciences se dm kii oopaadhi hasil kii.
 vah patna sthit indira gandhi ayurvigyaan santhaan main farivary 1999 se disambar 2002 tak senior racident rahe jabaki lakhanu ke sanjay gandhi post graduate insticute aaf medical sciences main farvari 1999 se disambar 2002 tak senior racident rahe. iske baad unhone aadhra pradesh ke vijaybada sthit nagarjuna haspital main nefrologist ke rup main farvari 2003 se navambar 2003 tak kaam kiya. disambar 2003 se farvari 2010 tak faridabad ke fortis ascarts haspital main nefology vibhaag ke pramukh rakhe. iske baad faridabad sthit asian insticute aaf medical sciences main farvari 2010 se march 2013 nefrology ke pramukh rahe tathaa april 2013 se aktubar 2017 tak nefrology evam transplant medicin ke nideshak rahe. navambar 2017 se vah faridabar ke kyuarji health city main nefrology evam transplant medicin ke nideshak hai.
