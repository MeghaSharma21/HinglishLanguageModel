 nishkarshan dhaatukarmiki yaa nishkarshan dhaatukarm ke antargat kisi ayask se dhaatu praapt karnaa, usko shuddh karnaa evam uski punarchakran karne kii prakriyaaon evam usase sambandhit vishyon kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. 
 prithvi ke garbh main paayi jane vaali adhikansh dhaatuen oxide yaa sulphide yogicon ke rup main hoti hai. isaliye inase dhaatu praapt karne ke liye inkaa apchayan karnaa pehali jarurat he. in yogicon kaa radox do vidhiyon se kiya ja saktaa he - rasinic vidhi yaa vidyut apghaton kii vidhi se. kuch dhaatuon ke nishkarshan ke liye dono hi vidhiyon kaa prayog karnaa padataa he. kintu nishkarshan ke pehle dhaatu ke ayask main se dhaatu ke yogic ko upyukt vidhi dwaara alag karnaa padataa he. iske liye ayask kaa rasinic prasanskaran, yaa bhautik prasanskaran, yaa dono prasanskaran karne padate hai.
