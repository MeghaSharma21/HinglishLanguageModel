 yah niyamadhaarit masini anuwaad karata he. yah aisi bhashaaon ke liye vishesh rup se upyogi he jinamein nikat kaa sambandh ho . nikat ke rishte vaali bhashaaon main paraspar anuwaad ke liye apartaan sachmuch apratin he. jiskaa armbh GNU ke antargat kiya gaya tha. 
 spain kii sarkaar dwaara vitt poshit apartaan ek mashin anuwaad ingine ke rup main kaary karata he. yah mul rup se nikat se sambandhit bhashaaon ke bich anuwaad kar banaaya gaya tha. ise ab ore vistrut kiya gaya he taaki ye or adhik alag bhashaaon ke saath kaam kar sake. nayi mashin pranali banane ke liye koi bhi vyakti bhashaai data kaa nirmaan karke acchhee tarah se nirdisht .XML swaroopon main istemaal kar saktaa he.apartaan ke liye nirmit bhashaai data jinke sahyog se banaaya gaya he usame kai badi companiyaan bhi shaamil he jaise prompasit language ejineering, imexin software tathaa elekaa enginiaritza linguisticoya aadi. yah pariyojna Google Summer of Code 2009 tathaa Google code in 2010 main hissaa le chuki he.
 ye un bhashaa jodo kii soochi he jo abhi uplabdh he.
