 ambika prasad bajpeyi kaa janm disambar maas main 1880 main kanpur main hua. shiksha kanpur main hui. inhone sanskrit, urdu, angreji tathaa farsi bhashaaon kaa adhyayan kiya. san 1900 i. main aapane intress pariksha paas kii. kalakatta se prakashit 'hindi bangavasi' tathaa 'bharatamitra' ke aap sampadak rahe. 1920 se 1930 tak swatantra kaa sampadan kiya. san 1904 se 1919 i. tak vyakaran kaa adhyayan kar aapane 'hindi kaumudi' naamak pustak likhi. 'hindi par farsi kaa prabhaav' aapkaa prasiddh niabandh he. hindi seva, sampadan kala tathaa vidwata se prabhavit hokar 'hindi sahitya sammelan' kaashi ne aapako apana sabhapati banaaya. aap uttar pradesh vidhaan parishad ke manonith sadsy bhi rahe.
