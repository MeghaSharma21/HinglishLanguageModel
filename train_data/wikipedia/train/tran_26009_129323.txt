 ithiopian lipi athwa geeiz, ek lipi he jo ithiopia tathaa iritriya kii kai bhashaaon ko likhane main prayukt hoti he. isaki vishesh baat yah he ki is par brahmi lipi kaa spasht prabhaav dikhataa he. ek hi vyanjan ko alag-alag swaron se milaane se vyanjan kaa swaroop thodaa sa badal jaataa he, jo brahmi parivaar kii lipiyon kii visheshta he.
