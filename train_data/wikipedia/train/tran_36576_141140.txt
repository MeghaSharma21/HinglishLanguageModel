 vidhyalay vah sthal he jahaan shiksha pradaan kii jaati he. ""vidhyalay ek aisi sanstha he jahaan bacchon ke shaareerik,maansik,boudhik evam naitik gunon kaa vikaas hota he.' vidhyalay' shabd ke liye angl bhashaa main 'skool' shabd kaa prayog hota he, jisaki utpatti greek shabd 'Skohla'yaa 'Skhole' se hui he, jis se taatpary he- 'avkaash'. yah arth kuch vicitra - sa lagata he parantu vaastaviktaa ko to yah he ki pracheen yunaan main avkaash ke sthalon ko hi vidhyalay ke naam se sambodhit kiya jaataa tha. avkaash kaal ko hi 'aatm -vikaas' samajha jaataa tha, jiskaa abhyaas avkaash naam nishchit sthaan par kiya jaataa tha dhire-dhire yahi avkaash sthal ek nishchit uddeshy tathaa paathyakram kaa gyaan pradaan karne vaali sansthaaen arthath skool ban gaye.
