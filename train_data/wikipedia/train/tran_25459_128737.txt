 angaami, bharat ke nagaland kii solah boliyon main se ek boli tathaa rajya kii pramukh bhashaa he. yah tibeti-burmi bhashaa parivaar kii angaami-pochuri shakha kii angaami upashakha kii ek bhashaa he. rajya ke nivaasiyon ke bich yah sampark bhashaa ke rup main viksit ho chuki hai. desh kii 1652 bhashaaon evam boliyon main se ek he. iske bolne waalon kii sankhya ek lakh se zaraa adhik he. yah chini-tibeti parivaar kii asmi-burmi-shakha kii ek tonim pradhan bhashaa he, jinamein taan ke chadhaav-utaar se kisi-kisi shabd main aath arthon tak kaa bodh hota he. ise roman lipi main likhaa jane laga he. nagari lipi main bhi bhashaa or sahitya ko likhit rup dene kaa prayaas ho rahaa he.
