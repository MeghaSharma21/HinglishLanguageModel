 vinifred selina sturt 1910 se 1916 tak bharat ke vysarai rahe, lord harding kii patni thi.
 vinifred allington ke bairan henry jerard sturt kii pehali patni, ladi augusta bingem se duusari santaan thi. 17 april 1890 ko sturt ne apane parivaar ke sadasyon kii aapattiyon ke baavjood charles harding se vivaah kar liya. is yugal kii ek beti, diamond harding or do bete edward or alexander hue.
