 gadhwali bharat ke uttrakhand rajya main boli jane vaali ek pramukh bhashaa he. 
 gadhwali bhashaa ke antargat kai boliyaan prachlit hai yah gadhwal ke bhinn bhinn kshetron main bhinn bhinn pai jaati he. 
 gadhwali main sahitya praaia: nahin ke baraabar he, kintu lok- sahitya prachur maatra main he. iske liye devanagari lipi kaa prayog hota he.
 gadhwali kaa vyakaran hindi se bhinn he.
 gadhwali bhashaa main do hi ling hain pulling or streeling.
 pulling praayah ukarant hota he.jaise daalu, chhavaaru, dhaibaru aadi.janaini, janvai 
 streeling praayah acarant ikarant ukarant hota he. jaise- bitalhar, lhodi, bo aadi 
 gadhwali main do hi vachan hain ekvachan or bahuvachan. vachan ling anusaar badalte hain.
 streeling ekvachan praayah acarant ikarant ukarant or bahuvachan nasiky yaa ikarant hota he
 jaise- kazan -kajanibwari -bvaarin
 
 pulling ekvachan praayah ukarant hota he or bahuvachan praayah karant hota he. jacedalu -daal
 
 gadhwali main sarvnaam praayah hindi ke samatuly bhi hain or hindi ke viparit bhi.
