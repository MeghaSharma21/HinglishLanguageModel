 8- madhya rail
 madhya rail, bhartiya rail ke 16 sabase bade anchalon main se ek anchal he or iskaa mukhyalay mumbai main chhatrapati shivaji terminus main sthit he. iske antargat bharat kii pehali yatri rail line bhi aati he, jise 16 april 1853 ko mumbai se thane ke bich chalaaya gaya tha. ise laghurup main mare kaha jaataa he.
 madhya rail ke antargat maharashtra kaa adhikansh, karnataka kaa uttar-purvi kshetra or madhya pradesh kaa dakshini hissaa aata he. is rail anchal kaa gathan 5 navambar 1951 ko kai sarkari swaamitv vaali relon ko milakar kiya gaya tha, jisamen great indian peninsula railway, purv riyasat gwalior kii sindhiya state railway, nizaam state railway or dhaulpur rail shaamil thim.
 pehle uttari madhya pradesh, chhattisgarh or dakshini uttar pradesh kaa ilaaka bhi madhya rail anchal ke antargat aata tha jiske chalate yah kshetra, railpath kii lambaai or karmchaariyon kii sankhya ke hisaab se bharat kaa sabase bada rail anchal ban gaya tha. april 2003 main in kshetron ko milakar ek naye rail anchal pashchim madhya rail kaa gathan kiya gaya.
 iske antargat aane vaale mandal hai:mumbai, bhusaval, pune, sholapur or nagpur
