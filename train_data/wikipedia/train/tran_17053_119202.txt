 tamanna madhya asia, mongolia or uske aasapaas ke ilaaqon main basane vaale pracheen kanaabadosh qabeelon kii samskruti main vishesh moharon or chihnon ko kaha jaataa tha. yahi shabd in logon se sampark rakhane vaali bahut si anya samskritiyon main pravesh kar gaya, jinamein bhartiya upmahadweep, iran, turki or kuch anya desh shaamil hai. hindi main 'tamanna' shabd kaa matlab pramaan ke rup main die jane vaale kisi bhi vishesh chihn ke liye prayog hota he, masalan khel main jeetne par pramaan ke rup main milane vaale padak ko aksar 'tamanna' kaha jaataa he. parmparik rup se sarkari shulk adaa karne par yaa anya sarkari dastavezon par lagane vaali mohar ko bhi uttar bharat main 'tamanna' kaha jaataa tha.
 'tamanna' main bindu-vaale 'ga' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'ga' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'galati' or 'garib' shabdon ke 'ga' se miltaa he.
