 sherpur bhoomihaar brahmanon kaa ek bada gaanv he. sherpur, gajipur se 35 kimi par sthit he. nikatatam railway stesan yusufpur yahan se 8 kimi kii duuri par he. sherpur, gajipur jile main mohammadabad, baliya ke bich rashtriya rajmarg 19 se kundesar ke paas sthit he. varanasi hawaii adde se yah 110 kimi dur he. avadhesh raay shastri purv vidhayak is gaanv ke hai. sherpur swatantraaa senaniyon kaa gaanv he sherpur se aath log 1942 main 18 agast ko mohammadbaad tehsil main british raaj ke virodh main shahid ho gaye. 
 yah gaanv ganga nadi ke kinaraper sthit he. sherpur ek badi gram panchayat he. sherpur kalaan, sherpur khurd, semra, shiv raay kaa pura or bachchhalpur is panchayat kaa ek hissaa hai. 
 swatantraaa senaanee-amar shahid dow shiv pujan raay, vansh narayan raay, vansh narayan raay dwitiya, vashishtha narayan raay, rishikesh raay, raja raay, narayan raay, ram badan upaadhyaay sherpur ke ashtashahid hai. ab ham aapako in aatho veeron ke shahid hone kii kahani bataate hai. uttar pradesh kaa jila gajipur jise lahuri kaashi ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he uski muhammadabad tehsil par 18 agast 1942 ko tirangaa lahraane ke prayaas main aath log shahid hue. is andolan ke netaa dow shivapujan raay the darasal muhammadabad ke andolan ke baad gajipur or baliya jile aaazaad ho gaye the yaheen par unhone 18 agast ko fahraaya tha. 1942 main unhone uttar pradesh ke gajipur jile main british sarkaar se khud ko swatantra hone kii ghoshnaa kii thi. muhammadabad ke tehseeldaar ne logon kii bheed par goli chalaa di, jiske kaaran sherpur gaanv ke aath log maare gaye. maare gaye adhikansh log congressi the.
 angrejo ne 10 agast ko yahaan 129 netaaon ke khilaaf giraftaar warrant jari kar diya tha. 19 agast tak sthaneey logon ne lagbhag puure gajipur par kabja kar liya or teen dinon tak yahaan sarkaar chalaate rahe.
