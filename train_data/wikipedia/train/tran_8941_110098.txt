 sangharsh yaa dwandv se taatpary do yaa adhik samoohon ke bich matbhed, pratirodh, virodh aadi se he. ek hi samooh ke andar bhi dwandv ho saktaa he. is sthiti main antajsamooh dwandv kehete hai.
 sangharsh apane swapnon ko praapt karne kaa bhi ho saktaa he. yah sangharsh paristhitiyon se hota he. jisame vyakti/jeev swayan ko tapaata he.
