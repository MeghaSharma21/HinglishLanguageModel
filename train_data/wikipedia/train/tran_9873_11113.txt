 dangri purv raajapootaana ke bade thikaanon main se ek he.jaisalmer ke purv rajgaddi vaaris hone ke naate purv jaisalmer maharaval jawahar singh ne inhen jaisalmer se baahar kar diya tha. biharidasot bhaati ke sabase bada thikaana hai dangri aaazaad bharat se pehle jaisalmer kii raajnneeti main dangri thakur sahab kaa bada haath hua karata tha. jaisalmer ki sena ki kaman hameshaa dangri thakur ke haathon hua karti thim. jaisalmer ke sansthaapak bhi inhen ke vanshaj the. jaisalmer ke maharaval ki akaal mrityu ore sadyantra ke kaaran inhen jaisalmer tyaag naa pada. aaj dangri thakur ke vanshaj dangri main rahete.
