 anagarik dharmpal prasiddh buddha bhikshu the. inkaa janm srilanka main 17 sitambar 1864 ko hua. pita kaa naam don carolins hevavitaran tathaa mata kaa mallika tha. inkaa naam don david rakhaa gaya. shikshakaal se hi inhen isai skulon main padhane europea rahan-sahan or videshi shashan se ghrinaa ho gai thi. shikshasmapti par prasiddh buddha vidvan bhadant hivakduve srisumangala naamak mahasthvir se pali bhashaa kii shiksha or buddha dharm kii diksha lee tathaa apana naam badalakar anaagarik dharmpal rakhaa or saarvajanik prachaar kaary ke liye ek motor bas ko ghar banaaya or uskaa naam "shobhan maaligaanv" rakhkar gaanv-gaanv ghoomte videshi vastuon ke bahishkaar tathaa buddha dharm kaa sandesh dene lage. pratham mahayuddh ke samay ye paanch varshon ke liye kalakatta main najarband kar die gaye. mahabodhi sabha inke hi praytn se sthapit hui. meri faster naamak ek videshi mahila ne inase prabhavit hokar mahabodhi sosayadi ke liye lagbhag paanch lakh rupye die the.
 dharmpal ke prayatnon ke parinamswaroop unke nidhanoparant raashtrapati dow rajendraprasad ke haathon buddha gaya baishakh poornima, san. 2012 arthath 6 may san 1955 ko baudhon ko de di gai.
 13 july 1931 ko unhone pravrajya lee or unka naam devamit dharmpal hua. 1933 kii 16 janvari ko pravrajya purna hui or unhone upasanpada grahan kii, naam padaa bhikshu shri devamit dharmpal. 29 april 1933 ko 69 varsh kii aayu main igalila sanvaran kii.
 unki asthiyaan pathar ke ek chote se stupa main mulgandh kuti vihaar ke parshv main rakh di gai.
