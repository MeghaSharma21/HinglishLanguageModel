 vioming sanyukt rajya america kaa ek rajya he. rajya kshetra main dasvaan sabase bada rajya he lekin isaki aabaadi sabhi rajyon main sabase kam he. rajya ke uttar main montana, purv main south dakota or nebraska, dakshin main colorado, dakshin-pashchim main uta, or pashchim main idaho he. 2015 rajya kii jansankhya kaa anumaan 5,86,107 tha, jo ki america ke 31 sabase bade shaharon se bhi kam he. rajya ke pashchimi do-tihaai hisse ko zyaadaatar pahari parvatamala or rocky parvat shrunkhlaa ne dhak rakhaa he, jabaki rajya kaa purvi tihaai ilaaka ucch oonchai vaali preri he. vioming main lagbhag aadhi bhoomi you.es. sarkaar ke swaamitv main he. 1950 ke baad se vioming ek raajnneetik rup se roodhiwaadee rajya rahaa he jisamen republican party ke ummidavaar ne 1964 ko chhodkar sabhi chunaav jite hai.
