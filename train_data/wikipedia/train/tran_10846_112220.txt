  raksha mantralay kaa pramukh kaary he raksha or suraksha sambandhi maamalon par niti nirdesh bananaa or unke karyanvayan ke liye unhen suraksha balon ke mukhyalayon, antar sena sanghathanon, raksha utpaada pratishthanon or anusandhan va vikaas sanghathanon tak pahunchaana. sarkaar ke niti nirdeshon ko prabhavi dhang se tathaa aavantit sansadhano ko dhyaan main rakhkar unhen karyanvit karnaa bhi uskaa kaam he. raksha mantralay chaar vibhaagon kaa mila jula rup he. isamen raksha vibhaag, raksha utpaada vibhaag, raksha anusandhan evam vikaas vibhaag or purv sainikon ke kalyaan or vitt prabhag ke vibhaag shaamil hai.
 vibhaag ke pramukh kaary is prakaar he -
 kargil samiti kii report ke aadhaar par mantriyon ke samooh dwaara liye gaye nirnay ke anusaar 1 oktober 2001 ko integrated dephence staff kii sthapana kii gai. idies ke liye staff teen senaaon, emai, diardio, sashkt sena mukhyalay, civil sevaaon tathaa raksha vibhaag se muhaiyaa karaayaa gaya he. vartmaan main integrated dephence staff sioessi ke salaahkaar ke rup main karyrat he. vartmaan main aiesadi adhyaksh sioisi ke salaahkaar staff ke rup main kaary kar rahaa he.
 raksha mantri ko raksha sambandhi gatividhiyon main madad dene ke liye anek samitiyaan hoti hai. staff samiti kaa pramukh ek aisa manch hota he jo teenon senaaon kii gatividhiyon se sambhddha maamalon par vichaar kaa sthann hota he or yah mantralay ko salaah detaa he. staff kaa pad sabase lambii seva vaale sena pramukh ko diya jaataa he or yah teenon pramukhon ke bich baari-baari se aata he.
 raksha mantralay kaa vitt vibhaag vittiya maamalon se jude sabhi muddon par vichaar karata he. raksha sevaaon kaa raksha salaahkaar iskaa pramukh hota he tathaa yah raksha mantralay se purnatah sambhddha hota he or salaahkaar kii bhoomika nibhaataa he.
