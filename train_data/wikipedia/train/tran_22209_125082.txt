 tagar ek prakaar kaa ped jo afghanistan, kashmir, bhutan or konkan main nadiyon ke kinare paaya jaataa he. bharat ke baahar yah madagascar or zanjibar main bhi hota he. isaki lakdi bahut sugandhit hoti he or usamen se bahut adhik maatra main ek prakaar kaa tel nikalataa he. yah lakdi agar kii lakdi ke sthaan par tathaa aushadh ke kaam main aati he. lakdi kaale rang kii or sugandhit hoti he or uskaa buraada jalaane ke kaam main aata he. bhavaprakash ke anusaar tagar do prakaar kaa hota he, ek main safed rang ke or duusare main nile rang ke phool lagate hai. isaki pattiyon ke ras se aankh ke anek rog dur hote hai. vaidyak main ise ushn, veeryavardhak, sheethal, madhur, snigdh, laghu or vish, apsmaar, shul, drushtidosh, vishadosh, bhutonmad or tridosh aadi kaa naashak mana he.
