 gramin bank of aryaavart lakhanu ke aasapaas ke gramin kshetron main kaary karne vaala ek bank he. isaki sthapana bharat sarkaar dwaara 1 april 2013 ko hui jab 'aryaavart kshetreey gramin bank' tathaa 'shreyas gramin bank' kaa vilay karne is bank kaa nirmaan kiya gaya. samprati is bank kii 704 shakhaayen tathaa 11 kshetreey karyaalay hai jo lakhanuu ke aasapaas hai. yah bank bank of india dwaara pryojit he tathaa kshetreey gramin bank adhinium 1976 ke adheen kaary karata he.
