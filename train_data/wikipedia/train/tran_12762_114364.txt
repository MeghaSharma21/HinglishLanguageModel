 sopvid tabloid british biplen khel vimaan the jinka kii baad main senya upyog kiya gaya. sopvid uddan company dwaara banaae gaye yah kuch pehle vimaanon main se ek tha. isane november 1913 main pehali udaan bhari va san 1915 main ise seva se bahaar kar diya gaya. aise kul 42 tabloid av 136 shenidar vimaan banaae gaye. inkaa upyog france main shahi vaayu corps va shahi nausena ke vaayu corps dwaara pratham vishwayuddh ke shuruaati dinon main kiya gaya.
 22 sitambar 1914 ko inkaa upyog bombar vimaanon ke tor pe bhi kiya gaya va inhon safal rup se jerman jameen par bam varshaaye.
