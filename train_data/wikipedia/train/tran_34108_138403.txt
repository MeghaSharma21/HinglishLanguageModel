 atmarati, vyakti kaa swayan ke prati asaamaanya kamatmak prembhav. unani myth "narsisus" ke aadhaar par ukt manovikruti kaa namakaran kiya gaya tha. narsisus nadi ke devtaa sefisus tathaa apsara liriope se utpann ati sundar balak tha. bhavishyavaktaa tirasius ne ghoshnaa kii thi ki narisesus kii umr kaaphi lambii hogi, basharte vah apana cheharaa na dekhe. "eko" naamak apsara athwa "aminiyas" ke prem ko thukaraane ke kaaran unani devtaa narsisus se aprasann ho gaye. falswaroop jalashay ke kinare jane par usne apane chehre kaa pratibimb pani main dekh liya or usapar mohit hokar praan tyaag die. mrityusthal par ek pushp uga jise maranevaale ke naam par "narsisus" kaha jane laga.
 uparyukt myth ke aadhaar par frid ne "atmarati" naamak pratyay athwa kalpanaadhaaran ko prastut karte hue kaha : "jis vyakti ke aakarshan kii vastu bahm jagat main nahin hoti, vah apane se prem karne lagata he or aisa hi vyakti atmapremi kehalaata he." tanaav se mukt hone ke liye bahari vastuon ke prati ruchi athwa aakarshan kaa hona aavashyak he, yah manovaigyanik satya he or jab vyakti bahm vastuon athwa vyaktiyon main ras nahin le paata to uski vrittiyon kaa kendrikaran swayan ke prati ho jaataa he. saamanyat: aisa antarmukhi vyaktiyon ke saath hota he. sanvibhram or akaal manobhrashan ke rogi bhi iske shikaar hote hai. praarambhik atmarati main balak ke prem or aakarshan kii vastu uskaa apana sharir maatr hota he. frid ke matanusaar yah manolangik vikaas kii praarambhik avasthaa he.
