 kshetravaad se abhipraay kisi desh ke us chote se kshetra se he jo aarthik ,saamaajik aadi kaarnon se apane pruthak astitv ke liye jaagrit he. 
 kshetravaad kii utpatti main itihaas kaa doharaa sahyog rahata he - sakaaratmak or nakaaratmak. sakaaratmak yogadan ke antargat shiv sena kaa or nakaaratmak yogadan ke antargat dravid munetra kadgam kaa udhaaharan diya ja saktaa he.
 bharat pracheen kaal se vishaal pradeshik rajyon vaala desh rahaa he. pracheen kaal main shaktishaalee raajaaon ke shaktiheen hone par adhinsth pradeshon or samanton ne apni swatantraaa kii ghoshnaa kar di tathaa apana swatantra shashan sthapit kar liya tha.
 praaia: bhashaa or samskruti bhi kshetravaad kii bhaawanaaon ko utpann karne main bahut sahyog dete hai. bharat ke vibhinn praanton or kshetron kii apni-apni bhashaa he. apni bhashaa ko ve adhik shreshth maanakar anya bhashaaon ko hin maan lete hai.
 swadhin bharat main desh kaa aarthik vikaas kaarykram kuch is prakaar chalaa ki kuch kshetra bahut adhik viksit ho gayi jabaki kuch kshetra pichad gayi. pichde kshetron main upja asantosh bhi kshetravaad kaa kaaran bana.
 prashasnic kaarnon se bhi vibhinn rajyon kii pragati main antar rahaa he. kuch rajyon main prabhaavshaalee audyogic niti ke kaaran vikaas hua he to kuch rajyon main bahut dhimi gati se ho rahaa he jo kshetravaad ko badhaava detaa he.
