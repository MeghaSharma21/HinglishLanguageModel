 lungisani tru-main angidi ek dakshin africa cricket khilaadi he jo dakshin africa kii rashtriya cricket team ke liye khelte hai. 2015 africa tinti-20 cap ke liye unhen northerns cricket team ne apni team main shaamil kiya gaya tha. inhone apane test cricket kii shuruyaat 13 janvari 2018 ko bharat ke saamne kii.
 ye mukhya rup se ek tej gendbaaj he jinhone apane ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket kii shuruyaat 7 farvari 2018 ko bharat ke hi khilaaf kii he jabaki tinti tinti karier kii shuruyaat 20 janvari 2017 ko srilanka ke khilaaf kii. 2018 indian premier lig kii nilaamee main inhen chennai super kings ne kharida, is prakaar yah inkaa pehla ipel he.
