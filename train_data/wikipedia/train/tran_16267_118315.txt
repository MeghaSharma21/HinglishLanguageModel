 puduccheri,, bharat ganraajya kaa ek kendra shasit pradesh he. pehle puduccheri ek francisi upnivesh tha jisamen 4 pruthak jilon kaa samavesh tha. puduccheri kaa naam pondichari iske sabase bade jile puduccheri ke naam par padaa tha. sitambar 2006 main pondichari kaa naam aadhikarik rup se badalakar puduccheri kar diya gaya jiskaa sthaneey tamil main arth nayaa gaanv hota he. bharat kaa yah kshetra lagbhag 300 varshon tak francisi adhikaar main rahaa he or aaj bhi yahaan francisi vaastushilp or samskruti dekhne ko mil jaati he. puraane samay main yah france ke saath hone vaale vyapaar kaa mukhya centre tha. aaj anek paryatak iske sundar samudr taton or tatkaaleen sabhyataa kii jhalak paane ke liye yahaan aate hai. keval paryaton kii drushti se hi nahin balki aadhyatmik or dharmik drushti se bhi yah sthaan bahut mahatvapurn he. is kaaran prativarsh lakhon kii sankhya main paryatak yahaan aate hai.
