 kanakavakta yaa karnamakshikta yah bahyakarna kaa ek chhot kaa rog he jo praaia: shravanchhidr par hota he. aisa mana jaataa he ki ye rog bahya jeevanunashak aushadhiyon ke prayog kaa kuprinaam hota he. ye jeevanunashak aushadhiyaan fngus jeevanuon hetu ek peedivardhak poshak kaa kaary karti hai. is rog main mukhyat: bhaag lenevaale fagus yaa to espargilas samooh se sambhddha hote hai yaa phir kendida elwicans samooh ke hote hai. yah rog ooshna yaa adhoshna jalwaayu main adhik prakhar hota he.
 kaan kaa pareekshan karne par pataa chalata he ki karnachhidr ek prakaar ke bhiige hue safed sokhta kaagaj kii tarah mulaayam padaarth se bharaa hota he. kabhi-kabhi espargilas nigar naamak fngus kii upasthiti ke kaaran yah mulaayam padaarth safed ke sthaan par gadhe bhure yaa kaale rang kaa bhi paaya ja saktaa he. is padaarth ko chhidr se hataa dene ke baad yadi turant hi doosraa padaarth ban jaae to karnakvakta naam kii beemaari kaa andeh kiya ja saktaa he. phir bhi nidaan kaa andeh mitaane hetu padaarth kaa sookshmdarshi dwaara pareekshan karte hai jisamen shakhayukta tantu micelium banaate hue tathaa bijayukt espargilas fngus ke tantu athwa cadida elwicans fngus ke yeast kii tarah kii koshikaaye bhi ho sakati hai.
 is rog kii dasha main kaan main khujali hoti he, kabhi-kabhi rangahin srav or yadi karnachhidr kaa puraa vyas rugnak padaarth se bharaa ho to dard ke saath bahraapan bhi paaya ja saktaa he. upachaar ke liye karnachhidr kii shushk safai tathaa "nistatin' naamak fngus vinaashak aushadhi kaa upyog yaa to powder kii tarah karte hai yaa phir maraham ke rup main lagaate hai. 2 kshamtawale selicylic amla ko 95 kshamtawale alcohol ke saath milakar athwa uparyukt ghol main bhiige hue feete ke tukade karnachhidr main karte hai. vaise anilin abhiranjak kaa bhi karnachhidr par lep karte hai, kintu iskaa prayog uparyukt aushadhiyon se kam prabhaavshaalee he.
