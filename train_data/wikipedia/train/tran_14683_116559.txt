 jethalaal champakalal gada yah tarak mehta kaa ulta chashma dharawahik kaa ek kirdaar he, jise dilip joshi ne nibhaayaa he.
 yah mukhyatah apane dukaan main tivi, mobile aadi saamaan bechata he. iske alaava iskaa mukhya kaary atmaram bhide se ladaai karnaa or krishnan subramaniyam ayyar ko sataana he.
 ise jalebi fafada khaanaa ati priy lagata he. iske alaava yah har shaam dukaan se ghar lautne ke baad abdul kii dukaan main apane mitron ke saath soda pine main bhi acha mahasoos karte hai. yah har din der se jaagnaa bhi pasand karte hai. iske kaaran kai baar vah apane babuji kii daant kha chuken hai.
 yah har din kisi naa kisi kaaran se pareshaan rahete hai. ghar par dayaa or tappu ke kaaryon ke alaava yah atmaram kii baton or kabhi kabhi ghar main aae apane saale sunderlaal se bhi pareshaan rahete hai. iske saath hi ve dukaan main baga ke kaaryon ke kaaran bhi dukaan main pareshaan rahete hai. in pareshaani se bachane ke liye ve tarak mehta kii sahaayta lete rahete hai.
