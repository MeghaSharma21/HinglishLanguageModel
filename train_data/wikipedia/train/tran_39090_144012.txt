 jalkumbhi ek ekbijpatri, sapushpak jaleey paudha he. jal kii sataha par tairne vaale is paudhe kii prayek pattee kaa vrunt phula hua evam spanji hota he. isamen parvasandhiyon se jhund main reshedaar jade lagi rahati hai. iskaa tana khokhla or chhidradaar hota he. iske phulon ko manjari kehete hai. yah dakshin america kaa mul-nivasee he. yah duniya ke sabase tej badhane vaale paudhe main se ek he. yah apni sankhya ko 2 saptaah main hi degunaa karne kii kshamta rakhata he. yah bahut adhik pariman main beej utpann karata he tathaa iske bijon main ankuran kii kshamta 30 varshon tak hoti he.
