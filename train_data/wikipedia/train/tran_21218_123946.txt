 mausami chatarji ek hindi film abhinetri hai.
 mausami chatarji kalakatta main janmi hai. unke pita fouj main the. iske alaava unke dada jaj ke rup main kaam kar chuke the. unka ek bhai tathaa bahan bhi hai. unka vivaah bahut hi kam umr main jayant mukherjee se hua tha. ve mahaan gayak hemanth kumaar ke putr the. unki do betiyaan hai jinke naam paayal or megha hai. unhone apana filmi safar shaadi ke baad shuru kiya tha jo ki us samay badi hi anokhi baat thi.
