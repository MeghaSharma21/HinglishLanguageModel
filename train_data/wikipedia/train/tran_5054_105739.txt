 gram-rinatmak bacteria ve bacteria hote hai jo gram abhiranjan pareekshan main crystal violet naamak rang se sane jane par us rang ko nahin pakadte hai or rangahin hi rahete hai. aise bacteria kii koshika bhitti peptidoglaican kii patali paraton se bani hoti hai. gram-rinatmak bacteria vishvabhar main milte hai. inaki kuch jatiyaan rog kaa kaaran hoti hai or antibiotic kii kuch shreniyan aisi hai jo keval is prakaar ke bacteria dwaara failaae gaye rogon kii rokthaam karti hai.
