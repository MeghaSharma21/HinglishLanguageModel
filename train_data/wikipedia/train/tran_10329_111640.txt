 al-amin hossain ek bangladesh cricket team ke khilaadi he jo teenon hi praroopon test, tinti tinti tathaa ek divasiy antarrashtriya praaroop main khelte he. 
 al-amin hossain ne apane test cricket karier kaa aagaaj oktober 2013 main newjiland cricket team ke khilaaf kiya tha. jabaki pehla vanade match 17 farvari 2014 ko srilanka cricket team ke khilaaf or pehla tinti-20 match 6 navambar 2013 ko newjiland ke khilaaf hi khelaa tha.
