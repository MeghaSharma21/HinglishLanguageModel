 purajivi mahakalp jin chah yugon main vibhakt kiya gaya he unamem se dur se pracheenatam yug ko ordovishi yug yaa avar pravaladi yug kehete hai. isi ko angeji main 'ordovisian period' kehete hai. san 1879 i. main lapworth mahoday ne is avar pravaladi yug kaa pratipaadan karke marachisan tathaa sejavik mahodayon ke bich pravaladi or trikhand yugon kii seemaa ke vishay main chal rahe pratidwandv ko samaapt kar diya. is yug ke prastaron kaa sarvapratham adhyayan wales praant main kiya gaya tha or ordovisian naam vahaan basanevaali pracheen jati ordovishai par padaa he.
 bharatvarsh main is yug ke str birle sthanon main hi milte hai. dakshin bharat main is yug kaa koi str nahin he. himalay main jo nimna str milte hai, ve bhi keval kuch hi sthanon main simit hai, yatha spity, kumaun, gadhwal or nepal. vishwa ke anya bhaagon main is yug ke prastar adhik milte hai.
 ordovishyan yug ke praaniyon ke avshesh cambrian kalp ke sadrush hai. is yug ke prastaron main graptolite naamak jeevon ke avasheshon kii prachurtaa he. trilobite or bracayopod jeevon ke avshesh bhi adhik maatra main milte hai. kasherudandi jeevon main machhlee kaa pradurbhaav isi yug main hua. america ke big horn parvat or blike parvat ke orchhovician balukashmon main praathmik machhliyon ke avshesh paae gaye hai.
