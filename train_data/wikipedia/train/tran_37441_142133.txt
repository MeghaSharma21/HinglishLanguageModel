 bhartiya rashtriya congress yaa congress bharat main ek raajnneetik party thi jiskaa gathan tab hua jab indira gandhi ke nishkasana ke baad congress party vibhajit hui.  
 12 november 1969 ko, indira gandhi ko party anushaasan kaa ulanghan karne ke liye congress party se nishkasit kar diya gaya. party kaa ant main vibhaajan hua or saath hi, indira gandhi ne ek pratidwandvi sanghathan kii sthapana kii, jo congress ke rup main janaa gaya. akhil bhartiya congress samiti main, 446 ke apane 705 sadasyon main se, 446 indira ke paksh main chale aaen. bhartiya rashtriya congress ko kabhi kabhi anaupchaarik rup se sindicket or indira gut ko indicate kaha jaataa tha. kamraj or baad main morarji desai congress ke netaa the.
