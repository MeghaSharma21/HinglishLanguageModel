 dinadayal shodh santhaan nanaaji deshmukh dwaara sthapit ek gramin vikaas sanstha he. isaki sthapana san 1972 main kii gayi thi. iskaa mukhyalay dilli main he. iskaa mukhya uddeshy dinadayal upaadhyaay ke vichaaron ko faleebhoot karnaa he.
 iske dwaara sabase pehle uttar pradesh ke gonda jile ke jayaprabha gram main san 1978 main kaary armbh kiya gaya. gonda main kaary kii safalataa ke baad ise anya rajyon bihar, madhyapradesh, maharashtra aadi main iskaa prasar kiya gaya.
 santhaan ke dilli mukhyalay main bahut si gatividhiyaan chalaayi jaati hai. vahaan ek pustkaalay he, sandarbh prabhag he. ve log rashtriya mahatv ke vishyon par kaaryshalaayen yojit karte hai. vidhyaarthiyon ke liye spardhaayen yojit kii jaatin hai. bhavishya main samyak praudyogiki anusandhan kendra kholane kii yojana he.
