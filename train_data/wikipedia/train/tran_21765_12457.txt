 uttrakhand kaa sthapaty bharat ke maidani bhaagon kii tulana main bhinn he or yah uttrakhand rajya ke pahari parivesh ke anuroop he. is rajya ke logon kaa jivan bahut samay tak mahanagareey bheed-bhaad se dur or prakruti ke nikat rahaa he, jo yahan ke bhawan nirmaan ke dhang or yahan kii samskruti main bhi dikhataa he. 
 aadhunik samay main yahan bhi bhawan nirmaan maidani kshetron ke aadhaar par hone laga he lekin yahan ke puraane bane gharon main yahan ke sthapaty kii chhap spasht rup se dekhi ja sakati he. visheshkar gramin kshetron main bane gharon ke daravaajon or khidakiyon par kii gai nakkashi or lakdi kii kaamon main yahan ke vishisht sthapaty kii jhalak dikhti he.
