 kentua khaad yaa varmicompost poshan padaarthon se bharpoor ek uttam jaev urvarak he. yah kentua aadi keedon ke dwaara vanaspatiyon evam bhojan ke kachare aadi ko vighatit karke banaai jaati he.
 varmi compost main badboo nahin hoti he or makkhi evam machhar nahin badhate he tathaa vaataavaran pradooshit nahin hota he. taapmaana niyantrit rahane se jeevanu kriyasheel tathaa sakriy rahete hai. varmi compost dedh se do maah ke andar taiyaar ho jaataa he. isamen 2.5 se 3% nitrogen, 1.5 se 2% sulfur tathaa 1.5 se 2% potash paaya jaataa he.
 kentua khaad kii visheshtaayen :is khaad main badboo nahin hoti he, tathaa makkhi, machhar bhi nahin badhate he jisase vaataavaran swasth rahata he. isse sookshm poshit tatwon ke saath-saath nitrogen 2 se 3 pratishat, phosphorus 1 se 2 pratishat, potash 1 se 2 pratishat miltaa he.
 kentua krishakon kaa mitra evam 'bhoomi kii aant' kaha jaataa he. yah sendriya padaarth, humas va mitti ko ekasaar karke jameen ke andar anya paraton main failaata he isse jameen poli hoti he va hawa kaa avaagaman bad jaataa he, tathaa jaldharan kii kshamta bhi bad jaati he. kechune ke pet main jo rasinic kriya va sookshm jeevanuon kii kriya hoti he, usase bhoomi main paye jane vaale natrajan, sphur, potash, calocium va anya sookshm tatwon kii uplabdhta badhati he. aisa paaya gaya he ki mitti main natrajan 7 guna, phosphorus 11 guna or potash 14 guna badhata he.
 kechune akele jameen ko sudhaarney evam utpaadakta vruddhi main sahaayak nahin hote balki inke saath sookshm jeevanu, sendrit padaarth, humas inkaa kaary bhi mahatvapurn he.
 kechune sendriya padaarth, evam mitti khaane vaale jeev he jo ceprofegus varg main aate he. is varg main do prakaar ke kechune hote hai :-
