 pratyakshvaad yaa tathyawad ke darshan ke anusaar keval vahi gyaan pramanik gyaan he jo gyanendriyon se praapt anubhav par aadhaarit ho. 
 beej rup main tathyawad kuch pracheen vicharakon kii shiksha main vidyaman tha, parantu ise ek darshan shakha kaa pad dene kaa shrey august kaant ko he. kamt ke siddhant main moulik sthaan maansik vikaas ke us niyam kaa he, jise usne aitihaasik rup main gyaan kii sabhi shakhaon par lagu kiya. yah maansik vikaas kii teen manjilon yaa avasthaaon kaa niyam he. uskaa kathan he-
 vigyaan kii sabhi shakhaayen samaan veg se pragati nahin karatin, koi aage nikal jaati he, koi peeche rah jaati he. kamt ne vigyaan kii shakhaon ko krambaddh kiya. saamaanya or saral se chalakar vishesh or asaral kii or badha. is kram main usne ganit, jyotish, bhautikee, rasayan, jeevaidya or samajashaastra ko sthaan diya. samajashaastra kaa to vah janmdata hi samajha jaataa he.
 kamt ne dharm or darshan ko bhootkaal kii vastu kaha, parantu apane siddhant ko positive philasophi naam diya or "maanavtaa ke dharm" kii neenv rakhi. manushyon main bhi stri ko pratham sthaan diya or kaha ab aage manushya kaa sir stri ke saamne hi jhukegaa.
