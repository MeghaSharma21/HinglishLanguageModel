 sultanganj kii buddha pratimaa ek pracheen vishaal tamra pratimaa he jo 1861 i main bhagalpur jile ke sultanganj main east indian railway ke nirmaan ke samay khudaai main mili thi. samprati yah pratimaa british sangrahalay main he. puraatatvavidon ne is murti kaa nirmaan kaal 500 se 700 i bataaya he. iskaa bhaar 500 kilogram se adhik he. yah 2.3 meter unchi tathaa 1 meter chaudee he.
