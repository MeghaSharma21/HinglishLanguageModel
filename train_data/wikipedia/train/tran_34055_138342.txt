 bhartiya rajya madhyapradesh main pichley kuch dinon se kaaphi baari barish hone kii vajah se baadh kaa rup le liya he jisamen lagbhag 22 logon kii mrityu ho chuki he.
 pradesh ke raisen, sihor, harda, hoshangabad, baitool or sagar jile main tej barish darj kii gai he.ab tak baari barish ke kaaran baadh kii vajah se lagbhag 2360 se zyada makaan ekadam kshatigrast ho chuke hanjabaki 17,263 ke kareeb makaan aanshik kshatigrast hue hai. baadh kii vajah se madhyapradesh main ab tak 3,32000 log prabhavit hue hai.
