 rashtriya mahila ayog bhartiya sansad dwaara 1990 main paarit adhinium ke tahat janvari 1992 main gathit ek sanvidhik nikaay he.yah ek aisi ikai he jo shikaayat yaa swatah sangyaan ke aadhaar par mahilaon ke sanvaidhanik hiton or unke liye kanoonee suraksha upayon ko lagu karaati he. ayog kii pehali pramukh sushri jayanti patnayak thim. 17 sitambar, 2014 ko mamata sharma kaa kaarykaal puraa hone ke pashchaat lalita kumarmangalam ko ayog kaa pramukh banaaya gaya tha,magar pichley saal sitambar main pad chhodane ke baad rekha sharma ko kaaryakari adhyaksh ke taur par yah sambhaal rahi thi,or ab rekha sharma ko rashtriya mahila ayog kaa adhyaksh banaaya gaya he.
 rashtriya mahila ayog kaa uddeshy bharat main mahilaon ke adhikaron kaa pratinidhitiv karne ke liye or unke muddon or chintaaon ke liye ek aawaaj pradaan karnaa he. ayog ne apane abhiyaan main pramukhataa ke saath dahej, raajnneeti, dharm or naukariyon main mahilaon ke liye pratinidhitiv tathaa shram ke liye mahilaon ke shoshan ko shaamil kiya he, saath hi mahilaon ke khilaaf police daman or gaali-galouj ko bhi gambhirtha se liya he.
 balaatkaar peedit mahilaon ke raahat or punarvaas ke liye banane vaale kanoon main rashtriya mahila ayog kii mahatvapurn bhoomika rahi he. apravasi bhartiya patiyon ke julmon or dhokhe kii shikaar yaa parityakt mahilaon ko kanoonee sahaara dene ke liye ayog kii bhoomika bhi atyant saraahneey rahi he.
 1.ayog ke kaaryon main samvidhan tathaa anya kanoonon ke antargat mahilaon ke liye upbandhit surakshapayon kii jaanch or pariksha karnaa he. saath hi unke prabhavakaari karyaanvayan ke upayon par sarkaar ko sifarish karnaa or samvidhan tathaa mahilaon ke prabhavit karne vaale anya kanoonon ke vidyaman praavdhaanon kii samiksha karnaa he. 
 2.iske alaava sanshodhano kii sifarish karnaa tathaa aise kanoonon main kisi prakaar kii kami, aparyaaptta, athwa kami ko dur karne ke liye upachaaratmak upaay karnaa he. 
 3.shikayaton par vichaar karne ke saath-saath mahilaon ke adhikaron ke vanchan se sambandhit maamalon main apni ore se dhyaan dena tathaa uchit praadhikariyon ke saath mudde uthaanaa shaamil he. 
 4.bhedbhaav or mahilaon ke prati atyaachaar ke kaaran uthne vaali vishisht samasyaon athwa paristhitiyon kii sifarish karne ke liye avarodhon kii pahchaan karnaa, mahilaon ke saamaajik aarthik vikaas ke liye yojana banane kii prakriya main bhagidaari or salaah dena tathaa usamen kii gai pragati kaa moolyankan karnaa inke pramukh kaary hai.
 6.saath hi karagaar, remand grihon jahaan mahilaon ko abhiraksha main rakhaa jaataa he, aadi kaa nirikshan karnaa or jahaan kahin aavashyak ho upachaaratmak karrwai kiye jane kii maang karnaa inke adhikaron main shaamil he. ayog ko samvidhan tathaa anya kanoonon ke tahat mahilaon ke rakshopyon se sambandhit maamalon kii jaanch karne ke liye civil nyaayalay kii shaktiyaan pradaan kii gai hai.
