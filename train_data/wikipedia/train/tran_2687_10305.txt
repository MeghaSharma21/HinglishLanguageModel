 patua yaa flox polimoniasi kul kaa ek chota sa paudha he, jisaki kareeb 60 prajatiyaan hai. nile, gulabi, laal or safed rang ke sundar phool ke kaaran yah vatikaaon main lagaaya jaataa he. phool deepikacar hote hai or gucchhon main nikalate hai. iske ugane ke liye acchhee prakaar kii mitti evam thande ardra sthaan kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. vatikaaon main bahudhaa flox drmandaai lagaaya jaataa he. shail udyaan tathaa kyaariyon ke kinare choti jativaale flox subulata, jise "moss pink", athwa ground flox kehete hai, lagaaya jaataa he. is paudhe kii adhikansh jatiyaan ekvarshi hoti hai, par flox painiculeta varshanuvarshi flox he, jo chaar foot tak unchaa hota he. isamen safed athwa gulabi rang ke sundar phool lagate hai.
