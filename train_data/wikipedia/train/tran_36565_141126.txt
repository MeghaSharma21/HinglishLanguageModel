 kisi tatva yaa yogic kii 1 gram-anu maatra ko oxigen main sthir aaytan par purnataya jalaane se jitani ushma nikalati he, use us tatv yaa yogic kii dahan-ushma kehete hai. dahan ooshmaa kaa maan nimnlikhit ikaayiyon main vyakt kiya ja saktaa he-
 dahan ooshmaa ko bomb calorimapi kii sahaayta se mapa jaataa he. 
 udhaaharan:udhaaharan ke liye nimnlikhit sameekaran se spasht he ki mithen kii dahan-ushma 2,12,800 calories he :
 carbon ko oxigen main jalaane par do yogicon kaa bananaa sambhav he-
 yah baat dhyaan dene yogya he ki carbon kii dahan-ushma 94,300 calories he, 26,000 calories nahin, kyonki pratham kriya main hi carbon purnataya jalataa yaa oxykrut hota he. duusari kriya main carbon, carbon monoksaide main parivartit ho gaya he, parantu abhi uskaa dahan purna nahin hua kyonki carbon monoksaide kaa or dahan karke use carbon dioxide main oxykrut kiya ja saktaa he.
 kisi padaarth ke ikai dravyaman ke dahan par jo ooshmaa nikalati he use uskaa caloriesnan maan kehete hai. kisi padaarth kaa caloriesnan maan uskaa vaishishty he. iske maatrak hai- kJ/kg, kJ/mol, kcal/kg, Btu/lb aadi. in maatrakon main aapas main nimnlikhit sambandh hai-
 calorijan maan do prakaar kaa hota he-
 dhyaan den ki carbon, carbon monoaksaide tathaa gandhak ke dahan kii ucchtar or nimnatar ooshmaaon main koi antar nahin hota kyonki in yogicon ke dahan se jal utpann nahin hota.
