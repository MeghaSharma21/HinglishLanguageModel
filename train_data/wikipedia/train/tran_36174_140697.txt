 jarnal shabd pracheen latini 'dvirnalis' or french shabd 'june' se vyutpann he jiskaa arth "dainiki" hota he. shabd kaa pracheen or vartmaan prachlit arth "dainik lekh" he, yadhyapi lekhon ke sanskaran or kalkram kuch bhi ho sakate hai. is prakaar "jarnal" main diary kii bhanti dainik lekha nahin hota varan masik, tremasik or vaarshik hota he. 20veen shatabdi main "jarnal" shabd kaa prayog patrikaon or samikshapatron ke liye hota he.
