 bogra kaa yuddh bharat paak yuddh 1971 kaa bhaag tha, jo vartmaan bangladesh main hua tha. is yuddh main pakistani sena ne bharat kii sena kaa jam kar muqabla kiya tha jis ke chalate baari kshati uthaane ke baad bhi bharat yuddh jeet nahin paaya tha. lekin bhartiya sena kii jabardast nakebandi se trast hokar paak sena ne dhaka main aatm samarpan kar diya or is prakaar yuddh samaapt hua.
