 
 tendua panthra jinas kaa ek vidal he jo africa or asia main paaya jaataa he. yah vidal prajatiyon jaise sher, baagh or jaigur kii tulana main sabase chota hota he.
 tendue adhikanshtah pili chamadi ke hote hai jisapar gahare rang ke dhabbe hote hai. lekin tenduon kii vibhinn upaprajatiyon main bahut se antar bhi ho sakate hai. kuch tendue purna rup se kaale bhi hote hai, or unhen kaala panther kaha jaataa he. nar tendue, maadaa tenduon se lagbhag 30% bade aakaar ke hote hai.
 tendue mul rup se puure africa or dakshin asia main rahete aae hai. lekin ab tenduon kii bahut si upaprajatiyaan, visheshkar asia main, vilupti ke kagaar par hai. anya badi billiyon kii tulana main tendue sabase bade kshetron main rahete hai or is kaaran inaki bahut si upaprajatiyaan pai jaati hai. tendue vibhinn paryaavarnon main rahete hai jaise: varshaavan, van, parvat or savana.
