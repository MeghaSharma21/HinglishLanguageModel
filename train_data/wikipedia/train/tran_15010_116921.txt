 mandaleshwar madhya pradesh ke khargon jila kaa ek shahar he. maheshwar se 8 kimi dur yah shahar bhi narmada ke kinare hi basaa he. narmada par jal-vidyut pariyojna va baandh kaa nirmaan hua he. yahaan se sameep hi cholii naamak sthaan par atyant pracheen shiv-mandir he jahaan par bahut bhavy shiv-ling sthit he. yahan mandan mishra va aadi guru shankaracharya ke madhya shastrarth hua tha jile main is nagar ko shaikshanik kendra kaa rutaba hasil he . sardaar patel mahavidhyalay, sardaar patel engeering college sahit anek sansthaayen mandaleshwar ko vikaas kii raah par le ja rahi he .paryaton ke lien yah sthaan sarvatha upyukt he . anek mandir va sundar ghat, narmada tat par suryoday va suryaast ke drishy atyant manamohak hote he . pracheen mandiro kaa nagar aapaki yatra kaa majaa duguna kar degaa .
