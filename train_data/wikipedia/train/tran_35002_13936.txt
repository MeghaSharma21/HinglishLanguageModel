 baghelkhand madhya bharat kaa ek kshetra he. yah madhya pradesh rajya ke uttar-purvi ore sthit he. isamen madhya pradesh ke jile sammilit hai:
 
 The proposed state includes the following districts: baghelkhand main kuch jile uttar pradesh ke tathaa kuch madhya pradesh ke hai, vartmaan main baghelkhand kshetra kii sthiti bahut hi gambhir he. yah kshetra paryaapt aarthik sansadhano se paripurna he kintu phir bhi yah atyant pichhadaa he. iskaa mukhya kaaran he, raajnneetik udaseenta. na to centre sarkaar or na hi rajya sarkarein is kshetra ke vikaas ke liye gambhir hai. isliye is kshetra ke log alag baghelkhand rajya kii maang lambe samay se karte aa rahe he. yun to baghelkhand kshetra do rajyon main vibhajit he-uttar pradesh tathaa madhya pradesh, lekin bhu-saanskrithik drushti se yah kshetra ek duusare se abhinn rup se judaa hua he. reeti rivaajon, bhashaa or vivaah sambandhon ne is ekta ko or bhi pakki neenv par khadaa kar diya.
