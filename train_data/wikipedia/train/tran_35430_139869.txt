 ayaan mukherjee lakhanu, uttar pradesh main janmen bhartiya film nirdeshak or abhinayta hai jinhone apana nirdeshkeey pravesh 26 varsh kii aayu main karan johar ke saath 2009 kii bollywood film vek ap cid main kiya jo tikat khidki par darshakon dwaara khub saraahi gai.
 mukherjee bengali film abhinayta deb mukherjee ke putr hai. unke dada sashadhar mukherjee film nirmaataa the jinhone mumbai main filmalaya studio sthapit kiya or dil deke dekho, lav in shimla, ek musfir ek hasina or leader jaisi filmein di. yah studio ab parivaar ke anya sadasyon dwaara sanchalit kiya jaataa he. wo joy mukherjee ke bhatije hai or kaajol tathaa tanisha mukherjee ke chachere bhai hai or rani mukherjee ke bhi dur ke chachere bhai hai.
 mukherjee ne apana kaary 2004 kii film swadesh main apane ristedaar ashutosh goverikar ke sahaayak ke rup main armbh kiya or uske pashchaat kabhi alavida naa kahana main johar ke saath kaary kiya. filmon se ek laghu avkaash lene ke pashchaat, mukherjee ne vek ap cid kii patakatha likhi or yah johar ko di, jinhone is par film banane kaa nirnay kiya. ranbir kapur or konkana sen sharma abhinit yah film safal rahi.
 mukherjee kii agali film ye javaani he divaani he jo punah karan johar ke dharma production binar tale bani or ranbir kapur tathaa deepika padukon isamen mukhya bhoomika main hai. is film ko tikat khidki par baari sasafalataa mili. film ne saat din main sau karod kaa aankada paar kar liya.
 2010: filmafere puraskaar
