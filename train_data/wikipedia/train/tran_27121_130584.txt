 sahib, bibi or gangster tigmanshu dhulia dwaara nirdeshit 2011 kii bollywood premakahani, rahasyamay, naatak film he. is film main jimmy shergill, maahi gil or randeep hooda agrani bhoomikaaon main hai. film 30 sitambar 2011 ko jari kii gayi or samikshakon se sakaaratmak samikshaayen praapt kii. film uttar pradesh ke ek royal parivaar kii kahani sunaati he lekin iskaa filmakan gujarat ke devgarh baaria naamak sthaan par hua. is film kii uttarkruti sahib, bivi or gangster riterns 2013 main jari kii gayi or use bhi sakaaratmak samikshaayen mili.
