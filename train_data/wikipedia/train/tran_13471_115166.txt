 bukhoj riley vaidyut vitaran evam sampreshan ke kshetra main prayukt ek suraksha yukti he jo kuch tel-bhare transformaron evam prerakatvon main upyog kii jaati he. yah yukti inke 'conservetter' ke andar lagati he. jab bhi transformar yaa prerakatv ke andar kisi prakaar dielectric fail hoti he to bukose riley ko iskaa pataa chal jaataa he or yah upkaran kii suraksha ke liye power kaat detii he.
 bukose riley kai alag-alag siddhanton par kaam karti he.
