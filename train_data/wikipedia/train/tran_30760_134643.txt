 maranottaravidya yaa eshchatology gyaan-vigyaan kii vah shakha he jiske antargat nirvana yaa mrityu ke baad kii sthitiyon ke prashn par vichaar kiya jaataa he.
 eshchatology shabd unani shabdon-ishchatos evam logia se bana he, jiskaa taatpary he, antim baton, yatha-mrityu, nyaay evam mrityu ke uparant kii avasthaa se sambandh rakhane vaala vigyaan. iske do swaroop hai, jinamein ek kaa sambandh he mrityu ke uparant vyakti kii niyati, aatma kii amarta, paap evam dand tathaa swarg evam narak ke vishay kii charchaa se or duusare kaa sambandh he akhil brahmand, uski sristi, parinti evam uddhaar tathaa sabhi vastuon ke param ant ke vishay kii charchaa se. pracheen granthon main pratham swaroop par hi adhik bal diya gaya he, kintu aajkal vaigyanik drushtikon rakhane vaale log bahudhaa duusare swaroop par hi adhik sochate hai.
 mrityu ke uparaant maanav kii sthiti kaa prashn adikalin bharatiyon, mixriyon, chaldiyanon, unaniyon evam parsiyon ke samaksh ek mahatvapurn jigyaasa evam samasya ke rup main maujuud rahaa he. prayek dharm ke sastron main iske vishay main pruthak drushtikon miltaa he.
