 kulottung chol dwitiya kulottung kaa pautra or vikram chol kaa putr. iskaa shasankaal raajnneetik drushti se purnat: shanti kaa tha. isaki aitihaasik khyaati anya kaaran se he. iske pita vikram chol ne chidambaram ke suvikhyath mandir ke navinikaran or vistaar kaa kaary aarambh kiya tha. use isane puraa kiya. is kram main nataraj ke mandir ke aangan main govindraj kii jo murti pratishthit thi, use nikalava kar samudr main phenkava diya. kaha jaataa he ki ramanuj ne is murti ko samudr main se nikalwakar tirupati main pratishthit kiya tha. bahut dinon baad vijaynagar ke ramrai ne us murti ko apane mul sthaan par pun: pratishthit kiya.
