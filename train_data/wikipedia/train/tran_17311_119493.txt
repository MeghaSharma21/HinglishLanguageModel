 rab ne bana di jodi varsh 2008 main bani ek hindi film he. film ke mukhya kalakaar shahrukh khan or anoushka sharma hai. yah navdith tarika anoushka sharma kii pehali film he. film main amritsar ke ek daftar ke babu ko dikhaaya gaya he, jisaki shaadi se pehle kisi bhi ladki se koi pahchaan tak nahin hoti.
 suri punjab power kaa ek karmchaaree he. uski shaadi apane guru kii beti tani se tab ho jaati he jab tani kii shaadi ke din uskaa duulhaa ek bas durghatana main maaraa jaataa he or is vajah se uske pita kii hridaygati ruk jaati he. marate-marate wo suri se ye vachan le lete hai ki wo tani se shaadi karegaa. tani shaadi ke baad suri ko apana nahin paati he. wo suri ke saath to rahati he par suri se kahati he ki "main aapse pyaar kabhi nahin kar sakuungi". suri ghar ke upar vaale kamare main sota he or tani neeche vaale kamare main. donon main yada-kada hi baat hoti he.
 suri kaa jigari dost suri ke vesh-bhusha main parivartan laa detaa he. jab tani use nrity-prashikshan kendra main dekhtaa he to nae rangarup main use pahchaan nahi paati he. suri pehle to bachane kaa prayaas karata he par jab use tani kii anbhigyata kaa pataa chalata he to wo apana naam raaj bataata he. raaj shaam main to tani ke saath rahata he par apana vesh badalakar waapas ghar suri banakar aa jaataa he. kai dinon tak aisa chalane se tani ko raaj ke prati khichaav kaa ehsaas hota he. par suri ye nirnay karata he ki wo chahta he ki tani suri ke rup ko pasand kar naa ki raaj vaale rup ko. ant main tani ko raaj or suri ke ek hi vyakti hone ke bare main pataa chalata he. is film kii patakatha ko da academy of moshan pikchars arts and sciensej kii margrat heric librari main sthaan diya gaya he.
