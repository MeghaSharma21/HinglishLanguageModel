 bharat ne 27 july se 12 agast 2012 tak landan main hue 2012 greeshmakaleen olympic main bhaag liya tha. isamen bharat kii ore se 83 khilaadi kul 13 spardhaon main bhaag lene gayi the. yah sankhya bharat dwaara abhi tak olympix khelon main bheje gayi logon kii sankhya main sarvaadhik thi.
 bharat ko in khelon main kul chah padak mili jisamen 2 rajat padak evam 4 kaansya padak the. ye ab tak kisi bhi olympic khelon main bharat dwaara jite gaye sarvaadhik padak the.
