 mangalayan 2 bharat kaa mangalayan ke baad mangal grah ke liye doosraa mishan he. bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan dwaara mangal grah ke liye mishan ko 2020 main laanch kiya jaayegaa. yah mishan apane saath orbitor, lander tathaa rover le ke jayega.
 isro ke mangalayan kii safalataa ke baad isro ne 28 oktober 2014 bangalore main yojit ejiniers conklev 2014 sammelan main duusare mangalayan mishan ghoshnaa kii. isro ne 2016-2017 tak jieselvi mark 3 kii puri tarah parichaalan kii ummid he. isaliye isro ne mangalayan-2 ke liye jieselvi mark 3 ko chunaa he.
 janvari 2016 main, bharat or france isro ne sanyukt rup se mangalayan 2 ke nirmaan ke liye 2020 tak ke liye aashay ke ek patr par hastakshar kiye.
