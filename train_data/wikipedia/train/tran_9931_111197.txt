 raikv ek tatvagyani rishi the. ve vaayu ko sristi kaa adikaaran maanate hai . gaadi ke neeche nivas karne ke kaaran rekv 'sayugwa' kehalaae . janashruti he ki raja mrugaya ke samay do hanson ke vaartalap main inke punya kii prashansa sun, dhoondhata hua aayaa or inhen bahumoolya daan dena chaha parantu use asweekaar kar inhone ulte unhen hi apni gaadi daan main de di. tadnantar raja ne apni kanya tathaa ek gaanv inhen daan main dekar tatvagyan kaa upadesh grahan kiya.
