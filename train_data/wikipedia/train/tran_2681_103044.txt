 purvi tateey maidani kshetra dakshin bharat main purvi ghat or bengal kii khadi ke bich faile lambey samtal kshetra ko kahaa jaataa he. yah tamil nadu se lekar pashchim bengal tak 120 kimi kii ausat chaudaai main failaa hua he. kai nadiyon ke muhane is kshetra main aate hai. inamen pramukh hai mahanadi, godavari, kaveri or krishna. is kshetra main uttar-purvi or dakshin-pashchimi monsoon varshaaen hoti hai, jo 1000 mi.me se 3000 mi.me. tak hoti hai. is samtal kii chaudaai vibhinn kshetron main 100-130 kilometre tak he. mahanadi or krishna nadiyon ke bich ke is kshetra ko uttari sirkaar or krishna or kaveri ke bich ise carnetic kshetra kaha jaataa he.
 is kshetra main teen mukhya anubhag hai:-
 uttar main utkal samtal se dakshin main pulikat jhil tak ke kshetra ko andhra samtal kehete hai. pulikat jhil main hi sriharikota dweep he. is kshetra main hi godavari or kaveri ke delta banate hai. yaheen kolleru jhil bhi he.
 yah kshetra pulikat jhil se kanyakumari tak aata he. is kshetra main hi khaveri kaa delta banataa he. is delta kii upatyaka dakshin bharat ke liye annapurna he.
