 saboodana mikchar
 saamagree :
 100 gram naylon saboodana, 50 gram aalu kaa sookhaa hua kis, 150 gram kute yaa pise hue moongfalee ke dane, namak swaadanusaar, laal mirch powder ek bada chammach va 2 bade chammach shakar pisi hui, talane ke liye tel.
 vidhi :
 sarvapratham sabudane ko garm tel main daalkar phula len. uske baad aalu kaa sookhaa kis bhi tel main tal len. ab isamen pise hue moongfalee ke dane, laal mirch, namak va pisi hui shakar daalkar mix kar len. 
 sabudane kaa chiwda banakar taiyaar ho gaya. ise aap chaay ke saath sarv kar sakate hai.
