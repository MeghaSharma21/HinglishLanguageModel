 jelep laa himalay kaa ek pahari darra he jo bharat ke sikkim rajya ko dakshin tibet main chumbi ghati ko jodta he. antarrashtriya seemaa kii bhartiya taraf is darre ke charnon main prasiddh menamecho jhil sthit he.
 dhyaan den ki 'laa' shabd tibeti bhashaa main 'darre' kaa arth rakhata he. qaayade se is darre ko 'jelep darra' yaa 'jelep laa' kahana chaiye. ise 'jelep laa darra' kahana 'darre' shabd ko dohraane jaisaa he, lekin yah prayog phir bhi prachlit he.
