 surma bharat ke assam rajya or bangladesh kii nadi he. manipur kii uttari parvatamala se yah nadi nikalati he. is nadi kaa udgam japwo ke dakshini parvataskandhon ke madhya main he. yahan se nikalne ke baad yah manipur kii pahadiyon se hokar bahati he. manipur evam kachaar main is nadi kaa naam baraak he. kachaar jile main badarpur se kuch aage yah do shakhaon main bant jaati he- uttari shakha or dakshini shakha. uttari shakha surma kehalaati he or bangladesh ke silahat jile se hokar bahati he. dakshini shakha kusiara kehalaati he or yah pun: bibiana yaa kaalni evam baraak naamak shakhaon main vibhajit ho jaati he. ye donon shakhaayen aage chalakar uttari shakha se mil jaati he. bangladesh ke maimansingh jile ke bhairav bazaar naamak sthaan par surma nadi brahmaputra kii puraani shakha se milti he. udgam sthal se lekar is sangamasthal tak surma nadi kii kul lambaai lagbhag 896 kimi he. ab yah is sangamasthal se lekar narayan ganj evam chandpur ke madhya tak, jahaan surma evam brahmaputra kaa sanyukt jal ganga se miltaa he, meghna kehalaati he.
