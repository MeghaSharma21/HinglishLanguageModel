 rajgad bhartiya rajya rajasthan ke alvar jile kaa ek nagarpalika kshetra evam nagar he.
 rajgad kii maasat se oonchaai 479 meter he.rajgarh main ek kila jo bahut purana he.
 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar rajgad kii kul jasankhya 25,006 thi.2001 ke anusaar isamen 53% purush or 47% mahilayen thi. rajgad kii ausat sakshartaa 65% he jo rashtriya ausat 59.5% se adhik he.
