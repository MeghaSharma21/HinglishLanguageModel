 akruti-vigyaan, jeev vigyaan kii ek shakha he jiske antargat kisi jeev kii akruti, uski sanrachana or uske vishisht sanrachanaatmak gunon kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he.
 akruti-vigyaan ke antargat kisi jeev kii bahari rachana jaise ki uski akruti, sanrachana, rang, patternadi pahaluon ke atirikt uske aantarik angon jaise ki asthiyaan, yakrut ityadi kii akruti or sanrachana ko bhi shaamil kiya jaataa he. akruti-vigyaan, jeev vigyaan kii ek anya shakha sharir kriya vigyaan, kaa thik vipareet hota he, jisamen vibhinn angon ke kaaryon kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he.
