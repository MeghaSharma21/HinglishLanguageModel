 ubhaysanvedi aisa rasinic yogic hota he jisamen jalsnehi or jalavirohi donon prakaar ke bhaag hon. aksar iskaa jalavirohi bhaag vasasnehi bhi hota he isliye kai paribhaashaon main yah bhi kaha jaataa he ki ubhaysanvedi yogicon main jalsnehi or vasasnehi donon bhaag hote hai. jaevrasiniki main ubhaysanvedi yogicon kaa gaharaa adhyayan kara jaataa he. masalan lipid apane aap ko kai roopon main vyavasthit kar pate hai. lipid bahurupata kahalaane vaali yah kshamta prithvi par sabhi gyaat jivan kaa ek zaruri aadhaar he or lipidon kaa ubhaysanvedi hona hi unhen yah kshamta detaa he. sabun or disergent jaisi dainik aavashyaktaa kii kai chize bhi ubhaysanvedi rasayanon kii bani hoti hai.
