 asif shaikh bhartiya televijan or film abhinayta he. unhone sabase pehle shuruyaat dharawahik ham log se kii thi. unhone mukaddar kaa badshaah jaise sahaayak bhoomika se filmon main shuruyaat kii. unki mukhya bhoomika vaali film 1991 main yaara dildara bhi ai thi. ye film sangeet nirdeshak jatin-lalit kii pehali film hone ke saath-saath geet "bin tere sanam" ke liye jaani jaati he. haalanki unhen mukhya abhinayta ke rup main safalataa nahin mili or unhone kai filmon main sahaayak bhoomikaaye hi adaa kii. 
 anya kuch filmein jisamen wo najar aae:- karan arjuna, auzaar, bandhan, pyaar kiya to darana kya, hasina maan jaayegi, kunvaara, jodi nan 1, dil ne jise apana kaha or shaadi karke fas gaya yaar . vartmaan main wo hasya tivi kaarykram bhabhi ji ghar par he! ke apane kirdaar vibhuti narayan mishra ke liye prasiddhi paae hai.
