 soofivaad yaa tasawwuf islam kaa ek rahasywaadee panth he. iske panthiyon ko sufi kehete hai. inkaa lakshya aadhyatmik pragati evam maanavtaa kii seva rahaa he. sufi raajaaon se daan-uphaar sweekaar nahin karte the or sada jivan bitaana pasand karte the. inke kai tareeqe yaa gharaane hai jinamein soharavardi, nakshavandia, kadiri, chishtiya, kalandariyaa or shustariya ke naam pramukhataa se liya jaataa he.
 mana jaataa he ki soofivaad iraq ke basra nagar main qareeb ek hazaar saal pehle janma. raabia, al adaham, mansoor hallaaj jaise shaksiyaton ko inkaa praneta kaha jaataa he - ye apane samakalinon ke adarsh the lekin inako apane jeevankaal main aam janata kii avhelnaa or tiraskaar jhelni padi. soofiyon ko pahchaan al gajali ke samay se hi mili. baad main attaar, rumi or haafiz jaise kavi is shrenee main gine jate hai, in sabon ne shaayari ko tasawwuf kaa madhyam banaaya. bharat main iske pahunchane kii sahi-sahi samyavadhi ke bare main aadhikarik rup se kuch nahin kaha ja saktaa lekin barahavin-terahavin shatabdi main khwaja moinuddin chishti baqayada soofivaad ke prachaar-prasar main jute gaye the.
 sufi naam ke strot ko lekar koi ek mat nahin he. kuch log ise unani sofus se niklaa maanate hai. is mul se philosphi, theosphi ityadi shabd nikle hai. kai isko arabi safh se niklaa maanate hai. kuch log kehete hai ki ye soof se aayaa he kyonki kai sufi darvesh oon kaa chonga pahante the.
 sufi maanate hai ki unka strot khud pigambar mohammad hai.
