 kaja spiti ghati kaa up-sambhageeya mukhyalay he. samudratal se isaki oonchai 3,800 meter he. yah spiti nadi ke baane kinare par khadi chotii kii talahati par sthit he. kabhi yah spiti ke pramukh "naino" kaa mukhyalay tha. yah rajdhani shimla se 425 kilometre dur he. kaja main ek bharan stesan, aaraam ghar or kuch chote hotel hai. yah is kshetra main bhraman liye aadhaar ke rup main kaary karata he. anya sthanon main ki, hikkim, comoke or langiya mathon main ghuma ja saktaa he.
