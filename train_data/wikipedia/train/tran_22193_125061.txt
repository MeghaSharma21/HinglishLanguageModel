 sneha sharma, ek bhartiya racing chaalak hai, jon formula 4 neshnal racing championships main pratispardhaa karti hai, or saath hi main vah indigo airlines kii ek payalat bhi hai. sneha kaa janm kolkata main hua tha parantu unka bachapan mumbai main hi beetaa. unki schuli shiksha conossa kanvent skool main hui or flying training san francisko, miami, or kuala lampur se kii. 
 vah 16 varsh kii umr se hi racing kar rahi hai. dasanvi kaksha main 17 varsh kii aayu main udaan ke adhyayan ke dauraan unhone emref rashtriya carting championships main bhaag liya. 2009 main sneha ne jeke tair neshnal carting championships ke 4 stroke shrenee main doosraa sthaan hasil kiya. emaiai neshnal carting championships ke casiti ke final round ke liye arhata praapt karne vaali vah ekmaatr ladki thi. vah 2015 voxwagon vento cap or bharat main toyota atius cap ke liye shortlist hui thi. marcides yuva star chaalak kaarykram main sheersh paanchavaa number hasil karne ke baad unhen bharat kii sabase tej mahila resar kaa khitaab diya gaya. sneha ko jeke tair or indigo airlines dwaara apane racing karier ke liye praayojan praapt karaayaa gaya. unhen germany or malaysia main bhi racing sits di gayi hai. aaj tak vibhinn shreniyon main sneha ne 6 rache jiti va 14 ranor-ap pad hasil kiye hai.
