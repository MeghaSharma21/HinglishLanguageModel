 amur nadi duniya kii dasvin sab se lambi nadi he. yah nadi rus ke sudur purvi kshetron or purvottaree china ke darmiyaan antarrashtriya seemaa bhi he. rus or china ke bich zamin ke liye jo jhadpen 17veen shatabdi main armbh hui or 20veen shatabdi tak chalin, unamem amur nadi kii ahm bhoomika thi.
 amur nadi pashchimi manchuriya kii pahadiyon main shilka nadi or argun nadi ke milaap se 303 meter kii oonchai par janm leti he. vahaan se yah purv kaa rukh karti he jahaan yah china or rus kii seemaa ke rup main maanya he. 400 kilometre tak yah gumti hui dakshin-purv kii taraf bahane lagati he, jis dauraan isamen bahut se chote nadi-naale mil jate hai. is kshetra ke kai nagar-qasbe amur ke kinare based hue hai. phir yah dakshin kii ore bahati he jahaan yah rus ke blagoachensk shahar or china ke heihe shahar ke bich se guzarati he. yahan is se zeya nadi bhi aa milti he jis se iskaa pani or chaudaai kaafii bad jate hai.
 thodaa aage chalakar is se bureya nadi bhi mil jati he or phir 250 kilometre baad is se songaua nadi milti he. ab yah purvottar kaa rukh karti he or rus ke khaabarowsk nagar kii taraf jaati he. yahan is se ussuri nadi milti he, jiske baad yah china-rus kii seemaa nahin rahati or puri tarah rus main hi aa jaati he. is sthaan par is main itnaa pani hota he ki ek dhar kii bajaae isamen kai dhaare saath-saath ek chaudee ghati main chalati hai. lagbhag 200 kimi baad yah ghati thodi tang ho jaati he or phir nadi uttar kii ore chalakar amgun nadi se vilay karti he. iske baad yah achaanak purv ko mudakar lagbhag 20 kilometre baad okhotsk sagar or japan sagar ko jodne vaale tathari jaldamru main Pacific Ocean se mil jaati he.
