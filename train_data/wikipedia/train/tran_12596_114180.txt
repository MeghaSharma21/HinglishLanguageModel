 howard ellen o'bryan ke rup main 4 oktober 1941 ko luiziana main janmin,ain rice, ek prasiddh america lekhika hai, jo apana sahitya srijan angreji bhashaa main karti hai. rice kaa sahitya aamtaur par kaamuk, gothic yaa dharmik vishyon se prerit hota he. rice kaa vivaah persiddh kavi or chitrakaar stan rice se hua tha or stan kii mrityu ke samay tak ek dampatti ke rup main unhonen 41 saal saath saath bitaaye the. unki pustakon kii abtak lagbhag 10 karod pratiyaan bik chuki hai or is lihaaj se wo samakaleen vishwa kii ek sabase adhik padhi jane vaali lekhika hai.
