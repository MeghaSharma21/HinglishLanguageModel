 vasa amla carbon parmaanuon kii lambi shrinkhalaa dwaara gathit carbonic amla hai, jinke ek sire par carboxylic mulak hota he. yah santrupt tathaa asantrupt donon hi prakaar kaa hota he. jis vasa amla ke sabhi bandha ekal hote hai use santrupt tathaa jisamen ekal ke atirikt dwibandh yaa tribandh hote hai use asantrupt kii shrenee main rakhte hai.
