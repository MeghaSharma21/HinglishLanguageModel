 govind vinayak karandikar marathi ke prasiddh lekhak the. ve 'vinda karandikar' ke naam se bhi jane jate the. 
 marathi bhashaa ke mahaan kavi karandikar ko bharat ke sarvocch sahitya puraskaar gyanpith se nawaja gaya tha. ve kavi ke saath-saath nibandhkaar or aalochak bhi the. unhone mahaan unani vidwaan arestu kii kavitaaon ko marathi main anuwaad kiya tha. karandikar ko aadhunik marathi kaviyon main sabase prayogdharmi kavi mana jaataa he.
