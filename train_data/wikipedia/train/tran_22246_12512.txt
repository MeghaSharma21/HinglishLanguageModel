 adaalat bhartiya hindi dharawahik he, jiskaa prasaaran soni par 20 navambar 2010 se prarambh hua or 11 july 2015 tak prasaarit hua. jiskaa nirmaan abhimanyu singh va roopali singh ne kiya he. isamen mukhya bhoomika main ronit roy hai, jo ek vakil ke di pathak kaa kirdaar nibhaa rahe hai.
 yah kahani ke di pathak naamak ek vakil ko milane vaale prakaran par aadhaarit he, jise koi bhi prakaran miltaa he to vah usamen koi bhi apradhi ho use pakad letaa he or sabhi ke saamne uskaa sach saamne laa detaa he. jiskaa sahaayak varun jhaveri he jo har prakaran main saboot khojataa rahata he or jaankaree ekatrit karata he.
