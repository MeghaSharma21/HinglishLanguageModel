 prakaash ke prism se hokar guzarane par vibhinn rangon ke prakaash main bant jane ko parikshepan kaha jaataa he.
 surya ke prakaash se praapt rangon main bangnee rang kaa vikshepan sabase adhik evam laal rang kaa vikshepan sabase kam hota he.newton ne 1666 i. main paaya ki bhinn-bhinn rang bhinn-bhinn konon se vikshepit hote hai.varna-vikshepan kisi paardarshee padaarth main bhinn-bhinn rangon ke prakaash ke bhinn-bhinn veg hone ke kaaran hota he. atah kisi padaarth kaa apvartanaank bhinn-bhinn hota hai.
