 samasya aadhaarit sikh ek chaatr kendrit shiksha shaastra he jo chaatro ko ek open anded samasya ke hal ke anubhav ke madhyam se ek vishay ke bare main jaankaree detaa haichaatro ko ranneetiyon or vishay-vishesh kaa gyaan miltaa he.samasya aadhaarit shiksha kaa lakshya chaatro ko lacheela gyaan, prabhavi samasya ko sulajhane kaa kaushal, aatm nirdeshit, prabhavi sahyog kaushal or aantarik motiveshan. samasya ke aadhaar par shiksha ke vikaas main madad karne kii ek sakriy style he.samoohon main kaary karnaa se chaatro ko pataa chalata he kii unhe kya jaanane kii jarurat he, ve pehle se hi kya jaanate hai, unki kya kya pahchaan he, or kaise or kahan samasya kaa samaadhaan karne ke liye netritv kar sakate hai.prashikshak kii bhoomika samarthan, maardarshak or seekhney kii prakriya kii nigaraanee ke dwaara sikhaane kii he.
 samasya aadhaarit swaroop ko nimna tareeke se samajha ja saktaa he :1. chaatr kendrit shiksha.2. adarsh shiksha chote chaatr guton 6-10 logon, main kiya jaataa he.
 skulon or vishvavidhyalayon main lagu karne ke liye sansadhano kii ,yojana or sanghathan kii bahut zarurat he.

 •	shaikshik parinaamon naye paathyakram ko praaroop karnaa or paribhaashit karnaa.
 •	visheshgyon se salaah lenaa.
 •	yojana aayojan or prabandh.
 •	sanjanaatmak bhaar.

 •	istemaal samasyaon kii prakruti. 
 •	samooh par prabhaav.
 •	shikshaarthiyon kii prerana main ruchi me kami.
 niyokta ne chaatro ke anbhavi sanchaar, team work, sammaan or sahyog kaa sakaaratmak visheshtaon kii saraahna kii he.padhti main behatar bhavishya ke acchhee taiyaari pradaan karata hai.
