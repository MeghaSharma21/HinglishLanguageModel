 devisingh ransingh shekhavat bharat ke purv raashtrapati shrimati. pratibha patil ke pati hai. saath hi ye raajnneeti main bhi sakriy rahe hai.
 7 july 1965 ko devisingh raansingh shekhavat ke saath pratibha patil kaa vivaah hua tha. vivaah ke samay devisingh shekhavat amravati ke 'mahayor' the. donon pati-patni samajaseva ke kaary se bhi jude the.
