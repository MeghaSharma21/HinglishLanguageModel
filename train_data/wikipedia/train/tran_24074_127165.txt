 adhyaropapvaad ) advait vedant main aatmatatv ke upadesh kii vaigyanik vidhi. brahm ke yatharth rup kaa upadesh dena advait mat ke acharya kaa pradhan lakshya he. brahm he swayan nishprapanch or iskaa gyaan bina prapanch kii sahaayta ke kisi prakaar bhi nahin karaayaa ja saktaa. isliye aatma ke upar dehdharmon kaa aarope prathmat: karnaa chahie arthath aatma hi man, buddhi, indriya aadi samast padaarth he. yah 'praathmik vidhi adhyarope' ke naam se prasiddh he. ab yukti tathaa tark ke sahare yah dikhalaanaa padataa he ki aatma na to buddhi he, na sankalp-vikalparup man he, na bahari vishyon ko grahan karanevaali indriya he or na bhoga kaa aaytan yah sharir he. is prakaar aropit dharmon ko ek-ek kar aatma se hataate jane par antim koti main uskaa shuddh sachchidanand rup batch jaataa he vahi uskaa sachcha rup hota he. iskaa naam he apwaad vidhi . ye donon ek hi paddati ke do ansh hai.
 kisi agyaat tatv ke mulya or rup jaanane ke liye is paddati kaa upyog aaj kaa bijganit bhi nishchit rup se karata he. udaahranarth yadi k2 + 2k = 24 is sameekaran main agyaat k kaa mulya jaananaa hoga, to prathmat: donon ore sankhya 1 jod dete hai jisase donon paksh purna varg kaa rup dhaaran kar lete hai or ant main aropit sankhya ko donon ore se nikaal dena padataa he, tab agyaat k kaa mulya 4 nikal aata he.
