 valmiki ramayana main 2400 shloke hai. valmiki ji ne gayatri mantra ke 24 bijaksharon ko in shlokon main panityapurna dhang se jad diya he. in 24 bijaksharon se armbh hone vaale shlokon ke samooh ko 'gayari ramayana' kehete hai. kaha jaataa he ki in 24 shlokon ke paath se vahi fal praapt hota he jo sampoorn ramayana ke paath se miltaa he.
