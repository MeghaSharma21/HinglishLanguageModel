 romani prem, romaaniat, yaa romani bhaavana kisi anya vyakti kii ore unmukh prem kii anandpurn bhaavnaatmak samvednaa he. isamen prem- vyapaar ke sabhi kaary shaamil hai. romani prem kii bhaavana ko yon aakarshan se bhi joda gaya he, parantu romani bhavanaayen sharirik sambhog kii kalpana ke bina bhi ho sakati hai.
