 damoh bhartiya rajya madhya pradesh kaa ek jila he.jile kaa mukhyalay damoh he.iskaa kshetrafal 7306 varg ki.me. env jansankhya he.damoh 80% sakshar he.
 damoh kaa mahatv 14veen shatabdi se rahaa he jab khilji ne kshetreey prashasnic centre ko chanderi ke batiyagarh se damova sthannrit kiya. damoh maratha garvanar kii seat thi. british kaal ke baad yah madhya praant kaa bhaag ho gaya. tathaa 1867 main ise musipality bana diya gaya tha. yahaan aayal mil, handloom tathaa dhaatu ke bartan, bidi-cigarette, cement tathaa sone-chaandi ke jevar aadi banaae jate hai. damoh ke aasapaas badi sankhya main paan ke baag bhi he. yahaan se iskaa niryaat bhi hota he. yahaan par nagpanchami par vaarshik mela yojit hota he tathaa janvari main jatashankar meley kaa bhi aayojan hota he. yahaan par pashu bazaar lagata he tathaa kai chote udyog bhi he. saath hi hathkargha or mitti ke bartan bhi banaae jate hai. damoh jila 2816 varg kimi kshetra main uttar se dakshin tak failaa he. saath hi chaaron ore pahadiyon tathaa jangal se ghira hua he. jile kii adhikansh bhoomi upjau he. jile main mukhyatah do nadiyaan sunar or bairmaa bahati hai. jile main mukhyatah nadiyon se hi sinchai kii jaati hai.
 damoh se 30 kilometre dur hataa nagar main pracheen chandi mandir he yahan par adyasakti maan durga chandi rup main virajman he or yahan bhakton kii aasthaa kaa centre heiskaa itihaas gyaat nahin he ki kab or kiske dwaara nirmaan karaayaa gaya par isaki sthapana aaryo dwaara devi upasna kii aage badhane k liye kii gayi thijavera ke nohata main puraatan mandir khajaraho jile main milte he inkaa nirmaan chandel raajaaon ke dwaara hua abhi kuch samay se yahan par bhi nohata mahotsav manaaya jane laga he jisase paryaton ko badhaava diya jaye.damoh main boonda bahu mandir he jo kaaphi purana haiske alaava batiyagarh kaa kila, hataa kaa kila, damoh kaa kila, nohata kaa mandir he. kundalpur or nisai ji sookhaa kaa mandir jain bandhuon hetu prasiddh he.yahan par kai saare mandir he, jo kii puraane or poojyaneey he.yaha damoh se 17 kilo meter ki duri par badakpur main shiv ji kaa pracheen mandir he
