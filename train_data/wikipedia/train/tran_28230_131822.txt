 nakul mahaan hindu kavy mahabharat main paanch pandavo main se ek tha. nakul or sahadev, donon mata maadri ke asmaan judwa putr the, jinka janm divine chikitsakon aswin ke vardaan swaroop hua tha, jo swayan bhi samaan judwa bandhu the.
 nakul, kaa arth he, param vidwata. mahabharat main nakul kaa chithran ek bahut hi rupavaan, prem yukt or bahut sundar vyakti ke rup main kii gai he. apni sundarataa ke kaaran nakul kii tulana kaam or prem ke devtaa, "kaamdev" se kii gai he. pandavo ke antim or terahaven varsh ke agyatavaas main nakul ne apane rup ko kauravon se chhipaane ke liye apane sharir par dhul lip kar chipaaya. shrikrishna dwaara nirkasur kaa vadh karne ke pashchaat nakul dwaara ghud prajnan or prashikshan main nipun hone kaa mahabharat main abhilekhaakaran he. vah ek yogya pashu shalya chikitsak tha, jise ghud chikitsa main maharath praapt tha. agyatavaas ke samay main nakul bhesh badal kar or arishthanemi naam ke chhadmanaam se maharaj viraat kii rajdhani uppalavya kii ghudshala main shahi ghodon kii dekhbhaal karne vaale sevak ke rup main rahaa tha. vah apni talwarbazi or ghudsawaari kii kala ke liye bhi vikhyath tha. anushruti ke anusaar, vah barish main bina jal ko chhue ghudsawaari kar saktaa tha.
 nakul kaa vivaah draupadi ke atirikt jaraasandh kii putree se bhi hua tha.
 nakul naam kaa arth hota he jo prem se paripurna ho or is naam kii nau purush visheshtaayen hai:budhimatta, sakendrit, parishramee, rupavaan, swasthya, aakarshakta, safalata, aadar or shart rahit prem.
