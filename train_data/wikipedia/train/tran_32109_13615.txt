 iski yaa 'soochna antaravinimay ke liye bhartiya lipi samhita' ) bharat main prachlipt vibhinn lipiyon ko computer par nirupit ke liye nirmit ek maanak inkoding he. iske dwaar samarthit lipiyaan hai - asamiya, bangla, devanagari, gujarati, gurumukhi, kannada, malayalam, oriya, tamil tathaa telugu. 
 sattar ke dashak se hi electronic vibhaag or rajbhasha vibhaag kii vibhinn samitiyaan vibhinn kodon evam kunjipatalon kaa vikaas kar rahi hai jo bhartiya lipiyon kii samaan dhvanyatmak sanrachana ke kaaran unki aavashyaktaaon ko puraa kar saken. ISCII yah soochna ke aadan-pradaan ke liye 8 bit code vaala akshar samooh he. yah un sabhi computer evam sanchaar media main upyog ke liye banaaya gaya he jo 7 athwa 8 bit aksharon kaa upyog karte hai. ucchtar 128 akshar pracheen brahmi lipi par aadhaarit 10 bhartiya lipiyon kii aavashyaktaaon ko puraa karte hai.
 samaan inscript kunjeepatal se sabhi 10 bhartiya lipiyon kaa tankan sambhav he. yah kisi bhi vartmaan angreji kunjipatal par kaam karata he. angreji or hindi kaa baari-baari se upyog capslock kunji ke jariye kiya ja saktaa he. inscreept kunjipatal main swar evam vyanjan varnon kii taarkik evam sahaj vyavastha kii gai he. yah varnon ke dhvanyatmak gunon evam upyog kii saapekshik baarambarataa par aadhaarit he. isse kunjipatal ko siikhnaa na keval bahut aasaan ho jaataa he balki koi vyakti sabhi bhartiya lipiyon main tip bhi kar saktaa he.
 unicode 16-bit inkoding kaa prayog karata he jo 65000 se adhik kairactaron ke liye code point uplabdh karaata he. unicode standard prayek kairactor ko vilakshan sankhyatmak maan or naam uplabdh karaate he. unicode vishwa kii sab lekhnee-baddh bhashaaon ke liye prayukt sabhi kairactaron ko incode karne kii kshamta uplabdh karaata he.
 ISCII 8-bit code kaa prayog karata he jo 7-bit ASCII code kaa ek vistaar he. yah 10 bhartiya lipiyon ke liye apekshit mul varnmaala rakhata he jo brahmi lipi se utpann hui hai.
