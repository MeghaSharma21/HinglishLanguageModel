 teesri manzil san 1966 main pradarshit va vijay anand dwaara nirdeshit sangitamay-romaanchak hindi film he. jisamen shammi kapur, aashaa paarekh, premnath tathaa prem chopra sareekhe abhinayta mukhya bhoomika main he.
 ek ladki roopa kaa teesri manzil se girakar khun ho jaataa he. jaanch-padtaal main police kaa shaq uske mitra anil par jaataa he lekin anil ko roopa ke mangetar ramesh par andeh hota he kyonki uskaa roopa se jhagada hua hota he. police ko suraag main ek keemtee kot kaa batan miltaa he jo sambhavat: kaatil kaa he.
 film box offis par badi hit sabit hui. iske sangeet kii bhi bahut prashansa hui jisaki jhalak aaj bhi kai mujeec elbum main dikhaai padati he.iske saath hi sangeetkaar aar. di. burman sangeet kii duniya kaa ek bada naam ban gaye.
