 gona wema buddha reddy - vemna dakshin andhra kshetra ke ek telegu kavi or vichaarak the jinako vedon or yoga gyanopadesh ke liye janaa jaataa he. inkaa asli naam gona wema buddha reddy tha. inako yogi vemna ke naam se pukaara jaataa he. inkaa janm andhra pradesh ke jila nellore main hua tha.
 telegu saahiti itihaas main inaki padya rachanaayen "vemna shatkaalu" ke naam se prasidd hai. si.pi.braun ne inaki kavitaaon ko 19 veen shatabdi main sangrah or prakashit kiya. 
 vemna ke jivan kaal ko lekar logon kaa alag alag khyaal he. si.pi. brown ne vemna kii jeevani par parishodhan kiya, vemna kii kavitaaon ke anusaar is kaa maananaa he ki vemna kaa janm 1652 main hawa. kai anya ke anusaar inkaa janm 15veen 16veen or 17veen shatabdi he. 
 vemna ek kisaan ke parivaar se the. si.pi.brown ke anusaar vemna "jangam" kutumb se the jo lingayat kii ek shakha he.
 vemna ke pita gaddam vem the, vemna inaki teesri santan thi.
