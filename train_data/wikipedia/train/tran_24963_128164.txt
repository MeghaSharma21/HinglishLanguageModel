 samangan afghanistan kaa ek praant he jo us desh ke uttar-purv main sthit he. is praant kaa kshetrafal 11,262 varg kimi he or isaki aabaadi san 2009 main lagbhag 3.5 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. is praant kii rajdhani samangan shahar he. yahan ke teen-chauthai log farsi bolne vaale tajik log hai or lagbhag ek-chauthai log uzbek hai. yahan bahut se puraatan sthal hai, jaise ki takht-e-rustam, lekin taliban or anya faujon ke bich main ladaai ke kaaran itihaaskaaron ke liye yahan anusandhan karnaa filhal asambhav he.
