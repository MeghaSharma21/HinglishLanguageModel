 imtihaan 1994 kii harry baveja dwaara nirdeshit hindi bhashaa kii ection premakahani film he. mukhya bhoomika main sani deol, saf ali khan or ravina tandon hai. iskaa sangeet lokapriy rahaa tha. 
 viki ek lokapriy gayak hai, jo priti se pyaar karata he. vah uske pita se shaadi karne kii anumati maangata he, jo turant use manjuuri de dete hai. priti shaadi karne ke liye anicchhuk he lekin apane pita ke kehane par maan jaati he. jald hi, uske pita biimaar padate hai, or vah vaada karti he ki vah viki ko apane pichley jivan ke bare main nahin bataaegi.
 kuch saal pehle, priti apane pita kii ichchhaaon ke khilaaf raja se pyaar or shaadi kar chuki thim. haalanki donon ek saath bahut khush the, phir jab ve durghatana main gaayab ho jaataa he to ve alag ho gaye. priti apane pita ke ghar laut ai, jahaan usne dekha ki vah garbhvatee thi. usne beti ko janm diya; haalanki, uske pita ne bachche ko anathalay main bheja.
 viki se shaadi karne ke baad, priti uske saath kaaphi khush hai. vah anathalay main apni beti, pinki se milti he, or kanoonee rup se use apnaane kaa faisla karti he. viki, jo pinki se priti ke rishte ke bare main nahin jaanata he, god lene kaa virodh karata he kyonki vah kisi din khud apane bachche karnaa chahta he.
 sabhi geet faiz anwar dwaara likhit; sara sangeet anu malik dwaara rachit.
