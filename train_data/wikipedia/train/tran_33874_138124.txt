 ramesh kuntal megh hindi sahitya ke varisht sahityakar va samalochak hai. inhone pragativaadee aalochana kaa kshetra vistaar kiya. megh ne aalochana main antaranushasan ko vishesh mahatv diya he. aadhuniktaa, saundaryashastra or samajashaastra unke adhyayan ke pramukh kshetra hai.
 apane aalochanatmak karm kaa aarambh jaishankar prasad or unki kamayani se karne vaale megh ne madhyakaalin sahitya ke saundaryashastreey vishleshan main vishesh yogadan diya. saundarya drushti or saamaajik bhoomika unki aalochana ke beej shabd hai jinke madhyam se ve rachana kaa samgr aankalan karte hai.
 ramesh kuntal megh ko 2017 main unki saahitiyik samalochna vishwa myth sarit sagar ke liye hindi kaa sahitya academy puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya.
