 pehle asian sheetakaaleen khel 1 march se 8 march, 1986 ke bich saporo, japan main ayijit kiye gaye the. is nagar ne 1972 sheetakaaleen olympic khelon ke anubhav or khel-dhaanche ke saath in khelon kii mezbaani kii thi. in khelon main asia kii kul 7 rashtriya olympic samitiyon ne bhaag liya tha jisame 35 khel spardhaayen thi. in saat desho ke 430 khilaadiyon ne pratham asian sheetakaaleen khelon main bhaag liya tha.
