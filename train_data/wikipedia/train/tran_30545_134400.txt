 england or ireland main srilankai cricket team 2016srilanka cricket team kii 19 may se 5 july 2016 tak england daura nirdhaarit kiya he. isame teen maichon kii test shrinkhalaa, paanch maichon kii ek divasiy antarrashtriya kii shrinkhalaa or ek twanti -20 antarrashtriya england cricket team ke khilaaf khelenge. do teen din ke test match asex or lestershire ke khilaaf test maichon kii shrinkhalaa se pehle khelenge, or do vanade ireland ke khilaaf vanade shrinkhalaa ke pehle khelenge.
 
 kusal perera kii jagah dhammika prasad srilanka test team main. kris vokes ghayal ben stokes, jo baad shrinkhalaa se baahar ho gaye ke liye ek sthanapann ke rup main duusare test ke liye england team main shaamil kiya gaya. dushmantha chamira uski peeth ke nichle hisse main ek tanaav fracture kaa saamana karnaa padaa or daure se baahar ho gaye. chaminda bandara chamira sthanapann ke rup main namit kiya gaya tha. vanade maichon main ireland ke baad, shaminda iranga dublin main aspataal main bhartee karaayaa gaya tha uske dil par pareekshanon se guzarana. haalanki, ek hi din unhone antarrashtriya cricket parishad dwaara antarrashtriya maichon main gendbaaji se duusare test ke dauraan soochna ek avaidh karrwai ke kaaran nilambit kar diya gaya tha. tisare vanade ke liye pehle, lahiru thirimane ek peeth ke nichle hisse main khinchaav ke kaaran shrinkhalaa ke baaki ke shashan se baahar kiya gaya tha.
