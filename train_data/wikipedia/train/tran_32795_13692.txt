 domat ek prakaar kii mitti he jo fasalon ke liye atyant urvar ) hoti he. isamen lagbhag 40% silt, 20% chikani mitti tathaa shesh 40% baaluu hota he. pani tathaa vaayu ke pravesh hetu arthath adhik chhidril hone ke kaaran fasalon kii urvara shakti adhik hoti he. aisi mitti apane kul bhaar kaa 50% pani rokane kii kshamta rakhti he. is mitti main poshak padaarthon kii maatra bhi adhik hoti he.
 domat mitti main silt, baaluu or chikani mitti ke ansh alag-alag hone se bhinn-bhinn prakaar ke domat banate hai jaise balui domat, silti domat, chikani domat, balui chikani domat aadi. chikani mitti kii apeksha domat mitti main adhik poshak padaarth, adhik nami, adhik humas hota he. domat mitti kii jutai chikani mitti kii apeksha aasaan hoti he. isamen silti mitti kii apeksha hawa or pani ko chaanane tathaa jal-nikaas kii behatar kshamta hoti he.
 domat mitti bagwani tathaa krishi kaaryon ke liye uttam maani jaati he. yadi kisi balui yaa chikani mitti main bhi adhik maatra main jaivik padaarth mili hon to uske gun bhi domat jaise hi honge.
