 phu fiters ek america alternetive rock band he jo 1994 main sietal main bana tha. isaki sthapana nirvaana ke drumer dev grohl ke akele vyakti ke rup main court cobain kii mrityu ke baad kii thi. samooh ko apana naam kai ufo or vicitra drishyon se mila jinhen hawaii jahaaz ke pileton ne dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke dauraan dekhne kii pushti kii thi. is dharna ko phu fiters naam diya gaya. 1995 main apane pehle album phu fiters ko release karne se pehle grohl ne baas vadak net mendel or drumer wiliam goldsmith ko shaamil kiya jo pehle sani de real estate ke sadsy the, saath hi nirvaana ke pet smier ko guitar vadak ke rup main jod liya gaya. band ne shuruyaat portland, oregon se kii. goldsmith ne samooh ke duusare album da color and da shape ke baad sadsyta chod di jab grohl ne drum ke adhikansh ansh punah record kiye. smier ne bhi jald hi palaayan kiya. in donon ke sthaan par tailor hakins or franz stahl ko laayaa gaya parantu staahal ko samooh ke tisare album der iz nathing left tu luz kii recording se purv hi nikaal diya gaya.
 kuch kaal tak band tikdi ke rup main kaary karata rahaa or antat kris shiflate ne band main mukhya guitar vadak kii jagah sanbhali. band ne apana chauthaa album van by van main release kiya. iske peeche-peeche hi samooh ne in yur honor kaa do-discs sanskaran release kiya. phu fiters ne apana chatha album ekos, silence, peshans and gres 2007 main release kiya. apane kariyar ke dauraan band ke chaar albamon ko 11 grami puraskaar mil chuke he jiname chaar sarvshereshth rock album kaa sammaan shaamil he. band kaa saatvaan album vesting lite 2011 main release kiya gaya.
