 mairi tuder, 1514 main 3 maheene ke liye france kii rani thi. vah england ke raja henry ashtum kii bahan thi. mairi france ke lui barahawein kii, jo unase 30 varsh se adhik varisht the, teesri patni ban gai. unki mrityu ke baad unhonen charles brandan, safolk ke pehle duk se shaadi kar lee. mairi kii duusari shaadi se chaar bachche hue.
