 majaapahit samrajya, indonesia kaa samraat. inhen 'raden vijay', 'nararya sangramvijay', aadi naamon se bhi janaa jaataa he. inhone 1293 se 1309 tak shashan kiya. ye majaapahit samrajya ke sansthaapak or pratham raja the. is samrajya kii sthapana kaa itihaas kai abhilekhon main miltaa he jisamen paraaraton or nagarkirtagam sammilit hai. uske shasankaal main java par mongolon kaa akraman hua tha jisamen yuvan vansh ke shaasha kubalai khan kii sena or jalsena ke saath sangharsh hua or antatah kitirajas kii vijay hui or mongol samrajya kaa vibhaajan hua.
