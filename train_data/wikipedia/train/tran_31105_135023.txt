 bharat kaa mahaan mughal badshaah akbar swayan nirakshar hote hue bhi itihasagyon, chitrakaaron, sulekh likhane vaale kelligraphist, vichaarkon, dharm-guruon, kalakaaron evam buddhijivio kaa vishesh premi tha. kaha jaataa he akbar ke darbar main aise nau gunwan darbari the jinhen kalantar main akbar ke navratna ke naam se bhi janaa gaya. 
 itihasajna abul fazal ne akbar ke shashan kaal kii pramukh ghatnaon ko kalambaddh kiya tha. unhone akbarnama or ine-e-akabari kii rachana kii thi. inkaa janm agra main hua.
 faiji abul fazal ke bhai the jo farsi main kavita likhte the or jinhen akbar ne apane bete ke ganit shikshak ke pad par niyukt kiya tha. shaikh abu al-faiz, prachlit naam:faizi madhyakaalin bharat kaa farsi kavi tha.
 kavi tansen akbar ke darbar ke ek vilakshan sangitajna the. sangeet samraat tansen kii nagari gwalior ke liye kahawat prasiddh he ki yahan bachche rote hai, to sur main or pathar ludhakte hai to taal main.
 param budhimaan raja birbal akbar ke yuddh-salaahkaar the. hasya-parihaas main inke akbar ke sang kaalpanik kisse aaj bhi kahe jate hai. birbal ek kavi bhi the. brahm ke naam unhone ek kavi ke rup main kavitaayen likhi hai jo bharatpur sangrahalay rajasthan main surakshit hai.
 raja todermal akbar ke rajasav or vittamantri the. inhone bhoomi-paimaaish ke liye vishwa kii pratham maapan-pranali taiyaar kii thi. ye bharatpur alvar ke paas harsaanaa gram ke the akbar ke rajya ki pemis inhone kii thi. uttar pradesh rajya ke ekmaatr rajasav prashikshan santhaan kaa naam inke naam par raja todermal bhulekh prashikshan santhaan rakhaa gaya he jahaniees, ipies, pisies, peepies ke alaava rajasav karmiyon ko bhulekh sambandhi prashikshan diya jaataa he.
 akbar kii sena ke pradhan senapati maharaja maansingh ke amer kachhawa rajput raja the jinaki buaa/ bahan jodhabai}.
 rahim ek pratishthit kavi the or akbar ke sangrakshak bairam khan ke bete the.
 fakir ajion-din akbar ke salaahkaar the.
 mullah do paaza akbar ke salaahkaar the.
