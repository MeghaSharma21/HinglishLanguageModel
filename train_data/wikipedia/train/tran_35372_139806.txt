 someshwar tritiy pashchimi chalukya shaasha the. ve vikramaditya chaturth evam rani chandaladevi ke putr the. ve sahitya main abhiruchi ke liye prasiddh hai. unka mansollas naamak sanskrit granth bahut prasiddh he. 
 someshwar tritiy ne hoyasla shaasha vishnuvardhan ke akraman ko vifal kar diya. unhone 'tribhuvanmall', 'bhuulokmall' or 'sarvajnabhup' aadi padaviyaan grahan kii thi.
