 jeev shabd jeevigyaan main sabhi jivan-sannihit praaniyon ke liye prayukt hota he, jaise: kasheruki jantu, keet, padap athwa jeevanu.ek jeev main ek yaa ek se adhik koshikaaye hote hai. jinamein ek koshika paaya jaataa he use ek koshikeey jeev he; ek se adhik koshika hone par us jeev ko bahukoshikeey jeev kaha jaataa he. manushya kaa sharir vishesh utakon or angon main baanta hota he, jisamen koshikaon ke kai arabon kii rachana main bahukoshikeey jeev hote hai.
 maatr ati sookshm jeev hi ek koshikeey hote he.
 bade jaanavar, manushya aadi isi shrenee main aate he.
 
 jain darshan main jeev shabd mukhyatah aatma ke liye bhi prayog kiya jaataa he. acharya umaswami ne tirthankar mahavir ke mantavyon ko pehali sadi main sutrit karte hue tattwarth sutara main likhaa he: "parasparopgraho jeevanam". is sutara kaa arth he, 'jeevon ke paraspar main upkaar he'.
