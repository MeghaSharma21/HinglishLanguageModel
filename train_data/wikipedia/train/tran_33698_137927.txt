 vajrachchhedikapragyaparmitasutra mahayan buddha dharm kaa sutaragranth he. 
 is granth kii ek chini bhashaa kii prati beesvin shatee main praapt hui jo 11 may 868 main chhapi he. yah prati abhi british sangrahalay main rakhi hui he or 'vishwa kii sabase pracheen kintu purnatah bachi hua granth' he.
