 jajmau kanpur ke nikat ek up-mahanagar he. yah ganga nadi tat par sthit he. jajmau ek audyogic upnagar he. yah sabase purana kshetra main based jagah ke rup main mana jaataa he. mukhya udyog chamada udyog he. yah sabase bada chamada utpaadak nagar he. is kaaran ise kanpur kaa chamada nagar kaha jaataa he. bhartiya puraatatva surveykshan vibhaag ke dwaara kii gayi khudaai main 1200-1300 shatabdi ke bartan, kalakritiya bhi mili he. inhen vartmaan main kanpur sangrahalay main rakhaa gaya he.

 jajmau kai falon, jaise santara, aam, amaruud or kelaa, ke paidaavaar kaa centre he. yahan kai aawaara jaanavar shahar main hai. kanpur praani udyaan yahan se kareeb 20 kimi kii dur nawabganj main sthit he or yah pashu pakshiyon ko dekhne ke liye ek acha sthaan he.  
 jajmau ganga ke kinare basaa sabase shaharon main ek he. prasiddh jajmau tila bhi yahi he. jinnathan kii masjid taj mahal apni jaisi vaastukala or safed rang ke liye prasiddh he. firoz shah tughlaq 1358 main makhdoom shah naamak prasiddh sufi sant kaa makbara banavaya. kulich khan dwaara 1679 main banaaya masjid bhi yahan he.

 jajmau kii jalwaayu garmiyon main garm or sardiyon main garm he. jajmau disambar or janvari main baari koharaa padataa he, jiske parinamswaroop baari yatayaat or yatra main deree hoti he. barish july or sitambar ke bich lagbhag niyamit rup se hoti he. kuch varsha march - april ke mausam ke dauraan bhi darj kii gai he.
