 canada kii libral party kaa netritv chunaav 2013 main tab ladaa gaya jab purv adhyaksh maaikel ignatiff ne 3 may 2011 ko party ke canada ke sangheey chunaavon main kharaab pradarshan ke baad istife kii peshkash kar di. 25 may, 2011 ko bob re ko antarim adhyaksh chunaa gaya. april 14, 2013 ko otawa main party ne justin trudo ko apana nayaa netaa va adhyaksh chunaa. pehle yah chunaav 28-29 aktubar 2011 ko hi hone vaala tha lekin libral dal ke tamaam saansad va netaa haal hi main hue chunaavon ke baad itane jaldi koi chunaav nahi chaahate the or kisi antarim netritv ke nirdesan main dal ko haar se ubaarana chaahate the. iske baad ek aantarik samvidhan sansodhan karke chunaavon ko 1 march se 30 june 2013 tak ke liye taal diya gaya.
 *har sangheey chunavi jile ke 100 ank hote hai, jinka nirdhaaran jile main nirvaachakon dwaara kiya jaataa he.
