 bhartiya lok dal bharat main sattar ke dashak main bana ek rajnatik dal tha. yah 1974 main bana tha. jiske sansthaapak choudhary charan singhthe. is dal kaa vilay janata party main ho gaya tha 
 1980 main jab janata party tuuti to usamen se kai anya rajnatik dal bane jinamein bhartiya janata party pramukh he. parantu choudhary charan singh ji ne 1984 main alag dal lokdal kaa gathan kiya. jo 1974 main bane bhartiya lok dal kaa nayaa janm mana gaya.
