 janmejay saibabu ek chau kalakaar or guru hai. vah mayurbhanj chau kala main nipun he or pichley 40 saal se iskaa shikshadaan karte aa rahe hai. is kala main utkarsh ke liye inhe 2017 saal ke sangeet naatak academy puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya he. 
 janmejay saibabu ke naanaa radhamohan saibabu mayurbhanj raja ke darbar main chau nrity pariveshan karte the. tabhi se yah inkaa kaulik kala ban gaya he. inke chachaa ananthacharan saibabu bhi sangeet naatak academy puraskarprapt the . 1972 main janmejay centre sarkaar ke samskruti bibhaag main naukari kii sruvaat kiye the. 3 saal naukari karne ke baad inhone naukari chhod dilli main "gurukul chau dance sangam" naam se apana skool kholaa. is skool main chau kala ke shiksha ke alaava is nrity main or anusandhan bhi kiya jaataa hai. mayurbhanj jile ke baripada main inkaa "chhouni" naam se ek skool bhi he. janmejay ke donon bete bhi chau nrity karte or sikhaate bhi hai.
