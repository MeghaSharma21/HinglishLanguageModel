 rugveda main ullikhit arjuney kuts. inkaa naam anek baar aayaa he. inhone sushn daanav ko parajeet karne main indra kii sahaayta kii thi. inaki veerta ke ullekh bhi praapt hote hai. inhone tugn, vetasu aadi ko parajeet kiya tha. swayan inke paraajay kaa bhi varnan praapt hota he . indra ne bhi inhen atithigva tathaa aayu ke saath parajeet kiya tha. brahman granthon main bhi inkaa ullekh indra ke saath kiya gaya he .
 panchavinsh brahman main ullikhit kuts aurav. inhone apane purohit upagu soushravas kaa vadh kar diya tha. sambhavat: inhen ke putr kautsagin kaa ullekh shatapath brahman 10-6-5-9 tathaa brihadaaranyak upanishad 6-4-5 main hua he. kadachit inhen ko janamejay ke nagayagya kaa udgaata banaaya gaya tha ; or inhen ko rajarshi bhageerath ne apni kanya hansi kaa daan kiya tha jisase ve akshaylok ko praapt hue .
