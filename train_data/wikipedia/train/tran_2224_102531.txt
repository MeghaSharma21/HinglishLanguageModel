 maharana pratap antarraji bas adda athwa ai es bi tii, kashmiri get jo ki 'kashmiri get bas adda' yaa 'bas adda' ityadi naamon se lokapriy he, dilli main sthit bharat ke sabase puraane or sabase bade antarraji bas addon main se ek he. yahan se dilli or 7 anya rajyon, haryana, jammu-kashmir, punjab, himachal pradesh, uttar pradesh, rajasthan or uttarakhand ke bich bas seva chal rahi he. yah lagbhag 13 acre kshetra main failaa hua he tathaa yahaan se ek din main 1800 se adhik basen chalati he.
 kashmiri get bas adda 1976 main kholaa gaya tha. 1993 tak yah dilli kaa ekmaatr bas adda tha, jiske uparaant ise parivahan vibhaag ko hastantarit kar diya gaya. atyadhik bheed tathaa avyavashtha ke kaaran baad main do nae bas adde sarai kaale khan or anand vihaar main banaae gaye. 2011-2012 main 70 karod rupye kii laagat se parivahan vibhaag dwaara bas adde ke mukhya bhawan kaa punarnirmaan kiya gaya or uske baad 2013 main dilli kii tatkaaleen mukhyamantri shila dikshit dwaara iskaa udghaatan kiya gaya.
 bas adde main 45 prasthaan bas khand, aath niskriy bas khand or 13 aagaman bas khand hai, jahaan se anya rajyon yaa dilli ke duusare bas addon liye basen chalati hai. rajdhani ke anya bhaagon ke liye sthaneey bason ko mini bas stand se sanchalit kiya ja rahaa he.
 kashmiri get kshetra main sthit yah bas adda dilli ke ring maarg par aane vaala ek bas stop bhi he. yah ek chauraahaa he. ise hardev sahay maarg katata he.
 yah bas adda kashmiri get naam se dilli metro rail kii yelo line shakha kaa ek stesan bhi he.yah dilli metro rail kii red line red line shakha kaa ek stesan bhi he.
