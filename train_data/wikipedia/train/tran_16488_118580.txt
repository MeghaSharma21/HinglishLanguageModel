 kalka mail rail durghatana is saal ke sabase badi rail durghatnaon main se ek he. yah durghatana 10 july 2011 ko uttarapradesh main kanpur ke nikat fatehpur ke malwa stesan ke paas hui. is durghatana main kam se kam assi logon kii maut ho gai he. jabaki saadhe teen sau log ghayal ho gaye. is rail durghatana main prashasnic laaparvaahee or prashasnic udaseenta ke kai vaakye saamne aaye. pradhanmantri ne rail mantralay kii ore se mritkon ke parijanon ke liye paanch-paanch lakh rupee ke muaavje kii ghoshnaa kar di. magar, rail rajyamantri mukul raay apni jimmedaari se bachate najar aae.
