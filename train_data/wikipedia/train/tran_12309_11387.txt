 gluon kan bhautikee main ek moolbhoot kan he. iskaa aavesh shunya hota he atah yah vidyut chumbakeey anyonya kriyaaon main bhaag nahin letaa he. iskaa dravyaman shunya hota he atah yah gurutveey anyonya kriyaaon main bhi bhaag nahin letaa. is kan kaa prachakran 1 hota he. yah kan ek gej boson he. yah kan prabal anyonya kriya kaa bal vaahak kan he.
 gluon color aaweshit hota he is kaaran yah aath bhin aswadon main paaya jaataa he. jinhen ganitiya rup main nimna samikarnon se praapt kiya jaataa he:
 keval nimna sambhavit avasthaa ke gluon praapt nahin kiye ja sakate: 
 chunki color aavesh sahit ek kan mukt avasthaa main praapt nahin kiya ja saktaa atah isaki pahunch keval fermi koti kii hoti he.
