 mishrit jwalamukhi, ek lanbaa, shankvakar jwalamukhi hota he, jiskaa nirmaan jam kar thos hue lava, tephra, kusran or jwalamukheey raakh kii kai paraton dwaara hota he. mishrit jwalamukhi ko aisa isliye kaha jaataa he kyonki, inaki rachana jwalamukheey udgaar ke samay nikle mishrit padaarthon ke vibhinn staron par ghaneebhoot hone ke falswaroop hoti he. dhaal jwalamukhi ke vipareet, tikhi dhalaan or samay samay par hone vaale visphotak udgaar inaki visheshtayen he. in jwalamukhiyon se niklaa lava, dhaal jwalamukhi se nikle lave kii tulana main adhik shyaan hota he or aamtaur par udgaar ke pashchaat dur tak bahane se pehle hi thanda ho jaataa he. inke lave kii rachana karne vaala megma aksar felsik hota he jisamen, silica kii maatra ucch se lekar madhya str tak kii hoti he or kam shyanta vaale mafic megma kii maatra kam hoti he. felsik lava kaa vyapak pravaah asaamaanya he, lekin phir bhi ise 15 kimi tak behate dekha gaya he.
 dhaal jwalamukhi ke vipareet yah jwalamukhiyon kaa sabase saamaanya prakaar hai. do prasiddh mishrit jwalamukhiyon main se pehla kracatoa he, jisko uske 1883 ke udgaar ke liye janaa jaataa he jabaki, doosraa visuviyas he jiske udgaar ke kaaran 79 isvi main pompei or harkulenium naamak do italvi shahar puri tarah se nasht ho gayi the.
