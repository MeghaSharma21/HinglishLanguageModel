 bharatkund faijabad janapad main sthit ek tirthasthal he. ise nandigram ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. yahan rahakar bharat ne shriram kii charan paduka ko lekar chaudaa varsh tak ayodhya kaa kaarybhaar sambhala tha.
 bharatkund main tirthasthal par kai bhavy mandir tathaa devsthan he. yahan ek vishaal sarovar he jisamen camel ke aakarshak evam sugandhit pushp apni chata bikherte hai. yahan paryatakon kaa aagaman sampoorn varsh hota rahata he. kintu karthik tathaa caitra maas main yahan kii bhavyata anupam hoti he.
 caitra maas ke krishna paksh kii chaturdashi evam amavasya ko yahan prasiddh "pishachi kaa mela" lagata he.
