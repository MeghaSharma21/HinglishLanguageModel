 vaidic chand vedon ke mantron main prayukt kavitt maapon ko kaha jaataa he. shlokon main matraaon kii sankhya or unke laghu-guru uccharanon ke kramon ke samoohon ko chand kehete hai - vedon main kam se kam 15 prakaar ke chand prayukt hue hai. gayatri chand inamen sabase prasiddh he jiske naam hi ek mantra kaa naam gayatri mantra padaa he. iske alaave anushtup, trishtup ityadi chand hue hai. 
 pingal dwaara rachit chandashastra vaidic chhandon kii sabase maanya vivechna he.
