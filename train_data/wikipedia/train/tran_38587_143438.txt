 indonesia air asia udaan 8501 ek indonesian anusuchit antarrashtriya yatri udaan thi jo 28 disambar 2014 ko indonesia se singapore jate vakt durghatnagrast ho gai. vimaan main 155 yatri sahit 7 chaalak dalke sadsy the. malbe se vimaan or maanav avshesh durghatana ke do din baad paye gaye.
 vimaan airbas A320-216, kram sankhya 3648 tha, vimaanaka darta PK-AXC ke rupmen kiya gaya tha. is vimaan ne apni pehali udan 2008 sitambar 25 main kiya tha, tathaa nav rupmen ise air asiako 15 octubar 2008 main hastaantarit kiya gaya tha. yah vimaan lagbhag 23,000 udaan ghanta pura kiya tha jisame vimaanane 13,600 udaan kiya tha. is vimaanaka sabase pichhla marmat 2014 navambar 16 main kiya gaya tha. is vimaanamen do siefem anterrashtriya CFM56-5B6 engine tha tathaa ise 180 yatriyon ke liye tayar kiya gaya tha. 
 air asia vimaan ne jari apane vijyapti main aneko rashtrake 162 yatri tathaa chaalak dalke sadsy savaar the, jisame 144 vayask, 17 baccha sahit 1 shishu savaar the. cabin sadsy main chaar vimaan paricharika thi. iske atirikt, air asia vimaan main ek abhiyantaa bhi savaar the.
