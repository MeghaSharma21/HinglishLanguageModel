 katha, kahani, lokokti, gaan, bhajan, kavita yaa anya maukhik sadhano dwaara saanskrithik tatwon or parampara kaa sanchaar vaachik parampara kehalaati he. vaachit parampara ke dwaara vibhinn samaaj vaachik itihaas, vaachik sahitya, vaachik kanoon tathaa anya gyaan kaa ek peedi se duusari peedi main sanchaar karte aaye hai.
 anya samskritiyon kii bhaanti hi bhartiya samskruti main vaachik parampara ne mahatvapurn bhoomika adaa kii he. bhartiya kavy kisi na kisi rup main vaachik sanskaar se abhi tak suvsit he. kavita kii vaachikta abhi bhi he or vah lok main aadar bhi paati he. shruti kii raksha isi parampara se hui.
