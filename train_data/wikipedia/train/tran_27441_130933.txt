 banaphsha sardi-khaansi or kaph prakope ko dur karne vaali jadi-booti he, jo ayurvedic or unani chikitsa paddati main ek samaan rup se upyog main lee jaati he.
 banaphsha kii paidaavaar kashmir or himalay kii ghaatiyon main paanch se chah hajaar feet kii oonchaai par hoti he. iskaa aayaat iran se bhi kiya jaataa he or iran kii banaphsha bahut uttam jati kii hoti he.
 vibhinn bhashaaon main naam : sanskrit- nilpushp, hindi- banaphsha, marathi- banphshaha, gujarati- vanapasha, bangala- banosha, tamil- bayilettu, farsi- banaphsha, english - wild vilet, latin - viola odoreta
 gun : banaphsha kaa panchang laghu, snigdh, katu, tikt, ushn or vipaak main katu he. snigdh, madhur or sheethal hone se yah vaatapitta shaamak or thothahar he. yah jantunashak, pidaa shaamak or shoth dur karne vaala he.
 yah dravya iran se aata he or bharat main kashmir or pashchimi himalay ke samshitoshna pradesh main paanch-chah hajaar feet kii oonchaai par paidaa hota he. uttari bharat main banaphsha kii jagah isaki anya prajatiyon kaa prayog hota he, jinke naam hai viola syneria or viola serpens. iske pattey andakar, hridayakar, nukile, kanguredaar, phool bangnee rang ke or kvachit safed hote hai. isamen choti-choti dodi bhi lagati he. iske panchang or phool, donon aushadhiyon ke rup main kaam main aate hai.
 donon prajatiyon ke gun-laabh samaan hai or inamen banaphsha se adhik sugandh hoti he. isaki jad virechan gun vaali, fever shaamak, kaph nikaalane vaali, paushtik, pasinaa laane vaali, pyaas or jalan ko shant karne vaali hoti he.
