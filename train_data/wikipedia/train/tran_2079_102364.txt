 kapler antariksh yaan america antariksh anusandhan santhaan, nasa, kaa ek antariksh yaan he, jiskaa kaam suraj se alag kintu usee tarah anya taron ke ird-gird aise gair-saureey grahon ko dhundhna he jo prithvi se milte-julate hon. kapler ko 7 march 2009 main roket ke zariye antariksh main bheja gaya tha, jahaan yah ab prithvi kii parikrama kar rahaa he or anya taron par apni nazaren rakhe hue he. anumaan he ki yah kam-se-kam 3.5 varsh tak apana kaary karegaa.
 kepler mishan ke antargat kepler antariksh doordarshee prithvi jaise dikhne vaale grahon or unke soramandal kii sambhaavnaaon kaa pataa lagaataa he.kepler doordarshee ne ek chakree dwiadhari tarak vyavastha kii khoj kii he. iske antargat do taare ek duusare kaa chakkar laga rahe hai or ek upgrah in donon ke chaaron ore chakkar laga rahaa he. is saura mandal kaa naam kepler-16 bi or donon taron ke naam kramshah 34 bi or 35 bi he.
 kepler 22 bi ek saura mandal ke baahar kaa grah he. isaki khoj kepler antariksh doordarshee dwaara kii gayi he. yah grah awasaiya kshetra main paaya gaya he. kepler mishan ke antargat yah pehla grah he jo apane surya ke awasaiya kshetra main paaya gaya he. kepler 22 bi grah kii kuch pramukh visheshtaayen is prakaar hai-
