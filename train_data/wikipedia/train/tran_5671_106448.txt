 anuraag thakur bhartiya janata party se sambaddhta rakhane vaale ek bhartiya raajnneetijna hai. anuraag 2009 ke upachunaav evam 2014 ke aam chunaavon main himachal pradesh ke hamirpur se loksabhaa ke liye nirvaachit hue hai. vah himachal pradesh ke purv mukhyamantri prem kumaar dhumal ke bete hai.
 anuraag thakur himachal pradesh cricket association ke maujuuda adhyaksh hai. 25 saal kii umr main anuraag himachal pradesh cricket association ke sabase kam umr ke adhyaksh bane. vah bhaajapa adhyaksh nitin gadakari dwaara akhil bhartiya janata yuva morcha ke adhyaksh niyukt kiye gaye hai.
 anuraag 34ve or duusare kam umr ke adhyaksh he.inase pehle 1963 main fateh singh gayakwang ne 33 saal kii umr main pad sambhala tha.bijepi ke netaa or bisisiai adhyaksh anuraag thakur ab sena main leftinent ban gaye hai. sena pramukh general suhaag ne anuraag thakur ko khud teritorial army main shaamil kiya. anuraag sansad satr ke bich baqayada army kii training bhi lenge.
