 westeindies cricket team ne oktober 2014 main bharat kaa daura kiya. yah daura mul rup se teen test maichon, paanch vanade antarrashtriya match or ek twanti-20 international match se hone vaala tha. chakravaat hudahud ke kaaran tisare match ko radd karne ke baad ekadivasiy shrinkhalaa ko paanch maichon se chaar tak gata diya gaya tha.
 shrinkhalaa ke prarambh ke din, west indies ke khilaadiyon ne dhamkee di ki jab tak ve dablooisibi dwaara apni dey raashi kaa bhugtan nahin kar dete tab tak maidan par nahin aana chahie. bisisiai hastkshep ke baad, dablyooisibi ne apane khilaadiyon kaa bhugtan karne kaa vaada kiya or shrinkhalaa shuru hui. 17 oktober 2014 ko, toss time main, west indies ke ekadivasiy captain dwen braavo ne puri team unke saath laayi or ghoshnaa kii ki ve baaki ke daure ko chhod rahe hai kyonki unhen dablooisibi se apana vaada kiya gaya bhugtan nahin mila. baad main bisisiai ne pushti kii ki westeindies ke khilaadiyon, west indies cricket board or khilaadiyon ke bich chal rahe vetan ke chalate chauthaa ekadivasiy khel ke baad daure ke shesh judane ko radd kar diya gaya tha. bisisiai ke ek varisht adhikari ne vikaas kii pushti karte hue kaha ki westeindies team prabandhan ne apane nirnay ke board ko pehle din main suchit kiya tha. srilanka ne bharat main november main westeindies ke maichon ko chhodane ke baad november main paanch ekadivasiy match khilne ke liye principal par sahmati jataai he. iske baad bisisiai ne ghoshnaa kii ki vah agle notice tak west indies ke sabhi yojanabaddh daure ko nilambit kar degi, or bharat daure ke ant se samaapt hone ke liye dablyooisibi ke khilaaf kanoonee karrwai karegaa.
 june 2016 main, bharat ne pushti kii ki ve apane daure kaa west indies kaa sammaan karenge, jo ki agle maheene hone kaa aayojan kiya jayega.
