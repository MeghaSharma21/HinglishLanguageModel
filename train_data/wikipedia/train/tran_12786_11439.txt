 cryogenic ingine-20 bharat kaa doosraa cryogenic ingine he jise bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan ne jieselvi mark-3 ke upree charan ke liye viksit kiya gaya he.. yah ingine cryogenic upree charan pariyojna ke tahat viksit kiya gaya he. yah bharat kaa pehla gas jenerator chakra vaala ingine he. isaki pehali udaan jieselvi mark-3 ke saath 5 june 2017 ko honi he.
 isro ne 19 farvari 2016 ko ‘cryo sii-20’ kaa safal pareekshan kiya. yah pareekshan 640 second kii udaan avadhi ke liye kiya gaya.
 sii-20 gas-janarator chakra vaala pehla bhartiya cryogenic ingine he. ingine 200 kiloneuton ke thrust kaa utpaadan karata he, lekin 180 kiloneuton se 220 kiloneuton ke bich oparating thrust kii seemaa hoti he or un donon ke bich kisi bhi nishchit maanon par ise set kiya ja saktaa he. dahan kaksh taral hydrogen or taral oxigen ko 5.05 ingine mishran anupaat ke saath 6 megapascal par jalataa he. ingine kaa thrusta-vajan anupaat 34.7 he or vaikum main 444 second kaa ek vishisht aaveg he. isro ne 28 april, 2015 ko mahendragiri pareekshan kii suvidha main sii-20 kaa pareekshan kiya or ek safal lambii avadhi kii hot pareekshan hasil kiya. 16 july, 2015 ko, sii-20 kaa safaltaapurvak dhimi gati se isro proplson complex, mahendragiri main 800 second kii avadhi ke liye pareekshan kiya gaya. yah avadhi udaan main ingine jalane kii avadhi kii tulana main kareeb 25% adhik he. 19 farvari 2016 ko isro prapolshan complex, mahendragiri main 640 second kii avadhi ke liye sii-20 cryogenic ingine kaa hot pareekshan kiya gaya.
 5 june 2017 main aagaamee jieselvi udaan ke liye, ise 25 disambar 2016 ko 25 second ke liye phir se pareekshan kiya gaya tha.
