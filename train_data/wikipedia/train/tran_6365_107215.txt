 pieselvi-si37, bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan dwaara sanchalit pieselvi shrinkhalaa kaa ek upgrah pramochan vaahan he jisane 15 farvari 2017, buudhvaar ko kul 104 upgrahon ko prithvi kii kaksha main sthapit karke ek nayaa vishwakirtiman sthapit kiya. bharat ke 714 kilogram vajan vaale prithvi avlokan upgrah or 664 kilogram vajan ke 103 anya sahaayak upgrahon ke saath satish dhawan spes centre ke pratham launch pad se subah 9 bajkar 28 minute par ravaanaa hua. ye upgrah anek deshon ke hai. pieselvi kii yah kul 39veen or 37veen safal udaan thi.
 abhi tak kisi bhi desh ne itani sankhya main upgrahon kaa ek saath prakshepan nahin kiya he. pichhlaa record rus ke naam tha, rus ne naper rocket se 2014 main ek saath 37 satellite laanch kar varsh 2013 main minuteor 1 se 29 satellite ek saath chhodane ke america antariksh agency nasa ke record ko todaa tha. isro bhi 2016 main 20 upgrah ek saath launch kar chukaa he.
 14 farvari ko prakshepan ke liye ulti ginati shuru hi gayi, jahaan chaar rocket main pranodak bharne kaa kaam hua. 14 farvari 2017, mangalvaar ko subah 5.28 baje ise shuru kiya gaya. mishan radines rivyuu cameti tathaa laanch othrigeshan board ne 13 farvari kii raat hi 28 ghante kii ulti ginati kii manjuuri de di thi jo mangalvaar, 14 farvari subah 5.28 baje shuru ho gayi. 44.4 meter lambey pieselvi-si 37 kii bhaar dhone kii kshamta 320 ton he or thos strap-on motor ke upyog ke saath exel praaroop main yah isaki 16veen udaan thi. sahyatri upgrahon main 101 naino upgrah shaamil the jinamein israel, kazakstan, netherland, switsraland, sanyukt arab emirate ke tathaa 96 satellite america ke the. saath hi bharat ke do naino upgrahaianes-1e or ienes-1bi bhi shaamil the. in sabhi upgrahon kaa kul bhaar 1378 kilogram tha.
 rocket kii udaan solah minute main khatm hone kii ummid thi. upgrahon, uske baad alag hone lage, mote taur par yah das minute kii avadhi main shuru hua. cartosat 2 di pehle tainaat kiya gaya, iske peeche naino setllite kaksha main chodi gayin. neno satlite ko qopac dispensar main pack kiya gaya tha. puure oparation ke 28 minute tak khatm ho jane kii ummid thi. is udaan kaa doordarshan, utyub par sidhaa prasaaran kiya gaya.
 is mishan kii kul lagaat yues $15 million he, isro ke anusaar iskaa mishan kaa aadha bajet wo videshi upgrahon ke laanch se hi nikaal legaa.
