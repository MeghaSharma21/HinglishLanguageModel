 aaj railmargon or sadakon kaa mukhya jankshan hone ke kaaran yah nagar desh ke vibhinn shaharon se judaa hua he. yah vaayu maarg dwaara bangalore, kolkata, mumbai, dilli, hyderabad aadi desh ke vibhinn shaharon se judaa hua he. chennai desh ke anya bhaagon se rail dwaara bhi bhalee-bhaanti judaa hua he. yahaan se paanch mukhya rashtriya rajmarg shahar ko mumbai, kolkata, tiruchirapalli, tiruvallur, tindivanam or puducheri ko jodate hai. chennai mogussil bas terminus, chennai se sabhi antarraji bas sevaaon kaa adda he. yah asia kaa sabase bada bas-adda he. saat sarkari yatayaat nigam antar-shahari or antarraji bas sevaayen sanchalit karte hai. bahut si nijee bas sevaayen bhi chennai ko anya shaharon se sulabh karaati hai.
