 sansthapakawaad ek bauddhik drushtikon he, jisamen kisi rajya ke sansthaapakon ke liye sabal shradha hoti hai. yah shabd ek apmanjanak naam-visheshan ke rup main dekha jaataa hai, va yathachinhiton par aise vaishvik najariye rakhane kaa aarope lagaataa hai jo sansthaapan ko jadaasakti main badalane ke liye aitihaasik parishuddhi kaa tyaag karata hai.
 virodharthi sansthapakawaad-virodh unke prayukt kiya jaataa hai, jinhen "yakeenan lagata hai ki" rajya ke "udgamon main kuch to gambheerthapurvak galat tha".
