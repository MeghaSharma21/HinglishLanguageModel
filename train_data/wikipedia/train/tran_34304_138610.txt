  raajnneeti praveshdwar
 pakistan main sthaneey sarkarein, 2001 main jari adhyadesh he. "sthaneey sarkaar adhyadesh, 2001" ke tahat jila str par sthaneey sarkaar ke gathan hone kii sanvaidhanik vyakhya paayi jaati hai. pakistan main, zile, shashanpranali kaa teesraa str he, jinke upar prantiy sarkarein hai. sthaneey sarkaar adhyadesh se pehle, jilon ko sab-divijan kaha jaataa tha. pakistan main jila sarkaar ke pramukh zila nazim hota he jabaki prashasnic maamalon ke liye jimmedaar jila samanvay adhikari hota he, joki jila nazim ke adheen kaam karata he. is prakaar, jila nazim jile kaa sanvaidhanik or prashasnic pramukh bataaya jaataa he ki saarvajanik matdaan se chunaa jaataa he.zila samanvay adhikari, jila prashasan kaa pramukh hota he. unpar diye gaye adhikaar or kartavyon vistrut hote hai, jisase ve tie ke maamalon kii taiyaari, nigaraanee, ​​anupaalan or safal samaapan ke liye jimmedaar hota he. zila samanvay adhikari, jila nazim ko javabdeh hota he.jila nazim, jila prashasan or prashasnic parishad kaa pramukh hota he. jila nazim ke paas vyapak prashasnic or sanvaidhanik adhikaar hote hai jinke tahat vah jile main sarkaar kii rit or vikaas pariyojnaon kii taiyaari or purti sunishchit karata he. unheen adhikaron kaa upyog karke jila nazim jile main kanoon or vyavastha kii sthiti or maamalon ko safaltaapurvak chalate hai.
