 agyaat sthitiyon main tarkpurna akalan karne ko purvanumaan karnaa kehete hai. jaise do din baad kisi sthaan ke mausam ke bare main anumaan lagaana, ek varsh baad kisi desh kii aarthik sthiti ke bare main kahana aadi purvanumaan hai. purvanumaan ke saath anischitata or khatra kaa ghanisht sambandh he. aadhunik yug main purvanumaan ke anekanek upyog hai; jaise - graahak kii maang kii yojana bananaa.
