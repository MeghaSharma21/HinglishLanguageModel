 nandani surangen bhartiya rajya jammu or kashmir main udhampur jile main chaar surangon kii shrinkhalaa he. iskaa kaary 2015 main puraa hua. ye 1.4 kimi kii sanyukt lambaai main nandani vanyajeev abhyaarany ke neeche sthit hai.
 surangon ne jammu or udhampur ke bich yatra ke samay ko kaam kiya he tathaa duuri ko kam kar 6.8 kimi se 3.6 kilometre kar diya he.
