 pashchim europe kshetra main europe mahadweep ke pashchimi raashtra aate hai. lagbhag 1945-1991 tak chale sheet yuddh ke samay main prachlit ek anya paribhaasha ke anusaar pashchim europea sangh se sambhddha rashtron ke kshetra ko pashchim europe kehete the, jo ab europea sangh kaa hi bhaag he. pashchimi europea sangh 1948 ke samay asamyavaadee europea rashtron dwaara sangathit ek surakshatmak sanghathan tha or ye eastern block yaa warsaw sandhi ke viruddh sangathit hua tha. 
 pashimi europe ke raashtra atyadhik aay vaale viksit raashtra hai, jinamein adhikansh janatantric sarkaar dwaara shasit hai or mishrit arth-vyavastha ke sang nato evam europea sangh ke bhi sadsy hai.
 sanyukt raashtra sankhyikeey vibhaag ke anusaar pashchimi europe maatr 9 rashtron kaa samooh he.
