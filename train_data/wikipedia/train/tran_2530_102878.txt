 chaughadia panchang hindu vaidic calendar kaa ek rup yaa ang hota he. isamen pratidin ke liye din, nakshatra, tithi, yoga evam karan diye hote hai. inke liye prayek nagar yaa sthaan ke liye vahaan ke suryoday evam suryaast se sambandhit swatah sudhaar hota he. yadi kabhi kisi kaary ke liye koi muhoort nahin nikal rahaa ho evam kaary ko shighrata se aarambh karnaa ho yaa kisi yatra par aavashyak rup se janaa ho to uske liye chaughadiya muhoort dekhne kaa vidhaan he. jyotish ke anusaar chaujhadiya muhoort dekhakar vah kaary yaa yatra karnaa uttam hota he.
 ek tithi ke liye diwas or raatri ke aath-aath bhaag kaa ek chaughadiya nishchit he. is prakaar se 12 ghante kaa din or 12 ghante kii raat maane to prayek main 90 minute yani 1.30 ghante kaa ek chaughadiya hota he jo suryoday se prarambh hota he. is prakaar saaton vaaron ke chaughadie alag-alag hote hai. saamaanya rup se achche chaughadie shubh, chanchal, amrit or laabh ke maane jate hai tathaa bure chaughadiye udvaig, rog or kaal ke maane jate hai.
