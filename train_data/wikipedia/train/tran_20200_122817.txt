 
 Phalangeroidea
 possum australia, nayaa gini or sulavesi par paae jane vaale 70 dhaniprani jatiyon ke ek samooh kaa naam he. aadhunik yug main maanviya gatividhiyon se yah new zealand or china main bhi vistrut ho gaye hai. yah chote or madhyam aakaaron ke hote hai.
 possum chaar paanv par chalane vaale or adhiktar vrikshon par rahane vaale praani hai. inaki dumen lambi or moti hoti hai. inaki sabase choti jati 'nanha pigmi possum' keval 7 semi lambaa or 10 gram se bhi kam vazan vaala hota he. iske vipareet inaki sabase badi jati 'bhaaluu kuskus' 7 kilo se bhi zyaada bhaar rakh saktaa he. possum raat ko sakriya hote hai or shaakaahaari yaa sarvahari hote hai. kuch possum bahut hi vishesh khuraaq rakhte hai. udhaaharan ke liye 'pahari pigmi possum' keval keet khaataa he jabaki 'shahadi possum' shahad par guzaaraa karata he.
