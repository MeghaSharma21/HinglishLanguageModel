 digital -ankiy, kunjeey, sankhyikeey 
 digital electronics kii ek shakha he jisamen vidyut sanket ankiy hote hai. ankiy sanket bahut tarah ke ho sakate hai kintu binary digital sanket sabase adhik upyog main aate hai. shunya/ek, on/of, haan/nahin, lo/hai aadi binary sanketon ke kuch udhaaharan hai. jabase ekikrut paripathon kaa pradurbhaav hua he or ek choti si chip main lakhon karondon electronic yuktiyaan bhari jane lageen hai tab se digital electronic bahut mahatvapurn ho gayi he. aadhunik vyaktigat computer tathaa cell-phone, digital camera aadi digital electronic kii dein hai.
