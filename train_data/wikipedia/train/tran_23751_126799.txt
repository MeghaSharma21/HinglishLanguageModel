 riddhi hindu dharm main ek devi hai. bhagwaan ganesh kii patni he. unki do patniyaan thi, unke naam riddhi or siddhi hai. kisi bhi puja main ganesh ji ke saath riddhi siddhi or unke putr laabh shubh kaa bhi pujan kiya jaataa he.riddhi kaa arth safalataa,bhagya,shreshthata,acchhee kismat,dhan,alokik shakti se bhi he.
