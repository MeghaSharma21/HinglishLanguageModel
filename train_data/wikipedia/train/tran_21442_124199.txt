 sayed ali akhtar rizvi beesvi sadi ke ek parmukh vidwaan, vaktaa, lekhak, itihaaskaar or kavi the. 
 sayed ali akhtar rizvi kaa 19 september 1948 main gopalpur jila sivaan bihar main paidaa hue aapke pita kaa naam sayed mazahar hossain rizvi tha. aap jab 3 varsh ke the to aapke pita kaa dehanth ho gaya. pita kii mrityu ke baad unki maan ne badi kathin paristhitiyon main bhi na keval parivaar ko chalaaya apitu bacchon ko shreshth sanskaar bhi diye.
 iran ke parasiddh itullah hazarat nasir makaarim shiraji dwaara inako "adibe asar" nami khitaab se sammanit kiya gaya. ali akhtar rizvi kii kaavyatmak sheershak "shaur gopalpuri " tha.
 saiyad ali akhtar rizvi kaa 10 farvari, 2002 ko nidhan ho gaya. unke antim sanskaar main kai vidwaanon or prachaarkon ne bhaag liya or gopalpur, jila sivaan, bihar, bharat main logon kii baari bheed ke dwaara kiya gaya.
