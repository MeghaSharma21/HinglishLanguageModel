 bhumandal par taap ke kshetij vitaran ko pradarshit karne ke liye samtap rekhaon kaa prayog kiya jaataa hai. samtap rekhaaen ve kalpit rekhaaen hai jo samaan taap vaale sthanon ko milaate hue khinchi jaati hai. inhe khinchne ke liye vibhinn sthano kaa taapmaana gyaat kiya jaataa he, phir un sthano ke taapamano ko sagar tal par samyojit kiya jaataa hai, arthath sabhi sthanon ko sagar tal par maan kar sanshodhit taapmaana praapt kiye jate hai, tatpashchaar samtap rekhaaen khichi jaati hai.
