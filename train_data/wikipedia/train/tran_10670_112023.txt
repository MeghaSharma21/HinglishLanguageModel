 kumari lila samson bharat kii bharatnatyam kii prasiddh nritika hai. unhen san 2000 main padmashri pradaan kiya gaya tha. abhi haal main ve kendriya film pramann board kii adhyaksha niyukt kii gayin hai. 
 bharatnatyam main paarangat lila samson ne 1975 main dilli sthit shriram bhartiya kala centre main bharatnatyam kaa vibhaag shuru kiya. vartmaan main vah shastreey kala santhaan "kalakshetra" kii nideshak hai tathaa sangeet naatak academy kii adhyaksh bhi hai. kala main unke yogadan ke liye unhen padmashri se bhi sammanit kiya gaya he. samson ke alaava pratishthit vakil lalit bhasin ko film pramann apiliy panchat kaa adhyaksh niyukt kiya gaya he. 
 unhen 1991 main gandhi rashtriya ekta puraskaar or 2007 main raashtrapati dwaara rashtriya kanoon diwas puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya he.
