 varun kumaar ek bhartiya patrkaar he. jinka janm 28 april 1985 ko bhartiya rajya uttar pradesh ke bulandshahr naamak sthaan par hua tha. vartmaan main patrkaar varun kumaar ali baba samooh ke yusi news company main manager ke pad par 07 farvari 2017 se karyrat he.
 varun kumaar ne apane patrakarita karier kii shuruyaat to 2003 main kii thi. jabaki inhone ek televijan patrkaar ke rup main san 2007 main kaary kiya tha. televijan patrkaar ke rup main kumaar ne 3 saal tak kaary kiya phir 2010 main inhone star news ke naam se news channel main kaam karnaa armbh kiya tha. iske alaava inhone abeepi news main bhi patrakarita kii thi. iske baad inhone 4 saal baad 1 janvari 2014 ko rashtriya samachar patr amar ujaalaa ke liye apni patrakarita jari rakhi. baad main varun kumaar ne ek saal baad amar ujaalaa se kaary chhodkar hindustan times ke liye kaary kiya tha. varun kumaar ne hindustan times par 5 janvari 2015 se farvari 2017 tak kaary kiya iske tatpashchaat inhone ali baba samooh ke yusi news main kaary karnaa armbh kiya. varun vartmaan main yusi news par ek manager ke pad par karyrat he. 
 varun kumaar 2003 se hindi main patrakarita se jude hue hai is kaaran 2017 ke shikshak diwas ke maukey par inhen dilli main rashtriya media kaa sammaan diya gaya. varun kumaar ke parivaar ke sabhi sadsy lagbhag patrakarita se hi jude hue hai. iske alaava varun kumaar ko may 2017 main first prerana inspire aspire india puraskaar se bhi sammanit kiya gaya.
