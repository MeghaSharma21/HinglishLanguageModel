 karl gutsko unnisvin shatabdi ke madhya ke tarun german aandolan ke ullekhneey german sahityakar the.
 inkaa janm ek nirdhan parivaar main hua tha. lekin unamem pratibha or mahatvakanksha thi. sahityajagat main safalataa praapt karne kaa unhone nishchay kar liya tha. germany ke pragatisheel vicharonvaale yuvak lekhakon ke ye netaa ho gaye. 1835 i. main unka upanyaas ‘vaili di dowtor’ chapa jiske madhyam se inhone bade saahas ke saath jivan kii bhaitik aavashyaktaaon par bal diya. is pustak kii teevr aalochana hui or aniatikta ke dosha kaa tark dekar tatkaaleen shashan ne isapar pratibandh laga diya. gutskon ko bhi jail kii sajaa hui. ek doosraa upanyaas 'nebeniyand ira' main unhone germany ke tatkaaleen saamaajik jivan kaa bada vyapak chitra prastut kiya he. inhone naatak bhi likhe. ‘vil e kosta’ main inhone dharmik swatantraaa kii aawaaj uthaai he. inkaa ek upanyaas ‘da naitus ov da spirit’ he jisamen raajnneetik shakti ke prashn kaa vivechan he.
