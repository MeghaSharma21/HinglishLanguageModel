 matteyo renzi ek italvi raajnneetijna hai jo 22 farvari 2014 se italy ke pradhanmantri bane hai evam 2013 se democratic party ke sachiv hai. isse purv 2009 se 2014 tak wo florence ke meyar ev florence praant ke adhyaksh rah chuke hai. 17 farvari 2014 ko raashtrapati georgio nepolitano ne unhen nai sarkaar banane ke liye aamantrit kiya jise unhone 21 farvari ko sweekrut kiya. wo italy ke sabase yuva pradhanmantri hai.
 renzi kaa janm florence main 1975 main hua. unhone florence vishvavidhyalay se snaatak kii shiksha praapt kii. uske baad unhone 1996 main italvi pipuls party kii sadsyta grahan kii or 1999 main prantiya sachiv bane. baad main 2004 main 59% maton ke saath jeet darj karte hue wo florence praant ke adhyaksh pad ke liye chune gayi.
 renzi kaa vivaah ek shikshika agnes landini se hua jinase unke do putr or ek putree hai.
