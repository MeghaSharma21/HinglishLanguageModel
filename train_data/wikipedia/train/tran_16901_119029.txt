 bhil madhya bharat kii ek janajati he. bhil janajati ke log bhil bhashaa bolte he. bhil, gujarat, madhya pradesh, chhattisgarh, maharashtra or rajasthan main ek anusuchit janajati he, ajmer main khwaja moinuddin hasan chishti kii dargah ke khaadim bhi bhil purvajon ke vanshaj hai. bhil tripura or pakistan ke sindh ke tharaparadar jile main bhi based huye hai.
 ajmer sthit hazrat khwaja moinuddin hasan chishti rahamatullah kii dargah ke khaadim bhil purvajon ke vanshaj he, inaki do shakhaayen hai ek saiyadjaadagaan jo kii swayan ko khwaja fakhruddin ke vanshaj bataate he, khwaja fakhruddin jinka mul naam lakh bhil tha or jinhone khwaja sahab ke chele ban kar islam kabool kar liya tha, isi prakaar lakh bhil ke sage bhai ke vanshaj he shikajaadagaan samudaay ke log. khud ko shaikhjada bataane vaale khaadim sway ko mohammad yaadgaar kaa vanshaj bataate hai mohammad yaadgaar kaa naam teka bhil tha tha tathaa yah bhi khwaja sahib ke mureed banakar islam dharm main shaamil ho gaye the. 
 bhil shabd kii utpatti "bill" se hui he jiskaa dravid bhashaa main arth hota hai "dhanush". bhil jati do prakaar se vibhajit he-1.ujalia/kshatriya bhil- ujalia bhil mul rup se ve kshatriya he jo saamaajik/mughal akraman ke samay jangalo main chale gaye evam mul bheelon se vaivaahik sambandh sthapit kar lene se swayan ko ujalia bhil kehane lage malwa main rahane vaale bhil vahi he. inke riti rivaaj rajputon kii tarah hi he. inamen vadhoomulya nahin paaya jaataa or naa hi ye bhili bhashaa bolte he. inke chehre or sharir kii banabat, kad lathi pracheen rajputon se milti he.2.langot bhil-ye wanon main rahane vaale mul bhil he inke reeti rivaaj aaj bhi puraane he. inamen vadhoomulya kaa prachlan paaya jaataa he. m.pra. ke nimad main rahane vaale adhikansh janajati yahi he.
