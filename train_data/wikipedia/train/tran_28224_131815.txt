 maidan-e-jang 1995 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. ise ke si bokadiya. dwaara nirdeshit kiya gaya he. dharmendra, akshay kumaar or karishma kapur mukhya kalakaar hai. anya kalakaaron main amrish puri, manoj kumaar, jayaprada, gulshan grover, kadar khan, shakti kapur, mukesh khanna or dina pathak shaamil hai.
 daata guru ek gaanv kaa amir, shaktishaalee or prabhaavshaalee mukhiya he. vah sabhi graminon or unki sampattiyon kaa maalik he or unka niyantran karata he. koi bhi jo uske khilaaf aawaaj uthaane kii himmat karata he use uske karmchaaree shankar or uskaa betaa gumaan dwaara kuchal diya jaataa he.
 karan daata guru kaa sabase bada putr hai, jo apni shiksha puri karne ke baad gaanv waapas aata he. vah shankar kii bahan tulsi se pyaar karata he. is bich shankar gumaan se daata guru kii vidhwaa bahu lakshmi ko bachaata he, jo usase balaatkaar karne kii koshish karata he. jab daata guru jaanata he ki shankar ne lakshmi kaa apharan kar liya he or usase shaadi kii he, vah shankar ko mara hua dekhnaa chahta he. daata guru shankar ke khilaaf karan ko uttejit karata he. vah graminon kii pani kii aapurti main katautee karne kii dhamkee detaa agar ve shankar ko uske hawaale nahin karte. 
 karan jeeva se puri sachai jaanata he. vah datta guru ke khilaaf shankar kii madad karne kaa faisla karata he.
