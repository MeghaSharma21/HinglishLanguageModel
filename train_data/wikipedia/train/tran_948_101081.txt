 
 kunduri yaa kundru ek ushnkaribandheey lata he. yah saare bharat main swatah bhi ugti he or kuch jagahon par isaki kheti bhi kii jaati he. 
 isaki jaden lambii or fal 2 se 5 sein. me. lambey or 1 se 2.5 sein. me. vyasavaale andakaar athwa dirghavrittakar hote hai. fal kaccha rahane par hare or safed dhariyon se yukt hota he. pak jane par iskaa rang chatak sinduri ho jaataa he. kachche fal tarkari banane ke kaam aate hai or pakane par ye taaje bhi khaae jate hai. kuch log pake hue falon ko shakkar main paag dete hai.
 kunduri ke falon ke rasinic vishleshan se nimnaankit maan praapt hue hai:
 kunduri kii jadon, tanon or pattiyon ke anek virachanon kaa ullekh deshi oshadhiyon main paaya jaataa he jiske anusaar ise charm rogon, zukam, fefdon ke shoth tathaa madhumeh main laabhadaayak bataaya gaya he.
