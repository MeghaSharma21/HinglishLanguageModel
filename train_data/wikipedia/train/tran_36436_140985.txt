 bhautik vigyaan main drudh pind, thos kii us adarsh avasthaa ko kaha jaataa he jisamen koi virupan nahin hota. anya shabdon main, drudh pind ke die gaye kisi do kanon ke madhya duuri samay ke saath niyat rahati he chaahe is par koi bhi bahya bal aropit kiya jaye.
 drudh pind kii sthiti usamen samahit sabhi kanon kii sthiti ko nirupit karti he.
 veg va koniya veg kaa maapan nirdesh tantra ke saapeksh kiya jaataa he.
 drudh pind B kaa nirdesh tantra N main koniya veg, N, main drudh pind D ke koniya veg va D ke saapeksh B ke koniya veg ke jod ke samaan hota he.
