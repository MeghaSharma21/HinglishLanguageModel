 aa gale lag ja 1994 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. iskaa nirmaan saleem akhtar ne kiya or isaki kahani saleem khan ne likhi. iske kalakaar jugal hansraj, urmila matondkar, paresh rawal, ashok sarraf, ranjit, rima lagu, raza muraad or gulshan grover hai. is film se jugal hansraj ne pehali baar pramukh bhoomika nibhaai. unhone 1980 ke dashak main kai filmon main pehle bal kalakaar ke rup main kaam kiya tha. sanyog se, unhenne urmila matondkar ke saath film maasoom main unke bhai kii bhoomika nibhaai thi.
 suraj apane chachaa maamtaram  dwaara paala ek garib ladkaa he. roshni apni vidhwaa chachee shakuntladevi dwaara paali gai amir ladki he. suraj or roshni pyaar main padate hai, lekin uski chachee yah jaanakar vivaah kaa virodh karti he ki jugal urmila ke pita ke driver kaa betaa he, jo unka hatyare bhi he. jugal ke chachaa ne pushti kii ki uske asli pita vastav main jail main the, lekin ek tej trek dwaara rihaai par unhen maaraa diya gaya tha.
 suraj ke sapane bikhar jate hai. achaanak, koi roshni ke pita ke vyapaar ke bhagidaaron kii hatya shuru kar detaa he. suraj kii ore andeh jaataa he. hatya ke maamalon ko police inspector rambhajan singh ko saunpa gaya he. yah samajhane ke baad ki hatyara koi teesraa vyakti he jiske bare main koi nahin jaanata, inspector uske liye ek jaal kii vyavastha karata he.
 yah paaya jaataa he ki hatyara jugal ke mrit pita he. vah sabhi ko bataata he ki urmila ke pita ke vaastavik hatyare unke vyapaarik saajhedaar the, jo darate the ki uske pita unki dhokhadhadi kaa pardaafash karenge. unhone jugal ke pita phasaaya. inspector dwaara giraftaar karne se pehle, jugal ke pita aspataal main aakhri saathi ko maar dete hai.
 ant main jugal ke pita ne police ko aatmasamarpan kar diya or film samaapt ho gai.
 film apane madhur geeton ke liye jaani jaati he. 
 sabhi anu malik dwaara sangitabaddh.
