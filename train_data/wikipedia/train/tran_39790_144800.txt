 vajubhai vaala ek bhartiya rajaneta hai. vartmaan main ve karnataka ke rajyapal hai. iske purv ve gujarat vidhansabha ke adhyaksh the.
 shri vajubhai vaala ne rashtriya swayamsevak sangh ke sadsy ke rup main apana karir armbh kiya or 1971 main jan sangh ke sadsy bane. 1975 main apatkaal ke samay 11 maas tak ve karagaar main the. 1980 ke dashak main ve rajkot ke meyar bhi rahe.
 ve sitambar 2014 se karnataka ke rajyapal hai. iske purv 2012 se 2014 tak ve gujarat vidhansabha ke adhyaksh the. 1998 se 2012 tak ve gujarat mantrimandal main vitt, shram or rojgaar aadi ke cabinet mantri rah chuke hai. ve kai baar pashchimi rajkot se vidhansabha ke liye chune gayi the.
 unki do putriyaan or do putr hai.
