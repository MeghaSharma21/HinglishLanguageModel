 is asana kii antim avasthaa main pahunchne ke baad hamaare sharir kii akruti kuch-kuch oont ke samaan prathit hoti he, isi kaaran ise ustrasana kehete hai.
 jor se yaa jatke se is asana ko na karen. peeche jhukate samay jangha sidhi rakhen. antim sthiti main gardan se lekar ghutane tak kaa bhaag sidhaa rahegaa. waapas aate samay jhatka dekar na aaen. jin logon ko hernia kii shikaayat ho, unhen yah asana nahin karnaa chahie.
 is asana se ghutane, bldar, kidney, choti aant, liver, chaati, langs evam gardan tak kaa bhaag ek saath prabhavit hota he, jisase ki uparyukt ang samooh kaa vyaayaam hokar unka nirogipan bana rahata he. shwaas, udar, pindliyon, pairon, kandhe, kuhaniyon or merudand sambandhi rog main laabh miltaa he.udar sambandhi rog, jaise costraupeshan, indigeshan, acidity rog nivaaran main is asana se sahaayta milti he. gale sambandhi rogon main bhi yah asana laabhadaayak he.
