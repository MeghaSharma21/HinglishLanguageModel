 hypertext transfar protocol ek aplicoshan protocol he jo kisi jalsthal ke aage se aage lagata he jisase URL pataa khulata he arthath koi jalsthal khulti he. iskaa sankshipt naam arthath ech tii tii pi he. yah hameshaa kisi webpate ke aage hi lagata he udhaaharan ke liye 
 
 aajkal ke broujaron ke yuarel bakse main kisi jaal sthal kaa pataa likhane par yah uske aage swatah likhaa miltaa he. http ke thik peeche hameshaa : aisa chihn or uske baad // aisi tirchhi lakeeren lagaani padati he or iske pashchaat webpta likhnaa padataa he. 
 hypertext transfar protocol kaa avishkaar athwa isko pehali baar 1965 main ted nelson ne prayog kiya tha. iskaa pehla document varjan http vo.9 san 1991 main laanch hua tha.
