  shahbajpur toun, bangladesh main ek chote nagar, machhlee pakadaney ke bandargah or prashasnic mukhyalay he. yah titas kii apni vistrut prakritik nadiyon ke liye janaa jaataa he. yah sabase purana nadi bandargah he. yah bangladesh main sabase puraani nauka terminal bandargah kshetra he. shahbajpur toun bhi naam "shahbaaj" se janaa jaataa he. ke saath apane puraane naam shahbaaj yaa shahbajpur he. aadhunik shahbajpur toun shahbaaj ali se uskaa naam ho jaataa he, mughal samrajya ke samraat ke pratinidhi .
 aaj, shahbajpur toun, titas nadi bangladesh main sabase kaa daura kshetreey paryaton sthalon main se ek he. yah abhi tak ek pramukh jila paryaton sthal nahin ban gaya he.
