 sarita vaartak urmila matondkar, ek saadhaaran travel agent ek noujvaan vyavsaayee karan rathod saif ali khan se milti he or use usase pyaar ho jaataa he. ek din karan kaa ek dost use ek bag sambhaal kar rakhane ke liye detaa he. baad main police sarita ke ghar dhaava bolti hau or us bag se avaidh hathiyaar baromad karti he. karan kaa vakil kamalesh maathur aditya shrivastav sarita ko phusalaakar is aparaadh main shaamil hone kii baat manavaa letaa he. iske tahat sarita ko 7 saal kii sajaa ho jaati he. sarita ko apni galati kaa ahsaas hota he par tab tak kaaphi der ho chuki thi. jail main ek saathi pramila pramila kaazmi kii madad se vah jail se baahar nikalati he or karan ke saath badla lene ke khatarnaak khel ko shuru karti he.
