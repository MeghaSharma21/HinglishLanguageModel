 dabestan-e-majaaheb 17veen sadi ke dakshin asian dharmon kii tulanaatmak kriti he. ise san 1665 main farsi bhashaa main likhaa gaya tha.dabestan-e-majaaheb deen-e-ilaahi par likhe adhyay ke liye sarvaadhik janaa jaataa he. deen-e-ilaahi dharm ko mughal shaasha akbar ne san 1581 se shuru kiya tha. dabestan-e-majaaheb ibaadat khaanaa main hone vaali charchaaon ke bare main sabase vishwasaneey strot he.
