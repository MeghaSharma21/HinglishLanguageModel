 malaalai joya afghanistan se kaaryakarta, lekhika, or ek purv rajnetri hai.
 malaalai joya afghanistani sansad kii sabase jawan or mashhoor sadsy thi, joya afghanistani sansad ko 2005 main pashchim faraah praant se chuni hui. afghanistan kii prantiy vidhansabha ne malaalai joya kii sadsyta ko radd kar di. milaalai joya ne ek saakshaatkaar main sansad ko astabal se badtar qaraar diya tha. inkaa kahana tha ki sansad se behatar to astabal hota he jisaki gaay kam-se-kam doodh detii hai. or is main rakhe jane vaale gadhe barburdaari ke kaam to aate hai. saathi arkan ne apni us 'munh fat' sadsy ko sabaq sikhaane ke liye uski sadsyta ko radd kar di he.sansad ke nichle sadan ne joya ko 2010 main vidhansabha kii avadhi ke ant tak nilambit rakhane ke liye vot diya he. joya afghanistani sansad kii aisi mahila sadsy jinhone abdul rasul sayyaf or mujahidin ke anya mukhya netaaon par sakht aalochana kii he, yani ki is kaam ke vajah se joya duniya-bhar jaani-pahachaani shaks ban gai.
