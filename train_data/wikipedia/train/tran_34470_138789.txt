 yenicey nadi uttari asia ke siberia kshetra kii ek mahaan nadi he jo arctic mahaasaagar main vilay hone vaala duniya kaa sabase bada nadi-mandal he. yah nadi mongolia ke sayan parvaton se shuru hokar rus main daakhil hoti he or phir uttar kii taraf hazaron kilometre bahakar arctic mahaasaagar ke kaara sagar main bah jaati he. kul milakar yah 5,539 kimi lambi he. isaki ausat gaharaai 14 meter he or sabase zyaada gaharaai 24 meter he. kul milakar yenicey nadi ke jalsambhar kaa kshetrafal 25,80,000 varg kimi he. apane kul pani ke bahaav ke hisaab se yah nadi vishwa kii chhathi sab se badi nadi he.
