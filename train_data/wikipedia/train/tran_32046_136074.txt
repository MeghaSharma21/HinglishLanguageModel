 rashtrabhasha vichaar manch, ambala kii sthapana 26 janvari 2000 ko hui thi. iskaa uddeshy rajnatik swadhin bharat main rashtrabhasha kii upekshapurna niti se utpann samaaj main bhashaa-samskruti ke prati udaseenta ke kaaran rashtriyata kii bhaavana main hraas ke drushtigat, samaaj main bhashaa-samskruti ke vichaar ke madhyam se rashtriya chetana kaa sanchaar karnaa tathaa raashtra ke swabhimaan main abhivruddhi se raashtra ko param vaibhav kii prapti kii or le janaa he.
