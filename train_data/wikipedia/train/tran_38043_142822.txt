 namakan prakriya :
  brajabhasha chalchitrapat bhartiya chalchitr udyog he. jise brajawood bhi kehete hai.
 brajabhasha chalchitrapat kaa prarambh varsh 1982 main hua jab suprasiddh hindi film abhinayta shiv kumaar dwaara banaaya brajabhasha kaa pratham chalchitr braj bhumi pradarshit hua. yah chalchitr bahut bada sadabahaar chalchitr tha. iske baad suprasiddh hasya kavi kaaka haathrasi ne brajabhasha chalchitr jamuna kinare banaaya jo varsh 1983 main pradarshit hua.tathaa varsh 2000 main brajabhasha chalchitr braj kau birju pradarshit hua. brajabhasha cinema main saamaajik evam hasya manoranjan kaa vishesh sthaan rahaa he.
