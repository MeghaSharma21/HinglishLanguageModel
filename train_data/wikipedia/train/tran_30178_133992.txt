 virendra kumaar bhattacharya asamiya sahityakar the. inke dwaara rachit ek upanyaas iaruingam ke liye unhen san 1961 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. inhen 1979 main gyanpith puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha. samaajawaadee vichaaron se prerit shri bhattacharya kahanikar, kavi, nibandhkaar or patrkaar the. ve sahitya academy, dilli or assam sahitya sabha ke adhyaksh rahe. 
 unhone 1950 main sampadit asmi patrika ramadhenu kaa sampadan kar asamiya sahitya ko nayaa mode diya. inke charchit upanyaason iarungam, mrityunjay, rajpathe, ringian, ai, pritpad, shatghni, kaaler humuniyahahain. inke do kahani sangrah bhi prakashit hue, kalang aajiyo boi or saatsri.
