 dweep chaap ek aisa dweep samooh hota he jisamen dweep, jwalamukhi va samudr ke neeche ubhari chattaane ek chaap ke aakaar main susajjit hoti hai. yah dweep chaap aksar do bhugolic platon kii seemaa par sthit hote hai or us seemaa par ek plate ke duusari plate ke neeche dab jane se nirmit hote hai. chadhi hui plate uthne se dweep banate hai or dabi hui plate kii taraf ek gahari samudri khai bhi aksar astitv main hoti he. platon kii is juten se megma bhi ubharta he or aksar jwalamukhi bhi dweep chaapon main milte hai.
