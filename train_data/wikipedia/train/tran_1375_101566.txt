 bharat ke swatantraaa aandolan ke samay gandhiji ne sabase pehle buniyadi shiksha kii kalpana kii thi. aaj jise vishvavidhyalay str par "foundation course" kaha jaataa he, uski prishthbhumi main gandhi kii buniyadi yani basic shiksha hi to thi. is buniyadi prashikshan or praathmik str kii shiksha ke do str the- schuli bachche kaksha-ek se hi takali se soot kaatte the; rooyee se pauni banaate the or soot kii gudiya banaakar yaa to khaadi bhandaron ko dete the yaa baithne ke asana, rumal, chadar aadi banaate the. 
 shiksha ke bare main gandhiji kaa drushtikon vastut: vyavsyparak tha. unka mat tha ki bharat jaise garib desh main shikshaarthiyon ko shiksha praapt karne ke saath-saath kuch dhanoparjan bhi kar lenaa chahie jisase ve aatmanirbhar ban saken. isi uddeshy ko lekar unhone ‘wardha shiksha yojana’ banaayi thi. shiksha ko laabhadaayak evam alpavyayee karne kii drushti se san 1936 i. main unhone ‘bhartiya talim sangh’ kii sthapana kii.
