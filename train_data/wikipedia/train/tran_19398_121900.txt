 vitt evam arthvyavastha ke sandarbh main, un sansthaaon ko vittiya sansthaayen kehete hai jo apane graahakon evam sadasyon ko vittiya sevaayen dete hai. bank, bhawan-nirmaan sosayadi, beemaa companiyaan, pension fund companiyaan, dalal sansthaayen aadi vittiya sansthaaon ke kuch udhaaharan hai. kisi bhi desh kii pragati me vittiya sansthaanon kii ahm bhoomika hoti he.
 vittiya santhaan banking, insurans, mutual fund, share bazaar, grih rin, duusare rin, credit card ke kshetro me kaam karte he. vittiya sansthaanon kaa mukhya kaam desh me mudra ke pravaah ko niyantrit karnaa hota he. 
 udyog-dhandhon ko chalaane me puunji kii zarurat hoti he. ye unhen vittiya santhaan pradaan karte he. udyog, janata ko rojga uplabdh karaate he. vittiya sansthaanon kii madad se aam log udyogo me apni puunji laga ke ek taraf munafa kamaate he to duusari taraf desh ke vikaas me yogadan dete hai. 
 ye santhaan logo ko unki zaruuraton ko puraa karne ke liye tarah tarah ke rin dete he. jaise ghar kharidne ke liye grih rin, ucch shiksha ke liye shiksha rin, car or motorcical ke liye automobile rin or duusari zaruuraton ke liye vyaktigat rin. bainkon me log bachat khate khol ke apana paisa jamaa karte he. iske alaava log insurans yaa beemaa me bhi nivesh karte he. share bazaar or mutual fund me puunji nivesh me bhi aajkal vittiya santhaan logo ke liye share dalal kii bhoomika adaa karte he. 
 logon se ikkattha kiya hua paisa udyogo or desh ke vikaas me lagaaya jaataa he. vittiya santhaan na sirf nijee companiyon ko balki rajyo or kendra sarkaar ko bhi tarakkee ke kaamo ke liye puunji muhaiyaa karaate he. 
 pichle varsho me bharat me vittiya sansthano ne vikaas me bady bhoomika nibhaai he. bharat ke pramukh vittiya santhaan nimna he:
