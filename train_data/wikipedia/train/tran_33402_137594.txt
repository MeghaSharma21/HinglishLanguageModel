 
 chupunika yaa plyoni, jiskaa bair namakan 28 taori he, vrish taramandal main sthit ek tara he. yah krittika taraguchh kaa sadsy he or iskaa prithvi se dekha gaya ausat saapeksh kantimaan kaa magnitude +5.09 he. yah krittika taraguchh main apane se adhik tejasvi varshyanti taare ke pados main sthit hone ke kaaran kathinaayee se dikhataa he. prithvi se ek dikhne vaala yah tara vastav main ek dwitara he. iskaa mukhya tara "chupunika e" kehalaata he. yah suraj se 190 guna adhik kantimaan rakhane vaala bi-shrenee kaa tara he. isaki ghurnan gati bahut adhik he or yadi zaraa-bhi adhik hoti to tara tut saktaa tha.
