 washington sanyukt rajya america kaa sabase uttar-pashchimiya rajya he. iske uttar main canada, purv main idaho or dakshin main aurigan he. washington rajya kaa nirmaan origan sandhi ke tahat 1846 main britain dwaara die gaye kshetra ke pashchimi bhaag main hua tha. yah 42wein rajya ke rup main 1889 main sangh main bhartee karaayaa gaya tha. iskaa naam sanyukt rajya america ke pehle raashtrapati jorge washington ke naam par rakhaa gaya he. 
 washington 18waan sabase bada america rajya he. 2016 main isaki jansankhya 72,88,000 anumaanit kii gai he. jisase iskaa is maamle 25waan sthaan hua. olympia rajya kii rajdhani or sabase bada shahar sietal he. rajya sabase viksit rajyon main se ek he. washington main vinirmaan udyogon main vimaan or missile, jahaaj nirmaan or anya parivahan upkaran, lakdi, khaadya prasanskaran, dhaatu or dhaatu utpaada, rasayan or mashinari shaamil hai.
