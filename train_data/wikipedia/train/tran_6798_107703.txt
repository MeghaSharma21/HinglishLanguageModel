 general sundarrajan padmanabhan pivisaim, avisem, viesaim, bhartiya sena ke purv senadhyaksh hai . general padmanabhan 30 sitambar 2000 ko general ve pi malik ke uttaradhikari bane. unhone chif of staff cameti ke adhyaksh ke rup main bhi kaary kiya. 
 1956 main padmanabhan ne bhartiya rashtriya sainya college main shiksha grahan kii. baad main padmanabhan rashtriya raksha academy main shaamil ho gaye or 1959 main artilari ke regiment main daakhila liya . 
 unhone disambar 1988 se farvari 1991 tak ranchi ,bihar or punjab main ek infentry brigade kaa netritv kiya or unhen march 1991 se agast 1992 tak punjab ke ek infentry divijan ke general officer commanding ke rup main niyukt kiya gaya. unhone sitambar 1992 se june 1993 tak chif of staff, III kor ke rup main kaary kiya . leftinent general pad par padonnati ke baad, vah july 1993 se farvari 1995 tak kashmir ghati main XV kor ke commander the. yah unke kaarykaal ke dauraan XV kor commander ke rup main sena ne kashmir main aatankwaadiyon par badi vijay praapt kii . unhen XV corps commander ke rup main unki sevaaon ke liye ati vishisht seva padak se sammanit kiya gaya tha.
 general padmanabhan ne 1996 ne udhampur main uttari kaman ke jiosi-in-si ke rup main padbhaar sanbhaalane ke baad mahanideshak sainya  pad par niyukti hui . 1996 ko senadhyaksh ke rup main niyukt hone se pehle, vah dakshini kaman ke jiosi-in-si the. vah 31 disambar 2002 ko pratishthit sainya seva se 43 saal se adhik puraa karne ke baad sevanivrutt hue. unhone do kitaaben likhi hai. ve vartmaan main chennai main nivas karte he.
 general padmanabhan 2004 ke upanyaas "Writing on the Wall" sahit anek bhartiya sainya katha pustakon ke lekhak bhi hai.
