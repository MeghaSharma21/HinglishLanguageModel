 kaashi ke ghaaton main "hanuman ghat" vishesh rup se sadhu-mahatma logon kaa ghat he. is ghat ke upree bhaag main bahut hi puraatan bada hanuman ji kaa mandir he. is mandir main hanuman ji ke atirikt ram darbar navah evam ek ashv bhi sthapit he, jo ram ji ke ashwamedh vijay ke patik kii yaad dilaataa he. hanuman mandir ke praangan main bhagwaan dattatreya kaa bahut hi bhaby mandir he,jisaki diwaron par bhagwaan dattatrey kii jeevani se sambandhit adbhut chitrakaari kii gayi he . saath hi ram,lakshmana,bharat ,shatrughan ke aradhyon ko dattatreya mandir ke sanidhya main ling rup me sthapit kiye gayi hai . mukhya rup se hanuman ghat aivan mandir kii puujarchan or vyavastha dashanami june akhaada ke adheen he. hanuman ghat mohalla main adhiktar dakshin bhartiya kaa nivas sthaan he. hanuman ghat mohalla main shrungery shankaracharya kaa mandir he jisamen kumbh snaan ke uparant sabhi dash-nami akhaada ke sadhu darshanath avashya aate hai. hanuman ghat mohalle main pravesh karte aisa prathit hota he,jaise aap vaidic yug main pahunch gayi hon,jidhar jaanye vaidic swar hi sunaayi dete he.
