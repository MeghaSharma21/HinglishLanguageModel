 abu amrit khalipha ebn khayyaat al lethi al 'usfuri ek arab itihaaskaar the.
 khayyaat kaa parivaar iraq main basra ke mul nivasee the. unke dada ek prasiddh muhaddith yaa paramparawaadi the, or khalipha bhi iske liye prasiddh ho gaye. mahaan islamic vidwaanon main se bukhari or ahamed ebn hanabal jo unke shishy the.
