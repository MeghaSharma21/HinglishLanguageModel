 westeindies trikoniya shrinkhalaa 2013 main westeindies main ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket toornament he, jise bharat, srilanka or westeindies ke bich yojit kiya gaya tha. group fikchar kaa pehla daur duusare daur ke saath sabina park, kingston, jamaica main yojit kiya jayega or queen park ovel, port of spain, trinidad main yojit final he. shrinkhalaa kaa naam selcon mobile cap he. bharat ke captain mahendra singh dhoni ne shrinkhalaa main duusare ekadivasiy match ke dauraan daaen chhidrapurn samasyaon ke kaaran trikoniya shrinkhalaa se baahar hone kaa faisla kiya. isliye viraat kohli ko bhartiya captaini di gai thi. ambati rayudu kaa naam dhoni kii jagah he.
