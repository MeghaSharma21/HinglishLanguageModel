 abhiraj rajendra mishra sanskrit kavi, geetkaar, naatakkaar hai. ve sampoornanand sanskrit vishvavidhyalay, varanasi ke upkulapati bhi rah chuke hai. unhen 1988 main sanskrit main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. ve 'triveni-kavi' naam se jane jate hai. unhone sanskrit, hindi, bhojpuri or angreji main anek pustakon kii rachana kii he.
 pro. abhiraj rajendra mishra ke srijan karm kii ek lambi suchi he jisamen unke do mahakavy, 44 shatak-kavy, 64 ekanki, 6 laghukatha sankalnon main anekon kathayen, gazal, geet samahit hai. unki rasgarbhit kavyastylee, shringaar ke virah va pidaa ke geet, gazale, sahrudayon ke hriday kaa haar he, us par unki mantramugdh kar dene vaali madhur vaani kavi sammelnon va goshtiyon kaa praan he.
