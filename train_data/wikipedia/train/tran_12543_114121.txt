 sami-hami bhashaa-parivaar madhya-purv or uttari africa kii kai sambandhit bhashaaon kaa samooh he. is parivaar kii sami shakha soudi arab, philistin, israel, iraq, syria, misr, yardan, ithiopia, tunisia, algeria, morokko, ityadi main or hami shakha libya, somaliland, misr or ithiopia main faili hui hai. isaki shaami shakha main ibrani, arabi, arami, pracheen sumerian shaamil hai or hami shakha main pracheen misri, coptic, somali, galla, namaa, aadi bhashaayen aati hai. is varg kii bhashaayen antermukhi shlisht-yogatamak hoti hai. udhaaharan ke taur par arabi main k-t-l dhaatu se bich main swar ghusaane par kai naye shabd banate hai, jaise : katl, kaatil, kitl or yaktul .
