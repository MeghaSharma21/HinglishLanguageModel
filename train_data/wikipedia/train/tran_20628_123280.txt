 angira manyu athwa ahirman parsi dharm main nakaaratmak athwa vinaashak shaktiyon ke swami ke rup main varnit he. yah ahura mazda ke virdohi devtaa ke rup main sweekaar kiya gaya he. yah parsi dharm kii moulik sankalpana mana jaataa he, haalaanki, anya pracheen dharmon main varnit dwaitvaad kii bhaanti is ahura mazda - ahirman rupi sankalpana ko bhi ishwar-shaitan athwa shubh-ashubh shaktiyon ke dwaitvaad ke rup main dekha jaataa he. ahur mazd ko jivan, prakaash or naitikta kaa strot mana gaya he or ahirman ko andhkaar tathaa mrityu kaa.
