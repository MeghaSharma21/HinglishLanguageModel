 anugrah narayan singh college, patliputra vishvavidhyalay, patna kaa ek bhaag he. isaki sthapana 1956 me kii gayi thi or yah bihar kii rajdhani patna main sthit he. iskaa nirmaan mahaan swatantraaa senaanee or bihar ke pehle up mukhyamantri sah vitt mantri bihar vibhuti dow anugrah narayan singh ne karavaaya tha joki "babu sahib" ke naam se lokapriy the. abhi is college main 21 snaatak or 22 parasnatak vishyon kii padhai hoti he. yahaan turant naukari dilaane vaale vyavsaayik paathyakram jaise, bisie, emsie, bibie or emb aadi ke adhyayan kii bhi suvidha he. yah college 13 acre main failaa he tathaa iske parisar main kul 13 bhawan hai. college kii moolbhoot suvidhaayen bhi bahut viksit hai. biharvasiyon ke liye yah garv kaa parichaayak he.
