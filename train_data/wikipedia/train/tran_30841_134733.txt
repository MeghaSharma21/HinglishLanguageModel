 brahmasputsiddhanth, brahmagupta kii pramukh rachana he. yah sanskrit me he. isaki rachana san 628 ke aasapaas hui. dhyangrahopadeshaadhyay ko milakar isamen kul pachis adhyay hai. yah granth purnatah kavy rup main likhaa gai he. 'brahmasputsiddhanth' kaa arth he - 'brahmagupta dwaara sphutit siddhanth'.
 is granth main anya baton ke alaava ganit ke nimnlikhit vishay varnit hai-
 brahmasputsiddhanth main 25 adhyay hai jinke naam is prakaar hai-
 brahmasphutsiddhanth pehla granth he jisamen dhanatmak sankhyaon, rinatmak sankhyaon evam shunya se sambandhit thos jaankaree milti he. isamen maujuud kuch niyam neeche die gaye hai-
 dhyaan dene yogya he ki antim teen niyam sahi nahin hai kyonki shunya se bhaag dena field ke liye paribhaashit nahin he. kintu yah usase bhi mahatvapurn he ki sabase pehle shunya se bhaag dene kii koshish kii gai he.
