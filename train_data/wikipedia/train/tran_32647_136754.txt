 buddha acharya asang, yogachaar parampara ke adipravartak maane jate hai. mahayan sutralankar jaisaa praudh granth likhakar inhone mahayan sampradaay kii neenv daali or yah puraane hinayan sampradaay se kis prakaar ucch koti kaa he, isapar jor diya. 
 inkaa janm gandhar pradesh ke pushppur nagar, vartmaan peshawar, main duusari shatabdi ke aasapaas hua tha. 
 acharya asang dharmik pravartak hote hue buddha nyaay ke bhi aadi guru maane jate hai. inhone nyaay ke adhyaapan kii ek moulik parampara chalaai jisamen prasiddh buddha naiyayik diunag kii diksha hui. prasiddh he ki acharya asang ke bhai vasubandhu pehle sarvastvaad ke poshak the, kintu baad main asang ke prabhaav main aakar ve yogachaar vigyaanvaadee ho gaye. donon bhaaiyon ne milakar iske paksh ko bada prabal banaaya.
