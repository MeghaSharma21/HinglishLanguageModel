 shri jyotindra nath dikshit, jinhen je en dikshit naam se adhik janaa jaataa he, ek anbhavi rajanyik the jo ki bharat ke videsh sachiv bhi rahe. 1971 main bangladesh banane ke baad ve vahaan bharat ke pehle ucchayukta the. ve pakistan or srilanka main bhi bharat ke ucchayukta rahe. 1936 main madras main janme jeen dikshit 1958 main bhartiya videsh seva main aae or unhone duniya ke kai deshon main bharat kii numaaindgi kii. 1994 main sevanivruth hone ke baad se wo lagaataar desh-videsh main padhaane ke alaava akhbaaron main likhte rahe.
 ve hal, manchester, oxford,melbern, landan sahit kai anya pashchimi vishvavidhyalayon main bhi akasar lacture dene jaaya karte the. dikshit congress party main videsh maamalon kii ikai ke upaadhyaksh rahe. 2004 ke aamchunaav se pehle videsh, suraksha or raksha maamalon par unhone congress kaa agenda yaa ghoshnaa-patr taiyaar karne main ahm bhoomika nibhaai thi.
 ve 26 may 2004 se 3 janvari 2005 tak bharat ke rashtriya suraksha salaahkaar bhi rahe.
