 rashtriya rajmarg 44 bharat kaa sabase lambaa rajmarg he. yah uttar main srinagar se armbh hokar dakshin main kanyakumari main samaapt hota he.
 rashtriya rajmarg 44 apane puure faasale main jammu or kashmir, punjab, haryana, dilli, uttar pradesh, madhya pradesh, maharashtra, telugana, andhra pradesh, karnataka or tamil nadu ke rajyon se guzarataa he. isaki kul lambaai 3,745 kilometre he. yah kai mahatvapurn shaharon se nikalataa he, jinamein srinagar, jammu, pathankot, jalandhar, ludhiana, ambala, karnal, dilli, agra, gwalior, jhaansi, nagpur, hyderabad, bangaleru, dharmpuri, selem, karur, madurai, thirunelaveli or kanyakumari shaamil hai.
 bharat kii sabase badi surang chenanee-nashari surang isi mahamarg par sthit he. iske atirikt jawahar tunnel, kajigund banihaal surang aadi bhi ullekhneey hai.
