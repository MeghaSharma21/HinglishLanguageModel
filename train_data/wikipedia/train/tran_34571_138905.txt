 handiya ko adivaasiyon kaa vodaka kaha ja saktaa he, yah chaaval kii bani sharaab kaa naam he jo adivaasiyon ke jivan kaa atoot ang he. yah jharkhand, pashchim bengal, urisa, chhattisgarh or bharat ke anya adivasee ilaakon main kaaphi lokapriy he. adivasee aam taur shaam ghirne ke baad geet sangeet ke bich handia pi kar din kii baton ko bhula kar agle din kii taiyaari main jute jate hai yah log, yah soch kar ki shaayad nayaa din unke liye kuch nayaa lekar aae.
 handiya banane ke liye chaaval ko pani or khamir paidaa karne vaale kuch tatwon ke saath lagbhag choubees ghante chhod diya jaataa he, jiske baad yah seven karne yogya ho jaataa he. jaisaa ki aam taur par har tarah kii sharaab ke saath baat lagu hoti he, handiya kaa seven bhi alp maatra main karne par swasthya kii drushti se thik mana jaataa he. lekin adhik seven se nashe ke saath saath hi shaareerik or maansik swasthy kaa nuksaan hota he. adivasee samaaj main is buree pratha ko samaapt karne ke liye saamaajik muhim chalaaye gayi. unnisvin sadi main sabase majboot andolan birsa munda dwaara chhedi gayi thi.
