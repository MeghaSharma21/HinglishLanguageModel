  manipur ke mukhyamantri uttar-purv bharat ke manipur rajya ke sarkaar ke pramukh hai. bharat ke samvidhan ke mutabik, manipur ke rajyapal rajya ke nyaaypalik he, lekin vaastavik kaaryakari praadhikari mukhyamantri he. manipur vidhaan sabha ke chunaavon ke baad, rajyapal aam taur par sarkaar banane ke liye bahusankhya paksh ko aamantrit karata he. rajyapal mukhyamantri ko niyukt karata he, jinke mantrimandal main samuhik rup se vidhansabha ke liye jimmedaar he. yah dekhte hue ki unhen vidhansabha kaa vishwaas he, mukhyamantri kaa kaarykaal paanch saal ke liye he or yah koi avadhi seemaa nahin he.
 1963 se baarah logon ne manipur ke mukhyamantri ke rup main kaary kiya he inamen se paanch bhartiya rashtriya congress ke the, pehle padaadhikari marembam koirg singh sahit. maujuuda padaadhikari nongthombam biren singh bhartiya janata party ke pehle mukhyamantri he.
