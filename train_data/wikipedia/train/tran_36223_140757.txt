 hariom panvar bharat kii rashtriya asmita ke gayak hindi kavi hai. ve viraras ke kavi hai.
 hariom panvar ji kaa janm uttar pradesh ke buland shahar jile main sikandarabad ke nikat butna gaanv main hua tha. ve meerut vishvavidhyalay ke meerut mahavidhyalay main vidhi sankaay main professor hai. unhen bharat ke raashtrapati, uparashtrapati evam vibhinn mukhyamantriyon dwaara sammanit kiya ja chukaa he. unhen 'niraala puraskaar', 'bhartiya sahitya sangam puraskaar', 'rashmi puraskaar', 'janajagran sarvshereshth kavi puraskaar' tathaa 'aawaaj-e-hindusthan' aadi sammaan pradaan kiye gayi hai
