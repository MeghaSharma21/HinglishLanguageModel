 tibeti samrajya 7veen se 9veen sadi isvi tak chalane vaala ek samraj tha. tibet-naresh dwaara shasit yah samrajya tibet ke pathaar se kahin adhik vistrut kshetron par failaa hua tha. apane charam par pashchim main aadhunik afghanistan, uzbekistan, kashmir or himachal pradesh se lekar bengal or unnan praant ke kai bhaag iske adheen the. is samrajya kii niv 618 isvi main songtsen gampo ne rakhi thi or samrajya ne tezi se onggung, tuyuhun rajya or kai anya rajyon ko vijay kar liya. langadarma ko iskaa antim samraat mana jaataa he kyonki 841 isvi main unki hatya hone ke baad samrajya grih yudhon kii chapet main aakar khandit ho gaya haalanki tibeti raajaaon kaa silsilaa iske baad bhi jari rahaa.
