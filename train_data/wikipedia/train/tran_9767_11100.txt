 arnest trump ek german bhashavid or mishanri the jo angrezi shashan ke vaqt punjab or sindh kshetron main vichrate rahe the. 
 unhone sindhi bhashaa kii sabase pehali vyakaran pustak likhi jiskaa sheershak tha, "sindhi alfabat and garaamar". unhone afgaani bhashaa pashto kii ek vyakaran pustak bhi likhi. iske alaava unhone sikh dharm kii dharmik pustak guru granth sahib kaa bhi kaafii hissaa ko angrezi bhashaa main anuwaad kiya.
