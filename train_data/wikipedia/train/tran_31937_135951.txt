 getwe of india bharat ke pramukh nagar mumbai ke dakshin main samudr tat par sthit he. yah praveshdwar asitashm kaa bana hua sthapaty he, jisaki oonchai 26 meter he. is praveshdwar ke paas hi paryatakon ke samudr bhraman hetu nauka-seva bhi uplbdh he. praveshdwar ko banane ke liye pilaa asitashm prayukt kiya gaya he. praveshdwar kaa nirmaan raja jorge pancham or rani mairi ke aagaman 2 disambar, 1911 kii yaadgaar main hua tha. iske vaastushilpi jouj wintait the. yah san 1924 main ban kar taiyaar hua.
