  manipur ke rajyapal uttar-purv bharat ke manipur rajya ke sarkaar ke sanvaidhanik pramukh hai. rajyapal ko paanch saal kii avadhi ke liye bharat ke raashtrapati dwaara niyukt kiya jaataa he, or raashtrapati ke ichha ke anusaar karyaalay kaa kaarykaal hota he. bharat ke samvidhan ke mutabik, manipur ke rajyapal rajya ke nyaaypalik he, lekin vaastavik kaaryakari praadhikari manipur ke mukhyamantri he. bharat kaa samvidhan rajyapal ko apane swayan ke vivek par kaary karne kaa adhikaar detaa he; jaise ki mantralay ko niyukt karne yaa khaarij karne kii kshamta, raashtrapati ke shashan kii sifarish karnaa, yaa raashtrapati kii sahmati ke liye koi vidheyak arakshit karnaa.
