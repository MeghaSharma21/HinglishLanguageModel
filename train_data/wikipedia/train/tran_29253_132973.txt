 nodak ve rasinic padaarth hai jo urja yaa daabit gas paidaa karne ke liye prayukt hote hai. nodak se praapt urja yaa daabit gas kaa upyog kisi yaan, prakshepya, yaa anya vastu ko aage badhaane main hota he. petrol, jet eendhan, rocket eendhan or oxikaarak aadi saamaanya nodak hai. nodakon ke jalaane se athwa inkaa vighatan karne se nodak gas paidaa hoti he. kuch anya nodak drav hote hai jinako teji se vashpikrut karne se nodan praapt hota he.
