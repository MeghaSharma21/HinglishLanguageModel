 angaraag mahant, jinhen mukhyat: unke upnaam papon ke naam se janaa jaataa he, bharat ke rajya assam se aane vaale ek gayak, sangeetkaar or ricard nirmaataa hai. 
 enditivi good times ke saath hue ek saakshaatkaar main papon ne kaha tha ki wo duniya bhar kii yatra karnaa or duniya ke vibhinn sangeet samarohon main prastuti dena chaahate he. vah duniya bhar ke vibhinn sangeetkaaron se mil kar duniya bhar ke vibhinn ilaakon ke sangeet ko siikhnaa or in pratibhavan sangeetkaaron ke saath milakar adbhut or sundar sangeet rachana karnaa chaahate hai. papon dwaara rachit unka adhikansh shuruaati sangeet prachlit sangeet se puri tarah se alag tha. yah purvi lok sangeet tha or mukhyat: asamiya bhashaa main tha. unke dwaara rachit pehla hindi elbum 'da story so for' tha.
