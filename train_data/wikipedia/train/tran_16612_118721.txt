 rail shabdaawalee main tikona jankshan, rail patariyon kii ek tikona aakaar kii vyavastha he jiske prayek kone par ek pankh yaa switch hota he. railmarg kii mukhya line par sthit rail jankshan jahaan do rail line milti hai vahaan par ise gadiyon dwaara patari badalane ke liye yani ek patari se duusari patari par jane ke liye istemaal kiya ja saktaa he.
 ticone jankshan ko railway upkaran ko modane ke liye bhi istemaal kiya ja saktaa he. ticone jankshan kaa prayog karke kisi ingine yaa railway vaahan ko usee disha main modaa ja saktaa he jis disha se wo aayaa tha. jahaan par ticone jankshan ko mukhya rup se disha parivartan ke liye banaaya jaataa he vahaan jankshan banane main prayukt ek yaa ek se adhik patariyaan aam taur par ek stub siding hoti he.
