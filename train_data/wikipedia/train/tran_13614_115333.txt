 dashavikalik acharya shayyambhav kii niryuhanakruti he, ve shrutakewali the, unhone vibhinn purvon se dashavikalik niryuhan kiya. isamen 10 adhyay hai, isaki rachana vikaal main purna hui thi. dashavikalik main mukhya rup se dharmaka swaroop sadhu kii bhikshacharya, shramanya kii purv bhoomika bhashashuddhi, vinay samadhi aadi kaa sundar vivechan hua he.
