 jo vanaspatiyaan lata kehalaatin hai jo swayan urdhvadhar disha main khadi nahin rah saktin. inke tane patale or kamjor hote hai or itane lambe hote hai ki ye swayan khadi nahin hoti balki kisi anya vriksh, diwaar, jameen aadi par pasarte hue vruddhi karatin hai. angoor, lowki, kaddu aadi lataaon ke kuch udhaaharan hai.
