 sanjeev-darshan hindi filmon kii sangeetkaar jodi he. jodi kaa naam do bhaaiyon, sanjeev rathod or darshan rathod ke pehle naamon se liya gaya he. sanjeev or darshan hindi film sangeet nirdeshak shravan rathod ke putr hai, jinhone swayan sangeet nirdeshak nadeem ke saath nadeem-shravan ke rup main jodi banaai thi. gayak vinod rathod or sangeetkaar or gayak rup kumaar rathod unke chachaa hai. unhone sabase pehle man ke liye sangeet diya tha.
 iske alaava unhone diwane, hamaara dil aapke paas he, khilaadi 420, rishte, ye raste hai pyaar ke, grand masti or en ech 10 sareekhee film kaa sangeet diya he.
