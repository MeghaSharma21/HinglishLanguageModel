 assi ghat, asighat athwa keval assi, pracheen nagari kaashi kaa ek ghat he. yah ganga ke baayen tat par uttar se dakshin faili ghaaton kii shrunkhlaa main sabase dakshin kii ore antim ghat he | iske paas kai mandir ore akhaade hai | asighat ke dakshin main jagannatha mandir he jahaan prativarsh mela lagata he |
 iskaa namakaran assi naamak pracheen nadi ke ganga ke saath sangam ke sthal hone ke kaaran hua he. puranic katha he kii yuddh main vijay praapt karne ke baad durga mata ne durgakund ke tat par vishram kiya tha ore yaheen apni asi chhod di thi jis ke girane se asee nadi utpann hui | asee or ganga kaa sangam vishesh rup se pavitra mana jaataa he | kaashi ke paanch tirthon main se yah ek he | 
 iske paas hi nanakpanthiyon kaa ek akhaada he ore sameep hi shivaji kaa ek mandir he | asee sangam ghat kaa jirnodhdhar kar aadhunik sajaavat evam paryaton ke anuroop bana diya gaya he. yahan paryatak saayam kaal ganga aarti kaa anand lete he. naveen rasta nagava se hokar sidhe lanka ko jodatii he. saayam kaal yahan se sampoorn kaashi ke ghaaton kaa avlokan kiya ja saktaa he. videshi paryatak yahan vishesh rup se is sthaan ko kaaphi pasand karte he,kaaran yahan kaa vaataavaran kaashi ke saanskrut viraasat ke saath saste hotel vidyaman hai.vartmaan main mere sahpaathee pramod ji mishra kaa "subah e banaras" kaarykram se asee ghat main advithiya sundar vaataavaran kaa nirmaan hua he. saayam sandhya ke madhyam se ack manamohak vaataavaran kaa nirmaan hota he,saath aap ganga aarti kaa bhi anand le sakate hai .
