 
 marco polo ek italvi vyapaaree, khojakarta or rajdoot tha. uskaa janm venice ganraajya main madhya yug ke ant main hua tha. apane pita, nicholas polo or apane chachaa, maatayo, ke saath vah resham maarg kii yatra karne vaale sarvapratham europiyanon main se ek tha. usne apni yatra 1272 main liasus bandargah se prarambh kii thi. unki china samet, purv kii yatra kaa vistrut prativedan hi lambey samay tak pashchim main asia ke bare main jaankaree dene vaala strot rahaa he.marcopolo venice nivasee italvi yatri tha jis ’ madhyakaalin yatriyon kaa rajkumaar' kii oopaadhi di gai . iskaa vrutant ’da book of sar-marcopolo’ ke naam se hai, jo tatkaaleen bharat ke arthik itihaas kii dshti se mahatvapurn hai.
 unka yatra maarg is prakaar tha: armenia se hote hue ve turki ke uttar main gaye.
