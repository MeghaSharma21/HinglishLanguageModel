 rajbhavan dehradun bharat ke uttrakhand rajya ke rajyapal kaa aadhikarik nivas he. yah rajya kii rajdhani dehradun main sthit he. uttrakhand ke vartmaan rajyapal krishnakanth paul hai.
 uttrakhand rajya ke gathan ke parinamswaroop rajbhavan kii sthapana asthyi rup se bijapur house, new cant rod, dehradun main kii gai. tatpashchaat circuit house dehradun ko rajbhavan main badalakar uttrakhand ke pratham rajyapal surajeet singh barnaala ko 25 disambar 2000 ko iskaa pratham nivasee banaaya gaya. circuit house jise ab rajbhavan ke naam se janaa jaataa he, kaa nirmaan san 1902 main kiya gaya tha. us samay iskaa naam ’court house’ hua karata tha, jahaan tatkaaleen sanyukt praant ke british governor apane dehradun bhraman ke dauraan praayah nivas karte the. swatantrata prapti ke baad bharat ke pratham pradhanmantri jawaharlal nehru jab bhi dehradun aate the to yaheen thaharte the. samay-samay par bharat ke raashtrapati evam pradhanmantri is aitihaasik imaarat main nivas kar chuke hai. rajbhavan dehradun samudr tal se 2305 feet kii oonchaai par sthit he.
 yadhyapi rajbhavan ko 25 disambar 2000 ko circuit house main sthaanantarit kar diya gaya tha, kintu rajyapal sachivalay kaa sanchaalan bijapur house se hi hota rahaa. rajya ke tisare rajyapal shri bi. el. joshi dwaara rajbhavan parisar main rajyapal sachivalay tathaa prekshagrih ke navnirmit bhawanon kaa uddhaghatan dinaank 27 july 2009 ko kiya gaya. kuch samay pashchaat rajbhavan parisar main rajyapal kaa paden awaas nirmit kiya gaya, jiskaa uddhaghatan shrimati margaret alva dwaara dinaank 14 april 2010 ko kiya gaya tha. puraani imaarat jo mahamahim rajyapal kaa awaas hua karata thi, ko rajbhavan atithi grih ke rup main parivartit kiya gaya.
 rajbhavan parisar ke chaaron ore 160 acre main vistrut vanachhadit bhoomi he jisamen vibhinn prakaar ke vanaspati prajati tathaa pashu-pakshi paae jate hai. rajbhavan dehradun vishaal lon, bonsai garden, tathaa vibhinn prakaar kii pushp prajatiyon se yukt he.
 rajbhavan prekshagrih vibhinn mahatvapurn avasaron jaise shapath-grahan samaaroh, seminar, pustak-vimochan tathaa vibhinn saanskrithik kaaryakramon ityadi ke sanchaalan kaa vishesh sthal he.
