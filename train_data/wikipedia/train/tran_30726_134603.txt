 
 chaay mahatvapurn bagan fasal he. green tii or blike tii ek hi paudhe se milti hai. blike va green tii main samaan maatra main flevnayds paae jate hai. haalanki donon tarah kii chaay main paae jane vaale alag-alag prakaar ke flevnayds maujuud hote hai, jinaki karypranali bhi alag-alag hoti he. 
 bharat main 436057 hectare main chaay kii kheti hoti he. jisamen se 87993 hectare dakshin bharat main he. assam pashchim bengal, tamilnadu or kerala 8.44 pratishat) pramukh chaay utpaadak rajya hai. tripura, karnataka, himachal, uttar pradesh, sikkim, bihar, manipur, urisa, nagaland or arunachal pradesh main bhi chaay ugaayi jaati he. bharat main chaay udyog 150 se adhik varsh purana he jisase 6000 karod rupe kaa rajasav praapt hota he. is udyog se 10 lakh logon ko pratyaksh rojgaar miltaa he. jin par 40 lakh log ashrith hai. bharat main chaay kaa utpaadan 1947 main 25 karod kigra. tha jo 1999 main 80.5 karod kigra. ho gaya.
