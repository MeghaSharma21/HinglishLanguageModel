 pratyyojit vidhaan un kanoonon ko kehete hai jo karypalika dwaara banaaye jate hai. inhen 'dwitiyak vidhaan' bhi kehete hai. iske vipareet, vidhayika dwaara banaaye vidhaanon ko praathmik vidhaan kehete hai.
 praathmik vidhaan ek vistrut ruparekha yaa siddhanth pesh karte hai or karypalika ko adhikaar dete hai ki vah apni aavashyaktaa ke anusaar niyam bana sake. isi ke aadhaar par karypalika apane liye upyukt vidhaan banaati he jinhen 'pratyyojit vidhaan' kehete hai.
