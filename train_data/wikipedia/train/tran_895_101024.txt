 major dhyanchand rashtriya stadium, bharat kii rajdhani dilli main rajpath par sthit ek khel stadium he. yah mukhyatah ek maidani hockey stadium he, tathaa isaki kshamta 25000 darshakon kii he. is maidan par hockey vishwacap 2010 kaa aayojan kiya gaya tha. 
 iskaa naam prasiddh bhartiya hockey khiladi major dhyanchand ke naam par rakhaa gaya he.
 dhyanchand rashtriya stadium 1933 main banaaya gaya evam iskaa naam irvin amphithieatre rakhaa gaya. iske vaastukar anthoni es damilo the. 1951 asian khedo ke samay iskaa naam badalakar rashtriya stadium rakh diya gaya.
