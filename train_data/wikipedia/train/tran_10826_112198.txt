 holkar vigyaan mahavidhyalay indaur kaa ek mahavidhyalay he jisamen vigyaan vishyon kii shiksha kii acchhee vyavastha he. yah mahavidhyalay indaur main agra-bombay maarg par bhavanrakuna chauraahey ke paas he.10 june 1891 main holkar vansh ke maharaja shivajiravaholkar ne isaki aadharshila rakhi.yah 36 ekad ke bhu bhaag main failaa hua he evam vartmaan main ganit evam jeevigyaan sankaay ke 10 se adhik vibhaag sanchalit he.yashavanth haal, red building,acadmic haal yahaan ke pramukh shikshan or saahitiyik centre he.vartmaan main yahaan par snaatak or stakottar ke 30 se adhik pathyakram sanchalit ho rahe hai.1985 main is mahavidhyalay ko adarsh kaa jabaki 1988 main yah swashaasi kaa darja diya gaya.varsh 2015-2016 main ise nek NACC dwaara 'e' grad pradaan kiya gaya he.vartmaan main lagbhag 4000 vidhyaarthee yahan adhyayanrat he.
