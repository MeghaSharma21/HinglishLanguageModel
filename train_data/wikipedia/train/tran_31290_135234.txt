 badury yaa beril aadhunik yug kaa mahatvapurn khanij he. isse berilium dhaatu nikaali jaati he, jo halaki kintu kathor tathaa drudh hoti he. at: iskaa upyog vayuyanon main kiya jaataa he. anya dhaatuon ke saath isaki anek mishradhatuen taiyaar kii jaati hai, jo vidyut, camera aadi udyogon main kaam aati hai. beril kii paardarshak kism ko 'panna' kehete hai, jo ek ratna pathar he tathaa jiskaa upyog aabhushanon main kiya jaataa he.
 iskaa sutara Be3Al26 he. beril khanij ko kshetra main saralata se pahachaanaa ja saktaa he. yah shatkoniya samudaay main crystalikrut hota he tathaa iske crystal prizmiya hote hai. iskaa rang nila, haraa, yaa halkaa pilaa hota he. kabhi kabhi yah safed rang main bhi miltaa he. isaki tut shankhabh, kathorta 7.5 se 8 tathaa aapekshik ghanatv 2.7 he.
 beril ke aarthik nikshep pegmetic shilaon main milte hai. bharat main yah khanij rajasthan, jharakhand tathaa nelor kii pegmetic shilaon se praapt kiya jaataa he. vishwa main beril utpaadan main bharat kaa sthaan doosraa he. parmanviya mahatv kaa hone ke kaaran iske utpaadan kankde gopneey hai.
 'beril' naam bhartiya bhashaaon se vyatpann hua he. ise prakrut main 'veruli', pali main 'velluri' or 'bheliru' tathaa sanskrit main 'vaidury' kehete hai. inhi shabdon se 'beril' naam niklaa he.
