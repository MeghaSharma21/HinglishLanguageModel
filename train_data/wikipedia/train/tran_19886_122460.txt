 vistrut arth main kurdistan se abhipraay us pradesh se he jahaan kurd log nivas karte hai. kurd kattar sunni musalmaan, yoddha, kushal ghudsavaar, banjaaraa jati ke log hai. yah pradesh anatolia ke dakshinpurv pahaado tathaa jaagrus shrenee ke uttarpashchim sthit he or turki, iran or iraq teen deshon main banta he. kurd log garmi main pashuon ke saath pahari charaagahon par chale jate hai. jaade main ghaatiyon main rahete he. inke kheme gaare, mitti, itan or lakdi ke bane hote hai. inkaa atithisatkar prasiddh he.
 simit arth main kurdistan iran ke ek ustan kaa naam he jo uttar main ajarbaijan, dakshin main kirman shah, purv main iraq kii seemaa or pashchim main gerus or hamadaan ke ustaanon se ghira he. iskaa mukhya nagar sinandaaj he. yahan kaa mukhya udyog galeechaa, oon or namada he. yahan kurd aabaadi rahati he or yah turki tathaa iraq kii seemaa ke kareeb he.
