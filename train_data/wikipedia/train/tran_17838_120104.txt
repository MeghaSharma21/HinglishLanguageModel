 tantrasangrah nilkantha somayaji dwaara sanskrit main rachit khagolashastreey granth he. yah granth 1501 i main purna hua. isamen 432 shloke hai jo aath adhyyon main vibhakt hai. is granth main grahon kii gati ke parmparik model ke sthaan par pariskrit model prastut kiya gaya he.
 isaki do tikaayen gyaat hai- pehali tantrasangrahavyakhya tathaa yuktibhaasha . tantrasangrah main nilkantha somayaji ne budh or shukr grahon kii gati kaa aryabhat dwaara prastut model ko punah saamne rakhaa. is granth main diya hua in do grahon ke kendra kaa sameekaran 17veen shatabdi main kepler ke pehle tak sabase shuddh maan detaa tha.
