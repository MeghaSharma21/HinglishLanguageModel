 saadhaaran avakal samikarnon ke hal kii sankhyatmak vidhiyaan un samikarnon kaa bhi hal de saktin hai jo vaishleshik reeti se sambhav nahin hote. kabhi-kabhi ise 'sankhyatmak samakalan' bhi kehete hai .
 iske liye bahut si vidhiyaan hai jinaki apane-apane gun or avagun hai. inase milane vaala hal yatharth nahin hota kintu sannikat maan detaa he. haan is hal ko yatharth hal ke jitna nikat le janaa aavashyak ho, utana nikat le jaaya ja saktaa he. 
 pratham order ke avakal sameekaran kaa hal vastutah ek aarmabhik maan samasya he jiskaa swaroop nimnlikhit hota he-
 is vidhi ko 'predictor-karactor vidhi' bhi kehete hai. isamen ek spasht vidhi dwaara pehle ek sannikat nikaalte hai or phir duusari aspasht vidhi kaa upyog karte hue adhik shuddh maan nikaala jaataa he.
