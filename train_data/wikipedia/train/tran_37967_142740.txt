 christiano ronaldo dos santos aviero,, jise aamtaur par christiano ronaldo ke naam se janaa jaataa he, ek portugali peshewar footbal khilaadi he jo real madrid ke liye khelte hai or portugal kii rashtriya team kaa captain hai. aaj ronaldo ko footbal kii behtareen yuva pratibhaaon main se ek mana jaataa he.
 christiano ronaldo kaa janm portugal main fanchal, madira main 5 farvari 1985 ko mata maaria dalores dos santos aviero or pita jose dinnis aviero ke yahan hua tha. christiano ronaldo kaa ek bhai, hugo or do bahan elma or katiya hai. christiano ronaldo ke naam kaa doosraa bhaag portugal main apekshaakrit durlabh he. uske mata pita ne uskaa yah naam america ke purv raashtrapati ronaald reagan ke naam par rakhaa tha kyonki vah apane pita ke pasandida abhinayta the or is namakaran ke peeche ronaald reagan kaa america kaa raashtrapati hona koi kaaran nahin tha. 2010 se irina shyaks ke saath sambandh main he.
