 divakar sharma sanskrit, hindi tathaa rajasthani bhashaaon ke vidwaan the. aapane sanskrit kii kaladhisnatak pariksha kota main uteern kii thi tathaa piech. di. kii oopaadhi rajasthan vishvavidhyalay se praapt kii thi. aapako sahitya, samskruti tathaa itihaas kaa pratibodhan apane pitashri vidyavachaspati vidyadhar shastri tathaa chachaaji professor dasharath sharma se hua.
 duunger college, bikaner main aap sanskrit ke praadhyaapak niyukt hue, is pad se aapaki seva nivritti 1991 main hui. iske pashchaat aapane sanskrit, hindi tathaa rajasthani ke protsaahan hetu stapith sanstha, hindi vishwa bharti anusandhan parishad kaa nirdesan kiya. parishad kii shodh patrika vishwambhra ke aap jivan paryant sampadak rahe. aapke nirdesan main kai sanskrit shodharthiyon ne piech. di. kii oopaadhi praapt kii.
 rajasthan ke sanskrit sahitya-srijan main bikaner shetra kaa yogadan aapkaa mukhya prakashit granth he. granth main bikaner shetra ke sanskrit sahitya kaa vivechan tathaa sanskrit sahitya ke itihaas kaa varnan he. vicitra patran aapaki dwitiya rachana he. dow dasharath sharma lekh-sangrah evam girdharisingh parihaar-vyaktitv evam krititv aapaki sahalekhith rachanaayen hai. dow dasharath sharma lekh-sangrah main dow sharma dwaara likhit chaar sau se adhik lekhon kii suchi he. girdharisingh parihaar-vyaktitv evam krititv main kavi kii kritiyon kaa vivaran tathaa vivechan he. iske atirikt dow divakar sharma ke mahatvapurn lekh kai shodh patrikaon main prakashit hue.
 hindi vishwa bharti anusandhan parishad kii tremasik shodh patrika vishwambhra ke aap sampadak rahe. rajasthani gyanpith kii rajasthani bhashaa patrika rajasthani ganga ke sampadan kaary se bhi aap sambandhit rahe. varada,vaichariki tathaa kala-darshan patrikaon ke paraamarsh mandal ke bhi aap sadsy rahe.
 rajasthan kii anek saanskrithik or saahitiyik sansthaaon ne aapako sammaan pradaan kiye.
 aap anek saanskrithik tathaa saahitiyik sansthaaon ke sadsy rahe.
