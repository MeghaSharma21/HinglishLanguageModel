 asita isa purv 6thavin shatabdi ke ek tapasvi sant the jinhone siddharth gautam ke janm par unke asaadhaaran hone kii bhavishyavaanee kii thi. aisa kaha jaataa he ki asit or raja shuddhodhan bachapan ke saathi the. siddharth ke janm par ve pehali baar mahal padhaare. shishu kii adwitiya mahanata or ishwariya gunon se avgat asit jab navjaat shishu se mili to unhone shishu ke charnon main maathaa teka. shishu ke charan asit kii jataaon main fas gaye. mahatma ro pade. raja ne poocha aisi shubh ghadi main aap rote kyon hai? mahatma ne kaha main boodha ho gaya hoon or is hone vaale mahapurush ke aishwarya se vanchit rahoonga.
