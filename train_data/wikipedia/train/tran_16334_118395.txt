 2017-18 ranjai trophy ko ranjai trophy kaa 84 waan satr hona he, jo bharat main pratham shrenee cricket toornament kaa premier he. pichhlaa satr tatasth sthanon par khelaa gaya tha is sizan ke liye, gharelu captainon ke saath tatasth sthal pareekshan ke baad lokapriy nahin hone ke baad bhartiya cricket niyantran board ghar or dur sthanon par judane ke liye waapas jane par sahamat hue. haalanki, toornament ke naak out charan ko tatasth sthanon par khelaa janaa jari rahegaa. pichhali sizan se duusare badlaav teen se chaar se samooh kii sankhya main vruddhi hogi, saath hi group stage main chah phatakars khilne vaale prayek team ke saath. prayek samooh kii sheersh do timen quarter final main pragati karegi.
 sijan 6 oktober 2017 ko shuru karne ke liye nirdhaarit he, 7 disambar 2017 ko quarter final ke saath, 17 disambar 2017 ko semifinal, or final 29 disambar 2017 se 2 janvari 2018 tak yojit kiya gaya. gujarat pichhali champion hai.
