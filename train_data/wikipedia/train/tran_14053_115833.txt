 
 kustanay​ praant madhya asia ke qaazakhstan desh kaa ek praant he. isaki rajdhani bhi kustanay​ naam kaa shahar hi he. iske alaava rudaneey bhi is rajya kaa ek anya pramukh shahar he.
 kustanay praant ural pahaado ke paas or rus kii seemaa par sthit he. yahan kii bhoomi zyaadaatar samtal he or iske kaafii bhaag main bahut hi upjau kaale rang kii cheranogem mitti he. bahut se nadi-jharne is se guzarate hai. tobol nadi, jo irtish nadi kii ek mahatvapurn upanadi he, isi praant se shuru hokar uttar ko rus kii taraf bahati he. soobe main 5000 se adhik jhilein gini gai hai. uttar main chid ke van vistrut hai or dakshin main stapi ke ghaas-jhaad ke maidan faile hue hai.
 1950 ke dashak main, jab qaazakhstan soviet sangh kaa hissaa tha, to soviet sangh main anaaj kii bahut kami hui. us jamaane main soviet vyavastha main adhiktar anaaj rus ke upjau kshetron main ugaaya jaataa tha. soviet netaa nikita khrushchev​ ne elaan kiya kii anaaj kii paidaavaar badhaane ke liye madhya asia ke stapi ilaake kii zameenon par nae sire se kheti shuru kii jaaegi. is abhiyaan kaa naam 'nai dharti' rakhaa gaya. baari maatr main rus or ukraine se log uttari qaazakhstan ke nae kheton main aa based jis se sthaneey samudaayon kaa mishran bhi badal gaya. yahi kaaran he ki kustanay praant jaise ilaaqon main roosi logon kii bahutayat he.
 san 2006 kii janaganana ke anusaar yahan ke 33.8% log kazakh, 41.7% log roosi or 3.9% german samudaay ke hai. inke alaava yahan belarusi or tartar samudaay bhi basate hai. soviet sangh ke khatm ho jane ke baad yahan se roosi or german log dhire-dhire rus or germany ja rahen hai jis se inaki jansankhya is praant main ghat rahi he.
