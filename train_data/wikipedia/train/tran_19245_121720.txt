 bhashashastra main vrihad evam susangathit paath ko pathasangrah yaa corpus kehete hai. aaj ke electranik yug main pathasangrah ko electranik praaroop main sangrahit kiya jaataa he evam sanganak dwaara isaki bahuvidh jaanch-padtaal evam prasanskaran kiya jaataa he. is prakaar kaa pathasangrah sankhyikeey vishleshan karne, pariklpana-pareekshan, shabdon ke prayog kii aavriti nikaalane tathaa bhashaayi niyamon kii jaanch ke liye prayukt hote hai.
