 saser muzataag bharat ke jammu or kashmir rajya ke laddakh kshetra main sthit ek parvatamala he, jo mahaan karakoram parvatamala kii purvatam upashrenee he. dakshin, purv or purvottar main ise shyoke nadi gherti he jo shrenee ke dakshinpurv kone main mode leti he. pashchim main nubra nadi ise kailash shrenee se alag karti he. saser laa naamak pahari darra ise uttar ke rimo muzataag naamak shrenee se alag karata he.
 yah saser muzataag ke un parvaton kii suchi he, jo 7,200 meter se unche hai or 500 meter se adhik kii sthalakritik udagrata rakhte hai. is se kam udagrata rakhane vaale shikhar swatantra parvat nahin maane jate balki aasapaas ke anya parvaton ke hi shikhar maane jate hai .
