 kasauli bharat ke himachal pradesh praant kaa ek shahar he. samudri tal se 1795 kii oonchai par sthit kasauli himachal pradesh kaa ek chota parvatiy sthal he. yah shimla ke dakshin main 77 kilo meter kii duuri par sthit he or toy train par jo samay shimla kii pahadiyon ke paas pahunchane par kasauli dikhaai detaa he. apni safai or sundarataa ke kaaran mashhoor kasauli main badi sankhya main paryatak aate hai. ise kabhi kabhaar chota shimla kaha jaataa he or yah parvatiy sthaan fer, rhododendron, akhrod, ok or wilo ke liye prasiddh he. kasauli main 1900 ke dauraan pashchar santhaan kii sthapana kii gai jahaan anti rebies tikaa, pagal kute ke kaatane kii dawa ke saath hydro phobia rog kaa ilaaj bhi kiya jaataa he. kasauli prasiddh lekhak raskin bond kaa janm sthaan bhi he.
