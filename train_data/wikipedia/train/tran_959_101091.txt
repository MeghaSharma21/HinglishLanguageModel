 4 se 6 july 2013 ke madhya macau main ifa awards kaa aayojan kiya gaya. ranbir kapur-priyanka chopra abhinit film ‘barfi!’ ne 14 wein vaarshik ifa awards main takneekee khand main nau puraskaar hasil kiye. anuraag basu nirdeshit is film ne sarvshereshth cinemetography, sarvshereshth screenple, sarvshereshth production dizin, sarvshereshth sound dizin, sarvshereshth sang ricarding, sarvshereshth sound mixing, sarvshereshth becoground score, sarvshereshth costum digining or sarvshereshth mechap ke liye puraskaar hasil kiya. filmkaar anuraag kashyap kii ‘gangs of vasepur’ sarvshereshth ection or sarvshereshth dialaag ke do puraskaar hasil karne ke saath duusare sthaan par rahi.
