 akriyavaad buddha ke samay kaa ek prakhyat darshanik matvaad. mahavir tathaa buddha se purv ke yug main bhi is mat kaa bada bolbalaa tha. iske anusaar na to koi karm he, na koi kriya or na koi praytn. iskaa khandan jain tathaa buddha dharm ne kiya, kyonki ye donon praytn, kaary, bal tathaa veery kii satta main vishwaas rakhte hai. isi kaaran inhen karmwaad yaa kriyavaad kehete hai. buddha ke samay purnakshyap naamak acharya is mat ke prakhyat anuyaayi batalaaye gaye hai.
