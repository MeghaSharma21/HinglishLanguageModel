 maharana bakhtawarsingh madhyapradesh ke dhar jile ke amajhera kasbe ke shaasha the, jinhone 1857 ke swatantraaa sangram main madhya pradesh main angrejon se sangharsh kiya. lambey sangharsh ke baad chhalpurvak angrejon ne unhen kaid kar liya. 10 farvari 1858 main indore ke maharaja yashavanth chikitsaalay parisar ke ek neem ke ped par unhen faansee par lataka diya. madhyapradesh sarkaar ne amajhera sthit rana bakhtawarsingh ke kile ko rajya sangrakshit imaarat ghoshit kiya he.jila mukhyalay se 27 km indore a hamadabaad rajya maarg par hai.
