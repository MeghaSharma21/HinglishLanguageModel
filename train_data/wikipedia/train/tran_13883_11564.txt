 edimaral surish mehta oktober 31, 2006 se agast 31, 2009	tak bharat ke nausenadhyaksh rahe. bharat kii nausena ke ve 19 wein edimaral chif the.unhone arun prakaash se yah padbhaar grahan kiya tha tathaa unke pashchaat nirmal kumaar verma is pad par aae.
 edimaral surish mehta kaa janm 18 agast 1947 main hua tha. vah neshnal dephence academy ke chaatr rah chuke he.
 unhone apane karier kaa prarambh ienes vikrant main ek flit air aam ke rup main kii thi.
 edimaral surish mehta ko 1995 main ati vishisht seva padak tathaa 2005 main param vishisht seva padak mil chukaa he.
