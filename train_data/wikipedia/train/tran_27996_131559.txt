 
 vang yaa ranga yaa tin ek rasinic tatva he. latin main iskaa naam stannam he jisase iskaa rasinic pratik Sn liya gaya he. yah aavart sarini ke chaturth mukhya samooh kii ek dhaatu he. vang ke das sthaayee samasthanik praapt hai. inke atirikt chaar anya radioactive samasthanik bhi nirmit hue hai.
 vang kii mishradhatu kaa upyog aaj se 5,000 varsh purv bhi hota tha. vang dhaatu kii bani sabase pracheen bottle misr kii sthit samadhi main pai gai, jo lagbhag isa se 1,500 varsh purvakaal kii he. vang ke ayask misr main nahin milte. is kaaran vahaan yah dhaatu avashya hi baahar se ai hogi. isa se lagbhag 300 varsh purv england main vang ke dhaatukarm ke namoonay milte hai. yahan vang kii khaane thim. us samay yah dhaatu rome main jaati thi. dakshini america ke adivaasiyon ko vang kii mishradhaatuon kaa gyaan tha.
 bharat main sindhu ghati kii sabhyataa ke kaal ke praapt dhaatu padaarthon main vang paaya gaya he. aisa anumaan he ki us samay vang iran se aata tha. isa se paanch shatabdi purv ayurveda kaal main sushrut main trapu tathaa vagbhatta ke ashtangaruday main bhi vang ke yogic kaa varnan aayaa he. rasratnasamucchy main vang dhaatu tathaa vaig bhasma donon ke gunon kii vivechna kii gai he.
 vang mukt avasthaa main praapt nahin he. prithvi kii sataha par isaki maatra lagbhag 40 gram prati ton he. iske pramukh ayask hai : caciterite, or sulphide. malayesia, thailand, indonesia, congou, nigeria tathaa bolivia main vang kii mukhya khaane hai.
 vang ke ayask main praaia: 1 se 5 pratishat tin oxide upasthit rahata he. is kaaran ise sandrit karnaa aavashyak he. ucch ghanatv tathaa achumbakeey gunon ke dwaara hi caciterite kaa sandran karte hai. sandrit ayask ko koyale se mishrit kar paravartanee athwa vatya bhatthi main rakhkar apchayan karne se vang dhaatu praapt hoti he. ashuddh vang ke vishuddh karne kii anek vidhiyaan hai.
 vang shwet rang kii komal tanya dhaatu he. iske taar saralata se khinche ja sakate hai, parantu vang kii chadar modane par katakataane kii dhwani hoti he, jise vang kii chillaahat kehete hai. dhaatu ke do apararupi roopantaran hai. saamaanya avasthaa main yah shwet rang kii dhaatu he, parantu yadi vang ko adhik kaal tak 13ï¿½ sein. taap se neeche rakhaa jaae, to yah bhurabhura evam bhure rang ke churna main parivartit hokar vang kaa doosraa aparrup banaata he, jo nimna ta par sthaayee he.
 vang ke kuch bhautik sthiraank ye hai : sanket Sn, parmaanu sankhya 50, parmaanu bhaar 118.69, galnaank 231.9 degree sein., kvanank 2,272 degree sein., ghanatv 7.31 gram prati ghan semi., parmaanu vyas 3.16 angstrom, vidyut pratirohakata 11.5 microom-senmi. tathaa ynan vibhav 7.3 evo .
 saamaanya taap par vang vaayu dwaara prabhavit nahin hota, parantu ucch taap par usapar oxai kii parat jam jaati he. shwet taap par vang vaayu main jal kar dioxide, banaata he. yah tapt avasthaa main pile rang kaa or saamaanya taap par shwet rang kaa padaarth he. vang tanu amlon main dhire dhire ghulkar,, yogic banaata he or hydrogen mukt karata he. dhaatu par sandra nitric amla kii abhikriya dwaara jalayukt stanic oxide athwa metastanic amla banataa he. vang kshareey vilyan main ghulkar stanet banaata he, jiske falswaroop hydrogen mukt ho jaataa he.
 vang ke do prakaar ke yogic gyaat hai : ek stannas, jisamen vang kii sanyojaktaa 2 he or doosraa stanic, jisamen vang kii sanyojaktaa chaar rahati he. iske do oxide, stans oxai, or stanic oxai,, hote hai. gandhak ke saath vang ko garam karne se stans sulphide, praapt hota he. stanic salphid bhi banataa he.
 helojan ke saath vang stans hellide or stanic hellide banaata he. vang ke chloride rangabandhak ke rup main resham rangane main kaam aate hai. yah nitrogen, hydrogen or phosphorus ke saath bhi yogic banaata he. iske nitrate or phosphet asthaayee hote hai. chlorostainic amla kaa ammonium lavan, resham rangane main kaam aata he.
 vang anek upasahsanyojaktaa yogic banaata he.
 vang mulammaa karne or mishradhaatuon ke nirmaan main kaam aata he. lohe par vang se kalai karne par usapar na murchaa hi lagata or na amlon kaa jaldi asar padataa he. kaansa isaki mahatv kii mishradhatu he. khaadya padaarthon ke dibbon main vang kii kalai karne se ve jald aakraant nahin hote. vang ke anek yogic vastra udyog, rangaai, kaanch evam chini mitti ke paatr ke udyogon main kaam aate hai.
