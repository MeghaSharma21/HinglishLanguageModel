 baag-e-babar  mughal samraat babar kaa makbara parisar he. yah kabul aane vaale paryatakon kaa sabase pasandida sthaan he. isi baag main pratham mughal badshaah babar kii kabr he. yah park kai bagichon ko milakar banaaya gaya he. is baag kii bahari diwaar kaa punarnirmaan 2005 i. main puraani style main hi kiya gaya tha. is diwaar ko 1992-96 i. main yuddh ke dauraan kshati pahunchi thi. yah baag kabul ke chechalston kshetra main sthit he. babar kii mrityu ke baad unhen agra main dafnaaya gaya tha. lekin babar kii yah ichha thi ki unhen kabul main dafnaaya ja. is kaaran shershah suri ne unki icchaanusaar unhen kabul laakar is baag main dafnaaya gaya. isi baag kii prerana se bharat main mughal baadshahon ne kai baagon kaa nirmaan karavaaya tha.
 baag ke bichombich ek nahar he jisamen jal kaa anwarat pravaah ho rahaa he. paas main hi babar kaa makbara he, jahaan par goliyon ke nishaan 1990 ke dashak main hue grih yuddh kaa parinaam hai. yah nahar is baag ko do hisson main baantati he, ek taraf kaa hissaa pariwaron ke liye hai or doosraa sirf yuva purushon ke liye. duusari taraf dariyon par baithe pariwaron ke liye hari-hari ghaas ke ye baag picnic kii jagah he. gumti-phirti kabuli mahilaayein beshak kaale hijaab ko odhe rahati hai, baavjood iske unke chehre aasaani se pahachaane ja sakate hai.
