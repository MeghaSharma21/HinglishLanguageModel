 madhyapradesh janajaatiy sangrahalay ,shyamala hills bhopal main sthit he, iskaa locarpan bharat ke raashtrapati shri pranav mukherjee ne kiya tha. yah sangrahalay madhyapradesh shashan samskruti vibhaag dwaara sanchalit hota he is sangrahalay main madhyapradesh main nivasarat janajaatiy samoohon kii kala samskruti parampara or jivan upyogi shilp chitron rahan sahan tathaa reeti rivaaj rivaajon kaa chitron murtiyon evam pradarshnon ke madhyam se darshan karaayaa gaya he. saath hi samay-samay par yahaan par kai saanskrithik aayojan, kaaryshaalaayein aadi yojit kii jaati hai jisase ki logon ko adivasee samaaj kii manyataon kala samskruti ke bare main gyaan mil sake. kai pradarsh joki mul rup se adivasee samskritiyon main upyog kiye jate hai, yahaan pradarshit kiye gaye hai. 
 bhaaria yah mukhyatah jabalpur evam chhindwara jile main nivasarat hai. 
 in sabhi janajatiyon ke saanskrithik tathaa saamaajik paridrishy ko chitron pradarshan evam kalakritiyon dwaara atyant prabhavi dhang se is sangrahalay main pradarshit kiya gaya he.
