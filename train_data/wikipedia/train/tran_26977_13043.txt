 ashokamithran tamil bhashaa ke lekhak va upanyaaskaar the. unki kriti main 200 laghu kathaayen, 8 upanyaas, 15 anya lambii kathaayen shaamil hai. unki bahut-si laghu kathaayen angreji, hindi, malayalam, telugu or anya bhashaaon main anuvaadit kii gai he. unhen varsh 1996 main unke laghu kathaon ke sangrah appavin sinehidar ke liye sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha.
 varsh 1931 main sikanderabad main janmen ashokamithran varsh 1952 main chennai chale gaye the. jiske baad ve tamil sahitya ke ek prabhaavshaalee sahityakar ke rup main ubhare.ve sankshipt or sookshm hasya ke liye jane jate the.unhone 1960 ke dashak main apana saahitiyik naam ashokamithran grahan kiya tha. varsh 2014 main tamilnadu kii nivartmaan mukhyamantri jayalalita ne tamil sahitya main unke yogadan ke liye unhe 'thiru ve kaa' puraskaar pradaan kiya tha.
