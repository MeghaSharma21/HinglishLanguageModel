 sindhughosh shrenee kii pandubbiyaan bhartiya nausena dwaara prayog kii jane vaali deazal-bijli chalit pandubbiyaan hai. ye rus or bharat ke madhya hue samjhaute ke tahat bani hai. 
 
 in pandubbiyon kii visthaapan kshamta 3000 ton he. adhiktam gaharaai 300 meter va adhiktam gati 18 note he. 53 navikon ke saath yah 45 din tak akele oparate kar sakati he.
