 prasannataa maanvon main pai jane vaali bhaawanaaon main sabase sakaaratmak bhaavana he. iske hone ke vibhinn kaaran ho sakate hai:
 in kaarnon ke alaava bhi kuch log swabhaav se hameshaa prasann rahane kii koshish karte hai or jivan kii har chunauti ko sakaaratmak rup se sweekaar karte hai. hansee or muskuraahat prasannataa ke lakshan hai. isi ko khushi bhi kehete hai.
 buddhijiviyon kii raay he ki koi bhi un aatanrik karkon jaise aatmavishvaas, dhyaan, kendrit hona or santushti ko aasaani jagaa saktaa he jo prasannataa ke liye aavashyak he. yahi vah tatv hai jo ki sampurna prasannataa ko jagaa sakegenge or yah maanav kshamta or duusare manushyon kii madad kii bhaavana ko bhi jagaa sakate hai.
