 samasya-purti bhartiya sahitya main prachlit vishesh vidha he jisamen kisi chand main koi kavita kaa ansh diya hota he or us chand ko puraa karnaa hota he. yah kala pracheen kaal se chali aa rahi he. yah kala chaunsath kalaon ke antargat aati he. samasyapurti visheshat: sanskrit sahitya main bahut prasiddh he. 
 aise bhi udhaaharan hai jisamen kuch vidwaanon ne kalidas dwaara rachit meghdootam kii prayek kavita kii pratham pankti jyon kaa tyon 'samasya' ke rup main lekar swayan uski purti kii he.
