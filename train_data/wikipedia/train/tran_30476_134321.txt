 sonkar bharat main madhya pradesh or uttar pradesh ke bundelkhand kshetra main paae jane vaala ek hindu jati he. yah khatic samudaay kii ek up-jati he. aitihaasik rup se, sonkar rangiya kaa ek samudaay the. ve kai alag-alag vanshon main vibhajit hai, mukhya rup se magariyaa, gotiya or jaraalia hai. sonkar samudaay hindi ke bundelkhand kii boli bolte hai. uttar pradesh main, ve mukhya rup se hamirpur, kanpur, jonpur or jhaansi jilon main paae jate hai. ve saanp kii puja karte hai or unhen apane sangrakshak devataaon ke rup main maanate hai.
 chhattisgarh main, sonkar ek alag samudaay hai. ve bharat ke anya pichde virgon ke antargat aate hai.
 16veen loksabhaa chunaavon ke aadhaar par bharat main khatic samaaj ke 7 saansad hai. jinke naam manoj rajauria, vinod sonkar, doctor udit raaj, virendra kumaar khatic, neelam sonkar, doctor bholaa singh hai.
