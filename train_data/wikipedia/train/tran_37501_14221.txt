 13 janvari 1977 ko mumtaz ahamed khan ne amaanat ko-oparative bank kii sthapana kii or 26 saal ke chote-se samay main karnataka kii pehali anusuchit shahari sarkari bank kaa darja praapt kiya. 440 karod rupaye kii kaaryshil puunji report kii gai he.
 bank keval vyapaarik laabh ke liye nahin balki seva kaa bhi bhav rakhata he jiskaa saboot haridwar seva samiti ke antargat chal rahi isaki shakha he.
 amaanat bank ke adhyaksh or congress ke rajyasabha saansad ke. rahamaan khan ne in aaropon kaa khandan kiya he ki anderworld don daaud ibrahim ne unke bank main 200 karod rupye jamaa karaae hai.
