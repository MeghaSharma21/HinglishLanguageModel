 achal kumaar jyoti bharat ke mukhya chunaav ayukta he. dinaak 06 june 2017 ko mukhya chunaav ayukta ke pad kaa kaarybhaar grahan kiya. jyoti 7 may 2015 ko teen sadasyeey chunaav ayog main niyukt kiya gaya tha. ve bhartiya prashasnic seva main 1975 batch ke gujarat rajya ke adhikari hai. 03july 2017 ko bharat ke mukhya chunaav ayukta niyukt hue 06 july 2017 ko kaarybhaar grahan karenge jyoti ko bharat kaa 21waan mukhya chunaav ayukta niyukt kiya gaya he.
 uske pita kishor chand jyoti nai dilli sthit army head quarter main chif staff afasar or maa suhagaranee jyoti grahani thim. ye apane do bhai or ek bahin main sabase chote the. sabhi kaa janm dilli main hua or vahin shiksha praapt kii. inkaa paitruk awaas mitthapur ,jalandhar he jahaan inke bade bhai abhaya jyoti rahete hai.
 64 varshiya jyoti gujarat ke mukhya sachiv rah chuke hai. vah janvari 2013 main gujarat ke mukhya chunaav ayukta ke pad se ritair hue the. us samay pradhanmantri narendra modi gujarat ke mukhyamantri the. pehle ve gujarat ke mukhya sachiv the tathaa kandla port trust ke adhyaksh rah chuke hai. ve sardaar sarovar narmada nigam limited ke prabandh nideshak bhi rah chuke hai.
