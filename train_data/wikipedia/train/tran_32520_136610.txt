 bhoomi pednekar ek ubhartee hui bollywood abhinetri he. abhinay kaa kaam karne se pehle unhone yash raaj ke binar ke neeche sahaayak nirdeshak ke taur par kaam kiya tha. iske baad unhone usee company ke saath teen filmon ke samjhaute par hastakshar kiye. unhone varsh 2015 main release hui film dam laga ke haisha ke dwaara apana bollywood main padaarpan kiya. film ne release ke baad acha kaarobaar kiya or logo ne unke kaam kii kaaphi prashansa bhi kii. isi film ke liye unhen filmphere kii sarvshereshth debyue abhinetri ke award se sammanit bhi kiya ja chukaa he.
 bhoomi kaa janm 18 july 1989 ko mumbai main hua. unhone apni padhai mumbai main juhu sthitit arya vidya mandir main puri kii. unke pita maharashtra se hai or maan haryana se hai.
 unki ek film "dam laga ke haisha" farvari 2015 ko cinemagharon main pradarshit ho chuki he. is film main vah abhinayta ayushmaan khurana ke saath nazar ai thi. film main unhone ek shaadishuda aurat kaa kirdaar nibhaayaa tha. film ne bharat main acha kaarobaar kiya he. unhen is film ke liye "filmphere aword, producers gild film awords, screen awards, zee sine awards, stredst awards, big star antertenment awards or international film academy puraskaar sahit 6 puraskaar praapt hue hai.
 2017 main vah sah-abhinayta ayushmaan khurana ke saath film shubh mangal saavdhaan main nazar aa rahi hai. is film se purv svachh bharat ke mishan par bani film toilet: ek prem katha main bhi unka ahm kirdaar he. pednekar aglaa netfix anthology film last stories main joya akhtar ke segment main dikhaai die, jiske baad vah abhishek choube kii aparaadh film sonchiria main sushant singh rajput ke vipareet star hongi, jisamen vah ek daaku khelti he.
