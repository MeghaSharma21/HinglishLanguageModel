 robinson cruso, dainiel defo dwaara rachit ek upanyaas he. yah pehali baar 1719 main prakashit hua tha or kabhi kabhi ise angreji kaa pehla upanyaas mana jaataa he. yah pustak robinson cruso naamak ek angrej charitra, kii kaalpanik aatmakatha he, jo venezuela ke nikat ek doordaraj ke ushnkaribandheey dweep par 28 saal tak fansaa rahaa. in 28 saalon ke dauraan usne vahaan par vahaan ke mul nivaasiyon, bandhua or vidroh kaa saamana kiya or antat: use bachaa liya gaya.
