 sachin bhaumik hindi filmon ke patakatha lekhak the jinhen hindi cinema ke itihaas kaa sabase safal lekhak kaha gaya he. unhen visheshkar romani filmon ke lekhak ke rup main pahachaanaa jaataa he. unhone 100 se zyada filmon kii kahani likhi. sabase pehle sachin ne nargis abhinit lajvanti kii patakatha likhi thi. phir unhone anuraadhaa bhi likhi jisane sarvshereshth film kaa rashtriya film puraskaar jeeta tha. phir unke dwaara kai dashakon tak kai safal filmon kii kahani likhi gai jisamen gol maal, khel khel main, karma, main khilaadi tu anaadi, soljar, taal, koi mil gaya or krish shaamil he. 
 sachin dwaara nirdeshit rajesh khanna or sharmila tagore abhinit ek film raja jaani bhi ai thi. 1971 main unhone abhinetri kalpana mohan se vivaah bhi kiya tha lekin baad main unka talaak ho gaya.
