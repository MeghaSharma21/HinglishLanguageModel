 swadhinata ke svarnim ateet main jaanbaaji kaa amit adhyay ban chuke aadi vidrohi tantya bhil angreji daman ko dhwast karne vaali jid tathaa sangharsh kii misaal he. tantya bhil ke shaury kii chhabiyaan varsh 1857 ke baad ubharin. jananaayak tantya ne british hukumat dwaara gramin janata ke shoshan or unke moulik adhikaron ke saath ho rahe anyaay-atyaachaar kii khilaafat kii. dilachasp pahalu yah he ki swayan pratadit angrejon kii satta ne jananaayak tantya ko “indian robinhood’’ kaa khitaab diya. jananaayak tantya bhil ko varsh 1889 main phaansi de di gai.
