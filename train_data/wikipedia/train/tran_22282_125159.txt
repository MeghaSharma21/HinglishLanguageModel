 parl ek ucch stareey, saamaanya-prayojan, programing bhashaa he. iskaa vikaas laire wal dwaara san 1987 main unics screepting bhashaa ke antargat report kii prakriya ko aasaan banane ke liye kiya gaya tha. tab se, yah anek parivartano or sanshodhano se gujri he or programmeron ke bich vyapak rup se lokapriy hui he. parl anya programing bhashaaon jaise ki si, shell scripting, AWK or sed se samanta rakhti he. yah bhashaa paath prasanskaran ke liye shaktishaalee suvidhaayen pradaan karti he, wo bhi unics upkarnon kii data lambaai seemaa ke bina. parl ne apni parsing kshamtaaon ke kaaran 1990 ke dashak ke ant main ek CGI scrypting bhashaa ke rup main vyapak lokapriyata praapt kii. 
 CGI ke alaava parl, graphics programing, system prashasan, network programing, vitt, jaev soochna vigyaan or anya anuprayogon ke liye kaam main lee jaati he. apane lacheelepan or shakti ke kaaran parl ko programing bhashaaon kii "swiss sena ke chen-sa" kii sangya di gayi he. 
 san 1987 me programrar ke rup me vaol ne parl bhashaa par kaam karnaa shuru kiya tha. disambar 18, san 1987 me sanskaran 1.0 ko reeha kiya gaya. yah bhashaa kai saalo me mahaan rup se vistaarit hua. san 1988 me parl 2 ko nikaala gaya, jisame niyamit abhivyakti ingine kii suvidha thi. parl 3, san 1989 me reeha kiya gaya. yah bhashaa binary data kaa samarthan karata tha. multah parl sirf ek prisht kaa pralekhan tha. san 1991 me 'parl programing' naamak pustak ko prakashit kiya gaya. lekin vah pustak 'oont kitaab' ke naam se prasiddh hui. isi samay parl 4 ko nikaala gaya, taaki log pustak kii pralekhan ko samaj paye.sheeghra parl 5
 san 1993 me parl 4 ko kai rakrakhaav vijyapti se guzaranaa pada. is dauraan vaol ne parl 4 ko chhodkar parl 5 par kaam karnaa shuru kiya. iskaa dizine san 1994 tak taiyaar kiya gaya. usko is tarah banaaya gaya ki vah har manch par chal sake. isliye parl 4 ko parl 5 kaa praathmik, rakrakhaav, bandargah aavashyaktaa mana jaataa he. parl 5.000 ko 17 oktober 1994 ko jari kiya gaya. vah dubhashiya kaa phir se likhane ke or nai suvidhao ke jodne ke baraabar tha. mahatvapurn baat yah thi ki bhashaa badhaane ke liye tantra basharte the. is kaaran mul dubhashiya ko sthir banane kaa mauka mila. un dino se parl 5 sakriy viksit ho rahaa he.
 parl 5.001 ko 13 march 1995 ko jari kiya gaya. parl 5.002 ko 29 farvari 1996 ko nae suvidhao ke saath jari kiya gaya. yah suvidha lekhako ko parl-nirmaan dincharya banane me kaam ai. parl 5.003 ko 25 june 1996 ko suraksha se reeha kiya gaya. mahatvapurn ghatana yah thi ki parl 5 kaa itihaas bhashaa ke antarragat hua. oktober 26, 1995 ko parl kaa kosh nikaala gaya. san 2012 tak isame 9500 lekhak he. parl 5.004 ko 15 may 1997 ko jari kiya gaya jisame saarvbhaum vastuo ko apane kaksho ke saath joda gaya. vartmaan me, parl bhashaa kai parichaalan pranalio jaise microsoft par chalata he. parl 5.005 ko 22 july 1998 ko jari kiya gaya. isame kai vriddhio ko shaamil kiya gaya.
 parl 5.6 ko 22 march 2000 ko jari kiya gaya. isame bada parivartan string pratinidhitiv or bade file samarthan the. isko viksit karte samay yah nirnay liya gaya ki vah khulaa strot pariyojna rakhaa jaae. san 2000 me vaol ne suzav die, jisame parl 6 ke nirmaan me maardarshak bane. san 2001 me is par kaam shuru kiya gaya or aupchaarik pralekhan banaaya gaya. astitv main, parl 6 ek bhashaa kaa varnan ke rup me rah gaya. parl 5.8 ko 18 july 2002 ko jari kiya gaya. is bhaag me saare adhyatan ko joda gaya. isame nai karyanvayan, sankhyik sateekta aadi ko joda gaya. yah bhaag abhi tak kaa sabase lokapriy bhaag rahaa he. san 2004 may parl 6 kaa saransh banaaya gaya. or san 2006 me isko vaastaviktaa kaa rup diya gaya. taarikh 18 disambar 2007, parl 20 veen varshganth par parl 5.10.0 ko nikaala gaya, jo apane suvidha me parl 6 ke kareeb tha. november 2009 tak, masik vijyapti rahe he. vah ab parl 6 ke sabase purna karyanvayan rahaa he.
 12 april 2010 ko parl 5.12.0, 14 may 2011 ko parl 5.14, 20 may 2012 ko parl 5.16, 18 may 2013, parl 5.18 ko jari kiya gaya tha. in sab bhaago me nae tareekhe, nae karyanvayan or nae adhyatan kaa parichay diya gaya he.
 parl mul rup se "parl" namit kiya gaya tha. vaol apni bhashaa ko arth se jude naam dena chaahate the. vah daawa karte he ki unhone angreji shabdkosh main prayek teen- or chaar-akshari shabd par vichaar kiya tha. unhone bhashaa kaa naam apane patni ke naam par rakhane par bhi vichaar kiya tha. vaol ko pehle se vidyaman PEARL programing bhashaa ke bare me pataa chalane par unhone nen naam kii vartanee badal kar iske vartmaan swarup me kar di. vistaarit rup se parl ko angrezi me Practical Extraction and Report Language likhaa jaataa he.
 o'reilli media dwaara prakashit programing parl ke aavaran par oont ke chitra kaa ullekh he, jiske kaaran vah 'oont kitaab' ke naam se jaani jaati he. yah parl kaa anaupchaarik pratik mana jaataa he.
 parl foundation uski sahaayak companiyon ko license me jo ek vikalp kaa pratik he, ek pyaaj kaa he.
