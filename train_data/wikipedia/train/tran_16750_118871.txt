 saarangi ek gayaki pradhan bhartiya shastreey sangeet kaa vaadya yantra he. pracheen kaal main saarangi ghumkkad jatiyon kaa vaadya tha. iskaa pracheen naam sarinda tha, jo kalantar main saarangi hua. ek mat yah bhi he ki yah naam hindi ke sau or rang shabdon se milakar bana he jiskaa arth sau rangon vaala hota he. ise is prakaar dekh sakate he, bhartiya shastreey sangeet ke vaadyayantro main sau prakaar kii dhunon ko nikaalane vaala yah ek vaadyayantra he. 
 saarangi do prakaar kii prayog main laai jaati hai. ek sindhi saarangi va dusri gujrathan saarangi. sindhi saarangi thodi badi hoti he, vahiin gujrathan saarangi thodi choti hoti he jise gujarat main banaaya jaataa he.
