 jute, patson or isi prakaar ke paudhon ke reshe hai. iske reshe bore, dari, tamboo, tirpal, taat, rassiyaan, nimnakoti ke kapade tathaa kaagaj banane ke kaam aata he.
 'jute' shabd sanskrit ke 'jataa' yaa 'jute' se niklaa samajha jaataa he. europe main 18veen shatabdi main pehle-pahal is shabd kaa prayog miltaa he, yadhyapi vahaan is dravya kaa aayaat 18veen shatabdi ke purv se "paat" ke naam se hota aa rahaa tha.
 jute ke reshe saadharantaya chah se lekar das foot tak lambey hote hai, par vishesh avasthaaon main 14 se lekar 15 foot tak lambey paae gaye hai. turant kaa nikaala resha adhik majboot, adhik chamakdaar, adhik komal or adhik safed hota he. khulaa rakhane se in gunon kaa hraas hota he. jute ke reshe kaa viranjan kuch seemaa tak ho saktaa he, par viranjan se bilkul safed resha nahin praapt hota. resha ardratagrahi hota he. chah se lekar 23 prati shat tak nami reshe main rah sakati he.
 jute kii paidaavaar, fasal kii kism, bhoomi kii urvartaa, antaralan, kaatane kaa samay aadi, anek baton par nirbhar karte hai. capsularis kii paidaavaar prati acre 10-15 man or olytorius kii 15-20 man prati acre hoti he. acchhee jotai se prati acre 30 man tak paidaavaar ho sakati he.
 jute ke reshe se bore, hesian tathaa panking ke kapade banate hai. kaaleen, dariyaan, parade, gharon kii sajaavat ke saamaan, astr or rassiyaan bhi banati hai. danthal jalaane ke kaam aata he or usase barood ke koyale bhi banaae ja sakate hai. danthal kaa koyala barood ke liye acha hota he. danthal se lugdi bhi praapt hoti he, jo kaagaj banane ke kaam aa sakati he.
