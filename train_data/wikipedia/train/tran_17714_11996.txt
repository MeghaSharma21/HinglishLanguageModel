 vriksh kaa saamaanya arth aise paudhe se hota he jisamen shakhaayen nikali hon, jo kam se kam do-varsh tak jivit rahe, jisase lakdi praapt ho.
 vriksh kii ek jad hoti he jo praayah zamin ke andar hoti he, tathaa jad se nikalakar tana tathaa pattiyaan hawa main rahete hai. yah pradooshan kam karne main kargar siddh hua he. par lakadiyo tathaa zamin kii aavashyaktaaon ke kaaran log ise katate ja rahe hai.
