 
 tarang dairghy: 1 me se 100 mimi 
 para ucch aavruti, vah vidyut chumbakeey vikiran kii pattee hoti he, jisamen 300 MHz se 3GHz kii aavritiyaan hotin hai. ise desimetre pattee yaa tarang bhi kehete hai kyonki inaki tarang das se ek desimetre kii hoti he. iske upar kii aavritiyaan SHF pattee main, evam neeche kii aavritiyaan atyochchavriti kehalaati hai.
 UHF evam VHF sarvaadhik prayukt aavritiyaan hai, jinka prayog sanchaar ke kshetra main hota he.
