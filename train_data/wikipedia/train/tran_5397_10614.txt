 thyretron ek vishesh prakaar kii gasayukt electron nalika he jo ucch shakti kii niyantrit switch kii tarah upyog kii jaati he. is prakaar iske upyog se niyantrit dishtakaari banaaya jaataa he. yadhyapi tryod, tetrod evam pentod dizin vaale thyretron pehle banaaye ja chuke hai kintu tryod vaali sanrachana sabase adhik prachlit he. chunki inamen gas bhari hoti he, ye apane samakaksh ke nirvaat tubon kii tulana main bahut adhik vidyut dhara le sakate hai kyonki dhanatmak aayan bhi acchhee-khaasi dhara kaa nirmaan karte hai. thyretronon main hydrogen, paare kii vaashp, xenon, neon aadi bhari jaati hai. 
 thyretron visheshat: switch kii tarah prayukt hota he. iske dwaar sanketon ko raikhik rup main aavardhit karnaa kathin he joki tryod aadi nirvaat-nalikaaon ke dwara kiya jaataa he. thos-avasthaa kii yuktiyon main silicon niyantrit dishtakaari he jo thyretron ke samatuly he kintu yah thyretron jitna tej aan-aaf nahin kiya ja saktaa.
