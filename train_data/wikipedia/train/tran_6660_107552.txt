 hazarat nizamuddin railway stesan dilli ke teen mukhya railway stesan main se ek he. yah stesan sabhi mukhya va bade shaharon se judaa he. ise nai dilli railway stesan par sankulan va bheed bhaad niyantrit kar baantne ke uddeshy se bhi viksit kiya ja rahaa he. ys stesan uttar railway dwaara sanchalit evam anurakshit he. dilli kii do mukhya jeevanadhaaraayein ring maarg or mathura maarg iske donon ore se nikalati hai. sarai kaale khan antarraji bas adda bhi is stesan ke nikat hi sthit he, jo ki sabhi samipsth shaharon ko bas seva dwaara jodta he.
