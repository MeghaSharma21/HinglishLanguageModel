  akaash thosar marathi film abhinayta he. nagaraj manjule dwaara digdarshit marathi cinema kii sabase kaamyaab film 'sarat' main akaash ne parashya kii bhoomika kii thi.
 akaash kaa janm pune main hua. kushti ke shaukin akaash kii padavi tak kii padhai savitribai phule pune vishvavidhyalay main se hui. akaash jab khel khilne main magn tha tab nagaraj manjule ko unke bhaaine akaash kii tasviir khinchakar bheji. digdarshak nagaraj manjule ko akaash kaa cheharaa pasand aayaa or unhone akaash ko apni film main le liya. isse uske abhinay kareer kii shruwaat hui.
