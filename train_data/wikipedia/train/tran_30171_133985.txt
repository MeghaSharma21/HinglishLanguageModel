 ekaatmata stotra bharat kii rashtriya ekta kaa udbodhak geet he jo rashtriya swayamsevak sangh kii shakhaon main gaaya jaataa he. yah sanskrit main he. isamen adikaal se lekar ab tak ke bharat ke mahaan sapooton evam suputriyon kii namavali he jinhone bharat evam mahaan hindu sabhyataa ke nirmaan main yogadan diya. iske alaava isamen adarsh naariyaan, dharmik pustaken, nadiyaan, parvat, pavitratmayen, puranic purush, vaigyanik evam saamaajik-dharmik parvatak aadi sabake naamon kaa ullekh he.
