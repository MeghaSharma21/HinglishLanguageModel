 sar leonard "len" haton angrez cricket khilaadi the jinhonen 1937 or 1955 ke bich main 79 test khelen. wo daayen haath ke ballebaaz the. inhone 40,000 se zyaada pratham shrenee ran banaae. apane chathe hi test main inhone test cricket kaa sabase bada score bana diya tha.
 leonard kaa janm west yorkshaire ke leeds shahar main, protestant parivaar main hua tha. unhonen apana pehla match 1937 main yorkshaire ke liye khelaa tha, jab wo mahaj 17 saal ke the. unhonen county cricket main jaldi se khud ko sthapit kar liya. leonard ne england ke liye apana pehla test match 1937 main newjiland ke khilaaf khelaa tha jisamen unhonen 0 or 1 banaae the par unhonen agle hi or apane duusare hi test main shatak jadaa.
