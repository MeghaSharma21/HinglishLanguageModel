 
 comifora mukul Hook.
 guggul yaa 'guggal' ek vriksh he. isse praapt raal jaise padaarth ko bhi 'guggal' kaha jaataa he. bharat main is jati ke do prakaar ke vriksh paae jate hai. ek ko comifora mukul tathaa duusare ko co. roxbarghai kehete hai. africa main pai janevaali prajati comifora africana kehalaati he.
 kuch sthanon se praapt guggul kaa rang peelaapan liye shwet tathaa anya kaa gaharaa laal hota he. isamen mithi mahak rahati he. isko agni main daalane par sthaan sungadh se bhar jaataa he. isaliye iskaa dhoop ke sadrush vyavhaar kiya jaataa he. ayurveda ke matanusaar yah katu tikt tathaa ushn he or kaph, baat, kaas, krimi, kled, shoth or arsh naashak he.
 guggal ek chota ped he jiske pattey chote or ekaantar saral hote hai. yah sirf varsha ritu main hi vruddhi karata he tathaa isi samay is par pattey dikhaai dete hai. shesh samay yani sardi tathaa garmi ke mausam main isaki vruddhi avaruddha ho jaati he tathaa parnheen ho jaataa he. saamanyat: guggal kaa ped 3-4 meter ooncha hota he. iske tane se safed rang kaa doodh nikalataa he jo iskaa kaa upyogi bhaag he. prakritik rup se guggal bharat ke karnataka, rajasthan, gujarat tathaa madhya pradesh rajyon main ugta he. bharat main guggal viluptavastha ke kagaar par aa gaya he, at: bade kshetron me isaki kheti karne kii jarurat he. bharat main guggal kii maang adhik tathaa utpaadan kam hone ke kaaran afghanistan va pakistan se iskaa aayaat kiya jaataa he.
