 rootkit computor softwaron kaa sangrah he, jo aam taur par galat uddeshyon ko puraa karne ke liye banaaya jaataa he. ise is tarah se banaaya jaataa he ki ye computor yaa us kshetra main aise jagah par bhi kaary karne lagata he, jis jagah par software ko aam taur par anumati nahin hoti he. ye kai baar kisi anya uplabdh software kaa rup le lete hai yaa apane software ke astitv ko chupa lete hai. rootkit main "root" shabd adhikaar yaa anumati vaale khate se lee jaati he, jo kai saare unics yaa uske jaise oparating system main upyog hota he. vahiin "kit" kaa arth software ke hisse ko sthapit karne se lee jaati he. ise malware se jodne ke kaaran ise nakaaratmak arth ke rup main liya jaataa he.
 rootkit yaa root kit shabd aise upkaran ko kaha jaataa he, jo sanchaalan karne vaale upkaran ko parivartit kar ke banaaya jaataa he, jisase ve log unics yaa uske jaise oparating system main "root" khate kii anumati mil jaati he. ye maanak sanchaalan upkaran ko system se hataa kar rootkit se badal dete hai, jisase unhen system ke root par adhikaar mil jaye or is upkaran kii sari gatividhi system sanchaalan karne vaale kii hi tarah lage. shuruaati rootkit is tarah ke jaankaree ko praapt karne ke liye kaaphi nahin tha.
