 rashtriya vaahan suraksha tathaa trekking pranali kii sthapana kii manjuuri janvari 2014 ko bharat sarkaar dwaara di gai. bharat main saarvajanik sadak parivahan main mahilaon tathaa ladakiyon kii suraksha ke liye nirbhaya kosh ke antargat yojana ke sanchaalan kaa vistrut dhaancha taiyaar kiya ja rahaa he. 16 disambar 2012 ko dilli main chalati bas main hue gangrape jaisi ghatnaon kii punrariti rokane ke liye yah kadam uthaaya gaya he.
 is prastaav main rashtriya str par ekikrut kaman tathaa rajya str par par ekikrut kaman bananaa shaamil he taaki vaahan ke sthaan kii jeepies trekking ho sake, apatkaaleen batan kaa upyog kiya ja sake tathaa saarvajanik parivahan vaale vaahanon main ghatana kii video recording kii ja sake. pehle charan main desh ke 13 rajyon ke 10 lakh or usase adhik aabaadi vaale 32 shaharon main yah yojana lagu kii jaaegi.
 pariyojna kii kul anumaanit laagat 1404.68 karod rupe hai or ise 'nirbhay kosh' mad se vitt mantralay degaa. 10 lakh or usase adhik aabaadi vaale shaharon main ek baar is yojana ke chaalu ho jane par desh main surakshit, vishwasaneey tathaa aaraamdeh saarvajanik yatri bas seva sambhav ho sakegi.
 saarvajanik vaahanon ke margon kaa nakshaa taiyaar karne, niyat maarg par vaahan kii trekking karne, vijual tathaa likhit sanketon ke jariye niyamon ke ulanghan ko bataane, vijual, likhit tathaa dhwani sanketon ke jariye parivahan tathaa police vyavastha ko sachet karne ke liye khatre kaa batan dabaane ke maamle main is yojana kaa asar padegaa. iskaa asar permit, panjikaran tathaa license radd karne par bhi hoga taaki kam samay main sankat main fansi mahila yaa ladki ko suraksha uplabdh karai ja sake. saarvajanik parivahan vaahanon main baithne kii vyavastha kii video recording ko saboot ke taur par istemaal kiya jayega. isse sambhavit aparaadhon kii rokthaam ho sakegi.
