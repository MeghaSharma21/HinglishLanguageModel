 vivek utpann hone par oupaadhik dukh sukhadi - ahankaar, praarabdh, karm or sanskaar ke lop ho jane se aatma ke chitaswarup hokar avaagaman se mukt ho jane kii sthiti ko kaivalya kehete hai. 
 paatanjalsutra ke anusaar chit dwaara aatma ke saakshaatkaar se jab uske karttritva aadi abhimaan chhootakar karm kii nivritti ho jaati he tab vivekgyan ke udai hone par mukti kii ore agrasaarit aatma ke chitswarup main jo sthiti utpann hoti hai uski sangya kaivalya he. vedant ke anusaar paramaatma main aatma kii leenata or nyaay ke anusaar adrusht ke naash hone ke falswaroop aatma kii janmamaran se muktavastha ko kaivalya kaha gaya he. yogasutron ke bhashyakaar vyas ke anusaar, jinhone karmbandhan se mukt hokar kaivalya praapt kiya he, unhen 'kevali' kaha jaataa he. aise kevali anek hue he. buddhi aadi gunon se rahit nirmal jyotivaale kevali aatmrup main sthir rahete hai. hindu dharmagranthon main shuk, janak aadi rishiyon ko jivanmukt bataaya he jo jal main camel kii bhanti, sansar main rahete hue bhi mukt jeevon ke samaan nirlep jeevanyaapan karte he. jain granthon main kevliyon ke do bhed - sanyogkevali or ayogkevali bataae gaye he.
