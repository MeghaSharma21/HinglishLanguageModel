 jal main pattiyaan, tana, fal, phool yaa koi anya rasayan daalkar use ubaalane par jo padaarth banataa he use kwath yaa kadha kehete hai. 
 tantra ke anusaar in paanch vrikshon —jamun, simal, khirati, molsiri ore ber kaa kashay 'panchakshai' kehalaata he. yah kashay chaal ko pani main bhigokar nikaala jaataa he or durga ke pujan main kaam aata he.
 kashay paanch prakaar ke hote hai, jinhen sammilit rup se 'panchakshai' kehete hai—
 1. swaras
 2. kalk
 3. kwath
 4. him
 5. phaant
 ye paanchon prakaar ke kwath madhur, amla, katu, tikt or kashay ras vaale dravyon se banaae jate hai. swaras se kalk, kalk se kwath, kwath se him or him se phaant halkaa hota he. arthath inaki shakti kramshah ghatate hue kram main hoti he.
