 rajbhavan jammu bharat ke jammu or kashmir rajya ke rajyapal kaa adhikarik sheetakaaleen awaas he. yah rajya kii sheetakaaleen rajdhani jammu main sthit he. narinder nath vohara jammu or kashmir ke vartmaan rajyapal hai or unhone 25 june 2008 ko rajyapal kaa pad grahan kiya tha.
 jammu kaa rajbhavan vahaan ke maharaja ke bahut se mehalon, ranbir mahal, main se ek tha jo vartmaan main jammu or kashmir sarkaar kii kirayedari main he. rajbhavan prasiddh tavi nadi kii ore mukhagra he. yah 15.75 acre ke. etrafal main failaa hua he or iskaa ek vishaal durvakshetra he jahaan par sabhi kaarykram yojit kiye jate hai. bharat ke aadhikarik ucch padaadhikari rajya ke jammu daure ke dauraan yaheen rukate hai.
