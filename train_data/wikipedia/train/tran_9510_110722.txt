 tribindu yaa trisima kshetra aisa bhugolic sthaan hota he jahaan teen desh yaa anya rajnatik bhugolic ikaiyaan milen. anumaanit kiya jaataa he ki vishwa main lagbhag 176 tribindu hai, jinamein se qareeb aadhe nadiyon, jhiilon yaa sagaron par sthit hai. jahaan yah tribindu thal par hai vahaan aksar stambhon yaa anya nirmaanon dwaara unhen ankit kiya jaataa he.
