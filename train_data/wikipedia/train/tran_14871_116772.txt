 uposhnkaribandh prithvi ke ushnkaribandheey kshetra ke turant uttar tathaa dakshin se satakar lage hue kshetra ko kehete hai jo ki kark rekha or makar rekha ke kramshah uttar tathaa dakshin kaa ilaaka he. yah kshetra amuman donon golardhon main 23.5° se lekar 40° tak ke akshanshon main paaya jaataa he haalanki yah mausam adhik oonchaai vaale kshetron main yaa ushnkaribandheey ilaakon main bhi paaya ja saktaa he.
 jalwaayu vigyaani glen ji travertha ke anusaar, ek up-ushnkaribandheey kshetra main saal main kam se kam aath maheene ausatan 10° se. yaa usase upar kaa taapmaana hona chahie. 
 america jalwaayu vigyaani jon. epa. griphith ne apni pustak aplide clametolji main yah vivaran kiya he ki up-ushnkaribandh ke sabase thande maheene main taapmaana 6° se. or 18° se. ke bich hona chahie. 
 up-ushnkaribandheey jalwaayu main sardiyaan apekshaakrit gunguni hoti hai, lekin garmi ke mausam kii tarah garm nahin hoti hai. ye mausam shaayad hi kabhi paala yaa barfbaari dekhte hai or inamen tad, neembu-vanshi or kai chaudee pattiyon vaale sadabahaar vriksh panpate hai, un madhya akshanshon ke vipareet jinamein majboot parnpaati or shankudhari ped haaavee rahete hai. ushnkaribandheey paksh kii ore rukh karne se gyaat hota he ki gulabi sardiyon vaala mausam puri tarah gaayab ho jaataa he, jabaki up-ushnkaribandhon kii dhruvabhimukheey seemaa par sardiyaan zyaada prabhaavshaalee hoti hai.samooche up-ushnkaribandheey kshetra bhar main varsha swaroop vyapak rup se badalata he jaise garm registan, savana, monsoon ke jangalon, nam jangalon or bumadhya jalwaayu kshetra ke garm bhaagon main.
