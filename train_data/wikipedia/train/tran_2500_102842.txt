 
 mishan: imposible ek america televijan shrunkhlaa he jisame kufia america sarkaar ke agenton kii ek team, jise imposible mishan force kehete he, ke kai mishan va karname dikhaae jate he. yah kaarykram 1996 kii isi naam kii tivi shrunkhlaa kaa punarniman he. isamen puraani shrunkhlaa se vaapasi karne vaale pitor grevs hi ek maatr abhinayta he jinhone gym felips kii bhoomika adaa kii thi, haalanki do pichhali shrunkhlaa ke do anya sadsy atithi bhoomika main aate he.
 is shrunkhlaa kii ghatnaayen mukhya mishan imposible tivi shrunkhlaa ke aakhri satr se 15 saal baad shuru hoti he. jab kufia mishan imposible force kaa bhavishya kaa adhyaksh maaraa jaataa he tab gym philip ko uski nivritti se waapas laayaa jaataa he taaki ek nai iemef team banaai ja sake jo us hatyare ko pakadaney kaa kaary sampann karegi.
 uski team main nicholas blike, ek baharoopiya or behtirin abhinayta; maiks hartey, ek shaktishaalee vyakti; kasee randle, ek model jo ab easant he; or grant colier, barni colier kaa betaa jo ek takneekee jeniyas he, shaamil he. hatyare kaa pataa lagane ke baad gym ruuk kar team ko ek saath rakhane kaa nirnay letaa he. pratham satr ke bich main hi kasee ek mishan ke dauraan maari jaati he or sikret sarvice kii easant shainon read uski jagaa baaki kii shrunkhlaa main le leti he. is badlaav ke alaava philips kii team baaki kii puri shrunkhlaa main vahi rahati he.
