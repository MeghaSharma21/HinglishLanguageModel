 swen anders hedin sweden kaa bhugolashastri, khoj-yatri, photographer tathaa yatra-vrutant lekhak tha. 
 swen anders hedin kaa janm 19 farvari 1865 i. ko stokhome main hua or mrityu 1952 i. main hui. upasaala vishvavidhyalay main uski shiksha hui or tadnantar berlin tathaa haal main shiksha grahan kii. 1885-56 i. main vah faras or mesopotamia gaya or 1890 i. main faras ke shah se sambandhit auskar raja ke duutavas main niyukt hua. usee varsh usne khurasaan or turkistan kii yatraayen kii ore 1891 main kashgar pahunch gaya. uski tibet kii yatraaon ne use asia ke aadhunik yatriyon main pratham sthaan praapt karaayaa. 1893 or 1897 i. ke bich usne asia mahadweep ke aarpaar yatra kii. orenberg se chalakar ural paar kiya or pamir tathaa tibet ke pathaar se hote hue peking pahunchaa. do anya yatraaon main in margon ke gyaan main vishesh jaankaree kii tathaa satluj, sindhu or brahmaputra ke udgam sthanon kii khoj kii. san 1902 main vah sweden kaa nobul bana diya gaya or san 1909 main bharat sarkaar ne ke. si. ai. i. kii oopaadhi di. san 1907 main usne chini-sweden yatra kaa china ko maargdarshan kiya or iske parinaamon ke prakashit karne ke liye kai varsh parishram kiya.
