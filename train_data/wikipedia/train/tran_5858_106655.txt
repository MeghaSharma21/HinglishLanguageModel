 todra vaadi uttari africa ke morocco desh main atlas parvat shrunkhlaa main todra nadi dwaara kaati gai ek tang ghati he. yah todra nadi ne in pahaado main apane antim 40 kimi main kaati he, jiske bilkul akhir ke 600 meter dekhne ke qaabil hai kyonki yahan par jagah-jagah par ghati kii chaudaai simat kar 10 meter tak ho jaati he, jiske donon taraf 160 meter oonchi kii diwaar jaisi patthariilii chattaane hai. yugon ke saath-saath todra kaa bahaav bahut kam ho gaya he or ab adhiktar uskaa pravaah naam-maatr hi hota he, jo upar pahaado kii pighalati barf se aata he.
 yahan ke sthaneey log aksar apane gadhon or unton ke saath dekhe ja sakate hai. kisi jamaane main is tang ghati tak pahunchnaa bhi mushkil hota tha, lekin ab yahan tak ek pakki sadak ban gayi he or paryatakon ke liye todra vaadi kii shuruyaat ke paas kuch rahane ke chote hotel bhi bane hue hai.
 francisi bhi morocco main aam boli jaati he or usamen todra tang ghati ko "doores da todra" bolaa jaataa he - dhyaan rahe ke ji kaa sahi uccharan 'jha' or 'za' donon se bhinn hota he.
