 prem-pratimaa ek prakaar kaa maanav-aakaar kamottejak upkaran hota he. ling, yoni evam/athwa guda yukt is saadhan kaa prayog swatahmaithun ke liye kiya jaataa he. saamanytah prem-pratimaa ek sampurna deh-rupi putali hoti he, parantu kuch putliyon main keval dhad, sheersh yaa anya aanshik ang hi hote hai. kuch putliyon main vibhinn bhaagon ko nikaala yaa lagaaya ja saktaa hai.
 mogulakalin bharatvarsh main rasiyaa vyakti prem-pratimaaon kaa prayog karte the. satrahavin sadi main spanish evam french navik "dame-de-voyaj" naamak prem-pratimaa kaa prayog karte the. lambi tathaa ekaaki sagar-yatraaon kii avadhi main jab unhen kaam-laalasaa ksht detii thi, to ve iskaa sahaara lekar samay bitaate the.
 beesvin sadi main kaarkaanon main nirmit prem-pratimaaen lokapriy huin. kaha jaataa he ki dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke samay naji germany ne apane sainikon ke liye prem-pratimaaon kaa utpaadan kiya, parantu shri evan bloch ke anusaar vishwasaneey sutron dwaara is vivaran ko satyapit nahin kiya gaya he.
 1970 ke dashak tak vinil, letex or silicon se nirmit yathartwaadee prem-putaliyaan kaa vikray hone laga tha. ek baccha niti ke chalate china main prem-pratimaaon kii lokapriyata main vruddhi hui. japan main multah prem-pratimaaon ko "dach patni" kaha jaataa tha. varttman kaal main is shabd ko keval hawa se bhari sasti gudiya ke liye prayukt kiya jaataa he. tokyo nagar main purush prem-pratimaaon ko kiraye par le sakate hai.
 aadhunik yug main graahak prem-pratimaaon ko antarjaal par kharid sakate hai. vyaktigat pasand ke anukool pratimaaon ke naak-naksh, saundarya, kesh rang, kaaya varna, kapade aadi chune ja sakate hai. 21veen sadi main robot-rupi prem-pratimaaon ke lokapriy hone kii sambhaavna he.
