 banavaaripur bhagavanpur, begusarai, bihar sthit ek gaanv he.
 banavaaripur bihar ke begusarai jile ke bhagavanpur block main sthit ek bada gaanv he jisamen kul 700 parivaar rahete hai. banavaaripur gaanv main 3861 kii jansankhya he, jinamein se 2048 purush hai jabaki 1813 jansankhya janaganana 2011 ke anusaar mahilaayein hai.banavaaripur gaanv main 0-6 varsh kii umr ke bacchon kii aabaadi 670 he jo gaanv kii kul aabaadi kaa 17.35% he. banavaaripur gaanv kaa ausat ling anupaat 885 he jo bihar rajya ausat 918 se kam he. janaganana ke anusaar banavaaripur ke liye bal ling anupaat 887 he, jo bihar ausat 935 se kam he.bihar kii tulana main banavaaripur gaanv main sakshartaa dar zyada he. 2011 main, banavaaripur gaanv kii sakshartaa dar bihar ke 61.80% kii tulana main 69.01% thi. banavaaripur main purush sakshartaa 76.9 6% he jabaki mahila sakshartaa dar 60.01% thi.bharat or panchayat raaj adhinium ke samvidhan ke anusaar, banavaaripur gaanv sarpanch dwaara prashasit he jo gaanv ke pratinidhi chune jate hai.
