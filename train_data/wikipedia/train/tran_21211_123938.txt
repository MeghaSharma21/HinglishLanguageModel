 vias
 abu abd allaah mohammad ebn ismail ebn ibrahim ebn al-mughirra ebn bardizba al-jeuphi al-bukhari, yaa bukhari, jise saamanytah imam al bukhari yaa imam bukhari kaha jaataa he, ek farsi  islamic vidwaan tha jo bukhaara kaa). unhone haddees sangrah ko likhaa he jo ki sahih al-bukaari ke rup main janaa jaataa he, sunni musalamaanon dwaara sabase pramanik haddees sangrahon main se ek mana jaataa he. unhone anya pustakon ko bhi likhaa, jaise al-adab al-mufrad.
