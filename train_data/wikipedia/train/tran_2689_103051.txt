 preshan electron sookshmdarshan  ek sookshmdarshan takanic he, jisame electronon kaa ek kiranpunj, ek bahut maheen namoonay se hokar guzarati he or is prakriya ke dauraan kiranpunj ke electron namoonay ke saath sampark sthapit kar ek chhavi kaa nirmaan karte he, jise ek imaging upkaran jaise ek pratideeptishil parde yaa ek photographic film par aavardhit or kendrit kiya jaataa he. ek sisidi camere ke samvedak bhi iskaa sansuchan kar chhavi nirmaan karte hai. sabase pehla TEM maiks nol or ernest ruska ne 1931 main banaaya tha, 1933 me in dono me mil kar ek TEM viksit kiya jisaki rizolving power prakaash se bhi adhik thi. pehla vyavsaayik TEM 1939 main bazaar me aayaa.
 tiem prakaash sookshmdarshi kii tulana me kaaphi ucch rijolution kii chhavion kaa nirmaan karne me sakasham hote hai, jiskaa kaaran electronon kii laghu de brogli tarang dairdhya he. iskaa yah gun is upkaran ko ati sookshmavibhedan karne me sakasham banaata he, yahan tak ki yah parmaanuon ke ekal stambh kii bhi gahan jaanch saktaa he, jo ek prakaash sookshmdarshi dwaara vibhedit sabase choti vastu se bhi hajaaron guna kam hota he. bhautik or jeev dono vaigyanik kshetron me tiem ek pramukh vishleshan vidhi kii bhoomika nibhaate hai, jisame cancer anusandhan, padaarth vigyaan, wirology, pradooshan or ardhchaalak anusandhan, jaise kshetra mahatvapurn hai. 
 kam aavardhan par TEM chhavi kaa cantrast, padaarth main electronon ke avasorana, padaarth kii motaai or sanrachana ke kaaran hota he. ucch aavardhan par jatil tarang sampark, chhavi kii tivrata kaa niyaman karte hai, prekshak chhaviyon ke visheshgy vishleshan kii aavashyaktaa hoti he.
