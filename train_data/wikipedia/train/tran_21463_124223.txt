 yama ke chaar prakritik upgrah vaigyanikon ko gyaat hai: 1978 main khoj kiya gaya shairan, 2005 main khoj kiye gaye do nanhe chandramaa, nix or hydra or 20 july 2011 ko ghoshit kiya gaya this/2011 pi 1 . anya grahon or boune grahon kii tulana main pluto ke chandramaa uske bahut paas parikrama karte hai. chaaron ke bare main anumaan he ke ve barf or pathar ke bane hue hai.
 pluto or shairan kaa mail saura mandal main anokhaa he. shairan kaa vyas pluto ke aadhe se zyaada he or uskaa dravyaman pluto kaa 11.6% he - jo saura mandal main kisi bhi upgrah-grah kii jodi main sabase adhik he. tulana ke liye prithvi ke chandramaa kaa vyas prithvi kaa lagbhag ek-chauthai or dravyaman prithvi kaa keval 1.2% he. is kaaran se pluto or shairan kaa mila hua dravyaman kendra kaa bindu pluto ke andar nahin balki in donon ke bich ke khule vyom main padataa he or pluto or shairan donon is bindu kii parikrama karte hai. agar khagolashastra kii paribhaashaayen sakhti se lagaai jaaen to aisi sthiti main do khagoleey vastuon ko grah-upgrah na kahkar judwaan grah kaha jaataa he. phir bhi shairan ko pluto kaa upgrah hi mana jaataa he.
 pluto ke yah do chote se chandramaa 15 may 2005 ko habal antariksh doorabeen ke dwaara sab se pehle dekhe gaye. nix pluto ko 48,700 kimi kii duuri par parikrama kar rahaa he or hydra 64,800 kimi kii duuri par. aise do chote chandramaaon ko dekhakar kuch vaigyanikon ko shankaa he ke shaayad pluto ke ird-gird koi upgrahi challa ho, kyonki aise upgrah un paristhitiyon main aksar paae jate hai. filhal aisa koi upgrahi challa dekha nahin gaya he.
 is upgrah ke milane kii ghoshnaa 20 july 2011 ko kii gai thi. yah yama kii ek parikrama anumaanit 32.1 dinon main puri kar letaa he. iskaa vyas andazan 13 se lekar 34 kilometre ke bich he, yani yah yama kaa sabase chota gyaat upgrah he. mana jaataa he ki yama ke anya upgrahon kii tarah is nanhe chandramaa kaa bhi nirmaan sambhawatah yama or kisi anya kyper ghere kii vastu ke saath pracheen takaraav se hua ho.
