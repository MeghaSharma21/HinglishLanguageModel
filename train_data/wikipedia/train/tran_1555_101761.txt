 truti sansuchan or sudhaar kisi bhi prakaar ke dwiadhari ank ke dwaara bheje gaye kisi sandesh main truti ko pahchaan kar sudhaarney kaa kaary karata he.
 kisi bhi truti ko pahchaan karne ke liye kai paddati viksit kii gai he.
 bheje gaye sanket or vaastavik sanket main samanta dekh kar truti kaa pataa lagaaya ja saktaa he. lekin iske liye vaastavik sanket kaa hona aavashyak he.
 is paddati yaa vidhi main kisi truti ko pahchaan karne ke liye doosraa sanket jise truti-sanshodhan sanket kehete hai, kaa upyog kiya jaataa he. lekin iske dwaara kuch hi truti kaa pataa lagaaya ja saktaa he.
