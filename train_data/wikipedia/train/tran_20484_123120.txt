 atalas parvat yaa atlas parvat pashimottaree africa main sthit ek 2,500 kimi lambi parvat shrunkhlaa he. yah morocco, algeria or tunisiya ke deshon se nikalati he or iskaa sabase unchaa pahaad dakshin-pashchimi morocco main sthit 4,167 meter lambaa tubakaal parvat he. atalas parvaton kii shreniyan bumadhya sagar or andh mahaasaagar ke saath lage tateey kshetra ko sahaara registan se baantati hai. is parvatiy ilaake main rahane vaale log mukhya rup se barbar jati ke hai.
 atalas parvaton main bahut si aisi jaanwaron or vrikshon-paudhon kii jatiyaan hai jo africa ke anya bhaagon se bilkul alag hai or europe se milti julati hai. purvakaal main aisi bhi kuch jatiyaan thi jo ab vilupt ho chuki hai. inamen barbari makaak, atalas bhaaluu, barbari tendua, barbari hiran, barbari bhed, barbari singh, africa aurokes, uttari ganji ibis, atalas pahari viper, europea kaala chid, atalas seader va algerian balut shaamil hai.
