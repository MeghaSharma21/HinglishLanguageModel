 yuasabi flash smriti ek prakaar kaa vidyutiya smriti bhandarak he jiskaa prayog vibhinn electonic upkarnon main suchanaaen sanjone ke liye kiya jaataa he. iskaa avishkaar 1980 main japan ke phujiyo masuoka ne kiya tha tathaa 1988 main intel korporation ne pehali baar ise bazaar main vyavsaayik rup se vipanit kiya tha. iskaa prayog saamaanya computaron, digital camaron, digital gemon tathaa mobile phonon main bahut lokapriy hua he.
 yah sasta tathaa aaveg se lage dhakkon ke prati adhik majboot hota he jisaki vajah se pichley dashak main iskaa upyog bahut hone laga he. isaki takanic vidyutiya rup se mitaai ja sakane vaali smriti kaa ek prakaar he.
 "sphur smriti" jiskaa arth chamak ke saath kii smriti se lagaaya ja sata he, ko sabase pehle toshiba main karyrat dow phujiyo masuoka ne avishkrut kiya tha. unhone iskaa naam sphur smriti apane sahakarmi shoji arijumi kii salaah par isliye rakhaa kyonki yah unhen camere kii chamak kii yaad dilaataa tha. ise 1984 ke vaishvik vidyutiya tathaa electronic sammelan main prastut kiya. america electronic nirmaataa company intel ne iske prayog kii sambhaavna dekhi or 1988 main ise bazaar main prastut kiya.
