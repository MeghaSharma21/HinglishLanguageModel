 rashtriya pustak nyaas bharat sarkaar ke maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantralay ke adheen swaitshasi sanghathan he. isaki sthapana 1957 main hui thi. iske kaary hai -
  prakaashan
  pustak pathan ko protsaahan
  videshon main bhartiya pustakon ko protsaahan
  lekhakon or prakashkon ko sahaayta
  bal sahitya ko badhaava dena
 yah vibhinn shreniyon ke arntagat hindi, angreji tathaa anya pramukh bhartiya bhashaaon evam braille lipi main pustaken prakashit karata he. yah har duusare varsh nai dilli main 'vishwa pustak meley' kaa aayojan karata he, jo asia or africa kaa sabase bada pustak mela he. yah prativarsh 14 se 20 navambar tak 'rashtriya pustak saptaah' bhi manaata he.
