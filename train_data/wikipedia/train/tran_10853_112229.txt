 transistor ek saral yukti he kintu inkaa vyavhaar utana saral nahin he. isliye, transistor vaale paripathon ke vishwasaneey rup se kaam karne ke liye aavashyak he ki unka modelan aise kiya jaay ki vah transistor ke vyavhaar kaa sabhi sthitiyon main sahi tarah se pratinidhitiv kar sake. transistar ke bahut saare model banaae gayi hai jo jatilta main alag-alag hai or vibhinn uddeshyon kii purti karte hai. in modelon ko do bhaagon main baanta ja saktaa he, yukti kii dizin main sahaayak model tathaa paripath kii dizin main sahaayak model.
