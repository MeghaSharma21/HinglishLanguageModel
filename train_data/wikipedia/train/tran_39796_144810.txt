 pashchim bengal ke nadoya jile ke mayapur main sthit isckon mandir bhagwaan shrikrishna ko samarpit pramukh mandir he. mayapur apane shandaar mandiron ke liye puure vishwa main janaa jaataa he. mandiron ke alaava paryatak yahaan par saraswat advait math or chaitanya gaudia math kii yatra bhi kar sakate hai. holi ke dinon me mayapur kii chata dekhne laayak hoti he kyonki us samay yahaan par bhavy rathyatra yojit kii jaati he. yah rathyatra aapasi sauhaard or bhaichaare kaa pratik maani jaati he.
