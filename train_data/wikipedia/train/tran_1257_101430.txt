 aazaadee ke baad kii punjab kii sabase prasiddh sahityakaron main se ek ajit cor kii kitaab ghar se prakaashit is kahani sangrah main kul 15 kahaniyaan hai. isamen vartmaan samay or samaaj par sateek tikaayen hai. inamen bekasoor logon ke katl ke saath hi pedo ke katane or panchhiyon ke marane, chitiyon ke beghar hone, nadiyon ke sookhne or jangalon kii aakhri pukaar bhi sunai detii he. isamen syah or safed, shahari or gaanv kii, desh kii or pardesh kii, aadmi or aurat kii tathaa or bhi kitnon kii duniya he. inamen dilli or mumbai ke anek rang hai. isamen daad dene vaale hai, bhikhaari hai, devar-bhabhi hai, maami hai or clerk-maharaj bhi hai. ye sadhaaran logon kii asaadhaaran kahaniyaan hai.
