 arabi yaa ghuiyaan ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he | mukhya rup se isaki kheti kand ke liye hi kii jaati he | iske and kii sabji banaayi jaati he, upwas ke samay inhen kandon kaa upyog falahar ke liye kiya jaataa he | iske patton kii bhi sabji banaai jaati he | kuch prajatiyon ke kandon main kankanahat paayi jaati he | jo ubaal dene ke baad samaapt ho jaati he | pattiyon main kanakanaahat kand kii tulana main adhik paayi jaati he |
 patton tathaa danthlon ke rang ke anusaar arvi kii do kismein hoti he : ek kism main danthlen begni rang kii tathaa duusari main hari hoti he. 
 isi prakaar hari or gol kand vaali kismein hoti he. kuch kandon ke goode safed kuch ke pile kuch ke nanragi va begni hote hai |

 pusa komal, pusa barsaati, pusa phaguni tathaa pusa do fasali
