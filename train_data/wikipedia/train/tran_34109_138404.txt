 ahamed faraaz, asli naam saiyad ahamed shah, kaa janm pakistan ke nausheraa shahar main hua tha. ve aadhunik urdu ke sarvshereshth rachanakaaron main gine jate hai.
 unhone peshawar vishvavidhyalay main farsi or urdu vishay kaa adhyayan kiya tha. baad main ve vahiin praadhyaapak bhi ho gaye the. shaayari kaa shauk unhen bachapan se tha. ve antyakshari kii pratiyogitaaon main hissaa liya karte the. lekhan ke praarambhik kaal main ve iqbaal kii rachanaaon se prabhavit rahe. phir dhire dhire pragativaadee kavita ko pasand karne lage. ali sardaar jaafaree or faiz ahamed faiz ke padchihnon par chalate hue unhone jiyaul haq ke shashan ke samay kuch aisi gazalen likhakar mushaairon main padhin jinke kaaran unhen jail main bhi rahanaa padaa. isi samay ve kai saal pakistan se dur united kingdom or canada deshon main rahe.
 ahamed faraaz ne radio pakistan main bhi naukari kii or phir adhyaapan se bhi jude. unki prasiddhi ke saath-saath unke pad main bhi vruddhi hoti rahi. ve 1976 main pakistan academy of letters ke diarector general or phir usee academy ke chairman bhi bane. 2004 main pakistan sarkaar ne unhen hilaal-e-imtiyaaz puraskaar se alankrita kiya. lekin 2006 main unhone yah puraskaar isliye waapas kar diya ki ve sarkaar kii niti se sahamat or santusht nahin the. unhen cricket khilne kaa bhi shauk tha. lekin shaayari kaa shauk un par aisa chaaya ki ve apane samay ke gaalib kehalaae. unki shaayari ke kai sangrah prakashit hue. gazalon ke saath hi unhone nazmen bhi likhi. lekin log unki gazalon ke diwane hai.
 25 agast, 2008 ko kidney fail hone ke kaaran unka nidhan ho gaya.
 unki gazalon or nazmon ke kai sangrah prakashit hue hai, jinamein khanaabadosh, zindagi! i zindagi or dard ashob or ye meri gazalen ye meri nazmen shaamil hai.
