 ram gopal bajaj-bhartiya rangamanch nideshak, hindi film abhinayta or neshnal skool of drama ke purv nideshak he.ram gopal bajaj ko 1996 main theatre main unke yogadan ke liye padmashri or 2003 main sangeet naatak academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya he.
 oonhone neshnal skool of drama ke saath kai naatakon kaa nirdesan kiya. surya kii antim kiran se, surya kii pehali kiran tak, jaishankar prasad kii skandagupt, kede heyat ,mohan rakesh kaa athad kaa ek din pramukh he. ram gopal bajaj ne girish karnad ke rakt kalyaan kaa hindi anuwaad bhi kiya he. jiskaa nirdesan ibrahim alkaaji ne neshnal skool of drama or aravind goud ne asmita natya sanstha ke liye kiya.
 unhone utsav or godhuli jaisi kala filmon main ek sahaayak nirdeshak ke rup main kaam bhi kiya or baad main maasoom, mirch masala, chandani parajaania, guru jaisi filmon main kaam kiya. vah vartmaan main neshnal skool of drama, nai dilli kii academik parishad ke ek sadsy he.
