 slovene yaa slovenian duniyabar main lagbhag 24 lakh logon dwaara boli jane vaali ek dakshin slav bhashaa he, jisamen se adhikansh slovenia main rahete hai. slovene europea sangh kii 23 aadhikarik bhashaaon main se ek he.
 maanak slovene rashtriya bhashaa he, jo 18 veen sadi main kendriya slovene boliyon se viksit hui or 19 veen or 20 veen sadi main or samekit hui. haalaanki puraane kshetreey gramin boliyon se itar vividhtaayen abhi bhi maujuud hai, lekin lekhnee or bolchaal main bhashaa ek or maanak he. haalaanki is bhashaa par vilupt hone kaa asann sankat nahin he, lekin pichhali shatabdiyon main is tarah kii boliyaan lagaataar haas kii ore agrasar hai, bhale hi in par paryaapt shodh kiya gaya ho or unke istemaal ke liye sthaneey sarkaar dwaara protsaahan diya jaataa ho.
