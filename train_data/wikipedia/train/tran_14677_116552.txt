 
 sashastr seemaa bal bharat kaa ek ardhsainik bal he jisapar 1,751 kilometre lambii bharat-nepal seemaa kii suraksha kii jimmedaari he. is seemaa se hathiyaaron, gola-barood kii taskari or desh virdohi tatwon kii avaidh rup se bharat main awajahi kaa khatra rahata he.
 01 may 2014 se shri aravind rajan ve sashastr seemaa bal ke mahanideshak kaa atirikt padbhaar sambhaal rahe hai. ve kendriya audyogic suraksha bal ke mahanideshak bhi hai. unhone shri arun choudhary kaa sthaan liya jo 30 april 2014 ko sevanivrutt hue.
 farvari 2016 main archana ramasundaram ko sashastr seemaa bal kaa mahanideshak banaaya gaya. archana ramsundaram sashastr seemaa bal kii pratham mahila mahanideshak he.
