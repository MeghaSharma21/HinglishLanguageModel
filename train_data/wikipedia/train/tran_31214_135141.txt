 bai bhashaa yaa baip bhashaa china ke unnan praant or uske kuch padosi kshetron, main bai logon dwaara boli jane vaali ek chini-tibeti bhashaa-parivaar kii ek boli he. ise san 2003 main lagbhag 12,40,000 log apni maatrubhaasha ke rup main bolte the. anya chini-tibeti bhashaaon kii tarah yah bhi ek surabhedi bhashaa he or isamen aath suron kaa prayog hota he. bai bhashaa kii teen mukhya upbhashaayen hai - jianchuaan, daali or bijiang . inamen jianchuaan or daali ek-duusare ke qareeb hai or inhen bolne vaale bahut had tak ek-duusare ko samaj sakate hai, lekin bijiangin donon se kaafii bhinn he.
 haalanki is baat par bhashavaigyanikon main sarvasammati he ki bai bhashaa chini-tibeti bhashaa-parivaar ki sadsy he, lekin us parivaar ke andar isaki upashrenee ko lekar vivaad he. pehle yah samajha jaataa tha ki yah ek tibeti-burmi bhashaa he lekin san 1948 main aar.e.di. forest naamak bhashavaigyanik ne daawa kiya ki yah vastav main aadi-chini bhashaa ki vanshaj he. yah vivaad abhi tak jari he.
