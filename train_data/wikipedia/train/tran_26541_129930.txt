 basal-landshaft canton switzaraland ke uttar main sthit ek canton he. kisi jamaane main basal-landshaft canton or pados kii basal-shtaat canton donon ek hi 'basal canton' naamak canton kaa bhaag thim. german bhashaa main 'basal-shtaat' kaa matlab 'basal shahar' he or yah canton basal shahar or uske ird-gird ke kuch ilaaqon ko milakar bani hui he. iske vipareet basal-landshaft basal kshetra ke dehati ilaaqon se bani hui he. 19veen sadi main gramin or shahari kshetron main adhikaron ko lekar jhadpen hui or 26 agast 1833 main inkaa batwaaraa hua or do alag canton ban gai. aitihaasik basal canton san 1501 main swiss parisangh kaa hissaa bana tha.
