 garud yaa akvilla ek taramandal he. puraani unani khagolashastriy pustakon main ise aksar ek chil ke rup main darshaya jaataa tha. iskaa sab se roshan tara shravan he, jise angreji main "altere" kehete hai.
 garud taramandal main aath mukhya taare hai, haalanki vaise isamen 65 taron ko bair naam die ja chuke hai. inamen se 7 ke ird-gird gair-saureey grah parikrama karte hue paae gaye hai. is taramandal ke mukhya taare is prakaar hai -
