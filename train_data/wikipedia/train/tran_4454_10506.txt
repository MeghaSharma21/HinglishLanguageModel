 manmandir mahal madhya pradesh rajya ke gwalior shahar main sthit he. iskaa nirmaan 1486 se 1517 ke bich raja maansingh dwaara karavaaya gaya tha. sundar rangeen tilon se saje is kile kii samay ne bhavyata chhini jarur he kintu iske kuch aantarik va bahya hisson main in neeli, pili, hari, safed tiles dwaara banaai utkrisht kalakritiyon ke avshesh ab bhi is kile ke bhavy ateet kaa pataa dete hai. is kile ke vishaal kakshon main ateet aaj bhi spandit he. yahaan jalidaar deevaaron se bana sangeet kaksh he, jinke peeche bane janaana kakshon main raaj parivaar kii striyaan sangeet sabhaaon kaa anand letin or sangeet sikhti thim. is mahal ke tahkhaanon main ek kaidakhana he, itihaas kahata he ki aurangzeb ne yahaan apane bhai muraad ko kaid rakhwaya tha or baad main use samaapt karvaa diya. johar kund bhi yahaan sthit he. striyaan isamen kud kar johar karti thim.
 iske atirikt kile main is shahar ke pratham shaasha ke naam se ek kund he ’suraj kund’. naveen shatee main pratihaar vansh dwaara nirmit ek adwitiya sthapatyakala kaa namoona vishnu ji kaa taili kaa mandir he, jo ki 100 feet kii oonchai kaa he. yah dravid sthapaty or arya sthapaty kaa bejod sangam he. bhagwaan vishnu kaa hi ek or mandir-dway he saas-bahu kaa mandir. iske alaava yahaan ek sundar gurudwara he jo sikhon ke chathe guru guru hargobind ji kii smriti main nirmit hua, jinhen jahangir ne do varshon tak yahaan bandi bana kar rakhaa tha.
