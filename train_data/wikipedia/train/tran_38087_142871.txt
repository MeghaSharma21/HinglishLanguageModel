 pudinaa mentha vansh se sambandhit ek baramasi, khushboodaar jadi he. isaki vibhinn prajatiyaan europe, america, asia, africa or australia me paai jaati hai, saath hi isaki kai sankar kismein bhi uplabdh hai.
 angreji- mint 
 vaigyanik naamah mentha aravansis
 upyogi bhaagah pattiyaan'
 aisa vishwaas kiya jaataa he ki mentha kaa udbhav bumadhyasagariya basin main hua tathaa vaho se ye prakritik tathaa anya tareekon se sansar ke anya hisson main failaa, japani podina, brazil, parague, china, argentina, japan, thailand, angola, tathaa bharatvarsh main ugaaya ja rahaa he. bharatvarsh main mukhyat: tarai ke kshetron tathaa ganga yamuna doaan main uttari-pashchimi bharat ke kshetron main isaki kheti kii ja rahi he.
 japani mint, menthol kaa praathmik strot he. taaji pattee main 0.4-0.6% tel hota he. tel kaa mukhya ghatak menthol, menthon tathaa menthail acitate tathaa tarapin he. tel kaa menthol pratishat, vaataavaran ke prakaar par bhi nirbhar karata he. saamanytah yah garm kshetron main adhik hota he.
