 tirth prabandh solahavin shatee ke dwait darshanik evam sant shri vadiraj swami dwaara sanskrit main rachit pramukh granth he. yah granth ek yatravarnan kii bhaanti likhaa gaya he jisamen sampoorn bharat ke tirthasthanon kaa varnan he. is granth main kul 235 shloke hai jo chaar adhyyon main vibhakt hai. har disha ke liye ek adhyay he.
