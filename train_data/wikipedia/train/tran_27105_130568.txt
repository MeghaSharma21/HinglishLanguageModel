 bada sunda dweep samooh, malay dweepasmooh ke antargat aane vaale sunda dweep samooh kaa ek bhaag he, jiske antargat samooh ke bade dweep aate hai. chota sunda dweep samooh ke saath milakar yah sunda dweep samooh kii rachana karte hai. 
 bada sunda dweep samooh ke adhiktar dweep indonesia kaa hissaa hai. haalanki, borneo naamak dweep bruneii, indonesia or malaysia ke bich vibhajit he. is dweep par bruneii kaa adhiktar bhaag, indonesia ke chaar rajya kramash: madhya kalimantan, purv kalimantan, pashchim kalimantan or dakshin kalimantan; or malaysian rajya sabaah or sarawak sthit hai.

 chote asann dweep bhi shaamil hai.
