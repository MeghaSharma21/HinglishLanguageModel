 jeevvaigyanik jivan chakra, yaa keval jivan chakra, un badlaavon kaa kram he jisamen koi jeev nikalataa he or antat apni aarmabhik rup va sthiti main laut jaataa he. jeev janmata he or aayu ke saath viksit hota he or phir prajnan kar ke agali peedi ko janm detaa he. alag-alag jeevvaigyanik jatiyon kaa jivan chakra bhi bhinn hota he. kisi main prajnan langik hota he or kisi main alangik.
