 dharmendra singh coli dilli se bhartiya raajnneetijna hai. wo aam aadmi party ke sadsy hai or disambar 2013 main dilli vidhansabha ke liye simapuri nirvachan kshetra se nirvaachit huye.
 coli ne dasvin kaksha akhil bhartiya madhyamik vidhyalay pariksha se 2005 main kii.
 varsh 2005 se 2013 tak coli, arvind kejariwal or manish sisodiya dwaara sthapit parivartan naamak sanstha main saamaajik kaaryakarta va apni bahan santosh coli ke sahaayak rahe. santosh coli aam aadmi party kii sadasya thi. wo dilli se soochna kaa adhikaar adhinium evam saamaajik kaaryakarta thi. disambar 2013 ke dilli vidhaan sabha chunaaon ke liye wo aap kii simapuri vidhansabha seat se daavedaar thi. unhen chunaavon se purv june 2013 main dilli se sate kaushambi main ek tej raftaar car ne takkar maar di thi. santosh coli ke nidhan ke baad agast 2013 main dharmendra ko simapuri se aap ummidavaar niyukt kiya gaya. unhone is seat par bhartiya rashtriya congress ke vir singh dhingan ko haraaya. yah anusuchit jati or janajati ke liye arakshit seat he jahaan 2013 tak hameshaa congress ummidavaar vijeta rahaa he.
 coli avivaahit hai or sundar nagari, dilli ke punarvaas colony main apane parivaar ke saath rahete hai yah colony simapuri nirvachan kshetra ke antargat aata he. unke ek badi bahan he jinka naam ushaa he.
