 missile praudyogiki niyantran vyavastha, jise sankshipt main ium tii si aar bhi kehete hai, kai deshon kaa ek anaupcharik sanghathan he jinke paas prakshepastra va maanav rahit vimaan se sambandhit praudyogic kshamta he or jo ise failane se rokane ke liye niyam sthapit karte hai. june 2016 main isamen 35 desh shaamil the. 27 june 2016 ko bharat iskaa purna sadsy ban gaya.
 gair-sadasyon ko prakshepastra va dronon se sambandhit praudyogiki kareedne va bechney donon main kathinaayeeyaan hoti hai. sadasyon main yah aapasi niyam he ki yadi ek sadsy kisi desh ko koi takanic yaa upkaran dene se manaa kar de to har anya sadsy bhi us desh ko manaa kar detaa he.
 is sanghathan main sammilit hone ke liye yah aavashyak he ki koi bhi vartmaan sadsy prastaavit desh ke liye aapatti na jatlaye. san 2015 main bharat kii sadsyta ko italy ne roak liya tha, haalanki 2016 main bharat ke viruddh koi aapatti na aane se uski sadsyta kaa maarg saral ho gaya he. china ne san 2004 main sadsyta ke liye arzi rakhi thi lekin pakistan, soudi arab or anya kuch deshon ke saath uski dhili niryaat prakriyaaon ke sambandh main uthati rahi aapattiyon ke kaaran use roka jaataa rahaa he. bharat pahili baar kisi aantararashtriya str ke niryaat niyantran gat main shaamil hua he .
