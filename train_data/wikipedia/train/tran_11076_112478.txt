 nishchay 1992 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. film main vinod khanna, salmaan khan or karishma kapur ne mukhya bhoomika nibhaai he.
 ravi yadav manohar singh kaa naukar he. uskaa chota bhai rohan yadav hai, jise vah ek safal or achche vakil ke rup main dekhnaa chahta he. ravi par apane premika parvati se balaatkaar karne kaa aarope lagaaya jaataa he or use 12 saal kii jail kii sajaa sunai di jaati he. manohar kii patni renuka ravi ko aashwast karti he ki vah rohan ko vakil banane main madad karegi.
 sangeet o pi naiyyar ne diya he.
