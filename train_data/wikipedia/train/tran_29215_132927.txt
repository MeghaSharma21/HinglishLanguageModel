 hira kai rangon main miltaa he jinamein laal rang ke heere ko blad diamond kaha jaataa he. isaki prapti ke peeche hone vaale khun-kharabe ke kaaran hi ise blad diamond yani khooni hira kaha jaataa he. inhen kaarnon se ise bhidant yani kanflict diamond yaa war diamond bhi kehete hai. yah africa main sierra leone or liberia main sabase zyada paae jate hai. blad diamond ko kanflict diamond kahe jane kaa ek bada kaaran he. 1989 se 2001 tak iske utpaadak sierra leone or liberia grih yuddh ke shikaar the. aisi sthiti main liberian raashtrapati karlos ji. toiler ne amuly blad diamond ke badale hathiyaar kharidne shuru kiye the. is kaaran duniya bhar main toiler kii aalochana hui.
 asal main, blad diamond ke khanan kaa kaam mul rup se kisaan or majdoor karte the. heere kii taskari badhane ke kaaran in logon se kam vetan par din raat kaam karaayaa jane laga, jiskaa inhone virodh kiya. iske baad, liberia main kanoonee rup se diamond khanan udyog kii sthapana hui. 1998 main galobal vitaness naam kii ek santhaan ne africa main diamond or dangon ke bich ke sambandhon par ek report prastut kii thi. haal main super model naomi campbell blad diamond ke kaaran charchaa main hai. 1997 main toiler ne cambel ko blad diamond kaa ek paauch diya tha. is kaaran toiler kaa sahyogi bataaya ja rahaa he. philhal yah jaanch ke adheen he.
