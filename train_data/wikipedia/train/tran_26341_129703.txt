 tinitus sharir ke baahar se nahin, balki sir main anubhoot/sunai dene vaale shor ko varnit karne ke liye prayukt chikitsa shabd he. ve aksar bajane vaali yaa sisakaari bharne vaali dhvaniyaan hai lekin ve gaharaai se gunjyman, khadkhadaane, chitakane vaali dhvaniyaan, spandit shor, layaaddh, madhyavarti yaa sthai bhi ho sakati hai. ve ek kaan main, baayein yaa daayen, yaa donon main ho sakati hai. unka wolum bhi badalata rahata he. prabhavit logon ke liye, dhvaniyaan kaafii apriy ho sakati hai or unki sunne kii kshamta ko bigaad sakati he.
 lagbhag 40% aabaadi ko apane jivan-kaal ke ek hi bindu par apane kaanon main apriy dhwani kaa anubhav ho saktaa he or 10 se 20% chirkaalik tinitus se anubhav kar sakate hai. ye lakshan saamanytah 40 yaa adhik umr vaale logon main dekhe ja sakate hai. tathaapi, sabhi umr vaale vyaktiyon ko iskaa saamana karnaa pad saktaa he.
