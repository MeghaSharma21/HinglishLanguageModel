 ishan avasthi ) ek aath varshiya ladkaa, jo skool se nafrat karata he. uske liye har vishay mushkil he or vah sadaa us pariksha main vifal ho jaataa he. us main motor kaushal ke samanvay kaa bhi abhaav he or vah ek sidhi rekha main gend phenkane ke liye mushkil sa rasta dhoondta he. use jab apane shikshakon or sahpaathiyon se madad milani chahie us samay uske upar saarvajanik apmaan lagaataar kaam karata he isi samay, ishan ke aantarik duniya aashcharya se bhar jaataa he kii uski saraahna karne ke liye koi nahin he : jaadui bhoomi, rang or banabati pashuon se bharaa lagata he.
 ghar par bhi sthiti acchhee nahin he unke pita, nandakishor avasthi ek safal vidwaan / athlete he, jo ek tathy ke rup main use hameshaa yaad dilaati he. khoj ke baad ishan skool se nikal gaya, grad kam hone ke kaaran uske, mata pita ne nishchay kiya kii use anushaasit hone ke liye boarding skool main bhaag lene kii zarurat he
 boarding skool ke jivan main uske aatm vishwaas ke liye koi madade nahin kiya, utkrushtataa praapt karne ke liye Is rajan damodaran tanay chheda, ke saath dosti kii dekha gaya kii vah bhay or avsaad kii ek satat rajya ke rup main ab vah jaanata he kii use parivaar se alag karne ke liye aghat ke saath joraa gaya he haalanki, yah parivartan, tab hua jab ek nayaa asthaayee kala adhyaaapak ram shankar nikumbh yaa "nikumbh sahab" ishan ke boarding skool se jurate he. nikumbh, yah spasht he ki unki vishisht shikshan style ke saath uski sakht purvavarti, alag he jo jald hi chaatro ke bich lokapriy ho jaati haiunhone dekha ki ishan naakhush he or class kii gatividhiyon main gaiyr yogadanakarta ke rup main he pareshaan, nikumbh ishan ke ateet ke kaam kii samiksha karata he or yah chintan karata he kii vastav uski "vifaltaaon" kaa kaaran dyslexia he.
 uski chuttee kaa din par, nikumbh ishan ke mata pita se mila or apane kaam kii zyada samiksha karne ke liye poochta he. vah ishan ke chitron kii milawat se bhi stabdh he. nikumbh ishaan ke mata pita se kahata he kii ishaan ek ujjwal baccha he uske jaanane kii prakriya varg ke anya bacchon kii tulana main alag he. vah un se dyslexia varnan kiya or kaha ki yah ek snaivik sthiti he jo kii mand buddhi kii nishaani nahin he. vah unase yah bhi kaha kii vah unhone yah bhi vah atirikt padhai pradaan karenge jo ishaan ko safal karne main madad karegaa.is vaktavy ke samarthan karne ke liye, nikumbh ne ishaan kii kalatmak kshamta uske kai chitron or rachanaatmak kaaryon dwaara pradarshan ke rup main daalaa .aadesh main apni baat karne ke liye, nikumbh ishan ke pita se japani paath ke saath ek box padhane kii .maang karata he jab shri avasthi ne kaha gaya he ki vah paath nahin pard sakate hai, nikhumbh use fatkaar lagaataa he. aisa karne main, nikhumbh unhen ishan ke saath ek dainik aadhaar par sangharsh darshata he.
