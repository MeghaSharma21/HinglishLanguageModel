 larung gaar buddha academy, chini: 喇荣五明佛学院) san 1980 main, tibeti buddha vidwaan khanpo jigmed phunchhogas dwaara sthapit ek yiiuma sampradayik tibeti buddha viapeeth he. yah vidhyalay serta county, garaje, sichuaan praant main sthit he. is shaikshik santhaan uddeshy tibeti buddha dharm main ek duniyavi prashikshan pradaan karne ke liye or, san 1966-76 kii chinia saanskrithik kranti dwaara kiya gaya vinaashkari prabhaav kaa dhyaan main lekar tibeti buddha chaatravrutti naveekaran ke jarurat ko puraa karne ke liye kiya gaya he. iske duurastha sthaan ke baavjood, yah duniya main tibeti buddha adhyayan ke liye sabase bada or sabase prabhaavshaalee kendron main se ek he, yah kii chaatro kaa sankhya main 10,000 se adhik bhikshuon, bhikshuniyon he.
 beijing. china ke changdu se kareeb 600 kimi dur yah skool 1980 main ek imaarat main shuru hua tha. ab falkar kasbe main badal gaya he. kasbe kaa nirmaan sirf daan se hua he.
 naam he larung gaar buddhist academy. yah duniya kaa sabase bada rihaayshi buddha vidhyalay he. yahaan par tibet ke paaramparik buddha shiksha kaa adhyayan karne ke liye kai deshon se bachche aate hai. yahaan sabhi gharon ke rang laal or bhure hai. ladke-ladakiyon ke rahane vaale ilaakon ko sadakon se baanta gaya he.
