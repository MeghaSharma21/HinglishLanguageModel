 dashoguz, jiskaa purana naam 'tashauz' tha, turkmenistan ke dashoguz praant kii rajdhani he. san 2009 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 2,27,184 thi. yah shahar turkmenistan ke uttar main uzbekistan ke kaaraqalpaustan praant ke saath kii sarhad par sthit he. 'dashoguz' shabd kaa matlab turkmen bhashaa main 'pathar kaa chashma' hota he.
 dashoguz kii aabaadi 1999 main 1,66,500 se badhkar 2004 main 2,10,000 anumaanit kii gai. yahan ke zyaadaatar log turkmen or uzbek hai, haalanki yahan kuch roosi, kaaraqalpaq, tartar or korian bhi rahete hai. puraane jamaane main yah apane pani ke chashme kii vajah se aitihaasik resham maarg par rukne kii ek jagah hua karti thi, jis se is shahar kaa naam padaa. 19veen sadi main yah ilaaka roosi samrajya kii giraft main aayaa or unhone yahan ek qila banaaya jiske ird-gird basti bad gai. aadhunik dashoguz soviet-lahaze main bana ek shahar he jisamen bahut se smarak or sangrahalay hai. yahan se bahut se paryatak konya-urgench ke puraatan sthal pahunchne ke liye guzarate hai jo 12veen sadi main khwaaresm rajya kii rajdhani hua karata tha. aamu dariyaa kii nikatataa se yahan kapaas or jute paidaa kii jaati he. ek aitihaasik ghatana main 5 sitambar 1998 main ek 7 kilo kaa ulkapind aasmaan se is shahar par gira.
