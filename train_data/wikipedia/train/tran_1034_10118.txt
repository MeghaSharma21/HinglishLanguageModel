 plastic, shanshleshit athwa ardhashanshleshit carbonic thos padaarthon ke ek bade samooh kaa saamaanya naam he. isse bahut saare audyogic utpaada nirmit hote hai. plastic praayah ucch anubhaar vaale bahulak hote hai jinamein mulya kam karne yaa adhik kaaryaksham banane ke liye kuch anya padaarth bhi mishrit kiye ja sakate he.
 plastic padaarth or plastic alag-alag hai. ek gun ke rup main plastic un padaarthon kii visheshta kaa dyotak he jo adhik khinchne yaa taanane se sthaayee rup se apana rup badal dete hai or apane mul swaroop main nahin laut pate.
