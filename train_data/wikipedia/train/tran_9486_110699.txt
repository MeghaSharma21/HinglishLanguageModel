 mudranalay, printing press, chhapaakhaanaa yaa chapai kii press ek yantric yukti he jo daab daalkar kaagaj, kapade aadi par print karne ke kaam aati he. kapadaa yaa kaagaj aadi par ek syaahi-yukt sataha rakhkar usapar daab daalaa jaataa he jisase syahiyukt sataha par bani chhavi ulte rup main kaagaj yaa kapade par chap jaati he.
 chapai kii press kii rachana sabase pehle germany ke johaan gutenberg ne san 1439 mem kii thi. lakdi ke thappon evam muwable tip se chapai kii takanic kuch sau varsh pehle se hi china main vidyaman thi. lekin ve gutanberg kii tarah ek daabak kaa prayog nahin karte the. gutanberg ke press par aadhaarit chapai kii vidhi yorape main badi teji se faili. iske baad vah saare sansar main fail gayi. antatah printig press ne chapai kii paramparaagat vidhiyon ko ukhaad phenkaa. isi prakaar baad main ofaset chapai ke aa jane ke baad printing press bhi jaataa rahaa.
 printing press ke avishkaar se soochna evam gyaan ke prasar main ek kranti aa gayi. isaliye printing press kaa avishkaar ek mahaan avishkaar mana jaataa he. aisa mana jaataa he ki pehle bhashaa kaa prayog, uske baad lipi evam lekhan kaa prayog evam uske baad pranting press kaa avishkaar, gunatmak rup se duniya ke teen sabase bade avishkaar hai jinhone gyaan evam vidya ke prasar evam vikaas main baari yogadan kiya. isi kadi main chauthaa avishkaar antarjaal ko mana jaataa he.
