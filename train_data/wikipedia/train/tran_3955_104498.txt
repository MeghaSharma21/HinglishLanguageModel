 alkzandar hamilatan sanyukt rajya america ke pehle khazaane ke sachiv the. ve ek arthshaastri or raajnneetijna bhi the or unhen sanyukt rajya kii ek buniyadi hasti bhi mana jaataa he. america kranti ke daur main ve america ke rashtrapita george washington ke sahaayak rahe or america main sanvaidhanik raaj kii maang rakhi. kai itihaaskaar maanate hai ke "america ke prashasnic or rajnatik dhaanche main sab se adhik haath alkzandar hamilatan kaa hi tha".
