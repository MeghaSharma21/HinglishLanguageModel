 vikarn dhritarashtra ke sau putron main se, ek jo maharathi hone ke atirikt param nyaayi evam vivekpurna tha. yah kurukshetra ke yuddh main bhim dwaara maaraa gaya tha.
 vikarn ek nyaaypurna vyakti tha. usne chitrayuddh or chitrayodhin kaa vadh kiya. draupadi swayamvar main yah upasthit tha. yah bada nyaayi tha, evam draupadivastraharan ke samay, vidura kii tarah isane bhi is paapakarm ko ore ghrinaa prakat kii thi. mahabharat ke yuddh main iskaa nimnlikhit yoddhaon ke saath yuddh hua thah- sahadev, ghatotkach, nakul. ant main bhimsen ne iskaa vadh kiya. us samay iske liye usne bahut dukh prakat kiya tha.
