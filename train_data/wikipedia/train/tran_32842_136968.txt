 avadhnarayan mudgal hindi sahitya ke varisht chintak, lekhak, sampadak evam yatra-lekhak hai. aapane ‘sarika’ kaa 27 varshon tak sampadan kiya. iske atirikt aapane ‘vama’ evam ‘chaaya-mayur’ patrikaon kaa bhi sampadan kiya he. ‘meri katha-yatra’, ‘kabandh’, ‘mumbai kii diary’, ‘ek furlongs kaa safarnama’ charchit kritiyaan hai. aap sahitya academy dwaara sammanit kiye gaye hai.
