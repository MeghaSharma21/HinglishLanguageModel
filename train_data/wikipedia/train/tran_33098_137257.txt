 vishwa paryaton diwas 27 sitambar ko manaaya jaataa he. isaki shuruyaat varsh 1980 main sanyukt raashtra vishwa paryaton sanghathan ke dwaara hui is tithi ke chunaav kaa mukhya kaaran yah tha ki varsh 1970 main UNWTO kii murti ko sweekaara gaya tha. is murti ko swikaarna vaishvik paryaton ko badhaava dene ke prayaas hetu mile ke pathar ke rup main dekha jaataa he evam is diwas ko manaane kaa uddeshy vishwa main is baat ko prasaarit tathaa jaagrukta failaane ke liye hai ki kis prakaar paryaton vaishvik rup se, saamaajik, saanskrithik, raajnneetik tathaa aarthik mulyon ko badhaane main tathaa aapasi samaj badhaane main sahaayta kar saktaa he. 
 istambul turki main oktober 1997 ko barahavin UNWTO mahasabha ne yah faisla liya ki prayek varsh sanghathan ke kisi ek desh ko ham vishwa paryaton diwas manaane ke liye sahyogi rakh sakate hai isi pariklpana main vishwa paryaton diwas varsh 2006 main europe main 2007 main south asia main 2008 main america main 2009 main africa main tathaa 2011 main madhya purv kshetra yah deshon main manaaya gaya. varsh 2013 main is kaarykram kaa vishay paryaton or pani, hamaare saajhe bhavishya kii raksha tha or 2014 main paryaton or saamudayik vikaas. 
 da let ignatius amaduva atigabi naamak ek nigeria ke raashtra ne yah vichaar diya ki prayek varsh 27 sitambar ko vishwa paryaton diwas manaaya jaae or varsh 2009 main unke abhootpurv yogadan ko dekhte hue ise saharsha sweekaar bhi kar liya gaya.
