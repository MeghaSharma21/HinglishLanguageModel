 saint james house monmouth, monmouthshire, wales main ek grad dwitiya soochibaddh imaarat he. yah aitihaasik saint james squaire main madhyakaalin nagar deevaaron ke bheetar maujuud he. jabaki ghar par vartmaan samay main aakarshak 18 veen sadi kaa agavada he, parantu multah is ghar ne bargej makaan ke rup main janm liya tha. iske alaava ghar ke peeche khudaai main ek bhattha hone ke saboot mili hai, jisamen madhyakaalin va madhyayug ke baad ke samay ke mitti ke bartanon ke avshesh paae gaen hai. varsh 2010 main saint james squaire main hue puratathvik utkhanan main pehali baar monmouth shahar main madhyapashan kaal kii basti hone ke pramaan milen hai. saint james house ke haal hi ke nivaasiyon main monmouth skool ke boarding chaatr shaamil hai.
 saint james house whitecross street ke purvi chor par sthit he jahaan yah sadak ticone saint james squaire ko gherne vaale gol chakkar par ja kar khatm ho jaati he. imaarat chauraahey kii uttari disha main sthit he, jahaan 2009 main puratathvik khudaai kii gai thi. aadhunik samay ke kuch maanchitron main whitecross street kaa purvi hissaa, jo monk street ke saath pratichchhed se saint james squaire ko gherne vaale gol chakkar par ja kar khatm hota he, use bhi saint james squaire ke hisse ke taur par hi sanderbhit kiya jaataa he, is prakaar whitecross street par sthit kuch imaaraton ke do pate he. isi prakaar saint james house ke bhi do pate hai: 10 saint james squaire or 10 whitecross street.
 saint james house ko 27 june 1952 ko grad dwitiya shrenee kii imaarat kaa darja praapt hua tha. teen manjila is imaarat pe atharahavin sadi kaa agavada he. iskaa bahaari hissaa laal eenton se bana hua he. neeche kii manjilon kii be khidkiyaan ytaakar he jabaki sabase upar kii manzil kii vargakar. ek apwaad pehali manzil pe he jahaan ki bich kii ek khidki baaki sabhi khidakiyon se lambii or upar se golakaar he. daravaaje ke upar phanlit he. ghar ke andar satrahavin sadi kaa thodaa bahut plaster kaa kaam kiya hua he.
