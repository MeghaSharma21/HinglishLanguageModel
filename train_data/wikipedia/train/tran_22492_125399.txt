 mogul-e-aazam hindi bhashaa kii ek film he jo 1960 main pradarshit hui. yah film hindi cinema itihaas kii saphaltam filmon main se he. ise ke asif ke shandaar nirdesan, bhavy seton, behtareen sangeet ke liye aaj bhi yaad kiya jaataa he.
 film akbar ke bete shahzada saleem or darbar kii ek kaneez nadira ke bich main prem kii kahani dikhaati he. nadira ko akbar dwaara anaarakali kaa kitaab diya jaataa he. film main dikhaaya gaya he ki saleem or anaarakali main dhire-dhire pyaar ho jaataa he or akbar isse naakhush hote hai. anaarakali ko kaidakhane main band kar diya jaataa he. saleem anaarakali ko chhudaane kii naakaam koshish karata he. akbar anaarakali ko kuch samay baad reeha kar dete hai. saleem anaarakali se shaadi karnaa chahta he par akbar isaki ijazat nahin dete. saleem baghawat kii ghoshnaa karata he. akbar or saleem kii senaaon main jang hoti he or saleem pakadaa jaataa he. saleem ko baghawat ke liye maut kii sazaa sunai jaati he par aakhri pal akbar kaa ek mulaazim anaarakali ko aata dekh top kaa munh mod detaa he. iske baad akbar anaarakali ko ek behosh kar dene vaala pankh detaa he jo anaarakali ko apane hijaab main lagaakar saleem ko behosh karnaa hota he. anaarakali aisa karti he. saleem ko ye bataaya jaataa he ki anaarakali ko diwaar main chinava diya gaya he par vastav main usee raat anaarakali or uski maan ko rajya se baahar bhej diya jaataa he.
 sangeet naushaad dwaara diya gaya he. adhiktar geet lata ne gaaye hai.
