 minakshi shukla bhartiya antariksh anusandhan sanghathan yani isro main mangal yaan abhiyaan kii project manager hai. jis mangal abhiyaan ko lekar puri duniya main aaj bharat kaa naam roshan he, us abhiyaan kii minakshi bhi ek stambh hai. ve 1999 se isro main scientist he. minakshi par jimmedaari he ki vah mangal yaan kii har gatividhi par najar rakhen. saath hi unki jimmedaari yaan se mili tasveeron ko di-code karne kii he.
 minakshi madhya pradesh ke bilaspur shahar kii hai. unhone shahar ke barjesh skool main hindi medium se 10veen-12veen kii padhai kar puure rigen main top aane kaa record banaaya he.
