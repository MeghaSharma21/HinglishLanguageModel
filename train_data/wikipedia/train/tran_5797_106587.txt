 georgia sanyukt rajya america kaa ek rajya he, jo agrey kshetra main sthit he. iski rajdhani or sabase bada shahar atlanta he. iske pashchim main albama or dakshin main florida rajya sthit hai. uttari disha main tennesi, north kerolina or uttar purv disha main south kerolina rajya sthit hai. purv disha kii ore andh mahaasaagar kaa samudratat he.
 georgia 1788 main america kaa rajya bana. jarjia un 13 colony main se ek he jo britain se swatantra hone ke liye vidroh kiye.
 georgia kii rajdhani or sabase bada shahar atlanta he. yahan 1996 ke olympic pratiyogita khele gayi. georgia ke anya bade shahar mekan, columbus or savana hai.
