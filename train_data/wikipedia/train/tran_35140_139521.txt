 banu saqeef yaa banu qaqeef​ pigambar mohammad ke kaal main ek arabi qabeela tha. yah tif shahar kaa mukhya qabeela tha or iske vanshaj ab saudi arab, syria, lebanon, misr, jordan, iraq or tunisia jaise kai deshon main based hue hai. aksar inke naam main 'saqaafi' naam judaa hota he. islam ke aane se pehle yah al-laat naamak arabi devi ke upaask hua karte the.
 arabi lipi kaa ث‎ akshar bhartiya upmahadweep or iran main 'sa' uccharit kiya jaataa he jabaki arabi prayadweep main iskaa aam uccharan bindu-vaala 'ji' he. isliye 'saqeef' or 'qaqeef​' donon thik hai. dhyaan den ki bindu-vaale 'qa' kaa uccharan bhi 'k' se miltaa-julata lekin zaraa alag he.
