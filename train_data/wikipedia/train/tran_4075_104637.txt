 begum r'aana liyaaqat ali khan, pakistan kii pratham mahila, pakistan ke pehle pradhanmantri liyaaqat ali khan kii begum, pakistan andolan ke sadsy or sindh kii pehali mahila rajyapal thim.
 begum r'aana liyaaqat ali lakhanu, british bharat main 1912 i main paidaa huin. praarambhik shiksha nainital ke ek stri hai skool main praapt kii. lakhanu vishvavidhyalay se eme arthshaastra or dharmik adhyayan kii padhai kiya. kuch samay shikshak rahen. 1933 main khan liyakat ali khan se shaadi hui. pakistan banane se pehle aap mahilaon ke ek sanghathan "apua" kii sthapana kii. sthapana pakistan ke baad rojgaar exange ragava gayi ladakiyon kii talash or shaadiyon vibhaag unke hawaale kiye gaye. sanyukt kii baithak yojit 1952 main pakistan ke pratinidhi ke rup main shaamil thim. 1954 i. main netherland or baad main italy main safairaar sindh ke rajyapal bhi rahe.
 begum r'aana liyaaqat ali 1990 main harakat dil band hone ke kaaran karachi main nidhan based or tirth netaa parisar liyakat ali khan ke pahalu main ambeded hai.
