 pracheen roman danyube nadi ke uttar main rahane vaale "barbar kabeelon" vaale deshon ko germania kaha karte the, jiske naam par angrezi shabd 'Germany' padaa. ye kabeeley 'puraani german bhashaa' kii boliyaan bolte the. dhire-dhire inkaa isaikaran hua or german desh isai pavitra roman samrajya kaa kendra ban gaya.
 duusari shatabdi isa purv main pashchimi europe main german jatiyon ke abhyuday kaa ullekh miltaa he. kuch jatiyaan jaise alaamanni bargandian, frank lamboard ostrogoth or vijigoth purv main rin nadi ke muhane, pashchim main albe nadi or dakshin main uttari italy ke bhaagon ke bich dhire-dhire bases. unamem se kuch ne italy par akraman kiya or rome samrajya kaa vinaash kiya, anya france or britain main bas gai. rin nadi ke donon ore kaa kshetra kuch din vivaad main rahane ke pashchaat frankon ke roman samrat sharlmen dwaara naveen shatabdi main adhikrut kiya gaya. lekin shatabdi ke antim dinon german samrajya teen bhaagon main bant gaya.
 sascan samrat oto pratham ne 962 i. main italy or germany ko ek sutara main bandha. aage chalakar ashantipurna sthiti utpann hui. fredrick dwitiya ne apane shashan ko sicily main hi kendrit rakhaa, is prakaar germany lagbhag upekshit rahaa. 1273 main hapsburg kaa rudalph samrat nirvaachit hua, kintu uske liye bhi bade samrajya ko kaayam rakhana asaadhya ho gaya tha.
 roman samrajya jis samay ladkadaa rahaa tha, england, france ore spain shaktishaalee rajya ban rahe the. germany us samay samrudh tha- iske viruddh uparyukt teenon rajyon ne sandhi kii.
 lekin germany kii rajnatik asthirataa ke kaaran vahaan 16veen shatabdi main martin luther ke netritv main andolan hua. ant main is andolan ne 30 varshiya dharmayuddh kaa rup liya. isamen germany ke lagbhag 300 tukade hue. 18veen shatabdi main in choti-choti swatantra ikaayiyon ne prasha main atyadhik unnati kii.
 francisi kranti ore nepolian ke yudhon ke samay se germany main rashtriyata kii chetana kaa aavirbhaav hua. yah chetana aage chalakar udaaravaadi andolan ke rup main badali. 1871 main tatkaaleen chancelar atowan bismark ne austria, denmark or france se yuddh karke german rajya ko sangathit kiya. france kii paraajay ke baad germany ne sainik, audyogic or aarthik kshetron main teji se pragati kii. visamark ne is sthiti main anya europea shaktiyon se sambandh sthapit kiya. 1888 i. main wiliam dwitiya samrat hua. desh kii antarrashtriya sthiti main pun: ashaanti utpann hui, jisane 20veen shatabdi main pratham vishwayuddh kaa rup liy. is yuddh main german senaayen parajeet hui. is paraajay se utpann aarthik or saamaajik avyavashtaaon tathaa 'mitra rashtron' kii 'varsa-sandhi' ke anusaar aarthik dabaavon kii paristhiti main adolf hitler tathaa neshnal socialist german workars party ne 1933 main germany kii satta grahan kii. pratham vishwayuddh ke baad vemar samvidhan ke anusaar ganraajya ghoshit germany main hitler ne apana adhinaayakatv sthapit kiya. usne apane shasankaal main germany ko sabhi kshetron main sudrudh kiya. uski samrajyavaadee niti ne, jisase usne europe kaa bada bhaag 1939 tak kuch sandhiyon se or kuch sainik tareekon se germany main jod liya, dwitiya vishwayuddh kii paristhitiyaan mitra rashtron ke samaksh aatmasamarpan karnaa padaa. rus, britain, sanyukt rajya america or france ne germany ke chaar bhaag karke paraspar baant liye. 1949 main shanti samjhaute ke anusaar germany kii fedral german republic or german demacretic republic do bhaag hue. purvi bhaag, jisamen purvi prasha bhi sanmilit he, jo ki 1937 ke purv germanon ke or ab poland or rus ke adhikaar main hai.
