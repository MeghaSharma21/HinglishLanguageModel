 elvin ishak kaalicharan guyana se bhartiya mul ke purv westeindies cricket team ke ek khilaadi hai jinhone 1972 se 1981 tak team ke liye cricket khelaa tha. kaalicharan kaa janm georgetaun, british guyana main hua tha. vah baayen haath ke ballebaaj or daaen haath ke of spinner the. vah 1973 ke vizden cricketer of da year the. vah 1975 or 1979 tiimon kaa hissaa the jo cricket vishwa cap jiti thi. unhen angrezi county cricket main warvikshire ke saath safalataa mili. jab 1977-1978 main clive loyad ne istipha de diya tab unhen westeindies kaa captain niyukt kiya gaya tha.
 kul milakar elvin ne 66 test match khela jisamen unhone 12 shatak or 44.43 ausat se 4,399 ran banaae. 31 ek divasiy antarrashtriya main unhonen 826 ran 34.41 kii ausat se banaae. pratham shrenee cricket ke 505 maichon main unki 32,650 ran 87 shatak lagaakar banaae.
