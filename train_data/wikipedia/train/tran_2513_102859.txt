 andaaz david dhawan dwaara nirdeshit 1994 main bani bhartiya hindi bhashaa kii film he. isamen anil kapur, juhi chawla, karishma kapur or kadar khan mukhya abhinayta hai. anya kalakaaron main raaj babbar, shakti kapur, satish kowshik, or tej sapru shaamil hai. yah 1992 kii telugu film sundarkanda kii rimek he jisamen venkatesh abhinayta he. yah swayan 1992 kii tamil film sundar kandam kii rimek thi, jisamen ke. bhagyaraj or bhanupriya abhinayta the.
 ajay es tii skool kaa ek budhimaan purv chaatr he jisko ek shikshak ke rup main usee vidhyalay main niyukt kiya jaataa he. jaya, jo apni kaksha main padhai karte hai use shararti harakat khelkar pareshaan karti he. jaya ke saath rishte main ajay ke na aane se wo naaraaz he. usne use chunauti di ki vah ek din uski patni ban jaaegi. jaldbaaji main vah ek ashikshit anaath ladki saraswati se shaadi karata he. jaya sab kuch, khaanaa pakaane se lekar padhanaa sikhaakar saraswati se mitrataa kar leti he. ek din kuch aatankwaadi skool par hamalaa karte hai or bacchon ko bandhak banaate hai. ajay, jaya or sab funs jate hai. jaya ajay ko bachaane ke liye apana jivan de detii he. vahaan saraswati bataati he ki vah ajay ke liye jaya ke pyaar ke bare main jaanati thi or yahi bhavanaaen ajay ke dil main thim. jise usne apni patni ke prati apni vafadari ke kaaran kabhi prakat nahin kiya tha. film ke ant main jaya mar jaati he.
