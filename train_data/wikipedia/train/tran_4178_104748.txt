 jhunaka bhaakar ek shaakaahaari paaramparik maharashtra kaa pakwaan he saath hi goa or uttari karnataka main bhi banaaya jaataa he. ise pithalaa yaa pithale ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. iske avyav main besan kaa aataa hota hai, jise arddh thos paste banane ke liye pani ke saath taiyaar kiya jaataa hai. iske baad ise hari mirch, laal mirch powder, haldi, namak, tala hua pyaaj, sarson ke beej, adarak-lahasun, jire ke beej, dhaniya pattiyon jaise kisi anya mishrit saamagree or roti ke saath paaramparik rup se parosa jaataa he. pakwaan ko jhunaka bhaakar ke alaava pithala bhakri bhi kaha jaataa he.
