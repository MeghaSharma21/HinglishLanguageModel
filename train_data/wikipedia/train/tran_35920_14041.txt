 roza luiz makkoli parks africa-america naagarik adhikaar kaaryakrtta thim jinhone sanyukt rajya america kii congress ne "da first ladi of civil ritis" or "da madar of da freedom movement" naamon se pukaara.
 rojaa luiz makkoli kaa janm teskagi, alabama par 4 farbari 1913 ko hua tha.
 1 disambar 1955 ko montgomeri, alabama main jab bas main kaam se ghar waapas aa rahi thi or apni seat par bas main baithi hui thi. ek gore ne usko seat chhodane ko kaha. roza apni seat par baithi rahi. usko police ne pakad liya parantu agle din usko chadha liya gaya. adaalat ne usapar 5 disambar ko jurmaana kar diya. isi din ko africa-amareeki samudaay ne milakar bason kaa boycott kiya.
