 iceland ke athlete ne pehali baar olympic khelon main 1908 main bhaag liya.
 rashtriya olympic samiti 1921 main banaai gai thi or 1935 main antarrashtriya olympic samiti dwaara manyata praapt thi. 1936 ke baad, iceland ne har greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main pratispardhaa karne ke liye athleates ko bhej diya he. iceland ne 1948 ke baad se sheetakaaleen olympic khelon ke sabhi sanskarnon main bhi bhaag liya he, keval 1972 sheetakaaleen khelon main nahin hai.
 icelandic athleton ne kul chaar padak jite, athletics main do, judo main ek or handbal main ek.
