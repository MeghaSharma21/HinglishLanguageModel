 sanyukt raashtra nyaas parishad; United Nations Trusteeship Council; nyaas parishad ke paanch sadasyon main suraksha parishad ke sthaayee sadsy hi shaamil hote hai isaki sthapana aise nyaas pradeshon ke paryvekshan hetu kii gayi thi, jahaan dwitiya vishwayuddh kii samaapti ke baad swytt shashan kii shuruuaat nahin hoski thi. nyaas pradeshon ko sanyukt raashtra charter ke adhyay XII ke anusaar antarrashtriyanyas vyavastha ke adheen rakhaa gaya tha. is vyavastha kaa uddeshy antarrashtriya shanti va suraksha ko banaaye rakhana, nyaas pradeshon kii janata ke hiton ko savardhit karnaa bina kisi bhedbhaav ke maanav-adhikaron evam moulik swatantraaaon ke prati sammaan ko badhaava dena tathaa sabhi nyaas kshetron main sanyukt raashtra ke sadsy deshon kounke nagarikon ke saath samaan vyavhaar ko sunishchit karnaa he. mul rup main 11 nyaas pradesh the. nyaas parishad kaa kaary in kshetron main swatantra yaa swytt shashan kii sthapana main sahaayta dena tha.31 oktober, 1994 tak sabhi nyaas pradeshon ko pruthkra jyon yaa padosi deshon ke sathvilinikaran ke rup main swatantraaa praapt ho chuki thi. palau dweep samooh aisa antimanyas kshetra tha, jis par sanyukt rajya america dwaara prashasan kiya ja rahaa tha.palau main suraksha parishad dwaranyas samjhauta samaapt kiye jane ke pashchaat 1 november, 1994 ko nyaas parishad ne apane kaary aupchaarik rup se nilambit kar diye. phir bhi,nyaas parishad kaa vighatan nahin kiya gaya tha. yah ispravdhaan ke saath astitv main bani rahi ki aavashyaktaa ke anusaar ise punah kriyasheel kiya ja saktaa he. haal hi main, nyaas parishad kii bhumandaleey paryaavaran va sansaadhan pranali ke nyaasi rup main upyog karne kaa prastaav bhi saamne rakhaa gaya he.
