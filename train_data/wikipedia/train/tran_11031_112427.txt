 chukundar ek musla jad vaala vanaspati he. yah beta valgaris naamak jati ke paudhe hote hai jinhen manushyon ne shatabdiyon se krishi main paala he or kai naslon main viksit kara he. isaki musla jad aksar halaki-mithi hoti he or uskaa rang laal, jamuni, pilaa yaa shwet hota he.
 vishwa ke kai sthanon main chukundar kii jamuni jad ko kaccha, ubalkar yaa bhunkar khaaya jaataa he. ise akele yaa anya sabzi ke saath khaaya ja saktaa he, or iskaa achaar bhi daalaa jaataa he. bhartiya khaane main ise maheen katkar or halakaa-sa pakaakar mukhya khaane ke saath khaaya jaataa he. chukundar ke patton ko bhi khaaya jaataa he, inkaa prayog aahaar main kaccha, ubalkar yaa bhaap-dekar hota he. chukundar loha, vitamin or mineralas kaa bahut hi acha shrot he isiilie chukundar kaa aushadhiy upyog adhik kiya jaataa he|
