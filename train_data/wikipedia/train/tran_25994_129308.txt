 japan kii aatmaraksha sena japan kii ekikrut sena kaa naam he. iskaa gathan dwitiya vishwayuddh main japan ke aatmasamarpan ke baad kiya gaya tha. dwitiya vishwayuddh ke pashchaat adhikanshtah yah sena japan ke dwipon tak hi simit rahi he or videshon main ise kahin lagaaye jane kii anumati nahin he. kintu haal ke dinon main ye antararashtriya shantisthapana kii kaaryvaayiyon main bhaag liye hai. 
 haal ke tanaav ne phir se japani sena kii sthiti par bahes shuru kii he. san 2010 main ghoshit nai sainik dishanirdeshon ke anusaar ab aatmaraksha sena sovietsangh ke bajaay china par kendrit hogi.
