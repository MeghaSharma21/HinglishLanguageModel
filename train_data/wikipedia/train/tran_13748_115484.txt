  guan moye  ek chini upanyaaskaar or laghu katha lekhak hai. ve apane saahitiyik upnaam mo yaan se jane jate he or 2012 ke sahitya main nobel puraskaar praptakarta he. is shrenee me puraskaar praapt karne vaale ve pehle chini naagarik bane; haalanki 2000 ke vijeta gau chinjayan kaa janm china main hua par ve francisi naagarik he. 
 mo yaan kaa janm 17 farvari 1955 ko ek krishi parivaar main hua. 12 varsh ki aayu main unhone padhai chod kheti main kaam karnaa shuru kar diya. unhone 1981 main apni pehali pustak prakashit kii, lekin 1987 main red shorgam se unhe saahitiyik safalataa mili. ye upanyaas 1930 ke dashak main china par jab japan ne hamalaa kiya tha us kaal par aadharit he.
