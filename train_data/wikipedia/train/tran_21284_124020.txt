 apshisht jal ko upachaarit karne ke prakram ko apshisht jalopchaar kehete hai. apshisht jal us jal ko kehete hai jo ab us kaam main nahin aa saktaa jo iske thik pehle isse kiya gaya he. apshisht jal kaa upachaar karne ke baad praapt jal kaa punah upyog kiya ja saktaa he yaa use bahut kam paryaavarniya kshati ke bahisravit kar diya ja saktaa he jisase vah punah jal chakra main mil jaataa he. 'upachaar' kaa matlab apshisht jal se ashuddhiyon ko nikaalnaa he. upachaar kii kuch vidhiyaan saadhaaran jal or apshisht jal donon ke upachaar main kaam aatin hai. apshisht jal kaa upachaar karne vaale sanyantr ko 'apshisht jalopchaar sanyantr' kehete hai.
