 rangila rasul 1920 ke dashak main prakashit pustak he jo lahore ke raajapaal and sans ne prakashit kii thi. iske lekhak 'chamupathi em e' yaa kishan prasad pratab naamak aryasamaji the kintu lekhak kaa naam prakaashan ne kabhi nahin bataaya. yah pustak bahut vivaadaaspad siddh hui. isamen paigambar mohammad ke vivaah evam sex jivan kaa rochak dhang se varnan kiya gaya he. yah pustak multah urdu main chhapi thi kintu baad main iskaa hindi sanskaran bhi prakashit hua. yah pustak aaj tak bharat, pakistan or bangladesh main pratibandhit he. 
 iske jawaab main musalamaanon ke lagbhag sabhi guton kii taraf se moulana sanoullah amritsari ne urdu main mukadadas rasul bajawab rangila rasul likhi.
