 grasanal ke rog nimnlikhit vishesh rog hai:
 jiske antasth or bahirastha do prakaar ke kaaran hote hai. antasth main janmajaat rachanatruti, shoth, vran, sankat arbud tathaa tantrikajanya dashaayen ho sakati hai. cancer or saracoma donon hi prakaar ke arbud hote hai, kintu cancer adhik hota he. bahirastha kaarnon main grasanal se baahar ke sabhi prakaar ke arbudon se grasanal dab jaataa he. avatugranthi kii vruddhi, madhya antaraal kii vardhit lasakagranthiyaan, mahadhamani kii romurism, parihrid nissaran aadi bhi yah dasha utpann kar sakate hai. diphtheria ke kaaran tantrikashoth tathaa peshravsaad ke grasan ksht hota he.
 2. grasanal kaa shoth or vran tathaa vran ke pashchaat utpann hua sankat.
 3. grasanal kaa cancer neeche ke tritiyansh bhaag main, mukh main, adhik hota he. nigalane kii kathinaayee dhire dhire badhati jaati he. at: naal ek patali nalee ke samaan ho jaati he, jisase gadhi vastu niglana bhi kathin ho jaataa he. barium khilaakar eksre chitra lene se roganidan sahaj ho jaataa he. saracoma bhi hota he.
 4. hriddwar aakarsh - hriddwar sanvaranon peshi main baar baar aakarsh hone se grasanal kaa nichalaa bhaag vistrut ho jaataa he.
 5. shiravriddhi - vardhit shiraaon se raktastrav ho saktaa he.
