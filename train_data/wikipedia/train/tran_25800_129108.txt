 rabindra guha bangla sahitya ke bhukhi peedhee tathaa nimsahitya andolan se jude prakhyat kavi, kahanikar evam upanyaaskaar he. ve baan gladesh ke borishal jile main paidaa huye evam partishan ke samay apane mata-pita ke saath bharat chale aaye. padhaai tathaa naukari ke silsile ve kolkata, durgapur, bhilaai tathaa dilli main rah chuke he jiske kaaran unke likhane ke taur-tarikon main bengal ke baahar rahanevaale bengaliyon kaa bol-chaal zyada dikhaai detaa he. pravasike dwaara bole gayi unke sahitya bhashaa ko sukumaar chaudhuri, ajit raay, sudhanshu sen, barin ghoshaal, mrinal vanik, camel chakraborti, sanjeev niyogi pramukh vidwaanon ne naam diya he dayasporic. assam vishvavidhyalay ke upachaarya dctor tapodhir bhattacharya kaa kahana he kii rabindra guha lagaataar bhashaa ke vinirmaan main niojit hai. rabindra guha unke likhe drohpurush upanyaas ke liye jane jate hai.
