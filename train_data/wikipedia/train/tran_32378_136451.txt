 christopher columbus ek samudri-navik, upniveshvaadi, khoji yatri uski atlantic mahaasaagar main bahut se samudri yatraaon ke kaaran america mahadweep ke bare main europe main jaankaree badi. yadhyapi america pahunchne vaala vah pratham europea nahin tha kintu columbus ne europavaasiyon or america ke mul nivaasiyon ke bich vistrut sampark ko badhaava diya. usne america kii chaar baar yatra kii jiskaa kharch spain kii rani isabela ne uthaaya. usne hispaniola dweep par basti basaane kii koshish kii or is prakaar america main spani upniveshwaad kii neenv rakhi. is prakaar is nayi duniya main europea upniveshwaad kii prakriya armbh hui.
