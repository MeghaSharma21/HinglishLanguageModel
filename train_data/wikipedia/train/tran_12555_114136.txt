 viksit desh yani audyogic desh, un deshon ko kaha jaataa he jinka kuch maanakon ke anusaar ucch vikaas dar he. ye maanak koun he or koun se desh audyogic yaa viksit deshon kii shrenee main aate hai, yah vivaadaaspad vishay he. is charchaa main aksar aarthik maanakon ko shaamil kiya jaataa he. aise hi ek maanakon main, prativyakti aay he. jin deshon kaa prativyakti aay yani prativyakti sakal gharelu utpaada zyada he wo viksit yaa audyogic deshon main gine jate hai. doosraa maanak, audyogikeekaran he. jin deshon kii arthvyavastha udyog dhandhon par nirbhar he wo audyogic desh kehalaate hai. anya maanakon main maanav vikaas suchakank, jisamen rashtriya aay ke saath jivan pratyaasha or shiksha shaamil he. jo desh viksit desh banane kii hode main hai unhen vikasshil desh kaha jaataa he. vikasshil deshon ko teesri duniya ke desh bhi kaha jaataa he. viksit or vikasshil deshon se alag ek shrenee he aviksit desh kaa.
