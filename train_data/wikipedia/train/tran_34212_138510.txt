 sheshchalam kii pahadiyaan purvi ghat kii mahatvapurn parvat shrunkhlaa he, jo dakshin bharat ke dakshini andhra pradesh rajya main vistaarit he. 
 cambrian- purv yug main bani in pahadiyon main chunaa-pathar ke bich-bich main balua pattharon or slate jaisi chattaanon kii paraten hai. ye atyant bikhri huyi he or inamen anek lambii ghaatiyaan he. ye pahadiyaan pashchim or pashchimottar main royalsima ucch bhoomi or purvottar main nandayal ghati se simaankit he. sheshchalam pahadiyaan lagbhag 8000 varg kilo meter main faili he or unka saamaanya vistaar dakshin -dakshin purv kii taraf he. uttar main eramala parvat shrenee ke saath sheshchalam pahadiyon kii oonchai bhi 400 se 1370 meter tak ghatati-badhati he. aparyaapt varsha ke kaaran in pahadiyon kii dhalaanon par bahut kam jangal he. paneru kii sahaayak nadiyaan is kshetra ko apwaahit karti he. oobad-khabad sthalakriti, anurvar mitti or ardhashushk jalwaayu yahan krishi ko vaadhit karte hai.
 jowar, moongfalee yahan kii mukhya fasalein he. yahan par sbestus, barite or chunaa-pathar kaa khanan hota he. hathkargha, bans va eent nirmaan yahan kii anya mahatvapurn aarthik gatividhiyaan he or pulivendla ekmaatr mahatvapurn nagar he.
