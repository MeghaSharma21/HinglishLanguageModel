 gerze zila tibet kaa ek zila he jo us desh ke pashchimi hisse main sthit he. tibet par china kaa qabza hone ke baad yah chini prashasnic pranali main tibet swashasit pradesh ke ngaari vibhaag main padataa he. gegei zile kaa kshetrafal vishaal he - lagbhag 1,35,025 varg kimi, jo bharat ke chhattisgarh rajya ke baraabar he, lekin isaki kul aabaadi san 1999 main keval 17,000 ke aasapaas thi. gerze zile, gegei zile or tsochen zile par ek vishaal ghaas kaa maidan vistrut he jisapar junglee gadhe, yaks, neeli bhed, hiran or bhaaluu jaise jaanavar rahete hai. sthaneey aabaadi yaks-paalan karti he.
