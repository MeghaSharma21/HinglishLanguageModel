 
 bakala yaa kaaman been, ek vaarshik paudha he, jo ki mizo america or andes parvat par multah ugta tha. ab yah vishwa bhar main ugaaya jaataa he. isaki khaadya faliyaan or beej hi iske utpaada hote hai. isaki pattiyaan kahin kahin hari sabji ke kaam aati hai or iskaa bhusa maweshiyon ke liye kaam main aata he. jaivik drushti se yah ek dwibijapatri paudha he. isaki faliyaan legum shrenee kii hone se bhoomi ko nitrogen daayak hoti hai. yah prakriya rhisobia naamak nitrogen daayak jeevanu dwaara hoti he. 
 
 raajma yaa angrezi main kidney been, ko uske rang or aakaar ke kaaran gurde kaa naam diya gaya he. ise angrezi main red been bhi kaha jaataa he, kintu is naam se anya kism bhi hai. yah uttar bharat ke khanpan kii ek abhinn ang banane vaali kism he. ise yahan adhiktar chaaval ke sang parosa jaataa he.

 yah raajma kashmiri bhi kehalaata he. iske beej 1 senmi ke andar ke hote hai. inkaa aakaar sabase chota hota he. ye bhi kuch rani rang kii abha liye hue marun hote hai.
