 ert phan der niyar denmark kaa kalakaar tha.
 phan der niyar amsterdam main utpann hua. prarambh se hi use apane pairon par khadaa hona padaa, falat: baahar ek ucchadhikari ke yahan usne naukari kar lee. par vahaan uskaa man na laga or kuch samay baad vah pun: amsterdam laut aayaa. vivaah ke pashchaat chah santanon ke bade parivaar ke kaaran use anek aarthik kathinaaiyon kaa saamana karnaa padaa. jindagi se lagaataar ladaai or kashmakash karte hue use ek sharaab kii dukaan kholane ke liye bhi baadhya hona padaa; lekin baad main uskaa du:khad parinaam hua. tatpashchaat chitrakaari or kalasaadhana hi uskaa dhyey ban gaya. 
 sheetkaal ke katipay sajiv drishyon kaa chithran karne ke atirikt naharon, nadi-nirjharon or prakritik drishychitr - jinamein adhiktar ushakalin athwa sandhya samay ke banate-mitate mohak najaare tathaa chandani raaton kii shubhr jyotsna kaa prasar or sanmohan he- usne badi kii tanmayata se anke hai. is disha main koi bhi samakalik kalakaar uski tulana main nahin thahar saktaa tha. chaaya prakaash kaa dhoomil alok or jal main jhilamilaati surya athwa chandramaa kii snigdh, dhaval rashmiyaan yaa nadi kinare par kisi akashachumbit bhavy bhawan ke jharokhe or khidakiyon kaa jhilmil jhilmil pratibimb-in sab drushyankanon main vah badi hi sukumaar, komal kalpana saakaar karne main safal ho jaataa tha. uske shishyon main uske putron ne bhi usase diksha lee or uski prerana se kala-sarjan kii ore ve pravrutt hue.
