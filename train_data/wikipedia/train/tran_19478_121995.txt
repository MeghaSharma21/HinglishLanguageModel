 sukhoi esayu-37  ek single seat do ingine vaala vimaan he jo sukhoi dizin beuro dwaara dizine kiya gaya he yah praudyogiki pradarshak ke rup main kaam karata tha. ekmaatr vimaan mul rup se comsomolsk-on-amur aircraft production association dwaara banaaya gaya tha. vimaan ne april 1996 main apni pehali udaan bhari. sanrachanaatmak vifaltaa ke kaaran vimaan disambar 2002 main durghatnagrast ho gaya. sukhoi esayu-37 kaa utpaadan kabhi shuru nahin kiya gaya.
