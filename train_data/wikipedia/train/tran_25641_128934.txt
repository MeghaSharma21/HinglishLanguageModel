 epifani athwa 'prabhuprakash' naamak isai parv paramparaagat rup se 6 janvari ko manaaya jaataa he. yah lagbhag san 200 i. main praachya carch main prarambh hua. baad main vah parv paashchaatya carch main bhi fail gaya. prarambh main vah ishwariya shakti ke aavirbhaav tathaa isa ke janm ke aadar main manaaya jaataa tha. baad main chrismas hi isa kaa ekmaatr janmparv rah gaya or efini kaa parv, bible main varnit isa kii jeevani kii teen ghatnaon kaa, sansmarnotsav ban gaya. arthath gyani purushon kii aradhana, jordan nadi main isa kaa baptism tathaa kaana nagar main isa kaa pratham chamatkaar : teen ghatnaon ke sansmaran main yah parv manaaya jane laga.
