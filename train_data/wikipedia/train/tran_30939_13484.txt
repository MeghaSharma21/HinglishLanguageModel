 pionier 11 ek 259 kilogram kaa america antariksh yaan he. ise 6 april 1973 ko america antariksh anusandhan sanstha nasa ne ek rocket ke zariye antariksh main choda. hamaare saura mandal ke kshudragrah ghere or brihaspati grah se guzarane vaala yah doosraa maanav-krit yaan tha. 2 disambar 1974 main yah brihaspati se keval 43,000 kimi kii duuri se niklaa. 1979 main yah pehla yaan bana jisane shani grah ke sameep jaakar usapar anusandhan kiya or uski tasviren waapas prithvi bhejin. chalate-chalate yah hamaare saura mandal ke bahari kshetron main ja pahunchaa he. kam urja ke kaaran 30 navambar 1995 ke baad is yaan kaa prithvi se sampark tut gaya.
