 pali sahitya main mukhyat: buddha dharm ke sansthaapak bhagavan buddha ke upadeshon kaa sangrah he. kintu iskaa koi bhaag buddha ke jeevankaal main vyavasthit yaa likhit rup dhaaran kar chukaa tha, yah kahana kathin he. 
 yadhyapi tripitak main hi kuch aise ullekh milte hai jinase buddha ke jeevankaal main hi dharm ke kuch prakarnon ke vyavasthit paath kiye jane kaa pataa chalata he. udaahranarth, udaan main varnan he ki ek baar seen naamak bhikshu se swayan bhagwaan ne poocha ki tumne dharm ko kaise samajha? iske uttar main us bhikshu ne 16 ashtak virgon ko puure swar ke saath gaakar suna diya. isaki bhagwaan ne prashansa bhi kii. vinayapitak aadi granthon main bahushrut, dharmadhar, vinayadhar, maatrakaadhar tathaa panchanekayik, bhaanak, suttantik jaise visheshnon kaa prayog miltaa he, jinase spasht he ki buddha ke upadeshon ke dhaaran, paaran kii parampara unke jeevankaal se hi chal padi thi. is parampara ke aadhaar par buddha ke upadeshon ko suvyavasthit saahitiyik rup dene ke liye teen baar sangeetiyaan kii jane ke ullekh chullavagg, dipavansh, mahavansh aadi granthon main milte hai. pratham sangeeti buddhanirvana ke chaar maas pashchaat hi rajgrih main hui jinamein 500 bhikshuon ne bhaag liya, jiske karan vah panchashatika naam se bhi prasiddh he. isaki adhyakshata mahakashyap ne kii or unhone buddha ke saakshaat shishy bhikshu upali se vinay sambandhi tathaa sthavir anand se sutt sambandhi prashn puch-poochkar anya bhikshuon ke anumodan se ukt vishyon kaa sangrah kiya. isi prakaar kii duusari sangeeti buddha nirvana ke 100 varsh pashchaat vaishali main hui jisamen 700 bhikshu sammilit hue or isiilie vah saptshatika ke naam se vikhyath hui. isamen vaishali ke bhikshuon ke aacharan main anek dosha dikhaakar unhen vinay ke viruddh tehraaya gaya or anumaanath: vinayapitak main vishesh vyavastha laai gai. buddhaghosh ke matanusaar to isi sangeeti dwaara buddha vachanon kaa tripitak, paanch nikaay, nau ang tathaa churaasi hajaar dharmaskandhon main vargeekaran kiya gaya. teesri sangeeti buddhanirvana ke 226 varsh pashchaat samrat ashok ke rajyakaal main patliputra main hui. isaki adhyakshata moggaliputt tiss ne kii. yah sammelan nau maas tak chalaa or usamen buddhavachanon ko antim swaroop diya gaya. isi bich moggaliputt tiss ne kathavatyu kii rachana kii jisamen 18 mithyaadrushti buddha sanshdaayon kii manyataon kaa niraakaran kiya. is rachana ko bhi abhidhammpitak main sammilit kar liya gaya. is sangeeti kaa ullekh chullavagg main nahin he or na tibeti yaa chini mahayan sampradaay ke sahitya main. ashok ke bhabru ke lekh, jisamen saat prakarnon ke naam bhi uddhrut hai, usamen, athwa anya dharmalipiyon main aisi kisi sangeeti kaa kahin koi ullekh nahin kiya gaya. is kaaran isaki aitihaasikta main kith, vailaser aadi vidwaanon ko andeh he. kintu riodevids, winternitz tathaa giger aadi vidwaanon ne isaki aitihaasikta sweekaar kii he. jis sangeeti kii aitihaasikta ke vishay main pracheen ullekh evam aadhuni vidvan ekamat he, vah he vaishali kii dwitiya sangeeti. in sangitiyon tathaa anya prayatnon ke falswaroop pali tripitak kaa jo swaroop praapt hua evam jis rup main vah hamen aaj uplabdh he, vah nimna prakaar he :
 mul pali sahitya teen bhaagon main vibhakt he, jinhen pitak kaha gaya he - "vinayapitak", "suttapitak" or "abhidhammpitak". inamen se prayek pitak ke bheetar apane apane vishay se sambandh rakhanevaali anek choti badi rachanaaon kaa samavesh he. 
 pali ke in teen pitakon main i. purv chhathi shatee main hue bhagwaan buddha ke vichaaron or upadeshon kaa ek vishesh style main sankalan kiya gaya he. teenon pitakon main paraspar taratamya he. vishay kaa muladhar suttapitak he jisamen bhagwaan ke upadeshon ko shrotaaon ko hridyangam karaane ke liye saral se saral, rochak kathatmak style kaa alamban liya gaya he. yahan vastu ko sankshep main kehane kaa praytn nahin kiya gaya. uddeshy he nai-nai baton ko saamaanya shrotaaon ke grahan yogya bananaa or isiilie yahan upadesh ke mukhya bhaag kii baar-baar punrariti kii gai he. prasangvash in sutton main tatkaaleen dharmik, saamaajik evam raajnneetik paristhitiyon kaa chithran bhi aa gaya he jo pracheen itihaas kii drushti se bada mahatvapurn he. udaahranarth, digh nikaay ke samanayfal sutt, brahmajaal sutt evam mahaparinibban sutt main buddha ke samasamyik dharmpravartakon jaise mankhaligosaal, pakudhakachchayan, ajit cese kambali, sanjay baltthiputt niganthnatputt, aadi ke aachaar-vichaaron tathaa unke dwaara pravartit dharm sampradaayon kii buddha drushti se aalochana pai jaati he or saath hi un-un vishyon par buddha manyata kaa pratipaadan bhi paaya jaataa he. saamaajik chithran suttapitak main bikharaa padaa he, tathaapi khuddak nikaay ke antargat jaatakon main isaki prachur saamagree uplabdh he. sutton main sthaan-sthaan par buddhakalin magadh, videh, koshal, kaashi aadi 16 janapadon ke raajaaon unke paraspar sambandhon evam ladaai-jhagadon or daanv-penchon ke ullekh bhare pade hai. sutton ke upadeshon main jis buddha aachaar ke sanket paae jate hai, unheen kii bhikshuon ke yogya sadachaar ke niyamon ke rup main vidhi-nishedh-pranali se vyavastha vinay pitak main bhi kii gai he. isi prakaar sutton main jis tatvachintan ke beej sannihit hai, unka darshanik shabdaawalee main saidhantik rup se pratipaadan or vivechan abhidhamm pitak main kiya gaya he. is paraspar aanushanbikta ke kaaran bauddhadharm kaa puraa sangopang evam suvyavasthit parigyaan bina tripitak ke avlokan ke nahin ho saktaa. yah pali tripitak vishay kii drushti se swayan buddha bhagwaan ke upadeshon par aadhaarit he. uplabdh granth rachana kii drushti se i. purv tritiy shatabdi se pashchaat kaa siddh nahin hota. buddha paramparanusaar ashok samrat ke kaal main hi unke putr mahendra swayan buddha bhikshu banakar is sahitya ko lanka le gaye or vahaan pratham shatee i. main raja battagamani ke rajyakaal main use vah likhit rup praapt hua jisamen vah aaj hamen miltaa he. tathaapi usamen aisi koi baat hamen nahin milti jo bich kii do teen shatabdiyon ke kaal main lanka kii paristhitiyon ke prabhaav ke kaaran usamen ai kahin ja sake.
 apane namaanusaar vinayapitak kaa vishay bhikshuon ke paalane yogya sadachaar ke niyam upasthit karnaa he. iske teen avaantar vibhaag hai - suttavibhang, khandhak or parivaar. suttavibhang ke pun: do upavibhag hai - mahavagg or chullavagg. is prakaar apane in upavibhaagon kii apeksha vinayapitak paanch bhaagon main vibhakt he.
 suttapitak apane vishay, vistaar tathaa rachana kii drushti se tripitak kaa sabase mahatvapurn bhaag he. isamen aise sutton kaa sangrah kiya gaya he jo paramparanusaar yaa to swayan bhagwaan buddha ke kahe hue hai yaa unke saakshaat shishy dwaara updisht hai or jinka anumodan swayan bhagwaan buddha ne kiya he. sutt kaa sanskrit roopaantar sutara kiya jaataa he. kintu prastut sutton main sutara ke ve lakshan drushtigochar nahin hote jo sanskrit kii pracheen sutararachanaon, jaise vaidic sahitya ke shrout sutara, gruhm evam dharmasutra aadi main paae jate hai. sutara kaa vishesh lakshan he ati sankshep main kam se kam shabdon main adhik se adhik arth vyakt karnaa. usamen punarukti kaa sarvatha abhaav avidhyman he. kintu yahan sankshipt style ke vipareet suvistrut vyaakhyaan tathaa mukhya baton kii baar-baar punrariti kii style apanaai gai he. is kaaran sutt kaa sutara roopaantar uchit prathit nahin hota. vichaar karne se anumaan hota he ki sutt kaa abhipraay mulat: sookt se rahaa he. vedon ke ek ek prakaran ko bhi sookt hi kaha gaya he. kisi ek baat ke pratipaadan ko sookt kahana sarvatha uchit prathit hota he.
 suttapitak ke paanch bhaag hai jinhen nikaay kaha gaya hai - dighanikaay, majjhimanikaay, sanyuktanikaay, anguttaranikaay or khuddakanikaay. dirghanikaay teen virgon main vibhajit he. pratham silkkhandhavagg main 13 sutt hai, duusare mahavagg main 10 tathaa tisare patikavagg main 11. is prakaar dirghanikaay main 34 sutt hai. ye sutt anya nikaayon main sangruhit sutton kii apeksha vistaar main adhik lambey he or yahi is nikaay ke naam kii sarthakata he.
 majjhimanikaay main madhyamvistaar ke 152 sutt he jo 15 virgon main vibhakt hai - 
 inamen se 14wein varg vibhang main 12 sutt hai or shesh sab main das-das.
 sanyuttanikaay main chote bade sabhi prakaar ke sutton kaa sangrah he or yahi is nikaay ke naam kii sarthakata he. isamen kul 56 sutt yaa sanyutt hai jo in paanch virgon main vibhajit hai - 
 angutar nikaay kii apni ek visheshta he. isamen sutton kaa sangrah ek vyavastha ke anusaar kiya gaya he. aadi main aise sutt hai jinamein buddha bhagwaan ke ek sankhyatmak padaartho vishyak upadeshon kaa sangrah he, tatpashchaat do padaarthon vishyak sutton kaa or phir teen, chaar aadi. isi kram se is nikaay ke bheetar ekakanipat, dukanipat evam tik, chatuvak, panchak, chakk, sattak, atthak, navak, dasak or ekadask in naamon ke gyaarah nipaton kaa sankalan he. ye nipaat pun: virgon main vibhajit hai, jinaki sankhya nipaat kram se 21, 16, 16, 26, 12, 9, 9, 9, 22 or 3 he. is prakaar 11 nipaton main kul virgon kii sankhya 169 he. prayek varg ke bheetar anek sutt hai jinaki sankhya ek varg main kam se kam 7 or adhik se adhik 262 he. is prakaar angutar nikaay se sutton kii sankhya 2308 he.
 khuddak nikaay main vishay tathaa rachana kii drushti se praaia: sarvatha swatantra 15 rachanaaon kaa samavesh he, jinke naam hai - 
 pali tripitak ke tisare bhaag abhidhammpitak main bhagwaan buddha ke darshanatmak vichaaron kaa vishleshan or vargeekaran kiya gaya he tathaa tathvik drushti se unki suchiyaan or paribhaashaayen upasthit kii gai hai. is pitak main nimna saat granthon kaa samavesh he- 
 dhammsangani uski matika ke anusaar do bhaagon main vibhakt he. pratham bhaag main 22 tik hai jinamein se prayek main teen teen vishyon kaa vivechan kiya gaya hai. duusare vibhaag main 100 duk hai or prayek duk main vidhaan ore nishedh rup se do do vishyon kaa prarupan kiya gaya he. ye duk 12 virgon main vibhajit hai jinke naam hai - 
 is prakaar dhammsangani ke tikon or dukon kii sankhya 122 he. inamen se pratham tik kushal, akushal tathaa avyaakrit dharmavishyak he, jo sabase mahatvapurn he or uske vishay kaa prarupan chittupapad rup nikkshep or atthuddhar in chaar candon main kiya gaya he.
 vibhang kii vishaywastu 18 vibhaagon main vibhajit he.
 kathavatthu main 23 adhyyon ke bheetar 216 prashnottar hai jinamein virdohi sampradaayon ke siddhanton kaa khandan kiya gaya he.
 puggalpannati main 10 adhyay hai jinamein kramash: ek ek prakaar ke, do do prakaar ke aadi badhate kram main dasven adhyay main 10, 10 prakaar ke pudgalon arthath vyaktiyon kaa nirdesh kiya gaya he. vyaktiyon kaa vibhaajan pruthgjan, samyak sambuddha, prayek buddha, shaikshya, ashaikshya, arya, anarya aadi rup se kiya gaya he.
 dhatukatha kii rachana kaa muladhar vibhang he, kyonki usee ke pratham teen arthath skandh, aaytan or dhaatu vibhangon kaa hi yahan adhik sookshm vivechan kiya gaya he. isi kaaran is granth kaa doosraa naam khamb-aaytan-dhatukatha bhi paaya jaataa he. granth main inhen teen kaa sambandh dharmon ke saath baitaakar bataaya gaya he. matikanusaar in dharmon kii sankhya 125 he jo is prakaar hai - 
 inkaa paraspar sambadh prashnottar kii style se 14 adhyyon main kiya gaya he.
 yamak main dharmon kaa sambandh vishesh vishyon ke saath paraspar vipareet rup main prashnottar style se samajhaayaa gaya he or isi yugal prashnaatmak reeti ke kaaran is rachana kaa yamak naam sarthak he. jaise kya sabhi kushaldharm kushalmul hai? kya sabhi kushalmul kushaldharm hai? ityadi. isi paddati se yamak main abhidhammpitak main prayukt paribhaashik shabdon kii sunishchit vyakhya dene kaa praytn kiya gaya he. yah rachana in 10 yamakon main vibhakt he - 
 patthan main buddha tatvachintan ke aadharbhoot pratithya-samutpaada-siddhant kaa ek vishesh style main bade vistaarpurvak vivechan kiya gaya he. granth ke mukhya chaar bhaag hai - 
 in chaaron bhaagon main dhammsangani main nirdisht 22 tikon or 100 dukon kaa 24 pratyayon se sambandh nimna chah patthanon dwaara samajhaayaa gaya he : tik pan duk pan duk-tik pan tik-duk pan tik-dik pan or duk-duk pa. in chah patthanon kaa purvawat chaar vibhaagon main prarupan hone se sampurna granth 24 patthanon main vibhakt ho jaataa he.
 uparyukt tripitak sahitya kaa sankalan praaia: i.pus. teesri shatee main purna ho gaya tha, kintu pali sahitya kaa srijan iske pashchaat bhi chalata rahaa. yadhyapi yah anupali sahitya vishay kii drushti se praaia: purnat: tripitak ke antargat gyaan kaa hi anukaran karata he, tathaapi apan swaroop evam style main samay kii gati ke anusaar usamen vikaas dikhaai detaa he. i.pus. pratham shatee ke lagbhag nettprikaran naamak granth likhaa gaya jisamen abhidhammpitak ke vishay kaa hi kuch adhik sookshmata se vivechan kiya gaya he. isamen 16 haar, 5 nay or 18 mul padon ke dwaara buddha darshan kii paribhaashaon ko samajhane kaa praytn kiya gaya he. iske karta kaa naam kacchan he. isi prakaar kii ek duusari rachana isi kaal ke lagbhag kii petkopadesh he jisamen nettiprakaran kii hi vishaywastu ko kuch duusari reeti se upasthit kiya gaya he, jisamen pradhanta un chaar arya satyon kii he jo buddha ke upadeshon ke muladhar hai. praaia: isi kaal kii teesri rachana he - milind-panhon jo pali sahitya main apni visheshta rakhti he va aitihaasik drushti se bhi badi mahatvapurn he. isamen sakal ke naresh milind or bhikshu nagsen se bich hua vaartalap varnit he. milind us yavan naresh minadar ke naam kaa hi roopaantar mana gaya he jisane i. pus. dwitiya shatee ke madhya main bharat par akraman kiya tathaa punjab pradesh main apana rajya jamaaya tha. prastut granth ke anusaar yah raja bada vidvan or darshanik vishyon main ruchi rakhanevaala tha. usne bhikshu nagsen se bade paine darshanik prashn kiye, jiske uttar bhi nagsen ne badi chaturaai se sundar drushtaanton dwaara unki pushti karte hue die hai. granth main saat adhyay hai - 
 inamen se pratham teen adhyay hi granth kaa mul bhaag mana jaataa he. shesh chaar adhyay peeche jode gaye prathit hote hai. iske anek kaaran hai- ek to tisare adhyay main granth ke pun: prarambh kii soochna he; duusare, buddhaghosh ke avataran praaia: teen hi adhyyon se liye gaye hai or tisare is granth kaa jo chini anuwaad 400 i. ke lagbhag kiya gaya tha, usamen keval ye teen hi adhyay paae jate hai. yah rachana style main badi rochak tathaa buddha siddhant ke gyaan ke liye apane dhang kii adwitiya he.
 in rachanaaon ke pashchaat atthakathaon kaa yug prarambh hota he. shrut paramparanusaar jab mahendra or sanghmitra dwaara pali tripitak lanka main pahunchaa or bauddhadharm kaa prachar badha, un mul granthon par sinhalai bhashaa main tikaa rup atthakathaayen likhi gai. ye atthakathaayen ab uplabdh nahin he. anumaanath: iskaa kaaran un atthakathaon kii rachana ho jane se kramash: un mul atthakathaon kaa adhyayan adhyaapan chhot janaa hi he. in nai pali atthakathaon ke karta acharya buddhaghosh kaa naam pali sahitya main bahut prasiddh he. unhone jin sinhalai atthakathaon kaa ullekh kiya he tathaa jinke avataran apni atthakathaon main liye hai, unamem mukhya hai- 
 antim chaar atthakathaayen nikaayon athwa unke kinhin vishyon ke vishesh vyaakhyaan ke rup main prathit hote hai. in mul atthakathaon ke abhaav main yah kahana to kathin he ki kitne ansh main buddhaghosh ne unka anukaran kiya or kitna moulik rup se kiya, tathaapi unki rachanaaon ko dekhne se spasht prathit hota he ki unhone mul atthakathaon ko apane tripitak ke vishaal gyaan dwaara bahut hi pallavit kiya hoga. unki atthakathaayen vinayapitak, suttapitak evam abhidhammpitak ke adhikansh bhaagon par milti hai, jinaki sankhya 16 he. in atthakathaon ke atirikt buddhaghosh kii ek vishisht rachana he visuddhimagg, jise buddha darshan kaa gyaanakosh kaha ja saktaa he. atthakathaon main swayan buddhaghosh ke kathananusaar unhone visuddhimagg main digh, majjhim, sanyutt or angutar in chaaron nikaayon kaa saar bhar diya he. shrut paramparanusaar unhone visuddhimagg ko apni atthakathaon se purv unki rachana ko apni yogyata siddh karne ke liye hi likhaa tha. is granth main kul 23 pariched hai. pratham do parichchhedon kaa vishay he "samadhi" or shesh parichchhedon main "pragna" kaa vyaakhyaan kiya gaya he.
 buddhaghosh ke pashchaat atthakathaon kii parampara ko unke praaia: samasamyik do aacharyon buddhadutt or dhammpal ne paripusht kiya. buddhadutt ne buddhaghosh krit samantapasadika naamak vinayapitak kii atthkatha kaa saar apni uttaravinishchay or vinayavinishchay naamak do padyatmak rachanaaon main upasthit kiya tathaa buddhaghosh kii abhidhamm kii atthakathaon ke aadhaar par abhidhammavatar naamak gadhy-padya-mishrit granth kii rachana kii. isamen moulikta yah he ki jahaan buddhaghosh ne rup, vednadi paanch skandhon ke dwaara dharmon kaa vivechan kiya he, vahaan buddhadutt ne chittar, chetsik, rup or nirvana is chaar prakaar ke vargeekaran ko apanaaya he. isi vargeekaran ko unhone apni rooparoop vibhaag naamak rachana main rakhaa he. buddhadutt kii ek or rachana he madhuratth vilasini, jo buddhawans kii atthkatha he. dhammpal ne saat atthakathaayen likhi he. khuddakanikaay ke antargat jin udaan, itishuttak, vimaanavtthu, petavatthu, thergatha, therigatha ev chariapitak naamak granthon par buddhaghosh ne atthakathaayen nahin likhin, unpar dhammpal ne paramatthdipani naamak atthkatha likhi he. isi prakaar nettiprakaran par atthasanvaneena or usee par linatthavannana naamak tikaa, visuddhimagg par paramatthamanjusha, buddhaghosh kii chaar nikaayon kii atthakathaon par leenatthapakasini naamak tikaa, jatakatthakatha kii tikaa tathaa buddhadutt krit madhuratthvilasini kii tikaa likhi. in sab rachanaaon main sabase adhik prasiddh he unki visuddhimagg kii tikaa. shesh rachanaaon main koi vaishishty nahin he. yadhyapi ukt tripitak va unki atthakathaon kii rachana ke pashchaat nahin he. yadhyapi ukt tripitak va unki atthakathaon kii rachana ke pashchaat buddhavachan ke sambandh main adhik kuch kahana shesh nahin rahaa, tathaapi is atthkatha naamak tikaa sahitya kii rachana buddhaghosh yug main baraabar hoti rahi. inamen se kuch rachanaayen is prakaar hai :
 anandakrit abhidhamm mul tikaa, chulldhammpal krit 'sachch sankshep', upsen krit saddhammppajotik mahanam krit sadshammppakasini, kassap krit mahvichedni or vimatichhedni, vajrabuddhi krit sammtavpasadika kii tikaa, kshem krit khemppkaran, aniruddh krit abhidhammaththsangaho paramatthavinishchay or namrupapariched, dharmashri krit khuddakasikkha or mahaswami krit mulsikkha.
 12veen shatee se atthakathaon par magadhi bhashaa main hi tikaaon kii rachana prarambh hui. is kaary kii suvyavastha ke liye lanka naresh parakrambahu pratham ke kaal main sinhalai sthavir mahakssap dwaara ek sangeeti kii gai, jiske falswaroop samantapasadika aadi atthakathaon par deepani, manjusa, pakasini aadi naamon se aath tikaayen likhi gayi, jinamein se ab keval vinayapitak kii samantapasadika atthkatha par likhi gai saratthdipani tikaa maatr milti he. iske karta sariputt kii teen anya tikaayen bhi milti hai- 
 sumangala, saddhammajotipal, dhammakirti, buddharavikhat or meghankar - in sabhi ne bhinn-bhinn atthakathaon par tikaayen likhi he. inamen akele vaachissar ther ke mul sikha tikaa aadi das tikaa granth milte hai. 13veen shatee main sthavir videh, buddhapriy or dharmakirti dwaara samantakutavannana aadi anek granthon kii rachana hui. 14veen shatee kii rachanaaon main chaar kavy granth prasiddh hai-
 baad kii teen rachanaaon ke kartaaon ke naam agyaat hai.
 14veen shatee ke pashchaat pali-sahitya-srijan kaa kshetra lanka se uthakar brahmadesh main pahunch gaya. yahan ke sahityakaron ne abhidhamm ko vishesh rup se adhyayan kaa vishay banaaya. 15veen shatee kii kuch rachanaayen hai- 
 16veen shatee main saddhammalankar ne patthan prakaran par patthandipani naamak tikaa likhi tathaa mahanam se anandakrit abhidhamm-mul-tikaa par madhusaaraty dipani naamak anutika likhi. 17veen shatee main tripitakalankar ne atthasalini par kii bees gaathaon main visativannana, sariputtakrut vinayasangrah par vinayalankar naamak tikaa tathaa yasavadjnavatthu in teen granthon kii rachana kii. trilokguru ne chaar granth rache-

 18veen shatee kii gnanamivanshnamak brahmdes ke sangharaaj kii teen rachanaayen prasiddh hai- 
 19veen shatee kii brahmadesh kii kuch rachanaayen hai- nalatadhatu vans, sandes katha simavivadvinishchay aadi. is shatee ke chhakesadhatuvans, gandhavans or sasanavans naamak rachanaaon kaa parichay vans sahitya ke antargat diya gaya he. is shatee kii anya ullekhneey rachana he bhikshu ledisdaav krit abhidhammsangrah kii parpatthadipani tikaa tathaa yamak sambandhi pali niabandh jo unhone shrimati rice davidz kii kuch shankaaon ke samaadhaan ke liye likhaa tha. 
 pali-sahitya-rachana kii avichhinn dhara ke pramanswaroop 20veen shatee kii do rachanaaon kaa ullekh karnaa anuchit na hoga. ye hai bharatvarsh main bhandat dharmanand kosambi dwaara rachit visuddhimaggadipika or abhidhammatthsangrah kii navaneet tikaa. is parichay ke aadhaar se kaha ja saktaa he ki paalisahitya-nirmaan kii dhara dhai hajaar varsh ke pashchaat bhi abhi tak vicchhinn nahin hui.
 lanka main jis samay tripitak par atthakathaon kii rachana kii ja rahi thi, usee samay duusari ore kuch granthkaaron main aitihaasik ruchi jaagrath hui jiske parinamswaroop dipavansh kii rachana kii gai. in granthon main lanka kaa itihaas, pracheenatam kaal se lekar raja mahasen ke rajyakaal tak, varnan kiya gaya he. is rachana kaa upyog buddhaghosh ne apni atthakathaon main prachurtaa se kiya he or kahin-kahin uske avataran bhi die hai. is prakaar dipawans kaa rachanakaal i. chauthi paanchavin shatee ke bich siddh hota he. sinhal ke raajaaon ke saath-saath yahan buddha ke jivan, unke shishyon kii parampara evam teenon sangitiyon aadi kaa vivaran kuch badha chadhaakar kiya gaya he. anumaanath: iske vishay kaa aadhaar uparyukt sinhalai atthakathaayen rahi hai. yah rachana pali-gadhy-mishrit he, style bahut kuch shithil he, punaruktiyaan bhi bahut he or rachana bhashaa, chand aadi ke dosha se mukt nahin he. ye lakshan uski pracheenta ke dyotak hai or is baat ke pramaan hai ki usamen vishaywastu anek sroton se sankalit kii gai he tathaa usamen sampadakeey vyavastha laane kaa koi vishesh praytn nahin kiya gaya. kintu aitihaasik drushti se lanka main aadi se hi iskaa bada aadar rahaa he. kaha jaataa he ki i. 5veen shatee main hi sinhal raja dhatusen ne ek varshikotsav ke avasar par rashtriya gaurav ke saath iskaa paath karaayaa. bhartiya aitihaasik rachnaao main dipawans ni:sadeh ek prachinatam rachana he.
 dipavansh kii rachana se kuch kaal pashchaat mahanam ke dwaara mahavansh kii rachana hui. iskaa muladhar bhi ve hi sinhal kii atthakathaayen tathaa dipavansh he. yahan vishay kaa pratipaadan dipavansh kii apeksha adhik vishad or vyavasthit he. bhashaa bhi apekshaakrit adhik parimarjeet or style to kahin kahin mahakavyon kii paddati kaa samaran karaati he. mahavansh kaa mul bhaag 37 parichchhedon kaa he or vah dipavansh ke samaan mahasen ke shasankaal par samaapt hota he. prarambh ke parichchhedon main, jo lanka main bauddhadharm ke aagaman se purv desh kii dharmik paristhitiyon ke ullekh aae hai, ve aitihaasik drushti se bade mahatvapurn hai. udaahranarth, pandaskamay naresh kaa rajyakaal mahendra dwaara lanka main bauddhadharm ke pravesh se 60 varsh purv tak rahaa kaha gaya he. is raja ne 70 varsh rajya kiya tathaa rajya ke 10wein varsh main hi anuradhapur nagar kii sthapana kii. isse aage is rachana main samay-samay par parivardhan kiya gaya he. 37wein pariched kii 50veen gaatha se aage 79wein pariched tak kii rachana sthavir dharmakirti krit he or usamen mahasen ke kaal se lekar parakrambahu pratham tak 78 raajaaon kii vanshaparampara di gai he. mahavansh kaa yah tathaa isse aage ke pariched chulvansh kehalaata he. chulvansh 80 se 90 tak ke 11 parichchhedon ke rachayita buddharakshit bhikshu ne aagaamee 23 raajaaon kaa varnan kiya jo parakrambahu chaturth tak aayaa. 91 se 100 tak ke 10 pariched sumangala ther dwaara rache gaye or unamem bhuvnekbahu tritiy se lekar kirtishree rajsingh ke kaal tak ke 24 raajaaon kaa varnan kiya. yahan lanka main isai dharm ke prachaar kii bhi soochna milti he. aglaa 101vaan pariched sumangalacharya tathaa devarakshit dwaara rachaa gaya. tatpashchaat vahaan kaa rajya angrejon ke haath main chale jane kii bhi soochna he. mahavansh kaa antim parivadharn bhikshu pragyanand nayak dwaara 1936 main prakashit hua or isamen 1815 se 1935 i. tak kaa lanka kaa itihaas samavisht kiya gaya he. is prakaar mahavansh kii rachana main chah granthkaaron kaa haath he jinke dwaara isamen bhagwaan buddha se lekar lagbhag dhai hajaar varshon kaa lanka kaa itihaas avichhinn rup se ankit kiya gaya he. yah granth is baat kaa bhi pratik he ki ukt deergh kaal tak lanka main pali bhashaa ko kis prakaar jivit rakhaa gaya or usamen saahitiyik rachana kii dhara ko kabhi sookhne nahin diya gaya.
 dipavansh or mahavansh rachanaaon ne teevr aitihaasik vritti jaagrath kii. parinamat: anek anya vansh naamak granth rache gaye. isamen aitihaasik tathy to praaia: ukt donon granthon se hi liye gaye hai, kintu unamem jo naanaa vishyon kii shrutiparampara ko ekatra kar diya gaya he, vah mahatvapurn he. inamen vishay ko mahatv dene ke liye bahut kuch atishyoktiyon or kalpanaaon kaa bhi aashray liya gaya he, jiske kaaran kahin kahin itihasajna ko udvaig yaa ashraddha utpann hone lagati he. is prakaar kii mukhya rachanaayen ye hai - 
 ukt granthon main adhikansh bhaag pracheen tripitak, unki atthakathaon tathaa mahavansh ke antargat vaarta kii sakshep yaa vistaar se punrariti maatr he. kintu phir bhi unamem apane apane vishay kii kuch moulikta bhi he jiske aadhaar se hamen ukt vishyon kaa kuch itihaas buddhakaal se lagaakar ab tak kaa avichhinn rup main praapt hota he. yah pali sahitya kii apni visheshta he jo sanskrit praakrit main nahin pai jaati.
 pali main samay-samay par shastreey granth bhi rache gaye hai. vyakaran ke kshetra main kacchan or moggallan krit, vyakaran evam unki tikaayen prasiddh hai. san 1154 i. main brahmadesh ke bhikshu aggwans krit saddaniti, kacchan ke vyakaran par aadhaarit pali bhashaa kaa vyakaran he. iske teen bhaag hai- padmala, dhatumala or suttamala. isi par aadhaarit jinratna bhikshu krit dhatvarthdipani naamak padhyabaddh dhatusuchee he. pali vyakaran vishyak anya kuch rachanaayen hai- saddhammaguru krit saddabutti, mangalakrut gandhatthee or samner dhammadassi krit vachhvachak, arievansh krit gandhabharan, brahmadesh ke narsh kychha kii putree krit vibhaktyarthprakaran, jambudhvaj krit sanvaneenanay-dipana or niruttisangah, raajaguru krit karkpupfamanjari .
 pali ke "abhidhanppadipika" or "ekakkhar kosh" ye kosh granth suprasiddh hai.
 pali chand:shaastra par bhi anek rachanaayen hai, jaise vrittoday, chandovichiti or kavibharprakaran aadi. inamen thar sangharvikhat krit vrittoday hi sabase adhik prasiddh he. isapar vachanatvajotika naamak tikaa bhi likhi gai. inhen sangharvikhat krit pali kavyashastra par bhi ekmaatr rachana he- subodhalankar.
