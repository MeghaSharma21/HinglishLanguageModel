 kasam tere pyaar kii ek bhartiya hindi dharawahik he, jiskaa prasaaran colors par 7 march 2016 se hota he. yah sho har somvaar se shanivaar shaam 6:00 baje se 6:30 baje tak prasaarit hota he. iskaa nirmaan ekta kapur or shobhaa kapur ne kiya he. isamen kritika senger or sharad malhotra kramshah kritika or ranbir kaa mukhya kirdaar nibhaa rahe hai.
 rishi or tanushri donon bachapan ke dost rahete hai, jo alag alag bade hote hai. 17 saal baad rishi or tanushri kii shaadi ho jaati he, lekin tanushri kii goli lagane se maut ho jaati he. phir tanushri kaa phir se tanuja ke rup main janm hota he. rishi or tanuja donon kii car durghatana main maut ho jaati he. uske baad donon kaa janm ranbir or kritika ke rup main hota he.
