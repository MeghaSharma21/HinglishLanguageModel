 lohia auto industries . lohia samooh kaa vidyutiya vaahan nirmaan vibhaag he. san 1976 main muradabad main sthapit lohia samooh kaa kaarobaar bharat samet videshon main failaa hua he, aaj is company vaarshik rajasav 500 karod rupayon kaa he. lohia samooh ke tahat lohia auto industries kii shuruuaat varsh 2007 main hui.
 lohia auto industries kii kashipur main nae jamaane kii takneekon se yukt nirmaan ikai he jahaan par ucch takanic vaale vidyutiya dopahiyaa evam tipahiyaa vaahanon kaa nirmaan kiya jaataa he. german takanic se viksit is kaarkaane main do kismon ke vidyutiya scooter taiyaar kiye jate hai- fame or oma. ye donon scooter dekhbhaal kii aavashyaktaa se lagbhag mukt hai. inamen koi gear, engine, belt yaa chen drive nahin he, inase koi utsarjan nahin hota, pradooshan nahin hota ye electronic start va excilirator yukt hai in sab khaasiyaton ke alaava ye scooter kendriya vaahan panjikaran kanoon ke tahat bhi nahin aate hai kyonki automotive research association of india ne is prakaar ke vaahanon ko aisi aavashyaktaaon se chhot dilaayi he. inhen chalaane ke liye kisi panjikaran yaa license kii aavashyaktaa bhi nahi hoti.
