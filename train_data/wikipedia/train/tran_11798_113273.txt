 parasmaninath mandir parasmanidham rahua-sangram ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he. yah ek prasiddh shiv ke liye samarpit hindu mandir he jo rahua-sangram gaanv ke uttar-purv disha main sthit he. jo bihar ke madhubani jila antargat madhepur prakhand karyaalay se chah kilometre kii duuri par he.
 shiv praathmik devtaa he. ek badi shiv pratimaa ko sthapit kiya gaya he jo vahaan main sabase oonchi he bihar or jharkhand main he. ek pramukh tyoharon ke mandir main maha shivaratri par jo din lagbhag 20,000 shraddhaluon yahan jaaen. is mandir ke adheen he parasmani foundation trust.
