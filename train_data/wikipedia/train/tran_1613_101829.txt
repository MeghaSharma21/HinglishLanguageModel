 bharat main tihara talaaq yaa teen talaaq vah kupratha he jisamen koi musalmaan purush apni patni ko teen baar "talaaq" bolkar, likhakar yaa kisi electranik rup main bhejkar usase vivaah-sambandh-viched kar letaa tha.
 islam main do tarah ke talaaq ke bare main zikr he. pehla talaaq al sunnam, jise paighmbar mohammad ke hukum ke mutabiq kiya jaataa he. jabaki doosraa, talaaq al biddat, jise baad main ijad kiya gaya. koran main sidhe taur par teen talaaq kaa koi zikr nahin he or naa hi paighmbar mohammad ne iske bare main sidhe taur par kuch kaha he.
 quran main sirf ek baar talaaq bolne se hi talaaq hone kaa zikr he. lekin isse pehle donon ke bich sulajh karaane ke liye bhi kai upaay bataae gaye hai. sureh nisa-35 main kaha gaya he ki;
 iske baad bhi agar shuhar or bivi donon alag hona chaahate hai to shuhar bivi ke khaas dinon ke aane kaa intazaar karegaa. khaas dinon ke guzar jane ke baad jab bivi paaq ho jaae to bina hambistar hue kam se kam do jummedaar logon ko gavaah bana kar unke saamne bivi ko ek talaaq de, yani shuhar har bivi se sirf itnaa kahe ki "main tumhe talaaq detaa hum". talaaq dene ke teen maheene baad tak bivi sasuraal main rah sakati he. use koi nahin nikaal saktaa he.
 dilli ucch nyaayalay ne teen talaak adhyadesh ko chunauti dene vaali yachika shuukravaar 28 sitambar 2018 ko khaarij kar di. is adhyadesh main teen baar talaak bolkar patni ko talaak dene par teen saal kii jail yaa jurmaana kaa praavdhaan he.
