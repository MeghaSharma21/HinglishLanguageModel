 jawahar laal nehru puraskaar bharat kii sarkaar dwaara diya jane vaala ek antarrashtriya puraskaar he, jisaki sthapana 1965 main kii gai he. puraskaar duniya ke logon ke bich antarrashtriya samaj, sadbhaavna or maitri ko badhaava dene ke liye unke utkrisht yogadan ke liye diya jaataa he. puraskaar praapt karne vaale ko 25 lakh rupye pradaan kiye jate hai.
