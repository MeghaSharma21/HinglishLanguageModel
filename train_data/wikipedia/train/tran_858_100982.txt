 heshi galiyara yaa gansu galiyara aadhunik china ke gansu praant main sthit ek aitihaasik maarg he jo uttari resham maarg main uttari china ko tarim droni or madhya asia se jodta tha. is maarg ke dakshin main bahut unchaa or viraan tibet kaa pathaar he or iske uttar main gobi registan or phir mongolia ke is kshetra ke shushk pahaad hai. is galiyare main ek ke baad ek nachlistaanon kii kadiyaan milti hai jahaan chote-chote qasbe hua karte the. yahan se guzarate vyapaaree or anya yatri in jagahon par apane or apane ghode-unton ke liye dana-pani praapt karte the or thahar bhi sakate the.
 heshi galiyare se pashchim kii or jane par resham maarg teen upmargon main bant jaataa he - ek raah uttar main tiyaan shaan ke parvaton kii ore or us se paar aadhunik kirghizstan jaati he or baaqi do raahen tarim droni ke do taraf jaati hai. heshi galiyare kii purvi taraf lanjhu shahar ke baahar ke pahaad hai jahaan se guzarakar purv main vei nadi kii ghati or china pahunchaa jaataa tha.
