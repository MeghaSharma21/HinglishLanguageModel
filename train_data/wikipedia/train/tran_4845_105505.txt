 andhaa yug, dharmaveer bharti dwaara rachit hindi kavy naatak he. iskaa kathanak mahabharat yuddh ke antim din par aadhaarit he. isamen yuddh or uske baad kii samasyaon or maanviya mahatvakanksha ko prastut kiya gaya he. 
 yah naatak chaar dashak se bharat kii prayek bhashaa main manchit ho rahaa he. ibrahim alkaaji, em ke raina, ratan thium, aravind goud, ram gopal bajaj, mohan maharishi or kai anya bhartiya rangamanch nirdeshko ne iskaa manchan kiya he. iraq yuddh ke samay nirdeshak aravind goud ne aadhunik astr-shastr ke saath iskaa manchan kiya.
 ise nae sandarbh or kuch naveen arthon ke saath likhaa gaya he. isamen dharmaveer bharti ne rangamanch nirdeshko ke liye dher sari sambhaavnaayen chodi hai. kathanak kii samakaleenta naatak ko naveen vyakhya or nae arth detii he. natya prastuti me kalpanasheel nirdeshak nae aayam talash letaa he. is naatak main krishna ke charitra ke nae aayam or ashwatthama kaa taqatvar charitra he, jisamen vartmaan yuva kii kuntha or sangharsh ubharkar saamne aate hai.
