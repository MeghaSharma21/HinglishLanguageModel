 vetalghat tehsil bharat ke uttarakhand rajya main nainital janapad kii ek tehsil he. nainital janapad ke uttari bhaag main sthit is tehsil ke mukhyalay vetalghat kasbe main sthit hai. iske purv main almoda janapad kii salt tehsil, pashchim main almoda janapad kii ranikhet tehsil, uttar main almoda janapad kii bhikyasann tehsil, tathaa dakshin main nainital or koshyakutauli tehsil he. 
 tehsil ke adhikaar kshetra main kul 68 gaanv aate hai, or 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar isaki jansankhya 21,265 he.
