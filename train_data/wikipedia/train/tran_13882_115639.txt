 gres kujur bharat kii ek pramukh adivasee kavyitri hai. ranchi ke itaki main ek ‘uraanv’ adivasee parivaar main inkaa janm hua. inaki maatrubhaasha ‘kudukh’ he. inke pita patric kujur aspataal main compounder the or maa routh keraketta health vijitor thim. 
 inaki aarambhik evam ucch shiksha ranchi main puri hui. inhone kramshah ursuline convet skool ranchi se metric, sant jevier college ranchi se em.e, bi.aid. kii shiksha pai. farvari 1976 main unhone ol india radio kii sarvice main kaarykram adhishasi ke rup main jwaine kiya. is santhaan main rahete hue unhone ranchi, patna dilli, bhagalpur aadi jagahon par apni sevaayen di. baad main ve nideshak bhi baniin. 2008 main inhone ol india radio se mahanideshak ke pad se avkaash grahan kiya. 
 gres kujur ne 1966 se lekhan kaary aarambh kiya. ye muyakhtah hindi main likhti hai or inaki kavitaayen ‘hindustan’, ‘aaj’, ‘yuddhrat aam aadmi’, ‘aryavartt’, ‘jharkhandi bhashaa sahitya samskruti akhdaa’ aadi vibhinn patr-patrikaon main prakashit ho chuki hai. ‘kalam ugalati aag', ‘lokapriy adivasee kavitaayen’ or ‘kalam ko tir hone do’ naamak sangrahon main inaki kavitaayen sankalit hai. ab tak inkaa apana koi swatantra sankalan prakashit nahin ho sakaa he. gres kujur ne radio naatak tathaa prahasan bhi likhe hai. dian-pratha par ‘mahua gire aadhi raat’ naamak inkaa radio naatak charchit rahaa he.
