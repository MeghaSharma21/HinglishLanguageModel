 sakal mulya vardhit yaa grass vailu aded, arthshaastra main, kisi bhi kshetra, udyog, arthvyavastha yaa vyavsaayik kshetra main utpaadit maal va sevaaon ke mulya kii maap he. rashtriya khaaton main, jivie utpaadan shunya madhyavarti khapat hota he; yah rashtriya khaaton ke utpaadan khate kaa ek santulan mad he.
 sakal mulya vardhit, bataur maap, sakal gharelu utpaada se sambandhit he, kyonki donon utpaada ke hi maapdand hai. yah sambandh is prakaar paribhaashit he:
 kyonki, utpaadon par karon or utpaadon par sabsidi kaa kul samucchy, sampurna arthvyavastha ke str par hi uplabdh hai, isiilie sakal mulya vardhit ke aankade ko sakal kshetreey gharelu utpaada evam anya ikaiyon ke utpathan ko maapne ke liye upyogit kiya jaataa he. is sandarbh main ise is prakaar mapa jaataa he:
 atisaral rup main, jivie, antim bikri or sabsidiyon se kaarobaar main aayi sabhi rajasvon kaa mahayog he. in aayon ko, tab: vyay, bachat or karon ko dhakne ke liye istemaal kiya jaataa he.
