 giroh aise kisi samooh ko kehete hai jo aparaadhik gatividhi main lipt ho or kisi ilaake main gaiyr-kanoonee kaary sangathit taur se karte ho. is shabd kaa prayog saadhaaran samooh ke liye bhi kiya jaataa hai, jaise ladakon kaa samooh. lekin iske liye uchit shabd toli adhik upyukt he or gaiyr-aparaadhik prayog main liya jaataa he. giroh main ek hi ruchi or saamaajik sthiti ke vyakti sammalit hote hai. adhiktar avivaahit purush hi giroh kaa nirmaan karte hai.
