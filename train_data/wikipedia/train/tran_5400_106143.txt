 
 panna bhartiya rajya madhya pradesh kaa ek jila he. panna jila sagar sambhag ke antargat aata he. panna main hiron kii khan he, saath hi yah sthaan pracheen evam sundar mandiron ke liye prasiddh he. isi kaaran ise 'mandiron kii nagari' bhi kaha jaataa he. yahan par sthit sant prannath or shri baldev ji ke mandir tirthaganon ke bich prasiddh hai. panna rashtriya udyaan bhi he jahaan tigar rigerv or kai durlabh vanya jeev paae jate hai. 
 pranami sampradaay kaa yah mandir mohalla atlla chungi main sthit he . vaastu kala kaa yah shandaar mandir apane praangan main sthit vishaal jalkund kii bhavyata se paripurna hai .
