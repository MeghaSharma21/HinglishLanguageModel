 udanpattee vimaanashetra main ek bhumi kii pattee hoti he, jis par vimaan udaan bhar or avataran kar sakate hai. iske alaava anekon yuddhayas bhi karte hai. ranve maanav nirmit bhi ho sakati he or prakritik bhi. maanav nirmit ranve kii sataha praayah asphalt yaa kankreet se yaa dono ke mishran se bani hoti he. prakritik ranve kii sataha ghaas, pakki mitti ityadi kii ho sakati he.
