 1979 main pyniyar venus orbitor se parabangnee prekshanon se pragatshukr ke vayumandal main badal sanrachana.
 shukr kaa vayumandal, prithvi kii tulana main zyada saghan or garm he. sataha par taapmaana 740 K he, jabaki dabaav 93 baar he. shukr kaa vaataavaran salphurik acid se bane apaardarshee badalon kaa poshan karata he, jo sataha ke prithvi-aadhaarit prakaashiki or kakshiya avlokan ko asambhav banaati he. sthalakriti ke bare main soochna radar imaging dwaara vishesh rup se praapt kii gai he. mukhya vayumandaleey gaisen carbon dioxide or nitrogen hai. anya rasinic yogic keval thodi maatra main maujuud hai.
 mikhail lomonosov apane ghar ke paas kii ek choti si vedhashala main 1761 ke shukr pargaman ke apane prekshan ke aadhaar par shukr ke vayumandal ke astitv kii pariklpana karne vaale pehle vyakti the.
