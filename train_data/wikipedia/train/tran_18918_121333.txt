 south carolina sanyukt rajya america kaa dakshin-purvi kshetra main rajya he. rajya ke uttar main north carolina, dakshin or pashchim main georgia or purv main atlantic mahaasaagar he. 18veen shatabdi main south carolina carolina naam ke upnivesh kaa hissaa tha jo mul teraa upnivesh main se ek tha. 1861 main america grihayuddh ke samay rajya parisangheey rajya america main jane vaala pehla rajya bana tha.
 sabhi 50 rajyon main south carolina kaa kshetra ke maamle main 40waan sthaan he. 2016 ke anumaan ke mutabik rajya kii jansankhya 49,61,119 hai jisase uskaa sthaan sabhi rajyon main 23waan hua. angreji adhikarik bhashaa he. nikki heli america ke south carolina praant kii janvari 2011 se janvari 2017 tak governor thi. unhone bhartiya mul kii duusari america governor or pehali mahila governor hone kaa gaurav hasil kiya he. heli sikh mata-pita kii beti hai jinke purvaj amritsar se aakar yahaan bas gaye the.
