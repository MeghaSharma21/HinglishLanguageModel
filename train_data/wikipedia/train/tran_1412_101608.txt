 beta taori, jiskaa bair namakan main bhi yahi naam darj he, vrish taramandal kaa doosraa sabase roshan tara he. iskaa prithvi se dekha gaya ausat saapeksh kantimaan kaa magnitude) 1.68 he or yah prithvi se 130 prakaash-varsh kii duuri par sthit he. yah prithvi se dikhne vaale taron main se 28vaa sab se roshan tara bhi he.
 beta taori ko arabi bhashaa main "an-nath" kaha jaataa he, jiskaa arth he "sand ke sing". ise angrezi main bhi kabhi-kabhi "silanat" kaha jaataa he.
 beta taori ek B7 III shrenee kaa daanav tara he. isaki andruni chamak hamaare suraj kii 700 guna he. iskaa vyas hamaare suraj ke vyas kaa 5 se 6 guna or iskaa dravyaman suraj ke dravyaman kaa 4.5 guna he. rasinic tatwon ke nazariye se is taare main hamaare suraj kii tulana main manganeez adhik he or magnesium or calcium kam he. yah tara apane centre main maujuud hydrogen eendhan yaa to khatm kar chukaa he yaa karne vaala he or ab mukhya anukram tara nahin rahaa he. chand lakh saalon baad yah phulakar thanda hona shuru ho jayega or ek naarangi daanav tara ban jayega.
 beta taori akaash main jahaan nazar aata he uske bahut sameep ek dhundhala-sa tara bhi dikhaai detaa he, isliye khagolashastri ise ek doharaa tara maanate hai. aasmaan main kabhi-kabhi beta taori chandramaa ke bahut paas dikhaai detaa he or kabhi-kabhi uske peeche chhip bhi jaataa he.
