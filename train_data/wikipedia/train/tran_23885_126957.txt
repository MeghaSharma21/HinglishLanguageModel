 harish salwe bharat ke prasiddh vakil hai. ve bharat hi nahin balki vishwa ke sabase mahange vakeelon main se ek hai. ve 1999 se 2002 ke bich bharat ke solicytor general ke rup main bhi kaam kar chuke hai. ve desh ke sabase saphaltam vakeelon main gine jate hai.
 harish salve kaa janm maharashtra ke nagpur shahar main 1956 main hua tha. yaheen commers main graduate hone ke baad salve ne sie kii padhai shuru kii.sie banane ke baad ve karadhaan visheshgy bane. unhone apane kanoonee kariyar kii shuruyaat 1980 main kii thi.
 salve kai bade-bade cese lard chuke hai. ambani bandhuon ke bich krishna godavari gas basin cese main harish salve ne mukesh ambani kii ore se cese ladaa tha. tata group, ITC limited, tamilnadu kii purv mukhyamantri jayalalita samet kai hiprophile cese salve lard chuke hai.
 harish salve ke pita narendra kumaar salve congress ke netaa the. vahiin, unke dada prasiddh aapraadhik vakil the. vakaalat ke alaava harish salve ko sangeet or piano bajaane kaa bhi shauk he.
