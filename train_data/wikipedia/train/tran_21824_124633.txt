 gurdeep kohli ek bhartiya televijan abhinetri he jo star plus ke dharawahik "sanjivani" main dow juhi or zee tivi par dharawahik "sindur tere naam kaa" main vedika ke rup se jaani jaati he. haal hi ve soni tivi ke dharawahik dil kii baaten dil hi jane main najar aane vaali he. 
 gurdeep kohli kaa janm ek sikh parivaar main hua tha. kuch saal televijan par kaam karne ke baad unhone dharawahik sanjivani ke apane sahakarmi arjuna punj ke saath 10 disambar 2006 ko vivaah kar liya. gurdeep ne 24 march 2010 ko apni putree meher ko janm diya.
 gurdeep kohli ne apane televijan jivan kii shuruyaat vigyaapan filmon se kii. unhone apana sabase mahatvapurn abhinay star plus ke dharawahik sanjivani main kiya. baad main unhone zee tivi ke dharawahik sindur tere naam kaa main kaam kiya. apane pati arjuna punj ke saath ve star van ke kaarykram nach baliye 2 kaa bhi hissaa bani. inhone zee khaanaa khajaana par apane vyanjanatmak kaarykram bhi prastut liye. star plus ke prasiddh dharawahik diya or baati ham main inhone ek khalanayika kii mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai. gurdeep ne apane filmi jivan kii shuruyaat prabhu deva nirdeshit bollywood film roudi rathore se kii. ab gurdeep soni tivi ke dharawahik dil kii baaten dil hi jane main abhinay kar rahi he.
