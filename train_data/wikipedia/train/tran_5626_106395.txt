 yazat parsi dharm main un devtaa-jasi hastiyon ko kehete hai jo puja ke yogya hai. parsi dharmagranth avesta main vibhinn yazaton kaa ullekh miltaa he. sanjhi arya dharohar ke kaaran inamen se kai yazat vaidic dharm ke devataaon se milte-julate hai lekin kuch un se bhinn bhi hai. yazaton kii acchhee shaktiyaan parsi dharm ke sarvashaktimaan ishwar ahur mazd kii alag-alag shaktiyon ke pahalu samajhe jate hai. inhen farishton ke rup main bhi dekha jaataa he.
 'yazat' kaa matlab he 'puja yogya'. kyonki avastai bhashaa or sanskrit donon hind-irani bhashaa-parivaar kii bahan bhashaayen hai isaliye inamen bahut se sajaateey shabd milte hai or yah bhi unamem se ek he. 'yaj' shabd sanskrit main 'yaj' rup main miltaa he, jis se hindu-dharm se sambandhit 'yajti' va 'yajna' nikalate hai.
