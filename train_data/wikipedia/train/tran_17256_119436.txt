 bhanubhaktiya ramayana nepal ke bhakt kavi bhanubhakt acharya dwaara nepali bhashaa main rachit ramayana he. purv se pashchim tak nepal kaa koi aisa gaanv athwa kaswa nahin he jahaan bhanubhakt ramayana kii ramayana kii pahunch na ho. bhanubhakt krit ramayana vastut: nepal kaa 'ramcharita maanas' he. sanwat 1910 tadnusaar 1853 i. main unki ramayana puri ho gayi thi, kintu ek anya strot ke anusaar yukand or uttar cond kii rachana 1855 i. main hui thi.
 bhanubhakt krit ramayana kii katha aadhyatm ramayana par aadhaarit he. isamen usee kii tarah saat cond hai - bal, ayodhya, arany, kiskindha, sundar, yuddh or uttar. 
 bhanubhakt ramayana kii aarmabhik panktiyaan yahan di ja rahi hai-
 ek din naarad satyalok pugi gaya lokko garun hit bhani.
 brahma taahin thia paryaa charanma khoosi garaaya pani.
 kya sodhgau timi sodh bhanchhu m bhani marji bhayethyo jasai.
 brahmako karunaa bujher rishile binti garya yo tasai.1.
 he brahma jati hun shubhashubh sabai suuni rahyaanchhu kachhu.
 baanki chhin tathaapi sunn ahile ichha m yo gardchhu.
 aaula jab yo kali bakhatma praani duraachaar bhai.
 ganrya chan sab paap anek tarahaka nichka matima gai.2.
 saancho baat garain kohi arukai garnan t ninda pani.
 arkako dhan khanlaai abhilaash garnan asal ho bhani.
 kohi jan t parastrima rat hunan kohi t hinsa mahaan.
 dehelaai t aatm jaani rahanan naastik pashu jhain tahaan.3.
 kamka chaakar jhain bhayer rahanan streelai dyauta sari.
 mannan pitru r matrulai buji khup shatru sarika gari.
 brahman bhai can veda bechi rahanan kohi padhun ta pani.
 dhan thulo chha pani bhanya sahaj dhan arjan garonla bhani.4.
 jaati dharm rahvin kshatriharuma jo chan i nichahru.
 shudradi t tapasvi hoi rahanan brahman sarika baru.
 stri dher bhrasht hunan pati r sasurako droh thulo gari.
 yasta nasht casori mukt t hunan sansarsagar tari.5.
