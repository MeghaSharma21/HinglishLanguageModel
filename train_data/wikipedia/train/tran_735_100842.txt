 soochna-madhyamon kii paardarshita kaa arth media ke khulepan tathaa uttardaayitv-bodh se he. 
 media kii paardarshita ke kuch pahalu ye hai- mukt strot dastavejikaran, khuli baithken, media main nivesh kaa puraa drushya saaf hona chaiye, vittiya prakatikaran ke kathan, soochna kii swatantrata kaa kanoon, bajet kii samiksha, lekhapariksha, piar samiksha aadi.
