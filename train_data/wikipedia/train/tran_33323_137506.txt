 
 202 vanshon main 2,600 jatiyaan
 arekeciye sapushpak paudhon kaa ek jeevvaigyanik kul he. isaki sadsy jatiyaan tad se sambandhit hai or vrikshon, chadhane vaali lakdeedaar lataaon va kshupon ke rup main hoti hai. is vansh main 202 gyaat vansh hai jinamein kul milakar lagbhag 2,600 jatiyaan shaamil hai.
