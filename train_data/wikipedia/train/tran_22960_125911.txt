 england kii mahila cricket team oktober or november 2017 main austrailia daure ke liye australia kii mahila cricket team kii mahila ashes pratiyogita main bhaag le rahi he. team ek test match, teen mahila ek divasiy antarrashtriya or teen mahila twanti-20 antarrashtriya khelengi. test match ek din/raat ke rup main khele jane ke liye nirdhaarit he or mahila vanade 2017-20 isisi mahila championships kaa hissaa hoga.
 2013 ke baad se, shrinkhalaa main ek multi-formet shrinkhalaa shaamil he jisamen prayek maichon ke liye die gaye ank hai. prayek mahila vanade yaa mahila tii20i jeet ke liye do ank die jaaenge, test vijeta ke liye chaar ank, yaa test match drow main prayek team ko do ank die jaaenge.
 agast 2017 main, australia ke captain meg lanning ne ghoshnaa kii ki vah apane kandhe par surgery ke daur se guzar hone ke baad shrinkhalaa ko yaad karenge. agle maheene, rashel hens ko unke pratisthaapan ke rup main namit kiya gaya tha. sitambar 2017 main, cricket australia ne pushti kii ki pehla match, alan border field, bristben main mahila vanade ne pehali baar mahila ashes kaa kaam kiya tha.
