 alphaz ek america vigyaan katha naatakeey televijan shrinkhalaa he jiskaa nirmaan jack pen or maaikel kerno ne kiya he. shrinkhalaa kuch atimanviya kshamtaaon ke logon ke samooh pe aadhaarit he jinhen "alphos" ke rup main janaa jaataa he or ye anya alphos dwaara kiye gayi aparaadhon ko rokane kaa kaam karte hai. 
 shrinkhalaa sanyukt rajya america main saifai cabel channel par prasaarit hoti he or burmanbraun va universal cabel productions ke bich kaa sah-utpaadan he. iskaa premier july 11, 2011 ko hua tha. praarambhik reporton ke baad ki sho radd kar diya gaya tha, alphos sitambar 11, 2011, ko apane duusare satr ke liye 13 prakarnon ke saath naveekrut kar diya gaya.
