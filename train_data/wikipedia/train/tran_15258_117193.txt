 rus ke sabase prasiddh bacchon ke lekhak, har roosi bachche ke parichit paatro ke rachayita eduaard uspenski kaa janm 1937 main hua. 1961 main inhone moscow aviation institute se em.essi. kiya or uske baad moscow ke vimaan nirmaan kaarkaane main ejeeniyar ke pad par kaam karne lage.
 1960 se inaki hasyavyangy rachanaaon kaa prakaashan ho rahaa he. kavita-sangrah 'betuka hathi' ke baad 'magaramchh gena or uske dost' naamak bal-upanyaas likhaa or iske baad 'amar kashei ke teen din' naamak naatak. in sabhi kitabon ne itani dhooma machaai ki iske baad unhone darjanon bal pustakon kii rachana kii, lekin naam in teen kitabon kaa hi sabase zyaada liya jaataa he.
 eduaard uspenski ne 'radio aayaa' naamak radio serial kaa lekhan kiya or 'radio takanic ke bare main ek prasiddh pustak' bhi likhi. inke alaava eduaard uspenski kii 'yadi main ladki hota', 'jokaron kaa skool', 'calobki jaanch kar rahe hai', 'plastic kaa kaua' or anya pustaken bhi bacchon main bahut lokapriy hai.
 'magaramchh gena or uske dost' naamak bal upanyaas kaa anuwaad bisiyon videshi bhashaaon main ho chukaa he. bhootpurv soviet sangh kii to lagbhag har bhashaa main is kitaab kaa anuwaad hua he. eduaard uspenski ke bal-upanyaas 'jaadui nadi kii dhara main', 'guarantishuda maanav', 'professor chinikapha kaa lacture', 'chiburashka janata ke bich', 'fyodar ankal kii chachee', 'fyodar ankal kii priy bacchee' or anya bahut-si pustaken rus or videshon main lagaataar prakashit hoti rahati hai. 
 eduaard uspenski ne rus ke itihaas main ashaantikaal ke naam se jane vaale kaal ke bare main ek aitihaasik bal-upanyaas bhi likhaa he, jiskaa naam he - 'chhadmaveshi doosraa damitri hi sachcha he.' eduaard uspenski dwaara likhi gai patakathaon par bahut-si cartoon-filmein bhi banaai ja chuki hai. eduaard uspenski sarvshereshth bal pustak rashtriya pratiyogita ke vijeta rah chuke hai.
