 kinyaruanda, jise kabhi-kabhi keval ruanda bhi kaha jaataa he or jo uganda main fumbaari kehalaati he, madhya africa main sthit ruanda desh kii rajbhasha he. yah ruanda-rundi bhashaa kii ek upbhasha he, jo swayan ruanda, burundi va uganda main lagbhag 2 karod logon dwaara boli jaati he. ruanda-rundi bhashaa kii ek anya upbhasha, kirundi, burundi kii rajbhasha he. kinyaruanda or kirundi bolne vaale ek-duusare ko samaj sakate hai haalanki donon upbhaashaon main zaraa antar he.
