  nyaayalayik vish vigyaan aparaadh anusandhan kaa vah bhaag he jisame vish vigyaan or analitical rasayan kii madad se mrityu ke kaaran kii jaanch padtaal hoti he! nyaayalayik vigyaan kii is shakha kaa matvpurn kaary yah pataa lagaana he kii vyakti kii mrityu kaa kaaran koun sa zaharila padaarth he or kitna samay hua he use sharir main pravesh kiye hue! yah shakha kisi bhi vyakti kii mrityu chaahe aapraadhik ho yaa aatmahatya ho, uske shav kii jaanch karti he or sharieik padaarth main vish kii upasthiti or uski kitni maatra he uskaa gyaat karti he! kisi bhi aapraadhik sthal main koi bhi bhautik sakshya miltaa he to uski bhi aache se jaanch kii jaati he! is shakha main sharir ke bahut se padaarth liye jate he jo kii sahaayak hote he vish or rasinic vishon kaa parikshan karne ke liye! is shakha main kitnashak, praanivish, vanaspatik vish, alcohol, morphine, narcotic padaarth, petrol, gasoline or anya sabhi sambhavit rasinic vishyon kaa parikshan kiya jaataa he|
