 san.-viyogi hari prasiddh gandhivadi evam hindi ke sahityakar the. ye aadhunik brajabhasha ke pramukh kavi, hindi ke safal gadhykar tathaa samaaj-sevi sant the. "vir-satsai" par inhen manglaprasad paritoshik mila tha. unhone anek granthon kaa sampadan, pracheen kavitaaon kaa sangrah tathaa santon kii vaaniyon kaa sankalan kiya. kavita, naatak, gadhygit, niabandh tathaa balopayogi pustaken bhi likhi hai. ve harijan sevak sangh, gandhi smarak nidhi tathaa bhudaan andolan main sakriy rahe.
 viyogi hari kaa janm chhatarpur main san 1896 i0 main ek kanyakubj brahman vansh main hua tha. paalan-poshan evam shiksha nanihaal main ghar par hi hui. 
 viyogi hari ne lagbhag 40 pustaken rachi hai. inke mukhya rachanaayen hai- 
 'bhaavana, 'prarthana, 'prem-shatak, aadhunik yug, sahitya vihaar, vir satsai, prem pathik, veena, premanjali, premashtak, tarangini, antarnad, pagali, prarthana, chhadmayogini, vir hardaul, meraa jivan pravaah, mandir pravesh, mewar kesari, buddha vaani, vishwadharm, kabir kii sakhiyaan.
 bhashaa	komal shabdaawalee se yukt khadiboli. angreji, arabi, farsi aadi ke shabdon kaa prayog.
 style	vichaaratmak, bhavatmak, sanvadatmak aadi.
