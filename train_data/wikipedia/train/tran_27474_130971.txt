 anuprayukt bhugol maanav bhugol kii ek pramukh apekshaakrit sabase nai shakha hai. iske antargat vartmaan maanviya samasyaon jaise - alp vikaas, jansankhya kaa aadhiky, nagareey evam bhoomi-upyog, maanav-swasthya se sambandhit samasyaon, nagar-niyojan aadi kaa adhyayan adhiktam maanav kalyaan kii nazar se kiya jaataa hai.
