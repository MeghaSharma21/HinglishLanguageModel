 chinnar abhayaarany kerala main munnar ke paas sthit he. maryur se 20 kimi aage chinnar vanyajeev abhayaarany kerala-tamilnadu border par sthit he. raajmala-udumalapet road is abhayaarany ke bich se hokar jaati he jahaan se paryatak hathi, junglee suar, dhabbedaar hiran, sambar, gaur or mor ko dekh sakate hai. agar aap bhaagyashaalee rahe to aapako manjampattee kaa safed bhainsa dikhaai de jayega. sher or chitah bhi yahaan dikhaai de jate hai. van vibhaag paryatakon ke liye trekking, chinnar safari or water fall trekking kii suvidha bhi uplabdh karaata he. maryur se munnar kaa rasta 2 ghante kaa he. samay: subah 7 baje-shaam 5baje tak
