 
 hriday shah, pracheen bharat ke vartmaan madhya pradesh rajya sthit panna riyasat ke pratham raja the. inhone 1731 se 1739 tak shashan kiya. ye maharaja chhatrasaal ke jyeshtha putr the. inhen panna riyasat apane pita se san 1731 i. main vaarshik 39 lakh rupee ke badale praapt hui thi. 1731 main inhone reva riyasat ko apane adheen kar vahaan ke raja avadhoot singh ko riyasat chhodkar jane par vivash kar diya. avadhoot singh ne avadh main pratapgarh jaakar sharan lee thi. tab harde shah ne vahaan vivaah kiya va putr ratna utpann hua.
 harde shah ke raja sabha singh, rajkumaar prithvi singh, raja salaam singh evam kunwar umr singh naamak chaar putr hue the. inamen se prithvi singh ko apane pita ke baad gaddi praapt hui. unhone marathaon kii sahaayta se panna rajya ke shahgarh ko bhi adheen kiya va 1744 main vahaan ke pratham raja bane.prithvi singh kaa vivaah hone par raja hari singh evam raja kishan singh putr hue.
