 gorkhali san 1803 main nepal kii gorkha sena dwaara gadhwal rajya par kiye gayi akraman ko kaha jaataa he.
 aitihaasik vivaranon ke anusaar kedarkhand kai gadhon main vibhakt tha. in gadhon ke alag-alag raja the jinka apana-apana aadhipaty kshetra tha. itihaaskaaron ke anusaar panvaar vansh ke raja ne in gadhon ko apane adhinakar ekikrut gadhwal rajya kii sthapana kii or srinagar ko apni rajdhani banaaya. kedaar khand kaa gadhwal naam tabhi prachlit hua. san 1803 main nepal kii gorkha sena ne gadhwal rajya par akraman kar apane adheen kar liya. yah akraman lokjan main gorkhali ke naam se prasiddh he.
 gorkhaon kaa mukhya hathiyaar khukhri hota tha. is akraman ke samay maharaja kii sena yudhkala evam sansadhano ke maamle main atyant kamjor thi. bujurgon ke anusaar sena ke paas ladane ke liye talavaarein tak na thi yahan tak ki kinaragadhi ke raja ke sainik kirmod se bani lakdi kii talavaaron se lade. is akraman main bahut se sainik or naagarik maare gayi. haalaanki bataaya jaataa he ki gorkha brahmanon ko nahin maarthe the or brahman gaanvon par akraman nahin karte the.
 maharaja gadhwal ne nepal kii gorkha sena ke adhipaty se rajya ko mukt karaane ke liye angrejon se sahaayta maangi. angrez sena ne nepal kii gorkha sena ko dehradun ke sameep san 1815 main antim rup se parrast kar diya. kintu gadhwal ke tatkaaleen maharaja dwaara yuddh vyay kii nirdhaarit dhanrashi kaa bhugtan karne main asamarthta vyakt karne ke kaaran angrejon ne sampoorn gadhwal rajya raja gadhwal ko na saunp kar alakananda-mandakini ke purv kaa bhaag east india company ke shashan main sammilit kar gadhwal ke maharaja ko keval tehri jile kaa bhu-bhaag waapas kiya.
