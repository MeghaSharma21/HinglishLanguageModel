 christina hamok kaa puraa naam christina hamoke coach he jinka janm 29 janvari 1979 ko hua tha. christina hamok 2013 ke varg ke nasa kii antariksh yatri hai. jo pehle america samoa ke enoee stesan main chif thi. 
 hammock ne 1997 main daraham main uttari carolina skool of science or mathematics se snaatak kiya. or phir reli main carolina state uniwarsity main daakhila liya, jahaan se unhone 2001 main electrical ejineering or 2002 main bhautikee main vigyaan snaatak kii do degree arjeet kii. unhone 2002 main electrical ejineering main masters kii degree hasil kii thi.
