 dalhan madhyayug ke ek ayurvedacharya the. unhone sushrutsamhita kaa bhashya likhaa he jiskaa naam 'nibandhsangrah' he. is granth main dalhan ke purv ke anekanek ayurveda panditon or tikaakaron ke vichaar sangrahit hai.
 dalhan ne chakrapani ke vichaar uddhrut kiye hai tathaa hemaadri ne dalhan ko uddhrut kiya he. atah dalhan kaa kaal 11veen shatabdi ke uttarardh se lekar 13veen shatabdi ke purvardh ke bich hona chaiye.
