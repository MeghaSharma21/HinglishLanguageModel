 israeli nai shekel isroyal kii mudra he. shekel 100 agorot  main vibhajit he.
 nai shekel 1 janvari 1986 se puraane shekel se sthaan par 1000 puraane sheklim = 1 nai shekel kii dar se jari kiya gaya. 1 janvari 2003 ke baad se shekel ek swatantra rup se parivartaneey mudra he. 7 may 2006 se shekel vyutpann vyapaar bhi chicago marcentile exange par uplabdh kii gai he. is tarah se shekel duniya ke un bees mudraon main se shaamil ho gaya he, jo videshi mudra bazaar main mudra vaayada anubandh kiya ja rahaa he. yah ek kathor mudra he, jiskaa duniya main lagbhag kisi bhi sthaan main upyog kiya ja saktaa he. mudra kaa utpaadan israel main naa hokar dakshin korea main hota he, kyonki desh main koi taksaal nahin he.
