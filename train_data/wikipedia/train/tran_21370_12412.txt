 vishwa ameor mukkebaaji pratiyogita, mukkebaaji pratiyogitaaon main sabase badi hoti he. iskaa aayojan AIBA dwaara kiya jaataa he. is pratiyohita kaa pratham aayojan cuba main hawana shahar main 17-30 agast, 1974 main kiya gaya tha. varsh 2003 main bhaar shreniyan 12 se ghataakar 11 kar di gayin. yahaan halke madhyam bhaar shrenee ko hataa diya gaya tha. is tareeke kii mahila mukkebaaji pratiyogita kaa pratham aayojan 2001 main scranton, sanyukt rajya main kiya gaya tha.
