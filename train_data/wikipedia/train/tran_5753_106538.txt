 dakhni yaa dakkani yaa dakhini :Dakhni, or Dakkhani, ye boli urdu zabaan kii ek ahm boli he, jo janubi hindustaan main boli jaati he. dakhni hindi multah hindi kaa hi purv rup he jiskaa vikaas isa kii 14ve shatee se 18bi shatee tak dackhin ke bahamani, qutub shahi or adil shahi aadi rajyon ke sultaanon ke sangrakshan main hua tha. vah multah dilli ke aas paas kii haryani evam khadi boli hi thi jis par brajabhasha, avadhi or punjabi ke saath-saath marathi, gujarati tathaa dakshin kii sahavarti bhashaaon telegu tathaa kannada aadi kaa bhi prabhaav pada tha or isane arabi farsi tathaa turki aadi ke bhi shabd grahan kiye the. yah mukhyat farsi lipi main hi likhi jaati thi. iske kaviyon ne is bhashaa ko mukhyat 'hindvi', hindi or 'dakkhini' hi kaha tha. ise ek prakaar se aadhunik hindi or urdu kii purvagami bhashaa kaha ja saktaa he.
 is bhashaa ke adhikansh bhashi bharat ke dakkan main milte hai, jahaan vastutah madhyakaalin va uttaramadhyakaalin bharat ke tatkaaleen muslim rajyon ke kshetra hai, jaise karnataka, maharashtra, andhra pradesh va tamilnadu ke kuch bhaag.
 is boli par jonerafiayi aetatbaar se, aloqai zabaanon kii taasir nazar aati he. jaise, riyasat andhra pradesh kii urdu par telugu kaa thodaa asar paaya jaataa he. isi tarah maharashtra kii urdu par marathi kaa, karnataka kii urdu par kannada kaa or tammul naadido kii urdu par tammul kaa. lekin mukammal taur par janubi hind main boli jaani vaali dakkani ek khasusi andaaz kii urdu he, jis main marathi, telugu zabaanon kaa mail paaya jaataa he.
