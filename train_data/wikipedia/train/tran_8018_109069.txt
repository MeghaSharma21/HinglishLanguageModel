 vishwa vyapaar sanghathan ek antarrashtriya sanghathan he jo vishwa vyapaar ke liye niyam banaata he. isaki sthapana 1995 main dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke baad banaae gaye gat ke sthaan par laane ke liye kii gai thi. vartmaan samay main iske 164 sadsy hai.
 dablutio ke sachiv or mahanideshak geneva main nivas karte hai. sachivalay jisamen ab keval 550 log kaam karte hai or sanghathan ke sabhi pahaluon ke sanchaalan kaa prashasnic kaary sambhalate hai. sachivalay ke paas kanoonee nirnay lene kii koi shakti nahin he lekin aisa karne waalon ko yah mahatvapurn sevaayen or paraamarsh pradaan karata he.
 dablutio kii mul sanrachana main nimnlikhit nikaay haiâ€“ mantristareey sammelan : yah dablutio kaa shaasi nikaay haii yah sanghathan kii ranneetik disha tay karne or apane adhikaar kshetra ke tahat samjhauton par sabhi antim nirnay lene ke liye jimmedaar hota he. isamen sabhi sadsy deshon ke antarrashtriya vyapaar mantri hote hai. 
 saamaanya council : vyavhaar main, zyaadaatar maamalon ke liye yah dablutio kaa faisla karne vaala mukhya ang he. isamen sabhi sadsy deshon ke varisht pratinidhi hote hai. yah dablutio ke pratidin ke kaarobaar or prabandhan kii dekharekh ke liye jimmedaar hota he. neeche varnit nikaayon main se kai sidhe saamaanya council ko report karte hai.
 vyapaar niti samiksha nikaay : yah urugve round ke baad bane vyapaar niti samiksha tantra kii dekharakh karata he. yah sabhi sadsy deshon kii vyapaar nitiyon kii aavadhik samiksha karata he. isamen bhi dablutio ke sabhi sadsy hote hai.
 vivaad niptaan nikaay : jin deshon ke bich vyapaar sambandhi koi vivaad hota he to ve isi nikaay ke saamne apil kar nyaay maangate hai I yah sabhi dablutio samjhauton ke liye vivaad samaadhaan prakriya ke karyanvayan or prabhaavashiltaa evam dablutio vivaadon par die gaye faislon ke karyanvayan kii dekharekh karata he. isamen bhi dablyudio ke sabhi sadsy hote hai. 
 vastuon evam sevaaon main vyapaar par parishad : yah vastuon or sevaaon main vyapaar par hue aam evam vishesh samjhauton ke vivaranon kii samiksha ke liye tantra pradaan karte hai. yah general council ke janadesh ke tahat kaam karti he. isamen sabhi sadsy desh shaamil rahete hai. 
 vishwa vyapaar sanghathan ke samjhaute lambey or jatil hote hai kyonki inkaa paath kanoonee hota he or yah gatividhiyon kii vyapak range ko cover karte hai. lekin kai saral, moulik siddhanth bhi in sabhi dastavejon main hote hai. ye siddhanth bahupaksheeya vyapaar pranali ke aadhaar hai.
