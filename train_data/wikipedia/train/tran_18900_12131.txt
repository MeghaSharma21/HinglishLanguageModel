 gandbherund ek kaalpanik pakshi jiskaa ankan bhartiya kala main paaya jaataa he. iske ek dhad garud ke sadrush hota he. yah apane donon chonch tathaa panje main hathi daboche ankit kiya jaataa he. iskaa pracheenatam ankan vijaynagar ke aarambhkalik katipay sikkon par paaya jaataa he dakshin ke shaiva mandiron main utsav murtiyon ke rup main gandbherund dekhne main aata he. mysore riyasat ke rajchinh ke rup main is pratik kaa prayog hua he. is pakshi ke sambandh main janashruti he ki hiranyakashyap ke maarane ke pashchaat bhi jab nrisingh kaa krodh shant nahin hua tab gandbherund unhen apane panje main daboch kar akaash main le uda tha.
