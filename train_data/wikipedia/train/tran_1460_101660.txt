 mirat ul-urus nazir ahamed dehalavi dwaara likhaa gaya or san 1869 main prakashit hua ek urdu upanyaas he. yah upanyaas bhartiya or muslim samaaj main mahila shiksha ko badhaava dene vaale kuch tatwon ke liye prasiddh he or isse prerit hokar hindi, punjabi, kashmiri or bhartiya upmahadweep kii anya bhashaaon main bhi is vishay par aadhaarit upanyaas likhe gaye. chhapane ke 20 varshon ke andar-andar is kitaab kii 1 lakh se adhik pratiyaan bik chuki thim. yah aksar urdu bhashaa kaa pehla upanyaas mana jaataa he.
 yah kahani dilli kii do muslim bahanon - akabari or asgari - par aadhaarit he. upanyaas kaa pehla bhaag akabari kii daastaan bataata he jisane amiri or aaraam main janm liya. use aalsee or kam padhaa-likhaa bataaya gaya he. jab vah vivaah ke baad apane pati ke ghar jaati he to apane nasamajhi or bure bartaav se usapar tarah-tarah ke dukh padate hai. upanyaas ke dusre bhaag main asgari kii kahani he jo sadaachari, mehnatee or adhik shikshit he. use bekaar kii gapshap se ghrinaa he or vah apane mohalle kii pyaari he. jab uski shaadi hoti he to uske jivan main bhi kai kathin badlaav aate hai lekin apni mehnat, achche swabhaav or shikshit vyavhaar se vah apane pati ke parivaar or apane nae mohalle ke logon main prempurvak bas jaati he. kahani main in donon striyon ke jivan ko tarah-tarah ke utaar-chadhaav se guzarate hue dikhaaya gaya he.
 1873 main nazir ahamed ne is upanyaas kii duusari curry 'banaat ul-naash' ke naam se prakashit kii. isamen asgari ko apane mohalle main ek kanya-paathshaala chalate hue dikhaaya gaya he.
 pakistan television ne 'mirat ul-urus' hi ke naam kaa ek dharawahik banaaya jisamen aarifaa siddiqi ne asgari kaa paatr adaa kiya.
