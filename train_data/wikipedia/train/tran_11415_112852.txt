 bhitti chitra ko mitti va gaay kaa gobar kaa ghol banaakar deevaaron par lipai kii jaataa he. ise ghar kii teen khaas jagahon par hi banane kii parampara he, jaise- bhagwaan va vivaahiton ke kamare main or shaadi yaa kisi khaas utsav par ghar kii bahari deevaaron par. madhubani painting main jin devi-devataaon ko dikhaaya jaataa he, ve hai- maa durga, kaali, sita-ram, radha-krishna, shiv-parvati, gauri-ganesh or vishnu ke das avatar. in tasveeron ke alaava kai prakritik or ramya najaaron kii bhi painting banaai jaati he. jaanwaron, chidiya, phool-pattee ko swastik kii nishaani ke saath sajaayaa-sanvaara jaataa he.
 chatkh rangon kaa istemaal khub kiya jaataa he. jaise gaharaa laal rang, haraa, nila or kaala. kuch halke rangon se bhi chitra main nikhaar laayaa jaataa he, jaise- pilaa, gulabi or neembu rang. yah jaanakar hairaani hogi kii in rangon ko gharelu chizon se hi banaaya jaataa he, jaise- haldi, kele ke pattey, laal rang ke liai pipal kii chaal prayog kiya jaataa he. or doodh. bhitti chitron ke alaava alpana kaa bhi bihar main kaaphi chalan he. ise baithak yaa phir daravaaje ke baahar banaaya jaataa he. pehle ise isliye banaaya jaataa tha taaki kheton main fasal kii paidaavaar acchhee ho lekin aajkal ise ghar ke shubh kaamon main banaaya jaataa he.
