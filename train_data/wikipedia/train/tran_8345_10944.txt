 petrozavodsk rus ke sudur pashchimottar main sthit ek shahar he jo us desh ke carelia ganatantr naamak sangheey khand kii rajdhani bhi he. yah onegaa jhil ke pashchimi chor par basaa hua he or uske tat par 27 kimi tak vistrut he.
 20 sitambar 1977 main achaanak akaash main bahut oonchaai par tairti hui ek bhimkaay jelifish jaisi kai bhujaaon vaali vastu dekhi. yah afawaah fail gai ki yah kisi anya grah se aaye yatriyon kaa yaan he. kuch vidwaanon ke anusaar yah kisi soviet rocket kii udaan se paidaa hone vaale ajeeb akruti vaale dhuen ke mote badal the. anya logon kaa maananaa tha ki yah kisi gupt soviet parmaanu pareekshan se utapann badal the. soviet vaigyanikon ke anusaar yah ek prakritik ghatana thi. vivaad aaj bhi jari he or ise 'petrozavodsk ghatana' ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
