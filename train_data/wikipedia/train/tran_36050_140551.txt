 
 dhrol riyasat, british bharat ke bombai presidency kii kathiyawad agency or baad main, bharat ganraajya ke kathiyawad or saurashtra agency kaa ek shahi rajya tha. dhrol, britishkaleen bharat ke un 562 shahi riyasaton main se ek tha, jinke shashakon ko kshetreey swaittata praapt thi. dhrol riyasat ko british taj dwaara 9-topi salaami riyasat hone kaa sammaan praapt tha. is riyasat kii rajdhani tha kathiyawad ke aitihaasik halaar kshetra main avasthit dhrol nagar.
 dhrol riyasat kii sthapana san 1595 main, nawanagar riyasat ke sansthaapak, jam rawal ke bhai, jam hardholji dwaara hui thi. dhrol rajpariwaar, jadeja gotra ke rajputon kii varishthatam shakha ke vanshaj the, or shrikrishna ke vanshaj hone kaa daawa karte the, evam swayan ko dwarkadhish ke sammaan se nawaaza karte the.
 dhrol rajya san 1807 main british sangrakshan ke adheen aa padaa, jiske karanvash dhrol naresh ko apni or apane riyasat kii samprabhutaa angrez sarkaar ko saunpny padi. 1899 ke akaal ke samay dhrol ko jaan kaa baari nuksaan sahanaa padaa tha. dhrol rajya kii jansankhya 1891 main 27,007 se ghat kar 1901 kii janaganana main 21,906 rah gai thi. dhrol rajya ke antim shaasha, thakur sahib chandrasinghji deepasinghji ne 15 farvari 1948 main rajarohan kii sandhi par hastakshar kar apni riyasat ko aadhikarik taur par bhartiya sangh main parigrahit kar diya tha.
 kathiyawad ke anya shahi khandaanon ke tarah hi, dhrol ke shashakon ko "jam" yaa "thakur sahab" kaha jaataa tha, haalaanki british kaal ke dauraan dhrol ke shashakon ko "thakur sahib" kii oopaadhi se sambodhit kiya jaataa tha. british sangrakshan main,dhrol naresh ko 9-topon kii salaami ke sammaan pradaan kiya gaya tha, yah sammaan swatantraaa prapti ke pashchaat bhi bharat ganraajya main 1971 tak jari rahaa tha.
