 dow dhirubhai thaakar gujarati vishwakosh ke sampadak or jane-maane gujarati sahityakar the
 dhirubhai ko ranjit ram swarna pad pradaan kiya gaya tha tathaa ve gujarati sahitya parishad ke adhyaksh rah chuke the. 
 dhirubhai thaakar kaa janm 27 june 1918 ko kodinar main hua tha. unke pita lekhpal ke taur nokari karte the. mumbai kii saint jevirs kalege main unhone shiksha prapt kii thi. 1956 main piechdi kar ve modasa college main acharya bane or shiksha ke prati logon ko jaagrit kiya.
 dhirubhai ne manilal nabhubhai dvivedi jaise sahitkaar ke jeevanakatha tathaa aatmakatha likhi. dhirubhai ne pragati or parampara ke naam par ahmedabad ke udyogpati or samajasevi kastoorbhai lalbhai kaa jeevancharitra likhaa tha. manilal nabhubhai kii sahitya sadhana, ras or ruchi, sampra sahitya, pratibha, vibhavitm, natyakala naamak pustak sahit unhone kai sampadit-sankalit granth bhi likhaa tha.
