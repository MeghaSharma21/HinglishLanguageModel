 badi dudhi ek kharpatvaar he.
 bady duddhi ekvarshiya kharpatvaar he. yah unchi bhoomi main ugta he. varsha ritu main adhik vruddhi hoti he. pattiyaan va tana todane par doodh ke rang jaisaa taral padaarth nikalataa hai. jaden musla hoti hai lekin adhik gaharaai tak bhoomi main nahin jaati hai. tana kamjor hota he jo bhoomi par falkar badhata he tathaa tane par chote-chote bal hote hai. pattiyaan dderghaayat, laal rang liye hue tathaa sira nukila hota he. phool compojit, fal capsule bal yukt, 0.125 semi lambe hote hai.
