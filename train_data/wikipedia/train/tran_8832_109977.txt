 adaik sanyukt rajya america ke alaska rajya ke alucins pashchim janaganana kshetra main sthit ek kasbaa he. 2010 kii janaganana main yahan kii kul jansankhya 326 thi. adaik, america kii sabase pashchimi nagarpalika he or alaska main sabase dakshini nagar he. adaik sainya sthal or adaik nosena sthalaa kabhi yahan sthit hua karte the.
 sanyukt rajya janaganana beuro ke anusaar adaik kaa kul kshetrafal 330 varg kimi he jisamen se 317 varg kimi bhushetra or 13 varg kimi jalakshetra he.
 thomas spitlar yahan ke vartmaan mahayor hai.
 yahan kii paariwarik madhya-mulya ausat aay 53,889 dollar he or prati vyakti aay 31,747 $ he. lagbhag 3.3% parivaar or 4.7% jansankhya nirdhantaa rekha se neeche hai jinamein se attharah varsh kii aayu se kam yaa pansth kii aayu se upar kaa nahin he.
