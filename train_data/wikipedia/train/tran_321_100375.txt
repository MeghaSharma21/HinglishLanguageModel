 kiren rijiju bhartiya janata party ke sachiv evam bharat ke grih rajya mantri hai. ve solahavin lok sabha main arunachal pashchim se saansad chune gaye. unhone yahan par bhartiya rashtriya congress ke vartmaan saansad takaam sanjay ko 41,738 maton se haraaya. isse pehle unhone chaudaavin lok sabha main bhi is kshetra kaa pratinidhitiv kiya tha.
 kiren rijiju kaa janm arunachal pradesh ke pashchimi kameng jile ke naphra main hua tha. unke pita kaa naam shri rinchin khaaru or mata kaa naam shrimati chirai jijuju he. unke pita arunachal pradesh kii pratham vidhansabha ke asthaayee adhyaksh the.
