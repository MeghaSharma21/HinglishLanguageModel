  setusamudram jahajrani nahar pariyojna  bharat evam srilanka ke madhya paak jaldmarumadhya or mannar kii khadi ko jodne kaa prastaav he. iske dwaara setusamudram kshetra ke chhichley sagar main ek jahajrani upyukt nahar banaakar bhartiya prayadweep kii paridhi par ek nauvahan maarg sulabh hoga. abhi yah maarg chhichley sagar evam usamen sthit dwipon evam chattaanon kii shrunkhal jise ramaar palam, ramsetu or adams brij kaha jaataa he, ke kaaran yah maarg sulabh nahin he or jahaajon ko srilanka dweep kaa puraa chakkar lagaakar anaavashyak lambaa maarg tay karnaa padataa he. is pariyojna main 44.9 notical mile lambaa ek gaharaa jal maarg khoda jaayegaa jiske dwaara paak jaldmarumadhya ko manaar kii khadi se jod diya jaayegaa. 1860 main alfred dundas tailor dwaara isaki abhiklpana pehali baar kii gayi thi, jise haal hi ke varshon main bharat sarkaar dwaara sweekruti mili he. is pariyojna ke tahat ramsetu ke chuunaapatthar kii shilaon ko todaa jaayegaa, jiske kaaran kuch sanghathanon ne virodh jataayaa he. unka kahana he ki isse hinduon kii dharmik aasthaa ko thes pahonchegi, saath hi paryaavaran or aarthik str par bhi haani hogi. iske alaava kuch suraksha kaaran bhi bataaye gaye hai. inamen se kai partiyaan yaa sanghathan is pariyojna kaa karyanvayan pehle diye gaye 5 vaikalpik tareekon se karne kaa anumodan karti hai. in vaikalpik tareekon se shriramsetu ko haani bhi nahin pahonchegi jisase use pavitra maanane vaale hinduon kii bhaawanaaon ko thes nahin pahonchegi. filhaal nirdhaarit pariyojna main ek madhya maarg chunaa gaya he jisase laghutam maarg sulabh hoga or saath hi nunatam rakrakhaav evam anurakshan vyay hoga. isse purv vishwa ke anya jahajrani nahar pariyojnaayen jaise suez nahar evam panama nahar bhoomi sthit nahar pariyojnaayen thi.
