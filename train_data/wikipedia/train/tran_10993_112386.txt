 lambey chale swatantraaa sangram ke baad 1804 or 1826 ke bich dakshin america ke sabhi spanish upnivesh, swatantra ho gayi. portugali upnivesh brazil main, dom pedro pratham, jo portugali naresh dom joayo shashtum kaa putr tha, ne 1822 main brazil kii swatantraaa kii ghoshnaa kii or brazil kaa pehla samraat bana. portugal ke raaj parivaar ne ise shanti se sweekaar kar liya. guyana iske bahut baad 1966 main united kingdom ke niyantran or surinam 1975 main dach niyantran se mukt hua jabaki french guyana aaj bhi france kaa ek hissaa bana hua he. columbia is samay tak aantarik sangharsh se jujh rahaa he, jo 1964 main marxvaadi chhapamaron ke gathan ke saath shuru hua tha or ab kai vampanthi vichaardhaara rakhane vaale avaidh sashastr samooh or nashili dawaaon kaa vyapaar karne vaale mafiaon kii nijee senaayen isamen shaamil ho gayi hai.

 dakshin america mahadweep ke pashchimi tat par andies parvatamala uttar se dakshin ko faili he. yah parvatamala jal vibhaajak kaa kaary karti he. is mahadweep kii sabase pramukh nadi amejan vishwa kii sabase vishaal nadi he jisamen duniya kii kisi bhi nadi kii tulana main adhik jal pravaahit hota he. 6300 kilometre lambi yah nadi peru desh main andies parvat shrinkhalaa se nikalakar brazil desh main pashchim se purv bahati hui andh mahaasaagar main girti he. isaki sahaayak nadiyaan tapaija, reonigro, tokintins mairaanan tathaa medira he. vishwa kii sabase shaktishaalee yaa tivrvahini orinico nadi bhi dakshin america main hi bahati he. yah andies parvatamala se nikalakar gayana ke pathaar kaa chakkar katati hui 2,400 kilometre kii duuri tay karke venezuela tathaa kolombia deshon main bahati hui andh mahaasaagar main girti he. isaki sahaayak nadiyaan meta, apur, guaviar, kaara tathaa karoni hai. orinico nadi kii ek sahaayak nadi rio gayana par vishwa kaa sabase unchaa jalprapaat angel prapaat he jo niagara jalprapaat se bhi 13 guna unchaa he. brazil ke pathaar ke madhyavarti bhaag se parana or uski sahaayak paragway nikalati he. urugve sera do maar parvat se nikalati he. pilkomyo tathaa salaado nadiyaan andies parvat se nikalakar kramshah paragway tathaa parana nadiyon se mil jaati hai. argentina kii uttari seemaa par paragway nadi parana nadi main mil jaati hai. parana, paragway evam urugway nadiyon kii mili hui dhara ko laplata kehete hai. laplata ke maidan ko uttari bhaag main gran chaako tathaa dakshin bhaag main pampas kehete hai. dakshin america kii anya nadiyaan brazil kii saao francisco, kolombia kii magadalena tathaa argentina kii raayo colorado hai.
 latin america kii jansankhya kaa ek bada bhaag mexico, argentina va brazil main rahata he. in teenon deshon kii jansankhya kul milakar 30 karod se adhik he. is mahadweep kii aaj bhi adhiktar jansankhya tateey kshetron main paayi jaati he, jahaan tat evam tateey sagar main viksit yatayaat atyant sugam he or videshon se sampark aasaani se rakhaa ja saktaa he. aise kshetra brazil ke saao poulo or sentos tak tathaa pathari va purvi mexico, uttari argentina or venezuela ke tateey bhaag main hai. madhyavarti akshanshon main argentina or urugve se laplata nadi ke maidan main krishi or suvidhaon ke kaaran jansankhya adhik saghan milti he. uttari-purvi brazil main ushnardra kheti ke antargat kahavaa or kapaas paidaa kiye jane se jansankhya adhik saghan milti he. yahan par sabhi prakaar kii ushn, ardhoshna va shitoshna fasalein paidaa kii jaati hai. yahan par jan-ghanatv 25 se 100 vyakti ke madhya he. 65 pratishat aabaadi tateey nagaron main basii he. in kshetron ke vipareet, amejan ke junglee or daldali bhaag, andies kii unchaaiyon, patagonia, chile or peru ke marusthaleey tathaa madhya america kii malaria kaarak jalwaayu, grenchako ke garm, daldali or badhgrast kshetra tathaa brazil ke garm ghaas ke maidan main jansankhya bahut hi viral milti he. yahan kaa ghanatv 5 vyakti prati varg kilometre se bhi kam he.
 bolivia or peru jaise dakshin america deshon kii kul jansankhya kaa adhikansh yahaan ke mul nivaasiyon ke vanshaj jaise queshua or ymara se bana he. iske alaava adhiktar anya purv spani upniveshon main bhi inkaa pratishat, jansankhya ke hisaab se mahatvapurn hai. venezuela or kolombia kii jansankhya kaa lagbhag 25% shwet or europea vansh ke vyaktiyon kaa he. jabaki europea mul ke log argentina, urugve or chile main bahusankhyak hai. dakshin america main badi sankhya main africai mul ke log bhi nivas karte hai. africai mul ke log mukhyatah guyana, brazil, kolombia, venezuela, surinam, french guyana or ecuador main rahete hai. mestizo paragway, venezuela, colombia or ecuador kaa sabase bada jaateey samooh he. bhartiya mul ke logon ke vanshaj guyana or surinam ke sabase bade jaateey samoohon main se hai. brazil or uske baad peru main dakshin america kaa sabase bada japani or chini samudaay he.brazil dakshin america kaa sabase zyada vividhtapurna desh he, jisamen shweton, ashweton or mulatuon kii ek badi aabaadi ke saath saath madhya purvi or asian samudaay kaa bhi ek bada hissaa maujuud he. amejan basin evam latin america ke anya pradeshon ke bhojan ekatra karne waalon or pashuchari samudaayon kii jivan-style, arthvyavastha, samaaj, dharm, aasthaaen evam saanskrithik mulya adhikanshtah jalwaayu evam prakritik vanaspati dwaara niyantrit hoti hai.
 dakshin america khanij padaarthon kii drushti se vipann mahadweep he, yani khanij padaarthon kii prachurtaa yahan nahin he. chile, brazil tathaa argentina main lohe kii khaane hai. brazil vishwa main ucch koti ke louh-ayask kaa sabase bada utpaadak he. peru or chile taanbe ke sabase bade utpaadak hai. bolivia, argentina, tathaa venezuela main bhi tanba miltaa he. manganeez kii khaane brazil main hai. sansar ke tin utpaadan kaa paanchvaan bhaag keval bolivia se hi praapt hota he. brazil bhi tin ke sabase bade utpaadakon main he. guyana tat par aluminium miltaa he. chaandi utpaadak kshetra chile, peru tathaa bolivia hai. brazil, peru, bolivia, venezuela, kolombia, chile tathaa ecuador main sone kii khaane hai. khanij tel venezuela, argentina, chile, peru or kolombia main praapt hota he.
 dakshini america ke nivaasiyon kii aajeevika kaa mukhya saadhan krishi or pashupaalan he. krishi mahadweep ke keval paanch pratishat bhaag main hoti he. mahadweep ke do bade desh brazil or argetina krishi main agrani he. krishi yogya bhoomi ke lagbhag aadhe bhaag main khadyannon kii upaj hoti he evam shesh aadhe bhaag main anya vyapaarik evam audyogic kachche maalon kaa utpaadan hota he. gehun pramukh khadyann he jisaki upaj kaa 60 pratishat bhaag niryaat kar diya jaataa he. gehun pramukhataa se argentina, madhyachile, dakshin brazil ke rio grande du sul evam urgve main ugaai jaati he. kahave kii upaj is mahadweep main sabase adhik hoti he. saao poulo sahit brazil ke sabhi rajyon main isaki kheti hoti he. iske atirikt venezuela, kolombia, guyana, ecuador tathaa bolivia main bhi kahavaa paidaa hota he. guyana, brazil, kolombia, peru, tathaa ecuador ke maidani bhaag main dhan utpann hota he. brazil, argentina, peru, kolombia, venezuela, bolivia tathaa paragway main kapaas kii kheti hoti he. iske atirikt dakshini america main makka, jayee, tambaakoo, cocao, rabar tathaa falon kii kheti bhi kii jaati he. plata kshetra main maweshiyon kaa paalan adhik hota he. anya mavashiyon kii tulana main yahan kii ushn katibandheey jalwaayu bhedon ke liye adhik pratikul he, parantu peru ke ucch bhaagon main kaaphi sankhya main bheden paali jaati hai. pepanz or urugve ke ghaas ke maidan main inaki sankhya adhik he. krishi yogya bhoomi ke vistaar ke saath-saath inaki sankhya ab kam ho gai he. bhedon kaa maance, oon evam chamada bhi baahar bheja jaataa he.
 thal golarddh main dakshini america kii sthiti kendra se bahut dur simavarti peti main he. atah european deshon se pravaas karke jane vaale manushyon ne jab tak apana saath le jaai hui europea technology ko in duurastha deshon main nahin pahunchaa diya tha, tab tak is dakshini kshetra main adikalin logon kaa nivas rahaa tha. udyog-dhandhon kii drushti se dakshini america aviksit mahadweep he. yahan par krishi, pashupaalan, maance ko surakshit rakhane, makkhan tathaa paneer banane or bhedon ke paalane kaa udyog hota he. chini bananaa, aataa peesna bhi pramukh udyog he. yahan udyogon kaa aadhaar koyala nahin ke baraabar paaya jaataa he. khanij padaarthon ke vartmaan kshetra rikt ho chuke hai. khanij bhandar mahadweep ke pathari or parvatiy kshetra main hai, jinka vikaas avaagaman evam yatayaat kii kathinaaiyon ke kaaran nahin ho paaya he. mahadweep kaa ek vistrut bhaag sadabahaar wanon se dhakaa he. in kshetron main bahanevaali nadiyaan bhi badi or naugamya hai. parantu shram kii kami tathaa wanon kii saghanta ke kaaran kashtha udyog adhik viksit nahin he. in wanon kii lakadiyaan bhi shitoshna wanon kii tulana main adhik kathor hoti hai.
 dakshini america main yatayaat ke pramukh saadhan nadiyaan, sadaken, vayumarg or samudrin maarg hai. parvatiy or pathari pradeshon ke vistaar ke saath-saath vishuvat rekhiy ghaney jangalon kii durgamata ke kaaran mahadweep kaa adhiktar bhaag yatayaat ke sadhano se viheen he. mahadweep ke aayaat-niryaat main samudri margon kaa vishesh sthaan he. tateey pradeshon ke antarkshetreey vyapaar main iskaa upyog hota he. relon kaa bhi vikaas hua he. is mahadweep ke liye vartmaan yug main vayumarg kaa adhik mahatv he. centiago se uttar pashchimi kinare ke pramukh nagar vayumarg se jude hue hai. antardeshiy sambandh kii drushti se yahan sadakon kaa mahatv kam he. dharaatal kii vishmata ke kaaran sadakon kaa vistaar adhik nahin ho sakaa he, keval maidani bhaagon main kuch sadakon kaa nirmaan hua he. dakshin america kahavaa, kapaas, gehun, khaal tathaa chamada, sookhaa doodh, maance, tambaakoo, chaay, sona, chaandi, rabad, chini, tanba, aadi kaa niryaat karata he. mashine, kapade, lohe or ispaat ke saamaan, chaaval, sookhe fal, aataa, oonee vastra, tel, koyala, rasayan, tathaa dawaaon kaa aayaat karata he. iske vyapaarik sambandh sanyukt rajya america, britain, france, italy, japan, belgium, canada, netherland aadi deshon se hai.
 dakshin america saanskrithik rup se samrudh he. europe ke saath aitihaasik sambandh hone ke kaaran dakshin america logon par europea samskruti, vishesh rup se spain tathaa portugal kii samskritiyon kii jhalak dekhi ja sakati he. dakshin america par sanyukt rajya america kii samskruti kaa bhi prabhaav he. columbia, bolivia or amejan ke kuch hisse main upnivesh-purv swadeshi samskruti kaa gaharaa prabhaav tha. aupniveshik yug ke baad hue rajyon ke vibhaajan kaa aadhaar saanskrithik vibhinnata hi thi. dakshin america deshon ke sangeet main bahut vividhta he. kolombia kii kambia, brazil ke sambha or bossa nova tathaa argentina evam urugve ke tango pramukh paaramparik sangeet hai. iske alaava neuva conceon naamak avyavsaayik loksangit aandolan bhi bahut prasiddh he jo argentina evam chile main shuru hokar shesh latin america main bahut teji se fail gaya. duniya bhar main dakshin america samskruti vibhinn roopon main maujuud he. udhaaharan ke liye radian hastshilp jaise hastshilp ke rup kii europe ke vibhinn bazaaron main kaaphi maang he.
 dakshin america main kaaphi mahtvapurn sahitya rachaa gaya he. gabrial garsia marthes evam pablo neruda jaise lekhakon ko lokapriy sweekruti praapt hui he. khel dakshin america samskruti kaa ek abhinn ang he. footbal sabase mahatvapurn khel he, anya khelon kaa bhi khub abhyaas kiya jaataa he. mahadweep kii sabase mahatvapurn khel pratiyogita kopa america cap ko unmaad ke str par khelaa or dekha jaataa he. pratham fifa vishwa cap kaa aayojan 1930 main urugway main hi hua tha. basketball, tairaaki or wollyboul anya lokapriy khel hai. vibhann deshon main kisi vishesh khel ko rashtriya khel kaa darja praapt he.
 upniveshwaad kaa ant hote-hote adhiktar latin america deshon ne europea bhashaaon ko apana liya or unhen sthaneey bhashaaon ke saath rashtriya bhashaa kaa darja diya. spanish evam portugali bhashaa dakshin america main pramukhataa se boli jaati he. portugali dakshin america kii sarvaadhik boli jane vaali bhashaa he. portugali brazil kii aadhikarik bhashaa he jabaki spanish mahadweep ke adhikansh deshon kii aadhikarik bhashaa he. dach surinam kii aadhikarik bhashaa he tathaa angreji guyana kii aadhikarik bhashaa he guyana main baarah anya bhashaayen bhi boli jaati hai jinamein hindi or arabi pramukh hai. angreji fockland dweep main bhi boli jaati he. francisi, french guyana kii aadhikarik bhashaa he. ecuador main queshua, paragway or bolivia main guarani, peru, chile evam bolivia main ymara naamak sthaneey bhashaayen boli jaati he. mapudungun chile ke kuch kshetron or argentina ke kuch logon dwaara boli jaati he. queshua, ymara or guarani aisi teen bhashaayen hai jinhen mukhya bhashaa spanish ke saath saath rashtriya bhashaa ke rup main manyata mili he. 
 dakshin america kii anya bhashaaon main hindi or indonesian surinam main, italvi argentina, brazil, urugve, venezuela chile or german argentina, chile, venezuela or paragway ke kuch ilaakon main boli jaati he. brazil ke dakshini rajyon ke kai kshetron main german bhi boli jaati he. welsh ko bolne or likhane vaale argentina vaasi patagonia ke aitihaasik shahar trailue or roson main milte hai. japani-vaktaaon ke chote samooh brazil, bolivia, kolombia, paragway, peru or ecuador main milte hai. arabi lebanon, syria philistini mul ke logon dwaara evam brazil, ecuador, peru, chile, argentina ke arab samudaayon ke bich boli jaati he. is mahadweep ke adhikansh deshon ke ucch or ucch shikshit varg niyamit rup se angreji, francisi, german, yaa italvi bhashaaon kaa adhyayan karte hai or in bhashaaon me paarangat hote hai. un kshetron main jahaan paryaton ek mahatvapurn udyog he, angreji tathaa kuch anya europea bhashaayen aksar boli jaati hai. brazil se nikatataa ke kaaran uttari urugve ke kshetron main portugali boli jaati hai.
 dakshin america ke adhikansh log roman catholic isai hai, iske alaava protestant isaio kii sankhya brazil, chile, guatemala or purto rico main vishesh rup se bad rahi he.bolivia, guatemala, mexico or peru jaise deshon ke mul nivasee aaj bhi apane mul sampradaay or anushthanon kaa paalan karte hai. africa mul ke dakshin america naagarik mukhya rup se cuba, brazil or haiti main apane vibhinn puraatan panthon jaise ki santerio, cendombal, umbanda, macumba or adivasee-vudu aadi kaa paalan aaj bhi karte hai. duniya main alan cardek ke aadhyatmavaad ke sarvaadhik anuyaayi brazil main basate he. yahudee, mormonism, genova ke gavaah, buddha, islam, hindu, bahaai or shinto dharm ke maanane vaale bhi dakshin america main basate hai.haal ke varshon me dakshin america ke adhiktar deshon ne dharmanirpekshata ko apanaaya he saath hi naastik logon kii sankhya bhi bade nagaron or argentina, brazil, chile, cuba or mexico jaise deshon main tezi se badi he.
