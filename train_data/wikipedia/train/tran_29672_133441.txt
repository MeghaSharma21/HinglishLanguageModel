 kaprivi dakshini africa main sthit namibia desh ke 13 pradeshon main se ek he. iskaa naam kaprivi pattee ke bhugolic kshetra par padaa he. katima mulilo is pradesh kaa sabase bada shahar he.
 kaprivi lagbhag puri tarah anya deshon se ghira he or iskaa anya namibia se keval pashchim main kavango pradesh ke saath lagi seemaa ke dwaara sampark he. kaprivi main taapmaana unchaa rahata he. yahan disambar se march tak barish kaa mausam rahata he or baari maatra main varsha hoti he. yahan kaa ilaaka daldali kshetron, jangalon, badhgrast maidaanon or ardrabhumi se bharpoor he. zambezi nadi ke alaava is pradesh se kuando nadi or kwando nadi bhi guzarati hai.
