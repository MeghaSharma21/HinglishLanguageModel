 surajkund haryana main faridabad jila sthit he. yah apane hastshilp-mela ke liye prasiddh he.
 haryana kii paryaton vibhaag ne 1981 main shuru kiya tha. tab se har saal inhi dinon ye mela lagata he. is meley kaa mukhya aakarshan he ki bharat ke sabhi rajyon main se sabase acha shilp utpaadon ko ek hi sthaan par jahaan aap na dekh sakate balki unhen mahasoos kar sakate hai or unhen kharid bhi sakate he. is shilp meley main aap sabase achche hathkargha or desh ke sabhi hastshilp paa sakate hai. saath hi mela maidan ke gramin parivesh kii adbhut range aagantukon ko aakarshith kar rahi he. ye mela 15 farvari tak chalegaa. surajkund meley main is gaanv ke maahaul ko na keval shahar kii suvidha-nivasee gaanv jivan kii ek swaad paane ke liye, lekin yah bhi rashtriya or antarrashtriya khariddaaron ke liye pahunch praapt karne ke shilpakaaron main madad karata he.
