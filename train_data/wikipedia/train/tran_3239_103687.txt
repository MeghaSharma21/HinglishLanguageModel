 khagolashastra main seyafort gallexi sakriya gallexiyon ke do pramukh prakaaron main se ek he. quesar doosraa pramukh prakaar hota he. donon main sakriya gallexic nabhik bahut tejaswita se chamakta he, lekin jahaan quesar main gallexi kaa anya bhaag is kendriya tejaswita ke saamne dikhataa nahin he, vahaan seyafort gallexiyon main gallexi ke anya bhaag ko spashtataa se dekha ja saktaa he. puure brahmand main dekhi gai gallexiyon main se lagbhag 10% seyafort gallexiyaan hai.
 seyafort gallexiyon ke kendra main vishalkay kaale chhidr hote hai or unke ird-gird unamem gir rahe malbe ke abhivruddhi chakra hote hai. inamen dekha gaya parabangnee vikiran inhen chakron se utpann hota he or is vikiran ke varnakram ko parakhkar malbe main sammilit padaarth kii pahchaan kii ja sakati he. in gallexiyon kaa naam karl seyafort naamak khagolashastri par rakhaa gaya tha jinhone san 1943 main is shrenee kaa vivaran diya tha.
