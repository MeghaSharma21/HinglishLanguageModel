 lobia ek paudha he jisaki faliyaan patali, lambi hoti hai. inaki sabzi banati he. is paudhe ko hari khaad banane ke liye bhi prayog main laayaa jaataa he.
 lobia ek prakaar kaa boda he. ise 'chaulaa' yaa 'chaura' bhi kehete hai. yah haraa rang kaa ek sabji he jo ki ek haath lambey or bahut komal hote hai or ise pakaakar khaaya jaataa he. iske bijon se daal or dalmot banaate hai. isaki or bhi jatiyaan he; par lobia sabase uttam mana jaataa he. paudha shobhaa or bhaaji ke liye baagon main boyaa jaataa he or bahumoolya hota he
