 bachchan singh ek hindi sahityakar, aalochak evam itihaaskaar the. hindi sahitya main bachchan singh kii khyaati saiddhanthik lekhan ke kshetra main asandigdh he. 
 bachchan singh kaa janm 2 july 1919 ko uttar pradesh ke jonpur jile ke bhadwaar gram main hua tha. unhone udai pratap college tathaa kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay, varanasi se shiksha praapt kii. vibhinn vishvavidhyalayon main adhyaapan kiya. nagari prachaarini sabha, varanasi kii prabandh samiti ke purv sadsy evam padaadhikari rahe. nagari prachaarini patrika kaa lagbhag ek dashak tak avaitnaik sampaadan bhi kiya. varanasi main 5 april 2008 ko pakshaaghaat se unka dehant ho gaya.
 iske atirikt shahid krantikariyon ke jivan par aadhaarit unke ye upanyaas bhi kaaphi lokapriy hue:phaansi se purv, shahid-e-ajam, shahadat .
