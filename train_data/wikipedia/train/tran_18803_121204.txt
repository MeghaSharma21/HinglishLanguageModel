 ummat yaa ummah ek arabi shabd he jiskaa arth he "samudaay" yaa "raashtra". iskaa upyog aamtaur par rajyon ke sangrahit rashtron, yaa puure arab jagat ke liye kiya jaataa he. islam ke paripeksh main, ummaa kaa arth "vishvaasiyon ke samudaay" se he, arthath puraa islamic jagat.
 koran main ummaa vaahida kaa vaakyansh puure sanyukt islamic jagat ko sanderbhit karata he. quran kahati he “tum maanav jati ke liye sarvshereshth rajya ho jo achche kaa aadesh detaa he or bure ko pratibandhit karata he....” 
 vahiin duusari ore, arabi main ummaa kaa upyog raashtra kii paashchaatya paribhaasha ke liye bhi kiya jaataa he jaise, al-umam al-muttahida, arthath sanyukt raashtra.
 kaha jaataa he kii madina ke samvidhan ke ek aarmabhik dastauez par mohammad sahab dwaara 622 isvi main madina kii agrani vanshawaliyon se vaartalap kiya gaya tha, jo spasht rup se madina ke yahudee or pratimapujak nagarikon ko "ummaa" ke sadasyon ke rup main sanderbhit karata he.
 islamic sammelan sanghathan muslim ummaa kaa pratinidhitiv karne vaala mukhya sanghathan he.
 aadhunik ibrani main ummaa kaa arth "raashtra" he.
