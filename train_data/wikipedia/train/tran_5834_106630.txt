 dharavi mumbai kaa ek kshetra he. yah ek jhuggi basti he. yah pashchim mahim or purv sayan ke bich main he or yah 175 hectare, yaa 0.67 varg mile ke ek kshetra main he. 1986 main, jansankhya 530,225 main anumaan lagaaya gaya tha, lekin aadhunik dharavi 600.000 se 1 lakh se adhik logon ke bich kii aabaadi he. dharavi pehle duniya kii sabase badi gandi basti thi, lekin 2011 ke anumaan se ab mumbai main dharavi se bari chaar gandi bastiyaan hai.
