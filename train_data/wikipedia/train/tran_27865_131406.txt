 mount kozosco ke sameep parvatamala kaa ek drishy
 snoi pahadiyaan, jo da snois bhi kehalaati he, australia kii sabase unchi parvatamala he, jinamein australia kii mukhyabhumi kaa sabase unchaa parvat, 2228 meter lambaa mount kozosco, bhi paaya jaataa he. yah parvatashpankhalaa new south velz rajya ke dakshini bhaag main sthit he or mahaan vibhaajak parvatamala kaa hissaa he. snoi pahadiyon main har sheetarutu main badi maatra main himpat hota he jo australia ke liye asaadhaaran he. is karanvash yah paryatakon main bahut lokapriy he. in pahaado main mountain plums-pine naamak chote kad kaa konedhari vriksh miltaa he jo sambhavat prithvi kaa sabase dderghaayu vanaspati he. prithvi ke dakshini golardh main hone kii vajah se australia main sabase adhik sardi june, july or agast ke maheenon main hoti he or tabhi snoi pahadiyon main barf bhi girti he. aamtaur par yah basant ritu tak pigal jaati he. australian mukhyabhumi se baahar tasmania dweep kii oochchabhumi par bhi himpat dekha jaataa he.
