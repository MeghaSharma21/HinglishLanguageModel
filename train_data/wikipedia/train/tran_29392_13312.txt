 landan julongical garden duniya kaa sabase purana scientific ju he, jise varsh 1828 main 27 april ko landan main kholaa gaya tha. landan main yah regents park ke uttari chor par westminster city va cemden ke bich sthit he. shuruyaat main ise sirf julongical socity of landan ke sadasyon ke liye hi kholaa gaya tha. is socity kii sthapana varsh 1926 main stemford raddles ne kuch vigyaniyon ke saath milakar kii thi, jinka makasad tulanaatmak rup se aaazaad maahaul main jeev- jantuo par shodh karnaa tha. varsh 1847 main is julongical garden ko janata ke liye pehali baar kholaa gaya. baharhaal, yah pehla scientific ju to he hi, iske alaava is ju main pehla reptiles house, pehla public equarium, pehla insect house va pehla children ju bhi sthapit kiya gaya. 36 acre ilaake main faile is julongical garden main bees saal pehle tak takreeban 7000 jaanavar the, lekin aaj yahan saadhe saat sau se zyada prajatiyon ke takreeban satraa hajaar jeev- jantuo kaa baseraa he. yahan 130 se zyada prajatiyon se judy prajnan pariyojnaaye bhi chal rahi he.
