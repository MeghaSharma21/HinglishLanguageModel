 yah vikram samvath ke dasven maas pus ke shukla paksh kii 15veen tithi he.
 jain dharmavalanviyon dwaara is din ko shakambhari jayanti ke rup main manaaya jaataa he.
 chhattisgarh ke gramin anchalon main rahane vaali janajatiyaan pus maah ke poornima ke din chherta parv bade dhoomdhaam se manaati hai. sabhi ke gharon main naye chaaval kaa chiwda gud tathaa tili ke vyanjan banaakar khaaya jaataa he. gaanv ke bacchon kii toliyaan ghar-ghar jaakar paramparanusaar prachlit bol chher chherta lathi ke dhan her larika bolte hai or mutthi bhar anaaj maangate hai. raatri main gramin balaayein toli banaakar ghar main jaakar lokdi naamak geet gaati hai.
