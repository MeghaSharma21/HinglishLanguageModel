 khoraat pathaar, jo koraat pathaar bhi kehalaata he, purvottaree thailand ke isaan kshetra main ek pathaar he. iskaa naam nakhon ratchasima ke naam ke sankshiptikaran se bana he.
 pathaar kii ausat oonchaai 200 meter or kshetrafal vistaar 1,55,000 varg kimi he. iske bich se nikalne vaali phu phan pahadiyaan ise do bhaagon main baantati hai: uttar main sakhon nakhon droni or dakshini khoraat droni. pathaar me dakshinpurvi taraf dhalaan he or mun nadi or chi nadi isi disha main bahati hai - yah donon mikang nadi main jal le jane vaali upanadiyaan hai. mikang pathaar kii purvottaree seemaa nirdhaarit karti he. pashchim main fetchboon pahadiyaan or dong faya yen pahadiyaan tathaa dakshinpaschim main dangrek pahadiyaan pathaar ko madhya thailand se alag karti hai. in sab baadhaon ke kaaran pathaar anya kshetron se alag rahaa he or yahan ek sthaneey samskruti panpi he. in sab pahadiyon se varsha girane main bhi rukaawat hoti he or is kshetra main thailand ke anya bhaagon kii tulana main adhik shushkta he jis karanvash yahan chaaval ugane main bhi kathinaayee hoti he.
