 ekikrut parmanvik bhaar ikai, yaa dalton dravyaman kii atyant choti ikai he. yah praayah parmaanu yaa anu ke str ke dravyaman bataane ke liye prayog kii jaati he. ise kabhi-kabhi universal maas unit bhi kehete hai.
 paribhaasha anusaar, parmanvik dravyaman ikai 12C ke ek parmaanu ke dravyaman ke barahawein bhaag ke baraabar hoti he. 
 san 1960 ke pehle oxygen-16 ke dravyaman ke solahaven bhaag ko ek 'bhautik parmanvik dravyaman ikai' kehete the. isi prakaar 1961 tak oxygen ke ausat parmaanu bhaar ke solahaven bhaag ko 'rasinic parmanvik dravyaman ikai' kehete the. dono hi 'ikaayiyon' ko amu pratik se darshaya jaataa tha. 
 baad main, 'ekikrut parmanvik dravyaman ikai' ko 1960 main 'intaranational union of pyor and applid pisics' ne or san 1961 main 'intaranational union of pyor and applid chemistry' ne sweekaar kar liya or dono hi iske liye sanket u kaa prayog karte hai or ise carbon-12 ke ek parmaanu ke dravyaman ke barahawein bhaag ke rup main paribhaashit karte hai.
