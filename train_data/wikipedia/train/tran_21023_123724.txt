 rajeshwar ek mast maula fitarat kaa nami vaqeel he jo kaam kaaj main man nahin lagaataa he. ek din machhlee pakadaney ke liye vah jhil main jaataa he tabhi ek ladki jhil main kud jaati he. rajeshwar ladki ko bachaata he par tabhi police aa jaati he or tab rajesh ko pataa chalata he ki yah ladki nila ek koon ke jurm main mulzima he. usne apana iqbaal-e-jurm bhi kar liya he. rajeshwar nila ko bachaane kaa bidaa uthaa letaa he. lekin jab vah is maamle kii tah tak jaataa he to pataa chalata he ki qaatil to koi or hi he. kya rajeshwar nila ko bachaa paaegaa?
