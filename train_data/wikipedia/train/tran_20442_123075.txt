 daal main kaala bhartiya hindi hasya dharawahik he, jiskaa prasaaran star plus par 1998 main shuru hua. iske nirmaataa aashaa paarekh or nirdeshak gyaan sahay hai. iske lekhan kaa kaary vipul shah or sampaadan iqbaal ahamed ne kiya he.
 ram sinha, jiske paas ek dhaba he. vah auraton se nafrat karata he, is kaaran vah kisi bhi aurat ko apane dhabas main aane nahin detaa or chahta he ki uske bhai lakhan, bharat or shatrughan bhi auraton ko uske dhabas main aane na den. lekin uske bhai iske vipareet soch vaale hote hai. vah ram ke vahaan nahin hote samay apni marji se kai tarah ke kaary karte hai. jisase kai baar kai tarah ke aapadaa bhi aa jaati he.
