 uttar osetia-aalania cocks kshetra main sthit rus kaa ek sangheey khand he jo ganatantr kaa darja mila rakhata he. iskaa dakshini bhaag kaafii pahari he or cocks parvat shrunkhlaa ke ilaake main padataa he jabaki iskaa uttari hissaa stavropol ke maidan kshetra main aata he. iskaa kul kshetrafal 8,000 varg kimi he or yahan san 2002 main hui janaganana ke anusaar isaki aabaadi 7,12,900 thi. isamen se lagbhag 62% osetian log the or 23% roosi the. alp sankhyaon main ingush, armenian or ukraini log bhi yahan rahete hai. osetian log oseti bhashaa bolte hai or adhiktar isaai mat ke anuyaayi hai. uttar osetia-aalania kii rajdhani vladikavkaaz naam kaa shahar he.
 madhyakaal main osetiyon ke purvajon ko aalaan bolaa jaataa tha or unhone apane raaj kaa naam aalania rakhaa tha. is aitihaasik rajya ko dhyaan main rakhte hue hi is ganatantr kaa naam "uttar osetia-aalania" rakhaa gaya.
