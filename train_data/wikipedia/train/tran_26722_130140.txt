 
 jaise-jaise vaayu pradooshan badhata ja rahaa he vaise-vaise mausam bhi apana mizaaj badal rahaa he. garmi pad rahi he to itani pad rahi he ki saalon puraane record todatii ja rahi he thik aisa hi sardi ke maamle main bhi he. isliye doston hamako vaayu pradooshan ko kisi bhi haalat main rokna he.
 jitani tezi se ye pradooshan bad rahaa he mujhe to lagata he kii saans lene ke liye shuddh vaayu milani bhi bahut mushkil ho jaaegi. pradooshan ko samaapt to nahin kiya ja saktaa parantu isaki rokthaam kii ja sakati he. iske liye hamako vriksharopan karnaa padegaa or green house gaison kaa utpaadan bhi kam karnaa hoga. hamako chahie ki ham nijee vaahanon kii bajaay public transport kaa prayog karen.
 doston sab kuch hamaare haathon main hi he, chaahe to bana den, chaahe to bigaad den.
 doston hamako apane jivan kaa andaaz badalanaa hoga, nahin to agar mausam ne apana andaaz badal liya to hamaara jeena durbhar ho jaayegaa. 
 vishwa ke sarvaadhik 20 pradooshit shaharon main se 13 bharat main he .
