 avantiwarman 885 se 884 tak kashmir ke raja the. vah lalitaditya ke baad raja bane.unka rajya ek suvarna kaal mana jaataa he. avantipur nagar unke naam par he.
 utpal rajkul kaa yah pehla raja jab kashmir kii gaddi par baitha tab kashmir grihayuddh se lahooluhaan ho rahaa tha or usapar daridrata kii chaaya dol rahi thi. karkatak raajaaon kii kamajori se gaanvon ke diar jameendaar shashakt ho gaye the or unke kaaran praja tabah thi. na jivan kii raksha ho paati thi, na dhan kii. desh kii upaj itani kam ho gai thi ki ann sone ke bhav bikne laga tha. avantiwarman ne desh main shanti sthapit karne kaa safal praytn kiya. diaron ko dabaakar usne apane mantri suy kii sahaayta se desh kii aarthik sthiti sanbhali, naharen nikalwakar sinchai kaa prabandh kiya or jhelum kii dhara badal di. ek khirani chaaval kaa mulya, jo pehle 200 dinar hua karata tha, ab 36 dinar kaa ho gaya. avantiwarman ne avantipur naam kaa nagar basaaya jo vantpor ke naam se aaj bhi maujuud he. usne anek mandir banavaakar unhen devottar sampatti se samrudh kiya. vah panditon kaa aadar karata tha or usee kii sangraksha main prasiddh sahityakar aalochak aanandavardhan ne apana 'dhvanyalok' rachaa.
