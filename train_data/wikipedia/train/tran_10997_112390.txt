 haraare zimbabwe kii rajdhani he hone ke alaava zimbabwe kaa sabase bada shahar he tathaa zimbabwe kaa sabase bada prashaskeey, aarthik tathaa sanchaar centre he. tambaku, makaay aadi kaa yah vyapaar centre he. yahaan par steel tathaa rasayanon kaa utpaadan hota he. 
 haraare shahar kii sthapana 1890 main hui thi jab yahaan par pionier kolum dwaara ek killa banaaya gaya tha. shahar kaa naam tab selisburi rakkha gaya tha. 1935 main ise ek shahar kaa rup mila. 18 april 1982 ko ise haraare naam diya gaya
