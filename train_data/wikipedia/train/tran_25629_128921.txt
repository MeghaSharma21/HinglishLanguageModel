 general vijay kumaar singh, param vishisht seva medal, ati vishisht seva medal, yuddh seva medal, edisi bhartiya sena ko 26wein thal-senadhyaksh the. vartmaan main ve ghaziabad se bhaajapa ke saansad he tathaa narendra modi nit sarkaar main uttar-purvi bharat se sambandhit maamalon ke rajyamantri rahe hai evam ab videsh rajya mantri hai. general ke pad tak pahunchne vaale ve pehle prashikshit commando hai or ve ese pratham bhartiya sena pramukh hai jo sarkaar ko nyaayalay tak lekar gayi.
 vijay kumaar singh bhartiya rajya haryana ke bhivani zile ke bapora naamak gaanv se hai. wo lagaataar teesri peedi main adhikari hai. unke pita bhartiya sena main karnal the or unke pitamah junior kamishan afasar the. singh ne birla public skool, pilaani, rajasthan se shiksha praapt kii.
 singh ko 14 june 1970 ko rajput regiment kii dwitiya vahini main niyukt kiya gaya. baad main unhone isi ikai kaman sambhaali jab ise pakistan ke saath niyantran rekha par tainaat kiya gaya.
 singh 31 may 2012 ko 26 maah kii seva ke baad thal-senadhyaksh ke pad se mukt hue. unke baad is pad par bikram singh padast hue. sevanivritti ke turant baad singh ne anna hajaare dwaara chalaaye ja rahe bhrashtachar virdohi andolan kaa samarthan pradarshit kiya.
 11 march 2011 ko united stats army var college ne international felos haul of fame se pratishthapit kiya. antarrashtriya adhyeta ke rup main navaaje gayi wo pratham bhartiya sena-adhikari evam kul 33wein vyakti hai.
 vartmaan main general wike sinh bharat ke gajiabad sansadeey kshetra se saansad he. ve 1 march 2014 ko wike singh bhartiya janata party main shaamil hue. is maukey par general singh ne kaha ki "bhaajapa hi rashtrahit main kaam karne vaali party he or ek sainik ke naate 42 saal tak desh kii raksha karne ke baad ab main is party ke jariye desh kii seva karnaa chahta hum". isse pehle unhone haryana main bhaajapa ke pradhanmantri pad ke ummidwar narendra modi kii raily main unke saath manch saajha kiya tha.
