 kavalaahar muni ke aahaar kaa paryaayavachi he. aagam main kiye gaye muni ke aahaar ke chah bhed bataaye gayi hai- nokarmaahaar, karmahaar, kavalaahar, lepyaahaar, ojaahaar or maansaahar. bhagawatisutra, gaatha 211 main muni kaa adhiktam aahaar 32 or aryika kaa 28 kaval bataaya he. ek kaval kaa utkrisht pramaan 50 chawlon kaa bhaat he. gyatavya he ki muni kaa chatha bahya tap avamaudarya he. 
 mul manyata yahi he ki kevali ke kavalaahar nahin hota he kyonki unke sharir kii sthiti ke liye nokarm-karmahaar hi paryaapt hote hai. uttar kaal main savastr mukti ke samaan kevali ke kavalaahar kii bhi kalpana kii gai. falat: kavalaahar digambar tathaa shwetambar sampradaayon kii mukhya teen bhinnataaon main se bhi ek he.
