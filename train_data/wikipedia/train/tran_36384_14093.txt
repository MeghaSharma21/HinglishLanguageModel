 
 binturong, jise bhalobilli, asian bhalobilli yaa palawan bhalobilli bhi kehete hai, vivariday parivaar kaa ek standhari jaanavar he. iske "bhalobilli" bulaae jane ke baavjood yah na to billi he or na hi bhaaluu. iskaa naam ek vilupt ho chuki malaysian bhashaa se aayaa he or is shabd kaa arth agyaat he. yah pashu wanon kii oonchi shaakhon main rahanaa pasand karata he or purvottar bharat ke jangalon main paaya jaataa he. bharat se baahar yah bangladesh, bhutan, burma, cambodia, indonisia, laos, malaysia, china, philippines, thailand or vietnam main miltaa he.
 yah ek nishachari jaanavar he or raat ko sakriya rahata he. binturong adhiktar fal khate hai, lekin andey, tahaniyaan, pattey, chote jaanavar or chidiyaan sabhi isaki khuraaq main sammilit hai. iske prakritik vaas-kshetra main wanon ke cat jane se isaki sankhya bahut ghat gai he. agar iskaa peechha kara jaae or ise lage kii bhaagane kaa koi upaay nahin he, to binturong bahut khunkaar tarah se lard saktaa he. saadhaaran sthitiyon main yah halaki hansane jaisi awaz karata he lekin krodh yaa dar main unchi or patali chikhen nikaalata he. chidyagharon main isaki aayu 20-25 saal tak dekhi gai he.
 angrezi main ise "binturong" likhaa jaataa he or "bearcat" bhi bulaayaa jaataa he. indonesia ke riyau kshetra main ise "tenturun" kaha jaataa he. bharat main asamiya bhashaa main ise "young" or burma main ise "myouk-kya" kaha jaataa he.
