 druid duusari sadi isapurv ke lekar teesri sadi tak roman purv britain ke vaasi the. inako vishesh vedi, pujan, gyaan or pattiyon ke aushadhiy istemaal ke liye janaa jaataa he. ye maanav or pashuon kii bali dete the. soman samraat julius seizar ne samraat banane se pehle inke upar kuch lekh likhe the. iske alaave greek or roman lekhakon se inke bare main pataa chalata he.
