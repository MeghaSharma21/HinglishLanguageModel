 alekh patra british shashanrat bharat main bhartiya rashtrawaad kaa ek pramukh netaa the.. ahimsak naagarik avagya ko niojit karte hue, unhone bhartiya swatantraaa sangram main bhaag liya or naagarik adhikaron ke liye paryaavaran sangrakshan andolanon ko prerit kiya, odisha ke vibhinn kshetron main naagarik adhikaar..
 unhone 18 saal kii umr main swatantraaa andolan main bhaag liya. swatantraaa sangram ke dauraan, unhone apane doston ke saath british raaj ke virodh main nimapada ke police stesan ko jala diya. is ghatana ke dauraan, police golibari hui or unke kareebi sahyogi or mitra kii sthaan par maut ho gai. vah batch gaye or giraftaar kar liya or puri jail main rakhaa gaya. british jail use bhaagane se nahin roak paae or upniveshwaad ke khilaaf apane bhumigat sangharsh ko jari rakhe. vah kalakatta gaye or kuch amir vyakti ke ghar main, gharelu bhoomi ke rup main kaam kiya.
 lekin vah vahaan lambey samay tak nahin rah sake kyonki vah acharya harihar, gopabandhu das ityadi jaise kinvadantiyon ke saath khuleam ladanaa chaahate the, or apane doston ke anurodh par, vah odisha waapas aa rahe the. tabhi puri railway stesan par pakadaa liya jiske baad kolkata ko waapas le jaaya gaya, or phir jail main daal diya.
 jail ke andar, vah apane kapade banane ke liye kapaas katai kaa abhyaas karata the, svachh vaataavaran ko banaae rakhane ke liye shauchalayon kii safai karata the, dainik samooh kii prarthana karata the or gandhiji ke anya nirdeshon kaa paalan karata the. jail se reeha hone ke baad, vah swaraj, grih shashan or anya sarvoday kaaryon par prashikshan paane ke liye wardha gaye.
