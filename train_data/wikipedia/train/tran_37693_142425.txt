 kanshiram bhartiya raajnneetijna or samaaj sudhaarak the. unhone bhartiya varna vyavastha main achhuton or daliton ke raajnneetik ekikaran tathaa utthan ke liye kaary kiya. iske ant main unhone dalit shoshit sangharsh samiti, 1971 main akhil bhartiya pichhadaa or alpsankhyak samudaayon karmchaaree mahasang or 1984 main bahujan samaaj party kii sthapana kii.
