 kaila devi vanya jeev abhayaarany bharat desh main rajasthan rajya main savai madhopur evam karauli jile ke antargat 376 varg kilometre kshetra main sthit he. ise isvi san 1983 main vanya jeev abhayaarany kaa darja diya gaya tha. isamen mukhya rup se bagheraa,gosh, rich, suar, chinkaara, sambar, chital evam lomadee aadi vanya jeev paye jate hai. abhayaarany rajasthan or madhyapradesh kii seemaa ko jodta he. abhayaarany ke pashchimi kinare par banaas nadi or dakshin-purv disha main chambal nadi kaa pravaah he.kaila devi vanyajeev abhayaarany kaa naam kaila devi mandir ke naam se padaa he.
