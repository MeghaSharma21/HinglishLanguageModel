 tarkshastra or bhaashan kala main ek galat tark ko kutark kehete hai. kutark do prakaar ke hote hai : aupchaarik or anaupchaarik.
 aupchaarik kutark us kutark ko kehete hai jo sanrachanaatmak hota he.
 anaupchaarik kutark us kutark ko kehete hai jisamen galati kaa kaaran sanrachanaatmak nahin hota he. aise kutarkon ke arth kii jaanch karne kii zarurat hoti he.
 laal hilsa kutark us kutark ko kehete hai jisamen galat nishkarsh nikaalane ke liye kathan ko anjaane main yaa jaanboojhkar bhatakaane vaala banaaya gaya ho.
