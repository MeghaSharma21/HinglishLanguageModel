 sang hindi shabd 'swaang' kaa apbhransh he. uttari bharat main haryana, uttar pradesh va rajasthan rajyon main prachlit sang ek prakaar kii sangitamay naatika hoti he, jisamen lokkathaon ko loakgeet, sangeet va nrity aadi se natyabaddh kiya jaataa he. 
 is vidha main pandit lakhmi chand kaa naam bade sammaan se liya jaataa he. unhone 65 ke lagbhag sang likhe hai, jiske kaaran unhen sang-samraat tathaa haryana kaa suryakavi kaha jaataa he.
