  madhukhaali upjila, bangladesh kaa ek upazila he, joki bangladesh main tritiy str kaa prashasnic anchal hota he . yah dhaka vibhaag ke faridpur zile kaa ek upjila he. yah bangladesh kii rajdhani dhaka ke nikat avasthit he.
 yahan kii aadhikarik str kii bhashaayen bangla or angrezi he. tathaa bangladesh ke kisi bhi anya kshetra kii tarah hi, yahan kii bhi pramukh maukhik bhashaa or maatrubhaasha bangla he. bengali ke alaava angrezi bhashaa bhi kai logon dwaara jaani or samajhi jaati he, jabaki saanskrithik or aitihaasik nikatataa tathaa bhashaai samanta ke kaaran, kai log simit maatra main hindustani bhi samajhane main sakasham hai. yahan kaa bahusankhyak dharm, islam he, jabaki pramukh alpsankhyak dharm, hindu dharm he. chattagram vibhaag main, janasankhyikeek rup se, islam ke anuyai, aabaadi ke ausatan 91.19% he, joki bangladesh ke tamaam vibhaagon main adhiktam he. shesh jansankhya pramukhtah hindu dharm kii anuyai he.
 madhukhaali upjila bangladesh ke madhya main sthit, dhaka vibhaag ke faridpur jile main sthit he.
