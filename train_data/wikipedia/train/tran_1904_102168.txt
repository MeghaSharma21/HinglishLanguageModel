 finnish athlete ne greeshmakaleen khelon main kul 302 padak jite hai, zyaadaatar athletics or kushti main. finland ne sheetakaaleen khelon main 161 padak bhi jite hai, zyaadaatar nord skiing aayojanon main hai. 
 finland ke liye rashtriya olympic samiti finnish olympic samiti he, or 1907 main finland ek swytt hissaa tha, roosi samrajya ke finland ke grand dachi.
 finland ne ek avasar par khelon kii mejabani kii he.
