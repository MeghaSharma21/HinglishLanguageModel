 kings ilevan punjab indian premier lig kii punjab frenchizi he arthath ipel kii ek team he jo indian premier lig main khelti he. vartmaan main team ke captain ravichandran aswin hai. inase pehle team ke captain ke taur par bhartiya cricket team ke yuvaraj singh tathaa virendra sahvaag bhi rah chuke hanttha australia ke jorge beli bhi captaini kar chuke he. team ke coach bread hoj hai. team ke maalik vartmaan main bollywood abhinetri priti jinta ,wadiya group ke ness wadiya ,dabar company ke mohit burman or karan paul he. team kaa gharelu stadium pisie stadium mohali he. 2010 ke baad apane kuch match dharmashala stadium main bhi kheltaa he. team kaa sabase acha pradarshan 2014 main rahaa tha jisamen ipel main final main pahunchi tathaa champions lig tinti 20 main semifinal main apni jagah pakki kii thi.
 2008 main bisisiai ne indian premier lig naamak ek tinti - tinti toornament kii ghoshnaa kii thi. jisamen kul nilaamee ke pashchaat 8 frenchise tiimon ko rakhaa gaya jisaki nilaamee mumbai main 20 farvari ko hui thi. isamen kings ilevan punjab ko dabar company ke mohit burman, wadiya group ke ness wadiya ,bollywood film abhinetri priti jinta or apijay surendra group ke karan paul ne kharida tha. frenchise ko kharidne main kul $76 million kaa bhugtan karnaa padaa |
 2018 ipel sijan ke liye kings ilevan punjab team main aaarasicandran aswin, yuvaraj singh,keel rahul, eaaran finch or kris gail shaamil hai. aswin ko captain niyukt kiya gaya he. bread hoj ko coach ke rup main niyukt kiya gaya he.
