 nirmala, munshi premachand dwaara rachit prasiddh hindi upanyaas he. iskaa prakaashan san 1927 main hua tha. san 1926 main dahej pratha or anamel vivaah ko aadhaar bana kar is upanyaas kaa lekhan prarambh hua. elahabaad se prakashit hone vaali mahilaon kii patrika 'chaand' main navambar 1925 se disambar 1926 tak yah upanyaas vibhinn kishton main prakashit hua.
 mahila-kendrit sahitya ke itihaas main is upanyaas kaa vishesh sthaan he. is upanyaas kii katha kaa kendra or mukhya paatr 'nirmala' naam kii 15 varshiya sundar or susheel ladki he. nirmala kaa vivaah ek adhed umr ke vyakti se kar diya jaataa he. jiske purv patni se teen bete hai. nirmala kaa charitra nirmal he, parantu phir bhi samaaj main use anadar evam avhelnaa kaa shikaar hona padataa he. uski pati parayanata kaam nahin aati. us par sandeh kiya jaataa he, use paristhitiyaan use doshi bana detii he. is prakaar nirmala vipareet paristhitiyon se juujhati hui mrityu ko praapt karti he.
 nirmala main anamel vivaah or dahej pratha kii dukhaant kahani he. upanyaas kaa lakshya anamel-vivaah tathaa dahez pratha ke bure prabhaav ko ankit karata he. nirmala ke madhyam se bharat kii madhyavargeey yuvatiyon kii dayaneey haalat kaa chithran hua he. upanyaas ke ant main nirmala kii mrityri is kutsit saamaajik pratha ko mitaa daalane ke liye ek baari chunauti he. premachand ne bhaalchand or moteram shastri ke prasang dwaara upanyaas main hasya kii sristi kii he.
 nirmala ke chaaron ore katha-bhawan kaa nirmaan karte hue asambaddh prasangon kaa purnatah bahishkaar kiya gaya he. isse yah upanyaas sevasadan se bhi adhik sugranthit evam susangathit ban gaya he. ise premachand kaa pratham ‘yathartwaadee’ tathaa hindi kaa pratham ‘manovaigyanik upanyaas’ kaha ja saktaa he. nirmala kaa ek vaishishty yah bhi he ki isamen ‘prachaarak premachand’ ke lop ne ise ne keval kalatmak bana diya he, balki premachand ke shilp kaa ek vikaas-chinh bhi ban gaya he.
