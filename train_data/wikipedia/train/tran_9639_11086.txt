 jhalarapatan rajasthan kaa ek pramukh nagar evam rajasthan kii ek pracheen riyasat.
 vartmaan jhalarapatan nagar ek parvat upatyaka main sthit he. pracheen nagar kuch dur chandrabhaga nadi ke kinare sthit tha. naam ke mul ke sambandh main itihaaskaaron main matbhed he. kuch kaa mat he ki jhaalaa rajputon ke basane ke kaaran iskaa naam jhalarapatan prachlit ho gaya. iske viruddh anya vidwaanon kaa vichaar he ki nikatsthit parvat se nirantar jal nikalate rahane ke kaaran iskaa yah naam padaa. taad ke matanusaar, yahan ke pracheen mandiron me, jinka nirmaan 600 i0 main hua, adhik sankhya main ghante hone ke kaaran jhalarapatan naam prachlit hua. mandiron kii sundarataa kii prashansa general kaningham aadi anek lekhakon ne kii he. jhaam ke haathon in mandiron kaa vinaash hua.
 jaalim singh naamak ek sardaar ne san 1796 main vartmaan jhampaton or jhalarapatan chhavnee kii sthapana kii. nagar or ek sadak se jude hai. kalantar main chhavnee main bastiyaan ban gai.
