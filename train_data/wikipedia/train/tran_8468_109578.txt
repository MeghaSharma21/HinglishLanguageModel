 mashq bharat, pakistan, nepal or bangladesh main ek parmparik pani or anya dravyon ko le jane vaali chamade kii thili kaa naam hota he. yah chamada aksar bakrii kii khaal kaa banataa he jise jalrodhak banaaya gaya ho, yani jisaki aise tel yaa rogan se maalish kii gai ho jo usamen se pani chooney se roak sake. mashq alag-alag aakaaron main aayaa karte the. choti mashqon haath main uthaai jaati thim or unamem sharaab jaise dravya rakhe jate the, jabaki badi mashaaen kandhe par tangi jaaya karti thim or unamem pani le jaaya jaataa tha. mashqon main ek hi tang-sa moonh hua karata tha.
 badi mashq le jane waalon ko 'mashqi' kaha jaataa tha or aksar 'bhishti' naamak jati ke log mashqon main pani le jaaya karte the. bhartiya upmahadweep main jabardast garmi padati he isliye mashq se pani pilaane or dene waalon kaa yah naam farsi ke 'bahisht' shabd par padaa, jiskaa matlab 'swarg' hota he, kyonki is pani se garmi main tap rahe logon, pashuon or paudhon ko swarg jaise thandak pahunchati thi.
 'mashq' shabd main 'qa' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'k' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'qareeb' or 'qeemat' ke 'qa' se miltaa he.
