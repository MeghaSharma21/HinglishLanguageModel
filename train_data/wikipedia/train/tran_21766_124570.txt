 nuristaan afghanistan kaa ek praant he jo us desh ke purv main sthit he. is praant kaa kshetrafal 9,225 varg kimi he or isaki aabaadi san 2002 main lagbhag 1.1 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. is praant kii rajdhani paroon shahar he. is praant kii sarahadein pakistan se lagati hai. yahan ke lagbhag 95% log nuristaani hai.
 1890 ke dashak tak nuristaan ko 'kaafiristaan' ke naam se janaa jaataa tha kyonki is ilaake ke nuristaani log islam kii bajaae ek hindu-dharm se miltaa-julata dharm rakhte the. 1895-96 main afghanistan ke amir abdur rahamaan khan ne akraman kar ke yahan qabza jamaa liya or yahan ke logon ko muslim banane par vivash kiya. usee samay is ilaake kaa naam badalakar 'nuristaan' rakh diya gaya.
