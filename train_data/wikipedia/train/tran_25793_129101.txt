 radio tarangen ve vidyut chumbakeey tarangen hai, jinka tarangadairghy 10 centimetre se 100 kimi ke bich hota he. ye manavnirmit bhi hoti hai or prakritik bhi. maanav kii koi indriya inhen pahchaan nahin sakati balki ye kisi anya takneekee upkaran dwaara pakadi evam anubhav kii jaatin hai. inkaa prayog mukhyatah bina taar ke, vaataavaran yaa bahari vyom ke dwaara soochna kaa aadan pradaan yaa parivahan main hota he. inhen anya vidyut chumbakeey tarangon se inaki tarang dairghy ke adhaar par pruthak kiya jaataa he, jo apekshaakrit adhik lambi hoti he.
 
 radio tarangen vidyut dhara ko radio aavriti par prayaavartan karne par banati hai. yah dhara ek vishisht chaalak jise antina kehete hai, se paas karai jaati he. isaki lambaai tarang dairghy ke baraabar hi honi chaiye, jisase ki dakshata se kaary ho paae. atyadhik lambi tarangen prayogic nahin hai, kyonki unke hetu atyadhik lambaa antina chahie, jo sambhav nahin he. haalanki ve bhi kabhi kabhi tadit girte samay banati hai. radio tarangen antarikshawya prakriya se bhi banatin hai, parantu ve sudur gahan antariksh main hi banati hai.
 inhen tarang dairghy par aadhaarit, vibhinn patteeyon main baanta gaya he.
