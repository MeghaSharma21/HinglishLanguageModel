 licotenstein ne pehali baar 1936 main olympic khelon main bhaag liya, or tab se athleates ko greeshmakaleen olympic khelon or sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main pratispardhaa karne ke liye bheja he.
 licotenstein ke athlete ne kul nau padak jite, sabhi alpine skiing main. sheetakaaleen olympic main ek hi samaaroh main apane sabhi padak jeetne vaali yah ekmaatr raashtra he. yah sheetakaaleen main padak jeetne vaale ekmaatr aisa desh he, lekin greeshmakaleen, olympic khelon nahin.
 licotenstein olympic samiti 1935 main banaai gai thi. 
 desh ke enosi ke ek sansthaapak sadsy eksver frick, akele licotenstein athlete hai, jinhone garmi or sardi olympic khelon donon main hissaa liya he.
