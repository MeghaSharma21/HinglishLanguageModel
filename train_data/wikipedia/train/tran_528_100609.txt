 paalama girjaghar jise angrezi main Cathedral of Santa Maria of Palma yaa saadharantah "laa seu" kaha jaataa he, ek gothic style main bani roman catholic girjaghar ko kehete hai jo paalama de malorka, mayoraka, spain main maujuud he. ise usee sthaan par banaaya gaya he jahaan kabhi arabon ne ek masjid banaai thi. yah 121 meter lambaa, 55 meter chaudaa or iske minar 44 meter lambe hai. 
 yah katalan ke gothic vaastukala style main bana he magar isamen kai or uttari europi prabhaav saafe chhalakate hai. iskaa nirmaan aragon ke james pratham dwaara 1229 main shuru hua tha magar ise keval 1601 main puraa kiya ja sakaa tha.
