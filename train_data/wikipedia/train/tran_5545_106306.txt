 mahaasaagar ke jal ke satat evam nirdesht disha vaale pravaah ko mahasagariya dhara kehete hai. vastutah mahasagariya dharaaen, mahasaagaron ke andar bahane vaali ushn yaa sheethal nadiyaan hai. praayah ye bhranti hoti he ki mahasaagaron main jal sthir rahata he, kintu vastav me aisa nahi hota he. mahaasaagar kaa jal nirantar ek niyamit gati se bahata rahata he or in dhaaraon ke vibhinn rup dekhne ko milte hai. prakritik dhara main pramukh apvahan dhara evam stream karant hoti hai. ek stream karant kii kuch simaaen hoti hai, jabaki apvahan dhara karant ke bahaav kii koi vishisht seemaa nahin hoti. prithvi par registaanon kaa nirmaan jalwaayu ke parivartan ke kaaran hota he. ucch daab ke kshetra evam thandi mahasagariya jal dharaaen hi ve prakritik ghatnaayen hai, jinaki kriyaaon ke falswaroop saikadon varshon ke baad registan banate hai.
 mahasagariya dhara banane ke mukhyat: teen kaaran hote hai - pratham to jal main lavan kii maatra ek sthaan kii apeksha duusare sthaan par badalati he, isliye sagariya jal ke ghanatv main bhi sthaan ke saath-saath parivartan aata he. dravyon kii prakritik pravriti jisamen ve adhik ghanatv vaale kshetra kii ore agrasar hote hai, ke kaaran dharaaen banati hai. duusare kaaran main surya kii kirane jal kii sataha par ek samaan nahin padatin. is kaaran jal ke taapmaana main asamanta aa jaati he. iske kaaran sanvahan dhara paidaa hote hai. teesraa kaaran sagar kii sataha ke upar bahane vaali tej hawaaen hoti hai. unamem bhi jal main tarangen paidaa karne kii kshamta hoti he. ye tarangen prithvi kii parikrama se bhi banati hai. is ghurnan ke kaaran prithvi ke uttari hisse main ghadi kii disha main dharaaen banati hai. is prakaar mukhya kaarnon main nimna aate hai:
 prithvi par anek dharaaen bahati hai. in sab main gulf stream sabase mahatvapurn hota he. is stream main jal nila evam ushn ho jaataa he. iskaa bahaav mexico kii khadi ke uttar se canada tak hota he. yahi kaaran he ki landan evam paris kam thande rahete hai jabaki norge ke tateey ilaake puure varsh barf rahit rahete hai. iske alaava, brazil karant, japan, uttar bumadhya rekha, uttar prashanth mahasagariya tarang aadi vishwa kii pramukh sagari dhaaraon main gine jate hai.
 
 mahasagariya dharaaen sagariya jivan ke liye atyaavashyak hoti hai. ye sagariya jeev-jantuon ke liye aahaar kaa mukhya strot hoti hai. sagar tarangon se garm jal sheethal jal vaale kshetron tak jaataa he. iske vipareet sagariya tarangon kaa asar bhu-taapmaana par bhi padataa he. satahi sagariya dhaaraon kaa gyaan pot-parivahan par hone vaale vyay ko kaafii had tak niyantrit karne main madad kar saktaa he. inke kaaran hi eendhan kii khapat par bada prabhaav padataa he, jo vyay or yatra samay main kaaphi kami laata he. puraane samay main to sagariya dhaaraon va vaayu disha gyaan or bhi mahatvapurn hua karata tha. iskaa ek acha udhaaharan agulhas dhara he, jisane kaaran portugali navikon va anveshakon ko kaafii samay tak bharat aane se roke rakhaa. aaj bhi sansar bhar ke nauvahan pratiyogi sagariya dhaaraon kaa laabh uthaate hai. mahasagariya dharaaen sagariya jivan ke liye bhi mahatvapurn hoti hai. iskaa ek udhaaharan il machhlee he. 
 sagariya dhaaraon kaa gyaan sagariya karkat ke adhyayan main bhi sahaayak hota he. iskaa ulat bhi satya he. ye dharaaen vishvaparyant taapmaana nishchit karne main bhi mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaati hai. jo dharaaen uttari andhamhasaagar kaa ushn jal uttar-pashchimi europe tak laati hai, vahaan ke tateey kshetron main barf jamane nahin deteen. is kaaran vahaan ke pattanon main jalpoton kii awajahi baadhit nahin hoti. 
 haal hi main vaigyanikon ne dakshini mahaasaagar ke hind mahaasaagar ke kshetra main ek shaktishaalee jal pravaah ki khoj kii he. ye us tantrajaal kaa ek mahatvapurn bhaag he jo jalwaayu parivartanon ko prabhavit karata he. is sagariya pravaah kii maatra lagbhag chalis amejan nadiyon ke jal kii maatra ke baraabar he. yah sthaan australia kii rajdhani, perth se 4200 kilometre dakshin-pashchim main sthit he. unke anusaar mahaasaagar kii sataha se teen kilometre kii gaharaai par upasthit yah jal pravaah un mahasagariya dhaaraon ke vaishvik jal main ek mahatvapurn path he jo jalwaayu parivartanon ko prabhavit karte hai.
