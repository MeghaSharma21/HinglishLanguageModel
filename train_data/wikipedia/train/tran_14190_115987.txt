 vyinchan dakshinpurvi asia ke laos desh kii rajdhani or sabase bada shahar he. yah desh ke pashchimottaree bhaag main mikong nadi ke kinare par sthit he jo is sthaan par laos kii thailand ke saath antarrashtriya seemaa bhi nirdhaarit karti he.
 "vyinchan" kaa naam buddha dharm kii dharmik bhashaa pali se utpann hua he, jo ek hind-arya bhashaa thi. isamen "vyin" kaa arth lao bhashaa main "nagari" hota he jabaki "chan" kaa arth sanskrit ke "chandan" shabd se ubhara mana jaataa he, arthath "vyinchan" kaa arth "chandan kii nagari" tha. pracheen lao shila-abhilekh bhi is baat kii pushti karte hai. aadhunik kaal main kai lao logon main yah bhi manyata he ki "chan" vastav main sanskrit ke "chandra" shabd se upja tha, yani shahar ke naam kaa arth "chaand kii nagari" he. yah isliye hua he kyonki sanskrit ke "chandra" va "chandan" donon kaa aadhunik lao bhashaa main sankshipti uccharan "chan" hi he. lekin is nagar ko pracheen thai bhashaa sroton ne "เวียงจันทน์" or cambodia ke khmer bhashaa sroton ne "វៀងចន្ទន៍" likhaa he, jin donon kaa arth "chandan" hota he, isliye "chandan kii nagari" hi shaayad sahi arth tha.
 angrezi main shahar ko "Vientiane" likhaa jaataa he. laos par 19veen or 20veen shatabdiyon main francisi upniveshi raaj tha or roman lipi main yahan ke sthaneey naamon ko francisi bhashaa main likhane-padhane main aasaani rakhane ke liye likhaa jane laga. udhaaharan ke liye francisi main 'c' kii dhwani kathin he or is karanvash uske sthaan par 't' likhaa jane laga.
