 
 jyotiraditya madhavrao sindhiya bharat sarkaar kii pandrahavin loksabhaa ke mantrimandal main vaanijya evam udyog rajyamantri he. shri sindhiya loksabhaa kii madhya pradesh sthit guna sansadeey seat kaa pratinidhitiv karte hai. jyotiraditya bhartiya rashtriya congress se sambandh rakhte hai.
 jyotiraditya sindhiya manmohan singh ke sarkaar main kendriya mantri rahe hai yah guna shahar se congress ke vijayi ummidavaar rahe huun. inke pita swa.shri madhavrao sindhiya ji bhi guna se congress ke vijayi ummidavaar rahe the.
