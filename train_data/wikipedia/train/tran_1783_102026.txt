 
 yah vishalkay buddha pratimaa buddha tirth or paryaton margon main se ek bodhgaya, bihar main he. yah buddha murti dhyaan mudra main 25 meter unchi khuli hawa main ek camel par virajman he. yah buddha pratimaa puraa karne ke liye 12,000 rajmistreon ko saat saal lag gaye the. yah pratimaa balua pathar block or laal grenite kaa ek mishran he. yah sambhawatah sabase bada bharat main banaaya gaya or 14 wein dalai lamas ne 18 november 1989 ko pavitra kiya gaya tha. buddha murti ke liye neenv pathar 1982 main rakhaa gaya tha.
