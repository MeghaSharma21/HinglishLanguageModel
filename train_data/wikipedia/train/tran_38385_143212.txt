 khost afghanistan kaa ek praant he jo us desh ke dakshin-purv main sthit he. is praant kaa kshetrafal 4,152 varg kimi he or isaki aabaadi san 2009 main lagbhag 6.4 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. is praant kii rajdhani khost shahar he. is praant main zyaadaatar log pashto bolne vaale pashtun hai. isaki dakshini or purvi seemaa pakistan se lagati he.
 'khost' shabd main 'kh' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'kha' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'karaab' or 'kareed' ke 'kh' se miltaa he.
