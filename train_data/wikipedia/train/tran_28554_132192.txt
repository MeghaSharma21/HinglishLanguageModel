 rajendra sethu/ mokama pul - lagbhag do kilometre lanbaa yah pul ganga nadi par bana bihar kaa rail-sah-sadak pul he, jo uttar bihar ko dakshin bihar se jodta he.
 pul kaa sthaan mokshagundam vishweshwarayya ke kaam par aadhaarit tha, jo us samay 90 varsh se adhik purana tha.
 patna jile ke hathidah ke paas sadak-sah-rail pul kaa udghaatan 1 9 5 9 main bharat ke pradhan mantri jawaharlal nehru or bihar ke pehle mukhyamantri dow shrikrishna singh ne kiya tha. pul kaa nirmaan brawwite, bern and jessop construction company dwaara kiya gaya tha. yah lagbhag 2 kilometre lanbaa he or isamen do len vaali sadak or ek line railway trek he.
 rajendra sethu ke liye ek nae samaantar railway pul kaa nirmaan 12 march 2016 ko pradhan mantri narendra modi ne kiya tha. nayaa 1.9 kimi railway pul farvari 2021 tak parichaalit hone vaala he. nae pul ke nirmaan ke liye anubandh aavantit kiya gaya tha indian railway construction company limited .
