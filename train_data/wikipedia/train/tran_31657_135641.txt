 tumhaari sulu niabandh mumbai main raatri radio jocky kii bhoomika nibhaati najar aegi. film main vidya baalan ke pati ke rup main bollywood abhinayta maanav kaul kaa kirdaar nibhaate najar aaenge. iske alaava is film main neha dhoopiya vidya ke bos ke rup main, or prasiddh aaraje, maaliska, ke rup main albali anjali kaa kirdaar nibhaati najar aaengi. film tumhaari sulu duniya bhar main 24 november, 2017 ko relies hone kii sambhaavna he. 
 film tumhaari sulu yuvaaon or pariwaron ke liye ek mjedaar tukdaa he. film main vidya baalan niabandh, ek sundar, utsaahi or khush-bhaagyashaalee mumbai kii rahane vaali gruhinee kii bhoomika nibhaati najar aati hai, jiskaa niyamit jivan badalata he jab vah apratyaashit rup se ek agrani radio stesan par raatri ke liye aaraje main naukari mil jaati he bahut khush hoti he.
