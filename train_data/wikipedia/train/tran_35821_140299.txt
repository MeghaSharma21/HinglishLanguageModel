 dragon fiter ek american-balgerian 2003 Syfy film ke liye tivi he.
 baad ek mrit dragon vahaan lagbhag 1000 saal ke avshesh kii khoj kii gai he, vaigyanikon ke ek dal ke liye apane diene ke namoonay praapt karne ke liye tay clon kar diya gaya he. anubhav ke rup main kii yojana banaai janaa nahin tha or bhrun vikaas teji se teji se badhaane ke liye apane sankayon kaa puraa adhikaar main bhayanak praani ko jivan dene main dekhtaa he.
 is film main zyaadaatar nakaaratmak samikshaayen praapt hua he.
