 
 haraa gandhak bacteria yaa chlorobiasi avikalpi avayujeevi prakaashahari bacteria kaa ek jeevvaigyanik kul he. ignavibacteriasi ke saath sammilit hokar yah kul chlorobi naamak jeevvaigyanik sangh paribhaashit karte hai.
 hare gandhak bacteria agatisheel or anoxijani prakashasanshleshan main sakasham swaposhi hote hai. is kul main keval ek hi jati, chloroherpetone thalesium hi gatisheel he. jahaan paudhe prakashasanshleshan main oxigen aayan ko electron daata ke rup main prayog karte hai, vahaan hare gandhak bacteria gandhak aayan kaa prayog karte hai. yah aksar gahare samudri kshetron main milte hai jahaan suraj kaa prakaash kam maatra main pahunchataa he.
