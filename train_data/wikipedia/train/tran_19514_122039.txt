 manorama hindi filmon kii ek lokapriy abhinetri thim. unhone kai filmon main khalanayika or hasya charitron ko saakaar kiya tha. unki kuch charchit filmon main sita or gita, ek phool do mali, do kaliyaan, kaaravaan aadi thim. unki antim film deepa mehta kii 'water' thi, jisamen unhone vidhwaa ashram kii mukhiya kii bhoomika nibhaai thi. lahore main apana filmi kariyar shuru karne vaali manorama ne raja naksar se nikaah kiya tha. donon vibhaajan ke baad bharat aa gaye the, jahaan raja nirmaataa ban gaye. shuukravaar 15 farvari 2008 ko bombai main sankshipt beemaari ke baad ek nijee aspataal main nidhan unka nidhan ho gaya. ve 81 varsh kii thim.
