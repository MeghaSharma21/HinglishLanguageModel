 ferozepur jila bharat ganraajya ke uttar-pashchim main sthit punjab rajya ke bees jilon main se ek he. ferozepur jila 5,305kilometre ke ek kshetra shaamil hai. fazilka jile ke alaava ferozepur jile ke vibhaajan se pehle, yah 11,142 kilometre ke ek kshetra shaamil the.ferozepur jile kii rajdhani ferozepur shahar he. yah das daravaajo ke andar sthit he - amritsari get, vansi get, makhu get, zeera get, bagdi get, mori get, dilli get, maghani get, multani get or kasuri get.
 2011 janaganana ke anusaar avibhajit ferozepur jile 2,026,831 kii aabaadi thi. yah bharat main 230th kii ek ranking detaa he . jile ke 380 ke ek jansankhya ghanatv he-nivasee prati varg kilometre . iske dashak 2001 se adhik jansankhya vruddhi dar-2011 16.08% thi. ferozepur har 1000 purushon ke liye 893 mahilaon ke ek sex anupaat he, or 69.8% kii sakshartaa dar.
