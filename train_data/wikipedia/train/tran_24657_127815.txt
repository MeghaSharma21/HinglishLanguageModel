 mir osman ali khan kaa mazebara jo is masjid main sthit he.
 judi masjid hyderabad, bharat main sthit ek masjid he. yah masjid king kothi mahal parisar ke baayein taraf se bashir baag kshetra kii ore jane vaali sadak ke bich se oonchaai par jane vaali ek gali main he. 
 isi masjid main hyderabad ke antim nizaam shaasha mir osman ali khan ko dafan kiya gaya tha. yaheen par raaj parivaar ke kai or sadsy bhi dafan kiye gaye the. 
 is masjid se lagakar nizaam shasankaal main sathapit dharmik maamalon ke nirdeshak or auqaaf kamity ke karyaalay aaj bhi maujuud hai.
