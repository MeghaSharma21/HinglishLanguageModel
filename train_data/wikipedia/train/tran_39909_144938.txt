 gramya main sumitranandan pant kii san 1939 se 1940 ke bich likhi gai kavitaaon kaa sangrah he. inke vishay main ve gramya kii bhoomika main likhte hai--
 gramya main meri yugavani ke baad kii rachanaayen sangrahit hai. inamen pathakon ko graminon ke prati keval bauddhik sahanubhuti hi mil sakati he. gram jivan main milakar, uske bheetar se, ye avashya nahin likhi gai hai. graamon kii vartmaan dasha main vaisaa karnaa keval prati-kriyaatmak sahitya ko janm dena hota. ‘yug’, ‘samskruti’ aadi shabd in rachanaaon main vartmaan or bhavishya donon ke liye prayukt hue hai, jise samajhane main pathakon ko kathinaayee nahin hogi; gramya kii pahili kavita ‘swapn pat’ se yah baat spasht ho jaati he. ‘baapu’ or ‘mahatma ji ke prati’, ‘charkha geet’ or ‘sutaradhar’ jaisi kuch kavitaaon main bahari drushti se ek vichaar-vaishamy jaan padataa he, par yadi ham ‘aaj’ or ‘kal’ donon ko dekhenge to vah virodh nahin rahegaa. ant main meraa nivedan he ki gramya main graamy dosha kaa hona atyant swaabhaavik he, sahruday pathak usamen vichlit na hon.
