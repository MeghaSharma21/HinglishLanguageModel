 erik arikson ek america vikaas manovaigyanik evam manovishleshak the jinka janm germany main hua tha. ve apane manosamaajik vikaas siddhanth‎ ke liye jane jate hai. 
 15 june 1902 main paidaa hua tha or vah ek german mul ke america vikaas manovaigyanik or manovishleshak manushya ke manosamaajik vikaas par unke siddhant ke liye janaa jaataa tha. unhone vaakyansh pahchaan ke sankat ko naam dene ke liye sabase prasiddh saktaa he. unke putr, kaai. tii. arikson, ek prakhyat america samajashastri he.
 haalanki arikson bhi ek snaatak kii degree kami rah gai thi, vah is tarah ke harvard or yel ke rup main pramukh sansthaanon main professor ke rup main kaary kiya. general manovigyaan surveykshan kii samiksha, 2002 main prakashit, 20 veen sadi ke 12 wein sabase uddhrut manochikitsak ke rup main arikson sthaan par raheen.
 arikson kii maa karla ebreehmsan, copenhegan, denmark main ek pramukh yahudee parivaar se aayaa he. vah yahudee share dalal valdemar isidor salomoncen se shaadi kii thi, lekin kai maheenon ke samay main erik kalpana kii thi ke liye uske paas se bichad gaya tha. little sivaay iske ki vah denmark ke ek naastik vyakti tha erik ke jaivik pita ke bare main janaa jaataa he. uski garbhavastha kii khoj par, karla francfort, germany, jahaan erik 15 june, 1902 ko paidaa hua tha or sarnem salomoncen diya gaya tha ke liye bhaag gaye.
 pahchaan ke vikaas ke liye apane swayan ke jivan main ke rup main acchhee tarah se apane siddhant main arikson kii sabase badi chintaaon main se ek rahaa he lagata he. ek puraane vayask ke rup main, vah apane kishor "pahchaan bhram" apane europea dinon main ke bare main likhaa tha. "meri pahchaan bhram," unhone likhaa tha par samay par tha, "neurosis or kishor maansikta ke bich kii seemaa rekha." arikson kii beti likhata he ki uske pita ke 'asli mano pahchaan "sthapit nahin kiya gaya tha jab tak ki vah" ke naam ke saath apane sautele pita ke upnaam kii jagah apane hi avishkaar .
 prayek charan ke anukool parinaam kabhi kabhi "gun," arikson ke kaam ke sandarbh main prayukt shabd ke rup main janaa jaataa he ke rup main yah chikitsa ke liye lagu kiya jaataa he, jiskaa arth he 'shakti. " arikson ke shodh se pataa chalata he ki prayek vyakti ko siikhnaa chahie ek duusare ke saath tanaav main prayek vishisht jivan-charan chunauti ke donon charam simaaon dhaaran karne ke liye kaise, tanaav yaa anya ke ek chor nahin khaarij. keval jab ek jivan-charan chunauti main donon charam simaaon ko samajha or sweekaar kiye jate hai donon ke rup main aavashyak or upyogi he, kar sakate hai ki manch kii sataha ke liye ishtatam punya. is prakaar, 'vishwaas' or 'galat vishwaas' donon ko samajha or yathartwaadee 'aashaa' pehle charan main ek vyavhaarya samaadhaan ke rup main ubharne ke liye aadesh main sweekaar kiye jate hai, kiya janaa chahie. isi tarah, 'eemaandaaree' or 'niraashaa' donon ko samajha or karrwai 'gyaan' aakhri charan main ek vyavhaarya samaadhaan ke rup main ubharne ke liye aadesh main gale laga liya, kiya janaa chahie.
 arikson jivan charan punya, aath charnon main jo ve hasil kiya ja saktaa he ke kram main, kar rahe hai:
 1) aashaa, buniyadi trust banaam buniyadi avishvaas-is charan main bachapan kii avadhi, umr ke 0-1 varsh he, jo jivan kaa sabase moulik charan main he shaamil hai. bachche buniyadi vishwaas yaa avishvaas buniyadi viksit chaahe poshan kaa keval ek maamla nahin he. yah bahuaiami he or majboot saamaajik ghatak hai.
 2)marji, swaittata banaam bachapan ke aasapaas 1-3 varsh shame-cover. swaittata banaam shame kii baat he or shaq kii avadharana kaa parichay. bachche ko apane yaa uski swatantraaa kii shuruyaat kii khoj karne ke liye shuru hota he, or mata pita ke buniyadi kaary kar rahi ke bachche kii bhaavana kii suvidha chahie "sab khud / khud ko."
 3)prayojan, pahal banaam aparaadh-purvaskuli / 3-6 saal. bachche kii kshamta he yaa is tarah use yaa khud poshaak ke rup main apane dam par baaten karte hai, he naa? agar "doshi" apane yaa apane vikalp banane ke bare main, bachche ko acchhee tarah se kaary nahin karenge.
 4) kshamta, udyog banaam hinata-skool umr / 6-11 saal. bal swayan ke laayak dusron kii tulana main. bal vyaktigat kshamtaaon ke anya bacchon ke saapeksh main pramukh asamantaaon ko pahchaan sakate hai.
 5) fidelity, pahchaan banaam bhoomika bhram kishor / 12-18 saal. swayan kaa sawaal uthaaya. main koun hoon, main kaise main feet ho? kahan main jivan main ja rahaa hoon? arikson kaa maananaa he ki agar mata pita bachche kaa pataa lagane ke liye anumati dete hai, ve unki khud kii pahchaan samaapt hoga.
 6) pyaar, antarangta banaam algaav-yah vayask vikaas kaa pehla charan he. is vikaas aamtaur par yuva vayaskta, jo 18 35. dating karne kii umr ke bich he ke dauraan hota he, vivaah, parivaar or dosti ko apane jivan main charan ke dauraan mahatvapurn hai. safaltaapurvak anya logon ke saath prem sambandhon ke gathan karke, vyaktiyon pyaar or atmiyata kaa anubhav karne main sakasham hai. jo log asafal sthaayee sambandhon se alag-thalag mahasoos karte hai or akele may ke liye farm.
 7) care, jeneretiviti banaam tehraav-vayaskta ke duusare charan main 35-64 kii umr ke bich hota he. is samay ke dauraan logon ko saamaanya rup se unke jivan main based hai or jaanate hai ki kya unke liye mahatvapurn he kar rahe hai.
 8)buddhi, ahankaar akhandta banaam niraashaa-is charan main 65 or par ke aayu varg ko prabhavit karata he. is samay ke dauraan ek vyakti ko apane jivan main antim adhyay tak pahunch gaya he or sevanivritti ke kareeb pahunch rahaa he yaa pehle se hi jagah le lee he.
