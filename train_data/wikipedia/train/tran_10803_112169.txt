 
 vitaciye sapushpak dwibijapatri vanaspatiyon kaa ek jeevvaigyanik kul he, jisamen 14 vansh or anumaanit 910 jatiyaan aati hai, jinamein angoorbel shaamil he. kul kaa naam vitis vansh se utpann hua he. san 2009 se vitaciye kul ko akele apane alag vitales jeevvaigyanik gana main shreneekrut kara gaya he.
 vitaciye se milti-julati akruti rakhane vaale indovitis chitaleyae naamak jati ke bich madhya bharat main dakkan pathaar kshetra main mili hai. yah 6.6 karod varsh puraane jeevashm criteshas kalp ke ant main paae gaye hai or angoor kul ke sabase puraane gyaat jivashm hai. aadhunik vitis vansh kii jatiyon kii bhaanti inamen bhi har fal main 4 se 6 beej hai.
