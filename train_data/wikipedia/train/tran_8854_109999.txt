 pushpanathan pillai, kottayam pushpnath naam se charchit, malayalam bhashaa ke upanyaaskaar the. unhone malayalam sahitya main jasusi evam daraavne kahaniyon ke kshetra main vishesh yogadan diya he or 100 se adhik jasusi or daraavne upanyaas likhe hai. unke anek upanyaason kaa tamil, telugu or kannada bhashaa main anuwaad bhi hua he. unke kuch upanyaason par malayalam filmein bhi bani hai. unhen mukhya rup se isliye bhi janaa jaataa he kyonki unhone bram stokar ke 'drakula' kaa anuwaad malayalam bhashaa main kiya tha. apane jivan main ek shikshak rahe pushpnath us samay kaaphi charchaa main rahe he jab unhone apana pehla upanyaas 'chuvanna manushyaan' likhaa tha.
