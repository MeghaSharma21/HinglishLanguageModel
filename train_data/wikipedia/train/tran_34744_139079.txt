 swatantraaa andolan main shrimati sucheta kriplani ke yogadan ko bhi hameshaa yaad kiya jayega. 1908 main janmi sucheta ji kii shiksha lahore or dilli main hui thi. aazaadee ke andolan main bhaag lene ke liye unhen jail kii sajaa hui. 1946 main vah samvidhan sabha kii sadsy chuni gai. 1958 se 1960 tak vah bhartiya rashtriya congress kii mahasachiv thi. 1963 se 1967 tak vah uttar pradesh kii mukhyamantri raheen. 1 disambar 1974 ko unka nidhan ho gaya. apane shok sandesh main shrimati indira gandhi ne kaha ki "sucheta ji aise durlabh saahas or charitra kii mahila thim, jinase bhartiya mahilaon ko sammaan miltaa he."
 sucheta kriplani desh kii pehali mahila mukhya mantri thim. ye bantwaare kii traasadee main mahatma gandhi ke behad kareeb raheen. sucheta kriplani un chand mahilaon main shaamil hai, jinhone baapu ke kareeb rahakar desh kii aazaadee kii neenv rakhi. vah novakhli yatra main baapu ke saath thim. varsh 1963 main uttar pradesh kii mukhyamantri banane se pehle vah lagaataar do baar loksabhaa ke liye chuni gai. sucheta dil kii komal to thim, lekin prashasnic faisle lete samay vah dil kii nahin, dimag kii sunati thim. unke mukhyamantritv kaal main rajya ke karmchaariyon ne lagaataar 62 dinon tak hadtaal jari rakhi, lekin vah karmchaaree netaaon se sulah ko tabhi taiyaar hui, jab unke rukh main narmi ai. jabaki sucheta ke pati acharya kriplani khud samaajawaadee the.
