 kishanganj bihar kaa ek jila he. bihar kii rajdhani patna se 425 kilometre uttar-purv main sthit yah jagah pehle krishnakunj ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. bengal, nepal or bangladesh kii seemaa se sata kishanganj pehle purniya jile kaa anumandal tha. bihar sarkaar ne 14 janvari 1990 ko ise purna rup se jila ghoshit kar diya. paryaton kii drushti se yahaan par paryatak khagara mela, nehru shanti park, churli kila jaise jagah gum sakate hai. yahaan se panighat, gangtok, kalingpong, dajrling jaise paryaton sthal bhi kuch hi duuri par sthit he. yahaan kaa mukhyalay kishanganj he.
