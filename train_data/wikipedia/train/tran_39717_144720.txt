 chittaranjan shetty kannada or tulu bhashaa ke prasiddh sahityakar the. unhe kai rajya-str ke sammaanon se puraskrit kiya gaya tha.
 shetty kaa sabase prasiddh naatak 'ponnu mann-da bombai' tha jise unhone 1973 main tulu main likhaa tha. unhen is baat kaa shrey jaataa he ki unhone kambala par pehla vistrut niabandh 1983 likhaa jo tateey jilon main prachlit bhainson se jude khel par aadhaarit he or jiskaa hawala vartmaan yug main bhi diya jaataa he. 2006 main 'nir' naam se ek naatak likhaa. 'binnedi' ke naam se unhone lok-geeton par ek pustak unhone usee saal likhi thi.
 1990 main shetty ne kannada upanyaas alidhulidhavaru likhaa jo lokapriy hua. unka upanyaas teh bhoomi kii uttaradhikaar par aadhaarit thi. unhone 2005 main 'kudi' naamak kannada upanyaas likhi.
 shetty ne kannada or tulu - donon bhashaaon main apni kavitaayen likhi.
