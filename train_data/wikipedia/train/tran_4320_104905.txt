 sajaad gani lone bharat ke jammu or kashmir praant ke ek raajnneetijna hai. ve piples konference party ke sansthaapak evam adhyaksh hai tathaa handwara seat se vidhayak hai. unke pita abdul gani lone bade kashmiri netaa the jinaki 21 may 2002 ko srinagar main hatya kar di gai thi. 
 pita kii hatya ke pashchaat sajaad ne hurriyat se alag hokar piples konference party banaai jiske ve vartmaan adhyaksh hai. 2009 main ve swayan or 2014 main inke ummidavaar loksabhaa chunaav lade kintu haar gaye.
 pakistan ke samarthak swatantraaa jexlef netaa amanulla khan kii putree asamaa khan se sajaad lone kaa vivaah hua. unke do bete hai.
  2014 ke jammu kashmir vidhansabha chunaavon se purv sajaad lone ne pradhanmantri narendra modi se mulaakaat kii jisase bhartiya janata party ke saath inke gathbandhan kii atakalein lagani shuru ho gai. pipuls conference ne is vidhansabha chunaav main kupwada-handwara kii 12 siton par ummidavaar khadey kiye jinamein se do vijayi hue.
