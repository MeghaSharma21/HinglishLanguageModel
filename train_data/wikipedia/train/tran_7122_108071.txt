 tapan sinha bangla chalchitr evam hindi chalchitr ke prasiddh nirdeshak the. inhen 2006 kaa dada sahib phalke puraskaar bhi mila tha. tapan sinha kii filmein bharat ke alaava berlin, venice, landan, masco jaise antarrashtriya film samarohon main bhi saraahi gai thim.
 inhone bhautikee vishay main snatakottar kiya tha. ye sabase adhik gurudev rabindranath tagore ke kaaryo se prabhavit the. tapan sinha ne bangla film abhinetri arundhati devi se vivaah kiya tha. inke putr anindy sinha bhartiya vaigyanik hai. tapan ji apane jivan kii sandhya main hriday rog se peedit ho gayi the or antatah 15 janvari, 2009 ko paralok sidhaar gayi. inaki patni kii mrityu 1990 main hi ho chuki thi. sagina mahato or safed hathi jaisi ullekhneey filmein banane vaale sinha vibhinn shreniyon main abhi tak 19 rashtriya puraskaron se sammanit kiye ja chuke hai. swatantraaa kii 60veen jayanti par bharat sarkaar ne unhen film jagat main adwitiya yogadan ke liye award for life time achievement se sammanit kisia tha. tapan ji kii pehali film uphaar thi jo 1955 main release hui thi. 1956 me release hui film kabuliwala duusari film thi. iske alaava ek doctor kii maut, sagina or aadmi or aurat inaki behtareen filmon main gini jaati hain. inhone bawarchi jaisi kai filmon kii kahani bhi likhi he.
