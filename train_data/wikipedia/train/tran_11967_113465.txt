 camel kapur ek bhartiya bollywood abhinayta the jinhone lagbhag 600 hindi, punjabi or gujarati filmon me kaam kiya tha.
 unka janm 1920 main lahore, punjab main hua. unhone lahore ke hi diavi college se shiksha praapt kii. ve prithviraj kapur ke chachere bhai or goldi bahal ke naanaa the.
 unhone apane safar kii shuruyaat 1940-50 ke daur main nayak ke rup main kii thi. unki pehali film "dur chalem" thi jo 1946 me pradarshit hui. saath ke dashak se unhone khalanaayak kii bhoomika kerani aarambh kii, inamen se kuch lokapriy kirdaar paaqiza main nawab jafar ali khan, don main naarang or mard main general diar rahen.
