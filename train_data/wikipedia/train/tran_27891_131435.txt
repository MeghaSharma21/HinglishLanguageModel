 styendra nath tegore kaa janm 1 june san 1842, main kolkata main hua. ye gurudev rabindra nath tegore ji ke bade bhai the. inhe bharat ke pratham prashasnic adhikari banane kaa gorav praapt he. san 1864 main ye civil sevak ke rup main niyukt kiye gayi. inaki niyukti bombay praant main hui. inkaa nidhan san 1923 main kolkata main hua.
