 samudr vigyaan main jair kisi sagariya yaa mahasagariya kshetra main ghurnan karne vaale, yani ek kshetra main ghoomane vaale, jal pravaah ko kehete hai. jaairon main pani ek hi bade kshetra main gol-gol ghumta rahata he. inamen aksar saath main baari vaayu pravaah bhi chalata he.
 yah dekha gaya he ki hamaare mahasaagaron ke jair kshetron main samudron main nasamajhi se phenkaa gaya bahut-sa tairne vaala malba jaakar ekatrit ho jaataa he or vahiin ghumta rahata he. kuch samikshakon ke anusaar isane hamaare mahasaagaron main kuch kshetron ko koodaydaan jaisaa bana diya he. prashanth mahaasaagar ke ek kshetra ko ab mahaan prashanth koodaydaan kaha jaataa he.
 jair hamaare grah ke apane ghurnan se hone vaale coriolis prabhaav se banate hai. yah jal va vaayu donon main bhramiltaa se ghurnan kaa prabhaav paidaa kar detaa he.
