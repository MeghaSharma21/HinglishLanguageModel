 poliethlin yaa polithine ) sabase adhik upyog kiya jane vaala plastic he. vartmaan main iskaa vaarshik vaishvik utpaadan 8 karod ton he. iskaa mukhya upyog packaging banane main hota he. polithilin kai prakaar ke hote hai jinamein se adhikansh kaa sutara nH2 hota he. duusare shabdon main kah sakate hai ki polithine ek hi prakaar ke carbonic yogicon kaa mishran hota he jinamein n kaa maan alag-alag hota he.
 paliithilin ek bahulak he. yah ithilin ke anu dwaara banataa he. yah ek bahuupayogi padaarth he.
