 badri mahraaj bharat se fiji le jaye gayi ek kisaan, rajaneta tathaa maanavtaavaadi vyakti the. ve fiji kii vidhaan samiti ke do baar namit sadsy rahe kintu ve fiji ke bharatiyon ke pehali pasand nahin the balki manilal doctor naamak ek vakil ko pasand karte the. ve ek siddhantavaadi vyakti the jinhone fiji ke bharatiyon par 'pole tex' lagaaye jane ko anuchit maanate hue iske virodhswaroop vidhaan samiti kii sadsyta se tyaagptr de diya. ve kuch maamalon main apane samay se bhi aage the. unhone bhartiya prashasnic vyavastha 'panchayat' ko lagu kiye jane kaa prastaav rakhaa, bal vivaah kaa virodh kiya tathaa shiksha ke prasar par bal diya.
 ve aryasamaji the.
 unka janm 1868 main bharat ke uttrakhand main badrinaath ke nikat bamoli gaanv main hua tha. ve san 1889 main shramik ke rup main fiji laaye gayi the. apane parishram ke bal par ve ek safal kisaan ban gayi.
