 rashtrasant tukdoji maharaj nagpur vishvavidhyalay, maharashtra ke nagpur main sthit ek saarvajanik vishvavidhyalay he jiskaa naam pehle 'nagpur vishvavidhyalay' tha. isaki sthapana 4 agast, 1923 ko hui thi. yah bharat ke sabase puraane vishvavidhyalayon main se ek he. iskaa naam tukdoji maharaj ke naam par rakhaa gaya he. is vishvavidhyalay kaa mukhya lakshya sanskrit, marathi, hindi, urdu aadi bhashaaon tathaa ayurvigyaan, vigyaan, manviki, vaanijya evam ejiniyari aadi kii shiksha dena he. san 1947 se hi yah vishvavidhyalay medical kii degree pradaan kar rahaa he. 1 may 1983 ko is vishvavidhyalay ko vibhajit karke iske kuch sansadhano dwaara amravati vishvavidhyalay banaaya gaya tha.
