 detective byomakesh bakshi! bhartiya hindi bollywood film he, jiskaa nirdesan dibaakar benergy ne kiya he.
 varsh 1942 ke samay jab bharat aaazaad nahin hua tha, tab byomakesh kalakatta main the or unke sahpaathee ajit ke pita or rasayan vigyaani bhuwan benergy lagbhag do maheenon se laapata the. byomakesh ko lagata he kii unki htya hui he or apradhi ne uski laash ko kisi jagah chupa diya he jisase sabhi ko lage kii ve laapata hai.
 byomakesh, ajit ke pita ko dhundne se inkaar kar detaa he or kahata he kii uske pita dodagi ke vyapaar main mili hue he. isse ajit ko krodh aa jaataa he or vah byomakesh ko thappad maar detaa he. usee din byomakesh kii premika lila bhi uske paas aati he or bataati he kii vah kisi ore se shaadi kar rahi he.
 byomakesh ajit ke paas jaataa he or vah uske pita ko khojane ke liye sahamat ho jaataa he. vah bhuwan ke factory aadi main use khojane ke liye suraag dhundne lagata he. baad main use pataa chalata he kii bhuwan ne kuch aisa avishkaar kiya tha. jo vah galat haathon se bachaana chaahate the. jaise hi yah prakaran hal ho jaataa he to vah satyavati ko shaadi ke liye poochta he or vah maan jaati he.
