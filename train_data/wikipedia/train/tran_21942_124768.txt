 muharram kaa shok shiya or sunni musalmaan donon se jude anushthanon kaa ek dharmik kaary he.. islamic calendar ke pehle maheene muharram main smarak aata he.yah aayojan karbala kii ladaai kii saalagirah he, jab imam hossain ebn ali ko parivaar sahit duusare umayyaad khalif duvaara karbala kii ladaai main shahid kar diya tha. is ghatana ke dauraan is ghatana kii yaad vaarshik shok kaa, aashura ke din ke rup main, shiya sampradayik pahchaan ko paribhaashit karata he..
