 uzen gledstone o' nil  america naatakkaar the. 1936 i0 main sahitya main nobel puraskaar vijeta.
 uzen o' nil kaa janm 16 oktober, 1888 ko neuyork ke barat house main hua tha, jo us jamaane main ek paariwarik hotel tha. inkaa puraa naam uzen gledstone o' nil tha. inke pita james o' nil un dinon ke prasiddh abhinaytaon main se ek the. 7 varsh tak to balak o' nil apane pita ke saath unke abhinay ke silsile main jahaan-tahaan ghoomte rahe. garmi main inke mata-pita new landan main rahete the.
 o' nil kii padhai princeton vishvavidhyalay se chal rahi thi. vahaan inhen na to achche ank mili or na hi shrenee. atah padhai bich main hi chhod deni padi. 1907 main hi nil kii shiksha samaapt ho gayi. 1909 main sone kii khoj main dakshin america ke kshetron main bhatakate rahe or ant main vahaan se laut kar aaye to ek mallaah ke rup main bhartee hokar southempton gayi. 24 disambar 1912 ko un par kshay rog kaa akraman hua or vah walingford gellard form senetorium main bhartee kiye gayi. yahi vah samay tha jise o' nil ne apana punarjanm mana he, kyonki isi samay unhen apane samast kaaryon par punarvichaar kaa mauka mila or antat unhone naatakkaar banane kaa nishchay kiya. yahi nishchay unhen srijan ke saath-saath sammaan ke bhi shikhar par le gaya. o' nil kii teen shaadiyaan hui thim, jinamein se ejanad bolton se utpann ladki konaa ne prasiddh abhinayta charlie chaplin se shaadi kii thi.
 o' nil ko naatakon ke liye pulitzer puraskaar bhi mila tha. 1920 isvi main unhen kshitij ke us paar ke liye, 1922 isvi main anna christy ke liye or 1928 isvi main anokhaa vishram ke liye puraskaar mil chuke the. 1936 isvi main inhen sahitya main nobel puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
 ye akele america naatakkaar the jinhen sahitya kaa nobel puraskaar diya gaya.
