 mangist​uu praant madhya asia ke qaazakhstan desh kaa ek praant he. isaki rajdhani caspian sagar ke kinare sthit akatau shahar he, jo qaazakhstan kii sabase badi bandargah bhi he. is praant kii dakshini seemaa turkmenistan ke balqaan praant se or purvi seemaa uzbekistan ke kaaraqalpaustan praant se lagati hai.
 qaazakhstan kaa caspian sagar ke saath lagane vaale tat kaa adhikansh hissaa mangist​uu praant main aata he or is praant kii rajdhani bhi usee sagar par ek bandargah he. is rajya main registan, pahaad, pathaar or caspian ke saath laga maidani ilaaka sabhi shaamil hai. ust-urt pathaar par sthit rajya ke uttari bhaag main sardiyon main bahut thand padati he. praant kaa sabase unchaa sthaan 556 meter unchaa otpan pahaad he jabaki sabase neech;a sthaan caspian sagar ke saath kii caspian droni kaa samudr-tal se 132 meter neeche kaa kshetra he. soviet sangh ke jamaane main yahan zamin se tel niklaa tha or is praant main tel-sambandhi udyog se bahut vikaas hua he.
 1999 kii janaganana main mangist​uu praant ke 50.9% log kazakh or 32.9% roosi jati ke the.
