 sapaldaksh pracheen kaal main rajasthan main janapad tha. rajasthan bharat varsh ke pashchim bhaag main avasthit he jo pracheen kaal se vikhyath rahaa he. tab is pradesh main kai ikaiyaan sammilit thi jo alag-alag naam se sambodhit kii jaati thi. udhaaharan ke liye jaipur rajya kaa uttari bhaag madhyadesh kaa hissaa tha to dakshini bhaag sapaldaksh kehalaata tha. alvar rajya kaa uttari bhaag kurudesh kaa hissaa tha to bharatpur, dholpur, karauli rajya shurasen desh main sammilit the.
