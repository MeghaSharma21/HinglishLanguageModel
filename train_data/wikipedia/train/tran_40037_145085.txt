 ellora yaa ellora ek puratathvik sthal he, jo bharat main aurangabad, maharashtra se 30 kimi kii duuri par sthit he. inhen rashtrakut vansh ke shaskaon dwaara banwaaya gaya tha. apni smarak gufaaon ke liye prasiddh, ellora unesco dwaara ghoshit ek vishwa dharohar sthal he.
 ellora bhartiya pashaan shilp sthapaty kala kaa saar he, yahan 34 "gufaayen" hai jo asal main ek urdhwadhar khadi charnadri parvat kaa ek falak he. isamen hindu, buddha or jain gupha mandir bane hai. ye paanchavin or dasvin shatabdi main bane the. yahan 12 buddha guphaayen, 17 hindu guphaayen or 5 jain guphaayen hai. ye sabhi aas-paas baniin hai or apane nirmaan kaal kii dharmik sauhaard ko darshaati hai.
 ellora ke 34 math or mandir aurangabad ke nikat 2 kimi ke kshetra main faile hai, inhen unchi besalt kii khadi chattaanon kii deevaaron ko kaat kar banaaya gaya hai. durgam pahadiyon vaala ellora 600 se 1000 isvi ke kaal kaa he, yah pracheen bhartiya sabhyataa kaa jivant pradarshan karata he. buddha, hindu or jain dharm ko bhi samarpit pavitra sthaan ellora parisar na keval adwitiya kalatmak srijan or ek takneekee utkrushtataa he, balki yah pracheen bharat ke dhairyavan charitra kii vyakhya bhi karata he.yah unesco kii vishwa viraasat main shaamil he.
