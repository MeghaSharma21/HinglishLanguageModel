 sharasana taan, bas avasar yahi he, ghadi fir or milane kii nahin he.vishikh koi gale ke paar kar de, abhi hi shatru kaa sanhaar kar de. 
 shravan kar vishwaguru kii deshna yah, vijay ke hetu aatur eshna yah, saham uttha jaraa kuch parth kaa man, vinay main hi, magar, bolaa akinchan .
 narochit, kintu, kya yah karm hoga ?malin isse nahin kya dharm hoga ?hanse keshav, vrutha hath thaanta he.abhi tu dharm ko kya jaanata he ?
 kahuun jo, paal usko, dharm he yah .hanan kar shatru kaa, satkarm he yah .kriya ko chhod chintan main fansega, ulat kar kaal tujko hi grasegaa .
 bhala kyon parth kalahar hota ?vrutha kyon chintna kaa bhaar dhota ?sabhi daayitv hari par daal karke, mili jo shishti usko paal karke,
 laga radhey ko shar maarane vah, vipad main shatru ko sanhaarne vah, sharon se bedhane tan ko, badan ko, dikhaane veerta ni:shastr jan ko .
 vishikh sandhaan main arjuna nirat tha, khadaa radhey ni:sambal, virath tha, khadey nirvak sab jan dekhte the, anokhe dharm kaa ran dekhte the.
 nahin jab parth ko dekha sudharte, hriday main dharm kaa tuk dhyaan dharte .samay ke yogya dhiraj ko sanjokar, kaha radhey ne gambheer hokar .
 narochit dharm se kuch kaam to lo !bahut khele, jaraa vishram to lo .fanse rathchakra ko jab tak nikaalun, dhanush dhaaran karun, praharan sanbhaaluun, 
 ruko tab tak, chalaanaa baan phir tum ;haran karnaa, sako to, praan phir tum .nahin arjuna ! sharan main maganta hum, samarthit dharm se ran maganta hum .
 kalakint naam mat apana karo tum, hriday main dhyaan iskaa bhi dharo tum .vijay tan kii ghadi bhar kii damak he, isi sansar tak uski chamak he.
 bhuwan kii jeet mitati he bhuwan main, use kya khojna gir kar patan main ?sharan keval ujagar dharm hoga, sahaara ant main satkarm hoga. 
 upasthit dekh yon nyayarth ari ko, nihara parth ne ho khinn hari ko .magar, bhagwaan kinchit bhi na dole, kupit ho vajra-si yah vaat bole _
 pralapi ! o ujagar dharm vaale !badi nishtha, bade satkarm vaale !mara, anyaay se abhimanyu jis din, kahaan par so rahaa tha dharm us din ?
 halaahal bhim ko jis din padaa tha, kahaan par dharm yah us din dharaa tha ?lagi thi aag jab laksha-bhawan main,hansaa tha dharm hi tab kya bhuwan main ?
 sabha main draupadi kii khinch laake, suyodhan kii use daasi bataa ke, suvama-jati ko aadar diya jo, bahut satkar tum sabane kiya jo, 
 nahin vah or kuch, satkarm hi tha, ujagar, sheelbhooshit dharm hi tha. jue main harkar dhan-dham jis din, hue pandav yati nishkaam jis din,

 bade paapi hue jo taj maanga, kiya anyaay; apana raaj maanga .nahin dharmarth ve kyon haarate hai, adhi hai, shatru ko kyon maarthe hai ?
 hameen dharmarth kya dahate rahenge ?sabhi kuch maun ho sehate rahenge ?ki dage dharm ko bal anya jan bhi ?tajenge krurta-chal anya jan bhi ?
 na di kya yatnaa in kauravon ne ?kiya kya-kya na nirgin kauravon ne ?magar, tere liye sab dharm hi tha, duhit nij mitra kaa, satkarm hi tha. 
 kiye kaa jab upasthit fal hua he, grasit abhishaap se sambal hua he, chalaa he khojane tu dharm ran main, mrisha kilvish bataane anya jan main .
 shithil kar parth ! kinchit bhi na man tu .na dharmadharm main pads bheeru ban tu .kada kar vaksh ko, shar maar isko, chadhaa shaayak turat sanhaar isko .
 hansaa radhey, haa ab der bhi kya ?sushobhan karm main avaser bhi kya ?krupa kuch or dikhalaate nahin kyon ?sudarshan hi uthaate hai nahin kyon ?
 kaha jo aapane, sab kuch sahi he, magar, apni mujhe chintaa nahin he ?suyodhan-hetu hi pachhataa rahaa hum, bina vijayi banaaye ja rahaa hum .
 vrutha he poochna kisne kiya kya, jagat ke dharm ko sambal diya kya !suyodhan tha khada kal tak jahaan par, na hai kya aaj pandav hi vahaan par ?
 unhone koun-sa apdharm chhoda ?kiye se koun kutsit karm chhoda ?ginaaun kya ? swayan sab jaanate hai, jagadguru aapako ham maanate he.
 shikhandi ko banaakar dhaal arjuna, hua gangey kaa jo kaal arjuna, nahin vah or kuch, satkarm hi tha. hare ! kah dijiye, vah dharm hi tha.
