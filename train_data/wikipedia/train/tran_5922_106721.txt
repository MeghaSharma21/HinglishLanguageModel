 payum puli bhartiya tamil film hai, jiskaa nirdesan or lekhan kaa kaary suseenthiran ne kiya he. isamen mukhya bhoomika main vishaal hai, jo ek gupt police afasar kii bhoomika nibhaa rahe hai. isamen kaajal agarwal, samuthirakani, suri or aishwarya datta mukhya kirdaar main shaamil hai. is film kaa nirmaan es madhan or pi madhan ne kiya he. yah film 2 agast 2015 ko pradarshit hui. yah film telugu bhashaa main jayasoorya naam se 4 sitambar 2015 ko pradarshit hogi.
 jeeva main kaary karne ke baad suseenthiran ne vishaal ke saath pandiya nadu ke saath film kii kahani par agast 2014 se shuru kiya. chhayakaar aar madhi ke sthaan par welraj ko jagah mil gai.
 vishaal is film main jaisilan kaa kirdaar nibhaa rahe hai, jo ek gupt police afasar he. vah apane abhinay jivan main is tarah kaa kirdaar teesri baar nibhaa rahe hai. iske baad mahila mukhya kirdaar hetu shruti hasan se baat ho rahi thi. lekin nirmaan karne waalon ne lakshmi menon ko is kirdaar ke liye chunaa. jo isse pehle pandia nadu main bhi mukhya kirdaar nibhaa chuki hai. sitambar 2014 ke madhya vishaal ne is nirnay ke bare main kaha ki is kirdaar ke liye suseenthiran ko ek aisi abhinetri kii aavashyaktaa thi, jise tamil kaa acha khaasaa gyaan ho. menon kii pakad kaa khulaasa april 2015 ke pariksha main hua. usee dauraan lakshmi menon ke sthaan par kaajal agarwal ko chunaa gaya. jo disambar 2014 main mukhya kirdaar ke rup main vishaal ke saath pehali or soosithiran ke saath duusari film kar rahi hai. isse pehle vah soosithiran ke saath naan mahaan allaa main kaary kar chuki hai.
 kaajal kaa kirdaar film main ek aisi ladki kaa he, jise har chiz se dar lagata he. use saamaanya se hone vaale kaary jaise sadak paar karne main bhi dar lagata he.
