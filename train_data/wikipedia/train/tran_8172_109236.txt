 kisi khunti se latake aise bhaar ko lolak kehete hai jo swatantraapurvak aage-peeche jhul saktaa ho. jhula iskaa ek vyaavhaarik udhaaharan he. 
 dolan karata hua lolak kisi ek bindu jitne samay baad punah waapas aa jaataa he use uskaa 'aavartkaal' kehete hai. yadi lolak kaa aayam kam ho to iskaa aavartkaal aayam par nirbhar nahin karata balki keval lolak kii lambaai or gurutvajanit twaran ke sthaneey maan par nirbhar hota he. lolak kaa aavartkaal lolak ke dravyaman par bhi nirbhar nahin karata.
 jahaan L lolak kii lambaai he, tathaa g us sthaan par gurutvajanit twaran kaa maan he.
 is sutara se saaf he ki yadi aayam kam ho to aavartkaal alag-alag ayaamon ke liye samaan hoga. lolak ke is gun ko 'samakalikta' kehete hai. apane isi gun ke kaaran lolak kaa upyog samaymaapan main khub hua.
 kintu yadi aayam bada he to aavartkaal niyat nahin rahata balki aayam badhane par kramshah badhata he. udhaaharan ke liye yadi aayam θ0 = 23° ho to aavartkaal kaa maan sameekaran se praapt maan se lagbhag 1% adhik hoga.
