 jalayaan ke nodak, jinhen anaupchaarik bhashaa main scru bhi kehete hai, aise pankhenuma yantra hote hai jinase samudri jahaazon or naukaaon ke injanon dwaara utpann ghurnan kaa prayog pani ko peeche kii ore phenkakar nauka ko aage kii taraf dhakelne ke liye jaataa he. nodakon kaa aakaar aisa hota he ki jab vah ghoomte hai to unke aage or peeche ke pani ke dabaavon main antar paidaa ho jaataa he jis se pani tezi se pankhe ke peeche khinchaa jaataa he. vaigyanik rup se nodak ke aage or peeche ke dabaav main antar barnauli siddhant dwaara samajha ja saktaa he.
