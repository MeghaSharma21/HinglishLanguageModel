 surat nagar nigam kii kii yon swasthya pariyojna shahar or jile main karyanvit kii ja rahi jiskaa lakshya yon swasthya ke liye ek nodel agency ke rup main bhagidaari ke liye kaam ho. esemasi - pariyojna main yon swasthya pariyojna ke rup main sanderbhit kiya jaataa he. swasthya kshetra ke samooh antarrashtriya vikaas vibhaag, rashtriya aids niyantran sanghathan or pariyojna sanchaalan gujarat rajya main yon swasthya pariyojna ke prabandh ke liye gathit samiti ke madhyam se nidhi pradaan kar rahaa he. pariyojna ko lagu karne ke liye gujarat rajya aids niyantran socity sarkaar kii ore se abhinay ke bich anubandh par hastakshar kiye gaye the.
 surat nagar nigam ek nodel agency ke rup main is pariyojna ke bhagidaar sanghathan kii tarah kaam kar rahaa he. yon swasthya pariyojna ko acchhee tarah kriyanvit karne ke liye kul swasthya sevaaon ko protsaahit kiya gaya he jis main 23 shahari swasthya centre or prasuti-grih or ek medical college kii stathpana shaamil he. is pariyojna ke "astidi dekhbhaal pariyojna" ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he kyunki yah mukhyatah yon swasthya par kendrit he. prayek swasthya centre main ek salaahkaar or kai ghar-ghar pahunchnewale kaaryakarta jude hai.
 surat nagar nigam yon swasthya pariyojna ko lekar kaafii agrasar he. iskaa udhaaharan urban management centre namak sanstha kaa echivi maamalon kaa adhyan he jiske liye bharat ke ahmedabad, chennai, mumbai or surat shaharon ko chunaa gaya.
 vir narmada south gujarat vishvavidhyalay, gujarat rajya aids niyantran socity or surat nagar nigam ne sanyukt rup se 2009 main gandhi smriti bhawan main echivi / aids jaagrukta kaarykram kaa yojit kiya or rashtriya yuva diwas kaa jashn manaaya. is avasar par anya avasaron par aids jagriti kii disha main surat nagar nigam prayaas karata rahaa he.
