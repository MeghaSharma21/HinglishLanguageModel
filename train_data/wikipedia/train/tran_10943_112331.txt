 prakaashiya tantu kaanch yaa plastic se nirmit ek tantu hota he jiske lambaai kii disha main prakaash kaa sancharan ho saktaa he. aajkal inkaa sanchaar main khub prayog ho rahaa he kyonki inaki sahaayta se adhik duuri tak bina sanket ko parivardhit kiye lejaaya ja saktaa he. ye kisi vidyutchumbakeey interference se bhi bahut kam prabhavit hote he.
 vastutah prakaashiya tantu ek belanaakaar dielectric vevegide he jo prakaash ko apni lambaai kii disha main sancharan karaata he. is prakriya main prakaash kaa purna aantarik paravartan hota he.
 prakaashiya tantu ko ant main jab kisi upkaran se jodna hota he to uske liye prakaashiya tantu conector kaa prayog karnaa padataa he. ye conector praayah FC, SC, ST, LC, yaa MTRJ aadi maanak prakaar ke hote hai.
 prakaashiya tantuon ke do sironko paraspar jodne ke liye yaa to conector prayog main laaye jate hai yaa unhen splising prakriya se jodakar ek satat tarang digdarshak bana diya jaataa he. splising kaa kaary do tarah se kiya ja sakat he -
 purna aantarik paravartan
