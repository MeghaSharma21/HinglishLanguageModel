 tapopchaar un prakramon yaa vidhiyon ko kehete hai jo ushma ke aadan-pradaan ke dwaara padaarthon ke bhautik gunon ko badalane ke liye upyog kiye jate hai. kabhi-kabhi inaki sahaayta se padaarth ke rasinic gun bhi badale jate hai. inkaa dhaatukarm main bahut upyog he. kaanch aadi kuch anya padaarthon ke utpaadan main bhi tapopchaar kaa prayog hota he.tapopchaar dwaara vaanchit parinaam paane ke liye padaarth ko praaia: bahut adhik garam karnaa yaa bahut adhik thanda karnaa padataa he. 
 aneling, cese kathorikaran, pressipetesan shashaktikaran, tempring tathaa quenching pramukh tapopchaar hai.
 tapopchaar kii nimna vidhia he:-1. kathorikaran:- kisi bhi mulaayam steel ko ghisne se bachaane ke liye athwa tul ko kisi dhaatu ko kaatane yogya banane ke liye use kisi vishesh tapakram par garm karke thanda karne kii kirya ko kathorikaran kehete he|2.temprigan:- hardening se steel kathor va bhagunr ho jaati he| use dur karne ke liye tempering karte he|3.eneling. 4.narmalisigan 5. kesh hadirgan
