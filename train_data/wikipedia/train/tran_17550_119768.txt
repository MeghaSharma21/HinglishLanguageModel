 suzuki deisetz japan ke buddha sahitya evam darshan ke vishwavikhyath vidwaan the. 1963 main unhen nobel shanti puraskaar ke liye namit kiya gaya tha.
 aapane buddha dharm main prachlit 'dhyaan sampradaay' ko naveen rup pradaan kiya he. japan main yah sampradaay 'jain sampradaay' ke naam se prasiddh he. vaise to japan main jain sampradaay kii sthapana 'yei sai' ne kii, jo karmkand aadi ko hey samajhakar dhyaan evam aatmasanyam ko hi sarvshereshth maanate the, kintu japani darshanik dow sujuki ne jine sampradaay kii is moulik vichaardhaara ko or bhi parimarjeet kar aage badhaayaa. ve maanate the ki darshan or dharm kaa loukik uddeshy bhi he.
 dow sujuki kaa janm kanazava main hua. praarambhik adhyayan ke baad aap san 1892 main tokio vishvavidhyalay se snaatak pariksha uteern kar ucch adhyayan ke liye 1897 main america gaye. vahaan apane adhyayan ke saath-saath buddha dharm evam udaar chini darshan taowad ke anek granthon kaa angreji main anuwaad kiya. san 1909 main japan lautne par sujuki piar vishvavidhyalay main angreji bhashaa ke adhyaaapak niyukt hue. isi ke saath ve tokio vishvavidhyalay main bhi adhyaapan kaary karte rahe. san 1921 ke pashchaat aap otani vishvavidhyalay, kyoto main buddha-darshan-vibhaag ke adhyaksh niyukt kiye gaye.
 san 1936 main dow sujuki kii praadhyaapak kii haisiyat se america or britain gaye or unhone japani samskruti evam jine darshan par vidvatapurna bhaashan die. iske falswaroop aapako japan sarkaar kii ore se 'order ov culture' kaa sammaan pradaan kiya gaya.
 buddha sahitya ke kshetra main dow sujuki ko or bhi sammaan praapt hua, jab unhone jine buddha dharm par 30 sanskarnon kii ek granthmala likhi. isi ke baad aapane ek anya pustak 'zen or japan kii samskruti' japani bhashaa main prakashit kii. iskaa anuwaad angreji, french, german or portugali bhashaa main kiya gaya. is prakaar dow sujuki kii is anupam kriti ko antarrashtriya sammaan praapt hua.
