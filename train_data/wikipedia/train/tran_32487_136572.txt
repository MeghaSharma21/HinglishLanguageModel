 devvaad yaa tatstheshwarawad ke anusaar, satya kii khoj main buddhi pramukh astr or antim adhikaar he. gyaan ke kisi bhaag main bhi buddhi ke adhikaar se bada koi anya adhikaar vidyaman nahin. yah daawa dharm or gyanamimasaa ke kshetron main vishesh rup main vivaad kaa vishay banataa rahaa he.
 isai mat main dharm kii neenv vishwaas par rakhi gai he. jo satya ishwar kii ore se avishkrut hue he, ve maanya hai, chaahe ve buddhi kii pahunch ke baahar hon, uske pratikul bhi hon. 18veen shatee main, england main kuch vichaarkon ne dharm ko daivi avishkaar ke bajaay maanav chintan kii neenv par khadaa karne kaa ytn kiya. aarambh main alokik yaa prakrutiviruddh siddhant unke akraman ke vishay bane. iske baad aisi ghatnaon kii baari ai, jinhen aitihaasik khoj ne asaty bataaya; or ant main kaha gaya ki jis jivanavyavastha ko isiyat adarsh vyavastha ke rup main upasthit karti he, vah sweekruti ke yogya nahin. toland, chabb or borligbrok buddhivaad ke in teenon swaroopon ke pratinidhi tathaa prasarak the.
 gyanamimasaa main buddhivaad or anubhavvaad kaa virodh he. anubhavvaad ke anusaar, manushya kaa man ek kori takhti he, jis par anek prakaar ke bahya prabhaav ankit hote hai. hamaara sara gyaan baahar se praapt hota he. iske vipareet, buddhivaad kahata he ki sara gyaan andar se upajataa he. jo kuch indriyon ke dwaara praapt hota he, use plato ne keval "sanmati" kaa pad diya. buddhivaad ke anusaar ganit satya gyaan kaa namoona he. ganit kii neenv lakshanon or swayansiddh dharanaon par hoti he or ye donon man kii kritiyaan hai. aadhunik kaal main, decort ne nirmal or spasht pratyayon ko satya kii kasuti bataaya. spinoja ne apni vikhyath pustak "niti" ko rekhganit kaa aakaar diya. vah kuch paribhaashaon or swat:siddh dharanaon se aarambh karata he or prayek saadhy ko upyogi upapatti se pramanit karata he.
