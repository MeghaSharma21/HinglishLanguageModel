 2015 carlton mid trikoniya series australia main yojit ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket toornament tha. yah australia, england or bharat ke bich ek tri-raashtra shrinkhalaa thi. australia ne final main england ko 112 ran se haraakar apane 20 waan australian trikoniya shrinkhalaa kaa khitaab jeeta.
 pichley australian tirangi ekadivasiy shrinkhalaa ke vipareet, prayek team ne round-robin charan ke dauraan keval do baar khel liya tha, or final ko teen-teen sarvshereshth ke bajaay ek ekal match ke taur par khelaa gaya tha, 2015 ke cricket vishwa cap ko samyojit karne ke liye, jo shrinkhalaa ke turant baad kaa paalan kiya. cricket vishwa cap ke dauraan farvari 2015 main, bharat ke team ke nideshak ravi shastri ne trikoniya siries kii samay-sarini kii aalochana karte hue kaha ki yah samay or urja kaa "apshisht" tha.
