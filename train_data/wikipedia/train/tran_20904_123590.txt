 an-nafood yaa al-nafood yaa nafood arabi prayadweep main sthit ek bada registan he. iskaa kshetrafal 1,03,000 varg kimi he, yani lagbhag bharat ke bihar rajya se zaraa bada. nafood ek arg he, jisamen jabardast hawaayen achaanak chal padati hai. is vajah se yah kshetra ardhachandra akar ke tilon se bharaa hua he. yahan kii ret kaa rang thodaa laal he.
 nafood main barish saal main ek yaa do baar girti he. kuch nichle kshetron main nachlistaan hai jahaan khajur, jau, sabziyaan or fal ugaae jate hai. aise kuch kshetra hijaaz pahadiyon ke paas sthit hai. nafood ad dahana ke zariye rub al-khaali kshetra se judaa hua he. dahana ek patala or 1,200 kimi lambaa ret ke tilon or kankar-bajri se bharaa kshetra he. nafood ko kabhi-kabhi ret kaa samudr bhi kehete hai.
