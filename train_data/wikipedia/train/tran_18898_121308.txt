 aavalkheda uttar pradesh ke agra jile kaa ek gaanv he jahaan mahaan chintak evam gayatri parivaar ke sansthaapak shriram sharma acharya kaa janm hua tha. yah ab yugtirth ban chukaa he. jahaan samvath 1968 asina krishna tryodashi buudhvaar ko unka praakatay hua. yaheen kii dhul main khele, bade hue. yaheen 15 varsh kii kishor avasthaa main himalayasth rishisatta-gurusatta kaa saakshaatkaar hua. isi ke saath prarambh ho gaya 24-24 laksh ke mahapurshcharan kaa silsilaa. yaheen ve swatantraaa sangram senaanee ke rup ubhare tathaa shriram matt yaa matt ji ke naam se prasiddh hue. unke dwaara khoda kuan or lagaaya gaya neem kaa ped aaj bhi smritiyaan taaja karte hai. 
 yaheen se unki seva-sadhana prarambh hui. shiksha evam gramin swavlamban kii kai gatividhiyaan chalaayin. viraasat main mili prachur bhu-smapda kaa upyog apane or apane parivaar ke liye nahin kiya. ek bhaag se apni mataji kii smriti main daan kunwari inter college kii sthapana karai, shesh raashi baad main gayatri tapobhumi hetu samarpit kar di. 
 san 1979-80 main gayatri shaktipeeth evam rajkeey kanya inter college kaa shubharambh hua, jo aaj kanya mahavidhyalay ban chukaa he. san 1995 main pratham purnahuti samaaroh bhi yahan sampann hua, jisamen lagbhag pachaas lakh logon ne bhaag liya. mata bhagwati devi sharma rajkeey saamudayik chikitsaalay kii sthapana bhi ho chuki he, is avasar par bharat ke pradhanmantri ne ek kirti stambh kaa locarpan kiya. aanvalkheda agra se lagbhag 24 kilometre jalesar road par sthit he or puraane bijlighar se basen uplabdh rahati hai. vahaan pahunchakar desh-videsh ke bhaavnashil sadhakagana usee prakaar bhav vibhor ho uthte hai, jaise chaitanya mahaprabhu vrindavan main pahunchakar aanandit hue the.
