 denis lily australia ke purv cricket khilaadi hai. 1971 se 1984 tak chale apane kabhi-kabhaar vivaadaaspad antarrashtriya kariyar main unhone apane aap ko cricket ke sabase mahaan gendbaajon main se ek kii suchi main shaamil kiya. jeff thomson ke saath unki jodi kaaphi prasiddh he. wicketiper rord marsh ke saath unke paas test maichon main sabase safal gendbaaj-keeper hone kaa khitaab he. 
 denis ne 70 test match khelen jisamen unhone 355 wicket liye . saath hi 63 vanade main unhone 103 wicket liye. vah kisi bhi ek ek divasiy antarrashtriya main 5 wicket lene vaale pehle gendbaj the.
