 remi sen hindi filmon kii ek abhinetri hai.
 remi sen kaa asli naam shubhomitra sen he. unka janm 21 sitambar 1981 ko kolkata, pashchim bengal main hua tha.
 remi kii pehali hindi film hangaamaa thi. uske baad wo baagbaan main chote se role main dikhin. unki kuch safal filmein hai:- dhooma, diwane hue pagal, phir hera pheri or golmaal. big bos 9 main wo hissaa le chuki he. 2017 main unhone bhaajapa kii sadsyta lee.
