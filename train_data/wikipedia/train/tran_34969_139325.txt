 chunki apane mul rup main adhikansh sanket anolog rup main hote hai isaliye aankik sanket prasanskaran ke karne ke purv analog sanketon ko aankik rup main badalanaa padataa he. analog sanketon ko aankik rup pradaan karne ke liye anolog se digital conwarter paripathon kaa upyog kiya jaataa he jo aajkal praayah ai si ke rup main uplabdh hai. isi tarah, chunki prasanskaran ke baad antatah hame anolog rup main hi output chaiye hota he, isaliye aankik sanket ko punah anolog rup main badal diya jaataa he. iske liye aankik se anolog parivartak upyog main laaye jate hai.
