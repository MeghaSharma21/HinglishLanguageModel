 
 saniudiyaar, kanda tehsil main bharat ke uttrakhand rajya ke antargat kumaun mandal ke bageshwar jile kaa ek gaanv he. 
 uttrakhand parivahan kii ek bas saniudiyaar se dilli tak chalati he. yah bas anand vihaar se shaam 5:30-6 baje chalati he, or haldwani, almoda, kausani, bageshwar or kanda hote hue saniudiyaar pahunchati he. iske atirikt kemu prabandhan ne bhi navambar 2016 se is kshetra ko haldwani se jodatii bas seva kaa sanchaalan prarambh kiya he. bas rojaanaa subah saat baje haldwani se chalakar ravatsera, bansapataan or seraghat hote hue saniudiyaar ke havantoli main shaam chaar baje pahunchati he.
