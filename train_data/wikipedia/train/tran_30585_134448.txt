 ratirog yaa yon sanchaarit rog ) rati yaa maithun ke dwaara utpann rogon kaa samuhik naam he. ye ve rog hai jinaki maanvon yaa jaanwaron main yon sampark ke kaaran failane kii atyadhik sambhaavna rahati he. yon sampark main yoni sambhog, mukh-maithun, tathaa guda-maithun aadi sammilit hai. 
 yon sanchaarit rogon ke bare jaankaree main saikadon varshon se he. inamen upadansh, sujak, limphogrenuloma benarium tathaa ratij vranabh, aids pradhan hai.
 purushon me to ratirogon ke lakshan saamanytah dikh jate hai to ve jaagruk ho jate hai ki unke yonparak ang sankramit ho gaye hai. jabaki auraton ke sankraman ke lakshan dikhaai nahin dete jabaki rog lag chukaa hota he.
 es tii di se anya swasthya sambandhi samasaaaayen ho sakati hai. prayek es tii di se alag prakaar kii swasthya sambandhi samasyaayein hoti hai - kul milakar unase greeva parak cancer or anya cancer ho sakate hai jigar ke rog, anurvarakta, garbh sambandhi samasyaayein or anya ksht ho sakate hai. kuch prakaar ke es tii di ech ai ve/aids kii sambhaawanaaon ko badha dete hai.
 es tii di ke lakshanon main nimnlikhit shaamil hai -
  auraton main yoni ke aasapaas khajali or /athwa yoni se srav
  purushon me ling se srav
  sambhog ke samay athwa mutr tyaag ke samay pidaa
  jananendriya ke aasapaas pidavihin laal jakhm
  mulaayam twachaa ke rang vaale masse jananendriya ke aasapaas ho jate hai.
  guda parak sambhog waalon ko guda ke andar or aasapaas pidaa
  asaamaanya chhot ke rog, na samaj aane vaali thakaavat, raat ko pasinaa or vajan kaa ghatana.
 yah pradhanat: sankramak rog he, parantu janmajaat rup main bhi paaya jaataa he. praarambhik avasthaa main yah samanyikrut hota he or baad main sthanikrut or prakeern rup main kisi ang ko aakraant kar saktaa he. rogajanak jeevanu triponima palidam, yaa spirokita palidam he. upadansh ke jeevanu sharir se baahar kuch ghante tak hi jivit rah sakate hai. sharir kii twachaa yaa shleshmal upakalaa main pravisht hone ke baad inaki vruddhi twarit gati se hoti he or ye saare sharir main fail jate hai.
 bharat main videshiyon ke aa jane par yah yah rog adhik failaa, jisase ise firang rog naam mila. america main habshiyon main tathaa bharat main tarai ke kshetra main yah bahut hota he. yudhkaal main sainikon ke madhyam se praaia: yah sankramak rup se failata he. bade bade bandargah tathaa nagaron main, jahaan sansarg ke saadhan sulabh hote hai, upadansh bahut failata he. upadansh kii nimnlikhit avasthaaen hoti hai :
 praathmik upadansh praaia: jananendriyon par prakat hota he. kabhi kabhi gudadwar, jihva, onth or stan tathaa doctor nurse or daantsaazon kii ungliyon par bhi ho jaataa he. iskaa udbhavan kaal saamanyat: 21 din kaa hota he parantu yah 10 se 90 din tak kaa ho saktaa he. praaia: yah itnaa kshtdaayi nahin hua karata ki rogi ise bahut mahatv de. jananendriya par yaa anyatra kahin, jeevanupravesh-sthal par, kadaa, chota vran banataa he, jis ratij vran kehete hai tathaa uske paas kii lasikagranthi phool jaati he.
 praathmik upadansh vran ke utpann hone ke 48 ghanton ke andar rogajanak jeevanu sharir ke saare angon, twachaa, shleshmakala, netra tathaa tantrikaon main pahunchakar teji se badhane lagate hai. ratij praathmik vran ke hone ke 6 saptaah baad dwitiyak upadansh ke lakshan sharir main utpann hote hai. twachaa yaa shleshmakala kaa udbhedan hota he. guda tathaa onth ke paas jahaan ardrata rahati he vahaan udbhedan adhik hota he, jise condiloma kehete hai. saath hi onthon kaa katnaa, gale tathaa tonsil main pradaah, haath paanv or jodon main halkaa dard, haraarat, susti, aankhon main jalan aadi shikaayat rahati he. ye lakshan kai maheenon tak bane rah sakate hai or upachaar ke abhaav main bhi swayan lupt ho sakate hai. dwitiyak updanshgrast rogi rog ke sanchaaran kaa kaam karte hai.
 andhakshetra sookshmdarshi dwaara jeevanuon kii pariksha dwaara, yaa rakt pariksha dwaara pareekshan hota he. rinatmak pratikriya suchak fal praapt hone par bhi upadansh kaa na hona pramanit nahin hota. aisi sthiti main kuch samay baad pun: pareekshan karnaa chahie.
 upadansh ke lakshanon ke lupt hene ke baad rogi ko upadansh kaa koi ksht kuch kaal tak mahasoos nahin hota. aise rogiyon ko bahudhaa gupt upadansh ho jaataa he. gupt updanshgrast garbhvatee striyon kaa garbh samyak upachaar ke abhaav main gir saktaa he, yaa utpann shishu ko janmajaat upadansh hone kii sambhaavna rahati he. prasavkaal main neerog rahane par bhi kuch maas baad shishu main upadansh ke lakshan prakat hone lagate hai. uchit chikitsa hone par bhi shishu kaa bhavishya arakshit rahata he. chikitsa ke abhaav main shishu vikargrast hoga, jaise maathaa unchaa netra foole hue, chipti naak, dantavikar, bahraapan, mukhadwar ke aasapaas fatne tathaa daraar padane yaa ghaav bharne ke chihn, dhanushakar janghasthi. upadansh kii thik chikitsa na hone par praaia: 25 pratishat logon ko bhavi jivan main gupt upadansh ho jaataa he, jisase unki mrityu tak ho sakati he.
 1910 i. main eralikh dwaara avishkrut sailwarsan 606 or haal hi main penicillin ke avishkaar se upadansh kii chikitsa main safalataa milane lagi. iske purv chikitsa main sankhiya, vismath, potacium iodide tathaa paarad kaa prayog hota tha.
 yah sabase vyapak ratirog he or gonococus jeevanu dwaru failata he. yon sambandh dwaara sankraman hone ke do din se lekar do saptaah ke andar purushon ko peshaab main jalan or baad main taral yaa gadha mawaad, yaa raktamishrit peshaab, aana iskaa pradhan lakshan he. striyon ko peshaab main jalan tathaa safed taral kaa srav, pedu tathaa qamar main dard, dimbavahi nalee main sujan tathaa baanjhapan hota he. yadi is sthiti main yon prasang, madiraa aadi kaa sanyam bartaa gaya, to adhik jatilta nahin ho paati.
 navjaat shishuon kii aankh main silver nitrate kii boonde daalane ke nirodhak upaay ke kaaran netrasrav bahut ghat gaya he. sujak kii chikitsa main penicillin tathaa salphonamide kaa prayog aadhunik he or safal parinaam detaa he.
 yah vishaanujnya sankramak rog he. isamen jananendriya tathaa guda kii lasika grathiyon main pradaah haata he. iskaa sanchaaran maithun se hota he or udbhavan kaal teen se 21 dinon tak kaa hota he. yah chote se vran ke rup main aarambh hota he, jo kshtdaayi na hone ke kaaran mahatvaheen prathit hota he. do teen saptaah ke bheetar gilti ubhar aati he, yaa lasika granthi soojati he. gilti footati he or footkar nasoor ban jaati he. sirdard, taap tathaa haraarat kii shikaayat hoti he. striyon ko praaia: guda pradaah, fever, thand ke saath canpakanpi, sirdard or gaanthon main dard hota he tathaa baad main gilti ubhadati or footkar nasoor ban jaati he. gudanalika kii sikudan bhi hoti he.
 nidaan ke liye twachaa pareekshan or poorak sthirikaran pareekshan kiya jaataa he. chikitsa main salphonamidon or tetraciclin kaa upyog kiya jaataa he.
 isamen raanon kii lasika grathiyon main kanankur utak bad jate hai. yah rog jananendriyon par aarambh hota he or donon raanon tathaa muladhar tak pahunchakar laal vran ban jaataa he. rogajanak protozoa hai, yaa jeevanu, yah abhi tak sandigdh he.
 yah mulat: jananendriyon kii safai na rakhane se utpann hota he. sambhog ke 2 se 14 dinon ke bheetar jananendriya par dane ke rup main yah ubharta he or kramash: vran kaa rup dhaaran karata he. raan kii lasika grathiyon main gilti pad jaati he. yah vran mridu hota he. salphonamide se chikitsa kii jaati he.
 ratirog ke nirodh ke liye maithun ke samay rabar kii jhilliyon kaa prayog or maithun ke baad sabun se jananendriya kii safai sarvottam upaay hai. ratirog kaa pareekshan or upachaar sarvasulabh hona chahie or sarsaadhaaran ko in rogon ke sambandh main uchit jaankaree deni chahie, jisase ratiroggrast log bhay, lajja, sankoch aadi tyaag kar chikitsak kii salaah le saken.
 es tii di se apane-aap ko bachaaya ja saktaa he-
  swayan ek vivaah sambandh nibhaana or yah sunishchit karnaa ki saathi bhi use nibhaaye
  purushon dwaara letex condom ke prayog se chhot kaa bhay kam ho jaataa he agar sahi prayog kiya jaae. dhyaan rakhen, hameshaa sambhog ke samay uskaa upyog karen. mahilaon ke condom utane prabhaavshaalee nahin hai jitne purushon ke yadi purush na upyog kare to stri ko avashya karnaa chahie.
 yadi aapako ashanka ho ki aap ko es tii di he to madad lene se ghabraana yaa sharmaana nahin chaiye. doctor ke paas jaao or es tii di kii jaanch ke liye ho yaa agar aap purush hai to twachaa visheshgy ke paas jaao stri hai to stri rog visheshgy ke paas jaao. lakshanon kii upeksha mat karo or na hi yah intjaar karo ki aap chale jaaenge. es tii di rog bahut aam he or bahut chhot failaane vaale hote hai, agar jaldi pakakad main aa jaae to aasaani se thik bhi ho sakate hai.
