 bab al-mandeb arabi prayadweep par yaman or africa ke sing par jibooti, iritriya or uttari somalia ke bich sthit ek jaldamru he jo laal sagar ko adan kii khadi se jodta he.
 arabi bhashaa main 'bab al-mandeb' kaa matlab 'dukh ke dwaar' hota he. dhyaan den ki yahi 'mandeb' shabd hindi main thode parivartit arth ke saath 'mada' roopon main dekha jaataa he . kehete hai ki navikon ke liye is jagah se guzaarana khataranaak hota tha or unamem se bahut yahan pani ke teej bahaav main duubakar jaan kho baithte the. ek anya paaramparik katha ke anusaar yahan bahut pehle ek bada bukamp aayaa tha jis se asia or africa alag hue or isamen bahut se log maare gaye.
 bab al-mandeb hind mahaasaagar or bumadhya sagar ke bich kaa ek bahut hi mahatvapurn samudri rasta he. suez nahar main jane ke liye is se bhi nikalnaa zaruri he. san 2006 main yahan se rose 33 lakh bairal tel niklaa gaya tha, jo duniya kii 4.3 karod bairal dainik utpaadan kaa mapa ja sakane vaala bhaag he. yaman ke raas manheli se jibooti ke raas siyaan tak is jaldamru kii chaudaai lagbhag 30 kimi he. isamen perim naam kaa ek dweep he jo is jaldamru ko do hisson main baantta he. purvi bhaag 'bab iskundar' he jo sirf 3 kimi chaudaa or 30 meter gaharaa he. pashchimi bhaag 25 kimi chaudaa or 310 meter gaharaa 'daqt al​-mayun​' he. jibooti ke tat ke paas tapuon kaa ek gut hai jo 'saat bhai' kehalaate hai. purvi jaldamru main sataha par ek laal sagar ke andar kii taraf jane vaala pravaah he jabaki pashchimi jaldamru main sataha ke neeche laal sagar se baahar le jane vaala ek shaktishaalee pravaah chalata he.
 bahut se itihaaskaaron kaa manana he ki purvi africa main utpann hone ke baad maanav jati aaj se lagbhag 60,000 varsh purv bab-al-mandeb langhakar asia pahunchi thi. us yug main samudratal thodaa neeche tha or in jaldmaruon main yaa to pani kam tha yaa yah sookhe hi the. is dharna ke anusaar maanav yaheen se nikalakar sabase pehle us samay ke maanav-rahit bhartiya upmahadweep main pahunche the.
