 sangh jeevvaigyanik vargeekaran main jeevon ke vargeekaran kii ek shrenee hoti he. aadhunik jeevvaigyanik vargeekaran main yah shrenee virgon se upar or jagat ke neeche aata he, yani ek sangh main bahut se varg hote hai or bahut se sanghon ko ek jeevvaigyanik jagat main sangathit kiya jaataa he. dhyaan den ki har jeevvaigyanik sangh main bahut si bhinn jeevon kii jatiyaan-prajatiyaan sammilit hoti hai.
 jeevvaigyanik sanghon ke naam aksar latini bhashaa main hote hai kyonki jeevvaigyanik vargeekaran kii pratha 17veen or 18veen sadiyon main europe main shuru hui thi or us samay vahaan latini gyaan kii bhashaa maani jaati thi. yah rivaayat abhi tak chalati ai he. aadhunik kaal main istemaal hone vaali vargeekaran vyavastha 18veen shatabdi main karl liniyas naamak swidi vaigyanik ne kii thi.
