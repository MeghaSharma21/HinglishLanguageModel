 1992 main kroeshia ne swatantra raashtra ke rup main pehali baar olympic khelon main bhaag liya, or tab se har game main bhaag liya he. 
 kroeshia ke liye rashtriya olympic samiti kroeshian olympic samiti he, jise 1991 main banaaya gaya tha or 1993 main manyata praapt he.
 kroeshian athleton ne greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main tees-teen padak jite, jinamein 1996 or 2004 main purushon kii handbal team dwaara kai swarna padak or saath hi 2012 or 2016 main discs-throvar sandra parkovich shaamil the. sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main, jenakastelik ne 2002 or 2006 ke bich chah padakon jite, uske bhai ivika ne chaar rajat padak jite, or jacove fak ne 2010 main kaansya padak jeeta.
