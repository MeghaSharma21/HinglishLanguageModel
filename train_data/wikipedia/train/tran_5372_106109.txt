 
 asian shishamin yaa ambacidaye haddidaar machhliyon ke perceformez gana kaa ek kul he jo samudr va mithe pani donon main milti hai. iske 8 vanshon main 49 jatiyaan hai. asian shishamin asia, oshiania, hind mahaasaagar or pashchimi prashanth mahaasaagar main milti he. inamen se kuch kii lambaai 26 centimetre tak hoti he.
 in machhliyon kii kai jatiyon ke sharir shishe jaise paardarshee yaa ardh-paardarshee hote hai, jiske kaaran inhen minshaalaon main paalatu rup se rakhaa jaataa he. pehle inhen rangon ke tike lagaakar machhlee rakhane waalon ko bechaa jaataa tha lekin is se machhlee ko bhayankar peeda hoti he or jaldi mrityu bhi ho jaati he, or is krurta kii vajah se logon ne inhen kareednaa kam kar liya he.
