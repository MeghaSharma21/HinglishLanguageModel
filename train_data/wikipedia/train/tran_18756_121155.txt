 shimoga bharat ke karnataka praant ke madhya main sthit ek shahar he. iskaa aadhikarik naam 'shivamogga' he. yah shimoga jile kaa mukhyalay bhi he. nagar tunga nadi ke kinare sthit he. yah nagar pashchimi ghat ke parvatiy kshetra kaa praveshdwar he.
 yah nagar samudratal se 569 meter kii oonchaai par he. iske chaaron ore hare-bhare dhan ke kshetra or nariyal ke vrikshon ke samooh hai. 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar nagar kii jansankhya 322,428 he.
