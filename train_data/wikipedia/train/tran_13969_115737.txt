 phonishia madhya-purv ke urvar ardhachandra ke pashchimi bhaag main bumadhya sagar ke tat ke saath-saath sthit ek pracheen sabhyataa thi. samudri vyapaar ke zariye yah 1550 se 300 isa-purv ke kaal main bumadhya sagar ke dur​-daraaj ilaaqon main fail ga​i. unhen pracheen unani or roman log "jamuni-rang ke vyapaaree" kaha karte the kyonki rangarezi main istemaal hone vaale muraks ghonghe se banaae jane vaale jamuni rang keval inhi se mila karata tha. inhone jis aksharmala kaa izaad kiya usapar vishwa kii sari pramukh aksharmalas aadhaarit hai. kai bhashavaigyanikon kaa maananaa he ki devanagari-sahit bharat kii sabhi varnmalas isi phonishian varnmaala kii santane hai.
