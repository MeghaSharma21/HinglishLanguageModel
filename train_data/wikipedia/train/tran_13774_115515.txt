 joff praant, jise aupchaarik arabi main mintaqaah​ al-joff kehete hai, saudi arab kaa ek uttari praant he. isaki seemayen jordan ke saath lagati hai.
 joff praant kii seemaa jordan se lagati he or ise kisi jamaane main britain ke pradhan mantri winston churchill ne nirdhaarit kiya tha. is seemaa ke aakaar main ek bada-sa trikon-rupi jhatka he jise aksar 'winston kii hitchkee' kaha jaataa he. isapar yah mazaakia kahani bataai jaati he ki is sarhad ko naqshe par khinchne se pehle churchill ne dopaher main bahut khaanaa khaaya tha. lakeer khinchate hue unhen hitchkee ai or haath achaanak hill gaya.
 joff praant nafood registan se uttar main sthit he. yahan vaadi sirahan or al-joba naam kii do mahaan droniyan hai jinamein kai nachlistaan​ sthit hai or jo kabhi badi jhilein hoti thi jo suukh chuki hai. joff ke logon ke kai paariwarik sambandhi sarhad ke paas iraq or jordan main rahete hai.
