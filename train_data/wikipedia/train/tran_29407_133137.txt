 i shridharan bharat ke ek prakhyat civil ejeeniyar hai. ve 1995 se 2012 tak dilli metro ke nideshak rahe. unhen bharat ke 'metro main' ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he. bharat sarkaar ne unhen 2001 main padam shri tathaa 2008 main padam vibhushan se bhi sammanit kiya.
 i shridharan ne bahut kam samay ke bheetar dilli metro ke nirmaan kaa kaary kisi sapane kii tarah behad kushalta or shreshthata ke saath puraa kar dikhaaya he. desh ke anya kai shaharon main bhi metro seva shuru karne kii taiyaari he, jisamen shridharan kii medha, yojana or karypranali hi mukhya nirdharak kaarak honge. keralavasi shridharan kii kaaryastylee kii sabase badi khaasiyat he ek nishchit yojana ke tahat nirdhaarit samay seemaa ke bheetar kaam ko puraa kar dikhaanaa. samay ke bilkul paband shridharan kii isi kaaryastylee ne bharat main saarvajanik parivahan ko cheharaa hi badal diya. 1963 main rameswaram or tamilnadu ko aapas main jodne vaala pamban pul tut gaya tha. railway ne uske punanirmaan ke liye chah maheen kaa lakshya tay kiya, lekin us kshetra ke injarch ne yah avadhi teen maheene kar di or jimmedaari shridharan ko saunpi gai. shridharan ne maatr 45 dinon ke bheetar kaam karke dikhaa diya. bharat kii pehali sarvaadhik aadhunik railway seva konkan railway ke peeche ishridharan kaa prakhar mastishk, yojana or karypranali rahi he. bharat kii pehali metro seva kolkata metro kii yojana bhi unheen kii dein he. aadhuniktaa ke pahiyon par bharat ko chalaane ke liye sabaki ummiden shridharan par tiki he. isliye to sarkaar ne utkrisht kaaryon ko dekhte hue padshri or padam bhushan sammaanon se sammanit kiya. time patrika ne to unhen 2003 main asia kaa hero bana diya.2011 main i. shridharan ke uttaradhikari ke rup main mongu singh kii niyukti kii gai.
