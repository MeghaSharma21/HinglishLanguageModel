 
 jasta yaa zink ek rasinic tatv he jo sankraman dhaatu samooh kaa ek sadsy he. rasinic drushti se iske gun meganecium se milte-julate hai. manushya jaste kaa prayog prachinkaal se karte aaye hai. kansaa, jo tambe va jaste kii mishra dhaatu he, 10veen sadi isapurv se istemaal hone ke chinh chhod gaya he. 9veen shatabdi ipu se rajasthan main shuddh jasta banaaye jane ke chinh milte hai or 6thin shatabdi ipu kii ek jaste kii khan bhi rajasthan main mili he. lohe par jasta chadhaane se loha zang khaane se bachaa rahata he or jaste kaa prayog batriyon main bhi bahut hota he.
