 gasharabrum himalay kii karakoram parvatashrenee kii baltoro himani ke purvottaree chor par sthit ek parvaton kaa samooh he jo uttari kashmir main 35 ansh 44 minute uttari akshansh tathaa 76 ansh 42 minute purvi deshaantar par sthit hai. yah shaxgam ghati or gilgit-blatistan kii seemaa par sthit he, jinpar china va pakistan kaa niyantran he haalanki jinhen bharat apana bhaag bataata he. is punjak main vishwa ke teen aath hazari parvat hai, yani ve jinaki oonchaai 8,000 meter se adhik he. 
 sthaneey balati bhashaa main "gasha" kaa arth "sundar" or "brum" kaa arth "parvat" hota he, yani "gasharabrum" kaa matlab "sundar parvat" he.
