 rituparno ghosh ek bengali film nirdeshak the. arthshaastra main degree praapt karne uparant unhone vigyaapan agency main ek rachanaatmak kalakaar ke rup main apana karier shuru kiya. varsh 1994 main unki pehali film heerer angati relies hui. usee varsh parde par aayi unki agali film unishe april ko sarvshereshth feechar film ke liye rashtriya film puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. lagbhag do dashakon ke apane filmi karier main unhone kul 12 rashtriya or kuch anterrashtriya puraskaar praapt kiye. 30 may 2013 ko dil kaa daura padane se kolkata main unki mrityu ho gayi.
