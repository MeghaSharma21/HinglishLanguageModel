 admiral robin dhawan bharat ke vartmaan nausenadhyaksh hai. farvari 2014 main purv nausenadhyaksh admiral devendra joshi ke tyaagptr ke pashchaat vaais admiral dhawan ko kaaryvahak nausenadhyaksh banaaya gaya tha. april 2014 main unhen aupchaarik taur par nausenadhyaksh bana diya gaya. 
 
 admiral aar ke dhawan ko 01 janvari 1975 ko kamishan mila. vah nauvaahan or nideshan ke visheshgy hai or unhone anek nausena kamaanon-staff tathaa anudesh sambandhi niyuktiyon main 40 saal kii anukaraniya seva kii he. vah neshnal dephence academy, dephence services staff college or neval waar college newport, road island america ke chaatr rahe hai. 
 vah ienes khukhri, ienes ranjit, ienes dilli jaise agrani yuddh poton kii kaman sambhaal chuke hai or unhone chif staff officer ke pad par pashchimi kaman mukhyalay main sevaayen di hai. unhone bhartiya ucchayog landan main bhartiya nausena salaahkaar ke rup main sevaayen deen. vah purvi nausena kaman mukhyalay main flag officer commanding purvi bedaa, vishakhapattanam kii kaman sambhaal chuke hai. unhone apane vidhyalay neshnal dephence academy main commandent ke rup main sevaayen di hai. 
 admiral dhawan ne anya jin pratishthapurna padon par seva kii he unamem nausena mukhyalay main neval staff ke sahaayak pramukh, dipti chif of neval staff tathaa wois chif of neval staff shaamil hai.
 admiral dhawan kaa vivaah shrimati minu dhawan se hua or danpati kii ek putree or do putr hai.
