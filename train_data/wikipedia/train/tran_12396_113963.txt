 eu-tsang yaa tsang-eu tibet ke teen parmparik praanton main se ek he, jinhe tibeti bhashaa main tibet ke teen 'cholka' kaha jaataa he. anya do praant kham or amdo hai. eu-tsang tibet ke kendriya or pashchimi hisson par vistrut he, jisamen yarlung tsangpo nadi kaa jalsambhar kshetra, kailash parvat kaa ilaaka or changthang pathaar kaa adhiktar kshetra shaamil he. tibet par janavaadee ganatantr china kaa qabza hone ke baad uski sarkaar ne tibet kaa prashasnic vibhaajan kar diya or eu-tsang kaa bhushetra ab tibet swashasit pradesh naamak praant main sammilit he. aitihaasik rup se eu-tsang tibet ke 'eu' or 'tsang' naamak do ilaaqon ke vilay se bana tha or yah tibeti samskruti kii kendriya bhoomi mana jaataa he. gyantse or shigatse ke shahar tsang kshetra main aate hai jabaki lhasa eu kshetra main sthit he.
