 doha arab deshon main se ek, faras kii khadi main sthit prayadwipiya desh qatar kii rajdhani or yahan kaa sabase bada nagar he. yah shahar is desh ke purvi tat par sthit he or desh kaa pramukh aarthik centre he. doha shahar kii kul aabaadi 13,51,000 he jabaki iske upnagareey kshetron ko shaamil kiya jaay to kul jansankhya lagbhag 15 lakh ke aasapaas he. yah qatar kaa sabase teji se badhata hua shahar he or desh kii lagbhag aadhi aabaadi is shahar yaa iske upnagareey ilaake main rahati he.
 doha nagar kii sthapana al bidaa naamak shahari ilaake ke upnagar ke rup main san 1820 main hui thi or 1971 main angrezi shashan se azaadi ke paashchaat ise aadhikarik rup se rajdhani ghoshit kiya gaya. purana shahar al bidaa ab khud kaafii vistrut ho chuke doha shahar kaa ek nagareey kshetra he. vividhta or vaishvikaran ke yug main anya jagahon ke saath behatar judaav ke chalate aaj doha ek vaishvik shahar he tathaa madhya purv kaa ek mahatvapurn vanijyik centre bhi he.
