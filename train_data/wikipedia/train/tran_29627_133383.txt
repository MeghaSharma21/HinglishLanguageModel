 madai yaa 'danvari' yaa daunri krishi kii vah prakriya he jisamen kati fasal kii faliyon se dane ko vimukt kiya jaataa he. yah kataai evam osauni ke bich kii prakriya he. is kriya main kati fasal ko kisi prakaar itnaa jhakzora jaataa he ki uske pake dane alag ho jany.
 pehle kati fasal ko kisi sakht tal ke upar bikher kar bail athwa ghodon ko vrittiya path par uske upar baar-baar chalaaya jaataa tha jisase dane alag ho jate the. aajkal is kaary ke liye mashine bhi aa gayin hai jinhe 'thressure' kehete hai. kabhi-kabhi fasalon ko sadak ke upar failaakar chhod diya jaataa he or uske upar se aane-jane vaale vibhinn gadiyon ke pahiyon se fasal kii madai ho jaati he.
