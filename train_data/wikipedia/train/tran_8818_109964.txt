 alva reimer myrdal ek swedish samajashastri or raajnneetijna thim. vah 1982 main nobel shanti puraskaar mila. inhone 1924 main gunnar myrdal se shaadi kii.
 upsaala main janmi alva 1930 ke dashak main pehali baar logon kii nigaah main ain. alva ne sweden kalyankari rajya ke nirmaan main ahm bhoomika adaa kii thi. apane pati gunnar myrdal ke saath milakar 1934 main jansankhya se jude sawaal par 'Crisis in the Population Question' naamak kitaab likhi, jisamen logon ke vyaktigat utthan ke liye saamaajik sudhaar par jor diya gaya tha. inhen varsh 1981 main inke pati gunnar myrdal ke saath jawahar laal nehru puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya.
