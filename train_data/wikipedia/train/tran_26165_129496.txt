 1996 cricket vishwa cap, cricket vishwa cap kaa chatha sanskaran tha. yah pakistan, bharat or srilanka main 14 farvari se 17 march 1996 ko yojit kiya gaya. toornament wills dwaara pryojit kiya gaya tha or isame baarah timo ne bhaag liya tha. prayek match 50 over prati team kaa tha or rangeen kapadon main or safed gendon ke saath kheli gaya tha or adhiktam match dudhia roshni ke dauraan khele gaye the.
 toornament kaa final srilanka or australia ke bich tha, or srilanka ne australia ko gaddaphi stadium me khele gaye final me 7 wicket se parajeet kar apana pehla cricket vishwa cap jeeta.
