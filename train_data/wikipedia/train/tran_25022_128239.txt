 bhaayal parmaar rajput rajvansh kii ek shakha he jo adhikansh rajasthan ke barmer jile kii sivaana tehsil main based hue he. inke mukhya 12 gaanv he jo is prakaar he- gungarot, goliyaan, pipalod,padardi, moothli, devandi, mavadi, bhagava, telwada, maangi, dhira or jinpur he. iske baad sivaana ko bhi bhailo ke bade ganvo main gina jaataa he. sivaana vidhansabha kshetra ke vartmaan vidhayak shri hamirsingh ji bhaayal bhi inhi se taalluk rakhte he.
