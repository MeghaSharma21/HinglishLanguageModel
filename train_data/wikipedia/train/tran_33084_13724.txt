 aaliff laila yaa kii kathaayen multah farsi bhashaa main 'aaliff laila' sheershak se rachi gayi thi. yah sansar kii mahanatam rachanaaon main se ek he, visheshkar bal-sahitya ke kshetra main. adhiktar rachanaaen pracheen bharat, iran tathaa arab deshon kii puranic kathaon kaa sangrah he. kahaniyaan ati kalpanasheel, tilsmi tathaa jaadui ghatnaon se bhari hui hai.
 aaliff laila kii pramukh kahaniyon main - sindbaad kii saat samudri yatraaen, aladin or jaadui chiraag, ali baba or chalis chor, bolne vaali chidiyaan, kajur kii guthla, murge kii sikh, pari kaa coup, suraahi kaa jinn aadi prasiddh hai.
