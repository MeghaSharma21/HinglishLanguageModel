 ishwar chandra vidyasagar, ke bachapan kaa naam ishwar chandra bandopadhyaay tha. ve bengal ke punarjagran ke stambhon main se ek the. inkaa janm pashchim bengal main hua tha, karmatand inaki karmbhumi thi. ve ucchkoti ke vidwaan the. unki vidwata ke kaaran hi unhen viddasagar kii oopaadhi di gai thi.
 ve nari shiksha ke samarthak the. unke prayaas se hi kalakatta main evam anya sthanon main bahut adhik balika viddalayon kii sthapana hui.
 us samay hindu samaaj main vidhwaaon kii sthiti bahut hi sochneey thi. unhonen vidhwaa punarvivaah ke liye logamat taiyaar kiya. unheen ke prayaason se 1856 i. main vidhwaa-punarvivaah kanoon paarit hua. unhone apane iklaute putr kaa vivaah ek vidhwaa se hi kiya. unhone bal vivaah kaa bhi virodh kiya.
 vidyasagar ek darshanik, shikshashasthri, lekhak, anuvadak, mudrak, prakaashak, udhyami, sudhaarak evam maanavtaavaadi vyakti the. unhone bangla bhashaa ke gadhy ko saral evam aadhunik banane kaa unka kaary sadaa yaad kiya jaayegaa. unhone bangla lipi ke varnmaala ko bhi saral evam tarksmmat banaaya. banglaa padhaane ke liye unhone saikadon viddalay sthapit kiye tathaa raatri paathshaalaon kii bhi vyavastha kii. unhone sanskrit bhashaa ke prachaar-prasar ke liye prayaas kiya. unhone sanskrit college main paashchaatya chintan kaa adhyayan bhi aarambh kiya.
 inkaa janm bengali brahman parivaar main 26 sitambar 1820 or inkaa nidhan 29 july 1891 ko hua.
 shaikshanik jivan main ve bahoot hi budhdiman vidhyaarthee the.
 inhen sudhaarak ke rup main raja rammohan raay kaa uttaradhikari mana jaataa he. inhone vidhwaa punarvivaah ke liye andolan kiya or san 1856 main is aashay kaa adhinium paarit karaayaa. 1856-60 ke madhya inhone 25 vidhwaao kaa punarvivaah karaayaa. inhone nari shiksha ke liye bhi prayaas kiye or isi kram main Baithune skool kii sthapana kii tathaa kul 35 skool khulwaaye.
