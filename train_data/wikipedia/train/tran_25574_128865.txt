 shevrole cruge amareeki car company general motors kii shevrole divizan dwaara nirmit ek compact car he jo 2008 main launch hui thi. is naan se japaan main ek sab-compact hazbek car suzuki ke saath sanyukt-udyog dwa 2001 se 2008 ke bich pehle bhi ban chuki he. 2008 se cruge nemplate kaa vaishvik str par utpaadan, vikaas evam roopankan ek paanch-dwaar vaali sidan-compact car ke rup main 2011 se kiya gaya he. yahi sab ek stesan wagon rup ke liye 2012 se bhi kiya gaya he. australesia main holden cruge or dakshin korea main lacketi premier ke naye janareshan praturup iske pichley suzuki vyutpann praturup kaa purnataya riplacement nahin hai. varan ye do anya model: devu lacketi anterrashtriya str par tathaa uttari-america vishesh shevrole cobalt kaa riplacement avashya maani ja sakati he.
 cruge deazal pehali jiem yatri car thi jo deazal ingine ke saath amareeki bazaar main launch hui thi.
