 trinkomali srilanka ke purvi praant ke trinkomali jile kaa mukhyalay tathaa pramukh bandargah he. yah shri lanka ke purvi tat par jafana se 113 mile dakshin tathaa batticalova se 69 mile uttar main sthit he. lagbhag do hajaar varshon se yah tamil bhashaa or samskruti kaa kendra rahaa he. 
 yah srilanka ke uttar-purvi kinare par sthit nagar evam bandargah he. yah vishwa ke prakritik bandargahon main se ek he. is bandargah kaa mahatv nausainik adde ke kaaran he. yahan kii ausat vaarshik varsha 64.8inch tathaa ausat vaarshik taap 27 degree celsius rahata he. yahan se pashchim main taybalagaam ke anoopon main muktashukti milti hai. bandargah se chaaval evam bisatabane kii vastuon kaa aayaat tathaa dhan, tambaakoo, imarti lakdi, sukhi machhlee or harin ke sing evam chamade kaa niryaat hota he. trinkomali tamil logon dwaara sthapit srilanka main pratham nagar he.
