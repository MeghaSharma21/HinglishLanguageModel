 chakphaatama  jagadishpur, bhagalpur, bihar sthit ek gaanv he. yah gaanv sadak maarg se judaa hone ke saath saath, railmarg se bhi judaa hua he. sadak maarg kii baat kare to yah bhagalpur-bousi maarg main phulvariyaa se 4 kilometre purv disha main padataa he. or railmarg ki baat kii jaay to yah koili-khutaha halt se 2 kilometre dakshinpurv main tathaa gonudham halt se 2 kilometre uttarpurv main sthit he. yahan ke zyaadaatar log krishikarya se jude hue he. gaay, bhais, bakrii yahan ke logon ke aarthik aazaadee kaa mukhya saadhan he. jisase praapt doodh ko paas hin bhagalpur kii dudhmandi main bechakar aay kaa arjan kiya jaataa he. yahan hardin kariban 10 quintal doodh pashuo se miltaa he. shiksha ke maamle main yah gaanv kaaphi jaagruk he. yahan ke harek gramin apane bachche ko acchhee shiksha dena chaahate hai. iske liye vah harek praytn karata he. yahan ke kuch log apni sevaayen bharat mata kii sarahadein kii raksha karne main bhi de rahen hai. yahan ke log kaaphi milansaar he.
