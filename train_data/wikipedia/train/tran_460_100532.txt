 pratirohak do siron vaala vaidyut avyav he jiske siron ke bich vibhavaantar usase bahane vaali taatkaalik dhara ke samaanupati hota he. ye vibhinn aakaar-prakaar ke hote hai. inase hokar dhara bahane par inke andar ushma utpann hoti he. kuch pratirohak om ke niyam kaa paalan karte hai jiskaa arth he ki -
 electronic paripath main praaia: sabase adhik prayukt avyav he. vyavhaar main prayukt pratirohak vibhinn padaarthon, taron evam filmon ke dwaara banaaye jate hai. vaidyut-drushti se, heater, vidyut istari, vidyut balb aadi pratirohak hai.
 ooshmaa nikaalane kii kshamta ke anusaar pratirodhon ko 1/4 wat, 1/2 wat, 1 wat, 2, wat, 5 wat, 10 wat, 50 wat, 100 wat aadi, kaa kaha jaataa he.
