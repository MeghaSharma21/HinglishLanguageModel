 kaayen, dakshin america main sthit france ke ek vibhaag evam samudr paar ke kshetra french guyana kii rajdhani he. shahar atlantic tat par kaayen nadi ke muhane par ek bhootpurv dweep par khadaa he. shahar ke adarsh vaakya he "fort orum industariyaa" jiskaa arth he "karm dhan laata he." 2012 kii janaganana ke anusaar, vahaan ke mahanagareey kshetra main 121,308 nivasee the, jinamein se 55,198 keyen samuchit shahar main rahete the.
