 mathura maarg dilli kaa ek pramukh sadak maarg he. yah ai tii o kshetra ke nikat tilak pul se aarambh hokar, dakshin main dilli-faridabad seemaa tak jaataa he. iske baad bhi yah haryana rajya va phir uttar pradesh rajya main mathura shahar tak is hi naam se janaa jaataa he. 
 haryana main yah maarg faridabad, ballabgarh, palaval hodal ke baad uttar pradesh main pravesh karata he 2011 main ise 6 len kaa karne kaa prastaav rakhaa gaya or is kaa theka reliance ko mila
