 babusingh rathod ek bhartiya janata party sarkaar ke vidhayak he. babu singh shergarh ke 15 saalon se vidhayak pad par he, inhone ne lagaataar teen baar balesar ke vidhayak ummed singh ko chunaav main haraaya. 
 babusingh rathod kaa janm rajasthan ke jodhpur zile kii balesar tehsil ke nathdaoo gaanv main hua tha inke pita kaa naam "chainsingh rathod" he. vidhayak banane se purv rathod bhartiya jivan beemaa nigam main ek agent ke rup main bhi kaary kar chuke he. 
 babusingh rathod ne jodhpur kii vishvavidhyalay jainarayan vyas vishvavidhyalay se em.com kii padhai purna kii thi inke alaava ye is vishvavidhyalay ke chaatrasangh ke adhyaksh bhi rah chuke he.
