 
 andrew jaikson sanyukt rajya america ke saatve raashtrapati the. vah is pad par 1829 se 1837 ke kaal main rahe jisamen unhone do dafaa chunaav jitkar chaar-chaar saal kii do miyaaden puri kareen. vah ek rajaneta or sainik netaa the jinhonen mul america adivaasiyon or british sena ke viruddh yuddh lade or jite. unhone america kii kendriya sarkaar ko majboot kiya, mul america adivaasiyon kii ore krurta dikhaakar unhen apane nivas-kshetron se nikalvaaya or nagarikon kaa paksh lete hue sarkari afasarshahi ko kamazor kiya.
