 nadi ke kinar sthit shahi vanaspati udyaan kii ginati vishwa ke behtareen udyanon main hoti he. 40 hectare main faile is udyaan main 12,000 prajatiyon ke paudhe rakhe gaye hai. 1845 main ise banane kaa nishchay kiya gaya tha. 1852 main german mul ke vanaspatishastri furniand won mular ne ise banane kii shuruyaat kii. baad main unke uttaradhikari wiliam gulifoyal ne ise vistaar pradaan kiya. yahaan par kuch ped 100 saal se bhi puraane hai. vanaspati ke atirikt vibhinn prakaar ke pakshiyon ko bhi yahan dekha ja saktaa he.
