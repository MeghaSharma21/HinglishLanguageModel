 new mexico ek rajya he jo sanyukt rajya america ke dakshin-pashchimi kshetra main sthit he. europea khoj se hajaaron saal pehle tak yahan mul america nivas karte the. new mexico kaa 1598 main spanish dwaara upniveshikaran kiya gaya tha. baad main yah swatantra mexico kaa hissaa bana. 1848 main america se apni haar ke pashchaat yah ilaaka mexico ne america ko saunp diya. yah 6 janvari 1912 ko 47wein rajya ke rup main sangh main bhartee kiya gaya tha.
 new mexico america kaa paanchvaan sabase bada rajya he. 2016 ke anumaan ke mutabik rajya kii jansankhya 20,81,015 hai. isse iskaa sab rajyon main 36waan sthaan hua. 69.7% logon dwaara angrezi or 28.5% dwaara spani maatrubhaasha ke rup main boli jaati he. navajo ek mul america bhashaa 3.5% janata dwaara boli jaati hai. lagbhag 80% janata isai dharm ko maanati hai.
