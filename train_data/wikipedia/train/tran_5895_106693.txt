 tehran iran iran ke tehran praant kaa ek shahar or iran kii rajdhani he. is shahar kii jansankhya varsh 2006 kii janaganana ke anusaar 7,797,520 he.
 sthiti : 35°44' uthho acco tathaa 51°30' puchho desho. yah nagar iran desh tathaa tehran pradesh kii rajdhani or ek mahatvapurn audyogic, saanskrithik evam rail, sadak tathaa vayumargon kaa kendra he. samudratal se 1,175 meter kii oonchaai par caspian sagar se 105 kilometre dakshin tathaa elburj parvat shrenee se 16 kilometre dakshin sthit he. is nagar se demawand parvat naamak supt jwalamukhi kii himachhadit nukili chotii dikhaai detii he. yahan ke darshneey bhawanon main rajbhavan, sangrahalay, masjiden ityadi hai. rajbhavan main vah mayurasinghaasana he, jo 1939 itho main nadirshah dwaara dilli se laayaa gaya tha. 
 kazar vansh ke sansthaapak aagaa mohammad khan ne 1788 itho main is nagar ko apni rajdhani banaaya. tatpashchaat nagar kaa vikaas teevr gati se hota gaya. yah upjau khetihar pradesh main sthit he, jahaan gehun, chukundar, fal tathaa kapaas utpaadan mahatvapurn he. kapadaa, cement, dhaatu kaa saamaan, rasinic padaarth, kaach, chini, diyasalai, cigarette, sabun ityadi ke udyog mukhya hai. yah faras kii khadi tathaa caspian sagar se railmargon dwaara judaa he. nagar se 5.6 milometre pashchim main harabad hawaii adda sthit he, jahaan se kai videshi vayumarg guzarate hai. 1935 itho se sthapit tehran vishvavidhyalay ucch shiksha kaa kendra he.
