 kandla bharat ke gujarat praant main kuccha jile main sthit desh kaa sab se bada bandargah he. aayaat-niryaat se puure vishwa ke saath judaa hua he. kandla bandargah kaa prashasan kandla port trust ke haath main he jiskaa puraa niyantran bharat sarkaar ke jahajrani mantralay ke haath he..kandla kaa naam badal kar ab dinadayal bandargah kar diya gaya he.
 kandla bandargah kuccha ke akhaat main pakistan ke karachi bandargah se 256 notical mil, mumbai bandargah se 430 notical mil kii duuri par sthit he. kandla bharat ke sab se bade 12 mukhya bandargaho main se cargo hendling main sab se bada he. kandla bandargah se prativarsh 70,000 million ton se bhi zyada cargo handal kiya jaataa he. bandargah kaa sanchaalan sthanik taur par kandla port trust ke hasthak he, jiska prashasnic karyaalay gandhidam main sthit he. trust kaa sanchaalan bharat sarkaar ke shipping mantralay ke dwaara hota he. chermen kii niyukti bharat sarkaar dwaara kii jaati he. kandla bandargah se taralapadaarth, namak, loha, rasayana ityadi kii aayaat-niryaat hoti he.
 kandla khaas aarthik kshetra jo speshyal economik zone se janaa jaataa he. ye kandla port se sirf 9 ki.mi. kii duuri par he. ye puure bharat evam asia kaa sab se pehla khaas aarthik kshetra he. jisaki sthapana i.sa. 1965 main hui thi., kandla esaized sab se bada vividhlakshi esaized he. kandla bharat kaa sab se pehla free trade zone he. 
 bharat or pakistan ke batwaare ke baad desh kaa pramukh karachi bandargah pakistan main chalaa gaya tha. jis kii kami puri karne hetu i.sa. 1950 main pashvim bharat main arabi samudr ke kuccha ke akhaat ke tat par kandla bandargah kii sthapana kii gai thi.
