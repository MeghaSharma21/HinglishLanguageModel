 naoko yamazaki JAXA kii ek purv japani antariksh yatri he, jo ki arhata praapt karne vaali duusari japani mahila he. naoko kaa janm 7 disambar 1970 ko matsudo shahar main hua tha. 1989 main ochanamoiju vishvavidhyalay ke varisht hai skool se snaatak hone ke baad, unhone 1993 main tokyo vishvavidhyalay se airospes ejineering main snaatak kii degree praapt kii. 1996 main airospes ejineering main vigyaan main masters kii degree bhi hasil kii.
 yamazaki ko 1999 main japan ke rashtriya antariksh vikaas agency dwaara ek antariksh yatri ummidavaar ke rup main chunaa gaya tha. or unhone april 1999 main ieses antariksh yatri mul prashikshan kaarykram main bhaag liya, or sitambar 2001 main ek antariksh yatri ke rup main pramanit hui. 2001 ke baad se, yamazaki ne iesees advanced training main bhaag liya he or japani experiment modul ke hardware or oparation ke vikaas kaa samarthan kiya he. may 2004 main, yamjaki ne star city, rus ke yuri gagarin cosmonats training centre main soyuj-tieme udaan abhiyantaa prashikshan puraa kiya. june 2004 main yamazaki texas ke huston main johnson spes centre main apane ne antariksh yatri ummidavaar ke prashikshan ke liye pahunche or vahaan unka prashikshan shuru hua. vah vartmaan main antariksh yatri karyaalay robotics shakha main kaary kar rahi he. 5 april 2010 ko yamazaki ne mishan ests -131 ke hisse ke rup main shatal discovery par antariksh main pravesh kiya or vah 20 april, 2010 ko prithvi par laut aae.
