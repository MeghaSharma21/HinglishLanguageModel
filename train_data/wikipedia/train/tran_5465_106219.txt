 purvi antarctic antarctic mahadweep kaa purvi do-tihaai hissaa he. paar-antarctic parvatamala antarctic ko do bhaagon main baantati he. purvi antarctic is parvatamala se purv main sthit bhaag he. is ke vipareet pashchimi antarctic is parvatamala kii duusari ore he or yahan antarctic ke kul kshetrafal kaa lagbhag ek-tihaai hissaa aata he. antarctic ke in donon bhaagon ke bich purvi antarctic kii ausat oonchaai adhik he or mahaan gamburtasev parvatamala purvi antarctic ke thik madhya main sthit he. hamaari prithvi kaa dakshini dhruv bhi purvi antarctic main hi sthit he.
 kuch chote tateey kshetron ko chhodkar purvi antarctic sadaiv barf kii moti himchadar se dhakaa rahata he. vanaspation ke naam par yahan keval pattharon se chipki hui likene, haritha or shaiwal hi he. taton ke sameep penguin va seelein milti hai lekin taton se hatakar keval sutarakrimi or kiton jaise aksheruki praani hi milte hai or ve bhi adhiktar vahaan jahaan ve likene, haritha, ityadi kha saken.
 purvi antarctic vishwa kaa sabase sheet ilaaka mana jaataa he or prithvi par aaj tak maape gayi sabase thande taapmaana yaheen paye gayi hai. agast 2010 main antariksh yaan dwaara yahan −94.7 °centigrade taapmaana mapa gaya.
 antarctic sandhi ke antargat antarctic par kisi bhi prakaar kii sainik or odyogic gatividhi par pabandi he. apni bhayankar sardi or antarctic tak pahunch paane kii kathinaayee ne ise ab tak maanviya dabil se bachaaya hua he. bhumandaleey ooshmeekaran se isaki himchadar ko khatara he lekin purvi antarctic ke bhitari bhaagon main saadhe-chaar kilometre tak kii moti himchadar bichhi hui he.
