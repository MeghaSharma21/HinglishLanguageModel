 acharya baldev upaadhyaay hindi or sanskrit ke suprasiddh vidwaan, saahityetihaaskaar, nibandhkaar tathaa samalochak the. unhone anekon granthon kii rachana kii, nibandhon kaa sangrah prakashit kiya tathaa sanskrit vaanmay kaa itihaas likhaa. ve sanskrit sahitya kii hindi main charchaa ke liye jane jate hai. unke purv sanskrit sahitya se sambandhit adhikansh pustaken sanskrit main hai yaa angreji main. 
 acharya baldev upaadhyaay ko bharat sarkaar dwaara san 1984 main sahitya evam shiksha ke kshetra main padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha.
 acharya ji kaa janm uttar pradesh ke baliya jile ke sonbarasa gaanv main ek brahman parivaar main hua tha. unke pita kaa naam ram suchit upaadhyaay tha jo bhagwat puraana ke vidwaan the. unki mata kaa naam murti devi tha. baldev ji kii aarmabhik shiksha shaskeey hai skool baliya main hui, keval 1911-12 main ve banaras ke bengali tolaa inter kalege main 6thi kaksha main padhe. unhone kaashi hindu vishvavidhyalay se 1922 main bi e kiya. banaras ke sanskrit kalege se saahityacharya kiya.
