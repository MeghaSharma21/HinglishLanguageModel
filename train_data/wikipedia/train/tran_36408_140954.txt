 osman ali khan hyderabad riyasat ke antim nizaam the. ve mehboob ali khan ke duusare putr the. 1911 se 1948 tak ve is riyasat ke nizaam rahe or uske pashchaat 1956 tak uske sanvaidhanik pramukh. ek samay main vishwa ke sabase dhani vyakti the osman. 
 nizaam ko ek bahut hi kushal prashaasak ke rup main janaa jaataa tha. praja unhen pyaar se "nizaam sarkaar" or "huzur-e-nizaam" jaise naam se bulaati thi. 86,000 varg mile ke kshetrafal ke saath, yah mote taur par britain or scotland kaa aakaar tha. yah shaasha he, jo sabhi british bharat main sarvocch ranking rajkumaar tha, 21-bandook salaam ke hakdaar tha, jisamen "nizaam" kaa anoothaa khitaab tha or use "unke oonche ucchata" ke rup main akele ek bhedbhaav diya gaya tha.
 yah "golconda khan" tha jo nizaam ke liye rajasav kaa mukhya strot tha. 19 veen shatabdi main hyderabad or berer vishwa bazaar main heere ke ekmaatr aapurtikarta hai.
 nijee taur par shiksha grahan karne ke baad osman ali ne 29 agast 1911 ko chathe nizaam mehboob ali khan se padbhaar sanbhaala. vittiya sudhaaron ko badhaava dete huye handrawaad riyasat ko vaanchhneeya vittiya rup se shashakt sthiti main laane kaa shrey unhen jaataa he. riyasat ne apni mudra or sikke jari kiye or ek pramukh rail company kaa swaamitv grahan kiyaca 1918 main unki sangrakshan main osmania vishvavidhyalay hyderabad kii sthapana kii gayi. dwitiya vishwayuddh main unki riyasat ne nausainik jahaaj or do royal airforce squadran uplabdh karaaye. 1946 main unhen royal victoria chen se sammanit kiya gaya. janata kii pukaar ko dhyaan main rakhte hue, unhen 26 januari 1950 ko andhrapradesh kaa pehla rajpramukh banaaya gaya tha.
 hyderabad state ke lagbhag sabhi saarvajanik bhawano kii sthapana kaa shrey unako jaataa he, dharan hyderabad hai court, osmania general aspataal, unani aspataal, assembly haul, asafia pustkaalay aadi.
 apane shasankaal ke dauraan, unhone kai shaikshik sudhaaron kii shuruyaat kii. nizaam ke bajet kaa asaamaanya rup se ucch 11% shiksha par kharch kiyagaya tha. unhone banaras hindu vishvavidhyalay ke liye 10 lakh rupee kaa bada daan or aligarh muslim vishvavidhyalay ke liye 5 lakh rupee daan kiye.
 nizaam ke rajya ke record se pataa chalata he ke yah mir osman ali khan hi the, jinhone dow bi aar ambedkar ko 54 acre jameen aavantit kii, jahaan baad main sthapit milind ajukeshan socity banaai gai.shivaji hai skool-amravati, telugu academy hyderabad rajya se inke anya daan kaa laabh uthaane ke liye bhartiya sansthaanon main se ek he.
 unhone osmania vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana kii; aaj yah bharat ke sabase bade vishvavidhyalayon main se ek he. skool, college or anuwaad ke liye ek vibhaag kii sthapana kii gai thi. praathmik shiksha anivaary kar di gai thi or garibon ke liye muft pradaan kii gai thi.
 1941 main, unhone apana khud kaa bank, "hyderabad state bank" rajya ke kendriya bank ke rup main. yah 8 agast 1941 ko hyderabad state bank adhinium ke tahat sthapit kiya gaya tha. bank ne hyderabad kii mudra osmania sikaa main kaamyaab rahe. yah bharat kaa ekmaatr rajya tha jisamen hyderabad ke bankenots -hyderaabadi rupaya tha . vilay dwaara, bank marcentile bank of hyderabad, jise raja pannalaal pitti kii sthapana 1935 ne kii thi.
 begumpet hawaii adda 1930 ke dashak main nizaam dwaara hyderabad airo club ke gathan ke saath sthapit kiya gaya tha. shuruyaat main ise nizaam deccan airways ke liye gharelu or antarrashtriya hawaii adde ke rup main istemaal kiya gaya tha, jo british bharat kii sabase puraani airline thi. terminal building 1937 main banaai gai thi.
 1908 ke mahaan musi baadh ke baad, jisamen anumaanit 50,000 log maare gaye, nizaam ne do badi jhil, osman sagar or himaayat sagar kaa nirmaan kiya. purv kaa naam khud ke naam par rakhaa gaya tha, or duusari jhil unke bete aazam jahaan mir himaayat ali khan ke naam par rakhaa gaya. inkaa fida hyderabad kii praja aaj bhi uthaa rahi he. 
 nizaam hindu or musalmaan ko apni do ankhen maanate the. unhone kai mandiron kii pragati ke liye kai baar sone evam paison se daan kiya. andhra pradesh abhilekhaagaar kii filon se pataa chalata he ki nizaam ne yadagirgutta mandir ke liye 82,825 rupee, shri rambagh mandir ke liye 5000 rupee, bhadrachalam mandir ke liye 50,000 rupee or tirupati venkateshwar mandir ke liye salanaa 8,000 rupee daan kiya.
 usne prasiddh rudreshwar swami mandir ke alaava madanpet, shankarbagh, golnaakaa ke mandiron ke prati vaarshik anudaan bhi tay kiye hue the.
 varsh 1932 tha. bhandarkar praachya shodh santhaan, puna ko hindu mahakavy, mahabharat ke prakaashan or guest house ke nirmaan ke liye paise kii jarurat thi. saatven nizaam, mir osman ali khan ko aupchaarik anurodh kiya gaya tha. unhone 'farman' jari karne main koi samay nahin diya, 11 saal ke liye 1,000 rupaye prati varsh. guest house ke liye ru. 50,000 kii peshkash kii gai thi.
 1948 ke dauraan, hyderabad ke nizaam - HEH mir osman ali khan ko masjid kii manzil ke ¼ hisse kii marammat ke liye ₹ 75,000 kaa daan dene ke liye kaha gaya tha. nizaam ne iske bajaay ₹ 3 lakh daan kiya, yah bataate hue ki masjid ke shesh 3/4 purana nahin dikhnaa chahie.
 san 1965 main bharat-chini sangharsh ke chalate nizaam se sthapit rashtriya raksha kosh main yogadan dene kaa anurodh kiya gaya. san 1965 main, mir osman ali khan ne yuddh ke fund ko badhaane ke liye paanch ton yani 5000 kilo kaa sona kaa yogadan diya. maudrik sharton main, aaj ke bazaar mulya ke rup main nizaam kaa yogadan kareeb 1500 karod rupee tha yah bharat main kisi bhi vyakti yaa sanghathan dwaara ab tak kaa sabase bada yogadan he.
 1947 main, nizaam ne vivaah ke avasar par elizabeth_dwitiya ke liye hira gahane kaa uphaar banaaya, jisamen ek tiara or haar shaamil tha. is uphaar se broshas or haar abhi bhi rani dwaara pahane jate hai or nizaam of hyderabad necoles ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
 rajya swayamsevi sena, rajakar sahit majalise ittehad ul muslimin se samarthan paakar osman ali ne angrejon ke chale jane ke baad 1947 main bhartiya samprabhutaa ke samaksh samarpan karne se inkaar kar diya. angrejon ke saath vishesh gathbandhan ek khulaa vikalp rakhte hue unhone sanyukt raashtra sangh main hyderabad kii purna swatantrata kaa apana maamla rakhaa. parantu sitambar 1948 main bhartiya sainikon kii shakti ke saamne unhen jhukna padaa. 
 unhen riyasat kaa rajpramukh banaaya gaya. yah vyavastha 1956 ke saamaanya seemaa punargathan ke kaaran unki riyasat ke padosi rajyon main vilay hone tak kaayam rahi. iske baad unhone teen patniyon, 300 naukaron, briddh ashriton or nijee sena sahit sevanivritti kaa jivan vyateet karne lage. unhone apane purv samay ke lagbhag 10,000 naukar samet rajkumaron or das-daasiyon ko pension pradaan kii or philistin ke muslim sharnaarthiyon ko sahaayta di.
 mir osman ali khan ne 24 farvari 1967 ko king kothi pales main apni aakhri saans lee. unhen judi masjid main dafnaaya gaya, jise unhone 1936 main apane bete jawad kii yaad main banaaya tha, jo ek shishu ke rup main mar gaye the.
 unka antim sanskaar kaa juluus bhartiya itihaas main sabase bada tha, unki lokapriyata kii gavaahi yah thi ki anumaanit 10 lakh log nizaam bandook-gaadi juluus kaa hissaa bane. nizaam kaa antim sanskaar bharat ke itihaas main logon kii sabase badi gaiyr-dharmik, gaiyr-raajnneetik sabha thi.
 aakhri nizaam ke nidhan par, tatkaaleen andhra pradesh sarkaar ne unhen asaadhaaran rajptr jari karke yaad kiya. sarkaar ne unhen dafnane ke duusare din yani kii- 25 farvari, 1967 ko "rajya shok" ghoshit kar diya tha. rajya sarkaar ke karyaalay sammaan ke nishaan ke rup main band rahe; jabaki puure rajya main sabhi sarkari bhawanon par rashtriya dhvaj aadha-mastul main udaaya.
 shokiyon kii sankhya itani adhik thi ki hyderabad kii sadakon or footpath toote hue chudiyon se bhare hue the. kyonki telugana reeti-rivaajon ke mutabik, mahilaon ne apane kareebi rishtedaar kii maut par apni chudiyon ko tod diya karti hai.
 telugana rajya ke pratham mukhyamantri ke chandrashekhar raav ne 2018 main 'hazoor e nizaam' ko yaad karte hue nizaam shahi ko lekar 'faili bhrantiyaan' ko dur karne ke liye itihaas dobaaraa likhane kii baat kii hai.
 nism ke yogadan kii suchi kaa ullekh karte hue, ke.si.aar ne kaha, yadi hamaare desh main nizaam jaise mantri hote to aaj bharat ek behatar desh hota . sattarudh tiares party ne unhen aadhunik hyderabad ke nirmaataa ke rup main sammanit kiya or vruttachitron ke saath baahar aakar bataaya ki nizaam ke samay kaa hyderabad duniya ke anya pramukh shaharon se kaise aage tha.
