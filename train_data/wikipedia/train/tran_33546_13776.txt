 kalyanji-aanandji hindi filmon kii jaani maani sangeetkaar jodi he.
 kalyanji-aanandji gujarat se ek sangeetkaar jodi hai: kalyanji viraji shah or unke bhai aanandji viraji shah . ye donon 1970 ke dashak main hindi film soundtrack, vishesh rup se mardhaad par aadhaarit filmein par apane kaam ke liye janaa jaataa he.
 unke kuch behtareen filmon main "don", "bairaag", "saraswatichandra", "qurbaani", "muqadar kaa sikandar", "laavaaris", "tridev" or "safar" aadi hai. unhone "kora kagaz” ke liye sarvshereshth sangeet nirdeshak ke liye 1975 filmafere award jeeta.
