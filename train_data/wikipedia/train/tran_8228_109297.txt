 astor ghati pakistan dwaara niyantrit gilgit-baltistan kshetra ke astor zile main 2,600 meter kii oonchaai par sthit he. bharat ke nazariye se ise pakistan-adhikrut kashmir main mana jaataa he. astor ghati nanga parbat kii purv kii ore padati he. yah 120 kimi lambi he or iskaa kshetrafal lagbhag 5,092 varg kimi he. isaki mukhya vaadi se chaar choti vaadiyaan upashakhaayen banakar nikalati hai. puri astor vaadi ke daayre main lagbhag 100 gaanv based hue hai jinamein 1998 main 71,666 logon kii aabaadi thi. astor ghati main daakhil hone kaa sab se saral rasta gilgit shahar se aata he. astor ghati gilgit se qareeb 60 kimi dakshin-purv main padati he.
 garmiyon main astor kaa mausam bahut acha hota he or adhik garmi nahin padati. sardiyon main yahan bahut thand hoti he or barf padati he. har varsh ghati ke ilaaqon main ausatan 6 inch barf or ird-gird ke pahaado main ausatan 2 se 3 foot baraf padati he.
 astor kii ghati main basane vaale log shina bhashaa bolte hai, jo kashmiri bhashaa kii tarah ek dardi bhashaa he.
