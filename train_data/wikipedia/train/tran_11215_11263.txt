 pyarichand mitra lekhak, patrkaar, saanskrithik kaaryakarta tathaa udhyami the. ve bangla sahitya ke pratham upanyaaskaar the. unka chhadmanaam 'tekchand thakur' tha. ve 'yuva bengal samooh' ke ek sadsy the jisane saral bangala gadhy main likhakar bengal ke punarjagran main agraganya bhoomika nibhaai.
 unka 'alaler ghare dulaal' bahut prasiddh hua. isi parampara kaa nirvahan bankimachandra chattopadhyaay evam anya lekhakon ne kiya.
