 tamil nadu police tamil nadu kaa ek anubhag he, jo rajya main kanoon evam suraksha vyavastha kii dekharekh main sanlagn he. shahar kii police ke adhyaksh police ayukta, chennai hai or prashasnic niyantran rajya grih mantralay ke paas he. is vibhaag main 36 up-bhaag or 121 police-stesan he. shahar kha yatayaat chennai city trephic police dwaara niyantrit hota he. mahanagar ke upnagar chennai metropolitan police ke adheen aate hai, evam bahari jele kanchipuram evam tiruvallur police vibhaagon ke antargatt hai.
