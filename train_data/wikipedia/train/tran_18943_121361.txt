 scotland nivasee jaan nepier dwaara pratipaadit laghuganak ek aisi ganitiya yukti he jiske prayog se gananaaon ko chota kiya ja saktaa he. iske prayog se guna or bhaag jaisi jatil prakriyaaon ko jod or ghataane jaisi apekshaakrit saral kriyaaon main badal diya jaataa he. computer or calculator ke aane ke pehle jatil ganitiya gananaayen laghuganak ke sahare hi kii jaatin thim.
 ganit main kisi die hue aadhaar par kisi sankhya kaa laghuganak vah sankhya hoti he jisko us aadhaar ke upar ghaat lagane se uskaa maan di hui sankhya ke baraabar ho jaay. udhaaharan ke liye, 10 aadhaar par 100000 kaa laghuganak 5 hoga kyonki aadhaar 10 par 5 ghaat lagane se uskaa maan 100000 ho jaataa he.
 arthath kisi sankhya x, aadhaar b or ghaataank n, ke liye
 yah paribhaasha tabhi vaid he jab aadhaar b, 1 ke alaava koi anya dhanatmak vaastavik sankhya ho, arthath b> 0 y b ≠ 1, x koi bhi dhanatmak vaastavik sankhya ho tathaa n koi bhi vaastavik sankhya ho .
 prayek laghuganak kaa aadhaar hona aavashyak he. bhinn bhinn aadhaaron ke liye ek hi sankhya ke bhinn bhinn laghuganak hote hai. saadhaarant: aadhaar ke liye do sankhyaon kaa vyavhaar hota he, jinke anusaar laghuganak kii do pranaliyaan banaai gai hai.
 prakritik pranali main laghuganak kaa aadhaar ek aparimey sankhya e maani jaati he. iske avishkaarak jon nepier ke naam par aise laghuganakon ko 'neperiya laghuganak' bhi kehete hai. e kaa maan ek anant shrenee dwaara vyakt hota he or lagbhag 2.7182818...... ke baraabar he. ucch ganit ke saidhantik kaaryon ke liye isi pranali kaa upyog hota he.
 duusari pranali ke avishkaarak henry brig hai. is pranali main laghuganak kaa aadhaar 10 he. ise 'saadhaaran laghuganak' kehete hai. yah vyaavhaarik prayogon ke liye upyukt he.
 pratilghuganak : yadi log a = b to b ko a kaa pratilaghu kehete hai. jaise, log 39.2 = 1.5933, to antilog 1.5933 = 39.2
 jab x or b dono dhanatmak vaastavik sankhyaayein hon to, logb kaa maan ek adwitiya vaastavik sankhya hoti he. aadhaar b kaa nirpeksh maan 0 yaa 1 ko chhodkar kuch bhi ho saktaa he. aadhaar ke rup main praayah 10, e yaa 2 ko liya jaataa he jinke apane-apane upyog-kshetra hai. vaastavik sankhyaon tathaa samishra sankhyaon ke laghuganak paribhaashit hai.

 kisi sankhya ke laghuganak ke purnank bhaag ko purnash or dashamalav bhaag ko apurnansh kehete hai. udhaaharan ke liye log10 = 3.66048 hota he. 3.66048 main 3 purnansh or 0.66048 apurnansh he.
 kisi sankhya ke laghuganak kaa purnansh gyaat karne kaa niyam nimnlikhit he :
 apurnansh gyaat karne kaa niyam nimnlikhit hai :
 apurnansh sankhya ke maan or usamen vyavahrut ankon ke kram par nirbhar karata he. yadi do sankhyaon main ek hi prakaar ke ank ek hi kram main vyavahrut hon or keval dashamalav bindu kaa sthaan bhinn ho, to un sankhyaon ke apurnansh ek hi honge, kyonki apurnansh sankhya main dashamalav bindu ke sthaan par nirbhar nahin hota he. udhaaharan ke liye 4538 or 45.38 dono ke apurnansh samaan honge yadhyapi dono ke purnansh alag-alag honge.
 scotland nivasee jon nepier tathaa switzraland ke just burgi ne swatantra rup se laghuganak kaa avishkaar kiya. in donon ke laghuganak ek duusare se bhinn the tathaa prakritik laghuganak or saamaanya laghuganak bhi bhinn the. nepier kaa laghuganak 1614 i. main ednavar main miriffisi logerythmoram kenonis diskriptio sheershak ke antargat prakashit hua. 1620 i. main preg main just burgi kaa laghuganak arithemetice under geometrice progress tebulen sheershak ke antargat prakashit hua. is samay tak saare europe main nepier ke laghuganak kaa prachaar ho chukaa tha. unke siddhant evam pariklan paddati kaa purna ullekh, unki pustak mirifisi logrithmoram kenonis construcsio, main miltaa he, jo unki mrityu ke do varsh pashchaat 1619 i. main prakashit hui. dablue.aar. macdonald ne iskaa angreji main anuwaad 1889 i. main kiya. 1614 i. ke nepirian laghuganak tathaa prakritik laghuganak kaa parsparik sambandh nimna dhang se vyakt kiya jaataa he :
 nepier ne ikai ke laghuganak kaa maan shunya nahin mana tha. falswaroop inke siddhant ke anusaar, bina sanshodhan ke ladhuganak se sangat sameekaran bal = na sambhav nahin tha.
 1620 i. ke burgi ke prakaashan main pratilghuganak kii sarini he. isamen laghuganak laal or sankhyaayein kaale rang main chhapi hai. inkaa grundelikhe uterrikht 1856 i. main prakashit hua, jisamen ikai ke laghuganak ko shunya mana gaya he. burgi kii sarini kii kuch panktiyaan udaahranarth neeche di gai he :
 laal--- 0--- 10--- 20--- ........
 kaali--- 100000000--- 100010000--- 100020001--- ........
 pehali pankti samaantar shrenee he. duusari pankti gunottar shrenee he, jiskaa saamaanya anupaat 1.0001 he. duusari pankti ke ant ke aath ank dashamalav ank maane gaye hai or kaale rang main vyakt kiye gaye hai. yadi 1.0001 kaa laghuganak 10 he, to spasht he ki iskaa aadhaar = 1.00000999 ....... he. laghuganak ke aadhaar ke sambandh main burgi kaa gyaan nepier se adhik prathit nahin hota.
 jon wolis ne 1685 i. tathaa bernuli ne 1694 i. main laghuganak se sangat sameekaran bal = na kaa anumaan kiya. is vichaar par aadhaarit laghuganak kaa ullekh 1742 i. se miltaa he. iskaa varnan gardinurs tebulls ov logarithams kii bhoomika main miltaa he. iskaa shrey wiliam jomes ko diya jaataa he.
 pracheen bharat main moolbhoot laghuganak kaa upyog 'shatkhandgam' main dekhne ko miltaa he. shatkhandgam kii dhawala tikaa main ardhachchhed, trayached, chaturchhed aadi shabdon kaa prayog kiya gaya he jo bilkul laghuganak kii kriya jaisaa hi he. udhaaharan ke liye 32 kaa ardhachchhed 5 he jo bataata he ki 32 ko baar-baar aadha karen to 5-baar main 1 aa jaataa he. isi tarah 81 kaa trayached 4 he kyonki 81 ko baar-baar tihaai karen to 4-baar main 1 aa jaataa he. dhyaan dene yogya he ki ye kriyaayen kramshah log2, log3 aadi jaisi hi hai.
 saadhaaran laghuganak kaa vikaas jon nepier or henry brig ke sammilit prayaas kaa fal he. iskaa ullekh brig ke 'erithemetica logrithmika' main he.
 yadi r vrutt kii trigya he, to brig kaa suzav tha ki laghu r = 0 or laghu = 1010. nepier ke anusaar laghu 1 = 0, laghu r = 1010. kuch samay ke pashchaat laghu r = 1010 ke sthaan par laghu 10 = 1 kaa vyavhaar hona aarambh hua. brig ke 1624 i. ke prakaashan main 1 se 20,000 or 90,000 se 1,00,000 ke laghuganak kaa dashamalav ke 14 sthaan tak kaa ullekh he. 20,000 se 90,000 ke laghuganak adriain blaak dwaara nikaale gaye. brig kii 1624 i. kii sarini main kairecteristic shabd kaa ullekh he. 1693 i. main jon wolis ne apni bijganit kii pustak main mentisa shabd kaa prayog kiya he.
 sarvapratham prakritik laghuganak kaa ullekh nepirs discriptio main miltaa he, jo edward rhite dwaara anuvaadit 1618 i. ke angreji sanskaran main jon spadills new logarithams ke 1622 i. ke sanskaran main 1 se 1000 tak kii laghuganak sarini he. ye sab prakritik laghuganak he, keval dashamalav bindu lupt he. jusatze zu den logarithemishen and trigonometrisen tebilen main 1770 i. main johain hikerikh labert ne dashamalav ke saat sthaan tak 1-100 ke prakritik laghuganak prakashit kiye. dashamalav ke 48 sthaan tak, 1778 i. main wallphram ne 1-10000 ke laghuganak je.si. shultses samlung main prakashit kiye.
 yadi laghu10 a+laghu10 ba kaa maan gyaat ho, to bina a or ba kaa maan gyaat kiye bahudhaa laghu10 kaa maan gyaat karne kii aavashyaktaa padati he. is uddeshy se 1803 i main zikhini lonali ne ek nae prakaar kii laghuganak sarini nikaali. isi prakaar kii, dashamalav ke 5 sthaan tak yatharth, laghugunak sarini gouse ne 1812 i. main prakashit kii. ise gousiya laghuganak kehete hai.
 jon nepier, henry brig, jemy gregory, abraham sharp tathaa anya ganitagyon ne bhinn-bhinn paddatiyon kaa upyog laghuganak sarini ke nirmaan main kiya he. nicholas mercator ne 1668 i. main laghu kii anant shrenee praapt kii :
 kintu sanganan main yah adhik laabhprad nahin he. 1695 i. main jon walis ne nimnlikhit anant shrenee kaa prayog kiya :
 is shrenee kii abhisruti shighratar he. 1794 i. main ji.epa. bhegaa dwaara likhit thisoras main x = −1 maanakar shrenee kii abhisruti adhik shighratar kar di gai he.
 saadhaarant: sarini ke upyog main anupaati anshasiddhant kii sahaayta lee jaati he.
 saamne ke chitra main op-amp kaa output, input sanket ke laghuganak ke samaanupati hota he. aise pravardhak kaa upyog vahaan kiya jaataa he jahaan input sanket main parivartan bahut adhik hota ho kintu output main simit parivartan chaahate hon. isse sanket kaa gatiya paraas sikod di jaati he.
 laghuganak kaa prayog aise samikarnon ko hal karne ke liye kiya jaataa he jinamein agyaat raashi ghaat ke rup main ho. chunki laghuganakeey falan kaa avkalaj bahut saral falan hota he, isaliye inkaa upyog intigral nikaalane main hota he. laghuganak, ghaataank tathaa karani - ye teenon aapas main bahut ghanishtataa se jude hue hai. sameekaran bn = x, main b kaa maan karani dwaara nikaala ja saktaa he; n kaa maan laghuganak dwaara nikaala ja saktaa he tathaa x kaa maan ghaat dwaara.
 vigyaan main bahut si rashiyaan duusari raashiyon ke laghuganak ke rup main vyakt kii jaatin hai. isaki adhik vistrut suchi ke liye laghuganakeey paimaana dekhen.
