 chandrashekhar vaajapeyi 19veen shatabdi ke hindi kavi hai.
 inkaa janm san. 1855, pus shukla 10, ko mojababad main hua tha. inke pita maniram vaajapeyi bhi achche kavi the. inke guru asani ke karnesh mahapaatr the, jo "karnabharan", "shrutibhushan" or bhupabhushan" naamak granthon ke rachayita karnesh se bhinn 19veen shatee main rahe honge. 22 varsh kii umr main inhone darbhanga kii yatra kii. vahaan saat varsh bitaakar ye jodhpur ke raja maansingh, patialadhish karmsingh or maharaj narendrasingh ke aashray main rahe. inkaa sharirpaat san. 1932 vi. main hua.
 inaki 10 rachanaayen kahi jaati hai-
 inamen sarvaadhik mahatav kii rachana "hammirahath" he, jisapar kavi kii kirti avlambit he. isamen ranthambor ke raja hammir or samrat alauddin ke yuddh kaa varnan badi hi ospurn style main kiya ga he. iskaa pradhan ras vir he. varanasi ke lahari book depo se yah prakashit bhi ho chukaa he. rasikavinod nayikabhed or rason ke varnanan kaa granth he.
 vir, shringaar or bhakthi teenon rason kaa acha paripaak inaki rachanaaon main miltaa he. isiilie acharya ramchandra shukla ne likhaa he ki "utsaah kii, mang kii vyanjana jaisi chalati, swaabhaavik or joardaar bhashaa main inhone kii he vaise dhang se karne main bahut hi kam kavi samarth hue hai. viraras varnan main is kavi ne bahut hi sundar saahitiyik vivek kaa parichay diya he" . kavi kaa apni saahitiyik bhashaa par puraa adhikaar he. usamen vyavastha, pravaah or rasaanukool utkrisht padvinyas bhi paaya jaataa he. prasangvidhan purvavarti kaviyon kaa sa hi he. bahul anupras yojana rasabaadhak na hokar rasopakari siddh hui he.
