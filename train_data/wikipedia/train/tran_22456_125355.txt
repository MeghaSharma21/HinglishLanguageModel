 manushyon kii anuvanshiki main matruvansh samooh bil4 yaa mitocandria-diene hapalogrup L4 ek matruvansh samooh he. is matruvansh samooh ke sadsy striyon or purushon kii taadaad zyaada nahin he, lekin ve purvi africa or africa ke sing ke kai samudaayon main milte hai. tanzania ke hadaza samudaay ke 60-83% log or sandave samudaay ke 48% log iske vanshaj hai. vaigyanikon ke mutabiq aaj se hazaron saal pehle jis stri se is matruvansh kii shuruyaat hui vah purvi africa kii vaasi thi.
 angrezi main "vansh samooh" ko "hapalogrup", "pitruvansh samooh" ko "waahe chromosome hapalogrup" or "matruvansh samooh" ko "amtidiene hapalogrup" kehete hai.
