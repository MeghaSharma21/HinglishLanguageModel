 vashishthiputra palumavi ek satvaahan samraat bane jo satvaahan samraat gautamiputra shatakarni kaa putr tha. gautamaputra satkarni ke baad varsh 132 isvi main vah shanivaahan kaa raja bana
 apane shasankaal ke dauraan, kshatrap ne narmada kii bhoomi uttar or uttari konkan main le lee. pulumaavi or rudradaaman ke bich do baar yuddh hua . in donon yudhon main, rudraman ne vashishthiputra palumavi ko haraaya lekin unki beti vastiti ke bete shatakarni dwitiya ko iske kaaran samjhauta kiya gaya tha. vaishaliputra paloomawai apane swayan ke mukhauta ke saath chaandi ke sikke le aae the.
