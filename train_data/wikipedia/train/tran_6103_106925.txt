  raajnneeti praveshdwar
 20 sitambar 2015 ko jari hue nae samvidhan ke anusaar nepal ko 7 pradeshon main baanta gaya he. sabhi pradeshon ke jilon ko milakar philhal 75 jile hai. 3 nae jile banaaye ja sakate hai.
 nepal main jile dwitiya str ke prashasnic vibhaag hai, jo pradeshon main vibhajit hai. nepal main ab 78 jile hai jo 7 pradeshon main vyavasthit hai. prayek jila jila samanvay samiti ke adheen prashasit hai.
 sthaneey tah nirvachan ain 073 ke anusaar ab gounpalika or nagarpalika main nirvaachit pramukh, upapramukh, ward adhyaksh or sadsy sabhi jilla samanvay samiti ke pramukh ke ummidavaar ke rup main khadaa ho sakate hai.
 jilla samanvay samiti ke pramukh or upapramukh chune jane ke liye matdaan karne kaa adhikaar sirf grampalika or nagarpalika ke pramukh or upapramukh ko hoga niyam main aisi vyavastha rakhi gai he.
 jile nagarpalika or grampalika main vibhajit hai. puure nepal main 744 grampalika or nagarpalika hai.
 1816 main raja rajendra bir bikram shah] or pradhanmantri bhimsen thapa ke samay nepal 10 jilon main banta hua tha. dudhkosi nadi se purv ke saare kshetra ek jila dhankuta ke tahat aata tha.
 pradhanmantri bir shamsher rana ke shashan ke samay nepal kul 32 jilon main banta hua tha. pahari kshetra ke 20 jile thea or tarai kshetra main 12 jile thea. nepal do kshetra purvi nepal or pashchim nepal main bante thea. palpa or dhankuta in donon kshetron ke centre thea. ye donon kshetra kehalaate the. tarai kshetra main jile tahsil kehalaate thea.
 dhankuta centre ke tahat 6 jile thea:-
 13 april 1961 se pehle nepal main 35 jile thea.1961 main raja mahendra dwaara nepal ko 14 anchal or 75 jilon main baanta gaya.
 1972 main raja vikram ke dwaara nepal 4 vikaas kshetron main baanta gaya, lekin 1980 main nepal kaa sabase pashchimi vikaas samiti kshetra ko do hisson main baant diya gaya. madhya-pashchimanchal or sudur-pashchimanchal kshetron main.
