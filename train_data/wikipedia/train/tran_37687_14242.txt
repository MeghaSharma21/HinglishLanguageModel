 275
  raajnneeti praveshdwar
 pratinidhi sabha nepal ke dwisadaneey sangheey sansad kaa nichalaa sadan yaa lok sabha he, upree sadan ko rashtriya sabha kaha jaataa he. sadan kii rachana or shakti nepal ke samvidhan ke bhaag 8 or bhaag 9 main ullekith he. is sadan main kul 275 sadasyon praavdhaan he; 165 ekal-sadsy nirvachan dwaara first-past-da-post voting pranali dwaara chune jate hai or 110 anupaatik chunaav prakriya ke tahat chune jate hai. jahaan matadaata puure desh ko ekal chunaav nirvachan kshetra samaj kar raajnneetik dalon ke liye matdaan karte hai. 
 jab tak ki sabha bhang nahin kar diya jaataa, pratinidhi sabha ke sadasyon kaa kaarykaal 5 varshon kaa hota he. pradhanmantri ko karyaalay main niyukt hone ke liye pratinidhi sabha ke sadasyon ke dwaara bahumat kaa samarthan hasil karnaa hota he.
