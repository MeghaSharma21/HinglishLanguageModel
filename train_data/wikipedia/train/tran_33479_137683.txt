 paralampic khel ek pramukh antarrashtriya bahu-khel pratiyogita he jisamen vibhinn prakaar ki viklaangata se grast vyakti bhaag lete hai.
 paralampic games athleton kii ek badi antarrashtriya multi-sport event he jisamen asuvidhajanak mansapeshiyon kii shakti, andolan kii niskriy niskriy seemaa, ang kii kami, pair lambaai antar, laghu str, hypertonia, etexia, athetosis, drushti vikaar or bauddhik haani. sheetakaaleen or samar paralampic games hai, jo 1988 ke greeshmakaleen khelon main tseol, dakshin korea ke baad se sambandhit olympic khelon ke turant baad yojit kiye jate hai. sabhi paralampic games antarrashtriya paralampic cameti dwaara shasit hote hai.
 paralampix 1948 main british dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke diggajon kii ek choti sabha se ubhara he jo 21 veen sadi kii shuruyaat main sabase badi antarrashtriya khel aayojanon main se ek ban gaya he. paralampix 400 athleton se 1960 main 23 deshon se viklaangata ke saath landan 2012 khelon main 100 se adhik deshon ke hajaaron pratiyogiyon ke liye ubhara he. paralampian gaiyr-aksham olympic athleton ke saath samaan upachaar ke liye prayaas karte hai, lekin olympic ke bich ek bada dhanrashi antar he or paralimpics athleton.
 paralampic khelon ko olympic khelon ke samanaantar main vyavasthit kiya jaataa he, jabaki iosi-manyata praapt vishesh olympic vishwa khelon main bauddhik viklaangata vaale athlete shaamil hote hai, or deflempix main bahere athlete shaamil hote hai.
 paralimpics athleton kii vibhinn prakaar kii viklaangataaon ko dekhte hue, athlete pratispardhaa karne vaali kai shreniyan hai. sweekaarya viklaangata das yogya haani prakaaron main vibhajit ho jaati he. shreniyan kharaab mansapeshi shakti, andolan kii niskriy niskriy seemaa, ang kii kami, pair kii lambaai antar, laghu str, hypertonia, etexia, athetosis, drushti vikaar or bauddhik haani hai. in shreniyon ko vargeekaran main vibhajit kiya jaataa he, jo khel se khel main bhinn hote hai.
