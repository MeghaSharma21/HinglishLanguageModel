 mumbai, bharat
 newclear power korporation of india limited ), bharat sarkaar ke parmaanu urja vibhaag ke prashasnic niyantran ke tahat ek saarvajanik kshetra kaa upkram he. sitambar 1987 main company adhinium 1956 ke antargat is company ko saarvajanik limited company ke taur par panjikrut kiya gaya tha. is company kaa uddeshy parmaanu urja adhinium-1962 ke tahat bharat sarkaar kii yojanaayen or kaaryakramon ke anusaran main bijli kaa utpaadan karne hetu parmaanu bijli ghar kaa parichaalan tathaa nabhikeey shakti pariyojnaayen karyanvit karnaa he.
 enpisiiel, parmaanu urja vibhaag ke saath gyaapan patr hastakshrit karanevaali company he. vartmaan main enpisiiel 4120 emdblyui kii kul santhaapit kshamta ke saath 17 nabhikeey urja sanyantr kaa prachaalan kar rahaa he jisamen 5 riector nirmaanadhin he; donon kii kul kshamta 2660 emdblyui he. enpisiel ko 285 riector varsh se adhik varsh surakshit tareeke se newcleear power plant kaa prachaalan karne kaa anubhav praapt he. “pehle sangraksha phir utpaadan” ke adarsh ke saath enpisiiel apane plant kaa parichaalan karata he.
 enpisiiel ne eks yojana main kariban 90 arab unit bizli kaa utpaadan kiya he joki tay kiye gayi lakshya se 10% adhik tha or 1300 emdblyui kshamta ke lakshya ke samaksh 1180 emdblyui kaa lakshya praapt kiya jisase jaahir he ki tay kiye gayi kshamta se 91% adhik badhottaree kii he.
 sarkaar dwaara nijee companiyon ko parmaanu urja ke kshetra main aane kii anumati dene ke faisle ke baad se nigam apane karmchaariyon ko nijee companiyon dwaara pralobhan diye jane kii samasya se jujh rahaa he.
