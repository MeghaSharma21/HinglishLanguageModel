 easter dweep ;, dakshinpurv prashanth mahaasaagar main polinesian trikon ke sabase dakshinpurv bindu par sthit ek polinesian dweep he. chile ke is vishesh kshetra kaa 1888 main chile main vilay kiya gaya. easter dweep apni 887 movai badi murtiyon ke liye prasiddh he, jinhen pracheen rapanui logon ne banaaya tha. yah ek vishwa dharohar sthal he or dweep kaa adhiktar bhaag rapa nui rashtriya udyaan ke antargat aata he. aadhunik samay main dweep ko sansadhano ke aavashyaktaa se adhik dohan se hone vaali saanskrithik or paryaavarniya haani ke ek jeevant udhaaharan ke taur par dekha jaataa he, haalaanki ab is siddhant ko nrivanshavigyaniyon or puratatvavidon se kadi chunauti mil rahi he, unke matanusaar europea upniveshkon dwaara laai gayi bimaariyaan or dasavyapaar dwaara san 1800 ke shatak dauraan mulnivaasiyon kii sankhya main ai bhayankar kami kaa, paryaavarniya giravat kii tulana main bahut adhik saamaajik prabhaav padaa or europea upniveshkon dwaara apane saath laaye gayi jaanwaron, pehle chuuhe or phir bhedon kii vajah se dweep kii mul vanaspati ko bahut adhik nuksaan hua or dweep 1930-1960 ke bich lagbhag vanviheen ho chalaa tha. antarnihit dweep kaa bhuvigyaan, vilupt jwalamukhiyon main se ek he.
