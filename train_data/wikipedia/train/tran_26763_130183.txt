 meitei yaa mitei purvottar bharat ke manipur rajya kaa bahusankhyak samudaay he. ve manipur ke mul nivasee hai isliye unhen kabhi-kabhi 'manipuri' bhi kaha jaataa he. dharmik drushti se adhiktar meitei hindu hai. inaki manyataon main 'sanmaahi' naamak vishwaas-paddati bhi shaamil he jisamen ojhapratha ke kuch tatv hai. meitei samudaay ke ek mahatvapurn purvaj ke bare main manyata he ki ve paakhangba ke rup main prakat hue the.
 meitei log manipuri bhashaa bolte hai jo tibeti-burmi bhashaa-parivaar kii sadsy he.
