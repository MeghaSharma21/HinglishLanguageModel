 maanchitron ke sangrah ko maanchitrawali yaa atlas kehete hai. maanchitrawali main vishwa kii bhugolic or rajnatik suchnaayen hoti hai kintu vishwa ke vibhinn bhaagon kii saamaajik, dharmik or aarthik, bhashaai jaankariyon vaale atlas bhi banaae jate hai. ab to dharti ke alaava anya grahon ke atlas bhi banane lage hai. pehle atlas keval kaagaj par aalekhith soochna ke rup main hote the kintu ab electronic evam anya swaroopon main bhi atlas uplabdh hai.
