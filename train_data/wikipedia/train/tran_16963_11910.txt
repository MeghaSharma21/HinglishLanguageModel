 eliza cook chartist andolan se judi ek angreji lekhak or kavi thi. vah mahilaon ke liye raajnneetik aazaadee kii samarthak thi, or shiksha ke madhyam se swayan sudhaar kii vichaardhaara main vishwaas karti thi, jise usne "lavelinga ap" kaha tha. isse wo england or america donon main shramik varg kii janata ke bich had lokapriy ho gai.
 eliza cook landan road, southwark, jahaan unka janm hua tha, main rahane vaale ek braziyar ke gyaarah bacchon main sabase kam umr kii thi. jab vah nau saal kii thi, to uske pita vyavsaay se sevanivrutt hue, or parivaar horsham ke najadik saint leonard van main ek chote se khet main rahane ke liye gaya. uski maa ne kalpanasheel sahitya ke liye eliza kii aadat ko protsaahit kiya, lekin baccha lagbhag puri tarah se aap-shikshit tha. vah pandrah varsh se pehle chand likhane lageen; vastav main, unki sabase lokapriy kavitaayen, jaise ki 'ai amphlot' or 'star of glengari', kii rachana uske ladakipan main kii gai thim. 
 unka pehla dewan, leies of a wild harp, 1835 main prakashit huya, jab vah keval satraa kii thim. iske anukool swaagat se utsahit ho kar usne saptahik dispech, mahanagar patrika, nai masik patrika, or saahitiyik rajptr ko gumnaam rup se chand bhejnaa shuru kiya; wiliam jeradan ne inamen se antim main apni prashansa kii. kuch samay baad usne apane aap ko radical saptahik dispech tak simit kar diya, jahaan unka pehla yogadan hastakshar 'si' ke tahat 27 november 1836 ko prakashit hua tha., or vah agle das varshon ke liye iske prishthon par rahi. iskaa sampadak tha wiliam johnson fox or uskaa maalik tha landan kaa ek alderman james harmar. vah harmar ke nivas, ingras ebe, greenhithe, kent main ek samay ke liye rahi thi, or vahaan usne apni kuch rachanaayen ko likhaa tha.
