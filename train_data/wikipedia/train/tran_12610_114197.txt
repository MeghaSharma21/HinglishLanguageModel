 budania ek jat gotra kaa naam he jo bharat ke rajasthan or haryana praant main milte hai. rajasthan main churu, sikar, jhunjhunuun, jaipur, hanumangarh, jodhpur jilon main milte hai. haryana main bhattu kalam, darba, sirsa, adampur main milte hai.
 gotra kaa naam budiya rajya ke naam se hona bataaya gaya he. sthaneey parampara ke anusaar yah naam budhaa ji ke naam se bataaya gaya he. budhaaji kaa ek mandir chicharoli gaanv tahsil khetadi main sthit he. thakur deshraj ne megashthaniz kii pustak indica main varnit Ordabae se budiya kii pahchaan kii he.
