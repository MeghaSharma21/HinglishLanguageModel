 vaaman grah yaa bouna grah kisi akashiya pind kaa chakkar lagane vaale un pindon ko kehete hai jo grah tathaa upgrah se itar hai. 'vaaman grah' kii sangya 2006 main sweekaar kii gayi or surya kaa chakkar lagane vaale pindon ko teen shreniyon main vibhakt kiya gaya. is tarah 'vaaman grah' kii ek alag shrenee laane ke vichaar kaa kai logon ne aalochana kii jabaki kuch logon ne ise sahi nirnay kaha.
 hamaare soramandal main paanch gyaat pindon ko 'bouna grah' kii sangya di gayi he :1) yama 2) siris 3) haumeya 4) makemake 5) siris
