 itivi network ek bharat sabase bada satlite upgrah television network he. is network par media baron ramoji raav kaa ekadhikaar he. itivi shaayad ekadhikaar vaala desh kaa pehla media network he. itivi network kaa mukhyalay hyderabad ke vishwaprasiddh ramoji filmacity main sthit he. itivi network ke antargat baarah televijan channel sanchalit ho rahe hai, jo choubison ghante samachar, shiksha, manoranjan or gyaan-vigyaan se sambandhit kaarykram prasaarit kiye jate hai.jab nabbe ke dashak main bharat main satellite tivi kranti kaa janm hua, usee samay andhrapradesh ke inadu telegu dainik ne telegu bhashaa main ek channel kii shuruyaat kii. telegu se ek channel kii shuruyaat hui, magar jald hi itivi network ne desh ke vibhinn kshetreey bhashaaon main kai channelon kii shuruyaat kii. bengali, urdu, kannada, gujarati, marathi sahit hindi main chaar channel khole gaye. itivi kaa bharat main sabase bada news reporting network he, jo jila or prakhand str tak faile hue hai. itivi network kaa prabandhan newstode privet limited ke haath main he. itivi ke channel samachar ke saath saath gyaan-vigyaan or manoranjan ke kaarykram prasaarit karte hai.
 itivi kaa yah network ab tivi 18 group ke haath chalaa gaya he. aarthik akhabaaron main chhapi khabaron ke mutabik desh ke diggaj kaarobaari mukesh ambani ab itivi network ke maalik he or tivi 18 kaa usamen share he. tivi 18 ne dhire-dhire itivi ko apane haathon main sametanaa shuru kar diya he. sutron ke mutabik agle kuch maheenon main hyderabad se itivi kaa mukhyalay dilli athwa noida main sthanantarit kar diya jaayegaa.
