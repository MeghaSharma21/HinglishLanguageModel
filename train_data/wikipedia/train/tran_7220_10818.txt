 hinduon ke shakt sampradaay main saptamatririka kaa ullekh mahashakti kii sakkaari saat deviyon ke liye hua he. ye deviyaan ye hai- brahmani, vaishnavi, maheshwari, indrani, comari, vaarahi or chamunda athwa narasinhi. inhen 'maatrakaa' yaa 'maatar' bhi kehete hai. 
 kisi-kisi sampradaay main maatrikaaon kii sankhya aath bataayi gayi he. nepal main ashtamaatrikaaon kii puja hoti he. dakshin bharat main saptamatrikas hi poojit hai. kuch vidwaan unhen shaiva devi maanate hai.
