 kushaan kala kushaan vansh ke kaal main lagbhag pehali shatabdi ke ant se teesri shatabdi tak us kshetra main srujit kala kaa naam he, jo ab madhya asia, uttari bharat, pakistan or afghanistan ke kuch bhaagon ko samahit karata he. 
 kushaanon ne ek mishrit samskruti ko badhaava diya, jo unke sikkon par bane vibhinn devi devataaon, unani-roman, irani or bhartiya, ke dwaara sabase acchhee tarah samajhi ja sakati he. us kaal kii kalakritiyon ke bich kam se kam do mukhya shailigat vividhtaayen dekhi ja sakati hai: irani utpatti kii shahi kala or unani roman tathaa bhartiya strotone se mishrit buddha kala. irani utpatti kii shahi kala ke sarvshereshth udhaaharan saat kushaan raajaaon dwaara jari swarna mudraaen, shahi kushaan pratikrutiyaan or afghanistan main surkh kotal main praapt rajasi pratikrutiyaan hai. kushaan kalakritiyon kii style kathin, purohitvaadi or pratyaksh he or vah vyakti kii aantarik shakti va gunon ko rekhaankit karti he. isamen maanav sharir athwa vastralankar ke yathartwaadee chithran ke prati kam athwa lagbhag koi ruchi nahin he. yah us duusari style ke vipareet he jo kushaan kala kii gandhar va mathura shaileyon kii visheshta he.
