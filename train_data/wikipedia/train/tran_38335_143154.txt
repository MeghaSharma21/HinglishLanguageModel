 zij al-sindahind yaa zij al-sindahind al-kabir bharat main rachit ek jyotishiya granth he jisamen khagoleey pindon kii sthiti kii gananaa karne ke liye saraniyan di gayin hai. arabi-farsi main is prakaar ke jyotishiya granthon ko 'zij' kaha gaya he. 'sindahind' shabd 'siddhanth' se aayaa he jo 'siddhanth jyotish' yaa 'ganit jyotish' kaa suchak he. isaki rachana 770 i ke aasapaas baghdad ke khalipha al mansoor ke darbar main hui. al mansoor ne sanskrit se is granth ko anudit karne ke liye mohammad al-fazaari ko niyukt kiya tha.
