 aryavartt hindi kaa ek samachar patr he.
 darbhanga naresh kameshwar singh ne indian neshnal press patna se 1941 main aryavartt kaa prakaashan prarambh kiya. iske pratham sampadak pan dinesh dutt jha the. unke baad brajanandan aaazaad tathaa shrikant thakur vidyalankar iske sampadak rahe. 20 varsh ke sampaadan ke baad vidyalankar ne hindi ke is shreshth patr kii bagdor 1 june 1968 main jayakaanth mishra ko saunpi. apane ucch str ke kaaran iskaa ab rajyavyapee prasar ho rahaa he. vartmaan main hiranand jha shastri `aryavartt' ke sampadak hai. yah patr rashtravaadi niti kaa poshak hai.
