 panndit gangaaprasad agnihotri - 10 navambar, 1931 i) hindi ke sahityakar the. unhone go-sahitya ke sanvardhan liye apana sampoorn jivan laga diya.
 gangaaprasad agnihotri kaa janm nagpur ke nayapura naamak mohalle main hua tha. unke pita pandit lakshmanaprasad agnihotri uttarapradesh ke raibareli janapad ke chauhattar gram se vyapaar ke silsile main nagpur aakar bas gayi the.
