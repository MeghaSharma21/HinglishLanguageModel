 bantustan un kshetron ko kaha jaataa tha jahaan aparthid naamak rangabhed niti ke antargat dakshin africa kii shwetvarniya taanaashaahee sarkaar ne kritrim rup se kuch tathakthith deshon kii sthapana kar di thi or yah kahana armbh kar diya tha ki dakshin africa ke shyamvarni log dakshin africa ke naagarik nahin balki in chote alag deshon ke naagarik hai. dakshin africa main africa shyamvarni logon kii baari bahusankhya thi or in bantoostaanon ko banaakar shwetvarniya vyavastha is tathy ko jhuthalane kaa prayaas kar rahi thi. yahi bantustan kii niti dakshin-pashchimi africa main bhi apanaai gai jahaan dakshin africa kaa qabza tha or aaj namibia kehalaata he.
 kul milakar das bantustan dakshin africa main or das bantustan dakshin-pashchimi africa main sthapit kiye gayi. aksar yah kai chote hisson main khandit the. dakshin africa ke sivaay vishwa ke kisi bhi anya desh ne inhen manyata nahin di or inaki sthapana ko kaalon par ho rahe atyaachaar kaa hi bhaag tehraaya. 1994 main jab dakshin africa main rangabhed se mukti hui to naye samvidhan main in bantoostaanon kaa turant ant kar diya gaya. inke kshetron ko dakshin africa ke punargathit praanton main vilay kar diya gaya.
