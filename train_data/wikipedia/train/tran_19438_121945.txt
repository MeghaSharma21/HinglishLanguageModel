 pracheen bharat main gurukul ashramon ke pradhan 'kulapati' kehalaate the. ramayana kaal main vashishtha kaa brihad ashram tha jahaan raja dilip tapascharya karne gayi the, jahaan vishwamitra ko brahmatv praapt hua tha. is prakaar kaa ek or prasiddh ashram prayag main bharadwaj muni kaa tha.
 kalidas ne vasishtha tathaa kanv rishi ko 'kulapati' kii sangya di he. guptkaal main santhaapit tathaa harshavardhan ke samay main apni charmonnati ko praapt honevaale nalanda mahavihar naamak vishvavidhyalay ke kuch prasiddh tathaa vidwaan kulapatiyon ke naam hvensang ke yatravivaran se gyaat hota hai. buddha bhikshu dharmpal tathaa sheelbhadra unamem pramukh the.
 pracheen bhartiya kaal main adhyayan adhyaapan ke pradhan centre gurukul hua karte the jahaan dur dur se brahmacharee vidhyaarthee, athwa satyanveshi parivrajak apni apni shikshaaon ko purna karne jate the. ve gurukul chote athwa bade sabhi prakaar ke hote the. parantu un sabhi gurukulon ko na to aadhunik shabdaawalee main vishvavidhyalay hi kaha ja saktaa he or na un sabake pradhan guruon ko kulapati hi kaha jaataa tha. smritivachanon ke anusaar 
 spasht he, jo brahman rishi das hajaar muni vidhyaarthiyon ko annadi dwaara poshan karata hua unhen vidya padhaata tha, use hi 'kulapati' kehete the. upar uddhrut 'smritah' shabd ke prayog se yah saaf dikhaai detaa he ki kulapati ke is vishishtarthagrahan kii parampara badi puraani thi. kulapati kaa saadhaaran arth kisi kul kaa swami hota tha. vah kul yaa to ek chota or avibhakt parivaar ho saktaa tha athwa ek bada or kai chote chote pariwaron kaa samaan udgam vanshakul bhi. antevasi vidhyaarthee kulapati ke mahaan vidyapariwaar kaa sadsy hota tha or uske maansik or bauddhik vikaas kaa uttardaayitv kulapati par hota tha; vah chaatro ke shaareerik swasthya or sukh kii bhi chintaa karata tha.
 aajkal 'kulapati' shabd kaa prayog vishvavidhyalay ke 'vischancelar' ke liye kiya jaataa he.
