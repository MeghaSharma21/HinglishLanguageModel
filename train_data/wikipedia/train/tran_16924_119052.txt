 takneekee anuwaad ek vishesh prakaar kaa anuwaad he jisamen takneekee vishyon se sambandhit dastavejon kaa anuwaad karnaa hota he. udhaaharan ke liye swami kaa manual, prayokta maargdarshika aadi. isamen kaknikee vishyon ke shodhpatr, takneekee pustakon kaa anuwaad aadi bhi sammilit he. is prakaar ke anuwaad kii mukhya visheshta isamen vishisht paribhaashik shabdaawalee kaa hona he. iske atirikt sambandhit vishay kii jaankaree bhi vaanchit he anyathaa kai jagah arth kaa anarth ho saktaa he.
 takneekee anuwaad ke liye anuwaad smriti bahut upyogi he kyonki isamen prayukt shabdaawalee simit hoti he, dohraayi gayi hoti he, tathaa usamen ekarupata hoti he. takneekee anuwaad kaa takneekee sanchaar se ghanisht sambandh he.
