 unani mithkon main agamemnan  ek homareey vir he jo raja atriyas kaa putr he. vah sambhawatah aitihaasik vyakti tha. elliod main use yunaan ke ekian or mikini rajyon kaa swami kaha gaya he. sparta main uski puja 'jeus agamemnan' ke naam se hoti thi. 
 yah atriyas or irop kaa putr or menlas kaa bhai tha. pita kii hatya ke baad bhaaiyon ne sparta ke raja kii sharan lee, phir vahaan ke raja kii sahaayta se agamemnan ne pita kaa rajya pun praapt kar use badhaayaa or greece main raajaaon main pradhan ban gaya. sparta ke raja tinderes kii kanyaayein in donon bhaaiyon se byaahi thim. pashchaat menlas, tinderes kaa uttaradhikari hua or yah uskaa sahaayak. bhai ke patni hellen ke traay ke paris dwaara apharan ke pratikaar main unani raajaaon ko nimantrit kar agamemnan ne traay ke yuddh kaa netritv kiya. traay vijay ke baad swadesh lautne par uski patni ke premi augustas ne isaki hatya kar di. uski kabr mikini ke khandhiron main dikhaai jaati he, jise traay kaa punaruddhar karne vaale puravid shliman ne khod nikaali thi. par us kabr kii satyata pramanit nahin.
