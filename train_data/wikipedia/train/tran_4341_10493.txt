 krishnadas hindi ke bhaktikaal ke ashtchhaap ke kavi the. 
 unka janm 1495 i. ke aasapaas gujarat main chilotara gram ke ek kunbi patil parivaar main hua tha. bachapan se hi prakrutti badi satvik thi. jab ve 12-13 varsh ke the to unhone apane pita ko chori karte dekha or unhen giraftaar kara diya falat: ve patil pad se hataa die gaye. is kaaran pita ne unhen ghar se nikaal diya. ve bhraman karte hue braj pahunche. unheen dinon naveen mandir main shrinath ji kii murti kii pratishtha karne kii taiyaari ho rahi thi. shrinath ji ke darshan se ve bahut prabhavit hue or vallabhacharya se unke sampradaay kii diksha lee. 
 unki asaadhaaran budhimatta, vyavhaar kushalta or sanghatan yoyata se prabhavit hokar vallabhacharya ne unhen bhetia ke pad par niyukt kiya or phir sheeghra unhen shrinath ji ke mandir kaa adhikari bana diya. unhone apane is uttardaayitv kaa badi yogyata se nirvaah kiya. krishnadas ko sampradayik siddhanton kaa acha gyaan tha jiske kaaran ve apane sampradaay ke agraganya logon main maane jate the. unhone samay-samay par krishna lila prasangon par pad rachana kii jinaki sankhya lagbhag 250 he jo raag kalpadrum, raag ratnakar tathaa sampradaay ke kirthan sangrahon main uplabdh hai. 1575 or 1581 i. ke bachi kisi samay unka dehavasan hua.
