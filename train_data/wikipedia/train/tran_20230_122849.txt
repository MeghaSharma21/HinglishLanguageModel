 lhokha vibhaag, jise janavaadee ganatantr china kaa prashasan chini main shannaan vibhaag bulaata he, tibet kaa ek prashasnic vibhaag he jo vartmaan main janavaadee ganatantr china dwaara niyantrit tibet swashasit pradesh kaa hissaa he. yah kshetra tibet ke dakshin-purv main sthit he or isaki dakshini sarahadein bharat va bhutan se lagati hai. chini sarkaar ke anusaar bharat ke arunachal pradesh rajya ke kuch hisse is vibhaag kaa bhaag hai, haalanki bharat unhen apni bhoomi maanata he or unpar niyantran rakhe hue he.
