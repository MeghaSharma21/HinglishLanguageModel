 sukhwad nitishastra ke antargat naitik apekshaaon kii abhipushti karne vaala siddhant he. sukhwad ke anusaar achchhai vah he jo anand pradaan karti he yaa dukh-pidaa se chhutkaaraa dilaati he tathaa buraai vah he jo dukh-pidaa ko janm detii he. saidhantik taur par sukhwad nitishastra main prakrutwad kaa ek roopaantar he. uskaa aadhaar is vichaar main nihit he ki anand manushya kaa mukhya nirnaayak gun he, jo uske swabhaav main nihit he or uske samast kaaryaklap ko nirdhaarit karata he. siddhant ke rup main sukhwad kii utpatti pracheen kaal main hi ho gayi thi. yunaan main sukhwadi aristippas ke nitishastra ke anuyaayi rahe. sukhwad epicuras kii shiksha main apane charam shikhar par pahunchaa. sukhwad ke vichaaron ko mil tathaa bentham ke upyogetavaad main kendriya sthaan praapt he.
