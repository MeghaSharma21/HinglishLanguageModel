 agnishman aag par niyantran paakar use bujhane ke kaary ko kehete hai. adhiktar samaajon main, visheshkar shahari kshetron main, aniyantrit aag jivan or maal ke liye ek bada sankat ban sakati he or agnishmak is khatare se bachaav karte hai. vibhinn paristhitiyon main aag par kaabo paakar use band karne ke liye bahut si takneekein seekhni padati hai or un kathin paristhitiyon main jaakar unhen jhelane ke liye shaareerik-kshamta bhi zaruri he. agnishman ke liye vishesh saamaan or yantron kaa prayog bhi hota he. inamen pani, aag-nirodhak rasayan, bhinn prakaar ke agni-kavach, agnishmakon kii aag-nirodhak poshaaken, jal giraane vaale vimaan, agnishmakon ke vishesh vaahan, vakairaah shaamil hai. alag-alag tarah kii aagon ke liye agnishmak bhinn chizein prayog karte hai, masalan bijli se lagi aag ke liye pani kaa prayog nahin kiya jaataa.
