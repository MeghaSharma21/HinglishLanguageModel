 sitaaji ke prakat hone ke sthal kaa naam sitamarhi he, yah pracheen mithila me ke antargat he, lakhandeyee nadi ke pashchimi tat par sitamarhi basti he, ghere ke bheetar sitaaji kaa mandir he, paas me hi ram, lakshaman, shiv, hanuman tathaa ganesh ke mandir hai, yahaan se ek mile par punauda gaanv ke paas pakkaa sarovar he, yaheen janaki ji prithvi se utpann hui thi, paas main thakurbari he, nimivanshaj raja sirdhvaj akaal padane par sone ke hal se yajna bhoomi jot rahe the, tabhi halagr ke lagane se divya kanya utpann hui thi, yahaan urvija naamak pracheen kund he, striyon me yah tirth bahut prasiddh he.yah bihar rajya kaa ek jila he.
