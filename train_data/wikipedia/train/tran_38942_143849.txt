 china pehla hack pruf kwantum communication satelit prakshepit karegaa. 
 kwantum communication satelit may 2016 main charchaa main tha kyonki chini vigyaan academy ke anusaar china july 2016 main apana praayogic kwantum communication satelit prakshepit karegaa. 
 isse china pehla desh hoga jiske satelit antariksh se encoded soochna bhejenge jise hack nahin kiya ja saktaa. 
 is pariyojna se sanchaar vyavastha main krantikari parivartan aa sakate hai. is pariyojna main satelit prakshepan evam chaar buildingon kaa nirmaan shaamil he. iske atirikt ek antariksh kwantum teleportesan kaa bhi vikaas kiya jaayegaa. 
 kwantum satelit antariksh vigyaan ke liye samrik praathamikta kaarykram ke tahat prakshepit kiya jaayegaa. is par research kii shuruyaat china main 2011 main kii gayi. china pehle hi varsh 2015 main dark matter satelit prakshepit kar chukaa he. iske baad april 2016 main micro gravity satelit esaje-10 prakshepit kiya gaya. varsh 2016 ke madhya main antariksh main neutron taron evam blike holes ke shodh ke liye satelit choda jaayegaa.
