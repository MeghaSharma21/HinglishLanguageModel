 tata prashasnic seva tata samooh ke liye aantarik netritv or vikaas prashikshan kaa kaarykram he. yah samooh ke karmchaariyon ke liye vibhinn tata samooh kii companiyon ke prashasnic gatividhiyon kaa hissaa banane kaa mauka pradaan karata he. kaarykram 1956 main shuru kiya gaya tha or kai tata companiyon main netritv tiimon ke liye ek feader ke rup main kaary karata he.
 kaarykram jearady tata ne shuru kiya tha taaki tata samooh apane kuleen prabandhan kader ke vikaas ke liye tieyes par nirbhar rah sake.tieyes ne samooh kii companiyon ke mukhya kaaryakari adhikariyon main se zyaadaatar ko viksit karne main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai he.
