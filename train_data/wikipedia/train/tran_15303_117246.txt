 patric henry sanyukt rajya america ke aarmabhik daur ke ek rajaneta or vaktaa the jinhen kabhi-kabhi us desh ke rashtrapitaon main bhi gina jaataa he. unhone america ke virginia rajya main britain ke khilaaf chalaaye gaye swatantraaa sangram kaa netritv kiya tha or san 1776 se 1779 tak or phir 1784 se 1786 tak ke kaalon main us soobe ke rajyapal bhi rahe the. unhen apane die gaye "mujhe azaadi do yaa maut do" ke naare ke liye yaad kiya jaataa he.
 patric henry ke paariwarik naam 'henry' ko kabhi-kabhi devanagari main 'henry' bhi likhaa jaataa he, lekin dhyaan dijiye kii isamen 'ha' par lagi 'ji' kii maatra kaa uccharan 'e' kii maatra se zaraa bhinn he.
