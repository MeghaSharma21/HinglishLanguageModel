 shakti samant ek prasiddh chitra nirmaataa evam nirdeshak the.
 shakti samant bharat ke suprasiddh nirmaataa or nirdeshak rahe hai. shakti samant kaa janm 13 janvari 1926 ko pashchim bengal ke vardhaman main hua tha. unhone dehradun or kalakatta vishvavidhyalay se oochch shiksha grahan kii. 9 april 2009 ko unhone mumbai main antim saans lee. 83 varshiya shakti samant pichley kuch dinon se biimaar chal rahe the. shakti samant ne kati patang, amar prem or aradhana jaisi superhit filmon kaa nirmaan kiya. wo paanch varshon tak film produsron ke sangh ke adhyaksh bhi rahe.
 apane filmi jivan ke aarambhik daur main wo abhinayta bananaa chaahate the or isi sapane ko lekar mumbai aae. unhen isamen turant safalataa nahin mili or unhone shikshak kii naukari kii. varsh 1948 main unhone sahaayak nirdeshak ke rup main bollywood main pravesh kiya. unke nirdesan main bani pehali film thi bahu jo 1954 main release hui.
 varsh 1957 main unhone shakti films naamak production house banaaya. is production house kii pehali film haavda brij thi. shakti samant ne 43 feechar filmon kaa nirdesan kiya, jinamein china toun, kashmir kii kali or en evening in paris jaisi filmein shaamil thi. unhen aradhana, anuraag or amaanush kii apaar safalataa ke baad filmafere puraskaron se sammanit kiya gaya tha.
