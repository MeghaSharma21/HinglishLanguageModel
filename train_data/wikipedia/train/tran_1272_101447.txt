 yuddh tathaa rashtriya suraksha ke sandarbh main manobal-hrasikaran manovaigyanik yuddh kii vah prakriya he jiskaa uddeshy shatru ke sainikon kaa manobal giraanaa tathaa unhen yuddh karke parajeet karne ke bajaay unhen peeche hatane, aatmasamarpan karne, yaa bhaag jane ke liye utsahit karnaa hota he.
