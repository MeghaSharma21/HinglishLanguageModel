 ruapehu parvat, jise keval ruapehu bhi kaha jaataa he, newzealand ke uttar dweep ke taoopo jwalamukheey kshetra ke dakshini chor par sthit ek sakriya mishrit jwalamukhi he. yah taoopo jhil ke dakshini tat se 40 kimi kii duuri par tongariro rashtriya udyaan main virajman he. uttar dweep ki sabhi himaniyaan isi parvat kii dhalaano par sthit hai.
 ruapehu duniya ke sabase sakriya jwalamukhiyon main se ek he or newzealand kaa sabase bada jwalamukhi he. yah uttar dweep kaa sabase unchaa bindu he or iske teen pramukh shikhar hai: 2797 meter unchaa tahurangi, 2755 meter unchaa te heuheu or 2751 meter unchaa paretetatonga . in shikharon ke darmiyaan ek gaharaa jwalamukheey cretar he jahaan se samay-samay par visphot hota he. visphoton ke bich ke shant kaal main isamen pani bharne se ek jwalamukheey jhil ban jaati he.
 maori bhashaa main 'ruapehu' kaa arth 'visphoton vaala gaddha' he.
