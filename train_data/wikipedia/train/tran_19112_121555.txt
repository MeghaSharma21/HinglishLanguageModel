 thetum /ˈtɛtʊm/, jise tetun naam se bhi jaanate hai, ek austronishian bhashaa he jo ki timore dweep par boli jaati he. yah indonesia ke pashchim timore ke belu regency main boli jaati he tathaa purv timore main boli jaati he jahaan yah do aadhikarik bhashaaon main se ek he. yah purv timore main creole rup timore dili ke rup main boli jaati he. iske creole rup kii vyakaran saral he tathaa isapar portugali bhashaa kii chhap dikhti he.
