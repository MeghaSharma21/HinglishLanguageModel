 jiangshi janavaadee ganraajya china ke dakshin-purvi bhaag main sthit ek praant he. iskaa vistaar uttar main yangtse nadi ke maidani ilaaqon se lekar dakshin main pahari kshetra tak he. jiangshi kii rajdhani naanchang shahar he. is praant kaa kshetrafal 1,66,900 varg kimi he, yani bharat ke urisa rajya se zaraa zyaada. san 2010 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi 4,45,67,475 thi or yah bhi bharat ke urisa rajya se zaraa zyaada thi. jiangshi kaa naam tang rajvansh ke kaal se he jab ise 'jiangananashidao' kaha jaataa tha, jiskaa matlab 'pashchimi jiyangnan praant' he . is praant se gaan nadi nikalati he isliye iskaa chini bhavchitron main sankshipt namakan '赣' he . is praant main prasiddh poyang jhil bhi he, isliye ise kabhi-kabhi 'gompotaiti' bhi kehete hai, jiskaa arth he 'gaan nadi or po jhil kii mahaan bhoomi'.
 jiangshi apane padosi praanton se pichhadaa hua he. yahan chaaval kii kheti par jor he or ise kabhi-kabhi 'chaaval or machhlee kaa desh' bhi kaha jaataa he. china main jiangshi ke logon ko kam mahanti or aaraam-pasand bhi samajha jaataa he. yahan 'gaan chini' naam kii bhashaayen boli jaati hai or praant ke dakshini ek-tihaai ilaake main hakkaa chini boli jaati he. jiangshi kii apni vibhinn chini nautanki kii style bhi he. yahan kaa khaanaa mirchon par jor detaa he or achaaron or sirke kaa bahut prayog hota he. mana jaataa he ki jiangshi main puure china ke sabase achche chini mitti ke bartan banate hai.
