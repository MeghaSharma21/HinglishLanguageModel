 shri kuppahali sitaaramayya sudarshan rashtriya swayamsevak sangh ke paanchaven sarasanghachaalak the. march 2009 main shri mohan bhagwat ko chhathvaan sarasanghachaalak niyukt kar swechha se padmukt ho gayi. 15 sitambar 2012 ko apane janmasthan raipur main 81 varsh kii avasthaa main inkaa nidhan ho gaya.
 shri ke es sudarshan kaa janm raipur main ek kannada bhashi parivaar main hua tha. unhone jabalpur ke rajkeey ejiniyari mahavidhyalay se doorsanchaar praudyogiki main snaatak kii oopaadhi arjeet kii. 16 janvari 2009 ko meerut ke shobhit vishvavidhyalay ne unako 'doctor of arts' kii maanad oopaadhi se vibhooshit kiya.
