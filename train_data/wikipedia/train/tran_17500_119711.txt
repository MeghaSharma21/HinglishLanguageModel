 jwalamukheey chaap jwalamukhiyon kii ek shrunkhlaa hoti he jo do bhugolic takhton kii sanmilan seemaa main nimnaskhalit takhte ke upar ban jaati he. upar se dekhne par jwalamukhiyon kii yah shrunkhlaa ek chaap ke aakaar main nazar aati he. yadi yah kisi mahaasaagar main sthit ho to aksar yah jwalamukhi samudr sataha se upar nikalakar jwalamukheey dweep bana dete hai or yah shrunkhlaa ek dweep chaap ke rup main nazar aati he. aksar in dwipon se saamanantar ek mahasagariya gart bhi sthit hota he.
