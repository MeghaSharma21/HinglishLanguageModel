 pad : jahaan puneatma maranoparant apane aradhya devi yaa devtaa ke lok jate hai use pad kehete hai. bhagwaan shri hari kaa pad sabase adhik prasiddh he. kapot or kapotki kii katha brahmpurana main sachitr di hui he. kapot or kapotki ko bhagwaan shri hari kaa pad maranoparant praapt hota he. maan bhagwati ke pad kaa varnan shrimaddevibhagwat main diya he. bhagwaan vishnu kaa pad vaikuntha he. bhagwaan shiv kaa pad shiv bhakton ko miltaa he.
