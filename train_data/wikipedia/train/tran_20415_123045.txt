 prem das rai bharat ke sikkim democratic front se sambhddha ek raajnneetijna he. 2009 chunaavon main vah sikkim nirvachan kshetra se lok sabha ke liye chune gaye. unhen bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan evam bhartiya prabandh santhaan se dohari oopaadhi-gruhit pehla sansad sadsy hone kaa gaurav praapt he. 2002 main vah bharat se ijanhavar faillo bhi rahe.
 rai kii schuli shiksha musuri main hui. tatpashchaat unhone san 1976 main bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan kanpur se rasinic abhiyantriki main snaatak evam 1978 main bhartiya prabandh santhaan, ahmedabad se prabandhan main snatakottar diploma kiya.
