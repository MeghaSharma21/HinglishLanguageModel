 vaasu bhaganani sindhi mul ke hindi filmon ke film nirmaataa he. unki filmon main kuli nan 1, hero nan 1, bade miyaan chote miyaan, mujhe kuch kahana he, rahanaa he tere dil main, om jai jagadish or shaadi nan 1 hai. unki haal kii kuch filmein hai:- kal kisne dekha, rangarez or yangistan jisamen unhone apane bete jaiki bhaganani ko prastut kiya.
