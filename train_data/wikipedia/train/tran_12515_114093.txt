 bicheng zila yaa bai zila janavaadee ganatantr china ke shinjiyang rajya ke aqsu vibhaag kaa ek zila he. iskaa kshetrafal 15,889 varg kimi he or san 2002 kii janaganana main isaki aabaadi lagbhag 2 lakh thi. yah zila oksu vibhaag ke uttari kshetra main muzaat nadi kii ghati main tiyaan shaan parvaton se bilkul dakshin main sthit he.
 yah zila tushaari samskruti or buddha dharm se sambandhit pai gai gufaaon ke liye mashhoor he, jo muzaat nadi ke kinare sthit hai.
