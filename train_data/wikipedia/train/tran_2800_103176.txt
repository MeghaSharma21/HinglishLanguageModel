 gajendra chouhan hindi filmon ke ek abhinayta hai.gajendra singh chouhan, jinhe vyavsaayik rup se gajendra chouhan ke naam se janaa jaataa he, ek bhartiya televijan or film abhinayta he,inhe aitihaasik televijan shrinkhalaa mahabharat main yudhisthir ke paatr ke chithran ke liye janaa jaataa he. 2015 main, unhen film or televijan santhaan ke adhyaksh ke rup main niyukt kiya gaya tha.
 chouhan kaa janm dilli main 10 oktober 1956 ko hua tha. unhone akhil bhartiya ayurvigyaan santhaan se radiography main diploma kiya tha, isse pehle ki vah abhinay ke liye mumbai chale gaye.vahaan unhone roshan taneja dwaara sanchalit skool main abhinay kaa adhyayan kiya, jinhone pehle apatiai main padhaayaa tha.
