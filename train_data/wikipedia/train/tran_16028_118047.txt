 city pales kii sthapana 16veen shatabdi main armbh hui. ise sthapit karne kaa vichaar ek sant ne raja udaisingh dwitiya ko diya tha. is prakaar yah parisar 400 varshon main bane bhawanon kaa samooh he. yah ek bhavy parisar he. ise banane main 22 raajaaon kaa yogadan tha. is parisar main pravesh ke liye tikat lagata he. baadi paul se tikat lekar aap is parisar main pravesh kar sakate hai. parisar main pravesh karte hi aapako bhavy tripolia get' dikhegaa. isamen saat arq hai. ye arq un saat smavaranotsaivon kaa pratik hai jab raja ko sone or chaandi se taula gaya tha tathaa unke vajan ke baraabar sona-chaandi ko garibon main baant diya gaya tha. iske saamne kii diwaar 'agad' kehalaati he. yahaan par haathiyon kii ladaai kaa khel hota tha. is parisar main ek jagadish mandir bhi he. isi parisar kaa ek bhaag city pales sangrahalay he. ise ab sarkari sangrahalay ghoshit kar diya gaya he. vartmaan main shambhuk nivas rajpariwaar kaa nivas sthanan he. isse aage dakshin disha main 'fatah prakaash bhvan' tathaa 'shiv nivas bhawan' he. vartmaan main donon ko hotel main parivartit kar diya gaya he.
