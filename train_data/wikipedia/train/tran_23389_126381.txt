 gasharabrum 2, jise ke-4 bhi kehete hai, vishwa kaa 13vaan sarvocch parvat he. yah karakoram ke gasharabrum punjak main sthit he. rajnatik simaaon ke anusaar yah paak-adhikrut kashmir ke gilgit-blatistan kshetra or china ke kabze vaali shaxgam ghati kii seemaa par sthit he. bharat ke anusaar gilgit-blatistan or shaxgam ghati donon bharat kaa bhaag hai. gasharabrum 2 baltoro himani ke upar sthit he. gasharabrum 3 is se judaa hua he or kuch strot use gasharabrum 2 kaa ek upaparvat bataate he.
