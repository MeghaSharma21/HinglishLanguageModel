 manith jora ek bhartiya abhinayta he. jisane film main abhinay karne kii shuruuaat yash raaj films duura nirmit film band baaja baraat se kiya yah film 2010 main bani hauske baad vaki lav shagun film main mukhya bhumika main nibhaai. yah film 2016 main bani he.uske baad vah balaji telefims ke web siris pareekshan kaa maamla main najar aae or vartmaan main vah ji tivi par prasaarit ho rahe kaarykram kundali bhagya main mukhya bhumika nibhaa rahe hai.
 manith jora kaa janm - ek punjabi parivaar main 05-june-1982 main hua. vah nai dilli bharat ke rahane vaale he unka ek bahan -kanika jora or bhai- himanshu jora he.
 manith jora ko bachapan se hi abhinay karnaa bahut pasand he or ipane skul ke dauraan vah kai naatako main abhinay kiya he jiske liye logon ne inca bahut taareef kii thi. vah apni shiksha puri karne baad mumbai agaye uske kuch samay baad unhen ji tivi ek sho 12/24 karol baag se tivi par abhinay karnaa shuru kiya.
