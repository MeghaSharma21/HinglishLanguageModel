 is suchi main vishwa ke deshon ke sankhya ke aadhaar par sena or ardhsainik bal he . isamen purnakalik sena or ardhsainik bal sammilit he . 
 
 international institute for stratagic studies dwaara salanaa prakashit "da battlens balance" ke 2017 ke sanskaran se nimnlikhit suchi ko praapt kiya gaya he.
 
 
 suchi main shaamil nahin kar rahe hai, senaaon kaa abkazia, afghanistan, bhutan, uttari cyprus, maldive, monako, Nagorno-Karabakh, san marino, saint kits or nevis, saao thome or principe, somaliland, swaziland, tonga or vanuatu.
