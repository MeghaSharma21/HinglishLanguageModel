 nepalimgar
 syanja jilla nepal ke pashchimanchal vikaas kshetra ke gandki anchal main sthit ek atyaadhik urvar evam ghanaa vasti sameta hua jilla hai. is jilla kaa kshetrafal 1664 berg kimi or jansankhya karive 4 lakh hai. is jilla ke purv main tanhun, uttar main kaski, pashchim main parvat or gulmi jillaayen hai.
