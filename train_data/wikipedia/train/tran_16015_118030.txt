 shilpakala kala kaa vah rup he jo trivimiya hoti he. yah kathor padaarth, mridu padaarth evam prakaash aadi se banaaye ja sakate hai. murtikla ek atipracheen kala he.
 bharat ke vaastushilp, murtikla, kala or shilp kii jade bhartiya sabhyataa ke itihaas main bahut dur gahari prathit hoti hai. bhartiya murtikla armbh se hi yatharth rup liye hue he jisamen maanav akritiyon main praaia: patali qamar, lacheele angon or ek tarun or samvednapurna rup ko chitrit kiya jaataa he. bhartiya murtiyon main ped-paudhon or jeev jantuon se lekar asankhya devi devataaon ko chitrit kiya gaya he.
 bharat kii sindhu ghati sabhyataa ke mohenjodaron ke bade-bade jal kund pracheen murtikla kaa ek shreshth udhaaharan hai. dakshin ke mandiron jaise ki kanchipuram, madurai, shrirangam or rameswaram tathaa uttar main varanasi ke mandiron kii nakkashi kii us utkrisht kala ke chir-prachlit udhaaharan he jo bharat main samrudh hui.
 keval yahi nahin, madhya pradesh ke khajuraho mandir or urisa ke surya mandir main is utkrisht kala kaa jeeta jaagta rup he. sanchi stupa kii murtikla bhi bahut bhavy he jo teesri sadi i.pus. se hi iske aas-paas banaae gaye jangalon or torana dwaaron ko alankrita kar rahi hai. maamllapuram kaa mandir; sarnath sangrahalay ke line capital main morya kii pathar kii murti, mahatma buddha ke jivan kii ghatnaon ko chitrit karne vaali amravati or nagarjunaghonda kii vaastushilpeey murtiyon iske anya udhaaharan hai.
 hindu gupha vaastushilp kii paraakaashtha mumbai ke nikat elephanta gufaaon me dekhi ja sakati he or isi prakaar ellora ke hindu or jain shail mandir vishesh rup se aathvin shatabdi kaa kailash mandir vaastushilp kaa yah rup dekha ja saktaa he.
 itihaas ke kala khandon ke samrudh sakshya sanket karte hai ki bhartiya shilp kala ko ek samay puure vishwa main ucchtam sthaan praapt tha.
