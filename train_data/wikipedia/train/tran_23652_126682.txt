 
 manganeez ek rasinic tatv he jo rasinic najariye se sankraman dhaatu samooh kaa sadsy he. prakruti main yah shuddh rup main nahin miltaa balki anya tatwon ke saath bane yogicon main miltaa he, jinamein aksar lohe ke yogic shaamil hote hai. shuddh karne ke baad iskaa rang saleti hota he or agar ise ispaat main milaayaa jaye to ispaat zang nahin khaataa he. oxigen ke saath milakar iske jo aayan hote hai vah paramanganet kehalaate hai, or jab yah potacium jaisi kshar dhaatuon yaa kshareey parthiv dhaatuon ke saath yogic banaate hai to vah bahut hi oxikaarak hote hai . manushyon va anya jeevon ko thodi maatra main manganeez apane aahaar main zaruri hota he lekin us se adhik maatra main yah vishaiylla sabit hota he.
