 soviet sangh kaa swatatra rashtron ke rup main vighatan san 1985 ke armbh main shuru hua. soviet sangh sainik rup se bahut shaktishaalee ban gaya tha kintu gharelu aarthik sthiti bahut kharaab thi. iske alaava dhire-dhire anek kaarnon se sthiti karaab hoti chali gayi. antat: san 1991 main soviet sangh tut gaya. takhta-palat kii ek asafal koshish hui. ant main rus kii satta boris yelsin ke haath aa gayi.
