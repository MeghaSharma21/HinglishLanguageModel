 prakrutivaadee vichaardhaara se prabhavit gaay di mopasaan, nirvivaad rup se france ke sabase mahaan kathakaar hai. ve jab gyaarah varsh ke the tabhi unke mata-pita alag ho gaye the. unki praarambhik shiksha dharmik skulon main hui jisase unhen chidh thi. unhone france or germany ke yuddh main bhaag liya, alag alag naukariyaan kin or patron main stambh likhe. inaki pratham kahani sangrah bal op fat thi jiske prakashit hote hi ye prasiddh ho gaye. 1880 se 1891 tak kaa samay inke jivan kaa sabase mahatpurna kaal tha. in 11 varshon main mopansaa ke lagbhag 300 kahaniyaan, 6 upanyaas, 3 yatra sansmaran evam ek kavita sangrah prakashit hue. yuddh krishak jivan, stri purush sambandh, abhijatya varg or manushya kii bhaavnaatmak samasyaayen mopasaan kii rachanaaon kii vishay-vastu bane.
