 bhashaa takneekein aisi soochna takanic hai jo maanav bhashaa ke prasanskaran main prayukt hotin hai. maanav bhashaa vishwa main sabase jatil va sabase purana soochna kaa madhyam he.
 maanav bhashaa kaa astitv do roopon main he - bhashik evam likhit. yadhyapi boli, bhashaa kaa sabase puraani evam sabase sahaj rup he kintu jatil suchnaayen evam maanav gyaan kaa adhikansh bhaag likhit bhashaa ke rup main hi vidyaman he tathaa iskaa sanchaar bhi likhit rup main hi adhik hota he. spich takanic evam text takneekein bhashaa ko in do roopon main prakalan karti he. kintu spich evam tect takneekein hi paryaapt nahin hai. isaliye bahut si bhashaa takneekein aisi bhi hai jo spich yaa text kii processing karne ke bajaay bhashaa ko gyaan se jodne main sahaayak hotin hai.
 bhashaa takneekein bhavishya main bahut mahatvapurn hogin. inke viksit hone ke baad anek kaaryon ke karne ke tareeke main vismayakari parivartan aane vaala he.
