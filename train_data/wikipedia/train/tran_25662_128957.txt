 aksharam bhashaa, samskruti or sahitya ke kshetra main karyrat ek antarrashtriya sanstha he. yah lagbhag 6 varsh se desh-videsh main hindi ke pravardhan or sahitya ke prakaashan kaa kaary kar rahi he. euke hindi samiti, euke, ke sahyog se aksharam gat 4 varshon se 'hindi gyaan pratiyogita' kaa aayojan kar rahi he, jiske antargat har varsh pratiyogita ke vijayi hindi ke 10 vidhyaarthiyon ko puraskaar swaroop do saptaah kii bharat yatra kaa package diya jaataa he. bhartiya saanskrithik sambandh parishad, sahitya academy aadi sansthaaon ke sahyog se aksharam gat 4 varshon se pravasi kavi sammelan, pravasi utsav aadi kaa aayojan kar rahi he, jisamen hindi ke jwalanth vishyon par vichaar vimarsh kiya jaataa he, mahatvapurn sifaarishein kii jaati hai or naatak, rachana paath, kavi-sammelan, aadi kaaryakramon kaa aayojan kiya jaataa he or hindi sevi sammaan diya jaataa he.
