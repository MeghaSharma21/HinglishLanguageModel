 
 1997 asia cap, chathe asia cap toornament tha or doosraa srilanka main yojit kiya janaa tha. toornament 14-26 july, 1997 ke bich hua. chaar tiimon ne toornament main hissaa liya: bharat, pakistan, srilanka or bangladesh.
 1997 kaa asia cap ek round-robin toornament tha jahaan har team ne ek baar duusare khel liya tha or sheersh do tiimon ne final main jagah banane ke liye arhata praapt kii thi. bharat or srilanka ne final ke liye kwalifai kar lee, jahaan srilanka ne 8 wicket se jite apana doosraa asia cap jitkar bharat kii lagaataar teen championships ranon ko samaapt kiya.
