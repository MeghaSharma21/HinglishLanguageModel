 phinian brotherhud angreji shashan se ireland kii mukti ke hetu nirmit ek sanghathan tha. jon o mahoni ne 1848 main neuyark main isaki neenv daali. iske sadasyon ko 'pheniyans'  kehete the.
 phinian brotherhud kaa uddeshy shastrakranti or sainik kaaryvaayiyon dwaara ireland ko swatantra karnaa tha. 1866 main brotherhud ne canada par akraman kiya. phinian krantikari ireland bhi gaye or vidroh kii aag bhadakaani chaahi. vidroh safal nahin hua. tab unhone england kii bastiyon par bambaari aarambh kii. 1867 main unhone clerkenwel jail par dhaava bol diya or visphot se uski diwaar tod di. in ugr gatividhiyon ke baavjood andolan adhik dinon tak jivit na rah sakaa; phir bhi, irish swatantraaa kii chetana jaagrath karne main inaki bhoomika mahatvapurn rahi.
