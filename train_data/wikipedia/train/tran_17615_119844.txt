 parinamwad mandandak nitishastra ke siddhanton main vah vichaardhaaraen hai jinke anusaar kisi kriya yaa vyavhaar kii achchhai yaa buraai kaa aankalan antat isi buniyaad par hota he ki us kriya kaa parinaam acha tha yaa bura. parinamvaadiyon ke anusaar jis kaam ke karne ke nattejey achche hon, vahiin kaam bhala he. udhaaharan ke liye, adaalat main shapath lekar jhooth bolnaa aparaadh he, lekin parinamwad ke anusaar yadi aisa karne se kisi nirdosh vyakti kii jaan bachati he to aisa hi karnaa chaiye. parinamvaadiyon ke liye likhit niyamon va kanoonon kaa paalan karnaa apane-aap main ek acchhee yaa buree baat nahin hoti.
