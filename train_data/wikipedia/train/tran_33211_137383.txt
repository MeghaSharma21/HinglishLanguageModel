 manoranjan ke liye bagichon main donon ore paudhe athwa baadh is prakaar lagaai jaati he ki nikaas maarg tathaa bageeche kaa centre gyaat karnaa kathin hota he england ke hampton court rajmahal main bageeche kii bhulkulaiyaan kaa sarvotkrisht namoona vartmaan he. ab to bahut se khel bhi is aadhaar par banaae gaye hai. inase khilaadi kii kushagra buddhi kii pariksha hoti he.
