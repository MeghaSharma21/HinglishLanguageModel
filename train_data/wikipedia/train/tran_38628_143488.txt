 oregon sandhi united kingdom or sanyukt rajya america ke darmayaan 15 june 1846 ko kii gai ek sandhi he jiske zariye uttar america mahadweep ke uttarpashchimi oregon ilaake main in donon ke bich chal rahe matbhed ko suljhaayaa gaya. is kshetra par "1818 kii sandhi" ke baad donon deshon kaa mila-jula qabza tha. oregon sandhi par hastakshar hone ke baad is kshetra main british-niyantrit canada or america kii seemaa 49 degree uttar kii akshansh rekha par nirdhaarit kii gai.
