  lamhaa ek 2010 mazaa saamaajik-ection thriller film he likhaa he or rahul dholkia - dwaara nirdeshit. kashmir main set, film sena ke ek aadmi ke bare main ek kahani he, sanjay dutt dwaara khelaa jaataa he or uske pyaar byaaj, bipasha basu dwaara nibhaai vibhinn muddon ke bich main, ki kashmir tha saal ke liye saamana karnaa padaa. yah bhi bhoomikaaon ke samarthan main anupam kher or kunal kapur suvidhaayen.
