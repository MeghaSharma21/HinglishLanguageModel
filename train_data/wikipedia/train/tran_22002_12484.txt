 hamaari saas lila bhartiya hindi dharawahik he, jiskaa prasaaran colors par 30 may 2011 se 30 disambar 2011 tak hua. iskaa nirmaan asit kumaar modi ne kiya he. iskaa nirdesan kaa kaary rakesh shekhavat or kushal javeri ne kiya he.
 yah kahani ek amir aurat kii he, jiske paanch bachche rahete hai. vah apane chaar bacchon kaa vivaah kara detii he. pehle, duusare or tisare bachche shaadi se bahut khush rahete hai or apni acchhee jindagi jite hai. lekin sabase choti bahut bahut hi bekaar tareeke se vyavhaar karti he or puure parivaar ko alag karnaa chaahati he. uske baad uski saas lila ko apni galati kaa ehsaas ho jaataa he.
