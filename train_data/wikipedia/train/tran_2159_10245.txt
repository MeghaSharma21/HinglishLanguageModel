 jhou me/jhu me main hua tha. me china kii ek mahila badminton khilaadi hai. apane adhikansh peshewar jivan main unhone chini janavaadee ganraajya kaa pratinidhitiv kiya lekin 2007 ke baad se wo hong kong ke taraf se khel rahi hai. hong kong prashasnic taur par china kaa hissaa hote hue bhi apana alag khel kaarykram chalaata he or china se alag timen rakhata he. 2010 main me ko drug pareekshan main anuteern hone par 2-varsh kaa pratibandh jhelana padaa.
 1998 ke baad se jhu ne 20 se zyada antarrashtriya mahila ekal khitaab jite hai or kai baar sheersh variyata hasil kii he. 2001 ibief vishwa pratiyogita main unhen rajat or 2003 kii vishwa pratiyogita main kaansya padak mila. 2002 ke asian khelon main unhone gong ruina ko haraakar badminton kaa mahila ekal kaa swarna padak jeeta. jhu pratishthit ol england open badminton pratiyogita main teen baar finlist rah chuki hai or 2003 main yah khitaab jeet bhi chuki hai.
 beadbluef ne 4 sitambar 2010 ko jhu ko 2 varshon tak pratibamdhit karne kii ghoshna kii. unpar clenbuterol naamak drugs lene kaa aarope tha. pratiyogitaaon ke itar june main liye gayi ek namoonay main unhen is pratham shrenee ke pratibandhit upapachan kaarak lene kaa doshi paaya gaya.
 me ne apane upar lage pratibandh kaa virodh kiya or apane nirdosh hone kii duhai di lekin unpar se pratibandh nahin hataa. mana jaataa he ki clenbuterol sankramit bhojan lene se bhi sharir main pahunch jaataa he.
