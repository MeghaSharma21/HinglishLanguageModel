 mithusen ek nami kalakaar he.
 unhone shanti niketan or glassgo skool of art se kala kii padhai kii he.
 mithu ke kaam kaa dayera vishaal he, jo chitrakala, video, sthapaty, instolation, kavita, sound or parformens tak faila hua he.
 ve apni kala ke zarie youniktaa, raajnneetik, kshetreey, bhaavnaatmak or bhashaai pahchaan se jude masalon par samaaj dwaara thope ja rahe ravaiye par sawaal uthaati hai.
 unki kai kalakritiyon main kaamuktha mukhar ho kar ubhartee he.
 is bahaane mithu logon ko jatil muddon par sochane par vivash karti hai. 
 mithu ke kala kii pradarshnee duniya bhar kii pramukh art gailariyon main lag chuki he. 
 iske alaava unhen samakaleen asian kala ke liye 2015 kaa ‘prudential ai aword’or 2010 main samakaleen bhartiya kala kaa ‘scoda aword’ mil chukaa he.
