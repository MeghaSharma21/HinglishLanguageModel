 nobal athwa no-ball cricket naamak khel main kshetrarakshan kar rahi team par lagane vaala dand he jo mukhyatah gendbaaz dwaara niyamavali ke anusaar gend nahin phenkane par lagata he. cricket ke adhiktar praroopon main, no-ball kii paribhaasha emsisi- lau of cricket ke anusaar rakhi jaati he haalanki yuva cricket main tathaa anterrashtriya cricket main bimar par kathin niyam lagu hote hai evam bounsar par shithil niyam hote hai.
 kisi bhi no-ball par wide-ball kii tarah diya jaataa he or kuch niyamon main yah chetavanee bhi ho sakati he - yadi koi gend niyamavali ke charam par he to ise nilamban ke rup main bhi ek or gend phenkakar puraa kiya jaataa he. iske alaava, ran-out ke atirikt anya kisi bhi tarah se no-ball kii avasthaa main ballebaaj ko out nahin kiya ja saktaa. tinti tinti evam haal hi main ek-divasiy khelon main kisi bhi prakaar kii no-ball ke baad ballebaaj ko ek 'free-hit' bhi milti he. iskaa arth yah hua ki no-ball ke aage vaali gend par ballebaaj, out hone ke bhay ke bina kisi bhi prakaar gend ko khel saktaa he. no-ball asaamaanya nahin he or chote praaroop ke khelon tathaa mukhyatah tej gendbaajon dwaara lambe ran-ap ke kaaran aisi gendbaaji dekhne ko milti he.
 kuch prakaar kii no-ball khatarnaak tathaa anuchit maani jaati hai. yadi is tarah kii gend phanki jaati he to gendbaaj ko, gendbaazi se turant nilambit kiya ja saktaa he.
 no-ball vibhinn kaarnon se ho sakati he. mukhyatah yah, gendbaaj dwaara nimnlikhit main se koi ek niyam todane se hoti he. 
 khatarnaak gendbaaji iskaa anya saamaanya udhaaharan hai.
