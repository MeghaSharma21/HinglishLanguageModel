 rajkeey politechnic, soron, uttar pradesh kaa takneekee college he. ye uttar pradesh pravidhik shiksha parishad ke adheen va akhil bhartiya takneekee shiksha parishad dwaara sambandhit he. yah college pashchimi kshetra ke antargat aata he.
 iskaa shilanyas san 1983 isvi main tatkaaleen mukhyamantri shri narayan dutt tiwari va shikshamantri shri sunil shastri dwaara kiya gaya.
 rajkeey politechnic, soron in sabhi tradon me diploma pradaan karata he-
 yah college lagbhag 25 ekad mai failaa hua he. iskaa apana khud kaa imaarat, prashasnic bhawan, prayogshaala, karmshaala, maikenical khand ityadi he. iskaa apana khud kaa hastal bhi he lekin wo asthaayee rup se police line ke adhin he. khel kaa maidan bhi kaaphi bada he.
 college sadak va railmarg se achche prakaar se juda hua he. soron shukarakshetra railway stesan jo yaho kaa mukhya railway stesan he maatr 1.5 kimi kii duri par sthit he tathaa kasganj yaho se maatr 16 kimi duuri par he jo jila mukhyalay bhi he. yahan par zyaadaatar chaatr purvanchal yaa jonpur jile se hai.
