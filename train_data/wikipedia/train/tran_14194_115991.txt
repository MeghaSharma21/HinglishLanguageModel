 katni bhartiya rajya madhya pradesh kaa ek jila he. chuunaa pathar ke shahar ke naam se lokapriy uttari madhya pradesh kaa katni jila 4950 varg kilometre ke kshetrafal main failaa hua he. dhimarkheda, bahoriband, bilahari or karondi roopnath karitrai yahaan ke lokapriy paryaton sthal ke saath saath moff gupat chandel kalchuri kaaleen itihaas samete hai. katni, choti mahanadi or umaraar yahaan se bahane vaali pramukh nadiyaan hai. katni kaa slimnabad gaanv ateet me pilaa roomaal se galaa kasakar maarake lut ke liye ab sangemarmar ke pattharon ke liye prasiddh he. kurmi samaaj kii patrika jagriti kaa parkashan isvi 1990 se yaha se hota he. paanch dishaao ke liai railbe kaa mughay jakashan he. motilal ke waradoli se navaji gai katni mahakoshal - bundel - vaghelkhand kii thiveni he.
