 shekhar dutt bharat ke chhattisgarh rajya ke rajyapal rahe. iske pehle vah bhartiya prashasnic seva ke adhikari ke rup main bharat sarkaar ke raksha mantralay main sachiv ke pad se sevanivrutt hue.
 dutt bhartiya prashasnic seva kii 1969 batch ke madhya pradesh cader ke afasar he. vah bhartiya thalsena main short sarvice kamishan adhikari rahe evam 1971 ke bharat-paak yuddh main shaury ke liye unhen sena padak se bhi nawaja gaya. dutt madhya pradesh sarkaar ke aadim jati or anusuchit jati kalyaan, skool shiksha, khel evam yuva kalyaan vibhaagon main pramukh sachiv rahane ke saath hi vibhinn padon par apni sevaayen de chuke hai. unhen bharat sarkaar ke swasthya evam parivaar kalyaan mantralay main ayurveda, yoga va prakritik chikitsa, unani, siddh evam homeopathy vibhaag kaa sachiv niyukt kiya gaya tha.
 bhartiya khel praadhikaran ke mahanideshak ke rup main dutt ne 2003 navambar main hyderabad, bharat main yojit hue pratham effro-asian khelon kii mejabani main bhoomika nibhaai. baad main vah raksha utpaadan vibhaag main sanyukt sachiv bane evam antat san 2005 main bharat ke raksha sachiv kaa padbhaar grahan kiya. july 2007 main shekhar dutt sevanivrutt hue. iske turant pehle unhen do varsh ke liye rashtriya suraksha up-salaahkaar kaa kaarykaal mila.
