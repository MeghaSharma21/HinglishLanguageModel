 bhautikee main bhautik tantra brahmand kaa ek chunaa hua ansh hota he jiskaa adhyayan or vishleshan kara jaae. is tantra ke baahar brahmand ke anya sabhi anshon ko "paryaavaran" kaha jaataa he. is paryaavaran ko adhiktar adhyayan se nazarandaaz kar diya jaataa he or iskaa adhyayan keval usee had tak kiya jaataa he jahaan tak vah bhautik tantra ko prabhavit kar rahaa ho.
