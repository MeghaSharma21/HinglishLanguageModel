 ismailia suez thaldmarumadhya main timsa jhil ke uttar pashchimi tat par misr kaa ek nagar he jo bumadhyasaagar se 75 kimi tathaa kahiraa se 135 kimi dur he. yah suez or port saiid ke bichobich sthit he. ise san 1863 i. main suez nahar kii khudaai ke samay kedio ismail ne basaaya tha, at: iskaa naam ismailia padaa gaya. isaki
 isaki galiyon tathaa makaanon kii swatchta tathaa kram main aadhuniktaa kii gahari chhap he. yah teen ore udyanon tathaa ek ore jhil se ghira hua he. suez nahar ke kinare par 'ke mohamat ali' he, jahaan nahar kii khudaai ke samay fardinaan de lapes nivas karte the. ghat ke ant main jalakal he jo port said ko meetha jal pahunchaata he. is nagar main bahut se sarkari karyaalay, godaam tathaa saanskrithik bhawan hai. isaki jansankhya lagbhag 7,50,000 he.
