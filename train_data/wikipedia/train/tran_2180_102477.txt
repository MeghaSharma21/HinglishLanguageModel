 daawaah yaa daawah; yah ek arabi shabd he. is shabd ko farsi or urdu main daavat uchhar karte hai. is shabd ke anek arth hai. mul yaa sthool rup se iskaa arth "aamantran" he. anek jagahon or avasaron par iskaa arth mul rup vahi rah kar bhi thoda badla sa rahata he. 
 islamic dharmprachaarak, logon ko islam ke bare main bataate or samjhaate huve islam kii taraf bulaane ko "daawaah" kehete hai. is tarah ke nimantran karne vaale ko "dai" kehete hai. yani islam dharm main aane yaa usko sweekaar karne kaa nimantran dene vaala. 
 islam main dharm prachaar ko "tabalig" kehete hai. prachaarak ko "mubllig" kehete hai. yah mubllig yani prachaarak bhi dai kaa kaam karte hai, yani islam dharm main aane kii daavat dete hai. saath saath, ek muslim duusare muslim ko bhi dharmik rup se paripurna hone kaa nimantran dete hai isko bhi daawaah kehete hai. 
 daawaah kaa mukh uddeshy yah he ki muslim or gaiyr-muslimon ko islamicy tatv samjhaana, allaah kii tauhid ko samajhaana or mohammad sahab or unki pravaktata kaa parichay dena he. logon ko islam kii taraf bulaanaa or islam kaa prachaar or vistaar karnaa.
 ha. mohammad ke kaal main, mohammad sahab ne apane anuyaayiyon ko kuch samooh banaakar kai deshon main bheja ki logon ko akeshwaropasna kii taraf bula saken. aap ke baad bhi, aap ke anuyai is kaam ko jari rakhaa.
