 ashkunu yaa sanu yaa gramsana ek nuristaani bhashaa he jo afghanistan ke nuristaan praant main madhya pech nadi ghati main vama kshetra ke aasapaas or aaleengaar nadi ke upree bhaag kii kuch upanadiyon kii vaadiyon main boli jaati he. is bhashaa ko ashkunu, sanu or gramsana naam ke teen alag nuristaani qabeele bolte hai jis karanvash is ek bhashaa ko bhi in teen naamon se janaa jaataa he.
