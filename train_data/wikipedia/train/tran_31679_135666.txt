 bhogeshwari devi phukan bharat kii ek krantikari mahila thim jinhone 1942 i. ke bharat chodo aandolan ke samay 70 varsh kii vriddhavastha main assam main naugaanv jile ke beharampur kasbe main angrejon ke viruddh vidroh kaa netritv kiya.
 unhone kasbe kii mahilaon kaa sanghathan banaaya tathaa unhen ghar kii chaharadivari se baahar aakar aandolan main shaamil hone ke liye protsaahit kiya. unke netritv main vidrohiyon ne angrezon dwaara sil kiye gaye congress karyaalay kaa sil todaa or karyaalay par apana adhikaar kar liya. 
 13 sitambar ko vijayadashami ke din samaaroh main ekatrit bheed par police dal ne achaanak aakar laathiyaan barasaani shuru kar deen. yah samachar sunate hi kasbe kii mahilaon kaa netritv karti hui bhogeshwari devi tirangaa haath main lekar angrej sena ke saamne ja pahaunchin. angrej captain fins ne ugr hoti bheed ko dekhakar goli chalaane kaa aadesh de diya. kuddh bhogeshwari devi ne jhapatkar jhande ke danda se fins par hamalaa kar diya. ghayal fins ne bhogeshwari devi ko goliyon se chalni kar diya or vah mahaan virangana 1942 main veergati ko praapt ho gayi.
