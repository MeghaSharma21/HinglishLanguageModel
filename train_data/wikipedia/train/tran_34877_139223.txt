 nyaayalay kaa taatpary saamanytah us sthaan se he jahaan par nyaay prashasan kaary hota he, parantu bahudhaa iskaa prayog nyaayadhish ke arth main bhi hota he. bolchaal kii bhashaa main adaalat ko kachhari bhi kehete hai.
 bhartiya nyaayalayon kii vartmaan pranali kisi vishesh pracheen parampara se sambhddha nahin he. mughal kaal main do pramukh nyaayalayon kaa ullekh miltaa haih sadar divaani adaalat tathaa sadar nizaam-e-adaalat, jahaan kramshah vyavahaarwad tathaa aapraadhik maamalon kii sunwaayee hoti thi. san 1857 i. ke asafal swatantryayuddh ke pashchaat angreji nyaay-prashasan-pranali ke aadhaar par vibhinn nyaayalayon kii sristi hui. england main sthit privi council bharat kii sarvocch nyaayalay thi. san 1947 i. main desh swatantra hua or tatpashchaat bhartiya samvidhan ke antargat sampurna-prabhutv sampann ganraajya kii sthapana hui. ucchtam nyaayalay bharat kaa sarvocch nyaayalay bana.
 nyaayalayon ko unke bhedanusaar vibhinn virgon main baanta ja saktaa he, jaise ucch tathaa nimna nyaayalay, abhilekh nyaayalay tathaa ve jo abhilekh nyaayalay nahin he, vyaavhaarik, rajasav tathaa dand nyaayalay, pratham nyaayalay tathaa apil nyaayalay or sainik tathaa anyaanya nyaayalay.
 ucchtam nyaayalay desh kaa sarvocch abhilekh nyaayalay he. prayek rajya main ek abhilekh nyaayalay he. rajya ke samast nyaayalay uske adheen hai. rajasav parishad rajasav sambandhi maamalon ko pradeshik sarvocch abhilekh nyaayalay he. katipay maamalon ko chhodkar uparyukt nyaayalayon ko apil sambandhi kshetradhikaar he. jile main pradhan nyaayalay jila nyaayadhish kaa he. 
 anya nyaayalay kaaryakshetranusaar is prakaar haih 
  vyaavhaarik nyaayalay, jaise civil jaj tathaa munsif ke nyaayalay or laghuwaad nyaayalay, 
  dand nyaayalay, jaise jila dandadhikari, anya dandadhikariyon ke nyaayalay tathaa satr nyaayalay, 
  rajasav nyaayalay, jaise jiladhish tathaa ayukta ke nyaayalay.
