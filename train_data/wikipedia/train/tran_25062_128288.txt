 varali ek prakaar kii janajati he. jo maharashtr rajya ke thane jile ke dhaanu, talasri evam jwahar talukaaj main mukhyat: duusari janajatiyon ke saath paayi jaati he. ye bahut mehnatee or krishi pradaan log hote he. jo baas, lakdi, ghaas evam mitti se bani tiles se bani jhopadiyon main rahate he. jhopadiyon kii deevaare laal kadu mitti evam bans se baandh kar banaai jaati he, deevaaro ko pehle laal mitti se lepa jaataa he uske baad upar se gaay ke gobar se lipai kii jaati he.varali chitrakala ek pracheen bhartiya kala he jo kii maharashtra kii ek janajati varali dwaara banaai jaati he. or yah kala unke jivan ke mul siddhanto ko prastut karti he. in chitron main mukhyatah fasal paidaavaar ritu, shaadi, utsav, janm or dharmikata ko darshaya jaataa he. yah kala varali janajati ke saral jivan ko bhi darshaati he. varali kalaon ke pramukh vishyon main shaadi kaa bada sthaan hai. shaadi ke chitron main dev, palghat, pakshi, ped, purush or mahilayen saath main naachte hue darshaae jate he.
