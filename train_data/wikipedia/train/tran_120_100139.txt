 shivpur uttar pradesh ke mirzapur main sthit he. puranic katha ke anusaar bhagwaan shri ram chandra ne apane pita raja dasharath kaa shraadh vindhyaachal kshetra main hi kiya tha. mana jaataa he ki bhagwaan shri ram bhagwaan shiv ke upaask the. is jagah par bhagwaan ram ne pashchim disha kii ore bhagwaan shiv kii pratimaa sthapit kii thi. isi kaaran yah jagah rameshwar naam se prasiddh hui or is jagah ko shivpur ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
