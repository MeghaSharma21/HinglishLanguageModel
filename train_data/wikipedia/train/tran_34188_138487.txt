 masaoka shiki antarrashtriya hiku puraskaar, matsuyama main "shimnami cado 99 international hiku kanvension" main sitambar 1999 main aadhunik japani hiku ke sansthaapak ke naam par yah ghoshnaa kii gayi. hiku ek japani kavy vidha he, jiskaa prachlan 16 veen shatabdi main prarambh ho gaya tha. ek japani kavi basho ko hiku kaa janak mana jaataa he. ek shatabdi purv san 1900 i0 ke lagbhag japani sahityakar masaoka shiki ne vishisht japani chand “hokku” ko ek nayaa naam hiku diya jisane lokapriyata ke bade maanakon ko praapt kiya. yah puraskaar japani kavita hiku ko vishwa str par pratishthapit karne or duniya kaa dhyaan aakarshith karne vaale vyaktitv ko diya jaataa he. 
 masaoka shiki antarrashtriya hiku puraskaar unhen pradaan kiya jaataa he, jo hiku kii rachanaatmakataa kii sthapana ke prati jaagrukta paidaa karne ke uddeshy se vikaas or rashtriyata yaa bhashaa kii parvaah kiye bina ullekhneey yogadan detaa he tathaa hiku ke madhyam se apane kshetra main ek majboot hit or vyapak antarrashtriya drushtikon sthapit karata he. yah puraskaar, vishesh rup se kisi kshetra tak simit nahin he, yahi kaaran he ki hiku likhane vaale kaviyon, lekhakon, anuvaadakon, nibandhkaron, sampadakon aadi ko puraskaar chayan main samaan sthaan diya jaataa he.
