 housephul 4 ek aagaamee bhartiya hasya bollywood film he, jiske nirdeshak sajid khan hai. yah housephul film siries kii chauthi film hogi. isamen akshay kumaar, ritesh deshmukh tathaa bobi deol mukhya bhoomikaaon main honge. film kii release 25 oktober 2019 ko prastaavit he.
 sitambar 2016 main yah khabar aayi thi ki nirmaataa sajid nadiadwala ne ek baar phir sajid khan ko yah film nirdeshit karne ko kaha he. usee samay ye khabar bhi aayi ki akshay kumaar or ritesh deshmukh pehali teen filmon ke baad is film main bhi mukhya kirdaar nibhaayenge. 27 oktober 2017 ko nadiadwala grandsans limited ne ek aadhikarik bayaan main pushti kii ki sajid khan film kaa nirdesan karenge, or yah 25 oktober 2019 ko diwali ke maukey par release hogi.
 film kii kahani punarjanm par aadhaarit hogi, or sajid khan ke anusaar isamen pichhali sabhi housephul filmon ke abhinaytaon ko shaamil karne kaa prayaas kiya jaayegaa. film 25 oktober 2019 ko 3di main release kii jaayegi, or is prakaar yah 3di main release hone vaali pehali bhartiya kamedi film hogi.
