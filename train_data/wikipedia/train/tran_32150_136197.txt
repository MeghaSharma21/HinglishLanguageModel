 bhartiya paaramparik vishwaas ke anusaar mukta gaj, megh, varah, shankh, matsay, sarp, shakti or ven, aath sadhano se praapt hote he. gajamukta isi prakaar kii ek mukta he jiske sambandh main kaha jaataa he ki vah hathi ke mastishk se praapt hota he. kintu aadhunik vigyaan is prakaar kisi moti kii utpatti ko sweekaar nahin karata. uski drushti main yah kori kaviklpana he.
