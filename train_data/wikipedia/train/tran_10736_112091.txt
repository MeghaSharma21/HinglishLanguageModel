 jakarem bharat ke purvottar rajya meghalaya kii rajdhani shilong se 64 kimi dur mavakirvat block sthit ek gram he. yah apane garm jal ke soton ke liye janaa jaataa he. yahaan meghalaya main anya paryatak sthalon kii tarah ghani aabaadi nahin milti he. is kshetra main garm gandhak ke pani ke sote hai, jo jharne ke rup main jameen se footate hai. in sundar garm jharanon main pani ko upachaaratmak aushadhiy gunon kaa samavesh bataaya jaataa he. adhikansh paryatakon dwaara achche swasthya ke liye garm snaan ke rup main upyog kiya jaataa he.
 jaikrem samay ke saath saath ek swasthya resort ke rup main prasiddh hota ja rahaa he.
 yah shilong se adhik dur sthit nahin he makirwat-shilong sadak maarg dwaara yahaan se pahunchaa ja saktaa he. iske liye taxi yaa bas le sakate hai or jaikrem pahunch sakate hai. yah mavakirvat se 10 kimi or jila mukhyalay nongstoin se 50 kimi dur he.
 jaikrem ke raste main kai gaanvon main ruk sakate hai or maarg main meghalayan vyanjan bhojan bhi le sakate hai.
 yahaan kii jansankhya 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar 2,974 he. yahaan kul 501 parivaar rahete hai or linganupaat 993 he.
