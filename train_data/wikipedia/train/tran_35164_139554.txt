 hathidaant yaa gajadant hathi ke un do daanton ko kehete hai jo hathi ke munh se baahar nikle hote hai or bahumoolya maane jate hai. puraane samay main inase kai prakaar kii bahumoolya vastuen nirmit kii jaati thim, yahan tak kii manushya ke nakli daant bhi inase banate the. hathidaant ke kaaran haathiyon kaa avaidh shikaar kiya jaataa he tathaa unki sankhya tezi se girane ke kaaran vah jangal main sankatagrast ho gayi hai. yahi kaaran he ki anterrashtriya str par hathidaant ke vyapaar par roak laga di gai he.hathidaant ke kai sajaavati or vyaavhaarik upyog hai. jin mai haath mai pahan ne kii chudiyaan, kangan ,gahane rakhane ke bakse aadi shaamil hai ! plastic ke ijad se pehle, yah billiard gendon, piano chaabiyon, scottish bagpipe, batan or vistrut sajaavati saamaan ke liye istemaal kiya gaya tha. phir hathidaant ke liye kritrim vikalp viksit kiye gayi. plastic piano chaabiyon ko piano ke jaankaar ek nimna vikalp maanate hai, haalanki haal main viksit anya kritrim saamagree hathidaant se bani piano chaabiyon kaa behatar ahsaas karaati hai.
