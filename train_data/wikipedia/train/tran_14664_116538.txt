 sova rigpa tibet sahit himalayan kshetron main prachlit pracheen upachaar paddati he. bharat ke himalayan kshetra main 'tibeti' yaa 'aamachi' ke naam se jaani jane vaali sova-rigpa vishwa kii sabase puraani chikitsa paddatiyon main se ek he.
 bharat main is paddati kaa prayog jammu-kashmir ke ladakh kshetra, lahaul-spiti, sikkim, arunachal pradesh tathaa darjeeling main kiya jaataa he. sova-rigpa ke siddhant or prayog ayurveda kii tarah hi hai or isamen paaramparik chini chikitsavigyaan ke kuch siddhant bhi shaamil hai. sova rigpa ke chikitsak dekh kar, chhu kar evam prashn poochkar ilaaj karte hai.
 aisa mana jaataa he ki yah paddati bhagwaan buddha dwaara 2500 varsh pehle prarambh kii gai thi. baad main prasiddh bhartiya vidwaanon jaise jeevak, nagarjun, vagbhatta evam chandranandan ne ise aage badhaayaa. iskaa itihaas 2500 varshon se adhik kaa rahaa he. sova-rigpa pranali yadhyapi bahut pracheen he kintu haal hi main manyata pradaan kii gai he. yah pranali asthma, bronchitis, arthritis jaisi puraane rogon ke liye prabhaavshaalee maani gai he.
 sova-rigpa kaa mul siddhant nimnlikhit he 
 sova-rigpa maanav sharir ke nirmaan main paanch bhautik tatwon, vikaron kii prakruti tathaa inke samaadhaan ke upayon ke mahatv par bal detaa he.. uttar pradesh or karnataka main sova-rigpa ke kuch shaikshanik santhaan hai.
