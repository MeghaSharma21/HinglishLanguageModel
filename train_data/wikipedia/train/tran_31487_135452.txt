 hidayatullah rashtriya vidhi vishvavidhyalay bharat ke pehle muslim mukhya nyaayadhish mohammad hidayatullah ke naam par chhattisgarh ke nayaa raipur main varsh 2003 main kii gai. yah vidhi vishvavidhyalay vishvavidhyalay anudaan ayog dwaara ujisi adhinium 1956 ke section 2 ke tahat anudaan pradaan karne vaale vishvavidhyalay kii suchi main shaamil he. vishvavidhyalay ko baar council of india dwaara manyata pradaan kii gai he. chhattisgarh ucch nyaayalay ke mukhya nyaayadhish justise naveen sinha vishvavidhyalay ke kuladhipati or pro. sukhpal singh kulapati hai.
