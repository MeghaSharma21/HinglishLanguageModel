 chalati kaa naam gaadi bhartiya hindi hasya dharawahik he, jiskaa prasaaran sab tivi par 25 oktober 2015 se shaam 7:30 baje prasaarit hota he. iskaa nirmaan dipti bhatnagar ne kiya he. isamen mukhya kirdaar main romit raaj or sristi rode hai.
 karan ko ek aisi gaadi milti he, jo usase baat karti he. uski aawaaj keval karan ko hi sunai detii he. jab jab vah kisi pareshaani main pad jaataa he, tab tab use gaadi koi suzav de detaa he. jiske baad uski samasya kaa samaadhaan ho jaataa he. mukhyatah samaadhaan kaa hal gaadi se judaa hota he.
