 borispil anterrashtriya vimaanashetra  ukraine main borispil se 6 kimi pashchim main evam kayiv se 29 kimi purv main sthit ek anterrashtriya vimaanashetra he. yah ukraine kaa sabase bada vimaanashetra he jahaan se desh kii sarvaadhik anterrashtriya udaanen jaati hai. kayiv ko vayuseva dene vaale teen vimaanakshetron main se ye ek he.
