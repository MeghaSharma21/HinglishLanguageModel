 zeta oryonis, jiske bair namakan main bhi yahi naam darj he, akaash main kalpurush taramandal main sthit ek teen taron kaa bahu tara mandal he. iskaa mukhya tara ek ati-garam nila mahadaanav tara he or yah prithvi se dikhne vaale taron main se 31vaan sab se roshan tara he. yah hamase 700 prakaash-varsh kii duuri par sthit he or prithvi se iskaa ausat saapeksh kantimaan 2.04 he. agar teenon taron ko ikaitha dekha jaae to inaki mili-juli chamak 1.72 magnitude he. dhyaan rahe ke khagoleey magnitude ek vipareet maap he or yah jitna kam ho chamak utani hi zyaada hoti he.
 zeta oryonis ko angrezi main "olanit" bhi kaha jaataa he. yah arabi bhashaa ke "an-nitaq" se liya gaya he jiskaa arth "kamarband" he. kalpurush taramandal main jo kalpurush kii kaalpanik akruti banati he, zeta oryonis uski kamarband ke ek kone kaa tara he.
