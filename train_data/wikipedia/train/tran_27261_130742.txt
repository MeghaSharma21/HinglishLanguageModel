 bhashaa ke shabdon ke itihaas ke adhyayan ko vyutpattishastra kehete hai. isamen vichaar kiya jaataa he ki koi shabd us bhashaa main kab or kaise pravisht hua; kis strot se athwa kahan se aayaa; uskaa swaroop or arth samay ke saath kis tarah parivartit hua he. vyutpattishastra main shabd ke itihaas ke madhyam se kisi bhi raashtra, praant evam samaaj kii bhashik, saanskrithik, aitihaasik evam saamaajik prishthbhumi abhivyakt hoti he.
 'vyutpatti' kaa arth he ‘vishesh utpatti’. kisi shabd ke kramik itihaas kaa adhyayan karnaa, vyutpattishastra kaa vishay he. isamen shabd ke swaroop or uske arth ke aadhaar kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he. angreji main vyutpattishastra ko 'Etymology' kehete hai. yah shabd unani bhashaa ke yatharth arth main prayukt Etum tathaa lekha-jokha ke arth main prayukt Logos ke yoga se bana he, jiskaa aashay shabd ke itihaas kaa vaastavik arth sampusht karnaa he.
 bharatvarsh main vyutpattishastra kaa udbhav bahut pracheen he. jab veda mantron ke arthon ko samajhnaa sugam nahin rahaa tab bhartiya manishiyon dwaara vedon ke clisht shabdon ke sangrah rup main nighantu granth likhe gaye tathaa nighantu granthon ke shabdon kii vyakhya or shabd vyutpatti ke gyaan ke liye nirukt granthon kii rachana hui. vedon ke adhyayan ke liye vedaagon ke chha: shastron main nirukt shaastra bhi sammilit hai. nirukt shaastra sankhya main 12 batalaaye jate hai, lekin is samay acharya yask pranit ek maatr nirukt uplabdh he.
 shabdon kii utpatti kaa duniya main sabase pehle gahan vishleshan karne kaa shrey bharat ke sanskrit vaiyakarnon ko jaataa he jinhone nirukt naam se sabase pehle vaidic shabdon kaa nirvachan va vyakhya kii. sanskrit ke chaar vaiyaakaran sabase prasiddh hai:
 sanskrit ke shabdon kii vyutpatti main uparyukt vaiyakarnon ke kaaryon kaa sandarbh liya jaataa he. sanskrit ke is agrag kaary kaa sandarbh lekar aage chalakar pashchimi vidwaanon ne bhi vyutpattishastra kaa vikaas kiya.
