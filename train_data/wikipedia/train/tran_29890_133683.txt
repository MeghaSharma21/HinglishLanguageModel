 rashtriya parishad bhutan kii do sadaneey sansad kaa ucch sadan he. bhutan kii sansad kaa gathan rashtriya sabha ,rashtriya parishad or bhutan ke raja ko sammilit kar hota he. yah sadan sansad kaa sahyogi sadan he. vitt vidheyak ke paas karne main isaki bhoomika mahatv nahin rakhti. bhutani kanoonon ko paas karne main is sadan kii bhoomika mahatvapurn he. is sadan kii mukhya bhoomika he ki desh kii suraksha ,akhandta aadi mahatvapurn prakarnon ko rashtriya sabha ,pradhan mantri or raja ko avgat karana. 31 disambar 2007 or 29 janvari 2008 ko pratham rashtriya parishad ke liye 20 sadasyon kaa nirvachan hua. 
 is sadan main sadasyon kii sankhya 25 he jisamen 20 sadasyon kaa nirvachan bhutan ke 20 jilon se hota he. shesh 5 sadasyon ko raja dwaara namit kiya jaataa he. is sadan kaa sadsy hone ke liye snaatak kii oopaadhi anivaary ho jane kaaran parishad ke sabhi sadsy 40 varsh kii aayu ke neeche hai. 23 april 2013 ko dwitiya rashtriya parishad kaa nirvachan hua.8 may 2013 ko bhutan naresh ne 5 sadasyon punah namankit kar diya
