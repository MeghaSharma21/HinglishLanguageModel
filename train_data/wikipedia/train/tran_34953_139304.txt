 
 'dalbargia sissu
 shisham bhartiya upmahadweep kaa vriksh he. isaki lakdi furniture evam imarti lakdi ke liye bahut upyukt hoti he.
 shisham bahupayogi vriksh he. isaki lakdi, pattiyaan, jaden sabhi kaam main aati hai. lakadiyon se furniture banataa he. pattiyaan pashuon ke liye proteinous chaara hoti hai. jaden bhoomi ko adhik upjau banaati hai. pattiyaan va shakhaayen varsha-jal kii boondon ko dhire-dhire jameen par giraakar bhu-jal bhandar badhaati hai.
 shisham kii lakdi baari, majboot va badami rang kii hoti he. iske antahkashtha kii apeksha bahya kashtha kaa rang halkaa badami yaa bhura safed hota he. lakdi ke is bhaag main keede lagane kii ashanka rahati he. isliye ise nila thotha, zinc chloride yaa anya kitrakshak rasayanon se upachaarit karnaa jaruri he.
 shisham ke 10-12 varsh ke ped ke tane kii golai 70-75 va 25-30 varsh ke ped ke tane kii golai 135 semi tak ho jaati he. iske ek ghanfeet lakdi kaa vajan 22.5 se 24.5 kilogram tak hota he. assam se praapt lakdi kuch halki 19-20 kilogram prati ghanfoot vajan kii hoti he.
