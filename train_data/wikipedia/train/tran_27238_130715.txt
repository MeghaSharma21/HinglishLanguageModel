 balvir sab tivi par prasarit hone vaala ek bhartiya bal televijan dharawahik he jiskaa prasaaran 8 oktober 2012 ko armbh hua. is sho ne 8 june 2016 ko apane 1000 prakaran purna kar liye. 4 november 2016 ko 1111 prakaran purna hone ke baad kaarykram ko samaapt kar diya gaya tha. balveer bachcho ko sahsi banne ki prerna deta hai
 yah kahani ek bachche kii he, jise pariyon ne paala or use chamatkaari shaktiyon se paripurna banaaya jisase vah buraaiyon se lard sake. use dharti par ballu naam se rahane ke liye bheja jisase vah dharti par rah kar dharti ko buree takaton se bachaa sake. vah dharti par jis ghar main rahata he, vahaan use ballu ke naam se hi jaanate he. iske meher or maanav dost ban jate hai. vah balvir ban kar longon ko bachaata rahata he or buree takaton ko samaapt karata rahata he.
