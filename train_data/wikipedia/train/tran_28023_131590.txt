 mohammad akbar aurangzeb kaa chauthaa putr tha or 'akbar dwitiya' ke naam se itihaas main janaa jaataa he. usne apane pita aurangzeb ke viruddh vidroh kar samraat banane kii koshish kii jisamen use safalataa nahin mili. 
 san 1678 main pashchimottar bharat ke antargat jamaruud naamak sthaan par jodhpur ke maharaja yashavanth singh kii mrityu ho gai. aurangzeb ne tatkaal maharaja ke rajya par kabja kiya or unke balak putr ajit singh ko uski maan ke saath shahi haram main kaid kar liya. swayan aurangzeb ajmer pahunchaa or jijia lagu kar diya. isi bich durgadas rathore lard-bhidakar ajit singh or maharani ko dilli se nikaal laayaa. udhar apni sanshayalu prakruti ke kaaran aurangzeb ne akbar ko chittod kii soobedaari se hataakar marwar bhej diya. isse kshubdh akbar ne maharana jaisingh or durgadas se milakar swayan ko mughal samraat ghoshit kiya or mughal samrajya par kabja karne ke iraade se ajmer kii taraf badha. aurangzeb tatkaal is sthiti main nahin tha ki vah akbar kii 70 hajaar sena se takkar le saktaa. at usne dhokhadhadi bharaa ek patr akbar ke naam likhaa or yojananusaar use rajputon ke haathon pad jane diya. patr paakar rajput shankit ho uthe or unhone akbar kaa saath chhod diya. vivash akbar ko yudhvirat hona padaa. kuch samay uparaant patr kaa rahasy khul jane par durgadas swayan akbar se mila or may, 1681 main surakshit use dakshin bharat pahunchaa diya, jahaan vah ek varsh se adhik shivaji ke putr sambhaji ke darbar main rahaa. pashchaat akbar faras chalaa gaya. vahaan san 1704 main uski mrityu ho gai.
