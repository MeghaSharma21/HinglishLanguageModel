 client kisi computer yaa computer program ko kehete hai, jo kisi or computer dwaara pradutt sevaao kaa upbhog karata he.client saamanytah kisi saajha network ke sansadhano kaa upbhog karata he or use sarwar dwaara swayan ko pramanikrut karana padataa he. ek sarwar kai alag alag clyanton ko seva de saktaa he or ek clint kai alag alag sarvaron kii seva le saktaa he. clint or sarwar niji networko main bhi ho sakate hai.
 client kii prakruti evaman kaary ke aadhaar par inhen teen bhaago main vibhakt kiya ja saktaa he--1. fat client - iske madhyam se vibhinn prakaar ke aankado ko saralata se praapt kiya ja saktaa he. isaki nirbharta sarwar par aanshik rup se hoti he. iskaa pramukh udahaaran personal computer hota he, jisame vibhinn prakaar ke aankade bhare hote hai.
 2.thin client - iske dwaara us computer ke shroton kaa upyog kiya jaataa he joki poshak yaa mukhya shrot ke rup main kaary karata he. jaise vibhinn prakaar ke aankade offis web apps ke madhyam se praapt kiye ja sakate hai.
 3.hybrid client - yah fat or thin client kaa sanyukt rup mana jaataa he. fat client ke rup main yah sthaneey rup se aankado ke sangrahan kaa kaary karata he parantu is prakriya main isko sarwar ke upar nirbharta pradarshit kerani padati he. duusare shabdon main fat client kii tarah yah multimedia kaa samarthan karata he tathaa thim client kii tarah prabandhkeey kshamta evam lochashiltaa kaa pradarshan karata he.
