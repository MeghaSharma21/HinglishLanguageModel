 gopinath mohanti ek oriya sahityakar hai. inke dwaara rachit ek upanyaas amritarasantan ke liye unhen san 1955 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. inhen 1973 main gyanpith puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha. inhen bharat sarkaar dwaara 1981 main sahitya evam shiksha ke kshetra main padam bhushan se sammanit kiya gaya tha. ye urisa se hai.
