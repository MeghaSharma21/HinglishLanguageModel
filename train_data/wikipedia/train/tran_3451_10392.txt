 mesopotamia kaa unani arth he "do nadiyon ke bich". yah ilaaka dajala or furaat nadiyon ke bich ke kshetra main padataa he. isamen aadhunik iraq babil zila, uttarpurvi syria, dakshinpurvi turki tathaa iran kaa quzestan praant ke kshetra shaamil hai. yah kaansyayugin sabhyataa kaa udgam sthal mana jaataa he. yahan sumer, akkadi sabhyataa, babylon tathaa asyria ke samrajya alag-alag samay main sthapit hue the. hadappa sabhyataa main mesopotamia ko 'meluha' kaha gaya he.
