 1984 ke sikh virdohi dange bhartiya sikhon ke kilaaf the in dangon kaa kaaran tha indira gandhi ke hatya unheen ke angrakshakon dwaara jo ki sikh the. usee ke jawaab main ye dange hue the. in dangon main 3000 se zyaada mautein hui thi. sibiai kii raay main ye sabhi hinsak krity dilli police ke adhikariyon or indira gandhi ke bete rajeev gandhi ke netritv vaali centre sarkaar ke sahmati se yojit kiye gaye the. rajeev gandhi jinhone apni maan kii maut ke baad pradhanmantri ke rup main shapath lee thi or jo congress ke ek sadsy bhi the, unase dangon ke bare main puche jane par, unhone kaha tha, "jab ek bada ped girtaa he, tab prithvi bhi hilti he".
 1970 ke dashak main indira dwaara lagaae gaye bhartiya apatkaal ke dauraan, swytt sarkaar ke liye chunaav prachaar ke liye hazaron sikhon ko qaid kar liya gaya tha. is chhut-put hinsa ke chalate ek sashastr sikh algaavvaadi samooh ko bharat sarkaar dwaara ek aatankwaadi sanstha ke rup main namit kar diya gaya tha. june 1984 main oparation blue star ke dwaara indira gandhi ne bhartiya sena ko swarna mandir pe qabza karne kaa aadesh diya or sabhi vidrohiyon ko khatm karne ke liye kaha, kyunki swarna mandir par hathiyaar baandh sikh algaavvaadiyon se qabza kar liya tha. bhartiya ardhsainik balon dwaara baad main punjab ke gramin ilaaqon se algaavvaadiyon ko khatam karne ke liye ek oparation chalaaya tha. in dango kaa mukhya kaaran swarna mandir par sikho kaa gheraav karnaa tha. sikho kii maang ek kaalistaan naam kaa ek alag desh banane kii thi jahaan keval sikh or sardaar hi rahenge parantu congress sarkaar is ke liye taiyaar nahin thi sikh kai chetavaniyon ke baad bhi mandir ko nahin chhod rahe the isi ke chalate indira gandhi ne swarna mandir par se sikhon ko hataane ke liye military kii sahaayta lee vahaan hui muthbhed main bahut se sikh maare gaye. yah dekh kar indira gandhi ke do angrakshak jo ke sikh the unhone 31 aktubar ko indira gandhi par goliyaan chalaa kar unki hatya kar di.
