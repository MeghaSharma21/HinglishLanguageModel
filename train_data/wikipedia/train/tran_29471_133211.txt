 unnav bhartiya rajya uttar pradesh kaa ek jila he. iskaa mukhyalay unnav qasbaa he. yah zila lakhanu mandal kaa hissaa he. 
 
 unnav jile main 6 tehsil hai: unnav, bangaramau, hasanganj, safipur, purava or patan.
 unnav lok sabha seat se vartmaan saansad sakshi maharaj hai. unnav lok sabha kshetra main 19 lakh matadaata hai. jile main 6 vidhansabha kshetra hai: unnav, purava, bhagavantnagar, mohaan, safipur or bangaramau.
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar unnav jile kii jansankhya 3,110,595 he jo ki ise bharat ke nagaron main 112vaa sthaan detaa he. jile kaa ghanatv 682 he. jansankhya vruddhi dar 2001-2011 ke dauraan 15.19% he. linganupaat 901 stri prati 1000 purush he. sakshartaa 68.29% he.
 avadhi, baisawari or hindi yahan kii pramukh bhashaayen hai.
