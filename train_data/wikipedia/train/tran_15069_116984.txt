 nashi dakshini china main himalay ke chote pahaado main basane vaali ek jati he. yah unnan praant ke uttar-pashchimi bhaag or sichuaan praant ke dakshin-pashchimi bhaag main rahete hai. unnan kaa lijiang vibhaag khaaskar is samudaay se sambandhit he. san 2000 main inaki aabaadi lagbhag 3 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. mana jaataa he ki nasiyon ke purvaj tibet se aaye the or zamaanon se yah china ke tibet or bharat ke vyapaar main ahm bhoomika nibhaate aa rahe hai. nashi log tibeti-burmi bhashaa-parivaar kii nashi bhashaa bolte hai. yah samudaay mosuo samudaay se bahut miltaa-julata he, haalanki mosuo log abhi bhi apni tibetiyon se milti hui pahchaan banaae hue hai jabaki nashi logon ke chini sabhyataa ke kuch pahaluon ko apana liya he.
 nashi logon kaa apana 'dongba' naamak dharm he. nashi bhashaa main is shabd kaa arth 'budhimaan purush' he. isaki shuruyaat tibet se aae hue ek dongba shilo naamak sadhu se hui maani jaati he jo aaj se lagbhag 900 saal pehle baishuitai shahar ke qareeb ek gufa main rahete the. is dharm main tibet ke pracheen bone dharm ke bahut se tatv milte hai.
 nashi logon main hinduon or baudhon kii tarah mriton kaa agnidah karne kaa rivaaj he.
