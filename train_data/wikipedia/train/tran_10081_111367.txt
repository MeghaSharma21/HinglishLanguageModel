 daasi 1981 main nirmit hindi film he. ise raaj khosla ne nirdeshit kiya he tathaa ke ke shrivastav ne iske samvaad lekhan kaa kaary kiya he.
 mata pita kii maut ke baad mangala apane mamaa ke yahan rahati he jahaan uski maami or 5 mameri bhai-bahan hai jo sabhi gaanv main garibi kaa jivan vyateet kar rahe hai. idhar anand ek andhaa gayak he or apane gaanv ke kai bacchon ko sangeet kii shiksha detaa he. mangala kaa jahaan bhi rist tay kiya jaataa he vahaan baraat ke ghar pahunchne se pehle hi dulhe kii kisi na kisi kaaran se maut ho jaati he. baad main ek jyotishi unhen salaah detaa he ki mangala kaa pati ek hi paristhiti main jivit rah saktaa he yadi vah aapng ho arthath langadaa athwa haath kataa hua athwa andhaa. idhar mangala bhagwaan se apane liye puja karke baahar nikalati he ki saamne anup miltaa he. kuch hi mulakaaton main donon main pyaar ho jaataa he.
