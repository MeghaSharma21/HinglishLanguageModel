 balpen yaa ballpen aadhunik yug main likhane kaa pramukh upkaran he. isamen ek syaahi-kaksh hota he jisamen apekshaakrit adhik shyanta vaali arthath gadhi syaahi bhari hoti he. yah syaahi ek chote se ball ke dwaara dhire-dhire chodi jaati he. jab likhane ke liye pen kaa munh kaagaj par ghumaya jaataa he tab yah bal kaagaj ke gharshan se gumti he jisase syaahi isse lagakar kaagaj par aa jaati he. yah bal ispaat, pital yaa tungston carbide kaa bana hota he or iskaa vyas lagbhag 0.7 mimi se lekar 1.2 mimi tak hota he. kaagaj par lagate hi syaahi lagbhag turant suukh jaati he. sasta, vishwasaneey, evam marammat na chaahane vaala hone ke kaaran ballpen ne fountain pen kii jagah le lee he.
