 pataakhaa ek choti-si visphotak aatishbaazi he jo mukhyat: baari aawaaj yaa shor utpann karne ke uddeshy se banaayi jaati he. pataakhon kaa avishkaar china main hua. isamen adhiktar kam-jvalanak barood prayog main laayaa jaataa he. pataakhe banane main prayogit mukhya rasayan krishi main prayog kiye jane vaale rasayan hote hai, jaise ki kalami shora va gandhak koyala prayog kiya jaataa he. yah badi hi aasaani se kisi bhi kheti-baadi kii dukaan se praapt ho jate he. puraatan kaal main is kaale barood kaa prayog topon main kiya jaataa tha or 20veen shatabdi main ise bandook kii goli bharne main bhi prayog kiya jane laga, jiske kaaran iskaa naam angrezi main "ganpowder" pad gaya.
 pataakhon ko angrezi main 'craikar', farsi main 'tarqe', gujarati main 'fataak' or portugali main 'panachaaun' kehete hai.
