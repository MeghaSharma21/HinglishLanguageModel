 andaman sagar bengal kii khadi ke dakshin purv, myanmar ke dakshin, thailand ke pashchim or andaman dweep samuh ke purv me sthit ek jal sangrah he. yah hind mahaasaagar kaa ek bhaag he. ye uttar se dakshin tak lagbhag 1,200 kimi or purv se pashchim tak lagbhag 650 kimi main failaa hua he or iskaa kul bhugolic kshetrafal 7,97,000 varg kimi he. isaki ausat gaharaai 870 meter he or adhiktam gaharaai 3,777 meter he.
 apane dakshin pashchimi kone par andaman sagar sankara hota jaataa he or malaka kii khadi kaa nirmaan karata he, jo malaysia ke malay prayadweep or indonesia ke sumatra dweep ko alag karata he.
