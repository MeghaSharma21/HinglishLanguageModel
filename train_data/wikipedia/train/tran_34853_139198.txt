 chu tsi, jise dakshin ke geet or chu ke geet bhi kaha jaataa he, ek chini bhashaa kaa kavita-sangrah he jisaki rachana paaramparik rup se chini itihaas ke jhagadate rajyon ke kaal main chu yuan or song yu dwaara kii gai maani jaati he, haalaanki isaki kuch kavitaayen shaayad baad ke haan rajvansh kaal main likhi jaakar isamen sammilit ho gayi. isamen 17 mukhya vibhaag hai. is sangrah main us samay ke dakshini china ke chu rajya kii bhinn samskruti jhalkati he, jo baaqi china se alag thi.
