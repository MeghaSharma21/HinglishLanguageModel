 nildarpan bangla kaa prasiddh naatak he jiske rachayita dinabandhu mitra hai. isaki rachana 1858-59 main hui. yah bengal main nil vidroh kaa andolan kaa kaaran bana. yah bengali rangamanch ke vikaas kaa agradoot bhi bana. kolkata ke 'neshnal theatre' main san 1872 main khelaa gaya yah pehla vyavsaayik naatak tha.
 bangla naatakaar dinabandhu mitra kaa 1860 main prakashit naatak ‘nil darpan’ ek shashakt natya kriti hi nahin, ek mahatvapurn aitihaasik dastavej bhi he. is naatak main bengal main nil kii kheti karne vaale angrejon dwaara shoshan, bhartiya kisaanon ke upar ammanushik atyaacharon kii badi bhavpurn abhivyakti hui he.
 naatak kii bengali samaaj or angrej shaasha donon main apane-apane dhang se teevr pratikriya hui. yahi nahin, is naatak ko padhakar us jamaane kii carch mishanri socity ke padri reverend james laung bahut dravit hue hai or unhone nilkaron dwaara shoshan ke pratvadswarup naatak kaa anuwaad angreji main prakashit kiya to un par angreji sarkaar dwaara mukadmaa chalaaya gaya or unhen ek maheene kii jail kii sajaa mili.
 bangla ke pehle saarvajanik, tikat bikri se chalane vaale manch par yah naatak jab 1872 main khelaa gaya to ek ore darshakon kii bheed umad padi or duusari ore angreji akhabaaron main is par badi tikhi tippanee hui. 1876 main angrej sarkaar dwaara drametic parfarmensej control act laana nil darpan jaise naatakon kii vidrohi bhaavana kaa daman bhi ek uddeshy tha. is naatak kaa aitihasik mahatva to he hi, iske alaava tatkaaleen angreji shashan main nyaay ke paakhand or pakshpatpurna vyavhaar par bhi prakaash padataa he.
