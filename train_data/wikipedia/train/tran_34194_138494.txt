 purang zila, jise chini lahaze main burang zila kehete hai, tibet kaa ek zila he jo us desh ke dakshin-pashchimi hisse main bharat or nepal kii seemaa ke saath sthit he. tibet par china kaa qabza hone ke baad yah chini prashasnic pranali main tibet swashasit pradesh ke ngaari vibhaag main padataa he. rutog zile kii rajdhani purang shahar he jise bhartiya or nepali log taklakot ke naam se jaanate aaen hai. hinduon ke pavitra maansarovar or kailash parvat tirthasthal isi zile main sthit hai.
