 pamolin tel aayaat ghotaale kerala rajya main varsh 1991-92 ke dauraan hui. karunakaran tha rajya or sattarudh morche ke mukhyamantri udief tha. yah uske aage rajya ke mukhyamantri ke rup main kaarykaal tha purv mukhya satarkta ayukta pi.je. thomas. us samay khaadya evam naagarik aapurti ke sachiv the. thomas bharat ke supreme court ne sivisi ke pad se ghumaya gaya tha kyonki adaalat ne paaya ki 8 wein number "shri thomas kerala pamolin dhara 13 ke tahat vishesh nyaayadhish, tiruvananthapuram aparaadhon ke liye, ke nyaayalay main lambit maamle main aarope lagaaya gaya tha 13  " bhartiya dand samhita kii.
 kerala sarkaar ne malaysian company se tad ke tel ke aayaat kaa faisla singapore main antarrashtriya keemat, jo kerala mantrimandal dwaara manjuur kiya gaya tha upar power or energy limited naam diya he. aayaat kii keemat prati ton 405.0 dollar he jo prati ton 392.25 dollar kii antarrashtriya keemat se adhik tha karne ke liye tay kii gai thi. aayaat aadesh thomas, jo tab tha kerala ke khaadya sachiv ne hastakshar kiye. jaanch agency ke maamle main aarope patr kaa kahana he ki is aadesh 2.32 se adhik kor ke ek sarkari khajaane ko nuksaan kaa kaaran bana. nirnay karne ke liye palm tel ke 15,000 ton aayaat kiya gaya tha. vipaksh aayaat main beyimaani se royaa. ek satarkta maamle karunakaran or saat anya ke khilaaf sahit Thomas.Thomas daayar kiya gaya tha 2003 main mili jamaanat. ek vishesh der kashmir karunakaran ne yachika chhod diya supreme court rahane proceedings.The adaalat disambar 2010 main unki mrityu ke baad kashmir karunakaran ke khilaaf kaarywahi band kar diya. 
 ek bahes thomas ke khilaaf naaraazgi jataai sivisi ke rup main sthiti sheersh ja rahaa he or palm tel ke maamle main aarope lagaaya. vah aakhir main supreme court ne is aadhaar he ki vah bhrashtachar ke ek maamle kii thi uske khilaaf lambit par 2011 ke farvari 3 par chhodane ke liye kaha gaya.
