 manikchandra vaajapeyi rashtriya patrakarita puraskaar madhya pradesh shashan dwaara sthapit ek patrakarita puraskaar he. yah madhyapradesh ke yashasvi patrkaar swa. manikchandra vaajapeyi kii smriti main diya jaataa he. puraskaar ke rup main ek lakh rupaye, shawl, shriphal or smriti pattica bhent kii jaati he. is puraskaar kii sthapana san 2006 main kii gayi thi.
 swa. vaajapeyi kii pratham punya tithi par san 2006 ke liye yah puraskaar varisht patrkaar tathaa rashtradharm ke purv sampadak shri bhagwatidhar vaajapeyi, ko pradaan kiya gaya. 
 san 2007 main yah puraskaar shri om prakaash kundra, bhopal ko diya gaya tha.
 san 2008 ke liye shri ramshankar agnihotri ko diya gaya he.
