 ganganagar jila yaa sriganganagar jila bharat ke rajasthan praant kaa ek shahar he. yahaan kaa mukhyalay sriganganagar shahar he. is zile ko "rajasthan kaa punjab " kaha jaataa he iskaa karan yahan punjabio kii bahutayat he. yah "rajasthan kaa anndaata " jese naamo se bhi mashhoor he.
 shri ganganagar zila 28.4-30.6 akshansh or 72.2-75.3 deshaantar ke madhya padataa he. is zile kaa kshetrafal 10,978 varg kilon meter yaa 1,115,466 hectare he purv main hanumangarh jila lagata he tathaa dakshin main bikaner zile se or pashchim main pakistan ke punjab praant ke bahavalnagar zile se or utar main bharat ke punjab rajya se lagata he. haryana rajya bhi shri ganganagar zile kii seemaa se 15 kimi dur he.
 is zile kaa naam bikaner ke maharaja gangassingh ke naam par padaa he. is samay yah bahut hi adhik nirjan sthano main se tha. pehle iskaa naam ram nagar tha. iskaa pracheen naam raamu jat kii dhani tha.vartmaan main ye bhubhag social garh or chungher jaise durgon ko apane aap main sanjoye hua he.
