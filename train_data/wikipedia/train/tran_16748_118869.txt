 liyu yang ek chini payalat or antariksh yatri he jo antariksh mishan shenzou 9 par ek chaalak dal ke sadsy ke rup main kaary karti he. 16 june 2012 ko, liyu antariksh main jane vaali pehali chini mahila bani. 
 liyu yang kaa janm 6 oktober 1978 ko jhengajhu, henon main linhu, aanyang mul ke ek parivaar main hua tha. usne changachun ke pipuls libration army air force aviation college se grajuation puri kii. liyu 1997 main piples libration army air force main shaamil ho gaye or ek payalat ke rup main ek udaan ikai ke up pramukh banane se pehle hi paatr ho gaye, jisamen pramukh ke pieeleef rank the. vah ek 1680 ghante kaa udaan anubhav rakhane vaali payalat he. antariksh yatri prashikshan ke do varshon ke baad, liyu ne ek or mahila, wang yaping ke saath, antariksh yatri ke liye ummidavaar ke rup main chune jane se pehle pareekshan main utkrushtataa praapt kii. liyu ko china ke antariksh stesan tiyaangong 1 ke pehle maanav mishan ke saath shen jhengu 9 ke dal ke liye chunaa gaya tha, jog hypeng ke saath, pehali baar dohraye gaye chini antariksh yatri or liyu vaing. liyu antariksh main jane ke liye pehali mahila chini antariksh yatri ban gaya. yah mishan 16 june 2012 ko shuru kiya gaya tha, 49 varsh pehle mahila antariksh yatri ke baad, antariksh yatri velentina terrescoco shuru kiya gaya tha. is maanav nirmit antariksh mishan ke dauraan, liyu ne antariksh dawa main bhi prayog kiya.
