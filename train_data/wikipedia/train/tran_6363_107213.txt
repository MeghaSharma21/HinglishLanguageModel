 vaishleshik tula yaa rasinic tula aisa tula he jo 1 milligram se bhi kam dravyaman ko maap saktaa he. atah is tula kii sanrachana main aise vishesh upaay kiye gayi hote hai ki dravyaman ke maapan main anya kisi kaarak kaa koi prabhaav na pade. udhaaharan ke liye, iskaa maapne vaala palada ek paardarshee deevaaron vaale kaksh main hota he or darwaja band karne kii vyavastha hoti he.
