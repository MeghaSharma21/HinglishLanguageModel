 durga saptshati ke siddh-mantra kaa arth hota he ki ve mantra jo maan ke liye prayukt kiya jaataa ho arthath mo durga ko naman karte hue unke charnon main apane aap ko samarpit karte hue unke siddh mantro kaa jaap karnaa jisase maan durga prasann hokar apane bhakton ko icchit fal prapti kaa avasar detii he. 
 durga saptshati ke siddh-mantra ke mantra vibhinn prakaar ke hote he, jo ki har ek ichhaao par nirbhar karti he, or in mantro kaa kam se kam 11, 21, 51 athwa 108 baar jaap karne se us vyakti kii manokamana purna hoti he.
 durga saptshati ke siddh-mantra ke mantra kuch is prakaar he:
 sharnaagat dinarth paritran parayane. 
 sarvasyartihare devi narayani namo stu te .
 sarvaswarupe sarveshey sarvashaktimanvite. 
 bhaye bhyastrahi no devi durge devi namo stu te .
 hinasti daityejunsi swanenapurya yaa jagat. 
 sa ghanta paatu no devi papebhyo nah sutaniv .
 rogaansheshanpahansi tushta rushta tu kaman sakalanbhishtan. 
 tvamashritanaan na vipannarana tvamashritha hmashrayatan prayanti .
 devkisut govind vasudev jagatpate. 
 dehi me tanyan krishna twamahan sharann gatah .
 evam devya varan labdhwa surathh kshatriyarshabhah
 jainti madgala kaali bhadrakaali kapalini. 
 durga kshamaa shiva dhatri swaha swadha namo stu te .
 sristi stithi vinaashaanaan shaktibhute sanatani. 
 gunashrey gunmaye narayani namo stu te .
  katyayani mahamaye mahayeginyadhishwari. 
 nandagopasute devi patin me kuru te namah . 
 patnin manorama dehi manovvrittanusaarinim. 
 tariniin durgasansar-sagarsya kulobhdvam .
 ye siddh mantra nimnlikhit pustako se lee gai he:
