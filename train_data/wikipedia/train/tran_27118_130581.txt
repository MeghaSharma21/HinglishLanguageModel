 mimaropa, jise kshetra 4-bi bhi kehete hai, dakshinpurvi asia ke philipinoz desh ke luzone dweep dal main ek prashasnic kshetra he. luzone dweepadal main shaamil hone ke baavjood yah luzone dweep par na hokar uske paas sthit kai dwipon ko milakar bana he. san 2002 tak yah or kalabarzone kshetra donon "dakshini tagalog mukhyabhumi" naamak kshetra kaa hissaa the jise vibhajit karke kalabarzone or mimaropa kshetra sthapit hue.
 mimaropa kaa naam is kshetra main nihit praanton ke naamon ko milakar gadha gaya: "mi", "maa", "ro" or "paa" . dhyaan den ki mindoro ek dweep he jo swayan do praanton - oxidental mindoro or oriental mindoro - main vibhajit he.
