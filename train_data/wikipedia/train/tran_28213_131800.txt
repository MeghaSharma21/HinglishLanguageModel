 anangarang, kalyanmall pranit ek kaamshastreey granth jisamen maithun sambandhi asana kaa vivarani he. 420 shlokon evam 10 sthal naamak adhyyon main vibhakt yah granth 'bhupamuni' ke rup main prasiddh kalavidgdh kshatriya vidwaan kalyanmall dwaara apane ghanishtha mitra lodivanshavtans ladkhan ke kautuhal ke liye rachi gayi thi. vishwa kii vividh bhashaaon main is granth ke anek anuwaad prakashit hai.
