 kasturba gandhi rashtriya smarak nyaas kasturba gandhi kii smriti main sthapit ek nyaas he. yah madhya pradesh ke indaur main sthit he. yahi ekmaatr aisa nyaas he, jise mahatma gandhi ne swayan sthapit kiya tha. 1944 main kasturba gandhi ke nidhan ke pashchaat gandhiji ne apane janmdiwas par saare desh se ekatrit ek karod 75 lakh 30 hajaar rupye kii raashi se is nyaas kii sthapana kii thi. iske pehle adhyaksh swayan gandhiji rahe. is nyaas dwaara desh bhar main gramin vikaas va mahilaon se sambandhit kai kaarykram chalaae ja rahe hai. kasturba gram main abhi tak sardaar vallabh bhai patel, dow rajendra prasad, pan. jawaharlal nehru, dow zakir hossain se lekar indira gandhi tak vibhinn kaaryakramon main aa chuki hai. indaur ke hukumchand seth ne iske liye 400 acre jameen daan di thi.
