 jammu se 62 ki.me dur sthit mansar jhil ek sundar sarovar he jisko jangalon se dhanke pahaad ghere hue hai. yah jhil lagbhag 1 mile lambi or aadha mile chaudee he. 32°41′46″N 75°08′49″E / 32.69611°N 75.14694°E / 32.69611; 75.14694 jammu se nearesth sthit shahar se baahar ke bhraman ke liye ek lokapriy sthaan he. is sthaan kii hindu dharm main manyata bhi he or isaki pavitrataa or kathaayen maansarovar jhil se judi hui hai.
 mansar jhil ke purvi tat par ek sheshnag ko samarpit mandir he, yah wo naag he jo bhagwaan vishnu ke liye shesh shayya banaata he or iske kai sir kehalaate hai. is sthaan par ek bada shilakhand he jiske upar kai lohe kii zanjeeren bandhi hui hai. ye sambhawatah sheshnag ke swaagat main pratiksharat chote sanpon ke pratik hai. navvivaahit yugal ko is jhil kii teen parikrama karnaa shubh or laabhadaayak mana jaataa he. 
 isi parisar main do anya pracheen mandir bhi hai jo umapati mahadev or nrisingh bhagwaan ko samarpit hai or kuch duuri par ek devi durga kaa mandir bhi he jahaan bahut se darshanaathi shriddhalu aate hai. tyoharon ke avasar par log jhil main dubkee bhi lagaate hai. kai hindu parivaar yahaan apane ladakon kaa mundan sanskaar bhi karwaane aate hai. mansar jhil main rajya paryaton vibhaag dwaara naukayan kii suvidha bhi uplabdh he.
 mansar jhil ek anya sadak se bhi judati he, jo pathankot ko sidhe udhampur se jodatii he. udhampur rashtriya rajmarg 1a par basaa samrik drushti se mahatvapurn shahar he. mansar yaa samba se udhampur kaa ek chota maarg bhi he jo jammu shahar ke baahar se nikal jaataa he or mansar jhil se nikalataa he. ek anya chota sarovar bhi mansar jhil se judaa he, surinsar sarovar. yah jammu shahar se 244 ki.me dur usee bypass maarg par sthit he.
