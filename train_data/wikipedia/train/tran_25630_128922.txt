 samskruti manishi sammaan har varsh bharat sarkaar ke samskruti mantralay dwaara pradaan kiya jaataa he. bharat sarkaar dwaara gathit dilli public librari board ke antargat ek samiti yogya ummidavaar kaa chayan karti he.is sammaan main dedh lakh rupye evam prashasti patr pradaan kiya jaataa he. bhartiya samskruti, maanviya mulyon, mahapurushon ke jivan, bharat ke gauravshaalee itihaas or pracheen vigyaan aadi se sambandhit aaajeevan samgr lekhan ke liye dilli librari board, samskruti mantralay bharat sarkaar dwaara diya yah pratishthit samskruti manishi sammaan sarvapratham prakhyat itihaaskaar tejpal singh dhama ko pradaan kiya gaya.uske baad har varsh kisi na kisi vidwaan ko diya jaataa he.
