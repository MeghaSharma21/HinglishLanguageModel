 
 chakotra nimbu-vansh kaa ek fal he, jo us vansh kii sabase badi jatiyon main se ek he. haalanki nimbu-vansh ke bahut se fal do yaa us se adhik jatiyon ke sankar hote hai, chakotra ek shuddh prakritik jati he. iske kachche fal kaa rang haraa, or pake hue kaa halkaa haraa yaa phir pilaa hota he. puraa bada hone par iske fal kaa vyas 15-25 semi or vazan 1 se 2 kilogram hota he. iske swaad main khatas or kuch meethaapan to hota he, lekin kadavaahat nahin. chakotra multah bhartiya upmahadweep or dakshinpurvi asia kshetra kii janmi hui jati he.
 vaise to chakotra swasth vyaktiyon ke liye laabhadaayak mana jaataa he, lekin chakotre ke ras main kuch aise rasayan hai jo kuch dawaaon kaa asar ko kam kar dete hai yaa unase milakar sharir main hanikaarak rasinic yogic bana dete hai:
 yah kuch hi davaayen hai jinpar chakotre kaa asar hota he. aisi or bhi davaayen hai jo is suchi main shaamil nahin.
