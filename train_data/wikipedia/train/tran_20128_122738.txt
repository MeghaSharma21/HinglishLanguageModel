 sulajja firodiya motwani ek bhartiya mahila udhyami he. vartmaan main ve kynetic motor company limited or kynetic ejineering limited ke sanyukt prabandh nideshak hai. ve kynetic kii ranneeti, bikri, vipanan or vitt gatividhiyon se judi hui hai. 
 sulajja kaa janm 26 agast 1970 ko pune main hua tha. unki praarambhik shiksha pune main hi hui. yaheen ke pune vishvavidhyalay se unhone 1990 main vaanijya vishay ke saath snaatak kii pariksha uteern kii. tatpashchaat ve aage kii padhai ke liye sanyukt rajya america chali gai or pittsburg main pratishthit caranegi melon vishvavidhyalay se emb kii degree lee. vidyaleey jivan main unhone hameshaa achche rank praapt kiye.
 sulajja apane swasthya ke bare main bahut jaagruk rahati he. ve hameshaa kii tarah, vishesh rup se badminton khel main dilchaspee rakhti he. unhone rashtriya str par badminton kii pratiyogitaaon main bhaag liya he. ve bhi skiing or scuba diving jaise saahasik khel ko sanchalit karne kii shaukeen he. unki shaadi manish motwani se hui, jisase unhe ek putr he. 
 sulajja ne kynetic company main shaamil hone se purv california sthit ek prasiddh nivesh vishleshiki company barraa international main chaar varshon tak apni sevaayen di. is dauraan unhone bharat main company ke parichaalan ko sthapit karne main ek sakriy bhagidaar kii bhoomika nibhaai. 4 may 2006 ko ve kynetic motor company limited ke sanyukt prabandh nideshak banaai gayi. ve kynetic kii ranneeti, bikri, vipanan or vitt gatividhiyon se sidhe judi hui hai.
 india today patrika ne unki vyayasayik gatividhiyon ke drushtigat unhe bharat kii sheersh pachchis udhyamiyon main shaamil karaate huye unhen "phase of milinium" se alankrita kiya he. unhen varsh 2002-2003 main vipanan or prabandhan santhaan vestowad ke dwaara mahila siio ke rup main utkrisht pradarshan hetu 'yang echievers award for bijness' se sammanit kiya ja chukaa he. isi varsh unhen 'bijness today' se yuva super achiver award bhi praapt hua.
