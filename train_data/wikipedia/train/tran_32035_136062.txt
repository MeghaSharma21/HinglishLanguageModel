 evor armastrang richards angreji ke prabhaavshaalee samalochak tathaa vaktaa the.
 ai.e. richards kaa janm san 1893 main hua. unka rachanakaal san 1924 se 1936 ke madhya mana jaataa he. harvard vishvavidhyalay main angreji ke professor rahe. ek darjan granthon main se ‘prisimple of litereri critisism’ sabase adhik prasiddh he. inkaa prabhaav hindi sahitya par spasht rup se dikhaai detaa he
