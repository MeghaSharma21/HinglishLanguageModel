 kavyanushasan praayah sangrah granth he. rajasekharake 'kavyamimasaa', mammatke 'kavyprakash', aanandavardhan ke 'dhvanyalok', abhinav guptake 'lochan' se paryaapt matraamein saamagree grahan kii he.moulikta ke vishyamen hemachandracharyaka apana swatantra mat he. hemchandra matse koi bhi granthkaar nayi cheez nahin likhata. yadhyapi mammatka 'kavyprakash' ke saath hemchandraka 'kavyanushasan' kaa bahut samya he. paryaapt sthanon par hemachandracharyane mammatka virodh kiya he. hemachandracharyake anusaar anand, yash ev kantatulya upadesh hi kavyake prayojan ho sakate he tathaa arthlabh, vyavhaar gyaan evam anisht nivritti hemchandrake matanusaar kavyake prayojan nahin he. 'kavyanushasan se kavyashasre ke pathakon kon samajane main sulabhta, sugamawa hoti he. mammatka 'kavyprakash' vistrut he, suvyavasthit he, sugam nahin he. aganit tikaaen hone par bhi mammatka 'kavyprakash' durgam rah jaataa he. 'kavyanushasan' main is durgamata ko 'alankarchudamani' evam 'vivek' ke dwaara sugamawa main parinat kiya gaya he.'kavyanushasan' main spasht likhte he ki ve apana mat nirdhaaran abhinavgupt evam bharat ke aadhaar par kar rahe hai. sachmuch anya grantho-granthkaaro ke udvaran prastut karte hemchandra apana swayan kaa swatantra mat, style, dashticonse moulik he. granth evam granthkaaron ke naam se sanskrit-sahitya, itihaas par prakaash pasta he. sabhi str ke pathak ke liye sarvotkrishth pathyapustak he. vishesh gyanavridvi kaa avasar diya he. atah acharya hemchandra ke 'kavyanushasan' kaa adhyayan karne ke pashchaat phir dusraa granth padhne kii zarurat nahin rahati. sampoorn kavy-shaastra par suvyavasthit tathaa surachit prabandh he.
