 bhartiya dand samhita kii dhara 7 un abhivyaktiyo ke bare main he jinhen ek baar samajhaayaa gaya he. iske tahat har abhivyakti, jo is samhita ke kisi bhi bhaag main samajhaai gayi he vivaran ke saath is samhita ke anuroop har hisse main prayog ki jaati he.
