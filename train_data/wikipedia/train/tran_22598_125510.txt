 nepal main 'nepal kaa samvidhan-2015' lagu he. nepal main barason ke rajnatik uthal-puthal or hinsak sangharsho ke baad 20 sitambar 2015 ko nayaa samvidhan lagu ho gaya. isane nepal kaa antarim samvidhan-2007 kii jagah lee he. anivaary avadhi main ek samvidhan kaa nirmaan karne main pehali samvidhan sabha kii vifaltaa ke baad duusari samvidhan sabha ke dwaara yah samvidhan taiyaar kiya gaya tha.
 nepal ke nae samvidhan ke anusaar,
 april 2006 main jab raja gyanendra ne satta kii sari shaktiyaan nirvasit pratinidhiyon kii sabha ko saunpane kii anumati di to sabhi dalon ne milakar ek antarim sarkaar bana di. antarim sarkaar ko hi shashan kaa kaarybhaar va samvidhan kaa nirmaan karnaa tha. usee kadi main nepal kaa antarim samvidhan banaaya gaya. kintu is antarim samvidhan main kuch pahalu vivaadaaspad the. isi kaaran yah samvidhan sabhi nepali logon ne sweekaar nahin kiya. antatah 2015 main naye samvidhan kaa nirmaan hua jisamen sabhi ko saath lekar chalane kii baat kahi gai.
