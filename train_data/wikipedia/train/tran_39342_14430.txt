 shri mansa ram san 1737 se 1740 tak kaashi rajya ke naresh rahe. mansa ram kaashi naresh raja balavant singh ke pita the jinka janm banaras ke gangapore gaanv main hua tha. jameendaar mansa ram banaras,jonpur or mirzapur ke aamil banane se pehle avadh ke nawab safadarajang ke maatahat the lekin rajasav prabandhan ke apane kaam-kaaj ke chalate wo apane putr balavant singh ko kaashi naresh kaa khitaab dilwane main kaamyaab rahe. is khitaab par baad main dilli ke mughal badshaah ne bhi muhar laga di.
