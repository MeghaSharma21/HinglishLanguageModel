 julian paul asanje, vikiliks ke sansthaapak hai. vikiliks par kaam karne se pehle ve ek computer programmer or haikar the. vikiliks par unke kiye kaaryon ke liye 2008 main unhen iconomist freedom of expressant award or 2010 main saim adams award pradaan kiya gaya. unhone iraq yuddh se jude lagbhag chaar lakh dastauez apni website par jari kiye the jisamen america, england evam nato kii senaaon ke gambhir yuddh aparaadh karne ke akatya pramaan maujuud the.
 america ke raashtrapati baraak obama tak ne unhen iske kilaaf chetavanee di thi. iske pashchaat giraftaar ke dar se unhen chhip-chhip kar jivan bitaana padaa.
 ek saakshaatkaar main asanje ne kaha ki vah jhuthe naamon se hotalon main rah rahe hai, apane baalo ko duusare rangon main rang rahe hai or credit card ke sthaan par nakad raashi kaa upyog kar rahe hai. iske liye aksar unhen apane doston se paise udhaar lene padate hai.
 30 navambar 2010 ko antarrashtriya lok abhiyojan adhikari ne asanje ke kilaaf red corner notice jari kiya, unke khilaaf yon aparaadhon kaa mukadmaa darj kiya gaya. 7 disambar 2010 ko asanje ko landan main giraftaar kar liya gaya.
