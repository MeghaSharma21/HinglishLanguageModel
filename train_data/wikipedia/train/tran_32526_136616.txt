 aisi koi kriya jo anukriya kii sankhya main vruddhi karti he punarbalan kehalaati he. maanav dwaara siikhe hue adhikanshtah vyavhaar kii vyakhya ham kriyaprasut anubandhan ke sahyog se kar sakate hai. kriyaprasut anubandhan main punarbalan kii nirnaayak bhoomika he. yah nakaaratmak yaa sakaaratmak ho saktaa he. 
 skinner ke prayog main chuha baar-baar liver dabaane kii anukriya karata he or bhojan praapt kar letaa he. ise sakaaratmak punarbalan kehete hai. is prakaar ek sakaaratmak punarbalan yaa puraskaar vah pravriti he jisase us vishisht vyavhaar ke prabhaav ko seekhney ko bal miltaa he. sakaaratmak punarbalan vah koi bhi uddipan he jiske madhyam se us vishisht anukriya ko aage jane kaa bal miltaa he. . ‘nakaaratmak’ shabd punarbalan kii prakruti ko bataata he .yah punarbalan he kyonki yah anukriyaaon kii sankhya main vruddhi karata he. is vidhi ko ‘palaayan’ adhigam kaha gaya kyonki chuha agar liver ko dabaata he to aghat se batch saktaa he. duusare tarah ke nakaaratmak punarbalan ke parinamswaroop jo anubandhan hota he use bachaav dwaara siikhnaa kaha gaya jisamen chuha liver dabaakar aghat se batch saktaa he. palaayan yaa bachaav dwaara seekhney main nakaaratmak punarbalan kaa prayog kiya jaataa he or praani palaayan dwaara isse batch jaataa he.
 anukriyaaon ko punarbalit kaise kiya ja saktaa he? yah nirantar punarbalan yaa aanshik punarbalan ke prayog se ho saktaa he. nirantar punarbalan main prayek uchit anukriya ko punarbalit kiya jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye jitani baar bhi chuha liver dabaata he use utani hi baar plate main bhojan prastut kiya jaataa he. vaikalpik taur par anukriyaaon ko kabhi aanshik yaa kabhi rukaawat ke saath punarbalit kiya jaataa he. nirantar punarbalan ke milane se naye vyavhaar sthapit karne yaa sudrudh karne main bahut sahaayta milti he. duusari taraf aanshik punarbalan siikhe huye vyavhaar ko banaaye rakhane main adhik shaktishaalee he.
