 bharat ke rajya punjab main jain dharm maanane waalon kii sankhya 0.2 % he. rajya main jain samudaay ke ek sadsy alpsankhyak ayog main niyukt kiya gaya he, parantu rajya sarkaar ne jain dharm ke maanane waalon ko alpsankhyakon ke rup main kisi adhyadesh ke madhyam se manyata nahin di he. 
 pramukh rup se punjab main nimna likhit jain mandir hai:
 in mandiron main aradhana ko asht prakaari puja kaha jaataa he. jain log murtiyon ko snaan dete hai, un par sandal malate hai, phool chadhaate hai, agarbatti jalaate hai, deep yaa mombatti jalaate hai or murtiyon ke aage pake hue chaaval yaa fal rakhte hai. saadhaaran rup se shraddhalu nange paanv mandir ke darshan karte hai apane sar ko kapade se dhakte hai.
