 paripath vichedak yaa 'paripath viyojak' swatahchalit vaidyut switch he jo dosha aadi kii dasha main kaary karata he jisase doshi bhaag swasth bhaag se alag kar diya jaataa he or duusare upkaran kharaab hone se batch jate hai. iskaa mul kaam doshapurn sthiti kii pahchaan karke doshi bhaag ko jane vaali vidyut shakti ko shighratisheeghra kaat dena he. fus se yah is maamle main alag he ki ise riset karke punah vidyut pradaay chaalu kiya ja saktaa he. 
 paripath vichedak bhinn-bhinn aakaar, kshamta, evam prakaar ke hote hai. 
 paripath vichedkon ke vargeekaran ke kai aadhaar ho sakate hai-jaise unki voltata shrenee, sanrachana kaa prakaar, viched karne kii vidhi aadi.
