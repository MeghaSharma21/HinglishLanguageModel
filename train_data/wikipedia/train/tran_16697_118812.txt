 nirdeshaank: 25°18′N 91°42′E / 25.3°N 91.7°E / 25.3; 91.7cherapunji bharat ke uttar-purvi rajya meghalaya kaa ek shahar he. yah shilong se 53 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. yah sthaan duniya bhar main mashhoor he. haal hi main iskaa naam cherrapunji se badalakar sohara rakh diya gaya he. vastav main sthaneey log ise sohara naam se hi jaanate hai. 
 yah sthaan duniyabar main sarvaadhik barish ke liye janaa jaataa he. iske najadik hi nohakalikai jharnaa he, jise paryatak jarur dekhne jate hai. yahaan kai gupha bhi hai, jinamein se kuch kai kilometre lambi hai. cherrapunji bangladesh seemaa se kaaphi kareeb he, isliye yahaan se bangladesh ko bhi dekha ja saktaa he.
