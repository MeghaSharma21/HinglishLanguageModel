 carmal chikitsa kendra israel ke hifa main sthit ek aam aspataal he. yah clelit swasthya sudhaar sanghathan ke antargat aata he. ladi davis aspataal carmal chikitsa kendra kaa ek bhaag he. carmal aspataal hifa, iske upnagareey kshetron or anya uttari kshetron main seva pradaan karata he. iskaa nirmaan 1967 main kiya gaya tha. da. avi goldberg yahan ke vartmaan nideshak hai.
 carmal chikitsaalay main utkrisht hriday-rog vigyaan, sanvahani shalya-chikitsa or vaksh shalya-chikitsa vibhaag hai. is aspataal main 4,400 bistar uplabdh he or yah sankhya samooche israel main uplabdh atipaati aspataal bistaron kii kul sankhya kaa ek tihaai he.
 duusare lebanon yuddh se sabak lete hue, 2010 main is aspataal main ek nae apatkaaleen kaksh kaa nirmaan armbh hua. isaki deevaare prabalit kancreet se nirmit he or 60 centimetre moti hai taaki prakshepastra se hue hamalon se ise surakshit rakhaa ja sake. nirmaan kaary puraa hone ke pashchaat apatkaaleen kaksh ko 2011 ke disambar ke madhya main khol diya gaya tha.
