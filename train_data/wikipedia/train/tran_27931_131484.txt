 kodamdesar bikaner se 15 mile pashchim main ek chota sa gaanv he, jo isi naam ke talab or uske kinare sthapit bhairav kii murti ke liye prasiddh he. bhairav kii murti jangalu main basane ke samay swany raav bika ne mandor se laakar yahaan sthapit kii thi. yahaan par 16veen tathaa 17veen shatabdi ke chaar lekh hai. inamen se sabase pracheen lekh talab ke purv kii ore bhairav kii murti ke nikat ke kirtistambh kii do ore khuda he. yah kirtistambh laal pathar kaa he tathaa iske chaaron ore devi-devataaon kii murtiyon khudi he. is lekh se paaya jaataa he ki 1459 i0 main bhadrapada sudi ko raav rinmal ke putr raav jodha ne yah talab khudhvaya or apni mata kodmade ke nimitt kirtistambh sthapit karavaaya.
