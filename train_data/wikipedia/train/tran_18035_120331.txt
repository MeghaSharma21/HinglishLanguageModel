 brahmad kaa janm ek mahavisphot ke parinamswaroop hua. isi ko mahavisphot siddhanth yaa big baing siddhanth kehete hai., jiske anusaar se lagbhag baarah se chaudaa arab varsh purv sampurna brahmad ek parmanvik ikai ke rup main tha. us samay maanviya samay or sthaan jaisi koi avadharana astitv main nahin thi. mahavisphot siddhant ke anusaar lagbhag 13.7 arab varsh purv is dhamaake main atyadhik urja kaa utsajarn hua. yah urja itani adhik thi jiske prabhaav se aaj tak brahmad failata hi ja rahaa he. sari bhautik manyataayen is ek hi ghatana se paribhaashit hoti hai jise mahavisphot siddhant kaha jaataa he. mahavisphot naamak is mahavisphot ke dhamaake ke maatr 1.43 second antaraal ke baad samay, antariksh kii vartmaan manyataayen astitv main aa chuki thim. bhautikee ke niyam lagu hone lag gayi the. 1.34wein second main brahmad 1030 guna fail chukaa tha or quark, lapton or photone kaa garm dravya ban chukaa tha. 1.4 second par quark milakar proton or neutron banane lage or brahmad ab kuch thanda ho chukaa tha. hydrogen, helium aadi ke astitva kaa aarambh hone laga tha or anya bhautik tatv banane lage the. 
 big baing siddhant ke aarambh kaa itihaas aadhunik bhautikee main jorge lemaitre ne likhaa hua he. lemaitre ek roman catholic padri the or saath hi vaigyanik bhi. unka yah siddhanth albert instin ke prasiddh saamaanya saapekshavaad ke siddhant par aadhaarit tha. yah eadwin habal the jinhone varsh 1929 main yah bataaya ki sabhi gallexi ek duusare se sikud rahe hai. havisphot siddhant do mukhya dharanaon par aadhaarit hota he. pehla bhautik niyam or doosraa brahmand siddhant. brahmand sidwant ke mutabik brahmad sajaateey or samdaishik hota he. 1964 main british vaigyanik peter higggus ne mahavisphot ke baad ek second ke arben bhaag main brahmad ke dravyon ko milane vaale bhaar kaa siddhant pratipaadit kiya tha, jo bhartiya vaigyanik satyendra nath bose ke boson siddhant par hi aadhaarit tha. ise baad main 'higggus-boson' ke naam se janaa gaya. is siddhant ne jahaan brahmad kii utpatti ke rahasyon par se pardaa uthaaya, vahiin uske swaroop ko paribhaashit karne main bhi madad kii.
