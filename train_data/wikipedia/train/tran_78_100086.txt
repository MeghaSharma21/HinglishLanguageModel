 makraana bhartiya rajya rajasthan kii nagar parishad evam tehsil he. is tehsil main 136 se adhik gaanv shaamil hai, or is prakaar yah nagaur jile kii sabase badi tehsil he. makraana yahan ke shwet pathar or sangemarmar ke liye prasiddh he. makraana ke sangemarmar kaa upyog tajmahal ke nirmaan main kiya gaya tha. makraana bada kasbaa he, jisamen vibhinn sthanon par sangemarmar kaa khanan kiya jaataa he. yahan ke nivaasiyon kaa mukhya vyavsaay sangemarmar khanan he.
