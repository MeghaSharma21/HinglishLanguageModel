 odisi odishapraant bharat kii ek shastreey nrity style he.adhyatan kaal main guru kelucharan mahapaatr ne iskaa puservistaar kiya. 
 odissi nrity ko puratathvik saakshyon ke aadhaar par sabase puraane jivit nrity roopon main se ek mana jaataa he. iskaa janm mandir main nrity karne vaali devdasiyon ke nrity se hua tha. odissi nrity kaa ullekh shilalekhon main miltaa he. ise brahmeshwar mandir ke shilalekhon main darshaya gaya he.
