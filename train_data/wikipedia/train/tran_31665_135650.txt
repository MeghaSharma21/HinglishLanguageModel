 shatkhandgam digambar jain sampradaay kaa sarvocch or sabase pracheen pavitra dharmagranth he. digambar parampara ke anusaar mul dharmavaidhanik shaastra mahavir bhagwaan ke nirvana ke kuch shatabdiyon ke baad hi lupt ho gayi the. atah, shatkhandgam ko aagam kaa darja diya gaya he or ise sabase shraddhey mana gaya he. digambaron ke liye shatkhandgam kii ahmiyat is baat se lagaayi ja sakati he, ki jis din shatkhandgam par dhawala tikaa ko puraa kiya gaya tha, us din ko shrut panchami ke rup main manaaya jaataa he.
 aisa kaha jaataa ki shatkhandgam digambar sadhu acharya dharsen ke dwaara die gaye aagam ke maukhik upadeshon par aadhaarit he. manyata anusaar, shastron ke ghatate gyaan se chintit hokar unhone do saadhuon, acharya pushpadanth or acharya bhootbali ko apane ashrayasthal, girnaar parvat, gujarat main sthit chandra gupha, main bulaayaa. acharya dharsen ne vrihad mul pavitra jain gyaan main se unhone dono muniyon ko yaad gyaan sampreshit kiya. unhone aagam ke paanchaven or barahawein angon ke kuch bhaagon kaa bhi gyaan diya. yah sara gyaan donon saadhuon dwaara sutron ke rup main lipibaddh kar liya gaya. acharya pushpadanth ne shruwaat ke 177 sutara likhe or acharya bhootbali ne baaki sutara likhe. kul lagbhag 6000 sutara likhe gayi.
