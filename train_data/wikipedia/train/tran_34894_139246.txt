 abhidhanppadipika pali kaa pracheen shabdkosh he. iske rachayita moggallan thero hai jinhone 12veen shatabdi main isaki rachana kii thi.
 is paalikosh kii rachana sanskrit ke amarkosh kii reeti se hui he or usamen pali ke paryaayavachi shabdon kaa sankalan kiya gaya he. 'amarkosh' ke anek shlokon kaa bhi isamen paliroopantaran he. isamen swarcond, bhupand or shramnay cond aise teen vibhaag hai. isaki rachana pratham parakrambahu ke shasankaal main hui maani jaati he. is kosh par 14veen shatee main rachit ek tikaa bhi milti he.
