 akal daad un daanton ke naam hai jo aakhir main nikalate hai. adhiktar logon ko chaar akal daad hote hai - munh ke har kone main ek - ye zyaadaatar javaani main nikalate hai.
 yah sambhav he ki akal daad jabadon kii haddi main atak jaaen, yaa phir nikalen hi nahin. aisa hone par baaki daant thasane yaa khisakane lag sakate hai, yaa najdiki daanton main sadan yaa sankraman ho saktaa he yaa masoodon main beemaari fail sakati he. jabadon main akal daad ke atakane kaa kaaran yah ho saktaa he ki ve kisi asaadhaaran avasthaa main hon, jaise ki sapaat, jisaki vajah se wo saamaanya rup se baahar nahin nikal sakate. adhiktar logon ko atake hue akal daad ko nikalwane kii salaah di jaati he. daant kii avasthaa ke anusaar, tisare daad ko aapke daant ke doctor ke daftar main, kisi bahari rogi clinics main yaa aspataal main bhartee karke nikaala ja saktaa he. saamanyatya ek vyask kii akal daad nikalne ke baad uske daanton kii sankhya 32 ho jaati he |
