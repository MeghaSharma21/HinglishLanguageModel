 yantrik abhiyantriki  tarah-tarah kii mashiinon kii banaavat, nirmaan, chaalan aadi kaa saiddhanthik or vyaavhaarik gyaan he. yantrik abhiyantriki, abhiyantriki kii sabase puraani or vistrut shakhaon main se ek he. yantrik abhiyantriki 18veen shatabdi main europe main audyogic kranti ke dauraan ek kshetra ke rup main ubhari he, lekin, iskaa vikaas duniya bhar main kai hajaar saal main hua he. 19veen sadi main bhautikee ke kshetra main vikaas ke ek parinaam ke rup main yantric abhiyantriki vigyaan saamne aayaa.
 iske adharbhoot vishay hai:
 yantric abhiyantriki kai yantriki vigyaan ke vibhaagon ke samooh ke rup main maani ja sakati he.| iname se kuch upavibhag, jo adhiktar purv snaatak paathyakram main padhaaye jate hai, neeche soochibaddh kiye gayi hai. inamen se kuch keval yantric abhiyantriki se hi sambandhit hai jabaki kuch yantric abhiyantriki or anya vibhaagon ke sangyojan hai.
