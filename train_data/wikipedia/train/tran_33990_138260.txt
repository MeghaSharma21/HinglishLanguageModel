 alaay-paar parvat shrunkhlaa madhya asia main pamir parvaton kii uttartam shakha he. yah us sthaan par sthit hai jahaan pamir pahaad or tiyaan shaan ke parvat aakar milte hai. alaay-paar parvat kirgizstan ke osh praant or tajikistan ke kuhistoni-badakhshaan swashasit praant ke bich kii sarhad par khadey hai. inase uttar main alaay vaadi or dakshin main musku nadi he. 
 yah alaai parvat se 5,000 foot unchaa he tathaa laal balua pathar kaa bana he. isaki chotiyon main mount stallin evam mount lenin ullekhneey hai. alaay-paar shrunkhlaa kaa sabase unchaa pahaad 7,134 meter unchaa lenin parvat he.
 yah parvat atyadhik himachhadit rahata he. iske dakshin or pamir kaa pathaar he. is varg kii anya shreniyon kii tarah yah bhi tritiy kalp kaa mana jaataa he, parantu vastav main ye sabhi shreniyan bahut pehle kii nirmit hai jo baad main ksharan ke kaaran praaia: samabhumi avasthaa main pahunch gai or ant main alpine utthan ke samay upar uth gayi.
