 junun 1992 main bani hindi bhashaa kii daraavnee film he. isko mahesh bhatta ne nirdeshit kiya or mukhya kalakaar rahul roy, avinaash vadhavan or puja bhatta hai. film safal rahi thi. film main daraane ke liye morphing kaa prayog kiya gaya he.
 nita bombai, bharat main apni maa or pita ke saath ek madhyam vargeey jeevanstyli jiti he or ek aspataal main sarjan ke rup main kaam karti he. vah vivaah yogya umr kii he or ravi ke naam ke ek sangharshrat sangeetkaar / gayak se pyaar karti he. ye uski maan ko pasand nahin he, jo chaahati he ki vah kisi aise vyakti se shaadi kare jo amir ho. ek din ek buree tarah ghayal purush ko aspataal main bhartee karaayaa jaataa he. use mrit ghoshit kiya jaataa he, lekin wo nita kii madad se thik ho jaataa he or use chuttee di jaati he. vah nita ke ghar jaataa he, khud ko amir vikram chouhan ke rup main pesh karata he or uske mata-pita se nita kaa haath maangata he. vah turant sweekaar karte hai. lekin nita ravi se shaadi karnaa chaahati he or uske saath bhaagane kii yojana bana rahi he. us durbhagyapurna din, ravi vahaan aane main naakaam rahaa or toote dil se nita ne vikram se vivaah karne kaa faisla kiya. vivaah bade dhoomdhaam or samaaroh ke saath hota he. jab ravi ne nita ke jivan main phir se pravesh karne kaa prayaas kiya, to usne use manaa kar diya. vah nahin jaanati ki ravi kii yojana vikram ne kharaab kii thi or jo vastav main din ke dauraan ek insaan he or madhyaraatri main ek krur aadamkhor baagh main badal jaataa he - or vah uskaa aglaa bhojan bhi ho sakati he.
 sabhi nadeem-shravan dwaara sangitabaddh.
