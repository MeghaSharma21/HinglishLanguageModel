 manushyon kii anuvanshiki main matruvansh samooh jetti yaa mitocandria-diene hapalogrup JT ek matruvansh samooh he. isaki do shakhaayen - matruvansh samooh je or matruvansh samooh tii - europe or madhya purv main kaafii faili hui hai.
 anumaan he ke jis stri se yah matruvansh shuru hua vah aaj se lagbhag 50,300 varsh pehle dakshin pashchimi asia main rahati thi.
 angrezi main "vansh samooh" ko "hapalogrup", "pitruvansh samooh" ko "waahe chromosome hapalogrup" or "matruvansh samooh" ko "amtidiene hapalogrup" kehete hai.
