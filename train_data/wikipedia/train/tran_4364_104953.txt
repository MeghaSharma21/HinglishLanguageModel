 christopher maaikel prat ek america abhinayta he. prat apane televijan bhoomikaaon ko lekar shoharat main aaye the, vishesh rup se enbisi ke 2009-2015 ke sitacom parks and ricriation main unki endi doir bhoomika ke liye, jahaan use aalochakon kii prashansa praapt hui, or sarvshereshth sahaayak abhinayta ke liye critics chwis televijan award namit kiya gaya tha. apane filmi kariyar kii shuruyaat mukhyadhara kii filmon main sahaayak bhoomikaaon ke saath kii jisamen wanted, jenifer'sa body, manibal, da five-year ingerement, jiro dark therty, delivery main, or har shaamil hai.
 prat ko safalataa, 2014 kii do vyavsaayik rup se safal filmon se mili, jo thi computer anemated kamedi lego movie or maarval studio kii super hero film gardias of da gallexi. 
 2015 main, prat ne jurassic park shrankhla kii, chauthi film jurassic world, main abhinay kiya, jo kii $1.6 arab ke box offis colekson ke saath uski ab tak kii sabase adhik aarthik rup se safal phikmon me se ek thi. prat aage da magnificent seven or paisangers main bhi mukhya bhoomika nibhaai.
 2007 main, tech me home tunit film ke samay prat kii mulaakaat abhinetri ana feris, se hua. 2008 main unhone sagaai kar lee or 9 july, 2009 ko bali, indonesia main shaadi kar lee. ve los angeles, california ke pados main hollywood hills main rahete he.
