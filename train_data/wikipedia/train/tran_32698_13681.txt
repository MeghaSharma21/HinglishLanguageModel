 sanyukt mexiki rajyon yaa mexico kaa dhvaj uttar america desh mexico kaa rashtriya dhvaj he jo tirangaa he or hare, safed or laal rangon se bana he or dhvaj ke madhya main safed pattee par udaan ke liye taiyaar chil he. samay ke saath in rangon ke arth main bhi parivartan aayaa he. ye rang mexico dwaara spain se apni swatantrata kii ladaai ke dauraan apanaae gaye the. vartmaan dhvaj 1968 main apanaaya gaya tha, lekin dizine 1821 se upyog main he. 
 vartmaan rashtriya chinh kaa kanoon, rashtriya shastrastra, dhawj or gaan kaa kanoon jo raashtra dhawj ke upyog kaa niaman karata he, 1984 main bana tha. laal, safed or haraa mexico kii rashtriya mukti sena ke rang hai. kendra main jo pratik chinh he vah pracheen azetek nagar tenoctilan kaa he, jo unke samrajya kii rajdhani tha. yah ek anushruti kaa samaran karaata he jisane azetek logon ko ek jhil-dweep par basane ke ke liye prerit kiya. rajya-chihn kii akruti sabase ant main, 1968 main sanshodhit kii gai thi. azetek kii dantakatha ke anusaar unka nagar us sthaan par milegaa, jahaan par ek chil cactus par saanp ko kha rahi hogi. rashtriya rangon main riban rajya-chihn ke neeche he.
