 bhojya padaarthon ke upar inkaree vikiran daalane se usamen upasthit sookshmjeev, jeevanu, vishaanu evam keet aadi nasht ho jate hai. is kriya ko khaadya padaarthon kaa kirann yaa 'khaadya padaarthon kaa vikiranan' kaha jaataa he. iske alaava khaadya padaarthon ke vikiranan ke falswaroop avaanchit ankuran ruk jaataa he; falon ke pakane kii kriya dhimi pad jaati he ; ras utpaadaka main vruddhi ho jaati he evam anya laabh hote hai. aisa nahin he ki vikiranan ke kaaran khaadya padaarth radiodharmee ho jate hai kintu kuch sthitiyon main maamuli sa rasinic parivartan avashya ho saktaa he.
