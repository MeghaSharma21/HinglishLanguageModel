 67vaan vaarshik kaan filmotsav 14 se 25 may 2014 tak yojit kiya gaya. newjiland ke film nirdeshak jine campion mukhya pratiyogita jeuri ke mukhiya raheen. palm door kaa puraskaar turki film winter sleep ko mila.
 yah mahotsav bahupratikshit film gres of monako se armbh hua. is film kaa nirdesan oleviyar dahaan ne kiya he or isamen nichol kidman ne gres keli kaa kirdaar nibhaayaa he. film sargiyo leone dwaara nirdeshit 1964 kii film e phisphul of dollars kaa 4ke sanskaran samaapan raat ko pradarshit kii gaya. europea sansad chunaavon ke kaaran 25 may 2014 ke sthaan par 24 may 2014 ko hi palm door vijeta kii ghoshnaa kii gai or an sarten riguard kii ghoshnaa 23 may ko kii gai. samaaroh ke poster 1963 kii federico felini kii film otto e mozzo ke abhinayta marchelo mastroyani ne chitrit kiya.
 mukhya pratiyogita sahit 2014 ke mahotsav ke liye filmon kaa aadhikarik chayan 17 april 2014 ko ghoshit kiya gaya. francisi abhinayta lambert wilson ne udghaatan or samaapan samaaroh mejabani kii.
 samaaroh main palm door puraskaar turki ke film nirdeshak nuri bilj jelaan kii winter sleep ne jeeta.
